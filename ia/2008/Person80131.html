Ingo Dean’s Summary

Experienced IT Senior Manager with proven success leading Global IT, planning and overseeing all IT functions. Problem solver skilled at understanding how IT can facilitate business requirements.
• Consolidated Virage Logic's development "islands" into a single cohesive network using VPN, MPLS, remote access, and video conferencing technology. This enabled the company to grow annual revenue from $20M to $59M with employee growth of 50 employees to 400 globally.
• Transformed Virage Logic's separate System Administration groups into the Global IT organization, while implementing a Global IT Service Desk embodying ITIL concepts with a defined escalation process and step by step customer feedback. Earned an overall ticket satisfaction rating of 96.7% (8.7/9.0 scale).

Leader proficient in defining strategy, leveraging technology, and fostering stability.
• When Sarbanes/Oxley was enacted, formally documented all IT processes and bolstered change control. Received Virage Logic Group/Product Line Award for Outstanding Contribution for completing the SOx Section 404 audits with zero material weaknesses, significant deficiencies, or even minor deficiencies.
• To postpone datacenter expansion costs, implemented hardware virtualization using VMware ESX 3.0 VI3 systems, reducing power consumption and heat output.
• To reduce costs, renegotiated telecommunications and support contracts to save 32% ($111K) annually.

Project manager adept at managing Global IT projects.
• As company size outgrew facilities, implemented over 5 major office and datacenter relocations in California, Washington, India, Armenia, UK, and Minnesota allowing further company growth.
• During acquisitions, successfully integrated the systems, accounts, and sites of two smaller companies over short timeframes, enabling those acquisitions to quickly become productive, integrated, and add to revenues.
