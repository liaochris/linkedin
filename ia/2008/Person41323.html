Crystal Blahnik’s Summary

I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Business with a major in Advertising and a Certificate of Direct Marketing, and have five years of distinctive marketing and advertising experience.

Fulfilling my desire to MOVE WEST, I relocated to Reno, NV in the fall of 2007 from Detroit, MI. I joined a small web firm as Project Manager overseeing SEO and Social Media Marketing. Unfortunately, the company faced client loss that forced a downsize. I am now looking for a position with a stable and growing organization in Reno. I am seeking an employer that is in search of an enthusiastic and hardworking individual with unique experience, and that will continue to challenge me professionally.

Back in Detroit, I worked in account management (with an amazing team!) at J. Walter Thompson on the Brand Content & Alliances / Entertainment team for our client Ford Motor Company. Prior to JWT, I started off my career in Traffic at McCann Erickson.

I love Social Media (myspace and stumbleupon addict...), Internet Marketing, Branded Entertainment, running unique and exciting on-air and web-based Promotions and the art of Advertising in general.
