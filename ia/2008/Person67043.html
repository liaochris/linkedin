Gadi Evron’s Summary

I am looking for a challenge, to use my skills and abilities to the fullest, impact the industry and go home in the evening feeling I made a contribution, and made a difference.

As a top-level security executive I have extensive experience in incident response, fraud, phishing, DDoS and botnets. Along-side with corporate security, strategy, compliance and industrial espionage. A technical background and good experience communicating with board-level management, and cooperating with marketing to open new channels.

I am recognized as a leader in Internet security operations and am arguably the world's top expert on botnets--certainly the first, and am known for having exposed several other major risks.

Having an extremely high press visibility, I have been featured on the front page of the New York Times, twice. On Slashdot, six times, and quoted in hundreds of other articles.

I'm a campaign manager, a networker in nature, and vastly connected world-wide with government, law enforcement, industry and academia.

----------

While developing my career I established many not-for-profit efforts, some are:

1. I assisted the Estonian CERT during the "war", and wrote their post-mortem analysis and recommendations.

2. I manage a group where VP Security, CSO and CISO level professionals discuss, and kept up to date on tech issues.

3. I founded several working groups to share, cooperation and mitigate on the latest threats, in a vetted trust environment.

4. I founded and run several task forces for incident response on the Internet level. For example, with US-CERT (DHS) and SANS ISC I established the cyber response to Hurricanes Katrina and Rita scams.

5. I founded the leading online task force for combating Internet fraud and phishing, back in 2004 (law enforcement, banking, eCommerce, ISPs, industry)

Gadi Evron’s Specialties:

I lecture regularly in industry conferences such as RSA, Black Hat, CISO summit, NetSec, defcon, etc. Also, in related conferences such as the United Nation's Internet Governance Forum (IGF). I recently gave a key note presentation at the NSA supported CISSE. I lectured at MIT, GWU, JHUAPL, and at other universities.

I write a column for cnet, and wrote for peer reviewed magazines such as Virus Bulletin (VB), Dr. Dobbs as well as for the esteemed Georgetown Journal of International Affairs.
