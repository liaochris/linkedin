Simone Kapsalides’s Summary

Simone "Boss Lady" Kapsalides is a journalist and marketing and events specialist. Published in international magazines from the age of 17, she graduated from the University of Technology (UTS) Sydney, Australia with a Bachelor of Arts in Communications (Journalism) degree in 2002.

After university Simone became editor of Sydney's first urban culture street press magazine, Request, from May 2002 – May 2003. This was her editorial training ground and in late 2003, she became founder/editor of Urban Hitz magazine, Australia and New Zealand’s longest-running, highest-selling and much loved on-sale urban music and culture publication.

In addition to her publishing career, through her company Boss Lady Entertainment, Simone has promoted youth parties and hip-hop workshops (and has done so from the age of 16) in her home city of Sydney, Australia, coordinated events for Sydney's premier nightclubs and has worked on exclusive parties for numerous acts.

Simone has appeared on MTV Australia reporting urban music news, and has held positions on numerous entertainment boards (including being a founding member of the NSW government’s youth entertainment body, the Indent Advisory Committee, plus the ARIA Awards Voting Panel).

With Urban HItz magazine closing in late 2006, Simone is currently fulfilling a life-long ambition, living in New York City to take up the newly created position of Content Manager of DrJays.com, DJPremium.com and the soon to be launched StreetStyle.com. Simone's duties include running daily blogs, organizing global marketing initiatives and hosting the successful DrJays.com Live TV portal. DrJays.com is renowned as the world's leading urban fashion retailer, and huge things are in-store for 2008 for both the brand....and the Boss Lady!
