Kirk Wicks’s Summary

My personal experience designing for the internet and multimedia spans over 13 years. I have a degree in Multimedia & Presentations and the roots of my career began as a Producer/Designer of CDROM demo's, speaker support presentations, and kiosks. Since the mid-90's and the birth of the internet I have worked in an agency setting, had a prosperous freelance business, and worked as a web design lead for Palm, Inc. I am highly experienced in designing ecommerce related website graphics, subscription emails, landing pages, micro-sites, Flash product demo's, animated banners, user interface design, client facing product pages, wire frame concepting, and much more. I have direct experience with mentoring other designers and the art directing of outside vendors and contractors.
