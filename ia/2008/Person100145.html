Julie Cornell’s Summary

My publishing background is varied and extensive, including seven years of copyediting and proofreading experience and seven more in print buying and production.

My work involves frequent contact with authors to resolve queries, and I have created documentation and training to help authors use an internal Word template to comply with a complex research architecture. I also maintain the company's house style guide, making weekly decisions about tech terms and other style preferences. Previous experience includes managing inventory and reprints for print and multimedia products, including obtaining competitive pricing, selecting vendors, and overseeing products from prepress through distribution. Over the years, I've built solid working relationships with clients, vendors, and colleagues.

I balance this with freelance work in TV production, working as a stage manager, which involves assisting home or visiting production truck staff and on-air talent in broadcast booth or on the field/floor at MLB, NHL, NBA, NFL, MLS, and MLL games and various college sporting events throughout New England.

I have an eye for detail, the ability to prioritize and work within deadlines, the flexibility to take on ancillary projects, a desire to learn and be challenged, a reputation for dedication, and a devotion to quality.
