Troy Alsop’s Summary

I create tribal designs for just about anything. Here are a few examples of what I can do for you:

** Design a business card and/or business logo in a unique style of tribal that will fit not only your business idea but really grab peoples attention (attention = $$$)

** Embellish your pre-existing tattoo, logo (company logo, clothesline logo, logo for your group and/or club, etc), picture(s)...

** Design a completely new tribal tattoo or logo (I mean think about it - do you REALLY want to walk into a tattoo shop and pick out some flash on the wall for a tattoo that doesn't have any specific personal value to you - and maybe even risk getting the SAME tattoo as 20 other people that are going to walk in there??) I will design a customized, wicked as all hell tribal tattoo or logo that will have specific, personalized value to you and you only...

** Are you an artist? Do you like the draw? Imagine some wicked tribal mixed in with your drawings...

** Ever thought of having your name sketched out in a pure tribal format?

If there is something not listed that you would like to inquire about let me know and we can discuss it...

I have been a tribal designer now for a little over 6 years or so. My designs were initially created for tattoos - (I don't actually do the skin work - just the designs) - mainly for people here in Pacific Beach. But since that point - I actually extended my designs from just being for tattoos to start creating designs for business cards, business logos, furniture, and most recently - pre-existing clotheslines that requested my unique form of tribal to embellish their own logos...


I also have my own clothesline that I am in the final stages of completing called (yep, you guess it) - Variance. I'll be marketing my my clotheslines early next year - so watch out for that (and buy as many as you can afford!!!) :)

Troy Alsop’s Specialties:

I have a very unique, specialized style of tribal design that can be utilized for many types of business platforms such as business cards, logos, flyers, etc...
