Laurent Kinet’s Summary

I started my digital career in 1996 as freelance Web consultant. I soon joined Cromozone, then DAD-LBi, before the 2000 bubble crash, and never stopped since that time to work in the fields of digital strategy, business and management consulting for key accounts. I've built during these years a broad experience in Strategy & Planning, helping clients to define the digital strategy that best fit their objectives. My specialties include: online communication consulting, media planning, benchmarks, audits, usability, creative briefings, multichannel, mobile and interactive television (idtv), etc. I also developped some skills in the early stages of digital projects (initial audits, project charters, RFP, market selection, steering committees, etc).

Since 2006 I manage the Strategy & Planning department within LBi. I also own and manage Sinople, a management consulting company, and I'm board member of Fair Wood Ventures.
