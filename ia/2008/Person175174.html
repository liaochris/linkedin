Mark Davidson’s Summary

Internet Marketing Strategist, Social Media Manager, Brand Builder, Promoter, and Content Provider.

I help people to create, manage, and promote their blogs online using social-networking sites, social bookmarking, and SEO techniques.

I’ve been in sales and marketing professionally since 1986. Between 1986 and 1992, I was a concert and event producer and promoter. My own events had attendances of up to 3,000 seats. I later worked as a production consultant for events with attendances of up to 15,000 seats. Promotion has included traditional media, local radio and television support, street-level promotion, and viral marketing.

Later in my career, I was an original employee of a successful financial dot com that was eventually sold to GMAC for $300m.

I've been marketing online since 1998, mostly in the real estate finance sector. This is a space that I know particularly well. I believe that social networking is the future of that industry.

In June 1997, I started Shift+One Media to explore opportunities in new media and apply what I've learned over the years to these new emerging social technologies.

Recently, Shift+One Media has began consulting and providing services to clients. We are still a new company but our client roster is growing.

Shift+One Media has recently also started a blog network that has been steadily in development since March, 2008. We are looking forward to our official release later this year.

Our goal is to continue to grow, create more value for our clients, and expand our blog network.

Mark Davidson’s Specialties:

What I do exceptionally well is to create online-cult followings. This is a skill I have developed over many years and I’ve become very good at it. To do this, I create a sense of community using web boards, social networking sites, and social bookmarking sites. I also deploy a number of viral marketing strategies.
