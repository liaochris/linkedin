Paul van Oppen’s Summary

I have a wide range of experiences in the display, electronic components and medical devices industry, specifically in product development, project management and quality. I am able to work on own initiative and as part of a team and have proven leadership skills involving managing, developing and motivating teams to achieve their objectives. First-class analytical and problem solving skills. Dedicated to maintaining high quality standards. I have a working experience of 16 years of which 10 years with Philips and the last 6 in China. Prefers to work in the Asia Pacific area. Mobility is global.

I specifically have working knowledge of: general technical management, six sigma, design for six sigma, process control, resource management, product development, quality management, customer quality, quality in design, regulatory, managing electrical and mechanical design teams and managing crisis teams during quality problems.

Paul van Oppen’s Specialties:

Quality management, 6 sigma, process control, product Development, bridging the gap between home and China organizations
