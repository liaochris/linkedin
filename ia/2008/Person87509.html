    ABS-CBN Global "MYX"

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    November 2005 — Present (3 years)

    MYX is the first music/lifestyle channel made for Asian Americans.

    Designed for Asian Americans and positioned for ethnic crossover, MYX is targeted to be the first nationally distributed cable and satellite channel made for this fast-growing mainstream market. Like Black Entertainment Television (BET) in its market, MYX will embrace the entire Asian American community through music, culture and entertainment, with particular appeal to the critical 15-24 demographic.

    Developed by ABS-CBN, one of Asia's biggest media companies, MYX is set to be the company's seventh Asian ethnic cable and satellite channel in US distribution. Already number one in the Philippines -- edging out MTV -- MYX enters the US as part of a new global expansion strategy.

    MYX will create a new marketplace for music and entertainment, fashion and style, targeted to the influential Asian American youth and young adult community.
