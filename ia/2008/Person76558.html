Great Big Plants - The First Energy Drink For Plants

Herbal Smoke Shop

Great Big Plants is now available to the public. For years, the secret to greener golf courses and better professional landscaping was the special ingredients in Great Big Plants. Now everyone can buy the world's first energy drink for plants.

Great Big Plants' unique formulation has been used for 15 years by farmers, horticulturists and landscapers, and it has now finally been made available for consumer use.

Great Big Plants is a liquid, organic compost that energizes and jump starts plants. It supplies the minerals and nutrients vital for superior plant growth that are missing from fertilizers. Derived from all natural sources and organically certified, Great Big Plants gives an energy boost to all vegetables, fruits, shrubs, trees, lawns and potted plants.

The product has been used by Dole Foods, Del Monte, Blue Diamond, Sunkist, Driscoll, Anheuser-Busch and many others. NFL and college football stadiums, and more than 100 golf courses use Great Big Plants to ensure the most robust plant health possible.

Great Big Plants is safe and easy to use. It improves the soil and energizes plants immediately.

Great Big Plants is highly recommended for new plants/transplants, "tired" or stunted plants and trees, root-bound potted plants and any plant that needs an energy boost.

About BioScientific

Great Big Plants is a product of BioScientific, Inc. Since 1992, BioScientific has been involved in the development, manufacture and sale of microbiological based products for the agricultural, horticultural, environmental services and consumer markets.
