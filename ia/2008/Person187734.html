Heidi Heyns’s Summary

I am One Lucky Woman...I have traveled all over the World, sailed across the Atlantic on a beautiful 66ft yacht, lived in the Azores, raced in the King's Cup in Thailand and the Antigua Race Week in the Caribbean. I've taught horseback riding and now...now I'm speaking into a microphone and hearing my voice all over the World and Teaching people to Laugh Freely in their lives for the first time since childhood!

All this! AND...ya know what... I've uncovered my destiny and it is to aid those I love and each of those whom I find along this path, in discovering their own hidden, deep well of great possibilities, joyousness and laughter.

Laughter in the Workplace!! Not as funny as it sounds!

Nosirree...Laughter can unite a team like nothing else and especially the kind of laughter that is not aimed at anyone or anything. This is not laughter based on Jokes or Comedy. This is laughter for No Reason..This laughter is mirthful laughter and is absolutely HEALING...it can heal teams that are struggling and teams that are at odds...Laughter can improve morale as it improves heart strength, as it strengthens abdominal muscles and discourages cancer!

I am available to speak and bring cohesion, clarity, compatibility and collective creativity to Corporate events, groups of any size, You may want me to lead Laughter Workshops or simply offer a presentations that can last anywhere from ten minutes to 90 minutes long. Groups may be of any size limited only by the available space. No special clothing or music is necessary. This may, in fact, be the happiest and most unified you will ever have seen your audience, group or workforce.
