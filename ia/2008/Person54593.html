Kyle Arnold’s Summary

I am a clinical psychologist and author. Clinically, my focus is on evaluation and psychotherapy of underserved clients diagnosed with addictive disorders and mental illnesses. I use an integrative orientation combining elements of contemporary dynamic therapy and rational-emotive behavior therapy. My writing includes work on the history of psychotherapy, psychodynamic theory, and psychobiography.

Addiction, Psychotherapy, Psychobiography
