Martin Blundell’s Summary

Category Manager for Life Science at ki work. ki work (http://ki-work.com) is the online marketplace for those looking to outsource or find online work, designed for greater transparency and development of trust for those looking to fill projects. There are no fees for registering, listing projects, or winning bids. The network is designed to help the right person find the right worker at the right price.

I lead and moderate the 25,000 member Biotech and Pharma Professionals Network, the Medical Device Professionals Network, the Medical Diagnostics Professionals Network, the Clinical Trials Professionals Network and the Life Science Mentors Network. Each of these groups is supported by an active Web Site and Forum.

Our goal is to provide a world wide social and business network within our limited niche: Life Sciences, Medical Diagnostics, Medical Devices, Biotech, Pharma, Clinical Trials, health care research and laboratory work. Within this niche we provide opportunities to communicate more widely between group members and we welcome recruiters and members offering business ventures.

Before this, I successfully led projects in biotech and pharmaceuticals for 19 years, meeting challenges in financial results, quality and schedule. I do this with an unusual combination of business and technical experience, MBA training and my research background in Molecular Biology.

I worked with Shell International Petroleum Company in marketing, training, research and strategic planning in London, Brunei, Puerto Rico and the Netherlands. Before that I held a postdoctoral fellowship in Molecular Biology at Washington University Medical School (St. Louis) and was a Fellow of Emmanuel College, Cambridge.

I was Board Chair for 8 years of Caribbean Preparatory School, an Independent Accredited K-12 School in San Juan, Puerto Rico, while the school grew from 524 students to 810.

Martin Blundell’s Specialties:

Influencing, work in matrix organization, very strong computer skills, oral and written communication, leadership / coaching / mentoring, molecular biology, strategic planning, biotech process.
