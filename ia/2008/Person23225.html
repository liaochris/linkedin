Bill Day’s Summary

Plaintiff-side employment and civil rights lawyer, primarily representing federal employees, with experience before EEOC, MSPB, U.S. District Courts, and U.S. Courts of Appeals. Licensed in the District of Columbia, Maryland, and New York, in the U.S. District Courts for the District of Columbia, Maryland, and the Eastern District of Michigan, the Courts of Appeals for the D.C. and 4th Circuits, the Court of Federal Claims, and the U.S. Supreme Court. Member of the American Bar Association, National Employment Lawyers Association (NELA), and the Metropolitan Washington Employment Lawyers Association (MWELA).

Bill Day’s Specialties:

Employment law, civil rights, civil litigation
