Jo Jordan’s Summary

I am a psychologist with background in both management/HR consulting and university teaching.

My experience as a psychologist is unusual. Most of my experience has been in southern Africa where I have had a broad range of opportunities to work in strategic HR with many household names based there. I have also been involved in restructuring companies with a national footprint during times of massive political and economic change. I have worked in most sectors of the economy, with big and small companies, in highly organized companies and in informal not-for-profits.

My experience includes setting up selection and affirmative action systems at all levels up to Country Director, designing and maintaining HR systems including compensation & benefits and performance management, facilitating team building and strategic planning, organizational design & restructuring and OD.

My university teaching has included professional psychology, MBA and entry level management. Both my consulting and university experience have been in culturally diverse institutions.

My current interest is in using positive psychology to cope with rapid change and the new possibilities of living and working introduced by 2.0. I am currently working with artists in creative professions, new media, web2.0, migrants & diasporans, and entrepreneurial genY. I am interested in collaborating in any and all of these areas.

Jo Jordan’s Specialties:

Positive psychology, coaching individuals and groups coping with change, 2.0, entrepreneurial possibilities in the psychology/2.0 nexus.

Conventional HR/psychology applied in culturally diverse and rapidly changing environments: strategic HR analysis, organization design, HR policy & processes, consultation and change management, OD, recruitment and selection, organizational change.

Teaching, coaching, consulting, project management and team building.
