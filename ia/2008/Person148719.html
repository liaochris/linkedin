Patrick Carney’s Summary

Patrick Carney is a professional artist, networker, keynote speaker and referral training consultant. He owns the Referral Institute - San Diego. In each of these roles, his focus is showing business professionals how to harness the power of referral marketing to drive healthy business growth.

Patrick believes his success lies in his ability to merge the strategic with the tactical, to understand the market and the competition, and to build a strong management team. No one function can be accountable for performance. It is the interconnection of everything and the combined strength of the management team that leads to results.

Success and improved corporate value is what he delivered throughout his eighteen year career as a member, a Regional Director, Eastern US Director and the President/COO of LeTip International, Inc. a professional leads exchange corporation based in San Diego, CA.

Most notable has been his success across diverse functional lines – from the strategic planning and product development to “hands on” management of operations, marketing, sales and customer service. He leads by example, expects consistently strong performance from his teams and in turn, has delivered strong and sustainable results.

Now, ready for a new challenge and new opportunity Mr. Carney has taken on the challenge of developing the Referral Institute – San Diego. The Mission at the Referral Institute is: “To direct professionals to proactively increase their business through implementing structured referral strategies.” The Vision is: “To be the world’s foremost provider of training programs, coaching services and corporate consulting on referral marketing.”
