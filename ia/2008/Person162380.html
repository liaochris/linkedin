Roberto Martin’s Summary

Manager with over 12 years of experience in development, management and execution of ICT and Sales & Marketing programs.

My experiences concern Management, ICT consulting strategy, Marketing analysis, Customer relationship and extended Supply Chain management with emphasis in Program Management, B2B and B2C enviroment process analysis, change management and organization.

I have held managerial positions with direct responsabilities on international projects and promotion of innovative solutions.

In last three years, I enforced my professional competences with a M.B.A. in order to cover also knowledge of sales and marketing organization, direct marketing, consumer analysis, channels distribution and so on.

My final MBA thesis concerned "Customer information Management and sales analysis solutions for an Ethical Products Pharmaceutical Industry".

I have long and deep experience in CRM systems, analytical processes and systems for CRM data, data protection law in Spain, e-commerce, and all "e" & internet related ideas for a commercial division. My position is between IT and Commercial divisions, "I speak both languages - tech and Mktg".

Experience in the complete process of implementing Sales Excellence strategies in a pharma salesforce.
