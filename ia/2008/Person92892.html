Joan Azarva’s Summary

Working on the college level for 15 years as a Learning Specialist and Adjunct Instructor, I see many students enter college sorely ill-prepared for its unique challenges, lacking the academic and navigational skills necessary to succeed. As a result, many become discouraged and begin to think they are not "college material".

Knowing from personal experience that many of these students CAN succeed, I decided to take a proactive approach. I wrote a one-of-a-kind course --CONQUER COLLEGE WITH LD -- (for high school students and their parents) that addresses the entire post-secondary transition process --- from what to do in HS to becoming a successful, independent learner in college. Knowing this information can make the difference between success and failure has made this course the highlight of my career,

I will be teaching CONQUER COLLEGE WITH LD in the fall and spring at Gwynedd Mercy College. For each student who enrolls, a parent may attend free of charge.
