Bob Derby’s Summary

I have been in some form of technical field my entire career. I started as an electronic technician in the Marine Corps. I took that technical experience and applied it to the IT field. My "claim to fame" (so to speak) is that I have been able to jump in as needed and learn a new product or service, quickly and successfully, to support the needs of the Company. I have primarily worked with Microsoft server products (OS, hardware and applications) but I have not been limited to that. I enjoy working with multiple environments and figuring out how to make them work together to meet the needs of the project.

I had previously completed the NT 4.0 MCSE Core requirements but the Cert was retired before I completed the track. I am currently working at it again for the 2003 MCSE + Security.
