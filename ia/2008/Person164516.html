Ron Pyles’s Summary

I left Continuous Computing Corporation (CCPU) in mid-2007 where I was President, having previously served as Executive Vice President for World Wide Sales and Marketing for several years. Earlier I held leadership positions at Intel, Trillium Digital Systems, Allied Riser Communications and AT&T. I have only briefly touched on my background in this profile. Please contact me directly for further details. After taking a year off to focus on family and personal projects I am now seeking my next executive level assignment while concluding a couple industry-related consulting assignments. Specifically I am seeking to be a part of a collegial group of high profile senior leaders who are collaborating to drive a business to huge wins in the market. While my most recent experience includes broad corporate responsibilities my greatest expertise is in building and nurturing sales, marketing and product management organizations. I am more focused on joining the right executive team then I am on scope of responsibility. Thus, while I am confident in my ability to serve at a CEO or COO level, I am very willing to accept an officer level position with responsibilities within my more narrow areas of expertise. Most likely I would best fit into an early or mid stage situation where the founders and/or current management team need a new wave of talent, experience and energy to take the business to the next level. My skills are transferable to other industries and I am exploring multiple options.
