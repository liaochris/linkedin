Derek Galvin’s Summary

Over 15 Years of IT Management and Leadership Experience within High-Profile Organizations * Successful Track Record of Implementing Large-Scale Enterprise, Network, and Systems Solutions to Meet Business Directives * Valued Group Leader and Member of Senior Executive Teams

Highly accomplished Chartered Information Technology Professional (CITP) with over 15 years experience and a proven record of accomplishment developing advanced technology solutions in congruence with business goals of global organizations. Focused and decisive leader with exceptional ability to analyse complex business processes and work with C-level executives to develop actionable plans aligned with client goals. A careful listener who employs Six Sigma, ITIL ® Framework and other formal methodologies to link customers to business processes and drive decisions and process improvements through the use of facts and data.

Visionary manager with strong collaboration, communication, analytical and leadership profile. Excellent client and vendor relationship management skills. Proven ability to develop new and follow-on opportunities and manage successful and profitable engagements.

Achieving success at global levels requires an individual who can truly listen to what the customer needs and communicate the expertise that develops a trusting relationship, proven by delivering complex projects on time and on budget. I have demonstrated this time and again in my career. Additionally, I have traveled internationally extensively, sometimes on short notice, when the need has arisen. I have a strong understanding of various cultures and work ethics, which has helped me in managing international teams of technology and business professionals in Europe, America, the Middle East, and Asia Pacific.
