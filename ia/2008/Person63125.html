Frank Diana’s Summary

An exceptional organizational leader with 25 years experience in technology and general management. Significant technology and business experience through various C-Level and consulting roles. Considerable experience in acquiring funding and managing M&A activity.

A strong blend of technology and Business acumen drives the effective alignment of technology and business objectives. Significant experience managing large organizations and multi-million dollar budgets. Successfully managed large reengineering initiatives separated systems and processes to support IPO's, and established centers of excellence and shared services. Particular areas of technology focus have been architecture, enterprise applications, and business intelligence, with deep expertise in large volume decision support applications.

Strong visioning skills enable the identification of industry trends quickly and broadly, with an ability to see the implications of trends years into the future. These skills have led to the effective identification of new business ventures, while strong organizational leadership skills enable the structuring of those ventures.

A win-win approach and strong relationship management skills extracts the most out of partnerships and alliances, while driving business development success.

Excellent communication skills were developed through the many years of communicating with senior executives at AT&T and Fujitsu Consulting, and fine tuned through the various magazine articles written and presentations delivered to Industry Analysts and prospects.

Please dialog with me by visiting my Blog at http://www.enherent.com/blogs/frank-diana.

In the last five years, I have worked on a special project composing and producing a music CD. This CD entitled "The View" is a reflection on the importance of keeping the right perspective in life. I welcome your feedback which you can provide by visiting my website at http://theviewdiana.wetpaint.com/.
