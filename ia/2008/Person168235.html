Samuel Viani Bastos’s Summary

I'm from South of the state of Minas Gerais, Brazil.
I work in interactive and graphic design.
I started studying and working with interactive design in 2004.
Since then I developed a strong taste for art, design and communication.

I like Kandinsky and the paintings of landscapes of Van Gogh. Music always inspires me.
I appreciate the kind of communication based on motion graphics (skill that I want to learn).

My focus so far has been art direction.
I use the art as a tool and design as a means to communicate.
The digital marketing continues to grow and good projects require increasingly of the interactive creativity aligned with a great marketing planning to achieving the goal of communication and the expected results.
I always looking for a future based on my knowledge.
