Brian Heaton’s Summary

Mr. Heaton has over 18 years of experience in all facets of networking. His terrestrial transport experience extends from legacy teletype systems through modern dense wave division multiplexed (DWDM) OC-192 SONET systems. His satellite communications experience covers the range from single-channel UHF and X-Band narrowband satellite terminals up to and including multi-channel wideband satellite terminals. Beginning in 1990 with an X.25 network, Mr. Heaton has worked extensively with all commonly used networking technologies. Since 1993 he has served in IP network engineering roles for private companies, government organizations, and service providers both in the US and abroad. Additionally, Mr. Heaton has extensive experience in voice telecommunications network optimization, least cost routing, and advanced network troubleshooting. In recent years Mr. Heaton has added extensive mobile ad-hoc networking (MANET), VoIP, network modeling, and network simulation to his already impressive technical skill set.

As a business leader Mr. Heaton has several years experience managing capital budgets in excess of $20 million. In the highly competitive telecommunications space, he has taken three markets profitable a minimum of 9 months before their predicted break-even. He is as comfortable and effective leading a department or presenting a cost-benefit analysis as he is diagnosing and correcting a complex network problem.

Security Clearance: TS/SSBI
