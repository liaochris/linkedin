Andrew McCutchen’s Summary

I am a Mechanical Engineer with experience in manufacturing product support and new product development with a medical device manufacturer. I have a thorough understanding of all the tasks and objectives which must be met, to not only develop an innovative product that exceeds the client's expectations, but to implement the product and get it to market efficiently.

My partner and I have assembled a very talented team of Mechanical Engineers, Electrical Engineers, and CAD Drafters that have experience across many industries such as medical device, aerospace, industrial design, and consumer products.

Where most new product development companies will design a new product, create some drawings and prototypes and then hand it off to their client, Baymar keeps going. We understand that their internal resources' time is extremely valuable and that they have existing priorities which is what drove them to get outside help. Baymar has the experience to do everything from developing requirements documents to creating ECO documents and preparing the clients assembly line.

Baymar will be on top of every minute detail to make sure the product is both innovative and reliable and that it gets to market in the most efficient manner and with minimal impact to their internal resources. We can also improve existing products and manufacturing processes using lean principles to reduce costs and increase reliability. There isn’t much that we can’t handle but you can trust that we will be honest and upfront if it’s something outside of our specialties.
