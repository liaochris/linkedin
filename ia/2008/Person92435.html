João Marcelo Arcoverde’s Summary

Information technology and services, with the aim of providing solutions addressing business challenges that depend upon modern information access methods on large databases or resorces, such as the Web.

There are many industries which can benefit from our technologies, such as competitive and strategic intelligence, BI, marketing, compliance, risk, credit, fraud, CRM, insurance, data quality, et cetera.

We have been continuously investing on R&D to exploit the potential of free text collections, the data extraction and integration process, specially in Portuguese language. We have been helping organizations manage the information overload toward within its supply-chain and internal business processes, providing valuable information access services, from collecting, organizing, analyzing and integrating [un]structured data.
