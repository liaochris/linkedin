Tim Saler’s Summary

Tim Saler founded Saler Republican Strategies LLC in 2007 and serves as the firm's President.

At age 20, Tim served as a Consultant for Election Day Operations to the Republican State Committee of Pennsylvania, assisting the re-election campaign of U.S. Senator Rick Santorum as well as many other candidates in the Keystone State.

One year later, at age 21, Tim was named Director of Communications for the Pennsylvania Supreme Court campaign of Judge Paul P. Panepinto. Tim is one of the youngest political professionals to ever hold these positions on a statewide campaign.

He will complete a Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science, magna cum laude, from Temple University in May 2008. He had worked full-time for the Pennsylvania GOP and the Panepinto campaign while pursuing fifteen credits or more each semester.

Tim was a recipient of the University Honors Scholarship, and he was named to the Dean's List and University Honors Director's List multiple times, all while working full-time for Republican candidates and campaigns.

Rewarded for his studies, Tim was a 2007 inductee to Pi Sigma Alpha, the national honors fraternity for political science students.

A native of southern New Jersey, Tim is a former attendee of the Congressional Student Leadership Conference and previously served as a Delegate to the United Nations Student Conference on Human Rights.
