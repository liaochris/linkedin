Randy Cheske’s Summary

I've worked on just about every operating system, phone systems, network systems, certified on Nortel, Avaya, Cisco, Genesys, Rockwell/Aspect, Novell, Microsoft, and many more. I've done software development and complex data network and telecommunications design work. I was a technical instructor for many different systems for our customers. Work for Bell Atlantic (now Verizon), M&I Data Services (now Metavante), Ameritech, SBC, (now AT&T), had my own business, briefly worked at Nortel, spent some great quality time working at Spanlink Communications and now back with AT&T in a new position. However, with all this technical background I also have a strong business background from running and owning my own businesses, started up new business groups within several companies, and was very integral in new business ventures at each company I've worked for.
