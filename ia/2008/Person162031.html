Robert Douglas’s Summary

Robert Douglas is a seasoned IT professional, having started two successful consulting companies and conducted long-term engagements for major IT players including Microsoft, SkyTel, NetCom Online, CompuServe, Digital Equipment Corp., and AOL, among others.

Robert Douglas’s Specialties:

Project Management, IT Turnaround, Negotiation, Hardware, Software, Networks, Operations, Merger and Aquisition, SharePoint; MOSS 2007, Microsoft Exchange, Sarbanes-Oxley Compliance, Microsoft System Center, Office Communications Server, Network Architecture

Robert Douglas’s Experience

  • President

    PlanetMagpie

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1998 — Present (10 years 8 months)

    In 1998, Mr. Douglas founded PlanetMagpie in Los Gatos, California. PlanetMagpie was conceived as a full-service IT firm providing IT consulting, network support and W3 services to start-ups, mid-market, and enterprise-level companies. Interware was folded into PlanetMagpie and became its consulting division. In 2006, PlanetMagpie was named the 7th fastest growing private company in Silicon Valley. PlanetMagpie achieved Microsoft Gold Partner status in 2005, a recognition that less than 1% of Microsoft partners achieve.

  • Vice President MIS

    Tessera, Inc.

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; TSRA; Semiconductors industry)

    November 2005 — April 2007 (1 year 6 months)

    In 2004 and 2005, Robert led a team of consultants from PlanetMagpie as they rebuilt the IT infrastructure at Tessera, Inc. In 2005, Robert was asked to join Tessera as its Vice President of MIS. With a market cap in excess of $1.5B, Tessera was in the midst of growing by acquisition. During Robert’s tenure, Tessera acquired 4 companies – located in North Carolina, Israel, and Japan – and merged them with the corporate network. Robert managed a global IT budget of $5M a year and was responsible for redesigning the Tessera network to allow for fast-paced expansion.

    In the process of implementing Tessera’s Sarbanes-Oxley strategies for IT 404, he streamlined corporate communications and engineering departments across the global network. Robert managed 8 in-house reports, as well as multiple consultants and vendors. In 2007, having helped Tessera through its heavy acquisition phase, Robert returned to lead PlanetMagpie full time.

  • President

    InterWare, Inc.

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1994 — January 1998 (4 years 1 month)

    In 1995, Mr. Douglas started his first consulting company, Interware, Inc., in Jackson, Mississippi. Interware specialized in back-end billing systems with customer self-service front ends based on IBM AS400 platforms. Customers included Microsoft, MCI, Skytel, Netcom, AOL, CompuServe, WebTV, and Net Channel, among others.

    In 1997, at the request of Netcom, Interware moved its operations to Silicon Valley.

  • Director Advanced Programming Group

    SkyTel

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    1993 — 1995 (2 years)

    Responsible for all Strategic programming projects for SkyTel. The advanced programming group was responsible for optimization of programming required to process millions of call detail records per night, rollout of new products and interfaces to the paging network. This included the revolutionary SkyTel two way paging.
