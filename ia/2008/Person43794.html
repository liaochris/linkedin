Daniel Melendez’s Summary

Mr. Melendez is a partner of Strata Ltda and Global Security Commerce ltda (Global Seccom). Stratra is a consultant company for Offsets Management. Global Seccom is a provider of supply chain risk mitigation consulting services. He also served as a military for 29 years, in the Colombian Air Force.

Mr. Melendez’s expertise is as a project manager on Balanced Scorecard, Risk Management, ISO 9001 and Quality National Award applied to supply chain and logistics design, focusing on how supply chains respond to disruptions without affecting logisitics velocity.

The main projets during his military and professional consultant career includes:
1. Offsets Project Management in Colombia for Embraer. 2007.
2. Colombia Public Procurement Optimization Process and Strategy Map, based on actual and future law requirements. 2006
3. Quality and Flight Safety System Design for an International Transport Airline, 2006
4. Quality System for an International Cargo Airline, 2005
5. Strategy Design and Execution for the Colombian Air Force Depot. 2002-2004
ISO 9001 & ISO 17025 Quality certification for the Colombian air force Depot, 2003 2004
6. Design of a flight operational risk assessment mode, 2000

Mr. Melendez’s research is on organization, and supply chain design, recently focusing on a design framework to reduce physical security risks in critical infrastructure, and a Forecasting approach to improve logistics planning in the military environment.

His work includes published articles, such as “How to implement a balanced scorecard in a military organization” in Air Force Science and Technology Review, June 2004, “Transform your office in a flight cockpit” Air Force Review, October 2003, “Courage without strategy does not produce results” March 2001, and “Re-engineering or Quality Management in logistics management” September 1995 in Military Review.
