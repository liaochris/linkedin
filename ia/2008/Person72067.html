Harry Frank’s Summary

Harry is a freelance motion graphics designer and instructor based in Detroit, Michigan.

As a classical percussionist, he earned a degree in music and audio engineering from the Fredonia School of Music in New York in 1995. Since then, his professional career has spanned the titles of audio engineer, music composer, video editor, graphic designer, finally settling on "motion designer".

Focusing mostly on After Effects, Maya and Cinema 4D as his tools, Harry is available for freelance work off-site or on location anywhere in the world. Past clients have included Digital Kitchen, Royal Pictures, MGM, NBC, GM, and Chrysler.

As an instructor and author, Harry has authored two training titles on After Effects Expressions, a scripting language within Adobe After Effects, and one title on Trapcode Form featured on trapcode.com. He has authored articles for Digital Arts magazine, and has written training for Red Giant Software. He is currently working on several more titles, yet to be disclosed, that are very large in scope. His expertise in software has earned him the position of being a product demonstrator for Zaxwerks, Trapcode and Red Giant Software.

See examples of his work, free tutorials, and various ramblings at
graymachine.com.
