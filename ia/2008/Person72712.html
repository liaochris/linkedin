Gregory Stoltz’s Summary

Technology and business veteran with the scars of dot bomb survival. I've performed darn near every technology and business role -- from mainframe computer operator to company President. I've started companies, built companies and survived too many failures. Although the failures I've experienced have taught me a great deal, success has taught me much, much more. I know what doesn't work and more importantly what DOES work.

Prior to becoming the President of MyCoupons LLC, I served as the CTO of Pittsburgh based Direct Response Technologies (DRT), Inc which I joined in 2001. During my 5 plus years with DRT, the company created, launched, and grew the industry leading affiliate marketing platform DirectTrack™ as well as the pay-per-click key word management tool Keywordmax™. If you've been to Ad:Tech or the Affiliate Summit, our clients are all there. We created the platform that powered the revolution in affiliate marketing.

My previous work experience includes time as CTO with Vention, Inc., the client program manager at Cerner Corporation, as well as co-founding eReserve.net, an ecommerce company. My initial experience was serving in progressively more challenging roles with NCR Corp. NCR will always hold a special place in my heart. I joined the firm fresh out of the army as a computer operator and NCR graciously financed my entire college education from nothing through my MA in management. I left NCR in 1995 just a few weeks prior to AT&T setting a great company free again to achieve greatness.

I've been involved in utilizing Internet (IP) based technologies to support business objectives since the very early 1990s. Making use of tools like NNTP, UUCP, Gopher, and of course HTTP to support intra AND inter company collaboration.

I hold both a Bachelor and Master's degree in Management from Antioch University in Yellow Springs, Ohio.
