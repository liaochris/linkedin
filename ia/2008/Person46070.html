David Bender’s Summary

David Bender is an award-winning creative director committed to designing exceptional customer experiences and developing creative strategies for brand distinction. With over 25 years of industry excellence in marketing communications, his wide-ranging expertise includes cross-media development in digital technologies, interaction design, relationship marketing and off-line communications.

David has collaborated with the worlds best companies across verticals, industries and categories. He brings fresh ideas and innovative thinking to everything he does, consistently solving tough and persistent marketing problems. He's successfully produced and launched high profile programs for Fortune 500 companies including; Microsoft, AT&T, Lexus, Disney, MasterCard, Procter & Gamble, Morgan Stanley and NewLine Cinema.

Davids Vision. "I believe that communication defines the human experience. We need to create experiences where people, tools and information interconnect."

David Bender’s Specialties:

Creative Leadership, Creative Direction, Creative Strategy, Communications Design, Conceptual Exploration, Art Direction, Design Infusion, Design Integration, Campaign Development, Content Strategy, Editorial Development, Copywriting, User Experience Design, Information Architecture, Interface Design, Brand Extension, Brand Immersion, Market Research, Strategic Branding and Digital Marketing.
