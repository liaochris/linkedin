Scott Blackledge’s Summary

Early in my career, I coded assembler-level applications running on MVS. Later, I kept up with IBM architecture changes and actually coded three implementations of the S/390 and Z architectures (Amdahl Macrocode firmware, Amdahl Merlin simulator, and Platform Solutions PCMWare). I also like coding debugging tools in C and Python.

I enjoy coding close to the metal, working on firmware or hardware simulators. I'd like to continue working in these areas. I'd also enjoy working with non-standard operating systems.
