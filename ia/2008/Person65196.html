Francis Ruggiero’s Summary

Admitted to practice in 1981; commencing his legal career in the areas of commercial litigation, and alternative dispute resolution. From 1987 until 2004, he conducted the law firm Behan & Speed, and was involved in the establishment and implementation of Quality Business Management for the legal profession in Australia with extensive involvement on the national, state and local executive committees.
In 2004, Francis Ruggiero embarked on the establishment of the Behan Legal group which would focus on the Business, Corporate and International areas of law.
His practices in corporations law focusing on structuring, business and personal asset protection, taxation and dealing with the managing directors, CEO's and shareholders of each corporate client.
In line with this background, the establishment of the Behan Legal group sees the expansion into Sydney focusing on broader range of non-legal services whilst focusing on business, corporate and international legal services.
In 2006, he completed his MBA on International Management to complement his global approach to working with his clientele.
Strategic consulting, including business plan & strategy development, which includes advising the company's management team on formation of additional corporations and business structures, drafting policies & procedures, and structuring commercial transactions.
