Antonio Arch’s Summary

Antonio Arch was born in Jamaica, grew up in the Cayman Islands and came to Canada in 1989. It didn’t take him long to fall in love with all things Canadian, especially this country’s infinite variety of visual art. Antonio bought his first work of art in 1995, a piece by Montreal painter Ghitta Caiserman-Roth and his life has not been quite the same since.
To quote a previous bio, it’s still safe to say that “Antonio Arch is crazy about Canadian Art”. After studying Classics (Bishop’s University) and Public Relations (McGill University) he sustained a variety of jobs with the primary goal of being able to buy a work of art every year, and secondly to cover his living expenses. Various PR-related jobs led him to Shelley Lambe Fine Art, where he began by doing PR for Toronto art consultant Shelley Lambe. It wasn’t long before he was promoting artists as well.

Today Arch & Company Fine Arts finds and markets Canadian Artists and their work to buyers, collectors and art investors in North America, the Caribbean, the Middle East and Japan while simultaneously keeping an eye out for great International art to bring home to Canada. Antonio currently represents a small roster of Canadian talent of various disciplines, as well as two international artists, The Israeli Artist Mira Maylor and Mexican photographer Klaus Enrique Gerdes. Antonio’s website (www.archart.ca) had three thousand visits in the first three weeks following it going live. 2007 has so surpassed all projections that Arch & Company has resentfully had to engage outside Marketing-PR and Antonio Arch is still paying for this bio!

    Arch & Company houses and curates a small gallery located in the heart of Toronto’s Yorkville Village, specialising in Canadian and International paintings, European prints, objets d’art and fine art photography from around the globe.
