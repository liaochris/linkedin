Henry Chang’s Summary

Henry Chang is currently the .Net developer for Business Support Tools team at EDS Central Hub. He was the technical lead for Compensation Data Warehouse (CDW) of EDS CIO HR Global Compensation & Benefits Division and was the application architect of the Service Request Tool (SRT) of EDS US Transportation Division where he provides advice and about system infrastructures such as networks setups and server software setups, helping companies decrease their service request turnaround time in order to lower their operational cost, designs solutions that shorten the development time and optimizing the code reusability. Henry has 11 years (7 years in the technical lead role) hands-on and consulting experience in Web development, Web enabling, solution design, and business reengineering. He brings demonstrated expertise in applying the latest Web technologies, data modeling, and coding techniques to optimize the application performance.
