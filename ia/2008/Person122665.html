Mark Spector’s Summary

In 25 years on Madison Avenue, I wore every hat from resident geek to award-winning creative director. And now I’m freelancing for clients that need:

• RESULTS: If you have programs to optimize or controls to beat, you want to work with someone like me. An ability to grasp and apply best practices helps me deliver 20%+ lifts online and off. And knowing when to dump those best practices often helps me deliver more.”

• REPAIRS FOR BROKEN AD PROGRAMS: Sometimes the smartest people get behind misguided efforts and I’ve been called in many times to turn things around. Frankly, if I got royalties for every time I succeeded, I would be living on a beach.

• STRATEGIC INSIGHTS: The bad news, I ask questions like a five year old. The good news, they need to be asked. All the rolled eyes and heavy sighs, though, are forgotten when I deliver $120-million in leads or identify a new market or suggest a better marketing plan.

• COMPLEX PRODUCTS MADE SIMPLE: Mainframe configuration. DNA stabilization and storage. I don’t know why, but I just get a kick out of this stuff. My portfolio includes award-winning commercials for bandwidth optimization and a humorous approach to IT lifecycle management.

• MESSAGING THAT’S HONEST AND ON BRAND: In my experience, a sincere and credible appeal will produce more high-quality leads and sales than any hyped-up bullBLEEP or cool for cool’s sake creative. If you value your name and reputation, but still need results —

Drop me a line at mark@markspectorwrites.com. You’ll see that I’m very easy to work with. And I deliver successful copy for about 20% the price Madison Avenue agencies charged for the same work.

Mark Spector’s Specialties:

Over the years, I've created successful television commercials, radio commercials, print ads, collateral, d-mail, e-mail, Web sites, microsites, landing pages, banners, and portals. I've also dabbled in social networking, search engine optimization, and public relations.
