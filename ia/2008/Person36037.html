Chris Thilk’s Summary

I've been working in the public relations field for eight years now, first at Bacon's Information and now at MWW Group. For the last several years I've been getting more and more involved in the new media world, first just as a blogger and now, at MWW Group, as someone who actually helps clients get involved with this world.

Going forward I'd like to do more of the same, including getting into freelance writing and consulting more.
