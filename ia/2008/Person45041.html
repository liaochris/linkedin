Darren Himebrook’s Summary

Career Summary

Darren Himebrook has had the pleasure of executing award winning integrated production campaigns (both interactive and broadcast) on time and on budget while developing strong relationships and contacts with some of the leading interactive development partners and productions houses. His skill set reaches far beyond that of a traditional producer. During his time in the production world, he has created a fee structure for clients, led interactive pitches and worked on strategy. He's had experience leading large teams, both internally and freelance. In addition, Darren has assisted in new business development and concept development for branded entertainment projects.


Some of his proudest work was developed at W+K and CP+B on projects such as:

Experienceoldspice.com (Old Spice) • Voiceofexperience.com (Old Spice) and associated online media (banners) • Keep It Clean Contest (Old Spice) • ”Sayna” :30 Spot with Olympic Gold Medalist Sayna Richardson (NIKE) • NIKE Zoom Digital Video Content/iTV (NIKE) • Black History Month online media units (Coke) • “Hellgate: London” Digital Content Webfilms and print (EA).

VWfeatueres.com “Safe Happens” & “Rabbit” (VW) • Nicecurves.com (VW) • Menofthesquaretable.com and associated online media (Miller Lite) • Tastetrial.com (Miller Lite) (Award Winning) • Whopperettes.com (BK) (Award Winning) • Sithsense.com (BK) (Award Winning) • Huckinchicken.com (BK) • Virginatlanticflights.com (Virgin Atlantic Airways) • Spreadtheknow.com and behind the scenes web films (Kaiser Family Foundation) • Atmjourneyofcourage.com and Cinema Spot (Compass Bank) • Earthlinkcyberheroes.com and associated online media (EarthLink) • BKmasks.com and associated online media (BK).
