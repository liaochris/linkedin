Deepak Ramachandran’s Summary

Vision and advice on questions of strategy and business development, for large corporations (Fortune 100) and some start-ups. At New Paradigm, our research focuses on how technology and demographics are combining to introduce another sea-change in business models. My contribution is informed by personal experience, especially new business ventures and cross-border mergers, in India, China, Europe, the US and Canada.
