Bill Stokes’s Summary

Bill Stokes is an Executive Search Consultant, managing business development and leading executive-level searches. Practice areas are (1) Public Relations (including PR, Marketing Communications, and Investor Relations), and (2) Public Affairs (PA, Government Relations, Lobbying, and Advocacy). Sectors covered include technology, defense aerospace, health care, energy, and financial services.

Concurrently, Bill serves Chairman of the Washington Network Group (WNG), an association of 650 professionals representing private sector firms, multilateral institutions, government agencies, the Congress, foreign embassies, and the media.

Previously, Bill was Managing Director of EndeavorConnnect LLC, where advised technology and new media companies on US market entry strategies and expanding abroad. Bill is a seasoned non-profit manager, serving at the American Institute for Contemporary German Studies (AICGS), the Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS), and the National Council for International Trade Development (NCITD).

He holds a bachelor's degree in History and Political Science from the College of William & Mary in Virginia and went on to attend George Washington University's Elliott School of International Affairs.

Other current affiliations include the Institute of Entrepreneurship at the Harry F. Byrd, Jr. School of Business, Shenandoah University (advisory board member); the International Business Exchange Council (IBEC), an initiative of the Fairfax County Chamber of Commerce in Northern Virginia (founding member). Past affiliations include the World Affairs Council of Washington, D.C. (board member); First Tuesday Washington, D.C. (city leader); the German American Business Association (board member); and the Phillips Exeter Alumni Association of Washington, D.C. (president).

Contact Email: Stokes @ WashingtonNetworkGroup DOT com
