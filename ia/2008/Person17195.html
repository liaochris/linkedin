Ashley Collins’s Summary

Ashley is a pragmatic technical architect with significant expertise in land-based digitisation and ISTAR technologies. Most recently, he has been engaged with bid work helping to architect the ground segment for a technology refreshment programme of one of the MOD's existing ISTAR platforms.

He maintains a sound working knowledge of the defence sector and has worked on projects with exposure to infantry/artillery fire control, undersea warfare, battlespace management, Electronic Warfare (EW), counter-terrorism, Network Enabled Capability (NEC) and ISTAR. He has experience in constructing technical architectures using the MoD's Architecture Framework (MoDAF). In addition, he has developed a good working knowledge of Infantry/Artillery doctrine and ballistics.

He has extensive client-facing experience and has participated in requirements capture, presentations and end-user workshop activities.

Ashley Collins’s Specialties:

He has a proven track record of leading small projects and teams involved in the delivery of software solutions. Ashley recently held the position of Technical Design Authority for the delivery of a ruggedised ballistic handheld computer for the British Army.

He is an experienced interviewer and participates regularly in recruitment initiatives. Ashley is a Staff Manager/Mentor responsible for the training and career development of fifteen staff.
