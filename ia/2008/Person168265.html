Sam Xu’s Summary

With over 10 years experience in executive management and marketing & public relations which has equipped me with a multitude of excellent communications skills, I am certain I would prove to be an asset at your company.

Throughout my career, I have demonstrated an exceptional facility for initiating and maintaining contacts with media, government, financial groups and communities. With experience of acting as an interpreter for the former US President Clinton, I was also responsible for client greeting, tour leading and other hospitality services. I have developed a reputation as an adept event planner with a background in journalism, having served as VP of Chint Group with about 14,500 employees for 3 years.

Last year, worked as the VP of Who's Who Media Group and the General Manager of Who's Who Movie & TV Shows Production Co. Ltd. (www.wswmedia.cn), I produced and marketed a very hot TV drama series (82 episodes) on CCTV, and acted as the chief director for the International Confucius Festival in September, 2006.

As the editor-in-chief of Oriental Times, Vancouver, I developed experiences with various marketing tools and communication vehicles, such as direct mail, print advertising, online banners ads, email marketing, special events and affiliate management.

As VP in charge of Investor Relations of the listed Panoramic Mirrors Inc., Vancouver, I developed an understanding of the local business community, financial and securities regulations and marketing techniques.
