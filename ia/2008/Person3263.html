Adrian Brown’s Summary

Veteran Recruitment professional within the Games Industry- More than 10 years experience.
I have worked with EA, Ubisoft, Codemasters, Rockstar, Mcrosoft, Disney, THQ, Vivendi and many more. I now work for one of the best Games Companies in the World - Sega Europe. All four studios have amazing projects and fantastic talent and with the support of SEGA they can now only grow from strength to strength.
I had no intention of taking on a permenant full time role at a Games Company and had received numerous offers. When Sega came along I suddenly realised that they were a company I really wanted to work for, Not only is SEGA a legend within games but the studios it had aquired were World Class (amazing IP) and the vision was ambitous and long term.
If you are working in the Games Industry and would like to know what kind of opportunities are available within SEGA then please feel free to get in touch - would be great to hear from you.
