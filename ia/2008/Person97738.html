Joseph Tate’s Summary

After finishing a Ph.D. on Shakespeare in 2005 and publishing a book the same year on Radiohead, I made the transition in 2006 from university-level teaching of literature (Ancient to Postmodern) and composition (expository, research and business writing) to web design and development. I worked for Modwest, Inc. from 2005 to 2006 and I'm now working for the University of Washington designing and developing websites and web application interfaces for their Network Management Tools Team. I've specialized in content, production management, and information architecture for the web and I'm now moving into research on data visualization and intensive Ajax development using JavaScript, jQuery, JSON and XML. I'm a member of the Information Architecture Institute, the Interaction Design Association and the Web Standards Group.
