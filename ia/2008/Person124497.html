Mathijs Suidman’s Summary

My focus as a consultant is on publishing and media. Specifically on product and business innovation.
I have experience both in traditional publishing and in new media.
I develop innovative publishing concepts and implement them (in existing businesses).

Besides consulting, I am an "entrepreneur by accident" ;-). Owning a company which started as a niche publisher with a focus on personalized content and grew into a organization selling personalized gifts and incentives for both consumer and b-to-b market. Within this companny (Personalgifts BV) the "folio" personalized products still are an important driver for their business.

Owning this (internet oriented) company learned me that despite the great business plans one can write, one should always dare to be completely open to customers even if "they" change your entire business plan and product/service offering. I always wanted to be a publisher, but now these customers drove me into the gift/incentive business. At least you can conclude that business is an adventure ;-).
