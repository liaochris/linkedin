Al Tanenbaum’s Summary

Al Tanenbaum, is a senior executive focused on building value for customers, shareholders, and employees. With over twenty years of progressive leadership in executive management, financial management, cutting-edge technology, merchant banking, and strategic planning, he combines his Brown University economics and Harvard MBA degrees with practical business solutions and real world insight. Mr. Tanenbaum has proven his leadership both in large corporate and in mid-sized entrepreneurial environments. His breadth of experience allows him to share a broad array of business styles and operating challenges with gShare’s customers.
