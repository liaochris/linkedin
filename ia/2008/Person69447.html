Gethin Rhys’s Summary

Minister of two churches (1) Castle Square church, Trefforest, south of Pontypridd, cheek by jowl with University of Glamorgan, where we work closely with the Ecumenical Chaplaincy to the University and with the Trefforest Regeneration Partnership (2) Porth United Reformed Church, Rhondda valley north of Pontypridd, a post-industrial area, where we have a parochial ministry (baptisms, weddings, funerals, work with Brownies and Guides and local schools and Town Centre Forum, ecumenical links with neighbouring churches).
Professional interests, therefore, in ministry in University and post-industrial locations; also in ecumenism, emerging church and missiology and retreats and lay training.
