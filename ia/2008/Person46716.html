David Gurle’s Summary

My dream is to transform the way people communicate with each other through any type of computers and networks. I believe that most good and bad things about what we do is either a consequence of bad or miscommunication or fruit of well communicated thoughts. While it is pretty to hard to make people say the right things to the right people(if you got the secret please let me know), it is in the realm of possibilities that in this decade we can give communitities communications tools which will enable people to create a virtual web of their contacts and pick the right communication medium (text, audio, video) to express their thoughts more effectively than what they do today with their phones, email, instant messaging and online and face to face meetings...

I have been carrying on making this dream a reality for the last 16 years from college to DEC, from DEC to France Telecom, from France Telecom to ETSI, from ETSI to VocalTec, from VocalTec to Microsoft and now from Microsoft to Reuters.
