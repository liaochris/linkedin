Paula Kvarnberg’s Summary

Paula is a seasoned executive recruiter and human resources consultant with 20 years of progressive experience. Paula began her career performing executive search for Fortune 500 companies. She progressed from an Entry-Level Account Executive to an Executive Director overseeing 30 executive recruiters. In 10 years of executive search some of her accolades include 7 company records for highest personal production, numerous “Producer of the Month” awards, “Largest Producer of Repeat Business”, and numerous “Top Recruiter of the Year” awards.

In 1997 Paula moved to the corporate side of recruitment and employment with OutSource International. After many promotions she became the Director of Human Resources reporting to the President/CEO/and Chairman of the Board. There she oversaw Employment, Employee Relations, Training, Compensation, and Benefits. Some of her accomplishments include the following: reduced turnover by 22%, reduced EEOC claims by 12%, reduced cost per hire by 10%, reduced outside recruiting costs by over $250K annually, and created an employee referral program reducing cost per hire by 15%.

In 2001 Paula began her private consulting practice of HR Advisor, LLC. HR Advisor focuses on Human Resources as a catalyst for companies that are underperforming or expanding. She provides human resources assistance in any number of the following disciplines: Employment, Employee Relations, Training, Compensation, and Benefits. Recognizing that companies perform best with key people in the right positions, Paula also created Search America, LLC., to serve clients with higher level executive search needs.

Paula graduated from the University of Miami Magna Cum Laude in 1985. She is a member of SHRM, EMA, HRPBC, and HRABC and actively pursues emerging trends in human resources education through seminars, conventions and various classes and is certified to perform Job Analysis and Assessment Analysis by SHL.
