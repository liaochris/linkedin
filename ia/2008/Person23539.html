Bill Walker’s Summary

With more than 28 years of entertainment media experience, Mr. Walker possesses a solid, proven background in sales, marketing, operations, merchandising, international and domestic purchasing, materials management, creative development of consumer packaging and point of purchase materials (including both permanent and temporary displays), retail management, and systems/project development.

Most recently, in 2007, he began publishing under Gene Poole Publishing, the regional Music & Entertainmeny monthly Amps11 Magazine. It can be found on-line at www.Amps11.com.

In 2004 he founded Darwin Media Publishing/Succession Records to develop, produce and promote music and other intellectual properties.

His primary focus, founded in 2001, is Media Evolved, LLC, a CD & DVD replication company servicing clients that include: DreamWorks, Lexmark, Sony Music, Compaq, BellSouth, Coca Cola and The US Government to name few. Media Evolved currently operates 8 CD lines, 4 DVD lines and offers 6-color disc printing and a full line of packaging and fulfillment options. Media Evolved currently employs 150 full-time and 60 permanent part-time associates. It can be found on-line at www.MediaEvolved.com
.

Prior to and concurrently with his positions. Mr. Walker has served as an operations, systems and economies consultant for the entertainment media industry. Clients have included: New Line Home Video, Warner Home Video, Cabin Fever Entertainment, Castle Rock, Mediacopy and UAV Entertainment prior to his employment with that corporation.

Additionally, In 2004 The Charlotte Chamber of Commerce and Wachovia named Mr. Walker their Entrepreneur of the Year. In 1992 Mr. Walker won an Excellence Award in General Superiority for package design in end-use toys, games, hobbies, crafts and recreational products by the National Paperboard Association for UAV Corporation’s Video Gift Pak packaging.

Co-inventor of the patented CoasterROM www.coasterROM.com
