Bill Weber’s Summary

I think in patterns. It’s a fascination for taking individual pieces of information and creating a view of where I am and where I want to go.

As an Architect, this puts me in the position of learning about the individual characteristics of a project and planning its future, minimizing the inevitable obstacles and plotting a course toward the ultimate objectives of my customer.

While the technical domain changes, the fascination and feeling of success it brings is the same.

My career is based on unearthing these challenges both with my customer and within myself.
