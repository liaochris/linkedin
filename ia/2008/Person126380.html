Maya Elhalal’s Summary

Internet entrepreneur, consultant and experienced manager of web applications design and development projects.

Co-founded Site On Spot in 2003. The company specializes in community websites, Internet marketing and online advertising. One of the company's most recent internet ventures is Beta Marker, a social network for software enthusiasts. Beta Marker is the first social network to allow content cooperation between the English and the Chinese users. Among its other activities, the company owns and runs Ad On Spot, an advertising network.

Provides consulting services regarding Internet marketing, Internet business and revenue models, technological feasibility and implementation. Customers range from internet entrepreneurs, who wish to transform their idea to a successful Internet business, to marketers of offline businesses that wish to enhance corporate online presence.

Co-lecturer in a course about Internet Entrepreneurship in the MBA Program of The Interdisciplinary Center Herzeliya, Israel.

Graduate of the first Zell Entrepreneurship Program.

Email me regarding interesting internet ventures to Maya at SiteOnSpot.com
