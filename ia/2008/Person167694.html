Samantha Hernandez’s Summary

Samantha Hernandez is an award winning creative designer and art director that has a cheerful outlook on life and brings a fresh approach to graphic design while meeting difficult challenges and tight deadlines. Samantha loves working with a variety of media on projects ranging from corporate branding to national advertising campaigns. She enjoys working with other creative professionals. She has won several awards for her creativity and has had her work exhibited three times in three different galleries. Additionally, she was published in Logo Lounge for exceptional logo design in 2007, and was a winner in the International PCOM Awards for 2 of her logo designs. Samantha is a self starter with an extensive desire to learn about all aspects of the design field and excited to expand her skill set.

Samantha Hernandez’s Specialties:

Art Direction, Branding and Identity, Graphic Design, Advertising Concept and Design, Project Management, Photography Direction, Project Development, Department and Vendor Liaison, Print Production
