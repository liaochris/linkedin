Brian Emerson’s Summary

I graduated from UC Santa Barbara in 1999, while their I co-wrote and directed an animated short that garnered some honors including a student academy award and the IFC grand prize for student animation. After college I worked at ICT, a graphics research institute, under Paul Debevec where among other things I was the lead artist on a short called the Parthenon. Since then I've done a bunch of freelance including "Fantastic 4." After that I spent two years at Sprite Animation doing mostly character modeling and rigging for some TV pilots and feature films. Since then I've moved to Colorado to spend some time in the mountains and pursue a freelance career doing animation and graphic design.

Brian Emerson’s Specialties:

I'm generalist, in animation my strengths are modeling (character/background), rigging, lighting, texturing, compositing, animation and mel scripting. I also do graphic design and motion graphics.
