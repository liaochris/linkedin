Jessica Wallin’s Summary

My name is Jessica P. Wallin, and I am a writer/blogger/model/amateur photographer. I write for Backseat Cuddler, a popular daily celebrity news/gossip site, as well as a Chattanooga weekly alternative called Enigma Magazine and other sites that can be found with a simple Google search of "Jessica P. Wallin" or "Jebbica". As a model, I'm working on my portfolio. I have been in one fashion show, modeled duds for clothing companies, and I am about ready to start looking at agencies. I'm also an aspiring photographer, though I only shoot with an 8 mpx Olympus Stylus 800 at the moment.
