Lawrence Sherman’s Summary

Global continuing medical expert with a proven history of designing, developing, implementing and evaluating strategic Continuing Medical Education (CME) and Continuing Professional Development (CPD) activities around the world. Working in collaboration with other accredited and non-accredited educational providers, partners, and other people and groups, the educational activities meet the identified practice gaps and are aimed at improving clinical practice, patient health, and (yeah, yeah, I know, in an ideal world...) population health.

New opportunities for collaboration, discussion, and networking are not only welcomed, but encouraged! These can take the form of consultations with other educational providers, potential faculty, people and organizations with an interest in assessing the needs of certain key groups of health care professionals. Currently, I have a keen interest in identifying the best methods for sharing both needs assessment and educational outcomes data.

Lawrence Sherman’s Specialties:

Comprehensive dynamic and ongoing needs assessments, mutli-modal, multiple-media CME/CPD activities, educational collaboration, global CME/CPD consultation, CME/CPD joint sponsorship, outcomes measurement and educational impact assessments.
