Benjine Gerber’s Summary

Benjine Gerber has been self-employed for the past 6 years and has established a self-starter community, the IT Emporium, to support self-starters. The focus areas are self-education, self-motivation and self-promotion.

She has been creating websites for 12 years, has graphic and web design, systems and content development and training skills. The web address of the IT-Emporium is www.itemporium.co.za and of the services - www.self-educate.com, www.self-motivate.com and www.self-promote.com.

She would like to see individuals skill up to better utilise any latent potential and more opportunities opening up for everyone in IT worldwide.

Attractive interfaces, process improvement and productivity, fine-tuning existing infrastructures, and getting systems in tune with users are some of her passions.

Benjine is interested in serving on the CT-SPIN committee and is involved with promotions, networking, collaboration, presentation, research and adhoc activities of the CT-SPIN, and views involvement in CT-SPIN as an excellent learning opportunity. She wishes to contribute to the growth of the software industry in the Western Cape. The web address for CT-SPIN is www.spin.org.za.
