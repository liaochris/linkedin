Pedro Roseiro’s Summary

I've started my professional career in the public sector. I was responsible for developing software solutions to deal with several administrative processes in municipalities and in the Portuguese Navy (regarding water sports, fisheries and boat registration).
Since then, I’m becoming more involved in BPM, CRM, eGovernment and eCommerce projects with a different maturity level.
Starting at the late 90s, I've been technical manager (and Project Manager) during the implementation of several eCommerce projects and a Digital Content Management Platform. Those eCommerce solutions were used in some of the most important portals in Portugal.
With the start up of "Digital Cities" in Portugal, I've been leading a team that is mostly focused in developing eGovernment solutions. I've been incorporating the private sector way of working in several municipalities.
Mostly, I'm establishing requirements (defined with customers), designing workflows and architecting the solution (including Intranet and Internet portals, connecting to the backoffice applications via Web Services) in a close partnership with AIRC (a important partner in this area – it’s a municipalities association that produces software for more than 50% of the municipalities in Portugal).
We're integrating software from several providers, implementing BPM, using Infopath Forms and Content Management Solutions to create integrated solutions for Internet and Intranet, giving a new business sense to the tools.
