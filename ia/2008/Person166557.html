Ryan Kelly’s Summary

Pear Analytics is a fresh, crisp, organic perspective on what web analytics should be. By using a unique approach and a variety of tools, we obtain the ability to actually listen to our website customers. Nothing is canned or prepackaged, instead, we deliver insights that are relevant and actionable.

In addition to web analytics, we also analyze and compile data on a broader scale to help drive better, relevant marketing. By analyzing other data sources such as sales, media buy, customer relationship and more, we can better understand customers and how they respond to marketing messages. After a period of time, we may be able to understand how to craft the right message, to the right person at the right time.

Ryan Kelly’s Experience

  • Owner, Chief Analyst

    Pear Analytics

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 2008 — Present (7 months)

    Pear Analytics is a fresh, crisp, organic approach to web analytics. Traditional analytical tools tell us a lot of "what" is happening on our website - such as how many visitors did we get today, or how long did they stay, or what pages were viewed - but usually tells very little about "why" - such as "why are my widgets not selling?", or "why do we have a technical support page, but people still call?", or "why are people not clicking on this link for free stuff?"

    Pear Analytics attempts to uncover the "why" by using various testing methods to address usability issues, page content and placement, user interface design and more. We deliver measurable outcomes and provide actionable insights into the behavior of your visitors in a one-page summary - not a 300 page computer-generated report that doesn't tell you what to do next.

    We specialize in high-level analytics, multi-variate testing, search engine optimization, search engine marketing, user interface and social media.
