Timothy MacDowall, RCDD/NTS’s Summary

Mr. MacDowall brings forth a broad business background, with extensive experience in telecommunications and other internetworking functions, as well as marketing, quality, operations, customer support, and training. His industry experience spans managed services, software, telecommunications networking and manufacturing. In additional to more than 15 cumulative management experience, Mr. MacDowall also maintains a very strong set of technical skills, with extensive hands-on experience in the design, implementation and support of telecommunications infrastructure ranging from cable plant to LAN, WAN, and Wireless (Wi-Fi) Networking. Mr. MacDowall has also overseen numerous major telecommunications implementations, and Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) projects.

Mr. MacDowall’s employment history includes both engineering and management positions with such industry leading organizations as International Business Machine (IBM), and Nortel Networks. While at IBM, Mr. MacDowall was part of a development team that introduced the first structure cabling system to support high speed data over Shielded Twisted Pair (STP). Mr. MacDowall was part of the development team at Nortel Networks engaged in the forefront of distribution design for voice and data communication systems, including pioneering work on the 100 Mbps, TP-DDI Networks; and instrumental in the planning and execution of the first public demonstration of TP-DDI at 1992 BICSI Conference at Tampa, Florida.

Timothy MacDowall, RCDD/NTS’s Specialties:

Dual certified Registered Communications Distribution Designer (RCDD®) and RCDD/Network Transport Systems (NTS) Specialist as a member of Building Industry Consulting Service International (BICSI). Member since 1990, RCDD since 1993 and RCDD/NTS Specialist since 1994. Nortel Networks Business Communications Manager (BCM 3.0) Certification since 2003 and Certified Identity Theft Risk Management Specialist (CITRMS) as a member of The Institute of Fraud Risk Management (TIFRM) since 2006.
