Pavel Dobrovský’s Summary

My journalist career started in newspaper DNES, where I cofounded mass scale internet portal (www.idnes.cz).

Over the period of six years I visited every country from Cape Town to Cairo (and many others in Asia and Indonesia) and I became active in lectures about Africa and publishing articles about vanishing tribes in some countries.

Experiences from travelling result in huge photo-archive (few thousands pictures), some of them were published in different Czech and US magazines nad brochures.

On the other side I am trying to bridge the gap between the public and interactive art. I gave lectures on art of computer games on Multimediale event in Prague, Masaryks University in Brno and in few other places.

For the Czech magazine Level I produce DVD with video content and special gaming podcast and videos for Hrej.cz, sister site of Level.

I decided to keep the freelance journalist status, which helps me to explore more the areas I am interested in. I can send full version of CV, if you are interested.
