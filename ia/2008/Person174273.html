Shari Steinhaus’s Summary

My professional background includes over ten years combined experience in career counseling, human resources, management, recruiting, sales and marketing dealing with fast-paced, rapidly expanding companies. My love for recruiting started back in 1995 when I was working in the Executive Development Program at The FACS Group. I had the fortunate experience to spend the majority of my time as a Recruiter in the Human Resources Department. After 2 and a half years of working directly in a staffing agency for Olsten Staffing, I spent 6+ years as the Career Development Director at Antonelli College, a local career college in downtown Cincinnati. In early 2007 I decided to make that leap into independent contracting. I work as a Professional Recruiter for Professional Staffing Solutions.

PSS is a locally owned recruiting company that has been in operation since 1998. We partner with companies to assist with staffing needs for professional personnel on a direct hire basis.

I value long-term relationships and respect the needs and concerns of both my clients and candidates. I work diligently to provide professional solutions that benefit everyone involved, and I get a great deal of personal satisfaction when these needs and goals are realized.

The best way to reach me is via phone @ 513-231-5429 or email @ shari.steinhaus@fuse.net. - S
