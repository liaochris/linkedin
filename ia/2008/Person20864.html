Becky Carroll’s Summary

Becky is the President and Founder of Petra Consulting Group, a management consultancy. Becky helps companies grow their business through improving their customer experience. Becky’s clients have included several Fortune 100 companies such as Electronic Arts, HP, Fujitsu, and Ford Motor Company as well as start-up companies. Projects have included customer strategy and process improvements, targeted marketing, social media strategy, contact center strategy, customer experience touch point mapping, as well as customer needs, segmentation, and insight research.

Becky teaches at UC San Diego Extension on Marketing via New Media. She is active in social media circles and helps her clients figure out whether and how to use social media to engage with their customers.

Becky is the author of the business blog Customers Rock!, listed among the Top 25 Marketing blogs and also a well-known customer service blog. She is also a radio personality for the nationally syndicated program The Big Biz Show. As a highly-regarded speaker with international expertise, Becky's speeches range from keynotes to workshops to webcasts.

Previously, Becky was a Senior Consultant with industry-leading Peppers and Rogers Group. During her time at Peppers and Rogers Group, Becky was involved with customer strategy, loyalty metrics, contact center strategy, and high tech planning. She also contributed to the company methodology in mobile commerce and strategy.

Prior to joining Peppers and Rogers Group, Becky worked at Hewlett Packard for 14 years. Most recently, she had served as Director of Marketing for the UK and Ireland. In the UK, Becky led a variety of strategic customer-focused marketing initiatives in the areas of customer support, mobile e-business, dot.com, and channels.

Prior to her time in the UK, Becky was the Worldwide Customer Loyalty Marketing Manager for HP Services. Becky also taught technical and marketing courses at HP.

Becky Carroll’s Specialties:

Customer experience, social media, marketing, customer retention and customer loyalty, service and support strategy in high technology, information technology, entertainment industry, gaming industry, automotive industry, contact centers.
