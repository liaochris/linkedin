Idrise Abdi’s Summary

I'm an experienced software engineer and am currently specializing in the Microsoft .NET platform. Most of my experience in Houston has been in the energy sector although, as a consultant, I've been involved in numerous industries.

I've the past several years, I've been focusing on key, senior-level consulting positions. The more recent projects have utilized custom SharePoint / MOSS applications. They included heavy integration of BPM (Business Process Management), SharePoint and ECM (Electronic Content Managment). I've completed three major BPM-related solutions for Schlumberger.

I'm a strong proponent of code generation. It can be applicable to most projects in some capacity, and when used wisely, will substantially decrease development and maintenance time.

One area that I'd really like to explore professionally are RIAs (Rich Internet Applications). I remember being fascinated with RIAs five years ago but the tool-sets were primitive and development was really time consuming. I'm excited to see this area finally having some potential to enter into mainstream development as a useful tool with the advent of Windows Presentation Framework (WPF) and related technologies.

Another exciting area is BPM/Workflow. After having used K2.NET Workflow for a couple years now, its been exciting to see the developments of Microsoft's Windows Workflow (part of the 3.0 .NET framework).
