Marco Hoppenbrouwer’s Summary

Marco Hoppenbrouwer is based in The Netherlands with Shell International BV He is a member of the Technology Investigations (TI) team that is part of Shell’s Corporate Centre. The TI department support the Shell Group of companies by exploring new information technology that could create new business opportunities for Shell, aligned with business goals and objectives. Marco’s investigation includes identifying and forecasting how emerging technology will shape the future IT solutions for the Shell Group, enabling the right technology decisions at the right time.

Prior to his current position, Marco spent 14 years in various Shell roles beginning in 1992 with Manufacturing in the administration & support function. In parallel to Marco’s early career he spend 8 years in evening classes that lead to a bachelor degree in IT Service Management in 2000. Marco has a broad experience due to the many assignments in various Shell businesses that covered regional and global initiatives. His career path sofar is from administrative support, to executive support, to webmaster, to web coordinator, to technical architect, to project manager for Sarbanes Oxley and now advisor technology investigations.

In social life Marco spends as much time as possible with his family. Marco's hobbies are travel, cars and video games.
