Duane Loose’s Summary

Award winning Production Designer, Art Director, Industrial Designer - my passion is learning, creating worlds and telling great stories. I lead with clear vision, humor and service to my fellow artists.

Duane Loose’s Specialties:

Production design, Industrial Design, Art Direction, precision visual effects and animation for entertainment, design, aerospace and military industries.

Duane Loose’s Experience

  • Head Dude

    alphaZED studios

    (Privately Held; Myself Only; Entertainment industry)

    June 2007 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    This is my studio. I am currently under contract with Electric Farm Entertainment to provide production design, CG supervision and art direction for their web based digital series Gemini Division.

  • Senior Production Designer

    Electric Farm Entertainment

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry)

    June 2007 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    Senior Production Designer overseeing visual development and production of Electric Farms' web based digital series.

    EF's first IP into production is "Afterworld". See it at www.afterworld.tv

    EF's second IP into production is "Gemini Division" starring Rosario Dawson. We are currently in pre-production.

    Electric Farm was created in 2007 by Brent Friedman, Stan Rogow and Jeff Sagansky. The business model is to create multiple web based digital content properties driven by great story, excellent visual (limited/anime style) presentation and aggressive use of technology.

    I joined EF to art direct their current project, distributed worldwide by Sony, and to production design the ongoing development of multiple new series.

    We use a FAT process: Fast, Agile, Technology Driven.

  • Senior Art Director

    Electronic Arts/Los Angeles

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; ERTS; Computer Games industry)

    March 2004 — June 2007 (3 years 4 months)

    Senior Art Director at the LA studio. I directed a team of 50 + artists in two locations (LA and Montreal) for 2005's Medal of Honor: European Assault.

    My last project at EA was art directing "Tiberium" a next gen game using Unreal 3 in the Command and Conquer universe. I served on the studio senior leaders working group and established a cross studio art directors cell to create and maintain working relationships between art directors and cg supervisors on our game teams.

    I mentored 2 lead artists from MOHEA into art director roles. In 2006 I served on the worldwide working group that redesigned the career track for artists and technical artists at Electronic Arts.

    In 2007 I was nominated to serve on the worldwide art director council for Electronic Arts worldwide studios.

  • Supervising Art Director

    Creative Capers Entertainment

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry)

    My work at CCE included story development, production design, art direction, lighting, compositing, VFX supervision and technical direction.
