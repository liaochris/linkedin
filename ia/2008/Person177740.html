Santiago Moral’s Summary

I have been working in technology consulting for more than 13 years, in several fields: groupware and Internet technologies, CRM and Business Intelligence and in the last four years in e-learning. Most of this time I was in IBM.
I worked first in technical matters, then managing teams, later in business development, and consulting and project management.
Now I am partner and responsible for the Barcelona team of MMBB, the Interactive Marketing Group (Adysa + Geanet + Gestazion). We define online strategy and implement online projects for important consumer and industrial brands.
