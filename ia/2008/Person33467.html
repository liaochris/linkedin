Charaighn Sesock’s Summary

Charaighn’s extensive experience in public relations and marketing communications comes from over 11 years of working in both PR agency and in-house corporate/nonprofit positions across a wide range of industries including consumer, music and entertainment, healthcare, environmental, and technology. Throughout her career, Charaighn has managed many PR programs offering clients various services including product launches, media and community relations, media tours and press events, branding, corporate messaging and positioning, crisis communications, media training, trade show management and collateral development.

Charaighn Sesock’s Specialties:

Sesock Communications understands the importance of developing and implementing effective, measurable public relations/communications programs that support clients’ visibly and business objectives. Our goal is to help clients create strategic, innovative and effective campaigns.
