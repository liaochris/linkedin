Alora Chistiakoff’s Summary

A career IT professional, with a strong background in project management. Actively involved in projects of all sizes and levels of complexity in numerous types of environments: custom software development and product management, Enterprise Application and Integration, infrastructure and operations implementation and management, business systems analysis, process engineering and change management. My direct industry experience has been in ecommerce (supporting mid-sized retailers and catalogers front-end development as well as back-end integration), in-game video game advertising (working with video game developers on integration, advertisers on AdOps requirements, and development team on product development and lifecycle management) and airline industry (custom software development, Enterprise Implementation and Integration, process development, standards governance and portfolio management).
