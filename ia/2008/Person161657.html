Rob Anspach’s Summary

I have a wide range of roles with Real Art Design Group. My responsibilities include; print production management, network systems administration, fulfillment and shipping.

Currently I've taken on the responsibility for starting a "young professionals creative group, called The Dayton Creative Syndicate. The Creative Syndicate will run alongside The Greater Dayton Advertising Association sharing members and functions but concentrating it's main message and goals towards promoting creativity in our community

This group will act for the purpose of education and networking, providing young creative professionals with the skills and tools they need to succeed in our challenging and rewarding industry.

So this is my main goal for 2008: Produce a young creatives group we can be proud of while establishing a solid foundation that will serve as a base for this organization for many years to come.

Rob Anspach’s Specialties:

Over 15 years of print production, technical expertise and project facilitation experience having worked on projects for such clients as; Char Broil, General Motors, Huffy, MeadWestvaco, NCR, NewPage, Pinnacle and Titleist.
