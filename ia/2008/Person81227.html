Ivan Farkas’s Summary

Ivan has extensive real estate experience servicing buyers and sellers in the Miami, Fort Lauderdale and Palm Beach area and through his network to all other areas in the US. He is known in the area as the premier Realtor® for those who demand the best, personalized service throughout the transaction. Ivan prides himself on his relationships with his clients and gets most of his business from referrals and recommendations.

He keeps up with the latest tools and trends to make sure that his properties are marketed to their full extent to also be a resource for buyers. Since 98% of buyers start their home search online, he has aggressive online marketing campaigns and networks with other Agents to reach the largest audience possible.

Through his dedication, attention to detail, integrity and communication skills, He will always strive to satisfy the most discerning buyers and sellers. With all of his expertise, he has gained a tremendous insight into the process of selling real estate. His over a decade independent computer consultant career gives him the ability and edge to truly tap into all high-tech technological advancements on all areas of real estate; marketing, prospecting, advertising and killer sales presentations.

You need a Realtor, like Ivan, who is well informed not only about the local real estate, but also national and global real estate and financial market trends, understands the complex relationships among market facts and trends and can draw the proper conclusion that will create the best Return on Investment (ROI) for you. He loves the challenges and the great potentials in the real estate business.

Ivan grew up in Budapest, Hungary, spent over a decade in the New York metropolitan area and currently lives in Miramar, Florida, near Miami and Fort Lauderdale. Contact Ivan for more information on any of his services with no obligation! He speaks fluent English and Hungarian.
