Derek Mehraban’s Summary

A graduate of Michigan State University, Derek Mehraban has 14+ years advertising, marketing and interactive experience. Mehraban is focused on marketing (online and offline), event marketing, web search, web 2.0, social networking, blogging and generational marketing.

Mehraban is President of Ingenex Group, an interactive marketing company located in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Ingenex blends expertise in generational marketing, market research, interactive media, new marketing, advertising and public relations to deliver fully integrated solutions to client marketing problems.

During his career Mehraban has helped the following brands with their marketing and advertising: the Pontiac, Cadillac and Chevrolet divisions of General Motors, ACDelco, Subway Subs, GDI Infotech, b’drizzled Gourmet Popcorn, Innovative Composites, Peliton, The United Way, NuStep, Asahi Kasei, ICPSR, The University of Florida, The University of Michigan, Eastern Michigan University and Terumo Cardiovascular.

Mehraban founded Connect Ann Arbor in 2007, a non-profit group focused on organizing compelling Ann Arbor based events around marketing, entrepreneurship and technology topics. Connect Ann Arbor brought Seth Godin to A2 in May and helped plan ArbCamp w/ Joseph Jaffe in November 2007.

A generational marketing expert, Mehraban is also VP Marketing for 60-60 Publications, a consumer magazine focused on providing relevant, engaging and informative content for Baby Boomers and their aging parents.

Mehraban has built a strong network in the marketing, technology and business communities by participating in groups such as Ann Arbor Spark, The Inforum, Ann Arbor Ad Club, Marketing Roundtable and A2B3. Mehraban recommends web 2.0 tools such as Linkedin, Plaxo, Facebook and Twitter to stay in touch.
