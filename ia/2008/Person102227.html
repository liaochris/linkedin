Karen (Blanchard) Stevens’s Summary

In my role at Spherion it is my goal to match my client companies with the best candidates. In my current recruiting role, I specialize in recruiting for Human Resources, Sales & Marketing, Customer Service and Executive Admin. For recruiting services please email me at karenstevens@spherion.com

My background in recruiting - both contingency and contract - along with my expertise in training around goal attainment and motivational strategies, makes me uniquely qualified to be a Professional Development Coach.

I work with my coaching clients to take the time to figure out if they are truly on the right path - if they are fullfilled and excited by their work. And I work with them on structuring their career and their job search to best get them to "that next level".

In workshops I work with participants to balance their career goals with other important areas of their lives so that they continue to be motivated to do well in all areas - in this way their careers or businesses soar while their personal lives are fulfilling. For coaching services, please contact me at karen.stevens@optimizeyou.net - I look forward to helping you think bigger about what you can accomplish!
