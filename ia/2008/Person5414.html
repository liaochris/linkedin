Alan D. Utley’s Summary

I currently work for StandardAero, a world leader in the aerospace industry.

Founded in 1911, StandardAero is one of the largest independent MRO businesses in the world, providing comprehensive services to commercial, military and business aviation, helicopters and industrial operators. Recently, we joined forces with three other long-established companies – Landmark Aviation’s MRO services (formerly Garrett Aviation), Associated Air Center and TSS Aviation. These four great legacies bring over 300 years of collective experience and expertise to form the new StandardAero.

The company now has over 4,000 employees working out of facilities strategically located in the US, Canada, Europe, Asia and Australia and serving customers from over 80 different countries. We provide services across five major categories:

- Business Aviation
- Airlines and Fleets
- Government & Military
- Helicopter Programs
- Energy & Industrial

I work for the Government & Military sector, within the Enterprise Services (ES) business. ES consists of the Affordable Readiness & Transformation Services (ART) and the Corporate University.

ART is a proprietary process aimed at redesigning an MRO business to maximize its workflow and efficiency. This unique cellular redesign process transforms MRO operations, creating a dramatic step change in performance. The “cell” system focuses on the entire business, not a single shop or process and emphasizes maximum ownership by employees and natural grouping of products.

StandardAero University offers comprehensive training solutions to educate the workforce on the critical aspects of Lean/Cellular transformation and sustaining long-term cultural change. We have the unique ability to bring together more than 70 years of practical experience, the example of our existing cellular factories and our expertise in course design and instruction to provide high quality training solutions.
