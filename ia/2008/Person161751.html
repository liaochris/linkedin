Rob Bunting’s Summary

Internet marketing professional since 1998. Applying my ten years of experience by helping both B2C & B2B firms generate more sales or sales leads online through online marketing strategy development and implementation, website traffic analysis, and search engine marketing.

Rob Bunting’s Specialties:

Internet marketing, online marketing, search engine marketing, search engine advertising, search engine optimization, website traffic analysis, PPC pay-per-click search advertising (Google AdWords, Yahoo Sponsored Search, Microsoft AdCenter), Web 2.0, business blogging, social media

Rob Bunting’s Experience

  • Cincinnati I-marketing Group (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Internet industry)

    September 2000 — Present (8 years 4 months)

    The Cincinnati I-marketing Group was founded in 2000 by me, Rob Bunting, MBA, as a networking & discussion group for people to learn more and discuss issues related to Internet marketing. In 2002, I began working as an Internet marketing consultant in my spare time and in 2004 decided to expand my business into a full-time venture serving firms looking to generate more sales or sales leads from their websites but not large enough to take on a full-time Internet marketing manager.

    The Cincinnati I-marketing Group is an alternative to taking on I-marketing projects in-house or working with traditional advertising and marketing agencies that lack the same level of experience with online marketing. I am “results-focused” and provide personalized custom services, closely monitoring my clients’ progress, not “cookie-cutter” programs on autopilot. Clients receive easy-to-understand reports so they can see their precise return on investment along with recommendations for improvement.
