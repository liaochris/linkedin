Tom Muyllaert (SEM & SEO Consultant)’s Summary

Personal mission statement: Listen. Learn. & Leverage value.

I have 10 years of experience in sales & marketing and had the opportunity to get involved in the commercial start-up of a spin-off company of the VUB back in 1997(ELSYCA - www.elsyca.com).
My sales profile is more of a farmer having experience in solution selling and handling complex sales cycle. Since 2 years now, I am enjoying my passion being the internet & online marketing in specific.

I used to work as senior SEO consultant at iProspect Belgium, part of Aegis Media Belgium since January 2008 (before part Belgacom Skynet).
