Nikolaus Ong’s Summary

EMAIL: nikolaus.ong@gmail.com or nikolaus.ong@xm-asia.com

An Advocate & Evangelist for the Digital (Interactive) Channel.

Nikolaus started out his career in as a Financial Accountant for over a year; he got his illustrious start when he decided to move into the Digital Marketing Strategy & the Integrated Marketing business and has never looked back since. As a seasoned marketer with a passion for strategic marketing leadership, serving international brands for more than 9 years, he brings with him a sound knowledge in Integrated Marketing Communications and that has translated into strategic marketing programs across several markets across Asia Pacific. Some of his key clients in the B2B space include Hewlett Packard, Cisco, SAP and the Development Bank of Singapore, other B2C clients include eBay, Coca Cola, Nokia, MasterCard, British Airways and Qantas.

With a regional portfolio, he has managed respective local agencies across the network to ensure excellent and efficient messaging architecture planning and implementation in 12 markets. His portfolio further includes business planning, messaging architecture, campaign analysis and the various relationship-marketing channels.

Some of his recent professional engagements were with OgilvyOne Worldwide, the Chinadotcom Group, M&C Saatchi and, consulting with the Grey Global Group while pursuing a Strategists MBA (International Marketing) offered by the University of Leicester, UK. With a passion for numbers, he also holds a certification of Financial Mastery in Advance Analysis for the U.S. Options Market.

Nikolaus Ong’s Specialties:

- IMC (Integrated Marketing Communications) Planning
- Relationship Marketing
- Measurable Metrics Driven Campaign Planning (Analytics)
- Messaging Architecture
- Digital (Web 2.0) Marketing Strategy & Communications Planning
- Acquisition, retention & usage.
