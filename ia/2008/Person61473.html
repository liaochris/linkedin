Eugene Curtain III’s Summary

Eugene Edward Curtain, III, is President & CEO of Diversity Search Partners, LLC.,and also leads the Firm's Integrated Services and Client Strategy efforts worldwide. Mr. Curtain is responsible for overseeing a group of offices across North America, as well as driving the Firm's state-of-the-art account management capability across all global markets and for delivering the Firm's best resources to its core clients via its proprietary product offerings. In addition, Mr Curtain serves as President of Diversity Initiatives for The Corporate Playbook and Chariman of The Curtain Group , which holds Diversity Search Partners, Diversity JobList.com, Diversity Times.net, Black Men's Health and Curtain Media. Mr. Curtain also serves as a consultant to HBCU Connect, the nations largest HBCU web resource and career web site for African American's. Mr. Curtain remains an active, hands-on executive recruiter. Over the course of 15 years in human capital services, he has built a world-class reputation for excellence and service delivery among consumer-oriented, marketing-driven companies around the world. Additional boards and committes include, Chair of the Minority Search Council, former Co-Chair, Political Action Committee, NAACP, Hampton, VA, Social Service Advisory Board, Hampton, VA and Delegate to the Democratic Party, State of Virginia. Formerly, Mr. Curtain served in management positions with L-3 Communications, and AT&T. Mr. Curtain is a former U.S. Army Paratrooper and Jumpmaster serving with the elite 82nd Airborne Division. Mr. Curtain has served in combat operations during Operations Desert Shield and Desert Storm and peacekeeping missions in Europe. Mr. Curtain attended Alabama A&M University, Hampton University and University of Maryland, Europe an holds degrees in Management, Leadership and Organizational Theory, and a Doctorate in Business Administration. Mr Curtain is named to the National Dean’s List and Who's Who in Black Columbus.
