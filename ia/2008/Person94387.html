John Austin’s Summary

At Timothy James Consulting, we have over 50 years combined experience, specialising in placing Project and Programme Managers, Strategic Management, Business Consultancy and Information Technology Professionals, in permanent, contract and interim positions. Based in Bristol, we have regionally focussed teams, supplying staff across the whole of the UK. We cover every aspect of permanent and contract resourcing – including niche technical specialists, public sector, senior and executive appointments.

Timothy James Consulting were established with a focus on Senior Appointments, therefore, over the last 5 years we have become specialists in the supply of Project & Programme Management, Strategic Management, Business Consultancy and high level Technical positions, however, our business has evolved and we can now provide candidates for the majority of skill sets or technical areas. Resources supplied cover the complete project life cycle including project management, IT management, business & systems analysis, design, development, architecture, implementation, networking & operations.

Our database is populated with the details of over 150,000 IT professionals covering a broad range of specialist skills and high calibre experienced candidates. We attract over 1,000 candidate CVs every week.

We pride ourselves in offering a true consultancy service, investing time to truly understand your business and recruitment strategy, ensuring we supply the most suitable candidates to help you to meet your objectives.
