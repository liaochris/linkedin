Robin A. Rothman’s Summary

Evidenced by my scholastic and professional choices, I am, in the parlance of our times, a pop culture junkie. The certificates and paystubs are simply the means to an end – that end being a constant, albeit futile, attempt to satiate my pop culture cravings. Even without the excuse that "pop culture's my job," I'd still attempt to pseudo-scientifically determine my top 10 songs of the year. I'd still sneak "Big Lebowski" quotes in whenever I can. I would still worship at the alter of TiVO. I would still be blessed/cursed with a Tourrettes-like compulsion to suggest double-entendre headlines. The truth is, I can't really tell the difference between my personal and professional lives anymore. I find myself working while I'm having fun, and having fun while I'm working. Things could be far worse.
