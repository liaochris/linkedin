Michael Mehr’s Summary

Computer software engineer with 31 years of experience, with emphasis on applications development. Primary interest in projects involving interfacing with hardware, particularly in machine control applications in the semiconductor industry. Also interested in general application structuring for efficient individual and team development, embedded systems, pattern recognition applications in machine vision, speech and audio recognition.

Looking for contract development work or consulting positions that would use my expertise and provide a challenge.

Major accomplishment: Responsible for design and implementation of Summit PR (pattern recognition), considered by many to be the best wafer-alignment usage of PR technology available in the industry. Developed across many years associated with Prometrix, Tencor, and KLA-Tencor, this application subsystem incorporated much of the latest technology available, including PatMax(TM) software from Cognex Corp. It was and still is highly respected and valued by the current customer base.
