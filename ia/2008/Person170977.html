Scott Garig’s Summary

My passion is as a creative producer with several of my own film and entertainment projects in development and one ready to fund.

My livelihood is as an independent "creative" consultant. This allows me to represent opportunities and/or act as a facilitator on projects that may involve commercial properties in the way of Casinos, Condos, Golf Courses, Hotels, Resorts, Land, Shopping complexes, Mixed-Use and other types of developments. I'm working on establishing a repore with several “Flags” “Flags” (Major Hotel chains) who seek to acquire additional properties.

Other deals involve New Innovative Products and Technology with some relating to sustainable energy; Precious Metals & Gems, Minerals (inquire for list), Crude Oil, Unique Ventures and More.

I am also a mandate for Bentonite, Leonardite/Humate and Zeolite. Humate is a "super" natural/organic fertilizer, that puts nutrients and proteins into the soil, increase growth from 12 - 25%+ and retains 40% more water. It costs a fraction of chemical fertilizer and it is safe for animals to ingest, when they eat greens in the pasture. Product is shipped worldwide by the metric ton, 1 - 5 year contracts.
