Michael Acton Smith’s Summary

Passionate about creating new products and brands that wow consumers.

Co-founded Firebox.com (multi-channel retailer of gadgets, games and gifts) in 1998 with Tom Boardman. Had a lot of fun scouring the world looking for new products and ideas. Invented the Shot Glass Chess Set along the way (bizarre fusion of chess and alcohol that was a surprise hit!)

Launched Mind Candy in 2003/04 to develop new brands in the interactive entertainment space. Our first project was a puzzle-based Alternate Reality Game called Perplex City - a global treasure hunt played simultaneously by tens of thousands of people around the world across many different types of media.

Very much enjoy working within the entertainment industry at this unique moment in time as new technologies turn existing media business models on there heads. There are a lot of opportunities out there for nimble start-ups to take on industry giants in the TV, film, music, and advertising industries.

Co-founded Second Chance Tuesday in 2006 with Judith Clegg. SCT is a networking event for London’s leading entrepreneurs and investors.

Love working in start-ups alongside smart, creative and passionate people.

Favourite business quote:

“Tech start-ups are the dynamos of our society. They create new wealth, jobs and inventions – and sustain our competiveness on the world stage. Best of all, they shower wealth on the most unlikely people. Start-ups are the engines of human liberation. They’re also scary. They upset the established order. They’re risky. And they attract obsessive, difficult, headstrong people fixated on changing the world” (Wired magazine)
