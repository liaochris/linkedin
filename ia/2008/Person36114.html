Christian Claudio (LION & TopLink.com)’s Summary

Christian Claudio has taken financial and business consulting internationally. With his holding company Tri-Covenant, LLC, he is currently working on international projects which span from Turkey, Dubai & Peru to name a few. Domestically he has taken his knowledge of the real estate market and has leveraged himself in the secondary market of REO's and Non-Performing Notes as a direct Intermediary to buyer and seller mandates.

He enjoys contributing to the company’s good reputation in the community. A native of Puerto Rico, Christian graduated from University of Oklahoma in 1995 and is a member of the Sigma Phi Epsilon Fraternity. He began originating loans in 1997.

During the last ten years, Christian has identified several key characteristics to becoming a successful business finance and corporate development leader. He thinks a drive to excel is the most important element. You have to want to succeed to be successful. That means you have to invest extra time, effort and energy.

Christian is also adamant that a supportive community causes. Which lead him to begin Tri-Covenant's A.R.K. (Act of Random Kindness) initiatives. Because his passion originated in mortgage and real estate, Christian cares deeply for the less fortunate homeless. This is why through A.R.K., Christian and his team supplied a half a ton of bags of blankets, coats, shoes and other clothes to Dallas inner city.

He believes that a major reason for his success is that he placed himself with the right people in his company. This 4 time, president's counsel 5 time sales summit, 6 time state, 3 time national, 2 time Olympic Team member for the Puerto Rican Taekwondo National Team is no stranger to fighting his way to the top.

"Winning is Christian’s passion, God is Christian’s fire and success is Christian’s life." Christian Claudio, Tri-Covenant, LLC
