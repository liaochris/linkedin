Fred Hopper’s Summary

An Information Security Consultant and Leader with a strategically focused approach to analyzing and enhancing information systems to ensure compliance, security and close alignment with the business needs of the enterprise. Successful track record in architecting, implementing and supporting process-based IT environments which are secure, cost-effective and reliable. Extensive experience in risk assessment, payment card risk management, network operations, systems infrastructure support and facility security in medium to large organizations. Proven ability in applying Information Security best practices to financial, manufacturing and high technology environments.

Recent Accomplishments:

- Led a project which performed a major ($2.5 million) Information and Physical Security Risk Assessment for a large, US-based Information Technology Outsourcer serving the US Federal and State governments. Project included an Information Security Policy Review, Physical Security Review, Security Technology Architecture Review, Internal and External Vulnerability Scanning, Firewall and Switch Configuration Review and a Wireless Security Assessment. This project was completed on time and on budget and included a detailed written report and presentation to the customer’s CEP and Executive Committee.

- Prepared and delivered training in Phone and E-mail Spoofing for the fraud investigation team of a major Canadian credit and gift card issuer.

- Assisted a major Canadian museum with the preparation of specifications for the largest IT Vulnerability Assessment in their organization’s history. Assistance was also provided in the review of submissions and final vendor selection.

- Conducted external vulnerability assessments for a major Canadian real estate and capital management company and for several advertising firms based in Canada and the United States.
