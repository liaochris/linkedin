Chad Kalmes’s Summary

Chad has over 9 years of management consulting, advisory, and audit experience including 4 years in the "Big 5" focusing on technology risk consulting.

He is the San Francisco Bay Area lead for Protiviti's IT Service Management products and his IT strategy and effectiveness background includes all major areas of IT. His clients come from all industries - including many pre-IPO and startup technology/web companies with assessing and structuring their IT functions and setting priorities for public company readiness. He has performed software selection reviews, overseen multi-million dollar software implementations, and performed post-implementation reviews to ensure integration with stated business objectives and requirements. Through his work with the IT Process Institute (ITPI), he has also performed analysis of IT process maturity for clients, assisting with developing prioritized roadmaps for business intergration and process re-engineering efforts.

His work in the security space has included data privacy reviews, regulatory compliance assessments, and security audits (internal/external penetration testing, wireless reviews, etc.), including significant work in regulatory and privacy areas (including FFIEC, HIPAA, GLBA, PCI, SB1386, et al).

Chad also has a great deal of experience with Sarbanes-Oxley compliance, including scoping, remediation, testing, and control-rationalization efforts for clients across all major IT domains. His experience includes leveraging a number of industry-standard frameworks and methodologies, including the IIA's GAIT approach and the ITGI guidance on CobiT controls scoping for SOX.

Chad is a CISSP-ISSMP and a CISA. He is a member of the IIA, ISACA, ISC2, itSMF, and the Silicon Valley Leadership Group (Federal Issues Committee). He is a frequent speaker at professional conferences as a subject matter expert in IT services and strategy, compliance, security, and privacy.
