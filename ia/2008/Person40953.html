Timothy Mbugua’s Summary

A business savvy creative consultant would summaries me.

I have worked in the new media industry for the past seven years and executed campaigns for major brands in the region - Coca Cola, East African Breweries, Nation Media, Central Bank of Kenya, Safaricom, Celtel (formerly Kencell). I have also worked with a number of NGO's - The Kenya Human Rights Commission, Transparency International, CRECO (Kenya) and Goal ICT.

I have just finished setting up a value added services firm to complement other service offerings that my firm Symbiotic Media currently has and are going regional in Uganda and Ghana.
