Patrick Bujold’s Summary

I am recruiting for a very early stage startup called MobileIron, currently being incubated by Storm Ventures and funded by Sequoia and Norwest. This is an opportunity for the right folks to join among the first 20 people in the company. As we are in stealth mode there is not much more I can say here.

I've recruited technical and management talent for over two decades. My years in the contingency search business taught me business development and cold calling skills. My experience in the semiconductor business at MMI and IDT taught me high volume staffing and refined my work ethic. Learning the telcom and networking business at SynOptics and Stratacom prepared me to effectively staff Juniper Networks as their first recruiter. Working with Pradeep Sindhu (founder)and Scott Kreins (CEO) and all the early birds at Juniper was very gratifying and rewarding.

After Juniper I was blessed to return to my lake home in Minnesota where I have seen my three children finish high school and college and begin their working lives with a passion. (My new definition of success.)

In the early 2000's I did breifly take some time to help my friend Mark Merrill, the founder and CTO of Netgear to staff up his organization. In the mid 2000's I went back to work full time to staff up another startup called Stoke Inc. My wife and I enjoyed living like students across the street from Stanford in Palo Alto.

I have a new view of life after nearly ending it last winter in a snowmobile accident.
