  • President

    Sustainable Strength-based Solutions

    (Management Consulting industry)

    November 2008 — Present (3 months)

    Dr. McNichols is an Organization Development consultant who specializes in facilitating change by promoting a strength based and appreciative organizational culture that capitalizes on individual, team, and generational strengths. The premise is that every organization is unique due to the strengths and skills of its people. A strengths based organization erodes communication barriers and fosters a sustainable high-performance culture in which diversity is recognized and celebrated as an essential component of competitive advantage.

    Services offered include presentations, speeches, and training designed to help organization's capitalize on their unique culture and core competencies realized through the strengths and skills of their employees.

    In a strength-centered organization employees focus not on differences between individuals but on recognizing the unique strengths inherent in each individual regardless of generation or gender.
