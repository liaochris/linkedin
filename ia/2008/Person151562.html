Brandon Piddington’s Summary

Since 1992 my experience includes employment as a hardware, software and network consultant for a well known retail chain, webmaster/designer, graphic designer, technical writer and project manager for various companies (start-up, small and mid-level). Since 2002 I have been instrumental in the conception, design and implementation of specialized content management systems that enable online news publishers to syndicate and monetize their content using custom co-branded solutions. As traditional print media continues to lose readership, it's becoming increasingly important for news agencies to transition from their usual revenue streams to new media.

The benefit I bring to clients/employers is that I'm able to undertake projects acting in a multi-functional manner; taking on the role of more than one job description, delivering a bottom-line cost benefit; my skill-set includes everything from developing fully functional websites, basic server admin, content writing, design, layout, function and various other aspects of the web; marketing and branding.

My goal is always the same for anything I do; take on interesting and challenging projects and help take them to the next level. When the goal has been met, I regroup and do it again.

Brandon Piddington’s Specialties:

My specialty revolves around creativity, which I inject into most facets of my work; it’s this creativity that pushes otherwise basic task’s into successful projects. Another specialty is an industry expertise; I have undertaken numerous projects in the Financial, Investor Relations and Investor Awareness arena where I have been responsible for conceiving, designing and implementing programs and campaigns, primarily for small and mid-size publicly traded companies.
