Emma McComb’s Summary

I currently hold the position of IP Associate at IMBcom and in this role I am involved with the analysis of opportunities for commercialisation arising from research at the Institute for Molecular Bioscience (IMB) and I assist in all aspects of patent filing and prosecution. To achieve this, I work closely with the IMB scientists to identify potential opportunities and I recommend and pursue processes for their development. I have a Medicinal Chemistry (Hons) degree from the University of Wollongong and a PhD from the University of Queensland. My PhD thesis studied the structural and functional characterisation of alpha-conotoxins and was a multi-disciplinary project across a number of scientific areas. I am also currently studying towards a Masters of Industrial Property law at the University of Technology in Sydney.

Emma McComb’s Specialties:

My scientific research experience is in the areas of medicinal chemistry, structural biology, physiology and pharmacology and biochemistry.
