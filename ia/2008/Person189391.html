Tim Ryan’s Summary

Just listed within The New Orleans 100 (see #66): http://www.alldaybuffet.org/neworleans100/

Recently posted video on post-Katrina New Orleans:
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qeNARLH7MFI

While executive director for the New Orleans Video Access Center (NOVAC), Tim founded the Louisiana Film Crew Training Program (LFCTP) which offers training and job placement within Louisiana’s film and television industry. In 2007, he also developed a business relationship with Apple Computer to create the only adult Apple Authorized Training Center in the state of Louisiana. In 2006, he created the NEA-funded Digital Filmmaking Institute (DFI). In addition, he has instructed workshops on Internet video and digital filmmaking. Through his expertise in Internet video production and marketing techniques, Tim positioned NOVAC as You Tube’s fifth most viewed non-profit in the WORLD! He also generated over $1 million in funding for NOVAC from a variety of businesses and foundations including: National Endowment for the Arts (NEA), Louisiana Recovery Authority (CDBG), HUD, Arts Council of New Orleans, Mary Freeman Wisdom Foundation, RosaMary Foundation, Betty Wisdom Foundation, Keller Family Foundation, Greater New Orleans Foundation, Newman’s Own Foundation, New Orleans Jazz and Heritage Foundation, Pearson Foundation, Louisiana Association of Non-profits (LANO), Andy Warhol Foundation and the Sweet Art New York Katrina Fund.

Tim has worked as an independent media producer for clients including CBS – Amazing Race, TLC – What Not To Wear, BBC-New York, RW Advertising, Roche Pharmaceuticals, The Oprah Winfrey Show, Rebuilding Communities, The Historic New Orleans Collection, New Orleans Outreach, and The National Alliance of Public Charter Schools. He has also worked in media, marketing, business consulting, and publishing for companies including The Tribune Company, United Airlines, Accenture (Andersen Consulting), and AllAdvantage.com, among others.

Tim Ryan’s Specialties:

Production planning and budgeting; Internet marketing and PR; Internet video production, Internet Media Producer, Production Coordination and Management, Digital Video Camera Operator, NLE Editing with Final Cut Studio, Business Development, Grant Writing, Digital Filmmaking Curriculum Development, Film and Television Industry Workforce Development, Quick Books Pro, Digital Filmmaking equipment procurement.
