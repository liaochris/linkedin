Mike Keough’s Summary

A successful professional executive who has led the successful development and execution of all corporate planning for technology leaders in conferencing, data communications, networking, systems, and transaction processing businesses.

Professional responsibilities have included leading corporate organizations, development and execution of overall corporate strategies, complete P&L responsibility for rapidly growing high technology organizations, participating as a board member, and working closely with investors and shareholders.

Other key areas include setting budgetary direction, strategic planning, and tactical implementation of all corporate activities focused on meeting specific operating plan objectives.

My current objective is to obtain a CEO, or senior sales management position, with a successful organization that provides reward commensurate to responsibility and achievement.
