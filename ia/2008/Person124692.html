Matt Carolan’s Summary

Versatile, highly skilled communications professional and integrated marketer with extensive technology industry, journalism and education backgrounds.

Well-read and experienced in multiple aspects of interactive communications, including messaging, new media, search engine marketing and audio/video production.

Complementing those efforts and balancing my "creative" personality are learnings from and ongoing training in data-driven and systematic approaches such as Six Sigma, Project Management and Web Analytics, which help to: clearly define problems to be solved; define strategy and tactics; and take the emotion out of decision making.

Humanities instructor at two Long Island colleges, and lifelong learner, with particular focus on the humanities, philosophy of science and technology, management theory and personal effectiveness.
