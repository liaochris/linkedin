Dave Killebrew’s Summary

Obtained a double major in Finance and Real Estate from California State University of Northridge in 1991. I am the Executive Vice President for Slingshot Products, LLC., a company specifically designed to help you sell your product to prominent retail, catalog and online companies. I am also a Senior Loan Officer specializing in Commercial Lending for tiers 1-4 and Hard Money situations. I have sixteen years experience as a Senior Buyer, Product Manager and Sourcing Manager. Skills include domestic and international sourcing, negotiating, implementing and developing product assortments for Mervyn’s, Brookstone, Discovery Channel Store, Home Express, RobinsonsMay and Land Home Financial Services. I have extensive experience partnering with online and advertising departments to help build my overall business. Some of my areas of experience include: Textiles, Domestics, Hardlines, Indoor and Outdoor Furniture, Decorative Accessories, Specialty Electronics, Outdoor Tools and Accessories, Astronomy, and Home Comfort Products (such as heaters, fans and air purification). Extensive experience in overseas and domestic product sourcing from tooling to completion.
