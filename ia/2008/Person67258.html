Belinda Van Sickle’s Summary

Writer and graphic designer with eleven years' experience making video game manuals, packaging and sales materials.

Belinda Van Sickle’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, design and layout of print materials for the video game industry such as manuals, packaging, sell sheets and collateral. My company also provides game writing, localizations and print buying.

  • President

    GameDocs, Inc.

    (Public Company; 1-10 employees; Computer Games industry)

    November 2005 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    President of GameDocs, a video game industry manual and packaging production company. With both large and small clients, GameDocs provides the highest level of experience at the most affordable price for game documentation and packaging in the industry. The company also designs new business pitch documents and other B-to-B materials.

  • Awards Committee/Awards Judge

    MI6-Game Marketing

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Games industry)

    April 2006 — November 2007 (1 year 8 months)

    I was an awards judge and a member of the awards committee for the MI6 Conference. MI6 is geared toward marketing and advertising for the video game industry. The annual conference has speakers and workshops on video game marketing. There is also an awards presentation for the best game advertising and marketing materials including packaging and manuals; online, print and broadcast advertising.

  • Part-Time Faculty

    (Educational Institution; Computer Games industry)

    December 2005 — September 2006 (10 months)

    I taught Portfolio Design to graduating seniors and Interface Design to first quarter seniors in the Game Art & Design B.A. program. The classes helped students create an online game interface or portfolio and supporting print materials.

  • Manager, Documentation & Quality Assurance

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    July 1999 — November 2005 (6 years 5 months)

    I managed the Documentation department at an advertising agency that specialized in video game marketing. I was in charge of project management for all video game manuals in the shop and did writing, editing, design and layout.

  • Documentation Writer/Production Artist
