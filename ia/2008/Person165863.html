Rupa Ranganathan’s Summary

Rupa Ranganathan is a Strategic Advisor who works with clients focusing on Multicultural and Healthcare business strategies. A Public Relations professional by training, she has provided executive intelligence through conferences and workshops for senior decision-makers during her long and successful tenure at Strategic Research Institute, a New York City based conference firm. Her specialty has been the emerging “Multicultural Economy” in the United States. Between 1998 and 2007, she has led the Multicultural practice for Strategic Research Institute as Ethnic Strategist. During this time, she has worked closely with thought-leaders, and highly specialized ethno-cultural marketing and communications gurus that have helped Fortune 1000 companies to unlock the growing influence of Hispanic, Asian-American, African-American, urban and other niche segments.

At the international level, she was advisor to the Apollo Hospitals Group and the Confederation of Indian Industry’s 4th Annual Health Summit, in New Delhi, in November 2007.

She is also a professional moderator of focus groups and has worked with financial services, entertainment, healthcare and other groups wanting to engage the emerging South Asian consumers in the United States and Canada.

Between 1997 and 1998, she was working as a producer with Global Business Research, a conference company, where she was responsible for 10 conferences on Nutraceuticals, Anti-Aging skincare, direct-to-consumer advertising and other healthcare topics.


A Literacy Volunteer, in Morris county, New Jersey, she conducts a course at the local library, to help immigrants with their Citizenship test preparation and tutors adult immigrant students in the community.
