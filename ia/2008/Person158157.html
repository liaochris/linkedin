Rebecca Murtagh’s Summary

A recognized expert in website strategy, web site performance, Internet marketing and promotion, Rebecca Murtagh is the Chief Strategist and Founder of Karner Blue Marketing, LLC. She leverages over 20 years of traditional marketing experience, complimented by extensive e-business expertise developed over the past 12 years to develop Karner Blue Marketing's proprietary SEO / SEM proprietarty methodology.

Karner Blue Marketing's "white hat" search engine optimization and internet marketing methods are ethical, as to never jeopardize our client's long-term ability to rank high on Google, Yahoo and other search engines. Most importantly, the Karner Blue Marketing methods are PROVEN to produce measurable results...100% of the time!

Ms. Murtagh has long been considered a respected expert in her field. Having educated business leaders, consulted clients and delivered seminars and lectures about the power of Internet Marketing since 1998, Rebecca quickly wins the confidence of her audiences with her depth of knowledge.
