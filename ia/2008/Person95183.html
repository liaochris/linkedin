John Matthews’s Summary

johnmatthews[at]comscient[dot]com
www.johnrmatthews.com
www.insightsforinnovation.com

John Matthews is the Owner/President of Comscient Group. John helps his clients make high stakes decisions, to become better decision makers, to be more innovative, and to connect with their markets and customers in new and innovative and effective ways. John combines insightful research and analysis consulting with in-person facilitative consulting to support effective high stakes decision making, achieve agreement between decision stakeholders with differing agendas, collaboratively explore future market scenarios, and to stimulate innovative collaborative and also critical thinking among stakeholders and within oeer groups and peer networks.

In 1991 while working for financial markets trading room consultants INFACT Ltd John founded the European software market intelligence and software management consultancy INFACT Research. In 1992 John co-led the management buyout of INFACT Research from INFACT Ltd to create INFACT Research Ltd. He led INFACT Research until 2003 building it into an established brand in the European software market research and software management consultancy marketplace. While leading INFACT Research, John personally consulted to some of the largest and best known brand names in strategy consulting, systems integration, and in software venture capital and private equity.

From 1984 to 1986, while with Artificial Intelligence Ltd, John collaborated with teams at Xerox Palo Alto Research Center (Xerox PARC) on artificial intelligence-based decision support systems and hypertext systems. In 1988 while with management consultants Inforem, John consulted to Citicorp Investment Bank Ltd (now Citigroup) to develop rules-based decision support systems for a new type of fund being created which became known as a Structured Investment Vehicle (SIV) kick starting a multi billion dollar financial market.
