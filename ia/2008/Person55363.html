W Dustin Goldstein’s Summary

Dustin Goldstein leads a team of Treasury Management sales officers responsible for selling, marketing and delivering Fifth Third Bank's domestic cash and treasury management capability to its middle market corporate client base located in the Greater Chicago area. He is a frequent speaker at Treasury Management related conferences and seminars. Dustin holds his Certified Treasury Professional (CTP) designation since 1998 and the Certificate in International Cash Management (Cert ICM) since 2001. He also speaks fluent Spanish.
