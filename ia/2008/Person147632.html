Paul Farrer (paul@pfj.co.uk)’s Summary

Established pfj in 1992.
With offices in London, Manchester and a majority interest in a Sydney based media recruiter, ICUR, pfj is developing a global network of leading specialist recruitment companies.
With around 100 employees pfj aims to have a physical presence on four continents within the next 3 years.
pfj is best known for media recruitment where we have specialist teams recruiting for media sales, media agency, new media and digital, editorial, conference, exhibitions and events. In addition we also have specialist teams servicing intellectual property, digital IT, corporate sales, graduate recruitment and recruitment to recruitment

We are particularly proud of our status as a Sunday Times Top 100 Company to Work for 2005, 2006, 2007 & 2008, after all we spend most of our waking hours at work so it might as well be fun.
