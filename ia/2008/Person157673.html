Vivek Hutheesing’s Summary

My goal is to help rBlock become a world-class enterprise in the eyes of its customers, employees, partners and investors.

rBlock has ambitious plans, and I believe my career has prepared me well to help the Company achieve them. My professional experience spans finance, marketing and now general management. Before B-school, i just dabbled in different areas as I never felt a strong connection to whatever paid the bills in those days. Following B-school, how I earned a living became much more important to me, as I had a family to support, but the strong connection was still not there. I found my calling with rBlock, and happily, am now quite certain about what I'll be doing for the rest of my career. Here's a quick flash-back on my post B-school career.

1) Investment banker (Media & Ent.) with Bear, Stearns (New York)
2) Financial advisor to private companies raising capital (SF Bay Area)
3) VC raising a hybrid venture / buyout fund (Bi-Coastal)
4) Consumer Internet entrepreneur, running rBlock (SF Bay Area)

rBlock is going to help every resident improve their life in the next few years, so please keep us in mind.
