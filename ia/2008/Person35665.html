Chris McGovern’s Summary

As president of Emerging Marketing, Chris McGovern oversees operations of this full service marketing, branding and interactive agency. His primary responsibilities include developing new business, strategy, directing creative and serving as chief consultant for the agency.

Chris has been recognized both locally and nationally for his outstanding accomplishments with his company being cited as the 4th Fastest Emerging company in Columbus, Ohio as stated by Business First (newspaper). They were a top ten finalist in the Small Business Administration sponsored “Small Business Person of the Year” Awards, and nationally as one of 23 Ohio businesses selected as a finalist for the “Blue Chip Enterprise Initiative” Awards. As well as 10 years in the top 20 agencies in Columbus, Ohio.

He has served as Community Relations Chair and Board of Trustee for the Nobel Peace Prize nominated International Visitors Council.

Chris was Chairmen of the Board for the first Contemporary African Art Gallery, Kiaca, founded by Artist Talle Bamazi. He is the Past President of FirstLink. He also served the Upper Arlington Civic Association for 9 years.

Chris has been a speaker for the Society of Marketing Professionals, Professional Insurance Marketing Association, American Marketing Association, Small Business Administration, Advertising Federation, Direct Marketing Association, Keynote speaker for the U.S. Postal Customer Council as well as moderator for one of only 6 national Department of State Town Meetings on West Asia.

Chris McGovern’s Specialties:

Our clients tell us we do everything from the Basics to the BIG idea. Emerging Marketing develops, designs, and delivers Accountable Marketing programs with measurable results. We help businesses generate leads, create sales, retain customers, strengthen customer relations, and increase brand awareness while providing clients with a higher return on investment.
