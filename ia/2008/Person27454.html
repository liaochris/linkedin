Brian Iriye’s Summary

Managing partner of the largest maternal-fetal medicine practice in Nevada. Board certified in maternal-fetal medicine as well as obstetrics and gynecology. Interest in ultraound, complex medical problems in pregnancy (hypertension, diabetes, etc.), prenatal diagnosis. Currently active in medical research with projects involving ultrasound, fetal heart rate monitoring, preterm birth. Other interests in preconception care and fetal programming. President of the Nevada Perinatal Foundation a 501c3 corporation interested in research and education in Nevada. Five time director of the High-Risk Pregnancy Solutions Conference in Las Vegas, Nevada. Multiple publications from 1993-2007 with the most recent in Journal of Ultrasound Medicine Dec. 2007 titled: Prenatal Detection of Congenital Heart Disease in Southern Nevada: The Need for Universal Fetal Cardiac Evaluation. Multiple presentations and lectures. Most recently lectured for March of Dimes Healthy Babies Healthy Families Conference, Las Vegas, NV Nov. 2007 and 2007 Perinatal Health Seminar, Billings MT, Nov. 2007. Member of the Planning Committee for The World Symposium of Perinatal Medicine, San Francisco, CA Oct. 2007.

Brian Iriye’s Specialties:

Obstetrical ultrasound, clinical high risk obstetrical care, management of a single specialty medical practice.
