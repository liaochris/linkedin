Lara Hanson’s Summary

The opportunity to intern at Sports Illustrated, HBO, and The Creative Coalition, my first few years in college enabled me to touch many facets of business at an early age.

Upon entering my junior year at Fordham University, and after having been deeply affected by the events of September 11th the year prior, I was compelled on a mission to unify college students from around the world with hopes of peace. This mission led me to develop The College Voice www.thecollegevoice.com, an international multi-media website where students could come and post the important news from their regions of the world, and discuss these topical events in an open forum. Under the tutelage and angel backing of a Fordham Graduate, this site was launched in 2002.

At The College Voice, I worked alongside friends, professors, and freshman interns to not only create The College Voice, but to hold the first NYC College Political Debate between the Democrats and Republicans of Columbia, NYU, and Fordham University. The College Voice was revamped to incorporate Web2.0 components in 2006, and since has been left completely open as a means for students to post and debate whatever they choose.

In 2007, I began with MTVu, managing new product developments for College Publisher, an online platform which assists university print publications enter the world of online publishing.

My goals for the future are to continue with product development in the digital space, focused in the arenas of college journalism, international education, entreprenuership, as well as domestic and foregin policies.
