Joey Stephens’s Summary

Joey Stephens is a senior SAP Basis Administrator with extensive experience with SAP R/3 versions 3.1h through 4.6c and ERP Central Component 6.0. His Database Administration expertise centers around Microsoft SQL Server, with a solid background in Oracle 7.3.4 and 8i on the Microsoft Windows platform. In his 9 years of SAP Basis Administration experience, he has been an integral part of backup and recovery planning, upgrade testing and implementation, support pack application, change and transport management, performance tuning, Archiving, QA system refreshing, and new system implementation. Some of the other products he has worked closely with are Adobe Document Services, BSI Tax Factory, Digital InfoLife, Symantec BackupExec, and HP StorageWorks EVA.
