Steve Cunningham’s Summary

Hi! I'm a lifelong entrepreneur who started his first business while in the 4th grade. Seriously. Regrettably, that didn't work out. I've had stints as a hockey player, musician, and as an insurance defence litigator. Thankfully, those didn't work out either. I now run a digital marketing and innovation agency, and love every minute of it.

I'm here, like most of you, to expand my network and to see if I can't help a few people out along the way.

I am seeking out, and will accept invitations from, people that share a passion for:

- growing their business

- digital marketing

- innovation.

One caveat: I will NOT accept your invitation if you don't take the time to tell me how you fit the criteria above. Why? Because I'm not here to create an audience, I'm here to connect with real people.

I believe that:

- we are living in incredibly exciting times;

- that recess should be renamed "business 101"; and

- you never know until you try.

Feel free to connect with me on Twitter as well (www.twitter.com/stevecunningham)
