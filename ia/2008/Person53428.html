Don Shirley’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

Director/VP Marketing – Product Management - Technology Commercialization

Veteran technology marketing executive with 24 years of experience leading the commercial deployment of new products, services and partnerships. A track record of creating new revenue streams by employing strategic alliances, integrated marketing programs, and e-Business strategies to launch offerings including online sales channels, wireless communications services, enterprise software applications, and B2B IT solutions.

SELECT ACCOMPLISHMENTS

- Successful Fundraiser and Start-up Founder
Founded, built and successfully sold venture-backed spin-out from Ericsson called Brightpod ASP, a Mobile Internet Application Service Provider funded by Ericsson and five venture capital firms. Led product development, marketing and business expansion efforts, increasing revenues until successfully selling company to Inphonic (NASDAQ: INPC), for significant return to investors.

- Honored as a leading Entrepreneur and Business Growth Specialist
Frequent keynote speaker at entrepreneurial events. Lecturer at Kenan-Flagler Business School Executive MBA program, UNC Chapel Hill, and Fuqua Business School Entrepreneurship program, Duke University.

- Accomplished Marketer and Global Business Developer
Developed, implemented and managed partnering programs for companies delivering multi-vendor product solutions to telecom carriers, large enterprises and global service providers throughout the Americas, resulting in $500 million in contracts over last 10 years.

- Decorated Officer in US Army Special Operations
First Communication-Electronics Staff Officer (CESO) for covert operations aviation in the US Army. Planned and executed global network deployments for anti-terrorism and drug interdiction missions including 1989 - “Operation Just Cause”, resulting in the capture of Manuel Noriega.

Don Shirley’s Specialties:

Company and Product Identity creation and promotion. Bringing products and services to market. Packaging, pricing and promoting new products and services.
