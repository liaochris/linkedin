Michael Brothers’s Summary

VoIP Engineering Director with over twelve years of experience in systems architecture, engineering, and software development. Manages the design and deployment of VoIP and unified messaging systems including architecture, evaluation, and recommendations related to purchasing and installing hardware and software to support a national E9-1-1 VoIP offering. Designs and manages VoIP infrastructure components such as application servers, session border controllers, load balancers, media gateways, and media conferencing servers. Demonstrated expertise in drafting and delivering presentations, request for proposals and other customer facing sales materials.
