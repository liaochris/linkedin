Steve Breibart’s Summary

A management consultant specialising in internal communication, culture change and corporate values, Steve has deployed his skills within many types of organisations both large and small, across many industry sectors. He works with all levels in organisations from the shop floor to Board Directors.

Most recently he has co-developed "The Performance Barometer", helping organisations to develop a performance culture within their people.

Steve leads, develops and works within teams, developing innovative metaphors for helping organisations to identify and overcome challenges. As an executive coach, Steve works with senior people to help them achieve their full potential. He co-founded and continues to run The Coaching Foundation, a national group for executive and life coaches and is a member of the Association for Coaching.

Steve’s first career was in information technology where he worked initially for user organisations in technical rôles, ranging from Operator to Programmer, Systems Analyst, Project Manager and IT Manager. Later on, he worked for one of the largest IT suppliers holding positions as Project Manager, Bid Manager and Account Consultant.

Steve was appointed to the Information Systems Examinations Board Panel for their Consulting Skills qualification at its inception in 2001. He also teaches regularly at the London Management Centre.
