Chris Mitchell’s Summary

Chris Mitchell has managed diverse product development programs at Microsoft. He is currently working on a start-up team building a v1 product. For the last 5 years he worked on core networking technologies within Windows Networking. While in Networking Chris's responsibilities included management of IPv6, IPsec, TCP/IP, High speed networking Windows Firewall and Windows Filtering platform Program Management activities. Chris's team was responsible for the design, development and deployment of these areas within government, corporate and consumer environments. Previously, Chris worked on various products and services within the Microsoft Network (MSN) for 7 years. Before joining Microsoft, Chris worked in the telecommunications industry providing customer premise equipment within the greater Seattle area.
During his first 12+ years at Microsoft Chris has been involved in a variety of projects and products including multi-million dollar network deployments and the initial product architecture, design and development of Microsoft Passport. He worked on MSN Messenger growing the active usage of the service tenfold and implementing operational standards and design improvements that increased service uptime.
