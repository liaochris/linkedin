Sameer Prakash’s Summary

A business consultant from a CRM IT background, I have moved into Business Intelligence consulting; applying my techinical knowledge to guide and propose solutions to the business.

My career so far has given me the unique experience of working closely on both business and technical sides of enterprise scale solutions.

I understand the business drivers (desired capabilities) and the technical (solution) possibilities. I also appreciate the technical challenges faced by a typical IT setup to deliver these the business needs with the (Business') desired flexibility within a IT supported framework.

I pride myself in the ability to interpret the core principles underlying the business requirements and discuss possible solutions with the technical architects within current IT constraints. I enjoy the challenge of engaging in brainstorming sessions to explore innovative out of the box solutions.

Upon completion of the evaluation, I propose possible solutions which meets the requirements to the business stakeholders. I highlight strengths, weaknesses and limitations of the final solution options and the associated risks & costs to help them make an informed choice.

In the delivery phase, I manage/assist delivery of end to end implementations, guiding & shepherding the solution through various hurdles and challenges along the way to deliver to agreed timescales and costs.

I see myself continuing to build in this experience in the near future; meeting new people, clients, visiting new places, exploring creative ideas & overcoming new challenges ... in the process.

I am part of Ardent Business Consulting Ltd (UK).
