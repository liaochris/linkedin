Greg Levine’s Summary

I am an experienced interactive and technology professional, with more than 12 years of success in application-web development. Passionate about bridging the gap where creative meets technology, managing large development groups and meeting objectives with tangible results.

My past work has touched Fortune 100 companies (Intel, HP, Columbia Sportswear, Microsoft, MSN) to companies leading the cutting edge of services and offerings (Amazon.com, Paramount Studios, Loudeye Technologies, Universal Music Group).

I have served in senior positions for the last 7-8 years managing internal and external development efforts from strategic planning to execution. Client communication, client presentations, documentation, team and project leadership are additional facets of my skill-set.

I am currently employed with a unique virtual world start-up based in San Francisco serving as a senior product manager. Our product focuses on social interactions predicated on like interests such as music.

Greg Levine’s Specialties:

Interactive development, user centric design, problem solving, technical documentation, web-software standards, ui expertise, team leadership, senior leadership..
