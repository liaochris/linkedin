Brian Contos’s Summary

I have over twelve years of tactical and strategic industry experience focused on security and compliance for global 2,000 and government customers. As a published author and frequent public speaker I have developed a reputation as an expert in the security space. I am frequently asked to interact with analysts and the media to share my business acumen. I have been interviewed extensively and written articles for industry and mainstream publications ranging from Forbes and The London Times to Computerworld and the Sarbanes-Oxley Compliance Journal.

Throughout my career I have worked in leadership roles across various disciplines including sales, solutions marketing, evangelism, consulting and IT. Much of my career has been spent traveling throughout the world working with business leaders to help increase the security posture of some of the world’s most sensitive environments.

In this capacity I have assisted customers with various strategies: aligning security with business directives, defining return on security investment, measuring, managing, and mitigating risk, leveraging security as a market differentiator, building global security infrastructures as well as establishing solutions surrounding compliance, insider threat, incident prevention, detection, and management.
