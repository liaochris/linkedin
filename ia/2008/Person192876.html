Timothy Roberts (ceosavvy)’s Summary

In 1995, I founded Savvis Communications Corp. (NASDAQ: SVVS), a global inter-networking services provider. I came up with the idea and design for the first multi-homed ATM meshed national DS-3 "PNAP" Network. In 1997, I went on to found Intira Corporation, a pioneer and leader in Netsourcing, where I served as CEO and CTO, again coming up with the idea and design for Netsourcing. Both Savvis, which went public in February 2000, and Intira, originated from my entrepreneurial ideas and were grown into multimillion-dollar companies. Currently I have been working to help others grow there companies being compensated to provide business advice, leadership and sales and marketing expertise. I also own companies which are merchandising products online, reducing the costs of the old bricks and mortar operations and providing savings to end users while increasing the profit margins to us at the same time.

Proven business leader with ability to grow businesses from idea stage to fully operational status.

Accustomed to start-up environment and known for excelling in team-building, mentorship and development.

Proven ability to managed 200+ employees
Highly motivated problem-solver with talent for seeing the "big picture."

Extensive experience in organizational structuring, developing and executing budget and operating plans, marketing and designing collateral materials, product development and directing sales initiatives.

Additional experience in private placement financing, including vendor and venture capital deals, corporate structuring.

Tax and GAAP Accounting; Ability to read complex financial statements.

Financial forecasting, financing and cash flow planning.

Expert in marketing development and strategizing.

Proven Leader in Sales.

Extensive Legal expertise; securities, corporate structure, liens, private placement memorandums (PPM's).
