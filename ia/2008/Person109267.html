Kshitiz Anand’s Summary

I am interested in User Experience Research primarily.

My views on design are from a broader perspective. I aim to look at it from a user, technology and art perspective equally, with design being the center of the triangle.It is my understanding that this viewpoint on design would lead to creating of better experiences.
Of late my research interest has been in analysing User Experience in emerging markets, and trans-disciplinary research in photography and User Experience.
I am a designer and a photographer with over two years of industrial experience as an Interaction Designer.

Kshitiz Anand’s Specialties:

User Centered Design Process, Ethnography, Focus Groups, Heuristic Evaluation, Contextual Inquiry, Remote Usability, Card sorting, Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Flash, MS Office suite, HTML, CSS, ActionScript.
