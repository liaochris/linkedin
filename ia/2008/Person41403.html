Carsten Schmidt’s Summary

Librettist and speaker of the audio production "Die Königin der Abruzzen" in 2008.
Performer of "The Show", a musical revue on the occasion of the 550th anniversary of the excellence university Freiburg in 2007 (amongst others: "Maria" from "West Side Story").
Librettist, stage director and actor of the "SongPlay 'East Side Story'" in 2004 and librettist, stage director and actor of the "amusical 'Vivat Hamlet!'"in 2001, both at the university of music Freiburg.
Soloist (tenor) and part-conductor of the mass "Message" by Sven Hinz in 2003 (university of music Freiburg).
Singer, actor and stage assistance of "Meet the beat, a revue of the 1960's" in 2003 (coproduction of the university of music Freiburg and the college of education Freiburg).
Assistant director of the scenic production of "Hans Zender: Schuberts 'Winterreise'" by the Young Opera Company Freiburg (YOC) in 2002, jury member of the auditions for the production of John Adams' Song Play "I was looking at the ceiling and then I saw the sky" by YOC in 2004.
Member (tenor) of the famous and international performing choir "Camerata Vocale Freiburg" (2001 - now).
Member (tenor) of the a cappella-group "halbacht" (2003 - now).
Several experiences as anchorman.
Hobby: to write poetry.

Carsten Schmidt’s Specialties:

As part of my studies in education science I'm very interested in reform pedagogics. Thus I read most of the books of Rebeca Wild and took part in a workshop by Rebeca and Mauricio Wild. Other important authors for me are for example: Alexander Neill ("Summerhill"), David Gribble, Olivier Keller, Joseph Chilton Pearce, Lienhard Valentin, O. Fred Donaldson, Emmi Pikler, Magda Gerber, Maria Montessori, Jean Piaget, Humberto Maturana, John Holt and so on.
