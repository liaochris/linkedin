  • Research Assistant

    Tennessee technological University

    (Educational Institution; 5001-10,000 employees; Chemicals industry)

    January 2001 — May 2002 (1 year 5 months)

    During my stay at TTU, I assisted in an industrial process development of an alternate polymerization route for a high temperature polymer. As a part of this research I,

    5. Characterized the polymer from the trial run using Thermo Gravimetric Analysis (TGA) and Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM).
