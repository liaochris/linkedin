sandro giacchetti’s Summary

After having spent more than thirteen years in commercial banking within a leading Italian bank, I know how to manage business-to-business and private customers. From years spent in different Italian industrial regions I took an astounding knowledge of the local SME and still holds contacts with a number of manufacturers and suppliers.
After this experience I worked as consultant for a major Italian bank with the mandate to set up the sales force. Afterwards I decided to start a market research company with two associates. Three years later I started an other company specialized in top and middle management personnel training, successfully run for 8 years. I have been working in the past seven years, before as manager for an important service company in Luxemburg. After years spent developing companies, H.R. and growing business, now I set up my own service company in Italy, the All Lexicon srl, based in Rome.
