Patrick O'Connor’s Summary

My expertise is focused in developing and managing complex high revenue strategic deals, typically valued in the 100s of millions, to Telecommunications Carriers, high profile New Economy companies, and Fortune 500 corporations in Silicon Valley and other targeted markets.
