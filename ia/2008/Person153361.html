Prasoon Ranjan’s Summary

Prasoon currently works in the Industry Strategy and Insight team at Oracle Corporation based out of UK and focussing/responsible for Communication, Media and Utilities vertical in EMEA. He is also a member of the Society of Industry Leaders specialising in the IT industry with focus in the outsourcing area.

Prasoon has over 8 years of work experience in the CRM industry as a Product Developer, Consultant and Solution Architect/Manager working at world leading organisations such as BaaN R&D (now Invensys), Deloitte, Infosys and Siebel. Prasoon's CRM experience spans across strategy (business and IT), outsourcing, process and technology.

Prasoon has an MBA from INSEAD. While at INSEAD Prasoon won the prestigious David Hall Trophy at the European Case Challenge competition held at Said Business School, Oxford University and conducted by European Case Clearing House in 2005.

Prasoon Ranjan’s Specialties:

Highly experienced in the IT industry with knowledge of ERP, CRM (8 yrs), ERM SCM and Outsourcing (managed a project as an Indian expat in Australia) mainly in the Telco & Media industry and in CPG and Public Sector.

Post M&A integration The last 4 companies I have worked for were either acquired or went through rebranding. Through these changes, I have seen various issues companies face during an M&A and gained insights into the area of Post-M&A integration.
