Rodney Liber’s Summary

Rodney’s focus is to bring the latest in Internet and Web Development practices as well as his extensive SEO experience to The Turn Group, its Clients and all of his Partners. With over 10 years of technical experience in Hardware, Software, Networking as well as Research and Development, Rodney brings several of many key components that make up the The Turn Group Team. Within the Technology world, Rodney’s experience has been geared towards customer satisfaction, customer education and consumer experience.

He does this by sculpting his talent in Website Design, Development, e-commerce solutions, innovative technology and project management providing clients of The Turn Group with the tools necessary to bring their project from concept to reality in a process that guaranties success every time.

Rodney says the awesome thing about technology is that it allows a level of self expression that empowers people with great potential to be creative by bringing to life ideas and concepts in a way that portrays one’s self at a higher level. Rodney is an “Artist of Technology” in that he takes an idea and builds the foundation upon which it sits molding creative thinking around the technology it takes to produce the work of art. Rodney does this not only by architecting projects but by sculpting client needs through hard work and commitment.

Rodney believes the meaning of The Turn Group and its success is a direct result of knowing how to market a brand and having the right people representing that brand. “We are approaching a new age of synthesis. Knowledge cannot be merely a degree or a skill. It demands a broader vision, capabilities in critical thinking and logical deduction without which we cannot have constructive progress.” We take this belief to the client. Reputation is very important to us because our reputation is not built on what we say we will do but by achieving successfully the goals set before us by our clients.
