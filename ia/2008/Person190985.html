Tom Hulsebosch’s Summary

I’m a 20 year veteran of the wireless telecommunication industry excelling in developing new products and markets. I take a hands-on approach to leadership and have a very diverse back ground through my time at EarthLink, Motorola and Strategy 2 Solution. Began wireless career at Motorola as a development engineer in the cellular infrastructure division and touched many different products and services and operated in a variety of different rolls during 19 years at Motorola. Experience at Motorola ranged from engineering and product management in the cellular infrastructure division, to business development and sales in the Internet Software and Content Group, and finally leading Motorola’s sales, marketing, and product strategy efforts in the wireless broadband space.

I led the sales effort for municipal broadband solutions to government, enterprise, and education customers at EarthLink.

At Strategy 2 Solution, LLC created a consultancy practice focusing on two areas:
i) Develop private and public wireless network solutions for municipalities, utilities, and service providers. ii) Create product strategies and sales distribution solutions for equipment vendors.

Tom Hulsebosch’s Specialties:

New business development, needs analysis for end customers, business models for the entire value chain (end user, service provider, equipment provider, etc.), pricing strategy, distribution strategy, indirect distribution, international sales, product management and product strategy, wireless broadband (WiFi, FWA, WiMAX), cellular technologies
