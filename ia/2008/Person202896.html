Zach Browne’s Summary

I have provided a brief overview of my 15 year professional career; I hope you find reading about it just as exciting as it was to live it.

I left the family construction business, Browne Construction, to start a technical sales career in 1998. After a successful career in sales and sales management, I left my sales career in 2003 to chisel my way into the mortgage world. I started a small mortgage company under a branch of Amerinet Mortgage located in Lake Dallas, TX.

After taking hundreds of loan applications with a majority of declinations, I felt starting a credit repair company next door would help my firm close about 25% more business. The idea was so successful that I opened a second office in the Fort Worth to keep up with the business. I successfully closed over 300 loans my first year with 10 employees with which I hired, trained, and managed. And did 2 million in sales with my credit repair company where I had an additional 16 employees. I handled all my own accounting, marketing, sales, technology, and human resources.

In 2006, I sold my business to one of my branch managers to start a web development firm. Since then I have been providing web development, hosting, and SEO services for small businesses in the Denton, TX area with some great successes. I am currently sharing an office with Fagan & Rawlings Capital Management where I have the contractual role of Technical Marketing Director as well as running my web business. I developed the website 1031junction.com where I have successfully driven over 500 hits a day and assisted in building a 100 million dollar pipeline with 1031 Exchanges in less than 6 months. I also conceptualized and built an online business directory where QI's (Qualified Intermediaries), CPA's, and Commercial Real Estate Brokers can communicate and share referrals.

I am finishing up my contract at Fagan & Rawlings and would love to discuss providing the same service for a new company.
