Dr. Kaz’s Summary

With 30+ years experience as an international speaker, author, coach and the world's first Brainologist I work in two main areas for speaking and coaching.

I have two main groups of people I work with. One is athletes - high school to Olympic and Professional - on the mental side of sport. I help them to train their brains to understand and accept winning. My athletes span the globe and have earned medals from the Olympics to world, national and state competitions. I speak at sporting clinics, hold bootcamps for ahtletes and work individually with colleigate teams on improving the mental side of their sport. I also developed a Brain Blend that is 99.7% accurate in identifying the skills and brain challenges athletes have and how to enhance their performances quickly and with minimal time input.

The second group i work with is business professionals who want to maximize their profitability while having a ball living their passions. Our company motto is "How to Out-Think, Out-Do and Out-Live your competition.

While helping business owners focus on time proven strategies coupled with unique and proven marketing strategies we create a common message that resonates from the inside out and attracts clients like a huge magnet.

One of my favorite quotes is that "I'd rather wear out than rust out."

My ideas will ignite your business, inform your mind and inspire your soul, to create a life you deserve and accomplishments the world deserves.
