Geordie Conyngham’s Summary

My name is Geordie Conyngham and my purpose is to help you get the sort of business you want. I've been delivering business improvement for many years in large and small businesses. I've done that through coaching, group facilitation, team building and leadership both locally and overseas.

My interest is in helpling other achieve the goals they always wanted to. This could be in business or professional life. When we work together the focus is on coaching for results - you set the agenda, you set the pace, you get the rewards and recognition you deserve - faster!

You can start experiencing benefits such as:

• Increased sales, cash flow and profitability
• Improved discipline so you get more for your effort and commitment
• Directed performance and productivity on meaningful activities
• Improved leadership, communication and relationships
• Moving your business from what it is now to what you want it to be
• Planning, organising and executing growth strategies
• Leveraging your knowledge and experience to create a sustainable competitive edge



As a senior manager working with government and corporate organizations, he has planned, coached and delivered many initiatives in Sales, Trade Marketing, Information Technology and Customer Service in Australia and Asia Pacific.

Geordie is active in the coaching industry and avid practitioner. He has appeared on local radio, he has been quoted in industry magazines and he regularly publishes material on the net. He is an accomplished speaker - presenting at industry forums eg: Las Vegas Leadership seminar & workshops eg: CIO Executive Board.

He helps people find better ways to grow their income, to increase profits and to gain new business so they can feel happier, self reliant and more successful.

Visit my website connect.to/coach for more information.
