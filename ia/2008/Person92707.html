Justin Montanino’s Summary

As a Producer, Editor and Writer for the last 8 years I’ve done and seen a lot, working for outlets such as ABC, CBS, Comedy Central, Cartoon Network, PBS and Spike TV. I’ve worked with Presidents & celebrities, covered the fields of lifestyle programming, music programming, breaking news, animation, live television, fashion & reality TV, interviewed the “who’s who” of Hollywood’s A-list (eliciting a few tears along the way), and once got bit by a tiger.

I’m equally comfortable around the pitch table developing a new series as I am producing in the field. I’ve led teams of up to 25 people, yet I also know the solitary joy of editing for weeks on end. I can think on my feet when doing unscripted television, or I can plot and plan and negotiate when working on more elaborate productions.

What you should know about how I work: I love ideas but I love execution even more. I’m always relaxed because I’m always prepared. I’m easy to get along with, and I love to work because I love what I do.

Justin Montanino’s Specialties:

Final Cut editing, Sapphire, Magic Bullet, Photoshop, Lexis-Nexis. Seasoned interviewer (sit-down, field, and red carpet). Formal management training. On-camera experience. Voiceover artist. Pop-culture guru and News junkie. Columnist for several publications.
