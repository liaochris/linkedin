Jason Scott’s Summary

Provision of short-term consulting services in the areas of capacity development, institutional assessment, programme cycle management, policy review, strategy development, trade and market expertise.

More than ten years international consulting experience in Sub-Saharan Africa for bi-lateral and multi-lateral donors including the World Bank, The European Union (EDF), DFID and the Commonwealth Development Corporation.

Globalisation offers unique opportunities for the growth and development of enterprises. My goal is to utilise my multicultural management experience in collaboration with stakeholders who are entering into cross cultural agreements as part of the expansion of thier supply chains and enabling an appreciation of the importace of cultural values in developing profitable business realtionships.
