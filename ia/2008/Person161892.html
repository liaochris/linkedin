Robert Bakker’s Summary

Willems Van der Baan Advocaten (Almere) is a commercial lawfirm, with a focus on company/corporate law and administrative law, building and project developement. Our lawyers are qualified and specilised in specific parts of commercial law, such as corporate law, building law, leasing, administrative law and employment law, so we can advise in all major legal issues.

Our clients require cost efficient, fast and personal service on any relevant legal issue. We offer high quality, cost efficient and fast service, with a strong emphasis on serving your business. We know your business and we believe in a strong an long lasting relationship with our clients.

Where required we have access to related lawfirms in Belgium, Germany and the UK, but also in other EC countries.

If you have any questions on commercial, corporate, building law or public law issues, do not hesitate to contact me or to make a referral to Willems Van der Baan Advocaten.
