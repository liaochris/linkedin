Manuraj Jain’s Summary

Manuraj is from IIT Kharagpur and has 4 years of engineering, operations and business development experience in Schlumberger ( US, UAE, India). After starting in the Middle East as a field engineer working for TOTAL, Maersk and BP, Manuraj built the Drilling and Measurement operations from the ground in the Indian Geomarket whilst spearheading the relationship with Reliance Industries.

Later, Manuraj moved to Intellecap and worked on strategy, research and capital structuring for MFIs in India. Apart from Valuation, financial modelling and due diligence he also led the Technology consulting initiatives at Intellecap.

Post his MBA from INSEAD Manuraj founded Vinculum Capital Partners which focuses on private equity syndication, research and capital advisory in India. Among others Vinculum is currently representing a healthcare company to raise equity capital and also engaged in a countrywide research project by UNIDO with the Indian Venture Capital Association. Manuraj is also an associate Partner with M2i Consulting on Debt and Equity syndication initiatives in the microfinance sector.
