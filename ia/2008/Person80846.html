Stavros Isaiadis’s Summary

Highly enthusiastic about Grid and Pervasive Computing, with a distinctive academic record so far. I have just finished my PhD on Pervasive Grid Systems and considering taking up a role in the industry to face new challenges!

Particularly interested and with a broad experience in all aspects of Grid Computing, Mobile and Pervasive Computing, Enterprise Distributed Systems, and Java Web Based Technologies (RMI, JMS, EJB, etc).

Always interested in discussing challenging and thriving opportunities!
