Sean Lynn’s Summary

As a Game Designer with over 11 years of experience in the industry, I’ve shipped over a dozen titles on platforms ranging from the PC, PS2, GameCube, Xbox, PSP and Xbox 360.

My hands-on experience runs the gamut of virtually all aspects of Game Design:

CONCEPTUAL DESIGN
- From the creation of numerous deep, immersive and believable game worlds, characters, creatures and weapons, to dreaming up gripping original intellectual properties and fleshing them out in detail.

SYSTEMS DESIGN
- Including play-testing and polishing intuitive control schemes, creating unique game-play mechanics and designing AI enemy behaviors, planning combat systems, weapons functionality, even random level generation.

LEVELS & MISSIONS
- The planning, consruction, lighting and texturing of 3D levels, including population and scripting, using 9 different proprietary tools, as well as Lightwave and some 3dsMAX. *Over the years, my work in this area has won kudos from management at all levels, including folks at Interplay, S.S.I. and Red Storm.

GAME BALANCING
- From visualizing and pre-testing weapons, armor and hit points, to adjusting damage, fire-rates and defining when enemies, weapons, and special powers are introduced to the player, so the end result is a smooth-playing whole and not a collection of random levels.

STORY & DIALOG
- Whether it's mission briefing text, in-game VO or a full-blown cinematic script, well-written characters and intriguing storylines can not only pull the player into the game world, they can also help create a memorable brand that will bring them back for more. *My work as a writer has gotten rave reviews from Disney, Cartoon Network, Wizards of the Coast, the makers of Tom Clancy titles and various other Hollywood and game industry veterans.
