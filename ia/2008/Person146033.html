Paolo Albertini’s Summary

Paolo's career started in the consulting field, first as a freelance consultant and then joining Arthur Andersen (Andersen Consulting) as Senior Consultant, working on both mainframe and PC environments (with different programming languages), developing special applications for selected customers, and also spending quite a significant amount of time as trainer for several italian firms (basic and advanced software courses).

The rest of his career has been mainly focused onto the Medical Devices field, from Logistics to Marketing and Sales, passing through QA. He was asked to develop new businesses from scratch, as well as relaunch mature declining brands/product lines, create and organise a dedicated sales force supported from a strong strategy and a long-term vision of the targeted markets/buyers.

Working for a large multinational US based company, leader in its reference markets, he has quite a good command of the English language, both oral and written, which is by all means considered mandatory for managing his daily business.

Paolo has always demonstrated a strong leadership, focused on building effective workteams, supported by a strategic approach oriented towards a long-term vision. He has a unique sense of ethics, based upon a true "Customer first" culture, and a high committment for achieving targets and delivering results even in the most challenging business environments.

Business Development (including special local and international projects, co-marketing with third party companies, joint ventures, distribution agreements, etc.), B2B & Relational Marketing, Sales Management, Customer Service & Tenders Management.
