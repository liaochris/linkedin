Thomas Mangum’s Summary

Changing lives by delivering experiences and adventures that get results that really MATTER! Start a Conversation. Collaborate. Contribute to get exactly what you want. What are you waiting for?

What's the problem right now? What do you need, want? What would change YOUR world? If you really want it, tell someone.

Above anything else, the ability to create deeper authentic relationships has made the biggest difference in my life and I'm obsessed with sharing that. How does it start? With a conversation; a simple conversation that begins with "Hey, I like the way you..Let's get together and talk"

WANTED: I'm looking for speaking & training opps. Companies that have over 100 employees, organizations..any venue that books speakers, trainers for their people. An experience is guaranteed. The old school "talk at them and tell what they SHOULD do" is over. I promise to deliver that experience because when someone shows up and invests their time, energy and money, they deserve it.

Since 2004, I've shown business owners processes giving them more of what they want, whether that's more time off, more sales, making better decisions, attracting better people or just feeling better. Clients have included industries as diverse as training, software, communications, public relations, manufacturing, real estate & network marketing.
