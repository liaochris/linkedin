Harry Love’s Summary

I am a big picture problem solver with a focus on simplicity, efficiency, automation, and Getting Real (see http://gettingreal.37signals.com/). I like working with ideas, visions, grand schemes, possibilities, and what-ifs. My current project is Jetrecord (http://jetrecord.com/), an online logbook for pilots.

I'm interested in working with creative, passionate, funny, smart people on projects that add value to the world. I especially appreciate projects that change the way people think. If I can make them laugh or cry at the same time, even better. Switching those last two statements also works for me.

What I love most about a project is seeing people interact with the finished product.

I enjoy creating and working with these things (in no particular order): web sites and applications, music and music composition, film and screenplays, writing, games, interactive play, and pranks.
