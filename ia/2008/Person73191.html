Ryan J. Corey’s Summary

I started All Around the Home (http://www.allaroundthehome.com) in February 2006 with two other partners (my cousins), the website has seen incredible growth steadily each month. We use all white hat SEO to generate organic search rankings for our site to convert leads for contractors. We sell the majority of the leads to contractors directly, but a portion of our leads go to a partner contractor referral company. So far, we really prefer the on-line lead generation as opposed to dealing with the contractors...

Still a work in progress, the revenue we now generate, and the potential we carry is something I am very proud of. This website has already been a huge success. We generate an excellent amount of traffic and hardly any of it is pay per click. We have definitely inched our way in to being a strong organic home improvement lead gen website. We have strong organic traffic that is growing rapidly and an excellent lead conversion rate of that traffic.

The success of AATH has brought on the acquisitions of a couple other home improvement lead gen sites. In 2008 we wil focus on the traffic growth of AATH and the development of our other sites. Fixthehome.com is already generating a decent amount of organic traffic and a few leads too. We are also going to grow into other industries of lead generation in 2008. Keep your eye on Lead Storm Media, LLC. http://www.leadstormmedia.com
