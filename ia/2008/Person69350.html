Gerry James’s Summary

Geographic Information Systems Professional with 12+ years of experience. I have been involved in myriad types of projects from creating custom GIS applications to deploying enterprise/distributed GIS applications for the federal government. I have experience in both open source GIS software (e.g. GRASS GIS, FW Tools, PostGIS/PostgreSQL) and commercial GIS software (e.g. ArcGIS/ArcINFO, ArcGIS Server, ArcSDE).

Gerry James’s Specialties:

ArcGIS Server development and implementation, General ArcObjects Programming, PostGIS/PostgreSQL development and implementation, UMN Mapserver development, Open Source GIS deployment for enterprises, GIS Implementation Consulting, GRASS GIS, StarUML, Quantum GIS, Project Management
