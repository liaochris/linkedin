Richard Stroupe’s Summary

Richard founded TRS Consulting Inc. in 2001 with the idea of providing a high quality alternative for information systems life cycle services, solutions and training within the U.S. Intelligence Community. Richard’s vision for TRS Consulting is to build an organization of highly talented technical staff that will work closely with our customers to produce mission driven results and foster a corporate culture that is fun, exciting and employee-friendly. As President & CEO, Richard maintains the strategic vision of the company and oversees all business operations, finance and business development activities. Aside from these duties, Richard also provides senior Oracle database administration support for several TRS clients.

Prior to founding TRS Consulting, Inc, Richard accumulated more than seven years of diverse experience in delivering software development life cycle support. During this time, Richard worked for Oracle Corporation, providing both core and applications senior database administration for the National Systems Consulting group as well as the U.S. Intelligence consulting divisions for SAIC and BAE Systems. Along with his consulting experience, Richard also has taught Oracle database administration courses for the past five years at various local universities and corporations and is co-author of Oracle Database 10g Insider Solutions. Richard also serves as a member of the review board for the IOUG Select Journal and is an advisory board member for Appalachian State University's College of Business Center of Entrepreneurship and Computer Information Systems (CIS) Department.

Richard holds a B.S. in Computer Information Systems from Appalachian State University, M.S. in Systems Engineering from The George Washington University and is currently enrolled in the Owner/President Management Program (OPM) at the Harvard Business School. Richard also holds Oracle Certified Professional credentials for Oracle 8i, 9i and 10g.

Richard Stroupe’s Specialties:
