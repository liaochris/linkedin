Maarten den Braber’s Summary

I am passionate about improving healthcare. Currently I writing my master thesis on building a model for comprehensive strategic orientation of Dutch hospitals.

Both the process of improving healthcare (strategy) as well as the application of strategy has my interest. Finding new ways to improve healthcare (health2.0, medical innovation, building new partnerships) is a challenge I love taking on.

I enjoy working with people that are not afraid to draw outside the lines and have an entrepreneurial mindset. I am tech-savvy and know my way around different technologies. Enabling people to let them do what they are best at and connecting them with other to build synergies is what I like doing. I like that put that into practice by organizing and engineering events of different types (theatre, music, conference) of any size.
