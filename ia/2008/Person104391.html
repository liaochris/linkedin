Chris Keller’s Summary

Currently, I am responsible for lead generation and online marketing activities to increase sales of our Service Center 6 kit for PC repair technicians. My work includes product development, affiliate marketing, email marketing, blog/newsletter creation, banner and print advertising. I am actively developing strategic partnerships and manage the collection, reporting and analysis of our web analytics data which drives my recommendations to increase our organic search engine ranking. I also manage our pay per click (PPC) ad campaigns

At HP, I was the World-wide product marketing manager of 3 multi-billion dollar Business Color LaserJet printers focusing on market research, product feature development, regional partner training, packaging, product literature development through product launch to end-of-life.

I completed an MBA from Brigham Young University and was the Student Association President. My areas of interest include real estate development, entrepreneurship, e-commerce and high tech product marketing

Previously, I managed a group of web designers, developed a usability research and QA test process and was an instructional architect working with faculty to improve teaching and learning using technology.
