Sam England’s Summary

I founded iVirtua Community in 2006. I have previously run several community and gaming orientated networks.

I also founded 2006 iNetGC with Lucas Mccartney, Kryptonlogic Networks with Jose Castillo, Florida, and iVirtua Learning and iVirtua Networks with Jay Taylor who is now a Director of Operations with both organisations that were founded, under the name iVirtua Holdings.

I also headed an outsource development team along with our UK Development Team Head Developer, Anthony Chu. We worked with Team leaders Salim Rahman (eMedia BD) and Sikander Ali (Mindurge Solutions, India). A partnership between iVirtua Holdings and Mindurge Solutions, India is complete.

iVirtua boasts a large team of professionals on the community and technical teams on a paid and voluntary basis, including moderators, community staff and editorial staff, from Europe, America and India.

iVirtua Networks takes on many blogs, communities and resources under the iVirtua Hosted Network - these are hosted alongside iVirtua and in collaboration via reciprocal link or affiliation or endorsement.

iVirtua Community is an exclusive online community targeted to, and designed for Professionals, Businesses and Students in the sectors and industries of Gaming, Creative New Media, Web, IT, and Computing, all closely related with it's Business and Industry, with articles, reviews, discussion, news, downloads, and debate. Share your news, views, reviews, ideas, experiences, questions... and debate, or contribute. iVirtua is based in the UK and at http://www.ivirtuaforums.com

I Founded RoxBox Hosting Inc with Kevin Martin of Synarchy in 2008 to provide web developers with server solutions in the US and UK, in a pioneering move to be self sufficient with our own racks and datacentre. We are likely to partner with Sun Microsystems and MySQL in a $500,000 initial investment deal.

iVirtua Media Group Ltd is likely to create assets in 2008 and is considering investors and related negotiations.
