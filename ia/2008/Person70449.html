Gitanjali Banerjee Bhattacharya’s Summary

In a world where there is increasing competition for resources between humans and wildlife, there is an urgent need to understand the dynamics of land use change, its impact on wildlife, particularly highly endangered large mammals and find ways to mitigate human-wildlife conflict. I hope to translate my training and field experience in addressing such issues into effective conservation policy.

My other main interest lies in empowering the youth of developing countries such as India who have a great power to bring about change at the grassroots level. I am a great believer in promoting a bottom up approach to development through education and empowerment. I am committed to encouraging personal initiative and enterprise amongst youth.

Gitanjali Banerjee Bhattacharya’s Specialties:

Conservation biology, conservation policy and education, mitigation of human-wildlife conflict, grazer-plant-soil interactions, resource partitioning and niche separation relationships,ecology of large mammalian herbivores, youth empowerment and community led development projects.
