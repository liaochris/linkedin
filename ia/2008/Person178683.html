Randy Premont’s Summary

I possess an extensive track record of spearheading successful business development and technical management efforts. Specifically, I offer proven abilities in driving dramatic improvements in sales revenues and organizational performance; identifying, analyzing, and resolving complex operational issues; and determining which emerging technology trends will become industry standards, including high speed wireless technology.

In 2000, I founded SpaceCycles, which offers business management/development consulting services. As President and Chief Executive Officer, I direct all aspects of daily operations and manage major projects for clients worldwide. Over the last seven years, I have grown annual revenue by several magnitudes. Previously, I fueled a 5,000% increase in annual revenue over nine years for Resource 2000 Inc. and supported client computer manufacturers, such as Digital Equipment, Hewlett-Packard, and IBM, in creating new sales plans to expand business into new markets. Earlier in my career, I increased revenue generation in the computer services industry for Boeing Computer Services and created custom imaging applications for the communications, entertainment, aerospace, and energy industries.
