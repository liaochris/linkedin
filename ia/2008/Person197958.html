Mark Vozzo’s Summary

Mark Vozzo works in the Digital Media industry as one of the internal Search Marketing experts at Fairfax Digital.

Fairfax Digital is wholly owned by Fairfax Media (publicly listed company ASX:FXJ). Fairfax is a leading provider of news in Australia and publishes the following:
- The Sydney Morning Herald
- The Age
- BrisbaneTimes.com.au
- AFR: Australian Financial Review
- BRW: Business Review Weekly
- and many others (see www.fairfax.com.au for complete list of sites)

He works as part of the News and Information Team that won the B&T Marketing Team of the Year award in Dec 2007.

He heads up the internal search marketing for this business unit and his main activities include:
- Management of large scale Paid Search (SEM) campaigns,
- Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) strategy & training to all levels of the business - senior management, technical teams, design teams and editorial
- Web Analytics expertise: producing regular reports on search marketing activities

His efforts have contributed to month on month growth in Unique Browser traffic (UBs) to sites: www.smh.com.au , www.theage.com.au , www.brisbanetimes.com.au, www.businessday.com.au and others.

Prior to Fairfax, Mark worked in the computer software industry for 8 years. As Online Marketing Manager at Citect, he managed the global online strategy, from web site infrastructure and design, bulk email distribution systems, online newsletters (MyCitect), paid search and natural search (SEO) marketing activities and more.
