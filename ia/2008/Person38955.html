Didi Zahariades, MA’s Summary

Therapist, Life Coach, Business Coach, & Motivational Speaker: With the experience as a Psychotherapist each session is a combination of personal goals, professional goals, and health/spirit. Specializing in work / life balance, while focused on goal attainment through individuals and team development. Clients are a combination of individuals dealing with complicated personal issues; depression, anxiety, and work related stress along with organizations suffering from these same stressors at the management level or team.

Motivational Seminars are determined by client's needs and include several topics such as: "Increasing Communication in the Workplace" "Dealing with Conflict Successfully" & "Attaining your Personal Best".
