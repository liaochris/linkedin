Ian McIntosh’s Summary

I am a veteran of fifteen years in the games industry with credits on over two dozen published titles. I've worked on platforms ranging from 16-bit consoles, through early, dedicated 3D consoles, personal computers, handheld game systems and multi-media mobile phones.
Always I try and know as much as I can be about the hardware and software the team is using. At the core, I'm a "technically-minded artist", I also enjoy game design, developing new ideas, reinterpreting licenses from other media, and mentoring other developers as they create content.

Ian McIntosh’s Specialties:

Developing game titles from first concept, through Game Design Document, production and Gold. Leading teams to produce high-tech and aesthetically-pleasing products. As an artist, I am most proficient with 2D Art and low-resolution art.
