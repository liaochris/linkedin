Griffin de Luce’s Summary

I've enjoyed working for startups, corporations, universities and governments as consultant, advisor or executive since 1995. Before that, I helped shape the future of the Web and large scale transaction driven marketplaces at Digital Equipment Corporation and JP Morgan on Wall Street.

I founded TGG.COM, an Internet consultancy serving IBM, MIT, Time Warner, AOL, American Express, Deloitte, McKinsey & Company, The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times and other Fortune 500s. As CEO I consulted to the head of the SEC and the CEO of MasterCard. By '99 I was bi-coastal between SF and NYC while becoming the digital agency of record for Netscape.

I later relocated full time to California, got married and began to incubate and occasionally invest in and a series of companies with a common Green thread. I started a VR agency (AxisVR); a Bioinformatics company (General Genomics) and a number of Social Media startups (Cozmo.TV, Urth.TV & Strike.TV).

I maintain press credentials and I've advised producers, executives and talent in print and broadcast (BBC, CBS, PBS, Middle East Broadcast Center, NYT, Newsweek, Fortune, Wired & The Wall Street Journal.) I've consulted in places as diverse as New York, Hollywood, Tokyo, Dubai and The Black Rock Desert. I have work on or consulted to international film productions and print and television interviews featuring luminaries like President Mikhail Gorbachev, General Colin Powell, John Sculley, George Soros and Bill Gates.

As President and CTO of Strike.TV (Hollywood Writers Strike) I helped reinvent the way in which television and film are financed, produced and distributed. I also serve as board member/advisor to companies in both consultative and non-profit capacities and I go out of my way to mentor young entrepreneurs who are similarly driven to better the world around them.
