  • Public Relations Specialist

    Nolde Forest Education Center

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    September 2005 — September 2006 (1 year 1 month)

    My task was to create a complete public relations package.

    I began by conducting an independent study of the grounds, hiking the Nolde trails and carefully snapping pictures that I believed exhibited the finest qualities of the forest.

    I then utilized the Nolde Library and local historical centers to dig as deeply into the history of the location as possible. Through these resources I was able to trace the history of the original Nolde settlers all the way back to their German roots.

    From their I streamlined the information and pictures I had gathered and created a series of brochures, fact sheets, advertisement pieces, business cards, and flyers. Each piece highlighted a different valuable asset that Nolde offered, from its Tudor styled mansion to its various hiking trails. Ultimately I created a public relations package that could be sent out piece by piece, or altogether as a comprehensive folder.
