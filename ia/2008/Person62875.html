Francisco Goncalves’s Summary

Having deep know-how in the areas of Information Technology, Computers Sciences, Telecomunications and the Project / Team leadership of Technical & Sales teams, I consider myself with the passion and knowledge to be actively productive and also innovative, in this new era of Information Sharing, Collaboration and Knowledge.

Previously worked as Chief Technology and on Specific Software Developments at ICL Portugal, Philips TDS and IT Consultant / IT Architect on Banking and Insurance Companies, always been dealing with innovative projects, delivering new ideas and also very proactive in the deliveries and support of IT services, oriented to the enterprise business.

From 2000 :
* Virtualization and Cloud Computing enabled InfraStructures, OpenOffice in the Cloud, Knowledge Wikis and Enterprise Social Networking Architectures.
* Linux e-Learning Systems Architectures, Moodle "Proof-Of-Concept" and Enterprise implementations.
* Investigation and studies on solutions for SOA and XML based architectures, enabling IBM AS/400 ISeries “legacy” applications to be modernised and face the today's requirements and challenges, migrating do Open Systems (Unix, Linux), with DB2 to Oracle and OLAP / BI capabilities.
* Architect / Consultant / Technology Advisor on the design of a new concept in the “virtual desktop environments”, for the personal workstations, with VoIP integration and the support of “thin Clients”, for a large bank organization. The concept involves Windows and Linux environments and the adoption of virtualization technologies, towards a "unified virtual desktop".
* Architect/Consultant, IT Solution Advisor and Project Management on a Server Consolidation and Implementation Project, that included VSAN (Virtual Storage Area Networks), adoption of O/S Virtualization Technologies, “Intel” based, with VMWARE ESX solutions, Storage and Data Replication between remote "Data-Centers" (Business Continuity) and IBM iSeries, with logical partitioning (LPAR).
