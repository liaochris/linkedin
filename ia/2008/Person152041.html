Stephane Pipon’s Summary

After receiving his degree in Tourism Marketing Management (1994) from the Université du Québec à Montréal, Stéphane Pipon was recruited by Tourisme Montréal, where, for 7 years he was Manager of Information Technologies under the direction of the VP of Research & Development. He was responsible for managing the official destination portal of Montreal for Tourism Montréal (www.tourism-montreal.org), a bilingual business-to-business and business-to-consumer site representing 750 tourism businesses in Montreal and reaching 2 million consumers a year.

Stéphane Pipon was also responsible for the development and management of two other sites: MontréalCAM (www.montrealcam.com), a major project consisting of a network of 10 Web cams dedicated to the promotion of Montreal as a destination, and the Website for the Hotel Association of Greater Montreal, an organization regrouping the biggest hotels in the Montreal area. Mr. Pipon was chair of the Web committee for the ENTER 2001 Tourism & Technology conference.

Since 2001, Stéphane Pipon is responsible to manage the business development strategy of Business Interactive Corporation for the government, retail, tourism, and intellectual property markets.
