    Tony Novia Joins Ryan Seacrest Productions

    May 15, 2008

    Ryan Seacrest Productions (RSP) adds another top executive to Seacrest's growing production company. RSP has announced the addition of Tony Novia as the Senior Vice President of New Media Development for the company.

    Novia will be in charge of general strategy and business development across all digital media platforms. Additionally, he will help oversee brand integration with advertisers for all RSP properties. Novia has 20 + years of media executive experience, having worked with companies such as Vivendi Universal.

    Seacrest commented, "RSP is in motion…my plan is to find the best people and build a media super store!"

    Novia joins Seacrest, who is RSP President/CEO and EVP Eliot Goldberg, who joined in April 2006. Jeff Refold, Seacrest’s longtime business manager, was named CFO of the company in January.
