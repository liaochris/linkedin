Finbar OHanlon’s Summary

• Over 15 years experience in IT and media entertainment systems
• Specialist in Convergance of Media appliances and Digital Media content
• Detailed knowledge core video architecture
• Global experience in delivering technical IP based video systems which produce instant incremental revenue

My vision is to deliver IP enabled video systems whose technological competency is invisible to the end user and "can be operated by a non technical consumer.

I am a digital media specialist, product designer and inventor of the Arrivell cognitive video platform. The Arrivell System adapts and translates video binary data to suit the playback platform from a single video feed. The system delivers a single source of content to Computers, Set top boxes, Cellphones regardless of the middleware client of media player.I am an IPTV media technology specialist heralding in all levels of film and television production (post production), audio - post- creation, content creation and development, I.P. based media systems core video architecture as well as delivering both conceptual and programmed solutions for large corporate clients all over the world.

I am proficient in object oriented programming languages as well as integrated advertising campaign management. I also held a position as creative director for an Australian advertising agency Fuel.

Some of my previous highlights include being commissioned as a specialist in delivering an I.P. based secure video distribution system for Nortel Networks Asia Pacific, MMDS satellite based T.V facility for the Sri Lankan Government and Rystar Communications, Managing large scale software web and database builds for Blockbuster and Holden Special Vehicles to producing binaural Stereo surround audio for TAS systems. Providing IPTV trials and or presentations to Bayern Munich, Ono (Spain), TF1 (france) Canal + (france) and successfully engaged global video studios into contracts to deliver IPvideo technology on a global scale.
