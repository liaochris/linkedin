Mike Thebado’s Summary

Strategic analyst, team leader, product manager and new product development director with extensive experience in technology, back office service, and financial product components of solutions for the wealth management industry.

Proven track record of predicting trends, eliciting and articulating client requirements, and defining and implementing successful solutions to capitalize on available opportunities.

Mike Thebado’s Specialties:

Focus Group organization and leadership, business requirements identification and communication, alternatives evaluation and prioritization, trends research and analysis, new products development and launch.

Client reporting, investment performance measurement, managed accounts, asset allocation, global investments, securities settlement, asset pricing, trust accounting, asset-backed securities.
