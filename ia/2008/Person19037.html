Rebekah Donaldson’s Summary

I’ve been a business-to-business (B2B) marketer since 1995.

In 2001 I launched Business Communications Group LLC, a B2B marketing consulting firm.

I help small and midsize companies influence teams of buyers within other companies. Usually, this entails building a business case, making a more findable and usable website, and writing interesting marketing communications.

I'm based in Davis, California.

Rebekah Donaldson’s Specialties:

B2B lead generation. B2B lead nurturing. B2B marketing communications. B2B public relations. B2B sales tools. B2B competitive intelligence. Build your business case.
