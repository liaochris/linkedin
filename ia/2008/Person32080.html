Christian Behrenbruch’s Summary

My professional experience is primarily based in the medical and healthcare domain. I have been involved in starting small companies, medium-sized public company activity as well as large corporate organizations.

Until September 2006, I worked for Siemens Molecular Imaging (A division or "Geschaft Gebiet" of Siemens Medical Solutions). I was responsible for advanced medical imaging reserach for drug discovery and clinical use. I managed a group of approximately 120 people in the US, UK, Germany, France and India.

Currently I have returned to my entrepreneurial roots in the form of participating in a small "angel" fund / advisory business. This is mirrored by a wonderful opportunity in the form of a Professorship at UCLA in the medical school. UCLA has some of the finest minds in biotechnology and medical devices and it's a rare opportunity to help bring some of these technologies to clinical fruition and beyond.

I still help a small number of UK, US and European start-up companies in a wide variety of areas. As a business angel, I am generally interested in small start-up companies looking for early funding. I also do a fair bit of work in China (particularly Hangzhou) both in terms of technology investments and advisory in the area of nano/biotech. If you are interested in establishing a relationship, contact me at chris@behrenbruch.net.
