Rob Simmons’s Summary

I entered the world of Travel Nurse Recruiting & Staffing at Procare USA in CT in 1999. I evolved from a Nurse Recruiter and Salesman to Manager and Businessman when I resigned from Procare in 2002 & accepted a position at ATC Healthcare Services in NY. I was Director of ATC's Travel Division, ATC Travelers from 2002-2005.

I am the Founder and President of Stategic Nurse Staffing, Inc. (SNS). SNS was incorporated in Late 2005. As of Jan. 2008 I have 2 partners and we operate out of Melville NY. Today, SNS is bigger than both Procare USA and ATC Travelers.

I am also the CEO of Salute The Dawn, Inc. a New York based indie rock group that successfully tours regularly.
