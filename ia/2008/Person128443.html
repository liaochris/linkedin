Meredith Coppolo Shindler’s Summary

Bill2Phone offers a reliable, alternate payment option for digital transactions, driving revenue from consumers who do not use credit or debit when making online purchases. Can be integrated directly into a merchant's payment platform or added to a suite of payment options offered by an ISO, MSP or other payment provider.

Additional payment processing services include gateway, authentication, merchant acquiring, credit card processing (both US and EU), ACH and risk management solutions designed specifically for ecommerce transactions.

BSG Clearing Solutions is a public entity, with NA headquarters based in San Antonio, TX for 22 years.
