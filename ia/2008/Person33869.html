Charlie Wallace’s Summary

Since 1987, I have been leading marketing, branding, channel development, and sales teams for leading OEM hardware and software technology companies.

From 1997 through 2003, I lead the Global Channel Development, Marketing and Programs team at Seagate Technology where we quickly took Seagate to the #1 position in Market-share in the indirect Channel by creating Seagate's award-winning global Partner Program. I led the creation of both the retail consumer channel as well as the System Builder and VAR channels and lead the creation of all sales tools, training, POS materials and sales programs.

In 2003, Hitachi Data Systems asked me to help transform the company from a direct to a channel focused enterprise. In only one year, we created a global reseller channel which accounted for 50% of Hitachi's global business, created and implemented the Hitachi TrueNorth Channel Partner Program and established the Hitachi Service Provider Program which certifies, trains and authorizes channel partners to sell and deliver a complete line of Hitachi branded services.

As an independant consultant with Core Strategies, I provided strategic channel consulting services to technology companies. Companies that utilized my services included: Microsoft, NetApp, Raritan Computer, Adaptec, and CMP Media.

In the Fall of 2006, NetApp asked me to build a global channel marketing and development team whose goal is to make NetApp the most "Channel Successful" company in the technology industry by developing programs that enable NetApp partners to sell and deliver support and professional services.
