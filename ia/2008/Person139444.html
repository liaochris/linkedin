Neil Burns’s Summary

Board level chemical industry commercial executive and general manager. Renewed private company via throughput increase, cost reduction and participation as investor / manager in LBO. Started up US chemical company for Indian parent. Negotiated consumer goods manufacturing plant acquisition. US and international chemical (surfactant) sales and marketing experience. Experience in strategic planning, supply chain, M&A, TQM. Currently CEO of US subsidiary of multinational chemical company. Interested in specialty chemical investment opportunities or board roles with for profit or non-profit entities.
