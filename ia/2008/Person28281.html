Michael Brothers’s Summary

My name is Michael Brothers, Broker and REALTOR® for Brothers Group Realty, Modern Day Realty, and Apartment Match. I am a dedicated father to my eight year old daughter and have a compassionate work ethic. I consider myself an efficient, motivated, passionate, successful real estate professional specializing in buyers agency in the San Antonio market. I'm also happy to inform you that I have acquired my ABR® (Accredited Buyer Representative) designation. Our personal goal is to simplify the process of buying real estate, and to satisfy everyone's needs to the best of our ability. We LISTEN to your needs. We use the latest technology to understand the current market and to make the connections necessary for our success. Please enjoy our websites. It is just one of the many successful tools we use to assist our clients in finding the perfect home. We are members of the San Antonio Board of Realtors, TAR, NAR, San Antonio Apartment Association, TAA, NAA, Greater San Antonio Chamber of Commerce & the Better Business Bureau.
