Craig Wallace’s Summary

I have worked in the entertainment industry for over 20 years as a development executive, talent agent, director of new talent and most recently creator and teacher of The Wallace Audition Technique - an audition and business class for both the aspiring and the working actor. I am also the author of the book "The Best ofYou--Winning Auditions Your Way," published in 2007 by Oyster Mining Company and have lectured extensively at Universities and Artists' organizations across the country. I am also a trainer for business professionals in need of improving their public speaking and for sales forces who want to create more dynamic and effective presentations; and have taught workshops at retreats and conventions.
I would like to take all of this experience further in the business world and work more extensively in corporate training and in developing programs geared toward the speaking and presentation needs of specific businesses.
