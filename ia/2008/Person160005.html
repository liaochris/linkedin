Richard J. Krasney, CFP®’s Summary

I act as Financial Coach and Personal Chief Financial Officer for a select number of elite business owners and entrepreneurs. Through collaboration with clients and my professional network , I provide clients with balance and peace of mind by helping them make smart decisions with their wealth. My clients look to me for advice on every major financial decision impacting their lives.

Charitable gifting- (Strategies, execution planning, and management)
