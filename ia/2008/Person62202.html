Huawei (from March, 2008. Current job)
PR & Communication manager for Huawei in Spain. My role includes media relations, external, internal and executive communications.

IBM (from may, 1999, to february 2008)
Communications Specialist: Working for the IBM Spain Communications Department, my role included the development and execution of communication plans; writing and editing of by-lined articles, press releases, backgrounders, media pitches, executive speeches and other documents; translation of corporate documents, organization of press conferences; briefing and consulting for IBM executives; response to media requests; etc. New media specialist (blogging, wikis, etc).

I graduated in History by the Universidad Autónoma de Madrid and got a master degree in journalism by the “Escuela de Periodismo UAM / El País”.
