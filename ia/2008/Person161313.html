René Kempenaar’s Summary

Born in the ‘Green Heart’ of Holland, I spent most of my youth on my father’s co-owned business. Here I supported the daily operational activities and in a later stage initiated new developments and processes.

After college, I sought new opportunities to develop my knowledge and experience in the field of finance and business. Moreover, to add value for the business I work in.

I like to challenge the status-quo of doing business, turning figures into real operational efficiency and effectiveness. I enjoy working in an environment where physical products form the core of the business and finance and production tackle the complexity of improvement.
