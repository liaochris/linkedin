Rick Stockfield’s Summary

Twelve years of experience in providing client and professional services to Fortune 500, Fortune 1,000 and Atlanta-based companies. Big Four (KPMG) background, successful business development background (JWI), commenced recruiting career successfully in October 2001, when recruiting wasn't fashionable. Successful tour of duty culminating in a Partnership at one Firm and the successful launch of two Firms since, including co-founding a recruiting dot-com (TALENThire.com).

Now 100% solely dedicated to consulting with Atlanta-based companies in identifying and attracting the accounting, finance, tax and audit talent they need. Consultative and thorough approach to my profession. I take pride in the services I provide to clients, candidates and consultants alilke. Competitive and aggressive nature spurns my desire to be the best recruiter in Atlanta.

Rick Stockfield’s Specialties:

Specialty areas include tax, audit, accounting & finance; working with Big Four professionals, CPAs/CIAs/CISAs, experienced from 2-25+ years of experience; fast-trackers, those looking to grow their careers. My clients and candidate first. For a list of some of the openings I'm currently working on please visit www.atlantasearchgroup.com

Also open to start-up companies seeking ideas, seats on Board of Directors, and Consulting Ventures for start-up companies having been-there-done-that.
