Jeffrey Barnes’s Summary

As a seasoned Business Development Leader, Jeff Barnes has leveraged diverse career experiences into an enviable portfolio of loyal senior executive clients and an exceptional network of business relationships. With expertise as a Rainmaker and Matchmaker, he can enable shortened sales cycles, higher close rates and reduced cost of sales. Accordingly, as an accomplished Decision Facilitator, Jeff can enable Decisions that Deliver: Implemented Solutions; Expected Value; Accountable Process.

Previously, Jeff served as Senior Manager with PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP. He founded and for eight years served as CEO of RAIN, a Managed IT Services Provider. Jeff also founded a popular Adult BA Degree Program in Business Information Systems that is currently in its twenty-fifth year of successful operation at Linfield College. He has held sales and general management positions with Aspect Communications, Compuware Corporation, and Computer Task Group. He has served as an expert witness in arbitrations, conducted IT strategies workshops, and currently provides business development and talent acquisition services through his consulting firm, Compelevent Solutions. Jeff attended Whitman College and earned a BA in Sociology from the University of Washington, as well as a MS in Social Research from Portland State University.
