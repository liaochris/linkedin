John Hood’s Summary

I am a columnist, author, political commentator, and president of the John Locke Foundation, a North Carolina think tank.

John Hood’s Specialties:

I specialize in state and local politics and public policy issues. My other journalism is primarily for Reason magazine, for which I am a contributing editor, and National Review, where I write on various topics, including state politics, and blog at "The Corner."

John Hood’s Experience

  • Chairman and President

    John Locke Foundation

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Think Tanks industry)

    November 1989 — Present (18 years 8 months)

    I am President and Chairman of the John Locke Foundation, a North Carolina think tank that issues policy studies, hosts dozens of events each year, produces radio and TV programs, and publishes Carolina Journal, a monthly newspaper with a readership of nearly 200,000 North Carolinians. I helped to found JLF in 1989.

    In addition to my duties at JLF, I am a syndicated columnist for the High Point Enterprise, the Gaston Gazette, the Durham Herald-Sun, and newspapers in 50 other North Carolina communities. I am also a weekly panelist on “N.C. Spin,” a discussion program that is broadcast on 16 television stations and 12 radio stations.

    I am the author of three books: Selling the Dream: Why Advertising is Good Business (Praeger, 2005); Investor Politics (Templeton Foundation Press, 2001); and The Heroic Enterprise: Business and the Common Good (The Free Press, 1996).

  • Contributing Editor, Reason magazine

    Reason Foundation

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Media Production industry)

    1988 — Present (20 years)

    I have written for Reason magazine off and on since I finished my journalism degree in 1988.

  • Bradley Visiting Fellow

    The Heritage Foundation

    (Non-Profit; 201-500 employees; Think Tanks industry)

    Wrote my first book, for Simon & Schuster, while on a yearlong fellowship at Heritage. It was me, Stephen Glass, and Tucker Carlson in an office suite. Fun times.

  • Reporter-Researcher
