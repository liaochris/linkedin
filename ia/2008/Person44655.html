Dan Weatherby "DANO"’s Summary

In addition to running my own Interactive media company, D Interactive, for the past 3 years, I have been Young Isaac’s Director of Interactive for a little over a year now.

Prior to these positions, I was Senior Interactive Art Director for SBC Advertising, serving such clients as Bob Evans Farms, Gojo Industries, Huntington Banks, Ohio Health, Big Lots, and Sherwin Williams, to mention just a few. Prior to that I also was Senior Interactive Art Director at Out of the Box Designs working on accounts such as ECOutlook, Ohio Health, and Sterling Commerce. While with Young Isaac, I have put my talents to work for a variety of clients, including The Ohio State University, League of Women Voters, Ohio Dental Association and Ohio Business Roundtable.

In my role at Young Isaac and D Interactive, I am responsible for the creative direction, design, strategy and management of web site interface design and site development, flash application development, in-store/in-booth plasma screen animation and development, CD-ROM application development and audio/video editing and production.

I hold a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from the Columbus College of Art and Design.

Dan Weatherby "DANO"’s Specialties:

Interactive strategic planning, interface design, user experience, usability, flash application design and development.
