Heather Landers’s Summary

Designer Heather Landers has been running her full-service design studio for over a year to great success. Studio Picotee offers a range of creative services, including large-scale graphics for retail and corporate environments; branding consultation and design; consumer packaging; personalized invitations; and custom wine labels.

Heather combines her creative inspiration, intelligent observation, and a decade of experience working for iconic brands like Apple and the Gap to offer fresh ideas to her clients. She specializes in developing design programs that work across a range of deliverables, exist within a specific space, or tell a story in multiple dimensions.

Current clients include: The North Face, BellyBar, The Power of Two, Wag Hotels, The Gap, Robert Frear Architects, Walmart.com, Kaia Foods, and Wanderlust Wines.

Heather Landers’s Specialties:

enviromental graphic design, architectural graphics, retail design, marketing, collateral, branding, inviations, special events, trade shows, signage, wine labels, curatorial work, space planning, fixture design
