David Flowers’s Summary

Mr. Flowers has over a decade of experience within the real estate and investment arena. He has an extensive background primarily in advising, working both the buy/sell side of investment and real estate transactions. He has dedicated his career entirely to the real estate sector. In addition, his concentration continues in mergers & acquisitions of/and investing in distressed real estate and companies; venture capital and private equity; working with clients who seek capital partners; banks who are trying to sell debt and non-performing loans; sub prime assets; taking great advantage of the opportunities at hand currently in the marketplace.

The Flowers Family is one of the oldest families in America. Collectively, the Flowers Family and members within the Flowers family have been involved in farming, ranching, cattle, horse and equine operations, the furniture business, cotton plantations, banking, real estate development, the food & wine business for generations; including Flowers Farms, Flowers Furniture, Flowers Plantation (s), Flowers Food and Flowers Winery & Vineyard. The family tradition of farming, however, goes back to 1635, when the first Flowers ancestor, John Flowers, gave up his life as a ship's captain to farm in Virginia. Succeeding generations of Flowers farmed and settled plantations in Virginia, North Carolina, Tennessee and Mississippi.

real estate, investment banking, principal opportunities (buy side) investments, investing and advising, funds & investment vehicles within the real estate, private equity & investment banking sector; special acquisitions and special opportunity funds; risk assessment, asset management, site acquisition, mergers and acquisitions.
