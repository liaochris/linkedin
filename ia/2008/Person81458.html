Iwan Streichenberger’s Summary

Iwan Streichenberger is a global senior executive with general management experience and proven success with large multinationals as well as earlier stage companies. He currently acts as an independent consultant and angel investor.
Prior to becoming an investor and advisor, Iwan spent twelve years in executive positions in Digital Media and Technology, Entertainment, Education, Publishing and Consumer Goods,
Iwan most recently managed Houghton Mifflin’s global strategy and M&A activity, reporting to the CEO and working with board members representing three leading private equity firms, Bain Capital, Thomas H. Lee and Blackstone. He also became the President of Edusoft, which he grew from a small loss-making regional player to a highly profitable nationwide leader in Education technology with over 150 employees.
Iwan joined Houghton Mifflin in 2001 when Vivendi Universal acquired the $1.5 billion publisher. Iwan had left the Boston Consulting Group to join Vivendi Universal to become the COO of its European online gaming portal, Flipside.
Previously, Iwan spent two years in South Africa working for L’Oreal. He worked very closely with the General Manager of L’Oreal South Africa to fully restructure and turn around this subsidiary, including a full reorganization of the headquarter, distribution facility and factory.
He is also an active member of the Euro American Angel Group and US Angel Investors Group and serves on the Board of the French American Chamber of Commerce of San Francisco (FACCSF)
