Mark Stevenson’s Summary

I am a Webby Award Honoree for my design and project management of Monty Python's SpamAlot website. I also recently helped develop the first online edition of Weekly Reader Magazine (go to www.markstevenson.com for links).

I have extensive experience developing online strategies, Web sites, online advertising, editorial content, DVDs and marketing campaigns, including email blasts, online contests, games and more. Clients include educational institutions, Fortune 500 companies, and leaders in entertainment.

My comprehensive knowledge of interactive development is the result of 8 years experience running my own interactive studio. My expertise includes leading development teams, managing client relationships and producing creative product. Prior to The Stevenson Studio, I held positions as sr. producer, art director, project manager and designer at several leading interactive agencies and marketing firms.

In addition to years of management experience, I am an expert user of Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Fireworks and especially Flash, with strong ActionScript, CSS and HTML/DHTML. I have strong Final Cut Pro editing skills and DVD Studio Pro authoring experience. I also have produced, art-directed and shot numerous photo shoots, both commercial and editorial.

Links and bio: www.markstevenson.com
