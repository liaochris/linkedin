Jesse Grimes’s Summary

A senior-level user experience consultant and information architect, with nine years of international experience applying User Centred Design approaches to a broad range of projects and platforms. Experience in team leadership and specific expertise in user research and experience design for mobile devices.

Jesse Grimes’s Specialties:

User-centred design activities (collaborative design, usability testing, contextual interviews, card sorting, affinity diagramming, experience modelling, low- and high-fidelity prototyping, and stakeholder and user needs analysis). Mobile application and UI design. User testing.
