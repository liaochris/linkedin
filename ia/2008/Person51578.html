Dik De Bruijn’s Summary

After 20 years with Shell in various line- and project management positions, I have started in 2005 as a freelancer in interim program- and project management. In this area I own certifications from PMI (PMP - Project Management Professional) and MSP (Managing Successful Programmes). In 2007 I joined ETD ExperienceTheDifference as partner and after a merger with BEST, Bank of Experienced Senior Talent, we are now called BEST ExperienceTheDifference. We attract 'senior talents' into our network and find suitable assignments for them.

My career has moved from Science (PhD in Physics) to industrial Research and then to Management Consultancy and further into the IT domain as strategist, services manager, and as project /programme manager. Over this period I have gained a broad experience in the application of IT for business benefits and in setting up and running all kinds of (IT) services. I consider my current activities, running a consultancy company, as a third career.

In summary I would describe myself as creative, intelligent, and a good (people) manager with a keen interest in business opportunities and new technology.

My current goal is to be successful in business and as an independant interim manager, for which I have choosen program management as a specialism. You can approach us at BEST-ETD with interesting assignments for senior talents (as interim manager, coach, trainer, or e.g. finance or HR specialist).

Dik De Bruijn’s Specialties:

Program-, project-, and portfolio management, risk management are my current specialties.
Earlier specialisms are a.o. knowledge- and document management, IT strategy and planning, performance measurement, services management.
