Richard Palarea’s Summary

PA & Associates, ranked #8 in the Top 50 Fastest Growing Private Companies by the Baltimore Business Journal (2007) provides cost reduction and management tools for logistics/shipping operations for companies of any size.

Through stringent analysis of present shipping patterns, a proven request for proposal (R.F.P.) framework, traditional consulting practices, third-party negotiating and ongoing data analysis, most clients enjoy 20% to 50%+ reductions in present shipping expenses.

Compensation of professional fees is based on a performance-contingent model; fees are only due if savings are realized and fees are paid out of the savings obtained. This means that there is no new budgeting required to pay the fees.

Due to the detailed and professional nature of each project, the organization accepts a limited number of new clients each year. New business is generated only through personal introductions to Presidents, CFOs or senior executives with a role in finance.
