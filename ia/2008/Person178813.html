Spencer Schmerling’s Summary

I have been an entrepreneur for the past 20 years. I have spent the past 10 years in Marketing and Consulting. My company, Recneps, Inc., started in 1998 as a one-to-one direct marketing company and evolved as the Internet evolved. About 5 years ago I started to focus more on consulting with other companies, helping them refine their marketing to keep up with the changing times.

Having a strong recruiting background, I started a search firm that specialized in recruiting loan officers for mortgage companies. This was my first experience in the real estate industry. After the mortgage market collapsed, I figured it was time to go back to what I knew the best, marketing.

That is when I started REMS. I combined my marketing skills with the vast amount of experience that I have using Craigslist and started REMS to help real estate agents find leads during this tough down market.

Spencer Schmerling’s Experience

  • Founder

    Real Estate Marketing Service

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2007 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    I started Real Estate Marketing Service (REMS) as a division of Recneps, Inc. in response to the collapse of the real estate market.

    With real estate prices spiraling down, buyers went into bargain-hunter mode. Since Craigslist is known for having great deals, it quickly became the place to go to find a house.

    I started by helping my niece, who is a real estate agent in Las Vegas. Craigslist was the only place that she was getting any leads from, but she was having trouble posting her ads. I helped her get her ads on Craigslist consistently and then I helped her improve her ads to get more response. Then I helped her friends and associates.

    Soon word got out that I was pretty good at marketing real estate on Craigslist and I was flooded with requests from real estate agents, brokers, and trainers. Seeing the demand for such a service, I started REMS to help real estate professionals market their properties on Craigslist.
