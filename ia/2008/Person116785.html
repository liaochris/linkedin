Luis Jurado’s Summary

A professional with a lot of experience in the IT industry, specially in the software industry. Has acted as general manager for a couple of companies:
- Software development company, implementing eBusiness solutions and outsourcing services for markets in the US, Ireland and Central America
- IT security company, focused on integrating services and products related to the secure management of information.

Worked for Microsoft for 7 years, initially developing the channel for selling to the Enterprise in Guatemala and El Salvador and then moved to the Enterprise segment and acted as a sales account manager, responsible for the achievement of the quota in this segment.

Amog the awards received:
- Best Performance of the Year – 2002 (Microsoft)
This recognition was obtained by over achieving the quota by 29% in the year and containing the competition in almost all the sector and investing in strategic projects for the company.

- Stars Award Program at Microsoft on Fiscal Year 2000
This award was obtained based on an outstanding performance, leadership and strategic thinking competencies.

- Sales Award on Q4 Fiscal Year 2000 at Microsoft
Obtained by over achieving the quota on the fourth quarter of the fiscal year 2000 and supporting the subsidiary to reach the year’s objectives.

- First Place on the Latin America Partners Program Contest in Fiscal Year 1997
Obtained by presenting a winning plan to develop the services area on a selected number of business partners in Guatemala and Costa Rica, using the principles of Microsoft Consulting Services. As part of the award, we obtained $50,000 from the Latin America Vice President to invest in the program.
