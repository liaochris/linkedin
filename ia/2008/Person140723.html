Nick Smith’s Summary

As a writer I am a contributor of features and reviews to newspapers including the Daily Telegraph, Literary Review, Times Higher Education Supplement and the Wall Street Journal. Magazine work includes regular contributions to Bentley, Bookdealer, Geographical, Flipside and the Explorers Journal.

My photographs have appeared in BBC Focus, Country Life, Organic Life, Outdoor Photography, Daily Telegraph, WEXAS Traveller, Geographical, Flipside, Engineering & Technology...

I currently hold three masthead positions: Literary Editor, Bookdealer; Contributing Editor, Explorers Journal; Management Editor Engineering & Technology. I am also editor of the James Caird Society Newsletter.

I have also published one book to date – 'Questions and Answers' (Carlton Books, 2001) – a popular science title in collaboration with author Robert Matthews. 'Imperial London', a book of photography is currently being prepared for publication in 2009.
