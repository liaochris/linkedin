Scot Duke’s Summary

My Mission as a consultant is to share my 32 years of business operations management experience. I have a fever for the game of golf. I also have a tremendous desire to help the golf industry improve and develop by reviewing each golf business's core business operations.

My mantra is to "Keep It Simple to Get Things Done!". I am inspired by the "Big Ideas" and the people that dream them. I choose to work with individuals that believe in making a buck while making a difference.
