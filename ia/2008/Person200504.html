Winne Soh’s Summary

Winne Soh had 5 years of extensive industrial experience in the interactive media industry. She had broad experience in working with international advertising agencies and IT solutions companies, creating and delivering effective business media for corporations such as Microsoft, Palm and Singapore Airlines. She had also worked in Shanghai, China, as a Marketing Supervisor at Fractalist China Inc., a mobile media and marketing company. She was engaged in the launches of various mobile marketing campaigns for multinational clients, such as Volkswagen and Nokia.

Winne had started the pursuance of her interest since her early schooling days. She had launched her first self-created CD-ROM production titled “Legend of the Modern City” when she was 15 years old, which reached more than 3000 students in Singapore. In 2000, she had been invited to showcase her production at the Learning Metropolis Convention held at Suntec City Singapore. Winne had also won numerous top awards in interactive media competitions, including a Platinum award in the prestigious ThinkQuest Internet Challenge 2002 with more than 200 competitors from countries such as USA, Netherlands, Australia, etc. She was also President of the IT Club in her Junior College and Secondary School, organizing and leading numerous nationwide IT programs and competitions for 4 years.

Winne obtains her Honours Degree in Computing with Technopreneurship from the National University of Singapore (NUS). She had also conducted a one-year faculty research at NUS in the behavioural study of consumers’ reaction to marketing information transmitted via the mobile platform. She currently runs her own interactive media business at Dream Axis Private Limited and serves as the Treasurer at the Association of MACHINIMA Arts & Sciences driving the Machinima movement in Singapore and Asia.
