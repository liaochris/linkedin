Dean O'Keeffe’s Summary

I have recently completed a number of projects for Streel Drum Studios, including the latest Comet commercial, a series promo for Animal Planet, an animation for Sony and CG sequences for a Disney commercial.

Currently I am available to work and particularly interested in any TD positions that require production level modelling, shading, lighting, rendering and compositing experience. As productions advance I am confident in my ability to adapt as my responsibilities change and I am willing to undertake any task with enthuisiasm and dedication.

During the three years as Operations Manager and TD at RJDM Animations a solid foundation of production experience was gained. Moving on as a freelance TD my strengths have developed and include a high level of technical and creative knowledge of TV and film production as well as strong problem solving abilities.

My production experience with Maya, Photoshop, After Effects, Muster and Smedge is of a high level, with Maya being used for over 8 years.

My role as TD and Operations Manager included the design, construction, maintenance and development of a full production pipeline, as well as documenting and training members of the creative team in production techniques.

Project management has included work for clients such as British Airways, Volvo, Land Rover, Audi, VW, SEAT, National Homebuyers, Oxygen Interactive, Prodrive, Castrol, Purple Media and Broncolor.

In addition to this my experience includes working with award winning agencies such as Saatchi & Saatchi, The Edge Picture Company, Mindshare, Iceberg Marketing and Joshua G2.

Working within a team environment is particulary enjoyable but my knowledge and experience also allows me to take any project through all stages of production, from concept to completion, when working independently.

A portfolio can be found on my website, www.dartist.co.uk so please have a look when you have a spare minute, thank you.
