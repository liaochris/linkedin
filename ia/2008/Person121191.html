Mario Gastaldi’s Summary

Bilingual (Italian and English) consultant, facilitator with over 20 years of experience, with different roles, in diverse organizations across different geographies, specialties and cultures.
Specializes in Employee Engagement, Motivation, Change Management, Organization design and Strategic Planning using approaches that “allow” meaningful breakthroughs to happen fast.
Rather than offering predefined solutions, Mario engages with diverse, complex organizational systems, interacting closely with people and facilitating collective learning and self-renewing processes that lead key players and stakeholders to discover their path, and define actions that are consistent, coherent, synergic, based on enthusiastic support and strong motivation.

An intense experience working closely with business leaders generated Mario’s peculiar business acumen and a meaningful sensitivity to multiple bottom lines.
Mario designs and delivers interventions that successfully impact both the business side and the human side of organizations enhancing their mutual relation and improving both in ways that are especially valuable.
