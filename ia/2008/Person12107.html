Andries Oudshoorn’s Summary

During my 7 years as a management consultant I gained a broad experience including corporate strategy, M&A, business unit strategy, organizational turnarounds, marketing, sales force effectiveness, operations. I worked at leading international companies in the UK, Netherlands, Germany, Belgium, Spain and South Africa, focusing on the telecommunications, internet, media industries.

I also have experience in the Internet world, first as country manager for a startup online retail company, where I managed to establish the organization and realize exceptional traffic and membership growth in the first 6 months, and afterwards at Schibsted Classified Media, the leading internet classified player in Europe.

I am currently based in Barcelona, Spain and responsible for various projects, including M&A, new business startups, business development and internet marketing.
