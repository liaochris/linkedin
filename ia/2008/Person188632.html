Tichaona P. Mpofu AInstSMM’s Summary

My business partner and I, Seb Robin are involved in various business ventures. Like anything in life, the success of these ventures will depend on our preparation, tenacity and ingenuity.

Born and raised in Harare, Zimbabwe, I relocated to the UK in 2000, and since then I have been on an entrepreneurial journey. Having worked in Real Estate for many years in the exhilarating Canary Wharf market, I packed that all in to carve my own path, and what a journey it has been to date. So the journey continues and I welcome you to explore my profile and connect if you think there are synergies.

To our success!

CURRENT VENTURES INCLUDE:
SBN (UK) - We are cost reduction specialists for SMEs. We offer “No Save, No Fee” Audits for our clients to determine how much we can save them on their expenses. In some cases we can save an SME up to 90% of their original cost dependent on the nature of the expense. Visit www.sbnuk.com for further information.

TOPLINE provides Business Development services for the IT Industry. Our service helps companies break into new territories or launch new products without the cost of employing full time permanent Sales Executives.

MAIZON Real Estate Investments – Provides a Portfolio Building Services for Investors in Scotland. We are currently developing a new Property Investment Product which will interest many Investors / FTB particularly in the current economic climate.

Bienvenu – Currently at Seed Stage this company is targeted towards the Hospitality Industry. We are currently welcoming and inviting Venture Capitalists, Business Angels and High Net Worth Individuals with interests in Hospitality projects, Telecommunications and Software as a Service (SaaS).

MG: Resource Brokers Limited – MG:RB is our holding company. All our start-up companies are held within this holding company until an appropriate time.

For further information on any of the above ventures please email tichaona@sbnuk.com / tichaona@mgrbrokers.co.uk
