Gunnar Krogh-Tonning (gunnar@best-consult.no)’s Summary

Executive Search / Headhunting assignments have been carried out for small, medium and large corporations within various business fields over the past 23 years, national and global, in the computer industry, telecom, high-tech, trade, manufacture and service sectors. The main focus has been on the selection of executives, line managers and specialists. Our services also include registration in our international board of management bank, not only for candidates who would like international board positions, but also for companies who are looking for international expertise for their boards.
