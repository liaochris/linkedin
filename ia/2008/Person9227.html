Amir Herzberg’s Summary

Broad experience in:
- communication network protocols, (Quality of Service, wireless, ...)
- security of communication networks and electronic commerce systems (e.g. SET, iKP, ...)
- applied cryptography (e.g., proactive cryptography, tolerant combiners)
- secure usability (e.g., TrustBar anti-phishing/spoofing toolbar)
- fault-tolerant distributed algorithms.

I like to consult in design and evaluation of systems, and I also enjoy giving lectures and courses.

Amir Herzberg’s Specialties:

Secure electronic commerce and payment systems; secure networking, including secure usability considerations.
