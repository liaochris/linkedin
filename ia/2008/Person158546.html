Steven Reinemund’s Summary

I am a bilingual general manager with a proven track record of strong execution. Whether a struggling or mature business, I have demonstrated the ability to revive a business in the short term while establishing a plan for sustainable long term growth.

I love retail & consumer packaged goods (CPG). I thrive on the opportunity to take a Steve Jobs/Apple approach to ESTABLISHING what consumers want & need by reading trends & anticipating needs, not just following & optimizing the competition's products.

I have been able to integrate my passion & entrepreneurship into the corporate rank & file environment by innovating within standard operating procedures & leveraging the power of inspiring great cross-functional teams.

Awards:
Wal-Mart Item Merchant of the Year (2005)

Career Quick Facts:
- spent several years in Financial Services, domestically & internationally, through gift cards, private label & third party (restaurant, etc.) I am well versed in card specifications & card-based services & transactions
- headed Wal-Mart Task Force & eliminated $2 billion in inventory in 2006
- developed, automated, & integrated into the required modular process a buyer tool for inventory management (GMROII v. % sales)
- developed program at Wal-Mart to put signage in Home & Domestics, which resulted in 10% increase in sales & 10% decrease in inventory
- managed multi-billion dollar private label gift card program, growing business at 30%, introducing over 150 new designs & dozens of in-store permanent display locations (check-outs and throughout store)
- layered on $70 million to footcare, reading glasses, & nicotine replacement therapy in <1 year
- turned myself into a marketing campaign selling footcare, creating personas such as WartMan & BigFoot to help drive sales & in-stock at the biannual Wal-Mart Managers shows - ask a Wal-Mart store manager next time you are in a store - they might have a picture with me!
