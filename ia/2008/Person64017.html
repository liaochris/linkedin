Finn Ritslev’s Summary

+15 years experience with business planning and development, strategic planning and innovation; as employee, consultant and founder of several startups.

I mostly facilitate development of companies starting with a strategic review including an action plan leading to increased Freedom to Act. Management is provided with a suite of tools to better decide winning strategies and mitige significant risks.

The strategic review is a facilitated down-to-earth and business-like process driven by practical experience - not by theory. The result after a few workshops is a jointly developed action plan complemented by a 360-degree external and internal situation analysis and an advanced SWOT with ratings of the effect from the impact of critical business aspects, positive as negative. A partly IT-supported process guarantees a high quality and reduces the time (and thus the bill) required by an order of 5-10 compared to traditional methods. The process lends itself to be platform for implementation of a cost-effective Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) for any company.

Further, I do "Introduction to Blue Ocean Strategy" seminars and facilitate innovation processes enabling companies to improve their competitive situation or even find uncontested market place. I have helped raise capital and assisted several companies outside Denmark finding Danish business partners.
