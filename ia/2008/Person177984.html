Søren Skifter Christensen’s Summary

Nine years of top level experience in business development, sales and leadership in Latin America and Europe. My educational background as well as working experience is a combination of commercial and technical practice, with emphasis on creating new business opportunities.

My brand building and communication skills have been very useful founding my company. With special focus on the Nordic markets, the strategy was to create consumer awareness about the “Danish Winery in Chile” using PR, resulting in frequent media appearances and finally a deal with Edeka Denmark.

At Danish multinational A.P. Moller Maersk and later E.M. Fiberglas, selling technical solutions, emphasize was on understanding customer needs and combining them with the technological possibilities. I have a good intuition of customer needs and see new opportunities in developing creative solutions.

Throughout my carrier I have a proven record of successful business development, always based on a well planned strategy. But although very strategic in my approach, I’m still a hands-on person that puts theory into practise. I’m experienced in taking over small as well as large accounts maximizing the business. However approaching new potential clients and convincing them is one of my absolute strongest sides.

I have proven my skills in business-, sales- and project management. For example in carrying out a strategic marketing plan for an entire company, implementing a new product line worth USD 3 mill, managing a research project involving universities as well as private companies or being prime mover in combing the Danish and Korean sales departments after a company takeover.

Throughout my career I have had leadership responsibility. I like the challenge of working with people and motivate them.

I’m fluent in English, German, Spanish and Danish.
