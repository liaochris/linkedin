Jerry Wells’s Summary

Starting in high school as a "student reporter" for KYW Newsradio and at my college radio station at Temple University, I've worked in broadcasting all my career, for a number of years at Philly's WDAS AM-FM
as radio newsman, morning DJ, and production director. I've interviewed numerous music stars, athletes, government officials and local celebrities of all types. For over 20 years, I've also been a freelance voiceover artist. Television and all types of personal appearances are also within my experience and I conceptualize, write, voice and produce all types of radio spots, promos and psa's. I'm presently the imaging voice of Philadelphia Smooth Jazz radio station 97.5 WJJZ, owned by Greater Media Corp. I voiced TV promos for the Philadelphia Eagles' 75th Anniversary Season (2007-08) and recently appeared as an"extra" on NBC's "30 Rock." I still seek full or part-time radio work and plan to continue and "grow" my freelance career while expanding my skills and network of colleagues. Let's create!
