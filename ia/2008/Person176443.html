Simon Lambert’s Summary

I have been a telecommunications engineer for over 25 years gaining a vast experience of many different military and commercial systems in projects all around the world, often in harsh environments. I have been involved at every stage from requirements capture to planning, deployment and managerial and technical support. My experience encompasses making sense of unstructured and chaotic situations and refocusing teams back onto the business goals, for which a flexible attitude and lateral mind is essential.
Currently I am in an international Business Development role for a start-up company in the commercial telecommunications industry.
Areas of expertise:
Project (PRINCE 2), programme and logistics management.
Technical Operations management.
Civilian and military radio, voice and data communications (HF,VHF,UHF,SHF, Satellite) planning and support.
Civilian and military transmission platforms (SDH, DWDM, Microwave) planning and support.

Simon Lambert’s Specialties:

Managing technical projects in challenging environments
Military and civilian liaison expertise
Convergence of military and commercial communications networks and systems
Telecommunications planning and troubleshooting
