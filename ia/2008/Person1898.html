Sean McNeal’s Summary

I have worked over twelve years directly in the electronic security industry. I currently work at Accent Alarms, Inc. (www.AccentAlarms.com) where we emphasize providing “Better Products, Services, and Prices”. We do this by being one of the first companies (the only one in Colorado) to offer our entire product line thought the internet, by not providing "boxed" solutions, and most importantly having a sincere interest in proving quality security solutions.

We have over 12,500 of the top video, security, and access control products available for secure/safe online purchase.

One of the largest challenges we face is educating buyers to overcome the general trend of believing that buying “security” through larger companies is the smart way to protect the people and things that are most important to you.

Would you say, "McDonald's has sold billions of hamburgers, so that they have the best hamburger." Of course you wouldn't. What you get with McDonald's is a familiarity of known product. Is that what you want with your security system? With that national company, how many employees, former employees, and general information is available about your security system? Do you want the same security system you watched on the Discovery Channels "To Catch a Thief" advertising to the general population how to defeat your alarm system?

I maintain a electronic security forum at www.AccentAlarms.com/forum. If you have any security related questions, experience in the industry, or even a security related story; please take a moment to partiticapte in our fourm.

I am also the moderator/creator of the LinkedIn/Yahoo group for US Naval Aircraft Carriers. I you are serving or served on any of the US Navy Carriers, please consider joining and participating. Join at: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/USNcarriers/
