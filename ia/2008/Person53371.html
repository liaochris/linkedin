Donnie MacNicol’s Summary

Project and team consultant who has married his 20 years of project delivery experience with a passion for personal development to offer consultancy and training services to clients through his own consultancy. Delivery of projects has been across a broad range of sectors including Construction, Telecommunication and eCommerce through working for leading consultancies.

Focus now on helping organisations adopt an appropriate project delivery culture and create an environment where teams can perform to their maximum potential. Regular speaker on the professional circuit presenting on areas such as project culture, branding and communication and is an experienced and sensitive facilitator, trainer and personal coach.

Donnie MacNicol’s Specialties:

Expert application of project and programme management to fit individual corporate cultures. Top talent development of project and programme managers. The branding and marketing of projects, programmes and project management.
