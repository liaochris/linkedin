Lukas Svoboda’s Summary

At heart a technology strategist and driven by an entrepreneurial spirit. My passion is using interesting technologies and frameworks to produce innovative and value-added results. I have focused on this at organisations I've worked for specifically around building practices and also in consulting engagements directly with customers.

Originally a software developer and system analyst/architect by training - I have been a people facing consultant and practice builder in various forms my entire career. I like to focus on specific tangible outcomes and work with large, complex problems spanning technology, people and process.

I enjoy doing but I am extremely passionate about managing, coaching and mentoring people in an outcome focused environment to produce outstanding results.

My current focus is on Governance frameworks around organisational structure, delivery practices and technology architecture - the notion of Strategic Architecture.
