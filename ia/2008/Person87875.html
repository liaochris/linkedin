Jeff Stone’s Summary

Bi-Lingual (German/English) sales and marketing professional with extensive experience establishing OEM, Distributor, Retailer and Consumer-Direct sales channels in Europe for American companies and in the US for German companies.

Jeff Stone’s Specialties:

Finding, evaluating, negotiating with and ramping-up channel partners in various industries such as Sporting Goods, Medical Device, Automotive and Polymers, at OEM, Retail, Distributor and Consumer Direct levels in Europe and the US/Canada. Fluency in German and English languages and cultural familiarity with, and experience in, most European countries and regions.
