Chris Griffiths’s Summary

I have worked in Residential Real Estate for almost twenty years. I started out in Residential Development, everything from sub-divisions and entitlements to project management.

I have held a California Real Estate license since 1991 (a Broker's license since 1999) and I have worked as a Mortgage Loan Officer and as a Realtor.

I have won numerous industry awards over the years and have been very involved in our local Multiple Listing Service (MLS) and Charitable Events for the Pasadena-Foothills Association of Realtors.

Chris Griffiths’s Specialties:

• Residential Sales
• Mortgage Financing
• Trust & Probate
• Property Management and Relocations.

My strong computer and communications skills mean that I am especially adept at helping out of town clients; in fact I have several clients who I have never actually met!
