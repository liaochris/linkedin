Simon Carter’s Summary

I help firms share know-how effectively with their clients.

Extensive experience of helping investment-banking firms and law firms to improve dialogue with their clients by sharing know-how more effectively.

Find out more at www.onethreefour.co.uk.

For example, I've helped investment-banks make their investment-research reports more client focused by having quick summaries upfront -- the heart of my work is helping firms to package-up content so that it meets clients' needs (as opposed to a mish-mash of what people inside the firm think is interesting, or think clients want).

Most projects involve: developing concepts based on existing materials and a review of competitor practice; testing and developing those concepts inside the firm and with clients. Finally, I help firms implement changes so that they can share know-how effectively with clients without my help -- this may include technical consultancy on automated MS Office templates or websites, and writing-and-structuring training.

Previous clients include two bulge-bracket investment banks I can't name for contractual reasons, Bank of Ireland, Citigroup, Clifford Chance, Davy (Irish stockbroker), Herbert Smith, Lovells, Practical Law Company, and Taylor Wessing.

Simon Carter’s Specialties:

Developing client-focused communication concepts, copy and messsage-structuring, client research, internal research and focus groups.
Advising firms on how to get the most out of technology by improving teh way that content is structured, written, and produced (includes websites, blogs, and automated MS Office templates).
Training lawyers and investment analysts to present their ideas more clearly.
Automated Word and PowerPoint document templates, XML-tagging of content and content re-use.
