Nishith Shah’s Summary

When I first put my hands on a computer in 1994, I had never thought that I would be making software for the next generation of computers within just one decade. Gradually my interest in IT industry turned into passion. I settled for the Masters in Computer Applications to nurture myself in the technical area. In past decade, my interest has grown as wide and deep as the IT industry itself. However, with passing time, I realized that I can't just limit myself to technical area. In the IT industry, there are certain perplex situations where decision making skills are tested thoroughly. Thus I decided to study MBA to polish my leadership and management potential and aptly utilize my abilities.

Enrolling in university as per my criteria drove me to Australia where I got a very good exposure to business skills. It was a bit difficult in beginning to understand the Aussie accent and hence I decided to involve myself in a role where I can interact with people very frequently and this was only possible in direct marketing or retail business. I accepted job as Customer Service Officer with Coles Group. Gradually my efforts drove me to my passion, that is IT industry. I was employed at Computing Australia where my task was to find new opportunities for strategic partnerships, outsourcing joint ventures, exploring new destinations for outsourcing, offshoring projects, project co-ordination. Being an Indian, it was a fantastic experience working in Australia. I got new experiences of working in cross-culture environment and diversified business class.

Due to some unfavourable circumstances, I returned back to India in early 2008 with a dream of working in diversified environment where I can get a similar kind of exposure. I met Nick and he welcomed me in his company and gave me a desired job profile. I am just 3 units away from MBA and on completion I will attain a Double Masters Degree Holder status. I wish to complete it before first quarter of 2009.
