Ayelet Noff’s Summary

I am a strategist in the world of social media marketing, community building, social software, and networking. I specialize in helping companies strategically market their brand on all relevant social platforms and enhance their product's offering. I consult companies on how to most effectively use social media tools in order to gain brand awareness, build online relationships, receive coverage in the blogosphere, improve product development, build an online community, and enhance the user experience.
In the past I worked at a few different startups and also served as ICQ’s Marketing and Promotions Manager for four years. I like to think of myself as a creative and “out of the box” thinker. I hold an MBA degree from Tel Aviv University, and a BA in Politics from Brandeis University.

Contact me if you’re interested in learning about the ways you can more effectively market your own brand online and reach your target audience using the correct social channels.
