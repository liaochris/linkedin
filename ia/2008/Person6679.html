Alex Faigel’s Summary

As the Family Dental Group Manager and former zTrace Technologies Inc. Vice President of Sales and Marketing, Alexander Faigel possesses superior management and marketing skills. He studied Entrepreneurship and Marketing at Babson College in Wellesley, Massachusetts in September 1994 and graduated with his dual degrees in May 1999. While in school, Alex Faigel was published in a successful case study still currently used in the Babson College M.B.A. program; he was also honored as a finalist in the Business Plan Competition. He became involved in athletics, as well: specifically tennis, running, and hang gliding. In college, Alex Faigel also founded a medical transcription company, which serviced medical offices in Greater Boston.

Alex Faigel currently manages the Family Dental Group of offices. As one of the largest dental corporations in Massachusetts, Family Dental Group provides high quality dental care to patients through their various locations. Alex Faigel oversees and manages the fourteen dental offices’ profits, new patient flow, delivery systems, location decisions, meetings, and future planning. He is also in charge of staff selection and evaluation.

As a company committed to its community, Family Dental Group also donates to Project Feed, which provides thousands of meals to needy school children in Rwanda through the United Nation World Food Program. Alex Faigel also generously gives to United Way, Greater Boston Food Bank, and UNICEF; he also volunteers as a tutor in his community.
