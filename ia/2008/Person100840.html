Justin Gaffney’s Summary

I am an experienced litigator in the niche market of employment disputes. I have brought resolution to almost every type of dispute that can occur in the workplace. This does not include Union disputes and Worker's Compensation.

I give practical business advice to small to medium business owners and I assist in shielding their personal assets from liability.

I am an experienced Real Estate Transaction attorney who has never had a closing die at the closing table. I can walk any client through this sometimes scary and confusing transaction.

I am a Real Estate Broker. I can list homes for people who want to pay me a commission for such service. I can show homes to prospective buyers of course for free as the seller has to pay the commission. I can list and show commercial properties to prospects as well. (I cannot be a client's attorney and Real Estate Broker on the same transaction).
