Jason Parrish’s Summary

Mr. Parrish brings a resourceful history of managing high-tech businesses with extensive experience as an entrepreneurial company builder. Most important, Mr. Parrish knows how to build a winning Team and get the job done. With 20 years of passion focused on technology and 11 years of executive management experience, Mr. Parrish and Team have had several past successes. They built an Internet Hosting Facility that was acquired by IXC/Broadwing for $3M. They pioneered the CRM market developing Customer Management and eCommerce software with the support of Austin Ventures, Origin Partners, Goldman Sachs and SAP Ventures. Recognized by Red Herring, Digital South, and KPMG for "Industry Leadership", the company was acquired by i2 Technologies for $94.5M. During his tenure with i2, the value of the acquisition rose to over $500M. They pioneered the Web Services and Portal market and were acquired by iSyndicate/Bertlesman for $35M. Having also served in Sales Management roles at three public companies, i2, Vitria and Motive, Mr. Parrish has a keen sense of what it takes to build a winning company based on value. Beyond the office, Mr. Parrish is active in the community and committed to his family.
