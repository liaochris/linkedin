Al Cannistra’s Summary

Real estate is an investment. Whether it is the home of your dreams, land, a ranch, a business or investment property, I feel a responsibility to make those dreams a reality. I pride myself on being knowledgeable and staying current with changes in the industry that will affect the success of your transaction.

My commitment to your satisfaction is the foundation from which a solid business relationship is built. Foremost is what is best for the client and building a long-term relationship based on trust.
