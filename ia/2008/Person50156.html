Derek Jenkins’s Summary

Results driven and goal oriented sales and business management executive with more than 13 years experience selling and managing event marketing programs associated with face-to-face events and integrated media properties including print, online, virtual programs, and custom marketing campaigns with a focus on the information technology industry.

Current experience with the VON Media Group includes strategy, execution, and revenue generation responsibilities across a vast portfolio of integrated media programs.

Current experience also includes the launch execution of the innagural VON Virtual Tradeshow which requires deep knowledge of the virtual marketing world and the production cycles and sales presentation strategies for all virtual event platforms including tradeshows, conferences, user groups, and corporate events.

Prior experience has also included business development, sales, and event management responsibilities for leading global IT associations:
SNIA (Storage Networking Industry Association)
AIIM ON Demand - annual AIIM Association event
Electronic Entertainment Expo (E3) - annual Entertainment Software Association event

Career experience has also included various event direction, sales management, and executive responsibilities for large non-association industry tradeshows and high level targeted conference programs.
