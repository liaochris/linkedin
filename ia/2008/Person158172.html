Rebecca Rivera’s Summary

I've been an advertising writer for 20 years at places like RPA, Team One, Anderson Lembke, McCann, Riney (when Hal was still there), DDB LA, TBWA\Chiat, Deutsch LA, AKQA, Y & R/Wunderman, Saatchi & Saatchi LA, Goldberg Moser O'Neill (RIP), eGreetings, the Designory, Hamon-Associates, Direct Partners, OIC, miller huber, Roxburgh, Fattal & Collins, PJA Advertising. And more.

Like most ad writers, I know a little about a lot of things and not much about any one subject.

Which explains how I can write almost as if I know what I'm talking about on the following subjects: cars, mortgages, biotech, beer, cruise ships, supplements, skin care, sneakers, perfume, tennis, coffee, tea, wine, the environment, the homeless, the life sciences, health care, hardware, software, bread, water, gambling, education, hotels, tires, entertainment, real estate, greeting cards, and piloting small aircraft.

I've applied my pea brain to these topics in interactive, print, TV, radio, design, and things you can stick on walls, in mailboxes, and in peoples' hands.

If you're still reading, you must be really bored or want to know where you can see my work. So now might be a good time to go to www.rebeccarivera.com.

Rebecca Rivera’s Specialties:

I can't believe you've gotten this far.

But since you have, I may as well remind you that I'm media agnostic and brand centric. I love working on the whole enchilada. That means writing everything from interactive, to broadcast, to print, to design, to direct, to packaging, to POP. You get the idea.

I'm also fond of pushing Creative Directors, Account folk and Clients past their comfort zone. Preferably without getting fired.
