Neil Howe’s Summary

I graduated with a Bachelors in Science and became a Physical Education and Health teacher. Although I enjoyed teaching and it was very rewarding, it did not pay enough to survive with a family.

In 2005, I retired from teaching and started venturing into the investment world. I cashed in my 401k and starting investing in real estate. It was very lucrative for the time and then the market became unstable.

I decided to start investing in other areas. I was introduced to several local business men who turned me on to some projects. I have my money working for me instead of working for it.

I am currently focusing on helping people raise money for their non profit organizations by making a product that everyone uses and needs available to them. Offering free gas will not only raise funds quickly, but it will be the only fundraiser the foundation will need.

I am always looking for new projects that will diversify my portfolio. I am open to any ideas. My ultimate goal is to do mission work in Scotland. I plan to open a youth center that will offer teens a safe haven.
