Tony Colafrancesco’s Summary

I have worked extensively in the video game industry for many years and have contacts at the higest levels with all of the major game industry publishers and developers. I also have vast experience in social networking, community building, partner management and negotiation.
Most recently I created the strategy and managed all gaming and entertainment related business for the AIM team, the largest messaging network worldwide numbering in the 100's of millions. I drove direction and strategy, and negotiated partnerships directly at the executive level.
In addition to my business role, I have previously served at the management and executive levels on the engineering side of the fence, so I have a strong technical understanding as well as business and strategy expertise.
There are also several successful startups that I have been involved in getting off the ground and becoming profitable.
Some of the recent accomplishments on the AIM team have been Turner Gametap, Open AIM, and the SCI partnership (Playlinc) which was recently acquired by EA.
I also have previous experience in the Graphic Arts and Digital Imaging fields.
Some of the companies I have worked at: America Online (AOL), Electronic Arts (EA), Wild Tangent, Xerox, Eastman Kodak, SGI (Silicon Graphics), Escend Technologies.

I have also spent several years in software development, mainly focusing on creating full development and testing processes, including source management, defect tracking, manual and automated testing, and lab building. My main focus was more on the Quality Assurance and testing area, and also direct interaction with partners and customers to define requirements and the need to drive products to be the most competitive in the market.

Game design and development has also been a passion of mine. I have been directly involved in the design and development of several top rated titles such as Ultima Online, The Sims, Jane's USAF, Triple Play 2000, and many more.
