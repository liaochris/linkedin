Jim Mitchel’s Summary

In the last five years, I started making a push to get a comic story published. My first published story was in Chimaera Studios' “Shocking Gun Tales” (http://www.cellardoorpublishing.com/previews.htm) where I had a seven page story drawn by Freddie E. Williams (http://www.freddieart.com/), who is now the artist on Robin for DC comics. Next, I had a 48 page one-shot comic entitled “Chance of a Lifetime” (http://www.cellardoorpublishing.com/previews.htm) published, which Freddie E. Williams also drew. After that I co-wrote, with George Singley, “Super-Crazy TNT Blast!” which was published by the now defunct Speakeasy comics.

At the end of 2007 and into 2008 I'm co-writing three mini-series for Chimaera Studios' Arcana imprint.
