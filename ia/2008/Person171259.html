Scott Miller’s Summary

My role at Skype includes company strategy and managing global business development and strategic partnerships. Skype strategic partnerships focus on (A) distributing Skype software through partners such as Google Pack, Toshiba, etc., (B) integrating Skype functionality into third-party products such as MySpaceIM with Skype, Salesforce.com, etc., (C) incorporating third-party products into the Skype software, (D) revenue generating partnerships such as Google Toolbar, FREE411, etc., (E) marketing and promoting Skype through partnerships with Intel, Oprah Winfrey, etc., and (F) building innovative products both on and off Skype.

Feel free to send partnership proposals to me through Linked In. And I am always looking to hire outstanding business development and account managers.

Before Skype, my ideal role was with an early-stage high-tech company which:
• Has initial and scalable product that requires various types of partnerships to maximize distribution and revenue;
• Has impressive management team with solid tech experience and high-caliber pedigree, energy and business sophistication;
• Has less than 50 employees;
• Has already received first round of VC funding;
• Is looking for aggressive, experienced, business and people-wise, well connected, team oriented, ready-to-go individual to:
* help shape company strategy
* develop partnerships and ventures for product distribution and revenue,
* pitch VC's for funding, and
* build and implement marketing programs.
