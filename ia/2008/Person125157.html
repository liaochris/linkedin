Matt has over 10 years of professional experience including consulting and a number of senior corporate roles in leading telecommunications organisations.

Matt’s 6 years of experience working for Telecoms covers business management and operations including roles with responsibility for major account sales, specialist sales, general management, telecommunications strategy, business development, marketing, product strategy and development, planning, project management, channel marketing, and business analysis. Matt has also worked on major transformational programs building new businesses and operational departments. Most recently Matt has lead and completed an AUS$ xxxMil, 2 and ½ year transformational program that established a replacement end to end triple play consumer business for the third largest Telecommunications carrier in Australia, AAPT.

Matts 4 years consulting experience, both with a global consulting firm and as an independent consultant, includes roles covering presales, OSS/BSS evaluation, tender development, business case development, business process design, requirements definition, executive and end user presentations, detailed design documentation, end user training and representation on the governing industry forum for all sizes of corporations from start-ups to multinationals. Matt’s most successful engagement was delivering an end to end Telco platform and operational capability for a start up Microwave broadband provider (Broadband Philippines) in the Philippines in 60 days, at a cost of US$ xxMil in professional services.

Matt’s roles have included Business Analyst, Senior Business Analyst (VOIP, e-Commerce & e-billing), IT Consultant, New Media & Communications in Manilla Philippines, Product Manager e-applications, Group Product Leader – Voice, Mobile, VOIP & VAS, Head of Online & Channels and Head of Virtual Solutions in Sydney, Australia.
