John Taylor’s Summary

My background is a mix of business and IT, which enables me to manage business, marketing and technical teams on eCommerce, eBusiness and eMarketing projects.

For the last five years I’ve been working with a number of companies and organisations to help them use the internet as a channel to support their business strategy and company growth.

John Taylor’s Specialties:
