Emre Ersahin’s Summary

Almost 18 years of progressive marketing career starting at an int’l ad agency & reinforced at a multinational FMCG company. Always pushing the envelope in marketing technology, led the 1st local Internet advertising company. Self-motivated, benevolent entrepreneur co‐founded the 1st youth marketing agency in the Eurasia Region. Recently joined Turkcell team for managing content services in VAS department.

An expert on adding-value, keen to pursue innovative international marketing practice building on brand management, marketing communications, web 2.0 and GSM knowledge. Now better equipped to tackle the future of marketing with recently developed skills in sociometry, influencer networks and creating & launching web mash‐ups.

Emre Ersahin’s Specialties:

New specialties such as social networks and mash-ups on top of the previous marketing strategy, youth marketing, advertising, brand management, guerrilla & viral marketing, mobile gadgets, trends & insights, brand extension specialties.

Also founder of the ReklamYAZILARI, a discussion platform of the Turkish marketing community with 1.400+ members.
