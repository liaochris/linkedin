Georg von Mérey’s Summary

My professional experience until now focuses on international agriculture, as I have spent several months in North-American, South-American, African and several European countries, in farms or research facilities. My current research project on maize biology follows in this internationally oriented perspective, as it includes several stays in Mexico. My professional goal is in strategy of global players within the agribusiness, upstream or downstream of the farms, be it input suppliers (Agrochemical industry, seeds industry, extension services, farming technology, etc.) or traders, processors and marketers of the food industry, where I can make my wide knowledge about several markets useful.
