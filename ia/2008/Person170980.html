Scott Gavin’s Summary

Applied Trends helps organisations of any size to leverage web2.0 tools and culture. We consult on the applied use of web2.0 tools and social media in a business context, otherwise known as Enterprise 2.0.

Scott has built up a reputation and following through his efforts as an 'Enterprise 2.0 Evangelist' and often speaks at industry events. Scott also authored the presentation 'Meet Charlie'. Go on, Google it if you havn't seen it already.

Services -
* Project Management – from classic project management to social software/web2.0 implementations
* Change Management – assisting the adoption of new tools and processes
* Training – tailored training for departments and project teams in the use of social software/web2.0 tools
* Applied Usage Consulting – identification of the right tools and approaches for teams and departments looking to utilise social software/web2.0 tools
* Guidance and mentoring – helping teams get the most out of new ways of working
* User Interface Design – utilisation of web2.0 design approaches to foster adoption and usability
* Content Management – assistance and mentoring in submitting content to social software/web2.0 tools
* Promotion and Communication – Creation of material to launch or communicate the value of social software/web2.0 tools
