Victoria Generao’s Summary

Currently working with a plaintiff/personal injury law practice (medical and legal malpractice case management). Recently consulted on human resources management, administrative procedures and contracts administration concerns for a creative inventor's coop company.

My past law work experience includes criminal prosecution and jury trial experience with the Solano County District Attorney's Office in Vallejo, CA; experience handling and litigating domestic violence issues arising in family law matters in San Francisco. I have been commissioned as a California Notary Public since 1979.

I am a practicing Ordained Minister and Certified Reiki Master-Teacher focused on providing individualized counseling as well as offering ministerial services. I specialize in work-related stress, womens’ issues and depression. I am interested in academic work as well, and perhaps advising corporations on metaphysical-mental health issues.

Victoria Generao’s Specialties:

Grassroots community organizer; activist and fundraiser for non-profits specializing in education and human services; public relations; networking; communication (oral and written).
