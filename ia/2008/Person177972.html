Mattias Söderhielm’s Summary

Entrepreneur in the Internet industry since 1994. Founded or co-founded three of Sweden's largest Internet companies: Passagen.se (Sweden's largest web site at the time), Framfab (Sweden's largest Internet Professional Services company at the time) and Bredbandsbolaget (Sweden's second largest broadband ISP).
