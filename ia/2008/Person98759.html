Jerzy POTOCKI’s Summary

15 years of experience in Search & Selection and other HR Consulting fields (AIMS, IDEA! Management Consulting, Watson Wyatt, ADP), specialised in high quality Executive Search and Assessment. Also consulting in BPR and IT projects. Working mostly with international Clients, managing the Polish office of AIMS International.

AIMS International has recently been ranked the biggest Executive Search organisation in the world and in Europe, with over 90 offices in over 45 countries worldwide. Our geographic expansion in the last few months includes new Partners in: Argentina, Croatia, India, Korea, Mexico, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Ukraine, United Arab Emirates and Venezuela. To read more about AIMS you are invited to visit us at http://www.aims.pl (AIMS Polska) and http://www.aims-international.net (international).
