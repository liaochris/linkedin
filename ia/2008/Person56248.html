Eddie Sutton’s Summary

Idea and development guy that is running his own company that creates websites for small business while creating a growing business of publishing online webzines. With experience in the development of interactive media that began in the late '80s and a laser focus on the internet since the early days, the online audience is a group I know how to reach. The greatest charge in my day is witnessing how the audience responds to online content that is published on websites under my leadership. That's when it's really fun.

  • Art Director

    AOL

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; TWX; Internet industry)

    December 1998 — December 2002 (4 years 1 month)

    Art Director for the Publishing Group of the Studio@AOL. My role included working with VPs, editorial staff, software engineers and graphic designers on a daily basis to develop strategy for story ideas, publishing plans with final development and launch of the live content.

The busiest channel, AOL News, required the publishing of daily feature articles. Of all the graphic design teams in the AOL organization, the team under my direction earned a reputation as that most capable of providing a quick response with complete success in meeting the business objectives.
