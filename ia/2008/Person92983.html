Joanne Bartone Photographer’s Summary

I am an international art photography award-winning photographer through WIPI (Women In Photography International). I also am an international award-winning wedding photographer through WPJA (Wedding Photojournalism Association), an exclusive international organization that represents select wedding photojournalists all over the world. Published in national magazines such as Towne & Country, The Knot and Wondertime, and local magazines such as Whirl, Metropolitan and Pittsburgh Magazine, I have been voted one of the best wedding photographers in Pittsburgh (2006-2007).

Having had the honor to photograph many world-renowned researchers, entertainers, authors, poets, play-writes, musicians and social/political figures, my celebrity photography has been published in newspapers and magazines and my commercial work has been published on CDs, in annual reports and internationally in European Union publications.

I believe strongly in paying my success forward by mentoring other photographers through national professional photography forums, through private mentoring sessions and through my seminars and workshops where my encouragement and expertise in business, photography and design are praised and sought after.

It is my goal to provide my clients with the highest quality photographic imagery, services and products and to build lasting relationships with my clients.
