René Savelberg’s Summary

Experience
I have experience in Tourism, ICT, Automotive, Mobility, Retail, B2B services, Industrial Markets, Car Park industry. During my career I worked with agencies as well as corporate organisations. My specialty is the development and execution of marketing - sales campaigns for market entries, product launches and campaigns to boost sales. I am familiar with building brands and online and offline market channels.


Approach
My approach to projects is driven by market requirements. Based on my experience in various industries I can quickly identify market opportunities and develop new concepts to put them to use for my clients. In consultation with the management I develop the market approach, after which I take responsibility for the execution of the plan, leading and guiding the client’s team along the way. My style is pragmatic with a hands on attitude. Plans and execution are based on business cases and ROI models which enables you to keep track of progress and achievement of objectives.
