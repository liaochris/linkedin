Khaidzir Abdul Rasip’s Summary

Khaidzir is a Chartered Valuation Surveyor by profession and has had 20 years of working experience covering the private, public and corporate sectors. Graduated in 1988 from University of Glamorgan, United Kingdom, he started his professional training in 1986 in London with an international firm of property consultants and upon graduation, with one of the London Boroughs.

Upon returning to Malaysia in 1990, Khaidzir joined reputable property consultancy firms including Jones Lang Wootton and gained further experience in the area of valuation, market and feasibility study, estate agency and property management. He then left private practice for the corporate world involved in the whole spectrum of property development and was responsible for development planning, business development, marketing and sales and asset management with conglomerates such as Johor Corporation, Sime Darby, Golden Hope and UEM World.

A professional member of the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors, United Kingdom and the Institution of Surveyors, Malaysia; Khaidzir is also a recognized figure within the industry having been very active with other associations such as FIABCI Malaysia and Persatuan Pengurusan Kompleks (PPK) Malaysia.

Khaidzir has been with TH Properties since July 2006 as the Assistant General Manager, Customer Solutions Division in charge of marketing, sales and customer care.
