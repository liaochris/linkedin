Todd M. Lee’s Summary

Account Executive for training, consulting, and communications company providing a wide range of services including educational and performance improvement program design & development, program conversion to web-based, instructor-led, virtual classroom, CD/DVD, and mobile device delivery, organizational development initiatives and professional certifications in safety, quality, and employee involvement, and corporate work/life and concierge services.

Founder and President of home improvement company specializing in the design and construction of custom kitchens, baths, and basements and offering the unique service of personal DIY coaches for remote or in-home project training and guidance. Committed to quality and focused on exceeding even the most discerning client's expectations, the company motto says it all, "your home is your castle and we treat your castle like our home."

Former manager of midwest operations for Global professional services and software company focused on development, implementation, and support services in the areas of Performance Solutions, Enterprise Learning, and Applied Technologies.

Former internal controls analyst for multi-national consumer goods company developing risk models for licensing agreements, performing analysis of third-party contract compliance, and conducting internal audits of company controls.

Former national training coordinator for leading full-service, casual dining restaurant concept operating more than 80 company and franchised locations worldwide.
