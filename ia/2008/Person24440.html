GUIDANCE FOR CONNECTING TO MY LINKEDIN NETWORK

Please read the following so we can ensure our networking will be of benefit to all of us:

1. I enjoy connecting people to great resources and other people who can assist in achieving their professional goals. Please link to my network - I try to respond to emails within 48 hours.

2. I always accept invitations from friends, colleagues, and clients to join my network on LinkedIn.

3. If we haven’t met I'm open to networking, but please send an introductory note providing a little information about your background and how we would both benefit from linking - a standard LinkedIn request to connect won’t likely be responded to.

4. I consider the connections in my network to be personal recommendations. Therefore, I want to ensure a quality connection for all concerned. I will not usually forward requests to connect you to members of my LinkedIn network if I do not know you and your work.

5. For introduction requests, if you send a thoughtful and detailed request regarding the benefit to the person in my network and why they would benefit in connecting to you I, in return, will promise to only send you relevant, well written requests.
