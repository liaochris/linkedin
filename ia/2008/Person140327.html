Nicholas Kho’s Summary

I grew up in Sacramento and then moved to the East Coast to go to boarding school in New Jersey at Lawrenceville School. Had great fun afterwards at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. And then spent a few years traveling the world with a small group of friends. Finally, we moved to Hollywood together into a mansion in the Hollywood Hills we named Project Hollywood Mansion. We transformed the former Dean Martin/Rat Pack Mansion into my office, where I build a global dating coaching company. We now operate in over 30 countries and 100 cities. I've moved out of the mansion, yet I still travel the world, and love living the Young Hollywood lifestyle. (http://www.nicholaskho.com or http://www.nickkho.com).

I had my first job at 12 years old, where I passed out flyers for a popular nightclub in Sacramento, and would be allowed backstage to hangout with the rock stars. I then spent my next half dozen summers as an intern for various companies: Intel, Senators, Governors, PaineWebber, Foley&Lardner, Fritz/UPS, NASA. While in college, I became a financial advisor for Waddell & Reed and World Financial Group. Then, I spent 2 years as a research associate for NASA and did zero-gravity experiments and graduated from NASA Academy. Although excited about my science projects, I became more excited about the nightlife industry and began promoting parties and special events for 2 years for Playboy Magazine as a marketing representative out of their College Marketing Office in New York. Although a lot of fun, I left my former job, and became an entrepreneur by founding Real Social Dynamics (http://www.realsocialdynamics.com).
