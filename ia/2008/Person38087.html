Clare Griffiths’s Summary

Owner and principal solicitor of boutique intellectual property and media law firm 'Be.', with offices in Soho and Brighton. The firm has an enviable client list including tv and film production companies, animators, musicians and labels, artists and designers, technology and software businesses and a growing focus on film work.

Be.undertakes the full spectrum of IP work, including patent litigation, trade mark and design filing, copyright clearance and dispute work. The firm is currently doing battle against British American Tobacco on behalf TANK Magazine (see the case develop: http://www.guardian.co.uk/uk/2008/jan/16/smoking.books and http://www.designweek.co.uk/liChannelID/1/Articles/137548/Tank+resists+challenge+from+BAT.html).

Film credits include production and international distribution legals for SCENES OF A SEXUAL NATURE (2006) starring Ewan McGregor, Sophie Okonedo and Catherine Tate; SOUTH COAST THE MOVIE (2007) (selected for Helsinki and Raindance film festivals in 2007); GUCA (2006); O ZELADOR (2007); IN BETWEEN SPACE (post-production); RAPTURE (pre-production); BARAFUNDLE BAY (feature film, pre-production); ROUNDHEADS AND CAVALIERS (pre-production).

Recently attached as Executive Producer and Production Lawyer for a major independent US/UK co-production - THE LAUGHTER CLINIC (2009) - a political comedy feature due to commence principal photography in New York in Summer 2008 with ED BLUM attached as Director/Producer, PAUL PROVENZA (The Aristocrats) as Producer/Co-writer and DAN PASTERNACK as co-writer.

Also owner and Managing Director of The Ideasafe (www.theideasafe.com), an online safe and witness service for creative work and confidential concepts.

Clare Griffiths’s Specialties:

Intellectual property law, film and tv, media law, trade mark and design protection, mediation, litigation.
