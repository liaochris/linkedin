Roberto Mayer’s Summary

I founded MBI at the end of 1990. Since then, I've led the company into continuosly building one of the biggest databases containing useful market information covering both the IT industry and their clients all over Latin America and Portugal.

We help our clients to succeed on the marketplace, by providing them with existing information, new information we develop on-demand, market research and analysis about their target markets.

I myself often act as a speaker, covering both research results, market insights and trends, political issues on IT and many related themes.

Over the years, I've also written some books (all in Portuguese), the most recent one covering the optimization of relational databases.

I've always been a networker (much before 'social networking' started on the Internet). If you want to meet me face-to-face in a networking environment, I suggest you have a look at Assespro events (www.assespro-sp.org.br) or at www.comunidadedainovacao.com.br, which is a multidisciplinar community oriented towards innovation.

Roberto Mayer’s Specialties:

Market research and database marketing for and about the IT industry, based on huge databases (covering many tenth of millions of people, categorized by address, city, country and research data collected over almost two decades of work).
