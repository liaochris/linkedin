Evelyn So’s Summary

I am a digital veteran who helps companies attract, maintain and grow dialogues with their customers.

For 16 years, I have delivered products and services that users/consumers notice, buy, love, keep and recommend.

As the Founder and President of Noesium Consulting Inc., here’s my story:

IN A NUTSHELL:
Bridging Customer Experience, Marketing and Business practices using the Right Technology at the Right Time at the Right Cost.

APPROACH:
An Integrated Framework based on the ability to “speak” technology, consumer, business, operation and marketing.

TRACK RECORD:
Various innovations that are today’s necessities: Interactive Web entities, Internet Banking, Video on Demand, Interactive TV, Broadband services, Adgaming, etc.

INDUSTRIES:
My consumer-centric approach has been successfully applied across many industries: telco, financial, technology, marketing, healthcare, lifestyle, to name a few.

TOOLS:
My toolbox is always expanding to meet market needs. Core tools include: Business Processes, Competitive Analysis, Market Landscape Audit, Consumer Research, Digital Strategy, Product Development (Usability, Information Architecture, Interactive Design), and Marketing Communications.
