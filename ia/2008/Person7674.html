Alisha Tillery’s Summary

Experienced public relations professional and freelance journalist specializing in media relations and features journalism.

I have worked as a public relations manager for a nonprofit for nearly three years, providing media relations for events such as the Tyson Foods/TV One kick off for the annual Southern Heritage Classic, National Hunger Awareness Day and Hurricane Katrina emergency outreach featured on the Oprah Show.

It is my goal to provide positive public relations to organizations, persons, groups according to their needs and goals. I believe in building long-lasting relationships to produce outcomes.

Additionally, my freelance work has been featured in Vibe Vixen Online, Honey Magazine Online, Clutch Magazine, N'Digo, Grace Magazine and The Fashion Bomb blog. I look forward to partnering with you to provide my writing skills for publications.

Alisha Tillery’s Specialties:

Public relations: media releases, pitches, feature stories, media kits, media placement (print, electronic, broadcast), blogger relations

Freelance journalism: editorials, features, music, fashion, lifestyle, culture
