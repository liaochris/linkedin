Sarah Drake’s Summary

I have focused my career in the financial services industry on building business relationships and managing cash flows and securities settlements for a group of wholesale banks. I am an enthusiastic team leader known for my ability to undertake large-scale programs and see them to completion.

During my many years with the Federal Home Loan Bank System, one of the world's largest issuers of debt securities and one of the nation's largest sources of funds for home mortgages, I have held various staff and leadership positions with the System's central funding office - from business analyst to division director. I have broad experience in developing, implementing and managing process solutions.

I hold a graduate certificate in Dispute Resolution and am a certified mediator. I have been deeply committed to community service in a variety of roles. In this next phase of my working life, I seek a position that combines social impact with personal meaning. The areas of home ownership and women's issues are of particular interest to me.

Financial services operations, securities settlement, funds transfers, business process development, customer service, community development, mediation, negotiation
