Mike Duffy’s Summary

Over a decade as a director including roles as Chairman, Managing Director, Finance Director and Non Executive Director with both profit orientated and not for profit organisations. I have recently qualified from the Institute of Directors and in the process of being appointed a Chartered Director.

Sales & Marketing engagements with Cable & Wireless, Kodak, Mercury Communications, Philips, Pitney Bowes, Racal, Siemens, Timeplex. Qualified with a Diploma in Marketing from the Chartered Institute of Marketing.

Strong Technical skill set from my my B.Sc. (Hons) in Computer Science and Industrial Management followed by two decades of working within the Hi-Tech Industries including Voice and Data Networking, Security, Computers and Applications, Electronic Registration, RFID and others.

Legal and Commercial expertise of Technology within the PFI/PPP areana. Experience of both large multinational corporations as well as start up businessess.
