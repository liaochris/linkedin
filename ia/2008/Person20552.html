Brian Gray’s Summary

I am the Head of Reference and the Engineering, Mathematics, & Statistics Librarians at Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. I am teaching a new workshop called Web 2.0 in Libraries at Kent State University School of Library & Information Science.

I am web coordinator for the Library Administration & Management Association (LAMA) of ALA (American Libraries Association). I am introducing web 2.0 tools and methods of collaboration and sharing.

In the past, I have taught information literacy classes for Bryant & Stratton College in downtown Cleveland.
