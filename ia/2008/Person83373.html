James Adams’s Summary

I have a very diverse professional background. I had an electronics background going through college as a manager/salesman for Circuit City, Sears and Radioshack. I started my own business installing audio/video components in-home. After graduating college I worked my way up the ladder at Enterprise Car Sales, from a salesman, to finance manager all the way to a Level 3 position-Area Car Sales Manager. I was one of the youngest to reach that position at age 25, grossing $12-15 million a year. From there I went into the real estate industry at Fremont Investment & Loan where I saw a lot of success and a lot of money. Unfortunately with the market shift, loans suffered and ultimately Fremont closed its Wholesale Real estate division. From there I moved to Boulder, CO to work in the payment industry with a start-up company called FASTLANE Secure Payments. After only 4 months of working there and building the sales/marketing department from the ground up, they also closed their doors. Since then I moved back to CA and started doing what I was built to do. Marketing. Since July of 2007 I have been working for RSI Roofing as the Director of Marketing and Business Development.
