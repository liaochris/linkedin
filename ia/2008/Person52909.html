Dom DeBellis’s Summary

I am focused on entrepreneurship and leadership development. I enjoy looking at new ideas and solving problems with creativity and teamwork. My passion is developing people. Of particular interest to me is the care and feeding of leaders, because they, in turn, serve and support so many.

I am committed to truth and integrity operate at the foundation of any workplace or lifestyle community.

My work is inseparable from my life because it is truly my calling, my vocation.

I believe community is based upon commitment, not compliance if it is to thrive.

I am actively seeking new associates who want to join the exciting culture of participation, value, contribution, and cooperation that we're building in our company. We're creating new business owners, not just employees who draw a paycheck, but entrepreneurs who build and control their own future. I am not satisfied with an enterprise unless each person has a stake and derives value from the experience.

Are you like-minded? Do you share similar values? Are you seeking opportunities? Drop me a line and invite me to connect with you; I'm open to linking as widely as possible.
