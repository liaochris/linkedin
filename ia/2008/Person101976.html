Kapil Khandelwal’s Summary

Kapil brings unique operations and consulting experience of connecting the healthcare and life sciences value chain from early drug discovery, drug development, care delivery, healthcare providers and intermediaries and payors. He brings over 15 years of industry and consulting experience and specializes in creating business strategy, process and operations improvement, multi-site services delivery for healthcare and life sciences, outsourcing and transformational models, disease management and modelling, consumer genomics and personalized healthcare, technology and innovation.
