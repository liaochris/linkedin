My keen interest in space has given me the opportunity to participate in many interesting projects (student rocket, student satellite, parabolic flight +++). Christina Aas’s Experience

  • SPEX (Spectropolarimeter for Planetary EXploration) team member

    SRON

    (Government Agency; 201-500 employees; Research industry)

    August 2007 — Present (6 months)

    For my internship I am part of a project team developing a spectropolarimeter for looking at reflected solar flux through the Martian atmosphere. We include polarization in the measurements to gain a better understanding of the microphysical properties (size, shape, composition) of the dust aerosols in the atmosphere of Mars. My job consists of performing radiative transfer calculations using the adding-doubling method by de Haan et al. (1987). This is done by programming in Fortran 77 and IDL.
