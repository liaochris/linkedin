Terry Reynolds’s Summary

Programme, Business Change and IT specialist with skills honed at director level in Blue Chip, Business start-up, and SME organisations. Able to define strategy, lead multifunctional teams, projects and change programmes. Experienced in the Retail / Distribution / Foodservice / Telecommunications / Gaming / Leisure / Manufacturing / IT Shared Service sectors.

> 6 years as Consultant / Programme Director for clients including 3663 First for Foodservice, Centrica, Alfred McAlpine, BAE Systems, Hughes Network Systems and English Golf Union – specialising in IT centric business change programmes.

> 7 years as VP, Director of IT & Distribution for US owned (TJX Inc) retailer TK Maxx from start-up to £350m turnover – developed and delivered IT systems and logistics services to enable rapid business growth into 3 countries and achievement of a turnover of £350m.

> 6 years as Technical Director (CTO) for Cap Gemini / Granada Group – developed and delivered solutions and IT Shared Services to internal and external businesses in the Retail, Hospitality, Travel, IT and Broadcasting sectors.
