Dawn Stott’s Summary

At some point during my life's work, I realized that everything is customer service. Even now, here, with you. I am serving up to you either an entertaining view into my career experience, or a window into the wisdom gathered from my journey. Perhaps both. Ideally I am providing you with a wholly new and fresh experience; something you'll never forget. That is the ultimate goal of a customer service experience: to carve out a few moments in time to connect, grasp, share, and co-create a mutually beneficial, pleasant solution to that which requires resolution.

My customer service experience began as a teenager helping children learn how to swim and guiding horseback rides over hillside trails of the East Bay. After studying psychology, fine art, and dance in college, I gained on-the-job training in restaurant, cafe, and clothing retail. Employing my customer relations skills, I landed an entry level position with a law office.

I spent approximately 10 years working off and on in legal environments including real estate law, insurance defense, trademark, bankruptcy, litigation, personal injury, and estate planning. I temped my way back through college where I studied multimedia and interaction technologies with a focus on graphic design and typography. My favorite hobby is digital photography.

Other interests include music, digital art, and dog training.

Dawn Stott’s Specialties:

Restaurant opening and management. Vendor selection and relations. Menu development, including portioning and costing. Purchasing. Procedures documentation. Staffing. Accounts payable and receivable. Research and writing.
