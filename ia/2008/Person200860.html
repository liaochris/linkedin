Pete Woodhouse’s Summary

I have been in product development leadership positions for technology-based companies for 19 years. I know the view from both sides of the R&D to Operations transition and I know how to get that transition right, time and time again.

In my current position at PRTM, I help clients improve the performance of their product development processes and organizations to bring new products from R&D into production, improving R&D effectiveness, productivity, time to market and time to profit.

I am an expert in the processes of product development, product data management, product delivery, portfolio management, PLM and supply chain. I understand the benefits and drawbacks of using software systems to automate processes. I have led implementations of many enterprise software products for product development (PLM, CAD, CASE) and operations (ERP).

Prior to joining PRTM, I was Director of Quality Engineering for Agilent Technologies, leading an organization with responsibility for engineering tools and processes across the corporation. Part of this role included complete responsibility for the product development portion of an Oracle 11i deployment and full enterprise deployment of the MatrixOne PLM solution. I also held various management and engineering positions at Hewlett-Packard. I have managed large, multinational hardware and software product development and support teams.

I have a BS Degree in Electronics Engineering from Plymouth University in the United Kingdom and an MBA from Santa Clara University. I have CPIM certification from APICS.
