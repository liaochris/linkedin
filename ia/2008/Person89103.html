Jennifer Weir’s Summary

I recently moved to a new life in South Texas on the Gulf Coast and am now working from home for two companies -- Global Representation LLC and Globalquest Solutions Inc.
Life is good!! When I'm not working or just feel like taking a break, I can go work in my all-season garden or go fishing or to the beach. Best of all, I'm able to spend a lot of time with my husband Karl and two children - Max (9) and Maggie (7). Last week we fished on our boat in the gulf and spent an hour playing with some dolphins who came to visit. What could be better?
