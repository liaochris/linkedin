Tawanda Thompson’s Summary

Tawanda Thompson is seasoned human resources professional with over 15 years of in-depth experience in the development and delivery of Human Resources programs tied to federal and state regulatory compliance. She is an accomplished Human Resources Executive with experience in some of the world’s leading corporations including AlliedSignal/Honeywell Inc. and Volt Services Group. Adept at addressing the diverse needs of sizeable organizations, managing teams as large as 52 and supporting client groups with over 10,000 employees and $10+ Billion in annual revenue.

Tawanda Thompson’s Specialties:

Experience includes AAP development, EEO investigations and programs, HR Function Audits and Assessments (compliance, efficiency and business alignment), HR due diligence and merger integration activities, sourcing and recruiting of high-demand, scarce talent, job evaluation/classification programs, market pricing analysis, salary structure development, salary administration guidelines, job descriptions, performance management systems, retention programs and employee relations.
