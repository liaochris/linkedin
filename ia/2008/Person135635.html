Morten Sissener’s Summary

Entrepreneurial engineering executive with 25 years of diverse experience in the development, financing, construction and operation of independent power generation projects including solar, wind, landfill gas, natural gas, coal and petroleum coke. Director/COO/CEO of multiple startups. Direct experience in team building and leadership, managing large capital projects, technology development, project financing, and sales and marketing. Strong technical apptitude combined with pragmatic business acumen, excellent communication skills and a habit of taking on tough challenging projects.

Am open to considering advisory, consulting, special projects and possible full-time engagement in renewables/green energy, technology development, and financing.
