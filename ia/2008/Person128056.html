Melissa Devitt’s Summary

Park Place Title, Inc. is a full-service residential and commercial real estate title insurance and closing company. We have offices in Orlando, Clermont, and Jupiter and can assist with transactions anywhere in the State of Florida and in 36 additional states. We are licensed agents of LandAmerica (Commonwealth) and also Ticor Title Insurance. We specialize in flexing with the current market. That means everything from short sales to commercial closings and everything in between.

Melissa Devitt’s Specialties:

As the Marketing Director, I am charged with the responsibility of maintaining our existing client base and developing new business prospects. Through branding, website design, e-newsletters, and tons of face-to-face time at various networking events, I have established our identity as a prominent local title company. The best part about my job is being able to make connections and assist others in building upon their success.
