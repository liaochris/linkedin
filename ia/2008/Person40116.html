Peter Van Wijnaerde’s Summary

My passion goes out for everything on a screen. I'm a television obsessed internet addict with a soft spot for gadgets like mobile phones (if you still call them phones).

Because I started out as a community builder I've always kept all my work user/consumer centered. Community building doesn't have a lot of secrets for me anymore either, this helps when I'm working on eCRM projects.

Working in advertising made me aware of what's hot and what's not but I don't let it get in the way of a good strategy. I like to keep things down to earth and realistic.
A creative way to handle logic is my way of working.

I always worked online but my ambition is bigger, Internet is fun but when is it combined with other media the fun just starts. That's why I want to build bridges between different media. I honestly think the future is in bridging...

Mobile phones and everything you can do with it also has no secrets for me. I worked 1 year in Holland working on products for mobile entertainment and playing with it on the brand new (in that time) 3G network. And while working for These Days with Nokia as the big client I was the key reference when it came to these lovely gadgets.
