Michael Fitzgerald, CPA/PFS, CFP, MST’s Summary

Fitzgerald Financial Partners, LLC looks beyond the traditional financial advisory services and believes in being innovative by using cutting edge technology and investment ideas to help meet our clients' long-term needs, goals, and objectives. We seek to establish long-term, multi-generational relationships with our clients to help them live the one life they have.

Our innovative and technologically advanced system offers in-depth solutions to our clients' personal needs and goals, through the use of institutional investment practices, with real face-to-face advisor/client interactions. We want to be there when you need us most.

From our personal experience, sales commissions too often drive financial advice. It is very rare to find a source of financial advice that is truly impartial, truly objective, and puts the clients’ interests ahead of their own, as well as works to build a long-term relationship based on high quality services, integrity and trust. We at Fitzgerald Financial Partners, LLC want to be your trusted advisor and help you and your family live a great life.

Fitzgerald Financial Partners, LLC's full range of advisory, portfolio management, and financial services has assisted many realtors, active professionals, entrepreneurs, executives, and families too busy with demands of their careers and lifestyles to reach many of their financial goals. We know that more than ever families do not have the excess time to spend together, we want to help you with the burden of managing your financial future.

Michael Fitzgerald, CPA/PFS, CFP, MST’s Specialties:

Michael is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), and is accredited as a Personal Financial Specialist (PFS) by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants, and a Certified Financial Planner (CFP) licensee.

Michael is especially knowledgeable in the issues facing Real Estate professionals and families. Michael's specialty in education planning is complemented by his knowledge of tax planning, investment allocation and retirement accumulation planning.
