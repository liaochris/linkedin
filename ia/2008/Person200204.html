William Shiflett’s Summary

I am a senior professional with a Master of Public Administration degree and broad experience in government administration. I have worked in local government and higher education for 20 years and have unique executive management experience – I was a member of the executive management team who created the Utah Department of Technology Services. I have reported directly to C-level executives and currently am at the University of Utah with responsibilities in project management, business analysis, software evaluation, and policy writing.

I am interested in moving my career in a new direction (i.e., enter the education sector, non-profit sector, or private sector) where I can use my past experience and business knowledge in a rewarding fashion. My areas of expertise include leadership management, business process improvement, human resource management, risk management, information technology management (with an emphasis in security), budget management, actuarial analysis/forecasting, and compliance management.

William Shiflett’s Specialties:
