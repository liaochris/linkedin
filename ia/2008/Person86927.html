Jeannette Draper’s Summary

Jeannette Draper is a Partner at Arena Films 3D. She has been an independent entertainment and digital media consultant and entrepreneur through her company Draper Consulting since 1998, focusing on investment stage start-ups and launching new lines of business for Fortune 500 companies. Jeannette works closely with her startup clients, consulting in areas of strategic development, organizational design, marketing, customer service, and operations. She has developed, launched, and managed three Internet businesses, raising $23.75 million in early stage financing, and co-founded two businesses.

Clients of Draper Consulting have included Dolby Laboratories, Cinea, Telemundo, Sony BMG Music Group, Warner Music Group, IBM, Time Warner Cable, PhatNoise.com (now owned by Harman International), BigWords.com, and Monstertrak.com, the college-affiliate of Monster.com. Jeannette's duties ranged from strategic business development, marketing, customer service, product development, and operations.

Jeannette began her career in film and videogames producing films for MPCA, Orion and MGM-UA, and PC CD-ROM videogames for Sega and EA. As Creative Vice President of Orion Interactive, her first online projects were uniquely immersive: a photo-realistic quest game "The Cover Girl Murders" and a VRML game of Robocop.

Jeannette is interested in Account Management and Business Development opportunities. She will consider relocating to the Northern California. She is interested in meeting with VCs and sponsors to help grow a great sports and entertainment property and sponsors who wish to reach an influential 20s-50s audience through 3-D filmed action, sports and music channels.

For fun and a great workout, she skates with the "Freshmeat" for L.A. Derby Dolls, voted "Best Wheels" by L.A.’s Downtown News "Best of Downtown" (pub date 6/23/07). You can visit us at http://www.derbydolls.com/la
