Sanjay Sethi (sethi100@gmail.com)’s Summary

I am an agribusiness professional with 17 years experience in various parts of the value chain. I have worked in variety of industries like fresh produce, edible oils & oilseeds, grains, spices, other agricultural commodities and food products, feed supplements and animal feeds, agricltural implements and consumer products. I have represented small and medium enterprises for their growth and cooperation. As Co Chairman of FICCI's Task Force for Quality in Agriculture and as Member of CII's National Committee on Agricultural Marketing, I represent the contemporary face of Indian Agricultural Industry. Trying to imbibe the values of Dr. V. Kurien, The Father of Milk Revolution in India, as his protege, I seek to improve the life of farmers in India.
