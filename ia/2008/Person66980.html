Gabriel Morgan’s Summary

Enterprise Architecture - Expert in enterprise architecture domain with specific attention of aligning IT to the business as IT asset simplification. Experience with the following frameworks; Gartner, Zachman, TOGAF and FEAF. Knowledge of ITIL, COBiT, CMM and Six Sigma processes.

Enterprise Solution Delivery - Demonstrated ability to deliver large, enterprise-worthy solutions with the full life cycle of application development through post-delivery support. Reengineered delivery methodology to focus on system quality, particularly system flexibility, reusability, maintainability and interoperability.

Team Leadership - Effectively mentor, motivate and train the team to achieve a heightened caliber of success driven performance. Collaborating with business partners to ensure that solutions are in synch with business strategies and priorities. Lead the development of technology plans and roadmaps that address current state challenges and how to achieve a target future state. Expertise in mentoring and coaching teams to develop strategies and architectures for back-office infrastructure as well as new systems implementation.

Technical Knowledge - Expert knowledge of design and implementation of enterprise workflow systems, distributed architecture, intranet/internet architecture. Extensive experience in software development life-cycle (SDLC) for the delivery of technological alternatives. Strong design of service-oriented application implementations, including directional guidance for Software as a Service (SaaS) and Software plus Services (S+S) architecture strategy.

International Experience - Lived and worked abroad gaining invaluable experience building strong relationships delivering enterprise-class solutions with partners for customers in Asian emerging markets.
