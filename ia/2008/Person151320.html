Neil Martinez’s Summary

I have been involved in managing IT/IS solutions design, development, and delivery for Fortune 100 companies and the public sector for longer than I care to admit -- over fifteen years. This includes stints with Price Waterhouse, IBM Global Services, Lotus Professional Services, EDS, and others.

I've performed a diverse assortment of client-facing roles. From technical writing, instructional design, art direction, and marketing; to business systems analysis, change management consulting, and project management.

I am a PMI-trained Project Manager. I deeply enjoy collaborating with smart and diverse people and implementing smart, elegant solutions.

Neil Martinez’s Specialties:

I have expertise in Change Management, Business Analysis and Project Management. The past five years have seen me splitting my time between e-business, finance, and pharmaceuticals. I am ideally suited for leading the needs analyses for mid- to large-scale implementations of web-based business applications "behind the firewall."
