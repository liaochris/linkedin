Betsy Lasse Stern’s Summary

As an executive recruiter with Management Recruiters of St. Charles, I am part of MRINetwork, the largest recruitment firm in the world. Our office has been located in the Chicago suburbs for over 20 years and our 12 recruiters partner with clients in a variety of industries. Our firm is ranked in the top 15 percent of more than 1100 offices worldwide.

I lead our Corporate Communications and Marketing practice and specialize in finding passive candidates for hard to fill positions in the construction and agricultural equipment industries. I typically place mid to senior level employees on a contingency basis. My firm also offers retained, engaged and contract services. My clients vary from small private firms to well known, Fortune 500 companies.

Areas I recruit in: internal communications, public relations, marketing communications, marketing, advertising, investor relations, public affairs

If you are a client looking to add top candidates to your team, or a candidate with a strong track record of success, please contact me at Betsy@stcsearch.com
