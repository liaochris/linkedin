Robbert-Jan van Oijen’s Summary

Robbert-Jan van Oijen’s Experience

  • Trainee Demand Chain Engineering

    SABIC

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Chemicals industry)

    March 2008 — October 2008 (8 months)

    Worked as a trainee at the department of Demand Chain Engineering of SABIC Europe.

    Executed a study in order to identify relevant warehouse location factors that SABIC needs to examine into depth when deciding where to locate a new polyolefin's logistics hub abroad.
    Subsequently, examined SABIC's LSP selection process and identified relevant LSP selection criteria to evaluate and compare alternative LSPs to select the most compatible one for operating a polyolefin's logistics hub.

    Finally, created a comprehensive but clearly structured and accessible 'logistics hub location guideline'. This guideline includes all stages/activities required to initiate and finish a logistics hub project and also presents especially designed templates and R.A.S.C.I. information at an operational level.

    The overall project performed at SABIC Europe's headquarters has been appointed an extraordinary 8.5 out of 10.0 by the exam committee of Tilburg University.

  • Trainee

    CKB Midwest

    (Chemicals industry)

    May 2007 — November 2007 (7 months)

    Worked as a trainee at CKB Midwest in Utrecht, the Netherlands.

    Performed a study in order to identify how CKB Midwest could change their demand pattern such that it would become more stable over the year, thereby creating a safer and less costly situation.

    By an extensive examination of academical demand management literature and system dynamics, a simulation model was build to be used to determine how various demand management strategies would work out in the context of the study.

    The final results and recommendations of the project were presented in front of 14 executive managers of the company in focus and their parent company.

    The overall project was appointed an 8.0 out of 10.0 by the Exam Committee of Tilburg University.
