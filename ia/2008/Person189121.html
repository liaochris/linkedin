Tim Murphy’s Summary

GOAL
To lead a high growth companies in a customer facing role to exceptional profit and return. Global consumer industries will require innovation, leadership, and strategy for sustainable growth. Excellent customer experience, innovative marketing and information technology will provide the context. Collaborative business relationships and a world class team will prove essential. The latitude to ask tough questions, debate hard issues and chart a new course is assumed. When successful, the organization will legitimately claim industry leadership.

PROFILE
Growth Company Operating Executive: Concurrently managed as many as five lines of business and enterprise capabilities. Domestic, international, and emerging market experience in multi-national and global conglomerates. Diverse market experience includes financial transaction processing, consumer credit services, advertising, outsourced customer service, loyalty marketing, consumer packaged goods, eCommerce, Web 2.0, information technology and asset management consulting.

Progressive Success Record with Market Leaders: Market share, revenue, profit and shareholder value growth for market leaders including Alliance Data, Epsilon, Air Miles Reward Program (The Loyalty Group), Inbev (Interbrew), Torstar, Ticketmaster online - CitySearch, (Interactive Corp) and Bell Canada Enterprises.

Exceptional Achievements: Air Miles Reward Program named Online Retailer of the Year by the Canadian Marketing Association; Toronto.com awarded Online Innovator of the Year by Editor and Publisher Magazine; Alliance Data named in the top 100 Financial Services Technology Companies by Business Week; Inbev President’s Award for outstanding individual leadership contribution; Post graduate studies in innovation, corporate strategy, negotiation and product development at Massachusetts Institute of Technology and Harvard University.
