Shadeed Eleazer’s Summary

I am a Senior Consultant of Microsoft and related technologies. I am a Microsoft Certified Technical Specialist and have been in the technology industry for 10 + years.

I am currently specializing in consulting engagements on the Office SharePoint Server 2007 platform from an architectural, requirements gathering and analysis, and enterprise planning and implementation standpoint.

Other skillsets include server platform integration (Project Server 03/07 and LCS/OCS, Team Foundation Server.

I am the Organizer of the greater Baltimore's fastest growing technology group.
Baltimore SharePoint User's Group. (www.baltimoresug.org)

I started off as the VP of Marketing for the Mid-Atlantic's largest SharePoint group. SharePoint User's Group of Northern VA.

My love for technology coupled with my entrepreneurial spirit (raised in small business setting) has lead me to work with numerous development teams to come up with the prototype for the next wave of technology especially to impact small business and most recently the music industry

As the music industry struggles to compete with the new Information Age, and adopt Web 2.0 principles into its struggling business model, I have found that my experiences in the publishing industry and marketing and promotions have made me a highly sought after commodity in bridging the gap between 21st century "new media" concepts and a community of listeners who now look to digital retail markets to discover new music.

I am now planning a series of Music Business and Technology workshops to empower and educate musicians on the advances in technology so that they can anticipate and capitalize on applications that allow them to reach the world in a split-second.
