Pam Baker’s Summary

A prolific and versatile writer, my published credits include numerous cover stories for international, national and regional media from women's and general interest to finance, business and technology magazines, online content and newspapers; analytical studies on technology; and, six books. Member National Press Club and Avant Guild/Mediabistro.com. 2004 nominee for the Templeton Cambridge Journalism Fellowship in Science and Relgion (UK). Wrote and produced an award-winning documentary on paper-making.

I am interested in finding a literary agent and am seeking freelance writing assignments for mainstream media or corporations. Also interested in building my contacts and expert sources data base for news stories.

Previously owned and operated a marketing, advertising and PR firm in Columbus, Georgia for 11 years. The firm’s client list included Mead, Pratt & Whitney, Disney On Ice, TSYS, Johnston Industries, Diaz-Verson Capital Investments, and other high-profile clients.

Pam Baker’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, interviewing, access to powerbrokers and high-profile individuals, writing, editing, copywriting, speech writing, public relations, media relations, branding and identity, analytical skills
