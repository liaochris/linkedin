Tess Cacciatore’s Summary

I have an extensive background (over 20 years) in producing, writing, and events that have to do with multi-layers of marketing and technology. My visionary approach is an non-exclusive invitation, to create a place where we can all work together to create a better world.

I am the Founder/Executive Director of WORLD TRUST FOUNDATION (www.worldtrust.org) a not-for-profit organization which bridges cultures and connects youth through media and technology. My passion is to empower youth to feel what it is like to be a leader in their own lives, as well as a place to mentor the future leaders of the world.

Along with World Trust, I am presently launching an interactive, broadband television network, which is a social network to bring cultures together and to connecct youth worldwide. WORLD TRUST NETWORK. It is a for-profit venture, to create sustainable revenue models for all involved. www.worldtrustnetwork.tv

Our team of industry experts range from broadcast, technology, creative, educational, etc. who are committed as I, to take the world of technology and create sustainable revenue streams for non-profit organizations, schools, orphanages, and other like-minded organizations around the world, who are striving to make the world a better place.

This vision can only be done with like-minded individuals, as well as businesses who are committed to this difference. Please feel free to contact me and join this fast-growing network to make the world a better place for now and for future generations.
