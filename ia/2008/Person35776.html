Chris Parkhouse’s Summary

Chris has a unique set of talents gained through 17 years of international and UK ICT software marketing and product management experience across the USA, EMEA, APAC and the UK. He has a balance of entrepreneurial and corporate experience gained in some of the world’s biggest American corporations and several small start-ups, including Paragon Software who were acquired for $500M in 2001. Chris has majored in Web2.0 and Enterprise2.0 technologies for many years, based on a foundation of communication, collaboration and social-networking software platforms and has a personal goal to play his part in delivering the semantic web or Web3.0. Chris’ experience and vision allows him to quickly identify and leverage new markets and business opportunities.
