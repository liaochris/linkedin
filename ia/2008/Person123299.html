Martina McDermott’s Summary

A Senior Executive Search Professional with 10 years recruitment industry experience specialising within the Phamaceutical Industry. Ability to work within both the retained search and contingency business model.

Currently working for Hays Pharma (formerly James Harvard) a leading Global Specialist Pharmaceutical firm. Recently relocated to Australia to develop the Hays Pharma Regulatory & Medical Group for the Australasia market. A Member of Topra - The Association for Professionals in Regulatory Affairs.
