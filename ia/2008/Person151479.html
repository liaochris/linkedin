Chris Holinger’s Summary

A highlight of Mr. Holinger’s recent client experience includes, but is not limited to:
• Project Management – Oversaw the Program Management Office duties for 4 IT Service Management teams involved in a reengineering effort; facilitated deliverable creation and reviewed for accuracy and completeness for all 4 processes; supported and served as the project liaison for a larger process reengineering initiative that integrated best practices from the ITIL, COBIT and CMMI frameworks; managed and delivered a variety of executive communications throughout the project lifecycle that reported status, project financials, change requests and other materials to support ongoing decision making.
• Change Management – Assisted with a Change Management implementation that redesigned it from an immature Change notification “process” to a mature Change Management program; facilitated meetings for various stakeholders, including a tactical executive steering group comprised of Directors and Vice Presidents.
• Incident Management – Oversaw and provided a variety of support services for the Incident Management process reengineering effort; gathered requirements and helped design the company’s Command Center process and layout; provided support for the Incident Management process and tool implementation.
• Training – Developed content and conducted IT Service Management process and tool training for approximately 2,000 staff within the company’s IT organization; provided a variety of other training and development support for the project lifecycle.
