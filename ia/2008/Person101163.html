Responsible, proven leader! Quick Learner. Proficient in the deployment of Information Technology to enhance employee and organizational productivity.

My background is comprised of both public and private sector service. Whether documenting submarine radar systems, supporting IBM engineers, deploying CMMS systems, managing complex financial systems, or supervising IT programs, MY STRONGEST STRENGTH IS THE ABILITY TO QUICKLY LEARN AND ADAPT TO THE NEEDS OF THE JOB IN ANY INDUSTRY.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT:
As project manager, have performed a broad range of project management duties on mission critical projects including: identifying and documenting requirements; establishing clear and achievable objectives; managing the competing demands for resources, time, scope, costs and quality; developing time-lines with tasks, resources, milestones and dependencies; establishing performance benchmarks; establishing and executing project budgets; managing project risks, over runs, delays and conflicts; adapting plans and approach to address the concerns of stakeholders; managing the scope and expectations for the selected product; implementing the final solution; and evaluating the project success.

SUPERVISION:

Major responsibilities include: assign and review work, sign time cards, manage and minimize conflicts, develop and administer performance plans, perform fact findings, train staff in job duties and expectations, initiate/administer disciplinary procedures, reward exceptional performance, etc. I enjoy developing satisfactory employees to outstanding performance through creative incentives and motivation.

LEADERSHIP:
Through determined persistence, have developed strong leadership competencies. Starting from college activities, through community involvement, I have successfully employed these leadership experiences as a project manager and supervisor.
