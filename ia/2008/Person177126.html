Skip Rawstron’s Summary

Thirty years experience working with real estate owners, managers, and developers. Extensive experience conducting insurance seminars for apartment building owners and managers, as well as working directly with Boards of Directors and property managers for community association/homeowners associations.

Membership in and affiliation with many trade associations including the United States Green Building Council, California Apartment Association, Community Associations Institute, International Council of Shopping Centers, and Institute of Real Estate Management.

Served on insurance carrier agency councils with CSE Insurance Group, California Insurance Group, Fireman's Fund, and Prudential-LMI Commercial Insurance Company.

Published author with emphasis on automated marketing techniques, identification
of multiple prospect sources, and tracking new building permits. Conducted seminars for Applied Systems and local chapters of the Western Users group of Applied Systems (WUGAS).

Skip Rawstron’s Specialties:

Superb relationships with all carriers represented, detailed analysis in risk management and risk transfer techniques, lease review and analysis, interfacing with commercial real estate lenders on insurance related issues. Superior negotiating skills with insurance underwriters, unique approach to presenting difficult and challenging risks to insurance underwriters. Current clients include the Chief Underwriting Officer of a major property and casualty company based in California.
