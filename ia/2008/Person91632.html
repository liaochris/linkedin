Jim Phipps’s Summary

James D. Phipps, CEO, AquaStruct, Inc., is an entrepreneur by nature and an international business attorney by training. Jim has over a decade of experience advising on global brand management and licensing, supply chain and channel management, protection and exploitation of intellectual property, and dispute resolution. Jim has managed large teams of professionals and outside service providers in billion dollar plus transactions and disputes. Jim has acted as a day-to-day member of a C-level management team, advising on all aspects of company operations — from domestic to international, from supply to distribution, from outsourcing to intellectual property management. Jim knows how to grow a business. During the three years before starting AquaStruct, Inc., Jim doubled his personal book of business each year, reaching $2.3 million in sales. Jim invented the AquaStruct™ system together with his wife of 21 years, Lori Phipps. Jim and Lori have five children ages six to 19 and reside in Montclair, Virginia. Jim is currently raising up to $1.54 million in capital (equity or secured debt) for AquaStruct, Inc.

Jim Phipps’s Specialties:

Product and packaging design; supply chain management, international business law, channel management, brand protection, intellectual property protection, dispute resolution, international arbitration, contract negotiation, business planning, long-term mutually beneficial relationships
