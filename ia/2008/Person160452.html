Richard Thompson’s Summary

I am a Business Development Manager with Kelly Services (KellyConnect) in the Seattle Metro region and a Certified Contact Center Consultant through Purdue University. My focus is on developing relationships and mutually beneficial business opportunities within the Eastside Market as well as driving Customer Contact Center business regionally. My primary objective is to identify and strategically develop business opportunities with the goal of providing people, process and technology solutions; proving Kelly's vision of being the world's best full spectrum staffing services company and to be recognized as the best.

I have experience in sales and account management within a 6 billion dollar global company as well as project management with a well founded and highly regarded local company. I believe those past experiences have aided in my development as a top performer in sales and service and have helped to give me a broader perspective of the fundamentals necessary for success.
