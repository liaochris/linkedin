Scott Hall’s Summary

I have spent the last 13 years helping companies ranging from newly-funded start-ups to Fortune 500 corporations locate hard-to-find, passive talent. My strengths lie in the ability to not only source candidates, but to effectively and passionately communicate the value proposition of my client companies.

I have experience creating recruiting processes for companies and directing internal recruiting teams. An entrepreneur at heart, I have successfully founded three staffing firms. My style has been characterized as both personal and professional.

Scott Hall’s Specialties:

Candidate sourcing, salary negotiations, recruiting process development, team management
