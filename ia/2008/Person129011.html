Michael C. Hardy’s Summary

"A Financial Planning Approach to Real Estate Lending." If there is a more strategic way to finance and structure your Real Estate, we will find it.

Paradigm Financial Group serves to compliment a Real Estate professional, Financial Advisor, Attorney, and CPA to fill the often overlooked "debt management" role on a clients wealth building team.

We offer Residential (Purchase and Refinance Loans), Commercial, Private Money, and Non-Recourse Real Estate Lending inside of a Self-Directed IRA.

Experience includes Traditional Real Estate Lending/Financing, Private Project Financing, Financial Planning, Securities (Series 7), and Asset Management (Series 65).

Significant experience in Real Estate financing and debt management strategy. Property purchase financing, refinance loans, and evaluation of overall debt efficiency for clients that compliments and integrates with a broader financial and wealth building plan. Creative thinking with financial planning analysis that take into consideration cash flow, appreciation, program and interest expenses, opportunity cost, tax implications, short, and long term goals.

Core Values: Honesty, Integrity, Experience, Advice.
