Julie Weight’s Summary

With an extensive background in boutique transcription services, my transcription service has been the leader in quality transcription and first-class service for clinics and hospitals since 1986. I am now focusing that experience on consulting. Because of ambiguous pricing practices and quality issues, facilities would benefit from having an industry insider evaluate transcription contract compliance and management, charge audits and price comparison, quality review and quality improvement, workflow automation, productivity enhancement, and training. I work with hospitals, clinics and consultants to reduce costs and improve contract compliance, workflow and productivity enhancement, gain a better understanding of the documentation process and how outsourced transcription services operate.

I have also developed job site and resume banks for the health information management and transcription industry: MTRegistry.com, for transcription, and HIMsJobs.com, for the health information management industry; and an open source reference and style manual for medical transcription.

I am honest, outspoken and don't pull any punches. I write and communicate effectively. I have a talent for cutting through the distractions and getting to the heart of an issue. I don't believe in change for the sake of change but I have the ability to see when it's a good thing and readily embrace it. I maintain the philosophy that there are people you like - and people you don't know. I like getting to know people. I like hearing about people and knowing what they're doing. I'm good at remembering what they tell me and keeping it to myself.

I am a proud affiliate of Mighty Ventures. This year is a year of change for me and I look forward to working with Christine and the team at Mighty Ventures to help make that change. I look forward to helping others find the same success.
