Gary Sheehan’s Summary

Cape Medical Supply is a home healthcare, Durable Medical Equipment, and respiratory care provider. We service all of Cape Cod, Nantucket, and Plymouth County and maintain our headquarters in Sandwich, MA, with additional stores in Plymouth, MA and Falmouth, MA. We work very closely with physicians, hospitals, nursing homes, and other healthcare agencies to ensure positive outcomes for patients in the home environment.
As President/CEO of the company my goals are to further solidify our competitive position in our own market, expand to new and attractive markets, and to ensure the service excellence that has defined our first 30 years remains a hallmark of our future growth and expansion. Additionally we are looking for attractive acquisition opportunities to allow us to continue to build on an aggressive growth strategy, broaden our geographic coverage area, and enhance the services we currently offer our patients and referral sources.
