Contact me directly: amandaloden@gmail.com or Amanda@Bluespeed.net


Bluespeed Technology is a Search Firm specializing in Information Technology Consulting Services. We work with client companies and job seekers across the US.

I am actively developing our Healthcare Information Technology and Security/Identity Management Software Practice. I work with hiring managers to find and hire talented employees for full time positions, typically on the business development side. Most of my key client companies are within Security Software, Identity Management, Email Security, Authentication, and other major Security specialties. I also work with HIT companies that provide Electronic Health Records (EHR), Practice Management Software (PMS), and Computerized Provider/Physician/Prescriber Order Entry (CPOE) and Identity Management Software (IDM), Security Software, Network Management (NMS). Some of my smaller clients are developing and marketing new technologies and medical devices.

I began recruiting while at an international executive search firm that specialized in Information Technology. When the "dot bomb" happened, I changed course but always remained involved in the recruiting industry at a lower level.

I am actively growing my network of professionals within my specialty. Whether you need help with hiring initiatives or are looking to take the next step in your career, my industry contacts may be invaluable.
