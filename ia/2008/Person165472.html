Richard Stilicha, PMP’s Summary

With 10 years of experience in project management, my background includes all aspects of project initiation, definition, planning, execution and closing. I am a goal-driven individual with strong ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and/or in "fast track" mode. During the last 10 years I have delivered 132 quality projects, ranging from $250,000 to $5,000,000, mostly on time and budget.

My creative logic and ability to meet deadlines have contributed to my past successes and guarantees my future contributions. I have a Masters Degree in International Relations and Bachelor Degree in Computer Systems. I am PMP (Project Management Professional) certified and member of PMI since 2001. While managing most of my project in compliance with the PMI standards, I also have experiences with RUP (Rational Unified Process) project methodology. My deep knowledge of Microsoft Project Server has enabled me to initiate, deploy and support this tool across various organizations.
