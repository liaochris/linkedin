Harold Cameron’s Summary

I am the creator/founder of The Be Positive Day and Be Positive Campaign. The Be Positive Day is celebrated on March 1st of every year. My desire is to partner with people, organizations, and companies from around the world who want to be part of a grassroots movement that promotes positive thought and action every day of the year.
My personal positive contribution to people and society is that I volunteer my time to serve others as a consumer advocate by helping consumers resolve their complaints or issues with companies or government agencies. I also answer questions consumers might have regarding various consumer issues.I also educate consumers concerning current SCAMS and fraudulent activities taking place across the USA to protect them from being taken advantage of financially.
Starting Tuesday January 6th, 2009 at 1 PM I will be hosting a live consumer affairs Internet Radio Show for Black Talk Media on the BlogTalkRadio Network.
I provide consumer advocacy services for the website IRipoff.com.
I am gifted at motivating people and helping them through encouragement to find their personal potential.
I serve as a life coach and mentor to a growing number of subscribers and friends through my YouTube Channel.
I am available for public speaking engagements.
