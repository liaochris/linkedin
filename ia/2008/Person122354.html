Mark Newman’s Summary

Founding executive with two key Internet firms. Specialty in developing content and programming at all media levels. Highly creative, managed high of 55 employees. Currently responsible for development and implementation of new content initiatives as Enterprise Editor for Major League Baseball Advanced Media, promoting goods and services and the game itself to millions of fans at MLB.com, 30 official club sites and other entities. Two-decade communications career includes being only recipient of prestigious National Magazine Award for General Excellence, in New Media -- put The Sporting News magazine online as VP/GM and ASME deemed it the best use of the medium by ANY magazine. Also past VP for AOL Time Warner's NASCAR.com property. Fundraising, budget and project management, developing strategic alliances and overdelivering to make it a delight to work with my company.

Mark Newman’s Specialties:

Startup entrepreneur; general management of new-media ventures; overseeing web content development; business development/negotiations; writing; marketing; public relations; project manager; P&L; innovation development.
