Marko Lackovic’s Summary

I have obtained BSc, MSc and PhD degrees from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and computing, Zagreb in 2002, 2004 and 2005 respectively, with the focus on telecommunication networks. My technical education has been extended by the MBA degree from the Faculty of Economics and Business, Zagreb in 2007.

I have started my professional career as a research assistant working in Zagreb and Lausanne Switzerland in the field of telecommunications and supply chain management. Further professional development has been continued in Ericsson R&D in Zagreb on the project manager position, and Huawei Technologies Co. Ltd., Representative office in Zagreb, Croatia, on the key account manager position. I have participated in several European Union projects, and have authored and co-authored numerous scientific publications.
