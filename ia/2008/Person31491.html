Carsten Suhr’s Summary

Experienced information specialist in Enterprise Content Management.

I am an information specialist and in the last 12 years I have worked with knowledge management and knowledge sharing. As Knowledge Manager, Web coordinator and Consultant in large Danish companies I have acquired a wide-ranging experience with business communication and knowledge management, i.e. developing and implementing portal, cms, document management and search solutions with focus on business strategies, processes, data quality and user needs.

Further more, my education as Master in Library and Information Science has given me a substantial understanding of how to manage knowledge sharing, communication and information seeking within organisations.

Carsten Suhr’s Specialties:

I have been working in both large private and public organisations with:

- Building efficient, well organized information systems for communication and knowledge sharing

- Preparing knowledge management strategy and action plans based on business goals

- Analysing business and user needs and functionality specifications to set up information systems and services with focus on usability

- Establishing information architechture/content management processes anchored in business routines
