Juergen Harter’s Summary

Experienced Business Developer, Chemistry Analyst, Information manager, and Project Manager in a variety of pharmaceutical knowledge management and business workflow applications. Latest focus is on expert systems and business intelligence. Good range of experience in automatic building, creation and visualisation of intelligence networks, knowledge maps, ontologies and taxonomies. Particularly interested in knowledge modelling, knowledge capture and semantic web technologies, and any techniques and methods to handle and make sense of the ever growing "information mountain". Always interested in challenging IT integration projects and knowledge-driven business development opportunities, particularly in the pharmaceutical, biotech and healthcare industries.
