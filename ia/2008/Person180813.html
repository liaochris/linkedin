Steve Gill’s Summary

Steve is the President of Chapman Gill International LLC, an advisory & management services firm which specializes in helping CEO’s, Entrpreneurs & Practice Owners achieve demanding short & long term business goals in fast changing conditions.

Steve brings more than 30 years experience of accessible, hands-on leadership of nationally & internationally dispersed businesses - spanning North America, Europe, China, India, Africa & across the emerging markets. He has helped build profitable businesses in health care, international trade, debt capital markets, investment banking, corporate & institutional banking, commodity finance, & risk management. Steve brings direct experience of 30 different mergers & acquisitions … & of business recovery post 9/11.

He was a founder member of the Standard Chartered Group CEO Leadership Team that successfully established an economic value approach to strategy & management. He also served as Chairman of Standard Chartered Trade Services Corporation & Executive Vice President of Standard Chartered Bank PLC. Steve has held senior executive responsibilities with HSBC Group, Midland Bank, Samuel Montagu, Crocker National Corporation & Bracton Corporation, one of the first “B-Bank” problem loan workout vehicles in the US.

Steve graduated summa cum laude from the London School of Economics with the Farr Medal & a Master of Science degree in Econometrics. He is a Sloan Fellow of London Business School, a member of the St. George’s Society of New York & of the New York Down Town Association. He also holds a Masters degree in Operational Research & Management Science & was a member of the Chartered Institute of Bankers & a Fellow of the Royal Statistical Society.

Steve lives in Larchmont New York with a passion for his family, his dog, rock & reggae music & West Ham United F.C.
