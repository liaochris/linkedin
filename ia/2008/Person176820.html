Rahman Mehraby’s Summary

Internet Marketing is based on Search Engines' behavior. It is basically called "Search Engine Marketing", which includes "Search Engine Optimization" and "Web Analytics".
As a result of this type of marketing, a website gets seen in search engine result pages (visibility). Therefore, Internet searchers can more easily find it and visit it (traffic). Then, visitors's interaction with the website can be supervised and led toward the goals defined by the marketer. All this is done to convert visitors to customers more than ever (conversion).
My goal is to provide this service for those website owners who really want to get some objective accomplished on their sites.
