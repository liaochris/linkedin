Karl Haddad’s Summary

The Founder & CEO of PreLaunch Properties Middle East, Karl Joseph Haddad, has sold more than $1.6bn in numerous projects over the 12 months. Having served as Head of Investments at one of Dubai’s largest real estate brokerages, the concept came to life as he noticed that bigger isn’t better as investors do not necessarily want all services offered as a One Stop Shop.

Investors prefer to deal with 1 individual; person to person, a confidant that they can trust, call for simple advise and most importantly, can get them that particular requirement at that particular time.
