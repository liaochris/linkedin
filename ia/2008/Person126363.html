Max Walker’s Summary

Experienced business-side project and personnel manager.

5 years project management experience Includes project selection, requirements gathering, partnering with IS&T and internal development organizations, driving time lines, managing scope, confirming final delivery. Most project management experience with Novell Technical Support department, but now also includes experience managing small technology projects as a liaison between web hosting/design vendors and small companies seeking help establishing their first web site presence.

2 years personnel management includes hiring and managing both local and remote personnel, motivating teamwork, and managing to a budget.

Max Walker’s Specialties:

Identifying actionable and measurable requirements in language that's understandable by both the client and the vendor (the business department and the developer). Driving time lines and managing scope. Creating an effective team work environment using clear roles and targets and based on open communication, integrity, and trust.
