Mike Love’s Summary

I am proud, and lucky enough, to have worked with four of the world's most exciting and powerful brands - Shell, McDonald's, Microsoft and now BT, and for Margaret Thatcher, one of the most important, successful and inspiring political leaders in history (no under statement required here and debate always welcome!). I have worked for and with great people committed to both doing the right thing and making a difference.

I build, promote and protect the single most valuable business, political and personal asset - reputation. I do this through the planning, management and execution of integrated communications leadership - and by working with very impressive people who also like to make things happen and have fun doing it.

Mike Love’s Specialties:

Political campaign management, integrated internal & external communications, advocacy of corporate social responsibility
