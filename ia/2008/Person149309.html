Nick Price’s Summary

Bright Purple is a well established technology recruitment firm, now in its 13th year of operation. Based out of Edinburgh but servicing clients across the UK and Internationally. Providing IT - contract & permanent resource supply and an Executive service for managment & 'C' level hires, where we have carried out single assignments and team moves.

The company has a great senior management team and an equally strong set of staff. We believe we are building something special here at Bright Purple, something we can all be proud of and that confidence in our people and their abilities has helped us to some great client wins in recent times.

We don't sell USP's, why bother - we simply deliver and that's the best USP anyone in the service industry can have. The repeat business we have is testiment to this, as are the many long standing client relationships that we have developed. We thank those people candidates and clients alike for their constant support.

Nick Price’s Specialties:

Recruitment across the IT and consulting sectors working for a variety of organisations from major Blue-Chip's to young start-up venyutes across the UK and Internationally. Hot topics of recruitment right now include Change/Business Transformation Management, SixSigma & Lean Processes, BPI, Cost Reduction, Revenue Enhancement, Oracle technology, financial services arena especially Wealth Management and Storage/Security.
