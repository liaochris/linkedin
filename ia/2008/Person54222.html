Dr. Ann Williamson’s Summary

Dr. Ann Williamson is the Joyologist, bringing joy to all that she does, whether it is speaking in a classroom or speaking in a room of thousands. Dr. Joy ~ Ann is the owner and principal coach of Williamson Comprehensive Group which specializes in coaching individuals who are Executives, Small Business 0wners. And Multipreneurs. Teacher Wisdom Educational Consulting Firm supports school districts in retaining and maintaining their workforce through consultative coaching practices while using the wisdom of teachers as the foundation of an effective, positive and well performing school district. She is the founder of The ButterflyHouse Inc.

Dr. Joy~Ann is an inspirational speaker and author.

Dr. Ann Williamson’s Specialties:

Helping to bring more Joy to the world through performance and empowerment coaching. I also provide consultative coaching for school districts. I provide training for owners of grouphomes. I am an inspirational keynote speaker.
