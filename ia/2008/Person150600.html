Mario Beccia’s Summary

I worked for a softwarehouse focused on web technologies (Sun solaris and linux with Java and various scripting languages), being often in deep contact with consultancy firms (Accenture). Most of my experience derives from technolgy supplying companies, but I spent also a considerable time in non software companies using technology solutions to support business processes and management decision making.
I've always had an hands-on approach and gained experience on the most known technologies (Microsoft .Net, J2EE, Perl/OpenSource, Oracle, SQLServer), and with some specialized ones (COGNOS, TIBCO Enterprise Suite, MS BizTalk Suite, Pivotal eRelationship).
I'm used to work on a target-oriented basis and customer oriented mindset, always trying to conciliate the two sides of my skills (business and technology).
I've a predilection for customer facing roles, having the chanche to gather the most out of my intra-personal relationship making skills.
I'm interested in multinational consultancy oriented environments, focusing on cutting edge technology solutions, to live new challenges being ready to offer all of my time and strenghts to reach the targets.
