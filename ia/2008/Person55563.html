Dwight Buck’s Summary

Dwight is a telecommunications and networking industry veteran with more than twenty years early and growth stage technology venture experience, focused on achieving excellence in product and business development, marketing and market introduction planning, web and online system development, and general management.

For the past several years, Dwight has worked as a management, marketing, online systems and business development consultant, and early stage venture investment advisor, as a partner in PowerLink Ventures. His work focuses on product concept, development, marketing, customer acquisition and support strategies, and early stage venture financing for start up companies. Dwight previously was a technical advisory board member for the Central Coast Venture Forum, where he nurtured early-stage ventures.

Dwight was founder and chief executive officer for Connected Systems, developer of the world's first Flash-based voicemail systems and unified messaging solutions for carrier services networks, and Structured Internetworks, designers of hardware-based solutions for Internet traffic flow management and optimization. Dwight also founded Nuera Communications, one of the pioneers in high performance voice-over-Frame Relay and VoIP technologies for carrier services gateway applications.
