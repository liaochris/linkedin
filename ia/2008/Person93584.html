Joel Breton’s Summary

JOEL BRETON

Director of Game Development at MTVN Games

As Director of Game Development for MTV Networks, Joel Breton oversees the development and deployment of games for MTVN.

With more than 15 years of interactive entertainment experience, Joel has helped produce games for both console systems and mobile devices with leading companies including SEGA, 2K Sports, Bethesda Softworks and InfoSpace Mobile.

Most recently Joel served as a project manager and producer for Visual Concepts. During his time there, he worked in collaboration with IP licensors and managed development teams to produce titles such as Major League Baseball, World Poker Tour, and Amped3, an Xbox 360 launch title.
