Laura Christianson’s Summary

Laura has 25 years of experience as an educator, writer, public relations professional, and blogger.

She co-founded He Blogs, She Blogs, LLC, a company that works with businesses who want to feature a topical blog as one component of their marketing plan.

She is the author of two non-fiction books, "The Adoption Decision" (2007) and "The Adoption Network" (2007).

A professional blogger, Laura hosts the popular Exploring Adoption blog, awarded Forbes’ “Best of the Web” rating. Her blog is featured in the book, "Blogosphere: Best of Blogs" (2006), and her logo received an Award of Excellence and is showcased in the book, "American Corporate Identity" (2006).

Since 1996, Laura has been a freelance journalist and PR professional, writing for magazines, publishing companies, non-profits, city and county governments, and serving as the communications manager for a fast-growing national franchise. She spent the first decade of her career teaching high school journalism and English. Laura enjoys public speaking and is a regular presenter at conferences. She lives with her husband and their two sons in the Pacific Northwest.
