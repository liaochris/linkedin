Phillip Gordon’s Summary

Over 27 years experience in information technology, with roles including project manager, consultant, architect, Director of IT, and CIO.

Extensive experience developing technology strategies aligned with business objectives, identifying new technologies and matching them to business needs, and delivering technology solutions.

Experience in business process analysis and redesign for improved workflow, increased productivity, enhanced service, and lowered costs.

Experience with global subsidiaries and remote offices.

Managed teams of developers working on multiple projects, including large, complex, multi-phase projects. Managed employees, contractors, and consultants, including remote and offshore teams. Worked effectively on matrix-managed projects.

Worked at two dot coms.

Formed technical groups with a strong team spirit, worked effectively with peers in IT, and at all corporate levels, including executive management.

Experience in "build vs. buy" decisions, vendor negotiations, and contract management.

Experience in systems design, development, and maintenance on a variety of platforms, systems and legacy integration, data networking, LANs and WANs. Some experience with telecommunications, systems operation and management (24/7), and direct customer support.
