Jason DeBoer’s Summary

While attending college, I held a full-time internship with the Gordon Food Service Bid Department. This internship lead to a full-time position while finishing school. Upon graduation, I was offered a marketing position on the GFS Canada National Team.

Throughout my tenure at Gordon Food Service, I have obtained a strong knowledge of the food service and distribution industry. Furthermore, my move to focusing on the Canadian marketplace has given me the opportunity to experience business on an international playing field.

As a member of the branding/marketing team for GFS Canada, I have been instrumental in growing our percentage of core and proprietary branded products by 10% since January 2006. Currently, we are poised to achieve the largest marketshare percentage for food service in Canada over fiscal 2008.
