Mike Morgan’s Summary

Mike Morgan is an ICT and CE industry channel sales and marketing expert with 19 years experience in the field. He is currently the Chief Operating Officer with Foundation Network, the leader in PRM SaaS. Mike previously held a number of interim and non-executive appointments with start-up and early-life technology and consumer electronics companies including the position of CMO with Altio the real-time data visualisation software vendor and Vice President of Worldwide Sales and Marketing with Tiger Telematics Inc. the gaming and telematics device vendor. Mike is also founder of ChannelSphere the specialist strategic consultancy providing partner lifecycle solutions to ICT and CE vendors.

Previously, Mike was the Director of Commercial Business for Sony Europe, Middle East and Africa responsible for the integration and subsequent sales and marketing of Sony’s B2B product businesses across the region. As a member of Sony EMEA's Sales and Marketing Platform management team, he developed Sony's commercial end-customer and channel strategies and business model, leading to the creation of commercial channel sales and marketing organisations across much of Western Europe.

Prior to joining Sony, Morgan spent 9 years with Compaq and then the 'new' HP in various senior sales and marketing management roles including that of Director of Channel Business Unit and Director of Small & Medium Business Sales and Marketing for Compaq UK and Ireland, and Director of the new Hewlett Packard's Enterprise Channel business in Europe, Middle East and Africa following the merger.

Mike's formative career was spent with General Computer Corporation, Rank Xerox and Pitney Bowes. He was also a Commissioned Officer, and trained as a pilot in the Royal Air Force.
