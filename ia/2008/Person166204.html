Ruben van den Oord’s Summary

I guess you could say I'm a "serial entrepreneur"...

I started my career at the early age of 18, being managing director of my fathers cleaning company, After working there for a couple of months, I got the opportunity to take over the company. While learning on the job, I most certainly got my share of hard nose experience. Although this time has been an excellent education, learning how to develop business from the inside out, it made me come to the conclusion that Facility Services weren't really my cup of tea.

Since I was always looking for new ways to expand business, generating one idea after another, I eventually learned that Marketing Communications was the industry for me. Since then I've set up several companies, bought and sold my shares and even went broke twice. All together you can say I'm quite experienced.

However, in the past 20 years I have worked as an employee from time to time as well. But although these experiences were absolutely valuable, they also proved where I "belonged". As the open mind I developed on doing business (and thus generating opportunities on the go) wasn't always compatible with my employers grid of my position. Let's say that some might find that the success rate of these periods is subject to discussion.

And so, entrepreneur it was and I guess it always will be. Thereafter I've set up several companies. Most of the time as an owner, but sometimes I was engaged as a consultant for other enthusiasts as well. Working for keen and eager people, who wanted their own taste of the freedom one has as an entrepreneur.

Nowadays I'm a dedicated consultant for companies big or small, for Marketing, Communications and Sales issues. And on the side I still love to help young entrepreneurs make their dream come true: Starting your very own business....

So if you're planning on starting your own business, please feel free to contact me: I'd love to donate my two cents during a brainstorm session....

Ruben van den Oord’s Specialties:

Consulting companies wanting to expand business. Providing creative input, mostly in a nonconventional way. Coaching entrepeneurs on a personal level, striving to create maximum exposure for brilliant ideas. If a company faces heavy weather, I can provide the essential structure and overview to get things back on course. My drive and energy helps to motivate all stakeholders involved (including clients) to walk the extra mile. I'm happy to get my network involved when needed to get the job done.
