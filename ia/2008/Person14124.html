Anshuman Tiwari’s Summary

I am a quality professional with special interests in managing large change initiatives, business excellence and Six Sigma. I have provided consulting services to a variety of companies - some of them winning national and international awards. I currently manage the Business Excellence program at Infosys. I also managed the Customer Satisfaction measurement program for Infosys in 2007.

I have several quality certifications including Manager of Quality/OE (ASQ) and Six Sigma Black Belt. I have trained over 50 Black Belts and 500 Green Belts and over 1000 on quality principles and methods.

I have played a leadership role in efforts to establish ASQ India.

Anshuman Tiwari’s Specialties:

Total Quality Management, Business Excellence, and Six Sigma.
Substantial experience in developing and deploying Business Excellence criteria/models.
