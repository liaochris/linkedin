Goncalo Caeiro’s Summary

I'm the the co-founder and I'm running Infosistema ( www.infosistema.com ).

Infosistema ( www.infosistema.com ) is a IT Consultancy company with focus on Banking (investment and retail banking), Insurance (insurance companies and brokers) and Public Administration.

Infosistema markets products in the financial compliance and retail banking segments. Infosistema Financial Anti-Money Laundering and Fraud Detection solutions helps institutions to cope with increased regulation and scrutiny from regulatory bodies. In a world where penalties from non compliance range from high fines to operations shutdown, Infosistema plays a key role in moving towards a risk free operation.

During the past years we build on a reputation for delevering the same value and quality of the traditional Big 5 at lower costs.

We mix business knowledge, technical knowledge and our own software intelectual property. We are building consultancy services around our business expertise and software:
1 - Anti Money Laundering & Fraud Detection
2 - Netbanks
3 - Connectors for Reuters and Bloomberg feeds
4 - CRM for Investment Banking and Insurance
5 - Core Insurance processes ( ex:claims ).
