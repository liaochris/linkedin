Lindsey Jordan’s Summary

I have a strong work ethic, am a proactive problem-solver and a self-motivated learner. I catch on to things quickly and constantly search for better and more productive ways to do things. Avoiding inertia-by-default and Murphy’s Law are my top priorities, especially when it comes to my clients' best interests. I strive to prevent problems before they happen, and react quickly if they do. I have almost 10 years of experience providing services to clients and understand the importance of trust, honesty and hard work.

I have a background in business administration that is supported by a BBA in Marketing from The University of Georgia. I understand how a successful business works in theory AND in practice. In addition to my degree, I have a minor in Psychology. I feel it helps to know how a target audience thinks and learns and also how to get them to remember a client's brand message. I can multi-task and pin-down the details, but I always keep my eyes on the bigger picture. At the end of the day, my job is to help my client achieve their goals within the budget they have.

I work very hard, and have fun doing it. I have a true passion for my craft and I've been told that I 'just get it.'
