Gary Major’s Summary

Degree-qualified from the UK’s most prominent Automotive Design course, I have over twenty
four years experience in the Transportation Design sector.

My current position is Head of Industrial Design for the global number three manufacturer of construction equipment based in the UK, overseeing the aesthetic design of vehicle interiors and exteriors, guiding a five-man team in creating design solutions for a range of over two hundred products -both construction and consumer products.

My role involves the generation of designs for progressive updates to the machine range and the
initiation of design concepts in response to new market opportunities.

In addition to this I have over seven years prior experience in designing for the Rail industry and two years consultancy work.

Gary Major’s Specialties:

Conceptual thinker, sketch/ rendering skills, design management, client liaison, design presentation, departmental ambassador.
