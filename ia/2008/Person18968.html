Azhar Din’s Summary

Highly experienced in the analogue and wireless domains through 15 years of experience in the industry. Innovative design engineer with a strong commercial awareness.
-Full awareness of product lifecycle management. Experienced in taking concept to production.
-Excellent Semiconductor product lifecycle management. Experienced in dealing with FABs, packaging houses, test houses, wafer probe, final test, product documentation, datasheets, marketing support, business/client management and vendor management.
-Excellent mentor and leadership skills. Builds strong rapport with everyone, wins trust with clients. Good track record of team leadership.
-Can present to large audiences.

Azhar Din’s Specialties:

Analogue and RF semiconductor professional with design and leadership experience. I am experienced in CMOS and Bipolar technologies with some exposure to SiGe. I also design board level RF circuits and have experience of ADC and DSP techniques in RF transceiver designs.

Experienced in Cadence tools, Mentor Graphics, ADS, Matlab, Mathcad and linux system administration.
