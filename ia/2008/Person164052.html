Ted Romanowitz’s Summary

Seasoned strategic product marketing and business development manager with more than sixteen years progressive experience in the computing and consumer electronics industries. Proven expertise in uniquely defining and positioning products, developing distribution channels and capturing market segment share. Highly motivated, results-oriented manager who thrives on challenges. Excellent communication, interpersonal and leadership skills.
