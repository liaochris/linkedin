Marissa Carranza’s Summary

After having my first miscarriage in 2005, I decided to quit my office job in order to heal and rethink what career would most align with my personal goals for having a family someday. Thus, began my interest in digital scrapbooking, for it's cost effective and decreased environmental impact the hobby is characterized for. I quickly found that I enjoyed and am quite good at designing digital scrapbook kits for my personal use. Last year, I was asked to join Kjoistudios as a scrapbook page layout artist. Soon after, I began selling kits at Kjoistudios.com.

Although I graduated with a Bachelor’s in Psychology, I’ve always been artistic. My earliest influence was none other than Ms. Barham, my 5th grade art teacher. So, it’s really been a long time coming, to finally be doing what really makes me happy.

I am in the process of developing my website CACTUSmangoDesigns.com to house my growing product line of digital scrapbook kits, in addition to my current store at Kjoistudios.com.

Marissa Carranza’s Specialties:

Independent graphics designer using Photoshop CS2 and Macraomedia Flash 8, specializing in digital photo restoration/alteration,invitations, thank you cards, business cards, T-shirt logo design, digital scrapbook paper/embellishment design and traditional/digital/hybrid album layout design.
