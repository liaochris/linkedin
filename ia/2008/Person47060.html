David Leppek’s Summary

I have over ten years experience with eCommerce and credit card processing. My current role is responsible for managing, maintaining, growing and supporting a large portfolio of existing direct processing merchants as well as a full ISO channel. To accomplish this, I manage a team of Account Managers that regularly train and present a suite of products, monitor transaction patterns and regularly work to resolve operational issues. In the past my responsibilities have included initial integration, enterprise sales presentations, application development, product and project planning through every stage of a complex life-cycle. I work very closely with Risk to mitigate our liability as the front-line of communication, especially related to technical solutions to prevent fraud.
