Philip Raskin’s Summary

Philip Raskin has been involved in the field of corporate communications for over fifteen years as a producer, creative director and consultant. He has a degree in composition and performance from Berklee College of Music. He has been featured on recordings and in live performance with many internationally recognized artists.

Combining his creative background with his experience in live performance, Mr. Raskin began producing events integrating video and interactive multimedia. His original approach began to attract industry attention and a growing list of Fortune 100 clients.

For more than ten years, Mr. Raskin has been the President, CEO and Creative Director of Raskin Associates. He has produced award-winning media and events in thirty seven countries on six continents. His prestigious list of clients includes Merck, Johnson & Johnson, United Healthcare, Young & Rubicam/WPPP, Perrier, Reuters, NYSE Euronext, and NASDAQ among many others. He has been a featured presenter at executive conferences worldwide and is a member of Meeting Professionals International, the Healthcare Convention and Exhibitors Association and the International Communications Industries Association.
