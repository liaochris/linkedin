Craig Stevens, PMP, CC’s Summary

Over 20 years in highly competitive manufacturing, information technology, and service environments, both for commercial, non-profit, and government clients. Responsible for many millions of dollars worth of sales, proposals and grants. Worked with every level of management and labor in a number of different industries related to: HR, Manufacturing, Transportation, Shipping and Receiving, Product Design, R&D, Waste Management and Environmental Restoration, Procurement, Construction, Healthcare, Government and the Restaurant/Hotel/Motel Industry.

Organized, started and managed business ventures/programs/projects. Improved organizational/business processes/productivity/quality. Performed strategic planning and scheduling. Developed world-class models on key management, financial and organizational issues.

Designed, developed, lead and facilitated team building and team problem solving programs. Improved productivity and motivation. Developed performance standards, performance based technical training, and public and internal customer service training. Developed Customer Relationships Management programs.

Developed programs for operations, systems interface definition and control, configuration management, information/records management, reliability engineering, customer feedback and requirements and performance/risk management. Performed major facilities design and development and value engineering, human factors, pollution prevention/waste minimization, site planning/plant layouts; automated plant design; lean manufacturing; life cycle cost analysis; activity based costing; safety analysis; production-process flow analysis; shipping and receiving analysis; probabilistic risk assessment. Provided support on environmental impact research, hazardous waste transportation, energy-related subjects, international business and product design. Managed and participated in the planning, programming, budgeting, enactment, execution, and auditing of major systems.
