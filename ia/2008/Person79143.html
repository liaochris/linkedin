Izabela Czekaj’s Summary

Knowledge, skills and experiences
• quantum chemical methods (density functional theory);
• theoretical simulation of surface processes;
• molecular mechanism of heterogeneous catalysis and chemical reactions;
• solid state chemistry; modeling of catalytical active surfaces;
• interpretation of experimental results (UPS, NEXAFS, STM, XPS, AES);
• experimental surface analysis, UHV conditions, ESCA equipment;
• soft condensed matter: colloids, dispersed systems, rheological properties, quality control of dispersed systems (cosmetic, pharmaceutical and biological emulsions);
• colloid particles interactions, structure and properties of newtonian and non-newtonian liquids;
• chemical engineering: reactors (bio- and membrane reactors), mass and heat transport, kinetic, mechanical/technical constructions and solutions in chemical processes;

Scientific Education

2004, July, PhD thesis (with distinction): „Electronic structure of V-O, Ti-O, Cr-O and V-P-O systems with metal on III oxidation state”;
