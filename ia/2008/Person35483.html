Chris J. Snook’s Summary

Chris J. Snook (www.chrisjsnook.com) Started Action Potentials, (www.actionpotentials.com) with his wife in 2003. Prior to Action Potentials he has spent over 10 years as an author, entrepreneur, and investor. He has built a global marketing and distribution business in the U.S. Canada, Japan, and Hong Kong, and has spent the last 5 years in the investment community as well. He co-authored a best-selling book entitled Burnout: How to Transform Frustration to Fortune, (www.burnoutbooks.com) that teaches Personal Trainers how to successfully run and start a profitable business. In his entrepreneurial experience he has raised over $2 million dollars in the last 24 months for various micro-equity deals and partnerships, and his overall responsibilities have included strategic and financial planning, business plan development, financial management and executive oversight responsibilities, as well as arranging for accounting, legal council, and banking services. He is currently coaching Enterprise Sales companies, mentoring LifeSuccess Consultants for Bob Proctor in over 13 countries, as well as launching a "Lifestyle Enhancement" private club in downtown San Diego in partnership with Fortress Financial Partners. His most recent personal development smash hit book "Weath Matters...Abundance is your birthright" www.wealtmattersbook.com, is available in bookstores and online worldwide. Mr. Snook holds a BS degree from West Chester University in Exercise Physiology and did his graduate work in Exercise and Nutritional Science at San Diego State University. He attended San Diego State University and received his MSBA in Entrepreneurial Finance in 2005.

Chris J. Snook’s Specialties:

Private Equity, fitness and nutrition consulting and planning for individuals, health club acquisition and operations, business coach to middle-market enterprise sales companies in partnership with Sellmasters, Inc. www.sellmasters.com and www.universalselling.com
