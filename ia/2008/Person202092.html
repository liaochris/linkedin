Yiannis Kouropalatis’s Summary

Yiannis Kouropalatis joined the School of Management at the Unviersity of Surrey in February 2008 as a lecturer. Prior to that, he was employed for more than 2 years as a research associate at Cardiff business school following an international management consulting career. He has worked in international environments including over 4 years as a management consultant for PA Consulting Group (UK), Logica UK Ltd and KANTOR Management Consultants (Athens – Greece). As part of his management consulting engagements, he led board level strategic development, change and turnaround projects for large multinationals in the UK, Greece, France and the Balkans. His consulting portfolio includes clients such as BP (IT strategy development), AstraZeneca (Global resource forecasting), Eurobank (Strategic IT development in the Balkans), UBS (On-line private banking re-deployment strategy), COLT Telecoms (European network management), etc. Prior to his management consulting appointments and following his graduation from Sussex University, he gained industrial experience in the microelectronics design and production industry (3 years) before moving to the Strategy consulting sector.
Yiannis holds a BEng (1996) and a PhD (2000) from Sussex University as well as an MBA (2002) from ALBA (Athens Laboratory of Business Administration). His current research activities include projects in the areas of strategic peripheral vision , strategic competitive reasoning, organisational perception and 'health' as well as organsiational behaviour and corporate social responsibility.
