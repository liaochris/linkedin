Erica Dooley (Jacobs)’s Summary

Erica Dooley joined Preferred Public Relations & Marketing (PPR&M) as an Associate Account Executive in June 2007 and was promoted to Account Executive in January 2008.

At PPR&M, Erica fully manages her accounts, which includes, but is not limited to, development and implementation of public relations strategies, day-to-day client relations, media relations, planning grand openings and special events and the creation of press materials.

Prior to joining PPR&M, Erica worked at The Hoffman Agency. Erica’s responsibilities at the The Hoffman Agency included communication with top-tier publications, press releases writing, coordination across the Agency’s global offices, day-to-day research and analysis of coverage and pitching and securing editorial calendar, speaking and award opportunities for her clients.

Erica holds a bachelor’s degree in public relations from San Jose State University.
