Steve Suhrheinrich’s Summary

I have extensive knowledge of capital markets and a wide range of experience in the securities industry – from equities and fixed income trading to structured finance. I enjoy the study of Corporate finance, especially within the PIPE market. It's my desire to finance growth expansion and acquisitions strategies for micro and small cap public companies

Yorkville Advisors provides equity investments and innovative financing solutions to growing companies in the micro and small-cap sector, worldwide. We work closely with management to design custom-tailored, alternative financing structures that meets a company's capital needs and corporate goals. We finance each transaction through our family of investment funds.

Steve Suhrheinrich’s Specialties:

Assist emerging companies with a range of financing options such as straight equity purchases, structured equity financing, convertibles, equity-linked notes, and asset-backed loans.
