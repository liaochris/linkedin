• Includes 2 years conceptualising, marketing and driving the environmental transformation programme and UK environmental offerings for LogicaCMG, targeted at lowering the company’s environmental impact and raising awareness across staff worldwide.

I am currently group environmental champion for LogicaCMG, including developing strategy and policy, managing the UK wide Carbon Footprint Reduction programme, developing and promoting environmental offerings, and co-ordinating the global role out of this programme across the LogicaCMG group. This has enabled me to become LogicaCMG's expert in this field.

My goals are to help business realise their targets for growth and profit, alongside helping them transform their businesses into the low carbon economy. The next 10-20 years are going to see big changes in business practice as energy supplies change and become a much greater influence on business processes, products and profits.

My experience spans Environment, Energy & Utilities, Telecommunications (mobile and fixed line operators), Space and Transport & Logistics markets. I have delivered international projects across multiple countries including Australia, Italy, UK, Ireland, Belgium, Portugal, Finland and Malaysia, including using French, German and Italian within the business environment.
