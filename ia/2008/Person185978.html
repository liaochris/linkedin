Tim Bealert’s Summary

I currently serve as a Principal for The Laramie Group and am a founding member of the firm. My role focuses on business development, client management and execution of key searches for Laramie.

Prior to helping form The Laramie Group, I worked at a top 5 National Executive Search firm in Chicago in a generalist capacity helping to manage and execute the CEO and founder’s book of business. I also was a lead Principal at JobPlex, the Middle Management arm of this leading Executive Search firm. I have vast experience in executing retained search assignments, while identifying the best and the brightest candidates for their chosen field.

I also have significant experience working on a variety of one-off search assignments across various functions and industries. I have managed large volume enterprise recruiting projects where I worked on-site with two major Fortune 500 companies. I have partnered directly with senior leadership and functional heads to establish and implement a recruitment process while also managing the full recruitment life cycle. Before joining the Middle Management Search firm, I worked as a Recruiter for a national boutique Executive Search firm focused in the healthcare industry.

My goal is to service my clients with the best talent while working efficiently and effectively on a cost effective basis for my clients. By using our proven process and methodologies, we give our clients the value of a retained search, at the middle management space, traditionally serviced by contingent firms.

Tim Bealert’s Specialties:

My specialty is multi-position project recruiting and direct recruiting for Executive level and Middle Management opportunites. This includes: retained search, research, sourcing, name generation, ATS management, candidate development, screening, interviewing, cold calling, networking, business development, process design and implementation, diversity recruiting.
I work across various industries including non-profit, manufacturing, financial services, CPG, pharamaceuticals
