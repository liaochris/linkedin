Vicki Davis’s Summary

Currently at Westwood Schools, in Camilla, Georgia, Vicki is transforming the way people think about teaching. Her co-created Flat Classroom project has recently won ISTE's award for Best Online Learning Project for 2007. She also recently completed the Horizon Project with classrooms in Bangladesh, Austria, China, and Australia and is part of the effort to research and document the improvement in thinking skills and cultural awareness through global collaborative projects.

Vicki blogs at the popular Cool Cat Teacher blog, is a blogger for Tech Learning, is a co founder of the Women of Web 2 and a co-host of the popular weekly webcast/podcast WOW2 over at ed tech talk at 9 pm Eastern on Tuesday nights where many amazing educators who are using Web 2 technology talk about best practice in the classroom.

Vicki has presented at many online conferences and at some face to face conferences (when her teaching schedule allows.) She is a member of ISTE and the CSTA.

Vicki has written for several publications about Web 2 technologies in the classroom and continues to pursue freelance work both writing for magazines and course content development work and consulting.

Vicki's goal is to publish inspirational, instructional books and blogs helping people adapt and learn in a changing technology-centric society and further the cause of global collaborative projects between the classrooms of the world.
