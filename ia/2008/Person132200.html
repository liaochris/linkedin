Mike Hawkins’s Summary

People and financial management with new business development within an Internet Based Software services organisation. Fast growth period specialist.
Mike manages the nationwide sales team and is responsible for marketing, sales and customer service. Mike brings vast sales and recruitment experience from his lengthy recruitment consultant career with such companies as Adecco and Dunhill (now Robert Walters). Combining his recruitment background with senior executive roles across diverse industries including financial, logistics and FMCG, Mike has taken BigRedSky to the largest Australian provider of e-Recruitment systems and is now embarking on the co-ordination of the international expansion of the BigRedSky product.
In 2006, added retail management to the portfolio operating a small niche business based in Hawthorn Victoria.
