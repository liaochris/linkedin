Shefali Gaur [1700+]’s Summary

I am the Community Manager of Communication and Communities products of Yahoo! India based in Bangalore. In this role; I facilitate, manage and help my communities engaging and interacting better with each other and I am their SPOC for any concern regarding the product.

I am an MBA and Engineering graduate with about 5 years of experience in the IT services & Products and Internet industry. So far in my career, I have worked in almost all the disciplines of business development like Sales, Sales Support, Presales, Marketing, Knowledge Management, across multiple domains. Now I have landed up in a completely new role that I not only find exciting but very fulfilling as well.

I have had my clientele across the globe so it was quite interesting working with people of such varied background and national origin. I have managed bids for millions of dollars, created loads and loads of Marketing collateral, optimized processes, done market research and competitive analysis, done front-end and back-end sales assignments etc. I have also taught Brand Management and Customer Relationship Management at B-schools and conducted several workshops for Group Discussion and Personal Interview skills.

With all this diverse experience, I have attained the desired communication and interpersonal skills for me to aspire to be a community person. The focus has to be on the people; facilitating their communication and interaction within the community, proactively finding better solutions for their problems; finding innovative solutions for better collaboration; and implementing ways to increase productivity and efficiency of community.

I am person of positive attitude and unbreakable spirit. I exist to do anything and everything that makes my community – and all people who contribute to it – more successful. My core competency is in my perspective towards people and the cause for which they get together as a community.
