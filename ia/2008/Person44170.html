Dan Knauss’s Summary

Moving from humanities academic background to free-lance web design/IT/communications and related services. Work focus targets Milwaukee small businesses and community non-governmental organizations (NGOs).

Hands-on experience with property management and rehab, neighborhood organizing and development, voluntary associations, neighborhood organizations, municipal politics.

Educational background and experience in writing, editing, speaking, and teaching. Graduate study in early modern literature, history, and religion (medieval and renaissance, 14th-17th centuries) with an emphasis on old/new/emerging media: manuscript and print culture, the history of the book, micro-history, and material history.

Dan Knauss’s Specialties:

Open Source; Content Management Systems; Mambo and Joomla CMS; HTML/CSS design; writing; editing.

I am interested in geographically rooting and de-virtualizing the world-wide-web by contributing to the emerging hyper-local web for practical complements to daily, public life: citizen journalism, participatory politics, self-education, bringing the "public record" of individuals, corporations, government and non-governmental agencies into the "public interest" with IT.
