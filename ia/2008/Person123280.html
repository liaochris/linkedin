Marti M. Taylor’s Summary

Marti began her career in open heart surgery in 1985 with Dr.Denton Cooley. She is a Board Certified First Assistant in plastic and reconstructive surgery as well as general, vascular, cardiovascular, and gynecological surgery. With 23years of experience, she was elected to the the Governing Council Board of RNFAs representing and mentoring all RNFAs in Texas, Arizona, Arkansas, New Mexico and Oklahoma in her role as Region VI Representive. Marti has authored publications in medical journals, and is proficient in the use and application of all laser types. Marti performs major surgical procedures with 22 surgeons in the Texas Medical Center and Houston metropolitan area.
