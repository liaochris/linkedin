Carrie Vander Pas’s Summary

Artist/Designer Carrie Vander Pas has had a passion for the arts for as long as she can remember. As a child, growing up in the small town of Little Chute, WI, she spent most of her time sketching and doodling various popular cartoon characters. She developed a particular love for characters from Disney movies, and began to draw them over and over until they looked just like they did in the movies.

In high school, Carrie began to refine her artistic style (drawing, painting, etc.), steering away from the "cartoonish" style and focusing more on realism. During this time, she was also introduced to the world of graphic & web design. After completing a course in web design her senior year of high school (2004), she was hired as an Intern at Appleton Papers.

After graduating high school, Carrie attended the University of Wisconsin-Oshkosh for 2 years, but realized she belonged in a more artistic environment. In 2007, she began attending the prestigious Milwaukee Institute of Art + Design in Milwaukee, WI. She is currently pursuing a degree in Communication Design and will graduate in May of 2010.
