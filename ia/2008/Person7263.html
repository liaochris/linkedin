Alfred Aoude’s Summary

Aerospace structural consultant highly experienced in finite element analysis and classical stress analysis. Strong experience of overall aircraft primary structural components, cockpit, fuselage, wing, pylon, nacelle, empenage, interior and systems.
CATIA V5 Customization using CAAV5. Solution development for Boeing Rapid FEM program.

Founder and owner of AeroStruct.

Alfred Aoude’s Specialties:

Structural Analysis, Composite structures. Nastran, Patran, CATIA V5, CAAV5, CatScript, Catia VBA.
