Juan Mateu’s Summary

I become founder of Solaiemes (innovation start-up focused on mobility) after 12 years of professional experience in IT, foreign trade, consultancy and telecom (working in cellular infrastucture and mobile devices supplier). I developed several roles in my previous jobs and I was assigned 4 years abroad, I spent 1 year in SouthAmerica, 1 in china and 3 among several european countries.
Currently I am trying to use all my background about different telecom and IT markets, the know-how about mobility trends and the adquired skills to create new compelling solutions for the challenging world of telecom. :-).
Solaiemes target is to become a reference supplier of soft infrastructure for IMS based mobile solutions.We are pioneers in using VideoShare for enterprise use cases and mobile to web 2.0 interaction.
