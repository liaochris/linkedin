Luca Lupotto’s Summary

My graduate studies are Business Administration (2004, Università degli Studi di Torino - Economia aziendale) and General Management (2006, Univesità Commerciale L.Bocconi, Milano). I had an international experience in Monterrey, Mexico, where I spent one semester in late 2005, and a quick experience of 3-month stage in IBM Italia in the business partners support office.
My final graduation work was around the role of Competitive Intelligence in the dynamics of strategy.

I have two years of job experience as a consultant in Business Services.
Main sectors I have been working in are: TLC, Public Sector, Service & Fashion, Energy, Heavy Industry.
I especially deepen my knowledge in Business Process Management and Improvement, Supply Chain Management, Six Sigma Model, Quality and Health&Safety Management Systems.
I like to be involved in projects aimed to analyze and solve complex and strategic business issues.
