Jiahong Juda, Ph.D.’s Summary

At BurnBright Partners, I help people take their professional success to a totally different level. Starting from critical issues at hand, I help people achieve desired impact by creating a safe space for them to fully use their cognitive capacities, creative energy, and uncover their own authentic leadership voice. I work with them primarily as a thinking partner to challenge their assumptions, expand their perspectives and support their long-term growth.
