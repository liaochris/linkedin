Gregory Foss’s Summary

Certified Employment Specialist (CES) and Certified Personnel Consultant (CPC).

San Francisco Division Director for Legal Specialists and The Specialists Group LLC, a leading provider of legal professionals on a project and full-time basis. The company specializes in the placement of attorneys, paralegals and legal support personnel for law firms and corporate legal departments. Additionally, the organization provides executive, managerial and technical staffing for a wide variety of other nationally and internationally recognized professional services organizations.

Gregory Foss’s Specialties:

25 years of experience in matching qualifications and experience in management, technical, and professional career fields; career consulting; salary negotiation; new recruiting trends and techniques, reference checking, obtaining background information, and problem-solving clients' employee relations issues.
