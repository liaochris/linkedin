Paul Copier’s Summary

As a structural engineer/advisor/consultant & project manager, i have experience in all angles of building-processes. I am familiar with aspects of initial designs, specifications, buiding-proces, costs, delivery and program & maintenance inspections. I am client focused and through my analytical skills, I am able to help clients clearly define their problem, advise them, solve problems accured and manage the building-proces. Goal is to find the desired and most fitting solution. Besides this i have experience in managing a company and devise new stategy for company and custumors. I have worked with several well known architects, contractors, real estate companies, system engineers, salesmen, managing directors and CEO's.

My goal is to manage and complete several great civilian projects in leading positions. Furthermore i want to implement new communication-forms within all kinds of industries, like e-learning and rich media, to improve communication, enlarge databases with company content and to accomplish lower costs.

Paul Copier’s Specialties:

I'm specialized in prestressed concrete, concrete slabs, e-learning and rich media
