Tadeusz Kifner’s Summary

Independent consultant and adviser in relation to IT governance issues, controlling environment creation and process improvement. Possessed ability to reformulate business strategy and operational aims into IT services and business processes.

Experienced IT manager dealing with all management levels in various organizations with different business models, cultures and approaches. Involved in challenging multinational undertakings like: implementation of first electronic card for Polish Credit Unions, building from a scratch new ERP system for warehouse company or building one of the first medical portals in polish Internet. Familiar with all aspects of creation and implementation of modern information systems based on newest technology. Lead several information technology projects for banking, administration, financial institutions, trade and servicing companies, which all were successful.

Author of several books about IT adaptation, especially about IT security management. Speaker at numerous conferences and business meetings.

Today focused on IT Governance, Security and Risk&Control Management in organizations and on Internet Application Development and Certifications.
