Sondra Fuller’s Summary

"Pure" is a retained executive search firm located in Decatur, AL and Ft. Lauderdale, FL.
Specialties include C-level executives, with emphasis on Regulatory Affairs, Operations and Manufacturing, Informatics/IT, Oncology, Finance, Proteomics, Genomics, Business Development, Instrumentation, Sales & Marketing, Microfluids/Microarrays and Molecular and Cellular Biology.
She has located talented, hard-to-find individuals from the senior scientist level to senior management. for both growing innovative companies and major pharma.
She was interviewed for the 2006 book "LinkedIn for Recruiters."
She brings to this experience owning/running an entrepreneurial & life style management training company which was also a very successful contractor to the US Department of Labor and the VA as well as the State of CT. Empower ran intensive entrepreneurial training programs for unemployed US service veterans as well as for at-risk middle and high school students in CT. It pioneered the use of graphic novels or comics to teach subjects such as Physics or Managing Money. It created the NeXT School (New eXperiments in Transition), an innovative and experimental school for at-risk 9th graders, for the Hartford CT Board of Education. Prior to this, she spent several years as publisher of the Connecticut Business Journals.
Earlier, she was a SEC Registered Investment Advisor and, in what seems now like a much earlier life, a political campaign manager and a president of several League of Women Voters groups around the country. Additionally, she was the Lobbyist at the CT Legislature for the CT League where she was one of a small group who were instrumental in getting asbestos cement water pipes banned from use.

\Featured in "LinkedIn for Recruiting" by Bill Vick and Des Walsh: http://www.happyabout.info/linkedin4recruiting.php
