Pranab Prakhar’s Summary

Pranab Prakhar is Broadcast Journalist based in London and New Delhi. He is Reporter & Producer. He works as On Camera Live Anchor and Radio Presenter. He is qualified Video Journalist and documentary film maker. Pranab has worked in India, UK & UAE and provided his professional expertise to well known media organizations around the world in India, UK, Belgium, Germany, UAE, USA. He is a media professional with over 11 years of Broadcast expertise.
While working as a Broadcast Scriptwriter, he has worked on variety of subjects for international TV and Radio channels.

His area of media reporting are: News & Current Affairs, Information Technology & Telecommunication, Features stories, Business and Finance, Health & Fitness, Sports, Entertainment & Music.


Pranab Prakhar studied MA International Broadcast Journalism and was awarded Commendation for his academic excellence. He studied Diploma in Music Technology & Sound Engineering from London.

Pranab Prakhar’s Specialties:

On Camera Live Broadcast Anchoring, Scriptwriter, Documentary Films, Radio Presenter/Jockey, Sound Engineering, Music Recording/making, Video Editing, Camera works.

Undertaking overall TV productions - Editorial, Programming, Production,Graphics & Animation, Camera & Aesthetics, Live Studio & Location Filming.

Leading, Managing & Administering teams of creative media professionals in mid-size organizations for conceptualizing, planning & producing Audio-Video Productions & Scriptwriting.
