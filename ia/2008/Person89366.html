Jeremy Meyers’s Summary

I am a veteran of the online and digital media world, being professionally involved since 1999 and playing a small part in shaping the landscape for music in the online space.

I started at indie TVT Records, helping to secure promotions for hugely successful acts including Lil Jon's breakout record "Kings of Crunk" and Sevendust.

I was hired by Sony Music to run their word-of-mouth department in 2004, which included monitoring messagebaords, and introduced the execs to a fledgling site called MySpace. This culminating in my helping to launch the MySpace Music brand with a band called Midtown.

Currently, I oversee all online marketing and sales for the classical, film score and broadway division of SONY BMG. Additionally, I coordinate delivery and marketing of SonyBMG's massive collection of music videos, as well as maintaining sites for our cutting-edge podcasts and blog.
