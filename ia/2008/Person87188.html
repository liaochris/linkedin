Jeff Justice’s Summary

Jeff’s career began as Producer/Director for Arisen Corporation's Alive Today television program which aired globally on the Inspirational Network and continued as an On-air Announcer & Production Technician at Arisen’s 7-station FM satellite network. After 4 years with Arisen, Jeff returned to his home state of Florida.

There Jeff launched Godspeed Communications, a freelance "integrated" marketing, media & technology group. Godspeed grew quickly providing creative services to small businesses, non-profit & government agencies.

While at the helm of Godspeed, Jeff took the position of Media Director with Brenmar Advertising, Inc. in Lakeland, FL. There he managed the firm’s design, direct mail & media purchasing departments. His tenure saw the 2 most profitable years in Brenmar's history.

Later, Jeff returned to his hometown of Orlando. There he grew Godspeed to include live production & technology design. Jeff oversaw the business development of a national live production company and with the Godspeed team directed convention productions for the Department of Defense, multimedia design for several Fortune 500 companies and managed the design & construction of a multi-use production/demonstration facility for Altec Lansing Technologies.

In 2004, Jeff joined Dynetech Corporation, a leading Business Process Outsource provider as Multimedia Specialist. There he received the Circle of Excellence award for his exemplary work in the Sales Division's Multimedia & Audio-Visual Departments.

2006 brought Jeff to Compass Knowledge Group as Sales Support Specialist. There he designed effective sales tools and implemented strategic marketing initiatives for 10 universities and provided education services to global military commands & personnel.

Now as a Freelance Creative Consultant, Jeff has spent 2008 melding his years of successful experience with his distinct & innovative approach to bring relevant & powerful Sales & Marketing concepts to clients worldwide.
