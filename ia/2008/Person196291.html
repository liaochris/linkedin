Vijay Goel, M.D.’s Summary

Entrepreneur developing new models to engage and empower consumers by helping them to shop for healthcare services (and have better assurance that they will receive services optimized for their needs/wants).

Also, independent management/ strategy consultant (previously led engagements at McKinsey & Co) as I build my business.

We're creating a web-based platform/network to promote transparency, choice, personalization, and service in the health services space. We're starting in wellness and looking to help consumers engage in their everyday health by providing deep information to help them pick the right services and activities for them.

We're helping providers make more money by highlighting their value proposition and helping them book more clients.
