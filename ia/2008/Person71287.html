Giorgio Borroni’s Summary

GOLEM is a European Sport Agency with branches in UK, Luxembourg, Italy and others to come soon.

We are leaders in organizing inter-business team building competitions, having specialised for more than 5 years in creating high performance teams for businesses through a unique team building formula.

Our events put company against company in what is publicly recognised as one of the most succesfull team building ideas: playing and competing against your day by day competitor. Our clients include some of the biggest organizations in the world.

The concept is tried, tested, proven and rapidly growing. Its success means that GOLEM now attracts many of the greatest companies to play sport events like the CHAMPIONSCHIP, the CORPORATE LIGA, the SIM CUP, the FINANCE FOUR , the SILICON VOLLEY and our most loved success: the OLIMPIX.

The OLIMPIX project is an event dedicated and tailored to every single company who wants to involve as many employees as possible, by offering them the chance to create fun and good relationships between colleagues even outside the office. Through our unique multi-challenge formula, OLIMPIX gives you the opportunity to play as many sports as you want, choosing between soccer, jorkyball, bowling, gokart, golf and minigolf, tennis, volley and beach volley, kayak, basketball and many others.
