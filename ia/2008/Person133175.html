Todd Marks’s Summary

Todd Marks is an information architect and user experience specialist, focusing on the design and development of cutting edge information display systems. Todd is the founder and President of Mindgrub (www.mindgrub.com), makers of user centric knowledge systems, and co-founder of viaPlace (www.viaplace.com), a location based services and mobile social networking framework.

In 2000 Todd moved from teaching Mathematics and Computer Science in the public sector to Vice President of Research & Development at Digital Organism. In 2003 Todd worked as a Program Manager for Exceptional Software where he grew mediaEdge's Training division. In 2005 Todd co-founded the location based services company Spencer & Marks and established the first geospatial experience registry, viaPlace.com. In 2006 Todd worked as a Senior Consultant for NYC based Roundarch Inc. where he designed and developed leading Rich Internet Applications. Todd also works for the University of Maryland and Future Media Concepts as an Instructional Technology Adj. Professor and Adobe Certified Trainer.

Todd is currently growing Mindgrub Technologies, Spencer & Marks, speaking, and instructing. Todd's efforts have garnished several awards including a 'Best of Baltimore' ADDY Award, Macromedia Shocked Site of the Day and a 1st Place at DCI's Portal Excellence Awards Competition for Best Content Management System. Todd is an Adobe Certified Developer & Designer, has spoken at several conferences, been featured in several technical magazines and has written or been a contributing author for a number of books including Flash MX Video, Beginning Dreamweaver MX, Flash Magic, New Masters of Flash and Web Design in a Nutshell.

Notable Clients: Adobe, Motorola, ORACLE, ARINC, A&E, Discovery Channel, Bear Sterns, Northern Trust, US Intelligence Community, FBI, PBGC, USAF, NAVSEA, US NAVY, Universal Records, Verve Music, Motown Records, Kurzweil AI, New Riders, Friends of Ed, Wrox Press, Wiley, O’Reilly Media.
