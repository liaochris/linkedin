Gordon A. Rogers’s Summary

Established a business (Meliora Systems, Inc. 1988-1998) that created one of the pioneering products in the training management and e-learning field (Ingenium). Guided the growth of the company from start-up to a sale to Paul Allen's company, Asymetrix Learning Systems (ASYM), coinciding with its June 1998 IPO. Asymetrix evolved into Click2learn.com(CLKS), merged with Docent (DCNT), and is now known as SumTotal Systems (SUMT).

After leaving CLKS/SUMT, invested in and served on several advisory boards of various tech startups in the e-learning and online coaching sectors. Currently working with LTLConnect (www.ltlconnect.com) a UK-based company focused on improving the performance of individuals and the productivity of the workplace, using an online coaching and support model.

Working with several other Atlanta area tech founders/entrepreneurs to develop a new model of fostering start-ups and providing the requisite mentorship and guidance needed to get out of the garage and ready for prime time.

Also exploring new ventures in the GIS, LBS and mobile geospatial applications universe.

Extensive experience serving on boards of non-profit organizations (YMCA of Greater Atlanta, Interfaith Outreach Home, Educator Support Network, The Lionheart School).

Gordon A. Rogers’s Specialties:

Product strategy, web 2.0 services, (e.g. online health & wellness and professional coaching/ support,) E-Learning. Social networking in the GIS, LBS and mobile application space.
