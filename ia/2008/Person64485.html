Florian Urmetzer’s Summary

At present Florian is concluding his PhD at the University of Reading. Florian is a researcher who is keen to apply his valuable skills in industry as a R&D researcher or consultant. At present he is a guest researcher at the Politecnico di Milano, where he is mainly bettering his italian language skills and awaiting the PhD viva. In the past he has achived two distinction degrees, a MSc in Network Centered Computing and a BSc in Media Technology and his PhD, he has gained excellent experience in using and applying analytical and research tools. During his time as a researcher he has presented and conducted research on all continents and has therefore gained valuable communication skills and international awareness. Florian is a native German speaker, speaks English accent free and is bettering his Italian at present to conversation level.
