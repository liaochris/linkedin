Rosnin Mustaffa’s Education

  • Universiti Teknologi MARA

    Master, Engineering (Instrumentation), 2004 — 2008 (expected)

    For my master, my thesis is entitle "Development of Artificial Intelligence (Artificial Neural Network) for fiber optic Biosensor Instrumentation using Programmable Intelligent Controller". It is to research and develop an instrument using Photo detector and Fiber Optic as Biosensor for optical detection of Organophosphate pesticide. The instrument is able to work independently, battery operated and having a stand alone display module, as well as capable to be connected to a personal computer for data storage and manipulation. To reduce power consumption and simplify the design, a Programmable Intelligent Controller is used to replace the functions of most analog components in the previous Fiber Optic Measurement Devices design.
    The Graphical User Interface is written using Visual Basic and Visual C++ is used for Artificial Neural Network program in estimating the amount of Organophosphate level (calculated in p.p.m) in the sample.
