    The Capability Alignment Professionals was founded to improve project productivity and success rates.

    The OCB (Online Community Builder) monitors risk on IT projects. Where project management tools track the past, OCB shows you today what people working on the project predict will happen tomorrow.

    The Incentive-Contract Alignment Network (I-CAN) links incentives to the completion of deliverables to align worker motivations with company goals. It also creates an internal market to discover and effect probability of a projects success.

    I-CAN gives people not only the opportunity to work on projects, but they can invest in them too.
