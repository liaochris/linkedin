Keith Shirley (K.Shirley@Shercom.com)’s Summary

MY APPROACH:

Ability to think “out of the box” is very important on many projects where the currently available solutions are just not enough. Working together with analysts, developers, testers and the users I help come up with suitable implementations of IT that fit the real needs of the business.

I have learned that being open with the customer and getting them involved in the problem solving process will make a major difference in the success of a project. Thorough reporting to the decision makers and involvement of all the shareholders in a project meant that I could consistently deliver projects within budget, to requirements and on time.

I have excellent interpersonal, organizational and communication skills. I’m blessed with intuitive IT skills and have a love for all things technical. A burning desire to always learn and experience new challenges has given me a solid background in management and IT theories and practices.
I have 13 years experience in IT and telecoms, 8 years experience running my own project management and IT consultancy company and I was on the board of directors of a multinational telecoms company based in Germany.
I am very good at working in a team environment but I have the drive needed for innovation and exceeding expectations.
