David Scheffler’s Summary

I lead the recruitment compliance practice at HRworks, that provides consulting to clients on how to modify their current recruitment process to comply with the OFCCP regulations. The recruitment compliance department also provides consulting on recruitment process engineering, applicant tracking system selection assistance, recruitment and OFCCP compliance training and recruitment compliance audits. In my current role I am also over the process engineers that partner with our clients to map out the recruitment process and define the success metrics for the project. After the requirements for the project have been gathered the process engineers assist the recruitment team in creating the training material that outlines how the clients applicant tracking system will be integrated into the overall recruitment process.

I have worked with many Fortune 500 companies in my career which a small sample would include TI, SunTrust, Jacobs Engineering, Waste Management, Nokia, HP, Cingular, IBM, Countrywide, The Home Depot, ADP, and Starbucks. In the past I have led sales engineering teams and sales teams that provide professional services as well as software.
