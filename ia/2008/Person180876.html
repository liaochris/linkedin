Stephen J. Walsh’s Summary

Mr. Walsh has over 15 years of experience as an award-winning copywriter and creative supervisor. He has made major contributions to top-shelf accounts at some of the earth's premier advertising and direct marketing agencies. Steve has a rare ability to combine dead-on strategic thinking with a sophisticated tone and, when appropriate, a sharp sense of humor that creative directors, clients and consumers find irresistible. See his online portfolio at tcbwalsh.com.

Stephen J. Walsh’s Specialties:

Steve has done profit-generating, award winning work for CItibank, Lexmark, Pfizer, the US Mint, AT&T, The Central Park Conservancy, MasterCard, Cancer Treatment Centers of America, Procter & Gamble, AICPA, Pharmacia & Upjohn, Verizon and many other clients. Steve is also extremely knowledgable and passionate about music and comedy, and combines the two at his popular website Rock Turtleneck: rockturtleneck.blogspot.com
