Malcolm Campbell’s Summary

After a 20+ year career as a college journalism instructor, technical writer and corporate communications director, I now focus on freelance writing and editorial services including grant writing for museums and other small nonprofit organizations.

In addition to ghost-written web content and trade articles, my articles have appeared in the former Atlanta Journal-Constitution Sunday Magazine, the Rosicrucian Digest, Living Jackson Magazine and Nostalgia Magazine.

I am the author of the novel "The Sun Singer" and currently have another novel ready to begin making the rounds of prospective publishers.

Malcolm Campbell’s Specialties:

grant writing, mission statements, nonprofit publicity, feature articles, book reviews, web content, press releases
