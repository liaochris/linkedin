Gregory Keller’s Summary

Professional Objective:

My professional objective is simple: To leave a lasting positive impression on the technology industry and those I am associated with through constant innovation, evangelism and inspiration to the teams I manage. The underlying mantra that I formulate my professional strategies with is to encourage the belief that we all have the opportunity to leave our DNA within the technology we inspire by nurturing the confidence in what we create will help push technology at large to greater plateaus.

Executive Profile:

---Software Product Management, R & D and Release Lifecycle Management

---Technology Evangelism and Presentational Speaking

---Executive and Team Management

An accomplished leader in the research, development and go-to-market introduction of advanced software-based technologies designed to meet business, financial, competitive and customer demands. Effectively manage and motivate multi-disciplinary and highly technical and non-technical/business teams. Solid financially-conservative mentoring has enabled a demeanor to successfully lead and grow organizations through varying economies such as the mid to late 90’s frenzy to its post .com fall out. Record revenue and market share growth was made possible in these post .com periods despite restricted spending by understanding the economy faced by customers and coaching internal R & D and marketing teams to recognize this fact and respond accordingly with the right products, for the right buyers at the right price points. The execution of this strategy requires a complete buy-in beginning with the customer methodically up through and including board level and investment entity agreement. Evangelism and a realistic plan to execute is at the core of this strategy.

Gregory Keller’s Experience

  • Vice President-Products

    Embarcadero Technologies

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Computer Software industry)

    July 2007 — Present (11 months)

    In 2007, the Product Management infrastructure was expanded in order to make a larger entity designed to focus in on a more thorough product lifecycle. The Vice President of Product Management position was augmented to absorb the ownership and management of pre-sales engineers to ensure this team of technical evangelists was deeply involved product training and domain expertise in addition to taking on road map articulation including the management of customer needs.

The Vice President – Product Management position was deeply involved in the positioning of the product portfolio to ensure exactness of fit prior to the acquisition and now is principally involved in the analysis and prioritization of M & A opportunities being driven by the parent, Thoma Bravo.
