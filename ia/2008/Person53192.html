Don Durkee’s Summary

Don Durkee is a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) with over 20 years of counseling and consulting experience. He has treated clients with depression, bipolar disorder, acute stress, posttraumatic stress disorder (PTSD), and dissociative disorders. He holds M.S. and Ed.S. degrees in Professional Counseling with a specialization in psychotraumatology. He also is trained in stress management, clinical hypnosis, and Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR).

Don also provides clincal supervision for other mental health professionals. He is currently the Chairman of the Ethics Committee of the Licensed Professional Counselors Association of Georgia (LPCA), and he is listed in their Registry of Clinical Supervisors. He is also a part-time instructor at Georgia State University and an adjunct faculty member at Argosy University. Don can be contacted by phone at 404-943-9401 or by e-mail at lpcdurkee@mindspring.com. See also http://therapist.psychologytoday.com/38847.

Don Durkee’s Specialties:

Conducts educational workshops for clients and advanced clinical workshops and supervision groups for practicing clinicians. Has also spoken and led sessions on clinical depression, managed care, schizophrenia, psychopharmacology, and ethics at annual conferences of the American Mental Health Counselors Association (AMHCA) in Boston, Chicago, and Philadelphia and at the Licensed Professional Counselors Association (LPCA) of Georgia conferences in Savannah, Peachtree City, and Calloway Gardens.
