Cameron Conway’s Summary

I'm a Community Consultant with Satmetrix. I work with our clients to set the strategy and manage online communities with large memberships made up of their customers.

Previously I was at Fair Isaac in their EDM Strategy Group. Primary work includes marketing strategy and segmentation, customer centricity and loyalty marketing in retail/CPG segment. Prior to FIC I was in GE's Experienced Commercial Leadership Program (ECLP). In the program we do four six-month assigments in sales and marketing. My first project focused on market segmentation and business development for GE Consumer & Industrial in Houston, TX. My next stop was a market development and customer segmentation role at GE Plastics outside of Albany, NY. Then I moved to Manhattan for more segmentation work and strategic marketing planning at NBC Universal's television and digital media groups. In my final assigment I served as the Marketing Manager for CNBC.com with NBCU Digital Media Sales. I was responsible for customer segmentation, prospecting analysis and packaging unique advertising opportunities for our clients.

Prior to GE I was a student at Duke's Fuqua School of Business. This was preceded by six years of varied consumer marketing expeirence; corporate PR, higher education, as an entrepreneur and finally brand management and new product development work.
