Michael M. Cooper’s Summary

Cooper is an international consultant, entrepreneur, manager and bilingual screenwriter/script doctor.

Recently Cooper was founding C.E.O. of GTH, a California technology "transfer" company specialized in repurposing aerospace IP for secondary uses in all sectors.

He has extensive experience studying, working, living and traveling in the U.S. and abroad. Cooper is a 3rd generation New Yorker, lives in California and France, is a member of the S.A.C.D. (French writer's guild), member of the ASPE, a private pilot (FAA certificate), a long-distance runner (5 marathons, etc.), mother tongue English, fluent French, conversant in Spanish, o.k. on the clarinet.
