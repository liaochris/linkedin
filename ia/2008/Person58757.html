Emma King’s Summary

Emma started her career in England, working for Merrill Lynch, Cazenove & Co and Kimberley Clark, where she became an experienced Network Infrastructure Manager, and Implementation Project Manager. She then moved to The British Council to design and manage Systems Integration Policies for new systems implementations. In all roles she navigated the world.

During a year of travel in 2001, Emma worked in New Zealand, as a trainer for Microsoft Certified Courses and moved into the arena of Online Learning. On her return, she became the Support & Services Manager for a simulation company, eventually moving through the ranks, to bring Assima into the US Market, creating Business Alliances and introducing a new technology into the market place.

Emma now works for her own consulting firm providing technical analysis and services surrounding LMS Selection/Implementation, Simulation and Real Time learning projects, and works within the Brandon Hall and Chapman Alliance research teams, where she is researching, and writing reports focusing on new generations of simulation and real time technologies, learning effectiveness, and will be using her network background to write reports which focus on the technical infrastructure required to implement successful online training courses.

Emma is now holds a Greencard, and has chosen to make the North West Suburbs of Chicago her home.

Research, LMS Selection/Implementation, Emerging Technologies, RFP Writing, ROI Analysis, White Papes, Simulation Technolgies, Real Time Learning techniques, eLearning Implementation
