Joel Day’s Summary

I have 20 years experience in the graphic design and creative services industry. My professional experience includes 17 years at Nike, Inc. in various design and production art roles including Mechanical Artist, Production Artist, Graphic Designer, Image Editor, and Image Editor Manager.

Currently, I am Director of Graphic Design & Creative Services for my own company (Tweak). In this role, I've been able to leverage my knowledge and expertise across a much wider range of projects for individuals, companies (large & small) and non-profit organizations. I also work onsite on a freelance/contract basis for various companies from time to time when required to work onsite.

I founded Tweak after my departure from Nike, with the support of my children and reliance on God, without either I would not be half the artist I am. They are my source of strength and inspiration.

Joel Day’s Specialties:

I have a strong learning agility and adept at learning new processes and technologies in a short period of time. I consider myself a lifelong student of my trade. My primary areas of expertise are in Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe InDesign. I am also learning AppleScript programming language to automate common and redundant tasks.
