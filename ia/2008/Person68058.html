Gavin Costello’s Summary

My expertise as an Analyst, Test Manager, Development Manager and Product Manager, at many companies in the Media Space (Canon, Apple, Quickcut, Adstream and BigPond/Telstra) have given me a wide-ranging expertise in the product development process which helps facilitate commercial success.

Most recently, as Product Manager for Consumer Security at Telstra (for BigPond), I have extended my extensive experience in a highly process driven, multi-layered environment. Dealing with the diverse divisions within the Telstra Group as well as multiple external vendors I help continue established relationships and develop new ones in order to provide services which provide our customers with a high quality experience, both during use and when issues arise.
Previously, as Print Product Manager at Adstream, I used the knowledge of almost 10 years experience of the Quickcut Product Suite to assist our Global Staff in attaining higher levels of understanding of that suite and to provide our Customers with a greater level of expertise in our Products and Services as well as facilitating various integrations with systems at various points of the Print, Publishing and related Media Supply Chain.
