Eddy Badrina’s Summary

Eddy Badrina is the Director of Marketing and Corporate Communications for BellTower Technologies. In this role, he leads the the firm’s marketing, advertising and public relations. Working closely with top management, he develops, implements and budgets annual marketing plans, and is responsible for product advertising to support sales, coordination of conferences and special events, and development and maintenance of supporting collateral materials and its web site.

Previously, Eddy was a Business Development Officer for Wells Fargo Bank and its Business Banking Group. He cultivated relationships and determining the banking needs of small and middle market businesses with $2-$20 million in revenue. Prior to joining Wells Fargo, Eddy spent six years in Washington, DC, serving in the federal government. He was the lead legislative advisor for the Middle East and U.N. bureaus at the State Department during 9/11, facilitating communication between policymakers in the Administration and the US Congress. He then joined the Overseas Private Investment Corporation, conducting marketing campaigns and crafting strategic development goals. Finally, he was appointed Executive Director of the White House Initiative on Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders, where he served as the main communicator of policy and agenda between the AAPI community and the Bush Administration.

Eddy is a graduate of Texas A&M University with a degree in Psychology and a Masters in Public Administration and International Affairs. He is married and has a daughter.
