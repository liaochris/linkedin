Jeffrey K. Price’s Summary

I have an Interactive Media Design Bachelors of Fine Arts Degree from The Art Institute of Dallas as well as an Associate of Applied Arts Degree in Computer Animation.

While studying Interactive Media Design, I also interned at a Dallas company called Reflect Systems, where I gained valuable skills in creating live and on demand streaming web casts. In this role, I learned the proprietary software used to link web casts to web cast skins as well as supervised a live web cast held at the school. I was also able to work with the Flash Media Server 2 to create a streaming FLV player using Flash and XML.

In addition to my 8 months as a Junior Technical Developer at InSite Interactive and my internship experience, I have 3 years of experience as a Teaching Assistant at The Art Institute of Dallas where I assisted the instructors in teaching students a variety of software programs including Flash, XHTML, and JavaScript.
