James Latham’s Summary

Expertise in film and media including research, design, and implementation of marketing campaigns for Hollywood, independent, documentary, and short films. Promotion for theatrical and online releases including the creation and distribution of content as well as website development. Also outreach to the film community—companies, schools, film festivals / societies, student / alumni groups, online groups, etc.

Events / programming experience includes curating a film screening and speaker series held at the AFI, with involvement in all key aspects of the series. Also organized, promoted, and hosted a panel discussion at UCLA for a screening of animated shorts.

Marketing-related writing and editing includes interviews, blogs, newsletters, reviews, articles, PowerPoint presentations, program notes, and a book-length historical study of early Hollywood promotion. Also selecting video clips for trailers.

Presentation experience includes panels at professional film and media studies conferences as well as weekly gallery lectures at the Museum of Modern Art on topics including film, photography, and graphic art. Taught college-level media studies courses on subjects including film promotion, media history and analysis, race in media, Hollywood directors, and new media.
