Robert Kraft’s Summary

Attorney and Owner -- Injury, Disability & Immigration lawyers. I have been a lawyer in Dallas, Texas since 1971, representing injured and disabled clients. Kraft & Associates has five attorneys and more than twenty staff members. We have helped tens of thousands of clients over the years.

  • Partner
