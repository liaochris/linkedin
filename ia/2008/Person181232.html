Steve Coulson’s Summary

I have over 11 years experience creating and implementing new media marketing solutions, for Fortune 100 companies, primarily at some of the world's largest ad agencies. Check my portfolio for a small sample of the award-winning work that I and my team have been responsible for. This includes hands on experience building websites, rich media banners, games, email programs, and mobile marketing solutions. I've also dabbled in the more esoteric world of IPTV, ARGs, branded entertainment, widget building, and social media strategies.

I'm founder and editor of a leading pop culture blog that is read by up to a million readers every month. I'm actively involved in both audio and video podcasting, and created one of the first video podcast review sites.

My avatar is currently making a name for himself as Second Life's leading gonzo journalist, having been a contestant in the SL version of Big Brother.

All this and an impressive English accent.
