Richard Rosa’s Summary

I am the co-founder and co-owner of Buyers Brokers Only, LLC, which is an exclusive buyer brokerage firm located in Haverhill, MA. All our real estate brokers also are lawyers.

Our goal is to grow the company by associating around Massachusetts with real estate brokers that also are lawyers and who are interested in providing home buyers with superior customer service and ethical representation. We presently have associate brokers in Billerica, MA; Andover, MA; and Marshfield, MA.

My law firm, Rosa & Kres, LLP, primarily represents buyers purchasing real estate. Previosuly I practiced in the areas of criminal defense and civil litagation. I am admitted to practice law in Massachusetts and New Hampshire.

Richard Rosa’s Specialties:

Extensive negotiation experience, including negotiating with assistant district attorneys, police officers, probation officers, insurance adjusters, real estate agents/brokers and with other lawyers. Real estate law.
