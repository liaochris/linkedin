Miguel De Nicola’s Summary

Since I have draw my house walls when I was a little boy, I realise my future was with a pencil in my hand.

My experience started with industrial design in 90's when I was at school, but in the way I have expanded my knowledge and studied graphic design.

Since 2001 I've get very close in Graphic Design and Web design, learning programming languages like Perl, php, xml, html, etc. always web oriented. Then I have also start learning about marketing in order to promote my personal work and my clients needs.

Since 2005 I've realise that my hands and arms were not enough! So I have started to work as a project manager with designers and developers with some projects.

I like and enjoy working in any field around design, it's the fuel that makes me road.

Now I’m looking to expand my knowledge and experience. "knowledge has no end".
