Andrew Rabbitt’s Summary

In his current role Andrew oversees the day-to-day marketing activates of the VICAM Group of Waters Technology Corporation. His responsibilities include marketing budget management, trade show and industry meeting logistics, collateral material development, inventory tracking, maintaining memberships in industry organizations, product and pricing communications, and website additions and updates. In addition to his marketing communications duties he currently is the acting product manager for the VICAM mycotoxin testing product line. In this capacity he creates part numbers, develops pricing (based on gross profit analysis), writes technical copy, and conducts market research; he is also the liaison between marketing and the development team.

His background includes seven years of marketing communications experience, four of which he acted as acting product manager. While working for Bacou-Dalloz; in the Eye and Face, and Consumer/Retail departments; Andrew reduced overall trade show spending, followed up on all generated leads, increased eyewash sales eleven percent year over year, and developed a new consumer retail brand image. In 2004 he left Bacou-Dalloz and took a marketing specialist position with VICAM where he again reduced trade show expenses, developed a new ad campaign, and increased web usage. In 2006 VICAM was acquired by Waters Technology Corporation.

Andrew written several articles for the eye and face protection market, as well as press releases relating to mycotoxin testing. He has a demonstrated history of reducing marketing costs in the area of trade shows, print production, and development. He continues to work towards increasing sales by focusing on vertical markets and targeted campaigns using the internet and e-mail as a communications vehicle.
