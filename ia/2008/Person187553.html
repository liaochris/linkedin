Theo Veltman’s Summary

Theo has experience of managing both private sector and non-profit organizations in the banking, insurance, social insurance, health and government markets.

He has advised on and guided the mergers of several clients, providing both strategy and change management expertise. The result has typically been a better alignment of the interests of two to ten organizations, some of whom would normally be competitors, which then allowed them to achieve a shared objective.

Theo's work is notable for the improvements his clients have experienced in the areas of quality improvement, client satisfaction, productivity enhancement and improved employee morale.

Theo Veltman’s Specialties:

A strong focus on increasing market share and improving customer goodwill by improving service and products, a well as effciency in order to give the shortest possibel responstime to our clients whilst keeping the standard of service and product on the required level.
