Heidi Kay’s Summary

PediaStaff is the only staffing and recruitment company specializing in both the permanent and contract placement of pediatric and school based OTs (occupational therapists) PTs, a (physical therapists) and SLPs (speech language pathologists/ speech therapists)

My goals are to grow PediaStaff so that it is, without a doubt, the PREMIER pediatric and school based therapy staffing and recruitment firm in the nation. In addition, I want to develop a strong travel therapy component to our business so that we can be a true resource to our Pediatric Therapy candidates who are interested in finding interim positions between pediatric ones.
