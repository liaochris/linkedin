Jeffrey Ghannam’s Summary

Leading multimedia efforts to build capacities and reach development audiences worldwide. Lawyer engaged in media law review, improved access to information and media professionalism. Legal experience also includes advising in protecting intellectual property, establishing brand identities and managing corporate reputational risk. Served as a Special Assistant Attorney General for the State of Michigan.

Veteran reporter, writer, and editor with many years of experience handling major breaking news; national and foreign reporting with expertise in legal affairs and the Middle East. Former staff writer and editor at the Detroit Free Press; former legal affairs writer at the American Bar Association Journal, Chicago; contributor to The Boston Globe from Detroit, The New York Times in Washington, D.C., Time magazine in Cairo, Egypt, UPI Detroit and Nice-Matin in Nice, France, among others.

Awarded a James S. and John L. Knight Foundation International Press Fellowship to advance media training programs in the Middle East and North Africa.

Served as an editor/trainer for an investigative reporting project in Beirut, Lebanon for the U.S. Agency for International Development's Office of Transition Initiatives, and led training activities for the National Democratic Institute in Jordan and Morocco.

Analysis of legal protections and challenges confronting independent media and program design carried out in Morocco, Tunisia, Egypt, Jordan, and Lebanon for the Knight program. Analysis and recommendations to reinvent the news operation of Qatar Broadcasting Corp. in Qatar undertaken for the Voice of America.

Jeffrey Ghannam’s Specialties:

Lawyer, writer, editor, developer of multimedia platforms and media strategies in development and training courses. Media law, international law, intellectual property, negotiations, settlements, contracts, marketing and brand identities. Field-based experience in the Middle East, North Africa and Gulf regions; effective team builder, change agent in resistant environments, public speaker, and TV commentator.
