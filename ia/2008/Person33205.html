Chad Saunders’s Summary

I began my career as a busboy/barback at V Bar; at the time a very popular ultra-lounge inside the Venetian Hotel and Casino in Las Vegas. I immediately seized the networking opportunities that presented themselves and quickly worked my way up to door host, and eventually lead VIP Host. During my time at V Bar, I had the privilege of working with highly esteemed Las Vegas promoter Jack LaFleur, one of the creators of the Rehab pool party at the Hard Rock Hotel and Casino. He soon extended me an offer to be his “number two” at Rehab and the Hard Rock’s then brand new nightclub Body English.

I spent the next 2 and ½ years at Hard Rock as an independent contractor, filling a variety of roles within the hotel, as well as, simulataneously being involved in the creation/operation of several of Las Vegas’ other landmark weekly events/venues (Fusion Thursdays at Tabu inside the MGM, Worship Thursdays at TAO inside the Venetian and running the VIP Services for Late Night Empire, Las Vegas’ premier afterhours.)

In February of 2007 I decided to focus my attention and became an exclusive employee of Buddha Entertainment, the partnership that owns and operates TAO Restaurant/Nightclub in Las Vegas, the highest grossing restaurant in the country. My first role was as Daytime VIP Services Manager of TAO Beach, an exclusive topless pool above the existing nightclub/restaurant; by far the sexiest place under the sun. After a monumental first season, I accepted a role in TAO’s marketing department, where I concentrate on event booking, brand development, network management and marketing strategy, with a particular focus on developing TAO’s online and digital efforts. Although I spend 5 days a week in the office, I also double as a de facto VIP Host for TAO Nightclub, and will eventually transition back to full time VIP Hosting/Managing as TAO Beach enters it’s 2nd season and our forthcoming nightclub/restaurant, LAVO, opens in the Venetian’s Palazzo tower this Summer.
