Kirsten Osolind’s Summary

Kirsten is CEO of RE:INVENTION, a nationally noted marketing & PR firm. Her 18-year career includes roles as Whole Foods' National Marketing Director, WebMD's Director of Advertising/Media, General Mills' ethnic marketing manager, and brand manager at Coke & P&G. She has consulted to Fortune 500 companies like Genworth & Vanguard & startups like Jajah & PhatNoise. She has launched cookbooks & e-marketing platforms, introduced Hispanic kids to Go-Gurt, declared a national tea war, and convinced Dilbert to take the Nestea plunge.

One of the nation’s “Women 2.0 Experts," Kirsten has appeared in over 500+ women's and biz publications from Redbook & Pink to BusinessWeek & INC. A former Entrepreneur Magazine monthly marketing columnist (and current contributing columnist), Kirsten now edits one of the nation's top marketing blogs according to Ad Age Magazine, also the #1 GOOGLE-ranked women entrepreneurs' blog. She led creation of RE:INVENTION’s “Venture Funding for Women" Report, a groundbreaking analysis of gender disparities in VC dollars, often referenced as an industry barometer by media.

A '04 Stevie Awards for Women in Business finalist & elected member of the Economic Club of Chicago, Kirsten currently serves on the National Board of the Stevie Awards for Women in Business & on the Board of Directors for Invincibelle, a privately-held company. She has served as the National Association of Women Business Owners National Public Policy Forum Chair, chaired the 1st YWCA Chicago Future Leaders Board, served on the Springboard Enterprises Midwest Steering Committee, and was appointed to the Illinois State of Women in Politics Committee. She has been a speaker/panelist at conferences for PR Newswire, Northwestern, NYU, BlogHer, Women in Publishing & more. She received her MBA from Duke.

In July 2008 Kirsten relocated "From Chicago to Coronado" to reinvent herself. Stay tuned for the book. It's a story about love, adventure, and "getting off the grid."

Kirsten Osolind’s Specialties:

RE:INVENTION helps companies sell more of their products and services to working women (women entrepreneurs and executives). Our services include marketing strategy, promotions, & national public relations.
