Ben Thaiaporn’s Summary

Ben graduated from Suranaree University of Technology. He held the Bachelor of Engineering with a major in Metallurgical Engineering.
-Back in 1999 after an affect of Thailand's economic crisis. Ben was hired as a quality control engineer in a ready use mortar cement plant in Saraburi. He creates a standard operations procedure for applying a Thai Industrial Standard for mortar cement. The certification completed by an audit from Thai Industrial Standard Institute.
-He joined Namheng Steel which is a steel making manufacturer. Namheng's product including steel deformed bar, round bar and wire rod. His position was a QA division chief and quality management representative. In this period he gain a lot of knowledge on ISO 9002:1994,ISO 9001:2000 and his metrology experience. He founded CMIS MICRO CO.,LTD. with his two friends to start an ISO consultancy firm to serve the factory near by his area. He is one part of a consultant team that helps P.V. Explosives Co.,Ltd. and Lime Master Co.,Ltd. certified. Finally, the founder of the company decided to stop the business due to lack of time since their business is their part time job only.
-It is not only the skillful of metallurgical are and ISO system. He also found that he likes to spend a time on the metrological area such as; calibration principle, control of measuring device according to clause 7.6 of ISO9001:2000.He also likes to repair, PM his measuring equipment in the steel plant. He joined QES (Thailand) Co., Ltd which is a multinational company that trades much hi-tech equipment from US, Japan and Europe.
-He joins Thai Beverage Can Ltd which is a licensee of Ball Corporation. He starts to work here as an engineer to serve the technology transferring center.
