Edmond Davis’s Summary

United States History part I &II, The African-American Experience, World Civilizations I & II, Geography, and Slavery 101 are all areas of my expertise. My goals are to help slay the myths and ignorances of our cultures past & present. To bridge the gaps of time with a historical presentation formatted for all students. Edmond Davis is also the National Vice-Chair Of The History Committee for Groove Phi Groove Social Fellowship Incorporated 2007-08.

Edmond Davis’s Specialties:

African American History, The history of Slavery: Global Institutions, American History & Geography.
