Diane Roberts’s Summary

I have spent my entire career in international Sales and Marketing roles, and bring over 15 years rich industry experience, selling in the UK, USA, Europe and Asia Pacific. I have recently returned to Ireland from Silicon Valley, During this time I developed a strong network of contacts and an up close understanding of the some of the worlds leading technology companies, and in particular, the approach of the worlds largest venture capital community.

Xcell is a strategic consulting practice, which specialises in early stage business consulting, sales and market development, fund raising, mentoring, and advisory boards.

I has a wealth of experience, and an extensive contact base in the USA technology and media sectors. I have employed a team of professionals based in the USA, to work on a one to one basis with my client companies executing on sales activity, PR campaigns, overall market strategy and funding.

I have also recently launched a new Angels Network, which is entirely made up of USA based angels who have an interest in investing in European start-ups. This Angel network will not only bring funds, but come with US market knowledge and experience. This angel group is looking for big ideas, and capable entrepreneurs who can execute on them!

I bring an in-depth and detailed understanding of the global technology market and its’ challenges and opportunities.

Prior to this, I worked for several leading international technology firms setting up successful sales channel operations, both direct and indirect. This breadth of experience and domain expertise, uniquely places me to have a real impact on the success of my client companies.
