    DETAIL OF PUBLICATION:
    “A Robust ID card Authentication after Print-Scan Operation”
    Abstract_ We have addressed the issue of authentication of identification cards (ID cards) after print-scan operations in the context of statistical space. Experiment results on a number of encoded ID cards show that our approach is effective in the presence of the histogram and geometric distortion caused by the print-scan process. The innovations of this paper are implementing a model of authentication for an ID card without any added frame around the watermark location, and designing some preprocessing blocks in the decoder, with the following goals: Performing an automated localization algorithm, fulfilling a hierarchical binarization algorithm with use of a statistical space and considering a cropping criterion independent of the print-scan model. Finally, a simple but effective block-based encoder and decoder using the Hadamard patterns for this special application are presented.
