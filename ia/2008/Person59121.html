Enrico Neri’s Summary

As CEO at Vegan solutions I am developing the sales and strategic development for Eldy project.
Eldy, the PC and web2.0 for elderly, has a strong presence in Italy (over 150.000 users); i am developing Eldy into international markets.

*********************************************
ELDY, the PC/Web2.0 for the Elderly
*********************************************
The vast majority of Elderly (+60) population has been excluded from the innovation and revolution of the computer, of the internet and of the web 2.0.
This has happened because of barriers in usability that both hardware and software nowadays have, creating a strong e-inclusion problem.

ELDY has been designed and developed to cover this gap, and to provide elderly the richness of internet communication, in its information content, personal and social value.

ELDY is a hardware and software solution, based on:
1- EldyDESKTOP: multi-lingual user interface, very clear, with strong contrast and easy to be read, in big letters and clear language, with different application, that allow user to write/read email, chat, get news, get weather information, view digital picture, into a usable environment.

2- EldyLINUX : linux operating system distribution design for security (antivirus, firewall, etc) and for being very light so to be used also in low capacity computer, to host Eldy Desktop on top.The solution on linux guarantees stability, security and easiness of software distributiion.

3- EldyPC is the complete hardware solution that comes in two way.
- touchscreen video with build-in computer, tablet or standalone;
- standard pc with elderly design keyboard and elderly design mouse.
