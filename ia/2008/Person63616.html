Fernando Vives’s Summary

I started involved in Hotel Operations, then moved to Hotel Finance and went back to hotel operations. After almost 6 years of experience within Operations and Finance I ended up as Director of Revenue Management.

Revenue Management is a beautiful fild where you can apply many expertises, such as sales, operations, finance, technology.... ALL IN 1.

After being Area Director of Revenue Management for the top Spanish Hotel Chain I decided to go into consultancy. This would give me the opportunity to implement Revenue Mangament strategies and procedures in many companies, multipliying my knowledge and vision on the field and as well in project management and consultancy.
