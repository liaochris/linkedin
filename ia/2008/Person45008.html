Darren Cornish’s Summary

Senior Customer Experience/Customer Service Change Leader. Extensive experience of Operational Service Management (up to 1900 staff under direct leadership), organisational development (OD), & Sales/Business management. Excellent record of achievement with a continual success in delivering results across scorecard. Strong motivator and communicator with developed consultancy and leadership abilities combined with a positive attitude.

I have been told I am a charismatic leader who can rally large groups of people towards a business vision and create performance levels not previously seen. I am a strong communicator, across all levels of an organisation engaging and exciting individuals in the pursuit of service and operational excellence. I have a strong grasp of process improvement methodologies, people interventions and IT/ecomm service opportunities. I connect well with customers (both end customers and B2B) and am seen within my organisation as the 'voice of the customer'. I have experience of supply chain management including outsourcing and offshoring. I have sponsored large strategic programmes.

My goal is to become a 'Chief Customer Officer' (something that blends the Customer Service and Marketing disciplines) for a large blue chip.

Darren Cornish’s Specialties:
