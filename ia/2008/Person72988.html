Grzegorz Kozakiewicz’s Summary

While originally from Poland, I have lived in various locations in Europe, and currently reside in Sheffield, UK. I'm particularly interested in Interactive Mixed Media / HCIA and implementing handcrafted items as an interactive medium within web design and installations.

  • Designer / Art Director

    Pencil Rebel

    (Design industry)

    December 2006 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    The PencilRebel concept is a unique and refreshing one, taking everyday 'analogue' items and putting them into a digital world.

    The end result is a creative, mixed-media website with a unique, striking and playful style. For the end user it offers a thrilling website experience, comprising animation and at times challenging the visitor with puzzles. Overall, I feel this is far more interactive than many sites which simply involve clicking few menu buttons.
