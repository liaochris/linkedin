Scott Gilbert’s Summary

Scott Gilbert is a seasoned IT professional with over 18 years of experience in the IT marketplace. Scott's experience covers International Sales, Field Sales, Corporate Sales and Government and Education sales, as well as Technical Support and Technology Recruiting.
