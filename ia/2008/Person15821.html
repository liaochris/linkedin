Aris van Bijsterveldt’s Summary

Distinguished 20+ year career as an interim executive manager is highlighted by an impressive record of achievement in delivering strong operational and financial results.

Accomplished in managing strategic project portfolios in highly competitive business environments, executing mission-critical programs for renovation and internationalization, aligning technology with business to drive performance, and leading complex, long-term critical sales cycles.
