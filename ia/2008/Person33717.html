Grubb Properties:
For 40 years, Grubb Properties has been a leader in the real estate industry providing customers and partners with quality residential, commercial and development products and services. A fully integrated real estate company, Grubb Properties focuses on the Charlotte and Triangle (Raleigh, Durham, Chapel Hill and Cary) markets of North Carolina.

Grubb Properties is committed to securing the best locations in North Carolina, creating superior environments and enhancing the quality of life.

Tenant/ Buyer Representation:
Tenant Representation does not cost you a thing and could save you a ton. Leasing, renting, or buying office space is a complicated matter that effects both your company and employees well being. Correctly negotiating and understanding the terms of a real estate agreement will directly affect the operational cost factor on your balance sheet. A Tenant Representative helps you determine critical needs and evaluate all of your alternatives in the market. They get involved with the deal, know the designers, landlords, and other service providers involved with your move.
