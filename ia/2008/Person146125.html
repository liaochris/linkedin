Donna Papacosta’s Summary

I love to communicate. My early career was in systems analysis, but I soon realized that I preferred the communications aspect of the job over the techie part. So I left the world of systems behind in 1985 to study journalism and to start my own business, Trafalgar Communications. Soon after, I was writing for trade journals, then consumer magazines, and finally corporate clients. These days, most of my writing involves internal communications for $1B+ corporations. I also do marketing communications for medium and large companies. In the last few years I've developed a penchant for all things audio. Since September 2005 I've been producing the Trafcom News Podcast, which is a show for "people who care about communicating." I also help organizations develop their own podcasts, produce conference podcasts, and lead Webinars on podcasting. What could be more fun that this?
