Doug Thorpe’s Summary

Doug Thorpe, through his 25+ years in financial services, has earned a reputation of excellence and innovation. His involvement in major national initiatives to streamline and improve mortgage loan delivery has resulted in faster, more profitable delivery of over $80 billion in mortgage collateral. Most of Doug’s clients realized significant cost savings, more effective operations, and higher profitability by using his business process outsourcing models.

Prior to developing those models, Doug’s career spanned 19 years with JP Morgan Chase and its predecessor banks in Texas. While there, he managed the entire Real Estate Loan Administration Division that handled all facets of real estate loan financing; from high end commercial development all the way through to residential mortgage finance. He was the chief architect of various technology platforms that transformed the operating efficiencies of his division. Doug has an extensive network of contacts and associates through almost 30 years of “up-close and personal”, honest and ethical business practices.

Today, Doug provides independent consulting services for companies and executives seeking fresh ideas for expansion, cost control, process re-engineering, and change management. In particular, anyone thinking about business process outsourcing should call on Doug for advice and analysis.
