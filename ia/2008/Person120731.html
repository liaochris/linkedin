Maria Kludt’s Summary

I have over 10 years of technical recruiting experience and have a lot to offer from sourcing, college recruiting, process management to full cycle recruiting. I have been consulting for the past 8 years, and I have had the pleasure of working for many reputable clients in high-tech as well as industrial applications industries, small to large, public and private.

I am the ultimate multi-tasker having successfully managed many recruiting projects simultaneously: Crane Aerospace, Boeing Satellite Systems, Powerwave, Smiths Detection, Broadcom, Smiths Aerospace, QLogic, Newport Corporation, Accuride. As a Staffing Consultant, I manage my projects on-site or virtually.

I am able to build rapport with managers and have the ability to quickly understand technology. I am able to assimilate into the HR organization, adapt corporate processes and absorb corporate culture.
