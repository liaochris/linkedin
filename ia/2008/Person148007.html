Paul Murad’s Summary

Paul Murad is a real estate entrepreneur, author, and President of METROPLEX Group, a company engaged in real estate brokerage, investments, and development. The Group's two companies are METROPLEX Development and METROPLEX Realty.

Murad has 16 years of diversified experience operating his own businesses, being self-employed, and working in start-up and entrepreneurial environments, as well as in a publicly-traded corporation. He holds a degree in Finance and International Business. He has lived and worked across the globe, and is fluent in English, Russian, and Spanish.

Prior to entering the real estate business, he owned several businesses and worked in business development and finance for several multinational corporations. In his first year in real estate sales he became a top producer. He was subsequently selected by REALTOR® Magazine of the National Association of REALTORS® as one of the top 30 young professionals in the nation.

Murad is approved by the Real Estate Division of the State of Nevada to teach continuing education (CE) classes for real estate professionals and holds a Nevada Real Estate Broker license.

He speaks regularly at real estate industry seminars and conferences across the nation and is an author of the acclaimed book “Manhattanizing Las Vegas”, which has become a reference guide on Las Vegas developments for real estate professionals and the general public.

Murad believes in giving back to the community, and is involved in several charitable organizations. He serves on several Boards of Directors and Steering Committees of charitable organizations. Murad is a strong advocate of environmental initiatives, smart growth, and sustainable development.

In 2006 he was selected as one of “40 Under 40” leading business people on the annual list by In Business Las Vegas.
