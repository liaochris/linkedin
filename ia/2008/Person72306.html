Greg Godden’s Summary

Publicist for Morris Mohawk Gaming Group, located in Kahnawake, Quebec.

My focus as a publicist for MMGG is mainly dealing and raising brand awareness with traditional and new Canadian media, though my passion and area of expertise is the world of new media and web 2.0.

Traditional media is a dieing art form! Falling subscription rates and declining profits for most of the major publications across the globe are a clear indicator of the power of the web. Having a strong Internet presence is imperative for any company that wants to be successful and have their brand become a household name. This is why I have made my top priority establishing myself as an expert in new media and web 2.0 with a laundry list of hot contacts from the top new media and web 2.0 sites in a multitude of categories.

My efforts as a Publicist for the Morris Mohawk Gaming Group have generated stories in such major new media and web 2.0 sites as: Reuters, AP, NY Times, USA Today, LA Times, Miami Herald, Washington Post, San Francisco Chronicle, New York Post, Boston Globe, Boston Herald, Dallas Star-Telegram, Newsday, The Register, The Sun, MTV News, CNN, CNBC, CBC, MSNBC, AOL, Fox News, Access Hollywood, Spin Magazine, Vanity Fair, Variety, Radar, TVGuide, TMZ.com, Defamer.com, Eonline.com, TVGasm.com, BuddyTV.com, SlashFilm.com, Gizmodo.com, Kotaku.com, 1up.com, CNET.com, Wired.com, ZDnet.com, Engadget.com, BoingBoing.com, PowerPage.org, TUAW.com, NahRight.com, Stereogum.com, DailyKos.com, Wonkette.com and countless others.

I've also generated a number of stories featured in/on major international newspapers, magazines and TV programs.
