Stephen Fuerst, Esq.’s Experience

  • Vice President

    The Centrus Group, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    March 2004 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    For 15 years, Centrus has been recognized as one of the premier turnaround firms in the northern USA. Our goal is to improve the operational performance and maximize the value of our client companies, while preserving the operating integrity of troubled companies. We are also able to structure a corporate wind-down if necessary to maximum the value of corporate assets. While we offer a wide variety of business planning and consulting services, Centrus Group focuses on:

    - Turnarounds
    - Crisis intervention
    - Wind-down management
    - Financial/debt restructuring
    - Executive recruiting
    - Divestitures

    Based in Akron, Ohio, Centrus provides services throughout the United States and Canada. Our typical clients are involved in manufacturing, retail or healthcare and have annual revenue from $10 million to $250 million.

    Our senior professionals are dedicated to the development and timely implementation of the most creative and effective turnaround solutions within our industry.
