DS Wallace’s Summary

PRODUCTS
Emotive Music by Intelligent Design ~ composition, orchestration, and audio editing for games, movies, & other media.

QUALIFICATIONS
Short films, commercials, television, promotional shorts, and indie rock albums. 18 yrs performance, 12 yrs orchestral, 12 yrs music composition, 7 yrs production, 8 yrs sound synthesis, with performance experience on 7 instruments. I obtained a BA in Music in 2004.

PURPOSE
To combine intelligent design, appropriate aesthetics, and effective balance while creating music that is synthetic, acoustic, and orchestral, in line with production intentions. I strive to write music tied to emotion, the character, and the circumstance - a score that draws the viewer in organically.

I love originality and quality.To these ends, I work with live performers, the finest samples and sequencers, and the best electronic synthetics. I intend to have several game scores released by the end of 2008.

DS Wallace’s Specialties:

- Electronic and Symphonic synthesis & music scoring.
- Adept use of music for emotional stimulation.
- High-quality equipment for flawless sonic production.
- Integrity, drive, knowledge, and passion for the best music.
