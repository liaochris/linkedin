Matthew Barbour’s Summary

MATTHEW BARBOUR
SALES & BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

After attending college at Gettysburg College in Central Pennsylvania and receiving a BA in Philosophy and Political Science, Mr. Barbour went on to receive a JD from Boston University School of Law. Following graduation from law school, Matthew joined a small family business located outside of Philadelphia which specialized in commercial office furniture. While serving as corporate counsel, Matthew decided to make his way into the sales arena in an effort to assist with the company’s growth. Working in this capacity, Mr. Barbour logged in over three years of successful sales experience where he led the sales team in annual sales for all three years. In 2002, he moved to Sales and Marketing Manager and Vice President of Business Development where he continued to function for an additional 4 years, establishing his company as one of the premier office furniture dealerships in the greater Philadelphia market. He secured and worked with such clients as Temple University and Penn State University to name but a few.
In search of his next challenge, Mr. Barbour moved to the Colorado market where he took up employment with a commercial office furniture dealer and consulting firm. Mr. Barbour served as an account executive and business advisor specializing in business development while assisting management in the growth of their ever expanding Denver operation. Mr. Barbour's newest position is Corporate Account Manager for Allsteel, Inc. Allsteel is a division of HNI, the world's second largest furniture manufacturer. Allsteel, in conjunction with its sister companies Gunlocke, Lamex, Paoli and Maxon as well as with its strategic partner Ahrend, affords its clients a global portfolio of products and services.

In addition to work, Matthew actively pursues his many hobbies which include: skiing, fly fishing, mountain biking, hiking, backpacking, snowmobiling, art, philosophy and reading.
