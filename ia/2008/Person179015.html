Samuel Prasad, Ph.D.’s Summary

Dr. Prasad is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) with more than fifteen years experience in the management of software projects, which includes requirements analysis, planning, budgeting, quantifying risks, change management, implementation, deployment and high-level reporting to business stakeholders and department heads. He is a Certified Software Quality Engineer (CSQE) with a proven-track record in developing and instituting quality processes throughout the SDLC from inception to the transition phases. He is an expert in financial transaction processing related to credit/debit card transactions, ACH processing, processor APIs and Internet-based transaction routing. Sam currently manages the development of scalable gaming platforms for online casual games for major multi-media companies. Sam has published several papers and spoken at many symposiums on autonomous mobile robots.

Samuel Prasad, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

Sam specializes in maximizing the "Return On Investment" (ROI) of technology projects by optimizing software usability and compressing the time it takes to go from concept to production using an agile function-driven project management methodology in concert with a combination of stateside and offshore development resources from China and India.
