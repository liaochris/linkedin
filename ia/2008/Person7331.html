Al Gosling, Alistair Gosling’s Summary

Al Gosling, developed a passion for extreme sports during his early teens and following a number of jobs went on to set up a business that would rapidly grow into the Extreme Group. Aged 24 and unable to find a job that interested him, he set up Extreme with the goal of mixing sports and business, working with great people and having fun while making it happen. Running on a shoestring budget, from an office in a converted barn, Al and team rapidly agreed a number of deals with producers and broadcasters to sell and distribute television programes all over the world. In April 1998, Al had the vision to create and launch the world’s first TV channel dedicated to extreme sports, The Extreme Sports Channel. The goal was to launch a 24/7 TV channel broadcasting worldwide via satellite to an under-served audience who would be blow away by the action and for some be inspired to get out and try some of the sports. By 2002 with the channel was broadcasting into over 60 countries, 40million homes in 12 languages and Al and the team started to look out side broadcasting and began to develop the Extreme brand into specific targeted areas. Since then Extreme Hotels, drinks, retail, mobile phones, theme parks, footwear, eye wear, watches, equipment and clothing have all been launched. This year the group employs 247 people and is on target to reach $45m turnover. Al is completely passionate about driving the business forward, working with great people, helping promote Entrepreneurship, and when not in the office, in meetings talking at events you will find him either windsurfing, kite surfing, flying, chasing deep powder, ice climbing or sailing somewhere in the world.
