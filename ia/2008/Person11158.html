Andrei Rosca’s Summary

- worked as a Flash freelancer for 2 years with different companies
- discovered my passion for entrepreneurship at 17 yo.
- after a few unsuccesful tries, i managed to start a small 3-men-"business" in the production of speakers and high power custom sound systems.
- one year later i co-founded a small web-design company targeting small businesses; i made an exit in 2006, "giving" my part of the company to my associate; when i left, the company had 8 employees and somewhere around 200-250 websites built
- in march 2006 i founded bookblog.ro and started putting a team together
- in 2007 bookblog.ro, with a team of 15, won the "Best Romanian Cultural Blog" award and had ~20 media appearences in newspapers, magazines, radio and tv.
- bookblog.ro is the organiser of "Schimb de Carti" meetings, an idea similar to the worldwide Bookcrossing, but with some twists :). Schimb de Carti is organised monthly in ~15 cities (february 2008) in Romania, Thailand and extending at full speed.
- in 2007 i also started OctavianPaler.ro, a non-profit site dedicated to one of the most appreciated intelectuals in Romania, and BasicMarketing.ro - a marketing resource blog for "wanna-be" marketers, written by four resident writers and experienced guest-bloggers. I own 50% of BasicMarketing.ro.
- in 2008, bookblog.ro won the "Best Romanian Cultural Blog" for the second time.
- the last but not the least important, i'm the author of AndreiRosca.bookblog.ro, a personal blog about life, marketing, Romania and entrepreneurship.
