Eugene Curatu’s Summary

Optical Design and Engineering
Creativity lying on a solid mathematics and physics base, as well as on a broad, extensive experience in optical engineering/sciences.
Curatu Optics is a small company providing consulting services on a variety of optical design and engineering projects through all your product development phases from feasibility and conceptual design to prototype integration and testing. Our designs are aimed at reducing manufacturing costs as much as possible while satisfying the end specifications of the product. We always recommend the optimal fabrication process depending on production volume, cost, performance requirements, and lead time. We work closely with our customers as well as the optical / mechanical fabrication shops to ensure proper completion and testing of the prototypes in the shortest time possible.

Curatu Optics is located in Orlando, Florida, and will work for customers anywhere in the U.S. and Canada.

Eugene Curatu’s Specialties:

Various optical components and systems for different applications ranging from infrared to ultra-violet, including:
Camera and Projection Lenses, Telescopes, Microscopes, Eye-Pieces, F-Theta Scanning Lenses, Laser Optics, Fiber Optics, Collimators, Focusing Lenses, Metrology / Instrumentation Optics, Spectrometers, Ophthalmic Devices / Diagnostic Instruments, Intraocular Lenses.

Tolerance Analysis and Budgeting, Thermal Analysis, Image Quality Analysis.

ZEMAX, Mathcad, Mathematica.
