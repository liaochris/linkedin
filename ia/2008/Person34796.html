Christopher Reed’s Summary

Value-based, goal-oriented leader of suppliers and internal cross-functional teams.
Follows through to implementation, goal accomplishment, using cross-functional teams.
Certified Purchasing Manager with firm grasp of legal aspects (UCC, UN Convention on the Sale of Goods), bidding processes (SOW, RFP, RFQ), and sourcing strategy.
