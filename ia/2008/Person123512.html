Martin Hebig’s Summary

Maverick Software Consulting provides onshore outsourcing and college recruiting services. We accomplish this by working with local Universities to hire the top computer science students to work on real world software development and testing projects under the guidance of Maverick Software Consulting senior consultants. This solution provides students with on campus internship and employment opportunities while giving companies an easy, low risk opportunity to augment current staff or a feasible alternative to offshore outsourcing.

Maverick Software Consulting provides three services with a similar bill rate that standard offshore consulting companies charge for one service. Maverick Software Consulting provides Onshore Services, Training Services and Recruiting Services. Maverick Software Consulting provides more service (helping train and recruit future employees) with a lower communication, training and travel costs, which makes it extremely competitive with offshore providers without risk of an overseas engagement. See www.MaverickSoftware.net for more info.
