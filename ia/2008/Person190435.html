Todd Webster’s Summary

Todd Webster started in the Mortgage and Real Estate world after
leaving the Marine Corps in 1999. Starting his career with Realty
Executives Todd quickly became a top producer by using his knowledge
and understanding of sub-prime financing to act as an Accredited
Buyer's Representative (ABR).

As more agents asked how they could close the more difficult deals
Todd made the move into Loan Origination and eventually into Brokering
loans himself. His extensive knowledge in FHA, Sub-Prime and Alt-A
financing coupled with his understanding of organized file processing
quickly made him the broker for those difficult deals.

100% of his origination initially came from Internet leads and some
small scale print advertising. He designed his companies website and
worked closely with E-mail marketers to generate his own unique data.
Eventually he moved into purchasing leads from lead generators and
training his sales staff to work this type of data.

Todd relocated to Florida in 2004 where he began to work for
Juicyleads.com. At Juicy Leads, as the companies top producer, Todd
has maintained the highest reorder rate and client satisfaction.

Todd founded Closed and Funded Consulting. He is the only consultant in the industry today that focuses on one of the most controversial topics, Internet Mortgage Leads.

His work with brokers has expanded into a workshop series in
connection with Mastering Mortgages and a consumer education
program that teaches consumers the various loan types available to them
along with options for credit repair and accelerating the pay-off of their
homes.

Most recently Todd has taken the position of Vice President of Marketing for Revolution Credit Repair where his knowledge of the mortgage industry and underwriting guidelines has afforded him the ability to grow the companies Affiliate programs.

Todd Webster’s Specialties:

Credit Repair, Internet Mortgage Leads, Live Transfers, Trigger Leads, Mortgage Sales, Lead Generation, Lead Integration, Lead Management, Licensing Laws, FHA Lending, Alt-A Mortgaging, Alt-A Underwriting, Sub-Prime Lenders (if any are left)
