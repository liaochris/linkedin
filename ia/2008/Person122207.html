Mark Lewis, CISSP’s Summary

Mark Lewis is a Systems Engineer at Symantec (Canada) Corporation. With more than 12 years of information technology experience, Mr. Lewis has provided many Canadian small, mid-market and large enterprise clients with assistance in designing, acquiring and implementing a wide array of technical solutions. Mark’s technical credentials include Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Cisco Information Security Specialist, CompTIA Certified Security Professional (Security+) and Microsoft Certified Systems Administrator (MCSA). Mark is bilingual, possessing effective communication skills with fluency in written and spoken English and French. Prior to joining Symantec, Mark worked as a Systems Engineer at Dell Canada Inc.
