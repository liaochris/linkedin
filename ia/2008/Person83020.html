James J. Talerico, Jr.’s Summary

Over Twenty-Five Years of Diversified Business Experience.

Founder & CEO, Greater Prairie Business Consulting. Offer small, mid-sized and large companies results-oriented consulting services utilizing proven, proprietary process designed to improve business performance. Through strategic alliances, GPBC also provides important ancillary services, such as business financing -- including commercial real estate loans, business brokerage services, succession planning -- including financial planning & tax advisory services, business process outsourcing, IT consulting / web design, and bookkeeping & payroll services.

Management Consulting Experience:

Assist entrepreneurs of private and publicly-traded small to mid-sized companies maximize their business’ potential by aligning people, processes, assets & technology behind a competitive business model. Implement results-based changes from the methodical findings of comprehensive business evaluation designed to identify existing horizontal and vertical disconnects. Create a focused strategy, organizational structure, financial benchmarks, and measurement systems to monitor operational inputs, throughputs & outputs. Boost employee productivity. Strengthen existing systems, procedures & controls. Train management to control & continuously improve overall organizational performance. Elevate leadership & management skills. Also have experience in corporate training, customer surveys, and traditional merchant banking services -- including debt & equity financing, mezzanine financing, venture capital services, asset-based financing, SBA Loans, etc.

Collectively improved the sales and profit performance of these businesses by millions of dollars as a result of a multitude of quality, process, productivity, pricing, cost & revenue enhancement strategies, while also transforming the lifestyle of its owners, as a Senior Business Consultant, Project Manager, Director of Quality, and Division Vice President.
