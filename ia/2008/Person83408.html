James Wright’s Summary

James helps organisations grow through sales & marketing strategy; product/service development; new business hunting and operational management. He is currently at RedBalloon for Corporate working on Employee Engagement, Reward & Recognition and Incentive Programs.

His broad sector experience includes training and development, experiential marketing, leisure & luxury goods, gifting, travel, b2b, political ideas, financial services, recruitment, property and e-commerce.

Other interests include freelance journalism; from The Times, Spectator and Mensa Magazine and BizParade.com.au on subjects as diverse as hot air ballooning to political scandal.

James has also held positions with Chambers of Commerce in the UK and Australia and worked of a variety of high profile voluntary organizations.

James Wright’s Specialties:

Key skills lie in identifying, securing and managing commercial relationships; conceptualizing and progressing new products and services to market; developing and delivering marketing and engagement strategies and general business improvement.
