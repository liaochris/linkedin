Gary Bresien’s Summary

As Regional Director for The Prism Group, an IT Staffing and Solutions firm based in Dallas with offices in Texas and Colorado, my goal is to promote the growth of the company through networking with individuals and groups from varied backgrounds.

Prism Group is a leading provider of technical staffing to Fortune 500, mid-sized clients and private equity backed start-ups in the Dallas, TX and Denver, CO markets.
Our clients rely on our responsiveness to meet their on-demand requirements. Further, jobseekers have grown accustomed to Prism Group being an integral part of their career development – both pre and post placement.

Whether it's supplementing existing staff during peak periods in production, hiring permanent hard to find employees, or needing support on your next critical development project, Prism Group is the answer.

    The Colorado RFID Alliance is a non-profit technology organization dedicated to bringing together the Front Range RFID community. Our goals are to:

    Educate: Deliver educational programs that further the understanding of RFID technology, RFID applications and industry trends.

    Collaborate: Provide a networking forum for professionals interested in RFID.

    Promote: Work together to promote RFID solutions that will help strengthen Colorado's RFID industry.


    We encourage the following people to attend our events and get more involved in the Colorado RFID Alliance:
    People in companies developing RFID components and/or selling RFID Products
    Systems integrators and value added resellers specializing in RFID technology deployment
    End users who are using or investigating RFID technology and products
    Investors who are interested in, or actively investing in the RFID industry
    Individuals who are interested in getting more involved in the RFID industry.
