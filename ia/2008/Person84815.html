  • Senior Software Engineer

    Myplay

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    April 2000 — February 2002 (1 year 11 months)

    Myplay was a streaming music company which allowed users to have an online ‘music locker’. I worked with an amazing team using J2EE tools to develop a variety of products that were fault-tolerant, highly reliable and scalable, including:
