Jesse Rodgers’s Summary

Currently I am responsible for the user experience of the new co-op system at UW. I lead a small team of co-op students and a grad student working on AJAX, usability, and accessibility. It is fun. Also for fun I instigate local BarCamp's and brought the first StartupCamp to Waterloo.

Unrelated to technology, I have taken on the role of President of the UW Staff Association. The association is a volunteer organization represents UW staff on high level committees that work on policies that effect staff. Membership is optional for staff. It is a small organization but one I am trying to redefine and grow with an eye to provide more diverse services for its members.

For six years I was manager of web communication. I went through two re-developments of the universities public web space and learned a lot in the process. Using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, Perl, etc. Part of my job was also to foster a community.

I love the web, I love teaching people about the utility of web based technologies, and I love figuring out to use things.
