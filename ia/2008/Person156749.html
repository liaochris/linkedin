Randy Pomeroy’s Summary

I support mid and senior level leaders to develop and sustain effective leadership habits in the work place, inspire clear and consistant team behaviors, and create inspirational organizational alignment for better business results.

Focusing on Emotional and Social Intelligence, I inspire the APPLICATION of key leadership skills by supporting leaders actively engaged in growth and change initiatives, and proactively investing in high potential and star performance development.

My strengths include increasing people's self awareness and self management, social awareness and social management, communication and conflict skills, and ultimately developing a greater sense of curiosity, empathy and self differentiation.

I help people better understand the systems and sub systems they are functioning in, as well as the inter-personal, inter-disciplinary, and inter-cultural gaps which inevitably arise in the multicultural buisiness world.

Call on me and I will help you act your way into creating a better reality for you and your system.

Randy Pomeroy’s Specialties:

I have lived, worked, studied and traveled in 35 countries including Europe, Africa, Asia, N. and S. America, and the South Pacific. My background in Cultural Anthropolgy, Expedition Leadership, Applied Behavioral Science and Organizational Develpment affords me a unique perspective when working with systems, groups and individuals.
