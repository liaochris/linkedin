Andrea Klincokova’s Summary

Andrea is a Slovak national who has lived and worked in the Czech Republic for over 11 years. Originally from Bratislava where she built the HR department for DHL Slovak Republic, she relocated to Prague where she worked for several multi-national companies.
She is the current President of the ICF (International Coach Federation) Prague/Czech Republic charted chapter. This is still a small chapter and one of Andrea’s biggest challenges is to develop the chapter by increasing awareness of and demand for coaching in the Czech Republic. Her ability to absorb ideas in one language and seamlessly transfer them to Czech or Slovak is a powerful ally in this quest, making a broader spectrum of coaching materials and concepts more easily accessible to native Czech or Slovak speakers.
In addition to this she set up the Czech and Slovak offices for The Coaches Training Institute (CTI), to deliver coaching training accredited by ICF.

Andrea has had over 14 years of diverse sales, marketing, training, coaching and consulting experience. Currently her focus is on one-on-one executive and leadership coaching, team coaching, as well as on leadership and self-development programs.

Andrea Klincokova’s Specialties:

Andrea specializes in coaching, consulting and training senior level professionals.
