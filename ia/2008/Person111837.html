Lawrence Beasley’s Summary

Mr. Beasley has extensive administrative experience and is a planning and deployment specialist. As the Owner and Principal Engineer of Wolverine Technologies, he manages a company that provides technology services and solutions to non-profits and small businesses with a focus on open-source solutions. Primarily serving the needs of the Greater Las Vegas community, the company performs I.T. assessments, infrastructure analysis, compliance audits, and planning and deployment projects.

Lawrence has more than 20 years of small and large-scale deployment experience in the I.T. industry, holding positions from database programmer to I.T. Manager in the automotive, environmental, commercial technical, public and private industries. His responsibilities have included management of various server and client systems (UNIX, MacOSX, and Citrix/Windows), mobile phones, and internal phone and data infrastructure. This also includes managing the technical personnel and infrastructure needs for several remote offices, involving planning, design, and implementation of services worldwide.

Lawrence is also a member of several technical organizations, non-profit community groups, and has served on several Boards of Directors for public entities, and municipal/governmental planning and development committees.

Lawrence also manages to get a little mountain biking in as a member of IMBA's National Mountain Bike Patrol.
