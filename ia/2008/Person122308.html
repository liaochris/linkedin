Mark Mele’s Summary

Mark Mele has more than 22 years of leadership experience building and guiding top franchise brands. An accomplished corporate franchise development and operations strategist, his vision and expertise in business performance have driven notable franchise brands such as Century 21 Real Estate Corporation, Country Inns & Suites by Carlson, and Kumon North America, Inc.
Mark has achieved exciting company turnarounds and is recognized for his success in growing franchise brands. His strategic approach to expanding a franchise brand is reflected in his work as Vice President of Franchise Development of Kumon North America, Inc., where his leadership resulted in the opening of over 500 new franchised Kumon learning centers in 4 years. Under Mark’s leadership at Kumon, the company achieved franchise sales records that earned top honors with Entrepreneur Magazine.
In addition to Mark Mele’s exceptional track record in franchise expansion, Mark is also known for his ability to create and implement positive change in the areas of franchise operations and franchisee support. He has spearheaded franchisee support initiatives that have had positive impact on franchise system quality and the individual franchisees bottom line. His franchise achievements have been featured in Entrepreneur Magazine, Inc Magazine, as well as other business media.
