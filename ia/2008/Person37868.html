Chris Jones’s Summary

Managing SAP HR consultant with experience in implementing SAP based global template solutions together with major business transformation programs (shared service delivery model).

In the past few years I have worked on two Global SAP HR implementations at Nestle GLOBE and Philip Morris International (MySAP ERP 2004), gaining practical experience in a wide range of technical and management roles.

I have also previously created a start-up IT Training company specializing in Microsoft and Internet based courses. My clients included Redland Distribution, Sheffield TEC, Inland Revenue, Derbyshire County Council and Business Link.

Chris Jones’s Specialties:

My specialties include: SAP HR Integration, Global Template methodology, SAP Solution Manager, SAP HR Modules (PA, OM, PD & Management of Global Employees), SAP Query, Data Management, Testing, Offshore Service Delivery and project tools and documentation standards.
