Douglas Love’s Summary

Award winning television creator and show runner, author, playwright, director and theatrical producer, Douglas Love is dedicated to sharing the power of creativity. Central to all of his work is his development of unique and effective content that ignites the fire of imagination in young minds as a jumping off point for a vast variety of learning.

He is the author of more than 20 books for children and his best selling IMAGINATION STATION became the inspiration for Love creating and executive producing the Emmy nominated and Parent's Choice Award winning television series, OUT OF THE BOX for Disney Channel and JAMMIN' ANIMALS for HBO Family.

As a leader and innovator in the category of live theater for families and children, Douglas Love in conjunction with movie studio Walden Media commissioned seven original musicals and a new adaptation of their hit movie and Newbery award winning book, HOLES. He had produced US national tours and served as the Producing Artistic Director of Walden Family Playhouse and Family Stage for Shorenstein Hayes Nederlander Theatres.

Love's live entertainment career was launched when his adaptation of FREE TO BE YOU AND ME was accepted by Marlo Thomas and published by Rogers and Hammerstein Theatre Library.

Love is often brought into projects as the Creative Innovator to help corporations connect with their customers in exciting and meaningful ways. Love helped to revolutionize they way millions of children throughout the world use the encyclopedia by offering creativity and content to the new WORLD BOOK KIDS web site and spearheaded the branding of a new tradition to celebrate Earth Day with Organic Valley's EARTH DINNER cards. Douglas Love is a member of the Writers Guild of America, The Dramatists Guild, The Authors League and the Academy of Television Arts and Sciences.

Love keynotes and leads workshops for corporations about the application of his approach to creating content and marketing to families.
