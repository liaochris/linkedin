Carlos Miranda Levy’s Summary

Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) Consultant with over 10 years of experience developing projects and strategies on Information Society, Human Development, Education, Government, Open Content, Virtual Communities and Social Networks. Founder of a network of ICT for Development (ICT4D) reaching over 3 million people all over the world every month, including: www.civila.com, www.educar.org, www.bibliotecasvirtuales.com, www.americasomostodos.com, www.socinfo.com

Consultor en Tecnologías de la Información y la Comunicación (TIC) con más de 10 años desarrollando proyectos y estrategias para la Sociedad de la Información, Desarrollo Humano, Educación, Gobierno, Contenidos Libres, Comunidades Virtuales y Redes Sociales. Fundador de initiativas de TIC para el Desarrollo que involucran a más de 3 millones de personas al mes, entre ellas: www.civila.com, www.educar.org, www.bibliotecasvirtuales.com, www.americasomostodos.com, www.socinfo.com

Carlos Miranda Levy’s Specialties:

Development of ICT Strategies, Diagnostics, Conceptual Models and Prototypes. Familiar with Windows and Linux web development environments. Field experience in more than 10 Latin American and Caribbean countries. Broad experience with private, government, international and non-profit organizations. Has lectured on Open Content, e-Government, e-Learning, ICT Strategies and ICT for Human Development in several countries. Speaks, reads and writes in Spanish, English and Portuguese and basic French.
