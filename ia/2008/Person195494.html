Venkat Balasubramanian’s Summary

I strategize, prioritize and deliver on innovations to Citi's flagship online trading platform for its Global FX & Local Markets business.

With over 10 years of experience in FX & Structured Products, I have an in-depth understanding of Treasury products/processes, and what customers need from an internet interface to these products. My current passion is to deliver on those requirements within the challenging framework of a traditional 'bricks-and-mortar' bank.

I work with global multi-disciplinary teams to continuously rethink & enhance all aspects of the customer experience on the platform - all the way from initial design to client adoption strategies to end-user support. I ensure that the teams' objectives are all aligned towards successful execution on a platform strategy.

My strengths are my creativity, attention to detail and a calm tenacity to execute on multiple projects under pressure. My diligence, process expertise & people skills enables me to bring in a holistic perspective to resolving customer issues and creating solutions that make sense for all parties involved.

Venkat Balasubramanian’s Specialties:

Treasury - product expertise (FX, MM, Options, Structured Products), process expertise (sales strategies, middle & back-office), expertise on emerging markets & associated regulator controls (Asia, Latin America, Central/Eastern Europe, Middle East & Africa).

Corporate Internet - broad understanding of technical architecture, concept flows between multiple systems on each stage of the customer experience, competitive analysis and managing cross-functional teams & stakeholders.
