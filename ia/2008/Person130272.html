Michael Part’s Summary

Michael Part is a seasoned and successful IT and communications professional with over 20 years of experience in developing outstanding promotional films, creating customized PCs, and spearheading diverse IT projects for technology, manufacturing, and medical industry clients. Part possesses a demonstrated track record of building high-performing IT departments, facilitating communication between IT staff and customers, and delivering IT services on-time and on-budget. A proven leader committed to leveraging IT activities to reduce costs, he strives to improve company performance, and maximize growth. Michael Part currently serves as the President of consultancy Innerstation Technologies, where he’s aided multiple clients, including cultivating a 12-year partnership with Everett Charles Technologies. Additionally, he has also realized a successful motion picture writing career. Further details concerning his writing pursuits can be found at www.imdb.com.
