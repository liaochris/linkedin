Oded Volovitz’s Summary

Senior Product Manager, seasoned by many years of international experience in a variety of roles. Unique combination of experience in entrepreneurship, product definition, marketing, management, as well as R&D, QA testing and above all the most delicate and complex profession of managing people.
Oded served as a VP product in Ilogus Ltd. and as a Product Manager of an ecommerce system in SAP.
Oded Co-founded Smartap Inc, a NY based start-up for the mobile industry, and later on served as it VP Product.
Oded raised the pre-seed round from US investors, provided strategic and tactical services including strategy and business model formulation, business planning, product management, competitive analysis and business development.

Hold a double BSc. Degree in Computer Science and Mathematics and an MBA degree in Finance, Marketing and Information Systems.

Military service at the IDF’s Intelligence corps Talpiot program as a head of System Analysts Department, and as a product manager of an artificial intelligence specialist system.
