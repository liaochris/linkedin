Murtaza Ali’s Summary

I started my career working as a QA Engineer for ePatterns, a Software company based in Pakistan. I learned the fundamental concepts of Software Quality while working at ePatterns, which at the time had some of the very best people in their field. I left ePatterns after 1.5 years to venture a new opportunity with Abacusoft, a California based company with its development house in Pakistan. I was hired mainly to assist in formation of a QA department. I left Abacusoft after 5 months with a QA department live and kicking.

I left Pakistan and came to Ireland on new adventures, and got my first job at Transaction Network Services, a US based company in Dublin. I was hired as a Network Integration Engineer, which was something new and interesting. My passion for exploring Business Applications motivated my next move, and I accepted a role in QA at Ci3 consultancy, an Irish Software company. Working at Ci3 gave me an exposure to Operational Risk Management (SOX compliant) application. After gaining some valuable experience, I bid farewell on moved on to an exciting opportunity with Sophis Technologies, a French company based in Dublin.

I joined Sophis as a Financial Test Engineer, and got a kick start with an intensive two weeks training in Paris. I am still with Sophis, exploring real-world financial instruments like Swaps, Caps and Floors, Options etc. I have found the work which I enjoy doing, and I am good at it. The question is how to be great at it? As Jim Collins stated in his book Good to Great, ‘Good is the enemy of Great’ so for me the adventure will continue until I have mastered the skills to be the best in business.
