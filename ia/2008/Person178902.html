Dinah Randall’s Summary

I have been running Spike Systems Pty Ltd, a web design and technology consultancy, since 1998. My skills include business consulting, design, technical solutions, writing for the web, project management and training. I come from a broad background in I.T. consulting and software design for business, creatives and not-for-profits.

I have specialised in the Joomla! content management system since its inception in 2005. I am a big fan of open source technology - free software we can all share. My interest is leveraging Joomla! in creative ways, to build online communities and effective marketing and communication sites.

Dinah Randall’s Specialties:

As a Joomla! implementor, I design, build and produce online communities and portals in Joomla! open source CMS. I also run the Sydney Joomla User Group and Joomladay conference.
