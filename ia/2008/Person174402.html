Steven Harper’s Summary

Steven Harper is CEO and Founder of Plan 2 Win Software. He is also a Certified Sales Coach, Vice President of Quarterly events for NETSEA, (http://www.netsea.org) and President of Sharper Performance, Sales Performance Consultants. His background includes EchoMail where he was Worldwide Vice President of Sales and Business Development, Exodus Communications, where he was Regional Vice President of Sales, and Vice President of Exodus Canada, and BBN Planet / Genuity where he was the Sr. Web Hosting Specialist. He also ran North and South American sales for IME Systems, where in 1994; he was a speaker at the "opening" of the Internet in Buenos Aires, Argentina. He sits on the board of 2 non-profits, and chases his twins in New Hampshire.

Steven Harper’s Specialties:

Teaching skills/mindsets for Sales skills, Partner and referral relationships, behavior of sales teams, entering new markets, and selling higher in organizations. Screening and hiring high performing sales people. Selling technology without jargon. Some more interesting projects have included helping non-US sales people sell in the US market, overcoming cultural and communication issues, and Interim Vice President of Sales and Marketing for a SaaS document management comapny.
