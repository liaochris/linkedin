Kim Williams’s Summary

I’m Kim Williams with Pike’s Home Maintenance.

Pike’s Home Maintenance frees homeowners from the worry and effort of repairing and improving their home. Through seasoned project managers who oversee every job and designated, trade-specific workers, we save time and money for the homeowners allowing them to focus on living in their home rather than struggling with it. For over 16 years, we have been a one-stop provider of proven solutions for all non-crisis home maintenance needs- from the shingles to the foundation. We ask for no deposits and guarantee our work so there is no financial risk. Our customers have peace of mind about their home improvements and repairs because we are committed to completing our projects and are licensed, bonded and insured. At Pike’s we seek a lifetime relationship with each of our home owners and are committed to serving their home repair needs again and again!


Good Referral:

Anyone with a “honey do” list with small to larger projects including painting, installing a new deck and replacing vinyl siding and windows.
