Verena Kroupa’s Summary

I have deliberately dedicated my career to building project experience in different areas in fashion industry, by managing projects in different marketing areas in the HQs of international fashion and lifestyle brands, such as Diesel and Pepe Jeans.

Furthermore, I enjoy applying my hands-on skills by working on cutting edge and independent fashion projects, such as developing and styling fashion shoots, working on creative direction and brand identity.

Verena Kroupa’s Specialties:

International project management experience in a rapidly and ever changing lifestyle culture.

Strong visual and hands-on skills in terms of styling, visual merchandising and design in general; trend investigator and divergent thinker and doer.

Apart of being an overall nice person to work with, I’m fluent in German, English, Italian and Spanish.
