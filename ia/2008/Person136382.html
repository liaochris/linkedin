Monty Sarhan’s Experience

  • Vice President

    MTV Networks

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; VIA; Entertainment industry)

    May 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Vice President of Business & Legal Affairs for MTV Networks, which includes MTV, MTV2, mtvU, VH1, Nickelodeon, Comedy Central, Spike TV and CMT and over fifty leading internet properties.

    Responsible for transactions in several business areas for multiple MTV Networks channels and programming services including the MTV Networks digital businesses, the MTV, VH1 and CMT branded consumer products and its online and console gaming businesses. Oversee business and legal affairs for all online and console gaming for MTV Networks and head up gaming legal team. Helped launch the critically acclaimed video game Rock Band, which has now grown to become a $1 billion franchise for Viacom. Handle other video game products based on leading MTV properties, including Jackass and Pimp My Ride, and games for MTV, Comedy Central and Spike online properties. Successfully negotiated publishing agreements with Sony, Microsoft and Nintendo helping to establish MTV Networks as a major video game publisher.
