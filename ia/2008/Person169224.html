Saqib Jafri’s Experience

  • Internee

    Pakistan Industrial Credit and Investment Corporation (PICIC)

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; PICIC; Financial Services industry)

    July 2006 — August 2006 (2 months)

    Interned for four weeks and was rotated in three departments. First two weeks were spent in the Treasury Department where I got exposure at the Front and the Back Office. Projects undertaken included a revaluation of the portfolio of Pakistan Investment Bonds held by the DFI.
    The third week was spent in the Equity Department where I got first hand experience of the Equity Market, the front office operations, the back office settlement and the middle office analysis. Projects undertaken include a company analysis of the CDC (Central Depository Committee) and an analysis of the portfolio of stocks held by the DFI as of June 30th, 2006.
    The Final Week was spent at the Risk Management Department where I familiarized myself with the different sections of the department looking after various loans departments and keeping track of their exposures and non-performing loans. Also had an overview of the BASEL II implementation being carried out at the DFI.

  • Team Leader

    LUMS Disaster Relief Team

    (Educational Institution; Civic & Social Organization industry)

    October 2005 — February 2006 (5 months)
