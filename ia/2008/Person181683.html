Steven Crawley’s Summary

An INTJ style manager (Myers-Briggs Type Indicator, see link below) with twenty plus years of ‘does it work’ problem solving that produces out-of-the-box solutions and champions change management leadership. I am a self-directed and organized problem solver with a ‘big picture’ or systems orientation who does his best work leading or as part of a high performing team.

Multi-disciplinary experience spans: manufacturing, retail, services, and non-profit organizations in roles ranging from manager, staffing specialist, internal consultant, department & business unit head with profit & loss responsibilities, hands-on supervisor, trainer, and sales.

I enjoy variety and change: welcoming the challenges and risks of learning new products, businesses, & technology and meeting new people.

A firm believer in molding the job to person based on their strengths and passions and the critical role of business leaders in championing inclusive hiring and staff development practices.

Steven Crawley’s Specialties:

Leadership, continuous improvement programs, lean manufacturing methods, organizational development initiatives, change management design & implementation, strategic planning, budgeting, and project management.

HR: compliance, compensation, benefits, employee relations, conflict resolution, performance management, recruiting, contingent & retained search, assessments, direct sourcing, interviewing & closing, training & development.
