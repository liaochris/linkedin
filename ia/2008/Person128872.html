Michael Hurston’s Summary

Corporate Marketing and Design Manager with over a decade of professional experience, with 8 years being dedicated to the Commercial Real Estate and Gaming industries.

In addition to Michael’s design and marketing experience he is also one of the main writers for the game Ave Molech, a D20 Fantasy based RPG set in a Steampunk world setting.

Recently Michael G. Hurston has branched out into Real Estate investment, currently owning and managing properties in the South San Jose area and near the luxurious Boca Park in Las Vegas.

Michael Hurston’s Specialties:

AutoCAD Level I Certified, HTML, JavaScript, ASP, PHP, CSS, InDesign, Photoshop, Acrobat, Illustrator, Dreamweaver - Print and Web design.
