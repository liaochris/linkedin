Michael Femiano’s Summary

I have been doing mortgage financing for both Purchase & Refinance transactions, here in Arizona's "Valley of the Sun" for the last 7½ years. (That’s long enough to know what I am doing, but not too long to be burned-out!)

I have three main goals:

(1) TO FIND THE RIGHT LOAN FOR MY CLIENT - As a Mortgage Loan Consultant, my job is to not only find a great interest rate, but to find the right Loan Program/Product for my client – the one that best fits their needs and financial scenario. Sometimes the best deal is not the best choice! I take finding the right Loan Program/Product for my client seriously. It is very important to me and what I strive for in every transaction. I have never put a client in a loan that I would not want for myself, if I was in their shoes . . . and I never will! My business comes almost 100 percent from satisfied former borrowers, their referrals, and real estate agents who know that I will treat their home buyers right. If I can't get a good loan for my client's specific scenario, I will tell them quickly and we will part as friends.

(2) TO CLOSE MY LOANS ON TIME! - I am very proud to say that in 7½ years, I have not had one loan that did not close on time because of me.

(3) TO PROVIDE GFE's (Good Faith Estimates) THAT ARE VERY ACCURATE, "CLEAN", AND COMPETITIVE! - I take pride in how close they match the final HUD-1 Settlement Statement, signed at Close-Of-Escrow. YOU WILL NOT FIND ANY JUNK FEES! . . . JUST GREAT INTEREST RATES! I always reveal costs up front, never imposing any last-minute junk or garbage fees.
