Susan P Joyce’s Summary

Award-winning Job-Hunt.org links to 9,979 unique, carefully-selected career resources and employers by state. Over 150 articles cover topics of critical importance to online job seekers - protecting their identity and job while job hunting, using Google to gain advantage in the job market, tapping the hidden job market, avoiding spam filters, and many more.

Job-Hunt focuses on helping people leverage Internet technologies to build successful careers in the 21st century.

Advertiser-supported Job-Hunt receives numerous awards: a Forbes Best of the Web (since 2002), US News & World Report Top Site for Finding Work (2006), and PC Magazine Best of the Internet (2004). In 2007, 15,000 to 20,000 job seekers visited Job-Hunt looking for help with their job search on an average week day.
