Beryl Westby’s Summary

Want to eliminate the chaos in your life? Get your Free Idea-Kit, filled with tips and ideas to help you organize your home, your business, your life, at the No More Chaos website, www.nomorechaos. biz.

"I want to organize my life, but I don't know where to start." "I can't find my ..." These statements have been expressed by almost everyone. The good news is that anybody can get organized.

I started an organizing business called No More Chaos because I kept hearing my family and friends express frustration and feelings of being overwhelmed about the chaos in their lives. I experienced business disorganization first hand at several of the law firms I worked at over the years as a paralegal and wish I had a nickel for every attorney I’ve helped set up their offices. Long before I started No More Chaos, I would be the one friends would call to get their house ready to sell or to help them clean out their closets.

Disorganization can rob you of the precious time you should be spending enjoying your life. By overcoming disorganization, you can be set free to live the kind of life you've always dreamed of.
