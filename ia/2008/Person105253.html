Ken Yerrid’s Summary

Pure technology enthusiast with a strong penchant for learning, exploring, and ultimately advancing. My journey has taken me from web development to graphic design to project management and information security, and most areas in between. I surprise many people with the depth of knowledge in a particular aspect of technology, considering my resume reads like a generalist. I prefer strategic thinking versus tactical, and find myself referring to things that are still 12-60 months from mainstream during feasibility and planning stages of a project.

I obtained my Master of Information Systems Management in December, with a 4.0GPA. I recently finished my MBA, where I graduated with a 3.9 (economics was the A-). After taking the rest of 2008 off from formal educational programs, I will resume my studies with a the pursuit of a PhD in Information Technology. I am currently evaluating universities in which to study from.

My goals are to eventually get into 6th gear by moving to an Information Security role, preferably as a decision maker where I can shape the strategy and enable -- not disable -- my business partners.
