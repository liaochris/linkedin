Richard Quattrocchi’s Summary

An industry thought leader with many years of senior level management experience in the medical device, medical services, and technology market sectors. In 2004 Mr. Quattrocchi founded Bridge Associates to provide consulting services to healthcare and technology companies for marketing, sales, regulatory, business development and strategic planning.

Prior to founding Bridge, Mr. Quattrocchi co-founded Home Access Health Corporation and served as its President from 1993 to 2004. Home Access Health is the only company to market FDA approve at-home test for HIV and Hepatitis C using a proprietary platform invented by Mr. Quattrocchi for which he holds 4 patents.

Before co-founding Home Access Health Mr. Quattrocchi served as the Vice President for Marketing and Sales for Telcorp now Mutare Software. Mutare specializes in Unified Messaging, Business Continuity and Specialized Integrated Voice Response software solutions. The anonymous testing concept used by Home Access Health was inspired by a custom Mutare software application and Mutare provided the seed capital to fund the start up of Home Access Health.

Before Mutare, Mr. Quattrocchi was the Area Vice President for DAVID Systems in Sunnyvale California. DAVID Systems pioneered integrated voice and data solutions in the mid 1980’s. The company was sold to Cabletron.

Mr. Quattrocchi is a graduate of the University of Illinois with a Bachelor’s degree in Liberal Arts and Science.
