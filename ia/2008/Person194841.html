Valto Loikkanen’s Summary

I like to work with innovative ideas and innovative people in new ventures that have potential. I'm an owner in several companies and also networked with joint ventures with several other companies.

I want to turn plans into action, to get results. Plans are like good ideas, there can be many great ones but without result oriented execution, those rarely generate any value.

I have a graphics designer background and over 10 years experience in general business management and a good track record for identifying new business opportunities.

My first own start-up business VTG, was specialized in mobile phone design covers IE. personalization. Established in 1995 and quickly grew up, to 100 employees with offices in US. Australia and Finland.

Experienced in creating business plans, developing and managing sales, marketing, operations and personnel at both start-up and established companies in design, IT and telecoms industry, with international work experience in Europe and USA.

As a Business Advisor, I have advised hundreds of new entrepreneurs to start up their own business.
