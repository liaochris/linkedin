Ian Rutherford’s Summary

I have over 25 Years experience of working and advising Clients in the Food & Drink, FMCG, Engineering, Retail, Pharmaceuticals Industry Sectors.

For the last 10 years I have been leading the CSI Pre Sales Team where I have been providing advice and consultancy to help Clients maximise ROI through adoption of Best Practise business process through the deployment of IT systems (SAP, BPCS & Bann).

For the last 4 years I have been heavily involved in developing and then taking to market our SAP Business All-in-One Solutions for the Food, Furniture & general Manufacturing Marketplace.

Like all good consultants I believe my key abilities are to be able listen and comment in an adversarial manner rather than a prescriptive one. I am also able to demonstrate strong communication and analytical skills across all levels of an organizational structure.
