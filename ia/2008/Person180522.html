Stephanie Fullmer’s Summary

Professional experience in Top Shelf Sales Team formation and execution of goals.

Over the past ten years I've focused solely on building sales teams, sales territories, policy and procedure development for sales teams and enhancing sales operation within new or reorganized firms.

I have successfully opened two Robert Half offices in both Morgan Hill and Monterey CA; built a sales territory and client base for Intercall in Portland OR; expanded a stale mid-west sales territory for Chrome Systems and am currently building a team of Executive Recruiters for the Accounting & Finance division of UHCSolutions in Lake Oswego.

My goal is the same with each endeavor; to build a foundation within the sales division that will ensure solid revenue flow with portfolio diversity to withstand the ever changing economy, superior ROI, a strong growth plan for years to come and finally identify the leaders who will carry out the dream.
