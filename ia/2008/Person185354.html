Tanekeya Word’s Experience

  • Artist/Sole Owner

    Neka: Art by Tanekeya Word LLC (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Fine Art industry)

    December 2005 — Present (2 years 4 months)

    Vibrant Contemporary Art...ready to affect the lives of many with positivism, nostalgia and Joy...

    As a blooming artist--I am driven--by life and vibrant color. Fashion, (the notion of) Aesthetics and Women play a dominant role in my work. Why? I am a woman who happens to be a fashionista, writer and visual artist; therefore, I am always creating and de-constructing aesthetics. I have come to realize two things: I must write and I must paint! As I see it, I am a storyteller who is forever transitioning from the beginning of a sentence to the ending of a story; yet, I am trying to write my way out with a paintbrush in hand.
