Robert Luther’s Summary

I have worked as a multimedia / marketing guy since 2000. I have a decent mix of left / right brain perspective and it has served me pretty well. I have lead the charge in marketing for FOX IV for almost five years before I left the company to work as an internet SEO consultant at Pittsburgh internet consulting.

All and all I have a very diverse background in in-house advertising and design for print and web. I manage Pay Per Click campaigns for clients and continually work to improve their performance through testing and ad optimizations.

I have edited and taken some creative lead in a variety of videos in the construction and packaging industries and am very capable with non-linear editing software, and can even pick up a camera and produce some good footage.

Currently my focus has been on Search Engine Optimization and Marketing. It is such a challenging field because of the constant changes and I am loving it.
