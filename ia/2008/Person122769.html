Markus Barnikel’s Summary

MARKUS BARNIKEL, CHIEF SALES OFFICER, YAHOO!7

Previously Director of Global Sales for Yahoo! in Sunnyvale USA, Markus Barnikel joined Yahoo!7 as Chief Sales Officer in September 2006. In this role, Markus is focused on growing Yahoo!7’s advertising revenue and leveraging cross promotional opportunities with Seven and Pacific Magazines.

Markus joined Yahoo! in May 2000 to lead the Product Marketing Team in Germany, Austria and Switzerland. From December 2001 till January 2003 Markus joined the Yahoo! Office in Singapore as Regional Head of Business Development, Marketing and Communications for South East Asia. In this role Markus led the Access, Auctions, Wireless, Travel, Personals and Shopping business of Yahoo! in five countries (Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand and Philippines). He was also responsible for Consumer Marketing, Trade Marketing and PR in this region.

Prior to his move to Australia, Markus Barnikel was overlooking the Global Media Sales Business of Yahoo! in Asia Pacific, Europe and Latin America as Director Global Sales, based at Yahoo! headquarters in Sunnyvale, California. In this position Markus was responsible for driving the companies advertising and marketing services initiatives for Top 100 global blue chip advertisers.

Prior to joining Yahoo!, Markus spent six years in Senior Management positions in Marketing and Sales, most recently at Philip Morris responsible for a portfolio of market leading FMCG brands.

Markus Barnikel holds a master degree in International Business from the European Business School in London.
