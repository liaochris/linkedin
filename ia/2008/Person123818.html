Marty Levy’s Summary

Vice President of Sales for twelve years in the semiconductor and technology industries. A track record of delivering strong revenue growth and success in technology adoption with customers in No. America, Asia, Japan, and Europe.
Expertise in building and scaling sales organizations where technical customer engagements are essential, optimizing channel resources vital, and profitable revenue growth is required.

An exceptional leader with direct sales organizations and multi-tier channel sales processes. Have broad management experience with outside sales, inside sales, and technical field support teams. Significant experience in global OEM sales activities including ODM and EMS sales coordination. Strength in vertical market sales plan development especially with mid tier or SMB customer bases, for growth phase companies. Record of achievement in both public and private companies.

In the semiconductor industry, have extensive experience with analog, mixed signal, ASSP controllers, and programmable products. Solution selling expertise has enabled successful customer adoption of these products in communications, industrial, medical, and PC and small system applications.

In my consulting business I have utilized my expertise in sales strategy development and sales execution with client companies in a variety of industries. Earlier experience in enterprise sales has also been utilized in developing sales processes, infrastructure, and practices which have achieved significant revenue growth results for these clients. Integrated marketing and sales programs for lead generation, account base development, and new market expansion have been the focus of my work.

I am known by my brand and conduct myself in all activities, consistent with it.
"Knowledge. Insight. Action. Results."
