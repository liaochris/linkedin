Joe McDonald’s Summary

After 12 years on Wall Street, I decided to persue my dream of being a sports writer. I now cover mainly baseball and hockey for my website, NY Sports Day and four magazines, including NY Sportscene, where I act as managing editor.

I mainly cover the Rangers working every game and in the summer I am at Shea and Yankee Stadium pretty often.

I also cover the US Tennis Open, the Hall of Fame introductions and Baseball's Winter Meetings.
