Nabil Khatib’s Summary

GOOGLE ENGINEERING SITE DIRECTOR (ARABIC FLUENT) MIDDLE EAST

Google is looking for a highly technical, hands-on Engineering Site Director to drive Product and Technology initiatives for the Middle East/Arab World. As most senior engineer for Google in region, Engineering Site Director will be the face for Google's engineering effort in the Arab World.

Engineering Site Director will be responsible for the overall planning, execution & success of projects with both regional & global impact. In addition to leading the engineering team or teams in the Arab markets, the Engineering Site Director will work closely with Google's senior management team, as well as local officials, universities and professional organizations.

Note: Engineering management at Google is technical. Candidates will be asked technical questions during interviews.

Required:
1. At least 10+ years of exceptional technical competence and accomplishment with a strong product development track record and continuing and enduring technical leadership throughout career
2. Software background and large scale success developing web-based applications, ideally launched in the Arab market
3. Entrepreneurial drive plus demonstrated ability to achieve goals in an innovative environment
4. Relevant experience as hands-on manager of a large fast-paced dynamic engineering team
5. Solid leadership skills and success in building a strong engineering team
6. Strong project-management skills and proven history in product delivery
7. Ability to operate and thrive in the face of ambiguity
8. Can fit in well within an informal startup environment and quickly establish credibility with very capable engineers
9. MS in Computer Science/Computer Engineering or PhD (preferred)
10. Exceptionally strong record of academic achievement

Please email chronological resume to nabilk@google.com
