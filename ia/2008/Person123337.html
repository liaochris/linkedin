Martin Bloom’s Summary

Martin is Chairman of AIM-listed Chinese company Renesola and Advisory Board Member and Special Advisor for Asia of international telecoms and media convergence venture capital fund Argopolo Capital Partners. Martin is now spending 3 months a year in China and also visits India twice a year.

Martin is also UK Chairman of the UK-China Venture Capital Joint Working Group established by the UK and Chinese governments to promote cooperation between venture capital and private equity firms in the two countries.

Since 1997, Martin has been a full-time investor in early-stage high technology companies in the UK and US. These companies often lack spread and depth of experience during this, their most vulnerable phase. Martin is a value-added investor, filling this gap - providing them with strategic partnering assistance in Asia, the U.S. and Europe, together with strategic guidance and management support, building on almost 30 years experience. Companies must have the ambition and capabilities to attain leadership positions in their chosen sectors, and must have innovative and focused business models.
