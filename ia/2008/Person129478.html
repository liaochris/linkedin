Michael Cesneau’s Summary

I've started up in a small company of the French Riviera as a Multimedia and Computing teacher for professionals. In the search for something more challenging, I then moved to Paris and trained myself on .NET Technologies to work on a collaborative project. I also worked as a webmaster for small businesses, translator French/English for IT news website www.pcinpact.com and designed the official booklet of the Asian Pastry Cup 2006 held in Singapore. I finally got caught in the Web 2.0 phenomenon and the new ways of using Internet technologies to enhance web communication and viral marketing by joining the web team of the Communication and Marketing department of IONIS Education Group (15 Institutes such as Epita, Epitech, ESME Sudria, IPSA, ISG, ISEG - 15,000 students - 2,000 teachers - 50,000 alumni) led by former President of Publicis Dialog Marc Drillech.
