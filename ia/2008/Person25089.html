Bob Wescott’s Summary

Since 1987 I've worked in the field of computer performance analysis and capacity planning. My fundamental skill is explaining complex things clearly.

I've developed and joyfully taught customer courses at four computer companies and I've been a featured speaker at large conferences.

I have also had the delightful responsibility of being a part-time tour guide at Ben & Jerry's Ice Cream.

My goal is to be of service, explain things clearly, teach with joy, tour guide with verve and lead an honorable life.
