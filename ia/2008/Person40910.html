Tom Haynes’s Summary

Tom's career objective is to support significant sustainability projects through innovation and creativity including built environment & community development projects and sustainable technology enterprises.

Tom is a passionate advocate of sustainable development. He has a diverse range of experience in the field of sustainability in technical, academic and leadership roles. He has a vision of establishing highly integrated sustainability and community development projects in India, Asia and Australia. Tom founded Create Innovation Pty Ltd with a vision to support innovation towards sustainable outcomes. Create Innovation’s core focus is: key note presentations; “innovation hot-house” workshops; project identification and international business matching. Tom is an experienced international speaker on who has presented at conferences and workshops in USA, Canada, Australia, Singapore and India.

Tom has held a long standing position as Head of Research, leading the R&D program within WBa Research a division of Williams Boag Architects. He played a key role in conceptualising and developing the main research project called the “Sustainable Innovation Feasibility Tool”. Tom’s role at WBa Research is focused on the overall strategic development, business planning and commercialization. This department has successfully won a series of grants from AusIndustry, the Australian Research Council, and the Victorian Government in conjunction with Deakin University & the University of Melbourne.

Tom has been connected with India since 1983 when he studied in Chennai on a Rotary International Exchange for a year. From 1991 to 1997, Tom studied Indian culture and Vedic Science at Masters and PhD level in the USA. Since then he has visited India numerous times, drawing on his knowledge to help support India’s cultural leadership and sustainable development. Tom is working to establish a sustainability ‘industry chapter’ within the Australia India Business Council.
