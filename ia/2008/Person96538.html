Jon Davis’s Summary

I have been working in the music and entertainment industry for the last 10 years at a variety of media companies including major label, independent music and management companies plus my own dotcom start-up.

For the last six years I have been working within SonyBMG Music Entertainment in a variety of digital marketing and business development roles within the UK team and more recently in the Global team. I headed up this area for SonyBMG UK for four years from 2002 to 2006.

Since I have been at SonyBMG I have been at the forefront of developing SonyBMG's digital revenues which have grown rapidly over the last few years.

This has involved many aspects ranging from managing and building third party relationships, putting in place a variety of commercial deals with the likes of Apple, Vodafone, O2, T Mobile, Nokia and SonyEricsson as well as numerous service providers,building and maintaing business plans and P &L, presenting at all levels within the organisation, training and developing staff.

Along the way as well as building these revenues which now account for a double digit percentage of total company revenues, I have delivered a number of unique and groundbreaking deals including: the world's first concert broadcast over a mobile phone (Natasha Bedingfield - 3) , the world's fatest ever selling download (X Factor) and the UK's first artist digital music subscription site (Westlife Platinum).

I have been fortunate to work with a wide range of artists, artist management and talent ranging from Simon Cowell, Will Young, Dido, Faithless, Foo Fighters and Avril Lavigne.

My goal for the future is simple....to make a difference.
