Michelle Greer’s Summary

After selling Volusion's ecommerce software to stores such as Barack Obama, Crutchfied, and Omni Hotels and leading the sales floor for three straight months, Volusion promoted me to a Marketing Specialist position in June 2006. I now write company press releases, do regular market research for our COO and Marketing Director, and represent Volusion to the press and the online community. I also train every Volusion sales rep on the various types of ecommerce software and the players in the industry. You can find my b-2-b marketing blog at http://onlinebusiness.volusion.com.

I also write about the Austin tech scene at www.michellesblog.net and www.geekaustin.org.

I cannot figure out if I am a geek who likes marketing, or a marketer who is a geek. To me, it seems like an advantage to be a little bit of both.
