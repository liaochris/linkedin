Dan Martinez’s Summary

Dan is an international management professional who has worked with developers, builders, real estate & hospitality professionals, investors, and bankers for over fifteen years; enhancing profits through performance and cost-competitiveness/operational efficiency initiatives. He has served as a catalyst and facilitator, leading public and private sector efforts to help define, measure, analyze, improve, and control best practices in real estate, finance, and development.

With a background in resource allocation and strategic management: he has been responsible for directing capital planning, process improvement, sales force automation, business intelligence, client relationship management (CRM), contract negotiations, and business development strategies.

He has consistently delivered measurable results, managing transactions and operations of varying cost and scope on time and budget, meeting and exceeding milestones & objectives for diverse teams and organizations over the full course of a project life cycle.
