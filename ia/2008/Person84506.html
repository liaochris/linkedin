James Bradley’s Summary

James Bradley is a Digital Media Technologist and Educator who has spent almost two decades working in the areas of digital media, interactive multimedia, web content development, web application development, Information Architecture, Interface Design, Interaction Design, Usability.

Bradley is currently Executive Director of WebServesUs, Inc. – a non-profit Internet Technology service group (www.webservesus.org).

Bradley founded and directs LucidData (formerly UWdigital) – an Internet content and software development group based in NYC since 1996.

Since 1991, Bradley has taught in the areas of digital design, web design and development, interactive multimedia, and digital video. Bradley has contributed directly to the digital design, web and multimedia technology programs at such institutions as New York University, Hunter College (CUNY), Pratt Manhattan, among others. In addition, he has taught visual art, design and theory courses at numerous institutions in New York, New Jersey and California.
