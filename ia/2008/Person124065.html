Mary McGuire’s Summary

With over 15 years of International Management and Consultancy experience Mary has earned a reputation for making positive changes and improving performance. Her focus is on operational results and performance improvement by changing the behaviour of leaders and their operational teams. She has worked across many sectors and many countries including Russia, US, Caribbean, Azerbaijan and continental Europe.

Track record includes:
• Leading the development of a performance management process to a major public sector organisation affecting 140,000 staff and looking to make improvements of £50m + per year.
• Supporting a global Oil & Gas company with the development and roll out of Group HR processes and systems to over 60 countries affecting 100,000 staff
• As Chief Executive turning around a failing services organisation to a profitable niche provider which continues to have an enviable reputation in its field with a multi-million pound turnover
• Working with an HR department in Azerbaijan, on process improvement and IT implementation, developing a number of local experts in the field, for sustainable ongoing improvements
