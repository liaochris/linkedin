Jerrod Wheeler’s Summary

I currently manage and lead sourcing teams responsible for all sourcing strategies and candidate pipelining for the US Security Markets at Booz Allen Hamilton. This includes passive/active channels, Strategic Sourcing and Critical Sourcing teams focused on proactively pipling talent (Talent Acquisition) to meet current and future business demand for all security cleared requirements and the Western Region of the United States.
