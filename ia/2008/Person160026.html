Richard Longstreet’s Summary

Investment accounting systems specialist with over 5 years of implementing, troubleshooting, and analyzing systems data. Currently running my own practice with a partner to provide clients worldwide with the expertise they require.

Experience involves creating UAT scripts, supporting front/middle/back office operations, implementing software and secondary software such as market feeds, analyzing systems bugs, training new and experienced users, foreign and domestic travel, IFRS, Insurance accounting including Schedule D reporting, AIMR compliant Performance reporting, derivative processing and analysis, Mutual Fund & Tax accounting, Fixed Income processing, Partnership Accounting.
