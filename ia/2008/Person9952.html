Amy Robertson’s Summary

I am a serial entrepreneur who is always thinking of new business ideas and I am working on cloning myself to carry out them all. :)

I started and successfully ran an Errand Service in San Diego for a couple of years before moving to the Valley and then ran my own Virtual Assistance Business that focused on general admin and bookkeeping for small businesses.

A little over a year ago I started SignonSurprise.com, which is a local community website for the Surprise, AZ area. In one year it grew to over 1 Million hits and is a great resource for our community, but an a great way for small businesses to connect to the community.

I recently launced a womens publication called A Girlfriends Guide for Always Busy Women. It is a word of mouth directory for women... by women. My first publication is due out in October and inconjunction with the publication, I also put on monthly womens events where they can come out and have fun, relax, rejuvenate, network and connect with other savvy women just like them.
