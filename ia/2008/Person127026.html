Matt Conahan’s Summary

Web professional with 10+ years of Internet industry experience.

Quick details:

Early days involved putting the largest college newspaper in the United States online (before many major metros had a Web presence! ;), helping the Washington Post build their Web presence and then working with a pioneering media start-up in Chicago (U-WIRE).

The "middle" years were spent with AOL helping them build their community publishing products (AOL Hometown and Groups@AOL) and then helping citizens in a developing country (Belize) build their communities as a Peace Corps Volunteer.

In recent years I've been with a non-profit that introduces city kids to nature (Vida Verde Nature Education) and a hybrid non-profit/for profit investment organization helping build communities around the world (offline and online.)

In each of these roles I found the data related to how people interact with products and communities (offline and on) to be the most intriguing aspect of my work. This interest led me to my current focus as the Manager of User Experience Research and Site Optimization.

In this role I drive optimization of the StubHub site via a combination of testing programs (A/B, multivariate) and web analytics.

Matt Conahan’s Specialties:

Site Optimization (A/B, multivariate testing; site personalization based on user activity); Web analytics analysis and implementation; community building and management (online and off); development of community products; data analysis and reporting.

* Expert in the following tools: Omniture - SiteCatalyst, Discover, Data Warehouse, ASI; Offermatica; comScore Media Metrix; Hitwise
* Intermediate proficiency with Business Objects Web Intelligence
