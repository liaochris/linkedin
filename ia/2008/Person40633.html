Craig Coulter’s Summary

My passion is new market development; to both work in them and develop business units for them that respond quickly and profitably. I have over 15 years experience working in emerging environmental and international markets, focusing for the last several years on North American carbon markets and related environmental technical assessment needs.

A particular interest of mine is bringing the technical aspects of GHG inventories, projects, and verification processes together to provide a foundation for corporate sustainability efforts.

I am described as:

Being practical
Bringing results
Synthesizing ideas & concepts effectively
Combining strategic vision with technical know-how
