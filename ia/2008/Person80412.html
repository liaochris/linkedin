Hani Hong’s Summary

Hani has had a variety of creative stints over the last fifteen years in the design, advertising and marketing world for a variety of individuals, organizations, and corporations, national and international, serious and whimsical. She's covered marketing, advertising, graphic design, presentation graphics, budgeting, media planning, technical and creative copy writing, editing, trafficking, branding, and a whole lot of print work. She's an excellent performer in a team environment, independently, or in a supervisory role. She's a versatile self-starter, resourceful, adaptable, articulate, and professional.
