Spencer Lewis’s Summary

Spencer Lewis is a 22 year sales professional with 19 years in the IT industry, covering enterprise application software solutions, processing services, software tools/utilities, and consulting services. His experience encompasses both small and large organizations as well as start-up operations, new market development and existing market coverage. Spencer’s efforts have resulted in the creation of two new divisions that were predominately sales, but also gave him experience in building and managing customer service, development, delivery, marketing, and finance departments.

Building strong client relationships has always been Spencer’s strength. These relationships have spanned management levels, including C level, and crossed departments. He has worked with both large and small financial institutions, payments networks, information technology companies, and healthcare organizations, both domestically and internationally. His international experience includes two years living in Brazil establishing and managing a remote sales and delivery operation. As well as accounts in Canada and Japan.

During the past three years, he built and managed his own real estate investment business. Through this venture, Spencer has continued to gain valuable sales, management, marketing, and business experience.

Spencer Lewis’s Specialties:

Spencer is a strategic thinker with a good technical background and an ability to learn things quickly. He enjoys building strong, long-term relationships with people and organizations. He also has a passion for helping others, by sharing his knowledge and experience. This allows him to help solve their problems and challenges and ultimately achieve their goals and objectives.
