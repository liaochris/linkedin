James Thomas’s Summary

I have a formal education in Physics/Astrophysics with a strong emphasis on laboratory methods and instrumentation. I have also acted as project manager on a variety of smaller aerospace/defense contracts. I have worked in a variety of laboratory settings and continue to develop my knowledge of scientific & technology development. My core technical experience is in applied plasma physics, space physics and technology, optics and lasers, data acquisition & analysis, and computational methods.

I have also worked on the preparation of a considerable number of aerospace/defense related contracts with a higher than average industry win rate. Sponsoring agencies include Air Force Research Labs (AFRL), Missile Defense Agency (MDA), DARPA, NAVY, ARMY Tacom, and NASA.

I am also a web developer and SEO consultant with experience in XHTML/CSS/PHP and SEO/SEM/Analytics methodologies.

My current interests include the emergence of the new space industry and space commerce as well as in understanding how climate change will unfold and what advanced knowledge can be obtained. I have a specific interest in how climate change will influence resource and commodity markets and human civilization.
