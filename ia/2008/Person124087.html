Mary Robertson, CFP, EPC’s Summary

“Planning is about vision. It is planning for the life you want.”

Mary Robertson, CFP,EPC was a senior Bank Manager with a leading Canadian multinational financial services organization for over 20 years and was Director of Business Development for a large wealth management company. Mary is also past Vice-Chair/Director of Newmarket Hydro Ltd. Aside from her professional occupations, Mary has extensive volunteer experience and is currently sitting on the Brighton Tourist, Culture and Events Committee and the Property Standards Committee. She has gained extensive knowledge and expertise in helping people align their financial resources with their goals and values. “I decided to leave the world of large financial institutions and feel that I am in a better position to help clients as an independent financial planner. Mary holds the Certified Financial Planner and the Elder Planning Counselor designations and is a member in good standing of the Financial Planning Standards Council. She and her husband Doug, moved from Newmarket to Brighton in August 2006 and love their home on the water. They are looking forward to becoming part of the Brighton community and to enjoy their new lifestyle with their children and grandchildren.
