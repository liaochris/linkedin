Jim Welch’s Summary

I'm a seasoned entrepreneur who's been creating value in the mobile market since 1993. Prior to that I did a stint at Motorola's Wireless Data Group where we spent much of our time defining the wireless market and making some bold predictions for its future growth. Of course, our forecasts were about 10 years off but now they're on target!

After launching my company MobileSoft in 1993, I spent more than 10 years delivering computer-aided dispatch and navigation applications to the transportation industry. Our TaxiPak fleet management system provided efficient call center software, optimized the distribution of customer requests for service using geographic information systems, and interfaced to mobile data terminals installed in each taxi. The condensed version...we made ordering a taxi a more pleasant and efficient experience! Our products delivered dispatch and fleet management services to a diverse international market in locations down-under (New South Wales, Australia) to over-the-top (Oulu, Finland).

In addition, I served five years as a naval officer, surface warfare officer, and nuclear power plant supervisor. During this time, I made deployments to the Persian Gulf in support of military operations in the region.

A graduate of the United States Naval Academy (Go Navy!), I earned a degree in computer science and more recently earned an MBA from Ohio State's Fisher College of Business (Go Bucks!).

I recently joined deCarta, Inc. in San Jose, CA to provide technical leadership and direction in their sales team. deCarta is the leading provider of geospatial / LBS platforms across four distinct market segments: enterprise, wireless, personal navigation, and internet.
