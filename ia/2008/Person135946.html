Mario Pilz’s Summary

Having graduated from Dresden University of Technology with a master’s degree in Information System Technology, I looked back on rich practical experiences. Research projects led me to work with Infineon Semiconductors, Deutsche Bahn AG and also with the INRIA laboratories in France. In the latter case I elaborated a scientific paper on interactive evolutionary image denoising which was published by IEEE CEC 2005. My master thesis pushed forward the value of laser pointer interaction.

In the following I went to Paris to join the prestigious CDI MBA program to add a managerial dimension to my profile in an international context and to further evolve my entrepreneurial character. During the program I worked as Consultant for Arcelor Mittal which led me to work closely with top level management in order to implement CMMI quality standards into the IT work processes of the company.

My endeavor to gain more practical experience in both Product Management and Sales then carried me to Siemens in Germany.

Personally I spend lots of time on the latest web technologies (Web 2.0, Rails, Mashups, Open Social etc) and their meaningful applications in different fields.
