Davina Haisell’s Summary

I am a life coach and a writer.

My career background is in advertising and publishing where I was employed as a proofreader and a computer artist for close to 20 years.

I worked with words then and still do now, through life coaching and writing.

As an entrepreneur I run Crimson Compass Life Coaching and Writer Sense Writing Services.

The space held between the coach and client is the playground where intuition comes alive. I'm constantly blown away by the insights that drop in from out of nowhere.

Ah-ha moments light your way. They are best to be used, not admired and placed in a trophy case so to speak. The ego will step in and try to claim them but they will only be stored to brag about later or worse, forgotten.

Ah-ha moments are more than just the shooting stars of intuition, they are that spark that has the potential to ignite creativity and inspire change. But they are fleeting.

The coach will hold the client accountable to these moments. Together they will design a goal and a direction that is aligned with the clients’ best interests.

With the launch of a personal development blog my love of writing has given me the opportunity to share insights gained through my own journey.

I have enjoyed the process of writing the content for my website, in addition to writing and designing the sales page, or squeeze page as some call it.

I have also had the pleasure of supporting other writers, who have been thankful for my expert editing and proofreading services.

Sara, one of my clients, shares her feedback:

“You did an excellent job with my last post…. I couldn't believe how many things you caught and how much better the post read after your edits. Thank you very much!”

To read some of my own posts visit my blog at http://lovingpulse.wordpress.com.

Read about my experience doing a fire walk in The Fire’s Dance. Or, learn how feeling connected can lead to a balanced life in Awakening To A Balanced Life.
