Adam Turner’s Summary

A highly experienced senior Marketing and Product Executive who has a solid track record of success in blue chip and start-up organisations. A confident and persuasive communicator who is accomplished at developing and leading cross-functional and multi-cultural teams and who has a wealth of international experience gained working for both fixed and mobile network operators. He has led product development and marketing teams in the areas of content, software applications, devices, fixed mobile convergence, DSL, voice and data services for both B2C and B2B markets.
