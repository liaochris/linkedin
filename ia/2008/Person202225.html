Yoel Ben-Avraham’s Summary

Over the past twenty years that I have been involved with IT, I have had the opportunity to explore every role in the Software Development process for the Personal Computer platform. In parallel I have engaged in a variety of training activities that run the gamut from End-User training development & delivery, to technical & occupational training in Software Development and culminating in "soft-skills" acquisition for leaders and managers in the technical environment.
