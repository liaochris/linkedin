Dave O'Brien’s Summary

Accomplished, results-oriented leader with over 20 years of proven success in managing operations of companies, from start-ups to complex multi-national corporations.

Strong history of guiding businesses through rapid growth, globalization, expansion and outsourcing. Dynamic organizer of people, multi-national teams, and projects, with inventive flair for mobilizing and implementing business strategies and opportunities.

Personable communicator and team player, dedicated to helping others reach their goals. Passionate about giving back to the community and helping Non-profits achieve their mission.

Dave O'Brien’s Specialties:

Entrepreneurial Start-ups and Funding, Mergers and Acquisitions, Strategic Planning, Turnarounds, Business Process Re-Engineeering, International Perspective
