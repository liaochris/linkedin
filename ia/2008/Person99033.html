Jesse Samuelson (Jesse@bloomfieldco.com) LION’s Summary

Bloomfield & Company is a boutique personnel consulting firm specializing in the permanent placement of administrative professionals. My area of expertise is Executive Assistants, Administrative Assistants, Office Management, and Human Resource professionals, but I do work on client orders at all levels. Our clients vary but most are very well known firms here in the Washington D.C. area - consulting and professional service firms, corporate real estate, law firms, financial firms, lobbying, and non-profit organizations.

If you are looking for a new career opporunity in the D.C. area, I would be happy to make you aware of our current openings. Please forward an updated copy of your resume to: Jesse@bloomfieldco.com

You can also find more information on our website: www.bloomfieldco.com

I look forward to working with you!
