Jamar Cobb-Dennard (jamar@jamarspeaks.com)’s Summary

There we were, sitting in the Kona Cafe in the middle of 100-degree desert Arizona. We hadn't seen our fellow rider Tommy in hours, and our minds were ravaged with guilt at the thought of what may have happened to him. Even after calling the local police, state police, and every hospital in the area, we still had no answers - and then the phone rang...

Join Jamar Cobb-Dennard as he takes your audience through the motivational adventure of a lifetime – riding a bicycle 3400 miles from Los Angeles to Boston!

In this motivational keynote/seminar, listeners will learn how to:
• Never give up on their goals
• Improve their performance
• Stay perpetually motivated
• Push themselves
• Ignite their enthusiasm
• Be in control of their results
• Embrace a bigger cause for bigger sales
• Fight their way to the top

If any professional wants to get bigger results from their work, and enjoy their life more in the process, this presentation is a must hear!


"Jamar, God so loved the world that he sent you here to speak life into people and to help them to live their dreams ...."
-- Les Brown, Leading Motivational Speaker


Starting his sales career in 1999, Jamar has become one of the top representatives in the history of a $1/4 billion direct sales company. In 2001, he experienced 200% growth in sales, was Top 10 in the Nation in 2004, and was part of a national board that developed new products and sales programs for Vector Marketing Corporation in 2005.

Jamar released his first book, The Winning Mind, in mid-2007, and is known for electrifying and engaging his audiences with his vivacious energy, to-the-point instruction, and a style of story telling that hits home.
