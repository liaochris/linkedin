Rob Flammiger’s Summary

During my 12 years in Recruitment I have worked in the Telecom, IT, Finance, Commercial, Industrial and now Security Sectors.

In that time I have worked on Contract, Permanent, Retained and Executive Search Assignments with clients all over the World.

I believe I have the experience to apply what I have learned to any market and to any assignment and be successful.
