Tim Heineke’s Summary

// Studied Business Administration and got my B.B.A (1999 - 2003)

// I was a DJ during those years at various clubs, festivals, venues. A great way too make a living and got the "music bug" there. (1997 - 2003)

// Started off as a concert promoter, festival organizer, talent booker & agent at ClubRisk. At that time one of Hollands premier electronic music promoters. (2002 - 2003)

// Started Miles Ahead. Concert promoter, promotion, branded entertainment/music marketing, radio production (2003 - 2005)

// Started and co-founded Record Label Kindred Spirits putting out all the talent Amsterdam has, and trying to put Amsterdam on the musical map so to speak. I'm still involved. (2003 - present)

// Founded Digital Supply Chain Company IndependentIP (IIP). A web-based music management tool for the music industry, being able to control your whole supply chain and your partners. Privately funded and backed by early Skype angels. Company raised more then 2M euros, left it in April 2007, and got into Twones! I'm still a major shareholder. (2005 - 2007)

// Founded Twones. My biggest and hottest thing so far. Twones is a music service which brings all your music together. It's a new way to store, organize, find & share music played all over the web (such as YouTube, Myspace, Last.FM, Hypemachine, Muxtape and many more) and on your computer (iTunes). With Twones you can bookmark the musical web and receive your friends music feeds from all over the web in real-time. Privately funded by Live Nation angels & partnership with Live Nation. Twones will launch in private Beta on 28th September 2008. (2007 - present, founded in 2008)

http://twones.com

I'm 29, i live in Amsterdam
