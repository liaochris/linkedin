    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Information Services industry)

    2006 — Present (2 years)

    The Digital Pen & Paper technology is a new and original method of gathering data that is a powerful alternative to the expensive and difficult operations needed to validate solutions of Remote Data Entry.

    The availability of the original paper document with the authentic signature proves to be an enormous simplification of the validation of the acquisition system.

    Digital Pen Corporation is capable of not only transferring the information gathered within an existing CRM, ERP systems, but also of integrating other operating platforms with this innovative method of gathering data.
