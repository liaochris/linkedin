Cedric Crear’s Summary

Owner and operator of Crear Creative Group (CCG) a boutique design firm located in Downtown Las Vegas that provides community-minded and community-driven strategic communications. I have more than five years as a member on the board of the Red Cross as well as providing pro bono services for the American Red Cross of Southern Nevada. I am a volunteer with the Andre Agassi Charitable Foundation®, an organization dedicated to improving the quality of public education for underserved youth in America, and a member of the Nevada System of Higher Education Board of Regents for District 1. I also support the private, non-profit, multi-service Animal Foundation of Las Vegas (dedicated to the humane treatment and improving the lives of animals in the Las Vegas Valley) and involved with Bono’s (U2) call to action “One the Move,” dedicated to AIDS in Africa. “One the Move” is an auxiliary of the ONE Campaign devoted to ending poverty.

Cedric Crear’s Specialties:

Full-service agency specializing in branding, corporate identity, web design and media production for gaming and finance, hospitality, non-profit and education to regional and global businesses.
