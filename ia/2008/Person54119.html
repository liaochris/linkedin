David Prior’s Summary

Visionary Commercial/Technology Hybrid. Experienced as employee, intrapreneur, entrepreneur, and venture advisor.

With deep technology knowledge and strong business acumen, I bring the ability to look across a range of opportunities and to identify and enact strategic approaches to new business. As an advocate of rapid development cycles and the necessity of using startup approaches to provide incumbent organisations with competitive advantage, I recognise and thrive in environments that present unique opportunities to deliver real benefits to customer and employer alike. I am best described as a strategic technologist.

2007: Made the 'Courvoisier Future 500' list for Science and Innovation.
Nov2007: Just launched: http://www.toastandtrash.com

David Prior’s Specialties:

Internet exploitation (Web 2/3/n.0), GIS, exploitation of open-source techniques, venture advisor, investment advisor, open-standards, telecoms, data and information fusion and exploitation, knowledge bases, complex event processing, systems (and system of systems) design and development, branding, promotion, business strategy, leadership, inspiration, and startup techniques.

  • Practice Director, Internet Systems

    PBI Media (The Phillips Group)

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Market Research industry)

    August 1999 — December 2001 (2 years 5 months)

    I was a freelance consultant in the area of Internet technologies and wrote the report 'netpressure.com'. I was then recruited as a 'Practice Director' to lead research activities focusing on the impact of Internet Protocol on incumbent telecommunications companies and networks. I then delivered an additional three full research reports, contributing chapters to several others.

    Other activity saw the development and execution of multiple single client research projects across Europe, the USA, and Asia. I also provided thought-leadership through over 250 conference presentations and 'C'-level meetings across the worldwide telecoms and Internet sectors. I conceived and created the first ever model of Internet Protocol traffic across 50 cities in Europe, innovating the use of voice traffic as a proxy for intercity and cross-border data exchange.

    Online references to this work can be found by using the targeted search terms Tarifica OR "Phillips Group" "David Prior" in Google.
