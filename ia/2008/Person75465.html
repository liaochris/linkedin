This is Me!

I stepped into this world on March 28, 1980 and have been born & bought up in the city of dreams that never sleeps called ‘Mumbai’.

I am a chef & an entrepreneur at heart with a Hotel Management & a MBA degree. I have co-founded and simultaneously manage an organization - Think People Solutions which focuses on delivering solutions in the HR & Recruitment Space to fortune 500 organizations.

My area of forte is Business Development & Strategy. The complete spectrums of services that we cater to are: HR Solutions, Corporate Trainings, Recruitments, RPO, Recruitment Consulting and Recruitment Branding.

The establishment of this firm to me has been a journey of constant exploration, of knowledge through the emerging day-to-day challenges of stabilizing a start-up. More than anything else it has moulded me as an individual who dreams to grow beyond any limitations and strive for all that one can be.

I believe in the conjoining of Intelligence and Knowledge to practice excellence, which is a continual process and not a singular achievement, demonstrated with my attributes – Sales, Marketing & people management skills, Strategic Planning, Revenue Growth strategies, Team building & Team Retention, Negotiation skills, Branding & promotions, Operational successes, Productizing strategies, Expertise in New age technologies – Software and Hardware, market updates to summarize.

Added to the core strength in marketing and management, an undying spirit in business and public relations, have always served best, evident as added value in the assignments and accomplishments narrated.
