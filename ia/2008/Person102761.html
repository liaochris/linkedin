Karyn German’s Summary

Karyn is a product development and operations professional with over 20 years of experience in such diverse areas as Software Engineering, Product Management, Technical Operations, Corporate Planning and Professional Services. She is adept at ensuring these organizations work effectively with Sales, Marketing and Corporate Strategy to deliver high-quality products to clients. She has served as individual contributor, manager and executive in the aforementioned areas. Karyn has earned an exemplary reputation for building high-performing teams, establishing and promoting a healthy corporate culture and simultaneously executing to multiple diverse functions. In addition, Karyn has an innate ability to serve as a strong customer advocate and has proven this ability in numerous positions.
