Alex Wiederhold’s Summary

Starting in the pre-dot-com IT era my professional life has led to a variety of technical positions. The focus of my recent studies is on value-adding functions that drive organizations forward, e.g. IT based performance management and the most efficient use of personnel in virtual teams. This complements my technological expertise as I help align IT with business for a globalized world.

I hold degrees in marketing, business and communications from schools in Europe. Combining these studies with my IT background allows me to work in technical as well as business environments. A history of training and coaching for consultancies and institutions of higher education has made me a confident presenter, a rare find in IT.

Throughout my career organizations have appreciated my combination of technical knowledge, entrepreneurial spirit and understanding of business requirements. I have been described as a self-starter with strong interpersonal and communication skills and am known for my can-do attitude.
