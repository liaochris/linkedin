Stephen Oliver’s Summary

I have nearly 20 years experience working in the software industry delivering software products and services. I am also an entrepreneur and have substantial experience of business and technical consulting, as well as some experience within the aviation sector. I have worked outside the UK for 15 years. I am fluent in English (mother tongue), French and Dutch/Flemish, as well as having a basic grasp of German.

Stephen Oliver’s Specialties:

I specialise in business planning strategy, marketing and business development within technology companies. I enjoy working with start-up scientific, technical or engineering companies that have strong ambitions for growth.
