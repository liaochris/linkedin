Ger Vloothuis’s Summary

Residential estate networks and rural community networks for the delivery of Internet, telephony and television services (Triple play networks).

From the early all-coax networks through Hybrid Fibre Coax (HFC) networks to state-of-the-art Fibre to the Home (FTTH) networks.

Ger Vloothuis’s Specialties:

Network architecture and engineering. Product selection. Design, implementation and maintenance. Network operation and support organizations. Body Corporate and Stakeholder financing, reporting and fund raising. Consultancy in the development of residential and rural network projects.
