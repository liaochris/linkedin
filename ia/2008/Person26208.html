Rob Solberg’s Summary

Brainwave Studios is a producer of morally-uplifting, cutting-edge children's entertainment. Our vision is to build a successful and magnetic company that utilizes technology to provide kids and their parents with high-quality, culturally relevant, and morally enriching entertainment options.

From early 1996 through late 2006 we provided hundreds of clients across the globe with high-quality production services including original music, audio and sound design, graphics, animation, and web design. As of January 2007 we have changed our focus and are now bringing all of our skills and experience to bear on the production of our own original line of children's properties. We are starting with a massive multi-player website called Franktown Fun that will launch in early 2008. This will be followed up by a related music CD, and then a related animated series.

If you are a private investor interested in a great opportunity to make money while also making a positive cultural impact in the lives of kids through children's entertinament, please visit www.brainwavestudios.net/investor.

(NOTE: As of January 2007, Brainwave's commercial web & interactive design services are being handled by Cora Syndicate: www.corasyndicate.com)
