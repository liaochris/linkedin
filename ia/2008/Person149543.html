Peter Forsyth’s Summary

I help small businesses, nonprofit organizations, and political campaigns overcome communications and technological challenges.

Puddletown Consulting has been in business since October 2004. I am currently working with a wide variety of clients (see http://www.puddletowntech.com/links.html ), developing web sites, designing and executing media and outreach campaigns, and developing/maintaining technological infrastructure.

I also currently volunteer for Bus Project and Free Geek, primarily focusing on community and media outreach and communications. Samples of my writing may be found at http://peteforsyth.com/writing .

Peter Forsyth’s Specialties:

web development (especially Drupal-based systems), writing and distributing press releases, writing and editing, system optimization, backup systems, cross-platform integration, graphic design and production for print and web.
