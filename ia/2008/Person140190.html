Nhia Yang’s Summary

Throughout my tenure with the Bemis Company, I was commonly charged with the responsibility to evaluate, change and improve the polices, procedures and administration of the different areas I've had to work with.

My first challenge upon starting with the Bemis Company was to standardize the recruitment process for what was at the time the Curwood Corporate Human Resources group. The position was directly responsible for all salaried recruitment for all Corporate facilities as well as the 12+ manufacturing facilities within the Fox Valley area ( to include Oshkosh, Appleton, and New London ).

Shortly, our department underwent a merger with our Bemis Corporate HR Department, and I was promoted to the Employment/Recruitment Specialist. My role was re-focused to be responsible for high level salaried recruitment for Corporate Offices as well as Engineering recruiting. In this role, I was able to influence mananagers, directors and VP's regarding their recruiting processes and decisions. As well, I became a resource for other HR Managers regarding recruitment and Affirmative Action guidelines and policies.

Recently this year, I was promoted to my current position. My first task was to assist in a Kronos Time and Attendance Keeping System implemention in partnership with our Corporate HR Department. I am currently the primary contact for questions locally regarding its functionality. As well, I have had the responsibility of evaluating and improving all of our policies and procedures regarding FML, WC, Vacation, Payroll, and other related matters.
