John Iovino’s Summary

John Iovino’s 24 plus years of audiovisual facilities consulting and design, space planning, project management and client relations experience includes positions as Designer/Draftsperson, Senior Designer/ Draftsperson, Projects Manager, Associate and Senior Consultant (current). In addition to the aforementioned, Mr. Iovino has managed and coordinated entire construction scope on projects, from demolition to client move-in acting as the client representative and project manager to sub-consultants, trades and the design team'.

Mr. Iovino began his journey in the Audiovisual Facilities Design and Consulting field in 1984 with R.S.I., based in Plainview, New York. After an 11 year tenure, enjoyed a small, home-based free-lancing business in project management and space planning. In 1998, re-entered the audiovisual consulting field as a designer, consultant and project manager with Home-Theatre by Hi-Tech Audio. In 2003, Mr. Iovino joined C.M.S. in Mellville, NY, resigned in January 2005 and in February 2005 began his journey at SHEN MILSOM & WILKE, Inc.where he is currently employed. Starting as an Associate, Mr. Iovino was elevated to his present title of Senior Consultant and Project Manager in January 2006 (just 10 months after being hired) and hopes to be promoted to Senior Associate by Q4 2008.
