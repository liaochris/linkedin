Heather Bashaw’s Summary

I currently serve as a copywriter for Columbia, Mo. full-service advertising agency Visionworks Marketing & Communications. In this position I write copy for Web, print, radio, television, event, articles and public relations. I am also the go-to for all things Web, digital, social and interactive at the agency. They count on me to bring those kinds of projects from concept through fruition.

I also attained valuable experience with creative, event planning and client services through my eight years leading the Heavy Frequency Magazine staff. During my eight years running the magazine, I coordinated all print, digital and broadcast promotions and managed the entire advertising division of the company.

As Art Director of Mojo Ad, I worked closely with a team of ambitious advertising students, producing creative work for clients such as KOMU-8 TV, Long John Silver's and A&W. In this position I created a wide variety of print design work (print ads, posters, passes, CDs and inserts, billboards, window decals, merchandise) as well as Web, digital and interactive media.

I also honed key skills in creative consultation, project management and print media in my position as a project coordinator for FedEx Kinko’s.

In a company, I look for loyal team members who feel like family. I seek an environment with room to grow and hone strategic and creative skills in the advertising industry. I look for a company that provides opportunity to improve and advance. Most importantly, I look for an agency that produces consistently innovative, excellent work for its clients.
