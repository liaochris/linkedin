Brett A. Blake’s Summary

Brett A. Blake was selected as one of the 100 Top Venture Entrepreneurs in Utah in 2007. He has been active in entrepreneurship and venture stage investing as the Managing Director of Utah-based, Blake Capital, LLC, and is currently the President and CEO of ACCEPTx Software, LLC.

Previously, Brett was VP of Marketing at USANA Health Sciences which grew from $120mm to $270mm during Brett’s five plus years leading marketing. Brett also served as GM of the company's Australia and New Zealand subsidiaries.

Brett was a marketing executive at Frito-Lay, Inc. where he was part of the New Ventures Group that re-launched Cracker Jacks in 1997. While at Frito-Lay he launched six new products on the $2 billion plus, Lay's Potato Chips brand. He also served as the Director of Marketing for Xtra On-Line, Inc. and for Melaleuca, Inc.

Brett earned an MBA from the Harvard Business School where he was selected as a Dean's fellow, co-president of the marketing club, publisher of the yearbook and an elected representative to the Ethics Committee. He graduated cum laude with a bachelor's degree in communications from Brigham Young University and was elected as the school's student-body president.

Mr. Blake has served as Chairman of the Utah State Special Olympic Summer Games; as a member of the Board of Directors for Brigham Young University's Alumni Association, as delegate to the World Anti-Doping Agency Symposium on Supplements and Sport; and as a Board of Director for the following organizations: the Eastern Idaho Better Business Bureau, the BYU College of Nursing Advancement Board, the Utah Valley State College Direct Selling Alliance, and the Frisco Project for the Future. Brett Blake is an Eagle Scout and served a volunteer church mission in Washington D.C. He is the father of four and has been married to Erin for 17 years.
