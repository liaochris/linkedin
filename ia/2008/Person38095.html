Clare Munday’s Summary

An executive with 15 years of solid experience in developing and delivering exceptional user experiences in digital and physical user experiences for blue chip companies and the public sector.

Clare now works independently for a range of clients consulting and delivering everything from digital strategy, product and software concepts and user experience design to information architecture and usability.

Having delivered a huge number of varied projects I can quickly understand what's needed and support organisations to deliver on their visions. Having background in organisational and digital Human Factors and Ergonomics also means I can support the often complex organizational change required to support the design and delivery of new services and products.

Balancing the commercial business goals with the user interface is what I enjoy and seems to be where my expertise lies, achieving a balance for both.
My belief is that these can be mutually supportive rather than exclusive. Happy users and successful businesses - wonderful products and increased commercial prowess.
