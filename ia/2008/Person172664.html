Seneca "The Beast" Doss’s Summary

My background in music is extensive but rather short. My first internship was at HOT97 working under the Director of Music Advertising in 2001. Later on during the internship, I was put on payroll and was doing street team for CD101.9, KissFM and HOT97. I later left to become a personal assistant for Darrin Henson of Soul Food. It was with Darrin that I broke out and initially learned that my fate would soon lead to publicity because even though I was coined the assistant, the things I did where publicity driven. I got interviews on him with Lenny Green of KissFM, WPGC in DC and XM radio to promote his calendar. I also arranged his appearance at the Hue-Man Bookstore in Harlem for his calendar signing.

Eventually Darrin and I would go separate ways. I would do other internships and ventures to increase my strengths as a good worker at such places like Excite Media as a product placement intern and The Brooklyn Academy of Music just to name a few. In 2006 I worked for a Brooklyn based Music PR boutique as a music publicist with alternative acts like Dashboard Confessional and MC Lars. In my artist folder... I secured interviews, previews and etc. for alternative bands like: Nightmare Of You, DJ Hyper, Digweed and The Lordz.

Since going independent, I've gotten many of my own clients in such publications and online sites like Allhiphop.com, Black Beat Magazine, Rolling Out Weekly, Hip-Hop Connection, Allaboutjazz.com and many others.

*Most recently "The Cipha Sounds Effect/Shade 45 with Sirius Radio" (Dec. 04 2007)

On The Side:

*I'm also a writer for urban takeover sites, Hiphopruckus.com and AmalgamDigital.com.

*Entertainment site Starpulse.com

*Contributing writer for Brooklyn publication Courier-Life.

Seneca "The Beast" Doss’s Specialties:

I specialize in bridging ideas and strengths together for the greater good of all parties involved. From radio, to print or just a typical event... I've been apart of it.
