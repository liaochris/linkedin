Timothy O'Keefe’s Summary

I am an an Audio Designer/Composer and run my own e-label, Cozy Music. I have created/designed audio content for video games, film, television, theatre, dance and the web. I am composing/producing three personal music projects, and releasing other artists on my e-label, Cozy Music. I am always interested in creating audio content for various projects and have an online portfolio at www.cozyaudio.com.

I also am a Project Developer for www.indieartsri.com. I develop all aspects of IndieArts/ri including site concept, recruiting independent artists & groups as members/contributors, developing partnerships with other RI based organizations, and soliciting sponsors. IndieArts/ri is a splinter group of The Arts & Business Council of RI.

Timothy O'Keefe’s Specialties:

I can create music in a variety of styles. I am well-versed in a range of music hardware/software. I have a grasp of all aspects of audio design including music composition, sfx design, dialogue recording, audio engineering, scoring, midi, and mixing.

I have extensive event organization skills including concept, logistics, booking, and promotion. Well-rounded creative thinker.
