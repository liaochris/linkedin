Ram Garg’s Summary

Software Performance Assurance (SPA) focuses exclusively on outsourcing and consulting services for all aspects of software quality management, and testing. We are specialists, not generalists, in our field and integrate measurable and repeatable processes, patented technology, and highly capable specialists in the field of Software Performance Assurance to offer our clients an efficient and effective solution that reduces the time, cost, and overall risk associated with testing and verifying software products.

Quality: We are specialists in software testing. It’s all we do. Our people are experts leveraging purpose-built processes, methodologies, tools, and infrastructure to offer true value as your software testing partner.

Capability: We cover all aspects of the software performance assurance spectrum including Strategy and Planning, Infrastructure Development, Execution, Compliance, and Automation. Our patented IP is being used by major enterprises including US Airforce, Disney, and Chrysler.

Visibility and Risk Management: Our process-oriented Service Delivery model assures constant feedback so that you have real-time visibility of real and potential issues. We reduce unpleasant surprises and overall risk.

Flexibility: We offer a full range of engagement models, from complete outsourcing of the QA function to specific project execution, on-site or in our delivery center. We are able to provide contract personnel or assist with internal human capital development

Value: We leverage onshore and offshore capabilities to optimize the cost/value equation. We are multi-sourcing specialists with the expertise necessary to efficiently manage complex engagements.

Credibility: Our management team members are recognized and respected authorities and innovators in the software testing and QA, project management, and process improvement spaces. We have been and continue to be published in well-respected technical and business journals.
