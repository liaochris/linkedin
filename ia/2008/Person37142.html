Christy Lee Hughes’s Summary

Christy Lee Hughes is a professional actress, dancer, voice-over artist and Certified Les Mills BodyJam Instructor. She is currently a member in excellent standing with Screen Actors Guild and American Federation of Television and Radio Artists. Christy Lee is also a member Upstate Independent Filmakers. She has years of experience working as an actress in feature and independent films, commercials, theater and radio regionally as well as nationally. She grew up in Albany, NY and studied acting in Manhattan, NY as well as Hollywood, CA. In her free time, when she is not pursuing her dreams of acting and helping others learn the ropes of the business, she likes to dance, hike and run. In the beginning of her acting career, she was a contestant in the Miss NY USA pageant as well as a dancer on MTV. She graduated college with a BA in Communications and an AAS in Paralegal Studies as well as a Series 6/63 license to sell investments and insurance, but nothing comes close to the joy she feels when living her dreams.
She has just been cast as Janice in the film, Predisposed written by Ron Nyswaner and Philip Dorling, she is very excited about the production.
