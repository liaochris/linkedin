Trip Young’s Summary

After receiving my undergraduate degree from Davidson College and my Masters from Villanova University, I began my professional career with the Q Works Group. Recruiting exclusively within the Consumer Durables industry, I focus on mid-level to executive level positions throughout the country with an executive search team. We work with a variety of types of companies nationwide in matching high caliber industry experienced candidates to any position you are looking to fill. I am dedicated to serving the Consumer Durables industry and to be an integral part of my client companies' recruiting process. I seek a thorough understanding of their unique requirements and culture, and provide top talent with the right skill set, experience and attitude.

I pride myself on developing strong relationships and providing a superior level of service to both the clients and candidates that I work with. Because of my strong passion for the recruiting profession as well as for the Consumer Durables industry, it is my goal to provide companies in the industry with a superior level of recruitment service and the highest caliber candidates with specific career experience that will add immediate value to their organization.
