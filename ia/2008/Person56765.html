Edward Franklin’s Summary

My goal is to provide top-notch, cutting-edge, yet reliable services to my clients. Founder of this boutique consulting and application development firm specializing in Enterprise Portal Strategies, Content Management and Workflows, Information Architecture as well as interface design and implementation. Applied Elements has provided both strategic consulting and hands on services in Portal design and development as well as web based, sales and marketing , “touch-point”, application design and development. Applied Elements partners with such companies as Adobe, Vignette, BEA, FormRouter, Inc., Sena Systems, Wave Maker and Monster Productions to provide clients with everything from self-serve interactive forms libraries to highly customized Content Management Systems that are easy to use and maintain. Clients include: OppenheimerFunds, Inc., JP Morgan Chase, Deutsche Bank, Peoples Bank, Ziff Brothers Investments, Red 7 Media, IQ Financial Services, Edelman Leather, Inc, SENA Systems. Please contact for specific project experience and highlights.
