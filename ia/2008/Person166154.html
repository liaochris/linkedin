Ruth Vanderwey’s Summary

Ruth is an Executive with over 22 years in Business Solutioning and Information Technology in Corporate, Agency and Government cultures.

Her industry experience is expansive and includes: finance, telecommunications, retail, government, mining and exploration, utilities and aviation.

A results driven Executive focused on success of the business , who builds long term capabilities, while meeting the immediate day to day demands with excellence and efficiency.

A visible leader and change agent who excels in strategy, business process improvement, organizational change, and delivery.

Outstanding relationship building and communication style at all levels, from junior ranking employees to the Executive suite.
