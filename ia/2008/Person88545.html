Jennifer Kessinger’s Summary

International Trade Attorney and Consultant with over 12 years of combined experience with a Big 4 international trade consulting practice, a large mid-west law firm's Customs and International Trade practice, and as a clerk at the U.S. Court of International Trade.

Jennifer Kessinger’s Specialties:

All aspects of U.S. Customs law and compliance (including classification, valuation, special trade programs, and Customs audits), export control compliance under the Export Administration Regulations and the International Traffic in Arms Regulations, antiboycott laws, compliance with economic and trade embargo and sanctions, and international trade representation and litigation.
