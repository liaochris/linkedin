Grant Cardone’s Summary

Graduated from McNeese State Univ with Accounting Degree.

Reached the top 1% of Automotive Industry.

Revolutionized Automotive Industry with "Information Assisted Selling" (tm).

Training programs and technology have been adopted by nearly all manufactures.

Developed Negotiating Software to improve gross margins & customer experience.

Created a Real Estate Investment Company that has been involved in over 200 million dollars of transactions.

Delivered thousands of Sales, Business and Money Seminars both in the US and Internationally to hundreds of thousands of people.

Has been guest on Fox TV, Today Show, Hollywood Access, MSNBC, CNN Radio, Wall Street Journal, Sally Jesse Raphael, Dr. John Ralls, Michael Jackson, and his lectures were broadcast by Premier Radio Tour from Radio City Music Hall to over 800 sydicated radio shows.

Regular author for Huffington Post.

His first book Selling the Secret to Success sold out of the first two printings in ninety days and is being called, "the Think and Grow Rich of the 21st Century.

His audiences suggest he is Tony Robbin-Brian Tracy-Tom Hopkins-Dave Ramsey all in one.

Grant Cardone currently resides in Los Angeles with his wife, actress Elena Lyons in the Hollywood Hills, which was recently featured on KNBC TV's Open House LA: Open House LA Video.
