Zander Conway’s Summary

Since 2000 I have been a SAP consultant in SEM-BCS, EC-CS and BW, I am also a Chartered Management Accountant (ACMA). In 5 years at Deloitte Consulting I played a significant role in the successful delivery of four major national and international SAP projects. After a brief spell at Axon I became an independent contractor, my consolidation skills and experience are currently in high demand. After several years in London, UK I am now based in Melbourne, Australia.

I have full experience of design, configuration, training and testing of SEM-BCS & EC-CS on several varied projects. I also have a global network of experienced friends and ex-colleagues with whom I share knowledge in the Consolidation and Financial Reporting space.

I have full config. experience in BW administration workbench and reporting (BEx and Web).

I am an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants, UK (CIMA).

SAP BW (Business Information Warehouse, BI) configuration, testing, training and documentation in both the administrator workbench and BEx/Web reporting.
