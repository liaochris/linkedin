Robert Leach’s Summary

My experience in team and project management totals to 4 of my 6 years in the games industry. Those years have been neatly split between high-end console development, mobile development, web development, and handheld devices development.

I've worked on and helped to complete over 30 projects. I have extensive experience in sports-related and web-based media, and my extensive creative background lends to my ability to understand and assist art direction.

In my own time I love producing and directing plays and short films. I'm a writer and musician on the side, which keeps my creativity sharp at all times. I also enjoy dabbling in improvisational acting.

My goals are to stay the course, become a more experienced and more rounded project manager. I'm currently pursuing my PMP certification and will likely obtain a second degree, aimed at Project Management, as well.
