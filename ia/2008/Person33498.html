Charity Hisle’s Summary

Charity has played many roles within the past 5 years. From typesetter to accounting, customer service and data entry to site developement and management. Currently she is the E-Commerce Director and Catalog Manager for Benson Media, Inc.

Charity initiated one of the first corporate printing E-Commerce Departments dedicated to planning, developing, integrating and implementing B2B user accounts. Previously, corporate printers utilized CSR's or I.T. managers rather than an infrastructure dedicated to e-commerce. She supervises this new department which includes 20+ online shopping carts and printed catalogs for B2B accounts.

One of Charity's large responsibilities is to oversee all user permissions, customer contact data entry, and B2B account purchase reporting for 3,000 + customers and 2,000 + products online. Additionally, she manages product and vendor research, organization, pricing standards, and product implementation for all B2B online accounts.

Charity serves as a leader in the corporate printing industry by volunteering to Chair the Southeastern Regional EFI/PSI Group, bringing other users together to network and review current product technology for enhancement. She also serves as a volunteer consultant in the corporate printing industry by participating as a Web Council member for EFI/Websites Group, adding valuable input and feedback to enhance current user interfaces.

Recently, Charity has accepted responsibility for research and implementation of social media and the value of assisted MLS options and their potential effects on apartment marketing.

As the E-Commerce Director and Catalog Manager, Charity is responsible for researching and implementing new technology to enhance new and current customer shopping experiences and provide streamlined order processing workflow from start to finish. Charity is always looking "out-of-the-box" for solutions that will balance technology and the end-user experience for Benson Media, Inc.
