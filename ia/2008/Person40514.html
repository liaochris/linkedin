Peter Clough’s Summary

Internet advertising expertise in the creation, design, planning and placement of online advertising as well as selling online advertising. I started my internet marketing business in 1996, prior to that I was a broadcaster, working in all aspects of radio for 16 years.

I am a "creative mutant" as my screenwriting friend Craig likes to say. While my income has come from sales in one sort or another for years, my intersts and skills include photography (digital and film), blogging and podcasting, commercial voice-overs, comedy writing, advertising copy writing, developing sales presentations, developing sales training materials and creative cooking.

Peter Clough’s Specialties:

Expertise in online advertising from both the marketing side and the media side. I have worked with many large online direct marketers to develop their campaigns from conception to ROI analysis. In addition I have represented some of the internet's most popular destinations, including jibjab.com as a sales representative.
