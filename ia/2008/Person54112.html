David Posada Quevedo’s Summary

5 years ago David Posada began working for Tribal Colombia as operational director. Soon, he was working with online advertising and positioning himself as one of its pioneers in Colombia. After having multiple positions at Tribal, including the direction of a new local office and the direction of the regional digital media unit, in October 2006, David became the managing director of the company, facing the challenge of developing interactive media as a fundamental strategy to reach its client's brands.

David is involved in online advertising industry associations such as the IAB Colombia, where he is member of the board. He also speaks to universities about e-marketing and online advertising industry in the country.

Finally, David has consistently exceeded sales goals and customer service expectations and has experience in handling international clients.

David Posada Quevedo’s Specialties:

Online media business, interactive agency managing and direction, e-marketing, e-advertising, digital media strategy, planning and buying.
