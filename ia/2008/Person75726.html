Heather Moukalian’s Summary

My career has been characterized by my ability to work well with diverse teams ranging from the world of animal welfare (shelter clients to large-scale donors) to the construction industry (subcontractors to architects). Over the past 10 years I've been involved in all aspects of administrative work as well as more challenging arenas like event planning/coordiation, volunteer recruitment/ training and community relations.

To sum up the very common “tell me about yourself” question – this is it. I am a team player who believes there is an opportunity to learn something from everyone. I characterize myself as a very detail-oriented, enthusiastic, dependable professional with excellent communication skills. To be challenged is critical – it keeps the mind sharp and allows for innovative thinking.
