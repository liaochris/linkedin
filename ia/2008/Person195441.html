Phillip S. Patitsas’s Summary

Looking for that rare blend of sales savvy and entrepreneurial genius to energize your business?

Velocity Sales Consulting (VSC) is the elite sales acceleration company specializing in the delivery of rapid sales growth. Phillip has been at the helm since 2002.

A talented Entrepreneur, he is an MBA/Engineer/Sales practitioner with more than 20 years of Fortune 200 business experience. He inspires people to reach their dreams, exceed their goals, and achieve results in record time.

The VSC team discerns and deciphers sales obstacles, and sidesteps, transforms, or eliminates them by injecting the right strategies, people, systems and processes to get things moving again.

VSC is process driven. VSC methodically builds proven, repeatable, scalable sales processes for its clients.

VSC consistently adds value using a patented 4 step process to diagnose, prescribe, and execute sales.

Phillip’s achievements include growing sales at several businesses in excess of $500 million; numerous business launches, rescues and turnarounds; technology commercialization; high risk capital recruitment (>$70 Million); business integrations, corporate acquisitions, and a portfolio of long term power contracts valued at more than $8 billion to his name.

He's listed among Who's Who Top Executives, and has completed leadership programs at Wharton and U. of Chicago in the art of “Commercializing New Products and Technologies”, and “The Strategy & Tactics of Pricing”, respectively.

VSC invites you to register for Rainmaker Newsletter at www.velocitysalesconsulting.com to receive Phillip’s winning fast growth ideas.

When not helping clients, Phillip enjoys spending time with his beautiful family, hiking America's National Parks, and an eclectic mix of Opera, Classical and Modern music.

A firm believer in the “Pay it forward” principal, Phillip is a Board Member for a leading Dallas private school academy, and serves as its Marketing Committee Chairman.
