Philippe Loiseau’s Summary

Thinking to my 3 girls, I am convinced that our mission is to create a good future for the next generations. With this background spirit, I try to bring my contribution for this goal by being a Research & Development technical engineer and manager for more than 20 years.

I worked mainly in modelisation, simulation, software developments to improve the safety, the security, the comfort of people while keeping in mind the challenge for the future : To better protect our environment.

It includes the developments of less consuming, less polluting and safer systems for energy saving, alternative energies finding and optimisation for intelligent transports (safe, efficient, environment kindly and comfortable).

Philippe Loiseau’s Specialties:

- Physical phenomena modeling (Thermal transfers, Mass transfers, Fluids mechanics, Electromagnetics, Eltrasonics, Radio wave propogation)
- Software development (Fortran, C, Matlab, Scilab, Basile)
- Sensors (Ultrasonic, Electrostatic)
- Signal processing (Signal conditioning, Adaptive filtering, Demodulation, Noise reduction, Interference rejection, Source coding, Channel coding, Antenna diversity processing, MIMO systems)
