Winn Maddrey’s Summary

I have more than seventeen years experience in communications, especially in the public relations, public affairs and marketing area. For the past eleven years I have both sold the projects and served as a strategist, working with a team to ensure that the project was successfully delivered. Additionally I have advised on business and policy issues for the past seventeen years and worked on a multitude of different issues involving sensitive stakeholder issues. I have experience serving in interim roles, turnaround, restructuring, distressed, venture-backed and/or startup scenarios.

Specific skills include speechwriting, crisis communications and management, public affairs, advocacy, lobbying, grassroots outreach, grasstops outreach, bond referenda, civic or community engagement, public relations, marketing, strategy, consulting, consensus-building
