Subramaniam Vincent’s Summary

* Co-founder of Oorvani Media, Bangalore, publisher of Citizen Matters (citizenmatters.in) a city news venture.

* Analysis and systems inputs on telecommunications technologies and markets, Internet publishing (e-journalism), and e-governance (citizens interface). Co-author of Tonse Telecom's "The Future for Wi-Fi in India", a February 2007 industry report, commissioned by the Wi-Fi Alliance.

* Networking industry professional with 9 years experience in deployment, embedded software product development, research (internet protocols), and testing. Held positions ranging senior design engineer to project leader. Co-author of an Internet RFC and an internet draft.

* Co-founder and Editor/Publisher of online magazine India Together (www.indiatogether.org). India Together is an organisation structured around working with development experts and talented freelance journalists to do in-depth reporting and commentary on Indian public affairs. (Writers won three major awards in 2006).

* Co-Author with Ashwin Mahesh of a chapter "Journalism: The practice and the potential" in a 2007 Sage book on "21st Century Journalism", edited by Nalini Rajan, Professor, Asian College of Journalism.

* Author of chapter on "Community Radio" in 2006 Rupa & Co book, "Indian Media: Illusions, Delusions and Reality".

* Writer, interviewer and radio producer.
See: www.indiatogether.org/opinions/svincent/

* Regularly advise organisations in the non-profit/development sphere on internet, computer technology and web based communications.
