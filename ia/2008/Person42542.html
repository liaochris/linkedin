Dalton Hooper’s Summary

Dalton Hooper is currently a full-time paid speaker, consultant, and owner of Words and Wit, LLC.

Dalton delivers presentations, keynote and otherwise, in one of three categories: Self-Marketing, Leadership, and Presentation skills.

For more information, please vist www.WordsAndWit.com.

Dalton Hooper’s Specialties:

Mr. Hooper has an impressive range of speaking engagements which include technical, as well as leadership presentations. Mr. Hooper's specialty is delivering presentations dealing with the hiring process, both from the candidate's and the hiring manager's perspectives. Mr. Hooper has been the keynote speaker around the country at numerous local chapters of the Society for Technical Communication, as well as other venues.
