Marc De Geus’s Summary

Creative Director and Interaction Designer for small and middle size business agencies.

Marc De Geus’s Specialties:

Functionally designing interfaces using a sketchbook, pencil and Omnigraffle.

Visually designing interfaces using a sketchbook, pencil, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Flash.

Realising interfaces using W3C compliant XHTML with CSS and JS in Coda.
