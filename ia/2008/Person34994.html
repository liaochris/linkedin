Chris Bura’s Summary

Prior to starting Videre Ventures, founded and served as CEO of Alldomains.com and NetCorps - grew it from a few thousand of personal financing in 1997 to ~4M in revenue before being acquired by Hostcentric, Inc. that was then acquired in 2003 by Interland Inc. (NASDAQ: INLD).

From March 2000 to March 2002, served as co-founder and President, Domain Registration Division for Hostcentric. In March of 2002 negotiated and personally financed the buyback of Alldomains from Hostcentric.

As CEO again of Alldomains.com, solidified Allldomains.com’s position as leading corporate domain registration and brand protection company. Among its accomplishments, Alldomains.com was voted Entrepreneur Magazine’s “Best 100 Websites” both years the competition ran, acknowledged as Yahoo!’s “Useful Site of the Day” and won contracts with many of the best known companies in the world including AMD, Boeing, eBay, GM, Google, UBS, Time Warner and Yahoo!.

In October of 2003 Alldomains.com was acquired by MarkMonitor. From October 2003 until February of 2005 served as General Manager for MarkMonitor.

In February of 2005 left MarkMonitor to create Videre Ventures, an angel investment company focusing on technology startups and remain a large shareholder in MarkMonitor.

Prior to Alldomains.com served as Global Director of Sales for Walnut Creek CDROM a company that pioneered the distribution of open source software including FreeBSD and Linux.
