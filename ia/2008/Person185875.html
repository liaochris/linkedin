Tawfiq Alkilani’s Summary

Dr. Tawfiq Alkilani, received a doctor of dental surgery degree with highest honors from The University of the Pacific, Arthur A. Dugoni School of Dentistry. Dr. Alkilani ws named valedictorian of the school’s International Dental Studies class for achieving the highest overall grade point average in didactic and clinical courses during the two-year program for foreign-trained dentists.

Dr. Alkilani received a bachelor’s degree in dental surgery from University of Jordan, School of Dentistry in Amman, Jordan in 1998 and has more than 4 years of clinical experience prior to entering the IDS program at Pacific.

Dr. Alkilani has received numerous accolades for his academic accomplishments, including being inducted into Omicron Kappa Upsilon and Tau Kappa Omega dental honor societies. Dr. Alkilani also received the Dean’s Valedictorian Award, the F. Gene and Rosemary Dixon IDS Endowment Award and Herbert K. Yee Scholarship Award.

During his time at Pacific, Dr. Alkilani was active in student government, including serving as president of his class and a student representative at the California Dental Association.
