Christian Warden’s Summary

I've been using Linux since 1995, and working as a systems administrator and web developer since 1997. I work almost exclusively with open source technology, including Apache and Lighttpd, MySQL, PHP and shell scripting, Subversion and CVS, Exim and Dovecot. I have also worked with Solaris, AIX, and QNX in the past, but find Linux systems provide the most pleasant and productive working environment.

On the networking side, I've worked with equipment from Cisco, Enterasys, and Alteon for routing and load balancing using HSRP and VRRP. I've also designed high-availability, load-balanced solutions using Linux Virtual Server and supporting tools such as heartbeat and ldirectord.

I also recently returned to school to study economics part-time, and am a fan of the Austrian School.
