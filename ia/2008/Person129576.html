Michael Derrer’s Summary

Michael Derrer is founder and CEO of Ascent Swiss Business Management AG, which assists Swiss companies in their expansion to East and Central European countries.

Before he has been working for 12 years as a project manager, translator and consultant specialized on East and Central Europe and the CIS.

Michael Derrer studied political and social sciences at the Universities of Basle, Lausanne and Geneva and obtained a Master's degree in Public Policy Management at the University of Geneva. He also obtained the Diploma of Advanced Business Russian at Moscow State University.

He speaks German, Russian, French, English, Romanian, Italian, Polish, Ukrainian, Czech, Bulgarian, Serbo-Croatian, Spanish.

Michael Derrer’s Specialties:

Investment, export and outsourcing to Eastern European countries. Market analysis. Personnel recruitment. Consultancy. Translation and interpreting (12 languages).
