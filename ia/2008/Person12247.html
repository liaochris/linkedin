Andy Coote ’s Summary

A Friend in Every City, a book on Networking written with Thomas Power and Penny Power (founders of Ecademy), was launched in London on 18th May, 2006.

The book is published by Ecademy Press, of which I am a founder with Mindy Gibbins-Klein. We are actively building the list of books available which include - at time of writing - The Personal Brandwagon by Tessa Hood, Magical Sense by Sue Chambers and A Girlfriend in Every City by Geert Conard.

Our books are available through Amazon.com, Amazon.co.uk and Amazon.de and through our own site at http://www.ecademy-press.com.

My Background
10 years of retail experience with B&Q and Texas Homecare in the UK in a variety of IT and General Management Positions followed by 8 years of selling software and providing consultancy services to my customers. In 1998, I went freelance, initially as a trainer and consultant in e-business and, following a post grad Diploma course, as a writer.

I have had a number of articles accepted and published by SC Magazine in the UK/US on Information Security topics.

Areas of interest include IT, eBusiness, Business Performance Management, Knowledge Management, Information Security, use of e-technologies by the Finance Department as well as personal development, NLP, coaching etc.

Interested in meeting editors to discuss freelance opportunities in print and new media and with commissioners in industry to discuss copywriting asignments.
