    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    Heading up the New Zealand subsidiary for the Kaizen Institute (KI). KAIZEN Institute (KI) is the premier Lean / Continuous Improvement consultancy with more than 200 consultants in more than 30 offices around the world.

    Our focus is in the fields of Lean Enterprise Development, Organizational Culture Change, People Development. Kaizen and Lean Manufacturing. Together with you we redevelop your existing system to become a world class system by:
    1. Examining and analysing your value flow for losses and waste (MUDA) and recommend solutions for improvement.
    2. Helping you to develop a relevant benchmarking system and to introduce it on site in a way suited to your staff. (Visual Management)
    3. Facilitating Improvement Workshops to increase the acceptance for the change and improvement process, (e.g. optimisation of tooling procedures, 5S campaigns, Kobetsu KAIZEN measures, autonomous maintenance etc.)
