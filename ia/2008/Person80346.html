Edward de Leau (e@leau.net)’s Summary

I am a Senior Consultant within IBM AS Consulting Services. I advise on services and solutions within leading companies and organizations in several sectors and industries.

- I advice customers on improvement of products, services and processes.
- I propose improvement solutions, plans and/or products for the customer based on business ideas and/or plans and translate business plans to IT requirements.
- I investigate organizational processes and provide ideas and solutions for improvement.
- I advice and help gain the full business benefits out of improvement streams within areas such as governance, compliance, process improvement, change management, requirements management and configuration management and development factories.
- I coordinate solution directions within short and long term business directions and lead improvement projects within the defined business strategy, architecture and roadmaps.
- During my career I gained experience in the full project lifecycle in a wide range of advisory and operational roles.
- I am able to improve and facilitate processes within diverse industries and areas such as CRM, EAI, E-CRM, custom development and Portals projects.

From 1999 to 2006 I was part of the AIS group within PWC Consulting and later IBM BCS as a senior consultant / IT architect.

Starting from 2006 I will focus mainly on Rational solutions and products within the newly formed IBM BCS AS Rational Consulting Group.
