Judi Kennedy-Clarke’s Summary

An innovative and adaptable consultant, coach and change agent with substantial multi-sector experience.

Have worked up to and including HR Director level in the private (financial sector, manufacturing sector, retail distribution) and public sectors (local and national government and the NHS) - and been a freelance consultant, trainer and coach.

Passionate about personal and organisational development matters, ensuring those aspects of organisational culture that make having to work more enjoyable, are present and correct!

Expertise in enabling Leadership and Organisational Development in response to business challenges such as diversification, growth and down-sizing. .

A Chartered Fellow of the CIPD - have worked to improve the credibility of HR professionals by being a board member of several CIPD branches and the London HR Connection
