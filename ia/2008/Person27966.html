Bridget Ginley’s Summary

Independent artist and curator. My professional expertise in preparation of art includes framing, matting and installation of the work. I can repair and restore artwork on canvas, wall murals and damaged works on paper. Assisted in curating / coordinating several successful art auctions for charity. Currently, I install art in a small exhibition space located in Neighborhood Housing Services called Art in the Village - 5700 Gallery. I curate only local Cleveland visual artists on a rotating basis.

Painter, printmaker and collage artist creating original artwork available for commercial, private or public collections. Specialized ceramics, paper making / handmade cards and mural commissions are available upon request or proposal. Artist is available for curatorial consultation on installation or collecting fine art.

Bridget Ginley’s Specialties:

creative management, college recruiter, digital imaging, internet networking, fund raising, gallery sales & management, quick art installation, hand crafted designs, affordable framing and public relations.

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Arts and Crafts industry)

    May 2002 — July 2005 (3 years 3 months)

    Founded and organized Buzz Gallery, a co-op of artists from the Cleveland area. Acquired space in the Old Arcade and recruited other art spaces such a s Groop, Gallery U and Lemur Gallery.
