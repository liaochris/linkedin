In the words of Bruce Lee, I use 'no way as way',
and in the words of myself, I use no order as order.

should never be allowed in the same room as the word

Ney Pimentel Ney@BrandAnarchyGroup.com’s Specialties:

There's no greater feeling, career-wise, than the company-wide harmonization of its image. Of course there are many important aspects of branding, but none are more important to me than designing the perfect logo.

At the risk of sounding shallow, when I design a logo that's right for that particular project, I compare it to a woman conceiving a child, and branding is the next step, as a woman would nurture that child into being the best he/she can possibly be.
