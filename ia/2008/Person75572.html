Rev. Anita McKenzie’s Summary

Coming from a unique spiritually diverse life background, (Catholic Christian, Hindu and African Caribbean), Anita has developed her gifts and skills in pastoral care, healing, preaching, diversity work, group facilitation, event planning, and programme administration.

Anita runs Healing Image Therapies, a private practice working with other therapists to offer a range of non-medical, psychological therapies: Stress Management, Emotional Freedom Technique and Clinical and Cognitive Behavioural Hypnotherapy, Mediation training and Interfaith Spiritual Counselling for life-enhancement, well being and spiritual and personal growth.

Before entering professional ministry, Anita worked in creative industries as an arts educational facilitator, using photography; additionally running a successful picture archive, collaborating on numerous cultural history projects and exhibition resources with many national heritage institutions in the UK.

In addition, Anita also specialises in cross-cultural themes for Interfaith ceremonies and cultural workshops; she has been developing specific approaches to African-centred ceremonies and spiritual themes, and has initiated an 'African Interfaith' group on Facebook. Anita also brings many cultural insights and experiences into her role as an holistic practitioner.
