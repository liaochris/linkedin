Kent Ramquist’s Summary

An Executive Aviation Leader with twenty-eight years experience in the aviation industry and eighteen years leading, managing, and developing aviation professionals. Proven creative and deliberate leadership skills - leading organizations through transition and change; creating cohesive leadership teams; and nurturing key individuals through coaching and mentoring. Experienced with business planning and process development. Adept at crafting solutions through independent and collaborative thinking. Passionate about uncompromising customer service and satisfaction. Broad range of functional/technical experience within the aviation field, including personnel and organizational development; process management; safety and standards; training; P & L responsibility; aircraft and facility security; aircraft purchase and sale; contract development and negotiation.

Kent Ramquist’s Specialties:

Creative leadership for organizational change. Empowering team-building. Proficient in the Birkman Profile. Empathetic coaching and mentoring skills. Highly motivated toward excellence. Practical experience with process improvement, root cause principles, and key metric management. Certified with Birkman International as a Certified Consultant. Certified with National Business Aviation Association as a Certified Aviation Manager (CAM). MBA from Cardinal Stritch University.
