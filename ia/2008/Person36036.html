Christopher Thierry’s Summary

Mr. Thierry is the dynamic business architect and founder of Brand2Media and parent company ETelesolv.com Inc. He has spent the last 10 years developing strategic web projects for major enterprise customers such as Motorola, Bell Aliant, DraftFCB, Canon, Ingram Micro Logistics, Loblaws, Yahoo!, Tim Hortons and RBC Financial group.

With experience in e-commerce, supply chain integration, logistics, accounting, marketing, electronic billing, and telecom, Mr. Thierry has a unique blend of business savvy and technological know how helps companies understand the practical potential of enterprise web applications.

Mr. Thierry holds a Bachelor degree in Management Information Systems and Marketing from John Molson School of Business at Concordia University and is a member of Entrepreneurs Organization (EO) and the Retail Council of Canada.
