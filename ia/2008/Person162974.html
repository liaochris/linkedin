Rob Machin’s Summary

Rob has worked within the financial services industry for 14 years and is now an Executive Director at UBS Investment Bank.

He specializes in assisting companies within the banking and finance sector with large programmes of technology renewal. Rob has had a particular focus on payments and financial messaging since the late 90s, and has been engaged in payments and financial messaging systems specification, architecture and implementation at organisations across the UK financial sector.

Rob has worked with Object-Technology since the early 90s, in Smalltalk and Java, and is well known in the industry as both an author and speaker on payments and technology.

Rob is a member of both the British Computer Society (MBCS) and the International Association of Software Architects (IASA), and is one of only 100 architects worldwide to achieve the Microsoft Certified Architect (MCA) certification.
