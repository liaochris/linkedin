Patrick Fitzgerald’s Summary

I have a very diverse background. My career has included telecommunications and IT technologies and I have worked on fiber, video, satellites, microwaves, windows, apple and much more.

In my various roles I have been system designer, customer support, installer, quotations, training, order processing and project manager. This work has provided me the opportunity to travel the US and Canada and overseas to Bosnia and Haiti.

Recently arrived in Vancouver, BC, Canada from Montreal to contribute to construction of the Canada Line transit link to the Vancouver Airport. Employed by Invensys PLC / Westinghouse Rail Systems Limited (WRSL).
