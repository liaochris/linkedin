    Magic Touch Events

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Events Services industry)

    October 2005 — Present (3 years)

    n 2005, I founded Magic Touch, one of the oldest event planning
    services in the virtual world of Second Life. We create events for
    individuals and for business interests in virtual world space that
    may or may not incorporate real world events (piping in audio and
    video from a musician or conference, for example).


    We coordinate events for business, private parties, romantic
    rendezvous and commitment ceremonies.

    As more events began to incorporate and coordinate with the real
    world, it became clear that this was not a separate function, but an
    extension of my real life event planning.

    Now the extension is working both ways -- we are taking the next step
    of bringing virtual entertainment into our real world, so that a band
    or an artist can use second life art and animation as a multimedia
    element of their performance.
