Annemieke Voorn’s Summary

For sixteen years I work in a field with children, youngsters and adults which are in position of pupil or student. I have experienced these groups and individuals in a wide range of activities. Acitivities like a summercamp, youth society, kindergarten, afterschoolcare, preschool, primary school, secundary school. Next to that I coached students during their traineeship.

Coaching is what I like to do best and I found out I am quite good at it. I have experienced coaching of students during their traineeship. And I also coached a colleague who was going back to college during her first year of study (HBO-SPH).

What I love to do is coaching people to discover what they are capable of in a way which can be called their own way.

Annemieke Voorn’s Specialties:

Coaching students during their traineeship. Let them find their talents and needs by using communication on a level of true interest. In this way the student can find his/her own intrinsic motivation of being and working here.

Observing children who seem to be different and finding out what their needs are. Observing the behaviour and expressions of the children. Connecting with colleagues ,and when needed specialists, for giving eachother feedback on this.
