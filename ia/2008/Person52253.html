Dan Rubin’s Summary

Senior leader with successful track record in leading organizations through change. Background and training is in technology.

- Started career with EDS and progressed through the organization in a number of roles from Systems Engineer to Account Manager and Sales Support. Led technology initiatives and conducted due diligence for acquistions by clients, and led all merger activities for a large bank.

- Left EDS to assume management role at a national retailer which was then acquired by a large competitor. Led the IT function through the merger, then assumed CIO role for the acquiring company. As CIO, consolidated 5 IT organizatons and multiple data centers into one lean, effective organization. Member of senior executive team that managed the company through further acquisitions and a LBO by a financial firm. The LBO resulted in chapter 11 filings several years later, and managed the IT function through those turbulant times.

- Recruited by Hewitt Associates, the national leader in HR Consulting and Administration, to run the IT infrastructure that delivers HR services to nearly 20 Million employees of client companies. Invited to join the "owners" of this privately owned firm.

- After five years of driving change to mature the IT organization, moved to a consulting role where I led a team that assisted clients in developing HR Technology and Delivery strategy. Moved to the Federal Market team to open up a new business area for Hewitt. Retired from Hewitt in November 2007 when Hewitt decided to curtail further investment in the market.

- Looking to continue using my depth of experience and client relationship skills to help improve the delivery of technology to the agencies of the federal government and commercial enterprises. I especially enjoy leading IT organizations through times of transition and transformation, and am a very effective mentor to other IT leaders. I have deep expertise in leading HR Delivery through technology.

Dan Rubin’s Specialties:

CIO at two firms, over thirty years in utilizing technology as a tool to deliver business results, and aligning IT with business functions. Specialist in leading technology organizations in times of massive change, outsourcing, disruption, downsizing, mergers, etc. Last 13 years have been spent delivering HR solutions to clients through administrative outsourcing and consulting.
