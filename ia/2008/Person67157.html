Gaja Krishna Vaidyanatha’s Summary

Gaja Krishna Vaidyanatha has more than fifteen years of industry technical expertise working with Oracle systems, with over eleven years in engaging in real-life performance management. He is the Principal at DBPerfMan LLC, a specialty performance consulting firm that focuses on optimizing Oracle systems.

In the recent years, he has worked in a variety of product management roles providing strategic and technical direction to application performance and storage management solutions for companies such as Veritas Corporation, Oracle Corporation and Quest Software. Gaja also served as a Technical Manager at Accenture LLP, specializing in Oracle Systems Performance Management.

He is the primary author of the “Oracle Performance Tuning 101” published by Oracle Press. Gaja is also one of the co-authors of the book "Oracle Insights : Tales of the Oak Table", published by Apress LP.

He holds a Masters Degree in Computer Science from Bowling Green State University, Ohio. He has presented many papers at various regional, national and international Oracle conferences and has served as faculty of the IOUG-A Master Class University. Gaja is a member of the OakTable Network.
