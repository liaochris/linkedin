Darren Gladstone’s Summary

I need to update this at some point when I'm not running around. BUT, I can tell you right now that at PC World, I'm keeping watch over the notebook, component, and gaming beats.

Until the time when I actually do get a chance to work my magic on this summary of my life, I'll stick with what I was doing at my most recent gig. Because really, the only thing's that changes is what happens between 9am and 5pm. Everything else is pretty status quo. ;p


The 9-to-5 gig: Serving at Games for Windows: The Official Magazine (just rolls off the tongue, don't it?)--you might remember it as Computer Gaming World. I rustle up the cover stories and dork out in the tech section of the magazine.

The 5-to-9 gig: Writing about geekery and gadgetry in its myriad forms. While most of my experience revolves around covering the games industry, previous jobs have exposed me to other fields where I have become a regular observer, writer and analyst. I apparently don't get to sleep much. Just ask my wife.

The 9-to-midnight gig: You really don't want to know.

My goal, as cliche as it may seem, is to keep creating something unique. Or at least write differently and see what I can get away with.

Darren Gladstone’s Specialties:

Videogame industry analysis(Console and PC), hardware reviews and analysis/consulting, home Theater reviewer, mobile technology reviewer, consumer electronics reviewer, media critic of sorts (movies, music, books), news & technology trends analysis
