J. Daniel Velez’s Summary

Along with my Puerto Rican culture and Cuban heritage, my ability to speak Spanish and previous work experiences, my experience thus far at HU Law has afforded me the opportunity of meeting several attorneys and judges that are very active in their communities. Upon graduation from Hofstra University School of Law in 2009, along with being active in the Latino Law Students Alumni Association, Puerto Rican Bar Association and Latino Lawyers Association, I plan to teach a number of classes and become actively involved in mentoring programs with students in inner city schools. I come from a family of educators and as a product of the public school system, I expect to contribute to its preservation by serving in my capacity as a mentor to the future attorneys of America and the world at large.
