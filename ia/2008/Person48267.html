Danilo Bordini’s Summary

I've been working on IT career since 1996, in the last years getting specialized on high availability solutions for IT infrastructure and Internet solutions for enterprise customers. I've been taken part on several network projects, including operating systems and directory services migration and implementation, webfarms implementations, virtualization projects, security analysis, interoperability, etc.

Due my job, I am a community guy, in touch every minute with IT community using online (blogs, webcasts, foruns, user groups, writing technical articles) and offline (technical events, round tables, customer meetings) channels.
