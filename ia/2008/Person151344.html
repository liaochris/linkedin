Phil Savage’s Summary

I have 15+ years experience in the information technology industry ranging from the highly technical aspects to the sales components. My core competencies include design and implementation of Wintel (Windows on Intel Technology) platforms, (Server and Workstation) systems, processes and automation tools and utilities.

I have an excellent ability to manage any Windows environment from the workstation up.

I pride myself of my ability to think innovatively when searching for solutions to problems – I also have a tenacious attitude when presented with troubleshooting issues.

I pride myself on my network of expert peers regarding infrastructure management, enabling me to continuously learn and share expertise. I always believe there is a better way an outcome can be achieved and will search for the best way to achieve the goal.
