Spencer Waldron’s Summary

3Sixty Global Marketing
Fresh Perspective. Focus on Results.

3Sixty is a Marketing Agency for businesses requiring an outsourced marketing department for branding, product marketing, online marketing, marketing communications and new market penetration services.

The company is highly experienced in managing the strategic planning process as well as the implementation and development of fresh ideas, resulting in promotional materials, concepts and campaigns that are more than visually appealing, and will bring you business.

3Sixty differentiates itself from other agencies by understanding that marketing activities should not derive from design or content creation; they should be the result of good strategic planning, a fresh perspective and a high level of focus that commands results. 3Sixty is a company that knows its business and how to provide its customers with the competitive edge they are looking for.

Being highly experienced in the marketing arena and after working over the years for multiple companies across various markets, we believe that the knowledge base gained has put 3Sixty in good stead to provide a wide range of value-added marketing services

3Sixty is passionate about bringing a fresh new perspective to all areas of marketing, motivated by the strong desire to provide greater visibility and growth of revenue for our clients, based on focused results.

For further information please contact: spencer@3sixty.co.il
