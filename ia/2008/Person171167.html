Scott McArthur’s Summary

I have 10 years experience at Board and Management Team level, followed by 3 years as an Executive Consultant and development specialist with Atos Consulting (formally Atos KPMG).

I have delivered and facilitated 100's of leadership and learning events in the UK, Europe, Canada and in the USA.

In the media, I frequently contribute articles, comments and letters to leading magazines, newspapers and blogs. In 2007 I have been quoted in The Times, Financial Times, Personnel Today, Success Magazine and People Management. I am a book reviewer for Personnel Today and founding member of the editorial board and chair of the learning and development forum on HR circles.

Specialist interests include, whole brain learning technologies, peak performance, life and executive coaching, self confidence, motivation, persuasion and the application of new technologies to change and personal development issues (presentation techniques, social networks and online technologies).

Scott McArthur’s Specialties:

HR strategy, change management, communications, board and leadership development, personal development, executive coaching, L&D, facilitation, welfare, occupational health, employee relations, training, pubic speaking, organisational design, reward & performance management, talent management, selection & organisational psychology.
