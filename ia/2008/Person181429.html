Steve Hopper’s Summary

Stephen T. Hopper, P.E. is a results-oriented supply chain professional with a strong record of achievement in consultative sales, sales management, business development, and management consulting. Steve's strengths include an extensive personal network of thousands of industry contacts and the ability to develop and sell complex solutions that transform logistics and distribution operations and deliver measurable value, improving both the top and bottom lines. Steve earned his Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering (BIE) from the Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech) and is a registered Professional Engineer (P.E.) in Georgia.

To see a recent Logistics Online interview with Steve, see this link:

http://www.supplychainmarket.com/download.mvc/QA-Strategies-For-Improving-Labor-Productivit-0001

Outside his supply chain career, Steve is also an active investor in real estate. He and his wife, Lisa, buy single-family and multi-family homes. While leasing them to tenants, they hold the properties as investments for the long term. Steve and Lisa are always interested in buying properties that meet their investment criteria.

Steve, Lisa, and their two children live in the northeastern suburbs of Atlanta, Georgia.

Steve Hopper’s Specialties:

Sales management, business development, marketing, and direct sales of supply chain system solutions (e.g., WMS, TMS, LMS & productivity management, slotting, auto ID, RFID, parcel shipping, supply chain visibility, sales CRM solutions) and automated material handling systems; Strategic and tactical industrial engineering for warehousing, distribution & logistics; Analysis, design, specification & selection of supply chain information systems and integrated material handling systems.
