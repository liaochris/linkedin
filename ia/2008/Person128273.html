Bas Melssen’s Summary

In a consulting capacity, I have been involved in the different phases of the brand building process, from strategy development through to conceptual implementation. A proactive approach to developing new business opportunities has given me access to different industries and fields of expertise such as the petroleum, retail, IT, financial services, FMCG, entertainment, and travel sectors. Prior to joining Munchy’s, I headed the Malaysian office of global brand consultancy FutureBrand and worked on branding exercises for Guinness Anchor Berhad, Munchy’s, E&O Properties, Petrosains, Hong Leong Bank, HLA, Sapura and Multimedia Super Corridor amongst others.

Other clients have included: Magnum 4D, BP Malaysia, Bangsar Shopping Centre, The Star Newspaper, ViewPoint Software for Business, Philips, TV12 Singapore, Dutch Ministry of Defense, NedCar (Volvo/Mitsubishi), OAD Travel BV and others.

Bas Melssen’s Specialties:

With 10 years’ in the region in both client and consultant roles, experienced in the fields of brand strategy, brand communications, project management, design, advertising, IT and multimedia. Also spent 2 years in the video production and broadcasting industry in the BENELUX in the capacity of assistant producer/director.
