Brian Langenberg, CFA’s Summary

Experienced asset manager and analyst utilizing a "best use of cash" approach to value investing. Top five positions generally 30-50% of assets for concentrated approach but asset allocation, portfolio construction, and risk profile are determined by client needs.

We lever 20 years of portfolio management and analytical success by studying earnings power, management, control value and embedded expectations in security prices.

Focus is managing assets for CEO's, thought leaders and executives with at least $1M of liquid, investable assets, as well as research for a select list of institutional clients.
