RenaMarie Villano’s Summary

RenaMarie Villano sounds like the open road. With an uncanny coolness, she transforms everyday scripts into velvet smooth persuasion - clean and timeless. Her voice versatility ranges from the kind of lovely you could take home to mama, to that quintessential smoky sexiness you crave. Her VO credits include promos for NBC, documentaries for OLN and PBS, narrations for Apple, Citibank, eBay Live, HP, Microsoft, Motorola, Nautilus, SunCom Wireless and Washington Group Int'l — national commercials for Wal-Mart, Bowflex, Aegis Living, Inova Health System and Umpqua Bank, and regional campaigns for Chevron, Jackson's Food Stores and Moxie Java — to name a few.

She's making her mark on radio...one legendary station at a time. She's heard on Heritage Alternative, Rock, Kickin' Country, Smooth AC, Rhythmic CHR and Talk radio stations coast to coast. Q101 in Chicago. 99X in Atlanta. WAAF in Boston. FM 102/1 in Milwaukee. Y101 in Richmond. These forward-thinking radio stations chose to work with RenaMarie when they were looking to re-invent their identities and forge new ground with a strong female voice presence that's about much more than spice or simple sex appeal.

In her broadcast-quality home studio she records with a Rode NT1000 mic in a WhisperRoom sound booth using a Focusrite Platinum Trakmaster Pro mic preamp, a new 24" iMac, an MBox2 Pro, Pro Tools LE, Audio Technica phones, Event Electronics TR5 monitors and Mogami cables. She runs her phone patches through a Telos ONE and uses a Zephyr XStream for her ISDN sessions.

RenaMarie Villano's voicework is looked after by the best, coast to coast. She is represented by SBV in Los Angeles, IN BOTH EARS in Portland and CESD in New York. She is managed by Celia Siegel Management.
