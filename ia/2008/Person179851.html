Joel Libava’s Summary

About Joel Libava/Franchise Selection Specialists Inc.

Since 1990, The Libava family has been helping future entrepreneurs with their dreams of business ownership.

We will try to lower your risk with our personality assessment tools, our knowledge of the Northern Ohio area, and our years of real world franchise industry experience. If you decide to work with us, know that you will be treated professionally, and will not be sold a thing. We will however, present you with some dynamic opportunities in franchise/business ownership, and give you the tools to research them..safely, so you can make a fact based decision.

Joel Libava, Franchise Selection Specialists Inc. 2nd generation President, brings real world franchise industry experience to his clients, coming from a franchise management background in the automobile and restaurant/hospitality industries.

Joel uses personality profiling tools, along with his innate intuitive skills to match his clients to appropriate franchise/business opportunities that can help his clients build equity in their own businesses.

Go to www.JoelLibava.com

Joel Libava’s Specialties:

Expert matchmaking abilities.The Beachwood Chamber of Commerce, OBBA, resident franchise expert for Cleveland Corporate College, Lake County SBDC, Guest Columnists for the award winning website-Small Business Trends, and a popular speaker at numerous colleges, public schools, libraries, Veteran's gatherings,outplacement companies,career centers, and small business expos.
