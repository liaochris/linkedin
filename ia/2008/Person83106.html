James Mayfield Smith’s Summary

I specialize in Divergent Thinking, Ideation, and Innovation

Specifically, I train corporate executives, marketers, designers, engineers, artists, not-for profit organizations, fund-raisers, and anyone interested in transforming a situation or finding innovative solutions to access their own divergent thinking. I utilize proven methodologies that include outside-the-box strategies as well as traditional lateral thinking approaches designed to get clients out of their rational, linear minds and into the realm of creative potential and maximum ideation. My experiential workshops are original, engaging, and effective at producing results beyond expectations.

As a trained Applied Mythologist, I utilize non-traditional strategies and techniques and the power of story to help clients to think about a challenge from different vantage points and on different levels. If you want different results, hire someone with a unique vision and a distinct set of tools to help you get results that are different.

James Mayfield Smith’s Interests:

I will complete my Certificate in Applied Mythology in August 2008. The Mythic Imagination Institute certificate program in Applied Mythology is a year-long intensive program with some of today’s leading thinkers and teachers in the myth community. The incredible faculty for this program includes Diane Wolkstein, Michael Meade, Lisa Sokolov, David Spangler, Honorah Foah, and Ari Berk. The multi-layered focus of this program includes mythic literacy from a depth psychology perspective, including: • A deep understanding of myth and how it impacts people • Tools for increasing perception and structuring thought • An understanding of the stories we live and the archetypes that live us • Tools to reclaim and fully express the inner voice • Community-building processes • Applications of mythic tools and concepts to one’s life

James Mayfield Smith’s Groups:

How does my work with the Mythic Imagination Institute impact my consulting?
For interested clients, I apply some of these tools during my innovation consulting. I utilize mythic tools along with my natural divergent thinking skills and traditional lateral thinking processes to help clients to sidestep their left-brained linear thinking. We then utilize right-brained mythic imagination to address challenges from different perspectives and thus, achieve different results. Approaching professional challenges, organizational creativity, and ideation from a mythological perspective produces innovative results.
In my personal coaching, I help clients to question their personal life story or their greater cosmological story of what’s possible in the world. When either of these two stories changes, behavioral change is a natural result. Approaching behavioral change from a mythological perspective is effective.
