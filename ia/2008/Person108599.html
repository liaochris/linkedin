Krishna Kalagara’s Summary

A technocrat and a serial entrepreneur, strong in planning and visioning with 12 years experience in building successful companies spanning IT Systems and Network Solutions, Consumer Marketing, Sales and Distribution, Automobiles.

Last venture, Diginet Systems Pvt. Ltd was an Intel Premier Partner, Canon Premier Partner, and Microsoft Assurance Circle Partner.

Current venture, Diginet Gaming is one of India’s best known gaming brands. An avid gamer from long time, have in depth knowledge and many relationships & contacts in IT and gaming industry.

As a serial entrepreneur, not new to risk taking, start-up roadblocks and pressure. Always open to fresh and innovative ideas with flexibility for any mid-course corrections, in achieving what is envisioned.
