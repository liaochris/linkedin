Noam Sahbti’s Summary

Long time competitor monitoring: We will systematically inform you of specific developments and changes in your competitor’s strategy, R&D, partners, campaigns, and more, thus allowing you to react in time while using only the needed recourses.

Competition mapping in new and existing markets: Mapping your competitors lets you know what resources are available to them, who their partners are, what contracts they have with their clients, who they depend on, what their SWOT analysis is, what they invest in R&D, and much more.

R&D project monitoring and identification of emerging technologies: By “following the technological bread crumbs trail” BZHound will tell you everything you need to know about “time to market”, the stage of development of a product or technology, R&D funding, key inventors and engineers, and patent registrations. We also do patent research, assess technology development and do “brain mapping” of the company.

New product intelligence: Even before a new product is launched, BZHound will find out what is the intended market, the price, the cost, method of distribution, weaknesses and strengths of the product etc. You will get the information in time to react with effectively.

Assessment of acquisition candidates: BZHound will provide you with real first hand information about the company. We will make sure that the technology and the brains of the company stay in place after you buy the company. Using the “follow the technology bread crumbs trail” method, we will find out which employees are behind the company’s technology. We will also assess the company’s competitive environment and business partners.

Noam Sahbti’s Specialties:

BZHound gathers information that no ordinary research firm has the means of gathering. In addition to collecting information from primary and secondary sources we use Humint (human intelligence) techniques to gather intelligence that will help to pinpoint the strategic plans of a competitor, make a precise SWOT analysis and accurately assess prices and costs, product development stages and future plans.
