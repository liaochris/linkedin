Russel Rao’s Summary

I am a multi-disciplined technology professional with domain expertise in the areas of CRM, EAI, BPM and SOA supported by an extensive background delivering COTS, online and n-tier applications solutions.

Overall, I have approximately 14 years of experience in the software and consulting services industry with full SDLC experience spanning business case and strategy development through to production readiness planning and solution implementation into production. I am used to working with stakeholders at all levels of an enterprise from executive management to development and operational support teams.

My key strengths are Management, Architecture, Strategy Development and Strategy Execution. I am able to look at the big picture, decompose it into individual components and align them with the overall strategy supported by a detailed execution plan. Consequently, this ability has resulted in several client engagements on large, complex projects in the capacities of Lead Architect, Project Manager, Integration Manager, Technical Manager etc. working within both technology and business streams.
