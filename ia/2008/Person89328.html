Jeremy Kipnis’s Summary

Defining the Cutting Edge in Motion Picture and Sound Presentation and to elevate that to a new level of art and science! That's what it's all about. and beyond anyone's expectations!!! My mentors & idols: George Lucas, Francis Ford Coppola, Steven Spielberg, Ray Dolby, & Henry Kloss could nat begin to imagine what can now be done by correctly integrating the very best technologies and skill sets to produce heretofore unachievable fidelity!

38 years of searching, testing, and evaluating the very finest the world has to offer when it comes to recreating and even exceeding my childhood experiences of the very best 70 mm movie palaces in the world (like Radio City Music Hall, The Roxy, and Wanamaker's) and the most enjoyable and musical concert halls (like Carnegie Hall, Concertgebouw, and Symphony Hall), has led me to this conclusion:

I simply cannot find quality image and sound presentations reliably, anywhere in the world!

So, I've decided to make it, myself . . . and now, for you as well.

Imagine your own personal screening and listening room, where you become completely immersed in whatever you choose to watch or listen to!

Please allow me to completely "Blow your Socks, Away!"

I am totally committed to bringing back the very best of the Golden Age of Cinema, combined with the very finest technologies and design philosophies - beyond 15 perf 70 mm IMAX - and an evolution of Quad, THX, and Ambisonics, with miraculously realistic, all encompassing surround sound - So, it is like "you are THERE!" And somtimes, BETTER!!!

"It's the Greatest Show on Earth" - Let me demonstrate it for you!!!

Jeremy Kipnis’s Specialties:

Perfect Visual, Sonic, and Aesthetic Integration of the very finest audio and video technologies in the world! The goal: total immersion with your favorite film, television program, video game, still photography, 3D movies, music - you name it! I cannot invite you enough into an entirely new era of 21st Century fidelity!

Your expectations of picture & sound presentation will be entirely changed, FOREVER!

  • Producer, Engineer, Editor, and Mastering Engineer

    The Kipnis Mozart Historical Sonata Project

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Music industry)

    January 1990 — Present (18 years 9 months)

    As a personal project, I decided to take on a very significant historical project - Mozart Sonatas performed by my father, Igor Kipnis, on a Viennese piano of 1793 vintage - possibly owner by Beethoven.

    I was able to take a great (but abandoned) recording of my father's unique 1793 fortepiano and focus, edit, and refine it into one of the finest examples of Mozart solo piano music in the record catalog! And with an actual period instrument, no less!
