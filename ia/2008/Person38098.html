Clarence Fisher’s Summary

Clarence Fisher has thirteen years of business experience delivering strategic and tactical consulting services for small and middle market, Information Technology organizations. He has been responsible for full lifecycle project and application development engagements for startup companies as well as established brick and mortar businesses. Most recently he successfully completed an engagement to design and implement a Tier 3, Customer Service Support Center in Atlanta, Georgia to support all BellSouth.net internet customers. This referenceable consulting engagement included organizational design, process workflow design and the implementation of a custom and extendable Customer Resource Management (CRM) application to track their customer’s requirements from cradle to grave.
