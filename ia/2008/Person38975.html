Paul Litwack (LION linkedin @ the-coach.com)’s Summary

Paul Litwack is the Capability Improvement Coach®. He leads the Executive Coaching practice of the International Coach Academy (Australia) and is Director of the Institute for Professional Advancement (Canada).

Since 1976, his thought-provoking Keynotes, Coaching, TeleClasses and custom Training programs continue to help leaders to uplevel their personal, professional and business capabilities. References at www.the-coach.com/kudos.htm

Coach Paul's real world experience comes from years of successful projects from the 'back room' to the Boardroom. He's an active community leader too (example: 1 week after 9/11 he volunteered at GROUND ZERO )
