Maximilian Fellner’s Honors:

Author SPE Paper 113302 "Optimum Economic Development of Mature Eastern European Oil Fields - Case Study"

Abstract:

The paper shows a new application of classic portfolio theory in the field of strategic planning and management of an E&P division. The described system allows simulating and optimizing the performance of the whole E&P division of Petrom including all producing fields and all potential new projects out of a database of more than 2000 projects. A few hundred projects are chosen for implementation in an interactive process allowing true collaboration between the board of directors and the regional management. The system will produce the optimum portfolios of projects which best matches ALL of the goals given by the management, the only management decision left is how much risk it is willing to accept! There are no limits to complexity since each project can be shifted in time, linked to any other project and any KPI and combinations thereof can be taken as goal(s).
