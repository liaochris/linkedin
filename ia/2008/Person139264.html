Neal Lachman’s Summary

P.R.O.S.P.E.R

Public Rollover Offering through Strategic Private Equity Reinvestment.

By utilizing its P.R.O.S.P.E.R strategy, Lachman & Malik Capital Partners, LLC will change the futures of select depressed public companies by merging them with promising large-scale and unique start-ups that have tremendous growth opportunites.

The catch-22 (or -more appropriately- the chicken and egg) situation today is that early-stage companies (let alone start-ups) that have the ability to dominate their industries or markets may never get funded, simply because they don’t fall in the category of funding by a VC (capital requirement is too big) or a Private Equity firm (company is too young).

At present there are a few investment and Merger & Acquisition strategies being utilized to invest in Public Equity or to create Public (merged) trading entities. There are, for example PIPEs when it concerns investments, and Structured Buyouts (Leveraged Buyouts, Management Buyouts) when it concerns strategic ownership or Reversed Takeovers for small private/public combinations. Lachman & Malik (L&M) will not use these strategies for practical reasons.

We have developed what may become a powerful alternative for the most unique and innovative companies that have large-scale opportunities and promise immense growth and profitability.

It all revolves around the fact that the future of our private P.RO.S.P.E.R. company is bright (especially when funded and groomed for public listing) while the public company’s present is doubtful and its past has been problematic which makes it a depressed company. The Reversed Offering is also a boost in the shareholders confidence because it brings an immense potential to their stock, which they may have bought years ago at a very high or regrettable price.

Additional info will be posted on www.nealsperspective.com
