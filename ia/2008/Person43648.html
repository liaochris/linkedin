Daniel Smith’s Summary

I am a sales professional, entrepreneur and dabbling writer in Ottawa Ontario.

By day, I am the Associate Publisher and Advertising Sales Director for a chain of industry newspapers called the Construction News and Report Group.

In my free time, I enjoy reading (books, blogs, magazines), watching movies, playing soccer. A new free-time project of mine is to write a novel - we'll see how that goes!

I have run my own company in the past, and I learned a ton doing it. One day I intend to be my own boss again, but in the meantime, my employer (CNRG) offers me the opportunity to be very entrepreneurial and independent in my work and that's why I love what I do.

I also recently started a blog at www.smithereensblog.blogspot.com (Formerly briefly hosted at Wordpress.com, and there's a clickable link under the Websites section above) , covering a broad range of topics from sales and marketing to productivity, writing and life in general. Pop by sometime and have a read if you like- and be sure to leave me a comment if you do.
