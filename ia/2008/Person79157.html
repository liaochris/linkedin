Ida Martins’s Experience

  • CEO

    Media Luna Ent. GmbH Co. KG

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    January 1992 — Present (16 years 6 months)

    Media Luna Entertainment has been working as a world distributor company for over 15 years. Our main focus lies on sophisticated feature and documentary films. We are putting our emphasis on quality rather than quantity: every single title stands in the first row, every single title is especially important to us. Whenever we decide to take a film into our line-up we invest all our energy to present the film to potential buyers worldwide and to aim at sales in as many territories as possible.

    Depending on the kind of film and the respective potential of each film, Media Luna develops an individual marketing strategy in order to guarantee an effective and sensible placement in international markets. Our target group is first and foremost distributors (theatrical, Video/DVD) and TV channels as well as festivals and other multipliers and non-commercial buyers. Part of this marketing strategy is producing flyers, subtitles and posters – all this in close connection with the producers.
