Jared Barboza’s Summary

About Abbot Monroe, USA
********************************
Abbot Monroe creates music-related promotions and marketing programs for corporate clients, record labels, and the artists on its managemet roster.

To its corporate clients, Abbot Monroe is a one-stop source that bridges the gap between the entertainment industry and those looking to connect with their customers through rich, exciting, relevant media. From promotional downloads, to interactive CDs, to live concert bookings, and more; Abbot Monroe's services include complete program development from start to finish - handling all necessary production, clearances, and licenses.

In Sept.of 2006, the company launched its own artist management division with the signing of Pop/Electronic act, Bitter:Sweet. The current artist roster for Abbot Monroe includes: Morgan Page (Nettwerk), Samantha James (Om), Portrait of a Friend and Jessica Fichot.
