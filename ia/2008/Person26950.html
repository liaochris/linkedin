Brett Hyman’s Summary

We focus our attention on young Hollywood- the next generation of trendsetters and tastemakers. We build nights by matching particular crowds with elaborate environments. More defined than club promotion, we operate as a full scale marketing company with innovative approaches to the entertainment industry.

Night Vision Entertainment consists of 3 major components:

1. Event Marketing
Night Vision’s Event Marketing Division produces regular and special events in multiple nightclub markets

Crimson Lounge- Hollywood
Opera Nightclub- Hollywood
Les Deux Nightclub- Hollywood
GOA Supperclub- Hollywood
Tao Nightclub- Las Vegas
Stingaree- San Diego
Fluid- San Francisco
Marquee- New York

If you are looking for contact to a club in one of these markets,-contact us.

2. Corporate Event Planning
Night Vision Corporate Event Planning will translate your brand into an interactive experience. Night Vision Corporate Event Planning is building a new concept that will enhance both the way events reach the corporate objectives as well as the way the event synergizes with corporate PR and marketing.
For more info go to http://www.nightvisionent.com/nvbrochure.pdf

3. Corporate Marketing
Our Corporate Marketing and PR division helps define company objectives and implements them through a new media and event-drive perspective. Corporate Marketing on a guerilla level mixed with a complex PR strategy can become a less expensive way to launch your new brand or product. Similarly, Night Vision can use the most efficient and effective means to build and implement a specific marketing and PR plan for your company.
A few of our current clients: CollegeTonight.com, Ed Hardy Energy Drink, Ed Hardy Structured Water
