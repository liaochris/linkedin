Ari Mizrachi’s Summary

International Executive skilled in rapid development of strategic business relations and negotiation at the Chief Officer level. Over 10 years of experience and consistent results in sales, marketing and business development with technology organizations. Able to adapt skills rapidly to new environments, lead projects with persistence and creativity, and work flexibly with every functional and technical discipline in organizations both large and small, foreign and domestic.

Industry background: RF/Wireless, semiconductors, fiber-optics, Fiber to the x (FTTx), communications, protocols, Flash (non-volatile memory) semiconductors, enterprise software, Internet, telecom billing systems, electronics manufacturing and research.

Ari has over 15 years of experience in the high tech industry as employee as well as an employer. In his last position, Ari acted as a director of marketing at RFWaves, where he was involved in the marketing and positioning of the company as well as in charge of the sales activities in Asia and Europe at the same time to contributed his knowledge in various other fields. Prior to RFWaves, he co-founded and managed a software house, Edgeware Group, which provided a unique instant messaging scheme, and at the same time consulted and helped early stage companies in defining their product and uniqueness as well as helping in raising money. Before Edgeware group, Ari worked at M-Systems first as a technical marketing manager where he was in charge of the world wide technical support and technical training, later as product line manager, where he was in charge of defining a new line of products in R&D and marketing aspects. Prior to M-Systems, Ari worked as a programmer and team leader at Amdocs and ClassX.

He holds a B.Sc. in electronic engineering from the Tel-Aviv University and Executive MBA from Bar-Ilan university. Married, with three great kids.
