Simen Sommerfeldt’s Summary

I started my career as programmer, architect and project manager, working with GSM analysis systems and later value-added solutions for mobile operators. My tools are databases, UML, Patterns, C++, Visual Basic and Java EE.
I took the step from telecoms to consulting in 2003. I have lately specialized in project management (Scrum) and management. I am now part of the management group at Bouvet.
My short-time goal is to improve my skills in management and sales, while keeping in touch with the technical side of things. That's why you will find me listening at the Technical sessions at conferences like JavaOne and JavaZone. I attended a Dale Carnegie management training course in the fall of 2006.
