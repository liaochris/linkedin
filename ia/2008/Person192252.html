Tracy Crossley’s Summary

I am the principal for 13 Degreez, a company that provides help for businesses by offering outsourced traditional with on-line marketing and business development services. The business also offers a candidate to company matchmaking service. We provide qualified candidates for full time and freelance openings.

I was previously a VP/GM with TMP Worldwide, an international advertising agency.

My background includes a couple of decades of business development with hands on management and marketing, seven years as a business owner (Mad Media) providing marketing, graphic design and print brokering to companies such as 20th Century Fox; Golden Bridge Gourmet Foods; Cinetel-Sunset Films; Lawman Apparel; Bugle Boy and the Fox Latin Channel.

As the business grew, Mad Media strengthened its client offerings by adding promotions, public relations, event production and co-marketing. Clients who utilized these services include Klasky Csupo, The World Animation Celebration, LACER, Greater Relations Dating Service, AFI, McCormick & Schmick’s, Norm’s restaurants, Coral Tree Café, etc…

Other relevant positions include; Director of Marketing/Sales at Dimensional Impressions, General Manager at Cox Auto Trader and Regional Director for VMS. In these positions I was responsible for training and managing people, new business development, strategic planning, and closing deals.
