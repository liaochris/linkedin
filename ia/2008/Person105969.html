Kevin Reese’s Summary

For the past three years Kevin has been the Principal of Reese Sports Marketing. His key clients include: SportZu.TV Youth Sports Video Network, Zaring/Cioffi Celebrity Entertainment and Motive Experiential Marketing.

Kevin has over 24 years of corporate strategic experience in diverse industries. His experience involves an integration of ad agency management, marketing strategy, business development, public relations, interactive planning, media sales, event activation and sponsorship sales. Prior to launching his own sports consulting firm, he held the position of President of PGM Advertising Agency, a division of PRO Group, Inc.

Kevin graduated from Bloomsburg University of Pennsylvania in 1983 with a bachelor of science degree in economics and marketing. Beginning his career as the Marketing Manager of Yuasa Batteries, he was eventually recruited to Southern California to become Director of Spornorships for Advanstar powersports/automotive trade shows, racing and publishing. Relocating to Colorado, Kevin had the opportunity to work in media as the Technology Sales Manager for Disney/ABC, which was later acquired by Reed Publishing, the world’s largest B2B publishing company.

On the agency side, he spent seven years as Director of Client Services at Reese Advertising, a successful east coast ad agency founded by his father, then was Principal of his own integrated marketing agency in Denver which he grew to fifty employees before selling to Henry Gill Advertising.

Kevin’s passion and dedication to the Colorado community has encouraged him to become actively involved in a variety of professional organizations, non profit groups and foundations.

Kevin is married and has two sons. On weekends during the winter, he is a PSIA certified skiing coach with the Vail Development Team. In the spring and fall, Kevin is a staff coach and team coach with the Colorado Fusion Soccer Club and was named the club’s junior academy coach of the year in 2005.
