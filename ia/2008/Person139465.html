Neil Carlson’s Summary

Accomplished utility player and project manager with Ph.D. in political science. Vast experience and rapid learning ability with productivity software, survey packages (Inquisite, TSS, others), MS Project, SQL databases (Access, MySQL), web application development (PHP, ASP, QuickBase, Rails), GIS (ArcGIS, MapInfo) and statistical software (Stata, SPSS, SAS). Capable communicator in English and Spanish. Interested in everything, but especially in designing and building innovative decision-support software.
