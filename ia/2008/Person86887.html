Jean-Marie Luijkx’s Summary

Twenty-four years world-wide experience in all aspects of Reservoir Engineering (North and West Africa, Europe, Middle East, Southeast, South and Central Asia, Australia, North and South America), in operating companies, joint ventures and consulting companies, working on offshore and onshore oil, condensate and gas fields. Wide experience in reservoir simulation, well test analysis, reservoir management, production forecasts, follow-up of operations, PVT, core sampling, SCAL, data acquisition, field reviews and field development plans. Strong computer skills. Educational background: Ph.D. and MBA. SPE member since 1984. Spoken languages: French (native language) and English (fluent). Belgian nationality. Born in May 1957.
