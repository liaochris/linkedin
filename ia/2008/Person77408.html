Hollee Chadwick’s Summary

FREELANCE WRITER, EDITOR, COPYEDITOR

I was hired by Gibson Greeting Cards at age 17 as a freelance trainee. I've continued working as a freelance writer in this field for over 30 years.
I have sales to or projects with: Gibson, MorningStar Publications, Dayspring, Warner Press, Currents, Broderbund Software (authored 25 of their text-included greeting cards in their first Print Shop Deluxe with greeting card capabilities), Rockshots, Paramount, Drawing Board, Brandywine Art, Ltd., Snafu Designs, West Graphics, Oatmeal Studios, Gallant Greetings, and Argus.
I have edited, copyedited, or proofread books for Bridge-Logos Publishers, Sagamore Publishing, Strang Publications, Gallopade International, and Living Waters Publishers.

EDITORIAL

Christ for the Nations Publications: Audiotape Editor. Bridge-Logos Publishers: Non-fiction Editor, Acquisitions Editor, Copyeditor, Proofreader, and Typesetter (Stylebooks include: Chicago, AP, APA, NY Times, U.S. News)

NEWSPAPER

Reporter for The Banc One Wire Newsletter. Columnist for The Erie County Reporter. Stringer for The Toledo Blade. Humor columnist for The Huron Press. Editor of The News Democrat and The Ripley Bee. Freelance Contributor to The Brown County Press, and The Cincinnati Enquirer

ADVERTISING
Retail Advertising Executive for The Morning Journal. Retail Advertising Executive for The Huron Press. Advertising Executive, retail and classified, for The Marketplace. Classified Advertising Executive for The Columbus Dispatch.

OTHER
