Chris Coddington’s Summary

My career thus far has primarily focused on the implementation of internet technologies to meet project and business objectives. My work has spanned many industries to include Banking and Finance, Online Dating, Indian Gaming, and E-Commerce. I worked my way up through the technical profession starting out as desktop support in 1998, then moving into system administration, next into network administration, and eventually found my niche in software development. Along the way I have amassed a number of well known technical certifications. This range of experience has provided me an invaluable set of skills that I rely heavily on in my current role as a software architect. The career challenges that lay ahead for me center on leadership and technical leadership in particular. The path ahead is full of challenges but if nothing else I have learned that success does not come easy and requires a measure of hard work and dedication coupled with a strong network of support.
