January W. Payne’s Summary

A native of Harrisburg, Pa., January W. Payne is an associate editor in U.S.News & World Report’s health and medicine section. She started her career with The Washington Post, where she accepted her first full-time job out of college as a reporter in the newspaper’s health section. She has also written for The Atlanta Journal-Constitution, The (Harrisburg) Patriot-News and USA TODAY. In addition, she is an adjunct professor for journalism courses at the University of Maryland, College Park.

January W. Payne’s Specialties:

Reporting on health, medicine, children, families and relationships.
