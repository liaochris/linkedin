Andrew Freeman’s Experience

    (Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Graphic Design industry)

I lead the charge on all projects and oversee all client work myself, but I employ talented contractors to handle aspects such as intricate programming, professional photography and other aspects that are not my forte.

    Freeman-Designs offers graphic design services for websites, professional logos/branding, sales/marketing collateral, internal printed materials, original cover art, packaging, media production and more.
