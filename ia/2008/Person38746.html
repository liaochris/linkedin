Clyde DeSouza’s Summary

I have real world experience >= 15 years in Creative use of Technology.

This encompasses conceptualizing, creation and introducing Technology creatively into various markets and industries, such as solutions for Advertising, Real Estate, and Heritage awareness building.

I promote the use of "Serious games" - The creation or modification of computer games, to assist in training and learning in diverse fields such as emergency evacuation simulations, health care, and overcoming learning and psychological disabilities in children.

Also the use of Augmented or "mixed reality" in educational and learning products.

My goal is to make technology ubiquitous, with everyday living
