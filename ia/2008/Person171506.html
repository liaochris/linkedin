Anthony Underwood’s Summary

I work at BAKER Drivetrain. It is a rowdy, creative, non-corporate atmosphere that celebrates creativity, dissent, anarchy, and horsepower! My job is pure adventure in the big, bad world of Harley's and American Motorculture. I can't think of a better job for me as I get to excercise a variety of my skills. I manage the brand and reputation of my company via total immersion in the industry. Anywhere the brand interfaces the public, the media, the dealer network, and so on...I manage it all in a hands-on fashion and don't like other people touching it. This keeps us directed, laser-focused, nimble and consistent. Here's some of the tasks that I do:

_Graphic Design
_Ilustration
_Photography
_Web Development
_Package Design
_Model Searching
_Motorcycle Crashing
_Art Direction/Creative Direction
_International Marketing Strategy
_Fire Lighting
_International Magazine Advertising Strategy (25 magazines)
_Ghost Writing
_PR
_Custom Motorcycle Designing
_Sponsorship Management
_Trendspotting
_TV Advertising
_Trade Show and Events Management and Creative Direction
_Industry Manipulating

I get to travel worldwide. They ship my motorcycle to most events for me in the name of good branding. They get it. I network (party) with heavy-hitters and figureheads in this motorcycle industry to make friends which keeps us in the forefront of their minds - and gets them to listen to my ideas/requests. My travels keeps my finger on the pulse of things as I trend spot, scout for opportunity and formulate strategies.

This position is way better for BAKER Drivetrain than hiring an agency for quadruple the cost and a fraction of the speed. BAKER is small enough, and I'm diverse enough in my skill set, that this is a match made in Valhalla!

Anthony Underwood’s Experience

  • Brand Vandal (Director of Marketing, Creative and PR)

    BAKER Drivetrain

    (Privately Held; Automotive industry)

    January 2005 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    I'm am currently taking on BAKER Drivetrain as my exercise in building a dominant brand name in the Harley-Davidson aftermarket industry. After a couple years in-house, it's working. I am the BRAND VANDAL! I plaster BAKER Drivetrain everywhere!

    Through hands on graphic design, advertising design and strategy, illustration, identity design, trade show design, photography, copy writing, web site development, pr, direct marketing, TV product placement and cameos, sponsorship management, internal branding, chopper building, motorcycle mayhem, cultural immersion, aggressive traveling and networking worldwide I am taking this place from a company with a good product to a leading brand name. This all isn't merely pretty art. These are the tools at hand that support a deeper strategy that is derived from being deeply embedded in the culture of an industry - not peripheal studies, surveys and focus groups. What fun!

  • Cofounder and Lead Designer

    BlackBOX

    (Privately Held; Design industry)

    January 2001 — 2003 (2 years)

    BlackBOX was a graphic design, web design, and new media developer. I took my existing client base from freelancing through college and merged it with two other local designers to form BlackBOX when I graduated. A larger entity tried to buy our award-winning company and the others didn't want to sell. They bought me instead and we started Ignition New Media Workshop - which generated triple the revenue with a third of the manpower. BlackBOX died shortly after I left.
