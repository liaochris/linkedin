Andrei Nadin’s Summary

With Game Industry experience dating back over 17 years, I have worked at a select group of companies including: Teeny Weeny Games/Perfect 10 entertainment, Hasbro Interactive, Runecraft, Empire Interactive and Codemasters.

I have held a wide range of positions including: Producer, Senior Producer, Creative Director, Studio owner.

I have designed and/or produced some great games including: Discworld, Monopoly, Frogger, Caesars Palace II, Comanche Hokum, Mig Alley, Dungeons & Dragons Online, Bliss Island.

I have worked for developers & publishers in the UK, I have set up and run a development studio. This has given me strengths I believe few producers have as I have a depth of experience some may never achieve. It has taught me good communication skills, given me objectivity, made me more observant and more organised as well as teaching me empathy with my co-workers/employees.

I have extensive knowledge of 2D and 3D art, understanding of programming and code flow, IT & Networking skills.

I have designed & produced games working with difficult schedules and a variety of budgets.

As difficult as this industry can be, I am passionate about my work and an extremely hard worker who is good at high level thinking but also understands the efforts required to get a project out the door on time and on budget but always with one eye on the reason we do it - to make great games!
