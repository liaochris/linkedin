Tullio Siragusa’s Summary

Tullio Siragusa, is a 19 years veteran of Digital Media (search, publishing, and social networks), Software (product and services), Consulting (IT, M&A, Process Engineering, Professional Services), and Telecom (data and voice); within media and financial services companies.

Most recently focused on companies marketing to publishers and consumer advertisers worldwide. Background includes CEO of search syndication expert Ansearch (publicly traded on ASX), Sr. VP of Sales and Account Management, Operative Media. (Edison Ventures funded). Director and Partner roles with the likes of Ernst & Young, PricewaterhouseCoopers, Computer Sciences Corporation and Cognizant Technology Solutions.

A dynamic visionary executive who has impacted over $2.9BB in revenues, thus far in his 19 years career while launching 7 successful business initiatives in key executive roles in both public conglomerates and private start ups. Ability to take complex issues and create simple actionable opportunities that drive and meet business objectives.

Found, developed and launched the world's first face to face social scene network (http://m3mob.com). M3 connects you to the who with the what and the where -all in one place. The best of search, publishing and networking combined into a very personalized social planning and management tool, for both consumers and brands. M3 brings the power of mobile, web and real world events together. M3 connects brands to people and brands to each other, becoming a cross vertical public relations exchange that connects everyone to each other via the web and mobile, real time. You can read more about it, here: http://m3mob.com/blog_entry.php?user=Tullio&blogentry_id=18
