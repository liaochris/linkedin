Shahbaz Ali’s Summary

Shahbaz Ali is Founder and CEO of Tarmin Technologies. Highly successful startup guy who raised over $3.1m of initial funding for Tarmin and provided the vision to create Tarmin's killer app - NOADS.

Shahbaz has over 16 years of experience in bringing ground breaking technology solutions to new markets. He served as Director of Banking Systems at Mondex International acquired by MasterCard International (NYSE:MA). At MasterCard he was responsible for overall digital content and archiving strategy and led the online content retention initiative. Prior to that Shahbaz served as the Chief Architect for FIFA World Cup Communication project, working on the design of highly scalable unified storage solution for the entire communication of the FIFA World cup. He was also involved in Energis Communications GSI based E-Government project: Secure File Sharing and Archiving for Central Government agencies.

Shahbaz’ past engagements include technology management positions with Invensys Corporation, Lucent Technologies, Avaya Communications, Microsoft Consulting, Marconi and Aspect Communications. Most recently he worked on Active Archiving and pioneered Archive Lifecycle Management bringing Tarmin NOADS products into the market. With the massive growth in storage management software and tiered storage software platforms, within 15 months his startup Tarmin has already grown from 2 people into a 60 people Enterprise.
