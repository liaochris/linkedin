Jessica Mellott’s Summary

Jessica Mellott
info@jessicamellott.com
www.jessicamellott.com

Jessica Mellott is a bubbly, blonde, teen, pop singer from Frederick, Maryland. The 2006 Los Angeles Music Awards "Pop Female Singer/Songwriter of the Year" began her vocal instruction when she was only five years old. Her training includes classical music, jazz, musical theater and contemporary pop.

Jessica's compelling voice, songwriting skills and magnetic personality have attracted tremendous attention. Her song, Remember Me, was #1 at the Industry Resource for months, where she is highlighted as a rising star. Her song "Life Grabs You (And Starts to Rock) is featured in an Indie film. On Myspace.com Jessica has received so many song plays she was selected to be featured in a book on Myspace c. 2006, Wiley Publishing. Jessica was a main page featured artist on website Zorpia.com (8 million+ users) and is currently featured on Snapvine.com (5 million+ users) and other websites.

Jessica is also an accomplished actress who boasts both starring and supporting roles in shows ranging from "The Secret Garden" to "Working". In 2005, Jessica earned a certificate in Musical Theater from the respected New York Arts Camp Stagedoor Manor.

Jessica volunteers her vocal talents frequently to help the community. Her performances have ranged from entertaining the armed forces to shows that have helped raise thousands of dollars for the public school system.

Whether she is singing a gripping ballad or high energy song, Jessica captivates the crowd and has gained a world wide fan base. Get the latest news, listen to her music and view more photos at her websites.
