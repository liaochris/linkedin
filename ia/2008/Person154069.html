Philippe Scheimann’s Summary

Philippe Scheimann, 46, MSc & MBA, is a 'management technologist' and (social) entrepreneur.
Before receiving his MBA in 1991, he held a position in the Planning Department of Bezeq, the Israeli Telecommunications Corporation. He has been active on the Internet since 1990.
Since 1991, he has worked as a management and IT consultant for European, Israeli and American companies, startups and institutions
In April 2002, Philippe co-founded Ayala Alternative Organizational Consulting Ltd (http://www.orayala.com) , an innovative Israeli consulting company that is focused on improving technology and soft issues of communication between people and between companies.
Philippe has been, and continues to be, active in a number of Internet and telecom related ventures working on product development, requirement management, business model and information scouting. Philippe is presently VP Product Management at Limood Social Knowledge, a French-Israeli Start-up.
He is co-initiating the social project of 'Sustainable eSchool' in the framework of POLITECH Institute.
He has been consulting, teaching and giving seminars on web-based collaboration, Information finding and Knowledge Management, Efficient use of tools, Requirements Management and Process Improvement.
He has published a number of papers in conferences and magazines in Israel, Europe and in the US.
Philippe is married to Ophira and they have a daughter and a son. They live in the center of Israel.
