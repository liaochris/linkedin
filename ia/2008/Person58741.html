Work Bio

I specialise in photoreal compositing using a variety of media including green screen,cg and elements such as particles/dust/blood. I am very proficient in manual painting techniques and have therefore a very strong eye for detail and colour composition.
I have worked on a variety of award winning films including, Children of Men and Poseidon. My skillset ranges from traditional art through compositing and even into the 3D realm.
Having worked for both large and small facilities I find I am able to adapt quickly to changes in both my artistic role and my responsibilities in a team and as an individual artist.
I thoroughly enjoy working as a compositor and hope to develop my skills and credits further in the feature film industry.
