Gabriel Perera’s Summary

Senior executive and business builder with Board reporting experience who takes responsibility for delivering on commercial objectives and for driving and managing business growth in highly competitive and dynamic environments.

Management of clinical trials program, materials, and research relationships in addition to Professional products business for major Australasian natural medicines brand.

Development and management of three Phase II double blind, randomised, placebo controlled clinical trials on proprietary nutraceutical products with leading Australasian clinical and academic research institutions and commercial partners.

Formulating the business plan for a biotechnology company leading to clinical trials, venture capital relationships and commercialisation of two provisionally patented therapeutic products with leading Australian company.

Product development, production, marketing strategy and launch, for two new to market Practitioner Products business for leading participant in the dietary supplements sector.

Turning around the performance of a natural medicines business to achieve a 46% increase in sales, a 5% increase in gross margin and a positive EBIT within 15 months in the Practitioner Products (naturopath, medical practitioners).

Managed development & launch of 24 new therapeutic products in a short time frame of 10 months in the most demanding regulatory environment in the world.

Undertaking strategy consulting projects with Accenture in leading multinationals in the airline, automotive, dairy products and pharmaceuticals sectors Australia, New Zealand & China.

Gaining market leadership and generating $10m in sales within 18 months in a former monopoly market for a start-up private cardiac catheterisation laboratory through an targeted recruitment campaign to attract leading practitioners and professionals, securing preferred supplier status with New Zealand’s leading private health insurer.
