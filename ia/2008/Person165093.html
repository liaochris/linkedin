Roy Meyeringh’s Summary

VENEVISION INTERNATIONAL, LLC, Coral Gables, Florida (2003-Present)
Global entertainment unit of the Cisneros Group specializing in distribution, production, film and music, television, as well as Web-based and mobile entertainment with $100M in annual sales.

Having galvanized sales growth through multiplatform content acquisition and distribution channel development, I am proud to continue to play an instrumental role in the success of global media provider and Cisneros Group Company subsidiary Venevision International. My goal is to relentlessly identify and partner with new multiplatform delivery technologies that will act as alternative revenue vehicles for rich content distribution.

My professional experience ranges from strategic sales planning and relationship management to team building and mentoring to quality service delivery.

Highlights:

• Formulating and implementing strategies for selling digital media products and Web-based content to prominent new media outlets such as AOL Latino, MSN Latino, HP, Netflix, and iTunes as well as global cable, satellite, and Telecommunications organizations, including Time Warner Cable, Dish Network, DirecTV, AT&T HomeZone, Verizon Fios, and Telefonica.

• Securing content distribution agreements through non-traditional channels, such as in-flight movies, hotel and hospitality networks, and library systems, for premium video-on-demand products.

• Establishing Venevision as the premier provider of Hispanic content for the global marketplace, fueling massive revenue growth from only $38K in 2003 to a projected $1.7M in 2008, which led to the launch of a new cable film channel called VeneMovies in late 2006.

• Negotiating favorable contracts that captured more than $500K in savings over 3 years.
