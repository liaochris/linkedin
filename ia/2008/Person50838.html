Dan Hagendorn’s Summary

Dan Hagendorn
Market Manager, California

While spirits represent a new challenge for native New Yorker Dan Hagendorn, his varied professional experience and flinty, street-smart style will serve him well in the position.

After earning a degree in Film and Video from Marist College in upstate New York, Hagendorn moved to New York City as a video director for Chubb Insurance. Among his duties was to produce films that showcased the insurance giant’s services to Internet startups. From there, he went to cable outlet Telecare TV in Long Island, where he served as production manager, supervising all production aspects of the station’s original programming.

In 2004, he started his own production company with a partner, called D&D Precision Pictures. “I hit the streets with my Rolodex,” Hangendorn recalls. “Through my work with Telecare, I’d come in contact with a number of CEO’s and presidents of Fortune 500 companies who contributed to the station’s programming and funding. Those were the first doors I knocked on.”

And they opened. As his company grew, he produced industrial films, training videos, corporate podcasts and other filmed content for the likes of CBRE, General Electric, Bear Stearns and ConEd.

He and his partner’s paths ultimately diverged. “He wanted to make movies and I wanted to grow the business in the direction it was already going,” Hagendorn remembers. Trago International provided the opportunity to come West, and Hagendorn leapt at it. “Trago is such a sensual brand—that is, engaging all the senses,” he says. “And it’s visually stunning, which is rare in the tequila business.” Further, Trago’s place in California—in the Hollywood firmament and the entertainment industry—isn’t far from his roots. “As a producer, it caught my eye.”

“I am going to be working the market from the ground up,” Hagendorn promises. “Our California distributor has about 300 reps working the state, and I expect I will meet every one of them.”
