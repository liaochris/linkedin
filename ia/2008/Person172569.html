David Mattocks’s Summary

When you decide it’s time to sell your business, you’ll naturally want to achieve the best possible price and make sure your prized asset sells for all it’s worth. Equally, you would expect the professionally qualified adviser you take on to help you, to never put at risk one single penny of your hard-earned cash at any time throughout the process, wouldn’t you?

Of course you would!

It’s for this reason Sunbelt Business Brokers insist you pay nothing until the sale of your business has completed successfully. We don’t believe it’s right, ethical or morally justifiable to ask you to risk any of your own money on up-front charges, with no assurances that your business will sell for its full market value.

At Sunbelt, we guarantee never to demand engagement fees, or marketing fees, or retainer fees, or any other form of staged payment from you, while working on selling your business. Sunbelt Business Brokers get paid entirely on results and ONLY on successful completion of the sale at the right price, to the right buyer. Any other way just isn’t fair is it?
