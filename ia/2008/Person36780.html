Christopher Carl’s Summary

Walker-Elliott, LLC is an independent consulting firm located in Houston, TX. Our primary focus is on the Information Technology, Engineering, and Oil & Gas niches; however, we are staffed and qualified to provide professionals in any area or organization level to meet all of your staffing needs. We will respond to all your requests quickly and accurately, with both diligence and great results, regardless of location, skill set, or difficulty, via our large candidate database or through “passively” sourcing non-client organizations for those critical, hard-to-fill positions to ensure the best fit for your situation and company culture.

I take great pride in recruiting superior talent that relies on our opportunity matching insight in the areas of Information Technology, Engineering, & Oil & Gas. I find the hidden talent both locally and nationally that wants confidentiality in moving their career goals forward. I have industry focus in Information Technology, Engineering, Oil & Gas, and Environmental areas.
