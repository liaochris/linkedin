Mario Lazzaro’s Summary

Founder CEO of Consulting Argentina (CONSULT-AR) covering marketing, planning and business plan execution perfomed in foreign trade and business development field. Projects and contracts negotiated, act as Controller and executed in United States, China, former Soviet Union, Brazil, Chile and other countries from Latinamerica, Africa and Europe for products like: turn on key plants, food processing equipment, wine, grape juice and fruit concentrates / pulps. Consultant of many Argentinean companies as support of Export Department. Provider of Argentinean business host services of accounting, administration and financial to foreign companies willing to work or establish in Argentina. President of my own company producer and exporter of wine from Argentina. Consultant services to government entities for projects empowered by World Bank and Interamerican Development Bank BID.
