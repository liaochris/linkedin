Ernest Thornicroft’s Summary

Ernest is a multi-industry and solution-focused Senior Accounting and Finance professional. He is a resourceful problem solver that is able to see the 'big picture' and execute tactically.

He has hands-on experience acquired in diverse positions and has an outstanding reputation as a 'financial troubleshooter'. He's solid background is further supported by over 25 years of consistent, successful experience with national and international organizations ranging from entrepreneurial ventures to multi-national corporations.

Ernest specializes in optimization of financial and operational performance through the implementation of solid systems and processes.

He is at his best in situations where organizations are experiencing significant change.

Ernest has progressively performed at CFO, VP, Director of Finance and Controllership roles for several organizations in Banking, Mining, Software, Travel, Consulting and Construction industries.

Recognized as principal contributor by the Board, who added, "Ernest is a highly valuable leader and team player who always strives to provide immeasurable value to the organization."
