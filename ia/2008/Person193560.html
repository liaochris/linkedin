Julio Romo’s Summary

A bilingual and experienced PR practitioner and consultant with over nine years experience.

I have worked across the board, in-house and agency-side, in public relations as in public affairs and lobbying. Leading corporate communications and issues and crisis management projects, as developing and promoting consumer campaigns. All this experience has enabled me to offer strategic support to clients that want to be heard clearly and without distraction.

Since becoming a consultant I have worked with clients ranging from Creative London to De Beers/DTC. With agencies ranging from MS&L and Kaizo to Communique PR and Burson Marsteller.

I have helped campaigns get the publicity that they deserve and guided arguments away from areas that are contentious for clients.

As a Chartered Institute of Public Relations Accredited Practitioner I offer a full experience and support.

Julio Romo’s Specialties:

Corporate communications, strategic communications (corporate and consumer), media relations and management, media training, campaigning, reputation management, event management, photo shoots and online and viral communications.
