Jannick Sørensen’s Summary

I'm an researcher and interaction designer with a background in broadcast media production: DR / Danish Broadcasting Corporation, and Media Science: University of Southern Denmark

I'm applying user centred design methods from both media production and IT-development on my work as a researcher on wireless services and future Personal Network (PN) technologies in the MAGNET Beyond EU-IST research project.
