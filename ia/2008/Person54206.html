David Drake’s Summary

David Drake in 1997 joined investment bank Alfred Berg both in New York City and Stockholm, Sweden. In 1999 he joined Prudential as a Senior Director. In 2001 David started acquiring his own companies and invest in energy and real estate and expand into luxury products, hospitality and media.

David Drake went to undergrad at American University where he earned a Bachelor's Degree in Chemistry and Marketing. David was given the prestigious Swedish Wallenberg Scholarship awarded for excellence in undergraduate academic merit, and decided to stay in Washington DC to attend graduate school at George Washington University. He went on to receive two graduate degrees from this university, including an MBA in Finance and an MA in International Law and Economics. His vision is to make friends and identify people with positive energy to drive business and investments. His 1000 dinner parties at his home is a legacy to this effect.

LDJ Capital looks at investments in the $3mm-$20mm size.

David Drake’s Specialties:

LDJ Capital Partners is a boutique advisory firm focused on companies and assets in the luxury, commodities, real estate, and media / entertainment industries.

Although each situation is evaluated individually by the LDJ Capital team, we are generally able to provide customized assistance with business development, board building, technology, marketing, operations, and a wide range of financing issues.

Please contact us at David@LDJCapital.com
