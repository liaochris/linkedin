Nawal Cotran’s Summary

Over 20 years of experience with high tech manufacturing companies in positions of increasing responsibility. Managed Quality organizations in high volume manufacturing operations with multiple plants in the U.S. and overseas. Strong experience in supplier management and contract manufacturers. Extensive experience in Reliability and Process Quality of large and small companies at local and remote manufacturing operations. Worldwide customer interface and Total Quality Management system expertise. Acknowledged of being effective in implementing change, in particular change in corporate culture and business practices. Experienced in ISO Quality systems. Six Sigma Black Belt and champion trained.

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; ACTL; Semiconductors industry)

    January 2001 — Present (7 years 10 months)

    Actel designs, develops, manufactures, and markets Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGA) for the Space, Military, Communication, Consumer, Automotive, and Industrial markets.

    Established new processes and business practices that led to substantial improvements in yield and productivity.

    Improved critical systems such as Return of Materials (improved 30%), Products kept on Hold (lowered 25%) and FA turn around time (improved 15%).
