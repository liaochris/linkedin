Sam Srinivasan’s Summary

I have over 19 years experience in the IT industry in Sales, Marketing, Project Management, Change Management, Channel Management, Leadership, Manufacturing and Operations Management. During this time I have gained tremendous insights into business and customer needs in the market.

I have worked in three continents North America, ANZ and Asia and have a great deal of experience in conducting business in diverse cultural situations. I have travelled extensively and have successfully done business in a variety of countries around the world.

I have experience setting up new branch operations in countries, integrating over 17 acquisitions in and out over the last 18 years and have led many projects that required process and supply chain improvements to unleash trapped profitability in companies. I also have experience in commercialisation of technology and setting up Global Account Management strategies.
