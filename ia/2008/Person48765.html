Debbie Adler’s Summary

Debbie is a second year MBA student at NYU's Stern Business School where she is specializing in Entertainment, Media & Technology and Management. Outside of class, Debbie is active in the Social Enterprise Association. She exercises her ability to connect and sell by spearheading efforts to entice prospective students. At present, she is studying abroad at ESADE in Barcelona, Spain.

Debbie has developed digital distribution strategies for television, film and music. She is looking to parlay her experience and MBA into a business development or strategic partner manager position at a content provider or digital distribution company. She is skilled at relationship management, content distribution strategy, managing growth, working with creative people, implementing innovative ideas and technology, and knowing a good thing when she sees/hears it.

On the side, Debbie helps get her friends get jobs and spreads the word about GOOD Magazine.
