Jason Prescott’s Summary

Jason A. Prescott serves as CEO of JP Communications’ suite of online wholesale merchandise industry vertical search engines and directories. Sites include TopTenWholesale, Wholezilla, OffpriceNetwork and WholesaleU. Prescott and partner Joe Preston launched the B2B publishing empire to serve the needs of the general merchandise wholesale community in April 2005. Since then, the business has grown over 600 percent and continues to rise. Prescott gained expertise in this sector as sales director for Sumner Communications, a top publisher of wholesale trade magazines and directories since 1988. After a five-year stint with Sumner, Prescott was approached by Wholesale411.com to reform their business prior to acquisition by Liquidity Services Inc. After successfully building online wholesale merchandising revenues for others, Prescott launched JP Communications and never looked back. Prescott is a member of SEMPO and serves on the education committee. He has been a speaker at Search Engine Strategies, San Diego Software Council and is a member of the San Diego Venture Capital Group. Jas has been recognized as a “Top 40 under 40” in San Diego and nominated for Emerging Small Business of the Year by the San Diego Chamber of Commerce. Prescott graduated from Western Connecticut State University with B.S. in Law Administration, minor in Marketing, 2000.
