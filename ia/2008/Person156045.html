Raj Abhyanker’s Summary

Raj Abhyanker is a U.S. patent attorney focusing on U.S. patent preparation, prosecution, and due diligence litigation support.

Raj has drafted and prosecuted United States patent applications for numerous multinational and venture backed companies including Texas Instruments, Intel, Apple Computer, eBay, Adobe, LSI Logic, Rambus, America Online, Guidant, and Cisco Systems. Raj has excellent English analytical writing skills, and received a 99th percentile rank on the GMAT analytical writing section.

Raj has been registered with the United States patent bar since November 1999. Prior to starting his own firm in 2005, Raj Abhyanker was a patent attorney at large U.S. patent law firms including Blakely Sokoloff Taylor & Zafman LLP and Schwegman Lundberg Woessner & Kluth P.A.

Prior to entering law practice, Raj worked as an electrical engineer and business executive at numerous companies including Lockheed Martin, Hewlett Packard Company, and Juniper Networks. Raj has been an invited speaker at a number of national conferences and universities, including at the Harvard Business School, UC Berkeley Boalt School of Law, National South Asian Bar Association, University of Chicago College of Business, Northwestern Kellogg School of Business, and at TiE Silicon Valley.

Raj holds BS Electrical Engineering, MBA, and Juris Doctorate degrees from Arizona State University. In addition, Raj went to graduate school in Electrical Engineering at Stanford University. Raj is a Center Fellow of the Arizona State University Sandra Day O’Connor School of Law, and is a former President of IP Law Association of the law school.

Raj is a member of the State Bar of California (active), United States Patent Bar (active), and the State Bar of Minnesota (active).
