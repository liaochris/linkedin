Varun Singh’s Summary

Varun Singh is currently pursuing his Masters in Communication Engg (2006-*) from the Helsinki University of Technology, where he is also employed at the Network Laboratory as a Research Assistant (2007-*) working on technologies such as SIP, RTP/RTCP, Session Mobility, Rate Adaptation, Error Resilience in Video Comm, Delay Tolerant Networks, Bluetooth etc. Before coming to Helsinki, Varun was working with STMicroelectronics, Noida, India (2004-2006) for 2 years as a Software Engineer in Cellular Platform and Terminal Divisions, contributing to Multimedia (Still/Video Capture) and GPRS stack (SMS, PDP-Context). As an undergrad. he interned with LG Electronics, Bangalore, India (2004) contributing code to and testing the SyncML Device Management Client and Server.

Varun Singh’s Specialties:

Programming and the ability to quickly understand a new technology, problem and contribute. To think outside the box, and rapidly prototype solutions using existing software, technologies.
