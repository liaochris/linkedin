Gregg O'Reilly’s Summary

My current role is as CIO in Fujitsu Services Identity Management and Home Affairs business units.

My major current focus is on all aspects of Identity Management in the UK.

The role combines being CIO, Enterprise Architect and Consultant across all of FS's Home Affairs Business (Home Office, IPS, BIA, Criminal Justice, Police...). This role concentrates currently on strategic initiatives in the Identity Management and Police Information shared services and data sharing spaces.

Until joining Fujitsu, on 1st Sept 2006, my most recent position was Atos Origin Technical Director on a 6-12 year IT programme for Home Office IND (as was).

I was with Atos Origin since the takeover of SchlumbergerSema in Jan 2004, previously with SchlumbergerSema and Sema Group since 1997. Prior to that, I worked in the IT arm of Department of Work and Pensions (Department of [Health and] Social Security as was).

I have more than 20 years experience in the IT industry, in a broad spectrum of technical and management roles, from Analyst/Programmer to Enterprise Architect and Consultant and from Team Leader via Project and Programme Manager roles to Technical Director and CxO. I have worked primarily in Central Government (including Health) but also in Transport, Oil and Gas, Local Government and Finance.

In the last 5 years I have alternated between Enterprise Architecture/Technical Strategy and Risk Management, Technical Assurance and Due Diligence. Outside of my main roles, over this period, my primary focus was on Atos's global technical architecture community.
