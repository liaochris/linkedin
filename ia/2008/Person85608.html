Jay Schwartz’s Summary

Seasoned executive and entrepreneur responsible for founding and managing the strategic direction of IndieShares, the world's first company to accept direct public investment for the funding of independent films. Also established and ran the business development function for WaMu, the 6th largest financial institution in the U.S. Deep experience in business development at world class companies, and used JD and MBA education to hone skills. Strong legal and IP background and broad deal structuring expertise in the internet, entertainment, media, banking, mobile, and information technology spaces.
