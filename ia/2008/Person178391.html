Sony Joy’s Summary

mobME is a Mobile & Internet Technology company, started in December 2006, as a spin-off from Torque Technology Solutions. With five independent business units under its fold, mobME is betting big on MobShare.in, its flagship product. MobShare is a Mobile Content Sharing Social Network, or in other words, a platform carefully placed at the sweet spot between Mobile & Internet. Our goal is to become a globally recognized public listed company known for its innovative products & cutting edge technology.
