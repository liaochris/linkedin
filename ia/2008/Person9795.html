Amy Porter’s Summary

As you can see from my work experience I have held positions in the personal services market, legal and social work. As a social worker I held jobs in three hospitals, two mental health centers and had a private therapy practice. As an attorney I founded, with my Health Care Law Professor, two law firms, a private and a non-profit. Following this, I worked in corporate law at a fifty attorney law firm before accepting a position at Sapient as in-house counsel. When Sapient reduced its workforce from 3,500 to 1,000 I left and took the opportunity to found a very successful pet services business. After three years I sold my business to accept a job for Laurence H. Tribe (a former dog-walking client, and Harvard Law Professor of Constitutional Law), as his in-home personal and legal assistant.

Please take the time to read my position descriptions below.
I am intersted in a position as a personal assistant at an executive level, life-coach jobs, job coach, human resources, teaching at any level - and would be amenable to full or part-time. I am also open to sales/marketing positions and am flexible, creative, energetic, attentive to detaill and a fast learner.
