Wojciech Kruszewski’s Summary

Would you be interested if there was a group of creative professionals that could cover all aspects of your web application project - from working closely with you on the specs to maintenance and enhancement? A group that would share their ideas all the time and keep you informed throughout the project?

That's exactly why I'm here.

Please let me introduce myself, I'm Wojciech Kruszewski from Legendary Mill. Together with Marek and Darek - a Rails-ninja-duo, we will put your vision into work.

Having been a webdevelopment outsourcing mercenary for a couple of years now, I learned to value good communication, with project management software or just plain emails, supplemented with IM talks on a regular basis.

I always seeked diverse experience, starting a decade ago, before high school, with C programming. Then there was Software Engineering academic education, graphic design at an advertising bureau, typesetting for a newspaper, and most notably: everything that has to do with web development.

I'm a Drupal veteran now learning advanced Rails from two experts - my colleagues Marek and Darek.

I value and pursue:

» working together with the client on a clear specifications
» good project management augmented with appropriate software
» timely updates
» initial research and then stats gathering and analysis
» software design that allows reusability and maintenance
» putting business goals before our technological preferences (difficult!)
» tableless, standard compliant XHTML+CSS layouts with semantic markup
» wide cross-browser compatibility
» unit testing starting in early stages


I'm eager to hear about your project! Please contact me here on LinkedIn, so that we could discuss it.
