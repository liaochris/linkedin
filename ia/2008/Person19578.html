Barbara Bickham’s Summary

Barbara Bickham has 20+ years of experience in the computer software industry in strategic, management and architectural roles. She has spent the past 8 years in interactive and mobile strategy, management, architecture and implementation.

Prior to founding TechGenii, Inc., Ms. Bickham was the Director of Engineering for Thinque Systems. She was responsible for the strategic and architectural direction for Thinque's wireless products and new technology initiatives. Ms. Bickham has worked for Enterworks, RAND, Network Associates, St. Jude Medical, Xerox, and US. Robotics.

Ms. Bickham is the Private Capital Network, Los Angeles Chapter President. The Private Capital Network is an angel investment association, helping companies to grow.

Ms. Bickham is actively involved on the Community Advisory Board for KCET Los Angeles; an Executive Committee member of the Interactive Television Alliance; a Los Angeles Advisory Board Member for The M.I.N.D Institute; an Advisory Board Member to Continental Vista; an Advisory Board Member for VNU's DEMXPO; a Founding Board Member of the Mobile Development Association; a member of CTIA's Wireless Internet Caucus; a voting member of the IEEE 802.11 Standards Committee; and a member of Academy of Television Arts and Sciences.

She is a graduate of the New Entrepreneur's Program from the University of Chicago, has a Masters Degree in Computer Science from West Coast University and a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science from the University of California, Berkeley.
