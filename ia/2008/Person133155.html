WHO CAN I HELP?

I can assist foreign companies that want to approach the Italian market of consulting services (in particular project management and engineering consulting services in the Real Estate). Italian market is pretty ununderstandable for foreign companies. There are legal constraints, economical constraints, but above all cultural constraints. You have to be aware that Italy is more a Middle Eastern country than a European country. Having worked for International companies (British, French, American and German companies) in Italy and abroad, I can support to tune up an approach to local market and correctly develop the business.
