Jennifer Gibbings’s Summary

Results driven, creative and seasoned consultant promoting products & services through efficient application of marketing strategy and research, account management, and project management for Web, video, media, training development and communication programs, and direct response marketing. Demonstrated leadership abilities as a department director and senior business consultant. Contact me at jennifer@jennifergibbings.com.

Measurement Strategies:
•Qualitative and quantitative research, user design strategies including usability testing, observational field studies, interviews, surveys, Web analytics using Omniture & Google Analytics.

Account Management expertise including agency experience serving enterprise level clientèle and many regional and local companies.
•Passionate about creating competitive business advantages by favorably positioning compelling ideas.
•Strong ability to identify appropriate strategies and projects to support client marketing initiatives.
•Proposal development, including requirements gathering, scope considerations, resource assessment and costing, strategy recommendations and authoring/editing.
•Seasoned ability to lead teams of people to ensure that project objectives remain consistent with the overall business strategy.

Jennifer launched her own business communication consultancy in 2007 delivering results for many regional and local companies. Prior to starting her own consultancy, she worked for White Horse as the Manager of Strategy & Research, as well as Aquent, full service marketing agencies. Jennifer helped found Zanthus, a full service market research firm and also held a position at Gartner Group, an international consulting firm. Client work includes but is not limited to: Adidas, HP, HSBC, Nautilus, Nike, OHSU, Providence Health & Services, Waggener Edstrom. Jennifer is serving her second term on the board of the American Marketing Association's Oregon Chapter as Sponsorship Co-Director 2007-2008.
