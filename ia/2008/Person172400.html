Michael Santarcangelo’s Summary

Michael Santarcangelo’s keen ability to engage people in the practice of information protection has launched him to the forefront of the information security community. His unique insights, innovative concepts and truly effective strategies are informed by extensive research.

His speaking programs, training, coaching and strategic consulting services along with the ground-breaking “Security Salon” and his original Security Toolkits, motivate organizations to better information protection. Michael’s mission is to support those who are serious about information security through the sometimes confusing challenge of engaging people, developing processes and deploying the right tools and technologies. He helps organizations move off the rollercoaster of “security diets” and into a mindset of protecting information.

His first book, Into the Breach, will be released in the Fall of 2007. It will be followed by the Campaign Across America, a cross-country tour to spread the information security message.

As a speaker, Michael is known for delivering substantial content in a way that is energetic and entertaining. In fact, he is the first information security professional to become a full member of the National Speaker’s Association. As a coach, Michael encourages the integration of life, work and security in a way that develops focus and energy – helping professionals to achieve more with less. And as consultant, he is recognized for taking a positive approach to helping people change the way they think about security and, therefore, the way they act.

Michael is a true catalyst: he plants the seeds and provides the tools so that others may cultivate change.

Michael Santarcangelo’s Specialties:

We have success working with companies to protect information, prevent breaches and engage their employees in security awareness through innovative and simple approaches that anyone can (and should) apply.
