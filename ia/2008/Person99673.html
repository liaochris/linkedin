Jude Sweeney’s Summary

I preside over a staff of 26 professionals engaged in industrial asset management, and have managed the operations since 2002. Our revenue has grown from $11 Million in 2003 to $48.5 Million in 2007, while increasing our operations to three facilities. We have developed hiring procedures to find and develop the best employees, and we utilize metrics to continuously improve upon our quality and delivery. We continue to look for means to streamline our operations and reduce our costs wherever possible, while providing superior quality to maintain customer loyalty.

In my career, I have progressed in positions and responsibility where I have been able to streamline operations, reduce cost and increase profitability. I have provided leadership and vision to our employees generating long term customer satisfaction and loyalty.
