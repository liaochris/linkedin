Tom Hughes’s Summary

I am an attorney focusing on advertising and marketing law and work with some of the world’s largest corporations to develop truthful and substantiated ads and conduct national marketing campaigns. I represent clients before the Federal Trade Commission, state Attorneys General, and National Advertising Division, as well as in federal lawsuits under the Lanham Act.
