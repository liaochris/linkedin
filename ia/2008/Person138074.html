Nanci Schwartz’s Summary

Nanci Schwartz is a seasoned professional who offers clients comprehensive and far-reaching communications strategy.

Her 20-plus year background in broadcast news reporting and producing, public and media relations, political and crisis communications and event planning provides the expertise for creating the big picture strategy clients need to maximize their key messages to customers, employees, media, partners and other important audiences.

She has been recognized internally and externally for leadership and innovation that has generated positive exposure for her clients.
