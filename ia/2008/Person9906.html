Amy Powell’s Summary

Hi, I’m Amy…
a wife and a mom of two beautiful boys. In order to balance both of my passions: to be home with my children and have a successful career in the travel industry, I've started my own home based agency, AmyMeli Travel.

My career started in 1998 at a SCUBA shop planning dive trips for both individuals and groups. After a few years I moved to a full service agency for the remainder of my 10 years experience (so far), booking reservations from corporate travel to honeymoons to groups and tours.

I am a full service travel agent, specializing in personal attention. I am dedicated to helping you, the savvy traveler, keep up to date with the latest travel tips and tricks available – all the way from the excitement of planning your trip, through to your ride home from the airport. Let’s ensure that when you turn the key to your front door you won’t feel the need for another vacation from your vacation!
