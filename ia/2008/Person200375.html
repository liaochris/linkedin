Wil Radcliffe’s Summary

I am the creator and writer of several comic books, all slated to be adapted into other media.

In addition, I have 8 years of experience editing and producing television commercials, infomercials, and programming for regional and national markets.

A new edition of my young adult fantasy novel, Noggle Stones, is set to be published by Dailey Swan Publishing in March of 2008. Noggle Stones has received glowing endorsements from such professionals as Ernie Colon (The 9/11 Report), P.C. Hodgell (The Godstalk series), and Barry Cunningham (the editor who discovered J.K. Rowling.)

Chamein Canton of the Canton Smith Agency is representing Noggle Stones for publishing, film, and television.

I am seeking freelance opportunities in comics, film, television, and other media.

Wil Radcliffe’s Specialties:

I have experience in writing comedy (Cracked Magazine), horror (Platinum Studios), and fantasy (Angel Gate Press).

I have also written award-winning radio and television commercials, as well as long form infomercials and corporate video.

In addition, I am trained and experienced in writing screenplays, comic book scripts, novels, short stories, columns, and articles.

  • Freelance Creator/Writer

    Self-employed

    (Self-Employed; Myself Only; Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    October 1993 — Present (14 years 9 months)

    I have been a freelance writer since 1993, when I began writing scripts for local video production companies. Since then I have sold my writing to "Marion Zimmer Bradley's Fantasy Magazine," "Cracked Magazine," and "Comics Buyer's Guide" among others.

    In 2003 I published my fantasy novel, Noggle Stones. A new edition of the novel with added content is set to be published in March, 2008 by Dailey Swan Publishing. For more information about Noggle Stones, visit www.nogglestones.com

    I have created four graphic novels for Platinum Studios, which are set to be adapted into live action films. You can read about them here - www.nogglestones.com/hollywood.jpg.

    I am also the creator/writer of "The Giant of Killarney," a four-issue comic book mini-series published by Angel Gate Press. Ben Glendenning is providing the pencils and inks, while Dave Lanphear is doing letters.

  • Production Manager

    Q-1 Video Network

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Media Production industry)

    March 1998 — August 2006 (8 years 6 months)

    I started with Q-1 Video in 1996 as a freelance scriptwriter. I joined the staff full-time in 1998 as a video editor, and assumed the position of production manager in 2000. In that capacity, I produced national programs and infomercials for such clients as Ted Nugent, Ray Cook Golf, Crest Pontoon Boats, and the Catholic Church.

  • Head Copywriter

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Broadcast Media industry)

    November 1994 — March 1998 (3 years 5 months)

    While at Lake Cities I acted as a Copywriter/Producer/Announcer. I wrote the majority of the commercials, station promotional material, and entertainment pieces for five radio stations. I also produced and announced several commercials, including some award-winning spots. In addition, I was the on-air morning show personality "Professor Friday," providing humorous news bits and trivia contests.
