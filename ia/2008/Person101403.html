John Yuiska’s Summary

A proven marketing and creative professional with management experience in digital media, and broadcast media sectors. Expertise in branding, ROI web design, multi-player online games, broadcast design/promotion, usability, and producing digital experiences that drive results. Demonstrated proficiency in managing high volume projects with evolving client needs.

HIGHLIGHTS:
Re-branding: Designed and directed Tacit software’s web site and sales collateral. Managed local team to build site and content management system for client.

ROI Design: Created and produced persona strategies to increase traffic and conversion rates on Atlantis.com. Also redesigned transactional pages to address user’s needs.

Web Design: Redesigned and produced refresh designs for Johnson & Johnson oral brand web sites. Produced The Cove Atlantis launch web site with viral video designed and produced by Exopolis for acquisition strategy.

Online Games: Created and managed development team in Vienna to produce multi-player flash game for TV Land.com. Project included in game chat for all players and a rich content management system for the client to manage promotions and content based on licensing agreements.

Broadcast Design and Promotion: Negotiated, developed and managed multidisciplinary design team to produce promotion package for 9 hours of programming on Cartoon Network. Also produced numerous promos and show packages for MTV, VH1, CMT, and HBO

Commercial Production: Created and produced ESPN Games opening animation for all commercial end tags and game discs.

Music Videos: Shot, directed and produced various music videos and promotional spots for emerging hip hop and indy artists.
