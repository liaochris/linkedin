Suzanne K. Nusbaum’s Summary

Suzanne K. Nusbaum brings a wealth of experience to her fulltime neutral ADR practice. A seasoned attorney and former judge with specialized expertise in complex medical and intellectual property matters, she serves on many ADR panels, resolving disputes throughout the United States and internationally.

In her decade and a half as an Administrative Law Judge, she presided over many complex medical and legal disputes, at both the trial and appellate levels. Her judicial excellence has been acknowledged by public nomination, confirmation and reappointment in two states.

Recent experience as an arbitrator, mediator and settlement conference neutral includes whistleblower, medical malpractice, breach of contract, securities unsuitability, and fraud cases.

Experience as a litigator includes trade secret, patent infringement, and breach of joint development contract cases. Her specialties of intellectual property and medical law are founded on a broad base of civil and criminal law experience. Prior to the bench, she managed a private law practice and served as a state’s attorney prosecuting criminal cases.
