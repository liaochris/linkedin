Rasmus Elmann Ingerslev’s Summary

Rasmus Ingerslev, 34, is a fitness industry entrepreneur and is currently setting up a new company called Wexer which will offer online fitness services. He is the co-founder of DFHO (Danish Fitness & Health Organization) and fitness dk, the leading Danish fitness chain, where he has served as marketing director and CEO.

Rasmus was awarded the IHRSA (www.ihrsa.org) European Club Leadership award in 2007 and was pointed out as one of Denmark’s top 10 CEO’s under the age of 35 the same year. In 2008 Rasmus was a top five nominee for the Danish ”Leader of the Year” award given by the organization Lederne (www.lederne.dk).

Within sports Rasmus has been competing on the Danish National Karate team and holds several national and international titles, and is still a very active sportsman

Rasmus was a speaker at the IHRSA Asia-Pacific Forum in Shanghai, November 2008 and has served on the boards of fitness dk, DFHO and the Danish Anti Doping Agency. Currently Rasmus is a member of IHRSA's European Council.
