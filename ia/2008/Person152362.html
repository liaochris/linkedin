Richard Williamson’s Summary

Past executive in Sales, Marketing & Customer Service Industries for Technical, Scientific, and Bio-tech products.

Self-employed Entrepreneur since 1994 working in Costume, Clothing, Lingerie, Entertainment, Marketing & promotional fields helping companies create brand identities such as corporate mascots, private label brands, develop niche products and markets, and launch major client events and products.

Also involved in TV, film, theatre and other aspects of production as it relates to costume. Very versatile and able to adapt fast paced environments. Well known to be creative and to think seriously outside the box!

Owns and runs several large e-commerce companies. Has extensive contacts in manufacturing, distribution, and other networks as it relates to apparel and lingerie.

  • President & Owner

    Planetcostume.com - Multi domain E-tailers servicing the costume, lingerie, & adult industries

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Apparel & Fashion industry)

    Planetcostume was established in 1997.   The owner saw a change in the way that consumers were buying their Costumes and Santa suits. He started a company to meet those new types of needs and to better meet the rapid paced internet environment.

    Planetcostume has 2 separate locations in the Philadelphia area. The larger of the two locations is over 10,000 square feet, has warehousing, manufacturing, offices, and design facilities in the building. Planetcostume creates some very unique items for the Christmas and costume industries.

  • President & Owner

    Planetsanta.com

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Apparel & Fashion industry)

    1997 — Present (11 years)

    Planetsanta.com allows consumers and retailers directly order Santa Suits, Santa Beards and Wigs & many related accessories around the clock. The enormous stock that is maintained is really hard to imagine. For example, Planetcostume is one of the largest purchasers of Santa wigs & beards in the USA!

    Planetsanta will manufacture unique designs, distribute items it thinks are creative, and also assist smaller manufacturers in bringing goods to market.     Planetsanta also operates many other Santa Claus web sites including Santa Claus Bar crawl sites (such as barcrawlsanta.com) to specialize in the niche markets of the Santa Bar crawls. Planetsanta Prides itself that it has made $5,000 Santa suits for the white house and also sold $29 suits to college students for parties too.

  • President & Owner

    Pierre's Costumes - Rental, Sales & Manufacturing of Unique Costumes and Mascots Since 1943

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Apparel & Fashion industry)

    September 1994 — Present (13 years 9 months)

    Pierre’s Costumes was founded in 1943. Over the past 25 years, Pierre's has acquired costumes and stock from over 20 other historic large high end Costume Shops who were retiring.

    During the last five decades Pierre has maintained a complete full service professional costume house, designing and manufacturing on the premises.

    Pierre services professional and semi-professional theatrical productions, national theatre tours, school & college theatres, motion pictures, advertising agencies, party planners, production companies, professional sports teams, print & TV commercials, major corporations, televised parades, theme parks, and costume shops throughout the world.

    Pierre's has well over 1,000,000 different costumes to chose from, making it by far one of the largest costume shops in the United States.

    Pierre's also continues it's long history of creating and designing original mascots for Corporations, Sports Teams, Theme Parks, Colleges, Schools, parades and more.
