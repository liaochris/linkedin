Rob Martens’s Summary

An accomplished and internationally experienced procurement executive with a successful track record of change management and cost reductiont, specialized in creating and turning around purchasing and supply functions to align them to corporate strategy, bringing substantial results. Set up and expanded and international procurement office in India. Lead many complex negotiations in an international environment.

Experience in the Oil & Gas, automotive, construction, engineering, logistics, real estate and IT industries. Interested in academic work and best practices.

Rob Martens’s Specialties:

Strengths in total quality management, project management, change management, complex negotiations, operating in different cultures, outsourcing, low cost country sourcing, strategic planning, finance, six sigma. Experience with ERP systems including Oracle and PeopleSoft. Executive MBA from Keele University and MSc in Procurement from Glamorgan University.
