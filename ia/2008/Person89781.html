Jerrold Foutz’s Summary

SMPS Technology was founded in April 1981 as Foutz Engineering and changed its name to SMPS Technology in 1983. Power supply circuit design and application has been the company focus since the beginning. The company presently is in the business of providing content to power supply circuit designers via the World-Wide Web. This information is provided in a unique problem solving format based on a Transactional Analysis model of problem solving. Previously our key strengths were in power electronics consulting (technical and management) and in creating and marketing a problem-oriented data base of switching-mode power supply design literature. The company is based on the 48 years of power supply consulting, management and technical experience of its owner, Jerrold Foutz.

    September 1980 — April 1981 (8 months)

    Served as vice president of engineering for Almond Instrument Company, a custom power supply company with $5 Million a year sales, during its acquisition by EG&G, a Fortune 500 company (now the EG&G Division of URS). Introduced written procedures, formal design reviews, and improved testing methods to eliminate redesign due to engineering errors. Improved competitive posture by strengthening the engineering staff and initiating needed R&D.
