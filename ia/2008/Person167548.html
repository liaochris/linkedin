Sally Jones’s Summary

Creative Content Executive with nearly 20 years in publishing, the last 10 years spent overseeing strategy, direction, content development, and brand extension of consumer websites and online products. Experienced packager of women’s service content for the online audience. Seasoned top-editor, well-versed in product development, including repurposing, acquiring, licensing, assigning, editing, and syndicating content in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment. Adept manager, with experience supervising diverse teams of editors and producers and delivering results on time and on budget.

Sally Jones’s Specialties:

strategizing and launching new websites, packaging content for syndication partnerships, managing diverse teams of editors and producers
