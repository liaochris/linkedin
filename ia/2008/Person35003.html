Chris Butler’s Summary

As a professional recruiter I am keen to hear from those professionals who take their staffing and talent management issues seriously. I'm keen to hear from those professionals who feel I might be able to add value to their organisation in any of the following roles:

- People Management
- Recruitment and Staffing
- Talent Management
- Sales and Business Development
- Training and Learning and Development

With nine years in professional recruitment and executive search I have specialised in the sourcing of top tier purchasing and supply chain professionals across the UK.

My diverse client portfolio covered FMCG, Retail and Manufacturing and I have been retained to identify impact players as well as manage high value key accounts.

I have been responsible for the brand development of the Supply Chain and Procurement areas with other top recruitment organisations and have team management experience.

I am also an effective trainer and coach, and am keen to develop my next role to encompass these skills.

I am keen to network with Procurement and Supply Chain professionals who are looking to strengthen their own teams or who are tentatively looking for their next career move. I am also looking to develop my network with HR professionals and obviously other professional recruiters.
