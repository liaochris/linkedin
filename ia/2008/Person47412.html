David Maher Roberts’s Summary

Entrepreneurial and creative media executive with over 15 years of senior commercial experience in both new media and traditional media environments. Proven track record in identifying and developing business opportunities, taking them from concept to successful delivery.

I achieve by focusing on developing clear strategic plans through a natural ability to communicate at all levels. Enthusiastic and energetic personal style. Over 10 years of commercial P&L responsibility. Five years of UK Board experience within a PLC. At The Filter, I now operate within an Angel and VC-funded start-up.

• Leadership - I establish a clear vision for everything I do and always bring people with me.
• Strategic planning & implementation - recognised by peers as having a strategic mind and has a proven track record of implementing successful strategies.
• Commercial - proven track record in product management and marketing at both strategic and tactical levels.
• I understand and deliver on P&L responsibility - have had over 10 years of managing at P&L level within private, VC-funded MBO and PLC environments.
• Communication - a natural communicator, enabling vision and strategy to be successfully implemented. Can adapt communication style/method to suit.
• People skills - gets the most out of people by being clear about role and purpose,
what is expected and giving people the space in which to excel.
• Technological mind - I understand how technology works at a conceptual and
architectural level. Very comfortable working within IT and web tech environments.
• I learn fast and use my new knowledge/understanding to fine-tune strategies.
