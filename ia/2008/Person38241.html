Claudia Leurink’s Summary

For the last years I have been working in the field of (online) marketing, mostly in the media sector. Through my hands-on attitude and my pragmatic and practical way of working I intend to think and work out of the box in every project. And more so; I try to create an atmosphere in which new ideas and concepts evolve.

Because of my partnership in Yuca Media, I can now not only consult businesses on how to interact with their customers or clients.
