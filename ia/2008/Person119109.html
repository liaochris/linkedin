Manuel Breschi, Ph.D. Eng.’s Summary

Several years of international CRM consultancy have consolidated Manuel Breschi's experiences in various businesses (Pharmaceutical, eServices, Telecommunication, Banking & Security, Travel & Leisure, etc.) and roles (IT Project Manager, Business Analyst, Team Leader, System Architect). Starting as a developer, he has acquired an overall software development life-cycle and engagement management perspective, based on a high-level knowledge and understanding of CRM/PRM application architectures. His platforms knowledge embraces Siebel CRM & PRM, SalesForce.com, Microsoft CRM and SAP CRM associated with direct Business Intelligence experience (Siebel Analytics, Informatica, SAS) and integrated with a general overview of ERP systems (SAP R/3, Oracle). His PM and methodologies experience (profound UML knowledge) have encouraged Manuel Breschi's attention for details, documentation and focus on quality through all the phases of implementation (from installation through requirements gathering, business analysis, processes re-engineering, system architecture, test, training and release management) together with a proven high-level ability to manage resources and interact with business stakeholders.

Manuel Breschi, Ph.D. Eng.’s Specialties:

Business analysis, UML, CRM, Siebel, Analytics, SAP CRM, Microsoft Dynamics CRM, CRM Projects scoping, analysis and platforms comparison.
