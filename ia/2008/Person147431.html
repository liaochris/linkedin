Paul Borselli’s Summary

Strategic, results-oriented marketing professional with 17 years of experience in consumer goods, consumer electronics, cable entertainment and wireless telecommunications. Trained in integrated marketing; inclusive of brand development, traditional & online advertising, direct & ethnic marketing, customer communications, product marketing and pricing, in-store merchandising, promotions, public relations and special events. Excellent verbal and written communication skills up and down internal and external organizations. International experience with companies in the Americas, EMEA and Asia.
