Hilaire Brosio’s Summary

Six years as a Camera Operator with a focus on broadcast HiDef. Material includes scripted and unscripted programming, ranging from series television to live sports to documentaries. Recent work includes Dexter (Showtime), nContrast feature on Spike Lee (BET), Votre Vu Skin Care launch in Paris (VotreVu.com), Adidas Track Classic Live Broadcast (ESPN), Clarkworld (Film Pharm) and Open House LA (NBC/LXTV).

Over 20 years of experience in the Entertainment/Music Business -- 12 years in a management role -- with the primary focus being promotions, marketing, brand management and sales.
