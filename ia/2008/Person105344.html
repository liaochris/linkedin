Kerry Kozuki’s Summary

Kerry Kozuki is a luxury boutique hotel professional with more than twelve years of experience leading notable hospitality venues in Seattle. In 2004, Mr. Kozuki was tapped to serve as hotel manager at the Inn at the WAC, the luxury hotel within the Washington Athletic Club. The WAC is America's largest private city club and a four-time winner of the Five Star Platinum Clubs of America award.

Kozuki is responsible for the oversight of the Inn at the WAC and its transition from simple club lodging to a generously-appointed, fashion-forward luxury property within a city club. In this position he has led this transition through upgrading the service signature, guiding colleagues through the integration of design and construction with the brand promise, and through evangelizing the need to leverage CRM and PMS technologies to provide a highly personalized level of service to guests and members. The Inn at the WAC is now one of the premier properties within downtown Seattle.

Prior to joining the WAC, Kozuki served as the general manager of both the MarQueen Hotel and the Inn at Queen Anne for six years, where he was successful in greatly improving bottom line results and the overall product quality. Kozuki was intimately involved in the business development and channel management disciplines at these properties to significantly improve market share.

Kerry Kozuki holds an M.B.A. from the University of Washington and a Diploma of Techology from the British Columbia Institute of Technology. He also has achieved his Six Sigma Black Belt from Villanova University and his Certified Hotel Administrator certification from the American Hotel & Lodging Association.
