Edouard Brauer’s Summary

Based in Paris , France, Edouard Brauer manage an international team that assists both privately-held and publicly-traded european and emerging market based companies in a wide variety of industries by providing them sophisticated investment banking services. This includes seed and Venture capital transeuropean mergers and acquisition advisory, corporate finance support, and raising debt and equity capital ranging in size from $1 million to $500 million.

Mr Brauer has over 15 years of experience in competitive intelligence, strategy building and business management advisory for major european Internet service Providers , mobile operators and telecom providers.

Having a inexhaustible passion for technology and business, he has advised early stage and major IT companies in corporate finance and strategy and participated to successfull multimillion IPO'S and M&A

He has a particular expertise in assisting IT clients in France , Romania, Luxemburg, and Francophonic Africa where he can deploy his diverse experience and deep network of relationships.

Mr Brauer is also is the the co-funding partner of Blue Capital Partners an advisory firm that raise and manage sustainable oriented funds and projects and he is the president founder of Terractive, a non-profit organisation dedicated to narrow the digital divide and reduce E-waste by recycling computers trough a network of non profit social organisation and training centers mainly in Africa.
