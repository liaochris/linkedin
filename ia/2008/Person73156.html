Glen Turpin’s Summary

Glen Turpin is a communications professional with more than a decade’s experience in high technology. He understands the strategic role of technology in business, and can articulate complex ideas in simple yet powerful terms that everyone can understand.

Currently serving as a Director of Communications for electronic commerce and payments leader First Data Corporation, Turpin is responsible for media relations for the company's Prepaid business unit.

Turpin’s expertise in technology marketing and product management was sharpened at industry-leading software developer Quark, where his ten-year tenure included leadership of the company’s internal, external and marketing communications as Quark redefined its vision, values, and voice. Prior to Quark, he worked in video and interactive media production in the United States and Canada.

An avid technology evangelist, Turpin is the 2008 Chair of the Technology section of the Public Relations Society of America.

Glen Turpin’s Specialties:

Strategic communications, media relations, analyst relations, marketing communications, media technology.
