Rolien Andaman Sea Club’s Summary

Somebody ever called me a Surreal Orbital Transfer Specialist. Apart from the 'surreal' I always liked that expression since i never knew what to put up as my profession. I have always enjoyed doing lots of different things. In a project kind of way. As such i have travelled the world quite extensively. I have worked in 28 countries as tourmanager for bands, tourleader for tourist groups, diving instructor and a looooong time ago even as Croupier Black Jack in the Casino of a Caribean Cruiserrr. I have always come back a few times a year to my own home city, Amsterdam. To relax in between jobs but also to do one of my favourite jobs ie working in the organisation of music events, mainly artist handling and tourmanaging and working for several tv productions.
As for now, i have found a destination where i want to stay. Together with my man i have set up business on Phuket Thailand, the Andaman Sea Club, a sailing charter company. Sailing the Andaman Sea and it's regatta's. Everything comes together here. Organising, writing, marketing, being independent, sailing and diving the Andaman Sea and explore the beaches of all the islands that surround us here. My goal? To make this business bigger, more various, green (=eco) and secure. More boats, a club and fun people around us that all like to take care of each other.
