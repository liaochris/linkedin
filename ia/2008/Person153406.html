Pratul Kant’s Summary

Latest: Consulting Systems Manager at client location in Oakland, CA.

Pratul is an Electrical Engineer and has 14 years of hands-on Systems and Network administration experience, specializing in Systems administration of RedHat Linux Enterprise Servers, Windows 2003/2000 Servers including Active Directory. He is an expert in managing online java based Applications, File and network services like Terminal Services for Windows/ DHCP/DNS/Samba/NIS/NFS/Apache/Squid, Tripwire, Tomcat Web Servers and MySQL and Oracle database servers, MySQL and Oracle Dataguard replication, nagios, Mercury Sitescope and nimbus monitoring tools. He has extensive experience in managing large corporate Local Area Networks with Fiber Gigabit backbones, communication infrastructure and maintaining 7x24 production server farm IT operations. He is expert in managing corporate Email (using MS Exchange 2003 / Sendmail, Apache James configuration) and information security using Firewalls and secure configurations like DMZ servers, VPN and Intranets. He has wide IT domain knowledge and has worked with variety of industries like Internet Technology start-ups (Speedera Networks, Schoolcity.com, Clickability.com) automobile manufacturing [Tata Motors], Finance (World Savings Bank /Wachovia)Healthcare (Neoforma, CMOS Soft) and educational institutes [Stanford University, Sacred Heart Schools]. He has supported 24x7 IT operations and NOC/Help Desk support functions all along his career.
