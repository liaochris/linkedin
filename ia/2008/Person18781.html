Chinmoy Sarkar’s Summary

With more than 20 years consulting experience, across a variety of market sectors in India, UK, Australia, South America, Chinmoy has worked as a Business Consultant / Enterprise Asset Managementn consultant with some of the world’s largest and most successful companies; his contributions have focused mainly on the areas of strategy, business transformation, cost reduction, information systems strategy, IT bsuiness architecture, reengineering and enterprise applications management.

In the advent of the Knowledge Economy, business world has witnessed drastic and radical changes in last one decade which have tremendous impact on the maturity of the professionals too. The obsession of the economy is with “innovation” as the presumed path to riches for people and organization. Since the key generator of innovative spirit is with the knowledge professionals and the key implementer of innovation is business, there is an ever-increasing demand of synchronization between human capital (professionals), structural capital (organization processes) and relationship capital (customer intimacy) to succeed the changing business scenario. The partnership has never been so relevant with constant effort from all to change the culture and bridge the gap between the needs of the business and aspirations of stakeholders. Business space has to be populated with innovative and creative mind not disconnected from the reality. This requires massive transformation of organization strategy, culture, process and technology. AXELL, his model on business transformation addresses this change scenario with the help of 49 components; each component is made up of strategy, goals, resources and capabilities.

AXELL is a business maturity model whose application can transform an organization working in the Industrial economy into a vibrant organization ready to particpate in the knowledge economy.
