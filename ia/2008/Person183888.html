Susan Johnston’s Summary

Whether writing marketing copy, a magazine article, or a blog post, I cover the issues that matter to Generation Y: money and career management, relationships, lifestyle, and entrepreneurship. My articles have appeared in The Boston Globe, DailyCandy, Self magazine, WomenEntrepreneur, and Yahoo! HotJobs, among other places.

I am also available for copywriting projects including blog posts, brochures, press releases, newsletters, and web copy. Past clients include online retailers, non-profits, and small businesses.
