Ed Schmidt’s Summary

Senior Management, Marketing, Operations and Business Development executive, specialising in Integrated, Direct and Online Marketing and Media Industries. Senior business leader with more than 13 years of high level experience in technology start-ups, new venture finance, development and operations, across IT&T, (new) media, advertising, retail, consumer products, travel, automotive and financial services. Consulted in venture start-ups across business development, change management, marketing and corporate strategy with direct exposure to AsiaPacific VC investments and global product licensing agreements. Demonstrated ability to develop and direct complex global business models and technology solutions. Strong analytic and process focused approach to management and operations, professionally trained at industry leaders in advertising, technlogy and media. Results range from cost savings and higher returns in excess of 400% to marketing response rate increases of 250% and many others. Received multiple Integrated and Online media awards for best and most effective implementation of online media.
