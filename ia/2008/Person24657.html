Bob Apollo’s Summary

I’ve been lucky enough to work with some of the most interesting people - and in some of the most interesting places - in high-tech B2B sales and marketing. Many of these organisations emerged as leaders in their markets by creating scalable business models that embodied a superior understanding of who their best customers were, and what was really important to them - and by eliminating the key sources of waste and delay in their sales and marketing processes.

We founded Inflexion-Point in 2006 in order help high-potential companies with aggressive growth ambitions build scalable businesses. We take a systematic approach, and we’ve seen clients achieve great focus and clarity of purpose and deliver striking results through aligning their whole organisation around a superior ability to identify their most promising customers and relate to their most pressing challenges - and by diagnosing and dealing with the constraints that may be preventing their prospects from addressing them.

We've found that the principles can be successfully applied to almost any complex B2B sales environment. 9 out of 10 high-potential high-tech companies have substantial room for improvement in eliminating the common causes of delay and waste from their sales and marketing activities. It's highly likely we can help you do the same, and enable you to get focused, improve your performance and achieve tangible results quickly.
