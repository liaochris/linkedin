Spencer Turner’s Summary

I am the owner of a small residential handyman service. My educational background is in art and literature and I have a particular fascination with aesthetics. I have worked in secondary schools in the U.S., Brazil, and Peru as a Foreign Language (Spanish), Language Arts and History teacher, as well as taking on administrative roles such as English Head of Department and International Baccalaureate Coordinator. I have also served as an Assistant U.S. University Counselor.

Although my resume speaks loudly of my experience as an educator, I have worked in carpentry and landscaping for years. I have a passion for homes and yards and find the aesthetic of space in that context highly interesting. I am a self-motivated learner by nature and, with the help of skilled family and friends, have learned to do all manner of home renovation and garden care.

I feel that the greatest movement in life is toward knowledge and there is no knowledge too inane or obscure for serious consideration.
