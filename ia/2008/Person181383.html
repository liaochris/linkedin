Steve Grant’s Summary

WHAT I DO!

* run an independent recruitment consultancy operating in the Education, Finance, IT and Sales sectors www.acornps.com

* run the UK State Schools Information Service - see website for more details www.ukschoolsinformation.com

* offer consultancy services on selling any product ( from software to chairs! ) into the Education market

* sell the product for you if I feel it's going to benefit the school, teachers and students

* offer myself as a Presenter, Trainer and Facilitator - I have significant 'training' experience

Former secondary Deputy and Acting Headteacher, English Teacher originally, also experienced in Education Recruitment, now running a Recruitment, Careers Coaching and Training business - Acorn Professional Services. Acorn recruits in the Education, Finance, IT and Sales sectors, across the whole of the UK. www.acornps.com

I have also established the UK State Schools Information Service, a subscription based service for parents ( and students ) and schools, designed to do for the state sector what ISIS ( Independent Schools Information Service ) does for the Independent sector. See website for further information and to subscribe! www.ukschoolsinformation.com

If you are looking to launch or sell a product into the public sector market, and into Education in particular then I can help you on a freelance, agency basis.

Interests are very wide and varied - Literature, Music, The Arts, Historic Places, Sport ( especially Football ), Travel, Social issues, Philosophy, Politics, Business, Technology ( esp ICT ). I also write poetry and have been published; examples available on request!

Inactive member of MENSA, the society for the ( supposedly! ) highly intelligent.

If you share my business interests, goals or personal interests, or for whatever reason, please make contact.
