Ron Barber’s Summary

Methodical, organized and detailed, quality conscious management professional with 4 years of Office Management, 11 years of Facilities Management to include multiple construction projects, 10 years of Real Estate sales and management, and 14 years of Transportation Industry experience.

Team player with special attention to developing and implementing cost effective processes. Strong skills in the area of Facilities Management and Vendor Relations as a manager of clerical, maintenance staff, professional and operations staff and Process Management to include analyzing and implementing new procedures to maximize cost efficiency and contract negotiations.
