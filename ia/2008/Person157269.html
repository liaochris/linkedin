Ravindra Ramouthar’s Summary

With a strong academic track record that spans an NHD in Electronic Engineering, Bcompt from Unisa, Project Management from X-Pert, a Post Graduate Diploma in Strategic Business IT from Portsmouth (UK), Master of Science in Strategic Business IT from Portsmouth (UK), Microsoft MCSD, MCAD and a current Ph.d in Artificial Intelligence, working across the entire IT spectrum, including key leadership and management positions with well-known firms and brands such as The Central Energy Fund, The CSIR, The South African Government, Microsoft, Anglo American, Anglo Platinum and several Fortune 500 companies such as Royal Dutch Shell, British Telecom and Merrill Lynch, IBM and Tata Consulting. My experience encompasses growth through such positions as Sales Executive, Senior Software Developer, Chief Software Architect, Head of IT, Advisor, Founder, CEO, CTO, Director: Technology, Chairman and President of both companies and IT-Startups. Entrepreneurial by nature, I also have more than a decade of experience in start-ups (a construction company –revenues of R20 Million pa, an electronic component company –revenues of R5 million pa, a security company –revenues of R6 million pa and three IT Startups –potential global revenues of $+10 Billion), turnaround management and technology management, this evidenced by the successful startup of three IT companies which were successfully sold to software houses in the United States. Currently operating as the Head of Technology, Innovation and Research and Development for Corporate commercial ICT and Industrial ICT (Mining and Processing), I have estabished the Group ICT Applied R&D LAB, a research and development capability for Anglo Platinum ICT R&D.Architecting and developing software for over 15 years, my portfolio involves the evolution from developer to managing the development of end to end multi-tier, mission-critical, and services oriented distributed web applications.
