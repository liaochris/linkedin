Felix Arias’s Summary

Has held positions of responsibility in Technical, Sales and Strategic Planning areas in Telefónica, PESA, Interactive Television and Menta.

Was Director of the winning bid dossier presented by Barcelona to hold the 1992 Olympic Games.

Acted as consultant in companies as Bédaux (Industrial Engineering), Arthur Andersen (Management Information Consulting Division) and Facts & Figures (Project Management).

Has been teaching IT matters in ESADE (Business School) for more than 20 years.

He is an Industrial Engineer from the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya and a CPA (Censors Jurats de Comptes de Catalunya).
