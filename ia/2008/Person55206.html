Dulcimer Brubaker’s Summary

I am a writer, editor, and proofreader who has worked with both print and online media, generating content and following through until final publication, for over three years. I love working with graphic designers to create fresh, effective communications, and I especially enjoy writing and editing material that serves as a catalyst to thoughtful discussions.

Dulcimer Brubaker’s Specialties:

Writing and editing for the web, magazine writing, familiar with AP style. Also proficient in basic web edits in Dreamweaver, and can find my way around InDesign.
