Laura Zeigen’s Summary

My primary experience has been in libraries and computer support, including 8+ years of teaching experience helping others to understand and work with computers and their Web sites. I pride myself in being able to present sometimes challenging technical information to a broad cross-section of people in a humorous, energetic and engaging way. I have no fear of speaking before large audiences (and love it, in fact!).

Currently I am in the process of expanding my skills into multimedia and e-learning/distance learning.

Laura Zeigen’s Specialties:

I have focused my library work in the areas of web development (including web usability assessment), training, and systems support, including support for ILS systems.
