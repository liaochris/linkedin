Lonnie Mesick’s Summary

Lonnie has over 20 years of diverse Information Technology experience in a variety of industries including: energy, not-for-profit, banking, transportation, and manufacturing. He has been a CTO and Senior Technology Executive for over twenty years in global and regional organizations. Lonnie's key capabilities and extensive leadership experiences include: aligning technology functions with business strategies, writing and deploying technology/business strategic and tactical plans, improving the return on investment for technology investments, implementation of Technology Governance functions at the Board of Director's level, management of large technology projects and the implementation of Project Management Office processes and standards, and, implementation of System Development Methodologies for custom developed and vendor-supplied software
