Andrew McFadyen’s Summary

I have over 30 years experience in IT having worked as an independent consultant for 10 years, before that I was with Oracle for 10 years and Scicon also for 10 years. In that time I've worked in Asia for over 10 years as well as throughout Europe and in USA, and of course in the UK.

I've worked both as a consultancy manager, and as a hands on practitioner. And, you know, in the end I prefer be the guy who does the consulting rather than the guy who manages the guy who does the consulting. I'm the best data modeller I know, and I'm happiest working in Asia or Europe designing data warehouses or Enterprise Information Models, especially for Telecoms companies

Andrew McFadyen’s Specialties:

Date warehousing in telecommunications and other industries, enterprise data modelling (including TMF's SID and IBM's IAA models), enterprise architecture, IT consultancy and consultancy management.
