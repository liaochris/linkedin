Lawrence Ohlman III’s Summary

Six years ago I returned home from attending Arizona State University and began my involvement in my family's business, a fifth generation commercial greenhouse operation located in Toledo, Ohio. Through this position I learned the intricacies of running a business, which developed my aspirations involve someday running my own successful endeavor. I am currently an MBA candidate at University of Toledo and taking steps toward developing a new venture focused on creating everyday products with en environmentally conscious appeal , EcoLogic LLC (http://www.ecologicproducts.com) .
