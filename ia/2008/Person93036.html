Joan Farley Nyobe’s Summary

Farley's Legal Support Services includes but is not limited to Litigation support for attorneys, Criminal Defense Investigations: interview witnesses, take statements, conduct background investigations, locate missing witnesses,photograph crime scenes, take measurements of crime scenes and other distances, draw crime scene diagrams, prepare reports for attorneys, serve subpoenas and testify in court as needed. Child Abuse and Neglect Investigations: Locate missing parents and or caretakers, inspect homes, photograph living conditions, document inconsistencies, take statements from people pertinent to the neglect issues before the court, prepare reports for attorneys, prepare subpoenas, serve subpoenas, provide transportation if necessary, and testify in court if needed. Landlord and Tenant Cases:Post and Serve Eviction Notices, file return service notices in court...report to attorney and testify.
