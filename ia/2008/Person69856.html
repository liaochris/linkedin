  • Design direction and consultant

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    October 2007 — February 2008 (5 months)

    4 months working in a start-up environment developing branding architecture and user experience for a socially-conscious entertainment and lifestyle video site backed by founders of Skype, lastminute.com and Microsoft.   • User Experience Design

    Skype

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; EBAY; Internet industry)

    October 2005 — September 2007 (2 years)

    Worked on global online marketing initiatives. Developed complex directory listings and community applications, both for the web and the skype client. Worked on the re-architecture of the Skype website including interaction models, brand execution and design language, with much of the work based on analytics and user research, tied in with focused business requirements. Worked with some of the most talented and inspiring people one could wish to work with, good times.

  • Senior User Experience Designer

    BBC

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; BBC; Broadcast Media industry)

    November 1998 — November 2004 (6 years 1 month)

    1998 – 2004 BBC New Media.
    Design strategy, design direction. Helping with adoption of user centred design approaches within the BBC and mentored designers, developers and producers alike in this methodology developing a flexible, agile approach to designing user interaction across the BBC.

    Helped develop the bbc.co.uk’s global navigation, homepage and ‘top level’ architecture driven by my interest in creating appropriate and engaging emotional associations between user, product and company.

    Managed: both small and large design teams as design/creative lead and line-manager. Provided art direction to designers on short- and long-term projects. It was a lot of fun.

  • Web Designer

    Webmedia

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    October 1994 — January 1997 (2 years 4 months)

    Joined Webmedia in its first year, a new company, designing this new thing "web sites". A company described by some as the "seminal web agency of mid- 90s Internet-crazed London" (Mike Butcher). We had some big clients and for everyone of them it was their first foray in to the web world: Alliance & Leicester, Nationwide, Lloyds TSB, Lufthansa, and Bodyshop, among others.
