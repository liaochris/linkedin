Mitzi Szereto’s Summary

As a writer of short stories and long-form fiction and an editor of my own concept anthologies, I am continuing to seek out new publishing opportunities in the book world. Although the majority of my work has been in the area of literary erotica, I have become increasingly more multi-genre, crossing over into mainstream fiction, crime, romance, and non-fiction. Further to this, I have a substantial international public profile, with numerous appearances on radio, television, and in newspapers and magazines. I have performed readings of my work from Los Angeles to London.

My goals are to continue to write and create quality books as well as to expand my horizons in the media, furthering my appearances on television/radio in the form of presenting/hosting.

I am a US citizen and also have UK residency.
