Eric Stein’s Summary

Acquisitions & business development executive with extensive experience in all facets of new media content acquisition (music, film and television).

Over 8 years of experience, building and maintaining video libraries with hundreds of content licensors representing thousands of film and TV programs. Acquire with variety of web-based New Media purchase models: Ad supported, MOD, PPV, EST (Electronic Sell Thru) and subscription.

Bridging the divide between new media technology solutions and traditional production & distribution companies.

Program channels based on consumer demand including independent films (Sundance), music videos (3DD), sports (MLB), television (Endemol/Granada) and high-definition content (HDNET). Also, handle international acquisitions with concentration on Japan.

Attend all major film and television markets, with over six years experience at MIP, MIPCOM, AFM, NATPE, Home Media Expo and the Cannes Film Market, to develop and sustain relationships between new media distribution platforms and the film & television community, independent filmmakers, major studios, producers’ representatives, film festivals, studio professionals and other third-party partners.

Eric Stein’s Specialties:

License and contractual negotiations, international acquisitions, new media distribution, IPVOD, Internet based Video on Demand, video and audio content acquisition, content library research, manufacture on demand (MOD) licensing
