Jesse Kaczmarek’s Summary

Jesse Kaczmarek is a designer / art director currently based on the west coast—by way of New York. Originally hailing from Minnesota, he links his creative roots to early years of skating and snowboarding. The individuality and self-expression inherent to this culture became a natural stage for developing a creative voice.

Experience has allowed Jesse to refine a clean and graphic aesthetic—fused with illustration and type. While his capabilities expand across identity, print, packaging, retail, typography, and writing in third-person narrative; he explores and considers every facet of a brand. From this palette every project starts at a fresh vantage, free from stock styles and trends.

Although Jesse’s reserved and modest demeanor understates his ambition, his work has been recognized with praise by clients and rewarded honors from industry peers.
