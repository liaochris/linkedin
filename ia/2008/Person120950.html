Marieke Evertsen’s Summary

After 10 years in IT, working in various positions ranging from system engineer to European Data Centre Manager, it was time to make a complete career change. I have decided to go back to my roots and am now the owner of my own travel agency.

Even though the tourism industry differs from IT, my main goals and challenges have not changed. I am still about delivering the best service possible, except now this means making sure people (either being it a family, group of friends, business, etc.) are having the best vacation or other travel related time that they can remember.

In my newly started business, I will be able to further develop my communication skills, be inventive and learn all there is to know about owning one's own business.
