Jeff Ashcroft’s Summary

Over twenty years of experience in logistics and supply chain management from the warehouse floor to the boardrooms of major third party and retail organizations has prepared me to lead the Supply Chain Network Project. The SCN Project will act as a catalyst allowing me to facilitate companies in adopting emerging technologies such as RFID / EPC, GPS, Electronic Catalogues, Dashboards and IP / Database communications in unison resulting in delivery of a true future state supply chain through the co-ordination of these technologies with a set of predefined SCN Process Requirements. Member RFID Tribe and SCN

Jeff Ashcroft’s Specialties:

Logistics and supply chain operations, strategy, optimization, transportation, warehousing, distribution, systems, negotiation, integration, process redesign, micro / macro change management, pilot projects, rollout definition and facilitation, project definition and management, sales, marketing and business development, web/internet and communications strategy, automatic identification and systems integration, data cleansing and data synchronization, professional writing and presentation skills
