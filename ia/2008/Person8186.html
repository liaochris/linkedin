Aloke Bajpai’s Summary

Active in travel technology for nearly 7 years, in managerial, operational, and technical roles. Have worked closely with GDSs, Travel Technology providers, Aggregators, Cruiselines, Consolidators and Travel Agents.

In 2006, I founded iXiGO with ex-colleagues from Amadeus, to provide "travel 2.0" search to the Indian market. www.iXiGO.com was launched in June 2007 and is on track to become India's biggest travel search engine.
