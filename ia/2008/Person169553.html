Sarah Silverman’s Summary

I have a wide experience within the marketing function. I began my carreer in a broad marketing communication role at a mid-sized manufacturer. I was exposed to everything from coordinating a print campaign to writing press releases to designing & copy writing for a website.

As I moved on to an online health community based on providing emotional support during serious illness, I started to gain a specialization within the world of Web 2.0 and the role of the consumer within the marketing game. I began building expertise specifically in Word of Mouth Marketing & Consumer Community Development. This has expanded and developed as I have moved onto other ventures.

My future goals are to keep expanding and specializing my expertise around interaction with the consumer in the context of marketing. I hope to keep pushing the envelope and finding ways to engage the consumer and/or other constituents in ways never imagined before.
