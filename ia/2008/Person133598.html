Miss Kitty Kat’s Summary

I have done and held many hats in many fields of expertise. Everything from cook, retail, server, bouncer, music booking & production, band management, hairstyling & coloring, running a internet dating site, attempting at starting a non-profit, many office positions from secretary to vice-president.

I am one of those people who just learns as she goes and is a "Jill of all trades". I have no fear when it comes to learning new things. I actually thrive on it. I love public speaking and have spoken to crowds of over 500 people. I have been interviewed in newspapers, magazines and TV shows. I have even be on a few TV shows.

I love to assist people in anyway I am able to within my circle of people. I believe in 6 degrees of separation so you never know where you might get your next turn in the road of life. I believe we all are brought together for reasons whether small or large.

My Motto is from the Movie and Play Auntie Mame: "Live, Live, Live!!!"

Miss Kitty Kat’s Specialties:

Computers, Internet, basic office machines. Networking. Great people skills. Public Speaking. Financial & booking keeping skills. Sales. Organization. Eye for color & design. Creative persona and problem solver. I have a great way of keeping the peace among multiple personalities in a group environment. I am the one everyone goes to for advice or networking. The People Person=ME

There really is not anything I am not able to do but more a choice of what I wish to do.
