Kristin Lee’s Summary

I am a seasoned design professional with over 15 years of experience, most recently in branding. Major accounts included: Mazola, Weber Grill, Spice Islands, Tone's, Tesco, Des Moines Register, Anything Automotive, Karo Syrup, Fleischmann's Yeast, and others.

I am experienced in many levels of design including print (advertising, collateral, identity, newspaper, magazine, direct mail, etc.), in-store and trade show display, promotional materials, photography and image editing, branding strategy and collateral, email blast and some web and flash design. I have extensive experience in vendor and client relations and pre-press troubleshooting. I am also an expert level user in all Adobe and most other design programs (both Mac and PC based), as well as beginner Mac IT expertise. I am currently taking Apple.com's online class for Mac/Apple IT certification.
