Rajeev Jain’s Summary

Experienced modeler with demonstrated ability to properly frame business problems and create consensus-driven quantitative solutions.

Domains of my past experience and continuing interest are:

- financial (risk management, deal valuation, contract option valuation)
- data-driven identification/elaboration of corporate strategy (e.g. linking qualitative strategy to ERP data sources, mining large datasets, data imputation and outlier identification with noisy and incomplete data)
- marketing analytics (segmentation, database marketing, marketing ROI improvement, pricing, market forecasting)
- business process design and control (DMAIC, statistical analyses)
- general business decisions (e.g. optimizing global footprint of a multinational firm)
- environment and energy industry analyses (coal and commodity markets, natural resource management)

The rare three-way combination of 1) progressive boutique consulting experience, 2) the breadth of an MBA, and 3) the depth of a PhD, helps me select and execute at an appropriate level of detail and rigor - ranging from leaving it alone, to simple insights and quick fix solutions to advancements in the state-of-the-art.

If any of the topics above interests you, please introduce yourself to me. I promise to respond to all sincere invitations.
