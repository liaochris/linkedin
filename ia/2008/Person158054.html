Gerry 'RealtyMan' Bourgeois’s Summary

Gerry Bourgeois, consistently one of the top selling Realtors in North Central Massachusetts for over 13 years, is now leading his 2nd-generation Residential Real Estate Firm in the same direction. In a down market, Gerry was able to increase Towne & Country, Realtors' closed $ volume market share by over 50% ('06-'07) while many in the area saw substatial decreases. The difference: Real leadership, strategic vision, endless research, and a unique company culture!
