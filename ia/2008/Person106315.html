Krista Haimovitch’s Summary

Certified Hypnotherapist, with a Masters degree in counseling psychology. Currently working towards a Ph.D. in psychology, and creator of TAP -- the Travel Awareness Project -- to promote and educate a new global network of "conscious travelers."

15 years previous experience as a freelance writer, editor and filmmaker now supports innovative coaching techniques designed to unlock creativity and promote forward motion towards growth-oriented, calculated risk-taking for clients. Happier, more productive choices that align ones' inner experience with both daily and once-in-a-lifetime journeys are our birthright. Leave your comfort zone. A personalized blend of hypnosis techniques, creativity brainstorming and motivational coaching leads to new enthusiasm, vitality and curiosity... Which leads to...???
