Hazem Osman’s Summary

After a solid experience in Service/Product management in tourism sector (almost 7 years), I decided to start working in pharmaceutical where I began at R&D as a CRA and then as Local Trial Manager. During that period I had the chance to discover the industry fundamentals. Later on I moved to Medical Affairs as Clinical Operations (clinical/medical advisor) where I continued managing clinical trials in addition to providing scientific opinion on marketing tools, update Medical reps., initiate strategic Investigational trials. This was my first step in combining and consolidating my clinical trial management acquired knowledge with needs of internal and external clients.

My short term goals would be: first to acquire full management skills through my MBA, second to combine all the soft and hard skills learned till now in a way to contribute actively in developing correctly the business of my company.

My mid/long term preferences would be MKTG, Sales or Business Development Divisions.
