Mauro Pietro Gandini’s Summary

Character designer in independant animation movies, supervisor of 3D modeling, Sculptor for multiple brands (Disney, Warner, Hanna & Barbera and a lot of Comic characters) winning the prize of Walt Disney artist in 2000, Illustrator and co.author of a new cute brand (liludori.com) and author of two illustration books on this brand, Teacher from 2004 in a course of Digital Design on Istituto Marangoni (international fashion school in Milan, Italy), Iconist working on different Interfaces and websites.

Clients: The Walt Disney Company, Ricordi, Istituto Marangoni, Burghy, Warner, Infinitestatue, Editions du Cafè, Tera Mata Editions, Publicis, Clementoni, Nestlé, Createmotions, Disney Interactive, Accademia Disney, Giocoplast, Hanna&Barbera, Warner, Panini Group

Mauro Pietro Gandini’s Specialties:

Character design, Historical research for animation movies, sculpture, digital illustration, experience in teaching of illustration techniques and visual analysis, iconist, humorist.
