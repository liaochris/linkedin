Dwayne Conyers’s Summary

ENTERTAINMENT INDUSTRY

Mr. Conyers is a graduate of the Warner Bros. Comedy Writers Workshop and studied screenwriting with Luigi DiFonzo at Harvard and with Michael Hague and Carl Sautter via the American Film Institute.

Dwayne’s professional writing career started with an episode of the late ABC series “Living Dolls” (starring Oscar® winner Halle Berry and "King of Queens" star Leah Remini).

His portfolio also includes multimedia writing, including training video for the White House and interactive video programming for the Ministry of Defense, Republic of Singapore.

He is Executive Producer of independent film “High Point,” raising the majority of the production budget and arranging foreign and domestic distribution opportunities. Dwacon currently has other feature projects in development.

Dwacon, Inc. is a Keiretsu corporation with a rapidly expanding international profile. Corporate headquarters are in Beverly Hills, California. The company also has operations in Texas and the Washington DC area.

TECHNOLOGY CONSULTING

Dwayne's right-brain/left-brain ambidexterity includes work in the information technology arena. He is a Systems Engineering and Technical Assistance (SETA) consultant for commercial and government customers.

Dwayne is currently on assignment at the White House, providing technical architecture design and development in addition to managing customer requirements. His specialties include Software Architecture, Knowledge Management, and Business Intelligence.

Dwayne is a practicing member of the Worldwide Institute of Software Architects and holds certifications in Microsoft and IBM/Lotus technologies.

Many of his engagements have utilized his project management skills. Dwayne utilizes traditional and agile techniques. He achieved Certified ScrumMaster status in 2006 and anticipates receiving his PMP certification in 2008.
