Shane Lundy’s Summary

Prior to being promoted to the executive team as the VP of Product Strategy for AWS Convergence Technologies Inc (WeatherBug), and acting GM of the SponsorSelect business unit, my title was the Vice President of Business Development. AWS Convergence Technologies Inc is an Internet technology, sensor networking and media company that serves the public and private sectors, most notably through the WeatherBug brand. I am also the Co-founder of Reserva LaFlorida I, a real estate development company in Costa Rica focusing on raw land and urbanization projects. Prior to AWS I was the VP of Bus Dev for a small Internet startup, Net2itive, a Redmond Wa company that did not survive the bubble burst. Prior to jumping into the Internet, I created profitable businesses for 15 years in the tennis industry that focused on facility management, retail, repair services, professional placement, travel & event planning, lessons and camps.

My strengths include the following . . .

• Highly adept strategist with proven background envisioning business development solutions and leading the go-to-market strategy to grow revenues and expand market share.
• Expert analyst keenly focused upon identifying and seizing upon emerging business opportunities.
• Accomplished negotiator skilled in leveraging excellent interpersonal communication proficiencies and solutions-oriented approach to establish strong professional relationships and work closely with diverse staff and clients.
• Broad-minded leader with well-honed talents in process engineering, project management, consulting, P&L and budget administration.
