Kenneth Warner Weinberg’s Summary

Do you want help with the 15-30 (or more!) reqs that you handle simultaneously? Do you spend too much time sourcing and not enough time recruiting? We will liberate you from the day-to-day sourcing by doing the sourcing for you. We will partner with you to perform the sourcing that you do not have the time for. For the cost of one agency fee a month, We can fill more than 8 of your open job req's, with candidates that are not found on job boards, or ads in the paper, but from your competition. Our focus is finding the best quality people, faster and at lower cost.

Our efforts are concentrated on candidate identification, full behavioral/labor historical screening and profiling. As the economy continues to improve, organizations are looking for alternative ways to ramp-up talent, while managing recruitment costs and maintaining flexibility. Leveraging 70 years of workforce expertise, warner sourcing offers scalable, measurable recruitment solutions that generate a competitive advantage for our customers.

Warner Sourcing's solution applies to salaried, exempt hiring, as well as to hourly, non-exempt hiring.

* Reduction in overall recruitment costs
* Improved quality of service delivery
* Standardization of internal and external best practices
* Improved compliance with diversity goals
* Enhanced reporting for better decision making
* Retention of strategic role of HR
* Ability to accelerate organizational change
* Improved stakeholder satisfaction and productivity, including senior management, Human Resources function, hiring managers, and applicants

We’ve saved Warner Sourcing customers on average 25% in talent acquisition costs. warner sourcing has more than 40 sourcers with access to nearly 3.0 million resumes in its candidate database.
