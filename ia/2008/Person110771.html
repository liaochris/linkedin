Lars Andersen’s Summary

Lars is a serial entrepreneur with several successful exits to his name. He has more than 20 years experience in the Danish and international ICT industry, providing him with insight and network contacts that are essential to his daily decisions as a partner in SEED Capital.

Lars' domain areas include internationalization, sales, and management.

Previously, Lars was the co-founder and CEO of Columbus IT Partner A/S, with international responsibility for more than 1000 employees in 25 countries, as well as being responsible for the company’s IPO on the Copenhagen stock exchange in 1998. Since Columbus IT Partner, Lars has co-founded five other IT companies, and today is an enthusiastic and dedicated shareholder and board member. His latest achievement was the international exit of Audio Asics.

Lars has an MSc in marketing and management from Aarhus School of Economics.

Lars Andersen’s Specialties:

International management, IPO, M&A, International Sales

    Noiselimit

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Hardware industry)

    May 2006 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    Noise Limit has developed and patented the unique SilentFlux™ technology for noiseless high-end CPU cooling. Noise Limit technology offers exceedingly efficient low noise cooling systems of electronic devices at a very low cost.

    The decreased noise level is achieved through dissipating more heat with less airflow. Because airflow (specifically fan speed) is the primary source of noise in media centres, gaming PCs, and PCs in general, the reduction of airflow decreases the overall noise level dramatically.

    Noise Limit products are currently addressing the PC and home entertainment market with a scope of 60 million units on these two combined markets. Noise Limit is already gaining a first movers advantage in the development of noiseless CPU cooling technologies. Two patents have been made on the core technology and additional production specific patent application are emerging.
