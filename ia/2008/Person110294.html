Lalit Sarna’s Summary

* Proven Track record: 12 years of product development and management experience. Defined, launched and marketed several mobile platforms, applications and online products that currently support over 30 million users worldwide.

* Pioneer: Architected, developed and deployed one of the world’s first mobile video streaming platforms in 2000. Created one of the first text to multimedia transcoding systems for mobile.

* Product Management: Significant experience taking products from concept to execution. Full lifecycle experience including product inception, requirements, development, delivery and rapid iteration.

* Product Development: Full lifecycle expertise in scoping, designing, developing, deploying and supporting highly available/scalable platforms and applications.

* Usability Design, Analytics and Viral Marketing: Hands-on domain expertise with user modeling, predictive analytics, usability design, viral marketing, viral and usage loops.

* Technology & Business: Unique blend of technology and business acumen to rapidly identify capabilities of technology to solve business challenges, with a deep understanding of mobile and internet revenue models.

* Building Teams: Built several world-class teams at both startups and Fortune 500 companies. Specialize in creating cross-functional, cross-organizational, worldwide distributed teams.

* Patents and Standards: Authored 12 patents and several standards (OMA, IETF, 3GPP, MPEG) in the digital media space.
