Olivier Lemaignen’s Summary

I've spent the last decade and a half expanding on my experience in the field of direct marketing, from traditional direct mail, inserts, bangtails, outbound telemarketing and email, to DRTV, radio, newspaper and magazine ads. More recently (since 2005) I've focused on Search Engine Marketing, both paid and organic. At first, I was focused on Intuit's QuickBooks line of products. As a result of my team's success, we are now in charge of the majority of Intuit's Small Business Division's Search activity. In addition, when the time came to build a centralized Global Search Marketing practice for Intuit, I was selected to fill that role.

It's been a lot of fun so far, and I see a bright future ahead of us!
