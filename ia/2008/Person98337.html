Jos Strijbosch’s Summary

InspirIT Sales BV helps companies to perform better, to be on the right place, on the right moment and with the right offer.
Most organizations think that all aspects of sales are encompassed in one sales person but in practice that is mostly not the case, therefore causing holes in the whole sales process The Sales Department cannot meet expectations resulting in discontinuity in your organization.
One Sales person can find new leads, but finds difficulty in closing the Deal, another can be a good
Account Manager but cannot make the first contacts. Often it is the lack of experience in the different aspects of the sales process, especially the knowledge of human characteristics, (this last being of prime importance) when one has to assess the situation and make a Go/No Go decision.
When we say GO it’s a go and your sales team can only focus on the right opportunities.
