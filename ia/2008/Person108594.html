Krishna Gopal’s Summary

I am responsible for development of IT services business in the Middle East, Africa and SAARC region at Tech Mahindra. I deliver services revenues of ~ USD 25 million in the region. In addition I hold the responsibility to drive Global Alliances at TechM. With Tech Mahindra focus on Telecommunications as a vertical I and my team have a good understanding of OSS - BSS and solution selling. I have deep relationships with major telecom operators in the region like Zain (MTC), Batelco, Nawras, QTel etc and have done repeat business with most of them.

While I have been with TechM for the past 8 years, my previous experience at Sify, Asian Paints and Godrej have given me a wide exposure across several industries. This has helped me in honing my ability to consult and come up with innovative solutions and practices.

Krishna Gopal’s Specialties:

Consultative selling skills, large deal structuring and contracting, good understanding of OSS and BSS, deep understanding of the Middle Eastern market and the local culture. I can speak some basic Arabic.
