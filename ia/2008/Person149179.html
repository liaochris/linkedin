Pelle T Nilsson’s Summary

Since 1985 I have worked in media in one way or another. The bigger part of my professional life has been at the evening paper Expressen. My assignments there have been varied and last I worked as Sales manager and business developer for the newspaper (2001-2005). I have also been working as a journalist (news graphics designer), art worker, market coordinator and in the advertising department – all positions at Expressen.

Between the years 1997 and 2001 I was on a “small” break from the newspaper to learn other things. During this time I was part of building Torget.se (E-commerce website), Spray.se (Web portal and Internet Service Provider), LibertySurf.se (nowadays: Tiscali.se, Web portal and Internet Service Provider).

I am a selling creator and full of ideas, quick and extremely flexible. Things get done and they do so in the correct order, organised and structured. A terrific “doer” – I deliver! Have seldom missed a deadline or “failed” in a project.
