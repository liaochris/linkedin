Margaret Ryan’s Summary

Margaret Ryan is a senior public relations strategist with a proven track record of successfully launching new products and building brands across a broad range of industries ranging from “green” playgrounds to gourmet food and wine, fashion to consumer technology. Her primary area of focus is strategic marketing campaigns that build brands and advance clients' business objectives.

Ryan has a particular expertise in media relations and a keen insight into emerging trends. She anticipates the needs of the media and works with clients to define and tell their stories in a way that will reach and be heard by the audiences, from boardrooms to the blogosphere, most important to the success of their business.

Ryan's experience includes eight years managing and executing communications strategies and programs for America Online during the company’s critical growth period. While at AOL she conceptualized, managed and implemented numerous effective national public relations campaigns that were instrumental in introducing AOL to the media and public as one of the world's most recognized brands.
