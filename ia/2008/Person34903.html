Chris Bassett’s Summary

IT professional with over 10 years of experience in the software development industry, specialising in trade processing solutions for Investment Management companies; offering a comprehensive knowledge of the widest used trade processing applications utilised globally, including the CheckFree’s TradeFlow Suite and Cor-FS’s Salerio e2e product range.

I have a comprehensive understanding of the technical and business processes used in the global investment management back-office environment in the United Kingdom, United States and other international markets, and use this to support my analysis skills. I have lead projects to install or upgrage trade management software into multi-client, multi-instrument outsource environments. I now focus on the business analysis side of my role.

I aim to apply both my business and project lead abilities to build on an efficient, formal approach to project management with strong analysis and documentation skills. Contact with clients has always been an element of my job that I feel I excel in.

I hope that I have managed to build a reputation for credibility and integrity within the trade management community
