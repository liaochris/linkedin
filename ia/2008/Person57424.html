Ethan Labowitz’s Summary

I'm on a mission...
- To bring human enterprise closer to sustainability.
- To solve the problems of the developing world with technology.
- To create technological solutions that emphasize suitability to users, to their environment, and to sustainable local manufacturability.
- To work with small businesses in setting up healthy, safe shops to produce these solutions.
- To eventually train more trainers so the process itself is sustainable.

I am a student with interests in:
- small business management
- design for manufacture
- bicycle frame construction
- machining and metal fabrication (brazing & welding)
- jig and tool design
- CAD and parametric solid modeling
- appropriate technology
- sustainable manufacturing
- human factors and ergonomics
- shop layout and safety
- urban sustainability
- vermicomposting
- sustainable living and survival skills
- traditional, sustainable nutrition and cooking
- intercultural communication and business

Ethan Labowitz’s Specialties:

creative mechanical problem solving, design for manufacture; bicycle repair and construction; mechanical drawing, CAD, and parametric solid modeling; brazing (silver and brass fillers), soldering (especially copper), MIG welding, machining
