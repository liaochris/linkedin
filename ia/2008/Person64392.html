    simple production is a New York based art organization intended to feed the imagination of any curious audience. Inspired by themes drawn from the human condition to abstract concepts, the works include—but are not limited to—live performances, videos and film. Uniting the performing and multimedia arts, the original efforts and choreographies is a collaboration of talent from diverse cultures: innovative and like-hearted artists creating something generous, authentic and meaningful around them. simple production aspires to offer the experience of something different, beautiful and touching to its public: to bring the essence of strong and moving art to your life—although some things can never be made simple.
