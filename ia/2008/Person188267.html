Thomas Morelli’s Summary

With 15 years experience as a PR and brand communications consultant, Morelli has led award-winning programs and teams for global brands, start-ups and the world’s hottest communications agencies. He is known as a highly creative reputation turn-around specialist and insightful brand marketer with a focus on integration of cross-functional teams and disciplines, synthesizing corporate messages and strategy, corporate and product positioning, and creating innovative ideas to get clients’ key messages into the marketplace.

As a strategic communications consultant, Morelli's current and past clients include Mixx, IGE (Internet Gaming Entertainment), Affinity Media, MobiTV, NFL Football RushZone, Macrovision Zero G, Iomega, InstantService, Verizon, T-Mobile, VerticalNet, Escalate, IBM Crossworlds, Placeware and QPad.

Previously, Morelli held various executive positions at Fleishman-Hillard. Acting in the roles of vice president and group director, he directed the Business-to-Business e-Commerce Business Unit at the agency’s San Francisco office with an account portfolio in excess of $2.5m. His clients included Nortel, Entrust, Ecometry, Indus, ecVision, Mainsoft and Metricom (Ricochet).

Morelli has held client management and services positions in both San Francisco and Seattle for Publicis Dialog, Text 100 and Waggener Edstrom, representing clients including Microsoft, Visio, Corbis, Starwave (now Disney), Hotels.com, Computer Associates, CVS/Pharmacy (Soma), Xerox, Samsung and Aladdin. He served as a member of the editorial board for Microsoft’s end user magazine, and was a member of the communications team that introduced Internet Explorer and Windows 95 to the world.

Morelli holds a B.A. in political science from University of Washington, and is an award-winning PRSA member. He serves on the prestigious Silver Council for the National Italian American Foundation, and has received accolades from the Washington Journalism Education Association.
