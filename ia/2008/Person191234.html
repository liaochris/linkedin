Tom Paffen’s Summary

COO and co-founder of Residex Ventures. Continued activities in 2007 under the name Capital-C Ventures.

Since our spin-out of Residex B.V.- the Private Equity Captive of Achmea- in 2003, we have already succesfully exited several portfoliocompanies and with the recent secundary buy-out to Parcom Ventures B.V. and our new, fund Parc-IT, the Residex mission has been accomplised. Our new corporate identity will better reflect our expanded mission, including raising new funds, while building on our track record in fund management. The capital C reflects our approach towards Venture Capital: more than just providing capital, bringing the benefit of extensive operational and financial experience and network to portfolio companies is a critical part of our success formula.

More info about Capital-C Ventures and our Portfoliocompanies at
www.capital-Cventures.com
