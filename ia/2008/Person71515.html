Gordon Glover’s Summary

Gordon Glover has over twenty years experience as a management and IT consultant, including ten years as a partner at Deloitte and two years experience as the VP Professional Services (senior resource and portfolio manager) at a Microsoft Gold Certified Partner. Created high performance teams.

Experience as CIO in four organizations including project turnaround and rebuilding of IT departments. Has worked with diverse organizations (financial services, health, public, real estate, professional services and manufacturing) on technology and systems plans linking business strategy and systems. Strong expertise in enterprise transformation including process redesign, business strategy, change management and financial analysis, ERP / CRM package selection, modification and implementation, and web, client server and data management application development. “C” and Board level accountability for major projects / programs. Experience includes negotiating and working with external vendors and multi-organizational and multi-jurisdictional (including international) teams and clients. Able to build relationships at all levels of organization. Experience with both single multi-functional programs and multiple concurrent IT projects including staff of over 100. Has worked with several different IT, analysis and change methodologies and is fully familiar with PMBOK. Familiar with COBIT and ITIL.

Retains a strong technical capability and can provide “hands-on” mentoring to project managers and staff in analyzing requirements, architecture, sorting out technical difficulties and planning and delivering technical projects in the areas of business intelligence, data warehousing, software and web development, infrastructure and system operations. Most recent experience has been with Windows (Wintel), Microsoft SQL, SharePoint, Biztalk and .Net, but he has also had experience with Cognos, Oracle, Siebel, PeopleSoft, UNIX and mainframe projects.
