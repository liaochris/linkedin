Christian A. Aspegren’s Summary

A growing number of Leaders are realizing that although they have been successful in their change management efforts much more will be required already during the next few years. The coming globally turbulent times require that leaders aspire to take a driver seat in order to be able to ride the storms. Our economies are being hit by major strengthening undercurrents that have been building for some years at the same time as visible storms sweep often seemingly in opposite directions. There will be major eruptions and new foundations are established with explosive speed. The bold and ambitious activities in the developing countries are dramatic as such but the real effect is derived from the rest of the world taking traditional and far too cautious steps. The sheer number of innovations is rapidly rising as are major innovations. The dramatic increase in supply of knowledge and information increases the demand for creativity. Globally the balance is changing and major shifts of knowhow, knowledge and assets are already taking place. The winner organisations are those that see these patterns more clearly and employ additional creativity to design and implement milestone projects that drive innovation among all participants and position them above the rest. For these organisations I am a provider of insight and ideas.

Christian A. Aspegren’s Specialties:

I prefer to participate in major global efforts as they raise such waves which provide opportunities for a very large number of actors and ventures. I am currently participating in a few such major ventures and welcome opportunities to improve the penetration of innovations - I am a Facilitator and an Achiever.
