Peter-Paul de Goeij’s Summary

Over the years I developed from a mere sales oriented corporate account manager into an experienced all round telecommunications professional with experience in and a good understanding of General Management, the Interconnection- and the wholesale business, marketing and business development. My core experience is in general management, major account management, project management, invoice- and contract management, contract negotiations and legal and regulatory affairs. I have a broad experience on the management team of a large mobile operator group and I have dealt with various complex issues and deals (e.g. due dilligence and SOx).

I keep a sharp eye on the telecommunications industry developments - technology wise as well as on the corporate movements and I know how to translate this into forward looking strategy and advice. My experience, strong interests and solid ideas about general management, strategy and finance give an extra edge to this skillset.

My personality is best described as inquisitive, I have a positive mindset and I easily step into new and unknown territories - off the beaten track. My most immediate goal is to further develop myself, whilst helping to further shape a company into the shining example to its competition and to lead by setting the example.

° Interconnect & Wholesale
° MVNO's
° Interconnection (especially mobile carriers)
