Holly Waxman, Allied Member ASID’s Summary

I am an interior designer specializing in spaces shared by people & animals! If you have a pet, then you are an ideal candidate (also ideal are vet's offices, doggy day cares, groomers, pet hotels, pet boutiques). Inviting an animal into your life and home is incredibly rewarding & fulfilling...but it can also be challenging to maintain a beautiful space! Now, there's a style for every species. With smart choices of fabrics, finishes, materials and room layouts, an amazing, personal, functional environment can be created for everyone to enjoy, human & animal, alike.

Do you live in a humanimal habitat?

Holly Waxman, Allied Member ASID’s Experience

  • Owner/Design Consultant, Humanimal Habitat, Inc.

    Humanimal Habitat

    (Architecture & Planning industry)

    February 2008 — Present (4 months)

    Humanimal Habitat, Inc. is a full-service residential & commercial interior design company, specializing in spaces shared by people & pets. Throughout the years, I have found that my very favorite and most engaging design projects were for those clients who had pets.

    When an animal is invited into your life & house, it becomes their home, too. Just like us, animals love to be comfy & cozy--often on the furniture! Yet living amongst animals can present a wide array of challenges pertaining to your maintaining the house. With smart choices of fabrics, finishes, flooring and room layouts, people and their pets can equally enjoy a beautifully designed, personal space.

    No furry children? I still love to design interesting, functional spaces for humans, too! Whether it's new construction, a remodel or addition, making over a dated room, or simply a paint color consultation, I work with all styles and budgets.
