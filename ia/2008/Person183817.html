Susan Black’s Summary

Susan Black heads up the 7-year old strategy firm, Susan Black Associates (The Black & Wright Group). Clients include leading online travel brands, traditional travel distributors, suppliers and publishers, and travel industry start-ups. Susan is also Senior Advisor - Travel Industry, Chimney Rock Capital Partners investment bank. She serves on several corporate advisory boards, and was elected to ATME's 2008 Executive Committee (Association of Travel Marketing Executives) and TIA's 2008 eCommerce Committee (Travel Industry Association of America).

She recently served as CMO of CheapCaribbean.com, a top ranked website dedicated to Caribbean and Mexico vacations. Her previous posts include Executive Vice President, Marketing for $2 billion Liberty Travel/GOGO Worldwide Vacations; Founder, TravelCom Conference & Expo; Founder of the Travel Industry's Executive Women's Forum; Chairman of eTravelWorld; GM and Publisher of Thomas Publishing's Travel Group; and Associate Publisher of Corporate Travel Magazine.
