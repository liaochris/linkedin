Jason Kay’s Summary

Highly successful and professional technology visionary with ten years of experience in eCommerce, Internet services, and systems management. Several years as a software development leader at a top 5 US banking institution. Currently holds a senior position in a leading edge enterprise strategy and architecture organization leveraging eCommerce, Internet, open source, and software lifecycle expertise. Unique ability to both lead by defining a new paradigm, and manage within a current paradigm. A world-class technical professional with the requisite successes to demonstrate it excelling in a rapidly changing environment.

Leadership: Professionally trained in personnel management, human resources policies, project management, and professional development, with a demonstrated knowledge of team motivation, organizational communication, and
process development.

Financial Management: Fully versed in financial management, budget planning, forecasting, and reporting. Successfully managed an over $60 million annual budget for personnel, equipment, software, real estate, and other development costs.

Technology Skills: Twelve years of experience with Linux and Open Source Software, as a developer, a system administrator, and an architect. Recent exposure to developing and architecting solutions using technologies such as grid computing, single system image clustering, collaboration systems, and software management tools.

Community Involvement: Board member of several local community service organizations in Charlotte. Active in both local politics and national issues. Consistent contributor to technology freedom interest groups such as the Free Software Foundation, the Electronic Frontier Foundation, and Open Source Developers Network.
