Joe Santa Maria’s Summary

Joe Santa Maria has over 22 years of marketing and brand management experience combined with over 25 years of writing, performing and producing music.

As Brand Director/VP of Fitness Management Systems, Joe and his team help businesses define what a solid brand could mean to them in their unique market. Specializing in health clubs, FMS will design and implement an entirely new brand strategy for it's clients including positioning statement, brand identity, marketing budgets and plans, operational systems and best practices, staff training, logo and web site design, corporate photography, viral marketing and web 2.0 strategies and customer satisfaction polling.

FMS can take any business at any size and make it whole. Every aspect of each project is hands on and detail oriented.

Joe's goal over the next five years is to develop and expand the FMS brand to new markets, add locations in the Worcester, Massachusetts area and expand Fitness Management Systems market share as the premier developer of corporate health and wellness programs in New England.

Joe Santa Maria is also the Owner and President of Ghost Sin, LLP. Ghost Sin is a multi-media marketing, public relations and entertainment company that deals exclusively with independent artists, songwriters, arrangers and filmmakers. Currently with publishing, production and distribution divisions, Ghost Sin recently launched a music licensing division by signing a creative properties agreement with UE3 Records/Sony RED .
