Joe Dougherty’s Summary

Owner of a firm offering management consulting services to large corporations. Industry exposure includes software engineering, manufacturing, financial securities, consumer products, insurance products, legal services, publishing and the military.

Clients have included USAA, Standard & Poors (McGraw Hill), Moody’s, Pearson Education, Resort Condominiums International (Cendant), Metropolitan Life Insurance, Scholastic Publishing, Commerce Clearing House, Philip Morris, & Salomon Brothers (now Citigroup).
