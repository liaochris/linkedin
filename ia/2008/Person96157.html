Jonathan Davey’s Summary

(Written for students taking part in a business event, Nov 2007)

Born in Norfolk, raised in Leeds, studied in Newcastle. Took a year out which some thought was a waste but I corrected them, I got wasted!

First proper job with Nestle, trained as a sales rep by the chap who opened the ASDA account when they were in a garden shed! Loved breaking sales targets and winning competitions. Moved to J&J to launch Acuvue Contact Lenses into the optical market. The challenge of converting professionals to a new way of thinking, more money and a sports car attracted me to the post.

Enjoyed the first 3 years, but then with our competitors sponsoring adverse publicity I saw the corporate machines move in and we didn’t gel. Went off to Australia surfing, scuba diving and riding through the rainforest on a motorbike.

Set up my own business building websites 8 years ago, moving into email marketing a year later.

6 years ago I started Business in Berkshire and this has gone from strength to strength. I have run it very much as a life style business. The next year will see things change. I’m looking to be debt free by the time I am 45, with a tidy sum for a rainy day.

Tune into www.businessinberkshire.co.uk, watch our content fly high, watch our clients top the search engines in their chosen fields and come and join in when the time is right for you. Happy to answer any question you may have, call 01753 852904.

Jonathan Davey’s Specialties:

Internet Marketing is what I have been focussed on for the last eight years.

The key here is not how many widgets you have flying around your website but how the target market want to use your site. Listen to your customers.
