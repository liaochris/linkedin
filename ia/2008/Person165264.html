Renee Racine’s Summary

Renee develops interactive marketing strategy and directs creative services for top tier national brands.

She specializes in eCRM strategy, email program planning and the online user experience. She has made a career out of understanding the needs of brands, balancing them against the challenges of their marketplace and the behaviours of their consumers, then developing clear, acheivable and aspirational strategic plans.

Her insights have helped to establish leading internet communities and a variety of successful customer acquisition and retention programs across many verticals including financial, CPG and entertainment.

Oh... she's also an award-winning copywriter: DMA 2006 Bronze, CMA 2006 Merit, IAC 2006 Best in Show for her work on the Aeroplan New Member Activation Sequence.

Renee has had the honour of working with many notable brands (and their agencies) over the course of her career including A&P, Aeroplan, BMO, CBC, Clairol, Crest, Delta Hotels, Foresters, Hilton, HSBC, Lavalife, Olay and others.

Renee Racine’s Specialties:

eMarketing and interactive strategy, online user experience, wireframe prototyping, insight driven creative strategy, persona development, member/customer conversion, public speaking, acquisition, retention and social media concept development, content and communications planning, online campaign management.
