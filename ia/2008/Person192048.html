Chris Wortham’s Summary

Having started out as an automotive electronics installer over 20 years ago, I have worked in every form of automotive jobs there are.I started Total Image just as a car stereo shop but we now find that Total Image can morph into many things...Sponsorship Consultant, Corporate Marketing Vehicle Design and Creation, Public Relations, Vehicle Customizing, Event Coordinator, Specialty Vehicle Locator Service, Branding & New Product Launches... Total Image was created to offer differences in the way Automotive marketing was being done. Yes we do try to think of different things to do with vehicles but we also look at each project the same...Make it work for the client.
