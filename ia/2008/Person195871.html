Vibor Kalogjera’s Summary

After American University of Paris and upon my return back home to Croatia, I gained great experience in advertisng and marketing in several companies I worked in: BBDO Zagreb, T-Mobile, and Oglasnik (Trader Media East Zagreb). As the Internet Product Manager at Oglasnik, I lead a team of colleagues responsible for developing and positioning two top vertical Croatian classifieds websites - auti.hr and nekretnine.net. Being an Internet enthusiast, I also started first Croatian citizens portal called Xpotal.hr, and soon sold my shares in the project. In addition, I consulted in developing the portal for a new journal called Sutra (Europa Press Holding) . I also serve as strategy consultant for GoHome Board of Diretors. GoHome is Europe's first search engine for real estate. Currently, I am head of Croatian office of Gemius SA, the leading Central and Eastern European online market research agency.

Vibor Kalogjera’s Specialties:

Internet consulting, online advertising, web development, SEM, SEO, product marketing.
