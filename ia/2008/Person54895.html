David Sherman, Ph.D.’s Summary

Dr. Sherman is one of the founders of Criteria Corporation, an innovative cutting edge Internet company that provides businesses with HireSelect, a state-of-the-art suite of employment assessment tools designed to evaluate human capital. At the heart of this SaaS system is intellectual property that includes cognitive tests developed for NASA astronauts and patented by Harvard University. Delivery is accomplished through a unique web-based system that fully integrates the O*NET database. Built-in online resources include an interactive job requirements analysis, guided test selection, and legal defensibility tutorials that demystify the process of employee testing while simultaneously adhering to the highest standards of scientific rigor and security. Dr. Sherman was a founder and serves as Criteria's Chief Operating Officer, Chief Financial Officer, and Chief Technology Officer.

Dr. Sherman served as President of AIMS Multimedia where his primary effort was digital technology production. Drawing upon his microcomputer skills, educational psychology background in learning theories, practical experience in teaching and instruction, psychotherapeutic training in systems theory and Pinchot's seminal work Intrapreneuring, Dr. Sherman played an integral role in guiding AIMS to its leading position as producer and distributor of educational multimedia for schools, libraries, colleges, businesses, law enforcement agencies, health departments, and government agencies. In August 2004, Dr. Sherman sold AIMS Multimedia to Discovery Communications, Inc.

During his tenure at AIMS, Dr. Sherman conceived, designed and co-programmed all AIMS Multimedia’s software, over 400 programs, including AIMS’ flagship product, DigitalCurriculum™, SaaS and the first Internet streaming video-on-demand educational system with more than 20 million paid subscribers. Dr. Sherman also designed and produced AIMS internal sales and contact management software.
