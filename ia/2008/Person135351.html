Capable of evaluating and aligning new technologies via formal analysis and fiscal controls to ensure proper adherence with business objectives. Students learn the interaction between technology, management and strategy, and how to recognize and understand disruptive technologies, use frameworks and models in a case-based setting to analyze technologies and learn how to manage technology innovation and commercialization.
