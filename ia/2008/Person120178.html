Marcos Guevara’s Summary

Marcos is a Business Strategist with over 10 years of international experience in Venture Capital, Corporate and Financial environments and is skilled in:
- Designing and planning strategic change initiatives, following a practical, client-centric, results-oriented approach.
- Leading and motivating “change evangelists” in both small and large organizations, focusing on the human aspects of the initiatives under his tutelage.
- Involving and selling objectives to technical personnel and technology vendors, ensuring the successful execution of the change initiative.

Marcos’ expertise arises from practical experience in high stress business turnaround situations where strategic realignment was closely supported by operational and financial restructuring. Complementing this experience, he has also facilitated Strategic Planning workshops and taught graduate-level Business Strategy classes.
