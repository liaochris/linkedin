Arnaud Fischer’s Summary

User-obsessed and Competition-paranoid Search Veteran and Business Professional with 14 years experience planning, building, taking to market and monetizing Internet Search experiences, consumer Web services, social networks and vertical communities, online advertising solutions with Microsoft, AOL Search, Altavista and Infospace. Product Leader with proven track record in brand development, product positioning, product management and new product launch with the unique combination of strategic vision and tactical skills. Constantly on top of social search, social networking, online advertising, Web 2.0 user interaction models, Ajax-driven features, mashups, personalization, , API-driven syndication and distribution models, computational linguistic, data mining, sentiment analysis, buzz monitoring, online reputation management.
