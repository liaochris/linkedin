Kevin Feldman’s Summary

An innovative and gifted fundraising and marketing leader, Kevin can build a comprehensive and integrated development and communications program that will strengthen your organization’s brand and maximize the acquisition of donors, dollars, and other valuable resources.

NOTABLE CONTRIBUTIONS:
* Revitalizing the major gift fundraising program of a $200 million a year organization.
* Renovating acquisition program for international charity, leading to record numbers of new donors.
* Creating a fundraising campaign and event that raised broad awareness, and a significant number of new donors and dollars for a statewide charity.
* Inventing a Web-based service to donors that improves retention rates and leads to repeat giving.
* Leading the successful renaming and rebranding efforts for a worldwide organization.

Kevin Feldman’s Specialties:

Multi-channel marketing; brand management; customer service; writing; servant-leadership; building diversified fundraising and marketing programs; integrating fundraising, sales, and marketing programs into powerful, sustainable, and revenue-generating machines.
