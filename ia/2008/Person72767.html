Gremil Alessandro Naz’s Summary

Gremil Alessandro Alcazar Naz is an assistant professor of journalism at Bicol University, Philippines. He served as chairman of the university's Journalism Department from 2003 to 2006.

Naz has a master's degree in media, culture, and communication from New York University where he was full scholar with a grant from the Ford Foundation International Fellowships Program. He has another master's degree in development communication from the University of the Philippines. He earned his bachelor's degree in journalism, cum laude, from the same university.

Gremil Alessandro Naz’s Specialties:

Higher education instruction, journalism, development communication, communication studies
