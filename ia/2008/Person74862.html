Stanislaus Marc Haribowo MBA CISA’s Summary

Stan is a Manager in KPMG Vancouver's Risk Advisory Services practice. He has more than 5 years of public practice experience, focusing on the documentation, audit, evaluation and improvement of business processes and information systems and related internal controls and risk management. His clients have included financial institutions, data processing centers, technology companies, energy companies, distribution companies, biotechnology companies, state lotteries, major railways, and an airline company.

Prior to KPMG, Stan was with PricewaterhouseCoopers, where he was involved in a major forensic audit of an NYSE listed Indonesian telecommunications company. He developed and performed complex electronic data retrieval, analysis and reporting procedures and CAAT (Computer Assisted Audit Techniques) in connection with the forensic investigation of this audit.

Stan is also passionate in the field of Finance. In his MBA program at Thunderbird, he was Teaching Assistant to Prof. Jim Mills for the Financial Engineering course. He completed 26 Fixed Income case studies where he analyzed the risk and return for bonds, mortgage & asset backed securities as well as fixed income derivatives through the use of Bloomberg and other bond analytic software. In his sparetime, Stan manages his own portfolio consisting of 15-20 equities with a value investing style.
