My work at Modem (now d/b/a Publicis Modem) continues to focus on the development and management of various marketing/media programs for my clients that include brand awareness campaigns, web site traffic driving efforts, online promotions , acquisition/ROI initiatives, retention, etc...as well as all of the many related creative and production functions inherently associated with such projects. Clients include: Delta Air Lines, Dice, Cap Gemini, Kraft, Marriott International, and Time Warner Cable

Before arriving at Modem, I was the Account Director at Integrated Media Solutions where I was tasked with managing and developing direct response marketing programs specifically on the internet. In this role, I was responsible for strategic planning and tactical implementation of all direct programs (email, web marketing, search engine, affiliates programs, etc) to drive traffic and revenue for clients such as Hooked On Phonics, Cancer Treatment Center of America, and Bosley Medical Group.

Prior, I was an Associate Media Director for digital@JWT managing the media AOR for both Unilever and Whitehall-Robins Healthcare and was responsible for the overall development and management of their corporate contractual commitments with media partners including MSN, AOL, iVillage, DynamicLogic and DoubleClick DART.

I've previously worked at Young & Rubicam where I had the opportunity to develop online marketing programs for numerous top-tier global brands such as Phillip Morris, Whitehall-Robins Healthcare, Star Alliance, U.S. Army, Showtime Entertainment Network, Dr Pepper, 7 UP, Anderson Consulting, and Paramount Home Videos.
