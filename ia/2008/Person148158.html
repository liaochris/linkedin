Paul Risk’s Summary

Consulting and leadership professional with over 18 years experience in IT strategy, business process reengineering (BPR), business process optimization (BPO) organizational change and systems integration. Excellent skills in the areas of portfolio management, executive communication, team leadership, project organization, project management, change management, education, and strategic alignment.

Motivated by working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and thrive on taking on challenges which require innovative, out-of-the-box thinking to derive clever solutions. Significant and broad experience with a variety of client companies, including financial services, retail, pharmaceutical, real estate, and high-tech. Experience includes leading teams of up to 40 people, including associates, clients, vendors, and third parties, building practice areas and organizations, and creating intellectual capital.

One of my guiding principles is that the business drives technology, and that an effective consulting professional understands the company’s core business, its strategy, direction and goals, and how to selectively apply appropriate technology and strategy for the betterment of the business. Some of my key strengths include finding value through effective application of technology, process improvement, mitigating risk and improving efficiency.

In addition to 18 years of experience in technology and management consulting (5 of which were spent with Deloitte Consulting and Ernst & Young), my qualifications include an MS in Computer Engineering, an MBA with an emphasis in Quantitative Analysis and PMP certification.
