Lori Hughes’s Summary

I am a highly motivated pharmaceutical sales representative with 12 years of documented success. I was hired by Merck, Sharp and Dohme, right out of college in 1987. I spent 10 years building a career in which I was inducted into the Vice President's Club twice, was promoted to Senior Professional Representative, won numerous Awards for Excellence and was awarded many Stock Option Bonuses. I was three months shy of being promoted to an Executive Professional Representative when I gave birth to twins. I reluctantly resigned my position to raise my newborn twins and their two year old brother.

When the twins were 6 months old, their three year old brother was diagnosed with rhabdomyosarcoma, and we spent a full year in chemotherapy. This is what led me to selling AFLAC insurance. I missed pharmaceutical sales, and returned to the industry on a flex time basis with Wyeth. Wyeth Consumer downsized by half in January, leaving mostly major metro territories. The age of my children, my longing to return to my full time career, and the elimination of my territory makes this an excellent opportunity for me to take back my career in full time pharmaceutical sales.

While raising my children, I began a small decorating business, volunteered at their schools and little league in many capacities, volunteered for the American Cancer Society, decorated the church for Easter and Christmas, and Directed Vacation Bible School for three years.

Lori Hughes’s Specialties:

I have been trained in Customer Oriented Success, Customer Focused Selling, Negotiating Skills, and The Versatile Pharmaceutical Representative. Additionally, I have experience selling products for the following disease states: hypertension (including Ace Inhibitors, ARBs, CCBs and combination products), hypercholesterolemia, allergies, peptic ulcer disease and GERD, OA, RA, pain, glaucoma, conjunctivitis, BPH, Parkinson's Disease, i and nfectious disease.
