Gabriele Brencher’s Summary

DM Technologies provide comprehensive, leading edge technologies and services to the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, nutraceutical and allied industries.

Based in Singapore and supporting the APAC & MEA region, we offer specialized equipment ranging from single units to complete production lines that can be customized to our clients' needs. Areas of expertise include SOFT GEL ENCAPSULATION MANUFACTURING, DRUG DELIVERY PRODUCTION and LASER TABLIET DRILLING SYSTEMS.

In addition to equipment supply, we offer a wide range of professional consulting and engineering services specifically geared to pharmaceutical and nutraceutical manufacturing.

DM Technologies has been serving the pharmaceutical industry in the region since 1994. We recognize the growing demand for innovative technologies that improve progress toward new milestones in medicine. We are committed to ensuring that our products and services are of the highest quality!

Satisfied international customers and blue-chip organizations include GSK, Pfizer, J&J, MSD, Novartis, Abbott, Bayer Diagnostics, Rexall, Banner PharmaCaps, Cipla, Dr Reddy, Accucaps, Strides, Intergel USA, Natures Bounty USA to name a few. We are a recognized supplier to the Singapore Government and MOH.

For more information on our products and services, visit our website below.
