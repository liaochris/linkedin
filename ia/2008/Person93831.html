Joe Morris’s Summary

Formed by experienced IT professionals, IT Talent is positioned at the forefront of IT Recruitment and Managed IT Services. We pride ourselves on providing the 'Complete IT Solution' within all areas of the IT marketplace.

IT Talent provides tailored solutions to suit the needs of your business, incorporating both Permanent and Contract staff to meet your long-and short-term requirements. We can also provide you with the option of completely outsourcing all of your IT; thus reducing your costs and improving your business focus.

I lead a team of consultants that specialise in the placement of contract database professionals including Architects, DBA’s, Programmers, ERP Consultants and BI Specialists.

Please feel free in contacting me for more detailed information on the services we provide; alternatively access our website at www.it-talent.co.uk
