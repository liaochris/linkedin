Rosie DeCosmo, Ph.D.’s Summary

I have worked with fortune 500 corporations and public sector organizations in the areas of Leadership and Organizational Development. My expertise includes work in both domestic and international markets. I specialize in HRD/Organizational Development Strategies, Leadership Development, and Assessment. I collaborate with clients to develop OD and learning initiatives designed to produce business-focused results. Throughout my career, I have worked with thousands of managers.

My work consists of organizational, leadership development, change management, and measurement and evaluation with a focus on assessment and development of organizational talent. I work with my clients to identify work related issues to determine development initiatives through use of surveys, individual assessments, action learning projects, and formal development programs.
Additional areas of expertise include design and development of customized training, coaching, and teambuilding.

I am a co-partner and founder of the Institute for Organizational Development of South Florida and am a member on the International OD global and communications committee.
