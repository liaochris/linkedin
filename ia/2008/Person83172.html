James Senior’s Summary

A 2008 graduate of the MFA Design and Technology program at Parsons, The New School for Design, I am looking for an agency job in New York City.

An interactive and graphic designer with 12 years experience in the field, I've worked for agencies, in-house, taught web design, and also co-founded a company. I am particularly attuned to user psychology and the narratological aspects of design.

MEDIA ART

My video and installation art practice concentrates on questions of materiality, emotion and memory. How has our immersion in gaming, the Internet and media-heavy reality changed our affective responses?

James Senior’s Specialties:

Interactive design; Interface analysis; Narrative profiling; User psychology; Art research.
