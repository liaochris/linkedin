Stellan Björnesjö’s Summary

A young and driven entrepreneur with more than 4 years of experience as a business developer and project manager in the software and consumer industries, working with everything from business planning and strategic issues to handling of complex sales, contractual negotiations and management of customer relationships. Holds a M.Sc. in Innovation & Entrepreneurship from Chalmers University in Gothenburg, Sweden, and have studied law for 8 semesters at the School of Business, Economics and Law, also in Gothenburg, Sweden. During the next few years it is my goal to continue to learn by leading and participating in challenging projects, questioning persistent thoughts and working with experienced people at all levels in a dynamic environment.
