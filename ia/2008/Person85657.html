Jay Bailey’s Summary

I have been working in the automotive manufacturing industry for the past 7 years. I have worked in many facets of the industry, including Quality, Engineering, Lean Manufacturing and Production. I spent the first 4 years of my career traveling a great deal, either addressing quality issues at our customers (Ford, GM, Chrysler, Nissan, Harley Davidson) or at tooling reviews. The past 3 years I have spent most all of my time at the plant involved with day-to-day production and improvements.
