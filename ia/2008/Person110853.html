Lars Udsholt’s Summary

Lars Udsholt has robust management and leadership experience from top level positions in key development organisations, where he has implemented major organisational reforms and been instrumental in strategic reorientations. 20 years of involvement in international development cooperation through management positions, policy involvement, and research, enables Lars Udsholt to span a wide range of issues with deep insight, both in bilateral and multilateral aid policies and programmes. He has effectively leveraged his academic background and translated a lifelong commitment to development into high level management performance.

Based in Hanoi, Vietnam, Lars manages the two offices of capacitate a/s in Copenhagen and Hanoi and undertakes assignments for international development organisations in the areas of his professional specialisation.

Lars Udsholt’s Specialties:

* Aid effectiveness and organisational performance.
* Civil society organisations at local, national and international levels
* Policy analysis, strategy development and organisational assessments
* Helping organisations define their role and strategies and as well as improving performance.
