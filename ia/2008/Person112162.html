  • Stichting Bijlmer Breakz

    (Music industry)

    October 2005 — November 2008 (3 years 2 months)

    A side project in which I was (and still am) responsible for creating and maintaining the website, the online marketing strategy and all visual expressions (e.g. cd booklet, posters, site, banners).

    Bijlmer Breakz is a platform for young talented musicians from Amsterdam South East. The company recruited 7 young promising acts, produced and recorded 2 tracks per act and finally released a compilation album in september 2008 (get it on iTunes or in stores). To hypen things up a videoclio was shot and spread (check http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aKkG1VFFjZw), lots of live performances were arranged and we injected the project in the relevant online and offline media and ofcourse on the streets.

    The Bijlmer Breakz sound is a fresh, energetic musical crossover between dance, hiphop and carribean sounds. This combined with positive raps and singing vocals in Dutch, Spanish, Papiamento, English and Surinamese literally smashed the industry and music lovers.
