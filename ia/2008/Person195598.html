Verena Raveton de Castelmur’s Summary

HEDIARD SINGAPORE MILESTONES:

2004 - Opened first APAC regional office
- Hediard sets foot in SEA for the first time years with a central office at Forum, Orchard.

2004 - Corporate business
- As a leading French Luxury brand from Colbert Committee, Hediard instantly appeals to the precious first French companies top executives looking for quality items for their major customers.

2005 - Opened the Flagship boutique on Tanglin Road at Tudor Court
- With a unique freestanding boutique in the Orchard area, Hediard joins the top luxury brands in town and shows a touch of French classicism in the charming arcades stretch.

2006 - Launched the eStore with a selection of 250 products.
- Mainly composed of gifts, the ecommerce site encounters a swift success for returning customers with punctual needs for a gift to be delivered in Singapore while they are located in other countries or simply busy customers who need a delivery within 24 hours without going downtown for shopping, an easy way to browse Hediard’s interactive catalogue.

2006 - Initiated wholesale business for Hotels and Restaurants.
- With the launch of a dedicated catalogue and a wide range of Teas (260+) and coffees (35+) created or selected by the 2 in house tea and coffee masters, Hediard now seduces hospitality professionals.

2007 - Opened Hediard Café-Restaurant at Tudor Court.
- This extension completes the Café-Boutique concept. Chef Vincent Carayon from Hediard Paris flies exceptionally to Singapore for the press launch and to finalize the menu.

2007 - Tripled Corporate business over a year.
- Hediard has become a name for high society customers and corporate gifts.

2008 - Launched first 360-gondola at Marketplace.
- Hediard starts a series of geographical extensions with wholesale and seduces a larger crowd in the middle of Orchard road.
