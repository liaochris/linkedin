Chase Gunderson’s Summary

I love trying new things. For ten years I worked for the same company, Quality Services. I did every job imaginable for them and then I got bored and I decided I wanted to try something else. In 2006 I received my real estate license and began working as a realtor. In 2006 I sold $6 million in real estate. WaMu then contacted me and asked me if I would be interested in doing mortgages for them. I took that job and in my first year I was a multi million dollar loan producer. Now I am working for WaMu as a Personal Financial Representative. At the same time I am trying to get my own information security and services company up and running and I'm also a supplier of trees to landscapers and developers.
