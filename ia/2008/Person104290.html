Keith Nothacker’s Summary

Keith is the Founder and President of KHN Solutions, a manufacturer and distributor of breath alcohol testers used for personal and professional use.

KHN Solutions pioneered the consumer market for breathalyzers by positioning the product as an affordable, consumer-friendly safety device. KHN Solutions was the first company ever to receive U.S. Food & Drug Administration 510(k) marketing clearance to legally sell breathalyzers to consumers for personal use.

Keith has appeared on Dateline NBC as an industry expert, was quoted on the front page of the USA Today, and has appeared in a wide variety of additional media. In 2005, Keith was invited by the NFL’s Oakland Raiders football team to speak to players about alcohol safety. As President, Keith serves as a driving force for KHN Solutions’ development and distribution of cutting-edge alcohol testing products. Keith graduated magna cum laude from the University of Pennsylvania with Distinction in Economics.
