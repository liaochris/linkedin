Rob Garneau’s Summary

Founder of Bottom-Line Training.

Bottom-Line Training provides unique, targeted workshops that develop, and improve the selling skills of your Sales Application Engineers (or System Engineers) and will improve your bottom-line results. Our focus is to impart the skills Sales AEs selling high end software or hardware need to be successful and to help your company thrive.

We don't offer one of the many generic sales skills classes that only focus on the needs of the sales rep; our workshops provide specialized training that focuses on the specific skills requirements of successful sales AEs--proficient demos, winning with, and without, product evaluations, and much more. Our program teaches easy to remember and structured techniques that enable your AEs to quickly and sustainably implement the ideas learned.

Guaranteed.

For more information and contact information, please visit:
http://www.Bottom-LineTraining.com
