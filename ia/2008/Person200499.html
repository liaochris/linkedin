    PURAC

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Chemicals industry)

    November 2003 — November 2006 (3 years 1 month)

    Jr. Plantmanager (assisting in maximizing Operational Effectiveness)

    TPM = Total Productive Management, a Japanese proven management method to reach Operational Excellence. One of the founders defined TPM as “Increasing productivity, by stimulating the daily awareness of all employees”.To simplify the TPM-method, the following steps indicate the approach:
    1. Identify losses in the equipment and organization
    2. Create awareness for all employees
    3. Eliminate losses by making improvements
    4. Update standard ways of working.
    5. Reach level of continuous improvement

    TPM showed big organizational improvements calculated to be worth € 1.000.000+ benefit/year. All lines/departments showed more than 10% productivity-increase. In 2006 a reorganization-project was initiated using TPM-findings as input.

    As from 2006, a reorganisation is taking place. I was part of the transition-team in making choices, timing and ensuring communication.
