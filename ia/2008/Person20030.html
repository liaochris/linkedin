Bart Hansen’s Summary

Over the past 14 years I have worked on different assignments as an e-business consultant, functional designer, project leader, team coach or developer. The solutions I was involved in ranged from small, powerful mobile solutions to large scale high available e-government enterprise applications. Working in these many different environments and roles gave me the ability of managing and understanding the complete information technology top-down and bottom-up.

My professional goal is to assist organizations defining a clear e-business strategy, improving business-IT alignment and to assist in the design and development of agile, efficient and cost effective IT solutions.

Bart Hansen’s Specialties:

* Defining e-business strategies
* Improving business-IT alignment (COBIT)
* Modeling the Enterprise Architure (based on IEEE 1471-2000)
* Create base technical IT architecture (using SOA, CMS, DMS, Portals, ERP suites...)
* Selecting vendor solutions or outsourcing partners
* Defining master plans, programs and projects
* Manage projects (PMBOK, PRINCE2)
* Business and functional analysis (process, requirements)
* Object Oriented Design and Analysis (UML, Patterns)
