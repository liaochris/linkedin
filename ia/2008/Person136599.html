Mike Stachowiak’s Summary

Mike Stachowiak was most recently a founding engineer in charge of search technology at 4INFO.

4INFO (http://www.4INFO.net) is the leading mobile search company, delivering instant local directory, entertainment, and real-time information to mobile phones via text-messaging (44636 shortcode) and WAP (http://wap.4INFO.net). 4INFO is backed by U.S. Venture Partners, Draper Fisher Jurvetson, Gannett (USA TODAY), and several prominent angel investors.

As the founding engineer, Mike helped grow 4INFO from 3 to 35 employees over a two year period that included two prominent venture rounds. Mike played a pivotal role in building 4INFO's scalable infrastructure and high-talent engineering team.

Mike was previously a Senior Engineer, Production Support Team Lead at QuinStreet, a large private online marketing firm. After serving integral development roles on a variety of company projects, he was put in charge of the production support group for the company's largest client, contributing more professional services revenue than any other group within the company.

Mike has extensive experience in artificial intelligence, localized search, and information retrieval. He is considered an expert in natural language processing with multiple patents pending his inventions.

Mike has a BSE from the University of Michigan School of Engineering, where he was an honored Departmental Scholar.

His passion and expertise is in the field of artificial intelligence and information retrieval.
