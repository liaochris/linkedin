Brian Allen’s Summary

CREATIVE ABILITY
I have been an artist since early childhood. Through training over the years, I have become comfortable in many mediums. Attention to detail has enabled my artwork to maintain a high level of quality. I have been characterized by other artists as one that exists outside the box. One should not confine their creativity, their creativity should define them.

ADMINISTRATIVE SKILLS
I am capable of envisioning the end product and the process it takes to get there including the management of others throughout creative projects. Organization and careful planning enables me to handle a variety of tasks and responsibilities simultaneously. My internal motivation and professional attitude provides me the edge with both clients and my team. The development of strong leadership skills lead to the founding of Student Animators Workshop, a unique production based Recognized Student organization at Collin County Community College. As the President, I developed a production pipeline mimicking the environment of real animation studio. This pipeline allowed the team to minimize the amount of time spent on implementation and maximized creative flow.

PROBLEM SOLVING
I have the ability to quickly digest and determine the best possible solution to complete and or correct problems that occur throughout projects with tight deadlines. Complications allow me the opportunity to learn more about each program. My two years as a lab assistant at Collin County Community College has prepared me in all aspects of trouble shooting.

TECHNICAL ABILITY
I am experienced in a wide range of software and hardware necessary to refine projects with maximum efficiency. These include, but are not limited to Autodesk / Maya, Google / SketchUp, Adobe/Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, and Dreamweaver. These tools help me to implement the majority of my ideas not create them.
