Ben Koot’s Summary

I worked for a number of travel agents and tour operators and a brief period at the Radison SAS Hotel in Amsterdam before I started my own hotel reservation service. I was representative for LRI Hotel reservations, until it is was bought by UTEL and pioneerd Landlink, an dynamic packaging system created by Jetset tours in the pre-internet eara.
