Bill Heaton’s Summary

By nature I have a "Computer Scientist" type of personality with over 30 years experience and the equivalent experience of a Doctorate of Computer Science. In my areas of expertise, I rank in the top 10% and I'm consistently on the leading edge of technology.

My main passion is almost anything that has to do with communications, in particular Video (Multimedia). Interactive Technology, and Database driven web sites. When I am brought into a new project, it's almost invariably for one of two reasons:

* It's new technology, or an integration of disparate technology from multiple areas. For example, I was one of the pioneers in the use of "off the shelf computer" hardware to generate broadcast legal outputs for cable television.

* Or; as a Tiger Team leader or member. One of my talents is to quickly go through all aspects of a project (Hardware, Software, Resources) to determine what corrective actions are needed to turn it around and then implement those changes.

Startups and New Companies are always considered seriously, "Skunk Works" projects are a plus.
