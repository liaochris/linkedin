Jeff Cohen’s Summary

A senior sales and marketing executive whose career has been focused in the technical broadcast television and commercial audio visual industries. Most recently the National Accounts Executive for Chyron Corporation’s ChyTV Digital Signage Business Unit (disbanded December 2007). Prior, was Business Development Manager for Electrosonic Systems; President of the Broadcast Systems Integration Division of Video Corporation of America; President of BBC Systems Integration; Founding Partner of Digital Imaging Solutions (broadcast equipment supplier eventually sold to Video Corporation of America); Product Marketing Manager for Sony Advanced Systems (Sony’s HDTV product marketing group). Early career was spent in technical and sales/marketing roles in New York’s video and audio production and post-production industry.

Jeff Cohen’s Specialties:

Business-to-Business Marketing, branding, strategic partnership, new product development, channel development, major account management, negotiation. Technology Expert in Broadcast Production and Post Production, Commercial Audio Visual, and Digital Signage.
