Jason Wray’s Summary

I am currently a Marketing Coordinator at Canadian Tire, Canada's largest retailer and most trusted brand. I work in the automotive marketing department, working closely with the Category Management teams. My focus is on handling vendor quotes and adding new parts to assortment.

I have also worked as a Student Business Advisor (SBA) at Humber Student Connections, an Industry Canada initiative. SC's primary goal is to support small and medium-sized enterprises by providing e-business solutions. As a Sales and Marketing SBA, I provided my clients with cost-effective quality consulting and training, such as website plans and evaluations, business plans, and e-business and technology training. As well, I was an expert in delivering seminars on e-business to large groups at Enterprise Centres, Chambers of Commerce, trade events and educational organizations. I was also heavily involved in promoting Humber Student Connections through trade shows, direct mail, online promotion, and sales meetings.

HSC's secondary goal is to provide business skills training to students. Since SBAs work as consultants to real businesses and are responsible for key deliverables and building interest in the program, they gain invaluable experience that they can use in the real world. This program gave me the boost I needed to make the next step to a great company like CTC. I recommend it for all students.

Humber College's Marketing Management postgraduate program gave me the skills and knowledge I needed to enter the business world. I went from having little knowledge of modern business techniques to being well versed in current practices for effectively marketing a business in today's fast-paced marketplace.

While at Humber, I had the pleasure of working with Sundex Inc, a technology company specializing in asset tracking (ranging from equipment and raw materials to information). My team was able to assist in Sundex's marketing through B2B direct marketing communications.
