Karen Ortiz’s Summary

Thank You for taking time out of your busy day and visiting my website.

My name is KAREN ORTIZ and I am from the hills of Tennessee. Like most folks, I was always told if you ever want to make anything of yourself you must get a good education and a secure job. So, I completed my education and left for the big city of Los Angeles where I dedicated 15 years of my life to corporate America.

My husband, Jose and I, ready to start a family were seeking freedom from my very demanding stressful Systems Business Analyst position. We purchased our own business and moved back to the country. What we did was purchase the most expensive job we have ever had.

I found my home business11 years ago because I was very ill and needed help. I had recently lost most of my family to cancer I saw myself heading down the same path. The Wellness Industry was my ticket out of the generational curses that had taken my family to an early grave. My 4 boys have rarely been sick since! My entire family is healthier, happier and wealthier!

I am on a path of creating a 7 figure income and the future has never looked brighter for my entire family – we could see you at the Top As Well. THIS IS A MAGIC MOMENT – I can help you to the next step if you have the qualities of the kind of person we are looking to place at the top of a sales force in a Legacy Position.
