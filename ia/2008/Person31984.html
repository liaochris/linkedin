Catriona Macaulay’s Summary

Senior lecturer in design ethnography and interactive media design, developed and launched the BSc Interactive Media Design at Dundee University. Currently launching and developing the new Masters course in Design Ethnography, Background in information science, studies of information seeking in context, user and usability research. Currently research focus is on usability and interaction design for bioinformatics applications. Experience prior to entering academic life in 2000 includes project management in news analysis, software development, and international relief operations. Interested in both academic and industry research projects and consultancy.

Catriona Macaulay’s Specialties:

Key words: design ethnography, project management, requirements generation, people-centred design, information science, systems analysis and design, software project management.
