Dario Rossi’s Summary

Since April 2004 I've been working in Chevron Argentina where I'm responsible for selling the whole amount of the gas and oil production. My position poses daily challenges as the Government changes the rules frequently and I have to negotiate changes in sales agreements.
My expertise in the oil market has grown significantly because regulations, Government intervention and commercial issues change frequently. Pump prices have been frozen since 2003 regardless of the fluctuations of the international price of crude oil. This situation forces upstream and non-integrated companies, such as Chevron, to obtain a higher margin, which comes from the customer (refineries).
The gas market is the business environment where I started my professional career. With more than twelve years signing many different agreements, buying, selling and negotiating in a lot of hard environmental situations, I've gained enough experience to develop a great career in management positions. Throughout my career I’ve reduced costs, increased sales and margins and led commercial actions to improve the Company’s profits where I've worked.
