Jeroen de Bakker’s Summary

After several year of experience in advertising, I co-founded Qi (www.qi-ideas.com) in the last quarter of 1997. Qi developed numerous award winning interactive marketing and brand concepts for clients like Heineken Netherlands, Heineken International, Bols Distilleries, Amsterdam Airport Schiphol, Air Miles, Essent, Bacardi, Philips Europe and Unilever. Within Qi we also developed own digital media products like the virtual advertising agency Qineboko.com and the mobile content platforms Fanplay and Notones.

From July 2005 till September 2007, I worked for TBWA\Company Group. First as Managing Director at TEQUILA\ (www.tequila.nl). This agency combined brand activation and new media and I worked for clients like UPC, TomTom, ABN Amro, Albert Heijn (Ahold) and Playstation.

From the start of 2007 till October 2007 I was Director Strategy & Innovation of TBWA\. Apart from activities for clients, I was responsible for the local and international role out of a new initiative of TBWA\ called BrandGossip (www.brandgossip.nl) and I helped with the integration of Agency.com within TBWA\.

At the moment I am in the process of starting new ventures and consulting advertising agencies on how to deal with online media.

What interests me most, is applying new brand communications technology in a valuable, relevant way. Thus, I strive to make clients' differentiating power tangible and sustainable. Furthermore I frequently publish on weblogs and I am often a speaker at events and seminars.
