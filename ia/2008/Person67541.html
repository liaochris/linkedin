Garry Parker’s Summary

One of the UK's top 20 SME (small and medium-sized enterprise) facilitators
Founded the British & Commonwealth Chamber of Commerce in Finland (BCCCF)
Established the Forum of Private Business’ office in Brussels and within 3 years gained them recognition throughout the EU as the then leading UK small business representative organisation
Key in the campaigns for the EC Directive combating late payment in commercial transactions & reduced rate of VAT for labour-intensive services
Raised membership of the All-Party Parliamentary Small Business Group, Westminster, from 25 to 74 MPs/Peers (mentioned in Hansard)
Lead Brussels end start-up of the Pan-European Genesis Initiative, now Director in charge of Genesis Europe & Senate Policy co-ordination
Leading drive for qualitative and quantitative business policy input through the Genesis Senate's UK trade & professional associations, helping make Genesis the biggest voice for SMEs in the UK (c. 100 organisations with 1.2 million businesses represented, February 2008)

Garry Parker’s Specialties:

Experienced PR & PA professional with a proven track record of lobbying success for both large representative organisations, not-for-profit associations and SME corporate clients in the UK, Finland and other EU countries
Strong communication and drafting skills
Strong intellectual ability, working closely with in-house research teams, developing & managing market intelligence
Excellent influencing and networking skills
