Jennifer Utz’s Summary

New Media Strategies pioneered the industry of online brand promotion and protection. A full-service online marketing firm and subsidiary of the Meredith Corporation, NMS uses the power of the Internet to give clients a competitive advantage online, while safeguarding their brands from the effects of negative perceptions and attacks online. Leading brands and corporations such as ABC, Anheuser-Busch, Buena Vista, Disney, Dodge, Hershey's, Jones Apparel Group, Lexus, Paramount, PepsiCo, Sony, USA Networks, and Time Warner all rely on NMS to add value to their brands and guard their bottom lines.

Jennifer Utz’s Specialties:

Jenn brings 5+ years of social media marketing experience to the role of business development at NMS. Having honed her talents on the client services side, Jenn now helps clients develop creative and compelling marketing campaigns to engage their target audiences online. Her experience spans a broad array of business categories including apparel, automotive, entertainment, and CPG industries. Since joining the Sales team in '05, Jenn has been instrumental in growing NMS’ blue-chip client roster.
