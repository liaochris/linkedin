Ken Fredrickson’s Summary

Ken has over 15 years of experience in direct sales and sales management roles in the network, Internet, security, enterprise software, hardware and professional services technology industry.

Total solution and consultative selling approach to global enterprise, Service Providers, software companies and VC funded start-ups. Strong management and coaching skills. Excellent communication and contact with "C" level and project decision makers. Company experience includes Start-up to Acquisition, Start-up to IPO and Publicly traded companies experience.

Ken Fredrickson oversees new business growth in emerging markets for TechLink Systems. Prior to joining TechLink Systems, Fredrickson served as Sales Manager for InnerWorkings, funded by BenchMark Capital and MDV Mohr Davidow Ventures specialized in Performance Capability Solutions.

Before that Fredrickson was with one of Sequoia Capitals portfolio companies Guardent Inc., specialized in Managed Security Services & Security Consulting, which was acquired by VeriSign. Responsibilities included driving revenue from F500 companies on the West Coast. Strategically penetrating high profile accounts.

Fredrickson held multiple roles of Inside Sales, Field Sales, Sales Management at VeriSign, Guardent, Interwoven, CBT Systems, Maximizer Technologies, Gates/Arrow Distributing.
