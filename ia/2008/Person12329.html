Andy Headworth’s Summary

You can contact me on andy.headworth@sironaconsulting.com I am happy to accept your invitation to connect.

At Sirona Consulting - www.sironaconsulting.com - I consult with organisations to create, implement and manage direct recruitment campaigns,recruitment strategy, recruitment process, recruitment advertising, establishing an Employment Brand, retention strategy, blogging, social networking for recruitment, internal online networks, SEO, PPC and SEM activities to enable them to attract and hire the best people cost effectively. We are also working with companies around recruiting Generation Y'ers.
New for 2008 is a product that I have developed to help SME companies reduce their recruitment costs and recruit more staff directly - www.jobinablog.com, it is a blog format but allows a career site to be placed on a corporates website very easily.
I am an active recruitment blogger - http://blog.sironaconsulting.com

We have been recruiting staff for companies for over twenty years, and have in depth experience of permanent, contract, and executive search recruitment in UK, Europe and USA. We have worked with clients across a diverse range of industry sectors such as sales, marketing, IT, retail, engineering, construction, financial services and many more., which enables us to have a valuable insight into recruitment in many different markets.
