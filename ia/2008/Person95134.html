John Helm’s Summary

Highly innovative senior-level manager with a consistently successful career in providing vision, planning, design, development and leadership to enterprise-scale technology solutions at global Fortune 500 and small organizations. A “conundrum doctor” who blends business acumen with technical expertise and talent for complex problem analysis/solving to significantly improve operational efficiencies and customer service levels while reducing costs.

Proficient in project, budget and people management, including training, mentoring, and supervising large or small staffs to achieve peak performance. Rapid learner of new technologies; able to present complex technical information in terms easily understood by non-technical audiences. Team-oriented communications and interpersonal style.
