John Robinson’s Summary

John Robinson, REALTOR with Dunes Marketing Group, has lived on Hilton Head Island, South Carolina since 1990.

Originally from Maryland, John attended college in South Carolina on tennis scholarship and, upon graduation, immediately relocated to Hilton Head Island as he thoroughly enjoys the Island’s southern lifestyle and climate.

Specializing in residential new single family homes, condos and re-sale properties, John is well educated with each existing and future planned community in Beaufort County.

Before joining the real estate world, John Robinson had been a tennis professional for over 20 years at various facilities around the United States including the John Newcombe’s Tennis Ranch in Texas, the John Gardiner’s Tennis Ranch in California, Columbia Country Club in the Washington, DC area, the Sea Pines Racquet Club and the Van der Meer TennisUniversity on Hilton Head Island, South Carolina, and the Athletic Club of Weston in South Florida.

John has been a United States Professional Tennis Association (USPTA) Professional 1 member since 1988 and a Professional Tennis Registry (PTR) National Tester/National Clinician since 1993. John was voted PTR’s “National Tester of the Year” in 1998.

In addition to real estate services, John is associated with Lowcountry Sports Network where he conducts tennis classes, workshops, tournaments, and corporate functions in the Hilton Head Island, South Carolina area as well as traveling tennis events.

John Robinson’s Specialties:

Residential real estate including homes, villas, lots, fractional shares, boat slips, and referrals. I am a certified e-PRO REALTOR.
