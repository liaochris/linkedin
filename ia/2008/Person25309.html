Jeff Bonevich’s Summary

I am a Software Delivery Specialist - that is, I take a software development project from inception to delivery in a production environment. I have always found "software developer", "software engineer", "programmer", "coder", and other titles of their ilk too restrictive and poor descriptors of what I do. I work with others to develop requirements into software delivered into production environments in an agile fashion (business value is the top consideration, development in brief iterations, continuous feedback into the iteration and release plans, delivery of working software to end-users early and often). I work primarily with Java web applications.

I currently am working with Compendia Bioscience, Inc. (http://www.compendiabio.com) building the next generation of our award-winning cancer genomics data mining platform, Oncomine (http://www.oncomine.com).

I am also an independent consultant in data modeling and software development.

I am a committer on the Mevenide project (http://mevenide.codehaus.org)

Jeff Bonevich’s Specialties:

Agile software delivery, J2EE and Integration architecture, perl, Ruby-on-Rails, web application development, WebSphere, WebLogic, Tomcat, JBoss, ETL, healthcare information systems, electronic medical record, Eclipse plug-in and RCP development, Open Source Software development, cancer genomics, test-driven development
