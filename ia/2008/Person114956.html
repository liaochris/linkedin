LeRoy Lemke’s Summary

Attached please find a copy of my resume of qualifications. To review samples of my work, please visit www.coroflot.com/llemke.

I have had the opportunity to be a part of a variety of projects for some very well known brands. I am very strong on the front-end of the creative process, committing attention to research sound solutions, which will in turn provide lift and ROI. I am also a hands-on AD/designer, and prefer to create thumbnail sketches and copy treatments (to be presented to client for buy-in) before taking any idea to the computer. You will also notice some catchy headlines - another one of my strengths. I enjoy the brainstorming process, and have experience leading a team to world-class creative solutions.

From my experience(s), I’ve learned that the creative process evolves around five words that can make a difference in any creative/marketing opportunity: SIMPLIFY. INTEGRATE. MOTIVATE. MEASURE. SUSTAIN. Keeping this mantra in mind can keep any given creative solution clear and on brief, and will result in deliverables that hit the target. Brand is all about discipline and consistency, and these five words/objectives keep it all in perspective. Educating the client (and sales force/agent), and creating alliances (partnering-up) will further enhance the bond between ideas and results. Good stuff. Hard work. And fun along the way.
