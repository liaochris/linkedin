Laura Stamer’s Summary

Laura Stamer works in the field of architecture, lighting, photography and theater.

Architectural lighting projects in progress is facade lighting for the Royal Danish Playhouse, lighting for Copenhagen Airport, and lighting for Højbro Plads.

Recently Laura produced videographics and animations for the opera Lucia de Lammermoor in the Royal Theater, Miraklet at K2 and Med Venlig Deltagelse at the Royal Theater and the Ballet Nøddeknækkeren.

In the area of photography you can see examples of interior photos in the magazine Forum AID 3.07, where Laura has done a series of Krabbesholm Højskole 'the colored rooms', decorated by Nervous in the Service.

Laura Stamer’s Specialties:

Architectural photography - buildings to interior design and product design
Videograhics and animations for theater, performances and shows
Architectural lighting, interior installations to urban lighting
