Paddy Tarlton’s Summary

I am an engaging, stimulating communicator, poised and capable of projecting enthusiasm and warmth, and a motivator to other people. I have a strong sense of urgency, initiative and competitive drive to get things done, with emphasis on understanding effectively in influencing and persuading others to act. I am a confident and venturesome “doer” and decision-maker who will delegate details and can also delegate responsibility and authority when necessary. A self-starter who can also be skillful at training and developing others, I apply pressure for results, but in doing so, my style is more “selling” than “telling”.

At ease and self-assured with groups or in making new contacts, I have an invigorating impact on people, and am always “selling” in a general sense. I learn and react quickly and work at a faster-than-average pace; able to adapt quickly to change and variety in my work.

In general terms, I am an ambitious and driving person who is motivated by opportunity for advancement to level of responsibility where I can use my skills as a team builder, motivator and mover.

I am a motivating and enthusiastic teacher and trainer. If I can get 25 people in a room that's 105 degrees to do what I tell them for 90 minutes...I can teach anything and train anyone!
