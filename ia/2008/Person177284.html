Steven I. Lanzet’s Summary

Steven Lanzet is the Director of the Interactive Conversation Project at Healthwise. Along with his creative team, he is creating on-line interactive multi-media productions where participants can get personalized help with making better health decisions. Check it out. It's way cool.
He is a Idaho Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor and Marriage & Family Therapist and was Founder and Clinical Director at Boise Counseling Center, a private counseling and consulting practice specializing in helping people with Addiction and Metnal Health issues and has worked with Impaired Health Care Professionals. He fulfilled a long held dream of living overseas where he spent two years (2004 & 2005) in England directing a Treatment Centre for Addiction and Mental Health Issues.

Along with his behavioral health background, he has satisfied his interest in theater by co-founding a community theater company called Spontaneous Productions where he produced, directed, and performed.
