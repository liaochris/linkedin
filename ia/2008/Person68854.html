George C. Schwelling’s Summary

I represent more than1,500 CEOs, CFOs and COOs who have run start up companies up to $100,000,000 and they have net worth's of $1,000,000 to $10,000,000 or more and are willing to invest at least $250-500k in a company they would work for or run. I charge modest recruitment fees for these candidates. (Employers pay my fees, not candidates.) I am currently working with 7 private equity firms which say that my candidates are the best they have seen in the industry and plan on using my services for future executive search needs.

For companies which are very large and are not seeking any funding from their candidates I can draw on all of my candidates and charge a resonable flat fee per candidate and instead of being paid up front, I am paid 10% of whatever salary, bonuses and/or commissions may be paid to my candidates each month until my fee is paid in full. If for any reason my candidates leave your firm or are terminated, you do not owe me any futher payments and I do not refund any fees I may have been paid.

For companies which want to start up a new business, division or sub-unit and want an executives to come in on a sweat equity basis, I can provide candidates and take a fee of 10% of what you pay my candidates the first month once you start paying them and then for the next 24 months. What could be easier if you want high talent and want to pay them when they preform.

For companies seeking large numbers of salesmen, I can work on a small fee per placement plus an override on commissions or earnings of the salesmen over time.
