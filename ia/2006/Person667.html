Celeste Blackwell’s Summary

My strengths lie in the Training/Teaching, presentation, building physical infrastruture and creating solutions.

I am quite satisfied when I can serve people in putting technology to use as a “problemsolver”. I am very comfortable with both Macintosh and Windows technology, but I am not afraid to use other systems when the need develops.(Successfully trained as SUN administrator for example - unfortunately didn’t have the opportunity to implement this knowledge.)

English is my mother-tongue, my german is fluent in spoken and written word, and I have a basic command of spoken french. I’ve been primarily employed in the Advertising, New Media and Software and publishing branches, and my most recent position was with a System integrator in Germany, where Web Master, email based marketing, Sales support, Training, End User software documentation and troubleshooting belong to my list of responsibilities.
