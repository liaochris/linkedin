Diane Kulisek (dkulisek@capatrak.com)’s Summary

My passion is to help you consistently deliver quality to your customers. I bring more than 20 years of quality and organizational excellence expertise to the challenge of assuring that you have a long, rewarding and profitable relationship with your customers. In addition to my career experience qualifications, I hold a Master of Science in Engineering from CSU Northridge, a BA in Biology, and graduate certificates in both environmental planning and program management. The American Society for Quality has Certified me as a Quality Engineer and as a Manager of Quality / Organizational Excellence. I am registered as a Certified Professional Manager with the Institute for Certified Professional Managers in association with the National Management Association. For a more comprehensive overview of my professional experience, please see my resume on the CAPAtrak website at: http://www.capatrak.com/pdfs/DGKR.pdf.
