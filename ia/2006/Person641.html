Caroline Williams’s Summary:

Caroline M. Williams, co-founder of Spark, brings a pragmatic, solution focused, coaching style to her clients in a personable and effective manner. Caroline focuses her talent and enthusiasm on helping people reach their true potential. Her ability to see the big picture drives her vision for holistic coaching with no borders or limits.

With over 15 years of expertise in mentoring and coaching in such diverse industries as Insurance, Information Technology and Education, Caroline brings a unique perspective to assist her clients in clarifying their wants, exploring their gifts and discovering the important things that they have neglected. She is accomplished in strategizing in a way that is creative and fun. Above all, she is committed to nothing but the best for her clients.

Caroline, a graduate of the University of Toronto is an Adler Trained Coach. The Alder School of Professional Coaching of Toronto Canada is recognized by the International Coaching Federation and is an accredited coach-training institute. Caroline is also certified by Thomas Canada to administer DISC assessments.
