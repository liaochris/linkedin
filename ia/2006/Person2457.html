Lee LeFever’s Summary

I have created, designed and managed online communities since 1999. I founded Common Craft in 2003 to work with businesses to understand the realities of online communities and work with design teams to build websites that serve the community and the business. We call this "Social Design for the Web".
Common Craft specializes in early-stage community strategy and design. We provide strategic consulting, conceptual design and technology selection services. I have worked with a wide variety of clients such as March of Dimes, Boeing, Microsoft and Geffen Records.
