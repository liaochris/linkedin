Dannie Jost’s Summary

Consultant in intellectual property strategy with focus in start-ups and small and medium enterprises. Occasional innovation policy advisor to the Swiss government. Focus on fundamental aspects of sustainability within the social and technological contexts.

If it was up too me I would reform the whole educational system, however that is too big of a job for me and I often end being more interested in writing stories and books then on starting a revolution.

Dannie Jost’s Specialties:

Integrative strategy formulation. Dynamics of complex systems as applied to innovation and sustainability. Speciality in intellectual property.

Leadership development coach.

Research Fellow

More dynamics of desorption in the femtosecond regime. Worked in Wilson Ho's laboratory, and did some serious vicious programming with David Cohen.

Scientific Collaborator; Principal Investigator

Max-Planck-Institut für Quantenoptik

(Government Agency; 51-200 employees; Research industry)

1988 – 1992 (4 years)

Set up a new research laboratory dedicated to the study of the dynamics desorption from semiconductor surfaces upon picosecond and femtosecond laser excitation.

Did build up the whole laboratory together with colleague Stefan Kaesdorf who had a separate experimental set up, but shared lasers and other joint facilities. We started by buying screwdrivers, water distributors and the whole of what is necessary to run both the laser optics and the UHV equipment. My share of the budget to be managed was over DM 1 Mio.

Directed and supervised doctoral students.
