Christophe Herreman’s Summary

Christophe has been analyzing and developing medium to large e-learning applications for several years, ranging from standalone to intranet to Rich Internet applications.

His main interest and expertise lies in the field of Object Oriented Analysis, Design and Programming. He loves programming in ActionScript, .Net, PHP and Java.

As a passionate teacher, he has lectured on multimedia tools and general IT topics and is still giving talks at national academies and user groups in his free time.

He is also a strong believer in open source software and is and has been involved in a number of open source projects like AMFPHP - a Flash Remoting gateway, AS2lib - a ActionScript 2.0 framework and ARP - a patterns based micro architecture for Flash applications. Recently he has founded the open source Edumatic project, funded by Indie Education - its main goal is to provide an abstraction layer for developing e-learning applications for the Flash platform.

In his current position, Christophe is leading the Education team at Indie Group. He is working on projects for clients such as Selor, the Belgian Post, K.U.Leuven and many more. He enjoys mentoring and coaching, working with the team, taking on challenging projects and is always in for a good laugh.
