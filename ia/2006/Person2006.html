Jim Miller’s Summary:

20 years as a natural resource manager and supervisor of 18 employees over seven counties in southeast TN.

Seeking career in resource conservation field. I can retire early at my TN Wildlife Resources Agency job after 30 years, and I would love to continue working in the conservation field. Prefer field work as I really enjoy being outdoors, and it is what I have enjoyed most about my work with the TWRA. It is also where some of the most important work gets done.

I would love working for a governmental agency that has resource inventory needs. I would love to work for a land trust that needed conservation easements written to protect important or sensitive habitat, or to keep a farm from being developed.

I would also enjoy and be successful at helping private non-profit land trusts raise money from donors. In my volunteer capacity as the sponsorship officer for the Professional eBay Sellers Alliance, I commonly negotiated sponsorship amounts of $10,000, and less commonly negotiated sponsorships in greater amounts.
