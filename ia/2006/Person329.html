Valery Loranger’s Summary

Valéry started off on her own as an e-business consultant in 2005 after a decade of putting her strategic skills to work for such prestigious clients as Astral Media, Bell Canada, Bell Mobility, BMO, Fido Microcell Solutions, Nolitours, Royal Mint, SRC and Schering.
Valéry’s clients count on her to design creative, high-impact strategies for their online presence. Working in close collaboration with her partners, she provides a complete spectrum of services, from strategic planning and search engine optimization (SEO) to web development.
After earning her Masters degree in Fine Arts, Valéry launched her career in new technologies more than ten years ago. She helped found ICARI, a renowned training institute in 2D and 3D broadcast animation in 1993 (http://www.icari.com/). And she spent the better part of the last decade as a Group Account Director at Fjord Interactive Marketing + Technology.
Her responsibilities at Fjord, a division of Cossette Communication Group, the largest marketing communications firm in Canada, required Valéry to work in close collaboration with every field of expertise within the Group, from DM to PR and mass advertising. She contributed greatly to the business success of all the clients under her charge and gained enormous experience in how to integrate interactive strategies into the overall media mix of a mass campaign.
Valéry’s portfolio includes more than 20 award-winning websites and interactive campaigns for her main client, Bell Canada. One of her most recent campaigns, Bell's Olympic sponsorship, won at the 2005 Digital Marketing Awards in the site/event category.
