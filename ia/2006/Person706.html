Technical and Creative Recruiter at LBHR Media

(Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

May 2004 – September 2005 (1 year 5 months)

Utilising my technical skills, networking skills and general firefighting skills I decided to try my hand at recruitment consulting. I found it was too sales-y and not enough consulting for my liking, but I learnt an awful lot during my year as a recruiter.

My main functions and skills were winning and developing new business, and trying to be someone no-one expects with a title like 'recruitment agent'. I wanted to try and take a real consultative approach to companies' human resourcing needs, as well as be an advisor to my candidates on their best move for their careers.

My clients included Dare, Good Technology, Exposure, View UK, AKQA, 12foot6, Lateral, Tonic, Modem Media, Now Wash Your Hands (NWYH), uSwitch, UpMyStreet, The Search Works (formerly WSPS), Deal Group Media (DGM), Yahoo, Kelkoo, Overture, Ogilvy, and many more.
