Dave Greenwood’s Summary

I have around twenty years of telecoms experience spanning a wide variety of roles, including consulting, operations, interim management and Venture Capital. For six of the last ten years I have been based in India working initially as a CTO and Director of Operations for a GSM operator before pursuing various entrepreneurial projects.
I recently moved to Australia where I have launched a new venture as a strategic partnership with Ericsson to provide end-to-end testing of telecoms products and services in true carrier environments.
I also provide consulting services on strategy, telecoms operations and technology commercialization.
