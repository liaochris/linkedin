Gary Turner’s Summary:

Force of nature levels of passion and infectious enthusiasm for; technology, business, selling, marketing, being successful, communicating, winning, generating ideas and strategic vision.

At the age of twenty I was selling entire enterprise wide tech infrastructures to all manner of business sectors, large and small; from public transport operators and missile guidance design teams to brick manufacturers and media businesses.

Today I'm managing director of a 25 year old small to mid-market ERP software business whose apps are relied upon by over 30,000 businesses. Since assuming board responsibility for sales in 2002, Pegasus revenues have transformed from a state of decline to almost doubling inside of three years with profit running consistently above 30% of revenue throughout.
