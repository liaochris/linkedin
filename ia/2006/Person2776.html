Mike Broggie’s Summary

Over 10 years experience with virtually every link in the digital media value chain: acquisition and ingest; network storage subsystems; content delivery networks; and emerging sponsorship models accross multiple platforms. My early career spans 10+ years as a marketing and branding generalist and entrepreneur.

Work primarily with early-stage companies requiring strong business development expertise to establish early strategic wins.

Quick Bio:

Mike has a reputation for bringing innovative products and services to market quickly by securing key reference accounts and strategic alliances - helping early-stage media technology and content development companies open new revenue channels as they prepared for VC funding or acquisition.

Broggie is active in professional organizations which are literally defining the digital media landscape. He is a founding board member of the Global Society for Asset Management, is a member of the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences’ Interactive Media peer group, and serves as an executive committee member for the Interactive Television Alliance. He has moderated and presented on panels at several media, software, and technology industry events in the U.S. and Europe including: Digital Hollywood; National Association of Broadcasters; NATPE; SIGGRAPH; CES; MediaForum; CeBIT; Photokina; and the IEG Sponsorship Forum.

Prior to his consulting career, Mike led enterprise business development for Avid Technology’s streaming media and storage product group, Trilligent. And as the lead business development director for Webcasts.com, he helped ramp the online media startup from pre-revenue to a $155 million acquisition-to-IPO within 14 months.

As an agency account manager and operations specialist, he developed and executed national event and sponsorship marketing campaigns for General Motors, MCI, Ford, General Mills, and other Fortune500 clients.

Mike Broggie’s Specialties:

Global business development, digital media business strategy and technical architecture, strategic alliance sourcing and execution, startup-to-IPO experience, contract development and negotiation.
