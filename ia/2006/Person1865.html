Jason W. Womack, M.Ed., M.A.’s Summary

A member of the National Speaker’s Association, I present individual executive coaching programs and large-group seminars to large North American and Western European companies. I demonstrate my commitment to workplace education through speaking and writing on personal productivity and effective coaching models.

I do have a deep reservoir of inspiration and enthusiasm. My high energy and background as a professional educator create opportunities for people to learn and grow. Articles I write appear in Leadership Excellence, Santa Barbara Family Life, ASTD’s Training and Development Magazine, the Los Angeles Times newspaper, Personal Excellence and more. In addition, I contribute regularly to several web-columns.

My intention is to understand and demonstrate peak focus and high performance at work and at life. I use my experience in public education and in corporate environments to share the effectiveness of focus, purpose and control in workplace productivity.

I hold Master’s degrees in both Education and Psychology. While a teacher in California for 5 years, my teaching credentials included: Social Sciences, Spanish, English and Multi-culturalism (BCLAD). Oh, and I train and compete as an age-group athlete in sprint and Olympic distance triathlon competitions!

I present customized workshops, keynote presentations and individual coaching and consulting programs - all focused on life/work balance, goal achievement and time management.
