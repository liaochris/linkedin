Guy Kirkwood’s Summary

Guy is a market developer and advisor on sourcing; outsourcing, shared services, BPO and offshoring.

He is a member of and regular contributor to the shared services and BPO association (SBPOA), a member of the chartered institute of marketing (CIM), the European marketing academy (EMAC) and the European institute for advanced studies in management (EIASM). His papers have been published world-wide and he writes a syndicated column - BPO Backchat.

Guy works on European sourcing and transformation market development with EquaTerra. He is the sourcing council member for the Gerson Lehrman Group, is advisor to CityTonic and is on the European advisory board of Business Week. Guy is also a speaker at events organized by the likes of Management Today and the Economist.

Guy has lived in Germany, the UK and France. He holds a BA and an MPhil and has a background in training, publishing media and public service.
