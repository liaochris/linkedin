Michael Seaton’s Experience

Director, Digital Marketing

Scotiabank

(Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; BNS; Marketing and Advertising industry)

January 1998 – Present (9 years)

Michael currently leads Scotiabank’s digital marketing strategy in Canada, responsible for the strategic digital marketing across all channels for personal and business customers.

Michael is a thought leader and innovator in customer experience, digital marketing and branding. He has over fifteen years industry experience with extensive expertise.

He is an active speaker, winner of numerous awards, author of CMA’s Guide to eMail Marketing and various published articles, co-creator of www.canadianmarketingblog.com and creator of www.theclientside.blogspot.com, host of The Money Clip podcast and correspondent for Across the Sound - New Marketing podcast.

Michael is on the Board of Directors at the Canadian Marketing Association and Chair of the Digital Marketing Executive Council. Sits on the Board of Directors with AIMS and is on the Advertising Council Executive with the IAB.
