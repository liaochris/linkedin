Ludovico Magnocavallo’s Summary

I have spent the past 11 years working as an IT professional for demanding, fast-paced companies by day, and developing Open Source applications or running my own startups by night.

I am currently working as a Systems Architect for Italy's leading payments and credit cards processing company. My latest toy is Lightpress, a fast frontend for the popular Wordpress blogging platform, which is one of the few Textdrive sponsored projects and drives some of the largest blogs in Europe: Blogo (Italy, 10m page views per month), WeblogsSL (Spain, >10m page views per month), Ebay Spain Blog.
