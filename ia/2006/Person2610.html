Marco Casario’s Summary

Marco Casario is one of the most dynamic developers in the Adobe (formerly Macromedia) world. Flex and FlashLite certified Instructor, Flash and Dreamweaver certified, he intensively collaborates with Adobe Italy as a speaker and promoter of several events and roadshows.

Marco has recently found Comtaste S.r.l. (www.comtaste.com), a company dedicated to explore new frontiers in the Web 2.0 field, where the themes of accessibility and usability have added further importance to the PDF format and the relevant Acrobat application tools.

Marco’s blog entitled “hands on Adobe world” (http://casario.blogs.com) is currently receiving several thousands of unique visitors every day. In the section “ABOUT” of his blog there is a list of his most recent works.
