Ian Kennedy’s Summary

Ian is a strategic General Manager with broad business perspective gained from accomplishments in general management, customer service, operations, sales, marketing and accounting, in large companies and start ups, both here and abroad.

Ian is a Senior Consultant and Coach with The Schegg Group, LLC, a Human Resources consulting firm focusing on career transition, recruiting and executive coaching. Ian was CEO of Australian Skandia, Ltd., in Sydney, Australia. Prior to that he was a Senior Vice President and Director of Customer Service at American Skandia, Inc.. Before that he was a Part Owner and Vice President of Synergy Plus Sotware, Inc., a software development company serving the insurance industry. He also started Ian Kennedy Associates, a consulting firm focusing on leadership development, organizational change and team building. Prior to that he was Vice President and Head of Customer Service for the U.S. division of Sun Life of Canada.

Ian’s work building high-performance organizations is featured in two management books: Market Ownership, The Art & Science of Becoming #1, by William A. Sherden, and Fun Works, Creating Places Where People Love to Work, by Leslie Yerkes, and in periodicals: Mercer Management Journal, Boston Business Journal and U.S. News & World Report. Babson College developed a business case based on Ian’s work, for its MBA and executive development programs.

Ian earned his MBA from Babson College, Wellesley, Massachusetts and his BS in Accounting from Concordia University, Montreal, Canada. He is qualified in MBTI (Myers Briggs Type Indicator) and is certified to teach The Organization Workshop, developed by Barry Oshry, Power and Systems, Inc.
