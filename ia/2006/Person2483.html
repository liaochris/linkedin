Lisa Kofod’s Summary

Writer of marketing and new business proposals, web content, internal process documents, scripts, speeches and business-to-business direct mail, brochures and advertising copy for a variety of corporate clients including:
· All in One Productions, Inc., a full-service multi-language broadcast video production and post-production facility;
· Creative Entertainment, Inc., a division of the Disney Company, specializing in meeting and conference production and management;
· Encore Productions, an event and production company; and
· J. Walter Thompson, a business-to-consumer advertising agency

Director of Special Projects for O’Connell Meier, Inc., a business-to-business advertising agency. Key creative on a variety of Agency clients including the Direct Marketing Association and the American Association for Training and Development. Most recently account and project-managed the creation and implementation of four Portals for ASTD. These data-driven web sites were the centerpiece for niche-specific marketing campaigns that used a combination of live chats, interactive newsletters and other online events to drive registration and product sales.

Special Liaison to ASTON Associates, a financial restructuring firm. Completed work on three financial books: Reengineering the Bank (Irwin Publishing, Chicago, IL) by Aston's chairman, Paul Allen; the international edition of the book, Creating the New Bank, published by Lafferty Publishing in Dublin, Ireland; and most recently, the paperback edition of Reengineering the Bank, published by McGraw Hill. Produced a seven-city book and speaker’s tour of Reengineering the Bank in concert with the Bank Administration Institute (BAI).

Past President and Director of Sponsor Relations of the New York Chapter of the Media Communications Association International (MCA-I). Formerly the International Television Association, MCA-I is the largest trade association of non-broadcast electronic media professionals in the world.
