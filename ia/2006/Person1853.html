Jason Loverude’s Summary

I started my career at at The University of North Dakota as the webmaster/ web designer for my alma mater. I was charged to redesign the UND homepage - which I did twice - UND has released my latest design in November 2006, and my first one summer 2005. At UND I trained faculty and staff how to use/utilize the web templates, maintained/redesigned the Office of University Relations (OUR) and UND News Sites - www.universityrelations.und.edu, www.und.edu/news - the UND Interactive Map/Online Tour, UND Strategic Plan Site, many online magazines (eZines) and much more.

In both my undergraduate career and my first job, UND (and it's faculty/staff/co-workers) has really prepared me for a long & successful career.

Not long after starting at UND, I started my freelance web design business (NDEZIN Web Design, www.ndezin.com) for supplemental income. I have since taken this with me to Las Vegas, NV and am still getting new clients!

I took a position with Southwest Title Company in Las Vegas in June 2006 and I have designed a new website for SWT and am an integral member of the SWT Marketing Team.
