Or-Tal Kiriati’s Summary

I am a marketing strategy consultant specializing in branding, naming and web presence. I have very broad experience and many satisfied customers.

My favourite customers are startup companies because I enjoy the interaction with motivated and enthusiastic entrepreneurs. My contributions in a single project positively impacts the long-run success of my clients.

Some of the projects I have been involved in include:

*Analysis & assessment of brand and positioning
*Creating a detailed marketing plan, including advertising and PR
*Creating a branding brief - including both visual and verbal brief
*Naming - inventing a name (plus slogan / moto when needed) for products and companies
*Website critical assessment - analysis and recommendation for changes and improvements
*Web presence planning - designing the system flow and GUI brief
*Outsourced Marketing Activities - Implementing marketing strategy for clients. Creating presentations and marketing communications materials, designing exhibition booths and executive events. Supervising and liaising with outsources such as graphic artists, web site creators, copywriters, advertising firms and PR consultants regarding the actual implementation of strategy

Or-Tal Kiriati’s Specialties:
