Kelly Drahzal’s Experience

Software Quality Engineering Manager at IBM/Rational

(Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Computer Software industry)

April 2003 – Present (3 years 7 months)

Manager in charge of the Rational Common Components QE Teams, comprised of Rational Administrator, Rational Common Licensing, Common Windows Install, Rational SitePrep, and Common Test Services. Managed 12 QE engineers. Modeled strong cross-functional team, working closely with product management, development, documentation, program management and tech support engineers during all phases of project cycle. Actively contributed during all phases of the development cycle; including reviewing and editing software requirements specifications and design docs, defect triage, collaboration with stakeholder product groups, Change Control Board meetings, customer escalations, scheduling and resource planning. Created and maintained team and individual internal blogs and wikis. Conducted “bootcamps” on key team technologies. Strong advocate for the customer.

Sr. Software Quality Engineer at Rational Software Corporation

(Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; RATL; Computer Software industry)

2000 – 2003 (3 years)

Performed quality engineering for 5 synchronized Suite releases, and numerous patches and service releases. Team Lead supervising 2 test engineers, responsible for four components: the Rational Administrator; Rational Common Licensing; Common User Administration, Authorization, and Authentication; and the Rational Synchronizer. Each of these products interface extensively with other Rational products, requiring collaboration between many remote business units, and expertise across the product set. Utilized Rational’s full suite of products, including Requisite Pro, ClearQuest, Robot, Test Manager, ClearCase and Rose. Performed testing in all areas of QE including build verification, feature acceptance, functional, suites integration, defect validation, internationalization, localization, and final checklist testing. Created QE project plans, test plans, procedures, release criteria, and schedules. Collaborated with development and technical support to quickly resolve customer issues.

Software Consultant at Rational Software Corporation

(Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; RATL; Computer Software industry)

1997 – 2000 (3 years)

Test Engineer for a number of Rational Rose releases. Performed quality engineering for the following releases: Microsoft Visual Modeler 2.0, 3 releases of Rational Rose and Rational Suite 1.0 and 1.5. Utilized both manual and automated testing, using Rational’s full suite of products, in all areas of QE test including build verification, feature acceptance testing, functional testing, suites integration, and final checklist testing. Created test plans, procedures, release criteria, and schedules. Participated in the defect tracking and triage process. Developed training material and performed training for new hires. Worked closely with remote team members in Sweden, and across the country

Senior Consultant at General Electric Consulting Services

(Public Company; Defense & Space industry)

1985 – 1989 (4 years)

Remote Sites Requirement Task Leader, responsible for the Automatic Fault Location/Isolation software of the East Coast Over-the-Horizon (OTH) radar system, and for assuring that software requirements specifications were met. Testing efforts resulted in a re-design of the AFL/AFI software. Involvement in this system continued through Formal Qualification Tests, which resulted in customer sell-off.

Software Engineer at USAF

Delivered hardware/software interface applications developed in FORTRAN and assembly language. Produced documentation and test procedures to ensure adherence to design specifications and strict military requirements.
