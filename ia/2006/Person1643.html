Helen Keegan’s Summary

Retail and marketing professional with 18 years experience in several industries including fashion retail, property, telecoms and public transport. In the last 6 years or so I've specialised in mobile marketing and mobile media.

I also teach mobile marketing and media at the University of Westminster and the Institute of Direct Marketing (and direct to clients too).

I also run Swedish Beers Mobile Networking in London with Steve Flaherty - go to http://www.swedishbeers.blogspot.com for more info on our events.

I'm a co-founder of Women in Mobile Data Association alongside Lisa Modisette and Jessica Sandin.

Helen Keegan’s Experience

Managing Director

BeepMarketing Ltd

(Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

February 2002 – Present (4 years 11 months)

With 10 years retail experience coupled with 5 years marketing consultancy for blue chip companies like Alcatel, First Group, Hewlett Packard and TNT, I ventured into mobile marketing with ZagMe. ZagMe sent text messages to customers while they shopped at Lakeside & Bluewater shopping malls, two of the largest malls in Europe.

ZagMe is no longer. Since its demise, I've been running mobile marketing consultancy, BeepMarketing connecting clients with customers using the mobile phone. This includes using SMS, voice, ringtones, logos, animations, video, MMS, wapsites, games & more. Clients include Samsung, Sony Ericsson, Emap, News International & Danone & various retailers, brands & financial services companies.

I offer consultancy on all aspects of mobile media & marketing & training courses on mobile media & marketing. I run the mobile marketing modules for the Institute of Direct Marketing's courses & run bespoke courses for clients. Recent clients include Etisalat & NewsCorp.
