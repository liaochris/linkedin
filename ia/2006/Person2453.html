Robert Leathern’s Summary

In 2006 I founded Fynbos Group, a consultancy to help companies build new revenue sources and business models using online channels.

We combine our extensive experience in finance, market research, analysis and optimization with in-depth current online industry expertise to help companies build profits online. We also work with venture capital firms, hedge funds and investment banks to provide strategic/financial advice and due diligence on firms in the interactive space. More information and blog can be found at www.fynbosgroup.com

I am passionate about devising new businesses and the business models needed to make them work, as well as being a great manager who loves to manage product, marketing and business development teams that get the job done quickly and efficiently.

I grew up in South Africa and enjoy speaking to entrepreneurs and companies who have an eye on the South African market (consumer, labor or technology) or South African firms looking to expand in the US/globally.
