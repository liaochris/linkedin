Brad Anderson’s Summary

I am a Application Developer living in Atlanta, Georgia. I specialize in Rich Internet Applications [RIAs] and database driven internet applications. My 6+ years experience with Flash and ActionScript and 6+ years experience in application development [PHP, JAVA, .NET, and DHTML] and design allows me to create a rich and tactful user experience.

As a freelancer I develop using ActionScript 1.0/2.0/3.0, AJAX, PHP, JAVA and other technologies to build dynamic, web enabled, desktop applications. I have worked on projects for clients such as Coca-Cola, Deutsche Bank, ING, Hewitt Associates, Fox, The Weather Channel, POWERade, NASA, and Verizon where I developed both front-end and back-end applications and designed interactive interfaces. I have also previously worked for The Weather Channel, What's Up Interactive, Applied Theory, Avienda Technologies / SilverPOP Systems and Media1st and still maintain working relationships with them. I continue to build applications for clients as a freelancer and would love the opportunity to provide my services to you. Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions for me.
