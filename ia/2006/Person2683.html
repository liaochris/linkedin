Paul R. Marobella, Jr.’s Summary

Prior to joining Wirestone to be responsible for their Chicago office, I was the Executive Vice President of Strategic Planning, Client Services and Business Development for the 575-person agency, Ambrosi - Responsible for overseeing Ambrosi’s retail clients in Chicago as well as directing the agency’s strategic planning practice, which included research, consumer insights and trend forecasting. The client base included, The Home Depot, The Home Depot Expo, Sears, Office Depot, Godiva, Fannie May, Wahl Clipper, Sunbeam, Mr. Coffee, Linens N Things, The Great Indoors and OfficeMax.

Before Ambrosi, I was the EVP, Brand Strategy and the Managing Director of Greco Ethridge Group's Boston office- opening the office from the ground-up for the NYC-based agency and was responsible for all leadership/recruiting, strategic guidance and business development. We grew the agency from $10MM to $100MM in billings in just three years from clients such as IBM, 3Com, Siemens, Raindance, InformationWeek and CMP Media.

My love for advertising comes from my passion in positioning brands for explosive growth, working in all types of media channels and the fact that I don't have to wear a tie everyday.

I was lucky enough to be a two-time winner of Ad Age’s Best and Brightest Strategist Award in 1998 and 2001 And, our team won Agency of The Year from Ad Age's BtoB Magazine while at Greco Ethridge Group.

While still a city-boy at heart, I brave the commute to Chicago and live in the western suburb of Naperville with my wife Joann, step-son Nicholas, two-year old daughter Ava Grace and her goldfish, Dorothy.

When not in the office or on the road, you might find me prowling the local movie theaters for a good bag of popcorn, going all-in at a local poker game or barreling down a mountain on his snowboard.
