Beth Kanter’s Summary

I am a trainer, coach, and consultant to nonprofits in the area of effective technology use. I have worked on projects that include: training, curriculum development, research, and evaluation. I have expertise in how to use new web tools (blogging, tagging, wikis, photo and video sharing, etc) to support nonprofit goals. I also have experience coaching "digital immigrants" in the use of these tools. I am a professional blogger and get paid to write about the use of social media tools in the nonprofit sector for social change.

Please see my extended biography with links to my projects, articles, and blogs at http://www.bethkanter.org/about.htm
