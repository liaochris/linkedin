Josh Gampel’s Summary

I specialize in recruiting and sourcing top marketing talent across the country. My exact “niche” is VP of Marketing searches and Search Engine Marketing/Optimization. I understand the internet marketing space from the high level of strategy down to what it takes to acquire a customer and keep them coming back to your site.

At any given time I am recruiting for:

VP, Marketing- B-C or B-B high level strategy types that are not afraid to roll up their sleeves and lead by example to get the work done. Exact fit will depend on the role.

Director of Search Engine Marketing- This person has been in the SEM/SEO space since the beginning and has managed a staff of analysts as well as millions of dollars in budget on the client or agency side.

Account Manager for Online Marketing agencies- I am currently representing several internet marketing agencies across the country that are looking for specialties in SEM, SEO, Affiliate, Internet Analytics as well as the ability to establish relationships with a client.

If you are a candidate who fits the bill for any of the above or a client that is looking for top talent please feel free to give me a call to discuss.

I can be reached at 203-270-4800 ext 109 or email me at josh@mhrecruiters.com
