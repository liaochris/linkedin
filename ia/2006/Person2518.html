Louise Fletcher’s Summary

As a former HR executive, I bring an unusual perspective to career marketing. I founded Blue Sky Resumes to empower job seekers and change the way people think about job search. Our clients may come to us for a resume, but they get so much more than that.

We help each and every client develop a clear and compelling value proposition, we articulate that value proposition in career marketing materials ranging from resumes to web portfolios, and we provide our clients with a step-by-step strategy for taking charge of their job search.

In addition to managing Blue Sky, I recently co-founded Career Hub, a group blog which brings together some of the best minds in the world of recruitment and career management. Our goal is to share our knowledge with as many people as possible while also raising the profile of our industry.

I am passionate about technology and continue to explore how job seekers can use new technologies to market themselves effectively. I am currently writing the first in a series of books about proactive job search.
