Leonard Boord’s Summary

4 Time CEO.

Currently I am involved in two new ventures. A Web 2.0 reputational model and a series of sites focused on exploting new media opportunities.

As CEO and co-founder of ProcuraDigital. My two partners and I established ProcuraDigital as one of the leading providers of e-auctions and strategic sourcing for businesses operating in Latin America.

In March 2001, we succeeded in signing a purchase agreement with Santander Bank. We sold a majority stake for $20mm.
In 2005 transacting over US$1B in spend volume and generating savings of US$160 million for companies such as Johnson & Johnson, Nestl� and Grupo Bimbo.
Prior to founding ProcuraDigital, myself and other team members developed several other Latin American companies from conceptual stage to a fully operational business. In 1999, a group of investors including myself acquired agffinityLAC group, a computer technology company with US$1 million in revenues and operations in Miami. We took this small company and increased the company's revenues from US$1 to US$12 million within a year and completed a merger with Contelsa in Argentina and Crandal Telecom in Brazil. The merger resulted in the creation of Commlogik, now a leading computer telephony distributor in Latin America.

Straight out of college and recently married I was lucky enough to ride the PC revolution. Along with two partners we founded InterPC in 1983, a South Florida integrator and IT distribution company for businesses in Latin America.

We succeeded in establishing it as one of the top 20 Hispanic companies in the United States, with 5 subsidiaries, 200 employees and US$160 million in revenues. My partners and I found a willing buyer in a Fortune 500 Company for this business.
I studied Computer Science and Math at The University of Miami. I am fluent in English and Spanish and have a good working knowledge of Portuguese.
Married since 1982 I am the proud father of two boys soon to be college bound.
