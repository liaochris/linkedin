Tom Klein’s Summary

A leading authority on Enterprise Marketing Management, Tom Klein has led numerous business strategy, marketing strategy and transformation engagements for Global 1000 companies, including Merrill Lynch, Medtronic, Yahoo!, Intuit, Coca-Cola, Host Marriott, James River, ConAgra, Novartis, Borders, and Soda-Club.

He is the co-author of Enterprise Marketing Management: The New Science of Marketing - a book considered to be the definitive statement for transforming marketing into a discipline that can create sustained, profitable, organic growth.

Tom is a professional speaker on the subjects of marketing strategy, brand positioning, brand activation, customer relationship management, reengineering and customer-centered transformation.

Tom works with clients to position their brands to communicate brand benefits that sell, connect marketing metrics to financial outcomes, activate brands with customers and consumers, and reengineer marketing and sales processes leveraging enterprise information and technologies.

Tom has also served as Executive Director of Strategy at the Chanel Group, overseeing new market entry and strategy in Asia and the Americas. He has also served as a Senior Associate at management consulting firm CSC Index with Jim Champy and Mike Hammer, authors of Reengineering the Corporation. He started his marketing career at Nabisco Biscuit Company, managing well-known brands Teddy Grahams, Harvest Crisps, and Barnum's Animal Crackers, among others.
