Laurent Kretz’s Summary

With a Master in Management, major in Project Management, and a BA in Business and Administration, major in Marketing, I try to lead my career towards a general management prospective with an emphasis on Information Systems. My first experience was at Lafarge, the word wide leader in construction materials. I implemented a worldwide Purchasing intranet and assisted in deploying a european ePurchasing/eSourcing tool with a scope of €35M. To improve my purchasing and project management skills, I took over a broader mission for Air France in the US, with a scope of $50M. I also developed skills in web development and dynamic website construction. I took over a few projects personally and professionally, as for Altran where I developed an intranet for the French Insurance Companies Union.

Thanks to my various experiences (information systems, sales, marketing, HR support, purchasing), I have strong skills in problem understanding and solving. Understanding all the assets in a situation allows me to make efficient strategic decisions, targeting value creation, cost killing and image improvement.
