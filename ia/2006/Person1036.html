Danny Boice’s Summary:

Daniel (Danny) K. Boice is the Chief Executive Officer, Founder and Co-Chairman of Jaxara, the next generation of offshore software development. Jaxara projects revenues of US$2.5 million for the fiscal year ending January 2006, and employs more than 30 people in 3 countries and regions.

Boice is currently focused on launching Illuminate Bangadesh, a non-profit entity, which will focus on training and preparing underpriveledged Bangladeshi youth to participate in the Global Economy by teaching K-9 to read and write English and training high school and university students in modern information technologies and software practices. Illuminate Bangadesh also holds seminars in the United States to educate businesses on the benefits of outsourcing to Bangladesh. He visits Bangladesh 3-4 times per year to assist in the management of Illuminate Bangadesh.

Boice attended public elementary school and Washington DC's private DeMatha Catholic High School. There, he discovered his interest in software and business.

In 1999, Boice was contracted to program CatholicContacts.com, which is where he met Alexander S. Petty, Co-Founder of Jaxara.

Boice become a partner in CatholicContacts.com and after executing their exit strategy, he and Petty went on to found Jaxara in 2002.

Under Boice's leadership, Jaxara's mission has been to continually advance and improve global software development as a process, and change the way companies approach global software projects.

Currently, Boice serves on the Board of Directors of SearchToOwn, Inc. and Illuminate Bangldesh.

Boice has managed the planning and setup of offshore software development, user interface development, quality assurance and testing centers in Dhaka, Bangadesh, Bangolore, India, Cordoba, Argentina, Kiev, Ukraine, Dalal, China and various other countries.
