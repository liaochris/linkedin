Jin Guo’s Summary

Dr. Jin Guo is an established technologist in mobile Internet human language arena.

For Motorola, he productized the world-first mobile phone predictive text input method, which was 9 months ahead of AOL/Tegic's T9. For a Chinese company, he developed the world-first whole-sentence pinyin conversion input product, which was 6 years ahead of Microsoft's similar product.

Dr. Jin Guo is especially motivated by the sense of achievements from delivering innovative and useful products to the mass market. He is proud of seeing his invention and software serving hundreds of millions of users everyday.

Dr. Jin Guo has filed over 40 patents and published more than 30 research papers. He was a Distinguished Member of Technical Staff at Motorola.

Dr. Jin Guo has broad experiences at various managerial or leadership roles for small start-ups or big corporations in the US, Singapore or China. He also taught at leading Universities.
