Mike Williams’s Summary

Mike Williams originally started his career as a telecommunications engineer and spent over 17 years in a Telco environment, including over 10 years at operational management level, before moving into IT consultancy. Coming from a voice and data communications background he progressed into distributed, client/server computing and then onto Internet and E-Business solutions. He is an experienced Technical Architect, Business Analyst and Project Manager. His particular fields of IT expertise include: strategic planning; architecting end-to-end n-tier solutions; networking and infrastructure design; systems integration; web portal and enterprise system design. He is a Chartered Engineer and IS practitioner.
