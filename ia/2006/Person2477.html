10 years - Teaching, training, learning facilitation (including blended and e-learning programs)
5 years - Training manager (university, NGO)
5 years - Researcher (workplace learning, knowledge management, social software)
1 year - Research project manager

Current:
I work as a researcher: on my PhD ("Between passion and work: blogging practices of knowledge workers") and in several knowledge management projects with companies. In my work I bring together my experiences of facilitating learning and managing changes, my interest in technologies and my passion for understanding how people work. I write a weblog (blog.mathemagenic.com) in case you want to know more :)

Future:
Redesigning workplaces, personal routines, organisational processes and technologies to support knowledge worker productivity (and having fun at work)

Contexts: knowledge management, human resources development, technologies for collaboration, learning and knowledge sharing (including those under "social software" umbrella), technology adoption and change management
