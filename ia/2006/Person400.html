Michael Teitelbaum’s Summary

TruePresence is an interactive marketing firm that helps clients achieve greater success through an effective online presence. We empower our clients to realize their online potential by working with them to develop a strategically designed and developed website. Qualitiy site traffic is then generated by developing and implementing an online marketing plan that maximizes visibility across the web in relevant places including search engines, email communications and web advertising.

TruePresence is building a national brand through a franchise business model. We are committed to carefully identifying franchise candidates who have the characteristics required to be successful in our business. Individuals with a background or aptitude in sales and/or marketing is the first thing we look for. We then train and support them in every aspect of their business.
