Brian Bowman’s Summary

Brian Bowman is a skilled general manager with expertise in online product strategy, product management and marketing. With 16 years of experience, he has overseen the development of many award-winning consumer-facing websites such as: Yahoo 360, DisneyChannel.com, Oscars.com, Match.com, ZoogDisney.com, "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire” and Moviso.com.

Most recently Brian was VP of Community for Yahoo. While at Yahoo, he was solely responsible for all aspects associated with Yahoo’s online community applications (360, Groups, Photos and Avatars) and platforms (Blogs, Ratings & Reviews, Reputation, Message Boards) . As one of the few people in the world to have created more than one multi-million member online community, he has spent the past 10 years developing and running products based around member interaction and social networking.

Prior to Yahoo, he was VP of Product Management & Marketing for Infospace’s search and directory division, VP of Product Development for Match.com, VP and General Manager of ABC.com and held positions at Disney Channel, Turner, USWeb/Cybernautics and Metrolight Studios.

Brian graduated from Purdue University with a BA in Fine Art and is looking to connect with companies active in the consumer Internet space focused on the convergence of online entertainment, social software and community.
