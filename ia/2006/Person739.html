Chris Pallé’s Summary

Chris Pallé's professional career has spanned over 10 years. While primarily succeeding as a Interaction Designer, he has also contributed to award-winning animation projects; functioned as a print graphics artist; and a copywriter. Additionally, he has practiced health and wellness services as a personal fitness trainer for New York Sports Club.

As a visionary, Chris has focused on bleeding edge Internet technologies his entire career. Out of a near life-long desire to create interactive technology and media, he recognized the opportunities for an artistic, creative outlet on the Web when Mosaic and Spyglass were just beginning to merge graphics with HTML. Enamored by this, armed with SimpleText and a book on HTML 3.2, his journey began.

Even with all the technical abilities Chris brings to the table, perhaps most notable is his relentless work ethic. He is proficient in a telecommuting setting, maintaining discipline, focus, and thriving as a self-starter. He cultivated this valuable skill as he completed his multidisciplinary Bachelor's Degree in Graphics Arts, E-Business, and Technical Communications. He continues to nurture this skill by connecting, establishing, and maintaining professional relationships with colleagues all over the world.

Presently, Chris' projects include his own creative and interactive media company, Blue Flame, LLC; business development with SteelPixel (Web 2.0 Show) on the Podvine Project; serving on the board of directors at The Coopera.tv (a collective of Christian Web experts); and other activities currently under wraps.

Chris' unique skill set keeps him agile and motivated and is a rewarding asset for any team. He seeks to bring his leadership, solid work ethics, and technical proficiency to serve in an organization where he can bring encouragement; contribute to building up a successful, world-class team; and ultimately produce top-notch products and service for its customers.
