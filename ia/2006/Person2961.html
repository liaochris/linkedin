David Koopmans’s Summary:

David is a Director of Mokum Marketing, a specialist B2B marketing services firm he founded in 2001.

His experience in a broad range of business areas from Direct Marketing to Business Development and Strategic Marketing drives his practical, outcome driven approach to marketing. Since moving to Australia in 2001, David has held marketing roles with a range of publicly listed and private companies in particular in the ICT industry.

His mission is to help business navigate a marketing environment undergoing rapid change, driven by changing customer expectations, behaviour and technology.

David holds a Grad Dip in Marketing from Swinburne Unversity in Melbourne.
