Derek Fattal’s Summary

Over a decade of experience in online news pioneering the development of online newspapers. Having the good fortune to be in the right place at the right time has helped me meet and learn from many of the great people in this industry in both Europe and the USA.

I initiated the development and launch of www.jpost.com, and the subsequent growth of the site to become one of the most popular news sites outside the USA.

Strong expertise integrating print and online news staffs, developing 24x7 breaking news teams.

In 2002 I moved to Haaretz to take charge of online business development at the web site of Israel's top daily broadsheet. Increased online revenues and helped make Haaretz the most popular English language news source from Israel.

In May 2005 I returned to The Jerusalem Post to direct online content and service development once again. Initial challenges are to raise audience participation in the site, and expand its utility value.

To strengthen my business and operational planning skills I am currently studying for an MBA in Entrepreneurship & Innovation at ISEMI, which is introducing me to a whole new set of savvy people and great ideas.

My previous background as a lawyer has given me an insight into many of the legal and ethical issues involved in online development and deal making.
