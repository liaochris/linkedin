Mike Mann’s Summary

Michael Mann

Mike is the founder and Chairman of Grassroots.org (a 501c3 nonprofit), which is a global network providing free services to other nonprofits and promoting social action. Mike's book can be found online at http://www.grassroots.org/book/

From 1998-2005 Mike was the founder and president of BuyDomains.com which is the world's largest secondary market for Internet domain names, and one of the world's largest domain registration services, (Control sold to Highland Capital and Summit Partners) Mike also started the Seeq.com search engine portal.

Mike is a founder of BrowserMedia LLC, which has developed high end Web sites since 1998; Software.com LLC, a new download site; Phone.com LLC, a new VOIP service; X3O Emerging Technologies gaming centers (X30.com); Yield Software (web optimization) and Take It or Leave It furniture warehouse (TakeItOrLeaveIt.com).

Mike’s family operates Make Change! Trust which is a charitable fund supporting dozens of select 501c3 organizations. From 1998-2001 Mike served as the founding Chairman of the Board for ByteBack.org (501c3), which operates eight computer training centers serving the inner-city at no charge. From 1994 -1998 Mike was the founder and president of Internet Interstate which was a leading Internet provider and Web developer in the DC region (sold to Verio Inc, now NTT)
