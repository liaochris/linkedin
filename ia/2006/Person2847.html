Michael Martine’s Summary

I work full time, blog full time, write fantasy & sf, and create concept/character design art for stories/games. One of my main goals in life is to be at the edge of things and bring ideas to life by moving them from the edge closer to the mainstream. For example, creating GoogleTube Video of the Day was the right idea at the right time back in October of 2005, and it got noticed and it had a good run. I created a blog only for AMV's (Anime Music Videos) and got tired of it long before AMV's became popular (well, as popular as they would ever get, anyway). I created a blog to track the exploding internet video scene. Right now one of my main interests is in how so-called "virtual" currencies in MMOG's are crossing over with government-backed currencies (they're virtual too, really, when you think about it) and in-game socioeconomics.
