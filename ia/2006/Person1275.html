Pier Paolo Mucelli’s Summary:

eOffice [www.eOffice.net] takes advantage of a gap in the market for beautifully designed and high tech office environments for small businesses and start ups.
The first eOffice opened in London in 2002, the first open plan business centre in the UK. It offers virtual offices, hot desking options (with wireless access), flexible full time office solutions and a wide range of meeting facilities and videoconferencing. We bring people together and organize a monthly networking event called Net Thursday. eOffice has now served over a thousand clients, ranging from start ups to international companies who require a flexible office space, consultants, SMEs, and even large companies searching for creative meeting spaces.
eOffice was featured in CNN, BBC, and several international newpapers and magazines, including the FT, The Times, The Independent, Time Out and many more. We were Finalist at FX Design Award for Offices in 2003 and among 10 National Finalists at HSBC Start-Up Star Awards 2004.
