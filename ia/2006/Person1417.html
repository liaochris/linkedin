Frank Mulligan’s Summary

Siemens Ltd China, Strategic Planning Manager in the mid-90's. Chief Representative of largest UK headhunter, Norman Broadbent International until 2000.

Now CEO(owner) of Talent Software. We provide companies with an hiring solution that includes software (ATS) and outsourced staffing and operations (RPO).

More here on my blog:

http://www.talent-software.com/private/online_hiring_china

The flagship ATS product is called Talent and it includes functions to advertise job openings, generate name lists, manage a Career Section of a client website, manage candidate suppliers(recruiters), Phone Screen applicants, Record Phone Screens, do behavioural interviews, do video interviews, run Skills Tests and psychometric profiles, do reference checks, check degrees and certifications, test and make job offers, do exit interviews and so on. It is available in both Chinese and English.

We operate on an RPO basis so we can break up the application so that we handle any or all parts of the hiring cycle.
