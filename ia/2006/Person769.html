Chris Wilhelmi’s Summary

Chris Wilhelmi is a seasoned leader in the arena of CRM strategy, database marketing, and analytics. His core strength lies within the ability to integrate measurement with marketing and business decision making processes.

He started his career on the client side at MediaOne, which eventually became a key component of Comcast Cable. He directed all database marketing activities for the Bay Area and guided corporate-wide initiatives for marketing database design and deployment. Chris then migrated to the agency side with MRM Partners, where he managed the analytics practice for Microsoft's Small Business and Consumer audiences. Chris then served as Analytics Director for Publicis & Hal Riney, working on Hewlett-Packard, Sprint, and WellPoint.

Chris received his BA from the University of Colorado at Boulder, and his passions include photography, music, cooking, cigars, and wine.
