Dennis Detwiller’s Summary:

I began as an artist. I worked on comic books early on, went to college for illustration and then moved out to Seattle where I became a freelance artist for Wizards of the Coast and an Art Director for Tynes Cowan Corporation. I painted a bunch of Magic, Shadowfist and Mythos cards, and wrote and illustrated books such as Delta Green and GODLIKE.

Over the years, I've taken to writing. In fact I spend most of my time writing now. My first novel was published in 2004.

In 2002 I left for Canada, and there got a job as a designer for the video game company Radical Entertainment.
