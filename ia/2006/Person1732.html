Most current project:
%A L. Ingber
%T Ideas by Statistical Mechanics (ISM)
%R Report 2006:ISM
%I Lester Ingber Research
%D 2006
%O URL http://www.ingber.com/smni06_ism.pdf

Ideas by Statistical Mechanics (ISM) is a generic program to model evolution and propagation of ideas/patterns throughout populations subjected to endogenous and exogenous interactions. The program is based on the author's work in Statistical Mechanics of Neocortical Interactions (SMNI), using the author's Adaptive Simulated Annealing (ASA) code for optimizations of training sets, as well as for importance-sampling to apply the author's copula financial risk-management codes, Trading in Risk Dimensions (TRD), for assessments of risk and uncertainty.

Tools of financial risk management, developed by the author to process correlated multivariate systems with differing non-Gaussian distributions using modern copula analysis, importance-sampled using ASA, enable bona fide correlations and uncertainties of success and failure to be calculated. Marginal distributions are evolved to determine their expected duration and stability using the author's algorithms, i.e., PATHTREE and PATHINT codes.

This product can be used for decision support for projects ranging from diplomatic, information, military, and economic (DIME) factors of propagation/evolution of ideas, to commercial sales, trading indicators across sectors of financial markets, advertising and political campaigns, etc. Teaming with other groups is essential to obtain data and to develop cost/objective functions.

Lester Ingber’s Specialties:

I have developed algorithms for nonlinear stochastic systems, including ASA/VFSR PATHINT/PATHREE.

My projects have been in theoretical nuclear physics, statistical mechanics of neocortical interactions (applications to short-term memory and EEG correlates of behavior), statistical mechanics of combat (applications to baselining simulations to training data), and statistical mechanics of financial markets (applications to options, bond futures, risk management, and trading systems).
