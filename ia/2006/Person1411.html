Frank Gruber’s Summary

I am an avid blogger, active member of the blogosphere and technology and product development enthusiast with over six years of experience in web technology and product development. I am currently a senior product manager at AOL.

I have earned my Undergraduate Degree in Computer Information Systems from Purdue University and my Master's Degree in Computer Information Systems from Northwestern University. I have served as a consultant for several Fortune 500 companies for Technology Services Group. In addition, I am have developed online product strategy for Apartments.com and Tribune Interactive, a subsidiary of the Tribune Company.

I have written several articles for TechCrunch the popular blog tracking web technology and business.

In May 2006, I co-founded TECH cocktail, the quarterly tech/business mixer event, which connected the Midwest technology and business community. The most interesting point to mention is that the first event attracted over 250 guests solely through the power of "word of mouth" and conversations in the blogosphere.

Aside from my professional involvement in the community, I am involved in several extracurricular activities in my local community such as: Cystic Fibrosis Foundation events, United Way campaigns, the Leadership Education and Development Program (LEAD) a future leaders program for students, as well a number of other annual philanthropic activities, some of which even call for me to play some guitar tunes and sing, which is also a hobby of mine. I also have great love for the game of baseball which includes extensive knowledge of the game due in part to a 16 year playing career.
