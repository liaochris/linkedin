Joey Roth’s Summary

I use stories as the initial sketches for my designs. I think about characters, scenes, and conversations, then design an object that will feed this narrative. I'm fascinated by the meanings that designed objects convey and by ritual behavior.

I used to be a student of creative writing, and loved it because it allowed me to construct entire worlds for my characters. I now tell stories through product design, and still strive to make each product an immersive world.
