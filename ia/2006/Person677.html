Christopher Grant’s Summary

For the last ten years I have been led web projects of varying sizes in both the United States, Spain and throughout Europe. I have worked as a freelance designer on a number of web sites, as a project leader for multi-national project teams within Winterthur Insurance, as webmaster of Webinsurance.com. Currently I am the User Experience Specialist for Emagister.com, the 4th most popular web site in the Spanish-speaking world.

As a U.S. citizen living in Spain for the last 7 years, I thrive in dynamic, international working environments and have personally led projects for Spanish, Belgian, Portuguese, Swiss, French and German clients. I enjoy using my creativity and enthusiasm to improve a web site's design, usability and transactionability.
