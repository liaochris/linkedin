Interactive Applications Group, Inc. (iapps) is uniquely focused on the design, development and implementation of Internet solutions that advance the work of social enterprises.

Our clients include some of the world's largest and most renowned grantmaking foundations, as well as nonprofits of all sizes working on the front lines for social and economic justice.

Miles Fawcett founded iapps in 1994 with the goal of offering social enterprises a new generation of communication tools. As President and CEO, Miles oversees the company's direction, including strategic planning, operational management and new technology development. Over the last 15 years Miles has worked at the intersection of multimedia and online publishing, combining his technical expertise with his passion for storytelling through film, video and the visual arts.
