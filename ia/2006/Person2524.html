Leo Schrauwen’s Summary

Leo Schrauwen
-----------------------
In my career I did quite a few different things. I was a civil servant for the Belgian Ministry of Justice (Ministerie van Justitie) in Antwerp (9 years) and worked quite a long time (8 years) for the City of Antwerp (Stad Antwerpen).
Back then I became a selftrained database specialist and wrote several programs to support my own work but also for other departments, e.g. a registration program for incoming mail, a program for the management of the complaint files of the ombudsman and so on...

When I switched to Xerox in 1998 I became a consultant for document management systems. I have designed and build a lot of solutions for all kind of industries, e.g. financial, government, industry, mainly based on documentum, but also on DocuShare.
Some of these projects involved imaging (scanning) as well. One project (Predosa for Provincie Vlaams Brabant) won two silver awards.

After Xerox I worked at EDS Belgium for about a year, being a consultant in document management systems as well for the Flemish Government (ministerie van de Vlaamse Gemeenschap).

Since january 2005 I am a test consultant at ps_testware (Leuven, Belgium) where I co-ordinate the testing of software solutions for our customers, to ensure the quality of the desired implementation.
