Paal Leveraas’s Summary

I am certified professional co-active coach (CPCC) and relationship/team coach (ORSC), based in Oslo, Norway.

I'm also a writer and freelance journalist.

My experience from publishing, writing, editing and public relations makes me an excellent coach for people in these industries.

As an entrepreneur, I'm a great coach for entrepreneurs. I can help you take care of more than your creativity, but also your employees, your family, and not least yourself, while you're building your dream life.

As a father, I'm a great father coach, and have experience working with other fathers, and their kids and teenagers. Ask me what I can do for you or your friend.

What else? I have experience from several areas: Media relations, particulary in IT, with the Nordic countries as specialty. Public, press and investor relations, as well as freelance writing and editing, publishing projects, events. Many years of IT journalism. Project leader for The Norwegian Computer Society's (Dataforeningens) ROSING 2002 and 2003.
