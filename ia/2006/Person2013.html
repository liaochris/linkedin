Julie Kwon’s Summary

I have received my bachelors in special education and have continued into higher education due to my goal of working with children on an one on one basis. I taught special education for a year and realized that I wanted to provide more to children. I enjoy therapy and have found my experiences very challenging as well as rewarding. I have a special interest in the field of autism and hope to continue working with this population as well as furthering the current research. My ultimate career goal is to work with Asian immigrant families who have special needs children. Due to the stigma surrounding psychotherapy in Asia, I hope to provide services to those in need who are afraid to seek services on their own for whatever reason. Completing my doctrate degree will mean that I am yet another step closer to my goals. I have and will continue to dedicate my life to the helping profession. It is my calling and I find complete satisfaction in this area. I am an advocate for those who cannot advocate for themselves.
