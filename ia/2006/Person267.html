Alexander Osterwalder’s Summary

As a founder and owner of BusinessModelDesign.com I facilitated business strategy development in several consulting contracts and helped with their implementation. BusinessModelDesign.com focuses on strategic business design, management training and strategy and technology research.

With BusinessModelDesign.com I am currently assisting the University of Lausanne in the development of a multimedia strategy case study for the Swiss Virtual Campus. Before that I developed and implemented the strategy and business concept of a globally active start-up called The Constellation for over one year in Thailand. As a consultant I worked on various other contracts regarding strategy development, coaching, change management, business process design and research on strategy, business models and technology.

Prior to that, I received a Ph.D. at the Business School of the University of Lausanne, Switzerland, where I worked as a teaching assistant and research fellow. During this time, I was responsible for assisting in teaching several courses and introduced a new and popular seminar on IT and developing countries. My research on business models allowed me to publish extensively, participate and present at numerous conferences around the world, participate in different research projects and develop a global network. Through my work, I joined a number of review and program committees, participated in several panels and got invited to speak at different universities.

Throughout my career I was active in business model research and regularly published thoughts and comments on my Business Model Design Blog. Previously, I was active as an entrepreneur in the banking and investment sector and worked as an online journalist for the respected Swiss Magazine BILANZ. I participated in the inaugural meeting of the Open World Initiative (OWI) of the Evian Group at IMD, Switzerland. Since 1995 I regularly participate in volunteer activities in the education and development sector.
