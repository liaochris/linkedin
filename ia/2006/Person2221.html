Kelvin Eldridge’s Summary

JustLocal enables you to easily find products and services based on where you live, work or play.

Whether you are a business or a consumer, our aim is to encourage you to purchase products and services from businesses in your area, or from businesses that can deliver products and services to you in your area.

For you as a consumer you now:

- have single click access to businesses servicing your area. Gone are endless searches for products and services in your area.

- have access to significant savings.

- have access to the latest version of Australian English dictionary files soon to be used by over 20% of Australian Internet users.

- have the ability to sell your excess items at no cost.

- have access to our Free Software list of software we and our clients use saving thousands of dollars.

- have access to businesses who would be considered to be in the top 15% of businesses.

For you as a business you now:

- can advertise in a very specific geographical area.

- have access to extremely low cost advertising.

- have advertising where you retain full control.

- will be part of a service which encourages businesses to network and trade exchange.

- have a generous referral reward programme for introducing other businesses.

- have FREE access to the invaluable MyAnswers I.T. solutions service exclusive to JustLocal advertisers when you use the services of other JustLocal advertisers.

- have advertising with a money back guarantee.

- have an advertising service where only two businesses of the one type can advertise in an area.

- have rights to your advertising space making advertising an asset instead of an expense.

- have an advertising service where advertising space is limited, making your advertising more valuable over time.

JustLocal is a new approach to advertising. A new approach to strengthening local business and the local community.
