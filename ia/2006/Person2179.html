Jody Thomas’s Summary:

Mr. Thomas has been actively involved in the professional audiovisual industry since 1989. He has volunteered much time to the leading industry trade association, ICIA/InfoComm. He has served on the Board of Governors, and serves as an adjunct faculty member as a frequent speaker, instructor and facilitator for trade association educational offerings. He is former chairman and current member of the Information Technology Council and member of InfoComm's Professional Education and Training Committee, where he has been involved in curriculum development efforts for InfoComm's Installation School and new AV / IT certification initiative. He is co-author of the book "Basics of Audio and Video Systems Design" (ISBN 0939718197)

Before his role at Kayye Consulting, Mr. Thomas was with NEC Technologies as a Marketing Analyst. He was significant contributor to product development, support and training programs in NEC's Visual Systems Division, which manufactures large screen graphics and video display systems.

Mr. Thomas was previously Vice President of Sales & Marketing for A&V Company, a Greensboro, NC based audiovisual solutions provider. He was instrumental in the formation of A&V Company's systems solutions division, which served the customers of A&V Company with integrated and mobile presentation systems, as well as meeting productivity solutions.

He has also served as Chief Technology Officer for Pacifiq Technologies, where he was responsible for overseeing the information technology deployment within a geographically diverse startup company, in addition to defining technical specifications, and directing the efforts of developers the deployment of its services web portal and its e-commerce solutions.

Jody Thomas’s Specialties:

Jody serves as a business and product development consultant, encompassing many aspects of AV and IT systems integration, management processes, market analysis, facilities design, product support, development, training, and rental services. Particularly significant is his renowned expertise in applying the very latest of technologies to organizational structures and businesses processes in order to improve business efficiencies increasing revenue and profitability.
