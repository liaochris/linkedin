Thomas Marban’s Summary:

Thomas has founded werk3AT, one of austrias first and most successful web-agencies in the mid-nineties. In 2004 his company along with celum IMAGINE became the market leader in web-based Digital Media Asset Management for enterprise and professional use.

As a contributor to the CSS Zen Garden and jury member for the annual WebAwards, Thomas is also a renowned specialist for web accessibility and design. He covers the latest trends in his famous Screenblog.

In 2006 he created popurls.com - "The Dashboard for the Hive Mind" as wired founder Kevin Kelly called it. Today the site ranks among the top 50 most popular sites of all time and is member of John Battelle's Federated Media Network.

After 10 years of experience in web-related projects at the intersection of technology and humanity, Thomas will focus on applying varied in-depth skills to a new scope in marketing of web solutions.
