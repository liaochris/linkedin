Joe Houghton’s Summary:

Currently lecturing in Project Management at UCD Smurfit Graduate School of Business, and researching a PhD in Virtual Teams. I also consult in the areas of Strategy and Virtual Team management through my own company LearningTalk.

Prior to entering academia in 2005, I worked for 20 years in software and telemetry development, specialising in the management and implementation of mobile field force systems, CRM, Sales Force Automation. This involved managing and leading teams all over the world for a number of multi-national corporations including W.R. Grace, BetzDearborn, and General Electric.
