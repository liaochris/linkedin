John Hoo’s Summary

John Hoo is the founder and owner of Antapex Corporation, a firm that specializes in the management of Information Technology infrastructure.

By combining his in-depth knowledge of current and new technologies with strong business acumen, John is able to quickly identify solutions for his clients. John’s broad experience includes significant project based work in the following industries:
