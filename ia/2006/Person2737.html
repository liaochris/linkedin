Matthew Macfarlane’s Summary

Experienced business development and M&A manager as well as Chartered Accountant.

I have worked in very large and very small organisations as advisor and as a principal. I have been a member of the Board of Directors at large listed telecoms operators and currently sit on two advisory boards.

Recently co-founded website minti.com the first community ranked-advice site funded by Vibe Capital Pty Ltd. Raised A$1.5m for the start-up funding of Vibe Capital in February 2006.

My goal is to work and play with people who help me enjoy life more. A combination of intellectual (work and relationships) and physical challenges (tennis and lots of other stuff) provides me with a comfortably rounded existence :)
