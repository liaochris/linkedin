Jack Humphrey’s Summary

Internet marketing consultant, author, and Web 2.0 marketing expert with 8 years experience in online marketing, web development, project management, coaching, and membership site development.

Editor of The Friday Traffic Report, a popular website promotion, Web 2.0 marketing blog, formerly WebmasterTrafficTools.com.

Co-Founder of Content Desk which houses the RPM Institute, a private publishing community with 300+ members publishing high-end authority sites with exclusive software enabling "Post and Rank" capabilities for all users.

Author of Power Linking series of internet marketing courses from 2002-present. Power Linking has sold tens of thousands of copies from $67-$97 in the past 5 years to online business owners of all kinds, in all niches.

Authority Site Building Coach for Content Desk Elite Members. Members of this group partook in an intensive 6 month coaching session to learn to research, build, and market niche authority sites.

Consultant for WebFoxMedia with Peter Lenkefi. Managed and built many successful projects for online businesses who hired us to take them to the next level of design, exposure, and profits.

Jack Humphrey’s Specialties:

Online marketing, linking, buzz, PR, search engine optimization, coaching, consulting, project management, brainstorming, technology breakthroughs, and looking to the future of online marketing every day of the week for the past 8 years and counting.
