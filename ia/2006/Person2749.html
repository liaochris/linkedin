Matt Mower’s Summary

I am a rounded technical professional equally at home in development, sales, or marketing activities.

I have extensive development experience going back over ten years. In the last 2 years, despite not being in a full-time engineering position, I have become proficient with the Ruby programming language and contributed to the Rails framework as well as releasing one open source package and several libraries. Additionally I have a wealth of technical expertise in terms of protocols, formats, and systems admin.

Over the last 2 years I have gained considerable experience of product management and strategic marketing. I have worked closely with business development and CEO/non-execs to develop product & marketing plans. I have developed business propositions, run workshops, and presented to technical and non-technical audiences.

I have developed products from initial idea, through working prototype, to full-scale implementation. With Evectors I co-authored the K-Collector enterprise knowledge aggregator which, in 2003, was the first tag based business aggregator. In my previous company I produced one of the first (if not the first) successful tagging module for a blog package.

My career spans a diverse range of businesses including software houses, university, eCRM business, and knowledge management vendor.

For a more in-depth view of who I am take a look at my weblog: "Curiouser and Curiouser!" (http://matt.blogs.it/)
