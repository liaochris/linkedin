Edward de Leau’s Summary

I've been involved in the ICT sector for over 10 years and worked as a consultant for a major consulting company for the past years in which I mainly worked within the financial, public and distribution sector. During my career i gained experience in the full project lifecycle. My operational experience includes front, middle and back office systems including E-CRM, CRM, EAI, Rational Tooling, Microsoft .NET Technology, Siebel, KANA and development in most scripting and programming languages. I am an expert on Web technology and a so called first adapter.

From 1999 to 2006 i was part of the AIS group within PWC Consulting and later IBM BCS as a senior consultant doing many different projects with many different clients.

Starting from 2006 i will focus mainly on Rational solutions and products within the newly formed IBM BCS AS Rational Consulting Group.
