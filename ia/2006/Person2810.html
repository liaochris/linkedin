Mey Meenakshisundaram’s Summary

Meyyappan Meenakshisundaram is a Senior Program Manager in Microsoft Dynamics Product group focusing on Enterprise Portal.He is the coauthor of the book "Inside Dynamics AX". He has more than 13 years of experience in software engineering, consulting and management and the last 5 years at Microsoft. Prior to his current role, he led the engineering team that developed and implemented the portal, content mgmt and customer relationship system for Microsoft Sales Force. Prior to Microsoft, he has worked on software product development for helpdesk/asset mgmt/facilities mgmt and in manufacturing and finance sectors. He regularly blogs at http://blogs.msdn.com/solutions.
