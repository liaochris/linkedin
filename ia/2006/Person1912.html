Jeff Risley’s Summary

Jeff Risley has 15 years of communications experience helping organizations successfully plan and implement public relations and marketing communications initiatives. Prior to joining Barkley Evergreen & Partners Public Relations, Jeff was the channel marketing manager for an e-commerce company in Overland Park, Kan., where he developed and managed marketing communications initiatives with the company's partners, including: Chase Manhattan Bank, Wells Fargo Bank and Cisco. Jeff also spent several years managing trade associations with a consulting firm in Chicago and Washington, DC. His experience includes strategic planning, media relations, issues management, cause marketing and event planning for a variety of industries such as consumer products, financial, manufacturing, building and construction, health care, golf and travel and tourism. Jeff is a native of Kansas and has a bachelor's degree in journalism from the University of Kansas and an MBA from Benedictine College.
