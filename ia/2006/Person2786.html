Michael Cooper’s Summary

Michael Cooper has worked in all facets of the residential real estate market in San Diego, giving his clients the benefits of over 10 years of experience and a unique perspective. Michael began in property management, managing San Diego residential income properties including the leasing, rehabilitating, remodeling, and maintenance of a portfolio of over 250 properties for a wide array of private investors. Since 1996 Michael Cooper has been a California licensed Real Estate Broker originating and closing mortgage loans for purchases and refinances as well as representing buyers and sellers in real estate sales of residential and investment properties.

Michael Cooper is available for private real estate and mortgage consulting by appointment. Call 619-794-0850 or email cooper@adnc.com to schedule a one on one telephone or office consultation today.
