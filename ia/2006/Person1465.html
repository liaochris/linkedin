Gary Thomas’s Summary:

For more than 25 years, I have helped companies realize the full potential of their new products by applying integrated marketing communications concepts to product marketing. My experience includes Marketing Manager at Cisco Systems, Product Marketing Director at Nortel Networks and Bay Networks, Solutions Marketing Manager at SynOptics Communications, and Group Manager for Network Products at AT&T Bell Laboratories. I have been responsible for sales and marketing to the Fortune 1000 for product lines that generated in excess of $1 billion and have developed new channels through direct marketing, cross-selling and applying Internet technology.
