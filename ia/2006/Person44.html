Alice Fisher’s Summary

Alice aspires to make a difference in the word. No matter if it is one person, one campaign, one project, or one contract at a time. She strategically manages to grab the bull by the horns and run with "it" through written, spoken and visual communications.

Whatever the project has been, from living in steel toe boots as a recent contracted first responder during 2005 working 7 days a week, in LA and MS as a result of hurricanes Katrina and Rita, to managing JDG's largest contract win ever (www.jdgcommunications.com/html/news060227.lasso), Alice is a dynamic integrated communication business leader.

She understands, ultimately that communications is about people. But, as a senior manager of marketing communications her day to day activities include
creating, implementing and overseeing communications programs that effectively describe and promote an organization. She relies on extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals. Leads and directs the work of others.
She manages projects with the goal of improving cost expenditures, campaign efficiencies while specializing in strategic "old school and new school" integrated communications.

She has direct proven professional experiences in environmental, health, hospitality, aerospace, technology, and senior communications. Oh, she is also a Veteran of the U.S.A.F. with a previous top secret security clearance.

But what lights her socks on fire is Internet marketing/mobile media and advocacy work. Habitat Humanity, and DC metro area homeless shelters are current and recent projects she has been involved with at various community levels.
