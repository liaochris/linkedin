Christine Ho’s Summary

Christine is in the midst of living out her re-designed life and dreams. Life is too short to be the caretaker of the wrong details!

After 10+ years of combined sales and marketing experience with increasing responsibility and scope in home furnishings and corporate furniture in Singapore, USA, Hong Kong and Sydney, I've recently left the corporate world to pursue real estate full time, in the capacity of Investor, Realtor, and Developer. I bring to the table a passion for hunting down real estate deals, an intuitive sense of aesthetics for design (interior, architectural, landscape) and a win-win agenda in wealth creation.

Together with my husband & partner, Alan, our mission for Sage Concepts LLP is to create Sustainable Homes in harmony with the Self & the Environment. We seek to do this by acquiring existing landed residential properties, strategically remodel them, and sell. As a twist to the renovations, we tap on free renewable energy through solar panels to drive electrical needs for the home, and harvest rainwater off the roof for non-potable water use. The water quality can be brought up to potable level. The end result is a home of design merit, and low utility overheads.
