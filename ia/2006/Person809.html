With over 30 years of sales, marketing and business ownership, I am uniquely qualified to work with my our advertisers.

We offer small businesses a low-priced venue for getting their message out to the Howard County community. "EXCLUSIVITY" - we restrict ads to just one from each business type, keeping out competition! Our goal is to "BRAND" your business by creating distinctive ads and distributing to over 180 locations throughout the County. By "BRANDING" your business, we increase recognition and subsequent sales.

Our goals are:

1-Provide excellent service to our customers.
2-Become a trusted, well-known publication in Howard County and surrounding areas, by getting involved in the community and helping small businesses grow their business.
3-Did I mention, "Provide excellent service to our customers?"

Our networking abilities have been lauded by leaders in the business community and recognized as a major reason why businesses choose us. Karen, our VP of Sales & Distribution (and my partner for life!) is involved in many networking groups and is a virtual 'extra salesperson' for every advertiser we have.
