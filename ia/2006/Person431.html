I'm a consultant specializing in the creation and execution of developer networks, and other similar communities which consist of members who are more than just 'consumers' of that service or product. These are people that tend to be opinion formers or authorities/experts in a particular area.

I work with companies to empower their expert users to feed back product development ideas, and help market and innovative products/services in a way that is mutually beneficial to the host company, the expert user and also the wider community.

My work includes internal strategic planning, exposing APIs/feeds at both at a technical and policy/legal level, the creation of community platforms to facilitate such initiatives and the identification and retention of suitable members of such communities.
