Vincent Vaerman’s Summary

For 10+ years, Vincent has been active in Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) through various positions ranging from R&D to business development and project management. His background includes experiences in software development (object-oriented coding, massively parallel computing), product/channel management and sales-related activities (consultancy, negotiations, bid management, SLA definition).

He gained expertise in project management through the implementation of telecom solutions (major LAN/WAN combining voice, data and IP solutions) and IT projects (digital image & video compression, medical imaging, CTI, CRM) for various international companies and institutions.

Since end 2006, Vincent moved to Industrial Services for the management of multi-M€ export projects in Power & Water and Oil & Gas areas. Target region covers North, West and Central Africa.
