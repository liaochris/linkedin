David Rowe’s Summary:

I have been programming in C since 1988 on embedded, Win32, Linux, and recently uClinux platforms. I have built many DSP hardware and software products in the satellite modem and computer telephony fields.

I have built and successfully exited a small business (www.voicetronix.com) which sells computer telephony hardware for Linux.

I really enjoy working with Linux, uClinux, DSP and hardware. Although I am an effective manager I like debugging machines much more than debugging people. Most of all I love learning from smart technical people.

One medium term goal I have is to work in the US or Italy for 6-12 months. Please contact me if you can help.
