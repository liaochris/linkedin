Douglas Pritchard’s Summary:

My goal is to help people move seamlessly from where they are in life now, to where they want to be next. Sometimes it is a financial move (income property acquisition) and sometimes it is geographical in nature. Sometimes across an ocean and sometimes just down the street.

If you know someone who is thinking of making a move... Then you know someone who should be talking to me.

I service the Ottawa, Nepean, Kanata, Gloucester wards and surrounding areas such as North Dundas. I know the smaller communities within Ottawa such as the Glebe, Barrhaven, Bridlewood, and the smaller communities outside of Ottawa like Kemptville, Hallville, Winchester, and Chesterville.

I am comfortable assisting relocation clients from around the world and have happy clients who have used my services to move into, and out of the Ottawa area. I know REALTORs around the world who can help you too.
