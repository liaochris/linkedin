Floris Peters’s Summary

Over 5 years of experience in International Marketing and Business Development.

After practising sales as a Real-Estate Agent, Floris moved toward the newly born Internet marketing field in 1998 by Streampower.net and e-yes.tv (Online video streaming). He founded the Ucar.fr business platform the following year, pioneering the way companies find new Customers over the Internet.

In 2001 he joined Microsoft France, the Worldwide leader in Softwares and OS, and became Team Manager for the antipiracy branche for France in few months. This concept directly involved Microsoft Partners in the launch of Windows XP through efficient Marketing Campaigns to final Customers.

Floris moved to Monaco and joined Namebay.com in March 2005 as Sales Manager and responsible for part of the European Sales Development through WebMarketing and Direct Sales. Since October 2006, Floris integrated Cofisem, specialized in Financial Information and is in charge of the Business Development for Europe.

Enthusiastic about the impact of interactive media in the consumer-brand relationship, he is the author of several white papers ranging from "Driving and Transform Qualified Traffic" to "Luxury Brands in Digital Marketing."
Floris holds degrees in International Business Development and Marketing from ESCE Business School, specializing himself in e-business and Finance.
