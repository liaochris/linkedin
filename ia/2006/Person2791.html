Mat Morrison’s Summary

One of London's first dedicated digital planners, Mat has worked across a broad range of planning disciplines. Starting as a media planner/buyer at AKQA and Lowe Digital, he went on to become Commercial Manager, then Head of Strategy at Tribal DDB.

Later Mat joined Heresy/HHCL to develop an interest in online DM, CRM and data planning. Together with a broad understanding of technology and technical implementation, and a shallow-but-useful knowledge of usability and information architecture issues, this makes Mat one of the most rounded planners here on Linked in.

Mat isn't really interested in big ideas. He likes little simple ideas that work, and that stick around.
