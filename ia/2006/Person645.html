Alexander Casassovici’s Summary

I founded WaveStorm in summer 2003, in order to provide companies with an easy to use and affordable WiFi security solution. Now WaveStorm has gained a leading position in wireless management software targeting mainly operators, manufacturers and integrators. We launched a new range of wireless short-range modules enabling wireless connectivity for regular devices.

I currently am the managing director of WaveStorm focusing on strategy & business development.
I am also an active shareholder of Spotcoffe (http://www.spotcoffee.eu) a WirelessISP focusing on cafés, hotels and restaurants.

I am a shareholder of Mobiluck since 2005. Mobiluck provides a software layer built on bluetooth stack to enable easy message exchange between devices and provide third parties application with an easy-to-use communication layer enabling them to use local communication between devices

Past experiences :

I founded M-Distrib in 2004, after a one year long negociation, we signed a contract with a major to create his own rich media point of sales... unfortunately human factors had this oportunity break up.
Together with Denis we started again from scrath creating Kamayo, a mobile agency dedicated to creating rich media mobile and vertical mobile products.
I successfully left Kamayo in March 2006 to focus on WaveStorm's expansion.

I was leading CCETP (Telecom Paris Entrepreneurs club) and am involved in the AIST (Telecom Paris' association).

Finally I am always eager to find new challenges where my knowlege on mobile solutions can be a key factor to success and enjoy analysing and solving business cases in the mobile world.
