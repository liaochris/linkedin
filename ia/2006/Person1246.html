Elyse Chapman’s Summary

I delight in transforming wishes into creative, effective design solutions. My experience in creative direction and production spans the products and services of many industries.

Because of my design and publishing industry experience, I understand the challenges design and production staff face. I live or die by the same technology and have been doing so since version 1 of every current, major print design/production application in use today. Application manuals? Yes, I actually read them — and I can translate dry "tech talk" into direct, cut-to-the-chase explanation and "how-to."

You may contact me directly via email: eec @ mac . com

Brand/product identity, corporate identity, graphics standards manuals, retail & wholesale packaging, promotional specialties, point-of-purchase (posters, shelf-talkers, displays, coupons, can/bottle hangers,...), print ads, catalogs, direct mail & magalogs, trade show graphics & displays, package cuts, technical illustration, icon design, writing, brochures & collateral, book production & template design.

Publishing/design industry software and hardware writing, training & consulting.
