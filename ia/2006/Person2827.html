Michael Bolanos’s Summary

July 2006: eDrive joined forces with CBS Meteorologist Bryan Norcross to form Hurricane Enterprises LLC to market and promote Hurricane Almanac, Bryan's book from St. Martin's Press. HurricaneAlmanac.com contains vital information and preparedness products and is open to new ideas and partnerships.

eDrive is the Executive Producer of RichardSimmons.com, his Official Website. eDrive created and produced Official Websites for celebrities including Cindy Crawford, Daisy Fuentes, Marilu Henner and Britney Spears.

Prior to launching eDrive, Michael was active internationally in entertainment as a manager, producer, director and performer. CompuServe tapped Bolanos as Entertainment Consultant for the H&R Block-owned service. Beginning in 1994, eDrive attracted over 1 million members and was 1st to offer digitized entertainment from Universal Pictures, Walt Disney Pictures, Warner Bros. and over 100 other media companies.

eDrive's CyberStudio was 1st to introduce celebrities including Tom Hanks, Mick Jagger, Jenny McCarthy, Sylvester Stallone live online. eDrive was 1st to present live backstage online coverage backstage at the Oscars®, the Daytime, Sports and Primetime Emmys® and the Tony® Awards.

The William Morris Agency acquired an equity minority interest in eDrive, as did CompuServe.

Michael was on the Board of the Friends of the American Theatre Wing, and helped to bring the Artists Rights Foundation, ASCAP, and the first Muscular Dystrophy Telethon online.

Michael was named one of NY Magazine's "Cyber 60" and as a "CyberStar" by Newsweek. Michael and eDrive were honored as Innovators by the Smithsonian Computerworld Awards. Michael received his first US Patent for the "eDrive Movie Viewer" in 1998. Michael is listed in multiple versions of Marquis Who's Who In the World®, America®, Finance and Industry®, and Media and Communications®. He lectures on New Media frequently, including at Yale University. More information is at MichaelBolanos.com.

Partner

Hurricane Enterprises LLC

(Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

July 2006 – Present (7 months)

Hurricane Enterprises LLC was formed with award winning CBS Meteorologist Bryan Norcross, who literally "talked South Florida through" Hurricane Andrew in 1992 and, since that time, his level-headed advice and professional presence and have earned him the trust of viewers nationwide (hurricanealmanac.com/norcross.php). HurricaneAlmanac.com, the companion Website for Bryan's book "Hurricane Almanac" from St. Martin's Press, features Q&As, vital information, shopping lists and ecommerce with recommended hurricane and other disaster preparedness supplies (www.hurricanealmanac.com/supplies.php). The LLC was formed to provide helpful and important information nationally for consumers and businesses to be prepared and stay safe with the myriad of disasters we encounter every year.
