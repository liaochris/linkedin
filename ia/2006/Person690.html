Fraud Busters, Inc. provided proactive fraud prevention reviews and front-end verifications of the abilities of individuals or companies to perform as stipulated in business, financial or employment agreements. Through its proprietary systems, Fraud Busters provided cost effective, verified and analyzed data to assist in the decision making processes of business executives.

Fraud Busters' products and services were specifically aimed towards helping business operations which were either going through transitions, expanding, or which were vulnerable to loss through fraudulent activity. These categories encompass the financial community, private and public companies, firms seeking capital from outside sources, commercial insurance firms, and corporations and individuals involved in acquisitions, mergers, joint ventures, or in risk-taking operations.

With proper proactive fraud prevention, profits and savings can be maximized, and losses and exposure can be minimized.
