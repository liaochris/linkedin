Michael Hatscher’s Summary:

Experienced interaction designer / usability evangelist with 5+ years of industry experience in big software / internet companies in both enterprise and consumer software domains. Trained cognitive psychologist with background in HCI and personality/motivational psychology; additional training in Contextual Design, Cooper design methodology and related assets. Visionary and innovative (18 patents pending); highly analytical and independent work style. Focussed on detecting issues and finding solutions.

Recent activities:

Drive interaction design through thorough analysis and user research. Convincingly influence development and product management, winning them for usability. Team up with both parties for recurring usability testing. Monitor user experience changes for installed user base caused by new apps through Transition Management. Deliver consistent wording in new apps through Claims Analysis. Develop applications based on user interface patterns and derive user interface pattterns from usage patterns and reverse-engineered user interfaces. Presentation of findings and designs on CEO and VP level.

Goals:

Create the best possible user experience across platforms and devices. Blur the edges between offline / online, at-the-desk / on-the-go. Make a difference.
