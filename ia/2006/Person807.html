Damien du Toit’s Summary:

With a trained background in Computer Science, an eye for design, and a passion for anything web-related, I've been involved in the new media industry for the past 9+ years as a professional web developer/designer. I've gained a wealth of experience and knowledge in agencies of all sizes - from two-men teams to worldwide advertising networks - servicing a broad range of clients with a variety of skills.

I'm currently self-employed as a freelance developer, designer and consultant. Latest projects include the design and development of high-end web sites accompanied by bespoke, database-driven CMS and/or CRM applications. My work is best described as clean, usable, accessible, intuitive and effective.

As an advocate for web standards, I pay meticulous attention to usability and accessibility principles, and whenever possible, educate others on the benefits of standard-compliant design through my weblog. I also support the open-source movement.
