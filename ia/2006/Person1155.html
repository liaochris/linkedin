Doug Felteau’s Summary:

A true renaissance engineer with solid practical experience in all aspects of IT management. Using my skills I have transformed network architectures at several companies promoting faster and more robust connections. Also, I have introduced security by developing security policies and providing the security architecture so the company and it's users can adhere to the security policies.

One of my proudest accomplishments includes convincing management that security was important and selling my vision for security. At my current company, using a security strategy called defense in depth in addition to communication and teaching the staff, we have not had a virus make it inside our corporate firewall in over four years! The knowledge that we were not infected by Blaster, Slammer, Sobig, MyDoom or Sasser simply brings a smile to my face!
