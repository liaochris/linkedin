Gray Rothkopf’s Summary:

Management, marketing, sales experience with practiced, hands-on IT skills.

Started a computer consulting business in 1992 specializing in Apple Computers. Rolled the company into an ISP in 1995 specializing in serving businesses in the Chicago area. Sold the ISP in 2002. Joined Apple Computer, Inc., where I helped open the flagship North Michigan Avenue store, managed the Service Business, its Genius Bar, and co-managed the sales floor for almost two years. Learned so much from the great team at Apple Retail, made good friends and gained insight into one of the best companies in the world. Initiated radio show representing Apple on WGN radio, AM 720, 4 hours/week, answering tech call-ins. Left Apple at the end of 2004 to start a company specializing in consumer and business consulting about Apple technologies, named OnDeckTech. Still represent the Mac-side as a weekly guest on the radio show, Website Wednesday Nights with Steve and Johnnie, from 11PM until 3AM (!!) CST.
