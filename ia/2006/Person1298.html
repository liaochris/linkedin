Conference Organiser

Oz-IA 2006

(Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

June 2006 – October 2006 (5 months)

I organised a conference and retreat on information architecture on the last weekend of September 2006 in Sydney, Australia. This was the first such event dedicated to IA in Australia.

There were 16 speakers and 100 attendees, and it was hailed a great success.

The Conference/Retreat had a semi-formal series of practical sessions on information architecture and related topics, aimed at practicing IAs and those within the broader industry interested in expanding their knowledge of the theory and practice. Speakers included Mark Bernstein, Thomas Vander Wal, Dan Saffer, Donna Maurer, David Sless, Steve Baty, and James Robertson.

There were case study presentations, detailed how-to sessions, deep dives into niche subjects, and general 'where are we going' discussions. We also organised some group participation sessions.

http://www.oz-ia.org/2006/
