    Celebrate Connections is an idea, a blog and a portal.

    The idea is to celebrate the powerful connections we have through the people that we meet online and off.

    The blog follows the return of one person to the business world after disability sidelines them for nearly 7 years.

    And, the portal is to showcase the influence that sending out cards can have on your business.

    Top sales people know that sending a follow-up card is a key to making lasting connections and increasing sales.

    But, who has time.

    By automating the process of sending out personalized cards, Celebrate Connections allows you to act on your promptings and reach out in a real tangible way to your customer, clients and prospects.
