Yury Oreshin’s Summary

I expect to pass through this world but once. Any good thing, therefore, that I can do or any kindness I can show to any fellow human being let me do it now. Let me not defer nor neglect it, for I shall not pass this way again.
- Stephen Grellet


Yury is working to attract billions in to stem cells, biotechnology, and regenerative medicine to foster development of life extension products that deliver results.
ANGELICO Biotechnology Investment Fund is an instrument for creating a world free of ageing and disease. ANGELICO aspires to be the preferred investor in the fast growing biotechnology industry, particularly in developing and commercializing a cure for ageing.

Yury is the founder and CEO of one of the first investment funds with a focus exclusively on anti-ageing industry. Prior to Angelico Yury was an Advisor at a London investment company Edelau which focuses exclusively on cross-border transactions mainly between London, Paris, Moscow and Kiev. Yury has been an investment-banking director with Art Capital, a Kiev-based investment company. In this capacity, Yury originated deals for M&A, IPO and private placements. Prior to Art Capital, Yury has been a Sales Director for IBMED, a stem cell research and medical center. Yury has graduated from Moore School of Business at University of South Carolina and New York Military Academy.
