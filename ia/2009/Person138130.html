Laurens Bonnema’s Summary

Laurens Bonnema lives in Dordrecht, The Netherlands, with his wife, Nienke Blauw. He joined Atos Origin on October 1, 2007, where he is currently working as a Project Manager responsible for the further development of DigiD, the system shared between cooperating Dutch governmental agencies, allowing to digitally authenticate the identity of a person who applies for a transaction service via internet.

His career in project management started in 2003 when he was asked to head-up Sogeti's Java Delivery Center in Vianen. Having improved productivity measured in hours per Cosmic Full Function Point by 20% and reduced time-to-market for new hires by 60%, he was offered a wonderful position at Xebia Software Development in Hilversum to set-up a Project Management Office. After one year, Xebia had a PMO with five Project Managers successfully executing Agile projects and Laurens decided to move on to a larger organization: Atos Origin.

Before becoming a Project Manager, Laurens spent four years programming in COBOL, Visual Basic and Java at G&D Software in Rotterdam, a company that went on to merge into Sogeti. That was his first real job, after his studies in International Relations, Communication Sciences and Alpha Informatics at Rijksuniversiteit Groningen.

In his spare time, Laurens writes a lot, mostly short stories and poems, sometimes texts for a website or magazine. He also loves to read, travel, cook, dive, watch movies, and catch up on Java, Erlang, and Ruby.

As Chairman of the Program Group Agile Management from IPMA Netherlands, member of the Agile Project Leadership Network, and Treasurer of Agile Holland, Laurens strives to merge classic and agile project management in the conviction that therein lies the future of professional project management.

Laurens Bonnema’s Specialties:

Project management, bid management, resource planning, team management, process improvement, public speaking, sales support, Lean, Six Sigma, Scrum, XP, DSDM/Atern, RUP, Prince2, PMBOK, ITIL, CMMI.
