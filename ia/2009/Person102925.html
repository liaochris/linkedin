James Rees’s Summary

I am an enthusiastic and professional security expert with over eleven years IT experience, of which more than five have been involved in consultancy work, advising and designing IT security architecture, auditing existing security measures and auditing companies in preparation for BS7799 / ISO 27001 / Sarbanes Oxley / PCI DSS compliancy.

I have worked in several key areas of IT and have a great deal of experience in IT Security, Network administration, Consultancy and Customer Support, and have been involved with a wide range of operations and projects. These have been orientated around technical implementation and policy/procedure, countermeasures, antivirus solutions, content checking solutions, security auditing, network security architectural development, and others.

As a security consultant and a contractor I have worked for many large name organisations, including ICI, Credit Suisse, Cable & Wireless, Exeter Fire and Rescue, Alcatel, Nestle, Mitsubishi, Allianze, Travelodge and Pfizer.

James Rees’s Specialties:

IT Security Solution design, IT Security Troubleshooting, Security policy design, Risk Assessments.
