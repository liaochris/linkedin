Jon Nugent’s Summary

Business Intelligence Solutions has been committed to providing solutions and services to biotech, cosmetic, medical device, pharmaceutical and specialty chemical companies in the regulated process industry.

With pipelines looking less and less promising, companies are scrambling for new ideas to stay profitable during these troubling economic times. Many companies have turned to cost cutting and productivity improvements to stay competitive.

These approaches are suitable short-term responses. In response to this reality, scientific, research and development communities are searching for innovative ways to accelerate processes in a regulated environment.

Working with industry specialists and partners, we specialize in the following areas:

Developing and implementing Quality Systems and Programs required to ensure compliance with all appropriate FDA regulations including cGMP, GLP, FTC, and CLIA.

Experience with areas including general toxicology, developmental and reproductive toxicology, neurosciences, orthopedics, surgery, experimental therapeutics, drug metabolism, infusion toxicology, immunotoxicology, in vitro pharmacology, safety pharmacology, clinical pathology, and bio-analytical and analytical laboratory functions.

Experience in writing and assessing protocols and SOP’s to ensure compliance with GLP, cGMP, and FDA regulations.

Jon Nugent’s Specialties:

Performance improvement, workflow, data management and regulatory and compliance solutions.
