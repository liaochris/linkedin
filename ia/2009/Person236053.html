Tom Laszewski’s Summary

I have over 20 years experience in databases, middleware, software development, and building strong technical partnerships. I am currently the Director of the Oracle Modernization Solutions team. I established the initial business and technical relationships with Oracle's modernization SIs and tools partners (the Oracle Modernization Ecosystem). My main responsibility is successful completion of all modernization projects initiated through the partner ecosystem. I work on a daily basis with EDS and HP alliances, technical architectures, and account managers to ensure the success of joint modernization projects. I am also responsible for Oracle Modernization customer assessments and workshops, modernization reference architectures and modernization best practices.

At Oracle, I formly was a Technical Manager for Oracle's Partner Technology Group. I have engaged with over 600 ISVs, SIs and VAD/VARs during my 9-year tenure at Oracle. I helped land one of the largest Oracle database partner deals; the deal was projected to be worth $15 million in first year. I was the lead technical sales consultant for one of the largest Oracle ISV database and application server deals in the past four years. I also have extensive background in Java/J2EE, XML and Web Services. I was the lead content contributor and instructor in delivering these types of workshops to ISVs and the Oracle Sales Consultants "Top Guns".

I previously held key system engineering, consulting, project management and technical leadership roles at Sybase and Electronic Data Systems. At Sybase, my consulting roles included: DBA, developer, architect and trainer. At EDS, I led a project to develop an enterprise database and software application for the merchandising industry. I began my career at a start up software company developing custom applications for the auto glass industry.
