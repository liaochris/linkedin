Nick D. Anderson’s Summary

Nick Anderson is Vice President of Retail Banking of Shoreline Bank, with oversight responsibility for all branches, marketing, and bank-wide deposits. A 2008 graduate of the Pacific Coast Banking School, he has been named one of the industry’s ‘Rising Stars’ by the Independent Community Bankers Association. Prior to joining Shoreline Bank in 2005, Nick spent eight years with Bank of America (formerly SeaFirst Bank) in a variety of positions including sales, operations management, branch management and training & development. A creative and strategic thinker, Nick views the current climate for banking as both a challenge and an opportunity to reaffirm the integrity and essential role of the industry through the careful development of a new generation of leaders. Nick is active in his community as a board member, Rotarian and as a frequent guest speaker in local schools. He also participates actively with the Washington Bankers Association.
