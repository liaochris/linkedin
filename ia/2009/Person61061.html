Deirdre Branley’s Summary

With more than ten years of experience in institutional and programming communications, I have developed and executed all aspects of integrated communications campaigns. I regularly work with producers and corporate managers to create innovative media strategies; write publicity plans, press releases, bios and Q&A’s; maintain relationships with and pitch local and national consumer and trade press; generate and distribute internal communications; and report results to clients. I am comfortable working in a fast-paced environment where juggling multiple high-priority projects, short turn-around times, tight budgets, and fluid publicity teams are the norm.

After securing three promotions at my current employer, I am now seeking new opportunities in the field.
