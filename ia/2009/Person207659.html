Samantha Bedford’s Summary

I was one of the driving forces behind SearchAdNetwork’s launch in 2003. And currently play an active role in keeping the company’s technology and services on the leading edge. I maintain client satisfaction by working with developers to design and test client-driven system modifications—analyzing competitive offerings to recommend system enhancements and maintaining an open line of communication with clients. I recently spearheaded efforts to integrate a web analytics offering into Location3 Media’s services; and took responsibility of system implementation and now head up the analytics department. Utilizing fine-tuned leadership skills and natural aptitude for organization, I also maintain the highest quality of campaign performance by overseeing the campaign management department.

Prior to joining Location3 Media, Inc. in 2002, I worked with high-profile retail brands such as Shoprite, Checkers and OK, focusing on traditional offline marketing. Following a stint at Hunt Lascaris - Tequila Cape where I was the account manager for the Tiger Brands Group, I went to work for ForwardSLASH, Inc. While performing as a senior media marketer, I worked with a diverse range of interactive publishers and clients, and bring to Location3 Media a wealth of knowledge on the interactive media front.

I am a certified Ambassador for Yahoo!, a recognized Google AdWords and Microsoft AdXcellence Professional and a member of the Web Analytics Standards Council with the Web Analytics Association. I am also a certified Click Tracks Professional, Individual Google Analytics Certified, and completed the two year WAA-UBC Award of Achievement in Web Analytics. I graduated from the AAA School of Advertising in Cape Town, South Africa with a bachelor of arts degree in marketing communications and a bachelor of arts degree in media management.
