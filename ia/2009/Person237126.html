Steven Provenzano, CPRW/CEIP’s Summary

Steven Provenzano is President of ECS: Executive Career Services and DeskTop Publishing, Inc., a team of experts specializing in career marketing at all income levels. He is the published author of six career books including Top Secret Executive Resumes and Top Secret Resumes & Cover Letters.

He is a CPRW, Certified Professional Resume Writer, CEIP, Certified Employment Interview Professional, and has written more than 4000 resumes for staff and managers at all income levels during his 20 years in career marketing and corporate recruiting.

Steve has more than 19 years in expert career consulting and certified resume writing for top executives and professionals. He started ECS in 2006 to provide expanded services beyond the company he sold at that time: A Advanced Resume Service, aka AARS Inc., where he managed the largest group of CPRWs in Illinois for 16 years, and developed more than 8000 clients in all 50 states and dozens of countries overseas. His team wrote more than 1500 resumes for all of SAP America's domestic consultants.

He is co-leader of the Career Transitions Team at Willow Creek Community Church and has appeared numerous times on CNBC, CNN/fn, WGN-TV, NBC-5, ABC-7 in Chicago and on numerous radio programs. Steve earned his BA in Journalism from NIU in DeKalb, IL. His work is endorsed by Chicago Tribune career columnist Lindsey Novak, as well as top executives from Motorola, Coca-Cola and other firms.
