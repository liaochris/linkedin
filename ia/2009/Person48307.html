Colin Holowaychuk’s Summary

Colin Holowaychuk has over 15 years experience as entrepreneur and marketer with several successful start-ups, technology, advertising, and communication businesses. Mr. Holowaychuk was a pioneer in the creation of successful alternative payment solutions. Mr. Holowaychuck’s extensive operational and management experience compliments his product management skills in the ecommerce industry.

Colin Holowaychuk’s Experience

  • Founder

    Tyler Financial Services Corp

    (Internet industry)

    June 2006 — Present (3 years 7 months)

    As CEO at Tyler Financial, Colin consulted for several small, medium and large volume ecommerce merchants. Helping manage their payments needs and relationships with multiple concurrent banks while navigating through several concurrent jurisdictions. Specialized on developing new systems and business procedures, he lead projects ranging from user experience improvements to enhancing fraud and risk management systems, the introduction of multi channel sales and support to the creation of collections applications and training personnel.

    Past clients include 2 of the top 4 largest Internet Poker companies where Colin helped manage the processing of $50+ Million per month through over 35 localized and alternative payment channels and over 40 concurrent credit card MIDs. Focusing on global conversions these clients experienced a consistent 10-15% increase in deposit conversions while he introduced and lead several projects including multi channel payments and bonus marketing, MID and BIN management automation, multi channel decline sales (outbound and inbound), localization marketing and service provider collapse recovery.

    Colin represented his clients while managing relationships with Payment Service Providers and lead teams responsible for the introduction, integration and the rapid launch (and retirement) of deposit and withdrawal channels. Colin also created collections systems and lead a team that within 8 months grew collected debts from under $1000 to over $100,000 per month.

  • VP Business Development, & Director

    Okalla Corp

    (Internet industry)

    June 2006 — February 2007 (9 months)

    Okalla is an e-Business Solutions Provider listed on the TSX Venture Exchange. While at Okalla, Mr. Holowaychuk developed organic and acquisition based growth strategies for the company. He also negotiated technology partnerships and acquisitions for the company.

  • VP Marketing and Sales, North America & Senior Product Advisor

    Neteller/ Neovia

    (Public Company; neo; Financial Services industry)

    March 2002 — July 2006 (4 years 5 months)

    In his roles at NETELLER, Colin managed the early expansion of the company’s product offerings to introduce internationalization, including region specific transaction methods, multicurrency, multilingual customer support and online chat. Colin lead projects such as the redesign of NETELLER’s global website and the remodeling of the customer “sign up through first transaction” process. This project resulted in a 300%+ increase in customer first transaction conversions through improved usability, design and international customer identity verification.

    Colin introduced and implemented web traffic analytics and the use of business intelligence systems to recognize behavioral and transactional patterns of high value customers, and to provide differentiated products and services to customer segmentations. Colin lead teams responsible for the analysis and management of balancing transaction fraud risk with increased conversions, resulting in a 250%+ increase in customer lifetime value.
