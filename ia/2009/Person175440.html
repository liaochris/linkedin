Nikhil Rungta’s Summary

I have over 13 years experience in Sales, Marketing and Advertising. I have worked across a variety of brands like Microsoft, MasterCard, LG, Dabur, Revlon, NIIT, ISB and Blowplast. Two of my proud achievements have been launching XBOX 360 in India and building Yatra.com as one of the most preferred travel services brand in India, in a short span 3 years.

Over the years I have used all media types to promote my products - TV/Print/Outdoor/Radio/Internet/Mobile/Social Media/Emailers/Cinema. My feeling is that each media complements the other and companies need to follow a 'balanced media diet' to have a healthy brand.

I believe marketing today is about starting conversations and telling stories that spread. Best marketing is when one consumer speak to the other about your product and evangelizes.

Nikhil Rungta’s Specialties:

Sales, Marketing and Business planning and implementation. Deep understanding of the Indian consumer. Digital/Online Marketing. Demand generation, customer acquisition and customer retention strategy. Evangelizing online/digital marketing.
