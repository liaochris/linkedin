Garry Bridgewater’s Summary

An extensive internal communications and media background is well-suited to helping ensure that corporate messages are received and, more importantly, understood by internal and external audiences.

My involvement in the volatile airline industry has underscored the importance of keeping employees and customers informed and onside during new product and service launches, changes in company policies and the normal shifts in fortune that companies invariably go through.

Production of award-winning employee publications targeted to a worldwide audience, planning and implementing strategic communication projects, speechwriting for senior executives, researching and writing news releases, creating award-winning videos for employees, editing content on the company website and a variety of other writing and creative services are just a few of the skills I bring to the table.

Externally, some 25 years in the media have given me valuable experience in understanding what the media requires and the best way to deliver a corporate message in a form that is easily understood. As a journalist, the ability to write quickly, accurately and within tight deadlines is critical. This experience has provided me with a firm foundation in writing for a variety of general audiences.
