Sandeep Dixit’s Summary

Sandeep has over sixteen years of experience in application strategy, architecture, design, and development using Enterprise Java technologies, business process re-engineering, and project management. He has been consulting with fortune-500 companies and government agencies such as BP Oil & Chemicals, OfficeMax, Nortel, NCR, IBM, Aetna, National City, State of Missouri and New York State. He is a co-founder and the chief architect of Ohioedge CRM+BPM Server and is a guest speaker at JavaOne conferences.

Sandeep Dixit’s Specialties:

Java / J2EE / SOA Strategy, Architecture, Design and Development, SDLC Management, Technical Management and Project Leadership
