Liz Kerrison’s Summary

A driven, motivated, well organized team player, with seven years experience as a graphic and web designer. The Co-Director and the Director of Publicity for a Canadian charity for Teen Cancer Survivors, with an Honours Degree in Fine Arts Cultural Studies and a Certificate in Digital Media from York University as well as a Graduate Certificate in Interactive Multimedia from Algonquin College.

Welcomes constant change, new challenges and responsibilities in any environment. Flexible and versatile with the ability to meet deadlines no matter what it takes. Thrive in deadline-driven environments. Excellent organization and documentation skills that allow for clarity in communication and leadership. Organized many charity events such as Fashion Shows, Concerts and National Camps as well as many presentations and reports, from Creative Briefs to Design Documents.

Liz Kerrison’s Specialties:

Web design and development, Graphic Design, Photography

Expertise with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
Knowledge of preparing images for print and web.

Fluent in XHTML, CSS, JavaScript and XML.
Understanding of Lingo, ActionScript 2.0, PHP and MySQL.
Very proficient in both Mac and PC platforms.
