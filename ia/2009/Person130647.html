Kevan Kjar’s Summary

My passion is to help others find their own "stories that win", whether it is their company, product or personal stories. Once a person, team or company has a good idea of their own story, it gives them a purpose, passion and new power to perform. That is the purpose of ThreeQuest.

"Kevan draws out wisdom you didn't know the company had and helps hone it to perfection. Outstanding work that will change the way we do business."
-Janet Eden-Harris, CEO, Umbria

I help leading edge companies that are committed to excellence in sales and marketing to:
• Align their team around a central vision
• Create a clear product story that is clear, compelling & competitive
• Roll out that story to Sales, and give them skills to give killer presentations & demos

The ThreeQuest process is Vision, Story and Skills; 1, 2, 3.
Stories that win, skills that win, giving my clients breakthrough results.

Kevan Kjar’s Specialties:

I've had the opportunity to help thousands of professionals over the years learn their own message, and ultimately their story, and how to communicate that story to their peers and customers. Companies such as SAP, HP, Siemens, Schlumberger, and others have called on me on multiple occasions to help crystallize their story, and how to communicate it in a compelling manner. Being an certified practitioner of Neuro-Linguistic Programming has been a big help in drawing the best out of my clients.
