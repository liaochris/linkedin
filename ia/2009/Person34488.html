Brooke Coe’s Summary

I am an experienced marketer with strong brand development, community building, web development, strategic planning and business development experience.

As a founding team member of BeJane.com, I helped grow the company into a nationally recognized brand with more than $1 million in revenue and content across web, print, radio and TV, and major partners including MSN, B of A, Better Homes & Gardens, and more. I was also able to pen a deal which syndicated our content to more than 250 newspapers across the US.

Prior to Be Jane, I was instrumental in developing the online marketing strategies of several high profile brands such as Nestle, Honda, 20th Century Fox and NBC. In my role, I ushered in solutions to meet the clients' business objectives ensuring we met their ROI targets. My strong understanding of both front-end requirements and back-end development were instrumental in leading highly diverse teams to achieving strong performance results.

My enthusiasm for brand-building in non-traditional methods has aided me in developing innovative solutions such as the Mentos Freshmaker Tour which, in the rise of the Internet in 1995, was the first online worldwide cybercast concert tour. This tour was key to aggregating community both locally and worldwide and created instant conversation with the brand ultimately leading to increased market distribution in previously untapped channels.

Most recently my passion for home improvement and design has led me to follow my entrepreneurial urges and develop several businesses in which I've been busy honing my skills in interior design, project management and marketing and brand building all with a keen eye towards a limited budget.

Now I'm ready to bundle my experience, enthusiasm and dedication to meet a new opportunity which challenges my skills, feeds my passion and allows me to do what I do best - build brands.

Brooke Coe’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, brand building, publicity and media, creative development, business development, production, graphic design, web design, monetizing online communities, online brands, home improvement.
