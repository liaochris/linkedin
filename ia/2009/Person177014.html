Steve Novitsky’s Summary

Steve Novitsky has a background in technology, product and software sales, product development, change management, product management, and execution. His experiences have provided him with a solid understanding of the product development life cycle, strategic planning, process design methodologies, sales execution, contract negotiations, project management, writing / presentation skills, vendor management, and Six Sigma methodologies.

Upon graduating from NC State University with a BS in Computer Science, Novitsky decided to start his career through the consulting arena at Andersen Consulting (now Accenture) in the Technology Architecture group specializing in the transportation services products industry. While with Andersen Consulting, Novitsky took on roles as a developer, tester, designer, programming supervisor, deployment lead, team lead, and technical architect. Additionally, Novitsky taught two courses that are offered in St. Charles, IL

Several years later, Novitsky decided to continue his career with Norstan Consulting as a senior consultant. While with Norstan Consulting, Novitsky took on roles as a developer, team lead, project manager, engagement manager, and pre-sales consultant.. Later on in Novitsky’s career at Norstan, he focused the majority of his time gaining the project management and sales activities experience.

With a strong interest in sales, Novitsky ventured away from Norstan to find a position with Eontec as a client executive. He was responsible for lead-generation, cold-calling, presentations, RFI/RFP responses, demonstrations, vendor relations, contract negotiations, managing the services organization to supply resources to closed business, etc.

Learning a lot about the banking industry, Novitsky determined that he would utilize his unique skill set of technology, project management, and sales at Bank of America. He spent 6 years in the ATM and eCommerce space, and is now employed at RBC Bank.
