David Milroy’s Summary

My job/goal is to provide the client with the best design possible within the restrictions of each job using my design and typography skills in a unique and contemporary style. My design skills, education, project management skills and prior experience in the printing industry have provided me with the skills necessary for concept/idea development, teamwork, communication and managing others. These skills have been put to use on projects for organizations such as the LPGA Keebler-Kellogg Classic, a national recruitment campaign for Wendy’s International, numerous marketing campaigns for Northern Illinois University and various other Non-for-Profit/Private corporations. I have won awards such as the ADDY®’s on many of these projects.
