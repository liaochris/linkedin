Dr. David Lepp’s Summary

“The moment I experienced the amazing healing ability of the body first-hand, I knew I was destined to become a doctor. I fell from a waterfall, apparently cheated death, and then had my first exposure to Craniopathy. With multiple abrasions and a bad head trauma, I was really banged up and in a lot of pain. A doctor used cranial manipulative techniques to treat me. Immediately, we both saw the circulation return to the injured side of my face in the treatment room mirror. A big ‘black-eye’ formed where I had hit my head. I completely healed in two weeks, after only a few treatments. No-one could believe that I had even been injured. I knew right then, I had to learn this amazing approach to health care.

I love being a guide on the path of healing. Working with you to get the maximum out of your body’s potential and improve your experience of life, is what I’m all about. My passion constantly drives me to delve deeper, continually study, and expand my skills & knowledge. This quest has literally taken me around the world.

Understanding conventional health-care models, as well as alternative approaches has proven effective for the treatment of complex cases, and to efficiently address your needs. We’ll work together toward your optimal health, leaving no stone un-turned.

Originally from NJ/NY, I moved to California to attend graduate school. I fell in love with the Bay Area and knew it would be my home. I am so grateful to practice in this beautiful community, surrounded by those that are truly inspired by what’s possible for their health and in their lives. It is an absolute honor to work towards, and achieve these goals with such amazing people.”

Dr.Lepp practices at the Center for Cranial Integration in Northern California.
