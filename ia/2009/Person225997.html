Sue Armstrong’s Summary

I have worked in some form of marketing services my entire professional career. I started out working in the art department of a weekly newspaper in my late teens, graduated to become the lead designer for a couple of printing firms, and then began my career as an entrepreneur. Along the way, I was very fortunate to study under the incredibly talented, Steve Winegar, former Audio/Visual Director, Selkirk College, BC. After 23 years of owning and operating two successful businesses, in 2005, I decided to work for Tamarack Resort where I refined my resort and luxury brand marketing skills. I am currently looking to put my many years of experience to work again as a creative director or marketing professional.
