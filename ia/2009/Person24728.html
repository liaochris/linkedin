Jay Barua’s Summary

• Jay is an experienced IT professional and has been responsible for leading and architecting Data Warehouse/Business Intelligence and CRM initiatives for multiple business units for various large companies, from marketing ideation to stakeholder engagement/agreement to requirements gathering to design to development to quality control testing to production launch to maintenance to sunset.

• His past experience includes developing and architecting the first ever implementation of enterprise-wide data warehouse initiative at Healthways. The data warehouse was successful in meeting the reporting needs of over 80+ large customers for Healthways

• Jay is experienced in different verticals ranging from Healthcare, Telecommunications and Retail. Provided overall guidance for patenting the Patient Intelligence Registry(TM) - a Healthcare Business Intelligence Framework that supports programs such as disease management, outcomes management, clinical performance and process improvement, cost and waste reduction, quality accreditation, predictive analytics, and provides data for health care research. Co-authored a plan to improve the Oregon Health Safety Net. Plan awarded grant by NLM (National Library of Medicine)

• Jay was also responsible for managing and implementing the global sourcing program for Healthways. This program supported development efforts across North American, European, and Asian countries. Jay led this initiative from market strategy, business stakeholder acquisition to vendor selection, legal contracting, price negotiation, governance and implementation.

• Jay’s highest passions are for keeping customers happy, solving complex problems, turning failed projects into success and translating technology solutions into compelling business benefits.
