Ed Liversidge’s Summary

Total of thirteen years experience in Real-Time Embedded C/C++ and Digital Signal Processing within the defence, communications and consumer electronics industries. VxWorks consultant since 1995. Experience in a large set of communications busses, processors, embedded systems and development tools. DSP algorithm implementation (FFTs, encryption, speech compression/synthesis), optimisation and debugging experience with all major chipsets. Wealth of experience in Real Time Operating Systems including VxWorks and pSOS, Windows/WinCE and Linux, board support packages and device drivers.

CANbus, VME bus, PCI bus, MIL-STD-1553, ARINC-429, USB. C/C++, DSP Assembler, Java, UML, J2ME, JSP, JDBC, XML, XSP, XSLT, HTML, Javascript.
