Stanislas Gasnot’s Summary

Venue & Selection services for meetings / groups, MICE industry in Europe and world.
As a consulting partner, Business Group Management is dedicated to the MICE Meeting Industry in identifying and solving your company needs in the site selection of Hotels and resorts for Conferences, Business Meetings, Incentives, and Business Group Travel.

Our Sales Force based in the Center of the European Community permits us to have a large access to all the most important Venues, Conference Hotels, Resorts and Meeting destinations in Europe and Worldwide.

We base our performance on your success, that is why we strive for your company and put all our experience to work for you. BG Management offers client service that is consistent, proactive and flexible with local, regional and global capabilities, we truly provide Local Presence and Global Power.

Our international experience insures our quality standards of operating. We understand that working with hotels and travel partners worldwide, we represent the interests of our clients.

Dedicated in obtaining the highest standards of quality in the C&I Meeting Industry, BG Management believes in long term relationships with it's clients and in providing a personalized and dedicated service. We are committed to our clients success, your success.

We welcome the opportunity to fulfil your groups hotel and meeting needs with BG Management.
