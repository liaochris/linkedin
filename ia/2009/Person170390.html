Jamie Leonard’s Summary

I founded MyLongLunch in 2007 with a vision to change the way media and ad agencies communicated, and to offer a platform that is more in tune with modern advertising. With just under 10 years media experience across some of the largest media companies in the world, I decided to go it alone and make my mark in the industry. I originally joined Northcliffe in 1999 and moved quickly from customer service to sales. I then joined Monster Worldwide in 2003 and set up and ran the ad agency channel, growing the team from scratch to 8 sales people in 2 years and opening a revenue stream that today is a major part of Monster's overall revenue. I then moved to fish4 to set up the ad agency team.
