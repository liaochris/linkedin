Julie C. Ottewell’s Summary

A technology hand… a business mind.

Julie recently relocated to Halifax, after spending more then tens years as an Information Technology Leader at one of Canada’s big five banks. She has experiences as an enterprise lead on security technologies, ITIL governance and processes, disaster recovery and has been third level support on multiple technology platforms. She is a strong relationship builder with a proven track record of delivering quality results on time. Julie has an inquisitive mind and believes doing the right thing means working ethically while directly contributing to a company’s success.

She is a committed life long learner with a formal education from the University of Waterloo. She has enjoyed keeping up with trends in technology with SANS GSEC and ITIL Foundations training. Often found with a book or “googling” an interesting problem, she is a self learning whose curiosity inspires.

As an active member of the Halifax community, Julie enjoys volunteering: the local SPCA, the BlueNose Marathon and Relay for Life. She is a Big Sister with Big Brothers and Sisters of Greater Halifax. In previous workplaces, she has been the department lead for the United Way fund raising and has represented her company at diversity events such as the Canadian Aboriginal Festival. She enjoys photography and has had several pictures of Nova Scotia published by CBC.
