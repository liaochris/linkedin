Keith Crowell’s Summary

Eleven years experience setting company policy, implementing customer safety, service, and support strategies, developing product features, and establishing critical business relationships in the Computer and Internet Technology industry, with the past 3 years at a Social Networking and User Generated Content Company. This includes the defining of Terms of Service and Privacy policy and procedure, developing of new product features to assist in supporting these policies, the ground-up creation of complete and comprehensive customer care organizations, and the closing of strategic revenue generating business partnerships.

Extensive knowledge producing and delivering products and programs aimed at meeting the company’s business objectives covering such aspects as protecting the company, the members, and brand partners, ensuring requirements are met for any new product or policy release, obtaining and reviewing post-launch data to validate success or need for continued iteration and reducing operation costs.

Proficient and effective at managing large teams to accomplish a broad spectrum of goals and objectives throughout the company. Some examples include setting specific performance objectives for the contact center and service center operations, the creation of a content programming team, and the managing of ad operations personnel. The daily monitoring of service level metrics for all groups is key to maintaining overall performance.
