    August 2008 — Present (6 months)

    Our Mission Statement: We believe citizen control of government is essential for peace, prosperity and freedom. We believe that the citizen initiative process is imperative for citizens to truly control their government. The Citizens in Charge Foundation (CICF) works with activists, legislators, media, opinion leaders and voters to protect the initiative and referendum process where it exists in 24 states and to expand the process to the 26 states where voters currently lack that right.
