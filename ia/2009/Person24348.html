barry bell’s Summary

I’m currently RE-developing wurk.net after a brief (my arse!) hiatus. Again, it's tipped to be the freshest resource on the web for industry-specific career advice, jobhunting tips, work hacks, and jobs themselves.

I'm also an experienced freelance advertising, marketing, communications and recruitment creative/writer. In the past 24 months, I've worked on a huge number of projects ranging from recruitment campaigns for local government organisations to a 200+ page branding document for one of Europe’s largest companies.

Up until April 2004, I worked as freelance head of copy for MKP Manchester, working with a senior team of Art Directors and covering brochures, DM, retail, radio, email, brand guidelines and nationwide POS campaigns for clients including T-Mobile, The Royal Bank of Scotland, NatWest, The Co-op, E Top-up and Fox’s Biscuits.

Before that, I was head of copy at TCS Manchester (ASDA, First Direct, Focus, and GNER), senior writer at McCann Erickson Manchester (Disprin, Lemsip and Senokot), senior writer at Barkers Manchester (Reebok, Umbro, Iceland, Cable & Wireless and William Hill) and regular freelancer for Moonfish (Sharp and Intel) and BLD Europe in Brussels (Yamaha Motorcycles and Lexus Cars).
