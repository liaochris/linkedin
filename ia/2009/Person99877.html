Embroidery Digitizing India’s Summary

Embroidery Digitizing India - Ishan Technologies is specialized in Web Desiging, artwork, digitizing, logo creation, artwork editing and Customer Support services using the following methodologies: Live Web Chat Support, Email Support, Inbound Lead Generation, Outbound Lead Generation, SEO, and Content Development.

Ishan Technologies, India offers cost effective, highly accurate custom Embroidery Digitizing Services with quality output. Our main objective is to provide good quality digitizing services at reasonable prices. We digitize your designs keeping the final production in mind. So, we deliver properly digitized designs which run smoother on the machine with minimum thread breaks.

We have professional embroiders, embroidery digitizers and skilled embroidery technicians who dedicatedly work for the satisfaction of our customers through quality performance and timely delivery. We are providing services to many countries like USA, UK, Canada, Italy, Australia, Ireland, India. We digitize artwork for uniforms, caps and hats, sweatshirts, shirts, denim shirts, jackets, bags, resort wear, blankets, etc.

Ishan Technologies provides the following Embroidery Digitizing Services:

• Chain Stitch Embroidery Digitizing
• Multicoloured Embroidery Digitizing
• 3D Embroidery Digitizing
• Gradient Embroidery Digitizing
• Satin Embroidery Digitizing
• Tatami Embroidery Digitizing
• Appliqué Embroidery Digitizing
• Wilcom Embroidery Digitizing
• Schiffli Embroidery
• Sequin Embroidery
• Chenille Embroidery
• Art Work Conversion

You can send us the artwork in any image format (BMP, JPG, GIF, TIF, PCX, CDR, AI, PSD, PDF, PCT, WMF). The file sent to us can be computer generated, hand drawn or a scanned image of the finished product. As long as the image is clear, we should be able to convert it into digitized form for you.
