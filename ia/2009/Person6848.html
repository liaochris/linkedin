Aldo Grech’s Summary

Behind every Great organisation, there are Great Leaders.
My Passion is helping people discover the Leader inside by operating from their Essence. My Purpose is to help myself and others operate from our abundant Essence of self, rather than the greed of Ego, thus creating a prosperous future for all, NOW.

My Management Consulting History
Since 1997, I have been a Catalyst for change, assisting organisations large and small in the processes of developing solutions, raising funds, cost-cutting, revenue-acceleration, staff development, restructure and mergers and acquisitions.

Aldo Grech’s Specialties:

Strategist, CxO Networker, Complex Sales Specialist, Technologist, Mobility Technology Leader, Team Developer, Eternal Student and Hands-on Operator.
