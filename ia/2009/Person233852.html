Tim O'Brien’s Summary

22 Years of progressive sales and sales management experience across a broad range of industries including technology consulting, recruiting and executive placement, business services, consumer, pharmaceutical and medical industries.

While here at Spherion/Technisource I have been recognized as the "Rookie of the Year", as well as winning multiple Performance Forum recognitions (top 10% performer). I was also nominated for the Spherion's Pinnacle Award which recognized my contributions as a Top 2% performer.

Beyond delivering on our full service professional recruiting capabilities at Technisource, our business development efforts have been actively engaged in a growing and servicing a number of client discussions regarding our strategic consulting and technical services offerings within "The Provali Group".

The Provali Group delivers solutions to our clients most pressing business and technology challenges relative to topics such as:

* Strategy Management Services such as M&A Planning, PMO & Project Management, Requirements definition, Vendor Selection and Business Process Re-Engineering

* Application Management Services focused on areas such as ERP, CRM, HRIS, BI, DW, Portals, Application Development & Management services

* Infrastructure Management Services including Data Center operations, Remote and On-site Systems Adminstration & Management, Network Management, Help Desk, Deskside and Mobile technology support, Technology Deployment Services as well as Turnkey IT services

* IT Outsourcing Services inclusive of full and partial outsourcing models scalable to your business requirements.
