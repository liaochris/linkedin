Frederic Lalonde’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    openplaces

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    April 2007 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    Openplaces is a project to build the world's largest organized repository of maps, pictures, details, and advice about every place in the world that anyone has ever travelled to. Anyone can contribute.

    We started Openplaces with one idea in mind: planning a trip is fun, but it's not easy. We got tiered of spending hours gathering pieces of information from 100's of sites, most of them useless. The web has empowered us, but now we're overloaded by dead-ends and commercial opportunism.

    We think Openplaces can become the one search engine for travel that helps put all the pieces together.

  • Board of Directors

    MakeMyTrip.com

    (Privately Held; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    2007 — Present (2 years)

    I joined the Board of Directors of MakeMyTrip in 2007. MakeMyTrip India is India’s leading online travel company, catering to the Indian market. MakeMyTrip books tickets for all major airlines as has a tie up with over 1000 hotels in the country.

  • Vice President, Hotels & Packages Product Planning

    Expedia

    (Public Company; EXPE; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    2004 — 2006 (2 years)

    I managed a team of 10 people in Bellevue, Dallas and Montreal. I was in charge of product planning for Hotels and Packages on the Expedia.com website. I also ran reporting and forecasting for Expedia’s Hotels & Packages North of business. I participated closely in various M&A activities, including Hotels.com, Trip Advisor and eLong.

  • Vice President, Hotel Supplier Strategy

    expedia

    (Public Company; EXPE; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    2002 — 2004 (2 years)

    I oversaw the integration of the Newtrade development team in the Expedia software engineering group. I was responsible for developing the Expedia Direct Connect technology product strategy. I was also responsible for signing-up the major hotel chain partners for Direct Connect.

    In 2 years we connected over 10 000 hotels including major hotel chains (Marriot, Hilton, Hyatt, Carlson, Cendant, Choice Hotels, etc.) and independent hotels. When I left Expedia, Direct Connect technology was processing over 6 million rate & inventory updates and 125 000 reservations a week.

  • Founder & CTO

    Newtrade Technologies Inc.

    (Internet industry)

    March 1997 — October 2002 (5 years 8 months)

    I was co-Founder of Newtrade Technologies Inc. in 1997. We raised 7M$ CAD of venture capital in two rounds from local venture capital firms, and built the company up to 70 employees. I led a team of 45 software engineers, testers, and system architects in building one of the world’s largest connectivity platforms for the hospitality market. In 2002 we reached profitability with major accounts that included Pegasus, Travel Web, Best Western and Hotels.com.
    In 2002 we sold Newtrade technologies to Expedia Inc, 21 months after first round of funding.
