Ferhan Hamid’s Summary

Mr. Hamid was previously Director of Primum Public Realty Trust, a subsidiary of FBR Capital Markets, focused on public-private partnerships in real estate. While at FBR, Mr. Hamid also served as Director of FBR Investment Management, where he developed and evaluated new strategies for equity funds, hedge funds, and externally managed businesses.

Mr. Hamid was as a top aide to Chicago Mayor Richard M. Daley serving as Managing Deputy Comptroller, Deputy Comptroller, and Assistant Comptroller for the City of Chicago. Mr. Hamid was an integral part of the City’s finance team in the successful long-term lease of the Chicago Skyway, the first concession-sale of a toll road to a private operator in the U.S.; the City’s long-term lease of the Downtown Public Parking System; and the privatization of Midway International Airport, the first privatization of a major hub airport in the U.S.

While at the City, Mr. Hamid participated in innovative lease transactions and numerous municipal bond financings. He managed a $375 million budget and twice negotiated a new benefits package with 43 labor unions.

Prior to joining the City, Mr. Hamid served as Counsel to the Chicago Park District where he drafted and negotiated dozens of complex transactions, including concession agreements and lease agreements. In addition, Mr. Hamid served as issuer’s counsel on numerous public finance transactions.

Mr. Hamid previously served as Assistant Illinois Attorney General concentrating on state and local tax and bankruptcy-related matters. He holds a B.A. with honors in Political Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana and a J.D. from DePaul University College of Law.

Ferhan Hamid’s Specialties:

Mr. Hamid’s practice is concentrated on public finance and corporate transactional work. Mr. Hamid also has a broad range of experience in public-private partnerships and governmental affairs. He has worked on both the business and legal side of the capital markets and government, giving him a unique perspective into the needs of his clients.
