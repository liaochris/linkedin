William Luciw’s Summary

William is Managing Director at Viewpoint West Partners LLC, a technology advisory & research company.

Previously, William was Sr Director of Media Intelligence Services for ZillionTV, where he developed the business strategy, market and product requirements for ZillionTV Media Intelligence Services, including targeted advertising intellectual property (patents).

William was the Sr Technical Product Manager / Solutions Architect for Audience Research & Measurement at TiVo, Inc. [ http://stopwatch.tivo.com] William was also the Director of Product Marketing for Zend Technologies, focusing on Zend Platform for the Enterprise.

William was Principal Product Mgr & Technologist for Mobile Charging at Openwave, managing technical requirements from conception to delivery, such as Openwave's MCI where he standardized the mobile charging interface for all Openwave mobile service offerings. William delivered the world's first true MMS Prepaid system at Vodacom using the Openwave MMSC and was responsible for the definition and deployment of OPWV's first MMSC product.
