Jeremy Johnson’s Summary

Hello, my name is Jeremy and I'm an User Experience Manager for a small UX Group here at Sabre in Southlake, TX.

I have a love for all things visual, and whether I'm watching indie films or producing stunning interactions for the web, I see things differently. Which comes in handy when I'm shooting photos, updating one of my fifteen web sites or arguing the superiority of the Mac. When it comes to Design, there's not much I can't do. And if I can't, you can be sure one of my hundreds of tech gadgets can.

During my day job I'm a web versatilist skilled in interaction design, user experience, information architecture and anything else loosely related to those topics.

In my off-hours I enjoy photography, Macs, travel, video games, and art.
