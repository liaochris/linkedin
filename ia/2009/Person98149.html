  • Founder/Owner

    Ill Willed Productions

    (Entertainment industry)

    2001 — Present (8 years)

    Ill Willed Productions specializes in the art of illusion. We provide a variety of specialized services for the film and television industry including state of the art Special Effects Makeup, advanced physical FX, Cosmetic Make-up, Fantasy Make-up, conceptual and character design, quality storyboarding, or any custom request for the Halloween/Haunted House industry. We focus on the technical aspects of special FX creation as well as the importance of design aesthetics, providing the optimal visual experience whatever your cinematic vision may be.

    SFX artist Tate Steinsiek established Ill Willed FX in 2001 in NYC on the historic Bowery, currently residing in the eclectic Collective Hardware at 169 bowery. The "Monster Shop" occupies a 2000 square foot facility on the Bowery. Recent Clients include Jim Henson Company, History Channel, The Discovery Channel, National Geographic, Dreamworks, Dark Sky Films, and Lionsgate.

    Tate Steinsiek and Ill Willed Productions have coordinated over 40 projects in Film and Television, including vast international experience, having shot on location in Romania, India, Lithuania, Morocco, Canada, and Turkey. Our team of 10 artists complete a team ready to excel on any level production.

    I.W.P. also makes available a number of different design and illustration options that can further the visual process of your project. We offer quick and detailed storyboarding, conceptual design of any character or creature your film requires; including creation of small scale maquettes to see "in the flesh" exactly what your creature/character design has to offer.We also offer custom renderings for any Art Dept. needs; i.e. painting fabrication, mural work, sculpture/statue etc.
