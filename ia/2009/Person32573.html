Brenden O'Neil’s Summary

Brenden O'Neil is an Atlanta/Miami based entrepreneur with a diverse background ranging from facility planning to technology infrastructure and security. After a brief tenure with Greenpeace International, Jamaica Plain, Boston, Brenden spent the next four years organizing secure communication systems for the United States Coast Guard in Haiti, Panama and Columbia. Brenden joined GSI Office Environments in 1996 and spent the next several years re-crafting the company's value proposition by integrating technology services with open plan furniture systems. Brenden took over leadership of GSI Office Environments in 2003 and since then has doubled the growth of the company. GSI has become recognized as a market leader related to strategic facility planning for businesses headquartered in the Southeastern United States. With Brenden's leadership and guidance GSI Office Environments has matured into a facility services company that has the capability to take a business plan from concept to reality.
With 14 years experience providing strategic facility solutions; Brenden has expertise specific to business start ups and turn-arounds that go far beyond what most office furniture dealerships will communicate as part of their "scope". Brenden has consulted with a diverse range of companies and has created and executed full operational business packages all over the United States. In 2003 Brenden expanded these services to Southern Africa and is Atlanta's foremost expert on establishing, recruiting, moving and executing support service business plans to South Africa.
In 2007 Brenden partnered with Kimball International who leads the office furniture industry in the applied design science of cognitive ergonomics. Together, GSI and Kimball provide a new and innovative perspective on how the new American creative class works.
