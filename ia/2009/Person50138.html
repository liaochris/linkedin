Craig McCourt’s Summary

Founder and director of GodPonders and'Nuf Said ministries.

Youth and family minister for 25 years, includes having spoken to thousands of students and adults in groups both large and small at schools, camps, and churches. Over the years he has been blessed to have the opportunity to speak both internationally as well as in many different places across the United States.

My mission is to “Share Christ through the great stories of Scripture and Life."

Craig McCourt’s Specialties:

Certified Presenter with Youth Specialties / Real World Parents, Short Team Mission Trip Speaker & Trainer, Certified Lay Minister in the Lutheran Church-Missouri Synod. Current President of the Minnesota South - Directors of Christian Education Conference.
