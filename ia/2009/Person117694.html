John Mulledy’s Summary

About Linium:


Linium is an IT management consulting and solutions integration firm specializing in IT service and asset management solutions, financial planning applications, and business intelligence. For over a decade, Linium has been delivering IT solutions and services that provide business value to the Fortune 500 and Government industries.

In addition to IT Management Consulting, Linium has niche expertise in the implementation of commercially off the shelf (COTS) products, including Oracle/Hyperion Software financial planning applications and Hewlett Packard (HP) Business Technology Optimization applications. Linium highly supports the use of COTS software as a means of educing the overhead of proprietary development and maintenance. Our consultants are fully trained on latest software offerings.
