Ravi Yajurvedi’s Summary

Ravi has more than thirty-five years of professional experience in the Engineering industry. He has extensively worked in the Electrical and Electronics domain. He has successfully served in varied roles at Larsen & Toubro Limited, in senior management positions. He has successfully enabled streamlining operations focusing on continuous improvement at the business unit, company as well as group levels. He has also successfully represented Larsen & Toubro Limited at various trade fairs in European countries. Managing complex projects and ambitious initiatives involving large diverse teams has been his forte. He has been instrumental in bringing about substantial cost savings for the company over several years.
He will bring a value-add to your organization, leveraging his techno-management skills. He has a vast experience in Multi-Party Negotiations, People Management, Process Consulting, Vendor Development, Vendor Management and Cost Optimization.
Currently as a part of ‘Globus Management Consultants’, he is in the process of initiating consulting engagements with dynamic, respected, ethical and global organizations.

He has had the privilege of representing L&T at various professional bodies in different capacities - BCCI, CII, NSC and AVERT Society.

Memberships of Professional Institutes:
a. The Institution of Engineers (India) (Fellowship of Institution of Engineers & Chartered Engineer, [India])
b. National Institute of Personnel Management (NIPM)
c. Indian Merchants’ Chamber (IMC)
d. Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM) (Former Member)
