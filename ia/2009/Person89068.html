Grant Wood’s Summary

Mr Wood is a visionary technologist who creates solutions to complex enterprise problems through hands-on application of the latest tools and practices. He began his career on billionaire Curt Carlson's pet project almost 15 years ago as an intern, outfitting rural U.S. cities with reward points systems. In 1999 he gained local and national attention for his work with advanced internet technology, and was invited to be the youngest panelist at Info World's first ever CTO Forum. This exposure led him to start his first company in 2000, where he invented several technologies to simplify the processes of collaborating within complex organizations.

Over the last decade, Mr. Wood has developed a track record of innovation, trend spotting and leadership, which has made him a sought after advisor for startup companies, for whom he has helped raise over 7M in funding. He has been recruited by companies in the advertising, bio-medical, insurance, health care, software and internet industries because of his record of converting ideas into functioning businesses. He is a strong advocate of agile management, agile development and iterative methodology.

Grant is an active community leader and co-founded the nonprofit Story Board which mentors high school aged filmmakers and provides them with experiences that encourage leadership, integrity and modern communication and technology skills.
