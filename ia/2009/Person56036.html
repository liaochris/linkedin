Dave Cash’s Summary

I have a vast range of work experience. Started out as a motorcycle mechanic, made my way to cars as a fleet mechanic and eventually heavy construction equipment in the Military. I also worked as a Multichannel Communications Equipment Operator and Hawk Radar technician while in the Military.

Always thirsting for knowledge, I have obtained my CompTIA A+ certification and completed MCSE training.
