Scott Koziol’s Summary

Scott Koziol is an enthusiastic and creative Sales Professional that reaches a diverse audience thru visionary marketing strategies and customer development initiatives. In his previous role as a Higher Education Account Executive for Apple Inc., Scott promoted Apple awareness throughout Southern California via workshops, presentations, and events.
