Lisa Grossmann’s Summary

My specialty has always been out of the box thinking which has lent it self to various careers.

Over the last 20 years I have built several large global distribution networks within the direct selling industry where I am a multi-million dollar earner. I have held executive management positions at the corporate level and have consulted to numerous companies with a focus on global expansion, communication and coaching systems.

I have worked with several internet startups that went to onto success and some that were spectacular flash in the pans that ended in failure.

Now I focus on consulting and working with groups that are looking for new and innovative ways to apply tried and true principles in the 21st century. When people ask me what I do my standard answer has become "tell me what you need and I will find a way to get it done, or tell me what your problem is and we will find a way to solve it."

Over the past few months as this recession has set in we have seen more and more people coming to us for new ideas. We see what others don't see especially in thier own businesses. What we are finding is the old answers don't work for the new questions and change and innovation is no longer a risk it is the only way to thrive and grow in a rapidly changing economy.
