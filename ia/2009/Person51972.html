Daen de Leon’s Summary

Specialist in design and implementation of software tools and information systems for biotech/pharma.

I greatly enjoy working with intelligent, dedicated end-users in a knowledge-rich and challenging industry like biotech/pharma, where rapidly producing custom software tools for unique applications, and coaxing every last nuance of meaning from (oftentimes) patchy and noisy data can mean the difference between financial success and failure. It is my belief that there are few other sectors to work in which can offer the creative buzz that comes from this.
