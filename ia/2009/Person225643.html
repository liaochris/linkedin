Stu Collett’s Summary

I am the Co-Founder and Design Director of Super User Studio Ltd, a user experience and GUI design studio that offers consultancy and design services to leading media and software companies, and online brands.

At Super User Studio, we uniquely combine a detailed UX design process with strong art direction; to optimise the relationship between a brand and its target user.

Stu Collett’s Specialties:

Art Direction (web, interactive, print, photography), Graphical User Interface Design, User Experience Design (competitive analysis, information architecture, wireframing / prototyping, user journeys, persona creation, style guides, usability testing), Creation of Themes & Concepts, Graphic Design, Branding & Identity, Website Evaluation & Estimating.
