Peter Hoang’s Summary

My primary background is in network administration in the field of Information Technology and developing internet marketing strategies & IT solutions for growing businesses.

My work experience has varied from start-ups to private companies and government agencies, which has given me invaluable knowledge and insight into the unique challenges that different organizations face in their respective sectors. In particular, as an entrepreneur and former business owner, I've gained incredible first-hand experience on how to benchmark success and to position strategies that proactively combat failure in the business world.

In 2006, I co-founded Techno+Rescue with the mission of emphasizing client data security, creating public awareness on sustainability, and advocating more stringent standards and business practices in the electronics recycling industry.

During my tenure, I defined our corporate branding & identity, managed internal operations, developed and manged marketing strategies, and was integral in business development--even launching a 2nd retail location within the first two years of operation. I also served as liaison to community outreach programs and educational community events.

I recently joined a young and superbly-talented team at machineLOGIC (www.machinelogic.com), an innovative technology solution provider for growing businesses, where I currently serve as Director of Business Development & IT Consultant.

Our company implements and supports IT-related solutions to help your business grow, reducing your costs, and enabling you to remain competitive in today's business environment.

In addition, we provide creative design services that enable our clients to deliver high-impact web and print marketing to their target audience.
