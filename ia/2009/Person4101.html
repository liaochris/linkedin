Adrian Punt’s Summary

Adrian Punt (Dr), BSc (Hons), MSc, PhD, CRadP is a senior level environmental professional providing consultancy support within the civil nuclear programme.

He is an experienced bid director, specialising in procurement strategy and business development particularly in the public sector aspects of the UK regulators and nuclear licensed sites managed under the NDA.

He remains activily involved in technical project delivery acting as a project director and strategy advisor on a number of legacy waste management and environmental and radiological assessment issues.

Adrian Punt’s Specialties:

Commercial strategy and business development, public sector procurement constraints and regulations, bid development, legacy waste management optineering, radiological assessment, environmental performance, environmental radioactivity, monitoring and modelling
