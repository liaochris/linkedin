Bruce Scoggins’s Summary

I have had a colorful career thus far, and I am sure the best is yet to come. I spent six years proudly serving in the Air Force as an electronics technician, and getting the opportunity to see Western Europe and experience other cultures.

Then I shed my monkey suit for a shirt and tie as I spent the next ten years traveling and working for Raytheon E-Systems as a field electrical engineer fixing little black boxes in not so good conditions at various locations throughout the world.

Next, I decided to settle down and give my traveling shoes a rest. Plus, I had a strong desire to get into the telecom industry. With a passion for the written word, I honed my technical writing skills first at telecom technologies as manager of the Technical Publications department. I continue my wordsmithing trade today as Principal Technical Writer at Sonus Networks.
