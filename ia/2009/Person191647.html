Rachel Gordon’s Summary

I am a Consultant on the IT Banking and Technology division at Huxley Associates in London. As an office we recruit for roles in London and the immediate Home Countries (Essex, Kent, Surrey, Middlesex and Hertfordshire).

My organisation is on the official supply list for a large number of Global Investment Banks, in addition to select hedge funds, asset management and investment management companies in the City.

I have a well-established and successful record of placing Developers of all levels of seniority with Financial Institutions.

I specialise in the recruitment of Developers for the finance world. I recruit for a wide range of development positions of all levels of seniority.

Development technologies that I cover include (but not limited to):

Java
C#
C++
Excel VBA

I also specialise in the Business Intelligence / Data Warehousing market.

I am based out of our King William Street Office (at Bank Underground Station).

If you would like some further information about particular roles I am recruiting for, require market advice or salary guidance then please drop me an email and I will get back to you as soon as I can.
