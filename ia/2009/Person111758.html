Jessica Santana’s Summary

I am interested in technology that meets the needs of the user without compromise. I design technology solutions that infuse with the user's environment to facilitate otherwise cumbersome activities through a healthy, holistic interface.

I focus my efforts on mobile software design and rural mobile information and communication technology (ICT). I invest in rural sustainable economic development, both abroad and domestically, with an emphasis on technology.
