Aron Bohlig’s Summary

Aron Bohlig has over fifteen years of investment banking, equity research and operating experience working with, and for, both startups and large global growth companies. In his work with next generation businesses Aron brings a keen understanding of how to help growth companies achieve successful strategic outcomes as well as an extensive rolodex of key technology partners and financing sources.

Aron's financial services experience includes tenures with Credit Suisse, Savvian and Investec where he focused on executing over fifty engaged financing and advisory transactions for clients and as well as business origination with digital media, traditional media, telecommunications and software accounts. Aron has completed approximately $2 billion dollars in advisory transactions and $1 billion dollars in financing transactions for growth companies. Aron began his financial services career as an Equity Research Associate at Investec focused on enterprise software.

Prior to becoming a banker Aron held management and individual contributor positions at Metropolis Software, Clarify Software and Nortel Networks. Aron has experience in the full lifecycle of product definition, development and release having worked as a programmer, product marketing manager, overlay sales person and divisional director of strategy. Aron is the co-author of a book on selling high technology products that is available on Amazon.com and at national retailers.
Aron received his B.S. from the University of Puget Sound, and his M.B.A. from The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania. Aron lives in San Francisco with his wife and two children. Aron is active as an advisory board member to digital media companies and as a board member for non profit organizations.

Aron Bohlig’s Specialties:

Domain expertise: digital media (local, mobile, social, video) and software as a service.
Functional expertise: M&A, financial restructuring/recapitalization, equity and debt financing.
