Joshua Winograd’s Summary

I graduated from Georgia Institute of Technology with a BS in Management and certificates in finance and marketing.

While attending Georgia Institute of Technology, I worked for Barry manufacturing, a men's clothing chain with 40+ stores and a manufacturing presence in Costa Rica. At Barry Manufacturing, I managed the relationship with the company's outsourced marketing firm and assisted the marketing firm and Barry Manufacturing's CEO in developing the company's advertising and marketing campaigns.

I am currently the director of MIS for Regency Hospital Company. I work as a liaison between the company's business units and IT. By leveraging my strong project management and business analysis skills, I have been able to, in numerous areas, help the business realize greater efficiencies through better use of technology while at the same time focusing IT on aligning its implementation and development of technology with the business goals of the organization.

I have a strong financial analysis and planning background that I have used throughout my career and use significantly in my current role to manage my department’s budget, develop ROI analysis for new systems, and build financial projection models.

I am entrepreneurial and I look for opportunities to strategize and think creatively outside the box to find unique opportunities and innovative solutions.
