Sébastien Léonard’s Summary

ZEROCO2
A unique carbon offset program that helps in the fight against climate change. The program revolves around a series of patented business applications and practices that actively promote the development of eco actions through the planting of trees, the sale of carbon credits and the advancement of value-added environmental projects.

ZEROCO2 is globally unique in its approach to the development of exclusive business practices. To date, the program has regreened hundreds of acres of land, all maintained and managed (ISO 14064) at no cost to host communities. Additionally, tens of thousands of trees have been planted, creating a genuine green revolution based on the highest international standards—accessible to everyone. Visit ZEROCO2.com

EcoAd
EcoAd is a unique corporate and municipal advertising program for financing large-scale tree plantations in urban areas. The program encompasses a series of business processes and expertise that promote the development and improvement of urban communities.

EcoAd works in conjunction with supporting marketing campaigns to help organizations increase the media value of their involvement in communities. To date, thousands of trees, countless urban green spaces, many high traffic areas, numerous points of interest throughout Quebec and various municipalities have benefited from EcoAd’s remarkable program.
_____
EnviroModex
A science, technology and technical expertise think tank for businesses and government agencies, offering consulting, training and the development of products related to climate change and the environment.

The company helps quantify, verify and validate carbon offset programs and other programs that claim to reduce greenhouse gasses by providing access to carbon markets, the financing of relevant projects and the implementation and execution of eco projects in line with the highest scientific standards for environmental rehabilitation.
