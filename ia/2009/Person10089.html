Michael Isaac Almond’s Summary

I have worked as a User Experience Designer for the last 8 years in both full time positions and as an independent freelancer. I have years of experience working with User-Centered Design principles, Visual Design and Branding, User Interface Design, and Interaction Design for consumer facing Web products, including Software as a Service(SaaS) and Social Media applications. I also have experience working on enterprise applications.

My professional skills draw on ten years of previous experience as an Art Director, and Campaign Organizer at a leading Social Marketing media agency. I have management experience as well: leading creative teams, working directly with clients and collaborating with key stakeholders. I am also a Contributing Writer to Digital Web Magazine.

Michael Isaac Almond’s Specialties:

User Experience design, User Interface Design, Interaction Design, Visual Design, Brand Identity, Usability, Content Recommendations, Copy writing and Editing, Front-end Technologies, Prototyping, Graphic Standards and Guidelines, Typography, Color Theory, Management Experience, Published Writer.
