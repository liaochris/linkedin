Gary Ambrosino’s Summary

I have spent the past 20 years as an executive in early stage businesses including venture backed startups as well as divisions of larger companies. My focus is expanding opportunity for product or service companies through effective marketing and sales, that result in accelerating revenue growth, market sector leadership, and increased company valuation. Roles have included CEO of three successful companies, VP of Business Development, VP of Marketing, and VP of Engineering. Successes include Cognio - acquired by Cisco, and Xionics (printer languages) which was an IPO.

Gary Ambrosino’s Specialties:

Leadership, strategy, execution, funding, and operations management of technology companies. Transforming organizations to realize their maximum value and provide an excellent return. CEO, General Management, Marketing, Business Development, and Sales experience. Cleantech, energy management, sustainable business systems and technology domain experience.
