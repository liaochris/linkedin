Ken Jacobs’s Summary

Jacobs Communications Consulting helps organizations grow business and develop staff, through its consulting, training and coaching programs. We help companies, from boutique-size through global corporations, enhance staff performance, motivation, retention and attraction.

We specifically help PR firms achieve their goals by consulting on agency new business/marketing campaigns and proposals/presentations; leading workshops on expanding business from existing clients; designing training programs and offering interactive training workshops; and facilitating idea generation sessions.

In addition, we offer coaching to individuals and teams.
