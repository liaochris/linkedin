Jon Reed’s Summary

I help publishers, NGOs and small businesses use social media to market themselves. I produce websites, blogs, podcasts and online video; and write, lecture, and run workshops. Previously, I spent 10 years in book publishing, most recently as publishing director for McGraw-Hill.

My businesses are:
1. Reed Media, a social media production company working with publishers, charities and NGOs.
2. Small Business Studio, a web design and marketing agency for small businesses and entrepreneurs. We offer web design and social media marketing services, plus logo design, branding and business stationery - everything you need to get your business up and running, under one roof.

My blogs are:
• Publishing Talk (www.publishingtalk.eu)
- about promoting books with social media. Also has the largest publishing group on Facebook (over 1,600 members), and is to become a podcast in 2008.
• Small Business Marketing (www.smallbusinessstudio.co.uk/blog)
- about how small businesses can take advantage of social media.

I lecture on a number of courses, including:
• Foundation Degree in Media and Business Applications (Birkbeck)
• Postgraduate Diploma in Digital Media Management (Birkbeck)
• MA in Publishing Studies (City University)
• MA in Creative Writing (Brunel)

I also do an increasing amount of writing, and enjoy being on the other side of the publishing fence! I've written articles on social media, have a short chapter in the 2009 edition of the Writer's Handbook (Palgrave Macmillan, July 2008), and am currently writing a book.
