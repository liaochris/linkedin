Shael Sharma’s Summary

Shael brings 15 years plus experience in Public Relations and Brand Marketing using diverse tools across print, internet, trade shows etc, both on the agency (sell side) end with top PR firms in senior positions, and then at the Client (buy side) end with market leading transnational companies.

As GM, for the Western Regon for 20:20 Media, Shael was the P&L Leader with general management responsibilities for a top PR firm. The roles here included business development including up-selling and new business acquisition; resource development, recruiting and anti-attrition strategy responsibilities; people management for Bombay and Pune branches with over 35 reports;

Earlier roles included Assistant General Manager for the Western Region from
ITC Infotech where he headed Corporate Marketing & Communications function, and his role spanned Strategic Global Marketing (North America & Western Europe markets), Events & Lead Generation, Public Relations, Hi Tech Recruitment Campaign Marketing, Advertising Alliance Management, and Government Relations.

Before joining ITC, Shael has had previous stints with IBM, Spin IMC-a Bombay (Mumbai) based marketing communications micro-consultancy firm as a founder-CEO. He has also held Global PR, Marketing & Communications roles at Grey Text 100, SAS, MphasiS - BFL and Siemens (SBS).

Shael works closely with industry bodies like NASSCOM, CII, FICCI and IBEF and is a frequent presenter at Industry forums as well as a prolific writer on Marketing and PR.

Shael Sharma’s Specialties:

PR & Marcomms offerings for Banking & Financial Services, Technology across Product Marketing and Offshore Services, Outsourcing & Global Delivery, Automobile, Travel & Hospitality and Luxury. Media Training, Advocacy and Industry speaking & Thought Leadership, Talent Marketing & University Relations and Analyst Relations.
