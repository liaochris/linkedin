Roger Martin’s Summary

I serve as the lead consultant and primary developer for my custom software company Tech Info Systems. I am also the creator and lead developer for Gallery Server Pro, an open source .NET web application that allows web sites to easily share image, video, audio, and document files.

I grew up in Fort Atkinson, WI and earned a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from UW-Platteville. After college I moved to Colorado and worked for five years as a Manufacturing Engineer in a mid-sized manufacturing company. My passion for computers motivated me to study programming, and soon I transferred to the company's IT department, where I spent three years writing custom software to improve internal business processes.

In 2004 I returned to Fort Atkinson to be near my family and to enjoy the benefits of living in a small, friendly community. My amazing wife Margaret has adjusted well considering the culture shock of leaving her native northern California.

I earned a Microsoft Certified Solution Developer certificate in 2001, and am an expert with using Microsoft technologies to enhance your business. I aggressively stay up-to-date on the latest changes in the industry, and am actively involved in local developer groups.

My #1 goal is to provide my customers with programs and services that fulfill their needs and come in within budget and on time. I am very proud of my work and I know you will be, too.
