Kat Donnelly’s Summary

Accomplished Professional Engineer with one year of self-employment, six years of project management and leadership experience at the San Diego Associations of Governments (the region’s MPO), two years in fast-paced consulting civil and environmental engineering firm, and eight years in the health insurance industry. Proven success supervising consultant teams and progressing engineering projects from conception to implementation with individual budgets up to $60 million. Award-winning scholar with dual Masters’ degrees from MIT in (Sustainable Energy) Technology and Policy, and Civil and Environmental Engineering.

Kat Donnelly’s Specialties:

Consulting on policy, technology, and consumer issues on demand-side, Advanced Meter Infrastructure and sustainable energy solutions.
