Debra Anderson’s Summary

Debra Anderson is founder of Culture Shock Marketing (CSM) and a dynamic marketing consultant and strategist for a growing client list of emerging galleries, artists, curatorial and creative clients, leading art organizations and corporations.

Known for her creative approach to marketing and media relations, Ms. Anderson has implemented successful campaigns for clients at Scope Miami (2005), Bridge Art Fair (2006), and Art Now Fair (2007) during Art Basel Miami Beach. In February 2008, Shepard and Amand Fairey selected Culture Shock Marketing as official firm of record to handle public and media relations for their newly relocated Los Angeles-based gallery, Subliminal Projects. In July 2008, Culture Shock Marketing is selected as the premier marketing firm to partner with Gen Art, the nation's leading art organization, to launch Gen Art Vanguard New Contemporary Art Fair, the world's first art fair dedicated to New Contemporary artwork, debuting in Miami, December 4 -7, 2008.

Anderson's diverse aspects of strategic marketing and communications planning are offered to enhance opportunities for market positioning and brand extension.

She understands what it takes to successfully build brand and partnerships. She posses the know-how and experience to manage and implement multifaceted, multilevel programs. Whether you have a specific project need or require long-term planning and consulting services, CSM will work with you to implement a successful, yet cost-efficient solution.

Anderson has worked on several successful art auctions and fundraisers, building partnerships between for-profit and not-for-profit organizations. Recently, Anderson has expanded her client base to include consulting for leading motion graphic companies.
