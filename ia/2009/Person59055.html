David Vanoverbeke’s Experience

  • Move!

    (Public Company; 1-10 employees; Human Resources industry)

    August 2006 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    Move! is a small international consulting practice, focussing on 'how people learn' in organizations. We help organizations build processes that effectively help people (individual, teams and the organization as a whole) to "change the way they do things" and by doing that improving their contribution to the mission and performance of their organization. We analyse 'why people do things' in an organization and try to identify the 'levers' that would help them change their behavior, not only looking at individual competenties and training but also taking into account organizational processes, reward systems, leadership and management climate, organizational structure, etc.. That holistic approach is set up with maximum involvement of all stakeholders in the organization and is meant to 'develop' in the organization the competencies to change and to learn and to develop a 'learning organization'

    Specialties:
    Learning, behavioral change, organization development
