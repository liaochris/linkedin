Doug Allan’s Summary

Coming to the sign industry from an architectural education & work experience, I am knowledgable in construction technology & detail oriented planning disciplines. With a fine arts background, my shift into graphic design & sign production has given me a career that capitalizes on all the natural talents & personal ambitions I have at my disposal. I am very fortunate to have found a career to suit me so well.

Doug Allan’s Specialties:

Besides logo design, I have trained myself to understand the many unique challenges in the visual communications field, that are of particular concern to the arena of outdoor signage. This allows Island Sign to offer an emphasis on effective solutions with our clients marketing objectives in mind, as well as an esthetically pleasing composition & a cohesive balanced layout.
