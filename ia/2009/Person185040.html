Per Vikström’s Summary

|| Experience ||

Over the years, I've worked in different businesses such as telecoms, finance, construction industry, government agencies and in different consulting companies. Often in an international environment.

One of my strengths is the ability to both understand the strategic and operational views, as well as the marketing and technical point of view. I'm very good at listening to and understanding what my customer wants and needs, no matter if it's usability issues or business processes.

I lead my project teams in a stimulating and engaging way. I want all project participants to feel positive about what they do and how they do it. The truth is - as a project manager, I'm nothing without my team.

|| Goals ||

I want to deliver solutions and ideas that help my customers achieve their goals. It's not about fancy features or cool technologies. It's about what YOU need to succeed on the digital media market - and I can help you.
