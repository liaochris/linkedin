Olivier AUDINO’s Summary

At world level, I am in charge of optimizing Service spend and better manage and control usage for all Service categories (55+M€ including Insurances, Industrial Properties, Legal, Finance, Real Estate, Taxes…)

My position is responsible for leading cross functional teams consisting of brand or department representatives, category experts, and functional expert (e.g., Finance and Legal) through a data driven objective strategic sourcing process with the aim to drive down the total cost of ownership.

Part of the Business Strategy of my position includes:
- Provide support, knowledge, feedback to Senior Managers for projects driven on a global basis;
- Ensure that these projects are well documented (data collection) and implemented successfully;
- Lead the development, implementation and alignment of world sourcing strategies for key spend categories to achieve business objectives;
- Participates in resolution of any constraints and issues related to sourcing strategies;
- Manages supplier events (e.g., RFI/RFP/RFQ) and reports result to end users;
- Measures and tracks internal performance within each category and defines budgetary impact.
