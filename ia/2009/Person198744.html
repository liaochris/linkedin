Rick Thompson’s Summary

Greentech Media was founded in February 2007 with the vision of creating a fully integrated online-media company comprised of cutting-edge news, in-depth research and analysis and focused industry events. Greentech Media was founded by Scott Clavenna and Rick Thompson, bringing diverse experience from technology startups, market-research firms and integrated online-media companies, notably Light Reading, the leading online company serving the telecommunications market. Greentech Media is headquartered in Cambridge, Mass., New England’s educational- and technology-innovation center, with operations in New York City and San Francisco. For more information, visit http://www.greentechmedia.com.

My previous experience has been with several of the industry's most successful private and public data networking and telecommunications firms focused on LAN and WAN infrastructure technologies. Specialties include IP data networking, IPTV, Internet Video, SONET & Optical Networking, Ethernet, and Wireless Networking. Functions include Product Management, Product Marketing, Research & Analysis, Program Management, and Operations.
