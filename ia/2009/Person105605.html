Jason Ryan’s Summary

I am the Communications/public affairs Manager for the State Services Commission, the New Zealand central government agency responsible for:
* the appointment and performance management of the Public Service chief executives
* standards of integrity and conduct across the State Services
* New Zealand's e-government programme

I am a committee member for the Network of Public Sector Communicators, a professional body of government public affairs practitioners based in Wellington, New Zealand.

In September 2006, I launched the Network blog, http://psnetwork.org.nz/blog/ to analyze and discuss emerging social media issues and how they impact upon the public sector.

Jason Ryan’s Specialties:

Strategic communications advice and social media and the role of technology in communications, particularly in the public sector.
