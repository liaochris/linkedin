Specialization
Ms. Tiffany Victoria Bradshaw is a dynamic nationally recognized speaker, organizational strategist, and business coach. She earned her BA in Marketing from Michigan State University and her MBA in Marketing from Pepperdine University. She is renowned as one of the leading authorities on Personal Branding (the Marketing & Differentiation of individuals).

National Speaker & Coach
In addition to Branding, Tiffany has a large catalog of various topics upon which she speaks. Her most requested topics tend to be in the area of Personal Improvement for Professional Success. She is based in Los Angeles, yet effective nationwide, traveling nationally to train and talk with entrepreneurs, executives, and business people on various sales, marketing, and other business related subjects. She specializes in working within corporate America, helping motivate management and employees so that they can become more productive for the company with techniques that make them happier and better teammates, employees and managers. She has completed and published the first book in her 10-book series, 10 Branding Strategies: Personal Improvement for Professional Success.
