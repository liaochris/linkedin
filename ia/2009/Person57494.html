David Fraser’s Summary

Dr David Fraser is a businessman and professional engineer by training with considerable international and local experience in telecommunications, mobile, broadcasting and transactional systems. He has founded and currently owns several businesses in the wireless, NGN, cellular (prepaid & services) and media environments. Currently, his key focus as CTO is on the group technology strategies of the public company Blue Label Telecoms.

David has operated businesses with interests in over 15 countries and is currently active in South Africa, Africa, India, Brazil & Europe.

He also enjoyed several years as a university educator and scientific researcher for a high-tech R&D company.

David has authored of over 50 journal, conference and research papers, and holds patents in telecoms/broadcasting technologies.

David Fraser’s Specialties:

Private equity (telecoms), mobile services, transactional services, media (broadcasting), prepaid cellular and TV, next-generation networks, broadband wireless businesses, geo-spatial enterprise solutions for wireless businesses, business development, RF systems, artificial intelligence systems, financial modelling and analysis, marketing and sales.
