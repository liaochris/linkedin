Guido Thys +31612828389 guido@nextnext.nl’s Summary

guido@nextnext.nl
+31612828389

Guido authored a new book on how to be a winner during and after the conomic downturn (in Dutch): WAKE-UP CALL, het gaat slecht dus moeten we weer even normaal doen (www.nextnext.nl/wakeupcall).

As an author and motivational speaker he specialises in creating and increasing customer awareness throughout the organisation. He delivers compelling messages through a unique combination of a profound sense for the absurd, 24 years experience in management, marketing & sales, and deeply-rooted educational skills.

He was born and raised in Antwerp (Belgium) in 1956. He studied Theoretical Linguistics, Philosophy of Language, Pedagogical Sciences, Psycholinguistics and Neurolinguistics.
At the beginning of his "commercial" career he headed the sales promotion and marketing departments of two newspaper publishers. In 1984 he founded his own below-the-line service agency, and subsequently the first telemarketing agency in Belgium, serving about every Flemish publisher and a multitude of Belgian and international customers.

In 1991 he became a senior consultant operating internationally and specialising in the implementation of commercial management instruments. One of his projects received a $ 4,5 million grant from the European Community.
As a Lecturer in Strategic Marketing Management at the Business School Nederland, he has taught over 400 managers.

As a professional speaker he has delivered more than 1.800 keynote presentations, workshops and seminars in 1991-2009, for more than 182.000 participants in 4 languages and in 13 countries. He has performed over 160 times in the past 12 months. In the past ten years he has published over 40 articles and (co-authored) 12 books, mainly in Dutch.
