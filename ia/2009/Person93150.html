Himanshu Dehra’s Summary

Dr. Himanshu Dehra conducts research in the field of 'Stresses and Oscillations' with operation areas of heat, fluid, electricity and HVAC. Dr. Dehra has participated in many national and international symposiums to present break-through research results of his Ph.D. thesis work (2001-2004) completed from Concordia University, Montréal, Canada. He has diversified experience in the field, research and teaching and has completed various projects related to acoustics, solar energy systems, renewable energy, waste-to-energy, energy auditing, biomass energy, solar thermosyphon, combined heat and power, building integrated photovoltaics, airflow window and solar power systems. He is a founding member of American Institute-Industry Forum on Energy (AIFE), a scientific society, for organising transactions on ‘Advances in Energy Research’. The research involves complementary numerical and practical observations to support the research hypothesis. The research topics cover problems related to Building Acoustics, Electrical Power Systems, Building Envelope, Solar Energy and Heat and Mass Transfer.
