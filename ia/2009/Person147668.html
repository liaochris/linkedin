Manish Dua’s Summary

Manish Dua has held creative leadership positions in advertising, integrated marketing and interactive agencies. At Ruch Degree, Manish leads a dynamic team in Los Angeles where he also plays Creative Director Role with experience in delivering marketing communication in both the traditional and digital space.

Manish is results-producing Director with innovative partnerships and a proven record of accomplishments. He is a team leader, manager, creative thinker and architect for rich media online advertising, internet applications, and digital marketing. Combining his background and passion for technology with his love of great design and creative thinking, he enjoys helping to conceive and lead the implementation of innovative ways of using technology for creative digital experiences. He also likes to play with data relationships, web services, social networks, Flash, video, mobile, graphics, great design and user experience, rich internet applications and other “Web 2.0” technologies.

He has conceived and implemented online strategies and interactive visual concepts for clients including MTV, Microsoft XBOX, Disney Online, Sprite & Discovery Channel.

Leadership, Strategy, Online Advertising, Rich Media, Business development, Consulting, Emerging Media, Create & Implement Unique Partnerships, Management,New Media, Marketing, Branding, Identity & Interactive Solutions.

Expert in Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop. Skilled in Flash, Fireworks, DreamWeaver, and Javascript coding. Knowledgeable about XML, CSS, AJAX, DHTML and other current technologies.
