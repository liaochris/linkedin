Michele Colonna’s Summary

Dynamic, strategic, innovative Brand Marketing Executive with extensive domestic and international experience in the luxury goods and premium sportswear industry, managing brand strategy, visual merchandising, retail marketing and brand communication. Effective team leader with exceptional people skills and proven success in building highly inspired and productive teams. Keen talent for developing new business and demonstrated ability to maximizing brand’s asset portfolio. Thrive in fast-paced environment. Fluent in English, Italian and Spanish.
