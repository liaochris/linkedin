Courtney Nichols’s Summary

First generation college graduate arts journalism writer. Background includes semester long internships at "The New York Post," "Drumhead Magazine" and various record labels and production companies, as well as three years as a staff writer and senior editor for The New School newspaper, "The New School Free Press." School awards include member of The National Honor Roll, National Society of High School Scholars, as well as Deans List recipient for all three years of undergraduate studies. Strong three-year background in journalism field, obtaining media credentials for various New York City events as well as insider access to coveted interviews and press screenings. Persistent and hardworking, as college degree was acquired in only three years. Love of journalism has no bounds.
