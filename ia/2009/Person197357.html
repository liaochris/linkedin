Richard Abbot’s Summary

I have been in the sign business for 30 years working with high profile
clients in special events and company-wide sign programs, as well as local
commercial and small business applications. We have become a Green
Certified company this year, adding eco signs to our sign solution programs.
My goals regarding signs and especially eco signs is to be part of
industrial permaculture - localized industry that mimics nature regarding
materials so that everything produced is either a "good" for use by the
customer, or a waste stream that is "food" for another manufacturing
"plant." I would love to be part of a future where industrial plants are
literally "plants."

Richard Abbot’s Specialties:

My company utilizes the most Green processes and materials available in our sign manufacturing. Green is a direction and a work in progress. We welcome and hope to drive innovation in this unfolding process of converting our society to a Green and sustainable lifestyle. We hope you share this with us. Please call with questions and special needs.
