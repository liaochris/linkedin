Ronald Hansen’s Summary

WOT Marketing works with individual drivers, teams, and sponsors in all areas of professional racing. Individual driver and team services include branding, career development, daily logistics, marketing, event planning and execution, sponsor solicitation, contract negotiation, budgeting and accounting, and PR.

Companies can work directly with us to sponsor and manage multiple teams and drivers through one central contact. We have marketing and hospitality packages available for any budget and can work with companies of any size to develope a comprehensive sponsorship package that will exceed expectations. Sponsors find motorsports to be a more exciting and unique way of generating new leads and developing existing ones, while spending less than traditional outlets.

We have clients in ALMS, Grand American, AMA, MotoST, WERA, Skip Barber, BMW Formula and more. We have extensive contacts with manufacturers, series promoters, teams and drivers coaches and can put together the most effective racing plan to meet your specific goals.
