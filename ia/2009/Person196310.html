Renee Swank’s Summary

Renee was elected Vice President - Professional Services in May 2003 after serving as Director of Professional Services since 1998. She was a Senior Consultant with Isogen International between 1996 and 1998. Prior to that, she served as an SGML specialist for Ericsson where she supported mutliple technical publications groups distributed worldwide.

Renee has over 16 years of experience in business process re-engineering, outsourcing of editorial services, and the implementation of standards-based content management systems for large corporations.

She has also successfully built an offshore IT services practice in India and an offshore Technical Writing practice in the Philippines.
