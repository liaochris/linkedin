Anurag Trehan’s Summary

VALUE ADDITION

Clarks Amer Jaipur & Taj Residency Nashik were the start points of my career in Hospitality. I started out with the sole aim of working hard to understand nuances of Food & Beverage operations in a Hotel & get to work in as many Food & Beverage outlets as possible.

My next stop was McDonalds, as an Assistant Manager the exposure I got about operational systems & processes required to build a successful Brand were phenomenal. It helped train my mind to think with discipline & process orientation which is most required to achieve results.

Pizza Hut, After joining as a RGM I went on to become the Manager Ops West India in a period of five years. Mid way in my journey I was recognized for my efforts & rewarded with the Restaurant Excellence Award by YRI for running great restaurants. Under my Supervision my Restaurants have won all awards instituted by YRI for excellence in Customer Service.

VALUE CREATION

In the past Three years I have contributed towards developing various Food & Beverage Retail business models with a focus on a unique Brand value proposition, standardization, scalability & profitability. During this period the projects that I have successfully completed include 6 months with Amore to set up Gelato retail, 5 months with Capital foods to create a front end retail concept for their existing Manufacturing back end set up, 11 months with Chateau Indage to develop a Wine Café & Bistro concept & currently evolving The French Loaf

The work undertaken in this process of value creation included creating a Business Plan & Guidelines for – Store Locationing strategy, Store look & feel, Branding & Marketing, Equipment specifications, Product lines, People profile, Hiring, training & development, Inventory Management, a typical store P&L & Business excellence tools & audits.

My goal is to “To become an icon for Excellence in Business Operations through Discipline, Execution, Leadership, Training & Building people capability”
