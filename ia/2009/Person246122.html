Ross Allchorn’s Summary

I have been involved in website design, development and marketing since 1999 and currently offer consulting services from strategy through to deployment.

My web services have been utilised by companies like Wireframe Studios for clients such as The Famous Grouse, Southern Comfort, Volkswagen and I have also successfully negotiated and closed deals with companies like the Dulce Milkwood franchise, Data Detect, Securis Investments and many others.

To complement my services, I have taken to photography as a medium in which I consider myself very proficient. I have successfully been contracted by clients like O'Mage to shoot events for the likes of Mercedes Benz & General Motors.

Also proudly on my portfolio of photography is the direct appointment to the likes of BMW South Africa for product launches (M3 and X6) and events for Porsche, food photography for the likes of Ocean Basket, Dulce Cafe, News Cafe, Lemon Butta and McCain Foods.

If you want the perfect website, or the perfect picture, please feel free to contact me.
