What do I enjoy about my career?
This may seem cliché but I like working with people. I get to connect with a lot of people and value the many professional relationships I've developed over the years. I love to listen to what someone wants and then look for avenues to fill that desire. I like sharing the secrets of my success and watching others succeed using and growing ideas I’ve planted. I also love to learn from others and implement and expand on their ideas.

Over the past nine years I’ve had the privilege and pleasure of helping a lot of people find new jobs. There’s nothing I like better than when someone shares with me how their new position has improved their quality of life.

I am looking to leverage my contacts on LinkedIn to provide compelling opportunities to software development professionals in Kansas City, assist job seekers in securing employment and partner with other recruiting professionals to assist them in their efforts to help people find great jobs.
