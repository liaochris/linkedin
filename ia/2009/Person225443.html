Carmine Tutera’s Summary

Street Wise Investments, Inc. is a commercial real estate brokerage company that specializes in finding conservative real estate investments for high net worth investors. Our offices are in Florida, but because of our extensive network of participating brokers, we can can work deals anywhere in the United States.

Carmine Tutera, President of Street Wise Investments, has been a Florida real estate Licensee for over 17 years. Carmine brings a wealth of experience in both NNN investments and land development. "How can you truly understand an investment’s potential until you have been an actual owner or partner in that type of investment", says Tutera. Carmine Tutera has been involved in NNN investments of his own, including nationally known brands like Pizza Hut and Steak and Shake.

Carmine Tutera’s Specialties:

Carmine Tutera is also experienced in land acquisition and development. Developing several parcels for Multi-Family and commercial radio tower sites in Florida. "One of the biggest factors in Florida Land Development is wetlands and mitigation. You really have to make sure you have the right team working with you if you want to be successful in achieving your goals and avoiding any potential obstacles."
