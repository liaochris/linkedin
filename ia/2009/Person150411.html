Marie Smith’s Summary

I design publications, logos, brochures, books, sales material, advertising, invitations, product packaging, signage, and websites. I collaborate with a wide range of creative professionals to deliver high quality writing and editing, photography, video, SEO and custom content management systems for tailor made websites at reasonable rates.

I am engaged in all aspects of my business, from promoting and networking, to soliciting and scouting new prospects, conducting portfolio presentations, writing project proposals and budgets, estimating costs, conducting research, formulating document specifications and requesting print estimates, prepress production, coordinating with other professionals, working with the general public, and project management from inception to finished product.

Marie Smith’s Specialties:

Publication design, redesign and production, logo design, business identity products including brochures, business cards and web design; copy writing and proofreading; project management and art direction.
