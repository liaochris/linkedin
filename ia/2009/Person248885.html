  • Project Manager / Sales

    (Privately Held; Music industry)

Beatport launched with only 75 labels In January 2004 (now over 8000 independent labels.)

    -Project Management: assisted with designing the functionality of the first Beatport online retail store.

    -Legal Negotiations: As the company was small, we saved alot of money by negotiating our own contracts with independent labels. I assisted in the creation of our first music contracts with labels. Later spending time creating training documentation to help other salespeople to learn to negotiate the contract.

    -Content Management: The first versions of our content ingestion system were very manual and everyone in the company would help to ingest content that came in the door for delivery to our customer base.
