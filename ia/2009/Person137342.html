La Tonya Walker’s Summary

My name is La Tonya Walker, and I am a Division Director with Robert Half International as well as a self-proclaimed full-time networker among accounting and finance professionals. I have over 15 years of progressive accounting and finance expertise.

I began my career in Accounting and Finance with Arthur Andersen LLP. I have also held positions with the City and County of Denver and with Texas Instruments. I am experienced as an Audit Associate, Director of Finance, and Assistant Controller. After relocating for my husband’s career, we decided to focus on starting a family, and I sought a position that would not require travel.
I was presented with the opportunity to join one of the country's largest accounting and staffing firms, thus enabling me to take my high level accounting/finance experience coupled with my passion for helping people into the realm of Executive Recruiting.

During my tenure, I successfully transitioned into the #5 Recruiter in the U.S. for a global recruitment firm with over 350 recruiters nationwide, so my rapid achievements have been widely recognized.

I have developed an extensive clientele and I have placed high level Big 4 CPA’s and high level and mid-management accounting and financial professionals.
My client list consists of Public Accounting firms, Fortune 100, Fortune 500 and an extensive list of fiscally sound privately held corporations.

Are you confidentially looking for a new opportunity, closer to home? Looking for a position that will take your career to the next level or just want to stay in touch with the market, changing industries, salary increases. Having an experienced accounting and finance recruiter in your back pocket is always an EXCELLENT idea. Call me confidentially to discuss current positions in the DC metro Area.
