Okie Whiteru’s Summary

Okie C. Whiteru, Esq. has five years of experience in the area of civil litigation. This includes conducting trials before juries, obtaining favorable verdicts and/or settlements in personal injury cases, obtaining divorces on behalf of his clients and litigating contract cases before judges.

Okie C. Whiteru, Esq. received litigation training directly from judges in the District of Columbia while obtaining a Master of Laws Degree from The George Washington University Law School. Further, Mr. Whiteru graduated from the University of Miami School of Law with honors.

Okie Whiteru’s Specialties:

Personal Injury, Divorce, Child Custody, Child Support, Immigration, Debt Collection, Contract Disputes & Business Formation.
