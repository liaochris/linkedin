John Runningen’s Summary

John R. Runningen is an innovative financial advisor with proven results. He has extensive investment banking experience and access to Private Equity Groups nationwide. He is known for his ability to facilitate innovative transactions that attract capital, generate new sources of revenue, cash flow, and value.

His track record includes 80 separate investment banking and M&A transactions for 44 companies, with aggregate value of more than $18 billion.

He has served on 12 corporate boards where he was a key strategic and financial advisor to the senior management of these firms.
