Chris Barnhill’s Summary

Chris Barnhill is an Account Manager for the Mobile Device Accessories Market at TESSCO Technologies Inc. Chris comes to the sales side of the organization with 2 years experience in product sourcing and new product development with TESSCO’s development team; he has since made a successful transition into sales, selling the product that he spent 2 years building and bringing to market.

Chris’s background in product development, and his different way of looking at problems has positioned him in a unique position to substantial improve retailer through put and gross profit, while at the same time allowing retailers to keep more money in the bank by cutting down on inventory.

Chris has also enabled many small retailers to succeed in this down economy with proven sales strategies that most small retailers are not exposed to; these include in store sales dialog, “guerilla marketing” tactics, as well as (and most importantly) allowing hi0073 customers to “outsource” there inventory to TESSCO. This unique combination allows customers to “Break Through” the doldrums in the economy and steal market share from their competition, both locally and on the internet.

Chris Barnhill’s focus on personal and professional effectiveness, combined with product knowledge and the complete offering of TESSCO, truly makes this combination Your Total Source ® for all of your wireless communications needs as one of his customers puts it “Having Chris on your team allows you to do the right things at the right time to get the right result.”
