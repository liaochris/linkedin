Cristina Walters’s Summary

A marketing professional and client coach with more than 24 years experience, Cristina credits her forward-thinking team with the success of C&M Communiqué. Forming C&M Communiqué in 1992, Cristina strategically grew her fully-integrated firm through initiative and a focus on the competitive differentiation that benefit clients in both the profit driven and nonprofit sectors.

Earning a Bachelor of Arts degree in English from UCLA, Cristina firmly believes in the power of the written word and the advantages of her agency’s persuasive writing, complemented by new/digital media and creative expertise.
