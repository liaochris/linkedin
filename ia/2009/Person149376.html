Marc Melucas’s Summary

I always strive to increase process performance, and apply my years of progressive experience in manufacturing, quality, engineering development, test, sales, and operations. I have had the good fortune to hold significant and diverse positions in the US Air Force, General Motors, Delphi, and Eaton. Other experience includes developing business relationships for the sales of custom designed and built HVAC systems and machine tools/assembly lines.

My most recent position at Eaton involved quality management for the manufacturing of electrical vacuum interrupters, which act as switches in electro-mechanical circuit breakers. I led the Eaton Horseheads, NY facility through its ISO 9000 Triennial Assessment with outstanding results.

I am currently searching for new professional opportunites to apply my experience and knowledge, as well as learn more and expand my abilities.
