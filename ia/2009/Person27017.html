Bernardo Sanchez’s Summary

I have had a wide and successful experience in the telecom industry, particularly in the field of Content Management Systems, Network Management Systems, Switching and Storage Area Network for Telecom Service & Technology providers.

I provide remote or onsite support executing all phases of project delivery, including software & network design, staging, implementation, handover, migration, and troubleshooting, leading teams that involve contractors & workers, and customer relation management.

I have been Web Project Director for the last four years.

Multidisciplinary 20+ years work in Project Management for software & telecommunication multinational companies such as IDC, NOKIA MSI, BT LATAM, AMERICATEL, CXR TELCOM and COLSEGUROS, and participation in international projects conducted both in English & Spanish, spanning Latin America and USA, which have included international business travel.

Bernardo Sanchez’s Specialties:

In addition to my Project Management expertise:

I install and configure Data Center infrastructure: Servers, Operating Systems, and Frameworks. Experience in Sun Microsystems J2EE, Microsoft .NET, LAMP. Also NOKIA GSM Data Center Architecture: NCC, AMD and SAFIR. Training in Nokia Dallas.

I perform analysis and diagnosis of highly complex networking problems. Knowledge in depth of Communication protocols and Network Management Systems. Experience in: TCP/IP & Frame Relay and SNMP.
