Mark Seely’s Summary

Mark Seely BS, MBA, is President of Mobile Systems Consulting, a 3G / 4G training and consulting service provider for teams responsible for the design, marketing, deployment, and optimization of CDMA / OFDMA networks, products and services. Mr. Seely has over 15 years of wireless industry experience and is a recognized expert in CDMA. He is a post-baccalaureate Professor for the University of California San Diego (UCSD) where he teaches in the CDMA Specialization Certificate Program. Mark is a certified Master Training Specialist, and an award winning instructor, who has developed and delivered IS-95, cdma2000, 1xEV-DO & UMTS /LTE technology and deployment based courses to an international customer list of nearly 100 companies.

Prior to founding Mobile Systems, he headed the 3G standards and engineering training group for Ericsson's Academy of Business and Technology. Preceding Ericsson, Mr. Seely served as a co-founder and Staff Instructor/Consultant for QUALCOMM's CDMA University. Before joining QUALCOMM, Mr. Seely co-founded 21st Century Wireless Group Inc. where as an executive director and corporate board member he was responsible for strategic planning, technology deployment, acquisitions, and corporate policy.
