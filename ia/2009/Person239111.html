Tyler Bell’s Summary

Tyler leads the Geo Technologies product team at Yahoo! and creates the strategy and roadmap for the company's Mapping, User Location, and Geoinformatic platforms. He most recently launched (May 2009) Yahoo! Placemaker (http://developer.yahoo.com/geo/placemaker), a geo-enrichment platform designed to make the Internet more location-aware.

A former archaeologist specializing in landscape analysis and the semantic integration of heterogeneous data systems, Tyler gained his doctorate at the University of Oxford before founding and managing a technology spin-out company from the University in 2001. In 2007 he returned to the US and joined Yahoo!'s Geo Targeting team.

Tyler's domain focus is on the broad range of spatial technologies that underlie and enrich the Internet, while his product expertise centers upon developing pragmatic product and monetization strategies that connect users with the world around them.

Previous responsibilities have included coding and technical management, system architecting and design, sales, business development and executive leadership.
