Christopher Roberts’s Summary

MIT educated and trained at Global Consulting firms, I have 15 years of experience delivering tough to execute projects. Most of my experience is the result of either post merger integration and/or large busines transformation projects. I enjoy both the business development and leading the subsequent work of business process re-engineering (BPR) and systems design and implementation. Past projects have involved work in Europe, Japan, China, and the Middle East where I have learned to speak fluent Mandarin Chinese and some basic Arabic and Japanese.
