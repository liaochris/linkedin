Ian Cleary’s Summary

Ian Cleary is a driven and experienced business professional, working in the Internet and telecommunications communities, he is also a lively, popular and engaging public speaker.

With over twelve years experience and a proven track record in business development, product management and technical marketing, Ian has demonstrable ability to add value; driving P&L performance improvements and raising the standard of product lifecycle management to an optimal standard.

"...there is no black magic in business, developing a rigorous and clear set of targets, equipping oneself with the right tools and remaining honest, meticulous and focused throughout the measurement process is key...".

In January 2009 Ian founded Magician, a provider of IT services designed to help business people exploit the full power of the Internet. Magician products and services are perfectly timed to meet the challenges of the current global economic climate.
