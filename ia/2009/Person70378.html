Eduardo Almeida’s Summary

As a Public Adjuster and owner of American Claim Adjusters I do exactly what I enjoy: help people "fix" their problems. My opinion is that if you pay for insurance benefits, then when you need those benefits, you should get those benefits, not a sob story or excuse why it's not enough. I make sure you get the maximum benefits you are entitled to when you have an insurance claim.

As an IT Consultant and Systems Integrator, I help small and medium sized businesses set up their computer networks, desktops, servers, and communications. We also provide full time support and 24/7 emergency on-site service. As with my other business, we make sure that your systems are running and operating at peak performance.
