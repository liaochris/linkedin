Art Stiefel’s Summary

As Chief Creative Officer I lead EngineRoom Brand Communications in the development of proprietary Brand Conversations™ for our clients and then execute them across all relevant communication platforms.

I am the lead brand strategist and I dive head first into the development of all brand identity, brand analysis, brand marks, logo marks, brand communications, brand innovation, brand ideation, consumer branding group implementation and facilitation, online branding campaign development as well as integrated branding platforms and campaigns.

Art Stiefel’s Specialties:

Brand Consultancy and Creative Marketing Group specializing in brand strategy development, brand positioning, brand identity, brand marks, brand continuity, brand communications, brand analysis, brand ideation and brand innovation. Develop proprietary Brand Conversations™ for our clients and then execute them across all relevant communication platforms.
