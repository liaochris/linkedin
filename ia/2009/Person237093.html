Dilip Saraf’s Summary

I am not just an everyday leadership/career coach, but one who shows how to pursue a joyful life AND put you on a purposeful track. This is my fifth career, so no trial and error for YOU, having worked with 4,000 clients globally.

I employ a preemptive approach to help pursue your dream. I help you discover yourself!

This discovery is rooted in uncovering your archetype. Archetypes are primordial forces, hidden within your unconscious, which normally lie dormant and silent, but which can suddenly irrupt into the conscious mind and produce amazing results. The catalyst for this is your own genius. I help uncover your inner genius through a process that will transform your message from what you have done to who YOU are, revealing your soul. This is magical! Genius-based verbal branding is highly differentiated.

If you are working, I'll coach to show you the difference it makes from just DOING a job to BEING in a job!

Mid-career professionals (35-45) with fresh MBAs often feel lost in how to move ahead. I'll show you how to leverage your new degree!

If you're looking I'll show you how to transform your message with a new concept called an inductive resume to get quick traction. If you are a senior executive, I specialize in their re-engagement. I'll also help you discover your true leadership force.

Most career coaches will help you target your message to a recruiter; I will show you how to message to get the hiring manager's boss' attention!

For students and fresh graduates missteps can be costly. I have helped many choose career tracks and curricula that excited them.

If you have lost your abilities to dream, I'll re-energize them. It takes the same effort to dream big as it does just dreaming, and when you know what you are doing the dreams pursue YOU! Remember, "pursue" also means to attract!

My five books are on Amazon.com and B&N.com
