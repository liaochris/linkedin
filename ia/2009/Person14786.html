Andrew Steele’s Summary

I am both personally and professionally passionate about consumer technology and digital media, and have focused my career on building and leading businesses focused squarely on the intersection of those sectors. Starting with an academic background in both engineering and business disciplines, I have since built a successful track record in business development, strategy, marketing and finance roles, and in both domestic and worldwide markets.

Most recently, I have built a consultancy focused on delivering virtual business development and strategy leadership and hands-on dealmaking, developing business and operational plans, and raising angel and institutional venture financing for my clients in the consumer web, mobile and digital media sectors.
