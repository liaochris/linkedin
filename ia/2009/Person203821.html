Rory Paterson’s Summary

Working in the digital media and marketing industry for the past 7 years I've built up an expert knowledge in an array of digital disciplines.

Starting out at BigMouthMedia I was fortunate enough to work on organic SEO strategies for some great clients, including BA, PlayStation and the Early Learning Centre.

Post BigMouth I went creative side. As well as managing build projects I concentrated heavily on the evolving world of rich media platforms and adserving, promoting their capabilities and use to both digital and traditional creative teams.

With a very keen interest in accountability and results I welcomed the challenge to join MediaCom Edinburgh in 2005. The role was to develop a digital media offering, build a team and integrate with the holistic media strategy, planning and buying offering of the agency. 3 years on and proud to say that we are now a multi award winning digital media department with an enviable client list including RBS, Subway Sandwiches, The Scottish Government, Little Tikes and VisitScotland. We've got all major verticals covered and have proven expertise in online Brand, Brand Response and DR. Billings have grown 300% yoy with income and head count increasing in line with our success. We continue to deliver truly integrated media solutions to our clients and have delivered media firsts such as Scotland only Xbox In-Game ads for the Scottish Government, bespoke day-part targeting for Subway and custom regional re-targeting strategies for our major DR clients. Take a look at www.mediacomedinburgh.com
