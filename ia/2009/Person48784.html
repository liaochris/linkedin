Suzanne Tucker’s Summary

SUZANNE TUCKER, PT, C.I.M.I.
Vice President and Co-Owner

PT, and Certified Infant Massage Instructor, Suzane has practiced as a physical therapist since 1992 and is the co-owner of Brentwood Center of Health with her husband Shawn. With a manual therapy approach and a focus on empowering individuals with tools for health and healing,
the vision of Brentwood Center of Health is "inspiring health" by offering personal care and a holistic team approach to rehabilitation and wellness.

Suzanne teaches group and private classes on Infant Massage at Brentwood Center of Health. As a guest lecturer for Parents as Teacher's National Conference, Illinois' First Steps Program, The Healing Art Center of St. Louis, Children's Hope International and other parent/child organizations, Suzanne strives to teach about the many benefits of loving touch for all children in a way that depends the bonds between parents and their child/ren. She is a Co-Founder of the Yogi Parenting approach to positive parenting and teaches monthly classes with her co-founder Michaela Turner at BCOH in St. Louis Missouri. Yogi Parenting will be released Mother's Day, 2009 as an eBook for parents as teachers alike. She has also been a Prenatal Massage and Infant Massage course instructor for The Healing Arts Center, a massage therapy school in St. Louis, MO and a guest speaker for the National Parents as Teachers Conferences three years running.

Suzanne Tucker’s Specialties:

Suzanne is passionate about teaching loving touch to various child advocate organizations including foster and adoption agencies and their families. Her vision is to inspire connection...to ourselves, each other and our children. She writes about this connection as Zen Mommy on www.mymommymanual.com.

    Author

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    October 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    My story is on the life changing gift I received in the darkest hour of my life, after experiencing our fifth repeat miscarriage. The book is called 'LET IT BE: acceptance in the midst of miscarriage'.

    It is not only my story but a guide to help any woman grieving the circumstances of her life including miscarriage, infant loss and infertility; to support readers in finding the great gift that is possible through all their pain, to bring them into a closer and intimate relationship with their God, and finally to help them live again, more fully alive than ever before. We are NOT the circumstances of our life...we are life, and we are love. We can love this day.
