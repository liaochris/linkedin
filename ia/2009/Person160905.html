Michael Brodzinski’s Summary

Michael is a highly skilled, versatile and experienced professional with a background in sales, marketing, underwriting, account management, strategic initiatives, innovation, and cross-functional responsibilities. He is experienced in leading teams and working with senior executives in the home office of Zurich North America, a $12B organization. Mike was a dedicated design member of a Zurich Financial Services top global initiative charged with transforming an intermediated sales culture with an implied premium growth of $1.6B. He has solid analytical skills with exceptional abilities in leadership, communications, and strategic use of relationships. Mike is currently employed as a Account Executive in Zurich Construction's Specialty Products, with sales, marketing and underwriting responsibilities.

His diverse experiences have taught Mike a great deal about business, working with others, negotiations, and conflict resolution. Every work experience listed in his bio has had a very steep learning curve. Mike has taken each challenge head-on, and has excelled by his work ethic and ability to comprehend ideas quickly. His ability to simplify and articulate solutions to complex problems has proved to be one of Mike's strengths. He has worked in both teams and as a technical consultant on major initiatives that span the scope of a very complicated organization.

His future goals involve his inherent entreuprenurial spirit and his desire to help others acheive success. He also hopes to continue to dedicate personal time and efforts to assisting others less fortunate.
