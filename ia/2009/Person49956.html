Craig Smith’s Summary

I am the owner and Principal Consultant for Flint Consulting. We specialise in helping clients to successfully implement transformational change. Research shows that 60-70% of organisational change initiatives fail.

Implementing change is more than just project management - change involves human beings and therefore requires attention to issues such as legitimate resistance, relationships, culture, values and behaviours to name but a few. Our methodology and expertise will help us to help you to develop a change strategy that attends all of these elements.

My personal skill set and experience includes:

- Extensive communication, facilitation and programme design / delivery skills and experience.

- Six years of multi-national experience in organisational capability development and employee engagement initiatives with PepsiCo International.

- Twelve years management experience in Blue Chip organisations.

- MBA with Distinction from an AMBA accredited university.


I am also a facilitator for Meeting Magic Limited who specialise in designing and facilitating high quality meetings, conferences and workshops for clients who need to get real results with maximum involvement from attendees (see more at www.meetingmagic.co.uk).
