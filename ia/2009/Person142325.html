Lisa Maria Stice’s Summary

Daytona native and resident Lisa Maria Stice has a long association with Noble House Hotels & Resorts for over 12 years. Stice held various sales and marketing positions with Noble House Hotels & Resorts from, including director of sales and marketing, regional director of sales and marketing and corporate director of internet marketing.

As corporate director of e-commerce marketing for Noble House Hotels, Stice is responsible for managing the development of the company’s entire resort web site portfolio in addition to upgrading the business technologies to support Noble House’s CRM strategy. She is also responsible for assisting the properties with all strategic e-marketing initiatives and business plan development.

Stice has received numerous professional awards and recognition, including two prestigious HSMAI Adrian Advertising Awards for website development and over 5 HMS Sales Professional Gold Key Awards.
