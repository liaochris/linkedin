Ellen Siegel, CIPP’s Summary

Certified Information Privacy Professional (CIPP) with 15+ years of diverse experience in designing, architecting, securing, and evangelizing web infrastructure and communication products and solutions. Recognized as an industry leader with broad expertise in email authentication, data privacy and information security technology and regulations, Java based web technology, Agile development, and SAAS. Adept at rapidly absorbing new technologies and regulations and balancing business needs with regulatory imperatives to define critical product and policy updates. Frequent writer and panelist educating in and driving adoption of email authentication and best practices at industry conferences, in industry publications and in online journals.
