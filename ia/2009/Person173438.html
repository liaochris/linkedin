Frank Undall’s Summary

Educated as a journalist at the Danish School of Journalism in Århus and at the daily newspaper Politiken, Frank Undall established his own company Newsline A/S February 1st 1979. Operating as a one-man-company for some years, Newsline A/S originally syndicated articles and features to foreign newspapers and magazines.
1980 Frank Undall became one of the first in Denmark to develop a professional turnkey production of Customer Magazines. Along came 17 years as Head of Information for a variety of information and event organisations working within exhibitions and fairs at Forum Copenhagen, i.e. the Sixdays Races.
1997 Frank Undall developed and implemented strategies for companies like CSC, including suggestions for the sponsoring of Bjarne Riis' cycling team. Also four years of PR management with the bicycling Team fakta.
Tuula and Frank Undall developed, produced and published the Coffee Table Magazine Ladies Golf - the first golfing Magazine aimed towards females in the Nordic countries. 2008 involved organising the Grand Opening of Lübker Golf Resort in Denmark. GOLF Magazine in USA published in its January 1st 2009 issue that Lübker Golf was designated “Best New International Golf Course 2008”.
Newsline A/S also specialises in Crisis Management. Best example is a serious crisis in Sweden involving 81 billion SEK from pension funds and the reputation of the Swedish Riksdagen.
Our client list over the years includes names such as Compaq A/S, Navision, Damgaard Data, SAS Hosting, Scandinavian IT Group, Børsen Golf, Business Traveller, Intermec Technologies, Scandinavian Tobacco Company, Danish Council of Nurses in Copenhagen County, Royal Copenhagen Golf Club, The Sicilian Agriculture & Forest Department, The Association of Danish Shoe Manufacturers, Lübker Golf Resort, Choice Hotels, Scandic Hotels and many more. A trademark is long relationships with the clients and the oldest did go for 29 years…

Frank Undall’s Specialties:

Strategic Consultancy including all aspects of Public Relations and Communications – focusing specially in areas such as Travel, Golf, Wine and Dine.
Event coordination in all areas from wine tasting to golf tournaments.
Digital photography and all types of graphic design and production.
Internet analyses and website optimization.
Turnkey projects for external customer magazines, internal employee magazines, newspapers, brochures and other printed matter.
