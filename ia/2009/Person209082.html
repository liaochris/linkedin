Sanjeev Gadre’s Summary

My professional goal remains simple - to add visible and measurable value to the business by developing and executing an innovative and effective marketing strategy.

Five key professional value-adds I bring to table:
1. Ability to attract, mentor and retain top class talent drawn from different industries and different cultures.
2. Proven record of getting things done - projects, programs, targets.
3. Significant knowledge and experience in B2B marketing - both enterprise and desktop.
4. Proven record of executing successful global marketing campaigns.
5. Above average IQ.

I have 15 years of experience in the marketing of software products around the globe. Prior to Adobe, I worked for Subex, Hughes Software Systems (now Aricent), Verifone India and Wipro in various marketing, business development and program management roles. I hold a MBA from IIM Ahmedabad and an engineering degree from IIT Mumbai.

Sanjeev Gadre’s Specialties:

B2B software marketing, enterprise software marketing, desktop software marketing, branding and identity, mentoring, talent management, international marketing experience, getting things done

Sanjeev Gadre’s Experience

  • Director - Marketing

    Adobe Systems

    (Public Company; ADBE; Computer Software industry)

    May 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    I have always been part of a software product marketing organization that has had a global remit while being centered in India. This unique experience brought me to Adobe.

    The Print and Publishing Business Unit (PPBU) at Adobe is the only BU that is truly centered outside United States – not just engineering but also executive management, product management, etc. Under my leadership, the PPBU product marketing team was successfully transitioned from the US to India. In transitioning the product marketing function to India, I have also successfully transitioned a number of support functions such as creative agency support, inside sales, etc. to India

    As the director of marketing for the US$ 200M~ BU, I am responsible for a number of products that address market opportunities ranging from the diverse publishing needs of technical communicators and e-learning professionals, to Adobe’s legacy type and OEM printing businesses.

    After over 14 years as an enterprise software marketer, I am truly enjoying my time with Adobe as I get to use my skills and experience to address the marketing challenges in a new paradigm – desktop software.

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Computer Software industry)

    September 2003 — April 2008 (4 years 8 months)

    The journey with Subex has been exhilarating. I have had the opportunity to participate in the phenomenal growth of the company as it has gone from ~ $5M in revenue to ~ $150M. These years at Subex have seen me bring to bear all my creativity, ideas, knowledge and experience as my team executes an integrated & multi modal marketing program.

    It is satisfying to help build a brand that is an acknowledged leader of its segment and considered amongst the future leaders of the fast maturing telecommunication OSS segment.

    Key learning at Subex comes from the company's aggressive M&A strategy. As part of the executive management team I get involved in a M&A transaction early on and also have an important role to play in ensuring smooth integration of people resources and processes.
