Jack Warnock’s Summary

Jack Warnock takes care of business leaders and financial professionals and executives by pushing them to act for their own benefit, drive higher revenues, generate greater profitability and achieve success. Also, through working together, trust is restored - in themselves, their teams, their customers, their vendors and their investors.

During his more than 30 years of business experience, he was the Office Managing Partner for Tatum LLC in Philadelphia, Chief Financial Officer for FTP Software, Thinking Machines, and Towers Perrin’s Administration Solutions Group. He was Managing Director- Mergers & Acquisitions at Bell Atlantic (now Verizon) and an investment banker principally with W.H. Newbold’s. He was a co-founder of Bridge Tech Partners L.P., a woman and minority-focused venture capital fund sponsored by Safeguard Scientifics. He was an executive search consultant at Wellington Management Group, and began his career as a CPA at Arthur Andersen.

He graduated from Villanova University with a Bachelor of Science degree - Cum Laude in Accounting. He has extensive credentials gained from hard work over the years on leadership, coaching, management, finance and technology, among others. His most recent volunteer work includes the Boy Scouts as troop committee chair, the World Affairs Council as a board member, and as a member of the Greater Philadelphia Chamber of Commerce, the Philadelphia Human Resource Planning Society, the International Coach Federation and the Mid-Atlantic Capital Alliance.

Jack Warnock’s Specialties:

Business Development, Governance and Advisory, Capital Markets and Investments, Mergers, Acquisitions & Joint Ventures, Individual Coaching, Role Assessments & Alignment, Succession Planning, Leadership Development, and Management Team Due Diligence & Integration
