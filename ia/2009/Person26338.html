Benjamin Whitaker’s Experience

  • Internet Strategy consultant

    Nokia Siemens Networks

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; NOK; Telecommunications industry)

    April 2008 — July 2008 (4 months)

    Most recent project:

    Revamping Nokia-Siemens Networks Indonesia's internet access strategy.

    3-week project yielded 450% jump in available bandwidth at 35% incremental cost.

    Additional bandwidth was brought online at a cost 13 cheaper for raw bandwidth numbers. New bandwidth was 47 times cheaper if private-line transport to remote offices is taken into account.

    Project also offloaded about 40% of their core network traffic. Under the new strategy, we dump internet traffic directly to the internet via multiple redundant DSL connections at remote locations. This offloads the core network of private lines and enables capacity cuts for additional savings.

    This "direct dump" strategy enabled speed increases at remote offices of anywhere from 16 to 140 times faster. Latency typically dropped by 40% as well.

    Great people at NSN, extremely hardworking and very sharp staff. A really rewarding project from start to finish.
