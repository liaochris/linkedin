    "new generation agency" adinteractive

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    April 2008 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    As Turkey's first and leading interactive agency founded in 1996, adinteractive provides 360º interactive marketing and communication services covering all online and offline media, specializing on digital marketing services with fully in-house digital production capabilities in 3D, animation, interactive video on web, mobile, IPTV and digital outdoor.

  • Founder

    Istanbul.com

    (Online Media industry)

    January 1996 — Present (14 years 1 month)

    With the urge of longing my beautiful city Istanbul - living away for quite some time - I was lucky to register Istanbul.com domain in 1995 and I personally produced the web site by the end of 1995. At first, the site was generated in English with city content and tourist info targeting international viewers. It was selected “site of the week” by the Miscrosoft Network by the end of 1996 and with not much of an alternative it had thousands of viewers a day. Then in 1998, the Turkish version was formed to serve locals as well. After General Manager Canan Erdogan’s joining to the team and reforming its design concept and content in 2000, Istanbul.com positioned itself as a commercial medium for advertisers targeting both international and local audience.

    In the meantime, Istanbul.com had also become a magnet for first of its kind in the online publishing successful business collaborations such as @istanbul.com e-mail offering via Gmail platform, Google Maps content partnership, travel booking partnerships with Booking.com and Expedia.

    After the huge demand by the advertisers in 2005, "In İstanbul" printed prestige magazine was first published by using Istanbul.com’s base content and more unique content created. It is being distrubuted to each room of Istanbul's most prestigious hotels and major bookstores throughout Turkey.

  • Founder / President & CEO

    adinteractive

    (Partnership; Internet industry)

    February 1996 — March 2008 (12 years 2 months)

    Turkey's first and leading digital agency was formed in Istanbul for creating new technologies for interactive marketing and communication services, providing multi-platform digital marketing services for global and local brands in Turkey.

    At first, main focus was to serve companies that needed global attention such as manufacturers and exporters. In order to create the awareness and the need for the Internet marketing, we had organized many private and sectoriel seminars covering hundreds of professionals.

    The agency gained exceptional success helping these companies achieving their goals and increased its reputation towards major commercial brands. After media’s attention for creating and leading such an innovative new sector successfully, it attracted some of the most valuable brands in Turkey and started to provide online and interactive solutions as these companies’ interactive agency of the record for the first time in their corporate history.
