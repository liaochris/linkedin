Bram Ellens’s Summary

Bram started his professional career at Roland Berger Strategy Consultants after having studied at the University of Technology Delft. After three years in strategy consulting, he joined eBay as a Business Developer at Marktplaats.nl. Rolling out several successful optimizations, Bram joined the eBay Global Classifieds team in San Francisco to broaden his scope. After fixing the basic global monetization issues at headquarters, he started working as a trouble shooter in Taiwan, Milan, Berlin and London.

In 2008 Bram moved to Berlin to become the interim General Manager at Kijiji, the leading German, Austrian and Swiss classifieds platform. In 2009 he moved back to the Netherlands to fulfill his entrepreneurial dream and founded Harvest.
