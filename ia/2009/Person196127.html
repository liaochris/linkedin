Rena Bussinger’s Summary

Rena Bussinger is a Seattle-based Production Designer and Art Director. Her film credits include Art Director on the forthcoming feature DEAR LEMON LIMA, directed by Suzi Yoonessi; Art Director and Associate Producer on TRUE ADOLESCENTS, premiering in the narrative competition at SXSW 2009; and Property Master on the BRAND UPON THE BRAIN!, a silent film and live cinema spectacle directed by Guy Maddin (THE SADDEST MUSIC IN THE WORLD).

Rena has worked on numerous other films and documentaries, including Lynn Shelton's HUMPDAY, three time-lapse animated shorts by David Russo (THE IMMACULATE CONCEPTION OF LITTLE DIZZLE) and as a costume intern on Darren Aronofsky's THE WRESTLER. Rena formerly served as the web manager for the PBS documentary series ADVENTURE DIVAS, on which she also worked as a production coordinator.

A graduate of the University of Washington with a degree in English, Rena's studies have encompassed multiple fine arts including drawing, graphic design, web design, film and photography.

Additional experience in Location Scouting, Graphic/Web Design, and Photography.
