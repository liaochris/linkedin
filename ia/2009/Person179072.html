Gina Woods’s Summary

Open Road Radio (ORR) on 9 FM: 92.5, 92.7 & 99.9 Illinois, Wisconsin, and Indiana-Sunday 11a-12 pm
ORR has been on the air for over 12 years. All of the hosts are people who ride motorcycles, or are involved with the motorcycle industry, with a combined experience of over 120 years! ORR is aimed at people who are passionate, adventurous and love living the good life.
ORR is helping to make the public more aware of the men and women from all walks of life who ride motorcycles. The rapid growth of riders, young and old, and women motorcyclists! The wonderful tours that help us learn about the world around us, products, services and real human interest stories. ORR encourages education of both new and experienced riders and people that just like the “idea” of riding.
ORR provides information about safety programs that help people learn to ride, or improve existing skills. We have lively discussions with callers about riding issues, helmet laws, motorcycle maintenance, pending legislation, motorcycle insurance and many things that apply to the daily average joe or affluent new age motorcyclist.
The purpose is to bring our audience a radio program that focuses on motorcycling and the motorcycle industry, using a combination of information, education and entertainment.
We take the time to get to know are listeners with surveys and direct interaction. This furthers the goal of truly serving our audience. We encourage our listeners to participate in the show with interesting weekly topics, live remotes and bike events. We realize that to provide our audience with timely, useful information, we must interact continuously with the motorcycling community and industry-related entities. This furthers our goal of informing our audience about up-to-date industry-related news from the AMA:American Motorcycle Association, MIC:Motorcycle Industry Council, ABATE:Motorcycle Rights Organization, industry press releases and industry-related web sites.

    Gina Woods' XX Chromes-All Woman Bike Builds

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    2006 — Present (3 years )

    Recognizing that women are the fastest growing segment within the motorcycle industry, more and more retailers are beginning to focus on women as a viable customer. In the U.S. alone, there are over 600,000 women motorcyclists who are drawn to bike riding because of the freedom, camaraderie, and adventure according to a survey completed by Harley-Davidson. Women make up about 12% of sales for Harley-Davidson and other motorcycle manufacturers report similar statistics. To continue to attract the women motorcycle rider, manufacturers are making bikes just for women and women motorcycle clubs are being formed around the country.

    Women riders boast an annual household income of $47,300 compared to men’s household income of $43,800. Fifty-six percent of female riders, versus 42% of male riders, have a college education. Most female riders begin riding in their mid-thirties and nearly 37% buy a brand new motorcycle when beginning.

    The Company
    Placing the emphasis on women in motorcycling, the XX Chromes All-Women Bike Build (AWBB) creates awareness about Empowering Women in Motorcycling!

    The XX Chromes- AWBB event creates an opportunity to showcase the motorcycle expertise of women around the country and raise awareness about our partners, sponsor and charity causes such as breast cancer research, prevention, or any other notable charities.

    The XX Chromes AWBB are sometimes a aprt of the current activities of Open Road Radio –and under the leadership of Gina Woods. Ms. Woods has been highly involved in the motorcycling lifestyle since birth and riding since the late 80’s and has held bike building events in the past & present, has been featured in many national magazine articles, national & local radio & TV.
