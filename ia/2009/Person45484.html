Christopher Tucker’s Summary

Christopher is President and CEO of Tucker & Partners, an international business development consultancy, and its venture consulting business, T&P Ventures.

Tucker & Partners and T&P Ventures have, between them, over twenty senior associates in the UK, EU and USA. Team members either have C-level experience in Fortune 500 companies, or director / VP level experience in investment banks, management consulting firms, or professional services companies.

Prior to founding Tucker & Partners, Christopher had spent most of his career in investment banking, where he specialized in the European telecommunications and technology sectors and held director/VP level roles in investment research or corporate finance for investment banks including Kleinwort Benson, Banque Indosuez, Banque Paribas and WestLB. He has substantial experience of IPOs in the technology and telecommunications sectors in Europe. While at Banque Indosuez he played a leading role in winning the bank’s first UK privatization mandate. While at Banque Paribas he was the lead analyst for the IPO of France Telecom, for which the bank was global coordinator.

Christopher spent his early career in the UK’s electricity industry, initially as a physicist and later as a strategic planner.
