Philippe Gimet’s Summary

{ Senior functions }
Partner and director, head of department, manager, consultant, project manager.

{ Vision }
To capitalize in Culture through its implementation of global strategies in investment. Cultural engineering helps industries to increase projects’ values (short and long term) and maximize profit by improving operational efficiencies and the use of optimal available resources, notably in private-public partnerships.

{ Profile }
My career has led me to specialize increasingly in the management of complex multi-cultural projects and their teams at national and international levels. I have experience in: strategic forward-planning; project management; operating within financial constraints imposed by ambitious budgets which I have managed or helped to plan and negotiate; methodical administration to deadlines (crisis management where necessary); and the application of modern leadership methods (through staff motivation and involvement in both decision-making and target-setting, clarity in communication, and easy personal inter-relations); define and promote investment opportunities for new businesses and markets. My background and analytical interests in other cultures, together with a command of global cultural engineering sectors, allow me to be confident in handling negotiations and projects that require the conciliation of different approaches to otherwise common problems.
