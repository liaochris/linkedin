    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Real Estate industry)

    1981 — Present (28 years)

    Stone Mountain Properties (SMP) is a family-owned enterprise which focuses on renovating, repositioning, marketing and operating apartment communities and commercial properties to fulfill targeted market niches. Its two principal themed multifamily community types are: Walden Internet Villages (waldenweb.com) targeted at an always-connected-at-high-speed lifestyle; and new Asian immigrant communities, targeted at providing environments conducive to making them feel at home & comfortable and to more rapidly adapt to and achieve success in their new, chosen country.

    In its multifamily communities, Stone Mountain focuses on creating inspiring garden surroundings, exceptional physical features and characteristics in its living units and a plethora of services and programs aimed at supporting creative and talented individuals who enrich themselves in the comprehensive infrastructure, physical environment, services and interaction with their neighbors.
