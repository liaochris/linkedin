Alex Andreyev’s Summary

I'm a serial entrepreneur. I empower passionate, visionary business people, making their dreams reality, by structuring their ventures and bringing large business disciplines into small business environment.

After developing strong communication and leadership skills, at the age of 17 I was promoted to manager of Carvel Ice Cream Bakery, driving down operational expenses by 15%. At 18, I built my first business, returning 8% of interest to our investors in a month. At 19, we expanded to 2 locations and generated over 240% ROI. At 20, I was privileged to become a Co-Owner of Tulipia Bridal LLC, an online wedding boutique and wholesaler, where I led majority of business activities, brand building and expansion of current business. Currently, I am consulting SMB, start-ups and non-for-profits, helping them achieve their goals and dreams.
