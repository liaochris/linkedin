Heidi Stine, Society of Wine Educ.-Director’s Groups:

Tempranillo Advocates, Producers and Amigos Society (TAPAS) - TAPAS’s mission statement is: “To promote Tempranillo and other varietal wine grapes native to the Iberian Peninsula and wines produced from them in the United States.” With more than 70 members TAPAS is, to American growers and producers of Iberian grapes and wines, what the Rhone Rangers and ZAP are to their constituents. Already TAPAS has, through several educational programs, publications, and wine tastings, promoted our products to thousands of trade professionals and consumers.
