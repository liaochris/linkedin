Thomas Barucchi’s Summary

With my previous experiences with Newmont Gold Mining at the Hope Bay greenstone belt in arctic as a senior geothechnician, I had the oppportunity to work in an unusual, remote environment. I was responsible for the massive "resampling" program during the first summer season. I therefore reinforced my skills in core logging and mineralogy as well as my aptitude to work under pressure with targets to reach. Moreover, I had to operate the Reflex Maxibor survey tool for the analysis/orientation of the drill holes.

The next season I was sent to the geotechnical rigs to analyse and log the cores used to design the future open pit (under the supervision of SRK geotechnical consultants).

Thomas Barucchi’s Specialties:

Exploration Geology/ Core logging. GIS (ESRI ArcGIS). Field Surveying (differential levelling, traversing). MapInfo.
