Ugur Arcan’s Summary

I am a technology and process professional with strong interest in continuous improvement and creative solutions to day-to-day technical, tactical, and strategic problems. My motivation comes from problems that are caused by outdated, ineffective and inefficient tools, and processes.

Through my Industrial (and Management) Engineering degree from college I have learned and honed my systematical, creative, critical and analytical thinking, process improvement, data analysis, effective problem solving skills. I continued to perfect my business acumen, project management, communication, marketing skills throughout my business graduate school.

In my spare time, I am heavily involved with new technology, web designing languages, blogging and tweeting, digital and social media, cutting-edge marketing techniques, and social networking tools. We are in the era of information and there is so much to learn out there. I am a continuous learner and I love every second of it!

Throughout my professional experience, I have been an intern at various large scale companies such as Cimtas (partially owned by Bechtel) and GE. Respectively, I worked on manufacturing processes as an industrial engineer and later, on corporate communications/events and transactional processes where I was a human resources generalist. I have also done technical recruiting for engineering and IT. Currently, I work as HR-AMS and Talent Attraction Processes and Technologies SME. My heavy focus is on workforce planning, process and technology improvements and implementations, social media initiatives, and reporting capabilities.

More detailed background information and my endorsements can be viewed on my profile. I am open to networking and truly believe in being passionate about what we do and sharing what we know.
