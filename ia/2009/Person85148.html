George Hayek’s Summary

IslandAndResort.com business focus is to offer resourced consumer friendly information for of the Bahamas, Caribbean, South Florida, Mexico Yucatan Peninsula, Costa Rica and Panama.

Compiling information on these islands and countries created a one-stop Internet experience translated in 10 languages where visitors access expansive information on Government Laws & Regulations, Folklore, Culture, Customs, Island Services, New Resort Developments, Real Estate Ownership, Geography, Investment Climate, Daily News articles, Environmental issues, Webcams, Blog, periodic Newsletters, Politics, Travel and Weather.

Island and Resort, Inc., as an advertising and marketing company, maintains an initiative to offer the largest selection of new resort developments in the Bahamas and Caribbean basin. Customer inquiries requesting real estate services are referred to Island and Resort Realty, Inc., a licensed Florida real estate brokerage corporation. We refer and cooperate with licensed and recognized local Island Country Real Estate Brokers throughout the entire region.

There are many opportunities to showcase your new resort development, rental program or business. IslandAndResort.com informed visitors from over 100 countries with more than 1200 pages of content.

Founders George A Hayek and Roger Washburn visualized an Internet portal to assist customers with helpful information about traveling and living in the Bahamas and Caribbean basin. Coming from multifaceted corporate backgrounds both gentlemen shared the business acumen and practical knowledge to accomplish their vision.

Our Global reach is powerful and our optimization superior.
