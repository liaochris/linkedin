Padraig McKeon’s Summary

Company leader and general manager since being appointed Managing Director in December 2003. Drury is a full service communications consultancy based in Dublin and is one of the largest of its type in the Irish market place serving a diverse range of commercial and public sector clients.

My day to day focus is on selection and retention of good people, client acquisition, high level relationship management with clients and effective management of resources and finance. The company has had sustained sales and profit growth through the period 2004 - 2008 and is trading ahead of budget in 2009.

I have been a Director at Drury with various specific responsibilities since 1997 and before that a Director of a subsidiary company of Drury Communications in Northern Ireland.

My objective is to broaden my management and business leadership experience building on both strands of my current role - the management of our own business and the provision of counsel to clients at a senior level.

I have extensive experience over 20 years in strategic PR and communications counsel with clients across a broad range of industry sectors (financial services, telecoms, IT, food, retail, utilities and development) and with companies and organisations of all scale and profile of ownership - multinational, publicly quoted, public sector.

Padraig McKeon’s Specialties:

Leadership and senior management counsel on all aspects with significant knowledge and experience of issue and crisis management, reputation management, Corporate and markets PR, sponsorship selection and management,
