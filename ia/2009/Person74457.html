Erick Laseca’s Summary

Erick is a strategic marketing communicator with experience in brand communications, public relations and social media. Erick provides strategic insight and an integrated perspective to his work. Currently attending Northwestern University’s full-time graduate IMC program, Erick is feeding his curiosity for emerging media and learning the latest skills from some of the industry’s leading marketers.

During Erick’s four years at Burson-Marsteller, he secured client placement on CNN, Good Morning America, Time Magazine and The Financial Times among others for clients like HP, Ford and Radio Flyer. Before joining B-M, Erick worked as Media Credentials Manager for the Sony Ericsson Open Tennis Tournament in Key Biscayne, Florida, listed as the 4th largest tennis tournament in the world. Prior to that, Erick was the Spokesperson for the Miami International Boat Show.

Erick is the PR Director for Northwestern’s IMC academic journal with Publisher Clarke Caywood and serves on the student advisory board with IMC Program Director Tom Collinger. He is the marketing committee co-chair for the American Red Cross of Greater Chicago and contributes to the publicity efforts of the Chicago International Film Festival and serves on the Junior Boards for both organizations.
