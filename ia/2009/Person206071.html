Ryan Finley’s Summary

With over twenty years of accounting and finance experience; I have worked in the construction, real estate, hospitality, publishing, resort, and finance industries. In addition, I have worked in a large corporate setting (a billion dollar company - Club Corporation of America and a seventy-five million dollar company - Southern Land Company).
