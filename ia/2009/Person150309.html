Maria Semple’s Summary

Maria Semple is an experienced prospect researcher, trainer and sought-after speaker on the topic of prospect research. She works with non-profit organizations and financial services firms interested in finding and researching their best prospects for long-term business relationships.

Maria is the author of two downloadable and interactive prospecting tools in her "Panning For Gold" series.

In October 2009, Maria became a Certified Expert Presenter with Constant Contact. Nonprofit groups and small business owners can benefit from her presentation on best practices in email marketing. Free 60-day trials included!
