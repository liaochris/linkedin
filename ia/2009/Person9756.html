Allen Ashe’s Summary

Software Quality Assurance/Test Engineer with 15 years of experience in: reviewing customer requirements, analyzing software design to verify it meets those requirements, and writing test plans & test cases for manual and automated tests to assure the delivered code fulfills those requirements.

Along with those "normal" QA functions I have also:
- Assisted in building QA departments
- Been a member of the team that helped to define test plans, procedures, and metrics used in the company
- Trained new people in those procedures
- Led internationally distributed test teams
