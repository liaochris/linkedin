Annemieke Deveer’s Summary

Allround marketing, sales, service manager with additional experience in general management in B-to-C and B-to-B-to-C markets (FMCG and Automotive). Resultdriven, creative, positive, persistent, analytical and teamplayer.

Obtained pHD in 1991 and started to work for Unilever Research Laboratory in Vlaardingen. Held various line- and project managers positions in the area of innovation and marketing(research) for Unilever worldwide.

Took on the responsiblity of Marketing Inteligence Manager at Pon Automotive in 2001. Prepared and implemented the business plan for "MotiWise" (an information service provider e.g. new Pon company). Developed and marketed as Director of MotiWise a range of new direct marketing and CRM services.

Joined RDC Group BV in Amsterdam, The Netherlands as Director Marketing and Sales a.i. in may 2008. RDC Datacentrum has developed information and communication services in the Dutch Automotive sector for more than fourty years. Current focus is to create new business opportunities.
