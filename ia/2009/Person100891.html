Jack Dalrymple’s Summary

Executive Director ♦ Chief of Staff ♦ Senior HR Executive

* Seven years experience as Executive Director of three independent Federal organizations ranging in size from 110-490 employees and subordinate managers.
* 34 months as Director of executive staff of senior analysts and planners in the Manpower and Personnel Directorate of the U.S. Pacific Fleet (4-Star Admiral) headquarters. Determined HR policies, manpower requirements, personnel assignments and retention programs for 100K employees in 504 field activities.

* 15 years supervisory experience at the GS 14-15 level, including eight years as GS 15 equivalent.
* Documented success as the managing authority for annual operating budgets up to $35M.
* Proven ability to effectively communicate to customers, staff and higher management. Author of agency policy documents. Briefer to senior officials in public, corporate and international forums.
* Six years experience in the classroom at Bachelors and Masters level in the subjects of leadership, management, ethics and decision making.
* Confirmed talent in establishing superb work environment of cooperation and high morale through mentorship and employee development.
* Broad background in administering EEO and Affirmative Action programs and objectives.
