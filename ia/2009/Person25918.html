Ben Curwood’s Summary

As a specialist Consultant within IT Development, my clients and candidates are based across a wide range of industries from Investment Banks, Financial Services, Retail, ISVs, Media, and Digital Marketing/Branding/Advertising.

I offer numerous recruitment procedures according to clients’ needs, from headhunting, search & selection, partnership advertising to regular recruitment processes.

Candidates can be assured that I address all aspects of a potential change in employment from career progression, financial aspects to office environment, discussing at length their aspirations and working to find the right fit for them.

Personally, I focus my attention across all seniorities with Java, Web & .Net Development, but not exclusively.

As a company, we work across the broad spectrum of IT from Java & .Net, to RPG/AS400/ISeries, GMI, SQL/Oracle, IT Business Analysis and IT Project Management and more. If you are looking for a Recruitment Consultancy that can work across the entirely of your IT staffing needs, please feel free to call and discuss what we can offer.
