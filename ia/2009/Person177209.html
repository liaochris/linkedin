Andrew Turnbull’s Summary

The word is out! Job Seekers are looking to more intelligent ways of attracting the right Job. What better way than displaying your skill set, directly to the Employers & Recruiters who are on the lookout for your skills?

Resume Databases have come a long way with advances in technology. One Player in Australia - www.nt3.com.au - has emerged with a business model that has gained immediate attention from some of the biggest Job Board Players in the market both internationally and locally.

Offering completely free access to Recruiters & Employers to search the candidate pool, unlimited Job Postings and the ability to message Job Seekers of interest for free to check they are still "Active & Available" is starting to win the minds of astute HR Teams & Recruiters across the country.

Prospective Employers or Recruiters simply pay to release the direct contact details of Job Seekers and approach them direct with an interview offer. There are no other charges than paying to release the details of the candidate on your shortlist.

With fully Searchable CV / Profiles, Recruiters & Employers can quickly identify suitable new talent, and satisfy themselves to the Job Seekers suitability for a Role on a "Self Serve" basis.

Job Seekers Identity is protected & they can block any Employer or Recruiter from viewing their profile. (Like their current Employer)
