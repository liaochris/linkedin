adam widener’s Summary

Born and bred in the rust belt capitol of Peoria IL with a plastic spoon stuck in his mouth, Adam Widener soon found himself honing his artistic skills upon arriving in Milwaukee a mere 5 years ago. After enrolling into the graphics arts program at the Milwaukee Institute of Art and Design and then following suit with advertising at Marquette, Adam has dove head first into the nuances that make up the different elements of cutting edge design, advertising strategies, and all around good times. When he’s not catching up on piling schoolwork, running his own arts and music magazine, or grabbing any internship or freelance gig he can, he's usually found crammed in a stinky van playing shows all over the United States with his rock n’ roll group, Plexi 3. Adam Widener; a true renaissance man of cultural communications!
--Sir Edwin Gallow (cultural terrorist)

Plastique Pop is the creation. An exploration into the expansive world that is art, design, communication, perception, and understanding. The idea is to craft a unique blend of of mass media to form an amount of work based on surrealism as well as personal and global identity. Plastique Pop is not only a modern design application, but an outlet for adam's art and music magazine which he publishes bi-monthly.
