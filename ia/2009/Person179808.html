Colas Overkott’s Experience

  • CEO

    Visiware (France)

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Broadcast Media industry)

    February 2006 — Present (3 years 3 months)

    Chief Exec / DG at Visiware (www.visiware.com), the global leader in interactive TV gaming. Visiware owns a catalogue of 200+ casual games and operates several premium television game channels (such as Playin'TV, Playin'Casino, Minikids) broadcast among others on Dish, Time Warner Cable, Bell ExpressVu, DirecTV Latin-America, TPS France, Canalsatellite France, Sky Italia, Digital Plus Spain, Starhub Singapore, Austar and Foxtel Australia, Sky New Zealand.
    Recently launched the world's first real triple play casual gaming offer, with the same games available on iTV, mobile, and broadband.

  • Executive Vice President Sales & Marketing

    SCM Microsystems (USA / Germany)

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; SCMM; Information Technology and Services industry)

    2002 — January 2006 (4 years)

    SCM Microsystems is global leader in smart-card readers for digital TV and for network / enterprise security, as well as in photo-kiosk flash card readers.
    SCM is listed on NASDAQ and the Frankfurt exchange.
    Managed the digital TV business, succesfully sold to Kudelski Group, as well as Sales and Marketing for all product lines.

  • CEO

    Lysis

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; KUD; Computer Software industry)

    2000 — 2002 (2 years)

    Lysis (www.lysis.com) in Lausanne Switzerland, provides software solutions for creating, managing and delivering digital television services. Lysis is in particular world leader in software allowing pay-TV operators to build pay-per-view and VOD services. Also develops interactive TV applications (among others the world's first commercial MHP EPG).
    Acquired by Nagravision / Kudelski Group 2001.
