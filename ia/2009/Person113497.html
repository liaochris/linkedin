Jim Vogel’s Summary

Jim attended Southwest Missouri State University, for a degree in Finance. Jim first sought a management position with a local computer store. "One afternoon, our lead sales person fell ill, and I had to fill in." Jim quickly found his true passion for sales. Within months, Jim had broken nearly every sales record the company boasted. Jim soon thereafter, decided to enter the family business seeking new challenges. Jim interviewed the top real estate agents in the world seeking strategies for success. In short, Jim found them, implemented them, and now has the opportunity to bring you a marketing plan that is fast-paced, high tech, and solution based. Selling over $14,000,000 in his first year with Reece and Nichols, for Jim truly the Sky is the Limit. "Kansas City native Jim Vogel is a 3rd generation Realtor. Jim's tireless work-ethic resulted in the sale of more than 100-homes in his first year with Reece and Nichols. He is currently the #1 agent in the College Boulevard office at Reece and Nichols. He credits his success to the personal attention he gives his clients, as well as a marketing plan that is aggressive, high-tech, and solution-based. "I cater to my clients on a 24-hour-a-day basis, utilizing the best technology available in the business, and I treat my client''s interests as if they were my own." says Jim. Jim brokers homes in Johnson, Jackson counties, and North Kansas City areas. He is a Member of Heartland Multiple Listing Service, the National and Kansas City Regional Association of Realtors, and the Leawood and Overland Park Chamber of Commerce." -Kansas City Magazine May 2004
