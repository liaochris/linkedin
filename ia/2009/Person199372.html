Ron J. Bonhagen, Sr.’s Summary

As a Broker in the Silicon Valley, Ron helps his clients reach their goals and achieves superb results in today's real estate market. He also helps his clients by getting the job done in any real estate market.

Ron started his career in 1989 as a Realtor in the historic districts of New Orleans. Ron brings to his real estate business 20 years of real estate and technology sales and marketing experience. Ron is continuously developing new systems to achieve the superior service and results that his clients deserve.

After a few years as a top producing Sales Executive with tech start-up companies which are now part of global Fortune 100 corporations such as IBM, Computer Associates, and Verizon Business, Ron returned to serving his clients and selling real estate. Ron's sales and marketing expertise in the technology sector in exceeding sales forecasts and exceeding client expectations serve him well in his real estate business by delivering the results desired by his clients.

*Strong sales, marketing and business development skills
*Strong client service commitment
*Strong strategic sales planning experience
*Focused on and driven by results

Ron's professional representation of his home seller and buyer clients has earned Ron their trust and respect which is shown from their many referrals. Ensuring his clients a good and pleasurable experience throughout the process of buying or selling a home also generates more and more "Raving Fans" every day.

Ron resides in San Jose, CA where his wife, Penny, grew up. When Ron is not selling homes or investment properties, you can find him spending time with his family - Penny and their 2 year old son, R.J.

Ron J. Bonhagen, Sr.’s Specialties:

Residential and investment properties, multi-family (apartment buildings), luxury real estate, bank owned (foreclosure) property, land development, international and local relocation in and around the Silicon Valley (Santa Clara, San Mateo and Santa Cruz counties).
