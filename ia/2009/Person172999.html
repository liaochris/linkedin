Neil Walker’s Summary

I have worked for the past ten years on the border between New Media and Traditional Media.

During this period I have worked on many high profile media projects in the UK and Europe including Big Brother Series 1, Pop Idol, Popstars and Survivor in the commercial sector and on iPlayer service whilst at the BBC.

During 2002 I spent several months in the USA as part of a start-up. An experience, which whilst not as financially rewarding as I would have hoped, I still see it as being a unique learning opportunity. I fully intend to try again in the near future.

I have never being happy with the status quo within my role or remained strictly attached to my initial job description instead I have aimed to provide the best possible contribution I could make with my personal skillset. I have therefore always dealt with the bleeding-edge, even in large blue-chips, and intend to carry on doing the same for some time yet.
