Jamie Shelton’s Summary

An expert veteran of over 20 years in the hotel industry in Chicago (and briefly in Milwaukee) with many of the industry's most recognizable hotel brands (Holiday Inn, Hilton, Clarion, Days Inn, Hampton, Radisson, Starwood's W brand), vastly experienced in every facet of hotel sales and marketing.

Currently an (award winning) artist / illustrator known for photorealistic pencil / pen illustration, pre production storyboarding, expert design / logo work and cartooning. Pre production / animation artist and sales associate for recently formed Acme Avatars and DNA Digital Media (where I produced pre production storyboards for interactive internet game promotions for Fortune 500 clients McDonald's, Kellogg's and Bubblelicious). Owner of Neophyte Art Studios LLC, a division of JamieArts LLC, which will be launching several art oriented web sites throughout 2009.

Also an independent contractor to conventions, conferences & special events, providing services encompassing all roles from onsite convention registration, security and lead retrieval to logistics coordination, special event hosting and trade show booth staffing coordination, set-up and breakdown.

Also an independent home based business owner of:
My Power Mall, an online shopping resource / portal- http://www.mypowermall.com/Biz/Home/25082
