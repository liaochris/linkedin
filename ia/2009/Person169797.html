Munir Mamujee’s Summary

I have 10 years recruitment experience and am extremely passionate and proud of what I have achieved.

My business was established in 2001 and we have grown from a UK recruiter of sales professionals to an international consultancy providing recruitment solutions to companies throughout the Gulf whilst still maintaining a strong UK presence.

My team and I visit the Gulf every four months, we develop new business and meet our clients. I find it infuriating when I hear of recruiters working in an overseas market and they have never been themselves!!!

We have partner offices in Dubai, Bahrain, Sydney and New Zealand, this means we can source candidates from anywhere and place them into our UK and Gulf clients. We have considerable experience in expatriation and local recruitment and have some fantastic testimonials!

I am looking for companies who wish to use the services of a professional recruitment firm whose vision is to be the premier supplier of talent to the Gulf by 2012.
