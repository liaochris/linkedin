Ersin Eker’s Summary

My belief is that the portfolio of routes consist the most valuable asset of an airline. If it succeeds in operating this portfolio profitably in the medium to long term, then not only will it manage to survive but also ensure sustainability in business. No doubt this will require meticulous market and marketing research, competition analysis, strategic planning, robust fleet planning, proper channel management, operational excellence, and finally, strategic brand management.

I have had the opportunity to see the big picture in airline management during my term as Strategic Planning and Investments Manager on top of my previous experience with operations in Line Maintenance Outstations Management.

Among the achievements during my work as Strategic Planning and Investments Manager are preparing a strategic plan for THY and drawing up a corporate strategy map and accompanying balanced scorecard model for the execution of strategic goals and objectives included in the strategic plan.
