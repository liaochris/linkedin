Gary Crisci’s Summary

Business Intelligence Professional with over thirteen years of finance experience, specializing in Hyperion Solutions software.

• Comprehensive implementation experience plus over 200 hours of formal training with Hyperion's Business Intelligence (BI) Platform and Business Performance Management (BPM) software. Including Hyperion Essbase Analytic Server, Hyperion Planning, Hyperion Financial Management, Hyperion Analyzer, Essbase Integration Server, Essbase Excel Add-in, Hyperion SmartView, and Hyperion Reports.
• Extensive expertise in all areas of multi-dimensional (OLAP, ROLAP, MOLAP) database design, implementation, and maintenance including meta-data outlines, load rules, calculation scripts, ESSCMD, MaxL, report scripts, and database optimization and tuning techniques for both Block Storage and Aggregate Storage database models.
• Highly proficient in the use of Microsoft Office applications, with advanced MS Access and MS Excel skills, including VBA macros.
• Additional experience and expertise with Microsoft SQL Server, Microsoft Analysis Services, Data Warehouse design, ETL, Informatica, DTS, SQL, MDX, Hyperion Enterprise, UpStream, Monarch, Lawson, People Soft, Timberline, Great Plains, and Microsoft Front Page.
