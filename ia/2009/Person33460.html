Brian Jeffries’s Experience

  • President, Founder

    Jeffries Management Group

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Consumer Services industry)

    Brigade Solutions

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Outsourcing/Offshoring industry)

    January 1998 — August 2001 (3 years 8 months)

Raised non-institutional start-up capital and operated company on $300,000 for first year while fine-tuning the business model, securing first customers, and successfully negotiating venture capital funding. Participated in two subsequent rounds of venture funding totaling $40 million. Strategized and drove implementation of physical infrastructure in U.S. and India. Led cross-functional teams to develop and implement business processes and technology infrastructure. Established company policies that bridged and synthesized cultural differences, enabling seamless integration between U.S. and India operations. Established a business culture that motivated employees to achieve optimum performance and realize goals well beyond their previous accomplishments.

  • Senior Consultant

    Gemini Consulting

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    September 1995 — December 1997 (2 years 4 months)

    Represented a premiere international consulting firm focusing on large-scale business process engineering projects. Led cross-functional team to redesign service activation processes for a state-owned telecom provider in Africa. Redesigned customer service processes, streamlined workflow, and identified metrics for a major U.S. telecommunications carrier. Successfully advocated a structure that facilitated the firm’s transition from being a protected monopoly to becoming an internationally recognized competitor. Established and led a regional team that simultaneously implemented elements of three key redesigned business processes at a state-owned telecom company in Africa.

    March 1988 — August 1993 (5 years 6 months)

Led a multidisciplinary team to design and implement new product offerings for the financial services industry. Analyzed and identified customer needs; worked directly with the banking-focused sales force to ensure that current products met requirements. Wrote sales support handbook mapping customer needs to the company’s cutting-edge data communications services. Collaborated with a team of managers to redesign a key service provisioning process in the face of a competitive threat.
