Stephanie L. Wong’s Summary

Stephanie Wong has over 15 years of experience in the fields of fashion, home accessories, product development, consumer trend analysis, branding and graphic design.

Recently developed trend reports for various clients in the travel, luxury, womens apparel and beauty industries.

Designed consumer products for the fashion, home accessories and consumer technology markets for Pottery Barn, 3M, Sony, and Signa Enterprises.

Founder of StyleMaven.com, an online magazine focusing on womens apparel, accessories, beauty, home and lifestyle products. StyleMaven also features a city shopping guide to specialty stores in the trend setting neighborhoods of Tokyo, London, New York, San Francisco and Los Angeles.

Stephanie has a B.S. in Marketing with an emphasis in the Fashion Psychology from California Polytechnic University SLO, studies at the City University in London and has traveled extensively in Japan.
