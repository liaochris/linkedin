David Lozovsky’s Summary

David Lozovsky is a senior advertising sales executive with over 15 years of sales and sales management experience. He joined the online space out of a need to channel his creative energies into the proper sales vehicle.

Since joining the online advertising industry in 2003 he has quickly learned about the space and within a few years went on from an entry level role to become senior level media executive with 360 degree understanding of the industry. David specializes in sales, business development, marketing and technological integration in the new media space.

David Lozovsky’s Specialties:

Highly skilled at various sales techniques. Purchasing and selling media. Contract negotiation. Development of systems and processes compliant with industry wide best practices. Creating strategic relationships. Consultative selling.
