Robert Houdeshell’s Summary

Robert Houdeshell is a 15 year veteran of Microsoft product development and implementation. He has applied his product management and ISV development skills at Solomon Software, Made2Manage Systems, FRx Software and in his own consulting firm, New Frontiers Consulting, Inc. At Solomon Software, he created the Solomon Development Advisory Council where he conducted ISV strategy, development specifications and beta testing of products. This watershed release propelled Solomon’s market presence with new quality standards and usability.

With Made2Manage and FRx Software, Robert managed ISV relationships which created value for the ISV and the clients of the respective products. Robert led the rollout of FRx 6.0 and FRx Forecaster budgeting and forecasting tool. In his own company, New Frontiers, he managed the development of a complex implementation of a custom developed fee collection system for the United States Department of Interior. His understanding of business requirements and processes allow him to make improvements that are instrumental to the success of the products and the businesses implementing them.

In 2006, Robert founded the International Association of Microsoft Certified Partners (IAMCP – Colorado). He served as president from 2007 to 2008, bringing in many new partners and conducting many successful events for IAMCP. He has provided expert testimony to Harvard Business School business studies for Dr. Marco Iansiti. Robert graduated with a Bachelors of Science degree with a 3.715/4.0 GPA (Magna Cum Laude). His approach has always been to facilitate and listen to ISV input while coupling this with the strategy and objectives of the software publisher. This has resulted in tremendous improvements in product development lifecycles and ISV rollouts timed very closely to the software publisher’s release date.

Robert Houdeshell’s Specialties:

ISV Strategy, Sales, Marketing, Consulting, Business Process Analysis, Dynamics SL, GP, FRx and CRM. Great Plains Solomon.
