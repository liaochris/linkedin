Stephen Debruyn’s Summary

Extensive global experience blending new web-based marketing channels with traditional marketing disciplines to maximize bottom line results.

WHAT I DO
Expertise includes branding, web marketing, search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing, and social media outreach. Organizations which typically seek my expertise:
* may not be generating sufficient revenue via their existing marketing channels
* know that social media can powerfully enhance their corporate presence and reputation, but have not yet fully implemented such strategies and tactics,
* have not optimized their web properties, thereby leaving money on the table
* realize there is a market for their products and services outside of North America, but have not been able to sufficiently capitalize on that opportunity, and
* know their corporate reputation and positioning in the marketplace is not strong and unique enough, which is impacting their revenue and profitability.

HOW I ADD VALUE
With years of experience in successfully and profitably devising and implementing marketing and communications programs, I can help organizations by:
* maximizing revenue and profitability via marketing initiatives, both domestic and globally,
* building and/or strengthening an organization's corporate reputation and market position, and
* allowing a firm to fully capitalize on social media marketing approaches and tactics

Stephen Debruyn’s Specialties:

Integrated marketing, marketing strategy, marketing communications, social media outreach, global branding, international business development, public relations, market research, corporate communications, CRM implementation and deployment, mergers and acquisitions, e-marketing, web-based marketing, Internet software solutions marketing

Stephen Debruyn’s Experience

  • Vice President, Marketing

    Cision

    (Public Company; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    2002 — 2009 (7 years )

    At Cision I implemented an integrated marketing strategy which blended together a variety of communications tactics with the aim to enhance the company's corporate reputation and to generate leads. Results surpassed original objectives by a wide margin. Marketing tactics combined e-marketing with thought leaderhip initiatives, social media outreach and a variety of activities designed to drive traffic to the company's web properties. Media outreach programs increasingly focus on social media - in 2008 I established a social media outreach program which highlighted the company's thought leadership position in this area, thereby bolstering its reputation and generating leads.

    In 2007 I drove the global re-branding of the entire organization, which involved thirteen separate corporate brands in ten countries worldwide.

    In 2003, I executed a complete corporate re-positioning of the company's services portfolio. This positioning was eventually adopted throughout the worldwide organization.

  • Vice President

    Millward Brown Precis

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2001 — 2002 (1 year )

    I established the first US East Coast office for this company, and was responsible for all marketing and operations aspect of the start-up.

  • Director, International Operations

    Media Report, United Kingdom

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    2000 — 2001 (1 year )

    At Media Report, a news management and monitoring firm, I established an automated news delivery process using outside suppliers which provided a new suite of services with superior features. The new process cut production costs and increased efficiency of service delivery.

    I introduced and sold new service categories as well as service innovations which increased the company's revenue by 50% in one year. I established an international client base, with clients including Orange, OpenWave and Global One.

  • Vice President, International Operations

    Delahaye Medialink

    (Information Services industry)

    1996 — 2000 (4 years )

    For Delahaye Medialink, a media analysis company, I successfully developed the company's global network of partners and affiliates across a dozen countries, and established remote offices in Holland, the UK, and Uruguay. Within two years, international revenue contributed 25% to the company's annual $8 million revenue stream, with clients including Procter & Gamble, Electronic Data Systems, Koc Holding, and Hewlett-Packard. In recognition, I received the 'Employee of the Year Award' in 1999 for outstanding performance in international business development.

    I made workflow improvements at the European offices that allowed for daily report services (as opposed to the standard monthly and quarterly reporting cycles) thereby generating a whole new range of client services. Clients for this service included Electronic Data Systems and AT&T.

    I managed and expanded a $1 million US-based portfolio of accounts including Digital, NEC, BMW and Cisco Systems.

  • Research Director

    MediaMap

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    1991 — 1996 (5 years )

    At MediaMap, a media research company, I created and managed media research services for PR professionals about European, Canadian and Mexican IT publishing environments, and established a European client base with clients in ten European countries. Clients included IBM, Hitachi, and Text 100.
