Matt Weeks’s Summary

Two decades of leadership and innovation in financial services software and solutions, payments, consumer software and automotive. Currently founding CEO of EyeTMedia, an online ad network optimization company, using predictive behavioral ad targeting to re-invent the way brands connect with and converse with consumers (currently in stealth). We will be licensing our solution across the ad community, including ad networks, agencies and publishers.

Past experience includes Intuit, Managemark, eAlity, FINTECA, Wells Fargo Bank, Bank of America, Security Pacific Bank, Crocker Bank, Moody's Investor Services, Morgan Stanley
