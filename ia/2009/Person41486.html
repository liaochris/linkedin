Charles Valle’s Summary

I have over 5 years experience in educational publishing and 7 years experience in small press literary publishing. My roles and duties have traversed the creative to administrative to process/procedure-intensive. I have an uncanny gift for problem-solving and analysis, which has led me to take on various projects that reside outside my given job duties and expectations. I am not averse to working on production tasks, though I derive the most satisfaction in working on projects from development to final delivery-- whether it be a literary journal or a multi-media assessment product.
