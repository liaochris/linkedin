Tom Ashburn’s Summary

I have been a Licensed Real Estate Professional and Broker since 1987.
My two great professional pleasures are, helping folks buy, sell or invest in real estate, and in teaching other real estate licensees the advanced skills they need to deliver outstanding custome service.
My Motto is "Real Estate Made Easy"
I belive heavily in Educating both Sellers and Buyers on how real estate actually works behind the scene, to limit their fears and concerns.
I provide constant feedback and communication throughout the process from first contact, to Contract and then to Closing, and beyond. I like to become one's "Realtor For Life", and provide help in any way I can.
Truly - Real Estate made Easy . . . and Enjoyable.

Ours is a "Real Estate Family".
My Father was a real estate developer and builder in coastal Gerogia.
My Wife, Margaret-Ann is a Broker-In-Charge (BIC) & Sales Manager for one of the 14 Russell & Jeffcoat Offices. Her brother is the BIC for another R & J office and his Wife is an agent. My Brother & his Wife are a Brokers in NC.

I have helped over 1,200+ Sellers get a quick sale, in 1/3rd the normal market time, at 98.33% of their List Price, with my "Exclusive 54 Point Ultimate Exposure Marketing System". Since 1987 I have helped thousands of families move and enjoy new surroundings.
