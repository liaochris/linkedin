Peter Nicholson’s Summary

In 2004 Pete Nicholson joined ServiceSource International, LLC where he held numerous positions including Senior Sales Rep, Team Lead and Business Development Manager. His focus on innovation, corporate strategy, strategic efficiencies, and maximizing revenues has driven millions of dollars of incremental revenue for his clients.

His passion for community involvement and education led to his involvement with Big Brothers Big Sisters of the Bay Area, where he holds a Junior Board position.

Nicholson received a B.S. in Business Administration from the Leeds School of Business at the University of Colorado at Boulder with areas of emphasis in Finance, Management and a minor in Philosophy.
