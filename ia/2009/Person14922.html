Andrey Golub’s Summary

Mr. Andrey Golub brings 10+ years of experience in IT/ Software Development, and 5+ in TLC/ high-tech and Internet sectors. Scientific Research activity in the field of Artificial Intelligence after successfully completed PhD studies on Systems Analysis and previously earned bachelor's diploma with honours in Applied Mathematics.

Broad experience with Web 2.0/ Social Media and Community projects, deep understanding and a good vision for Social Networking and Social Computing scene. After few years of active studies of what the others have done, he is now Social Media Researcher and Social Networks Manager for some upward-looking Open-Community/ 2.0 projects.
