Kevin O'Malley’s Summary

I have been in the recruiting industry assisting clients across the United States identify candidates with specialized skills within IT or Supply Chain operations for permanent hire for over ten years.

Prior to entering the recruiting industry, I was involved in the career direction of recording artists including Rob Thomas of Matchbox Twenty fame and the Florida based band, Spider Monkey.

I started my working career at age 19, marketing seven and eight figure shopping centers for purchase/ sale.

Kevin O'Malley’s Specialties:

I specialize in assisting manufacturing firms in the hiring of IT, Supply Chain & Accounting candidates with experience using specific ERP/ MRP systems (BPCS, MAPICS, SAP, JDE) or with specific skills/ processes/ certifications (APICS, JIT, etc.).
