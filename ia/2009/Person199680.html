Ryan Bearden’s Summary

My passion is working with customers - creating innovative and collaborative ideas and programs around improving customer experience and ultimately, loyalty. This passion quickly led me to the "agency side" where I have spent the past eight years working at ad agencies and marketing firms around the Austin area. My area of focus has been almost exclusively in the hi tech arena working with such clients as AMD, Dell, Avocent, BMC Software, Citrix Systems and many others. I have extensive experience in both strategic planning as well as execution and project management. Additionally, I have demonstrated consistent success in the area of team building and leadership.

Recently (April '06) I switched gears and made the jump to "client side" - I am now building an managing the customer reference program for Sun's Software Practice. Here I am not only managing Sales and Marketing reference requests, but I am also working to build more innovative programs that allows Sun to showcase customer success including PR activities, analyst relations, speaking opportunities and more.

In January of 2007 I added team lead/manager role to my responsibilities at Sun. In addition to growing the Software customer marketing group I also now manage the internal Creative Services and Events Management team for the Software Practice. Altogether I am managing a team of 6 people who reside both in Austin and the Bay Area.
