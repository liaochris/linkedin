Antonio Lapa Silveira’s Experience

  • President, Owner

    Epyx Media Solutions and WoodWing Brazil

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 2003 — Present (6 years 2 months)

    President and CEO at EPYX Media Solutions and WoodWing Brazil, entrepreneur and Internet visionary.
    Focused on publishing solutions integration for newspaper, magazine, book, web and multiple other medias editorial production workflow.

    With Epyx/WoodWing Brazil, provides newspaper, magazine, book publishing, advertising agencies and other corporations the best content production solution available on the market.

  • President, Owner

    WebSoluções Informática

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 1997 — September 2003 (6 years 1 month)

    Founded WebSoluções in 1997, a company dedicated to provide its customers with IT solutions focused on the web. In less than two years of operation have succeeded in the development of a major e-commerce portal for Visa, Brazil, many institutional sites and electronic stores, various corporate intranet solutions and the joint development (with one of the largest international web development company at the moment, MarchFirst) of two vertical portals (on women and technology) for Editora Abril, a large publishing group in Brazil.
    Sold the company and re-started MultiSoluções with a restructured focus to pursue better opportunities.

  • President, Owner

    MultiSoluções Informática

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1987 — September 1997 (10 years 9 months)

    Founded the company in 1987, focused on graphic arts, pre-press and publishing automation. Directed it for 10 years, to became one of the most important Brazilian distributors/resellers of the Desktop and HighEnd Publishing technologies from Adobe Systems, Aldus Corp., Apple Computer, Claris, Macromedia, Microsoft, Nikon, Screen Technologies, Umax and Varityper/PrePress. Grew the company to become the largest Adobe and Macromedia distributor in the country with annual revenues of up to US$ 9.2 mi. In 1997 sold the distribution operation (MasterDix) to the Tallard Group, a USA company located in Miami, Florida.

  • Partner Director

    Compucenter Informática

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    August 1985 — November 1986 (1 year 4 months)

    Cofounded the company and directed it to become the first Oracle distributor in São Paulo, Brazil. Left to found MultiSoluções.

  • Systems Development Manager

    Editora Abril

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; Publishing industry)

    May 1983 — August 1985 (2 years 4 months)

    Managed a team of about 90 professionals focused on the development of information system solutions to the Editora Abril Group. That group was formed of several companies focused on magazine and book publishing, cable-TV, phone catalog, logistics and direct marketing businesses. Participated in the Data-Processing (DP) Steering Committee in order to plan, get approval, implement and follow-up the usage of IT solutions by the whole group. Left the company to become a partner on a new venture.

  • Systems Manager

    VASP - Brazilian Domestic Airlines

    (Government Agency; 5001-10,000 employees; Airlines/Aviation industry)

    January 1978 — May 1983 (5 years 5 months)

    Starting with a small team, managed the recruiting, selection, hiring and training process to build a team of 60+ professionals focused on the development and maintenance of system solutions to that domestic airline - at that moment government owned - company. Organized, lead and motivated various groups of IT professionals charged with developing and/or maintaining the airline reservations, airport passenger check-in, sales and travel agencies, aircraft maintenance control, flight dispatch, crew scheduling, human resources, financial and many other corporate information systems. Successfully increased the user satisfaction and saved the company millions of US dollars on a better operated and maintained IT department. Due to changes on the organization of that government owned airline company, left to rejoin Editora Abril, which offered more appealing professional challenges.

  • Systems Development Coordinator

    Riachuelo Stores - Retail Chain

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Apparel & Fashion industry)

    June 1975 — January 1978 (2 years 8 months)

    Became leader of a 12 people professional team. Focused on the development and maintenance of a retail business management information system for Lojas Riachuelo, then the second largest retail chain, with headquarters in São Paulo and 146 store fronts spread over 10 of the largest states of Brazil. In almost three years successfully implemented a business retail management system aimed at the inventory and profit control of the whole business and each of the 146 store fronts. In the headquarter developed and implemented Purchasing, Financial, Accounting and Human Resources information systems. Left the company to face a more professionally appealing challenge in the airline industry.

  • Business Analyst

    SABESP - São Paulo Water Utility Company

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Utilities industry)

    May 1974 — June 1975 (1 year 2 months)

    Developed a business reorganization plan for the government owned, Sabesp, that had been recently created as a merge of six independent São Paulo state water utility companies. Proposed, negotiated and got approval for the organizational and information systems plan of several divisions of the company, including the complete integration of a couple of independent data centers.

  • Computer Programmer and Systems Development Analyst

    Editora Abril

    (Publishing industry)

    December 1966 — May 1974 (7 years 6 months)

    Started working in 1966 as a trainee. Successfully completed all related courses on IBM, Burroughs and Hewlett Packard and became one of the best computer programmers and system analysts for Editora Abril. Developed many programs for IBM-1401, IBM/360, IBM/370, Burroughs series B-500 and B-3500/3700, Friden, Hewlett Packard, Digital and Olivetti family of computers. As a systems analyst developed an statistical magazine distribution system focused on the Brazilian distributors and further on, developed another one focused on the newstands partners in São Paulo. After 7 years of success left the company for a more promising job as Business Analyst.
