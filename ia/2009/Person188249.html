Marzena Kmiecik’s Summary

Bilingual (English / Polish) Interactive Creative Director- a collaborator, an architect, facilitator, Art Director and contributor to online creative and marketing strategies that enable companies connect and build dialogues with their audience. For nearly 10 years, she has tapped into the fascinating power of the internet to help others grow their companies online, create buzz, generate leads and increase profits regardless of what's going on on Wall Street.

Marzena's ability to bridge innovative, captivating concepts with clients’ aspirations, offers businesses unique opportunity to continually create and maintain successful relationships with their customers. Her career has enabled her to bring together passionate visionaries from around the world and directly create intimate relationships focused on measurable results.

Pixelfanatix's team gravitates towards clients who are independent thinkers, who expect personalized, streamlined experience, high impact, innovative solutions, swift turn around and no-nonsense process. They frequently partner with agencies and corporate companies to help build unique marketing strategies, that harness collective intelligence, build relationships and give individuals control over data.

Marzena is always interested in working with startups-Fortune 500 companies that are facing challenges online and need solid, results driven strategies. Also visit her on Twitter where she frequently posts great tips, quotes, interesting links and other business building information.
