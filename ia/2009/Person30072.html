Bob Apollo’s Summary

I’ve been lucky enough to work with and for some of the most interesting people and organisations in the high-tech B2B space during key periods of transition - and been able to experience at first hand the things that separate the “also rans” from those that emerge as leaders in their markets.

Having held long-term P&L responsibility at UK, EMEA and Global level for a range of fast-growing B2B businesses, I have been able to learn at first hand how to both achieve the necessary results in the current quarter and build organisations capable of delivering sustained performance, quarter after quarter.

I’m now working with a rapidly growing number of B2B clients to help them improve operational execution in both the short and long term. We’ve just launched a ground-breaking pipeline analytics service (see www.revenue-insights.com for more details) that helps companies unlock the actionable information they need from their salesforce.com system to hit their numbers this quarter - and systematically improve future performance.

Bob Apollo’s Specialties:

- Sales performance improvement

- Mastering the complex sale - from the buyer's perspective
- Salesforce.com analytics
- Cloud9 Analytics AppExchange solution
