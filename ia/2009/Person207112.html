Saikat Saha’s Summary

Saikat Saha is an alumni of Delhi Public School R K Puram.

He completed his Bachelors in Business Administration Degree from Madras Christian College where he ranked first [92%] in his final year project report on 'Emotional Intelligence & Spiritual Intelligence at workplace' done at Indian Oil Corporation Ltd, Chennai.

He was the only candidate to be recruited by Tata Consultancy Services Ltd as a Process Associate from the Department of Business Administration, Madras Christian College.

He served in Tata Consultancy Services Ltd from May 2007- February 2008. After this period he completed his Certificate Course in Human Resource Management from the Madras Management Association while preparing for his entrance exam in Madras School of Social Work.

Currently he is doing his MA-HRM from Madras School of Social Work.

He is focussed to build his career in the HR domain and wants to contibute towards the future of HRM.

Saikat Saha blogs regularly at www.ideatezone.blogspot.com on various issues including Human Resource Management. He also has keen interest in Theatre,Travelling and Cricket.

He has done his field work in Reliance Communication[Overview of HR activities],Larsen & Toubro, Valves Business Group[Recommending HR practices with Low Cost & High Engagement impact], Courtyard by Marriott[Employer Branding] as a part of his course curriculum in Madras School of Social Work. He did his summer internship from ONGC on 'Competency Mapping'. Currently he is doing his research project on 'Execution orientation in work culture' at BHEL.
