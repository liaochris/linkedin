Julia Cunningham’s Summary

Born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri, I attended St. Louis University until I transferred to the University of Cincinnati, School of Business to finish my schooling with a double major in Marketing and Management.
I have always worked in some facet of Real Estate. While living in Kentucky, I owned a mortgage company and carried a Mortgage Brokers license in Kentucky, Ohio, and Indiana . I also worked as a Sales and Marketing Representative for Ryan Homes (NVR, INC.), specializing in new home construction.
My husband practiced medicine in Kentucky for 10 years until we fell in love with Charleston while traveling and decided to place our roots here. After moving to Charleston I joined Sandlapper Estate Group and enjoyed on-site sales for Julian Lecraw, a developer based out of Atlanta. While there, I sold condominiums for Concord West of the Ashley, a highly successful condo conversion project where I was able to meet and help hundreds of people find homes.
Recently, Sandlapper merged with Coldwell Banker United Realtors and I have been afforded many new opportunities and tools to help my clients.
I strive to focus on my clients' needs and believe in under promising and over delivering. I plan to combine my attentiveness to clients, understanding of the psychology of buying and selling a home and my ability to relate to the need behind the need to help people with their greatest investment and to form new relationships.
I try to keep abreast of current trends and benefits to my clients, which is why last year, I completed my Earth Craft Certification. Healthier and more energy efficient homes are important to my clients which means they are important to me as well.
