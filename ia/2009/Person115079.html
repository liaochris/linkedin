Jobeda Ali’s Summary

Fair Knowledge creates innovative learning formats and encourages the distribution of marginalised voices in the mainstream.

We aim to bring cultural and political balance into the global media and knowledge industries.

1. We source fresh voices and new perspectives from diverse and often marginalised content producers. We offer them media training including filmmaking and opportunities for exhibition.

2. We produce learning formats for formal education institutions as well as independent clients from public, charity and corporate sectors.

3. We create new story-telling formats for organisations including video and exhibition production.

4. We make documentaries and ethnographic films for broadcasters and corporate clients.

5. Our proprietary format, the Cineforum, is taking over the world! A blend of a film festival, conference and learning event, it takes on important contemporary issues and enables delegates to take a closer look and develop relevant solutions. Our last Cineforum advanced Muslim women as leaders and achieved 50 million eyeballs from around the world. For the next Cineforum in December 09, we are looking for ethnic minority voices on climate change and have a formal conduit to take selected perspectives to the Copenhagen World Climate Summit.
