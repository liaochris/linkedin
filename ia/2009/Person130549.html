Kerry Nesbit’s Summary

As a marketing-oriented, Internet-savvy freelance writer and graphic designer, Kerry Nesbit is exceptionally well qualified to help you determine what you want to say and how best to say it to the people who matter to you most.

Kerry Nesbit’s Specialties:

Marketing communications and media relations for health care and mental health care and advocacy organizations. Capital campaign case statements, annual fund solicitations and marketing materials for independent schools, colleges, churches and other non-profit organizations. Veterinary, legal and financial services marketing. Branding, packaging and label design, primarily for food manufacturers and processors.

Kerry Nesbit’s Experience

  • President

    Kerry Nesbit, Incorporated

    (Publishing industry)

    August 1983 — Present (26 years 1 month)

    Kerry Nesbit started her company as a writing and graphic design business in 1983 after three years as a writer-editor-photographer for an insurance company. As a freelancer, Kerry provides writing and graphic design services for print and digital media. Noteworthy client relationships include 26 years' writing fund-raising materials for clients of fund-raising counsel Robert Moore Allen, 10 years' designing packaging and sales collateral for Golding Farms Foods, 8 years' writing catalog copy for National Wholesale Company, 4.5 years as public relations and marketing communications counsel for Lexington Memorial Hospital, 4 years as public relations and marketing communications counsel for NAMI North Carolina, and 3 years as public relations and marketing communications counsel for Petree Stockton & Robinson. Kerry sells logo and stationery design services to veterinarians and lawyers through two e-commerce sites.
