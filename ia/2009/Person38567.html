Caroline Racine’s Summary

Currently serving as Director of Caribbean Franchise Development for worldwide lodging franchisor Choice Hotels International (NYSE: CHH).

This role is responsible for increasing the brand awareness and distribution of the company’s 10 brands throughout the Caribbean via conversion and new construction opportunities.

Ten year career at Choice Hotels includes a variety of roles within the organization.

Worldwide Sales & Intermediary Marketing division leading the charge for the company’s strategic direction with its largest leisure partnership, AAA/CAA, as the primary interface with executives and club presidents.

During this period, the motor club revenue more than doubled from $100 million to $224 million with steady incremental revenue growth averaging eight percent year over year.

In 2003, Racine was named "Hoteliers Rising Star" in Travel Agent Magazine.

Prior to joining Choice served as Account Executive for America Online’s interactive partnership marketing initiatives responsible for generating over $100 million in partnership sales revenue per year and overall communications to top-level advertisers and partners.

Other sales and marketing positions include International Sales Planner during the launch of The Discovery Channel’s international terrestrial sales initiatives in Asia and Latin America.

Haitian American born in Washington, D.C.

Collector and dealer of Latin American art and an active real estate investor.

Bilingual in French and Creole.

Caroline Racine’s Specialties:

Competitive analysis, realestate development, franchising, sales/marketing and public speaking.
