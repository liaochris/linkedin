Gideon Marks’s Summary

Gideon Marks a seasoned executive in high tech companies with strong background in finance and business/corporate development.

During his career Gideon took 3 companies public on the NASDAQ stock exchange and lead other 3 to successful M&A transactions.

Gideon's forte is in his operational background, good business skills and being a deal maker.
Gideon is well connected with the VC and investment banking communities.

Currently Gideon heads Corporate Development at Net Optics. www.netoptics.com

During recent years Gideon joined two companies as CFO/COO and helped them with turnaround projects and M&A transactions. Prior roles in the past included a Managing Director at Garage Technology Ventures, Managing Director at Montgomery Securities and CFO of the Rad Group of companies.

During the ten years Gideon served as the Rad Group CFO, lead the finance of 12 companies from inception to various stages of maturity including the 3 IPOs mentioned above.
