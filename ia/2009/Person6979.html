Alejandro Lugo Franceschi’s Summary

M.F.A. in Visual Effects, (exp. 2013, Academy of Art University, San Francisco, CA, USA)

- B.S. in Media Arts and Animation (2001, Art Institute of Ft. Lauderdale, FL, USA).

- I worked in Broadcast TV for the last 10+ years, & also have feature film experience: 2D-to-3D conversion/Roto/Paint for Warner Bros., Disney & Marvel (before their merger).

- Bilingual/Literate/Fluent: English/Spanish.

- My work experience ranges from: commercials, soap operas, TV features, TV series, DVD Authoring (both video and multimedia), Editing, 2D/3D animation, Motion Graphics, Photo/Video Retouching, Compositing, and Digital Visual Effects. Additionally, I have served as a technical consultant both on set and off, for SD/HD video technologies and proper recording for post production integration.

- 2005: "Emerging Artist of the Year" grant from the Cultural Board of the State of Florida.

- 2007: Emmy Award for Outstanding Children's TV Programming: Series "It's the Write Time" (sic); awarded by the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences (N.A.T.A.S.).

- 2008: Platinum AVA, awarded by the Association of Marketing and Communication Professionals (A.M.C.P.).

- 2009: Top #5 Comedy Podcast of 2008 as judged & awarded by Podbean.com (in our first year). 100k+ worldwide subscribers and growing.

- I am sincerely grateful to have been able to work with very large companies in high profile marketing campaigns, among them: Microsoft, L'Oreal, Maybelline, Pulte, Suzuki, Saban Entertainment Group, Play/Along Toys/Jakks Pacific, the Muscular Dystrophy Association, and Disney.

Alejandro Lugo Franceschi’s Experience

  • President & Lead Digital Artist/Compositor

    Tempest Digital, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    2006 — Present (3 years )

    Tempest Digital, Inc., is my company through which I freelance for various post production entities, both local and abroad. Among some of the jobs I have handled under this umbrella are: infomercials, corporate videos, music videos, movie posters, concept art, and show opens for soap operas/series (mostly for the Spanish-American market). I have also done the ADR, foley, musical score, and VFX for a 60-min. made for TV feature in the Spanish market - all completed p/t in 6 weeks! Additionally, the Inc. has recently expanded to handle HD Editing, Visual Effects, Colour Grading, and Video Compression services (YouTube, podcasting setups, etc.). On occasion, I have also handled Post on million-dollar+ weddings and bar/bat mitzvahs through an intermediary company; as well as authoring their multimedia, interactive DVD & Blu-Ray disks. Amongst clients: John Casablanca Modeling Agency, Jakks Pacific (PlayAlong Toys [sic]), Saban Entertainment Group, and Disney, et al.

    Legend Films

    (Privately Held; Entertainment industry)

    August 2009 — October 2009 (3 months)

    Depending upon assigned project(s):

    1.) Converting studio feature films originally released as 2D into digital 3D versions (shutter glasses versions, not anaglyph or polarized).

    2.) Masking/Paint/Roto fixes as necessary to assist in 2D-3D conversion processing.

    3.) 3D Modeling/Animation for projection mapping of 2D elements into 3D.

    Most recently, in 2008, I completed 60 minutes of: ADR, Foley, Scoring, Editing, Colour Correction, and "invisible" Visual Effects Compositing (sky & rig replacement, blemish/beauty correction, etc.) for their soap feature: "Verano Ardiente" ("Hot Summer").
