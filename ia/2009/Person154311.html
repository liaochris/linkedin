  • Founder & President

    grasshopper.tv Inc.

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    March 2007 — July 2008 (1 year 5 months)

    Grasshopper.tv Inc. provided online health and wellness video aggregation and subscription services. The company succeeded in negotiating licensing agreements for over 200 top-selling DVDs and making them available to individual and corporate subscribers via the grasshopper.tv on-demand streaming video portal. Its world-class content selection consisted of fitness videos – including aerobics, yoga, pilates, kickboxing, and dance lessons – as well as educational seminars by world-renowned stress management and nutrition experts. The company also launched a second web portal called StressManagementTV.com with a focus on mind-body content. Although the services succeeded in attracting both individual and corporate subscribers, they were discontinued in the summer of 2008 due to content partner concerns over the cannibalization of DVD sales.

  • Co-Founder & President

    .TV Technologies

    (Internet industry)

    2004 — 2007 (3 years)

    .TV Technologies developed an open internet television software platform that enabled video content owners to create their own live and/or on-demand internet TV channels and distribute them to any internet connected television in the world. The platform's three components – .TV ControlRoom, MainMenu.TV, and EasyPay.TV – gave content owners full control over their channels and content while making it possible for viewers to use a remote control to navigate a virtually unlimited selection of ad-supported, subscription, pay-per-view, and pay-per-minute video from around the globe. Although the platform was successfully demonstrated and gained significant interest from film studios, television broadcast networks, and major content owners such as National Geographic Channel and E! Entertainment Television, the company’s funders ultimately decided that the venture was too risky to warrant further investment. .TV Technologies was liquidated in 2007.
