Mark Lloyd’s Summary

Principal Consultant and the author of the Mobility Maturity Model (iMMM), a published methodology that has driven repeatable success with "Business Process Mobilization" by increasing efficiency & reducing costs at leading companies including Wal-Mart, Intuit, Ameritrade, and Bell Industries.

The iMMM methodology applied:

1. Analyze client's complex workflow using the iMMM Framework: BENEFIT: Client visually sees points the where mobility improves efficiency.

2. Implement with iMMM Templates and industry leading mobile devices/middleware: BENEFIT: Client trusts in the secure solution that guarantees access to the latest devices as they become available.

3. User Acceptance / Go-Live: BENEFIT: Client realizes the efficiency gains in hours/days – not weeks/months.

I am looking for organizations seeking to improve operating efficiency & reduce costs with a repeatable approach.
