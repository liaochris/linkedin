Lance Latham, MBA’s Summary

I have helped a variety of companies - spanning both traditional manufacturing, warehouse / logistics and financial services - to drastically reduce their operating costs by as much as 45% (average cost reduction = 25%). Savings have ranged from hundreds of thousands to millions of $'s per year - the larger the client organization, the greater the savings achieved. Client paybacks range from 3:1 to 10:1, and savings are guaranteed (as in, if we don't deliver the bottom-line benefits that we promise by the agreed-upon date, we take a proportionate 'hit' to our own compensation). Results come through the simultaneous rise of output quality and productivity, coupled with reduced operating costs. Managed cost-saving projects have included internal improvement programs (typically taking 1 - 4 months to implement) and large consolidation / outsourcing initiatives (6 - 12 months to implement). Through my consulting firm, Metrilogics LLC, I would like to work with as many clients as possible during these challenging financial times to help them achieve their short-term and long-term process improvement and cost-saving goals.
