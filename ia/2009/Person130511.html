Kerry David’s Summary

I've spent the past fifteen years in the film industry learning from the bottom up. I spent two and a half years in Business Affairs at Paramount Pictures learning the contract side of filmmaking and then on to work with Tom Cruise and Nicole Kidman for three years honing the practical side of filmmaking. With enough experience to start my own production company, I've since produced several feature films, three documentaries, educational content. I divide my time between producing content for Lucky Crow Films and in helping the next generation of filmmakers with Indieproducer.net.

My primary focus is producing filmed entertainment through Lucky Crow films. My current feature "Like Dandelion Dust" has been winning film festivals all across America and will be release by 20th Century Fox early 2010. I have become more & more enamored with documentaries for their ability to let you breathe with and move with them as the story unfolds. You don't have that luxury with features.

I am particularly excited to release my documentary www.seekinghappilyeverafter.com. A project I feel very passionate about and know will be widely received as it sheds light on a social phenomenon!

Seven years ago I Founded www.indieproducer.net a website dedicated to supporting new and emerging filmmakers through information, networking events, screenwriting and short film contests, and now a social networking platform. Indieproducer is a site FOR filmmakers and film enthusiasts, BY filmmakers and film enthusiasts.

  • Producer/Owner

Credits include Agent Cody Banks 1 and 2, Mercy Streets, Perfect Romance, My Date with Drew, Like Dandelion Dust, Alcatraz, Miles Davis documentary, Expert Insight Instructional Video Series: Short Game Golf, Poker, Blackjack, Seeking Happily Ever After.

  • Founder

    www.Indieproducer.net

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    2001 — Present (8 years)

    Indieproducer.net (IP) is a free, social networking community that helps educate and support new and emerging filmmakers.

    With a current member base of over 10,000 filmmakers and film enthusiasts, and 60,000 via our affiliates; iP provides information, resources, entertainment news, interviews, education, and networking opportunities (IP Schmoozefests) that connect new & emerging filmmakers with established working professionals.

    iP has is currently running its seventh annual, Worldwide Screenwriting Contest and Short Film Contest (two of IP’s winning short films went on to become Oscar nominated shorts). IP's Screenwriting Contest was the first independent screenwriting contest to be sponsored and endorsed by the Writers Guild of America, west.

    iP has honored such notable filmmakers as Jon Favreau, Bill Paxton, Andy Garcia and Michael Rapaport at their annual iP Awards Gala held at the Writers Guild of America Theatre in Beverly Hills.
