Ilene Perlman’s Summary

I am a travel and editorial documentary photographer based in Boston. I have worked for a variety of Non Profit Organizations, Universities, Travel and Cultural Publications, Corporations, and companies with a need for online content. My website www.ileneperlman.com will show you the breadth and diversity of my skills and clients. As a people person I have the ability to both create realistic images for use in Annual Reports as well as to capture the unexpected for editorial stories. One week I might be shooting for Target's Community Awarenesss Campaign in Boston and the next flying to Spain to photograph an Elderhostel travel program for their marketing materials.

Ilene Perlman’s Specialties:

Digital and film photography. Medium and 35mm format, Documentary, Travel, Wedding, Public Relations, Editorial, and Fine Art Photography.
