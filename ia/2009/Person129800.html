Ken Donaldson’s Summary

I have been based in Tampa Bay offering counseling, coaching, and educational programs since 1987. I have a master’s degree in counseling; am a licensed mental health counselor; and am board certified in addictions counseling and clinical hypnotherapy. I am also a graduate of the Institute for Life Coach Training and the Relationship Coaching Institute.

As part of my practice I founded the REALationships Coaching programs. These programs provide coaching and education to help singles and couples live more fulfilling lives, and have the most intimate and lasting relationships possible. I offer a number of classes and workshops as part of this program.

I provide individual and couples therapy and coaching in my office, over the phone, and on-site, whichever is best suited for your needs. Also, I am available to speak to businesses and community organizations on a variety of personal growth topics

Ken Donaldson’s Specialties:

Psychotherapy for depression, anxiety, addiction, eating disorders, relationships and trauma resolution. Coaching and education for performance enhancement, small business, customer service and personl/relational growth and development.
