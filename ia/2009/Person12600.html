Anand Giddabasappa’s Summary

Presently a Scientist (In-vivo Pharmacology), at GTx Inc., a biopharmaceutical company at Memphis, TN, USA. I graduated with a PhD from Department of Biology and Biochemistry (Dr. Donald Fox's Lab), University of Houston. I have a strong background in pharmaceutical sciences, drug discovery process and basic biology. I have a Diploma, Bachelor's and a Master's degree in Pharmaceutical Sciences, with industrial experience in drug discovery at Ranbaxy Research Labs (India). I believe that a multidisciplinary acumen is essential in discovery and development of novel, safe drugs and targets.

My Goal is simple - "to do good science for better and safer therapeutics".

Some experience in bioinformatics and functional genomics.
