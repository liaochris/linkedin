Tong Thomas’s Summary

I began my BSc studies in 1990 and I continued to study until 2002 to get my PhD. Through out the years, I spent about 10 years time in academic developments. When Internet was new to the world in 2000, we developed a remote control video monitoring system which can work on construction site's adverse environments.

I started to do public policy related works in 2006 for a public engagement exercise for our central ferry pier area. I am now working on a forecasting model for construction manpower requirements in Hong Kong. My vision is to contribute my knowledge on modeling and analysis in the City's policies and public works. I believe the insights from scientific knowledge can help city achieve a sustainable future in long term.

I have managed many government-based and non-government-based consultancy projects with university professors

Example of the projects are:

Government-based projects:
- Provision of Services for the Participatory Programme for Enhancement of Central Ferry Piers (Nos. 1-8) and their Adjoining Areas. Client: Planning Department, HKSAR- April 2005 to February 2006
- Remote-control Site Video Monitoring System, Client:Housing Authority, 2000-2003

Public organization-based projects:
- Manpower Research for the Construction Industry of Hong Kong, Client: Construction Industry Council, 2008
- Comparing and Analyzing Construction Productivity and Costs in Hong Kong, Client: Hong Kong Construction Association Ltd

Private projects:
- Customer Satisfaction Survey: Client: Hsin Chong Construction Limited, 2001
- Remote-control Site Video Monitoring System, Client: Gammon Construction Limited, 2001
- Route 3 BOT Project Study: Client: Sun Hung Kei Properties, 1995
