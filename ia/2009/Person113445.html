Jim Stegall’s Summary

As Executive Vice President, Managed Properties for Kemper Sports, Jim Stegall is the senior operations executive for 80 of the KemperSports third party management properties. He is an 18-year industry veteran who has held various senior operations, sales and marketing positions at American Golf, Troon Golf and KemperSports. Prior to entering the golf industry, he was a senior project director for Xerox Learning Systems and Learning International, which provided training and consulting services for Fortune 500 companies in sales, management and service strategies.
