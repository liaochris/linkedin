Mark Spreckley’s Summary

Recruitment Business Owner; Red Creative Moves Marketing Ltd, in Nottingham, specialising in the recruitment of Creative, Marketing & PR personnel. I enjoy meeting my candidates and ensuring that I match their experience to the relevant roles.

An enthusiastic advocate of e-commerce and online marketing, contributing regular articles and comment to our business website. The website enjoys success in Google rankings, regularly achieving front page search rankings. http://www.red-creative-moves.co.uk

Prior to 2008, my major experience has been in the franchising industry for the last eight years, working within three franchised brands.

Some of the most enjoyable and satisfying work in my career has been working with franchisees in various Operational Management roles, National Sales Manager at Cartridge World, and Area Sales Manager / Business Development Manager at Venture Portraits.

Relationship Management is one of my passions, which I successfully implemented into a variety of business scenarios with different franchise business owners. They all presented their own challenges!

Prior to 1999, I spent many years working in the Financial Services industry, latterly for the Prudential as a Financial Consultant and prior to 1997 for fourteen years for NAAFI the trading arm of HM Forces. Here my roles were as Assistant UK Sales Manager and Customer Services (Credit) Manager.
