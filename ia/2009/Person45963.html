Chuck Sacco’s Summary

Chuck leads PhindMe Mobile, whose technologies provide designers and marketers with solutions to easily design comprehensive mobile-experiences. These solutions provide businesses with a powerful set of digital marketing processes needed to reach and engage with an increasingly mobile consumer.

Chuck's career includes a track-record of launching products and growing businesses in the fields of enterprise software, customer relationship management and mobile applications.

PhindMe Mobile is Chuck’s second startup, previously launching an enterprise technology consulting business. He grew the consulting and client development operations of that self-funded startup before the business was sold to what is now a public software company.

Just prior to PhindMe, Chuck led SoftBrands (now Infor) Evolution division in the Americas where he successfully launched new products including an innovative Business Process Management solution for mid-sized businesses. SoftBrands is an int'l ERP, CRM, and BPM company in the manufacturing, distribution, and hospitality industries.

Chuck’s has specific expertise in selling and implementing technology platforms with all size companies, helping them improve their bottom line through the effective use of technology. He has also led the setup of national and international partner programs, helping local companies leverage local market expertise.

With an MBA in technology management from Drexel University and a Beta Gamma Sigma honoree, Chuck has authored articles and speeches on mobile marketing and business process management.

He recently presented at Mobile Monday Mid-Atlantic, the NJ Technology Council Venture Conference, and the Search Marketing Expo Local/Mobile discussing how businesses can use mobile technologies.

Chuck also sits on the board of Mobile Monday Mid-Atlantic and sits on the Mobile Marketing Assoc.'s Commerce and Academic Outreach committees.
