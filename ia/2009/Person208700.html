Sandra Dingli’s Summary

Sandra has designed a number of academic programs at the University of Malta including a Masters Program in Creativity and Innovation.
She is coordinating an EU funded project under Erasmus Socrates to design a new joint Masters in Innovation and Foresight together with the University of Potsdam, Turku School of Economics and University of Teeside. This will be targeted at mid-career professionals and delivered mainly via e-learning.
She has been Directing The Edward de Bono Institute for the Design and Development of Thinking at the University of Malta since 1992 where she also lectueres to undergraduate and postgraduate students on creativity, innovation, foresight, thinking, the Edward de Bono methods, innovation management, philosophy of mind and philosophy of artificial intelligence.
Sandra has delivered workshops and presentations at a number of international conferences in Australia, New Zealand, the US, Malaysia, Singapore, England, Scotland, Ireland, Finland, Sweden, Denmark, Germany, Poland, Czech Republic and Malta.

Sandra Dingli’s Specialties:

Innovation Management, Innovation Audits, Foresight, Dissemination, Project Management, Creativity, Edward de Bono's methods, Education, Children's TV, Philosophy of Mind, Philosophy of AI.

Sandra has conducted her studies on a part-time basis as an adult and has extensive working experience in banking, industry, tourism, real estate, training and consultancy. She strongly believes in the importance of academia - industry links).
