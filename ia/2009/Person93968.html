Helio Gomes da Silva Jr’s Summary

In the last 22 years I have worked for multinationals, initially in Information Technology and later migrating to marketing departments, specializing in developing new products or partnerships, regarding electronic channels (telephone banking, telemarketing, Automatic Teller Machines, Internet) or telecommunications (Asymmetric Digital Subscriber Line - ADSL, leased lines, Voice over IP - VoIP, wireless). During the last seven years I have been overseeing new opportunities for Telefonica, where my primary goal was to the search and assemble new products and services for the small and medium companies market, as well as budget controlling and alignment of these new businesses with the other units in Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Colombia, Peru and Spain.
At this time I am developing Telefonica´s New Business Incubator that is the nursery for emerging and disruptive products for the company in Brazil.
