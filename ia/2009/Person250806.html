Yvonne Rodriguez’s Summary

Aquila Consulting Services, LLC is a boutique consulting firm specializing in providing back-office operations solutions, catering to small- to medium-sized asset management companies in the capital markets industry. With this specialized focus, Aquila can service clients more personally through Business Consulting Services, Operating Model Design, Systems Selections, Systems Implementations, and Project Management. Aquila's approach to each engagement involves providing an integrated and holistic view of operational processes and systems support in a timely manner.

Aquila's team consists of exceptionally skilled professionals, offering on average 15 years of hands-on back-office operations and investment systems experience. With extensive experience in operations, Aquila consultants know the difficulties their clients are facing first hand. Aquila works seamlessly with clients in assessing their operational needs, providing advice on "best practices," and, most importantly, successfully executing the implementation of client projects. With in-depth industry knowledge and operations expertise, Aquila allows clients to focus on their day-to-day responsibilities while Aquila experts get the job done.

Aquila's core competencies include: current state assessment and operating model re-engineering, including core accounting and partnership tax allocation; and financial statement reporting systems selection and implementations.

Yvonne Rodriguez’s Specialties:

Aquila's team of professionals have implemented and worked with several industry-leading core accounting and partnership allocation systems, and are today, particularly centered on the industry-leading Advent Geneva portfolio accounting system.
