Christina Tang’s Summary

Christina Tang is a senior-level marketing professional with a background in research, marketing, and procurement. Her role at Tang Marketing Group is to coordinate and direct national and local marketing and media efforts, including PR, creative, online marketing, advertising and marketing communications, and lead re-branding initiatives that include developing marketing communications for established companies that range from small business owners to Fortune 500 companies.

Prior to Tang Marketing Group, Christina was a market research analyst at Added Value. There she was responsible in aiding the development and management of various phases of the research process including coordination of field logistic and meeting the allocated budget. Christina's experience also stems from her Marketing Director position for Generations Model and Talent Agency in San Francisco where she was instrumental in talent pitching and placement with clients such as Macy’s, GAP, Pottery Barn, and for feature films such as, The Sweetest Thing. Before her role at Generations, Christina purchased and managed over $3 million dollars worth of electronic components for Dynamic Details. Her role at Dynamic Details allowed her the opportunity to manage 7-10 customer projects simultaneously, and work closely with customers, internal sales reps, and program managers that lead to the completion and satisfaction of projects.

Christina Tang’s Specialties:

Christina specializes in client liaison,PR, advertising, brand ambassador, marketing, procurement, project management, report preparation, and data analysis.
