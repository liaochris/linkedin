Ed Callahan’s Summary

I provide sales advisory and business services to the CEO, management team, advisory group or investors of growing businesses of any size, including well-funded start-ups, who want to grow their top line, profitably. My methodologies are industry and channel agnostic.

My sales advisory services are either as a part-time advisor to the CEO or as a part-time VP of Sales. I also provide various project oriented business services, including LinkedIn training for sales teams.

For a complete description of my practice, see www.coherentsalesconsulting.com.

Sales advisory clients typically retain me for a few days a month for a fixed period of months. The retainer contract is very simple. Either party can cancel with 30 days notice for any reason or no reason. During the first 30 days no notice is required. So your only risk is the amount of my first month's retainer.

Business service clients typically engage me for specific projects which are quoted on a project basis.

Here are just some of the ways I add value, no matter how we engage:
- Help you craft a repeatable and scalable sales process
- Help you build and monitor a reliable revenue pipeline
- Work with you to create a sales management methodology which assures accountability, scalability and increased sales productivity,
- Facilitate your assessment of your current sales team and help you make what corrections are necessary in sales plans, territories or quota assignments,
- Help you align your sales compensation plans and your company goals,
- Manage your sales force while I help you find a permanent sales chief,
- Teach your sales team how to utilize LinkedIn to develop prospects, develop alliances and penetrate large accounts.

Experience: Managed organizations ranging in size from two to two hundred and fifty people, across all functions . . . Managed every type of sales channel: direct, OEM, VAR, systems integrator, distributors, and independent agents.
