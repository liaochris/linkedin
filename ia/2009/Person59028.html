David Rubin’s Summary

Created DavidTravel, a respected leisure and corporate travel company, specializing in selling custom designed luxury travel products.

Again in 2008, David was awarded TRAVEL & LEISURE MAGAZINE's prestigious "Travel Experts" "A-LIST" status.

Our business strategy combines targeted marketing programs for high-end clientele, including consulting services to key businesses in the travel and hospitality industry. Online services include innovative online booking-engine technology providing access to top luxury and boutique properties not found on Expedia or Travelocity.
See: http://www.davidtravel.com/hotels_special_choices.html

DavidTravel is frequently praised by clients, the media (including Elite Traveler, the large, glossy magazine on most all private jets in the US), and in the travel industry as a leader in designing luxury travel with great attention to style, quality, value, and every detail of the experience.
See: http://www.davidtravel.com/media/

Please see the December 2008 issue of Conde Nast Traveler where DavidTravel is featured in the Dream Trips article with two examples of exceptional travel planning with unique access and connections.

David Rubin’s Specialties:
