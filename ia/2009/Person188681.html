    Rotterdam Climate Initiative

    (Renewables & Environment industry)

    October 2008 — Present (10 months)

    Improving the climate for the benefit of people, the environment, and the economy; that is the challenge confronted by the collective initiators; Port of Rotterdam, the City of Rotterdam, Deltalinqs, and DCMR Environmental Protection Agency Rijnmond. The Rotterdam Climate Initiative aims to achieve a fifty per cent reduction of CO2 emissions in 2025, adapt to climate change, and promote the economy in the Rotterdam region.

    One of the focal area's is sustainable mobility. With the biodiesel impulse project the RCI aims to stimulate the transition towards using renwable and sustainable fuels for transportation by putting together a consortium of market parties from the complete biodiesel value chain. The goal to start a project with 2 filling stations and 60 - 100 end users at the beginning of 2010.
