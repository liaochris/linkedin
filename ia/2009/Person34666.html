Bruce Engel’s Summary

Highly motivated and passionate project manager, consultant and recruiter with a broad experience of more than 15 years in Information Management, Information Technology and Services and a solid IT background. Course of life and career show perseverance and strength of will.

Both technical background and experience in designing en optimizing business processes. Highly able to understand concepts and apply logic combined with flexibility and pragmaticism to define tailor made solutions. Knows how to align business and IT.

Highly energetic and able to handle pressure. Relatively easy-going personality and amenable to most anything not violating personal principles. Upright, straightforward, assertive, with a great sense of humor. Able to easily adapt to environment, organization, people and situations. Prepared to take risks and dares to stick out its ring. Capable of working in a team as well as individual. Ability to motivate and stimulate people to perform beyond their ability.

Communicator. Communicates easily through the spoken and the written word. Charming and sociable, easily moving among people. Commercial attitude with great persuasive force at disposal.
