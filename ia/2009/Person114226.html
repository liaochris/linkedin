John Marchese’s Summary

Leadership, execution and results. I have a primary interest in bringing new products & companies to market and unleashing their potential and building shareholder value.

I have led management teams and sales (P&L) organizations that controlled over $1 Billion in assets and $250 Million in revenues as well as pre-revenue startups in early formation. (And nothing is harder than getting your first dollar of revenue)

Perhaps best known by people in the know for being one of a vocal handful who believed in the Citrix vision in the early 90's, and that lead me down a road of adventure, hard work, fun and accomplishment. Then there was this time about 5.5 years later when I joined Citrix tohelp launch a new internet business division focused on the promotion of the ASP (Application Service Provision) marketplace. What a ride...

Much has been accomplished, experienced and further developed thereafter in terms of channel development, worldwide business development, M&A, strategic partnering, IP creation, invention and growth.

Today, based on the strength and market power of the Millennial consumers (Gen-Y), coupled with the recession and changes in buying behavior, I have launched a lifestyle brands corporation that will serve the Gen-Y marketplace with authentic premium brands in beverage, apparel and merchandise categories. Millennium Prime has been in development since late 2008 and will launch in to the marketplace in the fall of 2009.

Millennium Prime: Superior lifestyle innovations for the next 1,000 years...
