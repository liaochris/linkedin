Mark Macey’s Summary

Nutritional Cleanse Coach, Motivational Speaker and Published Author. My focus is transfoming lives. One on one mentoring or training leaders about health, wellness and weight related issues. I have a focused corporate strategy for improving company health and wellness investments.
There are few arenas in America today where it is possible to make a greater, more meaningful impact than in the health and wellness of our country. Obesity has reached epidemic proportions in the United States. 2/3 of adult Americans are now overweight or obese. Excess weight is a direct contributor to diseases including diabetes, heart disease, arthritis, liver disease and even certain types of cancers. Obesity leads to symptoms of metabolic syndrome including elevated blood pressure, high cholesterol, insulin resistance, inflammation, blood fat disorders and arterial plaque. The principal role of the current medical system is to treat diseases rather prevent them, promoting costly "sick care" vs. "health care", relying on medications, surgeries and expensive procedures to cure symptoms. The solution to our national health crisis is prevention, education and personal empowerment...that has become my mission and passion.

We offer health coaching and mentoring to our clients, which include medical practices, corporate wellness managers, fitness centers, wellness providers as well as private individuals seeking to reach healthy weight and lifestyle goals for themselves, their patients and their employees. We utilizing a very simple, cost neutral and effective meal replacement program, coupled with close coaching support, to allow them to reach their ideal weight safely and rapidly and then teach them how to maintain that healthy weight and develop new habits of health for a longer life. We have created an opportunity to make a significant, positive impact on the health care system in this country and state of health in America.
