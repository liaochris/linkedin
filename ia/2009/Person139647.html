Lennie Rose’s Summary

Big Ooga is a quickly evolving community of accomplished entrepreneurs and professionals dedicated to building prosperous relationships, referring business to each other, mastering new skills and forming new inner circles across industry and specialty. We are actively forming alliances with other quality groups with the goal of keeping people working.

Members of Big Ooga are people whose clients already love them, have a stunning track record and are based in the USA. Our goals are to keep jobs in our communities, ignite new business, unite incredible people and make it easy for entrepreneurs and professionals to get found by the people who need them.

We also have a sense of humor. As witness here in Video explained: http://tinyurl.com/ms5672

Our programs excite, enrich and expand horizons socially and professionally.

I’d love to tell you this has been part of the Big Ooga strategy. But it has evolved naturally through the synergistic talents of our members and friends.

If you have read all of this and you're still here - I want you to know that Big Ooga will be proud to introduce you, to showcase your skills within the context of your expertise and invite you to participate in our development, programming and membership evolution. At Big Ooga you will be with professionals who inspire you as much as you do them.
