I have specialized in architecting, designing and building distributed Web systems in Java for nearly fifteen years. As a prolific coder who is fluent in J(2)EE systems and Open Source technologies, I have provided a broad range of software solutions to the business community. I bring strong communications skills, both written and verbal as well as management expertise to any organization I am involved with.
