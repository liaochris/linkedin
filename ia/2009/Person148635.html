Marc W. Halpert’s Summary

FASTER, SMARTER PAYMENTS IMPROVE CASH FLOW: We can help your organization receive payment by credit or debit card, or by electronic check, in any medium of communication used today: wireless data transmission, face-to-face, phone, fax, e-mail or internet. We eliminate the costly delays in paper-shuffling, in the mail, and at the bank.

KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE: Many of our services are regulated by the Federal Reserve Bank or the National Automated Clearinghouse Association. Expertise and up-to-date experience in these services are essential.

To be most valuable to our clients, we make recommendations drawing on our in-depth understanding of the specific application to prevent any regulatory or implementation issues, augmented by our past work experience in the trenches, so to speak.

CLIENT SERVICE DEDICATION: We have been complimented for our dedication to excellence in customer service, a rare trait in business today.
