Andrew Coate’s Summary

About Andrew Coate:
Andrew Coate is an Independent Multimedia Consultant from Chicago, IL who features a tireless dedication to and wide variety of industry knowledge, skills, and experience in Arts, Entertainment, and Media. He is the co-founder of Collaborate, a multifaceted organization dedicated to helping independent artists and musicians network and better promote themselves through live showcase events. He also produces events with Chicago Noise Machine – a collaboration of 9 Chicago-area bands formed in late 2008 – and recently founded Social What Now, a project geared toward consulting musicians and businesses in the use of Social Media and the Internet to build/grow a business. Coate owns and operates Coate Films – as a freelance video producer and editor – and Monday Morning Music – as a music talent manager and marketing consultant. In the past, Coate worked as the General Manager of the award-winning Radio 1190 KVCU in Boulder, CO, where he not only fine-tuned his diverse musical pallet, but also gained invaluable practice in all aspects of music business, including: booking, promotion, sponsorship, event planning, recording, copywriting, legal issues, financial management, and general management. Coate further developed his industry know-how through his general management, marketing and event planning positions at various Chicago arts and entertainment venues include North Beach Club Chicago, Vivid Nightclub, and the Lakeshore Theater; and through his studies at Columbia College Chicago, where he majored in Arts, Entertainment and Media Management, with an emphasis in Music Business and Performing Arts Management.
