Michael May’s Summary

Giving my clients amayzing service is my central promise and it is built upon my ability to understand what my clients want. And no, I don’t read minds or tell the future, instead I genuinely listen to what you have to say and we work together as a team to help you achieve your goals. No tricks, no gimmicks, just listening skills and honest work.

My experience, which includes being an investor, along with selling property of all kinds (residential, land, commercial, etc) gives me a complete understanding of how I can meet your needs as a client.

Over the years, I have also developed an understanding of financing, so I can answer most of the questions you may have about mortgages or loans. If for whatever reason I am not sure of the answer, I have a tight group of contacts, masters in their respective fields, to whom I can refer you. Whether you have never purchased a home before or you are experienced in real estate, I can cater my services to your needs.
