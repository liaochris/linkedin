Daan Bakboord’s Summary

I am currently working as an Oracle Business Intelligence Consultant for Scamander Solutions in the Netherlands. Our main goal is to 'liberate' data from complex Oracle systems. In my job I do all sorts of BI Projects all over the country. At the moment I am working with the traditional Oracle BI Tools (Oracle BI EE, Oracle Discoverer and Oracle Reports). Preferably in an Oracle E-Business Suite environment (mainly modules GL, AR, AP, INV, OM).

As one of the early adopters of the new Oracle Business Intelligence Suite Enterprise Edition within our company, I currently focus on OBIEE (Oracle BI EE) and Oracle BI Publisher (known as XML Publisher).

Next to it I have strong interests in Business Intelligence and Data Warehousing in general (CBIP certified ).
