Trish Gorring’s Summary

Ingress Online founder Trish Gorring has more than 10 years' commercial web site design/development experience in Australia and the UK. She has been involved in the delivery of some of the largest and most popular high profile web sites in the world.

Trish founded Ingress Online to enable her to do what she does best — devise web site solutions for clients. Working for large corporations, she became removed from the coalface of web site design and development and needed to get back to basics and make quality, simple, clean web sites that work.

Ingress Online works to a professional standard and has a dedicated work ethic building quality end-to-end cross browser web sites. Our work is quality checked which saves time, money and ongoing maintenance.

A web site is not an island, it is an integral part of your overall marketing strategy and, as such, needs to integrate with your existing marketing campaigns. When Ingress Online builds your web site, we think about the end result at every step of the project.

Trish Gorring’s Specialties:

Content Management System, easy to update websites, website development, user interface, marketing, brand and identity, cross browser web sites.
