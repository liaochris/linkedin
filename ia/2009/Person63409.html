Richard Brand’s Summary

Dick is an analytical, success-driven Marketing Professional with more than 20 years of experience in consumer research, project management, and branding strategy. His expertise lies in evaluating market research data (secondary or primary), and developing and conducting the implementation of marketing strategies. He has participated in dozens of marketing research projects, and has seen these projects through from beginning to end. His own research has resulted in 17 publications in academic journals and publications, proof of his ability to identify gaps in the knowledge base, utilize appropriate quantitative methodologies, and develop realistic and successful strategies that address the problems or opportunities made apparent in the research.

Dick has significant industrial experience. He has worked in sales and business development for three firms, most recently in the IT security software space. He called on public entities in Tallahassee, FL (ranging from state agencies to universities to school districts to cities and counties), generally dealing with C-Level executives and their Chief Information Officers and Information Security Managers. He has proven himself to be very adept in planning and carrying out campaigns to appeal to such customers.

As a member of marketing faculties for 15 years, Dick has taught and mentored thousands during his career, and has proven himself to be very adept at training. Dick is a natural and effective leader, and has helped to develop many successful graduates. He is an especially strong communicator and skilled writer with outstanding verbal skills.

Dick is currrently providing marketing consulting services to small- and medium-sized businesses in the Metro Atlanta area. He is helping these firms develop marketing plans and strategies, and is also helping them to implement the developed strategies.
