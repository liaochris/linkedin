Paul Martin Schwartz’s Summary

Paul is a seasoned business developer and Internet
technologist. He has over 13 years of experience in the
Electronics, IT and Telecommunications industries from
management and consulting positions in start-up, small,
medium and large business environments.
In the last 9 years Paul has gained in-depth knowledge in
the planning, deployment and operation of fixed and
wireless broadband access services. While he is a specialist
in Fiber-to-the-Home (FTTH) / Triple-Play networks, he
also has experience with mobile services, i.e. Cellular (GSM), WLAN and Mobile Satellite Service (MSS).
His track record includes business planning, business
modelling, design of network and OSS / BSS architecture,
product development as well as provisioning and delivery
of services and content.
From his various positions, Paul has project management
experience from running product development and roll-out
projects. He has a solid foundation in the disciplines of
planning a project, maintaining a budget and delivering
according to plan.
His first-hand experience from the Service Provider space
and his knowledge of value creating content services has
enabled him to work as a consultant, helping a leading
Danish power utility companies to build Equal Access
Networks (EAN).
His professional strengths lie in his focus on the customer.
Paul is a self-starter and enjoys working in a dynamic and
entrepreneurial environment.
