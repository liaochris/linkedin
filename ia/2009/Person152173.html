Mark Levy’s Summary

Toplinked.com
LION - Linkedin Open Networker - Feel free to send me an invite at mark@maxxomedia.com

I am the CEO/Founder of MaxxoMedia a digital media and entertainment business advisory providing access to key decision makers, digital content strategy, development, acquisition, licensing and distribution for media and technology companies around the globe.

A 20 year veteran of the entertainment, online and telecom industries, my experience prior to opening MaxxoMedia includes, overseeing the global operations and strategy for the Mobile division of Corbis, Bill Gates' image archive; Vice President Content at Moviso/Infospace Mobile one of the largest mobile content companies in North America, where I oversaw the global content licensing and publishing strategy; Prior to Infospace, I served as the GM/EVP of Enband - a Hewlett Packard funded broadband media company; was the EVP of Media Ventures Entertainment Group - a multi media company with holdings in film and TV music production, Internet services and music publishing owned by Academy Award Winning composer Hans Zimmer. In addition, I held various positions at Propaganda Films and Geffen Records as well as being a Music Supervisor, producing and licensing music for numerous feature films, television shows and commercials.

I have sourced and closed hundreds of content license and production deals with leading media companies including: Universal Music Group, Sony/BMG Records, EMI Recordings, and Warner Music Group; Walt Disney Company, ABC Television, CBS Television; Universal Pictures, Fox, Marvel Enterprises, Associated Press, Major League Baseball Advanced Media and many others.
