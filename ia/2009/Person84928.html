Gennefer Snowfield’s Summary

Experienced, results-focused interactive strategist, creative director, writer and idea generator with a successful track record of building and growing businesses – and brands – through website innovation and targeted communications, with an emphasis on behavior-driving tactics. Background combines extensive team leadership, business development, branding and all facets of on- and offline marketing across verticals. Proficient in web technology and user experience principles to drive the development of compelling, user-focused initiatives. Highly skilled at articulating strategy behind creative problem solving. Expert at translating business requirements into creative solutions. Knowledgeable in leading- and bleeding-edge applications and solutions. A visionary with thorough understanding of integrating brand, business and creative strategies with emerging trends and technologies.
