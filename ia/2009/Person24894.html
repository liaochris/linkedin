Bas Van De Haterd’s Summary

Trainer and consultant on implementing new media in recruitment. I help companies understand new employees (generation Y, Generation C, Millenials, whatever you like to call them) and teach them how they see the world and use the new media. I also teach companies the possibilities of new media, a good (digital) candidate experience. Using social media in an optimal way.

Bas Van De Haterd’s Specialties:

New media and cutting edge technology, translated into recruitment possibilities, online and offline.
