Leslie Mccain’s Summary

Leslie McCain is a project manager/planner assisting in the integration of a special divestiture project within Boeing. Currently leading in managing and tracking the Information Systems working with cross-functional teams to develop and execute an Integration Strategy. As the IT Focal it is her job to ensure applications, websites, fileshares, documents, and products are developed, integrated, and maintained for all Integrated Systems throughout the transition program.
Leslie, began her Boeing career in 1984, and has a broad range of experience, from Administrator for Vice-President of Sales and Marketing, to Systems Analyst, Engineering Tech-Aide, Technical Writer, and now as a Project Manager.
Having left the Boeing company in 1992 to pursue other venues, Leslie returned in 1996 to work as a Technical Writer on one of the largest business process redesign projects in the U.S.
Leslie grew up in the west (Wyoming, Montana, California, Colorado, and Arizona). Lived in the West Indies in 1974 and shortly after returning to the states moved to Seattle, Washington.
Leslie is the Mother of two grown sons and Grandmother of 5 granddaughters. Very active in the community, with memberships in the Lions Club, Sons of Italy, Good Neighbor, and other social/fraternal organizations. For one of the organizations currently serving as President, and national web site developer, and webmaster for over ten years.
Hobbies include genealogy, web development, relaxing, and sharing in life’s adventures. Enjoy nature and the simple things of life while keeping current with the rapid changes of the 21st Century.
Current goals include obtaining varying degrees, several certifications, to broaden frame-of-reference, meet others, and have fun while learning and contributing in all aspects of life. 2009
