Dale Williams’s Summary

With his 2008 appointment as COO of Pacific Trade International and Chesapeake Bay Candle Co, Dale brings a history of successfully developing new markets, customers and suppliers. His management accomplishments have been characterized by strong team-building, a deep insight into real-world opportunities for growth, efficient use of resources, and detailed execution. His entrepreneurial experiences have shaped his approach, which emphasizes practical, actionable solutions for his clients. Dale has thirty years of expertise in marketing, business development, manufacturing, sourcing and logistics, and operational management in both entrepreneurial and corporate environments. As President of Waverly Lifestyle Group, he led growth strategies that resulted in new national retail partnerships and subsequent sales growth. As CEO of HomePortfolio.com, he developed a business plan that raised $48 million in private equity funding and established the world’s largest search engine for residential consumer products, powering hundreds of corporate websites. Dale’s unique background includes building strategic partnerships with leading retailers and manufacturers in the United States, China, India, Europe, Mexico and South America. He understands the complex requirements of today’s international supply chain and brings years of strategic planning and facilitation skills to bear on client projects at every level.

Dale Williams’s Specialties:

Organizational development and strategic planning, product development, brand management, licensing, international sourcing, sales management for home decor products.
