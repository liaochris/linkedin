Federico Pascarella’s Summary

Taniemusic: Ideas Innovation Insight Imagination.

In the 21st Century, ideas and innovation have never held more potential for driving successful commercial enterprise and, through expert application and composition, become the key to translating business potential into market returns. Contemporary advertising and media output must correlate to increasingly media-literate consumer marketplaces whilst also evolving alongside budding online and digital media. Insight and imagination are thus a palpable currency in this new media landscape, allowing branding initiatives to transcend standardised forms of media and consumer interaction.
 
Taniemusic is a music strategy consultancy that unites music and media to forge new avenues of advertising and media exposition, maximising branding potentialities through the power of sound design and original music composition - a leading agency in the burgeoning advertising techniques of sonic branding. Taniemusic’s ethic is versatility; founded on an inventiveness and originality to effectively encapsulate any brand identity through connection with one of the brain’s most evocative memory senses: sound. The unparalleled range of sonic branding offered by Taniemusic encompasses the full spectrum of music and sonic soundscapes and can be tapered to produce a range of compositions that fit exactly to the parameters of your brand.
