Peter Spinella’s Summary

Commercial Real estate consulting and Brokerage.
Lic Commercial Real Estate Broker in Florida and Rhode Island.
Head of Spinnaker's Commercial Real Estate Division from our South County Commons office. I am also the broker for the Florida office as well as overseeing the Commercial activities companywide. Prior to joining Spinnaker Group, I was in charge of a Commercial Real Estate team for Prudential in Florida. We were the top 3% of Prudential agents in the country and number one in the state of Florida. My team and I received the coveted CoStar award for top leasing brokerage in southern Florida.

I have extensive experience from relocating small businesses to new retail sites, to negotiating the purchase of multiple shopping centers for a single client. He has consulted on transactions in excess of two hundred and fifty million dollars involving thousands of acres for development.

I have worked with or for national and local clients including Harley Davidson, RH Donnelly, US Homes, K. Hovnanian, Lennar, WCI developers, Benderson Development, Morris Developers, CVS, Walgreens and Outback Steak House.

I am a contract broker for some national lending institutions. I dispose of the foreclosed assets, I manage foreclosed assets and I rehab foreclosed assets for the lenders.

As the Markets have changed Spinnaker has also we are now one of the Top Commercial REO firms in Rhode Island.
