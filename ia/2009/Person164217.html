Mike Estey’s Summary

Mike Estey founded Technology Solutions, Inc. (TSI Rep Firm) in 2002 and today is the primary point man for all of TSI Rep Firm's manufacturer's. He works closely with each manufacturer to help develop their sales and marketing strategies. He is also very active in the field working the key account relationships. Mike is also responsible for the management of TSI's national distribution relationships. Mike has worked closely with these accounts for the past 8 years, and has been continually recognized for his achievements including Sales Rep of the Year awards from companies such as Altec Lansing in 2004 and 2003 and Sales Rep of the Year awards in 2002 and 2001 while with the TAMCO Mfg Rep Group. Mike's aggressive approach to distribution goes beyond the tactical management of TSI's vendor's products, he engages all areas of the distribution partner's organization to make sure each TSI vendor is successful. Mike has more than 20 years experience as a Mfg. Rep and 15 years in the personal computer industry and has developed key relationships with all of the top accounts in the territory. Mike is committed to providing his customers with the best service, support, and continuing education in a constantly changing marketplace.
