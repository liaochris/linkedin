Joseph L. Bongiovi’s Summary

Joseph Bongiovi began his career working in motion picture production, working on over 250 music videos, dozens of commercials and various feature films. In 1989 Joseph made a change and took on a position at Great Entertainment Merchandise, a division of PolyGram. As Manager of Tour Production, Joseph was responsible for supervising all aspects of tour merchandising.

In 1993, Joseph took a position at newly formed Sony Signatures. Over 7 yeas he worked as Senior Manager of Artist Relations, Director of Licensing and Marketing and Vice President of Licensing and Marketing. As VP Joseph was responsible for all global entertainment licensing programs. He developed worldwide licensing strategies for such talent as: KISS, The Beatles, Ozzy Osbourne, Yellow Submarine, Britney Spears, Michael Jackson, Celine Dion and Madonna.

In mid-2000, Joseph took on the position of Vice President, Tour Sponsorships and Marketing for Clear Channel Entertainment. He was responsible for overseeing the tour sponsorship sales division. Joseph created brand integration strategies for some of America’s largest companies including Verizon Wireless, Miller, Kodak, Volkswagen, Polaroid and Samsung Wireless.

By 2002, Joseph branching out on his own and launched Monarch Entertainment Group, Inc (MEG).

In October of 2005 Joseph teamed up with a few veterans from the entertainment merchandising and licensing business to form Brothers Entertainment Merchandising (BEM).
