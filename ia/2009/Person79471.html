Daniel Firges’s Summary

Daniel Firges joined Boehringer Ingelheim Belgium in 2004, and worked as Head of Business Intelligence, CRM, and Customer Service. He has recently been promoted to the position of Global Project Leader Marketing & Sales Effectiveness. Daniel is a Belgian national with a professional background in Marketing & Sales Consulting. He has previously worked as a Senior Consultant at Andersen and Deloitte Consulting in a wide array of industries and countries. Beside speaking several languages, Daniel holds an MBA in "International General Management" and a bachelor degree in Business Administration, with a major in marketing.
