Richard Brandwein’s Experience

  • CEO and Chairman

    PlanetConnect

    (Education Management industry)

    January 1994 — Present (15 years 11 months)

    PlanetConnect has over 15 years of experience in producing custom research events in the Pharma, Telecom, and Financial sectors. These events bring up to 2000 internal R&D staff to a private 1 - 4 day event, where attendees interact in a secure, proprietary environment at an outside hotel/resort.

    In addition to a full schedule of talks, poster sessions, BOFs and networking events, PlanetConnect provides well-known, respected outside speakers and up to 200 approved exhibitors. PlanetConnect has over 6000 partner/customer companies in it's portfolio.

    PlanetConnect has a unique set of networking tools and devices for large research events, including iPhone platforms and social networking tools.

    PlanetConnect has and has had a number of active subsidiaries, such as "Event-IT", which wired and provided Internet access for years in a number of NJ area hotels such as the Hyatt Regency New Brunswick and Princeton Marriott; "The Browser", a technical bookstore in Red Bank; and "PCI Pharma", producing a popular public Life Science event series.

    Provide business development and technical project development to a wide range of companies, such as Deutsche Bank (Single Sign On enterprise development and product spinoffs); managed ten contract developers provided to Lucent Technologies for a number of forward looking wireless broadband development projects. Sit on Boards and provide funds for a number of ventures and growing companies.
