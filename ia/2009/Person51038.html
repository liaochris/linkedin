Chris Selland’s Summary

Chris Selland joined JAZD Markets, the leader in online B2B directory-driven Marketing as a Service (MaaS) platforms, in April 2009 to focus on building out JAZD Markets’ industry-focused business and content strategies in partnership with leading B2B Publishers and trade associations.

Mr. Selland is also a member of the Board of Snow Beverages, a manufacturer of uniquely refreshing, natural, sparkling beverages.

Prior to JAZD Markets, Mr. Selland was Vice President of Marketing & Business Development at EasyLobby, a leading provider of Secure Visitor Management systems, and Vice President of Business Development at SoundBite Communications, where he developed and managed SoundBite's key business and corporate development initiatives through the company's IPO in October 2007, and spearheaded the acquisition of Mobile Collect in February 2008.

Earlier in his career, he founded Reservoir Partners L.P., a CRM/enterprise software advisory & consulting firm in 2001, which merged in 2004 with Aberdeen Group (acquired by Harte-Hanks) where he served as Vice President of Sell-Side Research. Mr. Selland also served as an M&A/market advisor for Covington Associates, an Investment Bank specializing in Mergers & Acquisitions in the enterprise software and services industries. He also served as VP of Marketing at eSupportNow and as Vice President of e-Business Strategies at the Yankee Group, a technology research and consulting firm which was acquired by Reuters. Mr. Selland has also held senior sales and marketing positions at Primix Solutions, ON Technology, Computer Associates and Ingersoll-Rand.

Mr. Selland is past President of the Customer Relationship Management Association (CRMA). In addition, he has been frequently quoted in major business and industry publications, and is a past member of the CRM Magazine Editorial Advisory Board. He is an accomplished public speaker and presenter, having chaired and spoken at numerous leading industry conferences.

Chris Selland’s Specialties:

Strategic Alliance Development. Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) and Capital Raising. Technology Marketing and Positioning. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) services & strategies.
