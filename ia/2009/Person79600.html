Francisco J. Mata’s Summary

MIS and ICT specialist with experience in academic and international development organizations, including the Universidad Nacional de Costa Rica, the Universidad de Costa Rica, the UN-mandated University for Peace, the Earth Council, the Tropical Agriculture Research and Training Center (CATIE), and the Inter-American Institute for Cooperation on Agriculture (IICA). Consultant and Researcher for the Costa Rican Association of ICT Companies (CAMTIC)/Costa Rican Association of Software Producers (CAPROSOFT) in projects funded by the Inter-American Development Bank and the International Development Research Centre (IDRC).

Collaborator in international projects with the International Development Research Centre (Canada), the International Institute for Sustainable Development (Canada), the Stockholm Environment Institute (Sweden), the World Resources Institute (USA), the United Nations Development Programme (UNEP), and the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP).

Has published articles in MIS Quarterly, the Journal of Strategic Information Systems, the ACM-SIGMOD International Conference on Management of Data, the ICCC Conference on Computer Communcation for Developing Countries, ASEE Global Colloquium on Engineering Education, the Global Network for the Economics of Learning, Innovation, and Competence Building Systems (GLOBELICS), the International Symposium on Forecasting, the Ibero-American Congress on Higher Education in Computing (CIESC-CLEI), the Ibero-American Congress on Educational Informatics (RIBIE), and the Chilean Conference on Computer Sciences.

Francisco J. Mata’s Specialties:

IT strategy, IT Management, E-Commerce, E-Business, Data Mining, Web applications, E-Development, ICT Education
