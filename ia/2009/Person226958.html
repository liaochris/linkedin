Susan A. Enns’s Summary

As Managing Partner of B2B Sales Connections, I provide revenue-generating consulting services to sales organizations and sales professionals in the business to business marketplace, including the operation of Canada’s premier niche job board and career website for business to business sales professionals.

I have written the downloadable ecourses "Action Plan For Sales Success" and "Action Plan For Sales Management Success", and as the B2B Sales Coach, I write and edit the company’s newsletter, AIM HIGHER. Participating on the Leadership Executive of the Sales Professionals of Ottawa since 2008, I have served as Marketing Lead, and am currently the association's Vice President.

With over 22 years of successful direct sales, management and executive level business to business experience, I have excelled and been recognized in all areas of my personal and professional endeavors.
