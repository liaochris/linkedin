William Sell’s Summary

Currently Managing Partner of Advisor Communications, a Framingham, MA-based agency for brand, public relations, and marketing strategy development with specialties in small business and the event and face-to-face marketplace. Also working full time at CrossTech Media Inc., of Canton, MA as Vice President, focused on marketing and event development.

Most recently VP & General Manager of VON conferences and Publisher of VON Magazine, and before that VP Brand & Customer Development for CeBIT America, a new launch (2003) trade fair for enterprise computing professionals; part of the original business plan development and launch team.

Past experience includes seven years as VP/General Manager of COMDEX, including running the COMDEX Fall event in Las Vegas. Also 15 months with World Trade Center Boston as head of event development (a Fidelity Investments/Fidelity Capital company); and 15 years with International Data Group/IDG World Expo.

Seeking organizations who can benefit from a fresh look at their face-to-face marketing and public relations programs; business opportunities with events in any industry.

William Sell’s Specialties:

Business-to-business marketing with specialty in lead generation, business development; trade show development and management; public relations; content/conference development; direct marketing, face-to-face marketing
