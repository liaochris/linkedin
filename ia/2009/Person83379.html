Gareth Edwards’s Summary

Enjoying working with the National B2B Centre (in a variety of guises) to both help the organisation promote itself as an eBusiness centre of excellence in the West Midlands and to work with regional SMEs providing eMarketing advice.

Also a Director of VHQ Solutions Ltd, which provides web-based business applications to small and medium sized businesses and support organisations

And continuing to provide eMarketing consultancy to businesses across the West Midlands and beyond through my Arrowsmith Marketing business.

Since September 2005 I have been a volunteer business adviser with Young Enterprise and supported a number of companies based at King Edward VI school in Stourbridge UK. In 2008-2009 it is the turn of Eco-Oce, who are designing, making and selling eco-friendly bags.

I have had more than 20 years experience in the IT and Telecoms industry initially in a technical capacity before moving into sales and marketing, with a variety of roles in product management, marketing management, and business development.

My main goal is to use my corporate and consulting experience to help clients move their businesses forward.

Gareth Edwards’s Specialties:

Gareth helps clients maximise the strength of their “value propositions” in order to achieve increased sales and greater profitability.
He pioneered the “Pains > Issues > Drivers” methodology which identifies what customers want from products and services, and from their providers, and then improves the client’s overall offer to market.
