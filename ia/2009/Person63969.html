Dimitrios Papaioannou’s Summary

Until recently, Dimitrios was General Manager of CIVITAS-Ketchum (www.civitas.gr & www.ketchum.com), the leading strategy, communications and public affairs firm in SE Europe; the firm represents several technology brands, including: Intel, EMC, Atos Origin, Ericsson, Epson, Siemens and the World Wide Web consortium (past and present clients). In FY04, Dimitrios increased sales from 4 million Euros to 11 million Euros, increased available operating capital by 30% and reduced operating expenses by 10%.

Dimitrios offers over twenty years of experience in general management, operations, marketing & sales, research & development, consulting and venture capital; he is alumnus of Silicon Valley, member of the Churchill Club and veteran of several technology-based firms, including: Philips Research (Netherlands), Philips Semiconductors (Netherlands), Fairchild Semiconductor (Portland, ME), SAP (Greece), PRTM (Mountain View, CA) and Osprey Ventures (Palo Alto, CA).

He is currently board-level advisor to two stealth-mode startups in Greece and in the past to i-cube – a leading incubator in the region.

Dimitrios is principal inventor of 18 patents in medical optical imaging and location-based services; he holds a Ph.D. in Laser Physics from the University of Virginia and an M.B.A. from Cornell.
