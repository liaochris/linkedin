Chris Jupin’s Summary

My primary experience has been at Hewitt Associates where I have held both the roles of Setup Configuration Analyst and my current role as a Systems Analyst. Presently, I am responsible for analyzing client and legal requirements, implementing changes, and overseeing testing of the various benefits administration systems. I have over three years of experience with developing and testing Hewitt's proprietary web, graphical user interface, and interactive voice response systems. I have worked extensively in implementing projects for many notable clients including The Home Depot/HD Supply, Northrop Grumman, The Coca-Cola Company, Unum Provident, and Triad Hospitals.

Although my resume shows my academic record and professional career it fails to represent my character. I am a conceptual thinker, a generator of creative ideas, and a self-starter. I am highly motivated and adaptable to changing and challenging environments.
