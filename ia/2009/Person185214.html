Pete Herrera’s Summary

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

October 2005 — Present
Princeton Information is one of the nation's largest privately-held IT consulting firms, providing a clientele of primarily Fortune 500 companies with a full range of staffing, services and solutions. With annual revenues over $140 million, Princeton Information operates across the US with 12 regional offices.

We offer a wide range of IT expertise including application development, software quality assurance, database administration, business analysis, network engineering, and network support.

PI's success is grounded in the relationships we build with our consultants. We seek the best people; provide career path counseling; as well as the most challenging opportunities in business IT. As part of our culture of loyalty and commitment to our consultants, Princeton Information is committed to promoting consultants to management positions and developing all our people to their highest potential.
