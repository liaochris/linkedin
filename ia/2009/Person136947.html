Larry Butler’s Summary

I am a very motivated individual with over 16 years in the product manufacturing, services and fulfilment industry. I have spent most of my years in the quality and operations managment functions within the electronics manufacturing and services industries as well as medical device/biotech and RFID industries.

I am a dedicated leader and team builder. I have a proven track record of building successful teams to turn around an operation that may be going in the wrong direction. I have been a six sigma teams trainer and champion. I truly believe in reducing wastes and streamlining processes in business through lean enterprise concepts. Through my vast background and experience I bring a 'steady hand' to any business process and add value to operation as I am not afraid of a challenge.
