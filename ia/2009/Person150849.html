Mario J. Frabizzio Jr.’s Summary

I am a licensed clinical psychologist with decades of therapy experience. I began practicing clinical psychology in 1975 and have been licensed since 1981. My experience initially focused on pediatric and child clinical areas but has included the treatment of adults over the years. I have practiced in Child Guidance Centers, Community Mental Health Centers, Community Adult Service Agencies, Children’s Hospitals, Schools, and Colleges. This private practice is a general practice which offers a variety of treatment modalities including Individual (child and adult), Family, Couples, and Group therapies; School Consultation; and a full array of Psychological testing, including Psycho-educational batteries for School-age Children; ADHD Testing for Children Kindergarten through College; Intelligence Testing for Gifted Children, and Projective/Personality Testing of Children, Adolescents, and Adults. The major focus of my practice is the patient’s well being and emotional health. As such, treatment approaches and plans are discussed from this perspective.

Mario J. Frabizzio Jr.’s Specialties:

Specialty areas include Psycho-educational, ADHD, and Projective Psychological Testing; Pediatric Therapy (5 years of age and under); Therapy for spinal-cord injured patients; Hypnosis, and Treatment of twins and families of twins.
