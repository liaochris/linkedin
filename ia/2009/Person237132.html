Ross Blankenship’s Summary

Ross Blankenship is an admissions expert and founder of TopTestPrep.com. Ross attended the NMH School, Cornell University and Washington University School of Law. He has trained at the Harvard Business School and was appointed to the Harvard Venture in Management Program Board In 2004.

While at Cornell, Ross worked with several nonprofits and helped many students get into top colleges. He assisted with Cornell recruiting and admissions and is an active member of the Cornell Alumni Association, serving as former Vice President of the Cornell Annual Fund. Ross also was elected to Cornell's Educational Policy Committee and Academic Integrity Hearing Board, where he helped develop courses and maintained student governance.

He has been published in several magazines and journals, including Next Step Magazine, St. Louis Business Journal, Education.com, and Education Source.
