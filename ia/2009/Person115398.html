Joseph Cavallaro’s Summary

World Championship Fighting® is the North East Area’s leading mixed martial arts sports association, formed in June, 2006 by Joseph Cavallaro and Associates, LLC.
World Championship Fighting features a strong ownership and management team with a depth of live event management experience that spans the last 15 years.


WHAT IS MIXED MARTIAL ARTS?

Mixed Martial Arts ( MMA ) is an evolving sport in which competitors use interdisciplinary forms of fighting that include jiu-jitsu, judo, karate, boxing, kickboxing, wrestling and others to their strategic and tactical advantage in a supervised match.

The emergence of Mixed Martial Arts has become a worldwide story, including a featured segment on CBS' "60 Minutes" news magazine program and feature pieces throughout the newspaper and magazine industry. As stated by "60 Minutes," "MMA is the fastest growing sport in the country, appealing to the coveted demographic that advertisers have been losing steadily -- males ages 18 to 34."

MMA competitors are among the best-trained and conditioned athletes in the world. Fighter safety is of paramount concern to World Championship Fighting ownership and management.
