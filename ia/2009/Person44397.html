Christian Antonio Mejía Acosta’s Summary

I have a degree in Communications and Media from the Instituto Tecnológico de Monterrey ITESM-Mexico, and a Master’s Degree in Business Administration MBA at CENTRUM Business School-Universidad Católica del Perú.
My twelve year-work experience has been focused on Communications for Development, both at international and national level with emphasis on developing countries. I was involved in campaigns and multi-media production for UNICEF and other UN agencies, Red Cross and the European Commission in Rwanda, Djibouti, Ecuador, Peru and Mexico. I have expertise in producing broadcast video, delivering effective messages through photography, as well as writing skills both in English and Spanish. I also worked as a “one-man crew” Field Producer for UNICEF Headquarters in New York, where I was in charge of pre-production, camerawork as well as post-production and editing. Produced public service announcements and held international media relations for information transfer.
In terms of additional skills, I have good verbal communication and I am familiar with the business and communications’ jargon, in English, Spanish and French.
