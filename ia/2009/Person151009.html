Marisela Riveros’s Summary

Award-winning Television and Radio Producer.
A strong relationship builder, networker and collaborator to drive results in a fast paced environment empowering and energizing others. Creative, versatile multitasker with a keen eye for detail, recognized for having strong organization skills, storytelling abilities, and a production problem solver who builds solid teams that get high-quality results. All projects are completed within deadline and allocated budget.

Secured interviews with newsmakers and personalities of the financial world and entertainment industry.
Coordinated live coverage of international events.
Produced 2 award winning series, both winners of the first and second place of the New York National Association of Black Journalists Best Business Radio Award.

While in college, interned at Cutting Edge Entertainment, where I assisted in the production of Emmy-winner shows, such as the documentary 'Orozco in Greengoland'/PBS, and the Hispanic entertainment show 'Viva!'/WPIX; then Channel 11 WB, New York. Also, interned at CNN en Español in New York City, where assisted in the production of daily news packages.

During my school years at Montclair State University, acted as Multicultural Director and Operations manager for WMSC 90.3FM.
Built a relationship with major record labels, trained several DJs, and hosted own weekly show "Latinos in the House".
In my weekly radio show, played a variety of music like Latin House, Salsa and contemporary Spanish-Rock. Interviewed prominent Latin American artists such as El General, Aterciopelados and the legendary Argentinean Spanish-Rock band, Los Enanitos Verdes.

Marisela Riveros’s Experience

  • Senior TV Producer

    Bloomberg Television

    (Privately Held; Financial Services industry)

    July 2000 — February 2009 (8 years 8 months)

    In the Bloomberg Latin American channel, Riveros acted as the head Producer/writer for Intercambio Bloomberg, a 30-minute interview show of in-depth discussion of current market and economic issues. She secured high-profile interviews for both Intercambio Bloomberg and Reporte Financiero Bloomberg, with newsmakers such as Algerian Energy and Mines Minister/OPEC President, Shakib Khelil, London Metal Exchange CEO, Martin Abbott, U.S. Secretary of Commerce, Carlos Gutierrez, Senator Bob Menendez, Azteca America, CEO, Luis Echarte, among other personalities of the financial world.
    For Reporte Financiero, Marisela also acted as on-air reporter, presenting Latin American and commodities news.

    As Field Producer, Riveros coordinated the live-television coverage for multiple Bloomberg TV Channels of international events such as, The Americas Summit, OPEC Extraordinary Meeting, World Copper Conference, World Bank and IMF Fall Meetings and MERCOSUR (Common Market of the South).

    Previously obtaining this position, Riveros was part of the Radio and Television Affiliate Network Department.
    Before moving to the Radio and Television Affiliate Network Department, Riveros worked as a Satellite Feeds Coordinator and Master Control Operator for Bloomberg Television-US Broadcast Operations Department. During this time, she worked closely with the editorial and engineering departments trafficking satellite and fiber optic feeds, as well as monitoring the local, national and international air-chains.
