David Winterbottom’s Summary

I am a web application developer with 5 years of experience of object-oriented PHP as well as an advanced knowledge of Ruby, Perl, Bash, AJAX and advanced Javascript. I am experienced with server administration including configuring Apache and administering databases such as MySQL, SQLite and PostgreSQL. I am expert in several PHP frameworks including the Zend Framework and Code Igniter, and am proficient with development tools such as PHPUnit and Phing.

In addition, I have a PhD in Applied Mathematics and gained the top graduating mark in my undergraduate year at Nottingham University. I have extensive experience in mathematical modelling and numerical simulation (gained both at university and whilst working at the Treasury). I have published articles in international peer-reviewed journals, written prize-winning web sites and given talks at international conferences.
