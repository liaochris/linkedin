Richard L Goerwitz III’s Summary

I've done everything from data warehouse architecture, to Unix systems administration, to web programming and Apache administration. And I've deployed everything from tiny one-off programs in C to enterprise provisioning and identity management systems. I have no grand plan other than to try to be a good guy, serve my current employer, and help them forge ahead on the IT front. My latest charge has been developing and deploying my employer's first data warehouse (a role I share, as co-lead, with a trusted peer).
