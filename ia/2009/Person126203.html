Karen Denovich (Collinsgru)’s Summary

As president of The Write Stuff, I specialize in creatively distinctive marketing copy or words for marketing materials that set my clients apart. The Write Stuff has managed the marketing programs for two non-profits, bringing both from virtual obscurity to real prominence in their communities. From builder/developers to mechanical engineers to linen rental companies, I step into the individual client's world seeking the real benefits for their target market. Whether a company is looking for one piece to add to their marketing materials or several aspects of their campaign, I can work by the project, by the hour or on a monthly retainer basis on their specific needs.

Marketing Action Plan – A written plan that defines product, customers, plan and budget. The plan identifies features and benefits to be promoted. Target market is researched and analyzed to determine the most effective media. Timeframes and priorities are outlined.

Web Site/Brochure Copy – Words for websites/brochures providing information on the benefits of client’s product or service for presentation to the target market.

Press Releases – Newsworthy press releases for distribution to media list. Telephone contact is made to ascertain publication date. Acting as media liaison to facilitate the process, she ensures that reporters receive all critical information.

Public Relations – Representation at meetings/events to gain exposure through speaking engagements, networking and interacting with media and decision makers.

Turnkey Services – Project Management to contract and manage creative services, such as photography, web development & hosting.

Clients in the market for a more comprehensive program, who need extensive work to initiate the process, can choose a monthly or annual contract, determined by the client’s priorities and budget.
