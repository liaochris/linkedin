Mike Henry Sr.’s Summary

My passion is to help people exercise leadership from any position in any company. Every business needs more leaders in every position to compete and grow in our current economy. I have experience in several different industries and several different functional areas. Sound leadership fundamentals help you deliver value in every circumstance.

Areas of specialty:
Leadership Development
Change Management.
Consulting

Leadership Development
I developed the "Leader From The Pack" Curriculum designed to develop management agility in new and growing leaders. The training teaches leadership in four dimensions, your team, your peers, your suppliers, and your leaders.

Change Management
Make Change Happen in your business. I serve as a change catalyst, helping you define, motivate, and implement positive change in your organization. I've worked in financial services, telecom, transportation, non-profit and local government groups to implement changes, change management systems, project management and case management systems. In every case, I develop the leaders necessary to sustain and grow the company's change culture going forward.

Consulting
I have experience implementing technology projects and process transformation projects in organizations ranging in size from 50 people to the Fortune 500 with budgets of a few thousand dollars to over $12 million. Please contact me for a free consultation to discuss any internal change initiative.
