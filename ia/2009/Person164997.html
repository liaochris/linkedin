Mike Truesdale’s Summary

Mike Truesdale is known by most to wear many hats at any place he finds himself. Prior to his current position at Yellow Communications, Inc. he had been almost exclusively in sales; before, during & after obtaining his Bachelor's Degree in Marketing from California Lutheran University in 2006.

What's more is that the majority of sales were in industries that did not exist even 20 years ago, some even as recently as 5 years ago. That experience has given him a great knowledge on areas of Marketing and Advertising that was not yet in his college textbooks, such as the areas of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) & Social Media Optimization (SMO).

Copywriting is both Mike's preference and specialty. Just having started his career in copywriting, Mike is currently doing web copy and is available for freelance work in copy and editing as well. Eventually, he will work as a Junior Copywriter where he will hopefully make the move into Commercial Copywriting for Television & Radio.

Mike's background as a Stand-up Comic/Improv Comic & Comedy writer makes him the ideal copywriter for TV and Radio. He has been performing stand-up comedy in the greater LA area since the age of 22, gracing the stages of such popular nightclubs as the HaHa Cafe and the world famous Hollywood Improv. He also has spent the past 2 years training with the renowned Groundlings Theater in Los Angeles, which boasts such famous alumni as Will Ferrell, Jon Lovitz, Phil Hartman, Will Forte, Kathy Griffin, Lisa Kudrow, Cheri Oteri & many more. Mike also enjoys writing screenplays and sketch comedy which are indicative of the kind of creativity he has to offer.

To reach Mike for any questions, consultations or opportunities e-mail him at miketruesdale@hotmail.com
