Geoff Scott’s Summary

Technology leader with a track record of bringing winning client-facing software solutions to market and delivering bottom-line results. Experience with e-business, e-commerce, e-learning, advertising, direct marketing, email marketing, CRM, content management, and application integration initiatives.

Career Highlights
• Built an application development department for The Princeton Review. Delivered a 220% improvement in user satisfaction, double-digit improvements in operating efficiency, and an estimated $10 million in incremental sales.
• Lead the formation of the K-12 Services Division for The Princeton Review. Brought the company into the K-12 market, played a key role in its $59.4 million IPO, and now represents 10% of the company's total revenue.
• Pioneered the Internet application service provider model. Created a managed e-Commerce and electronic software distribution service offering for UniDirect.
• Developed some of the earliest Web-based CRM, content management, and e-Business applications. Customers included IDG Books and UniDirect.
• Co-founded The Internet Connection and served on the board of directors.
