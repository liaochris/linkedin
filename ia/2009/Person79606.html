Friedrich-Joachim Sack’s Summary

Highly experienced executive with over ten years of successful transaction / investment work as an Investment Director for a German PE house (in total around 500m Euro combined AUM) and as an in-house Senior Project Manager M&A for a DAX 30 quoted multinational corporation, respectively.

Projects carried out in Europe (e.g. Germany, UK, Spain, Turkey, Finland), Australia, Asia (e.g. India, Hong-Kong), Africa (e.g. South-Africa, Egypt) and the Americas (USA, Canada and Mexico). Experience comprises acquisitions, disposals and mergers of private and public companies (with the main focus on SME’s and an enterprise value range of 20-300m Euro; larger deals up to approx. 3bn Euro), foundation of joint-ventures / new-ventures / (commercial) strategic alliances.

Certain focus on SME verticals (and respective engegaments) belonging to the Leisure, Tourism and Hospitality sector (e.g. tour operators, hotels, airlines, DMC, corporate travel, travel technology etc.). Access to many CXO level senior executives worldwide.

Barrister-at-law with an additional degree in business administration / economics, former professional experience includes strategy consulting and accounting. 4-months intern in the German-Saudiarabian Liaison office for Economic Affairs, Riyadh / Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Friedrich-Joachim Sack’s Specialties:

Cross-boarder deals (international buy-and-build-strategies), contract analysis and negotiation, supervision of cross-cultural transaction teams, strategy assessment, specialist sector expertise: leisure and corporate travel (incl. cruise lines and specialist tour operators) / hospitiality / aviation, TMT, medtech and building engineering.
