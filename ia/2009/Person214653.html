Company Profile

AIT Global, Inc. is a Software Development & Consulting firm, headquartered in New Jersey. We specialize in providing innovative and highly skilled IT consultants who have the expertise needed to assist our clients in meeting their business and technical needs. We work with clients like Johnson & Johnson, Brulant, Harris Publication, Barclays Capital, UBS, Citigroup, MetLife to name some few.
We provide a wide range of professional consulting services, and we are known as the premier firm for developers, architects, analysts, and project managers. Our highest priority is developing strong client and consultant relationships through honesty, integrity, and professionalism. This has resulted in steady repeat business, high employee satisfaction, and a stellar reputation in the marketplace.

Since its inception in 1999, AIT Global Inc has maintained a strong reputation for quality and excellence throughout the Computer Consulting Industry. In addition, our on-going recruiting program allows us to continually provide our clients with a wide range of professional consultants that meet their specific needs and criteria.
Our consultants continually exhibit the necessary skills, motivation and integrity that we require when servicing any organization. AIT’s dedicated team of Project Consultants, Coordinators and Recruiters are always ready to provide you with valuable personalized service. We currently have more than 150 consultants on our W2. We have a vast pool of specialized talent not limited to Java, .Net, C++, technologies.
