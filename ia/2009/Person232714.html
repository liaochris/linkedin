N. Paul Thomason’s Summary

Thomason Venture Consultants (TVC) was founded on the principle of entrepreneurship. Many new inventions and concepts have come to fruition because someone in the private sector entertained “a good idea” and was willing to financially back that idea.
Entrepreneurs flourish when a mentor is engaged in their business from the beginning. Especially in the challenging economic times we face today traditional investment options are being challenged. TVC provides a vehicle to bring new ideas to market, create opportunities, and jobs in our community. Our investors find it personally fulfilling to give back to the community.
