Julie Levin’s Summary

We live in a culture that rewards hard work at the expense of caring relationships and personal wellness (physical, mental, emotional and spiritual). This is an exploitive system that creates anxiety and insecurity and then sells the idea of success and material acquisition as the "cure" for insecurity.

In my work, I help people break through the collective illusion that keeps us chasing happiness on a treadmill that goes nowhere. When we stop running, we can begin to hear the small still voices within that really need unconditional kindness, love, support and acceptance. As we develop a kind and nurturing inner ally, we can begin to truly provide for ourselves what we need, instead of chasing illusions.

Typical issues that bring people into therapy include low self-esteem, mid-life crisis, divorce, job loss/career dissatisfaction, food/weight problems, over-use of alcohol or other escape behaviors (internet, tv, gaming, shopping). All of these are signs that there is a disconnect inside.

I also coach therapists and other wellness practitioners who want to develop private practices that support their professional goals as well as balance and health in their personal lives.
