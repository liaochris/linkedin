TeacherSITY is committed to enabling the schools to play a defining role in preparing students for life; rooted in universal values and high achievement. We recognize that school education needs major reform in order to enable its’ school graduates to compete globally for jobs and opportunities. We believe that 14 years spent in the school have a defining impact on shaping attitudes, social and communication skills, and inculcating habits of mind. They, in turn, influence the quality of life beyond school years.

TeacherSITY is India’s first structured and organized Teacher Empowerment organization. We are working with more than 2,000 Schools, under our various programs, which range from Teacher Training, Students Assessments, Teacher Assessments, Pre-Service Teacher Development to In-Service Teacher Continuing Professional Development Programs.
TeacherSITY has now become India’s first organization to work with large school chains, imparting continuing professional development programs for thousands of teachers.
