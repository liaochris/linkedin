    GoLow

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    August 2007 — September 2008 (1 year 2 months)

    Chief Executive Officer and Founder of GoLow.

    GoLow provides an outsourced management sevice to help reduce the cost and environmental impact of taxis and private hire vehcles used by corporations, large NGOs, government institutions and the travelling public.

    Transport is the highest growing source of carbon emissions in the United Kingdom, and the road sector is the largest component of this. The taxi and private hire vehicle industry currently uses approximately 750,000 tonnes of fossil fuels on a yearly basis. There is an acknowledged 50% empty capacity in this industry, also known as dead mileage, which can rise to above 70%. This presents an unacceptable level of wastage from environmental and economic standpoints. GoLow was created to address this problem, providing a low-cost way to assisting corporations and governments to improve their environmental practices, as well as benefit from other efficiencies generated.
