  • Bonzobox, LLC

    (Internet industry)

    December 2007 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Bonzobox.com, founded in 2007, was started by an international team of programmers, engineers, marketing gurus, and active web users who were frustrated with not having a web venue that aggregated everything they did on the web in one place.

    From this frustration, Bonzobox emerged as an answer to the simple problem: How can I manage my online life in one place?

    BonzoBox is an interactive homepage tool that allows users to build their own customized “BonzoBox” home page with links to their favorite websites in a graphical "live thumbnail" layout. Since all of this information is stored on the web, the content is browser and computer independent.

    Bonzobox allows users to have all of their favorite bookmarks, email, and buddies laid out all in one location, making visiting commonly frequented sites and applications simple.

    BonzoBox utilizes simple Drag and Drop ajax based interaction which allows users to drag websites and content to create a custom homepage experience.
