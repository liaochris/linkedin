Yvonne Jackson’s Summary

In September of 2009, Yvonne will have graduated from the University of Cincinnati with a degree in Business Administration & Marketing. As a marketing student, she worked with the LiveWell Collaborative, P&G, General Mills, and Haney PRC on a high profile project to boost the 50+ consumer’s understanding of sustainability. She was also part of a project team contracted to design innovative concept ranges & ovens for LG.

Since 1999, she has provided project management and design services within the engineering and creative design industries and has worked for companies such as Alltel Communications, Jack Rouse Associates, and LPK.

After a successful career working on two international, entertainment design projects: The Ferrari Theme Park (Abu Dhabi) and Restless Planet (Dubai), she started CoSolutions LLC to provide design and administrative support for small businesses and entrepreneurs.

Her mission has always been to provide small businesses and start-ups with the tools necessary to organize their offices, procedures, and lives. She has helped managers in such industries as engineering, IT, children services, healthcare, beauty and entertainment increase their productivity and brand both on and off the web. Ms. Jackson currently specializes in Marketing & Web Design that allows businesses to fully take advantage of online tools such as blogs, social networks, and web content management system.

She is both a business woman and artist at heart. She paints, sings, writes and performs spoken-word poetry whenever she can find the time. Her background of art AND business puts her in the perfect position to bring her client’s visions to life. In 2008, she wrote and published an eBook entitled, The One Day Finding Your Way Plan: Find Your Niche and Target Market Overnight!
