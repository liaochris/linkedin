Larry Davis, MBA, CPA’s Summary

Senior financial executive with the unique ability to formulate growth strategies that generate superior shareholder returns combined with significant expertise in daily accounting operations and financial reporting requirements for publicly traded and venture backed businesses in the software, ecommerce, healthcare, retail, leasing, and financial services industries. Demonstrated successes include

-Selling a $400M family owned business to a private equity firm for 13x earnings

-Reverse merging a $500M retailer into a public shell while raising $250M in debt financing for expansion. Grew business to $1B in one year through seven acquisitions while increasing the operating margin from 2% to 3% versus an industry average of 1.5%

-Assisting CEOs of venture funded and private equity backed companies in raising $100M in convertible debt and equity financing. Designed forecast models to support these financings.

-Spearheading more than 20 acquisitions ranging in size from $2M to $100M that were accretive to earnings. Handled deal negotiations, the due diligence process, and post closing personnel and systems integrations


Other areas of expertise include

-Taking companies public via reverse mergers into public shells along with all required reporting to the SEC

-A high degree of accounting technical expertise to address complex financial reporting issues and drafting of footnotes to financial statements for auditors

-Resolving complex revenue recognition issues per SOP 97-2 and SABs 101 and 104 as well as determining if VSOE exists

-Advising CEOs on how to grow their companies along with the best method of financing this growth

-Designing and implementing financial reporting systems to provide the investors and management team with timely, accurate, and relevant financial and operating information

-"Cleaning" of general ledgers
