Robert Rice’s Summary

Robert is a serial “techpreneur” with 17 years of experience. He is a passionate innovator continually seeking to advance the state-of-the-art of dynamic and interactive technologies. He is an authority on persistent immersive environments, virtual reality and worlds, massively multiuser online games, simulations, and a leading pioneer in ubiquitous augmented reality.

Robert authored MMO Evolution in 2006 and contributed a chapter to Working Through Synthetic Worlds, expected out in Dec. 2009. Robert is the creator of Immortal Destiny (a next-generation MMORPG design), and is the former publisher of Storm Riders Online.

His career started in 1992 at the first virtual reality entertainment company in the US where he designed several multiplayer VR games. Over the years, he has gained broad and in-depth experience in design, development, production, project direction and executive management with a number of start-ups including Alternate Worlds Technologies, Arckosian Entertainment, ZReality, Orchid Media International, Neogence Enterprises, and others.

Recognized as an “Internet Gaming Pioneer” by Advertising Age magazine in 1996, Robert was also called “a Founding Father of the Digital Nation” in 1998 by The Raleigh News & Observer while he was designing immersive interfaces and next-generation concepts for a 3D internet.

Robert is currently the CEO of Neogence Enterprises and the Executive Director of the National Institute of Virtual and Augmented Reality and Simulations (NIVARS). He is available as a guest speaker for events and conferences. Robert lives in Raleigh North Carolina.

Robert can be found on Skype (rrice919), Twitter (robertice) and Plaxo.

All inquiries via email welcome.
