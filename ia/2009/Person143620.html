Lori Greaves Navalny’s Summary

I would like to take this opportunity to introduce you to my company.

This is a family owned business that was established in 1924 by my grandfather, my father took it over in 1963, I took it over in 1996. We have been handling our clients accounting, tax, and payroll needs continuously for 85 years.

I have been building the Payroll division since 1982. After several years of searching, I have found a payroll format that is technologically advanced, yet extremely cost effective for my clients.

Our Mission, as your payroll service, is to help you through every aspect of your payroll.

Unlike some other services you may be familiar with, we do not charge you for each and every option used to complete your payroll. One of the most obvious differences is that there is no fee at year end for your W-2 processing. This can be a great savings by itself when you consider most services charge between $4 and $5 an employee. There are many other *EXTRA'S* you are probably paying for now that come standard with our service.

For anyone currently using PayCycle - please contact me as the company has been sold. Have a say in who does your payroll, let me show you how The Back Office provides Service as well as Cost Savings, and will be here for you.

I would appreciate the opportunity of your time to discuss in greater detail the service, and how I will save you money in these difficult economic times.

Sincerely,
