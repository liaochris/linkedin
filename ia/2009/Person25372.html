Beatrice Esi Yarney’s Summary

My professional goal is to develop a career as a policy research and analysis expert. I would like to apply these skills in any area that promotes the socioeconomic uplift of the public.

My professional experience includes qualitative research on workforce centers in Virginia to facilitate the development of the “comprehensive” one-stop system requirement of the Workforce Investment Act, creation of a guide to cost allocation and resource sharing for Virginia’s workforce centers, assisting in staffing various Virginia Workforce Council and sub-council meetings, conducting research and analysis to explore the creation of a Work-Ready Community Certification Program in the Commonwealth of Virginia on behalf of the Virginia Workforce Council, and providing recommendations for design, implementation and evaluation of the Certification Program
