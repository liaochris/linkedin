Michael McGrath’s Summary

Inspirational Business Speaker, Corporate Adviser, Disability Commentator, Adventurer, Writer - the only disabled person in the world to have successfully led expeditions to both the North and South Poles

I work with private & public sector clients. I am engaged to speak at conferences, deliver masterclasses or workshops and am available for Awards. I help my clients expand their performance potential - as a change agent, my work is underpinned by my credo Quant Je Puis ('As Much As I Can')

Clients have included - Barclays, Roche Pharmaceuticals, Pfizer Pharmaceuticals, Lloyds TSB Women's Network, DHL Global, Hilton Hotels Corporation, Credit Suisse, Morgan Stanley, Shaw Trust, Capital Resourcing, Carillion Transport, the NHS, Shoosmiths, the Cabinet Office, Motorola, Medtronic Inc, Remploy, JP Morgan, plus many County Councils, Housing Associations, and places of learning from Ashridge Business School to junior, secondary and special education needs (SEN) schools

Michael McGrath’s Specialties:

Professional speaker, leader of people / team motivator, strong business development & project management skills, entrepreneurial fundraiser, strong communications & advocacy ie. highly developed interpersonal, ambassadorial / evangelical, deliverer
