Trudy Prins’s Summary

Being Manager of a Software Engineering department, my main expertise lies in combining technical/analytical skills with strong interpersonal skills. Having build up a lot of experience in user support, I passionately try to overcome the gap between technical units (developers) and end-users, striving to create software that really rocks :-)

Proper requirement gathering, speaking the end-users language, and using an Agile methodology are prerequisites, IMO.

As a former trainer, I have excellent presentation skills - very important to get support from peers and communicate your ideas.

I invest a great deal in relationships, because in the end, this really makes the difference.

As a former biologist and artist, dynamic complexity, creativity, energy and originality are essential components of everything I do.
