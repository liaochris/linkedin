Tammy Collier’s Summary

I am a very seasoned contact center professional. My experience has involved extensive workflow design, creating and performing training, writing documentation for processes, policies, procedures, and I have extensive management experience in Operations and Projects.

Successes and Relative Assets

* Created numerous training courses, policies, procedures, restructured NOC, workflow charts, and was the Director of compliance for start-up organization.

* Co-Designed, created, and implemented IP Contact Center Consulting Practice for a start-up Information Technology firm.

* As Project Manager, led Cablevision in their program rollout of the Optimum Online internet service to over 2 million subscribers. Organization saves over $1.5 million a year in technical installation, as a result of this project.

* Created and implemented corporate and operational training program in large marketing contact center with over 500 representatives.

*Part of management team that led the business process redesign project in 1994 for same organization.

It led to renewal contracts and increased profits by adding thousands of hours to the two contact centers.
