Carl Ashley (sales@irishwireless.net)’s Summary

Having gained significant experience in residential sales in the telecommunications industry for high-profile companies such as Eircom, Sky and Chorus(NTL) I have subsequently broadened my horizons by moving towards B2B sales.

At present I am working on a fantastic opportunity to market the EnGenius product range on behalf of Irish Wireless throughout Ireland. Specifically, this will involve building relationships with clients, arranging meetings and conducting presentations, closing business and thereafter account management.

On the PR side of things activities will also incorporate arranging press releases, organising POS merchandising for our resellers and retailers and providing ongoing support and after-sales service support.

Since IT is a dynamic working environment I am constantly conscious of the requirement to avail of updating my knowledge and skills, and to this end I would expect to regularly attend training seminars and industry-relevant courses.
