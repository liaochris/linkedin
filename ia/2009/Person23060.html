Gianni Tolu’s Summary

I stumbled into the industry when I was really young, 16 years old.

Joining a telecommunication company as a junior developer to make a few money while I was studying, I realized that, to me, zero&one were not just a mere job: Informatic Science and the ability to improve Communications had to do with passion.

The most powerful shove I had ever felt, together with the one of flying.

Over 20 years of experience and activity in the fields of Information & Communication Technology and Commercial Aviation, supporting Credit Institutions, small businesses, Airlines and related companies (handling, sales, aircraft maintenance, flight operations) in areas such as:
- Hardware Solutions, System Integration, Networking and IT Security;
- Web solutions, ERP, CRM and BI;
- General Management;
- Project Management;
- Facility Management.

Broad experience in international projects (ref. Sita, Alitalia, Meridiana, Eurofly Airlines, Telecom Italy, Vodafone, Tiscali, Navitaire, Jeppesen, IBM, Lotus, Microsoft, SAS, Saber Technology, British Telecom, France Telecom, Wanadoo, Brainforce, Bombardier), decision-making skills, problem solving, presentation and effectiveness in communication.

The ability to translate business requirements into solutions.

Gianni Tolu’s Experience

  • CEO & Founder

    Axoluta S.r.l.

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 2002 — Present (7 years 7 months)

    Why Axoluta®?

    The whole project started in May 2002 by a team of professionals with broad experience in managerial and operational positions in leading companies operating in the Financial arena, Marketing, Traditional and Executive Air Transport, Tourism and Information & Communication Technology fields.

    While drafting Axoluta launch, the heavy pace of financial and economic downturn has highlighted the biz need to focus on yield management choices.

    In traditional air trainsport, for example, the continuous need to streamline operations due to structural losses; the urgent need to negotiate new employment agreements; the inability to implement savings without substantial manpower cuts; signs of a demand recovery which ignition is almost unpredictable.

    More in general, the need to revisit the models of a business that - albeit helped by a hoped recovery - cannot but consider the most crucial recent experience: the advent of Low Cost Carriers, together with the necessary reduction of the price charged by the Airline in the near application period, have accustomed the final users at lower and lower fares, constantly sought over the Internet.

    A scene that has also helped the proliferation of new players among the tourism industry (in particular, Web-Based Tour Operator). It is therefore clear, in this scenario, the strategic importance of pricing policies directed to a more attentive and informed Customer Base, for which even a small difference between two bids lead the choice of purchase.

    The sum of those factors suggested a definite acceleration towards the preparation of some innovative elements with the purpose to boost an earlier implementation, yet able to support Revenue and Yield management by adopting modular price marketing strategies based upon data retrieval systems. A unique software platform, tested with success - for years - into key realities of Traditional and Low Cost Air Transport services.
