Robert Wickham’s Summary

Robert is a 12 year veteran of the Software industry in the DC Metro area with experience working with emerging technology companies and start-ups as well as established solution providers. Currently Robert is working for ImmixGroup, one of Washington Technologies' FAST 50 companies and Top 100 Federal Prime Contractors, specializing in growing the Public Sector Business for technology companies like EMC, Serena, Sourcefire, Information Builders, Intellitactics, Tibco, Vignette, Handysoft, Metacarta, Netezza, and Decision Lens. In his role at WSW Partners, Robert built a consulting practice focused on developing distribution channels and go-to-market strategies for software companies looking to do business with the federal government. WSW has executed for Sage Software, and Setecs, Inc.

Robert Wickham’s Specialties:

Robert has first-hand knowledge of the security requirements facing many government agencies having performed business development for companies responding to HSPD-12. Robert is skilled at developing and managing OEM and VAR partnerships.
