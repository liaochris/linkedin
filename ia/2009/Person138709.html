Keith German’s Summary

LCD-Serve, operating as an independent consulting company, continues to define and develop its services to nonprofits and those that service nonprofits. Currently, LCDS provides two primary services:

NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION SERVICES - LCDS exist to prepare in a consultative approach nonprofit decision makers and end users that have decided to replace existing business software technology solutions. These folks already have full time jobs and, more importantly, few were hired because of their software buying experience and/or skills. Many, however, take an "I can do this too" approach and the end result is often less than satisfactory; not to mention costly given already limited time resources. LCDS provides services using proven methodologies designed to expedite the search process and ultimately narrow solution options BEFORE allowing software resellers direct contact with and thereby utilizing the resources of the organization review and decision team.

SOFTWARE TECHNOLOGY RESELLER SERVICES - LCDS provides the valuable option of outsourcing software marketing and sales consulting that is commission-based. This model allows resellers to develop effective lead generation and sales services but pay for these services only when RESULTS have occurred. Imagine that! Instead of supporting the salaries (and benefits) of a new hire, investing in professional development training courses, and "hoping" the new hire produces measurable results and an ROI within a reasonable timeframe, the reseller companies can now invest critical dollars in a consulting service that requires payment only when results are provided. This model works in tough and exciting times.

In both service models - much like a buyer and seller broker in real estate - LCDS believes wholeheartedly that a consultant must LISTEN | COMMUNICATE | DELIVER...then SERVE. Contact me today to discuss these service options further and explore steps to move forward with a win-win business model.
