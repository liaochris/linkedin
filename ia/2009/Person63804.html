Lauren Engel’s Summary

Web Strategist - It's hard to explain how a Psychology Major ended up as Web Strategist for an interactive agency, but to folks that I work with and for, it makes perfect sense. Successful web design and development is at its core intuitive, universally appealing, and dirt simple to use - so understanding what truly motivates users helps keep dysfunctional websites in check.
At Digital Peabody, I lead all web design and development with a keen eye on web usability. I’m passionate about clean design, smart site engineering, and good ol' fashioned creativity. When not creating memorable online experiences, I am dazzling audiences with my dazzling sushi making abilities.
