Patrick Dennis’s Summary

The aim is to achieve a shared emotional experience. To connect a creator or company's vision with their audience. To disrupt, challenge, energize, celebrate and feed the human need for passion. Evoking an emotional response in the viewer or listener. Good marketing? The best! Attracting not only consumer interest but their personal involvement in the vision and ultimately their dollars in support. And more than that it's a responsibility to contribute passion and energy into our work and our world. In an image, a brand personality, a song, merchandise concept, or a stage moment.

Creative Producer & Director. Advertising & Music Industries. Collaborator. Touring Musician & Recording Artist. Disruptor. Illustrator. Writer. Apprentice to Masters.

Despite my varied work I am not all things to all people. I'm driven to collaborate with companies and individuals that are passionate about their product and their customers. Whatever the medium and end consumer. but always to find and draw out the passion in a project and the people involved. Discover its reason. Its internal value. Isn't that the only reason to become involved? To find purpose and life as a constant creation.

Patrick Dennis’s Specialties:

Creative directing, team collaboration (important!), disrupting, brand and identity, product and merchandise concept, inspiration, advertising imagery & packaging, producing, writing, songwriting, soundtrack, changing music business practices, challenging accepted approaches, tour managing, live performance and on-the-road networking.
