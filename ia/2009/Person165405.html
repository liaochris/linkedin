Evelina Szewczyk’s Summary

Evelina Anna Szewczyk works on all aspects of family law at which include Custody, Visitation, Child/Spousal Support, Dissolution, Legal Separation, Paternity, Asset/Debt Division, Stepparent Adoptions, and International Family Law.

Her experience in child support issues stems from working at the Family Law Facilitator by serving Orange County’s low-income community and assisting in the resolution of Department of Child Support Services cases.

Evelina's interest in family and domestic violence issues is shown through her affiliation with various charitable and civic organizations such as Community Service Programs, Inc. She is a member of the Orange County Young Lawyers Division where she serves on the community outreach committee. In addition, Evelina belongs to the California State Bar Association Family Law Section, Orange County Bar Association Family Law Section, Orange County Women Lawyers Association, and American Bar Association.

Personal Note: My passion to help people drives me to advocate for my clients during one of the most difficult times of their lives. I understand that every client that comes to the Miller Family Law Group is going through a difficult time in their life, and using my best efforts I am committed to guide and provide each client a quicker and smoother experience in the legal and divorce process. I pride myself on keeping my clients informed and minimizing their stresses. My compassion combined with my commitment to do my best assists me in effectively empowering my clients in re-building their lives.
