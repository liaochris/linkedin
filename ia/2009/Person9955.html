Allison O'Neill’s Summary

I am the author of 'The Boss Benchmark’ which is a book about how to be an AMAZING boss. I wrote it after seeing inside heaps of workplaces while running my staff survey company. I heard first hand from tonnes of workers what they really love (and really loathe) in a boss. The book includes 35 basics bosses need to master, and 55 real workplace stories (good and bad) that will cement the lessons forever! You can buy it at www.thebossbenchmark.com (get in touch to discuss bulk orders for conference goody bags, corporate gifts or as a learning and development tool for your managers). Allison O'Neill’s Education

  • Life

    Life, Life, 1985 — 2008

    Since workplaces are my life, that is where I got the education I really needed. I entered the workforce at age 16 to an industry I was sure I'd spend many years, love, and develop a fab career. What I saw instead was what workplaces were really like and how they needed to change. I then worked for a huge company and saw the negatives of the corporate environment. I left to start my staff survey company. My time doing that, taught me so much about bosses, workers and workplaces I penned and published my book The Boss Benchmark.
