Jay Elkerton’s Summary

I am a user experience expert that will bring your product interfaces to the next level of usefulness and usability to delight your customers and users. My engineering skills and training in psychology and human factors allows me to infuse knowledge about users into your product development process from concept to release. Leading your management, engineers, and user interface specialists, I will bring an engineering-level understanding of the user experience into your product organizations effectively and efficiently.

I am an expert in system management interfaces with experience in GUIs and web interfaces. I have worked extensively in consumer electronics and telecommunications.

Jay Elkerton’s Specialties:

Experience in facilitating iterative user product development with customer feedback in the full product lifecycle (requirements to release).
Skilled evaluator of software products: customer surveys, field studies, competitive product analysis, task analysis, user testing (on-site and remote). Expert in qualitative and quantitative data analysis.
Experience in use of product prototypes for development and customer feedback: storyboards, wireframes, product simulations, working interfaces.
