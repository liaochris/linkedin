William Horn’s Summary

Extensive IT, business analysis, program management and strategic planning experience at large global corporations as well as start-ups as a management consultant and a corporate executive.

Developed a career analyzing business problems, and crafting creative solutions by redesigning processes and applying technology. Very skilled at establishing broad programs influencing others to support them with their time and money.

The unique ability to positively influence senior management teams and integrate functions across decentralized organizations. Particularly adept at developing solutions that balance regulatory requirements, litigation risk and operational goals.

Most recently designed and implemented a global records management function at Fidelity Investments to govern all phases of the information lifecycle—storage, retrieval, categorization, retention, legal preservation and destruction of records in compliance with legal, regulatory and operational obligations. Led the Litigation Support / eDiscovery function as well.
