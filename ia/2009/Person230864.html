Terry Vaughn’s Summary

I'm a user-focused Web technology innovator going on a decade of experience. I spent the last several years doing information architecture, usability and user interface design. More recently, I moved into product management.

Terry Vaughn’s Specialties:

Product Management: User-centered, Agile approach to product design and development. Emphasis on empirical evidence to drive features and refinements.

Information Architecture: Defining metadata schema, developing controlled vocabularies, resource classification. Findability, SEO and PPC.

Usability: Planning and leading lab and remote tests. Mastery of usability heuristics and interaction design principles. Ever striving for elegance and simplicity in user interface design.
