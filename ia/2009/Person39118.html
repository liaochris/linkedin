John Macpherson’s Summary

Experienced, capable, adaptable, resourceful Programme & Project Manager and IT Consultant, with a strong track record of delivering enterprise-level technology and change programs in Insurance & Asset Management, and an enviable history of "repeat business". Internationally mobile, Asia-based since 1993, extensive regional and global project experience.

I focus on getting the job done, whatever it takes. Strategic vision, attention to detail, drive for results. Proactive, focus on communication, risk avoidance & issues resolution. Diplomatic, patient & tolerant, I achieve consensus, bridge gaps, & build great teams. My employers and employees consider me a trusted, go-to person, who can be relied on to find creative solutions, give good advice, and deliver more than expected.

Also an advocate of open source software and OSS-style development methods, interested in promoting OSS adoption in business, and ensuring the appropriate infrastructure exists in Asia to support OSS use.


Selected Achievements:

> Mobilised and managed a global program to standardise and off-shore Investor Services business processing, and migrate multiple legacy systems to a single global platform.

> Co-founded an Asian insurance and healthcare B2B service provider and a healthcare business in Thailand.

> Defined complete technology strategy and requirements, and selected appropriate systems for new Asian regional life and health insurance business - including support for multi-line and flexible benefits products.

> Solved the management information needs of a crisis-hit mortgage insurer, which allowed the business management to understand their potential exposure, define reserves, reduce or eliminate claims and minimise the size of the losses. (Also handled disaster recovery after being hit by IRA bomb.)

> Co-founded the Access to Broadband Campaign in Thailand, worked with the Thai government to promote affordable broadband nationally; webmaster for UK campaign
