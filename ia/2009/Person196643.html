Scott Crawford’s Summary

Among other things, I've been a writer, a musician, a webmaster, and a coke addict strip club owner in a virtual world, but really, I'm just looking for situations where I can be paid well to be myself quasi-professionally. I'm sort of retired/disabled, though, so I'm not actively seeking offers.

Some tips on contacting me:

Be real.

Any offers involving a set regular schedule need not apply, as I have chronic sleep issues which make situations like these virtually impossible.

If you're concerned about the mention of drug and alcohol use, relax. While these things are a key part of my history, I have been clean and sober for a very long time, and I'll be staying this way. The flip side of this is that I have little patience for situations where dealing with drinkers and drug users is the norm.

I would greatly appreciate it if you understand and respect that, while some of this profile may read humorously, I have been as frank, forthcoming and honest as possible in writing it, and the decision to do so was not taken lightly. Like it or not, this is my life.

Most importantly, I urge you to read this entire profile carefully before contacting me with any offer, so you can be sure that it's me that you'd like to work with rather than Diet Scott Crawford. I come with my search results, reputation, personal style, and some very well-formed (but not entirely inflexible) opinions. This should be viewed as a feature, not a bug.

Anyone who's looking for a tame, homogenized, team player version of me is doing not only me but also themselves a disservice by contacting me. However, if you're looking for someone who works hard at what they do, does so as originally and vitally as one can in an age and culture where everything's been done to death, and has quietly predated, influenced and outlasted many similar entities in my chosen fields, then I'm your man.

Thanks in advance for reading this far and taking what I've written here to heart.
