Karen Lamas Tobin’s Summary

Versatile executive with 20 years of national and local TV, radio, Internet, mobile and consumer sales and marketing experience who excels in constructing and implementing a vision. An outstanding track record of leading and mentoring teams coupled with the ability to build consensus of diverse teams.

Areas of expertise include sales expertise in new business development, territory management, e-commerce, database marketing, creation of marketing materials, research, brand positioning, strategic planning, product launches, creating and executing third party promotions and developing and managing a budget while implementing cost savings. Self-motivated who identifies market trends, ROI analysis and exceeds aggressive business goals.

Karen Lamas Tobin’s Specialties:
