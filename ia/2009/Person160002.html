Merritt Green’s Summary

Merritt J. Green is an attorney and entrepreneur.

In 2004, Mr. Green founded General Counsel, P.C., a law firm based in McLean, Virginia. Under Mr. Green’s leadership, General Counsel, P.C. has steadily grown. Today, it is a full-service law firm with fourteen attorneys providing comprehensive legal services for businesses ranging from start-up to multi-billion dollar organizations.

In addition to serving as the managing partner of General Counsel, P.C., Mr. Green co-founded and currently serves as the Chairman and a Managing Member of Bookkeeping Express Enterprises, LLC. Mr. Green is a significant shareholder of the company and, working with the executive team, is building a national franchise that plans on “Revolutionizing Bookkeeping in America.”

Finally, in May 2009, Mr. Green acquired a majority interest in Capital Bookkeeping, which serves as the franchisee for Bookkeeping Express throughout the Washington, DC metropolitan area.

Merritt Green’s Specialties:

Merritt has experience working with and helping start-up businesses grow and succeed. Currently, Merritt is looking for start-up businesses where he can provide legal expertise and incubation services in exchange for equity.
