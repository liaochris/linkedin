CJ Boguszewski’s Summary

Hello and thanks for visiting my profile. If you would like to contact me, please feel free to do so. I currently live in south Florida.

With my experience in marketing, I help companies get more for their marketing spend. The social media marketplace provides us all with opportunities to improve and get much more for our money than "traditional" approaches -- or remain on the sidelines in uncertainty. If you sell, generate leads, or register interest on your website, you probably already know just how hard it is to tie "driving traffic" to what you really want -- revenue and profit.

I don't suggest that traffic generation is unimportant - it is. But I believe in SEO and SEM/PPC in concert with Conversion Rate Optimization, or CRO. Why waste the traffic you've driven?

Over 15 years in positioning products in the technology arena, being the Top Marketing Officer in a publicly-traded company, and living in a variety of international settings means that I bring a holistic perspective and approach.
