David Herdlinger’s Summary

I am a leader and foremost authority on one of the most exciting new frontiers of the coaching profession: coaching corporate teams.

I help my clients balance their personal and professional lives, so that the motives and actions in both spheres are compatible and mutually reinforcing.

As an authority in the exciting field of Executive Coaching, I'd like to share a bit of my philosophy:

1.) Many people make the mistake of ineffectively compartmentalizing their lives and that sets up tensions that hinder accomplishment. Individuals maximize their potential when they view life holistically.

2. ) When individuals discover their potential and identify their purpose, they begin to align their attitudes, goals, and actions with their deepest motivations. That unleashes a tremendous amount of focused energy, which results in extraordinary success and fulfillment, in both their personal and professional lives. As an Executive Coach, nothing thrills me more than watching my clients experience success and satisfaction beyond their wildest expectations.

3.) Coaching all, or substantially all, of the members of the management team of an organization multiplies the positive power individuals and companies derive from executive coaching.

If you or your company need high-level, professional coaching, contact me at www.KashBoxCoaching.com, today...
