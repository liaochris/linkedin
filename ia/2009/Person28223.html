About Me:

I am a business intelligence and information management expert with 20 years experience creating and leading teams and companies in the Financial Services, IT, and Marketing industries.

Former member of Storebrand Norden in Scandinavia, Citibank in the US, EU and Middle East, ABN AMRO in The Netherlands and China, NatWest Bank in the UK, Information Innovation in the EU and US, and IAG, AMP, Gartner, ING Direct and The Business Intelligence Group in Australia.

In 2009 the Business intelligence Group (BIG) launched The business intelligence CORTEX (www.tbig.com.au/forums/), an onlne community for people and organisations active in the real-world application of BI and Performance Management in Australia and NZ. Membership is free - take a look via the url above!

Also in 2009 I am creating a new media strategy with the Sydney Symphony Orchestra (http://www.sydneysymphony.com/). The SSO is one of the world's great orchestras with over 300 performances in Australia and abroad in 2009. It is an exciting time for the orchestra: now led by Principal Conductor Vladimir Ashkenazy, a new CEO coming onboard and now a big push into new media. Stay tuned.

I am a regular speaker on these subjects at a range of international conferences and local industry events. I publish the blog Oz Analytics (http://analytics.typepad.com/) recent entries of which can be viewed below.
