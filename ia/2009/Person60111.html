Dean Perrone’s Summary

Recently launched 3 products wrapped around Twitter in the month of October, 2009. (YourTweetProfits.com, TurboFollow.com, MyTweetTool.com) All 3 products are at the top of the list for affiliate marketers on PayDot.com and are becoming very well known resouces for both newbies as well as experienced Twitter users.

I have been in the Network Marketing/MLM/Direct Sales industry for approximately 3 years now. Just like the very large majority, I struggled at the beginning trying to figure this out. I was chasing friends and family, cold-calling people, buying leads, floundering like a fish out of water and was getting little success.

Now I was doing what my sponsor told me but little did I know at the time that most of the "successful" marketers don't really share with you what they know. I had to seek out others in this Industry. I hounded people who were not even in my company. I found people who I knew were having success in the home business Industry and I begged them to let me in on how they were doing it.

After seeking out a few individuals, I came to realize that Every successful home business owner has a SYSTEM in place. I also became aware of a little thing called a Funded Proposal. I also learned how to monetize the 95% of people who didn't even join my primary business. I also learned the importance of personal branding. But the #1 thing I learned how to do was....MARKET online.

My business almost exploded overnight. It was then that I started NoBossByChoice.com as an online training facility to teach these so called “secrets to success" to other struggling network marketers.

It makes me sick to know So many great people are out there right now struggling in their network marketing / MLM / Direct Sales opportunities and it is not their fault. Most people are not being taught how to truly market both correctly and affectively online.

I am here to help as many people as I can!
