Alessandro Rimassa’s Summary

Professional Journalist, Multimedia&Tv Producer and Media Specialist.

My name is Alessandro Rimassa, I am 33 years old and I have been working in media for the past 13 years, as a journalist, author, creative and executive producer of multimedia projects, on TV and Radio Networks, on the web and for the press.
I have also had a chance to directly realize and coordinate TV programs, News, documentaries and reportages. As a result of the experience I have developed in various fields and sectors, I possess a knowledge of the Italian media and of their audience.

My professional background drives me today to searching for a position that may take advantage of that knowledge and expertise I have developed in the field of media and information. In particular my multimedia attitude, and the ability of managing a team, allows me to create and produce contemporary media products.

I always look for positions in media industry to use all the skills I have, managing an editorial and production team and creating original multimedia products focused on society, culture, trends and politics.

Alessandro Rimassa’s Specialties:

I possess strong social skills, ability to create and mantain public relations, inclination to team work and ability of managing a team, innate curiosity and passion for the media.
Moreover I'm specialist in observing people and society, producing multimedia products, creating new ideas and formats.
And most important, I own the will, the energy and the spirit to begin a new work challenge within an international, state-of-the-art reality.

Alessandro Rimassa’s Experience

  • Chief Editor

    Hachette

    (Public Company; MMB; Publishing industry)

    August 2008 — Present (10 months)

    I've accepted the invitation of Hachette Rusconi to work as chief editor/managing editor at Riders, italian men magazine. Riders (De Agostini Periodici, Hachette group), born in September 2007, is a forefront monthly magazine which has changed the approach to men in the press: not any more a non-specialized magazine, but a periodical with a strong heart which conquer and thrill the readers. This heart, for Riders' readers, is the passion for motorbike and for being riders inside.

    My job consists in managing, organizing and coordinating the work of in-house and out-house editorial staff and deciding, in collaboration with the editor in chief, themes, subjects, topics and articles for each number of the magazine.

  • Author of Jobbing

    Sperling&Kupfer

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year)

    I've written with Antonio Incorvaia my second book, Jobbing, a reference guide to find the right way in the labour market. Its aim is not to show how to find a job, but which job to look for. It is made up of 100 job descriptions illustrating just as many professions and three final appendixes dedicated to cv writing, job interviews and contract and wage types.
    Each profession is classified according to eight market segments: media & communication, fashion & creativity, events and tourism, marketing & economics, administration & hr, organization & services, ict and “evergreen”.
    It has been published in Italy on february 2009.

  • Author of Generazione 1000 Euro

    RCS Libri

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    December 2005 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    Along with Antonio Incorvaia, I am one of the authors of Generazione Mille Euro (Rizzoli – 24/7 series, June 2006. Bur edition: april 2009), a novel about the young generation and their working reality. Born in December 2005 as a free book that could be downloaded from the Internet, it has become a complete on-line format, still available at www.generazione1000.com, which includes a community, a forum, a blog and debate sections. There have been seven editions abroad, in Germany, Holland, Korea, Greece, China and Taiwan, Portugal and Japan. I have also collaborated to movie that will be in theatres by april 24th 2009 (an Andrea Leone Film and Rai Cinema production, 01 Distribution, directed by Massimo Venier, with Carolina Crescentini, Valentina Lodovini, Francesco Mandelli, Alessandro Tiberi and Paolo Villaggio).
