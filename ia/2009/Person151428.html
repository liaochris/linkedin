Mark Bustard’s Summary

I have worked in the live events industry since leaving university in 1994 - as a creative, designer and producer, initially within the music and then exhibitions industries, developing my experiences as a creative at MICE International and presently as Creative Director at WorldEvents.

My work is primarily within the pharmaceutical sector of the conference industry.

My interests are focused upon turning strategic insight into creative solutions that deliver a measurable ROI against clients objectives as part of an integrated marcomms plan.This involves the development of content and creative design of delegate experiences through to highly impactful production for live events.

Mark Bustard’s Specialties:

Strategically led creative design and production for live events.
