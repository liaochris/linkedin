Line Ulrika Christiansen’s Summary

Line Ulrika Christiansen recently moved to Doha, Qatar to fulfill the position as Director of Art and Design Foundation at the Virginia Commonwealth University in Qatar.

She is co-founder and partner of Interaction Design Lab in Milan, Italy and continues to collaborate and develop projects with Id-lab in spite of her position at the VCUQatar.

She is also member of board of the architectural and design oriented studio 3xU in Copenhagen, Denmark.

She holds a Master of Interaction Design from Interaction Design Institute Ivrea Italy (2003). She has previously a BA in visual communication concerning traditional graphic design and screen based interactive multimedia from Designskolen Kolding in Denmark (2000).
From 2003 to 2005 she worked as an interaction designer and project leader at the e1 (Exhibition Unit) of the Interaction Design Institute Ivrea: a design unit active on various projects for public and private clients.

She has taught at the Interaction Design Insitute Ivrea (2004), Architectural Association (London, 2005), Nuova Accademia di Belle Arti (Milan, 2006 to date) and given lecture at Umeå Institute of Design (Sweden, 2006), the Bocconi School of Management (Milan, 2008) as well as an international workshop at the cultural center Etagi in St. Petersburg.
Her latest talk were at the Interaction Design Symposium held at the Arts Academy of the University of Split in Croatia.

Line Ulrika Christiansen’s Specialties:

Interaction design, Exhibit design, Graphics & Multimedia, Teaching, Workshops and Project management.
