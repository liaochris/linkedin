Erik Tjernlund’s Summary

Experienced software developer with a passion for delivering great user experiences, all the way from the server to the client. More than 10 years experience of developing web applications and services in Java and related technologies.

Interests include agile development, rich internet applications (HTML5, JavaScript, JavaFX) and modern mobile plattforms (Android, iPhone).

This year my goal is to become proficient in mobile development, making iPhone (Objective-C) and Android (Java) applications .

My current professional overall goal is to lead small development teams to deliver great software and try to have an interesting career without losing touch with the technical side.

As a person I'm outgoing and social and I enjoy working in a group environment, especially using agile development techniques.

And I love my Mac!
