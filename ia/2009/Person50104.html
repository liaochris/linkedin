Craig Keegan’s Summary

I have been in the Computer Industry since 1987, mainly involved in the Networking side.
In 2003 I went to a Investment Expo and realised that Computing was not going to allow me to spend time with my family and travel the world. I then started my journey into changing my life and helping others.
I setup www.AussieInvestmentLinks.com.au, I then moved on to QBS-Box Hill, I found The IMP Group which then gave me access to Australian Country Recyclers (www.ACRecyclers.com.au ) and Drum Furniture (www.DrumFurniture.com ).

I have since moved onto Property and Internet Marketing along with Share Trading.
