Dave Lajoie’s Summary

Dave Lajoie is a rendering specialist involved in the entertainment business for over 15 years. Throughout his carreer he worked on several mental ray integration, starting in Softimage 3D Creative environment, Softimage XSI and Autodesk Maya. Dave was senior product designer responsible for driving Softimage and Autodesk rendering strategy. His knowledge has been tuned over the years through his involvement with top studios in the entertainment business. For over 15 years he is an authority on mental ray technology. He has worked closely with mental images from mentel ray 2.6 up to mental ray 3.7. His strong background in rendering, lighting pipelines have contributed to Softimage XSI and Maya Rendering features set. He is the creator of the Autodesk Maya Render Pass system, as well as Maya to Toxik Pre-compositing system. Dave was award his first patent while working on Autodesk Maya.

Dave Lajoie has strong and deep understanding of the customers. During all those years, he worked very closely with Softimage and Autodesk users to represent customers needs to product management teams.

His skills covers End-to-End Computer Graphics pipeline for TV series, Feature Film, VFX:
