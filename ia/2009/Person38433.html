Carol Snider’s Summary

I am an accomplished program and product manager, a dedicated educator and developer, and a creative producer of interactive media and consumer products (software, video, print, tabletop), with extensive experience managing people, processes and products.

From my extensive experience in educational game design and development, to my position as the executive producer of a video series for media-savvy teens, to program management with both Microsoft Teaching & Learning Technologies and with the Gates Foundation's education reform efforts, I exemplify broad-based, in-depth knowledge of education/edutainment media design and development, effective and organized program management, and confident and compassionate leadership.

I have also been both a Microsoft v-dash and a-dash agency contractor, managing enterprise-level content development and marketing collateral for such business units as Education Products Group, Unlimited Potential Group, Windows Mobile, Surface, Office for Mac, Windows Live, and others.
