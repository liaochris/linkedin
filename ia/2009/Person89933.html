Greg Silva | Senior Programmer/Analyst’s Summary

Greg has 23 years of experience in the software industry, having worked in a variety of settings: large corporation, non-profit, small start-up, and medium-sized companies. During the last 13 years, he commercialized a proprietary business system software for managing corporate purchasing cards. He and his business partners founded ExpensePath Software in 1995, which sold the corporate purchasing card management software to large organizations, such as NASA, the National Cancer Institute, Warner Brothers, University of Illinois and other businesses, universities, and laboratories. ExpensePath was purchased in 2005 by Harbor Payments, which was subsequently purchased by American Express in 2006. Greg enjoys all aspects of software systems management, including product definition, development, customer service, and training.

Greg Silva | Senior Programmer/Analyst’s Specialties:

Software development, project management, product management, business strategy. Visual Studio .NET, Visual Basic HTML, JavaScript, Omnis Studio, Oracle and SQL Server object design and development.
