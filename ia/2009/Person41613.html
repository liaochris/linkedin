Charlie Quirk’s Summary

An Aussie based in Portland, my current focus is working with a select group of clients in the US and Canada to help them define and articulate the central idea that drives their brand.

As cultures converge, brands are becoming more important in helping consumers define themselves. Utility is ubiquitous, only the brands infused with a loftier meaning and resonance will endure the Darwinian marketplace of the twenty-first century.

From the Fortune 500 to start-up, I have a diverse background as a brand strategist, project manager, communications director and press secretary, working with clients throughout the US, Europe, Asia and the Middle-East.
