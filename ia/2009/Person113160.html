Jim Lorden’s Summary

Strategic and innovative Communications Planning, Corporate Marketing and Agency-of-Record Executive with experience in packaged goods, travel, software, telecommunications / technology, automotive and retail industries.
- Proven ability to translate marketing direction into award-winning breakthrough communication strategies.
- Adept at motivating teams and partners to surpass expectations and contribute to business-building results.
- Known for rapid adaptability; motivated by accountability and measurable benchmarks.

KEY ACCOMPLISHMENTS
Client-Side Media Management – As key client representative at AT&T, inspired agencies to build world-class media plans for seven business units across all target segments. Led AT&T into new digital advertising platforms like interactive television, enhancing AT&T’s technological prowess among core target groups.

Broadcast Agency-of-Record Leadership - Set up an optimization-based Agency-of-Record structure for Procter & Gamble that efficiently managed over one million quarterly television commercial units and $2 billion in inventory. Gained trust of former adversaries while saving P&G over 15% of historical television expenditures.

Communications Planning – Contributed to record growth of text messaging business through deft management of AT&T Wireless $25 million “American Idol” sponsorship and supplemental first-ever broadcast and print programs. Awarded Mediaedge:CIA global ideas award for efforts.

Technology Adeptness – Developed mathematical models for several Procter & Gamble applications which were adopted company-wide, which helped to allocate franchise media spending among a number of product variants, and allowed brand agencies to test media plans using proprietary reach curves.
