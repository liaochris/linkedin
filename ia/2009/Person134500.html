Kriss Titus’s Experience

  • CEO/President

    The Kriss Titus Group

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2008 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    Kriss Titus is an innovator of travel and tourism strategy and business development, having leveraged an outstanding career of result-focused industry leadership and a proven track record of achieving extraordinary successes with complex, mission-critical goals.

    Working in travel and tourism since 1985, Kriss distinguished herself as a high performance, high profile industry leader. She most recently served nine years as the past Executive Director of Beaches of South Walton’s Tourist Development Council in Northwest Florida. Her accomplishments include developing a successful research-driven strategy to the branding, marketing and business development, positioning Beaches of South Walton as a premier Florida beach resort destination. Prior to her outstanding career with Beaches of South Walton TDC, Titus spent nine years as the Executive Vice President of the world-renowned Arabian Nights Dinner Attraction in Orlando, Florida.

    Recognizing the need for establishing a brand and marketplace positioning, Titus conducted comprehensive research to develop the destination brand and positioning in the marketplace and facilitated the strategic marketing of Beaches of South Walton destination. Of the many marketing strategies Titus put in place, she is most recognized for bringing that destination into the new age of Internet marketing, developing a successful Internet marketing strategy for the destination via online marketing, direct response, customer relationship marketing and e-commerce. Titus’ marketing successes helped in generating over a billion dollars in economic impact to Beaches of South Walton.

    A winner of multiple prestigious state and national awards and recognition for exemplary performance in the areas of marketing, advertising, Internet marketing, public relations and environmental stewardship, Kriss Titus is also known as an entrepreneur, recently launching her own company, the Kriss Titus Group.
