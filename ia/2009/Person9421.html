Alison Huntley’s Summary

LIFEQUEST is an Independent Coaching firm that is owned and operated by Alison Elizabeth Huntley. Based in Hopkinton, Massachusetts, LIFEQUEST specializes in Divorce Coaching, Divorce Advocacy and Divorce Recovery. LIFEQUEST supports individuals who are facing the emotional impact of separation/divorce by co-creating goals and effective action plans to stay focused, on track and moving forward. This partnership enables individuals to be fully present and engaged while processing the multitude of changes and life-style adjustments that are involved in divorcing.
