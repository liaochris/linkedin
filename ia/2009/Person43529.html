Christopher (Chris) Kennedy’s Summary

As a programming media strategist, consultant and market researcher, I distill market and customer information into company opportunity and profit while creating a product that satisfies both those who consume the product (the customer, such as radio listeners) as well as those who create it (the company and its employees). As a Program Director, I lead my team to help create entertaining and successful radio stations that emotionally connect with listeners while positively representing the local community and delivering profitable results for advertisers and stakeholders. Often, I serve those needing guidance due to extreme or near-impossible competitive situations and simply need valid answers, direction and leadership. I work in the radio, TV/cable, new media (satellite radio), marketing/advertising and select retail (beer/banking/audio-video-electronics) industries...while also spotting emerging trends in the media.

Christopher (Chris) Kennedy’s Specialties:

People and presentation/Speaking skills; Closing sales and serving client needs, exceeding expectations within budget; Bringing strategic ideas to successful completion; Influencing others to move forward together as a cohesive team; Advanced market research/Segmentation analysis and presentation; Launching new radio formats; Talent assessment and training; Protector of proprietary information; Ability to distill large quantities of information quickly; Author and editor of industry Jointblog.
