Norman Wirtz’s Summary

If you can use the services of a seasoned senior financial executive who has thrived in a fast growth environment, who is comfortable with the utilization of technology, who is sympathetic and empathetic with the sales and marketing function, who has a keenly developed service ethic, then we should meet.

I have been providing Chief Financial Officer and Computer Systems Selection and Integration Services to several companies (Sullivan & Cogliano, JWG Associates, Iron Mountain, Comforce, Job Link, Colby Industries, John Hancock, Diversified Corporate Resources, Cahners Publishing, WiredEmpire & Farms.com) including the implementation of the PeopleSoft ERP system for a staffing company.

I am also available to assist you in streamlining or reengineering or boosting the efficiency of your administrative efforts. I can also handle any merger and acquisition due diligence and integration, banking relations, collections efforts, P&L and Balance Sheet Forecasting and Budgeting. I have been instrumental in keeping the computer systems of staffing companies functioning at peak efficiency. I was the person who looked at TAD's pay and bill work flow and reorganized it to minimize the number of people required to accomplish the function.

In addition, in several of the companies that I have worked for, the founder or major entrepreneur was still at the helm when I joined the company. Each of those companies also enjoyed substantial growth in sales and earnings. I learned to foster and encourage that growth by putting in the appropriate control and reporting systems that did not stifle the initiative of the sales and marketing personnel while improving management's understanding and control of the business.
