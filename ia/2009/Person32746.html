Brent Swanson’s Summary

Versatile, award-winning artist with strong creative abilities and over 14 years of professional experience. Trained in design + art direction, with a passion for illustration + photography. Brings together communications expertise and a high degree of technical proficiency, balanced with an artist’s eye.

Develops the strategic marketing, brand standards, and identities of a wide range of clients, from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies. Experienced with creating effective communications, customized for small + large project scopes and meeting each client's needs. This has enabled strong, long-lasting relationships working with ad agencies, design firms, corporate creative departments, as well as directly with domestic and international clients.

Passionate about design done well. Approachable and easy-going personality with a strong work ethic and a desire to collaborate. Thrives most when working at a high level, managing complex, multi-faceted, integrated projects and exceeding clients’ expectations. Strives to find the best customized approach by using timely problem solving and lateral thinking abilities. Possesses the proven leadership, communications, talent and work ethic necessary to become an invaluable asset on any project.
