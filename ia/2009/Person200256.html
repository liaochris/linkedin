Robert Chandler’s Summary

Robert Chandler, fearless leader of General Levitation, is a copywriter, creative director and advertising strategist of wide and eclectic experience.

Majoring in technology, he is apparently the only person on the planet to serve as creative director for three cornerstone tech companies--IBM, Apple, and Microsoft.

Robert has led in senior creative roles at the world’s premiere agencies, including Chiat\Day, BBDO, and Ogilvy & Mather.

His group at Ogilvy originated the worldwide IBM "subtitles" campaign, "Solutions for a small planet" featuring Nuns, Frenchmen, etc.

At BBDO, he helped build for Apple their powerful brand. Highlights included the Randy Newman "I'm different" spot.

For Microsoft his campaigns launched many watershed products, including Excel, Word, & Windows.

As head of his own company, he designed the launch for Hitachi PC—renaming that company Hitachi Mobilized Computing. Under the theme “Make things happen from wherever you happen to be” Hitachi accelerated in six months from a standing start to the top tiers in market share for notebook computers.

He later launched NetZero, the pioneer free internet access provider with the highly successful “Defenders of the Free World” campaign.

Moving from microchips to taco chips, he created the first tv campaign for Baja Fresh, “Eat Life Up” directed by Leslie Dektor.

Other clients include Northrop, Chevrolet, Nissan, Mitsubishi, the Navy, Alaska Airlines, Continental Airlines.

His glamorous Hollywood twin focused on entertainment advertising for ABC, CBS, and the launch of E! As well as hundreds of movies including such classics as Jaws, All the President’s Men, One Flew Over the Cuckoo’s Nest, and Risky Business. Plus the not so classic, but a marketing and money-making triumph, The Blue Lagoon.

Robert's work has won every major ad award for his clients--Clios, One Show, Beldings, N.Y. Art Directors Club, + Gold and Silver Lions at Cannes.

Robert Chandler’s Specialties:

Turning complex propositions into simple, compelling marketing and advertising ideas.

Launching or relaunching companies. Both startups and new divisions of multinational corporations.

Marketing and business strategies. Commnunication strategies and brand narrative. Advertising campaign development and execution in all media, traditional and online.

Special knowledge: high tech, consumer tech, high finance and consumer financial services, Web commerce, and aerospace.
