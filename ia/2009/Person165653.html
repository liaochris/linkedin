Minh Pham’s Summary

I am passionate about educating investors, builders, developers and brokers who are interested in learning about creative real estate investing. My expertise lies within the topics of Lease Option Purchase, Subject To’s, Green/Blue Building Opportunities, and Business Automation Systems. One of my specialty topics is centered on the “green” buzz that is of developing amongst the real estate and building industry. Many builders and real estate developers are confused by the growing popularity among real estate buyers for green buildings or environmentally friendly real estate opportunities. This confusion in the marketplace opens up new opportunities for visionaries in the Real Estate Market. This is my area of expertise and I teach creative use of environmentally friendly practices will provide real estate investment opportunities for real estate sales agents, real estate developers, and real estate investors. My team and I are extremely responsive, very easy to work with, and only a phone call away. I have over twelve years of experience presenting dynamic, informative educational seminars and workshops to both the general public and the private sector, and in mentoring individuals to become successful investors.
