Anton Konikoff’s Summary

I am proud to be the Founder and CEO of Acronym Media (www.acronym.com), an independent, global search and Keyword-Driven Marketing agency, headquartered in New York's landmark Empire State Building, in Singapore, Sao Paulo, and around the world.

My exprerice in the search/online marketing industy goes back to 1995, with an consistent focus on search marketing and other Keyword-Driven Marketing (tm)disciplines.

Consistently rated as a "Top Ten Search Engine Agency" by Ad Age, my agency, Acronym, offers our Clients over a decade of search marketing excellence and a distinct approach to enterprise-wide keyword optimization. Our award-winning Keyword-Driven Marketing™ framework places the topmost importance on the language and keywords customers use and understand, to help Clients build effective marketing programs.

Guided by the real-time, granular keyword intelligence, Acronym's experts drive cross-media optimization and craft enterprise-level, large scale, multilingual SEO, paid search, contextual, and other digital keyword marketing programs, supported by in-depth expertise in web/campaign analytics and user experience optimization.

We are for the global industry leaders such as Four Seasons, SAP, BMW, Priceline.com, Sirius Satellite Radio, Nokia, The Wharton School of Business, Nokia, HP, and Humana.

I am also a regular speaker at Search Engine Strategies, Search Marketing Expo, Search Insider Summit, Online Marketing Summit, and eComXpo.
