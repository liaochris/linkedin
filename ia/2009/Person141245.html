Linda Hecht’s Summary

Linda Hecht has more than 21 years of experience in management, marketing, and programming in the high technology industry. Beginning her career at ESRI, the world’s leading geographic information system (GIS) software company, Ms. Hecht helped grow the small company from 100 staff to over 3,000 today. With more than $660 million in revenue, ESRI is one of the largest privately held software companies in the U.S. As Director of Marketing for ESRI, Ms. Hecht’s organization is responsible for business development, government affairs, and all areas of marketing to more than 40 industries worldwide.

Ms. Hecht holds a geography degree from University of California Los Angeles and a Master of Arts in Management from the University of Redlands. She has served on the Board of Directors of the Geospatial Information Technology Association (GITA) and the Corporate Advisory Board for the School of Business at the University of Redlands. She is a member of Zonta International and the American Marketing Association.
