Ole Schaper’s Summary

In my current position at Splitscreen Studios GmbH I am working on a new 3D browser-based game called Pirate Galaxy (http://www.pirategalaxy.com).

As Co-Founder and Director of Backend Technology, I am responsible for the backend part of our browser based game development framework which includes everything from persistence to network-layer, from performance to memory optimization.

Starting to work in the game industry in 2004 at elkware as Mobile Game Developer I gained a lot of experience in keeping tight schedules and got to know how the gaming industry works. After I finished studying in Wedel I started to work at bigpoint GmbH where I developed backend solutions for all kinds of browser based online RPGs and Action games.
