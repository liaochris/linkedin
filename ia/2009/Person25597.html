Beer Bergman’s Summary

Manager of beerbergman.com (Communication Agency, since 1996) and of Quixys France Internet Services (since 2006), I have been working in the field of communication and Internet since 1996.

Beerbergman.com is actually in the course of restructuring (Web site Audits, Workshops and Consultancy) and Quixys is registering domain names and hosting websites. Quixys also builds dynamic websites with its own Content Management System (Quixys CMS), thus providing a professional tool for small and big websites.

If teaching fine arts has taught me a great deal about perception and treatment of visual information, years of designing Web sites and printed matter have taught me to achieve an extensive know-how in realization of products that accomplish the client’s objectives.

Fields of interest and know-how are tourism, arts ("patrimoine"), education and administration (local, departemental and regional).
Inter-cultural matters (France - The Netherlands) have my special attention.

My personal goals are to develop consultancy in communication, enabling clients to render both the human and the communication parts complementary to the techniques of the Internet, and to make this knowledge available to clients who feel lost in the field of Web 2.0, "bientôt" Web 3.0 …
