Bobby Croghan’s Summary

The philosophy is quite a simple statement for CROGS LC; teaching others how to look at the overall picture of how they work and live to reduce their environmental impact. CROGS LC provides a clear and simple method of reviewing what steps can be taken to work and live more efficiently. Higher efficiency usually ends in lower operating costs and higher return rates. CROGS LC has an open business model; be direct and be sincere. With a background in commercial interior design and volunteer efforts in the residential field, CROGS LC has the knowledge of the interior built environment to help guide owners to initiate and maintain an efficient work and home life.

Bobby Croghan’s Specialties:

Stemming from a realistic view of being friendlier to the earth, implementing small green steps to reduce one’s environmental impact does not have to be an overhaul, life changing event; small actions lead to a greater impact. CROGS LC helps its client to look through the green washing and look at the essentials of working and living more green through realistic opportunities.
