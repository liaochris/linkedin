Charles Chesney’s Summary

Charles Chesney is a patent attorney in the firm’s Intellectual Property group. He represents clients in variety of intellectual property matters including patent prosecution, patent litigation, patent counseling and opinions, and trade secrets in the electrical and medical devices arts.

Charles has significant experience in practicing intellectual property law in the fields of wireless communications (including CDMA and OFDMA systems), data processing, graphical user interfaces, image and signal processing, networking, data communications, integrated circuits, computer architecture, digital and analog circuits, optics, medical devices and all levels of software.

Prior to returning to private practice, Charles was the Director and Counsel, Intellectual Property for a wireless communications company developing technology for emerging OFDMA based wireless communications networks. Prior to practicing law, Charles worked as a systems engineer in the telecommunications industry.
