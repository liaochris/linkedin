Daniel Kim, CISA, CISM, CISSP, QSA’s Summary

Daniel Kim is a Director in FTI’s Technology and Forensic and Litigation Consulting practice in Washington, DC. FTI is a specialized business consulting firm advising major corporations, financial institutions and law firms on their most critical issues, such as major litigation, mergers and acquisitions, and regulatory challenges. Daniel’s responsibilities include business development, project management and service delivery.

With over 20 years of professional experience in information technology and information security, Daniel developed his competencies by becoming effective in designing and implementing IT and security strategies that are well aligned with business objectives and consistent with applicable laws and regulations for a number of large & international firms

To discuss how we might be of assistance to you, feel free to contact me at: dan.kim AT fticonsulting.com
