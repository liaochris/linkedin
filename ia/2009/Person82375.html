    National Alliance for Mind-Body Medicine

    (Alternative Medicine industry)

    2007 — Present (2 years)

    Founded on the principle of empowering people to help themselves, the National Alliance for Mind-Body Medicine promotes education about the mind-body connection in health and healing. In addition to the label's roster releases, FUA has produced compilation CDs in conjunction with music magazines and radio stations and forged strong relationships within the film, advertising, and software development communities. Our music licensing clients include ESPN, Roxio, DMX Music, Warren Miller, XCZone Films and Hollywood Pictures.
