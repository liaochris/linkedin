Nancy Roberts Linder’s Summary

A law firm marketing expert, Nancy has published more than 100 articles and presented at more than 50 events on a variety of lawyer marketing topics. Nancy also conducts continuing legal education on topics pertaining to the ethics rules governing lawyer marketing which have been approved for ethics credit in Illinois and Wisconsin. For the past ten years, Nancy has written a monthly column on client development issues for Intellectual Property Today which is read by patent and intellectual property lawyers nationwide. Nancy is also a columnist on technology related topics for Marketing the Law Firm. Additionaly, she has published articles on law firm marketing topics in the Chicago Lawyer, ABA Young Lawyer and Administrator's Advantage.

Recent Presentations:
- "Identity Marketing: Creating & Protecting a Brand Called ‘You’," presented at ABA TECHSHOW, April 2009
- "How to Attract New Clients," Wisconsin Association of Legal Administrators webcast, January 2009
- "Ethics and Client Relationships: How to Grow Your Practice Without Violating the Rules," presented for the CBA Solo and Small Firm Committee, January 2009

Recent Articles:
