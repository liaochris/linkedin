Elliot Lederman’s Summary

Elliot Lederman specializes in the areas of intellectual property, entertainment and sports, having worked with and represented Fortune 500 Corporations (Chrysler Corporation’s Jeep division), a major entertainment studio (Universal Studios), filmmakers (Steven Spielberg) and celebrities (Paula Abdul) and world-class athletes (Tiger Woods). Mr. Lederman is well-known for his innovative approach to business development, strategic partnerships and his licensing expertise. During his 8-year tenure at Universal Studios as Vice President Sales and Business Development, Consumer Products Group, Mr. Lederman was personally responsible for producing over $2.5 billion at retail. Mr. Lederman was also responsible for managing the Jurassic Park brand, one of the most successful film franchises in Universal Studios’ history, resulting in over $600 million in sales.

Mr. Lederman served as a Second Lieutenant, Military Intelligence in the United States Army Reserve for eight years. He earned his bachelor's degree in marketing from the University of Hartford, Barney School of Business, in West Hartford, Connecticut.
