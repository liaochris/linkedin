Todd Beltz’s Summary

An American photographer that has been based in Singapore for the last 5 years, Todd Beltz is a fun-loving guy who enjoys capturing images with the heartbeat of a child. His imagery is characterized by bold compositions and light, offering a stimulation of colours, forms and textures, as he constantly searches for the beauty of everyday life.

Todd picked up a camera for the first time when he was 10 years old and hasn’t looked back since. With a passion and love for the art that has spanned 3 continents and many years, Todd pursues balance in his life through photography.

I cover a wide area of photography from Events, Spa/resort photography, Travel, Sports, Commercial, Editorial and occasionally, models.

My website: http://www.flickr.com/photos/shadou

His clients include:

Cartier, Singtel, Johnnie Walker, G.H. Mumm, The Singapore Polo Club, St. Regis Hotel (Singapore), British High Commission, Touche Spa, The Retreat Spa, Treetops Spa and Urban Fairways. His editorial works have appeared in Singapore Tatler, MillionaireAsia Magazine, Cleo Singapore and Cosmopolitan Malaysia, Singapore American Newspaper, The American Club Magazine, amongst others.

I am also a freelance model/actor.

I have appeared in more than a dozen TV commercials, a few Hollywood movies and numerous print ads in Asia. A partial list of what I have appeared in thus far:
