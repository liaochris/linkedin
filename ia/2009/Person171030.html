Nancy Broden’s Summary

I am a veteran user experience professional with extensive experience as an information architect, interaction designer and user researcher. For more than a decade I have envisioned and designed next-generation mobile, Web and software solutions for a range of industry-leading brands including Disney, Qualcomm, Microsoft, Sun Microsystems, Genetech, Federated Department Stores, and select wireless operators and mobile technology companies.

I am interested in creating digital products and services that enable people to create, maintain and grow their relationships with others. Looking for the balance between innovation and ease of use, I always strive to design exciting yet intuitive experiences.

Nancy Broden’s Specialties:

Areas of expertise include user experience strategy, information and interaction design, user research, usability testing, user interface prototyping, functional requirement analysis, specification and documentation.

Industries include E-Commerce, Financial (Banking and Insurance), Government, Fine Arts, Media and Entertainment, Technology, Wireless

To this end I performed user research in the form of contextual inquiry, focus groups and prototype & usability testing, in addition to detailed information architecture and interaction design artifacts, including business flows, site maps and wireframes.
