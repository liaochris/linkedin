Kenneth McLean, CPP’s Summary

Presently, I have responsibility for Dick’s Sporting Goods Arizona, Nevada, Utah and West Texas markets, our fastest growing areas. With the expansion and opening of new stores, this consistently requires me to exercise my ability to create teams and partnerships to accomplish specific goals. This skill is the cornerstone to my success, and the very reason I am responsible for these new markets. Prior to my current assignment, I had responsibility for both single and multiple store locations in various states, specializing in upscale luxury, specialty, and mass market. During my time with Pier 1 Imports, I had responsibility for over 400 stores in ten states managing all aspects of operational training & compliance, asset protection, investigations, and program/team development. All past and present assignments included financial responsibilities, such as budgeting, capital spending strategies and setting shrink goals, in which I worked very closely with finance and operations teams.

In addition, I serve as president of McLean & Associates Consulting. Our group handles compliance/process audits, security risk assessments, technical review and awareness program development for a variety of clients. For more information about our group, go to:

www.mclean-consulting.net
