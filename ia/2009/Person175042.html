Nicole Simon’s Summary

I am the main author of the German book on Twitter, released in December 2008; named "Twitter - Mit 140 Zeichen zum Web 2.0".

For a second edition I will be looking especially for usage in Enterprise / Corporate Environment aka inside the firewall, not limited to Twitter, but also other kind of microblogging solutions. Ping me if you have use cases / best practise examples: neezee@gmail.com or @nicolesimon.

Besides my interest in microblogging I am most known for "being cruel to be kind": honest and constructive feedback with a talent for finding the weak spots. This works especially well for companies trying to pitch or improve their products, and individuals trying to advance in their career.

Also experienced in helping others understand modern technology and improve their business / daily lives through it.

Background:
Deep knowledge of modern technologies in projects and applications such as blogs, microblogging, podcasts, communities and Second Life; from specs to production.

Involved in consulting and training around new technology since early 90s. Combining an enterprise background with being a digital bohème.

Often heard compliments from clients:
"Hmmm, never thought of that" or "Wow, never saw it that way".
"Thanks! Finally I am able to understand this!"

I am open for speaking engagements as well as workshops plus mentor roles / advisory boards. I look especially for European exchange.

additionally
Co-Moderator of Global Business Women on xing (over 11K members)

Nicole Simon’s Specialties:

pre / post conference podcasts for several international conferences
