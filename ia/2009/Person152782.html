Mark Toohey’s Summary

I have extensive experience in the television outside broadcast industry and was responsible for structuring, negotiating and drafting production contracts for major international events, Australia's national sporting competitions, top rating TV shows, major award ceremonies and broadcast engineering contracts.

My practical experience in website management and design means I revel in the opportunity to work with technology based clients (OK... I admit I am a geek at heart).

My other passion is business development and the drama of corporate strategy and business building. I can really relate to clients who are fast growing SMEs or those doing the hard yards of establishing and building a new business. That's exactly what we're also doing at Optim Legal: we're walking the talk....
