Audacia Ray’s Summary

I am fascinated both by sexuality and media - and I've crafted a living out of fusing the two in various combinations. I've been a blogger for four years (starting at WakingVixen.com and branching out from there), and in the past few years have honed other media skills like editing and video production.

I am also a well-traveled lecturer and workshop leader and have delivered talks at major universities, international conferences, and intimate gatherings. I'm currently co-teaching Human Sexuality 101 at Rutgers University at Newark.

I'm based in Brooklyn and am available for freelance writing, video production, curatorial, research, and media/event production work as well as consulting on a variety of subjects. I am also willing to travel for events and speaking engagements.
