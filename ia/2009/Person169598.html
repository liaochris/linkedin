Francesco Mucio’s Summary

At my last big job I used to manage and develop the data warehouse of Italy’s largest and leading retailer. I have been working on the architecture, ETL and performance of this Very Large Oracle Database for the past two years.

Few months ago I left Italy and moved to Poland, I'm working as Italian teacher and linguistic tester of video games, in the meantime I'm studying Polish and doing some php applications.

For the last six years I've been working as software analyst and developer in Windows systems, particularly in operational and office automation ambits. After few years as VB and C/C++ developer, I started to work with Oracle database developing in PL/SQL and Oracle Forms.

During the last two years the experience matured with Oracle database has led me to hold the management and evolution of the data warehouse of the leader of italian market of the large-scale retail trade.
