Cheryl Platz’s Summary

I am a member of the Management & Administrative Experience Team at Microsoft - our mission is to make life easier for IT professionals by creating innovative, streamlined, and intuitive interfaces for managing complex networks.

Prior to starting at Microsoft, I spent 4 years as a game producer and designer, with over half a dozen shipped titles to my credit. I managed teams of up to 15 developers and artists while handling external client relations and overseeing schedules and game designs. My platform experience includes Nintendo DS, Nintendo GBA, PC, and PS2/Xbox/GC, with a particular passion for handheld products.
