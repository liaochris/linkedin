Antal Takács’s Summary

I am a proven Manager, who is internationally mobile, trilingual and able to influence at a senior level with a 15 years experience in the automotive industry. I am also experienced in project management from “helicopter view” to detailed business analysis.

I have a proven track record of turning strategy and goals into bottom line results using Challenger Brand Marketing.
I have set up professional marketing and sales organizations from scratch, successfully launched products resulting in significant market share increase. I have built and led sales and marketing teams to make my employer the market leader.
My aim is to replicate that for your company.

I graduated from the Budapest University of Technology and Economics in 1994 as mechanical engineer. After having received the M.Sc. in engineering, I started studying finance and accounting at the College of Finance and Accountancy of Budapest Business School, from where I graduated as economist in 1998.

Having a global responsibility in big hierarchical structures worldwide I am accustomed to work in multicultural/multinational environment. I can also understand different cultures, ways of working and thinking.
My interest in history and social sciences helped me a lot to understand the various characters and to strike the right note with colleagues and business partners. I learnt how I should deal with people from different backgrounds and cultures.

I believe that my interpersonal skills, my qualifications, together with my international work experience would meet your expectations looking for Marketing and sales manager, Business development manager or General manager.
