Joe Hernandez’s Summary

Seasoned professional with diversified experience in accounting and bookkeeping systems recognized for successfully integrating advanced technology and accounting consequently improving organizational growth and profitability. Valuable experience gained predominantly by directing own accounting / bookkeeping practice and as an independent contract employee. Practicing public accounting and bookkeeping since 2002 servicing small businesses with emphasis in non-profit organizations. Broad experience in all aspects of accounting, auditing and financial management. Direct experience developing diverse industries chart of accounts and business procedures: Non-Profit Organizations, Law Practice (Criminal, Civil, Title, Immigration), Real Estate, Retail Merchandising (POS), Construction (light), Food Service (Fast Food, Restaurant), and Flooring/Dealership (Automotive, Boats, Airplanes).

* Experienced in the preparation of schedules and information for tax return (Schedule C, 1040, 1120, 1120S, 1065, Sales Tax/ B&O Tax).
* Proficient in web based Payroll services including the preparation of regulatory compliance reports and electronic filing (Federal Tax Deposit Coupon, W2, W3 and 1099, Form 941, 940, 944, State Unemployment Tax Return, WA L&I) using QB Payroll, PayCycle, ADP, Wells Fargo, Paychex and Payroll Relief.
* Expert QuickBooks (QB) Advisor in the areas of Installation, Setup and Training.
* Competent in the use of web based Accounting Relief and Payroll Relief systems from AccountantsWorld.
* Done extensive development of ad hock applications using Intuit’s Software Development Kit (QBFC).
* Specialist in the Unified Financial Reporting Standard for Non-Profit Organizations.
* Competent negotiating with IRS, Dept of Labor & Industry, Employment Security Dept, Dept of Revenue and other agencies regarding payroll and other tax issues on behalf of client/employer in the states of Washington, Florida and Georgia.
