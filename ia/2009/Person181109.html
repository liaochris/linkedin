Anish Parikh’s Summary

A talented professional with 10 years of successful leadership in various areas of web industry. Anish possesses Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics & Communication. His extensive experience covers web designing, web / software development, e-commerce operations and strategic planning functions. Anish has helped and continually helping companies of all sizes to define their online presence and maximize results through concrete execution in the fields of e-commerce and web/software development.

With Talash.com, he was extensively involved and heading E-commerce SBU in capacity of AVP – Ecommerce. In 4 years with talash.com, Anish was involved in strategic planning, forecasting, budgeting, ecommerce strategic planning, online user experience improvement, product assortment planning and development, Business Processes development and organizational improvement. During his tenure with talash.com, business increased multifold with 500% increase in revenue, 400% increase in customer base and increased products to 2000 to 18000 with vendors from across India. He setup logistics very effectively.

Prior to talash.com, Anish worked as Sr. Project Manager with Opal Infotech, one of the leading IT companies in Gujarat, India. He successfully handled projects of global and local clients on Intranet Solutions, Web Designing and Development, Web Maintenance. List of clients and projects handled by Anish is extensive and rich.
