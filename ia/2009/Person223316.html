Stephen Kember’s Summary

Since graduating as a postgraduate with honours from Cardiff University in 1976 Stephen has worked for in a variety of business development and marketing positions across the British, Polish, Western European, African [Tanzania], the Baltic’s [Lithuania & Latvia] and the Japanese marketplace.

He has a wide range of commercial, joint venture and business transformation expertise complemented by a broad range of practical experience focused on: market research, business strategy, marketing, 80-20 profit focus, using innovation to transform profitability, performance review, international market entry planning and business development coaching.

Stephen is an energetic and strong team player with an enthusiastic desire for success; someone that likes challenges and rises to them. He works well at senior levels and is strong in the areas of: motivation, presentation, listening, problem solving, lateral thinking, negotiation, people management, client handling and communication.

Stephen Kember’s Specialties:

Revealing: hidden assets, untapped and over looked opportunities, performance review and market research, marketing, sales and publicity, presentation; negotiation and PR, the development of joint ventures,internet marketing and search engine optimsation; and innovation and 80:20 focus.
