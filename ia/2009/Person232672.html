Thomas A. McDevitt, CFA, CFP’s Summary

My partner and I started McDevitt & Kline, LLC (www.ceworkshops.com) in 2007. We provide professional continuing education courses to CPAs and attorneys. Since the start of operations, we have awarded over 40,000 credit hours. Our mission is quite simple: to provide busy accounting and legal professionals with courses that are interesting, relevant, convenient, and reasonably priced.

We are an Accredited Provider with the PA Supreme Court Continuing Legal Education Board (ID# 5963). For additional information, please visit www.pacle.org. The company is an approved CPA CE provider for the PA State Board of Accountancy (ID# PX177627), the NJ State Board of Accountancy (ID# 20CE00207900), and the NY State Board of Accountancy (ID# 002292). Our webinars are accepted as Live/Group Study courses by the State Boards of Accountancy in AL, AZ, CA, CO, CT, GA, IA, ID, IN, KY, LA, MA, ME, MI, MO, NC, NJ, NM, NV, NY, OH, PA, and WY.
