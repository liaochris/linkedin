Fiona Doherty’s Summary

Over 12 years of PR experience, working in both New York and London. Dedicated individual and team player; proven at building trusted relationships with clients and co-workers and designing and implementing results-focused communications campaigns.

Special interest in the use of social networking tools and strategies to enhance communications with important communities and stakeholders.

Experience in hi-tech, business to business, emerging technologies and start-ups and consumer technology.

Current and former clients include IBM, Lenovo, Xerox, EarthLink, Cablevision, Unisys, Packard Bell, Computer Associates, Parrot, Nationalgeographic.com, Priceline & Evolution Robotics.
