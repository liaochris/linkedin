Fayyaz Alam’s Summary

I manage the IT Infrastructure for Emirates Group, which includes the data centers, servers, networks and end user devices. I and my team are responsible for provision of a cost effective, fit for purpose, scalable, stable and secure IT infrastructure which caters to the current and future needs of the Emirates Group.

Prior to this role, I managed the IT strategy and business solution development for Engineering, Flight Operations, Service Delivery and Network Operations divisions of Emirates. In previous roles with Emirates Group, I headed IT for Commercial, Planning and Revenue Accounting functions and has been with the Emirates Group since November 1991. Before joining Emirates Group, worked as a systems analyst and developer for National Computers in Pakistan .

I hold a Masters Degree in Computer Science from University of Karachi and a Masters Degree in Business Administration from University of Bradford.

Fayyaz Alam’s Specialties:

Proven hands on experience of Strategic IT Planning, Enterprise Architecture, Business Process Re-engineering, Relationship Management, Project Potfolio Management, Application Development, IT Infrastructure Management, and Service Level Management.
