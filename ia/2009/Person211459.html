Scott Harrington’s Summary

HAVA Partners is the leader in online training for Poll Workers in the United States. We have developed a solution for states, counties and cities to assure compliance with the Help America Vote Act (HAVA). In 2004, we trained over 16,000 Election Day workers.

HAVA Partners use the power of technology to overcome the problems of scale, quality, and cost to train Poll Workers. Our staff members are experienced and expert on-line educators, web-site designers, technology-interface designers, marketers, and legal compliance consultants. HAVA Partners understand well the challenges facing states, counties and individual Poll Workers.

Our team brings together all the skills necessary to train Poll Workers and educate voters on the changes brought on by HAVA. We bring unique expertise in online training, web design, technology, election administration, working with the public sector and talking to the electorate..
