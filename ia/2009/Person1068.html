Aaron Downes’s Summary

In this time of business and life as Unusual!! It is now more important than ever to be creative, innovate and IGNITE our potential.

Tactical Skills Development

If you are a business owner you will certainly be technically proficient in your area of business however you are also required to lead a team, generate new business, influence suppliers and manage yourself, this is what is known as the tactical side. Through learning, tactical skills development can be the key to unlocking the results you are looking for in your business. My aim is to support the development of these skills in business, turning one day workshops into 6 week long interventions designed to embed the learning and deliver performance improvement.

Creative Thinking for Business - "I2P" Ideas to Profit

Henry Ford once said “paying attention to simple little things that most men neglect makes a few men rich”

In today's business environment as business leaders we are required to relearn the things we may have forgotten or learn new ways of doing business in order to succeed. Taking the time out to take a 40,000ft view of your operation can and will deliver the fresh thinking you need.

"I2P" is a facilitated workshop which will define your business challenge, generate new ideas and a fresh perspective, test the validity of those ideas and pump them through a business model to see the revenue potentials. In either 1 hour or a full day you are guaranteed;

A new way of thinking
A set of new ideas
An action plan to deliver those ideas in your business
A business model ready for implementation
