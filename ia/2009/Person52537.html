Dana Ben David’s Summary

Founder and visionary of an innovative kids' virtual world startup with an urgent social agenda. We are developing a brand for kids, including cross platform applications and a TV format as complimentary media. We have our focus is upon creativity and social responsibility.
I'm an Award winning creative director and branding expert, in the animation, motion graphics and SFX field. At JCS I was responsible for leading channel branding processes in Israel and in the international market, with emphasize on Chinese market, leading a team of 30 designers and animators through out all development and post production phases. I was also managing the commercials department, working with advertising agencies and interactive agencies, and mobile firms as complimentary media to the advertising campaign or TV channel. I was also responsible for exploring new fields and opportunities working hand in hand with the marketing director of the firm.
A consultant for start up companies, interactive companies, branding firms, post production and animation studios.
Kids' virtual life expert who gives lectures and consulting services in that field.

Managing skills, marketing skills, creative vision, cutting edge design, networking, knowledge of all post production and special effects phases, strategic way of thinking and branding, knowledge of 2d & 3d SFX and animation technology, writing skills, presentation skills, business development, broad vision.
