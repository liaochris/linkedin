William Maloney IV’s Summary

I'm reaching out to the public to help inform the misinformed, to enlighten the entangled and to let loose those who are locked up by the flawed approaches they're offered to get and stay physically fit.

My aim is to provide truth and clarity in an industry where there is so much deception and confusion. When people attempt to find fitness, it usually begins with an attachment to conventional methods such as diet and aerobic exercise. Conventional methods have us eating less and exercising more to facilitate weight loss. This is known as the "calories in vs. calories out" equation to positive physical change. Conventional methods have us focusing on cardio because weight training doesn’t burn fat and cardio “must be at low intensity and long in duration” for fat burning to occur. Conventional methods have us eating high carb-low fat diets, higher protein-lower carb diets or all organic food to stay fit or get fit. You'll hear things like "have a salad, dressing on the side, no cheese no croutons" or "have a nutri-grain bar or a low-carb energy bar if you can't eat a meal". And when you attempt to make a change to get fit, but you fail, and then you try again and fail, and try again and again and again and again, it's human nature to return to a place of comfort (ex. the couch with your favorite comfort food). The reason you weren't successful wasn't because you failed, but rather your approach to getting fit failed you.

Conventional methods aren't working. America wouldn't have high obesity rates if conventional wisdom were truly result oriented. Through my various ventures and education, you will be intrigued with the most up to date and conventional-shattering information to have you possibly thinking differently about the way you move, the way you eat, the way you think and the way you feel.
