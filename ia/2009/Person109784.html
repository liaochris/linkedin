Jennifer Bradley’s Summary

Jennifer Bradley has over seven (7) years of non-profit experience in the Las Vegas community. Her former marketing and human resource background; changed it's course when her son was born with an undiagnosed syndrome. Thankfully he is doing very well; yet her career developed into a passion for wanting to be a voice for those who couldn't fight; and share, create and improve resources available in the healthcare and educational community. Jennifer has volunteered for Miracle Flights for Kids, Spirit Therapies, Inc., and the Make-A-Wish Foundation of Southern Nevada. She has lived in Las Vegas for over 10 years and is the proud mother of two beautiful children, Lauren and Brett. She plans to continue to collaborate with other charitable non-profits that all strive to make a difference and improve the quality of life for families in our state.
