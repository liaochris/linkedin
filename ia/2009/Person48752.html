Denise C. Gonzales’s Summary

As CEO, Denise brings a broad range of creative talents, goal-driven vision, and proven marketing and project management expertise to Conduit Development. A strategic thinker and innovator with a strong eye for design, Denise launched her professional career as an executive with a Fortune 500 company. Her tenure was marked by several key achievements, including holding an executive leadership position in the U.S./Pacific region for UPS. Prior to founding Conduit Development, Denise helped shape the way businesses approached their marketing and advertising structures through the creation of Conduit International, a strategic international advertising and marketing agency based in Long Beach, Calif. With her grandfather, John G. Franco, serving as one of the largest contractors for schools, military bases, housing tracts, commercial buildings and industrial facilities throughout Ventura County for more than two decades, Denise is proud to return to her roots in the development and construction field. Today, she is able to combine traditional business acumen and leadership skills with her family’s multigenerational commitment to building homes and businesses to last for years to come.
