Jack Simpson’s Summary

Named by Departures magazine as an "Arbiter of Taste", Jack is regarded as a leader in the field of personal menswear design. His services are directed to successful men who value refined self-expression, and his goal for each is the development of a wardrobe that is both classic and innovative, yet specifically geared to personal taste. The workmanship of the clothing is exemplary, as the Robb Report named his made to measure suit one of the world's finest. His company offers, in addition, a full bespoke service. These garments are completely handmade and rival the best of Savile Row, while imparting a natural ease more appropriate for American lifestyles.

The company offers a comprehensive image consulting service and is committed to wardrobe management by providing precise direction in accessories coordination, in addition to collecting digital images of each ensemble for easy reference.

Simpson is known for his personal tailoring service for celebrities, but he also designs complete wardrobes for business leaders, financiers, entrepreneurs and influential members of the legal and medical communities. He has been featured in Esquire, Gentleman's Quarterly, Departures, The Robb Report, Cigar Aficionado, Golf Connoisseur and other prominent lifestyle magazines and has appeared on the Today Show.

He is available for private consultation in the bespoke showroom in New York, or, if preferred, in one's home or office. His seminar, "The Thirteen Wardrobe Necessities for Accomplished Men" can be booked for group presentations.

Jack Simpson’s Specialties:

Luxury brand positioning, retail business development, wholesale business development, garment design, apparel textile design, home textile design, image consultation, lifestyle based wardrobe management.
