Michael Scovetta’s Summary

My current position as a security architect within CA gives me wide access to interesting projects. Though focused on Single Sign On initiatives, I am also heavily involved in a company-wide software security program and as a security advisor on many projects. I have also started a developer-to-developer "brown bag" session that works like a talk at a conference. I'm having a lot of fun with it!

Prior to my current position, I was a senior security consultant with Cigital, which gave me the opportunity to conduct penetration testing on a wide range of applications, perform code remediation, and to design a set of Java technical standards. Though my tenure at Cigital was relatively short, it was a richly rewarding experience.

Prior to Cigital, I was responsible for application security within the Risk Control area at UBS. In that capacity, I conducted a large number of security risk assessments on applications, application designs, policy exceptions, and business processes. I served as a subject matter expert in the space of information security, and functioned de facto as security architect on a number of projects.

Prior to UBS, I was a senior application developer at CA, writing internal applications to manage HR and financial processes. In this role, I functioned as developer, designer, and technical architect as needed.
