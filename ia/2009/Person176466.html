10 years experience in thermo plastics, injection molding, and mold tempering technique.

Anders Aberg’s Specialties:

In my occupation as manager of a Plastic pallet factory in Perstorp Sweden, I had the challenging task of production planning and managing of production personnel. Process, product and polymer material optimization was other interesting areas for me to work with.
As the production of pallets in Sweden, was similar to our production of pallets in the US, I have the great opportunity to use my skills to assist with quality, material and welding issues.
