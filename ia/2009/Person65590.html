Don Bain’s Summary

Technology-fluent business executive with proven ability to operate companies, manage programs and projects, to profitably grow small companies and to achieve business results for corporations with technology. Hands-experience with web 2.0, virtual worlds, consumer and enterprise applications. Extensive applied research on social software, behavioral economics and practical neuroscience for improved usability and insights into user behavior. Poly-cultural executive experience including positions in U.S.A. and Europe. Active in the venture community.

Don Bain’s Specialties:

Identifying business value of technology and organizing to achieve it. Executive leadership in software, technology and consulting including president, partner, vp and program manager. Web 2.0 and virtual world applications and business models. Supply chain and process optimization. Enterprise application implementation best practices. Early stage investor and adviser. Co-inventor on multiple patents.
