Joleen Firek’s Summary

Emmy Award - winning Writer / Producer and Director seeks a professionally challenging production environment in which quality programming is non-negotiable. From film & video production to Live events, candidate boasts an impeccable reputation in regard to project / product quality and relationship-building among project teams, production crews and clients.
