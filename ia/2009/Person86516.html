Gil Asakawa’s Summary

Writer, journalist and online content consultant currently working as Manager of Audience Development for MediaNews Group Interactive. Also writes a blog about pop culture, technology, media and Asian American culture at http://nikkeiview.com/blog. Co-authored "The Toy Book" (Knopf, 1991) with Leland Rucker and wrote "Being Japanese American" (Stone Bridge Press, 2004). Music critic in a previous life for 15+ years and went to too many concerts. http://gillers.com

Gil Asakawa’s Specialties:

Online content, new media, "Web 2.0," Asian Americans, Japanese Americans, pop culture, Internet, diversity
