Bex White’s Summary

Graphic Designer with over 5 years experience currently working for digitial agency Pancentric in London. My experience is predominantly in the digital arena focusing on websites, email design, banners and other digital media but I also have experience in branding, print design and illustration. Clients include Tesco, British Airways, Motorola, AXA, Cadbury, Petplan, Barclays and other top brands as well as experience dealing with smaller clients and family businesses. Experience creating projects for both B2B and B2C use.

As well as my design skills I greatly enjoy speaking at seminars and conferences, my experience here has been mainly around email design and best practice; preparing, presenting and holding post talk Q&A sessions. I also have experience preparing and holding training on design and coding to groups of both colleagues and clients as well as writing best practice and procedural guideline documentation.

Bex White’s Specialties:

Design: Web design. Email design and email complaint html. Basic Flash animation. Layout design for print. Illustration. Icon design. Logo design.

Software: Office, Adobe Creative Suite 3 (Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Indesign, Flash (Without advanced AS).

Personal: Teamplayer, Pro-active, Confident speaker, Imaginative. Knowledge of email industry. Experience training and presenting to clients and staff. Team leadership and project management.
