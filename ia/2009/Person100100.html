Crystal M. Craven’s Summary

Follow me on Twitter @ITccraven

SEPG North America 2010
http://www.sei.cmu.edu/sepgna/2010/

As one of three IT Department members of Hancock Askew & Co., LLP, Certified Public Accountants and Advisors, I assist in maintaining the daily network operations of 80 employees, 15 Servers, and 85 Desktop and Laptops in Savannah, Atlanta and remotely as a Network Administrator. In May, 2008, I was installed as president of ITPA (Information Technology Professionals Association of Savannah). In August 2008, elected as the president of the Creative Coast IT Connection. Currently, I serve as a board member for Armstrong Atlantic State University's Industrial Advisory Board and for the Rape Crisis Center of the Coastal Empire. I have a passion for promoting Savannah as a viable IT community. Working with members of the IT Industry with motivation from SEDA (Savannah Economic Development Association) and The Creative Coast Alliance, I am helping to develop, nurture and sustain IT Talent in Savannah and the Creative Coast.
