Guillermo Di Donato’s Summary

I am a passionate technology entrepreneur with a die-hard optimist attitude that can not stay still. As technology either, we make a good team.

Currently I am working in GEMALTO as a Solutions Manager for the South Cone (Argentina, Chile, Paraguay, Uruguay and Bolivia) leading an excellent team of professionals focused on promoting Software Solutions and Value Added Services (VAS) for GSM Mobile Telecom Operators. Previously to the company merge, I was working in GEMPLUS, also as a Solutions Manager for Latin America South.

I was part of a Technology Startup in the Software and Services Business.

I have also worked in ERICSSON Canada as a Software Engineer in the TDMA Development and Support Radio Department, and in DUPONT Argentina as an Electronic Technician within their Instrumentation and Electric Maintenance Team, thanks to a scholarship given to me by DUPONT when finishing my Secondary School.

I run my Blog (http://Blog.GuillermoDiDonato.com/), where I write about Technology, Telecommunications, Mobile Value-Added Services, Social Networks, Mobile Marketing and in general about the art of transforming raw technologies into appealing and easy to use services.

I enjoy playing with new technologies, reading books, blogs and biographies about successful Businessmen and Entrepreneurs that print with passion and personality their professional careers.
