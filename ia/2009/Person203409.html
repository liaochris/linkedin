Ronen Offer’s Summary

Ronen brings extensive management experience in international marketing, sales and business development.

Ronen is a co-founder and managing partner of April Business Consulting, a firm that provides marketing, business development and sales planning and implementation services to improve and expedite your company’s growth.

Prior to founding April, Ronen held several senior positions including head of Marketing and Business Development department at PricewaterhouseCoopers in Israel, CEO of the start-up company Veradis, and more.

Ronen holds a BA in computer science and economics and an MBA degree from Tel Aviv university (Wharton - Recanati program).
