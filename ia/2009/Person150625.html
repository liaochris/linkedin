Marilyn Hintz’s Summary

I am a certified athletic trainer and strength coach. I love the sports medicine and health and fitness industries. I am most passionate in educating America to become more knowledgeable with regards to understanding what it takes to get optimal fitness results and why it is so difficult for many people.
My specific interest lies within the prevention of knee injuries or chronic pain occurrences in female athletes. The basic principles of physics applies directly to our body, and I believe everyone needs to train with the ultimate goal of optimizing the center of mass of our body throughout movement. And from this, extraordinary results are produced.
