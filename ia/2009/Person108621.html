Jeffrey Sharp’s Summary

I am a consultant at Customer Returns Consulting. I specialize in enabling midsize through F500 to hear their clients real-time, and to execute actions that improve customer loyalty and overall operations.

I have an extreme passion for the customer experience movement. Companies need to understand the impact a negative customer experience has on their "good profit". Our economy will never settle until companies realize that buying more revenue through advertising and sales will never produce the "good profit" that is necessary to sustain companies in hard times, as we are seeing today. We know the economy will have ups and downs. History proves it. The companies that survive are the companies who make both their investors and customers alike happy through a superb customer experience.
