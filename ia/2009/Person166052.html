Sonja Franzmann’s Summary

A healthy body should be everybody’s first priority in life. The key to improving one’s health and fitness is to start from the inside out, and to make achievable goals along the way. It is important to enjoy the journey towards your health goals and not just the results – if you can’t find a way to enjoy your training you’re not going to stick with it. This belief not only guides my training philosophy but also my career choice as my own interest in a healthy lifestyle led to my career in fitness and nutrition.

Fitness and Nutrition go hand in hand - you cannot truly feel the benefits of one without the other, being both a Personal Fitness Trainer and Nutrition Consultant constantly reinforces this to me. Experience, education and a valuable training philosophy allow for development of specific programs for clients that not only achieve results, but are also fun along the way.
