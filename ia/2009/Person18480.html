Anuj Agrawal’s Summary

Zyoin is India's no.1 - Online Recruitment Market place, which combines the operations and features of a job site, a recruitment agency, employee referrals and online tracking onto one common web based platform. Not just this, there’s more in the offing…

What can you do on Zyoin?

Earn : History is testimonial to the fact that the best hiring has been through referrals. Zyoin gives you the opportunity to send jobs to your vast network of friends and aid them in their quest for the perfect career. Not just that, we pay you upto 3 lakh (USD 7500) cash to do so!!!

Ask Questions: Here at Zyoin we understand the importance of your career. Thus we provide you with an opportunity to Ask Questions pertaining to the different aspects of your career path and its development. A chance to learn from others’ experiences.

Refer Employers: You can let us know if you know any company is hiring and you could be eligible to win cash rewards upto Rs 5000.
