Mark McSherry’s Summary

Cisco Network Engineer / Architect, with current Routing & Switching CCIE (13615) and current Chartered Engineer.

I contract through IPTel Solutions and currently consult for a large Queensland utility.

Current design work includes wide area networking, across Queensland.

This network has integrated various access technologies, including fixed WAN links, ISDN dial, NextG, Telstra IP Services (in particular the Telstra GWIP offering) and satellite access for polling of SCADA type devices. I also provide detailled documentation and have knowledge of other technologies such as DNS and network monitoring and mangement applications.

I have previously worked in an internet Data Centre as Network Manager, using BGP and I am familiar with Internet service provider operations. Working in a startup ISP allowed me to gain many unique skills outside of the pure technical operations of the company.

Prior to this I worked as Internet Planning and Development Manager for all Internet based products for Jersey Telecom, based on the Island of Jersey. I worked on an extremely interesting project to design and install the first ADSL rollout for the Island.
