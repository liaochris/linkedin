Sandra Jennings’s Summary

Medicinal Chemistry: Leveraged tools for the design compound libraries to solve chemical series issues. Provided input into the building of pharmacophore and categorical models. Established a strategy for virtual screening by applying structure searching methods. Participated in projects that delivered candidates in Phase I studies.

Organic Synthesis and Process Chemistry: Technical expert in synthetic chemistry in small and large scale up to 300 kilograms, with a wide variety of reactions performed. Developed large scale synthetic routes for active pharmaceutical ingredients (API) in phase I – III studies.

Combinatorial Chemistry: Enabled new protocols and validated the chemistry. Use of equipment including Tecan, Gilson liquid handlers, Genevac.

Project Management and Coordination: Served group point of contact for projects. Coordinated assay capacity prioritized screening queues. Co-prioritized the use of internal and CRO resources for large library production and compound synthesis.

Sandra Jennings’s Specialties:

Chemical synthesis from 10 milligram to 300 kilogram scale; microwave assisted synthesis; near and far- shore outsourcing of libraries, compounds and scale-ups; API manufacturing under good manufacturing practices (GMP); Green Chemistry; combinatorial chemistry, protocol optimization, validation; library design, monomer selection, enumeration and cherry picking; virtual screening, HTS triage and chemical series selection.
