Suzanne Currie’s Summary

Experienced in Sales and Marketing of Alternative Investment Strategies. Manage platform of Alternatives including Asset Based Lending funds, Distressed Real Estate funds, Mortgage Origination as well as partnerships with Strategic Partners. Distribution through silos of robust institutional roladex in the endowment, foundation, single and multi-family offices as well as off shore banks in Europe and Middle East.
