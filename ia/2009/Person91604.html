Haim Sternberg’s Summary

twitter address http://twitter.com/drdata
I have been the president and CTO of Cherry Systems Inc. the best data recovery lab, for over 20 years. I love to talk about the many aspects of data and data loss, both from technical and psychological values to owners and users. I know the industry very well, Including most major suppliers and active data recovery labs in the US and abroad. I championed many customer service initiatives to help professional and novices in the process of recovery and prevention of data loss. Including data valuation as well as discussion of how to help C level realize and understand the value of the company data and information systems.

Haim Sternberg’s Specialties:

Data recovery from most digital media storage devices. Like hard drives, micro drives, flash media, magnetic tapes. Research and development of data valuation systems
