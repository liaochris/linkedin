Liz Pope’s Summary

Drawing from a rich experience working with top public relations professionals and organizations in Denver, I have worked with a broad range of clients specializing in technology, architecture, non-profit and health care.

With a background covering both agency and corporate experience, I specialize in social media, strategic planning, media outreach and results-driven solutions under the umbrella of public relations and marketing.
