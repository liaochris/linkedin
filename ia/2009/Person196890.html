Ron Quintero’s Summary

CFA review programs in New York and Connecticut. Award-winning lecturer and trainer. Leads more than 20 CFA review programs annually. Active worldwide as financial trainer. 30+ years experience in mergers & acquisitions, valuations, turnarounds & workouts, and accounting. Served boards of public, nonpublic, and professional organizations as member or advisor. Author of numerous books, articles, and professional training manuals. Audios and videos of lectures and seminars have been made available for commercial sale by leading professional and educations organizations.

Ron Quintero’s Specialties:

Most active CFA trainer in the world over the past 15 years. Professional fields of expertise include valuations of businesses, securities, and intangible assets: mergers & acquisitions; turnarounds, workouts, and bankruptcy; financial forecasting; and litigation support services. Testifed as expert witness on more than 50 occasions in courts throughout the U.S.
