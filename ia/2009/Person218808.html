Steve Kenniston’s Summary

I have worked for a number of different software technology companies adding value by combining good business sense and a strong technology background.

I have worked with a number of vendors as clients in the software and storage software arena assisting them with driving product and supporting their products and technologies. I have also assisted in driving business development at a strategic level with a number of partners.

I have published a number of articles on storage technology including thought leadership papers that have been published in a number of trade magazines on concepts such as "The Perfect Storage Brick", "Data Center Automation", "IT, The Value Center", and many more.

I also am a featured speaker at a number of Storage Trade Conferences discussing the future direction of technology from an end user perspective.
