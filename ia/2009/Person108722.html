Jeff Schnabel’s Summary

Serving SW Michigan, South Bend, Mishawaka, Granger, Elkhart & Surrounding Areas


Are you looking for an experienced real estate professional who knows the local market and can get the results you desire? Let me put my knowledge, experience, and dedication to work for you. Give me a call today and I will immediately get to work for you satisfying your real estate needs.

I was born and raised in South Bend, Indiana. I have a Bachelors Degree in Business Management through Indiana University. My background for the past 20 years includes leasing, buying, selling and managing real estate. I have experience with single family homes, condos and multi-family complexes up to1000 units. Since I have lived in the area all my life I know it well and have many connections that can prove beneficial to a real estate transaction. I can help you find the perfect property to suit your real estate needs.

I have served on various housing and city boards both in Indiana and Michigan that were responsible for zoning, housing decisions and architectural approval for requested changes. This, along with my property management experience, has afforded me the ability to possess a "critical eye" when it comes to real estate. This is not to say I pretend to know what an inspector knows; I do leave inspections to the professionals.

I have many local service contractor connections from construction to cleaning. This can prove to be a great time saver when you are ready to add the personal touches to the home you purchase through me. I am educated, hard working, honest and pay the highest fiduciary duty to my clients. Please let me put my skills to work for you and represent you in your next real estate transaction.
