Serge Fenenko’s Summary

Serge Fenenko started his career by setting up an own business in marketing of cultural events. Then, moved to the financial services industry and spent eight years with the Dutch Rabobank. In his latest position at Rabobank, he managed the Business Development department of Rabobank Corporate Clients. In 2005, he established the interactive marketing agency Novocortex.

Novocortex manages market introductions of innovative products. With the focus on online interaction with your clients. Novocortex is part of the Dutch Marketingfacts Network. Within the network, Novocortex is responsible for Interactive Strategy Audits and online advertising campaigns.

Novocortex won a number of awards on advertising festivals, such as Golden Hammer, Golden Award of Montreux, Red Apple in Moscow and Kiev International Ad Festival (see http://portfolio.novocortex.com).

Serge Fenenko studied History in the former Soviet Union and Business Administration in the Netherlands, and holds an MBA of Rotterdam School of Management, the Erasmus University's business school.

Serge Fenenko’s Specialties:

Interactive marketing using online and mobile channels.
Integration of the internet marketing in the overall marketing strategy of your company.
Product marketing and business development with the focus on market introductions of innovative products.
Development and marketing of financial services (online banking, online insurance, online payments).
