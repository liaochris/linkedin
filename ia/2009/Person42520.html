Chinar Deshpande’s Summary

With over 6 years of enriching experience as a CIO (out of total 17 years of professional tenure in the field of information technology), Chinar has lead various complex and innovative IT initiatives with direct business impact. Leadership with his team and with business teams (who have been his internal customers) has been his forte and he has been very successful in effecting change management at an organizational level. His achievements have reflected in him winning the CIO 100 Award in 2006.

Chinar is an Electrical Engineer from VJTI (Mumbai) and has MS (Computer Sc.) & an MBA from University of Louisville, USA. Chinar brings with him a deep experience with variety of latest technologies (including RFID) and advanced business solutions and has effectively developed relationships with top IT companies including the likes of SAP, IBM, HP, Microsoft, Accenture, at the global platform. Chinar's work as well as his breakthrough views in applying technology for successfully managing business challenges, have featured in many Magazines & News Papers such as Network Computing, CIO Magazine, Computer Express, Business World, The CTO forum, RIS News (US), Economic Times, Times of India etc…

Currently Chinar is
• On the editorial advisory board of … “CIO” magazine and “CIO Decisions” magazine
• Visiting faculty at two renowned management schools teaching “IT in Retail”
• Founding Member of Technology Forum of RAI (Retail Association of India)

After pioneering efforts in Retail IT at Pantaloon Retail, Chinar has decided to break free and expand the domain of influence to much larger space, by becoming a leader in technology & business process consulting in Retail & Distribution-led industry. At present he the CEO of Creative IT India Pvt. Ltd. (a 100% subsidy of a US-based technology company).
