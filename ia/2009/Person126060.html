Kara Smith’s Summary

Karasma Media is the first Harlem based public relations boutique specializing in social media campaigns for the legal marketing industry.

I have worked as a a New York freelancer for more than 20 years in the marketing, television, and film businesses in New York. My experience in the media industry and within the corporate sector is multi-faceted and varies from writing press releases and launching successful e-mail campaigns, to producing numerous stage, screen and local venue events.
