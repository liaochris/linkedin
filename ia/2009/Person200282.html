Bob Coombs (L.I.O.N.)’s Summary

With several $$ billion in generated revenue to his credit, Bob Coombs is regarded as one of the most successful professionals in the Australian Information Technology and Communications industry.
In a career spanning over 25 years Bob has been with some of the most significant ITC companies in the world, amongst them companies like DWS, EDS, CSC, KPMG and Getronics where he has held very senior sales and general management positions. In his former years Bob was Deputy CIO at CSR Limited.
His clients are also in the blue ribbon category such as Commonwealth Bank, Westpac, Optus, Telstra, Commonwealth Attorney General, Wesfarmers, AXA, Alliance, Dell, HP and Cisco.
Bob mentors sales professionals and sales teams to take advantage of his sales knowledge and network. HP was a client.
Bob has a Masters and Diploma from Sydney University, a Bachelor of Commerce degree from University of NSW, is a Fellow of the Australian Institute of Company Directors, qualified CPA and is the current Chairman of the University of Wollongong School of Informatics Advisory Board. He is also a past Director and member of The Australian Golf Club and a Director of Randwick Rugby.
.
