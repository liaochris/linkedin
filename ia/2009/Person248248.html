Bill Nelson’s Summary

I have been in the middleware space for the past 20 years. I have experience in both developing and managing products as they pertain to web services, educational services (i.e. training), corporate-wide directory (LDAP), portal, messaging, and for the past 3 years have specialized in Identity Management solutions.

I have been with GCA for roughly ten years where I have grown both professionally as well as intellectually. I have been the Internet Practice Leader, the Director of Education, and the VP of Education and Business Services. I am currently the VP of Professional Services where I am taking our extensive experience in the middleware space and building the consulting business.

My overall goals are to continue challenging myself with new and interesting opportunities and see where they take me. The ride has been great so far but you never know what is around the corner.
