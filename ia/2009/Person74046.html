Eric Brackett’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur who stays with a project and never gives up. I started BTI, then called Brackett Telecom., in 1985 while attending college. From there, it has been one thrilling experience after another. My experience includes:

1. Starting BTI in a one room office, growing it to a national network of partners with offices in Chicago, LA, Sacramento, and Minneapolis.

2. Becoming one of Illinois Bell's top authorized distributors and then reaching the top of Ameritech's program, which was disestablished by the acquisition of SBC and then AT&T.

3. Founding Great America Networks, Inc. and becoming a national competitive telecommunications carrier taking back most of my customers from AT&T.

4. Co-fouding Great America Networks Conferencing, LLC with my partner, Reno Provine.

5. Raising three wonderful boys.

6. Living in Chicago and Los Angeles.

7. Coaching and being coached by other business owners and managers through business groups including Vistage.
