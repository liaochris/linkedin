Adrian White’s Summary

My most recent work has focused on innovative content management solutions using Interwoven technology. My areas of expertise include development and consulting on web content management solutions (particularly using Interwoven TeamSite, SitePublisher, LiveSite, OpenDeploy, Metatagger), web content change management (using Interwoven OpenDeploy, Apache Ant), application deployment, front-end web technologies (HTML/XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX technologies, XML, XSLT) and integrations with various application technologies (J2EE, .NET, CGI, Microsoft Office Sharepoint Server/MOSS, Interwoven OpenDeploy) and service oriented architectures (.NET and Java Web Services, RESTful services).

I have built content management solutions for systems as diverse as the Inflight Entertainment System for the new Airbus A380 planes at Qantas, through to complex and highly customised document management systems to publish online help documentation for 10K+ frontline support staff in large insurance companies. Building content management systems for sophisticated websites is my bread and butter.

I have been technical lead on, and project managed a number of medium sized content management initiatives. I also have spoken at several public conferences on web change management and application deployment in Sydney and Melbourne.

I have consulted in Australia, New Zealand and most recently, the United Kingdom.

Adrian White’s Specialties:

Content management solutions, Interwoven software stack expertise (TeamSite 6.72, SitePublisher 3.01, OpenDeploy/DataDeploy, Metatagger etc), application change management and composite application provisioning/deployment, programming (expert Perl and strong Java), strong solution design skills, web development and application technology integrations. Very strong Unix/Linux skills.
