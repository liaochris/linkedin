Dominic Schmitt’s Summary

I'm an Internet and E-Commerce architect and business manager with over two decades of experience developing interactive advertising, retail, and website solutions for TENNIS.com, ABC News, ABC Sports, the NBA, Barnes & Noble, Churchill Downs, Golf Magazine, the Sporting News, and many other media properties.

Most recently, I was the General Manager of TENNIS.com, the leading sports portal focussed totally on the sport of Tennis.

Before that, I was one of the founding members of internet ad network TACODA Audience Networks, acquired by AOL Time Warner in September, 2007.

We deliver higher yield than general-purpose ad networks, provide control over the advertising displayed on a publisher's site, and present the most relevant advertising to consumers.
