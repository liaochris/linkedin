Paolo Airaghi’s Summary

Paolo Airaghi has began working as a copywriter in November 1999 at Arnold Worldwide.
In 2002 has changed to Grey Worldwide, first in Milan and then in Rome.
In January 2004 he has moved to Barcelona where he has firstly worked at the Spanish agency Atletico.
From December 2005 Paolo works as a creative director at ICS Adv Milan for clients such as Momodesing, Momo, Bindi, Iame, Rolling Stone, Drumohr among others.
During his career, he has worked for many brands belonging to the most varied fields, such as: Honda, Maserati, Seat, Allianz, La Gazzetta dello Sport, Eni-Agip, Twix, Daewoo, Lila Cedius, Boehringer-Ingelheim, Wind, Unilever and Procter & Gamble, achieving recognition at the most significant international advertising festival (Eurobest, Epica, New York Festivals, LIAA and Leaf) as well as 3 bronzes and 6 short lists in the ADCI Awards.

Paolo Airaghi’s Specialties:

Copywriting and creative direction. Communication strategy. Art direction.
