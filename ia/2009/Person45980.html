Chuck Swensson’s Summary

I am the President of Creatis - an emerging creative services company based out of Minneapolis, Minnesota. Creatis is changing the way Fortune 1000 companies manage creative and marketing services.

We provide flexibility to companies that drives creative execution, marketing services efficiencies and cost containment. With our unique combination of studio and staffing services, we deliver critical outcomes that impact your business.

Our services are delivered as a seamless extension to businesses. We provide adaptable creative and marketing solutions built around our client’s culture and challenges. We enable our clients to respond to market changes instead of reacting with short-term victories.

Clients that we are moving forward in this economy include Best Buy, Medtronic, Target, FAF Advisors, Thrivent Financial, Wells Fargo, UnitedHealth Group, SUPERVALU, Ameriprise Financial and many more.

If you would like to hear some great success stories, or you are interested in joining our company as a Creatis employee, please contact me.
