Julian Wong 黄立安’s Summary

I am seeking to create collaborative, integrated and adaptive platforms to launch regenerative, clean energy systems solutions. Such platforms will necessarily consider the full panoply of social, political, technological, financial and business and ecological characteristics of the local environment. How can we change the world for the better, in a practical yet scaled-up manner? I invite you to contact me.

Most recently, I was a Fulbright Fellow in Beijing researching China's renewable energy policies and clean tech industry development. I combine an understanding of the global energy market with professional experience in finance, corporate law and policy research in both Asia and the U.S. I post my reflections on my website: http://greenleapforward.com
