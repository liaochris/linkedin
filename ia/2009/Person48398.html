Jim Warren’s Summary

Economic Environment: Many leaders of small to medium size organizations face a complex, chaotic, and often brutalizing economic environment. Banks are reluctant to lend working capital, and investment capital is almost non-existent. Customers may buy less or at lower prices, competition may shift with the stronger competitors acquiring the weaker ones and becoming even stronger, former collaborators may not be available, key suppliers may go out of business. In this environment, understanding the "known-unknowns" and the "unknown-unknowns" is critical to survival and success.

Free economic environment assessment checklist is available to prospective clients: contact us.

Role: As Director of the Center for Collaborative Management (CCM), Jim Warren plays a key role in getting breadth of scope during diagnosis, and by involving all stakeholders when implementing change.

Experience: Jim has over 45 years experience as a management consultant and executive coach, integrating people and task issues in a wide variety of private, public and voluntary sector clients. He has helped with issues of leadership, teamwork, collaboration, conflict resolution, work processes, change management, strategic decisions and technology implementation. He is skilled at integrating the inputs of specialists both inside and outside the client organization.

Results: CCM validates its methods with a set of exemplary, results-based cases, many demonstrating performance improvement in excess of 20%. Our approach has helped clients reduce costs and staff turnover, and increase sales, productivity, profits, front-line performance and valuation of the technology portfolio. Bottom line impacts have been in the multi-million dollar range. .
