Nic Perez’s Summary

I am very passionate about technology and its possibilities, I was a consultant for 14 years with many industry leading internet enablers, such as Netscape Communications, Charles Schwab and America Online. Before co-founding ChumBonus, I was a Technical Director in charge of the CrowdSourcing development program within AOL. The crowdsourced development program implemented many projects across the entire AOL organization, one's that are used by millions of users each day.

For the last few years, I have been very fortunate to be associated with crowd sourcing, specifically, crowd sourced software development and design with TopCoder. I am a preferred value added reseller for TopCoder through my CrowdAdopter, LLC and www.crowdadopter.com

I have always had technical influence with causes and the greater good. I was the web team-lead for the American Red Cross's Biomedical Divisions NBCS project and assisted the founders of Vehicle Donation to any Charity (www.v-dac.com) as the technical architect of their site.

Nic Perez’s Specialties:

Senior software professional with 20 years of industry experience in software development. Proficient in all aspects of managing, architecting and building complex end to end software applications. Hands-on experience in building large internet applications, programming, databases and networking. Several years of experience in project management, mentoring, technical leadership, customer liaison, architecting solutions and business development.
