Jamen Griffith’s Summary

My name is Jamen Griffith.
In 1999, I helped develop and start up of a small website company. After displaying my sales talents, I was quickly promoted to a sales manager role. Over a four year stint, I helped grow the company from approximately 3 sales people to over 50, while training and managing teams as large as 20 people in size. Additionally, through much of my efforts, this company saw its revenue grow from 10k/month to over $200K/month. Today, this privately held San Diego based company is valued at over $20 million.
In 2003, I realized a company selling $500 websites was not large enough for me. Therefore, I joined the mortgage industry. I utilized many of my skills gained from my previous employer and became the top sales reps at Countywide. Due to my tenaciousness and willingness to be the best, I was offered a sales manager role. After accepting this role, I assisted the company in growing from 10 loan officers to over 30, in less than 6 months. Ultimately, this tripling in company size was reflected in its sales and revenues.
In 2006, noticing that the mortgage/real estate industry was going to be a long and windy road, I saw the need to diversify my talents. At that point, I launched my own credit consolidation and settlement company (Axiom Financial Solutions). In just over 1 year, we have grown the company to over 500 clients and $1.5 million in revenue. While much of my focus is centered on the development and growth of Axiom Financial Solutions, I still have an active role in the mortgage/real estate community. While my monthly sales volume may not indicate that, it’s just a matter of time before our real estate market turns back on course. And one of the major factors that I see assisting with our real estate marketplace includes “outside the box” thinking in regards to supplies, labor, and construction. Another way put, the construction process of your typical residence will see an amazing transformation in the coming years.
