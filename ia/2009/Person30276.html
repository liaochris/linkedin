Bob Ferngren www.advocare.com/0309312’s Summary

As President and CEO of Crown Business Services, Bob Ferngren has brought with him all of the knowledge and experience gained from his distinguished career with such industry leaders as Bennigan’s, Fresh Choice, and Longhorn Steakhouses. During his 12 years with Bennigan’s, Bob rose to President of this $450,000,000 in revenue concept, by consistently delivering sales and profit objectives while revitalizing the menu and marketing plans. As CEO of Fresh Choice Bob oversaw 56 units and $78 million in revenue. While concept head EVP for Longhorn Steakhouses, Bob was responsible for $180 million in revenue for 95 units. Bob’s rise to top management comes from his solutions-oriented style and by leading and developing his team through positive motivation, training, and support. Experienced at coordinating multiple assignments in highly visible environments with demanding time and budgetary requirements, Bob is able to obtain the necessary results for restaurants of all sizes and styles. Bob is a Certified Health Coach and Nutritional Advisor. Bob also serves as President of Equipping Pastors International, a non-profit organization. Bob and his wife of 32 years Joyce reside in Orlando, Florida and have 3 grown children.
Part time nutritional advisor helping people with their health and wealth goals.
