Tecoy Porter’s Summary

Dr. Tecoy Porter Sr. is a leader, speaker and an award winning author, noted for his innovative leadership skills, spiritual insight and financial acumen. He shares the role as Senior Pastor of the Genesis Church in Sacramento, California with his brother Dr. Ellington Porter. He organized the building project of the Dr. Robert Porter Family Life Center – a multi-million dollar, multi purpose facility in honor of his late father. The center houses worship services, athletic leagues, and various outreach services for the community.

In his newest book, Releasing Your Inner Treasure: 8 Kingdom Keys to Unlocking the Wealth Within You (Foghorn Publishers Mar.’08) Dr.Porter speaks out truthfully and tactfully about how the life that you've always dreamed of living, and all the things that you wanted to accomplish are locked up inside of you!

Don't settle for 2nd best! Live Full, Die Empty! Release your Inner Treasure!

Visit www.DrTecoyPorter.com to learn more about Dr. Tecoy Porter!
