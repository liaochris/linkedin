Jennifer Dizmang: Real Estate IRA Specialist’s Summary

Jenn Dizmang is a national speaker and trainer on The Real Estate IRA. In the past 6 years she has trained thousands of investors, realtors, attorneys and CPAs on self-directed IRAs and the IRS rules that surround them.

Her humor and understandable method of delivery on this technical topic make her an in-demand speaker and sought after consultant. Jenn draws on her background as a real estate investor and years as a financial advisor to intertwine relevant stories and example to capture and maintain the student’s attention and create a connection with her audience that is unparalleled.

Jennifer Dizmang: Real Estate IRA Specialist’s Specialties:

Jenn’s niche with the self-directed IRA is training Realtor to use this knowledge and information as a source to build their own wealth, both through additional transactions and self directing their own retirement plans.

Jenn “walks the walk” with forthrightness in her effort to protect investors. She will openly discuss the companies that she has seen that provide ease to work with and the companies that she has found to be questionable.
