Arnel Tanyag’s Summary

What chance? The chance for your business to provide the lifestyle you want versus what lifestyle you accept.

How? Drescher & Tanyag does three things:

1) Closes the gap between your business and your personal life

2) Develop a high performance team of your peers

3) Assist in the implementation


Drescher & Tanyag's core purpose is:

To give God the entire the honor, praise, and glory by helping people live fulfilling lives based on their Life Time Goals and their Life Plan (Jeremiah 29:11 reflects our heart for everyone that croses our path).


Drescher & Tanyag's core values are:

We value discovering the unique talents and strengths that every individual and company has within them, and developing a systems around the best of who they are.

We value the private knowledge that every individual possesses, and the collective wisdom that a group of the right people brings to a individual and company.

We value tangible results.

We value profitability (that is in alignment with one’s Life Plan).

We value transparency (we do the same things that we ask our clients to do).

We value helping people and companies become champions of their numbers.
