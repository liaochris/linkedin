Patrice Funderburg’s Summary

Sharp and talented Diversity & Inclusion practitioner with demonstrated success in driving change through influence, leading high performance work teams, and making strategy real.
Over 10 years of “Retain before Hire” recruitment experience including targeted diversity recruiting, developing and implementing creative sourcing strategies, applicant tracking systems, vendor management, and various interview & selection methods.
I also have solid business knowledge in the area of Supplier Diversity.
I have exceptional leadership capabilities including identification of critical integration points between organizational business strategies and bottom line results.
I possess a demonstrated ability to lead, engage, and deliver results in a highly matrixed work environment.

My education includes a Master of Science degree in Organizational Communication & Development from Canisius College in Buffalo, NY. I also earned a BA in Human Resource Management from the State University of New York at Buffalo.

Patrice Funderburg’s Specialties:

diversity program development and metrics, workforce analysis, sourcing, applicant tracking systems and RPO, various interview & selection methods, supplier diversity program development, social networking, social media and recruiting, emotional intelligence
