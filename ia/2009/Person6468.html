Alastair Crawford’s Summary

CEO and founder of i-CD Publishing (UK) Limited which owns and operates 192.com, the UK's largest directory site. 192.com has over 5 million signed up members on its consumer site and is used by 1.7 million unique users a month.

192 Business Services is a division within 192 that provides information services to over 700 large companies including the BBC and many UK insurance agencies. It is a leader in Global ID verification systems.

We have 70 staff based in Fulham, London.

Chairman and founder of Passado Limited, one of Europes largest social networking sites with over 5.5m registered users in Spain, Germany and France providing users with a powerful club platform for communicating with people around commonalities like school and hobbies.

We have 20 staff in Fulham and recently raised $13.4m investment from Tudor and DFJ Eplanet.
