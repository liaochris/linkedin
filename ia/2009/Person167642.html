Jacob Bettany’s Summary

Prior to launching the popular niche finance website, MoneyScience Financial Intelligence Network in October 2004, Jacob spent 4 years developing the scholarly journal Quantitative Finance for its former publisher, the Institute of Physics Publishing.

As of October 2008 MoneyScience is attracting 30,000+ unique visitors a month and the MoneyScience Financial Services Directory is fast becoming a significant presence on the online directory landscape.

In November 2008, Jacob was picked to be a member of the Courvoisier Future 500:

http://www.guardian.co.uk/courvoisier500/4

"Last year, Courvoisier, in partnership with The Observer, led a search for the country's most innovative business brains and creative talent. The results of the search led to the creation of The Future 500 - a vibrant online network of 500 of the country's most dynamic individuals. "
