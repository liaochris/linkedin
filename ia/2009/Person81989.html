Fred Skoler’s Summary

Innovative games business executive and hands-on producer, designer, manager with deep experience in development including PC, console, online, mobile, commercial and serious games.

A Creative Director with over 18 years of professional experience in interactive design, concept creation, story development, character creation, video direction, branding, and a knack for guiding production teams to reach their potential.

History of directing the development of award winning games in markets as diverse as family entertainment and military training. Proven capability in expanding business opportunities by leveraging cutting-edge technologies.
