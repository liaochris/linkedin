Andy Theimer’s Summary

I enjoy working with start-ups, early stage companies, and helping to grow Milwaukee's community of young entrepreneurs. Currently, I manage the day to day marketing and sales operations of Lansare's annuity and insurance research tools as well as consulting in the area's of online sales and marketing, HR technologies, and social media applications. I have my own web company called Recipebridge.com which is a clean and simple search of all recipes on the Internet.

Prior to joining Lansare I was the Director of Operations at Broadlook Technologies; a recruiting & staffing software company in Pewaukee, Wisconsin where I managed the online training, customer support, direct sales, and marketing departments of the business.
