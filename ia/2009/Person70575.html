Emily Cunningham Rushing’s Summary

I have a background in social sciences, law, and information sciences, and experience in research, analysis, writing, and instruction. I have worked primarily as a research specialist and strategic intelligence expert.

I have been a librarian and researcher since 2004. I am presently the Competitive Intelligence Specialist for Haynes & Boone LLP in Dallas, TX. Previously, I was the market researcher and database administrator for a management consulting firm. From 2005-2007, I worked as a Research Specialist with King & Spalding LLP in Houston, TX. I graduated from the University of North Texas’s MLIS program in December of 2007 after receiving a JD from the University of Houston Law Center in May of 2004. I also have a Bachelor’s of Arts in Sociology from the University of Houston.

In addition to research, social media, CI, and databases, my hobbies include reading, cooking, and reading about cooking.
