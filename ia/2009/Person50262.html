Craig Brown’s Summary

I have worked as a project manager and business analyst mainly in the Australian ITC, Banking industries. I have also worked in the law, education and welfare industries, including starting a law firm with some lawyerly members of my family.

I have worked on a variety of operations, sales and marketing projects that range from large ones that drag on for years and cost millions but leave everybody disappointed, to short sharp focused ones where everybody walks away a winner. And the ones in between.

Persevere was my high school’s motto, which has been something that has actually stuck with me. I then went to university and did a bachelor of business, mainly management and marketing.

in 2005 I went back to formal education and began a Masters degree in project management. Part of the course required the maintenance of a learning journal. That was the genesis of a blog I strated called Better Projects.

In March 08 my family and I relocated from Melborune back to the Central Coast, with the view to me commuting to work in Sydney.
