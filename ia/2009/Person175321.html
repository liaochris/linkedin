Nigel Russell’s Summary

Nigel Russell teaches, coaches and supports individuals and organisations confidently communicate, collaborate and learn over distance. With Carla Rogers, Nigel formed www.nomadmeetings.com - a vehicle for enabling powerful, communications, collaborations and action. It just makes sense!

Nigel has an approach which helps individuals and teams recognise and use their strengths - even when these people work at a distance from normal organisational support.

Nigel has trained, facilitated and coached in multinationals, not for profit and public sector through to community-based programs in Australia and overseas and across many industries including IT, Media, Health, Telecommunications, Primary Industries, Education and Financial Services.

Nigel:
TEACHES people & organisations:
• To make virtual and geographically separated teams work effectively
• To improve communication & collaboration
• To confidently use modern collaborative tools & techniques
• To use techniques and behaviours which engage others and build commitment

COACHES people & organisations who work at a distance:
• To create approaches to tackle business issues
• To create processes to support virtual teams
• To challenge assumptions and make decisions
• To analyse situations and recommend solutions
• By asking questions which raise insights

SUPPORTS teams and organisations who work at a distance:
• By facilitating workshops and other interventions
• By coaching and supporting the use of new processes and systems
• By training and coaching during organisational change

His experience includes:
• Facilitate Webinars and online meetings
• Build organisational capacity to richly engage internally and externally at a distance
• Coach trainers and facilitators to effectively and richly engage at-a-distance.
• Design and deliver distance education workshops in Mongolia resulting in skilled distance learners, and further distance programs for the country.

Nigel Russell’s Specialties:

• Communication and collaborative techniques and technologies for rich communication
• Producing and co-hosting live web-based Webinars, teleworkshops, focus groups and meetings
• Communication & teambuilding skills for distributed and “virtual” teams
• Individual & organisational coaching to adopt collaborative communications
• Leadership development to engender motivation and action.
