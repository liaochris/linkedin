Hugh McGrory’s Summary

Award-winning film director/producer, exploring the narratives of innovation.

Innovation Consultant. Delegate on International Digital Media Trade Missions as Creative Entrepreneur.

Commissioned as a writer/director/producer/executive producer on over 30 short films - film+digital, live-action+CG, doc+drama.

Director of media commercials and campaigns. Helping brands to lead the innovation conversation.

My goal is to document innovation and to network my collaborators who seem at first glance to have little or nothing in common.

"If you told him there were strange looking goats on the top of a hill, he'd be away up the hill" - My Mother

Hugh McGrory’s Specialties:

Film-making, imaging, social networking, online video and communication, video for mobile and other handheld devices, experimentation, innovation, motivation, positive energy, an open mind, seeing new connections between traditionally separate things.
