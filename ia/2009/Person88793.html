Graeme Codrington’s Summary

A leading world expert on the new world of work and multi-generational workplaces, providing services to CEOs, strategy teams, HR and marketing leaders.

Dr Graeme Codrington shows his clients how to get more from their teams and drive more sales by understanding how to influence others in the new world of work. More details at http://www.graemecodrington.com.

In the past ten years, Graeme has established himself as one of the world's leading platform presenters and experts in this area. Graeme has developed a unique way of presenting his topics in a high-energy, fun-filled, yet thought-provoking way. His multimedia-driven, interactive style, and immediately applicable "how to's" have caused clients to invite him back again and again. He has worked with large multinational and listed companies as well as professional associations, and non-profit organisations. He also serves as an adjunct professor for four different business schools, including London Business School.

Clients that have applied his insights have seen dramatic improvements in many areas, including strategy development and execution, staff interactions, productivity, leadership effectiveness (especially between generations), employee retention, customer service and sales. It all comes down to better connections between people: the heart of business success and differentiation in the 21st century!

Together with TomorrowToday, a consulting company Graeme co-founded, he is able to provide not just keynote presentations and workshops, but also bespoke interventions and services to ensure that his message sticks and makes a real difference to his clients' bottom line.
