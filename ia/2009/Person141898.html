Lisa Borg’s Summary

Lisa Borg is a respected marketing strategist, innovator and tactical leader with a notable 12-year career spearheading enterprise-wide initiatives that impact and elevate an organization’s influence and market status and result in sustainable, profitable growth.

Fluent in several languages, she is an accomplished communicator, presenter and public speaker, with a keen ability to build consensus across multiple organizational levels by forging solid and strategic relationships with key internal and external partners.
