John Roberts’s Summary

Hello and thank you for visiting my LinkedIn page. For the last decade I have provided businesses, law firms, and select individuals with specialized legal talent to help them with intellectual property matters, including patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets, as well as unfair competition, false advertising, and anti-trust issues.

Among other things, I can help you:

*Protect your ideas and intellectual property
*Determine the economic value of your intellectual property
*Negotiate contracts and licenses
*Avoid infringing others' rights
*Resolve disputes
*Turn your ideas into profits

Feel free to call me today with your legal matter or to discuss how I might be able to assist you and exceed your expectations.

I am admitted to practice before the United States Patent and Trademark Office throughout the United States, and am admitted to practice before the state and federal courts in the states of California and Indiana. Note that I may not provide legal advice with respect to non-patent matters in other jurisdictions except where admitted pro hac vice in connection with specific federal litigation matters around the United States.
