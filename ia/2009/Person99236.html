Alessandro Inversini’s Summary

Alessandro Inversini has a Master in Communication Sciences and Communication Technologies in 2004; he works as researcher at TEC-LAB (www.tec-lab.ch), at the School of Communication Science (University of Lugano - www.unisi.ch). His research activity primarily deals with usability, quality and new technologies of communication in cultural heritage tourism. His PhD research concerns the qualitative design solutions in the technological communication in the field of cultural tourism. He also works as instructional designer at the eLearning Lab (www.elearninglab.org) where he works on three Swiss Virtual Campus projects.
