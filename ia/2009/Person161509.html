Mike Gallagher’s Summary

Talented and accomplished career Test Manager with a proven ability of leading complex enterprise wide test strategies and managing risk based test plans. Strong sense of accountability and willingness to shoulder responsibility. Established record of providing direction and leadership to all members of a programme test team.
