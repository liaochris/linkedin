Ed Primeau’s Summary

Primeau Productions was organized in 1984 and has perfected marketing and advertising video production. Our future goals are to continue to grow with on line video production because the internet has become a place where all businesses can get new business.

We also believe in face to face contact which is why one of our goals is to grow our convention and conference production.
We have produced conferences in the past for The National Association of Personnel Services, Floyd Wickman Productions, The Womens Leadership Exchange and MIchigan Association of Home Health Care to name a few.

We have perfected internet video compression and have posted our clients videos on their websites, You Tube and Google. (Tuneman1958 on You Tube; Ed@PrimeauProductions.com on Google)

Ed Primeau’s Specialties:

Multi-Media Production, Internet Video, Audio and Video marketing, Broadcast Advertising, Documentaries, audio and video forensics, expert witness.
