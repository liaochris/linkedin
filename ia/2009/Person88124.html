Jonathan Goldberg’s Summary

Recent Ivey MBA graduate seeking employment.

In corporate and business development roles, within private equity and industry, I thrived on the analytical finance aspects of the work and excelled at relationship management. I learn quickly and I am a proven team player.

Having maintained my own investment portfolio since before attending the Richard Ivey School of Business for an MBA, I have a passion for financial analysis and valuation. While at business school I focused on refining these skills through specialized courses in investing, derivatives and corporate finance as well as through independent research for the professor who holds the Ben Graham Chair in Value Investing at the University of Western Ontario. By incorporating my passion for finance and investing within your organization I will prove to be a valuable member of your team.

While employed at Northam Diamonds, I managed business development initiatives in the diamond industries of Israel, Belgium and Canada. This experience enabled me to utilize my analytical skills and my strengths in relationship management. I was handed the responsibility of adding value to the firm in any way I saw fit, subject to approval from upper management. In addition to adding value via high-level strategic initiatives, such as joint ventures, I also was able to add directly to the bottom line through direct sales and by analyzing and refining the firm’s operations and marketing strategy. This experience was invaluable for me as I learned first hand what is involved in running a business and I became familiar with all aspects of the supply chain.

I am confident that my enthusiasm, together with my skills listed above, will allow me to add value to your team. I look forward to meeting you to further discuss my background and abilities. Please call me at (416) 473-7734.
