Emily Rompelman’s Summary

To a (somewhat) recent graduate, dealing with the word "experience" can be intimidating. When applying for jobs in the marketing field after college, the desired positions either needed a masters degree or 3-5 years of experience.

Seeing as I'd like to start paying down my first school loans and eventually go back for my masters, I opted for experience. Building on my college marketing internship with the Red Cross, I received an opportunity to become the Marketing Director for a championship golf course in Long Island. It was a great position on the east coast, but family reasons brought me back to the good ole' Midwest. I am currently the Marketing Director for a home improvements company in Kaukauna. I am very excited for the potential of the company and look forward to the results of my hard work!
