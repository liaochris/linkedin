Gail Anthony Greenberg’s Summary

I'm a longtime marketer, writer, publicist, internet product specialist and a bestselling author. It's because I have so much unique experience across so many disciplines - plus the training and mentorship of uber-marketer Jay Abraham - that I can identify a company's missed opportunities for making cash from their operation. I'm known for my ability to craft quick simple solutions that get big results - see "About Us" at my website for testimonials.

In addition to the positions listed here, I've worked with member site guru Len Foley on the member sites he runs for Millionaire Mind guru T. Harv Eker, and raw foods guru David Wolfe, among others.

I have a variety of superpowers including a Forrest-Gump-like ability to be right there when things are about to explode. I got the worldwide scoop on “The Amityville Horror” as a student journalist, worked in New Orleans with a down-and-out Paul Prudhomme, and got “Spider-Man” director Sam Raimi to endorse my bar mitzvah planning book.
