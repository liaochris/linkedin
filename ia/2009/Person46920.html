Claude Johnson’s Summary

Black Fives is for basketball fans, black culturistas, vintage fiends, brothas and sistas, sneaker heads, positive thinkers, culture lovers, risk takers, growth seekers, thrivers, and history makers.

Black Fives combines pre-NBA African American basketball history with modern culture to produce meaningful engagement, teaching, enlightenment, and inspiration you can use today. Our slogan: Make history now!

Black Fives researches, preserves, teaches, and promotes the early history of African American participation in basketball.

Black Fives is dedicated to being the best, the most authoritative, the most extensive, and the only company devoted exclusively to providing education, entertainment, events, merchandise, and intellectual property relating to the history of the Black Fives Era.

Black Fives is devoted to using the lessons, stories, and experiences of the Black Fives Era to teach, train, and inspire others toward personal growth to be, do, and have all that they dream; to building inter-generational and inter-cultural bridges of understanding; and to cultivating a wide appreciation and understanding of the significance of this history in modern-day society.
