Manju Sharma’s Summary

Manju Sharma is a senior consultant in FTI’s Corporate Finance practice and is based in Washington, D.C. Manju has over 13 years of professional experience in operational & financial due diligence, enterprise valuation and risk assessment, supply chain and product management. The primary industries he worked on are consumer goods, retail, manufacturing, automotive and transportation. He has in-depth management consulting experience in enterprise value analysis, financial risk assessment, M&A synergy analysis, supply chain redesign, and inventory analysis. Mr. Sharma holds an M.B.A. in finance and operations, from University of Michigan (Ross School of Business) and an M.S. in mechanical engineering from the University of Missouri. In addition, Mr. Sharma holds a B.S. in mechanical engineering from National Institute of Technology (KREC). He has passed CFA level 3 exam.

Mr. Sharma has worked and consulted with a wide variety of companies, including Ford, Chrysler, J.D Power & Associates, Deloitte Consulting, Detroit Diesel, Callaway Golf, and Perrigo.

Manju Sharma’s Specialties:

Operations & financial due diligence. Extensive experience in linking operational KPIs and company P&L and balance sheet to drive short-term improvement projects. Expert in identifying companies performing poorly using financial statement analysis, SEC documents, universal screening techniques in FactSet - F Score, Earnings Quality, Inventory turns, Piotroski Score (financial health), Capital Structure.
Databases/Software/Tools: Thomson Reuters, FactSet, Bloomberg, AFG View
