Christina Peterson’s Summary

Seasoned clinician with extensive clinical experience in headache syndromes and mild head trauma. Health and wellness educator and medical communications specialist; content specialist and medical writer, working toward AMWA certification. Interested in the comorbidities (associated conditions) of migraine; interested in consulting with corporations on the opportunity cost of treating migraine and its associated conditions as compared to the presenteeism and longer-term health costs that can arise from headache disorders.

Christina Peterson’s Specialties:

Wellness, headache prevention and disease management, headache and migraine consulting, speaking, health education, writing, and medical communications.
