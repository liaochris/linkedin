Samir Khadepaun’s Summary

KONEKT, is Mobikon's new product on Mobile Marketing and Services. Businesses can now Market their product and services on Mobile through Konekt.
The Konekt platform is useful for Companies to launch Customer Loyalty Programs on Mobile, Mobile Coupons and discounts, Travel Portals, Real time services on Mobile like Restaurant Table Bookings, Rent-A-Car, Ticket Bookings, Yellow Pages, City information, Maps, Mobile CRM and Sales force applications. For Banks KONEKT platform can be deployed as Mobile Concierge, providing its customers with value added services, finding ATM's, new product details, account details and host of services.
KONEKT helps businesses to be always connected with their customers.
