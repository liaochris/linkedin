Jennifer Moss’s Summary

Jennifer Moss is CEO of Moss Gathering Partners, makers of BabyNames.com, Parently.com and Ask-Anything.com. BabyNames.com has been lauded as one of the top parenting sites on the internet by such media as Reuters, CNN and MSNBC. Moss has grown BabyNames.com to one of the top 2000 most visited sites on the web, with over 1.5 million unique visitors per month.

As an internet business consultant, Jennifer Moss has been instrumental in bringing large, consumer-oriented sites to market. Clients and past employers include Korn Ferry's Futurestep, Withoutabox.com, eToys.com and CelebrityLoop.com.

Moss is also a public speaker and published writer on topics relating to the internet industry with work appearing in Red Herring and Working Woman Magazines. Her first book, The One-In-A-Million Baby Name Book, is now on Amazon and in bookstores (Perigee/Penguin, 2008).

Jennifer Moss is currently the President of the Northwestern Alumni Club of Los Angeles.
