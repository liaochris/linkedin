Jacob Bishop (Jacob@DPowerMedia.com)’s Summary

For nearly a decade I've been involved in Online Marketing, specifically either Email Marketing and Deployment, or Affiliate Marketing, both as an advertiser and as an affiliate.

Having spent five years in Email Marketing, I was responsible for acquiring high performing email campaigns, monitoring metrics for eCPCs and eCPM on each campaign deployed, assuring that benchmarks were met or campaigns were replaced with higher performing campaigns. I oversaw all the campaign selections, creative selection and testing, email deployment monitoring, bounce monitoring, blockage monitoring, ISP relationships and data acquisition, which included data license partners and rev share payouts.

Currently, my duties include overseeing all aspects of our Affiliate Network, including campaign selection and testing, affiliate approval and monitoring, Advertiser relationships, invoicing and accounts payable. I examine all proposed campaigns for exclusivity, longevity, viable CPA, CPC, CPL or CPS options.
