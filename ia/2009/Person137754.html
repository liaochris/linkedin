Laura Ramos’s Summary

Follow me on Twitter: @lauraramos

Marketing executive and industry/technology analyst specializing in demand generation, lead management, product marketing, Web 2.0/social media, business strategy, and product/vendor evaluation. Specializing in business-to-business (B2B) marketing: technology use and best practices for generating demand, managing leads, closing-the-loop with sales, emerging social and Web 2.0 media use, marketing measurement, installed base marketing, and customer satisfaction/loyalty. I place a high premium on public speaking, writing, and delivering high quality technology assessments, marketing effectiveness reviews, product positioning, competitive analysis, practical advice, and making the call on industry winners and losers.

Current activity focuses on B2B marketing in high technology, software, business consulting, media/publishing, energy, financial services, and healthcare.
