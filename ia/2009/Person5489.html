AJ Croce’s Summary

In his 12+ years in the marketing industry, A.J. has created unique, compelling concepts for everything from collateral to advertising to promotions. He has had the opportunity to work on some of the biggest names in technology, home care, beer and spirits. A.J. uses his variety of experience to create arresting work to get results for his clients. A true hands-on art director who loves to work with talented people passionate about work & life.
