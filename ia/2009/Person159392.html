Melissa Gayle West’s Summary

I help people in challenging transitions move from surviving to thriving, through coaching, psychotherapy, and inspirational teaching and speaking. People who work with me are wanting to find meaning both in and beyond their transitions, and reap the gifts of the process: deeper engagment with their own passions and lives, authentic happiness, and astonishing clarity about who they are and where they want to go. I bring wisdom, 25 years of professional work with folks in transition, a sense of humor, directness, and passion with supporting people move into deeply rewarding new lives.

Melissa Gayle West’s Specialties:

Psychotherapist, coach, retreat and workshop leader, public speaker, author of four books (Silver Linings: Finding Hope, Meaning, and Renewal During Times of Transition; Exploring the Labyrinth: A Guide for Healing and Spiritual Growth; two others), specializing in working with people in major transition.
