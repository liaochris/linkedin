Rui Dias’s Summary

Rui Dias is a social and organizational psychologist and works in recruitment since 1994. He is a founding member and managing partner at Shortlist - Executive Search Consultants since 2002. Previously, was senior recruiter at Vedior Group for seven years, and trainee at the Psychology Center of the Portuguese Air Force.

Shortlist is a successful retained executive search firm that provides top / middle management and functional expert recruitment and assessment services.
