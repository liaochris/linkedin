Meredith Mobley’s Summary

As a 2005 graduate from the Scripps Howard School of Journalism and Communications at Hampton University, Meredith Mobley is completely dedicated to her field. She uses her degree in public relations on a daily basis as the marketing communications coordinator for For Rent Media Solutions™, a division of Dominion Enterprises which is headquartered in Norfolk, Va. In this position, she coordinates public relations and internal communications for the company. Since she joined the company, there has been a significant improvement in public relations efforts, which have helped solidify her company’s position as a leader in the multi-family housing industry.

Meredith is also active in the Hampton Roads chapter of Public Relations Society of America, where she has held the position of public service committee chair, offering free public relations services to a local non-profit organization. She was also recognized as the May 2007 “Spotlight on Leadership” in the chapter newsletter. She currently holds the position of awards chair, where she coordinated the chapter’s 2008 Pinnacle Awards ceremony and will soon serve a three year term as a board member. Meredith was recently appointed to the position of executive director of the Hampton Roads chapter of Friends of St. Jude, an organization that gives young professionals the opportunity to network while raising funds for the children of St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital. In addition, she is a member of the Hampton Roads Black Media Professionals where she holds the title of public relations chair and secretary
