George Hernandez, CPA’s Summary

George is a dynamic financial expert and accomplished leader with more than 20 years of experience impacting the performance of start-ups through multimillion dollar global organizations, operations, and restructurings through strategic orchestrations of diverse fiscal strategies.

He is a high energy independently driven person working with people to win-win.

He provides a “hands-on” collaborative approach to managing, motivating, and developing people, and organizations. He has played key roles in the development and execution of financial strategies, turn-arounds, restructurings, acquisitions, integrations, and dispositions.

Selected areas of expertise includes:
• Strategic Partnering in Financial Statement & Cash Forecasting and Treasury Managment
• Hands-on Developing & Managing Financial Planning & Reporting Systems
• Communicating GAAP, Tax, SOX, SEC Technical issues with management
• Designing, Implementing & Reporting SOX 404 Internal Controls
• Financial Reporting & Anaylsis including SEC filings 10-Qs, 10-Ks, S-3s, S-4s and 8-Ks as well serving internal managers
• Inventory Management, Standard Costing, Product Pricing, Equity Compensation
• Designing, Implementing & Integrating Accounting ERP and Operating Systems
• Public Financings, Private Placements, and Restructurings.
