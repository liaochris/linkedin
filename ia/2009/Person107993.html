Jeff Ganter’s Summary

Eleventh Hour (11thr) is a full-service staffing and recruiting firm specializing in freelance, freelance-to-hire, contract, direct hire placements and on-site analysis. We are focused on assisting national and international clients within the Creative, New Media and Entertainment industries, including but not limited to TV, Film, Music, Fashion, Video Games, Apparel, Hospitality, Marketing, Advertising, PR, Talent Agencies, Start-up Companies, World-Wide Sporting Events, Human Resources, Financial, Business Affairs just to name a few.

Eleventh Hour's mission to our clients is to change the shape of the staffing industry one client at a time.

Our mission to our applicants is to give them an environment of counseling, support and resources while nurturing them down their career paths.

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Eleventh Hour, Inc. is proud to announce the launch of their new division Eleventh Hour Production Services, Inc.

Eleventh Hour Production Services is a leader in the temporary production labor staffing industry with their headquarters based in Marina del Rey, California. Combined with over 10 years of experience, we are comprised of a focused network, which delivers a flexible, productive, dependable workforce to meet fluctuating demands while eliminating payroll and administrative burdens for companies.

Eleventh Hour Production Services specializes in qualified temporary workers for Industrial, Special Events, Corporate Events, Concerts, World-Wide Sporting Events and Commercial projects, just to name a few.

Our growth continues because of our dedication to fulfilling our niche for qualified workers, and our ability to perform in skilled areas not normally addressed by our competitors.

The strength of Eleventh Hour Production Services continues to come from the commitment of providing exceptional customer service in the Eleventh Hour, at any time of day.
