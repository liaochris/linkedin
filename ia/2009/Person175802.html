Nimish Parekh’s Summary

I am currently President of Cecilia Healthcare Services Pvt. Ltd. a start-up in the healthcare information and information technology space. I am also currently the Non-Executive Chairman for UnitedHealthcare's group companies in India.

I established the first Third Party Administrator for health insurance in India called Sedgwick Parekh Health Management in 1996, which I then sold to UnitedHealth Group in 2002.

I am also the Chairman of the Board of Governors of Healing Fields Foundation, a non-profit venture to provide health insurance to the poor in India.

Nimish Parekh’s Specialties:

Senior leadership and management. Organizational set-ups. Health information, data and technology. Health plan administration. Have estabished call center services, web-based e-health services, health screening, occupational health, wellbeing, employee assistance programs, provider networks, branding and identity, government liaison, mergers and acquisition
