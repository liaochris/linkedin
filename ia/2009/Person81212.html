Frank Knipschild’s Summary

Audio Visual Communication and Digital Content Creation

A short introduction;
Frank can be called all-rounder with nearly a decade of experience in the audio visual production environment. As partner at quanvision S.L., an agency for audiovisual communication, he has done it all and he is of course still doing it all ! You name it; scriptwriting, story boarding, filming, editing, grading, converting… he has been there done that.

Not claiming to have all the required specialities but he surely recognizes them, knows where to find them and knows how to fit them in. He is an organizer and absolutely passionate about editing on Final Cut Pro. If you would speak about the -look and feel- of a movie he would more likely respond to the feel. Quanvision is the producer of the series “American Iron meets Driver” and “Eat Golf TV“.

Current projects: getting into social media and the production of the web/tv series “American Iron meets Driver”

You can connect with Frank on Twitter http://www.twitter/frankknipschild or Facebook http://www.facebook.com/frankknipschild
