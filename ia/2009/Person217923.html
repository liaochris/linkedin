Simone Blakeney’s Summary

I am an Australian and UK qualified solicitor.

My practice covers a broad range of IP issues, including trade marks, patents, copyright, designs. confidential information and licensing. I advise on both litigious and transactional matters, and have been involved in a number of contentious IP matters in the UK. I also have experience in media, e-commerce and IT matters.

My experience includes: advising on various aspects of matters in respect of trade marks, passing off, patent validity, enforcement and litigation, professional negligence and confidential information, in the UK and Europe, and assisting on a range of trade mark, passing off, patent and confidential information litigations; providing advice relating to the IP and IT aspects of various corporate and commercial transactions, including advice in respect of all relevant intellectual property, IT and data protection related documents and agreements; advising corporate clients on the structuring of intra-group licensing; and providing advice relating to the preparation of IP rights assignment and licence agreements.
