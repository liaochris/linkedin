Joanne Glasspoole’s Summary

I am a Joomla developer & consultant, search engine marketer and managing partner of Indigo Creative Solutions. I have been working in web development since 1999. My expertise is developing interactive, database-driven websites in Joomla, an Open Source content management system. My portfolio includes membership, subscription, eCommerce and community websites.

I am an active member of the National Association of Professional Women, American Marketing Association, Twin Cities Web Professionals and SEO Consultants Directory.

I graduated from Metropolitan State University in 1999 with a BA from the College of Arts & Sciences. My major was social sciences.

I live an hour outside of Minneapolis/St. Paul, with my husband Jim and our three pets (a cat & two dogs).

In my free time, I enjoy blogging, Facebook, listening to The Doors, reading (I am currently reading several biographies about Jim Morrison), social networking, true crime and Vedic astrology.
