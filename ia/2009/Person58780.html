David M. Schwartz’s Summary

The ROELMANA Group is a premier, privately owned and independent provider of executive recruiting, coaching and consulting support services to many different industries and executives. We pride ourselves in our ability to find the most cohesive match between employers and top quality prospects. The ROELMANA Group has developed a number of very unique and sophisticated procedures (and strategic alliances) that we believe are critical to success in conducting and completing professional, efficient and cost-effective executive search assignments. A major key to our success is our proprietary highly organized, research, comprehensive network and advanced database systems. All this is combined with our special tools for unsurpassed relationship building. These special capabilities, among other things, provide the ROELMANA Group with exceptional up-to-date career data and personal information on a broad cross-section of executive contacts covering a wide variety of businesses and industry segments. Additionally, through our many years of experience, we have collected diverse sources of proprietary information and solutions that are continually up-dated to help the ROELMANA Group evaluate and to very accurately screen and qualify candidates.
