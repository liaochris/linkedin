Randy Kellis’s Summary

Over 20 years of experience in the consumer products industry with a focus in key account sales, sales administration, National Account sales and senior sales management. Includes success and increased sales responsibilities with Fortune 500 companies such as Bristol-Myers Squib, Unilever, Ralston Purina and Honeywell International. As Vice President of Sales, most recently headed the Beech-Nut Baby Food sales organization.

As a member of Stark & Associates sales consulting group, this extensive experience offers clients critical insight on how best to improve company growth through the optimization of their sales functions. Consults with owners, Presidents and senior executives on the evaluation, training and overall development of their sales department.
