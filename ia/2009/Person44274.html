Christian Collard’s Summary

I am a professional in the Graphic Arts industry - self proclaimed Guru of Graphics. I am a Printing and Direct Marketing Specialist for GlobeDirect, a subsidiary of the Boston Globe. I also publish a printing blog.

I have hands-on experience in nearly all aspects of Graphic Communications from press operator, pre-press, graphic design, web design, to production management.

I publish PrintedPoof.com, a blog promoting printing products and services. How and where to print anything. My site is a great resource for creative professionals, small business, and marketers to find printing and direct marketing solutions.

My goal is to help business grow by integrating print marketing, direct mail, and social media.
