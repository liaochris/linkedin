Ben Kakimoto’s Summary

The Seattle Condo Blog (TSCB) is Seattle's most comprehensive condominium and loft resource. TSCB provides reviews, news and property descriptions of Seattle's many condominium and loft communities, market data information and resources for buying or selling your loft or condo.

The TSCB team's goal is to simplify your real estate experience and provide you with unparalled service, responsive communications and expert knowledge of the Seattle real estate market while maintaining uncompromising principles.
