Iulia Boiangiu’s Summary

I have been working as Product Manager for 5 years now, in different companies, each of them with distinct work styles, procedures, expectations and performance criteria.

As Product Manager, my major tasks are related to advertising, market research and competitor analisys, building and maintaining client-related business, purchase and follow-up on sales and promoting activities. Major advantage consists in finding, keeping and growing business with new clients.

Other relevant aspects from my professional activity as PM:
- research, selection, development of the company's products
- constant analisys of the competitors' products
- create a strategy to differentiate the products within the company portofolio

As Product Manager, I mainly dealt with are the first of the "4P"'s of marketing, which are Product, Pricing, Place, and Promotion. Shortly, this role can be described in finding successful solutions to the below tasks:

- What products will be offered?
- Who will be the target customers?
- How will the products reach those customers?
- Why will customers prefer our products to those of competitors (the distinctive attributes and value to be provided)?

Other relevant facts from my Product Management skills:
- giving product demonstrations in trade shows, creating marketing collateral like data sheets, and white papers.
- deep knowledge of more business oriented activities like conducting ROI and NPV analyses technology investments
- strategizing how the decision criteria of the prospects or customers can be changed so that they buy the company's product vis-a-vis the competitor's product

I greatly enjoy international travelling which actually is one of my constant duties in the actual job. From all these trips, Spain is the only country that my mind goes to every time I have a free moment.
