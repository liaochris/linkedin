Nuppu Gävert’s Summary

I’m a strategic consultant and socio-cultural researcher specialized in bringing fresh methods, trends & future perspective and people-centered approach to design, innovation and brand building processes. Natively Finnish, currently breathing the air of Brooklyn, New York, I look forward to finding interesting new collaborations, challenges and contacts both in the U.S as well as in Finland.

I have just started Wevolve, a boutique consultancy for trends and innovation, and offer strategic consulting for creative agencies and companies. During the last five years I have worked with advertising, trends and communication agencies, such as PSFK, Mindshare, Euro RSCG and Miltton PR as well as governmental and non-profit organizations like ISEA and National Consumer Research Center. I’ve helped clients to understand relevant socio-cultural shifts and consumer trends affecting their business landscape, discovered valuable insights by unlocking relevant consumer/user behaviors and cultural meanings from qualitative research and provided strategic guidelines for designing and innovating more meaningful brands, products and services.

In research and strategic planning I promote a holistic approach that looks beyond the immediate interaction between the consumer/user and the brand/product/service, and anchors these relationships in their social context, including values and meanings, social networks, needs and expectations as well as everyday spaces and places that drive and create meanings and behaviors.People-centered approach combined to trends knowledge delivers deeper insights and usually points out significant underlying factors that facilitate successful innovation by ensuring the relevance on every level from cultural context to human behavior. Holistic approach also adds a temporal aspect to design, setting up a steady base for creating long-term strategies and innovating products and services that resonate with people’s needs also in the future.

Nuppu Gävert’s Specialties:

Strategic consulting, qualitative trend research, design and innovation facilitation
