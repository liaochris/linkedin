Chris Lees’s Summary

Hi,

My company, Brandlogic Ltd, act as brand managers for the BENTLEY fashion brand and other brands.

We have several registered BENTLEY trademarks covering all class 25 (clothing) in the UK.

We also have a number of concession outlets across the UK in department stores.

We are always interested to hear from companies interested in:

1. licensing our brands;
2. interested in manufacturing/supplying articles of clothing for our brands;
3. or in stocking/retailing our brands.
