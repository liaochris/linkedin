Elmer Alinsog’s Summary

Elmer Alinsog - Founder | Managing Director

Mr. Alinsog has an extensive technology R&D and technology transfer background from Semiconductor, Flat Panel Display to Enterprise Software Architecture & Automation Design, with positions at Hewlett-Packard Labs HQ in Palo Alto, CA, as Thin Film Process Engineer. Mr. Alinsog is also the primary architect of a Fabricated Pixelation of Organic Light Emitting Diode display U.S. patent and co-author of numerous Field Emission Display white papers while at HP Labs; Qwest Communications as Business Development Manager in New York City's Wall Street corridor. Prior to architecting the Buy-Side and Sell-Side business model of AgentFactor (an International Travel Group Holdings Company), Mr. Alinsog held the title of Vice President, Business and Partnership Development at Fareportal (including CheapoAir, Farebuzz & Travelong) and was responsible for business growth in the technology, corporate and consumer markets. Mr. Alinsog brings wealth of knowledge base in private aviation charter, scheduled commercial travel and luxury travel sector. Most recent notable milestone of Mr. Alinsog contribution to travel industry is automating the availability and book-ability of private yacht charter in real-time. To bring this type of product to the mainstream market, to making it affordable to charter a yacht by extending the availability of per cabin charter and a marina based lodging as alternative to hotel accommodation. Two of next on Mr. Alinsog’s project pipeline is (1) automating the availability to charter a private jet; with air taxi model spin through shared flight charter-by-the-seat-sell sponsorship program by tracking dead-legs aircraft availability in real-time; (2) building a Travel XML Registry, an Open API Marketplace for business-to-business to buy/sell travel products, similar to how NASDAQ trades financial instruments and services.
