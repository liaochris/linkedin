Vicky Lee’s Summary

Olivia Vicky Lee is a psychic business and life coach, and author of the book,  Vision Boards: A Guidebook for Mapping Out Your Vision.

A VISION BOARD is a transformative tool which individuals and corporate teams use to map out life changes and business expansion, through an accelerated and breakthrough process.

Vision boards provides you with a strategic map of where you are now and where you want to go. It is a road-map to your dreams.

As soon as someone starts speaking about what they want, I see a map in my mind of their chosen life path, alternative routes, people who support or hinder them and challenges that block their success. This is the map of your soul path. The key is to using this map, is learning how to change it.

I am looking to expand my clientele list from individuals and corporate team members---- to companies, retreats and conferences.

Vicky Lee’s Specialties:

Understanding the soul of a product and how to brand it.

Defining and selling to your target market.

Developing a business model which supports your overall business vision.

Aligning what you want on a material plane with your higher soul purpose.
