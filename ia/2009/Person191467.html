Quynh-Huong Nguyen Thac’s Summary

Huong has been working in Max Communications (HCMC, Vietnam) as Media Relations Manager since 2001. She provides support for all the clients by her good understanding of public and media relations from her 8 years experience in this field. She also has a strong sense with media and writing skill has won her a number of successful editorials and news from all kinds – from print, TV, radio to online.

Huong has working in Max Communications right after graduated from HCMC Banking University in 2001, major in Crediting-Finance. She establishes and maintains good relationship with press and media in general. She liaises with the Account Directors to develop and execute PR plans and support Managing Director in analyzing, evaluating and interpreting public opinion on crisis and recommend the position the company should take in managing the crisis successfully. She also builds up and maintains productive relationships with key industry media as well as major media (interm of circulation) in obtaining favorable coverage of the company and the services. She can deal with the newspapers and magazines to place the company’s articles regularly, esp. the ones with high circulation. With her team, she strengthens the company’s image and the brands awareness through PR activities.

Now, with over 7 year-experience in PR industry, Huong has become one of the most sought-after PR practitioners. Huong has successfully implement great integrated PR campaigns for Tiger Beer, Heineken, Manulife, Suzuki, Samsung, LG, Unilever, Coca-Cola, Vinamilk, Electrolux, Lotte, ect..
