Justin Timmons’s Summary

My professional experience started at the Watson Realty office off Baymeadows Road in the summer of 2004. I went from busing tables at Red Lobster to answering phone calls as the receptionist to gain some experience on the inner workings of Real Estate. I started my Real Estate career the following year in January of 2005 at the ripe age of 21. It was a difficult year adjusting to the business, but there where many opportunities and the industry was booming. My main focus was working with my centers of influence and making sure I did the best job possible so they would feel comfortable in referring business to me. The last 2 years I've been in the top 5 and top 4 in closed business in my office.

My biggest goal for 2009 is being really focus on making others around me feel special. I wouldn't be where I'm at today if it wasn't for the people that I surround myself with.

Justin Timmons’s Specialties:

I specialize in residential sales, first time home buyers, relocation and with clients who are looking to move up. I work in all areas of Jacksonville, but primarily in the Mandarin, Southside and JTB areas.
