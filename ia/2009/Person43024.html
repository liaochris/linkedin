Chris Cavallari’s Summary

Chris Cavallari is the Award Winning founder and Director of Photography of Filmosity Productions. He has over 14 years of experience in the film, video, television, and new media fields, and has a client list that includes YES Network, Verizon, BlackRock, ESPN, RNN, and AT&T. Chris is also a contributing writer to Student Filmmakers magazine, and produces and co-hosts several podcasts, including create/consume/delete, a podcast about making media easier to make. Chris’ knowledge, skill, creativity, and professionalism has been praised by clients and colleagues alike.
