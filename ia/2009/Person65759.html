Don Martelli’s Summary

As former print journalist with more than 15 years of client and agency marketing experience, I'm a seasoned public relations veteran who serves as trusted social media counselor for MS&L’s Boston office.

As an early adopter and evangelist of social media (Twitter-2007 and blogging-2001) and other digital communication tools, I am often sourced by bloggers and traditional media both regionally and nationally on related topics.

I'm currently an editor for Technorati; a blogger for BlogCritics.org, co-founder of Every Other Thursday, a popular blog for dads; and, a writer for Shamable.com, an up and coming resource on social media for business.
 
For MS&L Boston, I am the go-to source for the best use of digital tools to create trust with online communities and elevate brand awareness. I have developed or participated in successful social media campaigns on behalf of top corporate, consumer and healthcare brands.

I started my career as a reporter for the Boston Globe, covering general assignment, political and business news. I was also a member of the team that created and launched Boston.com, now one of the most visited news websites in the country.

In addition to my agency experience, I have worked six years in education, working at Suffolk University and Malden Catholic High School in key public relations and marketing roles. I also worked as a public affairs assistant and graphic designer for the U.S. Department of Defense. My other agency experience stems from managing technology and corporate accounts for two leading New England-based agencies.
 
I graduated from Northeastern University and am the lead architect and blogger for MS&L Boston’s PR Finish Line blog, where I analyze social media and its impact on brands and audience engagement. Lastly, I run a successful part-time family photography business. Connect with me online at www.donmartelli.com.

Don Martelli’s Specialties:

Photography, graphic design, writing, target marketing, media relations, crisis communications, reputation management and social media strategy and marketing
