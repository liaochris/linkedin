Marc van Vliet’s Summary

After finishing my 4-year-study Information Technology (1994- 1998) at the Haagsche Hoge School in The Hague (The Netherlands) I worked as employee a few years for several IT companies. During this period I came for the first time in touch with the Oracle Products and databases. At my second Employeer I did an 3-months-internal-course learning programming in PL/SQL, Datamodeling and how the use the 4GL Development Tools like Oracle (Web)Forms,Designer and Reports and later on Oracle Warehouse Builder

My first projects I mostly had the role as Oracle Professional developing Client/Server applications.

In Januar 2000 I founded my own company Distinctive Consulting having my Working-Area in the Netherlands and the West-side of Germany (North Rhine Westphalia). I have al lot of experience with national and international Projects mostly for companies in the Insurance Industry. Besides of my technical Know-How I build up a lot of Business Insurance Know-how and started connecting Business & Technology, A quiet unique combination.

The last years I specialized myself more and more on Business Intelligence Solutions for Insurance companies. Mostly having the role as Senior Functional/TechnicalDesigner, Medior Architect or Team/Projectleader.
