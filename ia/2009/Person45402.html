Christopher Poor’s Summary

I have teaching experience in Sociology and research experience in qualitative methods. My blue-collar life experience and background in community activism supplements my academic qualifications.

My primary career objective is a teaching and/or research position in higher education or a research institute.

I hope, through my research and teaching, to contribute to greater understanding of the ways in which democratic social change from below can be achieved.

More generally, I aim to contribute to making the world a better place for the majority of people, improving democracy, and protecting the environment for future generations.

Christopher Poor’s Specialties:

Collaborative and interdisciplinary research. Political communication. Political Sociology. Organizational learning. Critical theory. Critical realism. Philosophy of the social sciences. Civil Society, Collective Action. Structure and Agency in the Dynamics of Political and Social Change. Complexity, Emergence and Causality.
