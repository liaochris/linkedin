Vince Carter Sanctuary’s Summary

Located in scenic and rural Flagler County Florida, about 20 miles north of Daytona and just minutes from the Atlantic Ocean at Flagler Beach, our clients are able to find peace within nature, and soon within themselves.

Our privacy and natural beauty assists clients in discovering the harmony and serenity that they seek, in combination with a program that provides clients the necessary tools to live a new clean and sober life.

Perennial NBA All-Star, 1999 NBA “Rookie of the Year,” winner of the 2000 NBA Slam Dunk Contest, and current New Jersey Net, #15 Vince Carter has seen up close what addiction can do to families and communities.

This is the reason Vince contributed $1.6 million and attached his name to this beautiful new facility.

Vince and his mother, Michelle Carter-Scott, are compelled to make a difference, and we thank them for their leadership in bringing recovery to those in need.

Vince Carter Sanctuary’s Specialties:

We offer the latest in medical and clinical interventions, individualized to the needs of each person. These include but are not limited to:

* Drug & Alcohol Medical Detox
* Physician provided Addiction Education & continued monitoring
* Individual Counseling
* Family & Material Counseling
* Group Counseling & 12 Step Meetings
* Equine-Assisted (horse) Psychotherapy at a nearby ranch
* Music & Art Therapy
* Nutritional Therapy
* Experiential Therapy
* Recreational Therapy
