Maurice Threatt’s Summary

Business Development Expert has over 20 years experience of technical management consulting in government contracting and developing relationships in commercial markets. Has solicited and managed contracts and projects throughout the United States for an array of services that include: business management, conference management, focus group training, surveying, marketing research, logistics support services, telecommunications and sales for the retail industry.

Mr. Threatt has utilized strategic planning expertise in identifying and navigating federal & commercial growth opportunities that led to establishing partnering agreements with companies such as Bank of America, BellSouth, IBM, SAIC, EDS, Lockheed Martin, Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL), NASA, AT&T Global Markets, National 8(a) SDB Contractor's Asociation, and the National Minority Supplier Development Councils (NMSDC) in Georgia, Maryland and Washington, DC.
Goals will be to improve the exposure and bottom line of clients. The focus is the federal, state and international marketplace for my clientbase. Strategy will be to showcase the outstanding reputation for excellence in providing business solutions in all facets of operations.
