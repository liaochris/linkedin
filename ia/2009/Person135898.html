Kyle Aulerich’s Summary

Kyle Aulerich is Chief Operating Officer and Co-Founder of 360 Vantage where he is responsible for overall operation management. Aulerich is focused on strategic, tactical, and short term OM as well as the design, operation, and improvement of 360 Vantage solutions and services.

Aulerich also has responsibility for developing and communicating 360 Vantage’s vision and strategy through positioning, branding, advertising, and associated growth initiatives.

Customer-and partner-focused, Aulerich believes in setting strategic direction, establishing clear business objectives and ensuring that these objectives are met. The ability to be flexible and adjust priorities quickly to accommodate changing customer demands is central to Aulerich’s management style, and the success of his organization.

Aulerich holds a B.S. in Information Technology from the University of Phoenix, Arizona.

Aulerich has honorably served in Operation Iraqi Freedom as a Sergeant in the United States Marine Corps.
