Antonio Spera’s Summary

Antonio joined Nokia Consumers Electronics in 1996 in charge of Sales Africa and Middle East at the TV manufacturing plant in Turku, Finland. His role was to set up a distribution and sales channel and to establish local parterships. To create a sustainable long term business network also by influencing and strategically promote hotel chains and industry leaders.

His extensive and successful international experience worldwide but most especially in the european market and the MEA region in business development, companies start up and project management, make him a highly business driven mind.

After few years chapter spent in Paris into the french multinational Thomson Multimedia, the fourth biggest consumer group in the world, in 2001 Antonio gets back into the hospitality industry to develop the concept of Interactive Hotel TV system as a New Media. In-room entertainment used as a powerful media to enhance new business models and to develop the future of guest user friendly content. It was about the time to replace the old traditional and obsolete business model which had been ruling the market with non-sustainable assumptions.

Antonio is always looking for new ways to imagine a more exploitable business model guest and consumers centric. Digital communications will improve quality of life, Internet will continue to have dramatic impact on the world of business, new technologies will transform other fields of human behaviour into more service oriented platforms.
