Vik Malik’s Summary

Vik Malik is a results-oriented Healthcare Executive & Consultant with over 15 years of Healthcare sector experience with the Development, Commercialization & Licensing of complex scientific-concepts/cutting-edge technologies for companies specializing in the: Device, Biopharma, Diagnostic & Healthcare IT industries to penetrate new & emerging Healthcare markets.

In addition, Mr. Malik has extensive consulting & operations experience with building customized Product Development, Strategic-Alliance, Sales & Marketing platform solutions as well as developing supporting infrastructures for growing market-share/sales revenues, increasing IP valuations and maximizing ROI/earnings with startup, growth & distressed U.S. & O.U.S. Healthcare organizations.

Mr. Malik has also secured an elite national & international “World-Class” medical professional & association network base (MALiX Clinical & Executive Advisory Boards); and has a vast new Product-Launch & IP Licensing track record of over 30+ Healthcare product & service deals in the U.S., E.U. & Asian markets.

Furthermore, Mr. Malik has been appointed interim posts as 'SVP of Marketing & Business Development' with I.V.Diagnostics and as ‘U.S. Director of Sales & Business Development’ with Bio-Scaffold Dental International. He also serves as an ‘Executive Business Advisor’ with: Notopos Medical, iStreet Capital & EndoNex BioMedical as well as on numerous other advisory boards & councils for several early-stage companies & non-for-profit organizations.

In addition, Mr. Malik serves as a regular-guest with an In Time T.V. segment to discuss ongoing emerging medical-technologies & healthcare startup strategies with the “On the Edge” weekly medical talk-show. He has also served as a 'Continuing Education Speaker' for the Licensing Executives Society & Intellectual Property Law Association of Chicago; and served as a 'Track Co-Chair' for the Biotechnology Industry Organization "BIO 2006" lnternational Conference.
