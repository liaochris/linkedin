John Weiss’s Summary

I have 10 years experience in the creative field with the majority of my time spent in interactive and experience design.

While building and leading creative teams, I have been responsible for conceiving, prototyping and implementing innovative, visually compelling interface, application and experience designs with great attention to detail.

My work is guided by purpose, passion, simplicity, beauty, innovation and surprise.

I am deeply passionate about creating authentic experiences that are truly meaningful. Experiences that are rooted in elegant design and built with innovative technology. Experiences that create lasting relationships that shape the future.
