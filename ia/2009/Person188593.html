Pete Leibman’s Summary

TMA List Brokerage and Management helps organizations research and acquire lists/data to more effectively reach their target audiences and to find NEW customers.

Direct Marketing’s power lies in its ability to provide measurable, one-to-one communication with prospects and current customers in a rapidly evolving media environment that is becoming more and more convoluted.

Along those lines, I am writing a Direct Marketing Series, entitled "Maximize ROI Today AND Tomorrow" (see above or below for several of the white papers) to discuss major challenges and to propose solutions to a number of key issues currently facing marketers. Topics Include:

1. How to Squeeze Every Dollar out of Your Database
2. How to Use the Best Data for Direct-Mail and E-mail Campaigns
3. How to Build a Bigger and Better Email Subscriber File
4. How to Increase Deliverability, Open, Click, and Conversion Rates for E-mail Campaigns
5. How to Use a List Broker

Sources of content for these papers include TMA’s 20+ years of industry experience, in addition to extensive secondary research that we have conducted through a wide variety of third-party references.

I am always interested in building my network and forming mutually beneficial partnerships, especially with others in Direct Marketing roles.
