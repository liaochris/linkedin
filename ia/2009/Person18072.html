antoinette hoes’s Summary

Entrepreneur with a strong strategic marketing & digital media background.
Interactive strategist with a passion for eBusiness development, interactive campaigns & media strategy.
Founder and managing director of Leylines, a 5 people strong interactive strategy and media hub that works for advertising agencies & a broad range of clients.

Before founding Leylines a 5 year track record in business consulting in change processes related to strategy, communication and IT & managing a business unit with consultants and interim managers.

I spend some of my spare time at MBA level teaching, mixing theory with the development of essential entrepreneurial skills. Subjects range from reputation management to new media management and the use of Kaplan & Norton's strategy maps.

Spent 8 years in a corporate environment learning the ropes of management, communication and sales.
