Dan Routh’s Summary

Commercial and editorial still photographer shooting advertising images for local and national Fortune 500 companies.

Visit my blog at http://danrouthphotography.blogspot.com/

Dan Routh’s Specialties:

Photography of people, fashion, lifestyles, outdoor recreation, sports, travel, agriculture, heavy industry and construction, autos and auto racing. Studio and location.

Photoshop, retouching, and digital image manipulation. Digital imaging consulting and Photoshop training.

Headshots and executive portraits.

Location scouting, casting, props and production coordination for still photography. Specialization in men's and women's casual, sportswear and outdoor apparel catalog production.
