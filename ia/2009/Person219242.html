Stuart Preston’s Summary

MyBusiness Advisors, Inc. helps small business owners develop and grow their businesses. I specialize in working with project-based service business, such as web designers, graphic designers, and contractors. MyBusiness Advisors guides small business to success!

About Me:

I am a small business owner. Since I left the military as a First Lieutenant, I have done nothing but own my own companies. I have the bruises, the scar tissue, and the accolades to prove it. I have likely been where you are today and where you want to be tomorrow. I’ve made mistakes (some big ones, too!) and I’ve had my successes. And along the way, I’ve learned what it really takes to run a successful small business.

Here are a few of my credentials:

* Received the 2000 Mark of Excellence Award in his first business
* Helping over 250 businesses to improve their bottom line
* Increasing sales by 80 percent as an independent sales rep in the slowest economic years of the decade
* Owning and operating a website design and marketing firm that includes the implementation of the first shopping cart for Southwest Specialty Foods
* Author of “Small Business Fitness”
* Bachelors Degree in Computer Systems Engineering, Arizona State University
* MBA and Graduate Certificate in Entrepreneurship from Keller Graduate School of Management (with Distinction)
* Member, Gilbert Historical Society Board
* Member, Gilbert Small Business Alliance
* Former Chair, Go-Getters Leads Club of the Chandler Chamber of Commerce
* Graduate, Gilbert Leadership Class XIV
* Member, Chairman’s Circle, Chandler Chamber of Commerce
* Former District Captain for the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation’s Government Relations Comittee
