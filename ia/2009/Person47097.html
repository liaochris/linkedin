Claudio Marconato’s Summary

Trainer and Consultant that let companies to hire my experience in professional layouting techniques specially with InDesign, set up color consistent workflow using ICC profiles and automatic publishing using complex XML databases as data-sources.
Another field of expertise is the use of Adobe Acrobat and Adobe PDF as a standard in communication for my clients.
I'm involved in some important italian training events like "Arrivano i Guru" and "Ischia Foto Workshop".
I'm also moderator of a forum list that discuss about color management in Italy named "colore_digitale", owned by Mauro Boscarol.
In the last few years I worked in some elearning projects with Adobe Captivate and Adobe eLearning Suite.
