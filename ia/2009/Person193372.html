Ramon Kool’s Summary

I'm a creative entrepreneur and firm believer that if you put your mind to it, you can accomplish anything. With that reason I want to do as much as possible. I build professional websites, I give advice on using the internet, I was a comedian for 8 years, I am a live musician without using instruments, I’ve written a childrensbook, I’m a freelance photographer, I have blogs in two different languages, I write a weekly column, I try to find out just how far the internet can be stretched, I want to do it all.
