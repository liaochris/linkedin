Tim Savigar’s Summary

I have come from an application development background, moving in to infrastructure work. I have worked in both technical and business focused roles for a variety of business functions including, marketing, HR, corporate events, finance, engineering and sales.

I have experience of outsourcing – both from a provider and customer perspective as well as managing the process of TUPE and the detailed process of service cut over etc.

I have a variety of roles including, project manager, e-business consultant, team leader, applications architect, application developer and systems administrator. I have worked with a variety of technologies including SAP, NAS, SAN, XP, AD, NT4, Windows 2000, DB2, SQL Server, Oracle, MS access, Citrix, HTML, XML, Java, Lotus Notes, MS Exchange, Norton AV and IBM Websphere and for a variety of platforms including WINTEL, AS400, HP-UX and Sun Solaris.

He has managed large UNIX and WINTEL infrastructure projects, application migration, development projects, custom off-the-shelf solution implementations (COTS), run business process re-engineering projects and enjoys the challenge of translating business requirements in to IT solutions.
