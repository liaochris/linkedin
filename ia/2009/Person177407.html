Jan-Nicolas Vanderveken’s Summary

In 1997, on the force of his valuable experience at some of the large information technology consulting firms such as DMR and LGS, Jan-Nicolas Vanderveken began his independent practice. He has leveraged a deep expertise in collaborative, workgroup and Internet technologies, to provide innovative business systems solutions for his customers. Mr. Vanderveken has more than 15 years of experience within the field of information technology, and works as technical lead and designer for numerous large-scale projects. In 2005, Mr. Vanderveken co-founded TP1 Internet 360.
