Louise Scott’s Summary

Events Professional with 10 years experience in corporate events project management, along with strong decision-making and team leader skills. Excels in setting up the event infrastructure from project brief through to summarising an event's ROI overview.

Having successfully project managed a wealth of corporate events throughout Europe & Middle East regions, great pride is always taken in the “hands-on” approach given to work and equally towards steering and coaching a designated project team.

Confident in interfacing with key senior management decision makers, marketing & sales departments, destination management companies, hotels, and conference venues. Equally at ease to effectively manage agency partners and suppliers. Communicates very well at all levels and possesses exceptional interpersonal skills. Outstanding work ethic, detail-oriented, and organised.
