Adrienne Fritze’s Summary

I am a mom, artist and social entrepreneur driven by creative urges. My mediums include collage, assemblage, doodles, steel, mixed media and business building. I sleep little, play full out, love my life and the people in it.

WHAT'S NEW

Strengthening the Bonds of Love

Art is life. Art is change. Art can heal and empower. The arts are critical in the development of healthy human beings. These are the things I believe, and the beliefs I act upon in my business - and in the lives of my clients.

Imagine having the last word in creating healthy, loving, authentic bonds with the people who matter in our lives. In 2003 I created a workshop to facilitate just that. I called it, Empty & Meaningless: the Box Project.

To date, over 500 people have taken part in Empty & Meaningless: the Box Project [E&M]- an arts-based workshop about relationships and our point-of-view that creates the experience we have of our relationships.

Video about E&M: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wREaIFFFMP4

If you like the story you see there, please visit my websites to learn more about my self, Empty & Meaningless: the Box Project and Working Artists LLC.

* Working Artists LLC: http://workingartistsonline.com
* Empty & Meaningless: http://adriennefritze.com/empty
