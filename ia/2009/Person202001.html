    • Using Direct Mail
    • Through Social Media Marketing
    • Through Email Marketing

    3. We Train the Network Partners How to Expand their Businesses
    • Using their existing customer base
    • How to increase their bottom line profits
    • We Provide a vast support system of other Network Partners
