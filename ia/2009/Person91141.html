David Gutelius’s Summary

I'm interested in applying my experience in machine learning, social network analysis and behavioral studies to solve important world problems. I like starting things.

  • Co-Founder, CTO

    Social Kinetics

    (Internet industry)

    January 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Social Kinetics' mission is to provide positive support systems in people's lives to help them achieve better health, their life aspirations and greater overall happiness.

    Serving employers and providers alike, Social Kinetics creates online health engagement environments that deliver proven wellness and disease management programs with a system that optimizes all consumer touch points. By carefully managing interactions across channels, Social Kinetics can more effectively help people move through step-wise behavior changes towards health and wellness.

    Our products represent a radically new approach to healthier behavior that uses social media and integrated contact strategies to guides individuals to better wellness and health decisions. Using proprietary machine learning technologies, Social Kinetics learns how people make health decisions and creates coordinated delivery of staff, services and resources in a way that is optimized for individuals and groups.

    The Social Kinetics technology was developed as part of DARPA's PAL program, history's largest AI/Machine Learning project. The foundational research was conducted by members of the Social Computing Group at SRI International's Artificial Intelligence Center. The early versions of our smart push and machine learning technologies proved to dramatically change information sharing behavior in command-critical collaboration networks in the military.

  • Board Member

    Teachers Without Borders

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    2002 — Present (7 years)

    Teachers Without Borders (TWB) envisions a world where teachers are viewed as the natural and obvious key to human welfare . At over 59 million, teachers are the largest single group of trained professionals in the world. They know who is sick, who is missing, who is orphaned by AIDS. They often administer the polio drops, protect their students from danger, identify who is at risk for human trafficking or a military gang. Teachers Without Borders is an organization of teachers, worldwide. We are non-profit, with a small staff, and a large community of talented teachers.

    Teachers Without Borders supports teacher leaders, worldwide, with professional development opportunities and tools that connect them with information and each other so that they may play a more vital role in their communities. We focus on teachers with initiative, who are passionate about their subjects and compassionate towards children.

    Teachers are acupuncture points. A great teacher can pinpoint the exact place to stimulate the health of a village, a region, an entire continent. They know who is sick, who is missing, who has been abducted into the sex trade or conscripted into a military gang, who has been orphaned by AIDS, who is achieving and who is not.
