Nancy Nally’s Summary

Owner and primary content provider for family-oriented online media company Balalaberry Media LLC. Online properties of Balalaberry Media include ScrapbookUpdate.com and TrueTechLife.com.

Also do freelance writing for print publications, primarily in the area of genealogy, scrapbooking and consumer technology.

Nancy Nally’s Specialties:
