Robert Montgomery’s Summary

An experienced technologist, skilled in requirements analysis, architecture, specification, and deployment support for enterprise, technical, and high performance computing environments, including global Web-services and petascale HPC environments.

Accustomed to working closely with customers to define and implement large-scale transformational IT capabilities has seasoned Mr. Montgomerys abilities to contribute in teams dedicated to developing and deploying mission-critical IT installations. Mr. Montgomery's experience in the IT lifecycle encompasses technology evangelism through requirements analysis, systems, network, storage, administration, software middleware and management architecture, detailed configuration, and out-years support and technical refresh for a wide variety of enterprise systems, storage and technical computing platforms, including distributed, high-availability, and clustered networks for purposed N-tier, DBMS, Portal and Web (SOA /EAI) architectures.

Mr Montgomery has contributed to IT initiatives for US Army and Air Force enterprise logistics support and intelligence, Navy SPAWAR, USDA, The National Archives, FBI, NGA; Northern Telecom, Federal Express, Intergraph, NASA Marshall and Stennis Space Centers, Naval Oceanographics, Vanderbilt, Auburn, and Mississippi State Universities, TN & AL State & local governments.

Mr Montgomery has contributed technical content to and acted as primary author/architect for HPC tech refreshes for USAF/ Arnold AFB, DIA Missile and Space Intelligence Center, USA Advanced Research Center (Colsa/ARC), the Alabama Supercomputer Center, USA SMDC Simulation Center; Petascale storage archive for National Archives and Records (NARA).

Mr. Montgomery is an able individual contributor adept at leveraging and coordinating efforts of engineering, product marketing, technology suppliers, channel partners and other resources in achieving IT objectives.
