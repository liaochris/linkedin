Melih Ozcanli’s Summary

Texfolio & Gem Design provides professional sourcing service for the fashion industry. Our fast-paced buying service keep the retailers/stores/brands abroad ahead of their rivals, unique and trendy.

Since 1991, we have targeted to serve as the buying office for major retailers around the world, including chain stores, department stores and mail-order catalogues as well as for wholesalers, importers and distributors.

We source a wide variety of “Made in Turkey” merchandise including jeans, t-shirts, sweatshirts, polos, formal and casual shirts, blouses, casual pants and shorts, trousers, jackets, suits, socks, leatherwear, underwear, lingerie, nightwear, belts and shoes, etc.

Melih Ozcanli’s Specialties:

Acting as your buying office in Turkey, we can offer you all the advantages at a fraction of what it would cost you to establish your own office in Istanbul. We will give you all the necessary counselling and assistance to best meet your demands and objectives. Our services include:
1) Sourcing
2) Product Development and Private Labels
3) Organization of Buying Trips
4) Order follow-up, Quality checks and Shipping
5) Field Studies
