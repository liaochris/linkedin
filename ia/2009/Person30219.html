Bob Cesca’s Summary

As a pioneer in Flash animation beginning with the very first version of Flash (called "FutureSplash") I've produced over 600 animated shorts between 1998 and today. My credits on these shorts range from "everything" to executive producer to voice actor to animator to character designer, and all points in between. Throughout the last 10-plus-years, the shorts have been produced for the Camp Chaos website (now a corporate portfolio for the studio) as well as clients including Warner Bros., Sony, Paramount, Heavy.com, Capitol Records, IMG Media, Fremantle Media and so on. Nearly half of the shorts were produced for my animated sketch comedy series for VH1 and MTV2, titled ILL-ustrated. The show ran for two successful seasons, gaining the highest M18-34 ratings for the network. Presently, our animated series 'Kung Fu Jimmy Chow', created and produced for Heavy.com, has surpassed 5 million views, as has 'The Superficial Friends'. These shows join our 'Napster Bad' cartoons and our 'Spongebong Hemp Pants' cartoons on our roster of viral video success stories.
