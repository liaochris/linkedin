Mary-Claire Burick’s Summary

Mary-Claire Burick is a top performing media executive with over 19 years of experience in the media and broadcast industry. As a result of her mix of network, service provider and corporate experience, she has access to, and regularly works with the top businesses, vendors and professionals in the industry today. She now brings these key strategic alliances to bear in her consulting practice, MC Strategy, Inc.

Mary Claire has a solid history of producing and managing large scale events and high profile projects on time and within budget. She is a proven team leader who specializes in broadcast television operations and production, project management and media integration into corporate environments. Her top 8 market experience includes network, facility and studio build out and startup, operations consolidation for maximum efficiency, change management and process improvement. She is technically proficient in transmission, assignment desk and guest booking operations, and has produced and managed all aspects of many large-scale field productions.

In addition to her technical skills set, Mary Claire has a keen ability to define organizational needs and identify strategic initiatives and solutions for problems that interfere with mission critical tasks and goals. She enjoys helping executives and managers create and implement systems that are designed to produce significant improvements in operational effectiveness. She understands how to develop flexible environments that create workplace efficiencies, encourage open communication and improve process and workflow. Mary Claire is an energetic and dynamic public and small group speaker who enjoys connecting with a diverse audience. This skill enhances her project management work, allowing her to initiate and integrate change smoothly and effectively, while minimizing the impact to the organization and its employees
