Joshua Hammond’s Summary

Josh Hammond is a senior consultant in the Digital Strategic Services Division of Nielsen Online. Prior to joining Nielsen, Josh was a supervisor at Northlich, a Cincinnati-based communications agency focusing on corporate, crisis, political, and new media communications.

He has also held a variety of political communications and government affairs positions in the Midwest and Washington DC. Josh has worked for U.S. Senator Dan Coats (IN), U.S. Congressman Steve Buyer (IN-4), Infotech Strategies and Varian Medical Systems.

During his tenure with Congressman Buyer, Josh served as a legislative assistant in the Congressman's Washington, DC office and later as campaign manager for Buyer’s 2000 congressional campaign. He also lobbied for the high-tech industry working for the Computer Systems Policy Project (CSPP) at Infotech Strategies and at Varian Medical Systems. He started his career on Capitol Hill in the office of Senator Dan Coats.

Josh is a graduate of Hanover College. He is an accredited (APR) member of the Public Relations Society of America.

Contact him at joshua.hammond at gmail.com
