Stephen Fernandez’s Summary

Following his graduation from University Stephen spent 10 years working within the telecommunications and internet fileds much of this time was spent in a Pan-European capacity. More recently following a move to Spain, Stephen has been working for a Marketing Services and Digital Signage company based in Madrid.
