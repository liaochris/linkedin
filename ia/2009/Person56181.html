David Hamlin’s Summary

Senior level Marketing Communications Professional with a specialty in strategic media planning & stewardship across multiple product & service categories. Creative planner with a proven record to exceed client expectations, while providing exceptional team leadership and collaborating effectively with partners.

• Strategic Planning & Problem Solving – Effectively applies integrated communications planning approach to all assignments. Implements systematic approach to analyze, organize and clarify processes. Especially resourceful in finding workable solutions. Approaches assignments with great thought and integrity.

• Team Leadership – Decisive team leader with innate ability to integrate and balance skill sets of different individuals while incorporating team strengths throughout the organization seamlessly. Uses participative approach in setting goals to motivate team and to provide guidance. Open to feedback and suggestions, incorporates the perspective and expertise of others.

• Category Knowledge - Experienced with wide range of marketing situations from new product launches to securing market share in highly competitive segments. Versatile acumen in major business categories: retail, consumer packaged goods and durable goods.

• Industry Knowledge - In-depth expertise with all major media vehicles, including interactive. Continually monitors the changing media landscape, and adapts solutions, as appropriate. Proactively explores and experiments with new media to discover new solutions and adopt best practices.

• Client Service – Easily establishes himself as the “go-to” person on accounts by building strong rapport with clients. Relates to clients exceptionally well. Patient, competent and professional.

• Communication – Persuasive communicator with well-developed presentation and negotiation skills. Able to develop productive relationships with colleagues, clients and supplier partners.
