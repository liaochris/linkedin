Paula Dean Wood’s Summary

I have been buying and selling since since my early childhood. My parents owned and operated a Truck line and Livestock Auction in Kahoka, Missouri. Before the auctions, there was a junk sale shall I call it. The auctioneer would sell what ever was brought in. So I guess you can say buying and selling is in my blood. I have owned and operated 2 Hair Salons in the Las Vegas, NV area for 10 years. The last 12 years plus Paula Dean seller store Woodyswag Recycle 4 U has been selling and marketing her items on Woodyswag.net eBay, iOffer, Blujay, ArtFire, and Bonanzle. Living in the middle of Amish Country in Missouri, my husband, Woody, and I travel the adjoining states to bring valuable treasures to our buyers. I do love this marketing.
