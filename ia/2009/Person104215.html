Jan Rezab’s Summary

Jan Rezab, is Founder and CEO of HungryMobile, a new consultancy firm in mobile delivering consultancy, research, and advising to clients worldwide, and also is an executive Managing Director of LOKOLA, a search orientented business in mobile and web, from a completely different angle (business around you)

Prior to HungryMobile and LOKOLA, Jan Rezab worked 8 years in the mobile business as President / CEO of Mliven / Redboss. Redboss was a company he founded in 2002 and built up, primarily focused to mobile entertainment and games. One of Redboss key focuses was mobile games where together with the Redboss Games brand, it delivered top mobile games to clients like ICQ (AOL company), Vodafone, and Telefonica O2. Redboss was later acquired by a mobile software and services company MLIVEN, where his main achievment was to restructure the company from a service to a product company.
