TECHNOLOGY
Berk was creator of Production Office Electronic Manager (Poem), entertainment industry software that debuted at Paramount in 1994. Poem is used on projects at every major film studio in the U.S.

TV / FILM
Jeffrey Berk is an entertainment executive with 10+ years executive and producing experience in sponsor-driven, brand-integrated consumer TV, and 15+ years feature film management experience. A longtime Producers Guild member, his credits include concert specials, commercials and web videos. He has worked at every major film studio and on TV projects at Discovery, Disney, A&E, TBS. etc. A partial filmography is available at: imdb.com/name/nm0075268/.

COMMUNITY

PARK BUILDING

EDUCATION
Berk received his B.A. in Political Science (U.S. Foreign Policy) at Columbia University in New York.

FAMILY
Berk lives with and loves his wife and 2 girls in Studio City, CA.

PASSIONS
