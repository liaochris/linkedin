Tracey Amos’s Summary

Accomplished executive with broad functional expertise in business strategy, mortgage finance, operations and management consulting. A skilled executor who takes calculated risks delivering impactful business results. Strong people manager and change agent with a distinguished track record for building high performing teams and for attracting, developing and promoting talent. Extensive experience in leading and motivating individuals and teams to be both results-driven and execution focused. Excellent communicator who works effectively at all levels of an organization.

Tracey Amos’s Experience

  • Vice President Single Family Operations

    Fannie Mae

    (Public Company; FNM; Financial Services industry)

    February 2007 — February 2009 (2 years 1 month)

    Drove execution of operational strategy across the Single-Family Operations division. Led the custody group, which custodied over 2.5 million mortgage notes and certified data for approximately 15 percent of newly acquired loans. Led the custodial oversight function that performed initial and ongoing due diligence for third-party custodians supporting Fannie Mae business. Managed the data quality analytics teams. Expanded the role of the team to improve data quality and the satisfaction level of internal and external consumers of Single-Family Operations data. Led the MIS, Metrics and Reporting team, the primary developer of technology enabling tools and management reports used by Master Servicing and other internal and external groups. Led the Disclosure Operations and Trust Compliance group that produced investor disclosures for at-issuance and ongoing MBS, REMIC and structured products issued by Fannie Mae. Ensured Fannie Mae's compliance with its MBS Trust Agreements.

  • Director of Platform Design - Alternative Delivery Solutions

    Fannie Mae

    (Public Company; FNM; Financial Services industry)

    September 2005 — January 2007 (1 year 5 months)

    Built a new team in the Community and Multicultural Lending Group within the Single Family Mortgage Business. The new group focused on driving Fannie Mae's multi-phased/multi-pronged strategy to develop and automate an alternative delivery process designed to forge stronger relationships with the wholesale channels of Single Family lender partners. Authored the strategic plan for the Community and Multicultural Lending organization. Defined the strategy and execution plan for the Alternative Delivery Solution process and platform and secured over $2 million in initial investment funding to launch the initiative.

  • Director of Strategy and Business Management

    Fannie Mae

    (Public Company; FNM; Financial Services industry)

    April 2001 — September 2005 (4 years 6 months)

    Provided strategic, analytical and business management support to the Executive Vice President and senior leadership team of the Single Family Mortgage Business. Developed and maintained the division's first comprehensive multi-year strategic plan. Established annual goals and performance metrics and tracked and reported upon performance against goals. Developed mitigation plans for off track objectives and designed strategies to remediate performance gaps. Managed the divisions $200 plus million dollar administrative and capital budget. Updated the strategic plan annually to maintain alignment with organizational and divisional strategic priorities, divisional reorganization, investment spending to support new strategic initiatives and business execution.
