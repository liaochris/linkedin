Angus Warren’s Summary

Angus Warren brings integrity, creativity and a track record of getting results to capturing your organization's opportunities and resolving its unique issues.

He is the principal of Warren Business Consulting where he deploys his considerable business experience and insights to create value for large corporations, small and medium sized companies, private equity investors, investment banks and entrepreneurs, in sectors such as energy, oil and gas, commercial real estate, software and automotive parts.

Angus has been providing consulting and business advisory services since 2005. His specialties are in strategy formulation and implementation; negotiations and relationship building; joint ventures; business planning; international business; and training, facilitation and coaching.

Before setting up Warren Business Consulting, Angus had 15 years with BP in assignments that included gas exploration and production, oil pipelines and refining in the U.K., and leading the commercial effort for BP's involvement in the Angola LNG Project. With BP he gained deep commercial, business and international experience in many parts of the value chain of the oil and gas industry. His strong business skills have been applied in the upstream, midstream and downstream sectors.

REALESTATE INVESTOR
Angus is also a real estate investor. He has investments in Texas and Turkey, and is looking to invest further in these and other markets. He is interested in hearing from other entrepreneurs, investors, service providers and potential partners.
