Jamie Gaymon’s Summary

I grew up in and around network marketing-my Dad was a rep for on of the largest players in MLM for most of my childhood, and I have been involved with three different opportunities within the past 15 years. I did what I was told, too-built a big, burly list of everyone I knew (my day job is professional recruiting, so I ALWAYS had no trouble with that), stalked people through the malls and bookstores in my area, and basically did everything within my power to get "The Plan" in front of as many people as possible. I've heard NO so many times in this business that for awhile, I thought I was going through a second childhood!

I knew that network marketing worked, I just couldn't figure out why it wouldn't work for me.

After as much rejection as I could humanly stand, I decided to walk away. I felt like a complete failure, and couldn't figure out WHY I was failing.

One of my best friends convinced me to pick it back up recently, and I resolved that no matter what, I am not giving up until I figure out the secret formula to make network marketing work for me, even though I didn’t have a clue as to what I was doing wrong.

I became a student of the science of network marketing, and spent countless hours researching and studying how different people were achieving success in this arena.

Here's the problem-I was putting all of my time, effort, energy and money into talking people into my business, rather than marketing my business, building a network of people, and letting them decide whether they would join me or not.

MLMMitosis.com was created to help other network marketers, no matter what the business opportunity, enjoy excellent business growth.
