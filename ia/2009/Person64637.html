DonKokko "DK" Burnaby, HCS, CSBA’s Summary

DK Burnaby is an entrepreneur and professional in the fields of Recruiting, Human Capital Management, Sustainability, and Real Estate.

Recruiting & Human Capital Management:
DK Burnaby is an HCI certified Human Capital Strategist. He is an accomplished professional and consultant in the field of Recruiting and Human Capital Strategy, through both his own consulting practice, TalentSeed, and heading up North American technology recruiting for Concur. Leveraging a background in technology and business management, DK offers rare expertise in streamlining recruiting and human capital processes and implementing technology solutions to enhance efficiency and productivity.

Real Estate & Sustainability:
As a partner in Green Choice Realty, LLC and as a Real Estate Investor/Developer via NW Natural Homes, LLC, DK Burnaby is committed to doing business in a way that is good for the world and helping people live in healthier, more environmentally friendly homes. DK is a Certified Sustainable Building Advisor (CSBA), with expertise in Green building, including site development, construction, and operations. He works in partnership with his wife Vanessa Burnaby, a highly successful full time Realtor with Coldwell Banker Danforth & Associates. Vanessa and DK are both EcoBroker certified.
