B. (Brian) Nash’s Summary

I am managing a multi-faceted advisory, consulting & asset management firm dedicated to the alternative investment industry. After 6.5 years working with pioneers in the Fund Derivatives & Structured Fund Products space, I joined with my brother, whom brought 6.5 years of intensive alternative asset (hedge fund) investment management experience, and formed Twin Advisors, LLC and Twin Red Asset Management, LLC and have now launched five investment fund vehicles. The key to our success has been that our unique but complementary experiences, skill sets & networks combined with our drive, intelligence, and ingenuity yield innovative and compelling services & solutions which are attractive to a wide array clients including fund of fund managers, hedge fund managers, family offices, wealth management firms, private banks, capital markets divisions of investment banks, fund service providers, endowments, foundations, & all other types of hedge fund investors.

Thus far we have AUM in excess of USD 25 mil through 6 fund products (3 offshore and 3 onshore) and asset under advisement in excess of USD 4.5 billion.
