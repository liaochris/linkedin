Nancy Gold’s Summary

Gamen Group has been placing technology management consultants in industry-leading tier-one Big 4 Consulting Firms for 10+ years. Gamen Group is motivated to achieve the most positive outcomes for our clients and our candidates -- we like to say that we make marriages, not dates.

For clients – both professional services firms and corporate – Gamen Group has a very deep network of very talented people at all career levels, and experienced in a diverse array of functional and technology areas. A particular specialty is the ERP space, where our expertise goes back to SAP R/2 and some of the initial SAP implementations in North America. Gamen Group can connect you with the talent you need in today’s competitive worldwide marketplace.

As principal of the firm, I’m committed to partnering with my clients’ recruiting teams to fully understand the strategic and tactical imperatives behind a hiring decision and preparing candidates as fully as possible. I invite you to take a moment to peruse the recommendations on this page, many of which are from recruiters with whom I have had long and positive professional relationships.

From a candidate’s perspective, working with Gamen Group is truly a unique experience -- we are not like other search firms. Our long experience working in the consulting space and deep connections with our clients gives us the ability to offer unbiased advice and correct positioning to help achieve positive outcomes.

We are committed to sharing information, coaching, and advising our candidates at every step of the hiring process. For junior candidates, our knowledge and experience can help you to take the next step in your career; for more experienced candidates, my firm’s unbiased perspective can assist you in making a strategic, highly-leveraged career move.
