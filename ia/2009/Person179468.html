Orvel Ray Wilson, CSP’s Summary

Guerrilla Sales and Marketing. A 28 years as a professional speaker, author and consultant, OrvelRay's work has earned international acclaim from clients including IBM, Choice Hotels, United Airlines, Apple, and Ingram Micro. He has spoken in more than 40 countries on every continent except Antarctica.

He is the author or co-author of five books in the legendary "Guerrilla Marketing" series, including: Guerrilla Selling, Guerrilla Trade Show Selling, Guerrilla TeleSelling, Guerrilla Negotiating and Guerrilla Retailing.

His work has appeared in INC, Fast Company, The Wall Street Journal, and dozens of trade and industry journals.

He is a contributing author of "How to Position Yourself As the Obvious Expert: Turbocharge Your Consulting or Coaching Business Now!" with Elsom Eldridge et. all.

Orvel Ray Wilson, CSP’s Specialties:

Orvel Ray speaks to sales meetings, small business groups, conferences and trade associations about unconventional sales and marketing tactics that build business. From a general-session keynote to an all-day workshop, he will arm your group with literally hundreds of simple, low-cost or no-cost sales and marketing tips, tactics and tricks that can turn big profits.
