Robert Cornish’s Summary

Richter10.2 Media Group is a web PR firm specialized in building online attention for our clients to attract new leads and generate business. We build our clients reputation and image online making it look pristine while also expanding their companies reach on the web to get in front of prospective new clients. Our goal is for our clients to control search results, control public perception about their company and generate more traffic to their site and further to generate more business to their company using powerful web PR campaigns

PR is the vital step before marketing an advertising, we ensure that our clients Web PR campaigns are standardly done while using the most innovative and effective methods of controlling PR to maximize attention.
