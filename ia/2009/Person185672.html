Peter Gregory, CISA, CISSP, DRCE’s Summary

Corporate business security strategist, meeting business objectives through improvements in process and technology. Experienced in software engineering, sales support, product engineering and support, IT architecture, IT management, computer operations, and security management.

CISSP (2000), CISA (2002), DRCE (2008).

Published author of twenty books on technology and security, with editions in four languages (complete list http://peterhgregory.com/books/ ). Interviews and published articles in Computerworld, BusinessWeek, Software Magazine, CIO Magazine, Information Security Magazine, Forbes, Tech Republic, C|Net News, SearchSecurity, and the Seattle Times. Speaker at RSA, SecureWorld Expo, Western Pension & Benefits Conference, WTIA, SIM, West Coast Security Forum, InfraGard, and Cybercrime III.

Expert witness / Friend of the Court in 2006, leading to the successful federal prosecution of a high-profile computer crime.

Founding member of Pacific CISO Forum. Board member of InfraGard, active in (ISC)² and ISACA. Graduate of FBI Citizens' Academy, active member of FBI Citizens' Academy Alumni Association.
