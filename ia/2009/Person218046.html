Simon Ho’s Summary

Passionate and innovative problem solver. Apply a unique combination of functional and technical approach to problem solving and bringing about highly effective online solutions to the market. Self-starter who can take an idea from concepts to successful implementation. Excellent in sharing a vision and capable of assembling a team of like minded individuals across geographical regions and time zones to achieve the common goal.


Founded two companies that offer consulting and innovative online services to the child care industry. The last company, a SaaS based center management solution company, was acquired by the largest childcare provider in the United States.

  • Founder

    Childcare Online Services

    (Education Management industry)

    2002 — 2007 (5 years )

    Founded Childcare Online Services, a start-up company delivering a SaaS based center management solutions to the childcare industry. The small start-up company successfully adopted Thomas Friedman “The world is Flat” model with a small development team based in Chennai, India. The company acquired many clients paying monthly subscriptions. It was acquired by Knowledge Universe in 2007 to become its internal online solution platform as well as to offer to the broader childcare market.

  • President

    AutoDraftPro

    (Education Management industry)

    2000 — 2002 (2 years )

    Developed the AutoDraft solutions, a hassle free tuition payment solution for childcare parents. Implemented the solutions for many child care providers, which significant improve their cash flow, reduce directors’ administrative burden and enhance customer satisfaction.

    Consulted with a large for-profit childcare provider, Childrens’ Creative Learning Centers, in the design, development and implementation of an employer sponsored back-up child care online solution. This solution enables the provider to partner with local employers to offer back up child care benefits to employees.

  • Manager

    PricewaterhouseCoopers, LLP

    (Management Consulting industry)

    1995 — 2000 (5 years )

    Consulted with many national and multi-national companies with broad Information Technology implementation and financial management consulting experience:

    - Managed the implementation of a 3-year multi-million dollar financial reporting management system to improve the Chinese Central Bank’s accounting and management information and to reform the financial sectors reporting in China. Led a 30-staff team and successfully implemented the Accounting Information System in 6 provinces.

    - Engaged in the analysis, design, construction and implementation of the Television Syndication Accounting System for Turner Broadcasting System (TBS-CNN) in Atlanta, GA. The system produced significant annual savings because it allows the company to pay participants associated with film and television products on a cash basis rather than on a billing basis.

    - Implemented SAP for IBM Storage Systems Division (SSD) in San Jose, CA. This is a large, complex, full life cycle project covering three geographical areas: San Jose, Germany and Hungary, for over 1,000 users. Project team lead for General Ledger and Accounts Receivable.

  • Senior Auditor

    Consulted the Franchise division of Holiday Inn Worldwide in Memphis, TN. Assisted in the implementation of Dunn & Bradstreet Accounts Receivable which significantly streamlined the credit and collection function.
