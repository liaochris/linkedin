Natacha Delpeche’s Summary

I am a graduate from The Art Institute of New York City with an Interactive Media Design/ Multimedia and Web Design Degree.My multimedia skills cover a wide spectrum. I am an experienced Graphic Designer, Illustrator, Video Editor, Videographer, Audio Engineer, Producer, Script Writer, Painter, Script Supervisor, and more. My multimedia professionalism has enabled me to accomplish and conquer any project given to me at any given time.

With that said, I have a very large Audio Production background. I owned and professionally controlled my own in house recording studio for 4 years where I've produced, recorded, engineered, and mastered multiple recording artists. Along with other media programs, audio programs that I have mastered includes Protools, Fruity Loops, Cool Edit Pro, Reason, Garage Band, and more. I also have much knowledge of the Akai MP 2000 and the Triton keyboard.

While in college I worked at the Emmy Award Winning Onscreen Entertainment where I successfully met deadlines for the Post Production of The Miracle's Boys series, Reading Rainbow, and That's What Im Talking About episodes.

While at the American Movie Company I had the opportunity to prestigiously create and write 3 scripts for the Bar Angels web series, I professionally edited and composited videos for the UFT (ATR) website that are currently online , and I've worked on How I Met Your Mother along with many more media productions. Furthermore, I have also worked as Video Production Manager, Teleprompter Operator, and Script Supervisor for various productions including the Movie Loft, UFT, Caprisun, Dan Solin, MTV, Suze Orman, and many more.
