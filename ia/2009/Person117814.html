John Osborn’s Summary

Creative and experienced strategic media planning/buying executive with a history of servicing blue chip clients and successfully navigating agencies and clients through emerging media opportunities. Grounded in traditional media and committed to realizing the potential of emerging media, I am looking to take my established skills and experience to the next (i.e. “new product development”) level at a forward looking company. Here I will be a co-creator of the next business model for paid advertising. With the depth of experience to lead clients, media/content suppliers and agencies in the right direction I can show how big spenders have many more options to be successful in this changing landscape than merely shifting marketing budgets from traditional to online. The next 5 years in marketing communications require uncommon applied imagination to seize the digital interactive platforms that are already arriving (interactive TV delivered by cable and satellite, digital broadband, IPTV and more) -- even as commercial avoidance is growing astronomically. I bring a talent and approach that few media people can match, combining creativity and innovation studies and facilitation, top level client experience with blue chip companies and a track record of actualizing the potential of new media forms. I have learned in the last 4 years that I work best in teams of inspired innovators with a goal. My goal is to help create the next successful platform for the business of advertising. If your goals can use the support of my skill set & experience, let’s talk.
