Darla Sycamore’s Summary

I am an experienced financial executive. My background includes a period in the National Accounting Standards Department at Ernst & Young as well as an accounting professor at the University of Toronto and Lecturer at McMaster University in Hamilton.

I also worked in industry as the Deputy Chief Accountant of the Toronto Dominion Bank and as Treasurer and Director Finance (CFO) at the Ontario Pension Board.

Darla Sycamore’s Specialties:

I specialize in financial reporting standards and conversions to International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS). (see My blog).

Decision Making using soft data is also an area of speciality.
