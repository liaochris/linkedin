Liz Moise’s Summary

In October 2008 Liz joined BluePoint Venture Marketing as Manager of Account Services.

Liz brings a diverse background in marketing, public relations and design to her clients at BluePoint. Leveraging extensive experience in graphic design, Liz now focuses her creative talent on marketing and public relations with specific expertise in social media, blogger relations and community-building to deliver campaigns that harness both old and new media opportunities.

As Marketing Communications and PR Manager at InnoCentive, a global crowd-sourcing company, Liz and her team drove high-profile coverage through extensive relationships with national media, social media and bloggers and created a broad community of "Solvers" to tackle global design, manufacturing and engineering challenges in the areas of science, IT, clean tech and healthcare. She also launched and maintained Innocentive’s first corporate blog, and deepened ongoing relationships within the community through Twitter and Facebook.

As Art Director and Account Manager at Nokia Interactive Advertising (formerly Enpocket) for mobile clients including Sprint, Hyundai, Pepsi, MTV, and others. Liz worked closely with high profile clients who were beginning to experiment in mobile marketing and delivered customized mobile web sites and interactive campaigns integrated with traditional media campaigns. Liz also built and managed a team of in-house designers and freelancers to deliver custom solutions.

Liz’s other experience includes a design and marketing positions at the Massachusetts Secretary of State, Convoq Inc., Chedd-Angier-Lewis and Picasa.

In her spare time, from 2006-2008 Liz was Creative Director of a women’s apparel company which she founded with two college-friends in 2006, and has recently moved on to start on her own collection. She has also freelanced as a Graphic Designer since 2002 (www.lizmoise.com).

Liz Moise’s Specialties:

Marketing and PR specialties: Account Management, Social Media strategy, Community building, email and web-based marketing.
Graphic Design Expertise: Web (PS, DW), Print Collateral (all things Adobe), Mobile (MMS and WAP), Corporate Branding, Exhibit Design
Other areas of Expertise: Team Management, Art Direction, Copy Writing, Fashion Design, Illustration, Photography
