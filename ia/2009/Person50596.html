    A professional business consulting center... these are the words that best describe BV Business Center. Our company provides exclusive consulting services in different areas such as: Marketing Consulting,General Management Consulting, Web/Online Consulting, Legal Consulting and others.

    We address not only existing Romanian companies but also future developing companies that want to develop and take their business national or even worldwide.
    Providing support to foreign companies which plan to enter and expand their services/products onto the Romanian market is also one of our main concern.

    In a "sick" business environment we can be your "remedy" and not only cure the "sickness" but also prevent it and giving further assistance to teach companies how to "heal" themselves.
