Dan Borkat’s Experience

  • Senior Account Executive

    The Halcyon Group

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    August 2006 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    Mr. Borkat is Senior Executive Recruiter at The Halcyon Group, an established Executive Search firm with a successful twenty-four year operating history. As an affilliate office of Management Recruiters International (MRI), The Halcyon Group is part of a worldwide network of 1,100 offices, providing access to the resources necessary to complete even the most challenging searches. The Halcyon Group remains under the same ownership and management since its inception in 1985.

    The Halcyon Group's client base consists primarily of companies within the consumer products space. We have an extensive network consisting of currently-employed professionals ranging from mid-management to the C-level that are open to discussing the 'right' opportunity. Mr. Borkat's focus is working with Sales, Marketing and Product Development talent.

  • Director of Marketing

    Nestor Traffic Systems

    (Public Company; NEST.OB; Public Safety industry)

    April 2003 — April 2006 (3 years 1 month)

    Prior to joining The Halcyon Group, Mr. Borkat served as Director of Marketing for Nestor, Inc., a publicly traded company focused on providing safety solutions to municipal, state and federal government agencies.

    In this role, Mr. Borkat worked cross-functionally with Nestor's executive management team, sales team, engineering department and municpal customers to define and develop appropriate product and service solutions to meet each customer's needs.

    Additionally, Mr. Borkat managed all aspects of traditional marketing communications including public relations, video, web, print and tradeshow presence.

    In addition to his primary marketing responsibilities, Mr. Borkat worked directly with the CEO to build a national sales force consisting of two National Sales Managers and five Regional Sales Executives generating over $10 million in annual revenue. Mr. Borkat had dotted-line management responsibility and day-to-day accoutability for the sales team.
