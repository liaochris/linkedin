Jana Novak’s Summary

Novak Consulting has been in existence since 1999, when the founder and executive, Jana Novak, first took a leave from full-time, in-office work to concentrate on using her writing, editing and communications skills for a variety of clients. Since then, Novak has moved seamlessly between working freelance for clients as diverse as political campaigns, large businesses, and small educational organizations to working full time for magazines, small businesses, and prominent national politicians.

During this time, she has offered superior writing and editing products, while providing strategic advice in communications and market positioning. From providing speeches for CEOs and politicians, to writing and editing newsletters and opinion articles, to emphasizing smart and concise communication tools in both new and old media, she has helped numerous clients share their visions with their specific audiences and the greater marketplace.
