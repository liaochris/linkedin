Doug Beabout CPC CSP’s Summary

Doug Beabout brings over thirty years of expertise in top billings, placement firm ownership and industry training. His reputation for training excellence has placed him repeatedly as a guest speaker and author for non-profit, private, national, regional and state recruiting associations.

Doug works a top-producing desk every day. He is uniquely qualified as a professional personnel services trainer. Having successfully thrived and survived the last three recessions, Doug knows the trenches. Doug speaks from experience to thousands of personnel services professionals every year and is tuned-in” to the specific and unique practices necessary to excel in this challenging and recovering marketplace.

Doug Beabout is owner and president of Doug Beabout Group in Miramar Beach, FL. Prior, Doug was owner and president of his own personnel services firms, The Douglas Howard Group, a personnel services firm in Kent, Ohio for seven years and Douglas William Associates, for ten years in Dayton, Ohio. Doug has held the title of CPC; certified personnel consultant since 1981. Doug’s professional experience started as a USAF Strategic Air Command Officer in B-52 bomber units throughout the world.

As an in-house personnel services firm trainer and business consultant, Doug brings success to all he serves. Doug has personally trained thousands of recruiters and established hundreds of recruiting firms in the U.S. and abroad. He has established over a dozen, highly successful, independent recruiting firms in the last twelve months

Doug also provides the most comprehensive, multimedia recruiter training materials available anywhere. Doug’s training seminars, personnel services development resources and materials are in great demand throughout the recruiting industry. Doug’s materials and tools are available on his website: WWW.DougBeabout.com
