Carlos Gonçalves’s Summary

Avila Business Center offers unique corporate visibility, with attractively furnished executive offices in a professional setting including conference rooms, broadband internet and a full range of services. Located in the heart of the Central Business District: Av. João Crisóstomo, 30, 5º 1050-127 Lisboa (near Av. República and Saldanha Square), is also structured for small and large companies, independent workers and other activities that do not require a physical space to operate.

Since it was recognized as an innovative company, Ávila Business Center became Eoffice Ambassador for Portugal in July 2008. eOffice is a Premium Business Center located in UK ofering flexible workplace and virtual office solutions to help small and growing businesses enjoy the same professional edge as blue-chip organisations (www.eoffice.net).

We have our very own dynamic conception, betting on a quality and personalized service in diverse areas, such as:

- Office Renting
- Virtual offices
- Hot Desking
- Work Meeting Room

The business world requires furthermore practical and rational solutions. Ávila Business Center was one of the companies created to fulfil this modern economy requirement. It is a business centre, structured for small and large companies, independent workers and other activities that do not require a physical space to operate.

The Office Centre offers you Telephone attendance, reception desk administrative and logistic support.
