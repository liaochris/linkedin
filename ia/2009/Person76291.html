Ethan Stillman’s Summary

I build ideas and turn them into products and businesses.

My over-arching agenda is to dramatically enhance and increase our culture of technological literacy and build a broadly accessible industrialized project incubation infrastructure.

Starting new businesses is tough, but with the declining price of technology production the biggest hurdle has become organization and that is a very, very, cool fact.
