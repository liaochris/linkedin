Sarah Bard’s Summary

Sarah holds a BS degree in Horticulture and has three years of experience working in an environmental lab. She also has experience as a personal assistant, house-sitter and pet sitter. She worked for three years as a companion/homemaker where she had highly satisfied clients. Sarah is highly organized, friendly, dependable, and efficient and finds great reward in her proven ability to help others. She works well independently, and has experience training others. In the scientific realm Sarah a strong interest in tissue culture, micro propagation, environmental/biological science and genetics. Her goal is to secure a rewarding position which entails helping others, whether it be directly through personal assisting, or through contributions to valuable research in the laboratory.
