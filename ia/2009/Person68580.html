Dward Greenbird’s Summary

I want to complete the card game I created called "RAFFUS®" by adding to the directions and card deck a teaching DVD where one person can play against the computer (up to three other players) and learn how to play the game. I am working on a 2nd playing card deck. It will be a Native American playing card deck where it will speak of natives past and present. It has major design changes compared to the everyday playing card deck. There are many Indian Casinos that have expressed interest in selling the decks at their casinos. The non-profit business that I am starting will work with Education, middle school children and mentors.
