  • Founder

    Duša Journalism

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    February 2009 — Present (4 months)

    Founder, writer, designer, web master @ dusajournalism.nl

    Everyone can leave a part of him / herself on the world. The influence we have on our own lives (and those of others) is often underestimated. Whether it is because of laziness, fear or simple ignorance. We often choose not to do something because of these things, we are often afraid to fail.
    At Duša Journalism we ask you one question: Why not?
    Why not use your full potential, why sit and be quiet when you can run and shout? When you can work to make sure the world hears your voice, if even a small chance exists that you can reach someone?
    Doing what you have passion for, trying even though you could fail, is what I believe in. The answer to the question 'Why not?' should never be 'Because I am afraid'.
    Writing, researching and discovering are some of my passions. I want these passions to shine through in my everyday life, I want to share them.
    I want my footsteps, my voice and my soul to leave an imprint on this world. Have an impact, however small it may be. Inspiring only one person would already be more than enough.

    Duša Journalism - Leave a mark.
