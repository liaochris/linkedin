James Hallam’s Summary

I have 13 years of experience leading the development and design of web sites and web applications, leading larger projects for enterprise, government and higher education clients, and smaller projects for tourism, real estate and entertainment clients. I have a broad set of skills covering information architecture, development and planning roles, but typically focus on finding roles that emphasize design, strategy and leadership. Most recently, I have been working to bring social media strategies to communities previously under served by a collaborative online presence. I emphasize adherence to standards, participation in open source projects, accessibility, user experience and the consistent and appropriate implementation of technological solutions.

The scope of my work is now expanding to include Industrial Design, which is helping me develop new skills and enhance my existing ones. Building on an existing base of Fine Arts training in theatre and opera (performance, design and directing), I am now integrating the concepts of experience-based design, affordance, sustainability, biomimicry, social responsibility and craft into my work both online and off. I am passionate about the development of human scale networks that successfully integrate internet technology, connected devices, offline social networks and sustainable practices.

James Hallam’s Specialties:

Industrial Design, UX Design, Web Design, Graphic Design, Web Development (CSS, HTML, Javascript, XML, PHP, MySQL, Drupal, more), 2D Design Software (Photoshop, Illustrator, In Design), model-making and fabrication (turning/machining, vacuum-forming, more).
