Mitch Phillips’s Summary

I am a lucky and thankful person. Lucky that I get to talk for a living everyday and get to work with incredible creative and innovative people, and thankful that I can do all of this while wearing Dark Side of the Moon pajamas.

I'm not sure anymore if my job description is "voice actor" or "voice talent". I concentrate on in promos, imaging and branding for television, radio, cable, sports teams, and the web. I also love working with producers, writers, creative directors and advertising directors; addressing their needs and keeping up with the changing directions of our industry so I can fine tune and adapt. Some audio (voice) samples are available at www.mitchphillips.com. See you on the beach!

Mitch Phillips’s Specialties:

power voice, movie trailer voice, commercials, voiceovers and promos for radio, television, satellite, movie trailers, sports teams, corporate presentations, and voices for websites, games, meetings, robots and sashimi parties.
