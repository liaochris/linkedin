Danny Brown’s Summary

18 years experience with all aspects of sales and key client account management. Mainly focused on Telecoms, Technology and Media during the past 10 years.

Now with Cambridge Helpdesk the focus remains telecoms and tech, but now with startups and SME's as well as large corporates.

Cambridge Helpdesk was set up to support businesses who value technology but prefer to leave the day to day management to someone else. We work with one person startups through to SME's with more than 200 staff.

Our services include;
* IT Support & Management
* 24/7 Server 7 workstation monitoring
* Telecom system installation & VoIP
* Office moves & Cabling
* Anti-Virus, Email Security & SPAM Management
* IP Security (network testing, Hardware & Software)
