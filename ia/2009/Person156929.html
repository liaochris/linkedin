Maureen Nelson’s Summary

Co-author of "Getting Your Ideal Internship," 2nd edition (WetFeet).

Career counselor and job search coach experienced in all aspects of career development: assessments, career exploration, personal branding and packaging (resumes, portfolios, websites, interviewing), career transitions.

Twenty-plus years in the corporate world in a variety of industries (technology, real estate, consulting, training, retail, publishing, social services). Able to leverage multiple generations and cultures in the workforce, maximizing knowledge sharing and competitive advantage. Available to partner with talent management professionals to create and manage employee career development programs.
