Krista Rundiks’s Summary

Hi my name is Krista and I recently graduated from Metro state in Dec. 2006. I have studied Multimedia encompassing graphic design, video, audio, interactive projects utilizing flash, and pre-production and project planning.

From 1999-2004 I studied at Red Rocks Community College where I developed a strong sense of software applications further backing this up with 2 years at Metro focusing on writing and planning for projects.

While I was a student I worked part-time as a free-lance designer (since 2000) and currently have been employed for 3 years at successful online background screening company based in downtown Denver.

Outside of work I spend much of my time being a mom to a 7 year old boy named Kailer and working on my creative projects such as intricate/abstract collage work and music. I believe in hard work, dedication, sacrifice to make things happen, and being optimistic and open to new opportunities in the present and future.

I see myself developing my skills as technology progresses. In the near future I would like to expand my professional horizons and obtain more work as a contract designer or transition to a company that offers a well rounded compensation.

My dream would be to work for a large music label creating websites and graphics.
Further down the line I hope to one day produce a CD of my original songs and one day create a multimedia driven art show encompassing video and audio, large-scale mixed media pieces, and whatever else the budget may allow.

If you have any questions let me know.
Have a great day!

Krista
