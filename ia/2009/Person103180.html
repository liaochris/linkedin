Jamie Ashe’s Summary

Jamie Ashe is a pioneer of the digital online and mobile advertising industry. Jamie is far above the competition when it comes to being in the “now” instead of following the “yesterday” of trends. A graduate of high school at the young age of 15-years-old, Jamie, went on to graduate with a B.S. in Computer Science, quickly followed by a B.A. in Journalism and continuing her education until earning her MBA by the age of 21 and a Ph.D. just four short years later.

Jamie quickly became an industry powerhouse by achieving top spots with multi-billion dollar corporations such as Cox Broadcasting, America Online, and Microsoft where she earned the title as one of the “Top 500 Executives in the World” by the software and computer giant. Jamie branched out into the private sector where she received Media Post’s “Industry’s Movers and Shakers” and “Accounts on the Move” awards.

As Vice President of Sales and Vice President of Business Development during the past decade for Medium 21 and Media Mayhem Corporation, Jamie launched hundreds of campaigns for high line clients such as 20th Century Fox, NBC, Disney, Starz Entertainment, Paramount Pictures, New Line Cinemas, and hundreds of others. Her campaigns are frequently visible in People, US Magazine, Star Magazine, TMZ, Newsweek, AOL News, Forbes, Business Week, Associated Press, Reuters, and hundreds of other on and offline media outlets and wires. Various entertainers globally seek after her to host 5-Star red carpet events from Hollywood to New York to Milan and Cannes for film premieres or charity poker and gambling events.

Jamie Ashe is a prudent and powerful force that not only covers all of the bases but also excels in winning the game.
