Brad Margol’s Summary

I am a senior level business strategy leader with 20+ years of experience in management consulting, corporate development, and financial restructuring including completing several acquisitions valued from $7MM to $7B and building two strategy consulting practices. I have extensive vertical industry experience in banking and credit cards.

I am currently a member of Houlihan Lokey's Financial Restructuring Group. I am licensed with FINRA as a General Securities Registered Representative (Series 7 and 63).

Previously, I was head of Corporate Strategy for the second largest debit and credit card processor. Prior to that, I led Corporate Development for the #6 credit card issuer where I sold two distressed card portfolios for over $700M and managed due diligence and integration of a $6.4B merger.

I spent 13 years as a management consultant, with Visa Inc. as my biggest client. I managed and performed Visa Functional Cost Studies, developed new products such as Visa Cash and Visa Buxx, and presented marketing strategies for segmentation and risk-based pricing. I developed a consumer segment for Bank of America worth over $500MM and chaired a monthly strategy roundtable for senior executives from the largest credit card banks.

I have also consulted to the telecom, entertainment, and utilities industries, and held Interim CFO positions for an international telecom firm in turnaround, an Internet start-up, and an independent motion picture production co.

Previously, I was an international banker for Sumitomo Mitsui Bank, where I managed a corporate finance portfolio of over $400MM and led the corporate restructuring and film financing areas. Earlier in my career, I was a CPA with Ernst & Young, where I helped take 3 entertainment companies public and led a high-profile bankruptcy-fraud engagement.

Brad Margol’s Specialties:

Financial restructuring, management consulting, international mergers and acquisitions, corporate finance, strategic planning, functional cost studies, portfolio management and analysis, profitability analysis, motion picture financing, risk management, asset management, Chapter 11 bankruptcy, distressed asset sales, profit and loss accountability, product development, marketing campaign development, client services, LBOs, payment processing, credit and debit cards, and 3 CFO roles.
