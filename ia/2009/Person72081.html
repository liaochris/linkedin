Ellen Wasylina’s Summary

A multicultural, multilingual international affairs professional, with over 20 years of international experience in the United States and France, as a manager, communications and marketing expert. Expertise in
*Transatlantic issues, Central and Eastern Europe, NATO and EU enlargement, defense and strategic issues thereto;
*Conflict resolution as it relates to interethnic, interreligious strife, terrorism and insurgency, and its effect on democracy, human rights, economic and social developmental issues.

My goal is to provide expertise in transatlantic issues so as to improve relations between the U.S. and Europe generally, and to new and future EU and NATO members specifically, assisting and contributing to the amelioration in political, economic and commercial relations, as well in energy and defense related areas.
