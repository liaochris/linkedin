Jai Sharma’s Summary

Jai has over 14 years' cross-functional and Technical experience in various modules of SAP. He has been a senior consultant at SAP Australia for over six years and has been contracting in the SAP space for the last seven years.

Jai seeks a strategic role as a solutions architect/senior I.S professional for corporate SAP systems. His substantial cross-functional and technical experience would be invaluable within global/regional competency centre type environments to architect and support a large SAP foot-print.

Jai Sharma’s Specialties:

Significant experience in various SAP modules notably: SRM, MM, PS/IM, PLM, FI/CO. Has also worked in MRP and Sales/Distribution domains in non-SAP environments. Technically, significant hands-on experience in workflow and Web-dynpro for ABAP.

Considerable experience in the manufacturing sector particularly in Pharmaceuticals/bulk chemicals and FMCG
