Jeff Peterson’s Summary

I know I'm biased, but I think I have the greatest job in the world. I'm fortunate to be the Director of Sales of PGC, a highly successful company that facilitates the placement of international consultants into roles within the US market. Our unique business model is centered on the Australian consultants we represent and the strong relationships we value with our Direct Partner Agencies. Our all-star team, comprising of American & Australian professionals, gives us the in-depth knowledge and track record of success needed to best represent consultants for opportunities in the US.

Marked by their own unique individuality, everyone in life has attributes that make their stamp on the world of true significance and a common stream to which they seemingly gravitate towards. Although the scope of my professional experience contains a broad spectrum, it can most easily be defined within the encompassing label of "Business Development".

Over the years I've most enjoyed building teams, and creating a culture of unity, confidence, and success, while working toward common goals through Sales and Marketing initiatives.

The Marketing aspect of business has always been a key factor in the mindset with which I have approached the Sales aspect of business. Although Sales & Marketing have always had a "which came first, the chicken or the egg" type of relationship of pulling for power, I have found it equally important to marry the two in order to achieve true cohesiveness and keep the company vision strong. On the same note, the knowledge base of the Operational aspects of business has also aided in equipping me with the functionality knowledge I have needed for Feature/Benefit type of selling in various vertical markets of business development.

I'm pleased with the many prospects and incredible potential of utilizing all of the above with the expansion of our team at Precision Global Consulting.

The best is yet to come...
