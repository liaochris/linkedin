Dr. Rex M Rogers’s Summary

Currently serving on behalf of The Timothy Group as Interim Executive Director for SAT-7, www.sat7.org, a satellite television ministry producing quality Christian programming for Arabic peoples in the Middle East and North Africa.

Co-authored with Dr. Rick Amidon, "Today You Do Greatness" (FaithWalk Publishing, forthcoming Fall 2009), a book about the value choices one faces climbing the ladder of success.

Leadership and fundraising consultant serving as Vice President with The Timothy Group, experience with nonprofit organization board and exectuive leadership and administration, speaker, author, radio host. Consult with boards of directors and organizational executives, work in leadership partnering or coaching, develop capital campaigns. Leadership partnering is direct personal interaction over a sustained period that helps leaders achieve their aspirations and add value to their lives, colleagues, and organizations.

Since 1993, author and voice of "Making a Difference," a syndicated radio commentary offering a Christian perspective on wide-ranging religious, political, educational, social and cultural issues and current events. Also write similar material for a blog at www.rexmrogers.com.

Author of books: "Gambling: Don't Bet On It" (Baker Books, 1997, Kregel Pub, 2005); and "Christian Liberty: Living for God in a Changing Culture" (Baker Books, 2003).

Written articles on leadership, political and social issues, higher education, gambling, fundraising, nonprofit boards and administration, planning, current events, Christian living, and more.

Speak regularly at commencements, banquets or luncheons, church services or events, school programs, Rotary or other local community group meetings, corporate or business meetings, etc. Sample speaking topics and testimonials may be found and speaking arrangements may be made at www.rexmrogers.com.

Dr. Rex M Rogers’s Specialties:

Leadership Development, Non-profit Board Development, Fundraising and Capacity-Building, Major Donor Development, Capital Campaigns, Strategic Planning, Organizational Structure and Administration, Name Changes and Mergers, Leadership Position Searches, Writing and Speaking.
