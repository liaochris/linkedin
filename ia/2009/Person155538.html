Matt Felton’s Summary

Dynamic blend of technical, financial, personnel, management, and marketing experience. Strong ability to balance the needs of people with the capabilities of GIS technology.

8 years of training experience, with a healthy mix of communication skills that range from public presentations to meeting facilitation and business development. Strong technical background and ability to help others understand the value of GIS.

Recognized by peers in the industry as a diligent, trustworthy, and creative individual, and this reputation helped me contribute to the growth and success of Towson's GIS efforts to become the premier GIS consultancy in the region.
