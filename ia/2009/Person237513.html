Tracy DiMarino’s Summary

I'm an energetic senior public relations student in the E.W. Scripps School of Journalism at Ohio University. I will be graduating Summa Cum Laude at the end of March; therefore, I am currently looking for an entry-level public relations or marketing job in the Cleveland/Akron area.

I have done external relations for the McClure School for the past 2 years. In addition, during my summer at Lief & Karson Communications, I worked on projects for clients such as Chipotle Mexican Grille, the Ohio Design Centre, Italian singer Patrizio Buanne and North Union Famers Market.

My classroom experience includes a variety of public relations, journalism and business classes. Some highlights are a strategic planning class where four classmates and I researched and wrote four public relations plans based on real and hypothetical situations and a campaigns class where my group is working on increasing student interest in on-campus housing at Hocking College.

I am also the treasurer of Colleges Against Cancer and a member of the American Cancer Society's Ohio Collegiate Leadership Team.
