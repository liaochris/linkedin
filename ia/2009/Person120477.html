Joselito Montalban’s Summary

Much work in the past involved extensive writing. These include drafting of a number of health plans and project proposals as the municipal health officer/rural health physician of a small, remote rural town in the southern Philippine island of Mindanao. Also, there is some experience, as a health development consultant mainly at the University of the Philippines’ National Institutes of Health, in writing scientific papers that saw print in both local and international journals, web content, and a comprehensive end-of-project summary report.

The writing aspect of work in the past is secondary to other responsibilities like project management, field research and teaching, as well as clinical work as a community doctor. Current work with the Philippine subsidiary of Synchrogenix Information Strategies, a leading American medical and scientific communications company, is regulatory writing on a more full-time basis.
