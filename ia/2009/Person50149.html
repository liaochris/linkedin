Craig Merritt’s Summary

I bring thirty years of manufacturing, financial and IT solutions experience. My experience both as a business owner and consultant, has encompassed most functional areas of business operations. Product development, production control, cost accounting systems, and ISO certification (13485) of medical devices are among my past projects in a diverse set of industries including beverage, medical lasers, wireless systems, petroleum and long term care. I have an MS and BS in Engineering from Stanford, and an MBA from the Haas School at the University of California at Berkeley. I've developed IT solutions in many of these industries using a variety of tools including most recently MS SQL Server, InterSystems Ensemble, Caché, ODBC and others.

I'm seeking to introduce more consultants, particularly in healthcare IT, to the power of applying our groundbreaking mobile data collection, rapid application development and integration framework. Business Intelligence is totally dependent upon good data, and data needs change as fast as the environment. Bayr Solus mobile enterprise solution framework enables the most futureproof, evolvable solutions ever dreamed of to address any business problem imaginable.

Craig Merritt’s Specialties:

Requirements development, Systems Integration and Workflow development using InterSystems Ensemble and Bayr Solus "Informed Now!" Mobile Solution Framework.
