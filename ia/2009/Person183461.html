Paul Murnane’s Summary

Paul is an Account Director and Brand Strategist based in Southern California. He specializes in brand strategy and integrating branding with business and marketing strategies. He is responsible for developing long-term strategic client relationships that enable client companies to leverage the power of their brand in the marketplace.

Paul brings broad-based industry experience that includes brand consulting, network consulting, banking and finance as well as being an entrepreneur. He earned an MBA in International Management from the Thunderbird School of Global Management and a BS in Finance from Santa Clara University.
