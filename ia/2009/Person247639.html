William Walla’s Summary

William Walla is a Manager of Network Infrastructure located in Houston, TX. In this role William is responsible for network design, planning, implementation, break/fix and infuencing purchasing decisions.
Before being in this role William has had much experience in the Consulting and Senior Network Analyst role. Most of this experience has been in the fast paced environment of Consulting and the Energy Industry, developing and supporting trading floors that actively participate on the NYMEX.
The majority of William’s focus has been on routing and switching in both Cisco and industry open standards. He is equally versed in dynamic routing protocols and spanning-tree, wireless and has good exposure to security.
William has a customer-centric attitude and great ability to manage a project from both a technical and business perspective. This talent comes from his last position where he managed his own technical deliverables as well as his resources and vendors to delivery.

William Walla’s Specialties:

He has earned leading certifications in networking including: Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert in Routing and Switching (CCIE R&S #18690), Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP), Cisco Certified Design Professional (CCDP) and F5 Certified Systems Engineer for Local Traffic Manger SE (F5-SE-LTM.)
