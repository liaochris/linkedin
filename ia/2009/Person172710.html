Neeraj Tiwari - The Match Maker of Recruitment’s Summary

Founder and MD, Jobkeeda.com / Bhiring.com. (Visit jobkeeda.com or bhiring.com for more detail)

A management graduate from Mumbai University, with specialization in Finance, I started my career from Kotak Securities Ltd. After a brief stint at Kotak Securities, I then moved on to research and writing for www.myiris.com and was there more than one and half years, before starting my own venture.
_______________________________________________

""Why I Jumped into Recruitment?""

Starting jobkeeda.com and bhiring.com was not pre-planned. Some of my office colleagues was looking out for job and also my juniors in the college was looking for internship. But unfortunately, they didn't find anything. The reason was not the non-availability of the job, but it was that all the jobs was not there on the jobs portal.

Seeing my friends and other not able to find the jobs and most importantly not able to see the job openings available in my neighborhood, I started Jobkeeda.com in May 2008.

www.jobkeeda.com is a new type of job portal, which aims at showing the hidden jobs in the market. Means, jobkeeda.com shows the jobs which is most of the time not available on any other job portal or with the HR Placement firm. We aims at bringing the jobs of even local Tata Indicom Gallery or a showroom.

Later, in December 2008...I started a b2b recruitment portal, called www.bhiring.com. It aims at reducing the recruitment cost of the corporate and making the hiring more productive. It also offers, recruitment contract to the HR Placement firms. Bhiring.com is turning out to be a websites (which I am hoping) will be a new benchmark in the recruitment world.

Bhiring.com is the WORLD FIRST RECRUITMENT PORTAL which pays for posting job and first such site in India which will help co.s in reducing cost and helping consultsnst in expanding business.

Read more at: http://www.bhiring.blog.co.in
