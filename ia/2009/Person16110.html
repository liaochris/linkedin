An Hilven’s Summary

I am an Information Security engineer with a geeky edge.

Currently building up knowledge and experience in the field of digital forensics. I mainly attempt to achieve this by having graduated as a Masters student in Digital Forensics, followed pretty much all EnCase courses, and lots of selfstudy.

Completed the first phase to certify as an EnCase Certified Examiner (EnCE), and currently working on second phase. For 2009 my plans are to complete the EnCE, and also certify as CISSP, GCFA and/or CHFI.

As a personal interest and drive to learn, I try to bridge the gap between Information Security and Digital Forensics by gaining insights in ethical hacking and incident handling in general.

Experienced with the day-to-day configuration, maintenance, support and management of firewalls, intrusion detection systems and SSL VPN devices for customers with a need for (managed) security services. Further information security experience includes threat analysis based on alerts triggered by intrusion detection systems, and responding either through escalation or mitigation as required.
