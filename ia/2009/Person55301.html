Daria Rippingale’s Summary

Daria is the Marketing Manager of Brisbane based payment processor, ASurePay. Joining the world of eCommerce in 2007 as the Marketing Executive of Intabill, Daria has been developing her expertise in the online payments and prepaid cards industry ever since. Daria has a background in online marketing, working as an Affiliate Manager at online gaming giant CarbonPoker before making the switch to the payments arena.
