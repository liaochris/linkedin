Cody O'Neill’s Summary

I graduated in May of 2008 with a BA in Economics from the University of Arizona in Tucson, Arizona. I attended the University of Arizona on a full academic tuition waiver. During college I was an active member of KAMP Student Radio and the National Society of Collegiate Scholars, where I eventually held positions for both. I was also a Resident Assistant for 3 years which greatly expanded my leadership, planning, and communication skills.

During my summers, I have built on the experience I gained through college with various other jobs. The past four summers I have worked as a Program Coordinator for the Foundation for Teaching Economics. This leadership role has expanded my project planning and communication skills during week long camps teaching economics and leadership to young adults. I also interned for AEG Live - Rocky Mountains, which is the regional branch of one of the top live entertainment promoters in the world. This internship provided me with experience in entertainment marketing, advertising accounting, and contract negotiating. Both of these experiences have provided me with valuable experience to be applied in my future career.
