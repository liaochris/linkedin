Christy Wise’s Summary

With 10-years of experience in the public relations industry, Christy has garnered success in areas of the business ranging from general entertainment and music, to well-known personalities and non-profit organizations. After promoting a variety of clients through traditional media outlets, Christy shifted her focus to the web in early 2005, when she joined Fanscape as the Manager of Online Publicity. After moving up the corporate ranks, Christy now serves as Fanscape's VP of Marketing, a position that allows her to oversee all company digital publicity, online promotions, and social media services.
