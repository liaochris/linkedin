Chad Pollitt’s Summary

Web Marketer with a track record of over ten million dollars ROI. Particularly interested in planning, deploying, implementing and tracking of robust eCommerce and eBusiness solutions to include SEO, SEM and conversion strategies.

In 2009 Chad H. Pollitt earned an Internet Marketing Masters Certification from the University of San Francisco's prestigious School of Business and Management and in 1999 graduated with a BS in Entrepreneurship from the Indiana University Kelley School of Business. Since completing his undergraduate degree he's won multiple awards in sales/marketing and sales management while working and residing in Fort Wayne, IN. Chad is also a Lieutenant in the Indiana Army National Guard and a decorated veteran of Operation Iraqi Freedom.

He started his web career working for a small web development firm in the midwest called Cirrus ABS as a web technology and sales consultant. Since then he's played an integral role in designing, developing, deploying and executing robust web marketing strategies for over 80 client companies and organizations. Chad currently works for Digital Hill Multimedia, Inc. as a Web Technology Consultant and Internet Marketing Manager and has been on The Diane Rehm Show on NPR and featured in The Wall Street Journal.

Chad also consults with more than 30 organizations in matters of Internet Marketing, SEO, Social Media Marketing, Web Design, Conversion Strategy, eBusiness Analysis and Analytics while managing four geotargeted microsites: seofortwayne.com, websitedesignfortwayne.com, websitedesignindianapolis.com, warsawwebdesign.com

Chad is available for Speaking, Training, Agency Partnerships and Consulting on web topics ranging from SEO, Affiliate Marketing, PPC, Unique Value Propositions, Calls to Action, Conversion Testing, Social Media and many many others.
