Aldo Colucciello’s Summary

PhD in: Ethnoanthropology - Literature, Myth and Rite at the University "La Sapienza" of Roma; Title of research: Le feste di Holi e Diwali nella diaspora indiana. Il caso Lisbona tra storia e transnazionalità. (The Holi and Diwali Festivals in the Indian Diaspora. The Lisbon Case between History and Trasnationality)

Research Activities:
1. With Prof. S. M. Pandey (Professor of Hindi Language and Literature at Istituto Universitario Orientale di Napoli) we are editing the dictionary Hindi~Italiano, by Lexicographic Study Center of IsIAO (Istituto Italiano per l'Africa e l'Oriente) of Roma and Istituto Universitario Orientale of Napoli; (current occupation/employment)
2. Specialist in Contemporary and Modern History of India at Istituto Universitario Orientale of Napoli (now Università degli Studi di Napoli "l'Orientale");
3. Specialist in Anthropological Topics at Istituto Universitario Suor Orsola Benincasa of Napoli;
4. Project Leaders for “Laboratorio di Rituali e Pratiche Festive” (Rituals of Feast and Festival), Università degli Studi di Napoli – Suor Orsola Benincasa of Napoli;
5. General member of IsIAO (Istituto Italiano per l'Africa e l'Oriente) of Roma;
6. Collaborator as Anthropologist and Press Agent at Museo delle feste e tradizioni popolari in Casertavecchia;
7. Member of Scientific Commitee of “Rivista storica del Sannio” (Historical Review of Samnium);
8. General member of Gujarat Studies Association;
9. General member of AISEA (Italian Association of Ethno-Anthropological Studies);
10. Coordinator of the activities of the Study Project of “Le immagini e la memoria” (The Images and the Memory) in Rocchetta Sant’Antonio (FG).
11. Temporary Professor at Università degli Studi di Macerata teaching Letteratura e cultura Hindi (Hindi Culture and Litterature).

Foreign Languages: English, Portugese, and Elementary Hindi;
