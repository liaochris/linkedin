Jeff Elofson’s Summary

Real Estate Financing and Sales became my primary focus four years ago with the goal of offering my clients an honest and sane alternative to the Wild, Wild West environment of early 2000's mortgage industry. I have always focused on providing each client with personalized advice and competitive financing programs based on their personal situation to finance their real estate assets.

Sometimes, this means telling them something they do not want to hear. But, Iit always means that they will benefit from honest, accurate and understandable information so they can make an informed, wise financial decision.

As we weather the current economic crisis that can be attributed partially to the overwhelming level of debt that most Americans live under, I have sought to find and offer a solution to my clients to free themselves from this burden.

As a result, I have begun offering them a revolutionary finance management system that helps them pay off their mortgage and other debts in up to a 1/3 of the time. In my opinion, if they take the time to understand the power of this system and decide to use it, they will save hundreds of thousands of dollars in interest payments over the life of your mortgage.

What kind of impact would that make in your life?
