Bill Thomas’s Summary

I am an accomplished business development and IT consulting professional with deep domain knowledge of the Internet and the enterprise IT marketplace. I am a strategic thinking entrepreneur who is adept at quickly translating product features into revenue generating value propositions while also defining the IT centric responsibilities required to deliver the product/service. My domain expertise in both marketing and IT afford me the ability to lead projects cross-functionally.

As a consultant I have helped companies define their critical business challenges; from enterprise architecture, business intelligence and CRM issues to business transformation and product development deficiencies. Extensive experience in both Fortune50 and SMB; Director level, end-to-end technology implementation and development experience. Senior executive ownership of programs representing SBU's in both the front of the store [marketing & sales] as well as the back of the store [IT].

I am a results oriented self-starter who enjoys team building and the creation of top performing brands through the careful listening of client motivations and combining thoses with meaningful, problem solving solutions. I work well in complex, changing environments; I enjoy understanding and resolving business and cross cultural issues. I understand the importance of creating relationships that result in an environment of trust and professional friendship - these values allow everyone to succeed.
