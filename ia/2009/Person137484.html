Laura Dapito’s Summary

Laura Dapito has most recently consulted as a Graphic Designer and Production Director for up-and-coming boutique agencies all over New York City including Paganucci Wolfington and HK Creative, as well as the creative and luxury PR consultancy Gorgeous Group in London. Her principal agency experience was earned as a Graphic Designer at the seminal New York advertising and brand imaging company Laird + Partners. Working with renown Creative Directors, Laura supported the creative teams for Juicy Couture, DeBeers, GAP, Donna Karan International, and Nautica as well as all new business accounts and acquisitions including clients such as Sarah Jessica Parker’s Lovely Fragrance for Coty/Lancaster, Tanqueray No. 10, and Cîroc Vodka.

Laura received her Master of Fine Arts degree from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design in 2007. As an installation and mixed media artist, Laura collaborates with musicians, artists and designers across the US. She returns to the commercial arts with a unique approach to visual communications, creating a truly multi-disciplinary form of practice.


SKILLS: Senior level proficiency in design and production for all forms of print media focusing on specialty publications, print advertising, editorial layout, brochures, mailers, billboards, packaging, new business presentations and branding development. Production for high-volume print, outdoor, and special format advertising. Photo-retouching and color correction. Precise manual/comping skills; box and bookbinding techniques. Master of Fine Art in digital composition and installation.

Laura Dapito’s Specialties:

graphic design for print media, graphic environments and surface graphics; production coordination and direction.
