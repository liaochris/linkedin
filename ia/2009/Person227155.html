Susan Oppelt (Sullivan)’s Summary

Over 16 years of experience as creative visionary. Recent work includes creative strategy and user experience visual design for online, cross-product and cross-platform products, brand identity, SEO, mobile, monitzation, community, ecommerce and involvement with user research studies. Former experience encompassed online and offline advertising, direct, packaging, signage and illustration.
