Russ Jones’s Summary

Publishers Russ and Jackie Jones purchased The Wichita Chronicle from founding editors Lynn and Karen McBride in July 2005. The publication has faithfully served the Christian community in south central Kansas for eleven years. Changing its name to the The Chronicle - the paper is the largest Christian newspaper in Kansas featuring news from a Christian perspective and hosts the most exhaustive Christian events calendar for the area. In October 2008 the paper changed its name to Christian Press.

Christian Press Newspaper, a division of the BIG Picture Media Group, is one of many avenues the company helps people better communicate their message. BIG Picture can also assist with video production, media buying, placement of display ads in other newspapers, newsletters, direct mail campaigns, brochures, web and graphic design and public relation efforts.

I also serve as the current president of the Fellowship of Christian Newspapers.

Russ Jones’s Specialties:

Publish one of the largest Christian newspapers in the US with some 150,000 monthly readers - as well as operate a fulls service advertising, marketing and public relations firm serving clients across the US.
