    Clear Channel Communications (WTKS-FM)

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Broadcast Media industry)

    June 1988 — September 1997 (9 years 4 months)

Prior to that, worked as general assignment reporter and news anchor under various station ownerships including, Gannett Broadcasting, Paxson Broadcasting and Press Broadcasting before the sale of radio properties to Clear Channel in 1997. Received recognition and awards for spot news and features reporting from American Women in Radio & TV, the Associated Press, the Central Florida Press Club and United Press International.
