Jennifer Cloud’s Summary

I am a young professional in St. Charles, MO who has been working in marketing and communications for close to a year since my graduation from Missouri Baptist University in April of 2008.

I currently represent HDT Industries, LLC which includes the sister companies Gateway Restoration Services and Gateway Construction & Design.

Gateway Restoration Services has been providing full-service disaster restoration assistance to the St. Louis, St. Charles and southern Illinois communities since 1996 and can provide or coordinate all services from initial mitigation to full reconstruction!

Gateway Construction & Design is our Green Building initiative, and with a restoration company as our core, we are in a very unique position to offer a suite of eco-conscious products and services to our clients! From our reclaimed materials to our LEED AP's knowlege of and access to emerging "green" technologies, we have the ability to provide a vast suite of services for just about any green home or small commercial aspiration you may have!
