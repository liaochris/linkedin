James F. Keith’s Summary

Mr. Keith is the Chairman and CEO of the Enxit Group, and as such he provides overall strategic and investment direction to the firm. Born into the age of moderation Mr. Keith’s intrigue with the culture of capitalism and the rapid integration of the world is still the primary driver of his entrepreneurial and educational initiatives. Mr. Keith’s formal education is rooted in engineering and mathematics, he has studied Liberal Arts and Finance at Harvard University, and MBA works at Lawrence Technological University, which were all sparked by his foundation in Mechanical Engineering and Physics at Tuskegee University’s CEAPS. Prior to founding the Enxit Group, LLC Mr. Keith held executive roles at ESI Connect, (Pty) Ltd. in operations and finance, engineer consulting roles at Eskom Holding for energy supply and distribution, and project implementation roles at Beijing-Benz and DaimlerChrysler NA. Mr. Keith has led large-scale and start-up capital investment and operations initiatives in Asia, North America, and Africa to build and implement solutions for the increasingly interdependent global economy.
