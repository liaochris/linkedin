Christopher Hoffman’s Summary

A unique approach, effective results:
• Strategic creative ideas — to distill the meaningful truths of a product or brand into stories that drive consumers’ thinking.
• Targeted, engaging copy — to capture the most compelling stories about a product, and the essence of a brand.
• Crisp “global English” copy that can translate cleanly into virtually any language, for any world market — to maintain the clarity of the product message and the spirit of its brand.
• Crafting the message to the medium — for creative that makes the most of interactive, web, collateral, print or POS.
• Deep product knowledge — to elevate the most inspiring and unique features, benefits and stories to the most effective level for each marketing medium.
• A combination of creative and engineering backgrounds — allowing the communication of highly technical products in clear, conversational and emotionally captivating language, whether to consumers, clients or internal colleagues.

Christopher Hoffman’s Specialties:

People do still read. (Look at you!) Great words make people notice and appreciate your products. I translate technology into conversation, show what makes your brand different, and make it matter. I've done it for the world's finest cars, and for some that are far from that. Without lying.
