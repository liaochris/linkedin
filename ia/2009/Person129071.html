Keith Pape’s Summary

Senior social media and digital marketing executive with 13+ years of experience driving business growth, leading teams, launching brands and products, and achieving results through creative-, results-driven and technology-enabled marketing.

Bio:
During the first dot com boom, Keith was CTO at Mendelson Associates, a west coast web and technology consultancy specializing in early stage web development projects, in the Los Angeles and Bay Area markets.

In 2001, with several partners, Keith co-founded Virtual-Image a ColdFusion development house that worked with ABC Television, Disney, Boeing and early stage VC startups that survived the first Bubble, to create many of the first Rich Internet Applications (RIA’s) and was a key Macromedia partner in those early days working to build rich front end experiences with robust back-end systems that would make the Internet truly interactive.

In 2005, wanting to bring even stronger marketing and creative assets to the Rich Internet Applications that he had become known for, Keith joined forces with Big Man Creative, a leading Southern California Design shop, as Vice President. This combination of Big Man’s award winning creative executions and Keith’s experience in Flash and RIA’s, won Big Man Creative, renamed FrontGate Creative, numerous awards and accolades for their work with brands such as Del Taco, IHOP, and Sony. This expertise brought in business supporting larger agencies such as Saatchi & Saatchi, Universal McCann and tier 1 interactive shop FirstBorn Media.

In 2007 FrontGate Creative merged with White Barn Group, an award winning Restaurant Marketing and Advertising firm. The new White Barn Group combined their traditional power in Branding and Advertising and now added Keith’s digital/. leadership as Partner of Interactive strategy, creative and development; all within an Agency footprint that allowed flexible execution and their ‘scrappy’ attitude that won over brands.
