Alec Clews’s Summary

Alec has over twenty five years in software: managing development and implementation projects; consulting; design work and programming. Alec is unusual because he can manage wider project issues whilst still remaining very technical.

Alec has been part of the Open Source Community since 1993 and using the Internet since 1992. His experience spans embedded microprocessor systems to z/OS mainframes and includes significant Windows and Linux/UNIX knowledge.
