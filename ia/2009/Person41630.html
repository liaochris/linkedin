R.W. (Charlie) Turner’s Summary

Business Development, Licensing, and Finance professional within the biopharmaceutical industry. Over twenty years of industry experience ranging from global pharmaceutical companies to small specialty pharma development, venture capital and consulting. Two years of Big Four public accounting experience. MBA, CPA, Certified Licensing Professional (CLP).

Currently providing services as an independent consultant.

R.W. (Charlie) Turner’s Specialties:

Development of biopharmaceutical licensing/collaboration deal structures, analysis and valuation of deal terms, leading of cross-functional teams, coordination of due diligence and negotiation of agreements. Acquisitions, divestitures, product development collaborations, supply agreements, commercialization agreements, in-licensing, out-licensing. Financial management, planning and analysis. Controller. Strategic business planning.
