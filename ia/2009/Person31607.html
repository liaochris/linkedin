Brad Kenney’s Summary

I'm a multi-functional project manager and multiple award-winning analyst, writer/editor, and business consultant. My background in anthropology, technology and business gives me a critical understanding of the drivers of collaboration and consensus, and how to best use technology to effect bottom-up organizational change management strategies that stick.

I've got 15 years of experience researching and developing thought leadership and straight reportage on trends, people and issues in business and information technology. I've covered everything from the biggest tech tradeshows (SAPPHIRE, Oracle OpenWorld) to the newest startup on the tech block.

My editorial and consulting experience covers all manner of project, content and knowledge management.

I'm also an owner/operator/small businessperson, with 12 years of client service in online marketing and digital productivity coaching under my belt.
