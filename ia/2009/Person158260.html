Michaël Defroyennes’s Summary

Since 15 years working for video-game industry I had the opportunity to work on a wide range of disciplines ( 2D sprite-based games, concept design, 3D modeling and texturing for environments, objects and characters, keyframe and motion capture animation,...) for some really exiting projects, as Outcast, Outcast 2 or Totems.

A few years ago I decided to use this wide range of experiences for coaching and managing artist teams as well as teaching to students. I really enjoy to work on both sides such asset management and art direction : trying to reach the best visual result as possible, dealing with asset lists, plannings and deadlines.

Michaël Defroyennes’s Specialties:

- Managing an art department team is my main interest.
- I'm particulary interested by 3D environments : not only assets, but also level design that is for me one of the most creative process.
- I really like to work on real-time characters/animation.
