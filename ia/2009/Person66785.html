Dan Pagano’s Summary

Leadership in Engineering Turnaround: As a result of working with the best hardware, software, RF, and mechanical engineers in the world at Motorola and my instinct to continuously improve, I have cultivated practices to drive cycle time and costs to a minimum, sustain schedules, and consistently deliver quality products and services. My management leadership success up through Director of Engineering was the result of excellence in recruiting the best talent, sustaining high standards of competence, retention skill, an incremental yet aggressive approach to discipline and process improvement, and drive for continuous innovation.

Proven Record in Operational Discipline: Leveraging 12 years in engineering development discipline, a law degree, and entrepreneurship, I have successfully brought diverse cross functional groups, especially engineering and sales, together through collaboration, tools, and practices to efficiently develop multi-million dollar proposals. I continued this success by finding a key niche in bridging the gulf between the quality and productivity needs of operational stakeholders and the IT departments that serve them.

Entrepreneurial Experience: Over a 3 year period during my spare time I utilized the experience gained in Fortune 500 corporations and my law degree to develop a business plan for a billion dollar pro forma consumer electronics product/service Company and spent over a year pitching to investors. The experience enabled me to measure all my decision through the eyes of shareholder value, model the financial and operation modes for a complex business of products and integrated services, competitive strategy development, and positioning innovative ideas.

Unique Attributes: Transparent, honest, root cause problem solver, fast and aggressive recruiter, staff and process centered manager, analytical business planner.
