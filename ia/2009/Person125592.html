K. Aleisha Fetters’s Summary

Cheeky, personal, and always honest, I have a voice that's all my own. It's my trademark. But so is my diligence, detail-oriented nature, and eagerness to take on new challenges.

Having trained and worked both in traditional and new media, I'm turning our current journalistic challenges into opportunities for growth and creativity.

Writing both domestically and abroad, I love to explore the world through new media capabilities and traditional media news values. Journalism is all about going where you are needed, and I apply that principle not only to the content of my assignments, but also to my work within those assignments.

Whether it's working alone or in teams, I mold to best fill the needs of my assignment. I go where I am needed. It's my job.
