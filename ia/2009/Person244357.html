Vivek Agarwal’s Summary

Vivek is a senior sales and marketing professional with extensive experience in IT products, solutions and services sales through personal network, direct sales team and alternate channels. He has been working with Saksoft group since its inception in 2000 and is currently responsible for sales & marketing in Europe and Middle East region for parent company as well as its subsidiary Acuma Solutions UK. Prior to this, he was the head of business in Asia Pacific region based out of Singapore, where he successfully established Saksoft as a focused business solutions provider in banking and financial services industry from scratch. Vivek has successfully built and managed large global customer relationships at CxO level and has significant experience in selling business solutions as a trusted advisor. He has conceptualized and launched new solution offerings in credit card and retail banking industry and has built strategic partnerships to reach a wider customer base for the same. He has successfully developed and led high performance sales teams to deliver revenue and profitability growth objectives. Vivek has been the face of company in his regions and has represented Saksoft in several public events as a speaker and panel member to articulate its offerings with contextual relevance.

Before starting a career in IT industry, Vivek has worked in FMCG industry for 6 years in sales & marketing as well as operations and has a proven track record of delivering strategic business objectives through entrepreneurship and leadership. Vivek is an alumnus of NITIE Mumbai and has been actively involved in Alumni activities in Singapore.

Vivek also has a passion for Indian Tribal and Folk art and has founded ‘Vivami’ with his wife Amita to increase public awareness and provide a global platform to these artists.

Vivek Agarwal’s Specialties:

Business Development, Sales and Marketing, Key Account Management, Building and leading sales teams, sales channels development, large network of banking contacts, strategic information management, performance management solution development.
