Mark Hibbs mark.hibbs@reedglobal.com’s Summary

Experienced International recruitment and executive search professional posessing years of experience working in the staffing and recruiting industry. Experienced at both contingent and retained level recruitment for clients both in the UK and Middle East region.

I have recently joined Reed's specialist recruitment team here in Qatar to assist in further developing the Information Communication and Technology (ICT) desk.

Founded in 1960, reed is a leading global recruitment business with a proven track record of delivering human capital solutions across Europe, Asia Pacific and now the Middle East. Reed employs over 4,000 people in 400 offices world-wide who operate in over 24 vertical markets. This global network gives access to specialist consultants, each with superior knowledge of specific professions and industries.

Reed established their office here in Doha, Qatar in 2007 and are licensed by the Qatar financial centre (QFC).

Utilizing my experience, innovative approach and the fact I am totally committed to my clients, I aim to source and attract the best people worldwide to work for them.

I have experience of recruiting into small start-ups through to global blue-chip organizations, undertaking assignments at the technical level right through to CxO / boardroom level.

As an ICT vertical market specialist I am able to assist clients by recruiting skilled technical individuals into their organization and leverage my experience recruiting for very senior positions to recruit CTO / CIO professionals.
