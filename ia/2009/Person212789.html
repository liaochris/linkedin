Sean Robinson’s Summary

Sean Robinson is currently a PRoject Manager at TRI Leadership Resources. Originally from Iowa, Sean currently makes his home in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains in Boulder, Colorado.

Sean holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the Leeds School of Business at the University of Colorado at Boulder. During his time at CU-Boulder Sean capitalized on his experience as a scholar in the Presidents Leadership Class and his international travels to discover a passion for global business and leadership development that has taken him to Asia, Europe, and Australia.

At TeamTRI Sean utilizes his skills in a variety of roles to capitalize on the broad impact of TRI. Whether conducting a leadership training, coordinating an event production, or coaching student officers, Sean continually works to ensure the client’s mission is carried out and that their goals are not only reached, but surpassed.

Sean helps bring vision to life as an event logistics specialist for nonprofits and government agencies and initiatives, including DECA, FCCLA, FBLA, HOSA, Helping America’s Youth initiative, White House Office of the First Lady, and the White House Summit on Malaria. Sean ensures these organizations are able to deliver their message in a coordinated, seamless and inspiring manner.

Sean has made it a priority to help develop future leaders and works with state and national officers of career and technical student organizations as a leadership coach and trainer. He coaches students in program development and action plans to ensure officer teams end their term in office encouraged by the work they have accomplished and impact they have made.

Inspired by the mighty Rocky Mountains, Sean lives with purpose believing that through active excellence, service, and teamwork every individual can climb to the summit of success.
