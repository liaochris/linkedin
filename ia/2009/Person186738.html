Phil Baughn’s Summary

Proficient in the employment of technology as both a business and marketing tool to the front line and to the customer, and as a product and service offering itself. An expert in technology direction, implementation and oversight; strategic business, product and service direction and improvement; risk and cost reduction; and P&L improvement.

A hands-on leader who creates loyalty, trust and following. One who energizes people and teams, and causes cross-functional cooperation to occur. A strong believer in entire "process-based" thinking and action as opposed to functional or "silo-like" operations. A self-starter and a proactive, entrepreneurial-oriented executive who leads by example and is effective working at all levels within a company.

Expert in the areas of turnaround, ramp-up, change leadership and problem correction. An excellent talent for identifying and resolving complex issues, quickly generating new growth, and putting into place the programs, policies, procedures, and mechanisms to remain on a track of continuous improvement.
