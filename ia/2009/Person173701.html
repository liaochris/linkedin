Niall Kennedy’s Summary

I build product development teams focused on exceptional web performance. I focus on front-end development, syndication, and widget technologies to optimize browser and search performance. I syndicate web properties beyond a single website and into smartphones, social networks, desktop, and enterprise dashboard environments.

I produce Widget Summit, an annual widget conference in San Francisco bringing together top widget publishers and platforms for two days of intensive learning. I built StartupSearch.org, a web technology startup directory tracking key data on startups in the United States using aggregated public data sources.

I have worked in the world of search for over 9 years with a work history spanning blog search, product search, institutional investments, and small business services. I leverage cutting edge technology to a business advantage.

I am a feed syndication and widget expert, delivering large-scale solutions to consulting clients since 2006.

I develop Python applications using the Django and YUI frameworks on Google App Engine.
