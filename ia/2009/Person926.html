Aamir Mirza’s Summary

Real Estate Broker, representing a real estate investment fund other high net worth investors & groups and individuals that buy residential & commercial REO's & short sales in CA.

We also represent buyers and sellers in retail real estate sales. With over 17 years of real estate lending and operations we have a streamlined system for the short-sale process based on mortgage industry contacts and understanding of the legal technicalities, the securitization process of MBS and the back end investor relationships.

As a licensed real estate broker who has quickly built his business after transitioning from the lending side, I personally consult with every homeowner / co-op realtor, investor, & client, helping them with strategies and plans to achieve their individual objectives.

My team aims to build long-term relationships with all our listing agents, our clients, our sellers and our investors.

We offer clients and our co-op agents personalized support.

We will help the seller by explaining the short-sale process clearly and give them a sense of security and understanding of the professionalism their transaction will be handled with, before they sign any listing agreement.

We help homeowners with FREE down payment assistance, (FREE) Foreclosure defense guidance and (FREE) forensic loan audits to try and save their homes. We leave no stone un-turned, to help our clients and agent, achieve their goals.
