Eric Strom’s Summary

Operations/program management and leadership professional. Customer-focused problem solver. Results-oriented, willing to make decisions with limited information and time. Comfortable advising enterprise leaders or interacting with functional principals. People-centered leadership style.

Keen insights on planning, requirements and business process analysis, concept development and evaluation, decision support, and business intelligence. Trained and skilled in using scenario planning and intelligence as operations multipliers. Technically and mechanically knowledgeable, quickly grasp systems and engineering issues. Effective and valuable liaison between the operations and information technology (IT) arenas.

-- Running operations (centralized, dispersed, 24x7) and programs
-- Planning, analyzing requirements and processes, developing and evaluating concepts, redesigning organization structures
-- Administering budgets, reporting financial results; accounting for assets and inventory; auditing
-- Aligning IT plans, resources and services to enterprise strategy and operations
-- IT Governance and Best Practices (CMMI, COBIT, ITIL).
