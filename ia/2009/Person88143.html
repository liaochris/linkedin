Di Xue’s Summary

Di Xue is currently a part of the core development team for IBM’s DB2 zOS (mainframe) software. He plays a vital role in providing solutions and services to the Fortune 500 customers who depend on IBM’s mainframe and database to run their mission critical banking and business applications. Prior to IBM, Mr.Xue worked for the Condor High-Throughput Computing at University of Wisconsin at Madison. Condor Grid Computing is currently applied at NASA Advanced Supercomputing Facility with 350 servers in the computing grid.

Outside of his job, Di Xue is an entrepreneur at heart. He quickly grasped the opportunities in the web domain industry, and became an expert in the idea of direct-navigation and monetizing domains. Even with 40 million domains names already exhausted, Xue still came up with an innovative solution that enabled him to cultivate valuable domain names when others could not. This business idea has earned him a third place in SCEA business competition in 2007.

Additionally Mr.Xue is an avid blogger about investments on his Blurtsout.com. When he is not doing any of the above, he spends time thinking of new creative business ideas that can make an positive imapct. As of now, Di Xue holds a B.S and MS in computer science from University of Wisconsin at Madison.
