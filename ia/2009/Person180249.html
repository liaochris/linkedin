Oscar Padilla’s Summary

A proven executive with 14 years experience in strategy, business development and marketing of interactive services. At Vertis Communications, Oscar is responsible for directing and executing the company’s new media strategy.

Before joining Vertis, Oscar served as Managing Director at Mindshare Interactive Campaigns where he led strategy and technology teams to plan, and execute integrated online programs for marquee clients. Prior to Mindshare, he worked with web-based solution provider Bridgeline Software implementing enterprise web systems in support of clients’ communications and business needs.

As part of the start-up team at Bixler Inc Oscar provided leadership in overall business development leading the company to a 700% growth over a five-year period. Oscar led e-business consulting engagements for Hitachi as well as an independent consultant.

Throughout his career, Oscar has led the development and execution of nearly 100 major client engagements including work for clients such as Verizon, Southwest Airlines, Oprah’s Angel Network, and the Bond Market Association to name just a few.

Oscar earned a BS Degree in Business Administration from the University of Maryland and is a Native of Caracas, Venezuela.
