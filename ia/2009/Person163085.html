Michelle McCormack’s Summary

I have worked as a Waiter, Bartender, Club Promoter, Photographer, Stylist, Model Maker, Model, Hair and Make-up Artist, Web Designer, Graphic Designer, Art Director, Interactive Producer and Company Director.

I have lived in five cities (four countries), as follows: Boston, New York, Paris, London and Florence

And I have both American and European citizenship.


I owned a company in London.


I have lost all of my belongings three times. First time in NYC when sadly Manhattan Mini-storage burned down, then enroute from London to Milan, and finally in Paris where you should keep your bags close.

During and after (leaving) college I traveled around the world as a Photographers Assistant and Photographer. I worked for all of the best magazines in Fashion, Interiors and Travel. I was scouted to be in a Levis commercial and I did not accept screening and instead I went on Safari in South Africa for three weeks shooting for Town and Country. I was a Black and White Printer for Annie Liebovitz. Weeks of street shooting in the South of France for Town and Country, weeks of shooting in Hawaii for Wallpaper, and countless other trips and experiences have taught me about design, fine things and hardwork.

I've been Stylist on music videos, TV commercials and print.

Early aughts I became a Web Designer, then a Project Manager, then Company Director, then Interactive Producer and now I have my own company, Cloud Out Loud, as a consultant for all things creative, especially, at the moment, Social Media.
