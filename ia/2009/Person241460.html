Vanessa M. Ramos’s Summary

Over the past four years I have been employed at the accounting firm of R & V Income Tax Service Inc., as a staff accountant and income tax practitioner. I reported directly to the CEO of the business. My responsibilities included maintaining efficiency of the financial operation of the organization, preparing the budget, monthly bank reconciliations, journal entry adjustments, and closing of the books. Additionally, my duties included preparing monthly, quarterly, and annual reports forecasting and summarizing the business activities and financial position of areas of income, expenses, and earnings based on past, present, and expected operations.

Furthermore, I prepared tax returns and conducted tax research for individual and corporate returns including: corporation's, partnerships, and limited liability companies. Additionally, I was responsible for the coordination, preparation and reporting of: quarterly and annual payroll, and state sales use and withholding taxes. Thus requiring communication with the IRS and New York State Department of Finance tax professionals, and accessing these authorities’ websites in order to download current and prior year tax forms.

I am a resourceful, diligent, reliable, and organized individual who possesses a strong attention to detail, sense of urgency, and knows how to overcome obstacles to complete any task at hand. The most quintessential working environment for me to both thrive, and achieve my career goals is one that offers both a team atmosphere, and advancement of the organization.
