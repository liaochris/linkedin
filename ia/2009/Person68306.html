Pascal Duriez’s Summary

I think I have been lucky enough to encounter several opportunities in my life that have turned out to be truly rewarding. I started my career with the leading BDDP a top creative Agency in France now part of TBWA worldwide network. Entrusted with the golden opportunity to work on a major retail account, McDonald’s was the leap start for me to be motivated to continue in this stream of business.

I have 20 years of expertise working for leading Advertising Networks of the likes of BDDP, EURO RSCG, Bates and Ogilvy & Mather having scaled the career ladder in quick succession heading responsibilities across countries. Currently as the Managing Director of Ogilvy Action, I spearhead operations and drive business growth for Shanghai.

So my core expertise is piloting creative ideas, value-adding to bottom-line and top-line growth, fostering strong client relationships and bringing cross-cultural teams together to achieve common goals
