Michael Dote’s Summary

I'm currently a published freelance photographer with a good eye, a good camera, good lights and big ambitions. I've been freelancing for about a year now, and in that time, I've shot everything from sports, to food, to interiors, to formal portraiture.

I'm a hard worker, and will do as much in a day as is humanly possible. I also stand by my work- I am more than willing to reshoot if things are not according to standards. My business emphasizes customer service- because I highly value the patronage of each and every one of my clients.

My goal in the short term is to build my client base, profiting by the volume of work I produce at a reasonable cost instead of wringing my clients dry by overcharging for my services.

In the middle term, I'd like my work to grace the cover of a magazine, be featured in a gallery exhibition, and appear on a billboard.

My long term goal is to craft a business that generates profit while maintaining a very high standard of work. To that end, I would like to have a stable of exceptional photographers working under me, as well as retouchers, interns, and a slew of assistants.
