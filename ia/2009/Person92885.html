Tamara Haskins’s Summary

With over 20 years of combined experience in the accounting and financial services industries. Tamara has been entrusted by her clients to build, manage and preserve their wealth and financial well being. Prior to establishing the firm, Tamara worked for Merrill Lynch, for 10 years as a Certified Financial Manager, Tax and Financial Advisor. She appeared on WABC Channel 7 “Money Talk”, “Good Morning America” and FOX Channel 5 “Good Day NY” discussing tax planning strategies for high net worth clients.

I specialize in working with individuals who find themselves in a transition - whether professional (career changes, impacted by lay-offs, retirement), or personal (marriage, children, divorce, empty-nesters). Together we turn the transition into an opportunity for financial and personal success.

Tamara practiced as a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) for 10 years at two prestigious CPA firms; Ernst and Young in New York City and Amper, Politziner & Mattia in New Jersey. She has extensive experience serving closely held family-owned businesses and advising high net worth individuals and corporations on tax strategies and financial planning.

Tamara graduated from Rutgers College, The State University of New Jersey, with a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting. And also has her Master of Science in Taxation from Baruch College, The City University of New York. She has her Series 7, 63 and 65, and Life and Health Insurance Licenses and conducts business in various states.

Tamara and husband Dwayne are the proud parents of son, Dwayne Jr., and daughter Tamia. Their children are the center of their lives. “Haskins Wealth Management Group's desire is to leave a legacy of financial independence for our clients and children to enjoy.”
