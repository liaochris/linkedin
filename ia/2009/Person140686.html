Liane Jamason’s Summary

I service all areas of Tampa Bay including St. Petersburg, Clearwater, South Tampa, New Tampa, Ybor City, Tampa, Brandon, Riverview, Gibsonton, Lithia, Apollo Beach and more.

Contact me if you would like to buy or sell a home for personalized service. I can help you find your dream home in less time! I also specialize in new home construction.

Please visit TampaRealEstateFinder.com to search Tampa Bay Homes, find free mortgage calculators, how much house you can afford, and more! You can also search my website for a free list of Tampa Short Sales and Foreclosures!

I have a background in technology and marketing. I use the latest technology and my widespread internet presence to create a marketing plan for each home I list. The key to finding a good realtor is finding one who will market and advertise your home to the fullest extent - which will help to get it sold faster and bring in more offers overall. You can be sure that I will market your home aggressively online and offline to produce the best results for you.
