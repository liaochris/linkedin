Ivelin Brachev’s Summary

My mission is to take part in the process of Bulgaria’s integration in the EC by
1) assisting the small and middle sized Bulgarian companies to find reliable partners within the European Community and to sell their products in the community member-countries and by
2) assisting the foreign companies that are looking for reliable Bulgarian partners and/or have plans to invest in Bulgaria. I am offering two basic types of co-operation – different forms of foreign trade representation and a wide range of consulting services
