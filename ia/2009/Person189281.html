Peter Perfetti’s Summary

Peter Perfetti has been working in information security for more than twelve years. For seven years he was involved in IT Security for the financial services industry where he was responsible for vulnerability and threat management; and security engineering and architecture. He has also worked for Viacom and MTV as the Manager of Systems Administration. He has a broad range of experience in both operations and security. Previously, Peter provided governance and guidance over risk and compliance issues for the Americas region of ABN AMRO as Local Security Officer. His responsibilities were primarily to manage the risk for infrastructure related technology and operations. Other duties included audit, business continuity, investigations, and security operations oversight. Most recently, he was the Director of IT Risk Management for the National Basketball Association. He was the VP of the NY/NJ Metro Chapter of OWASP and is currently a board member of the local chapter. Peter is also on the IT Security Advisory Board for the Technology Manager’s Forum.

His accomplishments have been documented in two books on achieving high performing, stable, and secure infrastructures. Peter is credited as a technical contributor, and wrote a testimonial for the book “The Visible Ops Security Handbook - Achieving Common Security and IT Operations Objectives in 4 Practical Steps”. In addition to providing technical contributions, at the request of the authors, he wrote chapter summaries for each of the four sections of the book. Regarding Operations, Peter has extensive background in Systems Administration, Enterprise Management, and Security Architecture and Engineering. His effective and efficient style of IT Operational Management has been used as a case study on high performing IT Infrastructures for the book "The Visible Ops Handbook - Starting ITIL in 4 Practical Steps".
