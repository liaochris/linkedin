Adam Hendry’s Summary

Adam found that very few financial consultants understand the liabilities side of a Sponsor/Company, and likewise very few "mortgage brokers" understand the asset management side. Working with a consultant like Tzadik Consulting on a relationship basis instead of a transactional basis will lead to better business. By working with someone who understands a client's balance sheet management, the members of Tzadik Consulting create an experience that the majority of investors and developers have never found.

Being a student of wall street, Benjamin Graham, Warren Buffet, Sir John Templeton, and many other legendary investors, Adam is always seeking to learn from those that came before him. As many market pundits influence a viewer to get excited at every new market ripple, Adam has found that taking a step back and looking at the macro picture has always allowed, and will continue to allow, larger (and arguable better) scale decisions to be made that many would fail to see.

Adam really believes it takes more than just experience to be successful. At Tzadik Consulting he develops trusted relationships with the clients so they can navigate the twists and turns of an assignment in unison. If all the t’s are crossed and the i’s are dotted, which is very important in and of itself, then he thinks that there must be good chemistry whenever an investor or lender is approached. The Tzadik Consulting website and Blogs are to acquaint clients and prospective clients with the company market mindset, so they can hopefully align everyones interests and see if there is a good business relationship. Check us out!

At Tzadik Consuting we always like to say "Creating solutions for projects you never thought possible, in ways you never imagined."
