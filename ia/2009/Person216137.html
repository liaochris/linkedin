My Mission
My childhood dream had at a certain point become a reality and now what has grown from it is the urge to always be at the centre of the ‘web’, to be a true LEADER and hostess. To ORGANIZE, ARRANGE and monitor PROJECTS from A to Z, to break patterns, MOTIVATE and COACH others. To be a true PROJECT MANAGER.

My Passion
Books on PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT, psychology, mental and physical health are very important. I also enjoy traveling and DISCOVERING the world. The experience I gain through meeting people from other cultures, discovering their habits and being confronted by their way of living is priceless.

My Past
In my professional experience I have trained and tutored other managers in their role as leaders. I also have extensive experience in starting and MANAGING PROJECTS. I find it extremely intriguing and pleasant to COOPERATE with people, not only on a professional level, but also on a personal level. Over the years, I have stimulated many people to reach their goals, mainly through my PERSONAL APPROACH of management. With a sense of humor and an objective point of view I am capable of breaking through patterns and MOTIVATING PEOPLE.

* I am entrepreneurial & flexible
* In my mind I always think “when can we start?”
* I like to explain, to describe, to host, to speak in public
* I love to peer over the horizon. The future fascinates me
* I can sense the emotions of those around me
I believe that the above points make me a good LEADER and a true PROJECT MANAGER

My Future
I would like to use my enthusiasm, power, knowledge, experience, skills and integrity to move forward as a MANAGER. My goal is to, as a PROJECT MANAGER, use my energy and drive to help companies realize their goals. I have professional experience in various sectors and my goal is to employ my skills across a broad range of industries.
