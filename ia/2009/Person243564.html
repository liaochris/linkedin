Vincent LARREGLE’s Summary

Born and raised in France, I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science before achieving a Master’s degree from the University of Miami in Florida, where I took two years worth of Spanish coursework while living with a Cuban family. After graduation, I moved to the Midwest where I worked several years for software houses and became an expert in my technical skills as a programmer. I first became acquainted with the manufacturing world working for a large automotive tier-1 OEM supplier as a Systems Analyst to implement a new ERP at a new Aftermarket subsidiary in Italy. I explored further manufacturing concepts while working for SYMIX to develop a complex interface between the SyteLine ERP and an Advanced Planning and Scheduling tool. In addition to this hands-on experience which gave me the big picture about what an ERP is supposed to do in a manufacturing environment, I also achieved CPIM certification in 2000. In France, I have been working as a consultant in the software service industry, with various experiences in run and/or build operations; I am now managing projects in various IT contexts.
