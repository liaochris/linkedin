Damon Duncan’s Summary

I am currently a 3L student at Elon University School of Law. I will graduate in May and take the North Carolina Bar in late July. Upon passing the bar I will join my father and wife at Duncan Law in Charlotte, North Carolina or Greensboro, North Carolina.

I hope that Duncan Law will be a law firm that strives to give back to the community in which it is located. Service and community involvement will be at the core of our business. This, we hope, will allow us to be active members in the community while also operating a profitable company.
