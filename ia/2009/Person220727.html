Parminder Singh (Supply Chain Professional)’s Summary

Parminder Singh brings more than ten years of total industry experience and four plus years of enriching consulting experience to his role as Regional Practice Manager - North & East India at Wipro Consulting Services

His consulting experience includes strategizing and identifying the most suitable cost reduction opportunities for clients viz. e-sourcing, value engineering, process improvements, strategic sourcing, TCO, etc and subsequently implementing the same as per clients business requirements

He has also worked in the areas of supply chain process consulting, process improvements, etc and consulted various large clients
