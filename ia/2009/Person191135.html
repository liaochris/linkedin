Peter Waugh’s Summary

Facilitator of business growth solutions. Areas of primary focus include strategic and business planning, financial optimization, organizational effectiveness, and turnaround/interim management.

My role as a management consultant is supported by 25 years business experience, including 23 years in the financial services industry with RBC Royal Bank and Commercial Capital Corporation. I have also had direct involvement with businesses in a variety of industries including plastics, transportation logistics, precision machining, and financial/data security.

Passionate about helping entrepreneurs to succeed in growing businesses with sound management practices and a focused strategic plan. Through EnGrowth Business Solutions, we actively seek out attractive high growth businesses requiring assistance to maximize on their potential. In addition to arranging the required financial resources, I also play an active role assisting management with strategic planning and enhancing financial performance.
