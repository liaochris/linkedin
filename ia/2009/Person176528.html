Noam Kostucki’s Summary

I became passionate about coaching as I started to deliver Judo courses at the age of 15.   

I started my leadership career at the same age with exciting projects leading 11 teams of up to 50 people. It is thanks to an overwhelming number of projects being successful beyond expectations that I was given more and more important leadership roles.

  I am thrilled to have trained and coached for AIESEC (the world’s largest student organization) in the UK, USA, India, Turkey, Malta, Slovakia, Poland, Lithuania, Latvia and Georgia.

I qualified for a merit scholarship at RealAcad (Venture Management Program) at the end of which we had started an events management company in Singapore.

  One of the most challenging time of my life was running my own corporate training and executive coaching for 2 years in Poland. My clients mostly consisted in English speaking middle managers of multinational corporations and senior managers of Polish banks, insurance and IT companies. Training topics covered interpersonal skills, building relations, leadership and management.   

Within the academia, I am proud to have given training and speeches at Yale University (USA), London School of Economics (UK), 3 Indian and 6 Polish universities.

I now give training and coaching for charities at the Directory of Social Change,

...and most importantly we are developing the concept of Seeducation, the education revolution. Get in touch for more information!
