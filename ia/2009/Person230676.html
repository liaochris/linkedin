Terri S. Alexander’s Summary

Over twenty years of professional experience in curriculum design and development primarily for call center and customer service support environments across a variety of industries.

A unique blend of technical writing and instructional design experience; within the area of instructional design, breadth and depth of experience across all media and deliverable-types including instructor-led training, job aids, self-study workbook, and e-Learning.

Terri S. Alexander’s Specialties:

Instructional Design: Converting instructor-led / paper-based learning material into engaging, interactive, and more cost-effective Web-based training

Technical Writing: Policies & procedures documentation; System release notes, On-line Help documentation

Academia: Thesis & dissertation word processing and editing
