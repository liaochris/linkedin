Robert Clarke’s Summary

Robert has over twenty years of leadership experience and over sixteen years serving as the Director of XONITEK's Consulting Services Group. Robert also serves as one of the key strategic leaders and decision makers within XONITEK’s overall organization.

Robert's leadership experience began while serving as a Platoon Leader with the US Army. From there Robert carried other various roles, each complementing the previous, such as Executive Officer, Company Commander, Battalion Logistics Officer, and Battalion Operations Officer. Robert utilizes this invaluable leadership experience to build his group into a synergetic fighting force.

Robert is responsible for the complete command and control of the overall deployment and optimization of resources in support of solution implementation. In addition, Robert provides directional input on the competitiveness of XONITEK’s technologies and methodologies by researching, developing, and integrating the latest toolset and trends into the daily operations of XONITEK.

Robert has successfully spearheaded the latest XONITEK ERP offering with SAP USA by developing the business plan, and executing the strategy for deployment.

Currently Robert is working on developing XONITEK's Lean & Six Sigma business plan as it pertains to Operational Excellence, Turn Around Management, Operational Due Diligence and Reverse Operational Due Diligence.

Robert has performed various presentations on leadership methodologies; utilizing current technologies to improve business process management, product velocity, as well as quality control measures

Robert received his Bachelor of Computer Science degree from the University of Scranton. As an Officer in the US Military, Robert has attended various schools, most notably the Army Airborne School, Infantry Basic Course, Infantry Advance Course, Infantry Mortar Platoon Course, Combined Arms and Services Staff School, and the Army War College.
