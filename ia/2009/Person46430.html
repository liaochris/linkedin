Demetria Graves’s Summary

Who Am I:

I assist individuals who wish to amicably dissolve their marriages, which often includes the following: custody and visitation, child support, spousal support, property division. My firm also handles guardianship cases and dissolution of domestic partnerships and dissolution of domestic partnerships (and depending how the votes go in November dissolution of same sex marriages). Please take a look at my website for more information: www.attorneygraves.com.

At the Law Offices of Demetria L. Graves, we pay close attention to our clients' needs and concerns. We work to achieve outcomes that enable our clients to regroup and reorganize their lives, and ensure that their rights and interests are protected.

The Website:

I currently launched a new website, www.attorneygraves.com, that has several interesting new features. I have five "Podcast" or "free" informational series about several interesting topics. There is also very useful information about the divorce process, TV Clippings in which I gave my "expert" opinion regarding a celebrity case..... So if you are someone you know is contemplating divorce, lead them to the cite! Very Useful!

How Am I Different:

I am an exclusive family law firm in Glendale, Ca (also serving Pasadena, Los Angeles and surrounding areas) and I also limit the practice to a specific number of cases to effectively serve my clients. Because I am dedicated to creating a positive experience with each one of my clients, I offer a "Five Point Guarantee."
