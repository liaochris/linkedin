Cathal McHale’s Summary

I am a Software Developer working in the I.T. industry since 2003. I have worked in a number of different markets within the software domain, namely retail, telecommunications, e-commerce, healthcare, and cash acceptance. I am an extremely motivated individual with a genuine interest in my profession. I have a real sense of excitement about where the technology industry and I are headed.
