Jeff Vinson’s Summary

Experienced and successful Senior Manager for Business Development and Partner Alliances using Hardware and Software solutions for engaging digital asset sharing opportunities for the entertainment and information consumer of the future.

Unique understanding of both the business and technical requirements for successful new product introduction. Programs include;
• Nokia Connected Living Applications using DLNA
• Reference ATSC/IPTV STB adopted by Sanmina SCI and Wistron
• SlingMedia remote access STB (SlingCatcher)
• VOD STB for Netflix and Amazon
• Alcatel OMP Product upgrade
• OpenTV Email for television (MCA, South Africa)
• DLNA Marketing Committee

Working with MSO, CE and ISP partners in the Connected Living Network Eco-System to create and develop new revenue streams and compelling use cases for a true Connected Living experience.
• Lower Churn (subscriber retention)
• Increase ARPU (avg. revenue per unit)
• Reduce Calls for Service (call center and truck rolls)

Digital TV Engineering Project and Program Business Development positions at Nokia, NXP Semiconductors, Alcatel USA and Open TV. Awarded OpenTV Inventor Award. Involved in Network Digital TV application deployments since 1999 in the US, Canada, Mexico, UK, Chile, South Africa, and Trinidad & Tobago.
