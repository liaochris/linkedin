Leopoldo aka Polo Estol’s Summary

Graduated as Medical Veterinarian, earn a post graduated diploma in Public Health with Specialization in Sanitary Administration.

My activities include work in animal health, welfare, and in veterinary public health. In the academic area I develop from its very beginning a private Veterinary School. I was the Dean during 14 years (who help me to develop an excellent team working attitude) and also during that time I became the first and only Animal Welfare and Ethics Professor in Argentina. I have a broad experience working at national and international level

Lectures (80/07):Ethics, Animal Welfare, in more than 70 Congresses (Argentina). Organizer, President, or Secretary in 6 conferences; and abroad in 41 conferences (92/07) on Ethics and/or Animal Welfare.

Published on Ethics, Animal Welfare, Allergy, Dermatology and Environment (84/07), 30 publications and legislative projects

Publisher, First Edition in Spanish of the Manual for the Care and Use of Animals of Laboratory, Canadian Council on Animal Care(97)

Leopoldo aka Polo Estol’s Specialties:

Veterinary education and training of employees on the Implications of animal health and welfare on food safety, and the environment. Passionate in the field of animal welfare, strategic thinker and politically aware of the consequences and responsibilities of the issue at local an international level.
