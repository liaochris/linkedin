Peter Sutton’s Summary

Peter at work

Peter is CEO at Converged Management Ltd which has three main strings to its bow:

1. Converged Management Ltd is the UK and Ireland Distributor for PARI Profile, a Danish company with a complete portfolio of Personality Profiling products that include products for Recruitment and Staff Development as well as traditional Personality Profiling. Converged Management is establishing a sales network across the UK and Ireland and is on the look out for interested parties.

Resellers can expect to earn in excess of £40,000 per year as an ongoing passive revenue stream.

2. A Corporate Finance Borkerage fulfilling requirements from £15,000 to £15,000,000. This is achieved through association with organisations such as the Business Advice Bureau Ltd. A popular solution is totally unsecured funding of up to £250,000 subject to eligibility as the guarantee is underwritten by the DTI.

Peter has an Alliance Programme which pays up to £30,000 a year for successful Corporate Finance opportunities.

3. Providing Business Management Consultancy working as a member of the ICFBA with experience from working many sectors and company sizes from SME to corporates.

Peter at home
Outside work Peter has many interests, singing with the Mendip Male Voice Choir, preparing for his first gig with his band at the end of July and is also a member of several Operatic and Dramatic Societies recently having performed Oliver! and preparing to perform Rebecca at the end of June. Occasionally he gets to spend an evening with his family as well as playing cricket, hockey and football in the vain hope that the onslaught of old age can be deferred, just call him King Canute! and regularly attending the local church.

Peter enjoys Social and Business Networking as these are often complimented by good conversation, a pint of beer or a glass of wine. Peter has also been known to read books, Michael Crichton and Tess Gerritson being a particular favourites.
