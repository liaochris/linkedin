Emmanuel Peninoy’s Summary

Experienced and creative sales, marketing and public relations executive, within the travel/tourism and hospitality industries. Independent professional and open-minded with extensive experience in inbound tourism to the United States and specifically to San Francisco as a premier destination whilst contributing to local communities. Not only have "I left my heart in San Francisco," I am also a staunch advocate in embracing, improving, and preserving the beauty and history of the Mid-Market area of San Francisco as opposed to allowing its historical landmark to continue in the direction of decay and neglect. As a proud voting Californian, I also firmly believe in preserving the compassion that is embedded in this City crucial to the survival of America's fledgling souls.
