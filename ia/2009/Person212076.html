Scott Weiss’s Summary

Scott is known world-wide for his leadership in mobile usability with respect to design, prototyping, and user testing. After eleven great years leading Usable Products Company, a usability agency in New York, he now works for Human Factors International's London office as Executive Director.

More about Scott:
* Seasoned market research and information design professional with significant management experience.
* Joined Human Factors Europe as Executive Director in February, 2008.
* Founded Usable Products Company is 1996 in New York City.
* Frequent lecturer world-wide.
* Author of "Handheld Usability" (Wiley: 2002).

Scott Weiss’s Specialties:

Usability, Information Architecture, and Market Research with a specialisation in mobile
