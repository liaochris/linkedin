Katie Smillie’s Summary

My research focuses on enterprise architecture best practices, business architecture, architecture roles and responsibilities, and EA resources. I create surveys and manage Forrester's Enterprise Architecture Research Panel, a growing panel of more than 350 EA professionals from around the world who participate in monthly surveys from Forrester. I also helped develop and continue to contribute to Forrester's TechRadar research methodology. Before working at Forrester, I worked in politics on a United States Senate campaign in Washington state and a Lieutenant Governor campaign Massachusetts.

I'm interested in opportunities to build my skills in marketing, product strategy, and business strategy and management. I'm particularly interested in media, publishing, food & wine, government, and technology industries.
