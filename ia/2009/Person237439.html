Tracey Robinson-English’s Summary

Journalist with 20-plus years experience. Produced reports for BusinessWeek, The Chicago Tribune, The Chicago Sun-Times, Newsweek, The Washington Post and CBS affiliates. Former communications director for corporate and nonprofit with specialization in media relations and strategic partnerships. Proven track record of strategically leading large projects from conception to completion.

Goals: To build a high-growth company by serving clients whose mission is to create positive social and economic change globally. Areas of of interest include corporate social responsibility, education, advocacy and public policy, healthcare, green environment and diversity.

Tracey Robinson-English’s Specialties:

Ability to lead the development, communication, acceptance, revision and implementation of an effective strategic plan for non-profit and corporate realms.

Tracey Robinson-English’s Experience

  • President and CEO

    English Communications, Inc.

    (Media Production industry)

    January 1998 — Present (11 years 11 months)

    English Communications, Inc. is a Chicago-based marketing media and public relations consulting firm with a focus on social change and leadership. We work with companies whose mission we admire and values we share. Our team of professional journalists and public relations experts, graphic designers, video editors and photographers creates effective communication and media relations campaigns to maximize media coverage of a client's story on multiple media platforms.

    We present your story right to engage your audiences with well-crafted, effective presentations. What differentiates us from the competition is our passionate commitment to elevating important local and national issues with high integrity through an array of media tools. We bring award-winning expertise and senior-level thinking to the task of transforming your ideas into attention-grabbing messages that connect you to your audience.

    We have created positive media attention for a wide array of clients including the Kellogg School of Management and the Media Management Center at Northwestern University; The McGraw-Hill Company; Chicago Public Schools; Museum of Science and Industry, among other reputable institutions.

    Additionally, English Communications, Inc. can assist your messaging needs in other ways by helping you produce high-quality written reports, video presentations and productions, annual reports and Web site content. We also can organize local and national media tours, provide professional training and coaching for public appearances and media interviews.

  • Interim Director/Consultant

    Media Management Center, Northwestern University

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2007 — February 2009 (2 years 2 months)

    Tracey previously served as the interim director for the McCormick Tribune Fellowship Program for 80 minority media executives at Northwestern University, where she steered development of the new McCormick Tribune Fellows website and the annual professional development forum. She also coordinated media relations for MMC research reports, assisted with grantwriting that secured more than $1 million in MMC funding and wrote a major case study on media leadership, among other special projects. Her distinguished journalism work recently has generated front-page coverage in The Chicago Sun-Times' real-estate section on neighborhood development in Chicago and has included profiles of business leaders in Business Week, national reporting for Newsweek, features in The Washington Post, Chicago Magazine, The Chicago Tribune and columns on personal finance as well as lengthy features on national trends in Ebony, where she was an associate editor.
