Frank Urro’s Summary

My professional objective focuses on the development of solutions for businesses and consumers to maximize the value of Person-to-Person, B-to-B and B-to-C communications. Without such solutions the benefits of low cost communications are mired by the resulting abuse and misuse of, what should be, personal resources. This abuse then leads to consumer frustration, use throttling, increased cost for protection, consumer desensitization, or worse technology abandonment.

Frank Urro’s Specialties:

Maximizing profitability through the exploration of business/technology processes to uncover dormant intrinsic value. The most recent out-come of my experience is the development of “Personal Value Control™”. A process by which Internet communication is both defended, and financially supported, by its intrinsic monetary value. The process completely replaces the costly expenditures, for battling spam and mobile messaging abuse, with new bottom-line profits and free (un-abusable) services.

Frank Urro’s Experience

  • Vanquish Inc - Inventors of Personal Value Control

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    July 2002 — Present (6 years 9 months)

    Co-Founder, inventor and architect of Personal Value Control, a consumer empowering infrastructure that will allow consumers to enjoy complete protection from commercial information overload in any of their purchased digital communications mediums.

    Revolutionary in nature - Personal Value Control was designed to eliminate the cost consumers must bear to protect their digital identities from irrelevant commercial content

    Personal Value Control means that the consumer’s interrupt value is now under their control. Therefore, liability, in the form of a digital cash guarantee, is motivating to the commercial sender to ensure the message is relevant. In this model, in place of the consumer paying for protection, a dissatisfied target consumer would enjoy the instant redemption of their personal guarantee.

    For the very fist time in history, Personal Value Control places the consumer in personal control of their highly traded commodity – personal attention.
