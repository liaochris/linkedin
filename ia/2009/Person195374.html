Rebecca McCormick (rebecca.mccormicktravel@gmail.com)’s Summary

Author of over 300 published articles, Rebecca McCormick is a full time freelance travel journalist who anchors her pen in Hot Springs, Ark., where she writes bi-weekly travel columns for her hometown newspaper and monthly features for a local resort lifestyle magazine.

Southern charm personalizes the spirited text of her travel writing for Gannett News Service, The Miami Herald, AAA Southern Traveler, New Orleans Homes & Lifestyles, Weddings with Style, Hot Springs Life & Home, Hot Springs Village Voice, Gumbo, and G for the Modern Golfer (Spring 09).

When not on the road, Rebecca manages Four Seasons String Quartet, the group with whom she plays cello for weddings and special events.

She is married to Myron, an avid golfer. Together, they have three married sons and three grandchildren.

Travel journalism with expertise in arts and culture, golf and spa destinations, luxury properties and soft adventure.

    Rebecca publishes a column every other Sunday, with the goal to provide her readers enough information to make intelligent travel decisions.

    "In this Internet age of citizen journalism where anybody can write a blog and nearly everyone has a camera in their cell phone, travelers don't need me to tell them what they can read in a brochure. They want reassurance the hard-earned cash they spend on a trip to celebrate their significant event will not be squandered or wasted, but will be wisely invested in valuable memories that will last a lifetime. That puts a lot of responsibility on me to write as a journalist, not just as a cheerleader for my favorite destinations."
