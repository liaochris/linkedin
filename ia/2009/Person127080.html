Kasia Kujawa’s Summary

Kasia is the new Director of Marketing at Rhoddy Design.

Prior to my current position, I was Director of Marketing for Onext Media, a digital narrow casting/ advertising solutions company. At Onext I managed and led all sales and marketing solutions and initiatives for the British Columbia corporate head office.
I am interested in consulting regularly with executives from a variety of fields to help create synergistic solutions to a world-impacting team at Rhoddy Design.

Rhoddy Design is a multi-disciplinary agency offering design solutions from concept through to production. With over 15 years experience, our team of designers, project managers and fabrication specialists have created custom and dynamic experiences for the world’s most recognizable brands.We are a full service design agency specializing in innovative and inviting spaces that communicate your brands message to a remarkable experience.
