Joe Cruz’s Summary

I have been in healthcare sales since 1985, when I left my position as the Deputy Public Affairs Officer of the US Army’s 3rd Infantry Division to return to the US from Germany.

My subsequent experience includes assignments at companies with a focus on healthcare, including Procter & Gamble, SpaceLabs Medical, Eclipsys, scheduling.com and McKesson. I most recently worked at CliniComp, a provider of clinical documentation, fetal monitoring and surveillance systems.

While there as Director, DoD Sales, I was responsible for new sales to all Military Health System (MHS) hospitals. Between 2007-2009, I sold over $70M of the company’s software and services to MHS – culminating in a 2009 $55M contract award which made our software the standard clinical documentation solution for all Air Force, Army, Marine Corps and Navy facilities worldwide.

Whether working at a Fortune 16 company or as part of a sales team at smaller, nimbler healthcare enterprises, I have learned to maximize the resources available to me in order to work for the customer's success.

I am customer- and solution-focused in my approach and take seriously my responsibility to ensure we deliver value to the customer.

I now work with an amazing collection of scientists, engineers and sales professionals at SAIC, whose employees collectively seek to help DoD deliver value to their customers around the world.
