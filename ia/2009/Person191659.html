Rachel Horvath’s Summary

I have been a freelance video editor for the past two years, during and after college. I have edited numerous projects, two of which have won awards. I have edited documentaries, music performances, summer camp videos, several short fiction films, TV roll in specials and slide shows.

I am experienced with Final Cut Pro, DVD Studio and the whole Adobe Collection including After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator, Premier Pro, Fireworks, Encore, In Design, Soundbooth, ExtendScript and Dreamweaver.

I currently work as an Assistant at Lunchbox Studios.
