My knowledge revolves around what I use the most when completing projects for my clients. Those things being CSS, (X)HTML, PHP, Ajax, JavaScript, Photoshop, Illustrator, Image Ready, and a few other design programs/techniques. I am proficient in current trends with hand coded CSS and (X)HTML using a simple text editor. Cross-browser compatibility and Wc3 Standards are just a few key guidlines that govern my work.
