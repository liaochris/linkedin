Kapil Khandelwal’s Summary

Kapil brings over 20 years of industry, operations and consulting experience of connecting the healthcare and life sciences value chain from early drug discovery, clinical development, care delivery and intermediaries and payors. He is a member of several leading industry boards and incubator and mentor of industry groups, start-ups and subject matter experts. He specialises in creating business strategy, process and operations improvement, multi-site services delivery for healthcare and life sciences, outsourcing and transformational models, disease management and modelling, consumer genomics and personalized healthcare, technology and innovation. He has managed over 25 multi-country/cross-border deals in M&A, restructuring, reforms, divestment and alliances across healthcare, life and agri sciences and CPG sectors.
