Ami Daniel’s Summary

As a naval officer, Ami has been at the operational and technological forefront of the Israeli Navy. Today, he integrates operational experience with in-depth technological understanding and is able to succinctly and quickly communicate complex technologies to potential investors and customers.
Ami is a graduate of the Israeli Naval Academy and of the Advanced Naval Command Course, and has specialized in Command and Control Systems, Data Link and Air-Sea cooperation. During his service as a Tactical officer in the Corvette Division, he has executed assignments in extreme battlefield situations, special operations and international co-ops in Asia and Europe.

Since 2000, Ami has led many social struggles in which he has mobilized hundreds of people and recruited massive resources. One of which- was an establishment of a community center, including recruiting more than five hundred thousand dollars for the cause. Moreover, another initiative focused on the development of ground-breaking program for neglected youth.

Ami is acquiring education in Law and Business Management.

One of Ami's greatest hobbies is kayaking.
