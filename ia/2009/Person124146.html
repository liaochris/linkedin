Julie Miller Stewart’s Summary

I spent the first 10 years of my career working at The Atlanta Journal-Constitution. My background includes marketing, print and online sales. I have worked with many different verticals including: entertainment, sports, telecom, furniture, consumer electronics to name a few and have a proven track record. I am continuing my career in digital media sales with Myxer as of October 08. I am very passionate about digital media and believe in the results in can bring.

Myxer is the leader in ad-supported mobile content. Myxer’s “Web-to-Mobile” advertising platform provides a unique opportunity for marketers to engage and target their audience by age, gender, zip code, carrier as well as mobile.
