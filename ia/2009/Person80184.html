Ruslan Fomkin’s Summary

Research and development in database technologies with focusing to scientific applications.
In my research and development I extend and apply database techniques to manage and process big volumes of data in scientific applications. I focus on implementing scientific applications in declarative query languages, e.g. SQL, cost-based query optimization, efficient data integration, and privacy protection.
