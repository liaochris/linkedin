Kimberly Wong Hartmann’s Summary

Dedicated to extraordinary client service and committed to powerful results, Kimberly creates a relationship where your needs are surpassed, through integrity, professionalism, and honesty. She carefully listens to your needs and follows through with passion. Due to this high level of service, Kimberly’s business is entirely based upon referrals.

Leaving behind a decorated career with Prudential Preferred Properties, in 2005 Kimberly made the decision to join the energetic team of @properties. Entering her sixth year in Real Estate, Kimberly is working to expand her business while maintaining a standard excellence and has recently taken on a licensed personal assistant with plans to build a team to better serve her clients.

A born and bred Chicagoan Kimberly offers her clients a lifetime of knowledge about the area. Born in Chicago, raised in the northwest suburbs, she earned her Bachelor of Science Degree in Business Administration at Bradley University in Peoria. Recently married in June 2007, Kimberly presently resides in Chicago’s River North neighborhood; however she has lived in several Chicago neighborhoods giving her the advantage of a real working knowledge of this great city.
