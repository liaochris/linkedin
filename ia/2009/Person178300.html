Oliver M.T. Zock .·.’s Summary

Global Arena is the definitive marketplace for Foreign Direct Investment. Dedicated to matching businesses with compatible locations, it is the only resource that gives investors the ability to search for and review locations online in the full context of local economic and social indicators.
Think of our platform as the Facebook of global business locations. Consider it a Linkedin that connects business locations, companies and service providers. Global Arena uses its unique and proven scientific solution to combine triple-A economic and location data for the purpose of finding, searching, comparing and promoting global business locations. We use key technologies from Google Search and Maps, Youtube and Flickr to create the best user experience

Oliver M.T. Zock .·.’s Specialties:
