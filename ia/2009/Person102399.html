James DuCanto’s Summary

Twelve plus years of clinical practice of Anesthesiology.

Teaching has become a major interest of mine in the past 5 years; I am the medical director of the Milwaukee Area Technical College's Anesthesia Technology Program (since 2002), and I run the Anesthesiology rotation for the preliminary year (interns) at St. Luke's for the past 4 (or more) years.

Airway management is a major focus and passion of mine, and I am often referred cases for my expertise in this area.

I have an interest in "Alternative" and "Complementary" medicine, and I am an independent Reiki Master that teaches classes in Reiki through Aurora Complementary Medicine.

I have recently become board certified in Integrative and Holistic Medicine.

I also have an interest and affinity for holotropic breathwork and am a member of the International Breathwork Training Alliance, though which I am working towards professional certification.

James DuCanto’s Specialties:

Anesthesia in a large tertiary care medical center. Specific interests include airway surgery, liver transplantation, transfusion medicine, and blood conservation. Teaching many aspects of Anesthesia to resident physicians, nurses, respiratory therapists, paramedics, anesthesia technologists.

Reiki system of natural healing and holotropic breathwork.
