Derek Lawless’s Summary

I'm an experienced software developer based in Dublin, Ireland and have specialised in Web technologies for the last 10 years. I'm a Microsoft.NET and associated technologies stack expert.

I've worked with leading organisations including Vodafone, Toyota Ireland, o2, RTÉ, Lever Fabergé, Kerry Group, Independent Digital, and Enterprise Ireland - helping to realise their online projects.

I'm a veteran practitioner of web standards and web accessibility and was an early advocate of both practices in the Irish web industry.
