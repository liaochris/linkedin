Jack Milunsky’s Summary

My career spans nearly 20 years in software development in both large and small companies. I am an entrepreneur with a passion for startups. I am co-creator of Agilebuddy - the best way to implement Scrum online. I am an early adopter of Agile practices including Scrum, Lean, XP and TDD. I believe in solid engineering practices and good design to build the highest quality software products. I firmly stand behind simplicity and elegant user interface design.

I am currently COO at Brightspark and help manage the myriad software projects on the go there. My hands-on experience helps me tremendously with my ability to coach and consult with other companies trying to adopt Agile practices.
