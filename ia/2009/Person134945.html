Kristi Ramsey’s Summary

Kristi Ramsey is a Partner on the Logistics/Supply Chain Team with a background in Recruiting and Business Development on a National Level. She has strong relationship building skills and her proactive approach to develop business through consulting has laid the ground work for a solid foundation in the relationships she has established on both the Client and Candidate side. Kristi believes that there is no greater satisfaction that the opportunity of successfully bringing together a great talent with a great client company. She has been recognized for her success driven and consultative approach and with that she brings a quality of integrity. She is someone that you can count on as she continually strives for excellence. Her work includes working with Sales and Marketing in Asset and Non-Asset Based Freight Forwarders, Logistics, Supply Chain and 3PL Companies.
