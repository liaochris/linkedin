Ehsun Jalali’s Summary

Ehsun was born in the UK to Persian parents from Iran. Upon graduating he pursued a career in the supply and construction industry in the UK. After several years out in the countryside, Ehsun decided to pursue a professional career in the City of London.

In 2007 Ehsun joined WoodHamill in London looking after WoodHamill specific client accounts across Banking and Commerce and Industry. Following a successful year in London working with the WoodHamill team and following a trip to Dubai in 2007 he was keen to return to his Arabian/Persian routes and joined the Dubai office of WoodHamill Ingram as a Researcher working within the Commerce and Industry team.

Ehsun speaks fluent English, Farsi and is honing his Arabic, French and Spanish. A keen sportsman and traveller he is looking forward to exploring the Middle East region and spending more time in his homeland, Iran.
