Hanna Hasl-Kelchner’s Summary

I’m a seasoned business attorney, a no nonsense lawyer and MBA, who has dedicated her career to helping business leaders achieve their objectives with integrity and without turning into a litigator’s chew toy.

My career has spanned private and government practice, including service at the U.S. International Trade Commission where one of my cases was decided by President Ronald Reagan. I’ve also had the privilege of developing a diverse portfolio of increasing responsibility in various in-house positions at major corporations.

These experiences formed basis of my book The Business Guide to Legal Literacy: What Every Manager Should Know About the Law (Jossey-Bass, 2006). They were also the foundation of Legal Leverage®, a course I taught as an adjunct professor at top-ranked Duke University Fuqua School of Business and as visiting professor at the Darden School at the University of Virginia.

I feel strongly that it’s a lot cheaper to learn from other people’s mistakes and therefore use my blog (http://www.legalliteracy.com) to illustrate and update the concepts discussed in my book.

Future projects include a series of Legal Leverage® Action Guides designed to help business owners and managers keep their legal risk in sync with their business objectives.

Hanna Hasl-Kelchner’s Specialties:

Corporate generalist and good negotiator with strong collaborative and interpersonal skills -- a trusted business advisor.
