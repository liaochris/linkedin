Roxane Möllenkramer’s Summary

I am experienced in various subjects relating to New Media and Communication from an interdisciplinary perspective founded primarily on new media studies, social studies. Other areas include gamestudies, philosophy and women's studies.
My studies at Utrecht University are primarily theoretical, offering me a wide basis and various perspectives into transformations of culture and identity thanks to new media. To extend my expertise I have taken up several research internships in experience, behavior and motivation (engagement) of specific target groups. These internships were result-oriented and required interviews, quantitative research, statistical analyses, reports and presentations.

My goal is to occupy a research position in one of the advancing companies dealing with new media, games and/or identity. Whether this is a conceptual position, a research position, development position, or all of the above, I am very interested.

Roxane Möllenkramer’s Specialties:

Gamestudies, engagement research, user centered design, statistical analysis (SPSS), transmedial conceptual design
