Ken Edwards’s Summary

Aardvark Swift Recruitment - is a specialist Video Games Recruitment Agency.

Established in 1989 and based in the UK, ASR handles career opportunities across the UK, Europe, North America and further afield in Australia.

My area of speciality within the business is sourcing Sales, Marketing and PR professionals for manufacturers, distributors and publishers of video games hardware and software.

I am a preferred supplier of many of the largest companies in the games industry such as Sony, Microsoft Xbox, Nintendo, EA, Sega, THQ, Ubisoft, Eidos, Activision etc

I work at all levels in the industry from Graduate level vacancies to Senior Exec.

If you work in the games industry, please connect to me and I can keep you informed of potential opportunities.

All details are handled confidentially and should you choose to send me a CV - this will only be sent to vacancies once you have viewed a job spec and given your express permission.

Ken Edwards’s Specialties:

Identifying suitable candidates for positions in the Sales and Marketing divisions of the Video gaming industry.
