Timi Aguilar’s Summary

• Strategic and Brand Positioning – We can help you shape the right message that establishes you in the marketplace, in the community and with the right audiences.

• Public Affairs/Government Affairs – We provide PR services that support your relationships with state and local governments. We partner with government affairs and civic groups to create successful media strategies and events.

• Community Relations/Philanthropy – We can help you establish the right relationships to ensure better visibility and build trust. We can reach target communities that are important to you and identify strategic philanthropic and community investments to help you build key community relationships.

• Media Relations – We plan and execute the right media strategies for businesses and organizations of all sizes, including media planning, writing and issuing press releases, and serving as a media spokesperson. We also provide speech writing, media training and management of press events, as well as press kit items, such as fact sheets, management biographies, company backgrounders, timelines, and Question and Answer documents.

• Analyst and Media Tours – Whether you need to meet with media or industry analysts. We also provide investor relations briefings that support your brand development.

• Trade Show and Event Management – We manage complex media announcements and trade show presence, as well as community and corporate events.

• Marketing – Through our marketing partnerships across the country, we provide marketing resources that helps you reach your target audiences.

• Social Media - Built into your PR strategy at various levels depending on your audience.
