Branko Šabarić’s Summary

Since 1993 I'm working in graphic design industry. For years I was hired by Galerija stari grad in Đurđevac to create layout for many exhibition catalogues, concerts and other manifestations. In the year 1994 I was editor of the local newspapers "Domaća gruda" and remained for a year. I worked on identity for local radio station, handball club, folklore group, youth club etc. As the world turned and Interned evolved I started my career in web design.
At the end of 2000 I moved to Zagreb where I live and work today. I was working for HRT (Croatian national radio and television), as a graphic and web designer since that year. At the end of 2007 I was appointed as a head of web department where I worked on numerous in-house projects until the end of 2008.
In the year 2003 couple of my friends and I started our own bussiness. We called it M2 (Marketing and Multimedia). We release our creative potentials in graphic and web design, consalting, marketing and other areas within the reach of the visual communication through it.
Since 2006 I've been constantly outsourced by a design studio dzDESIGN from Zagreb for which I've realised a number of graphic and web projects. At the beginning of 2009 I've continued my career as a creative director at dzDESIGN which is my current position.
