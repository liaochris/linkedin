Ravi Nagarajan’s Summary

Ravi Nagarajan is a private investor and writer focusing on applying value investing techniques to find securities trading well below intrinsic business value. Ravi has over 14 years of experience in the financial markets and started investing on a full time basis in 2009. Over the past 13 years, Ravi held executive level positions in the commercial software industry. Ravi graduated Summa Cum Laude from Santa Clara University with a degree in finance.
