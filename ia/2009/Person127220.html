Kate Dillon’s Summary

Coming from a history in Education, Vocational training and Human resources Kate has developed a strong base in business and service communication and delivery.

Utilising her business acumen and her talent for meaningful communication she connects with people and has a unique talent for helping others to define their needs.

Her strength is the ability to transform difficult and often dry concepts into practical related information which her audience can put into practice as well as providing thought provoking ideas.

Kates' approach uses wit, humour and acquired knowledge.
Her skill at tuning into to her audience as a group and as individuals is what makes her a highly valued trainer and her approach ensures that they walk away satisfied and stimulated.

Kate Dillon’s Specialties:

Human behaviour, Effective leadership, Effective communication, Interpersonal skills, Workplace harassment/bullying, High quality customer service, Environmental awareness in the workplace, Ethical practice, OH&S, Teamwork, Goal setting & review, Interview techniques, Interview techniques for employers - how to gather in depth insights & information from the interviewee, interview time management.
