Bradley Foster’s Summary

I am an experienced executive, leadership and life coach known for keen insights, clear perspective and intuitive interventions which have helped hundreds of my clients reach their goals.

I earned my Master’s Degree at the University of Toronto and undergraduate degree at McGill University and Columbia University. I completed the three year training program in leadership and psychotherapy at the Gestalt Institute of Toronto and have lead several workshops and coach training classes. I have fifteen years experience as a communications consultant to major international corporations including Reuters Information Services and Thomson Newspapers.

I have experience coaching executives of Fortune 500 companies; CEO’s and lawyers in leadership and communication skills that helped them achieve greater goals both personally and professionally. I bring a balanced perspective and judgement to relationships and interpersonal communications which help my clients reframe their situation.

Bradley Foster’s Specialties:

Executive, leadership, communication, relationship and life coaching; polarity management.
