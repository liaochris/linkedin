Emily Keller’s Summary

I am a journalist and non-fiction writer with five years of professional writing and reporting experience and a master’s degree in interactive media and business and economics reporting.
I have a wide breadth of writing, reporting and editing skills, a strong sense of journalistic ethics, a keen eye for detail and a dedication to accuracy and clarity.
I am particularly interested in working on books about social and economic issues, the environment, civil rights, legal issues, health care, domestic and international politics, and personal memoirs.
I live in New York City and I am available to telecommute.
