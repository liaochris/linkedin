Greg Berry’s Summary

In 2008, I'm launching the Business Catapult and also consulting on interesting, world-changing projects. In addition to figuring out how to increase the flow of capital (both angel capital and venture capital) into all kinds of entrepreneurs (with a particular focus on progressive, sustainable and social entrepreneurs) at BC, I'm helping design a system to coordinate the learning and reporting on the world's health information systems, defining what it means to be a virtual nation and helping organize the integral health community.

In 2007, I helped organize community thinking around Millennium Development Goals on both global health and agriculture, working with the leading organizations in those fields. I also had a special relationship with young arab leaders, who are sorting out how to engage a shifting global culture.

Including my current suite of new projects, I have launched eight communications / media businesses either by myself or as part of a founding team, and led AOL's Digital City Denver creative unit during a transition and integration into the pre-Time Warner AOL. I have been integral to launching approximately a dozen new media products, most of which have become leaders in their respective niches.

For 20 years, my personal practice has focused on building COMMUNITIES OF ACTION that not only learn (Fifth Discipline-style), but create tangible change. Although I have emphasized digital media, I also spent three years working on elite conferences on several continents, gaining a different perspective on how communities accomplish goals.

Greg Berry’s Specialties:

* Creation and development of conversations among global thought leaders.
* Design and deployment of community-based media.
* Minessence, which is the refinement and simple communication of complex concepts to aid in understanding.
* Introduction of disruptive technology, innovative systems and new memes.
* General trend research (social, business, technology), and strategic development that creates organizational evolution.
