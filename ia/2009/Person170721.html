Nadia Nassif’s Summary

Nadia is a specialist in coaching for international professionals, offering a break-through in effective verbal and written communication. She has a decade of experience teaching English as a Second Language (ESL) to a variety of demographics both in the US and in Japan. In addition to her exemplary client service model, Nadia's coaching style and the results she helps coachees attain has set her apart from traditional corporate ESL programs offered by local language schools, community colleges and private vendors. She is raising the bar in quality communication coaching for Boston's professional international community.

As a trainer, Nadia focuses in helping her clients with presentation skills, meeting management, business writing, networking, and many other nuanced areas of business communication. She also addresses areas beyond the verbal, including body language, cross-cultural references, protocol, and considering the political culture of the office as a backdrop to communication. Throughout the training, Nadia also works at a deeper level, helping clients to refine grammar and English pronunciation.


Raised in an English and Arabic speaking family, Nadia has a rich background in language, culture, and travel- she has lived in France, Lebanon, and Japan, and has traveled across Europe and the US. A broad background in multicultural living and language experiences, as well as a host of entrepreneurial roles in language education, has extended themselves to Nadia's unique ability to understand and work with the international community.

"Coaching clients has been very eye-opening to me- there are so many unnecessary and unattended to challenges that our international peers face on a daily and weekly basis, some the full length of their careers! My goal and commitment is to meet this group with break-through communication coaching that will drive change both for the individual and for the organization."
