Young Photographers United (YPU), a visual Concept Builder

YPU eats, breathes and talks photography.

We believe in the power of an image. It has been said over and over that “a picture is worth a thousand words”. It suggests that “seeing” reveals what words alone cannot describe.

The total of images, from the most exclusive billboard images to the tiny images used online, creates a company’s look and feel.

When all communication is synchronised, this visual approach can turn any regular brand into a Love Brand.

A Love Brand transcends normal brands. It reaches the heart as well as the mind, creating an intimate, emotional connection with the customer.

YPU helps companies to stay or become a Love Brand by providing strong images, visual concepts and visual consulting.

More info at http://wwww.ypu.org
