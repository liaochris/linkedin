Go Natural - Michele Kish’s Summary

Licensed Cosmetologist / 1978.
Business Owner - Beauty Salon 1985 - Cosmetic Company 1990 - Go-Natural Inc.
Inventor of the All-In-One Cosmetic ® - Go-Natural ® - 1999.
The Multi-Functioning, Single Shade Cosmetic - Worldwide distribution since 1999.

Go Natural ® Inc. founded by Licensed Cosmetologist / Beauty Professional Michele Kish, who began her career and has been passionately involved in the professional beauty industry since 1978 - Michele strived to bring affordability and simplicity to beauty to allow all women to have an equal opportunity to look and feel their best by enhancing their natural beauty - easily, as well as address the growing need for the busy women to have an alternative to the mainstream complicated application of multiple cosmetics.

Go Natural ® and The ALL-IN-ONE Cosmetic ® began in 1999 in London Ontario Canada, our Corporate Headquarters which continues to offer direct distribution and service to our Canadian customers and our US fulfillment center. We entered the United States in 2001 - covering the Southeast and expanded to the West Coast where we established our professional US and International Distribution Center in Las Vegas NV in 2005.
Our products are made with the strictest of professional standards and of the highest quality ingredients available - manufactures well within FDA and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), offers multilingual packaging options, proudly made in America and distributed by licensed beauty professionals retailers and distributors worldwide since 1999. We wholesale and retail our products as well as exhibit in over 80 Professional Beauty Industry and Consumer Trade Shows annually in North America - including New York, Las Vegas, Orlando, Chicago, Toronto, London, Ottawa, Hamilton and much more.
With now well over 500 retail, large channel and independent distributors - worldwide and hundreds of thousands of satisfied Go-Natural ® customers.
