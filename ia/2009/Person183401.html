Paul McAfee’s Summary

In 2006, Paul co-founded eXubrio C-Level Leads, LLC (www.c-levelleads.com). This firm makes sales appointments at the Vice President to CEO or Owner level.

In 2005, Paul founded eXubrio Group LLC (www.exubrio.com). This is a full-service advertising, marketing, and public relations firm.

In 2002, Paul McAfee founded eXubrio, LLC (www.exubrio.com). In early 2005, he co-founded eXubrio C-Level Leads, LLC (www.c-levelleads.com) to provide sales appointment setting services at the VP and higher level to his clients.

Paul was pro bono Entrepreneur in Residence at the University of Rochester's Office of Technology Transfer until 2005. He also currently pro bono Entrepreneur in Residence at the State University of New York's (SUNY) University at Buffalo (UB) Technology Incubator.

History:

Achieved highest launch sales for all U.S. markets. Named Vice President of National Ethnic Marketing.

Developed Best Practices for Marketing of Kodak’s Hi-Tech and Internet Products. Served as Director of Marketing Process Development reporting directly to Chief Marketing Officer of this $13 billion leader in photographic equipment and supplies.

Danaher Results = 25% Sales Increase for Danaher Division - American Sigma.
