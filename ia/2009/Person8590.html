Alex S. Brown, PMP IPMA-C’s Summary

Expert project manager with expertise in managing complex projects for on-time, on-budget delivery. Have solved complex business and technical problems negotiating creative solutions that satisfy conflicting goals. Mentoring individuals and speaking as a professional expert to project management professional groups.

For more details, visit my website at http://www.alexsbrown.com. It has my articles on project management and slides from all of my public presentations.

Alex S. Brown, PMP IPMA-C’s Specialties:

Project manager handling projects ranging from corporate strategic planning, marketing, Information Technology, and business process automation. Experienced with both property & casualty insurance and U.S. brokerage industries. Experienced speaker and trainer.

  • Founder and Moderator

    Toastmasters International LinkedIn Group

    (Internet industry)

    September 2007 — July 2008 (11 months)

    Founded an on-line LinkedIn networking group for Toastmasters International members. Created the http://www.toastmasters-networking.org/ web site to support the group, and to help Toastmaster members find other on-line networking opportunities.

    Recruited other moderators to help run the group. Started in September 2007 with the first member, and grew the group to over 800 members by July 2008. Grew quickly, by word-of-mouth, and relied on volunteers and member honesty to confirm who was and was not actually a Toastmaster member.

Found that our screening process had kept the quality of the membership very high. Well over 90% were current or former Toastmasters, despite the fact that our volunteer moderators never had access to the official list of Toastmasters members.

  • Strategic Planning Office Manager

    Mitsui Sumitomo Insurance Group, USA

    (Insurance industry)

    2003 — 2007 (4 years)

    Administered the MSIG Strategic Plan, including start-up and post-project review for every effort in it. Trained and mentoring over 60 project managers in the 400-person company. Projects included OFAC/anti-terrorism screening, Sarbanes-Oxley compliance, marketing campaigns, branding initiatives, and the replacement of the policy issuance IT system.

    - Recommended project changes to reflect changing strategic company goals
    - Created a system of policies and procedures for budget, schedule, and change control
    - Presented up to ten new project ideas per quarter to senior management
    - Crafted clear business cases and streamlined processes that typically allow senior management to decide whether or not to pursue new opportunities in 10 minutes or less
    - Developed and ran six introductory classes in project management (two-day), as well as monthly one-hour informational sessions for experienced project managers
    - Created four on-line training modules with Human Resources, covering critical company policies and procedures
    - Introduced project management discipline to the marketing and sales area, working closely with the Chief Marketing Officer to run more effective, better organized sales campaigns
