Tre (Tracy) Rodriguez’s Summary

I am a born problem solver & people person. In each role I assume I capitilize on a keen ability to understand the people & processes around me at a super ordinate level. I use this comprehension to improve processes whenever I can. Efficiency, ease of use, & professionalism are the hallmarks of my solutions.

While at JMS North America I created a system utilizing novel programming in Excel that reduced by 75% the Sales, Logistics, & Finance administration for our Latin American business. Our team was happy to have that time back to pursue bottom-line driven goals.

I acted as liaison between HQ in California, sales in Florida, suppliers in Belgium, Italy, Germany, Singapore, China, & Japan, as well as customers in Latin America & the Caribbean. All excellent experience that allows me to comfortably operate & think in relation to our increasingly global economy.

At Northgate Environmental Management I gained my first exposure to consulting. The opportunity to work on new & varied projects with a regular focus on problem solving struck a cord with me. Closely working with project managers, clients, subcontractors, & billing gave me a great basis for understanding how consulting works at a dynamic, growth oriented firm willing to teach.

Additionally, I am committed to promoting the empowerment of women. I've founded two organizations to do so. One, Women Reaching Upward, is a career support group. The second, Bay Area Back Roads, is a hiking and adventure group. I believe in mentoring, the strength of networks, & that everyone has something to offer.

Recently, I've returned to school to complete my bachelors in Economics, exp 2010. Upon graduation I intend to work in consulting. I also have plans to complete an MBA.
