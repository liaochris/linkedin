Jesse Powell’s Summary

Having started 3 very different businesses from the ground up in the past 7 years, I've worn every hat on the rack. Several common factors have been responsible for the success of each company: my complete dedication to each project, strong management skills, familiarity with and utilization of cutting-edge technology, guerrilla marketing tactics, and visionary ideas.

The secondary market in virtual worlds is a cut-throat industry, increasingly penetrated by countries with the lowest labor costs and least regulation. To remain competitive, we have developed software to automate many processes, and have partnered with firms in Southeast Asia and Eastern Europe to handle the more mundane labor-intensive tasks. By saving money in these areas, we are able to provide native English speakers for all customer service and sales inquiries.

Having a deep understanding of gaming, from collectible card games to MMORPGs, and of current technologies from Facebook to the IPhone, I have a good idea where the future is headed. The opportunities are boundless. Unfortunately, I am seeing a lot of companies grind out failures of products because they have not invested sufficiently in security, or they have not sufficiently examined their target market.

My goal is to improve education, foremost in the United States, but also around the world. I am currently exploring some ideas for accomplishing this through games, but I'm also considering the possibility of entering the political arena.
