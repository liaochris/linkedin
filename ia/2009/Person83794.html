Gary Lumsden’s Summary

I am a marketer and facilitator; a GM and tactical planner. I design and deliver cross-border call center services from nearshore, US and Canadian call centers. I have serviced the US English and Hispanic Market since 1999. I work with a talented team of professionals on highly coordinated marketing efforts. If you have a product or service to market, contact me at glumsden@ gmail.com y tambien, se habla espanol.

We pay special attention to each facet of the inbound customer service or outbound sales acquisition campaign we deploy --- lead generation; collateral; requirements analysis; contracts; workflow; copy/scripting; database; training; quality and benchmarking; logistics and reporting.

For newer centers, we work as an outsourced business development organization. Send me an invite to receive regular "status updates". I post to forums with opportunities, and don't hesitate to email me directly at glumsden@ gmail.com

Recently, in an effort to bring clients marketing opportunities in the mobile space, I began collaboration with Allscenes, an exciting digital media company that creates quick and effective promotional presence on mobile phone platforms. “On the go” mobile users want to be “plugged-in” and informed by sources that are accessible and reflect their tastes and preferences. Allscenes helps clients reach these new groups thru their rich new media experience.

Allscenes believes that many clients will seek to promote to mobile first and then integrate content back to their websites. Allscenes has defined a unique portable multimedia format, ideally suited for mobile use and easy re-use to multiple platforms --- blogs, websites and widgets without redundant effort. Allscenes’ template-based system minimizes technical needs and organizes content uniquely around "Scenes" that users can subscribe to and follow from their phone.
