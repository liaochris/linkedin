Lindsay Fultz’s Summary

Lindsay Fultz's professional experience spans across multiple industries which includes health and fitness, travel and leisure, sales and marketing in professional sports and entertainment and event services. Fultz joined Grosh Scenic Rentals in Feb 2007 as a Sales Asst.

Shortly thereafter, she was promoted to Sales Coordinator, a position that was created specifically for her. In addition to her previous duties as an assistant Fultz was responsible for overseeing the Sales Consultants, reporting and tracking the daily sales activities and ensuring individual and team goals were met.

After serving in this position just a couple of months and streamlining many of the sales departments processes, she was promoted to Sales Manager. In this position, coupled with her previous duties as an assistant and coordinator she implements, develops, reports and analyzes sales practices and programs, tracks the ROI of the sales and marketing outbound efforts and oversees and ensures that company sales goals are met. Outbound calls increased by over 300%.

While at Grosh Scenic Rentals Lindsay developed the company's virtual "Word of Mouth" New Media campaign. With the success of the marketing campaign she was promoted to her current position Manager of Sales and New Media Marketing where she currently maintains and reports all of the social networking sites associated with Grosh Backdrops and Drapery. She continues to monitor, track and implement new media trends to further optomize, establish and brand the Grosh name and survey competition.

She is the sales and social media contact at Grosh!

Prior to joining Grosh Scenic Rentals, Fultz graduated from Old Dominion University in 2002 with a degree in Sports Management, emphasizing in Sports Marketing and Promotions and a minor in Marketing. She also was a member of the Division I Cross-Country and Track team
