Claudia Cardozo-Tarullo’s Summary

Claudia currently works for Every Company Counts as a Small Business Community Liaison representative responsible for fostering economic development in the Spanish community. As part of her duties she is responsible for providing business development services to all up-and-coming companies, start-ups and established small businesses throughout Rhode Island’s, Latino business community. She specializes in fostering new partnerships and helping them to reach success in Rhode Island.

A graduate of the Rhode Island College Outreach Program where she specialized in Insurance, Claudia is currently attending RI Community College, pursuing a Business degree with a Marketing Concentration. She presently carries licenses in Rhode Island for Property & Casualty and Life & Health Insurance. Claudia is also a recent graduate of the Rhode Island Latino Civic Fund’s, Latina Leadership Institute 2007, and the Dale Carnegie training course on Human Relations and Effective Communications.

Through her position Claudia participates in many trade shows and networking events throughout Rhode Island, in addition she also serves on several focused marketing and business relations committees such as, Emerging Women in Business, The Broad Street Project (Central Falls, Pawtucket and Cumberland), and Empowering Latino Business Seminars. She is currently an advisory board member of United Way of RI and a board member of the International Institute of RI.

Claudia has always supported community development issues, continues to maintain strong ties to the Spanish community, and expects to continue serving on local and regional projects and committees of interest in the future.

Claudia who is bi- lingual has worked in the past as a moderator and co-host of the weekly Mira television show on a syndicated Spanish television network. Prior to moving to Rhode Island in 2002, Ms. Tarullo was a clothing designer in her native country Columbia, South America.

public relations, marketing, new business development, bi-lingual bicultural (Spanish-English) communications, community relations.
