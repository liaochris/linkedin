Deb Di Gregorio’s Summary

Deb Di Gregorio founded Camarès Communications in 1982. Camarès Triages, Accelerates and Invents e-Business

Camarès improves the bottom lines of retail, B-to-B enterprises and online publishers. We do this by systematically reviewing markets, determining differentiators, designing highly optimized web sites and delivering the most efficient marketing programs available, anywhere. We make our clients money.

Camarès builds strong, resilient e-businesses, or e-business channels. Our advice is salient, prudent and often visionary. We understand that success may not be in how every one else is approaching the market but in the unique way your company can approach a market successfully.

We are...

One Part Geek
Camarès was founded as a marketing communications company strictly focused on technology companies. Our clients were major corporations, growth companies, start ups and publishing conglomerates. We’ve researched and written about dozens of technologies. Our command of IT issues from security, to architecture to regulatory is fluent. We've taken our deep tech knowledge and transferred it to older retail and B-to-B companies as well as publishing companies: triaging, accelerating and inventing their e-businesses.

One Part Marketer
Deb Di Gregorio was inducted into the NJ Advertising Hall of Fame in 2003 for her work promoting tech companies in NJ. Our track record is stellar, proven and reliable. Our approach is candid, clear and reasoned. If it does not advance your company and increase revenue for a reasonable investment, it doesn’t make sense.

One Part Sensible Entrepreneur
Our experience has brought us to this: you can’t succeed if the product and timing isn’t right. So we developed methodologies to test, measure and prove market assumptions, write business plans swiftly with powerful ROI measurements and then execute them on behalf of our clients.
