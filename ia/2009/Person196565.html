Glenn Turner’s Summary

Glenn Turner is a licensed attorney in the State of Illinois with over 16 years of legal experience. Glenn's legal experience ranges from criminal defense work to handling family law matters like divorce and adoption proceedings.

Glenn also has twenty two years experience in law enforcement rising to the position of Lieutenant. Glenn is most proud of forming and leading a drug task force and organizing and facilitating an alcohol awareness program.

This experience is utilized in his capacity of faciltating leadership development. The processes utilized focus on positive behavioral change combined with a professionally designed goal setting process that produces transformational changes.
