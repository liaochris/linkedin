Richard Parsons’s Summary

Organizational development specialist with experience in project management, facilitation, and design of large-scale organizational change.

As a skilled Software Implementation Project Manager with an outstanding background in ROI driven project oversight, I am seeking to align myself with a professional opportunity to provide leadership and direction. I relish opportunities to work at the executive level to create strategic collaboration across the organization and then follow through with identification of tactical executables to ensure big-picture and task-specific success. As a leader, I seek a thorough understanding of the capabilities and limitations of the team as a whole in order to leverage every possible advantage for the organization and mitigate potential risk. I take responsibility for challenges and obstacles and will provide direction to overcome or circumvent them. I have achieved outstanding results by facilitating and maintaining the following required ingredients:

 Executive sponsorship
 Objective alignment
 Team effort
 Recognition and reinforcement of progress toward goals
 Thoroughly planned hard work
 Communication

Throughout the course of the implementation project I give constant attention in the form of re-evaluation and renewal of agreement. Although the project is considered successful if delivered on time and on budget, I believe that the true ROI comes after the “Go-Live” and is measured by the adoption of the new processes, procedures, and technology.

It has been my extreme good fortune to have the opportunity, through my work with Disney, Oracle, NASA, and others, not just to have developed a set of skills which enable me to work with individuals and teams at every level of an organization, but even more importantly to have acquired a professional education which helps me utilize, share, and impart those tools and talents efficiently and effectively in order to achieve outstanding results.
