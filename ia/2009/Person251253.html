Andrei Zdetovetchi’s Summary

CESEVEDE (CSVD) is a multimedia company, specialized in interactive virtual reality photography, 360° spherical images, immersive multimedia experiences. Using hi-tech cameras, cutting edge technology and specialized software we create dynamic and unique experiences, resulting in longer website visits and increased revenue for our clients.

Services include professional still and 360° photography, fully flavored virtual tours & mini-sites with any level of interactivity, online/offline multimedia presentations.

Our clients are among the following industries but not limited to:
hospitality, travel & tourism, health care, education, sports, architecture and building trades, web/interactive agencies and studios.
