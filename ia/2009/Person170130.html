    LionPages

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2001 — Present (8 years 7 months)

    Core business is the creation of inter- and intranet webpages and consultancy work on database design/development. Most web projects are databases driven and based on the Symfony PHP framework. Some of the larger projects:

    The WebTicket application, part of the Infotec - a Ricoh company - Printticket suite. The Webticket (intranet) application allows for users to upload their printjob including all relevant information (stapling, folding, delivery addresses, inserts, tabs, printmode, etc..) to the central repro. Printing of the job can be completely automated from there on. After printing accounting information is updated in AccountingTicket thereby closing the order-to-cash process. Webticket is installed at numerous locations throughout Europe. For Infotec an advisory position is filled by LionPages on the design and development of the database used by the entire Printticket suite.

    A calculation site for XSPlatforms. A highly AJAX based application in which users can create, store and modify calculations for the suspended platform and lifeline protection systems. Thereby providing management with critical information about their customers.

  • Intern

    Océ Technologies B.V.

    (Public Company; oceny; Printing industry)

    September 2006 — April 2007 (8 months)

    Internship position for master project. Performed research on formal verification of Rose RealTime software models. Title of master thesis: The formal verification of Rose RealTime software models using Petri nets.

    In short: Rose RealTime software models (UML and C++) are translated into Petri nets. Analysis of these Petri nets is peformed using standard and well-known mathematical algorithms. Error-traces are generated as UML sequence diagrams in the Rose RealTime model when verification of a certain Petri net fails.
