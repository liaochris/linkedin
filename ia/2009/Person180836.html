Paola Bonavolontà’s Summary

P.R. Bonavolontà is marketing consultant and a counselor who help people to make a better living doing what they love.
Paola has a significant experience in marketing and training in a variety of areas including developing strategic approach, managing changes, team building, facilitating effective personal and relational communication; she is also considered very much creative especially because of her “emotional paintings”.
Former Global Marketing Director in NY, where responsible for developing international Marketing strategies and programs and supporting the development and launch of new products around the globe. Prior to that she had interesting marketing experience in Italian subsidiaries of Revlon, L’Oreal and Colgate-Palmolive. Native Italian, she is fluent in French and English. Holds a Graduate Degree in Economics and Business from the University Federico II of Naples final grade summa cum laude; she is also a certified counselor and a Master practitioner in NLP. She’s currently attending to a tree years Artherapy Master.
Thanks to her experience in leading marketing industries she combines perfectly a managerial and creative approach with an extreme capacity to understand the environment of her professional clients together with strong capacity to turn plans into action.

Paola Bonavolontà’s Specialties:

Strategic and operative marketing consulting with different medium and small firms. She designs and holds programs on relationship marketing, fearless self-promotion, and entrepreneurial success to corporate clients, professional associations, small businesses. As counselor she follows as well individuals in order to build and reinforce personal skills, improve work/life balance, solve emotional conflicts.She also provides services for carrier path evaluation and development.
