Jacques Hart’s Summary

I have 10 years+ internet marketing experience launching new ventures, managing marketing programs and growing revenue for leading publishers and Internet companies in a wide range of industries. I have implemented successful Internet marketing programs for numerous Fortune 500 companies, deploying a full spectrum of products, including search-engine marketing (SEO and paid search), social media, banner advertising, Web-development, e-mail, RSS and more.

Along the way, I have acquired an in-depth understanding of blogging, RSS, photo-sharing software, online video, open-source platforms, custom applications, social-tagging and messaging tools, among others. Additionally, I am well-versed in consumer behavior, seasonality variables, brand positioning, and conversion analysis and lead-generation metrics to implement successful e-commerce and Internet retail programs.This knowledge is applied in the context of brand equity, engagement and awareness, reputation management and consumer dialogues, media spend and production costs and product endorsement and trial.
