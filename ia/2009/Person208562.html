Sandip Gupta’s Summary

Sandip is a results driven senior executive with 17 years of experience managing and growing global high-tech product and service businesses. In various roles, Sandip has demonstrated to be a decisive and passionate leader with expertise in delivering short-terms results while driving long-term objectives.

- Well rounded executive with skill set spanning various CEO and VP level operating and corporate development roles in companies with 10M+ in yearly revenue with 50-100% Y/Y growth.
- Trustworthy leader with ability to attract and retain strong team members in a distributed organization of 200 people
- Creative deal-maker that can identify, negotiate and maintain long-term key revenue-generating strategic partnerships and contractual customer relationships in 30+ countries across the globe
- Astute financier with strong understanding of start-up related fund raising ($85M) and cross-border M&A transactions
- Technology savvy individual with keen sense of using technology to create business value in telecommunication, entertainment, retail, financial services and pharmaceutical industries

Mr. Gupta holds an MBA from the Stern School of Business at New York University, New York, NY. He also has a Bachelor's degree with Honors in Mechanical Engineering from the MNR National Institute of Technology, Allahabad, India.

Sandip has been a regular speaker at various industry conferences and meetings.

Sandip is an avid hiker, regular soccer player. He is an YPO member in the SF bay area, a TiE charter member and a chair for the TiE CEO forum.
