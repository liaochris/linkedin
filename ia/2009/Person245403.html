Wallace Zheng’s Summary

Company Profile

Wallace Life. Co., Ltd., founded in 2005, is located in Fuzhou city, Fujian province. We are specialized in consumer goods such as promotional, premium incentive products (including lanyard, bag, wristband, bottle opener, keychain, sport water bottle, mobile phone suspender), Apparel (including T-shirt, underwear, sportswear and workwear), Chinese traditional artwork and so on. As a big professional consumer goods company, we own three standard manufactories: Knitting factory, PVC and EVA products factory and special printing factory, which totally cover about 19,000 square meters. In order to insure the reliable and high-quality products for our clients, each product should be under strict quality testing.

Meanwhile, in order to meet client's requirements for different logos, we have more than 30 specialized designers and have introduced new advanced technology. We have advanced logo-craft devices like laser-sculpting machine, heat-transfer printing machine, off-set printing machine, silk-screen printing machine, and transfer printing machine. We plan and produce presents wholeheartedly for you, which will help popularize your brands and obtain the biggest commercial value.

Our products are sold to over ten countries and regions including USA, Japan, UK, Canada, Spain, Sweden and Australia. Our company pursues high-tech product research and development and upholds the corporate values of business worth its true value, manufacturing to higher standards. Adhering to the constant belief that the current quality means the future market, it sets out to deliver best products and service to domestic and overseas customers.

We look forward to cooperate with domestic and overseas businesses of interest that aims for mutual prosperity.
