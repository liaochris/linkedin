Rusty Citron’s Summary

Rusty Citron, President of The Motion Picture Strategy Group, is a respected leader and innovator in global marketing for entertainment, online digital media and consumer products.

The range of his experience covers online advertising and merchandising, as well as traditional marketing publicity and promotion campaigns for Fortune 500 clients including: Berkshire Hathaway, US West, Time-Warner, Microsoft, Melvin Simon, McDonalds, Disney, MGM, SONY Pictures, NBC, ABC, and Columbia Pictures to name a few.

More recently, he developed the marketing and promotion of digital high defintion media for online and video on demand distribution. He led a team of designers and sales people who executed online merchandising programs generating $200k in daily revenue. In the early days of online advertising, he was responsible for the marketing and promotion for online affiliate and email marketing campaigns that built a base of 7 million subscribers.

And he created and started one of the first online entertainment merchandising services in partnership with NBC.

Serving as Worldwide Senior VP at New World Entertainment, he led the team that revitalized Marvel Comics and the franchise of classic characters developed by Stan Lee; in addition to marketing the release of 30 motion pictures and 8 TV series.


His feature film credits includes work with all the major studios on hundreds of major motion pictures including; Return of the Jedi, Dances with Wolves, and Rocky V. As a personal manager, his association with Mel Brooks, Anne Bancroft, Dom DeLuise, Larry Gelbart, and Sha Na Na cover every aspect of the industry.

His television production and marketing credits encompass over 30 major television series and specials.

Rusty created and founded The Actors Hall of Fame; a non-profit foundation honoring lifetime achievement in film, television and theatre, and is dedicated to restoring dramatic arts education in schools.

He lives in Seattle and Los Angeles.
