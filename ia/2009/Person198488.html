Rick Dane’s Summary

TWITTER: audaciousweb

have been working to develop my company which produces 3D interactive websites. I use 3D modeling software to create the animations and they are animated within the browser using javascript so the websites can play without the need for any browser plugins. I am hoping to meet people who may be interested in my services and I invite anyone to contact me or to invite me to join your network as I am happy to accept.

I invite you to view my demo projects linked to above.

I see great potential for making use of realistic 3D graphic animations to build interactive web applications ranging from basic sites that have impressive graphic animations to full fledged 3D vritual worlds. All of my projects make use of "pre-rendered" images which are streamed to the user's browser cache, the advantage of this over real time rendering, which is used in video games, is that a more realistic animation can be achieved and the speed of the end user's computer is not a factor in properly running the animation.

I am looking to expand my network and meet people who may be interested in my services. I will be posting more projects as I complete them, I am confident I can help you if you are looking to take the next step with your company's web presence to move beyond static 2D sites.
