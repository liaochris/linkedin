Ron Suarez, Ph.D.’s Summary

Loud Feed's mission is to create a new middle class of musicians.

Loud Feed "Gets Your Music Heard" and provides tools for independent music labels and emerging artists to grow their business.

In 2007, Loud Feed provided consulting services to Tunecore.com and wrote the code which enables delivery of music and related metadata into Apple iTunes, Amazon MP3, Emusic.com, Rhapsody and other online retailers.

In 2008, Loud Feed launched new products:

1) DIGITAL MUSIC STORE TOOLS AND WIDGETS
Loud Feed offers both sales and promotional tools. We launched MyReggae.com with Gary Himelfarb of Tafari Music. Loud Feed is also offers sales "widgets" that can easily be copied and pasted into blogs and web sites.

2) DIGITAL MUSIC PROMOTION TOOLS AND DIGITAL ALBUMS
Promotional widgets carry feeds of music data and metadata. Dick Huey of Toolshed.biz will also uses Loud Feed for his digital media strategy consulting business. Gary Himelfarb and Dick Huey are both Sound Exchange Board members. We built the original Toolshed.biz web site in 2005 and since then have been creating new digital media promotional tools to make them available at low cost.
