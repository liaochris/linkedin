Elianne Ramos’s Summary

A seasoned professional, Elianne has 13 years of Copywriting, Editing, Translation, Public Speaking and TV Commercial Production experience for premier clients such as Procter and Gamble, Panasonic, Anheuser Busch, SlimFast and HSBC Bank, among others.

Her specialty is combining her passion for writing with a superior understanding of the US Hispanic and Latin American markets, as related to culture, influences & traditions.

An avid traveler who has lived in the United States, Latin America and Europe, Elianne believes that insights derived from cross-cultural experiences ultimately produce more effective communications. As a frequent speaker, she’s taken her industry insights to international marketing conferences in the United States and abroad.

In addition to TV, Radio and other media, her writing has appeared in the 2007 English edition of Chicken Soup for the Network Marketing Soul, and she’s currently editing the Spanish version of the book "El Camino, The Journey", by Angelo Solera.

Elianne is Spanish-native, English fluent, Italian proficient.
