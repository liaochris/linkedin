Chuck Mulberg’s Summary

After 20 years of industry experience Charles created Mulberg & Associates PLLC with the single purpose of helping small to mid-size companies succeed in building their business through providing the tools necessary to make better management decisions. His experience comes from a wide range of industries including Aviation, Technology, Retail, Services, and others. Charles draws on the experiences he gained working with small to large size companies and uses the knowledge to design the right strategy for each of his clients.
Charles is a Certified Public Accountant licensed in the state of Washington and holds a Master of Business Administration degree. He serves as a board member and Past President of his local chapter of the Washington Society of CPAs (WSCPA) with over 500 chapter members and was recognized as the Chapter President of the year by the WSCPA for 2006. He enjoys helping other CPAs and his local community.
Also in 2006, Charles completed his first Ironman distance triathlon in twelve and a half hours. Charles knows the passion that drives business and understands that just like a athletic coach can help you succeed with athletic goals, Charles coaches his clients to successfully reach their business objectives. All he asks is that his clients bring the passion necessary to make it happen. Charles will provide you the tools necessary to take your business to the next level and beyond.

Chuck Mulberg’s Specialties:

Assisting small to mid-size companies from various industries with adding value to their businesses through Business Coaching, Management & Consulting, Tax & Accounting, and Training Seminars & Workshops. Building Better Business.
