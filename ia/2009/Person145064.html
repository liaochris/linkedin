Luis Fitch’s Summary

UNO
Luis Fitch – Founder and Creative Director

One of the most esteemed and sought after Hispanic creative directors,
Luis has lead award-winning branding efforts for top 500 fortune companies including: Target Corp., Mervyn’s, Frito-Lay, Nash-Finch Company, Josten’s, Kimberly-Clark, and MTV Latino.

His prevalent work is profiled in international design and marketing publications around the world. Luis is a proud member of the Advisory Council Group for Diversity for The American Institute of Graphic Arts in Minnesota.

His work has been awarded by the prestigious Peter Glen Special Award for Public Service, highly recognized by the Retail Advertising & Marketing Association and was featured in Graphic Design USA 500th Issue Special People to Watch in 2006.

Luis Fitch’s Specialties:

Hispanic Advertising, Brand strategy, Event branding, Package design, Brand identity, Retail design, Private label branding, Brand revitalization, Corporate identity, Promotions, Brand extensions, Holistic brand relationship, Brand harmonization, New product development, Traditional advertising, Brand architecture, Guidelines, Guerrilla marketing, Brand environments, Print and print systems.
