Pavan Thatha’s Summary

After graduating from National Institute of Technology(NIT) Warangal with a Roll of Honor in year 2004, joined Philips Bangalore as a Software Engineer. During the stint at philips, primarily worked in software application framework project across all the phases of Software Development Life Cycle in Embedded systems Domain.

Also actively involved in a project of Philips which aimed at understanding the Indian Rural Market. Did Product Feature Analysis, Market Research and managed partnerships with NGO's for launching new innovative products in Rural India.

After two years of professional experience joined SJMSOM, IIT Bombay to get exposure about the business fundamentals and Entrepreneurship. Was actively involved in consulting small organizations and startups.

After completion of MBA took up the Project Management role in Photon Infotech (internet consulting firm) and handled projects on various cutting edge technologies including Mobile Application Development in iPhone, Blackberry, Symbian and Windows Mobile Platforms, Web 2.0 Application Development including development of applications on Facebook Platform. Also involved in various consulting roles by providing analysis of global smartphone market and providing roadmap and strategy to adopt for Facebook Application Development.

Held leadership positions at various capacities through out the years in education and professional life.
