Lisa Concepcion - Giassa’s Summary

After 15 years working for top PR agencies, I decided to take my experience and form my own consultancy. In May 2009, LCG Communications was born (www.lcg2.com). Six weeks later, I was in the green room at the Today Show with my client. In 15 years I was only able to get a Today Show hit ONCE and it was at the at the window. Six weeks into my own business and I scored a sit down couch interview for my client with Ann Curry. This led to my client having the biggest sale day in the history of his company, not a shabby way to start off.

That being said, I seek to represent brands and companies with a strong product or service but who may not necessarily be able to afford a larger PR firm. There are plenty of great small businesses out there who may have never done PR but can certainly benefit from the discipline. These are the companies I hunt for. These are the companies that benefit most from what I offer, bare bones no glitz no glam publicity.

My gift is looking at a product and then looking for ways to make it interesting to media, then getting them to cover the product in a meaningful way to raise awareness, perhaps drive sales and create buzz.

So who is a match for LCG Communications? Well people who have a clear vision for where they see their company headed and know how to pounce on opportunities to meet their goals.

If you know anyone who needs top notch media strategy, big agency thinking and someone who can take their business to a whole other level then send them my way. There are tons of cool and affordable tactics we can implement including:
Editor Events
Media Outreach
Blog Outreach
Sampling & Consumer Events
and more...

Specialty areas include beauty, personal care, consumer health, travel/leisure, food and beverage.

For more info visit www.lcg2.com
