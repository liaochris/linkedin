Anne McCrossan’s Summary

I develop robust innovation strategies to support corporate reputations and the way organizations take themselves to market.

Visceral Business offers a full-service approach to social business design and management. Visceral Business are socially calibrated to perform; they have a dynamic pulse. They’re able to develop powerful experiences in conjunction with their audiences that have the capacity to move people to contribute to the development of the business. Critical business processes can be streamlined, costs reduced, and organizations can be aligned with their ecosystem of stakeholders and customers by developing social leadership and influencing skills and strategies for inbound marketing.

My background covers extensive experience across a range of marketing disciplines including business model generation, new product development, brand strategy, advertising, internal/investor communications and social media and marketing. I've led a range of blue chip and complex brand change and multi-channel marketing communications programmes with some of the world’s most successful companies and worked with a wide variety of global clients across a diverse range of industry sectors.

In July 2008 I became one of the founder members of www.triiibes.com, a private think tank created by Seth Godin. I’m also a co-creator of the Business Model Generation Handbook and the author of the Change This manifesto ‘Cracking the Genetic Code: A New Way Forward for Organizations’.

I’m also the Brand Communications and Community Trustee of the Child’s i Foundation, which won the 2009 Vodafone World of Difference Award as an innovative charity setting a benchmark for the next generation of non-profits.

My blog ‘A Bit Visceral' covers neuroeconomics and psychology, organizational identity, leadership, brand management, corporate social responsibility, social media marketing and corporate and consumer behaviour.

Anne McCrossan’s Specialties:

Organizational change management, facilitation and consensus-building, executive coaching, brand marketing, internal and external engagement, social marketing, community organization.
