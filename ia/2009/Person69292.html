Elizabeth Davis, CPA, CMA’s Summary

Over 10 years experience in a small business performing the Controller and CFO functions. Managed an accounting department which grew to a total of 4.5 FTE. I also have been part of the Executive Team, which has served as a strategic planning and steering team for the company.

In 2009 Elizabeth Davis transitioned away from Short’s Travel Management and launched Davis CPA Solutions, LLC where she is a sole proprietor providing accounting solutions for small businesses and individuals.

Elizabeth Davis earned her BA in Accounting from the University of Northern Iowa in 1998. She is both CPA (Certified Public Accountant) and CMA (Certified Management Accountant) certified. In 2000 Elizabeth Davis became a member of the Institute of Management Accountants. She has served in several board positions in the local Des Moines Chapter since becoming a member. The local Chapter holds monthly technical meetings where members are able to network with peers as well as participate in an educational session earning CPE credits. Davis is currently serving as Chapter President for the years 2008 – 2010.
