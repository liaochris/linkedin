Chinna Reddy Katireddy’s Summary

Chinna is a SAP FICO / FICA certified consultant, working as a Senior Consultant in the, Insurance, Utility, Public Sector and the Cross Industry Solutions practice at AXON. He is involved in a wide range of consulting assignments with expertise in FICA (IS-U, PSCD and FS-CD) and FI-CO modules of SAP. He has over 13 years of working experience of which 9 years of experience are in SAP Implementation and operations.

He has ASAP implementation experience in FICA and FICO, Sub Modules such as FICA – Master Data, FICA - Account Management, FICA - Payments, FICA - Dunning & Collections, FICA – Periodic Processing including Batch Automation, FI-General Ledger, FI-Accounts Receivable, FI-Accounts Payable, FI-Asset Accounting, FI – Cash and Funds Management, FI – Special purpose ledger, CO-Cost Center Accounting, CO-Profit Center Accounting, CO - Profitability Analysis, CO – Product Costing. He is having good exposure in integration with other modules FI-MM, FI-SD and FI-AM, working knowledge on MM & SD module configuration, SAP Report Writer and Report Painter. He is also trained on CRM and ISMW. He has implementation and operations experience in SAP in Public Sector and Manufacturing Industries. In addition, Mr. Chinna has over 4 years of functional and technical experience in systems development, Financial Accounting, Costing, Budgeting and Auditing
