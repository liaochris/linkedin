Scott Spiegel’s Summary

Market research analyst and senior project director with experience conducting multiple types of quantitative and qualitative projects and generating revenue from custom research.

* Custom quantitative work
* Custom qualitative work
* Strategic planning and competitive intelligence
* Product development, brand building, advertising
* Project and budget management
* Client and staff management
* Global marketing experience
* Knowledge of SPSS syntax and scripts

General skills: Ability to handle high project load. Extensive experience with national and international clients. Focused and detailed-oriented. Strong qualitative and quantitative analysis skills and writing/presentation skills.

Specific skills: Microsoft Office 2007: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, FrontPage, Access; SPSS: 16 years’ experience; Acrobat; ATLAS qualitative software; EndNote referencing software; FTP; HTML; SNAP survey programming software.

The aspects of market research I enjoy most are (1) learning about a client’s needs/goals, and helping them design research that will answer their questions and fulfill their needs; (2) examining project results to detect patterns and induce broader findings that help clients make sense of large, complicated datasets; and (3) discovering unexpected results that motivate clients to reexamine assumptions about the marketplace and their products/services.
