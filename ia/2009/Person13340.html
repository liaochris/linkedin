Andrea McBride’s Experience

  • Founder and CEO

    eco.love

    (Wine and Spirits industry)

    January 2007 — Present (2 years 4 months)

    eco.love wine brand is a key supporter of the environment. eco.love's philosophy is to produce value wine with minimal environmental impact. Commitment to this philosophy is apparent through all aspects of the company's business from:
    -Habitat Restoration
    -Insulated Warehousing
    -Waste Water Recycle System
    -Heat Recovery
    -Organic Fertilizer
    -Recycling
    -Sustainable Growing
    -CarboNZero certified

    "eco.love is the true expression of the passion we have for wine and our global environment. With our dedication to sustainable practices, our wines have been hand crafted to the best standards, while keeping in tune with nature. Made by the worlds first CarboNZero winery".

    Care of the environment is the long-term future; The global financial crisis is the short-term challenge.

  • CEO and Marketing Officer

    Lineage Imports LLC

    (Wine and Spirits industry)

    January 2006 — Present (3 years 4 months)

    Lineage Imports LLC sources the most distinguished wines from New Zealand. The selection of these wines are regional specific sustainable producers, that represent the terroir of each unique micro-climate from around the North and South Islands. The significance of each offering is that it represents the transition from New World wine region to focused elegant wine producer.
    Many products in the Lineage portfolio are certified CarboNZero and registered with the United Nations Climate Neutral Network. We encourage all the companies we work with to manage their energy efficiency and work with Landcare Research New Zealand to become certified. We are proud to introduce to the US market the worlds first CarboNZero Wine and bottled water company.

    St Lukes Estate Wines Ltd

    (Wine and Spirits industry)

    January 2004 — Present (5 years 4 months)

    Premier Grape Grower of New Zealand
    Years of dedication to the art and science of viticulture have earned Saint Lukes the reputation as one of New Zealand’s foremost grape cultivators. Renowned for mature vineyard holdings in the Wairau Valley and ambitious vineyard and irrigation developments in the Awatere Valley of Marlborough.

    Exclusive Wines from Exquisite Grapes
    All Saint Lukes Estates Wines embody the company founders’ incomparable dedication to viticulture and winemaking, most notably our highly acclaimed sub-regional Sauvignon Blanc from Marlborough. Saint Lukes’ master winemakers meticulously cultivate and hand select our vineyards’ finest grapes—grapes that consistently yield distinguished wine worthy of our premium label. Our spirit of innovation and relentless pursuit of excellence compel us to pursue new ways to elevate the winemaking craft and enhance the taste experience and memorability of every offering in our elite collection.
