Jonathan Heinl’s Summary

I'm a Phoenician seasoned internet marketing and advertisement with a background in sales & customer service. I began my working career at an early age as a sales professional which lead into a customer service manager position at Phase 2 Solutions (now eTelecare Global Solutions) in Phoenix, AZ.
