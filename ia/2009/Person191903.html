radRounds Radiology Network’s Summary

radRounds Radiology Network was founded based on the time honored tradition of "Radiology Rounds" radRounds is a new tool for education, professional networking, and collaboration - restricted to radiologists and medical imaging professionals.

radRounds Radiology Network is analogous to general social networking sites (like Facebook, LinkedIn, or Sermo) but restricted to your professional life and focused on all things medical imaging. radRounds is the largest and fastest growing professional social network in radiology.

• Connect with colleagues and build professional network in radiology
• Find and share interesting cases (unlimited storage space)
• Find answers and advice
• Form an online group for your society, practice, and department
• Seek unpublished job and career opportunities
• Stay up to date via News, Radiology Tools, and Resources

radRounds Radiology Network’s Specialties:

All specialties of radiology; the premier social media platform for radiology and medical imaging. We have members from all specialties of radiology including expertise in neuroradiology, thoracic radiology, abdominal imaging, interventional radiology, women's imaging, mammography, MSK, nuclear medicine, MRI, CT, ultrasound, PACS, medical informatics, management, and health policy.
