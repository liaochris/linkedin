Stacy Meyn’s Summary

The saying goes: “those who can’t do, teach.” However, from what I have seen in others who have taught me, that is a gross misconception. Every instructor and trainer worth their salt has been more than capable of actually performing the duties related to their curriculum, and I was fortunate to be under their tutelage. Through various positions at my current job, I have expanded my career curriculum and helped students expand theirs. I have made efforts to improve the work environment as well as employee performance.

Yet by continuing my formal education, I hope to awaken and utilize dormant skills, gain new ones, improve my efficiency, and learn to filter disparate information into usable formats for my students to hone their own abilities. I need to “train up” for the next step in my career, as it is likely to be somewhere that requires me to have a more developed skills set. We are what we know, and the day any of us stop learning may as well be our last. It has become more apparent than ever that I am not done yet.
