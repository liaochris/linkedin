Navamani Geymeier’s Summary

As a Fashion Designer, I observe and analyse what surrounds me to grasp the trends and the innovations that come from the society and use them to inspire my inventive process.

The skills I have been refining through my studies and professional experiences allow me to take part in the different phases of a collection such as concept development, fabric sampling, making of toile, sketching and technical drawings.

I am a passionate designer with good interpersonal relations, especially within a team that I consider as an opportunity to exchange ideas, experiences and to improve the creative process.
