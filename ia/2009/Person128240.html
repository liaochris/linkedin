Katy Evans-Bush’s Summary

It’s not just fairy dust! To get your message across to the people you want to reach – that is, your audience – you have to know how to say what you mean.

As a freelance copywriter and editor, blogger, communications consultant, critic, journalist and poet, I know that “all the fun’s in how you say a thing.” I’ve honed my skills over many years of writing for different purposes and different clients. I’m well-versed in how to use words in different ways, for different purposes, to achieve different goals.

I can write formal business prose, engaging consumer copy or funny stories. I can produce your annual report, advertorial or service user magazine. I can write SEO copy, press releases, or a communications strategy.

I’m available for contract work or one-off jobs.

In addition to writing, I offer mentoring, advice, and social networking assistance. I can help you get started on Twitter or Facebook, or help you reach more readers with your blog. I can ghost-write your blog.

I also offer poetry workshops and one-to-one poetry mentoring.

My poetry collection, Me and the Dead, is published by Salt Publishing and I write reviews, critical essays and blogs for publications in the UK and the United States - most recently The London Magazine and The Guardian.

My literary blog, Baroque in Hackney, is regularly cited as one of the top book blogs and is listed in Mediadisk.

I am a full member of the Chartered Institute of Public Relations and the National Union of Journalists.

I live in Stoke Newington, London, and have three children.
