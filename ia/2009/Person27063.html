Bernhard Aggeler’s Summary

I see myself as global in every sense.

Growing up and travelling all over the world, I have developed a multicultural background allowing me to quickly adapt to my environment.

Also from a professional point of view I gained competences in different areas from working in different positions and areas:

After completing the KV Business School in Switzerland I collected experiences in IT support, training and up to software design.

As CEO of a phone marketing company I got insights and experiences in management, administration and sales.

I co-founded and managed a company with a local and an offshore development team providing services for corporate clients like UBS, Xerox, Ringier and more.

After developing an event/project management software for UBS I expanded my expertise and experience in sales, event marketing and project management. In this period I trained corporate professionals all over Europe in how to measure the ROI for events. Clients among others were Accenture, Ernst & Young, Novartis, Kodak, Siemens.

In 2008 I developed both, a concept and prototype for a portal platform for 20+ Mio sport members, clubs and associations for the DOSB (Deutscher Olympischer Sport Bund, the German Olympic association) and managed the project to develop and build a large international business community platform for WTCM (World Trade Center Marketing), a project connecting 316 World Trade Centres and their 1 Mio+ members.

Since the financial crisis I am looking forward to join a new project or organization that share the view that we need to build a future based on sustainable and cooperative concepts for our business and our society.
