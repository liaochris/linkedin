Scott Thomas’s Summary

Scott Thomas is the professional services manager at CDW Berbee in charge of the Cisco practice for the Chicago office. He leads the CDW Cisco services team focusing on Cisco infrastructure planning, design, and deployment. The team’s primary focus surrounds IP telephony, enterprise call center, wireless, and route/switch & security technologies. His responsibilities include the management of project managers and engineering staff, project delivery, client project satisfaction, contract negotiation, sales forecasting, and budget fulfillment.

Prior to CDW Berbee, he was the Global Collaboration Senior Manager and Enterprise Architect for Baxter Healthcare. He was the leader of an Enterprise Architecture and Operations team managing its 32,000-seat infrastructure as well as setting the strategic collaboration roadmap. Mr. Thomas was also a Director within the Infrastructure Integration Solutions group for marchFIRST (Whittman-Hart). He was responsible for the design and review of client e-architectures. Mr. Thomas was also a Senior Project Manager within the Infrastructure Technologies practice at Price Waterhouse.

Since 1992, Mr. Thomas has led a distinguished career within the information technology community. He has served as the architectural project and sales manager on many highly visible clients. He is also the Senior Manager responsible for international teams running 24x7 systems. Mr. Thomas has published one of the definitive books on Lotus Notes System Administration & Architecture as well as the number one best selling Lotus Notes Certification Exam Guide, both published by McGraw-Hill. Mr. Thomas also wrote a monthly IT security column for e-Promag magazine published by Penton Publishing.
