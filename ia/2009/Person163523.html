Midori Connolly’s Summary

Pulse Staging is proud to be the nation's first provider of green(er) A/V. We are responsible for creating the first set of guidelines for sustainable A/V staging in the United States.
We are currently pursuing our BS8901 certification (Environmental Management System for Events) and will be the first supplier in the United States to do so!
I love educating and training so I've found I just naturally empower our clients with my very personal, educational and FUN approach.
As a frequent speaker, my latest ventures include:
Writing a bi-monthly column on Green AV for ProAV Magazine;
Platinum Speaker for MPI on the topic of Strategic CSR,
Co-Chairing the APEX Green Meeting Standards for AV;
InfoComm Green AV Task Force;
Teaching "Meeting Responsibly" course on Green Meetings and Events at CSU San Marcos;
Writing a monthly AV Girl advice column for PlanYourMeetings.com

Midori Connolly’s Specialties:

Green Meetings, Consulting, Hybrid Meetings, Live Event Streaming, Webcasting, Content Archiving and Cataloging, Sustainable Events, Sustainable A/V, Event Design, Audio Visual Production, Audio Visual Rentals, Corporate Social Responsibility, Speaking, Writing, Marketing.
