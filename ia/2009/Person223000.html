Stephanie Whittier’s Summary

Working at Orchard Trails, a student housing complex under American Campus Communities, I have had the opportunity to work with great people in a professional environment; something I had never experienced before with my summer waitressing job and my part time job at the mall.

At the beginning of May I was hired as a marketing intern for Day's Jewelers. This summer I will be working to meet several different objectives as an intern and as a social marketer.

My career goals are never-ending. I plan to graduate from the University of Maine with a degree in Journalism with a minor in Public Relations. After that I am looking to land a great job right away and get to work! Ideally, I want to be in a bigger place, preferably a city like Boston or New York. My long term goal is to be in a position where I can lead, but also work with a team in a fun, organized and successful environment.

Currently, I am very much looking forward to starting my first internship this summer! As a college student having just finished my sophomore year, I think it is important to begin interning sooner rather than later in order to accomplish my goals.
