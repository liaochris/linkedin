Gaynor Bramall’s Summary

I am responsible for new business accounts for idibu, idibu is a multi-posting software launched in February 2007. After an excellent first year and a half of business we have become one of the leading advert distribution systems in the UK and Europe.

idibu lets you broadcast adverts out to over 400 job boards, provides detailed reporting information via its iTrack Applicant Tracking System and is used by a large number of agencies through out the UK and Europe.

We are a young business, keen to talk to other businesses and individuals about collaborations and partnerships.If you would like to know more about our software or simply price compare, please feel free to contact me on 0800 311 2750 or you can email me gaynor@idibu.com.
