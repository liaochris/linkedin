Melanie Haiken’s Summary

After many many years as a magazine editor, I've now spent more than eight years on the other side of the desk. I wear many hats: health writer, business writer, travel writer, science writer. Most recent stories have appeared in: Health, Fortune Small Business, Fitness, Yoga Journal, and the late lamented Business 2.0.

More and more, I help corporate clients like Adobe, Wells Fargo, and Kaiser Permanente tell their stories: how an innovative product or service is making a difference for a client. White papers and case studies a specialty.

As a health writer, I can "translate" the latest studies and pharmaceutical news so it's meaningful for both consumers and medical professionals. My in-depth knowledge of health and medicine allows me to generate top-quality copy for Websites and newsletters for hospitals, medical practice groups, and HMOs.

I also spend a good deal of my time overseeing the Cancer Channel and other health content on http://www.caring.com/, a new website (launched in 2008) for those of us caring for our aging parents and other family members.

My latest passion is reigniting my focus on travel, with numerous stories (on New Orleans, Nashville, San Francisco, Santa Fe, the Grand Canyon, and the Bahamas) in http://family.go.com/travel/ and www.naturaltraveler.com. Focused on placing green travel, adventure travel, and family travel stories this year.

Melanie Haiken’s Specialties:

Business and finance writing; research and investigative reporting; health reporting; science writing; profile writing; long narrative; the ability to tell stories.
