Ivana Taylor’s Summary

It's all about getting chosen. To grow your business today, you have to become the obvious choice for your customer.

My process takes the passion you have for your business, helps you identify your ideal customer, figure out what's important to to them when they are buying what you sell and then sets up the turnkey system to consistently delivery happy, appreciative customers that you love to do business with that love to do business with you.

Typical Services include -

Qualitative and Quantitative research - that uncovers information your customers simply haven't shared and discovers opportunities for growth.

Marketing Strategy Development - which converts this information into actionable projects.

Marketing System Implementation - Turns the projects into your customized turnkey customer-generating and retaining ystem.

Ivana Taylor’s Specialties:

Third Force specializes in making our clients the obvious choice for their ideal customers.

Our clients include:

Manufacturing companies who have either lost a large customer or whose largest customers have reduced their orders.

Services companies who are dedicated to being the very best at what they do and who consistently want to be the provider of choice for their clients.
