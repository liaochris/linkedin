Nicki Muir PhD’s Summary

I accept freelance work from a number of sources including, International Science Writer, run by Dr Sue Pearson, a freelance science writer specialising in biotechnology, who also writes regularly for GEN covering topics, such as protein and DNA-based therapeutics, bioprocess and automated drug discovery systems, as well as writing for a number of international biotech and life science companies. I also accept work from Elsevier, writing news articles based on primary peer review papers for Materials Today. Freelance science writing allows me to utilise my science background, together with my excellent writing and editing skills to effectively communicate science-related stories to the public and enhance science understanding.
