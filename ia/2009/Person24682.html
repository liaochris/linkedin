Bart Schrooten’s Summary

Hi, I am an ambitious entrepreneur and business developer, born in The Netherlands, living in Finland, working where ever there is an internet connection.

Currently developing an online application in cooperation with the University of Helsinki and industry partners. It's a social service trading platform which enables online business collaboration.

My experience comes from various sectors (IT, Telco, New Media), negotiating on executive level (both as seller and buyer) and working with some of the brightest and innovative people in business. My goal is developing new ideas in to profitable businesses while having fun on the way!

In my free time I enjoy good food, rock climbing, traveling, spending time with my wife and dog.

Bart Schrooten’s Specialties:

developing people and organizations with a commercial and global mindset, mobile and web 3.0 technologies and services, keep a positive and go-for-it attitude!
