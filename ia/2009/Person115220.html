Jodi Cisman’s Summary

I have been working on newspapers since I was a freshman in high school in the year 2000. My professional experience includes staff writer on The Challenge, editor on The Voice and administrative beat reporter on The State Press. I have since been published as a freelance journalist in The Blue Guitar magazine, PHOENIX Magazine and West Valley Magazine.
