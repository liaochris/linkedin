Chaz Freutel’s Summary

I started Get-U-Connected to help individuals and businesses to get connected to new business opportunities. I have been blessed to associate with many of our regions leaders, and have assembled a package of products and services to support entrepreneurs.

One of my clients, The "DEC" Dublin Entrepreneurial Center, serves small business entrepreneurs by providing affordable office space, business development training, and networking opportunities. I am privileged to serve as the facility's site manager.

I recently purchased The Columbus Region GREENPages business directory. We look to grow this publication to broadly serve the green business and consumer community.

I am also priviledged to be a Staff Coach for the Ohio Premier Eagles Soccer Club, and serve The Fellowship of Christian Athletes in northwest Central Ohio. I have a passion to see kids inspired through sports and be introduced to Jesus Christ through the ministry of FCA.
