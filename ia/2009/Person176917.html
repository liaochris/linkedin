Ernesto Lopez’s Summary

I am a Seattle-based graphic designer. I currently work at Washington Mutual Bank (WaMu) / JPMorgan Chase, as a Graphic Designer III. I work with the company’s internal clients leading design processes from conceptualization to production and implementation for both print and rich media projects.

At Meridian Group and for independent contracts, I have primarily worked for corporate clients. Prior to working at Meridian Group of Companies, I interned for Lemley Design Company and for the Seattle Sonics and Storm. I gained invaluable experience and inspiration from these positions.

I was honored with the Art Institute of Seattle Best Portfolio Award in 2005. I served as an assistant for the director of public relations at The Art Institute, where I applied my graphic design, layout and organizational abilities to set up various gallery shows and create promotional campaigns and events. Independently, I have developed logos, identity systems, brochures, promotional pieces, signage and website interfaces for various businesses, organizations and individuals.

My creative and technical qualifications, experience, my education and background in architecture enhanced my design skills, and appreciation for precision, craft and the creative process. I am a motivated and flexible worker who builds rapport with clients and adheres to strict deadlines.

Ernesto Lopez’s Specialties:

Logo design, identity systems, web design, advertisements, publication design, brochures, CD covers, posters, signage and packaging.
