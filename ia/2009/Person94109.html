1997 - 2002

5 years of expertise as a Training Engineer. I have trained people worldwide on technical topics related to the Alcatel-Lucent product portfolio, from Call Handling to IP Next Generation Networks. Other training topics included General Telecommunications, The World of Internet, Adobe Flash, Video Editing and Creativity.

I taught Alcatel-Lucent employees and customers in Singapore, China, Malaysia, New-Zealand, Europe and Saoudi Arabia,

*

Next to the day-to-day training activities I developed a strong passion for e-learning and community learning.

*

Team Leader for the integration and implementation of streaming video applications to support e-learning across the company. I have set-up an automated system for recording, editing and publishing presentations into a rich user application that could be used on every browser. We developed both a client-side product for automatic recording and annotation of live and on-demand recordings and a server-side application for managing and consuming the recorded presentations. This home-brew video recording and management system is still very popular at Alcatel-Lucent after 6 years.

I was involved with new product concepts at the early beginning through to the first rapid prototype.

I am passionate about anthropology and co-design or how to translate real user needs in useful and fun applications.
