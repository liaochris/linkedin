William "Bill" Campbell’s Summary

Born and raised in Queens, New York. He graduated Brooklyn Technical High School and worked in downtown Manhattan for ten years. Having migrated to South Florida in December of 1985, Bill has been a resident of Broward County for over 24 years. Bill became licensed as a Florida Real Estate Salesperson in 2001. He then obtained Graduate Realtor Institute designation-GRI 2004 and his Broker’s license 2005. As an investor, Bill has personally purchased and managed his own residential rental properties in Broward County for 11+ years.

Bill is also licensed by the FAA as a Private Pilot and A&P Mechanic. He enjoys motorcycling, boating, and restoring old cars, especially Corvettes. Bill is also a proud member of Aircraft Owners and Pilots Association (AOPA) and the Florida Aero Club.

William "Bill" Campbell’s Specialties:

Bill proudly maintains the highest quality ethics and attention to detail in every thing he does. These characteristics help enable his customers to have a well informed and successful transaction.
