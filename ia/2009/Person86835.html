Ginger Dodds’s Summary

For over eight years at Shaker Recruitment Advertising and Communications, I have been working with my client base to develop and implement strategic advertising campaigns for their recruitment needs. In addition to recommending specific media for their open positions, I coordinate with my copywriting and creative teams to ensure my clients' message is consistent with their employer brand.
