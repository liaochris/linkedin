Gary Smith’s Summary

Gary uses his training with Dale Carnegie Corporation, Zig Ziglar Corporation and 37 years of sales experience to significantly contribute to the revenue and profit of any venture.
From his earliest years, Gary was taught to sell, and to excel: an eagle scout, Junior Achievement's President of the Year in Los Angeles and Dallas, and graduated from college magna cum laude.
Gary has been a top performer for the organizations he’s been privileged to serve: Nine Systems, TBN, Verizon, Marriage Plus Ministries, Church on the Rock - North, and Word of Faith Bible College and Satellite Network.
As a pastor and ministry leader, Gary and his wife Patti have been Singles Pastor, Marriage and Single Seminar teachers and assistant pastors at three churches over 18 years.
Today, Gary leads Trumpeteer Corp - lowering clients’ costs with their people and facilities, enhancing wellness programs, providing speakers and trainers for corporate events, lowering corporate electricity rates and providing top-tier resources for business construction (new builds, finish out and green converions).
