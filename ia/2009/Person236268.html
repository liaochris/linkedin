Tom Parish’s Summary

Results-driven manager with extensive Web 2.0 experience leading people and project teams, implementing and overseeing strategic social media, multi-media and IT projects in a diverse enterprise environment. Passionate about team-building and motivating others to be creative and engaged with a special focus on new media content development and distribution and building community online and offline. A proven thought-leader and technologist in the area of social media strategies, social media production and deep experience with SEO/SEM tools. Highly experienced with open source solutions and CMS implementations using international teams. 18 years of success in planning and directing activities that provide innovative technology and people-oriented solutions for small, medium and enterprise scale applications for improving internal processes, as well as external marketing programs to improve customer relations. Excellent team building and negotiating skills and adept at engaging in a positive manner with ambiguity to discern and identify mission critical objectives and respond appropriately. Posses global project experience, internationally traveled, and culturally sensitive. Direct experience and management of community-based solutions and skills with 10 + years of experience with online communities. Hands-on experience with nearly every type of Social Media Technology and Web 2.0++ Strategy, ranging from Blogs, Podcasts, Vlogs, Streaming Media, CDN, Micro-Blogging, Forums, User-Gen Content, Social Communities, RSS, Wikis, aggregators and social tagging. Deep knowledge of Social Psychology, Web Trends, effective Grassroots Viral Marketing, ebb and flow of Social Graph. Experienced in MS and Linux based server environments with a focus on disaster recovery planning. Engaging verbal Communication skills with proven Technical and Best Practice process
problem solving.
