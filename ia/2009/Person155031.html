Mathias Lin’s Summary

Mathias Lin, born 1978 in Hamburg/Germany, is founder and managing director of SYSVISION Limited (Hong Kong) as well as Vice President, Products & Technologies at SYSVISION USA, Inc.

Mathias' technological focus lies on Java Enterprise (Java EE) technologies. He is an open source enthusiast with a strong technical background.
Currently he is researching the demands of healthcare information systems, especially in the Greater China area, and also supporting open source projects in the healthcare field.

After receiving his high school diploma in California, USA in 1996 and in Germany in 1998 he established his own e-business/new media company together with two partners, which he was successfully managing for 5 years (1998-2003) beside his studies of business informatics at the University of Hamburg. By the age of 20 was one of the youngest entrepreneurs of the Hamburg IT industry and is socially well connected to decision makers and other enterpreneurs in the IT/new media industry in Hamburg and Hong Kong.

In 01/2004 he joined SYSVISION GmbH which he served as managing director until 12/2005. The company works for national and international customers such as Lufthansa, Bayer, China Telecom, Unilever, Dräger, META Group (later acquired by Gartner), Philips, Atos Origin. Core business is IT consulting, data integration, legacy modernization, software engineering.
In 12/2005 he established the Hong Kong branch SYSVISION Limited.

During the past ten years as a business professional he gained profound practical experience in software engineering, project management as well as managerial experience.
He has strong organisational, communication and interpersonal skills combined with the ability to deal with and adapt quickly to new situations, locations and people. He is a good linguist with extensive project experience.

Mathias Lin’s Specialties:

Software engineering, data integration, middleware, legacy modernization, open source, OpenCms, Java/JEE development, business-critical applications, tailor-made ERP/CRM/SCM solutions, openEHR, EMR, EHR, HL7, HIS.
