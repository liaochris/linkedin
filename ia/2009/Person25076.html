BBK Engineering Limited’s Summary

Beam Services has been established for some 20 years providing expert engineering and knowledge in bespoke waste processing and handling of equipment.

We undertake the installation, repair and servicing of various waste disposal, waste recycling, screw technology and other plant equipment supplied by different manufacturer’s. We also undertake project management contracts for the installation of new, or removal of end of life equipment for many respectable manufacturers and companies requiring our recycling expertise.

Take a look at our website for an overview of what services and products we have to offer. www.beam-services.co.uk
