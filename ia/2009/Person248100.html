Will Iverson’s Summary

Will Iverson is the Development Manager and Enterprise Architect for All Star Directories.

Previously, Will served as the General Manager for Radiant Machine, the entertainment group at SolutionsIQ, a Pacific Northwest Consulting and Staffing firm. Before that, he served as the Practice Director at SolutionsIQ, where he built the custom software professional services team from apx 35 to 85 developers, SDETs, and project managers.

Before SolutionsIQ, Will Iverson served as Developer Relations Manager for the VisualCafé group at Symantec, as the Java & Runtimes Product Manager at Apple Computer and led Cascade Technology Group, a private consulting firm, from 1999 through 2004. He has spoken at a variety of technical conferences including JavaOne, MacWorld, ComDex, and more on behalf of a variety of companies including Symantec, Apple Computer, Sun Microsystems, and Canal+ Technologies. He is the author of four books, including "Mac OS X for Java Geeks," "Real World Web Services," "Hibernate: A J2EE Developer's Guide," and "Jakarta Commons."

Will Iverson’s Specialties:

Extensive work with Java, developer tools, and developer marketing. Current role involves work around SEO/SEM.
