Karen Loh’s Summary

I have extensive experience in global/regional/local marketing communications, which involves advertising and promotions (production of video/commercials, collaterals, interactive promotional websites, both above- and below-the-line work), events organisation, overhauling the brand architecture and corporate identity of the Singapore Airlines Group. Key projects promoted Singapore Airlines' various innovative seat/inflight entertainiment/inflight service products, especially on the B777-300ER and world's first commercial Airbus 380. I project-managed the Singapore Airlines A380 First Flight Charity Auction, a partnership with eBay and various other sponsorship organisations, which raised about S$2 million for global charity organisations in the span of 1-2 months. This was handled through singaporeair.com/a380, another key promotion channel online for the SIA A380.

I am currently involved in Air Services Agreement negotiations between the governments of Singapore and other countries. We may negotiate with countries from all over the world, from Argentina, France, Egypt, Oman, Nigeria to the Philippines, Bangladesh and Japan, to obtain traffic rights to land in and to overfly other countries. This involves frequent and regular aeropolitical and crosscultural dealings. My focus has been in the developing economies and emerging markets of Southeast Asia, Africa and Latin America, encompassing both passenger and cargo aviation issues.

Karen Loh’s Specialties:

Branding and corporate identity, international diplomacy, international aviation law/regulations, air services negotiations, marketing communications
