alberto pasqualucci’s Summary

, I am an Italian electronic engineer with more than seven years of experience in real estate project management.

I started my career as a project manager for a leading planning and implementation company, specialised in electrical, thermal and heating systems for residential buildings.

I finished an Executive MBA in 2008 in Milano, Italy. I became an asset manager in order to manage and develop real estate into profitable properties. Also, I am a member of The Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (RICS)

At present I am an assistent of CFO of Kreiamo spa, a global service company in real estate, where I manage a concept strategy, from business planning, feasibility and design to construction. I am fully accountable for project controls, managing in-house and consultant expertise, coordinating project progress and minimising investment risks to ensure quality end-products. I love what I do and have an extensive list of satisfied clients, and long-term relationships.

In the past few years, I was also the managing director of 2 engineering business units that help hone my ability to develop, implement and manage real estate assets on an industry-leading scale.
