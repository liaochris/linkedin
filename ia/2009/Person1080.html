Aaron Evans’s Summary

I'm an independent consultant specializing in software quality assurance and web development, with more than 10 years experience in the field.

My company, One Shore, offers open source testing solutions.

A "QA Site" is a managed test environment including tools for version control, documentation, test management, defect tracking, and build/deployment/test automation.

I also provide training in QA methods and testing tools.

I'm looking to network with other testers, developers, system administrators, and web designers.
