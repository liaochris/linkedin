Mike Dwyer’s Summary

Mike is currently a Senior Consultant within Aon's Organizational Performance & Implementation group. In his role, he consults human resources and corporate communications executives on ways to engage target audiences via social media. In his past roles Mike has focused his on- and offline word of mouth networking presence in business development (Fortune 500) and candidate recruitment (mid to senior level marketing executives). He is an avid user of LinkedIn, Twitter, Digg, Facebook and a growing list of other emerging technologies. He explores daily social media and how it builds his community network and life experiences.
