Nick Payne’s Summary

Nick Payne’s Specialties:

Graphic recording and facilitation enables teams of people to organise their thinking and create common understanding by exploiting the fact that the overwhelming majority of people are visual learners.

We can turn what may seem to be a chaotic situation into complexity, complexity into choices, choices into direction and ultimately positive decisions and actions.
