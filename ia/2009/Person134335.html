Kris de Leon’s Summary

I am a leading Green Building recruiter and Smart Grid recruiter at the Greenman Alliance. I enjoy my work since it combines both my passions for the Green Business movement and helping candidates find the ideal career opportunity. I'm a member of the Nevada chapter of the U.S. Green Building Council, and currently studying for the LEED GA exam.

In addition to recruiting, I provide clients with value added service, such as advising them on getting state and federal stimulus grants, tax credits, etc. I've connected my network of architects, construction, engineering firms, contractors, and manufacturers in the Green Building supply chain so they can identify new business opportunities. I'm also helping foreign companies find business partners in the U.S. so they can launch their green technology in this market.

If you are interested in exploring opportunities in the fast growing Green/Cleantech space, or looking to hire outstanding Green talent to add value to your business, feel free to contact me at kris@greenmanAlliance.com.

Some of the roles I recruit include:
- LEED AP (Engineers, Sales Executives)
- Certified Energy Manager (CEM)
- Commissioning Agents
- Building/Energy Modelers
- Energy Auditors
- Smart Grid Engineers
- Product Manager
- Project Managers
- Scientists / R&D

For the latest updates on job opportunities and industry trends, follow me on twitter http://twitter.com/krisdeleon
