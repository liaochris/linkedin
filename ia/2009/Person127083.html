Kasia Barbara Turajczyk’s Summary

I started painting and drawing as a child. At the age of thirteen I started taking art classes and training. Two years later I sold my first painting. I also wrote poems and short plays for my high school/college cabaret.

I studied art at private ateliers in Lodz in Poland and in Vienna in Austria.

My main specialisations at the high school were mathematics and physics (a lot of quantum physics)

I have a university degree in Slavic languages and literature, which I obtained in Lodz and Amsterdam. I have also studied Philosophy and did Postgraduate study in Management in Amsterdam.

Besides painting, photographing and writing, I have had different businesses in different fields, including advising non-profit and charitable organisations in the Netherlands and in other countries.

Since 2006 I am started to study "internet marketing' and after a two years trainings and courses I am a kind of expert in diverse areas of Internet Marketing.

I am busy at the moment with few diverse internet projects. Not only art and fantasy art, also ecodriving, environment, internet marketing.

One of my arty project is FantastyDragonArt.com - it's a multimedia project / book, ebook, videos, paintings, drawings, animation - a story about two unusual baby dragons from Nebula in the constellation of Orion. They landed per accident on our planet......C'est la vie!
