Francis Prévot’s Summary

Who am I?
An entrepreneur.
Above and beyond simple management, I enjoy using my imagination constructively, to create and develop projects and ideas. Leading and motivating people are key components of my professional life. Helping others become more successful through better management strategies motivates and energizes me.

How did I become who I am?
My earliest professional training was as a military air traffic controller – an unusual start to a career in business, but taking an alternate route has come to define me personally as well as professionally. During the following phase, I worked as a sales representative both for small business marketing industry and high end products, then as a network and system administrator. I quickly rose to management positions in this and related fields. I managed the micro-computing park at Airbus, then moved to a large French bank where I was in charge of the department of Electronic Data Interchange, and finally became sales director for an IT company. Overall, I managed teams and budgets in addition to developing portfolios, increasing gross revenues and implementing numerous new ideas.
I currently act as managing partner of OpenSphere, a French based IT company specialized in information system security which I co-founded in 2003.

Which future?
Today, at 44 years of age and living in the Silicon Valley, I am cultivating international opportunities for Opensphere, which offers a unique approach to information system security. I also continue to look for new projects to develop.
I would like to help people who feel alone facing their challenges and realizing their dreams.
