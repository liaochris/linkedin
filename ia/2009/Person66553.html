Douglas Shipley’s Summary

Doug has held senior sales and marketing posts at Sega, Mattel and Infospace, where he focused on business development and sales strategies that drove record growth. In his most recent role as VP for Lquest, he served global publishers and tier 1 carriers, including EA, Iplay-Oberon, Verizon and AT&T, in the areas of licensing and strategy. Doug has been on the viral edge of the digital entertainment industry, and worked with start ups like GetaMovie (rebranded RedBox) by media giants such as ABC, Warner Bros., Disney, and leisure brands such as Harley Davidson to help them facilitate brand exposure, product launch, strategic relationships and sales. Doug has an unparalleled history of finding creative and innovative ways to grow businesses.
