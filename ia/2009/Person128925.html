Keith Crosley’s Summary

Professionally, I direct corporate communications for Proofpoint, Inc., the leading independent provider of email security, email archiving and data loss prevention solutions. At Proofpoint, we focus on delivering email security and risk management solutions that provide maximum security with the lowest total cost of ownership.

My job entails the promotion of Proofpoint email security solutions (which are available in SaaS, hybrid and appliance versions) to press, analysts and the enterprise email security market at large. My blog (http://blog.proofpoint.com) covers a wide variety of email security topics including anti-spam, phishing, identity theft, data breaches and the policy, culture and technology issues that surround email.

As a key spokesperson for Proofpoint and email security evangelist/researcher, I am occasionally let out of my cage for television and radio appearances. Find my latest research on the Proofpoint blog or at http://www.proofpoint.com/outbound.

Avocationally and semi-professionally, I'm a filmmaker, musician and all-round multimedia enthusiast.
