thierry dettloff’s Summary

Thierry Dettloff is a specialist of financial management of large listed international companies and of M&A transactions.

Thierry Dettloff was successively Finance Director of international groups such as Chargeurs (Cac 40: CR), Pathé, Remy Cointreau (Cac: RCO), Infogrames Entertainment (Sbf: IFG)/Atari (Nasdaq: ATAR) and Carlson Wagonlit Travel (Emea division).
Thierry has lead numerous transactions in many different industry sectors, either in France or abroad.

As a partner for 7 years with Deloitte Corporate Finance, Thierry Dettloff has coordinated M&A engagements, servicing major industrial corporations and private equity funds. He has helped clients in negotiating, structuring, preparing purchase contracts, raising financing and exercising contractual guarantees. He has also coordinated financial restructuring to prepare for carve-outs.

Since 2006, Thierry Dettloff advises several Private Equity funds for an alternative and exclusive deal-flow.

Thierry Dettloff has organized financial integration of acquired companies on a worldwide basis and has implemented in many occasions advanced reporting tools and financial reporting in US Gaap’s or Ifrs.

Thierry Dettloff was Investor Relation Officer of Chargeurs, Pathé, and Remy Cointreau, dealing with analysts and portfolio managers in Paris, London, Switzerland, Scotland and USA.

Thierry Dettloff holds an MBA degree of Hautes Etudes Commerciales (HEC - 75) and is a CPA. Thierry also attended Harvard Business School financial management course in 1987.
