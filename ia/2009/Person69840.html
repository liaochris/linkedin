Ed Schor’s Summary

My wide-ranging professional experience has uniquely equipped me to deal with the elusive and nearly impossible. I make deals come together. I began my career as a corporate M&A lawyer, negotiating and litigating multi-billion dollar deals; I then built a successful practice as a real estate attorney and founded ABA to offer buyers a new way to purchase New York City real estate: the Buyer’s Rebate.

When New York State changed its laws in early 2009 to allow real estate brokers to offer rebates, I jumped at the opportunity. Through this unique and innovative program, ABA now offers buyers a refund of up to 1.5% or more of the cost of their new homes.

I'm dedicated to giving clients the very best service and support. Thanks to my knowledge and skill, your purchase will be superbly negotiated, well-informed, carefully considered, transparent, understandable and painless

    THE BUYER’S REBATE – A NEW ALTERNATIVE FOR NEW YORK CITY REAL ESTATE

    FOR BUYERS

    Apartment Buyers Alternative offers a simple, cost-effective alternative to traditional real estate firms.

    When you work with ABA to purchase your New York City condo, co-op or brownstone, we’ll pay you a rebate at the closing table!!
    You’ll receive a refund of up to 1.5% or more of the cost of your new home. How? By taking advantage of our Buyer’s Rebate program, which capitalizes on our small size, efficient management and customer-centric focus to pass a portion of our earnings back to you.

    At ABA, we represent buyers and only buyers, and we limit the number of our clients to make sure you receive the very best service and care.

    Are there other differences? Of course!

    Your broker is Ed Schor, a skilled, licensed real estate and mortgage broker who also draws on years of experience as an accomplished real estate attorney. Ed will help you locate and evaluate the properties that interest you, negotiate any issues that arise, obtain financing and buy the right home at the right price.

    HELP FOR SELLERS TOO

    ABA does not represent sellers. But If your New York City condo, co-op, or brownstone is “for sale by owner,” we can help.

    For a low fee, ABA will list your property on professional real estate databases, where it will be searchable by buyers and seen by all licensed NYC real estate agents and brokers.

    Your listing can contain photos, virtual tours and floor plans. It will appear on Realtor.com, Trulia.com, StreetEasy.com, and other respected websites where home buyers can access your information and contact you without using a buyer’s agent. In addition, many real estate brokerages will re-post your listing on their own websites, further increasing the number of prospective buyers who will see it.
