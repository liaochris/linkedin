Todd Phillips’s Summary

I have been doing Executive Placement specifically for the architecture, engineering and construction industries since 1993. I started my own firm in 1997. The distinct difference was that I only placed people on a permanent basis. My goal was to develop a national client base that was diverse and would need my help in growing their firm. I had the luxury of working with my previous clients while developing a national client base.

Since then, T3 Search has established a national and international
client base and a reputation as a respected industry leader who will consistently develiver the results that both companies and candidates are seeking for their future. I like to partner with firms in helping develop their vision for the future and implementing it by positioning their companies for success through strategic business planning, key leadership hires, and the targeted merger and/or acquisition of companies that will help them reach their goals of becoming a better company.

T3 Search and myself, in particular myself as it's Owner, believe in the power of relationships and networking. I am interesting in meeting and getting to know potential candidates and companies and getting to know them both personally and professionally. Knowing who you are and what is important to you in either looking at a new career opportunity or hiring an employee is the most crucial thing is enabling us to assist you find that "ideal" fit. At the end of the day, business and life are about people and relationships. We hope to develop a beneficial relationship with you and introduce you to a firm or employee that will culturally meet and exceed your expectations. In turn, we would appreciate it if you would refer other firms or employees to us knowing that we will treat them with the utmost respect as we did with you.

I look forward to getting to know you!

Best regards,
Todd Phillips
President/CEO
T3 Search, Inc.
