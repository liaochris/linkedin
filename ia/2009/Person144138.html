Dominic Hofer’s Summary

Loylogic is the leading provider of global reward solutions, helping clients worldwide to differentiate their loyalty programs by combining loyalty expertise with state-of-the-art technology and a fast-growing reward merchant network. The company’s aim is stated as: Making Miles & Points More Valuable than Money™. Loylogic Inc. is a global, independent company with headquarters in Zurich, Switzerland and an office in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. For more information, visit www.loylogic.com.
