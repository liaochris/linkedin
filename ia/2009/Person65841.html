Donna L. Quesinberry’s Summary

Proposal Consulting and Management Oversight is a specialty of DonnaInk. Fiscal year 2008 (FY08), the award ratio remained at 100% through August and 98% from September onward. Donna, and her team, perform cradle to the grave proposal oversight with a 4-step kick-off meeting process and Shipley's standards.

Subject matter expertise (SME) in a number of fields (e.g., information assurance, voice over Internet protocol, Department of Defense Information Assurance Certification and Accreditation Process [DIACAP], global information grid [GIG], GIG-Bandwidth Expansion [GIG-BE], social services, science and medicine among others).

Standard daily rates begin at $960.00 per day with per diem for travel. A chargeback retainer/surety is required to begin work and a .1 bonus incentive for award or profit margins is negotiated. Clients interested in fiscal year 2009 (FY09) should contact Donna at 703-270-9440 or donnainkpubs@donnaink.com. Available 24/7/365.

DonnaInk also provides clients with a full spectrum of operational business development practices, including white papers, magazine articles, brochures, newsletters, slicks, advertorials, press releases, etc. We have a unique vision of business development that encompasses a full suite of service support from public relations, to publications, to the act of proposal development and submission itself.
