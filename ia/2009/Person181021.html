Fabio Paracchini’s Summary

Content and strategic communication consultant, translator and freelance editor for several publishers (mostly young adults fiction and scientific popularization) and copywriter for clients such as Sony, Volvo, Mercedes, Microsoft, Palm, Nastro Azzurro. Founder and editor of the online magazine Trax, editor-in-chief of the contemporary art and culture magazine Cross, author of the non-fiction books "CyberShow - Cinema e teatro con Internet" and "American Movies 90". Author and director of the play "Roba minima". Consultant, transcreator and dubbing director for several videogames and multimedia projects. Teacher of Multimedia Publishing, copywriter, textiles & vacuum cleaners door to door salesman, bass player in a ballroom orchestra, ghostwriter of degree thesis, cameraman for weddings, author of tourist guides, father and tango dancer.

Fabio Paracchini’s Specialties:

Brand strategy, creative media, unconventional marketing, digital advertising, 2.0 communication.
