Annabelle Morris’s Summary

Annabelle Morris is Senior Key Account Manager at Behringer Harvard, responsible for managing all of Behringer's West Coast account relationships. Before joining Behringer Harvard, Annabelle was Vice President, Manager of the Sponsor Relations Group at Wells Fargo Investments responsible for the Strategic Partner Platform.

Prior to joining Wells Fargo, Ms. Morris held a Senior Business & Product Development position with John Hancock Financial Network in Boston, MA. Annabelle managed all Sponsor Relationships while leading the design and implementation of a dedicated website to educate JHFN’s consultants on the various products that were available to them through these partnerships. While at JHFN, she performed due diligence on all new products and was a major contributor to revamping and implementing the firm’s due diligence procedures.

Ms. Morris started her career with State Farm Insurance and rapidly became one of California’s top selling life insurance sales specialists. After several years, she was offered a National Account Manager position with a boutique mutual fund company. Annabelle concentrated her efforts on cultivating strategic partnerships across all channels for the firm’s products. She was responsible for growing the number of selling agreements to 500+ while gaining access across all channels and on all Wirehouse platforms.

Annabelle received her BA degree in Education from the University of Wisconsin, Eau Claire. Ms. Morris serves on the Board of Directors for the Financial Women's Association and is co-chair for the Marketing and Communications committee for this organization.
