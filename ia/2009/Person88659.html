Greg Pfaff’s Summary

Jobspring Partners is a nationwide technology recruiting firm. We help highly skilled software developers, network engineers, systems administrators and interface professionals find jobs in growing, technically-advanced companies. We help companies with challenging technology needs locate the talented IT professionals they need to move their businesses forward. Jobspring Partners uses a positive, team-based model that’s unique in recruiting and placement. We are committed to setting the standard for client satisfaction in our industry.

Jobspring Partners has been in Los Angeles for over ten years. We are split up into two different practices which allows us to be extremely specialized; one practice places professionals in ASP.NET, C#, VB.NET, Flash, Flex, ActionScript and SQL.

One practice places software professionals from the Java, J2EE, PHP, Perl and Ruby.
