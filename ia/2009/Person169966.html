musharraf chawdhry’s Summary

Musharraf got enrolled in Delhi Bar Council in September 1988. She joined a reputed Supreme Court Firm ‘ P. H. Parekh & Co.’ where she was involved in (i) drafting; (ii), briefing Senior Advocates; (iii) giving legal opinions to companies (iv) handling voluminous matters and their follow up action besides being involved in taking major legal decisions related to them.(v) Documentation such as drafting contracts, wills various Deeds, Agreements etc. The focus of her practice has been in litigation majorly and documentation work along with it.

At present she is working independently as an Advocate in the High Court of Delhi and Supreme Court and is involved in looking after the work of the State of Madhya Pradesh in Supreme Court besides other private matters. She has also been involved in Zee Turner Litigations such as disputes in TDSAT and disputes between Zee Turner.

Musharraf has participated in many Legal conferences, workshops and Seminars held in Delhi by various Legal Associations of India

She has been issued a Certificate from the Law Society of England and Wales to appear for the solicitor’s Exam by December 2008 to be enrolled as solicitor also in Law Society, England and Wales.

Some of the key assignments handled by Musharraf, amongst others, cover the following:
Telecom Sector
