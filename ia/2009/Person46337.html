Andy Ciordia’s Summary

I used to think of myself as a classic jack of all trades, now I know I am a jack of all trades. Years ago when I made this profile I was running a systems department. It was fun, proactive solution building to issues you may or may not have to handle. Preparedness won the day.

Time has changed but my methods, my character, and my abilities just continue to evolve. I still consider myself a problem solver. I hate drama and want to understand the problem so we can develop a solution as quickly and painlessly as possible.

I believe in efficiency; using a small amount of a catalyst to create an enormous positive effect. If you think through your logistics on anything from psychology to databases you can build intelligent chains of good planning. Networked with a strong team you can achieve any results.

Sadly a lot of the world has broken teams. That's why it's great to understand the art of communication and negotiation. We have to keep an open and stable line of communication and see eye to eye so that what we are planning is executed effectively.

I am an artist, a designer, a photographer, a technologist, an engineer, a friend, and a husband. While I cannot do it all I love a good challenge and the experience of making a difference in whatever I participate in.
