Ken O'Connor’s Summary

I help Enterprises with Data Governance, Data Migration, Master Data Management (MDM), Data Profiling, Data Quality (DQ), Customer Data Integration (CDI) and much, much more.

Data Migration is an ongoing fact of life for all enterprises. Business drivers include:

Replacement of existing systems
Data sharing with Business Partners
Population of new regulatory systems (e.g Anti Money Laundering, BASEL II)
Ad hoc provision of data to satisfy regulatory requests
Population of CRM system
Provide toxic loan details to NAMA (National Asset Management Agency – Ireland)

For the past 15 years, my work has centred on Data Conversions, Data Migrations and Data Governance. As a Senior Consultant in IBM’s Euro Centre of Competence, I helped develop End-to-End processes to handle Euro Changeover for clients in Ireland and across Europe.

As an independent consultant, I have worked with multi-national clients on Single View of Customer programmes, and major compliance programmes, including Sarbannes Oxley (SOX), BASEL II and Anti Money Laundering (AML).

All Data Migrations face similar challenges, regardless of industry type. The challenges are Data Governance ones, which include:

How to populate the target repository?
Where is the source data?
Which of the multiple sources is Master?
Who owns the data?
Where are the business rules?
Which business rules are master?
What to do about conflicting business rules?
What data quality is required for the new system?
What is the quality of the existing data?
What will we do if the quality of the existing data is below the quality required for the new system.
etc.

I have faced and solved the above challenges and more. My goal is to help your Enterprise implement good Data Governance processes, to enable you to successfully manage your data, and to help you complete successful Data Migrations.
