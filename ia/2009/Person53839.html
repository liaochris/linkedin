Danielle Friedland’s Summary

Danielle Friedland is the founder and former Editor in Chief of the Celebrity Baby Blog, the first and largest website dedicated exclusively to celebrity pregnancies and celebrity babies. The idea was born from Danielle watching a red carpet awards show and seeing the number of pregnant celebrities and she seized the opportunity to cover this trend. In 2009, the site celebrated its fifth anniversary. It is the #7 parenting site, with its robust community that helps fuels sales of baby and maternity fashion, accessories and other products like strollers, baby carriers and diaper bags.

In May 2008, Danielle sold the Celebrity Baby Blog to Time Inc., where it became part of the PEOPLE family.

As Senior Editor at People.com, Danielle helped grow the site traffic from 10 million monthly page views (in June 2008) to 24 million (in June 2009).

Danielle became a mom herself in 2005 to daughter Anya, who has also become a pseudo celebrity from her presence on the site.

Danielle has become a celebrity in her own right, as an expert in the blogosphere and in communicating to moms. She has frequently been asked to speak about why the American public is so intrigued with the offspring of celebrities. She is also an expert in the juvenile market, having covered the field for the site for the past three years and been quoted in Earnshaw's.
