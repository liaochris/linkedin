Dr.Badarinath S. Murthy’s Summary

I have been a pathologist for 25 years and out of personal interest I have developed additional expertise in laboratory diagnostic hematology, clinical hematology and infection control. I have received training in developing lab related softwares, accreditation (ISO, CCHSA), lab designing ,development of clinical pathways, antibiotic policies etc. I am basically interested in returning back to my country soon and practise what I have learnt here so far.

Lab designing, lab software, accreditation, infection control, clinical pathways, standard operating protocols, policy manuals,

  • Junior Resident in Pathology department

    Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER), Chandigarh

    (Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    June 1980 — May 1981 (1 year )

    Got admission to MD Pathology course by merit through this 1 year Junior residency. Selections were based on written test and oral examinations. We after selection (8 out of 140 who were interviewed who were selected out of 7500 doctors who wrote the written test) were rotated through departments of hematology, surgical pathology, cytology, immunopathology, autopsy & morbid anatomy with strict internal assessments. At the end of 1 year we all were subjected to written and oral tests and those who qualified were taken as Residents for a 2 year course that resulted in MD in Pathology. The disqualified ones were given a second chance at the end of 6 months again.
