Sarah Clark’s Summary

I'm a highly analytical, energetic leader with an entrepreneurial spirit. I have 14 years of experience building strategic planning, product development and project management processes at software companies. My forte is driving the process of new product development - creating product feature road maps that deliver what customers want, take logical and technical synergies into account, and utilize beta launches - allowing for an overall optimized approach.

I've always had a passion for protecting the environment. This passion plus my ongoing annoyance with Syrofoam coffee cups has led me to recently build a distribution company for compostable disposable food and drink containers in my free time.

Sarah Clark’s Experience

  • Owner

    Infinite Canopy

    (Business Supplies and Equipment industry)

    2009 — Present (less than a year)

    Global consumption of single-use disposable food and drink containers is estimated at 438 billion units per year, or 140,000 per second. The vast majority of these products are made from materials that harm the environment and languish in landfills (or as litter polluting beaches, rivers and other areas) after their use - this is known as "white pollution".

    Luckily this problem is solvable. Infinite Canopy recognizes that humans will not give up the convenience of disposable items altogether. We therefore sell environmentally sustainable, forward thinking alternatives which are 100% plant based and compostable (meaning that in the right conditions they will degrade so that they return nutrients to the soil). Our products are made only from highly renewable sources such as sugar, bamboo, rice and corn, never contain any toxins or chemicals, and use production methods that reduce pollution. There is no reason to use tree paper, plastic or styrofoam (both made from petroleum) products at your restaurant or next special event. We have alternatives appropriate for all types of events! We work with restaurants, caterers, schools, government institutions, special events, and individuals - our products have become very popular with weddings and summer picnics. Go green today!
