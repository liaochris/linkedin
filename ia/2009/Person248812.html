Yue Wu’s Summary

I finished my B. S. in Chemistry at University of Science and Technology of China in 06/2001, then joined Prof. Charles M. Lieber's group in Department of Chemistry and Chemical Biology at Harvard for my graduate study and got my Ph. D. degree in 06/2006,
Currently I am a Miller Research Fellow in Department of Chemistry at University of California at Berkeley, working in Prof. A. Paul Alivisatos' group. My focus is on the development of nanocrystal-based photovoltaic solar cells.
From August 2009, I will start as assistant professor of Chemical Engineering at Purdue University.
