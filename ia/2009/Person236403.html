Tom Szabo’s Summary

In 1999 Tom opened A Thomas Image a Commercial, Portrait Underwater and Wedding photography studio. He creates portrait photography for families and individuals, commercial photography for business, underwater photography for sale and publication and photographs Weddings. Mr. Szabo has had his photojournalism works published in leading scuba diving magazines.

Mr. Szabo was downsized after a successful career in manufacturing that included Engineering, Sales, Operations Management for private business.

He has served as a part-time Instructor at the local community college and county career center.

Tom is a PADI certified Scuba Instructor, Teaches 4 levels of scuba certifications, 13 specialty scuba courses, CPR, First Aid and AED (Automatic External Defibrillator) and has authored a PADI approved distinctive specialty certification photography course. He has led divers on domestic and international travel.

In 2001, Mr. Szabo co-founded Downsized Into Business Startup (DIBS) Networking group for “downsized” executives and professionals who have started their own business.

In order to learn and share skills using Adobe PHOTOSHOP software, he started a PHOTOSHOP users group for Lake County, Ohio in August 2008.
