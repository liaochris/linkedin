Peter Kingsley’s Summary

A marketing leader with +13 years of experience building businesses and brands within major multinational companies including Sony (Consumer Products/PlayStation), Microsoft (Xbox and Zune), Levi Strauss & Co. (Red Tab) and Lucas Arts (Star Wars, Indiana Jones).

Strong experience in the start-up, development and general marketing management of Games, CE, Technology, Music and Fashion businesses.

Experienced with managing the full breadth of the marketing profession (in-bound, out-bound, brand, marcom, product management & sales).

Versatile leader capable of bridging the gap between technical, creative and business worlds.

Passionate student of popular culture, consumer behavior and the role of technology as a lifestyle enabler.
