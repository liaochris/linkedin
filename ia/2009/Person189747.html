Praveen Mishra’s Summary

Praveen is an MBA in Human Resource Management. He is a practicing HR Consultant in areas related to HR Process & Strategy. He is also an entrepreneur in the HR Services area. He adds value to companies business and HR Process through his expertise in understanding of the interrelationships and alignment of conceptual models in business & HR strategy and their impact on organizational performance. The aim is to deliver “simple to understand and implement” yet high impact solutions for business.

He has been exposed to a wide array of HR best practices and has worked in diverse areas encompassing responsible roles in HR Strategy, as well as HR Operations.


Apart from heading the business and leading the consulting practice at Exsear Partners, he is engaged in setting up a venture focussed on HR Process Consulting & Outsourcing.

As a Consultant and HR Specialist he has been responsible for designing and implementing HR Tools, to enable and support HR Processes for various organisations. His areas of work are primarily HR Strategy, Organisation Design and HR Processes. He has been associated with clients in Manufacturing, IT, ITES, Telecom towards implementation of Scorecard based Performance Management System, Organizational Restructuring and advising on the employee recognition strategies.
