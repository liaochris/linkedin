Adam Buckman’s Summary

Editor, columnist and reporter on the television beat for more than 25 years -- a body of work comprising nearly 4,000 published columns and stories.

My goal is to continue to build my career -- as a manager, content provider or both -- in the business of news media.

  • TV columnist

    New York Post

    (Newspapers industry)

    August 1998 — December 2008 (10 years 5 months)

    Frequently quoted, always talked about: One of the most widely read columnists in the TV business.

    Wrote up to five or more columns per week – reviews, commentaries, opinions, celebrity interviews, features, breaking news stories – on every conceivable television subject: New shows, trends, the Super Bowl, the Oscars, Emmy Awards, “American Idol,” morning shows, prime time, late-night, Letterman, Leno.
