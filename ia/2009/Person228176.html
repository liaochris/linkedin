Sylvie Dewaele’s Summary

With over 15 years experience in the travel industry.
Held positions with Air France and Alliance Hospitality Hotel Group before joining HRG in 2004 where I began my experience in HRG Client Management by looking after a large multinational company on a European scale in 20 countries and successfully managing this account through some of the key changes in the industry for over a period of 3 years. In 2007 I took new responsibilities in the role of Global Business Manager responsible for one of the largest clients in France, GDF SUEZ.
Prior to HRG, I have successfully developed my client approach through various positions within the airline industry and most notably at Air France in London before reaching the position of Corporate Sales Account Manager. In this role, I successfully identified new business opportunities whilst managing a portfolio of national and international accounts. I built up valuable relationships in the industry which I maintain to date, and gained significant experience in contract negotiation and performance analysis.
In 2003, I joined Alliance Hospitality Hotel Group as Director of seminar and incentives responsible for strategy and financial conditions in the hotel industry. My outstanding relationship management expertise, proven analytical, negotiation and presentation skills make me confident and composed with all levels of management.
