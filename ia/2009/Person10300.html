Anthony Simpson’s Summary

Experienced Chief Operating Officer who brings energy and passion complimented with a broad experience in operational management as well as financial analysis, compliance, technology, marketing, sales training and human resources. Has international experinece in UK, Italy and South Africa.

Current and previous role both entrepeneurial start-ups in the specialist finance sector, creating and running high volume, multi-million £/€ turnover processing platforms for a unique form of purchasing and funding of public sector trade receivables in UK and Italy, which have proved to be credit-crunch proof.
My goals are based on what I really enjoy and am good at: designing and setting up new operations or trouble shooting and sorting out broken or poor performing operations. I draw on my knowledge and experience to find cross functional solutions enabled by the efficient use of technology to maximise the human expertise in an organisation.
I am very keen to seek out opportunities in the US in the coming years working with new and existing international contacts and partners.

Anthony Simpson’s Specialties:

Receivables financing, public sector procurement, heathcare and pharmaceutical industries, process re-engineering, Six Sigma, kaizen, technology utilisation, sales training and techniques, tactical and strategic marketing, HR management, people development incl. personal profiling.

Anthony Simpson’s Experience

  • CEO

    SF Trust Servicing plc

    (Financial Services industry)

    August 2007 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    A finance business providing a revolving securitisation-type facility combined with a servicing platform for Italian and other European sovereign trade recevables. This funding provides valuable cashflow for clients, using a non-recourse, receivable purchase structure. The unique servicing platform enables efficient processing of high volumes of transactions and allows all parties to the transactions to have acces via the web to information and functionality that in turn enables them to become more efficient and reduce their back office costs.
    Our clients are typically private sector providers of goods and services to the public sector.
    Since starting in Aug 2007, we have contracted €300m of receivables.

  • Chief Operating Officer

    Resource Partners Group Ltd

    (Privately Held; Financial Services industry)

    January 2000 — June 2007 (7 years 6 months)

    A specialst healthcare business providing a novel cashflow funding model and outsourced processing platform for clients. The business was a start-up and I joined to oversee the creation of the technology platform and processes and head up the operations. In the time I was with the business, from a standing-start, the company purchased over €1bn receivables with minimal credit losses and had a gold-class servicing platform and capabilities which achieved a private servicer rating from Fitch in 2005. When I decided to leave Resource Partners, I had board responsibility for Operations,Sales & Marketing and HR.

  • Operations Manager

    Prudential Assurance Co

    (Public Company; Insurance industry)

    April 1997 — July 1999 (2 years 4 months)

    Moved to the Pensions Review Unit as Business Planning Manager, but within a matter of months asked to head up their largest operations area which was underperforming and in danger of not meeting FSA compliance rules and deadlines with only months to go. The unit consisted of over 80 staff and was tasked with individually reveiwing over 12,000 personal pension plans and meeting FSA deadlines for compensating policy holders where personal pension plans were deemed as mis-sold. The task was completed and the deadlines achieved.

  • Senior Finance Analyst

    (Public Company; Insurance industry)

Also involved in assisting with assigned business units in developing operating and business plans, budgets as well as assessing capital expenditures projects and making recommendations to UK board.

  • Various trouble shooting operations and finance assignments

    Prudential Assurance Co

    (Public Company; Insurance industry)

    April 1986 — December 1994 (8 years 9 months)

  • Senior Claims Manager

    British National Reinsurance Company Ltd

    (Insurance industry)

    June 1981 — April 1986 (4 years 11 months)

    Was recruited as the first batch of staff by an American holding company setting up new insurance operations in UK. Managed department dealing with complex property and casualty reinsurance claims, class actions and litigations - specialising in toxic waste claims, asbestosis,as well as marine and US catastrophe large losses.
    Successfull re-calculated loss reserves and obtained agreement and deposit payments from multiple co-insurers for these multi-million $ reserves. When I took over the portfolio, there were poor administrative procedures, bad records and a large backlog.
