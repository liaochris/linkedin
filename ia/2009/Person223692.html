Steve Boland’s Summary

Nonprofit consultant helping to build community and opportunity in Saint Paul, Minnesota with over 20 years experience working with diverse groups toward social change.

Steve Boland’s Specialties:

- Focus in building horizontal as well as vertical partnerships in community; uniting government, communities, nonprofits and business.
- Creating community and economic development strategies unique to each situation and serving both geographic- and ethnic-based organizations.
- Expertise in technology implementations to accomplish community outreach goals.
- Communications experience across all media to integrate messaging while understanding the unique strengths of each medium.

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    September 2009 — Present (2 months)

    At Nonprofits Assistance Fund, our mission is to build financially healthy nonprofits that foster community vitality. As a 501(c)(3) Community Development Financial Institution (CDFI), we support nonprofits through transformational lending, encouraging healthy financial practices, and by providing strategic financial guidance. Our partnerships with nonprofits in Minnesota and its adjacent communities allow these organizations to focus their energies on achieving their goals and serving others, rather than worrying about their own financial situation.
