Benjamin Brandes’s Summary

Highly accomplished investment fund executive who creates wealth for partners and financial backers through proven ability to successfully build and manage multi-million dollar investment vehicles investing across the capital structure. Extensive expertise directing all aspects of investment strategy, sourcing, execution, monitoring and disposition; completed over $1B of transactions as lead investor. Responsible for structuring all acquisition, financing, operating, and investment agreements. Recognized for strong negotiating, mediation and turnaround skills. Significant industry knowledge in media, communications, business services and real estate. Maintains sustainable relationships with leading hedge funds, middle market private equity funds, and senior and mezzanine lenders.
