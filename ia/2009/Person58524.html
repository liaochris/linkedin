David C Pinkowitz’s Summary

Founded in 1996 by David Pinkowitz, MBA and MSEE, DCP Marketing Services LLC is a full-service Long Island marketing firm that helps technology and professional services firms build awareness, develop relationships, and generate sales leads through creative strategies, effective communications, and introductions to potential business partners. Its clients include electronics hardware, software, and Internet services companies, as well as law firms and accounting firms. For many of his clients, David serves as their part-time head of marketing.

DCP Marketing Services helps its clients increase their sales by positioning their company, differentiating their products, segmenting their markets, and implementing the marketing mix:
-- Copywriting
-- Press Releases
-- E-mail Newsletters
-- Web Sites
-- Success Stories
-- Sales Sheets
-- Advertising
-- Marketing Plans
