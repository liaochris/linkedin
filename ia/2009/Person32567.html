Brenda Wiley’s Summary

I currently work with businesses and sales professionals to increase revenue by building customer relationships with clients and prospects. I increase customer retention, and word of mouth referrals through a unique automated personalized greeting card system. Cards are generated online in the clients' own handwriting for about $1.00 a card. The REAL card is printed, stuffed and mailed through the US Post Office by us. You can even create your own customized or photo cards and postcards.

Showing appreciation to customers, past customers and contacts increases sales. There is no system easier, less expensive, or more effective than Send Out Cards.
A 15 minute walkthrough over the computer lets a client try the system and send a free card, without having to give credit card info.
