Michael Schnellbuegel [LION]’s Summary

Michael has 30 years of experience in project/process management and sales in high tech industries. During his industrial career Michael lived and worked several years in France. Based on the requirements of space and aerospace (working beside others on Ariane 1 through 5 and Space Shuttle) he got an in-depth view into business process management in an area where quality and therefore processes are literally mission-critical. Based on this experience Michael started in 2000 a consulting business for process analysis and optimization together with a few selected partners. Within this scope he worked for different industries as well as public administration throughout the world.

Out of this consulting business PROMIS Software & Services GmbH and through a merger with a tool vendor PROMIS Solutions AG emerged.
