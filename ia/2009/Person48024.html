michael B’s Summary

Michael is an interactive developer with an extensive understanding and practical usage of industry leading design standards for media applications. He is experienced in a wide range of media formats that include, broadcast and web video, graphic print and web design, sound design and mixing, DVD authoring and various current web coding languages for UI component applications.


In the last six years, Michael has managed projects from conception through development to implementation. He is persistent in achieving successful final products within the given budgets. Michael is hard-working, willing and productive, successfully completing his projects to the satisfaction of his clients.


While knowledgeable, resourceful and open to learning new concepts, Michael has a keen sense and passion for emerging interactive technologies, their viability, requirements and relationships to each project, as well as assessing their feasibility for usage on future client work. He has demonstrated an ability to lead a project while still working closely with a team, giving it his undivided and enthusiastic attention.
