Evandro L. Oliveira’s Experience

  • Principal

    Mercer

    (Public Company; MMC; Human Resources industry)

    November 1994 — Present (14 years 11 months)

    Evandro Luis de Oliveira is currently the Leader of Retirement Consulting for Mercer in Brazil.

    He performs a Principal role and has more than 15 years of background experience in Consulting Services. His expertise is focused on advice and solutions to local and global clients on issues related to design, evaluation, accounting, implementation and governance of pension plan and medical care, including for retirees. He has also participated in several international benefits projects for multinationals operating in Latin America. Evandro is a member of the committee responsible for business development initiatives for local and global clients.

    Evandro has a wide expertise in many market industries, including consulting services for clients on retail, consumer goods, pharmaceutical, automotive, financial and industrial equipments.

    Evandro has lectured in several national and international conferences related to design, implementation, administration and communication regarding benefits granted to employees, specifically on Pension Plan. He also actuates as a lecturer in market trends conferences held for clients.

    Evandro has a B.Sc. degree in Business Administration, Actuarial Sciences and Accounting and has a MBA in Actuarial and Financial Administration from Universidade de São Paulo (FIPECAFI). He is also a member of Instituto Brasileiro de Atuária, IBRACON, Conselho Federal de Contabilidade and permanent member of the Committee of Post Employment Benefits at International Actuarial Association (IAA).

    In addition to his native Portuguese, Evandro is fluent in English and Spanish.
