Lacey E. Buteyn, CFP®, CFM’s Summary

Lacey's passion is client care. As a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ professional, she cherishes the opportunity to develop a meaningful relationship with each client that enables her to fully evaluate their financial goals and dreams. With a personally tailored touch, each client receives professional advice, customized to their specific needs and situation. Lacey then enjoys the privilege of guiding clients on their financial journey, as a trusted advisor.

Lacey is a partner in the Alexander Buteyn Group that specializes in financial planning, charitable gifting, managing client portfolios, and wealth education. They focus their energy on a select group of clients seeking their unique brand of client care and specialized attention to detail. The team also works closely with each client's CPAs, Attorneys, and other trusted advisors in crafting their estate and tax plan. Many of their clients come to the Alexander Buteyn Group via referral from other satisfied clients.
