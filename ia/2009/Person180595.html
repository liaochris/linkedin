Pam Hobson’s Summary

A career professional with over 30 years’ sales and general management experience in the IT Industry and in experiential learning and development. She worked for the the world's largest technology company (HP), worked as an Independent Business Consultant and Personal Coach when running her own business and was Director of Business Development at ProfitAbility Business Simulations. She has now returned to her passion of helping people and companies as an Independent Business Consultant and Coach. She understands client and business needs and devises and implements business solutions to meet those needs—essentially she "makes things happen".
