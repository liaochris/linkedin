Nazrul Kamaruddin’s Summary

Nazrul swings on the web, life, culture and open source technology.

A Web developer, User Experience specialist, Graphic & User Interface designer with 10 years of experience in branding & identity design, web standards design & development, PHP web development for businesses coming from technology, entertainment, maritime, food & beverage, education & government agencies.

He personally write a trail of thoughts as Mr. Buyot, embracing independent music & videos at nuGrooveTV and reconnecting the geek factor with GTUG Kuala Lumpur. Not to mention his daily anecdote juxtaposing web design & Drupal together for websites & web applications.

Nazrul Kamaruddin’s Specialties:

Drupal module & theme development, PHP web development, web standards design, development & implementation, data mining & analysis, web architecture design based on open source software, corporate branding & identity design, user experience & interface designer, community management, branding & development

  • IT Consultant

    Lada Langkawi Consolidated Sdn. Bhd.

    (Maritime industry)

    December 1999 — January 2003 (3 years 2 months)

    My time with Lada Langkawi Consolidated was an interesting one. Not only being able to live in a beautiful island for several years, working with LLCSB was a start for my career. During my tenure here, I was responsible with the company's IT infrastructure, which also includes a developing an in house IT management system.

    L.I.B.A is a vessel management & billing system for LLCSB jetty operations in Kuah, Kuala Kedah & Kuala Perlis. L.I.B.A act as a database collection of vessels & ships operating in the northern region of Malaysia. It also functions as a billing system to manage the daily transactions & operations for berth hours, mooring, stevedore & other services for each jetty. I've successfully designed, developed & implemented the solution on 4 different location sites. L.I.B.A was initially built as a MS Access application, but later ported into a full Web based system running on Linux, Apache, MySQL & PHP (LAMP).

    Working in Langkawi was definitely an experience for a city boy like me.
