Kenneth Tanikie’s Summary

The Security and Close Protection Experts On Hand 24 Hours A Day, Whenever And Wherever You Need!

Tanmore Security Services are a cutting edge, dependable, specialist security organisation, delivering Door Supervisors (Hosts), low profile bodyguards through to high profile close protection teams, VIP security, resident security and even drivers.
Tanmore Security Services’ is able to provide a comprehensive, quality orientated security service. Within any business activity, Security and Health and Safety are of paramount importance. The safeguarding of our business activities, our customers, colleagues and the general public is essential.

Our work is specifically tailor-made to the individual needs of the client. Your security solution will be implemented, monitored and controlled by our experienced, professional management team to your specific requirements.

Tanmore Security Services has been running as a business for just over 3 years, supplying security services to individuals, companies, night clubs and bars. Tanmore Security Services prides itself on the professional and customer focussed approach it provides and its ability to exceed client expectations.

Our Hosts have a background within the security industry where they have acquired qualities of self-discipline and responsibility. All our Hosts are carefully screened prior to employment and are, upon appointment, given thorough specialist training in all aspects of security and client relationship management.
