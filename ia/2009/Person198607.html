Rick Lucash’s Summary

Involved with emerging technology companies as member of boards of advisors, consultant, and founder of LaunchPad angel investment group and member of boards of advisors. Help technology companies manage the legal function; obtain funding (as a founder of the LaunchPad angel group, I have extensive contacts within the angel investor community); review and critique business plans and help with presentations to investors; provide insights into organizational behavior; advise on compensation – monetary and equity (restricted stock, options, etc.). Extensive contacts within the high tech community and service providers, so that I can help companies identify potential strategic partners and service providers such as accounting firms.

Prior extensive experience as law firm partner concentrating on emerging companies

Partner in Lawrence Associates www.lawrenceassociates.com. Focus on executive compensation, and organizational, capital formation and other legal and business issues pertaining to compensation. Advise hospitals, foundations, universities, colleges and private schools, other nonprofit and for-profit organizations, and technology and other entrepreneurial companies on executive compensation matters, including compensation levels, processes for complying with the IRS Intermediate Sanctions regulations, and transition issues.
.

Rick Lucash’s Specialties:
