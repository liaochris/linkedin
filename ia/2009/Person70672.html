Eric Herrera’s Summary

Experience as Lead Technical Advisor and Director in Engineering for multi-national-corporation’s leveraging over 19 years engineering and management experience from NASA, The Boeing Company, Bombardier, Xerox, Freudenberg-NOK and Alliant Techsystems (ATK). Started business units with success in part due to my alignment with company business strategy and my consulting talents. My approach incorporates my technical and marketing skill set to enable a cost efficient and time effective first market launch.
I have championed and developed innovative principles and ideas; applied theories, concepts and technologies to areas of engineering for highly complex and technical products and manufacturing processes. For example, I have been awarded numerous patents.
I have been awarded World Excellence Award’s for my innovation in using CAE and Six Sigma methods to save OEM's millions annually in testing, prototyping, manufacturing and optimizing. Utilized quality control management to insure not only the development of product but also the manufacturing from the factory to a flawless first launch. I received awards from Boeing for significantly reducing customer complaints using SPC and code I wrote. I utilize control management in my daily work and I have received my Six Sigma certification.
One of my talents is to recognize talent in others and utilize it in a synergistic way. My accomplishments are due to my team environment that I, we, have created. I lead by influence, trust and reputation, not strictly by appointment. I have a reputation in my field as a mentor and coach for developing individuals. A talent I find quite valuable is being able to convey complicated concepts/theories in a simple way thus empowering my team to work with me leading to success. This ability has been recognized not only in my, and my employer’s success, but also in awards for best oral presentations at SAE and other professional symposia.
