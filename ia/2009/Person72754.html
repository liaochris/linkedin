Emily Blunt’s Summary

Emily Blunt serves as the Strategic Initiatives Coordinator for Vision 2015. As coordinator, Blunt assists the Vice President of Strategic Initiatives by providing administrative support and meeting management on select projects in the areas of education, livable communities, and urban renaissance. In addition to supporting key initiatives, she also assists with the coordination of special events and helps manage communications with the Vision 2015 Board of Directors and various other groups.

Blunt is a graduate of the Northern Kentucky Chamber of Commerce’s Most Likely to Succeed Program and the Congressional Leadership Program. She is actively involved with several volunteer activities associated with Big Brothers Big Sisters, United Way, and LEGACY.

Blunt graduated from Northern Kentucky University in 2007 with a B.A. in English and Political Science. She currently resides in Covington, KY.
