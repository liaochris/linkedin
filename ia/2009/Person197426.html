Richard Birks’s Summary

I advice on all aspects of real estate development and investment. I have particular experience in major infrastructure projects, acting for sponsors, authorities and funders. I also have expertise in acquisitions and disposals, acting for funds and institutional investors, including transactions with an international element.

Further experience includes advising extensively on the real estate aspects of corporate deals and disposals and acquisitions with insolvent parties.

Richard Birks’s Specialties:

Major infrastructure projects, acquisitions and disposals, real estate

Richard Birks’s Experience

  • Director Doha

    Eversheds

    (Partnership; Law Practice industry)

    November 2006 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    Currently a Partner specialising in major real estate development, project finance and infrastructure projects, with a particular focus on health and education but including work in the energy, transport and satellite/defence sectors, acting for both the public sector and private sector sponsors and debt providers.

    My recent work includes advising the Northern Ireland government on the regeneration of the iconic Maze prison and surrounding Long Kesh estate, a university relocation to King's Cross Central, one of the largest regeneration schemes in Europe, the Trust in the £1 billion redevelopment of Barts and the Royal London Hospital; the banking syndicate in the SkyNet Satellite financing; LDA on the infrastructure arrangements being put in place at the Olympic Park to serve the 2012 Olympic and Paralympic Games including the CCHP plant and Electricity concessions; and English Partnerships in the £250 million regeneration of Blackwall Reach in London Docklands.

    I relocated to join Eversheds in the Middle East in February 2009.

  • Partner Real Estate Developers Team

    Eversheds

    (Law Practice industry)

    November 2006 — February 2009 (2 years 4 months)

    I previously worked as a partner in our real estate developers team and headed up the education estates team in London. I acted for various clients on inner city and brownfield regeneration sites with a broad experience in major infrastructure and accommodation projects, as well as general PFI, PPP and infrastructure projects.

    I have particular experience in the eduction and health sectors having worked on the redevelopment of Barts and the London Hospital for the procuring NHS Trust and for the sponsors and funders of a number of NHS LIFT and BSF schemes. I have also advised various HE and FE institutions on their estates strategies, including one central London institution's relocation and consolidation of its existing property interests, a major capital project of a value in excess of £100 million.
