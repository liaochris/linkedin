Alexis Washington’s Summary

Urban Star Communications, LLC. (USC) creates and distributes top-notch media that empowers the urban woman's voice. It is a media where reality is allowed to meet dreams, truth meets fiction and the future outshines the past. USC provides products and services that focus on lifestyle issues, interests and concerns of urban women.

Urban Star also provides advertising, marketing and public relations services.

SOLE Magazine

By focusing on the readers, USC developed a winning media platform that is interactive and exciting. The aggressive event marketing strategy is the result of our determination of being a part of the urban woman's life. SOLE Magazine is a must see and a must read for every woman. It is both a blueprint and journal. It's the good advice of a friend, the ear of a best friend and the care of a sister. Everything an urban woman needs and everywhere she wants to go can be found in SOLE.


USC provides an avenue for marketers to reach urban women that spend more for clothing, entertainment and personal care products. Through lifestyle events and lifestyle publication,SOLE, marketers have numerous opportunities to reach urban women.
