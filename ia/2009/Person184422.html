Ed Schrader’s Summary

Everyone has finally realized that it's not the resume, the product or that special service you have that gets you in the door, it's the people you know and network with that may know someone whom can open that door for you. Networking is vogue, it's hot and is now the most accepted means of how a true professional gets ahead in the game. LinkedIN is a great example of professional networking via the internet; and there are other mediums and tools that are as effective, if not more so.

The focus of PDQ is helping people connect with other people and businesses connect with other businesses via networking seminars and face-to-face/phone-to-phone speed networking events. So while the internet (i.e. "static event") might be one way of connecting with a person or an opportunity, face-to-face and phone-to-phone speed networking (i.e. "dynamic events") are a more effective means of finally presenting that "elevator speech" you have honed in hopes that it will click with one or more of the people you are interfacing with live!
