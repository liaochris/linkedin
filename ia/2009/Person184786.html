Pierre Pellerin’s Summary

20 years of professional experience in large multi-national companies such as GE, DSM and Autobar. 8 years of line management of international teams, long-term assignments in France and The Netherlands, speaks English and French.

Joined GE in the mid-90's at a time the company was getting engaged into Six Sigma. During over 10 years, led large teams of Master Black-Belts and Black-Belts through the learning and the use of Lean Six Sigma to solve customer problems.

As Corporate Quality Leader for GE Capital Europe, supported 18 European businesses to achieve their objectives in terms of growth, productivity, development of e-business and customer satisfaction (in areas such as: insurance, consumer and commercial financing, equipment renting, etc.).

As European Lean Six Sigma Leader at GE Equipment Finance, GE Fleet Services and GE Capital Solutions, managed the LSS function and the deployment of Master Black-Belts and Black-Belts in Europe, resulting in increased sales force effectiveness and improved capabilities in terms of customer satisfaction, process efficiency and volume growth (in areas such as: credit approval and funding, equipment/vehicle delivery and resale, assistance to end users/drivers).
