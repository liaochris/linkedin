Alain Dang Van Mien’s Summary

Alain brings 20 years of experience in the IT industry. He work for the Industry Strategy & Insight Team at Oracle. The team of business & IT strategists is responsible for supporting Oracle' sales license business with strategic consulting and C-level business development services. Before Oracle, Alain spent eight years at Gartner where he had advised executives to align IT strategy to the business on a wide range of issues from enterprise performance to risk management. He also held management positions in the financial services industry.

Alain holds a global MBA from INSEAD and a master's degree from the Ecole Centrale de Paris.
