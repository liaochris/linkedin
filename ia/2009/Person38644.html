Carrie Moore’s Summary

Executive Staffing Professional with over 10years of experience in corporate and agency recruiting. Specializes in the recruitment of “rock star” candidates at the senior executive level for the Internet, Technology and Media industries.

Carrie Moore’s Specialties:

Ability to communicate with a variety of levels within the organization (Vice Presidents, Directors, Managers, Employees). Extensive experience in Recruiting, Staffing Consulting and various aspects of human resources. Ability to devise and develop effective sourcing plans and analysis for business partners. Source, screen, evaluate, offer and close qualified candidates. Efficient experience in employee relations, hiring, reductions in force and staffing consulting in a fast paced environment.
