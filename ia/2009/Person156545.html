Matt Neznanski’s Education

  • Iowa State University

    M.S., Journalism & Mass Communication, 2005 — 2007

    Graduated top of cohort. Presented research papers at two AEJMC conferences.
    Co-author, "Online journalism and the war in cyberspace: a comparison between U.S. and international newspapers," published in Journal of Computer-Mediated Communication, vol. 12 (2006).
