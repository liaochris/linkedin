Jason Cozens’s Summary

Jason currently owns and runs Bite Digital, an award winning Internet Marketing Agency with joint Managing Director Declan Cosgrove.

Bite Digital helps businesses acquire new customers and generate new sales. It provides Search Engine Marketing (SEO & PPC), Web Design & eCommerce solutions to companies.

It specialises in identifying the most beneficial sources of sales enquires for businesses and maximising the return on their investment by achieving prominent search engines listings in the most cost effective way possible.

Jason has previously been responsible for the development and evolution of three of the UK’s biggest consumer orientated e-commerce channels; for JD Sports, Simply Games and HiFiBitz as well as business focused solutions for the likes of Mitsubishi and Weidmuller.

He built up his own company (www.visuality.co.uk) specialising in e-commerce software, consulting, development and on-line marketing which is still trading profitably today.

After leaving as CEO of Visuality, Jason ran D-ISC where he ran the employee and customer loyalty schemes for the likes of the BBC, Capita and Cendant, Jason's responsibilities covered the direction of internet application development, marketing, customer service, supply chain integration and sales. D-ISC is a wholly owned subsidiary of Bartercard PLC, the world's largest trade exchange.

He has 15 years of solid experience in making companies profitable using the internet.

Jason Cozens’s Specialties:

Entrepreneurial online business development: online strategy, online marketing, User Interface design, internet application design and scoping. eCommerce. Affiliate, email, PPC & search engine marketing (SEO & PPC).
