    Columbia Credit Union

    (Non-Profit; Banking industry)

    January 2003 — Present (6 years 11 months)

    Columbia Credit Union is one of the largest local financial institutions in Clark County. Based in Vancouver, Washington, it holds $759 million in assets and serves more than 64,000 members.

    Create multi-channel, online marketing campaigns; manage the content, design and implementation of Columbia’s public website; increase product sales by optimizing natural search and PPC advertising; develop and analyze online tracking to achieve measurable gains; plan, research and make online advertising buys; act as the point person for electronic marketing vendors; write and design graphics for print and electronic mediums.

    • Created an email campaign about Columbia’s Appreciation Certificate and conducted A/B to increase open rates, resulting in $11.5 million in new deposits (43 percent above the marketing goal).

    • Leveraged sales initiatives through various online delivery channels including promotional microsites. Created www.WinLoanPayments.com in 2009 and www.Win2008Smartcar.com in 2008. Honored with first place for websites at the 2008 WCUL Spectrum Marketing Awards and a 2008 merit award from CUNA for online marketing.

    • Initiated pay-per-click advertising in 2006 and have continued to successfully manage our PPC budget with conversion rates that continually trend upward. Columbia’s use of Google AdWords was featured in a white paper by the Filene Research Institute as an example for other credit unions.
