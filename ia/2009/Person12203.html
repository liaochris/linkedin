Amy Li’s Summary

WeiLeen Group (weileen.com) is a private company with three operations:

1. DonxingUSA (donxingusa.com) is one of the leading custom fashion accessory, gift, jewelry, headwear, craft, and lanyard manufacturers based in China. DonxingUSA has an overseas office located in the U.S. to directly interface with our clients residing in the Americas and Europe.

2. East Colors (Dongcai) Packing and Printing Co, Ltd. (eastcolors.com) specializes in packaging and printing solutions for its OEM customers such as Nike, BOSS, Staples, and others.

3. Xi Pu Scale Model Co, Ltd. (xpscalemodel.com) is a high-end scale model manufacturer and supplier for Fortune Global 500 Companies across multiple industries. XP Scale Model has participated in some important projects such as Shanghai and PuDong Development, Shanghai PuDong International Airport (PVG), Peru LNG Project, World Exhibition Shanghai 2010, China Nuclear Energy, China 60th National Anniversary Beijing Parade, Japan Hitachi Construction, Japan Bandai, and others
