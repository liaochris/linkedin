Jose da Silva’s Summary

Jose joined GuestCentric in June 2007 as Director of Development with responsibility for the development of the software that powers GuestCentric’s innovative services. Jose is an experienced leader in Web 2.0 development that has hands-on experience of what it takes to launch successful scalable applications.

Prior to joining GuestCentric, Jose was a leading systems architect at Segula Technologies, allocated at Sapo, the leading Portuguese portal with over 500 million visits per month. At Sapo, Jose launched context-sensitive rich media advertising targeted at diverse Web 2.0 properties. Before joining Sapo in 2005, Jose was responsible for the Internet development at Chiptec, where he led intranet and web application projects including complex integration with leading ERP and CRM systems and e-commerce platforms like PayPal.
