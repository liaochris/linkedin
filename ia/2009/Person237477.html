Traci Rochon’s Summary

I am a licensed real estate agent in Virginia, Maryland, and DC. I am an owner of a Keller Williams Realty in Alexandria, Va. I lead a real estate sales team that specializes in selling short sale and REO property. I own a real estate investment company.

My real estate sales team specializes in selling REO and short sale properties in Virginia, DC, and Maryland. Our highest priority with REO sales is property preservation and immediate sales. I have a full team to serve our REO clients including runners, buyers agents, accountants, and administration. I currently complete more than 200 BPOs each month in a timely and accurate manner.

Our Services Include:

* Completing occupancy checks within less than 24 hours
* Securing and rekeying properties
* Cash for Keys
* Evictions
* Clean Outs
* Winterization
* Weekly property inspections
* Monthly status and market reporting
* BPO's
* A full scale marketing and advertising campaign
* Consistent communication with our Asset Managers from prelist to close

Dan and Traci & Consultants with Keller Williams Realty are a professional real estate sales team in Alexandria, Va. In addition to guiding clients with traditional sales, we also assist homeowners that have a financial hardship and are upside down on their mortgage and do not know that they may have options other than foreclosure or bankruptcy. Our team believes in applying strengths; collectively, we serve our clients better than we could individually. Feel free to contact us anytime. We welcome the opportunity to make a difference for you.
