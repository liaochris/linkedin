Christine M. Nicastro’s Summary

Licensed to Practice at the U.S District Court for the District of Massachusetts; Supreme Court of the United States of America; Supreme Judicial Court for the Commonwealth of Massachusetts; Supreme Court for the State of New Hampshire; as well as Licensed Real Estate Broker. Lead Counsel for Appellant overturning cases on appeal by the Massachusetts Appeals Court; published in Northeast Reporter and Rescript Opinions. Lead trial counsel in over 40 jury trial cases. Co-Author of the publication, Massachusetts Discovery Skills For the Paralegal, Copyright, 1991; Faculty lecturer for Professional Education Systems, Inc., (PESI), continuing legal education; Community Pulse Television Talk Show, was personally chosen for and appeared for a 30-minute personal interview on the “Status of the Paralegal Profession” with talk show host, George Norton, on Cablevision, 1992. Speaker for Massachusetts Bar Association Elder Care Law division on Alternatives to Nursing Home Care. Goals are to provide Effective, Efficient, Ethical, Economic, Legal Services to Clients with an Excellent Result.

Christine M. Nicastro’s Specialties:

Real Estate Conveyancing; Condominium Conversions; Real Estate Litigation in Superior and Land Court; Title Agent for First American Title Insurance Company; Divorce, Custody, Visitation, Domestic relations; Probate and Estate Planning; Criminal Defense; Personal Injury; Bankruptcy; Immigration. All forms of Litigation.
