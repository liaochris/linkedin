Deepak Srinivasan’s Summary

Deepak Srinivasan has over 20 years experience that spans general management, strategic sales, entrepreneurship and product management.

He has spent the last decade in the area of consumers, devices and media interaction with hands on experience in building and launching end-to-end delivery models.

Deepak now shares his time between Bangalore and Silicon Valley, helping incubate and mentor startups. He most recently helped incubate and joined the Board of C-Drive Careers, a web 2.0 recruiting company focused on startups and high tech. From Aug '07 through Jan '08, Deepak also headed Mobiance, a location based services company where he continues to serve on the Board.

As VP of Product Management at 24/7 Real Media, Deepak is responsible for the coordination of product management, product marketing, and product innovation worldwide, exiting after the acquisition by WPP. Prior to this, Deepak was with Reliance Infocomm (India) and was involved in planning and engineering for a multi-million home Video-over-IP rollout.

Before moving to Reliance, Deepak co-founded Enscaler Inc., a provider of middleware, applications and services for rights managed and secure streaming media. As CEO, Deepak grew Enscaler to multi-million dollar revenues with over 40 Fortune 1000 clients in enterprise, education and entertainment.

Deepak's past experience also includes working with the Product Management team at Sharewave, a wireless home networking chip startup, since acquired by Cirrus Logic. As Director of Product Marketing with Elcom Technologies, a home networking startup, Deepak launched several consumer electronics products into North American retail, OEM and service provider channels. Earlier in his career Deepak consulted in software /IT in the US, Europe and India.

Deepak has a BS in Aerospace Engineering from IIT Kanpur and is a passionate military aviation and geopolitics buff. He counts a flight in a vintage Tiger Moth among his happiest moments.
