Miguel Todaro’s Summary

He has been consultant for important corporations on marketing, multimedia and e-commerce projects.
His unique methodology challenges conventional wisdom and delivers meaningful change and measurable Return On Investment. He achieve exponential results by unleashing an array of integrated, strategic and tactical online functions.
In 1997 he developed one of the first automotive e-commerce project on the net, for the Italian company Fiat Auto International Corporation (FIAT).

He has been privileged to help international major companies including IBM, Peugeot, Oracle, Siemens, Herbalife, GE, UNICEF, Chevron and many others.
He also developed experimental multimedia e-learning ventures for different companies in Canada, including BP and Methanex Corp.

He is also the author of the book "Internet Marketing Methods Revealed", published by Atlantic Publishing Co. (ISBN-10: 1601382650)

Miguel Todaro’s Specialties:

SEO Strategy and implementation, Strategic Planning, Sales Strategy and Planning, more than 15 years of Marketing and corporate communication experience, strong knowledge in Automotive and Software Development industries, 7 years of experience as a University Teacher.
