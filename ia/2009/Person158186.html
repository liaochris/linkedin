Mark Schiller’s Summary

Mark has a wide range of experience for a number of the worlds largest companies in a range of market sectors.

He has a wide range of experience and able to deliver solutions either to small sme's or global corporates. A realist when it comes to the delivery of security and a firm believer in aligning security to the strategic goals of the client business.

He has excellent risk analysis skills and in depth experience of engineering security into business processes whilst maintaining a level of balance between security and business needs.

A firm believer that security needs to have a clear demonstratable business benefit and not a box checking excercise and that its also about the people that operate the business and ensuring processes are workable and delivering real business value and security that meets the business goals.

Security in most industrys shouldnt be the security tail wagging the business.....At times balancing pragmatism against reputational risk and of course regulatory risks is a difficult task but thats what I find interesting about security, its a challenge to his intellect and ability to use all the required softskills to bring these issues to a successful resolution.

The magicial magic bullet for security simply doesnt exist. Part of my role is also to separate out the snake oil solutions from the real business benefits.

Mark furthermore enjoys a challenge and has undertaken for various organisations security turnaround projects and engaged with organisations at all levels including board level of multinational organisations on both an adhoc short term basis and a more longer term relationship.
