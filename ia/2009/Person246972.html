Alan Whitehead’s Summary

Began my first business at the age of 25 providing various types of office partitions, etc. to clients including; British Telecom, 3i, Sotheby's, Arab Insurance Group, Letts Diaries, NIG, Royal Philharmonic Orchestra, Weidenfeld Publishers

During the eleven years spent building & running this business, also `rescued' and developed into modern offices a previously derelict four-storey Singer Sewing Machine factory in Southwark.

Started the `Bureautel Business Club’, providing a `Virtual Office' presence for SMEs without the need for any actual office space. Services included:- - Telephone Answering in own company name, Mail Forwarding/Collection, remote secretarial assistance, etc. (all before the advent of the web & email!)

Spent six years or so helping to build my last company into a cash-positive, debt-free, highly profitable business, where clients included:- esure, Heineken UK, Marks & Spencer, ING-Barings, Nescot & Schal, I sold out to my co-directors/shareholders to spend some time `smelling the roses'.

Got fed up after a couple of months of doing nothing but play golf (I never thought I would ever hear myself saying that) so decided to take the plunge back into the world of commerce.

As a `poacher-turned-gamekeeper' I now advise end-user clients helping them to steer clear of the minefield of getting involved in their own office moves, interior fit-outs and refurbishments where they either haven't got the time and/or necessary expertise in-house to design, co-ordinate and manage the projects themselves!!

So WHO do YOU know who may be contemplating an office move, fit-out or refurbishment but also has a `million' other things to do to manage their own businesses.

For a no-obligation discussion about how I may be able to help `lighten the load' give me a quick call or a brief email outlining your requirements - I'd be delighted to help in any way I can.
