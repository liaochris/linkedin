Eric Stevenson’s Summary

This comprehensive background was intentionally structured over time to provide knowledge and experience in as many management areas as possible. The depth and breadth of this experience with companies from diverse financial industries to marketing and promotion provides a sound foundation from which to build in the face of an ever-changing and complex online publishing environment.

Eric is recognized in America as one of the Nation's leading Management Analysts. Fully versed in all aspects of Securities Law, he provides highly skilled services virtually unattainable in an individual capacity in corporate America today.

Eric's skills have been adapted to the Internet publishing community and
have grown in management stature, along with the web's global complexities
and rules and regulatory changes, which his years of corporate development
and financial management skills have aided him greatly in his capacity to
work in a virtual global business environment as an editor and publisher.
