Tonya Rochette’s Summary

My experience in Human Resources has been shaped by exposure to vastly different organizations including large corporate manufacturing/retail, big box retail divisional management, small company distributor, as well as medium size global manufacturing. This has allowed me to gain exposure to a wealth of HR experiences and the obstacles presented, developing my background to be well diverse. I am particularly passionate and strong in the technical HR areas of employee law, performance management and leadership development.

Tonya Rochette’s Specialties:
