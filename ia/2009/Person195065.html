Rafael Diaz-Tushman’s Summary

While an undergraduate at Columbia University, I co-founded a catering company. In 2003 I designed and started two internet-based businesses, a Netflix competitor and a fantasy sports website. My experiences as an online merchant using various payment processors has prompted me to pursue starting my own, which will be launched in 2009. I exited the catering company in 2005 and the Netflix competitor in April 2007. I received an MBA from the Darden Graduate School of Business’s in May 2008, where I was elected President of the Entrepreneurship and Venture Capital Club. Currently I run a web-design firm and am starting a payment processing company.
