Sara Ortloff Khoury’s Summary

Currently I am Walmart.com's Senior Director, User Experience.

I have over ten years of experience leading teams to deliver broad portfolio of web-based user experience and ecommerce solutions, as well as award-winning interactive marketing communications across many platforms.

At Bank of America, I was a Senior Vice President and Creative Director for the User-Centered Design & Research group. This internal team works within the eCommerce organization to consult-on and execute the majority of the organization's online banking, ATM and mobile initiatives. I oversaw an Editorial and Design team that executed major site releases and a Marketing team that developed onsite advertising and promotional campaigns. I led a major initiative to define and set a vision for the future of the online banking channel.

At Organic, I was the Executive Creative Director, and later Executive Director of an Internet Professional Services business unit in the San Francisco office. There I oversaw the successful launch of sites for Nike, Starbucks, The Home Depot, Washington Mutual, Macys, and Sony.

Before joining Organic, I was Vice President and Design Director for Ikonic Interactive, where I built an interdisciplinary Creative department. While at Ikonic, I directed ITV, CD-ROM, kiosk, and Web-based projects for a variety of clients, including General Motors, Gap, Microsoft, and Time Warner. Prior to my work with Ikonic, I developed educational CD-ROMs for the K-12 market with institutions such as The New England Aquarium and Software Toolworks, and consulted for companies such as The Voyager Company, and Synapse Technologies.

I hold a B.F.A. in Design from Pratt Institute, and an M.F.A. with a focus on interactive communications from The Art Center College of Design. I've been an active public speaker. I've presented various creative development topics at industry conferences, and taught seminars in user-centric design.
