James Mathewson’s Summary

* I am a proven leader of large organizational content teams. My legacy in these positions is to improve systems and processes in order to ensure higher content quality and more efficient content processes.

* I chair ibm.com's Search and Syndication Council, which engages dozens of subject matter experts in content creation and delivery to improve search and syndication effectiveness for ibm.com.

* I have extensive experience as a writing instructor and in delivering self-help writing and editing educational resources.

* My M.S. degree in Scientific and Technical Communication was completed on August 31, 2008. My thesis relates to writing for the Web as distinct from writing for print media.

* My career goal at IBM is to continue to lead content standards organizations, which improve the ibm.com user experience through better, more integrated Web content and architecture.

James Mathewson’s Specialties:
