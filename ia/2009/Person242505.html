Vicki Donnowitz’s Summary

Over 25 years of sales, marketing and general management knowledge and skills in both start up, entrepreneurial, rapid growth and corporate companies. I have built and managed a vast array of customer focused sales organizations: direct, indirect, independent, field sales, national account managers, direct store delivery, retail and direct mail. I have been on both the buy and sell side of the transaction. I work best is a fast paced, multi tasking, think outside the box organization with well defined vision and goals. It is critical in today's market to be flexible, always forward thinking to anticipate and best prepare for what may come next.

I am an innovative and solution-oriented senior executive with extensive multi channel experience in real estate, consumer wholesale products and the retail/catalog business. My optimistic, collaborative, and entrepreneurial nature gives me the ability to inspire people to maximize achievement. I am fair yet decisive, able to make tough choices that benefit the business. I have a strong committed “can do” work ethic and I am a creative solution oriented thinker.
