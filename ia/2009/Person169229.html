Martin Shanahan BA LLB MBA’s Summary

My expertise covers director level appointments, senior managerial, professional and technical appointments across a diverse range of functions. My profession lets me work with people at many levels in a wide range of organizations discussing career options and opportunities.

I enjoy opening doors, creating innovative candidate identification and attraction campaigns and selling my clients to prospective employees. I am very good at what I do and can demonstrate how to secure effective results - contact me!

My career to this point is in two parts. For much of the last 13 years I have actively worked as a recruitment consultant either in consulting firms retained by client organisations or as a senior internal recruiter.

Prior to joining the recruitment industry I gained some 14 years' experience in a variety of editorial, marketing and general management roles with Australia's pre-eminent law publisher.
