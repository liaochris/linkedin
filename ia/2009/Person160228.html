Michael Fiore’s Summary

Michael Fiore is an award-winning director, producer, and editor for film and television, with offices in New York and Los Angeles. He is a storyteller and craftsman with a keen ability to work in any genre and medium. His versatility, combined with his unparalleled ability for creative collaboration have made him a highly respected and sought-after filmmaker.

Fiore’s talents have been recognized by Steven Spielberg and Mark Burnett, as they named him a "Top 100 Filmmaker" for their FOX filmmaker series "On the Lot".

Fiore's works as a director, producer, and editor include: National commercial campaigns for such brands as A&E's "Biography Magazine" and Genealogy.com; "Stalking Delirious", the behind-the-scenes featurette for Tom DiCillo's feature film "Delirious" starring Steve Buscemi and Alison Lohman; "Making Game 6", the behind-the-scenes featurette for the feature film "Game 6" starring Robert Downey Jr. and Michael Keaton; and Jack Ketchum's "The Girl Next Door" (editor), the horror feature film which Stephen King called "the first authentically shocking American film since 'Henry: Portrait of a Serial Killer' over 20 years ago".

Fiore's film "Cadaverous" was broadcast nationally on the Sci-Fi Channel and screened at over 25 film festivals where it received multiple top honors. Joseph Stefano, screenwriter of Alfred Hitchock's "Psycho" remarked, "'Cadaverous' is tense and sub-textually terrifying...a stroke of cinematic genius."

Fiore is currently developing several feature films, including the mockumentary "Release!" starring indie filmmaking icon Tom DiCillo.

Michael Fiore’s Specialties:

Balances technical and performance direction with ease; Integrates visual effects with live action seamlessly; Experienced green screen director; Owner and operator of Final Cut Pro HD and Avid Xpress edit systems in New York and Los Angeles; Well-versed in Avid, Final Cut Pro, After FX, Photoshop, and other supporting graphics software; Storyboard artist and pre-viz specialist; Team player and people person.
