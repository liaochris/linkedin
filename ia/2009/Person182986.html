Paul Fleet’s Summary

My experience specialise in the provision of IT resource related and IT project delivery related solutions.
My remit covers everything from providing straight IT recruitment (Contract and permanent) services through to providing fully outsourced, off-site software development projects and a wide range of solutions in between, opening many new accounts over this time including Blue Chip companies and smaller, privately owned businesses.

Managing, mentoring, targeting and directing the sales function of both the Recruitment business and the Managed Services Business across multiple sectors with IT as the core skill, including the Legal Sector, Retail, Investment Banking and Financial Services, Cards and Payments, Data Warehousing and Business Inteligence, Telecoms, Media, Travel, On-Line gaming and gambling, Mobile Banking and Pre-Paid Cards.

This gives RDF a helicopter view of the market and a network within the industry which keeps us tuned into the ‘passive’ candidate scene. We have the added advantage of having over 200 of our very own technical staff from Program Managers and Solutions Architects to Software Developers and Testers who can technically screen & test our candidates or even fill the gaps when required.

We consistently keep ourselves up to date with regulations, compliance and developments within the sector and adapt our working practices accordingly. We add value to the standard recruitment supply model, a boast often made but rarely delivered on by many in the industry.

Our extensive customer networks allow us to leverage collaborative relationships and to deliver recruitment solutions at a strategic level. We monitor and adjust to specific sector requirements so that we can identify and target a range of options for our customers that go beyond the limits of traditional recruitment agencies.
