Garry Holland’s Summary

I was in the civil and mechanical engineering industry for near on 20 years, with 9 of those years in the armed forces as a mechanical engineer.

Due to the recession I went into teaching English as a second language, TEFL teacher, utilising my experience in the engineering field to teach business English students.

With the help of other professionals in the field I set up my own language school 15 years ago teaching all ages and levels, specialising mainly in Cambridge English exams at all levels for general and business English as well as specialist exams to enter university in the UK and the students own country.

Worked closely with a teaching training company training students to teach English to enable them to have a better chance of getting a job in their own country.

For future I intend to continue doing this for quite some time, but on a much smaller and more personnal basis in order to deal with specific areas of the language the students have problems with. Also designing specialist courses to suit the individual students requirements.
