Martijn van Balken’s Summary

Experienced as TV Product Manager in interactive TV, advanced advertising, VOD and Set Top Box development (Sky+). Responsible for product definition, content strategy and closing Pan-European commercial deals.

Defined and developed new customer focused interactive advertising products and commercially deployed on Europe's largest cable company (UPC) and satellite (Sky).

Over 50 interactive TV applications launched including iTVPortal and Games Portal and multiple enhanced TV and i-ad campaigns.

Succesfully founded a company in IP Telephony with leading Blue Chip clients like Philips and KPN Telecom.

Holding a MSc in Digital TV, Management & Production - graduated with honors - and a Bachelor in Industrial Product Design Engineering.
