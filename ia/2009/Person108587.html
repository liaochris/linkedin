Jeff Polino’s Summary

VP of Sales for The Alternative Board (TAB), bringing together owners of privately held companies to grow and optimize their businesses. Advisory boards we create, manage & facilitate are comprised of local business owners, presidents, & CEOs who run non-competing businesses.

TAB members meet monthly—led by an executive coach—to share their business expertise and address each others’ challenges confidentially. Members learn from one another's successes and mistakes enabling them to overcome challenges and seize new opportunities.

► I enjoy helping our members to identify and take advantage of great opportunities. With over 25 years of leadership experience in sales, sales management and sales training, I have earned a reputation as a leading entrepreneurial sales executive and sales trainer.

► An entrepreneur who is also an “intrapreneur”. I truly believe that your attitude and outlook determines the sale. Henry Ford said, “Whether you think that you can, or that you can't, you are usually right”. I believe this is true – we need to understand that we do not need to be victims and that power is not external – it’s internal. You can either be buying limitations or selling opportunities. I love being a source of giving hope and opportunity – finding a path to a better way.

► The key is understanding before you attempt to be understood. You need to understand people’s agenda and why they feel they can’t do what they need to do. I have an active interest in psychology and with the art of communication sciences. It’s these interests that really help me to understand, connect with people and help them realize the potential in their efforts.

► Extensive experience as a privately held franchise business owner with a knack for leading and training high performance sales teams. Develop cohesive teams that focus on increasing both sales and overall profitability.
