Gina Danner’s Summary

Gina is CEO of Mail Print, Inc. and leads the on-going growth of the organization. Her experience includes marketing, writing, sales and entrepreneurship. Her desire is to fuel growth in her team, clients, friends, business associates, and her community.

Gina speaks on a variety of topics including strategic relationship development, business building efforts, entrepreneurship, and personal growth and wellness.

She is passionate about her community and involved in a number of organizations including Operation Breakthrough (www.operationbreakthrough.org), WIN for KC (http://sportkc.org/winhome.aspx?pgID=867), KTEC Pipeline (www.ktecpipeline.com) and the Luekemia and Lymphoma Society of Kansas CIty.
