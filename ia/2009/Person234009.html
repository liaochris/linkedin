Timothy Quinn’s Summary

Senior technology leader with over ten years of hands-on experience in the interactive media sector, possessing a strong portfolio of project management and technical implementation aptitudes with a particular emphasis on enterprise-scale application development and project management process re-engineering. An accomplished author and instructor who has taught at both the City University of New York and New York University; courseware developed for NYU is still offered as a key requirement of their project management curriculum.

Timothy Quinn’s Specialties:

Project management... information design and usability... technical specification and business documentation... process workflow development... application development... team leadership.
