Victor Zambrano’s Summary

Born in Boston MA (US), I graduated from Architectural School (VE). I worked for Creative Communications (IT), a Milano based communication studio as a graphic designer, where I created and implemented stands, graphics, presentations and live events for companies such as Pirelli, General Electric and Heineken.

I obtained a Master in Design degree from Domus Academy (IT). I was hired by Domus Academy Research Center as a Design/Visual Researcher. As such I created, designed and implemented, among a multidisciplinary group of professionals, research projects focusing on Interaction and Interface design.

In the following years I specialised in Interaction Design and Information Architecture working with companies as Domus Academy Research Center (http://www.domusacademy.com), Interaction Design Institute Ivrea (http://www.interaction-ivrea.it), Wikot Technologies (http://www.wikot.com), Fluidtime GmbH (http://www.fluidtime.at) and LBi London (www.lbi.com/london).

User Experience Design, Information Architecture, User Interface Design, Interaction Design, New Services/Products, Design Research & Development.
