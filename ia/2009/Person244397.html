Vivek Merani’s Summary

Head the International Business division of Magnon, which is a leading Indian Interactive Media company. Have been with the company and in the business of Web Design, Web Development, E-commerce, Internet Marketing since the internet really started taking shape in India, since 2000. My role in the company has continuously evolved from being the front line Business Development person to a Key Account Manager and now to the Sr. Vice President for the International Business.
