Rex Robinson’s Summary

After spending nearly 15 years in real estate acquisition, I found myself with an opportunity to be involved with my first love, sports.

For the past 5 years I have built a growing customer base that allowed me to receive salesman of the year honors in only my second full year with a 32 year old company, and set a sales record for one month of over $175,000. A 65%increase of over the previous sales record. I did this with dedication to one principle.

IT'S ALL ABOUT THE CUSTOMER.

I will always strive to do what is right by my customers; from the quality of my products, to the value both real and percieved by each and every customer.

GREAT PRODUCTS, FAIR PRICE & EXCELLENT SERVICE.

This is the hallmark of great companies and I want Total Team Sports, LLC to be great.

Rex Robinson’s Specialties:

Much of the success I have experienced has been my desire to help my coaches raise money to benefit their programs and thereby improve the quality of their gear from uniforms to equipment. My fundraising packages get the young guys and girls excited about the fundraisng so common in today's economy. The parents love it too because much of the merchandise they normally pay retail prices for is included in their packages. It's definitely a win-win for everybody.
