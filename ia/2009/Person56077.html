David Czarnecki’s Summary

DAVID R. CZARNECKI BIO


David R. Czarnecki is an executive with over 10 years experience in operations, manufacturing and engineering. He has particular expertise in the design, manufacture, procurement and testing of hardware for automotive, aerospace and power generation applications. He is adept at identifying flaws in processes in time to resolve them in advance of a crisis. He seeks to reduce costs while improve manufacturing processes through lean and six sigma initiatives. He has worked in project management and strategic planning throughout his career and is currently directing operations for a nationwide provider of aerospace and industrial gas turbine services. Czarnecki has about 80 direct reports, manages P & L and gives significant attention to establishing best practices in operations. He handles over $16 million worth of hardware procurement annually through an entirely outsourced supply chain. He monitors vendor capability, capacity and value. He has consulted for a private equity firm in the due diligence stage of their acquisition of a turbine company. He possesses experience in training, developing, coaching, and managing staff to achieve defined performance objectives. He is a hands-on leader, and has helped each of his companies improve its productivity across operations.
Although Czarnecki has a strong technical, operations and business background, he has a strong belief in effective communications. He admires executive teams that establish both trust and efficiency. His management style is direct with emphasis on employee ownership. Czarnecki utilizes his undergraduate Engineering degree in combination with his graduate business degree to provide financially sound solutions to technically challenging issues.
