    (Architecture & Planning industry)

    June 2008 — Present (11 months)

    Building Passion presents 100 passionate and influential women working in the building business. We would like to depict their portraits and life profundities in our book 'Building Passion'. We do this to show the building world why they work with such passion and how these women are indeed responsible for the extra balance needed in today’s modern, healthy companies, thus proving how these ambitions help the positive development of the companies in which they work.

    Do you have a balanced, passionate and influential woman in your organisation? Does she help towards a more balanced work atmosphere? Or, are you in search of these women because you realize the necessity of a balanced influence?

    Update Building Passion!

    (Architecture & Planning industry)

    June 2008 — Present (11 months)

    Latest NEWS: As Leading Lady of Building Passion, Laura has been very successful these last few months in organizing the whole book and photographs of these strong women.     Our TOP- list includes Liesbeth van der Pol, Rijksbouwmeester, Sybilla Dekkers, vmlg Minister VROM, Anneke de Vries, CEO ING Real Estate, Francine Houben, Jeanne Dekkers and Joke Vos, all well known architects, Anne-Marie Rakhorst, general manager Search, Irma van Oort, architect and co-director KCAP, and Pamela Bouwmeester, Directievoorzitter NS Poort.

    The interviews and photo shoots have given both Tosca and Laura unlimited inspiration for their book. These are some VERY special ladies! And the list continues to grow....
