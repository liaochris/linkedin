  • Partner & Co-Founder

    BACKLINE Digital

    (Entertainment industry)

    September 2009 — Present (2 months)

    After 5 years, we are excited to be taking a broader approach to the digital music space.

    STICKMAN Entertainment is now BACKLINE Digital.

    We are maintaining our focus on musicians while shifting our revenue model to the commerce of virtual products.

    BACKLINE represents the digital merchandising rights of celebrity musicians in the online space. Our virtual retailing platform allows us to sell digital products across virtual worlds, games and social networks.

    For those of you who aren’t aware of the “lingo” here are a few definitions and industry facts which make us so excited about this space!

    - Virtual goods are items which may include clothing for avatars, furniture and artwork that are purchased using in-game/in-world credits which are acquired by spending real world dollars.

    - $2+ billion is spent annually in the worldwide digital goods market and it is forecasted to grow to $6 billion by 2013 according to industry experts at Piper Jaffray.

    - Branded products dominate 70% of items sold in the “real world” global retail market, representing a huge potential opportunity in the virtual world.
