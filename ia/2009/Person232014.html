Gene Urban’s Summary

The Urban Team includes Ron Urban, Gene Urban and a dedicated staff of people who believe that "Excellent" is the minimum standard and "WOW!" is the goal.

Ron Urban brings 18 years of experience to the process. He is a detail guy with a big heart. He listens well and simply loves to negotiate on behalf of his client. Ron brings a wealth of knowledge on every aspect of buying and selling homes, land and investment property.

Gene Urban provides marketing, analysis and Internet support to the team as well as assisting Ron. Gene is the author of UrbanLifeBlog.com... our blog site and as well as being active in Central and Downtown Phoenix politics and policy.

Gene Urban’s Specialties:

We focus on residential & investment real estate in downtown, central and north Phoenix as well as the Scottsdale market.

As listing agents we are way ahead of the pack. We create a unique marketing plan for every home we list and offer Internet and traditional marketing that is uncommon in the real estate industry.
