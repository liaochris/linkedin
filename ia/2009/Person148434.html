Marcello Rinaldi (rinaldi.marcello Gmail)’s Summary

Marcello is a skilled digital manager, with 8-year experience (2000-now) dealing with major companies (Intesa San Paolo, Miroglio Textile Group, Bang&Olufsen, MvAgusta Motorcycles, Rai Italian Television, Sky Digital Tv, Costa Cruises, Iulm University, etc.).

He served (2002-2007) as one of the managers at NextMedia Lab (www.nextmedialab.it), helping the company build a solid marketing function; working directly with top clients; developing new product as R&D Manager for the last few years.

He is now (2004-now) involved in Unist Distance Learning Company (www.unist.it), responsible for digital learning projects with major Italian Universities (ie. Turin University, Rome Sapienza University, UniSofia Digital University, Padua University, etc.).

He has been giving classes for the last 5 years in major Italian Universities about digital media (Bocconi University, Turin University, Iulm University, Politecnico di Milano, etc.). As a researcher about digital media, he published Social Networks, a look forward in “Web 2.0. Internet has changed” (Sole 24Ore Publishing, Milan 2007), and “Tokyo Innovative Communication Solutions for the public space” (funded research, Tokyo 2003).

He works (2002-now) with European leading futurist Professor Vito Di Bari (Innovation Designer of Milan 2015 Expo) (www.vitodibari.net/en), on innovation projects for private corporations, universities and institutions.

Previously (2000-2001), Marcello worked for Iulm University web administration office for the development of the academic website (www.iulm.it) and developed private web projects for mid-size companies.

Languages: Marcello is Italian mothertongue and speaks fluent English and Spanish.

Technical skills: Cms: Joomla, Wordpress. LMS: Docebo, Moodle. E-learning: Articulate, QuizMaker. Html, Css, Javascript. Apache server, MySql basic knowledge. Net Intelligence, advanced level.

Open Networker: rinaldi . marcello @ gmail . com.
