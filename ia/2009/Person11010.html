Ambar Kumar’s Summary

A Mechanical Engineer from Gorakhpur University, I received a general management MBA from the European School of Management and Technology (ESMT) Berlin, where I was a SAP mentored scholar and a member of the esmt Investment Club.

I have more than 6 years of work-ex diversified in multiple Industries. In my last position with Infosys, I led multiple projects for key customers in the United States, analysing large business systems and managing multi million dollar systems implementations.

Besides my strong analytical and financial backgrounds coupled with IT infrastructure, I see my key strengths in motivating and empowering my team members, project management, business analysis and client interfacing
