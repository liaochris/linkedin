Carol Brodie’s Summary

A luxury brand expert with 20+ years experience creating award-winning marketing and communications campaigns for international brands. Gained ability to innovate, shape and articulate brand vision through agency, client, media and retail background. Passionate entrepreneur with a proven ability to lead by example and drive successful business practices. A sophisticated global brand ambassador with an in-depth understanding of the motivations and needs of the HNW.

Brand architect, marketing and communications strategist, luxury spokeswoman, television personality, image consultant, business development, special events guru and advertising executive depict the professional talents and passions of Carol Brodie, Founder and Chief Luxury Officer, Carol Brodie & Co.

Ms. Brodie is the first executive to hold the title, Chief Luxury Officer. In 2005, CurtCo Media, publisher of Robb Report, Worth, Showboats International, Art & Antiques and a family of titles targeted to the world’s most affluent readers, appointed Ms. Brodie to this unprecedented position.

Assuming this leadership role was a natural transition for Ms. Brodie, who joined the magazine publishing powerhouse after eight years as Global Director of Communications for world-renowned jeweler Harry Winston, Inc. In that position, Ms. Brodie has been credited with expanding the reach and image of the old-world brand.

Ms. Brodie has become well-known through her appearances on entertainment, business and lifestyle programs, discussing topics such as fashion and business trends, luxury living and Hollywood style. Her leadership and voice is regularly sought after by organizations and individuals who seek her knowledge and expertise on the global affluent mindset.
