David Hurst’s Summary

David Hurst is MD of the Onrec group and oversees operations across the UK, US and EMEA,

Since inception Onrec has grown from a monthly newsletter to a well respected international magazine, online news portal, recruitment industry directory and conference provider. Onrec’s reach now covers 57 countries and sends over 90,000 daily email newsletters.

Onrec is established as the worlds leading resource for information, comment and opinion about the online recruitment industry, it’s trends, it’s key players and it’s future. For more information please visit www.onrec.com.

As part of Onrec’s continuing mission to further discussion between HR, recruiters and the online recruitment industry, David has chaired conferences in Sydney, Las Vegas, Hong Kong, London, Brussels, New York and Chicago.

Onrec Expo and Global online recruitment conference will take place in Chicago 3rd & 4th November 2009 Donald E. Stephens Convention Centre
www.onrec.com/expo2009

He is a regular contributor to Hr.com and Classified Intelligence LLC and founder of the Online Recruitment Marketing Council www.ormc.org.uk.

In 2006 David was appointed Director to the Board of the Digital Content Forum where he represent the ORMC and the interests of the UK online recruitment industry to UK government.
