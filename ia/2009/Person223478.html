Stephen Sellars’s Summary

Steve Sellars is currently a Senior Consultant with LeapFrog Systems. He is actively engaged in business analysis and technical project management for enterprise application development in the brokerage industry.

He has been a key contributor on a variety of large-scale projects in the U.S., Europe and Asia, adding value by providing Process Consulting, Project Management, Business Analytics and Application Development leadership through maintaining shared expectations, building strong client relationships and technical experience.
