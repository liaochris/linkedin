Joshua Ellis’s Summary

Currently working as IT manager within the Creative Services Unit of Outreach Marketing and Communications and the Pennsylvania State University. Area of focus is technical implementation and operational management of online / e-mail marketing solutions for higher education.

What I hope to offer in my daily work:
* The ability to explain complex issues to individuals of varying technical skill at all levels of the organization.
* Enough comfort with public speaking to present in the front of a boardroom, classroom, or conference.
* The natural curiosity and strategic thinking ability to stay current with the evolving technical needs of online marketing and communication.
* The tact, diplomacy, and interpersonal communication skill to work within a campus community (faculty, students, staff, and administration).
* The knowledge and skill to interact competently with writers, editors, designers, Web developers, and technical staff.
* The project management, change management, and staff management experience to work effectively with supervisors and direct reports in a team environment.
* The integrity to speak up when something is wrong, and to admit when I don’t know something.
