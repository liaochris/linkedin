Nicole Governile’s Summary

I am a believer in conscious living. Be kind to each other, the environment and yourself.

I help empower people to create a more balanced, energetic and healthy life through our proven therapeutic lifestyle program, FirstLine Therapy. Bringing conscious awareness to the fact that what we put in our bodies and how we take care of ourselves if our first line of defense against dis-ease. Through diet, exercise, nutritional supplementation and stress reduction, FirstLine Therapy is effective in preventing, as well as treating and reducing chronic lifestyle diseases such as diabetes, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, weight concerns, hormone imbalance, fatigue and many others.

I also support our green industries through my volunteer positions with the Riverwalk Trust Sustainability Task Force and the U.S. Green Building Council. I do my best to walk lightly and support business and others who do too.

I am also in the process of achieving my Yoga Alliance teacher certification and look forward to more fully integrating this aspect of my life into my profession. Namaste!
