Krista Guerrero’s Summary

In 1995, I graduated from Diablo Valley College in Pleasant Hill, CA, with a degree in Major Broadcast Communications. I did multiple internships in TV, film, and radio - most notably a three-year internship with Chancellor Radio in San Francisco (KABL, KBGG/KISQ, KNEW, and KSAN). I then moved across the street to Bonneville International Corporation and took a job as Promotion Coordinator for Z95.7 (KZQZ), where I also started learning graphic design.

In 2003, I packed up my bags and moved to San Luis Obispo, CA. I went to work for American General Media, where I was Director of Marketing and Promotions for six radio stations (KWWW, KZOZ, KWSR, KKJG, KIQO, and KKAL). Working in a smaller market, I had to learn to be creative without a budget, and I truly feel that this restriction strengthened my creative thinking skills.

I took a position with SMG in Oakland as Marketing Manager for the Oakland Arena and Network Associates Coliseum. I loved every aspect of it, from putting on the shows and working with promoters to becoming a boxing event producer. This was a newly created position, so I had the privileged task of building an in-house marketing department from the ground up.

In 2006,I decided to leave the Bay Area and move to Sacramento, CA, where I got hired on at First Broadcasting, which owned KXCL and KREL. I was Director of Marketing. I then segued out of radio and into the mortgage business. Partners Mortgage in Roseville, creating marketing pieces and programs for their loan officers, for about a year before landing a job as Marketing Communications Manager for New Generation Software in Sacramento. After 13 years of being a graphic designer, I officially launched my own company in April 2007.

In my spare time, I like to golf, spend time with family and friends, travel and take road trips, making an annual stop to none other than - you guessed it - Disneyland.
