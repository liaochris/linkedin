Chinello Ifebigh’s Summary

Co-Leader of the the Zaan area regional club of Ecademy, the largest online social business network community. Avid social networker, blogger, humanist, free thinker, conceptual artist and lover of all mankind. My interests like life itself are constantly evolving towards how to make a worthy contribution and add value to this world. Through this I have developed a passion for out-of-the-ordinary concepts and value-based entrepreneurship.
Social networks; bringing 2 people together = bringing 2 worlds together. This ultimately always results in the creation of more value.
I am interested in the idea of the evolution of business interaction and in unusual meeting forms and meeting places. I believe that as the nature of the work space is congruently changing to resonate with the emergence of more socially rooted businesses (hubs); so will the way we organize and carry out meetings and our meeting places evlove to become open and supportive ecosystems that play a recognizeable rol in leveraging a positive outcome and thereby attaining a win-win situation for all involved.
I host and organize Think Tanks and Business Intervention Groups at The Network Salon for various types of businesses ranging from small one-man start- ups to middle/larger sized businesses further along the line.
I am a firm believer of the concept of winning by sharing and deploy a supply-based business model whereby many can benefit from pooling our resources together for a greater good.

Furthermore I am a certified Firstaid/Emergency First Resonse (EHBO) trainer, active in the female and youth empowerment and support initiatives aimed at eradicating poverty in the world.
