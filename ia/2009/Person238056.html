Trevor Poapst’s Summary

During the past 10 years, I have been director of global marketing for an innovative software development company, creative director for a national home furnishings company, leader of an award-winning e-business centre and Western Canadian E-Business Team, and marketing director for an e-business solutions company.

I am an accomplished public speaker and have travelled extensively in North America, Europe and Africa. My professional interests and experience center on integrated marketing communications, visual design and e-business.

My goal is to become VP marketing or CEO at one of the world's most innovative and admired companies such as Apple, Google, Nike, Adobe, P&G or a leading interactive digital agency (Organic, Blast Radius or Critical Mass).

Trevor Poapst’s Specialties:

VP marketing, marketing director, technology marketing, brand strategy, international business, business development, channel sales, e-business, web strategy, creative director, graphic design, SEO, public speaking, copywriting, project management, Spanish interpretation and translation.
