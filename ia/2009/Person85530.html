Georgina Smith’s Summary

Georgina Smith – IT Support Manager / Servicedesk Manager (ITILv3)
Tel: 07876 471389
george@georginasmith.co.uk

I have over twenty years experience working in the service delivery industry and twelve years experience working in IT at BBC Worldwide Ltd. I am a Microsoft Certified Professional and I have ITIL v3 Foundation and ITIL v3 Intermediate certificates in Service Operation and Operation Support and Analysis (equivalent to v2 practitioner modules). And I have implemented ITIL methodology throughout my IT career.

I have managed multiple support teams – “Expert” Servicedesk, Mac desktop support and PC desktop support. I am good at identifying inefficiencies and my particular strength is in service improvement by designing better process models for Incident Management, Request Fulfilment, Problem Management and day- to-day support functions.

I have experience of managing structural change and implementing two fully integrated service delivery tools. I foster respect and communication between all areas of IT support, most particularly with infrastructure and development teams as this leads to the efficient resolution of problems and major incidents. I also make a point of familiarising myself with a company structure in order to learn how different parts of the business work. I have a good relationship with managers at all levels across the business, including Sales, Marketing, Production and Editorial.

I believe it is important to work closely with all stakeholders, both business and IT service delivery in order to understand everyone’s priorities. Then it is possible to build achievable SLAs and cost-effective service portfolios to suit the needs of the business.
