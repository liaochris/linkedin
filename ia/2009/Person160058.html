Rodney Mesriani, Esq.’s Interests:

Mesriani Law Group is a full service law firm which specializes in all facets of Personal Injury Litigation, including Wrongful Death Claims, Car Accidents, Slip and Fall and Premise Liability claims, Employment and Labor Law, Social Security Disability (SSD/RSDI) and Supplemental Security Income (SSI) claims, and Corporate / Business Law. We have local offices in Los Angeles, San Fernando Valley, Irvine and Orange County, as well as other satellite offices with skilled and well-experienced professional attorneys and legal staff dedicated to serving clients in all counties in the state of California. Call us Toll Free at 1-866-325-4529.
