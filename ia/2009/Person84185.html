Gavin Byrne’s Summary

Communications professional with nearly 15 years experience delivering award-winning campaigns for some of the worlds leading results-driven marketeers.

Past and current clients include Unilever, Mars Masterfoods, Carlsberg UK, Diageo, Littlewoods Ireland, Glen Dimplex Group, Johnson & Johnson, Eircom, Nestlé Purina, Irish Ferries, Irish Distillers, Miele Ireland, Smyths Toy Superstores...

The depth and breadth of in-house capabilities in First Advertising provide a unique ability to create and implement brand and retail communications that deliver, across ALL on and offline communications platforms.

The goal is to leverage these resources for the mutual benefit of our clients and the agency.

Gavin Byrne’s Specialties:

Brand identity development and positioning. Product and service launches. Above the line campaign-planning and implementation. Total online communications; End to end website delivery, online rich-media display campaigns, search and social marketing. Multi-lingual, multi-market B2B communications. Sponsorship strategies, rights negotiation and consulting.
