Tina Lowe’s Summary

Tina has 18 years experience in business start-ups. At the age of 21 she established her first business and quickly realized the thrill that she could create, build and sell businesses! Six businesses later, she jokes that she is a “serial business starter”, but has the experience needed to take an idea from conception to implementation. Tina has vast experience in creating business systems and organizing and leading teams to success. She has been with Sales Partners-North Point since May of 2007.

Focus: Establishing the Sales Partners-North Point brand in the North Fulton region of Atlanta as well as continual development of the business and the businesses of our clients.
