David Dain’s Summary

I produce the entire KTTV website, "myfoxla.com" which means I research, edit, and write articles to post on all of the sections, and I place photos, polls, and videos with the stories. I also edit highlights, voice overs, sound on tape, and teases for nightly sportscasts and news shows on othe CBS affiliate in L.A.

I can write any story for the web or for broadcast, and I can edit video on just about every format, tape or computer-based. My experiences as a Producer have enabled me to put a superior product on the web or on TV that people want to see.

My goal is to take these skills and experiences and find my next position, whether it's in L.A. or another part of the country.
