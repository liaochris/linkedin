Alexander Wolfe’s Summary

I attended the Columbus School of Law from 2004-2005, and received my J.D. from the Texas Wesleyan School of Law in Fort Worth, Texas, in December of 2007 where I graduated 9th in my class. I am licensed to practice in the State of Texas, and my article "Wrongful Selection" was recently published in the Thomas M. Cooley Law Review, 25 T.M. Cooley L. Rev. 475 (2008).

Alexander Wolfe’s Specialties:

Legal research and writing, estate planning, tax, bankruptcy and social security disability.
