Nancy E. Harrison’s Summary

ATMS is the perfect 'job' for me - I get to work with the best adventure travel providers on the planet and tell their story to writers and editors. I love making the connection that shines a light on a trip or destination and fulfills the needs of the editor for their publication or broadcast. I've been fortunate to meet terrific people all over the world and travel to many wonderful places and have experiences that I have only dreamed about. Working with two dynamic partners and leading a team of intelligent and committed staff is exciting and energizing.

I founded Adventure Travel Media Source (ATMS) in 1999 to provide PR and media relations services to the active and adventure travel industries. ATMS was an outgrowth of my company Talisman Group, a marketing consulting company, in operation from 1992 - 1999.

ATMS is headquartered near Fort Collins, CO with account managers located all over North America. We serve over 100 clients worldwide who are tour operators, outfitters and destinations travel markets.

OUr PR and Media Services are served clients including:
• The International EcoTourism Society (TIES),
• The Dude Rancher's Association (DRA),
• Sustainable Travel International (STI)
• America Outdoors (AO).
We are the preferred provider of communications services to the members of these associations.

News and Information about our clients is distributed to writers and editors via the Media Messenger and NEWSFlash; electronic newsletters, three times per week. To subscribe to our services, please email nancy@atmstravelnews.com.

The goal of ATMS is to be the premiere provider of news and information about the adventure travel industry.

Our clients are regularly featured in such publications as
• The NY Times Travel Section,
• National Geographic Traveler,
• Outside,
• Men's Journal,
• National Geographic Traveler,
• Conde Nast Traveler,
• Travel & Leisure
and many other specialty publications.

    STI's current priorities include:
    Provide educational resources to travelers and travel providers, and assist them in making positive contributions to environmental conservation and socio-cultural sustainability.
    Develop, adopt, and market sustainable tourism standards and practices through the Sustainable Tourism Eco-Certification Program™ (STEP)
    Support the use of carbon offsets, enabling travelers and travel providers to offset unavoidable greenhouse gas emssions related to travel, business and their personal lives.


    Facilitate donations of financial resources, time, talent and economic patronage to help travelers and travel providers protect and positively impact cultures and environments around the world.
