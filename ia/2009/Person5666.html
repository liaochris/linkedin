Alycia Wicker(alycia.wicker@gmail.com)’s Summary

I graduated from Brooks College in 1997 with a AA in Interior Design. In college, I was a member of the International Business Society and a student member of ASID. In my senior year I received an Honorable Mention for my design of a Commercial Project.

I have worked in various design fields including residential design, commercial design, model home design, product development, furniture design, and construction project management.

I am a Professional Member of ASID which is accomplished by passing the NCIDQ exam. Once I passed the NCIDQ exam, I sat in for an exam so that I could be certified by the California Council for Interior Design Certification.
