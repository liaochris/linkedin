Tony Wanless’s Summary

I am a wanton explorer and thirster for knowledge who has reinvented myself many times. Engineering student, traveler, bum, crime reporter, political journalist, teacher, editor, financial planner, entrepreneur, writer, business strategist, and more.

For me, life has always been a journey of continual learning, and passing on my knowledge. So I had no choice but to become a management consultant and coach.

I have a knack for piercing and sometimes blunt analysis and an ability to cut through mental and organizational clutter to reveal relevant issues and problems and then devise solutions.

I won't bore you with history, but suffice to say that Knowpreneur prefers to advise and coach entrepreneurial-thinking organizations on processes such as innovation, creative and critical thinking, decision making/planning, change and marketing/communications.

Tony Wanless’s Specialties:

-- Transformation facilitator and guide
-- Incisive business analyst and planner
-- Coach for individuals and organizations in reinvention, creativity and innovative thinking
-- Product and service innovation
-- Commercializing knowledge and expertise

    Knowpreneur is a strategy, marketing/communications and change consultancy that provides guidance in creativity, innovation, and reinvention for organizations that want to revamp, expand, or diversify their product or service lines.
