Nigel Horncastle’s Summary

Currently working on extracting the maximum business benefits from Microsoft OCS R2, Nortel ACE platform and Cisco products and services. Looking ahead to OCS 2010, CEBP, Wave 15 and innovative G.711/G.729/RTA transcoding solutions based on the Howler Caraya/Banshee/Screamer product set. Communicate: Collaborate: Profit, Unified Communications is all about customer choice!

30+ years experience in the telecommunications and IT sectors. In the late 70's entered a world of TXK3 crossbar telephone exchanges, DTMF in-band signalling and Fortran before fax, mobile phones, satellite TV, email, the internet and Microsoft were around. In the 80's went there, did that with CCITT #7 signalling, 5ESS and AXE10/MD110 switches, engineered the BT Digital Derived Services Network and Mondial Digital International Switching Centre. In the 90's moved into UNIX, Uniplex and Staffware then VoIP, workflow, eDRM and Unified Communications. In the 00's embraced Microsoft Digital Dashboard/Sharepoint/MOSS and eDRM in the Freedom Of Information, Data Protection Act, MiFID and AML/KYC verticals.
