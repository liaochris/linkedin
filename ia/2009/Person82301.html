Dan Bradley’s Summary

I have 30+ years experience in local television news working for network affiliated stations in Toledo and Dayton, Ohio and Tampa, Florida. I have worked in the field as a photojournalist and inside the newsroom as an assignment editor, managing editor and news director. For the last 8 years I have worked as the Vice President of News for the Media General Broadcast Group. In this capacity I have developed operational guidelines for our large and small market news operations, developed market research and assisted in creating strategic plans that leverage the knowledge that research provided our local management teams. I have been instrumental in working in our company's convergence, or multimedia strategies. I also work as a primary trainer for many aspects of the television news business.

Dan Bradley’s Specialties:

I think I have a very good understanding of local television news and how it must connect to the communities it is licensed to serve. I am a very good teacher and see that playing a greater role in my career in the future.,
