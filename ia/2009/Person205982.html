Ryan Chartrand’s Summary

When people ask me what I do, I like to say that, in general, I am a web producer -- someone who is planting the seeds of the Web, one site at a time.

And ultimately, that's what I do. I bring innovative ideas and strategies to a wide variety of work that I do on the Web - consulting, social media, content producing, online recruitment strategies, web design, writing, blogging, video, and more.

Most recently:
• Developed online recruitment strategies, utilizing Web 2.0 and effective recruitment marketing techniques.
• Consulting clients on how best to create a coordinated online recruitment campaign, attract passive candidates via social media

Past:
• Editor/content producer in writing, video, podcasting, interactive media
• Tech enthusiast with proven innovative ideas ahead of the competition
• Several years working in a multimedia environment at a broadcast station and major metro newspaper.
• Graduated with a B.S. in Journalism and a concentration in new media/Web.
