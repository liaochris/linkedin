John Grimsey’s Summary

I have been building websites for ten years. I have extensive experience with standards-compliant and accessibile front-ends, object-oriented server code and databases. In my spare time I keep up-to-date the latest acronyms, and absolutely hate poorly designed user-interfaces. I love to work in technical roles that require input on the creative side and am capable of working as a front-end or server-side developer. I am also a capable designer who is very much at home with Photoshop.

Nearly all projects I work on involve LAMP (Linux/Apache/MySQL/PHP). Much of my recent work has been working with a large OOP systems, taking full advantage of the extra features offered by PHP5 whilst also working with HTML/CSS/Javascript/AJAX for the user-interface. I have done many Flash/database integration projects in my time using XML and PHP/ASP. I have also done several jobs that have not required any internet-related skills but rather audio production, using Cubase SX or Logic Pro to produce soundtracks to animatics and interactive videos, working on projects for the likes of the BBC and Disney.

I have three years of full-time agency experience and have been freelance for almost three years. I'm a good team player who has high standards of coding and attention to creative detail. Independently minded and a good motivator. I have an outgoing personality and the determination to succeed. Used to working towards strict deadlines. I am a good communicator and enjoy explaining painfully technical details to those whose eyes are on the project timeline.
