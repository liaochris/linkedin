Andee Silverman (andee@PracticeEnhancer.com)’s Summary

I train and coach physicians that provide elective healthcare procedures, including Spinal Decompression learn to improve their patient relationship building skills and communications during treatment planning consultations. This will improve your client retention, increase productivity and practice revenue. During my 20 + years of experience in multiple therapeutic specialties, I've worked with dentists, plastic surgeons, dermatologists, refractive surgeons and hair replacement clinics. Most recently I have worked with MDs, DOs, neurosurgeons, orthopedic surgeons and chiropractors that provide Spinal Decompression. I help doctors define the purpose for their style of patient communications and redefine their patient relations based on their intentions. This allows them to empower their patients. Educated patients are in a better position to choose the care they want. For the patient, they are more involved in making their own healthcare choices. For the doctors, this increases productivity and profitability. Elective Healthcare Strategies is a valuable resource for other businesses that can use these types of services as a value-added or complimentary service to their existing products.
