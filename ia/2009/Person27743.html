Brian Gardel’s Summary

I am a web application developer and owner of Advanced Digital Consulting (ADC). ADC is a Fort Collins freelance web design and development company that provides its clients a range of services aimed at increasing exposure on the Internet. These services include: website design, search engine optimization & marketing, application development, database development, hosting, shopping carts, and video production.

I am always interested in new projects. My ideal client is one who looks at their web presence as an integral part of their business, and views service providers as partners.
