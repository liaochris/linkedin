Bob Rosa’s Education

  • Audio Engineering School of Hard Knocks!

    Audio Engineering 1981 — 1991

    Before there were recording schools and degree programs for engineering, you had to hope that a studio would let you in and hang out and clean the place, log the tapes, detail recall sheets and maybe someday hope to touch the equipment. I had the good fortune "back in the day" to start out at Intergalactic Recording Studios where Jay Burnette was that person. Jay took me under his wing, showed me the ropes and a few tricks and trusted me enough to let me live at the studio and work without pay and learn my craft.

    I will be forever grateful to Jay for giving me that opportunity.

    Also, to Bobby Nathan at Unique Recording Studios, who gave me a break as a staff engineer and later as an independent, and where I spent many a night sleeping under the console just so I could keep learning and growing.

    Thank you Bobby, your support to this day means everything.
