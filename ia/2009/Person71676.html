Stacy Small’s Summary

The founding Editorial Director of Elite Traveler Magazine, I am the owner/founder of Elite Travel International, a Brentwood, CA-based luxury travel boutique affiliated with
Third Millennium, a VIRTUOSO agency. We cater to young affluent professionals (clients include many current and former CEOs, entrepreneurs, celebrities) and offer a distinct level of personal service for our clients' upscale leisure & business travel needs. I also write The EliteTravelGal (www.elitetravelgal.com) blog featuring inside tips/deals/news on luxury travel; am a contributing editor to Agent@Home Magazine; and provide Social Media training/consulting to hotels/travel companies worldwide.
