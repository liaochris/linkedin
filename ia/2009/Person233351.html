Tilak Kasturi’s Summary

Tilak Kasturi brings over 18 years of business and technology development experience in Mobile and Enterprise technologies. His experience includes developing key strategic partnerships, solution development and service delivery for Media and Entertainment, Brands, and large enterprise customers.

In his current role as VP Strategic Accounts at iLoop Mobile,
responsible for developing key strategic accounts strategy, and driving sales growth within strategic accounts. Responsible for identification of successful business models and packages to provide upsells to existing strategic customers in Media/Entertainment and Direct Marketing segments.

Also, until 2008, managed product management and services delivery, launching over 150+ innovative Mobile campaigns for leading Global brands and Fortune 100 companies.

As a Venture Technologist at eVolution Global Partners (a $100M fund created by Kleiner Perkins, Bain, TPG), he was responsible for venture technology due diligence, technology planning, and working with technology providers/vendors. Prior to joining eVolution, as CTO of e-Phase Software, he was responsible for technology strategy and software development for Fortune 100 enterprises including Disney (Media Relations), Lockheed Martin and Rogue Wave Software. As a Lead Software Architect at Time Warner Communications, Tilak designed and developed the world's first Internet browser for interactive TV environments.

Tilak has received a BS in Electrical Engineering from Regional Engineering College (Warangal, India), a MS in Computer Science from the Florida Institute of Technology and a MBA from the University of Central Florida. Tilak founded the Orlando Java Users Group, participated in the ebXML Standards Committee, is Vice-Chair for the Bay Area CTO Forum in Silicon Valley, Chair of dot Mobi Advisory Group Compliance Task Force, and is a member of the Mobile Marketing Association's Mobile Advertising Committee.

Tilak Kasturi’s Specialties:

Mobile Marketing, Advertising, Content Technologies, Technologist and Entrepreneur with both business and engineering background.
