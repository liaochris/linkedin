Chris Richards, PMP, MBA’s Summary

Twenty years of experience providing project management, technology solutions, and leadership in the financial, eCommerce, and merchandising industries. Leadership experience as the VP of Information Technology in a mid-sized company.

Certified Project Management Professional (PMP) skilled in performing industry best practices and developing project methodologies. Successful record of leading cross-functional teams in the delivery of mission critical systems on time and within the project budget.

Hands-on experience with all phases of the SDLC (software development life cycle) and 24/7 infrastructure practices for high availability, fail-over, and disaster recovery. Technical background includes leading the development of Internet, Intranet, client/server, and distributed application projects.
