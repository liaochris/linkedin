Dr Bradley Fordham’s Summary

Dr. Bradley Fordham currently heads Research and Product Management for Xiocom Wireless. In this role he manages Xiocom's product roadmap, research efforts, and new product development across the Xiosphere(TM) -- Xiocom's hardware, software, and service ecosystem for global wireless networking. Prior to Xiocom, Dr. Fordham was Executive Vice President, Chief Technology Officer, and Director of Online Insight where he brought his experience leading advanced systems research and development to oversee all strategic technology initiatives, including all development, deployment and training of the company's flagship guided selling and market intelligence solutions. Prior to Online Insight, Dr. Fordham served as chief technology officer for several start-up organizations and held key leadership positions in advanced technology and product development with Oracle Corporation, The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), AT&T and GE. He was a Professor of Database Theory and C Programming and has advised and consulted with NASA, Computer Sciences Corporation (CSC), several other U.S. government agencies and Fortune 100 global technology companies.
