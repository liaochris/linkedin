Paul Dumouchelle’s Summary

Making leaders more effective is what motivates me. I combine my experience in marketing, management and consulting with people insights provided by Predictive Index to help clients sharpen strategy, strengthen leadership and accelerate sales.

To Sharpen Strategy I link the Plan to Performance:
1. Develop & Align Vision, Mission & Objectives
2. Develop & Clarify your Unique Competitive Advantage
3. Evaluate & Redesign your Organizational Structure
4. Assess & Assign Personnel within the Organization
5. Establish Short Term Goals
6. Create a Performance Scorecard to Track Progress

To Strengthen Leadership I Assess Strengths then Train and Coach in these areas:
1. Personnel Assessment and Selection
2. Decision Making
3. Motivation
4. Change Management
5. Teambuilding
6. Communication
7. Performance Goals & Standards
8. Coaching & Discipline
9. Conflict Management

To Accelerate Sales I Train and Coach:
1. Sales Leadership Development using the elements
noted above.
2. Sales Skill Assessment
3. Sales Skill Training in Opening, Investigating,
Presenting, Confirming and Positioning for long-term
growth and profits.
