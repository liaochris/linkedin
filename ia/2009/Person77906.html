www.FantasticSamsFranchises.com

As a leader in the $59 billion hair care business, we offer our franchise partners a unique and compelling business model, including the following factors that make our hair salon franchise especially attractive:
• A simple business model
• Flat fee royalty versus percent of sales
• Single and multi-unit franchise opportunities
• Manager-run units

To help get your salon business open as quickly as possible, we provide:
• Contacts with franchise financing sources

    FANTASTIC SAMS HISTORY
    Our story begins in 1974 in Memphis, Tennessee, when Sam Ross opened our very first salon. Two years later in 1976, we began franchising and marketing a “fixed fee” system and “no appointment necessary” concept to entrepreneurs across the country. Since then, we’ve built an organization of nearly 1300 salons that offers brand awareness, experience, and leadership.

    Our success is built on empowering our owners. We offer solid, proven systems which are continuously refined to keep us ahead of the competition. Our brand’s strength is reinforced by a network of over 700 salon owners and over 13,000 experienced stylists who deliver fantastic service each and every day.
