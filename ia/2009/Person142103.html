Lisa Krohn’s Summary

Lisa Krohn is a leading Personal Organizer and Life Coach. She is a former Hollywood Archivist, Event Planner, Manager of a preeminent European antiquities gallery and previously an Executive Assistant to entertainment executives and entrepreneurs on both coasts.

Lisa has devoted herself for 25 years to organizing, coaching and producing various aspects of people’s lives. She is a creative problem solver, a detail-oriented and imaginative project manager with the ability to establish order out of chaos. Lisa has a track record of success in achieving client goals with legendary entrepreneurs and executives. Lisa’s integrity, discretion and grace under pressure are renowned. Quest Magazine has recognized Lisa as a premier personal organizer.

Lisa’s career began in the Oval Office of the White House where she interned while attending college and was President of her Student Government at George Washington University, formerly Mount Vernon College. Upon graduation, she went to work at International Creative Management in New York, as ICM’s only in-house floater/trainee. After moving to Hollywood, Lisa continued to work in talent management at the William Morris Agency Television Department. She spent the next few years as an Executive Assistant to Peter Guber, Dawn Steel, Steve Tisch, Wendy Finerman, Don Simpson and Jerry Bruckheimer. Lisa moved to Sony Pictures Entertainment with Peter Guber where she spent four years creating and producing Columbia Pictures’ Archival Department.

Lisa returned to New York and co-produced Harry Evans’ celebrated book parties and events at Random House. She was an Executive Assistant to Martha Stewart and to the General Counsel of USA Networks. Lisa was also the Manager of Phoenix Ancient Art a European Antiquities Gallery.

Today, as a private consultant, Lisa works with a limited list of individuals who place a high premium on time and life-style management.

Lisa Krohn’s Specialties:

My clients needs range from temporary organizational and coaching. To individuals who have attention deficit, learning disabilities and obsessive-compulsive disorders. I teach how to organize and structure every facet of their careers and personal lives. I assist them in specific ways to achieve their goals. These range from designing a daily schedule of tasks to restructuring their lives to achieve greater balance and direction.
