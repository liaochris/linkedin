Bob Martin’s Summary

Advertising/Media executive with an award-winning track record in developing high-impact, immersive marketing platforms. Developing "Tradigital" strategies and solutions for media sellers, creative and media ad agencies, and marketers.

Bob Martin’s Specialties:

Developing (or inventing) impactful target audience experiences, particularly within the out-of-home, outdoor video, digital signage, cinema, and interactive TV advertising media landscapes.

Bob Martin’s Experience

  • President & CEO

    LevelVision LLC

    (Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    September 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    LevelVision designs and deploys innovative video display technologies, and provides marketing solutions to the place‐based, ambient, and out‐of‐home marketing industries.

    An extensive, global IP portfolio complements the company’s undisputed leadership within the floor‐based and horizontally‐oriented video display and digital signage marketplace.

    The Company's latest innovation ‐ a 42‐inch 1080p LCD display that rises only one inch off of the floor – is compatible with virtually every existing media player and digital signage network.

    For retailers and place‐based media companies, LevelVision provides ‐ quite literally ‐ a new dimension from which to reach both customers and audiences.

  • President & CEO

    IntelliMat

    (Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    September 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Based in Roanoke VA, IntelliMat, Inc. is the parent company of LevelVision LLC.
