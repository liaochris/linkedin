Massimo Gaetani’s Summary

I am a Business and Executive Coach, fully dedicated to help my clients to boost their performance and achieve their success. I work primarily with business owners, senior managers and professionals, helping them to define and execute a structured and detailed plans about their business, career and life stretching them beyond their comfort boundaries.

I support my coaching qualifications and work with over 20 years of corporate experience in small, medium and large enterprises in roles ranging from Systems Engineer to Marketing Director and Business Consultant. I have a strong passion for technology and during most of my career I was involved, directly or indirectly, with the development of leading edge, innovative technologies and the transfer of existing technologies into new applications.

Over 30 years of martial arts experience helped me to train many people to succeed both in martial arts and in their whole life. I am combining all these experiences and merging them into tailor made, client oriented coaching solutions that can suit individuals, companies, professionals and teams.

Massimo Gaetani’s Specialties:

Business, Executive and Team Coaching. Strong Marketing and Sales background with emphasis on online marketing, brand identity and corporate image. Sound, hands on technical background and high tech expertise, particularly in areas of software development, hardware, automation and process monitoring and control.
