Rob ! Veldt’s Summary

It’s our aim to create a new market that significantly changes innovation at the intersection between the fields of business development, product development, labor markets and knowledge management.

Our mission is to leverage innovation by making it cheaper, more accessible and fun; Let both people and organizations encounter the great experience of collaborative creativity. We translate new (online) trends in the area of knowledge exchange and innovation to useful tools and adaptable advice.

Our activities are organized through the following four pillars:
[1] tools (e.g. Cash4idea.com, white labeled think tanks, mobile applications, knowledge exchange widgets, etc.)
[2] ideavents; events (lectures, workshops, brainstormsessions, thinktankthursday)
[3] ideavents consulting (guides organizations while exploring open innovation)
[4] ideavents investment (ii); guiding start-ups from idea to enterprise
