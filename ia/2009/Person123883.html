Julie Liebhard’s Summary

I am a brand experience strategist with over 13 years of experience in the industry. I work with traditional and interactive agencies as well as directly with clients. I’ve developed pioneering brand strategies for Fortune 100 organizations, drove multi-million dollar revenue growth through innovative customer-centric programs, and acted as a visionary leader who has received multiple performance awards and honors. I can work with companies in a variety of capacities – from collaborating on pitches, developing experience strategy to providing creative direction.

My experience building brand strategies includes leading the creative and strategic direction for a variety of clients in forming new and compelling interactions that would result in measurable business impact. By using a mixture of primary and secondary research combined with industry and interaction experience my teams have developed solutions that have not only delivered on the brand promise, but have driven revenue growth. I share key ideas with the creative team in a way that encourages them to be passionate and creative with the solution and provide them with the insights necessary to create relevant creative solutions.

The current industry focus on multi-channel marketing, touch point design, and full lifecycle engagement strategy, and the convergence of traditional and interactive agencies have provided a unique opportunity that I am really excited about.
