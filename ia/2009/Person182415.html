Paul Jackson’s Summary

Having worked with one of the Directors, Mike Beardshall, in a previous career, I knew moving to Portfolio Software would be fun. I initially started within the the consultancy arm - Epsilica Consulting - but later moved to managing applications development, although I still dabble in consultancy when the need arises.

Having initially worked as a Business Change Analyst, I later moved into traditional Project Management, as well as working in a variety of HR and Logistics roles during my 28 years in the Royal Navy. I have wide management and operational experience and have recruited, built and led multi-disciplinary teams, working in a number of organisations worldwide. I have wide financial experience and been responsible for budgets and assets in excess of £40m.

Since arriving at Portfolio Software I've fully embraced Agile Software Development methods and Scrum in particular; I have built a reputation for successful project delivery using iterative methods, and managing distributed teams across a number of continents and time zones.
