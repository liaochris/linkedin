Chris Conti’s Summary

As most of my friends know, trading the financial markets is my passion. It is not something that I do solely for financial gain. Rather, I do it because it is my passion. I consider myself very fortunate to be able to make a comfortable living doing what I love. As someone once said, "If you love your work, then you will never have to work a day in your life"!

Over the past decade plus, I have been trading the financial markets successfully. Due to my success, I have been approached by several friends, colleagues, companies, etc. asking me to trade their money for them. I have always had to regrettably delcine the offer due to the fact that I was not set up properly to do so.

Well, I finally decided to persue my passion and move forward into the investment world by opening my own Commodities and Futures Brokerage, Conti Capital Investments. With my new company, I am a full service. Meaning, clients can open accounts with Conti Capital Investments and trade on their own, or I could trade their account for them. What seperates Conti Capital Investments from other "similar" firms is this: I PUT MY MONEY WHERE MY MOUTH IS. When I place a trade for a client, I am also taking the trade in MY OWN PERSOANL ACCOUNT. Therefore, I am riding side by side with my clients, and am with them on all winning AND losing trades.
