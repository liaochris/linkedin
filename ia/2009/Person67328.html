Rhino Mike’s Summary

After many years in the plains of Africa I developed a keen interest in keeping up with some of the faster wildlife around me.

So, I got into shape and moved to Florida where a new energy product has finally been designed for every day people. Yes, taken once daily this can help you get through the day whether at work or home, staying focused and alert, but without the aid of caffeine.

This truly lasts all day, and, what's more, features me, Rhino Mike, on the bottle!

My goals are to see that everyone in America has access to this amazing new energy drink.
