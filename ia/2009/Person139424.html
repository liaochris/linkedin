Leigh Fazzina’s Summary

So I'm a seasoned healthcare PR pro with 15 yrs experience in communications development, management, strategic planning, program implementation & measurement. I've worked on the pharma, hospital, clinical trials/patient recruitment, medical society & agency sides identifying comms/PR opps. & developing & implementing a broad range of strategies & programs that raise awareness, create a positive image, & strengthen a brand. I’m an innovative strategist with a record of award-winning initiatives & programs (see below).

I'm proficient (& passionate) in working with the news media & I believe this work is an art. My work in the PR arena over the past 3 years has naturally segued into the social media space. I believe strongly that social media communications requires a certain type of expertise that draws from a media relations background. I was one of the first to put a national healthcare social media communications program in place, & I'm now developing & managing programs that work in tandem of both media relations & social media channels.

Volunteer? Yep!
•. I've held a leadership role on the PRSA Health Academy’s Executive Committee since 2003. I’ve been a driving force for the healthcare communications industry for many years; always giving 110% to advance the industry & the profession.

• I’m a Sr. Judge of the PRSA Bronze Anvil Awards/ Judge of the Silver Anvil Awards. A great way to learn what makes for a winning entry, mediocre entry & a weak entry.

• I’m a professional mentor mentoring young PR pros of PRSA. Young pros seek advice all the time & need our help. Several pros helped me along the way & I believe in “paying it forward.”

Lastly, I believe the work I do as a healthcare comm. pro helps people live healthier lives & this work truly moves me (…& I love teaching it to others). While I am passionate about my work, focused on my career, and work hard - I only work to live, not live to work. I know how to relax & play hard. Life is good.
