Tom Troceen’s Summary

I have enjoyed working with DW Simpson along side Claude Penland as the webmaster of the Actuarial Outpost. I continue to work my hardest to develop a rich environment for actuaries to network online and discuss issues that interest actuaries. You can also find our linkedIN Actuary Group by simply typing actuarialoutpost.com/linkedin, and you will be directed to our group.

My main goal is to provide a forum for actuaries to work together and pass actuarial exams. The Actuarial Outpost (aka `The AO`) is also a great resource for actuaries to discuss their specific field of the actuarial profession, and also discuss general topics such as career advice, actuarial software, actuarial jobs, actuarial science universities, and more.

You can always check it out for yourself at http://www.actuarialoutpost.com and if you would like to take part in the discussion, you can register for an account at http://www.actuarialoutpost.com/register.
