Chris Chiera’s Summary

I was born and raised in Akron, Ohio. In 2005 I graduated from the University of Akron with a Bachelors Degree in Fine Arts in Graphic Design. I am currently a full time freelance designer. If you are interested a quote or working with me please contact me here. I specialize in building web sites that use XHTML, CSS, FLASH, and JAVASCRIPT technology.

I have worked full time with firms such as American Greetings, Hitchcock Fleming & Associates, The Camber Corporation and Lunar Cow. I have done freelance for companies such as Billups Design and Genex. Right now I am open for freelance jobs.

Chris Chiera’s Specialties:

Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Flash, Illustrator, CSS / XHTML, Jquery, MooTools, Prototype ect...

Chris Chiera’s Experience

  • Founder

    Push-Pin Interactive, LLC

    (Graphic Design industry)

    January 2009 — Present (6 months)

    Push-Pin Interactive is a Limited Liability Company (LLC) established in January of 2009. Since then I have been building up a network of business professionals interested in my talent.

    I am interested in partnering with marketing and design firms around the country who allow me to work virtually through a contract basis or full time basis.

    I am specifically interested in web design trends and front end web development. In my spare time I learn how to use different content management systems like Drupal, Wordpress and Joomla.

    I also frequent websites like Twitter, Facebook and Flickr. It is hard for me to market myself through direct e-mail and have time to meet deadlines.

    I believe social networking websites like the ones listed above are a great means of communication and exposure.

    I am very strong in Photoshop, Dreamweaver and Flash. I do not use Fireworks. Everything I do is hand coded and I do not rely on Dreamweaver components to code websites.

    I primarily work in a table less environment to avoid confusion. However, I am not apposed to using tables in certain circumstances because they can be very beneficial to use in certain situations.

    Throughout the last two years I have been getting into javascript libraries like jQuery, MooTools and Prototype. Since these applications are so easy to use I try to work with them as much as possible. There is also an abundance of information on the web regarding these libraries.

    For more information about my capabilities please visit my website here: http://www.chrischiera.com/

    My website is powered by MooTools (http://mootools.net/). Many people mistake my website as a flash site because of it's effects.

    To make myself clear I refer to my site as a web portal than just a portfolio site. There you can see examples of my work since I began in 2003 at different companies full time and by contract basis.
