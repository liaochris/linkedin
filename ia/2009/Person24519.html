Bart Bierman’s Summary

Bierman Pharma Biomedical (BPB) is a Dutch consultancy company in the pharmaceutical industry and life sciences biotech. Core business is interim management “in and around” clinical development of drugs (phase II-IV clinical studies), medical affairs, (medical) marketing, medical dpt. mgt.and small company general management. BPB offers services in NL, Belgium, and the UK; but also in other Western Europe, US, Middle East and Eastern European markets.

Via BPB Bart Bierman works as self-employed professional (freelance contractor) and will, further to pharmaceutical, healthcare clients or CRO’s, also take projects from executive search agencies, network colleagues.

Bart Bierman (1958), MD, graduated in 1985. After 2,5 years clinical practice, he started at MSD in 1988 as a cardiovascular CRA in phase IIIb-studies. In 1990 Bart joined Warner-Lambert Cy. as a NL based affiliate Manager Medical Affairs, from 1993 on as affiliate Marketing Manager, and from 1997 he serviced as a Medical Director of the NL Parke-Davis branch.

Between Oct. 2000 and April 2002 Bart had several positions in life sciences biotech business development and clinical site (SMO) mgt. Since mid 2002, he focused on interim project mgt. in clinical development, medical mgt. pharmaceutical marketing, business development, general mgt., private financing of life sciences SME and daily operations.

Bart Bierman’s Specialties:

Bart is qualified as Pharmaceutical Physician. Further to clinical experience in CNS, (haemato)oncology, IBD and sepsis, his main focus has been vascular and metabolic medicine, contributions to phase II-IV development, pre-marketing and introduction of several lipid lowering compounds and ACE-inhibitors (Zocor, Lopid, Lipitor, Crestor, Acupril) in the Dutch market.
