Evan Keith Langert’s Summary

I specialize in commercial real estate leasing and development in the San Francisco Bay area (although I have done deals in over twenty states). With over twenty three years experience and hundreds of transactions under my belt, I can handle any corresponding needs that you may have. I am listed in "Who's Who In Real Estate", a publication that honors only 1% of all real estate professionals. I first became a broker in the early 90's, and also spent nine years as the seniormost transactional attorney for Glenborough Realty Trust, a multi-billion dollar NYSE-traded real estate investment trust. Glenborough owns several prevalent office buildings in San Francisco's financial district, as well as large office buildings and shopping centers in Boston, Los Angeles, Washington, D.C., and other cities throughout the United States.

I have negotiated leases with hundreds of companies including Lord & Taylor (The May Companies), Circuit City, Outback Steakhouse, Merrill Lynch, Clear Channel Communications, Metlife, Starbuck's, and Chipotle.

Other real estate experience includes four years as a commercial real estate lender with Maryland National Bank, then one of the 25 largest banks in the U.S.. I also spent one year as an internal auditor for the bank. I have developed real estate projects, and am also active in their purchase, sale, and financing.

My grandfather was a landlord, and my father and brother were/are builder/contractors. I've been in and around commercial real estate my whole life. I've been involved in construction, syndicating, leasing, and/or buying and selling office buildings, shopping centers, industrial properties, hotels, and multi-family properties for many years. I understand real estate from every side of the transaction, and have a good command of the numbers and financial analysis involved.

I am on the national teaching faculty of KW Commercial, and am also a member of it's national commercial leadership council.

Evan Keith Langert’s Specialties:

Commercial real estate dealmaking, syndication, negotiation, leasing, and purchase and sale.
