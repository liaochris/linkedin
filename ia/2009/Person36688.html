Cameron Herold, CEO/Franchise Coach & Speaker’s Summary

Cameron Herold joined 1-800-GOT-JUNK? in October 2000 was Chief Operating Officer until May 2007. From May 1999 until joining us, Mr. Herold was employed by Ubarter.com, a division of Network Commerce (NASD: NWKC) in Seattle, Washington, as Vice President of Corporate Development. From June 1998 to May 1999, Mr. Herold served as President of Barter Business Exchange in Vancouver, British Columbia, and from October 1994 to March 1998, Mr. Herold served as Vice President of Franchise Development for Coast to Coast Franchise Services. In these positions, Mr. Herold was involved in the sale, branding and integration of 120+ franchise locations, development and deployment of e-commerce and Internet strategies, negotiation of corporate acquisitions and development of numerous strategic partnerships. Hey has been a Keynote Speaker, and Public Speaker for dozens of companies and organizations around the world.
