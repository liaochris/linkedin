Nick Crawley’s Summary

Moving mountains to achieve goals.

I have spent the last 15 years understanding and improving the financial modelling process underpinning the complex project finance process.

I have recruited and managed several highly talented and successful team have worked extensively in both consulting and investment banking environments.

This experience is founded on an early academic career in Theoretical Astrophysics and a drive to solve complex problems with simple and elegant solutions.
