Pierre Burns’s Summary

Pierre is a Marketing and Technology Executive with over a decade of experience helping companies succeed.

As the Director of Strategic Marketing at Sage Software, I lead the marketing strategy for our existing customer install base, new product and new acquisition efforts.

Integrated Marketing Strategy: Traditional and New Media, CRM, EDM, DM, Social Media Marketing/community building and sentiment monitoring, PPC/SEO/SEM, PR, for direct sales channels, web and retail.

Launched www.sagespark.com
" Empowering Entrepreneurs Across North America to Get Motivated, Get Started and Get Paid"

Before moving on to Sage Pierre most recently was Director of Channel Marketing and Business Development for DOXIM increasing revenue for DOXIM by approximately 50% in one year.

Prior to this Pierre worked at Vancity, Canada's largest Credit Union and their subsidiary Citizensbank in roles of increasing responsibility. Pierre drove the Digital Presence Strategy where he pioneered research into Social Media and championed its application on two large scale social media projects. Most recently one of these projects for Vancity was nominated for a Webby award in 2008 in the same category with Facebook, Ning, Flock and Bebo for Social Networking.

In addition Pierre managed the Interactive Services team and directed the online channel, including online banking and the external websites for Vancity, Canada’s largest Credit Union and their numerous subsidiares including Citizensbank of Canada, Canada's first online bank.

Pierre served on the E-commerce committee at Credit Union Central of Canada where he participated in directing and overseeing the development of the Web Services Product now used by over 300 Credit Unions, Trust Companies and Banks in Canada.

Pierre has spoken at leading industry functions such as BarCampBank and the MBA class at UBC.

Recently, Pierre completed an executive program from MIT in Strategy and Innovation.
