David Repyak’s Summary

David Repyak / Interactive Creative Director (Web Designer, UI/UX Architect, Flash Artist, Web Project Manager) has been a force behind interactive information design, developing experiential solutions in the greater Seattle area since 1992.

David’s singular focus is to bring greater meaning to the interactive customer experience. David’s goal is to design web sites with stunning efficiency using tomorrow’s technology. He understands the integrity of an effective idea, the value of profitability, how to juggle, and most importantly how to have tremendous fun in the midst of chaos.

David has shaped the conceptual direction and managed the production of eighteen web sites, dozens of Flash animations, and hundreds of advertising and rich media campaigns. He can claim dynamic web sites, splash pages, screen savers, project management, and extensive client service to his success. David has repeatedly exceeded client expectations on many projects.

David Repyak’s Specialties:

Flash animation and dynamic web site design, UI/UX architecture. Conceptual branding and identity. Broadcast, video, outdoor board, direct mail and collateral. Rich media Interactive campaigns. Complete proficiency and expert level with the Adobe CS4 Suite, Flash and Action Script.
