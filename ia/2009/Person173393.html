Dennis Lasini’s Summary

WHY I’M ON LinkedIn:

As the Manager of Recruiting, I am responsible for all hiring activities throughout NewGround in all five locations in the US and Canada.

I am interested in networking with professionals who possess Design / Build experience, specifically in Commercial Architecture and Construction. We hire Project Engineers / Managers, Construction / Project Development Managers, Mechanical / Electrical Engineers, Superintendents, Architects, Consultants, and National Account Managers. I am particularly interested in networking with Senior Project Managers who possess a strong network and contacts at the highest levels within the bank and credit union construction arena.

NewGround pioneered the development of digital signage for retail banking in 1999. We recently announced the completion of our 1,000th installation of digital signage in less than 10 years. “This is a milestone for NewGround. It’s not only a measure of market buy-in, but it also demonstrates the success of NewGround’s approach. One thousand digital sites is a testimony to the quality of NewGround’s product and service,” said Jim Kueneke, NewGround’s Senior Vice President, Retail.

NewGround is a leading international design and implementation firm serving the financial services industry. Since 1913, NewGround has been a premier provider of growth solutions for financial institutions, helping organizations advance by focusing on the design and delivery of the customer experience. NewGround provides strategic solutions through the integration of their core offerings, which include: Brand, Consult, Design, Build, Retail and Culture.

The company’s operational headquarters are in St. Louis, MO, with principal offices in Chicago, IL; San Francisco, CA; Portsmouth, NH and Toronto, Canada.

To learn more about NewGround visit www.newground.com.
