Mark Johnson’s Summary

I'm a product manager who was trained at SAP and then plunged into the startup world at 3 search startups. I've managed successful product launches and can play inbound and outbound marketing roles with equal dexterity. I have a passion for creating innovative, useful interfaces and can lead designers from brainstorming to usability. I also can channel my inner geek to work with engineers to understand technology, create solid requirements, and deliver projects on time.
