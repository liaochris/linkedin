Jesper Popma’s Summary

Producer bamboo shirts:
I can help you promote your brand by providing you with printed bamboo shirts that your target group will love to wear (because of its softness and fit). If you are thinking of using shirts to promote your brand, make sure you go with beautiful design. I have lots of promotional shirts in my closet witch I only use for “Dirty Jobs” (like painting my house). Because of their bad fit I don’t like to wear them in public. So where’s the promotional / commercial value of these shirts?

Creator:
I just love watching shows on the Discovery Channel like: Megastuctures, Extreme Engineering, Megabuilders, How do they do it....etc. I can watch these shows for days. It just fascinates me how things are made. I guess I’m a creator at heart.

Creative:
As a creator at heart I enjoy making all kinds of creations. If it’s graphic design, video, photography, illustration, animation, 3D-design and even cooking count me in. My first love is graphic design, but actually I don’t really care what discipline as long as I can make something from scratch. And now I’m into creating bamboo shirts.

Volunteer:
Radboud Kindertelevisie is a tv channel for sick kids at Radboud University Hospital. For two years I worked there as a volunteer making videos with the kids. Working as a cameraman, editor, actor I had the time of my life making all kinds of funny videos. If you have a place like this nearby I recommend you join. It’s great fun!

Recreational skier:
To bad I live in a country without mountains, because I love skiing. So I’m limited to hit the slopes in the Alps ones every couple of years.
