Rafi Dudekula’s Summary

I am currently involved in creating collaboration, workflow and integration applications primarily using a combination of Microsoft technologies including SharePoint (MOSS), Windows Workflow (WF), InfoPath (VSTO), Windows Communications Foundation (WCF) etc. The objective in most of these applications is to provide employees, partners with self service collaborative intelligence solutions that enable information worker productivity and effective decision making.

Apart from working on enterprise projects for clients, I lead the development and marketing of web 2.0 yellow pages (FunduSearch), collaboration (AgileCircle) and eLearning websites, for our company ElementSign, LLC.

I have over 16 years of experience in enterprise software development, architecture and management. Prior to starting ElementSign, I was an applications architect at Microsoft Corporation for 5 years. During my work as a MCS consultant I worked on a number of challenging projects that needed unique, innovative approaches to executing large, complex, multi-site, multi-million dollar projects successfully. As a part of Microsoft Consulting Services, I worked as an application and/or integration architect for companies such as Wal-mart, CGEY, Verizon, PepsiCo, Radio Shack etc.

I have an MBA from Southern Methodist University (SMU), Dallas and an Engineering degree from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT).
