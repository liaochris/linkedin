Daniel Fox’s Summary

I have successfully worked in the telecoms industry for 12 years, currently effectively liaising with the world’s major tier-one device suppliers and directly reporting to the CTO of my present company. I am a fairly unusual and therefore sought-after employee as I have a very broad experience: I have worked for most of the industry’s top companies, and my unique value lies in the fact that I have great understanding of, and insight into, the inter-related worlds of testing, development, systems integration, product ranging, road-mapping and even technical marketing (where I can ‘sell’ our current technical opportunity).

I am often complimented upon the speed with which I can technically assess the pros and cons of an issue; furthermore, it is always appreciated that I am able to translate these ensuing technical considerations to a broad audience (CTOs, HR, Marketing, Test Teams etc) in terms they can easily understand and relate to. I thrive on professional contact and have excellent interpersonal skills. I am confident in creating and undertaking presentations and in utilizing my strong technical knowledge to influence and affect colleagues and suppliers. For example in my recent trips to Japan, Korea, Taiwan, Hong Kong I have proved my ability to be able to engage with a wide variety of people; right up to board level, with heads of companies, and to communicate clearly and effectively. This has enabled me to become very well-connected within the industry. Furthermore, it results in the ability to efficiently liaise with companies with which we have an established relationship and also prospective ones.

My current aim is to further develop these aptitudes by having even more influence with leading-edge technologies in a strategic/planning capacity to guide the direction of the future.
