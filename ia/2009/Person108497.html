Jeff Hardy’s Summary

I rose up through the ranks of technology-based organizations from the Communications and Marketing side of the business. I was fortunate enough to have the opportunity to work with talented professions who taught me what I needed to learn in order to grow professionally. At EFData Corporation and Honeywell I learned the disciplines of precision and focus; as the owner of my own business for 8 years I learned the true fine line of profit and loss and developed a true "ownership" attitude that I bring to everything I do; at Clear Choice I learned personal resolve and gained the strength I needed to overcome obstacles; and now at SmarterTools I have the opportunity to participate on the most productive and dynamic team I have seen in my career and to work for an organization that effectively teaches me how to reach my maximum performance.

I am truly a blessed man and I look forward eagerly to see what lies ahead.
