Richard Lannon’s Summary

Richard Lannon is more than a seasoned professional. He’s marinated! In fact, Richard left the corporate world to form a BraveWorld.

BraveWorld is a company dedicated to bridging business and tech organizations to clarify goals and objectives and train their leadership and staff on how to achieve them. Richard is a dynamic speaker, trainer and facilitator with senior management and leadership experience in business and the technology industry. Alongside his experience is a cheeky and fun personality. When Richard speaks, get SET to be entertained! With his no nonsense approach, he links organizations and professionals’ business brainpower with bottom-line thinking.

He has worked in oil and gas, education, transportation, technology and professional business service industries successfully contributing to PricewaterhouseCoopers, Enbridge, Enmax, Canadian Pacific Railway, ATB Financial, Fraser Health Region and many other organizations.

Richard’s clear presentation style and edgy ideas have been featured in publications around the world including the National Post, the Business Analyst Times and the Project Times, where he’s provided comment on the importance of bridging technology skills and business brains.

He is the forefather of the SET Initiative, Mount Royal College’s Business Analysis Program and the program author of Leadership Mastery for the Technology Professional, Facilitating Business Requirements Discovery and Eliminating Client Casualties.

Richard will provide a unique blueprint for your organization to get SET (structured, engaged and trained). That’s why clients call him the SETability Expert!
