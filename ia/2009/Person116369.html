Johan Lundgren’s Summary

Today I’m working as a Lead Business System Analyst at IFS World Operations AB, developing integrated systems for Maintenance and Engineering. My main background is as a mechanical, piping and process engineer. Working within the following businesses:
- Ship and oil rig building at Arendal ship yard in Gothenburg 1 year
- Energy producing companies at Vattenfall 7 years and OKG ½ year
- Chemical process industry at Akzo Nobel 7 years
An important part for me during the above period was ordinary detail engineering. However my main interest has always been the possibility of improving database driven engineering tools. This is achieved by using CAD tools together with a centralized database for sharing data between all engineering disciplines. Another main milestone was when I participated in the project of developing integration between the engineering environment and maintenance – inventory/procurement system at Akzo Nobel, 1997-1998. The maintenance – inventory/procurement system was at this time IFS Applications 1997 and engineering tools was IDOK 5.1.
It was then clear for me to continue at a software industry company like IFS, with the possibility of contributing when developing engineering tools and integrations. I joined IFS R&D early 1999.
I have during my 9 years time at IFS R&D participated in a lot of different projects and in roles like: functional adviser/designer, sub project manager and project manager.
