Steve Hartman’s Summary

Steve Hartman is President and Creative Director of Creativille, Inc., a brand strategy and design firm located Edwardsville, IL. Specializing in corporate communications his entire career, Steve Hartman leads Creativille as a firm continuing the tradition of great thinking for corporate and brand communication, but also passionately designing for publications, brand development and all things creative.

Creativille’s clients have a diverse range of needs from brand development to corporate collateral; from books to magazines; from employee communications to financial communications; from event marketing to corporate advertising.

Steve Hartman, has successfully designed for a variety of industries including biomedical, chemical, communications, education, entertainment & gaming, financial, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, telecommunications, textile, transportation, and non-profit.

Steve sits on the National Board of Directors of AIGA, The Professional Association for Design. AIGA is the world’ largest design association. Steve has taught design and visual communications at Washington University, Webster University, Maryville University and the University of Missouri St. Louis. Steve was invited to attend the premier session of AIGA Business Perspectives for Design Leaders at the Harvard Business School.

Steve has created brand solutions for a wide range of clients including The Abraham Lincoln Presidential Library and Museum, Southern Illinois University Edwardsville, The Missouri Historical Society, University of Illinois Foundation, Arch Coal, AEP River Operations, Helzberg Diamonds, HBF Textiles, Dillards, Inc. and more. Steve’s reputation has awarded him design accolades, international judging opportunities, featured stories, and advisory panel roles through out the country.

Steve Hartman’s Education

  • Harvard Business School Executive Education

    Business Perspectives for Design Leaders 2003 — 2003

    A select group of senior-level creative leaders come together to discuss, network, debate and grow with each other. Business Perspectives for Creative Leaders is designed exclusively for design executives who work with clients to develop strategic design responses to client challenges. This response may be in the form of a comprehensive communication or positioning strategy, or the design of a range of products or services. Participants are likely to view the design process as an integral part of solving complex problems, whether that experience involves communication, functional or strategic design. This program is particularly beneficial to design professionals who deal with top-level executives and/or larger corporate clients.
