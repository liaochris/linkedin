Steven Craig’s Summary

Steve Craig is a business consultant focused on Exit Planning. Using a comprehensive planning process, he prepares the owners of closely-held enterprises for the optimal exit from their business.

In 1983, Steve entered the financial services industry and began advising business owners in the areas of succession and retirement planning. In 1991 he started his first company, Craig & Associates which had 19 employees in Los Angeles and San Diego. The firm served business owners and professionals needs in the areas of Estate Planning, Qualified Retirement Plans, Executive Compensation and Disability Income. After selling the firm, Steve took a job in Massachusetts as the senior executive in charge of a major life insurance company's independent distribution channel.

In 2003 he and his partner, Susan Laine, formerly of Citicorp Investment Bank, set up Structured Financial Partners to meet the needs of baby boomer business owners who have not begun to think about how to extricate themselves from the businesses they've spent their lives building.

Steven Craig’s Specialties:

Exit planning, sucession planning, estate planning, executive compensation, non-qualified plans such as deferred compensation, uses of life insurance in business planning, structured settlements, settlement planning
