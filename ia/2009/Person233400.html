Tim Allik’s Summary

I am the director of social media public relations at Topaz Partners. I also serves as senior counsel and PR account director at the agency. Current clients include Language Weaver, the Scuderi Group, and Black Duck Software. Along with producing podcasts and webcasts and designing and executing social media PR strategies for clients, I've secured coverage in numerous top tier publications and outlets, including USA Today, Wired, The Christian Science Monitor, The Boston Globe, NPR, Mashable, TechCrunch and ABC News. Before joining Topaz Partners in 2006, I worked at Boston.com as an online editor, producer, and project manager, launching numerous website sections including Boston.com Real Estate and Boston.com Business News. Previously, I produced WBUR-FM's Morning Edition program.

Tim Allik’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, podcasting, webcasting, blogging, media relations, website design and strategy, social media strategy and measurement
