Paul Bennett’s Summary

I am a former Microsoft employee and Program Manager for 12 years, including several years as a Senior and Lead Program Manager, and a former gold star award-winner. I have extensive experience in specifying and delivering software and web applications to customers worldwide.

I am seeking new opportunities in project management, with a focus on computer software or Internet applications. My interests and experiences are primarily in consumer-facing applications.

I believe that I can add significant value, and bring extensive experience to an organization with goals to succeed in a global marketplace.

I hold dual citizenship of the United States and the Republic of Ireland.


Highlights:

- Several years of experience in program/project management and driving projects to completion, working with development, test and business teams, including geographically distributed and culturally diverse teams.

- Experienced in software/internet feature design and specification in both waterfall and agile/scrum development models; also internal process design and specification.

- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills; detail-oriented; excellent written and verbal communication skills.

- Documentation, evangelization and education of new technologies to Microsoft internal teams and external partners.

- Extensive experience in coordinating and driving products throughout the product lifecycle, releasing products in over 30 languages simultaneously.

- People management experience.

- A thorough depth of knowledge to understand, drive and document technical requirements with development/engineering teams.

- Extensive experience in localization processes for large, complex projects, including software applications, websites, documentation, support and legal.
