Michael Marasco’s Summary

As a Circuit City Sales Manager for the Entertainment Department I was responsible for staffing, training, performance management of associates, implementing the companies strategies and meet or exceed department sales goals.

Staffing responsibilities included recruiting and taking part in the interview process. In the area of training I created a calendar based process that utilized both computer training and on the floor workbook type exercises. I also implement ongoing training for senior sales and for any associate that was not achieving his or her set goals. Our department was continually in the top of the regional stores in sales and continued to be so until my departure. I attribute this success to my associates motivation and passion to be the most knowledgeable which created an atmosphere of confidence and trust that customers keyed and led them to buy at our store over our competitor.
