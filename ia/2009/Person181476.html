Vinod K. Singh’s Summary

Master’s in Biotechnology with well developed skills and experience in patent searching, analysis, mapping and reporting. Experienced in variety of Patent searches such as novelty, freedom of operate, invalidity, and opposition searches using various patent and non-patent databases, Patent Portfolio management, patent claim interpretation and analysis, licensing and merger, Prior Art Searching, state of art search, Patent Research and Analytics, Patent and Intellectual Property Valuations, White Space Analysis, etc.

Proficient in use of commercial databases such as Dialog, Delphion, Micropatent, as well as the non-commercial databases provided by the USPTO and Intellectual Project Digital Library (IPDL) hosted by the WIPO and Medline, Embase, SCIRUS, Google scholar etc in finding relevant scientific literature, analyzing and reviewing contents.

Focused and efficient data collection from patents, publications and continuous competitive monitoring, with powerful data aggregation and presentation online, interaction with clients, generation of innovative ideas and commercial ideas.

Expert in project management and data visualization, searching of technical, business and market research & integrating in a meaning full manner and thus generate actionable intelligence, enabling better strategic decisions.

I have developed novel tools and techniques for Invention Lifecycle Management starting early from pre-invention patent strategy, R&D, product development to product commercialization thought various go-to-market strategies. Thus, creating positive impact on the companies balance sheet.

I have been writing technical article, blogs and papers on competitive and patent intelligence in various web resources and technical newsletters.
