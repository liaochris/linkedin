Kevin Popovic’s Summary

Welcome to Ideahaus®.

I work directly with my clients to help them communicate to their markets to create sales and build brands. I serve as their Communications Director to develop their annual communications plan, including schedules, budgets and metrics for:

• Marketing Strategy
• Corporate Identity/Branding
• Advertising (Print, Radio, TV, Outdoor)
• Marketing (Brochures, Packaging, Direct Mail, E-Mail, etc)
• Public Relations (Press Release, Media Relations)
• Internet Development (Planning, Design, Development)
• Multimedia
• Events

Upon approval of the plan I lead the development of the creative resources and tools required. My team includes Senior Communications Professionals as needed, including Art Directors, Graphic Designers, Marketing Specialists, Producers, Copy Writers, Editors, Photographers, Internet Developers, Programmers, and Planners.

Kevin Popovic’s Specialties:

My clients appreciate working directly with me, and the personal service I bring to my exclusive relationships. After 25 years of professional experience I help them understand each type of communications, their opportunities and how we can utilize to build their brands.
