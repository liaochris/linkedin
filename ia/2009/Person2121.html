Aaron Brown’s Summary

I focus on business-to-business public relations campaigns designed to reach and influence decision makers. As account manager of Emerson Network Power’s Liebert business, I'm interested about what's going on in global data centers so i can best oversee strategic planning, media and analyst relations, social media initiatives, and direct content development in the form of case studies, white papers, news releases and trade articles. I've earned numerous awards from Public Relations Society of America for outstanding public relations campaigns and tactics, including a “Best of Show” honor from the Dayton PRSA chapter and the “Rising Star” designation from the Central Ohio PRSA chapter.
