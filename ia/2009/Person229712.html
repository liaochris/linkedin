Tyler Banks’s Summary

Tyler E. Banks serves as a vice president for Marsh specializing in the sales and risk consulting in the Private Client Services department. In this position Banks is responsible for a sales team of highly specialized individuals focused on High Net Worth business providing property & casualty and life & health insurance products and services to address risk exposures and optimize wealth preservation for high net worth individuals and families.

Prior to Marsh Banks served as a regional director of Sperry Van Ness’ National Advisor Organization targeting the company’s growth in the West region of the United States as well as training advisors on marketing tools and services.

Banks also served as an investment analyst and team manager for Safeguard Realty Investments in Costa Mesa, California. While there, Banks managed a team of more than 20 advisors in addition to the marketing and disposition of company franchises.

Banks earned a Master of Business Administration with an emphasis in Entrepreneurship and Real Estate from San Diego State University and earned a Bachelor of Science with an emphasis in Marketing and a minor in Japanese from the Marriot School of Management at Brigham Young University. Banks also completed advanced curriculum and has earned his California Broker’s License.

Banks continually volunteers through his church, mentoring youth on interpersonal communication, organization and managerial skills. He also volunteers with the Boy Scouts of America as a varsity scout leader, educating youth on outdoor survival skills.
