Julie Smith’s Summary

I am a recruiter working exclusively in the moving and storage industry. For the past six years I have progressively advanced my career and now serve as one of the industry’s best known leaders in helping other people advance their careers. I have gone from working for a small multi-industry recruiting firm to owning my own specialized business. Along the way I have developed a network of relationships which continue to grow and flourish on a daily basis.

As the owner of Westbrook Consulting Services, I take extreme pride in my work and my reputation. My clients range from small independent movers up through multi-million dollar companies having 50+ locations. I work with van lines, agents and independents. I have contributed to the improvement of some of the nation’s most influential and well recognized transportation companies.

One of the most important decisions you will ever make in your life is the decision to advance your career. As your recruiter, I will be there with you every step of the way. I will be your eyes and ears in the marketplace as we confidentially explore new opportunities together. You will find that I am both honest and helpful in my approach. I can assure you that your search will be professional and confidential and together we will be able to reach your career goals.

I am hard-working, motivated, intelligent and competitive. As we begin this journey together I will learn of your history, accomplishments, strengths, weaknesses and your true motivation behind making a change so that I can present you with opportunities accordingly.

For my clients, I will do my best to find you exactly what you are looking for and to give you multiple options to choose from. Not only do I source candidates, but I qualify them, check their references and truly get to know each of their personalities. I prefer to build long-lasting, mutually beneficial partnerships. I believe that you will find my approach to be unique and refreshing.
