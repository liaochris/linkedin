Adam Grant’s Summary

Adam Grant runs the Michigan State University student magazine called ing magazine which is distributed in the dorm rooms at MSU. Before starting up the 1st full scale Michigan State Magazine Adam Grant represented General Motors, Alltel, Microsoft, Crest, Jones Soda & Mountain Dew on the campus of Michigan State University. As the General Motors Campus Representative, Adam Grant orchestrated the standing room only prerelease of the "Tenacious D" movie to promote the Chevy Aveo. While working for MSU Career Services in 2008, Adam Grant directed the marketing of the Earn, Learn & Intern event which increased from 400 attendees in 2007 to 1,600 attendees in 2008. The event allowed him to help individuals on the job search. Adam Grant continues to look for opportunities to educate himself and help people with marketing/networking. Currently he is working on helping brands with their peer to peer marketing efforts on college campuses by delivering a quality pool of college students to represent brands, monitoring those students, and providing basic college info.
