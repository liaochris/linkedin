Manu Madhusoothanan’s Summary

Manu has 5 years experience in GIS and water-wastewater projects. She obtained her Masters in Remote Sensing and GIS from University of Edinburgh and holds a Civil Engineering degree from India. She is currently working on wastewater network modeling.

Prior to joining Atkins, she was involved in the design of Wastewater treatment works in Scotland. She also worked on many GIS based assert management and modeling project for Scottish Water.

Her experience in Remote Sensing and Image processing includes various applications areas such as water quality monitoring, weather forecasting, satellite positioning, climate change studies, ocean studies, medical imaging, infrastructure management,natural resource management and environmental studies.

She is interested in hydraulic design&modeling ; GIS network modeling; civil and structural design; Remote Sensing and Image Processing. Her experience includes the use of software packages such as, Infoworks CS, AutoCAD Civil 3D, MapInfo, ArcGIS, Smallworld GIS, ERDAS and ENVI.

Manu is currently working towards CEng with ICE.
