Bob Sproull’s Summary

General Overview:

- 8 Years as a Senior Consultant with Caledonia Group, Inc., a Detroit-based TPS implementation firm. Worked closely with private equity firms performing due dilligence assessments, TPS implementations, LSSTOC implementations, etc.

- VP of Quality and Engineering with Morgan Truck Company. Implemented company's first Lean Six Sigma TOC initiative in 7 facilities.

- VP of Quality and Continuous Improvement with Cambridge Industries, manufacturer of fiber glass parts.

- General Manager with ASC completing a full plant turn-around in 4 months using LSSTOC Methodology.

- Quality Manager with Michelin Tire Corporation.

- Author: Process Problem Solving - A Guide for Maintenance and Operation's Teams - Productivity Press, 2001.
