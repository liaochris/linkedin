Mike Martin’s Summary

I am a 16 year old student, blogger and internet enthusiast. I actively blog about motocross and cycling (my 2 favorite activities) on my personal blog MikeMartinRacing.com. I also blog occasionally on topics regarding the internet, web 2.0, and social media on MikeMartin.me. Furthermore I am a Contributing Editor on 2WheelTuesday.com. While I love writing articles and informational pieces, I am beginning to dabble in copy writing and wrote all of the copy for my fathers lakewinnipesaukeevacationrental.com website.

While writing and editing are some of my favorite activities on the web, in school I am interested in science, technology, and am seriously considering going into the fields of biotechnology or medicinal chemistry as a career path.

If you are interested in working with a dedicated, hard working and motivated 16 year old, contact me and hopefully we can work something out.
