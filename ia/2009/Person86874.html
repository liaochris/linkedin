Ginny Grimsley’s Summary

GOAL

Currently, I am in the market for a job in advertising or public relations in the Tallahassee area. Though my Ph.D. is in English, and my work experience derives primarily from other fields, I am convinced of my value to this business, and I am passionate about breaking into it. I do not want just a job; I want a long-term creative challenge, and a good advertising and/or PR firm creates this. That is where I want to spend my time—the time that it takes to solve that creative puzzle and get it done.

I have spent the last six months studying all aspects of the field in order to make an informed decision about this new pursuit, and I find it to be absolutely fascinating, especially in regards to new media applications. Looking at the field from a consumer's point of view (which, in itself, offers a strong, new perspective), I have utilized my critical thinking, problem-solving, and research and analysis skills to envision, explore and discover fresh strategies for SEO, SEM, SERP, social media, e-commerce, viral marketing, conversion optimization, KW utilization and other tools for clients who need public relations or advertising services. I can not wait to offer these ideas to a team that is up-beat, spirited, progressive, productive and fun, nor can I wait to learn what they will teach me. People say that it is impossible to get into this business without direct experience. I am undeterred; I refuse to believe it; I have decided to do it, and I will be the exception.
