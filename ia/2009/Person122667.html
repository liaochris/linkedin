Jonathan Starets’s Summary

I have helped companies grow their mobile divisions & built a division from scratch. I have been the main point of contact with my clients to better understand their needs, all to find ways to make what I do work better w/ their objectives. I have spent years understanding the intricacies of the mobile marketing eco-system, combining that w/ what works for consumers & also good for retailers.

JStar Mobile is not bound bound by non-compatible corporate rules, pricing, processes, etc. We focus on eliminating the main reasons I have seen retailers hesitate to participate in mobile. The first being that it would be adding another item to manage to their already 'full / busy plates.' In about 2 hours the first month and another hour a month is all that is needed for me to keep your program running, exciting. Really not too much, I promise. All clients will experience personally managed, well planned and executed campaigns 95% done by JStar Mobile.

Once we begin to see the strong results and strong we can track strong ROI from this channel you may want to become more involved (many clients do at this point). So, interactive planning and client (you) engagement is a key priority for me as we move forward. I love seeing clients feeling like they are a part of their mobile marketing efforts & I believe this will eventually enable you to learn & use your own creative input & have this positively reflect in your ongoing mobile marketing.

"Give a man a fish or teach him to fish..."
