Douglas McCready’s Summary

Doug has worked in a variety of executive management and individual contributor roles at Cardinal Health, Hexion Specialty Chemicals, Elmer's Products, Inc. and The Borden Company.

At Cardinal Health, Doug is their Director, IT Mgmt − Stabilization − SAP.

At Hexion, Doug led Merger & Integration initiatives directly related to the on-boarding of entire Information Technology departments of acquired companies. Prior to this, Doug leveraged his years of I/T, Supply Chain and SAP experience in leading Global Sales, Distribution and Transportation I/T initiatives and related processes around the globe. He also integrated SAP and Non-SAP ERP Systems into a Single Global SAP Instance and worked directly with business sponsors and stakeholders in leveraging Technology to achieve significant business synergies and competitive advantages.

While at Elmer's, Doug served in several leadership roles including I/T Director / CIO, Director E/Commerce & Customer Service, and MIS Manager.

As I/T Director / CIO, Doug provided company-wide direction and leadership for all aspects of I/T, including company-wide SAP ERP Strategy & business alignment, E/Commerce, staffing, budgets, and all other facets of I/T.

As Director E/Commerce & Customer Service, Doug directed Global Service to Customer initiatives, CRM, deductions, Customer Strategy & Service, company-wide service measures & analysis processes, supply chain process improvement, and E/Commerce Strategy & deployment.

As MIS Manager, Doug provided company-wide management for all aspects of I/T, including software management, Project Management, ERP Selection and implementation (SAP), E/Commerce strategy and delivery, staffing, vendors & contracts, policies & procedures, and data centers.

Doug is a graduate of the University of Phoenix and (Summa Cum Laude) and Coleman College (San Diego, California).
