David Lemus’s Summary

Solid background in GSM and UMTS Wireless Networks and general RF Engineering with strong emphasis in Ericsson and Nortel GSM RF parameters, procedures and equipment. Skilled engineer with seven years of experience in network design, optimization and performance projects. Experienced in managing projects from conception to completion from a technical perspective. Experienced in handling miscellaneous tasks and activities such as system dimensioning for coverage and capacity, drive test post-processing and analysis, KPI monitoring, site surveys, troubleshooting and acceptance; reporting and presenting results to prime engineers and project managers. Skilled in developing and implementing new procedures to expedite the optimization process. Basic knowledge of other telecommunication technologies: WiMAX, 1xEV-DO, UMTS LTE.

David Lemus’s Specialties:

2G/3G RF Wireless Networks, System Design, Optimization and Performance activities, Ericsson GSM & UMTS Base Stations, Nortel GSM equipment and RF parameters. System dimensioning for coverage and capacity, drive test post-processing and analysis, KPI monitoring, site surveys, troubleshooting and acceptance, Quality Assurance (QA).
