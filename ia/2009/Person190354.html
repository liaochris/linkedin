About us.....

Profile Solutions is a major player in the permanent and contract placement business. We are actively searching for dynamic and enthusiastic computer professionals. Our focus is to help our clients solve information technology staffing problems.

And by doing so, we establish trust in our client relationships, by understanding our clients' business needs and by providing the most qualified, talented professionals for their needs.

Our rapid growth and market leadership is due to our strong ethics, professional integrity, respect and confidentiality, aggressive marketing, skilled employees and ability to offer highly competitive pay and benefits.
