Dr. Patrick Seifried’s Summary

In past years I collected experience in different topics with the objective to build up my own company ModusNext.ch GmbH with its first division TranslationArtwork.com and YOOSOCIAL.ch.

Let’s consider my experience in two dimensions: different companies and various functions with the base of information technology. I worked for companies with diverse sizes and branches to get an overview how company’s act appropriately. Further I worked in complementary functions to extend my mindset and business behavior. A important value to have a comprehensive understanding of business and information processes to consult companies in improving their business - hence to bring them into the next modus with our company ModusNext.ch GmbH. This is our business philosophy, to understand the customer exactly and bring them to a higher and better level to fulfill his business objectives. My two dimension experience is based on my international education in the special field of information technology, specialized in Media and Communications, eBusiness, eCommerce, software development as well as optimizing business and information processes.

The first move of our company is the business unit TranslationArtwork.com which translates Microsoft Word documents and designs Microsoft PowerPoint presentations for business managers throughout our worldwide network of translators and designers.

A further business unit is growing up called “Artwork” which built up Social Media Marketing, Blogging, SEO, SEM, eBusiness or eCommerce platforms, any kind of software development, web portals, and web designs, implemented with our teams in India and Asia. Therefore we have high quality by optimized cost.

If you like to have documents highly qualified translated, outrageous designed presentation, reliable software developed or business processes optimized consistently, we are happy to be your reliable and qualified partner for the future.
