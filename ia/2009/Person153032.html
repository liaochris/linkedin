Marky Stein’s Summary

Marky Stein is an internationally recognized career coach and author on the subjects of job interviewing and career transitioning. She is well known to readers of the Wall Street Journal, USA Today, the Chicago Tribune, the Boston Globe, the Los Angeles Times and other publications. Ms. Stein has worked as a career consultant to over 75 of the Fortune 500 companies.

She is the best selling author of “Fearless Interviewing: How to Win the Job by Communicating With Confidence” (McGraw-Hill 2003) and “Fearless Career Change: The Fast Track to Success in a New Field”, (McGraw-Hill 2004) and co-author of "How to Become a Life Coach (FabJob 2008).

A regular guest on national television, radio and web casts, Ms. Stein is also recognized as Interview Expert on Monster.com. Marky has a private practice career coaching firm serving entry level to executive clients who are seeking employment and/or changing careers with offices in San Jose, CA and San Francisco, CA. She also does web and telephone-based coaching for individuals and groups on Job Seeking, Career Change and Goals for Women.
