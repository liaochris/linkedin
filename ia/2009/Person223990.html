Steve Hockey’s Summary

A board level strategist with experience of FTSE 250 plc's and SME's. Responsible for the creation of start ups, large man management roles and a hugely successful track record within the recruitment and business services sector. Expertise in turning around recruitment companies, providing mentoring and training at senior levels and successfully managing businesses in a recession.

Steve Hockey’s Specialties:

Strategy, People development, Legal, Finance, Interim and having fun.

Steve Hockey’s Experience

  • Associate

    The Recruitment Industry Development Agency (RIDA) Ltd

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    January 2009 — Present (5 months)

    The Recruitment Industry Development Agency (RIDA) Ltd is the largest business support provider to the Recruitment Industry, with a passion for delivering practical business solutions to the complex and challenging issues facing the recruitment industry today.

    RIDA uses hands on experience to help organisations realise their strategy, whether this be a business start up,creation of online strategy, launch of new sectors/markets, driving through service improvements or delivering complex projects within an established organisation.

    My Speciality is business consultancy to Professional Services recruitment companies from FTSE 250 plcs to Start ups looking for advice on how to gain market share in a recession. In addition I also offer training and mentoring for business leaders in the recruitment sector.

  • Managing Director

    SH Ventures

    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    April 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    SH Ventures invests in high performance businesses in the Human Capital and Talent Acquisition markets.

    All our companies embrace the very latest in advanced technology and are encouraged to blend traditional business models with free thinking and progressive ideas to keep ahead of the curve and the competition.

    Backed by investors with over 100 years recruitment experience between them we are interested in any ideas that might require expertise, funding, mentoring or Non Exec Directors.

    Current projects include a traditional search and selection business, a business that places compliance and regulation professionals and a venture that is looking at the wine industry.

    We also offer a range of consultancy services including how to make profits during a recession and how to develop an online recruitment strategy that will blow the competition out of the water.
