Gary Darna’s Summary

My name is Gary Darna and I am the Vice President and cofounder of Chapter Zero. We are an advanced web design and development firm specializing in customer web-based software for information and digital asset management. We've had the opportunity to work on projects for Procter & Gamble, the International Council of Shopping Center (ICSC), and a handful of other diverse clientele. We're currently a startup company looking for partnerships, investors, and talented web developers to join our rapidly expanding engineering team.

Personally, I have been designing for the web since 1999. I am self-taught and fluent in XHTML and CSS in addition to the entire Adobe Creative Suite. I began using Flash when it was on v4 in 2000. I use a Mac Pro and swear by Apple for all my computing needs. My goal is to build an organization that offers innovative software solutions for use on the web that help my company's clients succeed in their endeavors, whatever they may be.
