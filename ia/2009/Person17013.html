Anne Gronow’s Summary

Anne has over 18 years experience in the marketing and communications profession locally and globally - senior marketing and communications management positions within financial services, recruitment, change management, human resources professional services, utilities and mining industry environments.

Shell Co of Australia and AGL consultancy - global and local employee communication and change strategies that set benchmarks for other change projects locally and globally.

ASIC - recruitment review and recommendations for process improvement and efficiencies across the ASIC Directorates. Anne also developed and implemented an integrated communications strategy for a major change program.

LINK Recruitment - National Marketing Mgr. Qualified financial and professional services experience - responsible for branding, business strategy and planning, resource planning, workforce planning, marketing, PR/media, events, communications, business development and proprietary research.

Arthur Andersen - National Marketing Mgr. Wide professional services experience -integrated business planning and strategy, marketing programs, internal/external communications, resource management, PR, advertising, business development, events and market research.
