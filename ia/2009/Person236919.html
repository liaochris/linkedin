Tony Ockleshaw’s Summary

Tony Ockleshaw is a highly experienced, board-level executive with a track record of business achievement in several countries in the Automotive, Marine, Aviation and IT sectors. He is a proven leader with outstanding skills in sales management, motivation, communication, business development, account management, organisation, negotiation, innovation, sales & staff training, marketing, customer service, corporate governance and strategy.

Functionally, he is an expert in business development with impressive credentials in sales, business start-ups, dealer and distribution networks. He is also an accomplished public speaker.

Prior to starting his business in 2003, he spent nearly 15 years in business development, sales and operations, leading and building teams and businesses across 6 countries.

He has completed Microsoft Sales & Marketing Certification, ScotWork Negotiation Skills Training, Prince II Foundation Certification, IBM Technical Training amongst others and is currently completing his MBA.

Outside work, he has represented Victoria in Tennis and Hockey and Australia in Sailing and Hockey. Competed in 3 Sydney Hobart Yacht Races and the round Australia yacht race and enjoys sailing, golf, business, reading, technology, health and fitness.
