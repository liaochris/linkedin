Cloud9 Design’s Summary

Wife, Mama & All-around Creative Gal
_____________________________________________________________

Fashion designer of an eco-friendly, hand silkscreened apparel line for adults, maternity, baby, kids & accessories. Committed to using earth friendly fabrics, sustainable practices, recycled packaging and generally doing my part to leave a lighter footprint on the earth while creating handmade wearable art.
