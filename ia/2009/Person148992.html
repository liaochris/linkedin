Marco Busi’s Summary

My responsibilities cover project managing all consulting activities in our portfolio, planning and managing our business growth and interfacing with stakeholders. Our clients vary from multi-billion dollar (> 10 Bill USD) American corporations to small and medium Scottish Enterprises (<50 Mill GBP) and Government.

I have several years of experience as project manager, consultant and researcher working on multimillion projects, improving and innovating business processes related to strategic sourcing and procurement, supply chain design & management and performance measurement.

My consultancy activities cover feasibility studies, business planning, process mapping & improvement and working with Universities to build research excellence. My portfolio spans various industries: automotive, electronics, business and financial services, IT, business process outsourcing, entertainment and hospitality, advertising and media. I am used to interface with practitioners and policy makers at national and European level.

I hold an MSc in Industrial Engineer (Italy) and a PhD in Performance Management of Collaborative Enterprises (Norway). An Entrepreneurial Scientist by choice, my background is in applied contract research and development and innovative consulting, which I have built working in Academia and Industry in Italy, Norway and the UK.

My work is published in international journals, books, trade magazines, and newspapers (among others, The Times). I am often invited to chair and present at industry and scientific events worldwide.

Beside my role as CEO of Carisma RCT, I am Editor-in-Chief of "Strategic Outsourcing, an International Journal", published by Emerald Publishing Group and currently part of a portfolio subscribed by > 6,000 Institutions worldwide. I teach Supply Chain Operations Management and Logistics and International Trade at the University of Liverpool and I am project supervisor at the University of Strathclyde Business School.
