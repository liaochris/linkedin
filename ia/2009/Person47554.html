Clive Hornsby’s Summary

A confident, self motivated, articulate business manager, capable of working under pressure and adhere to schedules. Excellent analytical and technical experience combined with listening, communication skills, initiative, motivation and a team player. With a strong ‘Customer Care’ focus, thrives on a challenge, tenacious and does not require supervision. A broad overview of all aspects of business and IT allows for an open minded, flexible approach and quick adaptation into new environments.

Excellent customer facing and technical skills provide a commercial, human interface between the customer, the business and complex multi platform IT systems. A sound knowledge of all business functions, with a unique overview of organisation structure and relationships. This comprehensive experience is a critical factor for correctly determining strategy, requirements capture and accurate analysis. Development of effective toolsets and integrated processes with built in internal customer dependencies, which ensure data integrity and self governance.

Comprehensive knowledge of master data and it’s evolution through the product lifecycle. A dedicated practitioner of data normalisation methods to eliminate duplication and data integrity issues, to reduce the huge resource costs of manually managing multiple datasets. Successful implementation of cost effective solutions to achieve business milestones and satisfy customer needs, through identification of business strategy, business needs, customer integration, and end user profiling. Improved toolsets and processes deployed into the business have improved customer service levels, employee morale, productivity and profitability. Extensive sales, marketing and design experience ensure corporate goals are achieved with electronic trading, enhancing internal and external communications, through development of Internet, Intranet and Extranet services.
