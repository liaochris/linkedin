Judith Morgan’s Summary

I have run my own businesses for 30 years. The first 20 years I created and then sold my own firm of accountants, based in Clapham, South London. My next business interests were in travel, stress management and cleaning. My Cleaning Biz is licensed across London and the South East.

Now I concentrate on sharing my business acumen with young businesses want to understand and implement E-Myth strategies into their Business to Love.

I teach Wealth Creation for Entrepreneurs.

I am a representative of Cartel who help clients get out of debt - www.ClaimWithJudith.co.uk

Judith Morgan’s Specialties:

small business mentoring, coaching, wealth creation principles, investing in property, entrepreneurial activity, creating a business to love, creating profits and assets, solopreneurship, marketing admin and finance for small business, the Law of Attraction in action, success principles and DebtBusting
