Byron Sletten’s Summary

I'm a partner and creative director for MindActive, a St. Louis based company that is focused on strategies, tools and tactics for e-learning, online training and info based marketing programs. This approach leverages social media and web communication tools to engage, teach and lead.

We are the process of rethinking Learning Management Systems - and developing some ground-breaking technology for rich media community learning. One of which is now available as a module for Moodle (an open source LMS).

My real professional passion is helping solve creative challenges - working through the process from focusing intent, visualizing what is possible, to tactical execution - the realization.

In addition, as Assistant Professor at the University of Illinois, I taught classes in computer graphics, design and fine art. With an in-depth background in 3D animation, video editing, motion graphics and interactive design, I still love to teach college-level workshops on a regular basis. One of the reasons I'm so excited about our focus on e-learning is my interest in education.
