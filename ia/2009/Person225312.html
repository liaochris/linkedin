Christopher Watson’s Summary

The online marketing industry is exploding and represents the best opportunity business has ever had to get more customers and control your own destiny. I believe in working with the best and producing the best. This area of marketing is changing at a breakneck speed so staying on top of the game is paramount.

The work I did 15 years ago with Infolink was the best measurable media on the planet, we were the first form of reader response publishing in Australia

The work I did with my own company Downloadit was the best form of online measurability in print publishing

My work at Reed gave me an insight into how larger publishing companies are combatting and adopting new media technologies

My work with Eyespygolf is taking the very best technology available and delivering a product that has most golfers who use the system..utterly loving it

The work I'm currently doing with Free IQ and StomperNet is literally working with the very best minds in the world when it comes to online marketing. Brad Fallon, Andy Jenkins, Leslie Rohde, Don Crowther, Andy Edmonds are online giants and I get to work in their world on a daily basis...talk about a learning curve.
