Michelle Batten’s Summary

Michelle Batten is a digital marketing and advertising veteran whose strategic thinking and creative passion enables clients and partners alike to be successful in tackling the unknowns of the digital medium. A dynamic new media visionary, Michelle applies her understanding of clients' industry and business objectives along with knowledge of the most relevant media usage trends to develop and manage a strategic direction and plan for their digital initiatives. She has spent nearly fifteen years in the interactive arena developing and executing integrated communication strategies to enable brands to connect with young consumers, especially influencers, across multiple channels, including web, kiosk, e-mail, search and social networks. Most recently, her focus has been on helping brands understand how the influence of consumer dialogue within the social media realm (such as blogging, podcasting, tagging, virtual worlds, etc.) impacts visibility and engagement with their audience.

A former digital agency/Big 5 consulting leader, Michelle successfully engages senior-level marketers with knowledge, know-how and creative passion to deliver value and grow relationships. She has overseen strategic development and execution of digital marketing programs for clients such as Alltel Wireless, CNN Pipeline, the Coca-Cola Company and Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines, including the launch of youth-focused microsites, social media applications and other lead acquisition and community building experiences.

Michelle founded iMediaWorks, a marketing consultancy that creates Digital Roadmaps for Brands, in order to help marketers and public relations leaders integrate their communications so they can stay connected with their consumers.
