Sydnie Moore’s Summary

With more than 20 years of writing, media and public relations and special events experience, Sydnie Moore currently heads Moore & Moore Communications, handling work for a variety of entities, including the law offices of Casey Gerry Schenk Francavilla Blatt & Penfield, Trigild Corporation and the new Grand Del Mar Resort.

In addition, she is also a freelance writer/copywriter, and writes brochures, newsletters, e-blasts and web copy. She has contributed to the San Diego Daily Transcript and the San Diego Union-Tribune.

With special expertise in media, special events coordination and community relations, Ms. Moore's past client experience includes Chive/Kensington Grill, the American Society of Interior Designers, Chipotle Mexican Grill, ArtWalk San Diego, Coronado School of the Arts, the American Council on Exercise and Ringling Brothers and Barnum & Bailey Circus.

Ms. Moore has years of experience working with local, regional and national print, electronic and online media. Throughout her career, Ms. Moore has publicized and developed special events and promotions for a full range of local, regional and national accounts in the restaurant, hospitality, entertainment, professional services, real estate, retail and healthcare industries.

Prior to founding her company, she worked as an account supervisor for several leading San Diego public relations and advertising firms. Ms. Moore was also a reporter, covering politics, lifestyle and business for The Light Newspapers; she also interned for the Public Affairs Department of KFMB TV (CBS).

She has earned numerous accolades for her work, including awards from the Public Relations Club of San Diego and Public Relations Society of America.

She earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in telecommunications and film from San Diego State University and attended the University of Madrid, where she earned a certificate of proficiency in Spanish language, history and culture.
