Gal Steinberg’s Summary

As an enthusiastic technologist I enjoy combining my love of technology and my passion for innovation. Those interests have led me to the area of product marketing - I especially enjoy identifying market needs and translating them into great software products. I have created or improved on internet consumer software as well as software for the enterprise market and I had the privilege of working on initial concepts taking them from an idea all the way to a product with a successful market launch.

Having worked in start-ups as well as larger companies I know and have done what it takes to get things executed beyond expectations and work outside comfort zones. I am not afraid to "get my hands dirty" and enjoy the challenge of moving fast to achieve the company's objectives.

I'm a huge believer that the most critical component in any business is the people and team. I would welcome an opportunity to leverage my experience in new opportunities.

LinkedIn is a great way to connect and stay in touch, so if you know me, please send me an invite to join your network!
