Nick Christensen’s Summary

New urban planner endlessly taken with big cities and their demons. Particularly interested in transit policy and how it relates to energy, land use, climate change, and economic development.

I have experience researching policy, along with the quantitative skills used for analysis, budgeting and financing projects. Excellent communications skills: as writer and editor I have a sharp sense of how to I know how to trim complex issues down to their essential parts and convey a strong, simple message. I am very good with Microsoft Office, Photoshop, and have advanced experience using ArcMap GIS to answer geospatial questions and solve problems. Being multilingual and having overseas experience, I'm also eager to explore how planning can be applied as a tool for international development.
