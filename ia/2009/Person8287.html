Alex Jouravlev’s Summary

I’ve been working in IT for 25+ years, first in academic research (Object & Deductive Systems) then in software development for large IT Shops. From 2002 I am a Principal Consultant with Business Abstraction, working as a trainer, mentor, methodologist and modelling practitioner.

As a co-convener of Business Systems Architecture & Design SIG for ACS NSW, I contribute my time to enable more effective solution development across Australian IT. I facilitate networking between the experts in Business & Requirements Analysis, Solution Design, EAI and other Software Development disciplines.

I regularly speak on visual modelling, specifically Business/Conceptual Modelling for SOA. I am also involved in OASIS activities.

Alex Jouravlev’s Specialties:

- Implementing cost-effective Business & Conceptual Modelling practices for SOA implementation & Service-Oriented Development;
- Implementing Model-Driven Architecture, Analysis & Design practices based on Sparx Enterprise Architect;
- Training in Visual Modelling in UML, BPMN and other notations, effective modelling techniques;
- Mentoring junior modellers, taking teams and projects through learning curve;
- Conducting modelling sessions on strategy/policy, business and technical levels.
