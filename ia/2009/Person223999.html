Steve Hough’s Summary

Steve Hough is a graphic designer with extensive experience producing compelling designs from initial concept through final product. He's designed and produced over 100 published book and magazine titles as well as several card games, catalogs, and websites. Steve is a dynamic team player skilled at developing creative solutions to the toughest design and marketing challenges.

During his decade of experience, Steve has worked on many high-profile properties, including Farscape, Stargate SG-1, Legend of the Five Rings, 7th Sea, Deadlands, and Spycraft.

Steve has worked with some of the most innovative teams in the hobby game industry including Wizards of the Coast, Alderac Entertainment Group, and most recently Crafty Games.
