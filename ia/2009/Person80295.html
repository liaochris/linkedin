Forrest Didier’s Summary

Apsara Ventures LLC is a global investment company based in Seattle, Washington and partnered with Beijing Linden Consulting Company. Mr. Didier’s company helps businesses throughout Asia to gain access to US markets and venture capital resources and to identify and negotiate opportunities to bolster international expansion efforts. It also assists US-based businesses to identify, target and negotiate international expansion opportunities in China, India, Japan, Korea and Southeast Asian markets.

Mr. Didier has over 25 years of international experience, including the last 15 years actively involved in the Asia Pacific region. He lived in Hong Kong for six years (1999-2005) and Sydney, Australia for two years (2005-2007), responsible for regional operations across Asia Pacific markets. He led negotiations with the Chinese government that resulted in the initial and only approval to launch internet research services in China in 2001 and has conducted many other negotiations with senior officials in China in the past seven years. He has also negotiated and successfully completed deals in every market across the region – including many Southeast Asian deals.
