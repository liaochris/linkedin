Denise Renee Phinn’s Summary

I am at an exciting juncture in my personal and professional life where my passion for music is about to become my career. In addition to currently working on a personal CD project, I am now offering my audio production services for businesses on a full time basis after servicing clients on a part time bases over the last few years.

I also now work with my husband in our internet marketing and consulting firms where we offer health, beauty and home products to consumers and provide companies with consulting services and business equipment. In addition to our joint business ventures, my husband and I also pioneered and pastor a unique Christian ministry dedicated to helping people from all walks of life develop an authentic relationship with God.

My past career experience has taken me from secondary education, to higher education, to event and project management and finally to sales. I am excited to see how all these experiences combined will make for personal and business growth in 2009.
