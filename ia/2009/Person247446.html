Will Conley’s Summary

I have been writing professionally since 1999; since 2004 I have focused exclusively on the Internet and social media technologies.

Print media is going the way of the stone tablet; witness the deafening roar of the mighty Tribune Company as it crumbles into bankruptcy. The traditional barriers between producer and consumer are crashing down all around us. Cable news heavyweights, major pharmaceuticals and platinum-plated marketing firms are waging war on the likes of Huffington Post, Twitter moms, and some guy who wears your branded shirt and puts it on YouTube.

Social media is resequencing the very DNA of the business world. Many companies are beginning to wake up to the power of the small voice. Those are the companies who will not only survive the recession but thrive in the new bull market to come. Those are the types of companies I am so excited to be working for. In all my professional writing and social media activities I make it a point to:

-remain genuine;
-cultivate lasting relationships with real people;
-pursue quality and effectiveness to the best of my ability;
-stay up-to-the-minute on new technological developments;
-leave no stone unturned in the pursuit of market dominance;
-enjoy every minute of it.
