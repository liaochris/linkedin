Mark Cira’s Summary

Mark is a veteran entrepreneur of 15 years who’s founded and grown multiple SaaS ventures that deliver transformative change to business. Mark’s record in building successful companies include Spatial Data (acquired by Experian/First American in 1998), and SureClose.com (acquired by Fortune 700 Stewart Title in 2004).

Mark also founded PrintDepartment.com in 1996, and grew the company to a successful acquisition in 2006

In 2006 and 2007, Mark closely studied emerging technologies in 1-to-1, digital publishing, and also the growing adoption of on-demand CRM and Salesforce.com. In Fall 2007, Mark placed a bet that marrying real-time, data driven, digital publishing closely together with CRM on-demand, would transform how businesses publish and communicate, in print and online.

A course was charted, Print Sf was formed, and a team of top talent was assembled. One year later, 1-to-1 publishing on demand was born with Print Sf’s launch at Dreamforce 2008.

Mark lives in sunny Encinitas, California, blocks from the blue Pacific. He was born and raised in Virginia, where he earned a BS in Media and Communication from James Madison University in 1990. Away from the office, Mark enjoys flying his Cirrus SR20, running, and spending time with his family and 4 dogs.

Mark Cira’s Specialties:

Since my 1990 graduation (JMU), I have worked exclusively at founding, growing, and selling a series of technology companies, normally related to document management and/or marketing/print-on-demand solutions as an extension of CRM.
