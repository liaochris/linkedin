Valerie Grimes’s Summary

For many years, I headed my own Dallas ad agency, producing ads and sales collateral for companies in the construction industry. We did good work, made a profit and never missed a deadline. However that required a tremendous amount of dedication on the part of me and my staff and I also witnessed the toll it took on our healths and family relationships, especially mine...I lost 10 pounds that last year and on a 5 foot frame...that isn't good.

So, when the market turned the wrong corner in 01, I jumped. I landed in a field that is a little like advertising, one that uses the subconscious to effect behavior, but in a way that is beneficial to the participant...it's hypnosis.

Hypnosis assisted me in changing my reactions to certain situations as well as unlocking barriers to future growth. Since it was so beneficial to me, I started my own practice in 02, sold it in 06 and went back to advertising for a short time, only to realize I "just couldn't do it anymore", that side of me wasn't interested in that work. So, I ramped my hypnosis practice back up in Feb of 08.

I call myself a "mind coach" to emphasize the fact that we spend very little time coaching or training our minds in the area of behavior and emotional response.

From this modality, I have started two companies:
COMPREHENSIVE TRAINING SERVICES (sales coaching and sales materials) and
MENTAL STRENGTH CORPORATE WELLNESS PROGRAMS (combines hypnosis and fitness trainer with my partner Dennis Sheridan)

In addition, I see clients for private sessions and conduct workshops on improving sleep patterns, reducing stress, improving health and women in transition.

My ultimate goal is to conduct nationally attended seminars.
