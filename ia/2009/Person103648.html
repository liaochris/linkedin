Jane Emberty’s Summary

More than 25 years in the human resources industry within the retail, hospitality, government, higher education and non-profit industries has provided great opportunities and exposure to a variety of challenges within each of those industries. I loved HR work but have found coaching to be my passion. I love helping individuals discover their life passion and achieve their dreams. Whether you are looking to change careers, want to pursue some childhood or current dreams, looking for more balance and sanity in your life, this coach can help you pull it all together. I love focusing on people's strengths and use the assessment tool from Leading From Your Strengths. It is awesome and great tool for you and your team.

Jane Emberty’s Specialties:

My HR experience is quite diverse from training to employee relations to strategic planning and beyond. I am a possibility thinker and if you have a challenge that you can't seem to overcome, give me a call for your free consultation. If I can't help you I will recommend someone who can help you with your challenge.
