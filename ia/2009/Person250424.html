YP Lai (BNI)’s Summary

Presently the Regional Director for Business Network International (BNI) Malaysia and the National Director for BNI Thailand, Vietnam and Korea. With BNI, we have created a positive business environment to help thousands of business people connect, and increase their business through the building of strong & trusting business relationships.

Able to assist business people intending to trade and start business in Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam and Korea to connect with trusted and reliable local business partners and service providers.

A Civil & Structural Engineer by training, having had a tertiary education in Canterbury, New Zealand, I have been active in the construction and property development industry for the past 15 years, and have led the development of more than RM1 billion worth of property.

Also a certified trainer in Junior Chamber International (JCI) Malaysia, actively training young people to become effective leaders and entrepreneurs. Have been given the life time award as a JCI Senator.

Happily married with two lovely daughters, I have a strong passion in developing the human potential, and my two burning desires are:

1. to inspire young people to become positive minded global citizens, and

2. to create a community of business people that believes in the "Givers Gain" philosophy i.e growing business through trust and cooperative efforts

Really LOVE food! Have a goal to travel all around the world and taste the best local food in town. Visit www.bestplace2eat.com to check out some of my favourite food. Please add your own favourites at www.bestplace2eat.com/member so that other Food Lovers will benefit from your recommendation.

Other interests include reading, networking and spending time with family.

Thanks for visiting my profile. Please let me know how I can help you get connected to the right people, or in anyway at all.
