Bob Swelgin’s Summary

Accomplished CEO/COO/President/VP with expertise and value in International Manufacturing Operations, Sales, International Restructuring, Integration and Growth. A proven P&L leader with a unique value and ability in leading a restructuring/turnaround challenge while organizing for and driving, long term growth. Turning distressed and underperforming global assets to positive sales and earnings results by driving customer centric thinking in all segments of the business while leading operational excellence through LEAN tools and increasing brand equity. Product and technology experience in electronics, plastics and metals in automotive, medical, consumer, defense electronics and industrial (valve and motor controls) markets. Executive leader in Private Equity and Public companies. Open to Relocation.
