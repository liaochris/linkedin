Brett Emison’s Summary

I am an attorney at Langdon & Emison, with our primary office near Kansas City, Missouri, and additioanl offices in St. Louis, Missouri and Chicago, Illinois.

Our practice focuses solely on cases involving catastrophic injury and death. Our attorneys are recognized nationally as some of the very best in our field. I, and our other attorneys, have authored legal articles on products liability and negligence issues.

We try cases across the country against some of the largest corporations in the world, including all major auto manufacturers, railroad companies and trucking companies. Our cases have been featured in national media, such as the New York Times, USA Today, major network newscasts, CNN and more. Our experience, focus and resources allow our law firm to maximize the recovery for our clients, with total verdicts and settlement exceeding half a billion dollars.

Our law firm battled General Motors to the Supreme Court of the United States ... and won. Baker v. GM, 522 U.S. 222 (1998) held that corporations could not buy the silence of a former employee (Ron Elwell). This 9-0 decision is taught in law schools across the country.
