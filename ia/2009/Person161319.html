Michael G. Lutz’s Summary

Relentless revitalizer proven to establish lean operational excellence for multi channel marketers and distributors specializing in distribution, order processing, inventory , warehouse, logistics, third party logistics, reverse logistics, transportation, and customer service management. Leads proactive change by improving processes, productivity, and profitability. Accomplishments include managing multi-site, high volume distribution centers and call centers and third party logistics businesses including design, start-ups, consolidations, and re-engineering (DCs 450,000 to 2M sq.ft. and call centers 30 to 300+ seats – 100 in India). Launched and managed two new third party logistics divisions, one at no cost that added $24M revenue in one year. Benchmarked best practices processes in turn-around, high growth, buy-out and start-up environments. Extensive multi-million dollar P&L experience with know-how to reduce and control expenses. A proactive leader adept at building and retaining a strong team as well as building and maintaining strong cross functional, vendor and customer relations.
