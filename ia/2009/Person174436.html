Nick Wiley’s Summary

An energetic, results-oriented director. Strong operations, logistics and supply chain management background in several sectors, including fmcg, telecoms, manufacturing, publishing, high street retailing and the pub & restaurant industries.
Strategic and entrepreneurial with a ‘hands-on’ approach and problem solving skills, and proven capability to make things happen. Quickly grasps key business issues, a logical and lateral thinker, and a creative and flexible leader who builds strong relationships and loyal teams.

Always makes a difference, whether in permanent or interim roles.

Track record of delivery in plc and private company environments, including Marks & Spencer, Grand Met, Geest, Exel and Novar. Has led significant change programmes to turn around under-performing companies and functions through cost base realignment, marketing innovation and revenue growth.
• Developed and implemented clear strategy for private telecoms group implementing full management restructure, reducing headcount by 25% and overhead costs by £6m per annum, reducing losses from £400k to profit of £50k per month within 3 months
• Led re-engineering of Germany based pan-European supply chain resulting in improved next day service levels from 87% to 96% and reduced inventory holding levels from 23m to 18m euros
• Performed full strategic review for publisher owned distributor and identified a clear vision and strategic plan. Turned operation around, reducing costs by £1.2m on £28m cost base, and grew income by £1m achieving profit within 5 months (first time for 3 years)
• Led strategy development process for newly created European Logistics sector and won £50m pa in new revenues, £100m in renewed contracts and delivered £300k contribution from consultancy based ‘projects’.
• Started up new-build food factory in South Africa from scratch delivering profitable (17% EBITDA) £10m t/o company in first year of operation.

  • CEO

    Invomo

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Invomo is a new company formed from the acquisitions of PNC Telecom and Band-X, to serve companies across a range of markets that share a need to improve the way they communicate with customers and stakeholders.

    The role continues the turnaround and strategic development activity undertaken at PNC, providing a fresh start for the staff, business and new opportunities for customers.

    With the combined resources of Band-X and PNC, Invomo has a future-proofed infrastructure offering the best of IP and TDM telephony based hosted services. The new company has the collective experience of delivering for over 5000 customers.

    Invomo is launched to fill a gap in the market for an independent supplier of communications services that rejects the traditional telecoms sales approach of maximising traffic and volumes, instead taking a fresh approach; focusing on understanding customers’ market dynamics and solving their business challenges.
