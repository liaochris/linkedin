Tiago Soromenho’s Summary

Entrepreneur and award-winning creative professional focused on leading companies to create best of class online brand experiences that exceed expectations, satisfy customer needs & business goals, and provide return on investment. Currently focused on growing StickyStreet into the next cool SaaS killer app for small businesses and organizations around the world.

Tiago Soromenho’s Specialties:

Executive management and planning. Award-winning experience, vision, and leadership in emerging media. User Experience planning, architecture, and design. Technological and creative expertise in defining marketing strategy, ID and Brand development. Internal and external process definition, componentization, localization, and asset management. Expertise in the automotive, fashion, beauty, technology, financial, pharmaceutical, aviation, entertainment, hospitality, and publishing industries.
