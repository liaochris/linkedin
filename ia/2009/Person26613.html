Ben Patterson’s Summary

Ben Patterson’s Specialties:

Highly qualified, deadline-oriented, original, and meticulous writer and editor specializing in technology. I've written gadgets product reviews ranging from the newest smart phones and MP3 players to DVD recorders and LCD TVs. My longer features tackle such issues as wireless security, the dangers of driving with a cell phone, the foibles of Microsoft Outlook, and the varying array of HDTV options and programming.
