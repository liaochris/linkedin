Rob Arnett’s Summary

Rob has more than 25 years of marketing research and consulting experience, much of it dealing with the unique marketing and research challenges of new product development. This experience spans the entire development process, from opportunity identification to national rollout, and cuts across a variety of categories as diverse as consumer packaged goods, technology, and services.

Rob Arnett’s Specialties:

• New product forecasting
• Product and service optimization
• Pricing
• Market segmentation and opportunity identification
