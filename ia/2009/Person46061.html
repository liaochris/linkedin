Ciaran Foley’s Summary

To boards, executives and senior management of rapid-growth companies or start-ups requiring expert guidance and assistance evaluating and managing the forces that drive organizational performance, Ciaran brings the strategic focus and mature business judgment gained over fifteen years of diverse startup and operational experience within a variety of entrepreneurial, high-technology and market-making businesses.

Ciaran is an accomplished entrepreneur, having founded or served as an equity partner in several companies holding positions as CEO, COO or Board Member and has participated in advisory, project management, fundraising, business development, technical, sales and marketing roles.

Ciaran has been twice nominated for The Ernst & Young, LLP Orange County Entrepreneur of the Year Award and was recipient of the Bank of America Award in Computer Science. He has served on the Boards of Directors of the Advertising Production Association of Orange County (APAOC), the California Branch of the National Association for Missing and Exploited Children and The Entrepreneurship Institute. Ciaran also has experience as a featured speaker and expert moderator.

Ciaran currently serves on the National Board of Directors of WebWiseKids and the Honorary Board of Discovery Arts. He is also an active member of the Orange County venture and entrepreneurial community, serving on the Executive Committee of the Tech Coast Venture Network. In addition to accelerating entrepreneurial success as a Strategic Business Consultant, Ciaran is also a resident advisor to the Rancho Santiago College District Digital Media Center and Incubator.

Ciaran Foley received his B.S. in Computer & Information Science at the University of California, Irvine (UCI) where he served as the local chapter President of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) and studied under internet pioneer Einar Stefferud.
