Danny Huynh’s Summary

I currently oversee a team of search marketing experts. In addition to managing my team and fostering client relationships, I am also charged with building vendor/publisher relationships for innovative advertising solutions. In addition to my work at Razorfish, I am currently in my last year at the University of Notre Dame. I will complete my executive MBA in the summer of May 2010.

My experience includes traditional media, national advertising, local advertising, branding campaigns, and performance campaigns. They have allowed me to take an active role to advocate the importance of integration across consumer touchpoints, attribution modeling for optimization, offline/online advertisng planning, and true integration of search and display opportunities.
