David Drescher’s Summary

Entrepreneur who founded Roam Secure (www.roamsecure.net) in 2000, built cutting edge and patented emergency alerting solutions for the largest cites, states and government agencies in the US, grew the company and lead the emergency text alerting/mass notification industry, sold Roam Secure in 2007 to Cooper Industries (Ticker: CBE,) a Fortune 500 global company. Managed integration of Roam Secure into Cooper Notification and expanded the business across the US and internationally.

I enjoy innovating and working with motivated and talented professionals to transform ideas into reality with the goals of: (i) making the world a better place; (ii) making customers extremely satisfied; (iii) empowering employees to strive for more and appreciate their work; and (iv) generating attractive returns for investors.
