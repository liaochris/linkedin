Scott Rynbrandt’s Summary

Health education and life skills volunteer. Raising community awareness of health risks and preventable diseases through seminars, workshops and lectures. Teacher at local school, responsible for 8 classes.

Collaborating with other NGOs such as Millennium Challenge, IMF and World Bank. Facilitating several community based groups and clubs for children and adults. Creating and editing a local newspaper.

Purpose of Peace Corps service is to fulfill Peace Corps' three main goals.

1. Helping the people of interested countries in meeting their need for trained men and women.

2. Helping promote a better understanding of Americans on the part of the peoples served.

3. Helping promote a better understanding of other peoples on the part of Americans.
