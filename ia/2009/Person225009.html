Steve Williams’s Summary

Co-founder of Innersync, a web design/software firm that creates powerful web experiences for business and education, Steve blends a highly developed creative sense with an intuitive understanding of the progressive technologies that enhance every Innersync product and execution. Steve's mantra in producing optimal user experiences focuses on creating "powerful connections" through content tools that are easy to use, and design that's easy to access.

In addition to directing the creative strategy for Innersync's clients, he oversees the sales, marketing and client development for the company, where he's played a pivotal role on such accounts as Yahoo!, Delta Airlines, International Paper.
