Damian Smith’s Summary

UK - North West based Consultant. A Company Director and Member of Professional Contractors Group.

An Oracle e-Business Consultant since 1994, 3 years of which working permanent as a consultant for Oracle UK and has undertaken assignments through Oracle Resources as a Contractor.

Performance & e-Business Architect for the world’s largest Oracle e-Business system. This payment and accounting system has yearly volumes of around 700 million payments to over 20 million clients with a total annual value of over £120 billion (10% UK GDP).

Damian has worked on many high profile projects for well-known organisations including; DWP, Siemens, Sony, Airtours/MyTravel, Lloyds/TSB, British Telecommunications and Coca Cola. The quality of work is shown by the fact Damian maintains long term working relationships with a lot of his clients, returning to many of these to work on other projects.

e-Business Tech and Functional v9 to v11.5.10.2 and R12. GL, AR, AP, IBY, XLA, PO, FA, CM, OE/OM, HR, iproc, AK dev, INV, BOM, ENG, WIP, MRP
Extensive knowledge of Application Object Layer (FND).

RDBMS 6 - 11g, SQL & PL/SQL, Reports v2 to 10 (at design, delivery, training and audit level) and Forms 3/4/4.5/9i (Build and design. Workflow 2.6, Des 2000.
Extensive tuning of Core Database and Oracle Applications.

UNIX (vi, scripting etc.), DOS/Windows o/s, Relational Systems Design, C/C++.
