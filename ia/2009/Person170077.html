Menno van der Zalm’s Summary

I am an experienced business development an people manager. In 2006 I started working for Altran as a consultant and was involved in business process consulting focused on Innovation Management for large companies.

As a specialist in managing large scale projects and as Business Manager at Altran since februari 2007, I am currently focussed on building a network to enforce our consulting proposition on business- and IT-consultancy. In this role I am responsible for business development, recruitment and management of the consultants in my unit. On the client side I am focussed on utilities and telecom.

Business- and IT consultancy:
Business Process Management (BPM or BPR), Enterprise Content Management (ECM), Business Intelligence (BI), IT Service Management (ITSM), Project- and Program Management (PM), Work-flow (WFM)

Innovation Management:
In-licensing and out-licensing of Intellectual Property (IP), patent portfolio management, IP strategy, business development, New product development management and innovation management (innovation portfolio).
