Marco Ryan’s Summary

Mark (Marco) Ryan is an independent Senior Executive consultant with proven ability in developing global eCommerce and marketing strategies. His experience includes Europe, Asia and the USA. He has built an enviable reputation for helping FTSE, 250/FORTUNE 500 Boards and senior executive teams to understand the impact of web 2.0 and digital marketing technologies on their existing and future business, as well as helping them identify, create and execute ecommerce strategies and organizational change, particularly in a multichannel and multi-cultural environment.

Marco ran the global eCommerce channel within the group, including the establishment of a global P&L, central ecommerce team and the group wide "e" change management strategy to support this. He grew the online channel from 13% to 35% (c£300m) of group revenues, which remain on target to rise to 70% within 18 months. He established aggressive ecommerce strategies for APAC, the Supplier Community and created step change capability around web2.0 and digital marketing best practices, globally. For the last 3 years Marco was on the Executive leadership team and operational board.
Marco continues to mentor Senior Executives on eCommerce strategy
