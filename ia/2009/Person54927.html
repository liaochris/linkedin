Dan Riehl’s Summary

Technical services and Management -
Small Business formation and Management -
Project Management

My goal is to do interesting and challenging work, whether in management, or in providing technical security related services for the IBM i (AS/400.)

I really enjoy teaching the technical classes on AS/400 security. I especially enjoy the jaw-dropping reactions of IT auditors when they learn about the common exposures found on AS/400s.

I enjoy the 'treasure hunt' for AS/400 exposures during my AS/400 security audit. And then helping these great companies to improve the security and resilience of their IBM System i (AS/400).

  • Head of US Operations

    Cilasoft Security Solutions

    (Computer Software industry)

    August 2009 — Present (2 months)

    CIlasoft provides software to help IBM System i customers in the areas of security, audit and compliance. Cilasoft has customers in over 40 countries including small companies and large multinational corporations.

    The CIlasoft QJRN/400 software is a full feature database change auditing tool that allows for very granular reporting and alerts, in real time or in regularly scheduled reports. QJRN/400 also provides reporting and real time alerts when security related events occur.

    The other major CilaSoft offering, named CONTROLER is unique in the way it controls and monitors STRSQL, FTP, ODBC, DRDA, and CL command usage.

    Cilasoft has introduced a completely new paradigm in their CONTROLER and QJRN/400 products that make auditing and controlling these interfaces highly customizable, yet simple enough so you do not have to be a System i technical expert to configure the monitoring and protection required in today's heavily regulated environments.
