Nick Kelly’s Summary

To provide a brief insight into my background I will start by admitting the fact that up until I commenced this career I had no real sense of direction regarding a career/academic/life path. Over the years I have developed the belief that a successful person is one who has amalgamated all aspects of their personality and the life surrounding them into a workable "solution".
My objective is to bring about a level of transparency in an industry that is sometimes fraught with misconception and false pretense. I have the ability to comprehend not only a professional's ideal place within the culture of a business but have the intuitive nature and resourceful capacity to apply this as the basis of a workable, recruiting solution.
I focus on identifying energetic, passionate and driven executives who bring a clear business acumen to the fore. I enjoy dealing with the most senior of professionals through to entry level engineers as I believe there are a few unifying traits that exceptional people possess - Vision, Self drive, Passion & Innovation to name a few. I find myself constantly learning and growing through the people who I deal with and am inspired by the technologies that I am exposed to yet will probably never fully comprehend: everyone has their strengths.
I don't think I could appreciate the spectrum of requirements that I come into contact with unless I had the ability to wholly understand and empathise with each individual. This belief in myself as a unified, confident individual is more than evident in the way I conduct business and generally live my life.

Nick Kelly’s Specialties:

I enjoy listening to people. I enjoy dealing with people who are brilliant in their chosen fields. I enjoy talking to people who have accomplished feats that are massive in scope and vision.
My understanding of the motivation fueling these achievements and my subsequent ability to translate these into avenues of success is my speciality.
