Brynne Tillman, President Linked User Group’s Summary

Guess Free Selling Sales Training & Coaching

Let’s face it — the sales techniques of the past just don’t work well when pitted against today’s professional buyers.

Most customers have unraveled the standard pitches and insulated themselves from the power of persistence. The “dance of enthusiasm” doesn’t do it, either. Regaining control is the solution to handle today’s highly conditioned or professionally trained purchasers.

Sales Evolution’s methodology, Guess Free Selling, is a systematic approach focusing on sales skills and the most effective processes to improve business development efforts. While most sales training concentrates on technique or motivation, Guess Free Selling balances methodology with behavioral assumptions to create a powerful combination buyers cannot resist.

This program is for business owners, company presidents, sales managers & salespeople who are frustrated, exasperated, annoyed & concerned with low closing percentages, giving away free consulting, quotes that don’t close and not meeting profit goals.

Brynne Tillman is a Sales Consultant & Coach for Sales Evolution and the Founder and President of Contact Point Networking Group. Brynne is a natural networker who's passion is connecting businesses to help grow local commerce. Her primary focus is on social networking, business to business networking, committee obligations with local organizations including Business Alliances and Chambers of Commerce. Brynne also promotes small businesses and entrepreneurs through assisting them in low cost marketing opportunities and helping them maximize their personal network.

Brynne, as Sales Trainer and Coach, offers classes on LinkedIn and other social media as well as help professional's build there prospecting and sales plan. She also offer unlimited coaching to Sales Evolution Clients that is geared to help them close business.
