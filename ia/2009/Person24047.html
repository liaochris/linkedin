Barbara Maldonado’s Summary

I am a firm believer that online promotions as a stand-alone tactic or in conjunction with other marketing strategies such as experiential marketing, provide significant value to brands.

By utilizing online promotions, consumers are encouraged to engage with the brand, provide data, and participate in surveys. I have found that interactive promotions whether online or SMS are great ways to drive purchase and measurement immediately and in ways that traditional marketing alone cannot accomplish.

In addition to the work I do for Marketing Resources, I also work with small businesses in the Chicago and Milwaukee area and helping them structure and implement their social media brand voice and strategy utilizing Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter. I can be found on Twitter as @bmaldonado @MRI_PromosMktg and @OUTUGO
