Jesse Silver’s Summary

I've worked in a lot of capacities. I started as a traditional matte painter -- painting live-on-location glass shots. About 15 years ago I started painting in Photoshop, transitioning from traditional to digital matte painting.

As a traditional and digital matte painter I also designed other aspects of the shot from concept through final delivery--that's old school--my model being Albert Whitlock who didn't just deliver a painting, he delivered the goods!

To do that I learned and had considerable experience supervising location and stage plate photography, several times operating camera myself. I've also worked as a VFX supervisor, VFX art director, VFX illustrator, conceptualist, maker of strong coffee, Art Department head, dog walker, Photoshop instructor, Animation Background artist, title artist, layout artist, Background Supervisor, 3D CG production manager/art director/supervisor, visual development artist, reference photographer, and whatever the heck else was needed.

The point in all of this is to make the show better by being of service, having fun and learning, and sharing new discoveries along the way.

Jesse Silver’s Specialties:

First and foremost, I've been gifted with a truly exceptional eye and sense for color. Better than, perhaps, all but a few. I can't take much credit for it as I was born with the gift. I was so off-the-charts that I was subjected to special testing when I was a kid. That gift impelled me into the work I now do.
