Simon O'Day’s Summary

Eservices has been my main focus since 1999...other than my wife Claudia, child Ethan and of course football (soccer).

At its heart I have recognised that technology based companies have to lead with superior services and client management - and this defines success. Especially in a marketplace where the technology's features and tools can become discounted so quickly.

The period is about the continuation of this cultural approach to all parts of Eservices and to work as hard as possible to maintain the difference we see in how to provide services.

Simon O'Day’s Specialties:

- Email Service Provider Industry (locally and US), Email platforms, processes, reporting, integration and best practices, Lecture and write about the use of online Direct Marketing processes and solutions, Corporate and agency consulting on the use of online channel for DM and customer communication, Mentoring of staff, clients and other individuals in management and professional technique
