David Levy’s Summary

An American based in greater China for over 20 years, expert in turn-around, restructuring and expansion of manufacturing facilities in China.

A high-profile, China-based, change leader with a drill-down focus on cost, quality and service improvement.

A turnaround specialist utilizing time-tested strategies in the most pragmatic ways to achieve rapid, sweeking and vital improvement of manufacturing facilities in China.

Adept at identifying, attracting and developing the local Chinese talent required to implement and follow-through with lean, JIT and other pragmatic, performance-improving initiatives.

David Levy’s Specialties:

Turn-around leadership,restructuring, business process improvement, manufacturing process improvement, management by objective (MBO), continuous improvement, value-stream mapping, demand flow, kanban.
