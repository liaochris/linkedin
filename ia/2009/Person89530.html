Greg Huntoon’s Summary

An anthropologist and writer by education, Greg converted a lifelong passion and enthusiasm for architecture and technology into a successful career as a designer and art director. Greg is currently employed as the Creative Director of Musicane. He is particularly focused on user experience, branding, social media, site architecture, navigation, and grid-based designs. Incredibly interested in the cultural and social aspects of media, Greg is always watching how new technologies are shaping our world and its future.

Greg Huntoon’s Specialties:

Expert Photoshop, Illustrator and Flash skills with long hours and experience with AS 2.0, HTML, and CSS. Strong knowledge of type, color and grid, with a penchant for mixing a clean and functional grid with a dirty or roughed-up aesthetic. Well-versed in both platforms, with a (current, and recent) leaning towards working on a Mac.
