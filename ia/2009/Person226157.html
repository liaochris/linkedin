Sudha Jamthe’s Summary

High Energy, Passionate, Internet Marketing Executive with 14 yrs

My experience lies in consumer web marketing for cool technology products, focusing on marketing strategy, brand building and business operations.

I am passionate about the evolution of social media marketing. I organize the Bay Area Facebook Developers Meetups, which I helped grow from 20 to 1000 people using grassroots marketing within 1 year, all thanks to an awesome team of volunteers and the fun social platform ecosystem around facebook and opensocial.

I led Social Media Strategy for DEMO and helped make demofall08 successful with an online community using Facebook Fan Pages working with a team of fun people across geographies.

I always blog, but this Sep, demo gave me focus to do live videos and I did live streaming with Qik and vidoes using my flip camera and Canon450 to go live on various video sites, blogs and on brightcove along with twitter, facebook and friendfeed. From there, I have a new hobby, what I call as "Live social conference tracking” with blogs, videos, tweets, feeds of events I attend. See Gigaom's Mobilize 08 at http://tinyurl.com/mobilize08 and Social media summit at http://tinyurl.com/charleneli

My most recent success is from Dash Navigation in infusing contagious passion for a tech consumer brand with integrated marketing programs where I worked on some experimental social media marketing with meetups combined with PR in 6 local markets in US.

Its a pleasure to market something that customers adore so much with a team that wants market feedback, all I had to do was wear my marketing hat and connect with customers and build metrics and processes in our blogs, forums and email, web campaigns.

Sudha Jamthe’s Specialties:

I can infuse enthusiasm for brands and team-buyin across the organization and thrive in matrix or cross-functional organizations, and build partnerships in the industry. Marketing anything that involved cool technology and lot of people works for me!
