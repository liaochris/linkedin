Sharon Paxson’s Summary

Prior to my career as a Realtor, I worked in the healthcare industry in long term care. I started and operated a contract therapy business which operated in 7 states for 18 years. Following, I worked for several years for a large healthcare company selling both contract rehab services and institutional pharmacy services. I have had tremendous experience in sales, and customer service. As a Realtor, I strive to be a resource for my clients, and understand the importance of customer service and follow through.

I specialize in the city of Newport Beach, Corona del Mar, Costa Mesa, Huntington Beach, and Laguna Beach. I represent both sellers and buyers with their real estate transaction.

An area that I specialize in is technology to assist my clients in obtaining the most exposure for their property, and help buyers to find their new home.

For more information about me and the community of Newport Beach, please visit my website at www.SharonintheOC.com.

Sharon Paxson’s Specialties:
