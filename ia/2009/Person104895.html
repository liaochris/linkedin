Jason Baker’s Summary

Jason has over fourteen years of experience working with Internet and telecommunications organizations in Minnesota. He is Chief Technology Officer and Co-Owner of VISI, a large midwest regional provider of datacenter and managed hosting services. Prior to VISI, he co-founded Digital North and was the technology leader within the organization. In his capacity as Director of Operations, Jason helped Digital North expand into multiple datacenters serving thousands of customers.
