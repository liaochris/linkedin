Philipp Kemper’s Summary

Philipp is a high-tech professional with 8 years experience in direct-to-consumer businesses in Europe, Asia and North America. He's been mostly focused on digital and retail distribution of casual games. Philipp holds a degree in computer science, from Universities Furtwangen and Berlin, Germany and is experienced in executive account management, product management and -development, business process reengineering and business consulting.

Philipp has contributed to the success of corporate- and start-up environments and has managed and led small- to medium sized teams. What makes him tick are entrepreneurial challenges like jump-starting new businesses from scratch with a focus on true scalability and customer satisfaction. Philipp has contributed to build and sustain competitive advantage through excellent products, services, operational efficiency and profitability.

Philipp is trilingual - a German native speaker with fluent English- and French language skills.
