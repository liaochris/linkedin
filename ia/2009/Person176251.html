Ed Bennett’s Summary

I am a very experienced search engine marketer. For the last 6 years I have played a key role in building a successful marketing company; Edit Optimisation. In which time I have worked for many clients from sole traders to huge multi-national companies such as eBay and Universal Studios.

At Edit Optimisation I have been exposed to all sides of this fast changing market place, usually acting as a coordinator between the technical and the business sides of companies, in order to make sure that marketing campaigns are carried out in their entirety and then measuring and appraising correctly.

As well as having experience in search engine optimisation and pay for click, I have an extensive skill base in complementary technical skills such as Dreamweaver, Adobe image manipulation packages, e-mail marketing platforms and am proficient at coding in raw HTML.
