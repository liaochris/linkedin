Jaime E. Soto, PMP’s Summary

Andes Consulting LLC is a California company specializing in project management, quality management, software development, and business analysis for the government, energy, and public safety sectors.

We strive to improve our clients’ ability to meet the challenges of a changing world and develop sound project management and software development methodologies.
