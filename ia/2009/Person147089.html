    MAKE ME MY JERSEY (“3MJ”) is a premier online apparel store that speaks the language of sports to fans across the country. More than a sports outlet, 3MJ represents the spirit and the culture of college sports strewed across generations of athletes, teams and their fans.
    3MJ goes beyond the conventional outline: it redefines fan-ship through nonconformist style and pizzazz, distinguished by its idiosyncratic creativity, high quality standards and superior craftsmanship.
    Through 3MJ, customers are able to design and build their custom jerseys that reflect a sense of pride, aspiration, vigor and zeal towards their favorite stars, teams or alma mater 3MJ strives for incomparable authenticity towards customization.With a constantly expanding portfolio,

    3MJ currently offers:
    Jerseys (including NCAA)
    Custom Jerseys, T-shirts, Polo Shirts
    Sweatshirts
    Hats and other head gear
    Wristbands
    Bandanas

    Makememyjersey (“3MJ”) announces its official launch for July 28th, 2009. In the next couple of weeks, 3MJ will be celebrating its pre-launch on facebook by giving away exclusive designer samples to reinforce the paradigm shift in college fashion. 3MJ is proud to represent 12 schools for the upcoming football season, and is continuing engaging institutions across the nation for the seasons to come.
