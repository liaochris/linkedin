Vincenzo Russo’s Summary

Vincenzo Russo works at the Risk Management Department of EurizonVita S.p.A., the Insurance Company of Intesa Sanpaolo Group.
His main task consists in the market risk estimates and derivatives pricing.
He obtained degree in Statistics and Economics (2003) at the University of Palermo and then (2006) attended the Master in Finance at Coripe (University of Turin – Compagnia di San Paolo).
Recently he collaborated with the University of Palermo in some activities relating to the quantitative finance and is the author of the article "Autoregressive conditional moments in VaR estimate with Gram-Charlier and Cornish-Fisher expansions" published in the International Journal of Risk Assessment and Management (2008).
