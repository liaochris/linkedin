Norm Hare’s Summary

Norm has been employed in the computer support field since beginning his career in 1977. He prides himself on being able to troubleshoot and repair most any system or peripheral and has had extensive training on everything from HP Enterprise systems to Dell desktop computers including optical jukeboxes, high speed printers and SAN storage devices.

In the last several years, Norm has become proficient at administering a host of Windows products including Server, Exchange and SQL Server. He has also installed, configured and administered a Blackberry Enterprise Server environment.

In addition to his computer skills, he is tasked with the administration of the Comdial telephone system at ECC.
