Kim Renberg’s Summary

I got 10+ years of Italian business and management experience working mainly with Project Management, Sales and Promotion Management, International Market Research, Marketing and Strategy Development, Web Marketing and International Partner Management.

I am Danish and have an interdisciplinary university degree in Administrative Sciences. I have been living and working in Italy from 1992 and have always been working in multicultural environments.

I am able to work in English, Italian and Danish, and got a very good understanding of German, Swedish and Norwegian.

From June 2000 I have been working in interact: a Rome based company that has been operating successfully in the Italian content management, digital media, streaming and video communication market for 10+ years, both for web based communication, IPTV and mobile environments.

Our core business is focused on digital content management and publishing of content and in this space we operate as an application developer, service, consultant, system integrator and SW/HW reseller company.

Some key words expressing our operational areas are: Content Management Systems, Usability and Accessibility , Web Sites/Media Portals, Web TV, IPTV, Mobile TV, Video Recording, Video Digitalization. Video Restore, Value Added Services, Digital Media Rights Management.

The main values expressed by interact in the market are based on the understanding of our clients communication needs, business models and preservation of value over time. These values permits us to create fruitful dialogues and partnerships with a wide range of people with different communication visions from marketing and PR people, civil servants in the public administration as well as people with technological responsibilities.

interact is a UNI EN ISO 9001: 2000 (vision 2000) certified company.
