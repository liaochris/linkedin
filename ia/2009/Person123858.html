Julie King’s Summary

Highly regarded workshop leader, public presenter, and private consultant to parents and caregivers of young children ages two to twelve. Most popular workshop is based on the bestseller HOW TO TALK SO KIDS WILL LISTEN by Faber and Mazlish. Currently working on a sequel, tentatively titled HOW TO TALK SO PRESCHOOLERS WILL LISTEN. Supporting parents to raise respectful, confident, empathic children and to survive the daily challenges of family life.

Julie King’s Experience

  • Parent Educator

    Julie King, Parent Educator

    (Individual & Family Services industry)

    March 1995 — Present (14 years 9 months)

    I teach concrete skills and offer practical solutions to real life, every day parenting challenges for parents of children ages two to twelve. Through experiential exercises and examples, parents acquire specific tools to address such common challenges as whining, morning rush madness, bedtime struggles, sibling rivalry, and more! Parents can choose from four-week workshops, one-day intensive workshops, monthly "refresher" workshops, and in-person or telephone consultations.
