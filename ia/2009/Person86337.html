Gianluca Pollesel’s Summary

General manager and owner.
A business center is a place where companies can set up their business in a snap. In fact we provide for all the logistic services within a common location. The facility is commonly compared to a office residence.
We provide for furnished offices, telephone lines, internet high speed connections, secretarial support and personalized calls answering, color laser net printer and copier, cleanngs and general expenses and many more services to help companies in office work. It's right in order to avoid our clients to think to those problem, letting them to focus on their real core business.
