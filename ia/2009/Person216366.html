Stefan Henningsson’s Summary

Works to co-ordinate climate work at the WWF Sweden office to support a global deal between countries and companies to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by at least 80% until 2050.

Stefan Henningsson’s Specialties:

Knowledge of environmental management in business, governmental regulations/support concerning sustainability, ecodesign, environmentally sound product development, innovation systems, general sustainability and climate change.
