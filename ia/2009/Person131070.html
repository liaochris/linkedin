Kevin Knecht’s Summary

We have a ton of time together as a family since we work from our home here in beautiful St. Johns, Florida. We're newbies here in NE Florida by the beach (St. Johns). We lived in gorgeous Scottsdale, Arizona the last three years, but felt it was time for a move. That's the beauty of having an internet business. Portability!! (Plus ... no boss!)
