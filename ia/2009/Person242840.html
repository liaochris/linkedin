Victor M. Claudio’s Summary

i-Marco Polo offers global buyers a cost reducing Procurement Outsourcing Solution, delivering a Centralized approach in managing and protecting your asset every step of the supply chain, from end-to-end. Creating a controlled and verifiable chain-of-custody as well as a common hub for communication exchange effectively and efficiently connecting the international trade community to their China Value Chain.

Victor M. Claudio’s Specialties:

The i-Marco Polo's Procurement Outsourcing Solution provides entrepreneurs, small and medium size enterprises, as well as large company’s with a comprehensive turnkey global trade solution that includes supplier discovery, tactical sourcing, sample and prototype management, order settlement, import and export compliance, and online shipment tracking.
