Paul Taylor’s Summary

Currently leading VocaLink's European business. Directly responsible for driving VocaLink's expansion into SEPA and Europe over the last five years.

VocaLink is one of the world's leading providers of providers and transaction banking services. VocaLink delivers clearing, Euro and SEPA services, card and ATM services, faster payments, channel and network services.

Played a key role in developing VocaLink's SEPA, PSD and Euro propositions. Led activities to secure VocaLink's network of user, client and reach banks from across Europe, which now includes BAWAG PSK, Dexia, Fortis, ABN AMRO, SECB and West LB. Played a leading role in the BGC transaction.

As Managing Director for Europe I have played a leading role in building VocaLink's European business. In little over five years, we have built a multi-million pound business from scratch which is confidently expected to quadruple by 2012. This has been achieved by spending a lot of time in the market building a broad network of senior contacts from the thousands of financial institutions in the UK and Europe, interpreting their requirements and crafting solutions which respond to their needs and their business cases. I have also recruited and built a senior European team within VocaLink from nothing and we now have employees based across Europe and customers from Sweden, Belgium, Germany, Austria, Switzerland, the Netherlands, France and Italy.

I have built up a network of senior contacts in banks in the UK and Europe who are specifically related to the payments, strategy and operations areas within the banks.

Prior to VocaLink, I worked as a strategy consultant at Deloitte in the financial services practice, serving clients such as Barclays and ABN AMRO.
