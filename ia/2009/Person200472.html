Robert Hammar’s Summary

Robert Hammar is an International award winning Wedding Photographer by Wedding Photojournalist Association, www.wpja.com.

Thank you for your interest in my work! I hope you enjoy viewing the images on my website.

I'm originally from Sweden I live with my lovely family in a Finnish city called Vantaa near Helsinki and the Helsinki-Vantaa airport. I specialize in portraits and in photoreportage of weddings, fashion and all kind of events.

My style is based on of years of experience and it's influenced by working with other great photographers. Special thanks to Brigitte Grenfeldt, Paul Gero, Bruce Smith and Ibarionex Perello for sharing their vast knowledge and experience. I encourage you to visit the websites of these great photographers; you will find links to all mentioned photographers on my website.

In addition to my work as a photographer, I'm also a founder for a Swedish community of photographers. If you are interested in the art of photography you're more than welcome to join the community Foto iFokus.se. I'm also a the founder and host for a Swedish marketplace for photographers, http://www.fotografer.ifokus.se, please join or visit us if you are interested of finding great Swedish photographers.

All my best!


Robert Hammar

Robert Hammar’s Specialties:

My special interest of photography is to take photos of people in weddings, fashion, lifestyle portraits, cherubs, glamour and boudoir photography.

My camerabag is loaded with 2 Canon bodies, lenses, flashes and a battery packed studio light set from Elinchrom.
