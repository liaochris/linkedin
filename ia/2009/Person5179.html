Aidan Martin’s Summary

While I never intended to head down the broadcast path, I'm certainly glad I did. Writing has been a passion for me for many years, and I always imagined I'd end up in print. Thanks to a new professor my senior year in college though, all of that changed.
Since graduation I've spent countless hours learning the ways of television production. From live news to pre and post game shows, from in-depth packages to half-hour specials, and from TV to the web, my time at FSN was full of variety and challenge.
Now I hope to take that experience and combine it with my concept creation/writing abilities in order to launch a successful freelance career. I have always been one to embrace change and welcome challenges, so why stop now?
