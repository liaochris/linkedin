Tracy Harden’s Summary

Comprehensive marketing strategist with 20 plus years experience implementing a full gamet of advertising and communications programs.

Advertising, marketing, communications strategy and media sales arena – offer clients broad knowledge with a focus on targeting budgets to areas of the best ROI while understanding the value of a both print, broadcast media and new media.

• Communications professional with expertise in crisis communication, media training, corporate communication, media relations, and publicity. Very adept at developing media angles and identifying press opportunities. Experience in interfacing with government entities including the US Forest Service and Summit County, Utah and Park City Municipal.

• Excels at producing websites by identifying objectives and audiences, designing site architecture, developing the content and graphics and interfacing with programmers, graphic designers, and search engine optimizers. When the website is on-line – build CRM through e-marketing, promotion and database segmentation.

• Media buying and planning expertise with a background in media sales, publishing and negotiating media purchases.

• Art direction for professional photography shoots including lifestyle, architectural, and landscape photography. Scope includes hiring photographers, models, stylist, location scouting and directing the shoot.

• Collateral development including award-winning brochures, newsletters and direct mail pieces. Art direction, copywriting and press oversight.

• Educator and presenter within various associations and religious studies classes. Enjoys public speaking.
