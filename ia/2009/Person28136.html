Krzysztof Bieganowski’s Summary

"Experience is one thing you can't get for nothing.” - Oscar Wilde.

In search of "the question", my life has taken me many places. I enjoy travelling to explore and experience other cultures. This has also developed into a love for cooking and of course eating. Being interested and open-minded, I believe is important in meeting people. Working in a team or independently. I'm equally comfortable in a position of leadership as a contributing team member. Having a jounger brother, has also taught me a fair bit of diplomacy and responsibility skills.

I have a strong sense of justice and ethics, which translates into a desire to work with people in vulnerable situations. Many will describe me as a playful and secure person. My outlets for playfulness are mostly physical activity - such as swimming, Nordic Walking, and weight training. Being confident with computers, also gives me an arena for play and creativity.

Professionally, I hope to discover many "answers", but even more "questions". I am a quick study and like to collect knowledge to perform my profession accurately and with quality. I am therefore the type who will ask questions and thrive on instruction. I want to work abroad, and being multilingual. Polish, Spanish, French, German and English, gives me the freedom to do this.

Is the glass half full or half empty? To me, it is half full.
