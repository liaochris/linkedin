    The Public Interest Network is the family of organizations that has grown out of the public interest organization known to many simply as "PIRG" (the Public Interest Research Group). The Network now includes more than a dozen groups and independent projects, each with its own mission: The Student PIRGs focus on organizing students; U.S. PIRG, on research and action in the public interest; Environment America, on organizing and advocacy for the environment; The Fund, on citizen outreach, both for our network and such partner groups as Sierra Club and Human Rights Campaign; Green Corps, on training young environmental organizers and advocates; Progressive Future, on building grassroots support for a progressive agenda; Frontier Group, on research on social problems and policy solutions; and so on.

    Green Century Capital Management

    (Privately Held; Environmental Services industry)

    January 1991 — Present (18 years 10 months)

    Green Century Capital Management, Inc. (GCCM) is the administrator of Green Century Funds, a family of environmentally responsible mutual funds. It was founded by non-profit environmental advocacy organizations and is wholly owned by non-profit advocacy groups.

    The Green Century Funds remain among a small number of fund families that focus primarily on environmentally responsible investing.

    As a result of GCCM's non-profit ownership, 100% of the net profits earned by GCCM on the fees it receives for managing the Funds belong to the non-profit advocacy organization founders. These earnings will help to further efforts to preserve and protect the environment - efforts that include: campaigning for the protection of clean air, clean water, and open space; filing lawsuits against companies that illegally pollute; and advocating for reduced use of toxic chemicals and reduced emissions of global warming gases.
