Lucien Engelen’s Summary

My focus is : Stimulating the use of the capabilities, knowlegde and network to create better Acute Health Care for day-to-day basis aswell as in disaster situations. The use of Healthchain information-management is an important enabler within this proces.

Currently we are also implementing an online community for the Acute Healthcare professionals. www.acutezorg.nl

Lucien Engelen’s Specialties:

Health 2.0, E-health, Web 2.0, Building People-networks within the Acute Care resulting in better understanding and thus better Care. Knowledge of Disasterhandling proceses, EMS-knowledge, Traumacare, HEMS, LifeLiner, General Practitioner-proceses (out-of-office-hours). Only together we can cope the challenge to help the patiënt to change the care into an patiënt-driven-chain.
