Angie Jones’s Summary

My specialty is character and creature animation. I enjoy the challenge of moving back and forth amongst the "sweet and endearing" and the "evil and deadly." I love the challenges of bringing characters to life in all formats - games, film and television.

CONSULTING: Everyone needs a little help with the challenges that production brings and the tools artists use. I have consulted on pipeline structures for feature films with Disney; studio structures and team building at the commercial boutique Motion Theory and game studio Angel Studios; advised on character pipelines at Zoic Studios for episodic TV and commercials; and guided the tech on artist friendly character tools for Houdini v9 software at Side Effects.

I offer specialized guidance regarding:
-- leadership, mentoring and building of animation teams
-- pipeline consulting, internal file systems and structures, and cg tool design
-- studio development from infancy to adolescence and growth into a mature production
-- creation and refinement of artist friendly tools
-- organization of shows - film, game, television and commercial
-- creative development, pitches, and original content

I have worked on 13 features, 38 commercials, 1 awards opening, 2 music videos, 1 ride film, and 32 AAA video games. Highlight credits include:
