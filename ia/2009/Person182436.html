Paula Bean’s Summary

30-year veteran Realtor with vastly honed skills in consulting, negotiating, marketing and training.

My expertise lies in making the impossible happen.I love to be challenged! The fastest way to get me to do something is to tell me
"it can't be done "

In addition to selling real estate, I consult with agents all over the World via phone and or webinars to help them further their careers while learning how to have fun making money.

I have a high degree of ethics and customer satisfaction, which probably came from my first job when I was 15 working at Walt Disney World. Attending WDW University and learning how to do whatever it takes to make happiness and satisfaction #1, because people spend thousands of dollars for their vacation of a lifetime set an example for me.
