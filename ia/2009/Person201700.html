Rob Paladino’s Summary

Seasoned Sales, Marketing and General Management Executive having grown rapidly a consumer goods company specializing in Financial and Telecom services to newly immigrated Hispanics. This follows a term as CEO of INZONE Brands, Inc., makers of the popular children’s drink, Bellywashers and a host of consumer housewares. Prior to that CEO and Founder of Distribution Partners Limited, a Southeastern US construction supply business, specializing in lighting -- founded in December of 1997 and sold in 2003.

Prior to that, eighteen years of progressive consumer products business development and marketing experience. The majority of this time was spent with PepsiCo's Pepsi Cola unit, as an executive in a number of steadily increasing sales, marketing and general management capacities throughout the US -- notably the large Southern California business unit -- and Coors Brewing Company in Strategic Sales Development and Category Management.
