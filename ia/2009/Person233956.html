Timothy Kennedy’s Summary

I am an attorney and business consultant practicing law in San Francisco and Silicon Valley. My practice is focused on business counseling and business law.

I spent years working first as a Financial Analyst and then as a Business Analyst for companies in Silicon Valley in both the financial and hardware-engineering industries. I have worked in established companies with a formal corporate structure, and I have worked in the informal settings of a start-up.
