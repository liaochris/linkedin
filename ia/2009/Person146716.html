Mike Jacobson’s Summary

In my 2.5 years of creative and strategic planning experience, I’ve worked on everything from video games (and mobile games) to burritos, cars to coffee, the academy awards to beer and more. I’ve always been fascinated by our constantly evolving culture, and how people act and react- which is why I love what I do. I’m also an award-winning magician & member of the Magic Castle.

I co-designed the international Xbox Live Pipeline; the first online interactive database of Xbox Live features and downloads- which paved a path for Xbox Live’s newly acclaimed dashboard. I not only helped develop the viral strategy behind Coffee Bean’s Mascot Roommate (featured on CNN & USA Today), but was also the tiger who tackled the mascot during an episode. I’ve ran focus groups for EA Sports, co-planned strategic direction with our CSO, and worked as a new business assistant, developing killer insights on industries and consumer culture. I can't stop/sleep until compelling, useful work is created.

And I’ve done all of this as just an intern.

So why me?

Because apart from my experience, I'm insanely passionate, entrepreneurial, and have a great attitude (ask around!). I think these are three things that are not only important, but that can’t be learned. This is what sets me apart, and what would make me an awesome asset to any team.

I don’t consider myself an “account planner.” I think that traditional role is outdated. I specialize in everything strategy. Account planning, communication planning, new business planning, writing, etc..I love uncovering insights into our world.

I pride myself in creativity, strategy, insight, and foresight. I’m about creating work that’s relevant in culture- not just the category. Every single employer I’ve ever had will attest to my abilities and potential. And I can do magic.

Feel free to call me at 310.500.9945 or email me at mrmikejacobson@gmail.com. Thanks!
