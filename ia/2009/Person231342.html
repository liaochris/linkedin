matthew bigg’s Summary

The collective is a small but perfectly formed design agency based in St Albans, Hertfordshire. We apply our creativity to a wide range of projects and clients. Testament to our practical approach, 'can do' attitude, competitive pricing and general good humour is the fact that we are still working with the same clients since we started trading over eight years ago. Our clients are directly responsible for generating 90% of our new business leads and are highly complimentary about working with us. As far as we're concerned that's what it's all about.
