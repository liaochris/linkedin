Michael Bacon’s Summary

Michael is one of the UK’s leading information security professionals.

For the past 20 years he has worked in senior positions developing and enabling professional risk management practices both as a consultant and a CISO.

Over 30 years experience of information security and risk management, in over 50 countries.
Unrivalled experience in developing and implementing infosec strategies, policies, tactics and technologies in various national and business cultures.
Over 25 years experience of staff management with sustained track record in team development and leadership.
Proven programme/project direction skills; successfully managed multi-million ($100m+) budgets and P&L ($80m+) for over 20 years.
Strategic thinker and planner; innovative, with keen commercial awareness.
Wide-ranging experience of ISO2700x , PCI DSS, SOX and other compliance regimes.
