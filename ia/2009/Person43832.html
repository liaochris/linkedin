Chris O'Neill’s Summary

Chris O’Neill is the Business Development Manager with Antarctica Digital, an online marketing company based in Vancouver, Canada. He works with agencies and direct marketers to help them engage with consumers and businesses online. His in-depth knowledge and experience with online engagement technologies and strategy allow him to provide comprehensive interactive marketing solutions for B2C and B2B campaigns.

Antarctica Digital makes marketing easier. Our award-winning demand generation solutions provide email marketing, lead nurturing, lead scoring, and closed-loop reporting capabilities to help marketing and sales teams work together to generate and qualify sales leads, shorten sales cycles, and demonstrate marketing accountability. Built for marketers by marketing veterans from Epiphany, Siebel, and Intuit, Cultivate is easy to use and boasts an on-demand model that gets customers up and running in just one afternoon.
