Scott Moore’s Summary

I am the CEO responsible for the overall operational stewardship and business development strategy of an extremely successful full service online marketing and advertising agency. Our company has grown successfully over the last eleven years. 2008 was a record year for our company and 2009 is already off to a 25% stronger start than 2008. My team continues to successfully lead the advancement of technology solutions to meet the rapidly changing needs of online consumers and the Internet marketplace.

Prior to working in online advertising I led the global technology architecture, integration standardization strategies as a Senior Director for a Fortune 50 Telecommunications company in North America, Europe and Asia. My work there included participation in the leadership team which developed the Internet Solutions group. I managed the project team for Sprint's COO which analyzed and recommended integration strategies for the three major IT divisions into a single operating unit.
