Ted Cohn’s Summary

Ted has over 25 years experience as a technologist, senior executive, engineer and entrepreneur. He has a passion for developing cutting edge products and services, and building collaborative teams. He has won awards and has contributed to two patents and has filed many trademarks. He has taught graphics and computer courses and written technical articles. His experience spans Internet, graphics, database, systems architecture, gaming, networking, and application development.

Ted developed the first Apple II 6502 debugger (BugByter) in 1981. He has worked for a number of high profile Silicon Valley companies, including Apple, Radius, NeXT, Catapult Entertainment, WebCrawler/Excite, TenSquare, and Bridgespan. He developed one of the first image search engines called ImageCrawler and survey web apps called SurveySez.

Ted cofounded Ugobe in 2004, which brought Pleo, an advanced animatronic dinosaur, to market in 2007. Ted developed Spautica in 2006, an innovative spa product to relieve stress and pain serving the trillion dollar health and wellness market. He presently serves on the advisory boards of FohBoh, Dreamslice, Embarka Digital Media. He has a Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science from the University of California at Berkeley.
