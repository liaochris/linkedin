Fiona O'Brien’s Summary

I imaginate whenever I can. I'm addicted to ideas, I just can't help myself, hence 'Fi the Imaginator'. My background in Advertising (don't hold that against me ha!) has a lot to answer for, it created this brand loving, design focused, creating machine you see before you, well I can't blame it all on the Advertising industry, I do remember, aged 5, creating my own ads about my favorite bubble bath! Now my design addiction has lead me to the greener fields of yonder, working as a super duo with my husband, he makes pretty pictures, I do something useful with them, that was a joke Ben.
