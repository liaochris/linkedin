Eric Meyer’s Experience

  • Attorney

    Dilworth Paxson LLP

    (Privately Held; Law Practice industry)

    September 2005 — Present (4 years 3 months)

    I dedicate my practice to litigating and providing counsel to employers on labor and employment issues affecting the workplace, including collective bargaining, discrimination, employee handbook policies, enforcement of restrictive covenants, H1N1 preparedness, and trade secret protection. I frequently train both management and their employees on workplace harassment issues. Additionally, I often serve as both a volunteer mediator for the United States Equal Employment Opportunity Commission and an arbitrator in the Philadelphia Court of Common Pleas.

    I am also on the cutting edge of social networking. My experience includes preparing social media policies for clients and I have been quoted in regard to such policies in Business Insurance, ABCNews.com and Mashable.com. I also recently presented at the Christie’s Great Estates 2009 Annual Global Conference on “Risk Management In The Age of Social Networking.” Additionally, I blog labor and employment law issues for The Legal Intelligencer, the oldest legal publication in the United States.

    Among my other accomplishments, I was recognized by the Legal Talk Network and Law.com in the field of electronic discovery. I often lecture on the topic of electronic discovery and records retention.


    Bar Admissions: PA, NJ, DC, E.D.PA, M.D.PA, D.N.J., D.D.C., 3rd Cir.
