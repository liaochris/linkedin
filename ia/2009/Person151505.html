Mark Cross’s Summary

Mark is an experienced telecommunications engineer and personal effectiveness coach based in Wellington, New Zealand. He is also a graduate with honours from the University of York, UK with a degree in Biochemistry.

Over ten years in the IT industry, Mark has successfully demonstrated skills in a variety of areas ranging from information management, network design, operational support and most recently managing the deployment of carrier grade VoIP networks. He presently holds industry accreditation for ITIL, Microsoft and Cisco systems and also has experience of Juniper and Alcatel-Lucent core devices.

Mark is also an accomplished personal coach practicing a range of techniques developed from principles of NLP. He is recognised as a master practitioner by the INLPA and IANLP and as a practitioner of Time Line Therapy by the TLTA. He is also a trained trainer who is developing a range of courses and literature for individuals who share his interest in the fields of coaching and NLP.

Mark Cross’s Specialties:

* Developing processes and documentation in line with ITIL v3 guidelines.
* Overseeing the deployment of ICT solutions built from Cisco, Juniper, Alcatel-Lucent, ACME, Sun and HP hardware.
* Management, workplace communication and teamwork.
* NLP based coaching, training and therapeutic consultation.
* Certified instructor the the Transforming Communication programme of problem ownership and conflict resolution.
* Graduate of the National Foundation for Teaching Entrepreneurship.
