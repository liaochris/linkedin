Catherine Warrilow’s Summary

My background initially was in the children's toy and nursery sector, and over the years I have gained experience in marketing and PR across a wide number of consumer industries.

Today I work with lifestyle and entertainment clients including toy brands, restaurants, bridal companies, dating agencies, and opticians.

I love writing. I am passionate about making words work and believe that a well written article can really capture the imagination of an audience and inspire people.

Creativity is key to PR and forms a really fundamental part of a successful campaign. Great words, great ideas, equals great publicity.

Backed by a small but highly creative team, we deliver all singing all dancing publicity campaigns that get people noticed and get results.
