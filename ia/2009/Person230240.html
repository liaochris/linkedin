Ted Southard’s Summary

Ted Southard is working on advancing the state of MMORPG arts with an online persistent world tentatively named "Epic Frontiers". He has over a decade of experience in the IT field, as well as experience in the construction, administrative and military fields. As an avid traveler in the real-world, Ted embarks on many travels which form the basis of some of his ideas for changing MMO features.

Ted Southard’s Specialties:

Server Administration, Windows Server 2003/2000/NT, Windows Vista/XP/NT/9x/3.x, Cisco switches, Riverbed WAN accelerators, Putty, Opticon, Look@LAN, NexPose, Xiotech SAN, IBM Blade Servers, Pertrac, Packhedge, MS Office, Visual Basic, C++/Visual C++/C, Torque Game Engine/Advanced, Torque Game Builder, Torque Constructor, Caligari trueSpace, AI, Procedural geometry/content generation, GUI, 2D/3D artwork
