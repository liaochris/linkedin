Allison (Alla) Pasique’s Summary

Allison worked as a consultant for corporations and agencies – both in Canada and abroad. Her extensive travel and exposure to different cultures has made her an effective facilitator.

She has the intuitive ability to understand her clients’ needs and match them with the strongest candidates.
Allison earned a BS in Law and an MS in Biochemistry.

She is currently working toward her Certified Human Resource Professional (CHRP) designation and is scheduled to write the National Knowledge Exam® (NKE) in 2010. Allison is an active member of Organization of Women in International Trade (OWIT), The Human Resources Professionals Association (HRPA), certified Role Based Assessment Consultant by the Gabriel Institute. She is volunteering at Dress “Dress Rehearsal” program for Success.

Allison speaks both English and Russian.
