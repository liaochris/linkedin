Robert Griffiths’s Summary

Management Training & Development Professional with 20 years of experience as a General Manager in the Corporate & Investment Banking business.

I am Managing Partner of Dunlop & Brown, a Director & Trainer at INFOVA, and as Associate Professor at ESCP-EAP Europe.

I work as a Trainer, Facilitator or Consultant with Senior Executives from large multinationals, helping them build their teams, refine their business planning, and in the design, organization & facilitation of their business-critical team meetings.

Fluent in English, Spanish and French.

Robert Griffiths’s Specialties:

General Management at senior level, Trainer / Coach in High Performance Teams, Change Management & Innovation and Leadership & Communication.
