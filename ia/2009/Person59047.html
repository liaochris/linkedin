David VanArsdale’s Summary

I am the founder of Resource, an Atlanta based product development and consultancy studio. Resource specializes in custom short run products including custom commercial furnishings and fixtures. We also happily lend a creative hand whenever needed.

I am also part-time faculty in the Industrial Design department at Georgia Institute of Technology, where I teach junior level design studio and a sophomore 'sketching and making' class.

When I am not designing or making, I also DJ around town to get my music kicks.

David VanArsdale’s Specialties:

I specialize in finding and making exactly what you wanted, in an object you would have never imagined.
