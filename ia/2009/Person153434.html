  • Producer

    In'tent Belevingscommunicatie

    (Events Services industry)

    September 2004 — August 2007 (3 years)

    Creating and producing corporate events for companies like Twynstra Gudde, Rabobank, Nationale Postcode Loterij, ENECO, Warchild and AmsterdamDiners (charity diner with a focus on the AIDS problem).   • Founder

    Brandexpedition

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2004 — July 2004 (7 months)

    In the spring of 2004 I travelled 17,000 kilometres through Europe with my German oldtimer ambulance. During this 3 month trip, I visited 12 special brands to find out what it takes to make a brand give people a special feeling. During the visits of these brands I had interviews with the marketing managers, and in one case with the president or founder. The brands I visited are; Harley Davidson Benelux, Ben & Jerry’s UK, EasyJet, Orange, Mini, Duvel, Chupa Chups, Swatch, Victorinox, LEGO, Bang & Olufsen and Kronan. Articles on the trip were published in High Profile magazine. After the trip I spoke to several television producers about making a television programme about the concept. Unfortunately, this didn’t work because of the costs of 1.6 million Euro to make the programme. Now, in 2009, I am doing a second attempt to do the project again!
