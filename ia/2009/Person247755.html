Will Sherwood’s Summary

Over the years, Mr. Sherwood has won more than 300 regional, national, and international design awards. (see http://www.thesherwoodgroup.com/2008_addy.html for current winnings) Plus, he has more than 30 years of experience successfully meeting and exceeding the marketing communication challenges presented by his corporate clients, small and large. His clients love the results they get from his creativity, and consider his firm's services a real bargain.

Will is also writing a book: "Success Secrets of the Graphic Design Superstars." His objective is to create a "need to have" book, rather than a "nice to have" book.

This book is coming about as a masters project in connection with his master's degree in spiritual psychology. Right now, his blog (http://www.willsherwood.com) is a collection of interviews from the best-of-the-best designers and creatives, but in the book he plans to add a categorized listing of their responses under such headings as, “What do I need to do to succeed?” and “How do I get big clients and/or big projects?”
