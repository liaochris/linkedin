Iain Campbell’s Summary

Who I am:
A Marketing Intelligence/Competitive Intelligence Consultant with over 15 years experience, I specialize in global B2B research and analysis. Foreign language capabilities and an extensive international network of contacts enable me to assist my clients by sourcing highly strategic information. I work across many sectors on projects for consulting firms, VC firms, and vendors. I am logical, analytical, entrepreneurial, adaptable, objective, a self-starter, a team player, and a relationship-builder. I bring a fresh and innovative approach to intelligence gathering and analysis, and strong project management skills.

What I do:
Work from a blank sheet of paper on projects where an external and unbiased perspective will often provide the greatest benefits.
Work in markets where little research currently exists.
Question and challenge given assumptions and data within a firm.
Identify new trends for products and services.
Interview senior corporate executives world-wide, internally and externally.
Identify market analysts/practicing consultants and interview them.
Provide valuable sales leads and potential new employee candidates.
Provide customer insights.
I use an extensive international network of contacts to great effect.
Identify and profile the real competition for a client.
