Dane DeValcourt’s Summary

The majority of my career experience has been in WAN/LAN routing and switching with an emphasis on Cisco products. For the last few years my fascination and concentration has been in the various VoIP and security technologies.

Even as a young child I was fascinated with the world of digital communications, from running an early BBS to navigating around ARPAnet / Internet. These things fed my thirst for knowledge, spending countless hours pouring over everything I could find and read via gopher and archie. I knew then as a kid that I had to know how all of this worked and how it was that I (young kid in Louisiana) could connect to places all over the country without ever leaving my room.

Now some years later I continue to be fascinated with it, and continue to learn as well as actually get to play a small part in building some of these interconnected networks.
