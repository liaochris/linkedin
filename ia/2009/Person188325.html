Peter Bell’s Summary

Peter Bell is an accomplished senior executive with an extensive global business background focusing on Business development in more than 50 countries, coupled with sales, marketing and general management. He is creative, entrepreneurial,logical and decisive. He has a strong personal presence, is a strong negotiator & communicator and is very comfortable in presentation roles to any audience, including on camera presentation for training videos or live TV.

Initially commencing his working career in a laboratory and scientific environment as an industrial chemist, he then moved through various technical roles with an increasing focus on customer service and territory management. The progression of his career took him through a period of conventional product marketing management and then in to regional sales and customer service with promotion to National and International sales, marketing and general management roles and duties at board level, including the successful implementation of an IPO in the USA.

He embraces a “hands on flexible business approach”, is a good team leader, mentor and trainer and has the innate ability to be highly productive in a position calling for high degrees of travel. The first 15 years of his career was focused on Australian business with responsibilities for New Zealand and progressing to more senior International roles encompassing Asia, Europe and North and South America. Extensive sales planning, forecasting, budgeting, General management, mentoring, controlling, logistics and reporting experience; as well as being able to quickly assimilate new sales communications and customer service technologies are all part of his technical competencies, coupled with highly developed computer skills.

He has a degree in Industrial Chemistry, is an Associate Fellow of the Australian Marketing Institute and has resided in Castle Hill since 1980 with a dedicated home office/computer & communications environment available.
