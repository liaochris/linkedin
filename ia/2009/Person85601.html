Gerald King, CKMP, CISSP’s Summary

Chief of CAC-K’s Knowledge Services Division of Battle Command Knowledge System working at the intersection of people, process and technology, bringing transformation to the Warrior Mission Area through IT enabled collaboration and Knowledge Management tools.

Mr. King is a former U.S. Army Communications and Electronics Automation Officer with Army Signal Center training in Director Of Information Manangement and Echelon Above Corps Operations, Teleprocessing Operations, Branch Automation. Holding a BS in Electronics Management from Southern Illinois University, and a graduate education in Public Administration from the University of Oklahoma he has 34 years of experience in voice and data network operations, data center technology, application development, and end-user support. Before joining BCKS in 2007 he specialized in building, leading, and managing teams in high availability data centers, network operations, and desktop support environments.
