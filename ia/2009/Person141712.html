Michael Liskin’s Summary

Accomplished consultant, communications specialist & university instructor, creative rainmaker, and business trainer in communication technology and emerging media. This includes social and collaborative media as well as emerging forms of optimization and metrics. Skilled at coaching and managing business professionals. Projects recognized by U.S. national media.
+++
These are exciting times--it's the "wild west" for the online world, with traditional boundaries reforming in such areas as marketing, PR, advertising, branding, and internal communication. I am gladly immersed in the tools and practices providing new opportunities for collaboration and communication that are altering the business and social world, along with emerging media platforms that exemplify the term 'disruptive technology.' It is fascinating to integrate new communication technologies with the strategic goals of business, from the perspective of effective organizational development as well as community outreach.
