Team Qivana’s Summary

Team Qivana is led by Alicia Sutton. As an Interactive Marketing Executive with 14 years of experience in building online strategies for some of the world's largest brands. Microsoft, HP, Levi's & Reckitt Benckiser have all worked with Alicia in both an agency model and as a consultant. Starting with Intel in the mid 90's, Alicia helped develop, justify, plan and execute Intel's foray into online advertising at the inception of acceptance for online media. Alicia leads Team Qivana online and offline efforts in helping our organization grow with the Qivana system.

Team Qivana is supported by Chad Sutton. Chad is an Entrepreneurial Leader with 10 years experience in building and developing B2B and B2C sales infrastructure for small businesses, handling everything from lead generation to channel growth and client management. Under Chad's guidance at Team Qivana the mission is clear, develop the Team Qivana organization into the early market mover for Qivana. Chad has succesfully helped businesses weave technology into operations as evidenced by launching a virtual accounting division for a US based Firm and in establishing his past firm’s as the leaders in their web presence and SEO/online marketing.

Team Qivana also enjoys training and advice from Mr. B. Sutton. Bringing 15 years of increasingly complex financial and investment management services experience to Team Qivana. In his role as advisor and specialist he has sold and marketed financial services to many of the most influential institutional investors in the US over his career, including public pension funds, corporations, foundations & endowments and the investment consultants supporting these groups. Overseeing 100's of successfully completed transactions representing Billions of invested US Dollars under his guidance. Among these transactions are maiden investment strategies for clients, brokerage related transition management across equity, debt, foreign equities and currencies.

    When was the last time you felt really alive? There are thousands of untruths that people live every day. I am stuck in my job. I can’t pay off my debts, I have no options. And with each untruth a bit of life, of aliveness, dies.

    Qivana is here to reverse the dullness that comes with mediocrity. To turn a light on life and prove to you there are options, if you’re willing to work for them.

    Much like climbing a mountain, you don't just end up on top. You work for it, step by step. Sometimes you focus on the path at your feet and sometimes you look toward the summit. Every step adds meaning to your journey, as if you’re saying, “I want it. I’ll work for it. I have faith it can happen.”

    Qivana can’t hand you success. But it can show you the path and walk you step by step to the summit. We are here to help you realize what you've always been deep inside. Happy. Successful. Excited. Alive. You lose a little bit of life every time you settle for less than your dreams. Fight the urge to be mediocre and push on. There is greatness at the top of the mountain.

    Your success is our focus.

    Qivana is the first network marketing company to put you, the Independent Business Owner (IBO), at the center of everything. Our sole mission is to make you and others like you the most successful entrepreneurs in the world.

    * IBOs are empowered through premium support. Everything about our network, product systems, sales tools and trainings has your success in mind.
    * Our singular business model focuses on the long-term, sustainable success of millions of IBOs.
    * Every aspect of our business has been designed to maximize sustainability and retention. We want your Qivana business to thrive for years to come.
    * We promote deliberate, meaningful financial goals. Financial independence is only relevant when it’s realistic and attainable.

    Are you ready to climb?
