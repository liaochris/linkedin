Eve Dmochowska’s Summary

My passion and expertise lies in New and Social Media. I share it with small and large business to help them build a social network, establish an online presence, and speak more efficiently with their customers.

I work on my own online projects and occasionally with online and offline clients. I address the ever-present challenge: how does one harness the power of New and Social Media to increase awareness and interactivity with an offline/online business, brand or product?

I stay on top of things by reading about 400 articles/blog posts a day, participating in the social media–sphere, talking to as many people who are influencers in this field as possible, writing a lot on the subject and applying my conclusions to my own ventures, and those of my clients.

I am a keen observer of online usage trends, and how they translate into new regions which have a longer - and delayed - adoption cycles (such as my country of residence, South Africa).

I have been active in the Internet world since 1996. I have a failed startup behind me, but my passion for the possibilities of online as a medium is endless!

I speak, read and write English and Polish, and can get by in Afrikaans. I am also presently studying French. I have called four countries home, and have an EU passport. Although I am very happy to live in South Africa, I and am open to the idea of living in a new region, for a temporary stay.

I am an excellent negotiator and an accomplished public speaker.
