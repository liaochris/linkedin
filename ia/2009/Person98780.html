Daniel Robles’s Summary

Mechanical Engineer / MBA with a comprehensive set of experiences in aerospace, entertainment, education, construction and international policy.

My objective is to propagate a broad based conversation about the tangibility of knowledge in an innovation economy. An independent innovation system can be married with the financial system to create a far more balance economic development. There is an extraordinary opportunity to integrate social media in this fashion.
