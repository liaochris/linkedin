Pär Johansson’s Summary

With a MSc in Civil Engineering (1995) I started my career at the University in Luleå as a PhD Student.
After finishing my Licentiate Thesis (1998) I was offered a the job as Director of Studies at the education of Project Mangement in Civil Engineering. This was the first education in Sweden that was offered simultanously to students on site i Luleå and students off-site at 4 other cities.
After four years (2002) I decided to leave academia and start my new business Designtech, focused on developing web-based Collaborative Tools for Projects.
This became the start of a six year long journey as CEO of Designtech - a journey filled with a lot of meetings with interesting people and a lot of business experiences from all over Europe. Designtech has today customers in 15 countries and the majority of the customers can be found within the R&D sector.
Early 2008 I decided to leave Designtech and went back to academia in order to finalise my PhD Thesis and at the same time I was offered a job as Project Manager at Volvo Aero in Trollhättan.

What is it that really drives me?
I'm always driven by business ideas and new business models - no matter what application area. I have had the benefit of participating in several start-ups as mentor and also founder of two companies; Incentor and Designtech.
I'm also a person who enjoys meeting new people - and that in combination with new 'impossible' missions is something that I really find positively challenging.

My private interest are divided into three areas:
- My family (Wife and 2 sons)
- Fly fishing (As often as possible)
- Food, Wine and Travelling (Preferrably combined with my family and some fly fishing)
