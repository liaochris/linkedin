    May 1997 — March 2003 (5 years 11 months)

    Financial reporting of a public company, grossing $1 billion in revenue, reporting to the CFO.

    Consolidation of financial statements in multi-currencies for approximately 20 corporate entities within the group, using both Canadian & US generally accepted accounting principles.

    Extensive involvement in corporate acquisitions including business valuation, due diligence, tax, financing structures and systems integration.

    Supervising 4 people at head and 12 plant controllers throughout North America.
