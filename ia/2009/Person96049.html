Rachel Choppin’s Summary

Rachel Choppin: Principal, HR 4 You
Instructor, UCLA, UC Davis and UCI Extensions

Rachel Choppin is an accomplished teacher, human resources consultant and career counselor with 20 years experience in facilitating professional development, management training and legal compliance over three continents. She is passionate about bringing out the best in people, engaging participants in applying learning in real time and contributing to their organization's success.

Ms. Choppin is a highly-rated instructor and a frequent speaker and facilitator at corporate retreats and professional associations. She has successfully hired, coached, trained and transitioned hundreds of professionals. She has consulted and conducted training with organizations such as Boeing, Northrop Grumman, Nielsen, Newegg, Ingersoll-Rand, Razor USA, Klune Industries, Medtronic, Intercare Therapy, Advanced Medical Review, the City of Sacramento and the Metropolitan Water District of Southern California. For full list of courses see www.hr4you.com/teaching

Ms. Choppin has created professional development coaching programs, Career Your Passion, Navigate Organizational Politics and Manage Yourself for the Life You Want, and has guided numerous professionals in achieving their work-life goals. She is the Professional Development Chair of Professionals in Human Resources Association, District 5 and the facilitator of Schmooze and Learn, a member of the Society of Human Resource Management, California Chamber of Commerce, Human Capital Institute and American Society for Training and Development and the UCLA Extension Human Resources Management Advisory Board.

Ms. Choppin earned an MA in Career and Educational Counseling from UCLA, a Certificate in Counseling from The London Clinic and Institute of Psychotherapy and a BS and Teaching Certificate in Science from The Hebrew University of Jerusalem. She is a certified UCLA Extension Online Instructor.

Rachel Choppin’s Specialties:

Professional development training and coaching, performance and change management, personality assessment, talent acquisition, HR audit and legal compliance
