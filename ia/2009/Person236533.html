Tonia Farman’s Summary

My experience and success have served many clients in various areas of marketing and communications: everything from advertising and graphic design to writing to building online brand strategies.

I would like to take this foundation of diverse talents and integrate it somehow with the needs of an industry that I feel deeply passionate about: Renewable Energy. Wind and/or solar.

Tonia Farman’s Specialties:

Graphic Design, Creative Marketing Strategies, Communication, Word of Mouth Marketing

Tonia Farman’s Experience

  • Executive Director

    Association of Wind & Water Sports Industries

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    The AWSI represents the manufacturers, dealers, and schools of the windsurfing, kiteboarding, and Standup Paddlesurf (SUP) industries. I manage the direction and communication of the organization in the interest of our members and the future of the wind & water sports industry.

  • Community Organizer & Director

    Kiteboarding 4 Cancer

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    April 2006 — Present (3 years 7 months)

    Formed from the ground up by myself, Garret Zallen, and Gregg Gnecco, Kiteboarding 4 Cancer serves to raise funds for cancer awareness, research, and care programs that use little administrative overhead but achieve volumes in directly touching the lives of those with cancer.

    Our primary source of fundraising is an action sport event in the summer involving kiteboarding -- one of the fastest growing sports in the world. KB4C has raised over $200,000 since we began this mission 2 years ago.

    KB4C's healing power of wind has spread across the globe, drawing in hundreds of donors and participants each year to travel to Hood River, Oregon for the event.

    Using a strategy of word of mouth, email, blogs, forums, and a handful of social network tools online, I aimed to excite and inspire every individual about the cause and the event. The goal was always to make it something that people wanted to be a part of.
