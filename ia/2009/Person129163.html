Keith Weaver’s Summary

I'm an Art Director, Designer, and Developer with over 10 years of experience working in the interactive space creating unique experiences in both online and offline media. I've lead both small and large teams of creatives, developers, editors, animators, producers, and account people on projects for brands such as National Geographic Channel, Royal Caribbean, Celebrity Cruise Lines, Audi, REI, IBM, ING, and Showtime Channel.
