Ray Knight’s Summary

Remarkably accomplished Chief Marketing Officer in both public/private sectors; possesses extensive high-profile business consulting experience as the President/CEO of RaynMaker-marketing and advertising outsourcing services.

As Chief Marketing Officer in public and private companies and as an entrepreneur, been passionately building businesses and brands for over 30 years in 40 industries.Majority of experience in the (B2B) arena.

Researched/developed marketing plans to find sources of revenues in various industries through new sources of prospect generation, product & service development and by researching/structuring strategic alliances/partnerships.

With right brain creative skills/left brain analytical skills, I find new angles to pursue. I bring this approach developed through the years to all I do.

I am committed to lifelong learning and leveraging the best tools available, I easily adopt new technologies and bring them to the table which translate to my employers or clients being ahead of the curve.

Understand and adapt to different markets and have recently worked with companies serving affluent markets and women-owned businesses, collaborating with women senior executives and assisting companies that are seeking to serve the largest market in the world- the US women's market.

I currently have strategic alliances and partners that are leaders in their specific industries that include interactive agencies, entertainment attorneys,online video distribution, online video production experts and media research and buyers.
