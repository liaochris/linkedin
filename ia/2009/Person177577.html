Obaid Hanafi ....P.Eng’s Summary

After earning a baccalaureate degree in mechanical engineering Obaid has gained decades of progressive Plant Engineering and Project Management experience. He has played key roles in the Feasibility / economic study, development, specification, design (FEED & Detailed), engineering, procurement, construction, contracting, sub-contracting, fabrication, erection, construction, commissioning, turnover, operation, warranty coverage, defect rectification, revamping etc of several grass roots EPC projects of entire Plant Complexes and Product Handling Terminals in U.S., U.K., Middle East, Canada, Brazil and Italy. For several projects Obaid represented the Owner / Operator Companies. He managed few on behalf of the main EPC Contractor. Finally he managed some more projects while employed by Technology Provider / OEM Vendor. Obaid has also established & managed teams of multi discipline engineers and provided Technical / Engineering Support to operating Petrochemical plants & Refineries.
