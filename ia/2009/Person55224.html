Darby Felton’s Summary

Darby Felton has professional experience working with people and information technologies since 1996. During this time he has performed in various related roles, including web application and software development, systems design, administration and analysis, instruction, and training. He has built high-performance systems handling millions of users per day and has taught degree courses in information technology. In 2003 he founded an independent consultancy to provide high-quality, low-cost business solutions leveraging open source technologies. After three years as an independent consultant, Darby accepted a position with Zend Technologies, where he made core contributions to Zend Framework, including authorship of components, tests, and documentation, and became a member of the Zend
Framework Education Advisory Board. Darby holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from the University of Missouri - Columbia, as well as several professional certifications including Zend Certified Engineer (PHP 4, PHP 5, and Zend Framework).

Darby Felton’s Specialties:

PHP, Zend Framework, Flex, ActionScript, JavaScript, Apache, MySQL, UNIX (FreeBSD, CentOS, Debian, Redhat, Cobalt, AIX, IRIX)
