Charles Pimentel’s Summary

Seasoned energy professional with a proven entrepreneurial track record selling into high technology industries such as solar, fuel cells, wind, and telecommunications. Currently act as Eastern Regional Sales Manager for the US Office of Canadian Solar Inc., a world leader in the development and manufacture of environmentally sound photovoltaic solar modules. Also owned and operated a boutique technical staffing and recruiting firm serviceing all levels of Sales, Marketing and New Product Development energy professionals throughout North America.
