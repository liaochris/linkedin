Mary McDonald, Process Improvement’s Summary

- Internationally recognized leader in providing Process Improvement Consulting to a wide variety of customers in the high technology, service, automotive, and manufacturing disciplines. Process Improvement include process flow mapping, identification of efficiencies, and streamlining of processes.

- Leading expert in ISO 9000, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001, RoHS/WEEE QC 080000, and industry-specific standards. Involved in implementation and continuous improvement of management standards. Mary instructs a wide variety of topics in ISO/SPC/TQM/ Baldrige/Performance Excellence management methodologies, and provides cost effective consulting services for clients.

- First consultant east of the Rockies to be qualified to implement a Hazardous Substance Process Management program, and to perform external audits to register companies as Hazardous Substance Free via now QC 080000.

- Built Device Engineering group (from 1 to 28 employees) responsible for transfer of technology into manufacturing. Implemented automotive quality standard QS9000, instilled Project Management (PM) methodology and Performance Excellence (Baldrige) in Motorola’s Bond Assembly and Test facility as part of long-term assignment (4 years). Was ranked as Project Management Master (only 18 in entire corporation at that time).

- Plexus certified trainer for ISO9001, ISO9001 for Healthcare, QS9000, TS 16949, and ISO14001.

- Subcontract to third party registrar; perform third party (external) ISO/TS/OHS registration/ continuing audits.

- Dedicated to improving anyone's health by educating them on the importance of proper nutrition with Juice Plus+ whole food. Juice Plus+ is 17 raw fruits, vegetables and grains dehydrated into convenient capsule form. www.healthyfood123.com provides more information.
