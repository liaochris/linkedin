Ken Stasiak’s Summary

GOALS

Senior Software Designer/Developer position utilizing object-oriented development languages such as Java, C++ and VB and relational database technologies such as SQL Server or Oracle, with an emphasis on Product Development. My strengths and interests involve Middleware as well as system- or server-level processes. The opportunity for mentoring and technical leadership is desirable.
Pure and Applied Research in Computer Science or Software Engineering is also desirable.
At this time, I am only interested in positions in the Ottawa area.

BACKGROUND

Over twenty-five years of software design and development experience, supplying creative solutions within unusual constraints in a variety of computing environments. These include the utilization of both distributed and centralized data storage as well as many combinations of mainframe, PC, co-operative, and uni- / multiprocessing.
Able to handle both low-level physical implementations and high-level logical abstractions, and has been a key participant in almost every phase of a project lifecycle, including research, analysis, design, development, testing and problem resolution, product and maintenance release preparation, presentations of results, and training of staff.
