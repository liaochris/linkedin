Tami Diehm’s Summary

My practice is quite diverse and includes advising clients on all aspects of real estate transactions with a special focus on land use and zoning matters. In addition to the traditional buying / selling / leasing work that most real estate attorneys do, I enjoy representing clients before local boards and councils. I serve as a city attorney in a suburb of the Twin Cities and I also serve as an elected city council member in the city that I live in. I find that this breadth of experience often brings a fresh perspective to many real estate transactions

In addition to local government and land use work, I regularly advise corporations, non-profit organizations, trade associations and political action committees in matters related to formation and governance as well as assisting with filings and enforcement matters involving the IRS, the Minnesota Campaign Finance and Public Disclosure Board and the Federal Election Commission.
