Jami Becker’s Summary

I am a creator of space and art. I am a master at turning any idea into a concrete comprehensible reality with an intuitive understanding of current social and cultural nuance. I am able to work quickly and am talented both as a traditional artist, trained as an architect and fully competent with all the software.

I created 98% of the 3D models for The Sims, the worlds number one selling software game.

I created environments for Star Wars Pod Racer and (it is true) one mean shooter game. I have decimated a few cities (on computer)!

I produced, conceptualized and art directed a 3D home design software, 3 children's educational software titles and 3 fantasy tradtional game titles.

I have launched my own kids clothing line and gotten into major retail distribution-26 shops of a single chain.

I taught graduate school web design in San Francisco at The Academy of Art College.

If you need magic...architectural, set, logo, and web design and programming, print, copy, illustration, mobile software design I am it. I love what I do and I am not done until you are buzzing with excitement.
