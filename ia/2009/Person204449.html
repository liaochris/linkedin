Roy Bassave’s Summary

I have worked in Journalism for over 25 years, in Newspapers, Magazines and on online covering consumer products, entertainment, Video/DVD, high-tech, video games and toys and more. In addition, I have 9 years of experience as Mobile Notary Signing Agent.


I am most proud of creating in 1989 the original weekly video game review format called "Videogame of the Week" and "CD-ROM of the Week" in the Miami Herald and the Knight Wire. This includes in 1991 a Video/Consumer Electronics page in Spanish called "Videomundo" in El Nuevo Herald.

I own my own Consumer Products review website & Notary business.

Roy Bassave’s Specialties:

I offer my expertise in Consumer Electronics Writing, editing, product reviews, Hispanic consumer shopping trends.

I am very skilled with Internet Web browsers, online content editing, Video Editing, Dreamweaver, Excel, Photoshop, Windows Vista, MSWord and Publisher.

I attend the Consumer Electronic Show CES yearly and have attended in the past the Electronic Entertainment Expo E3 and Video Software Dealers Association Show (VSDA).
