Roger Lee’s Summary

I enjoy working in the newspaper and new media world. I find the rich relationships with newspaper clients to be rewarding. At TownNews.com we work very hard to help newspapers grow their online business. Our technology continues to grow steadily, and our people are the best.

I spent 10 years working in the Lutheran Church, then about 10 years working in the insurance industry, mostly with Prudential, before I began with a little company based in northern Minnesota, CityPapers. We were soon purchased by PowerAds, which merged with AdOne to become PowerOne Media. In May of 2002 I began with TownNews.com. In February of 2006 TownNews.com purchased Zwire, AdQuest, and ypEngine as PowerOne was concluding business. Throughout my career, building good business relationships has been the key to my success. During this time of economic stress, those solid relationships are paying off at TownNews.com.

Roger Lee’s Specialties:

I enjoy serving and advocating for my newspaper clients over the long haul! We focus on "what can we do next?" to grow your online news business!
