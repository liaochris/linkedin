Peter Carpenter’s Summary

Thoroughbred Design Group is my baby. Born in April 1998, Thoroughbred Design Group has developed into a Creative Communications Agency that works with businesses of all sizes.

Thoroughbred Design Group's boutique appeal assures the client the attention they deserve. We pride ourself by guiding clients efficiently and creatively through their marketing efforts and graphic needs. Thoroughbred Design Group approaches each opportunity with an open mind and a sharp pencil. Share your cocktail napkin concepts and watch as they develop into powerful communication tools.
