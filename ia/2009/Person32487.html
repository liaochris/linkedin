Brendan Cruickshank’s Summary

Brendan has had an extensive career in sales and sales management with a focus on the recruitment sector. He has been quoted as an expert on recruitment trends in numerous publications including the Wall Street Journal and Washington Post.

He began his career as Senior Financial Advisor at Merrill Lynch for over 5 years in the private client group.

He moved in to the recruitment sector in 2002 when he joined jobsinthemoney.com the leading job site for financial and accounting professionals. He was the Head of Sales at jobsinthemoney until early 2006 and helped to substantially grow the business.

He then joined indeed.com as the Senior Director of Sales and helped to grow the sales team and the revenue.

He is now the Vice President of Sales at Juju.com.

Brendan Cruickshank’s Specialties:

Brendan is a specialist in recruitment strategy and sales leadership. He has been in the recruitment industry since 2002 and has a strong network of contacts throughout advertising agencies, job boards, human resources departments and hr outsourcing firms. He has built and grown sales teams at several leading online job resources. He has an extensive knowledge of SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and SEM ( Search Engine Marketing)
