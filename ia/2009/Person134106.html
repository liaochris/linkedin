Konstantin Kovshenin’s Summary

I'm an experienced Web Application Developer and in the web business for over 5 years. I specialize on application prototyping, coding & development, usability, scalability and network administration as well as bug tracking, teamwork and collaboration.

I love public APIs and currently specialize in the following: Twitter, Flickr, Google Maps, Bit.ly, Delicious and I can't wait to get my hands on Google Wave API.

In June 2009 I've released a Twitter app called Foller.me (www.foller.me) which is a mashup of the Twitter API and Google Maps.

In the early 2009 I started the development of numerous opensource plugins for WordPress, including Twitter Friendly Links, Quick Flickr Widget and more. I still support and update the released plugins as well as come up with new ones from time to time.

I also specialize in the Amazon Web Services (EC2, EBS, S3, MapReduce, etc), worked with scalable code and database structure. Server & databse performance tuning (therefore experienced in Linux administration, generally Fedora or Fedora Core).

In late 2008 I developed websites based on WordPress. From simple one-page RSS feeds to complex corporate websites, e-commerce points and social networking resources. Yes, all this is possible with WordPress. I believe that any website could (and perhaps should) be WordPress-driven. I drive the experimental version 2.9 on my blog while the rest of the world just switched to 2.8.

From 2007-2008 worked freelance and developed fairly simple websites using PHP and a couple of PHP frameworks, together with MySQL. Worked full-time at Android Robotics Corporation, specializing in .NET development including C# and Microsoft Robotics Developer Studio.

During 2006 worked part-time while studying. Basic PHP and MySQL development, as well as graphics and web design. Studied C++, OpenGL, DirectX and the basics of Game Development.

In 2004-2005 worked for a couple of small clients. Used ASP and Microsoft SQL Server.
