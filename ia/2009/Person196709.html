Reydel Santos’s Summary

My personal life experiences have prepared me to provide anyone with sound advice in a broad range of areas, including: affordable and supportive housing, real estate investment and development, and decision making.

From the time that we are old enough to understand the spoken language we are urged to "succeed" in everything. Oftentimes the message we hear is that unless we are financially successful we are a failure. This is the farthest thing from the truth.

Albert Schweitzer stated that, "Success is not the key to happiness. Happiness is the key to success. If you love what you are doing, you will be successful". I believe that you know you have achieved success when you reach the end of your life and you have no regrets, no qualms or grudges.

My goal is simple: to share what I have learned in my life with those who would be willing to learn from my experiences in order that they may enjoy a more balanced life, one that brings no regrets. All success is highly dependent on the choices we make. Will you make the right choice? Ask me and I will gladly offer you my opinion.
