MARCOS LOPEZ DE PRADO’s Summary

Marcos Mailoc López de Prado y López has received a Ph.D. in Economics "Summa Cum Laude" and a Diploma of Advanced Studies (D.E.A.) in Financial Economics from U.N.E.D. He also holds a M.Sc. in Economics from Royal University of Santiago de Compostela.

Dr. López de Prado is Vice President at TUDOR INVESTMENT CORP., one of the world's top 5 hedge funds, where he is also Head of High Frequency Futures. Previously, he has been a Partner at PEAK6 Investments, a global leader in options market-making. He has also been responsible for trading short rates bflys at Citadel Investment Group, Head of Quantitative Equity Research at UBS, and Equity Relative Value trader and quantitative analyst at BBVA. He is Associate Professor and member of the Scientific Commission for several post-graduate study programs organized by European Universities, and responsible for the Finance and Investment Theory area at U.N.E.D.'s M.B.A.

He is the author of the first book in Spanish about alternative investments, “Invertir en Hedge Funds: Análisis de su estructura, estrategias y eficiencia”, written in colaboration with Prof. Dr. Carlos Rodrigo Illera and published by Díaz de Santos in 2004. In the last 10 years he has published over 50 scientific papers about quantitative strategies and investment models, most of them in English and German. His scientific paper "Measuring Loss Potential of Hedge Fund Strategies", published by The Journal of Alternative Investments in 2004, gave birth to the use of "Draw-Down" as an alternative to VaR models for determining the atemporal probabilistic loss of an investment.

He has been recognized with the National Graduation Award in Economics by Spain's Ministry of Science in 1999 (National Valedictorian), the Regional Graduation Award in Economics by the Government of Galicia in 1999 (Regional Valedictorian) and the Extraordinary Academic Achievement Award by the Royal University of Santiago de Compostela in 1998 (University's Valedictorian).
