Willy Lai’s Summary

Willy Lai is a user experience design leader with 14 years of experience designing some of the world's most popular Web commerce sites, Web portals, and Web applications. By strategically designing Web sites to align with customer needs and business goals, Lai has crafted award-winning user experiences that drive business results.

http://willylai.com

Willy Lai’s Specialties:

• Building and managing on-site and remote design teams.
• Complex, large scale site-wide Web user experience design/redesign (Strategy, Concepting, Information Architecture, Interaction/UI Design, Visual Design, Usability)
• Emphasis on leveraging design to drive business results.
