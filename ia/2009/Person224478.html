Steven Ledgerwood’s Summary

Steven Ledgerwood is a consultant to other professionals and their clients, in tax, business and family wealth matters.

Steven has served as general tax counsel to Fortune 100 and privately owned companies and as a tax and family wealth consultant to families with net estates of eight to ten figures, in several states.

He also has extensive experience representing publicly traded and private companies in mergers and acquisitions and in large-case tax appeals and tax litigation matters. He is included in The Best Lawyers in America in Tax Law and is a Fellow of the American College of Trust and Estate Counsel.

Steven Ledgerwood’s Specialties:

Business reorganizations, mergers and acquisitions, business transactions, family wealth transfer planning, tax appeals/litigation, taxation of business entities
