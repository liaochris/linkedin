Michael Cradock’s Summary

Michael is a capital raising management consultant to high-growth and predominantly technology-driven Australian companies. Michael's value proposition to clients he works with with is this: To Improve Individuals' Success in Raising and Use of Capital.

Prior to living in Australia, Michael was the founder and managing director of a LexisNexis award-winning tax practice in London. He attracted angel investment in 2005 and then grew the practice's client base to include a number of FTSE 100 companies. Michael was then recruited by PricewaterhouseCoopers London in 2006 bringing with him his client base.

In 2008 Michael moved to Australia with his Australian wife, Toni Morgan, and is now really enjoying the overall higher quality of work-life balance.
