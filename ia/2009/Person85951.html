Gery DiMarco’s Summary

Having been in and around the network marketing field for over two decades I have been mentored by some of the best in the business and happily shared those teachings and experiences as a mentor to others. Learning by trial and error has some benefits but being able to work from a proven system provides much quicker results.

I have been associated with one of the most widely recognized executive teams in the business for over a decade and am once again returning to active business development and distribution with them. Known worldwide for their commitment to quality products and fairness to those who butter their bread it is honor to share their vision and mission. I am driven by principle and by a commitment to support the success of our team and our customers.

My experiences in retail sales and management, account management and broadcasting have provided me with the skills needed to communicate and understand the needs of others and relate with them in a manner that encourages development and growth and profitability of their business.
