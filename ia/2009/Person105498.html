Howdy!

My name is Jason Syptak and I am a freshman Business major from San Antonio, TX... but more importantly, I am a proud member of the Fightin' Texas Aggies Class of 2012! AAAA!

I have also been exposed to the field of Public Relations and Law through Independent Study Mentorship, a program at John Marshall High School that allows students to study in a field of their interest.

I also created and hosted a segment on John Marshall's Morning Announcements, "Marshall Cares, Marshall Votes." I broadcasted political updates, political information, as well as information on how to vote.
