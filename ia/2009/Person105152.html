Jason Kucherawy’s Summary

Over the past decade I have worked in all aspects of the educational travel industry, from sales and marketing, to operations and management. Having been a founding member of the Impact Educational Tours team and launching it from a profitable upstart in its first year to an industry leader five years later (as it became part of First Choice Student Travel) has been an incredibly rewarding venture.

I am constantly seeking to test myself and lead a life as rich in experience as possible. My journey has led me to live and work in places that have exposed me to culture shock (Japan), humbled me with beauty (the Canadian Rockies) and brought me as close to war as I ever want to be (Afghanistan).

In all that I have done, have learned a great deal about myself - and most importantly that having the opportunity to influence, educate, and inspire others is my true calling.

Jason Kucherawy’s Specialties:

Leading tours of major cities in Canada and the USA, group tour itinerary building, brand development, marketing via social networks, leadership training, public speaking, client engagement.
