Laurie Berenson’s Summary

Laurie works with her clients one-on-one to highlight their professional strengths and accomplishments through highly customized career documents, including résumés, cover letters, thank you letters, and executive biographies. Laurie prides herself on creating effective, powerful documents, one client at a time.

Industry Certification:
Laurie earned her Certified Professional Résumé Writer (CPRW) designation in 2005. Awarded by the Professional Association of Résumé Writers & Career Coaches, CPRWs are among the leading experts in the career industry in the development of strategic résumés. Laurie's CPRW designation demonstrates her knowledge, talent, and writing expertise in meeting the industry standard of excellence and in serving client's career interests.

Professional Background:
Laurie has nearly 15 years of professional experience that includes search firm, on-campus recruiting, and hands-on experience as a finance professional. After graduating cum laude from Georgetown University with a double major in accounting and finance, her career began in corporate finance and equity research before transitioning to recruitment and career services.

Before establishing Sterling Career Concepts, LLC, Laurie was most recently an executive recruiter with a leading national search firm. Laurie specialized in working with accounting and finance professionals in the $70,000 to $200,000 salary range but her recommendations and techniques are universal in nature and can benefit professionals across industries and at entry level, mid-career, and management levels.
