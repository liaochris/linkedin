Gavin Gomes’s Summary

An entrepreneurial executive with 18 years blue chip experience rising through organizations via national sales, marketing, general management and director level roles.

A motivational management style with a proven track record in building highly successful and engaged sales, marketing and operational teams. Through the application of key business levers to achieve revenue growth and programmatic cost reduction, profitability and contribution targets have been consistently exceeded.

Able to influence decision makers to secure internal support and resources, and execute these plans by developing solutions for customers through direct and indirect channels, in both start-up and established franchises.

Gavin is currently the Director of Sales & Operations with P&L responsibility for Telstra’s Indirect Channel, contributing $1.2B in revenue, serving consumer, business and enterprise customers. Gavin's responsibility includes, revenue growth, remuneration, productivity, customer satisfaction, field services, training, incentives and support for over 800 dealers and 5,000 locations.

Gavin has held senior sales and marketing roles in Telstra Enterprise, Retail and BigPond, Procter & Gamble and IBM Australia.

Gavin holds a Bachelor of Economics & Finance from Sydney University, Masters of Commerce from the University of NSW, is a member of the Institute of International Affairs, Institute of Management and an Associate of Institute of Bankers.

Gavin lives in Sydney with his wife and four children.
