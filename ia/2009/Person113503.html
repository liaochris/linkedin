Jim Warner’s Summary

With the dramatic shift in the Real Estate Marketplace I have limited my Real Estate Practice to Private Consulting. As of December, 2008 I am putting my 20+ years in Real Estate Sales and Management Experience to use Building a Support Services Organization that will allow Aspiring Independent Business People to Survive and Ultimately Thrive in this Challenging Economic Environment. Focusing on the Burgeoning Wellness Industry and in particular Anti Aging, we have partnered with some of the most respected and forward thinking companies in the world to offer and support the creation of Independent Distribution entities through Direct and Network Marketing. Nu Skin, Pharmanex, Enagic and Medi Consult all offer cutting edge technologies and income opportunities within this market space. The result of this shift in direction is Phoenix Wellness Technologies - We invite you to take a closer look -www.youthloc.com
