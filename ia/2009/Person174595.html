Nicolas Abelé’s Summary

Extensive experience in Micro-Electro-Mechanical System (MEMS) product- and business development through previous activity at STMicroelectronics ( France), acting as the liaison between the engineering-, marketing- and sales teams.

Strong knowledge of large volume industrial environments and innovation technology transfer through IP creation. Nicolas Abelé’s Experience

  • CTO and co-founder

    Lemoptix SA

    (Semiconductors industry)

    December 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Lemoptix designs, develops and commercializes plug & play laser microprojection systems based on optical MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical System) scanning mirror technology. The Lemoptix microprojection modules are developed around the proprietary MEMS scanning mirror platform and integrate control electronics, laser light sources and simple projection optics. This unique technology combination enables unprecedented miniaturization and integration of microprojectors directly within information display systems and mobile devices.

    Lemoptix microprojection modules are destined to be embedded within various Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) products.

    (Public Company; STM; Semiconductors industry)

    November 2006 — November 2007 (1 year 1 month)

    MEMS device for time reference application. Project leader, coordination between the process team, circuit design team and back-end packaging team.
