Steve Golding’s Summary

Steve has worked in the Information Technology and Telecommunications industry for over 18 years across a diverse range of industries. From the Legal sector to Business Services, Conference and Events to Banking & Finance, including service in the military.

As a senior executive operating at board level within these organisations, his experience is both broad and deep. Whether it’s a privately held, or publicly listed company on a local or global scale Steve has demonstrated a track record of improving top-line performance with P&L responsibility and driving sales & marketing initiatives at the same time as improving bottom-line performance through efficient deployment of assets and services.

Winner of the PMI Group (Global) Chairman’s inner circle High Achiever of the year 2004. Steve has gained his credibility through delivering high standard results continually exceeding expectations.

After arriving in London Steve established an independent consultancy, SNS Management Consultants and with his track record of delivery utilising his practitioner certifications in both Prince 2 and ITIL he is typically in high-demand and is often engaged directly as a result of client referral.
