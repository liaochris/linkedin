Bill Vasas’s Summary

Signature Values

Manage the Workforce. Drive change. Utilize the concept of Forced Ranking, where the poorest performing 10% of the workforce is constantly scrutinized, improved or eliminated. Replace marginal performers with A Level performers. Deal with problems aggressively.

Employee Advocate. Nurture employee relations to always be the sounding board to senior management for talent management, capability assessment, compensation, and benefits.

Performance Benchmarking. Continuously raise the standard of performance of the company. Develop mutual goal setting that achieves company objectives and drives individual performance improvement. Hold accountabilities, reward high performers, drive towards Employer of Choice status.

Strategic Team Builder. Align employee talent with strategic objectives. Work with senior management to evaluate talent, develop a gap analysis, create developmental plans and build teams.

Defining Career Achievements

Operate Human Resources as a profit center. Analyze controllable cost opportunities in: benefits, worker compensation, contract design(s), carve out opportunities, self insurance and reinsurance options, unemployment compensation rate, good vs. bad employee turnover costs and develop a strategic plan to capitalize on the opportunities.

Cost Savings Execution. Realized a $750K positive cost shift in Worker Comp costs, driving the Experience Modification factor from .93% to .71%. Instituted creative access fee structure to reward healthy lifestyles, reducing health and medical benefits costs from $5,465 to $4,500 per employee from 94 to 99. Drove U/C costs to State of CT minimum of 0.9% of taxable wages, holding turnover to under 5% per year.

Customer Service. Drive the concept of internal and external customer service. Act with a sense of urgency to assist your customers, while building team credibility and inter-dependence. Become a true business partner and a value added resource.
