Terry Grapentine’s Summary

My background offers a unique combination of work experience (client and supplier, B2B and B2C), leadership development, and analytical and statistical expertise.

As a marketing research consultant, I have worked with some of the largest corporations around the world, on both domestic and international studies. I have helped Frito-Lay sell more potato chips, Energizer more batteries, and Honda more generators. On the client side, I worked for a Fortune 500 company, reporting to the CMO, and started that company’s consumer insights department.

In the area of leadership, I have served as president of two prestigious industry boards.

My technical experience includes teaching marketing research and statistics at the university level, leading AMA and MRA Workshops/seminars on market segmentation, and publishing widely in the applied marketing research field. I received an AMA’s Best Article award in 1997. Organizations such as the American Marketing Association, Marketing Research Association, and The Conference Board have invited me to give presentations at their conferences.

I am a great leader and mentor to those wanting to make marketing research their career.

Terry Grapentine’s Specialties:

Primary research, both quantitative and qualitative; have written articles on segmentation, model building, measurement, and various aspects of marketing management.
