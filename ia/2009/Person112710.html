Jill Magaard’s Summary

User experience and interactive marketing professional with over twelve years experience in strategy, planning, information architecture and project management. Extensive knowledge of interactive marketing, traditional advertising, and cross-channel integration. Well-grounded in web development and e-commerce principles. International experience: 7+ years in the U.S., 5 years in the UK. Founder of an award-winning interactive marketing agency. Experience with national and international consumer and retail brands, including: Room & Board, Best Buy, Magnolia Audio Video, Target, Jostens, US Bank, Time Warner and Levi Strauss & Co. Adept cross-functional collaborator who can bridge the worlds of marketing, creative, technical and other teams, with both agency and client experience.

Jill Magaard’s Specialties:

Interactive marketing strategy and planning, user experience architecture, content strategy, brand development, multi-channel advertising & marketing integration, customer centricity, youth marketing, wireless marketing.
