David Maples’s Summary

David Maples, Executive Vice President of Sales and Marketing

David Maples specializes in re-energizing sales organizations, and maximizing high growth opportunities. As an Executive in Sales, Marketing and Business Development, he builds companies, creates teams and delivers sales revenue growth for services, enterprise computing and software industries. His focus is “repeatable and scalable success” - based on solid business practices/infrastructure, seizing new business opportunities, driving hot technologies in new markets and executing opportunistic "go to Market" strategies. As Vice President for North American Sales and Marketing, Mr. Maples heads Allinea Software sales and marketing operations.

Maples leadership positions include General Manager, V.P. and Director of Sales, Marketing and Business Development at Cisco, Sun Microsystems, Digital Equipment Corporation, and rapid-growth entrepreneurial companies including GridApp Systems, DataSynapse, and Platform Computing. He established scalable direct and channel sales organizations, built marketing and business dev groups, created partnerships with IBM, Intel, HP, Oracle, AMD, Sun, etc. and spearheaded U.S. Sales operations. Successes include, Platform Computing - grew software sales from $3 million to $35 million in three years.

Mr. Maples' entrepreneurial record is as a market leader introducing advanced technology and solving new problems. He co-founded Streamvision, a streaming video company and Silicon Valley Resources, plus filled executive or advisory roles at m2mi Corporation, Modviz, Optena and Tiva Systems. He received a BSc. from the University of Dubuque in Iowa and pursued MBA at the University of Puget Sound in Tacoma, Washington. He is active in education, mentoring, and community service, as a regular guest speak at Stanford University continuing education programs.
