Laura Mears’s Summary

After graduating college and finishing my internship at the Portland Rose Festival in June 2008, I've been able to use what I've learned and growing work experience gained from internships to create marketing and public relations campaigns for two businesses!
My professional experience includes generating press releases, information packets, fact sheets, biography pages, web sites, slide shows, and related materials. I've worked independently, on committees, in teams, and with boards of directors.
I've taken a variety of communication courses including grant writing, coaching for commitment, integrated marketing communications, advertising and promotion, and writing and production for communication professionals.

Laura Mears’s Specialties:

Special Event planning, working with budgets, client and sponsor contract fulfillment, merge mailings and document procurement, creation of promotional materials, scheduling and leading committee meetings, and on-site event management.
Proficient in Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Movie Maker, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop CS2. Domain registration, website hosting, html coding, graphic design, blog posting, video blogging, e-mail, and a variety of research, grant, and media databases.
