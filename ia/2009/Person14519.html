Andrew Smith’s Summary

I have worked professionally as a designer in the fields of Architecture, Interior Design, Marketing, and Advertising. My experiences as a Graphic Designer/Production Artist range from Brand Development to Creative Direction, and Print Collateral to Front-end Visual Web Design.

As a Designer and AutoCAD Tech in the A/ID industry, I have produced Architectural and Interior Design construction documents, as well as finish, furnishings, and fixture specification for commercial projects. I have communicated with industry product reps, and produced project presentation materials for client meetings.

Ultimately, my main career goal is to attain a position as a Creative Director. I am a talented Concept Developer, and I very much enjoy taking a project from it's inception all the way to it's launch. I believe that open communication and collaboration are the two main elements for generating the most appropriate solution for any creative problem.

Andrew Smith’s Specialties:

Concept design, graphic design, brand development, creative direction, construction documents, finish/product specification, front-end visual web design
