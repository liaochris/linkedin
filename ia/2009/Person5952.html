AK Stout’s Summary

I work in the corporate office of a company called Senior Helpers (www.seniorhelpers.com) which provides professional in-home care so seniors can maintain their independence in the comfort of their own homes. I love everything this company does for people.

I also sell real estate for Long & Foster and especially enjoy helping buyers find a place they can truly call home.

I donate regularly to my favorite charities and also fundraise to not only earn additional funds but to also generate awareness.

When I'm not doing these things you can find me walking my boxer puppy to train for the two, 3-day, 60 miles Breast Cancer Walks (www.the3day.org/goto/soles4souls ) I will be participating in this year.

AK Stout’s Specialties:

There are many qualities & skills that go into being an excellent realtor; integrity, market knowledge & effective negotiation skills - all are hallmarks of how I work.

I've found that providing the best service is about putting my clients first. This means being accessible, being a good listener as well as a good communicator, & responding quickly.

This "client first" philosophy has always been my approach & it requires me to continually improve my skills & ways of doing business.
