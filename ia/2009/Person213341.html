Steve Boylan’s Summary

BEACON Business Group is a lower to middle market mergers & acquisitions and business valuation firm that works with business owners with revenues from $500,000 to over $25 million. We specialize in Wisconsin-based companies, so we are acutely aware of the economic and industry factors that affect the value of your business. Unlike most brokerage firms, we limit the number of active listings we work with to provide our clients with more guidance throughout the exit strategy process.

Steve Boylan’s Specialties:

Business valuations, mergers & acquisitions and buyer's agent. Manage Exit Strategies for business owners.
