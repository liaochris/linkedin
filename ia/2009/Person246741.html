Wes A Shattuck’s Summary

Wes offers over ten years of consultative and strategic sales within the Healthcare and Recruitment & Staffing industries. Throughout his career he has been able to marry the two industries and has become a trusted source on attracting quality healthcare, sales and IT talent.

His background includes positions with two market leaders within the healthcare industry: Johnson & Johnson and Cephalon, Inc. During his time with Johnson & Johnson that is when his interest and leadership began within the recruitment industry. Throughout his tenure he conducted all the initial interviewing for upper management and it was based on his suggestions as to who the top talent would be to interview with the District Managers and Regional Vice Presidents. He was selected as one of a handful on representatives nationwide to be put through Johnson & Johnson’s Management Development Lab which focused on and strengthened core competencies within leadership roles.

Following a successful career in the medical industry he transitioned to Monster Worldwide. Initially, he was Director of Business Development for a multi-state territory. He advised clients on enterprise wide recruitment strategies involving print & media plans, recruitment branding and corporate communications. From there he spearheaded the healthcare vertical for Monster.com in the Western Region. He consulted with many notable healthcare systems such as Kaiser Permanente, Catholic Healthcare West, Sutter Health, Banner Health, Scripps, Providence Health, American Medical Response and Apria Healthcare.

Considered by his clients to be an advocate to their needs and a true business partner, he approached each client with individual assessments and needs which brought valuable results to their overall strategy.
