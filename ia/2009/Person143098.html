Franco Lodato’s Summary

Nature inspired design—biomimicry or bionics—is not something you’d normally associate with Product Design. But it’s what inspires Franco Lodato during his career.
Franco’s trailblazing work in biomimicry and bionics is leading to fascinating new applications in everything from wearable technologies to boats. As a result, he is achieving a whole new level of simplicity, beauty, and functionality in the design of things we experience in our everyday lives. His innovations are a great example of how design can create measurable value for businesses and consumers in ways people may never have thought about before.
Experienced in the world of wireless communications, wearable technologies, and consumer markets for 20 years, he spent much of that time implementing bionic design in these arenas. “Bionics draws from the simplicity, efficiency, functionality, and beauty of design in nature and applies these principles to the design of everyday things,” says Franco
In his book, Bionics in Action, he explores these principles. He has expanded upon this exploration in companies like DuPont,Gillette ,Motorola, Herman Miller and Pininfarina, among others. Franco’s visionary approach implementing design innovations based on nature’s paradigms and the holistic consideration of the users’ experience is well-published in leading design and technology periodicals, Franco has also worked with the MIT Media Lab and taught biodesign methodologies and innovative design in Universities and at conferences worldwide.

Franco Lodato’s Specialties:

Electronics, Consumer goods, industrial design, Wearables technologies, Biomimicry, Biodesign, Biomimicry, Ecodesign, Luxury Goods Interiors (Private Jets and yachts)
