Spencer Maus’s Summary

Over 15 years of representing entrepreneurs and mid-level corporations, helping both public and private companies deliver their message to media and important business targets. Has a thorough understanding on how public relations, marketing, the Web 2.0, online video, etc work as one to raise profiles and generate revenues.

My business strengths are client and project creation and management, new business development and working with reporters. Areas of expertise are: Change Communications, Start-ups (Startup Accelerator), Social Media Training, Crisis Communications, Media Relations, Publicity, Writing, Trade Show Assistance, Media Training and Article Placement.

Spencer Maus’s Specialties:

Representation of entrepreneurs, public and private corporations with proactive and crisis communications.

Industry expertise includes: investment and financial services (investment banking, venture capital & private equity), real estate, international trade missions, medical products design, law, technology, home health care, banking, eight political campaigns and five public affairs projects, B2B entrepreneurial enterprises and entertainment.
