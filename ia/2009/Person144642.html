Luciana Penna’s Summary

Independent filmmaker (writer, director, producer) with experience in Music Videos, Short Films and TV programs. Currently engaged in a Research on Early Twentieth-Century Brazilian Film History.

Formerly self-employed at Cinestesia, Internet Film Review Publication, as Editor and Writer, and at Tela Brasilis, monthly venue at MAM-Rio, as programmer and producer.

Luciana Penna’s Specialties:

Screenplay Writing, Film Direction and Production, Film Research and Review
