Janine Hammond’s Summary

I have just began working for a company called Rialto Care Services (See Rialtocare.org). I am the business development manager expanding the services that the company offers. Our aim is to be the number one independant mental health care supplier in the UK. We specialise in the treatment and support of patients with a history of violent behaviour.
I have previously had experience as a Senior Sales Manager for Nth West and North Wales for Merck. Have worked in pharmaceutical industry for twelve years for two Blue Chip companies - GSK and MSD. Have worked in the following disease areas:-
Respiratory, diabetes, depression, erectile dysfunction, migraine, CV including lipids and hypertension, pain relief, osteoporosis.
Prior to working for pharmaceutical companies I worked for thirteen years in the NHS as an ITU nurse, Midwife, and Practice Nurse. Also worked for Liverpool University as an assessor for the Practice Nurse Diploma course.
I am seeking a new role that will be challenging and rewarding and remain open minded as to what that may be.
