Nicolás Calabrese’s Summary

In 1997 I began studying Graphic Design in the University of Buenos Aires, and almost inmediatly I got into the interactive area of HK, a small design study where I made my first projects on web design.

In 2003 y traveled to the Dominican Republic, where I stayed for a year working in the interactive department of MSTN, a company that brings web solutions to very important local firms.

When I came back to Argentina, I was hired by SCI, where I worked designing email marketing pieces, interactive presentations and websites, for local clients as well as latin american ones.

In the end of 2005, I had the opportunity to work in an American company, Latin3, where with another colleagues developed banners, emails and websites for big multinational companies such as Dell and Nextel.

One year later, I received a work proposal from a former employer, and I returned to SCI, where I developed all the web related projects of the agency.

Since June 2008, I work at my own company "Web2", wich provides web design and development services and consultancy from Buenos Aires, Argentina. Please visit our site www.web2.la for more information.
