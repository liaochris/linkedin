Ray Hutchins’s Summary

Please feel free to connect with me at rh@raymondhutchins.com. I am a seasoned serial entrepreneur, internet marketer, and professional mentor with wide-ranging experience in many businesses and industries.

Through my business consulting company, RH & Associates (raymondhutchins.com), I provide companies, both large and small, with creative, cost-effective solutions to virtually any tough problem. I am an internet and search marketing specialist and have written two comprehensive reports that are available exclusively to my clients:

1. "The Domain Monetization Report"
2. "The New Search Marketing Paradigm"

As President of the SimGame Exchange (simgameexchange.com) and the Interactive Gaming and Simulation Alliance (IGSA.biz), I also provide services for business leaders in the interactive media industry.

Via the SimGame Exchange, I help businesses understand and use interactive gaming and simulations technologies to increase business performance. I have assembled a large investor network specific to the gaming space, and my company is the only video game recruiting firm that specializes in growing the Colorado market, so if you are a gaming professional and want to live in Colorado, please contact me.

In addition, I serve as Chairman of the award-winning TiE-Rockies Mentoring Program, which is responsible for helping entrepreneurs of all kinds launch and grow their companies in Colorado.
