Ron Overbeek’s Summary

Statement is a full service marketing and communication agency that develops strategic marketing and communication plans, for governments, ngo's, companies and organisations. Besides product introduction campaigns we also develop creative concepts for (e-)marketing and pr campaigns. A core team of seven consultants produces all sorts of media - within a network of specialists - such as tv, radio and cinema commercials, corporate films, short (web) clips, dvd's, websites (internet/intranet), e-newsletters, point-of-sale materials, folders, brochures, posters, advertisements, events, etc. Please check: www.statement.nl or www.statement.tv for more information.

Ron Overbeek’s Specialties:

Creative communication strategies (for marketing and public relations purposes) development of (e-)marketing, e-participation and public relations campaigns, media production and project management..

  • Strategic Communication Consultant

    Statement

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 1997 — Present (12 years 5 months)

    Statement is a full-service communication agency, situated in the centre of Groningen. Our consultants advise governments, companies and organisations how to achieve their goals by developing effective communication strategies. After that, Statement helps implementing these strategies, by producing creative concepts, texts and all media: films, radio-, tv- and cinema commercials, events, internet & intranet sites, e-newsletters, posters, folders, brochures, etcetera. Statement is a network organisation: we work with a core team of six consultants. Together with various business partners, specialists, freelancers and trainees from the University of Groningen we make powerful statements!

  • Radio host

    OOG Radio

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    January 1989 — Present (20 years 5 months)

    Since January 1989 Ron has been making a weekly radio show about the local popscene in Groningen. At first the show was called "Siberie Lokaal", but after a few years it was renamed into "Scherpe Oren". The show is broadcasted live, each Friday night, between 18 and 19 hrs, by OOG Radio. Since a couple of years the show has it's own website (www.scherpeoren.nl) and it's own live stream at www.oogradio.nl The formula is still 100% local. Scherpe Oren is Holland's Most Local Pop Show. Go check it and support your local heroes!
