Michelle Finegan Saunders’s Summary

Hello, I’m Michelle Finegan Saunders! I coach women and men toward designing a more fulfilling work / life balance for themselves! As a career transition coach, I help my clients get very clear on what they really want and need from life. My approach is very methodical, direct and focused on results.

During my 23 years of executive management experience in marketing and promotions, I’ve had the pleasure of teaching, mentoring, and helping many of my colleagues to achieve a more satisfying work and personal life. Now I bring my strengths and passion together to guide women and men to identify those elements of life that will bring them fulfillment and pleasure. I help my clients to design their lives for success by creating a detailed, achievable plan to reach their new goals and enjoy a more balanced work and family life.

I have completed over 100 hours of training at the Life Purpose Institute in San Diego and continue to participate in advanced training courses to further my skills as a career transition coach. I am a member of the San Diego Professional Coaching Alliance and The Women’s Alliance Network.

Change is inevitable. Why not make it a change you design—a change for a happier you? Design your life for success!

Michelle Finegan Saunders’s Specialties:

Professional coaching to help you maintain balance in your life, and attain what you want.
