Bob Roberts’s Summary

I have extensive experience in public education and planning in disaster preparedness and mitigation, sustainability, homeland security, business continuity, and international humanitarian law.

I've enjoyed being an instructor at many levels, with the Red Cross, FEMA, and currently on a contract basis for the Western Community Policing Institute at Western Oregon University doing certified Homeland Security classes across the country. I'm also a collaborator who believes that lines were meant to be blurred, boundaries crossed, and windows smashed into the sides of silos. Collaboration and communication are our biggest weapons against all the hazards we face.

Basically, all I want to do is save lives, prevent injuries, protect property, preserve the environment, and make the world a better place for us, our children, and our children's children. That's it. Oh, and find the perfect barbecue place.

Bob Roberts’s Specialties:

CISM certified. ICS/NIMS. BDLS. CERT Trainer. FEMA Exercise Design & Eval Instructor. DHS Instructor, Public speaking educator.

I am an extreme advocate of lifelong learning. The day there is nothing left to learn is the day I curl up and die. Fortunately, I don't anticipate that day coming for a long, long time.
