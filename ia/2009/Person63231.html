Diane Asyre’s Summary

Helping Businesses Meet Communications Obligations:
I have more than 20 years of experience in writing, editing, coordination of graphic design and fulfillment. My work covers the spectrum of business fields: academic, entertainment, nonprofit, for-profit, union and nonunion businesses. I've helped them to create and manager the communications involved with healthcare communications, mergers, acquisitions, divestitures, benefits policy updates, enrollment processes, etc.

Mentoring Communications and Human Resources Professionals:
Recruited, managed, grew revenue and profitability of award-winning communications group in a major consulting firm through three acquisitions. Worked with clients worldwide to improve employee communications. Projects were recognized with national, regional and local communications awards for creativity and application. Began the first national Human Resources consulting practice for the consulting firm.

Professional Speaker:
My experience ranges from preparing mock legal trials, participating in educational panels that emphasize the legal impact of Human Resources decision-making to serving as a go-to person for authors and interviewers.

Who Hires Me Most Often:
Vice-Presidents of Human Resources, CEOs, employee benefits specialists and and labor lawyers are those who most often hire me to research problem situations, design remediation and implement solutions.

I've worked with businesses you will recognize:
Monsanto, CMA St. Louis, Emerson Electric, Whirlpool, St. Louis Cinematheque, Civil Pictures Inc., Willis, Goody's Department Stores, Clark Oil, St. Louis Children’s Hospital, Teledyne, HON Industries, Thompson Coburn LLP, Laird Technologies
