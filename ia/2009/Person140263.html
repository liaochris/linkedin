Leslie Woodcock’s Summary

For 14 years I have helped clients who want my input to make a real difference to their business. I have improved their performance through strategic thinking, innovation and planning; strategic marketing planning techniques; and above all by interacting with and coaching the people and teams with whom I have had the privilege to work! It is said that my presence lingers long after I have gone.

"the real value we gained was a change of attitudes throughout the business"

"it is unlikley that we would have reached such heights without without the professionalism and enthusiasm of Enigma"

PERSONAL PROFILE
Seasoned change agent with multi-national experience; good negotiation and presentation skills; track record in sales and profit growth management and strategic planning.

Thrives on providing long-range vision to organisations; initiating change. Capable of rapid assimilation of complexities of new markets and sectors and of effectively transferring acquired skills to the analysis of complex problems, determining solutions and managing their implementation

Optimistic, influential, persuasive leader who manages directly and naturally generates enthusiasm in others in a team environment

CAREER HIGHLIGHTS
~ Managing Director of Daewoo "The Biggest Car Company You’ve Never Heard Of"
Responsible for the strategic and operational start-up of a new car company from scratch in 5 months
~ Pioneered Daewoo's revolutionary direct retailing strategy, by-passing traditional independent dealers to gain maximum channel control. Created platform to execute radical policies of true customer focus and accountability and thus dramatic differentiation for a "newcomer" in a crowded, competitive and mature market place
~ Strategic rationalisation and operational restructuring of pioneering, nation-wide, Personal Computer retailing and support services business. Eliminated losses - increased sales rate by more than 200% in 6 months
