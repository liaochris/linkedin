Willem Don’s Summary

Looking for a graphic designer, a Dutch-language editor or proofreader?
Do you need an article written or a lay-out developed?
Or, do you need advice on combining text and image?

Besides designing several publications I have experience in editing a wide variety of articles. I have a natural command of the Dutch language.

In my editing and designing, I can switch between being extremely meticulous, or very fast, whatever the job requires. Whilst always retaining the original ideas, I can:

• Design an accessible and stylish lay-out, tailored according to the publication concerned.
• Edit and proofread your documents for spelling, grammatical, and punctuation errors.
• Rewrite awkward sentences and restructure texts if needed.
• See the big picture: What is this text trying to achieve? Where will it be read? By whom?

For any kind of creative writing, editing, and graphical designing, do not hesitate to contact me. I will always approach my chosen projects with enthusiasm, reliability and creativity.

Willem Don’s Specialties:

Creating a page design that is soothing to the eye, without becoming dull to look at.~~~Writing enticing article-leads.~~~Advicing writers on writing reader-friendly: being concise, not boring.~~~Old-fashioned newspaper design: recognizing and recreating that feeling newspapers gave when they were still our main source of information.
