Michael Blaber’s Summary

Externally-funded academic research programs in protein folding and stability, protein engineering of fibroblast growth factor-1, and enzymology of kallikrein-related peptidases, with applications for human therapeutics. Goal-oriented management of Ph.D.-level researchers; training of graduate and undergraduate students. Grant and manuscript preparation; experienced peer-review of manuscripts and grants. Chemistry, biochemistry and biomedical instruction at undergraduate, graduate and medical student levels.

Michael Blaber’s Specialties:

Protein chemistry, biophysics, protein engineering, structural biology
