David Tomen’s Summary

It occurred to me that a university degree in economics and finance combined with practical experience in marketing and sales would be a good foundation for senior management. That proved to be true. Growing up on the water and learning to love to race yachts took me to the Caribbean. There I used my entrepreneurial skills and management experience to help build a start up cellular company whose initial focus was on the marine to become the dominant wireless carrier in the Caribbean.

I returned to the USA to take part in the early stages of the wireless Internet. That soon led me back to my main passion and I am now one of less than 450 Certified Professional Yacht Brokers in the United States (and possibly the world).

The yachting industry has been slow to take advantage of the world-wide reach of the Internet and new technologies. I intend to bring that industry together and get them connected online using new social networking technologies. I am also currently building my brokerage business focusing on what were formally considered "poor" areas of the world who are beginning to experience new wealth.
