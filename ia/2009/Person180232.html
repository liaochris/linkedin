Robert Padbury’s Summary

I am a User Interface Designer with a passion for creating beautiful, elegant and practical user interfaces. I am not afraid to knock down walls and light fires to create the best user experiences possible.

User Interfaces that I've designed are already being actively used by millions of people around the world.

I have a Bachelor of Science in Videogame Art & Design, and was awarded Best Portfolio for Winter 2008, nominated by the faculty of my university.

In addition to User Interface Design, I have strong skills in graphic design, high-level game design, motion graphics and creating environmental art for games. I have also worked in television, and journalism.

I have strong written and public speaking skills, and a strong passion for community. I have worked extensively with the San Francisco Chapter of ACM SIGGRAPH and in 2002 co-organized an event at my high school that contributed AUS$25 000 to help eliminate leprosy around the world.

I am eligible to work in Australia and the United States.

Robert Padbury’s Specialties:

User Interface Design, Game Design, Environmental Art for Games, Motion Graphics, and Graphic Design.
