Clayton McMartin’s Summary

The Clean Fuels Clearinghouse is an organization that delivers business solutions for cleaner fuels. The company was born of the true American Spirit – literally in an office over the family garage, with funding from a mortgage on the same house. Employing 11 terrific associates, this unique company is poised to grow even more in the near future.

Most recently the company has developed and operates the Nation’s only renewable fuel registry – known as RINSTAR®. Growing to process over 650,000 trades in the first 17 months, RINSTAR® interacts daily with over 500 companies in the renewable fuel supply chain. The RINSTAR® brand is most recognized for the high level of confidence it brings to the renewable fuel credit market through its patent pending certification processes.

A serial entrepreneur with a long-term strategic vision, Clayton has guided this company, and hundreds of others, in the motor fuel industry as they prepare for and profit in the ever changing arena of environmental regulations.

Clayton McMartin’s Specialties:

Clayton brings value to his clients with a thorough understanding of environmental regulations and practical knowledge of commercial operations. With experience in both upstream and downstream petroleum operations, as well as the burgeoning biofuels industry, he is able to see and quickly understand the big picture when it comes to America's shift to low carbon fuels.
