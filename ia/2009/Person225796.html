Subham Sarkar’s Summary

I have more than 8 years of experience in Business and Technology Consulting.I worked on various projects starting from large scale organization transformation to Business IT alignment for large group of companies in India.

I worked in Insurance, Government, Retail Education, Franchises, Telecom and IT Sectors.

My key experiences include:

1. Business Transformation for National Insurance Company of India (NIC): The third largest general insurer of India has undertaken a major transformation program. This involved:
- Process re-engineering of : Product Development, Underwriting, Claims, CRM, Marketing, Sales & Distribution, Funds Flow, Corporate Communications.
- Business and IT Alignment, and IT Strategy & Roadmap: A process driven solution architecture involving 18 application portfolios and Green-Technology Infrastructure architecture, effecting 1000 locations, 24 hubs, and more than 50000 users.
- Business case to assist NIC in decision making of an investment of more than 90 million USD for a 7 year term.
- System & Vendor Selection and Bid process management for the IT Implementation.
- Currently I am involved in the Project & Program Management of the initiatives under the strategic plan.

2. Business Process Re-Engineering for India's second largest retail education company - Aptech: This involved re-engineering of business processes for: New Product Development, Sales and Marketing, Logistics and Distribution, Cash flow management, Procurement, CRM, maintenance, Branding, etc. I also developed the long IT Strategy and Roadmap. Based on that system implementation and integration initiatives were undertaken.

3. IT Strategy & Enterprise Architecture (EA) advisor for ICDDR, Bangladesh: Designing detailed EAbased on IT Strategy, and proposing modifications in IT strategy for ICDDR, Bangladesh. This involved detailing of Business, Data, Application, and Technology Architecture

4. System Design & Implementation for more than 10 clients.
