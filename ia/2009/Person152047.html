Mark Grossman’s Summary

Experienced public relations professional, radio/TV producer, and government administrator, with an in-depth knowledge of the Long Island community. Excellent political network throughout New York State. Skilled in board development and board governance issues.

Current member of three Workforce Investment Boards: Hempstead-Long Beach, Oyster Bay-North Hempstead-Glen Cove, and Suffolk County.

Teacher, religion and culture classes, Mastic Beach Hebrew Center. Former board of education member, South Country Central School District. Former adjunct assistant professor, New York Institute of Technology. Former board president, Literacy Volunteers of America, Suffolk.

Mark Grossman’s Specialties:

Government relations, public relations, media relations, and community organizing. Web site development. Short form writing (op-eds, opinion pieces, issue essays).
