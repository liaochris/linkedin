Craig Boyers, CPA, MS’s Summary

Mr. Boyers is President of C. S. Boyers, PC CPA and C. S. Boyers Consulting, Ltd. Located in Waynesboro, Virginia, the CPA firm services clients in Waynesboro, Staunton, Augusta County, and throughout Virginia and other mid-Atlantic states. The consulting company serves clients across the United States.

C. S. Boyers, PC CPA provides income tax return preparation and tax planning services, payroll processing, bookkeeping and accounting, CFO/Controller temporary staffing, business management advisory, business needs analysis and future state evaluations, ERP and accounting software selection, and personal financial planning. Our accounting services include audits, reviews, and compilations, as well as, internal control and process reviews. C. S. Boyers Consulting, Ltd. sales, implements and supports Intuit QuickBooks, Intuit QuickBooks Enterprise Solutions, and Microsoft Dynamics GP (formerly Great Plains).

Mr. Boyers has over seventeen years of combined experience in public accounting, financial management and business systems consulting, including prior positions as a Chief Financial Officer, controller, and over ten years related to Microsoft Dynamics GP and Dynamics AX. His strengths include accounting theory, strategic planning, ERP systems design and implementation planning, and knowledge of financial, project and contract management, and manufacturing and distribution systems.

Mr. Boyers is a Certified Public Accountant and has also received certifications as a Microsoft Business Solutions Certified Master, a Microsoft Certified Business Management Solutions Specialist, a Microsoft Certified Professional, and a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer. He earned his Master of Science degree in Accounting from the McIntire School of Commerce at the University of Virginia, and he earned his B.S. degree from Eastern Mennonite University where he double majored in Business Administration and Accounting and minored in Computer Information Systems.
