Sindy Martin’s Summary

Sindy's mission in life is to help people trapped in a circle of unsuccessful networking, poor business etiquette, lack of sales, career changes, problem relationships, or addiction. She shows people how they can Open the Circle to success and happiness.

Sindy's life story is a true example of how Opening the Circle can change your life.

She is an award-winning speaker and trainer. Her fresh, fun and fast-paced style entertains, inspires, and motivates audiences of all ages.

Sindy posts a daily Smartin-Up Your Professionalism Tip on Twitter. Come follow her http://www.twitter.com/SindyMartin
