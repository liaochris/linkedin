Jean-Noël Goumain’s Summary

In the Information Technologies field, take up challenges, offer relevant and innovative solutions and contribute to the growth of the company I'm working for.


Some Achievements

- In the Healthcare IT field, conceived and supervised the development of Digimed-MD, an Electronic Medical Record able to communicate with medical devices. This software is licensed by Health Canada and the Ordre des infirmières et infirmiers du Québec.

- In the IT Management field, setup open or free solutions adapted to SMBs, such as Scalix (messaging), SugarCRM (customer relationship management) or XenServer (virtualisation).

- In the Quality Management field, implemented and maintained ISO 13485:2003, a standard derived from ISO 9001:2000 which is required by Health Canada for the manufacturers of Class II, III and IV medical devices. This certification was acquired within 6 months without any nonconformity.

- In the Signal Processing field, conceived an algorithm improving the spectral analysis of mobile sources.
