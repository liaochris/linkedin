    August 2000 — Present (9 years 3 months)

    Internet Consultants (ICs) may not be what you've come to expect when you think of Internet "experts", particularly if you've been approached by other Web Developers in the past. Not restricted to technology wizards, they come from a variety of backgrounds, giving you the benefit of their own business experience in addition to their Internet know-how. That's an important distinction, as ICs focus on delivering true business solutions, rather than simply creating websites.

    Another difference that sets ICs apart from others is their goal of creating an ongoing business relationship with you. It's almost common practice in this industry for a firm to design a website for a client and then move on to new business, never looking back. This may work well for the Web Design firm, but it certainly doesn't work well for you. In reality, websites are only the beginning - an IC's services will deliver far more value to your business and help you to achieve a real return on your investment.
