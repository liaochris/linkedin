Florian Peter’s Summary

Just Launched: The Mobile Trendpool - a one-stop portal combining the latest mobile innovations with big picture consumer trends to keep you one step ahead in the competitive mobile industry. Check it out: http://mobile.trendpool.com


Founded CScout in 1997. Initially positioned as Info Broker from US to Germany.

Developed into an international trend consulting network with offices in New York, Munich, Tokyo, and Beijing as well as 20+ scouts in global hotspots.

Clients range from Fortune 500 companies such as BMW and Vodafone to advertising agencies and management consultants.

Florian Peter’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    CScout

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    July 1997 — Present (12 years 4 months)

    CEO of CScout Inc, partner in CScout GmbH (German subsidiary). Oversee international business development, buildout of global scouting network. Key Account management of BMW and Siemens Mobile. Frequent speaker and participant at media and trend conferences in Europe.

  • U.S. Publisher

    Flyer Magazine

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Publishing industry)

    May 1998 — September 2000 (2 years 5 months)

    U.S. Publisher of the lifestyle magazine Flyer. Launching the magazine in colaboration with it's European founder Marc Wohlrabe as part of the international expansion from the initial six German cities (Berlin, Cologne, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg, Leipzig). Florian oversaw the international development leading to a total of 12 global cities (6 in Germany, Tokyo, New York, LA, San Francisco, Miami and Philadelphia) with a monthly combined circulation of 500k issues. Flyer was renowned for it's compact size, branded-covers, and grass-roots reporting on nightlife, urban culture and trends. In 2000 Florian devoted his attention to marketing the trends as a consulting practice and focused primarily on CScout.
