Eric Stern’s Summary

I am a web programmer who creates custom web sites and applications tailored to suit the exact needs of the client. I work closely with my clients to ensure that their expectations are met and that they can be successful with the applications I produce. I have worked as a systems analyst and am able to work with non-technical customers to understand their personal and/or business needs from a technical aspect. This has ranged from simple blog setup to custom inventory tracking tools.

Eric Stern’s Specialties:

Creation from scratch of dynamic websites custom-tailored to clients' needs. I focus on technologies in the LAMP stack, but am also familiar with Windows/.NET-based development. Specifics include: Object-oriented PHP, MySQL, valid XHTML, CSS, Javascript and AJAX, XML/XSLT, and cross-site integration through APIs and Web Services.
