Charles Krugel www.charlesakrugel.com’s Summary

Labor & employment law & human resources counseling practice. 14+ years of experience; 7+ years running my own practice.

Clients: Businesses in all industries; see my "Client Testimonials" page for more info., http://www.charlesakrugel.com/clienttestimonials

Juris Doctorate & a Master of Arts in Industrial/Organizational Psychology.

Published in the Labor Law Journal; frequently interviewed on TV, radio & in print publications (see my website for these interviews).

Prior experience: In-house labor & employment lawyer with the Chicago Public Schools, the YMCAs of Metropolitan Chicago & the Airline Pilots Association, representing United Airlines' pilots.

Community involvement & affiliations:

* Member Board of Directors, Greater O'Hare Association, one of Chicagoland's largest business associations, & one of Chicago's most important advocates for business. Also, member of the GOA Government Affairs Team.

* President & Member, Board of Directors, Youth Communication Chicago-multimedia publications about teens, for teens & by teens

* Chair of the Board & Member of Board of Directors for the YMCA Alliance, a Chicago-based job development & welfare-to-work job training agency affiiliated with the YMCAs of Metropolitan Chicago. Past Treasurer.

*Member Board of Directors, ChildServ, founded in 1894. A Chicago-based child advocacy, group home & adoption agency.

* Past: legal counsel for Bikers Against Child Abuse (BACA), Chicago chapter. Motorcyclists advocating against child abuse; and Volunteer "Big brother" with the Jewish Children's Bureau of Chicago.

* Member, Junior Board of Directors, Urban Gateways. UG is an arts education association based in Chicago.

* Member, Labor & Employment Relations Research Association, Chicago Chapter.

* Member, Chicagoland Chamber of Commerce (via The Human Resource Store). Member of the Employment Law Committee & the Workforce Education Quality Committee

* Member, World Trade Center Illinois
