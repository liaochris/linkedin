Olivia Burden (LION) 1000++’s Summary

I was educated at Southampton Solent University, graduating with a Bachelors degree in Business Information Technology.

After University I commenced my Recruitment career in Bahrain, as an Associate Consultant for Gulf Executives. Ijoined Gulf Executives as the first employee, where my key responsibility was to assist in the start up of the Executive Search Firm. I was responsible for supporting Consultants in all engagements for executive search and selection for various level of positions and sectors including HR, Banking, Finance, FMCG, & IT. I have had experience working in Kuwait on special projects, whilst also managing candidates from the GCC, Europe, USA & Asia.

My key achievements whilst working at Gulf Executives includes the Integration and administration of the company’s content management & database system and successfully assisting with the marketing of the company at its initial stages and beyond, using e-marketing, SEO Techniques, online recruitment sites & utilizing some of the leading business schools & Alumni’s e-job boards.

Whilst working with Gulf Executives I completed my SHL Level A qualification, which enables me to ability test candidates of all levels in any areas. A year after joining Gulf Executives I was promoted to Senior Associate Consultant, where I was responsible for training and managing a small team, whilst managing other areas such as searching, screening and short listing candidates profiles and utilizing employee referrals & personal contacts.

With hmy 18 months of recruitment experience, I joined Talent Partners Dubai working as Senior Research Associate, with core understanding and skills being;

 Regional experience across the GCC & 2 years working in Bahrain
 Sound knowledge of developing & managing candidate flow and ensuring best database operating practices are adhered to
 Technical background with a history of working in multi cultural teams
