eWorkPlace MN’s Summary

eWorkPlace is a state-sponsored program for metro area employers, funded by a state appropriation to Mn/DOT of $3.2 million. Its purpose is to reduce congestion on the highways in and around the Twin Cities by encouraging employers (big, small, private, public or nonprofit) to offer the option of teleworking. Teleworking enables employees to work from home or a remote location and connect to the office, coworkers and clients via the Internet and other technology. The program goal is to recruit and retain over 2,700 participants.
