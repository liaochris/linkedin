Developed personalized, custom portfolio analysis for HNW and Institutional clients and served as liaison between the investors and the fund managers. Been responsible for communication with institutional investors and family offices and marketing alternative investments across the board.

Experience launching, managing, raising assets and managing client relations for hedge funds and private equity offerings. Consult on several levels for a select group of institutional investment managers including hedge funds by raising assets and advising on structure, marketing process, marketing materials and operations.

Having worked at many levels of the alternative investment management business, it is clear that the rapidly growing industry is lacking the right tools to better navigate through increasing number of opportunities and hurdles.

I am currently focusing on leveraging my experience and resources to work on improving tools that will help investors avoid the landmines in the industry more efficiently. Imagineer’s customizable products currently address the areas of client relationship management, fund of hedge fund portfolio management, online investor reporting and website design, providing value by maximizing the efficiency of collecting, communicating and reporting information specifically associated with alternative asset management.

With a solid and growing client base, Imagineer’s solutions provide the right platforms for compliance, transparency and collaboration in hedge fund investing. My immediate goal is to expand their operations globally and create strategic partnerships with key industry players across the board.
