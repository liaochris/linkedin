Tommy Semereaux’s Summary

Cypress initially broke into the Staffing Services Market by providing recruiting assistance to the Brick and Mortar Retail Industry. The success within our Retail Portfolio led Cypress into the E-Commerce & Software Development Industry working with large marquee clients throughout the Silicon Valley and San Francisco Regions. The Full Suite of programs and services include Contingent Placement, Direct Hire / Retained Search, Employer of Record, and Master Service Provider Programs.

In an effort to maximize direct value and efficiency to our clients, we developed a flexible proprietary platform, COMPAS. Unlike the traditional and antiquated staffing models, COMPAS interfaces with all stakeholders that utilize the program. Built on a Software as a Service model, COMPAS delivers the following applications:
• Applicant Tracking
• Client Relationship Management
• Requisition Management
• Time & Attendance Management
• Task Management
• Document and Contract Management
• Applicant Careers Portal
• On Boarding & Off Boarding Management

The utilization of this centralized platform has enabled Cypress to provide clients with full reporting of all their services and metrics in real time. This gives Cypress a competitive advantage with regard to strategic staffing initiatives, and provides the ability to deliver a direct, measurable, and positive return on investment.
