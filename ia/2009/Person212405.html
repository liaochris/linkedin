Sean Carpenter’s Summary

Sean Carpenter is the Director of Training and Agent Development for the Ohio NRT Companies - Coldwell Banker King Thompson in Central Ohio and Coldwell Banker West Shell in the Greater Cincinnati area. Licensed with King Thompson in 1998, Sean led a successful career in real estate achieving over 10 million dollars in sales prior to becoming manager of the Coldwell Banker King Thompson Hilliard office in 2003.

Sean is the creator of the company’s highly regarded B.A.S.I.C Training class, and is a certified facilitator of both the Integrity Coaching and Integrity Selling courses as well as the Certified New Home Sales Professional course (CSP) through the National Association of Home Builders. Sean presented at the Coldwell Banker International Business Conference in 2007 (Las Vegas,) 2008 (Orlando) and 2009 in San Antonio. He was also a featured speaker at the 2008 Ohio Association of Realtors convention and appeared at the Coldwell Banker Global Management Summit in Jacksonville, Florida in October, 2008.

Sean will be the headline presenter at the Coldwell Baker Arkansas State Rally in April and will appear at the OAR Spring Conference as well.

In 2007, Sean helped to create the acclaimed CBOLT system - Coldwell Banker On-Line Training - which serves as a "green initiative" long distance learning platform that utilizes the Webex technology and alows associates and staff to increase their skills and knowledge from their home or office. Currently with over 100 classes on various topics from technology, prospecting, sales skills and personal development, CBOLT continues to offer on demand training opportunities to the Ohio NRT family.

Sean's hopes to educate, motivate and inspire new agents to achieve the levels of success they desire as they enter the real estate business and continue to encourage and challenge experienced agents to reach their goals and exceed their wildest expectations in the real estate business and life.
