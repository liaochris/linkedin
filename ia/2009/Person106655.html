Jenna Brossman’s Summary

Jenna Brossman is a senior at San Diego State University majoring in Public Relations. After transferring from Saddleback Community College having received her associate degree in Television, Film and New Media, she decided to focus on the study of public relations having seen the positive outcomes the profession could facilitate for all types of organizations.

During her time at SDSU, Brossman worked for two nonprofit organizations developing comprehensive PR plans in order to overcome specific challenges outlined by the clients. Furthermore, Brossman conducted primary research on SDSU's faculty and staff which included in-depth interviews and on-line questionnaire construction and dissemination for SDSU's University Relations and Development Department.

Brossman interned for a boutique PR agency in the summer of 2008 and she's currently an intern in the PR and Marketing Department at The Hotel Del Coronado.

In the future, Brossman wants to work in the PR field. She looks forward to having the opportunity to put her training and skills to good use.
