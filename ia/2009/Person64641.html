David Kerns’s Summary

My recruiting efforts focus within the chemical and life science markets where I conduct searches for clients involved in manufacturing everything from intermediates for the pharmaceutical market to the coatings and personal care markets. We have a strong network with CMO's, CRO's focusing on the pharmaceutical and biotech services market.

We have grown our office over the past two years to enable us to also conduct searches in the energy, consumer products and financial consulting industries as well.

We plan to continue to grow and develop our expertise within the markets we serve and would welcome the chance to confidentially network with you about your career interests and to keep you aware of positions we are working on.
