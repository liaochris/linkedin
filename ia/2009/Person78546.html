Benjamin Feist’s Summary

I am an interactive technology leader with many years of comprehensive experience in web, multimedia, and application development.

I've just started as Director of Technology at TAXI where I've been asked to help move TAXI into the Digital space in a big way by taking TAXI's famously good ideas and industry leadership into the online space. It's going to be a blast!

Prior to TAXI I was Senior Manager of Interactive Development at Sapient where I worked within a wide variety of disciplines to ensure that quality work was produced for our clients.

Prior to Sapient I managed the Flash development team at dthree as Associate Director of Technology, and helped to grow that company's capabilities greatly after joining them in December 2004.

Prior to dthree I was at Organic for five years where I built a number of Organic's more technically complex projects as both a technical team leader and a developer.

Prior to Organic, I was a Senior Systems Architect at VBDI (then part of Vickers & Benson Advertising), where I both lead and participated in the development of many large-scale Internet applications and interactive kiosks--this included the development of Canada's first online commercial lending system.

Benjamin Feist’s Specialties:

I'm constantly looking for the next big thing. I create technology agnostic hardware and software solutions by managing a team of top talent, but am not afraid to go back to my coding roots and pitch in.
