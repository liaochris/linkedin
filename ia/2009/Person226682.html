Supran Sarkar [LION]’s Summary

Over a decade of experience in different sectors like insurance, telecom, retail and services industry, played multifarious roles on Sales, Training & HR in the areas of strategic man power development and management, relationship management with C level executives, excellent team player for top insurance companies in India . Mostly India-based engagements but with good international exposure and experience, understands how insurance companies perform their operations and their strategy

Joined Landmark Retail as a part of Management Team for HR & Training Operation in the Western Province of KSA and heading the HR & Training to streamline the operation for LMG to enable manpwer development & employee welfare for the smooth running of business and had control over Manpower Attrition rate. At RGICL I was instrumental in migrating the Training needs as suites of core business applications used by RGICL in the Central region and also establishing a national presence in Training vertical in the first year of operation.

Prior to Landmark I was Regional Training Manager with Reliance General, A subsidiary company of Reliance Capital (ADA Group), one of the largest business houses in the country and was heading the the Training vertical for Central Region for Insurance Training and Vendor Management. I had also work extensively on content development and has been instrumental in setting up some of the well known multimedia contents rolled out Pan India. I had also worked as a Cluster Training Manager with ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, managing team, vendor management & responsible for TNA & Activisation of channel partners on behalf of Sales team, I have got extensive experience on content development with Landmark Retail, Reliance General, ICICI Prudential Life, Bajaj Allianz Life, Bharti Airtel and implementing the modules after identifying the training need analysis.

I am a certified Licentiate from Insurance Institute of India, Hyderabad.
