Gavin McDermott’s Summary

Based in Phoenix, Arizona, HyLo as a concept and company grew from many conversations shared with business owners and media companies over the years. As we studied the market, it became increasingly obvious that although customer mobility and smartphone adoption was increasing, businesses were growing more confused about how to effectively reach this large market. At HyLo, we realized that the process of going mobile just was too expensive, time consuming, and complicated. A change was needed.

So we decided to develop our own answer to going mobile. Enter HyLo.
