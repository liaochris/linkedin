    (Biotechnology industry)

    January 2000 — Present (9 years 11 months)

    Cell Sciences offers immunochemicals for life science research. Recombinant cytokines, cytokine receptors, chemokines, kinases, phosphatases, ELISA and ELISPOT kits, monoclonal and polyclonal antibodies, and matched antibody pairs for do-it-yourself ELISA and ELISPOT assays are available.
