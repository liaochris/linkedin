Steven HUSAK’s Summary

Mr. Husak joined the CBK Styles company, a member of the Blyth, Inc. family of companies as Regional Vice President in March, 2008. His region was expanded to include Central America, Florida, the Caribbean and Southern Georgia. In August 2008 he was transferred to handle all strategic and e-commerce accounts for those areas plus all states east of the Mississippi River and Europe. This also involves the launch of a new drop ship program in support of those initiatives.

Steven is a sought after speaker, having been the keynote speaker for conventions and annual general meetings for organizations such as the Society of Interior Designers (ASID and BSID)and the Physiotherapists Association (BPA) on topics such as effective use of space vs. efficient use of space and ergonomics. He has been awarded numerous industry awards for customer focus and sales volume.

He is also sought after as a consultant by such financial powerhouses as Palmyra Capital, Friess Associates, McKinsey & Company, Franklin Resources, Soundpost Partners, Eminence Capital, Greenlight Capital, TowerBrook Capital Partners, AEA Investors & MSD Capital.

Prior to joining Brighton as Managing Director in 2001, he was Vice President & General Manager with US Office Furniture, a Country Manager with Steelcase International, and President of Garden State Insurance. He purchased Brighton in July 2002.

Before joining Blyth, Inc., Steven was Baer's top producer at their 83000 sq.ft. flagship showroom. He has experience in Bermuda, the Caribbean, Europe, Asia and the USA, primarily in turnarounds and consulting in the residential and commercial furniture industries.

Steven HUSAK’s Specialties:

Throughout his career Mr. Husak has shown a penchant for "making customers want to do business" through his unique style of customers first marketing.

Steven has led successful turnarounds of failing or underperforming companies and business units in Europe, North America and Asia, primarily in the Commercial Furniture, Residential Furniture and Accessories industries.

Currently he is working to develop e-commerce and catalog accounts to market his company's products on-line.
