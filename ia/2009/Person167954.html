"There is no use whatever trying to help people who do not help themselves. You cannot push anyone up a ladder unless he is willing to climb himself."
-Andrew Carnegie

One of my personal missions is to empower other business owners with the vision, training, support and tools necessary to be successful. I have expertise in the development of sales networks, social media, business development, technology, personal care and nutrition. In the last 24 years of I have partnered with thousands of business owners around the world and generated hundreds of millions of dollars in revenues and profits.

I have been fortunate enough to become of one of the most successful distributors in Direct Sales history. At the age of 21 I began my business career. In just a few short years I had already earned my first million dollars.

More than 24 years later my initial efforts have produced a global sales organization that has created hundreds of top business leaders who have earned more than one million dollars. Currently I consult and provide training for several hundred thousand business partners in more than 40 international markets.

I also work exclusively with a small team of business partners that are currently launching their businesses in numerous international markets.

I am a highly-recognized trainer, business coach and mentor to top-performing business owners around the world. I am an industry consultant and have specialized skills in social media and marketing.
