Bill Crandall’s Summary

Highly accomplished brand marketing, advertising and new business development executive. Exceptional new business track record in securing introductions for credentials and capabilities presentations and competitive brand reviews. Current contacts with all major U.S. agency search consultants and selected Fortune 500 companies at C-level. RFP and cost proposal skills second-to-none.

Solid research, branding, advertising, sales promotion, PR, internet and related social media communications experience at both largest and smallest of agencies, across a wide variety of product and service categories. Core experience includes consumer packaged goods, hospitality, travel & tourism, consumer electronics, media & entertainment, fashion & apparel, retail, transportation and government. Specialized expertise in luxury and consumer lifestyle brands.

Bill Crandall’s Specialties:

New product introductions and line extensions, brand positioning, repositioning and/or revitalization, and C-Level account management. New business development foremost.
