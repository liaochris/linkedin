Tom Whitehead’s Summary

I am a success-driven marketing professional with an accomplished track record of leading and motivating diverse teams to achieve strategic objectives. I have extensive experience in consumer, business to business, and services marketing environments, and have successfully launched a large number of new products into the market utilizing a variety of gate-based methodologies.

My career is comprised of progressively responsible positions in Marketing, Product Development, and Product Management at both national and regional levels. I have been fortunate to work at organizations that possess sharp focus on customer needs. As a result, I have extensive experience in turning Voice of the Customer data into innovative, customer-focused solutions.

I have specific experience managing P&L, staff management and development, cultivating and managing licensing relationships, communications strategy, cost control, process improvement, and solving large complex business problems.

I possess excellent analytic and problem solving skills, combined with strong communication and interpersonal abilities.
