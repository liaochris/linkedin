Christopher Grant’s Summary

Designer, user experience specialist and web project leader. Since 1996, has worked and consulted for startups and multi-nationals in the US and across Europe.

Deeply interested in user psychology and motivation, specifically relating to participation in virtual communities. Developed the concept of "digital altruism" based on experience recruiting and leading an online community of 2,000 expert users for Emagister.com.

Now a part of the Sclipo team, building a new version of this award-winning social learning network for teaching through video and webcam.
