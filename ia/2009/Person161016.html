Michael Conti’s Summary

Michael Conti is seasoned on the tactical side of getting things done within the rapid pace and fluid environment of today's ever changing business demands and expectations on digital media.

With over 20 years of experience in account management services for video, web, live events, consumer products and print. I utilitized a stregetic approach to marketing within a variety of digital and traditional spheres, specifically utilizing web 2.0 with streaming video, podcasts, rss feeds, blogspheres.

I have also worked as a producer, editor, director and educator in film and video. I hold a Bachelor's degree in English from Colorado College and attended filmmaking programs at the American Film Institute in Los Angeles.

  • Executive Director

    The Shoot Out 24 Hour Filmmaking Festival Boulder

    (Non-Profit; Myself Only; Entertainment industry)

    July 2004 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    I brought the first ever 24-hour filmmaking festival of its kind to Boulder, Colorado, in October of 2004. At that first event, nearly 400 people attended, divided into 74 teams, I executive produced 66 short films in just 24 hours. The event included several mini-events including a sponsor party, a breakfast bar, preliminary judging venues and an after party. I enlisted the help of over 50 volunteers, and filled the historic Boulder Theater to capacity (900 seats) for the big event screening of the top ten films of the contest. The event has been voted the Best New Film Festival by Westword in 2004, and Best Film Festival in 2007, and has received wide regional press coverage.
