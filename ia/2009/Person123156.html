Judith Currin’s Summary

In the 1970's anything seemed possible and I started a glamourous career as a bricklayer's apprentice. For the next eight years I laid bricks in the summer in Virginia and I spent my winters in south Florida bartending. I fell 35 feet one day and hurt my back. While recovering I realized the '70's were long gone, so I packed my shoulder pads and moved to NYC. I began my NY career at Ogilvy & Mather. My work history in NY includes Public Relations positions at both O&M and A&E Television Networks; TV Production experience at A&E as a Programming Coordinator; extensive advertising awards show experience and expertise as the Awards Coordinator for OgilvyOne worldwide; and Interactive Project Management experience at OgilvyInteractive, DIGITAS, R/GA, FCBi (now DraftFCB), G2 Interactive and Beyond Interaction (now MediaCom Interaction.) I am also a Licensed Massage Therapist in NY and a former Stand-Up Comic with Comedy Central, A&E, and Starz Comedy Channel credits.

I'm a finisher, a proven outcome driver, and I just plain get things done.

I'm on the lookout for a team which shares common goals and a vision for the future. In advertising the work is predictable, but the people you work with determine whether you have a life you can live with every day. I like to have fun while I get people to do things they don't necessarily want to do. I have no agenda. I love to make people laugh - it's what I like about life.
