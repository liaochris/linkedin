Jennifer Mahar Mulder’s Summary

Meeting Planner Resources, LLC is the publisher of Meetings and Events Las Vegas a bi-annual publication distributed nationally and internationally by request to Business Meeting Planners that are planning an event or meeting in the Las Vegas area. As the Associate Publisher, Jennifer has overseen the editorial, advertisements, marketing, circulation and the dedicated team at the publication for over 4 years. Relocation Resources publishes local relocation guides that are distributed by request to corporate re locations, individuals and families relocating to the Las Vegas Valley prior to their arrival.
