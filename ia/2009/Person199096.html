Rise Gluck’s Summary

Creative, innovative designer with many years of experience designing for the textile and carpet industries.

I design rugs and create fabric designs for all areas of the home textiles industry. I am an efficient, dedicated worker with excellent communication and organizational skills. I excell at interpreting clients' ideas with the end result being a beautiful product. Please contact me so we can speak in more detail about what I can do for you.

Rise Gluck’s Specialties:

Computer skills - Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Ned Graphics, QuarkXPress, Pagemaker.

Painting and sketching by hand.

Exceptional design and color sense.

Team player, work well under pressure, meet deadlines, self-motivated, multi-task well, able to work independently and with others, adaptable, welcome challenging and difficult projects, dependable, communicate well with others, bring out creativity in others, detail oriented, perfectionist.

  • Senior Rug Designer

    Patterson, Flynn and Martin

    (Design industry)

    November 2001 — February 2009 (7 years 4 months)

    • Created original designs and color concepts for the Patterson, Flynn & Martin and Schumacher lines for distribution to the company’s corporate and outside showrooms across the country. Many designs were top sellers and featured in industry magazines.

    • Computer generated and hand-painted renderings in scale.
