Renee Heath’s Summary

I have worked in both the public and private sectors to help organizations gain business and exposure both on and off line. I deal with local businesses, SMEs, national companies, to corporations that have a global reach.

I currently help clients through various channels to gain more business online, some of which include: SEO, PPC, Email & Affiliate Marketing, Display Advertising, ePR, Social Network and Viral Marketing.

My marketing strategies are all based around ‘direct’ marketing as opposed to ‘blanket’ marketing principles; i.e. my customer acquisition strategies are based upon costs of acquiring customers (CPAs) and associated life time value calculations.

I also help clients online with Accessibility Compliancy, Back-end Integration, Competitor Analysis, Content Creation & Distribution, CMS Systems, Digital Branding, Ecommerce, Hosting, Landing Page Design, Link Building, Multi-Channel Design, Natural Search, Rich Media Content, Technology Platforms, Usability, User Experience, Web Analytics, and Web Design.

In addition to the above I help companies offline with Branding & Marketing, Public Relations & Public Affairs, Graphic Design & Print, Digital Media & Photography, Internal Communications & Training, Advertising, and Events & Exhibitions.
