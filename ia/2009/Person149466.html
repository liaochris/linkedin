Marc Meyer’s Summary

I have held positions ranging from web developer, internet marketing specialist, software sales representative, and sales manager, to positions of VP of sales, VP of marketing, Dir. of Internet marketing and Social Media and SEO, to Dir. of e-commerce and CTO.

Those companies include The Internet Consulting Group, Executrain, Computer Enterprises Inc., Marblejar, Mind Over Media, Emerson Direct and Digital Response Marketiing Group.

My current experience over the last 2 plus years, has been focused on using interactive components to server social media in the direct response marketing space; Primarily to drive traffic,create brand buzz and enhance reputation management. This has included evangelizing and writing extensively about social media and marketing on my nationally recognized blog, to me taking a more of a consultant and educational type of role with numerous companies on how best to implement social media into their overall digiital marketing plan.

In parallel I continue to focus as well on search engine optimization, search engine marketing, email marketing and brand management for consumer products through our direct response marketing company.

My sales and marketing background consisted of being one of the top sales people at CEI (Computer Enterprises Inc.) for almost 3 years. CEI is an application development, integration and management services company in which I generated over $9 million in revenue and was consistently a leader in everything from call volume, to opportunities uncovered, to training new hires, to closing sales.

Prior to that I cut my teeth in various tech sales and marketing positions, learning, growing and understanding all things related to web development, internet marketing and software sales as I went along.

As we enter into the challenging and exciting unchartered waters of social media and the enterprise web 2.0 environment, I think I am uniquely qualified to guide and lead companies into the deep end.
