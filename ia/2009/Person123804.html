Julie Franki’s Summary

As an experienced Art Director and Designer, I'm fluent in many mediums.

For PRINT:

-Strategized new and enhanced existing brand identities with a consistent, deliberate connection to core mission.

-Developed advertising campaigns, brand identities, collateral and display materials and graphic standards for a broad variety of industries including publishing, health care, arts, finance, social expression, hospitality, entertainment, travel, food and beverage, non-profit, real estate, research and consulting.

-Served as lead Art Director/Designer for agency-of-record accounts such as Physicians Plus, Dane County regional Airport, and Regional Cancer Collaborative (Prevea).

For ELECTRONIC MEDIA:

-Proficiently operated the Adobe Creative Suite applications for press-ready production as well as Windows Office (for PC-based clients needing user-friendly templates).

-Storyboarded and produced HTML-compatible Flash and non-Flash-based web sites, ad banners, e-mailers, and on-line exhibits, in collaboration with back-end programmers. Successfully joined brand identity with a fluid, user-friendly interface.

For BROADCAST:

-Co-scripted/directed 60-second radio spot for Physicians Plus Insurance event.

-Concepted and storyboarded 15-, 30- and 60-second TV s-pots in collaboration with writer and creative director for Jimmy Johns Gourmet Sandwiches, Regional Cancer Collaborative (Prevea) and Physicians Plus.

For INTERIOR:

-Designed interior signage to refresh brand message at
The QTI Group, Inc. headquarters in Madison.

-Made scale mechanical elevation and plan drawings for three-dimensional exhibit space.

-Envisioned the overall look and maintained graphic continuity for museum exhibits through floor plans, identity graphics, case designs, scale mechanical drawings, publicity materials and on-line exhibits.
