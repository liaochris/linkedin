Vivek Sehgal’s Summary

I currently work with the world's foremost supply chain solutions company as a Director in the R&D. My role is to build supply chain solutions that will continue to lead the supply chain solution space for years to come, and continue positioning my company for success.

My previous work experience includes various leadership positions at Fortune 20 companies such as The Home Depot, and GE and other leading supply chain companies such as i2 Technologies. I have skills in all areas of supply chain from planning to execution, and have helped several large and small companies in strategic supply chain planning, deployment and realization of direct benefits.

My functional experience includes:
• Supply Chain Management (Demand, Supply & Replenishment Planning, Warehouse & Transportation Management, Inventory and Purchase Management). Enterprise business applications (ERP).
• Enterprise Architecture, Strategic IT Planning, Control and Governance, TOGAF, SAP EAF
• Business Intelligence and Data Warehousing
• Product Management and Development, Project Management (PMP)

My business applications experience includes:
• SAP-SCM, SAP-GTM (Global Trade Management), SAP for Retail
• Manhattan Associates (Transport Planning & Execution, Performance Management, Warehouse Management, Yard Management, Slotting, Labor Management, Trading Partner Management)
• Troux Enterprise Architecture suite ( consisting of Troux Architect, Troux Metaverse, Troux Semantics, and Troux Explorer)
• i2 (Supply Chain Planning, Demand Planner, Inventory Planner, Profit Optimizer, Supply Chain Analytic Solutions)
• Siebel Analytics (Opportunity and Order Management)
• Logility Voyager (Demand Planning, Supply Planning and Performance Management)

I have a pending patent on the purchasing costs optimization process; and have authored several papers on supply chain planning, purchasing, data-mining and multi-dimensional data modeling for enterprise strategic planning.

Vivek Sehgal’s Specialties:

Supply chain planning and management for Retail, Distribution, and Manufacturing Industries. Enterprise Architecture and strategic planning for Retail, and Software Vendors.
