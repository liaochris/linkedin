Brad Simek’s Summary

Over the past year, Human Capital Management, Inc has gone through exciting changes and extreme growth. HCMI has become part of Intelligent Capital Network, a network of dynamic firms aimed to utilize a unique blend of talent and insight to understand the human talent landscape that affects your business. By blending that knowledge capital with strong acquisition methodologies, we produce a strong and timely answer for our clients’ business issues. As ICN, we now offer staffing services for Marketing, IT, and C-level professionals as well as have an in-house consultancy which is able to form full project teams to complete IT and Marketing initiatives for our clients. Together, Intelligent Capital Network provides a unique and powerful solution to fuel the growth of our partners.
