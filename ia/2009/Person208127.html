Samson Mow’s Summary

Samson is currently responsible for operations and business development at Sitemasher Corporation. His role at Sitemasher also includes the management of the web development division and technical support department. Samson joined Sitemasher from the games industry where he was an associate producer at Relic Entertainment (THQ). His most recent project at Relic was the "Company of Heroes Online" MMO RTS, a joint venture between THQ and Shanda Interactive Entertainment and also the first MMO game in THQ's product line. Prior to that, Samson was part of Relic's online division and responsible for the development of online platforms and infrastructure. Samson also staffed and managed Relic's game balance department which was a key driving factor in the quality of Relic's games.

Samson holds a bachelor's degree in business administration from Simon Fraser University in Vancouver and has also studied in China at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
