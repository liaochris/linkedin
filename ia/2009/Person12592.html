Anand Desai’s Summary

We started as an HR assessments and recruitment processing organisation way back in Jan 2006. The start was rocky and challenging. Simultaneously we were working on the idea of career development for students. Our aim was to build a socially useful, commercially viable and professionally satisfying business proposition.

Our work told us that it’s not just the lack of career development which is a concern in India, but lack of career development initiatives that has led to un-employability and low return on investment in education. Hence we launched India’s the first career assessment portal www.dheya.com focussed on career assessment, career planning, career development.

Today we are an organisation focussed on Competency Assessment career assessment, planning and development and employability
Our corporate Services include competency design, competency assessment, designing assessment tools, designing and conducting assessment centres and development centres. ( www.assessbq.com). We have build the most comprehensive competency models for our corporate customers and designed and implemented competency mapping and tools.

Our Education Services include career assessment, career planning and career development for students in 8th grade to post graduate levels. (www.dheya.com ) Our intellectual property includes the assessment we have developed (Seven ability tests, Two personality assessments, One interest inventory). We have developed a matching engine that helped us in matching the results of the assessments to 600+ careers we monograph. We have a web content of 20000+ pages that includes information on 600+ careers, 250+ areas of education, 140+ degree programs, 270+ specialisations.

Our rural employability initiatives include building life skills and personal competence among rural and tribal youth. We work with the local schools/ colleges and individuals (leaders) to build employability and life skills in young tribal and rural individuals.
