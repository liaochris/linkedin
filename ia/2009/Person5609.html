Adrien Jolly’s Summary

Test and measurement Specialist, LabVIEW, LabWindows/CVI, TestStand expert, automated test bench designer, I offer my services for the development of your validation and automation systems by developing the software and hardware. Working offshore, my prices, below the European market, are ideal for firms wishing to increase their capacity in term of volume as much as technicality.

www.ajolly.fr/english

My experiment is based over 10 years of test and measurement systems realization for customers of various sectors; here are some examples:

* Messer (Petrochemistry and pharmacology )
Design and realization of gas quality measurement system.

* Lafarge (Construction)
Design of a sand quality control system for cement manufacture.

* Intertechnique, Zodiac group (aeronautical equipment)
Design and system development of automated avionics calculators tests (Airbus, Boeing, Bomber, Macdonald Douglas , Embraer...), breakdown codes analysis software, calculators eeproms management and in particular project leader of the validation system of a electric power management calculator of A380 plane.

* Schlumberger (energy oil)
Test benches and calibration system of the oil probes, optical coders test Sequencer.

Mobile test bench for high voltage transformers (treatment of the signal and analyzes partial loads)
