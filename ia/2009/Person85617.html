Gerald Roden’s Summary

Multidisciplinary strategic and operational experiences in the watch industry at the service of international groups. Proven track records in definition and implementation of marketing strategies as well as in industrial reengineering of companies within multicultural environment focusing on results.

On some occasions, the reengineering of companies has been achieved in the middle of tough human and psychological environment.

Gerald Roden’s Specialties:

- Elaboration of business plans taking into account group or
shareholders strategies,

- Definition and set-up of marketing strategies including all aspects of the marketing mix, including product development,

- Set up of teams of professionals and management of their performances within a financial, administrative and Total Quality organisation,

- Implementation of an industrial strategy, evaluation of make or buy, integration of strategic know-how, set-up of strategic alliances.
