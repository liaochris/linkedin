Tim Lea’s Summary

Have been involved in the structuring & delivery of Working Capital facilities for over 20 years - both here in Australia and Internationally. The facilities have typically been geared towards funding "change events" (e.g. MBO's & Acquisitions) and business growth, both as lender and adviser.

We are passionate about sourcing facilities that do NOT require Real Estate Security and focus upon:

• Receivables Finance (including factoring)
• Inventory Finance / stock finance
• Asset Based Lending Finance
• Cashflow Finance
• Short-term finance

Deal sizes have varied from start-ups & re-structures through to acquisition and MBO finance in the mid-market space (up to $60m).

Our objective is always the same - Maximise the cash available from our panel of lenders for the LEAST amount of security - all within a sensible pricing matrix.

If you are not sure about how many of the facilities work, please check out the Learning Centre in our web-site where we have created a series of introductory videos (5-7 mins each).

In the interim, happy to chat through any scenarios to explore options available. We're always prepared to think outside the box - especially the 4-walled box ! Please feel free to give me a call (0415 556653)
