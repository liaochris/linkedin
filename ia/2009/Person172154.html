Hussein Natsheh’s Summary

Mr. Hussein T. Al Natsheh is currently the general manager and the founder of Ciapple. Mr. Al- Natsheh is also the head of Trizmatic institute which is a division of the Queen Rania Center for Entrepreneurship, Amman, Jordan. He has a Master degree in computer engineering from Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST). Previously, he was the technology commercialization program manager at QRCE for more than two years. Before that he worked as a technical support engineer after he had his B.Sc. degree in computer engineering from Al Balqa' Applied University (BAU), Jordan. He is also the founder and ex-chair of BAU Institute of Electronic and Electrical Engineers (IEEE) student branch. He had presented a several trainings on leadership, teamwork, entrepreneurship, networking, and presentation skills in El Hassan Science City, Jordanian Engineers Association, JUST, and BAU. Voluntarily, he is the students activities committee chair at the IEEE Jordan section. His main interests are systematic innovation, Intellectual Property (IP), artificial intelligence, and knowledge-based economy.

Artificial Intelligence (AI), data mining, knowledge-based economy, systematic innovation, entrepreneurship development, and technology commercialization.
