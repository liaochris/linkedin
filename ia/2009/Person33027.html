Brian Alcorn’s Summary

I’ve been leading Web design initiatives for ten years for clients ranging from small start-ups to Fortune 500 companies, in industries ranging from consumer packaged goods to technology to media.

Hard skills: strategy, information architecture, interaction design, user research.

Soft skills: product innovation, project leadership, empathy for users, communication and client service.

I love working through tough problems, balancing business requirements, user goals, and development needs, and bringing it together in an elegant design solution.

Brian Alcorn’s Specialties:

Web products for media companies, esp. news organizations
