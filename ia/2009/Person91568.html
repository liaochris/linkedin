Matthew D. Hagarty, JD’s Summary

Matthew D. Hagarty, AOL LLC., Dulles, VA. Matthew originated and currently manages the Corporate e-Discovery Services group and forensic team responsible for ESI identification, preservation, collection and production for civil litigation and regulatory inquiries at AOL LLC. Matthew is a member of The Sedona Conference Working Group on Electronic Document Retention and Production (WG1), and recently co-edited The Sedona Conference® Commentary on Preservation, Management and Identification of Sources of Information that are Not Reasonably Accessible, and is presently working on the The Sedona Conference® Cooperation Proclamation. Matthew also speaks at educational events (including CLE) and legal conferences across the country.

Mr. Hagarty received his B.A. degree from James Madison University, M.A. degree from Georgetown University, and his J.D. from George Mason University School of Law. He is licensed in Washington, D.C.
