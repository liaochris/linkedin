Nick Van Wagnen’s Summary

I got my start in the printing industry where I had a great introduction to production processes, sales and design. After this experience, I moved on to Homedics, Inc. Here I was able to further my design experience laying out instruction manuals for six product categories and taking care of home page graphics for three websites.

My most recent employment has been with Brogan & Partners. Here, I was able to do double duty as a copywriter and art director, as well as having the opportunity to do some video editing and a little bit of photography.
