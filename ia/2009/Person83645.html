Gary Comerford’s Summary

I provide consultancy services for organisations wishing to implement or improve their Business Process Analysis and Managament capabilities. I can work with groups or departments from the board level down, to define what their BP structure should look like, help facilitating sessions where processes are defined, and round off the whole service by providing consultancy on the implementation of those processes within the organisation.

Hailing from a background in Financial Systems application implementation, I also have extensive Financial and IT Audit experience, as well as being a key resource in the area of Business Process definition and implementation. Assignments in the past include managing a European team of Enterprise Architects, defining and implementing a European governance framework for process and architecture modifications, and I also have broad experience in all aspects of auditing, process management and project management.

Direct experience includes business process design through facilitation, business process software implementation and I have also presented at several regional and local business process conferences on this subject.

I am able to talk to all levels of the organisation from senior management right down to the shop floor workers

Gary Comerford’s Specialties:

Business Process Analysis: "ProVision" process modelling expertise: Rummler and Brache Methodology knowledge: Six Sigma experience. Presentation and training skills: Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
