Arturo Busleiman’s Summary

I've been developing and learning GNU/Linux since late 1994. I started programming when I was very young, 8 years old. I think of myself as highly experienced in the field of Linux and Network security, systems programming, and eMail Server Deployment.

A typical day of my life begins with an analysis of all my servers (the ones I own and my customers'), going through my inbox, checking different security sites, IT news, and of course Slashdot. Once I finish the morning routine, which does usually include programming for different FLOSS and private projects, I rest for a while.

OK, I don't rest. I just read lots of books on programming, security, participate in different online forums and mailing lists, etc.

As I work at home, I can stop any time and watch some Star Trek, Friends or Moonlighting. Although I have to admit I die for Extreme Makeover, Scrubs and... Project Runway :$

I share most of the day with my wife, who's also a Geek just like me :)

The rest of the day is devoted to the same activities, although I try to learn new technologies every day, compose some music, and have a social life :)

I think I should add "work-out" some day, too. My belly's getting bigger and bigger. Heh.

Anyway, to summarize, you might like to hire me when you:

* Need someone that can answer any IT question you might have, or can give you precise directions of whom to contact.
* Have Bandwidth / Spam issues and need to solve them (remember: extra bandwidth usage is usually CHARGED by ISP and Hosting companies).
* Need a Linux expert.
* Need very specialized programming.

I have several associates round the globe, so I'm sure I/we can fulfill any of your needs.

You might wish to read some comments form my customers. Feel free to visit:
http://www.buanzo.com.ar/pro/ - The site is in Spanish, but there are comments from my English-speaking customers, too.

Well, thanks for taking the time to read this summary!
