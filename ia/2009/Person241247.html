Jim Baran & Kristi Boyer (LION) 1700+ Connections’s Summary

Twitter: Follow VSL on Twitter: LEANVSL

Value Stream Leadership is retained executive recruiting firm dedicated to building and growing LEAN business systems. Our LEAN community network consists of 16,000+ senior and mid-level lean leaders on 4 continents in over 120 industries. All share one objective: advance the application of the Toyota Production System (TPS) and the Toyota Product Development System systemically and culturally in their respective organizations.

Since 1995, our firm has helped hundreds of companies hire distinctive LEAN leadership talent with systemic and cultural change management experience and success in achieving sustained results, increasing revenue and growing businesses.

Lean is more than identifying waste. It's about people. Why waste time with search firms who work buzzwords, not people? It's one thing to utter "respect for people". It's another thing to demonstrate it daily.

Our placement successes span the enterprise with impressive talent matches in product development, production, supply chain, and transactional administrative functions.

Jim's background includes 13 years as North American Talent and Management Development Leader for Volkswagen and 2 years as Director, HR for a Tier One Automotive Supplier. Jim also owned HRXPRESS - a management development, talent succession planning, and LEAN recruiting firm. Beyond LEAN, Jim's passions include: fly fishing, fly-tying, innovation,reading, and photography.

Kristi's background includes several years in motorsports public relations before joining Jim in 1999 in the role of business development - now Partner/COO. Kristi manages our staff of six lean talent specialists.

Relevant terms: Kaizen, Lean, Continuous Improvement, A3, JIT, Toyota, TPS, TPM, Change Management, Lean Employment, Lean Careers, Lean Recruiting, Lean Recruiter, Hiring Lean Leaders, Standard Work, lean production, lean engineering, lean business system.
