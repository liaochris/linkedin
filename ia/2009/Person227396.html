Mark Sutton’s Summary

Experienced leader with track record of building and growing revenues and users for consumer & B2B internet, e-commerce and online publishing businesses. Strong user centric approach and expertise an business development, product marketing and online marketing. Experience as a business unit leader with P&L responsibility and as well as building and managing teams. Key accomplishments include building an online fundraising business that has raised >$100 million for charity, forged and managed executive level relationships with fortune 500 brands that generated 80% of company revenue, developed positioning and go to market plans for web based services and iPhone applications. Adapted online marketing and social media approaches to product launches and lead generation. Strength at working cross functionally in high growth and agile work environments.

Mark Sutton’s Specialties:

Defining and executing go to market strategic plans. Product marketing of online and new media products, Business Development (strategic partnerships, early stage sales and major client management), Strategic positioning including use case development, competitive and market research, Online marketing (SEO, SEM, Webinars, affiliate marketing, blogs and direct marketing), Social media marketing (Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube), Non profit technology, major account management and e-commerce.
