Benjamin Malkin’s Summary

Attorney with a strong leadership and business acumen and significant counseling, drafting and negotiating skills. Particular emphasis on issues and opportunities that are specific to businesses and their officers, directors and owners in matters ranging from everyday operational concerns to guidance on significant events in the lifecycle of their businesses, including those relating to contract review and negotiation to advice on employment matters to assistance on real estate and intellectual property matters, as well as guidance on general legal compliance.

Benjamin Malkin’s Specialties:

I represent parties in the private offering of equity and debt securities. I also draft and negotiate agreements for the purchase and sale of business assets and/or equity interests. I negotiate and draft other complex documents, including distribution, supply and vendor agreements, as well as joint venture and strategic alliance documents. I also counsel clients on and draft documents for the purchase and sale of real property and leasing transactions.
