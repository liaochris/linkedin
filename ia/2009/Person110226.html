Jens Erik Hoverby’s Summary

I work with warm hearted people who desire to win. As a management and business effectiveness specialist, my mission is to help managers create the passionate and profitable business they dream of.
My role is to install a performance machine in the top and middle management groups that smoothly take the company first over the goal line. I do this by giving people the coaching and the people management tools they need to inspire others to achieve their breakthrough goals.
My experience shows that everybody create their own reality - but only self-leaders proactively decide their own. If you think that you are a loser – you are right. If you truly believe you are a warm hearted winner – you are right too.
