Kumar Gaurav’s Summary

Executive offering 12 years of experience in Sales & Lead Generation with verifiable year-after-year success achieving financial success for clients. An innovative and motivating leader skilled at managing and exceeding client’s expectations while producing results.
Founder & CEO of Beyond Codes, Inc. (www.beyondcodes.com) a Sales & Lead Generation company based in Los Angeles with 4 sales offices across the U.S. and a research center in India.

At Beyond Codes, Inc, we use different business models and offerings and our only objective is to grow sales for our clients in U.S. and U.K. Our clients have the flexibility of choosing any of our offerings based on their need and comfort.

We also help our clients stay on top of Request for Proposals (RFPs) by keeping them posted and updated on all new RFPs in many verticals (medium to large RFPs only).

Our client list is a mixture of startups and medium size companies trying to establish and grow in U.S. to Large IT and non IT firms. Currently, 7 of the top 15 Indian IT companies are our clients.

My responsibilities include getting new clients for Beyond Codes Inc, hiring energetic and talented experienced Sales Professionals in North America and manage the team. I also get involved in status calls with our clients and end customer meetings to ensure our clients expectations are not only met but exceeded.

Details of our offerings can be found on our website at www.beyondcodes.com

Should you have any questions or need additional information about our services or business models, please feel free to contact me or any of our sales professionals and we will be glad to answer all of your questions.
