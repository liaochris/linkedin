Christopher Francis’s Summary

I produce documentaries and video marketing to help organizations increase their business. This is achieved by genuinely connecting with their audience and sharing their stories.

I'm a firm believer that authentic, personal stories told by customers, employees, and volunteers are far more effective than any staged and scripted TV commercial or print advertisement.

Traditional marketing has a self-centered approach and relies on interrupting its target audience. This is neither desirable or cost effective.

Instead of wasting client's money and their audience's time, we create original videos that showcase the client in an approachable, authentic fashion as well as provide their audience with the information and entertainment they desire and want to share.

Christopher Francis’s Specialties:

Not only creating compelling video content, but developing a thorough plan with the client to maximize the viewership of the video and the impact it has on its audience.
