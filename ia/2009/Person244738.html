Matthew Allen’s Summary

VerticalMove is an Executive and High-Tech Headhunting firm for clients in the Enterprise and Consumer Software segments located throughout Northern California and the Pacific Northwest. I founded VerticalMove under the premise of doing things radically differently. I was tired of the status quo, sick of the numbers game, fed-up with other recruiters plagiarizing and proliferating their clients job descriptions all over job boards with little value for the fee(s) they collect. We've since successfully built a highly respected business based upon referrals and true headhunting tactics.

We don't advertise on job boards. We don't search on resume boards. We offer our clients a truly unique network of passive individuals that they would likely have NOT had prior. We'd rather avoid putting our clients into overly competitive situations and only deal in passive individuals who are likely happily employed and not announcing themselves to the masses.

We've built relationships with an extensive number of professionals throughout our industry, individual contributors all the way to the 'C' level executives. In fact, we have documented more than 124,685 conversation with more than 22,858 professionals since we started started tracking in 2004. With our combined resources and expertise in the staffing industry we can significantly improve your competitive edge by attracting the best possible talent - before your closest competitor does, and helping your company reduce its time to market.

We're excited to represent some of the most prestigious companies in high-tech such as Netflix, Microsoft, Amazon, EMC, Network Appliance, BEA and CNET to name a few. Of course we’re not going to list the names of our smaller, earlier-stage clients, they're top-secret, but if you contact me I’ll tell you all about them.
