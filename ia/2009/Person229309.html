Taras Netrevozhko’s Summary

My goal is to build a global telecommunication and software technology company Devetel with the potential to radically change the way video, voice and data services are provided. Our new generation of Multi-User Multi-Antenna Technology will solve broadband communication problems associated with density of users in large cities, QoS adaptations and so on. It will establish itself in a number of specific applications, such as Video, IPTV, and VoD over wireless channel. It meets the demand of booming emerging markets where density of users and low-cost of user equipment are in high demand.

Devetel is an Eastern European technology company which provides high qualified custom R&D in software and hardware with a focus on innovations. Our customers receive significant competitive advantage, leveraging their core business and shortening time-to-market.

We focus on such scientifically intense areas of R&D as telecommunication technologies, media processing, video analytics and visualization systems. We gained strong expertise in such complex applications as business intelligence, security and location systems, as well as high-performance systems for complex event processing in 24x7 real-time. Our strong engineering capability in different areas, such as digital signal processing (DSP) hardware development, brings immediate results to our customers. Devetel’s expertise and focus on innovations allow to develop completely new products from scratch based on customers’ general directions, or in close cooperation with customers’ engineering teams.
