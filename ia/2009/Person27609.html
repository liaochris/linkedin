Betty Zakheim’s Summary

I love software marketing; I can’t think of anything I’d rather do for a living. Really, I’m not kidding.

My technical background allows me to get past being enamored of the technology and quickly get to the business value. Don’t get me wrong, I love the geeky stuff. But as everyone knows, business people don’t buy features and functions/speeds and feeds… they buy solutions to their business challenges.

And while everyone know this… not everyone can execute it. That just makes it MORE fun for me.
