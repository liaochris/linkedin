Sara Allen’s Summary

I recently completed promotion for a national book tour for a New York Times bestselling author based in Brooklyn, for whom we garnered press on the Today Show, TIME, and Newsweek, to name a few outlets. My role in publicity for the tour involved ensuring media coordination between the publisher (Penguin Press) and “on the ground” public relations teams in each community; fostering partnerships with community organizations such as Girl Scouts, local schools and other parent organizations; ensuring promotion via social media marketing; and confirming event logistics in select cities on the tour. Attendance was a great success, ranging from 300-1100 at each event. Learn more about client at www.rachelsimmons.com.

My framework is a deep appreciation for, and desire to inspire and stretch, each client’s knowledge, network, and resources for impact. My approach eliminates the strain of planning and management, delivers strategies and tools to improve initiatives, and encourages clients toward sustainable integration of these tools with day-to-day operations. My objective is to lend fresh perspective that cultivates lasting impact for each client.
