Annalisa Holmes’s Summary

This year marks my tenth year as the business owner of The Transcription People Pty Ltd, which birthed as a micro home based business. Whilst exiting the Corporate field to raise a family The Transcription People has now become a highly successful entity.

The business currently operates from an office at Erina CBD on the Central Coast, provides service to a substantial and elite client base, offers traineeships to school leavers and contracts home-based transcriptionists and proofreaders, throughout Australia and New Zealand.

Ultimately my primary professional goal is to facilitate the growth of the business to an international level, whereby we provide an exceptional transcription service to businesses and companies including transnational corporations. To achieve this level of success, I would firstly like to direct the business in becoming the leader in transcription and online dictation specialists within Australia. Throughout this process I also aim to achieve a highly recognized brand, which portrays and carries the business’’ ideals, values and successes. I believe that this journey will also provide me with the skills and ability to voluntary mentor and present my knowledge and experience to newly small business owners, which will inspire and project growth, at various media type functions.

I am extremely proud of the development of the business and my accomplishments, which I achieved without any formal business education or qualifications.
