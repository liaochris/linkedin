Brad Farris’s Summary

Anchor Advisors, Ltd. is a team of small business advisors working with owners of small businesses, particularly creative service firms (Designers, Product Development, Web Design, Marketing & PR firms, Architects & interiors) with between 10 and 100 employees. Our work focuses on four areas, Purpose, People, Process and Performance Measures.

Owners of growing businesses often find that they are the only one in the business that owns problems or decisions. It all gets dumped on their desk and it's starting to be overwhelming. Business owners that work with us find that they have a partner for their business, one who does more than just take things off their plate, but also creates systems, trains staff members, & holds them accountable. Plus they enjoy a wealth of outside resources that we can engage to help them make progress faster and with fewer false starts. The owners report feeling more confident; they have a plan that allows them to manage the growth for this year and beyond. Several of them even went on vacation without checking in on the business!

In just a few hours a week, we can free a business owner to do more of the things that they are good at and that got them into the business in the first place.

Budgets, employee reviews, planning, monitoring, measuring and reporting, recruiting key employees, negotiations, we do what ever needs to be done to make your business more successful.

We also have a recruiting model that is designed specifically for Small Business. Don't let the arduous process of finding a new employee keep you from getting rid of a poor performer or prevent your growth. We can help.

Visit http://www.AnchorAdvisors.com to find out more.
