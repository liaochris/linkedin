Norbert Riksman’s Summary

Norbert is a self taught entrepreneur. Coming from a family where owning a business is as normal as it gets, he followed these footsteps but sought after a modern way of doing business.

He studied Law, Economics and various Human studies ( NLP, Psychological studies ) to get to the bottom of the Why, the How and the When. He started a career in sales and excelled in this. His personal touch and care is highly appreciated and an example for many new rising salespeople.

Norbert is a leader, but mostly a person with a vision for innovation, personal resilience and entrepreneurial flair. He is great in creating a vision for companies and their CEO’s and managers. His leading efforts and adapting to change in new environments make him a perfect coach in any company. His independent mind brings solutions, possibilities and change to many problems and projects. His personal care and strength supports people with a vision and he can implement the solution that drives change.

Both in personal life and his career as a sales- and personal coach he is an adventurer. He adopts well to change and challenges; he enjoys the possible outcomes and consequences of new approaches. Strongly focused on pragmatic solutions, his personal confidence can provide others with stability and security. He behaves with a clear sense of personal values and shows respect and positive regard to others.

His entrepreneurial interest in business, commerce, finance and being competitive and ambitious mixed with his interest in the choices and possibilities of human power makes him a great support for personal and business matters.

Norbert's online business is Polleo.eu an online-coaching platform where strength is combined with speed. Fast responses to personal and business matters from the best coaches available on the subject of the matter.

Now busy with yet a new venture called HelpOutOnline,soon to be launched.
“reaching all the potentials”
