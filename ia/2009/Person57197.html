David Dressler’s Summary

I am an Electronics Engineer with a broad background of design with a focus on digital implemenations for wireless and communications systems for commercial, military, avionic and spacecraft applications. My career has spanned from system-level to chip-level product development starting from concept through product release and support. I have ventured out of my comfort zone on several occasions and am looking to do it again now. There are many evolving and revolutionary projects going on and I plan on being involved with at least one of them.
