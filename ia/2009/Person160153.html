    Meu Móvel de Madeira (MMM) is a brazilian company that belongs to Celulose Irani (www.irani.com.br), a company that began to produce and export wood furniture in 1994 aligning quality and good taste with the concept of environmental preservation.. The group is also responsible for manufacturing paper, paperboard and corrugated cardboard packaging. Due to the products’ success among consumers, Meu Móvel de Madeira has now gone on to meet a new demand in the international market: providing ready made furniture, without a minimum quantity requirement. Yet another advance that proves the sales potential of the company’s products and the emphasis on environmental responsibility.
