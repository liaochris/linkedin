Simon Hénault’s Summary

Hi. Welcome to my LinkedIn profile.

Having being involved in the production and delivery of thousands of print and Web content for close to 100 companies of all sizes and industries since 1995, I guess it's fair to say that I am an expert in corporate communications.

Companies are managed to offer products and services, not so much content. POD Media intervenes by creating and delivering great content (blogs, podcasts, newsletters...) intended for our clients' target audiences.

Through relevant and useful content, audiences learn and better appreciate the publishing companies, leading to higher profile, greater visibility and increased sales.

POD Media's success is built on:
- An integrated approach: production, integration and delivery of content
- Innovative Web communication tools
- Experienced writers, Web integrators, graphic artists and PR experts

Simon Hénault’s Specialties:
