Edward Kleinjan’s Summary

I'm an IT Architect with more than 11 years of IT experience. My work is geared to understanding the vision and goals of a business and knowing what their requirements are for a specific project. My job is to translate that into an IT solution that can consist of applications, integrations by middleware, databases, servers, storage, networks, maintenance, hosting, etc. I am ultimately responsible for the final solution and work with many specialists to make sure that we all work toward one solution that meets the company's goal, but is not too expensive. My skills include team leadership, communication, and challenging engineers to meet their potential.

I have experience in making proposals and working in BID-teams on BIDS of 100+ mio deals.

My area of interest is Enterprise Architecture and governance.
My area of knowledge is infrastructure (servers, operating systems, storage, network, middleware, applications).
I have a special interest in sizing, availability, performance and security.
