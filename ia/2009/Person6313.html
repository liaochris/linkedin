Alan Kaye’s Summary

Professional & Entrepreneurial Accomplishments

Alan has 14+ years of experience in the Sports & Entertainment marketing industries. His experiences include working with both current and retired athletes & entertainers such as Derek Jeter, David Wright, Reggie Jackson, Hank Aaron, Lawrence Taylor, Yogi Berra, The Lead Cast from the Jersey Boys, Steven Van Zandt, and many, many more... He has orchestrated well over 500+ autograph signings, charitable events and corporate appearances. Additionally, Alan co-owned Ballsink, a sports memorabilia & trading card retail storefront located in East Rutherford, NJ and Select Signatures Inc, a sports memorabilia retail business located in the Frozen Ropes baseball training facility in White Plains.

I have managed more than 350 auctions for charities of all sizes and helped them raise more than $15,000,000 in the last twelve years.

He has founded, managed, and sold, Collect Safe Inc. He patented software to curtail Internet Fraud in the Sports Memorabilia industry. Collectsafe’s vertical applications not only protected the signer and the collector, but it also protected limited editions for manufacturers who had thousands of pieces of inventory. The title page featured a digital image of the item along with a short description of the commissioned signing where it was created, and/or any other pertinent documentation related to item’s origin, provenance, etc… Title Pages are stored in the protected back end database.

Alan’s charitable contributions of both personal and business items for silent/live auctions, raffle prizes, and incentives to organizations including Leukemia Lymphoma of Westchester, UJA of Westchester, The National MS Society, The Barth Syndrome Foundation, National Breast Cancer Association, Children’s Village of Westchester, The American Red Cross of Greenwich, The National Board of Realtors, The pediatric Hematology foundation of NY Cornell/Pres and many others.

Alan Kaye’s Interests:

Alan’s personal collection comprises memorabilia of entertainment, political and sports memorabilia from the late 1800’s-1950’s. Major subjects in the collection are rare Complete tobacco card sets from the late 1800’s, complete card sets from the 20’s-1960’s, Game Worn Jerseys of Ruth, Gehrig, Autographed items of Ty Cobb, Babe Ruth, Lou Gehrig, Joe Dimaggio, Mickey Mantle, Jackie Robinson, Cy Young and the list goes on and on. Containing hundreds of thousands of items, the entire collection is too numerous to list completely.
