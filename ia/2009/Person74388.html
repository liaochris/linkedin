Eric Jafari’s Summary

Mr. Jafari is the co-founder and Chief Executive Officer of BridgePoint Ventures LLC, Ascent Acquisitions LLC and Vertical Realty Advisors LLC as well as being a co-founder and Co-Managing Director of EquityBridge Capital. In 2001, Mr. Jafari was hired by First Meridian as a wealth integration advisor advising the ultra affluent in the areas of asset management, business succession, mergers and acquisitions, private equity investment and estate planning. In 2005, Mr. Jafari originated and oversaw the recapitalization of DDU Express in addition to the originating and aiding in the orchestration of the merger between First Meridian and the InvestLinc Group. Also, in 2005, Mr. Jafari founded BridgePoint Ventures, LLC, Vertical Realty Advisors, LLC and Ascent Acquisitions, LLC. Between 2005 and 2008, Mr. Jafari oversaw BridgePoint’s $400,000,000+ in real estate acquisitions and succeeded in not only preserving 100% of the investment capital in addition generating double digit annualized returns in a number of the projects. Mr. Jafari was responsible for overseeing BridgePoint’s acquisition in the Mandarin Oriental Chicago, IntraWest’s Towne of Sea Haven, Panam’s Praderas Del Yaque in the Dominican Republic in addition to over thirty other acquisitions during this period. Mr. Jafari has sat on the corporate advisory boards for Strategic Wealth Alliance, Source China and EquityOne in addition a myriad of philanthropic initiatives such as founding the Caleb and Lilly Foundation and the Make-A-Wish Planned Giving Council. Mr. Jafari has been requested as key note speaker and panelist for conferences globally including China’s Ministry of Construction International Exhibition on Housing and Canada’s Pro-Seminars National Advisor Conference.
