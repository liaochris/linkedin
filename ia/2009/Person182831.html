Paul Zimmerman’s Summary

Currently Head of Business Development for Play.com, one of the UK's largest e-commerce retailers. Working closely with the owners, I drive Play.com's corporate and web development strategies. Since 2005, I have launched the following initiatives:

- Redesign of Play.com (2008)
- Product expansion: led the growth of selection from 500k products (2005) to 12m (2009)
- Category expansion: Clothing (2007), Digital music / MP3s (2008), Tickets (2008), Mobile (2005)
- Overhaul of search (2009), CMS (2009), SEO strategy (2008)
- Play.com's marketplace, PlayTrade (2006)
- Play.com's credit card (2009)
- Play.com's community strategy: product reviews (2007), seller ratings (2006)
- Play.com's gift vouchers (2007)

Previously a senior manager at both Amazon.co.uk (4 years) and Woolworths Group (2 years), where I led the business units responsible for driving e-commerce retail sales for both physical and digital music products.
