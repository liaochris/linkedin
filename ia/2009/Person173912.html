Nick Whiteley’s Summary

SMART are a leading ISV focused on workforce management solutions for the enterprise. We help organisations reduce costs whilst improving work-life-balance.

Our solutions are visionary and lead the market both in terms of technology and functionality. We formed the first Open Standards body (www.workforce-xml.org) to create global standards in our market place to mature and ease integration.

All of these are great reasons to work for SMART, but the most compelling reason is to work with such a talented and committed team of individuals.
