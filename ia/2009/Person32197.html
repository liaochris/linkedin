Brandon Rhodes’s Summary

After nearly a decade of both research and enterprise IT software development experience at Georgia Tech, I have moved into full-time independent consulting in order to have more opportunities to enjoy web development — the stimulating mix of graphic design, agile programming, and document-centric processing that, when done well, can make information and relationships visible like never before.

Brandon Rhodes’s Specialties:

Python programming, Django, Turbogears, Zope, relational databases, PostgreSQL, Javascript, Unix, Linux, system administration, technical writing, technical editing, training, seminars

    Georgia Institute of Technology
