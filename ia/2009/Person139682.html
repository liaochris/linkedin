Len Rosen, PHR’s Summary

Senior human resources management professional with more than thirteen years experience advising management in work-related problems and their resolutions, contract administration, employee and labor relations, compensation analysis, staff reorganization, and training. Business partner and change agent to senior operating and leadership executives to guide the development of performance and market driven organizations.
