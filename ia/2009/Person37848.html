Carlos Sera’s Summary

Carlos has an MBA with honors from the University of Rochester in finance and applied economics. He received his BA from Johns Hopkins University in Natural Science. Carlos has been involved with many non-profit organizations including the Boys and Girls Club of America and Citizens for Safety Now (which he founded).

Carlos Sera’s Specialties:

In addition to his role with Chicago Wealth Management, Carlos has a financial blog: www.financialtales.com and has been a speaker on investments and financial planning. He is fluent in Spanish.
