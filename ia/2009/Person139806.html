Leonard Kish’s Summary

Accomplished international science and technology-focused professional with a deep track record of leading and supporting efforts to drive company formation, market entry, and product improvement in the software, management consulting and biomedical industries. Known for achieving objectives with minimal resources using collaborative techniques to drive productivity. Sought as a thought leader in business strategy, innovation management and intellectual capital valuations. Motivated by challenge; excels within fast-paced, high-pressure environments; adapts quickly to new demands and situations. Leverages combined biomedical science and IT background with solid statistical and analytical skills to devise innovative solutions to complex business challenges. Charismatic team player, mentor and leader; interacts effectively between multicultural business, technology, and science professionals.
