Tonia Mosteller’s Summary

Experts in Design, Redesign and Real Estate Staging.

Let us transform your home or business into a work of art. We specialize in new construction, builder models, one day decorating, interior re-design, staging, color consultations, organizing services, space planning, furniture selections, flooring selections, event decorating, professional shopping services, lighting selections, faux finishes, move in service, resale and vacant homes. Staging your home so it will appeal to buyers, creating the "First Impression".

Redesign + More creates a room(s) that not only functions well but also shows the home owners personality. We find satisfaction in helping homeowners personalize their home and working with builders to create wonderful model interiors. Creating spaces that are warm and inviting, your personal retreat.

We work with Residential and Commercial properties.

About Me:

After spending many years in the corporate world working in management, sales and recruiting, I realized my passion has always been decorating. I have 15 years of experience in Interior Design, Faux Finishes, Home Redesign and Staging. Creating spaces that are warm and inviting, reflecting your lifestyle and your personality. Your personal retreat!

My decorating career has not only allowed me to work with homeowners. I have been able to utilize my corporate professional background and decorating talents to branch out to hotels and resorts, resturants, builders, model homes, architect offices, furniture marketplace and specialty retail businesses.
