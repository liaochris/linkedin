Chris Borg’s Summary

Freelance web and software developer; involved in the development industry since the year 2000. Interests include music(John Denver), football(play/watch), tennis(play/watch) and motorbikes(watch).

Selected in May 2005 to attend the “Conference on Youth Portals and young people” in Brussels as technical expert in the field of youth portals following the design and development of the youth web section for the ministry of education, youth and employment.

Selected in both the 2004/2005 and 2005/2006 academic years to represent the Malta College of Arts, Science and Technology in Microsoft’s Imagine Cup competition.

Academic projects include “Click Bank PLC”, an object oriented banking application written in C#, “HawkEye”, a message board system developed in classic ASP, “Muzikale”, an ASP 3 based e-commerce web site, “sensACEional Tennis Manager”, a tennis management system application written in C# and “TomaHawk”, a replica of Dot Net Nuke written in C# and making use of XML and COM+ technologies.

Near-future projects include the Technology Specialist: .NET Framework 2.0 Web Applications certification.
