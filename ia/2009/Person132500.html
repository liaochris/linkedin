Kim Ferguson’s Summary

I assist businesses and individuals with Relationship Marketing, using Send Out Cards. Send Out Cards allows you to send a printed greeting card in about 60 seconds. Simply choose or create a card in our system, type in your own message, add a picture if you would like and hit send. We print it, stuff it and mail it! Using this system will help you remember people, share appreciation, motivate, uplift, encourage and truly make a difference.

I can show you how to use the system in about 15 minutes, feel free to contact me at 863-852-1013. I am also looking for motivated and driven individuals who might be interested in a new business or who could use a second source of income. Check out my website and send a FREE card www.sendoutcards.com/kimandpaul
