  • Nextvoice247 - A Multimedia Professional & Personal Development Network

    (Online Media industry)

    December 2007 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Nextvoice247 is an online multimedia community for personal and professional development informing and influencing the world’s next voices of change. Providing democratized access to cultural, business, social, and political thought leaders.

    This information exchange medium targets young adults just entering business or taking on larger business responsibilities. They will participate in shaping learning and social reform that is authentic and relevant to them through blogs, podcasts, webcasts, and a range of evolving media.

    The guiding principle of Nextvoice247 is to reduce stress in the workplace, provide timely, meaningful, lessons & differentiate “experiences versus experience”. Taking a centuries-old method of teaching, storytelling, & integrating modern & refined video, audio, documentary & photojournalistic approaches.

    Melott’s passion for inter-generational dynamics & his desire to create a future of action-oriented leadership are the genesis of Nextvoice247.
