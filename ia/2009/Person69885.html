Ed Torres [LION] (torresinc at gmail . com)’s Summary

Established in 1998, Torres Enterprises is an independent agent for MonaVie Health Sciences.

Introduced in January 2005, MonaVie develops and markets scientifically formulated, premium quality nutritional products, specifically for person-to-person distribution.

The products feature a proprietary blend of the power of açaí berry, found only in remote regions of the Amazon, and other nutrient-dense fruits, each selected for its specific nutritional properties.

As an independent agent we are seeking partners from Australia, Brazil, Canada, France, Japan, Monaco, New Zealand, Puerto Rico, Singapore and USA (more to come) for a profitable and professional business relationship.

The corporate headquarters are located in Salt Lake City, Utah.
