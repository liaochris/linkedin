Robert Friedman’s Summary

I have been in financial services for most of my adult life; beginning as an account executive with Walston & Co. in 1973 and later becoming a registered investment adviser and a registered securities principal. I was associated with Prudential Bache Securities, Paine Webber Jackson& Curtis and Thomson McKinnon Securities before going indepedent with Linsco Private Ledger in 1986. While working as a financial services professional I specialized in tax shelters and financial planning before deciding to leave the investment industry in 2002. I devoted efforts to selling and marketing supplemental health insurance from 2002 - 2006.

In 2007 I formed Sundownnetworking.Com, LLC with my partner Rob Bronson and in late 2008 I created StoptheCallsFast.Com

Robert Friedman’s Specialties:

My areas of expertise are building organizations, networking, integrating technology applications for practical uses in business environments, and finally helping consumers stop threats and frivolous lawsuits from rogue debt collection agencies.
