    Executive Privilege provides a vehicle for secure encrypted communication and collaboration for teams of top executives and decision makers outside of the inquiring eyes of their organization’s staff - be it administrative, information technology, etc.

    Executive Privilege is designed specifically with the upper-level executive in mind. The easy to use interface provides functionality generally used in most office technology environments - yet it is hosted and maintained completely outside of the organization’s local information technology structure. The secure and encrypted hosting environment ensures that secrets are shared only with those who are chosen.

Through our collective executive experiences, we saw the need for an executive and legal planning environment free of any information leaks. Executive Privilege is that environment.
