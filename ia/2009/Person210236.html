Ramesh Sarukkai’s Summary

Dr. Ramesh Sarukkai is a seasoned technologist with over 15 years experience delivering top-class products used by millions around the world and makes millions of dollars for businesses, managing engineering teams that build and support these products, and fostering innovation that has an impact on the bottom line to the company. Attestments to this are the numerous best-of-breed awards that the products Ramesh delivered with his teams: e.g. COMDEX award Y! Small Business site, COMDEX award L&H VoiceXpress, Yahoo! Impact Award for Y! Video/Video Search, Innovation Hack awards, managing top-3 trafficked media/video search destination, managing and completing SoX compliancy infrastructure for >$125MM revenue e-commerce systems at Yahoo! are just some examples.

Dr. Ramesh Sarukkai currently leads next-generation advertising initiatives including mobile monetization infrastructure team at Yahoo! Prior to this, he headed up Video Search engineering where he managed video search/core media search/publishing platform infrastructure. Over his 7+ years tenure at Yahoo, Dr. Sarukkai has built and managed many engineering teams (upto 25-30) including the Small Business (e-commerce stores, hosting, domains) billing platform, and architected wireless applications/voice portals including 1-800-my-yahoo. Prior to Yahoo, he has worked in Kurzweil A.I. (Ray Kurzweils startup acquired by L&H), IBM T.J. Watson Lab, and has collaborated with HP Labs. He holds a Masters and PhD from the University of Rochester, NY.

Dr. Sarukkai is a prolific speaker at conferences/panels and has also authored a book on web technology (Springer/Kluwer). In addition, he plays advisory roles in many enterprenueral efforts.
