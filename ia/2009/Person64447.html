Leslie Cullum, PHR’s Summary

I have experience in distribution operations, pharmaceuticals, hotel & casino, and construction.

I have managed up to 5 employees and administered HR for 4,200 employees.

These are my current responsibilities:
•Manage all aspects of HR for Nevada to include, but not limited to, the development, implementation, maintenance, and monitoring of employee relations; recruitment; employee benefits; local, state and federal reporting; employee training and development programs; safety administration.
•Supervise and train one to three HR Assistants and the Receptionist to support Corporate functions and assist 850 office and field employees with benefits, payroll, workers’
compensation, and employee relations issues.
•Manage the recruitment process for all positions, orientation, and retention strategies.
•Successfully administer employee relations programs, including conducting investigations, and providing advice and counsel to managers on employee and personnel issues.
•Conduct monthly HR training with management on various topics including harassment avoidance, employee development plans, employment law updates, performance review process, and leave of absence administration.
•Administer all benefits, FMLA and workers’ compensation compliance to include participating on the safety committee and reducing the number of recordable injuries.
•Create, administer, and maintain personnel policies, procedures, and employee handbook.
•Review weekly payroll registers to ensure accuracy.
•Implemented & administering the outsourcing of unemployment and verifications.
•Created & developed the succession planning initiative for the company.
•Continuously audit systems and processes for accuracy, efficiency, and effectiveness.

Goals:
1. Complete my Master's Degree in Business Administration
2. Get my SPHR certification within the next 2 years.
3. Become a Director of HR within the next 5 years.
