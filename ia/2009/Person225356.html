Matt Batt’s Summary

I've been working as a media relations professional for 10 years primarily in the technology industry. My experience is balanced evenly between corporate communications positions and agency positions. Over the past 3 years I've been focused on working exclusively with the national business media such as AP, Bloomberg, BusinessWeek, Dow Jones, Forbes, Fortune, Oprah & Friends, The Today Show, USA Today, WSJ and many others. My primary objective is generating placements for my clients within these outlets and in turn helping them turn those placements into sales or revenue for their companies.
