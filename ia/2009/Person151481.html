Mark Colby’s Summary

Technology Executive - operations, energy (renewables, electrical, nuclear) engineering, strategic planning, partnerships, and executive management of renewable and cutting edge residential energy solutions.

Led three companies at the senior VP level ... solar, electrical power analysis software, and hydrogen fuel cell. Formed new technologies, managed product roadmaps, built sales pipelines and developed strategic business relationships in renewable energy, high energy efficiency buildings and high end residential markets.

Worked strategically with SunTech, NewCon/NewCore Steel, Kyocera, BP Solar, UniSolar, Skanska, Shell, DoD, DoE, DoT and fortune 500 companies.

Hosted premier "BattleGround Earth" TV show on Planet Green - providing education and awareness on residential solar power.

Developed logistics and installation relationships with multiple companies to provision, install and maintain "first to market" residential and light commercial hydrogen fuel cells. Hiring and trained Field Service team of over 60 direct reports responsible for maintaining ever growing number of fuel cells.

US Navy - Commanding Officer of two ships. Nuclear Surface Warfare Officer - 22+ years engineering, operations, leadership, strategic planning, project management, process improvement, QA/QC with proven track record in nuclear power, electrical (power) engineering, and leadership with over 600 direct reports. Served in two Admiral staffs, led ROI studies, acted as executive agent for fleet operations and training for Fleet Commanders. Developed objective measures of performance. Mentored and trained senior military officers (Destroyer Squadron Commodores and Strike Group Commander Admirals). Subject Matter Expert and Principle Technical Advisor to THIRD Fleet on Naval Operations, Technology and Training.

Mark Colby’s Specialties:

Technology/Energy Executive - Nuclear/Electrical/Renewable. Vast experience and technical expertise in training, maintenance, operations, product development, project management, process improvement, emerging technologies and market analysis. Navy Nuclear Engineer, MSEE (power engineering, power production/distribution/transfer and modeling/analysis). VP of two companies. Commanding Officer of two $100M US Navy warships - direct responsibility for over 600 Sailors and Marines.
