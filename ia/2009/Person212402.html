Sean Canton’s Summary

I want to make technology work for your purposes. Whether organizing a photo library, buying new equipment or learning how to use your software, I can help.

I strive to create high quality productions that both communicate ideas and leave an impact. It is important to me that this impact and message be socially responsible and informational, as well as entertaining, if possible. Thus, I have gathered a large number of skillsets surrounding the idea of effective communication harnessing the power of video, audio and the Internet.

I believe in proven technology or ideas with tremendous potential. I want to explore technology which pushes the boundaries of human experience and comprehension. I enjoy challenges as they are opportunities for growth. I would achieve great things in a company that has an existing structure in place, yet allows me the flexibility to develop my own workflow over time.

I practice sound information architecture techniques whether I am designing a web page, editing a film, making a map, mixing a concert, or conceptualizing a project with a client. Separating content from noise using structure is an art in itself. Directed attention.

I have always appreciated maps as a crafted representation of reality, as well as the subtle nuances of culture in every form. I strive to find employment which would use my Geography background, in addition to my media production skill.
