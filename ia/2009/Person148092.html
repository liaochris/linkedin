Manu Gysels’s Summary

I started as an Art Director in advertising (LG&F, Motunuï, Tequila). Through the years I liked to work with mixed media (on/off line) as an Art & New Media Director (Ad!dict Creative Lab - NY & Belgium). Keeping my off line skills as an allround knowledge, I started as a webdesigner en 'e'-volved as an e-consultant (BBDO Belgium).
In 2004 I teamed with Frank at BBDO (Proximity)... we matched! Since the start of our company (to:communication) we like to fire-up ideas through these on & off line integrated knowledges... Since 2004 we do strategic & creative work for several national/international clients (Certipost, WWF, Electro Rent, Mobistar, Telenet...) and we still work for some ad agencies as a creative contractor (Duval, proximity/BBDO, Cypres, Ogilvy, Draft...).
