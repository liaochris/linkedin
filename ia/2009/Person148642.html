Marc Helfer’s Summary

Currently Marc excels as the Internet Marketing and Sales Manager for TradeWinds Islands Resorts, a 4-diamond beach resort with 785 rooms. Marc has held this critical management position since October of 2008 following three months of initial service in the department as an eMail and eContent Manager. His present leadership position allows him to participate extensively in the global world of e-commerce, where he utilizes his technical acumen to promote the resort via the latest Internet marketing strategies. By optimizing key channels including Global Distribution Systems and Online Travel Agents and employing creative email promotional campaigns, Mr. Helfer recurrently expands the client base and facilitates continued improvements in customer satisfaction levels.

Prior to his employment in the Internet marketing and sales division, Marc was an indispensible member of the TradeWinds sales team as an Associate Sales Manager. With a creative approach to business development that included use of direct and indirect marketing and sales techniques, he adeptly mined and qualified leads and significantly grew over 500 qualified new sales accounts in 18 months.

With his superb interpersonal abilities Mr. Helfer also outperformed his colleagues as a Business Development Coordinator in his initial position with TradeWinds in December of 2006. He was acknowledged for his talents with the promotion to Associate Sales Manager.

To gain the expertise necessary to thrive in prominent roles within the hospitality business, Mr. Helfer completed his MBA in International Tourism & Hotel Management in 2005. He graduated with a GPA of 3.6 and received the International Hospitality Award for achieving a 4.0 average in his core hospitality classes. Mr. Helfer continues to expand his skills through various professional development seminars including a certificate in Meeting & Event Planning from the University of South Florida.
