John Conti’s Summary

As a senior IT Project Manager and systems design engineer for over 20 years, I have helped many businesses successfully build new IT data centers or expand their existing IT technology. These projects include designing and implementing secure and resilient network infrastructure, communications, and many other aspects of business technology.

Currently a major focus is helping businesses become highly resilient. This includes creating Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery (BC/DR) Plans. My practice provides solutions and services for small and mid-sized business with a practical approach that covers Risk/Needs Assessment and Business Analysis. The assessments are used to creating Technology and Business Solutions that will keep people connected and businesses protected.

John Conti’s Specialties:

- Managing complex IT projects
- Performing a Risk/Needs Analysis of technology and business systems
- Creating solutions to reduce business risks
- Designing and implementing highly reliable IT solutions
- Writing easy-to-follow system documentation
- Keeping members of a project up to date, using web-based collaborative technologies
