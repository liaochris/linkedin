Michael Abehsera’s Summary

A musician at heart, I first picked up the drum when I was 4 years old.

Even so, from a young age I knew I would be an entrepreneur. Building companies is my passion, and at the heart of this passion stands new media.

I love the idea that media one day will be owned by individuals instead of big powerhouses. This whole revolution of the individual got me really interested in marketing and working in the new media field.

It became a passion, a religion for me — the idea of decentralizing media and putting the power in the hands of individuals.

I became very active on social media sites including Digg (Top 350 users of all time), Reddit and StumbleUpon, among others.

Besides that I've worked with the Woo Agency (part time) on couple of projects including:
• Samsung, my DJ fantasy project
• Fred (top 10 YouTube subscriber)
• ZipIt wireless project
• Memeo share

I've also been in management and marketing for a couple of startups:
• Zoopri, a virtual world/search engine for children, set to launch in late 2009.
• Rooji.com, which I got funding for and own a stake in and is set to launch in the spring.

Besides that, I've managed all kinds of web projects, from marketing to developing.

Michael Abehsera’s Specialties:

Branding, sales, management, marketing, social media, SEO, drumming and much more.
