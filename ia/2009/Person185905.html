Peter Merkle’s Summary

From Operations Manager at a Chicago Special events design firm, to becoming the most requested disc jockey at a national entertainment service, then the manager in the Chicago office of North America’s largest DJ service. Peter has been in special events for over 15 years.

He has had the honor of being voted Member Of The Year at Disc Jockey America.

He continues his proud affiliation with the American Disc Jockey Association as the President of the Chicagoland Chapter.

He is moderator of the Professional Disc Jockey Think Cycle Sessions taking place at the annual Mobile Beat Convention.

His warm, motivating personality, ability to perform at any event, and extensive experience ensure that your event will be fun for everyone.

As an experienced MC and award-winning member of Toastmasters International (Speaker Of The Year 2005), he takes an enthusiastic approach to controlling the flow of events, motivating a crowd, and maintaining an exciting atmosphere.

He is the recipient of the Peter Merry Leadership Award 2006 in recognition of exceptional service, dedication and commitment to the American Disc Jockey Association.

Peter has published articles in Disc Jockey News, Chicagoland ADJA and Toastmasters Diplomatic Dispatch.

Peter has excellent experience in event planning and coordination. He is a team player with the ability to follow along when necessary and lead with integrity.

Peter Merkle’s Specialties:

Innovative, he seeks out further education, training and networking opportunities.

Background includes radio, voiceover, improv, acting, public speaking, performance, dance, team building, speech scripting, children’s activities, beat mixing, special effects, climbing volcanoes and firewalking.

Proud member of the American Disc Jockey Association and Toastmasters International. Well respected locally and nationally among special event professionals.
