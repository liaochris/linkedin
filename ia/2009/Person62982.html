Dhiman Mukherji’s Summary

Dhiman has spent over 12 years in the Media Industry. He has wide experience in the business of Print media as well as the Digital Media.

He has worked with leading brands such as The Times Of India Group, Rediff.com, Google and AOL.

Dhiman has worked extensively in the area of Advertising Sales, Business Development and Alliances.He has strong expertise in the area of sales management and managing key client relationships.

He has had long standing business relationships with several companies from varied domains. He has helped these companies in evolving online marketing strategies using the digital media.

He has vast experience in the Search part of the digital business and has helped many advertisers kick start their advertising on search in order to get better ROIs.

In the past, Dhiman, has been instrumental in publishing of several International Industry and Country Supplements through the IMR division of The Times of India Group.

He now leads a Sales function at AOL for their India portal.
