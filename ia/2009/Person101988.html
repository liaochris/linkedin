Jakey Toor’s Summary

I am an independent theatre / dance artist and teacher. My theatre / dance work fuses narrative text and spoken word with organic, gesture-based movement. I hold a Master of Education from UC San Diego and am interested in the different ways people learn and create meaning. Additionally, I am interested in / focus on incorporating technology into classroom teaching (the use of wiki's, classroom discussion boards, individual student blogs, collaborative student blogs etc.).

I hope to help create a world in which all ways of thinking and being are respected.

Jakey Toor’s Specialties:

Teaching and Learning, Theatre, Dance, Arts Education.
