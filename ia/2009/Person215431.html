Sharon Bibb’s Summary

I am an editor, content developer and trainer with a diverse career at major media companies. My passion for emerging technologies led me to play a vital role in systems development and training. I focus on innovative solutions to minimize impact on users so they remain free to focus on their talents. On two major training initiatives, I promoted and instituted online simulation technology where no formal training system existed. I developed an in-application Web portal for just-in-time adult learning that mitigated costly, dated documentation and over-reliance on tech support resources. Adopting SIT TESOL methodology allows me to capitalize on experiential learning and inductive peer teaching. My successes have been marked by improved efficiency, dynamic team collaboration and transitioning users from IT dependence to self-sufficiency. Juggling priorities and changing landscapes on deadline has been a constant theme.
