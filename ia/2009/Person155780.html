I have a willingness to work, do the basics correctly, do the job no one else wants to do. And then improve on what I am doing. Growth is hard and respect is to earned.

I've taken all the hospitality training, and a chefs mentality and apply them to my current position.

The customer is not always right but they are correct;
Sell/produce a product/service that you will be proud of;
Everyone can make a mistake but how do you fix it;
Take something simple, keep it simple and make it amazing;
Learn techniques/skills and they can be applied in many different ways

I prefer to do business in person, when we can.
Merchant can contact me without 20 voice prompts or being put on hold for more then a minute or two...
Full disclosure of fees and rates and contracts. (My sales agents loved me for this but all of our merchant understand now what they are being charged.)
I spend a lot of time training our sales agents on all aspects of the industry.
I want to keep well trained knowledgable agents. Paying them well helps...
I love what I do.
