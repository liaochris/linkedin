Jerry Brouwer’s Summary

Strategic Buyer at Stork Food & Dairy Systems B.V. (Stork FDS) based in Amsterdam, The Netherlands.

Main activities of Stork FDS are the development, production and supply of integrated processing and filling lines for the dairy, juice, food processing and pharmaceutical (neutraceutical) industries. See below for more information about the company.

In close cooperation with my colleagues (development, engineering and procurement), this job focuses on the following key aspects:
 Programme Management (Modular Sourcing Projects);
 Modular sourcing of (sub-) assemblies;
 Cost reduction programs, TCO-approach;
 Optimization of the supply chain;
 Expand cooperation with (new and/or early involved) suppliers;
 Project management (procurement) process improvement;
 Contract management;
 Category management

Stork Food Systems B.V., of which Stork FDS is a subsidiary, is owned by Marel Food Systems.

Sources:
www.fds.storkgroup.com/
www.marel.com
