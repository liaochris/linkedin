Mary Findley’s Summary

Retired professional cleaning expert now happily owning and operating Mary Moppins. Professional speaker giving green cleaning seminars to any interested group.

Book author and publish a monthly cleaning newsletter. Am beginning work on a 3rd book concerning health, diet and alternative healing.

Developed and patented a new mop that uses ordinary terry cloth towels to mop floors, clean ceilings, dry RVs and it even does windows. The original mop has developed into a full range of environmentally safe, eco friendly cleaning tools and cleaners.

Goals are to start teaching individuals how to clean their homes on a one to one basis. Also to establish franchises so others can learn the Mary Moppins way of cleaning and also teach others. My goal is to help others become self-sufficient or add additional income to their family.

Mary Findley’s Specialties:

Specialities involve developing and creating new cleaning equipment and forseeing the growth and direction of the cleaning industry.

Mary Findley’s Experience

  • owner

    Mary Moppins

    (Consumer Goods industry)

    1992 — Present (17 years)

    Mary Moppins evolved from 12 years of professionally cleaning. Frustration with mops drove me to develop a new mop that uses terry cloth towels. The line grew to include green cleaners and cleaning accessories that are kind to both humans and the Earth.

    Specialty tools soon followed for people with disabilities, the RV and boating industries.

    As I began to present cleaning seminars, folks asked for a book and Whistle While You Work was written. Articles were soon placed in RV magazines and Todd Braeburn with Spread the News placed my company in Woman's World Magazine and articles in other magazines.

    Linda Formachelli asked me to help her write "The Complete Idiot's Guide to Cleaning" in 2005. Penguin books offered me the chance to rewrite that book to "The Idiots Guide to Green Cleaning," coming out March, 2009.

    The year 2009 will find an expansion of the company and programs available that will give folks an opportunity to earn extra income.
