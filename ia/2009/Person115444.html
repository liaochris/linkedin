Giuseppe "Joe" Di Donna’s Summary

Giuseppe "Joe" Di Donna

I have obtained a Master Degree in Political Science with a sociology specialization and a thesis on Communication care of the Università degli Studi of Milan and, recently, a Ph.D. of Business Administration in Marketing Management care of an American University. I have started off by teaching in an international business school. I have worked as a public relations manager, marketing manager, press office manager for Italian Manufacturers’ Association, national and international private associations, publishing houses, CONI federation, luxury resort in Florida, companies in the beauty arena as well as in internet companies. I have taught for several years at the National Chamber of Italian Fashion and Tessile of Como. I am presently a company communications consultant and I am teaching care of MBA, MSc, BA courses at European School of Economics in Milan and Lucca/Florence campuses and in other international business schools. I have more than 20 years of experience in marketing, communication, sales and moreover in the education sector. I am member of the International Coach Federation. I am a Coach specialized in Leadership
I speak English, Italian and I understand quite good French and Spanish.
