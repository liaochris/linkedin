Lee Boatright’s Summary

Experienced Visual Communicator specializing in print design, logo and branding and print production.

In my history, you will see that I have worked both in agency and direct with client. In all, I have constantly strived to produce the best possible materials to suit the client need.

My goal is to give the client what they want and help them push their envelope while doing it on-time and on-budget.

I am currently expanding my skills at Web Design and Flash User Interface Design. I am training toward a Web Designer Certificate from Emory University graduating in the Summer of 2009.
