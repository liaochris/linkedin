Ellen Silverman’s Experience

  • Owner

    ESAmarketing; Freelance Copywriting

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    May 1981 — Present (28 years 8 months)

    I started the company to provide small businesses with the insights, methods, and creativity to compete in their markets and grow their enterprises to success. My niche market is small to mid-size service businesses, professionals and Realtors. Through ESAmarketing I am a speaker, trainer, and consultant on marketing and entrepreneurship. I work with the Small Business Development Centers, teach for the Women's Business Center and at several community colleges. As a freelance copywriter I provide content for graphic designers and web production companies as well as developing copy for businesses and people who "need their stories told" for award nominations, presentation packages, sales letters, etc. Over the years I have counseled and taught over 700 entrepreneurs and business owners; have spoken to business groups in NY, PA, and throughout NJ; and been published in NJ's business media as well as the major dailies.
