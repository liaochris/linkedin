Truckee Lynch’s Summary

Dynamic, flexible designer who works closely with clients and employers to meet the needs of the project and brand. From brainstorming to deliverables, I focus on the end result with a clean aesthetic and intuition of the audience. I am meticulous, self-driven and goal-oriented with excellent communication skills.
