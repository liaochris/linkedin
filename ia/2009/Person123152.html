  • Legal Secretary

    Maples & Calder, Cayman Islands

    (Law Practice industry)

    1996 — 2000 (4 years)

    I was Legal Secretary in the commercial aircraft leasing department of the leading law firm on Grand Cayman.

    This role involved a high volume of work and demanded absolute attention to detail - the complex legal structures required lengthy legal documents and correspondence, often involving a network of laywers in other time zones around the world.

    Working late in the evening was quite often the order of the day, and in one memorable instance, I stepped in to host a closing meeting when my boss was suddenly taken ill. With lawyers and clients visiting from New York, London and Brazil, we worked until 4am, before re-commencing at 7am for the final document signing.

    Believe it or not, I have to say that was one of my most enjoyable roles!
