Dustin Sparks’s Summary

I am an Artist and Programmer. Some refer to our breed as Interactive Artists, Interactive Designers, Interaction Designer, Interactive Developers, Crazy Mutant Hybrids. Personally, I prefer the latter, but generally I go with interactive developer. I have also been known to use the title Creative Technologist.

Professionally I do most of my work freelancing in web development where I work with such languages and environments as; Photoshop, Illustrator, Maya, After Effects, Flash, Actionscript 2/3, LAMP (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP), and AJAX (Javascript, XML, HTML, CSS, LAMP).

I am also an avid follower of Open Source Flash applications. I regularly make use of such open source projects as Papervision 3D, AMFPHP, Tweener, APE, and Box2DAS3. I also maintain and act as the sole contributor to several open source applications and code libraries.

In professional environments I have been attributed with the ability to quickly learn and implement new and leading-edge technologies. Such as quickly building rapid prototypes and fully functional works in short periods of time using languages and technologies that have not been around long and typically have little or no documentation. I also take pride in my ability to take new technologies and find ways to find real world applications for them.

I am currently an Adobe Flash CS3 Certified Expert, and an Adobe Dreamweaver CS3 Certified Expert and have several years of experience in each.
