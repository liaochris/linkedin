Tiger Bitanga’s Summary

The Design People creates business websites. Since 1999, we've grown into a web development studio with over 7,000 customers.

Our goal is to become the web marketing agency for small businesses. We'll achieve this by recruiting the best people, providing extraordinary customer experiences and maintaining a design portfolio that speaks for itself.

We're best known for Agent Image, our real estate marketing and technology arm. We provide real estate brokers and agents with websites, marketing, technology solutions and leads. We've established a reputation for for representing top-producing agents and high-end brands including Sotheby's International, Hilton & Hyland, Coldwell Banker, Keller Williams and Prudential California Realty.

This year we're honing in on small business owners in key verticals including legal, accounting, dental, mortgage, cosmetic surgery and restaurant industries in the U.S. As part of our strategy of developing niche brands, we also recently opened a full-service Internet marketing division called imFORZA.

I'm currently in the Philippines working on expanding our Manila office. If you're interested in working with us in any way, please don't hesitate to contact me on LinkedIn.
