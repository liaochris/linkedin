Geoff Paddock’s Summary

Geoff Paddock has wide experience in digital media, and launched ICI's corporate website in the mid 1990s. He has worked with many large companies on digital media issues,

Since it was formed in 1999, his company GP Media has worked with major companies in the chemicals, paints and coatings,distribution, engineering and project management areas.

GP Media managed the ICI corporate website for eight years until the company was taken over in 2008 by Akzo Nobel, and transformed the Wolseley plc corporate site in 2006, managing web content until August 2009.

Geoff is one of the original three founder members of the FTSE 100 Website Effectiveness Network which now has more than 200 members from top FTSE and Fortune 500 companies. Latest ventures include Newmediacounsellors , an issues and training consultancy based around social media.

Geoff Paddock’s Specialties:

Strategy and management of high-level corporate websites: Crisis media relations: Media training and interview techniques; Qualified Journalist
