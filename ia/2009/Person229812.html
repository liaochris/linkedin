Terry Cates’s Summary

Cobalt owns half of the web properties for the 22,000 automotive dealerships in North America, who collectively spend $8B on advertising annually. Most of this ad spend is currently in newspaper and radio, but is rapidly shifting to the Web. I’m accountable for the development of platforms for web sites, lead management, email marketing, display advertising and paid search.

Given the industry shift, our business is nothing short of explosive and our success is all about execution of good ideas that consumers respond to. We incubate ideas by getting something out there, see how well it works, improve it. I’m always looking for innovative Java / SQL / presentation developers who want to change the web. We operate in small groups that are rewarded for innovation and ownership. If this is an environment you’re interested in, send me your resume and a note that says what your dream job is to tcates at cobaltgroup
