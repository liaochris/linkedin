Trevor Bain’s Summary

A proactive general manager with a record of applying process and change-management skills to establish and grow business value across a range of industries, from commodity to technology to service.
Success based upon ability to develop and implement business strategies, effectively communicate meaningful goals and objectives from the shop floor to the boardroom, and establish team environment while mentoring members to new levels of achievement.
