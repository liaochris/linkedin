RASA RESTAURANTS INC:
RASA Restaurants Inc., was founded as a new "Tropical Fusion" concept food service company. RASA owns and operates the critically acclaimed Mahalo Grille Restaurant, located in Pleasanton, California which is firmly positioned as a leading tropical fusion concept restaurant. The Mahalo Grille brand is currently available under a licensing opportunity in select areas. For further details, view the slideshare presentation.

RASA FOODS INC:
RASA Foods is a specialty food company that manufactures and distributes gourmet Tropical Fusion cuisine under the Mahalo Grille brand and several other co-branded names. You will find "Mahalo Grille" branded products at Costco Wholesale*, the fifth largest retailer in the United States. Other retailers that carry the Mahalo Grille brand include Whole Foods Markets, another leading U.S. retailer and Draeger's Super Markets, a regional chain of markets in Northern California.
