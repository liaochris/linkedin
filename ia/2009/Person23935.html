Barbara Bitondo de Arene’s Summary

Pythagoras’ Daughter, established in 2007, advances individual transformation using a unique blend of Earth-Centered Spirituality and Frontier Science Processes that help participants develop a life-giving relationship with the world.

Mystery School is an adventure into conscious taking us deeper as we transform the lives we are already living into a journey of awakening.

Our twenty-first century version of this great and ancient tradition is dedicated to the examination of the world's mysticism, the plethora of great masters and spiritual paths.

Sessions are intellectually rigorous and highly experiential. Each focuses on a different aspect of this vast course of study. We study original texts when available learn ancient wisdom and apply Frontier Science processes.

An experiential adventure, Mystery School is ideal for anyone who feels called to serious transformation and spiritual discovery.

Pythagoras’ Daughter is committed to treading very gently on the earth which is why our preferred form of communication is email, printing is done on tree-free paper, and all events are held near public transportation options.

Barbara Bitondo de Arène, the founder of Pythagoras’ Daughter brings to the school a global world view, a Masters Degree from Smith College and a decade of Training and Development at The World Bank Group. She is a certified Appreciative Inquiry Practitioner, a student of Human Capacities with Jean Houston and a member of the Institute of Noetic Sciences.
