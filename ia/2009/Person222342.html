Stefano Oosthof’s Summary

During my working experience in the past I obtained a lot of knowledge about creative marketing, communication and PR. I have invested in an effective personal network and have proved to be a creative mind when needed.

I also have learned a lot about the cultural/entertainment/music-industry. Not only on a work-based level, but also as a participating musician. Driven by an 'out of the box'-way of seeing things, combined with a 'hands-on' mindset, I aim to do things a bit more different, a bit more creative than written in the books.

After several employed jobs within the industry mentioned above I decided to listen to one of the many urges inside of me. No, not the one about Italy and me but the urge of focusing on the freelance marketing, communication and pr-territory! This to obtain more knowledge from working on loose projects, meeting more inspiring people and sharing more creativity with the world!

What does this mean?

That I am yours for the taking when there is a need for creative marketing, communication, publicity or PR (and even a bit of organisation). As a freelancer I can support you whenever and wherever needed. All you have to do is read more about me below, be convinced and contact me!

Kind regards,
