YEARS OF EXPERIENCE:
The founders of ANR have technical engineering and scientific backgrounds and have been in the recruiting industry for over 15 years and in the technology industry for over 25 years.
Our Sr. Account Managers take the time to meet with hiring managers or onsite recruiters so that they not only understand their business but also get a feel for the type of personality that would fit well into a particular group.
DIVERSITY:
ANR is a woman-owned (certification # 237484 through WBENC) technical contract staffing company We are also a member in good standing of the American Staffing Association (ASA).
Our goal is to provide a manager two pre-screened qualified candidates within 48 hours.
VIRTUAL OFFICES:
Unlike the typical brick and mortar companies that were started before the internet, ANR Consultants is built on technologies such as host based systems, video conferencing, messaging, VOIP(Voice Over Internet Protocal), Microsoft Exchange and dash-boarding to allow us to have representation anywhere in the world.
MAJOR ACCOUNT FOCUS:
We are focused on delivering excellent results to our clients. Companies like Genentech, EMC, Blue Shield and Yahoo chose us because of our knowledge database recruiting engine and senior account managers who understand the details of working on difficult searches. Our clients are in numerous industries, including but not limited to, banking, financial services, computer software and hardware, facilities and construction management, health care, insurance, pharmaceutical, telecommunications, manufacturing and distribution, pharmacy /biotech and e-commerce.
