Jorge Franchi, Ph.D.’s Summary

Strong professional relationships with city, county and other state agencies whose mission are to promote economic development. Executive level experience designing, developing, implementing and managing human resources policies and procedures; developing corporate training (stand-up, computer- and web-based); designing process improvement initiatives; identifying organizational issues, and putting together corporate strategy; proven ability to provide critical support in the development and implementation of strategic goals, objectives and initiatives that achieve business results; equally adept at optimizing performance through organizational change; experienced providing general consultation to managers and employees at all levels to address employment issues; excellent skills in problem solving, multi-tasking and communications in technical and strategic services; a trusted company advocate; performed national and international business consulting services for a broad array of clients; currently provide efficient and cost-effective professional consulting services for national and international investors.

Professor of several MBA courses: Human Resources Management, Performance Management, Organizational Communication, Entrepreneurship and Managerial Marketing.

Trilingual (English, Spanish, Portuguese), sharp, disciplined, highly energetic and confident that my technical knowledge and professional, administrative, and management experience will significantly contribute to the professional development, growth and success of any project and organization.
