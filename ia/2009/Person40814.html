Chadwick Boyd’s Summary

LOVELY & DELICIOUS Enterprises, Inc. is a brand consulting and national media collaborative. We are a group of seasoned business consultants, each with at least 15 years of experience, that creates innovative and customized business solutions for some of the most respected and leading food & lifestyle experts and brands.

We leverage our collective experience to bring greater value and worth to your business and most importantly your brand. We enable you to reach your business goals much faster – increasing revenue and saving money overall.

We specialize in:
• Developing purposeful & meaningful brand strategies to help launch or evolve a brand to break through the crowded marketplace.
• Creating inspired & strategic brand marketing plans that clearly align business objectives with the brand strategy to ensure marketability and sustainability.
• Devising & executing marketing & publicity programs to help launch new products, generate sales, drive website traffic, or raise a spokesperson’s profile or company’s overall brand awareness.
• Providing ongoing business counsel so that a brand is protected and the relationships with customers are nurtured and maintained.
• Creating motivational support systems, particularly for CEOs and other key business managers, that provide insight and tools to help them be the best brand stewards possible.

We have led national partnerships with Sigourney Weaver, the Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund and Universal Studios. We also have served Mission Foods; The Atlanta Hawks & Thrashers; DuPont; Stouffer’s; Weber-Stephen Products Co.; Gap; Target; Chef Tyler Florence; Sara Foster; Random House; Accenture; Ralph Lauren; Simon Malls; Food Network: Animal Planet and Discovery. Additionally, we have counseled Bella Cucina, California Pizza Kitchen, Panera Bread, Popeyes Chicken and Biscuits, Seattle’s Best Coffee, The Athlete’s Foot, QVC, Inc. and Marriott Hotels.

Chadwick Boyd’s Specialties:

Brand strategy development; business plan development & counsel; national media; national, regional and local media tours; book proposals and television treatments; brand partnerships, and product roll outs.
