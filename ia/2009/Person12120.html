I have a proven ability to meet deadlines and produce usable, accurate documents. Amy Guenther’s Specialties:

My specialty, and my passion, is editing; I love creating order out of disorder, and cleaning up mistakes which could otherwise undermine a beautiful piece of writing. I have additional experience coordinating reports, which I enjoyed because, like Technical Writing, it requires meticulous organization, careful planning, and consistency.
