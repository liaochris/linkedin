Callie Carling’s Summary

Moonpoppy Enterprises is a “Virtual Assistant” business, providing administrative, secretarial and specialist support and consultancy services to global clients.

With 18+ years of executive-level experience within HR, legal and public sector environments, Moonpoppy Enterprises was set up to provide exceptional high-quality, efficient, flexible, confidential and expert ‘virtual’ solutions to meet today's fast-paced requirements for every business type.

Moonpoppy Enterprises acts as a professional “virtual hand” to individuals, new businesses, SME's (small-medium enterprises) and organisations who fully appreciate the value of partnering with a Virtual Assistant (VA) and the benefits that such a partnership can bring, enabling them to focus on developing and growing their business while the more routine elements are tackled by their cherry-picked VA.

Our ever-expanding client list include:
:: an eclectic mix of small businesses who need assistance with spreadsheet development, developing HR and administration systems and any other type of service which can be easily handled 'remotely'
:: organisations who need an overflow service for transcription or development of system manuals (e.g.)
:: wellbeing professionals, businesses and therapists who need know-how and IT skills to make their presence felt online, including development and design of online forums and e-courses and social networking.

Dovetailing nicely with my therapist training, I am also qualified to teach deep guided relaxation techniques (stress management) to private clients, or in workshops - both private and within the corporate sector.

I am committed to empowering people to develop their own talents through networking and individual opportunities.

I am an active member of 1230 The Womens Company.
