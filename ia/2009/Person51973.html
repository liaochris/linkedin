David Engel’s Summary

I have experience in the toy design industry dealing with preschool, infant, activity/craft, novelty, candy, party favor, and art material products. In my career I have successfully worked with fellow designers and product/brand managers to conceptualize and develop product lines and extensions by brainstorming, sketching, rendering, and producing control drawings in order to bring our ideas to life. I have worked with China in the development process for engineering and the sourcing of products I have worked on and managed. My experience has also been expanded to include working on graphics, illustration and packaging for various products I have worked on.

I hope to continue learning as much as I can about the toy industry and related fields that go along with it. I also always aspire to get better artistically and more knowledgeable of new techniques and technologies. This truly is what I love to do. I went from toy collector to toy maker and nothing has been more enjoyable than seeing ideas and designs come to life.

David Engel’s Specialties:

I have strong sketching and ideation skills. I am extremely proficient with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop and work with these programs extensively in my design process. I have strong control drawing abilities and work mainly in a 2-D medium but I do have a basic knowledge of Vellum Cobalt. I work well with others and I am a dedicated and hard working individual with a passion for design and this industry.
