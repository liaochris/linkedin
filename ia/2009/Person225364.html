  • Music Host / Feature Producer / Music Recording Engineer

    Chicago Public Radio

    (Non-Profit; Broadcast Media industry)

    September 1995 — December 2008 (13 years 4 months)

    Like many public broadcasting professionals, I wore many hats during my 13 years at WBEZ, but primarily my focus was with music and arts coverage:

    Host/Producer of live evening music programs, both jazz and eclectic/free formats

    Local news anchor for, overnight and NPR Morning Edition

    Contributor/Producer of music and arts segments for news and talk

    Producer/Host of long-form music and arts specials

    Producer/Editor/Writer and Series Developer of a weekly nationally syndicated arts/performance series

    Producer for Chicago Jazz and Blues Festivals national broadcasts, carried by 150 public radio stations across the country

    Music Recording Engineer/Producer for in-studio music performances for nationally syndicated and local programming, as well as commercial clients

    ISDN/Studio Rental Engineer: clients include NPR, BBC, Public Radio International, American Public Media and independent producers

    Technical Support Specialist: provided technical and creative training to new hires, on-going production training workshops, audio production assistance and consultation for reporters and producers, managing and purchasing audio equipment for programming staff and for the station’s performance studio

    Programming Daily Duties: produce and prepare programming for broadcast and website content depots, prepare commentary and music selections for my on-air music shifts, anchoring local news shifts that required reading funding copy, news, and meeting the network clock, maintaining programming public files, recording transmitter readings, and more

    Interviews, music engineering and other production work for programming included features on Kurt Vonnegut, Studs Terkel, Frank McCourt, Don DeLillo, Sam Shepard, Kurt Elling, Von Freeman, Sandra Bernhard, Laurie Anderson, Yoko Ono, Swell Season, Nick Cave, Calexico, Ray Davies, Dave Brubeck, Elvin Jones, Buddy Guy, Koko Taylor, and many other internationally recognized and local artists.
