Sherry Eyzaguirre Beckman (LION)’s Summary

As C.E.O. Sherry Eyzaguirre Beckman brings 16 years of leadership experience to STI. Sherry’s background is in training, technical writing and public relations. Formally trained in Entertainment Business at UCLA and USC, her background includes sophisticated and effective project management, technical writing and curriculum development experience in the education, software, entertainment and communications industries. Sherry is skilled training design and delivery, marketing and public relations.

Sherry brings effusive energy and dynamism to STI. Sherry’s project management and business development for STI have encompassed strategic planning, developing partnerships and managing business relationships. Sherry is able to direct multiple tasks effectively and has demonstrated capacity to combine keen business acumen and deal closing skills to successfully drive project initiatives.

Ms. Beckman has served as a mentor for the Los Angeles Unified School District’s Venice High School Empowerment Media Program and is a member of Minority Business Development Association (MBDA), National Defense Industry Association (NDIA), Women in Defense (WID) and Women In Technology International (WITI). Ms. Beckman has a Bachelor’s degree from University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) in Motion-Picture, Television and New Media Production, where she graduated Valedictorian, magna cum laude, under full scholarship - as well as a Master’s Degree in from University of Southern California (USC) in Cinema-Television Production, where she again topped her class, summa cum laude and the producer of 1 of 4 projects selected by committee for full funding.
