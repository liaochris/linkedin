Srivani Dasaraju (srivani.dasaraju@applabs.com)’s Summary

Srivani has significant experience in engaging and managing Software Testing, QA engagements for large scale BFSI, Enterprise Clients as well as developing customized delivery models for ISV’s of all scale. She is presently with AppLabs.com the world's largest software testing and quality management company. With over a decade of experience behind it, AppLabs has become a trusted partner to more than 600 companies, providing both quality assurance and third-party validation. AppLabs is a CMMI Level 5, ISO9001 and ISO27001:2005 accredited.

Srivani is open to questions, regarding how Applabs can derive, deliver and measure QA solutions to enterprise as well as ISV, with a customized solution. If you are looking for one-stop solution for all your QA pain areas, She can facilitate that both in terms technical and business benefits.
If the recent recession has made you stretched of how to control cost and deliver QA results then She can suggest you with flexible engagement models (near shore, onshore, offshore, mix) anything that suits your budget and can ensure smooth delivery. She has developed engagement models, where AppLabs has worked parallel to multiple IT vendors as an independent Test Auditors, ensuring that our relationship with in-house QA and other third-party vendors is collaborative and competitive.
Drop me a note if you need any inputs regarding optimizing your QA and software testing? You can reach me at srivani.dasaraju@applabs.com
