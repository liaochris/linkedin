Julianna Christiansen’s Summary

My experience of 22 yrs. in the Global Consumer Products Industry has given me vast knowledge of brand development & management processes.
I have the unique and proven ability to execute initial concepts & drive the entire process to a finished product, analyzing & incorporating new ideas, themes, & market trends. My creative strengths in color & design, through graphics, textiles and apparel, have been key factors in my success as a designer & merchandiser. I have a strong & proven understanding of the importance of color & graphic direction for specific markets. I have utilized my outstanding artistic skills throughout my career.
My product development experience has covered a broad range of merchandise including apparel, gifts, collectible bears & dolls, greeting cards, & toys. I have had significant experience in overseeing both brand management & licensed product. The major elements of my responsibilities in these positions were to ensure a well-balanced product mix, develop & meet product deadlines, & negotiate critical price points. Directed print & internet catalog development and layout.
For a significant part of my career, I have worked closely with international vendors & manufacturers in development & sourcing. As an example, I lead the development of the “Aristocats” Marie character for Disney Special Events, from the design stage to personally overseeing the product launch in Hong Kong.
I also have outstanding written and oral communications skills. Throughout my career I have worked closely with graphic designers, buyers, merchandisers, sales & management teams. I have supervised up to 10 people on a team.
