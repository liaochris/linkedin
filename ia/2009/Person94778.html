  • General Manager

    (Partnership; 11-50 employees; Research industry)

    May 2005 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    The European Research Consortium for Informatics and Mathematics (ERCIM) is a European Economical Interest Grouping (EEIG) aimed to foster collaborative work within the European research community and to increase co-operation with European industry in the fields of Informatics and Applied Mathematics.

  • W3C/ERCIM Site Manager

    W3C

    (Non-Profit; Internet industry)

    June 2006 — February 2009 (2 years 9 months)

    ERCIM is the European host of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)

  • President & Founder

    Sophia Complexity

    (Biotechnology industry)

    April 2001 — April 2005 (4 years 1 month)

    The objective of Sophia Complexity was to develop, in Sophia Antipolis, the Institute of Life Modelling and Simulation, destined to be a centre for international research, a think tank, a conference centre, a training centre for applying mathematics, IT and automatic control engineering to the complexity of living organisms, and to life modelling and simulation.

    August 1995 — March 2001 (5 years 8 months)

    Chief Information Officer of the genomics company GENSET, listed on the Nouveau Marché and NASDAQ stock exchanges, and lead one of the largest team of bioinformaticians.
