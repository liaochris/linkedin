Steven Metcalf’s Summary

A proactive management professional with a technical and design background backed by two decades of game industry experience. Key positions held within publishers and developers have included Head of Development, Head of Studio, Creative Director, Executive Producer and Lead Programmer.
Extensive history of product delivery and recovery as well as a proven track record for establishing and improving processes to maximise efficiency. Creative skills and an eye for quality have enhanced many titles and generated numerous concepts.
Experienced in all areas of the business including acquisition, finance, legal, negotiation, new concepts, on and off-site project and team management. Clear understanding of the commercial needs of the business while remaining creative and committed to delivering the best possible products.
