Francois Dechery’s Summary

My professional experience is above all diverse and it has always been by design. I have worked in companies of all sizes and stages of development, from startup to established global groups. And in very diverse functions as well, from management consultant to project manager or business unit director. But let's talk about Red Hat...
Red Hat was already the absolute leader of the enterprise Linux market (>60%!) and is now by far the open source leader in middleware thanks to its acquisition of JBoss in 2006. The great growth numbers of JBoss business worldwide provide evidence of a very successful acquisition. I am proud to have been part of this combination of the Linux and Java worlds in a unique company. I do not like the word "integration" by the way. I think Red Hat has reinvented its future with this acquisition and is expanding its presence and influence in the IT world every day.
