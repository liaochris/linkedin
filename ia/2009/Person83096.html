Gajraj Singh’s Summary

Marketing and Product Executive - Information technology, Networking and Security products and services, start ups and high growth initiatives.

Gajraj Singh is founder and CEO of Zivio Networks. He brings to Zivio Networks over 20 years of experience in building successful business in information technology industry.

Gajraj is an accomplished marketing and product executive with extensive experience in various leadership positions including marketing, product management, sales, business development and profit center management. A strategic executive with a unique blend of experience in security, networking and information technology, Gajraj, spearheaded corporate marketing, product management and overall go-to-market strategy for iPolicy Networks, a leading integrated security company. At iPolicy he successfully led repositioning of the company in enterprise market through solid understanding of network security technologies, competition and market requirements. He established and leveraged key relationships in gaining market recognition and traction, acquired major channel partners and marquee customers to attain the technology leader position in the network security market. Through Gajraj's initiatives, the company was recognized by Gartner as the "most visionary network security company" for 3 years in a row.
