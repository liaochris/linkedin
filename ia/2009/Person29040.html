Ghose, Biren’s Summary

Biren is presently responsible for the 'integrated studio model' within EROS International Films. He is excited about enhancing EROS’s global reach by producing, commissioning and acquiring films across all media formats. Formerly COO at Asia's largest animation studio UTV Toons 2001–03 with over 700 animators! Worked with Disney, Teletoon, Cinegroupe, Funbag, Decode, Teleimages, Futurikon & Fox. Created an original series for Walt Disney in 2002 from development to screen. Lead the strategy planning for UTV which was the tipping point for their broadcasting and motion pictures foray in the big league! Biren earlier co founded with UTV a broadband streaming internet company in 1999. Prior to that he was in Corporate Planning at UB, where for 13 years he transitioned across various assignments. UB is today the world's #2 Spirits company. Major brands McDowell, Black Dog & Kingfisher! He also strategised UB's forays into Biotechnology & Engineering. He spent 3 years at international HQs in Dubai, UAE.
Rates his AMP at MIT (Sloan School), USA in Corporate Planning and Management Control as a take off point!.
Biren has been a prolific speaker on convergence, entertainment, animation and new media at a variety of inter-national forums having spoken at Kid Power Asia, Kidscreen Summit; NASSCOM Annual Conferences [since 2002]; Animation Asia, Singapore [wef 2003]; Streaming Media; IT Comdex, E-Biz, Convergence; etc.
A trade show “warrior” GDC; TIGA; MIP/MIPCOM at Cannes [over 14 times!]; E3, AFM; NATPE; & MIFA Annecy. Judge at Superpitch Singapore since 2006 and India 2006 till date. He is an advisor to Copenhagen Business School on computer games. Co-founder of iGITA [Game Industry Trade Association of India] & Chaiman for Animation & Gaming. Conducted a leadership programme for media majors in Australia in November 2007. Animation Magazine voted him one of Asia's 'top toon titans' in April 2008.
