Tami Magaro’s Summary

Every since I was a child, I LOVED the movies! When watching movies, I would look to the deeper meanings and unconscious behaviors to the characters. It has always fascinated me why people do what they do and create what they create. It was no surprise that I would get my degree in Psychology.

I always thought I'd write movies filled with character arc's, personal change and life lessons like In the Bedroom, Sex, Lies, and Videotape, or Interiors. I enjoy watching movies and taking them to a philosophical level of understanding. Why this character reacted this way or something better out there for them. I take this thinking into all my projects by coming up with questions that most people don't really think about asking. I like to go deeper into who they are and how they got to their greatest achievement, through the failures and the setbacks.

I started my professional directing and editing career at Pacifica Community Television. What I loved about this job was the ability to learn all aspects of production. I started as an intern 2 years before being hired on as a Producer. Through this job I was introduced to many people including Sparky Parker who later became the host of Breakers: Exploring the Stoke. I learned so much from creating this show and it continued my love for surfing. That was my true highlight at PCT.

I also have a background in business finance. I've had several jobs as a Jr. Accountant, Accounts Payable, and bookkeeping. I come from a family of financial wizards.

Since co-creating Mindeliver Media, I have directed and edited the majority of our projects and received several awards. In 2009, our company has expanded into the Social Media arena. I spend a lot of my time keeping up to date on all the new applications for social media, twittering and blogging. I'm an excellent Project Manager and excel in time management. My favorite part of a new project is the detail going into the schedule and deadlines.
