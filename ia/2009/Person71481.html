Erin Lindholm’s Summary

I am passionate about contemporary art, food, fashion, media—all things pushing the boundaries in our culture at large. I am seeking a staff editorial position in print or online media where I can apply my breadth of knowledge in these subjects, generate ideas and stories that are timely, relevant and interesting, and utilize my technical and management skills.

Erin Lindholm’s Specialties:

I am proficient in InDesign, InCopy, Quark Express, familiar with Photoshop, and fluent in basic HTML and Web CMS software. As an editor I have the macroscopic vision to think about a project conceptually and the microscopic vision to pick out where in a paragraph there's an extra space.
