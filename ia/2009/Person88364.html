Gopal Shenoy’s Summary

1) Accomplished 13 years product management leader with a track record of building highly successful SaaS-based and enterprise software products.
2) Experienced in developing product strategies and then leading product management and cross functional teams to execute these strategies using a very rapid, iterative product development process (Agile, modified waterfall).
3) Passionate blogger on product management, product usability and social media topics at Software Product Manager (http://productmanagementtips.com) - one of the prominent product management blogs.
4) Experienced in Market Research using qualitative methods such as customer visits, focus groups, phone interviews and also quantitative methods such as surveys. Have personally conducted more than 300 on-site customer visits in 10 countries.
5) Strong international software product management experience having worked with companies ranging from Fortune 500 companies to small to medium businesses in 10 countries.
6) Speaker at User Conferences, ProductCamps, Product Management conferences.
