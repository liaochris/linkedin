Christopher Beach’s Summary

Christopher Beach is currently the President of Illuminositi.com, Inc. - with rev stream Bid2Learn.com. A new venture, where he is bringing to bear the culmination of his 15 years of Executive and Entrepreneurial knowledge for the single-minded goal of success - from vision to start-up activity, to business strategy implementation & execution to real world day-to-day ops, to macro level finance, both Private and Commercial, to Exit Strategy.

Mr. Beach has a broad range of consulting and business experience with an emphasis on Strategic Venture, Business Development, Sales and Marketing, M&A, Operations, and the Capital Markets.

As Vice-President of Strategic Ventures, he honed his skill set by orchestrating multi-million dollar communication contracts with Fortune 500 corporations, from conception to closing, to execution and implementation.

He was the driving force in taking the CGMW Company from a $0 entity to a $45MM global importer of Granite and manufacturing supplies from 7 countries located in South America, Europe, the Middle East and Asia. He created currency risk strategies, risk assessment, metrics for all aspects of the business, and negotiated directly, the pricing and time to port/facility. He was also tasked with managing a complicated RTO of CGMW as the Company went Public.

As VP of Strategy for CBR, Inc., he supervised and managed the 109% year over year growth of the company during its’ expansion from Southwest Florida to an additional 4 States within 9 months; including multi-million dollar contracts with The Home Depot. The Company was eventually sold through highly successful negotiations to an AmEx PubCo.
