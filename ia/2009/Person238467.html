Troy Bohlke’s Summary

Troy Bohlke, age 42, is one of the Co-founders and Director of Investor Relations for the Westward Fund as well as Managing Member of Arizona Acquisitions Group, LLC. He also founded and currently serves at Chief Executive Officer for Phoenix based design communications and public relations firm, Niche Focus Group, LLC. Early on, Mr. Bohlke demonstrated his natural affinity for business leadership, as President of the Entrepreneur Club during his time at Arizona State University. In February of 2001, he co-founded Emissions Technology, Inc, an Emissions equipment manufacture and until September of 2008 fulfilled various roles within the
company, including President, Board Member, Vice President of Investor Relations, and Vice President of Marketing. Earlier in his career, Troy founded and was former Publisher of the New Homeowners Guide, a community service guide designed specifically to help home buyers adapt during the relocation and "move in" process. His entrepreneurial background includes fund raising, public relations, marketing, real estate investing, and publishing. Troy has over twenty years experience with starting and building businesses, during which he has successfully raised millions of dollars to build the enterprises he founded. Real Estate has always been a passion because of its tangible aspect.
Goals are to build our Agency growing through our Social Networking Friends at the Social Media Bible and building our 50M Real Estate Fund to 100M in the next 12 months for Fund II. Be happy and healthy.
