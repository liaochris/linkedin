Jim Coakley’s Summary

Hi. I'm Jim Coakley. I spent 30+ working in advertising agencies before moving over to the client side. Hard work, smart strategic thinking and great teamwork pay off - I've won three EFFIE Awards from the American Marketing Association for generating double-digit growth with creative marketing and communications strategies. I know my way around developing innovative marketing strategies and launching profitable campaigns.

My industry experience includes: education, consumer packaged goods, retail banking, supermarkets, shopping centers, "bricks and clicks", restaurants, beer, wine, consumer durables, photography, hotels, HBA, toys, soft drinks, and travel.
