Laura Rodrian’s Summary

I am a combination of a designer, programmer, people-person and information specialist who is passionate about creating meaningful, potentially world-changing user experiences. My daily quest is to find my niche in the realm of user experience engineering and to make the ever-growing digital world a more people-friendly place.

I am currently pursuing a M.S. degree in Information at the University of Michigan School of Information. My specializations include Human Computer Interaction and Incentive Centered Design. My design approach focuses on user-research and contextual inquiry to dig deep for the best, most profitable solutions. I am particularly interested in how mobile devices and digital services will become increasingly integrated into our daily tasks and how this can improve our lives rather than merely “add to the noise.”

Laura Rodrian’s Specialties:

Contextual inquiry, persona and scenario development, interaction design and prototyping, mechanism design, information architecture, online communities, e-mail marketing and analytics, project management, Flash prototyping, XHTML and CSS, web accessibility, web standards
