Responsible for Execution of Executive Search & Selection (ESS) assignments for various KPMG clients…

The job involves candidates sourcing & evaluation as well as all related documentation to meet unique client needs.

- Key Responsibilities & Achievements:

• Executing international search assignments and recruitment of Regional & Senior Professionals for KPMG ESS clients.

• Assist in formulating and further development of ESS strategy for KPMG - KSA by suggesting and implementing new ideas and more efficient work processes.

• Assist in Marketing of ESS services to new and existing clients through clients-relationship management, & prepare proposals for new clients.

• Search for suitable candidate profiles (CVs) in KPMG Database and job sites, as well as through references and head hunting.

• Match available CVs to specific client requirements to develop shortlist, & conduct interviews to ascertain profile match between candidate background and client requirements.

• Document candidate assessments and forward the same to the client for further coordination on client interviews and selection.

• Check references and suitability of applicants before referral to employers for interviews and final selection.

• Maintain contact with successful candidates to ensure joining of the candidate efficiently.

• Communicate with colleagues in other KPMG offices as and when required to facilitate ESS work.

• Work as an integral part of the team, and actively contributing to the work of ESS function by collaborative problem-solving.
