Art Italo’s Summary

- Legal marketing and management consultant

- Marketing advice for building law practices

- Management tips on strategic planning, productivity, staffing

- Working exclusively with the legal profession since 1992.

- Over 300 legal clients in Atlanta and across the U.S.

- Frequent speaker in US and Canada.

- Over 15,000 hours in consultation with lawyers and law firms

I work with lawyers in Atlanta face to face and by phone with lawyers from across the US. I serve the role of both consultant and success coach to numerous lawyers. I work to develop close supportive relationships with my clients to motivate them to establish and achieve goals for their practices consistent with their personal and professional aspirations.

I constantly look for new ways to bring value to existing clients and help them to adapt to changing conditions in their businesses. I take pride in the long term relationships I have established with clients, with many lasting a decade or more.
