Anna De Souza’s Summary

Originally from Kearny, NJ, this New York City resident has worked in publicity/communications over four years for an eclectic client listing that has ranged from fashion to travel and celebrity to publishing. An admitted news junkie, additional interests also include journalism, social marketing, and art/photography.

Her experience within public relations extends into script writing, media training, event coordination, and extensive work with editorial stories and books that are revamped and pitched for television, print, and online media coverage. At Workhouse Publicity, she handled 20+ books per season for two years, ranging in genre from home, fashion, food/wine, travel and more. She currently handles publicity initiatives on behalf of Fitness, More, and Ladies' Home Journal magazines at Meredith Corporation where she is well-versed in story promotion, specifically in the health, celebrity, fashion, beauty, and lifestyle sector, as well as viral campaigns to promote cover stars, events and breaking news stories.

De Souza graduated from Rutgers University with a B.A. in Psychology. She loves writing, traveling (and tricking natives into thinking she’s a local herself!), volleyball, photography and painting.

Anna De Souza’s Specialties:

De Souza has considerable experience working within the food/restaurant, lifestyle, luxury, travel and fashion sectors and is knowledgeable in event coordination and logistics having overseen the More Magazine Marathon + Half Marathon , the More / Wilhelmina 40+ Model Search, and the Fitness Mind, Body, Spirit Games, and book tour events at Bergdorf Goodman, Saks Fifth Avenue and Sephora with sponsors such as Paper and Elle magazines.
