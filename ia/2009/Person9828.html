Allen Miller, DC, DACBSP’s Summary

Dr. Allen Miller has worked with several companies across the nation helping them reduce their work related injuries, and immediately increasing their bottom line profits.

Allen Miller worked his way through college as a bodyguard for numerous celebrities, including Dinah Shore and Farrah Fawcett as well as the owners of Herbalife. As a body guard and an avid horseman, Allen was no stranger to injury, breaking numerous bones on more than one occasion. During those injuries, Allen became very familiar with Chiropractic Treatment and rehabilitation. With this experience Allen decided to seek a career as a chiropractor and specialized in Sports & Industrial Medicine. Dr. Miller spent many years on the Track & Field Circuit in the US, Africa and Europe treating elite athletes. His career culminated when he was invited to be a team doctor for the 1992 and again for the 1996 Olympic Games.

Dr. Miller is now President of Kinematic Solutions adapting the same computerized testing equipment, treatment and rehabilitation techniques he used as a Sports Doc. he is responsible for creating and managing medical provider networks, Safety Programs, Human Capital Management Solutions that reduce workers compensations injuries. These programs have been proven to dramatically increase Profits, Productivity and Employee Moral.

In one instance Dr. Miller helped Lincoln Industries, that were on the brink of losing their Workers Compensation Insurance and going out of business to one of the Forbes 500 safest companies in 18 months. In another instance Dr. Miller reduced injuries and cost 84% enabling the company compete with international competitors.

Dr. Miller has been on MSNBC, The Big Idea with Donny Deutsch, and interviewed on several news stations nationally. Dr. Miller is a noted speaker and published author.

Allen Miller, DC, DACBSP’s Specialties:

Medical claims management, implementing Ergonomic, Wellness, Safety, Hiring programs as well as implementing workers compensation reduction strategies.
