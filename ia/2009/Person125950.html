Kapil Daga’s Summary

Kapil Daga has a Masters Degree in Chemical Engineering from MIT, USA and Masters degree in Chemical and Pharmaceutical Engineering from NUS, Singapore. He has earned his Bachelors in Chemical Engineering from IIT Bombay in the year 2006. Currently, Kapil is working as a Process Engineer in Novartis Singapore Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Ltd where he is involved in trouble shooting of production equipments, process improvement and in commissioning and installation of new process equipments. Prior to this role Kapil has worked as a Technical Consultant for BASF in Ludwigshafen, Germany and GSK in Raleigh, USA. In this role he developed simulation softwares for various chemical processes and provided solutions to optimize their performance. During his undergraduate studies Kapil has also worked as a Technical Analyst at EPFL, Switzerland for a construction material group on a technical consulting project in cement industry. He frequently gave presentations to his clients and his key achievements in this role included developing a succesful and a robust model for predicting the strength of a concrete with time.

Kapil has won prestigious awards and scholarships for his academic excellence. His long term career goal is to acquire leadership positions in public/private enterprise. With strong technical background, at present he is working to hone his skills in the business arena. Kapil has demonstrated quick learning capability and adaptability by his diverse and successful project work experience. Over past few months he has constantly involved himself in learning concepts in finance, marketing, operations and strategy.
