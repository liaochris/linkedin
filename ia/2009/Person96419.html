Hugh Griffiths’s Summary

A results orientated senior executive with experience in telecoms, media and technology. Proven capability in business strategy, marketing, sales, product management, digital content licensing and digital advertising. A leader in the telecoms/digital media market with experience across online, TV, and mobile supported by strong commercial experience from other sectors.

Hugh Griffiths’s Specialties:

P&L & man management Europe wide. Marketing, commercial negotiation, fixed and mobile networks, digital devices, content & applications, press and PR. Also experienced with M&A's and IPO's. Highly numerate with a strong intellect, a good problem solver. Experienced, respected man manager.
