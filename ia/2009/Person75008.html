Erika Claessens - writer/journalist’s Summary

I have a high degree of excellence in Dutch. I have 20 years of experience working as a writer and as a reporter for newspapers and magazines in Belgium and The Netherlands. I can work on a wide range of subjects.

As a reporter/writer
I am a meticulous editor and highly versatile writer with a creative, clean approach to content. My writing specialities include (amongst others): travel, business, ‘human interest’, lifestyle, women, environmental causes, politics and Corporate Social Responsibility. I am very interested in all kinds of new technology/(social) media.

As a trainer
I am dealing with the following matters: 'new media technologies', 'entrepreneurial skills for starters', 'skills for government employees', 'writing skills', 'certified management training'.
