Alex Bernstein’s Summary

Since I stopped living the life of an NFL superstar *, and started to use my head for more than a battering ram, I've spent the last 9 years working as a senior executive, board member, entrepreneur, and investor at the intersection of new technologies and digital media surrounded by genius technologists from MIT, a billionaire entrepreneur, and a rock star team of branding and marketing gurus.

I'm currently part of an amazing team at North where we work with smart investors, and entrepreneurs to find, filter, and fund innovative new ventures.

Our secret sauce is helping investors determine whether or not a venture warrants investment, and if it does, how to give that venture the best possible chances at success.

* Truthfully, I was a NFL league minimum earning, tiny division III college undrafted free agent, a perennial 6' 3" 340 pound long shot academic All-American, who was traded twice, released 5 times by four teams over 4 seasons, and who was once affectionately referred to by a Pro-Bowl teammate as just another "all-effort no-talent" player, which may be why my John Madden NFL '99 video game rating was only a 63. In my defense, he (Madden) rides in a giant bus everywhere because of an irrational fear of flying, so I don't think anyone should take anything he says too seriously.

Alex Bernstein’s Specialties:

The North sweet spot is helping entrepreneurs and investors make a go or no-go decision. We support this decision process with an extremely strong evaluation methodology called the Venture 360 Report.

If you are sitting on a remarkable product or business idea and you are not sure how to make it happen, then you should talk to North.

    2006 — Present (3 years)

    Working with a number of other retired NFL players I set up the Relentless Foundation in early 2007. We operate a number of platforms that seek to accomplish this mission. 1) We run free leadership and football skills camps for elite high school football players that are coached by retired NFL players. 2) We publish a free social networking site for former NFL players to connect directly with young football players.
