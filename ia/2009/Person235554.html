Tomas Restrepo’s Summary

I have more than 8 years of experience as a professional software developer, working on several different technologies.

I'm a frequent contributor to the Microsoft developer community: I've written several articles on .NET technologies, answer common questions on the various newsgroups and forums in the community and have contributed in a number of Open Source and Public Domain projects, including NAnt, NAntContrib, FKSec and Apache Qpid.

My blog (Commonality) is where I share a lot of things I find interesting about software development and different tools I use day to day, including .NET, BizTalk, WCF, PowerShell, Vim and Linux.

I co-founded devdeo, a small consulting company in 2006. I hold a bachelors degree in computer science from Universidad EAFIT. I've been awarded several times since 1999 as a Microsoft MVP in the categories of Visual C++, .NET and now BizTalk Server. Before founding devdeo, I worked for 5 years as a senior developer and architect at InterGrupo S.A.
