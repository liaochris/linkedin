Linda Castrone’s Summary

Seasoned writer and editor, writing teacher and coach with experience in marketing, publicity and public relations. Good visual and planning skills make me an excellent project manager and packaging consultant.

Linda Castrone’s Specialties:

Business journalism, travel and tourism, health and fitness, social issues, urban design and city planning. Special expertise in creativity enhancement, team building, effective brainstorming, and narrative non-fiction writing.
