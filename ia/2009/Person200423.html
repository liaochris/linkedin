Robert Garner’s Summary

Thirty years of management, architecture, and design engineering in both product development and research at Sun Micro, Brocade, Xerox PARC, and IBM Research. Innovative, high-performance and award-winning products include: Sun's SPARC architecture; Sun-4/200 SPARC Workstation; UltraSPARC-I microprocessor; SPARCCenter-2000 enterprise server; Sillkworm FibreChannel switches and ASICs; Xerox STAR Workstation; first 10-mbps Ethernet NIC; and the scalable, fail-in-place, liquid-cooled 3D "IceCube" server.

Robert Garner’s Specialties:

Engineering management; computer systems and networking technology/design; storage systems
