Deanna Ferrari’s Summary

I am an account executive at WordWrite Communications responsible for day-to-day media relations, writing, and account management for several clients including Redstone Highlands Senior Living Communities, KIT Solutions, and Regency Global Transportation Group. I am also WordWrite's internship program coordinator and the agency's resident social media specialist.

Prior to WordWrite, I was an assistant account executive at MARC USA in the PR department. At MARC, I provided support on several national accounts including Bryant Heating and Cooling Systems, All-Clad, Krups, and Culver's restaurants.

In addition to my role at WordWrite, I am an active board member for the Pittsburgh chapter of Public Relations Society of America, where I serve as the Speakers Series chair identifying and arranging major speakers for the chapter's professional development events and the professional advisor to the Clarion University chapter of PRSSA.

Finally, I am very active in the social media space, having helped numerous companies in the integration of their social media initiatives. I am also available for speaking opportunities. Most recently, I spoke at the 4th Annual PA Small Business Technology Conference.
