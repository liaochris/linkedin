Husain Hakim’s Summary

I am passionate about the user experience of digital and online environments, and my leadership and effective project management skills have resulted in inspired and efficient teams.

With 10 years of progressive work experience and a deep understanding of user behaviour, I have developed and delivered user experience strategies and built design solutions that have resulted in satisfying and desirable Web sites, intranets and Web applications.
