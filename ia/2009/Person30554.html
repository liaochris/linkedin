Bob Taketani’s Experience

  • Director of Operations

    Leiner Health Products

    (Executive Office industry)

    1999 — 2008 (9 years)

    Softgel Manufacturing (Vitamins-Nutritional Supplements)
    Responsible for all aspects of the plant operations including Q.A.. and H.R. dotted line with a $6.5 million annual budget. During my tenure through-put time was reduced from 52 days to 8 days. Process time was reduced from 19 hours to 13 hours (Industry Standard: 48-72 hours). Material turns were increased from 3 to 35 turns per year. Out Put was increased from 5.8 million to 8.5 million per day while rejects were reduced from 3% to 0.05% (Industry Standard: 3%). Reduced direct labor force from 92 to 60 and indirect labor force from 18 to 11 (a cost savings of $850,000 annually). Therefore, under my leadership, plant operations were under budget or met budget at all times with an on time delivery performance that exceeded 99%. On call 24/7, 365 days.
