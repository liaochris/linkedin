Theos McKinney’s Summary

A highly motivated, resilient and creative lawyer and leader. Expertise as senior employment counsel in defending charges of all types of discrimination at federal and state agencies, partnering with outside counsel to defuse or defeat employment lawsuits, and executing strategies to reduce risk by increasing inclusion and employee engagement. Experience as a diversity executive in achieving significant objectives in complex organizational environments by providing insight, sharing vision, shaping strategies, counseling leaders, and influencing executives.
