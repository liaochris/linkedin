Andrea Stenberg’s Summary

Andrea J. Stenberg is publisher of The Baby Boomer Entrepreneur blog. The blog helps Baby Boomers with information and advice about marketing their small business with traditional marketing strategies combined with social media and other online tools.

"It's not about what buttons to push. It's about how to integrate social media into an overall marketing and business strategy," says Andrea.

In addition, Andrea Stenberg publishes the More Effective Marketing email newsletter. This weekly ezine is tightly focused on providing specific marketing tips and strategies.

Subscribers to the ezine also receive a free copy of her ebook "The Baby Boomer's Ultimate Guide To Getting Publicity" and a recording of the teleseminar "How To Build Your Network Using the Social Networking Site LinkedIn with Mickael Nadeau".

Andrea's More Effective Marketing Podcast is a more recent addition to her offerings. Recorded before a live teleseminar audience, this series of interviews with leading business experts provides valuable marketing and business advice.

The next live teleseminar is Tuesday August 19th at 1:30 pm eastern (Toronto). "Get Smart About Your Cash Flow & Extending Credit" with business consultant and credit management expert.

For advertisers looking to reach this important demographic, Andrea is selling a limited number of advertising spaces on the blog and the podcast. Please see www.TheBabyBoomerEntrepreneur.com/advertise for more information.
