Phil Hopkins’s Summary

I own and manage Colvert Group - a company that specialises in recruitment and retention for the renewable energy, energy efficiency & clean-tech sectors.

With clients & candidates across all disciplines of renewable energy we are certainly well placed to assist you whether you're looking for a new position, or indeed, looking for the next member of your team.

Based in Macclesfield - Cheshire, with a southern office now open (Waterlooville - Hampshire), we're a dynamic, ambitious and motivated business that recognises the value of forming mutually beneficial partnerships with our clients & candidates alike.

I'm always on the lookout for new additions to the Colvert team so if you're a seasoned consultant with proven ability, or indeed, if you're looking to begin your career in the recruitment sector, don't hesitate to send me an e-mail or give me a call.
