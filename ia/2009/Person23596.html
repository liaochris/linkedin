Interested in early-stage startups as well as companies that are in trouble and need a consultant to help turn them around.

Marvin Baker’s Experience

  • Partner

    Cafe Nashville

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    March 2008 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Nationally syndicated Radio & TV show featuring emerging and established Country Music acts. Cafe Nashville airs on Billboard/R&R and Mediabase chart reporting radio stations from Broadcast groups that included Citadel and Clear Channel stations. The Television version of the show is aired on its on VOD channel in Comcast in most markets. Cafe Nashville has featured everyone from Rascal Flatts, Carrie Underwood, Billy Ray Cyrus, Toby Keith and Keith Urban to emerging artists such as Bomshel, Clay Underwood and Katie Armiger.

  • Co-Owner

    Baker/Gillis Film & Television Syndication

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    January 1994 — Present (15 years 11 months)

    As an independent Film & Television producer and TV syndicator, films & TV projects produced by Baker/Gillis have been seen on NBC, ABC, CBS, CMT, CMT Canada, CBC Canada, CTV Canada, VHI, MTV, The Today Show, Montel Williams, Entertainment Tonight, Hard Copy, Inside Edition. Partners, Marvin Baker & Ann Gillis directed & produced their first motion picture with Bret Michael's, Charlie & Martin Sheen and released it to 38 countries in 1998. Baker/Gillis produced & theatrically released the Animal Band Children's film via Regal Cinemas. That film earned an "Excellence in Media Award" presented by Senator Hillary Clinton & Senator Conrad Burns at a Washington DC gala for the E-911 Institute. Baker/Gillis are currently under contract with ABC and Comcast, offering their own brand of "TV Concepts & Ideas" as television network affiliate support contractors through Baker/Gillis Film & Television Syndication.

  • President

    Venice Film Merchants

    (Privately Held; Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    January 1992 — Present (17 years 11 months)

    Distributes Feature Films in 38 countries around the world.
