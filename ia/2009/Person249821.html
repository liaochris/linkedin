Yemi Onigbode’s Summary

A budding entrepreneur, with a wealth of skills.

Over 15 years of experience in Financial and Management systems development and maintenance.

He has progressed from the early days of the IBM mainframe based systems to the present day of web based distributed systems.

Worked with large multinational firms on projects including Oracle Corporation, Sun Microsystems, Accenture, Fujitsu Consulting, CMG and JP Morgan Chase.

IT Trainer, Programmer, Developer, Architect and Project Manager, specialised mainly in financial models.

Project managed and developed a number of software applications

Keen believer in self-development and research – at par with the current developments in the IT and communications industry.

Currently developing and implementing e-business solutions for large and medium sized companies, fully integrating their business applications with the Internet and providing a totally integrated solution.

Developing in Java since 1996 and implementing / customising Oracle Applications since 1997.

Oracle Financials Certified Trainer, Sun Certified Instructor and a Fellow of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants. Bachelor of Science (Honours) degree in Pure and Applied Mathematics.

Technical writer on Accountancy and IT issues.

Presents at Accounting and IT seminars; Motivates and mentors young adults on career management and planning.

Currently researching into sensor based technology integration with Oracle e-business suite and SAP. With a particular interest in the use of Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) in asset tracking and monitoring key indicators, to enable organisations to gain greater visibility into their operations, and make decisions based on real-time information.


If you are interested in RFID developments lets Synergise!
