Vicky De Vynck - Vanhaerens’s Summary

After having spend a large number of years in sales and purchase functions in sportswear I changed for the broadcast industry. In the latter one I had positions as purchaser and event manager. In my last position as event manager I was responsable for the creation, development and management of exhibits for trade shows and road shows. Start of 2009 I have decided to start my own business in the tourism and leisure sector. The company name is Oenomad, in other words guide touristical nomads to let them discover the largest wine region in the world : the Languedoc-Rousillion in the South of France.
