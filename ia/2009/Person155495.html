Matteo Minotti’s Summary

I work as ICT senior Project Manager within BluePhoenix. BluePhoenix Solutions (NASDAQ: BPHX) is a leading provider of value-driven modernization solutions that enable companies to leverage their IT assets for increased business value and improved competitive advantage. I am currently involved in Portals modernization at CartaSi, the Italian leader in electronic payment systems. The activities are performed Off Shore throughout one of our Delivery Center. My role is to coordinate the migration activities and update the customer management about the working progress, results achieved and facilitate the solution of potential issues.
Previously I worked as ICT Project & account manager at Keyclient. KeyClient is one of the most important Credit Card player in Italy born in September 2007 after Bankamericard spin-off from Deutsche Bank Group.
I followed Credit Cards Issuing, online services projects and starting from December 2007 I was nominated ICT Innovation Manager aiming to introduce innovation and improvements in the company.
Within Deutsche Bank I worked as Project Manager and I was involved in two high critical projects: IT infrastructure moving following Bankamericard spin-off and setup of a new Credit & Collection System accordingly to world wide Deutsche Bank strategies.
I worked one year for IBM UK (Greenock Scotland) as System Analyst and Project Manager. The multi cultural and multi ethnical IBM environment have contributed to improve and strengthen my ability to work as a member of a team and boost my communication skills.
