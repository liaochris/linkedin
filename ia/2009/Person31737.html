Brad Nimmons’s Summary

A diverse career that's included work in just about every communications medium: cartoonist and reporter for The Florida Times-Union; disc jockey for 2 different Florida radio stations; Account Executive for The Nielsen Company—handling 9 television markets; on-camera entertainment reporter for WFLA-TV; and 13 years as WFLA-TV’s research director (overseeing countless research projects).

Brad earned his bachelor’s degree in Florida and master’s degree in Texas. A few years back he began work for WJKS-TV (now WCWJ) in Jacksonville and was moved quickly by Media General (MG) to WFLA-TV in Tampa. He's now Director of Research for MG, with oversight of the research, sales training and information needs of its newspapers, online properties and television stations throughout the U.S.

Brad Nimmons’s Specialties:

Management of primary and syndicated research; supervisor of numerous software systems and web sites; PowerPoint maven and presentation guru; creator of numerous sales training and other interactive learning management system materials; negotiating skills due to the myriad vendors with whom he's dealt over the years.
