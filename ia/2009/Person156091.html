Matt Carroll’s Summary

Reporter with extensive experience analyzing complex issues and boiling them down into plain English. Specializes in using/creating databases to find trends and stories. I also cover stories of regional interest south of Boston.

Matt Carroll’s Specialties:

Databases, using Access or Excel. Versed in online production so others can search for information.
