Laura Hatcher Havis’s Summary

Laura Havis has over 17 years of utility experience with the Lower Colorado River Authority (LCRA) in Austin, Texas. Some of her career achievements have been managing the development and deployment of the first online bill presentment (B2B) and customer extranet used by LCRA's electric wholesale customers; directed the delivery of the Solar for Schools program to central Texas schools; and oversaw the development of a program and services billing database reducing monthly billing processes and providing timely financial reporting.
