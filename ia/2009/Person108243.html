Jeff Mendelson’s Summary

Over the past 10+ years, I have built a strong track record of success in meeting business goals through building sales, restructuring processes, leading teams in executing major projects, and improving key client relationships. My contributions have consistently and repeatedly strengthened competitive advantage by enhancing efficiency, quality of service, market share, product offerings, and overall professionalism.

Throughout my career, in positions that have drawn on the combination of my sales, leadership, and technical skills, primarily in international/multinational, technically oriented companies, I have built a strong portfolio of accomplishments, including contributions to sales gains, cost savings, and overall bottom-line performance. I have a natural proclivity for leadership and innate business savvy, and have built on these talents through my degree in business management, as well as my MBA in International Business (completed in December 2006).

My early training and career was in hands-on technology roles (I possess the MCSE credential), providing me with knowledge that has been extremely beneficial in technology companies, as I effectively bridge the divide between sales/marketing departments and developers. Besides my native English, I speak Spanish and Hebrew fluently, skills that have been valuable in overseas assignments in Israel – where I helped grow a start up telecommunications firm – and Mexico – where I was the sole representative of a communications solutions provider.

My current passions are Joomla (Content Management System) and SugarCRM (Customer Relationship Management) - These are the best open-source applications available today. I am a Joomla expert. I have launched hundreds of websites based on Joomla across many different verticals including social networking, real estate, eLearning, eCommerce, etc. I have also done custom programming of components, modules and templates.
