Michael Crockett’s Summary

Michael Crockett is a native of Texas. He graduated from Southern Methodist University in 1997, where he received a Bachelor of Science degree in Economics. His multi disciplinary degree focused on Econometrics and International Trade.

Having completed internships with Dean Witter and Alex. Brown, Michael began his professional career in the Financial Analyst program at Goldman Sachs in New York. After returning to Dallas to complete the two year Analyst program at Goldman, Michael spent 5 years managing the trading desk for William K Woodruff, a $100 million family office. Responsibilities included running the trading desk, beta-testing new ECN technology for Option Trading and managing private equity investments. In 2004 Michael spent a year as an institutional salesman in Southern California with The Seidler Companies, a boutique investment bank.

Michael subsequently joined Brazos Capital Management as a Sr. Equity Trader in September 2005. His responsibilities include managing best execution technologies as well as the unbundling process between executions, street research, and the coordination of soft dollar payments.
