Tonya Witherspoon’s Summary

Tonya is currently the manager of eLearning for Global Education Services at LSI Engenio Storage Group. She has over 20 years of experience in training and education focused on areas of engineering and computer sciences. Tonya has managed private companies developing and delivering training services as well as consulting to business and education sectors on incorporating new technologies in their training and education programs. She was the Director of Technology and Virtual Services for Educational Services of Central Kansas and worked as an educational technology specialist at Wichita State University. She continues as an online instructor at Wichita State University teaching graduate classes in robotics, distance education, web 2.0 technologies and linking technology to curriculum standards.

Tonya Witherspoon’s Specialties:

Web Development, LMS, CMS, Remote Application Sharing, Voice, Data, and Video over IP, Web 2.0 Tools, Wikis, Blogs, Webcasts, Podcasts, Vodcasts, RSS, Desktop, Classroom, and High Definition Video Conferencing, Instant Messaging and other Presence Technologies, Digital Video and Graphics, Interactive Media, Digital Portfolios, Digital Storytelling, 2D Animation, 3D Modeling, Clay and Stop-Motion Animation, CAD, Scenarios, Simulations, and Reusable Learning Objects and Frameworks.
