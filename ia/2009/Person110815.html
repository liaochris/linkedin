Jeroen Bouserie’s Summary

After several positions in online media; from the web-content side over on line news and classifieds to e-advertising and e-marketing I moved into the consultancy area where I can use all my experience gained over the years to assist in developing web concepts and on-line strategies. The broad knowledge gained in e-marketing, web 2.0, web analytics, SEO, SEM, usability and webdesign, e-mailmarketing, campaign management, internet advertising traficking, adwords and adsense, on line special projects (with offline crossover) and CRM makes me a valuable asset for your team.

    Spotter was founded within VumRegie (now Corelio Connect) as a classifieds project that covers Travel, Auto, Immo, Jobs and Varia (others) ads (i.e. classifieds) on line and off line. Started as a classifieds-project for the VUM newspapers it was soon transformed into a larger scale project together with PassePartout (the largest freesheets-publisher in Belgium) . When VUM Media changed into Corelio, Spotter became a division (an NV to be exact) on its own. At the end of 2006 the startup-work was done but an improved version - lets call it a 2.0 version of the Spotter-concept - was allready underway. During this period I carried out a very wide range of tasks from making webpages and artwork, making newsletters to visiting potential national and international partners.
