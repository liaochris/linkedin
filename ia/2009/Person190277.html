Ryan Sawchuk’s Summary

Graduate of Princeton University

Denizen of the Net since 1997: Co-founded Regional ISP, Dream Communications located in Boston Massachusetts and Serving Southern New England and New Hampshire. Sold interest in Dreamcom in 2000 and went to work for Amazon.com in 2000. Later moved to X10 Wireless where I was lead analyst for one of the most successful Direct Marketers in the history of the Internet -in 2002 X10 was 6th most visited Website in US. Moved to California where I helped LowerMyBills.com grow into one of the largest Internet Advertisers while also launching BillPay Plus, an online bill payment and presentment service from LowerMyBills.com.

Left LowerMyBills.com after it was sold to Experian & moved to PayPal & eBay where I managed the Marketing Program for PayPal's Consumer Credit Card, Business Debit Card, Online Bill Payment service as well as PayPal's industry leading Money Market Fund. Additionally helped develop strategy for PayPal Buyer Credit, a private label capital program focused on both on and off eBay transactions.

Currently Head of Online Marketing & Social Media Strategy for Pure Digital Technology, check out www.theflip.com.

Can be contacted @ rwsawchuk2002@yahoo.com
