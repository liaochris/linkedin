Gavin Ger’s Summary

Gavin has over 15 years experience in professional services, consulting, business advisory and recruiting, as well as significant experience developing new business ventures in the private equity sector.

During 11 years with Accenture where he worked as a Senior Manager, Gavin managed dozens of mission critical engagements for over 30 major clients in Australia and the UK - covering all 4 key consulting disciplines - strategy, people, process and technology.

In 2005, Gavin took on a line management position with a new payments business, securing its key alliance partnerships and helping establish the business model.

Since 2006, Gavin has focused on building specialist businesses in professional services and recruitment.

Gavin Ger’s Specialties:

As a professional: Developing business opportunities; Building lasting business relationships; Developing and leading people; Shaping the strategic agenda; Shaping go-to-market strategies; and Crafting innovative solutions to difficult problems.

Technically: Consultancy; Financial Services; Utilities; Change Management; OD&D; Program Management; Strategic Alliances; Business Development; Business Strategy; Leadership Development.
