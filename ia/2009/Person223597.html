Aimee Stern’s Summary

I transform health, science and technical subjects into concise messages and outreach campaigns for the general public. I am at the point in my career where I can successfully do communications strategy, media, products - basically an entire campaign from start to finish.

In a current project with the American Association for the Advancement of Science we are researching and writing a book on the science of alcohol for kids and their parents, developing curriculum, and conducting a broad-based consumer media campaign to share what we learn. Also includes event and exhibit development and partnership development.

I have worked for federal agencies, Fortune 500 corporations, trade groups and a variety of non-profits.A former journalist, my work has been published in more than 100 magazines and newspapers including The New York Times, Business Week, Fortune's Small Business, Money, and U.S. News & World Report. Check out the Stern Communications network at www.sterncommdc.com.
