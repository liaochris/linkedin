Henry Tan’s Summary

I always want to take the road less traveled. My personal mission statement is not the impactful kind where people talk about changing the world. My mission is simply to "Live a life without regrets".

I set a goal to become a millionaire by age 30. One year into my job as a management trainee, I quit my job to pursue internet marketing which i believe will lead me to my goals. I also took up real estate to learn how to sell, which i believe is integral to any business.

I am also a serial entrepreneur and have started SgWeddingFavors.com to provide people with alternatives to the wedding favors provided by hotels. I want to eventually be a Personal Development Trainer and a Sports Coach.

My desired lifestyle would be that of a vagabond lifestyle, where i travel around the world with my wife every few months and then come back recharge for my business endeavors.
