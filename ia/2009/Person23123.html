Ayesha Ahmad’s Summary

As an independent research analyst, I provide my clients with market analysis and industry insights that help them make key strategic decisions.

I work with senior business development and marketing executives in companies of all sizes across a range of sectors and provide them with comprehensive analysis of and insight into the markets and sectors in which they are interested.

Before becoming an independent research analyst, I worked at Merrill Lynch in Hong Kong and Indonesia, Allegheny Energy in New York, Current Analysis in Washington DC and The Economist Intelligence Unit in Boston and Singapore.

I’m based in New York City and have lived, worked and traveled extensively across the Middle East, South Asia and South-East Asia.

I received my MBA from NYU Stern as a Director’s Fellow and graduated magna cum laude from Mount Holyoke as a Sarah Williston scholar.

If you would like to discuss your project, please email me at ayesha(at)ayeshaahmad(dot)com.

Ayesha Ahmad’s Specialties:

market and industry research, analysis and insights; market trend analysis; operating environment assessment; competitive analysis; in-country primary research; financial modeling and analysis; project management; emerging markets including Asia, Middle East (Gulf countries, North Africa, Turkey), CIS markets
