Becky Gould’s Summary

Becky Gould has over 9 years experience in collegiate and professional sports. Her passions are fan development, retention, marketing and special programming.

Becky has worked for Fresno State Athletic Marketing (NCAA Division I), Golden Eagle Athletic Marketing at Oral Roberts University (NCAA Division I), the Memphis Grizzlies and Washington Wizards (NBA), and the Washington Redskins (NFL). Her professional roles have included sales, fan retention, sponsorship sales, marketing, promotions, operations, and customer service.

Becky enjoys meeting colleagues in the sports industry. Becky passionately enjoys helping others achieve their goals, and is always open to building valuable business networks.

Strengths:

Management - Solid background in planning, executing retention, sales and marketing plans.

Marketing - Proven ability to recognize and capitalize on market trends responsible for strategic planning and market research.

Team Building - Decisive team leader and player with experience in developing talent and creating effective training programs.

Communication - Persuasive communicator with well developed presentations and negotiation skills. Able to develop productive relationships with clients, colleagues, and management.
