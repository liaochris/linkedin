Adam Tiberi’s Summary

Based in Washington D.C., Adam is the Senior Vice President of Global Product Management at Travelex Global Business Payments (TGBP).

Adam works with Travelex executives and sales managers, existing customers and prospects to define Travelex's global product strategy and go-to-market initiatives to drive revenue growth. Adam is regularly involved in M&A and strategic partnership discussions.

Adam is also responsible for the Global IT development team comprising Project Managers, Business Analysts, Technical Writers, Configuration Management specialists, Architects, Developers, Quality Assurance and Database Administrators. Adam's team is responsible for delivering all technology enhancements needed to support Travelex's business imperatives across all lines of business including product, sales, compliance, operations, treasury, trading and finance.

---

Adam was responsible for the design, build and launch of Payee Manager. Launched in January 2009, Payee Manager combines a global payment platform with a full vendor management system. Unique in the industry, Payee Manager enables corporates big and small, to automate the process of on-boarding new vendors, and collecting the details needed to pay them electronically. It provides vendors with full visibility of incoming receivables and the ability to keep their banking and master data up to date online in a secure self-service portal.

In April of 2008 Adam was responsible for launching Travelex's flagship global payments platform, Travelex GlobalPay. GlobalPay was the combined product offering for corporate customers post the acquisition of Ruesch International. Adam has successfully built a global product and presales team to support GlobalPay and is responsible for developing sales content and marketing collateral. Since its launch there has been a number of major enhancements made to GlobalPay with many more currently in development that are planned for release throughout 2009.
