Peter Bromberg’s Summary

I am the Assistant Director of non-profit library consortia in South Jersey. I have worked in libraries since 1992, including work as a reference librarian, a young adult librarian, and a special librarian the EPA. Prior to working as a librarian I worked for Nordstrom and got all customer servicey.

My areas of experience and interest are:

- Customer Service
- Training (including train-the-trainer)
- Change Management
- Communicating for results
- Speaking
- Coaching
- Virtual Reference
- Practical application of Web 2.0/Social Software
- Improv

I am available to speak or present on these topics.

Peter Bromberg’s Specialties:

    Head of Reference, Periodicals and Interlibrary Loan services. Supervised a staff of fifteen professionals and eleven support staff, ensuring quality reference service to all patrons. Developed a strong, customer service oriented reference team, bringing in technology to improve skills and streamline workflow, delivering service on two busy public service desks. Participated on Strategic Planning Team that used PLA’s “Planning for Results” model. As Grant Coordinator, oversaw the provision of twenty Internet classes per year to colleagues throughout South Jersey. Oversaw customer education training program that provides Internet classes to over one thousand patrons per year. Developed and taught various information literacy Internet classes. Oversaw all content and design of library website (http://www.camden.lib.nj.us). Designed and implemented a web-based periodicals database and a web-based version of popular print and electronic, “Guide to Elected Officials”.
