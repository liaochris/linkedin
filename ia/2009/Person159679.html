Melyssa St. Michael’s Summary

Snapshot:
I am a multiple skill-set, technically-integrated health communications specialist with a proven track record of results. You'll find that I am a strong project manager that delights in delivering a high level of pertinent health communication strategy paired with meaningful program design to achieve optimum project outcomes for my clients.

My Background:
I set out to “raise the bar” in the fitness industry, when I was just 18. I founded my first personal training company at age 20. Named one of the top 40 entrepreneurs under age 40 by Baltimore Magazine, I have lent my experience-based insights to well-recognized brands such as Gold’s Gym, Shape Magazine, Muscle & Fitness, Muscle & Fitness Hers Magazine, Looking Good Now, American Media, ELLE, Wal*Mart and Sam’s Club, contributing to national product roll-outs including proprietary health/wellness/fitness creation, production and branding.

I am an author and a spokesperson, having appeared as a fitness expert on national news channels CNN, CBS, NBC, and ABC. I have also been interviewed by such publications as Prevention Magazine, the Los Angeles Times, the Wall Street Journal, U.S. News & World Report, and the Sunday Times (London).

My Core Competencies:
Able to efficiently identify and integrate best-practice solutions in accordance with applicable HIPPA, OSHA, and AMA standards •• Passionate about understanding my client’s vision at the 10,000-ft level, define and develop the framework, infrastructure, and all supporting componentry to successfully achieve program objectives •• Engaged and empowered mentorship of diverse team members •• Excellence in charting new program attributes, defining project benchmarks, and setting performance expectations through client-defined metrics •• Keen awareness of end-user perception and need drives an evergreen commitment to provide on-going program sustainability through project refinement specific to core demographics and performance benchmarks

Melyssa St. Michael’s Specialties:
