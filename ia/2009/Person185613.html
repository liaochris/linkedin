Peter Ginn’s Summary

Have worked with both high wealth individuals as well as public sector in their real estate investments. Peter Ginn’s Experience

  • Senior Development Manager

    Hendricks Developent Group

    (Real Estate industry)

    March 2009 — Present (11 months)

  • Regional Director

    First Industrial Realty Trust, Inc.

    (Public Company; FR; Real Estate industry)

    July 2003 — December 2008 (5 years 6 months)

    Responsible for the maintenance and growth of the SE Wisconsin region industrial portfolio for a NYSE traded Real Estate Investment Trust, growing it from 400K SF to its present 2.8 million square feet. During my 5+ year tenure with First Industrial I was able to grow the portfolio through the acquisition of value add properties equaling 13 transactions comprising 2.8 million SF and an aggregate value of $94.8 million. Additionally, I completed 26 lease transactions totaling 1.4 million SF with a total transaction value of $40.2 million. I developed 3 separate build to suit transactions with Rustoleum 600KSF, QuadGraphics 389K SF & Affiliated Foods Midwest 721K SF while also developing and leasing two speculative buildings in Oak Creek, Wisconsin comprising 184K SF. Consistently during my time with First Industrial the portfolio enjoyed mid 90% occupancy levels along with strong rent growth and high customer service marks from both the tenant and well as the brokerage communities.

  • Vice President

    Inland Companies, Inc.

    (Partnership; 51-200 employees; Real Estate industry)

    January 2001 — July 2003 (2 years 7 months)

    Responsible for the day to day marketing of over 1.5 million square feet of investment grade property, brokering over 45 transactions totaling 618,000 square feet with a total transaction value over $11.8 million for both private as well as public investors. In 2001 helped secure the 375,000 SF 5 building management and leasing portfolio that was purchased by GE Asset Management from Opus North.

  • Vice President - Industrial

    Trammell Crow Company

    (Public Company; TCC; Real Estate industry)

    October 1991 — December 2000 (9 years 3 months)

    Used my knowledge and experience to represent a variety of institutional investors, CenterPoint Properties, First Industrial Realty Trust, The Estate of James Campbell, AIP REIT and CalEast Industrial Investors on the acquisition and leasing of their portfolios. During my tenure I was instrumental in achieving high occupancy rates and accretive yields for each clients portfolio. Additionally represented a number of clients on a tenant representation basis, most notably the 450,000 SF BTS DC for Anixter Inc. in Alsip, IL - the first LEED certified warehouse property in Chicago.
