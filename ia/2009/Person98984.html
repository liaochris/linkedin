Mike Belleau’s Summary

Mike B is the president of INSIGHTS Management. He has more than 19 years of experience in the entertainment business, with more than 15 in radio as Program Director (Major Market 2 million plus), Marketing Director and Director of Production within the prestigious radio station CKOI-FM (www.ckoi.com) in Montreal, owned by Corus Entertainment.

He manages the careers of the comedy group “Masked Avengers” (www.justiciers.tv) for radio, television and other various media. The Masked Avengers are well known for their radio pranks with Sarah Palin, Paul McCartney, Mick Jagger, Britney Spears and many others.

He also manages the radio and TV careers of:

- Claudine Prévost
- Luc Cauchon
- Julie Boulanger

In 2008, he signed John Nathaniel (www.insightsmanagement.com/clients/johnnathaniel/) to launch his career as a singer/song writer in North America. He also signed Julie Lefebvre and the concept group "The Dizzy Racers".

Mike B was the president of MAF Productions for 5 years. He managed talents, produced albums and managed the publishing side of the company. During those years, he managed his first talent, a young rock artist from Quebec City, Andrée Watters (www.andreewatters.com). He signed a license with SONY BMG.

Andrée’s first album, Mike B’s first production, went gold within a year and won the Rock Album of the year in 2004 for “AW” and rock album of the year in 2006 for the second album “A Travers” which was produced with Vito Luprano (Céline Dion's A&R).

In 2006 he founded and became President of INSIGHTS Management Quebec in Canada and INSIGHTS Management LLC and exported his knowledge and his business in United States.

In 2007, Mike B in a joint venture with Big Boom Entertainment Quebec Inc., developed strategic associations with major broadcasters in Canada and produced compilations and DVD's for various clients:

- MusiMax (CD)
- Musique Plus (CD)
- Radio Energie (CD)
- Radio Rock Detente (CD)
- Dre Nadia (4 DVD's)
