Joanna Lohman’s Summary

I just became the Vice President for Tenant Consulting, LLC, a commercial real estate company that started in 2002. Tenant Consulting, as it name suggest, represents exclusively tenants in office space transactions. We complete extremely creative and complicated deals in the Washington, D.C. area. We have completed unprecedented deals of size and stature including the longest negotiated lease in DC and we created the first ever carbon neutral calculator for office space that we implemented in The Wilderness Society Lease. TWS was then completely carbon neutral and helping the environment by planting trees.

With this deal, I was able to meet Al Gore and see him speak. It was inspirational and life changing. We have clients in all facets, law firms, non-profits, corporations...

We can find you space...the best space....in the most amazing and fun ways! Love it.

    THE GREENLACES MOVEMENT
    GreenLaces (at greenlaces.org) supports environmental causes and provides a solution for a better planet through a simple promise and GreenLaces. Beyond the promise and the laces, it is GreenLaces' goal to leverage the iconic presence of athletes to inspire the world to be more eco-friendly. We have had a thousand individuals, 50 high profile athletes, and 30 Olympic athletes join the GreenLaces movement and the best part is, everyone is an athlete.

    In their support of the GreenLaces organization, each GreenLaces owner has committed to the two simple steps it takes to become part of the cause. Each will wear their recyclable green shoelaces to show the world that they are a fan of the planet and each will make the GreenLaces Promise to the Planet to reduce their environmental impact, which they remember and keep with each tie.
