Clement Yeung’s Summary

How marketing savvy are you?

I think you will agree that having more paying customers means more turnover, which means more profit, which means more success.

Unfortunately, more often than not business owners concentrate on old and outdated marketing tactics such as cold-calling and leaflet drops.

Do these efforts reap benefits? Perhaps... but not likely.

I actually used to work for someone that made the mistake of spending $20,000.00 on a radio advertisement slot over the Christmas period and not measuring its success rate!

And yet, it’s not uncommon to see this happening with smaller businesses. It’s these uneducated decisions that lead many of us to part with our well-earned cash, and ultimately, that cause the majority of business start-ups to close down within the first five years of trade.

In today’s world, even last year is a far cry to how we’re operating today, and next year will see even more change as the speed at which our tactics adapt grows faster and faster with the emergence of new technologies and markets.

So what should we do? Well, we have to be aware of how the game is being played today, and also keep an eye on how it will be played tomorrow.

At the end of the day, no one wants to be left behind – and not staying in touch with developments could cost you your dreams.
