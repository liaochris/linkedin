Jo Dyson’s Summary

I specialise in GIS and archaeological surveying. My GIS experience is wide ranging, from producing 3D models in 3D Studio Max and adding them into an elevation model in ArcScene to collecting raw data using Differential GPS to create an elevation model. I have utilised a vast array of data sources such as both historic and modern Ordnance Survey mapping, geophysical data and aerial imagery
I would like to do a Doctorate degree within the next five years. I am a fast learner, extremely motivated, hardworking, and able to work well in a team or on my own. I put 100% into everything I do.

Outside of work, I live with my boyfriend Stuart. I enjoy cooking and I am foodie blogger. I love watching motor racing and enjoy being busy.
