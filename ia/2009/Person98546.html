Dan Finney’s Summary

Dan Finney Design, Inc. develops stunning graphic design solutions, corporate presence, and brand identity for web and print applications. Dan has over thirteen years of experience working in ad agencies and marketing service firms designing web sites, catalogs, logos, and printed marketing materials, as well as developing search marketing campaigns.

Dan Finney’s clients include global volunteer organizations, ad agencies, manufacturing companies, real estate developers, technical consulting firms, healthcare providers, warehousing companies, television stations, IT companies, and other businesses across the U.S.

Dan Finney’s Specialties:

Design, xhtml/css, web design, catalog design, print design, product photography, ecommerce websites, content management, xhtml/css front-end for web applications
