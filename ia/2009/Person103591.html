Jan Dolecek’s Summary

My professinal vision is to help organizations to grow, perform and be a great place to work at the same time. My passion is to show that HR is able to bring value to the business.

I joined CET 21 recently. This amazing company is the major TV & entertainment operator in the Czech Republic (TV NOVA, NOVA Cinema, NOVA Sport and MTV, tn.cz, nova.cz). I am leading the HR team and my biggest challange is to convert traditional transactional HR into fully customer oriented function.

I have been working for Vodafone for almost 4 years. I lead the HR business partnering team and one of my biggest excitements was to deliver true value to the business and help the top team to create environment in which people feel great while providing excellent performance.

One of the biggest challenges I faced was design of new HR delivery model and its implementation. This exercise resulted in decrease of headcount to approx 50% and significant increase of satisfaction with HR services. Together with the new delivery model I also delivered new Training and Development model and established personnel cost management. I managed outsourcing of high-volume recruitment that created savings of approx 1 M CZK.

In general I have 11 years experience within the HR area. I finalized my study at VŠE (University of Economics) in 1997 with excellent results (business economy) and joined T-Mobile (former RadioMobil) where I participated on the development of this start-up (in 1997). I had several managerial roles within the HR team and led projects like SAP HR implementation, design and implementation of job grading, personnel cost management or performance management.

After 6 years in T-Mobile I joined one of the worlds biggest pharma company Aventis. I stayed there just one year due to a merger. Despite the short period I managed to implement an HR information system, launch new recruitment assessment centres for sales force or introduce new commission system.
