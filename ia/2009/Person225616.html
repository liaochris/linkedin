Stuart Schaefer’s Summary

Stuart is a visionary leader who can see opportunities to launch, develop and grow businesses, and has the discipline, skills and track record to execute with excellence. He has a unique ability to blend profit and social responsibility, while improving both.

Stuart is a true connector who believes that connecting diverse people, technologies and consumers is essential for solving the today's and tomorrow's business and environmental challenges.

Stuart is an expert in all phases of the product life-cycle from concept to launch to rapid growth to maturity and to exit. Stuart has developed young businesses in the millions of dollars, and led global businesses processes for portfolios in the billions of dollars. Stuart has years of experience working with young start-up teams, and years working directly with the top management of a Fortune 25 company.

Stuart has deep experience working with outside partners and complex systems and brings the focus and motivation for the teams and systems to function as designed. Stuart has a broad functional background and he understands and places a premium on communication and empowering the best work from partner companies and in marketing, sales, R&D purchasing, manufacturing, engineering, and legal.

What can Stuart do for you? Bring to your business new sustainable products, technologies and business methods that did not exist just a few years ago. You too may delight your consumers, improve your profits and advance your environmental sustainability; all at the same time.
