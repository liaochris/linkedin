Mark Mulholland’s Summary

mark.mulholland@raymondjames.ca

Mark has over twenty-two years of experience in the financial services industry. His unique skill-set enables him to deliver a comprehensive, value-added, multi-disciplinary approach, providing clients with well-crafted and appropriate solutions.

Mark advises his clients on financial matters and offers a full range of services including financial planning, retirement planning, and tax-advantaged portfolio planning, with full access to professional asset management. In conjuction with his estate planning team, he also provides clients with estate planning services.

Mark's clients include investors with substantial means, business owners and people enjoying or planning for retirement. He works closely with his clients in designing and implementing investment portfolios, and retirement financial strategies including cash flow management. He also assists business owners with the many financial needs specific to them.


Mr. Mulholland’s clients appreciate his level of integrity, sincere approach, and hard work that he brings to the management of their financial needs. His greatest satisfaction comes from the long-term relationships that he develops with his clients and their financial success.
