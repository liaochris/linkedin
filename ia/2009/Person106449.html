Jose Baerga’s Summary

I have over 10 years of experience in the marketing and design industry and currently hold the position of Internet Marketing Creative Director at Westgate Resorts, which generates annual revenue exceeding $1 billion. My experience ranges from Architectural / Engineering, Print & Web design, complete corporate re-branding, and implementation of printed and internet marketing strategies with a keen eye for web usability.

Having been successful in conceptualizing, developing and executing effective creative marketing and advertising campaigns (such as magazine articles, e-newsletters / email campaigns, press releases, extranet, intranet, internet, mobile websites, company trade shows and presentations) I feel I can meet a variety of challenges in today's market.

I am a dynamic, goal oriented, design professional, experienced in all aspects of Visual Design, User Experience and Internet Marketing.

I am also self directed and self motivated. As a strong leader, I have the ability to guide and direct associates to achieve their maximum potential or be part of a productive design team. I enjoy the opportunities to significantly contribute to a company’s efficiency, organization, growth and profitability

Some of my specialty ranges from:

• Creative direction & development through all phases including conception, design and production.

• Development and management of corporate identity from identity packages, collateral, internet presence/ marketing and all facets of digital communication.
