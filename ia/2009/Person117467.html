John Leaver’s Summary

I try never to forget that recruitment is a distress purchase: if clients and candidates didn’t have to use agencies they wouldn’t. Before I got into recruitment I spent many years selling, and running teams that sold, ad space and airtime. I learned then that listening properly is half the battle. As a media recruiter that’s what I try to do, to understand exactly what sort of people my clients are looking for, and to appreciate my candidates’ aspirations.

My main clients now are commercial conference companies but I also, having sold airtime for a long time, have good commercial radio contacts. Additionally, I’ve placed many people in digital media roles. I've found positions for candidates in London, Dubai and Johannesburg. Colleagues at Reilly People fill jobs in media sales, media planning/buying and research..
