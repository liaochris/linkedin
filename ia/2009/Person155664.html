Matthew Batten’s Summary

I started my career in the video game industry at Sony Online Entertainment (Bellevue, WA) for the not yet released MMO "The Agency". Initially my responsibilities were to weight clothing models and ensure that they were optimized for efficient performance. I then progressed to take on many other responsibilities, mostly in the tools programming side of the art pipeline.

I have extensive experience with Maya MEL scripting and GUI design. I am also well versed in scripting in Autodesk/Softimage XSI and have a firm grasp on many other languages:

- MEL, Maya API
- C# (Console apps, server/client apps, services, etc)
- JavaScript
- PHP
- Actionscript
- BASH
- Unrealscript
- TorqueScript
- XHTML, CSS, XML
- MySQL
- C++

I have experience in bridging the features of Maya and XSI to harness the powers of both applications for the sake of the artists. In short, I make things that are hard to do in Maya or XSI easy.

One of my keystone projects at SOE was the development of the queuing application that is used by the Character Team for it's texture baking process. This tool alone has saved the team countless hours.

I seek to further my career as a Technical Artist or 3D Modeler and continue advancing my skills in all areas of video game content production.

Matthew Batten’s Specialties:

Scripting, modeling and animating in Maya 8. Scripting in XSI 6/7. Level design in Unreal 2 & 3. Programming-- MEL, Javascript, C#, PHP, Actionscript, Unrealscript, BASH, TorqueScript, XHTML, CSS, XML, MySQL, and C++.

Software: Maya 8, Photoshop, XSI 6 and 7, headus UVLayout, Unreal 2 & 3, Torque 3D, MS Office, MS Visual Studio, Flash, Dreamweaver, Perforce, Tortoise SVN.
