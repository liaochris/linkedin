David Weston’s Summary

Strategic Intelligence Analyst, Business Advisor and Consultant: working at executive management and governance levels.

Provides, ongoing independent assessment and advice on strategic risks and opportunities in the external business environment and in relation to business change; especially change resulting from, or incorporating, new technology or business critical technology.

Has the ability to undertake complex analysis to identify critical risks and opportunities and identify practical responses, based on a broad awareness of political, geographical, economic, environmental, military and technology issues.

Experience gained in over thirty years in working for, or with, organisations that are reliant on the interpretation of external information, or the creative use of Information and Communications Technology for their bottom line.

"Extrinsic provides discrete and bespoke business services that require the application of intelligence analysis, knowledge, experience, and business skills to specific topics of interest to you, and in ways that are relevant to your business needs. We do not offer a 'one-size-fits-all' service".

Goals are: 1) to provide a range of services that help people and businesses succeed; 2) to do so in non-adversarial ways that protect and enhance reputations, and 3) to leave this world having made a small, but positive, contribution to its betterment.

David Weston’s Specialties:

Strategic Intelligence Analysis, Risk Assessment, Opportunity Identification, Business Change Assessment, Project and Programme Reviews.
