Dion Magee’s Summary

Dion Magee, hailed as an artistic visionary whose combined skill, creative talent and outstanding ability have made him a pioneer in the social events & corporate events arena. Mr. Magee is the country’s event and wedding
lifestyle expert.

At Dion Magee Events & Weddings, Magee manages full scale weddings and social events, consolidated meetings and events program, serves as corporate event/travel director, manages strategic projects, providing hotel contract/guest room management, pharmaceutical programs, and maintaining quality industry event & hospitality standards for all programs.

As a sought speaker, Dion loves to plan events and weddings. With over 16 years of experience and 1,000+ weddings and events, Magee is truly a pioneer in his field.

Magee is Executive President and founder of “Black Bride and Groom”, trendy and chic magazine dedicated to elevating and showcasing African American wedding experience. Black Bride and Groom is the #1 resource for the African American bride and groom (with over 9,000 subscribers).

Recently named Young and Inspiring Event Professional in Industry, by Special Events Magazine, Dion planned,
executed and managed strategic meetings, large scale galas, international conferences, national events, trade shows
conferences, meetings, and incentive programs.

Dion was voted Corporate Planner of the Year by Event Solutions Magazine and Wedding Designer of the Year by Wedding Guild. He’s a contributing writer for Events Solutions Magazine, which reaches a readership of 27,600, and teaches at event industry conventions and to students at Temple University. His work and expertise is featured in
magazines and media books such as: In tents Magazine, The Knot, Wedding Channel, Examiner.com, Weddingwire, NJ Premiere Brides, Special Events, Atlanta Tribune, BizBash NY, Real Weddings and Brides Inc. He currently holds certifications as: Meeting Management and Certified Wedding Consultant.
