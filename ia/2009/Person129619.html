Kelsey Proud’s Summary

I am an up and coming multimedia journalist attending the School of Journalism at the University of Missouri. After completing an internship with them in London during the summer of 2008, MSN UK News afforded me the opportunity to produce a US elections blog, "We, the people". I have also been a Senior Staff Writer for The Maneater, the official and independent newspaper of the University of Missouri.

Next semester I will be doing stints of work at KOMU (NBC affiliate), KBIA (NPR affiliate), and The Columbia Missourian, a Columbia city newspaper run by university students and staff, all as a part of my convergence reporting class here at MU. I'm very excited to get started!

I call St. Charles, Illinois, a suburb of Chicago, home, and am actively looking for an internship for this summer, so if you or someone you know is interested in hiring someone who will work their socks off, let me know!
