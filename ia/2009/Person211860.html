Scott Parker’s Summary

Scott Parker is the Brand & Wholesale Director of Made by Niki.

Scott was the founder of Amp Associates, the London design studio with clients including Jasper Conran, Full Circle and Island Records.

During his 17 year career he worked at Intro, Ministry of Sound and Orange Mobile. His team designed product and advertising for the UK's biggest selling dance compilations of the 90's and a diverse range of magazines, events, stereo equipment and leisure venues.

He has commissioned and directed photoshoots and artwork with Kylie Minogue, Sir Peter Blake and the late John Peel, and was the brainchild and designer of Vortex and Born-To-Kick-Ass T-shirts, worn by EMF and The Wonder Stuff in the early 90's, selling nearly a million units all over the world in outlets such as Topshop.
