Rick Ladd’s Summary

Though I have been described as such, I have never thought of myself as a "Futurist"; merely as one who seeks to solve problems with all the tools at our disposal. I am, however, a relentless advocate for technologies that provide for greater communication, collaboration, and findability, as well as innovative possibilities far beyond what most businesses and organizations are aware of.

Of particular interest to me are what Professor Andrew McAfee refers to as Emergent Social Software Platforms (ESSP), which I believe will play a crucial role in defining the conduct of organizations in improving their innovative efforts, both in terms of products and services, and with respect to the processes they engage in to achieve their strategic objectives.

I approach these tools and services not as "silver bullets", but as an integral part of the "toolset" we need to succesfully navigate the continuing evolution of capabilities available to those of us who wish to make use of them. As a philosophical Materialist I believe how we perform our jobs has tremendous impact on the nature of our culture and, therefore, the greater our use of social computing, the closer we get to a learning and sharing culture.

I have consistently been in the forefront - in the last twenty and more years - of the Aerospace industry's recognition of Knowledge Management as a discipline worthy of further development and application. As such, I have both championed and helped to lead Rocketdyne's innovative efforts in this field.

I am currently providing my expertise in Web/Enterprise 2.0 design concepts, practices, and principles to the development of new capabilities at PWR, and am the team Lead for our company-wide integration efforts we call PoWeR 2.0.

Rick Ladd’s Specialties:

Knowledge Management as a system, synergized with Program Management, IT, HR, Training, and the enterprise in general. Systems Thinking for improved teaming, collaboration, and innovation. Web/Enterprise 2.0 design concepts, principles, and practices.
