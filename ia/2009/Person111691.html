Jessica Manna’s Summary

As a seasoned marketing professional with a deep knowledge of financial services, Jessica Manna has expertise leveraging data and analytics to drive high-response marketing strategies within the mortgage banking sector. Prior to joining RFC, she served as the VP of Business Development for marketing agency, Intellidyn, where she was the Senior Marketing Consultant to Ditech, Countrywide Home Loans, Aegis Lending, GMAC Mortgage, and Indymac Bank. In February 2008, she and her team were honored as to receive the 2008 "Gold Consumer" Award Recipient by MarketingSherpa for a customized e-mail marketing solution developed for GMAC Mortgage. In 2008, Manna was awarded DM New's "Top 30 Under 30 Award" honoring the nation's top marketers under 30 years old. Manna’s trainings and certifications include: MarketingSherpa Email Marketing Certified Professional; Mortgage Bankers Association FHA Product Training; ProActive Training & Consulting Selling as a Consultant; Dale Vermillion's Mortgage Champions Mortgage Sales & Leadership Training; and National Center for Database Marketing Database Marketing Certificate. She is currently taking the Six Sigma Blackbelt Certification at Ohio State University with expected completion in April 2009. She is a member of the American Marketing Association; Public Relations Society of America; Mortgage Bankers Association; International Woman’s Writing Guild; and serves on the Financial Services Council of the Direct Marketing Association. She attended Florida Atlantic University for Marketing and Management and is actively involved with several charitable organizations, including Big Brothers Big Sisters.
