Scott Hawley’s Summary

I have worked in marketing and advertising for 18 years, and am open to new opportunities. My strength is in concepting and executing photoshoot/commercial projects (advertising, web, in-store and collateral), and have managed design, copy and (shoot) production teams. I am always intrigued and inspired by the process of defining and growing a brand, both in pictures and in words. If you can get from point A to point Z with a sense of humor, all the better.

I have also written creative advertising and branding copy for different kinds of companies and projects.

Scott Hawley’s Specialties:

Brand Imaging/Development, Photoshoot Concepting and Execution, Commercial TV writing and execution, Copywriting, Team management.
