Chris Winchester’s Summary

Twitter: @chriswinchester

I'm seeking work as a generalist web manager/developer/creative (like an old-fashioned in-house webmaster or a user-experience-focused project manager/front-end developer at an agency) in New Zealand. I have now been granted NZ residency, and expect to be available to work in Wellington from the start of July 2009.

A creative and technical professional, I have been active in many aspects of website content development, design, production, hosting and management. The bulk of my recent experience? Expanding and enhancing the web presence of a UK-based development and relief agency over 7 years. With a background straddling technical and creative areas I am looking for a job where I can do some of both and bounce new ideas off like-minded people.  

I am equally at home editing copy or code, devising a new site’s information architecture or tweaking old pictures in Photoshop. I am also an experienced project manager, trainer, mentor and negotiator, and I enjoy good jokes and bad puns.  

My primary role producing the sites listed under "Websites" below was as an in-house web projects manager leading small outsourced creative and development teams.

And I'm developing a sideline as a self-publicist and on-the-fly marketing guy... (You can read about my recent marketing adventure in NZ in an interview, I did if you like: http://tinyurl.com/chriswinchester-on-sitepoint )

When not at work I enjoy spending time with my friends and family, playing in various bands, dabbling in writing (and occasionally performing) comedy and satire for the stage, for radio and online. My ambition is to take over the world with a crack team of exploding superhero honeybees.


If you'd like to know more (even if it's just about the bees!), or you think you have a job for me, you are very welcome to email me: chris[AT]chriswinchester.com
