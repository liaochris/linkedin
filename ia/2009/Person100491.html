Ivar Hagerud’s Summary

I passed Ericsson Core Leadership Program in 1999. As Project Manager in Service Delivery Platform project to Saudi Telecom for two years I have gained valuable experience in the delivery phase of System Integration business and how sales can be improved to mitigate risks and increase profitability. During the years I have had the privilege to work with different categories of people as well as cultures. Daily customer contacts during most of my working life. My plan is to be PMP certified, Project Management Professional, this year. I am a generalist and have been able to adapt in different businesses rapidly. I am interested in details but can easily lift myself to get an “aerial view” of a situation.

My next goal is to take on an executive role in Sales, Delivery or General Management.
