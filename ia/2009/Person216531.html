Sherry Lawler’s Summary

With my Linguistics background, I have cultured an amazing capacity for teaching many English grammar. In fact, I must admit, my love for grammar could coin me a grammarian. Geeky, but true.

I have been freelance editing for almost 10 years now, falling into it out of necessity for friends. I am able to mold to almost anyone's writing style, while ensuring their grammar is as perfect as their style allows. With several years in the business arena, I have also been able to hone business writing and editing skills, ensuring the client receives clean, concise copy the first time - allowing clients to get back to business, faster.
