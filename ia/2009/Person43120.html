Chris D'Amico’s Summary

As a senior marketing executive for over 10 years, I have extensive experience in almost every aspect of marketing. Combined with my advertising agency experience as a senior vice president and my experience at the Sun-Sentinel newspaper, I also have over 10 years of management experience and nearly 20 years of business development/sales experience. My career path has enabled me to master a diverse realm of sales and marketing mediums across different industries. I am also adept at managing and motivating my staff so that projects are successful, as well as completed on time and within budget.

Although my most recent marketing position was in residential real estate for a nationally ranked homebuilder, my previous agency background enabled me to identify and respond to a broad spectrum of marketing challenges for industries outside of real estate. This myriad of experience has allowed me to become skilled in all areas of print, Internet, direct mail, television and radio marketing, including detailed research, reporting and analysis of each. I also have vast experience in billboards, signage, public relations, corporate/charity events, displays, presentations, collateral materials, sales aids, videos and focus groups.

With such diverse exposure to so many marketing arenas, I am proficient in orchestrating people at all levels from executive to administrative and from clients to vendors. I have excellent people skills and pride myself on being an impressive team builder, as well as team member. As a senior executive, I have been responsible for marketing in all its phases—identifying challenges, researching possibilities, proposing plans, creating budgets, assembling the players, carrying out solutions and tracking the processes before, during and after implementation. I am a forward thinker who specializes in unique and innovative strategies.
