Craig Bloomfield’s Summary

I have 20+ years of experience in business communications, including journalism, public relations, and integrated marketing. My primary area of focus is commercial and corporate real estate, with an emphasis on energy and environmental strategies to enhance the sustainability of owners and occupants. I can also address interior workplace strategy and how leading-edge office layouts are increasing worker productivity, enhancing the attractiveness of companies to younger workers, and saving on operational costs. I am recognized as a talented business writer and an accomplished public speaker.

I work for a best-in-class company in my field with strong opportunity for internal career mobility. I have no career goals beyond what can be accomplished within the company.
