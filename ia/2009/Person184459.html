★ Michael Peachey ★’s Summary

Michael was founder and CEO of General Interface (GI), a privately held company launched with a mission to develop new technologies and techniques for Rich Internet Application UI frameworks - the ability to deploy software functionality into a plain web browser. GI was acquired by TIBCO Software in 2004 and remains the RIA platform for both large-scale commercial enterprise deployments, as well as with individual developers worldwide. Michael currently runs GI engineering efforts at TIBCO, as well as User Experience design and development across a variety of RIA products for TIBCO and TIBCO clients. Michael is a frequent speaker on topics from RIA engineering, to cross-functional team building and user experience development.
