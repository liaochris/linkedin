Josh Levine’s Summary

Josh is a seasoned business development and marketing professional with 15 years experience in technology, entertainment, and manufacturing. He has demonstrated success in building new businesses, creating and launching new products, driving customer acquisition, and managing teams for both large and small organizations.

Prior to joining Firescope, Josh was Vice President of Marketing for IceBreaker, Inc. The company helps people connect through mobile services that are engaging and easy to use. Josh helped create and launch Crush or Flush in January 2007 - a mobile social networking service that helps people meet, connect with friendships, and express themselves through their mobile phone or PC. Within its first 18 months, Crush or Flush generated over 1.5 million unique and validated mobile registrations, 100 million monthly page views in the U.S.

Before leaving for IceBreaker, Josh spent 5.5 years at Microsoft. He worked in business development for Microsoft.com, ran the Windows Vista web team for the Windows Marketing Group, and built marketing training programs for marketers at Microsoft in conjunction with the Kellogg Graduate School of Management.

Prior to joining Microsoft, Josh worked in business planning and development for Disney and spent several years launching new products for small and mid-size companies in the technology, entertainment, and industrial manufacturing industries.

Josh Levine’s Specialties:

Domestic and international experience in strategic partnerships, contract negotiations, OEM and alliance deals, mobile marketing, social networking, mobile customer acquisition, online customer acquisition, Internet marketing, guerrilla and grass roots marketing, branding, advertising, corporate communications, and promotions.
