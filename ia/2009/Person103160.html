James Zhuo’s Summary

I am trained as an engineer and I started out in the software industry not long after graduating. In my earlier consulting years, I’ve worked mainly with .Net, Microsoft SQL Server and Oracle DB. The range of work I’ve undertaken is challenging and diverse, from low-level hardware integration to high-level web applications.

In addition to my great analytical skill, I am flexible and creative in solution design. Writing software is not about coding at speed for me, but rather finding the right solution to the problem at hand and appropriately negotiating requirements.

In addition to my solid .net consulting experience, my recent entrepreneurial venture at hirem.com has enabled me to step outside my comfort zone and experiment with a diverse range of new and upcoming technologies, including Ruby on Rails, Amazon Web Services, Python, Django, Google’s App Engine platform and a myriad of other service and platform integrations.

I am looking for consulting roles, .net development roles, .net team lead roles or project management roles, preferably in the government sector, large well-known companies or the banking and financial sector.
