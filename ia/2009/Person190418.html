Hal Macomber’s Summary

Goals:
> Continue to work with ambitious people and firms on challenging projects.
> Advance the state of safety in the construction industry.
> Collaborate on research and writing projects on lean construction and lean project delivery.
> My current project is a book on the language action perspective as the basis for delivering projects.

Professional Experience
I've been fortunate to work in many industries involved in fast-paced and changing business circumstances. I've lived and worked in Europe, travelled to Japan to learn lean approaches first-hand. And I studied with and worked for Fernando Flores.
I've been a Chief Operating Officer of a design-build firm in CO. I've worked in power generation, copper mining, software development, computer manufacturing, and construction.
People call on my skills as a collaborator, innovator, writer, and coach.

Hal Macomber’s Specialties:

Leadership coaching, project management, lean project delivery, project kaizen, general operations management, writing, design of coaching programs
