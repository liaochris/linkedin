Jorrian Gelink’s Summary

For those that wish to add me to their LinkedIn network feel free as I am an Open Networker: jorriangelink@gmail.com. Also follow me on Twitter: http://www.twitter.com/jorrian

Jorrian is a "Management Architect", one who studies business/corporate management and its effects on the economy, people and society. He maintains the management hub Jorrian.com, a resource dedicated in providing theories and practical advice on leadership and management practices. Jorrian also owns "Peter Drucker's Management Philosophy" blog at http://peterdrucker.wordpress.com, where he writes on "the father of management's" best practices in order to continue Drucker's legacy.

Outside of Jorrian.com, Jorrian Gelink currently manages an electronics retail store in Edmonton, Alberta where he works on building high performance sales teams and develop new leaders.

With the knowledge and practice of management, Jorrian wishes to share his knowledge and experiences with executives in order for corporations to become more effective. This year he plans on writing more articles and networking with those who can help him achieve his goal of spreading effective management across the world.
