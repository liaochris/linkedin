Jeannette Brooks’s Summary

Hello. My name is Jeannette and I’m happy to say that I am a proud mom of four great kids. I’ve wanted to be a mom my whole life but I knew early on that I did NOT want someone else raising my children while I spent 40+ hours a week away. I wanted to be the one instilling MY values, morals, and life lessons in my children. And, of course, I didn’t want to ever miss out on anything.



So I began a long, tiresome search for a way to be home and be available to my children. It’s a hard road to travel but I refused to give up. I fell victim to scam after scam and to disappointment after disappointment. I had to do the “full time job” thing for a short while and we were all miserable. They hated going to daycare every day and I hated having to leave them there.



So when I came across this company I was skeptical, like I’m sure you were and maybe still are. I spent a couple weeks researching the company and asking around if anyone knew anything about Ameriplan. I was extremely impressed by the information I found. I had always wanted to do something where I could help others and this was a fabulous opportunity. Now I’m able to help other moms and dads, just like me, find a way to stay home with their children AND I can help people save money on their healthcare costs. It sounds like a great plan to me!!



Please come and join me on my journey to happiness and financial success. You will be just as impressed as I am.
