Andrew Wood’s Summary

An experienced contingency recruiter / head-hunter focused on the technology industry with + 10 years experience.

Since 2001 my focus has had a strong bias to the Enterprise Content Management market helping clients recruit the best available technical & sales talent in the industry.

After selling my interest in my last company in early 2007, I set up Digerati to focus on the technology market. We specialise recruiting technical experts and experienced sales professional in the ECM, CMS, BPM space.
