Michael Zeuthen’s Summary

I have a passion for technology and am at the forefront of discovering and developing Web 3.0 into a successful network for businesses. At Business 3.0, my team and I follow new technology and figure out how to best help businesses interact with their consumers, suppliers and employees. With the multitudes of new media consumers are presented with today, its hard to know how to effectively drive sales, and this is where we come in. We have the vision and know-how to consult, create, and connect your business to hundreds of millions of social users globally. I hold a degree in Political Science from Portland State University.

Business 3.0 is a start-up company with the sole purpose of monetizing the social web by allowing the business world to manage a presence for marketing and sales within social networks (creating what we call a Relationship Economy). The business world now has the ability to manage their social business application (or profile) on the entire social web through one well-known business network. If you believe social networks are the wave of the future, like many do, than Business 3.0 has the competitive advantage over any other company today.
