Robert Cardarelli’s Summary

Currently working at HCD Research, an innovative company in providing insights, including positioning, price analysis, segmentation, social network diffusion, media allocation, econometric modeling, copy testing, and financial and brand portfolio management. Strong emphasis in pharmaceutical marketing research.

Prior to HCD, during his tenure as part of the Millward Brown Strategic Services and Marketing Sciences Group, Robert consulted with the company's client service groups on developing new strategies and methodological innovations around brand strategy, marketing and advertising effectiveness, segmentation, and media allocation recommendations.

Robert spent close to five years working at Dynamic Logic, a Millward Brown Company, as Director, Integrated Media Solutions. He primarily focused on the development and "hands on" implementation of cross media and online advertising effectiveness research. During his tenure at Dynamic Logic, he has identified numerous business partnerships and has sustained various client relationships (Fortune 500 companies, media agencies, and media content providers). He has presented at several industry conferences and authored papers for the ARF, ESOMAR, and Worldwide Readership Symposia.

From 1998 to 2003, Robert worked at the KMR Group, as Director of Operations for TGI Latin America. He was accountable for all technical aspects, ranging from sampling methods, survey design, data processing, analytics, and reliability and validity assessment of syndicated media and consumer studies conducted throughout nine countries. During his tenure at KMR, Robert worked closely with a team of media and brand experts to establish TGI as the local currency for media and market measurement. Other contributions to the KMR Group included data management of the MARS Pharmaceutical Readership Study, software development, and sales support.
