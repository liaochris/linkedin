Arun Asthana’s Summary

Call me a multimedia skilled Creative professional with an experience of around 19 years in India, UK and other countries. I am exposed to and expert at almost all the media verticals - print, Radio, TV and digital.

Content is my forte - from strategy and planning to ideation/ conceptualisation to production for any platform. Being a published novelist and short story writer, a international journalist, an avid traveller as well as having a good insight of media industry globally and of course a very practical approach towards content development, I know selling is as important as creating in the media industry. So blending Creative and business sense is my especiality. I create the content of high creative values and develop it as a hot property ready for market.

Recently I handled strategy, planning and production of content for the first IPTV network in India. I also consult new and existing media players and consultancy firms to set up and launch TV channels. I Produced entertainment programmes for Indian TV channels and associated with film production.

As Journalist I have been contributing occasionally to the BBC, Foxnews (US), France 24, Canada Broadcasting Corporation, CBS radio network (US) and many other networks.

These days I am launching a web portal and IPTV for a Tata group company.
