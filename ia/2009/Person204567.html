Roy Zimmerhansl’s Summary

Almost 30 years in the securities services industry with the past 16 years dedicated to the securities lending markets. Broad perspective in securities services gained over a career with investment banks, custodian banks, a central depository and currently an interdealer broker. Experienced in both leading an existing business as well as launching new business lines at banks.
