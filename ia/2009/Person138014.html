Lauren Elliott’s Summary

Part of the Founder's Institute, building a team for a company based around a consumer retail product.

Founder and CEO on PNN - Personal News Network. A web based publishing platform for woman.

20 years experience in forming popular products from new technologies, creative direction, management and development. Founder and Creative Director of software development/ Internet development / and book publishing companies.

Author of 20+ original computer software titles including the Where in the World is Carmen SanDeigo software series.

* Co-founded and created The Big Network an Internet entertainment portal. Created content channels including "The Neighborhood", and "The Daily Post". The Big Network had over 160,000 registered users when it was sold to eUniverse - which was a big deal back then.

* Founded SodaMail Press LLC, an e-mail publishing company with one-to-one advertising and publishing capabilities.

* Founded and managed Elliott Portwood Production Studios - a CD Rom multimedia development company. Authored titles for Maxis Software, Software Toolworks, Time Warner Interactive and Virgin Sound and Vision. Raised over 3M in development funding.

* Created the first educational software titles using the Japanese Mario character. The two titles 'Mario is Missing' and 'Mario's Time Machine' computer game programs sold over 300,000 units and generated over $13 million dollars in revenue.

* Created the first popular sound interactive children's program, which received press coverage and exposure to over 12,000,000 readers and television viewers worldwide.

* Founded and managed Scrapbook Press, a book publishing company. Designed its Small World Distribution Program for Internet marketing and sales.

* Founded and managed Third Rock Mining, an internet application company.
