Dr. Sandy Chong’s Summary

Dr. Sandy Chong's professional approach is solution-providing, creativity, and dedication. Her professional goal is to provide up-to-date, relevant and creative business strategies for individuals and organisations that want to innovate and grow.

As an expert in Communication and IT, Sandy advocates effective, non-technical method for organisations facing constant technological and dynamic changes in the business environment. Her softer skills have got her involved in online business development, business process improvement, corporate restructure, public legacy system replacement, IT procurement, strategic marketing management, branding & advertising, market research, and executive training projects. Sandy has vast experience working with organisations and individuals from different cultural backgrounds in various countries such as Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong, China, Japan, Australia, Germany, Austria, to US. The role she plays include: senior consultant, executive trainer, advisor, business developer, senior researcher, project leader, analyst, mentor, coach, and speaker.
