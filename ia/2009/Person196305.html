    Mindful Mastermind Group

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    August 2009 — Present (2 months)

    You know the expression “two heads are better than one”. That is a mastermind group in a nutshell. Sometimes called “think-tanks” or “brain-trusts”, mastermind groups are everywhere!

    The opportunity to collaborate, unite, brainstorm, create goals and accountability to achieve what you might have only thought of as a dream is possible!

    Mastermind groups can be formed for a variety of reasons whether on a personal or business level and that is what the Mindful Mastermind Group is about, bring mindful people together to unite for a common good.

    Mindful Mastermind is about giving space to those creative, out of the box thinkers who are looking for an opportunity to collaborate with other like minded individuals.

    Each Mindful Mastermind session is professionally facilitated by Renee Shupe, a personal strategies coach and professional mastermind facilitator. This lets you as a participant enjoy the experience for what it is and not worry about the details of the group!
