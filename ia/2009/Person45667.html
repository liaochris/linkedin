Chris Cooke’s Summary

I'm a journalist, editor, publisher and marketer which, as you can imagine, keeps me busy.

I founded and co-own UnLimited Media, a London-based media and marketing company which owns its own media - CMU, ThreeWeeks and CreativeStudent.net - as well as providing marketing, content, research and training services to other media and consumer brands. UnLimited is also a partner in careers website Unicorn Jobs, and stages a monthly music networking and showcase night, the CMU Social.

At the heart of all of UnLimited's media and projects is a desire to support and enable innovation in music, media, culture and communication, and to that end we also run and are involved in a number of innovative education projects.

As well as overseeing the operations, marketing and development of UnLimited Media, I also co-edit the CMU Daily, and as a result I'm a bit of an expert on the UK music business and music media, and often comment on those industries for other media, including Radio 4, Radio 5, BBC News Channel, Sky News and CNN. I have also co-edited ThreeWeeks every year since 1996 and as a result am quite an expert on all things Edinburgh Festival too.

In addition to my UnLimited projects, I also work as a freelance journalist (specialising in music, cultural and business journalism, as well as copywriting and business research) and as a freelance consultant (specialising in media, marketing, and music).

If you want to get in touch, want a comment on a media, marketing or music topic, or want to discuss a writing or consulting project, or a possible business venture, just get in touch - email chris@unlimitedmedia.co.uk.

You will find a full biog and can access my current CV on the 'about me' page on my blog - www.stuffbyme.co.uk
