Mark Slatin’s Summary

Mark works with firms in professional services and relationship-oriented sales to grow their business by building trusting relationships.

• 25 years in sales and sales management with a $9 billion and $1.2 billion dollar B2B corporation, respectively, leading a Mid-Atlantic sales organization from $28 million to over $50 million in less than four years.

• Expertise setting and executing strategy as leadership team member at Boise Cascade, consultant to C – level business clients, and serves as an advisory board director for multiple small to medium size businesses.

• Passionate about coaching, training, and developing business leaders on how to build trust at each stage of the client’s buying cycle to improve customer loyalty and profitability.

• Effective communicator, eloquent public speaker, and influential writer. Appeared on The Arnette Report (Comcast 28 TV Business Magazine), as an Expert Panelist for CEO Junction, and Featured Expert on The Action Plan Marketing Club’s national audio series. Contributing Editor to OnPoint Magazine, and published in Statement!, Leading Edge Alliance, and The Business Monthly.

• Designed and developed a nationally NASBA accredited sales training program, Trust Centered Selling©, now incorporated into the Business Learning Institute’s print and on-line course catalog that serves the professional services market.

• Deep expertise in best practices for of all aspects of sales management from recruiting systems, performance management and sales methodologies including SPIN selling, Solution Selling, Customer Centric Selling, Miller Heiman, Trust-based Selling, PSS, and CRM applications such as Salesforce.com, Landslide Technologies, ACT!, Business Contact Manager, and Goldmine.

Mark Slatin’s Specialties:

Mark offers one-on-one and team coaching. He is a public speaker and offers on-site training of Trust Centered Selling, a program he designed and developed now accredited by the NASBA.
