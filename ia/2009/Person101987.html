Jake Yarbrough’s Summary

Accomplished executive marketing manager with more than 15 years of experience creating and managing strategic branding and interactive communications plans for agency clients. A passionate proponent of providing insightful advice based on brand strategy objectives, research and business goals. From creating one of the travel industry’s earliest Web sites, to conceiving, programming and launching a custom-created CRM system, a true veteran of integrating interactive solutions into more traditional tactical programs by leading cross-functional teams to success. Understands how to initiate conversations between brands and buyers through the power of meaningful, authentic relationships, as evidenced by a track record of demonstrated return on investment across industry categories, including: animal health, apparel, furniture, hospitality/tourism, manufacturing, real estate, retail and technology. Excels at connecting with and presenting to audiences from one to one thousand.
