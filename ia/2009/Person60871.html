Deepak Gulati’s Summary

Currently working as Project Manager to handle Americas projects. These projects includes deployment & integration of Roamware state-of-art GSM products at various mobile operators. These products include Welcome SMS, Missed Call Alerts, Virtual Home Environment, Manage roaming, Roaming Replicator(Broker), Local Roaming Number, Optimal Call routing of VoiceMail, CLI Delivery platform etc.

Profession career started as Software developer from Wipro customer site DSE. Have worked as Module leader with Satyam's Telecom Vertical.

Joined Roamware as Senior Software Engg. & later promoted to Technical Lead while handling implementations.

Have worked with world wide GSM operators including Swisscom, Saudi Telecom, Vodafone, Proximus, Belgacom, MTC-Vodafone, BSNL, Reliance, Idea, MTNL, Aircel, Mobinil Egypt, SmarTone HK, Taiwan Mobile, SingTel, ATT, TMobile.

Have onsite experience of almost each geographical region.
