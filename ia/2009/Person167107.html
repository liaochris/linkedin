Moaffak Ahmed’s Summary

I have 20 years of marketing and business development experience in Europe, North America and Asia. I have created - and successfully executed - business and marketing strategies for rapidly growing high tech firms.

After a successful exit in 2004 from Trema, a company I co-founded in 1992, I have been dividing my time between Europe and Asia. I am a board member in a number of technology companies and mentor senior management in several others. As a serial entrepreneur I understand the challenges of rapid change and growth in an international context. I have co-founded two software firms, established two consulting practices, and co-founded one trading house.

As the co-founder of Trema I was responsible for creating the brand and taking it global. Trema is the market leader in treasury, cash and asset management software, with clients such as Citibank, DaimlerChrysler, the European Central Bank, GE Capital, and Unilever. For instance, about 80% of Euroland central banks’ foreign reserves flow through Trema’s system. At the time of my exit in 2004, Trema had USD 50 million in revenues and over 300 employees in nine offices globally. Trema was merged with Wall Street Systems in 2006 [www.wallstreetsystems.com].

I have extensive experience from the most demanding target segments:
• Fortune 500 corporates
• Large financial and government institutions.
• High end of the high value (EUR +1 million) markets

In addition to marketing and business development assignments, I have assisted companies in:
• Establishing presence in new geographical markets
• Building partnerships
• Recruiting
• 10+ funding rounds
• IPO preparations
• Mergers & acquisitions
• Due diligence processes.

Interpersonal & communication skills:
• 15 years of daily working experience with 50+ different nationalities and cultures.
• So far, I have lived in: Helsinki, Finland; Cote d'Azur, France; Boston, USA; Stockholm, Sweden; London, UK; Geneva, Switzerland; Bangkok, Thailand.
