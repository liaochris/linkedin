Sree Balaji’s Summary

Sree has overall responsibility for the strategy, vision and performance of iLink Systems. His specialties include business ventures, business transformation, and best practices in the Healthcare and eCommerce verticals. A seasoned veteran in the Healthcare industry, he leads the company’s healthcare framework and product development efforts. Prior to starting iLink Systems, Sree played a lead role in the technical team at Lumedx in aiding their product development efforts. Prior to that, he worked as business intelligence (BI) expert at drugstore.com. Sree has graduate degrees in both Pharmaceutical Sciences and Computer Science from Auburn University, and a bachelor’s degree from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, India.
