Angie Maizlish, Executive Resume Writer/Interview Coach’s Summary

With over 15 years of experience in the development of unique resumes, I help you not just get that interview, but achieve your goals and aspirations. Located in Park City Utah, I help clients around the world convey their unique brand and core differentiators.

From a Senior Executive looking to make a career change, a client looking to increase his/her income or a Corporation in need of affordable Outplacement Services, First Impressions offers a vast array of programs that will help put your plans and ideas into fruition. Our services include targeted programs that are focused on resume development, job search strategies, networking, interviewing, salary negotiation skills and leadership coaching geared to our customer’s specific and/or desired position. We offer personalized and branded resumes, as well as a host of other specialized programs.
