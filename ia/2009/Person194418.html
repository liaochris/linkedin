Ray Berardinelli’s Summary

Co-founder and principal of Kalpa Consulting, which provides turnkey services across a range of sustainable projects from assessment and planning to program management and marketing of results.

Graduate of Bainbridge Graduate Institute with certificate in sustainable business management.

Providing leadership to Clean Technology Alliance of Software Association of Oregon as member of CTA Steering Committee.

Helped shape and drive outreach efforts of Oregon Environmental Council as OEC's director of marketing.

Ray Berardinelli’s Specialties:

Sustainable business practices, marketing communications and public relations, business development and program management.
