Toni Birdsong’s Summary

Toni Page Birdsong is an award-winning writer with 20 years of experience. She launched her career as a reporter covering courts, business, politics, the arts and entertainment. In that time, she's penned more than 2,000 newspaper, magazine and Internet articles. Toni has spent 10 years writing for The Walt Disney Company (7 with Imagineering) profiling the amazing people behind "the magic." As a Senior Communications Specialist at Disney, she helped roll out targeted communications — both printed and online — to 120,000 employees worldwide. She is currently co-owner of Birdsong Creative, a leading visual design and communications company located in Historic Downtown Franklin, Tennessee, a burgeoning entertainment and publishing economy outside of Nashville. Birdsong Creative clients include: New York Times bestselling author Andy Andrews, The Walt Disney Company, Big Idea, Propeller Consulting, Thomas Nelson Publishers, Indelible Creative Group, Gaiam, Vaco, InternetSafety.com, Genius Products, Word Entertainment, and a number of fantastic clients located between Los Angeles, Atlanta and New York.

Toni Birdsong’s Specialties:

Branding, Communications, Logos, Ad Campaigns, Visual Graphics, Web, Naming, Environmental Graphics, Copywriting, Fiction Writing.
