Magnus Penker’s Summary

Magnus Penker has 20 years experience as CEO, Management Consultant, Project Leader as well as Chairman and Board Member in the TMT sector (Technology, Media, and Telecom). He is also the author of several best selling Business- and IT-books (Wiley&Sons, USA), as well as frequently invited keynote speaker and moderator.

Magnus Penker has well-documented results running turnaround and breakthrough projects building scalable structures and internationalization, especially in the service and knowledge industry.
