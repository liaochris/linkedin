Computer Helpers Without Borders Campaign

Women Helping Women Computer Support Services

My Drive is Green Campaign

Going Online 15 December 2008. Maybe you have questions?

A. Job retraining for home based businesses. We will provide online training to any woman that is supporting her family. Computer experience is minimal, but a helping attitude will be essential. We are currently seeking relationships with women's empowerment and training centers.

B. Energy efficiency: When a PC is tuned up on a regular basis, much like the vehicle you may be driving, the efficiencies realized are cumulative, meaning, that with millions of PC's running more efficiently. Our goal is to save 1000 MW a day, which means that around 14 million PC's will need to be TuneUp on a quarterly basis.

C. Each of our paid sessions(at 1.00/minute) will provide 28% of that service calls' revenue to a local charity, good cause, or community group, as designated by the End User whose PC is TunedUP4Good. That contribution, though NOT tax deductible, will be matched with an 'In Kind' allocation of time, such that after only THREE(3) sessions, the good cause will be entitled to a complementary TuneUP4Good, to support the continued efforts of that group.

If Your PC is running slow, then you may wish to consider working with one of our campaign teams, to support the growth of specialized Computer Helper Teams with a TuneUp4Good.

We look forward to your response, and participation.
