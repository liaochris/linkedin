Leo Cunha’s Summary

Over 20 years experience designing and developing mission critical software systems. Most recently, involved in the integration of Learning Management Systems (LMS) to Student Information Systems (SIS), and development of CRM applications for higher education institutions.

Consulted for SuperOffice CRM, providing add-ons and plugins for ERP, Customer Service and Quote/Contract applications. Assisted in large scale CRM deployments.

Served as President and CTO and chief architect of the Bidchaser.com / QShopNow.com electronic commerce sites. Responsible for network, database and application design of this high-capacity Web property, and selected all architectural elements, hardware, software and the system's development environments.

Posses an extensive understanding of enterprise computing, systems programming, applications programming, network computing, and relational databases.

Worked previously as a consultant for organizations such as the Federal Aviation Administration's (F.A.A.) Financial Information System, the Nuclear Regulatory Commission, the IBM Corporation, Senior Campus Living, LLC, Sunterra Resorts and CORT Furniture Rental before starting with Bidchaser.

Attended the University of Maryland before serving in the U.S. Armed forces from 1982-1991 where I received my initial computer systems and programming training.

Fluent in English, Portuguese and Spanish.
