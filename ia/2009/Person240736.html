Johnny Uvestor’s Summary

Uvestor.com is a 'Real Estate Communication Platform' designed to improve the process of locating, marketing and closing real estate; with a strong emphasis on foreclosure, bank owned and distressed properties. Real estate transactions are a dynamic process that can become extremely complicated and tedious for the many parties involved. Foreclosures and bank owned properties can even further complicate the process for the buyers, sellers, title companies, lenders, agents and brokers in each deal. Uvestor.com’s “Productivity Suite” alleviates these information-related problems by providing productivity tools like the “Closing Manager” that dramatically increase the efficiency of each party through communication, collaboration and sharing documents, all in real time. Uvestor.com, is the only online platform where buyers, sellers and industry professionals can research, manage, and connect in one marketplace for FREE.
