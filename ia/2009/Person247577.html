Bill Standen’s Summary

Established Commercial Construction Superintendent. Seeking an opportunity to apply my skills to the benefit of both prospective employers and myself.

Willing and prepared to travel / relocate as necessary. Prefer East or Southeast US but will consider all locations.

My experience includes complete site management, substantial participation in sub-contract scope development, contract issuance, insurance maintenance, submittal process, RFI development, tracking and resolution. Capable of participating in the review subsequent change order scopes and contributions to the evaluation of sub-contractor payment requests. Well versed at developing, seeking input, adjusting and maintaining detailed "full-project" and "look ahead" schedules. Experienced with the management of weekly safety, sub-contractor and Owner - A/E meetings.

Successfully developed detailed sub-contractor pre-punch lists, implementing these, along with the Owners - A/E punch lists, seeing them through to project completion and end user satisfaction.

Bill Standen’s Specialties:

Comfortable with development of detailed project schedules using SureTrak. Proficient in the use of MS Office products. Experienced with the field applications of ProLog.

Fully capable of initiating, customizing and implementing site safety programs.

OSHA 30 Hour Certified.
MDEQ Certified Storm Water Operator.
First Aid, CPR and AED Trained.
