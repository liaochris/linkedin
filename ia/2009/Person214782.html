Brent Stafford’s Summary

Brent Stafford is a Sr. New Business Development consultant specializing in digital media start-ups, online advertising and online video monetization. Brent oversees product development, technical integration, marketing and business development for VideoClix, the premier provider of interactive video for tier-one companies on the web.

With over 20 people reporting directly, Stafford provides the steady leadership needed to navigate a critical emerging technology through the often tumultuous digital landscape. Directly responsible for building the business model & value propositions, he is solely responsible for pitching & closing MTV Networks & ESPN, establishing VideoClix as their official clickable video vendor.

Stafford also has extensive experience creating & executing innovative marketing campaigns & joint-promotions. He's worked with over 50 tier-one brands on strategies and tactical programs which leverage the power of entertainment properties & brand assets to build consumer engagement & drive sales.

Mr. Stafford created national & global joint-marketing campaigns & intellectual property agreements with companies such as Procter & Gamble, Coca-Cola, Wal*Mart, Electronic Arts, New Line Cinema, Disney, NASCAR, Super Bowl, Hasbro, GRAMMYS & Lucas Films to name a few.

Stafford negotiated & executed the largest brand partnership for Disney’s "The Incredibles" bringing the studio 11 brands from Procter & Gamble. He negotiated & executed a four country Pringles partnership and promotion with "The Lord of the Rings: Return of the King" & he delivered to P&G the Star Wars franchise for a 16 country theatrical deal in Europe.

Stafford is also a nationally recognized expert on video games, delivering a $2MM anchor brand partnership to Comcast for the launch of the G4Tech-TV cable channel.

Mr. Stafford holds a Masters Degree in Communications from Simon Fraser University and is a former CBC television news reporter/producer.
