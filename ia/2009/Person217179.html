Shumin Wu’s Summary

• Researched in creating intelligent tutoring systems for helping people rapidly acquire spoken communication skills. Worked with a team of 30 who built the large-scale, award-winning project Tactical Language & Culture Training System (TLCTS), which is one of the most widely used intelligent learning environments across the United States.
• Designed pedagogical agents using knowledge representation and statistical student modeling techniques, to trace training objectives, customize scaffolding levels and learning plans, as well as to evaluate learning performance and recommend activities that learners need to engage to improve their communication skills.
• Worked on system improvement and evaluations of learning efficiency and effectiveness. Analyzing large-sized structured and unstructured data collected from real training sessions to mine sequential behavior patterns and rules over user characteristics, behaviors and learning outcomes.
• Developed C++ and Python toolkits for data gathering, reporting, and student assessment.
