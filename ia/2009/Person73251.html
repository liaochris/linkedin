Jim Janco’s Summary

We appreciate people oriented business practices that still demand high performance, and elevated success standards.

With 30 years as a builder, developer, trainer, and business owner, we currently strategize and consult for small business owners, builders and developers in a number of states.

As "Agents of Epiphany, Preditors of Possibility, Perceivers of Probability", our workshops and strategies improve our clients market and internal performance, helping them realize greater profits, high quality projects, higher efficiency, and enhance quality employee retainage.

Among our add service proficiencies is working with out-of-town owners, buyers, and property developers, working closely with Realtors and Builders. We utilize a detailed method of progress reporting, pictures, and status observations. We can also operate as owner liaisons between owners/ buyers and their builder.
