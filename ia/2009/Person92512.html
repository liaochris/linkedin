Harold Good, CPPO’s Summary

Achievement oriented public procurement adminisitrator. Successful track record for bringing projects online on time and within budget. Portfolio includes federal, state and locally funded capital projects. Agencies served include, county, city, airport, convention center and major teaching hospital.
