Jason Wagner’s Summary

Currently acting as a project manager at Oculus Inc. where my focus is the renovation of existing financial facilities for a major national client. I also assist them with ADA upgrades of their existing facilities, and gather information across a large portfolio of facilities for Facilities Information tracking. We provide architecture and interior design, move management, and strategic planning. The majority of our work involves quick roll-out projects that require fast turnaround from schematic design through permit. We are a small company with big intentions. 90% of the projects in our office are produced in BIM (building information modelling). While other firms say they use BIM- we mean it. At Oculus my main interest is putting the "Information" into Building Information Modeling and providing the client with a more robust understanding of their building from design to construction and beyond. Basically I would love to create a Google for Building Information.

Secondary to my role at Oculus I have created ThreeFourteenDesign, which grew out of my love for residential architecture and real estate. Today, floor plans are for online listings what photos were two years ago. They help agents by allowing potential buyers to get a better understanding of a residence prior to a showing appointment. This pre-screens potential buyers so the agent spends less time showing them homes they may not be interested in. In addition to plans, we also assist the agents with design ideas to market the property, as well as stay on after the sale with the buyer if needed to help them with future remodels. My goal with LinkedIn would be to work on both my professional business relationships as well as my personal business relationships. I constantly have ideas for new projects and business ventures in media, architecture, and product design and would love to link up with like minded individuals in a design co-op.
