Erin Anderson’s Summary

Working at the American Cancer Society for nearly six years lead me to my passion in technology and especially social media. Through my changing roles I realized how much easier it was for volunteers to engage with the organization, connect with one another and control their own fund-raising. When I started designing and building RelayForLife.org I discovered the power of user generated content mixed with the burgeoning open source CMS, Drupal.

To me social media should be an extension of your every day life and not something you have to think about. Social networking is something we do every day and the online and mobile delivery should enhance, not hinder the user's experience.

Raised in a family of engineers has offered me the nerd-perspective, which I find to be a valuable skill I rely on daily. I thrive in an environment of creativity, varying perspectives and the combination of high-touch and technology.

Erin Anderson’s Specialties:

Social networking strategy and implementation, online community/forum building, blogging strategy, brand sentiment research, user experience, non-profit fund-raising, micro-philanthropy, web marketing, newsletter/ email campaigns, segmentation, web analytics, public speaking, open-source software evangelism, Drupal/php project management and implementation.
