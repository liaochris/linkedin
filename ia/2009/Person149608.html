Marc Villacorta’s Summary

I have an advanced knowledge of Linux systems and I am also familiar with UNIX standards. Most of my experience has been under Debian-like and RedHat-like distributions. I feel confident setting up a system and running daemons and applications on it and I am not afraid of more complex tasks such as kernel patching, software customizations from source code and system tuning/hardening. I have developed basic linux daemons in C, combining Sockets, MySQL, SSL, IPC, Semaphores and Threads. (Other keywords: IPSEC, VPN, IPTABLES, TCPDUMP, SSH, DHCP, SAMBA, APACHE, MYSQL, SNMP, DNS)

After many years of using Windows systems, I have become an advanced user with skills to successfully track down complex problems. I also have developed basic kernel drivers (ring 0) and services (ring 1) and therefore I have a basic idea of windows system internals. In my early years as a helpdesk analyst I acquired skills to deal with customer situations.

I am an intermediate but still learning programmer, most confident in C but also skilled in Bash and HTML. I also had some experience with JavaScript, PHP and Java. I have a base of knowledge about networking protocols such as Ethernet, TCP/IP and SIP/SDP. I have experience capturing network traffic and analyzing complex NAT and routing scenarios.
