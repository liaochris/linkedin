Jane Shears’s Summary

In my "other life", as I like to say, I was involved in the business world and working in law offices for a number of years. When my husband and I decided to start a family, we made the decision that I would stay home - which I have absolutely loved!! Now that they (we have two children) have somewhat grown up, I thought it was time to become involved in the business world again, but in a way that was flexible with my schedule so that I could still participate in my children's/family's activities, when needed. The extra money would be a bonus too!! I had never really entertained the idea of becoming involved with network marketing before, but I found something that I am passionate about, so why not give it a go!! And it easily fits in with my schedule!

Marketing is a new world for me and I am loving the learning curve that goes with it. As an added bonus, I am meeting a lot of great people along the way. Who could ask for more ... personal development and new friends. How many J.O.B.S. can offer that?!
