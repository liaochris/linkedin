Roland Legrand’s Summary

I am setting up and leading teams involved in the production of content for high quality electronic publications like classical wire services, websites and web 2.0 projects like blogs, online communities, asynchronous and synchronous interaction. I have experience in electronic and print publishing for the business community.

I organize social media workshops for journalists or people involved in communication.

Lately I became very much interested in the development of virtual 3D worlds, like Second Life. I run a personal blog about this on http://www.mixedrealities.com.

I also blog on MediaShift about how journalists can adopt a more conversational approach to news gathering and reporting: http://www.pbs.org/mediashift/roland-legrand/

Roland Legrand’s Specialties:

Financial news coverage, team management for mixed media outlets, implementing changes in editorial organizations, organizing social media workshops
