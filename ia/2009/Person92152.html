Hans Nieuwenburg’s Summary

My career began in 1987. In my career i've been involved in many different projects in various markets. My interest goes mainly to human computer interaction and User Experience. I think that not only the technical performance of the system is key for success but the performance of the man/woman who is using it.
Interaction, functional design, advisory functions and optimizing the development process are issues that engage me.
