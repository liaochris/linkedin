Gunes Yilmaztuerk’s Summary

I was born in Turkey and grew up in a Turkish/American household in USA. After college, I moved to Germany to experience European culture. Shortly after, I accepted a position with Johnson Controls where I coordinated and monitored the performance of a sales-team from eight countries. This further entailed assisting in strategic planning for our region and monitoring growth.

In April of 2005, I was hired by Levi Strauss as a Forecast Analyst. My responsibilities required working closely with the marketing team in planning marketing strategies, promotions and advertising across Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. I also developed a monthly demand forecast for the Levi’s brand to facilitate purchasing worth about $135m yearly.

In 2008, I started working for Syndicate Sales, the largest supplier of hard-goods to the floral industry. My role as Director of Product Management & Marketing included leading all marketing communication efforts. I revised marketing tactics to research consumer behavior and customer segmentation in order to customize communication and advertising. My responsibilities further involved partaking in planning of company goals & strategy, planning of ads and editorial content, planning promotions, and writing press releases.

In Fall of 2009, I founded Sollé Coaching; a company focusing on Marketing Strategy & Online Marketing Media needs of businesses. The rules of business are changing. Consumers are tuning out the costly and traditional marketing methods; instead, they want to communicate & be heard. No longer are brands managed merely through glossy ads, but through online interaction with a demanding community. We help business embrace these changes to empower their brand, their consumers, their employees and their markets. We give you the tools to help you plan & implement an online marketing strategy that is fully integrated with your off-line marketing strategy and goals. Welcome to Sollé Coaching. How may we help you?

Günes
