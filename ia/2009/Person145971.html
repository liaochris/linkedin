Lynton Pipkorn’s Summary

A conceptual thinker that is passionate and intuitive about consumers, brands and communications, I am a senior marketing and communications strategist with 10 years varied experience, agency and client side, in Australia and the UK.

With a mind centered on demand and value creation outcomes, I have a strong commercial acumen with key strengths in delivering analytics, insights and planning solutions. I have been responsible for developing a wide range of insight based strategies from brand positioning, communications (TV, online, DM, content, sponsorship, PR), channels marketing (including social networks) and segmentation.

I am used to working with C-level management and organisations with complex portfolios, business issues, structures and channels, driving effectiveness, return on objectives and ROI. In particular I love the problem solving part of strategy (analysis, insight generation, ideation = value creation), making the results simple and single-minded be it a business plan or creative brief.

I also enjoy fostering strong client relationships. I have a proven ability to effectively deliver integrated strategic planning solutions across all media and communications touch points to leverage maximum business impact.
