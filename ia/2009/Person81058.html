Frank Buonemani Cost Segregation Specialists’s Summary

ELB Consulting has helped our clients defer millions of tax dollars by using cost segregation to accelerate the depreciation of expense deductions.

We are the nationwide specialists, and have performed thousands of studies in all 50 states. Our highly experienced staff is drawn from the ranks of the Big-4 and Big-5 accounting firms of Price Waterhouse Coopers, Ernst and Young LLP, and Arthur Anderson. We have an in depth knowledge in a wide range of industries, including: automotive, health care, hospitality, manufacturing, restaurant, retail establishments, apartment complexes, and recreational facilities. ELB’s experience in these markets range from small office remodels to billion dollar high‐tech manufacturing plants.

ELB is dedicated solely to cost segregation, so it’s our only focus. Why? For audits, it’s critical that the cost segregation report be above question: our studies stand alone. We use an engineering methodology that uses stringent processes and comprehensive reports for our clients. ELB works twice as hard as our competitors, to ensure our clients receive the best service and tremendous tax benefits.

The advantages of cost segregation apply to diverse types of properties and property owners: New construction, purchased property, building renovations or expansion, and it even benefits owners of properties that were purchased in the past.

Cost segregation is often overlooked by property owners because they aren’t aware of the magnitude of potential benefits. ELB is accessible, responsive, and trained on current IRS regulations and laws. We will succinctly explain cost segregation, how your company can benefit from it, and why ELB is the only trusted source for a cost segregation engineering study.
