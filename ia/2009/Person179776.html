Jayant Tewari’s Summary

Practice Memorandum
--------------------------------
Oriented towards Small and Medium Technology Companies (Revenue range USD 0 –5 Million) providing a range of de-facto CFO Services on a retainer-ship as well as project assignment basis.

Engagement Model
--------------------------
The professional qualifications, experience, expertise and skill set of an experienced CFO are brought to bear through this Practice, with a committed hours-per-month being available to each client under a retainer and further time requirement being chargeable on a per hour basis. Provides a member of the clients’ Management Team, representing the clients’ as their CFO vis-à-vis the outside world.

Client Profile
==========
Given the sensitive nature of the engagement, current clients are individually identified only immediately prior to taking up an engagement to eliminate any possibility of a conflict of interest.
