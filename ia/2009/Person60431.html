Deborah Johnson’s Summary

Tynan provides strategic business consulting and specializes in solving business problems facing companies today. We also provide Transaction Advisory Services to help clients maximize value and minimize risk during all stages of the M&A process including, strategic introductions, negotiation assistance, deal-closing support, value proposition positioning, and assistance with management presentations.

We offer candid, realistic in-depth assessments, evaluate competitive positioning, uncover risks and opportunities, identify synergies within and outside organizations, evaluate build-buy-partner, organic growth strategies as well as new market penetration strategy. We help our clients to identify, understand, prioritize, and act on new Initiatives-we build just in time divisions and companies.

We specialize in developing and launching strategic initiatives by uncovering new business opportunities. We do gap analysis, market research, build business models and create new revenue streams. We talk to suppliers and analysts and utilize this information to determine future strategy and direction.
