Patrice Chollet’s Summary

Strategic thinker and hands-on portfolio/project manager in information technology and knowledge management with a proven track record of moving organisations towards better information integration and a more collaborative environment.

Specializing in people to people connections, organizational network analysis and learning loops, Patrice thrives in cross-cultural groups, fostering a positive and inclusive mindset that helps people draw on resources they did not know they had to deliver innovative solutions and achieve operational excellence.

Passionate about leveraging Enterprise 2.0 technology as a means to help entreprise knowledge and human assets create value in a massively collaborative environment.
