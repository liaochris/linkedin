Loni Lowe’s Summary

With a background in advertising and interior design, I provide a unique perspective on real estate that many cannot. I have helped clients sell and purchase high end homes or find "diamonds in the rough".

With my contacts in the trade I have been able to assist clients both selling and purchasing homes. Sometines work is needed to get a home ready to go on the market. I can help with that. Have you ever found a home that you absolutely love...except for one thing? I have helped buyers change "that one thing" so they can have the home of their dreams.

If you have ever looked for a new construction home on your own you know how overwhelming it can be. There are so many builders out there. Who should you pick? Did you know many of the lots in the developments are available on the builders lot exchange? You have choices. The agents in the development offices represent the builder and the builder's best interests. It is always best to have your own representation when you are embarking on new construction. If you are building I can assist you in the process. Please contact me prior to meeting with the builders. Many new developments are not in the RMLS, but I keep up with the ever changing construction market.

It has often been said that relocating to a new community is the number 3 stress producing event in your life. My expertise as an agent specializing in relocations means I can give you valuable information about the area, schools, taxes, local housing values and trends. I spend time doing a lifestyle and needs analysis for my relocation clients which provides information to help with the home search.

I will look only for homes that fit your needs and lifestyle. I set up your home tour trips to be a very productive time for you. And if you are ready to make an offer I have inspectors and lenders on call and waiting to help.

From initial consultation to the closing, I will make your next home sale or purchase as stress free as possible.
