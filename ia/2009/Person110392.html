Jeremy Johnson’s Summary

My never-ending curiosity and persistence combined with my ability to understand and solve complex problems motivated my academic career, culminating in the completion of a PhD in electrical engineering. I've increasingly focused my energy on creating solutions that directly impact human life, primarily in the medical device industry. The foundation of what innovation means to me was poured during the 10-months as a Biodesign Innovation Fellow: a systematic approach to needs finding and the invention and implementation of novel solutions. My own framework of innovation has matured during my on-the-job experience at Medtronic leading early-stage research projects, market opportunity assessments, and internal innovation campaigns.

Jeremy Johnson’s Specialties:

Innovation process and strategy, project management, social media and collaboration tools
