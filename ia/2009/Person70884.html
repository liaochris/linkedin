Eric Kant’s Summary

For the past five years Eric Kant has been Director of Field Operations and Product Manager for NC4/E Team, the world’s leading supplier of emergency and event management software and related products. His years of hands-on experience as a paramedic, firefighter, and emergency manager formed the foundation for his career focus on advanced technical operations and command and control systems architecture.
Mr Kant has been responsible for many operations, serving in a command capacity for five presidentially declared disasters including tornados, floods, and wildfires. He implemented the first completely Internet-based emergency management system in Florida, a sophisticated EOC that provided satellite-based weather monitoring, geographical information systems, and Internet-based workflow.
He has provided technical expertise in preparation for or response to most of the significant events and disasters of the past decade. He provided operational support during the World Trade Center Disaster in NYC, Columbia Shuttle Recovery, and many other large-scale operations. He has also provided his expertise to ensure secure events for the Olympic games in Athens, the G8 summit in Georgia, World Cup in Munich, and other national security events within the US.

Eric Kant’s Specialties:

Technology architect, experience in deploying fully-integrated disaster/event technologies and intelligence collection and analysis systems. Use of interoperability standards, including EDXML and NIEM. Utilization of structured data and data standards within social media for information collection, dissemination. The use of social networking technology as part of integrated decision support system and synthesizing complex intelligence pictures over multiple social networks for operations.
