Matthew Kaufmann’s Summary

Matthew Kaufmann: thinker.emoter.creator

I am currently entering my senior year at Michigan State University after spending the summer interning in New York City. In a business full of competitive prospects, I decided to immerse myself head first into the real world atmosphere that thrives in "The Big Apple."

I learned many valuable lessons and gained first hand experience in the fields of marketing, advertising, and social networking. Through my internship with The New York Musical Theatre Festival, a non-profit organization, I saw how to dig down to the heart and soul of a community and create amazing successes through passion and innovation. Through my second internship at LiveIntent Inc, I was able to expand my online awareness and functionality by strategically managing numerous Twitter accounts. This unique experience and business venture challenged my creativity and writing skills by demanding unique voices and tweets within the 140 character limit.

In addition to this amazing summer experience, I have also studied Advertising and Public Relations in Rome, Italy and attended the 2008 Cannes International Advertising Festival (more description attached to my education).

My dual majors in Creative Advertising and Acting have uniquely shaped my work in many ways. My concepts and copy breathe emotion, honesty, relation, and innovativeness. My passion in two different fields allows me to draw from numerous sources of inspiration while remaining focused and concentrated on a direct goal.

Post-graduation, I hope to find a job in the creative field of advertising; specifically as a copywriter where I can continue to create meaningful and thought provoking ideas.
