Marco Nicosia’s Summary

Hands-on management of geographically diverse teams of systems administrators. These teams have been comprised of high-level unix experts focused on providing software as a service in demanding production environments.

My emphasis on promoting sensible processes allows me to: 1) Manage a complex project backlog while enabling the team to fire on many cylinders. 2) Manage the product life-cycle: deployment planning, change control, post-mortem and future release planning. 3) Set appropriate expectations by creating simple to understand interfaces for the operations team.

My background in systems administration and homegrown, enterprise and open source software allows me to: 1) Propose and manage both outsourced and in-house projects. 2) Connect with engineers and project managers from all departments. 3) Suggest creative solutions that excite the teams as well as advance our technical agenda.
