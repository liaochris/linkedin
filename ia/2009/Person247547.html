William Moulton’s Summary

William has successfully pioneered a variety breakthrough software / hardware technologies for industrial and consumer markets. He specializes in total life cycle systems design, disruptive technologies, emerging markets, pattern recognition problems, and improving human-computer interaction. He has delivered solutions for mission critical floor trading of securities, management information, industrial process controls, adaptive life support control systems, internet search, 3D data visualization, trend forecasting, and multidisciplinary research.

William has delivered solutions for mission critical floor trading of securities (NYSE, ASE), management information, industrial process controls, production workflow systems, tools for professional arts, and consumer electronics.

His teams have engineered:

-- Multimedia conference boards for Video Network Communications (now TalkPoint Communications)

-- Speech recognition systems for Goldman Sachs and Company, Merrill Lynch, and RW Presspritch

-- Assembly robot failure recovery expert systems for General Motors

-- Industrial control knowledge engines for Alcoa, Reliance Electric, JPL, LANL, Army, TRW, NASA and IITRI

-- Production aquaculture ecosystem controls for Oceanic Institute

-- Defense satellite failure mode prediction for Aerojet Electrosystems

-- 3DTV displays and motion graphics software for Atari; game console and glove technology for Hasbro and Mattel

-- Consumer and professional music products for Amiga and Commodore

-- Radar assisted face and voice dubbing for Tricoast Films

-- Software agent based search engines for Navagent, Inc.

-- Collaboration platforms for EPRI and Energy Voyager

-- Social presence visualization for social network sites and software companies

-- Semantic data visual navigation browsers for government applications

-- Real-time inference engines and expert systems for various clients
