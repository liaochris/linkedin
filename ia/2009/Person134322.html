Kris Bjørn Jeppesen’s Summary

I am a very outgoing person who enjoys talking to different people. On the other hand I am very serious with what I engage in e.g. study, work and own businesses. I see myself as an entrepreneur and my big goal is to create an international company which contains dynamic and innovative aspects. Start-ups appeal to me because I think it is the perfect combination of communication between people and an economic aspect.

In my spare time I love to make business and relax with my friends and family. Also I train karate twice a week. In this way I meet a lot of new exciting people and I get some exercise!
Earlier I have played badminton, soccer and a lot of music with my former band.
