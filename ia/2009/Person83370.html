Gareth Harvey’s Summary

I've produced over 200 newsmagazine reports and documentaries for a wide variety of programs and networks both in Australia and internationally, including National Geographic Television & Film, Discovery Channel, HD Net, Channel Five (UK), Film Australia, Australian 60 Minutes, CBS 60 Minutes, ABC News 20/20 and SBS Network Australia – covering all aspects of television journalism & filmmaking in all regions of the world – as well as studio and “specials” productions. I'm also the author of two novels and a book of humour, currently writing a fictional trilogy set in medieval Europe.

Gareth Harvey’s Specialties:

Newsmagazine, breaking news, documentaries.
