Joshua Parker Toulson’s Summary

I have over ten years of experience in the creative field ranging from hand illustration to 3D animation and I am a
2006 graduate of the University of British Columbia Multimedia and New Media Studies. I have most recently
been employed as the Sr. Graphic Designer and Network Operations Manager for the Optometry News Network
for over a year. The Optometry News Network is a digital signage company based in Toronto that supplies News
and Advertising content to Optometry clinics all over Canada. As well as creating all creative content for ONN,
my duties included building strong relationships with clients and advertisers and gain valuable insight towards
technical and creative media solutions for the future. I worked closely with several major advertisers in ad devel-
opment, creation, and final output medium.
