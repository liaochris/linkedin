Arthur Alston MD MBA’s Summary

I am a South African doctor with an MBA who lives in Switzerland and works as a marketeer. I have lived and worked on three continents and plan to add another before I turn 40, preferably in a city by the sea.

I am an iconoclast, an aggregator, an early adopter, a strategic thinker and a pragmatist. I am curious and love to learn new things.

My interests range from digital media and marketing, the pharmaceutical industry to economics, politics, sport (in particular kitesurfing and poker), social media, peer-to-peer networking and genealogy.

I am actively involved with pharma social media like Twitter, and you can find references to me on the following blogs:

Health is Social: Remarkable Pharma People to Follow on Twitter
(http://philbaumann.com/2009/07/21/1001-remarkable-pharma-people-to-follow-on-twitter/)

Dose of Digital: Pharma and Healthcare Social Media Wiki
(http://www.doseofdigital.com/healthcare-pharma-social-media-wiki/)

Whydotpharma: Follow the engagement- visualizing #followpharma
(http://www.whydotpharma.com/2009/07/21/follow-the-engagement-visualizing-followpharma/)

eyeforpharma.com: Pharma is Twitter-pated! (http://social.eyeforpharma.com/story/pharma%E2%80%99s-twitter-pated)

Besides the aforementioned, my passion is personal productivity (based on the GTD methodology) and how to deal with information overload. I write about that at http://www.theproductivityparadox.com
