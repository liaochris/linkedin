Tom Eagar’s Summary

I have helped hundreds of clients in more than 30 countries how to better communicate with their clients and prospects, showcase their products and services, and grow their business with extraordinary results. For more than 8 years, I have created custom software and online programs and portals for small start-up businesses to multi-million dollar corporations. I help clients explore and expand upon their ideas and convert them into profitable, user-friendly solutions for their business. An example of the types of products I’ve helped to create are; engaging web seminars, educational tutorials, secure eBooks products containing printable forms, interactive calculators, streaming audio and video, dynamic eBooks linked to a user online control panel, maximum security systems for eBooks, software and recorded webinars, online security programs to protect product download links, and access to online membership portals. My services also include research, planning, code development, to full installation and support of a client’s intellectual property.
My goal is to serve 5,000 clients with our Live Conference PRO web meeting, recorded web seminar and video postcard service by the end of 2009.
