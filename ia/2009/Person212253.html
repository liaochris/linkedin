Steven Greenberg’s Summary

With two decades of experience in the international sales and marketing trenches, I have learned to rapidly define and internalize company messages - and communicate them at eye-level to the target audience. My writing reflects a deep understanding of business, communication, and technology - coupled with a hard-hitting and concise, yet elegant writing style.

My guiding principles for communicating company messages: subdued and to-the-point creativity, that still retains humor and pep; a flare for the dramatic – keeping in mind that not everyone responds to drama; a taste of the exotic, with the understanding that some prefer the mundane.

Steven Greenberg’s Specialties:

Copywriting and creation of hi-level English marketing collateral aimed at international audiences, including: company and product names, taglines, scripts for Flash and other video presentations, web site content, brochures, articles, white papers, press releases, and more.

My client list and a library of samples may be found on my web site - www.sdg.co.il.
