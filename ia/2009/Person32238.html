Brandon Toth’s Summary

Brandon Toth has worked for the better part of the past decade in business consulting and communications, having stared his career in technical communication and business development in 2003. He has been a consultant for publicly traded companies involved in the natural resource development sector in corporate development and strategic communications since January of 2006. Through this experience it has lead him into a series of roles including CEO, President, Consultant and Director of several corporations. Mr. Toth is also one of the editors of the investment newsletter "The Market Order" with a subscriber base on the thousands. Under these roles Mr. Toth has excelled in macro management of operations, structuring management teams and operatives, due diligence and execution of acquisitions, funding structuring/arrangements and has been mentored under various business consultants, investment bankers, petroleum geologists and members of SCORE (Security Corp Of Retired Executives).

Brandon Toth’s Specialties:

Investment / Trading, Business Consulting, Acquisitions, Capital Financing, Corporate Structuring, Investor and Public Relations, Negotiations, Macro and Micro Management.

  • CEO, President, Director

    Green Star Energies, Inc.

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    February 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    Mr. Toth came to Green Star Energies (fka Titan Oil and Gas) in February of 2008 where he stepped in to lead Green Star Energies as company President. Mr. Toth has been an integral part of the ongoing turnaround process and development of the business plan. Through his role with Green Star he has first been an investor providing the bulk of the seed capital himself that the Company needed to initiate the turnaround process that the Company has recently completed. In his short term thus far with the Company he has successfully completed a full scale proxy involving thousands of stockholders. Then he completed the due diligence and acquired Green Star’s first oil and gas project acquisition in years only to subsequently complete a re-sale of the project for $1.62M securing management income for Green Star over the following 3 years and booking an 800% gain on the transaction for Green Star.

    In early 2009, Mr. Toth was responsible for the formation of the Green Star Stockholder Committee which has provided expertise and counsel to Green Star including execution of operations and the required financing. Then he negotiated and executed the gas purchase contract for Green Star’s Texas operations with NYSE listed DCP Midstream as well as secured disputed easements paving the way to the eventual connection of the well. His recent compounded successes with Green Star have led to his recent appointment to CEO in addition to his role as President.
