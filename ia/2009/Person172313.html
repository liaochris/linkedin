Navyo Ericsen’s Summary

EXPERIENCE
A lifetime of experience in the arts. Piano from age 8. Classical music education in London. Owner and engineer of two project recording studios. Live sound reinforcement for a 3000 seat auditorium, India. Senior Audio Editor and Mastering Engineer in a London A/V facility for clients including BMG, Sony and CBS. Successful performing and recording musician with various artists in UK, US and India. Group facilitator for the Bay Area-based Breakthrough Performance Workshop. Founder and bandleader of SpiritMoves improv music ensemble. Film and video producer, director and editor. Graphic designer for web, motion and print. Web developer in Flash environment.

Currently building SacredWorld Productions, a transformational media production company.

30 years on the spiritual path has enabled me to bring a level of depth, wisdom and inspiration to my work that is a pioneering blend of consciousness and creativity.

GOALS
My goals are to
a) establish my production company
b) build a global team of conscious creatives
c) use our creativity for projects that are for the awakening of consciousness and the evolution of humanity
