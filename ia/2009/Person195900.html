Reinjan Mulder’s Summary

After 25 years of writing reviews and interviews, and editing NRC Handelsblad, I joined the new independent publishing house De Geus as a publisher of Dutch literature and non-fiction. In 2003 I helped restructuring the Amsterdam publisher Meulenhoff, and started as a independent writer, editor and consultant for up-market publishing. In november 2008 I started at www.reinjanmulder.nl the Independent Internet Weekly 'Das Zahngold'.

Reinjan Mulder’s Specialties:

organising and stimulating publications, branding and selling
