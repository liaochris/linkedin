Dan Moon’s Summary

I am an experienced professional, with a broad range of analysis, design, delivery and operational expertise.

An exceptional ability to grasp complex business issues and understand the related business / technology implications of these; I work well in either a strategic or tactical project environment and am capable of quickly building trust and respect within a project's business community by delivering results. My skills include: requirements gathering, business analysis, process design, operations and project management.

My experience includes establishing and managing business operations for Walt Disney International; providing project delivery for Vodafone UK; delivering Service Improvement for Virgin Mobile UK; as well as having provided various operational improvements and project deliveries to numerous start-up companies.

Strong interpersonal skills are utilised to engage with individuals and teams at all levels to make a tangible difference to any organisation.

My goals are to work with companies, cross-functionally, on scaling operations and supporting project delivery.
