Mitchel Byrne’s Summary

I have been involved in the Commercial HVAC/R trade for over 15 years.
I started out doing Boiler Demolition. I went to the Coyne Institute to further my education in the heating, air conditioning and refrigeration trade. I have worked at residential and commercial companies and am proficient in commercial refrigeration.

I am committed to helping customers reduce electrical consumption when possible, and I plan to institute "green" sustainable concepts whenever possible.
