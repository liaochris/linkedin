Oliver Bodnar - SEO, SEO Expert, Search Engine Optimization’s Summary

I did a soft launch of www.OneArmedSEO.com...by soft, I mean very soft! hahaha Taking on clients now, but only very few so that all will get the time they are paying for!

Originally started out as a web designer, I kind of fell into Search Engine Optimization (SEO) by osmosis...after building sites for clients they would often ask me: "Why doesn't my site come up in Google when I search?" I would scratch my head and I probably looked like a Deer in headlights...I had no idea; I then became consumed with the process (keyword), and began to read as much as I could about search engine optimization and studied what other sites that ranked well were doing...eventually it started to click...now about 10 years later...it's what I do and love. I've been Sr SEO Analyst at the last few companies I've worked for...and will be officially launching OneArmedSEO.com soon.
SEO is like one of those puzzles where you have to move the pieces around and try to get them in the right order to complete the picture...I've always liked those!
SEO, basically is acquired knowledge, like anything, the more you know, the better off you are. My definition of search engine optimization: Instinct fed by acquired knowledge.
I'm more of a hands on kind of person and won't ever shovel any bullsh*t your way...those who know me will concur...if they don't, they have a shovel in their hands and don't know me! I'm always straight forward!
Which would you rather have, a piece of candy totally covered in sugar that'll rot your teeth out, or one that is sugar free that is not only healthy for you, but will keep your teeth intact?
I've worked on a lot of sites in the past decade, implementing "whitehat" techniques every step of the way. It's been a learning process and will forever be that way. Your teeth are safe with me!
