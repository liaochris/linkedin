Andrew Gerrard’s Summary

I'm a skilled consultant, specialising in the development and integration of online digital and offline traditional marketing strategies for businesses that need to take advantage of their marketing and digital activities.

I use a managed process to work with clients, understanding their business requirements and helping them build their activity. I advise and formulate relevant and appropriate marketing strategies and tactics, including digital, and then execute an agreed set of activities to help achieve the required business objectives.

My experience spans the broad range of marketing mix activities including online & offline PR, digital (web, email, wireless, broadcast, viral) activity, direct marketing, events and others. Based in the UK, I have worked with both start-up and larger international organisations, and have operated at a senior marketing level in European markets for several clients. My regional network of contacts means clients are assured of finding the best marketing partners and service suppliers wherever they want to target.

I like to work with interesting, creative and innovative companies and people where my approach of hands-on management and "can-do" excellence means I can get results.
