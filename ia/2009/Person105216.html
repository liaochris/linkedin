Jason MacFadyen, M.M.’s Summary

I currently am the Director of Information Technology for Visiting Nurse and Community Health, Inc. (VNCH) in Arlington, MA. Originally hired by VNCH as MIS Manager in October of 2000 (from a consulting background). With interim positions such as IT/IS Manager, Billing and Information Technology Manager; I have served in my current position as one of three Directors for the organization for more than two years. In my current role I oversee the Medical Records Department, Information Technology Department, as well as many regulatory and operational aspects of the business. I serve as the primary resource to the Fiscal Department in matters requiring Medical Billing expertise (as I have years of experience in all aspects of Medical Billing and reimbursement). I also serve as the agency's Project Manager, HIPAA Officer and as a key member of the Strategic Planning Committee, guiding the way for the business's future. I have led the organization through the implementation and integration of state-of-the-art systems including automated payroll and the implementation of Enterprise-wide EMR (Electronic Medical Records) system including IVR/Telephony and point of care devices at bedside, as well as a proprietary Decision Information System that I designed.
