Ashwin Razdan’s Summary

Ashwin Razdan is a marketer and a New Media enthusiast. He holds a Degree in Computer Science Engineering, with specialization in Internet & Ecommerce, from AIUMT, Dubai. Ashwin has also recently authored a book of New Media, which has become a comprehensive guide for students and young marketing professionals.

He currently works for Cyber Media Services, where he implements online promotional campaigns to market the publishing services of the company worldwide. He is also a guest lecturer at Apeejay Institute of Mass Communication, Dwarka, New Delhi.

He has worked for other well known media houses like Jasubhai Digital Media (New-Delhi) and Sterling Media (Dubai, U.A.E). The clients that he has serviced in the past include The Dubai Government, Reebok India, Yahoo India, Pepsi India and SX4 (Maruti).

Ashwin Razdan’s Specialties:

Author, New Media – Techniques & Trends, 2008

About the book
The book describes 25 different forms of new-media initiatives with examples. The book intends to showcase the potential of New-Media and all it can do brands. New-Media can be beneficial and productive, not only companies with large advertising budgets, but also for those who have just started out and wish to promote their products with fairly low advertising budgets.
