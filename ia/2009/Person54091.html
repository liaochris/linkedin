Daniel Pinkney’s Experience

  • Founder and MD

    Boro Takeaways Online Ltd

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    2009 — Present (less than a year)

    Currently in development, and due for launch this coming October, borotakeaways.com is an online takeaway ordering service covering Teesside and North Yorkshire

    The website will enable customers to place an order with local registered takeaways for delivery or collection and also to pay by card through our secure checkout system (or cash on delivery if so desired). The site will also provide up to date news and features on our takeaway and restaurant partners and well as a whole host of additional features.

    Our package includes a whole host of advertising and marketing services for our registered takeaways from social media and online promotions through to PR and offline promotion (print/press) of the Boro Takeaway Service as a whole. So as well as taking our local takeaways and restaurants online we are also eliminating their costs associated with advertising, marketing and printing.

    As a local 'community' based website we are always looking to build key local relationships and sponsorship opportunities.

    To keep update with our progress you can follow us on Twitter, become a db fan on or facebook page or subscrive to our blow. Feedback and suggestions always welcome.

    If you own a takeaway or restaurant and are interested in becoming a takeaway partner please email us at sales@borotakeaways.com or call 0844 330 8967

  • Owner & MD

    Bytheway Stores Ltd

    (Design industry)

    May 2007 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    Purchased a freehold convenience store two years ago in North Yorkshire. In that time developed and incorporated various systems and procedures to enable the store to run smoothly and efficiently under independent management operation. Gone from having one part-time staff upon acquisition to seven at present.

    Boosted store turnover from £14k to £17k per week through incorporating additional services and a shop refit. En-route to becoming a Booker 'Premier' outlet and National Lottery service provider which should increase turnover dramatically.

  • Creative Director

    Ellahi & Pinkney Ltd

    (Design industry)

    2000 — 2006 (6 years)

    Founding partner of a creative design agency based on the outskirts of North Yorkshire. Managed and worked alongside the in-house design and production teams as well as installing and maintaining the IT network/backup systems. Also worked alongside MD Tanvir Ellahi in developing new business openings.

    The company saw turnover increase to £8.2million in 2004/5 thanks in most part to becoming the National design agency for NTL. Unfortunately E&P lost the account in 2006 due to the Virgin takeover.

    Established in 2000 E&P has produced work for many bluechip clients such as Tiscali UK, NTL, Electrolux, Husqvarna, McCulloch, Cleveland College of Art and Design, Business Link, Northumberland College, Odyssey Systems, Middlesbrough College, Ringtons, Flymo and Forest Holidays.

  • Creative Director

    Citrus Creative

    (Design industry)

    1998 — 2000 (2 years)

    Design agency established with a fellow graduate as a vehicle for freelance projects gained through University. Produced work for Muddyfox, Northern Arts, Cleveland Arts, Buzzwords and Tees Forest.
