Laurent Benameur Sauvaire, CISSP’s Summary

I'm a results oriented IT network and security expert, graduated in 1999 with a master degree in computer engineering from the ESIL school in the south of France. I gained the prestigious CISSP certification later at the beginning of the year 2008, I'm also holding the Cisco CCNA certification.

My 10 years progressive experience in networks and IT security solutions design and management was achieved using well-known vendors of the market, open source tools and compliance to standards, always with business and people in mind.
I gained highly technical skills in IT security audit and penetration testing while working for the Information Security Expert Laboratory (LEXSI) and strong team leadership and project management skills with the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Marseille-Provence.

My current role is a well-balanced mix of day-to-day administration of the company's whole network and security infrastructure on one hand and team-based project management on a broad range of IT projects reported to top-management on the other hand.

I also spend a lot of time representing the company expertise with professional association memberships and pursuing professional development opportunities including external trainings and sharing with the team.

I'm fluent in French and English.

I'm currently available for new great fresh challenges, now living in Dublin/Ireland, and opened to the World... with a preference for Europe.
