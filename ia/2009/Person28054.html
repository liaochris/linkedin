Bhuti Mbele’s Summary

I have spent 11 years doing web development using different languages from the old asp, coldFusion, asp.NET and PHP. Having spent so many years doing web development has made me understand the business and be able to offer businesses with business solution other than just a website.
I also spent most of those years doing web support and web mastering. Also did lots of web architect.

From 2004 I increased my time in application development using C#.Net and SQL Server 2005 and decreased my time for web development. I do lots of PHP development as a hobby. I love web development and would like to keep my knowledge in check so that I don't end up forgetting.

Since I have been lucky enough to work for such a wonderful company that I work for and I have been exposed to a great deal of experience, I want to plough back what I have to the community. I mentor young stars who are interested in IT and I have created the company Sompisi IT Systems so that I can get this young stars temp work while they are training. I have so far produced 2 wonderful individuals who had no experience and I understand how difficult it is in South Africa to find work if you don't have an experience. I basically expose the young stars that I mentor to companies and for them to work in IT and I feel how it is to work in IT.

I put that enthusiasm in them so that they start having a passion about what they do and they become proud about their job. I know there is nothing better for a company than to have an employee who is proud about his work. A person like that can be trusted to deliver.
