Dr. Aziz Faisal’s Summary

EIF focuses on management buyouts, add-on acquisitions for portfolio companies, corporate divestitures, family buyouts and recapitalizations. EIF also focuses on venture capital investments (start-up or early stage companies with no history of revenues or cash flow) or companies that require a significant turn around.
Generally, EIF's investments are common equity, although they can be structured as preferred stock and/or subordinated debt with equity features. EIF’s primary objective is to create long-term capital gains.

EIF offers equity stakes to the management teams of companies in which it invests and also to entrepreneurs and start-ups, thus aligning EIF's long-term capital appreciation objectives with entrepreneur's objectives.

EIF's targeted investments are businesses of all sizes and we invest up to £100 million.

Dr. Aziz Faisal’s Specialties:

EIF's strategy for enhancing the value of its investments includes supporting the entrepreneur through a prudent capital structure allowing for growth and profitability.

EIF's focus is on manufacturing, production, fabrication, distribution, and service companies with a preference for profitable niche businesses. We also invest in high-tech, financial, real estate, or retail operations.
