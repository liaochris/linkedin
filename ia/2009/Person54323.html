Daniel Shannon’s Summary

Professional Graphic Designer. Very heavy entertainment print background. Creation of DVD and CD packaging and all collateral elements for Television and Motion Picture titles. Design, layout and production of one-sheets, header cards, stickers, merchandisers and standee graphics. Scanning and retouching of project images for publication or product packaging. Design and production of motion picture screening invitations, display toppers, channel strips, in-store graphics, merchandisers, kiosks and POP displays. Creation of business cards and letterheads. Logo design and development. Creation of full-page magazine and newspaper ads promoting current films or TV shows. Ability to create or work within all types of branding guidelines. Preparation of art files for 4-color output, as well as 5th color or foil print. Creation of mock-ups of final packaging or product.

Daniel Shannon’s Specialties:

Fluent in all major design programs. InDesign, QuarkXpress, Photoshop CS3, Illustrator CS3, Acrobat Professional - Reader & Distiller, Bridge, Stuffit, ScanWizard Pro, BarCode Pro, Toast Titanium, Extensis Portfoilio and Entourage.
