Frank Short, M.A.’s Summary

If résumé writing were an Olympic sport, Frank Short would have achieved multiple gold medals for his creative work as a professional résumé and LinkedIn profile writer.

Frank Short has 25 years of experience in promotional writing in the greater Chicago area. For 15 of those years, he has written or edited résumés, cover letters, college application essays and other forms of career promotion, including professional bios and LinkedIn profiles. His clients range from entry-level college graduates to corporate CEOs, from lawyers to doctors. He has written résumés for people from coast to coast and around the world, with some living as far away as England, the Middle East and Hong Kong. No wonder Frank Short’s business card unabashedly states that he “writes résumés that work as hard as you do.”

Frank is frequently called upon to provide service as an expert panelist, as a provider of résumé critiques at career fairs and other venues, and as a broadcast-media guest on topics pertaining to résumés and career promotion.

Previously, Frank was a copywriter with leading advertising agencies, including Leo Burnett Company and DDB Needham. He worked on accounts such as Frigidaire, Kellogg’s and Nestle.

Perhaps that is the secret behind Frank's ability to put such a sharp creative and persuasive edge on the résumés he writes. In turn, that is why Frank Short is also able to help his clients get so many interviews, even in today’s recessed job market. But the real reason for his success is that he so obviously loves writing résumés. He says, “I get a deep sense of personal satisfaction by helping people get where they want to go in their careers, especially in these recessionary times.”

Frank Short holds a master’s degree in English from DePaul University and a bachelor’s degree in communication and English earned at Northwestern University. He also earned a 10-course certificate in writing from Northwestern, approximately equivalent to a master's degree.

Frank Short, M.A.’s Specialties:

• Write, edit and produce creative copy writing and promotional materials for corporate executives, managers, supervisors, physicians, accountants, attorneys, consultants, educators, office support staff, service industry and other professionals

• Create world-class résumés, biographies and profiles

• Teach résumé and profile writing seminars

• Make special appearances on broadcast shows, on industry panels and at various career fairs
