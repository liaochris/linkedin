    (Research industry)

    December 2007 — November 2008 (1 year)

    Duties: Primarily mathematical modeling for clients of DataVerify.
    1. Forecasting of early payment defaults (EPDs) in mortgage portfolios.
    2. Characterization of strong/soft/weak mortgage markets using polynomial regression and (Bayesian) hierarchical modeling.

    May 2006 — December 2007 (1 year 8 months)

    Duties: This position involved mostly the mathematical modeling of home equity products, including fixed rate and variable rate home equity loans and their risk analysis using the SAS programming language.

    4. Performed CHAID (waterfall) analysis for the home equity credit decision process.
