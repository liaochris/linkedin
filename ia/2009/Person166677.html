Mahesh Kumar Jothi’s Summary

Mahesh Kumar Jothi is a Network Consulting Engineer working for the Cisco Advanced Services (AS) Worldwide Service Provider Practice team in Bangalore, India.

In his current capacity, Mahesh is responsible for providing planning, designing, implementation and operations consultancy services for large and complex Service Provider clients of Cisco. He undertakes as Advance Services Network consultant for Service Providers globally.

Prior to joining Cisco, Mahesh has worked extensively on planning, designing, implementing, supporting and operating medium to large service providers and Telecom operators.

At Cisco, Mahesh has been involved in various off-shore transactional projects in US, EMEA and APAC regions.And has extensive experience in Cisco's AS Life Cycle Services.

He holds multiple industry recognised certifications and a recent recipient of expert level certfication -- CCIE Service Provider (CCIE 22014).
