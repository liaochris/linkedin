Adam Bedford’s Summary

Adam's media career began as a presenter on Sydney FM Radio at age 15. After working up the ranks to Production Director, Breakfast On-Air and then Program Director, Adam decided a bigger challenge was in order. He attended the Australian Film, Television & Radio School and industry renowned North Sydney Technical College where he majored in Film & Television Post-Production.

While working on various Corporate & Commercial projects, Adam directed several Music Videos including the debut for “Jonah’s Road” which garnered a place as one of the Top CMAA Music Video’s of 2004.

In 2005, Adam left Australia for the UK where he joined global agency “?What If!” as Senior Editor and reorganised their Motion & Sound Department into a full-service Post Production facility, working on campaigns & projects for clients including AOL, EA Games, Samsung, Citigroup, Astra Zeneca and the UK Government. Adam also supervised Media Production & QC for all of What If’s offices in London, Manchester, Sydney, New York and Shanghai.

In late 2007, Adam left the UK and relocated to Los Angeles where he began working on freelance projects
doing motion graphics and editing for trailers, commercials, sizzle reels, non-scripted Television, features and short films.  Adam’s Motion Graphics expertise includes show titles, lower-thirds, transitions, green screen compositing and IDs.

In 2008, Adam edited “Rift”, a feature thriller from Gjenius Productions, and began work as Senior Editor & Post Production Producer on a 13 episode syndicated travel series entitled “Passport to Explore” for DAVED Productions. He also worked on series pilots, commercial campaigns and Music Videos.

In 2009, Adam has just completed Series 1 of Post Production for “Passport to Explore” and is currently
at work on a new drama for director Lazrael Lison entitled “Kontrast”

He also loves playing golf, travelling around the world, playing with new technology and spending time with his wife & daughter.
