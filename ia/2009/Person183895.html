Paul Welsh’s Summary

Do you need a writer who wins awards or one that drives sales? With Welsh Writes you get both.

AWARDS
Many clients like to work with award-winning writers because they see that as a way to elevate their brand. They want to work with the “best.” Welsh has been recognized with local awards like Omni/Addy’s, but also has had a substantial amount of work recognized at the national and international levels. For example:
• CLIO
• New York Ad Club
• New York Art Directors
• The One Show
• PRINT magazine
• CA Annual
• Outdoor Advertising Association
• National Agri-Marketing Association
• Magazine Publishers Association

RESULTS
• A mortgage campaign for a local bank increased mortgage market share by 425% with a budget that was about the same as the previous year. The campaign was chronicled in Bank Marketing magazine and the bank department head said they could have done better but they weren’t prepared to handle the volume.
• An ad for a dog vaccine was in large part responsible for 300%+ market share gain in a market that doubled. The results were chronicled in the Magazine Publishers Association Objectives & Results competition which, as the name suggests, is based on results.
• An ad campaign for an electronic scale company contributed to the doubling of sales in one year and had scales back-ordered for the first time in company history.

Paul Welsh’s Specialties:

Promotional writng for all media, compelling sales letters that open doors, collateral materials that explain while they persuade and other forms of creative writing.
