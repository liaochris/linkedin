Simon Clarkson’s Summary

Classically-trained board-level marketer with client-side experience in FMCG as well as media and entertainment, my employers have included BBC Worldwide, Cadbury, Jacobs, Whitbread and W Jordan Cereals.

Brands are my passion. As the most potent source of competitive advantage - and valued signposts for us all in a complicated world - they offer the perfect win-win. I'm dedicated to making brands and brand marketing work at their best in all business environments.

Since starting my consultancy business in 2005 I have worked with major and global brands in the media, FMCG, automotive, sports, tourism, consumer electronics and B2B sectors.

The same passion led me to found Welcome Media Company, and the WelcomeHomeBox brand.

Simon Clarkson’s Specialties:

Brand management, diagnosis and development; strategic planning; new product development; pricing; communication planning and execution; marketing accountability; marketing processes; marketing coaching
