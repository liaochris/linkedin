Todd Sullivan’s Summary

Highly innovative, energetic, entrepreneurial management professional with an MBA plus 13 years of remarkable achievements in creating and driving revenue growth through strategy planning, product development, business development and strategic partnership initiatives in customer-facing ecommerce businesses.
