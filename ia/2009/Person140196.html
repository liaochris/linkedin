Leslie Helm’s Summary

Extensive experience as a staff reporter and editor. Worked for Business Week and the Los Angeles Times in Tokyo, Boston and Seattle. Was responsible for the transformation of Washington CEO Magazine into one of the nation's leading regional business magazines. The magazine received the MAGGIE award from the Western Publishers Association. Fluent in Japanese and French.

Leslie Helm’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, managing, story generation, marketing and branding, cross-cultural communications, public relations.l
