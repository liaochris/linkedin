Wayne and Lynda Gomillion’s Summary

Wayne E. Gomillion, ABR, GRI, e-PRO, QSC has been a licensed real estate practitioner since 05/96 and became a broker in 03/00. Gomillion and his wife Lynda, work together as the Pinehurst Home Team, serving the Moore County, NC area, as brokers affilated with Hagan and Hagan GMAC Real Estate. Gomillion has been actively involved in Business Networking International since 03/00 and has served in various leadership capacities. The Gomillions can found on the web @ PinehurstHomeTeam.com or by phone (910) 585-2400

Professional training and designations include: Accredited Buyer Representative (ABR), Graduate of the Realtor Institute (GRI), Certified Internet Professional (e-PRO), Quality Service Certified (QSC) and Relocation Specialist.

Professional Experience: Real Estate Broker, Accredited Buyer Representation, Institutional REO Representation, State Certified Residential Building Contractor.

Professional Objective: Develop a thriving and self perpetuating business consisting entirely of repeat and client referred business

The best referral for us is someone considering buying, selling and investing in real estate!... Another great referral is someone considering relocation… Whether the move is across the street or across the nation… We can help!

Wayne and Lynda Gomillion’s Specialties:

Serving the needs of buyers and sellers of single and multi-family properties. We specialize in new and existing residential properties for individual, military and corporate relocation clients. Our experience includes: resort communities with country club memberships; gated communities offering golf, tennis, equestrian facilities and access to private lakes.
