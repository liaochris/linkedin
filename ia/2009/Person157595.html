Mark Badertscher’s Summary

Benefits consulting and administration professional, possessing a unique combination of expertise in client management and retention, benefit plan design and operation, and organizational improvement.

Advised Fortune 1000 firms, providing analysis and recommendations on issues of plan design, operation, and fiduciary compliance.

Proven relationship management skills with a demonstrated ability to exceed client retention goals.

Specific expertise in the application of ERISA, the IRC, DOL regulations, and other relevant legislation, on the establishment and operation of a variety of benefits programs, including defined contribution, defined benefit, health and welfare, and deferred compensation plans.

Practical “hands on” experience analyzing and providing solutions to the challenges employers face in establishing and maintaining attractive and cost-efficient benefit programs for their workforce.

Strong written and oral communications skills, adept at managing conflict during difficult negotiations while maintaining and strengthening client relationships
