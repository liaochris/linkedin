John Burris’s Experience

  • President

    Silver Chalice New Media

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    November 2008 — Present (9 months)

    Silver Chalice is working with leading media companies, strong brands and sports teams / leagues to take advantage of the evolving online and mobile spaces. We specialize in mobile programming, video networks, and new media technologies. We are backed by the ownership group of the Chicago White Sox in a way that gives us a lot of business flexibility.   • Vice President Wireless Data

    Sprint

    (Public Company; S; Telecommunications industry)

    March 2002 — April 2008 (6 years 2 months)

    Oversaw and led the teams that managed Sprint's $5B wireless data business. Privileged to have led the teams that ran numerous product/service lines: internet, text, TV, music, social networking, mobile advertising, portals, enterprise services, etc. With Sprint media teams and agencies integrated mobile experiences into all marketing channels: TV, online, retail, direct marketing, sponsorships, etc.

  • Founder

    Orbit Commerce, Inc.

    (Internet industry)

    January 1998 — October 2001 (3 years 10 months)

    Co-Founded a profitable eCommerce business that enabled small and medium sized businesses to go online. Business model had Orbit selling an ASP platform to large enterprises with large customer bases (e.g. Verisign, Qwest, FedEx, Prodigy, etc.). Held top roles in Marketing and Business Development. Company was sold to Digital River (DRIV) in fall of 2001.

  • Tech Consultant (Palo Alto)

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; ACN; Information Technology and Services industry)

    August 1992 — October 1995 (3 years 3 months)

    Technology consultant working out of Accenture's Palo Alto office (Stanford Research Park). Notable clients included AirTouch, Master Lock, US West, and Netscape Communications (pre-IPO).
