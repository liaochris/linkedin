R. Paul Herman’s Summary

Advisor to accredited, institutional and everyday investors -- and consultant to companies and entrepreneurs -- on realizing sustainable, profitable growth, and doing so by solving human problems. Measurement and Scorecards of HIP (Human Impact and Profit) published at www.fastcompany.com/investing/

Entrepreneur founded, grew and sold iCanBuy.com targeting $200B market of teens, kids, parents; scaled Ashoka.org from $12m to $20mm in under 3 years; advised large firms on strategy, supply chain, finance, operations and results measures while at McKinsey and CSC Index. Shaped investments and grantmaking that deliver social impact and financial sustainability at Omidyar.net.

R. Paul Herman’s Specialties:

Investment strategy, portfolio design and measurement of human, social and environmental impacts - and how they drive financial returns and profitability.

Strategy and scaling for Global 1000, Fortune 500 or venture-backed start-ups; innovate new markets and products; operational excellence in sustainability, including "green" supply chain partnerships; eco technology and data centers, and leadership of great teams across large-scale programs and initiatives.
