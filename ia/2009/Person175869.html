Ninapilar D’s Summary

A proactive, detail‐oriented, and achievement‐driven team player with more than 10 years of progressive experience in concept organization, illustration, design, and project management.

My background experience has provided me with the knowledge, skills, spontaneity and flexibility necessary to fulfill tasks required in implementing project goals. Growing up amongst "techies" and my inherent love for design led me to start Sky Sister Studio three years ago, simultaneously pursuing a degree in Aviation Management.

My innovative and creative skills are exercised within the emerging urban planning and the evolving public relations fields. These responsibilities require a direct implementation of the basic principles of web design: the incorporation of triple bottom line strategies (people, planet, profit), requires delivery of 2.0 media campaigns, online and offline community interaction, and e-collateral and copy materials. Design is critical to conveying the intended message to an audience, especially in the current environment where demands are high and attention spans are low!

My passion for communication through great design via illustrated imagery, photography, and typography is thoroughly exercised as Concept Designer at Sky Sister Studio, Brickerati Green PR Implementation Agency, and ki work!

When I'm not designing something I'm reading about design. When I'm not reading about design, you can believe I'm thinking about it. (Smile).

My goals are to do my part in Greening my community, get as many people as possible to fall in love with typography, and to fly to as many places as possible.
