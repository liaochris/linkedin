Richard Shin’s Summary

A Certified Project Management Professional (PMP) and a member of the Project Management Institute, Richard returned to Southern California and has been back at Health Net since 2002. Richard has worked in various departments (business, information technology, enterprise PMO) over the years, performing both project management and program managament.

Before his family's return to California, Richard was a Project Manager with Centrifusion, Inc., an e-business and software development firm in Chicago. His role involved managing relationships and coordinating resources with both clients and internal staff. To ensure the successful delivery and implementation of clients' applications, Richard managed several teams of specialists (including analysts, schedulers, developers, architects, and creative designers).

Richard possesses a wealth of experience with technical and management processes and methodologies. He has developed and honed these skills as a Systems Analyst and Project Leader in the Information Services department of Health Net, and as a Project Manager and Customer Service Manager for DIRECTV. An honors student with an MBA from Loyola University Chicago, Richard has gained considerable experience with both Information Systems and Internet-based strategy, as well as marketing, through coursework, internships, and full-time employment.
