Peter Hetherington’s Summary

An experienced in-house Recruitment Manager with both HR and consultancy experience developed in a range of top brand organisations, predominantly financial and professional services.

My current role involves the creation of a recruitment function at The Pension Protection Fund, the statutory public corporation which provides, for the first time, security in retirement for thousands of eligible DB/Hybrid pension scheme members. With ambitious recruitment targets (>100 new hirs in FY09/10) my goals include the implementation of a robust ATS, and a more comprehensive route to market for our various attractive opportunities.

My single largest project is the realisation of a broad strategic secondment programme, and I am interested in nurturing alliances/partnerships with a range of organisations including: actuarial firms, pension trustees and administrators, pension consultancies, insolvency practitioners and law firms. Please do get in touch if you feel there may be synergy with your organisation!

Peter Hetherington’s Specialties:

Recruitment IS / Applicant Tracking Systems, recruitment function start-ups, UK Employment Law, competency based interviewing, recruitment & selection skills training, Agency/Supplier management.
