Frank Davi’s Summary

Frank Davi joins david mast design with a remarkable creative flair that was easily infused into the creative design team. Frank graduated from the San Francisco Art Institute in 1999 with his Masters in Fine Art and Digital Media that only enhanced his innate talent. He started his design career as a Creative Director for several creative and marketing agencies while continuing to pursue his art style.
In early 2000 he was asked by famed New York artist Sol Le Witt to assist in his Career Retrospective at the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art where Frank was challenged with creating a 3-dimensional space derived straight from one of Sol’s original 2-dimensional paintings. This sparked Frank’s interest in Environmental-Site-Design, one of the key principals of david mast design’s philosophy, the branding of a space to meet an individuals needs and personality.
Frank was nominated for a SECA Award through the SFMOMA and has had solo shows at Terrain Gallery, Gregory Lind Gallery and Limn Gallery and has had two pieces placed in the permanent collection at the Di Rosa preserve in Napa, CA.
Frank has designed several homes, landscapes, furnishings and accessories on the caliber of david mast design. Frank’s passion spills into his avid art collection and when he is not designing with the team he is an incredible outdoorsman and is a passionate snowboarder. Frank is a member of the cutting edge team exploring design to new levels for the 21st century.
