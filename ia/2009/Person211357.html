Scott W. Carpenter’s Summary

As a creative systems strategist my approach to a business challenge is to listen, ask questions, and challenge legacy approaches, then with the key stakeholders I help guide the creation of a solution. The solution delivers business results while building the organization’s ability to execute over the mid and long term.

I have over 25 years of corporate and consulting experience. My corporate experience is comprised of general management roles in supply chain management, customer service, process reengineering and merchandising. My experience is in many industries including financial services, pharmaceutical and related industries, professional services, consumer goods, manufacturing, technology and not-for-profit. Additionally, I have worked in and with a number of functions including senior leadership teams, cross functional business teams, IT, Sales, Operations and R&D.

As an adjunct professor I have taught at the Graduate and Undergraduate levels for several years.

My education includes studing business strategy at Dartmouth, Tuck School of Business in Hanover, NH and I have an MBA from Fordham University in New York, NY.

Scott W. Carpenter’s Specialties:

My expertise is in three areas: strategy implementation, team development and coaching leaders.
