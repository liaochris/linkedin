Bernie Slome’s Summary

Bernie is a serial entrepreneur with extensive, diversified expertise in all facets of successful business operations management. A proactive executive with a consistent record for prudently managing finances, building businesses, recruiting and retaining competent employees, developing and implementing proven business strategies while increasing bottom-line earnings, and achieving profit-driven goals.

For the past 20 years, Bernie has either led or consulted with companies in the computer software, computer hardware, CRM, distance learning, enterprise technology, wireless location-based services, e-commerce, fundraising, packaged goods, distribution, services, and multimedia sectors. His extensive experience in forming business development alliances, and building infrastructures have enabled the companies he has worked with raise millions of dollars in funding.

Previously, Bernie co-founded ABCO Distributors, a computer products distributor that he built from a start-up into an $100M company with more than 100 employees and nationwide sales offices. As CEO, Bernie worked with many of the nation's largest retailers and publishers, including CompUSA, Best Buy, Circuit City, Wal-Mart, Cendant, Intuit and Microsoft. As a result of his skill in introducing new products into the marketplace and having introduced such products as the Sound Blaster™, CD-ROM software and the CD-ROM kit, he was named by MultiMedia Merchandising magazine as one of the “50 Most Powerful People In the MultiMedia Industry.”

Bernie also founded BSR Associates, a consulting company with offerings in strategic consultation and implementation. Bernie also been active as an angel investor, whereby he has invested in several start-up ventures.

Bernie has been a frequent speaker and panelist at the Software Publishers Association (SPA) conferences and has hosted a weekly radio talkshow covering the computer and Internet industries. He has also authored several trade publication articles.
