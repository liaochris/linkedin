Tony Burke’s Summary

I am a marketing professional, sales leader, trainer and voice-over talent with years of success having worked for three Fortune 500 companies, Westwood One, Tribune Publishing and The Washington Post. I am known for conceptualizing solutions and developing strategies to solve marketing and sales challenges. At a recent company, I was asked to develop an online lead generating solution for customers to augment the exposure we provided in print direct marketing. In collaboration with the marketing team we developed a new online marketing platform complete with rich content, including video, which provided SMB owners with more online awareness and incremental leads via online forms.

Recently I have performed narration voice-over work for well-known organizations and working hard at doing more voice-over work to include narrations, commericials, message on hold and audio books. I have also recently written, produced and directed numerous online short form videos for local businesses.

With my broadcast and print journalism education and significant business experience I have developed into an accomplished commercial copywriter for print and broadcast with a vast amount of results producing multi-channel, marketing products to my credit. I have conducted significant research into the senior housing market and authored a book on the topic of senior housing choices targeted to baby boomers. The book is all about understanding next phase living accommodations for seniors today and I am in the process of updating the book.

I am entrepreneurial and have been described as being a “ high energy trainer and sales leader with a coaching style of management who champions people who do great work and encourages all to work toward team goals”. One colleague said..

“I am privileged to have worked with Tony in various capacities over the years. He is a gifted marketer and guide for both individuals working with him and for the companies he represents...."
