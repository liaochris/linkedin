brad mehl’s Summary

Brad Mehl has 18 years of experience in growing media, publishing and Internet businesses. He is currently Vice President of Marketing at Thomasnet.com, the flagship property of Thomas Publishing, best known for the Thomas Register. He is responsible for web/product development, marketing and alliances.

Mehl joined Thomas in 2006 after five years at LexisNexis. At LexisNexis, he was Senior Director at Martindale-Hubbell with overall responsibility for Specialty Products publishing businesses and internet marketing. He also led strategy and marketing for LexisNexis Practice Management, the technology services division.

From 2000-2001 Mehl was President of Leap Ventures, a business development firm for early stage ventures. Prior to that, he was Marketing Director at CDNOW, (currently the music channel of Amazon.com) where he created and led the company's marketing function and launched its brand in the U.S. and overseas.

Mehl also held positions in direct marketing at Time Warner and brand planning at Saatchi & Saatchi Advertising.

He holds a B.S. from Cornell University, where he received a Cornell Tradition Fellowship, and an MBA from the Stern School of Business at NYU.
