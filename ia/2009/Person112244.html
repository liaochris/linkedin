J. Gary Ellis’s Summary

"Man's mind, once stretched by a new idea, never regains its original dimensions."
-Oliver Wendell Holmes

Among the fortunate few working their passion every day, Gary combines a deep devotion for Alabama and the southeastern United States with more than 25 years of experience in custom publishing, destination marketing, and consumer trend research.

A true pioneer in customized travel solutions, Gary helped establish some of Alabama’s earliest and most successful promotional efforts in the 1980s, including the Southern Home & Garden Show and the Alabama Hospitality Association.

He founded Compass in 1988 as a one-man operation (his first office was a 1988 Toyota Camry) and grew it to a multi-million company serving more than 800 hospitality clients in 12 states.

Recognized for its contributions to the local economy, Compass in 2008 was named Alabama Gulf Coast Business of the Year.

A practical yet tenacious visionary and an unapologetic research junkie, Gary’s ability to pinpoint consumer and marketing trends has fueled Compass’ assertive expansion into emerging markets and technologies.

Blurring the boundaries of traditional media, his latest venture is a new interactive Compass business unit which offers a unique suite of interactive platforms and solutions to serve the Southeastern leisure travel market.

A strong advocate for authenticity in travel marketing, Gary works to capture and showcase the authentic voice of the South, and knows how to effectively market its irresistible allure to leisure travelers worldwide.

Gary not only is an outstanding entrepreneur, but an outstanding leader for his associates, his community and his industry. He was presented the 2009 Alabama Governor’s Tourism Award for his role in enhancing the state’s local, regional, and national image as a tourism destination, and gives his time to many local and regional professional associations.
