Scott Vayo’s Summary

My professional experience includes extensive use of CAD and BIM applications, both AutoDesk and Bentley products (AutoCAD, ADT, Revit, MicroStation, Triforma, Bentley Architecture, Parametric Cell Studio, ProjectWise). Since joining NBBJ, I have transitioned into Design Technology, relying heavily upon my past Architectural experience to inform my technology-focused interaction with project teams. It's easy for me to put myself in the Architect/Designer's shoes because I have been there myself; this gives me unparalleled insight into their needs. I have natural talent for problem-solving. I handle difficult situations well because I am cool under pressure. I also have excellent negotiation skills and the ability to see the "big picture" and plan/organize/strategize accordingly. It is my professional objective to continue to develop my specialization in Parametrics and Model Data Management (in all major software platforms that support these). I am particularly interested in the potential to re-use this information throughout the project lifecycle (including Facilities Management by the client/owner).

Scott Vayo’s Specialties:

I have two areas of specialty within Design Technology: Parametric Object Generation and Model Data Management (for BIM/FM). In layman's terms, these specialties are:

1) The creation of single digital objects that represent many different versions (sizes, models, etc.), their differences controlled by variables.
2) The integration of critical data with the 3D object (model #, size, clearance, cost, manufacturer, color, weight, power consumption, etc.). The 3D object "contains" the data.
