Tom Feary’s Summary

Tom is a 14 year veteran of the media industry from within the marketing function providing thought leadership and strategy. He currently is General Manager at Interactive Financial Marketing Group based in Richmond, Virginia.

Before joining IFMG, Tom served as GM of Advanced Media for Charter Communications in their St. Louis media operation. There Tom was responsible for defining the new media road map including marketing and sales of long-form advertising in On Demand, Internet advertising, and interactive television. Prior to joining Charter, Tom served as Adelphia's VP of Marketing of their $300M media operation.

Tom is a graduate of Auburn University and has an MBA from Washington University's Olin Business School in St. Louis. He and his wife Tracey have two daughters.

Executive management, thought leadership, strategic planning, brand marketing, communications, multi-media, new media, interactive advertising, team leadership, client relationship management, customer segmentation, consumer behavior
