Ian Daly’s Summary

Experienced TMT and coverage banker in the UK & Ireland Investment Banking team at Nomura (previously Lehman Brothers).

At Nomura, I provide financial advisory services to my clients across a range of industries that are tailored to meet their long term strategic objectives - including the full range of investment banking products e.g. equity/debt capital raising and buy/sell side M&A.

Prior to Nomura, I obtained 9 years international grounding across multiple roles in the mobile communications and software industry. Previous responsibilities included: Corporate strategy, product management, sales & marketing, research & development and operations.


KEY ATTRIBUTES

• Commercially Astute, extensive experience in business case development, obtaining investment capital, partner/supplier selection, contract negotiation and product P&L ownership

• Creative Thinker, continuously looking at how to best exploit both current business assets and emergent trends into a net company gain

• Drives Business, knowing and having what it takes to turn concepts or visions into solid business objectives, and meaningful products or services

• A Powerful Communicator, translating industry jargon into evocative messages for a broad audience and in various forms: from internal evangelist, through customer and partner presentations, to conference speaker and panellist, to media liaison and press releases, to white papers and workshop facilitation.

• Solid Technical Understanding of mobile and complimentary wireless IP and IT technologies, brought about by core engineering background and managing complex product developments
