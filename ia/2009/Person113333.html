J2O Designs:
_________________________________________________________________

J2O Designs is based in Fair Oaks Ranch, Texas, just outside of Boerne, and is one of the state's premier Web design and marketing firms. With over 15 years experience working with Fortune 500 Companies, Universities and small businesses across the state, J2O Designs is proud to bring its services back home to the Texas Hill Country.

Our team of graphic designers, coders and writers work together to provide excellent customer service while implementing successful web designs and strategic marketing campaigns that get results for our clients.

But don't just take our word for it. View our Portfolio and see for yourself (http://www.j2odesigns.com/portfolio/).



19th Hole Productions:
_________________________________________________________________

19thHole.com is the Premier Online Social Network for Golfers. The community is absolutely free to join and connects golfers with similar interests through online communities allowing members the opportunity to find friends, golf partners and expert advice from industry leaders.

19thHole.com was originally established in 1994, during the “birth” of the Web. The “New” 19thHole.com re-launched on April 1st, 2007. As we grow our network of Members I hope you will come in and have a look around. Membership costs nothing, the people are friendly and the barstools are comfortable.
