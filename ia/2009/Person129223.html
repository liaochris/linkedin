Kelley Long’s Summary

As a CPA and "reformed" trust officer, I combine my tax experience and estate planning knowledge with investment advice to provide holistic financial planning advice. I'm all about planning, both for today and the future!

I use my experience and love of teaching to help people better understand the complicated terms used in the marketplace, as well as bring people together to learn from each other through round table discussions.

I'm passionate about guiding clients and friends toward becoming their personal best, and make it my mission to give them the necessary tools to be financially secure.

My consultative approach is unique to each person I work with, recognizing that money is a very personal subject.
