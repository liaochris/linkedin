Kay Stout’s Summary

Experienced Professional in the areas of Career Advice, Career Transition. Develop and deliver innovative career transition and coaching programs to professionals in all industries and disciplines.

Promoted Small Business intiative with former First Lady Shirley Bellmon of Oklahoma. Provided on-air interviews and answered callers questions on a one-hour show "Your Career Connection" for Clear Channel's local radio station in Oklahoma City, KTOK.


Involved with the Oklahoma State Chamber of Commerce' nonpartisan Oklahoma Prosperity Project as the Director of the Speaker's Bureau (www.okprosperity.com)

Frequent contributor to newspapers and on-line career sites.
