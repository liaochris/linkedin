Logan Hale’s Summary

For the past six years I have worked in event production and experiential marketing. After living and working in such competitive metropolitan markets as New York City, San Francisco and Los Angeles, I have extensive experience in creating the best impact for the right audience. Whether it be an exclusive, intimate gathering in the Hamptons or parading 3,000 guests through the streets of San Francisco, my primary objective is always to give my clients confidence that their guests are having the time of their lives.

I know, and thrive on, the intensity and perfection associated with high-profile events. I see them as live, real-time “movies” with an audience waiting to be entertained. It’s a challenge that I love to meet. I have had a 30-piece marching band snake through a three-story venue, guests dressed in couture fashion in the middle of a party and 10,000 cans of Coca-Cola distributed in the heart of Times Square in a matter of minutes. Past clients have included Starwood, Ketel One, Village Voice, Vogue, Playboy, Pernod-Ricard & Sony.
