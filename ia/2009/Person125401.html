David Kadavy’s Summary

Product Manager, Interaction Designer, and Web Developer with an Entrepreneurial bent. A very early-stage team member of online job board WorkMetro (acquired by Jobing, Inc.), and "green Yelp" SustainLane.com. Creator of Through a Friend, ( http://snipr.com/28w0e ), a sort of "Craigslist on social," and Flatmate Meetup, which is like Speed-Dating for roommates.

Diverse in experience, and most comfortable when frequently switching hats. Experienced with Product Management, Identity and Brand Strategy, UI Design, SEM, SEO, and Monetization. A leading theorist on Social Media, publishing articles on the topic on http://kadavy.net.

David Kadavy’s Specialties:

Graphic Design, User Interface Design, Interaction Design, Product Management, Project Management, Social Media Theory, Branding, Identity, SEO, SEM, Google Analytics, Adwords, Adsense, User Requirements Documents (URD), User Persona Modeling, Application Specifications/Mockups, Adobe Creative Suite, Flash, XHTML, CSS, PHP, JSP, Django Templates, Subversion (SVN).
