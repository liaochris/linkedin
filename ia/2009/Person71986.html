Ellen Howard’s Summary

I have been in the recruiting business for over 10 years. I place all levels of candidates, from recent college grads seeking entry level positions to senior Vice Presidents. My clients are in various industries: fashion, finance, media, executive search, etc. I have established, long term relationships with many hiring managers/recruiters in a variety of industries. I am quite thorough in my interview process, and provide feedback to both clients and applicants. I try to place candidates in positions in reputable firms where they can grow professionally and
become an asset to their companies. I listen to the needs of both the firms and the candidates so that when a resume is sent, we are all on the same page.
I welcome inquiries from new clients or candidates who would like to find out more about my services .
