Robert Peek’s Summary

I am a corporate communications professional with 20 years of experience in the field, and I am accredited by the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA). I hold a master's degree in public administration and a B.A. in journalism. My professional strengths are strategic public relations planning, publications and web management, crisis communications, media relations, branding and special event planning. Also, I'm an all-around good guy, so I've got that going for me. :)
