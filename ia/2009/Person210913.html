Louise Holden’s Summary

Choose carefully. We do.

We recruit healthcare professionals both nationally and internationally.

Meet the team:

Meet Frances – specialist recruiter to the Allied Health Sector.

Frances is very passionate about working within healthcare, she feels inspired by her clients and candidates and understands the very special work they do.
Working with both private and public healthcare providers including Brain Injury, Frances recruits primarily for the following positions:
Physiotherapists, Occupational Therapists, Clinical Psychologists, Neuropsychologists & Speech-Language Therapists.

Meet Vicki – specialist recruiter for Registered Nurses and Dental professionals.

With a genuine love of recruitment and being a stickler for working with ethics and professionalism at all times Vicki has carved out a reputation for being honest, responsive and delivering on promises.
Vicki has carved a niche market within healthcare and she really knows her registered nurses and dental professionals. Whether it be for Mental Health, Rehabilitation, Aged Care, Primary Health or the Dental sector Vicki will track down the top talent.

Meet Louise – specialist recruiter and candidate sourcing guru

Candidate sourcing that is out side of the box is a passion for this recruitment specialist; this enables scarletBLACK to hunt down top talent in the market both nationally and internationally.

Working predominantly with aged care providers and private hospitals Louise recruits primarily for (but not limited to) registered nurses, charge nurses, clinical nurse leaders, nurse managers, clinical advisors, facility managers and senior healthcare managers.

We have the added bonus of being owned by Debbie Graham & Associates. A New Zealand icon within the recruitment industry.
