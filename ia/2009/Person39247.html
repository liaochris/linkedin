Catherine Poole’s Summary

Catherine Poole, is known internationally as “The Queen of Color”™ and is an expert in color and human response to color.

Catherine joined the WIN Firm and applies her color expertise to the marketing and advertising industry. As Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) Catherine brings her unique talent to the firm in the form of “intuitive corporate consulting” focusing on the non-verbal communication between a brand, the corporation it represents, the consumer, and the role of color in the entire communication process.

Catherine is the National Spokesperson for FreshAire© Choice paint brand (carried exclusively at Home Depot) and proudly represents their “The Color Prescription” Campaign. This campaign focuses on educating consumers about how color can enhance personal well-being, influence mood and how The FreshAire© Choice Brand can create a positive impact on your health, your life and the planet.

Catherine has presented her unique perspective of color and energy medicine to medical schools, including Indiana University School of Medicine, Eastern Tennessee State University (both leading family practice programs) as well as hospital environments. Catherine’s client list includes, doctors, celebrities, psychologists, successful business professionals, authors, speakers and political figures. Her corporate clients have included Eastman Chemical Company, Reebok International, Newell-Rubbermaid as well as universities, smaller companies, institutions and organizations.

Internationally, Catherine has lived and worked in Den Haag, Netherlands and periodically conducts presentations and talks in Den Haag and Amsterdam. She lived in Warsaw, Poland and taught The Philosophy of Color at Warsaw University for graduate and doctorate students in Philosophy.
