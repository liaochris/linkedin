Grant Johmann’s Summary

Wiredset LLC – New York, NY
Senior Vice President of Digital Marketing

Grant is a digital marketing maven with nearly a decade of experience in the development, design and execution of engagement marketing and social media campaigns for leading entertainment, music and consumer brands. As the SVP of Digital Marketing for Wiredset, Grant has used innovative thinking and the creation of next generation tools to spearhead successful digital initiatives for brands such as Comedy Central, Harper Collins, Last.fm, Spike, ESPN, Oxygen, Ticketmaster, Universal Music, and many more. Grant's area of expertise stretches across the digital spectrum, from social media, search engine, email and relationship-based marketing, to audience and community building, online analytics, media planning and brand consulting.

Prior to joining Wiredset, Grant was the Director of Online Marketing for Elektra Records where he managed campaigns for Metallica, Missy Elliott, Staind, and all other label artists.

Grant grew up in Queens before obtaining his BS in Engineering and Management Systems from Columbia University.
