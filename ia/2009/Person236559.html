To continue to develop the accountancy practice and to hone my skills in respect of Charity client assignments. I am currently developing a collaborative business relationship with Charity Funding Solutions in Cambridge.
To provide a good standard of living for my family and to travel the world.
To continue helping people improve their health and wealth.
To continue to develop my speaking skills and confidence by attending Hunts Speakers, a local Toastmasters group that I help to form and currently act as Treasurer
