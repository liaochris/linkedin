Max Henry’s Summary

Max Henry has spent 18 years in Asia, living and working in Tokyo, Singapore, Hong Kong, Taipei, Bangkok, Beijing and Shanghai.

Graduated from ISG multinational business school, Max initially started his career in trade publishing and media.

For the past 10 years, Max has engaged actively in developing supply chain and logistics solutions for both overseas SMEs and foreign-invested companies in China: running a strategic sourcing consulting firm Sinosources, developed a B2B platform Sparkice.com and managed a supply chain solution provider ECnet. More recently, he was the China Operations Director for a France-based value-added logistics services provider called Trans-Access Solutions.

Max is now the executive director of the Global Supply Chain Council and dedicates his full time in leading and developing this fast-growing professional organization in some of the hottest markets like China, India and Vietnam.
