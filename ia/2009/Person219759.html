Valerie Elizabeth’s Summary

About Society Stylist

Society Stylist was founded by Valerie Elizabeth, a Glambassador for GLAMOUR Magazine and author of the style blog, The Diary of Elizabeth Hearst. Society Stylist works with individuals, designers, photographers, agencies and shows. Some current clients include Kids of Dallas Fashion Show, Dallas Symphony Orchestra League 2010 Debutantes, Junior League of Dallas 2010 Fashion Show and many more.

Your Unique Look: We want to help each of our clients find their individual style. It is important that each person find a look that works for them. It must be comfortable, manageable and most importantly, uniquely theirs.

Making It Your Own: We emphasize the importance of putting your own twist on whatever you own. We love Phillip Lim's concept of having each of his employees wear the same item of clothing to prove how everyone can make an item their own.

Working Your Wardrobe: We strongly believe in starting at home. Most Americans have wardrobes that could clothe a small third world country. Those same Americans would adamantly insist that they have "nothing to wear." At Society Stylist we believe in refining, restyling and streamlining your wardrobe so your favorite boutique is right behind your wardrobe doors.

Buying What You Love: When shopping for and with our clients, our goal is to educate and empower our clients to select pieces with meaning, with style and with staying power.

Style Shots: A unique aspect of our styling services is that we provide our clients with "Style Shots," photos or written idea cards detailing what to wear, when. We help our clients plan for future events and provide everyday ideas in advance for those days when they don't have time, or desire, to sort out their own looks. "Style Shots" come with each styling package and are available on their own for clients who have completed The Wardrobe Cleanse.

Visit our website to inquire about packages and learn more about us.

Valerie Elizabeth’s Interests:

About Society Stylist Owner, Valerie Elizabeth Valerie graduated from Pepperdine University, in Malibu, with a degree in Public Relations and Business Administration. Valerie started her fashion career at the, once glorious, Ralph Lauren boutique at South Coast Plaza. Valerie developed a love for fashion and design at a young age and learned to create unique looks on the go, often with limited resources. She is an avid philanthropist and an active member of the Junior League of Dallas and Kappa Alpha Theta. Valerie lives with her husband and son, in Dallas.
