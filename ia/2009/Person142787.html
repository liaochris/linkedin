Liz Colville’s Summary

As an online journalist specializing in music, I see the Internet as indispensable to my career and those of the musicians I write about. My goal as a music writer is to focus on the good music, events, trends and stories happening in the indie music world and focus less on bad music, doomsday studies about falling CD sales and how piracy is ruining everything. Because I don't think it is.

The Internet is making all this happen, and it's an emerging area of study. Talking about how the Internet is changing society, business and the brain is necessary no matter what industry you're in, I think. Alongside my professional roles, I plan to be a keen observer of Internet trends, as well as a guinea pig.

Liz Colville’s Specialties:

Experienced music writer and online journalist with focus on Internet technology; fluency in established and emerging social media tools including StumbleUpon and Twitter; strong SEO skills; excellent Web search skills; studious tracker of trends in Web 2.0 and online journalism publishing; amateur website designer.
