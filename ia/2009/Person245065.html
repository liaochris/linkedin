Vladislav Solodkiy’s Summary

I have some interesting strength and characteristics:
1. 10-years work experience - including 6-years experience in marketing in several areas of business - finance, telecommunications, entertainment (work with FMCG-leaders) - allows to achieve synergy effect from mix of experience in different areas.
2. High-quality education in strategic marketing (MBA, Stockholm School of Economics) and public administration (postgraduate study in the Moscow State University, magistracy of the St.Petersburg State University, grant from Moscow State Institute of Foreign Affairs) allows to look at a situation from different sides and on macro-level.
