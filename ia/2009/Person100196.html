Jennifer Niehouse’s Summary

As a Wardrobe & Image Consultant, I help...

1) Corporations, small businesses, universities/high schools, groups/organizations—Presentations to educate employees and students on the importance of ‘appropriate business attire in the workplace’ and how it reflects upon their company’s image, productivity and attitude. “Business casual can become a casualty.”

Gen-Yers are talented and intelligent but can lack the expertise of professional dress for the job interview, at the office, business dinners and business social events.

If you see cleavage, short skirts, flip flops, or any sort of TMI fashion 'don'ts', let me help you define the 'do's' and make better sense of your company's or classroom's dress code.

2) Individuals—I can easily get a feel for your style by just working with your clothes, accessories, shoes, etc. I am thankfully a girl of many fashionistas... bohemian, classic, vintage, modern, conservative (only a little) : ). This way I connect with my clients quickly and find ‘their look’.

Many times we get stuck in 'the rut' put the same things together or gravitate to the same kinds of clothing when we shop. A strength of mine is making suggestions and knowing what types of clothes will flatter you. With this said, my client's feel as ease about trying something new, stepping outside their box/comfort zone when I do make suggestions and are excited about the fresh look. We, women, can be hard on ourselves with negative thinking/body image and miss out on fun opportunities with fashion by saying, “oh that would never look good on me or I look horrible in that color.”

These truly are my passions...helping others with fashion.
