Joel M. Blatt’s Summary

Joel M. Blatt brings more than 20 years of domestic and international sales and marketing experience working for some of the software industry’s biggest players. Joel is responsible for setting Sales and Marketing vision and strategy, as well as driving overall execution for SigTran Consulting and Sparkbliss.com.

Prior to founding SigTran Consulting and Sparkbliss.com (also dba Private Online Dating dot com,) Blatt held sales leadership positions at Oracle Corporation and Siebel Systems. As a Vice President of Oracle, Joel managed the sales side of a global swat team, responsible for facilitating “significant transactions” around the world. In addition, he was accountable for the CRM Division’s global sales forecast. At Siebel, Joel enabled multi-billion dollar global accounts such as 3M Company, Medtronic, and Philips Medical Systems to meet profitability initiatives aimed at driving higher top-line growth, productivity, and cash flow.

Joel brings a wide array of enterprise software experience working for companies such as Microsoft, Vantive, CrossWorlds, Spry, and FourGen.

Joel M. Blatt’s Specialties:

Consultancy specialty focuses upon building sales pipeline from scratch. Unique methodology rooted in practitioner-model and pay for performance emphasis upon results. Strategy produces results with a "lead by example" approach. Strengths include abilities to prosecute complex, enterprise, significant transactions; improving sales forecasting accuracy; optimizing lead generation, "3X" pipeline development, and enforcement of sales methodologies through best practices.
