Judy Herzanek’s Summary

SKILLS AND ABILITIES:
• Art Direction/Diversification—
I have extensive design experience working primarily for large corporations. Throughout my career I have partnered with illustrators, photographers, calligraphers, freelance designers, structural engineers, brand managers, copywriters, translators, production managers and printers to successfully guide a project to completion. Past projects include packaging, POP, advertising, website, trade show booths, annual reports, books, calendars, catalogs, brochures, branded merchandise, signage, websites, videos and sales materials.

• Management Capabilities—
I have effectively and efficiently managed 1,700+ creative projects per year for the Hain Celestial Group. Staying organized, shifting priorities and tight timelines are routine and I know how to deal with these issues daily—smoothly and without stress. Teamwork and dedication are important to me. I value these traits and I model this behavior for others. I have a solid ability to write and communicate clearly and am proficient in InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Word, Excel, Project Path and Function Point—with above average typing skills.

• Passion—
Most importantly, I have a strong passion for strategic creative design and I truly enjoy my work. I believe in positive reinforcement, treating my direct reports and team members with patience and respect. I approach each project (large or small) with enthusiasm.
