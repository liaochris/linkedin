Dean Mix’s Summary

I have been with Seattle Financial Group for 18 years. I started in 1990 as a Residential Loan Officer for Seattle Mortgage Company. I was a top producer during my six years in the Southcenter Branch. In 1997, I was promoted to the Residential Construction Department. My background in lending was a great stepping stone into this department, as I was able to work with many builders and developers during his early years understanding their needs and product requirements. I understand the importance of working together in partnership with his clients. Since joining the Residential Construction Department, annual production has grown from $65 Million in 1997 to over $250 Million in 2006.

Additionally, I am providing businesses with cash management services and lines of credit to grow the operation.
