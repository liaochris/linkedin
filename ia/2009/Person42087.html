Cherie Concannon’s Summary

Over 19 years Cherie has written, developed and delivered hundreds of programmes motivating thousands of people Europe wide to achieve outstanding results in their sectors. Her techniques and strategies have an exceptional reputation within organisations for increasing profitability, raising productivity, quality and efficiency. Cherie built CCC Inspirations, a flourishing coaching and development business from a standing start.

Her coaching style is objective driven and involves developing a close relationship with her clients. Through identifying a strategic plan and troubleshooting to deal with obstacles, Cherie coaches and supports her clients all the way to reach their goals and refocus, reposition or transition in any area.

Early 2009 Cherie pioneered the Wartime Leadership programme based on adapting leadership styles to maintain the power and impact required for surviving under pressure in a crisis, or critical conditions.

Cherie is also author of forthcoming book ‘Cherie Concannon’s 7 Golden Rules for Any Woman to Succeed in a Male Dominated Environment’ based on her experience as a Leadership coaching for top executives (90% of whom are male).

Cherie is a larger than life character who is charismatic and energetic and has an incredible ability to inspire, empower, and problem solve. She is a popular keynote speaker who is regularly invited to global conferences and public speaking engagements.
