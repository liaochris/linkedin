Ian Smith’s Summary

My mantra:Companies are either invisible or remarkable. You are either one or the other. Ian works as a hands on business angel to invest in and manage technology companies.

Ian has built and sold over 20 companies by applying
'Repositioning' techniques to create sustainable and attractive businesses.

Ian's hobbies include competitive masters track where he is ranked #1 in the country at 800m for his age.

Ian Smith’s Specialties:

Repositioning Companies Organically and by Acquisition to create remarkable businesses.

  • Divisional MD

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; CPI; Information Technology and Services industry)

    1994 — 2000 (6 years)

    During this period of Ian's leadership it was the preferred supplier to the prestigous Institute of Directors and their 40,000 membership.

  • MD

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Investment Banking industry)

    1988 — 1993 (5 years)

    Livingstone Fisher as it was called in 1988 was one of the first Corporate Finance Boutiques in London. Founded by the M&A guru Barrie Pearson, Ian was the second employee and assisted in growing Livingstone over a 5 year period into the leading European M&A adviser for SMB deals. (Under $50m)

  • Divisonal Finance Director

    The Thomson Corporation

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; TOC; Information Services industry)

    1982 — 1987 (5 years)

    Ian joined Thomson as a young chartered accountant in Thomson's London Head Office.
    He was fast tracked through 3 promotions in 18 months to become one of the youngest Finance Directors in the UK at 26.
    In 1987 at the age of 27 he was awarded the prestigous Young Accountant of the Year Award for his commercial work turning around the Consumer Magazines Division.
