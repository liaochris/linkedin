Jack Niner’s Specialties:

Our specialty is being able not only to provide a comprehensible set of "remodeling" plans, "as-built" drawings and specifications acceptable to the plan reviewers in obtaining the building permit, but with the added expertise to write details, scope of work and specifications without the "mindless and distracting key noting" thus striving to provide a readily understandable scope of work for the owner, customer, field laborer, sub-contractors, and project manager.
