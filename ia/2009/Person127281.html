Kate Leydon’s Summary

Twenty two years experience in the professional beauty industry in sales, marketing, management, education, branding, distribution, manufacturing, store development.

President Ruby Room 13,000 sq ft Urban Sanctuary in Chicago which includes a spa, salon, yoga lounge, healing sanctuary, three boutiques and eight overnight guest rooms, private label line and advanced education academy.

Bumble and bumble three years as National Sales Director identifying areas of opportunity, opening new markets, hiring, training and creating sales structure for US market, identifying areas of growth and expansion.

Aveda network, eight years, specializing in areas of sales, marketing, education, systems management and expansion. Positions include Account Executive, Central Region Sales Trainer, National Sales Director Canada, Managing Director Aveda Lifestyle Stores.

Vision: to leave a beauty mark on the face of the wellness industry through our committment to healing, energy and all things positive.

Kate Leydon’s Specialties:
