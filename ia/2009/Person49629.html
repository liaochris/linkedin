Joe White’s Summary

Joe White is a Transportation Expert with a MBA and 25 years trucking Operations Management and Operations Improvement experience that started his own consultancy practice in 2005. CostDown Consulting provides Programs & Training that reduce the Cost of Trucking. Specific services include Operations Audits, Performance Management Programs, Driver Manager Training and Driver Retention. To date, CostDown Consulting has saved industry companies over $11 Million.

Prior Industry Experience Includes:

• Directed a corporate planning department for a $1 Billion transportation company
• Developed and managed Operations Improvement programs at 80 terminal locations across N America that reduced cost and capital needs by $8 Million
• Directed post merger fleet plan for 4,000 rigs that reduced required fleet size by 4.5%
• Managed national and regional load building teams (3 Million lds/yr)
• Developed network strategy for Centralized Dispatch
• Full P&L, administrative and customer service responsibility for regional trucking terminals servicing both foreign and domestic customers

Joe White’s Specialties:
