Gina-Marie Cincinnati’s Summary

Graphic Designer & Photographer since 1999. Graduated with a BFA from MICA (2003) and specialize in Digital Media/Web. Currently working towards expert certifications and an MFA.

My experience is wide and varied. I have managed to hold a job handling almost every aspect of the design industry. I've been a consultant, production artist, designer, director, photographer, critic, copy writer and editor. I enjoy it all, and prefer to have my hands in most of it. I have a very social and outgoing personality, and maintain my contacts well. I enjoy meeting new people and hearing different points of view. I value honesty and hard work above all else.

My end goal is to teach at the college level.

Gina-Marie Cincinnati’s Specialties:

Teaching & instruction, technical expertise in programs, identity & brand standards, web integration, pro bono, negotiating
