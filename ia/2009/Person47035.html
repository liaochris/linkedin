Claudia Walker’s Summary

Claudia Walker and Associates takes pride in our consultative process. We aren’t just about full-time, direct hire staffing, our consultative process works symbiotically with our screening process, to find highly skilled employees able to adapt to any industry and impact any corporate environment.
The search for top talent intensifies in order to define your company’s value, we encourage you to take your talent management to a new level.
We follow though, we listen and we partner together on strategic advice and spotting trends.
Claudia Walker and Associates’ approach is consultative, whereby we can understand your companies’ specific search, recruiting, and staffing challenges. We interact with you, the Client, to get an in-depth analysis of your company, your management, the available position, and current search methods to date.
We strive to develop a partnership that insures satisfaction.
