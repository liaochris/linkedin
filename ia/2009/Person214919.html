Shane Coburn’s Summary

My old (both in terms of his age, and the chronology of my life) English teacher once said “Writing is about getting your point across in the most efficient way possible.” There's a lot of truth in that statement, and I think it speaks to more than just writing. However, I might change “efficient” to “effective.” No, I definitely would.

Ode to Mr. Looney…

I have years of experience in creative direction, marketing, copywriting, scriptwriting, project management, product development, video editing, and producing. I spent 12 years building new companies within the youth market of inline skating, and was responsible for creating 6 of the most popular brands in the marketplace during that time. My last company, Mindgame (2000-2007) -- which only utilized black and white graphics -- was widely known for its distinct messaging and continues to be one of the most revered inline brands of all time. I've spent the last 2 years in online marketing and project direction, specifically reaching out to the 18-34 demographic via niche social networks. In 2008, I helped to make 'quarterlife' the most-watched web series on the Internet. Having worked in a variety of industries with very different demographics, I've learned to customize my voice to best reach each target community. Basically, if ‘Ninja’ were a degree one could hold in branding and communications, even those guys would be afraid of me.
