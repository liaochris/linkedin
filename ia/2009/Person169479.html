Mike Taulty’s Experience

  • Developer Evangelist

    Microsoft Ltd

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; MSFT; Computer Software industry)

    December 2003 — Present (5 years 2 months)

    Working with the community of Microsoft developers in the UK to help them get the best from current and future technologies through broad communication mechanisms such as public events, blog sites, the MSDN UK site.

    I have represented Microsoft at technical conferences such as TechEd Europe, DevDays Holland, MIX UK, UK product launch events and also at smaller sessions with developers working for Microsoft's customers across all sectors. Audiences range in size from 5 to 1200.

    Over the years in this role I have had strong associations with technologies such as BizTalk Server, SQL Server, Visual Studio, XML and Web Services, the Windows OS.

    Then there's the .NET 3.0 technologies such as WPF ( and Blend ), WCF, WF and the 3.5 and Sp1 technologies such as LINQ ( to SQL, to Entities and to XML ) and Astoria that I've built a strong association with.

    Through 2007/2008 I've also taken on a lot of activity around the Silverlight 2 platform and associated frameworks and tooling.

  • Application Development Consultant

    Microsoft Ltd

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; MSFT; Computer Software industry)

    December 2000 — December 2003 (3 years 1 month)

    Providing technical and architectural advice on Microsoft developer technologies to a small number of dedicated customers on an annual basis.

    I have worked with Microsoft ISVs from the very smallest start-up companies through to the largest ISVs in the UK providing deep developer consultancy across Microsoft's client, server and database technologies.

    I also managed the UK Microsoft scalability lab and ran customers through performance and scalability testing of their applications.
