Davina Haisell’s Summary

Who is Davina Haisell?

A fantastic proofreader, a brilliant editor, a talented writer, blogger and life coach.

I’ve used no adjective to describe life coaching. Why?

Because trying to explain life coaching is an invitation to use too many pretty words. Words like authentic, inspiring, fulfillment, momentum and illumination. Being the writer that I am, I would get too carried away.

I will tell you that life coaching takes two. I ask thought-provoking questions, and the client always has an answer.

Why someone works with a life coach determines if life coaching is right for them.

When a person works with a life coach because they “choose to” rather than “need to”, it makes all the difference. Choice puts you in the driver’s seat. A life coach won’t let you forget that you are doing all the driving.

Now, I’ll tell you something about that fantastic proofreader and brilliant editor.

I have a background in advertising where I was employed as a proofreader for 20 years. I design and format books and ebooks. I’ve read menus for Boston Pizza, advertisments for TELUS and BC Hydro, and packaging for Sun-Rype to name a few. Clients and colleagues continue to call me by my nickname of eagle eyes.

One colleague says, “Davina understands the bigger picture significance of quality control. Without her dedication and diligence, it wouldn't have been so easy for us to deliver consistent brand experiences for our clients and their customers.”

Launching a personal development blog has satisfied my insatiable love of writing. It has also given me the pleasure of supporting other writers and bloggers who have been thankful for my expert editing and proofreading services.

One client says, “You did an excellent job with my last post. I couldn't believe how many things you caught and how much better the post read after your edits.”

I encourage you to visit my websites or blog for more information. Or join me on Twitter at http://twitter.com/DavinaH
