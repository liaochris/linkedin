Daniel Hindin’s Summary

After living and working in the San Francisco Bay Area for the past eight years, I recently relocated back home to Chicago and am pursuing a position in communications/marketing/public relations/social media.

While my journalism background gives me expertise in the editorial and production aspects of the industry, my time as a real estate broker gives me the marketing-based approach to fulfill promotional goals and my experience as a membership director illustrates my abilities in outreach and relationship building.

During my time in newspapers, I was recognized for my ability to focus on the tiniest of details while not losing sight of the big picture. Both a self-starter and team player, I was quickly promoted from writer to editor to nighttime newsroom director, where my increasing responsibilities included coordinating operations between four separate departments, developing the editorial calendar and design concept for six weekly features sections and overseeing the work of dozens of freelancers. I also created multiple editorial elements that attracted attention to our product and managed the website.

As a real estate broker, my meticulous client services approach included developing marketing campaigns, creating marketing materials and acting as the public “face” of the properties I was selling and the clients I was representing.

I have also dedicated countless hours to nonprofit organizations – both as an individual volunteer and as a member of several boards of directors. Most recently, I served as Director of Membership for a fledgling community organization, where I contributed to the development of a marketing strategy we employed to attract media attention and build our membership from zero to more than 700 in less than one year. I was responsible for maintaining our member database, coordinating communication with existing and prospective members and producing branded marketing collateral for distribution to the public.

Daniel Hindin’s Specialties:

I’m a talented communicator whose career is founded on relationship building and consumer outreach through personal interaction and the written word. My strength in producing printed materials for distribution to targeted groups and the public has lent itself to a variety of sales campaigns and organizational operations, and my earnest yet aggressive approach to harvesting relationships with members of the business, political and media spheres has made me a valued asset to employers and causes.
