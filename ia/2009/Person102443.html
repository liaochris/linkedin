James Farbelow’s Summary

An innovative and driven operations manager with over 10 year’s experience creating high performance teams. Although recent focus has been spent project managing the voice and data integration of former Comcast and Adelphia subscribers into the Time Warner Cable Corporation, my background lies in operations. Where focus areas in my past have varied, one thing that never waivers is success. Whether the task is to launch the new VoIP product, build the next generation NOC, or simply turn around the day to day operations from poor performance to "best in class" service -I've done it, and succeeded with vigor. When taking on a new challenge my goal usually remains the same -turn business as usual into “change” using the principles of momentum. I look for all opportunities to focus individuals, increase team performance and teach employees at all levels how to apply the principles of performance management and process improvement.
