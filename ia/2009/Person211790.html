Scott Jensen’s Summary

Well-rounded executive with 16 years of experience driving innovation with a track record of forming, restructuring, and profitably growing technology and media-centric companies.

Poised, articulate, and a creative thinker with business acumen to successfully launch digital content and forge first-of-their-kind partnerships for motion pictures, the Internet, and mobile phones. In 2006 joined Vindigo, a NY-based mobile content and services provider with annual revenue of $40M, and served as CEO during a restructuring of the 60-employee firm. Personally responsible for growing Vindigo’s carrier distribution network which became the largest in North America, and managed key partners such as MapQuest/AOL and The New York Times.

Offers keen insight into the digital content needs of consumers and the ability to strategically identify new opportunities that will drive awareness and revenue for companies in the following business areas:
• Media & Entertainment: Familiarity with leading companies including Disney, News Corp., and Viacom
• Sports: Worked at a professional NFL team, founded a fantasy sports ASP, and forged deals with star athletes, coaches such as Bill Parcells, NCAA, CBS SportsLine, FOX Sports, & professional leagues including MLB
• Games: Has run game development studios and partnered with leading game publishers like EA and Activision
• Technology/Telecommunications: Extensive knowledge of business models and active relationships with key mobile value chain participants, including AT&T Mobility, Nokia, RIM, Sprint, Verizon Wireless & Vodafone

Experienced and adept at succeeding within a wide variety of functional roles and teams, including, but not limited to:
• Executive and C-Level Management
• Sales and Marketing
• Business Development and Partner Relations
• Content Licensing and Programming
• Business Affairs & Intellectual Property Management
• Corporate Development
