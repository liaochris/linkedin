Erica Mueller, ACIR, CDR’s Summary

What you need to know about Pinstripe:

We’re widely recognized as an RPO leader. We’ve been featured as leading RPO thinkers and practitioners in a host of industry publications, including HR Executive, HR Magazine, Talent Management, HR Pulse and others. HRO Today magazine recently named Pinstripe as among firms that are “recognized RPO providers with significant deals in the marketplace.”

We’re tech-savvy. We help clients bridge the gap between proven technologies like Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and the most powerful Web 2.0 and Enterprise 2.0 recruiting tools and practices.

You may even say we’re “passivists.” That’s neither a typo nor a political stand. It’s our recognition that the organization that’s best at recruiting passive candidates will have a profound advantage in a hyper-competitive employment climate. Talk to our clients or RPO industry experts, and you’ll learn that Pinstripe has earned a reputation for excelling at sourcing high-quality, passive candidates.

Our approach to RPO completes the circle of talent acquisition and engagement. We’re the first in the marketplace to integrate functions that extend beyond the traditional boundaries of recruitment and hiring. Pinstripe’s approach to RPO extends the value chain on both ends. It begins with workforce planning, advances internet sourcing, and recruitment branding/marketing and ends with the new employee's onboarding, orientation and initial training, maximizing the new employee's performance results and the client's ROI.
