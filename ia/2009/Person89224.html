Todd Erickson’s Summary

Current: Greenovation, LLC

Licensed General Contractor & Green Design/Build Consultant, Phoenix, AZ.

Registered Energy Star & USDOE Builders' Challenge Partner.

Providing design/build, land development, energy conservation, project management and feasibility consulting & contracting services for home & landowners, investors, developers and businesses.

Focus on hybrid energy efficiency and energy production projects and “green” design implementation in both new construction and retrofit settings.

Mission:

To emerge as an industry leader in the incorporation of Energy Star, LEED and other energy-efficient and sustainable resource conservation and hybrid building technologies into market offerings and services.
