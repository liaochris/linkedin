Diego Ostrovsky’s Summary

My experience is based on the digital marketing, design and comunications fields. Over the last years, I've been working on several projects in Agencies and Television, where I focused on interactive design, motion graphics and Web applications.

Also, I am a former professor from the Image Design & Sound and Graphic Designs Careers of Palermo University, where I taught subjects such as Web design, Introduction to Design and Brand Design. In addition, I published articles related to design, effective communications and NPL.

Currently, I´m an active member of the UDGBA (Union of Graphic Designers of Buenos Aires) and I was invited to participate as a guest professor at the Architect Roxana Meygide class of the Graphic Designs career at the University of Buenos Aires (UBA).

I have been developed several works for companies related to the Internet, multimedia, television, corporative image, graphical advertising and creativity. I was in charge of several projects and campaigns in different companies.

I´m a graphical designer from the ORT Technical School at Buenos Aires Argentina.
