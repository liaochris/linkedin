Jerome Albert’s Summary

Staff IC Designer with extensive experience in Timing Closure on high performance SoC. As an STA expert, led back end teams through chip timing closure phases, on award winning 405EX and 460xx processors.
I am currently working on AMCC Power PC dual core solution, built around CAXI interconnect, with proprietary packet engine architecture. As my next career move, I am willing to get more customer visibility while strenghtening my competencies in the physical design area.

Jerome Albert’s Specialties:

Performed chip timing closure on ASIC and COT design flows :
- Timing closure with physical variations aware timing.
- Floor planning, clocking and placement guidelines.
- DDR2 timing closure from 333 to 1066 Mbps.
- Defined voltage and frequency binning solutions.
