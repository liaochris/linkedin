Paige Freeborn’s Summary

Over the past 18 years, I have seen a myriad of titles printed on my business cards, all of them a variation on the same theme: helping businesses define themselves, what their brand story is, and find the best way to tell that story to the people who need to hear it.

This "story telling" activity falls into many categories including branding, communications, advertising and design, but it all starts with the core strategy of your business.

Most recently Identica Branding & Design, a Cossette group, hired me to build and lead the client service team of their Toronto office. This means I'm responsible for making sure everything is done with exceptional quality, creativity and results. I hire the people who do this work, put the processes in place that guide us all through the maze, and, of course, help you define who you are and how we tell the story of your brand. What this means for you is that I'm looking after your best interests from the minute we begin speaking about your business.

I've got a client list that some consider kind of impressive, but all it really means is that I've learned some significant lessons in building brands and business from people who really know their stuff. Some of these people (a.k.a. clients) include: Microsoft, Rogers, AstraZeneca, Bombardier, Adobe, Research in Motion, Nortel, Wal*Mart Canada, Grand & Toy, OMERS, Altamira and The Taylor Group.

I think it's also important that you know WHY I do this work. I love it. Pure and simple. I'm fascinated by the process and learn new things every day. And if I can share that knowledge with you and it helps your business grow, well then we're all happy.

This passion of mine sits at the intersection of your brand and your audience and we're waiting to have a great conversation with you...

Follow me on Twitter @pageby_paige
