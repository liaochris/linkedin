Abdul Aziz Seyid’s Summary

Aziz is currently Head-Business Domain and Leadership Development Training with Talent Transformation and Intrapreneurship Development, HCL Technologies Ltd based out of Noida, India.

Prior to joining HCL in November 2006, Aziz has had 15 years of international management experience plus 6 years of academic teaching and training experience. His professional career commenced in London with Lloyds Bank as an International Management Trainee. Subsequent postings were in Hong Kong, Taiwan, Japan, Papua New Guinea and Singapore in various areas of corporate and investment banking. From 1993 to 1997, he was Executive Director and Country Head Lloyds Bank- Singapore. He was also Vice President with Chase Manhattan Bank (now JP Morgan Chase) - Singapore with regional marketing responsibilities for S.E. Asia and the Indian sub-continent.

Upon his return to India in 2000, Aziz decided on a career change into academics He was at The American College- Madurai as Director from 2000-2003. In 2003, Aziz moved to Delhi to join ICFAI-B-School- Gurgaon as Professor in Business Strategy and Marketing. He has also taught at Amity Business School and Birla Institute of Management. He has conducted training programs for Indian Society for Training and Development and the International Chamber of Consultants.

Aziz was educated at St. Columba’s School, Delhi (ISC), St. Stephen’s College-Delhi University (BA Honors), Cass Business School- City University London (MBA) and INSEAD- France (Advanced Management Program). He also holds a MSc degree in Psychology from University of Madras and a Diploma in Investment Management from the Institute of Banking and Finance-Singapore. He is a member of various professional bodies.

Aziz’s interests include reading, traveling (over 50 countries), playing golf, flying (private pilot’s license holder) participating in business -quiz competitions at the national level and plays the drums in a rock band.
