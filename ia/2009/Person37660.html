Carlos Atherton’s Summary

An employment agency for busy professionals...

A diversity focused employment agency, based in Hartford, CT and providing employment services to multiple New England, NJ and NY area based companies, but not excluding the southern United States.

Computer Staffing USA provides technical career search services to multiple industries, specializing in biotechnical engineering, manufacturing, research scientist, health and DNA research.

Also, highly skilled engineering fields like fiber optics, G3 RF connectors, wireless telecommunications, plastics injection molding, system architects, VoIP, FPGA, and emerging technologies "green energy".

Aerospace Quality Control and Manufacturing people like our engineering knowledge and understanding.

On the consulting side; 22 Years combined experience designing and developing Fiber optic, Hybrid and RF connectors, plastic injection molding, die castings, sheet metal packaging, electromechanical integration, fuel cells for automotive, medical equipment, blower castings, electrical diagrams, across multiple industries.

IT installations, including ProEngineer 2001, Wildfire and Pro PDM configurations and installations. Familiar with most Microsoft products, SAP, Baan, Informix, Oracle, SQL, Visual Basic, FrontPage 2003, Expression Web, etc.
College teacher for 3D modeling, and computer software, for 7 years.

Recruiter for Engineering and IT - Started as a hobby in 1998, helping out a friend who needed someone to interview RF Engineers and Oracle Database Architects.
To make a story short, in 2000 I opened my own agency and continued to help other companies and recruiters place technical people and split the commissions.

Now, I am back to working with the same friend that I started out with and doing splits when placing professional technical people. Full circle of life!

Carlos Atherton’s Specialties:

My engineering and IT backgrounds allows me to interview people on the same playing field. Having a great deal of research time and interest in the "Green Energy" field lets me recruit in the BioFuel, Hydrogen, Solar and Hydropower fields.
I believe that field-specifiec knowledge is very important to match a company to an employee.
Computer skills allows me to organize, schedule, follow up and screen the 'story tellers'.
