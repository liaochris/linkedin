Troy Henson’s Summary

Troy Henson is not only the principle art director at Blue Kaboom, he also leads daily operation and is responsible for building and maintaining client relations.

Troy draws upon years of design, marketing and client service experience through employment with such companies as Kings Island, CBS Companies, Provident Bank (now National City), and Cincinnati Bell Telephone.

Troy began his career as a designer at CBS Companies, as he finished his Bachelors of Fine Arts at Northern Kentucky University.

After graduating be became employed at Provident Bank as a in-house designer. He moved onto a start-up company called ZoomTown.com (later would be assumed by Cincinnati Bell Telephone), where he was responsible for developing the first ZoomTown ADSL manuals and many of the marketing materials. Troy was promoted several times before leaving to start-up his own design firm, Blue Kaboom.
