Keidra Chaney’s Summary

My professional communications experience runs the gamut from magazine/newspaper writing, communications, web design and grant writing for non-profits, and has now moved into web/social media content strategy and web analytics.

I blog about social media and online culture at thelearnedfangirl.com and i frequently conduct workshops with organizations about blogging, social media and web analytics.
