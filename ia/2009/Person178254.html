Oliver Clay’s Summary

Founder & Director of Red Carpet; “Barcelona’s Only High End Destination Designers and Tailor Made Concierge Service”.

Fulfilling personal ambitions through the provision of unique services to others has proved to be a challenging life goal. Barcelona with its increasing number of cosmopolitan visitors presented the ideal market for an independent high-end tailor made international concierge service. Red Carpet has been founded to meet this need and fulfilling my calling.

Travelling the world from super yacht to private estate in the service of the Saudi Royal Family has provided insights into the nature of exclusive personalized service or ( what a premiere client could and would expect.)

It has been my privilege to manage the smallest details as host of their many guests. So surpass their expectations was my pleasure.

The move to Barcelona in 2002 to join the dynamic butler team of the world famous penthouse apartments of the Hotel Arts (Ritz Carlton) provided the opportunity to deliver a similar quality of service attending to exclusive VIP clientele.

In 2007, I joined the BI&HBS Worldwide Group in forming Red Carpet BCN. This international marketing and event group is based in Barcelona. It has a solid reputation, a profound knowledge platform along with an exclusive local network developed over the past 5 years. There is no better source for service and hospitality than you can imagine.

Red Carpet BCN…at your service for Work or Play.
