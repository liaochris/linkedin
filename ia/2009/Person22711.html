Michael Jayes’s Summary

I am the proud owner of Avenger Aviation. I currently hold a Commercial Pilots License and a Mechanical Engineering Degree, with specialisation in Aerodynamics, having gained experience in Aeronautical Design and in Project Engineering.

Avenger Aviation offers advice to clients, who wish to open the doors of the aviation world, either in their personal or business capacities. Avenger Aviation provides accurate, up-to-date and relevant technical and financial information required for building the solid foundation, necessary for making an informed decision to meet the client’s unique aviation need. Avenger Aviation presents the data and findings in an easy-to-read document with graphical representation of aircraft capabilities and operating costs.

Avenger Aviation sources the best new and pre-owned aircraft to meet the client’s specific needs according to the decisions made. The scope of Avenger Aviation is not limited to any specific aircraft type, category or class but rather looks at the unique requirements of the client to advise them on the best aircraft available.

Avenger Aviation investigates aircraft operating histories; aircraft service histories and aircraft condition and the best available aircraft are selected and presented to the client for their own inspection and evaluation. Test flights and/or charter flights on the selected aircraft can be arranged depending on aircraft availability and owner agreements.
