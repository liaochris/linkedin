Kenneth "Adam" Simmons’s Summary

The past three years I have worked at the Lessard Group, a large architecture firm in northern Virginia. I was hired right after graduation specifically to launch the firm’s conversion into the Revit platform. This is a challenge that I took on with great passion and dedication. I currently head the Lessard Group’s Revit training program. I have led several training sessions to acclimate employees without any Revit experience to move onto a team that would start a new project in the Revit platform. Recently, my responsibilities have carried over to training people in Lessard’s newly established office in Ahmedabad, India. In addition to coordinating and conducting employee Revit training, I am also responsible for consulting on project set-up and organization for Revit projects within the office. I have successfully served as a consultant on several large Revit projects while simultaneously continuing to manage my own work as a project coordinator. My experience in this area has allowed me to ensure such projects run smoothly and efficiently at all times. My expertise in Revit has also allowed me to develop and manage an extensive catalog of content for the firm’s Revit library.
