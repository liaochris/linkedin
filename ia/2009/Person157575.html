Maz Nadjm’s Summary

I am responsible for Sky Portal’s social media strategy and implementation. The Sky Portal has over 1 billion monthly page impressions and many millions of unique visitors. I work closely with each Sky site, providing on-going technology and strategy assessment within the context of the competitive landscape and Site needs.

I Co-Founded Rareface a product of hard work and determination since 2004. Right from the start, I saw and believed in the exciting possibilities that social networking technology could bring to companies to promote their products and services, generate new revenue and raise awareness.

I Co-Founded InfieldParking.com, a social networking site and growing online community of NASCAR racing fans in commercial partnership with its most popular driver, Dale Earnhardt Jr.

Prior to Rareface and Infieldparking, I was in charge of setting up the social networking department at iConcertina, an interactive media agency. I Founded 2Bmates.com a social networking site for music lovers sponsored by iConcertina.

I was born in Iran and raised up in Sweden. I speaks Farsi, Swedish, Norwegian and Danish. I completed my higher education at University of Lund, Hawaii Pacific University and Leeds Metropolitan University.
