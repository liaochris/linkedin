Biography

Joe Hamm brings significant expertise to Lancope in the area of optimizing IT network and security operations. In his current role at Lancope, he works to assist large enterprises in achieving their network monitoring and reporting goals. Prior to Lancope, Joe served as the technical account manager for Internet Security Systems, where he provided ongoing support and account management to enterprise IDS/IPS customers. Before joining ISS, he gained considerable networking proficiency at El Paso Energy Corporation and Cisco Systems, serving as a network engineer at El Paso and developing executive briefing environments at Cisco. He has also served as the Technical Editor of Linux Routing, a book by New Riders Publishing covering common routing and security issues. A recognized network and security expert, Joe has spoken at numerous industry conferences, including Interop. A continuing supporter of the technology community, Joe is an active member of IEEE and the Information Systems Security Association.

Strong interest in social networking, Web 2.0 technology, mashups, software as a service, and viral marketing.
