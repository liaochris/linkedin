Daniel Sarno’s Summary

Sarno Photography & Graphic Design™ is a division of Sarno Creative Arts, Inc. ™ and is owned and operated by artist Daniel Sarno. Daniel’s interests of touring the great outdoors, preserving the environment, and helping others combined with art and architecture is why he created Sarno Photography & Graphic Design™. Daniel Sarno loves helping businesses grow by marketing their properties through the arts of photography, graphic design, and web design. Daniel Sarno earned his bachelor’s degree in fine art from Bowling Green State University and continues to develop his fine art whenever possible.

Mission Statement:
To market and promote properties through the arts of graphic design, web design, and photography, while providing professional, caring, finished solutions, and ongoing customer service with respect to community, preserving history, and the earth.

Daniel Sarno’s Specialties:

Branding and Identity, Complete design packages with custom photography, 360° virtual tours, architectural photography, landscape photography, panoramic photography, graphic design, web design, search engine optimization, SEO
