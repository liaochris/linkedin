Joseph Krengel’s Summary

A casual observer might describe me as a computer-savvy entrepreneur channeling his energy to finding work in the corporate world. Starting my own business is an end-game ambition however, and right now my driving force is in developing my career in Talent Acquisition, Retention, Organizational Development.

While I have a long history in administrative and technical jobs, my first real foothold in this career was my 3-month contract with MacDonald-Cole Associates, as a recruiter. Coming out of that I ended up at SNC-Lavalin, where I realized that my true passion isn't just in identifying and recruiting professionals, but in constructing a framework for that activity, managing the process behind it, and deploying Recruitment Strategies to advance broader company goals.

My role at SNC-Lavalin was largely undefined at the outset, and through my hard work, and the collaborative nature of my co-workers and supervisors I was able to carve my own niche as a recruitment and ATS "guru", all the while contributing to international recruitment strategizing, and engaging SNC's various clients and vendors. I was also available to offer recruitment support in a pinch.

When an opportunity to work with David Rempel (who had brought me in at SNC) presented itself, and move my employment much closer to home, I lept at the chance. At Wardrop my role has evolved along a very similar line; I am responsible for company-wide Recruitment reporting, manage our ATS, and am engaged in all levels of discussion regarding recruitment strategies and development; all the while lending support to our great team of recruiters.
