Steve Stein’s Summary

Addicted to the Internet and a gadget lover, I am always curious about the newest developments in information design, HCI, usability and web semantics. During the last decade I made my way from assembling computers as a sales clerk, to building web 2.0 concepts as a consultant; I started from learning media informatics and now I teach media design.

I would like to pursue an active career in a challenging environment that offers exiting opportunities to prove my abilities in demanding projects. I am looking for an employee that anticipates and values my qualities and also allows me to consequently expand my competencies to new interesting fields

My specialization is project management and consulting in the area of technical user experience design and concept creation for web applications, usability, accessibility and security. I am experienced in various web technologies and intercultural teamwork.
