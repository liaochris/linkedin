Jose Ramon Vasquez, M.Sc.,PMP’s Summary

Seasoned project management professional with a large experience in different strategic and complementary business areas, such as telecommunication services management projects, information technologies solutions deployment projects and business transformation projects.

My experience in telecommunication project management includes data communication private network (WAN) projects, managed communication service (MCS) development projects, design of marketing strategies and telecoms product life cycle management.

My achievements, in marketing of telecommunication services, include design and sale of telecoms solutions to large enterprises from a client business vision.

As information technologies project manager, my experience includes business intelligent and data warehouse projects and information system development projects.

My work demonstrates a high degree of autonomy and attention to detail. Recognized aptitude for teamwork, ability to work under pressure in a complex and constantly evolving environment.

Strong analytical, planning, organizational and problem-solving skills. Independence and leadership.

Innovative, creative and team work oriented.

. Member of Project Management Institute (www.pmi.org),
. Member of the Ordre des Ingénieurs du Québec.
