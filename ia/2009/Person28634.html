William McDonald’s Summary

Over 36 years in the satellite communications industry with SES Americom (formerly RCA Americom and GE Americom). Principal engineer past 7 years, previously regional manager, earth station manager, and earth station technician. MBA from University of La Verne in California. Worked past 2 1/2 years as a primary engineer on the SES Americom IP Prime project (MPEG4 encoded IP video for customers to receive from SES AMC9 satellite and distribute over IP networks to their subscribers). Will provide engineering and satellite communications consulting expertise to companies in the industry.

William McDonald’s Specialties:

Engineering, integrating, and testing complete satellite earth station communications systems including transmit and receive RF systems, digital commmunications systems, and monitoring systems. Project budgeting, satellite link analysis, technical documentation, and program management.
