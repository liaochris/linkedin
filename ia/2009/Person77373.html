Fabian Torres’s Summary

Fabian has served in several senior management and board level positions in large multinationals (Philips, Atos-Origin, VW-gedas, T-Systems-Deutsche Telekom and Steria). He has also actively participated in ITC initiatives at local and international scale during the last 25 years.
Fabian holds an Industrial Engineer Degree at the Universidad Politecnica de Madrid, a MBA from IESE (Universidad de Navarra) and other senior education programs from Malik Management Zentrum St. Gallen.
He combines his professional activity with the educational as professor of CEPADE (Universidad Politecnica de Madrid) and IEN (Industriales Escuela de Negocios).
Fabian lives in Madrid with his wife Carmen and his two childrens: Fabian and Alejandro.

Fabian Torres’s Specialties:

• Setting up and deployment of Business Strategies for ITC firms.
• Business Development: Account Management Policies and Portfolio Development.
• Operation Management and Service Delivery of : Consultancy, System Integration and Managed Services.
• Mergers and acquisitions.
