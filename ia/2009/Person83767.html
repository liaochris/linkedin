Gary Kretchmer’s Summary

Direct marketing specialist at developing risk-free, response-oriented advertising campaigns through radio, newspaper, yellow pages, Internet and other broadcast, print & online media.

Target + Response is a direct response agency specializing in pay-per-lead (per-inquiry) advertising.

We have maintained a leadership position in pay-per-lead radio advertising since 1987, and have expanded our services to include print, yellow pages, internet and other lead generation opportunites.

Our expertise is to provide highly qualified sales leads for direct marketers at a fixed cost per lead. We work with thousands of media sources to generate cost-efficient and virtually risk-free results.

You can save 80% in media costs. Visit our website at http://www.target-response.com for more information.
