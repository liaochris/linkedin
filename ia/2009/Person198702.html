Rick Schuett’s Summary

Smart, "out of the box" thinker, with rare ability for both strategic thinking PLUS tactical implementation when appropriate. Proven history as rainmaker for business process improvement and recognizing new market opportunities to grow existing businesses, especially those that might be experiencing slow/no growth. Strongest asset is the ability to see how to "connect the dots" and recognize hidden business and sales opportunities, create a gameplan and subsequently execute - and in the process not just grow but transform the business to create long-term sustainable growth.

Prior to my currently position I had overall responsibility for Lutron's Commercial Systems Business Unit (previously I had conceptualized, created and then developed the Residential Systems Business Unit). This included but was not limited to business unit P&L, worldwide sales, marketing, engineering, manufacturing/operations, and HR responsibilites. The primary products/solutions that we offered were architectural lighting control systems, energy management controls, high frequency electronic dimming ballasts, and high frequency dimmable LED drivers.

I'm enjoying my new role leading some great people and watching them grow, and would be interested in any BOD opportunities, networking to mutually grow our companies, lead-sharing, sharing of sales forces, and anything else that makes sense.

  • VP New Business Development, General Manager Residential Systems Division

    (Privately Held; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    August 1982 — September 2002 (20 years 2 months)

One of my most significant achievements was recognizing and developing the lighting controls category for the custom installation (CEDIA) channel for Lutron from 1991 to 2000. Additionally I conceived of and developed "The Ultimate Home Theater Experience" at Innoventions West at Epcot, which consisted of 30+ consumer electronics manufacturers who joined forces to demonstrate home theater to the masses. I also was elected to the CEDIA Board of Directors from 1995-2001, as well as the Consumer Electronics Association (CEA) Board of Directors from 2001 to 2004.
