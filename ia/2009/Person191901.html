Sam Weaver[LION]’s Summary

Traditional Radio, Podcasting, Internet Radio

Sam Weaver formed his Radio Coach Talent Training Service after 30 years as a successful Operations Manager, Program Director and Announcer. Sam's career experience covers CHR/Top 40, Mainstream Urban, Country, Urban AC, and Rock. He programmed top-rated stations like KPRS-KPRT/Kansas City, KRNB/Dallas, WAMO/Pittsburgh, WQMG/Greensboro, WDIA/Memphis and WYLD/New Orleans. As an announcer, his on-air stops included KSLQ/St Louis (legendary Bartel Radio Chain), WUSN/Chicago, KDIA/San Francisco, WDRQ/Detroit (Legendary Bartell Radio Chain), WJPC/Chicago and KMJM/St. Louis.

Weaver's commitment to teaching grew out of his role as an instructor at Columbia College in Chicago. Other career highlights include working as a weekly comedy writer for the nationally syndicated Urban Network countdown show. He also was the host for Westwood One's nationally syndicated Superstars of R&B concert series.

A popular speaker, numerous times he has moderated, or been a panelist, at conventions for Billboard, Radio and Records, NAB, The Conclave, BRE, and the Canadian Music Festival.
