Eric Cogswell’s Summary

Eric has sales experience across numerous industries and product lines. He began his career in Advertising and Human Resource Development Training sales, while completing his university degree.

He has worked with companies across industry segments, including: Packaging, Financial, Data Centres, Telecom, Cable, and E-Commerce.

Products include: IP-VPN, VoIP, Voice Services, Data Connectivity, Conferencing, Wireless, Billing Systems, and Consulting.

Eric uses a consultative sales approach to look for a solution for his customers and partners. He works broadly and deeply within an organization to quickly build rapport. Customer focused, he looks to resolve customer issues quickly, looking for a win-win solution. He works independently in a team environment, excels when he encounters a new challenge, and enjoys building teams within an organization.

Throughout his career, Eric has been able to leverage his skills, and develop new ones, though his volunteer activities. A volunteer for UNICEF Canada, he co-produced a concert event in Halifax that ran for two years, and was involved in coordinating talent, volunteers, the technical rehearsal, as well as being backstage director for the event.

Eric has held many volunteer roles within the sailing community:
• Vice Commodore of Fleet at National Yacht Club (NYC) for two years;
• Crane Director lifting motor and sailing yachts worth up to and exceeding $500,000 – NYC;
• Co-Chair of the Food and Beverage Committee – NYC;
• Judicial member of the Protest Committees;
• Chairperson of an Adult Racing course – NYC.

These experiences have required for Eric to be responsible for full-time employees and volunteers, while managing budgets and strategically planning the direction of the yacht club and its programs.

Eric has also worked as a Human Resource Development Trainer, has done some Project Management, and has had Retail experience over his career.
