Michael Coveney’s Summary

- 32 years in information technology as applied to business management
- course leader and public speaker on corporate performance management
- thought leader and writer (co-author of The Strategy Gap published by J Wiley 2003) on how organisations can overcome the gap between strategy and execution
- travelled extensively throughout the world conducting management workshops on how to apply technology to managing performance
