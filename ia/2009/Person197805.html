Richard Lukaj’s Summary

Richard S. Lukaj founded The Bank Street Holdings LLC, a private investment firm providing a comprehensive array of financial advisory services, primarily to middle market companies. In addition, Bank Street Holdings acquired control of The Bank Street Group LLC, a US Broker Dealer registered with the NASD and SIPC (www.bankstreetgroup.com). Prior to these latest ventures, Mr. Lukaj was a Senior Managing Director at Bear, Stearns & Co. Inc. where he served in a number of different roles since he joined the firm in 1991. He was appointed as one of the youngest Senior Managing Directors in the firm’s corporate history due to his significant contributions to Bear Stearns’ profitability and success. Mr. Lukaj headed the Global Emerging Telecommunications practice at Bear Stearns for three years prior to his departure. Mr. Lukaj has executed hundreds of transactions ranging in variety from mergers and acquisitions, underwriting (debt, equity and derivative securities), restructurings, exclusive sales and financial advisory. During his career, Mr. Lukaj executed over 200 transactions exceeding $100 billion in transactions value and contributed to the development of one of the strongest investment banking franchises on Wall Street, particularly in the areas of Media, Communications and Technology.

Mr. Lukaj is a David Rockefeller Fellow for the City of New York and is an active participant of the New York City Partnership. Mr. Lukaj is also on the advisory board of the New York City Investment Fund.

Mr. Lukaj was recognized for his philanthropic activities in New York Magazine. He was also featured as the cover story of an issue of Investment Dealers Digest and was selected as one of the “Top 40 under 40” business executives by Crain’s New York Business.

Mr. Lukaj received his business education from New York University’s Leonard N. Stern School of Business.
