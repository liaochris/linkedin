David Storrs’s Summary

Pica9 is a marketing automation company with a client list straight out of the Fortune 100. As Director of Programming, I function as a combination business analyst (gathering requirements, time and cost estimates), project manager, and team lead.


As to my personal background: I am a largely self-educated computer programmer. As my career progressed, I found myself in more Team Lead and Architect roles, doing some management and some programming. I was surprised to find that I actively enjoyed the design-, documentation-, and people-parts of these roles. Furthermore, as an English (as opposed to Computer Science) major, I have a strong ability to bridge the communications gap between businesspeople and programmers. This has lead to more precise specifications and more successful projects all around.

David Storrs’s Specialties:

Project management, requirements drafting, web programming, documentation, software team coordination, system architecture design, business strategy, business management.
