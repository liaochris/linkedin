Sean McVey’s Summary

Our goal is to give your venture start-up the spark it needs to grow by providing in-depth market research and fresh ideas for a fraction of the cost of a generic marketing company. No automated processes, no meaningless buzzwords, and no flashy corporate slideshows. We are real entrepreneurs like you and believe in a long-term strategy branding as opposed to cheap tricks that produce short-term results. Based in New York City, the media capital of the world, we understand how to reach the right channels and build a foundational network for your small company.
