Sebastien Arbogast’s Summary

I’m a software architect and developer, with 6+ years of experience with Java enterprise technologies. During the last 4 years as a software engineering consultant, I’ve worked for 9 different customers and environments, from Model-Driven Architecture technical lead to security and cryptography development going through agile architect in a .Net environment. I also participate a lot in Open Source projects and do a lot of technology watch and software experiments on my spare time, especially with cutting edge technologies including Flex, Grails, iPhone and OSGi.

Project Management: Scrum
