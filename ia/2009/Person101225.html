Jack Yan’s Summary

Goal: to create a better world for all humankind through business. Founded one of the earliest virtual companies in 1987, entered web publishing 1994, discussed moral global brands 2000, spirituality in business concept 2003, took a web magazine brand into print 2004 (and into two countries 2005). Designed over 150 typefaces. Member of think-tank Medinge Group (www.medinge.org) and several other global organizations; co-author of Beyond Branding (2004); author of Typography and Branding (2005); co-author of Brands with a Conscience (to be published); publisher of CAP and Lucire (www.lucire.com, www.lucire.net). BCA 1994, LL B 1995, BCA (Hons.) 1996, MCA 2000. Visit company site at www.jya.net. Read my blog at www.jackyan.com/blog.

Jack Yan’s Specialties:

Branding (connecting visions to business outcomes), international public speaking, strategy, media developments, future consumer movements, corporate social responsibility, intellectual property management, cross-media issues, business visions, trend forecasting, entrepreneurship, exploration of organic brands, and spirituality in business. See www.jackyan.com for more information about me.
