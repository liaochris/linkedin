Mira Emmerling’s Summary

Luxury enthusiast, entrepreneur, avid traveler and connector. I have been on the hunt and ravaging the world for the finest since I was young. Raised just outside of NYC I split my time between Europe and the US and continue to do so. Each destination brings new people, flavors, languages and styles. Absorbing each culture gave me a knack in style and connecting with people. Along the way I met many individuals with various interests and areas of expertise. I found myself purchasing for a small luxury boutique at a young age which developed into personal shopping and styling others during my travels. I strive to bring luxury services and products to clients daily. At the same time, many people I meet become close friends and I have taken the role of connecting expert friends with media friends and vice versa. Then, Schnell:Press social media consultants and media expert connection was formally launched with my partner. We help connect media with experts worldwide as well as provide social media services to various B2B, B2C, G2B, G2C and non-profit organizations. I am looking to connect with media professionals that are trying to find sources/experts, anyone that has an area of expertise that is sought after by the media, individuals in the luxury industry as well as individuals or organizations that are seeking to develop their social media presence.
