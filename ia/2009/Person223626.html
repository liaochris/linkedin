Stephen Allott’s Summary

Stephen Allott is a management consultant with over 30 years experience in IT and an impressive track record of delivering successful solutions. He’s worked for software suppliers, end user organisations and is currently Principal Consultant of ElectroMind, a consulting company he founded in 2002.

Stephen’s experience spans the finance, telecommunications, and travel sectors; his clients have included investment banks, BT (British Telecom), RBS (Royal Bank of Scotland), a mobile telecommunications operator, Flight Centre UK, and FCm Travel Solutions.

Stephen is an enthusiastic, pragmatic, IT professional, focused on helping companies deliver high quality software on-time, on-budget and, most importantly, software that meets the current needs of the business.

He is able to bring together a group of like-minded individuals, sourcing consultants with different skill sets, forming a cohesive team that will create innovative and cost-effective solutions in today’s fast changing business world.

Stephen is an advisor to Specialisterne, an IT company that employs people with Autism.

Stephen Allott’s Specialties:

Management consultancy, Strategic consulting, Test Strategy Development, Process Improvement, Formal reviews and Inspection
