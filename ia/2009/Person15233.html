Andy Grogan’s Summary

I am a determined technical professional whom has worked within the public sector for a number of years. I am driven and focussed with a proven track record in the design, sourcing and implementation of large scale technical projects. My main experience is within the Microsoft arena - in particular Active Directory and Exchange server, however I have solid experience of SQL, MOM / SCOM, SharePoint (Design and deployment), SMS, Windows 200x (including 2008) and many other Microsoft offerings. I have also worked in depth with many enterprise class storage solutions (HP and IBM) and have a good understanding of Enterprise grade Unix platforms. During my career I have deployed Exchange and Active Directory to over 120,000 seats (combined) and seen through countess technical implementations in other fields.
I like to think that I have a “get the job done” mentality and have the ability to motivate the people whom I manage via my own enthusiasm for the technical that we bring to the business.
