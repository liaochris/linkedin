James Murphy’s Summary

I recently joined Activision, where I am managing games based on Dreamworks Animation's Kung Fu Panda and Monsters Vs Aliens franchises. I am greatly enjoying the challenges in this industry, as well as getting to know the fantastic people at Dreamworks Animation. Passion is contagious, and the team at Dreamworks Animation is very enthusiastic about their stories and characters.

I worked at Mattel for four years, a fun and dynamic company that I would highly recommend to anyone looking to get into brand management. Mattel can be a highly entrepreneurial company, and a recent MBA can get tremendous experience in general strategy, marketing tactics, financial management and product development.

Before going to business school at the University of Southern California, I served as a Marine infantry officer for seven years, deploying to Iraq in 2003.

I pride myself on both my organizational ability and analytical skills, and on my general creativity. My long term goal is to grow into positions of greater responsibility at "story-oriented" consumer packaged goods companies.
