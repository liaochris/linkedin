John Bernardi’s Summary

John has hired and managed over 100 sales and marketing professionals, who have consistently achieved or exceeded objectives in customer satisfaction, personal development and financial results. His teams have enjoyed their work, developed their skills, and applied the right tools in an appropriate environment for success.

John is creative with exceptional organizational and communications skills. He builds relationships with decision makers and influencers. He recognizes the benefits of complementary marketing and works to identify appropriate partners who can assist him in delivering whole product solutions to his customers.

His strengths include strategy development and effective execution of quick-hitting, cost-effective actions in support of objectives to penetrate new markets, introduce new products or do a better job of selling existing products to existing markets.

John has also taught marketing, management and business math as an adjunct professor at Albertus Magnus College and Quinnipiac University.

John graduated with honors from the University of Notre Dame's College of Engineering and the University of Miami's MBA program.

John Bernardi’s Specialties:

Being a fast learner with a complete perspective across all functions enables me to navigate an organization horizontally and vertically in search of opportunities to enhance marketing results. I am unique in my ability to span analysis through implementation and continuous improvement through employees who are accountable for my deliverables. I have a broad industry perspective, a strong technology background and low overhead that enables a highly attractive and flexible fee structure.
