Teri Baskett PM & ITIL Cert.’s Summary

Was Vice President of IT for over 13 years. In that time, I managed many projects, small and large, intra and inter-departmental. For the last 2 years, IT focused on EMR initiative and disaster recovery/business continuity planning, creating a second data center. I developed the IT strategic plan for the company, and managed the agency technology budget. We often had 3 to 6 projects in various stages of implementation at once, in order to accomplish department and agency goals.

Saved 60 hrs/week in staff time through implementation of automated reminder system for appointments.
Cut $70,000 from IT networking expense by re-designing connections and traffic routing.
Cut $12,000/month from clearinghouse billing expense by billing Medicaid directly.

I am very interested in telemedicine initiatives and EMR implementation. I have created many Business Case documents and ROI reports for senior management. I am PMP and ITIL Certified, have a Master of Science degree. I understand HIPAA Privacy and ANSI formats, JCAHO accreditation, managed care and health care insurance. I have worked on Medicaid and Medicare billing processes. I have supervised technical and support staff positions. I have written trainings and end user documentation for software programs.

Past member and President of Toastmasters International Club in Jeffersonville, Indiana. Completed Dale Carnegie Effective Communication and Leadership for Managers courses.
