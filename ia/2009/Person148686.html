Marcia LaReau’s Summary

As the owner of Forward Motion, LLC, I build customized workshops and seminars and assist businesses, schools, sales teams, as well as college students who are ready to join the work force and individuals who are in career transition.

Forward Motion is focusing on helping college students transition into the workplace environment and in helping businesses with the orientation for entry-level employees.

The eventual use for the profits from Forward Motion, LLC will be used to work with orchestras in financial difficulties and implement a new model that will establish long term solidity with regard to their financial, cultural and musical health.
