Justin Phillips’s Summary

I have been working in the radio industry for the past 14 years. I have gained experience in on-air talent, station imaging, commercial production and voicework. I have recently began independently contracting voicework and received a part-time permanent position producing national weather forecasts, as well as voicework for regional television spots. To keep up with America's trends, I have created a blog and accompanying RSS feeds related to American Idol, with the goal of selling these services and personalized broadcasts to radio stations across the nation.

In addition to my radio experience, I have been working as a mobile DJ/Karoke (KJ) for the past two years, mainly performing by services at weddings and large parties. I can be booked for these services through Caught In the Act Productions (541-915-9957) at events in Lane County, Oregon.

Justin Phillips’s Specialties:

I have vast experience in branding and identity imaging production for FM radio stations. I also work with radio station clients on a weekly basis to coach and produce their own voicework for their businesses commercials.
