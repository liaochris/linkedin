Chris Berger’s Experience

  • Assistant Vice President, Marketing

    66 Federal Credit Union

    (Financial Services industry)

    May 2006 — May 2009 (3 years 1 month)

    Coordinated and managed the marketing and public relations/communication department for all locations in Oklahoma, Texas, Arkansas and Kansas. Managed five separate brand divisions under 66 Federal Credit Union, including KU Credit Union, ConocoPhillips Credit Union, Tyson Credit Union (now 66 FCU – Northwest Arkansas), and Community Source Credit Union. Utilized strategic marketing planning, product development and management, branding, positioning, public/media relations and social media expertise. Developed and implemented award winning promotions, programs, and services oriented toward member acquisition, satisfaction, and retention. Created liaisons with local and regional business and media contacts to develop partnerships and affinities for the benefit of all organizations.

  • Director of Marketing/Communications

    Mill Creek Companies

    (Retail industry)

    September 2003 — May 2006 (2 years 9 months)

    Managed an in-house marketing department for 31 company locations throughout Oklahoma, Texas and Kansas, consisting mainly of lumber and supply, home improvements, kitchens, granite and carpet & tile divisions. Managed departmental advertising budgets including all media buys, as well as negotiating and filing co-op moneys. Redesigned and launched new company web site at www.millcreeklumber.com. Graphically designed and copy wrote all collateral materials. Wrote and produced all scripts and TV commercials. Implemented media mix that included TV, newspaper, magazine, direct mail, outdoor, and all other collateral material. Statistically analyzed each company location for the most effective marketing strategy. Maintained strategic relationships with media in each location’s community for ongoing comprehensive company public relations campaigns.

  • Marketing Coordinator

    Tulsa Health Department (Oklahoma State Department of Health)

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    May 2001 — September 2003 (2 years 5 months)

Creative concepting and design for all radio and TV commercials, brochures, documents, and presentations; most of which are still used to date. Coordinated all media buys, press conferences, and press releases with local and national media. Compiled post buy analysis and coordinated marketing research through focus groups and targeted surveys.

    (Non-Profit; Education Management industry)
