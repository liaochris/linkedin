Jennifer (Jenny) Tucker’s Summary

Jennifer Tucker (Consulting Director, OKA) is an experienced group facilitator and trainer, management consultant, and project manager. Past and current work includes facilitating technical and scientific work groups, conducting leadership development and teambuilding workshops, leading organizational assessments, and managing public outreach and communication projects.

Jenny holds a B.A in Environmental Sciences, an M.S. in Management, and a Ph.D. in Science and Technology Studies. She is the author of the booklet "Introduction to Type and Project Management," part of the Introduction to Type Series from CPP, Inc, and has been a Certified Project Management Professional (PMP) since 2005.

Jennifer (Jenny) Tucker’s Specialties:

Organization development, workgroup facilitation, leadership development and training, teambuilding workshops, management consulting, strategic planning, project management

Qualified administrator of the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), the Strength Deployment Inventory (SDI), the Leadership Spectrum Profile (LSP), the Work Environment Scales (WES), Reversal Theory / Apter Motivational Styles Profile (AMSP), and FIRO-B.
