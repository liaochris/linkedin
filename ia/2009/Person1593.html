Abdullah Al-Yami MBus, BSc’s Summary

I am a Saudi national with strong analytical skills, data-literate, strategic thinker, proactive and ambitious individual, with almost 6 years of diversified expertise in consulting for major conglomerates on key areas of market dynamics, pricing, product development, CRM, market penetration and service optimization. I am aiming at broadening my skills and stretching my intellect to spot insights and make winning and influential business recommendations that have a last impact on the business.

My credentials have been built in a strategically focused career preceded by positions of increasing responsibility in multinational consulting and professional services companies. By combining deep strategic and functional expertise with powerful intellectual capital, I have been able to complete a range of compelling assignments with global companies operating in different industries such as Procter & Gamble, Nestle, Coca-Cola, Abbott Laboratories, Sony, Commonwealth Bank of Australia, Holden Corp, Zaaffran Foodservice Co, in which I developed marketing strategies, created local and international marketing plans, designed and launched products into new markets, wrote business plans, managed global marketing programs and advised on various customer acquisition, development, and retention strategies.

I have a demonstrable record of creating innovative business solutions, on time, to very exacting standards. I have a history of achieving tangible business results. My core skills lie in effectively leading and operating with multicultural business teams, delivering and running highly successful marketing programs, operations and business from ‘grass roots beginnings’. I have strong knowledge in the areas of business analysis, market analysis & project feasibility studies, competitive international marketing strategies & tactics, direct & database marketing, customer relationship management, integrated marketing communications and new product development.
