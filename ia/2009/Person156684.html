Matt Sharples’s Summary

Co-Founder and current majority owner of TriCom Technical Services in Overland Park, Kansas. TriCom is a $12M Information Technology staff augmentation firm with over 70 consultants and a multitude of corporate clients. Currently serve the greater Kansas City metro area but also have reach into other major metropolitan areas within the midwest.

Currently exploring the opportunity to venture into I.T. managment consulting as an expanded service to our current staff augmentation model.
