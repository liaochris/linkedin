Bart Brejcha’s Summary

My main passion is learning. Whether that is helping empower Product Designers or Engineers working with industrial designers through the technology training organization called Design Engine in Chicago or electronic technicians learn RSD & Pro/CABLE, its incredibly important to give others the tools to succeed. Success especially in today's financially volatile and competitive market means to be the best. For more information on our unparalleled advanced training techniques and workshops in Pro/SURFACE, ISDX and Pro/ENGINEER Wildfire please visit our client list and press pages:
http://www.proetools.com/press.htm

Our goals for each student that walks through the doors of Design Engine:

1. To provide a real world approach to problem solving based off of the extensive experience of the instructor, which
ultimately gives them the tools needed for getting the job done quickly and correctly.
2. To provide highly efficient and effective training methods in a professional environment.
3. To engage and excite the student about the skills and techniques they’ve learned so they can continue to improve,
cultivate and grow in their specialized field of expertise.
