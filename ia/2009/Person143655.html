Lori Martinek’s Summary

Lori Martinek’s experience (agency/client/media) has made her a sought-after branding strategist. As the owner of Presentation Plus (www.pplusonline.com), a national marketing and PR firm, Martinek gets her clients noticed, online and off.

Martinek is an accomplished speaker, consultant and coach to CEOs, officials and political candidates. She is a former president, board member and consultant to chambers of commerce and other non-profits.

Her economic development resume includes projects at the state, regional, county and municipal levels.

Martinek began her career at Foote, Cone & Belding Worldwide in Chicago, where she worked on accounts including S. C. Johnson and Coors. As a national marketing manager for Wickes Lumber, Martinek developed innovative programs for Owens Corning, Black and Decker and Kohler.

Her publishing credits include articles for national publications and bylines in the Chicago Tribune, the Wisconsin State Journal and other papers. Martinek’s first book, Be The Bulb!, was published in March 2009 and is available on Amazon.com.

Martinek presented Creating A Winning Online Brand with GoDaddy CEO Bob Parsons as part of the NFL's Super Bowl VXII Playbook for Small Business. She travels the country to speak to audiences on branding, online marketing, social networking, entrepreneurship and economic development marketing. She is a champion of women, minority and Veteran-owned businesses and a mentor to aspiring entrepreneurs. Her other appearances have included the Veterans Small Business Conference, the ASBDC Conference in Chicago, the Governor's Regional Development Conference, WomenVenture in Minneapolis and the IEDC's Leadership Summit. Martinek presented at the Women Entrepreneurs Bootcamp in Scottsdale in February. Next up: The Phoenix Chamber's Back to Business Series on May 7 ... followed by the Lake Land College social media conference in Illinois on May 20 ... and Good Morning East Valley on June 12.

Lori Martinek’s Specialties:

Brand development and strategy; strategic planning, exceptional design and marketing collateral development; community marketing and economic development outreach; new business development; digital consulting and reputation management; personal and professional branding strategies that work. Really. Ask me about it.

Lori is the author of Be the Bulb!, which introduces the concept of Attraction Marketing for entrepreneurs, growing businesses and career-minded professionals.
