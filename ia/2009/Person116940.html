John Drew’s Summary

In my role as President/CEO of Professional Image Photography, a full service multi-media communications company based out of Rockville Maryland, I am truly blessed by God to draw from a wealth of knowledge and experience in the field of Professional “Event” Photography for weddings and special events.

As a widely recognized Master Photographer and Artist among my peers, I’m very appreciative for a career that has been and continues to be a journey that has afforded me travel experiences with many professional and personal accomplishments along the way. This success comes in part from having had great mentorship’s with local and leading master photographers, to advanced training in the areas of Film/Digital photography and Photoshop techniques.

As a highly regarded and accomplished Mentor, Instructor and Speaker/Presenter, I welcome opportunities to share this knowledge and experience with individuals, organizations, associations and groups.

This journey has spanned over 25 wonderful years with images published in newsletters, annual reports, societal magazines, ad promotions. This voyage has taken me into Five Star Luxury Hotels and Resorts, Sprawling Country Clubs, Museums, The John F. Kennedy Center for The Performing Arts (DC), The Joseph Meyerhoff Symphony Hall (Balt., Md.), The Gimble Center for The Performing Arts (Phila. Penn.), Temples/Synagogues and countless family estates and other unique venues of beautiful architecture and design. Included this incredible journey is the privilege of photographing several current and past US Presidents, Ambassadors, National and World Leaders, Heads of State, Entertainment/ Athletic Personalities, TV Celebrities, Financiers and many notable business and political leaders from around the Globe.

There are no limits to traveling for assignments and special events throughout the world having photographed many high profile events and personalities.

John Drew’s Specialties:

Master photographer for commercial, contruction, corporate and special events along with strong leadership and management skill sets.
