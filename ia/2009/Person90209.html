Groove Heads Entertainment’s Summary

At Groove Heads we don’t just let you party like a Rock Star- we make your guest instant Music Video Stars! Our fresh fun interactive green screen entertainment brings people together by making them on-the-spot music video stars and at the same time promotes your company for years after the event.

Perfect for all types of events and celebrations our groovy hosts superimpose your guests’ heads on professional dancers’ bodies in music videos with the original artist’s music. Don’t worry about the talent part- we have that covered because participants don't have to sing or even know the song - just bounce their head to the music and they got their Groove Head on!

Groove Heads goes beyond being a standard entertainment company by customizing each souvenir show DVD with your corporate brand or promotional message printed on it. Providing you with a long term advertisement that guest will keep and show their friends for years to come.

We also provides full audio, video, lighting and staging production and photography services for concerts and events. We also have a recording studio for your mixing and mastering projects. Be it live or studio audio production we bring the right mix to corporate events, concerts, live broadcasts and social festivities.
