Jane Veldhoven, CPO®’s Summary

Jane is a Certifed Professional Organizer® specializing in working with business owners, senior managers and their teams and other busy professionals helping them to improve their productivity, efficiency and effectiveness and regain a sense of control in their life. Jane enjoys the challenge of teaching her clients how to manage e-mail more effectively, clear the piles of paper from their desk, and learn to integrate planning skills into their workday to ensure success! With a background in sales and management as well as training and coaching plus studies in records management and much more, this business is a perfect fit for Jane and she loves helping other busy people improve their organizing skills each and every day. Her passion is facilitating training sessions for teams and business owners alike.

Jane Veldhoven, CPO®’s Specialties:

Organizing, time management, productivity improvement, information management systems analysis and setup, office design, e-mail management, products for sale.
