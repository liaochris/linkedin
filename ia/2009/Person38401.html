Carol McClelland’s Summary

I have two passions: Helping people discover work they love and finding ways to improve the state of the environment. In 2007 I realized the time was right to combine my passions in my own work.

Now I'm the Founder and Executive Director of Green Career Central, a subscription-based membership site for motivated professionals who are ready to find a green career that fully expresses their interests, skills, and values. On the site we provide distilled and organized green career resources, how-to articles about networking and job search strategies that are suited for the green economy, and a community of green career seekers who share a passion for the planet.

In addition we provide access to green career resources and training for career counselors who support green career seekers.

Carol McClelland’s Specialties:

Helping people discover their green niche, making sense of the green economy, identifying green opportunities and innovations, training career counselors to bring green career expertise to their students or clients.
