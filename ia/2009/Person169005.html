Mark Prior’s Summary

DON'T PANIC

Mark's goal is to continue to develop his expertise in designing backbone networks by trying new techniques and troubleshoot them when things don't perform as expected.

Mark attended Cisco's first public training course in 1989 when the University of Adelaide purchased one of the first AGS routers in Australia and been using Cisco routers ever since.

While at AARNet he also started using Juniper routers and has been working through the Juniper certification programme, as it seemed more relevant than Cisco's Routing and Switching. Currently he has a JNCIP-M (#00377).

Mark Prior’s Specialties:

Backbone engineering, especially BGP routing and address management.
