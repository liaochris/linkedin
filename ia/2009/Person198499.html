Rick Docksai’s Summary

I am a diligent media professional with Master’s-Degree training in the research, reporting, and writing skills vital to the success of an employer’s online and print news services.

I am also a freelance writer who has crafted reports, abstracts, and articles for businesses, think tanks, and publications across the Washington metropolitan area. Contact me to help you and your organization generate winning print or Web-based content.

Rick Docksai’s Specialties:

My news stories are accurate, eye-catching, and always faithful to deadlines. I can cover practically any beat, though I specialize in government, health, and science and technology news.

In addition, I have extensive proofreading experience and can prepare a variety of documents in accordance with the Chicago Style or Associated Press writing formats.
