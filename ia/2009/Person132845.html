Kirk Roberts’s Summary

I am an independent website architect and Flash specialist with over eight years of experience designing and developing award-winning online experiences.

As a website architect I'm in the feel good business. I work with small business owners to craft a site they are proud of, and that helps give them the confidence to succeed. Whether the situation calls for a few important tweaks or a completely new design and deployment I am an internet-savvy ally that will make your site the best it can be without breaking your budget. Areas of assistance include brand definition, site architecture, usability and legibility, content selection and editing, domain-naming, search engine optimization and submission, code clean-up, and others depending on the project. Find out more at http://www.eyeandbrain.com/

As a Flash specialist I contract with designers to sharpen and implement their ideas using advanced Flash animation and ActionScripting. A longtime usability advocate and preacher of responsible Flash use, I strive to blend fluidity and wow-factor with pragmatic concerns such as loading and updating. My skills as a designer enable me to extrapolate comps with style and precision. See work samples and read more at http://flash.eyeandbrain.com/

Kirk Roberts’s Specialties:

Website consultation, website design, online branding, website architecture, Flash animation, Flash ActionScript, XHTML, CSS, Flash development, content development, user experience, usability, web design, and bits and pieces of other coding languages mixed together and baked at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes
