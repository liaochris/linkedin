Asavari Honavar’s Summary

Brand Strategist and Design Consultant for an eclectic mix of design needs, stretching from FMCG branding to heritage conservation design.... the full spectrum

We view design from a holistic approach, seeking to use colour, form, medium and text to communicate the many layered messages that a client needs to send out to his / her audiences, but most of all we seek to educate the client into believing that all design has an underlying intuitive logic that can work wonders for their brand, product or service. We work closely with each client to understand their corporate ethos, and strive to express the core values in our communication, subtly yet effectively.

Indian audiences are well-known to respond to colours and visual stimuli far better than to verbal messages. At Studio Ananya, we work with this basic premise, making each piece of communication a memorable visual and kinaesthetic experience for the specified target audiences.
