Tom Jones’s Summary

Starting my career "agency-side" within the advertising industry, I created an incredibly wide variety of award-winning, successful, profitable, creative marketing pieces from initial concept and copy through production and placement. Accounts I worked on have run the gamut from fast food to fast cars, medicine to malpractice attorneys and resort hotels to consumer loans of the last resort.

Following some 10 years of award-winning experience at the area's largest advertising agencies and 10 years at my own firm, I then went “client-side”—first as a marketing director within the retirement industry at the area's largest, non-profit assisted living facility and then at its newest and largest Continuing Care Retirement Community (CCRC). During that time, I completed my MBA in Marketing Management even as I honed my skills at relationship marketing and consultative selling—leading both of the companies I’ve worked for to new records in sales and success.

In short, it adds up to solid, skilled marketing experience that can profit any business in any industry. That means yours!

I invite you to follow the links to my online portfolio, pull up some of the case studies/success stories I have included below and read the recommendations of my peers. Then, I'd strongly suggest you contact me today to discuss how I can work with you to help your company attain its greatest possible profit and success.

Tom Jones’s Specialties:

Outstanding creative communication skills in all media, as well as public presentation, networking and social media skills. Think of me as an embedded entrepreneur—a highly motivated self-starter and team-player experienced in the management of a marketing staff.

Computer skills I’ve picked up along the way include Microsoft Suite, Adobe Photoshop, Acrobat, Quark XPress, CRM (REPS) and social media software. I can also fashion a phenomenal potassium gum bichromate photographic print.
