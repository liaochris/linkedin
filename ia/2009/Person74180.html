Eric Ellington’s Summary

Eric Ellington is a post-production specialist from Asheville, North Carolina, and a recent graduate from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with majors from the School of Journalism and Mass Communication and the Communication Studies department. He has served as a video editor, sound mixer, director of photography, producer, and television station manager. Ellington is proficient in Avid Media Composer, Final Cut Pro, Soundtrack Pro, Compressor, Logic Pro, DVD Studio Pro, and Adobe After-Effects. His past work ranges from narratives to music videos to corporate video.

Eric Ellington’s Specialties:

Post-production consultation, Standard Definition and High Definition video capture, nonlinear digital editing, compositing and motion graphics, advanced color correction, audio mixing and mastering, audio and video restoration, format conversions, audio conform, compressing for all delivery formats, and advanced DVD authoring.
