Chris Davis, LEED® AP’s Summary

I am a recognized entrepreneur with successful ventures in commercial sustainability, including Green Lease™, The Little Green Book™ and The Green Challenges™. In 2008, I authored “The Little Green Book™” as the resource for organizational sustainability, combining best practices in environmental stewardship and commitment to the LEED® for Commercial Interiors Rating System. I have combined my advanced certification in green building with a focused education in Urban Studies from the Edward J. Bloustein School of Planning and Public Policy at Rutgers University to develop a unique perspective on organizational sustainability, leading to the creation of The Green Challenges™.

    (Environmental Services industry)
