Kalim Khan’s Summary

Kalim Khan is a Certified Management Consultant and Chartered Mechanical Engineer with over 12 years post MBA professional consulting experience.

Kalim's private sector clients whilst at IBM included Royal Bank of Scotland, Ferrari, Lego, Continental Tyres - focusing on benchmarking and improving innovation capabilities.

While in the public sector Kalim's experiences includes Liverpool City Council as Lead Change Manager, Warwick University as co-founder of the National B2B Centre and E-Business Programme Manager for Advantage West Midlands - the regional development agency for the West Midlands.

Kalim Khan’s Specialties:

Programme definition and management, ICT & e-business policy development and project evaluation, change management and issue based consulting

Kalim Khan’s Experience

  • Senior Principal - Value Engineering

    SAP UK Ltd

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Computer Software industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    Companies are under increasing pressure to justify and maximize the value of their IT investment. Over 83% of technology projects still have no formal business case. According to Gartner, 80% of IT projects never realized the expected benefits. In fact, very few companies have either the skills or research and benchmarks necessary to develop comprehensive business cases and plans for value realization.

    Value Engineering is SAP’s answer to this challenge. VE is SAP’s end-to-end value selling and value implementation process. With over 1000 business cases completed to date, we have created the leading repository of key performance indicators, typical benefits and benchmarks. We capture customer benefits from our own engagements as well as analyst studies. In addition, we invite customers to participate in benchmarking studies to make our database as rich and credible as possible.
