Greg Pins’s Summary

Mr. Pins has over 20 years of experience in consulting, sales and marketing and operations in consumer products, retail, telecom, food and beverage and distribution businesses. He specializes in developing sales and operational strategies that increase market penetration and customer service while controlling supply chain and inventory impacts. Mr. Pins is a business process expert who has redefined workgroups and organizations in concert with major systems implementations. He has experience taking significant costs out of businesses, reorganizing sales forces and customer service organizations for optimal growth, and reducing working capital by focusing on those drivers that create excess inventory and excess receivables and bad debt.

Client list: Shell, Jiffy Lube, Royal Ahold, Georgia-Pacific, Exel (DHL), Philips, Interface, AT&T, BellSouth, Sprint-Nextel, US ARMY, Broadjump (Motive), Deluxe Checks, Vignette, Pervasive Software, Avaya, Dun & Bradstreet

Greg Pins’s Specialties:

Process redesign, organizational redesign, sales force effectiveness, growth strategies, balanced scorecards and management metric creation, customer segmentation, channel development, customer care and loyalty, customer profitability, transportation and warehouse analyses, SKU rationalization, procurement and strategic sourcing.
