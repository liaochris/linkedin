Mark Powney’s Summary

I have 12 years solid experience within marketing, publishing and the recruitment industry in general and have lived and breathed the development of the digital marketplace throughout this period.

I consider digital publishing, digital media development, recruitment advertising (all media), digital video production, creative design and marketing and more recently social media development as my core knowledge/experience.

In recent months I have taken numerous short term consultancy roles and work with a variety of technology based partners within the field of digital recruitment.
