Rich McCombs’s Summary

Prior to graduating from the University of Illinois at Chicago, with BS in Business Management and Finance, Rich started his career toward the end of the dotcom bust of the early 2000’s. He quickly established himself as a high-motivated, results-driven sales professional.

Rich has experience in selling professional services, marketing services, and a variety of technology products and services, including eCommerce, Software-as-a-Service, Content Management, IT Governance. He bring a dynamic and energetic personality to each customer interaction.

During his consulting career, Rich managed software partnerships with Microsoft, Blue Martini, Vignette, Day, Selectica, and Oracle. Rich leveraged his experience and leadership skills to build an Inside Sales Team of 20. He also generated over 20+ million dollars in revenue at a Chicago based IT consulting firm.

Today, Rich’s energy and vision is focused on the marketing, sales, customer retention and growth of his agency’s SaaS content management and eCommerce platform.
