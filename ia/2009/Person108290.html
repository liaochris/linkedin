Jeff Orr’s Summary

I founded ORR Technology in 2006 after 17 years of corporate and technology marketing. As an independent consultant and market analyst, I have been responsible for wireless technology assessment at The Walt Disney Company and authored market research reports for Maravedis and ABI Research. In addition to supporting customer projects, I write about emerging technology markets for online publishers such as Light Reading and WiMax.com. Two wireless communications industry conferences have benefited from my event architecture design and speaker recruitment during 2008.

I enjoy sharing my knowledge and stories with others. One way that I accomplish this is through Mobile Broadband News, an online portal about technologies that are enabling Internet access anytime, anywhere (EV-DO, HSPA, LTE, UMB, WiMAX, WiBro and Mobile TV). More than 700 articles are online and in 2008, I started the Mobile Broadband News Podcast -- a weekly recap of the top industry stories.

My marketing services include:

Marketing & Sales Alignment
Technology-Centric to Market-Driven Transition
Competitive Intelligence / Market Research
Portfolio Triage
Product Marketing and Management Coaching
Campaign/Event planning
Keynote/Guest Speaker
Webinar hosting, design and moderating
