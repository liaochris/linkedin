Lisa Williams’s Summary

If you need the healing and rejuvenation that can only come from a skilled compassionate, professional massage, you're at the right place.

Why Transformations? Quality, continuity and value. Lisa has been providing quality bodywork to her massage therapy and occupational therapy clients for more than two decades. When you return, you'll enjoy the continuity of seeing the same therapist—someone who knows your massage needs. And you'll value knowing that Transformations doesn't give you 45 or 50 minutes of massage and call it an "hour." When you book time, you're booking time on the table.

Our clients come to Transformations Massage when they suffer from chronic pain or the maladies of a stressful life. They also turn to us when they want to reward themselves or loved ones just for being who they are. Transformations offers the area's most therapeutic massage at reasonable rates in a safe, relaxing environment. No noise, no bustle, just transformation.

Situated in Knoxville's Bearden area at 18 Forest Court, Transformations Massage gives you an out-of-the-way feeling in an easy-to-reach location. Lisa Williams has made Transformations Knoxville's easiest massage experience as well as its most rewarding. Book massages, pay for them, get gift certificates, and peruse specials, all from the convenience of your computer. Or give Lisa a call and talk to her personally.
