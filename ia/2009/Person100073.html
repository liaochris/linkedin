Filippo Rosati’s Summary

Italian native speaker. Reliable, accurate and quality-oriented. Editing, proofreading and writing skills.
Sworn translator. Graduated with a degree in Translation (NL-DE-EN->IT) from the University of Trieste (Italy).
Broad experience as a free-lance translator (since 1990). Winner of the Literary Translation Prize "Premio Merano-Europa 1999".
Level III Certificate in Hungarian awarded by the Summer University of Debrecen (Hungary)
Acknowledged by the District Court of The Hague (The Netherlands). Official translator for the Italian Consulate of Amsterdam (The Netherlands).
Also available for voice-overs.
