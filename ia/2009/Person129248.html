Kelli Brown’s Summary

Since making aliyah in July 2007, my career has taken a few shifts. My retraining in technical writing allowed me to work as a technical writer at WritePoint Ltd. while indulging my creative side doing web development.

Recently, I left the fabulous team at WritePoint to build what I hope will be a successful career doing work I truly love: graphic design and all facets of Web development for companies, individuals and non-profit organizations. Finally, I hope to share my knowledge with clients to help them do more for themselves, whether it's social media marketing or magazine publishing. I am a proponent of social media marketing and can help companies and individuals implement it on whatever scale they choose to embrace.

My areas of expertise include typography, SEO, marketing and product outreach using non-traditional methods. I have extensive project management experience and work well as a leader or part of the team.

While in journalism, I had several different positions in a few different newsrooms across the U.S. My background has covered print design using QuarkXPress and Adobe CS. In web development, I learned open-source programming to handle site redesigns from the ground up. I'm comfy on Mac and PC platforms.

Kelli Brown’s Specialties:

My Web specialties are LAMP development, Joomla! development and template design, WordPress template design and Yahoo! widget development. I'm quite good with HTML, XML, Perl, PHP, MySQL, CSS and JavaScript.
I use Adobe CS for design and have extensive knowledge of typography and its effect on readability and usability.
