Douglas Turet’s Summary

At a fairly young age, it became apparent to me that I marched to the beat of a different drummer... so I became one!

In the decades since then, I've done my utmost to keep my eyes, ears, mind and spirit wide open, regardless of challenges, and have managed to accomplish far more than the expectations of those I was once told to look up to would ever have indicated.

My current goals are to continue to be the father I never had, to set as many wheels of positive change in motion as possible, and to help as many people as I possibly can to reach up and beyond the limitations they've been trained to believe constrain and define them.
