Adam May’s Summary

Adam has been involved with Film and TV production for the past 8 years, most
recently as Business Development Producer for Bigger Pictures, a production
company headed up by former C4 commissioning editor Darren Bender.
Adam has produced live entertainment programming for UK broadcasters
including Channel Five, ITV and Turner Broadcasting. He is also director of
Underline Films, and maintains strong network connections within film financing
and distribution. Previous short films produced by Adam have screened at key
international film festivals, including Intefilme in Berlin, The Raindance Festival in
London, and with ‘Bird in the Wire’, at the 2001 Cannes Film Festival. Adam
maintains close attention to trends and movements within the global film industry
and the emerging 3-D marketplace.
