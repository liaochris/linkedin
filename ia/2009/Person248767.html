Bill Taney’s Summary

I have managed engineering groups supporting high performance multiprotocol networks for 14 years. I have extensive experience in network design, operations and engineering. My primary hands on experience have been with Cisco, Juniper, F5 and Linux & Unix systems. Currently I manage the network for the premier trading EMS provider, Townsend Analytics. We process and disseminate market data from all of the worldwide markets as well as process real-time trades back to market at all of the worlds major exchanges 24x6. I have had previous experience as well in the time critical printing, publishing and creative industries.

Bill Taney’s Specialties:

Network Engineering, Operations & Design in mission critical real-time environments.
