Bryan Collings’s Summary

My Background:

My parents both own small business. One is an architecture firm, the other a mechanical engineering firm. I grew up talking about the ins and outs of running a business around the dinner table each evening.

I wrestled competitively from age 12 through age 18. My coach was a Navy champion. This experience was the principal driver behind my steadfast determination and will. Kickboxing is now a personal hobby.

I attended a technology "magnet" high school that specialized in making computer literacy and cutting-edge software applications a centerpiece of the curriculum. I was was the Technology Director for our student body and ranked 8th in my class upon graduation.

I worked construction and framed custom homes during the summers in high school.

I trained for and competed in an amateur boxing match for charity. Lost the fight, but learned a lot about myself. I plan on competing again soon.

I played classical piano for 8 years and have played guitar since the age of 12. I've been involved in creating artwork all my life, and I continue to draw and paint in my free time.

One of my biggest strengths is the balance of my analytical and creative skills. Some like to label people "right" or "left" brained, but you can label me "ambidextrous".
