Will Eagle’s Summary

I’m a senior level marketing professional with a proven track record in leading edge brand & marketing strategies for top tier brands, with experience in the technology and entertainment industries.

With The Much MTV Group:

Responsible for digital marketing strategy, implementation and evaluation for MuchMusic and MTV TV and web properties, to acquire, retain and convert new audience.

Expert level skill set in social media marketing, search engine marketing, search engine optimisation, display, email & database marketing, integration with traditional media.

Thorough and detailed approach to analysis of data, market research and ROI.

With The Virgin Group:

Senior Online and Creative Manager at Virgin Mobile (Canada), sent from the UK to be responsible for the branding, positioning, marketing and launch of Virgin Mobile in Canada on the web and within below the line marketing including direct and channel sales marketing, in store merchandising, product communications, customer relationship marketing, and events such as Virgin Festival.

Managed the creative department and set processes for cost saving efficiencies, managed a range of external agencies and suppliers.

Previous to that, I was the Site Manager at Virgin Management/ Virgin.com (UK), a team who acted as the global head office of the Virgin Group. Virgin.com is the company’s web portal.

My role encompassed web development, content, project management, marketing, and technical infrastructure for the 7 region portal site Virgin.com.

Co-ordinated Virgin Group marketing campaigns, digital marketing initiatives, and best practices within the worldwide marketing teams, managed Group IT initiatives, and a $2m budget.

Will Eagle’s Specialties:

Strategy and implementation of marketing and digital marketing (with experience in almost all forms of marketing including print, in store, out of home, radio, ambient, with a concentration in digital marketing.)
