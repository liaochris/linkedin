John Matthews’s Summary

I am the Founder and President of Comscient Group.a fact-based research and management advisory services firm.

Comscient helps clients:

* make informed and insightful technology, sourcing, ROI and CAPEX decisions.

* to stay abreast of, gain insight into, and anticipate the impact of emerging technologies.

* plan and perform technology and market due diligence when investing in a technology based venture or when buying a technology based product or service.

* bring innovation and technology to market.guiding them step by step through the process.

* develop and implement systems and best practices for managing knowledge that fuels customer insight and innovation.

* monitor and manage their online presence and reputation.

* obtain customer and competitive insight from online research and monitoring.

I have worked in technology research, social media, collaboration, CRM, knowledge management, and decision support systems for over two decades.

In the 1980s I was an affiliate of the Xerox Palo Alto Research Center (PARC) working on expert systems, knowledge management, and social media and collaboration projects.

I was the software and decision support systems consultant on the team that created the ground breaking SIV (Structured Investment Vehicle) structured finance product in 1988.

I have been a technology industry specialist, a technology analyst/researcher and consultant to the consultants (McKinsey, Boston Consulting Group, Accenture, EDS,...etc), advised media, marketing, events, and publishing organizations about new and emerging technologies, and been involved in implementing and advising on CRM for 20+ years.

For 10 years I provided technology knowledge center and advisory services to a top-tier global management consulting firm. I assisted client engagement teams to gain insight into technologies, technology markets, best practices, and in finding, vetting, and selecting technology solutions.

John Matthews’s Specialties:

Marketing and Advertising, Media and Entertainment Sectors, Technology Sector, Mobile, Entrepreneurial Sector, Green/Clean Technology Sector, Strategic Research and Analysis, Business Planning, Software Selection and Acquisition, B2B Market Research, Futures Research, Technology Forecasting, Scenario Planning, Data Driven Decision Making, Technology Due Diligence, Commercial Due Diligence, Business Analysis, Competitive Intelligence, Management Education, and Decision Making Facilitation.
