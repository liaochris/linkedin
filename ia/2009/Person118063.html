John A Salagaj’s Summary

Global corporate development and marketing executive with eight years of cross-border experience leading teams in the successful execution of sales and marketing strategy on an international basis, bringing increased profit to the bottom line in the process.

Proficient in Spanish, I started my career in Latin America, managing market development and client relationships for a commercial lender in Panama. Since that time, I have, as a management consultant, lead new market entry and product/service development teams for such companies as Boeing, FedEx, and UPS, and managing sales and marketing support strategy at Merrill Lynch.

I am currently looking to leverage my experience to assist companies in the development of new markets and relationships on a global basis, while helping to expand, retool or develop new products and services in the process. Currently seeking full-time and contract assignments.
