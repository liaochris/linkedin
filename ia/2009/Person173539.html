Dan Waterman’s Summary

I am an instructor and CEO for NFSTI.com, the largest grassroots REO training, resource, and certification company in the nation. Our objective is to "Elevate the Standards of REO Professionals" through Education, Valuation, and Marketing. NFSTI offers national seminars featuring training derived from top-performing asset managers, default servicers and REO experts.

Prior to working with NFSTI I was a licensed REO Specialist in the state of Colorado. I now reside in upstate NY and spend a good part of my year traveling throughout the country. Past clients include but are not limited to: Wells Fargo, Countrywide, Wachovia, JP Morgan Chase, Lighthouse, Asset Link, IAS REO, and Fidelity National Asset Management Solutions.

NFSTI is now expanding its consultative services to include asset management and default servicing companies. We now offer a comprehensive asset manager training consultation to include knowledge of asset management from cradle-to-grave.

Dan Waterman’s Specialties:

Extensive knowledge in the design, infrastructure, marketing, and operation of an REO/BPO real estate brokerage. SEO expert in PPC campaigns and organic ranking.
