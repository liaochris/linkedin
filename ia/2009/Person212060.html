Scott Sutton, Ph.D.’s Summary

I have over 20 years of laboratory leadership experience in the microbiology arena of the pharmaceutical, medical device, cosmetics and personal products industries with extensive publications and presentations. Contamination control, laboratory management, training, GMP, testing methodologies and microbiology-related project management are areas of special interest. Clients have included startups, generics, established Fortune 500 companies, law firms and investment broker houses.
I also operate an information source on the internet, The Microbiology Network (http://www.microbiol.org) which provides services to microbiology-related user’s groups and supports the PMFList, a microbiology Email list (http://www.microbiol.org/pmflist.htm) and the PSDG Email list (pharmaceutical stability topics) (http://www.microbiol.org/psdglist.htm).
View a more complete resume at http://www.microbiol.org/consulting.htm.
