Chris Corner’s Summary

A professional systems engineer, systems and software architect and project manager, very experienced in both a wide variety of technical disciplines and in the customer facing role, and currently working as a consultant. A specialist in the specification, design, development, integration and test of tactical data link systems including multi-link, forwarding and gateway capabilities.

Chris Corner’s Specialties:

Requirements definition and analysis using Use Cases and Sequence Diagrams.
Object oriented analysis, design and development using UML.
Development of behavioural and data models in XML.
Analysis and definition of roadmaps covering customer business and technical aspirations.
Architectural Framework (inc. DoDAF / MoDAF) techniques.
Systems and software architecture design in a variety of environments.
Application of System Quality Attributes to the solution design.
