Laetitia Attali’s Summary

After graduating with joint honours in corporate law and communications, working as a Summer Associate at SASM&F in New-York (Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom) and in the marketing department at Dalloz-Sirey (publishing company), I chose to orient myself towards a career in Communications.

I created the role of PR for Computer Channel – a TV channel for IT professionals. Co-founder of Langages Virtuels (a start-up specialized in IT), I developed a liking for new technologies associated with marketing services and communications. In order to facilitate our international business development, I decided, along with my partners, to sell the structure to Genesys Conferencing Group. I then joined the corporate communication department to manage communication and support marketing over 17 countries for the Genesys Open Media subsidiary.

In 2002, I co-founded Awak’iT – a plurimedia agency. As consultancy and communications director, I made good use of my experience of communication and use of IT for our clients (EDF, Total, France Télécom, Alcatel, Groupe Caisse d'Epargne, Crédit Agricole...) thinking up new uses and innovative concepts.

We received a large number of awards : UJJEF 2002, 2003, 2005 – TOP COM 2004, 2005, 2006 – 3rd of Deloitte Technology Fast50 2005 France and 27th of Deloitte Technology Fast500 2005 EMEA.

I joined Ogilvy (WPP) in April 2007, as a Director for OgilvyOne to manage Nestlé France digital and CRM account work.

Since June 2008, I am driving Digital into OPR’s EAME network, coordinating support for country and client needs and providing training and new business support.
