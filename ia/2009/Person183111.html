Paul Heller’s Summary

I have a diverse foundation of experience from both the construction and architectural fields. I have worked on a variety of projects including commercial, institutional, municipal, educational, religious, and residential building types. With the diversity of my background, from having bid, built and managed the construction of buildings as well as the design of them I am able to bring a broad knowledge base to each aspect of the architectural design process.
