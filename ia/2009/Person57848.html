David J. Gee is Information Officer and CIO for Lilly USA ($12B sales) He is a member of Lilly USA and CIO staff.

Formally was CIO and Director IT Japan for Eli Lilly and Company. A member of Lilly Japan Executive Committee and the Senior Management Forum. He also held the position as Chief Privacy Officer for Lilly Japan. David was also the Chairman for Lilly IT Zone 3 Steering Committee incorporating Japan, Asia and Australia \ New Zealand.

Previously he served as CIO and Director IT for Lilly Asian Operations and prior to that as Director of Lilly’s Australian & NZ IT. David has had international expatriate assignments for the past ten years and has lived in Shanghai, Hong Kong and Kobe, Japan.

Born in Darwin, Australia, David graduated from Sydney University in Australia with an Economics degree in 1981 and a Masters of business administration degree from Macquarie University Business School in 1998. He has also completed Post-graduate Software Engineering Certificate at the University of Technology, Sydney.

David is a member of the Australian Computer Society and Institute of Management. In addition is a Six Sigma Green Belt. David serves on the HP Personal Systems and Oracle Siebel Advisory Board.
