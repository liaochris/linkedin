Jak Wonderly’s Summary

Photography of animals; horses, wildlife, and domestic. Travel Photography. I try to relay the unique character, beauty, and history and of each animal. My clients include magazine editors, ad agencies, business owners, animal rescue facilities, wildlife NGOs, and veterinarians. I am available for commercial shoots and editorial assignments, and offer stock photography, fine art prints, and portrait sessions. My background with national advertising campaigns and large design firms ensures that my clients are not just hiring an artist, but a seasoned professional who always delivers top quality work on time.

Jak Wonderly’s Specialties:

Digital SLR and medium format film photography. Adobe Lightroom, Bridge, and Photoshop (processing, retouching, and print preparation). Advertising, editorial, stock, portraits. Print production, project management, client relations, account supervision, marketing communications, process development. Design; graphic, layout, industrial, interactive.
