Donna Bailey-Taylor, CDME’s Summary

Executive Director of small rural county in Eastern North Carolina, responsible for marketing to all market segments to bring visitors to the county. The Johnston County Visitors Bureau has an annual budget of approximately $950,000 in tax revenues paid by visitors. The Visitors Bureau also manages four separate campaigns with an additional dedicated tax collection by Towns of Smithfield, Selma, Kenly, and Benson. JCVB is a quasi-governmental destination marketing organization established in 1987 to attract and serve visitors to Johnston County.
