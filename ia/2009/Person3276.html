Adam Vero’s Summary

I have worked for over 15 years in a variety of IT roles including management, systems administration, service delivery, programming and teaching. My experience includes major rollout projects for blue chip companies and IT management for a large legal practice dealing with technical strategy, project management for office moves and systems integration to support large mergers.

My technical expertise is in designing and building computer networks for businesses, installing server and desktop operating systems and supporting applications. I have a focus on Microsoft software and hold MCP and MOS certificates. As well as setting up systems, I write and deliver bespoke training courses. I also have broad experience of legal and compliance topics such as licensing and computer use policies. As such, I am well equipped to deal with all aspects of my clients' technical and business requirements.
