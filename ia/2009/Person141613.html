Lingjie Guan’s Experience

  • Founder

    NvWah, LLC

    (Cosmetics industry)

    January 2009 — Present (5 months)

    I have worked in biochemistry field for 7 years, and achieved a PhD. And I have been a woman who loves beautiful skin for my whole life. But as many others, I fall victim of massive marketing campaigns put on by cosmetic manufacturers and often buy products that are utterly disappointing.

    One day it hit me. Why don't I put what I know to work and help others, including myself, make informed choices when buying skin care products? That's why I founded NvWah.com.

    It's as simple as this, if you know what's in the bottle, you know what it will do. But cosmetic ingredients are difficult for average consumers to understand. Not for me though. Thanks to my background I can read the ingredient label with ease. After I analyze and evaluate the ingredients based on their safety and efficacy, I give an objective review and tell you what the product will REALLY do to your skin.

    We read food label to watch what goes into our belly. Why would we not want to do the same for our face?

    We say no to animal testing. Why would we say yes to being human guinea pigs?

    Beauty should not be a mystery. Get the Know.
