    (Consumer Services industry)

    1999 — Present (10 years)

    We breed our puppies from our own stock, striving for good temperaments and health. We meet and exceed all guidelines of the breed standard. Puppies are registered with American Kennel Club (AKC), Continental Kennel Club (CKC), and United Kennel Club International (UKCI) as well our puppies can be registered with a combination of any of the three. Our puppies are always current on their puppy shots and de-worming. Additionally, our puppies are micro-chipped at 6-weeks-old for permanent identification. We are USDA/APHIS licensed. We are one of only two licensed breeders in the State of South Carolina.

    We will happily provide you with your puppies paperwork the day you take your new baby home. We do our best to assist families in finding the right pet. We take extra measures to make sure that our puppies are well socialized and healthy before they go to their new homes.

    We do a lot of belly rubbing with our expecting mommies and handle and socialize our babies from birth. We usually have a variety of puppies available or on the way. We do offer a waiting list for puppies yet to be delivered. Once the litter has arrived we will be happy to contact you. If by chance, we don't have what you are looking for, we would be happy to refer you to another breeder.

    Our facilities are kept clean, sterile, and organized. We love each and every one of our adult dogs and puppies as a family member. The adult dogs and puppies over 9-weeks-old go outside to play everyday, weather permitting, and in bad weather, they are completely sheltered inside with heating and air. We have a list of references available upon request.

    We were inspected by USDA in July 2008 and by AKC in April 2008, and as always we met and exceeded all of their expectations.

    We invite you, to visit our facilities, meet our moms and dad as well as our puppies.

    Country Girl Kennel

    (Consumer Services industry)

    1999 — Present (10 years)

    We are a family owned and operated Dog Breeder Kennel, located in Hardeeville, SC, between Hilton Head, SC and Savannah, GA. We are licensed with the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS), which is regulated and monitored by the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA). Additionally, we hold ourselves to a higher standard. We provide a loving enriched environment for
    each and every mom, dad, and puppy at our facility.

    Our facilities are kept clean, sterile, and organized. We care for and love each and every one of our adult dogs and puppies as a member of our family. The adult dogs and puppies over 9-weeks-old go outside to play everyday, weather permitting, and in bad weather, they are completely sheltered inside with heating and air.

    We raise and breed quality healthy puppies. All of our adult dogs and puppies are always kept current on vaccines and de-wormings. All puppies are micro-chipped for permanent identification, before going to their new homes. Our puppies are double registered with Continental Kennel Club (CKC) and Universal Kennel Club International (UKCI) and many are triple registered to include American Kennel Club (AKC).

    Shipping is available with Delta Airlines for an additional fee, which includes their crate. If you are purchasing two puppies that can be crated together, you will only be charged one shipping fee.

    You can contact us via email or you can call us at (843) 258-PUPS (7877). Debbie, Keith, or Ashleigh can help with any questions you may have on availability of breeds, puppies for sale, upcoming deliveries or general concerns.

    We believe that not only does the customer pick the puppy, but the puppy picks you!
