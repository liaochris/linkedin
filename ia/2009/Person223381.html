Stephen Moran’s Summary

Special Events Coordinator accountable for the development, planning, and execution of all fund-raising events, including all promotional activities, logistics, sponsorships, and budgeting. Interacted with the City of Detroit Planning Committee and the City of Detroit Tactical Operations in the planning, design, and management of events and associated geography. Maintained communication with Public Relations Firms and local media to coordinate major event advertising.

Owner and designer of a web development company that creates and designs email templates, websites, brochures, PowerPoint projects, and ads. Extensive experience in marketing research, project negotiations, invoice, billing, collections, and accounting.

As Event Coordinator of Heat the Streets and Walk for Warmth, I was in charge of the planning, coordination, and execution of this charitable event that raises awareness of and additional funds for low-income citizens of Detroit who are struggling to cover their increasing utility costs. Accountable for the training, direction, and supervision of appointed teams to accomplish goals set by the project timeline. Brought in over $180,000 in donations, recruited over 1600 participants and 200 volunteers within the first two years of operation.
