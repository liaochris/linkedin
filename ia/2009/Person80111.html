Michael Kennedy’s Summary

As an accomplished Senior IT Manager, with 35 years of experience, I have transformed my knowledge and abilities into providing professional IT Consulting through my corporation " e-Kapacity, Inc". In this role, I have conducted end-to-end infrastructure capacity assessments and evaluations of innovative IT products/services for improved business delivery and effectiveness. I have also developed and implemented IT policies for global organizations pertaining to escalation procedures and root cause analysis, security procedures, configuration audits, change control, implementations, and rollout procedures. In my capacity as an Infrastructure manager at JPMC and Citibank, I have successfully dealt with data center management, enterprise applications, designed enterprise networks, network management systems, and worked with credit card call centers to improve overall operations, production and availability. In my capacity as a Performance Engineering manager at JPMC, I am intimately familiar with network availability, server availability, and application availability.

I seek challenging opportunities as an IT Consultant for companies concerned with the ability of their IT infrastructures to deliver critical enterprise applications. My goal has been to develop my own IT Services business, where I can continue to leverage my deep experience to provide expert IT professional services to my Clients. I have been a highly effective and innovative IT Manager in the past and my experience at JP Morgan has been one of consulting to business leaders for the past 10 years.
Finally, I have realized a personal goal in developing e-Kapacity, Inc. allowing me to utilize my unique expertise in delivering IT services to medium to Large businesses within the New York Metro area, the US, or abroad.

Michael Kennedy’s Specialties:

1) Consultant that can work with SMEs to determine capacity and performance constraints within the IT infrastructure, and make precision recommendations for enough capacity and performance to meet traffic spikes, mergers, and acquisistions. 2) Ability to review and streamline IT policies and procedures to optimize overall effectiveness. 3) Vendor analysis of IT solutions through RFP,RFI processes. 4) Problem/root cause analysis, recommendations to avoid future outages and/or degradations.
