Jerry Hempstead, DLP’s Summary

Each morning I got up, took a shower, put on my armor and went to go do battle with the dragon.
I prospected for shippers
I qualified them
I parried with the pricing dept for a saleable value proposition
I wrote and presented proposals
I negotiated with the client
I implemented
We picked things up
We transported them to destination
We delivered them
We tracked them
We billed them
We applied cash to them when people paid
Not rocket science
Along the way I was party to the very first national account contract in the air express industry with GE in 1979
I was part of the first e prom based automated customer premise shipping device called Libra
I was party to setting up the first EDI customer billing exchange with Citicorp
I was part of the development of the first pre purchased air express shipping products.
I helped develop the first hybrid express carrier/ USPS delivery network
In 2007 I was invited to China by the chairman of DTW Group, the then agent for FedEx in China to help design, plan and develop a domestic hub and spoke ground transportation network linking all of the provincial capitols in China.
And mostly spent my career acquiring and keeping customers by delivering servant based value each and every day
