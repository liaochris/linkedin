Bruce M Webster’s Summary

Webster a graduate of The Art Institute of New York city has been cooking for over ten years and has added flair and style to his diverserse skills and creativity.

Carribean Cuisine, Regional American Cuisine are merely a scratch of the surface to describe his style. He likes to call it "Internationally influenced".

Recently Chef has been providing off site catering services, and has served as Chef for parties as small as a private dinner for two to as many as two hundred fifty people. Residents of the Hamptons, The Rockaways, New Jersey, Baltimore Maryland and all boroughs of NYC have experienced and appreciated the professional services and quality skills provided by Chef Bruce M Webster.
Bruce is also an American Culinary Federation medal recipient sponsored by the National Restaurant Association. He is also certified by New York State Department for Food Handing and Food Safety.

Bruce M Webster’s Specialties:

Caribbean cuisine, specific to Belize to include Curries, Mexican and West Indian influenced dishes, as well as Regional American Cuisine.
