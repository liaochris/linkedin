Theresa Leary’s Summary

I help you to explore worldwide learning opportunities to reach your educational goals. Working on the phone or in person I act as a guide, interpreter and facilitator for the entire college selection and application process.

During 20 years in the field of international higher education I have counseled students and families to evaluate their options and make sound decisions. I draw from knowledge gained by working inside admission offices in the USA and undergraduate and MBA programs abroad. I speak French and have worked in and traveled to over 35 countries in Europe, Asia and the Middle East.

Theresa Leary’s Specialties:

Dedicated to providing the highest quality coaching service, I wish to increase my client base to serve:
~Corporations who relocate families and want a successful placement
~Global Relocation Agencies who need experts in educational consulting who are sensitive to cultural issues
~International Schools who need a dedicated counselor for their global student body
