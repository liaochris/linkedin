David Perry’s Summary

I'm helping people connect, so feel free to add me as a friend. (dp@dperry.com) I'm now linked to over 5,300 people here (providing over 14.5M connections.)

David Perry was the President and founder of Shiny Entertainment, Inc.for 12+ years, he's now a well known 26-year Video Game Industry veteran.

During that time, Perry has developed or programmed over 100 games across 29 video game platforms. All told, Perry's games (including #1 Hits like The Terminator, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Disney's Aladdin & Warner’s Matrix project) have totaled an estimated billion dollars in retail sales.

Perry sits on the Advisory Board of the Game Developers Conference, Indiecade, VGEXPO, and has spoken at TED, E3, Hollywood and Games Summit, CGDC, MIT, USC, UCI, UCLA, QUB, Montreal Game Summit, Digital Hollywood, What Teens Want etc.)

Today Perry is:
(1) The CCO / Co-Founder of the new Acclaim Games, Inc. (Directing many MMORPG games, Social Network Games & Casual Titles. All based on the 'free-to-play' game model, supported by in-game advertising and micro-transactions via virtual item sales).
(2) Directing the first community created MMO called "Project Top Secret". (You can join the 60,000+ team members at: www.videogameteam.com).
(3) Doing Executive Level Video Game Industry consulting via www.gameconsultants.com
(4) Funding www.gameinvestors.com - The only online marketplace for doing business in the video game industry.
(5) An Expert Video Game Industry Columnist for BusinessWeek.com
(6) Helping Students into the Video Game Industry through a collection of Websites starting with www.dperry.com / www.gamedesignbook.org / www.dpfiles.com etc.
(7) Building www.gameindustrymap.com v3.0, a free industry tool used by job searchers, publishers, press, and investors looking to find people in the video game business.

  • President and Founder / Programmer / Game Designer

    Shiny Entertainment Inc.

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Computer Games industry)

    October 1993 — February 2006 (12 years 5 months)

    Perry was the President and Founder of Shiny, running it for 12.5 years.

    He works with all the technical, development, sales, licensing, legal, PR, marketing and management sides of running a leading video game development studio.

    Perry has a technical background also, he programmed the award winning games Earthworm Jim 1 & 2, Shiny developed other games too like Earthworm Jim SE, MDK, Messiah, Sacrifice, R/C Stunt Copter, Wild 9, Enter the Matrix & The Matrix: Path of Neo under Perry's leadership.

    Earthworm Jim became a TV show made by Universal Cartoon Studios, aired on the Warner Brothers Kids Network, a Marvel Comic book series and had just about every licensing category including fast food promotions by both Del Taco and Carls Jr.

    Shiny was partially bought from Perry in 1995 by Interplay Entertainment, then again in 2002 for $47M by Atari Inc. After Perry's departure it was sold to Foundation 9, the largest game development company in the world, in 2006.
