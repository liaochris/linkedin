Scott Bigley’s Summary

With over 20+ years experience in both the public and private sectors training, educating, facilitating, and motivating individuals from all walks to achieve to their highest levels, I now seek the next opportunity to help others become more productive, efficient, and profitable.

My background includes tenures with AIG, AEGON USA, Lucent Technologies, and the Pinellas County, FL School Board. These positions required me to assist in the development of programs, identify and secure the resources, develop the materials, and identify the people necessary to achieve stated goals and objectives.

Most recently I served as National Sales Trainer with AIG Retirement (VALIC). Within this position I traveled extensively throughout two assigned regions working with our field leadership teams coordinating and presenting training events to our field advisors focused on building and solidifying the corporate relationship with our customer/client groups. I am particularly proud of the fact that in support of that effort, we designed, developed, implemented and presented a training program to assist those advisors in meeting client needs while building their business productively, efficiently, and profitably. The effectiveness of the program was confirmed as 75% of those advisors achieved their goal of $1 million dollars in the first year sales.

In all my endeavors, I bring three key strengths to the role: the ability to communicate clearly and concisely across all levels, the leadership skills necessary to organize and motivate individuals to succeed to their highest levels, and the personal and professional integrity to enhance the corporate image. In addition, the expertise to optimize product exposure and increase revenue is something else I bring to “the table”.


Objective

Utilize proven Business Development, Training/Facilitation, and Sales & Marketing skills to enhance the corporate image, optimize product exposure, and increase revenue.
