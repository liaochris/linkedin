Marcus Laubli’s Summary

As a multi-lingual, multi-national, I have learned to enjoy wearing many hats simulatneously! Consultant, Business Owner, Lotus Notes Developer, Certified Search Engine Marketing Specialist and more!

As a consultant, I take great pleasure in helping non-profit organizations optimize their web message and web presence.

As a business owner, I am constantly seeking green products which will increase energy efficiency of homes, buildings, and vehicles of all sorts. I am a Thermo-Shield Ceramic Coatings dealer and own www.ThermoShield-Ceramic-Coatings.com. PermaFrost and other products are in the wings and we will soon be ready to offer those.

I have been a Lotus Certified Professional for several years and have enjoyed doing Lotus Notes/Domino Development for my own business needs and on contract since 1992.

Recently I got my Certification in Advanced Search Engine Marketing and am enjoying helping customers succeed in placing their message on the web in such a way that it drives their business and helps attain corporate objectives.

I am also interested in purchasing distressed websites and turning them around once they are making profit.
