Peter Andersen’s Summary

The only way start-ups, small and mid sized companies can get huge business and economical benefits from outsourcing is by leveraging the best practices found in Scrum and Agile.

Here at my company the entire project management staff are certified Scrum Masters. In fact the company runs Scrum as a backbone for every internal business process. The outsourcing setup that we provide to our customers, includes a full end to end continuous integration environment and development teams that are trained in Agile architecture and TDD.

You can reach me at peter.andersen@codelean.com
