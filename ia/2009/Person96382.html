Hudson Whitenight’s Summary

Working with leaders at all levels who want to be highly effective and develop high performing teams. Through experiential learning sessions and the use of video imaging we document the profound transformation of teams and leaders as they begin their journey to excellence. Then, providing facilitated follow-up, we ensure that the transformation is sustained and high performance is achieved. All team and leader development programs are customized to address the unique needs of the team. If you are conducting team building sessions or you are thinking of taking your team through a team building session, then you should call us at 302-540-4676.

Author of "Get the Picture" and "Teamwork: It's More Than Just Cooperation".

Hudson Whitenight’s Specialties:

Over 25 years of leadership in the field of Organizational Development across a broad range of industries: Pharmaceuticals, Aerospace, Consumer products manufacturing, Chemicals, Retail and Education. Certification in methodologies for change management, talent management, leadership, process improvement and team behavior.
