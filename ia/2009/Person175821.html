Nina Åkestam’s Summary

I started out as an account planner, switching to copywriting about two years into my career. I currently work with clients such as NetOnNet, Svenska Spel and Södra.

Since august 2009, I hold a management position at Garbergs.

Since 2006, I run one of Sweden's most popular blogs about communication and advertising. (in swedish only) I also write columns and debate articles for different newspapers and magazines. My work has to date been published in Resumé, Dagens Media, Civilekonomen and Råd&Rön.

Since 2007, I've been lecturing about communication at Stockholm School of Economics, Hyper Island and Berghs School of Communication. Starting fall of 2009, I am responsible for the course Idea and Concept Development at Berghs SOC.
