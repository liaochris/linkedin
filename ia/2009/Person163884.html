Michael Szabo’s Summary

l am a designer and digital artist with extensive experience in creating online, print, and broadcast identity and content for advertising and media companies. I work closely with creative art directors to produce concept visuals that drive brand experiences. My personal work is added to the permanent collection of the Smithsonian Institution, and appears in exhibition, film and publication.

Michael Szabo’s Specialties:

design, retouching, art direction, typography, concept development, branding and identity
