Roelof de Vries’s Summary

As a conceptdeveloper in interactive photography I try to mix different digital techniques to create new solutions for visual online presentation. All the products are based on panoramic photography of the highest quality that can be achieved, combined with other digital options.

Panoramic Photography is a technique that allows the photographer to make a 360x360-degrees image and allows the end-user to navigate in that image. Literally it gives the user 'a look around' at a specific location by using the Internet.

Combined with 3D artist impressions of future objects, panoramic photography will show an end-user how the object will interact with it's surroundings. This product is specifically interesting for Real Estate-presentation.

To visualize long term construction processes, panoramic photography is combined with timelapse techniques. The end-user can navigate through a collection of panoramic images that precisely fit on each other The application is supported by a timeline which presents the whole constructiontime of the object.

Next step in the process is connecting panoramic images to a database and CMS to add dynamic information to specific, clickable parts of a panoramic image. In this way, additional information about an object can be given to the end-user.

All techniques can be combined with aerial photography by using a heliumballoon.
