Sondra Santos LaBrie’s Summary

Working for Kane/Miller during these last seven years has provided me with a great deal of experience in online and direct marketing to consumers, educators, librarians, booksellers, wholesale buyers as well as to gift shops located within hospitals, zoos, museums. During this time I have played a key role in establishing new accounts with stores such as Urban Outfitters, Paper Source, and Anthropologie.

Collaborating with our graphic designer with regards to advertising has given me a fresh perspective on finding the right placement for ads as well as providing Kane/Miller with a consistent look and feel for branding our company, titles and authors, which, of course, makes my job that much easier.


I was eager to become a Certified Parent Educator after taking the Redirecting Children's Behavior (RCB) course since it made a significant improvement in my relationship with both my son and co-parent.

I enjoy working with families and educators and look forward to helping to make a difference in their homes and classrooms by teaching the RCB program myself. I also offer 24 separate one-hour workshops with a more specific focus. While my focus will be on improving relationships between adult and child, I also want to highlight the importance of nurturing and strengthening the relationship between co-parents.

Families with children of all ages will benefit from these workshops and course as well as educators working with young people (from toddlers - teens).
