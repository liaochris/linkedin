Walt Ribeiro’s Summary

Walt Ribeiro, founder of Graywave, is the host of 'Your Daily Music Lesson with Walt' which has achieved over 83,000 viewers/students and over 950 Youtube subscribers since its launch in March 2008. The show has been featured in Mix Magazine and Home Education Magazine, amongst others.

In addition, he writes powerful orchestra scores, which have been used for many projects. With a sound reminiscent of John WIlliams, Howard Shore, Tommy Tallarico, and Danny Elfman, he takes his handwritten scores and brings them to life via MIDI. His music has been mentioned in Skope Magazine, the Trend, and others and has even released a successful 12 song CD in 2006 independently. (NOTE: Music One-Sheet Link is Below)

Walt understands Social Media, Distribution, Marketing, Mixing, Music Production, and stays busy working on many projects teach, produce, and record his own works that are enjoyed by many.

Writing Orchestra Music for Movie Scores, Videogames, Commercials.
