Debra Costanzo’s Summary

Do you know how corporate executives are over-tired, over-stressed, over-worked and, occasionally, overweight due to the demands and long hours at the office? Well, it is my personal passion to help senior management regain control of their wellness by relearning healthy lifestyle choices. These choices include balanced eating plans and a fitness program that gets the body moving again --- doing something they truly LOVE to do! Increasing physical activity means a decrease in stress, as well as in the waistline. However, many corporate executives have compromised their health for their wealth. So, while they're trying to make a million bucks, I'm helping them look like a million bucks!

It's my commitment to help our business leaders improve the health of their bodies, minds, and spirits -- one life at a time! As they experience the energy and empowerment of overall wellness, they will be better equipped to meet both personal and professional goals.

Debra Costanzo’s Specialties:

As a former Assistant Manager for a national circuit training gym and a former Health Benefits Manager for over 300 employees, I understand the mindset needed in order to remain consistent in any exercise and fitness program. Also, I fully grasp the correlation between ill health in Corporate America and rising health insurance costs. Together, we can take steps to improve both.
