Diane Hamilton Kennedy’s Summary

As a business communications consultant in the Information Systems department at Northwestern Mutual, I provide support to project teams who are introducing change to the organization. Audiences can range in size from less than a dozen to over 12,000 in the home office and nation-wide field force.

Projects I typically work on involve changes to technology, processes, personal/team behaviors, or corporate culture.

My services include project assessment, audience analysis, risk assessment, critical success factors, channel analysis, and message scheduling, as well as effectively leveraging training, support, and project teams to ensure positive communication outcomes.

I also develop communication artifacts, including content and simple illustrations for web, video, print, presentations, and events. I am also experienced in working with graphic designers and video production teams.

In previous positions, I was also responsible for planning and developing corporate training, specifically in relation to software or process deployment. I have created curriculum, developed instructor and participant materials, written and recorded e-learning courses, and facilitated training programs.
