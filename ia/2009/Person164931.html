Mike Stead’s Summary

I'm passionate about mobile application development, and find most satisfaction bringing concepts alive from within collaborative, dynamic teams.

My field of expertise is interactive development - creating engaging experiences on constrained devices. Over the past three years I have focused squarely in this domain, working closely with platform engineers and user interface designers to create cutting edge Flash based products for major carriers and handset manufacturers.

Mike Stead’s Specialties:

Flash based framework (application and component) design and development for mobile, Flash optimization techniques, Flex and AIR development, Eclipse plug-in development.

While fluent in ActionScript 2.0 and 3.0 I'm also versed in Java and have experience in Python and Perl. I understand the importance of language versatility and focus much of my attention on agnostic principals and best programming practices.
