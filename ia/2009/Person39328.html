Cathy Areu’s Summary

Media entrepreneur, author, professional speaker, and journalist known for an innovative approach to media, marketing, PR, advertising, and all things creative.

Cathy Areu’s Specialties:

Publishing, marketing, PR, advertising, the U.S. Hispanic marketplace, U.S. politics, and women's issues.

Cathy Areu’s Experience

  • Publisher

    CATALINA

    (Media Production industry)

    September 2001 — Present (8 years 2 months)

    CDK Media is a multiplatform media company responsible for creating and executing national 360-degree marketing, advertising and sponsorship programs for national clients.

    CDK is the parent company of CATALINA magazine, created in 2001 "for the mind, body, and soul of today's Latina." CATALINA earned the “Lead Launch” distinction by Folio magazine, in its first year.

    By 2004, CATALINA became the largest Hispanic lifestyle magazine in the U.S., with a BPA-audited circulation of 500,000.

  • Contributing Editor

    The Washington Post Magazine

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Newspapers industry)

    2000 — Present (9 years)

    In addition to my work with CATALINA, I have been writing for The Washington Post Magazine since 2000. My “First Person Singular” columns are profiles of inside-the-Beltway luminaries including First Lady Laura Bush, Meet the Press’ Tim Russert, HHS Secretary Mike Leavitt, CNN’s Larry King, Former Secretary of State James Baker, and Former Attorney General Alberto Gonzales.
