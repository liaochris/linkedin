William Myrhang’s Summary

Product Manager with over 10 years demonstrated marketing expertise working in world wide high tech companies including Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories, AMD, and National Semiconductor. A proven liaison recognized for communicating effectively with all levels and meeting the needs of customers and the internal engineering team. Reputation for excellence in innovative product marketing and business development creating strategic plans that contributes to the bottom line.

In his current role as a lead product engineer at Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories, William specializes in marketing programmable automation controllers targeted at the high growth electrical utility markets. SEL’s focus is to “Make Electric Power Safer, More Reliable, and More Economical”

Before coming to SEL, William worked for AMD and National Semiconductor as a product marketing manager focused on low-power x86 processors targeted at the embedded computing, thin client, pos, kiosks, education, and information appliance market. His marketing job has taken him to various cities in China, Taiwan, South Korea, & Europe supporting leading embedded OEMs and ODMs device manufactures. William’s strong enterprise IT background allowed him to evangelize his passions of driving enterprise thin client and server based computing adoption along with leading K-12 education technology initiatives through National Semiconductor’s Thin Client@School program.

With a technical background, William has successfully taken numerous high tech products through various NPD processes at consumer electronic, medical, semiconductor, and electrical utility companies. Additionally, he planned, organized, and set up more than 25+ national based trade shows and executive events which included technology demonstrations shown to President George W. Bush, former Governor of Maine Angus King, Empower America and the National Semiconductor board of directors on the benefits of low-power embedded computers

William Myrhang’s Specialties:

Product lifecycle management, product positioning, strategic business plans, market and product requirement documents (MRD & PRD), roadmap development, pricing and forecasting, user personas, evangelism, marketing research, competitive analysis, branding, product launch plans, marketing collateral and sales tools creation, partner management, presentations, tradeshows, customer engagement, plus liaison to engineering & sales team
