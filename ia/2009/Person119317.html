Kevin Jones’s Summary

Over 20 years experience in the laser printing business or in HP speak Imaging and Printing. Innovative and inventive business builder who has sucessfully sold 3 business’s so far. Timing was invariably critical. One was just in time, another was in a nick of time and the most recent was the perfect time for me at more than I expected but less than the business was worth.

£15m pa market leading, sales driven, b2b, IT product focussed tele-sales business. Key business partner for Xerox, HP, Konica-Minolta, Lexmark and Epson. Xerox’s largest printer reseller in Europe. HP Preferred Partner ’08.

Ameiva.co.uk
Launched and developed online web business for printer supplies and hardware. 5 great people, from zero to £4m in 3 years.

Printware Direct
