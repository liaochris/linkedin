Michael Dorf’s Summary

Having run Knitting Factory/KnitMedia since inception, I was able to develop a number of various strategic partnerships which lead to the fomation of a number of creative media solutions for our partners. This included the Bell Atantic Jazz Festival, the Intel Music Festival, the creation of the Digital Club Network or Plug.in Conference,etc. etc. We worked closely with Apple Computer, Intel, Real Networks, Launch, and others streaming video in 1995 and 1996.

Currently, with the depth of positive relationships with artists, managers, and agents, I am producing concerts at Carnegie Hall, Lincoln Center, and other venues. This includes recently sold out shows of the music of Joni Mitchell, Bob Dylan and Bruce Springsteen.
