    (Music industry)

    October 2004 — Present (4 years 6 months)

    Our work includes:
    – songwriting/producing – artist development – music publishing – music for film/television & industrial training videos – programming & audio editing – post-production – sound design and foley – recording & sound engineering – remixing – consultancy – education – tutoring – mentoring –

  • Founding Member

    Xenovibes

    (Music industry)

    May 2003 — Present (5 years 11 months)

    Xenovibes promotes the craft of conjoining ancient art with new world technology and is known for championing progressive-electronic music in live performance.
    Xenovibes the Show tours internationally with high profiled musicians in a concert where 'disparate musical souls melding in a stream of consciousness exchange akin to jazz improv'.
    Xenovibes is in a nutshell a lifestyle vehicle that creates and markets its own products and generates its own publicity.

  • Founder and Director

    Electric Muse

    (Media Production industry)

    May 1998 — Present (10 years 11 months)

    EM's primary business goal was to build and market music technology for music edutainment applications. This business grew to include live performance, education and show design and planning. As demand for their work grew, so did their reputation for being a multimedia performance art business whose forte is to extend the musical norm in live performance.

    Portfolio:
    Projects (script, music, technological work, production) include two electronic operas that premiered at the Singapore and Melbourne Arts Festivals in 1999 and 2000 respectively and original music and performance for live theatre. Clients include the Infoccomm Development Authority of Singapore, CyberArts Studio at the National University of Singapore, ABC Australia, International Computer Music Conference 2003.

  • Senior Manager

    January 1993 — December 1997 (5 years)

Besides overseeing product design and development, the job included international marketing/sales and business planning. The Special Projects Dept. developed a series of award-winning Music Edutainment CDs "Agates the Rock Group" and a cyber playhouse for experimental research.
    A Singapore company with headquarters around the world.
