Valeria Aliperta’s Summary

I am a professional freelance interpreter & translator for Italian, Spanish, English and French.
I am an MCIL (IoL - http://www.iol.org.uk), an associate member of ITI (www.iti.org.uk) and AITI (Italy) and full member of ASETRAD (www.asetrad.org). I work for both private companies and agencies worldwide.
My interpreting assignments take throughout Europe: they involve conferences, workshops and business meetings.
My weekly average translation workload accounts for 15-20k words; I am a Trados user and also an Italian tutor for private students and language academies in Exeter. My client base is global and I am happy to communicate in any of my working languages.
