Steve Rescorl’s Summary

I am currently the owner and managing partner of Anifex | Animation Effects Studio. Besides the perfunctory stuff of running the business functions like, accounting, business development, project management, etc. I am still the obsessed professional 3d Artist/ Animator that started down the 3d rendering and animation path 10 years ago.

and that is why I am involved with Anifex...

Anifex is a 3d design company with a revolutionary mindset, approaching each undertaking with determination, infusing passion, originality and expertise into every story we set in motion. We provide creative ways to tell a story, that promotes, sells or educates an audience through 3d animation. We offer superior service, exceptional quality and competitive pricing.

Our innovative team of talented 3d artists push technology to the limits, focusing on creating modern visualizations that accurately reflect your vision. As artists, we thrive on new challenges where we can apply our 3d skills.

Our Creative Solutions include:
3d Animations
Lifestyle Tours

3d Renderings
