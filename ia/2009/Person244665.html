Vladimir Stojanovski’s Summary

Vladimir Stojanovski's background includes thirteen years of international experience as consulting engagement manager, solutions architect and pre-sales. Currently at IBM, he is architecting performance management solutions with higher education institutions and government entities, which map strategy to execution and enable outcome-based decision making.

His prior experience includes positions at consultancies such as EDS (now an HP Company) and Infor where he successfully envisioned and deployed enterprise-wide strategies in the CRM and Business Intelligence arena. Mr. Stojanovski's industry experience includes over a dozen world renowned clients spanning the manufacturing, financial services, e-commerce, hospitality, telecommunications, energy, real estate, and transportation industries. He is a published author of Analyst Reports and White Papers.
