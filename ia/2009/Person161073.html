Michael Bradley, CFA’s Summary

I lead an independent, private wealth management team in San Francisco. Our goal is to be the central resource for all of our clients' financial needs.

Prior to going into private practice in 2007, I worked as a Portfolio Manager with the Corporate Client Group at Citi Smith Barney, doing essentially the same work that I am now. I started at (then) Salomon Smith Barney in 2000, right after leaving graduate school.

My professional experience includes is working with executives of venture-stage and small cap firms. My work at SB primarily involved portfolio management for private clients, but also included pre-IPO planning, assisting executives with diversifying from concentrated equity positions, constructing hedges and retirement planning.

Since entering private practice, my work has centered on portfolio management, for obvious reasons.

I am increasingly looking to work with smaller, service oriented businesses in a consulting capacity.
