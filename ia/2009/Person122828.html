WHAT I DO

Building on formal training, technology knowledge, and broad industry experience to eliminate obstacles, I work to drive to align technology with business needs, to achieve business success and achieve corporate objectives.

This includes:
• Manage product lines for an optimal P&L
• Lead Business units to maximize profitability
• Develop new business from traditional products and services
• Create new markets for innovative business development
• Facilitate, lead discussions for internal, client, and public audiences

I address the problems most managers avoid. For example:

• Technology related: licensing issues, platform technology evaluation, and tool selection
• Operations related: business process engineering, M&A or joint venture opportunity assessment
• Product and service related: new development, P&L management, make vs. buy decisions, and end-of-life planning; complete operations responsibility for a business unit.
