Arthur C. Van Wyk’s Summary

I currently work as the Brand Evangelist for Sytech Supplies in Durban, South Africa. Sytech Supplies is an international signage, graphics and display solutions distributor and reseller.

After studying toward a BCom degree at the University of the Western Cape, I did the following:

Fresh out of UWC, I worked as a creative consultant to CANSA, where I was involved in developing material and content to promote various CANSA campaigns.

I accepted a position as Head of Creative Solutions Development with Ticketweb South Africa in October 1998. Here I was responsible for the information architecture and content development on the company website, which was also the retail interface and primary touchpoint between clients and the brand..

Whilst with Ticketweb, in my free time I developed and launched an entertainment-related virtual community named URBANTAINMENT, which was an event guide mashed up with satire, biographies, short stories and news snippets. My project became a premier resource for the clubbing and live events community around South Africa at the time.. It later evolved into a promotions platform/vehicle for event management companies and venue owners.


After relocating to Durban, I spent a number of years in the Customer Service division at MTN, where I constructed a tiny customer service intranet codenamed KZNWEB solely for the purpose of digitising and centralising all company, product and service-related information. This however became such a hit with top management that it was jacked from the KZN offices, cosmetically revamped and deployed company-wide as Yelloworld.

In December 2006 I left MTN to work in my own 3 person strong design and branding agency named KOOLOOMA, where I was largely responsible for (and successful at) developing brand identities for startup companies and helping existing businesses optimise brand-related resources already at their disposal.
