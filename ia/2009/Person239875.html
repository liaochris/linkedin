Kevin Myers’s Summary

I have been working as an illustrator for 12 years. After graduating from The Joe Kubert School of Cartoon and Graphic Arts in 1996, I then began working as an Illustrator/Graphic Artist for an internet startup company, Blairlake New Media. Then I moved to VML Advertising and worked my way up to a Senior Art Director. But I missed Illustration and in 2001, I became a Freelance Illustrator and I have never looked back. I specialize in creating robust Illustrations in various styles using numerous methods. Some of my clients include:

- AMC Theatres
- Bayer

- Colgate
- HCA Midwest Hospitals
- Hill’s Pets
