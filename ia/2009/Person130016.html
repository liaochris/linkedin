Kenneth Cook’s Summary

Self motivated, fast learner with nearly 17 years of proven hands on experience in all stages of the manufacturing of optical, electro-mechanical and telecommunications based equipment, assemblies and sub-assemblies from quote estimation thru shipping.

First hand experience is in such areas of the manufacturing process from order kitting, inventory control, ISO9000 process development, manufacturing of components and sub-assemblies for both copper and fiber optic based systems using hand tools and automated machines, inspection, testing and in-process quality control of components and assemblies using various test equipment (Mulitmeters, Light Power Meters, OTDR’s, Vision Inspection System, Calipers and other hand tools). My experience has also encompassed manufacturing group leader, overnight manufacturing supervisor, training coordinator and machine maintenance technician.
