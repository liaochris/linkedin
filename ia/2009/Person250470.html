Yvonne Thompson’s Summary

Currently I provide virtual business support. I am technically a virtual assistant. But I market myself and my services as a virtual partner, or more specifically, as a virtual project manager. My experience and skills sets are broad, so I am able to provide services in a wide range of areas from routine administrative support to system development and strategy design.

As the virtual world has grown, I have started to manage virtual teams for clients. Some have many virtual experts, and it can become overwhelming to try to manage them plus the business. Sometime those teams are poeple, sometimes they are processes and systems. Either way they need to be recruited or created, managed, and updated to run smoothly. Some clients have started to rely on me for this function.

In addition, I have added graphic design to my skill set due to my recent part time position as a public relations assistant at a local university. I love the direction my practice is growing and feel confident that it will evolve more as I grow more both professionally and personally.

Yvonne Thompson’s Specialties:

My specialties are project management, writing, team management, and some graphic design.
