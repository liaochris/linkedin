Carlos Paladino Alvino’s Summary

Carlos Alvino is a enthusiastic seasoned international sales, marketing & business development executive, technologist and writer, in the high technology field with extensive experience in South America, USA, Europe, Asia.

Mr. Carlos serves today as VP Sales at CIO Ltda, a global company in the field of wireless telecom equipment, software, services and consulting. Prior to joining CIO he served as Director of Sales Brazil at SEMA Group, a world leading provider of consulting services and software.

Before joining SEMA in 1998, Carlos served as IT Telecom Billing and Customer Care Director at CLARO wireless telecom former BellSouth Telecom, and played a key role in its growth to become a global leading provider of wireless telecom services and GSM network operations.

Until 1997, Mr. Carlos held senior positions with Bull, Ericsson, Alcatel, and GTE. Prior to that, managed his family Steel Production plant from 1975-1980.

Carlos holds degree in Mechanics, Telecom and Electronics and Management from the Mackenzie University and post grade at Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronautica - ITA and Electronics technical degree at the Escola Tecnica de Eletronica FMC.

Carlos has thirty years of progressive front-line and management experience in International Sales, Marketing and Business Development with technical expertise and hands on experience in wireless telecom, electronics, production systems, hardware, software, insurance and banking solutions and consulting services.

Mr. Carlos has extensive international experience: in 35 countries. Lived and worked in Brazil, USA and Europe.

His sales experience is of services, software, capital equipment, IS/IT and industrial systems to:

wireless telecom industry
aerospace industries,
governamental organizations,
insurance and banking corporations.
