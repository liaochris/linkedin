Russell van Kraayenburg’s Summary

As a student of The University of Texas at Austin my education has focused on building knowledge and a skill set in and relating to Radio Television Film. Most of my time outside of class has been spent representing students as an Executive Board member for UT's largest active organization, The Senate of College of Councils, which is charged by the Board of Regents with representing students in academic Issues. I have also served as various committee chairs in the Senate of College Councils and The College of Communication Student Council. I am the Founding President of both the Idiosyncratic Film Organization, a group built to facilitate communication and encourage projects for incoming UT film students, and UT's first fraternity for gay, bisexual, and progressive men. I have now begun to devote myself to achieving equality in academia. I am a Co-Founder and the Executive Director of The Out & Educated Foundation, a scholarship-based non-profit for in-need GLBT youth in Texas.
