Sven van Buuren interior architect’s Summary

Experience:
Together with other students, we started in 2005 our own interior bureau in the Passage, The Hague as an Academy Project. Instantly a giant success:
We designed the opening of the new govenment building for the Council for the Judiciary. Special guest at the opening was our queen Beatrix. We also designed 2 giant Chandeliers for the Theater aan het Spui foyer (Herman Herzberger). We did some interiors for shops, office buildings and homes as well.

As an Interior Designer / Architect, my plans are to start my own agency together with the collegues I did the above project, but first add a couple of years of experience at various architecture agencies to my resume.

Interested? Please contact me at info at svenvanbuuren.nl

Sven van Buuren interior architect’s Specialties:

I'd like the combination of interior architecture and exterior architecture. I'm interested in City Planning, Project Development and complex (high-rise) buildings.
As well as my interest in large-scale projects, I'd like to search for complex situations on a smaller scale.
