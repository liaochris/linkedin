Erin Genett’s Summary

I graduated from Northeastern University for my undergrad in Graphic Design & Multimedia Studies and my masters in Technological Entrepreneurship. Currently, I am working as an Information Architect for the Berklee PULSE Music Method. I have done freelance design for a number of years, ranging from print to web to video & motion graphics. I am interested in creating an overall experience that can be applied to any media. I enjoy working with interactive media, because it combines my love of technology and design, and is endlessly challenging.
