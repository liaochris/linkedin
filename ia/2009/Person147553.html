Mandy Vavrinak’s Summary

The Short Story:
We effectively market places, spaces, people, ideas and things using social networking, web/online channels and traditional media and collateral materials.

We help you define your goals, develop your strategy, implement the tactics and measure the results.

Our ideal clients are growing businesses (any size, the important characteristic is seeking growth) and growing communities.

We execute and consult in all the following areas:
Marketing Strategy > PR > Marketing Collateral > Social Media

Available for speaking engagements or seminars/panels.

The Longer Story:
We use effective, efficient and powerfully creative marketing to connect products, services and places with the people who need to know about them. With more than 15 years of experience in the communications arena, we're ready to help clearly communicate to your marketplace through a variety of media, or via one specialized approach... in short, we'll help you find the most direct route from here to there. We're rigorously careful with your marketing budget and we know that our success direcly correlates to that of our clients. We work with many small businesses, cities and municipalities, in addition to larger businesses.

We specialize in business-to-business creative and in marketing, research and analysis for economic development purposes.
