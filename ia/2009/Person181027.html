Jeffrey Hickcox’s Summary

Author of the new book Real Estate Investing Guide - Costa Rica: Fundamentals for Profit in Paradise. This book outlines the Costa Rica's real estate laws and characteristics, creative and institutional financing, development strategies and techniques, sustainable or "green" development methods, and provides readers with a vast network of experienced professionals.

Jeff Hickcox has 7 years of finance and real estate investment experience and education, and over 2 years of on-the-ground experience investing/developing in Costa Rica.

Managing partner of Costa Rica Land & Property: www.RealEstateCostaRican.com

President of www.ParadiseProfits.com

Jeffrey Hickcox’s Specialties:

Real estate investing techniques, Costa Rica real estate law, tropical development, creative real estate financing, and Costa Rica relocation.
