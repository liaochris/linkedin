Matt Drayer’s Summary

Over a decade of experience with a diverse set of tools spanning a wide range of technologies and covering the development of both public-facing and intranet-based web applications and web services. Design and construction of n-tier systems, exposure of legacy systems, and integration of enterprise applications in a service-oriented manner over LAN and WAN infrastructures.

Extensive exposure to industry-proven design patterns and techniques geared toward implementation of service-oriented architectures through my professional responsibilities, personal research, technology-related conferences, and volunteer opportunities.

Thousands of hours of professionally demonstrated theoretical and applied application production experience, utilizing both procedural and object-oriented programming concepts leveraging accepted frameworks and development best practices.
