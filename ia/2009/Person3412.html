Annelène Decaux’s Summary

In short:

I accelerate executives, managers, entrepreneurs and creatives on their journey to reach their full potential and make their unique contribution to their organizations and the world.

Call me today (650) 804-1600 or email adecaux@yahoo.com for a free consultation

In more detail:

I provide a supportive yet challenging place where I invite my clients to set their imagination free, connect with their deeper motives, embrace life with a spirit of wonder and adventure, actualize their dreams and realize their potential. I help my clients enhance their leadership abilities, create a strong vision that aligns business and personal leadership goals, engage in purposeful action in service of that vision, gracefully renew themselves in the face of changing conditions, embrace life and professional transitions with gusto, and enjoy greater joy, meaning, and fulfillment in their lives. I work with clients in a broad range of business and industry sectors, including high tech and clean tech.

My professional background includes consulting with clean tech start-up companies such as San Francisco-based Climos; managing Climate Change research programs with the Electric Power Research Institute in Palo Alto, where I initiated a program for raising funding overseas; managing Corporate Development for Business Objects, a large enterprise software company, where I helped with the acquisition and integration of a number of companies, and setting up the company's operations in India; and managing new product development for ABB Energy Information Systems, an energy trading software company.

I hold a Master of Science in Technology and Policy from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, a Master of Engineering and Management from École des Mines in Paris, and am certified as an Integral Coach through New Ventures West.
