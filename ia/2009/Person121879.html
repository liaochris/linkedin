Joy Wang’s Summary

A seasoned, well rounded consumer marketing and business development manager with proven track record of success in consumer healthcare, over the counter remedy and nutritional brand management in America, Japan and China with native level language skills of English, Japanese and Chinese. Proficient in all aspects of fast moving consumer goods marketing practices covering branding, TV/print creative development and execution, interactive / online / new media, PR, promotion, experience and engagement marketing, innovation and new product development, and total brand P&L management. My Business Development expertise covers all aspects of worldwide acquisition and licensing commercial valuation in consumer healthcare and oral care industries.

Joy Wang’s Specialties:

Consumer marketing, branding, communication / creative strategy, TV/ print / online creative planning and execution, media planning, interactive, PR, promotion, experience and engagement marketing, over the counter remedies, nutritional and oral care brands, worldwide business development, merger and acquisition, licensing, commercial and financial valuation, in America, Japan and China with fluent language skills in English, Japanese and Chinese.
