John Drew’s Summary

Drew & Associates is a management consulting firm that assists clients in grant-writing, fund-raising, leadership, survey research, program evaluation, and human resource management. John Drew is a former assistant professor in the Department of Management at Hope International University in Fullerton, CA. Business Reform magazine rated Hope’s business faculty as 7th in the nation among Christian business schools. He is an award-winning author, teacher, and a professional speaker. Combining his research skills with fundraising expertise, John has helped permanently turn around some of the Southland’s most famous charities including the Museum of Latin American Art, the Friends of the Cerritos Center for the Performing Arts, and Opera Pacific. John has taught at Cornell University, University of Oregon, and Williams College. He and his wife Patricia have lived in Laguna Niguel, California since 2003.

John Drew’s Specialties:

John has a proven track-record of success in helping founders, board members, and executive directors initiate successful fundraising programs – particularly for securing corporate sponsors for golf tournaments, galas, and other special events. He has expertise in direct mail, telemarketing, planned giving, and major gift solicitation. John provides clients with the skills needed to implement capital campaign feasibility studies, and to oversee the capital campaign itself.
