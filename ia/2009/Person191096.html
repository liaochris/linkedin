Peter van Kessel’s Summary

Peter of Kessel studied on the Academy for Journalism and Communications in Tilburg and did additional marketing training (Nima-A&B).
He started as a PR consultant at Write Media Consultants. Here he was responsible for gaining free publicity for clients such as Sony Netherlands, UPS, New Balance and Club Med.
He was also responsible for the product development, marketing and sales for a press database named: De Perslijst. This is an overview of 4.500 journalists with contact details and journalistic specialism & interests.

Afterwards Peter started at Edelman International where he was responsible for the free publicity on accounts such as British Telecom, Bol.com, Exact software and Canon.

In 2006 Peter joined Bex*communicatie in Eindhoven. In team Dienstverlening (Business Services) he now is - in the role of Advisor Press and Media Relations - responsible for the free publicity on accounts as Visa Europe, Rabobank, De Lage Landen, Ernst & Young, Rijnconsult, APG, ExxonMobil and the Dutch Ministry of Justice and Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
