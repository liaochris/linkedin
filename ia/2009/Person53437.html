Daniel Donnelly’s Summary

Through my In Your Face studio, I have designed and produced Web sites and online promotions for many large and small companies including Intel, AT&T, HP, JCPenney, IBM, Sun Micro, G.E. STARZ and many others.

I have designed online promotions, banner campaigns and integrated online and print sweepstakes for PCworld magazine, Intel, SONY, and AT&T.

Starting with the best-selling "In Your Face: The Best of Interactive Interface Design" in 1996, I have authored and designed eight books on Web interface design, typography, Flash and multimedia for Rockport Publishers. Additional books include "Cutting Edge Web Design," "Upload: Taking Print to the Web," "Web sites from Around the World," Flash Web Design," Typography on the Web," In Your Face Too!, and "Hands-on Flash".

I create digital illustrations that can be seen at: http://www,unstablevisions.com

I have an on-demand publishing company that focuses on designing and producing one-off, full-color hardcover and soft-cover design books and portfolios for designers, design studios and digital artists.

Contact me for a copy of a hardcover demo of my portfolio to see the quality of my books.

530-899-3223 donnellyda@butte.edu

Daniel Donnelly’s Specialties:

My area of expertise is in developing interface designs for Web sites and authoring books on design and new media. Clients include many large and small corporations such as Intel, AT&T, John Hancock, G.E. SUN Micro, PC World and many others. I have over 10 years of print design experience with magazine and book publishing as well.
