Ed Bales’s Summary

Open to domestic and international travel and OCONUS locations, to include emerging and frontier countries.

Over 15 successful years experience specializing in sourcing and full life cycle recruiting primarily within the US. However, do have some experience to include Canada, Mexico, EMEA, APAC, LATAM countries, to include emerging/frontier countries. Have also held positions of Recruiting Director, Recruiting Manager, Director of Professional Services, Corporate Technical Director, as well as being a small business owner.
