Scott Kosak’s Summary

Left Turn Marketing is all about creating interactive and engaging motorsports marketing platforms by working with businesses who want to engage race fans using FEWER DOLLARS to EFFICIENTLY ENGAGE MORE FANS throughout the entire racing season.

Left Turn Marketing has also created RacingIn.com, For Fans of Fast as a platform to engage race fans and racers across the United States in a grass roots way. You see, although we believe that major motorsports like NASCAR, IRL, and NHRA are incredibly powerful platforms, we also understand that not every business can afford the expense of those platforms, so we created one that allows businesses to engage local race fans all over the United States as they search for local racing.

We work with both Brands AND Agencies to develop turnkey strategies. If you'd like a free consultation on how our model can complement and enhance your current platforms, feel free to drop us a note at LeftTurnMarketing@comcast.net.
