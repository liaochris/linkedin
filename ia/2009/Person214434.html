Sanjay Gadhalay’s Summary

Have more than 15 years' experience in Marketing, Sales and Systems Management with the last 4 years as an Industry Consultant for Overall Business Restructuring from the perspective of uncovering hidden value to the enterprise through the overall Supply restructuring and E-Business enablement.
Born in 1966, Mr. Sanjay Gadhalay is a Gold medallist in Mathematics , a qualified Electrical Engineer from IIT Delhi since 1986 and an MBA in Marketing and Systems since 1989 from IIM Bangalore .
Sales , Marketing and Systems and General Management have been his primary areas of expertise for more than 15 years .
He has multiple industry exposure in India across the Information Technology , Electrical Equipment , Engineering , Telecommunication ,Automotive and Electronics Industries straddling the Services , Agricultural and Industrial sectors of the Indian Economy and the IT industry.
With an intention to develop and raise the overall competitiveness of Indian small and medium scale Industry SGC enterprises was set up in early Mar 2004 to offer targeted services for an overall Supply chain restructuring , Business Consulting , Marketing services and other related efficiency and profitability related improvements ( New market discovery and Outsourcing ) .Consulting services are also offered for companies targeting business out of out of European economies (Germany and UK ) .
His experience include, Overall management positions handling multiple teams and multi geographic spreads of businesses as Regional Manager operations , Sales and Marketing Lead Consultant and a Supply Chain expert .
He firmly believes that only the right mix of IT and Business Domain knowledge have a role to play in enabling businesses to uncover better efficiencies and profitability through intelligent usage and deployment. IT is a service for enabling business improvement ‘Necessary –But not Sufficient ‘
