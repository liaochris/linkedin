Michelle Corteggiano’s Summary

I spent most of my adult life in the car business working 70+ hours a week. My average American lifestyle included an annoying alarm clock and no chance for me to really become who I had the potential to be, a great leader. Network Marketing is the vehicle that found me as the diamond in the rough.

While educating myself on how to be the best networker and leader I was introduced to Attraction Marketing, or what I call Passive Prospecting. Passive Prospecting is a creative, un-intrusive and highly effective way to get permission to market to someone. It build's an audience and drives business towards you without “pitching your deal”. You become the hunted, not the hunter, and attract the “right” kind of prospect to you, your product or your opportunity.

I teach Passive Prospecting through my company, ATI Attraction Marketing. Along with growing a huge business with my network marketing company, I offer one on one courses on this type of marketing to anyone, in any type of business, who sees the value in staying ahead of the curve and being on the cutting edge. Predictions are that this type of marketing will soon crush the advertising industry as we know it.

To be truly satisfied with who we are, we must be willing to learn new lessons, expand our horizons, and make a choice to stop being comfortable with average. Once you start thinking big, there's no turning back.

I drive people to success by teaching them to be great, not average. Hopefully I’ll be able to enlighten or inspire you to take a bold step towards something new and undiscovered in your life.

If you want to build a real relationship with me, based on value and common interests, I’m seeking people who know (or who are at least open to) the potential they have inside to create the future they desire for themselves. I'm talking about people who know they can achieve anything they set their minds to.
