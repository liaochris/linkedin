David Upright’s Summary

I started Upright Advertising following a 25-year career in the
creative departments of major agencies.

I've been the Trix Rabbit,
a singing zebra and
a cuckoo bird.
I've been a doctor and a patient.
I've written for planes, trains and automobiles.
I've written national commercials and Push/Pull signs
that work both ways.
