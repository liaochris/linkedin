Jared Montz’s Summary

My professional experience includes being a professional soccer player for four years. I have recently recovered fully from my groin/hip surgery in August 2008 and I am currently looking for a new team. My goal is to play as long as I can!!

My business experience includes starting my own soccer camp in January of 2006 and being a long lasting positive affect on kids lives while running a great business. My goal is to continue to do that and give back to the youth in the community that are trying to take their game, and better yet life to the next level!

My new way of giving back to the community is with the start of my second business, America's 1st Online Soccer Academy. This new program offers youth players a safe way to learn soccer online on their own time. Besides learning soccer I believe my new Academy will act as a motivational center where players can get motivated and inspired to take on soccer and life's challenges with confidence!

Jared Montz’s Specialties:

I know a lot about soccer (I think!); playing and coaching. Plus I am hungry to learn even more. I think my ability to find a way to communicate with players on their level has helped me become a better coach.
