Leslie Brown,MA’s Summary

Leslie is employeed with the Office of Human Resources as an HR Professional with the City of Philadelphia. In this role she has recieved and processed 200+ applications for candidates competing to become civil service employees. She is also responsible for the creation and administration of civil service examinations. As an early career employee, Leslie has administered 19 civil service examinations which resulted in high level candidates for positions within the City of Philadelphia's Finance, Revenue, and Public Health Departments.

Prior to HR, Leslie's work experience centered around customer and/or client service. As a case manager for the City of Philadelphia she worked with under-served populations of families and children that were victims of abuse or neglect. This was both a challenging and rewarding expreience. Leslie has accquired an indepth understanding of culture and diversity, dynamic communication, client management, supervision and above all how to work well in a fast-paced environment in this work environment.

Leslie continues to diversify her skills set by continuing her graduate school course work at Keller Graduate School of Management. As an MBA student, Leslie is furthering her understanding of business practices in general, and is also laying the ground work to become a dynamic human resources business leader

Be sure to follow Leslie on twitter: missbianca1 or you can visit her personal website at www.LBianca.me.
