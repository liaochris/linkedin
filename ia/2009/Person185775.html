Peter Kestenbaum’s Summary

Peter advises leadership on improving business development capability and organizational issues which augment revenue and build a culture of "go to market agility". He has served in senior roles in F500 and emerging firms.

Recent assignments include helping/supplying assistance to;
-A F500 firm build, and execute a commercial Business Development strategy for a software product
-A Silicon Valley firm obtain first orders/partnerships in NY with a tier 1 server firm.
-A Marketing Services Firm rebuild.
-A young SW firm (open source/security) secure initial relationships with a tier 1 hw supplier and a tier 1 integrator
-A DC consultant work with technology suppliers to leverage new stimulus programs.


An early Sun employee, he led their entry into Financial Services as their first Wall Street employee, manager, group manager and Director. As Director of North American Marketing he managed a team chartered with the introduction of JAVA for Eric Schmidt. He is an expert in channel ecosystems. He has significant experience in business development issues for technology firms who wish to penetrate large firms such as wall street or telecommunication enterprises.

In the ".COM" era Peter was the Chief Marketing/Business Development Officer for Agency.Com, an on line marketing and services firm, as it grew from its early days through IPO and hit the $100M revenue mark.

At IBM, Peter served as a trusted advisor for leadership and supervised the acquisition of an integrator and formed the Migraiton Factory. He led it from startup to the $300M level.

Peter has held senior line management responsibility in sales, marketing, partnership and business development roles. He enjoys coaching and consultant assignments in the NY-DC corridor particularly with high energy emerging organizations attempting to secure footholds in larger enterprises.

Peter Kestenbaum’s Specialties:

Acknowledged expert in the area of alliances and go to market plans to enhance business development efforts- Experienced in Building and Coaching sales staff or partner and marketing teams to add agility for todays business demands. Deep understanding of technology needs for wall street and similar high performance real time markets. Unique ability to communicate and shape messages to a broad spectrum of users and suppliers as evidenced by major campaigns at IBM and SUN.
