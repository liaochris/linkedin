Gary Kellmann’s Summary

experiences while living in China for four years.

* 2008, while working at my office in Shenzhen, China; led my product development
team 12 times through product concept (invention) to reality (production scale prototype) to licensing in a total of 60 days.

mass retail with a focus on Solar energy.

* 2008, successfully designed and launched an ODM designed commercial solar
