    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    August 2008 — Present (7 months)

    Inside Assisted Living is a online resource dedicated to helping families evaluate and transition to an assisted living community. The site features a unique group of family and industry insiders that share their insights into how to make assisted living a positive and rewarding experience for your loved one.

    After my mother had a stroke in 2005, I found there to be few resources available for families that wanted to embrace the idea of assisted living, so I decided to create one.

    Inside Assisted Living was the first to commission a "by families, for families" study in which we interviewed 195 families to better understand their attitudes and preparedness toward assisted living. The research findings are available free to the public at www.insideassistedliving.com/family-survey/
