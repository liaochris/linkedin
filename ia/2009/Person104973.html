Jason Forsyth’s Summary

Over seventeen years of national and international experience with private and publicly held companies.

CAREER EXPERIENCE

As Assistant Treasurer of one of the largest mortgage companies in the U.S. was responsible for negotiating additional off-balance sheet repurchase facilities, structured as mini-securitizations, for a total of $16.5 billion in liquidity financing. This included valuation of fixed income products: Asset-backed Home Equity Loan securitizations. Actively involved in risk management: including hedging using swaps and options

CFO of publicly listed company located in London, U.K. which acquired three companies valued at over $200 million.

Knowledgeable in U.S. GAAP and IAS (International Accounting Standards)

Consultant to a variety of companies seeking debt or equity financing ranging from Consumer Product Company located in the U.S. to a $150 million cotton linter manufacturing plant located in the United Arab Emirates

Equity financing in the U.S. with Regulation D, Regulation S as well as SB2 filings

Perform professional business valuations of middle market companies for estate planning, employee stock ownership plans (ESOP's), mergers, acquisitions, divestitures and litigation support
