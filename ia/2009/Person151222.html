Mark Carbone’s Summary

AT WORK
Mark's passion when he founded XE Corporation was and still is to help people find ways to win back the new currency of success - TIME. His main goal at XE is to help companies leverage technology so their CEO's and employees can have more time to work on their companies instead of in them and more importantly to have more time for family and community.

The software he invented is a business management engine to help companies run more effectively using technology usually reserved for Fortune 100 corporations. A new features of the software is it's HR module's international human resource component to help CEO's with their most expensive investment - employees. It allows your company to run 24/7 utilizing lower cost human resources from around the world to do your non mission critical processes and tasks.

PERSONAL
Mark subscribes to the philosophy of, and in this order - God, Family, Country, Green, Work, and Community. He and his wife, Susan, are trying to demonstrate that the institution of husband/wife run companies is a great endeavor and they work to shine light on the millions of husband and wife teams who run successful businesses.

Mark is also a firm believer in alternative energy and our government and businesses moving forward to first, stop being dependant on foreign oil, and second to move forward and be the world leader in Clean Energy and Green jobs.

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    June 2000 — Present (9 years 3 months)

    www.xecorporation.com

    OUR TECHNOLOGY
    XE's XE-700 Business Management Engine is an all-in-1 software that bolts into organizations with 10 to 10,000 employees that will improve the way they run their company the day they turn it on. XE-700 is part .NET 3.5 and part Open Source.

    WHAT IT DOES
    It gives a CEO and management a real-time window into what their best and worst clients, vendors, and employees are doing to further the success of their company. It does this by managing all parts of a company - marketing, sales, project management, work orders, inventory, scheduling, and people management.

    UNIQUE TO XE
    Green Module - Inside XE-700 is a module that helps a company become Green and stay Green.

    Goals Module - Addresses a leading issue facing all CEO's - finding and keeping the best people. The Goals module, using Covey principles helps employees meet their business and personal goals. We believe people value their families more than their job and XE-700 helps a CEO help his employees have a balanced life.
