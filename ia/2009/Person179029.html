Ugo Guidolin’s Summary

New media and communications consultant and project manager, professor of “Theories of New Media Technology” at University of Padua, graphic designer and author. Guidolin has over 10 years of experience conceptualizing, designing and producing a variety of interactive, multimedia and printing projects for the major italian media companies (among which Mondadori, Fininvest, Enel, Gruner und Jahr, Edel) and artists (among which Aldo, Giovanni e Giacomo, Beppe Grillo, Paola Cortellesi).

Ugo Guidolin’s Specialties:

New media development, interaction design, social media technology, graphic design, multimedia design, web design, animation & video, new media and design professional training
