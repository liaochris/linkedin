Joanna Elliott’s Summary

The Start Company helps entrepreneurs, first time business owners and growing businesses with custom solutions for starting, growing and re-charging businesses. I have over 15 years experience providing quality professional services and leadership to fortune 100 companies and “mom-and-pop” shops. I blend technical knowledge with a creative flair and a bottom line results orientation.

I help incorporate new businesses, provide consulting on local business licensing and general corporate compliance, provide document assistance for trademarks and copyright registrations, design build and host corporate websites, design logos and create or re-brand corporate identities, create review and re-write business plans and provide custom consulting by building expert teams across many functional areas to solve critical and complex business challenges. See http://www.thestartcompany.com

I also blog, write articles, and provide commentary on small businesses and the economy at http://blog.thestartcompany.com
