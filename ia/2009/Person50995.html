Chris Sandlund’s Summary

I've spent more than 20 years working in media -- starting on the PR side of things, then moving to TV, and on to print before my current gig in online. After more than 15 years working in lower levels of larger organizations, I decided to work for myself. It was a great experience, but I recognized that I lacked many of the skillsets necessary to running a successful business. I got a wonderful opportunity to join Money-Media, a great online outfit, in 2002. It is giving me many of the skillsets (such as managing a P&L) that I need to eventually run my own publishing venture. I have stayed with this company through its sale to the Financial Times of London and am still enjoying helping it grow in new directions. But I know that at some point I want to be running my own venture.

Chris Sandlund’s Specialties:

All stages of content development, including business analysis for new product features; working across media; P&L-focus; managing an editorial staff
