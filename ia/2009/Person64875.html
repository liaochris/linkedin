Dimitris Mavrakis’s Summary

I am fascinated by technology and telecommunications. I have been lucky to be involved with some very motivated people and interesting projects during my studies and work that have provided me with good knowledge of the telecoms market, technical issues involved and the dynamics of business vs technology.

I now work as an analyst, specializing in telecoms and next generation wireless technologies. I have worked as a project management for radio measurements in the past and as an analyst, I have been the author of several leading strategic reports and have undertaken several consultancy projects on behalf of market leaders.
