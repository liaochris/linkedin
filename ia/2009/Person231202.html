Andy Ferguson’s Summary

The 9 Rules:

Business, like life is a game ... .. figuring out who to play with and which game to play can make it a lot of fun or be depressing, frustrating and painful.

We've all been there, the hard times and the bad times (if you haven't don't worry it will be your turn soon enough) that's part of life and reminds us to enjoy the good times when they come and focus on the important stuff ... all the time.

If you need a Speaker that will thrill you and make you laugh. If you need a speaker that will remind you to turn up for the important stuff everyday. If you need a speaker who will share a lifetimes experience of ups and downs, leave you and your audience breathless. Then give us a call.

If you need a mentor to help you find your way back to the path, get in touch.

If your wondering how to save $millions, create happier staff, have more loyal customers, keep ahead of the curve ... build a better business, thrive in the chaos.

Give us a call.

How can we say all these things? Because The 9 Rules started with one person and slowly became more. We know how to help you re-learn all the really important stuff you always knew and as importantly how to learn to build the most flexible and robust systems you'll ever need.
