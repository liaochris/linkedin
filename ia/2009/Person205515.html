Russ Hicks’s Summary

With some thirteen years of experience in public relations and over 32 years in the print industry, Genesis owner Russell Hicks offers customers a wealth of industry knowledge.

Following a trade apprenticeship and time spent working on the shopfloor, the production office, and in sales, Russell moved into the supply side of the industry, working with AM International and with Monotype Systems in both domestic and international marketing roles.

Having written over 30 articles on industry topics, Russell is also aware of what journalists require by way of input and assistance in their creative tasks.

Russell is a member of the Institute of Paper, Printing and Publishing and also of the Chartered Institute of Marketing.

Russ Hicks’s Specialties:

Genesis Marketing PR expertise has been brought to play many sectors of the print industry, including: commercial sheet-fed; newspaper; digital; and packaging. Products represented have included pre-press, offset-litho sheet-fed, web offset, digital, and post-press.
