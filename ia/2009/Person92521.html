Harold Kip’s Summary

Hi, my name is Harold. I’m hyper active, hugely enthusiastic and I have a ferocious appetite for everything in the digital space.

I strive to be an awesome dad, great husband, colleague and friend while I hope to be approachable, friendly, creative and inventive. I like to meet new people, go flying or ride my bike, study my Aikido, make life interesting and have a lot of fun, everyday!

I’m currently tasked with getting us ‘out there’ via online community building, social media and digital marketing. By leveraging past experiences and current creative thinking, I am moving towards integration of online conversations and our community vision.

Engagement and involvement is essential in that process and tracking behavior and trends that we can leverage successfully to get into the hearts and minds of our community is key in reaching that goal.

That is what I call ‘The Experience Factor’.

I walk in front of a team of online publishers, media specialists, consultants, community advisors and developers, who are listening to - and create new added value for - our community … every day!

Being an advisor for all internal and external social media initiatives and activities, from blogger relations to marketing support and from customer service to internal communications, I am also working on the integration of social media into our everyday business and personal activities.

I am an advocate of building and maintaining strong business and personal relationships and I have a creative and open view towards added value that produces results on both sides.

I write about the intersection and integration of sociology, psychology and marketing in digital space. Through my blog, I am teaching, educating and keeping myself up-to-date about social media. Hopefully this will help others understand what social media is, what it means for organizations, which tools are appropriate and relevant and how to incorporate those into the marketing mix.
