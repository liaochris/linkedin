Thomas Jagger’s Summary

Product Improvement Engineer – produce new designs, remanufactured designs, and repair kits for the transportation industry. Patents held are 7,533,764; 7,438,169; 7,311,189; 7,201,267; and 7,104,382.

Prepare Patent Drawings – Work with customers to develop drawings and sketches that can be used in patent submissions.

Aero Acoustical Design - design a virtual diesel generator enclosures for use in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) studies, for use in sound and airflow studies of engine intake and exhaust air.

Panel Design - 3D-CAD design of a 20 KVA inverter electrical panel used in the wind turbine industry.

Product Manager - Promote sales of processing machinery upgrades and services, preparing financial and operational estimates from drawings, plans, specifications submitted by customers. Managed and coordinated activities of service technicians that provide maintenance and repair of pharmaceutical process machinery

Project Manager - Project Engineer - Designer - Sales Engineer – Sterile filling machines for pharmaceutical drugs. Produce mechanical-electrical machines for use in sterile filling of injectable drugs. Interact with customers, engineering, machine shop, assembly, and vendors to develop quotes and produce projects for pharmaceutical packaging systems. Manage $15M in sales of aftermarket projects per year. Work with pharmaceutical companies to bring their equipment back into compliance after a non-compliance FDA 483 observation. Manage (2) sales engineers, (1) project engineer, and (3) mechanical engineers and (1) electrical engineer.

Field Project Engineer – Install process equipment as a site manager at process improvement start-ups. Solved problems related to material and the flow balances for the installed process equipment.

Manufacturing Engineer – manufacturing engineer for airline pitot tubes. Managed manufacturing of parts in the machine shop; wrote routing's for the parts to follow through the machine shop.
