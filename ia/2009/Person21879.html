Astrid van der Knaap’s Summary

Having travelled the world as a professional athlete the settled live in Holland made me restless. Ten years after my retirement another big life changing moment passed by in 2004: asked to stay in the Middle East while freelancing on the Big Brother reality show in Bahrain. Giving Dubai a try for a year I got hooked to the expat life and not intending to leave the Emirates for years to come.

It was a great time at MBC Television, growing from managing the setup of the Biggest (Loser) Winner reality shows in Lebanon to project manager for strategic projects at corporate level.

Now, October 2009, it is time to change again....,
