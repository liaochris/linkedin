Eric Fain’s Summary

I focus on the following areas in my client projects: working with clients to prototype new Web-based applications using Drupal or hand-coded HTML/CSS/PHP; and designing the user experience through conducting end-user interviews, performing task-based analysis, and creating mental models. Regardless of the deliverable, I believe it is critical to understand the thought processes and motivations of end-users in order to understand how any system is will be used and how it is designed. Finally, I provide project leadership through clearly stated goals, tangible scopes of work documented and approved by business and technical stakeholders, and through strategic and tactical communication throughout the project lifecycle.
