David Menzies’s Summary

My background includes over 17 years of professional internal and external communication experience writing, editing, speaking, and producing all manner of public relations and marketing deliverables for multiple audiences.

I have worked on both sides of the fence in media and public relations -- as a PR manager and account rep and print newspaper editor, in addition to editing online news publications -- so I understand what makes a story stick.

After experiencing great success at Duke University in technology-oriented public relations, I joined a small PR firm and was account lead for a growing data center provider, helping them realize unprecedented growth in media coverage and customer communications.

In August 2007 I resigned my position and created Menzies Consulting, Inc. to provide effective public relations and marketing communications to help companies reach their organizational goals. Past and present clients of Menzies Consulting, Inc. represent diverse industries including technology, legal, nonprofit, real estate, finance, pharmaceutical, health care and engineering.

Menzies Consulting, Inc. has developed numerous enhanced distribution methods for sharing industry-specific information via our interactive publications TriangleTechNews.com, PRPowerhouse.com, and ApexBusinessNews.com.

All three publications enable businesses to share information with other businesses and entrepreneurs, potential customers, investors, news media and the general public. These sites are extraordinarily powerful means of quickly and effectively spreading the word about a certain subject to a great many number of people locally, nationally and globally.

Through these publications I am able to provide the general public with fresh, original content, attracting more content contributors and visitors and thus achieving more exposure for Menzies Consulting clients while sharing quality news and information with readers via a professionally edited news source.
