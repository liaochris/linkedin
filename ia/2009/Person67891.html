Donald R. Simon, Esq.’s Summary

Before going into private practice, Mr. Simon was Director of Business and Legal Affairs for Questar, Inc. and Associate Counsel at Francorp, Inc. At Questar, he drafted, reviewed, and negotiated various kinds of agreements including, talent, production, distribution, and music clearance contracts for in-house documentary film and television projects. In his activities with Francorp, Inc., Mr. Simon drafted and reviewed Uniform Offering Circulars and franchise marketing material, and handled the company’s intellectual property affairs.

Donald writes extensively on a wide range of legal issues. He has published two law review articles and has appeared in DownBeat and Billboard. Currently, Donald writes a periodic legal column for Screen Magazine. He is also an Adjunct Professor in the Radio Department of Columbia College Chicago, in the Digital Photography Department of Harrington College of Design, and in the English and Communication Department of East-West University. Donald also volunteers at Lawyers for the Creative Arts.

Prior to becoming an attorney, Donald worked for 7 years in the broadcasting, cable, and publishing industries. He was as an award-winning producer and director for an ABC affiliate and later for CableOne Advertising. Donald also worked as a radio announcer for a classic/modern rock station, where he produced and hosted a local music show, and as the Operations Director for a classical format public radio station. He worked as a graphic artist at an advertising publication and as a promotions manager at a college television station.

Donald received his Juris Doctor and Master of Laws (Intellectual Property) degrees from The John Marshall Law School in Chicago, Illinois. He is licensed in Illinois. Donald received his Bachelor of Arts (Communications) from Missouri Southern State College (now Missouri Southern State University) in Joplin, Missouri.
