Jihan Antoine’s Summary

Premma-belle Media, LLC was established in 2007 to enhance the recognition and the fast-growing reputations of all the essential players in the entertainment industry. Our target is to promote unsigned artists as well as other pertinent characters in the industry to the fullest extent and generate the positive attention that will get them noticed, recognized, and remembered!

Our services specialize in the creation of professional press kits, press campaigns, and portfolios that clients will ultimately have to distribute to all the major companies and publications within this field.

ABOUT THE FOUNDER:

Jihan Marielle Antoine, 21, did not have the vision of a regular of a teen. She did not want to be a dancer, or an actress, or a doctor, but simply something greater. From age 13, Jihan had instilled in her family's mind that she wanted to be an "entrepreneur." She did not know what type of business venture to own, but she knew the she wanted to own it. In grammar school and high school, Jihan took business classes, workshops, and seminars, while others prepared to go to school dances. At age 18, she compiled her first business plan and proposal and prepared for the launch of her business. At 19, she received her business certificate for her public relations and marketing company, Premma-belle Media, LLC. By age 20, she had successfully landed a dream internship at Def Jam Records and an executive assistant position at S.A.C. Interiors, Ltd.

As a double major for Journalism and Business management, Jihan is anticipating her graduation this year. She is fluent in French and will be taking mandarin at Stony Brook. She recently received her license for reality and will be interning for a real estate investment firm in NYC.
