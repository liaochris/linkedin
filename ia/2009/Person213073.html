Sebastien Marion’s Summary

At the age of 24, sebastien co-founded, and is currently director of two IT companies, while finishing his PhD in Computer Science from the University of Kent (UK).

He is the co-founder of Chootta Ltd, the company behind comufy (www.comufy.com), a revolutionary communication platform putting the receiver in control of his communications.
Comufy is the first communication platform to allow the redirection of communications (voice and text) to numerous communication channels, including social networks, instant messenger, emails, and phones.

Sebastien is also co-founder and director of Whizlogic Solutions, a web development company focusing on delivering content management system with bespoke quality designs.
After only one year of existence, Whizlogic has an impressive porfolio of clients and offers its clients total satisfaction, through competitive prices and very high quality standards.

He has a very large experience as a developer, and as a manager.

Currently, he is also finishing his PhD in Computer Science from the University of Kent, which he will submit at the age of 25, on the 16th May 2009.
