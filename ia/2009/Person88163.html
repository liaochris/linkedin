Matthew Goldsbrough’s Summary

My advice on business strategy helps people to build stronger companies and make more money.

My clients refer to me as their mentor, strategy guru, coach, catalyst, advisor, or marketing expert.

With my help, my clients execute a clearer business strategy. They spend their marketing budgets much more effectively, because I provide them with a strong strategic marketing foundation. And they achieve challenging targets, which often include improved stability, more sales growth and enhanced profitability.

The clients I've helped range in size from individuals to large businesses, in a wide variety of sectors. They're based in the UK, the USA, and mainland Europe. Many of them are in the North West of England, around Manchester.

WWW.GOLDSBROUGH.BIZ has much more information. Please get in touch if what you see there interests you.
