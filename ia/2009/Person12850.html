Anca Vlad’s Summary

I am a real estate professional with a wide range of experience, especially in sales management, but also in development and investment counseling. As a serious business woman, I am dedicated to maintaining and achieving high performance in the real estate field by the mean of organizational skills, flexibility and work under pressure.

I had a major contribution in developing a new real estate concept in Romania, as President of Timis county and national Vice President of UNAI (National Association of Real Estate Companies).

The most important specialized trainings I took part in are the following:
– Certified International Properties Specialist, National Association of Realtors, USA;
– “Efficient Techniques in House Sales”, ARAI and Eastern European Real Property Foundation, USA;
– “Cooperation in the Domain of House Intermediaries”, ARAI, E.E.R.P.F. and U.S.A.I.D.;
– “House Deals on International Markets”, ARAI, E.E.R.P.F. and U.S.A.I.D.;
– “Sales Techniques and Property Management”, ARAI, E.E.R.P.F. and U.S.A.I.D.;
– Essentials Real Estate, Chamber of Commerce Industry and Agriculture Timisoara;
– Real estate valuations, ANEVAR (National Association of Romanian Valuers);
– Competitive Management, Financial Management, Customer Relationship Management, Open University Business School UK, CODECS Romania;
– General Management, Ost-West Management Training Bayern;
– The Negociate Workshop, Resources Development & Ideas SRL.

Having developed my own company, Imo Serv Group (www.imoserv.ro), we employed up to 18 employees and we are selling over 1500 real estate properties per year. The company is the market leader in Timisoara since 1993.

Imo Serv Group has gained the status of a major promoter in the development of new residential areas of Timisoara city. New investors are definitely welcome, as we are always looking to establishing new business partnerships based on seriousness, win-win approach and solid market experience.

Anca Vlad’s Specialties:

I specialized in real estate counseling (development and investment), sales management, residential areas management and marketing.

For challenging real estate opportunities, do not hesitate to contact me directly at anca.vlad@imoserv.ro. Thank you.
