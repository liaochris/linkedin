  • CTO

    AstraVoice

    (Telecommunications industry)

    June 2005 — Present (3 years 11 months)

    Chief Technology Officer of AstraVoice, a voice over ip consultancy in New York City. We focus on open source voice over ip deployments using Asterisk, among other available open technologies. We provide integration services, as our core offering but provide ancillary services such as hardware dimensioning and procurement.

    We also have a Hosted PBX product which we call On-Net. On-Net is a Hosted Platform that we developed in house. It comes as a bundled package for small to medium sized companies and includes the Software Platform, The Hardware Endpoints and our own DSL or T1 Broadband Connection. We provided managed QoS on this service so it is a true business class solution for businesses who require reliable phone service.
