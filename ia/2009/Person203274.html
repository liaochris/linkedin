Ron Marks’s Summary

I have run a nonprofit organization and developed community leaders in the context of grassroots community organizing; I have also worked with individuals who are the leaders of their organizations or companies, or who are self-employed, to develop strategies and change behavior in both organizational and personal contexts.

I want to work only with people who are passionate about making a difference in their field, especially fellow novelty-seekers and experimenters.

My current endeavor is expanding my coaching to help organizations and professionals develop actual strategies to take advantage of available new media tools and manage the organizational change required to effectively integrate these strategies into the life of their organization. I am currently focusing my networking on the fitness industry and the nonprofit sector, and would love to connect with the wine industry.

Ron Marks’s Specialties:

Nonprofits, political consulting, small and local companies, fitness industry, and religious congregations; multicultural, bilingual Spanish/English; practice a "brain-based" approach to behavior change in coaching and change management.
