Mark Norman’s Summary

Sales Professional with strong marketing and negotiating skills, Strategist, Technologist and Leader who has developed expertise in Web 2.0 sites with great passion for wine.

Mark has always successfully integrated his diverse educational background into each venture. His visionary approach to business has enabled him to succeed as an entrepreneur developing and incorporating state of the art concepts, as well as utilizing new tools and ideas in the corporate world to get the job done. His proven sales skills allow him to adopt from B2B to consumer, from tangible to conceptual, coupled with ability to learn quickly, allows him to strive in any environment. He envisions new and improved solutions-oriented approaches to many tasks to always move the business forward to a successful completion. Currently consulting to winery consultants on social networking sites and blogger at http://WineAndHospitalityNetwork.com. Developing unique approaches to increasing membership and overall activity on Web 2.0 sites.

Recently I agreed to become Director of Marketing and Business Development for the Wine Society (a web site devoted to helping buyers and sellers of vintage wines). I intend to use my Internet expertise to help grow this three year young company. I am also writing wine blogs for the Wine and Hospitality Network and for ewinejunkies and have my own HubPage site.
