Oleg Dulin’s Summary

The role of a software engineer is to improve business processes, reduce costs, increase productivity and competitiveness by delivering well thought out computer technology solutions to rapidly evolving business problems

My vision is a global economy where software engineering is utilized to its fullest potential as a discipline that can help businesses roll out their products and services and deliver results to their customers ahead of competitors.

My purpose is to work with an experienced manager or entrepreneur to improve their organization's software engineering practices one team and project at a time.

Oleg Dulin’s Specialties:

My main expertise is in financial and structured data processing including trading systems, payroll automation, data quality analysis and optimization, and software engineering process improvement.
