Frédéric Engel’s Summary

Market Engel designs, develops and implements marketing, communications and business development programs.

Recent programs include a go2market plan for Fairlead Institute, a start-up that produces Change Management training courses, the soft launch of TagPay, a mobile payment solution, and the design and implementation of a marketing & sales tool box for XIRING, a leading provider of EMV-based, e-banking and e-payment service provider.

Prior to founding Market Engel, I created and developed Livo Technologies, an Internet authentication start-up, between 2003 and 2007. Prior to Livo, I was the director of Marketing Europe of ActivCard for 10 years. Prior to Activcard, I was a Visiting Fellow at CSIS, Washington, D.C., and worked for 5 years as a marketing & sales project manager for U.S. and European Cable TV and OTA broadcasters.

Frédéric Engel’s Specialties:

International marketing & business development, market needs analysis, segmentation, product positionning, digital identity management services, strong user authentication, smart cards, personal security devices, strategic partnerships, direct and indirect marketing & sales programmes, marcom & PR, customer relations.
