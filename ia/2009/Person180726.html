Pankaj Agrawal’s Summary

I am a management professional with specialization in Sales, Marketing, Consumer Services, Partnerships, Alliances and Product launches. New businesses and projects are what keep me interested & engaged.

I have worked with the FMCG industry for over 5 years but for the last 12 years I have been associated with the Travel Industry managing Leisure & Corporate Travel, Transportation & Logistics.

With a background in Hotel & Business Mgmt, I have extensive exposure to Setting up New Businesses, Building Teams, Achieving Successful Project Implementations, Product Launches, Partnerships & Alliances which have been integral to the growth & distribution capabilities of the organizations I worked for.

I am now doing what I do best i.e. Start a New Business; in the Transportation & Logistics space for V-Link Fleet Solutions.

Feel free to contact me at pankajagrawal@hotmail.co.uk.

Pankaj Agrawal’s Experience

  • CEO

    V-Link Fleet Solutions

    (Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    October 2008 — Present (8 months)

    VLFS is a subsidiary of V-Link Travel Solutions which is funded by a Private equity company - IVF (India Value Fund). IVF is a premier private equity investment fund with over US$ 1 billion under management. IVF makes available financial and intellectual capital to growing middle-market companies in India.

    V-Link is a pioneer and market leader in the Surface transportation industry in India, having started off with creating transportation solutions for IT & ITES companies. V-Link has also created the Meru brand of metered taxis over the last 2/3 years which is positioned as the premium and most popular radio taxi brand in India.

    I am working with V-Link to help create transportation solutions for corporate and retail customers alligned to business and leisure travel.

    (Public Company; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)
