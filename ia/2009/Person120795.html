Joe Gillette’s Summary

At Break Media, I use my skills in interactive and graphic design to help develop new advertising strategies, games, interfaces, and products. My contributions include some site design, branding, games, and concepts, but my focus is on Flash design, animation, and actionScript.

All my life I've been a self-motivated, self-taught artist. I received my Bachelor of Fine Arts from the Minneapolis College of Art & Design in 2007, where I studied design, illustration, animation, video and multimedia. I'm an active member in my community and I work hard. I always love a challenge.
