Anwar Kazi’s Experience

  • COO

    Sybrid Private Limited

    (Outsourcing/Offshoring industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    I joined Cybernet (www.cyber.net.pk) in June 2007, the Lakson group (www.lakson.com.pk) then asked us to spin off into a new company and we did the same in July 2008. It made sense since we were taking care of all the group business for BPO (outsourcing) anyways including McDonald's, Colgate, Express News, Cybernet Tech Support and others.

    We had a different life cycle from Cybernet which is the largest ISP in Pakistan, working closely together over the last one year, Sybrid (www.sybrid.com) and Cybernet have now launched Pakistan's largest managed services project with initial capitalization of 500 people and 13 offices.

    Sybrid has four products/verticals:

    1. Health Care, we service US based customers (via a JV with United Care Group) in areas of Appointment Scheduling, Referral Management, Insurance Verifications, Medical Transcription, Medical Billing, Collections, Customer Satisfaction/Retention Survey Management, and Practice Management Solutions. We are installing our Electronic Medical Record (EMR) application on practices at this time

    2. Managed Services, using Cybernet's 11 year experience with corporates all over Pakistan, we are taking this forward by installing a Support Provisioning Group (SPG) model and servicing more than 1200 corporates (Most major banks, regulators, MNCs, etc.) from technology installation to maintenance. This includes all access networks (RF, DSL, Fiber, others), LAN and WAN, Application, and Power.

    3. Market Research: partnering with MEMRB-IRI we provide services in Brand Health Tracking, Customer Loyalty Measurement, Advertising Effectiveness, and other services

    4. Generic: general BPO services, we launched Dominos in Pakistan, and many others

    Sybrid has facilities out of 13 locations in Pakistan, Kula Lumpur Malaysia, UAE, and Florida.
