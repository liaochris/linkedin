Mark Sullivan, RPA’s Summary

Mr. Sullivan is responsible for solution development for corporations based in the Central U.S. and Canada on behalf of CB Richard Ellis' Global Corporate Services practice, which includes Corporate Real Estate Consulting, Transaction Management, Project Management and Facilities Management Services.

He is an experienced Alliance Director, Account Executive and Consultant with a consistent track record of delivering solutions that exceed expectations for clients such as Allstate, KPMG, Ontario Realty Corporation, IBM, NCR, Sprint Nextel, Convergys, Exelon (ComEd), McDonald’s, Xerox, Visteon, and Bank of America.

Additional corporate service clientele includes but is not limited to: Harris Bank and Trust, The South Financial Group, Mead Westvaco, Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools, and San Jose State University.

With significant experience in 1st and 2nd generation real estate outsourcing, Mr. Sullivan has led successful turnkey transitions and implementations on stringent time frames for each of the primary accounts he has managed. His account has been awarded “Account of the Year” (ComEd - 1997-1998); he and his teams were each awarded two global client communication awards (Visteon - 2005) and he has been recognized by Bank of America for his leadership in revamping the Business Continuity program (2006).

During his tenure on the Bank of America account he was responsible for a full service corporate portfolio of 10 million square feet and 800 properties supported by a team of over 100 employees, managed capital plans of $500 million and led teams in the discovery, strategy and execution of $170 million in operating expense savings over two years utilizing six sigma tools.

In addition to his corporate service experience, Mr. Sullivan is equally comfortable in the investment side of the real estate business having managed properties and leasing in Chicago for clients such as Allstate Insurance Company and other institutional investors.

Mark Sullivan, RPA’s Specialties:

Developing cutting edge solutions for corporate real estate users and investors including Portfolio Optimization, Facilities Management, Project Management, Transaction Management, and Consulting with an emphasis in customer relationship management, transition project management, and CRE outsourcing leadership.
