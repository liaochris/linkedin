Christian Bungard’s Summary

Hello! Here is a brief introduction to my professional background. Currently I am the Partner Director of Jumping Frontiers Ltd., an International Commercial Management firm based in Madrid, where I have the opportunity to share my experience with clients and associates, building future market entry strategies around the world for expanding companies .

Over the years I have gained a broad experience in international business and market development management in multinational and multicultural environments, having conducted operations in over 50 countries around the world.

Multi-disciplined experience in a wide scope of industry sectors; FMCG, Automotive and Aeronautic components, High-End Consumer Products, Architecture & Design, Furniture, Textiles, Food Processing, Drinks (Beers & Wines), Business Services and Energy Saving Products.

I have established dealership and distribution agreements throughout Spain, as well as a number of countries across Southern, Central and Eastern Europe, the UK, Middle East, North Africa, the Far East and North and South America.

We currently manage commercial operations in GCC countries (United Arab Emirates, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Bahrein, Kuwait), Iran, China, Greece, Italy, Spain, Portugal, India, Mexico, Costa Rica, Colombia, Brazil, Peru, Chile, Argentina, Uruguay and the USA.
