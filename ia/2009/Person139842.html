Leonardo Muñoz’s Summary

I have a MBA Degree, an undergraduate degree in architecture, and 14 years of experience in corporate environments.

My career have been consistently in managerial roles, leading results oriented work teams, and supervising multicultural businesses across the world:
• I started my career in Inmobiliaria Manquehue, a major Real Estate developer. With them I developed several housing and infrastructure projects in Santiago.
• Three years after, I worked as Commercial Operations Manager for Resic S.A., a company that provided equipment and value-added services to engineering companies, and was soon promoted to Computing Division Director.
• Then my work shifted to the Architecture and Construction Industry, where I was Managing Director of Graphisoft Chile, in charge of the operation of the national subsidiary of Budapest-based Graphisoft multinational. Aside from its presence in the Chilean market, I personally trained and supported work of Graphisoft software distributors from Peru, Bolivia, Ecuador and Uruguay.
• After that, I worked 4 years in Quintec, one of the largest IT vendors in Chile that serviced major domestic and international companies and corporations. I started in 2002 as Marketing and Quality Director, where I conducted several projects focused in the international expansion of the company. Additionally, I headed the new division of Product Development, with the main goal of proactively creating new distribution channels for our products and services.
• I started Archisoft in 2004, initially focused in providing services and software to the Construction Industry, but later we opened additional business units in some related, technological fields.
• I founded 3go - Idea Management in 2006, with the goal of becoming a national referent regarding innovation and creativity applied to business. I am fully dedicated to 3go since January, 2009.

Leonardo Muñoz’s Specialties:

I am fluent in English, both written and spoken, and I have broad experience with travel and establishing business relations across different cultures. I firmly believe in teamwork, intently focused on maintaining long term business relationships with customers. I am creative, flexible, and I have proven that I learn fast in various surroundings.
