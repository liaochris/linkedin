Christine Henry Public Relations Expert’s Summary

Founder and author of the My PR Tools suite of products, spent more than two decades creating successful marketing and public relations campaigns for companies across the Western United States.

Represents clients in a wide range of industries, including Psomas, one of the top-ranked engineering firms in the nation. Manages all public relations activities for Psomas offices across the Western United Sates. The company is regularly featured in a wide range of major industry publications. Designs client events to showcase the company’s experts and directs ongoing e-marketing campaigns highlighting the firm’s services, products, and expertise.


Created and directs a public communications program for Los Angeles World Airports to reach residents in noise-impacted neighborhoods surrounding LAX. Designed communications and public relations campaigns for the Boeing Company, Earth Tech, Loyola Marymount University, and Raging Waters Theme Parks, to name a few. Other clients include those in real estate development, high technology, entertainment, law, public works, and health services.

Created My PR Tools because saw a common misconception, especially among small business owners, that PR is largely just about sending out press releases. “Nothing could be further form the truth,”
"To be really effective, a public relations campaign should consist of a number of varied activities that all work in concert to promote company visibility."


My PR Tools products bring the benifits of my expertise to those who are not in the PR field.These step-by-step guides to communications are the culmination of my years of experience in the public relations industry.
