Sandy Hubbard’s Summary

My career has spanned film-video production, printing, project management and publishing. How fortuitous that, these many years later, this varied experience is converging in today's technologies.

At our disposal are tools to do our jobs better and more efficiently, and an ever-changing landscape filled with new ideas and new friends, many whom I have met through social networking. I look forward to meeting you!
