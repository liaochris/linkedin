Peter Cornbrooks’s Summary

Born and raised in Baltimore. Went to Gilman School. I continued on to Wake Forest University where I graduated with a bachelors degree in Economics. During my time at Wake Forest, I was a member of the Kappa Alpha Order in which I served on the philanthropy committee. Also, I was the head coach for a local middle school's lacrosse team for three years. In the summer between my junior and senior year, I interned at T. Rowe Price in Baltimore, Md. There, I was part of the Retirement Plan Services marketing/sales team. My primary responsibility was to help write sales proposals. I also took various corporate training classes during my summer internship at T. Rowe Price.
