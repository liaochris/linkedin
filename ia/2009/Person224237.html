Steven Audino’s Summary

Steven Audino – founder of Global Bank Vision.

Drawing on more than 12 years of experience in corporate finance, mergers and acquisitions and strategy, Steven has established a world-class competitive intelligence practice with retail banking at its core.

In the mid 1990’s Riegel Neal was the catalyst for a massive wave of consolidation across the financial services industry. The mortgage and credit crisis is spurring a second wave of consolidation as the stronger absorb the weaker – those facing certain failure.

Therein lies opportunities for banks large and small to acquire and retain displaced customers.

Maintaining a real-time understanding of competitor strategies and performance while monitoring for disruptive new entrants is an essential input to strategic planning and execution. Steven is an internationally recognized expert on the industry.
