Robert King’s Summary

Robert is an old-school software engineer who's written software for a variety of hardware in about a dozen languages and has been building human-computer interfaces longer he cares to admit.

At the moment he is most passionate about all aspects of web development used in good Web 2.0 experiences (such as POSH and semantic markup, CSS, and AJAX), software engineering, and classic RPGs - but not necessarily in that order - and his script-fu is wicked strong.

Although he's currently consumed by projects related to the eBay family of marketplaces at PayPal he's also pretty determined to get his 14th level rogue safely through what he swears is a "legitimate business venture" in the Dragonstar universe.
