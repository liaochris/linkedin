Gloria Green’s Summary

Over 13 years entertainment experience working in the Christian/Gospel music department of the William Morris Agency. As a Music Agent, booked concerts and tours for a diverse roster of faith-based artists including Contemporary Pop, Country, Urban, Gospel, and Rock, as well as comedians and specialty tours.

Aim of Gloria Green Entertainment is to help facilitate the needs of companies and ad agencies interested in working with faith-based artists for their corporate events and/or advertising campaigns. Gloria Green Entertainment will also help facilitate companies and ad agencies' reach within the faith-based community with their message and/or product.

Gloria Green’s Specialties:

Negotiated artists' performance fees for a variety of venues and special events. Packaged artists for tours, conferences, and festivals
