Michael Robinson’s Summary

Experienced in the design, development and delivery of products, as well as, management of cross functional teams.

Responsibilities have included the development of the product definition, project plan creation, identification of the scope and business requirements, development of detail specifications, creation of test plans, business cases, profit and loss reporting, user training and the final implementation of the a product or project.

Having worked in a variety of business sectors to include Retail & Wholesale, Telecommunications, Manufacturing, Petroleum and Energy Transportation gives me a unique background to draw upon for ideas, project execution and delivery.

I am a firm believer in driving projects and achieving business objectives based on practical requirements utilizing methodologies, best practices and stringent project planning disciplines.
