Alex Standiford’s Summary

I worked as an engineer for sheet metal pop displays from mid 2006, until mid 2009. I have created several renderings, technical drawings, and played roles in designing displays. I have also created several bills of materials, routings, and other manufacturing details that are crucial to the manufacturing process. I have used SolidWorks since 2006, and hope to become a CSWP soon. I also have extensive AutoCAD Knowledge, working with it since 2004.

I have worked with Adobe Photoshop, and GIMP since 2004, and feel that I know the programs well. I have enhanced pictures, edited photographs, and have also created images from scratch. I enjoy working with photographs, and have often found myself picking out random photographs on Google, just to see what I can do to them.

I plan on going to the Art Institute of Pittsburgh this fall, for Industrial Design. I ultimately hope to have a successful job in this field.
