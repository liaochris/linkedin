Colin Powers’s Summary

Colin Powers is Director of Production and Programming at Mountain Lake PBS in Plattsburgh, NY where he supervises and executive produces five primetime regional series as well as stand-alone documentaries for national PBS distribution. He is also Managing Partner at Headlamp Pictures where he is producing "Skatopia: 88 Acres of Anarchy."

    Colin Powers is Director of Production and Programming at Mountain Lake PBS in Plattsburgh, NY. He supervises and executive produces five primetime regional series on the arts, business, community affairs, outdoor living, regional history and architecture, as well as stand-alone documentaries for national distribution and multiple “specials” including live and studio broadcasts.
