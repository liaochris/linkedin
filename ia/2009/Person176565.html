Neil O'Donoghue’s Summary

Mr O'Donoghue joined Ricoh in 1999 in a technical capacity and held positions of connectivity product manager, solutions product manager and lastly solutions product manager team leader, whist in these roles Mr O'Donoghue provided technical support for Ricoh's office software proposition as well as coordinating the technical launch of new products and providing technical training to internal and external customers. Mr O'Donoghue has attained Microsoft accreditations of MCSE, MCP and MCP+I.

In mid 2006 Mr O'Donoghue moved over to become the solutions marketing product manager for Ricoh UK where his role is now more focused on the marketing side of the business, new duties include launching new software products, managing the lifecycle of the software product, interfacing with software partners both in the UK and internationally, designing the software solutions proposition, providing marketing and sales support to Ricoh's external and internal customers and managing the Ricoh SDK program.
