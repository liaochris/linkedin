Christie Shinn’s Summary

I currently am working freelance with a few projects at the moment. Currently, I am working with an up and coming hip hop artist called Son. My past projects have included: Workout On the Go for character design and One Lil' Snap for their clothing line for babies and toddlers.

My continuing clients are with: Olive Jane with release paperwork and mailers as well as patterns for their women's clothing line- Retro Resort 2008 as well as assisting them with their website. I also work with List Caddy for graphic design for print packaging as well as original designs to be published ON their products.

I have done Character Concepts with ArcNovo, Inc. for their upcoming children's animation as well as designed Flash Ad banners for IMS Barter's ad campaign for Forbes.com.Also, I have completed Character Concept and Environment concept for Ethereal Muse. I designed creatures and other settings for the web-based game Encore.

I am currently looking for something that will constantly challenge me and to grow bigger and better as an artist and as a person. I know I have the skills to implement it, but I hunger for the knowledge to acquire more. I do not mind if it is a long-term position or a freelance. Every bit helps.

Christie Shinn’s Specialties:

Production art: character concept, environment concept with Photoshop and Illustrator. 3D modeling using 3DS Max. I have also have experience in Flash animation and other forms of classical 2D animation. I have done texturing as well.

Oh yeah, for the admins in you, I also have expertise in Excel and the rest of Microsoft Office as well.

Fashion: Textile and pattern design, line and color swatch sheets, logo, and web graphics work and update.
