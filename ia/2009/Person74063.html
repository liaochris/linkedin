Eric Bryant’s Summary

As Chief Internet Marketer and Social Media Marketing Architect at Core Zero Creative I get to do a bit of everything. On the creative side: web design, copy writing, marketing banners and posters, and social media marketing. On the technical side: search engine optimization, internet marketing, and information technology at our headquarters including servers, analytics, VMware virtualization, networking, computers, and all else that keeps Core Zero on the bleeding edge of competitive technology.

I truly thrive in both the creative and technical arenas. In the early years I got into industrial electrical control and machinery computers called PLC’s. I also restored old muscle cars (still do) and got into Unix, Linux, and Windows System Administration and Network Engineering. Back then we made all our own integrated circuit chips--out of wood! I’m MCSE certified--that’s Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer--and I’m also a VMware Certified Professional (VCP).

Discovering that I have a natural gift for teaching led me to teaching Adult Ed and Community College computer courses including MCSE certification courses. Core Zero currently presents a wide variety of Social Media Marketing and Appreciation Marketing training workshops and seminars teaching local businesses and entrepreneurs how to harness the power of the Social Web to promote and connect their businesses using sites like Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, MySpace, Flickr, and many other Web 2.0 websites.

Whether you’re using social network sites, blogging, or expanding your website, great marketing and sales copy is an important key to your success. Over the past few years I’ve invested a lot of time in developing expert search engine optimization (SEO) skills which, combined with my creative and effective copy writing talents and knowledge of web design and the social web, have delivered top ranking in the search engine results pages (SERPS) for many of our sites and those of our clients.
