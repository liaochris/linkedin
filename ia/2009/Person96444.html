Hugh Cumming’s Summary

Hugh Cumming is an experienced technology professional with over 15 years technology and software development management experience in the Financial Services, Telecom and CRM industries. Hugh is currently the Chief Information Officer for ADP Canada, a Fortune 500 company in the financial services industry, where he overseas a team of more than 250 dedicated professionals responsible for developing and operating ADP’s proprietary processing technology. Before taking on his role at ADP, Hugh had returned briefly to Webhelp where he oversaw the development of Webhelp’s Arena VoIP platform and successfully saw the company through an acquisition in October of 2003 to a US/India BPO firm. Hugh was the Senior Vice-President, Product Development for 724 Solutions, a public wireless infrastructure company focusing on the financial services industry, where he was responsible for all aspects of worldwide development, support and product management. Prior to joining 724 Solutions, Hugh was one of the founders and the Chief Technology Officer for Webhelp.com where he oversaw the development of Webhelp's "Zero Latency" Web Application Event Framework. Previously as the CIO for SITEL Europe, he was responsible for defining the technology strategy for the company's global business units. His career has also included the roles of VP of Technology for SITEL Global Business in the Netherlands, VP of Technology for SITEL Canada, VP for MIS Canadian Telephone Corp., President and Founder of Computer Engineering Co., Software Engineer for IBM Canada, Ltd., and Software Developer for Ecolab Ltd. Hugh holds a degree from the University of Toronto in Engineering.
