Patricia Anderson’s Summary

My work as an Educational Psychologist focuses on development of human potential across the lifespan. I am deeply concerned about parent development, since what happens at home and between family members is key to brain development and social adjustment. And I am also dedicated to assisting individuals in achieving their dreams, by providing tools of literacy, writing and publishing, and motivation support.

My career as an educator, coach, program director, and curriculum developer has this as its goal: the betterment of humankind through development of individual talents and abilities and through support of prosocial attitudes.

Patricia Anderson’s Specialties:

Public speaking, writing, coaching, consultation and curriculum development (including online programming).
