Peter Bourget’s Summary

Based in Atlanta, Georgia, Peter Bourget brings over 25 years of entrepreneurial experience in consumer products, mass media and niche marketing, real estate construction and remodeling, business management, corporate investment planning and development, marketing and financing experience.
Mr. Bourget most recently completed over 7 million dollars of residential remodeling on an estimated 30 million dollars of Atlanta and area real estate. In his capacity as investment consultant, Mr. Bourget has assessed and assisted as consultant with the placement of over 200 million in bulk property acquisitions.
Mr. Bourget has held executive board positions with the National Association of the Remodeling Industry, Atlanta Chapter as Secretary for 2 consecutive years, 4 years overall and headed the marketing committee for 3 consecutive years.
Member of the Greater Atlanta Home builders Association, the largest in the nation, and member of the National Home Builders Association, The Remodelers Council and is in his 3 yr term as Vice Chair of Gwinnett Technical College Carpentry and Management program’s Advisory Board.
Between 1999 and 2003 Mr. Bourget was responsible for over 9 Gold Professionalism and Contractor of The Year Awards. His works have also been featured in Builder/Architect Cover/feature, Better Homes and Garden, Atlanta Home Improvement Magazine, Kiplinger’s Report and The Atlanta Business Chronicle.
