Witt Salley’s Summary

I am the e-learning director for Missouri's third largest community college district, Ozarks Technical Community College, where I also teach online courses in instructional technology and political science. In addition, I am a doctoral learner in the School of Education at Northcentral University, pursuing an Ed.D. in E-Learning and Teaching Online.

Witt Salley’s Specialties:

E-learning administration, supervision and evaluation of online teaching, educational planning and development for online programs, online faculty training and development, online course development, online assessment methodologies
