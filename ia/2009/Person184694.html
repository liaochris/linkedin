Peggy Hoyt’s Summary

Attorney/Family Wealth & Legacy Counsellor - I work with individuals and families who want to create a lasting legacy for loved ones. As an author, I strive to provide practical advice for creating estate plans that work - for individuals, pets, unmarried couples, families with special needs, high net worth families and business owners. I am also certified as a Circuit Court Mediator and FINRA Arbitrator.

For fun and extra income I am a Card Diva and Appreciation Marketing Consultant with SendOutCards.com/CardDivas. I use SendOutCards to stay in touch with family, friends and clients using heartfelt messages and picture cards.

As an author and publisher for Legacy Planning Partners, LLC, I've created and co-created a number of books designed to educate people about important estate planning niche areas like planning for your pets, planning for special needs individuals, planning for unmarried couples and same sex partners, planning for high net worth individuals and preserving and saving family legacies. Most recently, I am co-authoring a book on gratitude called "Thank Everybody for Everything - Grow Your Life and Business with Gratitude."

During my career I've worked as a Merrill Lynch Financial Consultant, Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University Director of Recruitment, Southern Bell Account Executive and CFO for Fine Foliage of Florida.
