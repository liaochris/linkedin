Arul Prasad M L’s Summary

Currently as a Lead Engineer at Fusiongarage Pte Ltd, I deal with the overall product development, architecting and building our 'stealth mode' product, and talking to/ evangelizing about the product to potential users, and 'customers'.

In my previous incarnation, as a Senior Engineer at Radixs Pte Ltd, Singapore, I was involved in developing a social networking and Instant messaging application VelvetPuffin. I worked on the desktop client application, developed using Actionscript2.0, Macromedia Flash 8 and mProjector.This application was then re-branded as 'trugo'. The application UI was rearchitected and rebuilt from scratch using Flex 3 as a web application, but built with hooks to port it to Adobe AIR.

I used to be a Senior Engineer, Software product development,
Symphony Services, Bangalore -India. before moving to Singapore. I was involved with developing Actionscript based RIAs using open source flash tools (MTASC, SWFMill).

Prior to this, I was a QA Tester at Macromedia R & D Pvt Ltd, Bangalore - India, in the Flash 8 Development team.

Before that, I was a software developer at Ascendum systems Pvt ltd, developing elearning applications using flash, actionscript and xml.

My professional career started as a Product Integrator, and visual designer at EI Design pvt ltd , developing SCORM conformant and AICC compliant elearning content for the web and CD media.

I've also worked as a freelance flash developer for aedes-design pvt ltd. I developed the basic framework and the first version for a website here (http://www.bpowatchindia.com/msn), which is now a part of http://www.msn.co.in

I've also worked as a freelance tutor teaching C and C++ language for engineering students, during my college days. I've also worked on making a college magazine, from designing it(using CorelDraw 9) to taking it to the publishers and getting it printed.
