Anand Jayaraman, PhD , CAIA’s Summary

After 6 years of experience in academic research in nonlinear dynamics, chaos theory, fluid mechanics and mathematical physics, I have started focussing on using mathematical methods in financial forecasting. I have developed (and currently trade) quantitative strategies that exploit short-term market inefficiencies. The strategies are general enough to be equally profitable in all 4 major sectors (equities, commodities, currencies and interest rates).
