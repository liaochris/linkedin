Shannon Wallace Jr’s Summary

After a number of years in the sales and finance industry, I decided I wanted to change my career from finance to fitness. An advid athlete most of my life, I wanted to help people bring out the athlete in them as well.

Kettlebell Teacher and Natural Trainer helping athletes obtain strength, speed and conditioning. Coach to high school, collegiate, and professional athletes. Consultant for fitness programs for organizations.

Administer post rehab training for clients just out of rehab to continue their journey of optimal fitness to give them a life of activity.

Youth Fitness Specialist ages 8-18 for overall health and sports performance. Emphasizing accepting adolescent where they are in the development cycle. Combatting obesity is paramount.

Implementing fitness regimens for men and women concerned about the anti-aging concept.

Shannon Wallace Jr’s Specialties:

kettlebells, resistance band training, plyometrics, speed & agility, dietary supplementation, deep breathing, rope training, fitness writing, and functional fitness.
