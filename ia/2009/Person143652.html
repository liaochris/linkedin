Lori Mae Tevault’s Summary

I began working for Regent Communications on a part-time basis after a brief internship with the company in May of 2006. I was hired as a full-time employee in January of 2007 as the Promotions Director and Morning Show Co-Host for 99.5 WKDQ. As Promotions Director, I contributed to the overall management of the station alongside our Program Director.

My goals at this point in time are to try new things and grow beyond the experience I've started with. I would like to take the education I've developed in Indiana and move forward. My passion is music and the message and feeling it provokes in people. My goal is to continue working with music and the artists, fans and audiences that believe in it as much as I do.

Lori Mae Tevault’s Specialties:

Specialty skills I possess range from audience-connection ability to advanced technological knowledge.
Another career aspect I feel I excel with is my work with charities to promote awareness and create fund raising opportunities.
Extensive production experience completes the backbone of my capabilities with a foundation of skills developed for the use of Profit/NexGen Systems, Cool Edit, Adobe Photoshop, InDesign and Premiere Pro as well as Microsoft Office.
