Philip Coverdale’s Summary

Philip manages the Operations Consulting group at Zenith International, carrying out complex technical and strategic projects across multiple continents and beverage sectors including bottled water, juices and other flavoured drinks. Philip has a masters degree in Manufacturing Engineering & Management and a career before Zenith with Accenture. He has a wide range of technical and commercial skills. His recent responsibilities have included managing projects in Algeria, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Chile, Nigeria, Russia, South Africa, Sierra Leone, UK and US.

Philip Coverdale’s Specialties:

Food and beverage manufacturing strategy; production equipment; capital project management; due diligence; feasibility and business planning; global beverage markets; product development; global beverage regulations.
