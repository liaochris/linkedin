James Hopper’s Summary

I am a recent MBA graduate from Georgetown University. After completing my MBA I have returned to New York and am now seeking full time employment.

As a full time MBA student at Georgetown, I focused on the financial/accounting coursework. This includes courses in Private Equity, Financial Statement Analysis, Corporate Finance, Mergers and Acquisitions, Investment Banking, Quantitative Methods, International Business, Negotiations, Derivatives, Fixed Income Securities, and International Finance. I was also part of a 5-person semester-long strategic planning project for a company based in the United Arab Emirates which needed an overseas expansion strategy into the US market. Also, I remained highly active outside the classroom where I participated in venture capital and international operations case competitions and non-profit development.

As an intern with the Inter-American Investment Corporation, part of my role was to interview every member of senior management and analyze the company’s business plan and pro-forma financial statements in order to create a dashboard application. This application serves as a real-time company assessment tool to guide senior management in overseeing the company's $1.2 billion in total assets. The internship also gave me valuable exposure to general investment and development in emerging markets.

I worked more than 5 years as an analyst for Western Union Payment Services. As an analyst who was involved first-hand in working with numerous third party vendors, and managing a large-scale production environment with various money transfer systems, I am proud to call myself an IT expert. As an IT expert, I would feel particularly confident in my general research and analytic skills as well as my ability to work with high profile clients and multi-task in a high pressure environment.

I am also moderately fluent in Spanish and speak at conversation level Portuguese.
