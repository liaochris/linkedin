Chad A. Holmes, CISSP’s Summary

Chad Holmes has over 10 years experience in the areas of Information Security Architect, Sales Engineering, Product Management/Development, Overall Security management, Information Technology management, Information Systems Auditing, Security Assessment, FDE, Firewalls, IDS, penetration testing, risk management and has advised clients in a number of industries including Financial Services, Health Sciences, and Manufacturing.

Chad has worked at software manufactures performing Sales Solution Engineering/Product Management duties which included all the products from Check Point Software.

Chad was the product specialist for the Firewall Management, Endpoint Security Suite, Data Encryption products and also worked with the IDS, firewall, and VPN

Chad also worked in product development when at Check Point on the SMB Edge Firewall product line during EVO research and QA. (Sofaware) Chad worked with development in Israel for all RFE's requests on Check Point Security products for customers.

Chad has also worked in Product development/management on the Empower Banking Application product line when he was with New South Federal.

Chad worked on Product development/management and security research on the ITI banking applications developing RFE's for the vendor.

His experience also has included directing the efforts of companies and client personnel on integrated Security audits, assessments of internal controls in connection with Sarbanes-Oxley Section 404, HIPAA, Graham-Leach-Bliley, attack and penetration security assessments, application source code vulnerability analyses, system implementation security/control reviews, and business process effectiveness reviews.

Chad has also managed and implemented various vendors’ equipment in different environments around the Southeast.

Specialties;

Product Management, Sales Engineering, Product Development, Information Security Architect, Firewall and Security Management
