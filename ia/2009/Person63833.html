Steven T Smith II’s Summary

Direct to consumer specialist with ten years of experience in the field at the highest order. Specialities include, integrated marketing strategies with a focus on wine savvy content. Implementation of social mediums to drive added value to the end user (consumer) and increase your rankings in the Google indexes. Key wording isn't enough, it's the actual constant search, discussion and interaction with your brand that will bring you the most new customers.

Digital Veraision's goal is to build a strategy that can be implemented with the consumer in mind. Concepts including backstage access and affinity marketing programs geared towards heavy consumer interaction with your brand and website.

The current reality is a website is only a house with some furniture. To fully capture and captivate your following; we've got to present the essence of you and your brand through video, community and allow the consumer to drive and capitalize on your content. The biggest mistake is being a 'chef cooking for other chefs and not the masses".

We want to project an image and allow those interested to participate. The point, although your brand is exclusive the internet is not. Those who want your brand can buy it directly from you, through a retailer or on the gray market; sacrificing margin tends to be a decision the brand makes by not building an interactive website that captures EVERYONE's attention and drives the most commerce through you!
