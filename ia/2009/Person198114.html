Richard Wise’s Summary

An inspirational award-winning Creative Director with a strategic business mind, delivering highly effective and award-winning creative solutions for a 50+ agency. Over 10 years experience working on top blue-chip B2B, B2C, integrated corporate comms and through-the-line accounts; presenting and pitching at the highest levels. Since 2007 - Creative Director at board level, heading up all major projects, directly responsible for the management and recruiting of a 22 strong creative team, mentoring developing and lifting the creative excellence of the company, in addition to winning a record number of awards over the last year. Prior to this - Associate Creative Director at The Forward Group (130+) and before; set up two smaller design agencies in London - lectured (UK and Europe), artist in residence and freelanced widely. EXPERIENCE: • Working on Global brands • Broad on-line experience • Extensive knowledge of pitching - concept to presentation • Running multi-lingual projects • Extensive personal contacts for top photographers, illustrators and designers • National and international commissioning • Implementing cost-saving initiatives across companies • Planning and overseeing the design direction of large numbers of creative’s and production teams • Ability to nurture and mentor, in order to maximise potential • Multi-disciplinary roots, from fine art, to teaching and design - digital, fashion, music, branding, corp-comms and publishing. LOOKING FOR: A top senior creative role within a highly creative agency. Hands-on, developing creative teams
or a company that needs its creative potential developed, added to and realized.
