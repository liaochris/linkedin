Stephanie Hinds’s Summary

Having gained a traineeship in a medium sized accounting firm straight out of high school i completed my degree part time at night. I moved quickly to preparing Small Businesses Financial Statements and helping them with their BAS reporting. A passion for Technology enabled me to work with business clients to ensure their reporting systems provided up to date numbers and tracked their business key drivers. It was this passion that inspired me to start Growthwise. My goal is to guide small - medium business owners through the maze of dealing with the pressures of growth, no matter where they are in their business journey.
