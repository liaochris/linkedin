Donald Herzog’s Summary

After receiving a BS degree in Technical Graphics, Don began working as a Technical Illustrator for The Boeing Company, first developing illustrations for military aircraft manuals, and later interactive computer systems. As his interest in multimedia grew, Don joined the Computer-Based Training team where he developed electronic courseware for maintenance and flight crews while completing his MA degree in Interactive Media Communications.

Don then accepted a Multimedia Specialist position with Capital One where he initially designed computer-based training for internal call center associates before helping the company move to web-based training for external call centers.

Recently, Don took advantage of an opportunity to pursue his long-time interest in Industrial Design and graduated magna cum laude with a BS degree from the University of Cincinnati's College of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning (DAAP).
