Karthik Chandramouli’s Summary

Karthik Chandramouli has driven global change initiatives across world-class organizations for more than 12 years. His accomplishments span operations, supply chain, product development, and corporate strategy, resulting in multi-million dollar cost savings, dramatic productivity improvements, and billion-dollar+ revenue growth.

Karthik began his career with Toyota where he led initiatives in strategic planning, e-business, and new venture creation, before being selected for an elite leadership team that localized strategy development from Japan to the US.

During his tenure with Toyota, Karthik became an expert in the Toyota Production System and when he moved to Motorola in 2005, he brought this exclusive expertise with him. Initially, he served as Director, Strategic Sourcing, where he facilitated hyper-growth in the mobile devices business by developing procurement risk management capabilities for the newly-formed Integrated Supply Chain. In this role, he completely transformed an under-performing team and created a strategic, multi-business group serving the entire procurement organization.

In 2007, Karthik was promoted into his current role as Director, Lean Transformation. As the global Lean Champion for Motorola, he is successfully implementing the Toyota Production System across 14 factories and distribution centers, spanning 15,000 employees in 10 countries.

Karthik graduated from the Harvard Kennedy School with a Master in Public Policy degree and was selected as a Woodrow Wilson Fellow.
