Shane Ostrowski’s Summary

Performance-focused and highly-organized professional with more than ten years of management experience. Effective in maximizing budgets of all sizes, setting goals and meeting deadlines. Skilled in recognizing and retaining talent, facilitating in-house training programs and leading others in the achievement of objectives. A career of notable political achievement and leadership in both Ohio and Virginia. Currently serving as Director of Government Affairs and lobbyist for Associated Builders and Contractors and promoting the merit shop industry.
