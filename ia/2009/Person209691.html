Sarah Hathorn, AICI CIP, CPBS’s Summary

Working as an executive for Federated Department Stores for over 20 years, I was in organizations all over training professional dress and within our internal structure I trained seminars such as team building, leadership skills, and DISC.

Work with an individual consultant with professional men and women to develop a more powerful and stylish image.

As a corporate image consultant and personal branding strategist, I provide corporate seminars on Professional Presence, What is Business Casual?, and Business and International Etiquette.

I also provide corporate workshops to better develop team rapport (DISC), developing long-term client relationships and leadership skills.

Sarah Hathorn, AICI CIP, CPBS’s Specialties:

Corporate organizations in need of enhancing their brand within a competitive industry.

Organizations that wish to invest in their people to gain more confidence, increase productivity, reduce turnover and create internal succession planning.

Individuals - Image coaching to elevate your presence through visual, verbal and non-verbal skills to create the best-version of yourself.
