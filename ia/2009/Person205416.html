Russell Bohannon’s Summary

Russell Bohannon is a results-driven marketing professional as well as an extremely fast learner. He has two Master's degrees (MBA and MS-MAS) with a cumulative 3.9 GPA. He fully embraces change and easily learns new skills when needed as the requirements of a job evolve over time.

His focus disciplines are Marketing Management and Product Management. He has gained extensive experience in all areas of the telecommunications industry, where he has worked with cellular, home, and business phone systems, as well as Internet and cable television. He has also worked for two advertising agencies, has worked in the software industry, and has more than five years of experience in restaurant operations.

To fill in gaps between contract jobs, Russell has worked on the set of several film projects, which include: a movie, a TV series, a reality show, and an infomercial.
