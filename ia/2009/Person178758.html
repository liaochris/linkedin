Omri Dolev’s Summary

Entrepreneur and digital visionary, business development expart with vast expirence, online media professional, digital music expert with massive cellular/mobile background.
Born and raised in Israel.
Omri has been involved in Internet ventures since 1999, specializing first in content media (was of founder of Ynet - Israel's leading news portal), online marketing methods, digital niche analysis and maximization, green/LOHAS and organic products (launched a chain of organic food stores in the US) digital marketing, mobile content, mobile music formats and promotional methods and old (TV) to new media transitions. Omri is a well known digital activist in the Israeli entrepreneur’s and VC arena.
He is presently leading the Lev Group Media - a privately held company mainly involved in digital music (web and mobile) in Israel and Eastern Europe. In Israel Lev Group is the exclusive rights holder of the Warner Music catalogue.
Omri is founder of YonYon - a web boutique focusing on turn key management with projects in South America, East Europe and Israel.
Omri is technology oriented and has combined his knowledge in content, online marketing, mobile, overall penetration strategies.
In the past Omri was of founding team chief editor and VP of Logiamobile - Israel's leading mobile content aggregator, was in charge of the digital re-launch of the Israeli sport channel web portal and was founder of NoveBox - the world's leading telenovela (south American soap opera) in Argentina.

Omri Dolev’s Specialties:

Business developement, Online content development, Online marketing methods, Mobile/cellular promotions and content development, Digital music formats and managment, Search engine market analysis, E-commerce platforms, Focused project managment, Green/Lohas, Organic food sector, SEO development.
