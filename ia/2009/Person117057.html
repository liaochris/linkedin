John F. Feilen’s Summary

John Feilen has successfully delivered complex initiatives in the interactive and emerging technologies spaces and financial sector. John has the ability to take a concept from initial stages, illustrate it for business partners and clients and work in a highly organized fashion to manage to a successful implementation.

Interactive initiatives include managing the design and implementation of eCommerce, online bill presentation and payment and an eCRM portal with a detachable navigation bar for sprint.com. More recently was the design and development of an eHealth portal delivering healthcare services to the patient in their home, including video conference appointments with their healthcare professional. Most recent project; John designed and built his new online portfolio; the one you are currently viewing. He also led the implementation of the Blue Martini eCommerce platform (sprint.com) as well as various other enterprise level application development projects for Sprint, The Chicago Board of Trade and Northern Trust Bank.

Emerging technology projects include leading the design and market testing of a smart home virtual butler (R&D for Sprint) featuring a voice user interface, natural language processing and data mining. Also John designed and delivered a conceptual prototype of the ultimate smart home of the future delivering media, data and user control over an IP network. Product management includes strategic planning, market research and following industry trends.

John shares his exploration of the intersection of technology, creativity and the user experience in his blog focusing on emerging technologies in the interactive and smart home spaces. Discussed are concepts, applications and products, which have changed (or will change) how we live and which we adapt as part of our daily vernacular.
Read John’s blog: http://newtechnoloiesnow.blogspot.com

Full CV available upon request: john@john-fred.com.
