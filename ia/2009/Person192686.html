Rajesh Taneja’s Summary

President of a Venture Technology firm, currently focused on running two business units within our portfolio, the technology services arm of the company and the social network, Urban Mixer.

The Company's primary mandate is to broaden its portfolio by uniting with outstanding entrepreneurs who need to fill technological gaps within their own organisations. In exchange for leadership, expertise and technology services, SSID Inc. may take a position in the client's company. We prefer to work with emerging entrepreneurs or early-stage organisations.
