John Newton’s Summary

I am a medical writer familiar with all aspects of pharmaceutical publications from preclinical and discovery, through clinical development, to pivotal phase 3 trials. Likewise, I understand the publication planning process that builds up to product approval and launch.

In terms of regulatory writing and submissions, I have played many roles in the creation and updating of NDAs, INDs, Orphan Drug Applications, as well as Investigator Brochures (IBs).

I uphold the strongest ethical standards regarding Good Publication Practices, both in terms of fair balance and presenting data that has undergone the rigors of quality assurance and control. On a personal level, I find a great degree of satisfaction in working with doctors and scientists in preparing their work for publication.

John Newton’s Specialties:

Scientific and medical abstracts, posters, and presentations; clinical protocols, clinical study reports (CSRs), regulatory submissions; preclinical and technical reports; SOP and template development; all copyediting, copywriting, and document design.
