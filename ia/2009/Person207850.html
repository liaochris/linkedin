Sam Horn’s Summary

Executive Book Summaries said I have "added to the legacy of ideas on dealing with people left by Abraham Lincoln, Benjamin Franklin and Dale Carnegie."

I have spoken to more than half a million people around the world and for such organizations as Young Presidents Organization, Fortune 500 Forum, National Governors Association, ASAE and Lockheed Martin.

My work has been featured in Investors Business Daily, Washington Post, Smart Money, Reader's Digest, Chicago Tribune and on MSNBC BusinessWeek.com, NPR, and on Jay Leno's Tonight Show.

In the past ten years, I have had the pleasure of helping hundreds of consulting clients crystallize Congruent, Compelling, Commercially-viable intellectual capital that has helped them get book deals, develop signature keynotes, craft trademarkable brand names, and position themselves as one-of-a-kind topic experts that catapulted their professional success.

Clients have ranged from NASA's former top astrophysicist to the director of the Disney Institute to former Presidents of ICF, NSA, and ASTD. Clients have also included homemakers, teachers, attorneys, non-profit executives, CEO's, and many speakers, authors, and consultants who have a valuable message they want to share on the page, stage, and online.

My presentation topics include:

- POP! Your Business

- POP! Your Communication

- Tongue Fu! What to say - when you don't know what to say

- ConZentrate; Focus for Success

- Got CLOUT? Increase Influence, Respect, and Results

- I Can't Believe I Wrote the Whole Thing
