Lars Asbjornsen’s Summary

Online marketing professional with fifteen years of comprehensive Internet business management experience including strategy formulation and execution, program management, business development, strategic partnering, contract negotiations, and leadership of cross-functional teams. Deep understanding and passion for customer-centered strategy and design in the context of developing company value propositions and pragmatic go-to-market strategies.
