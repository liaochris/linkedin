Maurizio Turatti’s Summary

I am a software integration architect with long term international and multicultural experience. My main focus is on service oriented architectures, business and technical integration, software design patterns, highly available distributed systems, agile project management and development, team building and team leadership, product technical management and development.

I like to stay on the leading edge of new technologies and participate to continuous learning environments. I can play different roles, from strategic work to actual coding, as needed. I think a good architect is first a very good developer, so I still love writing working software myself, experimenting new tools directly.
