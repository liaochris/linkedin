Aaron Danker’s Summary

I am a graduate of Savannah College of Art and Design with a Computer Art B.F.A Degree with 10+ years of full-time job experience in graphical user instructional design, interface design, program direction, and project management.

In my employment history, I have worked with Wheeler, Inc. of Chicago, IL for 1.5 years as the webmaster/ internet consultant. I was responsible for maintaining client websites and network configurations using Novell for Small Business systems. At HomeSide Lending, Inc. of Jacksonville, FL I was the Online Content Specialist involved with maintaining a consistent branding for Consumer/ Customer Service departments. I was also in charge of gathering monthly trends, customer feedback, and developing internal intranet applications.

After Homeside, Inc. was purchased I moved onto Pixelphish Interactive of Savannah, GA where I am currently employed as their Project Manager, 3D animator, and Web. I am responsible for Illustrative Online and Offline classroom Courseware for FlightSafety International as a primary client of the companies. I am also involved in GUI design/development of client web applications using Flash and PHP/MySQL databases. I have experience in all software titles of Adobe and Macromedia, but use Illustrator and Flash extensively for design layout and animation. I have been with Pixelphish, Interactive for a over 7 years, but seeking a fresh new start with a position that can offer some expansion, growth, and a competitive outlook for the future. I like to challenge myself in everyday aspects of life and the same applies to my work ethic. I would like to expand my knowledge and challenge my expertise and push my career in Interactive Learning or 3D Graphics and Animation.
