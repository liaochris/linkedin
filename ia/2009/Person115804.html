Joe Loudermilk’s Summary

During the course of my professional career I have had the opportunity to manage architectural and construction projects in both the public and private sectors. In the public sector, I have been both the Project Architect and Construction Manager on a number of secondary school projects in the Campbell Union High School District in Campbell, CA. Those projects included three $4 million ground-up 10,000 square foot science buildings as well as classroom modernization programs. In the private sector, I have worked as Project Architect and Construction Manager. My largest project was a $12 million single structure 250,000 square foot ground-up, mixed-use project consisting of a 200 room hotel, 10,000 square foot retail tenant shell space, twenty-eight unit apartment complex and a 300 car parking facility.

Over much of the last fifteen years of my career, I have been with companies such as Disney and The Gap Inc. as well as running my own consulting firm providing both senior management services and project management services for new store roll-out construction across the United States and Canada. During this period, I project-managed the construction of over 150 new stores throughout North America, led the development and re-organization of retailers’ construction documents, analyzed and developed processes and procedures for new store construction rollout for a retailer’s property development group, and developed database applications with MS Access and Excel for tracking contract administration, schedules, budgeting, change order management, punch list management and project close-out.

On all projects, I have monitored costs closely, maintained construction schedule adherence, and built strong lasting relationships with clients, end users, general contractors and vendors for successful turnovers.
