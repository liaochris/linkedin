Christopher Poulsen’s Summary

As a farm boy from Idaho, I knew about as much as the potato fields that surrounded me growing up. I was taught from a young age the value of hard work, the principle of the law of harvest, and many other valuable lessons that would help me forever. Still today my parents are hardworking people who uphold strong morals and values within our family. They helped me a great deal become who I am today. Because I was raised in a large family we never got to leave Idaho very often. Then the time came when I would journey to a new land, Sacramento, California to experience a whole new world. I lived there for two years involved in a very unique setting and intermixed with a lot of different nationalities. My eyes were definitely opened to the "real world." I learned of the endless opportunities that were available to anyone and everyone. Upon my arrival back home, my brother invited me to attend a business event where C.S. Hughes was one of the main speakers. He spoke about the need for people to take charge of their destiny and develop themselves to help attract like-minded individuals into their business. His story inspired me and I knew what he was saying held truth. After he wrote the following in a book I had: “Personal Development is key!” Romans 12:2, I decided to take action and buy a few self help books to put what he said to the test. Think and Grow Rich was my first purchase and I have come to the realization that I am the author of my own destiny and only I can be a roadblock for personal progression. I consider this a huge blessing and thank my Heavenly Father for allowing me to keep an open mind. I am grateful to C.S. Hughes as well for his example of sharing this wisdom with others. I look forward to each new day by planting new seeds of wisdom in my own mind as well as reaching out to others. I love thinking of the journey that unfolds daily and hope to continue on the path that has been laid out before me. What a blessing indeed!
