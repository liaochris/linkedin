Sheena Hardy’s Summary

Over 15 years international experience developing new business championing the most advanced broadcast technology solutions and platforms with Arqiva, SysMedia, DigiSoft.tv, ICTV now ActiveVideoNetworks, NDS and MagiNet Interactive now DoCoMo interTouch.
Began media career 1990-1995 in TV advertising and commercial production working with the worlds top brands. In 1996 moved into hospitality Pay-TV and the broadcast sector in 2000. Further to working closely with broadcasters and Pay-TV operators all over the world in TV service distribution over multi devices, now focused on the UK market, helping drive Arqiva's commercial plans to a) win differentiating content on the Freeview platform and b) developing collaborations with key partners to launch an all new hybrid DTT box to enable next generation TV in Freeview homes in the UK.
