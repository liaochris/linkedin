Patrick Ryan’s Summary

Lawyer with technical project-management background and extensive international experience. Licensed to practice law in California, New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania and Colorado.

Patrick Ryan’s Specialties:

Technology Law, Private International Law, Educational Administration

Patrick Ryan’s Experience

  • Partner

    PRMM Inmobiliaria S.A. de C.V.

    (International Trade and Development industry)

    November 2006 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    PRMM invests in purchase/leaseback properties for multinationals that are expanding into Mexico. In addition, PRMM provides consulting services for foreigners who wish to invest in Latin America.

  • Assistant Professor Adjunct

    University of Colorado at Boulder, Interdisciplinary Telecommunications Program

    (Educational Institution; 5001-10,000 employees; Higher Education industry)

    August 2004 — Present (5 years 1 month)

    From 2005-2008, held the position of Faculty Director (Chair) and Scholar in Residence of the Interdisciplinary Telecommunications Program, and as the senior academic officer, oversaw the curricular development of all academic modules, the coordination of 17 faculty members, supervision of $1.5m in tuition revenue and and the academic oversight over 160 students seeking an MS degree in telecommunications. The program experienced a tripling in enrollment during my oversight. Currently focus primarily on separate law practice but hold appointment as Assistant Professor Adjunct.

  • Managing Partner

    PSR lawfirm, llc

    (Law Practice industry)

    May 2004 — Present (5 years 4 months)

    I handle a variety of legal issues in my practice, with an emphasis on telecommunications law, regulatory compliance, government relations, zoning, right-of-way deployment and private international law. Licensed in California, New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania and Colorado (based in Denver).

  • Lecturer and Graduate Assistant

    Katholieke Universiteit Leuven (Belgium)

    (Higher Education industry)

    March 2002 — July 2004 (2 years 5 months)

    Worked full time at this university while completing my PhD. During this time, I lectured on topics of international telecommunications law, comparative law, and standardization. Invited as Visiting Professor in 2008.

  • Director of Network Implementation

    Broadnet Europe, SPRL (Belgium)

    (Telecommunications industry)

    June 2001 — October 2002 (1 year 5 months)

    Supervised the build-out and, in several cases, the sale and merger of various WLL networks in Germany, Switzerland, Spain, Czech Republic and France.

  • Director of Operations

    Tetra Tech Communications, Inc.

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    July 1994 — June 2001 (7 years)

    First job after graduation from college, "learned the ropes" of telecommunication siting project management as a leasing, zoning and construction manager in various locations. Ultimately supervised deployment of various wireless networks in the United States, Canada, Brazil and several countries in Europe. Managed various aspects of network buildouts for new-entrants into wireless such as Nextel, Comcast (Pennsylvania and Brazil), Algar (Brazil), Rogers Cantel (Canada), Broadnet (Europe). In 2000, appointed as Managing Director of the European operations, responsible for the founding and general management of the TetraTech's German subsidiary, TTC Global, GmbH.
