Drew Sygit’s Summary

The number one challenge facing consumers in the selection of a mortgage lender, is knowing who to trust. The conflicting information one is bombarded with today - can be overwhelming.

There’s a very simple way for a consumer to handle this challenge – question the credentials of those you speak with & what they tell you. If you’re unsure what to ask, we can send you a document with relatively straight forward financial questions to assist you. You’d expect someone handling such large financial transactions to know the answers, but you’ll be surprised by the number of self-proclaimed “experts” who don’t have a clue.

As one of a select few Certified Mortgage & Equity Planners in the state of Michigan, we strive to answer important questions you may not even know to ask. We ask our clients to complete a questionnaire to assist us in better serving their needs. After all, it doesn't matter how much & what we know, unless it's important to you.

A mortgage is typically the largest financial transaction you’ll ever make and selecting the wrong mortgage plan, or not having one at all, can lead to financial ruin. Google “Predatory Lending” or “Mortgage Fraud” and you may be shocked. Ever heard the saying 'penny-wise, but dollar-foolish"? Well many make a similar mistake when selecting a lender & mortgage product.

We believe now, more than ever, a mortgage should be used as a dynamic financial tool, personalized to compliment your short & long-term financial plans to secure your financial future & that of your family.
