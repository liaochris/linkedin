Ralph Finaldi’s Summary

President's Biography

Ralph Finaldi is one of today’s most influential design professionals. As founder, President and Chief Creative Officer of Novo Design, he has transformed the image of countless companies and brands delivering fresh, imaginative, and distinctive identities. As a creative director, his innovative work has fueled his reputation as one of the East Coast’s preeminent leaders in modern corporate marketing.

Mr. Finaldi and his team of designers masterfully work in a diverse range of categories; print, packaging, interactive, online, event marketing and retail branding. Clients have included internationally known consumer brands such as BMW, Pepsi and Lipton, business-to-business brands such as Dow Jones, and emerging companies such as Grandview Partners. Over the last 15 years, Mr. Finaldi and his team have produced a groundbreaking body of work, elevating the profile of his clients and contributing to extraordinary bottom-line results.

As visionary as he is talented, Ralph Finaldi is committed to mentoring young artists, and creating a forum for the debate of new ideas in design. He presides over ongoing design research projects and lectures extensively on the power of art and design to transform business. His work has been consistently recognized by his peers resulting in the industry’s most coveted design awards including those from ADCNJ, ADCNY, GD:USA, TELLY, PRINT MAGAZINE, and HOW MAGAZINE.

Based in the picturesque community of Red Bank, New Jersey, Ralph Finaldi leads Novo in an energetic, independent, creative atmosphere outside of the lockstep uniformity that arises in major metropolitan centers. Experience, talent, and perspective, translate into highly-original and compelling work for Novo clients.

Ralph Finaldi’s Honors:

Ralph has won scores of awards in nearly every creative category in almost every major awards show in the country including: ADCNJ, ADCNY, The American Graphic Design Awards, TELLY, HOW Magazine, Print Magazine.

Ralph served as Vice President of The Board of Directors and has been honored by SICA (The Shore Institute for The Contemporary Arts), a non-profit organization, for his role in providing graphic design services along with his marketing expertise including donating more than $50k in creative services. His dedication, expert design & marketing and generosity was honored by SICA with an invitation-only dinner part at Branches in West Long Branch, NJ.
