Alistair Dabbs’s Summary

- print publishing and editorial production specialist
- freelance magazine editor, writer and book author
- journalism teacher and publishing technology trainer

Over the years I have worked in every editorial and production role in magazine publishing, including editor-in-chief, chief sub, toilet cleaner and ad production (in order of remuneration). Freelancing since 1994, I worked on all the Mac magazines and all the PC magazines at one time or another, as well as in-flight mags, snow-sports titles and partworks (from planes and tractors to Japanese anime), while teaching sub-editing at the London School of Publishing and co-authoring a variety of tech books.

The 2009/10 edition of 'The Print Manager's Handbook' is out now from Unity Publishing (www.unity-publishing.co.uk).

I am currently two years into a rolling contract for delivering Adobe/Atex editorial training at Associated Newspapers.

Bento Media Limited is a creative all-media joint venture offering writing, editing, layout, digital imaging, prepress production, live interpreting, translation, editorial training and language training services.
