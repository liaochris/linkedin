Alessandro Cirinei’s Summary

My professional experience is mainly focused on Internet and the publishing business. After a long period in Sales with various management roles I moved to Marketing and Business management, co-ordinating a team of product managers. I have a deep knowledge of the online classified business and launched several new products, both online and print. Recently I created my own company (Xool) which is focused on internet & business consultancy for the SME through which I hold various roles for ADS Media group (based in Florence) and Buga Internet SL (based in Barcelona - Spain)
