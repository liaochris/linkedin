Martijn van der Meulen’s Summary

Martijn has been working in the gaming industry for over six years. Within his professional career he has been credited on over forty-five different titles on a wide variety of game platforms.

He is best known for his role as Creative Producer on PlayStation Home within Sony’s London Studio and previously as Producer on the multi-million selling The Simpsons Game, a third-person action game featuring an original storyline from the writers of the Emmy Award-winning animated series. Additionally he is the founder of two online media agencies which specialised in designing and building online community websites.

He manages creative direction and provides guidance to selected 1st and 3rd party development teams in order to produce AAA interactive environments for PlayStation Home. Additionally he manages project planning & design for a series of internally commissioned Home spaces and he takes care of external Home development in Europe.
