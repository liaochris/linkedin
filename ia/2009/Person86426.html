Daniel Gieber’s Summary

Quantum Solution offers complete life cycle recruiting services, customized to meet the specialized needs of each client. We formulate then execute a recruitment strategy to deliver interested and qualified candidates to the hiring managers desktop. And we do it in a way that adds value to each step.

We integrate all internal and external channels of candidate sources into one seamless, coherent, easy-to-use system of candidate tracking and response management.

We are focused to extract the maximum potential from each candidate channel to create and retain this high-value resource for each every client. Our approach is focused to harvest all potential value from every step and candidate interaction.

The benefits of our service;

1. Deliver rapid, high quality, low cost talent acquisition,
2. Systematize a more highly efficient, effective recruitment process,
3. Create a private talent community to meet future hiring requirements.

Daniel Gieber’s Specialties:

Executive level search to multi-functional staffing services designed to meet today’s demand for low cost high value talent acquisition. Turn-key solution includes ATS, resume mining tools, personal network, HR & Recruitment processes.
