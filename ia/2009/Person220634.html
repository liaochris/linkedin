Teresa Lee’s Summary

Throughout her career, Teresa has been known as a quick learner, self-starter, and dedicated worker. She has demonstrated a strong ability to handle a variety of tasks effectively and deliver on deadlines. Her diverse interactive media and technical experience encompasses Web Development, Content Management, Project Management, Online Marketing, Content Strategy, and Data Analysis. Due to her Business, Marketing, and Technical background, she is competent in managing a variety of projects.

Originally hired as a Project Coordinator at Blast Radius, her diligence and organizational skills were recognized, and within 3 months of employment she was given Project Management responsibilities. As Project Manager, she oversees various creative and technical based projects while coordinating multi-disciplinary teams of 5-10 people and managing budgets of $200 to $500K.

At Air Canada, as Senior Web Producer, Teresa processed content updates, administered the content management system and coordinated content creation and localization. This required collaborating with internal clients, international offices, and vendors in defining business needs and translating them into creative online solutions. One of her primary duties was to manage large scale email campaigns which had a subscriber base of 2 million customers and generated monthly revenues of over $1 million. She was also a key team member of the development of the award-winning online service, the Flight Pass. She consulted on the design, content strategy and user interaction, and managed the technical and marketing collateral.

In addition to Teresa’s technical and business confidence, she has excellent interpersonal skills. Her personal development education is evident in her pursuant of the PMP certification, and taking courses in digital strategy and business analysis. Her unique skills-set enhances her abilities to effectively identify and meet client requirements in a professional and efficient manner.
