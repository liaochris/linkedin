Gregory Gallagher’s Summary

International bestselling travel author and photojournalist working with publications like National Geographic Traveler, Smithsonian, AAA Living Magazine, Canadian Geographic Magazine, American Airlines in-flight Latitudes Magazine, Ulysse Magazine from Paris, Asian Sources publications (Hong Kong), Explore Magazine (South American airlines TACA), Better Nutrition Magazine, Doctors Review, Americas Magazine, Dreamscapes (Globe & Mail) and many more.

As one of the original writers of the Sesame Street Television Show, and as a script reader with Hollywood's William Carroll Agency, I have had the opportunity to work inside the entertainment industry with the best in the world.

I have worked on media projects in a one-on-one basis with Quincy Jones, Lena Horne, Oscar Peterson, Gene Kelly, Mickey Rooney, Roberta Flack, Duke Ellington, Count Basie, Jose Feliciano, George Carlin, Muddy Waters, Willie Dixon, Steve Allen, and many more.

My experience covers every type of media format and spans the globe with satisfied clients. One of my new clients, Explore TACA Magazine from South America has run one of my stories in their in-flight magazine about luxury ecotourism in Costa Rica for 2007. Ulysse Magazine from Paris meanwhile, is running another of my stories about Canouan Island in the Caribbean.

I am expanding my writing assignments to include corporate and government projects as well. New clients in this new territory include the Minister of Tourism Québec, Grenadines Estate Villas, Côté Nord Tremblant, VIA Rail Canada, and Barbados Tourism.

Gregory Gallagher’s Specialties:

International editorial project management, specialty brand development and media program creation and implementation.
