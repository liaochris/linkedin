About Fidelis BioPharm
Headquartered in Dallas, we are a forward thinking biopharmaceutical search firm comprised of experienced industry recruiters and account managers specializing in both permanent and contract placement with companies of every size. Executive search offerings include both contingency as well as retained search services to fulfill a wide variety of recruiting needs.

We provide staffing solutions to the Pharmaceutical and Biotechnology industries, maintaining our niche in Regulatory Affairs, Clinical Research and Development, including Pharmacovigilance/Drug Safety, Medical Affairs and Marketing recruitment.

For more information on current opportunities, working with me or my firm, or conditions in the marketplace, please feel free to contact me at cj@fidelisbiopharm.com.
