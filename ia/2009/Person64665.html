Donald James’s Summary

Full life-cycle recruiter with extensive Hardware & Software expertise. I know this sounds vague and general, but basically what this means, hiring managers, is that I'm not going to send you a UI Designer for a Front-End Web Development position.

On the flip side, if you're a job seeker with 15 years of Power Management design experience @ the IC-level, I'm not going to try and get you connected with a Front-End ASIC job.

In short, I make it happen. My goals? To find YOU your next position!

ESPECIALLY contact me if (updated 8/19/2009):

*You're a Lead Engineer/hands-on Development Manager with strong PHP development experience, and a web 2.0 background.

*You've got strong Ruby on Rails experience, preferably in a consumer-facing internet environment.

*You're a SW Engineer coming out of Facebook or Google, with a CS degree from a top-tier academic institution, and are interested in joining one of the hottest consumer-facing search startups in the Bay Area (strong LAMP or Python development experience doesn't hurt either!)

*You're a Web Application Developer with a CS degree from a well respected academic institution, with Facebook or Open Social development experience. Technological language expertise is not important. Must be living in SF or willing to commute there on a daily basis. 1-5 years experience is ideal.

*You have a strong background in Machine Learning/Information Retrieval/Data Mining/Search Relevance/NLP, have startup experience, and live in or are willing to commute to SF on a daily basis. Again, language is not as important as the ability to develop and implement new and exciting ideas. PhD's are welcome!

I've got more HOT startups looking to hire, but a great deal of them seem to be looking for candidates with either one of these types of backgrounds. Even if neither of these backgrounds look like yours, contact me anyways!
