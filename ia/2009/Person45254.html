Chris Hooper’s Summary

I am about to complete my Bachelor of Commerce, majoring in Accounting and minoring in Applied Finance with Uni SA. Prior to that I was awarded a Diploma of Business from SAIBT.

Following that I intend to complete the Chartered Accountants post graduate diploma. I am also considering post grad Finance. Additionally I plan on taking the Company Directors program with the Australian Institute of Company Directors, I am looking to get Fellowship there in the next four years.

I currently work at Brentnalls SA as an accountant. I am in business services accounting an advisory.

I am a director of Hooper Govan Captital. The company is a platform enabling us to invest in pre-seed ventures, trade listed derivatives and general equities market research.

I am one of the founders of The Start Up Club; it is a support community and think tank for young entrepreneurs, practicing professionals and academics

I serve on a variety of different company and not for profit boards in both an executive and non executive capacity. I am also a member of several other professional bodies.

I don't live to work, nor do I work to live. I work to create, to innovate, to inspire.
