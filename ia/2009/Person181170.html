Parker Thomas’s Summary

I am a tenacious product innovator, manager, and entrepreneur with more than 15 years of success in cleantech / greentech, business leadership, problem solving, external resource control, market trends forecasting, business development and planning, product development, profitable solutions, market and customer needs, responsive products and features, strategic alliances, business relationships, and creating the structure to support new businesses.

I am looking for a new opportunity in the cleantech or tech spaces with these criteria:
- A business with enough market potential to interest angel and venture funders.
- A business that will make a material difference in the world. I would like to change the environment and/or people's lives for the better.
- The opportunity to work with a bright, capable, energetic and enthusiastic team of equals or the opportunity to work with an experienced entrepreneur from whom I can learn.
- A business that can scale to a national or international level.
- A business with clear competive differentiators.
- A business that fits my moral and ethical beliefs.
