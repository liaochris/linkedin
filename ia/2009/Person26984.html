Bernard Gutnick’s Summary

For over 15 years, I have stayed focused on communications industry applications and technologies. I am presently managing product marketing for the fastest growing business communication systems provider in the world.

I have had the pleasure of managing the development of wireless handset software applications for 4G IMS network operator TerreStar Networks, having provided Technology consultation services to them in the past. Extremely rewarding to know this unique terrestrial/satellite solution will save lives in the future

Previously, I have been Vice President of Product Marketing for Sylantro Systems, and have held positions in product development, product management and marketing and direct sales of communications solutions.

I feel very fortunate to have such a fine network of people I have worked for or worked with, and will probably do both again in the years to come! Nice guys finish first. Believe it!

Bernard Gutnick’s Specialties:

My area of expertise is in business communications applications. In addition, I have extensive experience in product marketing programs formulation and execution. There is nothing more exciting than getting in front of an audience of 1,000 people and telling them why!
