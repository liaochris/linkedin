Larry Roos, MBA’s Summary

Larry is an innovative and strategic leader with a proven track record driving competitive advantage in a variety of industries (Hospitality, Foodservice, Retail, Merchandise, Manufacturing, Distribution and Healthcare).

Currently, Larry is a strategic business consultant for a Medical Services company.

Prior to this, he was Vice President, Information Technology for Disney ($38B) - leading a $284M transformation initiative called Destination Disney at Parks & Resorts (Team size under Larry was 200 IT professionals). Projects that Larry was accountable for at Disney generated over $350M in incremental annual Operating Income contribution.

Larry was CIO at 2 different companies previously. SVP, CIO for American Media ($400M) and before that CIO, VP MIS & Logistics for Restaurant Services Inc (Burger King's Purchasing Company).

His first job out of college was at $3B Harris Corporation Corporate Headquarters and later worked in their Semiconductor Manufacturing sector.

Larry earned his Master's in Business Administration and Bachelor's in Computer Science.
