  • System Development Department, Head

    Harita Genel Komutanlığı (National Mapping Agency of Turkey)

    (Government Agency; 501-1000 employees; HGK; Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 2001 — June 2006 (5 years 2 months)

    Leading a six person software team he worked as the project manager & project member where he designed and developed a standalone GIS software called as SAHADASU (Digital Map Supported Military Application) for the internal usage by Turkish Armed Forces (TAF) in order that all the military units in the field wouldn’t need any GIS software, DBMS and printed maps but SAHADASU. Microsoft ®-Visual Studio, Delphi ®, ESRI® -MapObjects, Installshield-X were the tools he used for that project. During that project he also initiated the 1:25 K topographic vector database project for the General Command of Mapping. During that project he had the experiences with database design using UML tools (i.e. Microsoft Visio & Rational Rose), database administration (ORACLE & SDE), ESRI® -ArcObject programming and project management using Microsoft® Project, as well.

  • System Development Section, Head

  • System Analysis & Design Officer

    Harita Genel Komutanlığı (National Mapping Agency)

    (Government Agency; 501-1000 employees; HGK; Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 1992 — April 1998 (5 years 8 months)

    There are a number of projects lead by Dr.Tastan during that period of time. One of the exciting projects he worked on was the design and implementation of topographic database (incl. creation of a feature and attribute coding catalogue) at 1:25 K using MGE (Modular – later Microstation - GIS Environment) Software by INTERGRAPH ®. Another important project he worked on was the design and implementation of an AML (ESRI ® -Arc Macro Language) based system for vector data acquisition from satellite (SPOT) orthorectified imagery within the frame of an international GIS program (VMAP Level-1) to develop a worldwide geospatial vector database at level one that corresponds to a data content and accuracy of printed maps at 1:250 K. Those projects gave him the opportunity to practice in developing his geospatial programming skills in C & AML as well as in ortorectification & block adjustment of stereo satellite imagery using the software tool “Softplotter ®”.

  • Project Officier in Geodesy

    Harita Genel Komutanlığı (National Mapping Agency of Turkey)

    (Government Agency; 501-1000 employees; HGK; Information Technology and Services industry)

His stint at Geodesy Department helped him develop a fundamentally strong understanding of global point positioning, especially ground control network adjustment at national level.He also developed strong programming skills in FORTRAN. It also inspired him to pursue an academic major in the evolving field of the geospatial information technology.
