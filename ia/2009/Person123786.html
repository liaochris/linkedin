Julie Fraser’s Summary

I am a life coach and trainer with a broad range of experience to share with you coming from over 20 years in the Finance Industry. I have been in various roles of advice giving and people development in Australia, UK, Europe, Middle East and Isle of Man!

My goal is to create more passion purpose and fun in the lives of those that I touch by sharing my experience and knowledge in a practical down to earth way. My warm and friendly approach enables me to communicate easily with people from all walks of life, which really strengthens and supports the results people achieve from working with me.

If you or your teams are looking to achieve any of the following then contact me NOW to discuss possible ideas and strategies to get you started:

- improve and hone your technical knowledge and people skills 
- have higher levels of self esteem 
- increase your confidence and communication skills 
- achieve career success 
- be more productive 
- develop and grow your business
