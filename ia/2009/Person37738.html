Carlos Gil’s Summary

Seven years ago Carlos Gil,a Florida native,was selling shoes as a part-time job at a mall in Fort Lauderdale. Since then,Carlos has spent the last several years working in Branch and Client Management positions for Fortune 500 corporations such as American International Group (AIG), Citigroup and Regions Bank. After years of hard work, dedication and professional development Carlos Gil is now the President and CEO of CXG Group LLC based out of Jacksonville, FL.

At age 25, Carlos has already made a successful name for himself while representing the world's largest financial corporations as an employee. Through his own consulting firm, Carlos is now making an even greater impact in Corporate America by offering consulting and recruiting solutions to corporations, organizations and individuals nationwide. Through CXG Group, Carlos' goal in 2009 is to help Americans find employment and help end the economic recession which led to millions of employment layoffs in 2008, including his own. As an entrepreneur Carlos hopes to become an inspiration and motivation to all of America. Through his leadership and dedication to career growth and professional development, his passion has always been to be a resource and mentor to others. Carlos is living proof that you can achieve and succeed in corporate America at the largest companies despite age or level of education.

As of late 2008, after being downsized by AIG, Carlos Gil launched CXG Group's newest division and initiative related to providing a useful resource to all Americans in spite of the economy, recession and employment crisis in the form of JobsDirectUSA.com. Through collaboration and support from major Fortune 500 corporations, the media and every American nationwide, Carlos is enthusiastic to help America and Americans rebound in 2009 through his campaign of "Saving America One Job At A Time". Please join in supporting our mission by registering at JobsDirectUSA.com today.
