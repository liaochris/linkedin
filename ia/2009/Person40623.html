Chad Collett’s Summary

I am a results oriented, high energy leader with solid business ethics, excellent communication skills and proven problem solving skills. I pride myself on being a strategic but pragmatic thinker that can quickly contribute to a company’s growth and success. My professional peers consider me to be a highly organized individual with the ability to adapt to the management of simultaneous activities that arise in a fast-paced environment.

Experience working in both Business-to-Business (B2B) and Business-to-Consumer (B2C) environments.

In addition to sales and marketing, I have been involved in operations, accounting and human resources which allows me to bring more the table from a variety of perspectives.

With marketing and communications as my passion, I have experience in many areas including (but of course not limited to) planning and strategy, advertising, branding and brand management, product development, CRM, public relations, direct and dimensional mail and online marketing .

My goal for 2009 is to become a social media marketing guru end educate not only my company but small to mid-size companies nationwide to the value that social media can bring them in growing their business.
