Paul Hunter’s Summary

Simple HR will enable you to maximise the return on your human assets - individually, corporately and financially.

Our mission is to maximise the return on your salary investment and to remove any financial risk. We do this by getting to know you, your employees and your business, working with you to harness the power of your people.

Your people individually and collectively deliver your business objectives. At a strategic, tactical and operational level, we develop innovative HR solutions to attract and retain the highest calibre people; to manage them effectively by developing them in line with your corporate strategy; and to create the right environment to enable your people to consistently perform at their highest level.

Do it right. Keep it Simple.

Paul Hunter’s Specialties:

We offer comprehensive Human Resource solutions covering every aspect of the employment relationship and we tailor exceptional people management solutions to meet the business needs of all those with whom we work.

We specialise in recruitment, management, engagement, release and legal compliance.
