Christopher Akins ’s Summary

I am an experienced senior manager and entrepreneur having worked for two large global organizations while building a successful training and consulting business. Presently I am a SCM Director for a small startup renewable energy company. My skills and background include:

* Supply Chain: Experience includes negotiating and managing Network Procurement supplier relationships, Continuous Improvement programs, strategic sourcing, project sourcing and capital sourcing.
* Project Management: Over 30000 hours project experience as project manager, project procurement specialist, and sub contracts specialist. I have been involved in a variety of different types of projects and teams, including sourcing strategy development, supplier selection, and management of high value projects in the Oil & Gas, Marine, Construction, Mining and Renewable Energy industries. During my last assignment as a Navy officer at the Defense Institute for Security Assistance Management I was the automation project manager responsible for the development of the first command intranet.
* International experience: I have led international teams both within Rolls-Royce and Alcoa Inc. While with Rolls-Royce I lived in the UK for over 4 years as a commodity manager leading global strategic sourcing teams. As a Global Commodity Manager with Alcoa, Inc. led a team of category managers in the US, Mexico and the UK .
* Negotiations: Experienced negotiator of high value contracts in the Oil & Gas, Marine, Construction, Aerospace, Mining and Renewable Energy industries. I have led negotiation teams as well as been a team member in major negotiation planning and execution.

In summary, I am a highly motivated and qualified professional. As my civilian experience and my experience as an officer in the U.S. Navy reflects, I am a skilled presenter and influencer at all levels of an organization, and have developed a broad business base, working in multiple industries and functions.
