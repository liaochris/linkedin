Amore Leighton Black’s Summary

Amore Leighton Black, is an active member of the film industry as well as a talented performing artist.

Amore enjoys the process of developing and identifying the complexities and nuances of characters and bringing them from words on a page to a real life person.

Amore is also the Founder & organizer of The New York Film Exposure Network. Her network consists of 810 film industry professionals

Every 6 - 8 weeks, Amore organizes and hosts ( Lights, Camera, Exposure!) a film industry networking event. The RSVP list for this event is usually from 147 to 275 guests.
For more info please visit. http://www.meetup.com/thenewyorkfilmexposurenetwork-com/
