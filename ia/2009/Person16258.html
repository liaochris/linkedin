Pramod Sahoo’s Summary

Trained by the veteran Russian animation directors and designers of “Just you wait” fame, and with 12+ years of experience in various Indian and International Projects I have taken up creative responsibilities and challenges in top Indian animation and gaming studios. My short films and projects have been nominated at various international film festivals in California, Italy Rio-de-Janeiro, France and Germany. In 2004 I was hired by the World Bank to help setting up an animation facility in Bangladesh. At NASSCOM 2007 Jan I was selected as one of the six finalists for Super pitch. As a producer recently I have delivered The dancing with the stars PC-game with a team of 40 artist and programmers.I look forward to be an integral part of the animation and gaming Industry
