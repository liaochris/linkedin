Joleen Deatherage’s Summary

SHOESTRING CREATIVE GROUP

As a Senior Project Director for Shoestring Creative Group, I bring the resources and best practices of a national, full-service marketing communications firm to my clients - while giving them the one-on-one attention that a consultant provides.

My diverse business experience encompasses such sectors as entertainment, education, government, business, finance (film funding), and venture philanthropy - allowing me to guide nonprofits in shifting their perspectives toward entrepreneurial thinking.

By mixing business savvy with effective storytelling, I help organizations clarify their messages and implement fresh marketing initiatives that help further their causes.

D'RAGE MEDIA GROUP

Through a variety of communication methods, our agency strategically efforts to:

» Educate communities,
» Influence behavior,
» Advance causes, and
» Inspire social action.

D'Rage Media Group engages in innovative practices that are at the forefront of America’s continuing trend toward more socially and ecologically responsible methods of conducting business.
