Pankaj Kedia’s Summary

Senior global business strategist, product marketer and business development executive with unparalleled achievements – launching industry-changing products, driving business growth, and leading I2A2 (investment, incubation, acquisition, alliance) efforts for Intel Corp. across mobile, handheld, Internet, and wireless segments. Instrumental in bringing the Intel Atom processor and Intel Centrino Mobile Technology to the market -- two game-changing products/technologies in the industry that have changed how we access the Internet on a day-to-day basis. Prominent industry speaker, often quoted in mainstream media including CNET News, Fast Company, InformationWeek, New York Times, PC World, Wall Street Journal, and Wired.
