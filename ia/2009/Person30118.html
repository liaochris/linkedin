Bobbi Newman’s Summary

I provide, create and host workshops to teach library staff about a range of topics including; using the Social Web to connect with patrons, using web 2.0 professional, library gaming and reference in the digital age.

I also currently work as a Digital Services Librarian. When I tell people my title the next question I usually get is, but what do you do? In a nutshell, I manage my library’s online contact with patrons. That includes blogs, readers advisory, databases, Flickr and various other social networking and social media sites. I also work at the reference desk, manage collection development for several areas, train patrons and staff about the Social Web, help Library staff make the most of their time with Web 2.0 tools, assist with public programs like Game Days, serve on committees, act as the Manager on Duty on nights and weekends and fill in anywhere else I’m needed.
