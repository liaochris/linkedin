Jonathan Guest’s Summary

Working on retained mandates sourcing Senior Management to Board level Executives across all verticals within Redgrave's Technology/Telco and Professional Services Practice Group.

PRIVATE EQUITY: We have relationships with many of the leading Private Equity firms, my focus is to work with them across their TMT investments evaluating and recruiting for their future board leaders who can stimulate the best turnaround and EBITDA growth for the quickest possible exit.

TECHNOLOGY AND TELECOMS: We are retained by FTSE 100's and SME's alike across the Fixed line, Mobile, ISP, Hardware and Software space. We collaborate with our clients to source them quality future leaders across all disciplines to ensure ongoing strategy and revenue growth is maintained and improved.

PROFESSIONAL SERVICES: Redgrave is a preferred Search partner to a number of Tier 1 ITO, BPO, SI, Offshoring and Advisory firms. We have the knowledge and network to find the very best individuals across multiple Leadership functions; we have successfully completed searches for VP/Sales Directors, Programme Directors as well as Advisory Partners within M&A, Performance Improvement, Insolvency and Restructuring.

Jonathan Guest’s Experience

    Redgrave Partners LLP was created from a 2008 management buy-out of two leading search brands, Imprint Search & Selection and WoodHamill, which were part of the quoted recruitment group Imprint Plc. Imprint is an AIM listed global recruitment business which was founded by Redgrave Partners Chairman, Brian Hamill in 2001 and in five years, became the fastest growing business of its kind in Europe.

    As a privately-owned business, Redgrave Partners has the flexibility and the agility to think beyond the conventional wisdom of our industry. We strive to deliver world class leadership services by delivering faster results, every time, for our clients. With over two thirds of our engagements coming from existing clients we are confident that our pragmatic and transparent approach and commitment to delivery is the future of executive search.
