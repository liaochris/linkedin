James Wright’s Summary

With over a decade’s experience in Experiences James is committed to giving people a good time.

James helps organisations grow through sales & marketing strategy; product/service development; new business hunting and operational management.

He is currently at RedBalloon for Corporate in Sydney, Australia where he works with key clients on Employee Engagement, Reward & Recognition and Incentive Programs; James writes and speaks regularly on these topics.

His broad sector experience includes training and development, experiential marketing, FMCG, leisure & luxury goods, gifting, travel, b2b, political ideas, financial services, recruitment, property and e-commerce.

James has also held positions with Chambers of Commerce in the UK and Australia and worked for a variety of high profile voluntary organizations. He is currently engaged with Young Achievement Australia supporting young people to develop their entrepreneurial skills and interest in business.

James is a foodie, cooks almost everyday for pleasure and writes a regular food blog http://eatwright.blogspot.com and follow him on http://twitter.com/eatwright

James Wright’s Specialties:

Key skills lie in identifying, securing and managing commercial relationships; conceptualizing and progressing new products and services to market; developing and delivering marketing and engagement strategies and general business improvement.
