Alexander Major’s Summary

Raised on a small horse farm in Colorado and with over ten years as an Air Force intelligence officer, Alex Major is now an attorney specializing in complex commercial litigationa t Venable LLP's Baltimore office. Prior to his present position, he was the judicial clerk to the Hon. Paul W. Grimm, Chief Magistrate Judge of the US District Court of Maryland and a litigation associate at the Washington DC firm of Arnold & Porter.
As a USAF intelligence officer, he has been stationed or assigned around the world specilaizing in intelligence collection. His training included focused course work and internships at national level intelligence agencies.

 Outstanding presentation skills gained from experience as a professional spokesperson, screenwriter, and stand up comedian
