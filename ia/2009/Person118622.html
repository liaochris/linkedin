Jonas Dhaenens’s Summary

COMBELL is one of the leading internet hosting companies in the Benelux. Our top priorities are support, reliability, speed and constant innovation for website- and server hosting and domain name registration.

Since 1999, COMBELL has been active in the internet hosting and domain name registration market. Currently COMBELL manages over 60.000 domain names, over 20.000 websites and over 800 servers.

Jonas Dhaenens’s Specialties:

COMBELL has many years of experience in Linux and Windows webhosting, worldwide domain name registration and dedicated server hosting. Moreover, COMBELL is at your service 24/24 and 7/7 (Sun- and holidays inclusive).
