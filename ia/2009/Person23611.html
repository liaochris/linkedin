Bas Bakker’s Summary

Passion for/interest in:

- entrepreneurship
- strategy/consulting
- business in general
- marketing/concepting

In my job I aim to be:
- a strategic partner
- an ambassador to others. Being in a professional network gives me the sincere pleasure and obligation to introduce my network to others. Thus making it able to create the success my network aims at.

I have developed myself as an all-round banker/consultant (some say even more an entrepreneur than a banker), with specialties in the business market. Conversations with these clients for all these years have given me the opportunity to learn a lot about strategy, finance, their motivation and all actions they have taken and decisions they have made that created their success. Due to these experiences I am increasingly able to give some advise in return. It is a great enjoinment to do so. I thank all clients for their trust in me as their advisor.

Besides that I have a broad interest and focus on the strategy of banks/companies in general. Banks for example: due to both regulation and strong competition, banks have to evolve in new types of organisations with a strong focus on the client through a combination of new different cross media/linking channels and therefore new products/markets. This will lead to opportunities for all banks to redesign their goals and their processes. Thus making it possible for banks to enter into niches for a more and more personal approach of their clients. All and All it is important not to lose contact with the client.

I want to enhance my knowledge and experience in most branches/industries to reach my goals mentioned.
