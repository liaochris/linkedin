Marco Ostini’s Summary

Founded in January 2001 Matthey & Team SA, an international Management Consulting company in strategic Marketing and Sales, developing - together with a network of worldwide known partners - processes such as Market Research, sales improvement programs, CRM programs, evaluation of new business opportunities and elaboration of business plans, Marketing and sales strategies including support in implementation, branding and positioning. Although the core competence remains in the air transport sector and the main targets are airlines and airports, the company shows a diversified customer portfolio in the fields of Information Technology, consumer goods, public services, hotel management, tourism and entertainment business

Marco Ostini’s Specialties:

Strategic management, restructuring and result improvement, alliances, marketing and sales, flight operations, ground handling, IT, cargo, catering, supply management, management development, start-ups, cross-functional projects and others.
We apply a systematic and comprehensive approach, which is driven by results, the market situation and the satisfaction of customers and company staff.
