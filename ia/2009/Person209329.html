Santosh Pandit’s Summary

I have 19 years of experience in Financial Services including Investment Banking, Structured Finance, Banking and Insurance in a variety of roles. I have strong experience in risk management and risk-based-capital.

As the Head of Asset Management of Cardif Pinnacle, my current focus is on fixed income buy-side, sterling investments and credit, market and liquidity risks.

I enjoy economic analysis. I write regularly on key events in the UK and the world economy that affect our investment strategy and insurance business.

Santosh Pandit’s Specialties:

Asset management, Enterprise risk management, Key risk indicators, Credit risk, Compliance risk, Operational risk, Risk based capital, Audit and inspection. Economic analysis, fixed income.

Businesses include Investment Banking, Corporate Advisory, Structured Finance and Insurance.
