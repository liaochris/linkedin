Alvaro Casella’s Summary

Over the past 20 years I have been involved in working with customers to reach their goals of purchasing their first home, upsizing, downsizing and employing stratedgies to manage debt.

The mortgage industry has changed dramatically over the last 20 years. However during my tenure in the industry I have held to my values of working with a customer to identify and map out the right monthly budget for them. Sometimes a customer had to be told that while they qualify for a larger mortgage, they would be better suited with a smaller one and reduce other consumer debt. This is something that has been very rewarding as past customers return to me because of this advice, and thank me for helping them improve their budget and balancesheet.

As my efforts have expanded to manage other loan officers, I make it a point to ensure that they take the time to interview and understand a customers income and debts before recommending a product or loan amount.

I would appreciate your consideration to use my services next time you or a friend has a mortgage need, whether purchasing or refinancing.

Alvaro Casella’s Specialties:

I have been involved in all aspects of the Real Estate industry and have had in the past a General Contractors license, Real Estate license, as well as a variety of Securities license.

In addition I make it a point to take continuing education both mandatory and voluntary every year so that I can be more proficient in my field.

I am fluent in Spanish, and have designations to work with construction to permanent loans, FHA/VA loans, short sales, foreclosures and many other.
