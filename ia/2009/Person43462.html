Christopher Castle’s Summary

Mr. Castle is a registered Professional Engineer with the State of Texas. He holds the position of Senior Application Consultant for MLC CAD Systems. Mr. Castle has 8 years of Solid Modeling and Finite Element Analysis experience. Mr. Castle’s duties include technical support and training for SolidWorks and COSMOS products. In addition to his normal duties, Mr. Castle also provides engineering services to MLC CAD Systems customers on a consulting basis.

Previously, Mr. Castle held the position of Senior Mechanical Engineer for Karta Technologies, Inc., a prominent government contractor based in San Antonio, Texas. While at Karta Technologies, Mr. Castle acted as lead engineer on numerous projects. Mr. Castle was responsible for winning and overseeing over 3.5 Million dollars in contract money over his career with Karta. Mr. Castle designed and fabricated testing systems, medical equipment, Non Destructive Testing (NDT) systems, and aircraft compents.

While working at EDI and attending college, Mr. Castle started Cybertronix Computer Consulting, a computer consulting and resale business. Cybertronix provided computer and network solutions to private individuals as well as small businesses in the San Antonio area. While operating Cybertronix, Mr. Castle interacted with many influential people in San Antonio’s business community and established many beneficial contacts. Mr. Castle successfully owned and operated Cybertronix Computer Consulting for 3 years before joining Karta Technologies, Inc. From his business experience, Mr. Castle learned a great deal about computer software and hardware technology.

Christopher Castle’s Specialties:

Computational Fluid Dynamic Analysis, Finite Element Analysis, Kinematic Analysis, Machine Design, Industrial Design & Advanced Surface Modeling, SolidWorks & COSMOSWorks Instruction, & Product Data Management Implementation.
