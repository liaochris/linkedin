David J. Hackett’s Summary

The Action Sports Industry is a Billion Dollar Industry that few had the vision to help create. It now permeates the very fabric of American Culture on every level. Over the last 25 years, David Hackett has successfully licensed his name and produced several pro model skateboards nationally and Internationally. He and his peers are responsible for inventing many of the basic moves all Action Sports have incorporated over the last 30 years. In 2005, at the age of 45 he won the European TS racing championship title, beating racers half his age. On August 2nd, 2006 he set a new World Record by being the first "Senior" Professional skateboarder to successfully complete the “Loop of Death” at Tony Hawk’s training facility. Dave Hackett has had an AMAZING life the past 46 years as a professional athlete, World Traveler, and Action Sports Pioneer. Now, he takes that experience as a motivational speaker and professional business, life, and relationship coach Specializing in Addiction Recovery, Family and Love Relationships. David J. Hackett knows from example, that once we know WHAT we truely are- We can put those assets and features to rise to our full potential. His mission in this lifetime is to help as many people as possible reach their goals and experience what he does on a daily basis- HEALTH, WEALTH, LOVE, and PERFECT SELF EXPRESSION! David currently lives in Encinitas with his wife Zannah, Golfing, surfing and skateboarding on a regular basis while operating their consulting firm, and Relationship/Life Coaching business; The Y.O.U. Consulting Group.
