Joseph Juriga’s Summary

After completing my bachelor’s thesis in Graphic Design, co-founding an AIGA* student chapter and graduating Magna Cum Laude from SUNY New Paltz in 2003, I moved to sunny Florida and began work as a graphic designer at 14 East Multimedia Group. In early 2005 I became the creative director of this small web-based design team.

In 2006 I joined the Advertising Federation of Greater Ft. Lauderdale as chair of the Programs committee and in 2007 was elected President to lead the board of directors in new and exciting directions. Since then, I’ve had the pleasure to serve as an Addy Judge at numerous locations around the country, including Ft. Myers, FL and Orange County, CA.

I moved to Orlando in the spring of 2008 and joined Marriott as their Online Creative Director for The Ritz-Carlton Club and Marriott Vacation Club brands. I was elected to the American Advertising Federation Orlando Board of Directors and currently chair the Public Service committee. I am currently pushing the limits of how vacations look online and refining our brand’s global web presence. In addition, I maintain a constant role as community activist, public servant, student and seeker.

* AIGA, American Institute of Graphic Arts
