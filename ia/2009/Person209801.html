Sarah McGovern’s Summary

Sarah is a Human Resources professional with a strong background in various areas of human resource management and development including but not limited to HR communications, project management, analysis, and customer service. Over 8 years of business experience and over 2 years of Human Resources Experience. Firm understanding of federal, state, and local employment law. Proficient with the Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc) in addition to HRIS (Ultipro and PeopleSoft), and various applicant tracking systems.

Her doctoral research at Columbia University focuses on understanding the learning process that some record industry professionals, who have seemingly made the shift over to the digital business model, went through in order to develop a conceptual framework that will facilitate this kind of transition for the rest of the organization.

You can contact Sarah via email at sarahmcgovern@mac.com.
