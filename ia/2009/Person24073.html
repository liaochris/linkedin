Barbara OBrien’s Summary

Creative and energetic corporate learning services executive with experience developing global software educational strategies and training programs for start-ups and Fortune 500 companies’ employees, partners and clients. Expertise in developing blended training offerings in formats for classroom, WebCasts, live virtual training, e-learning, video and audio podcasts, and workbooks. Proven skills at enhancing a company’s competitive position and increasing revenue. Skilled with offshore outsourcing and managing remote and international teams.
