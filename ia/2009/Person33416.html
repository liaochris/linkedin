  • Systems Analyst III

    Jacobs Engineering

    (Public Company; JEC; Oil & Energy industry)

    July 2003 — June 2007 (4 years)

    Worked on a contract with the Naval Research Laboratory at Stennis Space Center. I was responsible for the administration of around 150 solaris and linux workstations and 30 servers, with my main focus being storage, backup, and name services.

    Key Technologies and Achievements:

    Streamlined email delivery though sendmail with libmilter and UW-IMAP.

    Managed storage, including planning a brand new SAN with Brocade switches and various storage arrays. NRL was heavily based on NFS and automounter. The 50+TB of storage were primarily zfs, shared over NFS to linux and solaris clients. Also created an nfs to smb gateway with samba for use by windows clients.

    Along with the storage, I was responsible for managing backups. Legato Networker was our primary backup mechanism, to a four drive Quantum library over fibre channel.

    Refactored our name service with NIS and laid the groundwork for a move to LDAP.
