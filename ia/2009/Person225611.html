Stuart Cranmer’s Summary

• In my present position at Allergy Standards, employed to manage the day-to-day operations of the Irish and North American operations, with a particular emphasis on creating awareness of the Certification Mark and the generation of sales to consumer facing companies.
• Operating on a part-time basis, employed by Enterprise Ireland, a government agency, to mentor start-up and growing businesses.
• Within Biotrin, employed to create added value by devising growth strategies that ultimately led to exit upon successful sale of the Company. In my time the Company achieved a 20% increase of sales and around a doubling of value. Responsible for all sales and marketing activities with a direct responsibility for sales to USA reference laboratories
• In DiaSorin I was responsible for the refocusing of sales and marketing efforts and support operation development in the UK’s stand-alone subsidiary operation.I converted a kit sales company to a systems sales company in line with corporate objectives and developed the strategy of “preferential secondary supplier” to support the market leaders in managed services contracts, resulting in DiaSorin being awarded business in a significant number of UK tenders.
• Started the commercial operation of Sanofi Diagnostics Pasteur (SDP) in the UK. From zero sales I led the Company to a market leadership position with 50% share in its niche market of blood transfusion microbiology, within 5 years of operation start-up in the UK.
• Successfully integrated the sales and marketing teams of SDP and Bio-Rad Laboratories, maintaining all key accounts during the process.
• Responsible for the set up of exports operations and formalised sales structure for Ademtech (a French start-up company in biological sciences area), for which I acted as a Consultant.
