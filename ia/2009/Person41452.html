Charles Shillingburg CLSSS’s Summary

30+ Years of Marketing Research, Continuous Improvement, Business Management, Customer Satisfaction Consulting and New Product Development experience encompassing Customer Satisfaction/Requirements/Needs, Brand/Category Development and Sales and Service Operations Consulting, including: automotive, publications, consumer goods, finance, government and retail sales and service.

Accomplishments include: Developed and established process improvements that doubled capacity and production in a Call Center environment, Established Fruit of the Loom's Marketing Research Department, Key player in all aspects of new category and product investigation and development at Noxell Corp. (Cover Girl Makeup) bringing a cross-functional involvement approach to the Corporate Development Division, Working with a cross-functional team developed, marketed and sold a unique approach to Media Measurement that has been adopted as a standard means of measurement across multiple industries and became a major profit center, Developed the Total Operations Performance System (TOPS), a QFD system that includes a Trade-Off Model for planning purposes, Developed Nissan and Infiniti Divisions' Customer Satisfaction Measurement Systems that serve as the basis for their Sales, Service, Finance and Consumer Affairs training and operations deployment companywide and across their retail networks.

Presently, an independent consultant helping companies innovate and productively grow by identifying and meeting Customer Requirements/Needs using, in part, Lean Six Sigma deployment techniques.
