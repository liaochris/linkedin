Nicole Ostoya’s Summary

Nicole Ostoya, a founding member of the iDTV team, brings all her retail talents to the electronic retail world. Nicole brings with her over 20 years experience in merchandising in the fashion and beauty business. An accomplished leader, she has driven significant growth in retail, wholesale, and merchandising sectors, and is widely recognized as a visionary in her field.

Nicole offers the rare blend of creative and operational strengths; she has been recognized for setting and implementing marketing strategies while improving sales and profits. Dedicated, decisive, and detail-oriented Professional with proven skills, accomplishments, and experience expanding business and working with business partners in the Global Retail marketplace. Contributed to the growth and profitability of large trendsetting corporations; Nordtrom and LVMH. Motivational leader and “hands-on” team contributor that manages multiple responsibilities simultaneously in a fast-paced, time sensitive environment. Effective communicator that develops trusting relationships through integrity, professional character, and industry expertise.

In her personal life, Nicole believes in giving back to her community, and regularly donates her speaking abilities at fund raising events for the United Way and Easter Seals.
