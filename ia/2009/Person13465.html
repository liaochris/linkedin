Andreas Galanis’s Summary

I have been working as a CRM consultant for the last 3 years in Greece. I help companies which need to improve their customer strategy and support this strategy through a CRM system. Many times I’m asked to help them to select the appropriate CRM software for them, to design corporate procedures or redesign the existing ones. Furthermore, I participated in the organizing committee of the 1st and 2nd CRM Forum in Greece, a very familiar event, dedicated to the Customer Relationship Management

My second specialization has to do with business plans funded by Greek state or European Union funds (επιδοτούμενα προγράμματα in Greek). My partners and I design the whole business plan and undertake all the procedures to forward it to the proper Minister or secretariat

Additionally , I work as a lecturer for executive seminars in the area of CRM and E-mail marketing

Very often , I'm invited as a guest speaker in conferences, seminars and university classes and round tables
