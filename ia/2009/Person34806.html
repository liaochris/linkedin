Bruce Schulman’s Summary

Professional Sales and Marketing Executive with over eighteen years of success focusing mostly within the Pharmaceutical, Biotech and CRO arena. Impressive domestic and global track record (Europe, Japan and Canada); selling in vivo services and developing targeted marketing strategies for OTC and ethical products. Proven leader, skilled in building and motivating sales personnel, managing budgets and maintaining strong customer relationships. Expertise in strategic planning, brand and product management, problem solving and sales forecasting.
