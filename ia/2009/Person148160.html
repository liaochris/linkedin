Victor Treutel’s Summary

I originally created my Life Changing Investor Training for my family, so they would be financially secure if anything ever happened to me. With prodding from my family and friends, I have packaged my experience, investing strategy, and software toolkit into the Maracas Investing Life Changing Investor Training.

I have over 30 years of experience in the stock market, but am not an expert. I gained my experience just like the rest of us - through hard knocks. I have no formal investment training, but has listened to the experts and oftentimes took their advice. This expert advice rarely turned out successful, until I learned the secrets of some of the most successful investors in the world…which are surprisingly simple. I took that knowledge and created my Investor Toolkit.

The combination of my dynamic presentation, investing experience, and Investor Toolkit will change your life in a single day!
