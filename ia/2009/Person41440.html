Charles Runyan’s Summary

I am a patent attorney. I have represented a diverse set of clients ranging from individual inventors to start-up companies to multinational oil and chemical companies and multinational pharmaceutical and medical device companies. I served many of these clients in an in-house capacity. So, I have experience with how large companies work from the inside.

I am currently building a new practice in Arizona.
