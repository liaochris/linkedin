Dan Taggart’s Summary

I have founded several companies in the past two decades including Infobases, Inc., an electronic publishing company that made the Inc. 500 list in 1996, and MyFamily.com, Inc. (Now The Generations Network), currently the largest genealogy company in the world.

I have served in a variety of senior management positions in the process of building these companies, including President/CEO, VP Marketing, VP Business Development, and VP Sales, VP Product Development.

I was a recipient of the Earnst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Award in 2000 for my success in founding MyFamily.com.

I enjoy building new companies especially during the first two years where most of the value is created. I am an entrepreneur by nature and like to be involved in the creative phase of forming and building a new business venture.

My next goal is to take a company from start up to either succesful IPO or acquisition at a 1 Billion+ valuation.

Dan Taggart’s Specialties:

My primary skills are in business formation, fundraising, developing partnerships, strategy development, sales and marketing, and product development. While I am known for vision and creativity, I am primarily a doer, and excel in execution.
