Kim Shand’s Summary

In 1994 Kim Shand founded Vega Consulting Solutions and has helped build Vega into a very competitive, high-tech consulting company. Vega’s national presence and consistent growth has been credited to a reputation for integrity and quality. Vega has been recognized five times among NJ’s Top 50 Woman Owned Businesses.

Kim has been involved in the technology industry for two decades and became well regarded for her track record of leading the growth and development of the technology businesses that employed her. Before starting Vega, she held the post of Chief Operating Officer within a publicly held, international consulting company.

Kim appears frequently as a guest speaker on issues regarding women in the workplace, gender and technology, and high tech education initiatives. She has had interviews and articles published in magazines and newspapers including NJ Star Ledger, Computerworld, Vault and NJ Tech News.

In addition, Kim applies her business skills to the nonprofit sector as Chairman of the Board for the “Center for Great Expectations” a treatment center for homeless, pregnant women. Kim also donates her time to teaching for NJ’s Pass-It-On program which offers technical training for women and minorities.
