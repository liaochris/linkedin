Tony Cox’s Summary

Tony has over a decade of experience helping organisations bridge the gap between Business and Technology. Having acted as a Business Analyst, Project Manager, Business Process Consultant, and Operational Manager he always challenges business leaders to think beyond the next release, ensuring flexible and future-proof system designs. He’s also been known as the ‘Ideas Guy’ whose spiritual home is in marketing.
