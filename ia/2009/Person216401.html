Sheri Ashmore-Austin’s Summary

Strategic marketer experienced in helping brands (and businesses) find their truth and communicate their uniqueness in compelling ways that create opportunities for the people who steward them. The Alchemedia Project provides professional services in the areas of creative commerce, digital branding, marketing strategy, communication planning, technology and business development.

A creative collegue of Sheri's wrote about her--
"With an independent mind and gracious heart, Sheri is the woman little girls want to grow up to be. Admired as a strategic champion, a brand advocate, and a sage mentor, she is sought out for her trusted counsel in all matters of contemporary marketing.

Her agenda is to have none—approaching every encounter with a neutral clarity that makes her an ally of the brand. Asking “What if?” of her clients, her colleagues, and herself, Sheri recognizes that the best answer is not always the easiest."

Sheri Ashmore-Austin’s Specialties:

  • Partner

    The Alchemedia Project

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    December 2008 — Present (5 months)

    The Alchemedia Project was born of a shared mindset that truly integrated branding won’t occur in environments where solutions are bolted-on rather than built-in. Building upon the technology seed of iMarlin, The Alchemedia Project is a company where creative and media are inseparable, functioning as interwoven halves of the same whole. Because we believe content best communicates within a context that supports it, we imagine message and medium together. It’s our belief dynamic messaging environments mold and shape LiVING BRANDS™—where the brand constantly incorporates intelligence gathered from its feedback systems to become increasingly more relevant in people’s lives. We believe it should be impossible to discern where the creative ends and the media begins.
