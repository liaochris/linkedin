Thomas Kanady’s Summary

Thomas Kanady has been a part of several communications-oriented industries. As Director of Product Management at BroadVision, he launched content management products for online publishers and writers and worked as a content management and Web design consultant for HP, Sears, Motorola, Circuit City, USPS, BASF, Baan, Home Depot and others.

Tom has been involved with several firsts in technology, including successful forays into interactive television, eCommerce and Web content management. He was named co-author on US Patent 6287199 for contributions to an interactive TV game production system.
