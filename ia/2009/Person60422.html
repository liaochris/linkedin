Debonee Morgan, MFT’s Summary

I speak well and listen even better. I'm currently running a private psychotherapy practice in Long Beach, as well as speaking and facilitating for small workshops, large sessions and business teams. The past year has included a keynote address for the California Association of Student Councils at Stanford, leading the two-day strategic business development session for international manufacturer Kagome, teaching Diversity for the Leaders Forum, and talking to a high school class about the importance of myths, to name a few. I'm looking to expand my business as a speaker/facilitator, and begin writing professionally. (I suppose a few new therapy clients are always welcome too!) I'm also considering teaching at the college level - working on a cv and looking for opportunities there.

In addition to all this - I manage the development of custom software for a department of the largest restaurant company in the world. I'm good at it, but it doesn't quite turn my crank, if you know what I mean. Still, I get to work with a lot of cool people, so I try not to whine too much. :)

Debonee Morgan, MFT’s Specialties:
