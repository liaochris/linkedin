Chris Arkenberg’s Summary

I worked with Adobe Systems for 10 years helping ensure the quality of major software & server releases including Acrobat, Atmosphere 3D, Adobe Studio, & PhotoDeluxe. For the past 4 years I acted as QE Manager for Acrobat 3D & Rich Media delivering high-quality, real-time presentations of CAD, 3D, & AEC models in the Acrobat document space, as well as the authoring & display of interactive Flash content. This position expands upon my previous role as QE manager & community researcher for the Atmosphere 3D project which attempted to create an all-in-one solution for delivering immersive & social 3D content to the web. I have also regularly contributed to the strategic direction of the Acrobat 3D manufacturing feature set, CS4 & CS5 solutions, as well as investigations into mobile, media, and collaborative workflows.

My current interests are in social computing, mobile technologies, web services, smart objects, immersion & virtual worlds, building the semantic web, & dynamic data-sharing technologies that enable better communication & organization of collaborative & social networks & workflows. I find all of these fields to be extraordinarily rich & engaging. I would love the opportunity to help develop the future. I am also actively tracking issues in geopolitics, the failure of the global economic paradigm, & the resulting call to local community resiliency, sustainability & self-reliance.

My primary career direction lies in critical analysis of the emerging trends of technology & culture and how these trends are writing the future and informing the movements of industry players.

I've been a web user since 1993, a regular blogger for several years, am an early Twitter adopter, a web research power user, have been involved in many forums and social webs, and can leverage trends, networks, & profiles to propagate ideas & extend brand penetration. I also produce music for various content streams and have a good sense for design & aesthetics.
