Abhijit Pradhan’s Summary

After a glorious run in the corporate world for 16 years - helping launch brands, helping resurrect brands, etc. I have decided to answer my calling and follow my true passion - helping superstars at work find theirs !

Its been more than 3 months since I launched Superstar Business Solutions. While it was originally conceived as an Executive Coaching company, it immediately took off as a Change Management / Vision Alignment company using Executive Coaching, Organisation Storytelling and Vision Workshops as tools. The Superstar partnership helps top management realise its long coveted vision into reality through its key people.

My passionate belief is that Organisation Visions need to be SHARED VISIONs and build ground up and not be imposed by top management to create TRUE OWNERSHIP.

I also believe that once the shared vision is articulated and AGREED upon, it is important to plan out a tangible and solid action plan which brings the vision to life.

It is a combination of the above which I believe brings out the true potential of an organisation and its people.

Vision without Action is a Daydream.
Action without Action is a Nightmare
( Japanese Proverb)
