Brandon Fluharty’s Summary

ABOUT ME-

From lung-busting runs in the mountains of Romania to a grassroots education movement in American youth soccer, from DJ'ing in the heart of bohemian NYC to managing the audio environment of fashion's elite, from the prestige of the New York Times to the fun & excitement of television advertising; I am grateful to have walked many paths and wear several, diverse hats in my professional life.

One theme has remained constant - the ingredients that have carried me throughout early life are the same keys to my success today in sales, marketing and media consulting - integrity, determination, passion, commitment, a propensity to relate to others' needs, and a focus on achieving superior results.

My goal - become a Branding Expert. I've been in the trenches on the product development side, marketing via unique use of music, and advertising across a number of mediums (print, digital, tv). I have strategically planted myself in television - the most powerful advertising medium of all time, all to help companies better brand themselves and become top of mind when consumers think of a particular category.

Bottom line - I am committed to helping my clients succeed in 1.) establishing their brand at the top of the ladder and 2.) stay there, all while growing my team's overall sales revenue.
