Juan Luna’s Summary

I have nine year experience working in IT, where I have developed special skills on Business Analysis, Requirements gathering, Process Modeling, Project Management, Software Quality Assurance and Testing.

During my first years of experience I devoted my efforts to Quality Assurance and Software Testing. While testing applications I executed manual and automated test, created and maintained test cases, test matrices, test plans, work plans and traceability matrices; as Test Lead I was responsible of assigning and reviewing artifacts, deliverables as well as reporting the status thru significant metrics. As Quality Assurance Lead I was responsible of managing and reviewing the work and results of my team who reviewed and audited artifacts and activities from all software disciplines during the entire development life cycle.

In my last years of experience, using my knowledge acquired during my certifications in Six Sigma, Rational TestManager & Robot and in Rational Unified Process, besides Quality Assurance and Testing I joined other disciplines where I developed additional skills in management, requirements, use cases modeling, coding prototypes, version control, analyzing and modeling business processes.

Using my knowledge in Rational Unified Process and CMMI I analyzed IT processes versus the real business needs to determinate existent gaps and necessary IT solutions to enhance and improve their areas, processes, procedures, roles, responsibilities and deliverables.

Juan Luna’s Specialties:

My specialty area is Software Quality Assurance and Business IT Process Engineering where I am capable to develop and improve process, procedures, activities, roles, policies and other related components to successfully provide tangible and positive results.
