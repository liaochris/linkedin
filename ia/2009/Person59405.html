Dawn Suzzette Lambe’s Summary

After 15 years in the medical industry, I decided that it was time for me to branch out on my own. I started a retail gift basket company which was successful for several years before deciding it was time to move on. However, in my quest to learn "web stuff" while I had my basket company, I discovered that I had a knack for writing, blogging, internet marketing, and recently, social media optimization. I help create "The Network of You" so that you can build the relationships and grow your business, and help you manage your network.

Dawn Suzzette Lambe’s Specialties:

The "industry" of social media is SO new, it is very difficult to categorize any "specialty", as there are hundreds of social media outlets. However, having had my gift basket company, having dealt with event planners, bridal parties, and corporate america, I am well versed in the wedding professionals industries and that is where I'm focusing my attention.
