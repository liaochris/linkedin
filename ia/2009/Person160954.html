Michael Carnell’s Summary

I am currently the primary of Palmettobug Digital - a consulting, training, and development firm specializing in technology empowering people. I work with Windows and Apple technology on the front and back end including OS X, Windows of all flavors, IIS, Exchange, and SQL Server. Although I am Microsoft certified, frankly I love Apple products - they just work. I have done extensive web design and programming in the past and am currently working to help people use Web 2.0 and the so-called "new media". My main goal is to see what technology can do for people as an enabling tool, not just a cool toy. Through this I can hopefully make people's lives and communities richer now and in the future.
