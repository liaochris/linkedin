Daniel Tiganila’s Summary

Real Estate, with all that it means, plays a very big part of my life!
I simply love what I do.

Romania is a growing and developing market with incredible real estate investment opportunities.
I have 5 years of experience on the market, with transactions totaling over $ 70,000,000 .

If you are interested, my company will guide and assist you with :

- buying, selling or renting real estate in Romania ( apartments, houses, villas, office buildings, commercial land, industrial land, agricultural land, forests and farms )
- investing in Romanian real estate market ( we have appealing properties in developed and growing areas )

We also provide full service for foreign companies planning to relocate or open offices in Romania.
