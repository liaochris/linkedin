Lisa Meyers’s Summary

As a member of the executive management team, I work to develop and manage the vision of Magic Johnson Enterprises through primary focus on brand management. In this capacity, I work on behalf of existing properties and assets (Starbucks, SodexoMagic, Canyon-Johnson Urban Fund, etc.), vet and develop new opportunities, and provide significant guidance and counsel to the Magic Johnson Foundation (check out "I Stand With Magic" at www.istandwithmagic.com).

During my five and half year tenure with the American Cancer Society, I made my mark in the area of innovation—finding solutions to unique challenges, introducing new revenue streams and new media strategies, and helping the organization as a whole to think about emergent trends affecting the future fight against cancer. For example, I created Team ACS (www.cancer.org/TeamACS), launched the division's first online fundraising site in support of Making Strides Against Breast Cancer (www.cancer.org/stridesonline) and a host of other intiatives.

I also created critical partnerships for the American Cancer Society across several industries, a testament to my ability to pull together cross-functional, geographically dispersed teams. Some of the cause-marketing and promotional opportunities included projects with Yahoo, Unilever, Tribal DDB, Google, nytimes.com, and MissionFish/eBay to name a few.

I created the organization’s Communications and Marketing Advisory Board—a group of executive volunteers from organizations including the Ad Council, National Association of Broadcasters, DDB Worldwide, Kodak, and the NYC Health & Hospitals Corporation—in effort to extend resources, expertise, and financial support.

Leading a staff of 20 and countless diverse leadership volunteers, I was appointed to several national teams to provide guidance and counseling on issues related to communications and marketing, diversity and innovation.
