Margaret Jacoby, SPHR’s Summary

Human Resources Generalist with over 25 years of proven success; holds national "Senior Professional in Human Resources" (SPHR) certification; expertise in small and emerging companies and non-profit organizations. We provide human resources assistance to small businesses and non-profit organizations either on-site through our HR Department on Call (tm) program or by telephone and e-mail through our HR Help Desk (tm) program. We also provide a suite of customizable HR Forms through our WebForms(tm) program.
