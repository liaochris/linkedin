Liberation Iannillo’s Summary

My name is Liberation Iannillo and I am a website designer and Movable Type developer with over nine years of experience. The websites I develop are goal orientated with a strong focus on usability, search engine optimization, and are supported by additional online marketing such as blogs, social networking strategies, and HTML E-Mails.

I am looking for a full time position as a web master or blog master at a company in the creative industry where I can contribute my acquired knowledge and my wide variety of skills to help my employer achieve their project goals.

My ideal position would involve working with a content management system such as Movable Type, Word Press, or Drupal and maintaining a website or blog. My experience includes developing such projects from the ground up where my roles have included defining the target audience, choosing the appropriate technology, designing the website, developing content, writing copy, search engine optimization, and marketing the project online with social networking campaigns using Facebook, Myspace, Twitter, YouTube, etc.

My experience has been with companies in the entertainment, publishing and fashion industries and I thrive in a creative, well rounded environment. I am looking to stay in Manhattan.
