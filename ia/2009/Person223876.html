Steve Freeman’s Summary

Communications is the most fundamental and pervasive of all management activity. Because of that, I enjoy working with leaders who appreciate a unique perspective and desire real-world results -- whether the goal is influencing and informing a company's stakeholders or achieving objectives with other audiences, including the media. I have the experiences and training to deliver tactical and strategic results.

Steve Freeman’s Specialties:
