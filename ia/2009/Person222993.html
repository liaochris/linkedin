    Frankford Hospitals

    (Non-Profit; Hospital & Health Care industry)

Successfully handled strategic and day-to-day HR issues in both Corporate and in the field. Administered company policies and procedures, and coordinated efforts with the Navy, DoD and other government contractors and agencies. Developed Affirmative Action Plans and applicant tracking system. Authored several policies and procedures.
