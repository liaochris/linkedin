Milissa Rick’s Summary

Currently, as Vice President of Online Insights at Sway, I direct the research department. Current products within our portfolio include the Social Media Map (SMM) product, the Social Media Compass, the Social Media Calibration product, and packaged research reports. Our social media products are research-based plans for how to effectively target customers in an online environment. We identify where customers are spending time online, how they behave online, what motivates them, what influences them, and how to effectively reach them. Recent brands/companies that we have done Maps for include: American Girl, Navistar--International Trucks, Emerson, Allstate, Enzymatic Therapy, Fiskars, and Stabil.

As Director of the Wisconsin Innovation Service Center at the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater, I assisted over 2,500 innovative manufacturers, technology businesses, and entrepreneurs with making more profitable product and market development decisions. WISC provides detailed research reports on new product feasibility, competitive intelligence, licensing/strategic partnering, distributor assessments, and customer satisfaction. In addition as Director of WISC, I oversaw the business development strategies for the Center, and I was part of the business management team at the Center for Business and Innovation Development, which acts as a gateway to the resources of UW-Whitewater.

I have been a marketing consultant for over fourteen years, most recently working as a research consultant for a full-service, market research firm Chamberlain Research, in Madison, WI. I have worked with major companies including SC Johnson, Johnson Diversey, Kraft, Kimberly Clark, Capital One, Wachovia, and Potawatomi Bingo and Casino.

During my career, I have taught a multitude of workshops on new product development and early stage market research, as well as several undergraduate courses at UW-Whitewater on Marketing Research, Marketing Principles, and Product Policy.
