Jon Chin’s Summary

In a nutshell, I’m a digital strategist who is in love with analytics, social media marketing (networking) and SEO/SEM.

I’m presently residing in a little island just one degree south of the equator - Singapore and I am a permanent resident of Australia.

I have quack for learning on what makes consumers tick (how (and why) they behave, how they spend their money and finding out how the brands fit into their lives and last but not least in addition, I offer the analytical ability of data mining, understanding response rates, and using data to improve targeting.

where “data to insights to strategy” – success in one audience generate success in other. This is me.

My goals are twofold (1) is to make genuine sales impact on the client’s business through the engagement of insightful communication and (2) strategies that connects with the client’s brand though an understanding of how their channel consumption and how they are influenced by those channels. These goals will be achieved using a spectrum of solutions that may or may not include advertising.
