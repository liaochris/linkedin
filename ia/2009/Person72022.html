Ellen J. Harris’s Summary

I am a business strategist and I work with micro-business owners making the science of business and technology converge to uncover revenue streams that create sustainable top-line growth.

I do this by modeling macro business processes at micro levels.
I create lightweight, low cost micro-networks using technology business owners can understand and use near term and I achieve equity positions that generate cash flow for business owners transitioning from corporate to entrepreneur.

I mentor entrepreneur candidates at New York University Stern School of Business Entrepreneur Program and serve as Editor, Executive MBA Newsletter at the Lubin School of Business.

I mentor senior executives and MBA students transitioning into micro business-ownership. I collaborate and partner with former corporate professionals seeking to acquire micro business management skills in emerging markets and industries.

A new look for The Business-Accelerated Company is scheduled for release in Q1, 2010.
