John Zgourides’s Summary

For almost 20 years, John Zgourides has represented individuals and businesses in trials, mediations, and arbitrations. After many years working in insurance defense law, John now limits his practice to representing individuals and families in catastrophic injury cases and Federal Employers Liability Act (FELA) cases.

John Zgourides is peer review rated as "AV Preeminent" by the Martindale-Hubbell Legal Directory (see martindale.com and lawyers.com).

John Zgourides is Board Certified in Personal Injury Trial Law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization

Licensed by the Supreme Court of Texas

Licensed by the US Court of Appeals, 5th Circuit

Licensed by the US District Court, Texas (statewide - all divisions)

Licensed by the US District Court, Colorado (statewide)

Licensed by the US District Court, Arkansas (statewide - all divisions)

Licensed by the US District Court, Nebraska (statewide)

John is based in Houston with the law firm of Sammons & Associates, PC

John Zgourides’s Specialties:

Board Certified -- Personal Injury Trial Law, Texas Board of Legal Specialization

There are no sub-certifications for John's practice areas, which are FELA railroad injuries, products liability injuries, car/truck wreck injuries, premises liability injuries, and insurance law.

John Zgourides’s Experience

  • Senior Trial Lawyer

    Sammons & Associates, P.C.

    (Privately Held; Law Practice industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year )

    John Zgourides presents catastrophic injury and death cases to American juries. His practice includes a heavy concentration of Federal Employers Liability Act cases for railroaders around the country. John has served as lead counsel in many cases throughout Texas, as well as in federal courts in Arkansas, Colorado, Nebraska, New Mexico, Kansas, California, and Wyoming.

  • Managing Attorney

    Zgourides & Arnold

    (Legal Services industry)

    2003 — 2008 (5 years )

    In 2003, John went to work trying cases for Grover & Bruce, PC. The firm morphed into Pitts, Williford & Zgourides, and then to Zgourides & Arnold. In 2008, while retaining its name, the firm became the in-house litigation department for American Century Casualty Company, a Texas insurance company with policyholders in 13 states. During these years, John's practice was in insurance defense trial law, defending people covered by liability insurance in personal injury lawsuits filed against them. John also represented ACCC in direct actions and in commercial disputes.

  • Trial Attorney

    Archer, Shrode & Soule

    (Law Practice industry)

    2000 — 2003 (3 years )

    John defended insureds of the Farmers Group of Insurance Companies in personal injury lawsuits. John also represented the Farmers Group of Insurance Companies in direct actions.

  • Managing Attorney for Litigation Department

    Law Offices of Richard Pena, PC

    (Law Practice industry)

    1999 — 2000 (1 year )

    Richard Pena is a former president of the State Bar of Texas. John was honored to lead this personal injury firm's litigation department. The main office is in Austin and satellite offices are in the Valley.

  • Trial Attorney and Mediator

    Zgourides & Zgourides

    (Law Practice industry)

    1990 — 1999 (9 years )

    John worked with his father, Gus Zgourides, during his formative years. John's father has been licensed in Texas since 1955. Working with his father, John tried and mediated cases for almost 10 years.
