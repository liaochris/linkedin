Thomas Vedel’s Summary

I have been working with Indirect Procurement for a couple of years now, from a source-procure-pay perspective. I started my career as as a project assistant at Arla Foods, where I worked with e-procurement and as the assistant to the programme manager on a large SAP implementation.

Subsequently I've worked with e-procurement at The LEGO Group, implementing our e-procurement system internationally while also stepping up and assuming full project responsibility as the Project Manager.

I've recently worked as Sourcing Development Manager within Indirect Procurement. In that role I worked with strategic sourcing projects and BPO's for Indirect Procurement at The LEGO Group.

As Department Manager for Category Management, I run a small team of 6 employees, who are repsonsible for category managing the majority of the indirect spend at The LEGO Group. I'm also globally responsible for our entire Procure2Pay end-to-end process.
