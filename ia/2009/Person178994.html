  • VP Sales and Marketing/Business Development

    Fidelity Nat'l Title Insurance Company

    (Public Company; fnf; Real Estate industry)

    1978 — 1995 (17 years)

    Kathy moved to San Diego in 1978 after a few years representing Estee Launder and Elizabeth Arden at Nordstroms. She benefitted from the great customer service training that is the Nordstrom's way.
    Starting anew, Kathy entered the Title Business in sales. She was promoted and spent over two years as Sales Manager with 50 sales people covering the County of San Diego. Kathy returned to the field with a move to Commonwealth Land Title Company. When Fidelity came into San Diego County, they hired her to open North County S.D. Kathy was a consistent #1 top producer in her North County territory and made the highest level of Gold in the President's Club. Kathy formed the first title partnership in San Diego County when inviting Mark Boeh to join forces with her. They had a very successful team until 1995 when the market turned down and the team was dissolved by management.
    In 1996 Kathy opened On Track Success Coaching and the rest is a history of helping others with their career, business, and personal growth.
