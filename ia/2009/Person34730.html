Bruce Kemp’s Summary

A highly qualified, diligent and experienced People Manager, SQE Manager and Business Professional with thirteen (13) years combined experience in the public and private sector environments, Seven (7) years of which in senior leadership positions.
Preamble

In the increasingly competitive environment that is now part of everyday organizational life, many businesses that offer consumers similar products and services now need to look to the future and determine how they will continue to be viable and successful.

I believe that organisational development plays a key role in assisting organizations gain and maintain a competitive edge through performance enhancement by differentiating itself against competitors or service providers using planning, people and cultural interventions.

What would I like to achieve?

I have a long term goal to establish a career in senior business management in either the public or private sector where I can assist an organization develop and achieve desired outcomes through planning and people.
I will achieve this through;

• Rigorous research and analysis
• Participative and open communication, and
• Coaching, mentoring and facilitating using organisational development techniques

How could I achieve my goal?

• Short term goal – to gain employment in an organisation where I can continue to learn and build upon my experience and contribute to the success of the organization through utilizing my administrative, planning and people skills.
• Continue to explore, investigate and research material on the subject of organisational development, organisational change, planning and consulting through professional journals and literature.
• Pursue further post graduate studies which will complement my existing qualifications.
• Develop a network of people who can continue to guide me in this field of whom I trust and respect.
