Dennis Rose’s Summary

Career Objective: Head of Asia-Pacific for a tech (software, networking, services, integration, convergence, systems and packaged solutions) multinational, head of a line of business for a larger multinational or COO of a multinational's Asia-Pacific division.

I am a results-driven general manager with a career track built upon experience and success in hi-tech sales, marketing and operations in the US (14 years), Latin America (8 years) and Asia-Pacific (8 years) along with some project experience in Europe.
I have demonstrated consistent achievement and built experience in leading sales, marketing, system engineering, consulting, operations, call center, human resources and other related functions. My motivation comes from the opportunity to deliver value by driving growth holistically across an organization – through top line sales, profit margin, other financial fundamentals, organizational talent and alignment, brand power, the value delivered to channels and ultimately to the customer.

Dennis Rose’s Specialties:

International sales development, people development, new market entrance strategies, integrating acquisitions, growing revenue streams, strengthening governance, deep operational skills and understanding with significant experience in building high performing organizations along with accompanying processes and systems to enable growth.
