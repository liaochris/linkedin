Anthony Stramaglia’s Summary

Broad experience in Linux and Windows administration, network and security operations, incident response and forensics, information assurance, and server/workstation hardware support.

Worked in the enterprise, corporate, and SMB sectors, in on-staff, contract, and self-employed roles.

"I started out in computers during the golden age of 8-bit, having progressed from the TRS-80 Model III in middle school, getting my own VIC-20, graduating to a C64 and finally to PC's. I was fascinated with telecommunications and ran several BBSes duing the 80's and 90's. I was featured in The BBS Documentary movie, and to this day play with vintage (or virtualized vintage) hardware and software."

Moderately fluent in Italian.
