Tad Lukasik’s Summary

I am the current Project Manager / Producer for Mandalia Productions, LLC and am a proven leader and innovator with 22 years of business experience including 15 years in the demanding film/video and advertising media industries. Broadcast commercial production, direct response and high-end corporate video have been my specialization. Recent freelance experience includes a non-profit start-up, 6 months as an advertising agency broadcast producer and 3 months consulting for a marketing company expansion effort in San Francisco, CA. I am proficient in all aspects of project/production management and production support including prep, shooting and editing with a depth of over 1150 project credits to-date. Additional contributing experience in the finance, hospitality and defense industries expand my management capabilities. My project list includes sports, healthcare, gaming, automotive, emerging new media and technology companies including Nintendo, Intel, US Air, Taco Time, Fred Meyer, Legacy Health Systems, Nikon, United Way and more. Demonstrated strengths also include exceptional communication skills, client relations, networking, time management and organizational skills. I hold a BS from Oregon State University and am a honors graduate of the Air National Guard Technical Training School.
