JD Morris’s Summary

CONTACT:

J David Morris (JD) focuses on buyouts starting at $5 million EBITA and S.E.A. Program (See Slide Show on FULL PROFILE). He also serves on the board of advisors and board of directors of several private and publicly traded companies.

He is a General Partner of Red Hook Capital. He also has served in leadership roles in strategy, corporate and business development for Lockheed Martin, MCI Corp., and other companies where he played key roles in the creation and development of several new ventures.

JD's investment banking background has resulted in many successful deals. JD advises clients ranging on business strategy, mergers and acquisitions, revenue models and future growth opportunities.

He has been a speaker at various industry forums, has been quoted in numerous leading publications, such as the Wall Street Journal, and has made several appearances on CNET and CNBC.

He received a B.A. in economics with mathematics from Hampden-Sydney College in Virginia and Omega Rho student with focus in operations research (#4 country) from the George Washington University in Washington, D.C.
