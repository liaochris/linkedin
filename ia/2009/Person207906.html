Samir Desai’s Summary

Samir Desai is a Senior Technical Product Manager with 13 years of experience in the PLM industry. His primary responsibilities involve defining the roadmap for the product through interaction with customers, prospects and the marketplace by understanding how Teamcenter products are utilized and what the future needs are. Prior to this role, Samir has held various technical and managerial positions in PLM development, with the most recent being a Senior Development Manager responsible for the Teamcenter modeling services including Database Services, Core Model & its IDE. Samir has also held managerial responsibilities for Teamcenter Industry Solutions, such as Aerospace & Defense, Automotive, Consumer Packaged Good and High Tech Electronics and exposed to customers of these industries.
