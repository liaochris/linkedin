Elaine Lim’s Summary

I am a generalist who specializes in communication. This entrepreneurial spirit allows me to have knowledge in more than one area of a specialization, which proved to be very useful in the development and management of projects.

As of 1st July, 2009, I have exactly 4 years, 5 months & 17 days of experience as a freelance communication specialist and web content writer.

What I do is... I specialize on marketing solutions, data management and provide content strategies. I am great with graphic design softwares like Fireworks, Photoshop, Dreamweaver as well as all MS Office softwares. I am an internet geek with a soft spot for social networking tools, blogging and search engine optimization (SEO). Graduating from psychology has a lot of perks too when it comes to understanding human behaviors, even on the internet.

I am experienced in a multitude of written media including books, training manuals, brochures, newsletters, website, press releases, web content, articles and even speeches. The worldwideweb is a mass of opportunities waiting to be tapped, so I believe that user experience is important, along with structural and content optimization.

Aside from writing and web content management, I also have hands on experience in recruitment, business administration, teaching, and food critiquing. I am also an avid blogger with two personal blogs and ten other blogs where I contribute to frequently under a pen-name.

What I don't do .... is programming. While I am quite well versed on HTML with a great understanding for CSS, I am more comfortable letting certified programmers code, while I work along side them. I do know someone who does it very well, though.

I am currently open to full time opportunities and other prospects.
