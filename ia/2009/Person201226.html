Rob Garretson’s Summary

Award-winning business and technology journalist with more than 28 years experience — including as a writer and editor on the Financial desk at the Washington Post — extensive background in online publishing and more than 17 years in senior editorial management.

Rob Garretson’s Specialties:

Wall Street; patents/intellectual property law; technology deployment for competitive advantage; electronic securities trading & financial technology; cable/satellite/broadcasting; pretty much all things high-tech.
