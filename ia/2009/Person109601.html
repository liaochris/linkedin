Jennifer J. Kunkel’s Summary

I joined Quick Leonard Kieffer in 2005 and currently serve as a Principal. Previously, I served as an Associate Director, as well as a Senior Associate Director, assisting the firm’s Managing Partners on many assignments at executive and middle management levels.

Prior to this, I was involved in both the healthcare and consulting arenas as a member of a privately held medical device consulting firm in Chicago. I also completed a temporary assignment with the University of Illinois Medical Center, working closing with administration and physicians in the Department of Radiology. My work here largely centered on assisting the medical staff with preparing and executing presentations of their work for academic purposes.

At QLK, I have had a heavy involvement in the not-for-profit arena (working with hospitals and health systems of all sizes, including academic medical centers), and have also successfully assisted with Board searches for publicly traded companies. Additionally, my client list has included associations and public universities. I have extensive experience conducting searches at both executive and middle management levels.

I am originally from rural Illinois and, after completing a portion of my education abroad (United Kingdom), moved to Chicago and earned a bachelor of arts degree in communications, with dual concentrations in English and gender & women's studies, from the University of Illinois at Chicago.

I desire to continue serving my clients and candidates with a high level of attention and in-depth communication. I believe that each and every applicant deserves respect and to be treated fairly; I feel the same way about my clients. I strive to truly partner with all those I work with (colleagues, clients, candidates, vendors), to achieve satisfactory, long-term relationships that end with mutually beneficial results.
