Brad Christopher’s Summary

Looking at Brad Christopher's work, one can't help but notice his timeless classical style. It is the perfect blend of sexiness and sophistication. Its gritty undertones are thought provoking and help lend itself to the appeal of all types of people. Brad Specializes in the portraiture of celebrities, musicians, and other Artists. The opportunity to help any type of artist portray their personality to the public is his passion. It is this same passion that enables Brad's work to expand into the commercial world. With backgrounds in Advertising and Communications, fashion designers and corporations alike are drawn to BC for his ability to speak to the consumer image. Brad works and can be seen in major cities around the globe including Los Angeles, New York, London, Paris, Miami, and Milan.



My Mission Statement:

The movement of Brad Christopher photography is intensely fueled by realness. This realness is artists portrayed in the rawest of forms. In this advanced technological society we live in, photography and film have made massive steps towards perfection. Massive production budgets have helped create this clean almost supernatural appeal in most of the art we see today. As a result, I believe many have lost that connection with their subject. I believe it lacks nostalgia, feeling, and impact. My movement takes me a step backward in time to a place where Photography and the visual arts bring you that raw - realness that helps us identify with our subject. Beauty is within the emotion of the subject.

Brad Christopher’s Specialties:

I am a Los Angeles based photographer but my work can be seen around the globe. My expertise are, but not limited to, celebrity portraiture, fashion, editorial portraiture, musicians, artists, advertising campaigns, look books, and set photography.
