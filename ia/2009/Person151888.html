Mark Gray’s Summary

The Country's Most Recognized Name In Recruitment For The Household Goods Industry

Whether you're the hiring authority seeking the best possible talent or an industry professional contemplating a new career challenge, make MOVERS SEARCH GROUP your confidential and discreet starting point. As we enter our fifteenth year of serving the Moving & Storage Industry exclusively, we're known among your peers as THE place where talent meets opportunity.

Our principal, Mark Gray, is a product of the industry he serves. Having grown up in the moving and storage business (Mark's father, Sam Gray, was a 30+ year industry veteran), Mark has experienced everything from working on the trucks to selling relocation services to Fortune 500 Companies. This background gives him the knowledge and industry experience to help movers find the right candidates for open positions and further, provides Gray the unique insight needed to understand the goals and objectives of an industry professional looking to make a career change.
