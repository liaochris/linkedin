Capt. Berend Lens van Rijn’s Summary

Fourteen years of experience, together with a broad skill set and the right attitude have led to Berend becoming a well-known, published name in the UAE maritime world and abroad. As a successful captain, fleet manager and entrepreneur, Berend has established a solid and respectable reputation for himself in the world of yachting in the broadest sense, with a large and loyal personal network.
