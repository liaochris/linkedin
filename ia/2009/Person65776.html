Don Morrison’s Summary

Don Morrison
New Venture Funding and Growth

Focus on the needs of entrepreneurs completing Seed, Angel and Series A venture funding. Providing the support CEOs need to ensure their time is focused on product, people & customers.

Aligning the business goals, strategies and people essential to great execution and results. Experience spans startups to public companies across global markets.

From 2005 to 2007 Don serve as CEO of Remend an OnDemand company providing SaaS solutions integrating the mortgage banking and real-estate industry addressing many of the industry's current challenges.

Remend introduced the first Salesforce AppExchange OEM application in showcasing a new web application for realtors. In 2007, Remend became the first application providing a SaaS based mortgage servicing suite in partnership with Microsoft.

Don was President of Chordiant, an SOA based applications company serving retail banks and credit card issuers. Don joined Chordiant in 1997 with two customers and grew the business to hundreds of global customers, annual revenues of $120 million, a successful pubic offering and led five acquisitions.

Don has international experience introducing numerous new software products in Asia, Latin America and Europe. Additionally, Don initiated a number of international investments and research operations in countries such as; Taiwan, Brazil, India and the UK.

Previous experience included marketing and sales management, product planning, and industry partnerships with companies such as SMI, an AC Nielsen company, and field operations and marketing positions with the Santa Cruz Operation (SCOC). Don held marketing management positions with system companies in the UNIX and internet marketplace.
