Ian White’s Summary

Journalist, editor, sub-editor, columnist, copywriter, publisher. Experience at all levels in nationals, dailies, weeklies, b2b magazines, monthly magazines.

Ian White’s Specialties:

Ability to turn tech jargon into English. Spotted the potential of two tech revolutions early enough to launch magazines about them - mobile phones and Macintosh computers (wow ... do you think everyone in the world might use one of these gadgets one day?) but wasn't quite clever enough to get in front of the internet curve and spot the rise of social networking. Favourite business motto: “Meetings are indispensable when you don't want to do anything.”
