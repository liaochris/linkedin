Dawn Ward’s Summary

I have been studying wellness and preventative measures for wellness for years on my own. In the last 2 years it has taken a more professional turn when I went to work for a network marketing company that specializes in Glyco Nutrients and cutting edge wellness supplements. These supplements dramatically changed my health and caused me to begin learning as much as possible about how we can improve our health no matter what state we are in. Through my research in this area, I stumbled on some amazing psychological techniques for changing our emotional triggers at the pre-conscious level, again finding amazing results in my own life. This and my work with Klemmer and Associates led me to a company in Australia (www.slimminds-USA.com) that has taken these techniques and put them together with the latest in healthy eating and created a company called SlimMinds. I have recently trained with them as an independent facilitator and obtained my NLP and EFT certifications and will be traveling the country teaching these techniques to those who want to change their shape (physically, or any other shape in their lives). I am so excited to have the opportunity to create abundant change in the lives of all that I touch. I have also begun an informational website (www.wellness-without-drugs.com)for those lay people who want more information on natural alternatives for their health. This includes physical, emotional and spiritual. We have been told that Wellness is the next trillion dollar industry, meaning Americans are starting to understand how important it is to focus on staying well, instead of treating sickness. I am so excited to have such a passion about something that the world is truly ready for. I love working and coaching others to achieve and transform the dreams in their lives.
