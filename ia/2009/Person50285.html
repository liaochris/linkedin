Christopher Ralph’s Summary

I am a hockey writer with a focus on the future of hockey – the prospects and the entry draft. I am currently living in Calgary, but I grew up in Newfoundland. I also avidly follow the Calgary Flames, which is my focus at The Hockey Writers (THW).

I know am the Chicago Blackhawks correspondent for the Fanball Network (fanball.com) with my own website: Paint It Blackhawks - http://paintitblackhawks.com

Wayne Gretzky once said:”A good hockey player plays where the puck is. A great hockey player plays where the puck is going to be.”

I attempt to take readers not just where hockey is right now, but where it’s going in the future.

I am also employed as a Clinical Pharmacist in a Cancer Pain Clinic, working side-by-side two pain specialist physicians.

My other interests/hobbies include: playing guitar, playing ball hockey, tennis, golf, football, soccer, song writing, rollerblading, reading, and traveling.

My own blog can be found at http://hockeyspy.blogspot.com where you can find prospect profiles, entry draft rankings and my detailed mock drafts.

I am also experimenting with a football (soccer) blog on the Manchester United Red Devils by The Soccer Spy.
http://manchesterunited-reddevils.blogspot.com/

Lastly, I am also the creator/editor of an Oncology Pharmacist website/blog - Onco-PRN.
http://onco-prn.blogspot.com

Feel free to contact me at: cral66@gmail.com
