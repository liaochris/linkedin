Erik Qualman’s Summary

Erik Qualman is the Global Vice President of Online Marketing for EF Education headquarted in Lucerne, Switzerland. Qualman works out of the 950 person Cambridge, MA office. EF Education is the world's largest private educator with 26,000 employees in 53 countries.

Prior to joining EF Education in 2008, Qualman was the Head of North American Marketing operations for Travelzoo Inc. (NASDAQ TZOO). While with Travelzoo he oversaw the functions of brand marketing, trade marketing, subscriber acquisition, search engine marketing & optimization, Web site design, loyalty/retention and public relations.

Prior to joining Travelzoo in 2005, Qualman helped grow the Internet Marketing and eBusiness functions of Cadillac & Pontiac (’94-’97), BellSouth (’98-’00), Yahoo!(’00-’03) and EarthLink (’03-’05). Qualman holds a BA from Michigan State University and an MBA from The University of Texas at Austin (McCombs).

Qualman is a frequently requested speaker within the Internet and Marketing community. He has been highlighted in such media outlets as BusinessWeek, American Airlines Radio, AdvertisingAge, Media Life, eWeek, The Financial Times, Investor's Business Daily, Direct Response Magazine, Search Engine Magazine and Direct Marketing News.

Qualman is also a published author – more information is available at www.american-novel.com. A former basketball player at Michigan State University, Qualman still finds time to follow his beloved Spartans.
