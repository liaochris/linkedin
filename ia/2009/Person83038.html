Gail Hood’s Summary

I have an insatiable passion for marketing and advertising! My involvement in it has allowed me to serve my clients in several capacities over the years. I began as a graphic artist and eventually became an Art Director for a national publication. I later moved into agency work at both Schey Advertising and Metzdorf Advertising.

My industry experience includes Oil & Gas, Real Estate, Financial, Retail, Computer Hardware and Software, Manufacturing, Wholesale, Healthcare and Energy. It's exhilarating to be an integral part of your clients or your company’s success in the marketplace.

I enjoy creative project/account and creative services management, participating with and leading teams to successful outcomes and working hard to give my clients an edge over their competition whether it is a conception to launch campaign or a series of smaller projects that contribute to the whole.

My expertise highlights include strategic marketing programs, print and online initiatives, web content development, media relations, press releases, event development, production management and brand management. I enjoy fast-paced environments and am not intimidated in the least by tight deadlines. Please let me know how I can help your company succeed!
