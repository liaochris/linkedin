James Willney’s Summary

Project Management, Print Design, Print Production Management. Manage all aspects of client projects (Agency & Corporate) that include estimates, photography, pre-press, print, assembly, direct mail, fulfillment, invoicing and R&D. Review and negotiate pricing with external vendors to generate estimates and schedules. Supervise pre-press, color correction, retouching, proofing and press checks. Accountable for issuing purchase orders in a timely manner and checking all vendor invoices against purchase orders and estimates. Communicate with all internal departments and outside vendors to manage projects and meet deadlines.
