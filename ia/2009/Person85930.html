Gert Jordens’s Summary

My unique organic garlic beer is available in 6 countries in more than 200 beermarkets, more than 200 bioshops and more than 100 pubs. See my website for availability in your neighbourhood. Besides those sales activities I create my own beers at our experimental brewery at home in the weekend. During the week I spend my time on the computer as independent test consultant.
In several projects I guided junior members of the team to let them go as experienced members in new projects.
(Almost) All projects were finished within time frame & high quality level as agreed in front.
