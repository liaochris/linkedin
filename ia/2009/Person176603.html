Noelle Mena’s Summary

Opened Take Root and Write Publishing http://www.takerootandwrite.com/ and Christian Women Take Root http://www.christianwomentakeroot.com/ on July 17th, 2008.

Designed and maintain both sites and put together a team of over 45 writers to write for Take Root and Write where they each contribute to their own column covering their area of expertise and passion. All focus is on the Christian Woman.

Full Editing & Publishing Team are now in place as well.

The Digital version of the Magazine is delivered once a week.

We have a community I created called, Christian Women Take Root. There are over 750 Christian women there today.

The focus is on ministry for women to, Get Encouraged, Get Inspired, Get Rooted and Grow in all God has for them.

Take Root and Write is a Featured Blog/Magazine on Wellsphere and All Top

TRW Publishing has recently started Christian Women Tweet Up CWTU. This is hosted at TRW. CWTU started in Dec. 2008. We also have our CWTU Twitter Group page http://www.twittgroups.com/group/cwtu
Also our Facebook Page for CWTU

My goal at this point is to first, take care of the women who come to both sites, keeping true to the focus of ministry, and meeting todays Christian Woman where she is at. It is very important to me that the community not be about numbers but about change. Change in passion, perspective, and pursuit! That every woman who enters the doors of CWTR finds the support, encouragement and inspiration she needs to move into the next level of all God has called her to be.

Then to expand and affiliate with companies, services, sites, ministries that TRW/CWTR can work with to promote both them and us. So we can reach more and more Christian Women. Unite them together to create Affiliation, Accountability and Action!

Due to open in Fall 2009-
