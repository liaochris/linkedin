Michael Lefkowitz’s Summary

Michael Lefkowitz is the founder of the Benjamin Ross Group, a business sales, mergers and acquisitions firm, a nationally recognized expert in buying and selling businesses and author of WHERE’S THE EXIT, an exit planning book for business owners. Michael has more than 20 years of business sale experience.

Michael has received the prestigious “Merger & Acquisition Master Intermediary” (M&AMI) designation from M & A Source, a division of the International Business Brokers Association. Only 68 people in the world, including three in Pennsylvania, hold this elite status. In addition, Michael is a Certified Business Intermediary (CBI) – a highly regarded designation in the business brokers’ field which involves extensive education and testing from the International Business Brokers Association.

Before founding the Benjamin Ross Group, he was responsible for acquisitions of two publicly traded companies and was involved in acquisitions throughout the United States. In addition to his work in the public sector, Michael has successfully sold two of his family’s privately held businesses – and because of this – he really understands what it is to be the owner of a business for sale.

Michael has been an adjunct professor of business management at Temple University and at his alma mater, the University of the Sciences (formerly Philadelphia College of Pharmacy and Science). He is a member of several area chambers of commerce, the Institute of Business Appraisers; the Pennsylvania Business Brokers Association, the International Business Brokers Association, and Vistage International, the world’s largest CEO organization. With a strong belief in giving back, Michael not only shares his time, but has established the Benjamin Ross Group Scholarship in 2008 to assist in the cost of college tuition for deserving students, through the Golden Slipper Club and Charities, a group that sponsors a camp for underprivileged children and other philanthropic endeavors .
