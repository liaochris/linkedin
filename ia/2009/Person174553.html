Nicola Fragale’s Summary

I am a Software Engineer graduated at "Federico II" University of Naples, with a dissertation on Linux embedded systems with the title: "Realizzazione di un sistema Linux embedded per applicazioni critiche".

Currently I am working as consultant for MBDA missile system, in IRAD (Internal Research And Development), where I am included in the team that deals with the implementation of the Finmeccanica's Linux distribution.

I am also involved in manteinance and updating the kickstarts of some projects and with the installation/administration of those projects.

Before this experience I have developed some GTK+ applications released under the term of GNU GPL, Rubrica (http://rubrica.berlios.de) and Dispatcher (http://dispatcher.berlios.de).

Rubrica is an addressbook manager for the GNOME Environment, and is available for all major Linux distributions.
It was my software laboratory, where I tested each new technology I learned (Software engineering's principles, UML, GTK+, Glib, Gobject, XML/XSLT, OOP, rpm packaging and spec files, libraryes and plugins (shared object) building, autotools (automake, autoconf, libtool), Subversion, CSV and VCard file format,...).

Rubrica has been reviewed by some Linux Magazines (Linux Magazine n° 17 2002, Linux Journal Japan n°5 may 2003, the Italian Linux Pro n° 47 december 2006, Linux Pro Magazine n°83 2007). It Has been reviewed by Eugenia Loli-Queru on Gnome Desktop and by the Linux Magazin Online.

From december 2001 to agoust 2003, I have collaborated with the Pluto Journal, first as columnist, writing some articles on GTK+/GNOME programming (pluto journal's numbers: 34, 35, 36, 37, 38, 39) and then, from n° 36 (april 2002), as redactor too
