Andrew Wahlquist’s Summary

Independent filmmaker working with a split personality between the creative (writer/director) and the technical (producer/editor/multimedia consultant).

Creative endeavors include an audio play adaptation of GK Chesterton's "The Man Who Was Thursday" (http://www.manwhowasthursday.com) and an independent feature film "The Association" (http://www.associationmovie.com)

Past experience in the industry working at Phoenix Pictures (All the Kings Men, Zodiac, Holes), Mandate Pictures (The Grudge, Stranger than Fiction), Paramount, Artists Management Group, Ocean Pictures, The Canton Company. Technical experience as SpeedGrade colorist for Local Hero Post and A/V director for FBCD.

Andrew Wahlquist’s Specialties:

Independent film production, High Definition digital acquisition, Digital post production workflows, screenplay development
