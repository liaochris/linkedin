Chris Paton’s Summary

I trained as a doctor at Nottingham University in the UK. I've worked for the NHS, Cambridge University, Otago University and I'm currently a Research Fellow at the National Institute for Health Innovation at the University of Auckland, New Zealand.

My current research projects include:

- electronic drug adherence monitoring
- evaluation of clinical decision support systems
- adoption of HL7, CEN 13606 and openEHR standards
- electronic Case-Based Learning
- social networking and public health informatics

I also founded a medical e-learning development company called New Media Medicine Ltd. NMM Ltd create high quality 3D anatomical and biomedical animations and interactive Flash learning modules for healthcare workers. We also operate a social networking website for doctors and medical students with 45,000 members.

Chris Paton’s Specialties:

Health Informatics - Electronic Health Records, Mobile Computing (Smartphones, PDAs, Tablets, MCAs), Healthcare e-Learning, HL7, Personal Health Records

Web Development - Web 2.0, Virtual Communities, e-Learning, Flash Development
