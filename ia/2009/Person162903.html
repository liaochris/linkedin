Michelle Casto’s Summary

Michelle L. Casto is the principal of Everyday Enlightenment, a publishing, coaching, and training company that emcompasses Brightlight Coaching and Get Smart! Training.

She is the author of over 25+ books and ebooks on the topic of Life Empowerment including the reknown series of Get Smart! books:
Get Smart! About Modern Romantic Relationships, Get Smart! About Modern Career Development Get Smart! About Modern Stress Management.

Michelle holds a Ph.D. in Life Coaching from Universal University, a Master of Education degree from the University of South Carolina, a Bachelor of Science degree in Interpersonal Communication from Ohio University, and is a nationally Certified Global Career Development Facilitator Instructor and Customer Service Trainer. She is certified to administer and interpret the Myers Brigg Type Indicator. She received professional training from the Coaches Training Institute of San Rafael, California and Coachville.com, an international coach training organization. She has taught at numerous universities across the United States and is a sough after inspirational teacher whose experiential style engages audiences on a core level.

Training
Michelle specializes in important topics such as relationships, customer service, communication, diversity, MBTI personality assessment, creativity, career development, and stress management. Her philosophy of learning is to make it fun, interactive, and to get results.
Coaching
Her coaching practice is Brightlight Coaching. She specializes in entrepreneurs and small business owners and bringing coaching skills into the workplace.
