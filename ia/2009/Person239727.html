Uma Gupta’s Summary

Dr. Uma Gupta enjoys building partnerships between individuals and institutions and introducing her home country of India to the world. Originally from India, she is currently the President & CEO of USAsiaEdu (www.usasiaedu.com), a Divsion of Global Cube Consultants (www.globalcubeIT.com), a New York based company, that assists colleges and universities in the US recruit Asian students and build alliances with Asian institutions.

She holds a doctorate in engineering, an MBA, a graduate degree in mathematic, and is a certified Project Management Professional. She has twenty plus years of experience in higher education and corporate training and is a seasoned global traveler. She has served as a consultant to a Fortune 500 financial institution on talent recruitment and retention.

Additionally, Dr. Gupta has served as a Senior advisor for Women and minorities in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics to the State University of New York. She has published two major text books on technology management, edited a book on Artificial Intelligence, and published more than sixty research papers. She has been quoted in Forbes.com and Reuters and is a highly sought after speaker and author.

"My most cherished dream: to dedicate my life to help those in need, particularly opening the doors of education to the less privileged."
