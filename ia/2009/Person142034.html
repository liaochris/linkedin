Lisa Head’s Summary

My primary background is in sales and marketing. I have developed sales teams all across the USA and beyond and truly enjoy the team building and relationships. This talent has served me well over the last several years - it's not about how good you are individually, but how good you are together as a TEAM (Together Everyone Achieves More).

My desire is to help develop entrepreneurs/business owners in various industries, including the network marketing industry. My passion is speaking and training to sales teams across the nation and helping to empower and inspire belief in ones self and others.

My husband and I have a goal to travel the world and impact lives while we do it!

Lisa Head’s Specialties:

My primary area of focus is in the largest industries in the world -- Travel and the Internet. I've partnered with an amazing Travel company, WorldVentures/Rovia, and bring to market unique travel services. I also teach entrepreneurs how to capture a piece of the online revolution. I firmly believe that creating multiple income streams today is the best business move one can make, especially in today's economy.
