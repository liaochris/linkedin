David Purdue’s Summary

Customer focused IT Professional with extensive service delivery management experience for major corporations through the Asia Pacific and IT infrastructure management experience. Track record of liaising and using influencing and negotiation skills to ensure favourable outcomes for major global customers in a competitive environment for Sun Microsystems, and of managing teams to deliver superior quality IT services consistently within budget for Tattersall’s and Frontline.

Broad technical knowledge gained from wide IT industry experience, with a special interest in Sun Microsystems technologies. A passion for learning new technologies and leveraging them to improve business outcomes. A quick learner, with excellent written and verbal communication skills, and high level interpersonal skills who acts with honesty and integrity.

Holds B.Sc. and M.Sc. in computing, as well as ITIL foundation certificate. Sun Certified System Administrator.
