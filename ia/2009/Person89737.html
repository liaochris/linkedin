Greg Golden’s Summary

Luxury Home Broker for many years first in Malibu and then in the Palm Springs Valley. In Malibu it was beachfront estates and in Palm Springs it was superior golf course homes. Now focusing hard in the commercial & investment arena, especially in the high end distressed properties, including golf courses and large multi-family complexes. Now in Southern Nevada too. Look forward to participating in a big way in the rebirth of the real estate market both in Southern California and Southern Nevada.
