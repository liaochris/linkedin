Patrick van Abbema’s Summary

As Chief Business Analyst for AltNexus Inc., Patrick provides consulting expertise on enterprise software strategies for various public- and private-sector clients. He applies a disciplined approach to elicit, analyze, communicate and validate requirements for changes to business processes, policies and information systems, which enable him to recommend solutions that help organizations achieve their goals.

Patrick’s experience as both a Certified Project Manager and Business Analyst allow him to bring the unique skills and knowledge areas required for both disciplines to the table. His experience allows him to develop the strategies to ensure that the product will be built correctly, according to an approved set of requirements, on time and within budget.

Patrick van Abbema’s Specialties:

Expert in helping organizations better manage software delivery through Open Application Lifecycle Management. He addresses today’s chaotic software development lifecycle with solutions for:

* Enterprise Analysis
* Process Improvement
* IT Project and Portfolio Management
* Requirements Definition and Management
* Software Delivery Management
* Lifecycle Quality Management
* Change Management
* Enterprise Collaborative Content Management
