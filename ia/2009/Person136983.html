Larry Emsweller’s Summary

Mr. Emsweller has spent 35 years working in broadcasting, publishing, magazines, television production syndication, and marketing. He’s performed at the local, national, and corporate executive level of companies such as Viacom, Meredith, General Electric Broadcasting, Paramount, World Vision, and many others as an executive and consultant working directly with CEO's and group presidents of multi-billion dollar companies. During the last five years, he has consulted at the CEO level on a confidentiality-basis only as well as working with smaller businesses bringing his unique skill sets to increase their revenues through marketing and tactical strategies designed for any size budget.

Starting out as a reporter and anchor, Mr. Emsweller became a news executive. He has run television stations and is noted throughout the industry as a turnaround expert. He was critically involved in the creation of two Emmy-winning nationally syndicated shows, produced a number of network programs, started new divisions for media corporations, and developed many different business models for the media and other industires. These skill sets are transferable to any business.

As it relates to his news, media, advertising and PR background, Larry’s unique knowledge of the inner workings of what makes a good story and how to get through to the right people, select the right concet to get coverage for great PR is the experience that gives him the knowledge that few have. This is why his advice and mentorship can have a major impact on the success of your business.

Larry quotes the Bob Buford book HALF TIME as his reason for turning his attention to business consulting. He says he spent the first half of his life looking for substance in his life. Now he is seeking significance. He says his skill sets have contributed to major corporations making hundreds of millions of dollars. He now wants to work with smaller companies trying to make their dreams a reality.
