Carlos Herrera’s Summary

I'm absolutely in love with creating, I can easily say it makes me feel alive and I don't like restricting myself to just one medium or tool, always open to experimenting new techniques and pushing my own limits to see what's possible.

I've been Art Director at world-reknowned Advertising Agencies such as Young & Rubicam and DDB at their Mexico divisions.

I'm currently involved in occasional projects with heavyweight Drum n Bass label Lifted Music (www.liftedmusic.com) as a part of their visual/graphics crew.

Permanent news editor and contributor at Computerlove since 2006 (www.cpluv.com) and Region4 (www.region4.org.mx).

Work published in various books, magazines and design portals since 2001 (99 Silhouettes book, SIXIS Background Book, Worldwide Designers 2007, Scene360 Love Book, Blank Magazine, A! Diseño Magazine, Surfstation, Newstoday, Pixelsurgeon...) as well as panel speaker and exhibitor at domestic and foreign events: Flash For Mexico (Querétaro, Mexico 2004), CENTRO Architecture, Film and Design School (Mexico City, 2004); Online Flash Film Festival aka OFFF (Valencia 2004, Barcelona 2005, Spain); E3's Premiere Exhibition @Anónimo (Puebla, Mexico 2006), México: Diseño de Exportación (Sonora, Mexico 2007), E3: Especies En Extinción (Mexico City 2007)...

Selected clients: Nike, McDonald's, Philip Morris, Camel, Danone, Avon Cosmetics, Appleton Jamaica Rum, Lifted Music, Dave & Buster's, Vive Latino...

2 years experience in Mass Media Advertising and nearly a decade in Digital Graphics/Computer Art (1999+).

Carlos Herrera’s Specialties:

Identity and branding, digital photography, digital image processing, graphic design and illustration for bands/musicians/DJ's, poster design, product shots, portraits, action photography, location photography, travel photography, stock photography, nature photography, landscapes, urban art/culture, digital illustration
