Juan Mota’s Summary

VinoSystems is a System Integrator Specializing in Home Intelligence; we enable your home to make intelligent Money Saving decisions using a processor and sensors strategically installed throughout your house. At Vsys we have chosen to team with the highest quality systems available that is why we use Lutron for our lighting control, Crestron for our touch screens and Total control, and Kaleidescape for our Audio and Video Server.
We design and create a common platform, on which all the devises will communicate, we then design our User interface to be intuitive and it must be usable by anyone in any language.
VSYS designs a project, creates CAD documentation in a simple to follow format so that you’re Electrical Contractor can do the complete installation. We offer project management services to assure that the installation is completed as Designed. We work hand in hand with the Client to assure that the Programming addresses all the client’s needs. Our job is not complete until all the As-builts are in reality AS-Built; we supply our Client with Electronic documentation and keep a copy of all documentation on our Web Site where it will be accessible to our Client from anywhere in the World.
