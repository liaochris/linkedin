Shannon du Plessis’s Summary

I am a professional technical communicator who successfully creates technical writing solutions for clients from my fully equipped home office with the occasional on-site visit as needed. Complete details are available at www.techwriteontime.com.

My former life (when I worked for someone else) included heavy accounting and budgeting work that gradually morphed into documenting policies and procedures, and acting as lead user on several software conversion projects. I decided I liked writing much more than crunching numbers and have been writing for several years now.

I have documentation experience in the following industries: software, e-learning, real estate, medical, health and fitness, accounting and finance, travel, insurance, security, and state government.

I would be pleased to handle your technical writing needs. Please let me know if I can help you.
