Corey Turner’s Summary

Entrepreneur and Internet business developer with a background in network engineering, Internet marketing and owning/operating auto repair centers. I have been marketing aftermarket products on search engines/eBayMotors since 1997.

Outside-the-box concepts. I deliver a creative, yet analytical approach to marketing automotive repair and generating sales leads.

Less hype--More action--More essentials--Less of what is not important.

I have 11 years experience in Internet marketing and am certified by both Google and Yahoo.

My clients are automotive, transmission and body repair center owners in the US. Unlike most firms, we work exclusively with our clients in local areas to maximize the value of our services.

Corey Turner’s Specialties:

Our team specializes in generating sales leads for auto repair centers including transmission, collision and general repair leads. We deliver leads in real-time via fax, e-mail and text message. We also design custom shop websites and localized Internet marketing programs to drive traffic to our clients.
