Bill Boothby’s Summary

Performance driven Product Development / Engineering Operations professional with extensive experience leading a variety of functional areas throughout entire product life cycle including: feature definition, design validation and debug, product development, performance and yield enhancement, test engineering, volume production delivery, supplier performance management, customer support services, quality, reliability and failure analysis.

Demonstrated ability to successfully deliver solutions in a high-volume manufacturing environment through engagement with international customer and supply-base leadership to negotiate business agreements and solve complex problems.

Known for assembling and inspiring diverse, high-performance cross-functional teams. Unique talent for deriving useful information from technical data, understanding a customer’s needs, and translating those needs into results.

  • Department Manager, Product Development Engineering

    AMD

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; AMD; Semiconductors industry)

    1996 — 2000 (4 years)

    Assembled and led a team of product and test engineers developing AMD's initial “K7” microprocessor product in 0.25µm poly gate CMOS technology. Responsible for product definition, engagement with technology development groups and selection of appropriate test and debug features of the AMD-K7 microprocessor.

  • Section Manager, Product Development Engineering

    AMD

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; AMD; Semiconductors industry)

    1992 — 1996 (4 years)

    Managed several teams of experienced and new-graduate engineers, technicians and co-op students responsible for product development, production launch and high volume manufacturing of the Am486 microprocessor in 0.7µm and 0.5µm, and the Am5x86 microprocessor in 0.35µm CMOS technology beginning with initial design, layout and simulation of the device, continuing with multiple product reliability qualifications and product performance enhancements. Also responsible for overseas wafer fabrication area interface and high-volume production ramp of these products at several international manufacturing sites. Recognized with two AMD "Spotlight Awards" during this tenure for "Record-breaking Production Launch Schedule Execution" and "Exemplary Customer Service" for solving a critical customer problem.

  • Supervising Engineer, Product Development Engineering

    AMD

    Supervised a team of junior engineers to perform schematic capture and functional verification of the Am386SX device. Successfully completed reliability qualification and production launch of this device in 1.0µm poly gate CMOS process. Trained own replacement before transferring to the Am486 microprocessor development project.

  • Supervising Engineer, Product Engineering – Semi-Custom Products Division

    AMD

    (Public Company; AMD; Semiconductors industry)

Accountable for all key elements of product engineering including manufacturing, test, yield enhancement, quality and reliability, packaging, and customer interface.

  • Senior Product Engineer – Semi-Custom Products Division

    AMD

    (Public Company; AMD; Semiconductors industry)

Awarded prestigious "AMD Vice President's Award" for implementation of automated systems and corresponding photo mask cost and cycle time reduction in this role.
