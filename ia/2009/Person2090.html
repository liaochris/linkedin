Gregory Abrams’s Summary

Former legal counsel for the California Medical Association for nearly 15 years. Now in private practice as a partner in the Pacific West Law Group representing physicians, medical staffs, and other health care professionals. I am also General Counsel for the CMA's Institute for Medical Quality, an accreditation entity for ambulatory surgery centers and municipal and county jail health services. IMQ also works with the The Joint Commission and the California Department of Public Health to survey and inspect hospitals for accreditation and licensing purposes.

Gregory Abrams’s Specialties:

Representing physicians and medical staffs in numerous areas of law, strategies and policy regarding peer review, Medical Board discipline, reporting requirements, behavior/impairment, credentialing, EMTALA & on-call issues, medical staff bylaws/Rules & Regulations, medical staff committee operations, and all aspects of medical staff structure and function. Consistent with the policies and positions of organized medicine, I am a strong advocate for self-governance of my medical staff clients.
