Eric Snyder’s Summary

Eric Snyder is a proven strategic leader. As both senior executive and consultant, he has led visioning and strategic planning efforts for five corporations and dozens of divisions/departments.

He received his Master of Science in Industrial Administration (Operations) from Purdue University and has over 20 years of experience leading business process analyses and improvement efforts.

Eric spent 10 years as a business management consultant focused on the effective utilization of people, process, and technology to achieve maximum results.

He has excelled as a business advisor, mentor, and executive coach. He is a trained facilitator and educator and thoroughly enjoys helping people achieve successful lives. In 1999, in partnership with the Columbus Technology Leadership Council, Columbus Chamber of Commerce, and Ohio Business Technology Center, he created a network within central Ohio to connect entrepreneurs with legal and financial advisors, technology providers, and venture capital.
