Don Fahnestock’s Summary

My career with leading companies such as Colgate-Palmolive, Saatchi & Saatchi Advertising, R.J. Reynolds, NetJets and Bunge, has honed my in-depth experience in the marketing and sales management and general management functions, particularly in the areas of P&L management, sales, brand strategy, direct marketing, advertising and media, promotions, marketing communication, special events and PR. My U.S. and international business experience has been distinguished by consistent revenue growth, profitability, market share growth and new products launches.

I've developed and used my exceptional interpersonal, cross-cultural communication and relationship management skills to lead teams in moving projects and initiatives forward to successful and profitable outcomes in more than 30 countries and cultures.

I hold an MBA in Marketing and Finance from the Kellogg Graduate School of Management, at Northwestern University, am bilingual in English and German, a US citizen and a Swiss permanent resident.

Branding, brand strategy, brand development and launch, advertising, media strategy planning, consumer insights, C-level management, promotion, direct marketing, pricing, distribution, team building, leadership, multi-faceted, honest, goal-oriented, ambitious, turnaround, growth, change, decision-making, listening, cross-cultural, international.
