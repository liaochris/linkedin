Gregory Pettit’s Summary

I have nearly 25 years experience in corporate and financial communications, and a strong knowledge of strategic issues management and high-level corporate consulting. Over the years, I have advised companies of varying sizes based both in the U.S. and abroad on virtually all investor relations and corporate communications issues, including IPOs, managing of investor perceptions/expectations, turnarounds, bankruptcies and M&A and resulting integration.. I also have broad experience in mergers and acquisitions communications and regularly help companies list on the New York Stock Exchange and NASDAQ. I also provide corporate communications counsel to professional service firms and organizations on managing communciations and financial media relations.
