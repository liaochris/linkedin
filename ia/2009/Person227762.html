Sonia Vohnout’s Summary

Sonia Vohnout works at Ridgetop Group, Inc.. Ridgetop provides electronic Prognostics and IC Design services for critical applications. Sonia leads the efforts of securing government and corporate funding to support IR&D for existing and new company technologies. She maintains relationships with government customers and provides systems engineering support to the engineering staff. Sonia oversees the company’s efforts to build, grow, execute and sustain a solid business with government and prime contractors.

Sonia has business experience in the information technology, manufacturing, biotechnology, and electronics industries. During her career in the various industries, she has held executive management and senior technical positions. Sonia has also co-founded several companies

Sonia is a board member of the Society for Machinery Failure Prevention Technology (MFPT) (www.mfpt.org), an interdisciplinary technical organization strongly oriented toward practical applications. Recently she also joined the The Systems and Industrial Engineering Department and Engineering Management Industrial Advisory Committee at the University of Arizona. Sonia also stays busy with the Defense industry and other Government organizations, by being an active member of The National Defense Industrial Association (NDIA) Systems Engineering Division Enterprise Health Management (EHM) Committee.

Sonia recently founded the Prognostic and Health Management (PHM) Professionals LinkedIn Group (www.linkedin.com), a fast growing group whose objectives are to: Discuss PHM related topics, network with others in the PHM community, and increase awareness of PHM.

Sonia is a pro-active executive leader with a passion to apply new and innovative solutions to support corporate growth objectives. She is a creative and analytical thinker with a solid technical background and strong leadership, negotiation and communication skills.

A US Citizen, Sonia is also fluent in Spanish.
