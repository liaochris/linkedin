John Chubb’s Summary

As Chief of Construction, I manage $2.5B in construction and 252 major projects annually and direct 425 engineers and construction management professionals. Project growth increased 210% over the same period last year, requiring me to hire 212 new staff members in the last 18 months. I drove the adoption of earned value management by my district. Last quarter 16 major project were scheduled for completion and all 16 were turned over to the client on schedule and within budget. I also personally negotiated settlement of a $562 million dollar project bid protest, avoiding a six month project delay. Currently, I sit on the strategic committee for the implementation of software and acquisition management systems for USACE. I just presented QA web collaboration software at the Construction Management Association of America.

As business manager of Chubb Consulting Group, LLC, I provide certified Project Management Professional (PMP) training services in both public and on-site customized settings. I have trained 100s of students with extremely high pass rates for the PMP exam. My student ratings consistently average 4.6 on a 5 point scale. I specialize in cost, schedule, and earned value management consulting. I am currently developing a unique Train-the-Trainer program. I just presented project manage training at the National Hispanic Engineer Conference. Chubb Consulting, LLC is a Service Connected Disabled Veteran Owned Business. Our motto --Teach what you know, learn what you don't!
