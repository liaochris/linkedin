Stephen Beck’s Summary

With a focus in both creative theory and technicalities of online communication, Partner and Co-Creative Director at Engine Digital Inc., Stephen Beck has been working in interactive marketing for nearly ten years. Prior to founding Engine Digital in 2002, Stephen spent 3 years with Tinderbox (BBDO) as Art-Director, Interactive. Client experience includes Frito-Lay, Intrawest, Mission Hill Winery, Vivendi Universal, Paramount Pictures, Telus, The Blue Man Group, and Nintendo of America.

Please visit http://www.enginedigital.com

Stephen is also involved in typographic design and is currently designing his first typeface which should be released in 2008. Aside from design he also enjoys travel, photography, and vintage scooters.

Current Board Member, New Media BC
Past Board Member, Malaspina Printmakers Society
