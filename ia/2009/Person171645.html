Natalya Murphy’s Summary

Small business owner focused on fostering growth in our local community and enriching the lives of my clients and fellow businesspeople.

I first ventured into independent business with custom framing. I still love the framing business but I quickly realized that my greater passion was helping fellow businesspeople succeed and thrive in their own professional lives. With that goal in mind I started Website Remedies, a company dedicated to helping all organizations, especially small businesses, improve their website visibility.

I am a firm believer in giving back to others, so I volunteer my time to various civic and church organizations.
