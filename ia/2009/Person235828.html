Thomas Elam’s Summary

About 20 years of programming experience - not continuous. Most of my experience prior to my Wipro job was in systems and embedded programming.

Also worked a few years on electronics hardware, both digital and analog.

Recently retired from Wipro at age 58, per company policy. There I was doing mostly Lisp, JavaScript, and Scheme programming.

Always looking for ways to leverage Lisp/Scheme.

Looking for ideas on where to earn money since I retired from Wipro.

Thomas Elam’s Specialties:

I am mainly a C and Lisp programmer. I like both languages, but Lisp is absolutely amazing and fun -- extremely productive. There always seem to be ways I can bend it to my way of thinking -- unlike lower-level languages. But a much more interesting thing is that a certain degree of knowledge of Lisp explodes the boundaries of my thinking -- it changes the way I code and think about coding.

I have digital and analog design and assembly-language programming skills.
