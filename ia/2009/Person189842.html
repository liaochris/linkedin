Preeti Jhawar’s Specialties:

Her clothes spell vivacity and freshness in bubbly colours and prints accentuated with exquisite embroidery and uniquely processed fabrics. She focuses on a merged contemporary look—in prêt, diffusion and couture—utilitarian, Oriental, Indian kitsch and modern classic…. The look is fused vintage …in pulsating colours

Preeti Jhawar’s Experience

  • Fashion Designer & Managing Director

    Modstyl Designs Pvt ltd

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    November 2007 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Preeti’s collections take you on a creative journey that is unparalleled in the world of Indian couture. Mixing vintage styles with her current cultural obsessions, she effortlessly makes hip and exuberant original clothes. Her constant search for new ideas and challenges keeps her ahead of her times.
    The boundless energy and creative ingenuity of her ramp presentations always make her shows a high point.
    A fashion design diploma graduate awarded ‘Designer of the Year’,
    In 2002, she launched her own label, with outfits that have ‘robed’ celebrities in India and abroad.
    A true Indian designer with an international edge, Preeti Jhawar’s philosophy has cultivated into an eclectic collection that imbues an individual sense of style. Today, the label has grown to include kurtas, westerns, saris and lehengas, complemented with trendy bags and accessories, for Indian and international silhouettes.
