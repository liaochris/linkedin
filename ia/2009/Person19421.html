Arief Prabowo’s Summary

Over 8 years of experience in Object Pascal/Delphi language and malware analyst, the reverse engineering field, and application security.

As a antivirus engineer I design and develop anti-virus technologies with an inclination to enhance existing techniques as well as develop tools to automate malware collection, management, analysis and detection. I strive to stay up-to-date with the latest computer security threats and emerging malware trends.

I get down with debugging/disassembling tools to unravel the guts of today malware to exactly understand how today malicious authors are tailoring their attacks and system modifications in order to exploit computer users.

I also have knowledge in the aircraft field, (Boeing 737-200/300/400/500), experience in aircraft simulator (Software Engineer), and I had made the automated system for the aircraft (flight planning & load and balance).

Arief Prabowo’s Specialties:

Behavior based Malware analysis. Analyzing new and emerging threats and developing detection and cleanup routines. I've analyzed complicated malware like Conficker. Development of automated tools for malware analyst, static and dynamic reverse engineering, binary decompilation. Proficient with: OllyDbg, IDA, WinHex, SysInternals Tools, Virtual PC, VMWare, Sandboxie, and many others. Programming language: Deep knowledge Delphi/Object Pascal. Understanding Assembly, C/C++, VB, VBScript, HTML.
