David Nagy’s Summary

Software strategist, designer, and marketer with over 22 years experience in launching successful products and companies. Areas of expertise include Web, wireless, middleware, and networking technology and applications for the Manufacturing, Retail & Distribution, Consumer Goods, Transportation & Logistics, and Consumer markets.

Over the past few years have conceived, designed, and led development of system software, developer platforms, and applications for companies like FedEx Freight, General Electric Consumer Finance and Consumer & Industrial Divisions, Toyota, Boeing, Pepsi, and several mobile operators, as well as Web services and software for the consumer market.

David Nagy’s Specialties:

Product-market strategy and business development, Web and wireless technology, system design, enterprise applications, market segmentation & competitive analysis, go-to-market strategy and plans.
