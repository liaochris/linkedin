Luc Chase’s Summary

Senior consultant within finance, government and technology-led organisations. Extensive strategic planning experience with a view to planning and direction of cost effective solutions to business issues. An expert in analysis, design and implementation for business process optimisation in consumer and business-to-business markets. Extensive organisational, financial, staff management and business automation-design skills.
