Ali Bulut’s Summary

Ali Bulut, former CEO and Board Member of CNR Expo Holding, has been named the new Chief Executive Officer of Altinbas Jewelry Group. Ali Bulut (47), who has a degree on Mechaniceal Engineering, has spent his last 15 years on various executive positions at CNR Holding who is the Pioneer and leader of Trade show business in Turkey. His role was key on the success and growth of CNR since last 15 years

Ali Bulut said “Altinbas being the leader of the jewelry manufacturing and retail distribution in Turkey, has a vision to be a global name in this industry within coming 10 years period. We have a wide market penetration in Turkey and some access to the neighbouring countries like Bulgaria, Albania, Romania and Cyprus. Group has all the necessary resources to re-structure itself for a regional expanison Project. We have a very well educated and experienced manaement team which is our core competency to realize the new projects. Our strategic plan to be a market driven, global jewelry manufacturing and distribution group, encompass having 500 retail stores in Turkey of which one third will be abroad; outsourcing jewelry supply to the countries like Thailand and Vietnam, product differentiation through market segmentation, etc. We are considering to joint our forces with the other global and regioal players of the world jewelry business, as well. ”
