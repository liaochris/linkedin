Jeff Dumbell’s Summary

I am an experienced Oracle business intelligence specialist and Oracle 9i trained DBA with more than 12 years experience gained in a variety of industries. I have extensive data warehousing experience having worked on both green field projects and existing systems. I also have extensive experience of liaison with business users to enable requirements gathering, and to ensure that any developed data warehouse is fit for purpose and meets the needs of the users, whilst also being efficient in the storage and movement of large quantities of data.
