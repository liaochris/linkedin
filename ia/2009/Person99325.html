Gailen David’s Summary

Founder and Managing Director of Ionosphere Media and creator of the award winning training program, Jetiquette™. Extensive experience creating and facilitating innovative and engaging training / development programs that increase employee effectiveness and maximize customer experiences.

Track record of delivering highly effective business development strategies, employing new media to expand organizations’ market share and brand awareness. Proven ability to build successful entrepreneurial ventures across several industries, including Real Estate, Retail, Cosmetics and Transportation.

Ways in which Gailen David and Ionosphere Media work with clients to take their businesses to the next level:

WEB PRESENCE – Providing top-notch content and interactive areas, allowing clients to more effectively court potential customers and establish quality business relationships with professionals in related fields.

BRANDING – Setting clients apart from the competition by developing a comprehensive array of marketing tools and creative, including logos, taglines, graphics, company mission and values, blog and website banners, newsletter templates, press kits and much more.

MARKETING COLLATERAL – Creating distinct, favorable impressions of client’s companies while positioning products and services to target customers in the most compelling way possible.

VIDEO AND PODCAST PRODUCTION – Propelling clients into the 21st century and connecting them with future customers using the most up-to-date technology.

SOCIAL MEDIA SUPPORT – Leveraging new media (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.), allowing clients to expand their reach and connect with dynamic – and extremely valuable – demographics.

INTEGRATION – Delivering messages across multiple forms of media and assimilating content for the most profit-producing results

CUSTOMER SERVICE, ETIQUETTE AND LEADERSHIP TRAINING – Boost employee performance and raise the bar on the quality of care offered to clients.
