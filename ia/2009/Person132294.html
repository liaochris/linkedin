Kim Frank’s Summary

For more than two decades Kim Frank, Founder of Corp-Growth, has built businesses through an effective mix of Internal Innovation, Mergers & Acquisitions, Strategic Alliances and Fund Raising. Kim Frank has broad executive and technology expertise, having identified and negotiated more than 60 acquisitions and alliances ranging in size from $2M to $5B for start-ups, turn-arounds and Fortune 50 companies. Ms. Frank’s industry expertise includes semi-conductor, EDA, computer hardware and software, Internet and consumer electronics.

As Corp VP Mergers, Acquisitions and Alliances at Cadence Design Systems, Ms. Frank drove significantly higher acquisition success rates than tech industry benchmarks with 90% of acquisitions out-performing all targeted sources of value and 97% retention rates for acquisition employees over three years. This work was pivotal in bringing Cadence back to the global #1 technology and market position.

Ms. Frank’s experiences also include Assistant Treasurer responsible for M&A at Intel Corporation; M&A lawyer at distinguished law firms such as Latham & Watkins and Snell & Wilmer; General Counsel of a Japanese public company; Board Member of private technology companies; and interim executive of several technology start-ups and turn-arounds.
