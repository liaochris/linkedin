Steven Parkins’s Summary

Steve has been promoted to VP of Sales after 7 months as Director of Training and Recruitment at Brican America. Prior to this promotion, Steve had been hand-picked by the founder and President of Brican America to help this Miami-based international marketing firm recruit, hire, train and onboard a nationwide sales force.

He is a Member of the Fort Lauderdale Chapter of ASTD (American Society for Training & Development) and is in the process of becoming CPLP certified.

Steve is a 20-year veteran of global sales & marketing, leadership, and communications training and consulting. He lived in Asia and Europe for 20 years and worked in over 40 nations on six continents, bringing a unique international awareness to his training and management style. He has built numerous sales teams across the globe and designed and implemented international sales strategies for multiple organizations.

He has conducted direct sales to C-level executives in over 2000 organizations around the globe and has trained hundreds of others in the art and science of direct sales, leadership and communications.

Upon his return to the US after living abroad, he founded PICKS Training and successfully delivered training, coaching and consulting both nationally and internationally to a wide range of corporations before he was pursued by the President of Brican to head its nationwide build up.

His extensive experience in selling to C-level executives in virtually every industry provides internal and external clients a senior level, dynamic, energetic, humorous and worldly training consultant with broad commercial awareness and executive presence.
