Tait Grindley’s Summary

NZIM is a registered and accredited multi-site private training provider.

Our professional expertise and goal is to prepare current managers for management in the future. The Institute provides members with networking opportunities, management workshops and seminars, training and career development opportunities, professional accreditation and qualifications, management information, a member card and benefits package, a mentoring facility and scholarships and awards.

Tait Grindley’s Specialties:

At NZIM we aim to create an environment that provides incentives and support for today’s managers to develop their leadership and management capability through education and practical learning tools.

This involves placing greater emphasis on management skills in secondary, tertiary and industry training and ensuring that courses are designed and evaluated on the basis of their enhancement of management practice.
