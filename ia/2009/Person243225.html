Vikrama Dhiman’s Summary

Research, new ideas and innovation have been the key in everything I have done and do. I am ambitious, self driven and motivated to consistently learn and improve. I have worked with organizations with only a few rupees in credit to the ones which gave loans to others.

I would like to work with organizations in business analysis or product management where I get a chance to learn something new every day. I am also a bit greedy and would want the most challenging work to come my way.

I currently work as Product Manager for Wiziq.com and hope to grow the product to great heights.

I am a passionate Agile believer and conduct Agile trainings and coaching for teams across India [including being a course assistant for Certified Scrum Master course] and share experiences in implementing Agile. I am an active presenter at various Agile conferences and also moderate the Scrum-India Yahoo Group. I also blog at http://agilediary.wordpress.com/
