Elisabeth Redei’s Summary

Independent Senior SQL Server Consultant, Architect and Microsoft Certified Trainer with an emphasis on administration and infrastructure.I have spent the last 13 years delivering various technical services and training to Microsoft Premier and enterprise customers in the EMEA region.

For 10 years I have focused on Microsoft SQL Server and surrounding technologies in various senior positions within Microsoft such as Senior Premier Field Engineer, Teamleader and Senior Consultant.
