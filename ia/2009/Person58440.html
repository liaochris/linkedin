David Venter’s Summary

Vervean Media is a young, funky and talented team, which strives to produce professional design. We have a range of products and services targeted at small to medium businesses. Ignite the visual senses of your clients by having a sleek and sexy corporate identity designed by our dedicated team of professionals.

We provide marketing and media related services which help clients map out an appropriate strategy for their business, assist in optimizing their Corporate Identity so that it is performing at peak, and help them to make optimum use of their advertising and marketing resources.

Most importantly we focus on implemented results. We believe that a strategy which sits gathering dust on a CEO’s bookshelf is of no value. Change must be reflected in the bottom line.

We create, implement and measure advertising and marketing strategies, and since we perform multiple marketing services, there’s no need to look elsewhere to have different initiatives executed.

Our marketing teams take a scientific approach to marketing strategy development, planning, and implementation; employing research-driven marketing tools and models to dramatically improve business performance for our clients.
