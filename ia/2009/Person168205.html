Richard Morochove’s Summary

I explain information technology in plain English. I may be testifying in court as an expert witness, consult with a business to help improve efficiency, write as a journalist, or speak at a conference or live on television. I decipher IT jargon and make it understandable to mere mortals.

Richard Morochove’s Specialties:

Business information technology, computer forensics.

Richard Morochove’s Experience

  • President

    Morochove & Associates Inc.

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 1988 — Present (21 years 7 months)

    Assists clients with information technology, including strategy, evaluation, procurement, installation, use and trouble-shooting. Special expertise in computer forensics, accounting, ERP, financial systems, e-commerce.

    Freelance technology journalist: Net Work columnist and multimedia producer for PC World; Computer Monitor columnist and editor of Financial Software Vendor Directory for The Bottom Line. Formerly: Computer Watch columnist, Toronto Star (1991-2005); technology Buyer's Guides for AllBusiness.com; tech columnist, Financial Post; Contributing Editor/Reviews, Infoworld.

    Public speaker on business technology, e.g. Managing and Equipping Your Mobile Work Force; Portals, SharePoint and Alternatives: Exchanging Information with your Clients; Gadgets and Cool Tools – The Latest.

    Television commentator and technology analyst for BNN (Business News Network), CBC Newsworld.

    Lectured at the University of Toronto, York University and George Brown College.

  • Director of Computer Services

    Wm. Eisenberg & Co.

    (Accounting industry)

    September 1984 — May 1988 (3 years 9 months)

    In charge of computer consulting division for public accounting firm. Managed staff who assisted business clients to develop I.T. strategies, implement solutions, and improve the efficiency of business operations by more effective use of information technology.

  • Manager

    Arthur Andersen & Co.

    (Accounting industry)

    September 1975 — September 1984 (9 years 1 month)

    Qualified as a Chartered Accountant. Managed audit, accounting and IT consulting services for small and mid-sized enterprises. Developed and delivered courses and seminars related to business finance and personal computer applications.
