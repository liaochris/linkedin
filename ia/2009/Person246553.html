Wendy Cobrda’s Summary

"Renaissance Marketeer" with a passion for new methods and techniques. My skill set is broad, yet focused on target marketing techniques including survey generation & analysis, advanced data analysis as well as data delivery systems through the internet. My friends and colleagues joke that I'm a "data diva".

Before forming Catenate, I had a challenging career with leading broadcasting, research and target marketing companies. Those diverse experiences helped me achieve a unique perspective on all things data related. I formed Catenate (which means to "link") to connect experts in the marketing field together in July of 1997.

In March of 2007, I co-founded a new company, Earthsense to apply leading data collection and analysis techniques to the green marketing challenge facing all businesses today. How do you maintain profitability while changing your product formulations and distribution to meet corporate sustainability goals? Earthsense collects holistic data about consumers -- so that we understand the environmental, behavioral, social and attitudinal issues that impact household decisions.

Our new company is an Applied Marketing company and it features analysis tools such as modular geo-segmentation, advanced visual display (GIS), indices on the fly and custom research allowing marketers to probe for the reasons why people buy "green" and the obstacles or barriers in certain product categories. Add to that political profiling, demographics, religion, exographic data and life events, and you can imagine the powerful insights we get from 60,000 responses a year.

Earthsense take a common sense approach to sustainability. We give our clients confidence in the value of going green.
