  • Director, Distributed Development Framework Program

    Yahoo!

    (Public Company; YHOO; Internet industry)

    May 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Distributed product engineering is different from pure offshore model in core vs context. It is essentially a cost/value constrained optimization problem with several variables such as structure, culture, work partitioning, skill, collaboration maturity etc. Cost includes the opportunity cost of not doing at mother location, cost of distributed way of work in addition to the resource cost. The distributed development framework program is a risk management program to ensure that we get enough value. This is also a trust accelerator program and in business, trust is key. My job involves working with all the Yahoo! business unit R&D organizations that are distributed, come up with a framework, establish a common report card and help them to improve in a measurable way.
