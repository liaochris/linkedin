Jason Steele’s Summary

My background includes extensive experience in the Internet service/agency world as well as recent corporate endeavors, focusing primarily on building and marketing Fortune 500 companies on the Web.

I began my career with three of the true pioneers of the Internet services industry; Poppe Tyson Interactive, Modem Media and Grey Interactive. My assignments ranged from building the first web presence for ING Barings and T.Rowe Price to redesigning the entire Delta Air Lines Web site.

I made my first venture into the corporate world by joining Sony Electronics, where I led the online acquisition-marketing arm of their direct business. While there I implemented Sony's first web analytics platform, affiliate marketing program and $10 Million online marketing campaign. During my tenure at Sony I managed to bring in nearly 200,000 customers, resulting in nearly $50 Million in revenue.

Recently I dabbled in an Internet Start-Up, where I focused on creating an advertising revenue model to attract Venture Capital funding.

My current position at Liberty Travel gave me the opportunity to build their online marketing division from the ground up. My staff concentrates on e-mail marketing, paid search, web analytics, web site maintenance/merchandising, 3rd party media buying and partnership development.

I attended Pennsylvania State University and hold a BA degree in Advertising-Public Relations from the College of Communications in State College, Pennsylvania.
