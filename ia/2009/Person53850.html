Danielle Zeitlen Hughes’s Summary

With over 14 years of experience in the television entertainment field, I have written copy for some of the most renowned companies in the business - Discovery Channel, TLC, Animal Planet, Nickelodeon, National Geographic, Oxygen, ABC and more. Whether it is a brochure, email, web site, media kit, programming description, direct mail, trade advertisement, advertorial or client correspondence, I have done it all. I also co-founded Baby Fish Mouth, an infant and toddler t-shirt company that takes famous movie lines and puts a "baby" twist on them. All shirts come packaged in a popcorn box, making the perfect gift for any little film fan.
