Sashi Chimala’s Summary

I have expertise and demonstrated track record in IT services, Web 2.0, Internet Advertising, Social Media and UGC. I am experienced in Strategy, Branding, Online and Offline customer acquisition and Product Marketing. My background includes Technology, Internet Advertising, Social Media, Casual Gaming and Consumer focused industries.

I have over 18 years of experience as a senior executive at small to medium sized startup environments (10 to 150 employees). Have experience in creating startups, building core teams and managing distributed teams across US and Asia.

Sashi Chimala’s Specialties:

- Experience in IT services, online media and digital advertising
- Hands-on understanding of Social Media, UGC, Online customer acquisition (played active roles in a broad range of social media initiatives like CricTV.com, VoteKast.com, Knibble.com and several others)
- Strategy, ability to forecast trends and markets, building compelling brands and marketing
- Successfully negotiated acquisition of stakes in privately held companies by PE funds and large enterprises

  • Founder & CEO (acquired by FX Labs in 2008)

    Knibble.com

    (Computer Games industry)

    July 2007 — February 2008 (8 months)

    Knibble.com was a wholly-owned division within PayPod.
    Knibble.com was acquired for $3M in December 2007 by FX Labs Limited, one of the largest game development studios in Asia.
    Knibble.com was the largest aggregator of online casual games with over 10,000 games.
    Knibble was one of the earliest pioneers in bringing casual games to iPhone and Nintendo Wii. Check out http://iphone.knibble.com (ideally from your iPhone) and http://wii.knibble.com (and from your Wii).

  • Founder, CEO, CMO

    Qwiky's Coffee

    (Retail industry)

    October 1999 — October 2005 (6 years 1 month)

    As the Founder and Chief of Marketing at Qwiky's Coffee, Sashi is hailed by press and media in India as the 'one who started the coffee revolution in India".

    Sashi is well recognized for his extraordinary ability for consumer brand creation, innovative marketing and promotion. With very low start-up budget, Qwiky's has been featured on Fox TV, CNN, CNBC, BBC and many TV stations and news papers worldwide.

    Sashi has been featured on the covers and features of several magazines.

  • Founder & CEO (acquired by SSI & NASDAQ JV)

    Indigo Technologies

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    July 1991 — September 1999 (8 years 3 months)

    Indigo was sold in 1999 to a 2-way joint venture of SSI Ltd., a publicly traded company in India and NASDAQ.
    Indigo Technologies based in Cupertino CA, specialized in Banking and Securities technologies and IT services. Indigo developed TESA, a securities automation software that powered several stock exchanges in Asia. It was hailed by NASD as extremely scalable and fault tolerant.
    Based on the thought leadership and accomplishments of Indigo, Tandem Computers (acquired by Compaq) acquired a minority stake in Indigo in 1998.

  • Founding Member (acquired by CSC)

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; CVNS; Information Technology and Services industry)

Conceived and developed SIMPL one of the earliest software configuration management software that was successfully implemented at World Bank, SWIFT as well as GSA.
