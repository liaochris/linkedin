Anne-Roos de Joode’s Summary

Bjork thinks, breathes and delivers quality. Both in (executive) search and in deploying assessments for selection and development: we choose the optimum strategy for optimal results for every project.

So with us, it is never just 'someone' we happen to know. After all, you do not want so-so or average, you want the best candidates in the market. Targeted, screened and tested for the right knowledge, experience and behaviour. Due to the diversified composition of our team of consultants we have experience in the following areas:

* Executives & Senior Management (CEO, CFO, CIO, CCO, MD, managing director, business unit management, commercial director, senior line management)
* Operational Management (plant management, supply chain, logistics, procurement, IT)
* Commercial positions (account, sales, contract, bid, channel and business development management)
* Marketing & Communication (marketing intelligence, marketing communication, Business development, Com/PR)
* IT & Telecommunication (consulting, project management, management, development, architects, etc.)
* Consulting (management consulting, coaching, training)
* Finance & Procurement (financial management, auditing, controllers, purchasing, F&A)
* Human Resources (recruitment, personnel, HR management)

Our team has its primary focus on the entire Dutch market, and internationally we are the exclusive Dutch representative of Glasford with an extensive network of offices in Europe, Africa, North and South America and Asia Pacific.
