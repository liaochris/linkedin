Sandra Maki’s Summary

Yesterday

Co-founder of the InSights Group, an amazing collaborative learning organization, which has grown without traditional funding or a marketing or advertising budget. Creating the road map as we went, Al Curtis, my co-founder and I, have experienced an amazing journey of discovery since opening the doors in January of 2008. The social media strategies we teach at the InSights Group were developed as part of our journey.

I have studied Psychology and Business Administration, though I draw the most from my life experiences. I have also worked with holistic approaches such as Core Consciousness Transformation, and am a certified Reiki master.

A lifelong student, I continue to seek new opportunities for growth and change, always exploring and always chasing my bliss.

Today

I enjoy spending my days dreaming up fresh new ideas and opportunities for entrepreneurs.

I enjoy using my marketing imagination to plan new campaigns and social media strategies that help people focus on their goals, and get off their ordinary paths and go in amazing new directions.

I've helped people to chase their bliss and find their happiness as they pursue their entrepreneurial and business goals.

I'm working on a little project called Organic Marketing based on Marketing Tic-Tac-Toe, that is changing the way we view and understand marketing for small business to large corporations.

Tomorrow

My dreams include owning an InSights resort and training center on a Tropical Island by 2012. (I do have the place chosen, working on the details!)

Continuing to create fun and amazing opportunities, and being challenged, inspired and rewarded every day.

Travelling and bringing the InSights message to individuals around the world.

The InSights Group is a family of inspired entrepreneurs learning, sharing, growing and supporting one another.
