Kath Duncan’s Summary

Growing up in Toronto, Canada - I did my Bachelor of Commerce at Queen’s University with a minor in finance and accounting. Always having a passion for fashion and shopping, naturally I followed a more lucrative path to be able to afford these things – I did my Chartered Accountant (CA) designation with one of the big four firms. I spent eight months in San Francisco where while living steps away from Union Square, I got the idea for whataretheywearing.com. After seeing several Jennifer Anniston movies, I spent a few too many hours trying to track down what she was wearing!! The next three years, I worked in sales with an international recruiting company back in Toronto – learning business development and operational skills – and becoming one of the top ranked performers in North America in my first two years. It was fun but quickly became a bit redundant, I was craving change and adventure - so after a life-changing trip to Mount Everest base camp in December of 2008, I decided it was time to get incorporated and move to LA!!

At 29, I’ve visited 28 countries and six continents (I have a goal to hit 30 and all seven before I hit 30 next August) - I love adventurous travel and I’ve climbed Mount Kilimanjaro and hiked through the Andes in South America. I thrive in chaos and I’m always searching for a new challenge. I’m thrilled to be putting my passion for consumption into something constructive – offering women of all resources a little taste of celebrity – after all, when you look great you feel better!!
