Mala Desai’s Interests:

My desire is to work for a down-to-earth, environmentally-conscious design studio, advertising agency or publishing company (newspaper, magazine, book, academia, etc) until I'm ready to start my own design firm. I consider myself a maximalist and kindred spirit with lots of passion. I'm someone who believes everyone has a good heart. I also believe that our beautiful planet earth is facing a climate crisis, and have therefore become an active global warming awareness proponent. I've been told my smiles are contagious. I love blue, quick wit and sarcasm, chocolate icecream, playing piano, dancing, movies, shopping, admittedly reality tv, and soft cotton. I'm especially fond of the Victorian era, Rococo styling and the Baroque aesthetic. I design by the mantra "more is more" as equally as I do "less is more." Gustav Klimt is my favorite artist and Sarah McLachlan is my favorite musician. I think it's funny when people overuse drop shadows or have their store signage in Papyrus...or both.
