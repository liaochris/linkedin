Benjamin Ray’s Summary

PROFILE
For the past 23 years, Benjamin Ray has worked exclusively advising corporate decision-makers with numerous global companies. He offers council on high-level strategic and tactical programs, designed to bring vision and action. His ability to combine strategic development, with effective marketing planning is essential to his clients’ success. He is a visionary and a thought leader; a strong business driver who understands the value of cross-functional relationships. He is customer centric, has been called a competitive strategy guru, a brand advocate and champion, an expert digital marketer and a hyper-innovator. He has the ability to bring bigger, bolder ideas to market faster and with less expense.

SKILLS
His major strength is concepting innovative digital marketing strategies, that pinpoint clients’ / initiatives’ strengths and major selling points; then leveraging digital mediums and cutting-edge technologies to deliver strong results. The proven process involves setting a plan in place with c-level sign-off, then leveraging top-level teams to accomplish milestone-driven ROI. His ability to see the big picture, along with tactical details, is crucial to the success of his client relationships.
