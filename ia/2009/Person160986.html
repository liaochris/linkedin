Michael Chinnici’s Summary

I enjoy exciting challenges... turning dreams into realities... or bringing the next great idea to the world.

In 1982 I founded "Chinnici Direct" , a Direct Marketing Ad Agency to service the growing demands of the industry. My objective was to establish one of the most creative and strategic agency in the industry.

Then in 1996 I founded both "Icon Digital Photography" and "Brave New Media" to service the new world of Digital Media.

ICON Digital was relaunched in 2008 as a Still + Motion Digital Content Production Company featuring Photographers and Directors.

In 1999 I co-founded "M2 Design Group" a Graphic Design Studio for the Music and Fashion Industries.

In 2000 Chinnici Direct was sold, and I stayed on as CEO until 2005 when I stepped down.

In 2005 my desire to "change the face of the ad agency" led to "Scuderia O2" ...Italian for "Team Oxygen".

Scuderia O2 is a new kind of animal. A Hybrid of sorts. A cross between the aerodynamic and fuel efficient creative boutiques and the gas-guzzling Goliath's of the “so-called” integrated holding companies. We like to call it a MINI Cooper on steroids.

It’s the brainpower of a mega-agency, the intensity of a boutique, the can-do chemistry of respectful pros having a great time working together.

And by providing a “Best-in-Class” worldwide network of seasoned Creative and Strategic talent supported by an exceptional and powerful Account Services team, it’s a WIN-WIN-WIN for everyone. The client, the agency and our partners.

In early 2007 Scuderia O2 added a Motorsports Marketing Division to service Sponsorship and Event Marketing.

So... Breathe New Life into your Marketing.
