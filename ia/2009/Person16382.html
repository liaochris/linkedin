Anita Hermesch’s Summary

After nearly 30 years of working for others, I launched my own training and consulting company that specializes in educating executives, supervisors, and mid-level management on how to make wise choices and decisions. I focus on choices because, as we all know, one's success doesn't happen accidentally, but rather by one's choices. In essence, ROI is really ROC, Return on Choices and... Decisions! Feel free to contact me if you want more info.
