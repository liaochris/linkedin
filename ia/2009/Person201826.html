Robert Tsai’s Summary

I enjoy solving problems, working in concert with clients and partners, and communicating and selling solutions.

Currently, I work as a Solutions Consultant for Conductor, a New York based Search Engine Optimization (SEO) startup that provides a platform to marketers to manage their natural search. Most recently, I was a Sales Consultant at Rapt, a software start-up that focuses on helping online media publishers improve yield through better pricing, inventory forecasting and analytics. Rapt was successfully sold to Microsoft in 2008.
