Glenn Curry’s Summary

I've worked in animation/FX and taught college for a combined 10 years. Past projects include "SPACE CHIMPS", "WATCHMEN", "THE X-FILES: I Want to Believe", "SMALLVILLE", "BARNYARD: The Original Party Animals" and "THOMAS & FRIENDS". Presently I am finishing FX on "THE FINAL DESTINATION" due for theatrical release August 2009.

Glenn Curry’s Specialties:

FX TD/generalist, 2D/3D character animation, storyboards, post-secondary trainer (combined 10yrs exp.)
