Artie Isaac’s Summary

A career marketer and entrepreneur, I am introducing (with co-founder Rob Emrich) an experiment called SpeakerSite — an online social network of public speakers and event planners.

I also work with Young Isaac, providing advertising, identity (naming and logo development), marketing strategy and ideation (1) for values-driven clients and (2) for clients who need their customers to make momentous decisions.

Goals: To help. To teach. To learn.

Artie Isaac’s Specialties:

Public speaking and teaching on topics of creativity, advertising, idea generation, consumer behavior, crisis management, values-based (or values-driven) marketing, corporate culture, generational marketing, and the ethical use of speech.
Experienced board member for entrepreneurial companies and not-for-profit organizations.
