  • Principal

    Project o3

    (Privately Held; Myself Only; Graphic Design industry)

    May 2007 — Present (2 years)

    The name Project o3 started with the word “project”, which means: to convey an impression to an audience or others. It is how we take our clients input and vision and project it into a cohesive design that communicates to a given audience. The numeral three represents the three words that give voice to the essence of Project o3. Three words that the staff at Po3 consider the most important tenet of their business. Effective. Fluid. Relevant.

    The staff at Po3 are award-winning designers who are committed to creating inventive design solutions that communicate effectively. In order to accomplish this goal we at Po3 strive to build fluid relationships with our clients, which allows for truly great designs. Designs that are not only imaginative, but also relevant to the right audience with the right voice. This is the core of Po3.
