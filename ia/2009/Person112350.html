Jeremy Waen’s Summary

I am a research scientist by trade and a problem solver. I wish to make a lasting difference through my work for humanity and the world. Now more than ever our society is realizing the need for cleaner greener power, transportation, materials, and industries. I find this era inspiring and invigorating despite all the doom and gloom in the media. My immediate major goal is to complete a doctorate in materials for alternative energy through a materials science and engineering department at a prestigious university.

I am pursuing electrochemical research at my current employer. I assist in a variety of projects involving electrodesposition, electrowinning, & electrorefining systems. Other research that I assist with includes small scale and large scale battery technology and electrochemical synthesis of polymer feedstocks and biofuels.

I am always interested in making new connections relating to my research and career.
