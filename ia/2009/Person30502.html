Bob Rodkin’s Summary

A former Toronto Police constable I have been active in the Canadian magazine industry since early 1985 holding positions of Advertising Sales, Business Development, Marketing Manager and Publisher. I have launched and managed a B2B Advertising Agency (1988) two successful business publications in 1996 and 2004 and my current focus is managing the sales and marketing strategies for Blue Line Magazine, Canada's only independent publication serving law enforcement professionals.
