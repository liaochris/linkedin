Chetan Sangani’s Summary

Merus technology provides Engineering solutions towards Energy conservation, CO2 reduction while treating Oil&Gas process and treating water systems.

Merus from Germany has developed unique technology that is Non-Conventional way of treating Liquids of all kinds. At Water side Merus Solves the problems of Rust, Corrosion, Scaling & Biofouling applicable in almost all industries where water is a must. Power plants, refineries, Desalination Plants, Chemical Companies, Petrochemical companies, Industries, Commercial Complex, Hotels, Hospitals, residential and where ever you have water.

In Hydrocarbons Merus Minimizes the formation Wax in flowlines. Reduces emulsion. Enhances the separation process of Crude and Water, Enhances the process at ETP, Minimizes Corrosion, SRB, Scaling and Fouling.

Merus has been applied at various industries worldwide and as well worlds biggest Oil producers.

Merus Controls Water and Hydrocarbons at its molecular level by Software. The company gives Free Demo to potential clients and industries.

Chetan Sangani’s Specialties:

Wax Reduction, Enhancing of Crude/Water seperation process, Stop / Minimize Corrosion in Oil & Water, Solutions for fouling in Oil / Water systems, Scaling reduction in Oil/Water system.
