Linda Duffy’s Summary

Leadership Habitude helps companies solve their people problems. Typically, executives call when they are:
•struggling to find good people to hire;
•losing sleep over how to fire or layoff some of the people they’ve got;
•frustrated with employees who are technically really good, but don't play well with others;
•worried about losing top quality employees who are unclear about their career path;
•nervous about managers who don’t know how to manage;
•pressured because they don't have the time or expertise to develop their leaders and employees; or
•tired of being distracted by people problems when they'd rather be focused on their business.

Leadership Habitude also works with individuals in job transition who are:
*scared about finding a job in the current economy;
*confused about how to conduct an effective job search;
*frustrated that they aren't hearing back from recruiters after sending in a resume; and
*terrified that they won't get a job before exhausting their savings and/or unemployment benefits.

Leadership Habitude brings over 20 years of generalist experience in progressive leadership positions in a variety of industries (manufacturing, electronics, high-tech, entertainment, education, non-profit). We are a strategic business partner with experience in start-up, rapidly growing, mature, downsizing, merging and multi-site organizations.

Linda Duffy’s Specialties:

Leadership Training and Coaching, Recruiting and Retention, Employee Relations, Organization Development and Training, Risk Management, Executive Coaching, Conflict Resolution, Job Search Coaching, Outplacement
