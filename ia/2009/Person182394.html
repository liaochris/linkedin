Patrick Kelley’s Summary

I'm an experienced media and marketing executive with a proven track for creating and executing strategies in the new and traditional media spaces. From 1994 to 2009 I championed a team through a series of ownership changes and an evolving competitive landscape generating $200,000,000 of top line revenue and 52% EBIDA. These changes brought many new responsibilities and expectations in the ever-changing world of media.

We recently started The 14 Group...this company is a sales and consulting arm to new media companies who need help marketing and selling their brands to the advertising community. Presently we are working with companies in the sports, audience research and travel categories.

Patrick Kelley’s Specialties:

Partnering with small to mid sized companies to increase sales and create a strong sales/marketing brand within their industry.
