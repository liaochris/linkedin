Matthew Lowden’s Summary

Formerly a Human Resources Specialist in corporate law firms, I had a change of heart, and something just clicked when I immersed myself in the digital media and advertising world after meeting some great people in the industry. I'm currently a New Media Coordinator with USF MBA alumn, Jamie Pardi, and his start-up, SeaBridge, Inc. I also just completed projects as a Marketing Coordinator at Catalyst:SF with Professor John Durham. I've lead and helped develop marketing strategies for SeaBridge, Inc. as well as assisting in the development of new marketing and advertising strategies for Catalyst:SF. I'm working hard to gain experience in marketing, selling and advertising, post-graduation, via reading industry newsletters and reports, as well as attending industry events and training such as Upstream's Habitat and ad:tech.
