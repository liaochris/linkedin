Linda Panaro’s Summary

CAREER COACH  CERTIFIED EMPLOYMENT INTERVIEW CONSULTANT  CAREER MANAGEMENT PLANNING and DEVELOPMENT

The business and economic landscape has changed dramatically - causing those passing through career transition to pause and rethink their approach to landing the career they deserve and want.

Unique Perspective: Having been infused within the walls of the corporate machine for over 15 years, working with Executive Management Teams to hone into organizational performance to continually improve customer value, employee success, processes and profitable growth, I understand the mechanics, know the dynamics and have the insight needed to help those passing through career transition break through to the inside. I have blended my business, career management and coaching experience into career transition and outplacement services bringing a practical approach to the industry and a high-value proposition to my clients.

I have recruited, hired, interviewed and career coached/developed hundreds of people throughout my career.

My professional passion is developing crystallized visions, creating strategic plans, executing and implementing the plans flawlessly, seizing opportunities and generating solutions utilizing a systematic approach to achieve GOALS.

I brought these strengths to those in career transition and designed The Career Activator System.

The System: The Career Activator System blends two core principles organizations regard: The Employee Life Cycle and Strategic Planning.

The Career Activator System is a straightforward career management strategic plan broken down into four steps, packed with tools and techniques to guide clients through every step of the career transition cycle from exploring themselves to the finish line of the on-boarding process.
