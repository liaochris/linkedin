  • Deputy Campaign Manager

    (Political Organization industry)

    May 2006 — August 2006 (4 months)

    Designed and implemented eCampaign initiatives, including online fundraising of over $20,000, and the design and maintenance of an email supporters database with over 125,000 unique records. Website provided visitors video, audio clips, podcasts, and more.
