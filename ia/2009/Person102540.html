James Haft’s Summary

Entrepreneurial Merchant Banker with broad experience in all aspects of fund raising, business strategy and M&A. Over 40 domestic and cross border transactions with an aggregate transaction value of over $15 billion between US, Asian and Latin American markets, including the first listing of a Chinese company on the NYSE. Direct experience with Global integrated media, telecom, emerging markets, financial consulting, real estate and venture capital.

James Haft’s Specialties:

Strategic and Funding advisory and principal activities; Domestic US and Cross Border Merchant Banking in Asia and Latin America (China, Korea, Japan, HK, Singapore, Philippines. Argentina, Brazil, Mexico, Colombia); significant experience with Internet-based businesses, Renewable Energy, Real Estate, Telecom, VoIP, Wireless, Healthcare Services.
