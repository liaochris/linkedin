Jeff Pajor’s Summary

An accomplished and published technologist, Jeffrey Pajor has experience in public and private sector technology development. He has worked with clients in various industries including broadcast media, financial services, retail, e-business and telecom. Jeffrey has over 12 yrs of Java and C++ experience, is a Sun Certified Enterprise Architect, and experienced manager who has a proven track record of solutions delivery.
