Mike O'Brien’s Summary

I am a ‘results driven’ manager and a visionary entrepreneur. The majority of my experience is in sales, office management and business ownership. My commitment and drive, combined with effectively managing people in varied environments sets me apart.

While attending James Madison University I assisted in creating an Internet start-up company called eFirms.com. Shortly after graduating, I accepted an entry-level position with ADP (Automatic Data Processing).   

At ADP I quickly learned strategic selling skills and the importance of building strong client rapport. My main responsibility was to sell payroll services to current and prospective clients in Queens, New York. In 2002 I left ADP to join Foxtons North America, an online discount real estate company as an Outside Sales Professional.   

During my first year at Foxtons North America I sold over 150 homes, was named Top Producer and was promoted to Sales Manager. I was offered a position as a Sales Trainer, although I turned it down in order to start my own company - Green Apple Real Estate.

Green Apple Real Estate was located in Wantagh, NY and proved to be my most successful business venture to date. By the end of my second year, I was managing a full-time staff and had personally generated over $400,000 in revenue.

Currently I am the Associate Broker and Office Manager for The Condo Shop located in the Society Hill section of Philadelphia. Our clients are comprised mostly of owners and buyers of luxury condominiums in Center City, Philadelphia. My primary focus is on property management and sales management.
