Dan Flanegan’s Summary

Dan is the VP of Strategy & Client Services for BrandInHand, a full-service, end-to-end mobile media and marketing company that focuses on helping brand marketers win using mobile.

Dan is also the founder of Brand Anywhere a consultancy that provides advisory services for agencies and mobile startups.

In 2003 Flanegan co-founded Soapbox Mobile, a leading mobile marketing platform provider. While serving as CEO, Dan was responsible for the company's vision, market leadership and mission of helping companies improve communication via the mobile channel.

While at Soapbox Dan was instrumental in creating a next generation mobile marketing and CRM platform that has powered the mobile campaigns of numerous brands including Levis, MobiTV, Guinness, The Vans Warped Tour, Procter & Gamble, W Hotels, General Motors and many others. Soapbox was acquired by Single Touch Interactive in 2007.

Dan is a recognized mobile marketing & advertising expert and has been quoted by various publications and media outlets, including Red Herring, ClickZ, MediaPost, OMMA Magazine, Mobile Marketer, The San Diego Business Journal, ABC, NBC, Fox News, Box Office Magazine, The San Diego Union Tribune, UPI, Wired, Marketing Sherpa and Wireless Week.

He has also served as a featured speaker at leading industry events including Billboard’s MECCA at CTIA, DEMexpo, The CTIA Mobile Entertainment Summit, The Games & Mobile Forum, The DMA Webinar Series, The Mobile Marketing Association Forum, Dow Jones VentureWire, Mobile Broadband Americas, OMMA Hollywood, OMMA Mobile, OMMA NY, DPAC, Circulation Day LA, Digital Hollywood, Email Evolution, DigidayMobile, The National Sports Forum, MarketingSherpa, The MIT Enterprise Forum and The UC Irvine Graduate Computer Science Program.

Dan is currently an Entrepreneur in Residence at the UCSD Connect Springboard program, a Co-Chair of the Marketing Special Interest Group (SIG) of CommNexus and an advisor to a number of early stage start-ups.
