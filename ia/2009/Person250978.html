Zack Brandit’s Summary

I’m Zack Brandit (Zack for intimates) - hero of modern days.

My calling and main purpose is to provide new means to harness the properties of social media such as blogs, micro-blogs, social networks and others, by offering a unique set of features and services.

At ZackBrandit, we found a way to link marketing and branding campaigns directly to sales and distribution. Forget about wasted banner clicks, forget about brand control and forget about distribution complexities.

Whether you're a small or large company, we can support your online activities. ZackBrandit can help you get closer to your customers and interact with them, but also use different networks to brand-empower your existing advocates and reach potential customers wherever they are, provided they have access to the Internet.
