Marty Grogan, MSEE, PMP, CISSP’s Summary

In my early years, I learned hard work mowing lawns and delivering papers. In school I learned to never stop learning.

As a technology professional, I enjoy diving into new situations, rapidly adding skills needed to succeed and working with others along the way.

My Formula for Progress and Success:

Listen -> Learn -> Support -> Deliver

• Listen purposefully to develop insight and effective communications.
• Carefully learn stakeholder vision, needs and expectations to establish a roadmap.
• Support actions with negotiated strategies and adequate resources to minimize risk.
• Deliver maximum benefit by implementing plans to satisfy objectives.

My experience extends from circuit design thorough systems analysis with many stops along the way. As an individual contributor or experienced manager, I solve complex problems and deliver on promises.
