Julie McGowan’s Summary

I'm an accomplished leadership professional with 20+ years of senior-level management experience propelling revenues and profits for several high-profile media brands.

With a unique ability to bring depth and intelligence to new and plateauing brands, I'm a big-picture, conceptual thinker who has delivered strong and sustainable results throughout my career.

Most recently, I was promoted to lead American Express Publishing’s flagship business of just under $100 million as the Senior Vice President and Publisher of Travel + Leisure magazine. I successfully led Travel + Leisure to achieve its two largest revenue years and its largest advertising revenue issue, both historic highs for the 36-year-old brand.

After taking a hiatus from the corporate world to care for an aging parent, I'm now pursing and eager to land a position that will leverage my senior-management strengths in a forward-thinking, consumer-focused organization.

Julie McGowan’s Specialties:

Conceiving and spearheading strategic roadmaps, launching new products, facilitating operation turnarounds, and my passion: identifying and firmly establishing robust and compelling brand identities for my employers’ products.

Other strengths include marketing, strategic positioning, product development, event management, sponsorship, and joint venture initiatives.

Deep industry knowledge of and high-level contacts in travel, wine, fashion, beauty, and other luxury products and services.
