Weelit Lim’s Summary

Weelit’s interests in data analysis and visualization led him in the direction of search engine marketing (SEM). He relocated to Singapore in 2009 to help Asian companies leverage the untapped potential of online marketing to get worldwide visibility. He founded his own business on providing SEM services along with other digital marketing strategies to creatively project a company’s products and services to their targeted audience in their target markets.

Weelit Lim’s Specialties:

- Implementation of search engine marketing, web analytics, search engine optimization, other online marketing techniques and ecommerce.

- Forte in data analysis & statistical modeling, datamining, keyword translation and ad localization (English, Mandarin, Taiwanese, Cantonese, Korean, Behasa, French), market knowledge of Asia Pacific countries and USA.

- Experience in Google Adwords Professional, Yahoo! Overture and Panama, MSN adCenter, Naver, Baidu and other PPC service providers.
