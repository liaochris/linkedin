Ian D. Nock’s Summary

Consultant working in Digital TV, Consumer Electronics and Interactive products.

Experienced in the product development, project management, system integration, test, delivery and operation of Digital TV products and solutions. Have worked for the largest media and consumer facing service operations in Europe including UPC Broadband, Telewest, NTL and Virgin Media. I have been instrumental in the delivery of multiple platforms, services and products.

I provide people-leadership, commercial delivery focus and innovation in the delivery of consumer products and services, with great experience of large and small scale projects with geographically dispersed delivery teams.

My goal is to provide the customer with innovative and usable products that advance the in-home entertainment experience.
