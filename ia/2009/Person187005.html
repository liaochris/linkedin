I received “more than satisfied” feedback ratings from all the participants. The courses were: “Build a company website”, “Dreamweaver for beginners”, “Dreamweaver intermediary”, “Dreamweaver advanced”, “Flash animation 1”, “Flash animation 2” and “Paintshop Pro for web use”. At the end of my contract, I was asked to come back to do a second series.
