Wilco Jansen’s Summary

Born in 1967 in the Netherlands, currently living in the Netherlands. After years of being a programmer Wilco has worked as project manager and IT manager in various positions.

Beside his professional carreer Wilco is core team member of the Joomla! project. He discovered Joomla! when he was creating his own content management system, and never lost focus after then. Joined core team as development coordinator in September 2006 just helping to make Joomla! even better then it is already. Wilco is responsible for the overall organisation of Joomla! Development.

Wilco has a bachelor degree in business and information engineering and studied knowledge and information engineering at the Middlesex University in London.
