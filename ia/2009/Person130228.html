Ken Ross’s Summary

Currently Founder and CEO of ExpertCEO, a private on-line network for senior executives. Five years of experience as a general partner at RRE Ventures. 30years of experience as a founder, CEO and board member of numerous software and services companies in the information technology industry including, Seriosity, MontaVista, Adaptive Planning, Electric Cloud, Savvion, Extricity, Pillar and Ross Systems.

Ken Ross’s Specialties:

"hands on CEO", Venture Capital financing, Mergers and Acquisitions, Organizational Development and recruiting, Business Strategy development, Board of Directors interface.
