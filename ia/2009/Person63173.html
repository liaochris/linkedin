Diana Murphy’s Summary

Exceptional editor in chief, creative director, content strategist, and branding expert experienced in all phases of strategic planning and creative development of brands, magazines, websites, eblasts, online media programs, interactive media, books, catalogs, direct mail, promotions, advertising, media kits.

Extensive background in publishing, marketing, and retail, with emphasis on content strategy and creative development of editorial, advertorial, and marketing content in all forms of media, brand identity, custom publishing, licensed brand extensions, and licensed content negotiation. Expertise in the women’s and home categories including cooking, entertaining, decorating, home building, gardening, crafts, travel, family, health & beauty, sustainability, environmental issues, and green living.

Out-of-the box idea generator with a finger on the pulse of trends and what consumers want, and knows how to package it into beautifully designed, highest quality, compelling creative that resonates and keeps people coming back for more.

Enthusiastic team leader and experienced manager of staff, budgets, projects, and deadlines. Media trained spokesperson with numerous appearances on national television and radio, and lecturer at consumer and trade shows.

Available for consulting projects developing content strategy, providing editorial and creative direction, brand identity development, licensing negotiations, client relations, idea generation, editing, and writing services for brands, websites, magazines, marketing materials, online media programs, books, catalogs, newsletters, direct response vehicles, and presentations.
