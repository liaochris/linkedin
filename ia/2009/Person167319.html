Mohammed Barakat’s Summary

As an Industrial Engineer in a Financial Analyst position “Doing Right Things” and "Doing Things Right" are the principles around which my job exists. Financial Analysis in my function is a tool to measure the appropriateness and validity of some financial and pricing goals coping with the changes in the global market. Besides, it is a tool to measure the degree of achieving the set goals. On the other hand, analysis results are used to increase our efficiency in utilizing the resources and cutting down on cost.

As a Project Manager I direct, manage, and implement technical projects that boost productivity and efficiency.

As a Trainer I deliver training programs to my colleagues striving to guide them to the best practices in using Microsoft Technologies that will cut their manual work and increase their productivity.

Obsessed by professional development, I have obtained four remarkable certifications in 2008. Starting with the Microsoft Office Master Instructor (MMI), the Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT), the Project Management Professional (PMP), then the Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS) in MS Project 2007-Managing Projects. I continuously pursue new opportunities that would develop my profession especially in Management and Project Management.

Throughout my career I have attended various training programs, workshops, and seminars amongst which are:
Effective Project Management, Six Sigma Introduction, Leadership Skills, Leadership without Authority, Team Building, The Fundamentals of Requirements Elicitation, Effective Facilitation of Meetings, Mind Mapping, Course Design and Development, Business Writing Skills, Quality Awareness, ISO 9000 Documentation Workshop, Foundation Course in Quality Auditing, IRCA-registered Internal Auditor, and IRCA-registered Auditor/Lead Auditor.
