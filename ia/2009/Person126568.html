Karina Jennings, APR’s Summary

I am a public relations and communications professional with more than 20 years of experience in all facets of PR and communications. For nearly 10 years I've applied these skills in health care and had to opportunity to add marketing, brand management and advertising to my experience. While I'm a skilled communicator as a writer and speaker, I most love the strategic side of my work, especially from the marketing perspective. With my communications and marketing skills as a base, I hope to move more into the strategic planning and business development fields in the future. I'm also interested in exploring communications consulting and focusing on helping others solve communications problems, especially those associated with organizational complexity and cultural differences.

Karina Jennings, APR’s Specialties:

Corporate communications, media relations, community relations, crisis communications, brand management, Web development (Internet and Intranet), marketing, advertising, public affairs, strategic planning and vendor management (ad agency, graphic design and PR).
