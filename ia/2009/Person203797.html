Rory Dean’s Summary

As a recent graduate of the Academy of Art University in San Francisco, I am exploring the written and visual medium of Motion Pictures & Television through my own work and the independent projects of others. With a focus on directing and screenwriting, I am currently develping two feature length scripts and a documentary called Il Sogno del Pescatore or The Dream of the Fisherman - about San Francisco legend Giovanni "Papa Gianni" Giotta the founder of Caffe Trieste.

I am currently looking for collaborators for written and Motion Picture projects.

Rory Dean’s Specialties:

Scene study & analysis, script break-down, directing for film and video, working with actors, getting great performances for film and video, rehearsal techniques, casting director, producing, camera operator, digital, 35mm, and experimental still photography
A.A Degree Liberal Arts with coursework in poetry and literature
B.A Degree English with unofficial minor in film theory and creative writing
M.F.A Degree Motion Pictures & Television with emphasis in Directing and screenwriting
