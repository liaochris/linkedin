Lee-Ann Bedford’s Summary

I am a marketing professional with over 18 years marketing experience. My experience spans traditional marketing (marketing & product management) to specialised fields (research, CRM, direct marketing, digital/online marketing. data/database marketing & analytics). I have worked in a number of industries including B2C, B2B and channel. I specialise in marketing innovation, strategy and services (on and offline), writing services, setting up new departments and initiatives, research and marketing information, data and database marketing. I am tech savvy and have been a marketing driver on countless marketing projects particularly ones involving technology often also taking on the role as a go-between marketing and IT.
