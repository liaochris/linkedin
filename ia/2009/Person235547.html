Tomaso Veneroso’s Summary

Tomaso Veneroso was born in Udine Italy in 1974.
Married with two kids.

Has moved to NY ( USA) in 1999.

After the academic formation at the University of Trieste faculty of Statistical Science has continued his college education at the Long Island Univesity " CW Post Campus" and at the present time is completing a Master in " Science of Taxation".

Has devoted several years of his career working in the production line at the beginning and as a marketing manager after at the Fonderie Acciaierie Rojale ( a group of foundries specialized in wear resistant steel casting for the aggregate industry based in Reana del Rojale Udine Italy) in the year 2000 has become CEO of Am Cast Incorporated a NY Corporation that serve the castings and spare parts needs for the mining, recycling operations and quarries in USA and Canada .

Tomaso Veneroso is proudly rapresenting the Italian business comunity serving as the President of the Confederation of Italian Entrapreneurs in the World-USA( Confederazione degli Imprenditori Italiani nel Mondo-USA).
