Jennifer Walters’s Summary

I am a senior level global marketing and communications professional in the consumer mobile entertainment field with 20 years of experience. My expertise includes brand and consumer product strategy, visual marketing, consumer and industry events, new product launches, and communications. My current focus is in Games from Nokia.
I have deep experience in marketing new and emerging consumer technologies. Prior to the launch of N-Gage, I played a pivotal role in the launch of the first-ever online console gaming network, SegaNet. I was the managing Director of the team that developed and launched Sega Mobile in North America. I excel in leading projects from strategic development through final execution in the consumer marketplace.
I have excellent initiative and pro-active project and team management skills. I am an excellent product cheerleader and spokesperson.

Key Skills
• New technology product launch management
• Brand and creative strategy
• Trade show and event project development and execution
• Consumer testing
• Consumer messaging and communications

Jennifer Walters’s Specialties:
