Cyn Reneau Oss’s Summary

Thanks for your interest in my profile!

I am an account executive with DataTel Communications in Meridian, Idaho. I specialize in voice/data telecommunications – an industry in which I have two decades of experience. I am an authorized network agent for Qwest, Time Warner, Integra, etc.
I work with carriers throughout the USA.

I am able to assess your needs and truly find the best deals available. Even if you’re satisfied with your existing dialtone and data/internet configuration and/or you’re under a contract, I can review your invoices to ensure you’re receiving the best possible rates from your carrier(s). Best of all, there is no charge for this audit! Whether you have two lines or two hundred, your services should always be offered to you at the most competitive fees available.

In the future, if my clients have a concern or a change, they have the peace of mind of knowing they can contact a live person (me)…not an automated call center somewhere. Yes, I am a salesperson...No, your rates aren't higher if you utilize my services (they're tariffed).

Thanks in advance for your consideration. I hope to earn your trust and save you money! I look forward to hearing from you!
