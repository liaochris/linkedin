Bryan Katzenmeyer’s Summary

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in chemistry at The University of Akron. My current focus is studying synthetic polymers and biomolecules using electrospray ionization (ESI), and matrix-assited laser desorption/ionization (MALDI) mass spectrometry (MS). My expected completion date is 2012 - 2013. I would be open to an internship or co-op once I am in to the latter stages of my program. After graduate school, I am interested in obtaining a position at a company in northeastern Ohio that specializes in polymers or in biotechnology.

Beginning in January, I will be become the next Chair-Elect for the Akron Section of the American Chemical Society which oversees Summit, Stark, and Portage counties. I am looking to bolster our membership among undergraduate and graduate students at the surrounding universities. In addition, I hope to continue various programs already established within the section as well as to find new opportunities to expose chemistry and our society to the local community. Feel free to contact me if you would like to be involved with ACS.
