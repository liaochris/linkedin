Peter Coleman, MD’s Summary

I am the founder and medical director of The Coleman Institute, an Advanced Center for Addiction Treatment. We have been recognized as one of the most successful and innovative centers for addiction treatment in the country.

Over the past nine years, our programs have helped to free over 1,000 patients from the damaging effects of heroin use and other narcotics such as Methadone, Oxycontin, Percodan, and Vicodin. More importantly, our Accelerated Detox Technique (ADT), combined with Naltrexone therapy, has helped the vast majority of our patients to stay free of their addictions.

We are now offering an outpatient alcohol detoxification program that is easy for the patients and their families and alleviates their withdrawal symptoms very nicely.

Peter Coleman, MD’s Specialties:

Accelerated Detoxification, withdrawal counseling, and rehabilitation.
