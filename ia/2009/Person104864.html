Jason Lucas, JD’s Summary

In 2008 I joined a newer real estate brokerage called M Realty. Currently, we have 20+ brokers in our office along with our transaction coordinator and office manager. Our office is located in the industrial area just north of the Pearl District in Portland, OR.

Although we're a young company, our business has been rapidly growing since early 2007. That is due in large part to the other half of our company, HomeQuest. The people on this side of our firm are the developers of the HomeQuest real estate software that 1000's of agents in 6 major markets around the country are using. The real benefit of working with M Realty is that our clients have a decided technological advantage for their home search and market analysis. All of our clients have free access to our industry leading home search tool, and the developers of the software are literally in the next room.

M Realty is an entirely different type of brokerage in both look and feel so if you, or anyone you know, is thinking of buying or selling a home, I'd love an opportunity to earn the business.
