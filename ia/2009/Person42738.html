Choo Ki Khoo’s Summary

I started my career with a love for the written word, and plunked myself in the world of rigid educational publishing. Was an editor for a government-approved textbook, and then a content specialist (editor and writer) for secondary English courseware before taking on the opportunity to explore the PR industry.

Two and a half years later, I'm now a Corporate Communications enthusiast interested in accumulating cross-industry experience in field of public relations and communications.

I am addicted to righting things, or making things seem right. At the core of it all is my thirst for CRISIS!! I love managing issues and enjoy scrutinizing problems. That's why corporate communications - it feeds my thirst (hey I started out as an editor, why else?)
