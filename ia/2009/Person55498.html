Darrell Delamaide’s Summary

I'm currently working as an editor and writer on a freelance basis. I have more than 30 years' experience in business and economic journalism for media such as Dow Jones, Bloomberg, Institutional Investor and AOL.

After more than 20 years in Europe, in France and Germany, I moved back to the U.S., to Princeton, in 1997 and moved to Washington, D.C. in 1999.

I've published three books, two nonfiction and a novel, and I'm currently marketing an historical thriller.
