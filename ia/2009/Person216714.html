Katie Lee’s Summary

Director at Miramus Ltd, a production company specialising in content creation and new media consultancy.

Voted one of London's 1000 most influential people in 2008 by the Evening Standard.

Co-founder and editorial director of Shiny Media, the UK's biggest blog publishing company (35 blogs, 3.5 million unique users a month)

Founding editor of www.shinyshiny.tv, the first gadgets site for women.

Created and launched titles across the Lifestyle, Fashion, Technology, Games and Sports sectors, managing a team of 11 in-house editors and up to 50 freelance writers and editors.

Columnist for Look magazine and Observer Woman. Writing work for numerous newspapers and magazines, including the the Telegraph, The Guardian, The Independent on Sunday, Observer Woman and Woman & Home. Regular appearances on radio and TV as consumer technology expert.
