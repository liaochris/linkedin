Kent Smith’s Summary

Kent Smith, DDS is a passionate dentist. His goal is to create healthy, beautiful and functional smiles for his patients. By staying on the cutting edge of research and development of new materials and procedures, his peers consider him a recognized leader.

Dr. Smith leads the dental industry in the field of sleep. Several times a year, he offers a three day course on Sleep Disorder Breathing. This course is designed to equip dentists with the tools necessary to improve their patients’ sleep and health. In addition, Dr. Smith lectures and speaks nationally on various dental topics for organizations including the Las Vegas Institute for Advanced Dental Studies, Six Month Smiles and SomnoMed Academy .

Dr. Smith founded 21st Century Dental in 1985. His practice has been featured in Doctors of Dentistry (Premier Issue), Dental Economics, Dental Equipment and Materials, Dental Products Report, USA Today, Early Show with Bryant Gumbel and local television.

In the dental industry, Dr. Smith’s work is widely published. His publications can be found in RDH Magazine, IACA, American Dental Institute, Dental Economics, and Dental Equipment and Materials on topics including Sleep Breathing Disorders and Spa Dentistry.
