Todd Perkins, MBA’s Summary

Dedicated executive with superior problem solving skills. Exceptional range of experience in Safety, Regulatory Compliance, and Quality systems. Twenty years experience in the pharmaceutical and chemical industries including clinical and research academic settings. Motivated leader who cultivates loyalty and respect to achieve optimal team performance. Focused on proactive compliance, continuous improvement, and effecting positive changes.

Very experienced in auditing, compliance, quality, document control, project management, analytical methods development, and process improvement/automation. Comprehensive knowledge of Federal regulations. Firsthand experience in building regulatory compliance programs from scratch. Proficient at API formulation, testing, and composing CMC section of NDA/IND submissions.

I am widely recognized and respected as a technical and regulatory resource who will go the extra mile to make a positive contribution.

    St. Louis City LEPC

    (Government Administration industry)

    The St. Louis City LEPC promotes safety in the community through awareness, planning, partnerships, and education.
