Derek Hauffe’s Summary

Derek has worked in the video game industry as a programmer and artist since 1997. For the last three years he has focused on programming, working primarily in C#, with a little bit of C++/CLI added in. Prior to his work in the games industry, he worked as a graphic designer and interactive multimedia developer. He has worked with some extremely talented people and has contributed to several released products. He enjoys working with a team to create something new and engaging.

Derek Hauffe’s Specialties:

Game engine development (rendering, animation, user interface, and tools), web design and programming, interface design, tool scripting in 3D Studio Max, and 3D modeling and animation.

    March 2007 — February 2009 (2 years)
