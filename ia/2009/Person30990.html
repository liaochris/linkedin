Boon Sheridan’s Summary

Boon Sheridan’s Specialties:

My work starts with understanding target audiences’ needs and other motivating factors then translating the findings into concepts for compelling interactions and experiences. I develop scenarios, navigation models and prototypes for demonstration of concepts to clients. I follow that up by creating and documenting detailed information architecture and interaction designs. I also perform user research, concept testing, and usability testing when the need arises. I love what I do.
