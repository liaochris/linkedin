Erin Schreyer’s Summary

Sagestone Partners’ vision is to improve organizations by applying leadership principles and bringing out the very best in individuals, professional relationships and HR processes. We apply best practices and certified coaching techniques to enhance awareness, strengths, communication and potential. Companies who embrace this philosophy can revolutionize their organizations by building into key leaders, who can articulate vision and further engage and motivate others…and improve results across the board. Individuals will gain greater fulfillment as they maximize their potential.

Sagestone Partners recognizes that every organization and its culture is unique, as are the needs of the individual leaders and contributors. We work diligently to ensure the needs of both are met, as our goal is the long-term effectiveness and success of both people and organizations.

Our leadership brings the culmination of knowledge and experience in business development, recruitment, leadership, talent management, marketing/public relations and information technology. These skills have been built through successful career experiences, relevant relationships and education. Our accredited training in the field of business coaching brings added value, structure and methodology to the processes and best practices we employ. In addition, we bring our hearts, passion, energy and enthusiasm for what we do to every client engagement.

We believe in building valuable partnerships with clients, trusted relationships with organizational leadership and employees, and lasting benefits and improvement to your bottom line.

Sagestone Partners is a certified Minority and Women-Owned Business Enterprise (M/WBE.)
