Paul Johnson’s Summary

On your climb to the top, you’ve reached the plateau. It's pleasant there -- nice view, easy walking -- but you’re not getting any closer to the peak. So how do you climb higher? If you knew how, you would.

I help individuals and organizations learn how to climb beyond the plateau. Soon they're able to see the blind spots that block their progress toward the summit. I've learned that those blind spots come from fear of a future outcome. That feared outcome appears to us as trouble and threatens to hold us back.

We must be willing to cause trouble -- and assume responsibility for those troubles -- if we want to break through performance barriers and rise above our plateau.

Leaders hire me when they want to make a statement. "Trouble is not a problem. Complacency is."

Organizations hire me when they want to see results. I often speak at association conferences, company meetings and sales rallies. Together we create a legion of Trouble Breakers who become an unstoppable force for creating shortcuts to performance improvement within their companies.
