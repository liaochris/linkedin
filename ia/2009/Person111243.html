Jesaka Long’s Summary

A born storyteller with a gift for engaging audiences, I have helped companies craft their stories for more than 12 years. A full-time freelancer and owner of a.k.a writer in Denver, I work word magic for global and national retailers, consulting companies, marketing firms and leaders in consumer-focused green initiatives.

Jesaka Long’s Specialties:

Leveraging my expertise in retail, food & beverage, human resources, corporate and internal communications, I have built a strong reputation for writing dynamic content, providing strategic communications counsel and identifying the optimum way to deliver messages. I am known for delivering projects on time, within budget and with outstanding customer service. With humor and insight, I make even the most mundane “eat-your-vegetables” messages seem savory, sweet and tangy.
