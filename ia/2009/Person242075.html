About Us

Venture Capital Rescue works exclusively with a provider of private debt and equity for commercial projects for over 15 years. With the near-meltdown of the capital markets in recent months, developers and builders are faced with unique challenges. These owners and operators of commercial real estate may be able to benefit from a unique restructuring of the asset and the capital stack associated with the property.

The Joint Venture model allows the current owner of the asset to retain ownership of their equity and allows a preferential return to them for the equity contributed. Additionally, the owner of the asset can enjoy a shared interest of the profits or proceeds from the completion of value to the asset. Usually, the owner will recover all or more of the equity they have in the project.

If you have a project that needs a little CPR and needs to be financially re-engineered, please email us to see how we can assist you. We work in select markets all across the country and in multiple property types.
