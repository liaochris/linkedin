Paul Roberts’s Summary

As a Certified Crane Inspector I provide third party and annual inspections of Mobile, Tower and Overhead cranes, plus inspections of personnel and aerial lifts to insure compliance with OSHA, manufacturer's and industry standards. My goal is to be considered as a value-added partner with clients that they may get the best quality and longevity from their investments. I also investigate and conduct forensic evaluations of crane accidents throughout the country.

As Senior Engineering Technician I provide field surveying services, civil and structural design and support, and drafting services. I also work closely with the Insurance Industry conducting damage assessment and evaluations, and providing accident investigation services.
