Ruby Sahiwal’s Summary

Ruby started Accelcia Business Services to provide one-stop shop to take care of all non-core needs such as Finance, Accounting, HR etc. for small to mid-sized and high-growth companies.

Prior to strating Accelcia, Ruby's career has been a blend of 'hands-on' operating experience and broad strategic perspective. Ruby's operating experience includes line roles at GE, i2, VerticalNet, Aentropy, Ketera and now Accelcia; he held Strategic & Operational roles at GE and McKinsey & Company.

As COO of Ketera Technologies (a Kleiner Perkins funded American Express spin-off), Ruby created the first "on-demand" provider of Spend Management solutions. Revenues grew 10x under his leadership. Ruby was founder and CEO of Aentropy, a spend analysis technology company acquired by Ketera. As COO of VerticalNet Solutions, Ruby helped create this Enterprise Software arm of VerticalNet from its acquisitions of Isadra & Tradeum. Previously, as VP of eBusiness Services for i2 Technologies Ruby was involved in the creation of TradeMatrix & other eBusiness initiatives. At i2 he also lead the pioneering and highly successful value based selling Strategic Opportunity Assessment (SOA) group. Ruby joined i2 Technologies from McKinsey & Company.

At McKinsey, Ruby served senior leaders of several Fortune 50 companies in operations efficiency and new market entry. He is a graduate of GE's Manufacturing Management Program (MMP) and held several operations roles at General Electric for over 6 years - from Plant Manager to running a Joint Venture in Asia to undertaking post-merger integration of 5 acquisitions in Europe.

Ruby earned an MBA from the University of Chicago, and obtained BS in Aerospace & Mechanical Engineering from SUNY at Buffalo.

He currently serves on the Board of Directors of Serus, Inc. and is an advisor to Unimacts, OnDemand Resources, and Reachforce.
