  • Researcher (Graduating for MSc)

    University of Amsterdam - Intelligent System Laboratory

    (Higher Education industry)

    January 2006 — November 2006 (11 months)

    For my graduation project I teamed up with Max Pfingsthorn and under the supervision of Arnoud Visser and Nikos Vlasses we spent 11 months in what I still regard as one of my most fantastic experiences. We built a complete robotics system from scratch where we have incorporated state-of-the-art algorithms and approaches in order to acquire a team of robots that was able to autonomously explore rescue sites and to collaborate in finding victims and building a map of the environment.

    Using our system we participated in the RoboCup Rescue World Championships and won 2 awards there. After our thesis project the system was redesigned into the UsarCommander Robotics Control system. This was done as we extended our research to go into more detail on the area of team-level collaboration and coordination, frontier-based exploration and communication under the conditions of short range and limited bandwidth connectivity.
