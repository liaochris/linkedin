Richard Stewart’s Summary

Mindbench is an award winning and values driven, specialist recruitment firm.

Mindbench was awarded runner up in the 2009 Top-Consultant Best Recruitment Firm survey.

We recruit management consultants on a permanent and interim basis for the Big4, strategy houses, boutiques, private equity and industry. We have a talent platform of the best management consultants.

We look for consultants with management consultancy experience in strategy firms or internal strategy experience within industry. We also recruit consultants with M&A strategy, post-merger integration experience, operational process improvement, change management and supply chain experience.

We recruit management consultancy talent across industry verticals including TMT, FMCG, Financial Services, Energy & Utilities.

We recruit internationally for roles in London, across UK, Europe, Middle East (including Dubai), Asia (including Shanghai), and Australia (Sydney, Melbourne).

We are looking to further build our networks in the management consultancy industry and appreciate your support in building connections with us. If you or a colleague is looking for an opportunity within consultancy, or to have a discussion about the market in general, please do not hesitate to call us.

Richard Stewart is an entrepreneur with a background in international management consultancy services and financial services.

Richard is regularly quoted by the leading consulting press including Management Consulting International and Consulting-News. He is also a regular speaker at leading industry events including the annual conference of the Insitute of Management Consultants.

Richard Stewart’s Specialties:

Management consultancy recruitment; head-hunting; search and selection; project and interim recruitment services. Recruitment of strategy consultants and management consultants.
