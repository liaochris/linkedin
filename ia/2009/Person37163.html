Carazoo India’s Summary

Carazoo.com is India’s first car portal that provides 360 exterior and interior car animations for all car models available in India. Carazoo is here to bridge the gap between buyers, sellers, and dealers and make car buying and selling an easy, fun, and hassle-free process. Carazoo hopes to lessen the mystery that surrounds cars and the car industry and help people enjoy every aspect of cars and what they are all about.

Carazoo is a virtual showroom that allows a visitor to perform extensive research. The navigation of the site is simple and easy, so visitors can read car reviews, ask for e-brochures, request for car price quotes, check out car specifications, and book free test drives without leaving their seats.

A person can buy a new car or a pre-owned car from Carazoo with a simple click of the mouse. Carazoo also helps people sell their used cars online. Carazoo hosts an extensive list of dealerships all over India and a visitor can easily request for dealer information to purchase a car without too much commute involved. Carazoo also provides tips on how to buy new cars or used cars and how to get car loans and car insurance easily. Applications to ask for car loans and car insurance are available online. Various GIFTS, OFFERS and DISCOUNTS are available THROUGHOUT THE YEAR on Carazoo.

Visitors can register to receive free copies of Carazoo’s monthly car newsletter called Car Mantra.

We also help Dealers to sell more cars by creating interactive websites for them. We also generate leads for all the dealers in India and guide them in their online marketing section.
