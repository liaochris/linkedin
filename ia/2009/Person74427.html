Eric Kang’s Summary

Mr. Kang has 12 years consulting experiences in SAP. He is a well known SAP security expert worldwide. Many SAP customers and SAP management have recommended his consulting services over the years. He has extensive network in the SAP world.
Currently, Mr. Kang is a Managing Principal and a Founder of Adventier Consulting Group, Inc., where he is in charge of SAP security consulting business and supports key customers’ projects.
Mr. Kang was with SAP America since 1996, where he had been a SAP Platinum consultant since 1999, and National Security Practice Lead till June 2006
Mr. Kang has the sincere desire to understand technology, and the energetic drive to deliver top quality and best value to customers. He enjoys socializing and mentoring, his knowledge transfer approach is both systematic and intuitive.
Before SAP, Mr. Kang worked in the twin city area as a Sr. software developer and consultant for over 6 years.
