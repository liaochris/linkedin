Lary Kirchenbauer’s Summary

My professional life has been devoted to working with business leaders and their executive teams to drive improved personal and professional performance. I have 35 years of executive leadership and strategic growth experience. My career is uniquely diverse as a result of my experience as a commercial and investment banker, venture capital partner and investor, as well as several intensive and high growth CFO, COO and CEO experiences. Served as the Managing General Partner of a $35MM venture capital fund; an EVP and CFO of a high growth, consumer products company; and founded a merchant bank. Raised over $200MM for young, emerging businesses, and served as a director of seven companies, working extensively within the consumer, food processing, wine, manufacturing, and professional services industries.

Lary Kirchenbauer’s Specialties:

I have worked with accelerated growth companies as well as companies struggling to survive, as both of them require consistent focus on execution, comprehensive cash management and the development of superior leadership teams. I have helped companies raise over $200 million by working closely with them to shape their strategic plan and developing an action plan relentlessly devoted to getting results.
