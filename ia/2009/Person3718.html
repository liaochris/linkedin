Aditya Ray’s Summary

Aditya is a seasoned Marketing and Communication professional and a proven Business Leader to drive success in challenging business environments.

He has been particularly more successful in GEC space due to his ability to create astute content strategy and brand communication plan on one hand and ability to market them well (often by pre-sales and client collaboration and hard-driving the Sales) on the other.

He participated in the content strategy for Zee TV as Head of Marketing Services & Strategy in ’98-’99 and successfully thwarted Sony Ent TV from its march to numero uno position. As Channel Head, he turned around loss making Zee Bangla into profits in 2002-04 and made it as the leading Bengali Satellite channel. Later, as the CEO of MTV in Sri Lanka, he led the team to pull flagship Sirasa TV to No.1 position from a distant 3rd position within a record 1 year! Since then (2005) Sirasa has been enjoying the unchallenged leadership in Sri Lanka.

Helped BCCL (Times Group) establish its stronghold in East through pioneering city-connect moves like Kolkata Festival, Sharod Shopping Festivals etc during his short stint as the Zonal Head.

Played a key role in establishing the Times Private Treaties business on a strong Relationship Management platform in its early days.

As the CEO and Chief Evangelist of Last Minute Media he led a small, young team to launch world's first online, real-time, Media Web-Mall to buy & sell all conceivable forms of media inventories on lastminuteinventory.com. Almost all the leading publications and broadcasters and many of the niche media; almost all the leading advertising agencies and many direct advertisers have already become its member and started participating in its trade regularly.

Aditya Ray’s Specialties:

Media Content Strategy, Managing Profit Centers, Business Planning & Strategy. Brand Communication planning, Customer engagement solutions, Media buying & selling, Analysis & measurements.
