Nina Cherry’s Summary

I live and work on Maui and coach professionals across America by phone. I help people get more clients, and especially ideal clients. My expertise is in assisting business and service professionals to develop a step-by-step marketing plan and then be accountable in following through to achieve their goals for success.

Every month I teach a 6-week teleclass called GET CLIENTS NOW! You can find out more by emailing me at nina@ninacherry.com.

I also bring people here to exotic Maui on personal retreats of breakthrough coaching and wonderful adventures like swimming, hiking the dormant volcano, snorkeling with schools of tropical fish and sea turtles and kayaking with the whales.

A professional coach for twelve years, I graduated from Corporate Coach University and have a bachelor's degree in Human Resource Management. I have been in the field of training and development for over 20 years.

Two of the popular workshops I teach on site for businesses are: GET CLIENTS NOW! and Great People Skills for a Positive Workplace. I also teach core level counseling skills to health care professionals.

My goals are to enjoy life even more, rest more, work less, make more money ---- and to help others do the same!

Nina Cherry’s Specialties:

Marketing to get more clients, finding your true calling, starting a new career or business, building your business, internet marketing, finding more meaning in your life, work-life balance, and personal retreats on Maui.
