Simon Welsh’s Summary

I have 12 years sales experience and a profound understanding of the nature of conflict and an excellent framework for conflict resolution. This has paved the way for a surprising career in the business world, where I now find myself a senior account manager for the prestigious and very fast growing Pure360 Email Marketing platform.

I've never been able to say this about previous jobs. I adore what I do. And it shows in the way I entice my prospects and deliver to my clients.

My goal - I have but one - is to wake up from this prescribed and false reality into a world fueled by compassion and love.
