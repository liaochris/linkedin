Kevin Foster’s Summary

Kevin Foster, Senior Systems Administrator for Vignette Corporation has 14 years of system administration experience. Kevin has worked the past 11 years supporting research and development groups. He has managed lab environments with as few as 100 to as many as 1200 systems. While at Vignette Corporation, Kevin grew the lab systems from 400 to 1200 systems while the staff was reduced from ten administrators to two. His ability to automate, track, monitor and manage multiple operating system environments has helped deliver hundreds of product releases on time. Over the last three years Kevin, has been working to reduce the number of physical systems by deploying virtualization technologies and has earned his VMware Certified Professional certification. Kevin started at Vignette as a Microsoft windows administrator and is now also experienced in supporting VMware, Red Hat, Suse, Solaris, AIX and HPUX operating systems. He also anticipates the needs of research and development and is able to proactively support their needs.
