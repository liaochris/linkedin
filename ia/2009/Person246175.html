Anthony Richardson’s Summary

Anthony is an award winning Interactive Marketing consultant that employs a versatile number of tools to stay sharp in an ever changing arena. His consultancy, Fugitive Marketing, strives on understanding the growing irrelevance of old-style marketing and advertising techniques. Through the strength of the new media jungle, he helps his clients grow by building meaningful relationships, growing conversational exposure, and harvesting powerful online communications. To put Anthony’s services to work for you, please contact him.

BIO

For the past 6 years, Anthony has been tapping into the ever changing ways that we communicate in order to help people, brands, and companies adapt and grow. He began in Saint Louis, while still in college, working for small businesses and internet startups that needed some fresh perspective on how to get the word out. He most recently moved to Orlando Florida, and has been busting his ass to get a startup concept living and breathing. If you are an investor, entrepreneur, or advertising guru looking for an exciting new project please contact Anthony.
