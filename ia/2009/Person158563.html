Dr. Sudhi Gautam’s Summary

Dr. Sudhi Gautam - Surgeon & Engineer, Medical Innovation Facilitator

As CEO of MEDTECH BRIDGE helped accelerate innovation in medical products by providing consulting and corporate training services in Cardiovascular, Pulmonary, ENT, Renal, Orthopedic, Neurotechnology, Minimally Invasive devices, Blood Devices, Stem Cell-based technologies, Medical Implants & Healthcare IT.

Dr. Sudhi Gautam is a ENT surgeon-turned-Biomedical Engineering Ph.D. from the renowned IIT. His 8 years of surgical practice motivated him into a engineering Ph.D. after realizing the amazing impact that innovations in technology could have in addressing clinically unmet needs. For further info: e-mail: sudhi@medtechbridge.com or call 408-569-9521

Helped early stage Medical Device Startups and Large companies to conceptualize new medical devices and develop high impact market/business strategies. Evaluate prospective deals, competitive attractiveness of opportunities, due diligence, IP/Market Research.

• Teach high-quality cross-disciplinary courses/workshops on ‘Overview of the Medical Device Industry’, ‘Medical Devices- The Basics & The Business’ for University of California, Berkeley (extension), ‘Medical Device Design and Development’ for UC, Santa Cruz extension and ‘Cardiovascular Physiology & Engineering: Drugs and Devices’ for UC, Irvine extension.

• Currently Life Science Chair for SVASE (Silicon Valley Association of Startup Entrepreneurs) and Council Member for BioDevice Group, both very active groups in the Medical Device Segment.

• High-energy, motivational speaker, driven, out-of-the-box thinker with a keen sense of humor, openness and adaptability. Do motivational talks focused on trends in the Medical Device industry.
