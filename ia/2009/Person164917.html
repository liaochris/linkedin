Michael Campana’s Summary

Michael Campana is an accomplished engineering professional and customer relations advocate well known for his technical abilities and account management, regardless of the industry he’s in. His experience spans the software industry, high precision automation robotics and HVAC design.

While at Navis LLC, the leading provider of enterprise level software solutions for Marine Terminals, Michael managed high profile global accounts on a daily basis. He flourished in the high-pressure situations where he proved his ability to orchestrate efforts. His project engineering experience involved software upgrades and implementations. Mike established himself as the go-to person for tough problems, both with his customers and internally. His technical ability and people skills along with his confident and calm demeanor, fostered customer confidence which led to higher customer satisfaction and increased positive feedback. He was also a leader in the Training Department where he developed the curriculum for the new SPARCS N4 Billing ™ product and taught.

Before this he spent 5 years at the robotics manufacturing facility of Kensington Labs LLC in Richmond, CA. where he performed QA engineering and helped establish the Service Department for the Wafer Handler robots. He implemented best practices for robot/controller analysis and repair that decreased turn-around time and improved service failure rates. He also authored instruction manuals and training tutorials and taught.

Prior to that, Michael spent a couple of years as a Mechanical Engineer with a small HVAC design firm in San Rafael, CA. His work here focused on HVAC, Piping and Plumbing system design for commercial buildings.

Michael is now entering the exciting and fast growing field of Solar Energy where he’s seeking a role in which he can apply his unique combination of customer facing skills, technical savvy and project experience.

Michael Campana’s Specialties:

Account management and customer relations advocate. First-rate communication skills to convey relevant benefits and build relations. Detail-oriented persona good for training. Work well under pressure with ability to coordinate in volatile situations. Unusually versatile with Mechanical Eng., Robotics, Software and even construction.
