Diane Egner’s Summary

Journalist, writer, editor, communicator turned advocate for public broadcasting. Valuable cross-platform experience in creating and selling all forms of media content via newspaper, radio, television, web. Well-versed in supervising and directing cross-platform teams of diverse individuals. Best known in journalism for integrity, honesty, doggedness and thoroughness and in the community as a connector of people and a leader on nonprofit boards.
