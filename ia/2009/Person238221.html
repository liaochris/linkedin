Trina Hoefling’s Summary

Author of internationally known and critically acclaimed book, Working Virtually: Managing People and Organizations for Success, now in its second printing. Chapter author in The Handbook of High Performance Virtual Teams, spring 08 release of John C. Wiley.

I'm writing more, and am enjoying blogging at www.TrinaHoefling.com.

I project manage, consult, facilitate, support, and train Virtual Collaboration, Virtual and Global Work, and implentation of Telework solutions. We can provide custom or off-the-shelf training solutions that meet your needs. JIT online training will be available in 2009. For the Do-It-Yourselfer, I have developed The Virtual Team Tool Box, a set of resources, templates, assessments and other tools that will save you hours and hours of development time. Go to www.virtualteamtoolbox.com for more information.

In addition to virtual work solutions, I also provide emerging leadership development, facilitate executive team coaching and work team issues arising from crisis, and root strategy planning and entrepreneurial coaching.

I want to work with business, NGO, and government leaders invested in their organization and customers' success, and who committed to their people as well as profitability and strategic success..

Trina Hoefling’s Specialties:

Virtual Work, Relationship Management, Organization Development, Change Management, and Growth Strategy.
We also provide coaching to consultants who want to ensure their work is systemically sound. Much of our work can be virtual. We don't want money to be a barrier and have creative pricing strategies.
