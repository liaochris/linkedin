Richard Ackroyd’s Summary

My experience covers customer facing and internal roles, managing strategy, products and people, across a broad spectrum of services in the technology industry. My successes in recent years cover integrating mergers & acquisitions of Siemens over the past 3 years into the corporate process and technology landscape, designing structures to minimise the technology cost of carving out business units and the development of a north west europe wide enterprise architecture concept.

Strategy Development, Mergers and Acquisitions, Carve Out, Budget Planning, Project Management, Pre-Sales Support, Compliance management, Deployment of ERP, procurement, project and service mangement systems, ITIL service management,technology management, people management and coaching, developing teams, ARIS process modelling, Casewise enterprise architecture modelling, TOGAF
