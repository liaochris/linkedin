Chris Lott’s Summary

My favorite subjects/passions are technology and sales. I’m truly blessed to be involved with both.

For the last 20+ years I have been involved in technical sales and design, management (executive), and principle/owner.

Successfully designed and ran a large BBS, The Business Hub, and was given kudos from BBS magazine in 1993.

Built a multi-million dollar computer manufacturing company from ground up and was awarded as the top system in Windows Magazine in 1997-1998.

Last 10+ years I have been managing, selling, and supporting VoIP hardware and software solutions as well as network services products. I currently hold the position of VP of Sales for DataTel Communications at this time.

Current Design and Programming Projects include lottspace.com, totallysales.com and idaholeadsgroup.com.

Chris Lott’s Specialties:

I’m a proven manager and leader. Competent VoIP, SIP, Data CPE, Communications Equipment/Software solutions consultant. Web designer using PHP, XHTML, CSS languages and MySQL database design.
