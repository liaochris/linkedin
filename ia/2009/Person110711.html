Jeremy Stunt’s Summary

Jeremy leads Limbic Human Capital, a specialist executive coaching firm that helps senior executives succeed in a world of accelerating change and uncertainty. Learn more at www.LimbicHumanCapital.com.

Jeremy is an executive coach who coaches using the Ontological approach (see http://www.LimbicHumanCapital.com/2843.html to learn about Ontological coaching). He gained his formal education and training in coaching at the University of Hong Kong and the world-renowned Newfield Institute of Australia. As an adjunct to his coaching training, Jeremy studies the neuroscience of coaching and leadership. He took his first tentative steps as a coach ten years ago when he adopted a coaching style in his problem-solving conversations with senior investment banking executives.

For most of his career, Jeremy has worked with strong leaders; he understands their issues and how they operate. His personal leadership experience has been gained whilst holding a variety of senior positions during periods of immense change.

Coupled with his formal coaching training, particularly in the realm of Ontological coaching, Jeremy represents a unique combination of skills and experience, giving him the potential to add significant value to your organization. He brings immediate credibility and gravitas to a coaching conversation enabling leaders to engage more effectively and more efficiently to raise their own productivity and the productivity of their teams.

Situational experience includes:
• Business turnaround
• Strategic review
• Restructuring and rationalisation
• Business growth and expansion
• Takeover and change of ownership
• Distressed change situations

Jeremy is based in Hong Kong and has lived in Asia for over 13 years working with senior executives across a wide span of countries including China, Japan, Korea, Singapore, India, Vietnam and the Philippines.
