Scott Moore’s Summary

I have over 16 years of IT experience with various platforms and technologies. I've worked on some of the largest applications and infrastructures in the world. Loadtester has assessed, developed and implemented a performance Center of Excellence for companies like Deloitte, Kroger, Unum, and Fifth Third Bank.

I am a HP Accredited Systems Engineer (ASE) and Accredited Sales Consultant (ASC), as well as a Mercury Certified Product Consultant (CPC) and a Certified Instructor (CI).

Loadtester Incorporated is a Preferred HP Solutions Partner and Reseller of their BTO line of products. All of our consultants (100%) hold ASE and CI certifications. Our company has a focus on performance and availability solutions, also have skills to help with Quality Center, QuickTest Pro, SiteScope, and Diagnostics. We offer custom training classes around load testing and test management, as well and give the official HP courseware in the products we consult on.
