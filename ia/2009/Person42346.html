Cheyenne Peddle’s Summary

IT Manager / Project Manager with more than 12 years of progressive experience in IT with a college diploma in Computer Science and 'Strategic IT Leadership' training from Queens University.

I believe it is important for today’s senior IT professional to be business savvy with people skills and communication skills appropriate to all levels of the organization. I tend to be direct in my dealings with people, precise with work produced, and focused on established goals. I have a natural ability to lead and motivate people – treating everyone with respect while expecting superior performance and results.
