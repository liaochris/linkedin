Damian Farrell’s Summary

Creative director, animator & puppeteer. Responsible for creating and developing tv, film and online projects for broadcasters, agencies and online distribution.

Current chairman of the Animation CEO Forum.

Member of the Screen Directors Guild.

Bio: I started in the industry working in animation (Sullivan Bluths, Emerald City, Murakami & Wolf) working on tv and feature animation in a variety of departments from animation, to special effects to rostrum camera.

I trained as a puppeteer with the Jim Henson Company in London and have puppeteered on many tv shows including 2 years presenting Saturday mornings on BBC1.

I worked closely with Double Z Enterprises for almost 10 years directing commercials, music videos and tv series for their characters Zig & Zag, Dusin and Podge & Rodge's Scare At Bedtime.

Other tv projects include writing and directing RTE's hidden camera shows 'Anonymous' & 'I Dare Ya', directing an Arts Lives documentary 'Pulling Strings' on the work of Eugene Lambert and tv magic show 'Mind, Myths & Magic'.

Caboom attempts to bring together all these interests under one roof - well two actually, one roof in Dublin and the other at Caboom's office in LA.

Caboom has produced a wide variety of content for RTE, BBC, Disney, TBS & Fox - from kids to adult - from animation to liveaction.

Caboom is currently in development on 'Special 1 Tv World Cup 2010' - a follow on series to puppet comedy show originally created for Setanta Sports UK featuring characters created by Mario Rosenstock. The show airs in 10 countries and has become a mini youtube sensation taking full advantage of a variety of social media including youtube, facebook, twitter & various soccer blogs to engage with it's audience online and on-air.

Also just completed production on 'Toads Rules' by Jeff Muncy ('Pet Alien' & 'Pajanimals'). 'Toad Rules' is a co-production between Caboom and Telescreen in Holland.
