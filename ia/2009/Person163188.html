Michelle Silverman’s Summary

Michelle Silverman, a Realtor for over 17 years, is one of those rare people, a native San Diegan; leaving San Diego only long enough to receive a BA in sociology from UCLA. She is also a second generation realtor.

After graduation, Michelle owned her own retail ready-to-wear business for seven years. There she learned about customer service and discovered that customer satisfaction plays a key role in establishing loyal clients. She brings that same caring philosophy to her relationships with her real estate clients.

Whatever the current market conditions, Michelle's professionalism, coupled with market knowledge, negotiation skills and willingness to go beyond the call of duty, have allowed her to be ranked consistently in the top 1-2% of Prudential sales professionals nationwide.
