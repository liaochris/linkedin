Jennifer Cox’s Summary

My current position is Marketing Director for the Accident and Health Division of American General Life Companies. We have recently created a Social Media Task Force to develop our social media strategy. I’ll be honest. Implementing social media in a massively regulated environment will be challenging, but I love a good challenge. I believe that social media will change the way we market forever. Although the basic principles of strategy (Who is your audience? What do you want to do for them? What do you want them to do for you? Which tools best support your objectives?) remain the same, today’s consumer demands more access, information, ease and transparency. Companies who fail to deliver, will suffer. The upside potential is huge. If you're not convinced, read this study (www.ENGAGEMENTdb.com) from Charlene Li, author of The Groundswell, which correlates the level of social media engagement to revenue/profitability. Drink the kool-aid. You won’t be sorry.

Sorry for the social media tangent. I just love the stuff.

According to a recent personal brand survey of my network, my most outstanding attributes are my high energy level, ambition, intelligence, creativity, collaborative work style, organization, drive and well, just being an all-around likable person. They also said that if I were a car, I would be a BMW because I am fast, precise and high-performance. My personal philosophy is that life is too short not to do what you love. Everyday won’t be rosy, but keep the big picture in focus.
