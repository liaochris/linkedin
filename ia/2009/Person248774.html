Werner Heid’s Summary

High-tech CEO with strong track record launching new products, building large scale profitable businesses and executing major business turn-arounds. Twenty years successive P&L leadership at consumer electronic/technology companies, driving accelerated growth organically and through M&A. Broad experience in marketing product portfolios to consumers and small businesses through mass merchandising (e-direct, e-tail, retail, distribution) as well as value-add (Resellers, System Integrators, OEM) channels. Global experience in US, Asia, Europe and Latin America: fluent in Spanish, German and English. Decisive leader with passion for developing and leading high-performance teams, establishing strong customer loyalty, expanding market share and reducing operating costs.

• Led wide scale corporate turn-around of Iomega. Completely rebuilt business strategy, product portfolio, R&D focus, channel strategy and supply chain infrastructure during a time of rapid sales decline, returning the company to sustainable profitability. Created approximately $200M of annual sales with new innovative storage solution portfolio and rebuilt Iomega brand image which laid the foundation for the successful sale of Iomega to EMC corporation.
• Drove business growth strategy at Proxima ($125M to $240M) enabling two profitable acquisitions. Managed subsequent integrations (InFocus & ASK) and the creation of an innovative home entertainment business strategy.
• As HP’s Worldwide Marketing Manager, created and launched All-in-One and Color Copier Product Lines, currently a $4B business.
• As head of European Marketing, grew HP LaserJet to $1B in sales, increasing market share from 24% to 53% and created HP’s first consolidated printer supplies business strategy ($700M).
