Viable Medical Device/Pharmaceutical Recruiter

Who We Are:
Our office has been a leader in the executive search world for almost 10 years, and consistently recognized as one of the top performing offices within the 1,100-office network. This office has achieved “President Club” for performance in the top 10% of all offices.

Expertise:
I joined the recruiting industry in 2003, and have been a top performer in this office for the past four plus years. I try to understand the objective of my client as well as the objective of each candidate I work with. I expect all placements to be long term career moves, and approach the process with this in mind. I believe that networking and communication are the keys to finding the impact player my clients desire.

My Specialty:
I focus on Sales, Marketing, Management (Sales up to VP Level), and clinical positions in the Medical and Health Care industry. I only work with companies with a strong reputation and the highest integrity. I work with candidates to understand their career objectives and work to match their objectives with a great company opportunity. I screen with my clients exact needs in mind. This way I do not waste the time and money of my client, the candidate, and my firm. I primarily conduct all searches from networking. I will make every effort to understand the goals of both the candidate and hiring manager to assure a positive fit in the placement.

I would like the opportunity to speak with you about our services to determine if you have a staffing need where we may be of assistance. Please feel free to contact me with any questions you may have. Call me at 404.221.1021 x1187 or send an email to sadams@mriatl.com
