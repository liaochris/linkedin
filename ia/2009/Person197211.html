Ricardo Fernandez’s Summary

Design Director / Design Consultant (Architecture)

Experienced design principal with background leading multi-office, large firm project teams on all aspects of design, design engineering support and design management of diverse building types, including corporate, commercial, residential, transportation, education, research, arts and sports. Recognized success in high-performance design culture as well as in practice leadership focused on organizational realignment to achieve design excellence. Received professional and critical recognition for projects ranging from the Civic Opera House of Chicago to the Miami Intermodal Center to the competition-winning design of a protective structure for a Marc Chagall mosaic mural. Since 2000, lead designer/design consultant to multi-phase, $1B transportation hub and civic gateway project, Miami Intermodal Center.

Ricardo Fernandez’s Specialties:

Practice marked by critical thinking, strong concept development, formal creativity, complex problem-solving, development and nurturing of client relationships, professional staff mentoring, facility in oral and written communications and proactive interaction with civic, regulatory, professional and academic communities.
Strong background in commercial office, residential, cultural/education and transportation infrastructure.
