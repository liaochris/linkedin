Paul J Roberts’s Summary

I manage a medium sized sales team within the Aftermarket High Performance & OEM Automotive Parts Industry. Coach, train and mentor sales team members with an emphasis on new sales techniques, professional growth, and leveraging each individual’s core skill set. My focus is increasing annual sales revenue and exceeding the customer’s expectations.

Troubleshooter-

I troubleshoot and analyze company processes that have an effect on our overall efficiency, effectiveness and profitability. Departments of interest are Sales, Customer Service, Purchasing, Merchandising, New Product or Vendor Lines, Custom Order Products and Information Management. Determine source(s) of root problem and develop and implement new company wide policies and processes that increase our company’s efficiency, profitability and final product to the customer.

Project Management--

I specialize in Competitive Analytics with regard to product, price, policy and business strategy. Also focus on new and upcoming trends and technologies and how implementation may provide future competitive advantage.

* Analyze competitor business markets, practices, product lines and pricing
* Develop recommendations to implement changes in company strategies that improve our competitive advantage
* Develop presentations for senior level management personnel: operations, marketing, merchandising, advertising, sales, finance, and warehousing
