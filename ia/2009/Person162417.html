Michael W. Oldham’s Summary

Our company is growing with Realtors whose culture is to provide high quality service to clients and also to build a solid future for themselves in Real Estate. Our company consists of Residential and Commercial Realtors that continue to educate themselves to the shifting market.

My focus is to ensure company compliance with Real Estate laws and regulations, and along with others in the company to maintain and improve our company culture. In the past years I have successfuly closed escrows in residential, commercial, land, and housing develpments.

For the past 15 years, the Oldham Team has specialized in distressed property sales that include Short Sales, Bank Owned (REO), and probates.


Realtors looking for a profitable future in real estate or potential clients can contact me by e-mail or telephone.

Michael W. Oldham’s Specialties:

I am a designated CCIM, Certitfied Commercial Investment Manager, and have 15 years commercial expierence, and more than 22 years total in real estate. Analyzing real estate to ensure the buyer is meeting their needs and goals and marketing the sale to ensure that sellers come away with top dollar. I work hard to ensure that the seller pays minimal closing costs and taxes, or no taxes at all. I also have 5 other designations that relate to distressed property sales and REO/Short Sale realty.
