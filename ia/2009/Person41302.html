Chuck Fontana’s Summary

Multi-functional business and legal director for Cisco’s Voice (Unified Communications) Technology development unit: a global, 2000-person, $2B/year business.

A 17-year practicing and licensed California attorney, I've held multiple marketing, business development, operational and legal roles, currently comprising one of Cisco's broadest "utility players" in the Cisco Development Organization. Prior career successes included two technology startups as CEO and Business Development Vice President, along with a successful 8 years civil litigation law firm experience.

Current business expertise includes leading Cisco's Unified Communications Solutions Marketing strategy in Hospitality, Healthcare, and Public Safety verticals, and product managment of the Cisco Unified Application Environment product. Additionally, I lead Cisco's API strategy for IP Phone and Unified Communications Manager call control interfaces supporting third party application developers, driving a best-in-class developer experience for Cisco UC ISVs.

Legal experiences include broad general counsel-type duties: IP licensing/ management, OEM negotiations, sales contract support, SOX/revenue recognition compliance, patent strategies/cross license strategies, international regulatory compliance, and export license control.

In all, I'm a high-impact, multi-faceted executive leader and communicator, with strong managerial skills and consistent Top 10%/25% performance reviews
