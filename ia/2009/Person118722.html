Jonathan Bates’s Summary

Jonathan Bates is a senior creative director specializing in web content publishing and dynamic e-commerce platforms.

Before returning to the now-freezing cold Midwest, Jonathan accrued more than ten years experience as a Silicon Valley senior creative and team manager for CNET Networks and running the multimedia development group at the nation's first broadband provider, @Home Networks, serving original content to the network's 2 million-plus subscribers.

In recent years working independently, Jonathan has worked with some of the best-known brands in the world, with roles that include creative director, design director, site manager and creative consultant.

Typical clients range from well-known major national retailers to boutique design firms, and a host of mid-sized ventures who come to me seeking to create, rebuild or enhance their online presence; a surprising number of my clients have operated websites for years without ever considering the demographic, revenue-generation or user-oriented design aspects that make a site truly exceptional.

His specialties include strategic branding; dynamic site architecture and design; presentation design and speaker training; and management of creative and production teams in both project- and publishing-oriented configurations.
