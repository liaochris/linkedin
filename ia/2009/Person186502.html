Peyton Trent Collie’s Summary

Technologist at heart with deep expertise in the Internet arena. Fourteen year career in IT specializing in strategic consulting, pre-sales engineering, business development, product marketing and project management.

My career began in the early days of personal computing, as a System Operator (Sysop) of a nationwide bulletin board system (BBS). At an early age I began to develop and grow expertise around networking, hardware and software systems. In 1995 I co-founded Eventure 360 initially as a systems development and IT services delivery firm. Eventure 360 has grown up to become a leading IT consultancy, outsource advisory and service integrator.

I also spend a good deal of time within the New England Internet community, I serve as a trusted expert to my clients and associates and remain active in the Internet innovation and Web 2.0 scene.
