Nancy Reich’s Summary

Senior legal and compliance professional with significant regulatory experience related to the sale and trading of securities. In depth knowledge of the laws and rules governing securities industry. Extensive experience designing, implementing and overseeing internal compliance programs using tactical and strategic solutions to identify and mitigate risks associated with the sale and trading of securities, including surveillance and monitoring programs, examinations programs, development of written policies and procedures and creation and delivery of training. Extensive litigation experience.

Nancy Reich’s Specialties:

Litigation, creation of compliance and supervisory policies and procedures, sales and trading compliance, electronic communications compliance, gifts and entertainment compliance, Code of Ethics, employee registrations, employee trading and outside business activity compliance, anti-money laundering (AML), restricted-list, sales and trading and related surveillance; branch office examinations and market structure issues.
