Alex Fielding’s Summary

My interests lie in human rights, international law and the use of technology to effect social change. A dual Canadian and Swiss citizen, I am fluent in English, French and Spanish.

I'm currently working in the Vancouver office of Stikeman Elliott in commercial litigation, mergers and acquisitions, securities and real estate. I recently graduated with an LL.B. from the University of Victoria and have studied international human rights at Utrecht University in the Netherlands.

Past experience includes working as Online Communications Coordinator for Amnesty International's Make Some Noise campaign (http://www.amnesty.org), Youth Liaison for the UN World Summit on the Information Society (http://www.wsis.org), Multilingual Program Coordinator for international youth NGO TakingITGlobal (http://www.takingitglobal.org), as well as some consulting gigs for the International Center for New Media (http://www.icnm.net), British Council and the Canadian Commission for UNESCO.

Alex Fielding’s Specialties:

Legal research and writing, , project management, international law and policy, human rights, online marketing, e-communications, event coordination, web content management, translation coordination, youth/technology consulting
