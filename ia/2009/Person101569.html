Jackie Adams’s Summary

Experienced franchise executive who now works with individuals interested in business ownership, by offering a FREE service that helps you to logically investigate, evaluate and select a franchise business. By utilizing my 22+ years experience in franchising as a franchisor and franchisee, with established and start up companies, I will help you to evaluate and select a franchise opportunity. I have been "on both sides of the table" and overcome the fear of going into business for yourself. This is a great time to look at buying a franchise - and I can help you through the process!
Chairperson of International Franchise Association Women's Franchise Committee;
Board Member at KnowledgePoints, Inc., a national tutoring franchise.
Board Member for several non-profit organizations in local community;
Active in local and regional franchise and Chamber of Commerce events.

Jackie Adams’s Specialties:
