Mark de Kock’s Summary

Since 1995 part of the upcomming wave called internet. I studied at the Utrecht School of the Arts (Interaction Design). In the past years i've matured from allround Interaction designer to E-marketeer with special interest in Integrated marketing(combining all mediachannels if necessary to produce the best possible marketing solutions).

I have helped develop sites and campagnes for companies like T-Mobile, ABN-AMRO, Postbank among others. have been responsible for E-marketing at a dutch insurance and banking company called Delta Lloyd. This includes Deltalloyd.nl and their partner platform DDD.

Currently i am employed by Sara Lee and work there as int. E-Marketing manager Global Brands C&T / H&BC. Mainly focussed on developments across Europe for brands such as Douwe Egberts, Sanex, Senseo, Ambi Pur, Zwitsal etc.

Nominated for the title online mediaman of the year 2007 by Adlink, IAB, Marketingfacts and IPAN.
