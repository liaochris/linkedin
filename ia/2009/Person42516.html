Amit Ben-Yehoshua’s Summary

Mr. Ben-Yehoshua serves as the China Desk Partner of the leading Israeli law firm Shibolet & Co. Advocates & Notaries. (www.shibolet.com). The legal desk serves foreign investors in three languages English, Hebrew and Chinese.

Amit resides in Shanghai and specializes in China related business law.

Ben-Yehoshua is admitted to practice law in Israel and California as well as the Federal District Court of Eastern District of California. He also holds a Master Degree in Chinese Law (LL.M) from the leading Tsinhgua University of Beijing and is a member of the China Law Steering Committee of the American Bar Association.

Ben-Yehoshua has conducted numerous court and jury trials in both California and Israel and is member of the London Court of International Arbitration (LCIA-YIAG).
