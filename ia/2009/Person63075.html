Jon Moore’s Summary

Diamante Global, SA is a consultancy and service firm providing an extremely focused skill set to our clients in Latin America and select clientele in Canada and the US.
Diamante Global, dba Diamante Ventures is a consultancy firm focused on developers of high profile resort and residential communities. We specialize in "troubled" projects that need restructuring or turnarounds that are looking for traction and profitability.
On behalf of our clients, we properly program, position, plan, and execute development plans as well as arrange for capital infusion when necessary. Because of the fact we take on no more than four projects a year, we are able to provide the personalized and focused service that ensures that projects get done on time and under budget. We are consultants to two distinctly different real estate investment funds in which we provide planning/positioning services as well as the Latin American consultant to a major bridge lending source. Our ability to get quality projects entitled and properly positioned in the marketplace is our core competency.
Diamante Golf, SA (dba Champions Golf) is the exclusive Latin American distributor and North American agent for Interactive Sports Technologies, Inc.'s HiDefinition Golf Simulators and Golf Entertainment Systems. We provide the finest and most realistic golf simulation systems available in the world today. Originators of the high speed camera monitoring/measuring systems (the ONLY systems that can accurately measure clubhead position, ball spin and spin axis) that are the leading tracking technology in the golf industry today, IST provides a golf experience that is so advanced that the only thing more realistic is the golf course itself. More information can be obtained on our website at http://www.diamantegolf.net.
