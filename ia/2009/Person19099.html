Ara Surenian’s Summary

Cadent Resources Group, LLC provides supply chain consulting and technology solutions to help manufacturing and distribution companies:

* Reduce Inventory
* Improve Service
* Generate Cash

Our cornerstone DemandCaster® Demand Planning and Inventory Optimization tool is a low cost high impact alternative to comparable packages. Coupled with the expertise and training of our consultants at the Cadent Resources Group, LLC, DemandCaster® drives sustainable inventory savings and improved service levels.

Our goal is to implement repeatable processes that can be transferred to your personnel to assure ongoing operational excellence.
