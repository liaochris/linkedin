Marco Ranieri’s Summary

I have a Degree in International and Diplomatic Science (Specialization in Economics) and a Master’s Degree awarded by the Italian Institute for Foreign Trade. I have also participated in a ten-months long Programme ("South Eastern Europe and the EU") launched by the College of Europe.

I have studied and worked in Italy and abroad mainly in the consulting and teaching industries (EU PHARE Programme in Romania, Chamber of Commerce of Bucharest, University of Turin, University of Bologna, University of Bucharest, Univerisity of Rome Tor Vergata, EU COMENIUS Programme in Bulgaria, EU EMEA Agency in London, Italian Embassy in Tirana).

I have good communication and presentation skills and I speak Italian, English, Romanian, Spanish and have basic knowledge of Bulgarian. I have very good research and analytical skills, as well as organizational and technical skills.

I am a precise, dynamic, open minded and motivated person, able to work in an international team, assume responsibility and cope with stressful situations.

I am looking for a job in a private or public company or an international organization in the field of business consulting, international trade and development, finance.

My research interests are economic transition in Europe, Caucasus and Asia, FDI and SMEs development policies, EU enlargement.
