  • Executive Director

    Diablo Ballet

    (Performing Arts industry)

    January 2009 — Present (9 months)

    Diablo Ballet is an internationally recognized professional performing dance company committed to enriching, inspiring, enlightening, and educating children and adults through the art of dance. Diablo Ballet was founded in 1993 in order to address the absence of a professional ballet company in the region.

    Diablo Ballet offers exciting dance performances, educational and outreach projects, and productions on tour. The Company has achieved remarkable artistic success capturing enthusiastic public response, substantial critical acclaim, and international recognition. The freshness and vitality of the superb dancers and choreographers coupled with the highest degree of technical excellence and a dynamic, original repertoire continue to captivate audiences of all ages.

  • Former Executive Director now Vice-President/Treasurer, Board of Directors

    Hillbarn Theatre, Inc.

    (Performing Arts industry)

    November 1997 — December 2008 (11 years 2 months)

    Became Executive Director (C.E.O.) in July 2000, after three years on the Board of Directors. Created vision for the theatre that, over the last 8 years, created a renaissance. Over-arching responsibility for all aspects of the theatre including hiring and managing the Artistic Director. Oversight includes development, marketing, sales, public relations, accounting, human resources, and “front of house” operations with an emphasis on the marketing of our product. After a thorough analysis of the then failing non-profit institution, we completed a community focused strategic plan which concentrated on artistic and educational excellence, operational “best practices,” a targeted marketing program, fiscal responsibility, community collaboration, and capital improvements. After repositioning the theatre as “redefining community theatre,” we launched relevant programming such as seasons and productions that evoke the spirit of the community with appropriately high production values.
