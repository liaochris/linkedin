Harry John Kerker’s Summary

Mr Kerker is a unique talent. His fingerprints can be seen on the images of many of America’s leading brands. His advertising experience is international in scope and in other cases hands-on and local. He’s been the creative juice for Fortune 500’s and brilliant start-ups.

He’s recognized as one of the ad industry’s key creative talents and as an out-of-the-box conceptual, yet strategic thinker. His portfolio includes work for, Hewlett Packard, Westin Hotels, Federal Express, Timberland, Alpa Romeo, Peugeot, Kraft, and Mexico Tourism.

His roots are still based firmly in the arts a showing fine art photographer and painter. He’s a teacher and motivational speaker for creative talent on both TV and radio.
He has been honored with over 200 national and regional awards including Clio, the Art Directors Club of New York Gold Medal and a television Emmy.

His goals; New challenges that involve partners, clients or products that challenge conventional thinking.
