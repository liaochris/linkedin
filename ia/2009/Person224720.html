Steven Wolfe’s Summary

- I began my career in investment banking working at firms including The Blackstone Group, Salomon Smith Barney and Violy, Byorum & Partners.

- Combining my passion for technology with the desire to build a company, I joined Akamai in 1999 and served in a variety of management roles in corporate finance, international business development, marketing, network operations, and sales.

- In October 2004, I joined a colleague of mine to head up Business Development and help grow a private, DC-based, data analysis software company called Visual Sciences, LLC.

- After a consulting engagement with my old boss on some technology projects in Latin America, I began to work in late 2005 at Experian Digital / CheetahMail, the online marketing division of Experian, heading up Product Marketing and Business Development.

- In late 2006, I co-founded Platform Asset Management - a value-oriented, operations-driven investment management firm focused on technology and technology-enabled services businesses.

- In May 2007, we merged our fund and joined Platinum Partners, a multi-strategy investment management firm, to manage their Technology, Media and Telecommunications portfolio with a value-oriented investment strategy.

- In April 2008, I went back into industry and decided to apply my finance and technology background to a media company. I'm now heading up ad sales technology strategy, business intelligence and product marketing for Univision Communications, which was taken private in March 2007.
