Jonathan Rado’s Summary

Jonathan has more than 15 years experience in the financial services industry, particularly in investment advising.

Jonathan commenced his career at KPMG & Pitcher Partners in 1991 as a Accountant before taking up a role with Trust Company of Australia Ltd (Trust) in Sydney in 1993. Jonathan spent the next 7 years at Trust Co. gaining valuable experience & working in different area of the firm including Financial Planning, Asset Management for individuals, Asset Management for Trusts, Internal Audit & Taxation. In 1999 Jonathan returned to Melbourne with Trust Co. before taking up a investment advisory role with SAI Group/SAIPrivate in 2000. Jonathan then spent almost 9 years as a Investment Adviser with SAI Group/SAIPrivate/Peloton Private advising clients on their investments with a particular focus on Australian listed securities and unlisted Property.
