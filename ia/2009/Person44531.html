Christie Clough’s Summary

Christie Clough is a planner, a leader, and a challenger. Every day, she challenges herself and her teams to find inspiration from all around them, sometimes in the unlikeliest of places. To look at marketing “problems” as opportunities for inspiration and insight-generation. To ask all the right questions, and even the wrong ones, to get to a solution they never dreamed possible. To immerse themselves in a product, a brand, and even a consumer lifestyle to truly understand the thoughts and dreams of their target. To create an unbreakable bond and united front with their creative and account partners, providing them with inspiration and direction that will lead to work that truly works. To always be there to provide advice (but not always answers) and foster growth. And, finally, to make sure her team goes home at night confident in their decisions and with the knowledge that their contributions, no matter how big or small, are valued.

Christie Clough’s Specialties:
