Andrew Landry’s Summary

I am a lawyer practicing in Preti Flaherty's Energy and Telecommunications Practice Group from the Augusta, Maine office. Preti Flaherty is a full service law firm with offices in Maine, Massachusetts and New Hampshire.

I have extensive experience representing energy and utility clients on regulatory issues before administrative and regulatory agencies, such as the Maine Public Utilities Commission and the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC). I also maintain a practice in the area of corporate transactions, commercial finance and mergers and acquisitions. Indicative representations include:

- Served as counsel to a regional bank in connection with the commercial financing of a hydroelectric power project.

- Served as co-counsel to the purchaser in winning Maine regulatory approval for the acquisition of the landline telephone assets of a major national telecommunications carrier.

- Served as counsel to a Maine company in connection with a $30 million bridge line of credit to finance a significant energy infrastructure project.

- Served as U.S. Counsel to a syndicate of international lenders in connection with a $115 million term loan to a leading North American aquaculture enterprise.

- Assisted the developers of a proposed liquefied natural gas (LNG) receiving terminal in Washington County, Maine to reorganize their ownership structure.

- Served as lead counsel to a Maine bank in connection with the commercial financing of the restart of a biomass-fired generating facility.

- Served as lead counsel to a family owned restaurant and off track betting licensee in connection with the sale of these businesses to a major national gaming company for use as a casino site.

- Served as lead negotiator for a Maine utility in Regional Transmission Organization negotiations.
