Michael Arlen’s Summary

* Visionary
* Rainmaker
* Creative Catalyst
* Non-Threatening Change Agent
* Motivational Leader

I was a pioneer in the sales automation, desktop publishing and e-commerce revolutions. Early adoption of those technologies empowered us to do much more with far less; and generously rewarded us. I have 20+ years of Advertising, Marketing and Sales experience; and have enjoyed an enviable track record for hiring, developing, motivating and retaining high performance, cross-functional teams in fast-paced environments.

I have a law degree and extensive HR and Operations experience. I sold my International Ad Agency in October of 1999, but retained control of Arlen Medical Education Products, which designed and sold materials that taught health care professionals how to treat heart attacks and other medical emergencies.

After suffering a freak heart attack of my own, I decided to change my lifestyle and the focus of my work to that of helping the general public become more healthy and avoid heart attacks (cancer and other degenerative diseases) altogether.

I’m now in the process of launching a functional foods and beverage company.
