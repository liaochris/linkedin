    May 2009 — Present (4 months)

    Online Personal Reputation Management.

    By now, most of us will have entered our names into Google to see what is returned by the world’s biggest search engine. Again, for most of us, what appears (if anything) is as a result of “chance” or of completely unmanaged events.

    De Leon works with its clients to create a “Google Shadow” (also known as “digital presence”, “Google Profile”, “Google Image”) that reflects how they wish to be perceived.

    It is an old adage which says that “first impressions count” but increasingly, the actual first impression that we create is the one that “searchers” receive when they search our names on Google – often prior to meeting us in person.

    De Leon’s PR staff, copy writers and search engine optimisation staff will work on a client’s “profile” and ensure that valuable information and insight about the client is easily found by the search engines to give the searcher useful, in depth information about the client, his career, his company, his role, his achievements, etc.
