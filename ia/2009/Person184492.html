Michael Perry’s Summary

My Dream Job

To build national awareness of and support for Veterans and their families, to secure preferred employment opportunities; encourage veterans to utilize their extraordinary team skills, personal values and capabilities to collaborate by way of joint ventures and alliances to pursue Federal and State set aside contracts and foster Vet helping Vet programs to address the personal, social, and professional issues of today.

My first activities involved creating the Military Network and the Military Network - Businesses on LinkedIn and Ecademy in February of 2008 to help those transitioning out of the military and our veterans in finding secure employment.

The second stage of my plan, now in the initial stages of operation, is finding sources of capital and expertise for those who wish to own their own businesses – to find a way to mentor them to ownership and wealth for their families and generations to come.

In October 2009, I established the Military Network Limited Liability Company in Washington, to extend my services, with the continuing support of members and partner groups, to the broader veteran community with the intention of reducing the devastating impact of the economic crisis on our membership.

Our mission is to lead a Veteran-driven economic recovery by bringing together the national Veteran community to focus efforts, resources and support for Veteran-owned businesses and preferred employment opportunities for Veterans and their families.

The third stage of my plan has already begun. It is to unite vets; as a group, to help one another succeed – to use the force of their will, the building blocks of their fortitude, the expertise in their midst and the wealth at their command.
