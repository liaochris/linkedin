Brian Mays’s Summary

I have worked in a number of different graphic design fields over the last 10 years, including screenprinting, newspapers, and web design. Most of my experience comes from the web design field, but my original background and training are in the print field. This has allowed me to successfully bridge the gap between the print and web fields.

Lately my passions and goals in the design field have gravitated more to the conceptual and creative and less to the technological. I'm more excited about the big idea than the latest software. I've also begun to do more training and educating and would like to continue to do that more.

Brian Mays’s Specialties:

I'm experienced at using Photoshop, Freehand, Indesign, and Flash. I'm able to think creatively with these programs and come up with ideas that are outside the box to accomplish a certain task.
