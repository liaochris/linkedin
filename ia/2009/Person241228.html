Valerie Jacobs’s Summary

I’m Valerie of Valsjewels.com

I’m not one of these hi-pressure internet pitch men, I’m a real person, running my own website business from California. 

My background was in the corporate world, having worked with computers since UNIVAC was "THE" mainframe.  I’ve been a keypunch operator, payroll supervisor, full charge Bookkeeper and paralegal.  I became a Windows specialist and by necessity, learned programming language and other key technical stuff.  From then on, I just had to keep training and tweaking until I understood it.  

I’m an admitted user-geek who knows a little bit about an awful lot, and unlike many users I enjoy figuring these things out, or shall I say I am driven to get the answer.

So, I started my own website a little less than two years ago, and am now an Amazon merchant selling their products along with the stuff on my website and making a commission too!  I set up iGoogle, LinkedIn, MySpace, Blogger, WordPress, Facebook and Facebook Fan pages, linked Facebook pages to Twitter and my websites, now getting RSS feeds on everything I post.  I look for anyway to get the Internet Shoppers’ attention.  Boggled?  Don’t be!

Over the years I have learned so much the hard way, I came up with a way to share the information I’ve gathered so you can benefit from all the work I’ve done to get me here . . .
