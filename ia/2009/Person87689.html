Jan van der Meer’s Summary

According to Elsevier Jan van der Meer is a real video Guru. Helping prosumers and professionals in creating better productions and giving tips and information about latest techniques, camera's and gadgets.

Jan is the Founder of Global-DVC
(Global Digital Videographers Centre).
Based in Zandvoort (Amsterdam Beach) Holland.

Video Director Producer, Camera Consultant, trainer and editor for video-bulletins, magazins and newspapers.
Recommendations on this page will proof that DVC members appreciate his information and tips.

Jan trained over 10.000 prosumers and professionals in the practical and critical use of video camera’s and latest photo camera’s with access to HD video.
He trained reporters/journalists from TV and newspapers, staff members of the big camera brand names and many professional freelance video producers.

Together with his co-producer wife Lidy, they also organize yearly worldwide trips for videographers to scenic locations.
Favourite destination are wildlife parks in South Africa, Namibia, Tanzania, Kenia and Borneo.

Jan is also producing HD stock shots and web clip video productions for Hotels. Reason why he also is a growing expert in Marketing traffic, Affiliate Programs, Twitter and Facebook etc.

Global-DVC is the ideal centre for the
"one (wo)man does it all productions"

Jan van der Meer’s Specialties:

HD Video Productions, camcorder courses, education, schooling, accessories. webclip promotion, photo/video safaris, South-Africa wildlife stockshots producer and teacher, Borneo Proboscis Monkey ambassador, Supporting Masai kids at Technical School Father Joseph Nairobi Kenia.
