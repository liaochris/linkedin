Experience & Goals are good but a creed by which to apply your domain knowledge is even better!

Why Automate?
===========
Automation, fundamentally, is the mechanization of a manual task. Love, education, human interaction should not be automated.

However, there is no evil in taking a manual process & allowing a machine do that which would take a person longer, work a person harder or take the pleasure out of doing a task over & over. However, if by automating something, the existence of life, the values of morality, the sensibilities of one’s existence or the livelihood of those who would otherwise perform the task manually is usurped, than automation has defeated it’s very purpose.

Automation should be carefully conceived so that future generations are not burdened with undoing the damage of unwitting or well intended but misguided endeavors.
