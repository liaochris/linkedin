Richard Edge’s Summary

I'm a co-founder of Keima Ltd, a UK telecommunications consultancy and software company based in Cardiff. Keima. Since January 2006 thousands of next generation mobile base stations have been selected and optimised with our software solutions, saving our clients millions of dollars. See www.keima.co.uk for our website.

My background is in successfully overcoming problems in the wireless communications sector, including technical (developing 3G network planning tools and processes), regulatory (spectrum pricing, negotiating new spectrum bands for mobile) and commercial (launching new software products to global sales teams, achieving short duration payback). I have extensive experience of working in small, rapidly growing companies, and driving growth through innovation and developement of unique capabilities and expertise. I have completed an MPhil in Technology Policy at the Judge Business School of Cambridge University in 2006, gaining a letter of commendation.
