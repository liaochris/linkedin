Ketan Joshi’s Summary

I have 20 years experience in 3 FMCGs - Unilever, Mars and Danone as well as Fayrouz International (part of the Heineken Group) at Innovation / Research and Development Director level responsible for business growth via the introduction of Consumer Driven Innovative Products.

Currently I am active on two fronts as part of my own company - InnOrbit Ltd, which was set up in 2008.

1) Creating a portfolio of Functional Innovative products from Natural Raw Materials from India and Tibet in the areas of Energy, Relaxing, Slimming and Anti Ageing. We have developed Quick Release systems allowing the Active Ingredients to enter the blood quickly - thus having an 'Instant' benefit the consumer demands.

2) Additionally, InnOrbit offers specialist Qualitative Research using ETHNOGRAPHY techniques (Observational non-intrusive in the consumers environment) to observe consumers in the Food and Drinks areas to extract Deep unarticulated CONSUMER INSIGHTS which are then used to develop Consumer Driven Innovative Products or Environments to help a company Grow successfully.


However, I am also looking to return to the Food & Drinks Corporate world should a suitable role be available
