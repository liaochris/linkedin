Lloyd Melnick’s Summary

I have been involved with publishing and licensing computer and video games for over 15 years. As Chief Customer Officer of Merscom, I have built Merscom's licensing and co-production relationships with numerous media companies, including Lifetime, Starz Entertainment/Overture Films, CBS, CAA, Paramount, Showtime, National Geographic and Granada Ventures.

I was responsible for building Merscom’s relationship with Starz Media, which resulted in the first casual downloadable game released concurrently with with a major motion picture release, Righteous Kill starring Robert Deniro and Al Pacino. Righteous Kill generated a 9X ROI for both Merscom and Starz and helped the movie attract a 51% female audience. I also played a key role in the development and launch of Merscom’s number one hit title, Blood Ties, building a relationship with Lifetime Networks, selecting the game’s developer and managing the launch.

I have secured relationships for Merscom with leading portals and aggregators in the U.S., helping Merscom gain access to virtually all casual game customers. In Europe and Asia, Lloyd works with over 100 retail and online portals and distributors in over 30 countries to ensure Merscom’s titles reach customers in every market. I have also developed and implemented Merscom’s retail distribution network both in the U.S. and Europe.
