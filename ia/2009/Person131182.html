Kevin McBride’s Summary

In my role as Solaris/*BSD/Linux/Unix Systems administrator, I have dealt with every phase of systems management and integration, from specification, negotiating purchases, builds and installs, configuration, and ongoing maintenance/upgrades. I've managed large scale/high transaction rate email, DNS/Bind, http/s and Sybase / Oracle systems running on Solaris, HPUX, Linux, and FreeBSD, configured networks, and implemented detailed reporting and monitoring systems.

I excel at complicated trouble-shooting and enjoy teaching others how to troubleshoot issues. When assigned a task or asked by others to assist, I stay engaged until the issue is resolved.

Network Equipment knowledge of Stinger FS DSLAM, CopperEdge 200 DSLAM, Adtran DSLAM, Fujitsu FLM (600,2400), StrataCom BPX and MGX, line encryption devices, PVC, Alcatel 3X50 T1 Multiplexers, Harris remote testing units, Marconi TNX1100/TNX210/ASX1000 ATM Switches, Lucent 5ESS DRM/ASM Module and knowledge of CISCO and Redback router configurations.

Knowledge of Operating Systems/Servers includes most UNIX-“like” systems (FreeBSD, Linux, Solaris, Inferno, Windows XP/2k/2k3/2k8/NT Servers.
