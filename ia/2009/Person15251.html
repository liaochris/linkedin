  • Co-Founder and CEO

    Agave Lab

    (Computer Software industry)

    January 2009 — Present (3 months)

    Nearsourced software design and development.

    After a year of rest and relaxation in Guadalajara, I decided it was time to get back into things before I got soft. I looked around and realized that, due to the unique dynamics of Mexico and Guadalajara in particular, we were surrounded by thousands of talented software development engineers who are eager to work at surprisingly modest wages.

    We started Agave Lab at the beginning of the year to provide high quality software development, at prices that are more in line with today's leaner and meaner market reality. It's paying off already - we're staffed up, working on projects, and ready to rock.

    If you'd like to do more, and better, development for less money, then you need to talk to us.
