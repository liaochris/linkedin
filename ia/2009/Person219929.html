Daniel Stouffer’s Summary

I'm working to remove all barriers to solar generated electricity.

I'm detailing my profile here as a small business owner and as a concerned citizen. I'm here to build relationships and bring my message to all US homeowners. I'm here to promote a solar generated electricity service that homeowners can rent. The system will generate up to 100% of their home's electricity for no upfront investment or long term maintenance & operational costs. A simple solution to all of the barriers once limiting access to clean, green solar energy.

My Goal - To reach out to people who have more wisdom than I to help me on my quest.

My Mindset - We can all make a difference against Global Warming. Finally, it is simple to do the right thing. By switching your home to solar power, you become part of the solution and you stop being part of the problem. I have such a great message to tell, a service that will help us all live in an environmentally conscious way.

My Misery - So few are actually listening to the criticality of Global Warming! Carbon dioxide and other air pollution that is collecting in the atmosphere like a thickening blanket, trapping the sun's heat and causing the planet to warm up. We must curb global warming emissions now. Average U.S. temperatures could be 3 to 5 degrees higher by the end of the century.

How can we help cut pollution and slow down global warming?

It's simple: By reducing pollution from vehicles and coal-fired power plants. We can produce cleaner energy. We can choose to use renewable energy. We can Rethink Solar.

My Mission - To make a big impact on pollution and global warming by making clean, green, renewable solar powered electricity available to American homeowners. To build the largest, most successful Ecopreneur team promoting the rental of solar power equipment. How can we "Make this Happen"? I'm wondering if some of you will join me and become leaders in the our transition to a reliable and renewable energy future.
