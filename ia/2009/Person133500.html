Karl Monger’s Summary

Ten years experience leading and training combat soldiers in the US Army, including three years as a personnel and planning officer in the 1st Ranger Battalion. Post-military, successful sales representative for Case construction equipment dealer, branch manager for United Rentals, sales manager for Caterpillar dealership. Currently sales/marketing manager defacto general manager for heavy construction crane rentals. Advise owner on business opportunities and manage rental fleet. Two years experience running local non-profit Big Brothers mentoring agency.

Local community involvement includes former member board of trustees for Wichita Chamber of Commerce Leadership Wichita program, former chairman of the board of deacons for Eastminster Presbyterian Church. While at Big Brothers conducted largest single event fundraiser in Wichita history, generating $500,000 in pledges at a one hour event. Former board member and finance chair of the Sedgwick County Regional Prevention Center. Currently volunteering withe the Wounded Warrior Project and the VA hospital.

National leadership includes former central region director/board of directors and webmaster of the US Army Ranger Association - veteran non-profit organization.

Karl Monger’s Specialties:

Purchasing, fleet management and utilization, sales force leadership, non-profit leadership and fund-raising, web design.
