Todd Gross’s Summary

Twenty years of automotive experience in a variety of leadership responsibilities has provided me with insights into how business truly functions. Few individuals have been given the opportunity to explore all aspects of the business as I have, from developing next generation products to establishing long term commercial agreements. This insight allows me to explore untapped assets, waste reduction, and process deficiencies by looking across traditional boundaries (of engineering, manufacturing, purchasing, and sales) in unique and innovative ways.
