Charlotte Norman’s Summary

As a Candidate Manager I am responsible for generating candidates within the Secretarial and Support Division.

My job is to provide the consultants with experienced temporary and permanent support staff and to ensure that their experiences and role requirements are common knowledge so that when our clients release a job, we can effectively match our candidates to the role.

We cover financial services, commerce and industry and legal services on the secretarial and support desks.
