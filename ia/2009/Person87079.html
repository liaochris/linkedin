Giridhar Nayak’s Summary

Giridhar (Giri) Nayak is taking a well-deserved break after working with SunGard System Access for the past 13 years (System Access was acquired by SunGard in Q42006).

Prior to the break, as Vice President of Product Development of the SunGard Banking product, Giri fulfilled the role of the CTO and was responsible for product engineering and R&D of their SOA Core Banking product - Customer Service Manager (CSM). Along the way, Giri has specialised in developing integration architecture for Banks with needs covering innovation for their mutli-channel banking and retail banking with branch rollout with a focus on customer centricity achieved through operational CRM and transaction management.

In addition, to managing a team of about 100 development staff in his last role, Giri pioneered the evolution of 'customer centric SOA+BPM enabled Banking Platform' from conceptualization to development over the span of four years from 2004 onwards. In between these four years, he was in the role of Solution Director with the responsibility of ensuring the sales of the product.

In his previous roles, he was the mainstay beyond the scalability of SYMBOLS, the Universal Banking Solution of System Access, through multiple Benchmarks at innovation/lab centres of IBM & HP. During this time, he had a primary role in grooming the Java development team while managing the Technology R&D.

In his initial years, he had joined System Access as a programmer and worked on several initiatives of which some were TP Monitor/BEA Tuxedo, communication/network programming, and Retail Banking application development.

Giri received his B.Engg with Honors in Comp. Science from the University of Mysore, India. He is involved in social work for the under-privileged and special children in & around his native place in India. Giri and his wife stay with their two sons in Singapore.
