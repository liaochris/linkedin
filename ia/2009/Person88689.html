Gayla Schaefer’s Summary

Editor, writer, columnist, paid blogger and photographer with six years experience in diverse markets. Before becoming a contract writer, I worked in the nonprofit sector doing marketing, pr, development, event planning and facility management for a college and a large tourist attraction. I have extensive experience in the areas of market research, tourism promotion, eco-tourism, higher education administration, and creative nonprofit marketing and development campaigns relying heavily on public and media relations strategy and partnership development.

My professional goal is to work with a proactive organization that can benefit from my mix of entrepreneurial spirit and love for nonprofit teamwork.

Gayla Schaefer’s Specialties:

I have experience writing for business, women's issues, health news, interior design, tourism, parenting, surfing, community news and blogs/websites.

I also have experience in marketing, media/community/government relations, event planning, development, crisis communications strategy, strategic planning, political campaign management, higher education management and non-credit course programming.
