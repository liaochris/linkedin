Diana Epstein’s Summary

Diana Epstein is the Director of Research at Boyden New York. Her responsibilities range from working on industry research and candidate development to assisting the firm’s Search Consultants in placing highly qualified senior executives in a broad range of industries.

Ms. Epstein works closely with all the members of the Boyden research and recruiting team during the course of a search assignment. She partners with the Consultants to develop and implement sourcing tactics.

Elected in 2008 to be the Research liaison for the Boyden Research Network, Ms. Epstein assists with on-boarding new Research Associates in the Boyden Americas offices. She is also a key contact person between the researchers in the U.S.

Ms. Epstein is an active member of the New York Women in Communications, Inc. association. Recently, Ms.Epstein was awarded the AIRS certification as a Certified Internet Researcher. Ms. Epstein graduated from Boston University where she received a BS in Journalism and a minor in Art History. She speaks fluent French and is a citizen of the U.S and European Union.

Boyden is a global leader in the executive search industry with 79 offices in 43 countries. Founded in 1946, Boyden specializes in high level executive search, interim management and human capital consulting across a broad spectrum of industries
