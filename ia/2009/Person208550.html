Bob Schwartz’s Summary

Bob is the owner of Brokerforyou, and is not only a Broker but has also attained the Certified Residential Specialist designation. This is the highest national professional designation a residential Realtor can achieve. The CRS designation is really quite an honor; only 4% of all the Realtors in the nation can put these initials after their name.

Bob attributes his success to his specialization within the diverse real estate field as well as his very aggressive property marketing techniques. " My marketing plan continues to allow me to list properties for just 90 days and typically achieve top dollar for my client!"
