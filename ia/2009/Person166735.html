Mark Leaman’s Summary

Dynamic, creative professional with strong skill sets in design, digital media and project management. Creative problem solver, and extremely self-reliant. Not adverse to overcoming the challenges of a unique project, but at the same time recognizes the importance of task management and team work. 20 years of professional experience provides a knowledge set suitable to accomplish a wide array of digital media and web design missions.

Most recently: Project Manager and Media Producer for Bowdoin College's iTunes U site in Apple's iTunes Music Store as well as the development and production of podcasts. This includes the creation of web media portals that are now in Bowdoin's top ten most visited entry points for web content.

Mark Leaman’s Specialties:

Web development with Macromedia Director, Shockwave, Dreamweaver, Adobe GoLive, HTML, CSS, RSS, XML; design and prepress with Adobe Creative Suite and Quark XPress; 3D animation; video production and visual effects creation with Combustion, Final Cut Studio and Shake; professional audio production with SoundTrack Pro and Garageband; web media and podcast production, Apple iTunes Music Store; content mangement systems including Hannon Hill Cascade Server, Joomla, Moveable Type, Wordpress, Drupal.
