Larry Mueller’s Summary

I worked in sales, marketing, manufacturing and accounting for both Fortune Five Hundred companies and small closely held businesses. Since 1997 I have worked as a CPA doing accounting and tax preparation for individuals, sole proprietors, partnerships, LLCs. S and C corporations. I have worked with QuickBooks since 1995 setting up, training and assisting a variety of professional and service providers, consultants, small retail, manufacturing and wholesale businesses.
