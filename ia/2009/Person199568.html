Robert Dunn’s Summary

If you are an early-stage company, I can help. I have competence and experience in the following areas: HR; financial reporting and planning; operational planning; taxation; facility management; investor relations and debt financing; review and implementation of key governing agreements such as license agreements, stock option plans, and SPAs. I have a solid knowledge of business, securities, and IP law, as well as Indian corporate law. I have reviewed and negotiated license agreements with many of the world's largest technology companies. I have been involved in angel, Series A, Series B and bridge financing rounds. I have been through due diligence with several VC firms, strategic investors, venture debt firms and acquiring firms.
