Bruce Sinclair’s Summary

Bruce is an internationally recognized Creative Director and Art Director with over 15 years experience (predominantly in digital). Working for brands like Sony Playstation, Nike, General Motors, and Lego at agencies like DDB, MacLaren McCann, and Blast Radius. He's been featured and quoted in numerous publications, earned over 70 international awards, and judged shows like Cannes Lions, London International, Applied Arts, and most recently as Co-Chair for the 2007 Digital Marketing Awards.
Additionally, with much of Bruce's time spent mentoring, his creative teams have produced 3 different 'young creative' winners at the National Advertising Awards.
Bruce was born and raised in the Toronto area, originally trained in classical animation at Sheridan College in Oakville. He and his family moved to the west coast in 2002 where he led Tribal DDB Canada to become Marketing's Digital Agency of the Year 2005.

Bruce Sinclair’s Specialties:

Interactive, film, print, radio, design, animation, and photography.
