Hartmut Rast’s Summary

Professional in all aspects of the real estate business and the financial sector with sound knowledge about locations, projects, costs and feasibility and the mobilisation of earnings reserves.

Used to work within international operating units as well as being responsible for staff and leading teams of all kind (employees, project participants, builders etc.) and with a well-established track record.

In the function as Director with the Deutsche Bank Trust AG (P/L) having successfully developed and established an overall concept for real estate consultancy service of Deutsche Bank AG (P/L)'s Private Banking division, advising wealthy clients and so familiar with strategic thinking and all involved processes.

Experienced in start-up's and therefore having out-sourced the real estate property of Deutsche Bank AG within the DEBEKO Immobilien GmbH as their real estate company and as a professional developer.

Hartmut Rast’s Specialties:

Optimising real estate portfolios by drawing up utilisation concepts for new and converted properties including profitability analyses, re-zoning concepts and site improvements. Consultant of institutional investors and wealthy clients.
