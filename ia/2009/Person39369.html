Cathy Inness’s Summary

I sell fun and pampering. I have the best job in the world selling vacations. I match people with the right cruise or tour and work at getting them the best prices in the market. I love what I do. I do all your paper work for homeland security and make sure you have the correct travel documents. I am looking to grow the agency by making each trip a memorable one!
