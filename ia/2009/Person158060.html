Michelle McKenzie’s Summary

Michelle McKenzie has over 20 years in the publishing industry, working as an editor and writer, publisher, project manager, buyer, and published author.

Michelle also has more than 30 years in retail management, buying, merchandising, business analysis, store plannings (for new stores), just-in-time inventory and product development.
