  • President

    Matrix Integrated Marketing

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    May 2009 — Present (1 month)

    Three current and gargantuan market forces have led to the creation of Matrix Integrated Marketing (MIM). The first is the sputtering economy, the second is the proliferation of home based communication technology, and the third is the acceptance by both employee and employer of a virtual working relationship. MIM contracts with firms that desire MBA quality, commission only, virtual professionals. Essentially, what our team will do, is analyze the company as well as the industry seeking to build a virtual employment force. Mine the appropriate lead data and develop projections along a specific timeline. Seamlessly build, train, integrate, automate, and manage the virtual employment force. Track and report the results of the campaign over a period of time. The virtual integration philosophy is a low pressure, high value approach.Our competitive advantages & timing components are critical. Data – we have access to 1.13 million companies currently utilizing Google Adwords in an effort to build their businesses. Average CPC, Daily Spend, Ad Count, and estimated ROI per Ad is also available (95% of these companies are failing to return a positive ROI). Human Capital – Access to 1000 qualified new job seekers per day (both active and passive) searching for employment. Commission Only / Risk Free Model – The vast majority of our virtual teams are commission only or performance based, there is little risk involved for firms to contract with us. Slumping Economy – highly qualified candidates are now considering telecommuting, remote, commission only positions as serious career alternatives.Technology – over ninety percent of our qualified U.S. based workforce has access to both an internet connection and a phone (VOIP or traditional phone). Emphasis on Domestic – although “outsourcing” is occasionally used as a four letter word, over 87% of our work force is home grown in the United States.
