Bob McManus’s Summary

You can contact me at Bob.HillCityUSA@Gmail.com.

I was blessed to grow up in a family where I always knew I was loved. Through my years of college, God placed a dream in my heart...To be a Filmmaker. So, I turned my attention to this and went for it. I received a B.S. in TV & Video Production, I attended an N.Y.U. summer film program; I wrote, produced, directed, shot and edited freelance projects; I worked in broadcast TV (WDIO-TV in Duluth, MN & KSTP-TV in Mpls, MN); and I even worked as a grip on the John Hughes' film, "Curly Sue." But, it never really came together. Ultimately, I moved on to other things....always wondering, "What was that all about....."
It wasn't until I became familiar with Vision Forum and their teaching on Filmmaking from a Christian Worldview, as well as, The Truth Project with Dell Tackett, which shows most every other part of life from a Christian Worldview, that the dream began to stir again, deep within my soul.
Now, I believe, the Lord has opened up doors "Out of the blue" for me to once again, fulfill my...His Dream for my life.
I'm currently working on 2 Documentaries: One about the Sturgis Motorcycle Rally, and one about the JOY found in the people of East Africa. Does their JOY come in spite of their "lack," or because of their simple lifestyle and relationships.....

Bob McManus’s Specialties:

Vision, Desire, Passion and Continuity in shooting
