Born and raised in a coastal city in South China,Curt has been in China's TMT industry for 10 years. Following are some of the portfolio companies he is in:

* Jiashen Tongwen Translation: www.tiit.com.cn
* NDI Institution: www.ndi.com.cn

As one of the first generation skateboarders in China, he has also been participating in Chinese skateboarding circuit for over 10 years and still active in the circle.

Curt Shi’s Specialties:

With specialty in corporate communication and financing, currently Curt is sitting on board or working as advisor to a number of start-ups such as eFang, GeneralV, Qian8ao, NDI Education, Society Skateboards, among others, contributing his strength in building a more healthy entrepreneurship culture in China.

Curt is also adept at planning & organizing action sports campaigns and events, specializing in reaching the X-Games and trend-setting youth markets.
