Research Papers

Milestone Projects of the career

1. 5year ICT Policy Framework to setup 500+ well networked small size hospitals across the country with emphasis on data security, auditing and policy.
2. Migration of Ad Management infrastructure for an Ad monitoring agency from Macintosh & Windows to Linux.
3. Development of custom ERP built using MS Platform with modules ranging from attendance to procurement with relevant approval workflows along with audit controls.
4. Design & Development of Automated Fingerprint Identification System (AFIS) SDK for use by National Informatics Center (NIC). The same was integrated with NIC developed PMS (Prison Management System) in Asia's largest prison - Tihar Prison. The same was replicated in Jharkhand & Gujarat on pilot project basis.
5. Development of mMD (Mobile Medical Data) – Palmtop based information delivery application for doctors. Developed using CodeWarrior, C & C++.
6. Hard disk duplicator to make up-to 3 simultaneous copies irrelevant of the operating system and the data residing on the hard disk. Developed using Turbo C
