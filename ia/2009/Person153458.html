Martijn Dood’s Summary

Internet Strategist & Creative Consultant with a wide range of experience in business-to-consumer marketing. Companies I recently worked for are Media Markt, Sanoma, Centraal Beheer Achmea, Vitens, CliniClowns, Menzis, TNT / Cendris, Stop Aids Now, ING, ANP and BP.

I have a background of both Communications (Communication & Management degree specialised in online / one-to-one / interactive marketing) and Technology (web programming (HTML, PHP, MySQL, Flash, etc)).

My interests are social / community interaction, general marketing strategies, interactive marketing and consumer trends.
