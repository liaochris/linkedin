Alessandro Baretta’s Summary

I am an experienced IT & industrial engineer. I am currently seeking to broaden the range of my professional skills in the fields of sales and marketing. For this reason I have accepted a post as a marketing and sales manager at Tagliabene.

I was the IT Manager of a no profit organization running one of the largest privately held radio networks worldwide. Previously I worked as a free-lance industrial engineer, focusing on IT project management, electrical engineering, process automation and mechatronics. As such, in 2007 I have been called by the European Food Safety Authority, an agency of the European Commission, to act as the pro-tempore project manager of the Zoonoses Data Collection project.

I am currently interested in corporate finance and financial markets. For this reason I have engaged the Executive Master in Corporate Finance and Banking at the SDA Bocconi School of Management in Milano (Italia), the most prestigious business school in Italy.
