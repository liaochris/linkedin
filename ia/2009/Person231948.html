Daniel Simonsen’s Summary

I am a 24 year old visionary entrepreneur and CEO of The Simonsen Group which is an independent franchise company committed to helping people start their own business and generate permanent, passive income.

I am the co-owner of PeakPerformanceSupply.com, an online retail store marketing the highest quality Health, Beauty, and Home-care products. My primary goal is to promote optimal health through exercise, nutrition, and lifestyle modification.

I participate as a premier member of World Wide Dreambuilders LLC, which provides multi-million dollar business support. Through my connections to multiple, successful entrepreneurs, I am committed to preserving American free-enterprise.

Within sports, I have competed as a swimmer at the NCAA Division 1 and Olympic levels, and still am an active triathlete.

I enjoy meeting new people, especially in the sports industry. I started The Simonsen Group because I passionately want to help others achieve their goals, and I am always open to building quality relationships.

My goal, through The Simonsen Group and along with World Wide Dreambuilders, is to build a team of ambitious, self-motivated, and enthusiastic men & women who are committed to taking control of their lives and achieving freedom.
