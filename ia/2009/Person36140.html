Byrne Hobart’s Summary

I am a recruiter and researcher working at Hunter Green, an independent staffing firm. I recruit for IT and very senior financial positions, and help build tools to make the recruiting process easier.

I am also interning with a web design and online marketing firm (bluefountainmedia.com) where I contribute to their online marketing efforts and write a blog on tax and personal finance issues.

In my spare time, I invest. I try to find overlooked value (small- to micro-cap stocks with great assets and decent cash flow) and cheap call options on radical change.

I'm also working on finishing my degree.
