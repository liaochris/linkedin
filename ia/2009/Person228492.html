Chris Tait’s Summary

I continue to build on my 11-plus years of experience in the creative industry and my foundation established during my early years in the newspaper minor leagues. In high school, college and now in the business world — I strive to explore increasingly effective ways of blending technology and communication.

From spearheading and upgrading the Romeo High School journalism computer lab, helping maintain and troubleshoot the Oakland Post newspaper Apple network, to undertaking the roles and responsibilities of being the lead IS/IT person at the Turgeon Group marketing and communications agency — I’ve always looked to improve the tools my colleagues and I required to focus on the creative process.

While my original career path started me toward sports journalism, my interests and connections led me into the varied multiverse that is the creative trade. It’s there I’ve decided to stake my claim.
