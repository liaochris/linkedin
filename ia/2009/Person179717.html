Marco Ottolini’s Summary

I’m an ex-entrepreneur. I started my first company when I was 23. Over the years I have come to the conclusion that working as a manager could better allow me to use my talents as a leader and "visionary", while still keeping a careful watch on expenses and development of the company.
After a sabbatical, I would like to lead again a company whose goal is ambitious and challenging at the same time.

Marco Ottolini’s Specialties:

I begun my career as a software developer and a technical journalist. I managed companies of different sizes and now I'm a senior executive with experiences in the media, online, travel and IT sectors.
I created companies from scratch, I found funding for them, I managed the delicate start-up phase, I led technical, marketing and sales team, I even restructured, but I like to think that I've been a very good manager of talents I was able to exploit.
