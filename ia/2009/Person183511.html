Paulo Eduardo Dias Garcia’s Summary

Multidisciplinary qualification, result of experience in businesses development and in designs, works and projects implantation in the areas of consultative engineering and civil, industrial and heavy construction.

Generalist profile in view of performing in positions of works coordinator (7 years), projects implementation manager (9 years), studies and designs coordinator (4 years), development business manager, being commercial (8 years) and contracts administrator (8 years) and independent consultant (4 years) in projects, contracts and businesses management.

Activities rendered for several sectors of the infrastructure, in Brazil (São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro and Minas Gerais) and Abroad (Argentina and Portugal), such as supply, buildings and popular habitations (housing), power, sports and leisure, base and transformation industries, basic and environmental sanitation and transports in the subway, air, railroad and road modes.

Actuation in companies of consultative engineering (Etel, Hidroservice, Geotécnica, Jaakko Poyry and Etep), engineering special services (Sondotécnica and Griffin Drenasa), constructors (Nogueira Baptista, Beter, Adolpho Lindemberg and Jaú) and contractors (Serveng Civilsan, Ecisa, Ecel, São Luiz and MPE).

Services with customers of the public area (CDHU, DER SP, PMSP, Cedae, Sabesp, Metrô RJ, AEEA Argentina, Ministry of Finances and Plan /Portugal and Eletrobrás) and the private area (SPFC, Dynapac, Cosipa, Engefer, Cobrasma, Açominas and CSN), where developed expertise for the full exercise of the functions in units of production, technique, commercial, administrative and financial.
