Sabrina Herring’s Summary

During a career immersed in technology industries, I have been fortunate to have had the opportunity to acquire diverse functional expertise in both service and product-based businesses. Currently, as Director of Administration for Magnetic, I am privileged to lead a team responsible for the financial, human resources, back-office infrastructure and operations of Magnetic and affiliated companies. As a member of the Management Team my role includes collaboration in the strategic business planning and implementation processes.

Prior to my position with Magnetic, I most recently provided business consulting to a suite of clients ranging from sole proprietorships to multiple-entity structured service and inventory-based organizations. The consulting services included accounting, financial analysis, operational and organizational development.

Prior to providing business consulting, I served as Director of Operations, and later Vice President, of a niche telephony manufacturing and distribution company. I was responsible for coordination of the off-shore manufacturing process for timely inventory imports, North American distribution, sales and internal operations of the company.m

My career began in the technology training industry shortly after its emergence as a recognized business necessity through the industry's most profitable years. During my time in the technology training industry I gained valuable experience in sales, project management, and training working with clients such as Publix, Franklin Templeton, Raymond James and TECO.
