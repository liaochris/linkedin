Peter Gordon’s Summary

Online Banking Expert - Developed next generation payment approaches using the Web, Phone and Wireless including Account to Account (A2A) Transfers, Electronic Bill Pay and Presentment (EBPP) solutions for Venture Backed Firm. Provided strategy consulting for Two-Factor Authentication, FFIEC guidance and Online Identify Theft Best Practices.

Entrepreneur – Proven track record for stimulating new business growth, increasing revenues, and profits. Managed two consulting organizations from start-up to $10 million practices. Technical Solution Architect – Implemented package software application solutions to meet business objectives using e-Commerce, Client-Server and Mainframe architectures. Software package knowledge includes Siebel, Vantive/PeopleSoft, MarketSoft, MarketFirst, Ventaso, Cisco IP Telephony Solutions, Interactive Intelligence, Kana, IBM, Oracle and Microsoft products.

Outstanding Communicator and Leader – Recognized for skills at facilitating meetings, preparing and delivering presentations. Proven ability to build high performance teams. Recognized speaker on CRM and technology topics.
