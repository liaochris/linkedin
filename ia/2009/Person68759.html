Dylan Hunter’s Summary

17 yrs of Marketing, Business Development and General Management experience in Consumer Internet - Search, eCommerce and Online Media; High Motivator; Psycho-Quant. Have headed marketing & created multi-million user populations 5 times in the past 10 yrs. The How is no longer the challenge – what is, is the Why.

Served as Chief Marketing Officer of SecondRotation / Gazelle.com, a startup I re-oriented from purely cash-oriented to being values-led focusing on using the power of business in the fight against eWaste, resulting in growth from 0-100k monthly uniques in less than 5 mths, and landed $2.5M in new funding. Currently heading Marketing / Analytics Consultancy for values-led companies.

Focus is on using the power of business for social good. If you've come to the point in your life where you're motivated by more than just making your wallet fatter, (which really is the easy part) send me a ping and we'll figure out how to get that hope realized one way or another.

Check out my blog - http://dylanhunter.com - or contact me @ dylan AT dylanhunter DOT com - for more.
