Kate Nevins’s Summary

Enthusiastic Creative Product Designer who has accumulated over twenty years of experience in various creative and business fields both personally and professionally. Experienced in designing innovative products such as giftware, ceramics, toys, children’s wearable, jewelry, accessories as well as book illustrations. Managed freelancers and collaborating with product development teams to bring new designs into production, on schedule, and within budget. A self motivated professional who has excellent communication and problem solving skills, is very organized and takes initiative in following through on all assignments. A designer who upholds a high degree of aesthetic value on her designs a number which have been featured in a few major magazines: Cosmopolitan, Teen People, Woman’s Day and Parents Magazine. Proven ability to assume increased responsibilities; is detail-oriented and understands priorities. A team player who is able to multitask, enjoys a fast paced environment and maintains a high level of work ethic that has been expressly appreciated by both Management and peers.
