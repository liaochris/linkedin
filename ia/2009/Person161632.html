Michael Lashford’s Summary

I was raised in East Texas and educated in West Texas at Texas Tech University where I went through a great curriculum with amazing professors. After several internships I got my first job as a Junior Designer at SullivanPerkins in Dallas. I was thrown in the deep end and quickly learned to swim under the guidance of a couple great mentors. After almost three years working I moved to San Francisco where I worked a few great contract jobs until getting hired at Loomis Group, an integrated agency. I have been freelance designing the whole time as well. I find that after the years I've been in this business, what I require is simple, but not always easily found. I want to design for people who care about design.

Michael Lashford’s Specialties:
