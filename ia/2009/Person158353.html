Massimo D'Onofrio’s Summary

Human Resources has evolved from a traditional personnel function to an increasingly vital component in business operations and revenue growth, and successful HR Directors have recognized this change. We have all come to value the strength of a workforce and its influence over a company’s success.

To meet the challenge of this shift, I have managed HR organizations that are innovative, results-driven, and tied directly to bottom-line performance. These programs have ranged from specific business reengineering initiatives to corporate-wide recruitment, staffing, and leadership development programs. In turn, financial results have improved and stability has been enhanced.

I also have extremely strong generalist qualifications in all core HR functions with particular strengths in organizational development, employee relations, benefits and compensation, performance management, and staffing. Furthermore, my background in business consulting has given me the skills necessary to align HR functions to critical business needs.

I offer 10+ years of consulting and HR experience, an Executive MBA degree, the ability to develop productive relationships with internal and external contacts, outstanding skills in major European languages (Italian, French, Spanish, German, and English), and the ability to lead diverse international teams.

At this point in my career, I have looking for an opportunity to join a high-growth company in a senior or partner-level position. I am particularly suited to HR Manager/Director, General Manager, and Administrative Director positions.
