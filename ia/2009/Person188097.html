Antonio Pintus’s Summary

Position: Expert Software Engineer in the NDA area of CRS4.
Education: Master's degree in Computer Science Technologies, University of Cagliari. PhD student (in progress) in Computer Science, University of Cagliari.

I'm a member of NDA (Network Distributed Applications) group of CRS4 since 2000 and I contribute to R&D national and EC funded projects.
My main activities and responsibilities are in engineering, designing and implementing network distributed, platform-independent, software systems, writing of technical reports and scientific papers. The domains of activities are focused on distributed complex software architectures and Service-oriented Architecture (SOA). My interests and skills are in distributed systems, P2P networks and Web Services.
Since year 2000, I've (mainly) adopted Java programming language and platform in my projects.
I'm also member of JUG-Sardegna (Java User Group Sardegna) and I'm actively involved in Java community events organization, workshops and talks. I'm co-author of scientific publications in journals and I presented and co-presented scientific papers in international conferences.

Antonio Pintus’s Specialties:

Software Engineering. OOP and UML design. Experience on Java platform since year 2000, XML. Testing. Documentation. Web Services and SOA. Distributed Software Systems and applications
