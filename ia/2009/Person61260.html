Jan Pieter De Lugt’s Experience

  • Chief Commercial Officer

    TEN|Consortium Holding BV

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    December 2008 — Present (8 months)

    With its Simplified Thorium Gas-cooled Pebble-bed Reactor (STGR), TEN | Consortium is committed to contribute to the spectrum of a new generation of 'state-of-the-art' energy solutions.

    Our Simplified Thorium Gas-cooled Pebble-bed Reactor (STGR) is an intrinsic save (IAEA), modular, multi-purpose, concept energy station of 40 Megawatt Electric Power output (MWe) and 100 Megawatt Thermal Power output (MWth). Increase in output is possible by adding additional modular units i.e. 80 MWe is possible with (2 x 40MWe)). The STGR can provide quality power to digital networks and to local power grids (40 MWe unit provides enough power for a town with about 40.000 families and industry). Moreover, each 40 MWe unit produces 10 million liter fresh drinking water (from sea or river) and is capable to irradiate food (hygiene and preservation) as important side products.

    Standard features of our STGR:

    - Power Generation (40 MWe modular units);
    - Heat Generation for Process Industry- and residential use (100 MWth; Co-Generation)
    - Potable water production through Flash Desalination (10 Million liter per day / 40 MWe unit);
    - Food Irradiation (Preservation technique to reduce both post-harvest food losses and dependence on chemical preservatives) to create cleaner food-chains).

    Additional features:

    - Medical sterilization technologies;
    - Climate control systems;
    - Energy reduction technology (Aluminium Industry);
    - Maritime Propulsion engineering;
    - Energy Transition Carrier (Hydrogen fuel);
    - Steam exploration systems (Oil & Gas);
    - Barge mounted concept (Plug & Play).

  • Founder

    Sino-Europe Entrepreneurs Group

    (Privately Held; International Trade and Development industry)

    December 2005 — Present (3 years 8 months)

    Sino-Europe Entrepreneurs Group is a platform concept of building bridges between Europe and China by matching up the requirements of European and Chinese businesses.

    The Sino-Europe Entrepreneurs Group’s (SEEG) main goal is to introduce Chinese enterprises to the European market therefore profiting from the full branding opportunities. On the other hand SEEG will introduce international sourcing clients to their Chinese corporate client's enterprises. We strive to initiate bi-lateral co-operation between our corporate clients and foreign partners in order to create interdependence and sales & distribution channels of complimentary products or services.

    Other activities include the active support of Governmental Institutions in setting up bilateral co-operation ties or attracting foreign direct investments in order to develop mutual business opportunities. The Sino-Europe Entrepreneurs Group serves the role of a consultant, lobbyist, business partner allocator, trouble shooter and local supporter. We provide a full service package that covers greenfield project set-up up to business launches and Market Entry Strategy in the profit and non-profit sector:

    - Provide key economic and government information and data;
    - Advise on the alternative and appropriate type of business organization (Rep. Office, JV, WFOE, Branche Office, etc.;
    - Assist in negotiations with Chinese and Dutch partner(s) and/or government agencies;
    - Conduct market and feasibility studies;
    - Source suppliers for export/import commodities from/into China;
    - Find importers and distributors inside China or the EU.
