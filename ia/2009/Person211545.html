Scott Harris, MBA’s Summary

I have been working as the Director of Admissions at Leadership Institute of Seattle (LIOS) since April 1st, 2005 and have never experienced a workplace culture like this before.

In my four years as Admissions Director I have always met or exceeded my enrollment goals. The Fall of 2008 entering class was very challenging as LIOS was not legally allowed to advertise or recruit for new students for ten (10) months until our new affiliation with Saybrook Graduate School became official. I was finally able to proceed with admissions activities in late August, 2008 and still managed to meet my enrollment objectives for the fall semester.

I am very proud of my association with LIOS and believe that LIOS will have a long and fulfilling future.
