Adrian Bevan’s Summary

The Revelations Group is my own business and was created to help individuals, corporations and businesses of any size to understand there place in this world.
FOR INDIVIDUALS: I focus on assiting individuals though Career and life advice including creating a CV with a difference, Selecting recruitment agencies to work with, identifying companies that you want to work for and how to get an interview with them. I also support individuals to identify what they are looking for in there Life, Business and Career goals. I am a beliver in NLP, Manifestion and goal setting and I can work with you to develop a plan that suits your indivdual needs.
FOR COMPANIES: I focus on supporting them through developing recruitment strategies, implementing workforce planning and recruitment processes that source them the best pool of candidates available, this includes advice on marketing, business process, advertising and agency selection. More importantly I support companies with developing a culture that leads to minimal staff turnover. I am a big beliver in the retention or retraining of staff is always a better preference than replacement.

I enjoy public speaking and networking and have spoken on a range of topics for audiences up to 500. I was put on this earth to help others and am focused on achieving this goal.
