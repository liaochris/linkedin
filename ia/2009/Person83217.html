Fran Mulhern’s Summary

One of the UK's leading games recruiters - just ask any one of the numerous games specific recruitment companies who have tried to headhunt me. A proven track record of working with some of the world's leading names in games development, from a mixture of passive recruitment through to active and targetted search and selection. I target a relatively small number of studios because, no matter how many times other agents try to tell you other, you can't be everywhere at once or all things to all people.

For me, the video games industry really is the most awesome industry out there - I love being able to help great studios make great games.

Currently acting mainly for 8-10 studios in the UK.

I can be contacted on 0208 133 2536 or franmulhern@recruit3d.com. Likewise, I can be found on MSN at franmulhern@hotmail.com.
