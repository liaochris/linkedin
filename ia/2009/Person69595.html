Ed H. Smith’s Summary

Ed has broad business experience with particular interests in business development, marketing and sales management. He has worked with both large and small businesses, applying his vision and creativity to every aspect of his work.

He conceived a business concept 17 years ago and turned it into a successful business that continues today. He envisioned a revolutionary solution to the inconvenience and lack of security in identity management. This resulted in a voice biometric identity solution that is unparalleled anywhere.

Ed H. Smith’s Specialties:

Business development, strategic alliances, and consulting. Identity risk mitigation, including identity authentication, identity-theft, biometrics, voice verification, speech authentication, voice authentication, fraud detection, collections, and investigations. Compliance: USA PATRIOT, FCC's CPNI Rule, and FDIC's Red Flag Rules.
