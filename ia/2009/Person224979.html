Steve Tansley’s Summary

Steve is a cartoonist, illustrator and graphic designer and has been in demand for more than 15 years. His work continues to be published in newspapers and magazines. He works full time at Studio Productions in Tempe Arizona as their Graphic Design Manager and Illustrator.

He is the co-creator of the cartoon Goodfences with Bill Goodykoontz a bi-weekly cartoon found at www.goodfences.com

In addition, He is the lead illustrator for illuminating children's books and environments at ArchitectureKids.

His continuing goal is to evolve as an illustrator and help provide opportunities that illuminate ideas which excite and inspire.

Steve Tansley’s Specialties:

Intense creativity, lush illustration, rich punchy cartoons, considerate and well-thought graphic design, art direction and project management, strong interpersonal communication, strong management and administrative experience, digital and traditional design and illustration
