Luke Grange’s Summary

My Goal is to influence the business world as to the benefits of the New Economy and Web 2.0 by training, discussion groups and networking. It is only by embracing the concepts outlined in the Book by Don Tapscott and Anthony D Williams, “Wikinomics: How mass collaboration changes everything”, that modern businesses can ride the rising tide of open architectures and principals. It is time to embrace this change and promote peer collaboration and production utilising all the platforms which are now available and being developed.

I have been heading up Knowledge Solutions for over two years and together with our team of collaborative consultants we have helped clients to improve their organisations and lives by introducing them to the concepts of Software as a Service and Cloud Computing in Finance, CRM, Project Management and Business Performance Management. Our clients are diverse in nature and location but this has not stopped us delivering projects and solutions to them via Webinar's, Instant Messaging, Wiki's and Blogs and lets not forget e-Mail just yet.
