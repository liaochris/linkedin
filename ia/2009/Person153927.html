Martin McDermott’s Summary

International Recruitment/ Resourcing Manager with over 9 years experience managing and implementing effective human capital solutions to meet the needs of multi billion dollar companies

The Abu Dhabi Future Energy Company is the government-owned organization mandated to develop and execute the Masdar Initiative by creating the following business units;

Masdar Institute of Science and Technology
MIST is the region's first graduate-level, research driven scientific institution focused on education and research in the core energy and sustainability

Carbon Management Unit
This unit takes the lead in the development of greenhouse gas emission reduction projects, carbon monetisation and emissions trading to help achieve a low-carbon economy in Abu Dhabi and the region.

Industries
The industries unit is establishing world-scale manufacturing facilities in Abu-Dhabi and globally. These technologies currently include Polysilicon and photovoltaic.

Utilities and Asset Management
By investing or forming a joint venture in or alongside global companies that own technology of strategic significance for future energy development inc VC funding in Segway and Halosource and well as a joint venturewith BP to develop the first Hydrogen power plant in the United Arab Emirates.

Property Development Unit (Masdar City)
Masdar City offers a unique opportunity for leading global companies, organizations and individuals to become part of the world's first zero-carbon, zero-waste, city in the heart of Abu Dhabi holding 40,000 residents and 50,000 commuters.
