Karen du Four des Champs’s Summary

Strategic Advisor: From employee relations, organizational development, learning design & blended solutions, to facilitating high stakes meetings & creating long-term strategies to address corporate change. Connects organizational initiatives to strategic business goals. Natural talent for building trust and rapport quickly (counseling background), combined with a judicious manner of asking questions that lead to strategic thinking and problem-solving (legal advocate, HR, OD experience) and a strong desire to see others succeed (management experience). Acknowledged by clients and colleagues for strong organization, innovation and creativity, with an adaptive, goal-oriented work style characterized by high standards, flexibility, and EQ.
