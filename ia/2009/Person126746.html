The 5 of the 10 Points Code of Conduct of 2Bhunt:

1.Listen to the client’s needs
We listen closely to what the client has to say. Based on the needs and objectives we will make a search for the client and provide him with the best possible CV’s. Only by doing so we will be able to successfully deliver his project.
2.Promise what you can
Scan each need of staff and present with suggestions and different points of view to your client. This is more than your client expects.
3.Show your experience
Working at 2Bhunt means that you’re part of a team with years of experience and expertise. Be eager to share your experience (young and older consultants just alike) with our client. Integrate your knowledge in the client’s needs. Be passionate and dedicated.
4.Be available
The client wants to count on you. He has to know that he can call you all the time. When he picks up the phone or sends you a mail he’s the only one you focus your attention on. We at 2Bhunt have always to be available for the client.
5.Not the traditional win-win, at 2Bhunt we call it: client’s success is ours.
You have to contribute to the client’s (and our) success. You can do so when you remember that all his needs of staff (junior or senior employee) has to support and reinforce his business goals. Don’t forget: our client is our publicity agency!!
