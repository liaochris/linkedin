Tyron Coleman’s Summary

I enjoy "Motivating and Training" others. My mission in life is to reach one and teach one daily by my actions. I help businesses “find money” for their growth by connecting them with investors. I help people lower their monthly mortgage payments by finding lenders to fit their needs.

I have given over 150 public presentations to insurance groups, real estate organizations, business groups, colleges, high schools and community outreach organizations. I have sat on insurance and real estate panels as a industry expert with political officials and community leaders.

I am the author of a new book “Jump-Start How To Recharge Your Life.” This book catalogs my business and personal experiences and provides the keys to having a successful and balanced life. Published by Authorhouse Books.
