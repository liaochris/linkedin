Jim Berkowitz’s Summary

I am a seasoned executive with more than 30 years of professional services and product management experience in Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Accounting and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software solutions for small (rapidly growing), mid-sized and Fortune 500 companies.

Before founding CRM Mastery, I was a Senior Product Manager for predictive CRM applications at SPSS, Inc. where I played a key role in the design, development and role out of predictive analytic CRM solutions.

Finally over the past 25 years, as a Sales Force Automation and CRM Consultant, I've helped more than 100 organizations design and successfully implement CRM technology solutions; including companies like Sprint, Apria Home Healthcare, ADIC, Iomega Corporation, US West, Citicorp Diners Club, Hewlett-Packard, Times Warner, Mellon Mortgage, Charles Schwab and many others.

My writing appears in various industry journals including RealMarket, the Journal of Data Warehousing, TechTarget's SearchCRM portal and of course the CRM Mastery e-Journal.
