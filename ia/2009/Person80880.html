Franco Beschizza’s Summary

Franco started working in the Digital Media industry back in the years of the dot-com boom, working on projects for blue-chip clients such as BT and Unilever.

Building highly flexible and effective e-marketing teams based on 'what if', whilst underpinning with strong fiscal and project methodology.

He has work on major awarding winning Government campaigns targeted at the youth market like RUThinking, Playingsafely, Talk to Frank, Camouflage.

Franco Beschizza’s Specialties:

Developing new web2.0 services that will meet the future needs of customers, and ensure campaigns have a strong focus on a digital strategy. Campaigns that are fully integrated and focused on customer insights. Developing SMART objectives, critical success factors, planning, acquisition and retention, implementation, measuring the ROI, and optimizing the digital marketing.

With over fifteen years in the Internet marketing & communications industry and is an active speaker at digital events.
