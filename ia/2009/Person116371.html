Johan Meyers’s Summary

Researcher in the field of fluid mechanics. Current research is mainly aimed at turbulent flows, their simulation with LES and DNS, and their control or optimization. Specific topics include
- Subgrid-scale modeling for large-eddy simulations (LES)
- Numerical discretization and energy-conservation properties
- Computational error-assessment and prediction for LES
- Optimization and control of turbulence, using LES and DNS, combined with adjoint-based gradient estimation

Johan Meyers’s Specialties:

turbulent flows, fluid mechanics, CFD, LES, DNS, aerodynamics, heat transfer, thermodynamics
