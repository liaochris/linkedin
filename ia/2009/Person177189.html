SRINIVASAN Narayanan’s Summary

Currently an entreprenuer. A Management Accountant by qualification (AICWA), I have been in industry for around 30 years till now (2007.) I had worked in Hindustan Monarch P Ltd. an automotive chain manufacturing unit in Hyderabad; Duncan Agro-Industries Ltd, a leaf tobacco exporting company in Guntur; Indian Potash Ltd., an all-India joint-sector fertilizer import and distribution company in Hyderabad); Andhra Pradesh State Financial Corporation, a state run development financial institution in Hyderabad; SOL Pharmaceuticals Ltd., a bulk drug and formulations manufaturing company in Hyderabad. I was doing management consulting at Ajaya Kumar Associates in Hyderabad. In 1998 I became an entrepreneur by joining Swell Financial Services, Hyderabad. In 2003, I am a part-owner of Propart Solutions India P Ltd. a Business Process Management (BPM) in F&A, Currently I am a Director on the boards of Swell Financial Services P Ltd and Propart Solutions India P Ltd and Sreshtaya Softtech India P Ltd. all based at Hyderabad.

My core competency is in developing Strategy and Operations Linked Performance Management Systems.(SOLPMS.)

Goals: Develop world class best practices for Business Process Management (BPM) companies operating in the Finance and Accounting space, using Propart as the testing lab for such best practices.
