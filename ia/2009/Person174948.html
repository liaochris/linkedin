Nicole Marino’s Summary

PSI provides Direct and Contract or Contract-to-Hire Placement services for Information Technology, Accounting/Finance and Professional Services candidates. We are based in Western New York and provide services primarily in WNY and Ohio. Since 1980, our goal has always been to provide the most exceptional service possible to our clients and candidates. Whether the need is for contract project staff or for a direct hire technology or management professional, you can count on PSI to put your needs first!

PSI's clients have discovered the difference between hiring a "candidate" versus hiring the "right person" for a job. In turn, our candidates know that we are knowledgeable, work with integrity and have their best interests at heart, throughout their careers. We strive to meet the needs of both our client’s requirements and our candidate’s career goals, ensuring a sound solution for all. We want to build a partnership with you that will be of benefit to you for many years to come. If you are a business looking for talented staff, or a candidate looking for that next career step, PSI can help.

We place candidates in any Information Technology positions including: CIO, CTO, IT Director, Development Manager, Technology Manager, Business Analyst, Systems Analyst, PMP, Project Manager, IT Sales, Business Analyst, Systems Analyst, Implementation Specialist, ETL Specialist, Business Intelligence Analyst, GIS Specialist, Developer, Programmer Analyst, Software Engineer, Web Designer, Web Developer, Database Specialist, DBA, Data Warehouse Specialist, Architecture, Network Engineer, Systems Administrator, Telecommunications, Help Desk, Desktop or Workstation Support, Customer Support, Systems Programmer, Security, Trainer, Computer Operator, QA Tester, Technical Writer, Content Developer.
