Max McKeown’s Summary

Max McKeown is an innovation expert, a highly effective catalyst, and has been described as "Europe's unorthodox answer to Tom Peters".

- World Class Strategic & Innovation Facilitation & Consulting
- Highly Charismatic Leader who will Inspire Teams and Drive Innovative Thought
- Create, Animates, & Grows Strategic Innovation Inititiatives
- Strong Communication & Relationship Building Skills
- Proven Ability to Generate Change & Influence At All Levels
- Works With Teams & Leaders To Envision & Realise Growth Through Innovation
- Mitigates & Resolves Unhelpful Conflict To Drive Through Effective Consensus
- Acknowledge Global Expert With 15+ Years of Experience
- Strong Analytical & Pattern Shaping Skills
- Ready To Deploy Chief Innovation Officer!

He works as a strategic adviser for four of the five most admired companies in the world. He has spoken across the world about many subjects including innovation, engagement, human potential, customer experience, marketing, team building, and competitive advantage. He has been elected to the Customer Service Hall of Fame, been nominated as a “Star of Human Resources” by Personnel Today, and been featured on national and international radio, television, and newspapers.

He has written 6 books including THE TRUTH ABOUT INNOVATION, featuring lessons and secrets from innovative companies, UNSHRINK, about high innovation, high performance cultures, E-CUSTOMER, an insight into evolving customer behavior, and WHY THEY DON'T BUY, an end to end guide to building profitable customer relationships.
