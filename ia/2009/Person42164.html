Cheryl Antier’s Summary

Tired of staring at a blank computer screen? Cat got your tongue? Feel like you're just not finding the words to say what you need to?

Got these symptoms every time you have to write something? Dry mouth, sweaty palms, upset stomach, and an almost uncontrollable urge to clean out your junk drawer...You've got writer's block!

Get Instant relief in just 30 seconds - email me now - for a free 30 minute consultation. I'll even record it, so you won't have to take notes.

E-books/books, special reports, white papers, case studies, articles, interviews, scripts, speeches, press releases, proposals.

Expertise in writing Website copy that:

1. Communicates your company's value proposition to your visitors. Remember every time someone visits your Website they're looking for something - they have certain expectations. Does your current copy meet their needs & helps them find what they're looking for?

2. Stays focused on your visitor's needs. What you're selling "doesn't matter in the minds of your visitors!" The only thing they care about is whether you can help them solve a problem or fulfill a need. Your products or services are just the tool that help them get what they want. Does your Website copy give answers?

3. Helps visitors find what they're looking for. Don't make them hunt for it - time is precious. Know what they're looking for and make it easy to find.

4. Uses their words - not yours. Do your customers speak geek? If not, you shouldn't either. If you have to use technical words or jargon, make sure you "let them in on it" - so they feel like they're part of your inner circle.

5. Builds a relationship. Your visitors are checking you out to see if they can trust you. Trust comes over time - but there are ways you can build trust on your Website. Does your Website project security?

    So first of all, let's get this straight - effective sales copy is honest and compelling and comes from the gut. It's a reflection of who you really are and what you have to share with the world.

    It uses the power of persuasion - not lies, not hype, not high pressure. It's about your honesty, integrity and expertise, and the reason that you're in business doing what you do.
