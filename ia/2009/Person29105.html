Biswaraj Kar’s Summary

Biswaraj is a B.Tech. in Electronics and Tele-communication Engineering from College Of Engineering, Bhubaneswar, India under Biju Patnaik University Of Technology. He is a Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist in Web Applications (.NET Framework 2.0) and Windows Vista. He has been associated with Microsoft Corporation since 2007 as a Student Partner (previously called as "Microsoft Student Ambassador") and has been responsible for evangelizing Microsoft products and innovations at the college level and the student community at large. He has worked on technologies and methodologies like ASP.NET, HTML, C#, Java (J2EE), Web 2.0 Technologies, AJAX etc. He has done some projects using the above mentioned technologies which have been appreciated and rewarded in various software development competitions in India. He is the founder and Student Technical Lead of "InnoVision" - the Technical & Robotics Society and "CodePlex" - the Programming & Software Development Society of College Of Engineering Bhubaneswar (CEB). Also, he has been associated with the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) as the Secretary of the ISTE Students Chapter of CEB and with Mozilla Corporation as a Campus Ambassador.

Biswaraj Kar’s Specialties:

Biswaraj specializes in application and web development using Microsoft Technologies as well as Open source languages/tools.
