Lars Jonsson’s Summary

Yttra Consumer Communication helps companies and organizations to describe and communicate the environmental and sustainability performance of their goods and services in a credible way.

Yttra Consumer Communications was founded in 2003 by Lars Jonsson. Lars has worked with the Swedish national Consumer Agency on issues concerning the relationship between consumption and environment and has been head of Food & Environment at the Swedish Consumers’ Association. He has also spent several years in the coordination of Swedish industry, agencies and organizations participating in international standardisation work, particularly in the areas of greenhouse gas emissions, life cycle assessment (LCA), environmental labels and declarations, “green marketing”, environmental management and energy management.

In his previous professional roles Lars has led a large number of projects, carried out several investigations, coordinated research and attitude surveys and participated in and led a large number of expert and working groups at both national and international level. Lars Jonsson has conducted investigative missions for the Swedish Government on the interlinking of national environmental and consumer policy and has a well-founded experience in strategic development, communications efforts and media relations and has been author and editor of several books on consumer, environmental and food issues. Lars is also frequently employed as trainer and lecturer in "green marketing".
