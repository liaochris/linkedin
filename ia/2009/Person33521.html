Brian Fitzpatrick’s Summary

Outstanding, results driven Executive Manager with a proven record of successfully growing software and business processing outsourcing companies in the mortgage and financial services industry. Market-focused, 15+ year veteran of the consumer / residential mortgage lending industry with experience servicing both large and mid market lending and depository institutions throughout the United States. Possesses extensive experience in growing both software and business process outsourcing organizations as a key member of executive management in the capacity of Sales and Marketing, Professional Services, Product Management, President, Chief Operating Officer and Chief Executive Officer. Creative problem solver who specializes in start-ups and entrepreneurial organizations by setting corporate strategy, executing key business initiatives and building optimal infrastructure, controlling costs, creating strategic partnerships and alliances, selling key accounts and maximizing the effectiveness and accountability of sales, marketing, product management, information technology, professional services, client services, operations and finance and administration. Skilled negotiator in major information technology and services transactions and driving value based transactions in mergers and acquisition activity. Financial Services and Mortgage Industry thought leader that has been a sought out speaker for numerous industry panels and conferences and has authored many articles and white papers for a variety of mortgage and related financial services trade publications and in 2007 was named a “Mortgage IT All-Star” by Mortgage Banking Magazine.

Brian Fitzpatrick’s Specialties:

Start-ups, turn-around specialist, mergers and acquisitions, sales and marketing, contract negotiations, general business operations and revenue and profit maximization.
