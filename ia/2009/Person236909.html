Tony Murrey’s Summary

I have in depth experience in Management, Leadership, Communication, Sales, Business Development, Purchasing, Creative Solutions, Project Management, Financial Analysis, Mergers / Acquisitions, Personnel Development, Product Marketing and Profitability Studies. I have spent my career in various positions with the Foodservice channel of trade however am actively pursuing opportunities in multiple industries in that all my primary skills are transferable and highly beneficial to a multitude of potential employers. My experience in working 'both sides' of the desk gives me an unusual well rounded view of business transactions and an ability to apply that knowledge to assuring that long term partnerships / client - company relationships are formed.

In my next career move I would like to lead a large team towards achieving either a Sales, Communication, Purchasing, Marketing or Creative goal through the demonstration of an aggressive desire to succeed through partnerships, sound business principals, networking, vision and a deep rooted commitment to their industry and clients. I will not lead from behind the desk but rather by following in the footprints of those experts around me. I am looking to help set corporate benchmarks and project goals; then share, illustrate and advise on how to achieve them. I would like to serve for a company that demonstrates; exceptional customer / client service and outstanding interoffice communication and support. They need to be a respected leader in their industry and employ a 'can, want and will do' rather that 'have to' style of business management.
