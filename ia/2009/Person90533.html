Gueri Segura’s Summary

Gueri has 9 years of experience creating and designing advertising and communication solutions for clients of different industries.

Prior founding Detour, Gueri served as Creative & Art Director at the Hispanic-focused advertising agencies Casanova Pendrill and Olé advertising.

Before moving to the United States in 2004, Gueri lived in Lima, Peru and Mexico City where he found his passion for advertising in the award-winning advertising agency Oveja Negra.

Gueri graduated from CUMP (Centro Universitario de Mercadotecnia y Publicidad) with a BA in Advertising and a major in Strategic Planning.

He is fluent in English and Spanish. In his spare time he enjoys making music, watching movies and he is an enthusiastic photographer. Gueri currently lives in Irvine, CA with his wife and two children.
