Eric DELAGE’s Summary

Embedded system architect with more than 14 years of experience in all aspects of system-on-chip/-fpga architecture and design. Focus on realtime data-streaming computation-intensive applications and rapid platform generation/IP design reuse methodologies.

Highly interested in converting innovative ideas into profitable businesses, I will discuss any opportunity to start a new activity or a new company, to explore modern ways of working, in my area of expertise. Do not hesitate to contact me.
