Bentley Hall’s Summary

With a passion for social entrepreneurship and a love of food, I am launching Bee Well with a simple hope - that one day healthy, fresh, organic food will be more common and more convenient than the junk food that surrounds us today. We have a feasible and scaleable solution that accomplishes this dream... stay tuned for more details.

Bentley Hall’s Specialties:

I bring a certified accounting background and a broad range of experience in finance, operations, logistics, sales & marketing and IT at well known companies such as Johnson & Johnson, Clif Bar and Jamba Juice.
