John Bruce’s Summary

I have a passion for recruiting and leading high calibre teams to build successful companies. Though my skills span all operational areas, my core DNA is in sales and marketing. I particularly enjoy creating innovative and cost effective strategies that drive growth. As CEO of one of the leading Web2 companies, I’ve developed a fluency around the space and how to leverage these new technologies for business goals.

I’ve enjoyed success with companies from zero to 650 million dollars in revenue and from founders with an idea to public companies with offices around the world. Along with the growing pains that come with increased scale. But I do prefer small to mid-sized businesses, particularly when we’re driving to take a leadership position and making new markets.

Experienced in raising financing from top tier investors.. including Greylock, NorthBridge, Venrock, Accel and Bessemer. Along with the transactional expertise required to successfully acquire and sell companies.

Worked extensively across Europe, ten years in Silicon Valley and now based in New England.

John Bruce’s Specialties:

Executive leadership, strong operational skills, marketing and sales execution, early to mid stage technology start-ups, financing, social media, M&A
