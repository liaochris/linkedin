Michael Fisher’s Summary

At Community Health Network on the eBusiness team, I have worked on several high profile web application projects including a content management system, an online benefits application, the consumer portal and applications integrated with it (including a personal health record and a patient blog) and a redesign of all of the external web sites (ecommunity.com, indianasurgerycenter.com, and hearthospital.com are some examples). I developed mobile.ecommunity.com and an application interface for Pillbox, a medication tracking application for IPhones. I have presented our team's work at the annual Healthcare Internet Conference twice, presented once at the Greystone Client Conference and co-wrote a presentation for the Healthcare Internet Conference.
