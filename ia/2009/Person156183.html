Matthew Ray’s Summary

An opportunist to the fullest, I strive to go above and beyond expectations. I take every opportunity to increase my experiences in this forever-changing world. Being open to new ideas and radical changes, I will be an unbiased reviewer of a new idea to strategically break down all the pros and cons.

During my two years at Global Crossing, I have jumped on every opportunity to learn something new. After finishing up Phase I of Global Crossing's Intranet Migration to SharePoint 2007, and coordinating user-training sessions and tutorials, I expanded outward to work close with other organizations within the business.

My current role as a business analyst as given me the chance to help meld the internal culture of the organization with increased efficiency and communication with our customers.

Being a social media enthusiast, I am looking to help Global Crossing employees engage in social media aspects around the web - whether it be it Facebook, Twitter, AllTop, blogging, and LinkedIn. Social Media is where the innovators strive and potential customers are taking advantage to make an appearence. A social media tool such as Twitter could break down the walls of the "cold-call" and open up the doors to your new customer.

In addition to my professional experiences, I am an experienced traveler and a connoisseur of cultures. I have planned extensive cross-country road trips. These trips -include a road-trip to Arches in Utah, Badlands in South Dakota and Yellowstone NP in Wyoming, a trip through the Canadian Maritimes to St. John's, Newfoundland, and most recently a 14,400 mile continental road-trip to Alaska and back. I created, while on my trip, a live and up-to-date website entailing our travels, pictures, videos, blogs, and comments while we cruised across North America. You can visit at http://www.OurDriveToAlaska.com
