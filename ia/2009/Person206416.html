Ryan Schultz’s Summary

I've spent the last couple years actively exploring how new technologies and new collaborative processes are being harnessed to address the waste and inefficiencies that are currently plaguing the building industry.

Toward this end, I co-founded Studio Wikitecture, a group interested in exploring how the increasing use of Web 2.0 technology, along with the emerging 3-dimensional web, could allow the industry to design and build better buildings, more efficiently.

Through this group, we've conducted a number of award-winning projects to tease out the technologies and protocols to facilitate such a highly integrated, hyper-collaborative environment.
