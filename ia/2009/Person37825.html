Carlos R Hernandez’s Summary

Social Media Resource, Consultant & Trainer to Baby-Boomers desiring to be Web 2.0 savvy. I enjoy creating business solutions via strategic visibility and networking.

I am especially motivated to consult and train Baby-Boomers who feel "behind the times". My focus is on men and women who are tasking to find meaningful work, transitioning to a new career or striving to create innovative marketing approaches to their business challenges.

iRM's consulting and training practices are geared to create promotional strategies and tactics. It is rooted in client orientated business experience and a passion for social media and people-centric relationships. Web 2.0 networking and marketing tools such as LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter are emphasized.

Inspiring and teaching people to become tech savvy reflects our drive for results. Resumes, networking introductions, 30 second elevator pitches, branding statements and promotional programs become powerful and alive.

I made the conscious decision to being a social media consultant by combining my gifts of public speaking, problem solving and teaching. My passion and knowledge are grounded in a 28 year professional career working for Westinghouse Electric & Eaton Corporations in technical solution sales, marketing and project management. Academically, I studied Civil Engineering at Stanford University's School of Engineering.

I contribute to building community through the Phi Kappa Psi Fraternity, Catholic Professional Businesss Club, Archbishop Riordan High School Alumni Board Association, & Grace Cathedral's "GraceWorks" Program for those in job and/or career transition.
