Maurits Horbach’s Summary

With the training programs of Sandler, you'll learn one step at a time, at your own pace and on your own schedule. Sandler's President's Club, for example, is an ongoing series of workshops, classroom sessions and problem-solving clinics - more than 20 hours' worth each month - which you can choose to attend whenever you feel the need. In a way, it's like having a health club you can go to anytime you want to exercise and tone up your selling muscles. With repetition, you'll be able to keep those selling muscles from atrophying, while changing the self-limiting habits of thought that previously held you back. This approach gives you the flexibility to focus on the skills, techniques and attitudes you need to work on at precisely the moment you're ready to work on them.

How It Works
Finding the Right Problems...Working for the Best Solutions.

Do your prospects resist your selling points?
Do they fight you on price?
Do you find yourself becoming an unpaid consultant?
Do your prospects stall you with excuses?
Are your proposals being shopped all over town?
Is your sales cycle longer than you want it to be?
Does your prospect refuse to make a decision?
These are painful questions. But they're questions all sales professionals have to answer at some point. And if you answered "yes" to one or more of them...President's Club Can Help!

You Don't Just Learn New Sales Skills...You Build New Life Skills.

We believe that lasting change only comes about with ongoing reinforcement. With the President's Club, we're in the business of changing people's behavior...we reach people between the ears.

Our workshops offer you more than 20 hours of classroom sessions each month, a mixture of workshop training and problem-solving clinics. Your President's Club membership gives you the flexibility to work on building your skills in a wide range of areas and to focus on specific problems such as call reluctance, "think-it-overs," or dealing with money.

Maurits Horbach’s Specialties:

Traditional selling techniques simply don't work anymore. That's why the prospect can so easily control a sales call -- and destroy a salesperson's self-esteem in the process.

We teach honest, no-nonsense sales techniques that get results while preserving the sales professional's self-respect. Prospects are treated as they want to be treated: as intelligent, thinking individuals; BUT, the prospect NEVER controls anyone who masters the Sandler Selling System®.
