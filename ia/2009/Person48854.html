Sean Connolly’s Summary

I am a patent attorney in the Omaha office of Husch Blackwell Sanders LLP. I have represented clients throughout the patent prosecution process including appeals and reexaminations. I have worked with a variety of clients to identify and protect valuable intellectual property within their company and evaluate new inventions as they are made. I can also help with drafting and negotiating intellectual property agreements including licenses and nondisclosure agreements.

I am registered to practice before the United States Patent & Trademark Office.

Sean Connolly’s Specialties:

I have experience in counseling clients in technology areas related to food science, process design and control, medical devices, pharmaceuticals, polymers and coatings, automotive components, oil and gas production, and a variety of consumer products.
