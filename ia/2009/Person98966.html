Agnieszka Górnicka’s Summary

During my 15 years experience in market research, I have witnessed most of market development in Poland, and find it as thrilling now as it was in the beginning of my research career! Having served dozens of clients in hundreds of projects, I am now among most experienced specialists in my country, with strong qualitative and quantitative background. My experience includes 5 years as a Customised Research Manager in a large corporation.

Inquiry, a full service research agency, was founded in 2004 with a team of professional co-workers. As a member of One Planet One Call, Inc. Inquiry is responsible for research services in Russia, Poland and other countries of the CEE region.

Agnieszka Górnicka’s Specialties:

Quantitative Research, Advanced Statistics, Qualitative Research, Advanced Moderating, In-store Methods, Observational & Ethnographic Studies, Internet Surveys, Usage & Attitude Surveys, Brand Image and Brand Equity, Pricing Studies (conjoint and other), Product & concept tests, Advertising Research, other
