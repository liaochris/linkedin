Julie London’s Summary

I am a software engineer with over 9 years experience building enterprise-level web applications.

I began my technology career in 2000 as a technical producer of online animated shorts for Mondo Media. I quickly became fascinated with Flash and eventually went on to work as a Flash applications developer for Aplia, an online educational startup, and later for E*TRADE Financial. Between these 2 positions, I spent 5 years building complex Flash-based charting and financial modeling applications.

Since 2006, I've worked as a front end engineer for Yahoo! Finance. Currently, my major responsibility is to develop new high-profile Finance products using a single codebase that can be easily localized and deployed globally. Most recently I led front end development of an internationalized Currencies Center. In 2008, I led front end development of Tech Ticker, Finance's highly successful video blogging platform.
