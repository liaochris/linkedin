Darren Priest’s Summary

Trained and Accredited with Team Management Systems Australia and UK to deliver their psychometric tools.

Masters in Occupational Health Psychology at the Institute of Work, Health and Organizations at Nottingham University Business School.

Member of the European Academy of Occupational Health Psychologists.

Member of International Association of Facilitators.

Member of the International Positive Psychology Association.

Experienced Trainer in EFL and Teacher Training.

Darren Priest’s Specialties:

I am an experienced training/teaching professional, with a qualification (Msc) in Occupational Health Psychology, the science of how people’s health is influenced by their work, and how work outputs are influenced by people’s health. Bringing creative solutions to what seem like chaotic workplace issues. I bring tailored sessions to organisations, focusing on teams and team dynamics, treating conflicts and reducing stresses. Facilitate use of TMS, MLQ and Personal Synthesis Programmes.
