Stephen Silver’s Summary

Stephen Silver is a founding partner at Propel – Funding Accelerators whose mission is to save entrepreneurs time and money by taking the pain out of the fund raising process. Propel specializes in working with companies who will be, or are currently, searching for alternative funding. Over the past 6 months alone, Propel companies have raised $10.9MM.

Prior to founding Propel, Stephen was the Entrepreneur Director for the Southern California Keiretsu Forum, the nation’s largest angel investment group. He worked with over 550 entrepreneurs, matching them with the group’s 250 local Angel investors. His duties included: sourcing entrepreneurs, representing Keiretsu at many venture-related networking events, coaching companies on presentations, and organizing lineups for the monthly investor meetings at each of its four chapters.

Stephen attended Babson College, the nation’s #1 school for entrepreneurship. Babson systematically teaches the intricacies of starting companies from the ground up- from building sustainable business models and creating business plans, to ultimately starting companies, pitching them to investors, and donating any profits to charity.

Babson College did lay a solid foundation for business, but for Stephen it produced a much more profound introduction to his true passion, startups. He graduated with a B.S. in business administration, with concentrations in entrepreneurship and marketing.
