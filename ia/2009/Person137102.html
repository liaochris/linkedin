Larry Steinberg’s Summary

Larry Steinberg is the principle of Steinberg Financial Advisers (SFA), a Registered Investment Advisory Firm and insurance agency with offices in Scottsdale and Pasadena. Larry founded SFA in Pasadena in 1995 and moved the headquarters to Scottsdale in 2008. Larry specializes in providing investment and insurance solutions for entrepreneurs, business owners, and the self-employed. SFA implements 401(k) plans, employee benefits, and protects his clients by providing business and personal insurance, with a specialization in difficult to place business insurance for start up and unique companies. He manages investment portfolios like an institutional investor such as a college endowment, private foundation, or pension plan, utilizing both active and passive investments, tactical and strategic asset allocation, tax efficient and alternative investments. Larry also works with retirees and those near retirement, helping them secure their retirement.
As someone who enjoys public speaking, Larry has spoken to groups as diverse as Walton International, IBEW Retiree Club, and several IMN conferences.
Larry received his AB with honors and distinction from Occidental College in 1992, where he played football and rugby. While in school, he worked for Wells Fargo before going to work for Transamerica upon graduation. He then spent two years at Independent Capital Management before starting SFA in 1995. Larry continued his athletics after college, playing rugby for the Oxy Olde Boys RFC until 2004.

Securities offered through Associated Securities Corp. (ASC), Member FINRA/SIPC. Additional Advisory services offered through Associated Planners Investment Advisory, Inc (APIA) and ASC, Registered Investment Advisors.
Steinberg Financial Advisers is not affiliated with ASC or APIA.

Larry Steinberg, Registered Representative, Associated Securities Corp. CA Insurance License 0B19683
