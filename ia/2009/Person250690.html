Yves Bennaim’s Summary

Got my first computer in 1984 (in Geneva), my first email address in 1992, published my first website in 1995, offered web hosting and email services in 1996 (in Brisbane), became Art Director at Lycos Europe in 2000 (in Paris), built and managed Japan.com in 2003 (in Tokyo). Worked as an Internet consultant for clients such as Mitsubishi Electric Europe, Park Hyatt Tokyo, and AOL. Always looking for new things to learn and that’s one of the reason why I love the Internet.

Loving design, problem solving, creativity, and everything that challenges the brain, the eyes, the taste buds, or a mix of all of the above. Attracted to everything and anything artistic, and what’s clever but not pretentious. Overall, spending too much time in front of a screen.

Currently based in Tokyo.
