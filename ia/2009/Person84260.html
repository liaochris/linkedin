Michael Wilson’s Summary

I have more than 21 years of increasing responsibilities in business application presales, technology centric software presales, presales management, sales strategy, market messaging, business/partner development, product development / QA management, and various aspects of sales operations. I enjoy solution selling, sales strategy development, product positioning, building relationships, and orchestrating presales engagements for large sales opportunities. I have a wide range of software business consulting experience that encompasses in depth knowledge of SCM / ERP / CRM / SRM / WMS / APS solutions, Lean Manufacturing methodologies, Supply Chain Management, Cost Accounting, and Information Technology Management. My customer facing presales experience, solution/value based selling methodology, and understanding of various industry business drivers gives me a very well rounded skill set. My varied responsibilities in world class application software organizations coupled with a solid knowledge of a software organization’s functional dynamics have given me the ability to contribute on many levels. I have worked for some of the most well run business application software organizations in the world allowing me to develop my sales operational support skills.

I have recommended solutions for Marketing, Customer Management, Engineering, Manufacturing, Demand Management, Procurement, and Supply Chain operational challenges facing hundred global companies, including many fortune 500 and fortune 1000 corporations. My professional experience spans Industrial Manufacturing, Distribution, High Tech, Automotive, CPG, and 3PL market segments.
