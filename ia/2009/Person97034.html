Ian Copsey’s Summary

Consistent forecasting of price movement in today's Forex market is a forgotten art that requires a deep understanding of price structure and development.

Drawing from over 20 years experience as a technical analyst The Daily Forecaster is a comprehensive report that guides traders through the trading day and is based on an integrated blend of techniques perfected over years of study, research and practice.

Based on the techniques described in my book "Integrated Technical Analysis" my 9 page report is unique and read by a wide range of traders which currently includes hedge funds to banks and retail traders.

The analysis, whole or in part, is also available through distribution agreement to banks and brokers.

Author: Integrated Technical Analysis (1999, John Wiley)
Author: Relative Spread Strength & Rapid RSI (Oct 2006, Technical Analysis of Stocks & Commodities)

Ian Copsey’s Specialties:

Have developed an adaptation of the Elliott Wave Principal. Integrated into a framework of time cycles and momentum the techniques provides superior performance as well as more accurate support and resistance levels produced through harmonic ratios and derivatives of Fibonacci ratios.
