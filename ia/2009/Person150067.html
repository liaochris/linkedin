María Martín Gomez’s Summary

For the last 5 years my position has been CMDS Director. The CMDS is the Center of Monitoring, Diagnostic and Simulation for Electrical Power Stations and was developed from scratch with my first team of 5 people. Nowadays with 45 engineers of different profiles we support power stations in all aspects from an operational and maintenance perspective, analysing and learning from the past and trying to define rules that either prevent or detect in an early stage the behaviour of different components to avoid failures in the future.
Previously I worked as an Energy Trader working in different OTC and non OTC markets througout Europe. At that time I was also responsible for new markets analysis for the German and English speaking countries.
