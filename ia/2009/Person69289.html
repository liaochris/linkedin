David Curiel’s Summary

Recently turned translator and writer, I am a wine professional with more than ten years in sales, marketing and management.

I also sell wine while envisioning ways to change the world. Those who value the full nourishment — physical and emotional — that a good meal made from sustainable whole ingredients can to bring a person and a community will always find a partner and a friend in me.

I have vastly traveled the world and am enjoying life immensely. I hope to leave the world a better place than I found it—for my children and for those of others.
