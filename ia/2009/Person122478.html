John Schreiber’s Summary

I handle civil appeals in a wide variety of issues, including administrative, business, real estate, family law, personal injury, probate, and procedural issues. I also help clients and trial counsel preserve or create a record on appeal by appropriate pre- or post-trial motions. In addition to California's state appellate courts, I am admitted to the 9th Circuit Court of Appeals and the United States Supreme Court.
