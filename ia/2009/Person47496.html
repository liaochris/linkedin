Clint LeRoy’s Summary

Outgoing Business Development is the first focus in my own business and towards other working collaborations.

Outgoing Energy adviser, able to take in visual scope of an area with design and energy resolution that always exceeds the customers expectations. An ability to estimate and correlate the finer values of taking into consideration mother nature and respect the less invasive approach to successfully implementing energy gathering ideas. I approach every project with an open mind, leave nothing left unturned, being able to look at each project with more then one perspective. Renewable energy is a limited resource so using it at its maximum capability is taken into account on every project design.

What I do is look into all the facets of converting waste into energy, from the different sources of BioMass, Solar, Wind, Hydro, and anything else like new and remodeled homes with insulation critiques to improve on heating or cooling. I am an applications engineer per say with out the official degree. I take the different ways to accomplish the goal of finding the many different solutions that present itself from each property that I gaze upon. Each company that I am asked to review has unique qualities and problems in achieving there energy development. Most I can find a solution, and usually I can find them enough energy to generate more then they need, so a ROI. My focus is on developing the excessive behaviors of reducing our waste of materials, but with also a focus on wasted space. I am interested in how everything works, if it is put together or breaks apart I want to know how? If it develops an output that can be converted to energy I want to know more. Always curious, always inquisitive, I never stop learning, I am certified in many different things electronic, mechanical, and computer related, all my friends come to me with the odd and different questions knowing that I can always figure something out.
