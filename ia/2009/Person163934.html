Mike Bailey’s Summary

Ralph Waldo Emerson wrote, "The purpose of life is not to be happy. It is to be useful, to be honorable, to be compassionate, to have it make some difference that you have lived and lived well."

To that end, I am a Marketing Manager best suited to the promotion and support of a dynamic Sales Team. My skills run the gambit from Strategic Planning and Budgeting to Tactical implementation of promotions, events, website authoring with search engine optimization as well as implementation and management of CRM systems. In the past, I worked mainly as a Marketing Department of one. I am now in the process of developing my skills as a Marketing Manager with a team focused on lead generation, public relations and event promotion, developing both Marketing Professionals as well as Outside Sales Representatives from my Inside Sales Team.
