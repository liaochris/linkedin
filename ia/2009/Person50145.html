Craig McPhilimey’s Summary

I have been living and working in Thailand for 10 years, I have worked in many fields since my arrival a decade ago. I have focused on the real estate industry for the past 5 years, mainly on luxury condominiums in Bangkok as well as luxury property in other areas such as Hua-Hin and Phuket. I have a strong network of developers, agents and owners. This along with a deep understanding of the local people and their way of doing business makes for a huge advantage to anybody considering the purchase of a property in Thailand.

I offer up to date, honest and professional representation to investors and end users looking to relocate to Thailand or make an investment in the Real Estate market here.
Thailand, and Asia in general, is a great emerging market to be involved in. Be it a single unit as a holiday home, or a more sizeable investment option such as a resort. The R.O.I and freedom to use 'your' property as 'you' consider appropriate make Thailand a very interesting option.

If you are considering adding a little diversity to your portfolio, with a true chance at making solid returns then Thailand is a must. I am happy to offer free advice and offer direction to anyone that is interested in Property, Project management, Consultancy or Travel in Thailand.

I also have a small non profit organization that takes intrepid adventurers and explorers cruising around the islands and open oceans of South East Asia. We also take under water hunters and fishermen to some of the most remote islands on the planet, the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

  • Co-Founder and Safari Leader

    Andaman Spearfishing

    (Sports industry)

    March 2008 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    The Andaman & Nicobar Islands are one of the worlds most pristine marine enviroments. Commercial fishing is illegal, never have these waters seen longliners or dragnetters. The Indian Authorites keep a close watch on the waters and any boats trying to sneak in to fin sharks or catch Tuna will be caught, the crew put in prison and the boat impounded. As a result there are big fish everywhere, you will see numerous large schools of tuna every day, the size of some of the bait balls has to be seen to be believed.The Andaman & Nicobar are a group of picturesque Islands, big and small, inhabited and uninhabited, a total of 572 islands, islets and rocks lying in the South Eastern Part of the Bay of Bengal. Of the 572 islands, only 36 are inhabited, there are still stone age tribes on some of the islands, they have no contact with the outside world and are still hunter/gatherers having not discovered farming. Previous attempts to make contact with these tribes has resulted in primitive spears being thrown, they have made it quite clear they want nothing to do with the outside world.

    Andaman Spearfishing is the ONLY company offering spearing trips to this destination, prepare to join the food chain, hunt and be hunted, eat or be eaten, these trips are not for the faint hearted or green horns. Our last trip produced some great fishing and spearing and saw the succesful release of 7 marlin in 3 days on rod and line and 1 speared.

    We also offer more relaxed safaris closer to home in Thailand and Burma. These safaris are a great way for experienced Under Water Hunters that don't have the time to visit the Andamans to get a chance at a billfish or large tuna.

    Andaman Spearfishing is a non profit organization, I run safaris at cost and although I profit as a distributer for Pelaj in Thailand all trips, charters and safaris are strictly non profit. We also follow a strict breathhold hunting only policy.

    For more info email me at info@andamanspearfishing.com
