Blaine Millet’s Summary

Blaine is a catalyst for change in an organization – driving improvements in sales and profitability while increasing customer loyalty and retention. He functions as a leader/manager or senior level individual contributor for professional services, business development, operations or sales and marketing. He delivers measurable value for businesses in three ways:

• Working with your organization – provides Advisory/Consulting Services in Business Strategy, Sales/Profitability Generation, Customer/Employee Loyalty, Market Differentiation, and Operational Enhancement – building a Competitive Advantage

• Working inside your organization – contributes as an employee in areas of strategy, sales/marketing, leadership, operations and customer loyalty programs – including day-to-day functions for change and continuous improvement, turning ideas into action

• Working outside in the marketplace - conducts workshops, training and speaking engagements for organizations, associations and other venues – helping educate leaders and employees on “executable” strategy, increasing sales/profits, customer/employee loyalty and building an integrated customer experience process throughout the organization

Blaine has worked in start-up through Fortune 50 organizations in addition to his own consulting practice. His roles have been in sales, business development, consulting/professional services, management and innovation. His industry knowledge is broad, with a concentration in professional services, software/technology, and telecom.

As co-author of the book, “Creating and Delivering Totally Awesome Customer Experiences,” his thought leadership created a new way of thinking, by building tools to link their customers’ experience to their internal business processes. This approach spawned an entirely new way of connecting increases in sales and profitability directly to customer loyalty and retention – driving stronger competitive advantage and greater differentiation.
