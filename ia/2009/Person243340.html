Ville Tikka’s Summary

I'm a creative strategic professional with over six years of experience in trends research and analysis, innovation facilitation, interaction design, marketing, research and media. Born & raised in Finland, but currently living and working in New York City (or actually in Brooklyn :).

I explore the forefront of global and local societal transformations and cutting edge consumer trends - and apply the insights and foresight to strategy and innovation with design, product development, branding and marketing.

I'm a natural chameleon... I move fluently between planning and presenting in a boardroom, innovating at a design studio and investigating people and trends at shady alleys and online avenues.

Also, I'm a true global citizen: I've lived in many cultural epicenters (Barcelona, Helsinki, New York) and have ethnographic research experience all over the planet in locations such as London, Helsinki, Delhi, Bangalore, Mexico City, Sao Paolo, Rio de Janeiro, Buenos Aires and New York. And naturally have had the greatest vacations in many, many other European, Asian, and Latin American countries... :)

Ville Tikka’s Specialties:

▶ Endless curiosity towards everything new!

▶ I'm a lateral thinker who fluidly combines abstract theories to concrete insights, brings creativity to structure and structure to creativity.
