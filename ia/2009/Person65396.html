Dominic Tong, M.D.’s Summary

Enterprising and accomplished life sciences professional with nearly twenty years of diverse healthcare practice and management experience in the academic, public and private sectors. Clinical development and medical affairs experience in the biopharmaceutical and medical device space with the Johnson & Johnson Family of Companies. Extensive knowledge and reference base in science, internal medicine and diagnostic radiology. Exceptionally adaptive and able to rapidly acquire new skills and responsibilities. Major strengths include relationship-building, creative problem-solving, strategic thinking, cross-function collaboration and development of synergies within biopharmaceuticals, medical device and diagnostics across multiple disease and therapeutic areas.
