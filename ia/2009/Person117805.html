John Olival’s Summary

John has exposure to and experience in equity, money market and derivatives trading systems with knowledge of pre-trade and post-trade STP as well as trade and transaction publishing systems, e.g. Reuters. His skills include project management, product management and business development working with European, African and Asian countries.

As founder, John built Explican into a successful and profitable business focussed on Internet applications, such as B2B, eCommerce and eLearning. The business has subsequently been taken over by a respected Spanish firm, Novasoft. John oversaw all the companies UK and European (excl Iberia) activities, ensuring high levels of quality were achieved and maintained with all products offered.

John has led and managed local and international projects in the private and public sectors, having worked in and with the larger organisations like DaimlerChrysler, the Cabinet Office (Government Gateway standards) and Harvey Nichols, through to smaller established organisations and start-up companies.

John has significant experience in developing, implementing and managing internet-based solutions ranging from the complexity of secure n-tier architecture business applications to intricate design-sensitive front-ends. Product experience ranges from e-commerce and e-learning to e-health.

As a member of GAWDS and the UPAUK, John is acutely aware of and actively involved in solving and improving accessibility, usability and visibility issues in the Internet. Organisations that deliver on these aspects are well respected, and ultimately, profitable and successful. John strives to help organisations understand these principles, which ultimately benefit all parties concerned.

Studying an MBA at London Business School, with qualifications in mechanical engineering and a background in software programming John will give a good balance to any team, providing a wealth of management, technical and international experience.
