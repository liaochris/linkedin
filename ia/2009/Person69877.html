Peter Pappas’s Summary

I work with districts from across the nation as a consultant and staff trainer. I accept a limited number of projects assisting districts in initiatives designed to improve student performance and the quality of teaching and learning.

I reach out to educators through my suite of websites hosted on my two domains peterpappas.com and edteck.com. On a typical day I have over 1000 visitors with an average stay in excess of 10 minutes. The sites have attracted awards and links from over 800 sites including the Library of Congress.

Staff development, strategic planning, project management, learning environments, publications
