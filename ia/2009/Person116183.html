Joe Webster’s Summary

Over 20 years of telecom experience, from general management, marketing, sales, operations, and regulatory areas to network design, contract negotiation, service design, telecom expense management, mergers and acquisitions, funding, and start-up organizations. Have been President & CEO of large domestic and international telecom service providers.

Joe Webster’s Specialties:

Award winning entrepreneur and start-up expert with strong track record of building businesses to maximize investor return, nationally and internationally. Key strengths include early recognition and development of business opportunities based on fundamental industry trends, P & L management, strategic planning, sales and marketing, financing and operations. Experienced in Venture Capital funding, IPOs, M/As, P&L. Background in Broadband, CLEC, HFC, CATV, SDH, SONET, VOIP, TEM, & wireless. MBA.
