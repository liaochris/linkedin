Michael Tucker’s Summary

I am a Management Professional with 15 years of experience in the software and web industries. My career began before I graduated college with a web-based content driven company that I created. With that company, we realized success in our niche, and we later were acquired by a larger media company in the industry.

I have authored business plans and developed business processes that drive success at growing companies. I have conducted research and analysis, managed the development of products, marketing and business development, and international teams.
