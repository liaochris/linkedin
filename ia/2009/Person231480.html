Hana Shirt Co’s Summary

The Hana Shirt Co. is the world's leading retailer of vintage Hawaiian shirts. It is what we do, it is all we do !! We have been collecting vintage Hawaiian wear for over 30 years and are privileged to own the world's largest collection. On our vintage hawaiian shirts page you will find an array of the planet's coolest vintage aloha shirts, tribal shirts, tiki shirts and surf shirts.

If you are really lucky, you may even see Jimmy Buffett or Barry Obama wearing one of our finds !! If you have vintage aloha shirts to sell, we are always looking to buy vintage Hawaiian shirts, just email us for a free appraisal !!

We hope that you enjoy visiting our slice of paradise as much as we enjoy finding vintage Hawaiian & Aloha shirt treasures for you! Please feel free to contact us about vintage hawaiian shirts so we can share,

"A Little Aloha, One Shirt At A Time"TM

Mahalo

Hana Shirt Co’s Specialties:

Our Aloha Shirt Menehunes are out day and night searching for authentic vintage Hawaiian shirts. Hana's primo Hawaiian shirts are the treasures of years of travels and trading, they are the holy grail of wearable Aloha shirt art.

The pieces in this section are authentic vintage aloha shirts of the finest quality available anywhere. They were made in the 1930's - 1970's and are not reproductions.
