Peter Weijmarshausen’s Summary

My passion is to make new and exciting technology accessible for everyone.

Currently at Shapeways we are enabling co-creation through digital manufacturing. Using our portal 3D software users can have produced any product they design at acceptable prices.

At Aramiska and Sangine we designed and developed Satellite modems allowing people to easily access the internet through two-way Satellite connections. Enabling (mobile) broadband internet everywhere in Europe.

At Not a Number we gave away the Blender 3D software, allowing people to get access to a fully featured 3D modeler, animation package at no cost. Through our portal we enabled them to use it with tutorials and manuals.
