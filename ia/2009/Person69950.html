Eduardo Murillo’s Summary

Starting from specializing with graphic design, which encompassed both branding and marketing attributes, I continuously strive to progressively strengthen and enhance my career capabilities. Beginning with designing layouts for various web businesses and freelancing for numerous clients, the quality of work I produce, efficiency and reliability provided me an opportunity to work with one of Queenland’s leading web businesses.

While still in its humble beginnings from a small garage office on the Gold Coast with its two founding members, I contributed towards the reputable growth of the company and proved to be a pivotal member of the team. Between 2004 and 2007, the team which has now grown to over 8 employees and new offices worked with a variety of small, medium to large businesses.

From designing web concepts and logos my experiences now include, web design in CSS, liaison with clients, maintenance of sites, project manager, SEO and Google Adword’s online marketing. My continuous passion for learning and growth have now progressed to understanding programming and development which include Ruby and ASP.NET that are now being studied. From my experiences I have exceptional time and project management skills with ambitious drive to succeed.
