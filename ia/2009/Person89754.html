Gregory Herman-Giddens’s Summary

I am a member of the North Carolina estate planning firm, TrustCounsel. The goal of the firm is to provide our clients with exceptional service in the areas of wealth planning, protection and management. At TrustCounsel we maintain active knowledge of the current laws and best practices through attendance of national educational conferences and membership in leading professional organizations. We have two offices in North Carolina, located in Chapel Hill and Pittsboro.

TrustCounsel offers services in many areas including Estate Planning, Probate, Taxation, Trust and Estate Administration, Asset Protection, Guardianship, Business Formation, Charitable Gift Planning, and Non-Profit Organization Law.

I have been practicing law for close to 22 years, with over 16 years experience in Wealth Planning, Protection and Management. I am also the only attorney in North Carolina who has a Master of Laws in Estate Planning degree and is both a Board Certified Specialist in Estate Planning and a Certified Financial Planner.™ I am licensed to practice law in North Carolina, Florida and Tennessee.

Over the years, I have published articles in several publications including Taxation for Accountants; Taxation for Lawyers; North Carolina Lawyers Weekly, The North Carolina Elder Section Newsletter, and the North Carolina Estate Planning Blog (http://www.ncestateplanningblog.com/).

I also believe strongly in giving back to the community and I volunteer my time with Habitat for Humanity for Orange County, the Chatham County Chamber of Commerce and the North Carolina Center for Non-Profits Pro Bono Panel.
