Neil Dulohery’s Summary

Applying a background in science to help data-intensive organizations streamline their processes and gain an intuitive view of fragmented information. I am a SAS expert with over 20 years experience and SAS certified professional.

Supporting all aspects of information delivery, I specialize in designing and automating interactive graphical displays for decision support. My information dashboards consolidate diverse, actionable information into a single display and provide immediate access to supporting detail. Information dashboards can streamline decisions from executive offices to shop floors.

A well designed information dashboard brings actionable information to immediate attention and replaces many less effective reports. I design user interfaces that are lean and readable, presenting a high density of information from sophisticated models running automatically in the background.

When an organization needs to streamline and automate its processes, I design software and systems that render the technology as transparent as possible. User interfaces are made simple and intuitive. The sophistication of the software resides in the internal workings and business logic, where models refine and make sense of the raw data.

I can support your full project cycle from concept, process improvement, systems specification, development, testing, implementation to support. I invite you to visit Whitmarshinc.com and AutomateBI.com
