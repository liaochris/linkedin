Christel Gollnick’s Summary

A communications professional with a passion for big ideas and interest in the design and architecture of nearly anything and everything. I enjoy helping organizations achieve effectiveness and efficiency through visionary and focused thinking; clear, compelling and integrated communications; and practical partnerships.

Successful, committed, assertive, and common sense leader with nearly 20 years of marketing, media, public relations, and nonprofit management experience in multi-faceted local, regional, and national organizations.

A proven "vision-keeper," relationship-builder, complex problem solver, and change agent with a keen ability to take organizations through growth phases while improving quality, efficiency, and effectiveness through the responsible and respectful leverage of resources.
