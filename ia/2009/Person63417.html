Dick East’s Summary

Since 1965, Richard East has built his career in the oilfield supply and service industry. During these years, Mr. East has held executive level administrative duties for three oilfield and industrial supply firms. Under Mr. East’s direction, starting in 1989, East & Associates began providing professional environmental consulting and project management services to banking, financial, investment, real estate land development, construction, manufacturing, oil and gas, transportation, legal, governmental and other entities.

The key to the successful completion of a real estate transaction is to know the potential risk of exposure to any liabilities and to incorporate those risks in the evaluation. The most logical approach to handling these environmental hazards is to become informed about the problems so they can be appropriately managed.

Dick East’s Specialties:

•Phase I ESA reports for the investigation of environmental risk factors both and and/or near the subject property that could affect its value or use
•The ESA Letter Report is a custom-styled report that includes a limited physical review of the subject property. This report offers an economical savings over a Phase I ESA report
•An array of general environmental consulting services
•Formed strategic alliances with environmental consulting, contracting and service companies
