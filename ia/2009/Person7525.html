Alexander Meijers’s Summary

Hi all,

I have almost 15 years of experience in the ICT industry and i'm an experienced SharePoint Specialist. My knowlegde is widely around Microsoft Technology. This knowlegde is from hardcore development, execute and integrate small en large solutions at customers and architectural advice.

The last years i have been working on implementation or giving advice around SharePoint solutions at Sanoma Uitgevers, BAM, TNT, Facilicom, NS, Eneco, Logica (a complete renewed international intranet environment) and more.

I have been working as Senior Consultant at Tam Tam and Microsoft Product Expert For SharePoint / Solution Architect at Logica for the last 4 years. I also took part of a theme group around "The new world of work" which guides customers at optimalisation en improving their processes en working environment. Next to that i have done a lot of coaching of collegeas, in which they developed to medior and senior developers around SharePoint Technology.

Since september 2008 i have started the Dutch SharePoint User Group. A user group to bring people involved in SharePoint Technology together. Have a look at http://www.dutchsug.nl

I'm currently working at Sparked. My expertise's are Office System, SharePoint Technology, New world of work and Search.
