Dave Johnson’s Summary

I possess more than 10 years of expertise in branding, strategy, creative design and illustration for top-tier packaged consumer products and consumer focused advertising. The scope of my involvement includes directing/executing early conceptual designs; create marketing copy, conceptual packaging innovations, creation of original illustrations, and photo art direction & retouching. I also supervise and mentor junior designers and production artists. I am responsible for a considerable amount of the day-to-day objectives on my brand, as well as possess a deep understanding and passion for the strategy and goals of my clients.

Dave Johnson’s Specialties:

Concepts for online focus testing (many times concepts are created on very tight timelines). Expertise in digital illustration, retouching, and story-boarding for concept work. Expertise creating logos, nomenclature, design themes, etc. Expert character cartoonist/illustrator.
