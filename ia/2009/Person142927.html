Lloyd Starr’s Summary

As Vice President of Development, Lloyd Starr oversees the creation and quality assurance of software for the Beatport Digital Download Network, which includes more than ten core products and a number of ancillary web services, API’s, administrative tools and content management systems. Lloyd direct reports to the COO and serves as a member of the Executive Team. Lloyd is currently responsible for 30 employees and two offshore teams. Prior to being named Vice President of Development in 2007, Lloyd worked as one of Beatport’s original software developers. During this startup phase, Lloyd developed several flash based Graphical User Interfaces, including the integration of Beatport’s storefront into Native Instruments’ Traktor DJ software line.

Before joining the team at Beatport Lloyd was the Senior Flash Developer at Factory Design Labs, where he oversaw and developed Rich Internet Applications for several high-profile clients, including Adam Sandler, Happy Madison, Paramount, Sony, and Brine. Lloyd has also developed interactive pieces for Qwest, Sun Microsystems, Adobe, and IBM. While working as a freelance contractor, Lloyd developed cutting edge user interfaces including an Elevator case study that seamlessly communicated with an Executable UML model.
