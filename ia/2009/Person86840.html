Ginger Grant, PhD’s Summary

My focus is digital storytelling - the intersection point between the use of technology and the strategic use of story for branding or strategic initiatives. My passion is in the creation, design, development and implementation of innovative people programs that transform corporate culture. Working with high-performance teams, I use the Heroic Journey model as a foundational tool for digital storytelling, including internal and external branding initiatives.

Areas of expertise and applied research include:

• strategic intelligence and visual analytics
. corporate and marketing strategy
• cultural due diligence
• psychographics and demographics (predictive modelling)
• building high-performance cultures through strategic design
• organizational culture assessments and change management
• employment branding initiatives

Ginger Grant, PhD’s Specialties:

Author: 2009-Finding Your Creative Core. 2005-Re-Visioning The Way We Work. Specialist in digital storytelling, archetypal branding, relationship marketing, experiential marketing, employee engagement and retention and re-visioning retirement. Only Canadian in teacher/trainer group established by founder Michael Ray for the Stanford GSB "Creativity in Business" program. Further information can be found at http://www.mythsamongus.org
