Prashant Dixit’s Summary

I am a Communication Design graduate from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), (http://www.iitg.ernet.in). The IITs are counted among the premiere technical institutes in the world.

Currently employed with SAS R &D,Pune, India. SAS is the leader in business analytics software and services and the largest independent vendor in the business intelligence market.

As Senior UX designer/researcher,the premise of my responsibilities include conducting usability studies, design research, prototyping and producing detailed UI design specifications; ensuring user interface consistency within a multi-application suite; creating and maintaining usability standards; evaluating new technology.

Previously employed in the Internet industry-M/S Info Edge India Ltd (Naukri.com). Have worked on India's leading professional netowrking portal www.brijj.com as User Experience Designer.

Professional background entailed Interaction design as a consideration and design of the interface between user and the product, involving web based products, tangible products, multimedia etc.

Spheres of interest revolve around the pursuance of a career in the field of Information Visualization,UI / User-Experience design patterns, Interaction Design, Usability Engineering particularly that pertaining to Human-Computer Interaction and creative visualization.
