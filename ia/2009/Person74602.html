Eric Mersch’s Summary

Chief Financial Officer for Deer Valley Ventures, LLC. and portfolio company, DemandFlex, Inc., a virtualized managed services company. Former Chief Financial Officer (interim) for privately held RazorGator Interactive Group, Inc.Former Chief Financial Officer for VitalStream, Inc. (NASDAQ:VSTH) until February 20th, 2007 acquisition by Internap Network Services Corp. (NASDAQ:INAP). Former Vice President, Finance and CFO at Harrah’s Entertainment, Inc. (NYSE:HET) for Las Vegas casino resort properties, Harrah’s and Flamingo. Also served as VP, Finance for Harrah’s Western Division and Director of Corporate Finance; Former CFO of foodservice retailer, Andre-Boudin Bakeries, Inc.; Former Senior Associate, Corporate Finance at Robertson Stephens in the Internet Infrastructure Services group; Former Naval Submarine Officer assigned to USS Los Angeles (SSN-688); BS in Economics from the United States Naval Academy, Masters in Nuclear Power Engineering from the Submarine Officer Nuclear Engineering School and a Masters in Business Administration from Harvard Business School.

Eric Mersch’s Specialties:

Senior Financial Executive, specializing in mergers and acquisitions, turnaround & restructuring, financial planning & analysis, forensic accounting, taxation, and Sarbanes-Oxley. Technical knowledge of data center power, SaaS, server virtualization & cloud computing technologies.
