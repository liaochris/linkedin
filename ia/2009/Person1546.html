Dee Dee Cocheta’s Summary

Over 19 years in the entertainment industry specializing in interactive marketing, (promotions, public/media relations, publicity, product placement, event production, ad sales, media training and coaching). I have always been a freelancer, doing work on the side until I started my own of which I often get contracted to consult and handle positions at companies until they find someone to fill it (and usually am always asked, sometimes begged to stay on, which regretfully haven't found that one company to swoop me out of being an entreprenuer as of yet).

I am always in the know!!! The power I have is within my relationships which have proven to work when I am usually the "go to" person for my contacts, make the connections, broker ideas, get deals flowing, developed to completion. Another powerful asset - I am a very hands-on person - motivating and leading a team to execute! I can get clients or team members to do things they may have never liked to do or enjoy doing things they never thought of doing without realizing they are.
