Gopi Sangha’s Summary

I'm an independent individual who has a visionary perspective of pop-culture & entertainment in the 21st century, equipped with the know-how and skill sets to pragmatically realize the business potential of an ever changing, and growingly complex globalized culture. Creativity, research, know-how & hard work has allowed me to continuously inspire, innovate, and push the expectations from myself, from my employers & from my clients. Between non-profit organizing for Hip Hop Congress, founding & creating the HEAT Festival, weekly club promotions, artist development & running a thriving music blog, I have learned and developed a very broad set of skills, experience, & networking necessary for successful entertainment related business ventures & opportunities.

Gopi Sangha’s Specialties:

New Media Marketing, Event Consulting (Concerts/Festivals), Event Production, Branding & Identity, Talent Buying, Social Media PR, & Artist Management.

Gopi Sangha’s Experience

  • Owner

    East of L.A. Ent.

    (Entertainment industry)

    February 2006 — Present (3 years 1 month)

    East of L.A. is an independent marketing & entertainment company. It specializes in new media marketing & brand management and well as full service event management and consulting. Working alongside Universities & Colleges it serves as a one-stop concert/festival management & consulting service. It currently operates two weekly nightlife events in the Greater Los Angeles area along side a thriving music blog and represents the aspiring talents of: SPEAK, Pheo & DJ Alf Alpha. http://www.EastofLA.com

  • Founder & Producer, HEAT Festival

    University of California, Riverside

    (Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry)

    October 2006 — June 2008 (1 year 9 months)

    Founded and produced the HEAT Festival at U.C. Riverside in its monumental first two year's of operation. I led a team which brainstormed the event, prepared budgets, booked talent, designed, marketed, produced & executed the festival which attracted 6,000 guests in its first year and 10,500 in its second year.

    In its second year the HEAT Festival featured 3 concurrent stages, two of which under massive festival tenting, and a lineup composing of: 311, Mos Def, Moby, Girl Talk, The Clipse, Circa Survive, Lies In Disguise, Afrobots, Monte Negro, Aloe Blacc, DJ Low B, L.A. Riots, Villians, & DJ Morsy.

    2008 HEAT Website:
