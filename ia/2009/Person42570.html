Chip Burris’s Summary

Chip Burris has worked extensively with the Plumtree – Aqualogic Interaction (ALUI) portal product since version 3.5. He has assumed the roles of developer, technical architect, project manager, mentor, trainer, and analyst on a wide variety of portal implementations. His expertise with the product line is current with ALUI Interaction Version 6.5.

Mr. Burris has a broad knowledge of the internal workings of the ALUI Portal, Collaboration Server, Publisher and Studio. In his prior engagements Mr. Burris successfully modified the portal and collaboration functionality to fit interesting client requirements. These projects included major modifications to the user interface, adding additional functionality to the portal, as well as adding new content stores.

One of the most common feature requests by clients over the last few years is the desire to integrate existing Windows SharePoint Services, or SharePoint Server content into the ALUI enterprise portal infrastructure. To facilitate these requirements, Mr. Burris has worked extensively with the SharePoint product line to enable the integration of these two products. Often the SharePoint infrastructure would have to be rearchitected, web parts rewritten, and the SharePoint SDK used extensively to accomplish these clients goals.

This extensive knowledge of the portal makes Mr. Burris uniquely qualified for any role in a portal implementation project, and he has on many occasions filled a variety of project needs on the same engagement.

Due to the nature of an enterprise portal and its need to reach its tentacles into every aspect of an IT organizational structure, Mr. Burris has had to become knowledgeable, to the point of having convincing expertise, in other subject matters such as routers, firewalls, server provisioning, Active Directory, LDAP, network traffic analysis, capacity planning, managing executives and business sponsors, etc..
