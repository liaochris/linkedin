Bojan Boskovic’s Summary

An engineer and scientist with more than 10 years experience in carbon nanotube synthesis and applications from both industry and academia. Extensive network of contacts in the field.

Bojan Boskovic’s Specialties:

R&D - carbon nanotube synthesis and applications.
Carbon nanotube aerospace, electrochemical and electronic applications.
Collaborative R&D management.

Bojan Boskovic’s Experience

  • CNT R&D Manager

    Nanocyl SA

    (Nanotechnology industry)

    February 2009 — Present (6 months)

    Manager, Carbon Nanotube Reserach & Development at Nanocyl. Responsible for carbon nanotube synthesis and application oriented internal and collaborative R&D at Nanocyl, one of leading carbon nanotube manufacturing companies.

  • Principal Engineer - Carbon Scientist

    Meggitt PLC

    (Public Company; MGGT; Aviation & Aerospace industry)

    July 2006 — December 2008 (2 years 6 months)

    Principal Engineer - Carbon Scientist at Meggitt Aircraft Braking Systems in Coventry, UK.
    Carbon nanotube expert, leading R&D projects relevant to carbon nanotube aerospace applications.
    - Introduced carbon nanomaterials and developed process for in situ growth of carbon nanotubes and nanofibres for aircraft brake disc application (IP and delivery of carbon nanotube carbon-carbon composite brake disc proof-of-concept project).

  • Reserach Associate

    University of Cambridge

    (Educational Institution; Research industry)

    June 2003 — June 2006 (3 years 1 month)

    Carbon nanotube synthesis at low temperature and carbon nanotube - carbon fibre composites.
    - Produced and characterised the first carbon fibre epoxy composites with in situ grown carbon nanotubes and nanofibres (project sponsored by Boeing).
    - Optimised DC PECVD method for carbon nanotube synthesis on carbon fibres at low temperature (B. O. Boskovic et al, Carbon 43, 2005, 2643-2648).
    - Established collaboration with John Hart from MIT regarding carbon nanotube synthesis on 3D surfaces (A. J. Hart, B. O. Boskovic et al, Nanotechnology 2006, 17, 1397-1403).
    - Investigated carbon nanotube electronic interconnects applications (B. O. Boskovic & J. Robertson, in 3D Nanoelectronic Computer Architecture and Implementation, 2004, 139)

  • Senior Specialist

    Morgan Crucible PLC

    (Mechanical or Industrial Engineering industry)

    October 2001 — April 2003 (1 year 7 months)

    Carbon nanotube synthesis and carbon nanotube - carbon fibre composites.
    - Invented process for in situ growth of carbon nanotubes and nanofibres within carbon fibre cloth (GB Patent 2399092).
    - Introduced carbon nanomaterials and analysed the company potential and opportunities for carbon nanomaterials. Delivered market research report and set-up a laboratory.
