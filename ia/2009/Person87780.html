Gloria Quinn’s Summary

Gloria Quinn is an Account Supervisor in the Public Affairs Group at Ogilvy Public Relations Worldwide. She focuses on media relations, strategy and execution, program development and account management. Gloria’s current and recent clients include Iceland Naturally, Grant Thornton, LexisNexis, and Barclays Global Investors. Her work with Iceland Naturally has resulted in two media tours and coverage in the following top-tier media outlets; US Airways Magazine, MSNBC.com, and Travel & Leisure.com. Her work with Grant Thornton has resulted in features in the Associated Press, Reuters, and WSJ.com.

Prior to joining Ogilvy, Gloria worked at cho HighWater Group, a boutique PR agency that specializes in the interactive entertainment and electronics industry. During her three years at cho HighWater Group, Gloria managed PR programs for several accounts such as iHome, maker of the best-selling iPod accessories, and leading video game publishers Atari Inc., Majesco Entertainment Company, Midway Games Inc., and Her Interactive Inc. Gloria’s responsibilities centered on creating and implementing comprehensive PR plans for her clients, including numerous media tour events throughout New York, Chicago, Los Angeles, and San Francisco.

In addition, Gloria was charged with organizing and staffing large-scale trade shows such as the Electronic Entertainment Expo (E3), American International ToyFair, and the International Consumer Electronics Show (CES).

Gloria has secured numerous top-tier media placements for her previous clients in broadcast and print outlets. Her top national broadcast placements include segments featuring her clients on Good Morning America, E! Entertainment News, BusinessWeek Weekend and MTV. She has also coordinated corporate interviews and secured features in top newspapers such as USA Today, The New York Times, and The Wall Street Journal.
