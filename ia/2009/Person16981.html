Anne Colas’s Summary

Pharmacist specialized in hospital pharmacy, I initially worked in retail and hospital pharmacy. Later on, I acquired an M.B.A. and joined the pharmaceutical industry, working for Merck Frosst Canada during 21 years.

During those years, I worked in the following departments:

- Market Research
- Sales
- Human Ressources
- Regulatory Affairs
- Medical Services
- Health Science Services (Medical Liaison)

and acquired experience, among other things, working with computers.

I speak and write French and English. I also have a good knowledge of Spanish.

Goal: Work for a company and in a position where the experience I have gained over the years will be of benefit for both.
