  • Internship - CAD Engineer -Designer

    GMPT-SE

    (Automotive industry)

    2005 — 2005 (less than a year)

    Objective 1. Detailed studies of manual transmissions and the evaluation of shift comfort as well as getting familiar with the design department, assembly and manufacturing plant of SAAB in Trollhattän and Gothenburg. I eventually worked on the last proposed facelifts of the SAAB 9.3 of which later the final design was chosen.
    Objective 2. Making a visualisation of gears when shifting within a new gearbox design. After trying each of the proposed programs I eventually created a successful visualisation by using Adams which therefore become the standard program for visualizations.
    Objective 3. As part of my period at GMPT-SE I worked on the benchmarking of European and Asian gearboxes within General Motors to reduce the large amount of different transmissions and bring forward a selected group for future (shared) use.
