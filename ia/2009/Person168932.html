    July 1997 — June 1998 (1 year)

    AIESEC (pronounced as one word; originally an acronym for Association Internationale des Étudiants en Sciences Économiques et Commerciales) is an international, non-profit, non-political, organisation run by students and recent graduates of institutions of higher education. It's international office is currently in Rotterdam, Netherlands.
