Samir Sharma’s Summary

Current Projects:

Imagine London - brings all these people together for all their insights / ideas / thoughts. Involving every citizen, young and old, all ethnicities and genders, apply their talents to create a positive future for themselves, their community and for London. It will be a place where connections come alive and human lives are connected, where those people without a voice can be heard and their visions, ideas and energies are taken into account.

Advising a Mayoral election campaign - campaign strategic planning, team dynamics, leadership development, social media, themes development, community engagement

Is extremely interested in bringing greater collaboration in tri-sector partnerships between - government, civil society and the private sector.

The idea being that all of us have to work together, to pool our ideas and resources, to solve complex social problems, whether it is climate change, migration, urban renewal, poverty, or social exclusion, just to name a few. No one has all the answers.

One way to look at the tri-partnership approach is to view all partners and their diverse strengths – complementary resources, knowledge and skills – to jointly address the complexities surrounding social, environmental and economic development.

If you have the same ideas and cravings to make things better please let me know and we can work towards the greater good.

Completed his assignment in the Caribbean, programme managing a large transformation effort for the Government of the Republic of Trinidad & Tobago. Working towards becoming a successful and highly competent Organisational Analyst.

Also works closely with charities around the world on their fundraising activities. Is a highly successful Independent Charity Auctioneer with 5 years experience in organising, planning and delivering fund raising events. Sold the most expensive truffle in the world - $330,000!
