Laurence W. Norjean’s Summary

Norjean works in the media, advertising, & entertainment industries. Producing, aggregating content and technology for the broadcast, online, & mobile industries; plus producing live events, sponsorships & integrated media campaigns.

Clients include advertisers/agencies & media companies; WPP, GroupM, Mindshare, Saatchi & Saatchi, OMD/Omnicom, Havas, Winstar, Virgin, Telemundo, Time Warner, etc.

-Prior to Norstar, Norjean was CEO/Co-founder of FMiTV Networks, (broadband, entertainment & syndication.) technically driving & supplying content to major media partners: NBC, Earthlink, MSN, Terra/LYCOS, Bell South, Clear Channel & Comcast, etc.

-Norjean transitioned to Inxsys & FMITV after serving as President/COO of StratiComm America, a creative/marketing group - clients: CBS & ABC Networks, Westwood One, Orion Pictures, Interep, Katz Media, Crain Publications & Nielsen, etc.

-Norjean served as EVP (CMO), National Sales & Marketing, Radio Advertising Bureau, (Radio industry trade association) as Senior Marketing/Sales strategist, spokesperson & business development head.

-At Metromedia, Norjean - Director of National / Local Advertising, PR & Sales Promo for 60 Radio & TV stations. He previously worked at Metromedia News - WNYW-TV - NYC.

-Norjean started in theatrical film production, marketing, & promotion at Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, becoming Syndication Dir. for MPC-Wolper Productions, working on the National Geographic series, Untamed World, Cousteau Series, Hallmark Hall of Fame, Merv Griffin, etc...

•International Speaker on Media/Marketing - NAB (US & Europe), CES, Digital Hollywood, Nikkei Shinbun, Streaming Media, American Banking Association, RAB, 150+ Broadcasting & Entertainment, Media & Marketing Conferences in the US, S. America, Europe & Asia

•Author, “Consumer Cycle Marketing”

•Author, “The Essential Radio Spot,” – (Broadcast Marketing & Creative)

•Guest Editor - Ad Age, Adweek, Brandweek, Mediaweek
