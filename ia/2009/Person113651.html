Jitender Vashisht’s Summary

A passionate learner and implementer in the Supply Chain Management field. A thought generator and motivator who is committed to advancing the field and those professionals in it.

I started my career with TATA Motors, Commercial vehicles business unit. I have enriched experience of working in different verticals ranging from Vendor Management to rural marketing of TATA Vehicles, Demand Management to IT integration. Joined Passenger Car Business Unit of TATA Motors in production planning department, Indica plant. There I managed different roles such as Marketing Liaison Officer, Central Production Planner, IT projects implementer. I was also involved with various operational trainings for operators for new IT systems.

Primary achievements were process mapping and improvement, wastage reduction, scorecards development, business risk identification and mitigation. I also initiated Poka Yoke and total quality management approach in Production planning department.

I joined John Deere India in April'07. There I was working in offshore model of providing support services to John Deere Units USA in the area of Production planning, demand management, forecasting, Bill of Material design and Inventory Management. I also trained new employees in SAP and other John Deere IT tools.

Post MBA I have joined Britannia India Limited in its Middle East operations. Here I am working as AM Supply Chain and Logistics. Apart from planning the complete outbound logistics I am primarily involved in value stream mapping of complete logistics function, reducing cost at different nodes, analyzing the working capital for supply chain and defining inventory norms for ingredients and packing material.
