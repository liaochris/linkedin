Kevin Francis’s Summary

Personal profile

I spent my early years as an officer in the British Army developing the finer arts of leadership, people management, personnel development, technical strategy and planning in the IT / telecommunications area. After arriving in NZ 14 years ago, I have been involved in a variety of senior management and executive roles in the telecommunications, IT and Online Marketing industries.

The corporate experience has been rewarding with a variety of increasingly senior management roles, culminating in General Management roles for Asia Online, Ericsson Enterprise and Online Marketing Consultancy, First Rate.

My passion is people excellence and how best to leverage this to deliver bottom line results. I'm often described as the "make it happen" person. My drive to succeed has always seen me outperform my peers and exceed my performance goals.

I have a quick, intuitive mind that combines well with a pragmatic and open approach to problem solving. Thinking on your feet skills, honed by years as an officer in the Army in situations as intense as the Gulf War, have served me well in overcoming the trials of corporate life.

By maintaining humour and a sense of perspective, leading teams through periods of change has been a key success, often repeated. I thrive on the challenges and rewards that are the mainstay of the senior manager’s role and enjoy the variety and intensity that those challenges often represent. Integrity, determination, humour, perspective and passion are traits that I apply whatever I am engaged in.

Kevin Francis’s Experience

  • Chief Executive Officer

    Q New Zealand Limited

    (Online Media industry)

    July 2009 — Present (3 months)

    Q Ltd owns a group of specialist interactive advertising and digital marketing communication companies. Each company is a leader in their area of expertise.

    The two main specialist companies in NZ are First Rate and 3D Interactive.

    First Rate is an internet marketing consultancy that provides online strategy, search engine marketing (SEM), search engine optimisation (SEO), email marketing and performance-based online advertising and tracking services direct to a range of clients.

    First Rate helps clients develop online business strategies and execute targeted online campaigns, including permission marketing through blogs, content feeds, email and mobile phones and performance marketing across a network of websites. Clients can also measure the extent of their customers' interaction with their online activities through customised Google Analytics (GA) implementations, First Rate being the first NZ GA qualified company and having the highest number of GA qualified consultants in NZ.

    First Rate has offices in Auckland and Sydney.

    3D Interactive is an interactive advertising sales network that provides sales representation for a network of Australasia's premier permissioned databases and websites to advertising agencies and their clients.

    3D Interactive also helps advertisers and clients create, manage and track integrated advertising campaigns across highly targeted email, mobile networks, websites, wireless and other interactive media, as well as represent database and website owners' advertising and media placement interests.

    3D Interactive has offices in Sydney, Melbourne and Auckland
