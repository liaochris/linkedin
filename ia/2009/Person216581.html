Sheryl Roush’s Summary

Internationally top-rated trainer (8 countries) in communication skills, specializing in attitude-boosting programs for creating positive work environments and bringing your heart to work. (www.SparklePresentations.com)

Known for high-content, highly-engaging keynotes, she has presented on programs alongside Olivia Newton-John, Geena Davis, Art Linkletter, Howard Putnam (Southwest Airlines), Marcus Buckingham (Gallup Org.), James Ray (The Secret) Mark Victor Hansen (Chicken Soup), Robert G. Allen (One Minute Millionaire), Jane Seymour, Suze Orman and more.

She was only the third woman in the world to be honored with Toastmasters International's highest award for speaking caliber, the Accredited Speaker, earned by only 58 of 4 million members in 93 countries.

Sheryl has been a Professional Member in the National Speakers Association since 1990, and served on the San Diego Chapter Board for 13 years, as well as Hospitality Chair for the 2007 National Convention. She is a Past President of the San Diego Professional Coaches Alliance, affiliated with the International Coach Federation.

Sheryl is a 12-time author including Heart of a Woman, Heart of a Mother, Heart of the Holidays, Corazon de Mujer (Spanish), and Heart of a Woman in Business. (www.HeartBookSeries.com)
