Keith Forbes’s Summary

Fourteen years in climate change, corporate sustainability reporting, and sustainable development - backstopping, developing, managing, and implementing projects. Expertise includes climate change (impacts, adaptation measures, stakeholder outreach, mitigation, GHG inventories), climate change and land use, environmental governance, and corporate social responsibility (CSR).

I work with federal agencies in the U.S. as well as private sector clients. Multi-lingual with experience in Czech Republic, Poland, Portugal, and Zambia.

Member of the UNFCCC roster of experts for the U.S., an expert reviewer of the United Nations' Millenium Ecosystem Assessment, and occasional lecturer on climate change at Skidmore College.

Keith Forbes’s Specialties:

carbon sequestration, boundary development for greenhouse gas mitigation projects, afforestation, reforestation, greenhouse gas inventories, vulnerability and adaptation to climate change, climate change impact assessment, sustainability reporting, global reporting initiative, aarhus convention, access to information, stakeholder management, stakeholder outreach
