Mike Boyd’s Summary

My name is Mike Boyd and I’m an entrepreneur.

I live in Brisbane, Queensland Australia.

I have strong interests in innovative green business concepts, eco-products and business+environmental sustainability.

I am currently undertaking my 4th year of study in two Bachelor’s Degrees full time at the University of Queensland; Bachelor of Business Management & Bachelor of Applied Science (Integrated Resource Management).

I own and run a business in Brisbane called Coolybar - which specialises in mobile keg hire and frozen daiquiri/slushy machine hire for parties. We pride ourselves on delivering the VERY BEST service and the highest quality products in Queensland to the party hire market.

Coolybar’s keg dispensing systems are Queensland made for the Queensland climate and we believe that they are second to none. The coldest, clearest and most refreshing beer EVERY TIME. Head over now (no pun intended) and check us out. Don’t forget to bookmark the page (Ctrl+D), you never know when you’ll next get the urge for icy-cold, golden beer on tap!

When I apply myself to something, I try and try again until I achieve a level of excellence that I’m satisfied with. Confidence is everything! Agree or not, this is me - Mike Boyd.

Don’t get me wrong, I am not perfect. I don’t always win. I don’t always achieve. I quite often FAIL. But time and time again I continue to TRY. I work harder, research further, learn new skills and TRY AGAIN! “Failing something is merely motivation to succeed next time”. Never, ever, ever, ever, EVER give up! The rewards are waiting for the successful minority who are not afraid of hard work, dedication and perserverance!
