Bala Pillai’s Summary

Some people make the world happen, more watch the world happen, most wonder what happened. If the percentage of "make the world happen" types goes below a required threshold in a society, the crab mentality (aka stupidity) destroys progress. I unabashedly make the world happen and aikido the crabs.

"Zooming towards instantaneous synchronization of minds that matter" is my mantra. Social entrepreneur, corporate strategist and generalist with track record in leadership, articulation and inventiveness.

Am strong in conceptualising and in applying common sense eg "Problems = Opportunities; No Problems = No Opportunities", because of the combination of a) experiential learning (taking lots of risks/failing well across many cultures b) love for synthesis and pattern language and (c)conscious low resistance to learning

Am an avid connector of people. Particularly strong in connecting to (a)connectors (b)those in the social networks business anywhere in the world and (c)Malaysia, Singapore, India, Bay Area, Sydney and China.

My view: our goal is or is a subset of having us accelerate towards frictionless synchronization of and smooth conductivity between minds.

I am clued on, on the levers that give us the "best bang for the buck" for this. Methods include strategic change management leaders, next generation social networks, collective intelligence, emergent networked organisation structures, collaborative filtering, visualization, online-orchestrated communities, presence (eg Instant Messaging), online project management, foresight exchanges, business models metas, analytics/algorithms, semiotics, imagination, courage and sentience.
