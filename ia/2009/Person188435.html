Peter Kimmich’s Summary

I am currently a marketing copywriter for a medium-sized company based in Hollywood, and I'm also a freelance music journalist.

I'm constantly researching the latest know-how in copywriting, and am currently open to freelance opportunities in that field, too. My journalism work has appeared in publications like the pop-culture/fashion magazine Metro.Pop, the national independent magazine Synthesis, the award-winning student paper The Orion, and the small-town newspaper Chico News & Review, among others.

I have covered news, film, people and art, but my passion is music. I also have a background in Internet marketing, as well as being a copy editor, photographer and blogger. I've interviewed artists such as Lemmy Kilmister, Alanis Morissette, Jerry Cantrell, the Low Flying Owls, and many others.

You can find my most current work on my music blog, Monitor Down, as well as on any of my company's blogs. I also recommend visiting my company's Web site, for which I also did copy work and conceptualization. Cheers!
