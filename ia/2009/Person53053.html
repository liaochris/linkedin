Dan Goodman’s Summary

Since 1991, I have architected software, written patents and raised investment capital. I have been personally responsible for taking new products from concept through completion and market launch. My philosophy is that technology should be designed for immediate use – no instructions necessary

I am a stakeholder in a company that holds an issued patent for technology to process PIN debit card transactions on the Internet.

Brick and mortar retailers pay a fixed fee -- only pennies -- per PIN debit card transaction. On the Internet, however, debit cards are processed like credit cards. Because PIN debit transactions aren't currently possible, online merchants must pay a variable transaction fee based on a percentage of the sale amount. It is estimated that this costs Internet retailers close to a billion dollars annually in unnecessary debit card processing charges. Patent # 6980970 can change this.

More information is available by visiting my website: http://www.GoodmanProject.com
