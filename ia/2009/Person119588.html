Jon Pierce’s Summary

Hacker and startup guy experienced in building large web apps and leading teams. Cofounder of Betahouse, a coworking space for entrepreneurs, developers and designers, and Virosity, a small web development shop that works with startups.

Areas of Interest:
Social software, location-based services, maps, mobile applications, music, widgets, analytics, big data, visualization, search, NLP, collective intelligence, the open web (OpenID, OAuth, microformats, FOAF, Jabber / XMPP, OpenSocial), the (lowercase!) semantic web, (distributed | cloud | ubiquitous) computing

Jon Pierce’s Specialties:

Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Python, Django, Java, Java EE, Solr, Lucene, Sphinx, Spring, Hibernate, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, XHTML, CSS, XML, RSS / Atom, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Memcached, Apache, Nginx, Amazon Web Services (EC2, S3, SQS, SimpleDB), Git, Subversion, OS X, Linux / Unix

Jon Pierce’s Experience

  • Founder and Partner

    Virosity

    (Internet industry)

    April 2007 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    Virosity is a small web development shop specialized in building applications for early-stage startups. For our first client, good2gether, we built a platform that connects local nonprofits and causes to visitors at major media sites across the web. Within its first few months, it had already been deployed to four of the top ten newspaper sites in the country, Boston.com, SFGate.com, Chron.com and AJC.com, with more on the way.

  • Founder

    Betahouse

    (Internet industry)

    March 2007 — Present (2 years)

    Betahouse is a coworking space in which entrepreneurs, developers and designers collaborate, share ideas and expertise and make connections. Instead of working at home, out of coffee shops or in a more traditional office space, we work alongside each other in a shared environment. We also regularly host community-oriented events such as user group meetings, all-day hackfests and Jellies (free coworking days). We've received press in the Boston Globe, the Christian Science Monitor, Network World and GigaOM among others.
