Brad Elpers’s Summary

Branding strategist... in other words advanced planning with clients to effectively meet needs, reduce costs, and develop a creative and winning environments. How? BGLG works with all the branding needs of our clients, like: outfit company or organization with branded apparel; produce logo'd giveaways for brand recognition at tradeshows and events; produce promotional products for new product launches to create brand recognition; screenprint and embroider apparel for brand recognition or group identity; produce mass signage like magnets, floor advertisements, yard signage, decals, labels, etc. for awareness, P.O.P., or signature branding; develop simple incentive programs for revenue increase, recognition, and safety programs; produce promotional products and apparel for sporting and recreational events; produce promotional products, awards, and apparel for corporate or group meetings; produce branded books for group or corporate giveaways; produce branded lines of products or apparel for corporate, group, or retail needs; provide complete fullfillment programs, including web development, warehouseing, and real-time inventory; provide creative solutions to branding needs; we do all this, and we do it well!

Branding! You need to brand something? Call Big Guy Little Guy Promotions, based in Overland Park, Kansas. Our clients appreciate working with Big Guy Little Guy Promotions because we provide great service and we make their jobs easier! We take care of our clients, and we have fun doing it.
