Roy de Souza, ZEDO Ad Serving’s Summary

ZEDO is the third largest publisher Ad Server in the world. It provides ad serving and award winning Ad Network Optimization to Social Networks and the New Internet.

ZEDO's extensive Ad Serving features are built specifically for social networks and high volume site needs. It has unique features like advanced Ad Network Optimization that increase revenue from the unique traffic of these new sites. ZEDO Ad Serving also makes it much easier to analyze, understand and manage your advertising revenue.

The new ZEDO Ad Network sells high CPM behavioral ads for publishers that use ZEDO as an Ad Server. It pays the highest CPMs in the New Internet world.

ZEDO ad serving is also used by ad networks.
