Thomas J. McSorley’s Summary

--Managing Partner in Switzerland
--University Professor in Belgium, Finland, and Vietnam
--Gentleman Farmer in Italy

Personal international management background includes successful "hands-on" resident work assignments in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Greece, The Netherlands, Germany, Belgium, United Kingdom, and Italy. Passport has been stamped in over forty-three (43) countries with more than eighty (80) round-trip journeys over the North Atlantic Ocean; and more than twelve (12) round-trip journeys over the Indian Ocean. Live in Magliano Sabina (RI), Italy, and maintain a loft condo in Brussels (Ixelles), Belgium.

The Bourget International Group helps companies and government agencies enter new markets, establish operations worldwide, and secure capital for international expansion and acquisitions. Our human resource pool is extensive and multifaceted allowing us to provide services encompassing all areas of economic activity.

We offer professional know-how and services for short and long term projects in diverse areas of international business development. For over 20 years now, bilateral and multilateral organizations, NGOs, foundations, academic institutes and private clients have relied on Bourget’s expertise.

We have worked in business and in government service in over forty-three countries, in every region of the world. We understand the dynamics of international business ventures, how foreign governments work, how they make policy, and how culture affects their decisions.

The Office of the Managing Partners in Zug, Switzerland, manages dedicated multi-lingual, multi-cultural, multi-functional Project Leaders, Client Service Liaisons, and Research Analysts, to support our experienced Business Development Management Team. Key fulfillment and delivery is provided by the Principals and The Office of the Managing Partners, who determine investment intensity and resource choreography ensuring organizational alignment worldwide.

Thomas J. McSorley’s Specialties:

The Bourget International Group is a unique boutique investing and consulting service specializing in financial management innovation aligning export strategy with sustainable profitability. Bourget innovation leverages the client product portfolio for greater product success rates and overall return on investment. We deliver project management and general contracting as BOT (Build-Operate-Transfer), as well as coordinate between Governments, Financial Institutions and Industry.
