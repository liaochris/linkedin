China Rose’s Summary

Zodiac Muse and Feng Shui Advisor

China Rose is a feng shui expert who uses only the best of the east and west to demonstrate quantifiable results. This is a hybrid, fusion style of the art form referred to as NuVo feng shui by professional practitioners.

As Creative Director of her private company, China Rose - designs, produces and delivers world class feng shui products, services and media to the marketplace. Her main goal is to share the best methods for cultivating a lucky lifestyle with her global audience and international clientele.

The art of placement (feng shui) allows her to use a full range of personal and professional talents and abilites; acting, writing, painting, costuming, interior and landscape design and syndicated, online publishing.

Her products have been sold in Trader Joe's, Ralph's, Von's, Home Depot, FTD and many other independent locations. Currently, China Rose has lucky products in Whole Foods Markets.
