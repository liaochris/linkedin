Andrew Ford’s Summary

Andrew is an Associate with Booz & Company based in the Canberra office. He is a trade and industry economist by profession and has over 12 years experience working as a consultant in knowledge and information management, intelligence and information architecture. This has included IT strategic planning, process improvement and e-government solutions, including at the whole-of-government level.

Andrew Ford’s Specialties:

Andrew has extensive experience using IT as a catalyst for organisational change and successfully managing the interface between business and technology. He is a “big picture” person, able to think out-of-the-box and strategically while also being equipped with solid project management skills. Andrew is a thought leader in knowledge management, intelligence and information architecture, and is a superb communicator, having presented at both national and international conferences.
