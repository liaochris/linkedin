Prasoon Ranjan’s Summary

Prasoon has over 10 years of experience in the IT industry across all aspects of the software value chain starting from product development to his current role helping with software license sales. Prasoon has worked at world leading organisations in the IT industry such as Oracle, Siebel, Infosys, Deloitte and BaaN R&D (now Infor) and spent almost 8 years working in the field of CRM. Prasoon experience spans across strategy (business and IT), outsourcing, process and technology.

Since, 2001 Prasoon has consulted with several leading Telco's in the world advising them on their convergence strategy, enabling IP Triple Play, Customer Centricity, IT transformation, CRM strategy, and CRM implementations.

Prasoon has an MBA from INSEAD. While at INSEAD Prasoon won the prestigious David Hall Trophy at the European Case Challenge competition held at Said Business School, Oxford University and conducted by European Case Clearing House in 2005.
