James Sack’s Experience

  • Owner and GM

    BOGO Blue Value Card, Inc.

    (Fund-Raising industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    The BOGO Blue Value Card enables non-profit groups to raise more money, faster and easier, than ever before! It is a fundraising coupon card that folds down accordion-style, and conveniently fits in the customer’s wallet or billfold. There are more than 85 discounts offered by restaurants, entertainment, and goods & services merchants in the Pikes Peak region (Colorado Springs, Pueblo, Cañon City, Falcon, Monument, & Castle Rock).

    BOGO Blue combines convenience (no more forgetting that large coupon book at home or in the car), with great rewards (whitewater rafting trips, limo rides, skating parties, the Royal Gorge Bridge’s SkyCoaster), no risk (card sales are on consignment), a unique product (no more logistical problems with delivering frozen food, braided bread, cookie dough, or chocolate), ease (finally a product that non-profit groups are proud to sell, and that people want to buy!), and high profits for groups (each $20 sale nets a $10 profit).
