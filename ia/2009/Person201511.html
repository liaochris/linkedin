Robin Speculand’s Summary

Robin Speculand is founder and Chief Executive of Bridges Business Consultancy International, pioneering in the field of strategy implementation. Widely acknowledged as a thought leader in implementation, Robin has written the bestselling books "Bricks to Bridges - Make Your Strategy Come Alive", and "Turning It On - Stories to Ignite, Excite and Engage" and will publish this summer "Beyond Strategy - a leaders role in its implementation" with Wiley.

Robin's work begins once clients have crafted their strategy and are beginning the implementation process, adopting Bridges' proprietary Implementation Compass, a framework for effective strategy implementation.

Robin Speculand’s Specialties:

Robin is a specialist in implementing strategy.

He is also a masterful event facilitator and an engaging keynote speaker. His work has been featured widely in the media, including BBC Global, CNBC and the Financial Times.
