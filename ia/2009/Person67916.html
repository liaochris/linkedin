David Newton’s Summary

David brings a holistic background in talent management that encompasses all aspects of the talent management cycle from employee value proposition development up to and including the acquisition and retention of top performers for leading organizations.

In addition to the over 6 years of direct recruitment experience in high profile assignments, in both internal human resource roles and as an external agency partner, his experience in campus recruitment and marketing with Canada’s leading business school, and in a business to business marketing role with a global outsourcing firm, he has developed a consultative solutions approach to meet talent management challenges at all levels and various industries.

Expertise with leading global firms – supported recruitment and business service sales initiatives to Fortune 500 companies. David understands the target labour market trends and how to communicate value propositions to varied audiences - from senior management to recent college graduates. He has developed innovative programs including partnerships with associations, supported the communication and logistics to reach out to these various audiences.

Branding and Messaging – His ongoing work with advertising agencies and creative professionals for recruitment projects along with the associated technology tools gives David a high comfort level in this space and has a proven ability to manage projects to tight deadlines and budgets.

Human Resources Knowledge – Current human resources and thought leadership in talent management, in education and career experience in various Human Resources capacities, gives David the latest first-hand insight into organizations' challenges.

David understands that a successful business operation starts with attracting the right employees - promoting a corporate culture that sparks interest, whether it is in face-to-face interviews or in crafting messages and campaigns that connect with potential candidates.
