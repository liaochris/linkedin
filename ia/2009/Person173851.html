Nicholas Kaposhilin’s Summary

Nicholas is a Principal Consultant with Paragon Consulting Group, a global leader in Business Performance Management. He is also the Managing Director of Paragon Consulting Asia Pacific.

Nicholas has over 12 years of experience designing and implementing Business Performance Management applications for clients in corporate finance and operations including applications for enterprise planning, forecasting and reporting. He has worked with clients and partners in The United States, Canada, Australia, Singapore, Hong Kong, China, Thailand, Indonesia, The Philippines, Africa and South America.

Nicholas has specific expertise in enterprise data management architecture and enterprise application implementation as it relates to integrated BPM frameworks that leverage OLAP, data warehousing and web technologies to provide financial and operational planning, activity-based planning, financial reporting, consolidation and reporting. He has direct experience implementing these solutions in the following industries: Mining, Chemicals, Shipping, Port Management, Television Media, Telecommunications, Consumer Packaged Goods, Consumer Beverages, Consumer Electronics Manufcaturing and Sales and Airlines.
