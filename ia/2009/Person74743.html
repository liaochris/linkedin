Eric Reeder’s Summary

A distinguished PMP and PgMP who began his career in retail operations, Eric has strategized, managed and actively participated in every phase of the software development life cycle (SDLC). His approach to building and mentoring teams has resulted in several successful multi-million dollar initiatives and numerous small and mid-sized development successes. An accomplished strategist and leader, Eric's vision for Terminus is to become a go to consultancy for high quality services committed to the client's budget objectives.
