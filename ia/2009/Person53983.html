Daniel Micol’s Summary

I am a Software Development Engineer at Microsoft's Search Technology Center in Europe, part of the Bing Search Engine Team. Concretely, I'm working in the Web Relevance group located in Munich, Germany.

In the past, I was also a Software Development Engineer at Microsoft Corporation's headquarters in Redmond, WA (USA), working in the Full-Text Search Team of SQL Server. During this time I shipped SQL Server 2008.

Prior to joining Microsoft as a full-time employee I was an intern in the SQL Server Relational Engine group. Besides this, I did other internships in organizations such as the California Institute of Technology and the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN)

I am also pursuing my Ph.D. in Natural Language Processing remotely at the University of Alicante, in Spain.

Daniel Micol’s Specialties:

Information Retrieval, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Databases/SQL, Algorithmics.
