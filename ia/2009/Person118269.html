John Merrifield 213.435.3832’s Summary

My name is John Merrifield. I'm a creative marketing & branding oriented print & web designer in Los Angeles.

Check out the new Christian Audigier Watches website I produced this fall at: http://www.christianaudigierwatches.com - one of many sites I worked on this year.

Also be sure to check out my personal portfolio website at: http://www.johnthecreative.com

Over the past 10 years, I have been a professional commercial artist, production artist, production manager, account manager, graphic designer, digital prepress and printing specialist, website creator, Flash animator, and ActionScript programmer. Through this diversity, I have developed my strongest skill - the ability to adapt to the demands of today's changing mediums and apply them to the job at hand.

My goal has always been to become the Art Director of a large advertising firm, but lately I've been leaning in other directions as well, as I am becoming increasingly involved in visual effects and motion graphics, film, video, and interactive media.
