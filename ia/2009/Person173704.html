Niall Mccormack’s Summary

Coming from a traditionally trained design background and having a strong interest in technological development, interaction design and animation gives me a unique perspective when approaching design, animation or development problems.

In the last 18 months I have made the transition from a designer to a Flash / AS3 developer. I was initially drawn to this side of the industry as I have an interest in making websites and interfaces that are visually rich with slick interaction whilst being technically advanced. Combining these three elements can be taxing, but I believe I have the correct combination of skills to pull these together.

While I am still in the early days of my career in development, I feel that I have progressed to a relatively advanced level in a short period of time. I have no plans to leave Flash behind but I am already planning my next foray into iPhone development and OpenFrameWorks so I can progress onto other platforms and also into interaction design and environmental experiences.

I pride myself on being professional, communicative and approachable whilst having a very good work ethic.
