Conor Ebbs’s Summary

We launched SeniorCare.ie in 2007. Our goal is the development of a comprehensive online community of resources for older people in Ireland.

The website is undergoing a complete redesign at present to include the SeniorCare Jobs Board, Blog, Discussion Forum and Resource Centre.

I work as an Online Marketer for clients of a:m web ltd. In a nutshell, I help websites get targeted visitors and convert them.

My work involves Search Engine Optimisation, Pay Per Click (PPC) Marketing, Web Usability, Website Conversions, Landing Page Optimisation and Social Media Marketing.

I am a Google Qualified Advertising Professional and run several PPC campaigns in Ireland.

In my spare time I coach wrestling in University College Dublin, write poetry and music and record when I can, keep fit and healthy in body and mind and indulge in the simple pleasures.
