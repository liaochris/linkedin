Lisa Holmes’s Summary

In 1999, I formed my own marketing communications firm with my partner and husband. We have specialized in website and interactive design, as well as doing a lot of work in traditional print communications. I handle the graphic design and technical part of our business, and my partner handles marketing and content development. We have served a wide variety of clients, but our work has been concentrated in the economic development, business-to-business, architecture/engineering, life sciences and non-profit areas.
