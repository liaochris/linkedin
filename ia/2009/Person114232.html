Mark Arnold’s Summary

Mark speaks regularly to audiences around the country on branding, marketing, strategy, leadership, personal growth and generational issues. He speaks frequently to diverse audiences such as management, staff, executive leadership and boards of directors. His breadth of knowledge connects with audiences for keynotes, breakouts and training events.

Mark is the author of “My Best Service Ever” and “Marketing Across the Generations: Gen. X”. His upcoming book, “Think Huge: Elevating Your Life & Your Business” is due out fall 2009.

Mark is also senior vice president for a Neighborhood Credit Union in Dallas. He oversees and plans strategies for multiple departments, including marketing, business development, human resources, training and customer investments. He also helps direct activities and programs that promote a sales culture in the organization. He is a member of the senior management team, where he helps formulate solutions to organizational changes.

Mark holds a Bachelor’s of Journalism degree from the University of Texas at Austin and is an instructor at the Southwest CUNA Management School.

Mark’s recent personal accomplishments include summiting the highest peak in the continental United States (Mt. Whitney), finishing three marathons and placing first in his age division in the Hottest Half 10K.
