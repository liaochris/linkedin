Daryl Hepting’s Summary

I am a member of the Department of Computer Science and an associate member of the Department of Media Production and Studies at the University of Regina. While on sabbatical in 2007-8, I am Scientist in Residence at iQmetrix.com.

I completed my Ph.D. at Simon Fraser University in Burnaby, Canada in 1999 with the dissertation entitled "A New Paradigm for Exploration in Computer-Aided Visualization." My research has been focused on the development of tools to help people deal with and navigate complex information spaces, in application areas as diverse as environmental decision support, eyewitness identification, and multimedia composition. From my interest in user experience, I am interested in the management of software projects so that value for the user is in focus throughout.
