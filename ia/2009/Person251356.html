David Brown MD’s Summary

Most of my Business experience is in the private practice of Medicine and running a small S-Corporation. I have also served as a Director on 2 Corporate/Non-Profit Boards for over 15 years total. Over the past 2 years (2006 & 2007), I have become enamored with Information Marketing and Internet Marketing in particular. I have been a student of over 30 known experts or "Gurus" in that time and have learned a tremendous amount. I am working on developing joint ventures to provide even better information to market and to develop leadership in the field. This is a very exciting field and I plan to be a major player in the field and help develop newer talent. I also plan to use the power of the Internet to further the causes of World Peace and increasing Green consciousness as well as putting an end to the Abuse of Children, especially Sexual Abuse and Exploitation of Children (probably the thing that I'm most passionate about). What do you see in the future of Information Marketing? You can share your ideas or ask me a question at www.JVAccess.com. - David M. Brown, MD "ZenDoc"

  • President

    Total Health Care, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Medical Practice industry)

    June 1990 — December 2005 (15 years 7 months)

    I ran Total Health Care for 16 years, emphasizing quality patient care in the broad scope of Family Practice. I loved delivering and taking care of babies, which I did for nearly 20 years until the cost of Malpractice Insurance became prohibitive. I practiced Holistic Medicine with an emphasis on Nutrition, Stress Management, Whole Food Supplementation (organically grown), and other forms of Prevention. I really cared about my patients and their families and really preferred taking care of whole families. Patient Education was one of my primary tools to help people lead better lives. I loved my patients, but hated Insurance company and Government interference in the private practice of Medicine and had to do battle with them on behalf of my patients and my practice. For the last 4-5 years, my practice emphasis was Pain Management.

  • Owner

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Medical Practice industry)

    May 1981 — June 1990 (9 years 2 months)

    I began my medical career in Tacoma, WA as a Board-Certified Family Physician following my thee-year residency in Family Medicine at Swedish Hospital and Medical Center in Seattle, WA. I really wanted to do it all in medicine and especially enjoyed Obstetrics and Gynecology, Pediatrics, Surgery, Health Education, and Alternative Medicine. I shared office space with Charles Weatherby, MD and a couple other physicians later. My excellent on-call group included Mike Halstead, MD, Keith Demirjian, MD, Dr. Roy Virak for awhile, and Dr. Weatherby. Everything in medicine was fun, exciting, and challenging. We had a Tiger by the tail and we were taming it. For several years, I ran or served on the Board of Directors of the College of Medical Education for Pierce County, WA.
