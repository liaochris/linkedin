Stacy Kuiack’s Summary

My experience spans the technology and real estate sectors. Current appointments include the Executive in Residence at the University of Victoria and as an active venture investor and corporate finance and restructuring advisor. I am a director for several companies, Chairman to a professional sports organization, a member of multiple advisory panels and foundation boards. I have guided and/or participated in raising over $300million in debt and/or equity for various entrepreneurial projects.
