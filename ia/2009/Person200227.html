Robert Weissman’s Summary

I have been a litigator for fifteen years, with the majority of my experience being in the prosecution and defense of personal injury cases from the pre-suit stage through appeal. Over the past three years I have also become very experienced in the field of municipal civil rights defense, and I have argued sucessfully before the United States Court of Appeals for the Second Circuit.

My firm defends property and casualty lawsuits throughout New York State, with the majority of our practice centering in southern New York State and the New York City greater metropolitan area. We have offices in Manhattan and Rockland County.

I would like to connect with claims professionals who need experienced top quality representation for their clients, as well as representatives of self-insured companies, municipalities, or businesses capable of selecting their own defense counsel.

Robert Weissman’s Specialties:

Personal injury defense, municipal civil rights defense and representation of commercial landlords in landlord-tenant proceedings.
