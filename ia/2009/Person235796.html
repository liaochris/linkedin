Tom Dennis’s Summary

* Seasoned engineering executive with broad technical and business knowledge combined with outstanding leadership ability and creative problem solving skills, with many years of experience managing and leading product development organizations.
* Expert in applying appropriate level of methodology and process to significantly improve the capabilities of engineering organizations.
* Project management expertise to ensure timely product delivery and high product quality.
* Strong business perspective gained from both formal business education (MBA) and participation in numerous senior management teams.
* Strong experience with large, medium, and small companies, with knowledge of what works best in all.
* Expertise with product development in software/ firmware/ hardware/ mechanical development, quality assurance, performance engineering, usability engineering, technical documentation, and more.
* Experience with all aspects of product development, and in working closely with all other corporate functions.
