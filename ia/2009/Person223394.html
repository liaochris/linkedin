Steve O'Donnell’s Summary

Steve O’Donnell is a highly motivated and experienced senior IT Operations Executive with excellent business acumen coupled with deep and broad technical skills. A progressive career with over 29 years IT experience working up to and including board level in large multinational organisations. He has international experience in Europe, North America and ROW

Steve is recognized as a specialist in three main areas:

• IT Operational Improvement using Customer Experience Management. Recognised within BT as largely responsible for moving BT into first position in Retail Broadband Customer Satisfaction.
• IT Operational Efficiency, driving reductions in operational cycle time and improvements in right first time measures. Focus on reducing costs, improving productivity, and driving IT standardisation. Improving staff morale and employee satisfaction.
• Green IT, winner of three industry awards for the Green IT Project of the year in 2007 for delivering BT’s 21st Century Data Centre.

Steve has a clear understanding of application and infrastructure architectures in the Banking and Telecommunications industries.

Steve has a rare mix of business and IT experience, having taken a consulting business from start-up to winning a major industry award for rapid growth, followed by a trade sale as well as significant IT Operational experience in large corporations.
