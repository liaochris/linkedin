Nils de Witte’s Experience

  • BiD Network Foundation

    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    January 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    OUR MISSION is to contribute to sustainable economic development by stimulating entrepreneurship and creating access to equity for SMEs in developing countries.

    We try to achieve this by:

    1. Stimulating small and medium sized entrepreneurship to create jobs and raise income in developing countries.
    2. Engaging professionals, investors and organizations offering them the opportunity to directly contribute to poverty reduction through investment and SME development in developing countries.
    3. Inspiring people that business and poverty reduction can go hand-in hand.

    Presently BiD Network has national organizations in:
    - Philippines
    - Argentina
    - Peru
    - Bolivia
    - Columbia
    - Ecuador
    - Tanzania
    - Kenya
    - Uganda
    - Jordan
