Steven Senft’s Summary

Steven Senft is a Senior Broker with HREC Investment Advisors – Los Angeles. Steve has over eleven years of experience in hotel operations, ownership and real estate. Prior to joining HREC, Steve spent two years as the Managing Partner/President of 1st Link Financial Services, where he led the sales and marketing efforts and performed real estate financial analysis to create investor wealth and performed in-depth market analysis of commercial and residential transactions. Steve began his career in the hospitality industry on the operational side in a sales manager’s role with Hyatt Hotels and Resorts and Hilton Hotels for more than six years. His previous positions focused on hospitality, which gave him hands-on experience in hotel operations. Steve is currently based in Los Angeles and represents sellers and buyers of hotel properties nationally, with a focus on assets in the Pacific states. Steve completed his undergraduate studies in Hospitality Management from The Collins College of Hospitality Management and holds a MBA from California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. He earned his Certification in Real Estate, Development, and Hotel Investment from the Cornell University School of Hotel Administration. Steve is a licensed RE Salesperson in California and is the current President of the Collins College of Hospitality Management Alumni Chapter.

Steven Senft’s Specialties:

Financial real estate analysis, strategic sales planning, marketing initiatives, market analysis, conceptualization, P&L accountability, presentations, negotiations, and client relationship management.
