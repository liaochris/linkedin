  • "Follow the line of your car. Go trough your own inner experience"

    CARIUM

    (Automotive industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year )

    Sensuous Touch
    CARIUM offers an exclusive collection of tailor-made car upholstery. With its unique expertise in the field of car upholster, CARIUM advises each car driver and helps define the leather seats that best match his / her car needs and habits.

    Design the best to share it and reconnect with what matters most car-wise so as to create an enclosed space of pleasure.

    Mixing tradition and innovation
    CARIUM gives a new meaning to the car as an object. CARIUM reconciles / brings together different times by both reviving the car dream that used to enliven drivers at the turn of the 20th century and bringing materials and techniques at the leading edge of progress to car leather insides.

    Each project is carefully prepared by a regular and thorough communication. This starts with the Personal Trend Test which is the first step of your personal study. From the very first suggestions to your new car inside, you gradually create with CARIUM your own car upholstery.

  • About Me

    Marketing nd Communication

    (Automotive industry)

    2001 — 2008 (7 years )

    Diplômé d’école de commerce, je me spécialise très tôt dans le design automobile. A la direction marketing Renault pendant deux ans, je travaille notamment au lancement de la Mégane II et de l’Espace IV. Après une expérience en cabinet de conseil, je deviens responsable marketing chez un des leaders français de la sellerie automobile artisanale. Je supervise les projets les plus importants de la société, notamment la réalisation des modèles de prestige (Lamborghini, Mercedes, Porsche, Ferrari).

    Au carrefour de la conception technologique et de la relation client, j'y crée le premier configurateur interactif de siège automobile. Une de mes expériences les plus inoubliables fut la restauration d’une Lamborghini Countach, signée de la main même de Ferruccio Lamborgini.

    Passionné de sellerie et amoureux du cuir, je fonde CARIUM en 2008 pour revisiter le rêve automobile des origines à la lumière des dernières tendances de la mode et du design.
