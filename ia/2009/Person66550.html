Douglas Sayles’s Summary

Strategic corporate communications professional with 15 years of biotechnology/pharmaceutical industry and agency experience as a manager, advisor, spokesperson and writer, including extensive experience supporting C-level executives, managing public, investor and government relations, communicating with a broad range of stakeholders and conducting pre- and post-marketing healthcare PR campaigns.

HIGHLIGHTS:

• Managed strategic communications and branding initiatives throughout the drug development cycle
• Managed investor relations and facilitated an IPO and other equity raises totaling over $300M
• Conducted corporate and product PR activities that generated major media coverage (e.g., New York Times, Wall Street Journal, BBC, CNBC, regional and trade media outlets) in the U.S. and abroad
• Coordinated lobbying activities that secured $24M in government funding for product R&D
• Served as the primary corporate spokesperson on a daily basis and through various corporate milestones, issues and crises
• Guided creative design of corporate/product marketing materials, presentations and websites
• Conducted healthcare PR programs for blue-chip pharmaceutical companies, including Glaxo Wellcome, Rhône-Poulenc Rorer, Forest Laboratories, Teva Marion Partners, Pfizer and Hoffman-La Roche
