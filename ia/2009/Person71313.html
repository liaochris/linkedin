Eleni Lewandowski’s Summary

Eleni is currently a Fashion + Beauty Writer for Fashion Wire Press and Fashion Runway Photos.com, while pursuing a full time career as a Fashion and Beauty Editor or Editorial Director for a Print and Online Magazine. She currently travels throughout the world attending and writing articles about fashion collections. She also produces editorial story lines and photographs.

Eleni is committed and excited to bring her creative vision, extraordinary fashion writing and her experience producing editorial photography to any project.

Generating millions of dollars in her successful tenure for NLG, a Boston based company; Eleni has an extensive background in advertising and successful sales management.

Eleni attended both the prestigious Governor’s School for the
Performing Arts and the Fashion Institute of Technology in Manhattan.
