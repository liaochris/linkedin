David Caulton’s Summary

Specific Areas of Expertise and Experience:

Competitive Analysis
Including gathering intelligence, communicating results internally, meeting with senior executives quarterly, and driving company responses to competitors. Methods include first party investigation, third party competitive intelligence firms, rallying teams to do detailed product teardowns, building detailed P&L models and developing industry relationships.

Entertainment Technology strategy
In five years of Microsoft’s attempts to innovate in this tightly interlocking and complex space, I’ve worked to bridge the gap between our technology people (who don’t understand the business and industry issues) and business negotiators (who don’t understand the technology).

Strategic and Product Planning
I combine my research, usability, industry/business, and business strategy backgrounds to drive planning, both from tactical product design and business strategy within the Entertainment and Devices division.

Technology
My experiences at Microsoft have made me a technology generalist. I have worked on teams that have developed Speech recognition, Digital Media Client, encoder, server, codec, and DRM technology, and use this knowledge to constrain strategic planning. This background is also invaluable for communicating with internal and external partners.

Public Relations
I have built expertise and relationships with many leading analysts and technical reviewers at NPD, Gartner, PC Magazine, the Wall Street Journal, etc… I continue to leverage this work (in coordination with PR teams).

Usability and Customer Research Methodology
As a past leader of the Microsoft Usability community, I am conversant with customer questionnaires, usability benchmark studies, user segmentation using factor/cluster analysis, and the segmentation of task time into constituent task phases.

David Caulton’s Specialties:

Competitive Analysis, Entertainment Industry Strategy, Strategic and Product Planning, Digital Media Technologies, Public Relations, Usability, Market Research.
