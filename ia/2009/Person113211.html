Jim Melton’s Summary

39 years of experience in the software industry, including leadership positions in product development; most recent 20 years spent doing standardization in ANSI and ISO (no product development during that time). My goal is to keep doing standards work in the data management sector for the remainder of my career.

Jim Melton’s Specialties:

Highly skilled at standards work, including working and negotiating with my employer's competitors and with representatives of other nations. Good interpersonal skills, can talk to developers, customers, and executives with equal ease.
