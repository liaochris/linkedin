Dan Levine’s Summary

I am an accomplished Product and Project Manager at the Mid- and Senior levels in Telecommunications and Internet Website Hosting, having started successful on-line businesses and managed the complete life cycle of numerous others.

As a result of diverse management experiences and a variety of progressively responsible management roles, I bring a highly creative, and energized set of approaches to product development and product management. I have benefited from and directly applied the knowledge gained from working with an array of successful executives, managers, and staff members who have served as mentors, partners, and colleagues.

My motivation and determination have earned me a Bachelors Degree from the University of Colorado, a Masters Degree from Rochester Institute of Technology, and an Executive MBA from the Wharton School of Business.

Business Successes include:

* New Business Development (start up and Life Cycle Management)
* Project Management - Business Continuity Product Development
* Internet and Direct Publishing - eCommerce Application Development
* Defining Business Value to the Customer - brand positioning
* Curriculum Design / Teaching / training

Dan Levine’s Specialties:
