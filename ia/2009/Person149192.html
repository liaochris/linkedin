    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Retail industry)

    July 2003 — Present (6 years 1 month)

The core business of Touring Viaggi is to manage, to re-organize and to develop both the retail chain (13 own shop and 21 shops in franchising) and the tour operator of Touring Club Italiano; the turnover of the company is higher than 31 million Euro (23 million Euro coming from retail division and 8 million Euro coming from tour operator division)

    Position: CEO and Managing Director:
    I am responsible of P&L and the results for both divisions and I was the “legale rappresentante” of the company.
    The two retail chain (own chain and franchising chain) managers, the tour operator manager, the marketing and event manager, and the publishing manager directly referred to me while the controller functionally reported to me.

    • Defined a plan to develop a franchising network
    • Obtained 500.000 € saving and 15% increase in answering level, outsourcing the customer service
    • Generated 1,5 million Euro additional sales through outbound activities
