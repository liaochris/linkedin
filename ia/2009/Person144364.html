Luba Patlakh’s Summary

Hi, I am Luba Patlakh.
Currently I am a Contributer to the new, first ever Mother,Daughter Beauty Blog known as Beautyinthebag.com, check out the site. My alias is BCC(Broke College Coed)http://www.beautyinthebag.com/wordpress/
After graduating college in three years with big dreams, aspirations, and an intense yearn to work, I set out on a search for my perfect job. I wanted to do everything- and still do. I am a ALL AROUND communicator- I love and thrive off of communications- the entertainment/media industry, broadcasting, journalism, fashion & beauty, trends, music, photography, and nightlife. I can see myself working as a public speaker, a spokeswoman for a new line, planning events, hosting a show, you name it I want it!
Currently I work for Condé Nast Publications, The New Yorker Magazine in Editorial Assets & Rights database management.
During college I interned for Sixty USA, Extra Television show, D. Pagan Communications (PR), and Flying Television Productions.
After graduation I worked for Oxford University Press as a production assistant/coordinator and for ABC Networks, Good Morning America Weekend Show as a production assistant.
Through college I was part of HTV (Hofstra TV) and played several different roles including: Executive Producer, Assistant Producer, Copy-writer/editor, footage editor, and anchor. I also worked on Hofstra's Radio station where I was an engineer and on air personality for 88.7, WRHU's news show and The Jazz Cafe.
I have been performing on TV and in front of people since the age of 5. I was signed with John Casablanca modeling from the age of 7 and worked on air with QVC and in several print advertisements.
