Colette Mason’s Summary

If your website, software or printed information confuses, frustrates or bores the people who have to use it, you're making it very difficult for you to meet your business goals.

Your customers and potential customers quickly become alienated, heading off to your competition in search of clearer, more effective information and tools.

Since 2004, I have been helping companies and individuals create successful user-friendly products for a wide range of projects - from small sites for friends and colleagues to a £240 million project for Fujitsu and Royal Mail Group.

As well as offering consultancy, I run usability training courses and also speak at internet marketing seminars, showing others how to recognise weaknesses in their projects and work out ways to fix those problems.

Before focusing on usability, I was a website developer, building sites for blue-chip companies and spent a few years doing IT support and I have strong technical background.
