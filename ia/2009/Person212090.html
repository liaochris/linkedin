Scott Whitelaw’s Summary

I am an Environmental, Health & Safety Professional and for the past 25 years I have been working in the Energy & Chemical Industry protecting the environment and keeping my workplace safe from hazards. During my career I have been fortunate to have been trained and exposed to some of the best brains in the country. I am now contributing and serving in my community as a teacher, trainer, speaker and writer.

I am learning, growing, sharing, and having a blast in the game of life. I have been blessed to have met and studied with so many masters and have worked on products for the likes of Jack Canfield, Loral Langemeier and Joe Vitale.

I am schedule to speak for the National Safety Council in March 09.

Success Principles (Applies to Every Profession) TCC/ACIT Seminar, June 8-9, 2009.

Two day workshop. This high intensity two-day workshop helps you and your organization to reach higher levels of achievement. Learn a system model of: Think, Dream, Plan, Do and Improve, and how to apply the model and its success principles when you return to the workplace. Day 1 focuses on identifying traits required to be more successful in your profession, providing you the keys to reach your lofty goals. Day 2 focuses on building solid relationships for successful teams and learning to Measure What You Treasure. Be prepared to grow, learn and have fun.
