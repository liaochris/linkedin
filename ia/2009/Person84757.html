Gemma Atkinson’s Summary

I am an Apple Certified Editor, a director and my camera work has been broadcast on ITV, Sky One and Channel Four. Whether I’m directing, camera operating or editing I bring a unique style to each project whilst paying close attention to the client’s brief. Because I am experienced in every part of the filmmaking process I am constantly aware of the continuity of the edit and how to maintain clarity of message whether that be in documentary, fiction of corporate film. I am amiable and flexible when working with directors and runners alike.

Gemma Atkinson’s Specialties:

Producing, directing, editing on FCP, camera operating with Sony Z1 and EX1.

    January 2009 — Present (2 months)

    I have been running my production company White Night Films Limited since May 2004. I have since sold my shares in that company and started a new one called Fat Rat Films.
