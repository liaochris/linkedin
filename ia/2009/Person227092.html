Susan Miller’s Summary

Given the challenging economic situation, people in your circle may appreciate knowing of a CEO that has exponentially increased the ROI of 5 companies on sales ranging from $2 million to $1.3 billion in cash-constrained environments, and in the process achieved dominant market-share for a variety of products and services in Canada, US and Europe.

I offer contract services in North America ranging from company assessments and leadership coaching, through to hands-on execution as CEO. For long-term assignments or fulltime positions I prefer a Calgary-base, but have an excellent track-record of leading substantial teams in multiple locations– so am pretty flexible on location depending on the situation.

Proficient in start-ups, accelerated growth, turnarounds, business/product development, acquisitions & divestitures, I have:
● driven sales from $20 to $850 million & exponentially increased ROI, during industry-wide Gross Profit declines
● delivered >31% compounded annual sales growth generating >1 billion of $US revenue for 2 companies
● generated 14X the sales and 20% EBITDA by creating 2 spin-offs from a near-bankrupt company

A change catalyst adept at operating with-in cash constraints and improving customer satisfaction by streamlining processes, reducing costs and re-organization that has:
● increased operating profit from $1 million to $20 million in 2 years, across an 850-employee, 7-country operation
● achieved #1 in customer satisfaction & earned dominant market share for 2 companies
● generated $10 million revenue & $2 Million NIBT in one year levering just $50K of seed capital investment
● consistently reduced opex as a % of GP, as well as tripling revenues and integrating the acquisition of a $200 million competitor twice our size

Susan Miller’s Specialties:

Although I’ve led companies in “leading edge” technologies and life sciences, I’ve also delivered excellent results for “classic” manufacturing and distribution businesses.

A transformational leader that delivers the highest value to shareholders by forming motivated teams focused on optimal strategies,and ensuring cohesive execution that consistently meets or exceeds expectations.
