Murat Mutlu’s Summary

It’s a bit difficult to pigeon hole myself into one discipline as I have worked in so many different areas in the mobile advertising space.

Over the past 6 years I have worked closely with brands and agencies such as Vodafone, Nokia, Pepsi, Ford, Paramount, BBH, Wunderman and Dare to produce and deliver hundreds of effective mobile marketing strategies and innovative campaigns which integrate with TV, Online, Print and Outdoor. Some of which have even won or been nominated for MMA and D&AD awards.

More recently at Nokia, I worked as a Mobile Advertising User Experience Product Manager a key part in Nokia’s push towards ad-funded services.

Working with global service lines I utilized technologies such as location, mobile coupons, handset demographics, video, NFC, Flash Lite, image recognition to create relevant and contextual advertising experiences. It was also my job to prove what we were doing was quantifiably viable. I managed large scale qualitative and quantifiable consumer research into creative concepts and gathered vital insight prior to development, production and strategy.

More excitingly, I have also worked on developing new innovative features of Nokia handsets due to hit the market within the next two to three years.

I have a huge passion for working in mobile and thrive on producing work that truly engages people. My understanding of digital and mobile technology is extensive and I’m definitely a BIG ideas person.
