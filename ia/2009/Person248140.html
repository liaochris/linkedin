Bill Furlong’s Summary

Seasoned Online Media Entreprenuer with 20 years of online, magazine, newspaper and advertising agency experience. Currently, President and Partner of vertical search pioneer firm, SearchChannel. Also, Founder and CEO of venture funded B2B internet firm, B2BWorks, now called ClearGauge. Especially skilled in building a business, recruiting talent and nurturing client relationships. Broad experience in all aspects in online business building and well connected to valuable agency, media and investment constituencies in the online industry. Fund raising and M&A experience specifically, in online media segment, and serve on a handful of mature and emerging online company boards.

Bill Furlong’s Specialties:

Specialized Search Engine Development, in vertical, local, and topical environments: full technology installation and business model creation and consulting. Revenue modeling and sales strategy development. Monetization technology expertise as well as online staffing architecture and budgeting collaboration, senior team building. Business building: strategic and venture fund raising consulting.
