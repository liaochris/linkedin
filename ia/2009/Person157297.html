Maximiliano De Muro - Serial Entrepreneur and Seed Investor’s Summary

Since the age of 20 and over the course of the last 8 years, Maximiliano has founded several successful companies.

His main achievements include "SX Networks" (Software company now sold to a Spanish Group named "Grupo Crea"), "MediaGambling" (The biggest gaming traffic engine in the world, now sold to a French-Spanish Group).

Now he is merging "Latin Interactive Gaming" into a big Pan-European Gaming Group and acting as a Online Gaming Advisor for several companies in the industry.

Since 2008, he is involved in several startups related to Lifestyle Management (http://www.mardulcelifestyle.com), Medical information (http://www.adnblogs.com). among others.

Maximiliano is an active member of AJE (http://www.aje.org.uy), ADM (http://www.adm.com.uy) and CUTI (http://www.cuti.org.uy).

Also, Maximiliano works as a IT advisor for the uruguayan government, and he is an active speaker in several conferences about E-Commerce all over the world.

Maximiliano' main goal is to be a recognized as a global supporter of high-impact entrepreneurs and private investors looking for new amazing opportunities.

Maximiliano De Muro - Serial Entrepreneur and Seed Investor’s Specialties:

Maximiliano has a strong knowledge of the emergent technologies around the world, and he provide consultancy to all those organizations in locations like Europe and North America, which wants to get support for their entrepreneurs, Venture Capitals, Business Angels and Private Investors, looking for new opportunities in Latin America, and Vice-versa.
