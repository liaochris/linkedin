Victoria B. Edwards’s Summary

Ms. Edwards has held increasingly responsible administrative support positions at the CEO, Executive Director and Director levels of Fortune 500 and Michigan 100 companies for over 15 years. In these roles, Ms. Edwards has been directly involved in or responsible for the overall planning and direction of staff and service operations, executive office management, and human resources activities and functions.

In 2004, Ms. Edwards was appointed to the Third Circuit Court of Michigan to assist a Circuit Court Judge in his role as national chairman of the judicial section of the National Bar Association and as a result, was nominated for the Annual Officeteam Administrative Excellence Award.

Ms. Edwards has consulted area decision makers, community and business leaders on best practices in protocol, organizational, interpersonal and communication expertise in community organization, outreach, program administration/planning, public relations and office management.

Ms. Edwards has extensive fundraising, and community relations experience; an integrated knowledge of appeal and social media, and expert knowledge of MS Project and MS Office, Blackbaud's Raiser's Edge.

Ms. Edwards conducts training seminars and workshops to organizations serving low income, welfare to work, and women transitioning into the workplace with business etiquette and practical real world education that prepares them for a successful and respected career in administrative field. She has received various awards for leadership, civic service and community service throughout Detroit and has been featured in Who's Who in Black Detroit for the past 3 years.
