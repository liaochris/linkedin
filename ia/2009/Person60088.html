Deanna Lazzaroni’s Summary

When it comes to marketing communications, my philosophy is this:

It's all about connections. Touch points. Experiences.
People, Brands & Relationships.

I want to inspire change. I have embraced this industry because of its ever-changing nature. I am the type of person that loves learning something new everyday. I enjoy finding new and interesting things that are going on in the world and figuring out how to connect that information to my own life, to others, to people and to brands.

I want to create stories. I want to inspire people to feel something.
To connect to a part of themselves that is reaching out for something to hold on to. To influence emotional actions and reactions. And to tie these emotional strings to the foundations of brands.

Above all, I want to have an impact on the way that people and brands communicate. I want to change the way the advertising world does business by doing a lot more listening and a lot less shouting.

I am an Idealist. An Innovator. A Forward-Thinker.
Always looking for the next step, the better way, the bigger picture.

  • Ogilvy & Mather

    (Public Company; WPP; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 2008 — August 2008 (3 months)

    Having little interactive experience and realizing that the future of advertising resides in a more digital world, I sought to gain experience in the interactive department at Ogilvy this past summer. Alongside a Senior Project Manager on mostly Kraft Foods brands, I was able to get hands-on experience working on simultaneous projects ranging from a microsite launch to a rich media banner ad campaign to a series of viral videos to be placed on social media sites. I was also invited to participate in brainstorming sessions held in preparation for a global digital strategy pitch.

    On top of all of this, I worked with a team of five other interns throughout the summer to develop an integrated non-traditional marketing campaign for a pro-bono client. At the conclusion of the program, each intern team was invited to pitch their campaign to the client. Our team was awarded "Best Viral Campaign" out of six presentations.
