Carlo Martin’s Summary

Carlo Estrada joined InfoFlows Corporation in 2005 as Director of Operations. With over twelve years of experience focused on deploying operational infrastructure and support organizations for large online services, Estrada has held numerous senior level management and engineering positions at leading multinational technology companies.

Most recently with Microsoft Corporation, Estrada was a Senior Program Manager with the MSN division. In this capacity, Estrada was responsible for the software release process for all ad revenue related software for the MSN platform. Prior to working as Senior Program Manager, Estrada worked as Lead Program Manager for the MSN Platform Services Team where he led the team responsible for standardizing all ad platform requirements and technology for the MSN Network. Early in his Microsoft career, Estrada was the Operational Manager leading all MSN Internet Access services and held key software test positions with the MSN group.

Prior to his eight year tenure at Microsoft, Estrada worked for Tandem Computers as a Senior System’s Engineer and at Addison Wesley where he was a Project Manager. Estrada completed coursework at DeAnza College in California.
