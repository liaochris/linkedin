David Connolly’s Summary

Financial executive, CPA, accomplished in building and leading finance organization, developing accounting policy, evaluating internal controls, managing annual audits, (having worked with two of the “Big 4” accounting firms), and external financial reporting including preparation of annual financial statements. Skilled in treasury function including managing commercial paper, Euro-medium term notes, interest rate, currency, and liquidity risk. Experienced in overseeing federal and state accruals and deferred taxes, tax returns, audits, and sales and property tax. Knowledgeable in the preparation of short and long term budgets, with strong follow through to understand and resolve variances with actual results.

David Connolly’s Specialties:

Lease and loan Accounting, SOX 404 reporting and compliance, accounting policy, treasury including commercial paper and medium term note documenation and management, hedge accounting, interest rate and foreign currency swaps.

    Treasury: Managed the company’s interest rate, liquidity and counter-party exposure on periodic debt balance of approximately $700-900 million composed of commercial paper, bank debt and Euro-medium term notes. Maintained adequate liquidity even with unpredictable cash flows associated with timing of new business and asset sales.

    Utilized foreign currency interest rate swap agreements with majority of MTN issuances to create fixed interest rate US$ obligations. Ensured that all swaps received favorable hedge accounting treatment under FASB 133.

    Negotiated ISDA swap and loan agreements with major banks as necessary and managed annual update of Euro-medium term note program including updating legal documentation.

    Taxes: Oversaw federal and state income tax accounting, including monthly accrual, extensions, and reporting under FIN 48, and local sales and property tax compliance.
