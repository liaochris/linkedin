Michael Friedline’s Summary

Bottomline focused fundraiser creating comprehensive development programs that will generate desired philathropic income to sustain and promote the mission of the organization.

I prioritize the creation of major donor programs which often involve advocating for donors as investors entitled to regular and candid reporting, opportunities to provide input, and recognition. This often impacts the programs and especially the value propositions that organizations create. Are they relevant? Are they efficient? Are they effective?

I greatly enjoy training and mentoring development staff crfeating productive and enthusiastic work teams that implement an array of development strategies on behalf of their organizaitons. I find that on-the-job training is the mmost effective way for improving development teams and their results.
