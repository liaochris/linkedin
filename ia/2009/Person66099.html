Dorian Webb’s Summary

I am a Resourcing and Talent Professional with over 20 years' experience. I've met the ambitious growth plans of high tech and global clients by creating credible and effective resourcing departments, implementing diverse and cost efficient candidate attraction strategies that attract high calibre candidates. Global assignments have included defining and implementing integrated resourcing policies, e-recruitment solutions, recruitment brand strategies and managing the search and appointment of senior executives.

OD and change management experience gained in HR Director role
