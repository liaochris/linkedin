Kristof Janssens’s Summary

I handle Pearle's day-to-day advertising business (POS, flyers, folder, DM, ...). Next to that I also work on creating bigger campaigns (TV, radio, online, ...) for this brand over a longer time span.

I also work on project basis for Perfetti van Melle, a confectionary company that houses subbrands as Mentos, Frisk, Fruittella, Smint, Klene, Chupa Chups, Look-o-Look, etc... My work for this interesting brand differs from TV and print advertising to brand activation campaigns.

Recently worked for other clients such as: Menzo, Think Media Magazines, European Commission, Germinal Beerschot, Billy giftcard, Brax fashion, Instituut voor Drugs en Alcohol (overheid) etc...!
