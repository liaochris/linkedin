Jeff Price’s Summary

I am the founder and CEO of Synergy Sites LLC. We are an online publisher of membership, subscription, and social networking websites. Our clients are more than just clients, they are our partners. We partner with individuals or companies who have great content and a strong following of loyal customers or clients. Examples of the types of partners we take on are training companies, business trainers and coaches, motivational speakers, educators, and authors.

I personally love the challenges and the opportunities in the world of business, as well as the continual personal and professional growth required to succeed. In addition to the partnerships and joint ventures that I establish for Synergy Sites I am involved in facilitating various types of strategic initiatives for other companies.

I am one of the founding members of a joint venture/business growth mastermind group for leaders of small to medium sized business. We focus on projects or initiatives that benefit all parties in a variety of ways including: providing access to untapped markets, access to funding, increasing profitability, decreasing costs, and capitalizing on underutilized assets.

I wish you great success in your business. If there is any way that I can help you, or you feel that your business would benefit from the services of our mastermind group please feel free to contact me.
