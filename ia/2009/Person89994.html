Greg Walters’s Summary

SIGMAnet is a premium HP Value Added Reseller. We partner with you to help you reduce the costs associated with your printing and IT infrastructure.

Professional Selling as an Agent of Change

A seasoned business systems Partner since 1988, I've had the opportunity to work with companies of all kinds - from major corporations to mid-size firms to start-ups, government and non-Profits - in a variety of different industries. My primary role has been to develop clear, concise technological strategies and implementations that will generate significant Return On Investment, inspire action and get results.

Aside from working with some of today’s best known names in the office equipment field; Hewlett Packard, IKON, Ricoh, Canon, Konica Minolta, Océ and Panasonic, I feel fortunate to have worked with pioneers of office automation; WordPerfect, Novel, Compaq, IBM, Great Plains, Solomon, Platinum Accounting, Timberline, and Computer Associates to name just a few.

I have a strong relationship with all of my clients, for whom I provide ongoing services in configuring and designing custom Managed Print Services, Fleet Management and Strategic Technology Projects.

Greg Walters’s Specialties:

Analysis and recommendation of systems that reduce the costs associated with printing/copying.

Succinct project management skills securing smooth implementation and measurable results.

Experienced in a multitude of business environments and how correctly applied technology can either benefit or degrade the organization.
