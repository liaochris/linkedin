Polly Pearson (pollypearson.com)’s Summary

Employment Branding leader passionate about Web 2.0 engagement tools with nearly twenty years of FORTUNE 500-level experience spanning human resources, marketing, branding, investor relations, public relations, advertising, writing, and professional speaking with the objective of engaging audiences in the strategy or mission at hand.

My recent employment branding work has been featured in media outlets such as CBS News, National Public Radio, Financial Times, Boston Herald, Dice.com and in the new Penguin Press business book, "Closing the Engagement Gap; How Great Companies Unlock Employee Potential For Superior Results."

My career is dedicated to building relationships for business results with audiences such as the media, investors, IT customers and prospects, employees, and the global talent market.

I have worked in and for the Office of the CEO for each of EMC's 3 Chief Executives in Investor Relations and Public Relations capacities. During my 10+ year tenure managing EMC Investor Relations, EMC became the NYSE Stock of the Decade, increasing in value nearly 90,000 percent.During my years in Corporate Marketing, we established our first master brand architecture and transitioned the brand identity from pure storage to "information management and storage." During my tenure in HR, I established an Employment Brand Office and developed the company's first formal strategy and program to further the attraction and commitment of talent to EMC globally.

In 1999, I became the first woman at EMC to be promoted to Vice President.
