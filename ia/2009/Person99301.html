Ioannis Valmas’s Summary

At Athens Law Office we provide legal advice and representation in a wide area of the legal practice. Our primary focus is the conduct of our clients' legal affairs in a highly professional manner and, ultimately, the successful fulfillment of their expectations.

The office is supported by libraries, new technologies and online legal information banks, thus achieving the satisfaction of our clients' legal needs.

Through the adoption of a modern and business oriented practice, our client base now consists of a number of domestic and international corporate entities as well as individual clients and we have successfully advised and/or represented large international corporations and individuals on several areas of law.

We currently advise and represent several corporate entities and individual clients located internationally.
Within the agenda of the office is the broadening of our co-operation with international law firms. Already there is a number of international law firms located all over the world co-operating with Athens Law Office that Yannis manages.


Ioannis (Yannis) Valmas, the managing partner of Athens Law Office has been rated as Preeminent by our clients on Client Review ratings on Martindale-Hubbell.
