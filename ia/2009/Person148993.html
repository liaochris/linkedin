Marco Cacciotto’s Summary

I’m 39 years and I’m a political consultant since 1994, one of the first in Italy, giving strategic advice to parties, candidates, public administrations, interest groups and labor unions.
As a top expert about political strategies, issues management and message development I’m a frequent speaker at university’s courses, training programs and conferences, both domestically and internationally. I teach “Political marketing” at the Faculty of Political Sciences at the University of Florence and at the University of Milan. I’m member of the EAPC (European Association of Political Consultants) Board of Directors and founder of Public, network of professionals and firms specialized in public interest strategies, communication and research.
