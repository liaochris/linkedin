Michael Giannii Calvert’s Summary

Happiness began on the stem of a musical note. I still pursue it subconsciously. When I say pursue I mean this in the sense of "Walking down a dark alley following an exquisite sound, dubious of what may lie at the alley's end". (Very much like following Alice down the rabbit hole).

How would I describe my work ethic? In a word: Quality.

I truly enjoy everything that embodies the realm of sound.

As of late I have stumbled into a new realm, connecting with humans across the digital plane we call our second life. I am not talking about that life suck of a game. I was previously employed as the Community Manager at coComment connecting over 1.4 million users. I still do music but it has become more of a hobbyist gig. My calling is meeting new people, inspiring, conversing, and manifesting smiles across many faces.

Outside of these two realms I am a very capable videographer, animator, and linguist as well. I speak fluent Greek, Japanese, and semi fluent in Spanish. I can dabble in many other languages but it is just small talk.
