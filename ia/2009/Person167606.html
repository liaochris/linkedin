Mona Moore’s Summary

Focus Web Technologies provides web development and marketing services. We differentiate ourselves from other web development firms by focusing on critical philosophies that empower our clients.

Focus Web Technologies believes that businesses should be able to update their website whenever they need to! We not only include a Content Management System (CMS) with all our web development projects, but we also include one-on-one TRAINING for our clients on how to use the CMS!

Focus Web Technologies also believes that clients should know the Return on Investment of every advertising and marketing campaign they implement. We offer services that can track leads and sales for any ad campaign - either online or traditional (print, radio, TV). By providing metrics that identify which campaigns are driving more leads and sales, our clients can optimize their marketing results!

We believe that our business model is going to be the model for the effective modern web marketing firm!
