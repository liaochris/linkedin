Hollie Floen’s Summary

I have years of experience as an instructional designer, technical writer, applications trainer, and personal coach. And I'm sick to death of corporate America. So, I decided it's time to take my future in my hands and do something I love.

I am a huge animal lover and had cats for years. For the past 6 years, though, I've fallen in love with dogs, too. I have an American Field Lab and a border collie mix. As an avid outdoorsy type, I decided to start an e-commerce site that focuses on high-quality and durable supplies for dogs and also carries a line of "people products" such as portable shelters.

I am expanding my offerings as I find more really good quality items to promote. My focus is all about quality.
