    Dancing on the Edge Festival

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    August 2009 — Present (3 months)

    Dancing On the Edge is a foundation and a contemporary dance/theatre festival that focuses on the Middle Eastern region.

    The second edition of the Dancing On the Edge Festival will take place in December 2009 with a program of dance and theatre performances from the Middle East as well as collaborations between European and Middle-Eastern artists. This will be combined with a program of music, films, debates, discussions and an educational program for schools. The festival will take place in 5 Dutch cities (Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Groningen, Utrecht and The Hague).

    Dancing on the Edge seeks also to promote dance in the region itself and develops dance and exchange programs.
