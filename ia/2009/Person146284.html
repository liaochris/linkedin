Richard Mackney’s Summary

Creative, Designer, Photographer, Technologist, EarlyAdopter, Entrepreneur, Founder.

In 1996 the Internet was emerging as a serious business concern. I was working in traditional design and marketing and decided to go freelance in order to learn these new Internet technologies.

After immersing myself in the weird and wondrous HTML for two years I returned to work as a New Media Manager for a high profile Advertising Agency and after a further four years of working in this amazing environment I co-founded Fish Media in 2002.

I'm proud of how the team at Fish Media has developed over the last seven years, we work hard and pride ourselves on delivering outstanding levels of creativity and design expertise, while using all the cutting edge technical knowledge and technological insights that make it all work online.

We have attracted companies like Brawn GP, Center Parcs, Grattan Freemans, HarleyWorld, Campbell's and Noble Foods so we know we must be doing something right.
