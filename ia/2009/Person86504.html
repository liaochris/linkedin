Gilad David Maayan’s Summary

Gilad David Maayan is an Israeli technical writer and information architect. Over the past decade, Gilad has lead technical communication projects for over 50 companies including RAD, Amdocs, Check Point and Oracle. He is a three-time winner of STC technical communication awards, including the Trans-European Merit Award for an online help project and the California and Silicon Valley Award of Excellence for a wiki documentation site. Gilad is today Content Architect at GigaSpaces Technologies, and principal of WOH, a new consultancy that helps organizations implement wiki for online help.

Gilad David Maayan’s Specialties:

Wiki online help, traditional online help, technical writing, marketing writing.
