Gordon Dowrey’s Experience

  • Senior Project Manager

    Facility Engineering Associates

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Facilities Services industry)

    July 2005 — Present (4 years 5 months)

    I have over 17 years of experience in commercial real estate and facility management and have a CPM designation from IREM. I currently hold the position of Senior Project Manager with Facility Engineering Associates and also hold the CFM and LEED AP designations from IFMA and the USGBC. I enjoy serving on the Public Policy and Education committees with IREM National and am the 2009 Vice President for my local Indianapolis chapter. I had the honor to serve on the National Association of Realtor’s (NAR) Presidential Action Group for developing a policy statement on climate change and have hosted several webinars on sustainability to include Energy Star for Commercial Buildings, LEED for Existing Buildings and LEED for Commercial Interiors. In my practice I consult with building owners and managers in a wide range of areas to include due diligence and condition assessment, capital planning, facilities maintenance and operations, and strategic practical approaches to sustainability.
