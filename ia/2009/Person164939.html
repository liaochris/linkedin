Mike Stern’s Summary

Passionate marketing professional with diverse expertise in design management, marketing, communications, packaging, printing and leadership. Conceptual thinker who effectively translates strategy into compelling, on-target solutions. Proven expertise in the management of building global brand equity while leading teams, interacting with all customer levels, and maximizing effort through process improvement.

SELECTED ACHIEVEMENTS
➢ Contributed to a 97% sales increase for VIVA® seasonal prints (2007–2008) by leading the packaging redesign.
➢ Improved efficiency and increased speed to market by 25% through the development of processes to track, prioritize and expedite creative, production and pre-press.
➢ Successfully launched packaging for SCOTT® Towels new ‘Long Lasting’ variant of which resulted in the rest of the SCOTT® billion dollar brand adopting the ‘Long Lasting’ communication idea into its overall brand strategy.
➢ Exceeded senior leadership expectations for execution and timing by collaboratively creating a professional, multi-sensory customer innovation summit, which became a standard business practice to showcase Kimberly-Clark Corporation as an indispensable partner.
➢ Educated approximately 250 clients and peers on packaging insights and trends through the Brand Design “Packaging the Brand Promise” presentation.

Mike Stern’s Specialties:

➢ Execution – regularly delivering to fixed time schedules against all odds.
➢ Management – influence and optimize people and resources to meet objectives.
➢ Creation – marketing documentation, reports, collateral, presentations, processes and functional tools, which always enjoy extensive internal/external usage.
➢ Experimentation – relentless probing for new marketing and design approaches.
➢ Project Management – evaluate programs, initiate projects, develop and execute strategies.
