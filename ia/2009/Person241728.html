Vassilis N. Siakos v.siakos@alumni.alba.edu.gr’s Summary

Interested in Organisational Effectiveness related roles / projects in Europe and the Middle East region.

Holding over fifteen years of multi industry / cross functional "hands-on” experience, increasingly at international level.

Professional profile couples business consulting with commercial line management experience through diverse international organizations, such as Hay Group, Planwork Strategy & Development, Response Management Consultants, Johnson & Johnson, The Walt Disney Company and Metaxa / IDV (currently Diageo).

Primary expertise in working with Senior Executives, to translate business strategies into the design and implementation of effective & efficient operating models and organizations.

As a Principal Consultant at Hay Group, amongst others acted as the Project Manager of a major M&A integration consulting engagement in Egypt.

Through this and other Organisational Effectiveness consulting projects, I have actively been engaged in integrated consulting work streams including: organizational diagnostics, strategy decode & alignment, new operating model design, new organizational structure development & phasing, role profiles development, HR systems & processes development and automation (SAP R3) and the design & operationalization of Balanced Scorecards that translate strategy into action.

Author of the global “Balanced Scorecard Development” methodology guide for Hay Group consultants, as part of the “Building Effective Organizations” practice line.

MBA from “ALBA Graduate Business School”, BSc in Marketing Management from “The American College of Greece / Deree College”. Certified PMP with significant change and process management experience.

Visiting speaker at “ALBA Graduate Business School” for Hay Group’s post graduate HR Program / Module on Corporate Performance Management (Balanced Scorecard).
