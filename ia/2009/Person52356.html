Damián Lucas’s Summary

Marketing Specialist with over 21 years of professional experience in organizations, including 18 in multinational corporations in both B2B and B2C businesses.

Psychologist by training, I was Assistant Professor of Group Dynamics and Social Psychology at Del Salvador University, Buenos Aires. During the 90s I was International Brand Manager and later directed the global development of the market research function for British American Tobacco (BAT). I then became global CRM manager at BAT and Regional Marketing Director for Europe, Middle East, Africa and Asia at CEMEX.

Over the past 8 years I have been devoted to the design of commercial models and organizational structures. I have experience working under great uncertainty and driving paradigm shifts in very conservative environments. I have worked with operations in over 30 countries and lived in Argentina, United Kingdom and Spain.

Fluent English & Spanish (mother tongue); conversational Portuguese; basic French & Italian
