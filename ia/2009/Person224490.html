Steven Lindsay’s Summary

A proven successful and creative project organizer with the ability to work independently or with a team to design, develop, and implement solutions for short and long term goals. Planned and led organizations to educate and mentor others to achieve their potential. Knowledgeable in diverse disciplines with an expertise in leadership and communications.
Years of business experience; as comfortable in the Boardroom as on the front line. Expertise in communication with people as well as systems; from one on one to public speaking, from personal computer applications to networked organizational architecture. Successfully demonstrated ability in a wide range of design and implementation; from simple spreadsheets, newsletters, and other media; to complicated organization-wide database systems, from business presentations to full theatrical productions.
He is also an award winning actor and director, a past member and Chairman of the Board for Mesquite Community Theatre, and a current member of the Board of Stolen Shakespeare Guild.
