Maureen "Mo" Whelan’s Summary

BIO:
Maureen "Mo" Whelan is a former automotive gear head turned animation geek. She started her career in animation 13 years ago at a small L.A. game design company. Mo's worked at various production and post-production studios in Los Angeles as a 3D/2D animation computer artist, visual effects compositor, video editor, technical coordinator, grip for live action and stop motion, supervised artists in a post-production environment and was a Discreet Training Center manager for a few years. Some of her onscreen feature credits include South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut and Purgatory House. Mo is currently is in charge of Film Licensing and Acquisitions for AWNtv.com, and a Producer and Actress at Probell Films, the production company for “Client 14.” She is also a non-nude alternative model and an occasional Corpsepondant for the comedy/horror magazine Girls and Corpses Magazine. Mo has recently been labeled a Scream Queen by various Horror media outlets.
