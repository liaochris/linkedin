Ian Roughley’s Summary

Ian Roughley is a speaker, trainer, author, and consultant. Focused on a pragmatic and results-based approach, he is a proponent for open source, as well as process and quality improvements through agile development techniques.

He is an editor for InfoQ.com community web site; Contributor to “No Fluff Just Stuff 2006 Anthology”; Author of “Starting Struts2”; Author “Practical Apache Struts2 Web 2.0 Projects”; Committer on XWork and WebWork projects; Apache Struts PMC member; Speaks at conferences in the United States and abroad; and a Sun Certified Java Programmer, J2EE Enterprise Architect & IBM Certified Solutions Architect

Ian Roughley’s Specialties:

Consumer-facing and enterprise web applications; Project recovery; Requirements analysis/definition; Technical management; Agile software development; Consulting; Training, coaching and mentoring; Architecture, Design and Development; Open Source
