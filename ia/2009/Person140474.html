Lew Scharfberg’s Summary

For over three decades Lew Scharfberg has been involved with the cable television industry. In 2002 he created Broadband Product Management, L.L.C., a consulting practice specializing in the acquisition, marketing and positioning of content on video distribution systems. His company’s domestic and international clients have included Comcast, Time Warner Cable, Rainbow DBS, SES Americom as well as small start-up cable and telco video systems. Lew’s assignments have included extensive work with HDTV, IPTV and mobile video.

Beginning his career with TelePrompTer as a local ad salesperson he has worked for several MSO’s. Prior to forming Broadband Product Management, L.L.C. he was Vice President of Video Management & Marketing at RCN where he led the team responsible for every aspect of the cable TV product. Preceding that, Lew was Senior Director of Programming & Ad Sales at Ameritech New Media where in addition to internal duties he worked on the Americast joint venture. Immediately before Ameritech he was Corporate Director of Programming at Cablevision Industries, a top 10 MSO.

Lew holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Communications from Long Island University. He returned to the school as an adjunct professor designing and teaching two courses, “Cable System Operations” and “Fundamentals of Cable TV”. He is also on the Board of Directors of CTAM New York and has served as a panelist at several cable TV and telco video conferences.
