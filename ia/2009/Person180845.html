Paola Finocchiaro’s Summary

Based in Brussels, Paola advises international companies in a broad range of sectors through her company Imagine Public Relations. With over 18 years of experience, Paola has advised senior corporate executives in various industries and institutions on effective media relations. She has a track record in creating positive spin for issues initially perceived as risky to communicate, bringing dull or highly technical subjects to life for the press, and creatively building interest in a story. For industrial companies, she prides herself on achieving measurable ROI for PR, translated in terms of additional sales, and demonstrably superior in value to advertising spend. Paola’s clients have included the European Commission, Atlas Copco, NATO, SCA Packaging, Cytec Industries, Duval Guillaume, Accenture and Ansell Healthcare. She previously provided company analyses to the following financial institutions: Merrill Lynch, Robertson Stephens, SPEF, DG Bank, Apax, Atlas, Advent and GIMV.

Paola has extensive experience in coaching senior executives and engineers to present their ideas to the press, and to field difficult questions from the media. She has coached managers at leading multinational corporations internationally, and has personally crafted and managed training courses in media relations for over 150 persons. She regularly organises policy-oriented press conferences at ministerial level for her institutional clients as well as commercially-oriented press briefings and journalist visits for her industrial clients, this around the world.

An Austro-Italian, Paola is fluent in English, French and Italian, and also speaks basic Dutch and German. Paola holds a Bachelor of Science in Foreign Service from Georgetown University and has completed additional studies in law and journalism in Europe.
