Jackie Kollmar’s Summary

Jackie provides media-relations support for Padilla Speer Beardsley’s business-to-business, consumer, non-profit, online and professional-services clients. As an assistant account executive in Padilla’s New York City office, she also assists in research and production coordination tasks.

Prior to joining the firm, Jackie gained public relations and event planning experience as an intern at Obviouslee Marketing in Charleston, S.C., where she worked with clients in the consumer, technology and hospitality industries.

A cum laude graduate of the College of Charleston in Charleston, S.C., Jackie holds a bachelor’s degree in corporate communication. At the College of Charleston, she was president of her PRSSA chapter and volunteered for various charity events and festivals in the Charleston community.
