Bernie Gracy’s Summary

Experienced strategy, business development, marketing and product development executive focused on organic and inorganic growth strategies and their execution.

Member of management team that led growth of Document Messaging Technologies from $250M in revenue in 1997 to $1B in 2007.

Led the strategy, due diligence, negotiation, and/or integration of Group 1 Software for $321M in 2004, Emtex for $41.5M in 2006, and MapInfo for $441M in 2007.

As General Manager, led $10M EBIT turnaround of a failing software division and the companies first offshore development and professional services operation.

Led innovation in product, platforms, and solution development with UPS in its 3rd generation delivery driver handheld as well as with Pitney Bowes in document factory manufacturing execution systems, Customer Communications Management, and Location Intelligent Transpromo.

Personally responsible for bringing in over $20M in incremental business through high end solutions selling to the Fortune 500 and the service bureaus that serve them.

Featured speaker, panelist, and spokesperson at numerous conferences, tradeshows, events, seminars/webinars, and media events around the world.

Frequently called upon for briefing C-level executives, financial and infotech advisory analysts, members of the Board of Directors, and most importantly Customers on the value we can create for them and their customers.

Member of Board of Advisors to 5 companies.
