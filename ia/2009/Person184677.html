Peggy Bodenreider’s Summary

25 years in marine industry working with yacht and boat dealers and brokers providing retail financing to their customers. Sales leadership experience for 18 years managing remote employees working from small loan production offices or from home. Business development with manufacturers and dealers to jointly offer consumer-oriented finance programs. Interfaced with marine industry participants and IT group to identify electronic communication efficiences and commerce opportunities.

Has served 15 years on board of directors and is a Past President of National Marine Bankers Association (NMBA). Led development of NMBA Website while chairing communications and promotions committee chair. Serving 3rd year as co-chair of conference committee which produces the annual NMBA members' conference.

Previously served on board of directors of Southern California Marine Association.

Previously represented GE Money on Valley Economic Development Center lending council, which funds women's small business start-ups and expansions.

Completed several GE training courses including Accelerated Leadership Performance (2008), GE Growth Traits, Terra Nova Communication & Team Building, Finance for Non-Financial Professionals (all in 2007), Spin Selling, Six Sigma Green Belt, Selling at GE, Creating Customer Value, Introduction to GE Leadership, GE Growth Traits( all in 2006).
