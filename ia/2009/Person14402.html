Andrew J Macdonald’s Summary

An entrepreneurial Interim Manager specialising in the areas of Operational Change Management, Commercial and Business Development, Strategic Communication, Brand & Product Development, Marketing Strategy formulation and Efficiency Improvement. A registered PRINCE2™ Practitioner supported by 14 years in business with 7 of those in Change Management related activities and over 3 years FTSE 100 General Management/Executive Director level involvement. Combine these skills with over 6 years of formal mentoring and coaching from executive to shop floor and you have a unique, talented all-rounder with a proven ability to lead and develop major change programmes for International Companies, Public bodies and SME’s.

Always at the forefront is an empathetic approach and a drive for results aimed at achieving significant measurable improvements in company performance, profits and revenues with the key focus being on developing People, Systems and Procedures to enable and support continued growth.
