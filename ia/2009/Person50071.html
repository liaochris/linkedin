Craig Grobler’s Summary

I am a digital design manager with over 13 years experience across the UK and South Africa.

My experience includes; strategy, planning, consultancy & delivery of: interaction & interface design, e-marketing & eCRM solutions, effective design, business process flow, compelling user experience as well business case & proposition development.

I have worked with organisations of various sizes from boutique start-ups through to blue chip corporations and at various stages of their product life cycle development.

For the last 5 years I have specialised in:

- developing long term sustainable client relationships.
- building credentials to win work in new sectors, new areas of focus (e.g search), as well as new clients.
- defining client requirements (including research goals, time-scales, target audiences, deliverables and budgets).
- negotiate budgets and project parameters.
- scoping feasible research & design projects to meet client objectives within budget and time-scales.
- managing client expectations.
- developing sound business case and compelling proposal for work to be undertaken.
- logistical planning and project management to ensure project success.

Work has ranged from; small heuristic (expert) reviews & user research, large multiple location projects as well as large scale redesign initiatives running the complete UCD cycle from from stake-holder work shopping, early ethnography or contextual research, through personas development, scenarios of use & concept development, IA planning, Card Sorting, paper prototyping, iterative design, visual design through to final user validation.

Work has been conducted across multiple platforms such as interactive TV, auditory systems, out of box experience, web and mobile devices (both ergonomics & UI.)

I lead teams, as well as work within teams of varying experience levels.
