Michael Sallese, PSP, PMI-SP’s Summary

Construction Scheduler since 1986 using Primavera Project Planner from DOS through P5. Projects included buildings and transportation projects.

Performed claims analysis on numerous construction projects from 1986 until present. All claims were resolved through negotiations between Owner & Contractor. Prepared successful schedule based claim for Contractor.

Managed construction projects as the owner's representative since 1974. During this time I worked as a project manager for several years for earthwork and utility contractors.

Passed certification examination in December 2007 and received certification as Planning & Scheduling Professional in February 2008 through the AACEI.

Passed certification examination in March 2009 and received certification as Scheduling Professional from the Project Management Institute.

Michael Sallese, PSP, PMI-SP’s Specialties:

Project Manager for contractors and Construction Managers (fee). Conceptual, working and as-built project and program schedules. Used Expedition for documentation. Claims preparation and analysis.
