Justin Mog’s Summary

• Experienced leader in the design, adaptive management, evaluation and documentation of capacity-building programs for sustainable agriculture and rural development.
• Specialist in integrating research and development efforts to increase learning, local participation and sustainability.
• Experienced environmental educator and trainer of farmers and young people in conservation agriculture.
• Passionate about effective communication and institutional knowledge building.
• Fluent in Spanish, English & Guaraní.
• Experience in Africa, Asia, Latin America & the Caribbean.

Justin Mog’s Specialties:

Adaptive management; Integrating research into agricultural development projects; Monitoring & Evaluation; Project documentation; Participatory community organizing; Spanish language communications.

    (Non-Profit; 5001-10,000 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)
