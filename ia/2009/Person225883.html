Kevin McCarthy’s Summary

Motivational Speaker Kevin McCarthy Provokes Change By Helping People Think Differently About Success, Choices and Business Ethics

Straight Talk From a Succesful Entrepreneur and Business Executive Who Lost It All…Yet Discovered Genuine Success in the Process!

On August 9, 2004, Kevin McCarthy walked through a set of double doors for a scheduled 12 noon appointment.

What he experienced changed his life forever!

With a vulnerable style not often used by many motivational speakers, Kevin shares invaluable life lessons with his audience equipping them with practical tools for achieving genuine success.

In his Fun, Story-Telling, Thought-Provoking Style, Kevin Shows Audiences:

*How to Transform Self-Defeating Thoughts into Genuine, *Long-Lasting Success in the Workplace and at Home
*The Importance of Making Right Choices in Our Professional and Personal lives
*How to Recognize and Avoid the Slippery Slopes of the Proverbial “Gray Area”
*How to Discover and Enjoy Genuine Success in Business Without Compromising Integrity
*How to Develop Winning Attitudes Toward One Another in a Diverse World

As a motivational speaker, bootstrap entrepreneur and former business executive, Kevin blends real life stories with powerful points and practical tools to empower individual and organizational change.

He helps:

*Teams work better together to achieve optimum output
*Managers and executives become more successful and respected leaders
*Employees become more valuable contributors to organizational success
*Everyone develop an attitude that leads to genuine success

Kevin believes that success is an attitude and that everyone can be successful. Through trial and error, success and failure, bankruptcy and millionaire status, Kevin reached a pinnacle of success…then, suddenly lost it all. Only when he hit bottom did he learn the real meaning of success. Now he helps people learn from his mistakes so they don’t have to learn the hard way.
