Ceylon Blackwell’s Summary

Ceylon is a Real Estate Professional specializing in listing and selling residential properties in the Mid-Town, East Memphis, Cordova, Germantown and Collierville areas of Shelby County, Tennessee. He also has been involved in selling small and medium priced commercial investment properties.
He has been very involved in the real estate profession being past President of the Memphis Area Association of Realtors, the Tennessee Association of Realtors, the Tennessee Chapter of Certified Residential Specialists, the Mid-South Chapter of Certified Residential Specialists and the Memphis Area Association of Realtors Muliti-million Dollar Sales Club. He has received Realtor of the Year awards from the Memphis Area Association of Realtors and the Tennessee Association of Realtors and has received the prestigeous Medallion Award for outstanding service from the Council of Residential Specialists.
He also invests in income producing residential and commercial properties.
His goal is, and has always been, "to have fun and make money by helping his clients realize their real estate dreams."

Ceylon Blackwell’s Specialties:

He specializes in listing and selling residential properties from $100,000 to $1,000,000 and he also has quite a bit of experience in listing and selling smaller investment properties (under $1.000.000).
