Shaun Albin’s Summary

I offer design and development services for innovative technical sports apparel. I have experience in combining apparel design with technology, whether it be fabric technologies, custom sublimation or science. I have experience with thermo body mapping, motion analysis, and compression.

My services include providing graphic illustrations as well as construction direction and fabric specifications. I review sample proto types and advise sampling / pattern making on fit and revisions.

I develop categories based on the client specific price point and market position. The marketing of a product/garment is a very integrated part of design and development. Creating the right marketing story to complement the product is also part of my services.

I have launched a custom sublimation cycling and triathlon clothing line, Bio-Racer USA. BioRacer is a Belgian brand of cycling clothing that I have brought to the US and manufacture in the US. The Belgian brand BioRacer is an ever evolving brand who puts science and testing into every product that they offer.

Shaun Albin’s Specialties:

Sublimation printing, apparel design and development, technology application to design and development, fit analysis, fabric and construction specifications, graphic illustration, creation of a marketing story.
