James Duffy, Laria Saunders’s Summary

We are a couple that runs a new photography studio in the heart of West Hollywood. We are hoping to network in order to draw clientele who need headshots of any kind.

The studio provides both professional and natural light.
Our clients include celebrities, Freida Pinto of "Slumdog Millionaire," and Rekha Sharma of "Battlestar Galactica."

Our website includes Nature, Travel & Documentary photos with an "add to cart" feature for home or office decoration.

We have a blog called "Studio 311 Blog" which includes information about photography tips:
http://studio311.wordpress.com

We strive to be conscious of our footprint on the environment and use online proofs to save paper. We also chose a lab that follows state & federal environmental regulations and print promotional material on 100% recycled paper.

We know times are challenging right now so we offer affordable rates.

Please email us for more information.
email: info@photographystudio311.com
