Craig Clough’s Summary

Location:
Rock Island, IL

Experience:
Have been involved in music and fine arts type activities for a long time.

An actor or musician in over 40 musical theater productions. Have taught for many years, and still teach music to various levels of students. Am a multi-instrumentalist as well as singer. Have been involved in all facets of music ministry in Christian churches for over 36 years. These have involved adult and children's choir programs, handbells, handchimes, organ and piano as well as creating, writing and directing musicals in which church members might participate in various ways.

Being involved with a number of small businesses that I run, I have learned much about utilizing the Internet as an advertising tool and also ways that work in order to promote a business to the general public.
