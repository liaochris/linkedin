Priyank Goyal’s Summary

I started my career as Management Trainee with Arvind Mills Limited, Ahmedabad. There I worked at the shop floor and came to know about different denim manufacturing techniques, quality considerations and labour and maintenance management.

Two years later and equipped with the operational knowledge of running a loom shed, I participated in the Rope Dyeing Denim Project at Arvind Mills itself. There I set up the loom shed for the project looking after the civil work, maintenance, erection, commissioning and final stabilisation of the production, besides training the workers.

After my brief stints at Malwa Industries, for their denim project, and H.P. Cotton Mills, where I learned ring spinning operations, I joined Directorate of Technical Education, Haryana as a lecturer in Textile Technology. Besides teaching and training, I also gave consultancy to various local industries.

Two years back, I joined NIFT Delhi in their Masters Programme in Fashion Management to again enter the textile industry with a different perspective i.e. fashion marketing, merchandising and retail. Having completed the course in May, I joined Fabindia Overseas Private Limited as a buyer in Garment Fabric managing over 600 fabric SKUs.

Priyank Goyal’s Specialties:

I specialise in fabric buying, apparel retail projects, apparel branding, merchandising and denim projects
