Brian Pereira’s Summary

I have served in the Indian Publishing industry (in the capacity of technology writer and editor) since September 1994. During these years I did reporting assignments in India and abroad. I actively engaged in feature writing, online journalism, and special projects (booklets).
My most cherished project to date is Computer easy, for Vogel Burda Communications, Munich. For this I set up and lead a team for Content Outsourcing.
Moderating panel discussions at CIO/IT manager forums is a recent activity.
Beginning as a correspondent in 1994, I ascended to the position of Editor (CHIP) by 2007.
I've worked at companies like Infomedia 18 Ltd (CHIP), Indian Express Newspapers (Express Computer), Times of India (Times Computing), Jasubhai Digital Media (CHIP) and Aptech (a technology portal).
I have trained and mentored many budding journalists during these years.
Before this I did a 5-year stint as a computer instructor and helped a student set up his own computer institute at Dadar (W).
My passion for technology and journalism led me to set up my own website (now offline) called "Technology Journalist @ Work" around 1999 - 2000.
I am a Microsoft Certified Professional (Microsoft Office 2003).
My goal in life: To achieve perfection in everything I do, and do it with passion and integrity.

Other passions: Personality Development, Personal Organisation and Time Management, Personal Finance, Career Development, Civil Aviation, Music, Traveling.
