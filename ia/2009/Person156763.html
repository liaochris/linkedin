Matt Vance, CISA’s Summary

Matt is a Senior Auditor in the Internal Audit department at Hospital Corporation of America (HCA) in Nashville, TN. He has over three years of experience in technology auditing and information security. His principle responsibilities include leading complex IT security and operational reviews. Matt has a broad level of experience auditing enterprise class systems such as Windows/Active Directory, Oracle Real Application Clusters (RAC), IBM PowerVM, and VMware. In addition to auditing enterprise systems, he also experience leading reviews of clinical systems and operational procedures. Matt graduated Summa Cum Laude with a B.B.A degree in Computer Information Systems from Middle Tennessee State University and has obtained the CISA certification. He is also an active member of the Middle TN ISACA Chapter and serves as the chapter webmaster.
