Stephen Copeland’s Summary

With a background and skill set that has spanned most aspects of business operation, Stephen (36) has rapidly moved through a career path that to date has taken him to the position of Vice President of Operations for a Nasdaq listed PLC (responsible for a team of 40 and a £12M budget) and more recently Managing Director of one of the largest suppliers of educational products in the UK.

On route, after leaving Kingston Universtity with an Honours degree in Business Studies, he established a software-training company, which led him to develop a niche IT product that he progressed from concept to manufacture, marketing and sales.

Concurrent with this activity he went on to establish a rights-based media group raising over £1m of private equity and managing the production of a 52 part children’s television series that was broadcast on the UK’s ITV network.

Stephen is a founding Director of Black Lion Investments Ltd which to date has acquired TAG Learning Ltd, Economatics Education and the Sherston Publishing Group.

Stephen is currently the Managing Director of BLi Education responsible for the sales, marketing and supply of software and peripheral products to the primary and secondary education sector in the UK and overseas with a turnover in excess of £7m.

Stephen also provides hands-on interim management services to companies requiring problem solving, crisis management, product development, fund raising, process control, turnaround management and strategic and operational support.

He is also currently advisor to several senior executives on their management Buy in/Buy out strategies and options.

Stephen Copeland’s Specialties:

problem solving, crisis management, product development, fund raising, turnaround management
