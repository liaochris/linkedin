Peter Liepa’s Summary

A seasoned software developer with more than 25 years of experience developing innovative software and intellectual property for entertainment, visualization and design. Peter spent the last 15 years at Alias (now Autodesk) in a variety of development, research and management roles in 2D and 3D graphics software tools. His groundbreaking work for five products led to 9 patents and two publications.

Prior to Alias, his diverse experience includes medical visualization, developing a stereoscopic virtual reality platform for NASA, and creating the classic videogame Boulder Dash.

Peter is a strong believer in agile development and, in addition to chasing value by applying his mathematical and software expertise to tough user problems, is more than happy to take on anything that enhances the quality of software, process, and usability.

    2005-2006: Math lead for development of global deformation tools in Alias Studio. These tools include feature based and cage based deformation, along with twist/bend and conformal mapping. Two patents pending.

Many results were later incorporated into mainstream development.
