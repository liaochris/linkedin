Simon Baptist’s Summary

As a senior manager working in the technology sector for over a decade, I have had a broad product management experience.

My primary focus area has been Digital Advertising with a secondary focus in Search. Lately, I have become quite excited about the possibilities of Social Media.

My goal is that I always perform to the highest level that my skills, abilities and experience permit me and through that become an integral and trusted member of any team that I join.

Simon Baptist’s Specialties:

Product Management, Project Management, Team Leadership, Strategic Development & Implementation, Programme Management, Digital Advertising, Product Marketing, Search, Digital Advertising, Web2.0, Social Media
