Jackie Nickell’s Summary

Valuation Management Group is your nationwide compliant appraisal resource. VMG will take the compliant HVCC appraisal process to the next level utilizing a panel of your recommended appraisers, common sense assignments, hands on customer service and a higher level of quality control to save you time at no cost to you.
