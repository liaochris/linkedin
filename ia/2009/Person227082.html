Sue Johnston’s Summary

As a consultant, writer and trainer/coach specializing in workplace communication, I assess how well your communication is working and use that info to help you develop and implement communication strategies and messages that engage your key audiences and promote understanding. Thoughtful, strategic communication - it can work like magic.

I have an MBA with a specialty in communication management. I'm also an Accredited Business Communicator and a Certified Coach.

I'm working on draft two of "Talk To Me - Workplace Conversations That Work," a book and learning program for managers, to be published in 2010 by Portfolio Publishing.

And I'm currently on a challenging and fun contract with the University of Waterloo's Co-op Education and Career Services group.
