Ted McFarland’s Summary

Creative, innovative and passionate about getting things moving and getting things done. Executive level business developer who works well across cultures & technologies with strong bias to Asia.

- Former officer in NASDAQ listed company (PanAmSat:SPOT) with Asia Pacific regional sales of $200+ Million. Responsible for $1 Billion of satellite assets.
- Extensive Public Sector/policy/regulatory experiences; UN Treaties; ITU/FCC coordination, market access, export licensing, government and international affairs.
- DISAM trained in USG ITAR & Export controls and relevant compliance policies. US FCPA & reporting practices
- Extensive relationships with pay tv & satellite operators, manufacturers and service providers globally.
- Asia Pay-Television/ Media Pioneer; First Turner in-region President. Early leadership of Cable and Satellite Broadcasting Association of Asia (CASBAA); served as President and Council of Governors.
- Judge for 2008 & 2009 International Emmy Awards in two catagories.

Ted McFarland’s Specialties:

- Developing projects and operating businesses in: SatComs, Ethnic Media, DTH, IPTV & Telcoms and Broadband wireless areas.

- NASDAQ Company Governance, FCPA, ITAR & corporate compliance regimes.
