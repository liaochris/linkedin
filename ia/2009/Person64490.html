Dj Crash’s Summary

CRASH's career has spanned more than two decades and his sound remains original and cutting edge.

STYLE. Crash seamlessly blends dozens of musical genres: Pop, Rock, House, Progressive, Hip Hop, Disco, Funk, R&B, Electro, classic disco. His unique style continues to awe clients of all ages. Born in Los Angeles, CA, Crash's passion and natural talents propelled him into music at a young age. Crash amassed an eclectic musical selection including Beatles and Visage, Bach and Pete Fontaine.

THE 80's. Crash was co-owner Rhythm Crew DJ's. At the time, RCDJ one of Southern California's largest entertainment companies. It catered to private and corporate events. Crash spun at over 200 events per year.

Crash was resident at Fantasia and Red Onion. Crash is one of the world's first digital DJ's. Crash was resident at: Billboard Live, The Roxbury, Barfly, and Hollywood Athletic Club (now known as SOCIAL). He was selected to the Billboard Dance Chart panel. He has contributed articles and interviews to DJ Times, DMA Magazine, DJ, Muzik, Mixer, Urb, and Lotus magazines.

CLIENTS and guests included: Hugh Hefner, Jennifer Lopez, Mariah Carey, Howard Stern, Diana Ross, Herbie Hancock, Mick Jagger, Beyonce, Snoop Dogg, Lance Bass, Janice Dickinson, Donald Trump, George Lucas, David Spade, Justin Timberlake, Dave Chappelle, Bill Gates, Donald Trump, George Lucas, Brad Pitt, Lance Bass, Paris Hilton, Lil Jon, Black Eyed Peas, NBC, ABC, CBS, American Express, Fremantle...and many more.

Starting 2009 - Eco Friendly DJ. World's first GREEN DJ.
