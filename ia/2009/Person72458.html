Emanuele Mancini’s Summary

The activities performed include the study of the chemical processes, feasibility study, basic engineering and design, front-end engineering and design, assistance to the development of detail engineering, calculations in design alternative selection, SIL (Safety Integrity Level) study. These activities extended both to new units or plants and to revamp of existing plants, in cooperation with most important Process Licensor, such UOP, ABB, CLG.

Typical activities and experience:
• Definition and/or verification of Process Design Basis
• Thermodynamic process simulation
• Thermodynamic and hydraulic sizing of equipment, components, circuits
• Process Flow Diagrams and Piping & Instrumentation Diagrams development
• Coordination with Licensor and Client for process related problems
• Assistance to development of detail engineering
• Check of proposed/selected equipment compliance with process and operating requirements

The activities as process coordinator include:
• Coordinating process team up to 5 people
• Basic Design Data determination
• Requirements management
• Release planning
• Project documentation
• Interdisciplinary coordination
• Management of changes

SpecialtiesRefinery skills include Crude and Vacuum Distillation, Hydrodesulfurisation, Hydrocracker, Fluid Catalytic Cracking, triethylene glycol or amine dehydration adn regeneration. Main projects developed in petrochemical field are hydrocracker unit and mild hydrocracker unit revamping and front-end Engineering, Topping/Vacuum unit design and revamping.
