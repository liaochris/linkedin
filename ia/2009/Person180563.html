Pamela England’s Summary

Pamela England joined the State Bar of Texas in 2008 after earning her law degree from St. Mary’s School of Law in San Antonio. Pamela brings with her a wealth of experience as an active paralegal for over 15 years with a busy general trial practitioner in South Georgia, preparing and developing evidence for felony and civil trials at both the federal and state level. In addition to litigation, she now devotes her expertise to family and domestic law, including adoption, parental rights, divorce and paternity issues; probate; as well as fraud and contract cases.
