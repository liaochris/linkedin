Andrea Henry Sims’s Summary

I hope to be carrying out research on encounter complexity and interpreter fatigue very soon. Additionally, I look forward to formally studying the impact of health literacy and education disparities within the realm of healthcare interpreting.

Andrea Henry Sims’s Specialties:

13+ years of formal interpreting experience in healthcare settings; integration of techniques for health literacy disparity reduction in interpreted encounters; identification & assignment of acuity level for interpreted encounters; training of new and advanced students of healthcare interpreting; direct experience with development of interpreter services departments in adult and children hospital settings.
