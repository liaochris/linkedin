Bob Calvert’s Summary

Over the past four years I have been working full time to support our troops. I interview military personnel so that they can talk about their positive experiences in the areas in which they serve. I also interview military support groups, veterans and veterans groups, companies who do things to help our troops and their families, family members of those serving, Gold Star parents and More.

We have been to Iraq three times now embedding with our troops, going on convoys with them, filming and covering progress stories most Americans have not been hearing about, Interviewing Iraqis and More.

We are working on raising the funds for our 4th trip to Iraq and first to Afghanistan.

Past experiences have been sales and marketing related. I had a very large organization in a network marketing company years ago. I was also in the delivery and trucking business for many years in Kansas City
