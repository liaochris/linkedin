Matt Barker’s Summary

I attended the University of Wisconsin-LaCrosse, graduating with my Bachelor of Arts in Marketing in 1998. I then moved back into the Twin Cities area, where Brandon Hedges and I formed the Barker & Hedges team.

We were honored in 2007 by being named the #4 RE/MAX sales team in the state of Minnesota, after nearly 10 years in residential real estate sales. We work with all types of buyers and sellers in all areas of the Twin Cities. However, first-time home buyers are our favorite of all our clients. Our business continues to prosper because of the genuine enthusiasm we have for working with people buying their first homes.
