john lopez’s Summary

John is the Founder of Scout, an advertising agency in Los Angeles.

Over the years John has had the opportunity to oversee the production of many award-winning campaigns. John's passion for the mechanics of production goes back to his beginnings as a print production artist in 1993. "I've always been attracted to the way technological advances in our industry affect the way work is produced," John states. "And as we create consistent branding programs for our clients, maintaining consistency through the integration of technology is equally important."

Along with running Scout, John enjoys experimenting in the kitchen, exploring his diverse musical tastes and spending time with his wife and four children.
