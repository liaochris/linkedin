Natalie Tsang’s Summary

I help clients achieve outstanding financial and operational results by designing and implementing business strategies using Internet technologies and digital media. My thirteen years’ experience in this field have exposed me to a large number of projects across a broad range of business issues, technology solutions and client industry sectors. But what sets me apart is my comfort and expertise dealing with seemingly-intractable issues and complex client politics. My ability to inspire, lead, persuade and create consensus builds excitement and energy in project teams, and drives client programmes forwards.

In the last four years I have added operational management to my skills, by running a team of professional services strategy consultants. This has allowed me to develop excellent people-management and coaching skills, and exposed me to a wide variety of management situations.
