John Scott Smith’s Summary

Entrepreneur, business developer, and consultant with over ten years of experience in lending, real estate development, and property management.

I maximize my customers' outcomes and produce consistent, predictable, and repeatable results by teaching a systems approach to every aspect of the mortgage origination process. Special emphasis and expertise in FHA mortgages. I also provide exclusive FHA mortgage leads through my lead generation company, MyFHA.

All of our methods are supported by experience and constantly updated as we develop more successful ways of helping our customers convert our leads into mortgages.

Previous experience as a professional real estate investor, developer, and owner of a property management company with eight years of successful experience in purchasing, redeveloping, and managing residential and mixed-use real estate. Simultaneously held Illinois License as Real Estate Salesperson.

Extensive experience in vendor and lessee relations. Experience in all levels of real property management including marketing and leasing with a strong focus on campus housing with very high turnover. Demonstrated ability to build strong business relationships, implement new and innovative ideas, maintain excellent customer focus, and manage time-sensitive projects. Proficient in Loansoft, Encompass, Quickbooks Pro, and Microsoft Office. Sold both real estate portfolio and management business, January 2005.

John Scott Smith’s Specialties:
