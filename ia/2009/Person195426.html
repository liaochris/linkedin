Rebecca Smoot’s Summary

Rebecca has 14 years of corporate business experience with 8 years as a Senior Vice President for a Fortune 10 company. Her career encompasses technology project & portfolio management and for incentive reporting through the company intranet to online consumer product sales and marketing through www.bankofamerica.com.

Rebecca has spent much of her career identifying business needs and executing the appropriate technology solutions for clients based on their business objectives. Her focus is on keeping things simple and effective for her clients. She has spent many years focused on web solutions and sales for her corporate clients.

Through informally advising friends and family related to online sales and marketing through the use of the social web, Rebecca realized her knowledge and experience could benefit many others. In early 2009 she established Venture Buzz Media, becoming a business owner herself to apply her vast background assisting small business owners in executing their growth plans online.
