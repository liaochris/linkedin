Ani Kortikar’s Summary

Ani Kortikar : Search Marketing Expert * Speaker at Marketing Conferences * Executive with an Entrepreneurial flair * Lead teams as an executive and also as a CEO

Ani has 20 years of experience as an entrepreneur and as an executive. He has worked in consulting and in management capacity with various private venture funded companies as well as public companies. As a consulting technical architect Ani was involved with many global, multilingual marketing project implementations for Cisco systems as early as 1994. As an executive he was responsible for creation of information technology, training and knowledge management, and professional services divisions for a silicon valley based consulting firm.. He also worked as Director of Business Development at Webex (later Acquired by Cisco systems)

Ani is a regular speaker at various Marketing and web analytics Industry conferences. He has spoken on various topics such as - search engine patents, building virtual teams, building search marketing teams and project management. He has spoken at Search Engine Strategies, Ad-Tech, E-Metrics, WITI and SDForum

Ani completed his Bachelor of Technology from IIT Bombay as well as his Masters in Operations Research. Ani holds dual MBA from Columbia NY and HAAS school of Business - Berkeley, CA.

Ani Kortikar’s Specialties:

Venture funded companies in high growth industries. Improve ROI of online marketing campaigns.Create sustainable differentiation by improving margins. Plan for rapid and profitable customer acquisition.Scaling professional services organization.
