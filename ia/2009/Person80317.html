Thanks for taking aminute to view my linked in profile.

I work for the top broker in the area who has been in business since 1971. I am very proud to say that I work for a company who has kept their vision and committment to community service and philanthropy. I am a top agent for my Temecula office and I list and sell short sales, bank owned properties, luxury estates, and land, as well as representing buyers.

I recently started shooting a new movie written by my friend and real estate colleague Ellen Watkins. She is writing "The Offices" a film about the real life of a real estate agent. Some of the scenes are Farming, Insider Trading, Floor Time, Realtors Anonymous, BK Homes, and Caravan Day. The proceeds from the movie are going to the Realtor scholarship fund which gives away $500 scholarships to 18 area students starting college. Today we shot our first scene entitled Farming. The show is going to be very funny and stars many local real estate agents from the area.
