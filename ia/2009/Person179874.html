Owen Peery’s Summary

I have been a teacher for over 10 years. In the past 6 years I have used technology extensively in the classroom and been a computer and technology teacher. I like to focus on productivity tools to streamline and maximize time in our lives. Too frequently, the more tools we have the more time it takes us to do a task, it should be exactly the opposite, when you use the proper tools.

I also maintain a few blogs and teach English using blogs. I have extensive experience using Wordpress and Blogger. I have self hosted blogs and Wordpress and Blogger hosted blogs. I have modified a blog template to serve as my resumé/CV site but I also use a blog to host my English lessons so students can refer back to the lesson, see the videos and audio files I included, and always have access to the resources I used. I am constantly thinking and creating innovative solutions using technology.

I have over 5 years experience designing websites using HTML, CSS, and I am learning Javascript and PHP. When a site is finished, I love to hear, "It is so easy to use, it's so intuitive, it is easy to find the info I'm looking for". As much as I like to spend a lot of time on the graphic element of the site, what comes first for me is usability.

I would love to partner with people or groups who do technology consulting. I don't want to sell useless services. I also do not want to oversell a client. Often people purchase solutions that are far too robust for their needs. I would like to sit with clients, analyze their technology needs, find the right tools for them, train their staff, and offer ongoing support, either in person or at a distance with screencasts.

I would like to specialize in the needs of small businesses and their unique need to focus on the bottom line while not sacrificing utility and extensibility of the technology product utilized.
