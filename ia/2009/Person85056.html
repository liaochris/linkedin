Geoff Miller’s Summary

I am a diverse marketing communications expert with 20+ years of agency and brand side experience. Uniquely skilled in marketing communications, media and Web 2.0 brand strategy. I have a record of excellence in bridging the gap between marketing brand strategy, digital, media and creative – successfully increasing efficiencies and driving revenue growth.

I believe the future of mobile marketing is extremely bright. Although mobile is young in it's life cycle with huge potential, it is important to remember that it is still just a medium. Mobile stands apart from all other mediums because it encompasses them all. It has the ability to not only brand products, but it can also acquire and retain customers 24/7-right from their pocket.

But as happened with the web in mid 90’s, there seems to be some disconnect between this new medium and brands. The platforms know their technology, but not how it truly fits into the marketing mix of a brand. And brands not understanding the technology and how it works for them.

I have worked with every major and many minor mediums. I also have 12+ years of agency experience(7 with a major DDB Needham) and therefore know how they work and how to sell to them. I have worked in the auto space for the last 7 years, as well as with CPG, entertainment, and high-tech. I know a variety of industries and how mobile works with each. I am looking to leverage this unique marketing background to make this connection between a mobile company and brands.
