Jeff Koubek’s Summary

I am seeking a position as a Junior or Associate level IT Project Manager or Coordinator; Production Support Specialist; or data processing Developer. These are my areas of experience and passion.

For the past 15 months of my employment, I have worked as an Associate IT Project Manager at Washington Mutual Bank (WaMu). I managed the delivery on several projects of varying sizes and complexity. The largest project included a clustered OS and SQL Server 2005 data store with high performance redundancy across a WAN. This project resulted in better performance for the customer service and collection representatives as well as a real and viable disaster recovery data source.

For 7 years prior, I worked in the ‘letter shop department’ at WaMu as a Software Engineer. I was solely responsible for all applications, new and modified, to automated data pre-processing, letter file creation that was moved to production printers, and file handling for archiving. On average this shop produced 2.2 million regulatory letters a month. During this time I supported the around-the-clock production processing like a Production Support Specialist. I initiated many projects and process improvements that have saved the company about $500k annually.

I am uniquely qualified as I have a natural business acumen, technological curiosity, and decent communication skills. I’ve been able to reduce costs as a software engineer, a position of non authority, as well as a project manager - reducing time to delivery and managing cost reductions. I continue to hone my technical and communication skills by taking courses, reading books and actively being involved with Toastmasters.
