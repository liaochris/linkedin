Ted Fleming’s Summary

Forced to retire from law enforcement due to an injury, I was told my reports were so detailed I should have been a writer. That is what led me to my first love, sports, and got my first opportunity with the defunct Florida Sports Network and have been at it ever since.

Over the last decade I have covered virtually every major sporting event in the Tampa Bay area including the Stanley Cup, ArenaBowl, Honda Grand Prix of St. Petersburg, both PGA tournaments - Transitions and Outback ProAm - St. Petersburg and Outback Bowls, NCAA Mens Basketball Tournament as well as the five professional teams (Rays, Buccaneers, Lightning, Storm and Tuskers of the UFL) as well as South Florida Football.

My photos have appeared on the PGA Official website, Huffington Post, ArenaFan and the Florida Boxing Hall of Fame as well as the boxing programs for One Punch Productions. I have a number of slidshows of original sports photography on Examiner.com and my website, tbsn1.com

I was the creator and on-air host of "Speaking of Sports" which ran for five years.

Eventually I would like to focus on just one aspect of my sports experiences but right now I am having too much fun doing what I enjoy!
