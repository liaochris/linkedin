JoAnne Leff’s Summary

Since 1986, J.L. Associates has been involved in bold, effective marketing & sales initiatives on a national and international scale on behalf of its clientele of small to global firms with a specialty in patent licensing.

Apart from a master phone sales training program offered to approved companies of any size, J.L. Associates is primarily focused on bringing life-changing new business to the small business sector--the talented individual or small business owner who sells a product or service that can compete with the best, but simply hasn't had the opportunity to do so--yet. They do now.

Whether you are interested in a master phone sales training program, obtaining a patent and having each separate and non-competing application licensed to a major global firm, or in having J.L. Associates bring you the level and amount of new business your product/service truly deserves, simply contact JoAnne Leff to discuss your particular situation. All inquiries are welcome.

JoAnne Leff’s Specialties:
