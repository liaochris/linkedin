Lee Steele’s Summary

Over 25 years of experience in Sales Management, Product Management, Advertising, eCommerce, Strategic Planning, Market Research and Public Relations. Published author on marketing, internet strategy and business development.

Sales & Marketing experience with a number of consumer products companies, including Polaroid, Wilson Sporting Goods, Igloo and Penn Racquet Sports.

Former Director of Marketing for a leading software provider to the legal market.

As the Owner/Principal Marketing Consultant at Strategic Insight, Lee has provided expert marketing advice & counsel to over 100 small, mid-size and Fortune 500 companies in more than 25 different industries, including computer hardware & software, financial services, consumer products, healthcare, real estate, retail, public utilities, non-profits and more.
