Jeffrey Visser’s Summary

I started my career with a very technical focus working with hardware and software. As I accomplished my goals from a technical perspective, I focused on the client service skills and understanding what the clients needed. Having both of these backgrounds allows me to interface between the client and development teams to ensure that the development team understands the specifications and that the client teams get what they need. I have been through the full development and implementation cycles. I have worked in many different technologies. What I enjoy most is the ability to combine technology and innovation to create a unique solution that will solve the client needs while achieving the strategic and financial target. These unique solutions can range from creating a solution within one software package such as SAS BI, or across multiple software packages and platforms.
