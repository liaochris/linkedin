John Anderson’s Summary

As an Information Technology (IT) executive and strategist, I have managed and participated in numerous enterprise projects primarily focused on strategy development and technology solutions supporting business goals. My clients have included Sprint, Clear Channel, Catholic Health Care Systems, Acuity Specialty Products, Honda, Ameriprise Financial, Chase JP Morgan, Citigroup, Syngenta, Disney, Lucent Technologies, Time/Warner, Smurfit Stone Container Coporation, Meijer Inc., and Syngenta, Inc.

I am a creative, out-of-the-box thinker and strategist and know that when creative analysis is applied to any problem something happens that transcends boundaries in which greater truths are revealed.

Working with various relationships of a problem, I lead teams in exploring implicit truths that manifest through a creative use of inimitable sets of techniques, methods, instruments, variables and measurements. This unique process of abstraction and coalescence produces solutions that speak to customers needs and beyond.
