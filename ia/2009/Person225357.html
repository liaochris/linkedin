Ronda Del Boccio’s Summary

I am the co-author of the inspirational book I'll Push, You Steer: The Definitive Guide to Stumbling Through Life with Blinders On (available everywhere) and author of the new book The Kama Sutra of Storytelling: Positioning, Power and Profit, available now with bonuses at FreeIQ.com and in print later this year.

The Storyation Process(TM) teaches you to tell better storiese that grow your reputation, your fan base and your profits. Look for the Storyation VIEWSletter(TM) on various video and social networking sites. The 4 week "League of Extraordinary Storyators" course teaches you to tap into the power of your story, to adopt a power conscious mindset and to create specific stories that you will use consistently in your business. This course is a perfect program enhancer for anyone who is a coach, mentor, speaker, consultant, business owner, entrepreneur or solopreneur.

As a mentor for Glenn Dietzel's Awaken The Author Within program, I work with authors worldwide and help them design and build a thriving business around their book. Awakened has a unique relationship with Morgan James Publishing, the world's premier entrepreneurial publisher.

My previous work experience includes teaching wholistic health, stress relief, , time management and other courses at Metropolitan State College of Denver for 7 years and various other positions.
