Wes Quintin’s Summary

Established as a name known for quality and integrity, Heninger Toyota has served Calgarians for over 40 years.

My mission at Heninger Toyota is to further brand awareness and make Heninger Toyota synonymous with Toyota in Calgary.

This is being achieved by embracing and intensifying the Point of Purchase experience and increasing Heninger Toyota's online presence and engagement.

Heninger Toyota will have a completely re-designed site and online experience for its customers in October of 2009. This will be achieved through SEO best practices, analytics, a passion for positive customer experiences, and relentless testing.

I'm intrigued in all the facets that we as people use to communicate and engage with each other. Being a huge fan of pop culture and advertising campaigns, I'm enticed by what it is we're trying to achieve through these interactions and experiences.

My current passion is to increase my knowledge bank on the power of web metrics and data driven strategies. And thus furthering my understanding of how we engage and interact with one another.
