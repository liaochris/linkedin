Guillermo Sicardi’s Summary

I am a "Resultant", not a consultant. I used to be a Corporate Warrior and now I use my skills to help owners and managers to focus on Execution and getting Results.
We refocus companies giving support and co-management in: Strategy Mapping, Organize for serving better your customers & People Link - Connecting people with Business. I am good at start up processes, being creative, energetic and motivator.
Excellent speaker, doing presentations and pressing groups to go deeply, not only showing slides in a workshop.
I train people with Management Skills, assuring they apply this knowledge in the real world, not only academic.
