Alberto Cherubini’s Summary

Wide professional experience ranging from trading most financial instruments, including the whole spectrum of equity derivatives, to risk management, mathematical modeling, software development, team management, and several years as a researcher in nuclear fusion.

He is rare in combining the above with a deep understanding of markets and the systems and processes at wholesale banks, including the complex issues that have caused and exacerbated the current financial crisis.

Extensive knowledge of financial derivatives, both vanilla and exotics.
Risk management (market, operational, idiosyncratic)
Portfolio hedging, optimization and analysis.
Software and mathematical solutions.
Building and expanding customer business.
Leadership in crisis conditions.
Interface and engagement with senior management.
Training, mentoring and lecturing.
