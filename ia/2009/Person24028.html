Barbara Krementz, LICSW’s Summary

I provide psychotherapy services to individuals and families in Providence RI 02906. Please feel free to email me at LICSW@krementz.com if you would like to contact me. Please include your phone number and convenient times for me to telephone you.

You may also call me toll free at 888-818-7782

My preferred and primary modality is face-to-face therapy.

When appropriate, I offer tele-therapy services, such as telephone or Internet video, to permit ongoing treatment during separations such as business travel, summers and vacations.

Barbara Krementz, LICSW’s Specialties:

I work with children adolescents and adults. Barbara Krementz, LICSW’s Experience

  • Psychotherapist for Delta Consultants Providence

    Barbara Krementz, LICSW

    (Mental Health Care industry)

    March 2009 — Present (6 months)

    I am a psychodynamically oriented clinical social worker with a MSW from Smith College School for Social Work and a postgraduate certificate in art therapy from Phillips Graduate Institute.

    To assist clients with improved levels of functioning and increased life satisfaction, I conduct a careful bio psycho social assessment to determine whether brief solution-focused or long-term insight-oriented work is appropriate. I use systems theory and approach clients from a strengths-based perspective, looking to build on the client's innate resiliencies.

    I work in a culturally responsive way, assisting clients with issues related to diversity. I use both talk therapy and art therapy techniques to achieve relief of symptoms and significant long-term changes.

    I enjoy working collaboratively with psychiatrists on behalf of the client or with other clinicians who are treating different members of the family system. I am currently a member of the group practice Delta Consultants Providence, which provides neuropsychological testing, psychological evaluations, educational assessments, and psychotherapy for adults and children.
