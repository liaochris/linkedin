Umang Bedi’s Summary

I am a business leader with global experience across Collaborative Cross Functional Business Management, Large Enterprise Account Management, People Management, Strategic Sales, Business Development and Consulting led sales engagements across key verticals & industries. I have managed large business assignments, diverse teams and have worked in various markets and environments including the US, Asia South and India.

I am currently the Managing Director at Intuit Inc. wherein I am responsible for building of our brand and leading & executing our go to market strategies for "disruptive innovation" led products. Having worked with diverse clients across various segments, I have developed a fair understanding of strategies that drive business decisions across Banking & Finance, Telecom, IT / ITES, Manufacturing and Government. My strengths lie in the area of understanding clients business issues, using my technical background to formulate solutions to resolve these business issues and then articulating the value proposition of the solution to C Level executives to create compelling drivers for adoption of these solutions.
