Chris Mack chrisjmack@comcast.net’s Summary

Christopher Mack is a strategic senior sales executive with over 20 years of strong management experience in the operational flow of sales, organizational redesign, and business development for large organizations in the telco, software, technology, retail, consumer electronics, consumer goods, DRTV and energy industries. An accomplished team builder and spirited entrepreneur, he has consistently demonstrated his expertise in rapidly building revenue, negotiating contracts, creating strategic plans, and managing key resources in order to reduce costs and increase profitability.

    December 2007 — July 2008 (8 months)

Mr. Mack brings 20 years of professional management experience and joins Ronco as their Vice President of Worldwide Sales. Ronco, originally founded by inventor Ron Popeil, is internationally recognized and respected as the original leader in the “as seen on TV” and “direct television response” firm. He managed sales, training, forecasting, deal review, and other strategic business related items to ensure optimal performance and maximum return relating to the company’s dynamic sales growth.

  • Director, Channel & New Business Development

    Micron PC (MPC)

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Computer Hardware industry)

    October 1997 — 2007 (10 years)

    Mr. Mack, as Director of Channel Sales and Business Development, a role in which he built a new channel sales force with account managers covering key accounts, negotiated supply contracts, and launched the company’s line of PC’s, notebooks, and enterprise storage products to the largest retailers and direct market resellers (Best Buy, Staples, CDW,etc.) As Director, he oversaw the turnaround of Micron’s “Consumer” retail division to reverse multi-million dollar losses, and built the fastest growing division in the company, the “Indirect Consumer and Commercial Channel,” with revenues of $62M annually. He also served as a guest speaker on several occasions at Gartner technology events, CRN symposiums, and appeared numerous times as a live “on-air” guest for QVC’s technology showcase events.

  • National Account Sales Manager

    Quaker State/Pennzoil a division of Shell

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Oil & Energy industry)

    1992 — 1997 (5 years)

His accomplishments include taking the company’s car care market share from the #3 spot to the #1 spot in national retailers, and receiving the “Presidents Award”, Pennzoil/Quaker State’s highest achievement award based on overall business management and sales practices.

  • District Sales Manager

    Genuine Parts Compnay (GPC)

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; GPC; Automotive industry)

    1986 — 1992 (6 years)

    At Genuine Parts Company (GPC) Mr. Mack became the top performing district sales manager in the country by training local sales teams on high dollar automotive computer testing equipment. During this time automotive manufacturers were producing cars with increasingly complex computer based vehicle maintenance procedures and codes. Unless you were a dealer you could not diagnose these symptoms, therefore many independent auto repair technicians became apprehensive when it came to troubleshooting vehicles with these on board computers. Chris set up regional meetings at area hotel function rooms and conducted live demonstrations with automobiles, trained technicians on the proper use of complex equipment to diagnose symptoms. Thousands attended these “first of its kind” events which generated $16M in computer test equipment sales.
