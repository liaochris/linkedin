Robin Smith’s Summary

My personal career journey has been unique and exciting. I began my career in corporate business and finance roles and have over 15 years of human resource experience in the global technology sector. My breadth and depth of experience includes:

- Visioning and spearheading the implementation of long-range strategic plans focused on employee engagement, retention, change management, recruitment, training and development and diversity.
- Designing and facilitating forums to create dialogue for key human resource issues and top workplace trends.
- Mentoring project managers and leaders to help them develop their vision and leadership skills and identify steps to accomplish their goals.
