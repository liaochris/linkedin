Aruna Sampat’s Summary

Founded in Sept 2004, Career Catalysts is a One Stop Human Resource Solutions Company providing a complete gamut of Human Resource Solutions especially focusing on Outsourced People processes – Strategic or Operations – Short term or Long term.

A young and dynamic company, Career Catalysts is headed by ARUNA SAMPAT and ASHISH REDIJ, both coming with rich corporate experience , and having the entrepreneurial ability to build businesses.

The UNIQUE combination that the Core Leadership Team brings forth in each of its relationships with its clients is -

• Business comprehension and Expertise
• Strong Industry knowledge
• Human Resource Management and People Skills
• Ability to match Position with Candidate

……which is the strength that Career Catalysts impresses in its approach in any Senior Level Head Hunting Assignment for its Clients.

We offer an entire range of services which are divided into 2 focus areas

A.) Executive Search (helping in talent acquisition for clients in Mid and Senior Levels)

B.) People Process Outsourcing (handling all other people centric issues – Defining HR Strategy, HR Consulting, defining HR systems & processes, outsourcing HR department, payroll etc.) – Strategic and Operational – Short term and Long term

We invest heavily in learning about businesses, their ambitions, work cultures and styles, competition to provide clients' solutions as they seek out the best.

Our goal is to be recognized by our clients as a business partner that gives each and every assignment the requisite attention and focus in helping them in their pursuit for performance.

We always strive to offer a personalized and warm approach throughout the process with a view to ensure a happy and successful relationship for all the parties concerned.

We also welcome senior professionals seeking to discuss possible mandates that may reside with our firm, to get in touch with us, in complete confidence.
