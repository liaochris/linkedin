Patricia Maier’s Summary

Vice President Maier & Company, Inc. Published Author, Biblical Counselor, former Mrs. Florida Globe, Marketing Director, Video Journalist. Orphan Advocate, and World Humanitarian.
Board of Director: BBiTV http://bbitv.nextcom.no/ Board Advisor Elect: IllumAlliance International Humanitarian. Goodwill Ambassador “Day of the Child” World Concert. https://www.illumalliance.org/Day_Of_The_Child_Concert.html

Patricia’s passion for video launched to an all-new level after a trip to Africa over two years ago. When her husband Bob, an International Airline Captain started flying there. It was then God moved Patricia and Bob into mission work thus merging their ministries together.

Not long after Patricia’s husband started flying to Africa their passion for what they saw soon launched Africa missions within their church. Then an International Humanitarian Organization spotted their work to offer them a seat on it's board.

Patricia was made"Goodwill Ambassador" to the “Day of the Child” concert. To be held in Johannesburg, South Africa.

Currently Patricia works to bring awareness to the needs of women and orphans in Africa. Especially those who suffer from AIDS. She has been working closely with AIMS Executive Director, Seth Barnes on behalf of a woman and her children in Nsoko, Swaziland.

The amazing story can be read on Patricia's blog on: http://pmaier500.typepad.com

Patricia and her husband currently reside in Melbourne, Florida where they enjoy time with their two married children T.J,. and Sherrea. With Sherrea giving birth to a son, Chase Taylor Smith making Patricia and Bob first time grandparents.

Patricia was chosen to be featured in the newly launched website called " Women and Hope." A website and soon to be magazine which features inspiring women from around the world.

Nominated to be Included in the 2008-2009 Cambridge Who's Who "Executives and Professional Women" Honored Edition."
