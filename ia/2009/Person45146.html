Christopher C. Brown’s Summary

Christopher believes in the sheer power of the art of film and its ability to educate and enlighten, inform and inspire, create dialogue and enhance social change for a better world for future generations.

Christopher is a native of New Orleans, Louisiana. He is a graduate of Southeastern Louisiana University where he began his journey into filmmaking as a segment producer and telly award winning director at the Southeastern Channel.

He is a founding member of the Social Entrepreneurs of New Orleans for which he was recently recognized by the Tulane A.B. Freeman School of Business.

He has worked in a variety of positions for such production companies as Indigent, Fallen Films, Mpower Pictures, Big Sky Motion Pictures, Lifetime, BBC, Fox, River Sig, Off-Hollywood, and New Orleans Video Access Center. He Produced the 48 Hour Film Competition in New Orleans in 2008.

Currently, Christopher is the Producer on “The Price of Flowers”, which is in Post-Production and an Associate Producer in Post-Production on "Terrebonne".

- Coordinator experience, Production experience, QC and post-production experience

- Knowledge of data workflows for digital video (Thomson Viper, RED)

- Extensive Festival knowledge

- Knowledge of Final Cut Pro editing and licensed editor

- Knowledge of Production logistics & protocol, personnel, materials, and management

- Knowledge of New Orleans and Louisiana (locations, culture, restaurants & nightlife, geography, history, weird laws, etc.)

- Knowledge of DVD Duplication, rendering, writing, HD Cam SR, and Deliverables

- Knowledge of Business Plans, Digital Media Consulting

- Knowledge of companies in New Orleans, freelancers, editors, assistant directors, producers, gaffers, etc.
