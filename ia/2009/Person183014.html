Paul Gaffney’s Summary

Dynamic executive with over 20 years of experience delivering results using technology, operations, process improvement, and inspirational people management. Proven ability to improve revenue and profits while reducing costs. Demonstrated capacity to see new opportunities and to mobilize people to build new things.

As CIO of a Fortune 200 company, head of a multi-billion-dollar global retail supply chain, head of technology strategy for one of the most admired financial services firms in the world, and creator of one of the most substantial global e-commerce businesses, Paul provides visionary leadership that inspires people to deliver great results.

Currently Chief Operating Officer, Desktone, Inc. Delivering the promise of virtual desktop computing: slashing costs by cutting complexity. Paul is the vision behind Desktone's product and he built product development, software engineering and QA, operations, support, and the CTO function from scratch. Desktone is shipping the third generation of its virtual desktop platform in early 2009.

Executive Vice President and head of Supply Chain at Staples: transformed a multi-billion-dollar supply chain into a lean, synchronized operation that delivers materially better sales and margin results with substantial improvements in use of working capital. Also EVP & CIO at Staples - led the entire information systems global team at Staples to deliver technology innovation and high service levels at industry-leading low costs.

CEO and President of Website Pros: launched the company, raised over $60MM in venture capital and established long-term strategy leading to an eventual IPO in 2006.

Executive Vice President at Office Depot: built one of the world’s largest e-commerce business from scratch.

Senior Vice President of Technology at Schwab, transitioned technology from a narrow-market active trader boutique to a broader active trader marketplace, significantly accelerating revenue and profit growth.
