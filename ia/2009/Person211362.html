Scott Chapin’s Summary

In the workplace, technology and business are my strengths and passions. From my early days I have always been interested in applying logic and process to everything and I have been able to bring those skills to my career every day. My degree in Computer Science gives me the technical understanding to see beneath the surface of a system and my business background helps me understand how to make it work to create growth or efficiencies.

In my personal life, I am an avid tennis player and a huge Lego fan. My USTA tennis team was a national qualifier at the 3.5 level in 2004 and I continue to play recreationally and competitively today.

Balancing work, family and play are always a challenge. I am excited by all three and that keeps life interesting.
