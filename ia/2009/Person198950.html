Rik Briars’s Summary

Rik Briars is a Senior Railway Engineering Manager with a proven track record in multi-disciplinary railway projects , during his nineteen year career. He has worked on main lines in the UK and abroad, as well as London Underground infrastructures. He is an IRSE Senior Engineering Manager licence holder and has held Engineering Director level positions, as well as Contractor’s Engineering Manager, Signalling Contractor’s Responsible Engineer and Senior Project Management positions on projects ranging from major permanent way and overhead line alterations to both main line and London Underground signalling alterations, signal box abolitions and level crossing renewals as well as a multitude of smaller jobs. Rik is a Chartered Engineer and a Corporate Member of the Institute of Engineering Technology as well as being a Member of the Institute of Railway Signalling Engineers and a Member of the Permanent Way Institute. Rik’s key skills are his engineering aptitude and organisational skills as well as being an effective communicator with a logical mind making railway project work his ideal vocation.
