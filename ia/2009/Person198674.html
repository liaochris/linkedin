Rick Rey’s Summary

New media professional with razor-sharp creative instincts and an intimate knowledge of internet community and what works/doesn't work on the web. Recently completed an 18-month stint producing one of the web's most recognizable pop culture shows (EPIC FU). Consistent track record of writing/producing high-profile, successful content (non-fictional & fictional) for the web since the early days of videoblogging.

Interested in acquiring partners/talent for a variety of indie web series currently in development. Also interested in job/freelance offers from the new media divisions of major networks/studios, particularly in programming & development.

My ultimate goal is to develop a rich lineup of immersive shows that thrive across platforms & formats (web, television, console gaming, etc.).

Rick Rey’s Specialties:

Short-form storytelling, Story development & analysis, Segment producing, Web 2.0 culture, Online community-building, 400+ hours post-production work in Final Cut Pro & Premiere Pro, Personal relationships with key players in the new media space
