Janise Cookston’s Summary

I am a native of Texas and completed my undergraduate education in Birmingham, Alabama. I have been practicing design in Portland, Oregon for the last two years. My passion is designing in the commercial sector, especially hospitality projects like restaurants and hotels.

I am daily seeking to educate myself and stay aware of current trends in architecture and design through newsletters, industry publications, blogs and presentations. I am a LEED Accredited Professional and plan on sitting for the NCIDQ exam in the coming year.

Given my education and experience in both the Interior Design and architecture field I have developed a thorough understanding of both design and construction processes. I am knowledgeable regarding all aspects of design including: concept development, programming, schematic design, design development, production, presentation and business practices. I truly have a passion for the design process and work continually with a strong sense the design elements like form, space and order.

I am a certified AutoCAD user and have very strong skills in 3D modeling as well as presentation techniques and desktop publishing. In fact, my bachelors degree was granted to me with honors based on my thesis design of a restaurant/art gallery for which I produced a comprehensive 3D model and walk-through video of the entire project space. I was also solely responsible for the implementation of 3D modeling capabilities, rendering and presentation at my current position.

I have had the opportunity to study design, art and architecture not only in Houston and Birmingham but also in Chicago and London. I have worked in both the South and Pacific Northwest which has served well to educate me in the architectural vernacular of several different geographical regions. I believe this diversity of location and culture in my studies and work experience provides me a unique perspective in design.
