    Most people who are looking to achieve financial success have assembled and A-list team of financial professionals. These advisors usually consist of an Accountant/CPA, a financial advisor, an insurance agent, a banker, a money manager, a mortgage broker, an attorney, a realtor and a bank. These individuals are hired to provide guidance in their individual areas of knowledge. The questions that one should ask are; "who is looking at the big picture?”, and “how many times a year do I get together with all my advisors at the same time, in the same room for the purpose of making my financial life more efficient?”.

    The fact is people's advisors are working as individuals, providing advice in their areas of proficiency. The recommendations and financial products they sell are uncoordinated with each other. Without coordination of ones financial decisions, it is impossible to help maximize the performance of one's assets. Without an integrated strategy and process around those advisors, you are not positioned to make the best possible decisions for you and your family.

    Acting as a Financial Coach, I coordinate all the advisors and financial products that one has already in place, to work together in harmony, creating more efficiency, reduced risk, increased wealth and protection. This is accomplished with no additional out of pocket outlay, or change in lifestyle.

    By working with a Financial Coach, this brings a completely objective, and systematic approach to the table. As a result, your financial decisions are made with a complete understanding of you entire financial position, and not based on the performance of financial products alone.
