Debt Settlement, also known as Debt Negotiation, is an aggressive approach to debt reduction, which is appropriate for debtors with a serious amount of debt or who are considering bankruptcy. After the debt is settled, the creditor will send a letter stating the debt obligation was fulfilled, and will report to the credit bureaus that the debt has been, “Settled for less than full amount”, “Paid” or “Settled”.
