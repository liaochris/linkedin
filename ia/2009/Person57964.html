David Kindle’s Summary

With more than 15 years of professional experience in operations and technology, Mr. Kindle presently leads the operational and business development efforts of Astegic, Inc. Specializing in proposal management/development, operations supervision, and organizational development, Mr. Kindle was brought on as a key executive to expand the organization while capitalizing on the firms 8(a) status. Mr. Kindle provides strategic vision for the firms growth initatives in the UK, India, and South Africa.

Prior to joining Astegic, Mr. Kindle led the operation efforts of FBRDirect, the retail franchise of the global investment banking firm Friedman, Billings Ramsey. Mr. Kindle specialized in operational support management including strategy, market definition, technology, regulation, financial product & software development, and daily operation. Under Mr. Kindle's leadership, FBRDirect grew 13-fold from a profit center of under $10 million to over $130 million. While at FBR, Mr. Kindle successfully led over 110 Primary, 180 secondary, 4 Trust Preferred, and 30 DSP Investment banking transactions distributed electronically. Each representing several million in revenue to the firm.

Before joining FBRDirect in 2000, Mr. Kindle led several successful projects, including the $200 million FBI contract that supports 8,000 ten-print searches per day, with approximately 27 million subjects within the criminal features database. His team developed the ITN training program that led to the successful operational efforts of over 1600 FBI users.

As an effective team builder with a consistent track record of successfully assembling teams to solve problems, improve productivity, develop and reengineer processes, Mr. Kindle has consistently built and managed several teams with numerous successes for the Department of Justice, FBI, Navy, Army, and Marines.

Mr. Kindle holds a Bachelor in Business Administration and a Master of Science from the George Washington University.
