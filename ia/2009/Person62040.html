Derek Herr’s Summary

A skilled high-speed effects compositor, supervising editor, graphic artist and project manager, Derek brings a fresh cutting-edge approach to both feature films and prime time television series. His unique talents have redefined post workflows on effects-heavy episodics like House, M.D., Prison Break and Las Vegas, as well as feature films including Into The Blue 2: The Reef, Blood & Bone, and Legally Blondes.

Derek has also completed extensive VFX work for Numb3rs, Close To Home and Past Life, and designed the animated main title sequences for shows like The Forgotten and Dark Blue. His outside-the-box collaborative workflows have made creative adjustments to VFX work all the way through final color a seamless and fluid experience.

Derek has worked in Hollywood since 1996, completing reality and episodic work at Level 3 Post as well as episodic and commercials at Riot Santa Monica. He is a graduate of Florida State University with an MFA in Digital Post-Production and began his career as an assistant to Academy Award winning sound designer Richard Portman. He also holds a BFA degree in Fine Art specializing in Cinematography and a BSE in Computer Systems Engineering.
