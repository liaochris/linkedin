Ben Lehman’s Summary

A Bio of Sorts: Ben is an accomplished small business owner, a skilled creative & experienced professional. Also, Ben has great hair, it is tall.

THINKS LIKE AN OWNER. As Co-owner & Chief Executive Janitor of CREMA, he has overseen the growth of the coffee shop into a favorite of espresso snobs, celebrities, Googlers & the ilk. In nine short months it has been featured in, The Tennessean, Nashville Scene, Nashville Lifestyles, & countless blogs. www.crema-coffee.com

He also was Chief Imagination Officer of Buzz Design, an inventive web design company. He was distracted by a beautiful maiden & left.

SKILLED CREATIVE. Albert Einstein once said to Ben, “creativity is more important than knowledge.” The conversation is unsubstantiated, but the impact on Ben remains. He is a talented designer & dreamer. Adept at creating many sorts of media. Ben has been the go to person for top executives.

EXPERIENCED PROFESSIONAL. Ben is an exceptional contributor & team member. He was instrumental in managing an add-on sales program that doubled upsell revenue for employer Shop at Home.

He was an integral part of the team that won Healthways Biggest Impact on Success in '05 award. But this award lacked good design. Working with colleagues the team set about to earn a better one. The bounty for their efforts was an ASTD Excellence in Practice award for a scalable scenario based learning application. This award was more fetching in design, made of all glass! More rewarding was seeing the company’s name next to the likes of Ford & Ikea. This saved an initial $150,000 in costs, ongoing costs & amassed other humdrum wood plaque awards along the way.

Ben led the redesign of Healthways.com, updating it from its prehistoric state, reducing costs by removing the need for feeding & housing the data pigeons.

In his spare time he enjoys welding, flying & needless tinkering. Do not fly in anything he’s welded (or tinkered with!).
