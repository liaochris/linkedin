Brian Koester’s Summary

I started on the paste up board ordering type from Andresen, cutting it out with an Exacto, coating the backside in wax and then carefully splicing it into the existing ad using my Mayline. Now I build stuff for a world that has transformed to the internet. In between I created car ads for Mitsubishi, launched a 300 million dollar campaign for IBM software, created unconventional advertising for a Nestle candy, won a Belding bowl, and grew PeoplePC Online from 500,000 members to a million. What's next? I don't know.
