Jennifer Frias’s Summary

I began JF Website and Technology Consulting with the experience gained from my previous experience in sales, experience with real estate website and technology research, and my teaching background. All of these experiences have helped me to broaden my understanding of how to assist and teach Realtors and other sales professionals to expand their niche on the web. Knowing that Realtors have little time to devote to learning technology, I provide assistance in catching them up to the latest technological advances and get them started to promote themselves and their listings effectively. I assist in helping Realtors focus on thier clients, yet help them become a powerful force in the new market of the internet, which is becoming a powerful tool in real estate today!

Previously, I was responsible to maintain the current corporate website for a local DC suburban residential real estate company. In addition to regular maintenance, I was also responsible for finding a better web solution to market the company's brand on the internet. The process involved extensive research of lead generation methods, lead management, vendor negotiating, leading a team to accomplish the end goals, etc.

Editing written content was another facet of my position and encompassed web and print marketing edits. One of the lead generating projects included the Montgomery County Blog in which I networked with local businesses to create a blog for the community. I authored and edited content for this blog about local events as well as real estate-related topics.

I also spearheaded a corporate effort to actively give back to the community through charitable events such as blood drives for the American Red Cross, etc.
