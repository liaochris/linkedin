Mark Ingraham’s Summary

Mark Ingraham; Proprietor; ingraham.creative - Design & Advertising

Innate skills in art and typography led to a 20+ year career in Chicago
Ad Agency Creative Departments as an Art Director.
Art Directors are advertising-focused 'Designers', that hone the craft
of defining and communicating a company's strengths.
In short, The Art of Selling and Persuasion.

Taking the reins, Mark is now Proprietor of ingraham.creative,
a Design and Advertising firm specializing in aggressive branding
solutions built on a strong strategic foundation.
The key is to hone in on a company's most unique point of difference,
and then amplify that single-minded message in a different, relevant,
and bold fashion.

Mark's background enables him and his team to apply this focus in all
visual mediums; from web to print; brochures, signage, broadcast video
and guerrilla tactics.

ingraham.creative works with aggressive companies desiring growth
and attention. Companies that are launching new entities or going through
transition, that need to get the word out about "who they are".
These companies understand that they need bold communication that will
get noticed, and stimulate a relationship.

Your communication can, and should, INSPIRE you, your team,
and your prospects. Let's make a statement.
