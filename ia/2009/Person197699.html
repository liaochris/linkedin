Richard Baxter’s Summary

An intuitive, professional leader with a passion for taking on and seeing through challenging assignments. Adept at generating conceptual possibilities, I enjoy developing and implementing comprehensive systems to solve organisational problems.

I believe that making a noticeable contribution to business is paramount and relish the opportunities afforded to influence and mould future strategy. This belief has led me to work in a number of different industries and make a difference to them all.

My management style is such that I am a natural goal setter who enjoys coaching and mentoring my staff to achieve their full potential, whilst maintaining an interest in and consideration for them as individuals. I am happy to be hands-on when required and prefer to lead by example.

Strong IT operational leadership and strategic-level project / programme management experience. Results orientated and delivery-focused, with IT department, infrastructure & systems development skills.

Experience of change management, business-aligned IT architecture, strategy definition, technology roadmaps, ITIL, PRINCE2, ERP solutions, networks, server technology, technology evaluation, IT teams, technology evaluation and new technology deployment initiatives within leading-edge business environments.
