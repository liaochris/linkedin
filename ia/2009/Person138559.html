Lawrence Quarles, MAR, MAB’s Summary

We are expanding our unique and only plastic blending operations in So. Bay, FL. As of now, we are the only factory that can take commingled plastic and produce consumer products. Our objective is to raise the money to cover our expansion costs. This is a wonderful collaboration between the private sector, the disadvantage labor pool, and the government. Every truckload going to a dump cost the vendor and us as consumers to pay from $45 to $125 dollars. Our system takes all grades and we blend them at a fraction on the cost.

This is a 100% recycled process. In the past recycled materials were skimmed and the quality material went to other countries and the unusable material was left for landfills. It was not logical to send anything overseas. To have it returned back as consumer goods. The cost of recycled products made overseas is equal to the cost of virgin plastics.

The recent fuel crisis forced many box companies to evaluate their bottom line. Our advancement in all areas of alternative plastics and fuels has allowed us to become a leader in all consumer and industrial applications. We have established good relationship with various handicap networks across the country to assist us on this venture.

This is the beginning of what will become the future of recycled materials, world-wide and domestically while reducing the carbon footprint. Check our site: www.plasticpalletssolutions.com

My own personal strong broad-spectrum experience has allows our repositioning of business to quickly adapt to new markets and develop innovative and profitable business solutions. Professional experience has progressively reflected competency in management and leadership and the ability to supervise, coordinate, and direct all levels of staff. Demonstrated reputation in economic development, emerging technologies, global business ventures, consolidations, and corporate restructuring.
