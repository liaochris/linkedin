Laurance Yap’s Summary

For over 10 years, I've led a triple life as an automotive journalist, IT consultant and graphic artist. Doing three things at once has not only meant I've been able to play in three of my favourite sandboxes, but has also given me a skill set that has both breadth and depth.

I've set up a network for a client, then moved on to designing a magazine for the same client. I've driven cars, written about them, photographed them and placed them into a layout all within the course of a few days. I've worked on print publications as a writer and artist and, on the technical front, behind the scenes at the ad agencies they work with.

Laurance Yap’s Specialties:

I take complicated things - cars, computers, anything - and make them simple. Through one-on-one support, writing, broadcasting or over the Internet, telling a good story is what makes a product, technique, technology or message stick.
