Patrick Anthony Howell (howellpublications@yahoo.com)’s Summary

Patrick Howell is a business development and financial services professional with over 15 years of experience from Market Street, San Francisco to Southern California (largest banking market in the US) to Wall Street in global and Fortune 500 companies as Wells Fargo, BNP Paribus, RR Donnelley Financial and Fleet Boston Financial (Bank of America). Howell-Wagari Capital Holdings, LLC has a strategic alliance with Newport Capital Consulting (www.newportcapconsult.com)and Gary Bryant in Orange County California (31st largest economy internationally).

Mr. Howell is an aggressive sales closer who services clients above and beyond as well as creative thinker who intuitively looks outside the box for solutions. Mr. Howell has business development experience in banking, publishing, international trade, the arts and technology throughout the US, Latin America, Africa and the Caribbean.

Mr. Howell enjoys an extensive network internationally; serves upon sereral board of directors for non-profit and Silicon Valley technology companies; and currently serves his global community as a mentor within 100 Black Men of Orange County and upon the board for the Life's Second Chance Organization. Mr. Howell loves spending time with his family and is a regular attendee of COR AME church in Orange County, California.
