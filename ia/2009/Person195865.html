Rafal Kwiatkowski’s Summary

I'm a L10n/I18n/G10n(G18n/G12n) DTP Specialist from Poland. I have 4 years experience as a DTP Specialist (officially since 06.2005), L10n & I18n Engineer and Proofreader & Translator.
Beginning with SDL, Lionbridge, ForeignExchange Translations, Gambit, Transware, Lidex and other (CV is available in BOX.net app.). I can handle CAT tools (Trados, SDLX, Synergy, DejaVu, Passolo, Across etc.), DTP Tools (FrameMaker, InDesign, PageMaker, XPress etc.), Graphic (Illustrator, Draw, Photoshop, Fireworks, Freehand etc.) & Office (MS Office) Suites etc. I learn very quickly and i'm also very flexible what means that the time zone doesn't matter for me - i'm available when i'm needed (excluding the WIP time).
I can prepare files to translation from InDesign, FrameMaker, Quark, Page Maker, PDF, Word, XLS and many other file formats to translate with Trados.dot, TagEditor, SDL Edit (SDLX) or other CAT tool. Then i can revert the conversion to the original file format. By the way, i can transfer translation and TMs between systems (f.e. SDLX MDB to Transit TM) using f.e. XBench tool. There's no problem with QAing the translation by Editing modules or QA Distiller to check the consistency and other potential issues.
I can perform whole production process starting with engineering, preTranslation, preDTP, QA, LSO, Translation, DTP, QA, FQC, CR.
Of course i'm also reproducting, typesetting, designing, manipulating documents and graphics, producting publications, OCRing PDFs and scans or graphic files etc.
It's very rare but sometimes i'm constructing the procedures for internal problems, writing manuals and guides to prevent or solve some technical issues - looking for the solution.
