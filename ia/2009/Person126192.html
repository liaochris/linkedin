Karen Corban’s Summary

An entrepreneur with a mission to share leading success technologies with people worldwide to facilitate their personal and professional development.

I am passionate about business and enjoy working with teams of people to achieve goals. Universal Events is the third of 5 business I have founded and grown in my career. I established my first business at age 21 with the opening of a French and Italian import clothing store in Castlreagh St, Sydney for women and then 2 years later I expanded the store to include upmarket menswear clothing.

Universal Events was ranked #13 in 2007 and #37 in 2008 on the prestigious Business Review Weekly magazine Fast 100 list of the fastest-growing Australian companies.

Together with my partner and husband Ken Wood we established Christopher Howard Events in London in 2006 (now renamed to thinkBIG education Ltd). We are expanding into Europe in 2010.

In 2008 we launched thinkBIG magazine, now Australia's #1 personal development magazine. This is a magazine for anyone who wants to be the best they can be and aspires to massive success in all areas of life.

Karen Corban’s Specialties:

Leadership, marketing, sales and negotiation, events management. I enjoy the creativity I express through marketing and to reach millions of people with the message of personal empowerment to live a successful and fulfilling life.
