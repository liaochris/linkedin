Rita Young Allen, M.A.’s Summary

Rita Allen is an award-winning speaker, trainer and vocalist. Her clients include corporate leaders, celebrity speakers and housewives. Her presentations are absolutely unforgettable and absolutely life-changing!

The last three years have been spent in Corporate America and in earning a second Master's Degree, this one in the Science of Managment. In order for Corporate America to run like a finely-tuned instrument, it must BE a finely-tuned instrument! The principles of harmony in music are the same as the principles of harmony in business.

Rita began her "stage" life at age three which developed into a full-time travel-performance life by age 19. After being awarded second place for female soloist in the Florida Gospel Music Awards, her performance career soared. She became a member of the movie cast, "Kent State Riot," starred in numerous TV commercials and made hundreds of TV appearances from news programs to full TV specials. Three years in a row, Rita was a finalist in the Mrs. Alabama Pageant resulting in a contract pageant judging position with a national pageant, judging pageants from Maine to Florida.

Since 1982 Rita has enjoyed her own, very successful, performing arts studio teaching private voice, public speaking, stage presence, leadership, personal development, wellness, motivation, and stress/anger management. She trained hundreds of students, from ages 6 to 90, and from all walks of life. She designed and directed yearly revues with casts of up to 120 including speaking, singing, dancing and drama! In 1998, 2001, 2002, 2003 she performed at Carnegie Hall in New York with her choirs. She has also been a popular columnist in for over 30 years.

Rita is President of Network Birmingham, President Elect of the Alabama Speakers Association, member of the National Speakers Association and member of the National Federation of Music Clubs. She loves a challenge and cherishes adventure!
