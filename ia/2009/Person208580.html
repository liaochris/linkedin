Sandra Wong’s Summary

I have over 19 years of experience as a Professional Linguist (Translator, Editor, Writer, Terminologist, Cultural-Linguistic Consultant, Language Team Leader, Quality Control Supervisor, Project Manager, Language Business Consultant) in English, Spanish and French, mainly in professional linguistic services specializing in trilingual creative copywriting and translation/adaptation of marketing, advertising and all communications material.

During my almost two decades of practicing my career, I have worked for government and state agencies, legal offices, medical centers, community services and non-profit organizations. In addition, I have lead language-expert teams at an educational/technical magazine, a dubbing & subtitling TV station, tourism offices, embassies, several publishing houses, entertainment companies, translation companies, multicultural language services and multilingual advertising agencies.

Some of my clients include: Verizon, AT&T, Disney, T-Mobile, Staples, IBM, Honda, Mitsubishi, Washington Mutual, Allstate, Splenda, Bacardi, Nike, Western Union, Revlon, Calvin Klein, Wells Fargo, American Airlines, Citibank, General Electric, Netscape, Medtronic, Merck Sharp & Dohme, among others.

Over the years, I have welcomed the many challenges I encountered in a wide variety of language-related assignments, which in turn have allowed me to gain a vast experience in several broader areas. And up to this day my main field of interest still remains multicultural and multilingual advertising.

The most recent bilingual advertising agency I worked at for over five years is La Agencia de Orci & Associates where I was in charge of linguistic issues and terminology management as well as quality assurance of all marketing and advertising adaptations for the Hispanic market in the USA.

I currently work independently as a private consultant in research, creative writing and language services; in brief, I can say that I serve as a holistic communicator.
