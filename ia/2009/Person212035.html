Scott Tomlinson’s Summary

Setting imaginations alight with future product experiences, now. Constantly looking to inject inspiration and possibility into the arm of culture through creative direction.

-Researching the needs of consumers. Assessing market strategies to ground ideas in the context of competition and brand direction. Scenario building through storied illustration and presentation. Concept development in 3D CAD. Technology search and consultation with other disciplines and resources for immediate feasability. Form development and modeling for prototype and testing. Production ready surface development and manufacturing liaison. Product iconography, color, finish and materials specification. Production ready specification.
