Elle De Champagne’s Summary

I'm a master of all trades... no wait a minute, isn't it Jack of all trades and master of none! ;-) But really, my mom always said you can do anything you set your mind to and that's pretty much how I've lived my life! :) And I LOVE it!!!

My short term goals are to write/direct and produce my first feature film with the purpose of winning major film festivals and having a successful film with movie-going audiences.

I have a lot of experience with making movies having directed 7 shorts; written and produced 4 of them on my own. I have experience in all aspects of filmmaking from ADR and foley to editing and catering. I can't wait to make a difference in the world.

I am also a student of life and have been seeking ways of improving mine and others for many years. I've distilled all of my learnings in my first book called "Live Your Dream, Now! The recipe for happy, fulfilling, stress-free living."

Elle De Champagne’s Specialties:

Writing, directing and editing film, and music and song composition.
I'm also an expert in how to get rid of fear and I'm currently writing my second book on this very subject called "Live Free".
