Marita Roebkes’s Summary

As Alliance and Community Manager, Marita supports the strategic and day-to-day management of Xeequa communities, customers, users and alliance partners.

Xeequa is the leading provider of online interactive business communities connecting businesses and their teams with their customers, alliances, channels and suppliers around the world for a more trustful and more successful business experience.

Marita has over 15 years of experience in channels sales. Marita was a founding member of BlueRoads, where she also served as a Channel Consultant, helping large enterprise customers manage their global channels effectively. BlueRoads' impressive client roster includes enterprises such as Avaya, Hitachi, HP, Juniper Networks, Nortel, Sun Microsystems and others.

Prior to BlueRoads, Marita was a founding member of Internet2000/Infinigate, a leading Value Added Distributor (VAD) of IT security solutions in Europe. During her tenure, Infinigate grew its channel to over 4,000 partners in more than 20 countries.

Marita's early career included various sales, marketing and operations roles in the Hotel, Travel & Tourism industry in Europe.
