Laurent Masounave’s Summary

Graduated from the ‘Ecole Polytechnique de Montreal’ in computer engineering, I have been working in the information technologies field for over 11 years, including more than 4 years as a Project Manager. I'm a member of the OIQ (Ordre des ingénieurs du Québec - Quebec's Engineering Order) and a certified PMP.

I specialize in the management of software development projects and application systems implementation projects. My experience includes project definition, project plans creation, team leading, progress tracking including status report production, budget tracking, change requests management and client relationship handling. I managed teams of technical as well as functional resources.

During my career, I took on numerous challenges in different fields of activity, in management, project management, production of feasibility and impact studies as well as software development, proving thereby my leadership, my synthesis approach and my great capacity to quickly master new work environments and tools. My experience in object-oriented software development, on Windows platforms as well as UNIX, allows me to lead technical teams with competence and assurance.

I'm known to be an empathic manager who greatly values team work and who contributes to creating a collaboration spirit and a working environment where communication is facilitated. My Strong time management and organizational skills as well my capacity to prioritize tasks allow me to manage several concurrent projects and handle stress. My customers truly appreciate my motivation to meet or exceed all the requirements and my determination to deliver high quality projects within the required deadlines.
