Tom Garz’s Summary

I'm retired now, but willing to help others when I can - either for pay or sometimes as a volunteer.

The Mission of TG Ideas LLC is to "Help make this a better world by providing information to others on what has been done already and offer up ideas on what else might be done"

My Blogs are below -

Do It Yourself Health Care" See http://doityourselfdoctoring.blogspot.com/ - featuring my personal clinic made for under $500, my own healthcare reform plan, and resource list for do-it-yourself medical tests, etc.

"Innovation and New Product Development in Do-It-Yourself Healthcare" - See http://www.brainreactions.com/innovation-and-new-product-development-in-do-it-yourself-healthcare.html

"Tom's Wish List for Health Care Information/Tools - "Super Symptom Checker" - See http://tgideas.blogspot.com/2008/11/do-it-yourself-doctoring-wish-list.html

"Interfacing with the Doctor Within" - See http://doctor-within.blogspot.com/ - This is probably ahead of its time, but I can see great potential.

“Ideas for Products or Inventions” - See http://tgideas.blogspot.com/ - This is my oldest blog and covers a wide range of topics.

I'm mostly interested in those who can't afford much - e.g. low-cost healthcare, etc.

Best Wishes to all!
