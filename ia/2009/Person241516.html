Vania Tashjian’s Summary

We offer in-home and in-office organizing and design services to help you feel free and create an environment that feels purposeful.

Need a change? Or, going through one?
If you are experiencing any of the following lifestyle changes, organizing could be just the thing you need to gain a sense of control or enhance your career or lifestyle. Our clients have found us helpful in good times and bad.

We’ve been successful with the following...
⠂Bereavement
⠂Career change / growth
⠂Efficiency / productivity
⠂Marriage
⠂Maternity / parenthood
⠂Moving / relocation
⠂Weight change
⠂Anything else that may have thrown you for a loop!

A little bit about me...
If you need a change in your life, or need to manage one, I’m your gal. My goal is to help you create an organizational system or design that reflects your needs, not mine.

Organizing has been my hobby for nearly 15 years, helping friends turn their closets into boutiques and their homes into sanctuaries.

Before starting my business, my job was to understand how people use certain products and environments, to help companies better meet their customers' needs. Now, I uncover how you use your items and spaces to help you create an environment that feels purposeful and inspiring.
