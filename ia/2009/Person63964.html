Dimitri Mastrocola’s Summary

Dimitri Mastrocola serves as a Managing Director in the New York office of Major, Lindsey & Africa (MLA), the global market leader in executive legal search. He brings over 15 years of top tier law firm and in-house legal experience to executive recruiting.

Prior to joining MLA, Dimitri worked as an M&A attorney with Fasken Martineau in Montreal, a senior associate with Shearman & Sterling’s project finance team in New York and an Associate General Counsel with Citigroup’s corporate and investment bank.

He now focuses on placing General Counsels and senior in-house counsel and compliance officers with national and international corporations, financial institutions, investment banks and alternative asset management firms. He also works on senior associate and partner searches for law firms.

Dimitri has placed the General Counsel at Alden Global Capital and Eurand (a Warburg Pincus portfolio company); the Chief Compliance Officer at Sandelman Partners LP, Merrill Lynch Bank & Trust Co. FSB and Evercore Partners Inc.; and Associate General Counsels at Fortress Investment Group, Ospraie Management LLC, Merrill Lynch, Barclays Capital, Bear Stearns, Macquarie, Taconic Capital Advisors LP, USI Holdings and Parsons Brinckerhoff. Dimitri also helped build out the Dubai office of a Top 15 global law firm by placing its Global Head of Islamic Finance. In addition he has placed partners, counsel and senior associates with premier New York law firms.

Dimitri is adept at learning and understanding the professional requirements, needs, objectives, and philosophies of both his clients and attorney candidates. In so doing, he draws upon his unique combination of law firm and investment bank legal experience and strategic personal coaching skills. His singular aim is to help both parties achieve long-term and mutually beneficial professional relationships.

Dimitri received his Bachelor of Laws and Bachelor of Civil Law from McGill University in 1989.
