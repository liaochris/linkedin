Lou Susi’s Summary

Online media designer and developer with broad expertise and experience in user experience and interaction design, corporate identity, web presence, new media demos and tutorials, email and direct marketing campaigns. Particularly focused on developing crisp, memorable user-centered online experiences that promote return visitation through sticky media implementation and all the latest Ajaxy, Web 2.0 goodness. Would like to build long-term, mutually-beneficial business relationships and partnerships.

I am currently looking to establish new independent contract ( freelance | consulting ) relationships with medium-to-large size businesses. I am also open to competitive offers for full-time employment in a role that allows for strategic conceptual contributions as well as plenty of design thinking and process.

Let's innovate and keep things fresh and evolving.

No innovation wannabes need apply ... if you know what I mean.
