Robert Beyer’s Summary

Versatile top-performing Chief Engineer with 31-years experience in film and television production, post production, and broadcasting. A wide-range of people and project management experience along with hands-on experience working with a broad range of technology and professional video, audio, and media equipment. Extremely strong technical and management skills with an exceptional ability to quickly master new technology and systems to capitalize on their capabilities.

Excellent writing and documentation skills. Experience writing RFI/RFP, proposals for funding, FAQ, instruction guides, developing and executing capital budgets. Experience working with vendors in negotiating contracts, achieving deliverables, and meeting service level agreements.
