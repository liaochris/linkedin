Marc Appleman’s Summary

Strong editorial, programming, business and management experience in new and traditional sports media. Senior level experience in Internet, TV, radio, newspaper, and magazine. Has successfully merged strategic vision with creative and business implementation: President of Content and Programming at Nobok Sports; Senior Coordinating Editor for ESPN New Media and Director of SportsNation; Developed and launched the AOL Sports Channel; COO of Active.com; General Manager, Managing Editor and Director of Programming for arena1.com; Managing Editor of Fox Sports.com; Associate editor and Director of TV and Radio for Sports Illustrated For Kids; Sportswriter for The Los Angeles Times San Diego edition.
