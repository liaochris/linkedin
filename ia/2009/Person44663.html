Christina Merl’s Summary

Christina Merl has a background in translation science and communities of practice (CoP) and works as a language professional, community facilitator, and freelance writer from Vienna, Austria.

Christina's focus is the integrated "TalkShop" methodology, which promotes the development of soft skills in blended learning environments. Soft skills or interpersonal communication skills include language proficiency, cross-cultural communication competence, participative competence, interactive translation competence, diversity competence, media competence, and ICT skills.

Working languages are German, English and French.

Christina Merl’s Specialties:

Methodology mix, interdisciplinary approach, communities of practice, social media, f2f and online collaboration, PLE, editing, translation, negotiation tactics, conference terminology, writing skills
