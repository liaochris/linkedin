Davide Astuti’s Summary

Davide Astuti is currently working as Strategic Product Manager for Huawei, based in Stockholm, Sweden. He is responsible for product definition and planning process of Core Network products (PS, SAE) with focus on European key telecom operators, presenting Huawei's future PS network to operators' senior management responsible for decision making and collecting customer requirements.

Prior to joining Huawei, he held different positions within the telecom world for 8 years. He worked for Nokia, Nokia Siemens Networks, Alcatel-Lucent, University of Helsinki (project for European Space Agency). He has been involved in projects with major telecom operators, such as T-Mobile, TeliaSonera, KPN Netherlands, SFR France, O2 Germany, Elisa Finland, Telecom Italia.

He holds a Master's Degree in Electronic Engineering - Telecommunications. Final mark: 110/110 cum laude.
On his sparetime, he enjoys skiing, mountain biking and trekking.
