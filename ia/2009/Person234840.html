The Marketing Department works with entrepreneurial B2B and professional service providers frustrated with their current marketing plan and need fresh, tangible ideas to increase business visibility and growth. We specialize in building a targeted customer base.

► Marketing Coaching. Specializing in assisting new, established business owners and independent contractors develop a personal marketing plan, develop a new target market or a company marketing plan, using The Marketing Accountability Program or Get Clients NOW!TM

► Marketing Manager. Develop targeted, results oriented marketing plan to help gain visibility, increase customer base and establish you as an expert in your field. Once the plan is developed, execution of that plan is imperative. We can help implement marketing plans by establishing a teamed timeline.

► Marketing Consulting. Looking for tangible, executable ideas to take business to the next level? We conduct marketing analysis of your past tactics, provide solid ideas for future brand awareness improvement, develop a consistent, growing pipeline or help in making strategic marketing decisions.

► Promotional Products. Get more “bang from your buck” and create a lasting brand when you include promotional products in your marketing plan.

▌LOOKING FOR:

► Entrepreneurial B2B service providers/product specialists or established businesses who want to take their business to the next level.

►Professional Service Providers like Financial Planners, Lending Professionals, Accountants, Franchise Owners, Remodelers, Interior Designers

► Independent Contractors/Sales Professionals, Cosmetologists, massage therapists, hair stylists, insurance sales professionals or individuals responsible for their marketing and sales results.
