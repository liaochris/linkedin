Bernie Coyle’s Summary

Lean Business Operations / Transformation Executive with an established record of improving people, products, processes and profits, seeks opportunities to continue to help organizations remove barriers to change, competitiveness, innovation, and growth. Highlights include:
• Management proficiency rooted in the Toyota Production System
• Proven capability to train a workforce in Lean Thinking methodology by coaching and mentoring to ensure timely completion of quality and / or cost focused projects
• An expert facilitator for transformation initiatives including business case development, risk identification and mitigation, resource and budget allocation
• Operational Excellence achieved by using a cross-functional team-based methods
• Approach forged by both “C-Level” (Chief Executive, Chief Operating, Chief Financial, & Chief Technology Officer) and consulting experiences with business reengineering
• Key problem drivers / root causes identified and eliminated in organizations ranging from small entrepreneurial concerns to multi-national conglomerates; as well as, the not for profit environment
• Value added and creative solutions to operational and administrative issues instituted in a concise and economic fashion
• Facilitated consistent / uniform approaches for visual controls and productivity measurements; so as to communicate and promote progress, desired behavior; as well as, further opportunities for change
• Prosperity catalyst in over 40 NAICS (North American Industry Classification System) Industries with strengths in the Military / Aerospace, Pharmaceutical, and Medical Device segments
• Ability and willingness to travel
Put simply, with a consensus building style, the salient expertise is delivering measurable, sustainable, and positive results.
