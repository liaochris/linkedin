Mike Schulz’s Summary

Mr. Schulz is an accomplished professional with a background in Accounting Information Systems Development. He is a Managing Consultant within the Security, Privacy, Wireless and IT Governance practice for IBM’s Global Business Services, where he specializes in Information Security, FISMA Compliance and Critical Infrastructure Protection. Mr.Schulz applies industry best practices to ensure satisfaction of client needs while providing a high level of Quality of Service.

Mr. Schulz helps clients understand key security and privacy issues, risks, exposures and vulnerabilities by leveraging his knowledge and develops security and privacy programs to meet client’s business needs. Mr. Schulz strives to provide secure solutions to address specific security and privacy requirements.
