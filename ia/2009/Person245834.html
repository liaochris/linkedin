Wayne Buck’s Summary

My Professional Experience
---------
Growing organizations and businesses from startup to thriving concern is the focus of my career. Building a successful organization, developing efficient and effective organizational structures and procedures, and nurturing a supportive corporate culture has been the key to my success.

I am the Managing Director of RedKey Education, a supplementary educational services firm with offices in Cambridge, MA, Providence, RI, New Haven, CT and Paris.

I also provide consulting services to small and mid-size businesses as Second Stage Consulting.

I am a Partner of MarketStance, a growing and successful industry leader in the field of developing and providing strategic business information resources to the financial services industry. As COO of MarketStance I built the firm from 2 to 20 employees and earned for it an industry-wide reputation for innovative information solutions.

My Personality
------------
My Myers-Briggs type is INTJ. Following are excerpts from the description of this type to be found in Keirsey & Bates, "Please Understand Me." I found the results of the evaluation and the description to be extremely accurate:

"INTJ's are the most self-confident of all the types. Decisions come naturally to INTJs. INTJ's look to the future rather than the past. The essence of the INTJ is builder -- a builder of systems and the applier of theoretical models.

"INTJs have a drive to completion, always with an eye to long-term consequences. Difficulties are highly stimulating to INTJs, who love responding to a challenge that requires creativity. They build data and human systems wherever they work. They can be outstanding as executives who generate a plethora of implementations of ideas. INTJs usually rise to positions of responsibility, for they work long and hard and are steady in their pursuit of goals."

-from David Keirsey & Marilyn Bates, "Please Understand Me"
