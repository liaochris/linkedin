Simon Robertson’s Summary

Simon Robertson is the co-founder and owner of Del Sol Packaged and heads the English & Arabic speaking client department which manages the demands of relocating clients and their families to the southern coast of Spain.

Simon has more than 20-years experience working for Rothmans and British American Tobacco in Marketing, Account Management and laterly Senior General Management roles.

During Simon's career in the tobacco industry he has worked in more than 20 countries, lived in 9 across 3 continents, and has experienced relocating himself and his family numerous times.

As trained Arabist, a considerable amount of Simon's time has been spent in the Middle East and East Africa in a number of roles with both Rothmans and BAT. Immediately after his Arabic scholarship, his rookie posting was to Sales Management in South Yemen, which was followed by Account Management in East Africa and Country Management in both the GCC & Levant regions. Simon's final Tobacco Industry challenge was to lead BAT's ambitious market entry initiative into the Swedish market, which contributed to the PLC's Board decision to purchase the Scandinavian Tobacco Company in 2008.

Now with his own Relocation Company in the South of Spain, he uses this extensive International experience to support others in their ventures overseas. Simon has a deep understanding of personal needs and levels of complexity in every relocation.

Del Sol Packaged can support businesses, families as well as individuals through their transition into a new life in Spain, the Costa Del Sol & Gibraltar.

Simon Robertson’s Specialties:

Strategic Management, Human Resource Management, Relocation, Relocation policy, Expense Control, Supply chain, Personal effects logistics, Rental search, Real estate purchase, COL & reward Management.

    Del Sol Packaged

    (Human Resources industry)

    January 2009 — Present (9 months)

    Relocation Solutions for the Costa Del Sol and Gibraltar

    Del Sol Packaged is specialised in assisting individuals and families that have taken a decision to settle in Spain as well as companies that bring employees and their families to the Costa Del Sol and Gibraltar. We provide personalised and professional relocation services designed to meet the requirements of our clients and the special needs of expatriates.

    We offer an introduction to Spanish culture and language, assist in all contacts with local authorities and institutions and help our clients to make contacts with international networks in Spain.
    Our services extend from initiation through arrival and repatriation. Thanks to our broad business experience, we have developed a solid reference team of professional contacts both in Spain and internationally. Regardless of what problems we may encounter, our objective remains the same: to help our clients and their families to feel as comfortable as possible in their new environment.

    Our primary objective is to contribute to our clients’ productivity, minimise the administrative matters for companies, and help all concerned to settle in smoothly. This allows the company to focus on its core activities and the expatriate to concentrate on their new job from the start. We function as an efficient partner through the settling-in process, by giving our clients and their families a safe and secure feeling about their move. We recognise that every relocation process is unique and therefore calls for maximum flexibility and creativity. That’s why our tasks include finding solutions for schools, housing, and all other practical issues that may arise when moving to a new country.

    Del Sol Packaged is an independent relocation agency that has the market expertise required to save your time and money. Following a thorough briefing from the client we will tailor make a package to suit the clients’ needs.
