Jesse Akre’s Summary

Since 2002 – eCommerce has been my passion; and what a fun and adventurous journey it has been. When we started Edenvale Shoppes, we knew nothing about the web, but we knew business; possessing a war chest of experience. Edenvale Shoppes launched its first eCommerce site in 2003, and we haven’t looked back since.

As the Owner, I have coordinated every aspect of taking a business from start-up to leading internet retailer - representing over 100 leading manufacturers and suppliers on numerous highly ranked sites in the home and garden furnishings market. We build our own sites. We have built our own systems. We control our entire ecommerce process. Building the business from the ground up – you can say I have touched and done it all.

Prior to 2002 – I built a diverse experience and vast knowledge base within leadership positions at several Fortune 500 Companies – helping me to hone my inherent abilities; making me what I am today, and clearing the path for where I will be going tomorrow.
