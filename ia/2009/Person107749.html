Jeff Aulenbach’s Summary

After a successful 20 year career capped as Chief Operating Officer of a business I helped grow to 50 locations and 750 staff, I lost my position unexpectedly.

I learned the hard way how easy it is for good, hardworking people to be out of work. And I know first hand how difficult it can be to decide what you want to do next and then make a successful career transition.

That's why Be Your Best Coaching & Consulting, Inc. was created. As CEO, I combine my extensive business and coaching expertise with my passion to help people:

• Gain clarity about what is the ideal work for them
• Use cutting-edge job search tools, resumes and strategies to stand out from the competition
• Manage the interview process so they get hired

Recognized by the media as an entertaining and content rich expert, I've become a frequent media resource. Recent domestic and international media appearances including CBS television, NBC, Fox News, NY1 News, TVBS (Hong Kong) TV, WINS 1010 talk radio, HOT97 FM radio, and Time Out New York.

I am based in New York City and am easy to reach at (virtually) any time. Best is to e-mail me at Jeff@BeYourBestCoaching.com or call my office at 1-877-629-JEFF or 1-646.413.0020.
