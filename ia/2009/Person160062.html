Michael Stack’s Summary

I am a creative individual with a diverse background who possesses strong Organizational, Communication, Technical and Managerial skills. I achieved Executive Level Status in the International Independent Film Industry before returning to Graduate School where I obtained a Master’s Degree in Information Technology. While attending University, I maintained a 4.0 grade point average and was accepted as a Professor at an Accredited University in the Database Department immediately upon graduation. I was recruited into the Private Sector of the IT Industry by the world’s leading provider of CRM software solutions where I held the positions of Business Consultant, Business Analyst and Technical Trainer and obtained Industry Certifications in corresponding areas. During my tenure there, I had the opportunity to serve as Project Manager on numerous Siebel Software Solution Implementations at various “Fortune 100” Customer Sites. My most recent position was Manager of the Technical Support Call Center at the Software Industries Global Leader of providing Performance Optimization and Work Force Management Solutions to Medium and Large Corporations.
