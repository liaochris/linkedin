Stephen Sheppard’s Summary

Since 1992 Stephen has been associated with the Antiques & Fine Art business in various challenging roles. MD Greystones Gallery, New Business Manager at The Auction Channel and founding Chairman of the Lechlade Antique Dealers Association (40+ independent members). Stephen also served as Deputy Mayor of Lechlade on Thames, Gloucestershire (pop 2500).

Stephen Sheppard in 2008 was engaged as Saleroom & Internet Business Manager for The Cotswold Auction Company based in Cheltenham UK. Stephen assumed responsibility for the Cirencester, Gloucester and Cheltenham Fine Art & Antiques Salerooms, including the Live Internet Bidding platform and Bidmaster Auction Information Technology. Global Fine Art & Antiques IT & Multimedia Consultant.

In 2009 Stephen returned to Arts and Crafts Company as MD, a company founded by Stephen in 2005 specialising in Arts and Crafts 1900 - 1990. Stephen is always receptive to new challenges and opportunities in the world of Fine Art and Antiques, especialy in the challenging global Internet market sector and multimedia channels.
