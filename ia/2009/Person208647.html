Sandra Walston’s Summary

Contact me at swalston@walstoncourage.com

Courage Blog RSS link: http://social.successtelevision.com/pg/blog/Sandra_Ford_Walston

Sandra Ford Walston is the principal of a 15-year-old learning and leadership consulting firm based in Denver. Known as The Courage Expert and founder of STUCKThinking™, she is a national speaker, trainer, poet and internationally published author. With an emphasis on understanding courage behaviors and individual personality and leadership styles, the consultant/author specializes in organizational behavior that guides people to step up to their next level.

Sandra instructs for DU Graduate Tax Program CPE programs and a former instructor for the Colorado Society of CPAs. She is currently a candidate for an Honorary Doctorate Degree.

Walston is the internationally published author (Greece and Brazil) of the regional bestseller "COURAGE: The Heart and Spirit of Every Woman" endorsed by Marianne Williamson, Jack Canfield, Harriet Rubin and Neale Donald Walsch. Prior to contracting with Random House in 2001, she started her own publishing company and was the 2000 Colorado Independent Publishers Association EVVY winner. Even one of Oprah's producers learned about Sandra's first book when they called her be on the show "Amazing Stories of Courage."

Sandra's second book is currently agent represented. It extends the original research and highlights the male and female insights on the merit of courage consciousness. A few endorsements include Gail Evans, Harriet Rubin and Dr. Dorothy Firman, Marilyn Van Derbur, and Dr. Gary Namie.

Walston has written for "Chief Learning Officer","Training & Development" and "Strategic Finance," to name a few.

Walston earned her B. A. in Social Science from San Jose State University and she holds a Lifetime Teaching Credential in California. She is certified in the Enneagram and she has advanced qualifications to administer and interpret the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator.

Sandra Walston’s Specialties:

Learning to overcome the human condition at work to achive results more easily and with simplicity along with applying courage leadership; communication preferences; time management approaches; Enneagram; Myers-Briggs Type Indicator; linguistics; women and courage; coaching.
