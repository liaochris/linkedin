Kushagra Nagrath’s Summary

AN OVERVIEW

A Hotel Management graduate from the Taj Hotel School (Indian Institute of Hotel Management, Aurangabad) from class of 1996.
He started his hospitality career as a Management Trainee with the Taj Group of Hotels. Had
training and operational assignments at flagship Taj properties in Bangalore, Kolkata, Goa, Bombay, New Delhi and Madras.
In 1998 completed a Post Graduate Program from IMI in Switzerland where he also worked as a grill master chef at Planet Hollywood, Zurich.
Upon his return to India in 1999, joined Grand Hyatt New Delhi as part of its pre opening team. After the launch of the hotel, he was a key player in the development, launch and management of the award winning European restaurant “Brix”.
Subsequently in 2001, he was part of the pre‐opening team of the award winning Taj Exotica Resort in Maldives where he looked after food and beverage operations.
In 2003 he joined “The Imperial Hotel” in New Delhi during their redevelopment phase. He established the Banqueting Operations of the hotel after its renovations.
In late 2003, he and his friends formed a venture called Mahima Hospitality. During this
entrepreneurial phase at Mahima, Kushagra was instrumental in creating and managing award winning
and highly acclaimed restaurants in New Delhi such as Shalom, Laidbackwaters, Italic and “Line Of No Control”. They also launched a hotel in Goa called “Soul Vacation”.
Since October 2007, he has been heading Alchemist Hotels and Resorts as its COO. He brings with him entrepreneurial foresight combined with extensive hands‐on operational experience of more than 15 years.
