Saïd Chebacheb’s Summary

I had a lot of Theoretical knowledge during my academic training and during the training courses which I made in JEUNE AFRIQUE, a leader in press and news on the EMEA market, I had also several experiences in the private domain such as video games business, site and application programming, I have also a modest experience as a junior developer within a private computer company, I had also a great experience as developer, technical expert, trainer and Team Manager at STREAM, one of the leaders in the global services outsourcing.

Further to my experience that lasted 4 years at STREAM, I was next to the biggest French ISP and phone service providers such as CEGETEL, NEUF Telecom, NOOS, UPC NOOS, CLUB INTERNET, ORANGE and the French incumbent operator France Telecom.

During this experience, I acquired a big technical experience due to product training of these various ISP, an experience in the people management, operation management and production management, additional skills in customer relationship management and piloting of customer contact centre as well as on the methods and the practices used by those entire ISP.

I also had the occasion to discover several platforms of development under several operating systems to develop several applications and dynamic sites, to manipulate several programming languages, I also had the occasion to animate and spread several technical, commercial and communication training courses, oversee and coach teams of 10 to 15 technicians, recruit more than one hundred technicians within a technical customer service in strong growth, coach and manage production teams of 25 to 50 technicians with 3 to 5 team leaders in addition to the management of a back office team of 2 to 10 back office technician and 2 to 3 team leader, in this experience, I handled several satisfaction and KPI processes, I worked on a lot of management and coach styles and I brush up my technical and communication skills.
