Brad Nichol’s Summary

Brad is currently an Investment Management Associate at LNR Property Corporation, focused on monitoring a portfolio of 22,000 Specially Serviced loans with a team of 12 Analysts. Additionally, the team underwrites new and seasoned CMBS acquisitions for LNR. Prior to becoming an Associate, participated in LNR's analyst rotational program, which gave exposure to four core commercial real estate skills (as they pertain to the Commercial Mortgage Backed Securities industry): acquisitions, surveillance, loan management, and property management. Duties included reviewing, monitoring, and analyzing portfolios of CMBS loans that are specially serviced by LNR, underwriting new CMBS deals and the analysis of loan structures, resolution strategies, and underlying real estate valuation.
