Olivier Bourdais’s Summary

Ixxo is a Software company located in France. Ixxo develops the Exploration Engine Squido.

Squido is a high technology answer to issues of accessing to the relevant information on the Internet in a professional context. It goes far beyond the basic approach of meta-search engines and current competitive intelligence page monitoring software.

Squido, using a dynamic thematic monitoring of the Internet content, grants you a fast and accurate access the information that is important for your company whatever its origin (surface Web or deep Web), geographic location, language, format or protocol.
Squido detects new internet sources by continually browsing the Web. This is a major break-through in the field of competitive intelligence software.

Squido enables discovery, capitalization, classification, diffusion and retrieval of relevant web content without requesting any technical assistance inside your organization. The software also features multilingual technology and translates the relevant foreign documents (including Chinese, Russian, Arabic, etc.).


Our clients are professionals who manage complex information research projects. Their goal is to find first-rate information on very short notice with no prior knowledge of the web sources to explore.
