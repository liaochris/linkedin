Bryan Segal’s Summary

comScore Media Metrix is the standard in internet measurement. As we work with most of the major publishers, many government departments, media companies, and advertising agencies we have lots of experience and knowledge on the internet. comScore Media Metrix is the premiere Internet audience measurement service in Canada, and the only one to cover this vital market. We deliver the detailed, high-quality metrics that comScore Media Metrix clients have come to expect: individual user demographics for precise targeting, measurement of AOL proprietary and streaming media content for complete visibility of all major commercial online applications that attract consumers. comScore Media Metrix reports on more than 20,000 Web sites, organized into business segments that are co-developed with clients for maximum relevance. With 28 categories and 92 sub-categories, the finely calibrated category organization of comScore Media Metrix allows clients to align their marketing efforts against their defined competitive sets.
