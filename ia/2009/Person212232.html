Selena Delesie’s Summary

Test Manager, Trainer, and Coach

I have managed and coached on testing in small and large organizations for programs in wireless, media, embedded, telematics, web, billing, and speech technologies. The last seven years I have focused on mentoring and transforming teams to be successful within their environments. I help people develop customized solutions to project and organizational challenges, and do so by blending techniques from software testing, project management, process improvement, soft-skills and life experiences. I am particularly passionate about creating empowered and collaborative organizations, by way of individualized coaching, and creating interactive training experiences.

I Believe:
- There are no 'best practices' but an 'appropriate solution' for each situation.
- In putting people first - coaching, mentoring, and encouraging people to be happy and successful. Project and company success comes with happy and successful people.

Selena Delesie’s Specialties:

• Software management and project management, leadership/mentoring/coaching, building and enabling successful teams, context-driven test methodologies, software and organizational processes.
