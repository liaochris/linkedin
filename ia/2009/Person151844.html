Mark Garcia’s Summary

I began working on my own coding HTML for personal web pages. I then went to work for Spherion (staffing co.) where I worked at IBM and AT&T in their graphics departments before joining ESS, Inc. as their graphic designer & photographer. I work in Quark XPress, Photoshop, Illustrator, FrontPage and some Flash to create both their marketing/sales material as well as their web presence. I would love to move more to print (printed sales material, catalogs, flyers & postcards), photography (product photos, landscapes, real estate photos) and technical illustration.
