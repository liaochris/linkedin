    Blackbird e-Solutions is a consulting, web services and outsourcing company, specializing in Localization and Search Engine Marketing (SEM)/Search Engine Optimization (SE0).

    BbeS is also not your typical consulting firm. Our organizational structure is a fundamental example of how we are different. Instead of the old hierarchal system we use a horizontal structure, creating cross-functional project teams. Through collaboration with freelance technology and language consultants, BbeS is able to quickly organize teams with the right people, skills, and technology to deliver the quality solutions that our clients expect. This unique structure increases clarity in project direction and objectives, enables more efficient use of resources, better customer service and delivers the final product with a shorter life cycle.
