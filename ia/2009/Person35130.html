Bryan Huber’s Summary

I've been a designer for almost 20 years. Embracing the web after enjoying success in brand development and print design has been an energizing factor in my career since building my first site in 1996. My approach integrates analytics, search engine optimization, strategy, design and development to produce web standards sites, blogs, social media, email campaigns and other online components that help clients create a total internet presence based on business driven goals.

I've worked in both small creative boutiques and a large advertising agency. My work has won various local and national awards, has been featured in design and logo publications, and on MTV.

huber+co. interactive develops internet solutions for a variety of local, regional and national clients, which include the entertainment, insurance, financial services, non-profit, realty development, hospitality, property management, retail and publishing fields.
