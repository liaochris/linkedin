    WAKURIYA is comprised of two words: one from “WA” that means “Japanese”, “harmony”, and “gentle” in Japanese; another from “KURIYA” that means “kitchen” used in ancient Japan. We combine these two words to form WAKURIYA as our restaurant name with strong passion to present a new style of Japanese cuisine: one that maintains its deep roots in authentic Japanese culture and tradition, yet breathes new life into a deliciously innovative cuisine.

    Our dinner consists of two choices of prefixed courses with six and ten dishes. We use the best ingredients, including fresh sea food, poultry, meat, and seasonal vegetables, which may vary occasionally depending on market.

    WAKURIYA has a long and open kitchen counter with grills and ovens. You will enjoy and appreciate Chef Katsuhiro’s and Chef Mika’s culinary skills, such as, cutting sashimi, decorating sakizuke, freshly preparing Japanese desserts, and among others.
