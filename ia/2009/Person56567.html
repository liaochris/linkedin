Dave Updike’s Summary

I have a passion for both technology and the efficient use of the processes that use technology to create great software.

I am currently a Independent Consultant assisting companies, teams and individuals with Agile/Scrum/XP IT Projects as well as in transforming to Agile Project Management and Development.

In 2008 I was an Agile Coach and Mentor with Valtech (http://www.valtech.com) USA advising corporations and IT teams with transformations to Agile including managing their Agile projects. I am also a certified ScrumMaster.

Previously I have been a Software Development Manager of a large internet reservation application with globally dispersed technology teams.

My goals are to continue to evolve my agile expertise to create efficiently functioning teams and organizations focused on creating great software with deliriously happy customers.
