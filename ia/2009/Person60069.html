Deanna DellaCioppa’s Summary

HERstory * Most people in the music industry would list winning a GRAMMY as a #1 accomplishment, but ask Deanna and she would say, "I could die tomorrow & feel accomplished. I sang "Ribbon In The Sky" at Boston's The Wang Center in front of a crowd of thousands while Stevie Wonder accompanied me on piano... to boot I got a standing "O"! A GRAMMY would be great & I aspire to that, but I don't know what else could trump that night with Stevie"!

Born in a suburb of Boston, Deanna learned early how to drop her r's & sing her ass off! Her mother filled their home with the music of Wonder, Streisand & Presley, helping Deanna to appreciate musical diversity early on.

A full tuition scholarship recipient & graduate of Berklee College Of Music, with a BA in Music Business & Management, Deanna has performed with Stevie Wonder, Diana Ross, Yolanda Adams, Cece Winans, Joan Osborne, The Chieftains, War, & Average White Band to name a few! Deanna has been invited to entertain Patti Labelle, Oleta Adams, Arif Mardin, Alan Silvestri, Alan & Marilyn Bergman, & Pat Metheny.

Deanna's singing and songwriting can be heard in a long list of film and tv projects, but most recently in "Sex and the City", "The Sopranos", "Lipstick Jungle" & "The Hills". Deanna has sung on national campaign spots for Nabisco, Proctor & Gamble, Mattel, Siemens Technologies, Fila, ESPN, and BJs Wholesale Club. In 2000, she co-wrote and sang the massive dance single, "Higher", produced by 1999 Grammy winner, David Morales & Albert Cabrera, which went to 3 on the Billboard charts.

Deanna's album cuts include: Universal recording artist: Bret Ryan, Universal Classics/Decca recording artists: Three Graces & Sony/BMG artists: Storm...plus!
