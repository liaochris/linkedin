Cheryl Semick’s Summary

Words, to me, are living organisms. Connected just right, they can ignite a plethora of emotions. Tossed together, they fizzle. Thus, I approach my writing craft with care and respect.

With a deep foundation in sales, media and communications, my writing is concisly rich and captivating, evoking emotional response and quick action. I am impassioned by the challenge and deliver a rapid turnaround.

Educationally, I've drawn more experience from media jobs than from formal classrooms. I 'cut my teeth' at radio stations crafting ad copy - up to 18 ads a day, but I discovered my passion in writing books.

My years in freelance and on-the-job editing include newsletters, business proposals, masters theses, annual reports, resumes, ad copy, narratives, books, and more.

Along the way, I found a flair for desktop-publishing and create powerful advertisement pieces for expos, business, non-profit and private events in a wide demographic.

I work side-by-side with an award-winning web developer writing web copy, editing and proofreading, and am now learning to write html code.
