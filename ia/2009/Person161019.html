Michael Cooper’s Summary

Proven senior level executive with over 30 years of experience. Held 2 CEO and 3 GM positions and numerous high impact revenue producing executive leadership positions in Fortune 500 companies such as Reuters and Pitney Bowes, as well as emerging companies. Have consistently and successfully demonstrated ability to turn around and grow faltering operations in a timely manner. Proven leader with high energy and strong creative abilities, have continued to develop and expand revenues in media, document management, financial services and energy.
