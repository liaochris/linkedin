Tasha Lopez’s Summary

I’m an “engineer-turned-sales professional” who combines a sturdy technical background with an outstanding track record in major-account sales, charged by a desire to see my clients reach their business goals. I’m happy flying solo or working on a team, attuned to the range of communication and working styles my customers present, and praised by managers and colleagues for my contagious enthusiasm. My technical and relationship management skills, experience in a variety of industries, and ability to translate tech to non-tech-speak have been handy arrows in my quiver. I’m excited about bringing my down-to-earth problem-solving approach to the next hill that needs climbing.
