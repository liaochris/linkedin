Alain Morin’s Summary

Holding a Master’s Degree in Chemistry, a Master's Degree in Polymer Science and an Engineer's Degree in Polymer Science, I developed a 25-year experience in market development and technical marketing, in implementation of industrial R&D projects, and in management of technical teams for the Specialty Chemicals Industry (engineered elastomers & plastics, silicone & polyurethane technologies, sealants & caulks).
I held multiple key new product development positions in Europe and in the United States, especially targeted to Biomedical (implants, prostheses, medical devices) and Industrial (weatherproofing, sealing, coating) markets.

My professional attributes are the technological development (new product introduction, new markets evaluation, sales growth, partnership initiation), the human and technical means’ networking (team leadership, project management, technology transfer), the strategic analysis (growth and/or development strategies based on market analyses and business & competitive intelligence), the (internal and external) customers’ relationships (technical expertise, tech support to Production and Sales, technical assistance to customers, commercial teams’ training), and the regulatory affairs (American and European pharmacopoeia, REACH) & the Quality standards (ISO, cGMP).

Alain Morin’s Specialties:

A seasoned professional with a strong background in R&D, Project Management, Market Development, and Regulatory Affairs. Excellent teamwork and organization skills, and a long track record of success with innovative technologies, challenging assignments, and international positions. Extensive experience with technical teams management, innovation sales, and new technologies introduction.
