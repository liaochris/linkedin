Jon Bergan’s Summary

Jon Bergan is a devoted IT Professional and tech junkie with a passion for innovative web-based technologies and design. As a qualified web designer and developer, Jon believes in strongly in good design conventions, user interfaces and experiences and predominantly elegant coding with a focus on keen project management and organisation.

His industry background lies heavily in network engineering and administration, graphic and web design and other support type roles. The fusion of such roles resulted in a well spoken young man with quite a creative spark. Just the kind of spark that was necessary to ignite the flame that is Bergan Blue.

Bergan Blue is a creative design studio located in Terrigal on the Central Coast of New South Wales, Australia that specializes in web design, e-commerce, graphic design and custom web-based application development. Founded by Jon Bergan in 2006, Bergan Blue continues to grow while enchanting both website owners and users worldwide.
