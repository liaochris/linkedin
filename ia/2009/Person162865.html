Michelle Berry’s Summary

Appearances on Good Morning America and in The New York Times, USA Today and elsewhere. Trusted confidant, nationally and internationally experienced trainer, solid communications strategist. Talented media relations adviser, crisis PR strategist and grassroots activist. Former elected official and elected delegate and volunteer for Barack Obama for President. Deep experience working in grassroots, local and federal political campaigns. Additionally, can be known to sing and write or perform original poetry and one-woman shows throughout the nation. Significant experience with international and national media giants as well as regional and local stalwarts. In addition to these accomplishments, Michelle is proud to be partners with her spouse Professor Robert Lofthouse and a mother to their daughter Nina Berry-Lofthouse, who is named after legendary Nina Simone.

Michelle Berry’s Specialties:

Stress management negotiation during mergers, branding and identity, wellness in the workplace, accomplished experience working with law enforcement officers and stress management, media relations, inspirational speaking, "transformative" training, leadership and risk-taking.

  • Former Media Relations Director and Elected Delegate 22nd Congressional District

    Ithaca for Obama

    (Political Organization industry)

    January 2008 — October 2008 (10 months)

    As an elected delegate for Senator Obama in the 22nd Congressional District (which includes 8 Counties in both upstate and downstate NY), I worked with the local, regional and statewide effort to elect Barack Obama for President. Responsible for managing media relations, public relations and community relations aspects of the campaign including support to our upstate NY field coordinator, all other campaign members, promotion of events and rallies, letter writing campaigns, public events and more. After Super Tuesday results were in, our County (Tompkins) was the only county in NYS to win for Obama. Prior to the rally, our team held a "Barack the Vote" rally and concert with renowned musicians. Over 1,200 people ventured out on an icy, rainy night to be inspired by supporters and volunteers for Obama. This grassroots effort by countless people led to Senator Obama becoming President-Elect Obama!
