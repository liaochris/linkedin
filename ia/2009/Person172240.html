Naveen Jain’s Summary

Naveen Jain founded Intelius in January 2003 and is currently its Chief Executive Officer. Intelius Filed for its IPO in 2008 at NYSE under the symbol II. Previously, Naveen founded InfoSpace in March 1996 and was its CEO until December 2002. Infospace filed for its IPO in 1998 at NASDAQ under the symbol INSP. Previous to InfoSpace, Jain was a senior executive at Microsoft Corporation from June 1989 to March 1996.
