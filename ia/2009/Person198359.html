Rich Santoriello, rsantoriello@nc.rr.com’s Summary

A proven, passionate executive leader and mentor with 22 years of information systems experience in a variety of roles and disciplines. An accomplished hands-on executive manager of departments servicing multiple corporate divisions; a proven project leader, managing multi-million dollar projects over multiple sites; an effective team-builder experienced in recruiting & motivating others towards a successful project completion. An experienced executive manager, ready to contribute to the shaping and definition of the corporate IT strategy and helping to bring other departments and teammates on board; a reliable team-player who often likes to roll up his sleeves and get his hands in the mix to get the job done. Rich is an IT executive that consistently uses knowledge, experience and determination to save companies millions of dollars through the creative use of IT.

Currently, Rich is keeping his skills sharp and current by spearheading efforts towards designing, developing and prototyping applications for the iPhone and other mobile phones.

Rich is comfortable working with Social Media applications to distribute information, content and reputation management.
