Mike Weinberger’s Summary

Mike Weinberger is the founder, President of Internet Pioneering, Inc.

A results-oriented senior-level business executive, has built a successful career by utilizing his leadership abilities and sales and marketing expertise to generate significant growth by focusing on core market needs and pioneering e-commerce strategies. He has become one of the Automotive Aftermarket's most successful and respected Internet e-retailers and sought-after consultant in the industry. Mike has a proven expertise in website concept, design and marketing of automotive aftermarket accessories.
