Catherine White’s Summary

Professional Emcee, Professional Speaker, all Occasion Civil Celebrant, and Jazz/Blues Singer.

Catherine is a Freelance Writer/blogger and ghost writer.

Catherine worked in Politics and Government for over fifteen years in the NSW Parliament, and the Federal Parliament.

Responsibilities included all communications within the Macarthur electorate:

Speech-writing, Media and advancing all PR related to the electorate of the former Australian Finance & Administration Minister.

Events Catherine planned and promoted as a Professional Fundraiser are:
* The Heart Foundation, as National Happy Heart Day Coordinator,
(aka Miss Happy Heart)
* The Salvation Army as The National Red Shield Club Secretary, and
* A variety of projects with the Wesley Central Mission.
