Auren Hoffman’s Summary

Auren is CEO of Rapleaf. Rapleaf is the leader in automated search for people information on the web. Rapleaf has done more than 170 million unique searches for leading B2C companies. Rapleaf also has gathered the world's largest portable social graph. Companies can gain insight into their customers' experiences and consumers can search and individually manage their information online.

Auren is also a non-employee cofounder of BrightRoll and a non-employee co-founder of AdRocket.

Fmr Chm of Stonebrick Group. Fmr Chair ConnectorGroup, an influence marketing company. He was previously CEO of BridgePath, enterprise software to staffing firms (sold in 2002). Auren also founded Kyber Systems, an Intranet development firm (sold in 1997) and co-founded GetRelevant (sold in 2002). He is the founder of the Silicon Forum. Auren holds a B.S.E. in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research from the UC Berkeley.

investor/advisor: 750 Industries, AdRocket, BlipTV, BrightRoll, Delicious Brands, GoodRec, Huddler, LabPixies, Meebo, MerchantCircle, MesmoTV, Offbeat Guides, Palore, RateItAll, RichRelevance, Socializr, Structural Wealth Management, VoxPop.TV, Yotify, Zoom Systems.
