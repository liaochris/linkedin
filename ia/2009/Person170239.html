Mario Cubillos’s Summary

My Passion... the creative World.

I've been around the interactive / design world since it was first introduced to me in 1997. The first logo I ever created was for a surfing company in Santa Cruz, CA, which to this day is still in use with great success.

After professionally surfing for three years, I wanted to further explore my attraction to design. As I first leaned towards print I quickly founded out it wasn't enough to see my work in static form. I then wanted to see my ideas, concepts, become alive. This is when I fell in love with motion and interactive. The thought of interactive sites with the power to entertain and fully take shape became my new obsession.

I take pride in every project and am protective of creative integrity. I like to give birth to a concept, see it develop and be there to the last minute of its process.
I'm dependable, fast and flexible. I can work in a multiple array of palettes, introducing cutting edge designs to old school. Creativity, it's my world whether it's my first logo or the biggest challenge yet to come.
