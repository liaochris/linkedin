Carolina Chia’s Summary

Marketing and communications expertise runs the gamut of disciplines, including strategy and execution for TV, print, radio, direct mail, customer relationship marketing and event planning. Experience with a diverse portfolio of clients, including Levi’s, Reebok, American Express, IKEA, Apple, Land Rover. Recruited by global agencies such as Y&R, Wunderman (helped build the Singapore agency’s infrastructure & business from scratch), BBDO, and Bartle Bogle Hegarty, working in offices across Asia, in England and New York. Originally from Singapore, has lived in New York for the past 10 years. Speaks both English and Mandarin fluently and holds a green card as a permanent U.S. resident.
