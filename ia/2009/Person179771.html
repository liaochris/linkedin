Faye Hollands’s Summary

I founded Outshine Consulting in 2006 after gaining 14 years corporate experience working for global blue chip organisations including KPMG, Goldman Sachs, Citigroup and Michael Page International.

Realising the powerful benefits that professional coaching and training solutions can have on a person's life and career, I studied with Australia's leading provider of Life Coach training, The Coaching Institute, and am now a Certified NLP Practitioner, Time Line Therapist and Executive Coach.

I'm a highly motivated professional who has an infectious level of energy and enthusiasm, which I bring to every single coaching and training session. I practice what I preach and have continuously pushed my own boundaries, from cycling across Israel for charity, to migrating to Australia and dedicating myself to a complete career change.

In partnership with my clients, I strive to ensure they experience the value of coaching and appreciate the power that taking control of your life and business gives you.

At Outshine Consulting our mission is to provide individuals, groups and businesses with superior coaching and training solutions that assist in creating momentum, opportunity and transformation in all areas of their work, life and play. We serve to develop and support our clients by utilising cutting edge coaching methodologies and outstanding customer service principles to ensure that their experience with us is an invaluable and rewarding one.
