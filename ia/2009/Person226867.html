Susan Bagyura’s Summary

Blue Danube is a coaching, consulting and training company whose innovative service delivery model takes the best of products, skills and techniques to help clients achieve better results. Working closely with clients, essentially serving as an extension of in-house training staff, Blue Danube brings its focus and expertise to help clients set achievable goals, increase revenues with a better work/life balance.

Blue Danube holds the exclusive license for the Commonwealth of Massachusetts to market and sell Bob Proctor’s personal development programs. Susan Bagyura, founder and CEO of Blue Danube, is an exceptional leader within the arena of LifeSuccess Consultant organization and is fully competent to teach and coach clients through the various LifeSuccess products and programs.

With more than 25 years of highly successful sales and entrepreneurial experience, Susan works with clients to educate, motivate and develop their staff from entrance level employees up through the ranks of management and every level in between.
