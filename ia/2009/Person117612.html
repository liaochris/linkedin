John Cross’s Summary

Business executive with nearly 40 years experience in business management, corporate relations, government and government affairs, and news media. 15 years in business management, including senior corporate management, finance, marketing, public relations, and employee communications, 13 years in television, radio and print media as media company executive, reporter and editor, and eight years in government and government-related activities, including several years as U.S. Senate Staff and at a White House conference. Currently seeking new endeavors on part-time basis. In past five years have worked in information services, at a pharmaceutical company, and doing freelance writing.

John Cross’s Specialties:

Establish corporate communications and internal communications programs, develop business plans and marketing development, government relations programs, and general management.
