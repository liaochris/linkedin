Dick Olenych’s Summary

I'm very passionate about what I talk about. When it comes to business I'm right because I always put people first! It is how business should be done.

My first book, "Joe Sails" is about elevating employees' core competencies like empathy, commitment, passion. It is a business novel that incorporates humor to engage the reader in an introspective look at themselves.

It is the catalyst for change in any organization.

If a company is serious about customer service or better sales, then they need my book, Joe Sails.

I was lucky. It won a few awards. I even have a BINC number.

Dick Olenych’s Specialties:
