Drazen Jurman’s Summary

Media, Communication and Business Information Professional with special interests for ICT, electronic communication and business. Along with the implementation of new technologies in businesses and quotidian life and their functioning, I also study ICT, regulatory rules of electronic communication in European Union, knowledge and information society, Internet & new media and economic movements in the Union.
I have been writing about these topics in the last seven years for financial monthly Banka, Pc Chip and PlanB magazines.
I am also speaking on these topics on various seminars and events and provide free lance consulting services.
As Summits Division Executive at marcus evans I work on organization of world's leading legal, business and economic summits for senior decision-makers.
My interest include journalism, communication, ICT, event management, public relations (PR), economy, business and ecology.
I am member of Croatian Journalist Society and International Journalist Society.
