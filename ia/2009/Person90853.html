Gunjan Shah’s Summary

Gunjan earned his degree in Electrical Engineering from the nation's oldest private military college, Norwich University and holds an MBA from DePaul University. His experience ranges from defense programs to satellite communication provides him with a varied background on leading technologies. He is now pursuing LEED Certification and NABCEP certification.

Gunjan began his career at Raytheon in Marlboro, Massachusetts. Raytheon is a technology and innovation leader specializing in defense, homeland security and other government markets throughout the world. Gunjan was the lead reliability engineering on various missile, satellite, and top secret government projects. He moved to the windy city of Chicago working on fiber optic transceivers at Stratos Lightwave. He was the manager for the electrical reliability group. He led the team to improve the optoelectronic connectivity and signal strength. Gunjan recently directed multi-million dollar software projects for Motorola. His strong project management skills, financial acumen, and strong technical skills align Gunjan to be a strong member of the organization.

Gunjan's aspiration has always been to help social causes; the one he is focused on is the environment and help others in building a green future.
