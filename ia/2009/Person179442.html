OROORO Shoes Designer Italy’s Summary

OROORO by Oronzo De Matteis
1994 In Lecce he obtains the school-leaving certificate for the Institute for trade and handicraft and devotes himself to the entrepreneurship

2003 In San Mauro Pascoli in Emilia-Romagna,above all in field of footwear and of CERCAL (Research Centre and Footwear International school) set up by the stylist Sergio Rossi, he collaborates with important firms like BALDININI, BRUNO MAGLI, CASADEI, POLLINI, SERGIO ROSSI GUCCI GROUP

2003 He obtains the skills certificate after the training course for Operators in development and cut through CAD financed by the European Social Fund and by the Region E.Romagna. (The course based on the notions of the art of modelling, on the study of materials and quality and the study of the CAD/CAM system).

2003He works in a firm for women footwear, known worldwide for its elegance, quality and comfort: the Sergio Rossi Gucci Group one of the greatest name of the Made in Italy, in close relationship with stylists like Sergio Rossi, Edmundo Castillo and Francesco Russo for the Yves Saint Laurent

2004 He obtains with high marks the certificate of "PATTERN MAKER” footwear technician (the job consists in developing the outline of creative idea and in designing it in real scale with all particulars).

2004 He takes part in the fifth Edition of the International Competition (A Talent for the shoe) directed to new stylists. (The competition consisted in working out a woman footwear, inspired by the subject “At the great ball with Cinderella”; his work resulted among the first thirty, which were most appreciated by the jury on a total of about 181stylists coming from all over the world and it was exhibited in Villa Torlonia). After this experience Oronzo De Matteis creates the personal mark ORO ORO, abbreviation of his name.

2005 He work as model maker in Bologna at Bruno Magli company, women footwear producer and then at Baldinini in S.Mauro Pascoli, where he met the stylist Claudio Merazzi.

OROORO Shoes Designer Italy’s Experience

  • Owner

    OROORO di Oronzo De Matteis

    (Luxury Goods & Jewelry industry)

    April 2004 — Present (5 years 5 months)

    OROORO designs shoes and their related components (forms, heels). To a great extent he designs elegant women shoes and sometimes bags and dresses and Jewelry And Watchmaking of Luxury

    His main work is to offer his customer a range of sketches and ideas to be used as starting point for a design.

    Oro’s sketches are the product of his imagination and technique. Obviously, although the sketches are based on fantasy and technique, it is important to consider the feasibility of the project, the model and the various problems which could arise during the last phase before the product is finished. As to the technical designs the attention concentrates not only on the trends, but also on the technical details which are important too.

    These sketches can be a valid support for those companies which, though already relying on their own designers, want to widen their designs or to personalize them according to their taste
