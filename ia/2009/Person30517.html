Bob Scheier’s Summary

Tough times require more than the usual marketing and lead generation tricks. As a partner with marketing automation vendor Marketo, I can develop, deliver and analyze marketing campaigns that help generate, score and close sales prospects. My 16 years experience at PCWeek (now eWeek), VARBusiness and as technology editor at Computerworld give me deep insights into the needs of CIOs and IT buyers at medium to large customers. If you're an IT vendor looking to make the most of your marketing dollars, call to learn how marketing automation -- and top-flight content -- can capture more sales even in a recession.

Bob Scheier’s Specialties:

Lead generation, lead scoring, writing and editing white papers, case studies, bylined articles, and other marketing collateral for IT firms.
