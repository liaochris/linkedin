Akash Dongre’s Summary

Akash, IIT Bombay alumnus, handles operations and product development at MoFirst Solutions. Co-founder MoFirst, Akash has gained rich experience in the telecom, mobile applications and entrepreneurial arenas.

Akash has led multiple mobile game development and porting projects, for some of the largest game and application publishers in American and European mobile market. In a short career span of three year, he has developed various Multi-player games, network based applications and mobile clients for 6 different North American Wireless Operators in two different programming languages. A J2ME (JAVA 2 Micro Edition) expert, Akash has also developed applications in BREW (Qualcomm’s application development platform for CDMA) and Android (Google’s mobile platform).

Prior to MoFirst, Akash was founding team member at Octane Technologies, a US based startup in mobile applications and gaming space. He was responsible for managing and developing mobile game projects, porting mobile applications, managing client communications, drafting technical documents and handling recruitment for the company.

His entrepreneurial aspirations took shape in his early days at IIT, where he went on to become an integral part of the Entrepreneurship Cell at IIT Bombay, and ultimately led him to create an enterprise.
