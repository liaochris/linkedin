Mikey Rox’s Summary

I'm an award-winning copywriter whose life is consumed (for the most part) by crafting compelling copy for national B2B and B2C accounts, creating print and Web concepts and campaigns, maintaining comprehensive PR programs, and expertly copy editing all manner of content.

In addition, I have a strong background in entertainment news, op-eds, features and celebrity interviews, and my portfolio contains more than 700 published print and digital articles. My articles have appeared in regional, national and international periodicals based in Los Angeles, Chicago, Baltimore, Miami, Dallas, New York, London and Toronto, among many other cities. I also provide creative consultancy and copywriting/editing services to ad agencies throughout North America, creating print and online campaigns for their respective clients.

My professional strengths include writing editorial, Web and direct-response copy, print ads, press releases, e-mail blasts, collateral material, etc.; generating creative, breakthrough ideas; maintaining positive relationships with periodical editors, media professionals and executives across the country; proficiency in proofreaders' marks and AP style guidelines; and ensuring that all copy is edited correctly and on deadline.

Plus, I'm an all-around good guy. What more could you ask for, right?
