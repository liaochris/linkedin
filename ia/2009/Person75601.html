Eric Samson’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur. Strong, global knowledge of the software industry, with a clear expertise on object, database and transactional technologies.

As a recognized expert on object persistence I'm a member of the JDO 2, EJB 3, JPA 2 and SDO expert groups within the JCP. I'm also working on DAS (Data Access Service) with the Open SOA group and on SDO 3 within OpenCSA at OASIS.

I have significantly contributed to the standardization of data access, persistence and ORM in the Java world and contributed to the emergence of data integration and data services in SOA.

I've been programmer, project leader, research engineer, CTO and CEO of various companies ranging from very large international companies (oil company, airlines, telco manufacturer, ISV) down to smaller innovating IT companies (Versant, Business & Decision) including my own startups (Xcalia being the last one).

Eric Samson’s Specialties:

Understand market requirements and trends, fund raising (both government and VCs), explain high-technologies with simple words, federate teams on a global vision, speaker in international conferences (JavaONE, JAOO, BeJUG, SOA World), write articles and white papers, product roadmap, product marketing, hiring sales and technical teams, creating distribution channels, establishing partnerships (OEMs, ISVs, SIs, etc.).
