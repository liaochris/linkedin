Chris Thompson’s Honors:

Of all the challenges I faced at Saba, the most rewarding experience was bringing a company by the name of Central Parking back onto support. Central Parking (CP) was a Centra customer, who encountered significant frustration with their deployment at the end of 2006. After several months of discussions between Saba and CP, it seemed CP would not go forward as an active Saba customer. In June 2007, I decided to call Robbin Hessa at CP just to get my own perspective of where they were coming from. After Robbin provided me details on all their open issues, I could see why our relationship was so strained. However, that call led to another, and then to another with my VP and the CP CIO. Working through the details, we ultimately arrived at an agreement that benefited all parties. The experience taught me that persistence and listening to your customer can result in success, even when it seems impossible.
