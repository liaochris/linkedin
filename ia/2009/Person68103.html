Duane Schroeder’s Summary

My formal experience with foreclosures began while working at HFC (Household Finance). At HFC, I worked through the ranks from loan officer to the joint positions of Retention Manager and Asset Preservation Specialist (1983). I have also worked with repossessions of personal property and assisted with foreclosures since 1967. The skills learned at HFC transferred to Norwest Mortgage / Wells Fargo Home Mortgage, where I continued to not only work as a loan originator but also in the REO side with Premiere Asset Services doing seminars. My seminars were of two types:

1) one designed to recruit and train realtors in the approved procedures required working with and selling foreclosures

2) the other formatted to the buyers (investors and owner occupied)

Altogether, I have in excess of 26 years experience.
