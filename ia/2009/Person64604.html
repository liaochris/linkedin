Tina Turnbull’s Summary

DJ Tina T’s musical style is simply defined by the audience she is playing for. With over 11 years on the turntables, her versatile skills stretch from unique party-rockin mash ups of top 40, hip hop, 80’s and Rock to house and electro. Crossing genres throughout the night, mixing things up and keeping crowds at peak energy is what sets her apart from your typical DJ.

As LA Weekly’s first ever DJ Spin Off winner, Tina T also went on to battle as a top 6 finalist on Los Angeles’s #1 hip hop station. She has made her way through the Hollywood scene, popping up on MTV’s The Hills, E! News and also holding down hot spots like LAX & Mood. During summers, Tina hit the road with The Dew Action Sports Tour and also released multiple mix CD’s for Reebok & Skull Candy. As a featured Red Bull DJ, Tina travels regularly to do some of the most high profile events in skate, surf, snow, fashion and technology.

After moving to Las Vegas in 2008, DJ Tina T stormed into headlining spots at the countries top clubs: Tao, Pure, Rain, Studio 54, Prive, Cathouse, Lavo, Forty Deuce, V Bar, Krave & Tabu. Currently dominating between Las Vegas & Los Angeles, Tina is able to pack dance floors worldwide and make her mark as one of the top up and coming West Coast DJ’s.

About Camp Spin Off:

Camp Spin Off is “where DJ’s go to play.” Aspiring and Beginner DJ's ages 12-17 from across the country are coming together to learn the art of DJing during an action packed week of outdoor summer camp activities!

Camp Spin Off is the only overnight DJ camp of its kind. Escape the indoor studios and classrooms to join the worlds best DJ's, teachers, speakers, performers and counselors in the great outdoors! We promise new skills, new friends and a new place for you in the DJ community...
