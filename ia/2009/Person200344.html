Robert Dorman’s Summary

My goals are to buy, sell, rent and manage property in the United States for profit. To build a multi-million dollar portfolio of real estate for investment purposes using a team of local real estate professionals and institutions as well as internet marketing.

I also buy, sell, and give away self-improvement, real estate, and internet marketing educational materials.

I am adding to my professional power team of investors, accountants, realtors, attorneys, title companies, appraisers, mortgage brokers, lenders, property inspectors, management companies, and rehab contractors.
