Kris Bierek’s Summary

ShapeFit.com provides FREE health and fitness information to people from all over the world so they can live healthier and happier lives. We have over 10,000 pages of content with fun and interactive fitness tools to help people lose body fat, build muscle and increase their energy levels!

ShapeFit.com also has a FREE online fitness tracking and social networking program to help its visitors get into shape. FitTracker provides a database of over 10,000 food items allowing members to keep track of daily calories and create custom workouts using a database of over 400 animated exercises. Users create free profiles and interact with other members to share fitness tips.
