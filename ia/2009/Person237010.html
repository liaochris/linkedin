Tony Tino’s Summary

Experienced, detail-oriented creative professional with multiple years in visual and interpersonal communication. Background includes assigning, art directing and creating print graphic design; as well as coordinating bids, scheduling production and press checking print projects. Self-motivated project manager with traditional and contemporary design skills. Software skills include Photoshop CS3, Illustrator CS3, InDesign CS3, Adobe Acrobat Professional, Quark Xpress 7, and Microsoft Office for Macintosh - Excel, Word and Entourage.

Tony Tino’s Specialties:

Print design, final production, coordinating printing, creating estimates, press checking
