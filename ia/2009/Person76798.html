Evelyn Kalinosky, CRC’s Summary

As Founder & CEO of Evelyn Kalinosky LLC, my coaching practice specializes in helping C-suite and senior-level women executives 40 and older achieve a more sacred kind of success. For these women, the desire to play a bigger game requires a strong profit motive, but making money is no longer the goal. The goal is a search for significance, a journey to uncover the wealth of the self, a rite of passage to their highest purpose, and to a life that is as unique as their fingerprint.

I’ve worked as a leader in fund-raising, sales, marketing, management, and operations with both for-profit and non-profit organizations for more than twenty years. Over time I came to the realization that I was naturally coaching the people around me in every experience I had, and decided to take that next step and gain my coaching certification in order to do the work I feel most called to do.

I am emphatically driven by a visionary culture of excellence and heart, and believe in helping women cultivate extraordinary lives of passion and purpose from midlife and beyond. I truly enjoy working with women to create ways in which they can have a positive impact on others and the world around them.

Grandma Moses began painting in her late 70s, Laura Ingalls Wilder published the first of eight volumes of the Little House series at 65, and Marjory Stoneman Douglas began her environmental crusade for the protection of the Everglades at the age of 79.

As George Eliot said: “It’s never too late to be who you might have been.”

Visit me at www.evelynkalinosky.com or email me at evelyn@evelynkalinosky.com.
