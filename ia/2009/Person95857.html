Cindy Bryant’s Summary

We are a professional full service Home Staging Company serving the Houston and Galveston area.

~Cindy is the Regional V.P. of RESA (Real Estate Staging Association) for TX, NM, OK, LA, AR and AZ.

~Member ASHSR (American Society of Home Stagers and Redesigners).
-- Featured on ABC Channel 13 Houston-"Strech Your Dollar" in 2 Segments.
~ Featured in the Houston Chronicle and Cron.com.
~ Featured guest appearances on 1070 KNTH AM Live Talk Show Radio "Keeping you in the Loop"

Home Staging is all the "Buzz" when it comes to selling your home fast and for top dollar. Our home staging and interior redesign services will make your home stand out from the competition. Professional home stagers create the "WOW" factor of model homes. Our stagers will turn your home into a showplace that will out-distance other homes on the market in your price range and set your home apart as the best overall value. In a sluggish market, your home needs to "Outshine the Competition". Our professional home stagers will artfully create a home that appeals to a wide variety of buyers. We enable buyers to see your property at it's greatest potential, staging your house to sell. Selling in a buyer's market, we utilize our creative skills in staging, merchandising and marketing a property for a greater bottom line. Don't take a price reduction before giving us a call. Avoid costly mistakes...Prepare, Stage, List, Sell! Serving the Greater Houston and Galveston area.
