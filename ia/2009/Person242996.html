Viet Hung Nguyen’s Summary

Hung has about 10 years experience working in Information Technology outsourcing services. Since graduated in 1999, he joined Paragon Solutions Vietnam (PSV), one of the top 5 outsourcing firms for US market in Vietnam. The company later on was acquired by First Consulting Group and then Computer Science Corporate to become a powerful global software outsourcing provider. During his 7 year journey with PSV, he experienced working through several positions including Visual Basic developer, Business Analyst, Project Manager. He spent 2 year working in the US with PSV’s customers in which he had chances to learn, understand, and enjoy the global context of leveraging onshore and offshore resources.

Leaving PSV, he extended his outsourcing experience with the leading software testing outsourcing firm in Vietnam, LogiGear Test and Research Center (www.ltrc.com.vn). Contributed in the effort to build the engineering service team to a 200+ people team, he had more opportunities to practice people development, leadership, and management skills.

Hung holds BS degree from Polytechnics University of HCMC, majored in Information Technology. He completed his Executive MBA Program at Shidler School of Business (University of Hawaii at Manoa) in June, 2009. He has the passion for people and leadership development for Vietnamese market. He also spends effort in promoting Vietnam to be a high quality software outsourcing destination, by building and developing world class technical resources at KMS Technology (www.kms.com.vn).
