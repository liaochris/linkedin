Hussein Ayyub’s Summary

Holding a BSc in Computer Science from the American University of Beirut and an MSc in Information Systems & Management from the Warwick Business School, Hussein Ayyub has been working with Ericsson since 2007 in the global delivery of Ericsson's best practices in managing and operating Network Operation Centers (NOC). Prior to that he was serving clients in need of expertise in the ICT and real-estate development industries since 2002. His experience ranges from developing platform-independent database driven web applications, to designing, implementing and managing computing infrastructures and management support for start-ups.

Hussein Ayyub’s Specialties:

- Project Management expertise: PMP, PROPS-C
- Standards: eTOM, MS TOP, ITIL, CMMi, CoBIT
- Programming Languages: PHP, ASP.NET, HTML, C#.NET, Java, J2EE
- Administration and Development of applications using various databases in the market.
- Platforms worked on: All the way from DOS to Vista, both Client and Server releases, LINUX/UNIX, Mac OS, Cisco IOS for Cisco Routers and Switches Configuration
