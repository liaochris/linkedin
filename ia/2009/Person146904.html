Mahmoud Al-Haj’s Summary

I started my career journey as assistant production manager with one of the biggest food companies in Jordan, & left it as production & development manager after eight years, added a valuable improvement to the products & portfolio of the company's products. The moved to a juice & canned food company as quality department manager were I started implementation of HACCAP system at the factories & improving the quality procedures & controls. Then a moved to Saudi Arabia as factory manager with Al Muhaidib Food Industries where I enjoyed managing, improving, developing, creating innovative food products & training people for the best GMPs & HACCAP basics & creating the perfect working environment. After nine years I moved to the head quarter of the group as national logistics manager taking a chance to gain a new experience in the operation sector where I managed the inventory from the point the PO is set to the point it reaches the final consumer. Managing more than 120,000 m² warehouses at thirteen city & four production units & a fleets of seventy vehicles was an interesting challenge which I really could add a valuable experience & knowledge to my career.
I joined several training courses in different fields;
• Inventory Management: Improving Profit Performance/ MARCUSEVANS Professional Training- Dubai.
• Warehousing 2007Design, Construction & Operations/Institute of International Research IIR Middle East-Dubai .
• Development & Implementation of HACCP in Food Industries/ England.
• Registered Internal Audit course for ISO 9000/ SGS-UK.
• Production, Planning & Management & Control.
• Safe Practices in Handling & Application of DETIA DEGESCH Fumigants/ Germany.
• Good Manufacturing Practices./FDA Jordan.
• Strategic Planning.
• Food Additives & Antioxidants/ FDA expert
• Time Management.
• Food Safety Management ‘’HACCP’’/ TUV/FAAHS. Bahrain.
• Course in dairy production at Danish Dairy Co. Jordan.
