Allen Burt’s Summary

As a consultant, Allen's expertise lies in complex financial, economic, and statistical modeling and analytics for business strategies, asset and business valuations, and market research.

Allen is also a social entrepreneur. He co-founded and currently runs the not-for-profit corporation, SocioEconomic Ventures, Inc. (SEV Inc.). Through SEV Inc., Allen successfully launched the ONLINE blitz marketing campaign, LibraryForLaos.org to strengthen literacy in the country of Laos by funding reading programs and book purchases for rural village schools.

Allen has significant international experience including international business and political science studies in Luxembourg, the BUNAC work program in London, a literacy non-profit campaign in Laos, and extensive travel throughout Europe, South America, Northern Africa, and East Asia.

An extroverted and energetic self-starter, Allen is known for creative results through analytical decision making, common sense, superior communications, and group collaboration.
