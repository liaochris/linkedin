Allen Mireles’s Summary

Allen Mireles is principal of Allen Mireles Marketing PR, a social media PR consultancy based outside of Toledo, Ohio. She specializes in developing social me dia PR strategies that:
• Connect clients with customers and prospects
• Communicate value and stimulate conversation
• Build brands and thought leaders
• Grow networks, opportunities, new markets, and business

For more than 20 years, Allen has worked with regional and national businesses and organizations. She has developed and implemented powerful marketing PR strategies for businesses in healthcare, manufacturing and information technology.

Today, up to her eyebrows in social networking and social media, Allen works to help companies understand the powerful benefits of social media marketing and PR—especially in a difficult economy. If you find her anywhere, you’ll find her on Twitter.

In today’s changing world economy it is all about conversation. Listening is essential. Allen’s strength is in her ability to ask endless questions, to listen intently and then develop powerfully creative solutions. Allen integrates traditional marketing PR tactics with social media marketing to create results-based inbound marketing campaigns that generate:
• Buzz
• Traffic
• Placement in traditional media

Her background includes advertising, public relations, television and radio. Allen has also directed several major NW Ohio fundraising efforts for educational institutions and non-profits.

Educated at Earlham College in Richmond, Indiana, she is originally from the East Coast and is proficient in Spanish. Endlessly curious and a voracious reader, Allen is constantly learning—about everything. Her experience, high energy, compassion and creative nature make her a natural partner for companies who understand the power of emerging social media and inbound marketing.

social media consulting, traditional and online marketing public relations, branding, corporate identity campaigns, creative direction, project management.
