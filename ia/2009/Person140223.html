Leslie McLinden’s Summary

I started in a small agency in Fort Worth, TX. I was trained in every aspect of running the agency from receptionist to bookkeeping/payroll to billing to vendor orders. I was trained on the board in design & production of business cards, letterheads, brochures, outdoor billboards, and catalogs. This included illustrations in airbrush, painting, photo retouching, typesetting, and color separations.

As a Production Manager at Pier 1 Imports, I oversaw the creative traffic flow and production of newspaper advertising, in-store signs and roto gravure ads. I have done freelance work which has included designing trade show booths, designing and producing stationery, and post work/platemaking for flexo printed packaging.

I worked at Fritz Chemical Company starting as the Assistant Marketing Manager for their Pet Product Division. Within 3 months I was the Marketing Manager for the Pet Product Division and by the end of the year I was Marketing Manager of 5 of Fritz’s divisions which included a floor tile division and oil well additive division. This required me overseeing ad agencies, national advertising, trade show events and catalog production.

My background also includes seven years as a Pre-press Manager for a narrow web flexographic printer. Here I was able to explore different methods to improve the plant’s print quality.

At Dr Pepper/Seven Up, I was able to be a part of the national re-design and roll-out of Seven Up and the international re-design of Schweppes. I was responsible for timelines and approvals from various departments including Brand, Legal and Coupons/Promotions while at the same time managing projects with local bottlers across the nation.

Working at Saatchi has given me the opportunity to research and apply innovation. While traveling on press checks, I was able to work and talk with printers on their capabilities and the innovations they were working on.
