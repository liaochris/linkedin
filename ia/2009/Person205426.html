Russell DeCorte’s Summary

I am a Professional Musician and Music Educator turned Lead Generation Internet Marketing Expert and Entrepreneur.

I currently own a Lead Generation Internet Marketing Company called, "I Am Internet Marketing". As well, I am the founder and technical director of the guitar education site, "Guitar Google. In my spare time, I am currently working on a startup project, "The Jackson Jazz Institute".

I spent six years teaching instrumental music and guitar in K-12 schools, colleges, and Universities in Michigan and Nevada.

I am always interested in learning about new opportunities in new places, and it is my goal in life to be lucrative and helpful to others while creating the wealth to leave behind several extensive music libraries.
