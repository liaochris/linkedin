Lara Blanch’s Summary

As a Digital Game Immersive Learning facilitator, I act as a bridge between learning and technology. I am able to fulfill this role because of my educational philosophy that envisages learning opportunities/links/tie-ins beyond the classroom in a variety of industries.

I am a recent graduate of York University’s Masters in Education program. The focus of my research over the past three years has been on Education and Technology, specifically gaming and education. Since graduating in May I have expanded my research purview to include potential uses of Second Life and other immersive environments for K-12 education, as well as corporate simulation and training. To that end, I have joined several in world groups. My in world name is Olyfya Jewell.

I recently completed a post-graduate Diploma in Interactive Multimedia. This program allowed me to develop strong Project Management skills, as well as hands on familiarity with the Adobe suite of products (Flash, AS3, AfterEffects, Priemier, PhotoShop, DreamWeaver etc). The basic software skills that I acquired in these areas have provided me with the ability to interact and manage programmers and designers with greater ease.

Additionally, while earning my undergraduate degree, I co-owned my own business where my analytical, problem solving and creative thinking skills were utilized on a daily basis. Prior to returning to school, I worked in Administration for fifteen years, and have several years experience in the Tourism Industry and in Event Coordination in the Computer Sector.
