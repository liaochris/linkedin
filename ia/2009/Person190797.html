Paul Trowe’s Summary

25+ years in the interactive entertainment Gremlin Interactive, Infogrames, Atari, and Greenleaf Technologies.

In June 2003 formed Pulse Interactive and ran the company with help from George Metos (Kodiak). Paul sold off the game development and publishing component to SK Telecom in August 2006 and now consults for them as well as others in the video game space. The shareholders of Pulse still retain full control of the firm because of certain confidential IP inside the company. Paul helped to form Replay Games in June 2008 to develop Wii, 360, and PS3 Digital Dowload games. Win/win deals are the only agreements that get executed to form long term, mutually beneficial relationships lasting decades.

Paul is on a number of company boards and is a frequent speaker at industry related events. The games Paul's worked on have also won countless awards over the years and he knows how to create some of the best selling games in the world, period.

Paul Trowe’s Specialties:

Entrepreneur and self starter with over 25 years in the Interactive Entertainment Industry. Good friends, good times, and making lots of money for whomever he is working with at the time is the way he likes to live. He is a skydiver, skydive instructor, and coach.

    Gremlin Interactive

As a Producer, his team also closed large scale OEM deals with major harware manufacturers including Intel, AMD, Diamond Multimedia, 3Dfx, ATI, NVIDIA, 3D Labs, IBM, Compaq, and Dell to name a few.

  • Localization Producer, Associate Producer, Production Coordinator, QA Lead Tester, and QA Tester
