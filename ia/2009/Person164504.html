Mike Langer’s Summary

Mike brings nearly 20 years of marketing and business development experience to his position, where he will be involved with prospects in the areas of Web strategy, business development, and project management for Red Hawk Technologies. Prior he was responsible for leading Northlich's new business efforts and developing opportunities with existing clients. He also evaluated and enhanced the agency’s Interactive/Digital offerings to create additional value for clients. Mike utilizes his expertise in business consulting, which includes a broad experience in traditional and Web marketing, design and usability, technology integration, web metrics and social media.

Prior to joining Northlich, he worked for Strata-G Communications and Upright Communications, two Cincinnati-based firms where he led new business initiatives and advised clients in areas such as search engine optimization (SEO)/Internet marketing, Web site design and usability, business workflow reduction, lead generation, social networking, web metrics and analysis. His professional experience also includes positions as account manager, customer services manager, marketing coordinator, and business manager, which have contributed to his knowledge of e-commerce platforms, sales force automation, business process reduction, customer relationship management (CRM), internet marketing and Web integration.

Mike has had the opportunity to work with a number of significant clients, including Procter & Gamble, xpedx, American Express, Microsoft, Shire, Wyeth, Premier Health Partners, Convergys, and Diebold.

Mike graduated from Ohio University in 1990 with a bachelor's degree in marketing and interpersonal communications. He also was selected to participate in the Advanced Academic Marketing Program for Apple Computer.
