Kevin Cooper’s Summary

Significant in-house and large law firm experience on commercial transactions, marketing, and regulatory matters for financial institutions, including credit cards and other consumer payment products, loyalty programs, merchant processing, risk management, and strategic business alliances (such as, co-brand, affinity and joint marketing / sponsorship programs). A proactive leader with an exemplary work ethic and “business / client first” approach to providing legal services. Exceptional written and verbal communication skills and the ability to work with all levels of management.
