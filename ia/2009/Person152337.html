Mark N. New’s Summary

Mark New has over twenty years of progressive experience in HR leadership roles and is a certified Job and Career Transition Coach. He enjoys an impressive track record with leading, globally recognized organizations including three of Fortune’s most admired global companies—Johnson & Johnson, Colgate-Palmolive and Sony Corporation. He holds a BS in Communication and an MA in Organizational Behavior and Psychology. Mark has been affiliated with SHRM, HR Planning Society, Organization Development Network, and WorldatWork.

Mark founded The Newmark Group and, later, Careering Today to leverage his deep portfolio of HR expertise, extensive network and entrepreneurial drive to assist both individuals and organizations through job transitions. As a former HR executive, he has keen insight and a unique perspective in understanding client needs. His clients benefit from his comprehensive knowledge of corporate cultures, organization development, the principles of talent management and individual behavior and experience successfully coaching individuals at all levels and stages of their careers.

For organizations: The Newmark Group leverages Mark’s ability to identify best-in-class candidates and asses candidate fit both for skills and styles.

For individuals:, Careering Today draws on Mark’s understanding of the inner workings of the recruiting process and deftly assesses a job seeker’s plan of action while advising clients on long-term career development strategies inherent in Mark’s philosophy of “careering.”
