Pat Graham’s Summary

Pat is a Senior Technology Executive with a high degree of commercial acumen gained from building his own business (http://www.trivaeo.com) and working with multinational organizations. Pat worked tirelessly in 4 startups to establish technology divisions and has directed major change/transformation programmes for both private and public sector organisations. He is an exceptional leader with a natural ability to inspire. He consistently provides the thought leadership to business and technology leaders which form the basis for innovative solutions. Pat’s experience includes Executive Leadership in Telecom, Web Services, Government, Manufacturing, FMCG, Banking, Transportation, Healthcare, Logistics and Outsourcing.

Pat is passionate about increasing revenue and delivering exceptional business value through the innovative use of technology. He has worked in all regions of the world and is truly a Global Technology Leader with a solid grasp of international trends, capability and culture.
