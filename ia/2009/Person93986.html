Hellen Buttigieg’s Summary

Hellen Buttigieg is an award-winning Certified Professional Organizer®, Life Coach and Gemini-Nominated HGTV host.

You may have seen Hellen on neat as the compassionate yet firm professional organizer who helps homeowners understand their emotional obstacles, face their fears, and find freedom from clutter. Currently, Hellen is a regular guest expert on the national CBC talk show Steven and Chris.

She is the President of We Organize U (www.weorganizeu.com), an Ontario based company she started in 2001 to provide clients with a personalized approach to clearing their clutter and organizing their space to regain control of their lives.

She is a columnist for Home Digest and Home and Décor. Her work has been featured in Style at Home, Today’s Parent, and TV guide magazines and in National Post, Toronto Star Chicago Tribune newspapers. She has appeared as an expert on a variety of television programs including CityLine, Canada AM and Entertainment Tonight.

As a Life Coach, Hellen empowers her clients to take control of their lives and holds them accountable as they work towards achieving their goals. She has been trained through Coachville, Coaches Training Institute (CTI), and YWCA Life Skills Coaches Program and is a member of Coachville Graduate School of Coaching.

Hellen is a dynamic speaker who motivates and inspires her audiences to get organized. She is a graduate of the Dale Carnegie Effective Speaking and Human Relations Program and an award-winning member of Toastmasters International.

She comes from an extensive, well-rounded communications and wellness background. She graduated from Seneca College with a diploma in Radio and TV Broadcasting and has completed continuing education courses in Stress and Time Management at Sheridan College.

She draws on her time management skills to balance work with a busy home life as a wife and mother of two children.
