Rony Thomas’s Summary

I currently work with VoxPR, a subsidiary of Text100 India Pvt. Limited as a Senior Consultant on the IBM account. Prior to joining VoxPR, I worked with Text100 Singapore for a year on brands such as Symantec, Lenovo and Linksys.

I joined Text 100, Bangalore in August 1999 as a Trainee Account Executive. I started my PR career working on the Microsoft, Compaq and APC accounts. I was promoted to Account Manager within 2 years and made Account Director in November 2005.

Over the past nine years, I have managed the PR campaigns for several blue chip clients including Lenovo, ARM, Cisco, Borland, Acer, Microsoft and i-flex solutions among others.I have extensive experience in providing PR services for high technology companies specifically in the areas of Enterprise Software Products, Hardware and Consumer Technology. My key strengths are my media relations skills, consultancy, research and writing skills, industry knowledge and affinity for technical information.

I was awarded the Text 100 APAC Star Award for Excellence in Technology in 2004. In 2006, I was awarded the Text 100 India Person of the Year.

I have been instrumental in targeting and acquiring new business wins for the Bangalore office and played a key role in securing at least five high profile new client/project wins in 2003 including Yahoo, July Systems, Acer, Infineon, Unisys and Trilogy Systems.

I hold a Master of Business Degree in Communication Studies from Queensland University of Technology, Brisbane, Australia. The creative highlight of my professional life was working as a production coordinator on a Merchant Ivory movie that starred Greta Scacchi. Sadly, the film did little to rouse critics or shake the box office and my brief brush with fame ended. When I'm not looking for my next blockbuster, I enjoy playing the guitar, reading computer magazines, social networking, and catching the latest movie flick.
