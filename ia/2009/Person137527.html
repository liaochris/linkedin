Laura Fontaine’s Summary

Prior to joining SP Ausnet as the new Environment Manager, I was the Sustainable Business and Environmental Manager for Proserpine Sugar where I was responsible for maintaining the environmental compliance of the activities as well as participating in value adding projects such as composting, ensuring to the long term sustainability of the business.

I have been working in the environmental and sustainability fields for the past 9 years in Europe, South America and Australia.

In 1998 and 1999 I worked in Ecuador with the Research Institute for the Development. I published an environmental and social impact research report assessing housing developments in volcanic risk affected areas of Quito.

After gaining my masters and post-graduate qualifications in geography and environmental management (waste and pollution management), I worked for Renault in France where I was an Environmental Consultant implementing environmental management systems for two of Renault’s technical research centres.

In 2001, I moved from France to Australia, learning English as a third language in addition to French and Spanish. I first worked as an Environmental Auditor for an Australian local government inspecting building sites and industries to ensure compliance with the state environmental legislation.

In 2003, I joined Fuji Xerox Australia where I worked as the Sustainability Manager and National Environmental Manager. My role involved managing the company’s environmental management system and developing the Company’s Corporate and Social Responsibility Agenda.
