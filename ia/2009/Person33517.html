Brian Kenyon’s Summary

Currently I am an Interactive Producer, at iFactory the premiere web design firm in Boston.
I have a hands-on role gathering requirements, make design and interface recommendations, and leading teams to drive the production of websites and online marketing programs while tracking success through standard project tracking methodologies, website analytics, and user behavior analysis.

I am passionate about producing creative websites and marketing solutions that connect people online and meet business objectives. I bring vision, creativity, enthusiasm, team spirit, and hard work to help production teams reach their goals and grow their influence. Through open and honest discovery, effective design, compelling content, and efficient implementation I grow online market share through strategic online marketing efforts.

Most recently I was focused on Website production and project management as an interactive Producer for Cramer Productions a Digital Marketing and Event Solutions agency. Through this experience I have acted as an interactive producer for websites like the award winning and 2008 MITX Finalist www.LittleLeagueCoach.org, www.MacworldExpo.com, www.EforAllExpo.com, and www.LiveAutographs.com.

I was also formerly the CTO at Niche Concepts LLC and Partner, Niche Concepts LLC focusing on research and development of wearable mobile digital video solutions. I also directed the JonesCAM Helmet Camera systems branding, online marketing programs, and e-commerce website development with OScommerce.

I graduated Cum Laude from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 2000 majoring in Electronic Media Arts and Communications. and have several years on webdesign and online marketing experience.

I am looking for challenging projects that will allow me to grow my network with creative and talented people, improve on my website design and usability skills, and solve online marketing challenges with effective usability, SEO, SEM, and social network marketing.
