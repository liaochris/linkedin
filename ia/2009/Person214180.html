Seth Gordon’s Summary

Gordon | Diaz-Balart specializes in the Miami market. We know the history, the people, the customs, the institutions, the economy, and the politics.

One thing we impress upon our clients is that Miami changes so rapidly you have to move fast just to stay in the same place; stand still and the world will pass you by.

The corollary: today’s “high profile player” can easily become tomorrow’s “who’s that?”

In that spirit, I’d like to tell you a little about our firm.

Our core function is to help clients grow their businesses. Specific activities include:

- Business Development – We take clients directly to the decision makers with the authority to OK a deal

- Raising Capital – We match clients in search of angel, seed, early stage or venture capital with appropriate prospective investors

- Public Affairs –Big Government is back. We get clients in the right door to make their case to the right people

- Constituency Development – If a client needs to demonstrate public support for their business plans, we know how to enlist and deploy such allies

- Civic Positioning – We help clients understand Miami’s community dynamics and how to use the civic structure as a business platform

- Social Media / News Media – We blend broad knowledge of both traditional media and social media to deliver our clients’ messages to the correct audiences.

- Survival Communications – Attacks on business can come from many directions. Angry consumers. Antagonistic competitors. Aggressive government. We’ve been there before and know how to respond.

Please let me know if you would like to discuss how we could be of service to your organization.

Seth Gordon’s Specialties:

We work with clients in many sectors but have particularly strong records of achievement in healthcare, technology, entertainment, media, real estate, financial services and arts & culture.
