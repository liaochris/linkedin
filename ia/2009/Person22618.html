Automotive Vacaturebank’s Summary

With pleasure we would like to introduce you a new initiative: Automotive Vacaturebank! A new jobboard in relation to recruitment, specified for the automotive area.

Investigation showed high demand for a complete jobboard for this specialized division. Voluminous jobboards are wide, confusing and massive but Automotive Vacaturebank is innovative and purposeful.

You have the possibility to introduce your company on this website. There is beside a space for your vacancies also a space for your personal page. It is also possible to add a Wiqet on this page. Wiqet is a Media Player consisting of pictures with sounds to make a vacancy more dynamic. Besides that, all the vacancies of the company will be shown on this personal page.

Automotive Vacaturebank uses new and progressive SEO software (Search Engine Optimalisation). This optimized instrument ensure not only that the vacancies can be found by big reference engines, but also that your own website will be better registered.

Time for an acquaintance! Now you get the opportunity to use this pleasant offer. Please contact us to use this special offer.

See you at the Automotive Vacaturebank!
