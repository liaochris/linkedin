Drs, Govert Doedijns MSc HR Generalist&C-Level Coach’s Summary

Govert is Senior Partner & M.D. of the Paris Institute (PI, (Located in Paris & The Hague). He is a Certified Executive Coach, and Professor. He has coached top-level executives from over 27 nationalities and conducted training seminars in; Dutch, English and French for leading US and EU companies His engagement with globally recognized 'Industry Thought Leaders' resulted in several listings amongst the Global top 100 and top 50 Executive coaches.

Principal clients sectors: multinational coorporations or blue chip companies.
The majority of the individual engagments have been with C-Level & Senior Executives working for large Multinationals.

Group interventions have focussed around team building and team performance. Oftentimes these were preceded by conflict resolutions interventions.

Govert also has a substantial experience, both personal as well as professional, with familly businesses.
