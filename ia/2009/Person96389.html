Huey Tan, CIPP’s Summary

Broad range of legal, business, communications and management experience from over 15 years in the IT industry. 9 years as in-house legal counsel at Microsoft Legal and Corporate Affairs department in Singapore, Malaysia and Redmond. Prior to that, 5 years as an IP attorney with Baker & McKenzie based in Hong Kong. Currently at Skype is Director for Government and Regulatory Affairs based in London. Certified privacy professional with IAPP (www.privacyassociation.org).

Developed public policy programs to promote good corporate reputation within government circles. Effective public speaker and advocacy on a range of global policy issues – software innovation, consumer protection, network neutrality, privacy and data protection, intellectual property rights protection, diversity, etc. Changed perceptions around privacy and data protection. Developed innovative and creative legal and marketing programs. Brought landmark IP legal cases. Strong team player and builder as a team manager and individual contributor. Excellent relationships with government. High energy and passionate about software and technology. Positive approach to solving problems. Strategic thinker. Demonstrates flexibility, creativity and resourcefulness in challenging legal, regulatory and policy environments. English, Mandarin and Cantonese speaker. Strong background working in diverse environments and specializes in emerging markets.
