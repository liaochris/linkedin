Peter Beckwith’s Summary

I am a New Media Producer.

I work primarily with professionals and small business owners. I help them construct a vision for themselves and their businesses then create an on-line presence that is reflective of who they really are and what their business is uniquely qualified to deliver. I do this through media rich web-sites (lot's of audio and video programs); social networking & SEO; data gathering & results analysis. Finally, after the build out of the aforementioned is done, I move you into what I call an evolution management program. That involves consistent reviews of the results you are achieving, identification of successes and failures and implementation of improvements & new projects that move you ever closer to your vision.

Research shows that web-sites that feature podcasts and video clips starring you and your clients bring new opportunities into your universe.

Peter Beckwith’s Specialties:

Helping real people get comfortable in front of a microphone or camera. Creating (or modifying) "media rich" web-sites so that real people tell the real story about you and your work. Executing processes that allow you to use more audio and video clips to market yourself without spending more money than you already are. Helping you create audio and video assets that will generate a specific and significant return on your investment year after year.
