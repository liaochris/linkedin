Monique de Neef’s Summary

I support organizations and government in the area of (Health) Care, who are struggling with enterprise skills, creative thinking and innovation, so that they can improve and/or (re)develop their processes most effectively and efficiently.

My role in this varies between consultant, project leader, supervisor, trainer, mentor and coach.

A unique point in this is that I combine this kind of work with inspiration from Sweden.
Sweden is one of the most innovative countries. Not only because of their skills but also because of their national character. As an expert in the Swedish (Health) Care system and my knowledge of the Swedish language, I "bring Sweden to the Netherlands" and/or support those people who would like to visit Sweden themselves.

My background is strategic management and quality management. Further more I completed schooling in coaching and spiritual business management.

Free tips:
Do you want to learn about how enterprise skills, creative thinking and innovation capabilities can help you to get better results in improvements and developments?
Do you want to be kept in touch with news from developments in Sweden?
Please subscribe to my free monthly (Dutch) newsletters by sending an e-mail to info@mdnconsult.nl
and please ention 'Innovation newsletter' and/or 'Sweden newsletter" in the subject field.
