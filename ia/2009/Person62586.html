Devlin Holliday’s Summary

I don't have a degree, nor did I attend a university or trade school. But studying the fields of media, tech & business is all I've done in life. Son to a stock broker & a restaurateur, I've had large ideas & hard work forced on me from a young age.

Looking to break into the field & work w/ other digitally minded people. Managed my own company. Self employed. Senior developer on most projects. I've handled all aspects of the work. concept to prototype to production.

Started editing video & doing motion graphics, but through the need to improve my graphics, I progressed into digital art & photography. Then getting requests for web design & ended up a web developer. I'm the typical designer gone developer. but hope to complete the circle & get back into higher end video editing. Be able to master all the trades of multimedia, content management, advertising-marketing & production. One of the few that do it all.

Enjoy photography. Versed in photo touch-up & improvements, or all out photo manipulation for artistic works. Designed UI layouts & interfaces w/ Photoshop. Capable of exporting design into XHTML w/ Dreamweaver or rendering for Flash. I was using Actionscript 2, but I'm picking up AS3/MXML slowly. Able to code ColdFusion/MySQL web applications & have my own *alpha framework. Been dabbling in Flex/Air/FMS. Some level of sever admin skill. Familiar w/ Windows Server 2003, IIS6, Plesk, VMWare. Working towards Cloud Hosting. Premiere Pro to edit video. Soundbooth for audio engineering. Windows XP inside & out, currently using Vista for main OS. Good education in computer hardware & components, w/ years of building my own & consulting for others.

My goal in the industry is to be the best. Like to learn fast & use what I know. Try to keep up & constantly study the market of all things. Take pride in what I do & enjoy creating tools that help people improve their lives.

Born a month after MTV. What else would I be doing. :-p
