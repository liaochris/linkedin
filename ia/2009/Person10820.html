Amanda Sanguinet amanda@sanguinetconsulting.com’s Summary

CEO of Sanguinet Consulting an advisory firm that helps companies solve challenging strategy and operational problems, gain competitive advantage, and break away from the herd. Proven track record at McKinsey & Company and Deloitte Consulting of driving results across a range of industries and situations from start-up ventures to multi-billion dollar operations, requiring organic growth and/or accretive M&A sourced growth, across multiple channels. Has held sales and marketing corporate functional roles at Google and Gap Inc. Direct.

Experience consulting to leading retail pureplay, multichannel and specialty retailers and consumer brands, including Neiman Marcus, Michaels Stores, 7-11, eBay, Blurb, Shopping.com, Nextag, Stubhub, Yahoo Shopping, Famous Footwear, and Shoes.com. Additional B2B experience at Fortune 1000 Telecom, Media and Technology firms.

An effective leader whose key strength is the ability to balance strategic creativity with operational pragmatism. Passionate about helping clients drive profitable growth by identifying attractive customer, partner and market opportunities.

Amanda Sanguinet amanda@sanguinetconsulting.com’s Specialties:

Marketing expertise: conducting research; assessing competitive industry dynamics; bringing products to market; architecting customer loyalty and social media marketing programs; developing segmentation and marketing strategies; managing promotions; executing digital direct response and brand advertising campaigns. Operational strategy experience: implementing sales force effectiveness programs; leading process and organizational change M&A teams; corporate planning; and value-based management.
