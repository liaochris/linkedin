John Sulja’s Summary

High performance executive with over 15 years experience directing first-class technology operations, infrastructures, and business strategies. Award-winning track record for building full enterprise IT operations from scratch, improving service, and aligning the IT investment with corporate goals. Experience leading global technology operations.

Complements technology leadership skills with acute business insight to support executive teams on business development, operations, and strategic growth activities.
