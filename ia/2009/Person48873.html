Conor Maguire’s Summary

EU lawyer (competition / antitrust, state aid and public procurement law).

Participant on the Hothouse year-long comprehensive support and incubation programme for graduate entrepreneurs - http://www.businessandleadership.com/technology/news/article/14181/technology/hothouse-on-the-lookout-for-16-technology-entrepreneurs

Recipient of Irish Government support to develop the Brussels Matters business.

Practised EU law for over 20 years (in London and Brussels).

In Brussels, I have worked with large and small European practises including Cabinet Jeantet, KRB Kemmler Rapp Bohlke and Simmons & Simmons.

My recent legal practice encompassed preparing a public procurement complaint on behalf of an Irish client to DG MARKT, developing a competition law compliance programme for a trade association and coordinating multi-national, non-ECMR merger filings in the agro-chemicals sector.
