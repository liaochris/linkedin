Angie Johnston’s Summary

I am a passionate and detailed oriented person who enjoys producing family friendly content for children. My career in television and animation production has spanned over 15 years. During that time I have traveled internationally to Spain, China, Mexico and Argentina to manage the production and filming of various broadcast network initiatives and have had the opportunity to work on high visibility projects for Cartoon Network [Domestic and Latin America], Boomerang and Comedy Central.

In 2007, along with my mother, husband, and a dear friend and colleague, I created Storybook Anytime- a 100% advertising free and child safe environment for children to access originally written and illustrated stories on the Internet.

Angie Johnston’s Specialties:

On-air production coordination, commercial production, International television and animation production, project management, vendor relations, budgeting, scheduling, SAG/AFTRA union negotiations, voice over and on camera casting.

Angie Johnston’s Experience

  • Owner

    Storybook Anytime

    (Online Media industry)

    May 2007 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    Storybook Anytime produces "Sam's Interactive Reader"- an Adobe Air application that is the iTunes of children's content. The product contains an an online library of originally written and illustrated children's stories, activities and other content. It is featured in the Adobe Air Marketplace as a Staff Pick and has been used as a featured case study by Adobe at the Max Developers conference in Milan.

    The entire library of content is available to pre-schools, grade schools and public libraries- free of charge. Brand new stories and activities are always in production.

    The company has also created and maintains the Facebook application, "Sam's Fish Bowl". It allows users to adopt and care for a pet fish while earning points that can be shared with "Sam's Interactive Reader".
