John Petrocelli’s Summary

Regarded as a leader in the digital media industry with a deep and vast knowledge of the digital distribution of content and the digital media ecosystem, he has been instrumental in helping media companies establish optimal strategies to secure, distribute, and monetize their content for nearly 10 years. These companies and brands include: NBA, NHL, NBC/Universal, Discovery, Intel, EMI, Universal Music Group, Microsoft and multiplatinum music icon/Rock and Roll Hall of Famer, Prince.

Working with Prince he helped Prince launch the first artist owned and operated digital music store which won a 2004 Billboard Award for "Best Use of Technology by an Artist" and later garnered Prince a Webby award in 2006 and a Webby Lifetime Achievement Award.

John began his career with six years in award winning sales at NCR/AT&T and went on to co-found Millennium Productions with partners from MIT’s Media Lab in Cambridge’s Harvard Square in the mid 90’s before relocating to Los Angeles and working at the prestigious Endeavor Talent Agency and The Firm, two of Hollywood’s top talent agencies.

He brings a rich and detailed knowledge of the media, entertainment, and technology industries to the table and specializes in developing relationships with companies and partners in the digital media industry. His particular focus today is on securing and protecting premium content for cross platform distribution and consumption and deeply versed in interoperability and monetization of digital content.

John Petrocelli’s Specialties:

Complete and comprehensive knowledge of digital media distribution, content security and protection, and digital media/online content monetization. Vast rolodex of trusted relationships and contacts. Streaming media, online video, digital music, deep expertise on how an artist can digitally monetize their content and assets.
