Matthew Doyle’s Summary

Matthew Doyle is currently a senior studying advertising management at Michigan State University. With nearly 9 years of experience in the software services and consulting industry, Doyle brings a strong background of IT skills to his current internship at Ingenex Digital Marketing. Doyle will be contributing to Ingenex virtually from Spain, while he studies Spanish linguistics in the cities of Malaga, Granada, and Valencia.

Doyle is currently the President of the Spartan International Advertising Association in addition to serving as the online marketing chair on the leadership board for The Live To Give Foundation, a local non-profit organization started by two former classmates. Scheduled to graduate in December of 2009, Doyle plans to move to Madrid or Barcelona to pursue a postgraduate degree in marketing management at a top business school.
