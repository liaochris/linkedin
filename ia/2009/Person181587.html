Patrice Blakemore’s Summary

I am a confidence coach for mothers returning to work or working. I am also an attorney and stay-at-home mother who knows the challenges of balancing life with children. As a motivational speaker and confidence coach, I show mothers how to recreate their life. I give mothers tools to use to gain control of their lives and live the life they deserve.

Patrice Blakemore’s Specialties:

creating confidence, balancing work and home life; raising children; living your passion
