Karen Leeker’s Summary

Karen is a visionary leader who can see opportunities to develop and grow businesses and has the unique ability to “make something from nothing”. A Business Development & Innovation expert with diverse experience developing new products, new technologies and new business models for a Fortune 25 consumer products company.

Karen is a true connector; she believes that connecting with people, processes, technologies and consumers is essential in developing sustainable businesses. Also an expert in all phases of the product life cycle-new product concepts to execution of National brand and product rollouts.

She has uncommon people skills is team oriented, with excellent interpersonal communication, both written and verbal and a bias for action.

Karen loves and excels on the business side, having sought, structured, negotiated and managed collaborative arrangements with partners. Licensing new technologies, products and out-licensing Intellectual Property is my expertise. She is also an inventor with 4 patents of her own so she clearly understands the language of the inventors, attorneys, all those involved in IP.

Karen has developed new businesses in excess of $1 billion global sales throughout her career. She also has the distinction of developing the highest consumer rated product in P&G history, the product scored an overwhelming 94% overall consumer product rating, with a $200 Million Sales forecast. She also managed the development of the concept, product, process, supply chain and unique business model for the product.

What can Karen do for you? Give her an unexplored market, property or technology: That’s where she thrives, making something from nothing. She has a passion for exploring the unexplored, knowing which questions to ask to identify unconventional solutions and define an efficient path forward from concepts to investment, commercialization to launch.
