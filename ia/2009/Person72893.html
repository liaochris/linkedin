  • Event Manager

    Macy's

    (Public Company; M; Retail industry)

    September 2005 — October 2007 (2 years 2 months)

    I began as an Event Coordinator in the Corporate Special Events department in September 2005, and was promoted to Event Manager in December 2006. I planned and supervised the creation of advertising vehicles and promotional pieces for my events, and managed budgets that totaled over one million dollars annually. I was also the director of the Macy’s Corporate Board, which meant recruiting, hiring and managing 35 board members while they learned the inner-workings of a major retail corporation.
