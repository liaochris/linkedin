Gareth B Jamie’s Summary

eOffice takes advantage of a gap in the market for beautifully designed, high-tech office environments for small businesses and start-ups. The first eOffice opened in London in 2002, and was the first open plan business centre in Europe. It offers virtual offices, hot desking options (with wireless access), flexible full-time office solutions and a wide range of meeting facilities and videoconferencing. At eOffice we bring people together by organizing regular networking events called Net Thursday. eOffice now serves over a thousand clients, ranging from start-ups and consultants who require a flexible office space to SMEs and even large companies searching for creative meeting spaces.

eOffice has been featured on CNN, the BBC, and in several international newspapers and magazines, including the FT, The Times, The Independent, Time Out and many more.

Awards:

eOffice has now several locations across the main cities in the UK and plans to expand internationally.
