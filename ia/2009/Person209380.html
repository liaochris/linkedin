Mike Narducci’s Summary

Mr. Narducci's SAP Consulting company is always looking for ways to help people connect. Mr. Narducci currently has dozens of SAP, C# .net, Java, Oracle, Seibel and other positions that he is attempting to fill for a slate of premier clients. NEXTEP serves as manufacturer representative for Enterprise IT solutions. NEXTEP's products focus on scriptelss application testing, SAP Security, document management, archiving, imaging, credit card processing, IT Asset Re-sales, SAP Data Management, e-Document delivery and other SAP Tools. NEXTEP provides SAP Consulting Services on a project basis. NEXTEP also provides Talent Placement services and staff augmentation within the ERP market, currently placing people with a dozen major companies in the St. Louis market alone.

Mr. Narducci has a marketing and IT background. Started with media ad agency AdvAnswers in the early 80's. Moved to Seven-Up Company Media Services, then when 7UP was sold, moved to Anheuser-Busch for 16 years. Held various positions including Field Media, National Media control, Competitive Intelligence, and media systems design. Worked within SAP Solution Center since 1998 on several SAP implementations and upgrades before starting his own company. Now focus on talent placement, selling third party SAP Bolt-Ons and managing SAP consulting projects. Started the Missouri chapter of ASUG, the Americas SAP Users Group in 1998 and is currently Program Chair of the Chapter.

Mr. Narducci is also a proud founder of the City of Wildwood, Missouri, serving 4 terms as elected Councilman.

Mike Narducci’s Specialties:

Founder & President of NEXTEP Technology Solutions www.nextep.us, an IT Consulting Company. NEXTEP helps SAP installation companies find business driven complementary tools that improve the overall ROI of their SAP investment. NEXTEP provides Consulting Services with top level ERP consultants, SAP Talent Placement Services and staff augmentation. NEXTEP specializes in the SAP and ERP market, helping to improve TCO. Founder and past president of Missouri ASUG Chapter, currently Program Chair.
