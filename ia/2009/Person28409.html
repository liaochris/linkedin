  • Chief Operating Officer and General Manager

    Broadstreet Data Solutions

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    2009 — Present (less than a year)

    Broadstreet Data Solutions has grown into one of Canada's premier boutique data solutions consulting firms. Founded in 2001 and originally called DSC, Broadstreet has deep expertise in five key verticals:

    > Master Data Management
    > Data Integration & Data Quality
    > Business Intelligence
    > Application Development
    > Information Search & Access

    Broadstreet Data has contributed leadership and tactical support of critical data management projects at some of Canada's largest companies. Customers include: CIBC, Labatt, GlaxoSmithKline, Pernod Ricard, Celestica, Bruce Power etc.

    No one can state the reason that Broadstreet Data exists and has been successful better than the CIO of a very large and now failed European Bank:

    "The reason that we are in this mess is not about data. We had all of the DATA you could imagine… so we should have seen this coming! The problem is that we did not have the INFORMATION."

    Broadstreet Data Solutions offers comprehensive consulting solutions that enhance the data life cycle of our clients to increase productivity and maximize business results.

    We will help you to get the INFORMATION that you need to make better decisions, faster!
