Neal Kielar’s Summary

As an agency innovation, business development, brand and content marketing leader I continuously search for and harness what's next – trends, methods, people, resources – while simultaneously achieving tangible, real-time outcomes.

My mission is to infuse agencies with business development stewardship, innovations to service offerings and operations, and collaboration to advance objectives that are both "what is" and "what if." The results of my efforts have been more powerful connections with clients and customers, expanded revenue horizons, service improvements, and enhanced and motivated teams – all adding up to stronger enterprises.
