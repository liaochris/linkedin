Alex Korol’s Summary

I’ve been in the Real Estate industry for over thirty years, both in Europe and in the United States.As project manager, chief engineer and principal of various companies I have been involved in the construction and revitalization of residential, commercial, and industrial real estate. I was involved in multi-million dollar projects including major international ventures. with Occidental Petroleum, French and Swiss multinational groups. After arrival in the USA I joined MJE, International REAL ESTATE concern with offices in Los Angeles and London, as a project manager and worked my way up to senior vice-president and partner .
In the US I've been involved in the development of hundreds single-family homes, numerous industrial, commercial and residential ventures from acquisition of land, development, and construction through a final sale.
I was actively involved in the sales of Real Estate properties and mortgage sales. I had a telemarketing company and was one of the original investors in California bank .To sum it up : I've done couple of things in my life.I was very lucky in a way.I had very good teachers and mentors .They taught me many great lessons and among them one I believe is very mportant: “You have to share your experience and knowledge with others. If you are willing to learn, there are always people around you, who are willing to teach.”
