Iain Bluett’s Summary

I started my first company in 2001 selling tickets and hospitality packages to major national events such as Super Bowl and The Masters. My business partner and I launched Ticket Alternative in 2003 as an alternative to the typical big-box ticketing companies. By 2008 we had a staff of 16 in the US and an office in the UK. Currently we print over 500,000 tickets per month in the US and work with over 150 venues around the country providing superior customer support and client support. The UK office now has a staff of 3 and prints nearly 100,000 tickets per month to events across Europe.

Ticket Alternative is continuing to grow. We've already done business in 48 states and as our reputation as the boutique ticketing service provider with superior customer service spreads, I hope to see a dramatic increase in the number of venues and events we work with.

Iain Bluett’s Specialties:

Ticket solutions for advance ticket sales, box office software, access control, event marketing and high volume ticket printing.
