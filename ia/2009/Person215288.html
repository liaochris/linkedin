Sharad Mehrotra’s Summary

Over 20 years of industry and advanced academic experience. 10 years of extensive experience in all aspects of business formation and operation – product development, fund-raising, marketing, sales and finance – while launching two "big idea" infrastructure start-ups as employee #1 and CEO.

Excellent team-building, product strategy, and go-to-market capabilities. Respected as a highly motivational and lead-by-example leader. Exceptional customer-facing ability.
