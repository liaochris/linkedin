Rune Huvendick Jensen’s Summary

My field of expertise is digital communication. With a current focus on web and mobile I have produced and overseen a variety of communicative products ranging from webarticles to newsletters, websites, mobile concepts, lectures, research papers etc.

I have a firm background in journalism and a strong belief in the 'good story'. This I try to use in any of the projects and products I am involved with.

Also I have a more academic background from the IT-University of Copenhagen. From here I bring an interest in contemplating and theorizing the social and cultural implications of current technological change.

As part of the project-organisation CityNova I give special emphasis to the development and analysis of innovative forms of mobile and ubiquitous computing in public, urban spaces.

I have co-edited and co-produced various non-commercial activities that sparked my interest such as Flix.dk - Denmarks first outlet for citizen journalists - and Mobil Marathon initiated at Roskilde Festival 2006 by Floc.

Rune Huvendick Jensen’s Specialties:

Journalism with special emphasis on technology and culture. Webdevelopment and webjournalism. Project management. Concept development. Teaching and lecturing.
