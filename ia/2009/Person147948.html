Anthony Wright’s Summary

Experience:
I have extensively been involved with the planning, developing and maintaining website in LAMP environment. Most of my experience is in developing PHP/MySQL driven sites, and have extensive knowledge and experience with HTML, CSS, XHTML, DHTML, JavaScripting and AJAX. I have also professionally worked with PERL, Oracle, ASP (classic), MSSQL and MS Access sites.

I am a strong supporter of software engineering derived methodologies applied to website design and planning. I believe that a successful project should be appropriately managed with a clear stated goal, milestones, and adequate resources to be accomplished on time and in budget. I feel that the lack of such management is why most web development projects run over on both time and cost.

Goals:
My goal is to seek employment at any company that values the developer and all employees, that is well managed and that listens to all the people involved in a project. My hope is to be a valuable asset within the company while still being given opportunities to advance both my career and my skill sets. I hope to return to school to earn a Masters in Information Technology or Information Systems in order to further my career. Many years later, I hope to return to school for an MBA in order to move into a managerial role.

Anthony Wright’s Specialties:

PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, DHTML, XHTML, XML, JavaScript, PERL, AJAX, ASP (classic), PostgreSQL, use of Adobe PhotoShop and Illustrator. Also have solid knowledge of various ETL techniques and software, such as DataJunction, and some database schemas, particularly, OLTP and OLAP (and star schema).
