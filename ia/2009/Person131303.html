Kevin Rogers’s Summary

Kevin has really earned his wings in preparation for joining the Visualpropaganda team having successfully launched two companies in different industries all before reaching the age of 30. Corporate America soon learned of Kevin’s accomplishments and he was called to launch divisions for several household names. Kevin approaches his business from the ground up taking a look at the entire picture from manufacturing and production flows to Marketing and branding as well as bringing over 10 years of Sales management experience on an international playground. His wide ranging background gives him a unique position in assisting companies from start-ups to corporate giants in their branding and strategic positioning needs. His clients include but are not limited to: Optex America Inc., Follett Corperation, Aqcess Technologies Inc, Helen’s Foods Inc, Etrade Financial, LendingTree, Home Loan Center, Metrocities Mortgage, Kolective Developers and Non-Profits such as InTuneWithNature and 3-Day Cancer walk.
