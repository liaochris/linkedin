Tharwat Abouraya, CTIE’s Summary

Certified Travel industry executive who brings more than 20 years experience with retail travel, tour operators, leisure/commercial travel, multinational corporations/small agencies and promotion of travel to global historic sites, including ancient Egypt. Expert in turning around and building business as well as managing operations. Track record of success in designing and implementing innovative strategies that address customer needs, increase sales/revenue and improve market share.
