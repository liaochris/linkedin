Ralph Barbagallo’s Summary

A video game industry veteran having wedged my foot in the door in 1995. Held positions in design and programming for such companies as Neversoft, Ion Storm, and Papyrus/Sierra On-Line.

I wrote "the book" on BREW game development (http://www.wordware.com/files/brew/) as well as another on MIDP 2.0 (http://www.wordware.com/files/wireless2/). My company, FLARB, has been at the forefront of wireless games and entertainment since 2001.

I'm now working on a brand new project involving monetizing massively (and not so massive) multi-player casual web games across social networks and portals. It's great to be doing something new again!

Ralph Barbagallo’s Specialties:

Game development (programming/design). Graphics/Tools development. Platforms: Mobile (iPhone/BREW/J2ME), Social/Web (Flash/AS3, MySQL, PHP, etc.), PC, PlayStation 2.
