Shaun Botha’s Summary

I am responsible for shaping the company's overall vision. In addition to being a co-founder of TPS, I am also one of the original developers of the WAVE VoIP interoperability technology. My background is in software design and development, with a strong focus on communications infrastructure and technologies. Prior to co-founding TPS, I spent more than a decade in the telecommunications industry as head of Research and Development for Williams Communications. Prior to that, I co-developed the world's first software for reading smart cards. I am a graduate of Cape Technikon in South Africa and hold a graduate degree in Computer Science.
