Marion Cox’s Summary

Educator with special interest in the integration of information literacy, problem solving, curriculum, and individual goals. Began career as a classroom teacher and am most proud of my work as district coordinator for library media services and bringing district goals in line with new initiatives and statewide goals as well as articulating with other districts to ensure scope and sequence agreement for information skills. Personal interest in counseling and helping individuals develop potential. I have two masters degrees in 1. Library and Information Science and 2. Human Services. Taught parenting classes, ran student support groups, and storytelling groups.

Marion Cox’s Specialties:

Teaching students and adults; collaborating to create and implement engaging projects where learning occurs through solving real problems; negotiating contracts; researching and implementing initiatives; project management; presenting to school board, other staff members, parents, and at statewide professional meetings; selecting, purchasing, and managing library materials and services; budgeting; web developing.
