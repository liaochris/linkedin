Robert Ebers’s Education

  • William A. White Institute - The Organizational Program

    Certificate , Psychoanalytic Study of Organizational Processes , 1994 — 1996

    The Organization Program is a two-year program that helps OD/OE professionals learn how the dynamic life of organizations affects the ability to work. Through reflective practice, didactic and experiential learning, the Program uses the knowledge of organizational learning (group and system dynamics) to develop an individual’s capability to lead and work more effectively in her/his role. Participants examine different components of organizational life - the individual, the group, and the organization – to make sense of what occurs in the workplace.
