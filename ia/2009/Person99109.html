Bill Egan’s Summary

I have spent many years in the IT industry working as a consultant and later in senior marketing roles for a major global hardware vendor.

When that industry became very volatile in the 1990’s, I was involved in establishing a B2B telesales business.

I recently decided to change direction and got involved as a founding director at Interleado.

Interleado are a startup and have developed a suite of intelligent SEO software for SEO professionals and website owners.

The first applications in the series is the "All-in-One SEO Workbench" and the "Website Profiler" - Software tools for search marketing professionals and website owners
