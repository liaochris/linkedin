David Gilbert’s Summary

Technical professional with extensive industry experience in enterprise architecture, solution architecture, system life cycle methodology, process improvement, and applications design, development, implementation and support.

Strong, creative, and dynamic leader committed to the success of the organization, with a proven track record of solving complex business problems in mission critical global environments.

Leader of large-scale, global, systems development projects and cross-functional teams. Significant experience in technical portfolio management, leading IT strategy and planning efforts, directing development teams, and staff recruitment and mentoring.

Strong methodological orientation, with a detailed understanding of a range of lifecycles and techniques, including CMM, agile and lean techniques, and IEEE Software Development standards, and RUP and UML.

Pragmatic, critical thinker with a successful record of delivering flexible solutions to meet an organization’s short and long-term objectives.

More than 10 years experience as a project manager and systems architect, with over 12 years experience with Oracle technologies, and 20 years in the technology field.

David Gilbert’s Specialties:

Enterprise architecture, solutions architecture, program leadership, project management, systems development
