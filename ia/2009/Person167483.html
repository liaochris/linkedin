Dave Moldavon’s Summary

Over the past ten years, I have produced, directed, edited, and sound-designed work in a multitude of visual genres including music videos, commercials, TV shows, shorts, trailers, EPKs, concerts, sizzle reels, and DVD menus. More specifically, for over four years I have been a senior editor at 1K Studios, a dynamic and pioneering post-house in Burbank where I edit and sound design their DVD menus and sizzle reels for most of the major studios including Paramount, Warner Brothers, Universal, Miramax, HBO, Disney, and Nickelodeon.

With all of this diverse experience, I am prepared and excited to take on any project that comes my way. I look forward to whatever that might be.

Dave Moldavon’s Specialties:

Final Cut Studio 2, Adobe Creative Suite 3, Logic Pro, and AVID (from way back.)
