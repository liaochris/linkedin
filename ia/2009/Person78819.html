Dr. Ferkan YILMAZ, Ph.D.’s Summary

As a senior telecommunications researcher with a wide range of at least 10 years’ experience in telecommunications and embedded systems, he has been working for Vodafone Technology in Turkey as a senior telecommunications researcher, where he has been engaged in the development of Value Added Services (VAS) for cellular networks and active SS7 monitoring systems (Preferred Roaming, Border Roaming, and SMS Tracing/Spamming) since 2004.

He is an effective team member skilled at clarifying research / software developing situations. He is pro-actively assisted the full life-cycle research and implementation dependent on requirements, and manages projects from concept to production and to meet tight deadlines.
