Eric Misa’s Summary

Events Studio, Inc. based in Daly City, CA. Events Studio is a family owned photo and video Production Company that was established in 2004. We specialize in documenting weddings and all types of events. We are a proud member of the Daly City-Colma Chamber of Commerce, Bay Area Professional Videographers Association (BAPVA), Wedding and Event Videographers Association, Int’l (WEVA, Int’l), Professional Photographers of America, and the Daly City-Colma Chamber of Commerce.

Eric Misa’s Specialties:

Hiring the right vendor does make a difference. Weddings and Corporate events are live event and there are no retakes in such events. It must be done right the first time. By having us document your wedding and corporate functions, you get the exceptional benefit of a creative eye with the ability to capture flattering angles and compose terrific shots, the skills to anticipate important moments and ensure that emotional moments are captured forever.
