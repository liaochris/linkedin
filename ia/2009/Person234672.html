Tim Jones’s Summary

Since the mid 1980s, I've worked in university and industrial research, in technology and environmental administration for an international NGO, and held senior business development, strategy, and procurement posts in business. I hold a PhD from Birmingham University and an MBA from Warwick. In 2008, I left Tata Group company Corus, where I was Group Director for Supplies Strategy & Development, to return to my roots in science and technology. I'll shortly be completing a masters in Science Communication from Imperial College, and am freelancing in that area.

I'm available for broadcast, photography and writing commissions, as well as strategy development/deployment and project management assignments related to science/technology/environment.
