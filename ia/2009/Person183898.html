Paul Westmoreland’s Summary

Sr. recruiting professional with 15 years of recruitment experience in the USA, Oceania, Asia, South East Asia & India. Have worked in corporate staffing (Fortune 500+ companies), as well as RPO, contingency, retained, corporate and unbundled search. Roles have ranged from technical recruiting, to recruitment & sourcing management & development, to global and local talent strategy and execution, to recruitment technology development and go-to-market services.

Most recently, the architect and director of sourcing and research divisions in RPO and traditional recruitment firms. Now developing online sourcing applications & software technology which will change sourcing.

* INVENTOR of SimplifySearch names-generation and market mapping/research technologies (going to market in '09).

* ARCHITECT of numerous sourcing & research COEs in RPO, agency and corporate settings.

* TOP 6 WINNER, Worldwide Boolean Strings Master competition 2008, sponsored by eGrabber for RecruitingBlogs.com.

* AIRS Summit & ERE speaker/author.

* CO-INVENTOR www.salesflag.com and Nouvatec Research Platform
