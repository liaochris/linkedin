Bob Hertzka’s Summary

Our goal at Resorts Online is to help upscale travelers and agents to find the best places around the world to visit based on what they would like to do and where.

In the Global Give Back Circle we are looking to help those who are fortunate enough to be able to give of their time or resources to "give back" to those young girls looking to esecape the circle of poverty in which they were born and live and to instill in the girls the desire to "give back" once they can either in spare time or after they graduate and have a job.
