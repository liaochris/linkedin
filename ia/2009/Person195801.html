Rehana Khan’s Summary

I started my career in Sales & Marketing in Europe and was slowly learning the culture of Europeans when it comes to Marketing and Sales. I was in Europe for almost 5 years and then returned to India to continue my career.

I joined with the most exciting industry of India, which is Entertainment, and I was here marketing and selling the Animation Products and Services to the European Territory especially to Paris, France, which is the hub of Animation of Europe.

After this all European episode of my career, I moved to Visual Effects and Graphics sector of the Indian Movie Industry. I was engaged in the sales of Visual Effects services for Indian Films with EFX-Magic,a part of Prasad Corporation, a prestigious organization, founded by the Great Veteran Dr. L.V. Prasad.

I am now with K-Group of Companies founded by a Gen Y entrepreneur Mr. S. Kumar from California as the Operations Head and as Executive Assistant to Chairman, SK.
