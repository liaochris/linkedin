Christi MacNee, CPT’s Summary

Christi MacNee is an AFAA certified personal trainer and a certified NESTA GEI (group exercise instructor). T3 Fitness Energy's holistic approach to fitness is built on a philosophy of wellness integration (and a lot of fun, too!).

Christi MacNee, CPT’s Specialties:

Achieving fitness goals is important, but we take into consideration your mind, body and spirit. Through our network of wellness professionals, we provide access to massage therapists, yogis, nutritionists and ready-to-go meals/planning. Your "time for fitness" should lift your spirits, energize you, open the door to a path of health and wellness and you should have fun! Our boot camps and personal training programs provide you with the maximum return for your fitness goals.
