Ed Caracappa’s Summary

A no-nonsense, get things done executive who can manage through a myriad of challenges to deliver results. A veteran of the computer graphics industry I have the experience and passion to drive the success of growth oriented businesses.

I am currently managing a diverse set of responsiblities at NextComputing, LLC where I am committed to assisting in significant short term growth. I most recently served as Commercial Director for Escape Studios United States operations where my responsibilities included introducing the company’s flagship online training products to targeted universities across the United States and Canada as well as developing and growing a New York based software and hardware resale business. In a short 8 months I established a base of operations in New York City, was able to create resale relationships with the most relevant software providers in the industry, introduced online training products to the top 10 target universities and began generating revenue.

Prior to joining Escape Studios, I served as Director of Business Development for BOXX Technologies. In this dynamic role I was responsible for developing and maintaining business relationships with high profile customers, technology providers, software vendors, OEM accounts and the International reseller channel.
