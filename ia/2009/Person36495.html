    UVC Mission: To improve the climate for and condition of veterans in San Diego County by providing: (1) channels of communications for the dissemination of information and recommendations pertaining to veterans’ issues; (2) forums for the discussion of veterans’ issues; (3) information regarding the records of San Diego County municipalities and elective and appointed officials pertaining to the support of veterans’ issues; (4) non-partisan leadership and action toward effecting change in the region in support of veterans and their families.
