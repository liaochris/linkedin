Elyse Hastings’s Summary

Elyse Hastings has been in the line of research since her college internship at a biotech start-up. She began as a legal assistant, and as her directors realized her thirst for knowledge, they began assigning her research projects for their business development sector.

After college, Elyse was hired as a research analyst at a Private Investigations firm in Los Angeles. As she grew within the firm, she gained knowledge of investigative internet research, investigative interviewing, business intelligence projects and investigative report writing. As she gained responsibilities in the firm, she eventually directed the entire Pre-Employment Background Investigations division, in which she assisted in launching, creating and driving its success.

Once Elyse moved to the East Coast, she realized she wanted a different taste of research, and accepted a position in an Executive Search firm as a Research Manager. In this role she had the main responsibilities of generating names of potential candidates for the firm's high level searches, conducting industry trend analysis and keeping abreast of the firm's client activity within the Life Sciences industry.

Elyse started working as a Sourcing Recruiter at Guidepoint Global in September of 2008, and within 5 months was promoted to manager of centralized recruting for the firm. Her group recruits advisors into Guidepoint Global's advisory network for consultations with financial clients.

Elyse received her Bachelors Degree in Political Science with an emphasis on International Relations at University of California at San Diego, and currently resides in Jersey City, NJ.
