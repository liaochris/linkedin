David Brown’s Summary

Dynamic, results driven, and accomplished executive with 15 years of business success in marketing, strategy development, international and domestic sales, and consulting. Prior position titles included Director of Marketing, Senior Consultant, Founder and Director of Market Entry for “Big Four”, Fortune 500, Wall Street, Foreign Government and Internet startups. Consistently praised for out of the box thinking, innovative strategies, successful marketing and new program development. Extensive experience developing relationships with domestic and international clients.

Business Consulting: Experience with domestic and international clients understanding their objectives and developing strategies for them. Services presented to CEO’s, CMO’s, CTO’s, Sales, Venture Capitalists and Entrepreneurs at all levels.

Venture Capital Network: Prepared, developed deal flows and delivered Australian companies to US & Australian VC community for investment consideration in hi-tech and Biotechnology spaces.

International Consulting Experience: Delivery of business analysis, strategies, marketing and sales for 100+ companies.

New Program Development: Extensive experience developing new programs or reengineering old ones in order to deliver new sales and marketing success. This includes both domestic and international marketing and sales programs.

150M+Revenue: New export, investment and sales revenue generated over past five years.
