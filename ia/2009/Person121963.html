Jennifer Brown’s Summary

I have supported and led communications and change management for start-up businesses through Fortune 100 companies. Industries included retail, automotive, technology, manufacturing, health care, logistics and nonprofit.

My role has been to drive communications, provide executive counsel and understand the unique needs of different audiences. Business changes that I've supported include CEO retirements and interim leadership models, union matters, business restructuring, lay offs, business divestitures, transition of functions into and out of the business, increased or first-time global activity, and earnings restatements.
