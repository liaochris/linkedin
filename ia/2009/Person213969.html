Sergio Medrano MBA’s Summary

Based out of Toronto, Sergio provides innovative real estate solutions to Aerospace companies worldwide, through a dedicated group of professionals in investment sales, construction, leasing, facility management, finance, and development.

Sergio previously worked as Executive Director at World Trade Center, San Luis Potosi, Central Mexico where he was responsible for strategic planning, government and industry relations and commercial space leasing.

He previously was Program Analyst for Canada´s Federal Government´s Event Sponsorship Program at the Department of Communications, Ottawa. He designed and implemented complex, in-house advertising and promotional programs that raised public awareness of the “1-800-OCanada” info line and “canada.gc.ca” government online services.

In 2002, he worked with the Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade as Partnerships Co-ordinator for the Kananaskis G8-Summit. There, he liaised with private sector companies such as Air Canada and Telus to secure collaborative arrangements to offset many of the costs associated with the Summit.

Prior to his experience in Ottawa, Sergio was a freelance corporate trainer in Vancouver, B.C. where he taught cross-cultural communications and customer service skills. Among his clients were the Ministry of Skills Development and Labour of British Columbia and the Hong Kong Canada Business Association. Prior to this, Sergio was hired by Canadian Pacific Hotels (now Fairmont) as a manager at the Fairmont Waterfront Hotel in Vancouver.

In 1999, he was invited by the Japanese government to act as youth employment policy advisor to Japan’s Management and Coordination Agency, Prime Minister’s Office, while participating in the United Nations University (Tokyo campus) program “Ship for World Youth.”
