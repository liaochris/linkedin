Ignacio Meneu Oset’s Summary

I have been working as a traditional character animator since 1985. Throughout these years I have had the opportunity of working in very interesting projects and in the last few years I have undertaken further responsibilities in some of the productions that I have worked for, such as: Head of Animation Director and Director of TV series.
At Cartoon Producción Studio – ToonF Valencia, I‘ve been in charge of the Direction of all the animation projects (several Pilots and 10 episodes of ‘Pumby’ series) and studio Assistant Director supervising animation services.
For three years I’ve been working in MSL Audiovisual & Media, as a Director of the Animation Department, developing TV series ‘Marco Antonio’, ‘Bunnibal’ and several 2D/3D headings for TV Quiz Programs.
Currently I’m working as a Director Animation Departmet at Anexa Producciones SL.
