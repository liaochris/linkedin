Joe Griffin’s Summary

Joe Griffin ( www.JoeGriffin.info ) is a rainmaker with a blue chip pedigree including The Royal Bank of Scotland (RBS-WorldPay), VeriSign eCommerce, Visa International, T-Mobile and AT&T Corp. In addition, he's guided 2 firms through successful IPO's and later acquisition: General Magic and Aerial Communication.

Before joining RBS Joe was involved at VeriSign expanding the brand, (over 1 million websites rely on VeriSign "Trusted Site" SSL), wireless consulting, VeriSign payment services, OSS applications, Internet infrastructure enhancements, and monetizing digital media at the point of convergence.

His background also includes: enterprise software, consumer electronics and emerging technologies, e.g., RFID, smartcards, telematics, SEO, and fixed mobile convergence.

He brings a wealth of senior level contacts, detailed technical knowledge and hands-on experience in assessing market opportunities, launching products, marketing strategies, creating alliances, managing JV's, and technology licensing.

As a consultant he created and implemented award-winning "go-to-market" for B-2-B and B-2-C launches, corporate messaging, competitive landscape / threat assessment and opened new sales channel for clients.

As an entrepreneur he co-founded GCS-Team, LLP, [ visit: www.gcs-team.com] a high tech consultancy and served as a V.P. with several successful start-ups. For GCS clients he choreographed VC fund raising and directed industry analyst interviews, IPO road shows and media/press tours.

Earlier Joe led a strategic business unit at AT&T and served as a technology advisor and business development executive with Visa International, T-Mobile, VeriSign, Philips Electronics in Europe and Pioneer in Japan.

Joe's academic accomplishments include an MBA from De Paul University, BS in Marketing and Computer Science from the Univ. of Illinois. In addition, he completed the Harvard Law School Executive Development Program..

Joe Griffin’s Specialties:

Senior executive and rainmaker with a unique blend of experience in payment systems, enterprise software, telecom (wireless and mobile content, fixed mobile convergence), financial services, alliances, consumer electronics, plus consulting.
