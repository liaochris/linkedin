Liza Daly’s Summary

Threepress Consulting provides software development, training and problem-solving in digital publishing. We are a leading authority on real-world ebook problems and solutions, from InDesign development to XML-based automated workflows.

As president of Threepress, I consult and speak on ebook technologies and open source software. I've authored several articles on digital publishing and web development for IBM DeveloperWorks, Information Standards Quarterly, and was featured in the 2009 Best of TOC compendium.

In 2009 I've been an invited speaker at TOC 2009, the IDPF Digital Book conference, the American Association of University Press meeting, and have given two professional development seminars for the Book Publishers Association of Alberta.

In February 2009 O'Reilly acquired my site Bookworm, a web-based ebook reader showcasing the open ePub standard. It is now part of their Labs R&D program.

Past publishing clients have included Oxford University Press, Columbia University Press, O'Reilly Media, Rosen Publishing and SAGE Publications.
