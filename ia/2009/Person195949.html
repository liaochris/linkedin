Robert Kinney’s Summary

I am a former practicing attorney and the founder of Kinney Recruiting, Inc. Kinney Recruiting has become a leading provider of legal recruiting and attorney placement services in every market we serve, particularly the major markets in California, Texas, South Florida, New York, and Asia. We are different from other legal recruiting firms in some fundamental ways.

1. We operate as a team and under the principal that bigger is not necessarily better when it comes to legal recruiting.
2. Our focus is candidate-centric. Our belief is that we do both our clients and our candidates the greatest service by working with the largest possible number of legal employers.
3. We are relatively young and energetic.
4. Through research and hard work, we have developed a very strong understanding of the markets we serve and as a result we make an unusually large number of placements in those markets as compared to other firms.
5. Each of our recruiters is a former attorney with “Big Firm” credentials and experience, so we understand where our candidates are coming from and how the choices they make will affect where they are going.
6. We are supported by technology and research unsurpassed by any other recruiting firm. A large part of our budget is spent on research assistance and technology to assist us in our searches.
7. We are active members of the National Association of Legal Search Consultants and we regularly refer our candidates to reputable members of this organization when doing so is best for the candidate.
8. Finally, we understand and accept that we ARE meant to help everyone, not just the candidates most calculated to help us earn a fee. We want everyone who comes to us to leave with the impression that we did our absolute best to help him or her in whatever way we could.

Robert Kinney’s Specialties:

Legal recruiting - we recruit attorneys and place attorneys in various law firms and corporations nationwide, as well as in Asia and Europe.
