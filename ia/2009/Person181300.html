Pascale Lane’s Summary

Academic physician-scientist committed to communication and development of knowledge. Interested in challenges of increasing scholarly activity and developing faculty leadership skills.
Editor-in-Chief of ASN Kidney News, a magazine for the American Society of Nephrology.

Pascale Lane’s Specialties:

Expertise in clinical pediatric nephrology, basic and clinical research, and education at all levels of training, especially faculty development. Interested in using media, including social networking tools, to communicate biomedical news.

Pascale Lane’s Experience

  • Editor-in-Chief, ASN Kidney News

    American Society of Nephrology

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    Founding editor of a print newsmagazine dedicated to the nephrology community. Features news on medical practice, research, education, and policy that affects anything kidney. Developing multimedia presence for the publication as well, including podcasts, discussion boards, and social media.

  • Helen Freytag Distinguished Professor of Pediatrics and Associate Chair for Research;

    University of Nebraska Medical Center

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    February 1998 — Present (12 years )

    Primary research interest is the impact of puberty on the kidney in diabetes mellitus, especially the interactions of sex hormones and pathologic kidney growth. Other responsibilities include patient care, directing and developing the Pediatric Research Office, teaching medical students and residents, and directing the Administrative Colloquium, a faculty leadership course.
