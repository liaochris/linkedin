Frank Benford’s Summary

I have many years of experience as a professor of mathematics. I am now an independent consulting applied mathematician. I offer (1) consulting in applied mathematics to firms and government agencies, (2) subcontracting to consulting firms, (3) tutoring to local college math students, and (4) advice to writers of theses or dissertations in a variety of fields.
