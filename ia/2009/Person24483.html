Barry Silberzweig’s Summary

Barry Silberzweig is the founding partner of The Law Offices of Barry Silberzweig, P.C. Over the past fifteen years, Mr. Silberzweig has represented private and public companies in legal matters relating to Immigration Law, Real Estate Law, and Corporate Law. The firm has obtained the approval of countless temporary visas, Green Cards, and other immigration benefits for professional workers and other applicants. Additionally, Mr. Silberzweig has represented middle market companies in the purchase and leasing of commercial, industrial, and residential properties, prepared contracts and leases, advised new companies on the creation of corporate entities, and drafted and negotiated contracts. Mr. Silberzweig has a JD Degree from Brooklyn Law School and an MBA Degree from the Columbia Business School. He is admitted to practice before the Supreme Court of the State of New York and in the federal jurisdiction in the Southern and Eastern Districts of New York.
