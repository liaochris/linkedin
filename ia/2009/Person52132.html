Dale Cadwalader, MBA’s Summary

My background is insurance and financial services. I started my post MBA career with Abbott Laboratories work in Corporate Credit and Collections. I worked an AR portfolio against about $190 million in sales doing the credit analyst and collections work within the hopsital segment.

From that situation I helped start up and establish a very succesful insurance agency business. This experience included sales, business development, buisness planning, budgeting and consistantly making both underwriting and business profits over 14 years.

My most recent experience has been working with financial advisors wholesaling insurance an financial services. This experience provides the B2B skills necessary to sell over a broad territory, division or region.

My goal is to assist an organization in the process of generating review and sales in the B2B arena of insurance and financial service.
