Ricardo Salta’s Summary

My goal these days is to create a successful ideas factory in Portugal, starting with my own ideas and then opening the door to more visionaries to come and share.

When the ideas factory (Flydeas) is rolling, I want to put my efforts into a non-profit project that will question a lot of the common paradigms of the world we live in, and hopefully change mentalities. I idealize this as the Good Sense Project.

I've been a technologist since I remember, dissecating toys and making new ones out of electrical components has been one of my pastimes. I have been developing websites for more than ten years, as well as doing computer support work and the like. I've also always been interested in helping others and fighting injustice.

Ricardo Salta’s Specialties:

Because of my never-ending thirst for knowledge, I always invested generously on learning the World, starting by the scientific side, but not ending there.

Having actually spent most of my life online in the last decade, I have gained broad knowledge on the inner workings and the untapped potential of what is here now and of what is yet to come.

If I had to name just one specialty, I would say it is foresight.
