Isaac Christoffersen’s Summary

Isaac Christoffersen has over ten years of experience in system integration and software solutions development for commercial and government clients. His expertise also encompasses relational database design and development, software and systems architecture and analysis, telecommunications management, and systems and software design.

Currently he is involved in the design, development, and implementation of mission-critical, grid-enabled systems using the latest SOA, JEE, Virtualization and open source standards and technologies.

Mr. Christoffersen is professional member of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). He is currently the Vice-Chair of the Washington DC Chapter of the ACM (DC ACM).
