Bruno Lhopiteau’s Summary

Setup and development of hi-tech companies in China/Asia, mostly in relation to engineering and maintenance in asset-intensive industries.

I have been active in the Chinese infrastructure and maintenance market since 1999. In my current company Siveco China, we provide tools and services to assist our clients manage maintenance "with Chinese characteristics". We are considered the largest maintenance consultancy in China (2008).

I hold board memberships with several companies in this field and advise companies trying to enter the market.

Bruno Lhopiteau’s Specialties:

Construction and maintenance management
Startup, development, mergers and aquisitions

Bruno Lhopiteau’s Experience

  • Board Member

    ides China

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Ides AB is a Swedish group supplying engineering tools based on open standards. Ides' industry-focused solutions are optimized for plant engineering from EPC (Engineering Procurement Construction) to OO (Owner Operator). The best-known product is electrical engineering tool elMaster. Setup the Chinese operation, first contracts with power plants and engineering firms.

  • General Manager

    Siveco China

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    October 2004 — Present (4 years 4 months)

    By implementing innovative maintenance solutions, we assist facility owners in optimizing the utilization of their assets. Siveco China is affiliated to Siveco Group, Europe's leading supplier of Computerized Maintenance Systems.

    Based on my previous experience in China, we developed a specific approach to help companies operating here to address what I call "maintenance with Chinese characteristics". We also pioneered the concept of implementing maintenance systems during the construction phase. By doing so, we quickly established the company’s presence in emerging segments of the market.

    In 2006, Siveco China was ranked 69th in the top 100 IT suppliers in China by independent analyst AMTeam and ERPWorld.org listed us among the top 10 fastest growing IT suppliers in China.

    In 2008, though still a small company, we are considered to be the largest maintenance consultancy in the country (which also reflects the lack of maturity of the Chinese maintenance market).
