Andreas Prasetiya’s Summary

Andreas Prasetiya (“Andre”) is a recent MBA in finance graduate with four years of combined working experience in development finance, investment banking, corporate finance, database admin, and marketing research. In pursuit of an analytical and financially oriented position within development finance, international finance, investment banking, investment management, and/or financial risk management.

Currently he is working with the GH Tech Project at The QED Group, LLC as a Financial Analyst supporting the team in their daily finance and administration operation. At the same time, he currently utilizes his financial technical assistance skills as a pro bono Project Associate at Wall Street Without Walls working on structured finance for economic and community development projects within United States. In his spare time, he trades the U.S. equitites and options.

He was a graduate assistant at the Merrick School of Business assisting Dr. Hossein Arsham, particularly, and other faculty members with academic research, data management, editorial review, and administrative work. In summer 2007, he was an intern in one of Indonesia’s largest investment banking firm, Mandiri Sekuritas, doing equity and debt underwriting, corporate finance and advisory works.

When he was in University of Baltimore (UB), he serves as the President of UB Finance Society, a student organization at UB for those who are interested in finance, investment, and banking. As a student leader and his intangible contribution to student life and the Merrick School of Business at UB, he received the Wachovia Bank Scholarship in Fall 2008 Semester and two leadership awards: the 2008 University of Baltimore Spirit of Innovation Award and Leadership Merit Award Recipient.

He is an active student (recent graduate) and young professional members of Net Impact – Washington DC Chapter, the Baltimore CFA Society, and The United States-Indonesia Society (USINDO) at Washington D.C.
