Brian Fidler’s Summary

Internet marketing professional with over 10 years experience designing and developing strategic internet marketing solutions including: website functionality planning, website branding, social media strategy development, promotional email campaign development and implementation, pay-per-click campaign creation and execution, search engine optimization planning and implementation, and banner ad media planning.

Experience developing business and marketing plans, as well as leading a team in the authoring of a patent application.

Ability to analyze data, recognize patterns and trends within the data, and then act on the insights derived from the data.
