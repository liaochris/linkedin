Sean Kane’s Summary

I have been successful in combining my passion for user interfaces with my love of pushing limits. I have consistently delivered amazing products for every company I have been part of throughout my career. Now as Co-Founder of GetListed, I am working to change an industry by combining a solid and innovative technology approach with intuitive interfaces that become a natural part of a job-seeker's, employer's and recruiter's daily workflow.
