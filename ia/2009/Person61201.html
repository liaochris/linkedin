Gene De Libero’s Summary

25 years getting my hands dirty building various businesses in the technology and digital/interactive media spaces for myself and others.

Plenty of juicy project work with some really smart people and a number of turns as a senior exec with public and privately held organizations in more verticals than I can remember!

Instructor at NYU's School of Continuing and Professional Education since 2000. Currently teaching two classes in the Certificate in Digital Media Marketing program; "Digital Strategy: Creating Synergy in Your Marketing Program", and "Narrowcasting: Using Digital Media for Selective Marketing and Advertising."

Co-author of "Strategic Networking: From LAN and WAN to Information Superhighways" in 1996. The book was a definitive text about the Internet and World Wide Web and using computer networks as a tool for learning, communication and e-commerce. Finally making some time to work on the '2nd Edition' of "Strategic Networking" as well as a new book on the topic of targeted digital marketing.

Gene De Libero’s Specialties:

Innovator and change agent, recognized for possessing the critical skill set to discover and solve problems many others do not or cannot see. Complete knowledge of competitors, trends, and market conditions across a wide variety of industries. Broad knowledge and understanding of digital channels and related tools including content management tools, e-commerce engines, ad serving platforms, profiling and personalization systems and social/community and customer service applications.
