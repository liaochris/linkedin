David H. Potter’s Summary

General Practice Attorney with Potter Group Legal Services LLC in Chicago. Practice areas include Civil and Criminal Litigation, Corporate Legal Management, Business Formations, Wills and Trusts, Real Estate Investment and Transactions, Bankruptcy.

Prior experience includes litigating for the County and State in both Civil and Criminal matters while employed with the Cook County State's Attorney's Office. Advised various county governmental bodies on the effect of new ordinances and state statutes on government activities as well as advised County officials on legality and structure of proposed ordinances also while employed with the Cook County State's Attorney's Office.

Knowledge of Bankruptcy and related Laws as a result of experience with one of the largest personal bankruptcy firms in Chicago as well as course work in both Secured Transactions and Bankruptcy.

Corporate: General Advising, Formation, Legal Management

Bankruptcy: Chapter 7, 11, and 13 for Individuals and Corporations

Wills & Trusts: Drafting, Probate
