Debra Hamilton’s Summary

Goal: To help companies build a performance-oriented culture to drive overall business performance. Areas of expertise include communication strategy, leadership, team building, sales presentations, business writing, and interpersonal skills.

Experience: Develop and implement strategic learning and leadership initiatives for organizations, providing needs assessment, program design, implementation, and evaluation. Proficient at linking learning programs to business goals and assessing training transfer using the Kirkpatrick and balanced scorecard evaluative approaches. Consult with major corporations: Johnson & Johnson; Pfizer; Bristol-Myers Squibb; Firmenich; Innovation Interactive; Covanta Secured Services; FX Concepts; Bemis Company; Public Library Association; and American Institute of Certified Public Accountants.

Debra Hamilton’s Specialties:

Needs analysis; problem solving; designing effective learning programs; training delivery and facilitation; program evaluation; train-the-trainer; coaching; and communication strategy.

Additional: ASTD E-Learning Instructional Design Certificate and Myers-Briggs Indicator Professional Qualifying Program
