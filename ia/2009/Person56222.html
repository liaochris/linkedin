Dave Iannone’s Summary

Dave J. Iannone is a progressive, dynamic eMedia, B2B publishing, marketing, content management, social networking and public safety veteran.

Dave Iannone’s Specialties:

eMedia: Plannning, development and management of comprehensive leading web-based products and properties, including robust, dynamic content and feature-rich industry Web portal destinations, including online databases, search applications and multimedia.

Public Safety: Progressive thinking for public safety organizations and companies to deliver their mission, products and services, including a consultative approach to planning, refining and launching effective, targeted marketing.
