J.P. James’s Summary

Published and award-winning advertising/marketing professional and thought-leader with 13 years of Advertising Agency Account Management and Account Planning Expertise in general market and multicultural advertising agency with new business multicultural wins.

Client Experience: Alberto-Culver Company, AstraZeneca Pharmaceuticals, Boehringer Ingelheim Pharmaceuticals (Flomax), Colgate-Palmolive, Ford Motor Company (Lincoln Mercury, Mazda North American Operations), HSBC, Kraft Foods Inc., Masterfoods USA (3 MUSKETEERS® Brand,), The Home Depot Inc., Taylor Made–Adidas Golf Company, Time-Warner Cable, Toyota Motor Sales USA, Verizon, United States 2010 Census, United Healthcare

Professional Organizations/Activities: Radio Advertising Effectiveness Lab (RAEL) Research Committee, Northwestern University Medill Speaker’s Club of New York.

Awards: 2007 Effie Award Finalist for 3 MUSKETEERS® “A Lighter Way to Enjoy Chocolate” Campaign.

J.P. James’s Specialties:

Multicultural Marketing experience in Automotive, Hair & Beauty Aids, Financial Services, Pharmaceutical, Quick Service Restaurant, Retail, Telecommunications, Consumer Packaged Goods and Government Accounts.
