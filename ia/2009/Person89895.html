greg reece’s Summary

Greg, or as he likes to be called, Reece, has 25 years of experience in Recruiting and Business Development. This includes over 10 years of Senior-level Corporate leadership experience, as well as 5 years as Vice President/Director of Sales for North America. His background includes extensive experience in direct sales, marketing, business development and organizational management and restructuring.

Reece's pro-active leadership-by-example management and motivation philosophy has contributed heavily in his business as well as personal accomplishments. His talent as a highly effective platform speaker, trainer, and motivator lends itself perfectly to assisting both clients and candidates alike in fulfilling thier hopes and dreams of their careers.

Reece's total focus is within Alternative Energy and Green Building Technologies, as well as lending support to the companies Apparel team. Call or email him today if you are a proven "mover and shaker" in the APPAREL MARKET OR GREEN INDUSTRY.

On the rare occasion he is not working, Reece enjoys boating, scuba diving and any beach!
