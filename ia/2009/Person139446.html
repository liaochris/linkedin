Leigh van der Byl’s Summary

Having more than nine years of CG production experience, I can share many intriguing tales of wonder and woe from this gloriously bizarre industry (although I still await the day when I encounter a team of render wranglers comprised of oompa loompas).

These days I mostly specialise in texture painting (well, I say painting, but let's be honest, these days it's mostly photo manipulation and tweaking, with a few touches of a Photoshop brush to really bring out the pizazz), but I come from a generalist background from my days in television work.

Leigh van der Byl’s Specialties:

Texture painting, UV mapping (it's a dying art, I know), look development, lighting, modeling, and an almost uncanny knack for swiftly and cunningly solving strange software problems (which may, occasionally, involve the guileful use of extispicy in extreme circumstances).
