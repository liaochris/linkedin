Ray Stendall’s Summary

My experience is focused on Software Product Management/Solution Architecture targeted toward the Enterprise and Wireless/Wireline Service Providers. I have worked in large and small organizations building converged network management solutions that cover various service offerings that include: VoIP, IPTV, 3G Wireless WAN, WiFi etc. In addition I have built expertise in characterizing how end users experience an application/service across these technologies, which directly impacts the top line as well as employee productivity.

I have a Honours in Business Administration from the Richard Ivey School of Business at the University of Western Ontario in Canada and a Masters of Science in Computers and Information Systems from the University of Phoenix.

Ray Stendall’s Specialties:

Software Product Line Management including: strategic planning, market analysis, competitive analysis, product definition and requirements, pricing, sales tool generation, field sales support, tech brief generation.
