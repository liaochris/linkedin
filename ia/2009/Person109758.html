Jennifer Navarro (née Nicdao)’s Summary

I am a Senior Project Manager for the MVNSO (Mobility Voice Networks Solution Organization) group under TSG (Technical Services Group). I have been with HP since June 1999 and has 10 years of project management experience. Prior to joining Hewlett Packard, I was an active member of the U.S. Army as a Microwave Telecommunications Operator. I completed my military obligations with an honorable discharge as a staff sergeant.
My current activities include upgrading and deploying the Cisco network infrastructure of a Fortune 500 company, while implementing Cisco Unified Communications and a Cisco wireless infrastructure. I have been also working on proposing, developing and delivering HP’s Networking solutions including Unified Communications, Wireless, RFID and Network Infrastructure, for Cisco, Tropos, Procurve, Aptilo, BlueSocket, Alvarion, Motorola, and Aeroscout products. I have managed multimillion dollar, multi-vendor projects.
Before joining the Network Consulting Group, I was an Account Support Consultant, delivering high availability and ITIL consulting to enterprise and corporate customers. I was instrumental in assisting my customers in preparing for and passing Sarbanes-Oxley audits. I was a sought after resource and area focal point for Project Management, high availability, and ITIL processes.
