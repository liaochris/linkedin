Nancy Blum’s Summary

Specialty in evaluating students who are struggling in school. Psychological & neuropsychological testing of children, teens, and adults to evaluate attentional problems, learning differences, and emotional issues including possible autistic spectrum disorders; and make recommendations to help the person with school, work, & life. Many of my evaluations are for students needing individualized assistance in school (e.g., 504 plans, IEPs, testing accommodations). In my neuropsychological evaluations, I assess intellectual functioning, visual & auditory processing, language, attention and concentration, learning and memory, executive functioning, academics, and behavioral and emotional functioning. I also evaluate brain–behavior relationships for the effects of suspected brain injury such as traumatic head injury, brain tumor, stroke, exposure to toxic chemicals, seizure disorder, or other medical condition. Some of my evaluations are forensic, and may require expert testimony.

When time permits, I accept clients for cognitive-behavioral therapy for stress, anxiety, & depression.

More information is available at www.drnancyblum.com

Nancy Blum’s Specialties:

learning difficulties, attentional problems, depression, anxiety, stress. autistic spectrum disorders, head injury, testing, evaluation, accommodations, special education, IEPs, due process, 504 plans, cognitive-behavioral therapy
