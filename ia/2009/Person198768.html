Rick Whittington’s Summary

Rick Whittington is a website effectiveness consultant and owner of Rick Whittington Consulting (http://www.rickwhittington.com), a web consulting firm based in Richmond, Virginia. So what is a website effectiveness consultant? Rick uses both qualitative data and quantitative data to make decisions about the design and content of websites. This strategic approach includes the disciplines of web design, usability, web analytics and marketing strategy.

Website effectiveness consulting usually leads to improved business results for websites, including better conversion rates for ecommerce sites and better results for non-ecommerce sites.

Rick was the website site design manager for CircuitCity.com's creative services team for five years, where he led the 2004 redesign of CircuitCity.com. Under his leadership, CircuitCity.com won multiple awards including Bizrate Circle of Excellence (4 years), an Internet Retailer Top 50 award, and recognition from customer experience firm Vividence and industry publication Twice Magazine.

Early in his tenure at Circuit City, he managed a $5 million online media plan for AOL, MSN and Yahoo. He also led an email marketing program, helped pilot an affiliate program and established a usability testing program.

Rick has also worked at Crutchfield Corporation and the United Network of Organ Sharing.
