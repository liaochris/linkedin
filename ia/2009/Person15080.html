Andy Cheatwood’s Summary

I have over eight years' experience heading interactive editorial concept design and development for innovative and engaging high-traffic sites and broadband networks. Most recently I served as Director of Online Production at ChannelOne.com, where my work leading the creation of compelling, non-traditional interactive news packages garnered numerous online and journalism industry awards, including three Webbys.

Currently I serve as Director of Program Management for Total Beauty Media, Inc., a venture-backed startup for which I was one of the founding members. At Total Beauty, I spearheaded the design and architecture of the site's user experience from the bottom up, merging editorial, marketing, sales and biz dev requirements to establish a new and engaging brand on the web.
