Michael Hessler’s Summary

Senior Database Administrator/Developer/Engineer and trouble shooter with 10+ years experience in short term, high profile outsourced assignments.

Prefer to remain in the Jerusalem area, but open to relocation within Israel or telecommuting assignments.

Regularly contracted to correct business-crippling data problems or for interim coverage of urgent-need abandoned DBA positions, followed by mentoring and handing over duties to a junior DBA.

Development experience includes data migrations from various legacy sources, SQL and Oracle version upgrades, creation of new enterprise-wide database architecture and data modeling with CA ERwin, creation of stored procedures and scripts, security and performance improvement, data integrity resolution, reporting and documentation.

Knowledge of a wide variety of business applications gained through working with client’s legacy systems and in the migration efforts to the successors of those systems.

Prior to database administration, I worked as a chemical process engineer outsourced to clients in the petrochemical, energy, hazardous waste, defense, electronics, and beverage industries. I made the transition from developing software solutions for engineering problems to supporting the corporate-wide databases that businesses depend on. As a DBA I have worked in various industries dealing with medical, government, engineering and business data.
