Maurice Lynch’s Summary

LYNCH IEI LTD
Specialist Electrical & Mechanical Contractors to the Railway Industry.

Lynch IEI Ltd was formed in 1995 by Maurice and Paul Lynch. The Company has progressed now into being one of the leading medium-sized contractors in the Mechanical and Electrical industry, working from offices in Whetstone, North London and Wembley in West London and now has an annual turnover of in excess of £4 million.

Lynch IEI Ltd has over the years created a highly skilled team of electricians specialising in commercial and industrial electrical contracting and installation services, including fire alarms, access control, CCTV security systems and data systems. Our Mechanical Department widens our services offered to include full heating, ventilation, air-conditioning and mechanical services

Over the years, we have built a large client base within the rail and construction industry, specialising in fast-track contracts. These have obvious deadline completion dates and Lynch IEI Ltd are proud of achievements in finishing on time. Being employed direct to the client often requires Lynch IEI Ltd to undertake the role of Principal Contractor.

Engineers and supervisors undergo Health & Safety and manufacturer's training, keeping them up to date with the ever-changing regulations and developments within the industry.

Lynch I.E.I. Limited are now recognized as Mechanical and Electrical Contractors who provide an unrivalled service 24 hours a day 7 days a week. Our contract history proves that our quality of work is to the highest standards with special consideration given to HSE guidelines to insure safe working practice and conditions. Our hand picked engineers insure safety is foremost in their agenda so to provide a safe environment to public, premises, staff and themselves.

All works are to a high standard and adhere to current LUL and IEE Regulations.
