Yvette Bordley’s Summary

Hi i'm Yvette a Marketing, Communications and Web Professional with over ten years of experience in Marketing, Communications and Web development. My roles have included Market Researcher, Research Analyst, Public Relations, Marketing and Communications Manager, Web Content Manager, and Account Manager for various on and offline marketing campaigns.

Helping others use the internet to market themselves, develop relationships and grow online is what i love the most. I help people to get the best from the web through innovative marketing strategies. I also coach people on how to use social media - you know the cool things kids like Twitter, Facebook,Youtube etc.

I have managed the development and maintenance of large scale websites and online campaigns including Brighton & Hove City Council's website (rated in top 20 UK local authority sites).

I am currently using my expertise in ethical search engine optimization (SEO) and the broader areas of search engine marketing (SEM) & social media optimisation (SMO) to drive targeted traffic to web sites. I share social media networking, internet marketing & seo links online regularly. I use Wordpress to blog and social bookmarking sites like Technorati and the blogosphere to keep up to date with latest trends.

My ideal job would be web marketing consultant with an innovative ethical company, located on a tropical island with nearby yoga and meditation classes. If you see it let me know ;-)
