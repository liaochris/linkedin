Kyle Richardson’s Summary

I work as a Print Solutions Provider for Bridgetown Printing, a Consolidated Graphics Company in Portland. We area a full-service printing facility specializing in:
- High-end sheetfed offset printing
- Digital printing
- Excellent service
- Quick turn-around times

We also offer a wide range of other services:
- Complete in-house prepress
- Bindery
- Mailing
- Cross media solutions
- Online print procurement

We are located in a new 48,000 square foot facility in North Portland in the Swan Island industrial park. Our parent company, Consolidated Graphics (NYSE: CGX), operates across the U.S. as well as internationally.

As a part of CGX's Leadership Development Program, I spent three years rotating between various departments, learning every facet of the industry. From catching brochures off of the folder in the bindery, to washing blankets in the pressroom, to estimating potential jobs, to handling my own clients, the vast array of experience has contributed to a successful career in the printing industry.

Kyle Richardson’s Specialties:

Graphic design, estimating, production planning, bindery management, digital printing, shipping
