Edan Portaro’s Summary

A seasoned leader with over 20 years of experience in sales and business development. I gained extensive experience at TMP Worldwide (also known as Monster Worldwide and TMP Directional Marketing) the largest and most elite national yellow pages and local interactive agency (CMR) in the world. As the SVP of National Sales, I gained deep experience in new business development, client relationship management, budget development and strategic planning.

I also gained experience in operational efficiency, staff management, team building and administration as the SVP of Sales at YellowPages.com where I was recognized for my role in revamping the existing sales channels and positioning teams for future growth. Since leaving Yellowpages.com, I have spent the last several years as a consultant focusing on the development of sales channels and go-to-market strategies for a variety of small-to-medium, VC funded, and early stage companies.

Edan Portaro’s Specialties:

Deep domain experience with the print yellow pages, interactive advertising and publishing - skills include: Sales Management, Sales Team Leadership, Strategic Planning, PL Responsibility, Negotiating, Financial Analysis, Sales Skills, Process Improvement, Print and Web Marketing.
