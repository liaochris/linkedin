Timothy Koh’s Summary

Mr Koh joined Credit Suisse Group in 2006 as the Regional Vice President, Human Resources. He has responsibilities for a broad spectrum of human capital including HR Strategy, Recruitment and Selection, Career Management, Training and Development, Performance Management, Compensation and Rewards, Transition Management and Resource Management. His passion is in the formulation and execution of the HR Strategy and Operations at both country and regional levels. He was appointed in 2007 as the Global Lead for Credit Suisse HR Transformation - Global Service Delivery Centers and the added portfolio of Asia Pacific Operations Excellence (OE) Champion.

Prior to Credit Suisse, he worked for Caterpillar in Melbourne, Australia as HR Consultant spearheading the HR activities across Asia Pacific locations with a focus on compensation & benefits, succession planning, talent management & development, compliance and HR Shared Services / Systems & Processes. He was appointed in 2005 to the position of Manager & Head Asia Pacific Human Strategy, 6 Sigma & Global Diversity. Mr. Koh was the pioneer within the region to be awarded the Caterpillar Inc Chairman’s award in 2004 for his outstanding contributions to the enterprise. He has lived and worked in cities across the globe and first entered the HR function full time upon relocating to Melbourne, Australia in 2003. Timothy has been with various business units and JVs of GE/NBC as well as other MNCs & Info Technology / communications industries.

Mr. Koh holds a BSc (Honours) from University College Dublin (Ireland) and a Master degree in IT Management from University of Wollongong (Australia). He is a member of the Society for Human Resource Management in the US and Australia HR Society. He is 6 Sigma certified in GE & Caterpillar and held various Microsoft Technologies Certification.

Timothy Koh’s Specialties:

HR, IT, General Management, 6 Sigma, Competitive analysis, Mergers and Acquisition (M&A), Integration / Divestiture, Global Diversity & Inclusion, Ethics, Management Reporting & Financial Modeling / Analysis, Strategy, Due Diligence, JVs, Compensation & Benefits (C&B), HRIS/HRIT
