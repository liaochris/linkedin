Antti Haverinen’s Summary

I am a Web Communications Specialist, who has been working for several years in Web & Print Communication improving Web Presence.

Simply said, I have been responsible for ensuring that the most important thing – the content and the message (communication/e-marketing) – is being presented perfectly on functionally designed websites.

This is not an easy task. It consists of a number of sophisticated tasks and hand-crafted activities that must be taken into account in order to get benefits on the Web.

As Web Communications Specialist I am mastering all those wide-range of tasks and activities maintaining and developing Web Presence. One of my expertises is to oversee the Web Content (creating, manipulating and optimizing) and creating e-marketing plans.

Thanks to my journalist/marketing background I have excellent writing, editing and verbal communication skills to manage daily updatings. Moreover, my advanced knowledge of Communications Theories, practices and emerging Web Technologies enables me to design high-profile campaigns and websites optimized in SEO.

I love to produce Web Services by bringing innovative ideas and solutions to them. I am convinced of the importance of the proper e-marketing strategy, essential SEO-optimization and finally the well-written content.

Therefore, to get these results you will need a specialist, who has a wide-range knowledge of Web Communication.

Antti Haverinen’s Specialties:

1) Strong knowledge of Internet technologies and processes. A technical trouble-shooter.

2) Strong knowledge of Web Metrics and SEO.

3) Excellent experience in Project Management.

5) Excellent copy-writing and editing skills.

6) A positive, communicative and motivating person with ability to train users in classrooms or remotely.
