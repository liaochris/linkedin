Ian Weeks’s Summary

15 years recruitment expertise with in I.T, Telecommunications, Engineering, Medical Technology, Business Development, Finance and Sales. Currently running Redsquid Recruitment Limited, Looking to expand and become the professionals choice for first stop recruitment. Recruiting in the UK, Europe and USA

I started back in the early 1990's supplying contract and permanent I.T staff to major blue chip and international organisations. Since then I have helped to set up 2 recruitment companies (Absolute Solutions), 1 I.T division (Onyx Associates) and an I.T Training division. I gained a lot of experience from my former managers / directors at Absolute and JCC which has helped me to drive forward and become an expert in my field of recruitment.

I moved into Finance and Sales due to the cross over of skills in the late 1990's as well as mainting my expertise in IT and Telecommunications.

I’m able to supply the very best talented professionals on the market in a difficult and diverse market.

Providing advice to clients on the recruitment process and options available outside of the box to save money but still keep the highest quality of candidates for the right position.

Putting the right people in the right position not just as a number but as a valued member of the organisation.

Available at anytime to discuss recruitment solutions, provide career advice and market analysis. Contact me on 07904 890 322

I still enjoy the buzz of recruitment and the satisfaction of helping people to make the right career choice.
