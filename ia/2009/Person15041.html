Andy Batt’s Summary

Offbeat People & Sports-Motion Photography
Photographer-Storyteller-Good Guy

Andy is a photographer, and a damn good one. He feels really strongly about this, and his clients can confirm it for you. Andy decided to focus his talents on creating quirky images of people, and capturing beautiful moments of athleticism. His work always tells a story - a story of the the moment after the shot, or of what the hell is going on, or of captured beauty.

Andy would really like to tell your stories for you.

  • Co-Owner, Photographer

    andyBATTstudio

    (Photography industry)

    January 1997 — Present (12 years 2 months)

    Andy Batt is a national sports, motion, & people print and multimedia photographer. Andy shoots for regional, national and international clients, both in studio and on location. His photography is varied, but centers around quirky lifestyle work with real people, actors and models, and moody sports action photography of athletes and dancers. Andy graduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology in 1992 with a BFA in photography, and has been in the commercial photography world ever since. Andy's partner Therese is his producer, and an accomplished fine art photographer and bookbinder. Their studio, andyBATTstudio, is about to celebrate it's 10th year in business.
