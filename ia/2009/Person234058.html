Tim Paulino’s Summary

As President and CEO of Telecentrex LLC, I help small and home-based business professionals, entrepreneurs, solopreneurs and internet-based businesses sound more professional and communicate more effectively. By blending emerging business-class managed telecommunication services with budget-friendly pricing I'm able to bring value to small businesses in way that helps them be more productive.

I'm interested in partnering with network solution providers who provide voice/data network integration, installation and design and are looking to expand their current product offerings.
