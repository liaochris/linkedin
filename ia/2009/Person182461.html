Paula Constantino’s Summary

Passion: Creating a 21st century information highway - inspiring, enlightening and empowering women worldwide with practical lifetime skills - using technology to build virtual teams that develop and deliver audio information modules (AIM) - starting in South Africa with the Women Rebuilding Africa Program (WRAP) - providing life skills and information to banish poverty & enhance lives of women.

NOW is the time for Change - with information that is easy to access, understand and pass on to others - WE CAN CHANGE the World!

As an attorney, I combined my analytical, creative and technology skills with my motivation and team-building skills - to create a web-based resource - women helping women achieve more success with less stress globally through a team of Mentors, plus Coaches, digital info products, affiliate solutions, articles, resource links, and more at www.WomesSupportTeam.com. Then, I launched FindACoach Online.com, providing client-based coaching.

Embracing my true passion, I founded the1000 Women Can Change the World.
