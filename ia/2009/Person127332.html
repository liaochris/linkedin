Kate Pain’s Summary

I have worked as a journalist for national magazines for 13 years, and more specifically as an editor for the last six.

For the first eight years I specialised in consumer publishing, covering lifestyle areas such as travel, health, fitness and entertainment (art, film, theatre and books).

When I moved out of London to the leafy burbs five years ago, I switched my focus to the corporate market where I now work as an editor for PMGroup.

In addition, I continue to freelance for a number of national papers and consumer magazines.
