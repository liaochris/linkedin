Wayne Kulick, PMP, LSSBB, IPRC’s Summary

Wayne J. Kulick is a Senior Program Manager for Bell Labs Federal Systems of LGS Innovations. LGS is the wholly owned subsidiary of Alcatel-Lucent dedicated to serving the U.S. federal government market.

A retired Naval Officer, Mr. Kulick joined LGS in 2007 after his 25 year career in the Navy. Mr. Kulick came to LGS from the National Reconnaissance Office in Chantilly, VA where he served as a Deputy Division Chief in the Operations Group within the Communications Directorate. Prior to that, Mr. Kulick served as a Program Manager in the SIGINT Applications and Integration Office within the Signals Intelligence Directorate. Mr. Kulick is the recipient of the National Reconnaissance Office Bronze Medal for meritorious service. Notable accomplishments include selection as an Associate Fellow for the Chief of Naval Operations Strategic Studies Program and selection for the Naval Postgraduate School Center for Executive Education creative thinking course.

Mr. Kulick is a certified Project Manager Professional (PMP) with the Project Management Institute, Lean Six Sigma Black Belt Certification with Villanova University, a certified Program Manager and COTR with the National Reconnaissance Office, a DAWIA certified Program Manager (II) and a certified ITIL Practitioner in Release and Control (IPRC). Mr. Kulick holds multiple degrees to include a MS in Astronautical Engineering from the Naval Postgraduate School, a BS in Computer Science from the University of North Florida and a BS in Electronics Management from Southern Illinois University at Carbondale.

Appointed to the Board of Directors for the Project Management Institute's Aerospace and Defense Special Interest Group (PMI A&D SIG) for the 2009 term, Mr. Kulick serves pro bono as Deputy, Communications, in support of improving member benefits, membership outreach and member collaboration across a geographically diverse membership base.

Mr. Kulick is a member of PMI, ASQ, AIAA, itSMF and AFCEA.
