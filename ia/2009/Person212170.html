Steve Roberts’s Summary

My years of experience in casino operations – from front of the house as a cage cashier to executive level participation in more than 10 new casino openings - has given me the ability to understand this business from a management perspective with a customer focus. As a result, I am able to offer clients a time tested, real world, proven methodology for every type of technology initiative from system selection through contract negotiation, implementation, and post go-live support / custom development.

Working on such diverse projects has given me extensive experience with the following software packages:
• IGT Smart
• IGT ADVANTAGE (formerly Acres) - all modules and data structures
• Aristocrat OASIS (formerly CDS) - all modules and data structures
• Bally SDS, CMS, & ACSC
• Konami Gaming System
• MS SQL - database administration and Transact SQL
• Crystal Reports
• Cognos - all applications, stand alone or web deployed
• Microsoft .Net Framework
• Oracle with PL-SQL
• MS Project

In addition to my work in the casino and gaming industry; I am also a licensed stockbroker (NASD Series 3, 7, & 63), a multiple business owner familiar with all phases of business development from inception through IPO, and am a former member of both the Technology Business Alliance of Nevada (TBAN) and Nevada Development Authority.
