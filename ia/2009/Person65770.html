    (Government Agency; Utilities industry)

    June 2009 — Present (7 months)

  • Director, Risk Management

    Green Mountain Energy Company

    (Privately Held; Utilities industry)

    May 2007 — February 2009 (1 year 10 months)

    • Directed the Risk Management function for one of the largest independent retail electric providers in the State of Texas with over $400 million in annual revenue and 230,000 retail and commercial accounts.
    • Analyzed hedging strategies involving commodity futures and carbon offset contracts, assessed market and credit risk exposure, and oversaw compliance and risk policies for settlement with approved counterparties.
    • Worked closely with the firm’s traders to assess financial market conditions, analyze economic reports and third-party analyst research, and implement appropriate trading strategies on a risk-adjusted basis.
    • Developed a variety of Monte Carlo and parametric risk models analyzing portfolio value-at-risk, earnings at risk, correlation, and volatility stress testing and scenario analysis using Oracle/SQL, SAS, and Excel @Risk.
    • Assessed the effectiveness of various trading strategies by backtesting market movements against trading decisions, risk budgets, collateral exposure, and impact on portfolio risk measures and correlations.
    • Created and maintained corporate risk policies and monitored compliance of various third-party supplier agreements by internal and external sales, operations, supply, customer service, and finance departments.

    • Prepared risk reports for senior management and external auditors, including assessments of financial, operational, and technology risk exposures, collateral and mark-to-market hedge accounting, FAS133 analysis, and participated in the development of company-wide Sarbanes-Oxley reporting practices.
    • Evaluated retail and commercial customer credit exposure and payment default probabilities and worked closely with the customer services group and outside collection agencies to minimize losses.

  • Enterprise Risk Manager

    ERCOT

    (Non-Profit; Utilities industry)

    October 2005 — April 2007 (1 year 7 months)

    • Established the risk management group for the deregulated $34 billion Texas bulk electrical system – the largest financial marketplace in the State of Texas – with responsibility for identifying, analyzing, and developing mitigation strategies for identified market, funding liquidity, operational, and counterparty credit risks within the organization as well as involving external market participants.
    • Drafted corporate risk policies, authorizations and guidelines, and created financial and risk reporting metrics including a ‘risk dashboard’ as chair of the corporate Risk Management Committee.
    • Responsible for the review and quantification of risks associated with existing and proposed business activities, financial operations, counterparty credit, treasury investment portfolio, funding liquidity, and external market risks, and advising management on alternatives to mitigate and manage identified risks.
    • Developed recommendations to enhance the design and operation of the enterprise risk management program and led initiatives to assist business managers in identifying, evaluating, and monitoring financial and operational risk exposure employing the COSO Enterprise Risk Management framework.
    • Developed a risk based audit methodology in conjunction with the Internal Audit department and worked with external auditors and the Internal Control group in establishing compliance procedures.
    • Led the development of a corporate business continuity and information technology disaster recovery plan for the organization and chaired the internal Incident Response committee.

  • Director, Risk Advisory Services

    MSD Capital Risk Advisors, LLC

    (Financial Services industry)

    July 2000 — September 2005 (5 years 3 months)

    • Advised global financial institutions with respect to interest rate, foreign exchange, commodity, equity, credit, and operational risk management trading systems and technology including market risk measurement and reporting, financial modeling, and Basel II related regulatory requirements.
    • Directed a year-long initiative to implement a risk management and reporting framework for the Global Private Client group of Morgan Stanley resulting in an implementation of RiskMetrics’ RiskManager software to analyze over 4.5 million private client, high-net worth, institutional, and hedge fund accounts on an overnight and real-time basis to monitor and stress test market and credit risk.
    • Managed an eleven month assignment providing integration support for the acquisition of Scudder Investments by Deutsche Bank Asset Management by directing combined financial and infrastructure management teams in determining asset management products, procedures, financial systems, and operational structures to be employed throughout the combined entity.
