Betsy Shulman’s Summary

EXPERIENCE

I coach and mentor Internet entrepreneurs in all areas of online marketing such as self-branding, PPC, SEO, social media marketing, and copy writing. I use Carbon Copy Pro's entrepreneurial blueprint as the training ground for marketers who want fast results using a top Internet marketing system.

As a Wealth Masters consultant, I offer solutions for funding retirement, debt minimization, tax credits, and wealth creation by implementing correct money strategies like those taught by Robert Kiyosaki. WMI provides its members with "wealth secrets" through its educational products and the M2 and M3 events. It's an ideal way to build diversified investments and passive income using the same strategies as the very wealthy.

My decision to join Wealth Masters International is the culmination of years of balancing simultaneous interests in Fine Art and finance. My knowledge of business practices, finance and investing are my "alter ego" and they led me to this Internet-based company in early 2008, after working 15 years in the fashion and media industry.

Though business and finance may not seem like typical strengths for an artist, I managed my own freelance company "Betsy Lyn Make up," as well as my personal investment portfolio since 1996. I joined Wealth Masters International because I was looking for solutions for my own long-term wealth goals. (Artists need to plan for retirement too!)
