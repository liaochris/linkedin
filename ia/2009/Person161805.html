Michael Hsieh’s Summary

Developed and shipped two SPARC microprocessor chips
and five SPARC based workstations and enterprise servers
at Sun with millions to billions of dollars of recognized
revenue for the respective products.

Developed a Director class Storage Area Network (SAN)
switch at a start-up that was later acquired by McData
Corporation. That SAN switch was productized by McData
Corporation with millions of dollars of recognized revenue.

Comprehensive hands-on engineering experience in
developing chip and system products starting with initial
concept and architecture, going through stages of engineering
design, integration, and test, to delivering the final design to
volume production.

Extensive first-hand leadership experience in all phases
of developing chip and system products including attracting
& retaining top talent, team building, project planning,
managing schedule and deliverable, integrating and
orchestrating engineering teams of different expertise
and disciplines, coordinating product life cycle compliance
and releasing engineering design to production.

Noted by team members for delivering products with
superb mentoring and high cultural sensitivity. Known
by colleagues in leadership team for making timely
collaborative decisions to help deliver quality products.
Skilled at articulating visions and road maps to enable
team members to see the big picture. Proven record in
managing the bleeding edge challenges in developing
chip and system products.

Always be willing, able, and ready to join an innovative
team to work together to strive for a team success.
