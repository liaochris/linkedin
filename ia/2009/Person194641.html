Raymond Soneira’s Summary

DisplayMate Technologies has developed a fully automatic factory and/or field calibration system for all displays and HDTVs using a very fast and fully automatic advanced mathematical optimization algorithm that provides a visually perfect 0.5 MPCD Minimum Perceptible Color Difference calibration (throughout the entire color space) together with a substantially higher calibrated brightness than is achievable with traditional calibration methods. Single pass with absolutely no human intervention or tweaking needed. Manufacturers can use it to improve picture quality and increase brightness while substantially reducing panel costs and manufacturing time. AVMDO -- Adaptive Variable Metric Display Optimizer. Currently running via software - looking for a partner to get it implemented on a chip as a principal component for all mobile displays, computer monitors, projectors and HDTVs. Contact DisplayMate Technologies.

Our latest Display Technology Shoot-Out articles on LCD and Plasma technology have shocking results on Viewing Angles, Extended Color Gamuts, Dynamic Contrast, Response Time, and Motion Blur.
http://www.displaymate.com/LCD_Plasma_ShootOut.htm
http://www.displaymate.com/LCD_Response_Time_ShootOut.htm

Raymond Soneira’s Specialties:

DisplayMate is an industry standard for optimizing, calibrating, testing, evaluating and comparing all types of displays, monitors, projectors, mobile displays, HDTVs, and all display technologies, such as CRT, LCD, Plasma, LCoS, DLP, OLED. We produce Display Technology Shoot-Outs for manufacturers, publications, and industry tradeshows including the InfoComm Shoot-Out and the SID Display Technology Showcase. DisplayMate is used by hundreds of publications for editorial testing and lab reviews.
