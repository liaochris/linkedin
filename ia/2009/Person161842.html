Michael Muderick’s Summary

In a further expansion of Muderick Media, Family Video Stories was formed to create a loving gift of irreplaceable first-person memories, so future generations will enjoy, understand and appreciate their unique legacy, again and again.

Muderick Media was started in 1984. My background includes being a management executive with over 15 years of experience in non-profit organizational management, fundraising, Jewish community relations and building management. I had been executive director for Congregation Ohev Shalom for 15 years where I successfully helped manage a capital campaign along with major renovation projects. I managed vendor relations, member participation and departmental budgets. I currently hold an FSA certification-Fellow in Synagogue Administration.

My vast experience continues into the area of video production where I own and operate a successful production company. Muderick Media provides creative services in the area of Video/AV. Overall I possess 30+ years of experience in producing Award Winning AV/Video programs for broadcast, corporate, and educational clients.

I am interested in networking with professionals from both previously mentioned fields. I can be contacted directly at Michael@Muderick.com.
