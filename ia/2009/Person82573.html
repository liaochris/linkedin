Frank X. McCarthy’s Summary

After 27 years in the staffing industry conducting diversity searches, Frank X. McCarthy, a diversity recruiting pioneer, sold Xavier Associates Inc., the company he founded in 1973. He established Diverse Workplace Inc. in 2004.

Before he became involved in recruiting diversity executives, Frank was a Catholic priest in the Diocese of Paterson, NJ. He served as a paratrooper chaplain with tours in Korea, Japan, and the 101st Airborne Division. He later became pastor of an inner-city parish in Paterson where he was involved in many projects in the African American and Latino communities. In 1970, he joined the Office of Economic Opportunity in Boston. He received an M.A. in Counseling Psychology from Boston College.

Frank is a former President and current Board Member of the Brockton Neighborhood Health Center (www.bnhc.org.) BNHC had 92,000 patient visits in 2008 and provides health services to over 17,000 patients. He is a former president of the. Massachusetts Professional Placement Consultants.
