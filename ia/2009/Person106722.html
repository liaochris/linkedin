Jimmy Cappaert’s Summary

Together with my colleague Rick van Aspert (CEO) I am responsible for the daily management of the company for activities such as our commercial policy, ethics, marketing and communication. I also do quite a bit of software related work (mainly web applications).

We have high a quality strategy at the most affordable prices, and always with full use of our renowned support services included for every customer. Our company has the experience of serving over 10.000 customers and managing roughly 15.000 domain names.
