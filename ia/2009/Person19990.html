Arne Sybren Postma’s Summary

Arne Sybren Postma is a professional theatre-director. He specialises in directing theatre-classics performed in the original languages (Antigone in Greek, Le Misanthrope in French, Mutter Courage in German, Pinter and Beckett in English).
Postma works a lot in the combination of theatre and classical music. With symphonic orchestra Oberon he produced Midsumernightsdream with live-symphonic music by Mendelssohn, The Storm with Sibelius and Peer Gynt (Ibsen/Grieg). Postma gave workshops for Leiden University (Stanislawski- and melodrame-courses)
Postma is also an assessment actor for the city of Rotterdam, Deen Groep and Hof Psychologen.

Arne Sybren Postma’s Specialties:

I'm able to work with professionals, semi-professinals and amateur-players on their own level - and make them all feel comfortable.
