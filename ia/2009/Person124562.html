Justin Boland’s Summary

"I like to build cool stuff" was the opening line of my Statement of Purpose when applying to Caltech's PhD program in 2000, and I still stand by that. I have invented and built novel energy harvesting device using MEMS processes, built a company to commercialize that work, helped build an honors college at UTDallas, helped build a nationally competitive debate team at UTDallas (the "nationally competitive" success belongs to incredible teammates, I was just did it for fun),...

I am currently looking to build companies (plural) premised on technologies that solve large market problems. I am always looking for good people and good technologies to help in these efforts.
