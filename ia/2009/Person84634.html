Jeremy Geelan’s Summary

Exponent of "helping the Web be all it can," special interest in social computing/social software and its impact on business, society, politics, economics, culture, education, and entertainment. Domain expert on "the future of the future," with 29 years of continuous media experience from BBC TV, BBC Radio, and national newspapers in the UK to worldwide book publishing then online publishing in the US. Member since April 2007 of Guidepoint Global Advisors - an international network of executives, managers, consultants and other academic and industry professionals who provide consulting services to business decision-makers and leading investors from around the world. Founder of Social Computing Magazine and socialcomputingmagazine.com.
