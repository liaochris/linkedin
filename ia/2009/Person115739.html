Joel Jakubson’s Summary

I help people organize their personal and small business finances, with particular expertise in setting up Quicken to manage spending accounts, investment accounts, home and rental property, and sole proprietor small businesses.

In addition, I have had my own consulting corporation since January 1998. I specialize in helping newly funded telecommunications companies with product requirements, customer introductions, and economic models. I also counsel CEOs on interactions with the Board of Directors.

I was a Partner with Diamondhead Ventures from September 2000 to December 2003. I focussed on telecommunications systems, subsystems, semiconductors, components, and software.

Joel Jakubson’s Specialties:

Expert in both PC and Mac versions of QUICKEN financial management software.

Strategic consulting for early stage venture financed telecommunications firms. Particular strength in product marketing/product management.

As a VC, was involved in all stages of investment, including deal sourcing, deal screening, technical and market due diligence, termsheet development, and portfolio company management.
