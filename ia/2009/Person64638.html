David Kearsley’s Summary

As an experienced senior management executive, David has a proven track-record in profitably growing consumer hard-line product businesses and building highly competent management teams that deliver consistently “above-the-bar” in all performance matrixes. In addition to excelling in a progressive career of sales, marketing and general management, David has collaboratively developed significant and sustainable business platforms with the largest and most respected retailers, distributors, commercial suppliers, globally.

David’s experience has allowed him to pursue his passion in building, resurrecting and launching consumer product businesses. David’s skill-set and solution-driven mindset delivers a meaningful value proposition that requires strategic focus on innovative product and design, a solid go-to-market strategy, brand, managing a global supply chain and solid interactive customer relationships. David is a proven leader focused on teamwork and empowerment, providing a clear vision, while developing, nurturing, coaching management talent at all levels. Collectively, this provides the ability to optimize the business model for effectiveness and efficiency in a changing worldwide economy.

David is active in his community, church, and trade organizations. He is a past member of the Board of Directors for the Ronald McDonald House of Winston-Salem, Inc., a non-profit that provides a "home away from home" for families of children who are receiving medical care.
