Monica Hamburg’s Summary

At a recent conference, the brochure described me as:

“a Vancouver-based writer and social media consultant/evangelist. She is a researcher, investigator, and observer of imagery in today’s multi-media world, helping clients use online media to communicate their message”.

Sounds good - I'll go with that.
(See my Me Like the Interweb Blog for more information)

I also present on social media topics. And am co-organizer of Third Tuesday Vancouver, a social media meetup for people in communications/marketing.

In no particular order, here are some of the things I do:
• Speaking (on Crowdsourcing, Social Media for NonProfits, Blogging and Social Media)
• Writing (Web content (sites, landing pages), Blog posts, articles, newsletters, press releases. Some examples of my writing style can be found on One Degree or my social media blog . I also ghostwrite a fair bit of content which I obviously/sadly can’t direct you to. My specialty is adding a humorous bent to material.)
• Consulting (mostly teaching people about social media)
• Blogger engagement, relation, blogger pitching, campaigns
• Promotion ideas
• Video Scriptwriting, Editing, Filming, Acting/Voiceover
• Research (social media in general, and more specifically material for topics I speak on etc.)


Since I don't love how Linkedin presents the profile info, here's some background so who I am and what I've done gets put into context. I was an actor for 13 years while working other jobs and writing on the side. I self-educate continually, and have always loved psychology, advertising & marketing.

I discovered that I wanted more (more control, more mental stimulation, a career that had less-focus on looking like a supermodel). While marketing some of my online film projects I realized how much I liked online marketing and the scope it had and eventually had one of those "Aha!" moments (Yes, an Oprah reference, please don't stop reading yet ;)
