Eric Lammers’s Summary

Industrial Engineer with experience across multiple industries (automated robotic parking, automotive/non-automotive manufacturing, aeronautical, car rental, health care, insurance, pharmaceutical, retail, and steel). I have experience with simulation modeling, time and motion study, productivity/process improvement, packaging, workstation design, value stream mapping, customer relationship management, and supervising entry-level industrial engineers. My career ambition lies in designing and implementing improved business strategies as an Industrial Engineer and in time migrating into the field of Management.
