Ben Roth’s Summary

As co-founder of New York based strategic design collective, Brew|SDC, LLC, Ben served as the creative arm of a unique partnership; including specialists in areas of design analysis and consultation, service and architectural design. Ben speaks frequently on the topics of experience, brand and retail design at regional and national conferences, such as NY Retail Marketing Society, Connecting’07 (IDSA) and Digital Signage Expo.

Formerly an award-winning executive creative director with Publicis Groupe and the Interpublic Group of Companies, Ben has led marketing strategies for consumer promotions, interactive gaming, event communications, sports marketing, retail stores, and branded experiences. His client roster has included Merrill Lynch, Samsung, Wal-Mart, IBM, Home Depot, Brown-Forman, Turner Broadcasting, Kraft, sanofi-aventis, DirecTV, Coca-Cola, PepsiCo, Frito-Lay, Saab, the Smithsonian Institution, and the NFL Players’ Association.
