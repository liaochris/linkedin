Coert Visser’s Summary

Hi, I'm Coert Visser, a self-employed solution-focused trainer, coach and blogger. The solution-focused approach was developed within the field of psychotherapy but is now very broadly apllied in organizational settings as well. The approach is respectful, pragmatic and very goal-oriented. It has helped me to become much more effective as a coach and consultant and it has made my work more enjoyable at the same time.

Coert Visser’s Specialties:

I have dedicated the last ten or so years of my work to translating the solution-focused approach to applications like coaching, teambuilding, organizational change, personnel management and eduction. It's working great.
