Brad Tucker’s Summary

Brad Tucker is a recent graduate of Georgia Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Film Studies. He is a President’s Scholar, an Eagle Scout, and graduated with highest honors. He went to high school in Miami, Florida where he began learning about video editing and started making films in 11th grade.

Today, Brad has directed over 12 short films while here at Georgia Tech, ranging from large student productions of narrative short films, commercials for local companies, music videos, experimental films, and documentaries.

Brad will be traveling to the Cannes International Film Festival in France this summer to show his short film "Fanya Kaplan."

In the fall Brad will be perfecting his craft and getting his Master of Fine Arts in Directing as an AFI Directing Fellow in Los Angeles.

Brad Tucker’s Specialties:

Experience in all aspects of video production with emphasis in Directing, Non-Linear Editing, Digital Compositing, High-End Digital Cinematography, Motion Graphics, and Greenscreen work. Rigorous knowledge in After Effects, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Final Cut Pro, iMovie, Illustrator, Adobe Audition. Website design. Print design.

MATLAB programming, Microsoft Office Suite, experienced with both Windows and Macintosh systems, CAD (including AutoCAD, Solid Edge, Solid Works)
