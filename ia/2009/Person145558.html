Luv Luv Incorporated luvluvinc.com’s Summary

Luv Luv Incorporated – www.luvluvinc.com is a true fusion of creativeness in online specialty gift retail. Anyone who enjoys trendy, creative, artistic, colorful & unique quality style will “Luv” our store! Through our broad mix of categories and items, we cater to any type of individual consumer or business needs. Luv Luv works hard to provide great customer service excellence and personal service with care.

Our online store carries hand designed greeting cards & stationery, trendy clothing lines, modern and fine artwork, holiday gift baskets for various occasions, wonderful jewelry & accessories, books, personal mementos, home accents, e-gifts, items for men, women, children & pets as well as more unique specialty gift items to be found.

From corporate gift giving, ammenity programs, enhancing your current marketing and branding, Luv Luv "Wholesale" caters to various business and service needs.

We distribute and offer to our corporate clients a wide range of unique quality specialty gift products. Our items are composed of holiday lines, gift baskets, custom designs, private label and more.

Our inventory can be available for private label, personalization and/or creatively custom made for your company. If you are looking for a particular style or item, we will be able to find the right products for any of your ammenity needs.

Luv Luv, Inc. offers a variety of creative personalization for different products and packaging.

We understand that products, time, customer service and shipping is of utmost importance and we work hard to fulfill all orders in the most professional and timely manner.

Our professional sales consultants are available to assist you within your specific territory. Luv Luv will work one on one to assist your company and staff in choosing the right inventory.

For immediate attention, contact Luv Luv Online today to obtain more information.
