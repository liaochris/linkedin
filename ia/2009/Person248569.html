  • Senior Evaluator/Senior Consultant

    Brightsight B.V.

    (Computer & Network Security industry)

    2002 — March 2009 (7 years)

    As senior consultant/evaluator I have been part of the growth of Brightsight from a small 5-person research group in TNO to its current form as an independent large evaluation lab. The main focus of this lab is the evaluation of smartcard and related financial products, although the CC evaluations have also included more atypical products such as copiers and fighter jet components. I’ve worked on all aspects of an evaluation: as consultant to support customers in their (pre-)evaluation process, as evaluator to assess in a strict but fair manner whether the product meets the criteria, and as a trainer explaining these items to both the evaluators internally and the certifiers externally.
    Highlights include leading the worlds first CCv3.1 smartcard hardware evaluation and CCv3.1 composite of software on that smartcard, designing and giving the CC courses, designing the evaluation methodology for IP-enabled PEDs for a major credit card brand, and of course helping many, many customers.

  • Senior Policy Member

    (Government Administration industry)

    1997 — 2001 (4 years)

    As senior policy member I was involved in the evaluation of devices and software for the protection of Dutch national or NATO secret information, as well as preparation of policy, especially in the software and network domain.
    It gave me a taste for the high end, extreme attacker security requirements.
