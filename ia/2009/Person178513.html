Olivier Rouget’s Summary

I have spent 15 years at Goldman Sachs in New-York, Zurich and London managing large diversified investment portfolios for European families. With a team of ex-Goldman Sachs bankers we have founded Nevastar Finance, a London-based investment management company with offices in Geneva and Luxembourg realizing that many investors were interested in a relationship with a totally independent firm.

We have two businesses:

1) Wealth Management: we manage absolute return mandates with a very strict control of risk. Our clients are high net-worth individuals and families, endowments and financial institutions.

2) Fund Management: we manage Alpha+, a Luxembourg-based fund of hedge-funds with a low correlation to equities and a very low volatility.
We also manage ClimateChange+, the first fund of funds exclusively dedicated dedicated to Climate Change which is investing with the best equity managers in the following 5 sectors : clean energy, waste management, water management, sustainable living and infrastructure. The fund can also have an exposure to hedge-funds in order to gain access to activities such as carbon trading.
