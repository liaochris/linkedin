EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Outstanding record of success in technical operations, team building, and strategic planning in a diverse range of activities. Unique experience creating and leading Joint and Bilateral professional development programs with U.S. and Japanese military. Successfully led organizations focusing on everything from military planning, avionics technical training and evaluation, information technology, academic operations, human resources management and professional development.
