Jo Golden PhD’s Summary

I teach people how use technology effectively, educate themselves, and create the web presence they deserve with customized computer coaching and workshops. My company specializes in working with women, solo practitioners, consultants, and small businesses who want to get on the web and learn to do more with computers. It can be overwhelming to get started, and we make sure you gain confidence while we accomplish your goals.

I developed many useful techniques while teaching communication and conflict studies. Learning about technology can be stressful, so I coach, translate, and support clients as they empower themselves with computers and the Internet. Technology will always keep changing, so learning self-education strategies can only improve your life in a digital world.

I'm currently writing about self-education strategies and skills, knowledge work, identity, and conflict around learning and change in a digital world
