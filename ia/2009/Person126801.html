Karl Rohde’s Summary

I am passionate.

Passionate about everything I put my mind to. Whether I am working for my own companies, planning an activity with my son, writing an article or making a political observation on my blog. It is all done with uncompromising passion.

This passion represents the quality of work I undertake for clients.

I work with you, building understanding of what you want, and then ensure you and I know what it is you want, detailed in plain English. No gobbledygook. No jargon.

Using my knowledge from working with many small to medium business, you can be assured of creative and innovative ideas to drive your business to success. I have built an extensive experience base of cost effective and results driven marketing with technology in addition to getting the best out of technology, regardless of whether you are a start-up or early growth company.

If you are after a skilled, imaginative executive consultant who knows business and knows people, feel free to contact me for a confidential, obligation free discussion of your requirements and goals.
