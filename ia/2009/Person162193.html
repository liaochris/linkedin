Michael Skinner’s Summary

Specialising in business and process change, expertise in business process modelling and analysis (BPMA), management (BPM), redesign/re-engineering (BPR) and improvement (BPI), and organisational and people change management.

Run rapid improvement events (work-outs, kaizen and kaizen blitzes, and hothouses). From 5 to 20+ people, normal and with a competitive/team-based format, face-to-face, or by video and/or teleconference.

Michael Skinner’s Specialties:

Lean, Six Sigma, Business Process (BP) methodologies: Modelling and Analysis (BPMA), Management (BPM), Discovery (BPD), Redesign and Re-engineering (BPR), Improvement (BPI), Automation (BPA), IDEF, SPRINT, DSDM, RUP
