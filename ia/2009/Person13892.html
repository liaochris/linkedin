Het Wijninstituut gives winecourses for consumers and professionals on six locations : Amsterdam, Rotterdam, The Hague, Hilversum, Groningen and Wageningen.
We give the certified courses "Basiscursus"and "Wijnbrevet" to more than 1.000 students an year.

- Bubbles on the Beach
