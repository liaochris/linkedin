Shalabh Kayastha’s Summary

Shalabh brings 20 years of experience in hospitality at corporate, regional and property levels covering a wide range of functional areas. Prior to joining Fairmont Raffles Hotels International corporate office in Toronto, Shalabh worked with Raffles, Swissotel, InterContinental Hotels Group, Taj and Hyatt. He began his career in hotel operations covering Food & Beverage, Sales & Marketing and then held progressive positions at corporate levels in InterContinental Hotels Group and Raffles covering CRM, Campaign Management, Database Marketing, Revenue Management, Loyalty Marketing, Distribution and Ecommerce..

Shalabh holds a Masters in Business Administration from the Asian Institute of Management, Manila. He is a graduate in Hotel Management and post graduate in Hotel Administration from the Institute of Hotel Management, Mumbai and also holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics.
