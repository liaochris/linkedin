Chris Wilhelmi’s Summary

Chris Wilhelmi is a seasoned leader in the arena of marketing, analytics, CRM strategy, and database marketing. His core strength lies within the ability to integrate measurement with marketing strategy and business decision making processes.

He started his career on the client side at MediaOne, which eventually became a key component of Comcast Cable. He directed all database marketing activities for the Bay Area and guided corporate-wide initiatives for marketing database design and deployment. Chris then migrated to the agency side with MRM Partners, where he managed the analytics practice for Microsoft's Small Business and Consumer audiences. Chris then served as Analytics Director for Publicis & Hal Riney, working on Hewlett-Packard, Sprint, and WellPoint. Then Chris moved onto Clear Ink where he managed and led the Analytics and Media departments, working on Autodesk, 24 Hour Fitness, HP, and Sun Microsystems.

For the last year Chris has expanded his reach by creating the entire marketing function for Accolo, Inc. At Accolo, Chris rebuilt the website, implemeted marketing automation systems to aid the sales team, added valuable content to drive SEO, optimiazed SEO and SEM, and created marketing activites to drive demand.
