David Reitman’s Summary

David Reitman has over 19 years experience supporting clients within the Data Center & Emerging Technologies Services marketplace.

Mr. Reitman is the Vice President leading Switch and Data’s Content & Entertainment Practice. Within the Practice, David’s Team supports Content, Media and Gaming companies on Infrastructures by designing, developing, deploying and managing these environments.

Mr. Reitman has been an instrumental figure within the Online Gaming industry, as the lead Executive supporting the Team that has designed, built and deployed the following Online Games: World of WarCraft, BattleNet, EverQuest, EverQuest II, StarWars Galaxies, PlanetSide, Vanguard, Asheron’s Call, the Xbox 360 Live platform, the PS3 Online Platform, as well as, supported Codemasters, Konami and Big Fish Games. Mr. Reitman continually speaks at Gaming Conferences, in regards to the “Best Practices” associated with deploying an online gaming environment.

David was a committee team member within the Project Nigel JV. This JV focused on creating the methodologies needed to support digital distribution of content, their associated platforms and underlying digital rights management technology that needed to be leveraged in order to distribute protected copyright content. This JV’s members included AT&T, BMG & Universal Music.

David Reitman’s Specialties:

David is a Subject Matter Expert (SME) on how to build large scale Online Infrastructures, Digital Distribution of Media (i.e. Music, Film & Software), Streaming Services, Target Content Distribution, Digital Media Archive, Domestic & International Data Center facilities, IP Backbone Infrastructures and DWDM technologies. David is one of the few experts/pioneers associated with how to expand environments within mainland China.
