Corrie Tanner’s Summary

My career path started in the Health care industry. At the center of everything I do is a passion for people; I take great pride and fulfillment from being able to help people. I try to go above and beyond what is expected from me whether it's caring for the elderly or in my current position as an IT Business Development and Recruitment Manager. After a move to Calgary AB in 2006, I found my self in a new city and a very different market. I started with Modis in May of 2007 and very quickly it became apparent that I had found my niche. I enjoy a position where I am continually challenged, have endless opportunity for training and education, and where ideas and opinions are welcomed and encouraged in an open team environment. I have since moved with Modis to Vancouver BC in December 2007 to grow our business out here on the coast.
