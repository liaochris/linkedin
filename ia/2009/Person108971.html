Jelle Buizer’s Summary

My extensive experience in development, implementation and operation of new online business concepts helps me in running the internet business at FD Mediagroep.
Since my start at Viditel back in 1987 I worked with most media, like videotex, internet, radio, newspaper and TV. Interactive, online media is where my heart is!

Today I'm responsible for the internet strategy of FDMG and the commercial success of the websites FD.nl and BNR.nl. As productmanager I am responsible for profit/loss of our internet activities and also for exploiting our content in the corporate market.

I love working in or heading teams introducing new business concepts and services.

Jelle Buizer’s Specialties:

Extensive experience in publishing, internet marketing, search engine optimization & marketing (SEO and SEM), business models, website analytics, product management, project management and development of new business, multimedia, crossmedia, new business, marketing, sales, project management, operations, product development, legal and copyright issues, contracting.
