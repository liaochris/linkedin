Luca Ongaro’s Summary

I always tried (and hopefully managed) to develop my expertise in many different fields, as I tend to see things as a whole, and thus I give utmost importance to cross-competencies. Above all, I like to learn how things work and to get a global understanding of any kind of system, especially with the aim to be able to generate innovation out of it. I find it natural to work out models of reality, and I always try to use this understanding to imagine and design new approaches or concepts.

My main area of interest have recently been web 2.0 technologies and new paradigms in the field of information networks. My efforts are oriented both on the technical side, as I developed applications on a number of different platforms, and, especially, on the managerial side. I have a Bachelor Degree in Engineering and, currently, I am deepening my knowledge and expertise attending an international M.Sc. in Economics and Management of Innovation and Technology at the Bocconi University in Milan.

Luca Ongaro’s Specialties:

Management of innovation and technology. Automation and Robotics engineering, control theory. Internet apps and web development. Programming in HTML, XHTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript & AJAX, Flash & ActionScript, Python, Ruby, Processing, Java.
