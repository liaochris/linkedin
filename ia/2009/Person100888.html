Jack Cuffari’s Summary

Once called “a man who could thread a running sewing machine”, Jack has evolved from advertising agency owner to marketing communications and branding consultant, a Right Brain activator/activist who helps clients and their work move forward and break through the traditional, lop-sided business paradigm of logic and numbers to get to the MEANING all consumers - all humans - crave.

From management advisory to brand planning and strategy to impactful brand identity development, from Creative Direction to pitches, from building and managing crackerjack teams to consulting on organizational culture, from running seminars and facilitating workshops to nurturing client relationships, Jack serves as a catalyst - making good things happen wherever he's engaged.
