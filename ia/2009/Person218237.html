Simon Taylor’s Summary

I am a 24 year old enthusiastic animator who has just completed an MSc in Computer Animation with Distinction. I have been determined to be an animator since an early age and have been entering and winning festival awards for my animations since I was sixteen. I have the skills required to create believable and entertaining character and creature animation and have proven myself to always be reliable and hard working. My enthusiasm and motivation never fails in tough production environments.

Simon Taylor’s Specialties:

I have a good general knowledge of Maya 8.5 and 2008, as well as Adobe Photoshop, After Effects and Premiere Pro (CS3). I have also had experience with Apple Final Cut Pro and have a good knowledge of computer and audio-visual technology in general.

Through my MSc I successfully assumed the roles of Writer, Director, Animation Supervisor and Animator.
