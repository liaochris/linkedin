james kirkman’s Summary

I have worked in the IT industry for around 15 years, initially as a consultant and then taking a permanent position within a city finance firm, Dart. I progressed through the company until it was taken over by AFA systems, whereby I settled into a post sales technical role. I set-up and ran the IT recruitment division of Straker Simkin, a senior head hunting and recuiting company based in southern central England.
At present I am an account manager for Concurrent Systems Inc, a re-seller of CoCreate 2D & 3D CAD products.

Although my background is in Mechanical engineering, I have a huge range of hardware and software experience covering everything from networks and pc's throught to programming in various different languages.
I have also performed various sales roles and management positions, including the setting up of foreign offices. I have travelled the world with my job (the best places include Hong Kong and Tokyo, the worst includes Kuwait) and have now settled in Salisbury with my wife and two children in a small house with the most spectacular views.
