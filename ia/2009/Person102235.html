James Bickerton’s Summary

A highly motivated and creative art director with nine years experience working with clients such as: Orange, T-Mobile, Hugo Boss, Triumph, Microsoft, Toni & Guy, Kia, Patek Philippe, Intercontinental Hotels and Mitsubishi.

My talent for conceptualising ideas and realising them throughout the design and build process, allows me to meet the demands of a commercial brief on time and within budget. The ability to seek precise solutions quickly through lateral thought and self initiative make me a valuable member of any team.

My positive attitude and good humour enable me to cultivate good working relationships and excel in a team environment. A passion for design and interest in new technology help me to reach new and interesting possibilities within my work.

James Bickerton’s Specialties:

Conceptualising for advertising, Branding and Identity, Web 2.0, Social Media, User Experience, Rich Media experiences.
