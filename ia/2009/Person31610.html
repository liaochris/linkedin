Brad King’s Summary

Managing Director of Freedom Marketing Limited, an online publishing company specialising in personal finance.

Since its launch in February 2005, Freedom Marketing has built extremely strong relationships with key finance brands. Working closely with our clients such as MBNA, Egg, Barclaycard, HSBC, RBS/Natwest, Morgan Stanley Dean Whittaker, Abbey, Tesco, Alliance & Leicester, Sainsbury's Bank, Capital One and Halifax, we have seen significant growth in this short period with revenues of £10m+. We are now considered to be one of the UK's leading introducers & a finalst for Fast Growth Business Awards "Online Business of the Year".

Freedom Maketing has recently been acquired by US based creditcards.com Inc.
