Ronan Collins’s Summary

Managing Director - InteliBuild

InteliBuild are one of the worlds leading BIM + 3D Consultants and have established their headquarters in Hong Kong with business in Australia and the Middle East.

We have successfully implemented BIM on projects involving more than 3,000,000 sq. m. of GFA for large scale commercial developments.

Our services have been sought after for airports, hotels, residential complexes, railway terminals, educational developments, exhibition centres, casinos and large scale industrial projects.

We provide consultancy services on how to specify BIM, how to plan the implementation of BIM, how to manage a team of BIM modellers. We provide specifications for BIM, training and technical support for teams using BIM. We can provide key staff to work at consultants and contractors offices to lead and support project BIM teams.

In addition, we advise our clients on whether to use the BIM processes and technology for design validation, production of co-ordinated design drawings, 4D virtual construction simulation, building services co-ordination, shop drawing production and cost estimations. The BIM models can also be linked to facility management systems to gain benefits from the knowledge collected during the design and construction phases.

Save Time and Money on Construction Projects

The BIM process can be used to reduce the risk of delays on site, eliminate costly re-working through the early resolution of design problems and elimination of co-ordination clashes and improve communications between the numerous designers, contractors and suppliers involved on projects. All of the benefits accrue a saving of up to 5% of construction costs.

If you would like to learn more about BIM and the services offered by InteliBuild please contact info@intelibuild.com and we will be happy to arrange an informative presentation for you at your earliest convenience.
