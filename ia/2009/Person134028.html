Kostandinos Baltzoglou’s Summary

I've been creative since I was a little boy in Detroit
watching Mr. Dressup... now I live in the 'burbs
and watch HGTV. That kid with the bowl
hair cut is alive and well and looking to do great things...
minus the bowl hair cut, of course.

Currently, I'm employed as a Graphic Artist
at Macomb Community College. I work with frame and
vinyl vendors on a regular basis as well as project
clients and our print shop and web administrator.

I am self-motivated and extremely detail-oriented with
exceptional verbal and written communication skills. I'm
also "Monk-ish" in my organizational skills. I take
great pride in my work and excel both in working
on my own and in team projects.

My range extends from print to web to copywriting:
I am well versed in traditional design, such as printed
materials like direct mail pieces, business cards,
stationery, logos, and posters as well as basic HTML
web sites that focus on design, navigation, structure,
and ease of use for the end user. I also enjoy the challenge
of writing witty, eye-catching copy. More recently I've also
had experience with wayfinding systems and signage.

My goals are:
1) Gain full-time employment where people and their ideas are valued.
2) To improve my design and technical skills on a daily basis.
3) Learn how to screen print.
4) One day work on official Detroit Tigers' projects.
5) Have an abstract painting in the collection of the Detroit Institute of Arts.
6) Publish a collection of my creative writing works.
7) Work on a project for Morrissey.
There's room for more...

Kostandinos Baltzoglou’s Specialties:

Mac and PC: InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Flash (light so far), and Basic HTML.

Vinyl: Roland GX-24 and CutStudio software.
