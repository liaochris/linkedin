Andrew Zarick’s Summary

I'm fascinated by the convergence of technology, advertising and marketing and intrigued by the rise of social media and its influence on society.

This fascination has led to my position as Digital Strategist at The JAR Group, a Brooklyn based interactive marketing agency. In this role I help clients navigate the digital landscape, crafting strategies for continued revenue growth online through the use of SEO, paid search, affiliate marketing and social media initiatives.

I am proud co-founder of Digital DUMBO, a meetup of the city's brightest digital minds to discuss and showcase technology, while ultimately serving to foster business in the area.

Additionally, I am an active participant in the New York tech community through organizations such as NextNY, SEMPO and the NY Tech Meetup (NYTM).
