Steven Ford’s Summary

More than 20 years experience as an journalism editor and writer. Adept at making timely, informed decisions on how to best communicate to diverse audiences under strict deadlines. Confident in making editorial judgments focused on communication goals and audience expectations. Skilled at multiple communication tasks, from text editing (content and line) to non-linear video editing to social-media utilization and the effective use of Internet content-management systems and print pagination processes. Extensive print design experience, as well.

Steven Ford’s Specialties:

News and features editing and writing. Opinion editing and writing. General-science news writing. Online news presentation.

Steven Ford’s Experience

  • Senior editor

    Orlando Sentinel

    (Public Company; Newspapers industry)

    June 1993 — April 2009 (15 years 11 months)

    Senior copy editor responsible for editing a variety of features and news reports, ensuring all aspects of accuracy and relevance for readers, as well as writing headlines, photo captions and other content to attract, engage and inform readers. Content editor for the newspaper's online operations, too, working with the Orlando Sentinel's Web CMS to post and edit articles online. A-1 content editor for the print publication during a weekly night shift, as well. Back-up newsfeatures slot editor for the print product as needed. Also blog and videoblog for the newspaper's Web portal devoted to Orlando's theme parks. Prior to these duties, was a member of the newspaper's editorial board. In this role produced the Sunday news-analysis section, wrote for the section's science and medicine pages and produced other specialty sections, such as election guides and end-of-the-year news wrap-up editions. Started out at the newspaper as a page-design editor.

  • Associate Insight editor

    Orlando Sentinel

    (Public Company; Newspapers industry)

    1997 — 2004 (7 years )

    Responsible for editing the newspaper’s weekly news and analysis section, Insight. Edited content and designed pages, working with the graphic-arts editors and photojournalists to create engaging packages to reflect the section’s topics. Worked with freelance authors and non-journalist experts to produce content that was accurate, compelling and insightful. Also served as back-up Op-Ed editor as needed and helped the newspaper's editorial board form philosophical and practical views to promote or critique various issues before it. Also produced the section's science and medicine page and directed the topics covered. Wrote science articles for the page, as well, covering various topics for a general audience. Among some of the more high-profile roles:

    -- Produced annual election guides for statewide, gubernatorial and national races.
    -- Oversaw production of Florida Legislature guide to update readers on various state and local political issues.
    -- Produced annual year-in-review section and wrote year-end wrap-up of major news events.
    -- Wrote assorted centerpiece analyses on a range of subjects.
    -- Wrote various editorials on a host of issues.
