Giulio Pappalardo’s Summary

Senior Associate (Engagement Manager) at Booz & Company, formerly Booz Allen Hamilton. 9 years experience in management consulting, mainly in the retail, media, telecom and aerospace industries. Expertise in strategy, marketing, organization and due diligences. Worked for both leading international corporations and for main international organizations (EU, UN).

Broad international experience: worked and lived firmly in the US, Italy, Belgium and the Netherlands; working experience in various other countries in Europe (France, Germany), Asia (Mongolia), South America (Brazil), and the Middle East (Dubai). Speaks Italian and French native, English and German fluent, Spanish conversational.

Education: MBA at Sloan School of Management (US); MS in Electronic Engineering at Politecnico di Milano (USA); High School at Ecole Européenne (Belgium)

Recently relocated to Los Angeles, CA, for personal reasons, from Amsterdam, the Netherlands.

Work authorized in the US (green card) and EU (native).
