Philip Liebman’s Summary

As a former CEO, Creative Director and Serieal Entrepreneur I offer a diversity of relevant busines building skills and perspective to Chief Executives and business owners.

Working as a business and executive coach, strategic consultant, exectuvive peer-group facilitator or personal confidente, my goal is to increase the effectiveness and enhance the lives of my clients and group members. My objective is help people become better leaders, make better decisions and get better results.

For my clients a members this is realized by faster growth, greater personal and professional satisfaction, a better sense of balance between their personal and professional lives - and a greater impact on the world around them.

I am acutely aware of the special needs of transgenerational family-owned businesses - and work wtih owners and managers to craft effective managment strategies - and ultimately successful succession plans.

As an executive peer-group facilitator I engage a tremendous wealth of support, resources and personal/professional development - both in my role as a Vistage Group Chair for SMBs (Vistage is the World's Largest CEO Membership Organization - wtih more than 13,000 members in 15 countries) - and Strat-Forums for small, developing and emerging entrepreneurial companies.

Background:

I have worked as an agency head serving Fortune 500 companies in the publishing, financial services, investment, communications and high-tech industry sectors - and have served on the senior management teams for companies in youth electronics, internet media and publishing, embedded technologies and natural and specialty foods. I bring a unique perspective in terms of opportunity insight, marketing communications, corporate and investor communications, product development, brand management and team building.

My pesonal goal is to provide impact solutions to those who impact the world.

Philip Liebman’s Specialties:

I help companies anticipate, identify and capitalize on profitable new growth opportunities. A former marketing agency President and Chief Creative Officer, and seasoned entrepreneur with experience launching and running companies in the specialty and natural foods sector, youth electronics and high tech - with strong communication and presentation skills, market instincts and strategic planning experience.
