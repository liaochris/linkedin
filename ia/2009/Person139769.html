Leo LaGrotte’s Summary

As founder and President/CEO of Life Settlement Advisors, LLC, I have spent over 8 years working in the burgeoning life
settlement industry. I began my career as an insurance agent, operating my own independent agency for many years, giving me a broad knowledge of life insurance and industry-leading experience in the analysis and pricing of life insurance policies.

My extensive knowledge and experience in dealing with the industry funding sources gives Life Settlement Advisors a unique perspective in this dynamic new market.

I advocate for the development of responsible regulation and legislation for life settlements. In 2008, I testified before the Indiana General Assembly on behalf of the life settlement industry which ultimately led to the revision of a regulation bill that was not acting in the best interests of consumers.

I have authored articles on the life settlement industry in publications such as the Indianapolis Business Journal, Vistage International and Hoosier Banker and am a frequent speaker on the subject. I am currently on the Trust Committee of the Indiana Bankers Association (IBA) and have a pending seat on the Board of Directors of the Estate Planning Council of Indianapolis.
