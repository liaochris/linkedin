Philip Kearney’s Summary

Accomplished senior eBusiness leader with 15-plus years of demonstrated success in generating and re-starting profitable growth for digital brands in environments ranging from large corporations to new ventures. Deep experience building and leading teams focused upon the ability to attract, engage, retain and monetize target audiences and existing customers.

Simply put, I can take a Web site and reduce overall spend, optimize for engagement and identify additional revenue opportunities to increase profitability & grow your business.

Fifteen years and 150+ sites / approx. 100 digital brands from Fortune 500, mid-caps and ventures - working as a consultant with executive management teams to build, integrate and grow business in Digital Media, specializing in pharmaceutical, publishing and consumer packaged goods industries.
