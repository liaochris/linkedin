EFN Financial Svce’s Summary

Give your business the competitive edge with our unique eFundsNow Point of Banking (POB) solution!

EFN Financial Services specializes in innovative, easy-to-use human interface or “Manned ATM” solutions. EFN Financial Solutions introduces eFundsNow, a progressive alternative to traditional ATM machines. Our custom Point of Banking solution, eFundsNow, might best be described as a cross between a Point of Sale (POS) and an ATM machine. eFundsNow provides cash back at any Point of Sale location.

Through our unique eFundsNow solution, your business or organization is certain to realize tremendous bottom-line results with our fee based revenue stream. This is an extraordinary advantage for your company to generate consistent supplemental revenue with little or no investment. Not only will you benefit from ATM fee revenue, but you are allowing customers to access funds conveniently without having to leave the store or location.

EFN Financial Services and its associates are currently processing millions of transactions per month, and growing each day! Allow us to provide you with detailed information on how our eFundsNow “manned ATM” solution can benefit your organization.

Benefits of our eFundsNow Solution:

• Generate tremendous sustainable supplemental revenue on each approved transaction.
• Provide cash back services at the point of sale.
• Replace Fixed and Variable Fees with small monthly fee.
• Eliminate cost and liability of on site ATM machines.
• Funds (Principal and Surcharge) are guaranteed.
• Web-based monitoring
• PIN based transactions eliminate Chargebacks!
• Free employee training.
• State of the art customer service.
• Additional Marketing assistance available.
• Easy to integrate host connectivity.
• Integrated host application avoids additional hardware requirements.
