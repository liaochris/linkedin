David R Williams’s Summary

As Senior Vice President/Regional Manager of Colorado for WR Starkey Mortgage, I am responsible for the growth and expansion of the company throughout the State of Colorado.

I have been in the mortgage business since 1981 and with my previous company for nearly 17 years. The vision and passion of WR Starkey Mortgage was so inspiring that I had to become a part of the team.

While most companies are asking what the loan officer can do for them, we are asking what we can do for the loan officer. Our focus is to help increase the loan officer’s business and provide a platform to support their existing business while improving their quality of life. Our attention to our people makes us different.

If you are looking for a company where people truly do come first, I urge you to contact us and find out why our people do make a difference to our company and to the customers they serve.
