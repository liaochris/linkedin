Jim Moore’s Summary

Twenty-five years as a writer for government leaders, from Capitol Hill to the White House. Speechwriter and writer for four Cabinet Secretaries.

An accomplished and well-published journalist and photographer, my work has appeared in the major U.S. newsweeklies, Including Time, Newsweek, and U.S. News and World Report, as well as many major foreign newspapers and magazines.

Editor, Arrive Magazine; Editor, Military Exchange and Military Grocer Magazines; editor, the Senate Report on the Gulf War; Producer, Newslink; Editor, Department of Defense Office of Gulf War Illnesses.

Jim Moore’s Specialties:

Speechwriter, speech coach, journalist, editor, video producer, voiceover talent for radio and podcasts, government public relations, Congressional liaison for both the House and the Senate.
