Jessica Ryan Ohlin’s Summary

Brand Identity and Organizational Consultant, 1993-2007

Focusing on healthcare and not-for-profit space. Corporate clients including Genesis Healthcare, Prudential Health Care, Duke Energy, Bell Canada, Dexter Corporation, Inacom, S.W.I.F.T., ScotiaBank, Banco Popular, NASD, and JCPenney, among others.

Most recently have advised various organizations on issues of branding, communication, and outreach, including Kindering Center, Williams Syndrome Association, and Fairfield Special Education PTA. Organized Tri-State Community-building and fundraising events for national Williams Syndrome Association. Led fundraising efforts and chaired bi-annual auction to benefit Fairfield SEPTA's community programs for individuals with disabilities and their families.
