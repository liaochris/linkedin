Hunter Hebert’s Summary

Originally operated as Jaguar Consulting Group; London Financial Capital, LLC. was founded by Mr. Hunter Hebert, President and CEO, and Mr. Douglas Schulman, Vice President and CFO.

London Financial Capital, LLC was founded in 2002 in Auburn Hills, Michigan but relocated to Frisco, Texas in mid 2007. Our principal offices are now located in Grand Blanc, Michigan with satelite offices in Frisco Texas just 30 minutes north of Dallas..


London Financial Capital, LLC. is privately owned and has full control over all perspective loans, thus eliminating time delays due to insufficient funding. No project is to large.

Our team gives each project individual priority and care. At London Financial Capital, LLC., you are not just a number, you are our future. We recognize the value of your business. Our staff continues to excel in customer satisfaction, and reliable communication.
