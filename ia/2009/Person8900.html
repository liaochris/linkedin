Alfredo Tor Paz’s Summary

For the last 25 years, Alfredo has been involved in developing Hospitality and Travel companies, in the areas of Sales, Marketing, and Operations.

After finishing studies at Hebrew University, he was involved in student activities and later when he rejoined, Hilton Jerusalem`s sales team, and later, went to work for one of the largest tour operators in Israel, Unitours Israel, and bringing this company to the top of the local operators increasing the sales and branching into the first Charter plane consolidator. Together with partners, started the Latin american branch of Liberty International, introducing newer packages for Eastern Europe and Africa.
Involved in directing and handling the largest pilgrim movement to the Holy Land, during the visit of the Pope, John Paul II, that was a unique event.
Later worked as a consultant in Mexico, Spain, Israel, Panama and Uruguay, in diverse areas of the tourist industry.
He arrived to Panama to consult for the local tourist industry, and went to manage the Playa Blanca Hotel and Resort and making it the top resort in the country, and he is the GM of the new Radisson Colon 2000.
He has been involved in improving the training standards of the Hotel Industry and the local tourist industry.
Innovating in environmental methods for the Hospitality Industry, reducing costs and making hotel operations more profitable.

Alfredo Tor Paz’s Specialties:

Sales and Marketing in different countries, sustainable hotel development, developing new niche markets, operational design of hotels and lodges.
