David Greenwald’s Summary

The i2i Placement team seamlessly helps companies hire sales and marketing teams as well as support staffs to out-perform goals and reach their milestones. We "dig deep" to find, uncover and recruit talented/hard working people for great companies and great companies for top performing people in the Online Media industry.

David Greenwald, the company's Founder, President and Managing Director is a veteran of the Internet and Media Advertising business, having built the strategy, ideaiated, sold, managed and mentored sales teams at 7 Internet companies since 1996. Now operating a very successful recruitment company where we work 16 hours or more a day helping candidates and companies grow...and we love EVERY minute of it!

David Greenwald’s Specialties:

i2i Placement focuses 100% on the Online Media business. Whether it be Support/Admin, Sales, Marketing, Business Development, Business Analysis, Ad Operations, Client Services, Account Management, all the way up to C-level within the organization. Having managed people/teams, built P&L and sold off line and online media over the past 26 years, David and i2i Placement is uniquely qualified to help you find a new job or the best candidates for your company.
