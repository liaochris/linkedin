Gareth Williams’s Summary

Gareth, a formally trained graphic designer with 10+ years of interactive experience, has worked within the GPS/LBS portion of mobile industry since early 2006. As manager of the Visual Product Design Group at Networks In Motion, Inc., Gareth leads his team of interactive graphic designers, collaborates with product managers and engineers to create the visual design, UI, UX, and marketing materials of the world’s most popular mobile device-based ‘turn-by-turn’ navigation and hyper-local search software.
