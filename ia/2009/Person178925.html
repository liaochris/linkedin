Camille Fetter, Senior Partner

Camille Fetter is an Executive Recruiter for Lucas Group’s Search Division in Chicago. She recruits in the online marketing field. Camille focuses on placing the new breed of professionals in emerging media field. Her experience recruiting for these industries enables her to effectively match candidates and clients, providing the most beneficial opportunities for all parties.The winning combination of her personality and her in-depth industry knowledge has led to her success which has been nothing short of extraordinary. In her first year with Lucas Group, Camille set the record in successful placements for first-year associates.

Camille was the recipient of Lucas Group’s Rookie of the Year Award for her outstanding efforts in her first year with the company. She has superb experience working on both retained and contingent searches placing mid-to-senior level executives professionals at interactive marketing firms nationwide. Prior to joining Lucas Group, Camille began her career recruiting with a staffing firm where she successfully placed temporary and permanent positions in advertising, marketing, and creative.

A graduate of the University of Colorado at Boulder, Camille holds a BS in Advertising while minoring in Technology, Art & Media. She is currently on the board of the Chicago Interactive Marketing Association (CIMA).
