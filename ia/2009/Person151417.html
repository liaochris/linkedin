Mark Bult’s Summary

I'm a design veteran specializing in web design, redesign, and user experience.

» 20 years as a design professional.
» 13 years concentrating on web design employing the principles of user-centered design, including some of the world's largest online properties.
» 20 years as an occasional freelance writer/journalist.
» 14 years experience with nonprofit entities, including these roles: volunteer, staff, committee chair, and board member.

Portfolio:
http://www.enews.org/portfolio/

Mark Bult’s Specialties:

» Design of compelling and beautiful user experiences.
» Web design / redesign of small, medium, and large sites (Download.com, Webshots.com).
» Complex modern web apps (a.k.a. "Web 2.0") and UIs.
» Brand and identity design (logos, stationery systems, style guidelines).
» Editorial design (online and print periodicals, catalogs, etc.).
» Print and collateral design (all sorts).
» Working with nonprofits.
» Green design — design that observes and employs the principles of sustainability.
