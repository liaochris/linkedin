Fiona Mackenzie’s Summary

Experienced senior marketing strategist working across online and offline environments. Between May 2006- August 2007, Fiona was engaged by the Commonwealth Bank of Australia to design and execute a large-scale Intranet content site.

Prior to this, Fiona has provided consultancy to a number of small and medium sized businesses with a particular emphasis on their online channel strategy and organic ranking position with major search engines.

An experienced product manager, Fiona worked for Telstra Corporation for six years prior to her online consulting work.

There she managed a number of large product portfolios (up to $2B p.a.) across both B2C and B2B sectors.

Her experience includes all facets of marketing and product management, with particular emphasis on pricing and channel strategy, product roadmaps and the management of capital build programs and financial management (including P&L, costs, revenue and capital).

She has a MBA, plus Diplomas in both Marketing and Public Relations.

Connect to Fiona by emailing fiona (at) fionamackenzie.com.au

Fiona Mackenzie’s Specialties:

Strong capable strategic thinker and ideas developer; experienced trouble-shooter; high performer in product roadmap development and life cycle management; creative marketer; strong financial management skills (including P&L); can-do attitude and team player. Online experience includes excellent small and large scale experience in website design, navigation, useability and construction with a particular specialisation in search engine optimisation.
