Sanjay Dalal’s Summary

Sanjay Dalal is the Chief Innovator of InnovationMain.com. Sanjay launched the Innovation Bootcamp to spark new ideas, unlock creativity, and jump start innovations. He is the author of Innovation eBook used by 300+ companies.

Sanjay is the Chief Marketer of Semdia, an innovative Search and Internet Marketing business providing online marketing.

Sanjay introduced the Innovation Index that correlates innovation, business and stock performance.

Sanjay has 15 years leadership experience at technology companies in marketing. He helped Educomp Solutions with its e-learning business. At WebEx, he introduced innovative products, Training Center and Sales Center. He filed joint U.S. Patent on "Hands-on Labs" for hands-on training. He led the first two mergers, and drove 30+ strategic partnerships. He grew the combined product line revenue to $70 million. Sanjay led product marketing at eComLive, & worked in key product roles at IBM & Honeywell Bull.

Sanjay got B.S from University of Texas at Austin in EE (Dean’s Honor Roll, President’s Honor Roll at OU), completed coursework for M.S. in Computer Science at ASU, & holds executive certification on Leading Management Teams from Cornell University.

Sanjay was Basketball Coach three times for his son's teams, Art Master for his daughter’s class, & member of Technology Advisory Committee for Fremont School District, He is the secretary of Student Site Council, Web 2.0 adviser for CSU Fullerton, DLC UC Irvine. An active Rotarian, he founded the non-profit Osteoporosis Foundation.

Sanjay was born in Ahmedabad, Gujarat State, India in 1968. Sanjay, a Hindu, values the principles of non-violence, peace, kindness, honesty and respect. Sanjay came to U.S. in 1987 at OU, Norman with his sister Situ. Sanjay became a U.S. Citizen in 2008 to vote and enjoy the citizenship rights! "I am proud of my Indian roots and culture, and love the spirit of freedom, entrepreneurship and opportunity that U.S. offers."
