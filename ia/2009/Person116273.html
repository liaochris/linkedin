  • Director of Design

    Respond.com

    (Computer Software industry)

    February 1999 — September 2002 (3 years 8 months)

    Responsible for all aspects of the user experience on the Respond.com “Shop By Request” Web site, as well as, the system-generated Buyer and Seller emails, online promotions, and all other user interactions with the Respond.com shopping service.

    Held directly accountable for increasing both the Buyer conversion and Seller retention rates. Successes included:

    • Doubling the Buyer conversion rate over a two-month period.
    • Significant increases to the “requests per visitor” and “repeat visitor” rate.

    These changes brought the “cost per request” to an all-time low allowing us cut our Marketing spending while keeping the request
    volume constant.

    Managed a six-person Product Development team. Team duties
    included weekly Web site updates, enhancements to existing features, design and implementation of new features, and continual user testing.
