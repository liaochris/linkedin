Sherry Ziegelmeyer’s Summary

Through Black and Blue Media, I have had the great fortune of being able to create publicity campaigns for some of the most artistic and original director/producers in adult entertainment.

With Michael Ninn, my partner Jay Moyes and I created an alternate reality game to promote the production "Four".

Jay and I created an animated "spokestoon" based on our good friend Moxxie Maddron, to promote our client, Midnight Videos a VOD retailer.

Platinum Blue Productions allowed us to create interesting nominations packs and review kits and fan events.

With SLLAB, I was able to do a mainstream style publicity and marketing blitz for Frank Castle's 'The New Neighbors', including a special PSK "Terror-oake", review and nominations kits and many other great promotions.

I am very proud to be able to add 7 years as a member of the KLOS Community Switchboard, as well as five years of service with the KABC Community Relations and KLOS/KABC Creative Services departments to my resume, as it was a very educational experience, as well as a fulfilling state of employment.

Before I began working for Capitol Cities (KABC/KLOS Corporate Parent), I freelanced as a publicist and handled promotions and merchandise for Los Angeles based bands, including StoryTeller, Sin/Jag Wire and Rise.

Black and Blue Media's expertise and commitment to providing affordable and professional publicity and public relations services is not limited to the adult space. I and my partner recently worked with a financial investment and brokerage house and are currently working with a culinary technical school in New York, that focuses on training Hispanic students to move up in the restaurant circles in that very competitive city.

Any client that has a creative and exciting product or service, and is looking for someone to get them the publicity and media exposure that they deserve is a welcome addition to our client list.
