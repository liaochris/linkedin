song wei’s Summary

The Mobile Internet the Great Wall Club (MIGWC) is a non-profit organization dedicated to promote friendship, cooperation and globalization. It was officially set up on March 27th, 2008. The headquarters of the club is located in Beijing. MIGWC enables companies to keep up with the latest developments and exchange information with the top players in the world's number one mobile market. MIGWC continues to develop strategic alliances with overseas associations including IVP, Mobile Content Forum (Japan), Korea Internet Associations, Korea Wireless Internet Solution Association (Korea) and Service Mobiles (France) etc. Our members use these unique alliances to develop overseas partnerships for the benefit of growth. The annual meeting of the club will be held on March or April every year.
