Dave Friedel’s Summary

In the I.T. industry, my focus is making computer systems profitable to a business. No matter how good the technology, if a system costs more than it saves or earns, it's not a success.

Today, so much more than software engineering is involved. Integration and system solutions encompass far more than programming. They drive customer expectation, costs, timing, profit and loss. Technology is created to make business better not because of the wow factor.
