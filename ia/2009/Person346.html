Thomas Anderson’s Summary

Award-winning art director experienced with a special emphasis on conceptual design and communication goals. I am always looking for networking opportunities and collaboration on new projects.

Specializing in print, web design, point of sale, fleet marketing & event/environmental/trade show. I work mainly in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, IMovie-Quicktime shorts and a some Powerpoint on the Macintosh side of the world.

Excellent communication skills & marketing know-how, able to develop & present creative concepts for both campaigns as well as individual projects. I actually prefer working in a fast-paced environment and meeting short deadlines.

Dedicated to implementing new ways to improve process flow, WIP and project management tasks. Team orientated & understand the value of building strong relationships with clients & vendors as well as designers & co-workers.

My goal is to concept & deliver great ideas that convey a message to the world, we all can understand.
