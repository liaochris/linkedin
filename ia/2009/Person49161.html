Corbett Kroehler’s Summary

I am the Chief Media Officer of the Interstate Traveler Company. I also am the author of the high-traffic Climate Crisis blog (http://www.Keyboard-Culture-Global-Warming.com) which receives more than 1,000,000 unique visitors per month.
