Mark Friedler’s Summary

Mark is an online media entrepreneur and executive. His last company, GameDaily, was acquired by AOL in August 2006. He recently co-founded a strategic consulting firm, Worlds and Games LLC.

He is an entrepreneurial executive with a record of building and growing profitable organizations and brands that anticipate market direction and execute on new product and service opportunities. A strategic thinker and deal maker, who brings ideas to life, takes the straightest line to the goal and drives constant innovation based on real-time feedback. Passionate, persuasive marketer and experienced public speaker who cuts through ambiguity to deliver solutions. Decisive leader, who attracts smart, independent people, provides clear direction and allows the freedom to excel.

-CEO, GameDaily, sold to AOL in 2006. Grew to Comscore’s Number 1 Gaming Information site in September 2006. Director of Games at AOL and Publisher of GameDaily until November 2007.

-CEO and co-founder Gigex, Inc. - leader in game demo and trailer download distribution, included partner sites of Yahoo, MSN and 50 others.

-Founder/CEO of V-Cast, Inc - the first provider of Internet push delivery content, sold to Verizon Wireless.

-Dir of Bus Dev Sales at Reach Networks New York.

-Managing Director of Teleworld Sweden AB (a division of Philips Media) Operated interactive TV online services with MTV Europe, RTL and other major European broadcasters.

-Founder of Sweden’s first chain of chocolate chip cookie stores, www.americancookies.se. Featured: Wall Street Journal, Front page, June 8, 1988

Bachelor's degree with honors from Hamilton College. Graduate courses - business law, computuer programming: CUNY and New School, New York City.

Mark is frequently invited speaker as an internet advertising, entertainment and media expert at industry events including AdTech, OMMA, CES, E3, Digital Hollywood, Digital Media Wire, EMA, MI6, Tech Council of So Cal, Jupiter and others.
