Von Johnson’s Summary

Von Johnson has over twenty-five years experience in the entertainment industry including electronic distribution, global post production, technical services, project management, systems development and implementation, business and systems integration.

Von Johnson & Associates, Inc. is dedicated to assisting companies with strategic planning, marketing and implementation of their technology solutions for the entertainment industry. Visit www.vonjohnson.com for more information.

Prior to forming VJ&A, Johnson was senior vice president of Distribution and Sales Administration for Universal International Television, with responsibility over sales contracts, product availabilities and fulfillment. Johnson has also held senior management positions in distribution, acquisitions and systems at The Walt Disney Studios, Disney Channel, Disney Imagineering, Turner Entertainment Co., ITC Entertainment and DIVA Systems (a video-on-demand technology pioneer in Menlo Park, CA). Johnson is also co-founder of White Ash Broadcasting, Inc., licensee for central California’s NPR affiliate, KVPR-FM Valley Public Radio.

A member of the Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers (SMPTE), Johnson is co-inventor of “Method and apparatus for transmission of full frequency digital audio.” (U.S. patent 5,544,228). Johnson is a native of Southern California and holds a Bachelor Degree in Mass Communications from California State University, Fresno and an MBA from Woodbury University in Burbank, California.

Von Johnson’s Specialties:

Strong negotiating skills, broad international business experience and thorough knowledge of filmed entertainment distribution and technical services.
