Diego Martinisi’s Summary

Real Estate is an exciting working field, also because for me, as a building engineer, it's possible to follow a building from the "idea" in the planner mind to the construction phase, till the sale one. My experience has been enriched by the research done at the university on technical constructions, restoring ancient assets, economic potentiality of small heritage centres. Then, during my professional period in a primary national bank, I've learnt how to appraise an asset, what has to be "seen" valuating it, caring the "customer", trying to understand its real needs. The present experience, instead, enriches my competence because now I'm learning the commercial potentiality of an asset (real corrispondence between house = sum of money) and the managment of non performing loans.

Diego Martinisi’s Specialties:
