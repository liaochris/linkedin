Vinny Pedulla’s Summary

I have been performing as a guitarist, pianist and vocalist in the New York area for about 20 years now. One area has been in Contemporary Christian Music as a music director/worship leader. I have also been involved in music education both privately and publicly with the New York Department of Education for the same amount of time. I now run Underground Recording Studios where I can focus on both teaching and recording my music as well as perspective clients.

Vinny Pedulla’s Specialties:

Teach: Guitar, Bass, Piano, Organ, Drums and Voice. Also can compose and perform in all genres as well as produce and arrange in all styles of music. The studio provides both full-featured recording and music education in all the aforementioned areas.
