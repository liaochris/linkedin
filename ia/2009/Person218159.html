Simon Parkin’s Summary

Simon writes extensively for Edge magazine, Eurogamer, Yahoo, Gamasutra, and the Official UK Xbox 360 magazine as well as working as a game designer and producer at Littleloud, a Flash game developer based in Brighton, England.

In November 2008, the Channel 4 interactive drama Bow Street Runner, for which Parkin wrote the Game Design Document, won a BAFTA award.

He continues to work extensively as a freelance games critic. His fortnightly column ‘Chewing Pixels‘ can be read at GameSetWatch, and is occasionally cross-posted on Gamasutra.
