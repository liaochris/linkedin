Daniel E. Turissini’s Summary

Mr. Turissini has as more than 20 years of strategic, operating and financial management experience in high technology companies. His expertise and influence have made him a recognized leader in the industry, where he plays a key role in several national identity superiority initiatives and advises companies, organizations and government agencies.

Daniel E. Turissini’s Experience

  • Board of Directors

    Federation for Identity and Cross-Credentialing Systems®

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    May 2007 — Present (2 years)

    Mr. Turissini is on the Board of Directors of Federation for Identity and Cross-Credentialing Systems® (FiXs) where he currently chairs the FiXs Operations Committee.

    Mr. Turissini has taken a leadership roll with FiXs: first, by drafting the FiXs Logical Operating Rules supplement to the FiXs Operating Rules, and leading the effort in defining the requirements that need to be met for FiXs logical operations; and, concurrently, deploying a FiXs certified credential issuance solution that achieved IATO certification - fully posturing FiXs to fulfill its mission to establish and maintain a worldwide, interoperable identity and cross-credentialing network built on security, privacy, trust, standard operating rules, policies and technical standards.

  • VP, CTO

    Widepoint

    (Public Company; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    October 2004 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    Focused on the overall scientific and technical guidance across the Widepoint family of companies.

  • CEO

    ORC,Inc.

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    April 1991 — Present (18 years 1 month)

    Co-founder, President & CEO - innovator in systems engineering focused in the field of Identity Assurance and Security. Obtained ORC certification as first External Certificate Authority (ECA) by the Department of Defense to enable businesses, associations & individuals who wish to conduct electronic business & services with the Government. Certified by the General Services Administration under Access Certificates for Electronic Services (ACES), Shared Service Provider (SSP), & E-Authentication Credential Services. Obtained ORC designation as lead systems integrator for the DoD Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) implemented across all branches of the DoD (a user community of approximately 36 million personnel, devices, & applications); &, engineered successful deployments of HSPD-12 Personal Identity Verification (PIV) for over 25K users. Provide integration & testing, operation & maintenance, and research & development leadership for all aspects of Identity Superiority.
