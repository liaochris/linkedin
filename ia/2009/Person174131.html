Nick Harrison’s Summary

A CTO and experienced software architect with over 14 years experience in designing, developing, delivering and managing world-class large-scale web-based online systems.

Creating some of the most sophisticated web platforms, Nick's experience and foresight has been key in helping organisations develop technical strategy, reduce risk, deploy efficient multi-server platforms and scale environments to meet the demands of business evolution and explosive growth in usage.

In 2004, Nick was voted one of the UK’s 100 'Internet Pioneers' for his influential work and contribution to the growth and development of the internet and e-commerce in the past decade.
