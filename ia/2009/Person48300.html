Colin Hayhurst’s Summary

My interest is in helping to create and grow early stage technology and knowledge based businesses. I work with entrepreneurs and small high growth potential businesses and help them realise their potential.

I help to raise investment finance typically in the range £20,000 to £500,000 and organise a local angel investment club. I mentor and coach clients and help them in various ways including connecting them others that can help their business.

At the University of Brighton I provided business support and mentoring to entrepreneurs and academics and worked with other universities across the South East.

Previously I was a co-founder of Century Dynamics Limited, a scientific and engineering software company. Having successfully grown the company it was sold to ANSYS in 2005 when I decided to leave to pursue new challenges.

I am a chartered engineer with experience in a several industry sectors.

    (Non-Profit; Management Consulting industry)

    2) IP protection
    3) Disruptive market potential
    4) Substantial improvements over current products/services.
