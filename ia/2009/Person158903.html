Meg Zimbeck’s Summary

I write stories about the things I love - restaurants, concerts, and nightlife in Paris and other European cities. These have appeared in the Wall Street Journal online, BlackBook, Arthur Frommer's Budget Travel, CNN online, Gridskipper, Momondo, and in-flight magazines for United and Gulf Air.

I also write guidebooks (BlackBook) and give specialized assistance to groups (film crews) when they're visiting Paris.

Writing and photos can be found at http://megzimbeck.com
