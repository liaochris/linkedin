Michael A. Reid’s Summary

My 25+ years of diverse financial services experience make me a very knowledgeable, well-rounded professional. I am working to achieve a level of success that compensates me adequately and allows me to have life balance in order to spend time on the things that are most important in my faith, family, business, and community. I know that by helping my clients be financially successful, my financial success naturally follows.

I've been self-employed full and part time since 1996, and doing business as Reid Financial Group since August 2007.

Michael A. Reid’s Specialties:

My specialties have been mostly in financial institutions, especially in trust and investments, income tax and personal financial planning. I understand financial markets, estate planning, and many financial issues better than most of my peers in accounting, banks, insurance and investments.
