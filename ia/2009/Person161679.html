Michael Chang’s Summary

Nearly 8 years of mobile phone sales and marketing experience at Panasonic Taiwan with one year at Panasonic Mobile Communications in Yokohama, Michael has accumulated much sales and product marketing experience for mobile phones in the Taiwan mobile market. High-touch sales with the major mobile operators including Chunghwa Telecom, Taiwan Mobile and FarEastone with channel sales via Senao, Synnex, Aurora and Arcoa. Michael is a digital gadget freak who likes the latest mobile phones, mobile game devices, new internet and personal computing technologies. Departure from the mobile phone line of work, Michael has started his new career at Hudson Global Resources as a Consultant recruiting IT sales / marketing / technical professional for IT Software and Mobile clients.

Michael Chang’s Specialties:

Mobile phone operator and channel sales and product marketing, IT professional recruiting for global IT software and mobile companies.
