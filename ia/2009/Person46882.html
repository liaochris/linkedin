Karen Clark’s Summary

I currently work at BioWare Corp., makers of AAA video game titles Mass Effect, The Baldur's Gate Series, Knights of the Old Republic, etc.. I am project manager of an unannounced web project for the Marketing/Web teams. Initially I served as Project Manager for the Cinematic and In-Game Animation teams on Dragon Age.

I am a highly organized, productive project manager with experience in the online virtual space. I advocate the use of Agile methodologies including Extreme Programming and Scrum, and have completed Certified Scrum Master training. My educational pursuits continue - I am studying for my Master's Degree in Project Management (est. completion Fall 2010).

As Development Program Manager at Linden Lab, I managed the build and release process for a year, then moved on to serve as the Product Owner/Customer Representative for one of our Development Studios. During my tenure there, I watched Second Life grow from 40,000 total users to over 7 million. It was a remarkable experience and I learned a lot about working at a fast pace in an ever-changing environment.

In my free time, I volunteer as the Mentor Program Coordinator for Women in Games International. My goal is to aid women of all backgrounds to succeed in the electronic entertainment industry through mentoring connections that last a lifetime.
