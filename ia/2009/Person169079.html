Matt Britton’s Summary

Matt Britton is known throughout the advertising and promotions community as an expert in marketing to college students and young adults. Matt currently serves the company as the Chief Of Brand Development, and oversees all sales, marketing, and public relations activities within the firm

For the past twelve years, . Matt has developed a unique expertise in helping corporations reach young the 12-24 demographic through an innate understanding of what makes young people take action.

In January of 1997 while a Senior in college, Matt founded The Magma Group, a college marketing and direct promotions firm focused on the hi-tech industry. Britton's business development and marketing expertise helped grow the company to a multi-million dollar organization with $0 in outside investments. The Magma Group's clients included Yahoo!, Lycos, eBay, Sony Music, and MBNA America.

In June of 1999, Matt was named to the prestigious 30 Under 30 list by Entrepreneur Magazine of the 30 hottest U.S. entrepreneurs under 30 years of age

In December of 2000, The Magma Group was acquired in part by YouthStream Media Networks where Matt was retained as the Director of Event Marketing Development. Matt would go on to increase YouthStream's event marketing revenues five-fold over a two year period prior to joining Paul Tedeschi in September of 2002 to found Mr. Youth LLC.

In September of 2002, Matt became a founding partner of Mr. Youth LLC. Mr. Youth is now the fastest growing youth marketing firm the nation per PROMO Magazine. Mr. Youth’s clients include Microsoft, JetBlue Airways, Kimberly-Clark, and General Motors. In 2004, Mr. Youth was awarded with the International Globes Award for the best use of Event Marketing in the world. In 2006, Mr. Youth was ranked as the #16th agency in the nation by PROMO Magazine in its annual PROMO 100 rankings.

In 2007, Mr. Youth was named to the Inc. 500 list of the fastest growing private businesses in the United States.
