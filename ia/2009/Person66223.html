Dotan Saguy’s Summary

I'm a product guy at heart who can't work on anything without adding a healthy dose of out-of-the-box thinking and innovation.

My product work is informed by 15 years of experience online (that's right: since 1994) and the expertise I gained in many areas such as interface design & usability, online marketing, ecommerce, project management, viral media, etc.

That said, I'm a hands-on product person who likes to take ideas from the whiteboard to the browser. So while I enjoy 10,000-feet product strategy, I also love diving all the way down to rolling-up my sleeves and creating high-fidelity prototypes in Keynote or Powerpoint, testing them with users and working closely with smart developers to get them implemented successfully.
