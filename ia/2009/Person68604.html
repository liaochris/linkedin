Dwayne Nesmith’s Summary

Dwayne Nesmith has over 20 years of finance, marketing and consulting experience with high technology companies. He is the Chairman of the Board and a founder of ElectricApps, a leading Platform as a Service provider focused on non-technical business users needing to build custom rich web applications with little or no support from IT.

Dwayne was founder and CFO of Viant Corporation, a leading Internet consultancy that helped define the first generation of Internet applications in the 1990s by building digital businesses for firms such as Compaq, Charles Schwab, Merck, Raytheon, Radio Shack and Sony. He helped Viant grow into a 750-person company listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange with annual revenues of more than $125 million.

Dwayne also served as the CEO of Clear Technology where he grew the company's revenues 30% annually. Prior to his time at Viant and Clear, Dwayne worked at Oracle, Compuware, Uniface and as a senior consultant in the Oil and Gas Practice with Andersen Consulting (now Accenture).
