Robert Lavigne, PMP’s Summary

“Innovative, Reliable, Passionate”

@RLavigne42 routinely Encloses his Thoughts on Enterprise 2.0 via his Blog (http://rlavigne42.wordpress.com) and his Real Time Feed (http://twitter.com/rlavigne42).

Robert Lavigne, PMP, H.B. Comm. has acquired over 20 years of business-enablement via technological innovation. During these years, he has contributed to over 60 major projects and 15 commercialized products in both Startup and Enterprise organizations. He is passionate about growing businesses via the enablement of new technologies and innovative approaches.

Robert has headed numerous mission critical software development departments with multi-million dollar budgets. He has been accountable for systems responsible for the payment of millions of monthly employee payrolls. Systems under his care were responsible for calculating market-driving audience metrics for both the TV and Radio industry. He has also been responsible for products that have evolved from the “Paper Napkin” stage through to Tier-1 implementation at major Telecommunication Providers.

Robert has been often hired to radically improve the effectiveness and efficiency of dysfunctional departments. To achieve this, he uses a compliment of managerial facilitation skills with his diverse technical knowledge. Robert is a strong believer in the need for mutually respectful and trusted partnerships between business units and IT to meet these goals.

Robert is currently doing extensive research into the fields of Enterprise 2.0 and Web 3.0 to further revolutionize the delivery of corporate value. Robert strongly believes that the use of modern social networking and related tools can become a major force in improving business relationships and inter-departmental transparency. He subscribes to the need of further contextualization of accessible content to achieve real returns in the field of Competitive Intelligence and Business Innovation.
