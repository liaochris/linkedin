Sean McNeil’s Summary

Through AthletiCo Sports Medicine, I am currently the Head Athletic Trainer for the Moody Bible Institute. My primary role is to provide sports medicine services to all five varsity sports. I also developed and maintain the Sports Medicine website and provide complimentary injury screens to faculty, staff, and students.

Additionally, I volunteer with the US Soccer Federation as a Certified Athletic Trainer. Through the Federation I have worked with the U17, U20, and U21 women's national teams, the U18 men's national team, and the Futsal National team attending domestic and international training camps as well as two Nordic Cup tournaments, one Women's World Championship, and the 2007 Pan American Games. Most recently I assisted the U17 Women's National Team for their inaugural CONCACAF Championship tournament and their inaugural World Cup tournament.
