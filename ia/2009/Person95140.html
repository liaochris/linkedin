  • National Coordinator

    National Coalition of Survivors for Violence Prevention, Inc.

    (Civic & Social Organization industry)

    July 1997 — Present (11 years 9 months)

    Helen is the national coordinator of the Survivors for Violence Prevention,Inc.(SVP). During the last 11 years SVP and Harvard School of Public Health Professor, Deborah Prothrow-Stith, M. HTH will focus one track to support the efforts of providers who serve survivors of homicide victims; and the second tool kit would focus on the survivors themselves, and later will include victims of violence in general.
