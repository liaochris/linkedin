Martin Tate’s Summary

My contribution as a management consultant on capital projects is to get senior committees to make good decisions to time. I use formal decision-making techniques to do so. I have adapted a system selection & IT procurement method, created a tool for selecting tools and a method for building methods (this may make me the toolmaker for the knowledge economy).

As a management consultant, I have run workshops to evaluate strategies or locate a massive new factory and I have created a method for a VC to pick companies to invest in.
- See links: 'Decisions on capital projects'

My new joint venture, Defined Purpose, has workshops, a rigorous method and a web-based tool to help people at life crossroads answer the question... what shall I do with the rest of my life?
- See links: 'Decisions at life crossroads'

I turn my clients into sophisticated purchasers of IT who are attractive to vendors and buy systems that fit.
- As an IT consultant I have run 43 IT selections, interviewed more than 500 users and appraised over 750 systems.
- I have twice negotiated a system discount that was four times my fee, and once 11 times.
- On two projects, the winner was provably a better fit, but less than one-tenth the cost of the runner up.
- See links: 'Decisions on IT selections'
