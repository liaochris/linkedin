Katrina Gredona | I'm Here (tm) | LiON’s Summary

Katrina Gredoña is interested in making event planners' business lives easier. Having graduated in May 2009 with a Bachelor of Arts in a liberal arts program, she discovered her love for event planning and seeks to be the helpful assistant to event planners. As a person, Katrina is naturally warm in personality, calm, empathetic, and friendly. She is careful, detail-talented,attentive,patient, a great listener and an intelligent and diligent worker. Her other talents include Twitter for business and a friendly writing style that she plans to share through her new blog. She is a proud Event Peep and is here so that she could learn as much as possible from older, more experienced professionals and discover opportunities to help other event planners' business grow.

Katrina would love to help anyone and is available to assist event planners in the New York City/Northern NJ area that could use her skills.
