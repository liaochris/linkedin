Ralph Schroeder’s Summary

The AAE Group's professional focus is simple; we recruit exclusively for the Aftermarket and Specialty sectors of the Automotive Industry.

We specialize in the placement of management and executive level professionals whose skill sets include sales, marketing, engineering and operations. Their background and expectations reflect progressive responsibility and a passion for the aftermarket industry.

Our client's products are distributed through a variety of channels including the Traditional, Retail, Mass Merchant, Buying Group, Heavy Duty, OES and Tier level segments.

By targeting companies specific to this sector of the industry, we are better equipped to provide comprehensive job searches to our clients, locating only the most qualified candidates to meet their specific needs.

Our candidates are not lost among thousands of other users or unrelated job markets, and therefore receive individual attention specific only to experience developed within the automotive industry.

Because of this, we are better equipped to focus on the expectations and skills of each candidate, while ensuring their privacy, and guaranteeing important time and cost savings to both candidates and clients alike.

Most importantly, we understand how critical it can be for our client to identify and secure a candidate, possessing the requisite skills and experience, in a structured, yet expeditious manner.
