Eric Theise’s Summary

LAMP stack software engineer, formally trained in quantitative analysis and algorithmic efficiency, specializing in Internet applications since 1993. Happiest designing and coding in an environment that embraces agile, test-driven development. Former academic; published in refereed journals, and in mainstream books and magazines; early Internet entrepreneur and evangelist. Visual artist, have exhibited experimental films and antiquarian avant-garde photographs internationally; arts educator at colleges, centers, and institutes.

Seeking a position that enables me to combine my training in mathematical modeling with my programming and system administration skills in order to solve the problems—and capitalize on the opportunities—arising in networked society.
