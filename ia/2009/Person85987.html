Alesia Benedict’s Summary

Alesia Benedict's influence in the industry of resume writing is widely recognized. She is President of GetInterviews.com, one of the largest and most successful cover letter and resume writing firms for professionals and executives. She is a frequent public speaker, a widely published resume writer, and has served as a board member of the Professional Association of Resume Writers. Alesia Benedict's leadership position in her field is as visible as it is deserved. In an effort to help her peers improve their writing skills, Benedict served as Editor-in-Chief and developer of the resume writing industry's premier training manual, and wrote a whopping 60+ articles for the industry's major trade journal. Alesia Benedict has appeared on television programs, as a guest lecturer at colleges, community organizations, and meetings of government agencies, and is a valued resource for job seekers across the country.

A prominent figure in the career search field, Alesia Benedict took the forefront in today's online job search. A pioneer in electronic, scannable resumes and branding bios, Alesia Benedict and her team at GetInterviews.com is expertly conversant in all aspects online employment marketing.

GetInterviews.com was founded in 1994 and JIST Publications has called Alesia Benedict one of the "best resume writers in North America." She has also been named an expert in her field by media channels like the Chicago Tribune, The Wall Street Journal, and National Public Radio.
