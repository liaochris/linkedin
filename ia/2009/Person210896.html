Staffan Canback’s Summary

I've been a management consultant for more than 20 years and love every minute of it. To work on such a wide range of issues in many industries and be paid for the fun is truly a blessing. And to work with all the professional executives in client companies around the world on their most important problems is more than exhilarating.

My goal is to build the next generation management consulting firm based on much more rigorous analytical approaches than the traditional firms use. In practical terms, if a problem has not been analyzed statistically, the solution is probably meaningless. Not that analysis is valuable in isolation. My goal is to support executive judgment with robust analysis, to be "managerially relevant--analytically robust."

A love of travel and numbers, and having a creative mind all come to play to reach this goal.

Staffan Canback’s Specialties:

Understanding how large companies work and what makes them succeed or fail; understanding of marketing issues in emerging and other markets; understanding the mind of the executive and how to be helpful in creating their success
