    Sales2Grow it’s a Team of IT Sales people with 10+ experience. With Sales2Grow you can have an international presence at almost no risk.

    Establishing a presence in a new market is a time and money consuming process, with visible results only after 1 to 1,5 year, in average. That means companies must pay for office facilities, employees, taxes and other operational costs for a long time before they start getting results. Relying the presence in a new market only in partners with a low level of commitment toward the desired objective usually means to enrol a lot of companies as partners and develop a lot of work on following up for scarce results. Of course it's always possible to find "that" partner that will make very good results, but I believe a company cannot grow based on chance.

    In Sales2Grow we know the markets, we know the companies, we know the partners.     Sales2Grow is headquarter in UK and has offices in Portugal, Spain , German and Brazil.

    Our remuneration is based on commissions, or success fees, on top of effective sales. The only thing you need to pay is a small monthly fee to help support the infrastructure costs.


    The partnership process is based on defining quarter or year objectives, a sales prevision and monitoring that you can query via internet using a CRM system, and a contract that will establish how we work together. If you're not satisfied after the period we define in the contract, you're free to not renew it without further notice or explanations. No taxes, no legal issues, no compensations.
