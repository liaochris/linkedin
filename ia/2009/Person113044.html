    MyFitBrain (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Internet industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    Myfitbrain is a mental fitness internet application focused on the baby-boomer demographic utilizing a unique advertising business model that integrates products of its advertisers into the exercise itself. It is free to end users which is enabling it to quickly gain broad use. Myfitbrain offers advertisers an opportunity to have their consumers repeat their brand’s name or products’ name while playing exercises that are fun, stimulating, and beneficial to those consumers.

    The patent pending advertising model incorporates a company’s brand name or products (icons) directly into the playing of the mental exercise. During the course of play, users directly interact with the icons. While people perform the exercises, they repeatedly say the icon name. At the end of each game, an option for coupons or other sponsor promotions can be offered.
