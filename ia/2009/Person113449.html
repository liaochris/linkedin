Jim Stevenson’s Summary

Marketing executive with proven background of creativity, innovation and leadership in branding, advertising, public relations, interactive and marketing strategy and implementation. Deep experience on both the agency and client side in both business-to-business, and business-to-consumer from multiple and diverse disciplines including professional motorsports, not-for-profit, retail, commercial and residential real estate, health care, community mobilization, social marketing and collaborations.

Jim Stevenson’s Specialties:

Global brand product launches, brand identity, online marketing and interactive development, reputation management, public speaking and presentation development, business development, marketing training, creative writing, production supervision, video directing, budget development, staff supervision.

Jim Stevenson’s Experience

  • Co-founder, Marketing Consultant, Presenter and Writer

    International Center for Creativity

    (Think Tanks industry)

    October 2002 — Present (6 years 9 months)

    The International Center for Creativity is a creativity think tank and consultancy dedicated to serving the working marketing and design professional, entrepreneurs, and to inspiring and equipping young people for careers in creative fields like industrial, graphic, and interior and exterior space design.

  • Vice President, Marketing and Communications, Coldwell Banker Ohio NRT Companies

    Coldwell Banker Corporate

    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    October 2006 — March 2009 (2 years 6 months)

    Coldwell Banker is the largest and first national real estate brand in the world, and NRT is the largest residential real estate brokerage company in the nation. NRT posted more than $193 billion in closed sales volume in 2006.

    - Re-branded and reintroduced two local operating companies with fully-integrated, research-based marketing and interactive campaigns in two regions.
    - Launched local chapters of the Coldwell Banker Foundation.
    - Created, recruited and launched the Coldwell Banker Ohio NRT Companies’ Futuring and Innovation Center, a think tank to incubate new ideas.
    - Co-created a nationally-publicized New Home and Condos Division.
    - Co-created a first-of-its-kind partnership with the City of Columbus, local not-for-profit housing groups to represent and market bank-owned projects to rejuvenate neighborhoods.
    - Supervised staff of marketing directors, coordinators, graphic artists, and video coordinators. Budgets, plans and participates in executive-level decision making.

  • Vice President, Marketing and Communication

    American Cancer Society, Ohio Division

    (Non-Profit; 1001-5000 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    July 2003 — August 2006 (3 years 2 months)

    The ACS is the largest, not-for-profit voluntary health agency dedicated to cancer in the world with annual donations in excess of $850 million. With over 4,500 staff and 2.5 million volunteers nationwide, the brand ranks as one of the most recognized and trusted brands in the world, and is directly responsible for saving thousands of lives through investments in research, education, advocacy and patient services.

    - Leadership of all Ohio Division public relations and marketing.
    - Supervise the development of the Division’s annual report.
    - Ohio delegate for the concepting and deployment of a national intranet.
    - Represents Ohio on National Employer Initiative Leadership Group.
    - Delivered the first-of-its-kind, turnkey Corporate Relations program for the Ohio Division.
    - Exceeded presentation and implementation goals by delivering over double the amount of participating companies in the first year.
    - Frequent guest presenter for other Division and National trainings.

  • Vice President, Digital Marketing

    - Lead account manager for Borden Chemical, Worthington Industries, NiSource, United Way of Central Ohio, and Buckeye Power.

    Formula Marketing, LLC

The firm services such brands as Bob Evans, Mettler-Toledo, Scotts, Pinnacle Data Systems, Algorithm, Brown Motorsports/Ferrari Grand American Team, Guy Smith Racing and more.

  • Director of Marketing

    Tasman Motorsports Group/McDonald's Championship Racing
