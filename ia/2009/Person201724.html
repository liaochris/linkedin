  • Systems Development and Training Manager

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; TAP; Consumer Goods industry)

    November 2004 — March 2006 (1 year 5 months)

    Accountable for the availability, performance, maintenance and enhancement of all Coors web-based distributor tools. The tools include POINT (domestic beer ordering), InterPOINT (international beer ordering), MolsonPOINT (Molson beer ordering) and Reverse Logistics (web-based reverse order creation). Said accountabilities were handled through the management of a hybrid team consisting of Coors employees and outside resources in Coors as well as off shore. The position was created after feedback from customers that the web-based tools at Coors were no longer at the best in class level they had once enjoyed. During this time period, POINT was restored to a best in class level (based on distributor surveys) and the Reverse Logistics tool was created from the ground up. Latest surveys indicate the new RL tool is quickly headed for best in class as well. The function included responsibility for training of external and internal customers on the use of the tools.
