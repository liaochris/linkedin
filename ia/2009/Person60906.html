Deepak Shenoy’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur with experience in setting up, managing and growing a technology company. I've been involved in literally every aspect of company growth, from balance sheet analysis to figuring out which coffee machine was best for us.

I have founded Moneyoga.com, a startup focussing on stock market investors. Apart from a community for collaboration and action, Moneyoga will provide investors with analytics, tools and historically tested investment strategies. We mine the market for historically validated patterns that quantify and reduce the risk in investments. Specifically focussed on Indian investors, it brings together both my passion for the stock market and my skills in programming and management.

(I am, at heart, a programmer. I can write code in Delphi, PHP, C# and know a bit about databases.)

My personal interests are in stock market analytics. There's a tremendous amount of intelligence that can be mined from stock market data - and I like to write about it at my blog. Oh, and I like to write.

There's more about me at my personal web site: http://www.deepakshenoy.com.
