Morgan Meador’s Summary

Social media and online marketing is the future of business; it's not going anywhere anytime soon. With this in mind, since June of 2009, I have been working to enhance the brand perception and outreach of Meador Staffing and Career Consultants Staffing Services. I oversee the in-house social media programs for both companies, which focuses on equipping and engaging our audience with knowledge and valuable information to help them succeed. My efforts have been dedicated to successfully developing an online presence on various channels for both companies, as well as a strategy and social media policy.

I am also experienced in the tactics and techniques involved with search engine optimization. As Director of Communications/Social Media Manager, I am eager to constantly and consistently work to stay ahead of the game with latest social media/networking technology.

A few other hats I wear within Meador Staffing include managing the company's website, writing articles and press releases, as well as editing company contracts, proposals, and other documents.

In addition to managing the online campaigns for both staffing companies, I also oversee and manage the social media campaign for McDonald's Texas Invitational Basketball Tournament that takes place in Pasadena, TX. The tournament is every November 19-21. In its seventh year, 80 of the state's most talented high school boys and girls basketball teams will compete for the first place trophy. Visit the website at www.texasinvitational.com.

My strengths include understanding, evaluating, and implementing social media and its tools, opening up and fostering communication lines to connect people across disciplines and interests; evaluating data relating to website traffic, and exceptional communication skills, both verbal and writing.
