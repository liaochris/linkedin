Tanya Hay Ziegler’s Summary

Want to define and positively engage in your Personal Development Plan for your life? Are you looking for a more holistic set of ideas to living a fulfilling life? When was the last time you had someone to work with to develop your Personal Development Plan?

If these questions seem confusing or unclear to you – consider it this way! Have you ever noticed that corporations and educational facilities have programs in place to help you define your career plan to ensure that it fits your needs and desires as well as theirs? And – how often does the “treatment” of an issue or ailment you have get attention and a “fix” without considering the rest of the human body, mind and spirit that is involved?

THAT’S where I come into your life!!

My goal, as a coach, is to work with my clients/partners to find and engage within them the healthy living, passions, accountability and motion to make their time on earth as healthy and enjoyable as possible and in the process – making their dreams become REALITY!

Check it all out and connect with me if you are interested in becoming part of a holistic motion to living a fulfilling life!
