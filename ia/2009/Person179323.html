Organic Gurus’s Summary

With combined experience of over 30 years practicing an organic lifestyle, we’ve tried, tested and researched hundreds of organic food brands and eco-friendly products. We can enable you to make healthy choices for you, your family, your business and the planet.

Our consultants will tell you what groceries to toss and suggest healthy choices as replacements. Let us help you makeover your food pantry and cleaning closet.

Organic Gurus also offers consulting services / audits to restaurants, caterers, hotels, spas and fitness centers enabling them to offer organic dishes, select eco-friendly products, and go green.

We are independent consultants and are not affiliated with any food/product company.
