Isabelle O'Kane’s Education

  • Dublin Institute of Technology

    MSc , Advertising , 2003 — 2004

    While in semester one I focused on Marketing theory, Advertising strategy and Consumer behaviour and Marketing Communications Theory amongst other subjects, I elected to specialize as a creative for the remainder of my Masters and focused on copy writing and art direction. The year culminated in a pitch where we formed a full advertising agency and my role for this was Creative Director.

    My thesis title was "Towards evidencing the effectiveness of creativity in advertising as determined by "likeability" and "recall"."

  • Dublin City University

    BA , Communications Studies , 1999 — 2002

    A fabulous degree which spanned social psychology, media studies, film studies, linguistics, IT, etc...as well as practical media studies (Video, Radio, Photography).

    I specialized for years 2 and 3 in Photography and Digital Imaging and my thesis was a series of digital images communicating my ideas on the "Changing face of Dublin" with a view particularly on the sudden and rapid multiculturalisation of the city.
