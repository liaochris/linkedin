Brian Spencer’s Summary

I have gained many years experience within the computer industry in positions ranging from Programmer to Software Support Manager, predominantly within a Unisys 1100 / 2200 environment.

My skills include Unisys Assembler (MASM / ASM), COBOL (UCOB / ACOB / COB, DMS1100, QLP, HVTIP, FORTRAN (FTN / FOR), PDF, IPF, ED and SSG.

I have just completed a contract to provide high quality analysis, program development, testing and support for a real time Computer Assisted Despatch system deployed within London.

I have provided analysis, development and testing expertise for several major UK High Street banking organisations in addition to being the Project Leader of a software support team for a major Investment Bank.

Other responsibilities have included installation and support of OS1100 software, computer security, capacity planning, performance analysis and acceptance testing. I have complimented my computer and personal skills with a two year period designing and conducting technical training courses.
