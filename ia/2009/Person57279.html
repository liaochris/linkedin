David Duncan’s Summary

I currently own and operate a WSI Internet Consultancy franchise,and specialize in providing a full suite of internet services for the SME sector, from design, develop, host, maintain,to ongoing education and marketing support. We help our clients to use the internet to market their businesses online. So we specailse in the marketing aspects like SEO or search engine optimisation, we manage pay per click, and email marketing campaigns for our clients. We also provide web analytics services to help out clients really understand what is happening behind their web site and assist them to make changes to it that will result in greater online success.
I am a successful operational manager in the Information and Communications Technology business, with international experience in the Canadian, US, as well as the UK marketplaces. I have a sound track record of business improvements and have always been involved with the customer directly in one form or another. I am an energetic, organised and practical manager who enjoys seeing plans come to fruition.
