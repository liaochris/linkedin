Laurent Pontier’s Summary

Laurent works in SAP BusinessObjects Innovation Center in Paris, France.
Prior to SAP Business Objects, Laurent was senior software engineer and project manager at 4D SA and worked during 6 years on a Rapid Application Development software suite. Before joining 4D SA in 2000, Laurent spent two years in Menlo Park and Redwood City in the silicon valley working on the award winning ePicture at Beatware Inc, now part of Oracle.
