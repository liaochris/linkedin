Tanya Johnston’s Summary

As an independent graphic designer with nearly 20 years of experience in corporate, editorial and cultural design, I deliver positive and memorable communication solutions grounded by a commitment to ideas and design.

I have now expanded my passion for visual communication to art and illustration, thereby granting me more freedom to explore and create work that go beyond established boundaries.

I have recently accepted an invitation by Getty Images, a world leader in digital media, to become a contributing artist. A wide range of customers around the globe can now license a selection of my illustrations for commercial use.

Artist Statement
Through pictorial and graphic processes, I explore the realms of reality and illusion as reality.

I frequently combine the techniques of drawing, painting and digital imaging to illustrate and communicate a non-linear vision of thought and space.

I access principles of aesthetics and design to harmoniously represent the convergences that exist between art, culture and science.
