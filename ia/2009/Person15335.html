Andy Nicholson’s Summary

Andy Nicholson is a freelance Supervising Art Director with 15 years experience working predominantly on American Studio pictures.

He has been fortunate enough to collaborate with top Motion Picture Production Designers and Directors on projects as diverse as Band of Brothers, The Mummy, Sleepy Hollow, Spy Game, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, The Bourne Ultimatum & Rocknrolla.

He has just completed work on The Wolfman and Tim Burton’s Alice in Wonderland, both to be released in early 2010.

Andy Nicholson’s Specialties:

With an informed working knowledge of AutoCad, Revit, Photoshop, VectorWorks, Cinema 4D, Solidworks, Rhino & Modo. Andy continually pushes the use of 3D modelling and CAD software within Film Art Departments, actively seeking to assimilate digital technology with traditional workflows and techniques. Always on the look out for staff with new skills he is an advocate of immersive design and closely follows the opportunities within the Industry that increasing software interchange can present.
