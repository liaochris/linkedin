Martin Andelman’s Summary

My career has been spent as a writer, and marketing communications strategist focusing in complex subject matter including health care, financial services, insurance, accounting, public policy and law. I've authored hundreds of papers and articles on a wide range of topics from executive compensation to the impact of digital video technology on the political process. I've also written two books: "RFP NATION-How to Succeed in our Proposal Driven World," and "The Simplified Guide to Nonqualified Deferred Compensation". I've also produced and directed dozens of "documercials" which are digital videos that are designed in documentary style to change opinions or correct misperceptions.
My experience in writing for the financial services industry has led me to working with companies on the design and funding or various executive benefit plans.
My goals are to become more involved in our political process and work for a campaign or issue in 2010-2012.

Martin Andelman’s Specialties:
