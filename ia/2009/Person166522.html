Michele Weston’s Summary

Most American women, 62% of us in fact, just don’t fit the Size 0-12 fashion mold. Since most media doesn’t account for the reality of women’s bodies, there is a need for expert advice, delivered without bias.

Michele Weston is one of America’s leading plus-size authoritie and has a distinguished record of changing perceptions of inner beauty and personal outer style. Her career has been spent in the fashion/magazine publishing worlds as an editor for the past 17 years and expert for such: Conde Nast, Hearst and Hachette Publications. She held theposition of Founding Fashion and Style Director of MODE Magazine and author of the motivational style book: "Learning Curves: Living Your Life in Full and With Style" (Random House). Michele was recently "Proclaimed by 'O The Oprah Magazine' as the Plus Size Fashion Authority." in the Experts Issue (November 2006.)

Ms. Weston is a commentator, speaker and panelist for conferences and community events as a CurvyStyle Therapist. She has appeared on ABC/The Oprah Winfrey show, NBC/The Today Show, ABC/The View, the CBS Early Show, Good Morning America, E! Fashion Emergency 911, Lifetime Television and Oxygen Networks nationally – among many other affiliates.

Selling Style, Inc.is a company (a division of MJWStyle Media) that has worked with a 'who’s-who' in fashion/retail include I.e. Saks Fifth Avenue Group, INC, Avenue/United Retail Group, Old Navy/Plus, Marina Rinaldi, Lafayette 148, Calvin Klein Sportswear, Avenue Stores, Macy's Group, Bloomingdale’s,Richard Metzger and the EvansLondon.com launch into the American market in 2007. Michele's empowering store and event commentary and workshops have been presented in hundreds of major retail outlets.

Weston is a member of Fashion Group International since 1990 and an associate member of AICI (Association of Image Consultants International) since 2007.

Michele Weston’s Specialties:

MJWStyle Media and it's division, SellingStyle, Inc., opened in 2003 and develop partnerships with retail to educate sales staff and consumers regarding selling style, producing brand awareness, create media opportunities, launching new divisions of companies online into the USA, strategic marketing, creating and commentating fashion shows with personal consultations. Developing ideas and newsletters created for fashion and retail companies for re-positioning their brand.
