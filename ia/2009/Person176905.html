  • Ambulatory advanced technologies beta site

    Calgary Heatlh Region

    (Government Agency; 10,001 or more employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2002 — Present (7 years 5 months)

    Our clinic has been a beta site for several of the Calgary Health Region's (CHR) EHR initiative. Another regional project that extends province-wide (EHR) is being the test site for the Provincial Health Information Exchange's Chronic Disease Management Dashboard viewer. We are currently proving 2 way messaging capability from our in clinic EMR to the EHR.
