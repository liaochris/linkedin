Brian Firth’s Summary

I've been in the Internet design and marketing business for 10 plus years.

I'm very interested in leveraging all the new features of the web for personnel and professional growth.

I feel like I have a pretty good understanding of most aspects of "web 2.0" and can easily help a client achieve their goals online.

However, the landscape is so rapidly changing you constantly have to stay on top of things.

I'm working on a concept that I think will help a lot of people absorb and utilize a lot of valuable information in that arena. The bootcamp part of www.FluidArmy.com is the beginning.

What's going to make the idea come to life is that every topic is designed to create a discussion. I believe the best way to find the best solutions is healthy debate.

That and quality, regular blogs/podcasts and vloggs. is what's gonna make it fly.... I'm betting on video and would like to have every single page on the site available in video format for the fast growing segment that would rather watch and listen to information than read it..

I'm very interested in hearing from anyone that is on the same page. Especially anyone that has experience building any type of Internet community.

Cheers, -Brian
