Steve Biggs’s Summary

Steve Biggs,
- Founder, Managing Director of biggSolutions Ltd
- President & CEO of biggSolutions, Inc.

steve@biggSolutions.com -- www.biggSolutions.com

Steve initiated his progressive career in Information Technology, Consulting & Professional Services in 1984. Since 1992 Steve has been instrumental in managing & developing Global IT Recruitment Consulting businesses from initial start-ups, both in the UK & US.

Steve's International experience started with the expansion of the US operations for Elan Computing (Manpower) in 1996 & successfully built two additional US Technology Recruitment Consulting companies to $10.5M & $25M respectively. Entrepreneurial by nature, Steve created Elan Communications (Niche Network & Communications Recruitment Company in London) in 1993, instigated Elan International in the US in 1996, TechPartners International (Harvey Nash) in the US in 1998 and Founder / CEO of biggSolutions since 2002.

Steve Biggs’s Specialties:

Recruitment Solutions, Telecommunications, Information Technology, Enterprise Software Sales, Business Development, Bid Managers, Solutions Architect, Managed Services, Outsourcing, RPO, RTO, HRO, Onsite, On-site, Account Management and Recruitment.
