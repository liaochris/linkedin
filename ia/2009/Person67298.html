Dr Henry Hornstein’s Summary

I am an Organizational Psychologist with more than 27 years of experience in change leadership/management, organization design/development, team design/development, and facilitation. I am an adjunct professor of business in Norwich University's MBA, and also have taught in their Masters of Science in Organizational Leadership program. Additionally, I have been faculty in the Department of Applied Human Sciences, Concordia University, and continue to act as a field supervisor for students in their Masters of Human System Intervention program. I have worked with all levels of the organization from the shop floor (union and non-union) to the executive office in team and non-team environments. My clients and work experience have included companies such as AT&T, Avaya Communication, Bell Canada, Esso Petroleum Canada, Lucent, Levi Strauss & Co., Ltd., Merck, Oracle Corporation, and the Canadian Government. I have Secret (Level 2) clearance.

My approach focuses on helping develop an organization in such a way that it learns to develop competency. This means that I concentrate on ensuring that clients always are involved in changes that impact them. In addition, I have had experience organizing and facilitating large group events in which employees come together to mold their futures. I believe that I would provide the skills and experience that would be valuable for your organization as it engages in any organization design/redesign and/or change projects. I am open to travel.

While I am not currently seeking permanent employment, I will examine all worthwhile opportunities. Also, I am interested in sub-contracting should these opportunities be available.
