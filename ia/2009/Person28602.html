Bill Korbe’s Summary

High Tech product management professional with solid industry experience in startups, mid-sized and large company environments. Proven track record - transforming technology into business opportunity.

Career Objective includes roles in High Tech Product Management, Partner Management, Product Planning

Telecommunications Industry Expert in VoIP and legacy technology deployments, in both enterprise networks and hosted service provider networks

Demonstrated track record in the full range of product management activities including: Product Roadmap Ownership; Customer, Market and Product Requirements; Product Definition and Strategy; Leveraging 3rd Party Technologies; Partner Management; Business impacts of Technology Analysis; Business Case (NPV, ROI, PBP); Technology Trends Impact Analysis; Competitive Analysis; Proposals, Bids, Customer Engagements; Directing Engineering, Product Marketing, and Sales Priorities; and Advising Senior Management
