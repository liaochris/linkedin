Nicholas Webb’s Summary

As a Certified Management Consultant I combine my management expertise with my own successful inventing experience to assist my clients in accessing emerging technologies and to help them move technologies more quickly through the new product development process and ultimately to commercialization.I have spent the last 20 years in the development and commercialization of cutting-edge technologies. I have been awarded over 35 patents by the US patent and trademark office for technologies ranging from one of the world's smallest micro-silicone implants to computer-controlled cooling systems for protective headgear.
