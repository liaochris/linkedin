Gary W. Druckenmiller, Jr.’s Summary

Strategic and versatile marketer with 14 years experience including executive leadership positions with major global brands. Passionate about interactive marketing, branding, marketing strategy, and innovation. Proven ability to lead enterprise-wide initiatives and cross-functional teams, and achieve positive revenue results in B2C and B2B markets.

Over 12 years of dedicated internet and web experience with large global brands such as Sylvania, Swiss Army, and Timex. I have led or played a major role in several enterprise back/front-end technical launches and projects under categories such as CRM, social networking/media, consumer websites, intranets, and extranets.

I'm a brand builder at heart and love the challenge of building (and rebuilding) great brands. I've served in several functional capacities: Marketing, Interactive, Business Development, and Sales. I'm a proven and commanding business speaker and motivational team leader.
