Srikanth Velamakanni’s Summary

Srikanth Velamakanni
Chief Executive Officer, Fractal Analytics

Srikanth Velamakanni co-founded Fractal in February 2000 and is the Chief Executive Officer. Srikanth is responsible for growing Fractal’s global presence in the areas of Analytics and Predictive sciences. Srikanth is zealous about Fractal’s vision of being the most respected player in the field of analytics globally and making fundamental contributions to this field.

At Fractal, Srikanth has consulted with firms such as P&G, Citibank, HDFC Bank and SAP in the areas of predictive analytics, risk management and marketing effectiveness.

Prior to Fractal, Srikanth worked with ANZ Investment Bank and ICICI. At ANZ, Srikanth worked with the Global Structured Finance team on several structured debt transactions including IDBI and Haldia Petrochemicals Ltd. At ICICI’s Structured Products Group, Srikanth was part of the team which launched India’s first Collateralized Bond Obligation.

Srikanth is passionate about modern and contemporary fiction and evolution in management thinking. Srikanth loves listening to Audiobooks and is a gadget and technology buff.

Srikanth holds an MBA degree from Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad and a bachelors’ degree in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi.

Srikanth Velamakanni’s Specialties:

Risk Analytics, Marketing Analytics, Customer Acquisition, Cross Sell, Retention, Response Models, Customer Lifetime Value, Application Scorecards, Behavioral Scorecard, Collection Scorecards, Attrition Models, etc.
