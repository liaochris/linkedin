Abbas Zaidi’s Summary

I have always been a problem solver, actively finding or innovating solutions, which have resulted in numerous accolades for client situations improved.

A more recent background in Technical Writing & Training Development has enabled me to enrich a new breed of clients with a rare combination of converging skills honed in years of Management Consulting. (Primarily in multi-tier environments, roles ranging from app dev to configuring app servers to DB & OS server mgmt.)

8+ years as a solutions architect (meaning great communication ability,) saw me always placed at the front of market-development efforts. I am great at mentoring resources & Relationship Management, and have always been put in challenging situations, where I identify breakdowns and start with relationships in order to overcome issues.

My diverse background is my greatest strength: 5 years of experience in FrameMaker-based single-sourced documentation, & Oracle-based app servers for which I've had to maintain my own environments & play the DBA role. 2+ years of experience in NovaSoft (NovaManage) Workflow & EDMS, & Vignette StoryServer (V/5), & 1+ year with ColdFusion (mostly for RAD), and XDB (predecessor to IBM's UDB,) for a massige transaction processing application for which I created the robust front-end & UI design. Add to that a broad sprinkling of SaaS & other applications suites, and one can see how I can handle any set of roles.

I have taught and effectively managed every conceivable audience. My favorite specific role to date has been BPE as an Info. Mgmt Consultant on Document Mgmt and Workflow Mgmt Systems. e.g., SpyderWeb (by BTG at the GOMO, Pentagon,) and NovaSoft (worldwide) through the later 90's which enabled me to gain an advance perspective of the field. Applying lessons from strategic vision is a strength which I use to examine an industry from a variety of perspectives. I develop relationships & clearly declare helpful intentions up front, always welcoming feedback.

Abbas Zaidi’s Specialties:

Innovation and Architecture in any realm I am introduced to. Technical Writing (Self-Paced Training/ Documentation/ Courseware Development.) Training Delivery for all levels of audiences. Executive Support and Relationship Management. Also skilled in (Strategic) Business Transformation and (Tactical) Business Process Engineering, using creative applications of existing technologies.
