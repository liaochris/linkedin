Gina Snyder’s Summary

At PR Newswire, I deal with private companies, small businesses and small-cap public companies in the Central US. I counsel prospects on domestic, international and multimedia communication resources, help plan strategic public relations and marketing efforts, share industry knowledge and win new business.

Once upon a time, I was a news addict -- reading, writing, shooting, producing and hosting entertainment news. I now write a little blog on the day-to-day life of being a young Midwestern suburbanista.

I'm also a seasoned performer with a strong background in musical theater, comedy, vocal performance and various forms of dance. I still sing jazz with big bands and legendary pianists in Chicago from time to time, and I'm also a principal dancer for Dance du Soleil, a professional bellydance troupe in Northeast Ohio. Currently, I'm working on a one-night-only jazz show in Akron. I'm also the lead singer for the pop duo, Cool It With The Boom Booms.
