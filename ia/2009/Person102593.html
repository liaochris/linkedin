James Hutto’s Summary

I am an expert in Online Marketing as well as Information Technology who helps organizations understand and utilize the various tools and strategies that create a successful web presence. By offering results-oriented solutions that increase brand visibility, drive qualified web traffic, and increase conversions & sales; I have helped many organizations realize top-line growth through effectively marketing on the Internet. I have over 8 years of experience in the industry and am the owner of my second successful web development firm.

I am no stranger to matching business objectives with the strategies and technologies for success. With a background in Information Technology and a focus on infrastructure engineering and systems integration, my experience with large organizations such as Williams-Sonoma centered on high-visibility implementations that had to come together on time, within budget, and with zero downtime.

I am also an energetic presenter who speaks about how the many facets of online marketing come together to deliver a solid return on investment. I am available for speaking engagements and panels, as well as workshops on a variety of Internet-related topics.

I look forward to connecting with you!
