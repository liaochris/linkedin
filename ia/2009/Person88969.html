Carlos Granier-Phelps’s Summary

I'm available for consulting, advising and partnerships on new media and social networking projects or start-ups. Available for speaking opportunities at conferences and company events.

--

Carlos Granier-Phelps is the founder of RED 66, a firm specializing in social networking and digital media strategies. Carlos got his start in the industry in Venezuela where he founded the start-up PorMiCuenta.com, an online marketplace for freelancers and project managers, and later developed and implemented AudienceMeter, a ratings analysis system used by leading television networks.

Carlos currently acts as an advisor to TubeMogul.com, an online video analytics and distribution company, and collaborates with Technosailor.com, providing much-needed social-networking commentary in Spanish.

Carlos is also a licensed Mediation and Arbitration specialist, accredited by the Universidad Catolica Andres Bello, having collaborated with the OAS (Organization of American States) and William Ury. Carlos is a graduate of Tufts University.

--

Digital Media Strategist with substantial experience in integrating digital media offerings and customer generated content. Start-up experience. Comprehensive knowledge of latest technologies and trends and how they affect your business. Interested in startups as well as established companies wanting to understand and take advantage of digital media and social networks.
