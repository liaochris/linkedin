Joe Sokolic’s Summary

Six years in commercial property broking in Cape Town prior to 1997. I then spent the next 4 and a half years doing key accounts in the media industry in Europe. I have worked for and with large multinational companies and have always excelled. I own various online properties some of which are still in start up phase as well as a property brokerage and am a partner in a property investment company.

Joe Sokolic’s Specialties:

I have been active in the Sales industry for over 13 years. I have spent 4 and a half years of this time doing key accounts sales in Europe to large multinational companies. I have an in depth knowledge of both the Property and Internet Industries both in SA and abroad.
My latest venture is www.residential-property.co.za which is a sister site to www.commercial-property.co.za, an online search facility to find commercial property for sale or to rent in and around South Africa.
