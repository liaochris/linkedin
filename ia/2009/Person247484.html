Bill Hansing’s Summary

The Financial Partners Story

Bill Hansing's story is one of the financial services industry's most varied and interesting. In a career that extends back to the mid-Seventies in his native southern Indiana, this principled unconformist has pursued an often winding course across the financial services landscape.

Many Diverse Paths

He has moved from personal production in the 70's to management positions throughout the 80's. He worked developing investment management programs within financial institutions in the '90's. Now he is founder, owner and President of his firm, Financial Partners. All the while, he has remained true to his own vision.

His success in developing leading investment management programs within financial institutions helped define the genre. His time as a manager within the more traditional career distribution system received both company awards and industry recognition.

It is typical of Hansing that he followed many of his most recognized achievements with some of his most unpredictable changes. He has avoided remaining in one structure or distribution system. Rather, the unifying factors throughout Hansing’s career have been his passionate belief in his own entrepreneurial spirit, his coaching skill, his faith and the personal relationships developed with producers over more than 30 years.

Developing a Unique Process

Throughout his “sometimes bumpy ride,” Hansing has consistently demonstrated the unbridled passion of a financial services veteran who understands that self-renewal is the only way to avoid becoming a commodity. In an industry that has long been product-focused, his unique process has enabled him to remain a most significant manager and producer over time that now spans four decades.

Since 2003, over sixty fully-affiliated producers and brokers throughout the midwest have chosen to work in association with Hansing and his team at Financial Partners.

Bill Hansing’s Specialties:

Personal coaching, working within a financial institution, practice management, business continuation, branding within various business models and distribution systems in the financial services industry.
