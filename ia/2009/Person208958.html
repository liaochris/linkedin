Sangram Parelkar, Ph.D.’s Education

  • University of Massachusetts at Amherst

    PhD , Molecular and Cell Biology , 2001 — 2007

    Thesis Title: The ubiquitin E3 ligase Human Homolog of Drosophila Ariadne-1 (HHARI) is a structural and functional homolog of Parkin and is required for myogenesis.

    • Identified HHARI as a novel protein therapeutic molecule that can prevent the death of dopaminergic neurons in Parkinson’s disease patients. This research challenges former findings and alters the way most scientists investigate neuronal cell death in Parkinson’s disease.

    • Investigated the molecular mechanisms of skeletal muscle differentiation program and showed that HHARI protein is a crucial regulator of the same.

    • In depth research on apoptosis, aggresomes, neurodegeneration, muscle transplantation techniques and free radical generation with associated stress.
