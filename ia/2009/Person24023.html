Barbara Kincaid’s Summary

Barbara Kincaid is an experienced speaker and consultant for Personal and Corporate Branding. She has worked with many individuals as well as many small to major companies across the US. Her training comes from Idaho State University, where she majored in Marketing and Merchandising. Her experience comes from John Robert Powers of San Diego, Dale Carnegie, numerous major Hotel Chains, The Macon Telegraph and Yellow Book USA helping clients and employers to identify and brand a consistent message.

Currently she owns the Illinois branch of CardPayment Solutions. She works with small to medium businesses to help them grow revenues and decrease costs when accepting Bank Cards through the Card Swipe System, Wireless Solutions, Virtual Terminals, E-Commerce, Direct Withdrawal Payment programs and New Business Applications.
