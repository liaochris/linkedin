George Damian’s Summary

I am a self-employed Web Developer, with more then 8 years of experience in the WEB industry. I am specialized in building usable, functional and good looking websites.

I still struggle between the designer and the developer side of my work, because I like that I can make an idea turn into a functional website. The creative side of my work allows me to define the structure, determine the exact functionality and the "look&feel" of the website. My developer skills allow me to take this further on and to effectively make the website "run": to turn a design into a functional HTML+CSS website with advanced features based on web-programming.

I am able to manage a project from start to end (Software / Information Architecture): prepare specifications, define requirements and dependencies, create the design and implement a functional website. In my 8+years of work I was involved in the implementation of wide variety of websites, from simple presentation ones, to e-commerce sites, complex web portals or custom Web Intranet.

Between 2004-2009 I have coordinated and implemented with my team at Blue Soft Romania (www.blue-soft.com) more then 300 web related projects.

From the projects I have done, the 5 that I am most proud of are :
- www.e-timisoara.info (ideea, concept, design, implementation management)
- www.cadouri-mioritix.ro (full)
- www.blue-soft.com (concept, branding, design)
- www.cjcs.ro (concept, planning, design, implementation management)
- www.worldclass.ro (concept, design, implementation)
