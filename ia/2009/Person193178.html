Bill Jackson’s Summary

I am a Sales Representative for Coldwell Banker Town & Country Brokerage, independantly owned and operated, and work full time out of our head office in Orillia. Apart from my schooling at Lakehead University, where I attained a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and a Bachelor of Science in Natural Science, and Seneca College for Human Resources Management (co-op), I am a life-long resident of the Orillia area and now reside on Lake Simcoe at Bonnie Beach in Ramara (about 15 minutes east of Downtown Orillia.) My keen interest and background in environmental studies will be a great asset to those interested in lakefront, farms and rural properties. I was born and raised in Orillia and became interested in Real Estate through family. When I can find time for myself, I enjoy playing golf ,tennis and curling. I am very ambitious and look forward to assisting you in all your Real Estate needs.
