Chris Deary’s Summary

Online community manager, journalist and blogger with four years experience working with a wide variety of subject matters and audiences in the publishing industry. As well as writing and journalism skills, I have expertise in social media and online communities, with a track record of increasing user generated content and driving traffic to websites.
