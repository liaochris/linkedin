Shell Brodnax’s Summary

Shell brings 20 years of outstanding sales, marketing and team building skills to the Real Estate Staging Association (RESA). In 1994 Shell received her Private Investigators License in the State of California and created an Asset Recovery and Skip Tracing Company that serviced Subrogation Processes in the Rental car damage Claims industry.

After several years of successful Investigations Shell went on to develop a Skip Tracing and Collections department for one of the top, Sub-Prime Auto Lenders in the US. Shell managed a team of 22 agents and was nominated for manager of the year after only 8 months of service. Shell led her team to develop a new delinquency level for a test project and the project was a success and is still in existence today. Her team set company records that topped the companies 50 year history.

In 2003 Shell came to the staging industry and has played a pivotal role in setting the industry standings in staging. Shell served as the manager of the corporate headquarters, Director of Marketing and Director of National Corporate Accounts for a staging training company.

Over the years it became obvious that Stagers needed marketing support. In 2006
Shell developed the Staging Career Center. The Staging Career Center provides custom marketing support services to Stagers.

In 2007 Shell developed the Real Estate Staging Association™ (RESA) in order to
support professional Real Estate Stagers by setting REAL measurable standards,
and creating UNITY among Stagers. RESA is focused and ready to take the industry to the next level. RESA will allow Stagers from the United States and Canada to form local chapters in order to establish peer to peer support and form standards in the Staging industry.
