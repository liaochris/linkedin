Joseph Seboek’s Summary

15 Years Recruiting and Human Resources
10 Years Information Technology Consulting

Strategic staffing is the key to increasing productivity, reducing costs and increasing revenues to meet your business objectives.

Count the cost!

What does it cost your company to NOT have the right person for the role or project? Your customers, your boss and your shareholders, all want results. Do current employees have the expertise and the “bandwidth” to pick up the slack? Sometimes they do, but what about when they don't? Where will you quickly be able to find the “exceptional” talent to complete your projects and meet your mission critical objectives?

It Takes Great Employees to Grow a Great Company.
What attributes make a great employee?

How about: integrity, reliability, a consistent worth ethic, great communication, technical skills, independent thinking, the ability to be a productive member of a team, the ability to be coached, leadership qualities and industry expertise?

In my consulting practice and corporate career I have made it my purpose to find out what is important to my clients and internal managers. We collaborate on the best solutions, identifying “better than qualified” candidates.
