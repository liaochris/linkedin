Chris Forbes’s Summary

I am the MD of Ph.D. Search & Selection, I have worked for PhD for the past 12 years placing senior hires for world class global organisations in London, New York, Hong Kong and Tokyo. I have a deep knowledge of financial markets products, front to back office business processes, IT architecture, project management and outsourcing.

We work at VP/Associate Director to global head level. Most of our candidates are happy in their current roles, but we have the opportunities to make them happier. I believe honesty and integrity are the most important part of our business. Our market knowledge provides a consultative approach to search which has produced exceptional results for both our clients and candidates.

As part of our search service we are also now offering career advisory for senior executivces affected by the credit crunch.
