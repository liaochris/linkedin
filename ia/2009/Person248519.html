David Worrell’s Summary

An accomplished entrepreneur, analyst, and author, David Worrell specializes in helping small businesses grow rapidly to the next level by employing powerful financial and business development strategies.

Worrell is a graduate of The Ohio State University, the Sandler Sales Institute, and the E. M. Kauffman Foundation's FastTrack Entrepreneur Training program. He was twice recognized by DeLoitte & Touche as a "Fast 50" award recipient.

He has taught international business planning within the top-10 ranked MBA program at the Fisher Graduate School of International Business, in Monterey, California, and from 2002-2009 was a regular contributor to Entrepreneur Magazine on all aspects of business finance, acquisitions and exit strategies.

Worrell has run three successful companies of his own. The first, InfoQuest! International, Inc., is a telecom ASP and e-marketing company that he sold in 1999 to a Silicon Valley competitor. Subsequently, Worrell founded Monterey Venture Partners, LLC (MVP), a 5-member venture consultancy in Monterey California. MVP helped dozens of entrepreneurs design, build and finance their companies. Currently, Worrell is partner in his third venture, AmeriStart (www.ameristart.com).

David Worrell’s Specialties:

Quickly ramping revenue growth and profits by implementing market-dominating strategies and financial controls at fast-growing or emerging companies. Builds strong relationships with Angel Investors, VC, banks. Provides a roadmap to success through Financial forecasting, valuations and strategic, financial business planning.
