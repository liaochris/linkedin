Gaile Gray’s Summary

I won a writing competition at age 10, was a published editor-in-chief at 17, and managed a team of a dozen writers in my early 20s. A board gamer since toddlerhood, a computer gamer since the late 80s, my first game review was published in 1998 and many followed. My expertise in games has been sought and quoted by major publications such as The New York Times and The Wall Street Journal, and I’ve been interviewed by more gaming magazines and websites than I can count.

My passion is building, nurturing, and supporting online communities: creating a long-range community plan; developing website content; conceiving and hosting live and in-game events; resolving issues for individual players or the community as a whole; and providing day-to-day player support via wikis, forums, live chat, and social networks. I relish addressing the challenges of finding better ways to reach out to people and to bring them together.
