Elaine DeBassige D'Amato’s Summary

Licensed Professional Counselor specializing in life and sport transitions, separation of role performance and identity development, strengths and values-based decision making, and leadership development.

Great communication to create healthy relationships. Providing free 8-hour workshops in Colorado to promote healthy relationships for couples, singles, teens, and youth.

Interested in working with elite and professional athletes in transition, academic work, public speaking, training, change management, advising corporations on communication and strengths identification in employees and teams.

Elaine DeBassige D'Amato’s Specialties:

Transitions: sport and performance psychology, leaving sport for civilian living--either by force (injury/not getting signed) or choice, career change; moving, divorce, new marriage, new babies, retirement, empty nesting, injury recovery, death of a loved one, unemployment, marriage and family issues, pre-marital counseling.
