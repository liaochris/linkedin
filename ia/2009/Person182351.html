Patti Womack’s Summary

Eleanor Roosevelt said “A woman is like a tea bag—you never know how strong she is until she gets into hot water.” Shall we have a cup of tea?

There will always be unplanned bumps in the road, resistance from another or a firm “No” from an authoritarian. It happens both in our personal and work lives. It is in those moments, my greatest accomplishments have been created.

I have an innate ability to identify problems/opportunities & then develop a resolution strategy. A Chinese proverb says, “Those who say it cannot be done…should not interrupt the person doing it.” Non- believers, please move aside.

I enjoy coaching & mentoring individuals that cross my life’s path. I totally realize the value of human capital and can quickly identify individual strengths & provide guidance to help others achieve their personal & professional goals. Passionate people are the most productive people.

My vision is that Corp. America will soon expand its definition of sustainability to include employees & implement strategic hiring, employee development, training and retention programs. Companies will find cost effective solutions that will address company goals, ROI, & profitability while helping the environment for present and future generations. I want to find a way to implement my vision.
