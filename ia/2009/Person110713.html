Jeremy Sutton’s Summary

My first career spanned 10 years in a global technology organisation, where I held various leadership roles and was responsible for directing teams/developing markets across the Americas and EMEA. These years give me a deep understanding of organisational context; I have 'first-hand' experience of the issues facing people in organisations today.

I emigrated from Montreal to London to further my second career in business psychology. From our offices in West London, I currently design and deliver programmes of career coaching, talent management & leadership assessment/development for UK clients in the public and private sectors.

I am a graduate member of the British Psychological Society and hold certificates of competence in occupational testing. My first degree is in Biology & Psychology (Combined Hons.) from McMaster University and I am currently completing an MSc Occupational Psychology at Birkbeck, University of London.

Most of my work focuses on: supporting people through career transitions, leadership/management development & the impact of personality on team performance. I am a subject matter expert and qualified trainer in the Hogan Personality Inventory (HPI), Hogan Development Survey (HDS), Motives Values Preferences Inventory (MVPI) and Hogan Business Reasoning Inventory (HBRI) as published by Hogan Assessment Systems and am also a contributing member of the Hogan International Research team. Additionally, I am quaified to use the NEO, 15FQ+, 16PF5, JTI, MBTI and the application of temperament in organistaional/team settings.

Please check out my blog at: www.3-minute-mile.blogspot.com
