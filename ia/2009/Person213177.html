Neil Mathias’s Summary

Neil Mathias has over 20 years experience within Private Client Stockbroking, Asset Management & Fund Management.

During the 1980's Neil learnt his trade on the old Stock Exchange floor within a small firm of Private Client Stockbrokers, before moving on in the 1990's to generate significant business for Midland Stockbrokers, (later to become HSBC Stockbrokers). Neil quickly assumed the position of Senior Investment Manager, and successfully managed a number of Ultra High Net Worth Private Client Portfolios, specialising the UK quoted and Unlisted Securities Markets.

More recently, Neil has been instrumental in acting as a Consultant within the online Spreadbetting and Futures industry before turning his attention to Compliance & Risk.
Neil is an ‘Agenda free’ totally committed individual, with high levels of energy, enthusiasm and passion for excellent service. Securities Institute qualified, being well versed in financial and investment markets. Hard working, loyal dependable and above all, has the right attitude!
