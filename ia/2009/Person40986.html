  • Owner/Principal Consultant

    Glow Marketing

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2000 — Present (9 years 7 months)

    Glow Marketing is a consulting firm directed by Chandra Lynn, M.B.A. that offers integrated marketing solutions and specializes in artist relations, PR and promotions in the entertainment industry. Chandra previously worked for successful and legendary entertainment producers, publishing companies, and media. Most notably, Chandra held marketing positions at Digidesign, the leading manufacturer of computer-based digital audio production systems for recording and live sound; Tickets.com; Bill Graham Presents; Miller Freeman Publishing (now New Bay Media); San Francisco Focus Magazine/KQED; and KFOG Radio/Clear Channel.

    Chandra has a proven track record for securing positive press coverage, including product reviews and user stories. She has also managed numerous events and led successful marketing programs with artists that consistently top the charts including Black Eyed Peas, Brooks & Dunn, John Mayer, Linkin Park, Nine Inch Nails, Peter Gabriel, Sting, and many others.
