John Digles’s Summary

Visionary executive and marketing leader JOHN DIGLES has produced significant results driving sales and profitability, building powerful brands and spearheading category-creating initiatives.

As XanGo’s Chief Marketing Officer, Mr. Digles led a global program central in achieving a billion in sales, a million independent distributors and expansion to 24 markets for the 6-year-old company. John negotiated XanGo’s historic jersey-front sponsorship with Major League Soccer and Real Salt Lake -- the first deal of its kind in North American pro sports. Creator of XanGo.TV, he led invention of the market’s first brand-centric video sharing site, sparking new industry digital and social media platforms. His innovations earned John an invitation to address the WFDSA World Congress XIII.

Mr. Digles served as SVP with MWW Group, one of the industry’s top PR and marketing firms. As Chicago office GM, John advised well-known brands, government agencies, trade associations and new growth ventures. He gained national recognition for creating the "BlackBerry Thumb" campaign and multicultural programs to foster preventive health care advocacy. An energy practice lead, Mr. Digles advanced clean energy, utility choice and nuclear power education.

Architect of the premier agency offering for nutrition and functional foods, John earned a reputation for reinvigorating brands, successfully launching new products and bridging sales gaps. His creation of ScienceDialogue established a strategic methodology to promote scientific research through marketing.

Founder of Traveller Jones Productions, Mr. Digles is an award-winning filmmaker whose work has gained critical notice, including the Sundance Film Festival. A founding member of the Illinois Production Alliance, John helped gain legislative approval of Illinois’ first visual media production tax incentive which generated an 80% revenue increase and over 700 new jobs.
