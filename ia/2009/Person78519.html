Shemek (Przemek) Fedyczkowski’s Summary

Shemek Fedyczkowski has been actively involved in the software industry for over 12 years now. As Founder & President at Cleo Networks, Shemek is focused on building the next generation of Web to Mobile & Mobile to Web communication technologies & services. Shemek applies a hands-on approach in every stage of R & D; with experience and proven track record in business planning, sales & marketing strategies and project management.   • President, CEO

    Cleo Networks Corporation Ltd. (Canada) & Cleo Networks Sp. z o. o. (Poland)

    (Telecommunications industry)

    September 2006 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    Cleo Networks is a developer of web-based real-time global communication services that link with mobile devices & on-demand services. We're focused on pioneering innovation in unified WEB 2.0, VOICE / VIDEO / SMS / GPRS systems linked to mobile devices and used for global communication on-demand.

    Cleo Networks is currently fundraising for the first mobile to web interactive broadcasting portal. Also the world's first global personal communications service available on-demand. Call anyone from a single web-based service, forward all your calls and receive all your voiceMail, videoMail, SMS & eMail in one place. This project is code named iBabel.

    smsTickets. Cleo’s revolutionary smsTICKETS system allows companies to do away with printing and selling costly paper-based tickets for events such as concerts, sports games or even train tickets - and instead provides SMS based proof of purchase "Tickets" directly to customers' mobile phones. Yes, nothing new, but we use Mobile OCR technology to use one phone’s camera to scan the screen of another phone for the purpose of authenticating an encoded SMS number (event or train “ticket”).

    Mobile SMS Payment and Event Ticketing Solution is 100% Environmentally Friendly Technology for Event Admission, City Transportation or Long-Haul Train Rides that would work particularily well throught Europe.

    Cleo's iBabel comm. Portal, a technology Shemek designed, is a hosted SaaS (Software-as-a-service) turnkey solution to launching a complete social networking portal with embedded real-time communication features from your browser & mobile device. Cleo's 2nd product includes a SaaS SMS service for mass notification and interactive marketing. Specifically for event ticketing, mass notification, and also SMS Alert Emergency Services. Cleo Networks has offices in Canada and Poland.
