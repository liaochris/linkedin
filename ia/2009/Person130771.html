Kevin Chern’s Summary

Mr. Chern's focus is on Internet marketing, lead generation, legal industry consulting, web application development. His goal is to develop relationships within the legal, debt and mortgage industries.

His accomplishments include:

* Managing Partner of the largest consumer bankruptcy law firm in the country with over 180 employees in 19 states, retaining over 450 clients on a weekly basis.
* Administrator of numerous nationwide legal marketing cooperatives
* Author of "Life After Bankruptcy", a book that guides Debtors down the path to preserving their "fresh start".
* Consultant to over 1100 law firms nationwide on marketing, technology, business development and law firm management.
* Architect of four comprehensive law office databases and dozens of legal websites.
* As an attorney, personally filed thousands of consumer bankruptcy matters.
* Expert in law practice development, having created law practices in the fields of bankruptcy, class action litigation, lemon law, personal injury, workers compensation and DUI.

Chern believes one of his greatest career achievements is his track record of building successful businesses that provide their employees with stimulating and energizing work environments that wreak of entrepreneurship. At Infra-Strategy, he is proud of his close friendship with the employees and management team who enjoy developing ideas with the confidence that they can succeed.

In this can-do environment, Chern has helped Total Attorneys, Inc. expand from its small starting core to 130 quality employees. By contributing his expertise in the areas of law practice management, marketing, business development and technology, Chern is also helping small law firms stay competitive and prosper when they are faced with every day business challenges that larger companies often face.
