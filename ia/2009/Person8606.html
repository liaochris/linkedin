Alex Shalman’s Summary

Dental Experience:
Since the age of 15 I have been partaking in every nuance of dentistry that I could get my hands on. I have done front desk, insurance, billing, four handed dentistry, OSHA regulated sterilization, and management.

I'm currently applying for Dental School, with the intent of becoming a Dentist and create a world of beautiful smiles. I love the diversity that I see within the dental profession that will allow me to franchise, public speak, teach, write, and last but not least, spend quality one on one time with patients by giving them the best dental care.

Internet Presence and Blogging:
I set up AlexShalman.com on December 15th, 2006. Since then I've grown a readership of 60,000 per month, and 2700 subscribers. The simple answer for how I did it is by providing genuine value to my readers.

I provide one-on-one or group sessions that optimize blogging websites. Feel free to contact me at alex@alexshalman.com to learn more about my services.

Podcasting:
I recently got involved with Podcasting after my interview on the nationally acclaimed SIRIUS Satellite Radio. You can look forward to interviews with other high-profiled people as well as cutting edge self-improvement advice.

Life Coaching:
After reading hundreds of books on self-improvement topics, and speaking to tens of thousands of blog readers on such topics, I now provide one-on-one life coaching sessions. Feel free to contact me at alex@alexshalman.com to learn more about my services.
