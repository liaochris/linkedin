Anasha Cummings’s Summary

I have a very broad range of interests and goals right now, but I think I want to go into sustainability consulting. I see a green revolution as the only way to save our planet, but also a good way to save an economy which is becoming over taxed with externalities. The Idea of helping companies and communities find a balance with the natural world and a way of being works with rather than against the growth of life of all kind really appeals to me. I don't quite know what form it will take, but that is my goal.

    (Education Management industry)

    June 1999 — Present (10 years 6 months)

    Big Sky Learning is a summer camp and educational center which teaches math and science to kids ages 5 through 14 through innovative hands-on workshops where kids learn important skills while building electronic and robotic projects. After taking classes and camps with them for several years, I began helping out. I have since developed with the program and am now the chief project manager as well as co- developing many of the curricula with the founder.

    In my most resent position as project manager, I managed three simultaneous camps, aiding the lead teachers with the technical details and managing the team of interns to make sure all of the kids’ projects get done on time. I also helped design and build prototype projects and managed the inventory.
