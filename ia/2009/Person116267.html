Joey Silvian’s Summary

Joey Silvian is a creative professional who has been in the advertising and marketing business for over 25 years. He has held marketing and creative positions at Twentieth Century Fox, Mattel, Sandy Corporation, BBDO and VIA (Virtual Interactive Agency). One of his projects was the Jeep Off-Road Challenge, an interactive exhibit designed for the auto shows that was featured on the Today Show and Good Morning America

He has also recently created a trade show for Internet Security companies in Second Life, a current 3-D avatar-oriented environment. He has created numerous other high impact programs for many for major corporations in all of the other available marketing media – telemarketing, direct mail, e-newsletters and online media.

One of his passions is assisting start-ups both directly with helping individual companies early in their development to analyze and create their market and on a broader scale has also been instrumental in creating plans for start-up incubators both for Spark in Ann Arbor and BCU in Battle Creek, Michigan. He has a Bachelor’s in Marketing and a Masters in Education Psychology from the University of Michigan.
