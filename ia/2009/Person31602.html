Brad Kayton’s Summary

I have been a key member of successful technology companies throughout my 20 year career in the areas of IP networking and communications, broadband and home networking equipment, and end-user software / services.

I have the following track record:
–Serial entrepreneur (seven start-ups, three as founder) with two successful exits as CEO
–Recognized CE/Broadband product “category creation king” (to quote an analyst)
–Defined, managed, and marketed products that have become the market leader in its respective area numerous times and across different industries
–Created and implemented company strategies that have defined or redefined industries
–Conducted financing efforts for over $135M in numerous VC financings
–Identified, signed, and managed over 75 strategic partnerships in the last ten years
–Executed “Best of Show” award-winning product launches at trade shows or conferences for six (6) different products / product lines with five (5) different companies
–Recognized as having won a record number (27) of consumer networking product honors for a start-up new-entrant in a single year (2003)
–Managed PR programs achieving placements in nearly every major business and technical publication, most often executed on a shoe-string budget
–Speak regularly at technology tradeshows / conferences and on radio shows
–Invented and executed numerous creative online marketing programs that have led to hundreds-of-thousands of new unique monthly Web site visitors
–Have built departments from scratch, have managed teams and teams of teams for many years, have recurited complete mgt teams to my start-ups, and know how to run effective boards/board meetings
–Earned an MBA and MA in Communications Technology; began career as an engineer so understand technologies intimately
