Anthony Turano’s Summary

Anthony Turano began his career as a graphic designer in 1998. He then touched on other arts such as set design and construction for Columbia Records in Miami, assisting in designing the set for the Will Smith Music Video "Miami", which won Best Male Video of 1999 at the MTV Video Music Awards. After living in Rome and Miami he returned back to Montreal to work in the fashion industry and perfecting his photography skills.

Over the past 10 years, Anthony Turano has acquired all the skills to create stunning photographs. Most photographers pride themselves on one "look", but Anthony can really do it all. From high fashion shots, to eye catching commercial shots, variety is something Anthony does not lack. Whether you are a designer promoting your new collection or a magazine looking for that perfect shot, he can accommodate all your needs.

With a team of top makeup artists, hair and wardrobe stylists, he has a different approach to the world of fashion and commercial photography.
