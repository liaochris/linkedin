Helen Clover’s Summary

Financial Executive with broad experience of management and financial accounting practice in small business environment. Hands on Property Developer including project management of complete refurbishments. On Going Property Management in the commercial, residential and holiday sectors with associated financial management.

Driven by a passion for property and with a sound head for figures I am bringing my strengths together to become 'Edinburgh's Positive Face of Finance'.

I can source Conventional Mortgage products, Buy to Let Mortgages for Property Investors & Commercial Finance for property & business developement, all supported with a range of Protection Products so that exposure to risk is minimised. A fully FSA compliant service.

I will treat any loan application like a military campaign:

Know yourself - The business or investment plan
Know your enemy - The market, finance houses & the competition
Surround yourself with a willing and experienced army.
Prepare for the attack - You may only have one chance
Victory - How will you hold on to your new acquisition.
Protect - What happens if the unexpected happens.

Planning is the key to Positive financial arrangements and when it come to planning I'm here to help you.

In addition to my professional experience, I have lived in Spain and on the water for a total of 6 years renovating an old Finca and a 47 foot Formosa Yacht. Sailing is my other active interest. The Finca is currently rented out to holiday makers through holidaylettings.co.uk (see websites) the yacht remains a labour of love and favourite holiday destination. (Ask me about it if you are interested)
