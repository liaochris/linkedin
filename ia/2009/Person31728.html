Brad Monterio’s Summary

Accomplished financial and marketing executive with experience in FinTech, MedTech and CleanTech sectors, as well as ESG (Environmental Social Governance), XBRL/interactive data, financial planning/wealth management, accounting, legal, consumer goods, sustainable development and professional services sectors.

Counsel clients in variety of areas: investment banking, global private placement agent services, capital fundraising, debt/equity financing, sponsorship, XBRL, ESG advisory services, strategic planning, new product development, product launches, direct marketing, collateral and advertising design, copy writing/writing, speech writing, business plan/marketing plan/communications plan development, product/service positioning and branding, media relations, government affairs, media training, message point development, image management, new business development, and strategic partner development/third party outreach.

I speak regularly to professionals on marketing and communications topics, including “Communicating Across the Generations,” "Sourcing Premier Clients," “Microtrends in Marketing – An Exponential Shift,” “Media Relations Means Money in the Bank,” "Media as a Marketing Tool," “Simple Steps to an Effective Marketing Plan,” “Using Media Relations to Build Brand Awareness and Attract Clients,” and “The Art of Media Pitching.”

I have also authored articles on marketing; communications; trends/forecasting; ESG; sustainability reporting; XBRL/interactive data; business reporting, disclosure and transparency.
