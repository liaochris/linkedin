Riel Roussopoulos’s Summary

I've got experience in getting projects off the ground. Be they new product launches or new companies, I have a good ability to take a project from concept to functional business model.

My specialty is really technology evangelism. I have always used technology and particularly the internet to distinguish myself from "the rest".

Likewise I have an uncanny ability to evaluate a business or idea and visualize how to digitize their process and both streamline and vastly increase their work flow as a result.

I have experience in orchestrating large scale public events and creating and executing the plan to deal with thousands of people.

In business I've played all the rolls from sales to CEO to messenger to council member, which means that I bring a wealth of experience from all sides of the equation to every project that I take on.

I am looking for capital to help grow a new company which builds communities and business management solutions. Part of that includes showing business's, artists and other organizations how to use web based tools to better manage their client and content management needs.

I am always interested in consulting with people and groups on their technology needs and options and plans... I am a very good IDEA person that can show you avenues that you may not have been aware of.
