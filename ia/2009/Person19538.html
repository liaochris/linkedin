Ari Herzog’s Summary

As principal of Ari Herzog & Associates, a provider of online media strategy services for business and government, Ari uses a holistic approach to explain the benefits of embracing social networking, interactive marketing, and online branding as a means of reducing overhead, increasing productivity, and engaging with people.

With over 10 years of experience in new media, community journalism, and government administration, Ari understands the nature of the web and is adept as a consultant and speaker to cut through the clutter of Web 2.0.

Ari is seeking opportunities in consulting, speaking, and writing. He is especially passionate about narrowing the digital divide and empowering civic engagement and cross-agency collaboration.

Media relations with bloggers
