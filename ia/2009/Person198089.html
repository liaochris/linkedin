Richard Vetstein’s Summary

I am the Founding Partner of The Vetstein Law Group, P.C. based in Framingham, MA. We solve legal problems primarily for individuals, small businesses, real estate owners and managers, and financial institutions. We bring and defend litigation and resolve disputes in business, real estate, construction, condominium, zoning, environmental, banking and financial services, employment, and personal injury. We also appear before local town boards, zoning boards of appeal (ZBA), conservation commissions, and other administrative agencies to obtain permits and licenses for businesses and real estate projects. We also help individuals and businesses with the purchase and sale of real estate. Lastly, we form new business and non-profit entities such as corporations, limited liability companies (LLC), limited liability partnerships (LLP), professional corporations, and IRS 501(c)(3) tax exempt entities.

I am also the Founder and Managing Member of TitleHub Closing Services LLC, a Massachusetts based and attorney owned title and real estate closing services company. TitleHub will be one of the first Massachusetts closing companies to offer both electronic closing technology and a full platform of realtor and lender legal resources, seminars and internet resources. Plus, we have a network of over 1,200 realtors, financial planners, high net worth individuals, business owners, home buyers, etc. – that we will be distributing content to, and targeting referral opportunities for, our preferred partners, through our proprietary internet marketing and social media platform.

I am also the creator of the Massachusetts Real Estate Blog (http://www.massrealestatelawblog.com). I also contribute to the Boston.com Real Estate Now Blog (http://www.boston.com/realestate/news/blogs/renow).

You can reach me at rvetstein@vetsteinlawgroup.com or at 508-620-5352.
