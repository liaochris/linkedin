Peter Hornby’s Summary

Senior software architect with a broad technical skill set, deep industry knowledge and extensive practical experience in meeting mission-critical development challenges. Experienced leader in all phases of software development, from project initiation through research, design and implementation to deployment and customer support. Recognized as having a reputation for timely delivery of quality products in highly challenging situations. Bright, people-oriented, hard-working and strongly customer-focussed. Accomplished and experienced technical writer and presenter. Interested in taking on challenging roles in defining and driving software projects, turning customer requirements into successful and functional software products.

Peter Hornby’s Specialties:

Deep understanding of virtualization and its impact on enterprise IT strategy, especially its intersection with the arena of high availability.
Broad experience in enterprise IT management–aligning IT resources with business goals.
Strong advocate of integrated management technologies – providing a consistent view of the operational performance of a heterogeneous IT environment
Extensive implementation experience in key areas of the enterprise ecosystem – TCP/IP security, resource management
