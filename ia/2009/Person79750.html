Fletcher Garrett, CSTE’s Summary

Mr. Fletcher Garrett, CSTE is a SBE Certified Senior Television Engineer who has 20 years experience as a Broadcast / Video System Design Engineer. He currently works for Vitec Broadcast Services at the Bexel Division in the Chicago office as the onsite Engineer. Bexel has 10 offices nationwide and gives World-wide Broadcast / Production equipment support via equipment and systems rentals. Mr. Garrett is responsible for maintaining and repairing, to component level, a wide variety of specialized broadcast production rental equipment as well as building and calibrating customer specified systems. He has also performed EIC (Engineer-In-Charge) duties at various large scale concerts and events – such as Lollapolooza and televised football games – where he set-up and calibrated remote studios to perform to all applicable SMPTE and FCC standards in both standard definition and ATSC HDTV. In addition, he provides clients with technical support regarding operation and set-up of a wide variety of broadcast gear.

Mr. Garrett is a DeVry Institute of Technology AASE graduate and a member of the following organizations: SBE (Society of Broadcast Engineers) and IEEE: BTS (Institute of Electronic and Electrical Engineers in the Broadcast Technology Society).Mr. Garrett resides in Chicago, Illinois and enjoys the challenges that Broadcast Video Support provides.
