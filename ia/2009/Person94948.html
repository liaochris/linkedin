Himanshu Gahlot’s Summary

I am a Senior Undergraduate Student at National Institute of Technology, Allahabad, one of the premier engineering institutes of India. I am aiming for higher studies abroad, preferably U.S. I have a deep interest in computational aspects of Natural Language Processing and have undertaken a series of research projects in this area. My research includes works on LTAG spinal formalisms, automatic annotation in Hindi Language and more recently, annotation through Ontologies.

Apart from my research work I love hanging out with my friends, the trip to Nainital being one of the most memorable ones. I like watching movies too.

Himanshu Gahlot’s Specialties:

My broad area of interest is Artificial Intelligence with focus on Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I have worked on WEKA, WEKA Metal, CRF++, LibSVM, Maxent and various other Language Processing Tools. I have contributed significantly to the automatic annotation capability of Sanchay, a language processing tool for Indian languages (http://sourceforge.net/projects/nlp-sanchay).
