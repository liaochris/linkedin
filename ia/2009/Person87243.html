Giuseppe La Spada’s Summary

His unique works integrate the use of photography, graphics and consumer interactivity whilst retaining the underlying influence of visualizing what before was left to the imagination.

Aside from graduating summa com lade from the European Institute of Design in Rome, he ascertains that the roots of his work today is organic in the sense that he began to experiment with photography, video and then using post production techniques to individualize his internationally acclaimed works on the internet and in live art performances.

Always considered as one of the finest italian prospects in the digital arts, he was asked to lecture at his former university from 2001 to 2004. La Spada's experience has translated into over 360 multimedia works for numerous prestigious clientelle including italian and international musicians who had their work visually translated a La Spada, as it has become to be known. Some of these works have allowed La Spada to accumulate impressive recognition of his talent.

Giuseppe La Spada’s Specialties:

360° art direction. Interface design. Visual live performance for events and shows.
