Ben DeCamp’s Summary

TheStyleShark™ is the controversial nighttime persona of photographer Ben DeCamp. It all began one night when he walked down the street from his apartment and uninvited into a college frat party. Girls stripped off their clothing, a random guy waved a gun around the living room, and general debauchery escalated because a lens was pointed in their direction. He’s been called, “Reckless, impulsive, and a chauvinistic womanizer.” That last one may be a little too harsh; he’s partial to brunettes. Toss in a poker visor, oversized aviators, a mustache; and some bizarre image of Hunter S. Thompson wielding a camera emerged.
Always lurking…
