Gary Braswell’s Summary

Mr. Braswell has worked as a technologist using the C/C++/Java family of technologies his entire career of 20+ years. He has an even mixture of consulting and product development experience, with a breadth of industry vertical experience that includes: telecommunications, travel & hospitality, defense, government, and supply chain.

Mr. Braswell has been leading teams in various roles in sizes since 1995 with leadership experience over geographically distributed teams (both near-shore and offshore) over the past 5 years.

Mr. Braswell has demonstrated the capacity to quickly become competent in an industry vertical, and can quickly understand the underlying technologies and design forces at play for a given solution set. He has the communications skills and the demeanor that makes him adept in leadership roles, but has maintained a technical competency that allows him to be very hands on during the design, implementation, and testing of a solution. He is results-oriented, but always treats others with respect in the process of achieving them.
