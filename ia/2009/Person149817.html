Margaret Webster’s Summary

I am a freelance copywriter & writer and I help companies to communicate with their customers, employees, investors and other key audiences.

My projects focus on solving business problems for my clients. For example, I have helped companies to meet increased competition, improve employee engagement, communicate with many audiences via one website and provide practical advice to their members.

In summary, I:

• offer a cost-effective service
• work well with designers and IT specialists
• can help with small, one-off pieces or large, on-going projects
• am based in central London
• can work with companies anywhere in the UK or abroad.

Margaret Webster’s Specialties:

copywriting for websites, intranet sites, brochures & other promotional material and writing website articles & magazine articles
