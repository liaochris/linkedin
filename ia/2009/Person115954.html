Joe Nassau’s Summary

Joe Nassau is an executive with over twenty-five years of leadership and general management experience in the satellite, cable, ISP, and telecommunications industries.

Joe has been with the MDU Communications (USA) since June 2005 and is responsible for leading and managing the Regional Offices (Sales and Field Operations), the Callcenter (Sales, Customer Support and Tech Support), IT, Network Operations Center (NOC), Subscriber Management Support Systems, Training, Dispatch, and Backoffice operations.

His previous experience includes a variety of general and operational management roles. In particular, he led and managed the integration, growth and operation of HBO's satellite television call center and backoffice operations in Chicago that supported sales and service for over 1 million subscribers. He also held key leadership roles in the successful planning, launch, and deployment of DIRECTV services in Latin America, as well as the launch of EarthLink's High-Speed Internet Service throughout Time Warner Cable's network nationwide.

Joe also served as an Infantry Officer in the Unites States Army with 22 years of active duty and reserve component experience.

Joe Nassau’s Specialties:

Diverse background includes specific achievements in operations, administration, finance, sales, project management, customer service, callcenter management, systems, and training. Skilled problem solver; adept at maximizing productivity and motivating team members to achieve organizational goals.
