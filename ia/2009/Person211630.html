Scott Julian’s Summary

Scott Julian is a seasoned Internet industry professional with over 8 years of board level business experience.

His unparalleled understanding of the Australian digital media landscape has enabled him to grow and sell several businesses including social networking company TheScene & online business services company Gate13.

Scott currently holds board seats with 5 companies ranging from medical software to mobile content.

Prior to his directors roles, Scott held media communications rolls with, ISIS communications LTD, the Commonwealth Secretariat, the International Air Transport Association and in the Defence Industry.

Scott’s knowledge and experience have led him to be invited by David Koch to appear on The Seven Network’s “My Business TV” as their eCommerce expert.

Scott also travels the country to advise business groups on eCommerce & digital marketing issues, speaking at forums and networking events.

Scott is a Fellow of the Australian Institute of Management, and a member of the Australian Institute of Company Directors.
