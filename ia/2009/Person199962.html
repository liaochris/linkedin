Robert Brook’s Summary

With 20 years of experience, Robert has seen many sides of the GIS, utility, and pipeline industries, having experience in oil and gas exploration, pipeline construction and management, gas distribution, and in the development and implementation of ESRI industry solutions. He is currently responsible for the development of ESRI's distribution/transmission vertical integration strategy and pipeline and gas utility global marketing strategy. He currently serves on the APDM steering committee, the PODS Board of directors in addition to numerous other national and international committees.

Robert Brook’s Specialties:

Pipeline construction and management, GIS implementation, business management, client development, application development
