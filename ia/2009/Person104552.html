Jarod Hillerman’s Summary

Highly motivated Marketing professional, with experience in developing a company's brand strategy and correlating messaging, programs and sales implementation.

My goal is simple. Every day we are subjected to Marketing from companies that either do not know their identity and portray an unclear image, and/or do not effectively communicate the benefit to me of using their products/services, or just flat out have a ridiculous product a la most products sold by Billy mays. I simply want to help companies examine their products and business goals, and develop and execute a strategy that successfully achieves.
