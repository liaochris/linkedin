Steve Russell’s Summary

I enjoy the profound gift of having been qualified in the world of suits and business, and intellectual pursuit, together with the quieter side of self-realisation. I dig the buzz of it all.

If it were easy, everybody would be doing it. Why walk down the well-worn path that others have trod before you...where's the fun in that? Why be a paramecium in an ocean when one can be a big fish in a pond of one's own creation?

I've never really been an employee. I have been (excepting the LBO of my cousin's snow cone business at age 8, and another cousin's paper route at 10): hippie entrepreneur, traveller, R.E. broker, winegrower, student, R.E. investor, print publisher, and web developer.

Taking my 40 years' experience of street smarts, formal education, property development, print publishing, advertising, sales and marketing — together with eastern philosophies — I am now developing a very large network of branded websites. It is my goal to create a brand that evokes a rich, positive, proactive and informative level of involvement and experience for the end user.

It's very exciting, and daunting, to be dancing at the thin leading edge of discovery. It's full of risk and uncertainty; and, I readily admit I have always bitten off more than I can chew — which is precisely why I enjoy my life and what I do so much!

I'd very much like to connect with like minded folks from all aspects of the Web 2.0 lifestyle and community to share ideas.

Steve Russell’s Specialties:

I would have to say that my specialty — regardless of the industry — is in discovering and developing opportunity that is overlooked by mainstream players. This is not for everyone, but it sure has made my life interesting....

  • VILLAS.COM

    (Real Estate industry)

    September 2004 — July 2008 (3 years 11 months)

    After 13 years in print, Villas Magazine ceased print publishing. I developed an online version of my magazine, VILLAS.COM. We specialised in Property in Spain, France, Italy and Portugal with prices from USD 2.5 million up to USD 35 million. Having found that the domain name was better suited to the travel and vacation rental industry, this website has been repurposed.

    In July, 2008, the domain villas.com was sold to an internet developer. The terms of this sale, aside from the price which any competent domainer can find, are confidential.

  • Villas &... Magazine

    (Publishing industry)

    July 1992 — September 2004 (12 years 3 months)

    In 1993 I launched—from my spare bedroom—a B&W magazine on newsprint for the real estate market of Spain's Costa del Sol. I built Villas &... Magazine from a local rag into one of Europe's top glossies for property, architecture and décor. Villas covered the coasts of Spain, Portugal and France with a full-time staff of 10 and 40 contributors.

    With the Internet and a glut of lesser publications came pressure to lower advertising rates. Rather than sacrifice quality for which we were renowned, I decided that my goals had been achieved and exceeded, and to "go out on top." We ceased publication with our 137th issue, when "Villas" was considered the benchmark for all publications in our genre. Our quality and editorial standards have never been achieved since—so say our readers, contributors, publishing peers and advertisers—even 4 years after we ceased publication. A compliment, which, as a publisher, I continue to find very gratifying.
