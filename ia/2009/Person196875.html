Russell Lutz’s Summary

Russell Lutz founded RGL Consulting guided by his vision of a straightforward, results-oriented approach to executive management challenges for a varied client base. With over 30 years experience in Human Resources, Russ’ expertise includes executive coaching, organization design, and management development.

Russ has counseled executives at all levels of the organization from the Vice Chairman to front-line managers. He has provided consultative services to executives and department managers on a wide range of business and people related issues.

Russ is known for working with managers to define and implement both long term and short term strategic plans. This included people development, resource management and building team profiles by identifying position competencies.

Russell Lutz’s Specialties:

General Management Professional with Human Resources expertise in the following areas: executive coaching; organizational design; management development; and, leadership and management. Business partner responsible for developing and leading HR strategies for corporate clients. Spearheaded HR activities to positively impact the bottom-line and ensure the utilization of high quality HR processes.
