Grant Hutchins’s Summary

Right now I work for Spiceworks, an exciting new startup in Austin, TX, that makes a free network management application. There's no catch because it's ad-supported. Go try it out! It has next to zero configuration and is up and running in under five minutes. You have nothing to lose!

My heart is in startups. In fact, my alma mater, Olin College, is itself a start-up: I'm in its first graduating class. I love to do the odd jobs that a small group has to share responsibility for, from typesetting documents to developing websites to meeting with outside contacts.

I enjoy usability, human factors, DSP, computer architecture, and mathematics. My User-Oriented Collaborative Design and Human Factors Interface Design courses helped me appreciate the usefulness of involving users directly in the design process.

I have a special interest in audio signal processing, and have written electronic music and developed multiple software audio effects. My company, SoHa Sound Design, has a software synthesizer product on the market.

My goal is to gain experience in the workforce in areas that require both technical and user-oriented thinking.
