Terry Pierce’s Summary

I have a tremendous amount of energy that impacts those I am leading, teaching or training. An excellent coach, I enjoy working with people at every level in the organization. I have experience in both operations management and in programming. Having led many small teams, I enjoy team-building, problem-solving and working with diverse groups of people.

I am experienced in solving technical problems with a broad understanding of information systems concepts and practices and I am knowledgeable of an array of manufacturing with specialized knowledge of configured, make-to-order environments in the machine tool, window and door, furniture, and apparel industries.
