E. Vidar Top’s Summary

Author of "The 3 Energies Behind Sales Success", 3E and O®O (Triple-O) framework - proven persuasive methodology for complex sales. See http://oneonone.no/3e for information about 3E.

Published several research projects within sales and influence. (E.g. "Sales Call Planning - Planning to Sell, 2004" and "High-end Sales for Open Source, 2005".)

Founder of One-on-One, 2002 and 3E Code of Excellence, 2007. Frequent public speaker. Warmly engaged in the subject of "influence without manipulation".
