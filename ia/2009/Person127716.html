Kathryn Marion’s Summary

Real Solutions Press LLC publishes...you guessed it...real solutions to the problems and concerns of business and everyday life. We do this through two individually-branded lines of publications:

Our QwikSmarts™ Take Charge™ book series for students and graduates has just gotten off the ground with the first title, Grads: Take Charge of Your Career, Your Money, Your Life! Jam-packed with over 140 topics covering careers to credit to cooking (and everything in-between!), it's the most comprehensive resource available for the Class of 2009. Five mini-books round out the titles for new college graduates and Qwik-Tips™ cards on over 100 topics for the same audience are also available--perfect for take-aways from a counseling office or as long-lived promotional pieces for companies targeting new young professionals.

See www.QwikSmarts.com for more information.

The Smartest Thing I Ever Did...™ is a book series with titles such as The Smartest Thing I Ever Did...™ with My Money, …for My Health, …as a Woman in Business, and many more. Each book is a collection of stories about the solutions people have found that worked for them, in the hopes that they will help someone else who finds themselves in the same situation .

The contributor of each published story receives a byline as well as a 100-word bio to tell the world who they are, what they do, and how to contact them (optional). We are partnering each title with a worthy charity, which will receive a donation based on the sales of 'their' book.

Visit www.TheSmartestThingIEverDid.com for more information about the series. Featured Contributor and Co-Author opportunities are available.
