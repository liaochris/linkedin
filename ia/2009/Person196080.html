Remko de Jong’s Summary

In august 2007 I became a Bachelor of Multimedia, where I wrote a thesis on an animation series concept, as well as working on one named Wolleke. Throughout this work I've learned a lot about concepting, projectmanagement, animation, scriptwriting and video editing. With a small dedicated team we made 26 episodes of this television series which is aimed at the pre-teen audience.

After my graduation I got the opportunity to work in the games industry. I've worked as a gamesdesigner on the following titles for the Nintendo DS and PC/Mac: 10 voor Taal, Een tegen 100 and Denksport, to name a few. These are popular TV-shows in The Netherlands with the exception of the latter which is a Puzzles book series. We realised the games together with Triangle Studios.

After one year the company split up and I decided to go travelling for 3 months in South East Asia. Which was a great experience that really helped to develop your independence, cultural knowledge and sense of relativity. And ofcourse your collection of photographs ;)
