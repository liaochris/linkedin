Qingfang Rao’s Summary

I am a postgraduate at Sustainable Design Research Center, Kingston University. My study in London is funded by Audi Design Foundation. My undergraduate years were a design excursion - studying while traveling - from Shanghai to Beijing and Rotterdam.

My MA design research explores strategic design for sustainability in China. Currently, I am working for Greengaged, an initiative to engage wider design community in sustainability issues. I take Greengaged-China as the initial project to build a sustainable design community in China.
