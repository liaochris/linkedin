Ján Matejka’s Summary

Jan Matejka presently works as the principal attorney in his own law firm located in Prague, Czech Republic. His professional experience has spanned the areas of intellectual property and ICT law, contract drafting, negotiation and consultancy. Jan Matejka also focus on civil and commercial law, contract and business law consultancy.

His professional experience involves also e-government, electronic signatures, information security and telecommunications.

He is also very active in academic sphere. He works as the head of the Department of Private Law of the Institute of state and law of the Academy of Sciences of the Czech Republic. He is a senior lecturer of Internet and Computer Law at the Faculty of Law of the University of West Bohemia in Pilsen and senior lecturer of Data Protection Law of Faculty of Mathematics and Physics of the Charles University in Prague. He studied at Manchester Metropolitan University School of Law and the Universite d`ete Granade. He is the author of a number of texts on Telecommunication Law.

Ján Matejka’s Specialties:

Internet and computer law, Data privacy and (personal) data protection, Software law, Copyright law, Civil and commercial law, Contract and business consultancy, Labor law, Telecommunications law, Domain names, Privacy protection, License and service contract negotiation, Cyberlaw, Corporate general issues, Arbitration ...
