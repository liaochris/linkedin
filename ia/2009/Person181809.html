    Arrows Group International

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    March 2009 — Present (4 months)

    In recruitment, no two clients are the same, but each demands the highest level of service.

    To meet the diverse requirements of competitive industry sectors, Arrows Group has redefined the approach to traditional inflexible recruitment models that are out dated for today's market and instead operates a dynamic model, known as the AG HIVE.

    Bringing together the usually separate recruitment streams of Contingency, Campaign Management, Search and Selection and Contract/Interim, Arrows Group has created a unique system that gives clients total control and total transparency over their recruitment activities through a single source.

    Established in 2003, Arrows Group has grown quickly and built an impressive reputation based on the delivery of robust, quality-driven recruitment services. We are now recognised as an established key supplier within our core markets - Technology, Project Management, Finance and Healthcare.

    Today, Arrows Group works with over 250 organisations across the UK and Europe, providing Permanent, Contract and Temporary recruitment services. Clients include industry leaders and cutting edge innovators, all of whom have very different recruitment requirements, and all of whom have seen their requirements met by our uniquely flexible approach.

    The Company is fast paced, energetic and hooked on success; we love what we do and take enormous pride in delivering exceptional services to our clients.

    Arrows Group is a proud member of Association of Professional Staffing Companies (APSCo) and the Recruitment and Employment Confederation (REC) and has a sustained focus on business improvement, as demonstrated by the recent achievement of ISO 9001 Quality Accreditation.
