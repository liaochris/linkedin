Michael Kawecki, LEED AP, GA’s Summary

With ten years of experience in design and construction, Michael has performed various roles, including architect, owner, contractor and consultant. He is a LEED Accredited Professional, Green Advantage Faculty, the 2007 Chair of the North Texas Chapter of the US Green Building Council, the 2008 Chair of the USGBC South Central Regional Council and the founder of Axiom Sustainable Consulting

Michael Kawecki, LEED AP, GA’s Specialties:

sustainable consulting, real-world application of LEED for both design and construction teams, educational seminars
