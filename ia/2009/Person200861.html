Robert Ortalda’s Summary

Robert Ortalda brings to MARSYS Group years over 20 years of experience as a private-company CFO and management consultant to CEOs. His client roster and employers include companies in fields such as video games, computer peripherals, software, Internet, computer services and consulting. Clients and employers have include Electronic Arts, Synchron Networks, YackPack, Saama Technologies, Mariner Systems (MARSYS), Thunderware, Citibank and Hewlett Packard / Agilent.

In those engagements, he has helped companies raise tens of millions of dollars in venture capital and angel funding. He has built, staffed and managed finance, accounting, analysis, administration, legal and HR functions.

Earlier, Ortalda was a partner in a Bay Area full-service firm of Certified Public Accountants. His initial public accounting experience was with the international firm of Hurdman and Cranstoun (now KPMG).

He wrote How to Live Within Your Means and Still Finance Your Dreams (Simon and Schuster, 1990) and has countless print, radio and television appearances to his credit.
