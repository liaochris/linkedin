Alma Cardi’s Summary

The diverse combination of communication and new technologies has been the core of my international career.

My current double role as manager in two, only apparently distant, sectors (events & web) in a leading no-profit company was conceived in the perspective of an integrated communication strategy. This role combines different channels with the aim to create strong synergies between the departments of the organisation itself and to strengthen the relationships with stakeholders and external networks.

In this perspective the concepts of 'event' and 'web' broaden their scope: an event becomes more than just a communication channel, it is a tool for strategic and decisional concertation. In the same way, the web becomes instrument for policy strategy and development.


Professional milestones in brief:

- Journalist for passion since ever and wherever (Italy, Germany and UK).

- Founder staff members of the Multimedia Department of the University of Tübingen. I intensively worked with web technologies to support institutional communication and specialised in the management of multimedia projects.

- German National Expert at the European Commission, DG Information Society (seconded by the Ministry for Science, Research and Culture of Baden-Württenberg). As Dissemination, Communication and Project Officer I represented the Technology Enhanced Learning and Culture Unit as keynote speaker and chair at numerous events around the world.

- Taking full advantage of the power of new technologies I am enjoying the international remote working: living again in my native Sardinia I carry on managing development projects for the Cambridge based CRAC.

Alma Cardi’s Specialties:

Technology Supported Communication, Institutional Communication, International Project Design & Management, Think Tank, Team Building and Virtual Management.
