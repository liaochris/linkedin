Phil Zulli’s Summary

Phil Zulli is a natural health advocate who has enjoyed 34 years of success in the direct sales industry. He quickly advanced from top salesman to regional manager and then national trainer. He later developed marketing tools for a wide variety of businesses, and has worked as a marketing and communication consultant to the nutritional network marketing industry for 17 years. He has also served as a corporate officer for two companies and was the company spokesman, trainer, and in-house marketing consultant for Seasilver Inc., a $100 million nutritional company.

Phil has hosted hundreds of national and international teleconferences and training events. He founded FeelGoodAgain.com in 1996 to educate people about holistic wellness, and his BuzzSystem™ support system and Speed-Duplication™ trainings have helped more than a hundred thousand people to be successful in home-based businesses.

Strongly focused on promoting natural health and persoanl growth, Mr. Zulli has instructed doctors and healthcare practitioners on incorporating nutritional supplements into their practice, and has counseled thousands of individuals and groups on optimal health, weight-control, and persoanl growth.
