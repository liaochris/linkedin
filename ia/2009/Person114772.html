Joanna Akins’s Summary

Joanna Akins is a Chartered Marketer and a member of the Chartered Institute of Marketing.

She is an Associate at Evonomie and a professionally qualified marketer with significant experience in marketing research and ebusiness.

Joanna Akins holds the Chartered Institute of Marketing's Professional Diploma in Marketing and is co-author of Quick Win Digital Marketing which looks at social networking and how businesses can take advantage of new media tools.

She has ten years’ experience as a researcher and recent research projects include:

- Researching the UK market for storage.
- Gathering and analysing competitor information on registered social landlords in the West Midlands.
- Researching opportunities for online businesses and creating web business models.

Prior to working at Evonomie, Joanna previously managed the business information library for Tarmac responding to enquiries throughout the Tarmac group including competitor, economic and market research, budget management, producing a weekly information bulletin.

Joanna also worked for Grant Thornton in the corporate finance department providing a company acquisition search service involving matching buyers with companies.

She has direct experience of selling online and ran her own ecommerce store selling video games consoles and accessories worldwide.
