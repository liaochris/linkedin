Wendy Rosen’s Summary

Founded in 1981, by Wendy Rosen, the Rosen Group provides American and Canadian artists with opportunity and guidance to grow their business and compete in a competitive marketplace.

The Buyers Market of American Craft, is the nation’s largest wholesale trade show for luxury merchandise. Held in Philadelphia, the Buyers Market offers artists the opportunity to create long-term relationships with over 12,000 galleries and dealers. www.AmericanCraft.com

Niche Magazine (1988) is a publication for art dealers. Each issue focus's on best practices and marketing strategies art galleries and retailers promoting contemporary American crafts. www.NicheMag.com

AmericanStyle is a lifestyle and arts travel magazine for art collectors and fans of high quality handmade objects. Each year cities compete to be included in AmericanStyle Top 25 Arts Destination edition. www.AmericanStyle.com

The American Made Alliance, founded by Rosen in 2005, works with congress, and state officials to create and enforce laws that promote, protect and preserve the American craft marketplace. www.AmericanMadeAlliance.org

Crafting an AmericanStyle, a PBS documentary, exploring the rich history of the Arts & Crafts movement. Rosen is the author of the bestselling book "Crafting as a Business"

Concerned about the lack of business education in the art departments of colleges and universities, Rosen developed a series of workshops to teach artists the art of studio management and marketing. Today, the Arts Business Institute produces workshops coast to coast throughout the US and Canada.

The Rosen Group is located in the Mill Centre, an artist studio complex developed by Rosen in 1986. Rosen's renovation of the abandoned civil war era cotton mill was the catalyst for the renaissance of the village of Hampden in Baltimore City. Hampden, the inspiration for Hairspray and other films by John Waters.

Wendy Rosen’s Specialties:

Luxury products and arts marketing. Made in America, Made in Canada, arts travel, education, arts economic and sustainable community development. Business educational workshops, magazine publishing, trade show management. Made in USA. Handmade in America, Wholesale Crafts, Craft Art, Fine Art.
