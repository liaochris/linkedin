Marvin D. LeBlanc, (LUTCF,CNP)’s Summary

Marvin is a left-handed, right brained, intense, fun-loving full blooded Cajun Man. Originally from Ascension parish, for 18 years he was a State Farm Agent in St. Bernard Parish, a town of 70,000 people.

All 27,000 homes were damaged in Hurricane Katrina. His life would never be the same. And he's all the better for it.

Handed the Keys to the City as an Honored Citizen for his work on the Citizens Recovery Commission, Marvin continues to focus -- NOT on Surviving the STORM. But on learning how to DANCE in the rain.

Marvin D. LeBlanc, (LUTCF,CNP)’s Specialties:

Marvin currently speaks all over the United States sharing his Katrina Story & his Life Lessons on overcoming adversity and striving toward SIGNIFICANCE, not just Success. His current CD's are being produced & his book, "The Original Significant Agent" is currently in process.
