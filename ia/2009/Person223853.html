Stephen Felt’s Summary

I have been originating mortgage and business loans since 1999 and educating the public and other financial professionals since 2005 on Financial Literacy topics.

My goals are to assist my clients in securing capital for their business needs and be a trusted adviser when they are in need of financing and cash flow as well as when they are researching a purchase or sale of a business. I like to follow the words of Dale Carnegie when he said "Take the time to find out what our clients want and spend the rest of the time helping them get it."

Stephen Felt’s Specialties:

Financial market and interest rate analysis, Cash flow analysis, Debt analysis, Real estate equity management, Real estate investment analysis, Business Valuations, Commercial Real Estate loans, Residential Real Estate loans, Financial Literacy Education
