Steve Gold’s Summary

From his base in Sheffield, England, Steve has been a business journo/tech writer for 25 years, 23 of them full-time. He has specialised in IT security, business matters, the Internet and communications for most of that time.

Steve is a regular contributor to:

Computer Weekly
E-Skills
Euromedia
The Guardian's Smarthealthcare.com web portal
IPTV Magazine
Mobile News
Infosecurity Today magazine - where he is technical editor
Screen Media Magazine
- and its daily newswire portal www.screens.tv

He is also a writer for Eskenzi PR (www.eskenzipr.com), Neil Stinchcombe and Yvonne Eskenzi's public relations firm, for whom he writes press releases, white papers and case studies.

& a little history...

Former news editor (for 12 years) for SC Magazine (www.scmagazine.com) and Info Security News, the magazine's online newswire service, Steve is also a former UK bureau chief (1984 to 2002) for Newsbytes News Network, one of the pioneering online newswire services, which he and his co-founders sold to the Washington Post.

Widely regarded as an authority on business matters, communications and IT security, he has launched and edited several UK trade publications, including Telelink and PC Dealer.

Despite this track record, Steve has no pretensions about what he does - he's just a hired hand that researches and writes reasonably well.

He sleeps in his spare time :)
