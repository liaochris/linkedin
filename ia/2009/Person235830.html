Thomas D. Elliott’s Summary

Tech-oriented entrepreneur with nine lives in nonprofit management, advocacy & lobbying, political campaigning, strategic communications, and start-up marketing and management.

I live with one foot in the public and one foot in the private sector, and love both equally. In a given day, I'll be strategizing on a bill in the NH legislature, balancing the business checkbook, writing an email to 200k geeks, and consulting on a website for a nonprofit.

I seek consulting or job opportunities that bring together some combination of my professional passions, skills, and goals.
