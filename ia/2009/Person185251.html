Peter Malvasi’s Summary

Senior sales and business development leader skilled in professional sales practices, team “turn arounds”, strategic relationship development in software, telecom and professional services businesses. Enterprise and service provider, large and medium markets, direct and channel models.

Among latest roles are Verizon Business leader for JDSU, Team Leader for Nortel's largest customer - Verizon Wireless, Investment Banker firm senior sales practice consultant, VP Sales BellSouth Wireless Data, VP Sales for leading IP connected Data Walls firm.

Achieved several major sales team successful "turnarounds", initiated sales and marketing plan for emerging IP firm, developed single target strategic account to $500M plus run-rate business from zero, implemented several sales practice and sales effectiveness improvement programs and was responsible for major advances and gains with senior customer executive relationships.

Increased reliability and accuracy of sales forecasting processes and implemented new, effective CRM systems with positive impact to sales and supply chain results.

Changed sales team “culture” with 3 teams over 10 years to embrace best sales practices, improve team synergies, improve win rate from sub 50% to over 70%, reduced team turnover by 40% in 3 years.

Took Northeast region and NYC sales teams from last place to 2nd in 18 months. Fortune 100 and NYC and Boston financial markets. Intiated pilot “Blackberry” sales and marketing program in 1998.

Several Business Development roles developing markets from no position to those with significant revenue. This included developing professional services business.

Expertise

High technology products, major software, telecom products, professional services. Solution selling skills, customer listening skills, team mentoring, complex problem solving vision and skills especially in difficult, obscure conditions.
