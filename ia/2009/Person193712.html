Randy Levine’s Summary

Over a decade in streaming media technology. I have been a founder and executive of several companies in the industry. I am in a unique position with the mergers I have done and deals I have put in place to have a direct view into a majority of the CDN's and ancillary streaming companies. As has been the case since I began, my goal is to further the streaming media industry by pushing the envelope in the technology and business deals we put in place. I also serve as an expert consulting to an investment bank on the streaming media industry where I speak to their partners, clients and analysts.
