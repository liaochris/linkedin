Alisa Bondurant’s Summary

Over 15 years progressively responsible experience in marketing, sales and communications for the public and private sector. As a Senior Workforce Consultant with the Idaho Department of Labor, I provide customers with the necessary tools and resources to successfully sell their personal brand on paper, in person and online.

Earlier this year I developed and now facilitate Maximizing Job Searching through Online Networking, a free, two-hour workshop designed to introduce job seekers to various online networking tools, like LinkedIn and Facebook, to enhance job searching efforts. It has been a rewarding experience to share these social media tools to individuals who would have not known about it otherwise. I am able to reach different populations, from people with disabilities and criminal backgrounds, day laborers, and the recently laid off to older workers, professionals and executives.

I also serve as the Marketing Coordinator for the Idaho Department of Labor Employer Association of Ada County, providing organizations the resources, tools and training, to help them run a more efficient, profitable and effective business. We provide five affordable trainings a year tailored to current business, workforce, and human resource challenges.

The other part of my time goes to helping job seekers find opportunities to sell their value on their resume and in an interview. I help them develop their personal brand or value proposition as it relates to solving the problem the business is facing.
