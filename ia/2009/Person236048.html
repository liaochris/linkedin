Tom Lamar’s Summary

Mr. Lamar is an experienced industrial designer, brand management consultant, founder, and patent holder, having participated in the formation, and leadership of a number of successful companies.

Mr. Lamar is an independent consultant serving the consumer goods industry, information technology, and the medical industry. He has recently formed Lamar Consulting, LLC, a company that specializes in forming new companies as well as helping emerging and established companies create new strategies, brands or update old ones to grow their businesses.

Mr. Lamar Also owns and operates a Sustainable Landscape Design Partnership; Highlands Nursery, LLC, Yardera, Inc. and Sustenance Design, LLC. Working in the metro Atlanta area with LEED certification, this collective is creatively changing the way property is managed for the long term benefit of the environment.
