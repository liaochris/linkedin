Satish Jha’s Summary

I got an opportunity to head a business function and later take charge of a business in my twenties and have been known as a business leader, relationship driven, coach, mentor and an organization leader who creates long term value while delivering in the short term as well.
In the various roles that I have had opportunities to be engaged in, I have dealt with complexity, ambiguity, managing change, leading teams and creating value.
I have also been associated with the healthcare business across various components – from providers to payers to pharmacy benefit managers.

Overall, early on in my career I got introduced to creating customer centric organizations and learnt and practiced strategic planning, both for my employers and clients, as a way to reach our goal.

I have helped my client companies design products, manage vendors, create a portfolio of products and services designed to keep them ahead of the competition and managed various components of businesses from process to technology and people

I have an MBA in strategy, innovation and technology management, have been in executive and consulting roles with opportunities to manage various pieces that the role of the President of Pharmacy demands, have strong quantitative and analytical skills, strategic, excellent communication, presentation and inter-personal skills with an established record of professionalism, innovation and integrity with high energy. Most of all I have been acknowledged as a good coach with a track record of developing exceptional leaders.
