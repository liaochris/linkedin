Robert Cook’s Summary

During my career, I have commanded thousands of incidents. I have responded to a wide range of natural and man-made disasters. I have developed response plans, evacuation plans, security plans for events and plans for corporate continuity. My expertise covers every aspect of emergency management (writing, planning, and executing) and I have a documented record of utilizing a multi-faceted set of skills to produce successful results. For example, I have…

● Responded to over 10,000 emergency calls and commanded several thousand
● Consulted with corporations, municipalities, agencies, schools, and hospitals
● Led and trained hundreds of fire fighters to become superior fire officers
● Functioned as department Information Officer/liaison with municipal authorities, the general public and the media
● Expert in Mass Casualty Incident Management and Incident Command including EOC operations and field command
● Lectured at regional and national conferences on Emergency Management
● Taken part in the nation’s largest emergency situation – the World Trade Center Twin Towers attack on 9-11
● Expert in planning, exercise and implementation of drills and incident mitigation
● Wide skill set in overall command of operations including strategy and logistics
● Vast experience in operational communications
● Long time experience in coordination with different transportation industries including rail lines, trucking and cargo vessels
● Participated on command level in several natural disaster response including snow, hurricane, flooding and extreme weather
● Event management and security

I can assist your organization to improve response, command operations, operational planning, disaster planning, safety and security and emergency preparedness as well as public relations and training. My business acumen will also play a vital role in the operational aspect as well as my knowledge of continuity of operations.
