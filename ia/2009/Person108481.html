Jeffrey Hartmann’s Summary

With a variety of Web-coding languages and a capacity to learn many more, I am eager to restart my career as a Website developer.

As a Website developer with ten years of experience, I am basically self-taught, both front-end and back-end. I learned most of my Active Server Page (ASP) skills -- both "classic" and ASP.NET -- "on the job".

I am comfortable with projects which allow users to find information, simplify communication or otherwise do their jobs easier. Toward that end, much of my work includes Websites driven by relational databases. For now I consider myself at a junior or middle level, always willing to learn new technology and grow as a Website developer.

Much of my work up to now has been solo Web coding -- front end and back end -- with some database work, leaving other duties such as networks, installations, server management and technical support to others. Nevertheless I am open to working within a genuine, supportive team environment.

Although my Website work is often intertwined with database work, I am willing to consider and discuss positions in either field separately. I am also open to either front-end or back-end Website work.

As of May 26, 2009, I now have a "demo" Website at http://www.jephmann.com -- admittedly rather small, it nonetheless leads with a few of my strengths. You may also follow me at twitter.com/jeffreyhartmann; I am very new there.

I am also willing to discuss permanent/full-time alternatives to a career in Website development, in the even that such a career might become a "side" job for me at best.

Above all, I am eager to further surpass my prior achievements and work hard to make a significant contribution to the future growth of your organization. In any event, I thank you for your consideration and time and I look forward to hearing from you soon.

(Above revised 6/22/2009)
