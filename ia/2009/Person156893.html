Maureen Carroll’s Summary

Headquartered in Toronto, Canada, Head2Head now has 30 staff members, 120+ contractors on-site with clients, and offices in Vancouver and Calgary. A recognized leader in providing insourced and outsourced recruiting solutions to small, medium and large organizations across Canada, Head2Head was the first - and continues to be the only - recruiting company in Canada specializing in providing contract recruiters as an insourced solution to recruiting challenges. Over the past eight years, in response to client demand, this model has evolved into a wider range of RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourcing) solutions.

Head2Head provides extensive consulting, training and VMS (Vendor Managed Services). We are also very proud to launch the largest Canadian online recruitment portal, Recruiter2Recruiter.

Almost 70% of Canadian HR and recruiting professionals say that recruiting is more difficult than it was 18 months ago. Traditional 'head hunting' just isn't delivering results, leaving many organizations without sufficient capacity or capability to win the war for top talent.

That's where Maureen comes in.

Maureen's passion for delivering great Candidate Experiences means that when the best recruitment, HR, supply chain and IT professionals are thinking of making a change, they call Head2Head first. Thanks to Maureen and her 9-person sourcing and recruitment team, Head2Head has a reputation for delivering more A-list candidates, to more Fortune 500 organizations, than any other specialized recruiting company in Canada.

Since 2007, Maureen has transformed Head2Head's sourcing and recruitment team by developing and implementing detailed metrics models - which in turn has helped Head2Head grow by more than 25% annually.

Maureen Carroll’s Specialties:

Committed to long-term CRM (Candidate Relationship Management), Maureen specializes in sourcing, screening and assessing recruitment professionals, and leads Head2Head's unique recruitment certification designation, ARC (Associate Recruitment Consultant).

She also specializes in the design and implementation of sourcing and recruitment-related metrics, both internally (Head2Head employees) and externally (Head2Head clients and candidates).
