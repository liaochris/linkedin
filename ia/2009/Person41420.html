Charles Patrick Garcia’s Summary

Currently provide capital raising and merger & acquisition advisory services for companies focused on the global Hispanic market. My clients are both companies that offer products and services to the Hispanic market and companies that are controlled by Hispanic management teams seeking institutional investors. We focus on middle market Hispanic companies in the media, healthcare, financial services, consumer products, food & beverage, technology, real estate, and entertainment space.

Charles Patrick Garcia’s Specialties:

Services include public and private fundraising, and advisory services such as IPOs, private placements, private equity funding, M&A, Recaps, Fairness Opinions, assistance with business plans and board of directors formation, and Valuation Analysis.
