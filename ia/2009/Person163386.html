Todd Jungling’s Summary

Possess a unique combination of business skills, leadership qualities and a passion for educating. Have a proven track record of improving processes and leading teams. Strong analytical, organizational and communication skills; able to motivate and inspire.

It is the unrelenting focus on business objectives that differentiates us from “traditional” web designers. The bottom line is not just getting a website, but rather, getting a website that works. An Internet Solution is an investment – and with all investments you should consider not just the initial cost – but, more importantly, the return that your investment will bring. With an MBA from Michigan State University and several years of experience working for two of the largest retailers in the world (Lowe’s Companies, Inc. and Kmart Corp.) within their finance organizations, we understand the importance of providing solutions that work – those that increase revenues, decrease costs and streamline operations.
