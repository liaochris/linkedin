Marcie Steerman’s Summary

At the Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory, our engineers and scientists make unparalleled contributions toward solving the most critical challenges facing our country and her allies – most notably in the areas of national security and space systems. As the Laboratory’s manager for business communications, I help tell their story.

I lead branding and creative efforts, including both in-house talent and external vendors, in the generation of brochures, fact sheets, posters, presentations, multi-media and exhibits for the Laboratory. Since joining the APL, I have focused our team on:

>> establishing a new branding identity in accordance with strategic direction;
>> lowering development & printing costs by unifying our look, creating templates and batch printing; and
>> developing an on-line, multi-media tool kit for APL staff members

Now, we’re centering our efforts on bringing together the various communications efforts at the Laboratory to reflect our enterprise orientation, and we’re reaching out to our fellow research institutions to discover best communication practices in our community.
