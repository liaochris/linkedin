Dr. Jim Taylor’s Summary

Dr. Jim Taylor offers the highest quality customized consulting services to a select group of individuals and organizations in business, parenting, and sports committed to maximizing their abilities and achieving their goals. Dr. Taylor provides individuals and organizations with the psychological and leadership, and team insights, information, and tools that will enable them to perform at their highest level consistently and find meaning, fulfillment, and contentment in their lives.

Dr. Taylor brings over twenty-three years of consulting-psychology experience to his work. Initially focusing on athletic performance, Dr. Taylor has worked with world-class and professional athletes who have won Olympic and World Championship medals, set world records, won tennis and golf titles, and excelled in many sports. With an emerging international reputation, corporate organizations began inviting Dr. Taylor to apply his unique principles in assisting them in improving their individual and organizational performances.

Dr. Taylor focuses on solving "real-life" problems and provides information and skills to master the individual and organizational challenges that high-achieving people face in today's competitive world. Dr. Taylor is committed to producing meaningful and long-lasting positive change his clients' lives.

Dr. Taylor offers a comprehensive package of services that addresses psychological, interpersonal, leadership, and organizational needs. His programs are comprised of individual consultations, small-group seminars, and large-group workshops directed toward teaching people what they need to achieve their goals.

To learn more about the specific business, parenting, and sport programs that Dr. Taylor offers, please refer to the Services links at www.drjimtaylor.com.

Problem solving and decision making. Crisis mastery. Performance enhancement. Team culture development.
