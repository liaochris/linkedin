Alexander Demidov’s Summary

I have experience in general project management, through use of simple tools and motivation plans as well as regular control of specialists’ work.

The main focus of the company is investments in the commercial real estate development and fee-development in Russian regions. Typical IRR or preferred interest we offer to investors 13-20%.
I am responsible for all finance aspects of the projects, the team recruitment and project management (i.e. combining and coordinating of all different threads of the projects – finance, project design, construction, legal and civil work, marketing and tenant mix, etc.).


There were just 5 individuals involved at start of this project and in the company as a whole. Later we had grown to a 100-strong team, managing 10 projects. At the current stage we have dedicated construction managers for supervising working design and constriction works and other aspects are controlled by matrix-organized team. I am focused on project progress reporting to the board on a monthly basis and liaising between the investor and developer on the process of projects.



We also manage our own consumer electronic assembly and integration plant (1-2 million CRT-TV, LCD, DVD, etc. a year) and will be happy to provide our professional services to brands.

The group also manages - 33 thousand hectares of land at a distance of 50 to 100 km from Moscow, in the southern part of the greater Moscow region. The purpose of the project is to develop and dispose of the land over the period of 3 years in order to realize capital gains for current and prospective investors.
Conservatively, over a 3-year period, we expect the investor to achieve a pre-tax IRR of 40-70 %.
