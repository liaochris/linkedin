Sean Black’s Summary

As part of Trulia’s founding management team, Sean plays a critical role in Trulia's overall business strategy, growth, and development. Sean helped take Trulia from five people with an idea to the “Fastest Growing Real Estate Website" (Comscore) with 75+ employees and over 5+ Million monthly unique users growing at double digits each month. During Sean’s management Trulia raised $33 Million from preeminent venture capital firms Accel Partners, Deep Fork Capital and Sequoia Capital, the original investors in Google, Yahoo, Paypal and Apple Computer to name a few.

Before joining Trulia, Sean was Vice President, top-producing broker and member of the Multi-Million Dollar Club at The Corcoran Group, the largest real estate firm in New York City.

Sean’s prior business experience has taken him all over the world. Sean's first venture backed start-up was online car insurance company Everymile.com. Prior to that Sean started Europro, a wholesaler of nutritional supplements, with offices in Paris, Brussels and Rome. Sean successfully sold the company to its management team in 1999.

Between 1997 and 1999, Sean developed new business & investment opportunities for the Franciscans at the Vatican in Rome.
