Wiebke Thrasher’s Summary

As a transplant from Hamburg, Germany, I have gained my global marketing experience from working across a range of categories - from cars to cosmetic to wireless to consumer electronics.

I have worked on brands such as BMW (MINI), Hewlett-Packard, U.S. Cellular, eBay, SanDisk, American Red Cross, to name a few.

A strong believer in the power of integrated campaigns, my efforts have contributed to two EURO Gold Effies, one Gold Effie and a Gold Cyber Lion - one of only four awarded to US agencies in 2008.
