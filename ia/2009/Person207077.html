Jerry King’s Summary

Over 14 years experience in design, development and deployment of software applications, both from an engineering organization perspective and an IT organization perspective. Five years architecting and leading SharePoint initiatives for customers in the areas of implementation, portal design and data migration within SPS 2003 and MOSS 2007. Industry experience includes oil & gas, utilities, financial, education and healthcare. Roles and responsibilities have encompassed project and team leadership, project execution methodologies, business case studies, test plan design, and training material development and delivery.

Over the past few years I have been involved with two new business ventures, the first being a partner in Woodlands Technology which was sold to New Energy Associates in 2001 and the second being FLEX.net which was sold to private investors in early 2007.
