Sudarsan Vempati’s Summary

I am a JD Edwards functional consultant / analyst. After having various stints in
Supply Chain Management at Reckitt Benckiser, I worked as an in-house functional JDE resource for Worldsoft implementation in the Distribution module.
I moved to ERP Consulting, had a short assignment in Riyadh, then worked for one of the largest JDE implementations at Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited, Mumbai through a Big 5 Consulting company (Capgemini Ernst & Young).
I later on moved to Al Jaber Group, Abu Dhabi -a conglomerate of Contracting / Projects, Trading, Manufacturing companies to implement JDE - Distribution, Job-Costing fully in-house.
Currently, I am working with Al Tayer Group, Dubai - a conglomerate of Retail, Automotive, Real-estate companies as Business Systems Analyst - JD Edwards.

Sudarsan Vempati’s Specialties:

Business Analysis, As-Is and future state definition, JDE modules: Sales, Inventory, Procurement, Job-costing, Service / Warranty Management (CSMS), Bulk-Stock Management; Conducting CRP / ICRP, simulation of go-live, post-live support.
I held roles as Project Manager, Team-lead in systems evaluation, defining metrics, mapping KPIs, processes; defining standards, documentation templates, Standard Operating Procedures (SOP); Supply Chain Management in a multinational FMCG.
