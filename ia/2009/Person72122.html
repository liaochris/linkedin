Ellie Wellwood’s Summary

I encourage people to discover new possibilities allowing them more freedom to fully be themselves so they can live happier, more fulfilling lives both personally and professionally.

My style is faith based from the heart. I take a holistic approach coaching the whole person; body, mind and spirit. I coach my clients by telephone making it convenient for anyone, anywhere. I've coached clients all over the US.

Contact me for a complimentary 30-minute consultation to evaluate if we are an ideal coach/client match. ellie@newdirectionscoach.com or 845-452-1139.
