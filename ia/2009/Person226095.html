Sue Tupling’s Summary

Changeworks Communications is a provider of internal marketing, leadership communication and public relations. We will improve business performance and help to make change work by helping organisations communicate more effectively. This increases employee motivation and engagement, maximises sales and retention opportunities and helps to improve business performance.

Our philosophy is to offer high level expertise at unbeatable value. Offering public relations, leadership communications and internal marketing for medium to large organisations in the business to business sector, we work hard to build and manage your reputation internally and externally. We listen to your business objectives and create a strategic communication plan using the press and online media, driven by bringing your competitive advantage to life and guided by a strong messaging framework.

Effective communication begins on the inside, so we work to connect your strategy to a powerfully communicated vision or intention, and then provide leadership on behavioural outcomes using appropriate and creative communication channels.

Our clients include: Briggs Equipment/Caterpillar; National Skills Academy for Manufacturing; Stafford College;Staffordshire University; Staffordshire Wildlife Trust; Science, Engineering, Manufacturing and Technology Alliance (SEMTA); Cooper Specialised Handling Ltd; Brandauer Ltd.

Specific Projects
- Developing & executing PR programmes to effect perceptual brand change.
- Developing internal communications that are integrated across multiple media and targeted for effective behavioural change.
- Developing and implementing behavioural change programmes for strategic and cultural change inititatives.
- Coaching marketing teams, senior managers and key decision makers.
- Advising and consulting on strategic branding and change initiatives.
- University lecturing for the CIM's Professional Postgraduate Diploma in Marketing.
