Rick Matthews’s Summary

We are in the process of taking a mass-market fractional ownership business model to market. I am interested in networking with good people or businesses that desire to own a special asset they have always wanted but cannot justify the cost of sole ownership. Such assets include:
Private Aircraft
Recreational Boat
RV
Classic Cars, Rods, & Sports Cars
Beach Cottage
Mountain Cabin
Horse
Historical Preservation of an asset or a site.
...and combinations of the above.

Am also keenly interested in discussions with key people regarding the business itself as a career or investment.

Rick Matthews’s Experience

  • Founder / CEO

    Fractional Assets, Inc.

    (Recreational Facilities and Services industry)

    April 2006 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    Mr. Matthews founded and funded this company with a trusted and dedicated partner to bring forward all experiences and lessons learned about Fractional Ownership then known, to create an pioneering and unique business model to provide fractional ownership opportunities of desirable commodities to a mass market, so that people can finally enjoy ownership in something they have always dreamed of, which otherwise they may not ever get to have.

    Now expanding into fractional boats and national historic sites, they will follow region by region with aircraft (private and business), beach cottages, mountain cabins, RVs, hot cars, and horses.

    Mr. Matthews has become somewhat of an SME (Subject Matter Expert) on FO for a mass market, frequently being asked to speak on FO in front of various groups to promote the concept to fill the gap between renting something and owning it alone. The Company's goal is to be on the "Inc.500" list of fastest growing private companies in America.

    Fractional Assets will entertain the possibility of a financial partner / investor to help it achieve rapid growth.

    Fractional Assets also enjoys speaking to passionate and qualified professionals about being an employee-partner to help us provide solutions to problems in various recreational asset industries.
