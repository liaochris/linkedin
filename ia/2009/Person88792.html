Graeme Boyce’s Summary

As a corporate strategist, and over a successful 25+ year career in the management of people, companies and brands, Graeme Boyce as a skilled communicator has been bridging the gap between investors and new ventures recently, while assisting to take several ventures public. As a senior consultant with Authors Canada, Boyce – effectively described as an Agent of Change – enjoys and is capable of delivering goals based on sound business principles and proven marketing plans, whether developing or executing for an entrepreneur or corporation … or writing movie scripts for Civilized Productions and new stories (murder mysteries) for the popular CSI board game.

Graeme Boyce’s Specialties:

Boyce connects. On several levels, Boyce writes, talks and assesses talent - and has taught advanced Integrated Marketing and Small Business Administration courses. Occasionally, these days Boyce volunteers his time to give Career Days seminars in high schools, as well as lead Music Industry and Advertising Industry discussions for homeless youth in Toronto at the Yonge Street Mission, while also delivering motivational workshops.
