Deborah Hoard’s Summary

As experts in Marketing Communications program management, we specialize in preventing loss of revenue and market share by transforming overdue, over-budget, or under-resourced MarCom programs into on-time, on-budget, and on-target successes.

Our clients recognize us as the “go-to” specialists who produce complex marketing communications programs — such as brand initiatives, Web site creation, animated and interactive marketing tools, videos, e-marketing campaigns, and product launch materials — that further their business objectives and maximize their budget and resources.

With access to a virtual team of experts, we can provide for all of our clients' MarCom needs: from stategy to creative to production to delivery.

We have established a proven track record of consistently delivering powerful results for top companies such as Apple Computer, Cisco Systems, Inc., Informatica Corporation, and Oracle Corporation, Inc.

For more information, visit our website at www.get-net-resutls.com

Hello from Deborah:

Let's face it, not many people want to manage all the day-to-day details of creating and producing a marketing program. The challenges of keeping the team on schedule, obtaining approvals, working with all the personalities, and managing the review cycles to ensure the key marketing messages are carried through. Call me crazy, but I think all that is fun!

For almost 20 years, I've managed complex MarCom projects for many of the leading Silicon Valley companies. It's been challenging, fun, and highly entertaining. And I can't wait to do it all again.
