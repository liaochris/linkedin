Branding + Strategy + Design Company.

+

Our fresh-thinking, envelope-pushing, idea-rich artistes produce amazing, results-oriented, value-added work within budget without one iota of attitude or BS. This is how we think business should be done.

+

We've also incorporated fresh thinking into our own brand, partnering with New York agent Joe Lombardo / Artists and Creatives to rep us.

  • Founder / President

    Soffer Adkins

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2001 — 2008 (7 years)

    Soffer Adkins stood front and center as an award winning, rising star in the advertising business. The company had many years of great success and was populated with the most wonderful group of insanely talented people who all worked together toward a common purpose--great work for great clients. The company was like a family - home to friends who loved each other and enjoyed working together. We knew we could do anything. And we did. However, our constant eye on the changing marketplace made us realize that we needed to shift to remain relevant. From this, The Soffer Collec+ive was born, a new iteration that has maintained all the greatness of Soffer Adkins but has its hands on the pulse of how business is currently done in a hugely competitive and rapidly evolving marketplace. With love and respect to our former Soffer Adkins pals-- we say onward and upward!
