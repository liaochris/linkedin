Carol Lynn Pieper’s Summary

Carol Lynn Pieper is the owner of 2nd Level Skin, the mother of three and a widow. She has been in the industry since 1988. Carol Lynn is a stage IV breast cancer survivor, and she understands all too well the effects of cancer, about women’s health issues, and the body’s appearance without breasts.

She is a passionate advocate promoting cancer awareness and identifying options when diagnosed with cancer within her Denver-Metro community, and across the country. She has participated as a public speaker on bereavement and recovery at various local organizations and charity events. Ms. Pieper has sat on the board of directors for Breast Friends and is active in the Susan G. Koman “Race for the Cure”.
