Norman M. Richards’s Summary

While performance and music has always been a main motivator for me, Early in my career, I discovered industries that were tied to my background. I enjoyed seven years in radio broadcasting where I made programs, wrote advertising and sold it, produced news, introduced ideas to make radio better and shared in the excitement of being part of it.

In between broadcast jobs I hired into aerospace manufacturing at Bristol Aerospace then a division of Rolls Royce. For five years I was contract administrator and senior staff member for the company's largest component manufacturing and structure assembly under contract with Lockheed Aircraft Company, Los Angeles, California. You could not get better work experience than this.

Norman M. Richards’s Specialties:

Selling ideas, products or programs I believe in. Negotiation of these beliefs to bring to reality and enhance others lives. I continue to write, produce and develop story for film, television and other media.
