Steve Graham’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur and web designer specialising in Wordpress websites. Focussing on the key business objectives, my aim for all clients is to ensure that they derive measurable and sustainable direct results that drive business growth.

Every website remains in the control of the client from inception to delivery and beyond. The advantage of using content managed systems, such as Wordpress, is that clients can add, update, edit and delete information as, and when, they deem necessary.

With key social marketing and social networking accounts integrated from the outset, every client has the opportunity to run their own highly effective online marketing strategy from day one.

As a long-standing presentation and networking skills Coach, I am able to bring a different perspective to the messages that clients are delivering in the online world. Relationship marketing is key to success in the 21st century and having an understanding of how effective networks can be, whether they are online or offline is essential to the success of almost all businesses.

By providing full training I am able to ensure that all my clients can achieve the maximum potential from their new website and are confident in the use of social networking for business.
