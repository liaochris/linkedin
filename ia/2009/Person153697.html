Martin Dowson’s Summary

ENABLING ENTREPRENEURS to do what they do best:-

I am currently working with three entrepreneurs supporting them and their businesses. My role is to enable the entrepreneur(s) at the head of the business to free up their time to do the things they are best at. I do this by identifying the gaps in the business operations and development plans, creating the internal team and external business partnerships that are needed to take the business forward.

Where I can lend my particular skills around customer experience and proposition development I do, but my main function is to build the business and the team such that everyone is doing what they are best at.

I tend to work with entrepreneurs who share common passions with me notably environmental concerns, personal/professional and educational development and social entrepreneurialism.

------------

I have a specific background and my own consultancy in
CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE:-

Before forming Sinclair Experience Ltd I was a founding member of Accenture's Interactive Design Group, a gaggle of user experience experts evangelising the importance of placing the customer at the centre of the design process.

My background in Psychology and HCI (Edinburgh) led me to work on crafting compelling customer experiences for many exciting and award winning consumer and business focussed sites.

I now spend most of my consulting time coaching my clients to develop design processes that involve proposition development, user-centred requirements gathering, information architecture, interaction design, branding and usability testing to step change the quality and value they derive from their projects.
