Nathan Hayes’s Summary

Nathan Hayes is a project manager who's experienced in a variety of projects from product release and software development to IT infrastructure. Nathan has a broad technical knowledge and understanding of Project Methodologies combined with a passion for excellence. He is an excellent leader with the skills and training to take a project team through to completion. He has experience handling multiple projects within a program of projects. His ability to remain calm even in stressful situations makes him effective in managing crisis situations. He has experience with cradle to grave project management, including building SOWs and budgets, creating and maintaining schedules, and managing multiple cross-functional teams on projects.

Nathan has experience in the Energy/Utilities, Telecom, Wireless, and Financial industries. He has worked on Software Delivery, IT Infrastructure, and Product Launch projects.

As a child of a military family I have traveled all over the world. My experiences have given me the unique ability to be able to communicate effectively with people at all levels of busines; from executives in the boardroom to service reps on the front line.
