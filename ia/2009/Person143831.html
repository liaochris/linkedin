Jacobs, John’s Summary

I have worked in roles ranging from IT Director, Lead Architect, Enterprise Software Architect, Senior Solutions Architect, Lead Developer, Tech Team Manager and Senior Engineer with 14 years of architectural experience and 22 years of development experience (yes, I started young!).
My expertise and experience has largely been in the specification, architecture, design and development of large-scale enterprise and technical applications for Microsoft Windows platforms.

My extensive and diverse practical experience is based on a sound academic background. I have a First class bachelors degree with honors in Computer Science followed by accredited on-the-job training, and have been an ongoing member of various professional bodies since graduation.

I have had full project life-cycle experience and operated in a number of roles internationally. The roles I have undertaken range from enterprise systems architecture solutions architecture and as lead architect, customer-facing consulting, team leading and managing complex technical development projects, business requirements analysis, technical project management, technical and functional specification, software design, development, testing, release and support.

I have worked within large corporations, small to medium sized enterprises including start-ups, and as a freelance consultant, managing my own software consultancy business for over 7 years.

Articulate and probing, I am proficient at presenting at the board level as well as gathering and exploring requirements with business heads.

I have specific practical experience in sectors as diverse as finance (retail, investment and mortgage banking, insurance and reinsurance), internet applications and services, digital media, risk management, and software models (e.g. natural disasters, financial markets).
