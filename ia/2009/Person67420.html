Keely Killpack, PhD’s Summary

I am a seasoned consultant with background in organizational change, strategy & implementation, workforce transformation, coaching, training, communications, leadership development, management, competency models & framework, work motivation, and organizational design. I enjoy working with mature leaders, adapt quickly to new environments/industries, and will travel anywhere for a project worth my participation.

My talents are in helping leadership make decisions and implement changes that will excel employee performance, streamline processes, and appeal to shareholders and profitability. Visit my website for more: www.killpackgroup.com.

I am passionate about transforming the world's leading companies into green-centric operations that will significantly reduce the current levels of greenhouse gas and demand for energy. I have a proven track record of successful employee behavior modification and services to offer any company interested in becoming a participant in the green marketplace.

More personally; I am funny, energetic, detail-oriented, highly analytical, a keen observer, and a wellness oriented individual. I am a native Oregonian who loves the outdoors and a person who can relate to people in all ranks of business.

Keely Killpack, PhD’s Specialties:

My industries of specialty include; Energy & Oil, Travel & Transportation, Health Care Payers, Real Estate, HIgh Technology Global Giants, Legal firms, Small & Medium-sized boutique companies, Family-Owned businesses and Non-Profit Health Organizations.
