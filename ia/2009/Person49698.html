Courtenay Gysland’s Summary

I have high level strategic management consultancy background as PEO / Managing Consultant of a boutique strategic enterprise engineering services firm for the past 20 years!

My recent experience IS NOT at technical implementation level, but as a director / manager / mentor of technologists / managers! I am a registered Microsoft, EMC & Cisco Partner with additional significant exposures to transport & supply infrastructure / logistics as well as considerable ICT & technology infrastructure.

My first university experience, although not to degree level nor put into business practice, was 2 1/2 years of physical metallurgy engineering at Colorado School of Mines. I undertook further tertiary study whilst serving in the U.S. Navy, which included 1 year of business contract law at Memphis State University & independent study of oceanography to complement my undersea focus on anti-submarine warfare systems.

I became a naturalised Australian citizen in November, 1974. I have been deeply involved with infrastructure & program / project management [based on PMI's PMBoK for the past 15+ years] including substantial banking industry exposures, including clearings, with 4 banks directly and others as a vertical specialist with a vendor. My finance / insurance / airline / industrial marketing EDI / trading room technology clients over the years have been mainframe centric, so I have accumulated substantial experience dealing in this sector.

As a consultant, I undertook change management exercises through Forum Consulting Group using QA / TQM (the base philosophy which has led to both ITIL & 6-Sigma) as the vehicle supported by a an additional custom-developed competitive strategy workshop program based on Harvard Business School MBA subject..
