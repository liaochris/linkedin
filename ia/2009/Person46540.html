Claire Rottenberg’s Summary

I am the owner of a small, independent company that produces and sells ebooks for users of Macintosh computers. I am also the author of the ebooks and I currently have ten ebooks for users of Mac OS X 10.5 (Leopard) and 10.4 (Tiger). I also write and publish tips, tutorials and articles on Mac OS X.

In my spare time, I enjoy creating Automator and AppleScript programs and designing websites. In addition to my business websites, I have a website and blog about movie musicals, a new blog on President-elect Obama's new administration and a blog on world cultures.
