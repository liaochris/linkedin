Steven Lubetkin, APR, Fellow, PRSA’s Summary

Experienced, award-winning producer of business audio and video podcasts. Podcasts produced for NAPL (www.napl.org), graphic communications management industry trade association, won a 2008 JASPER Award from the Jersey Shore Public Relations and Advertising Association (www.jspraa.com).

Frequent speaker on social media at industry conferences, schedule at http://www.lubetkin.net/speaking.htm.

Clients: ACE-INA, NJ Academy of Family Physicians, NAPL, AICPCU, National Association of Realtors, NJ Bankers Association, Peter Shankman, president of The Geek Factory, PRSA, Rutgers University.

Corporate photojournalist and videographer w/decades of national photo credits including six cover photos of Philadelphia area CEOs for Smart Business Magazine. Public photo portfolio is at http://www.flickr.com/photos/lubetkin/.

Senior Fellow and Advisory Board member of the Society for New Communications Research (http://sncr.org/?p=81) Experienced technologist, counselor to senior management regarding the business and commercial implications of leading edge technologies. MBA thesis (1994), “Bond Ratings in Cyberspace,” was a seminal study of the attitudes of financial industry professionals toward "groupware" computer-mediated meeting technologies -- the "social media" of the early 1990s. Fluent in Spanish.

Host of three blogs and podcasts:

Blogs/Podcasts
"Lubetkin's Other Blog" - Commentary and interviews on public relations and journalism issues, with people making news in the PR and journalism worlds. http://feeds.feedburner.com/lubetkinsotherblog

"CompuSchmooze" - Enhances monthly newspaper column about technology and Judaism in the Jewish Community voice of Southern New Jersey (www.jewishvoicesnj.org, www.compuschmooze.com, or via RSS feed at http://feeds.feedburner.com/compuschmooze).

"Middle Chamber Books Podcast" - supports Middle Chamber Book Store (www.middlechamberbooks.com) with podcast interviews of authors and other creative artists.

Steven Lubetkin, APR, Fellow, PRSA’s Specialties:

Extensive experience in podcast production, creating compelling B2B audio and video programs distributed via podcast technologies. Fluent in applying broadcast quality production techniques to podcasting, ensuring high quality production values.

Keen understanding of corporate financial communications, organizational communications, executive (speechwriting) communications, and internal communications issues.
