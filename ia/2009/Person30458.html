Bob Oliver, Jr.’s Summary

OS-TECH is a provider of specialty chemicals, equipment, services, & consulting to industrial manufacturer. We also have the capability to custom formulate products to solve a problem or can toll-blend formulations for companies that do not want to setup mixing tanks or have to deal with all the government regulations.

Currently, we are involved in the following areas (but can also work in others as the need arises):

* Printed Circuit Boards
* Metal Finishing - Electroplating, Anodizing, Powder Coating, E-Coating
* Metalworking - Coolants, Lubricants, Drawing, Grinding, Stamping, & Extruding Lubes, Quench Oils
* Waste Treatment Chemicals, Haul-off, & Systems
* Environmental Consulting & Training
* Paint & Coating Strippers
* Rack Strippers
* Metalworking Coolants & Lubricants
* Pipe-Cutting Lubricants
* Metal Drawing Lubricants
* Food-Service Specialty Chemicals
* Custom Formulating & Toll-Blending
