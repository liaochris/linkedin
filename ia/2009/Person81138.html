Frank Frank’s Summary

President of a small printed circuit board design service and a budding specialty software provider. My goals include helping the electronics industrey with standardization methods that will assist companies to meet time-to-market goals, and creating a more efficient way to design and implement electronic creations.

I have over 30 years in the design industry, with over 10 of those years serving in the service industry as a company owner. My company has helped large corporations to streamline the design process. Some of our customers include Nation Semiconductor (www.national.com/cad for mutual data) Plexus, Raytheon, Cadence, Mentor Graphics, Cisco and many others.

Our software has been customized to meet the needs of many of the above companies. Additionally we have in-house experience with many differnet types of PCB's (printed circuit boards) that include high volumen, multi layer (as many as 65) high speed (as fast as 3g) and auto placement and routing.

My specific current goal is to take our experience in the design industry and the Ultra Librarian software we have created over the last 8 years and help make it a "standard" for delivering required engineering information to electronics designers.

Frank Frank’s Specialties:

I have run most CAD design tools, such as Allegro, Expedition, BoardStation, Pads, Orcad, PCAD, etc. In addition I am known for my ability to "customize" the tool for my own needs, or that of my customers.

I write software in several languages, and have created and support our company web site and email systems.

I have experience in creating and running a small PCB prototype lab, as well as other the machinery required by such an undertaking.
