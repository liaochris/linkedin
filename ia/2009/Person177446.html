Neil Willis’s Summary

After a couple of years at corporate communication specialists AB Publishing's design studio - subbing, scanning, designing, passing pdfs and generally getting into everything - I worked in, and then ran a team of AB people based in Barclays' head office. Then, to broaden my experience, I decided to go freelance. That, in a nutshell, is where I am now.

I can handle all things subbing, design and prodution. I can take projects from conception through to the final finished product - including managing budgets, staff and timescales - or just be an extra pair of hands when you are busy. I've managed to convince companies such as The British Red Cross, Incisive Media, Emap, Reed and The Guardian that I'm worth having around on a regular basis. Perhaps I can do the same for you?

See my website at www.nwillisn1.co.uk for more details.

Neil Willis’s Specialties:

Software skills: InDesign, PhotoShop, Illustrator, Acrobat; Quark Xpress; Fireworks and Dreamweaver; MSOffice; Televisual (Emap); Hermes (News International); Octopus (Guardian/Observer); various FTP uploaders.

Hardware skills: Scanners; WACOM tablets; Mac os9, osX and PC platforms.

Other skills: subbing; planning and scheduling workloads; team management - including appraisals; budget control; client liaison/handling; paper engineering, working with illustrators and sourcing print quotes.
