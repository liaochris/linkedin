Sheri Hebbeln’s Summary

Partner Relations Manager, Winery Direct Sales Evangelist and Customer Advocate skilled at Product Marketing, Project Management, Business Development, Customer Support and User Experience

I believe in always going the extra mile, something made easy by the fact that I love what I do. For the past ten years I have been active in ecommerce. My passion is to seek out and discover new ways for using technology to deliver untapped revenue channels to the wine industry. My experience is in using ecommerce and social media to help wineries build direct relationships with their customers. I thrive when working with a strong customer-focused and collaborative team that understands the importance of community and giving back.

I live in Napa with my husband and two teenage children.

Sheri Hebbeln’s Specialties:

Online Strategy, Winery Direct Sales, Product Marketing, Business Development, Financial Management, Partner Relations, Product Development

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    February 2008 — November 2008 (10 months)

    Inertia Beverage Group brought a new vision to the wine industry – the simple but powerful concept that technology could be used to increase consumer direct sales and reduce dependency on traditional channels. Inertia’s web-based technology enables wineries to connect directly with customers through an ever-expanding set of untapped retail and trade channels. I was fortunate enough to be made part of this incredible team in early 2008 and continue to be amazed by the unwavering focus of each and every individual on that original vision. My roles have included partner relations, product development, and marketplace services among others, and my ambition is to continue that focus by exploring new avenues for producers and consumers to connect.
