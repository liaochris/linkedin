Karen Lyu’s Summary

PERFORMING ARTIST & HOLISTIC VOICE COACH
Transform your voice and Express your soul! Karen's holistic and personalized voice coaching has improved the voices of over 450 students - from absolute beginners to untrained professional singers, speakers, life coaches, and actors. Karen has a BSS in Jazz Studies - Vocal Performance, studied with noted voice instructors such as Roberta Davis (Berklee School of Music) and Mira Kehoe (Guthrie Theater), and has sung for Dr. Oren Brown (Juillard School).

Karen is currently performing in Tennessee with the Pacha Mamas vocal trio and jazz pianist Anthony Belfiglio (Branford Marsalis). Since 1993, she has also sung with Global Jazz, Devon Evans (Bob Marley), Soli Hughes (Sounds of Blackness), Curly Martin (Etta James), Karen Lyu Quartet, Bomba, Via Brasil, Eddie Piccard Trio, Robert Robinson's MCC Gospel Choir, vocal jazz group Jazz Transit, and as a solo vocalist, at restaurants, festivals, conferences, theaters, schools, churches, parks, wedding receptions, and events. Her music has been featured on NPR, MPR, Democracy Now, film, TV, radio and cable. Karen is a MN State Arts Board grant recipient. For 2 years, Karen served as the Executive Director of the West Bank School of Music.
