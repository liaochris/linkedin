Ishtiaq Saddam’s Summary

Back in 2004 I joined ICCB, as the Corporate Protocol for His excellency Dr. Pascal Lamy (Director General WTO) for an entire conference.

After that, I got myself in GP in their CMD as a CM and worked there in various sub units. I was first assigned in their IR Unit's Call Center and later assigned as one of the first 11 people who started the Service Desk. I was transferred first to a remote location in Kushtia where I had to set up the office of the CMSD. After 2 months of around the clock service all was running smooth.Later for that success, I was transferred to Norshingdi to do a similar job. After setting up that I was again recalled back to Dhaka and was duly assigned under the BDR Unit of the same division.

I got selected for a permanent position in the Commercial SME Division. I joined in there as a Key Account Manager & was the guy to sell Business Solutions for the first time ever in GP & Bangladesh.

After leaving Gp in mid of May 2007 I joined Easter Bank Ltd. under the department of Business Analysis & MIS where I was involved in managing the sales executives of EBL. I had to prepare the commission, decide salaries & disbursement of any funds related to them & even handle the entire process of new joiners.

Finally I joined SSL Wireless as an Assistant Manager under Corporate Services & I am serving them till date. SSL Wireless is the biggest mobile solution provider of Bangladesh providing extensive corporate mobile value added services like Mobile Banking, Push Pull Services, Mobile Networking Solutions & more.

Beside this, I am also a part time blogger & an online entrepreneur for my site/blog where I display my personally developed SymbianSigned certified themes. Being a blogger I am also engaged in Search Engine Optimization and Blogging related jobs (including guest blogging, SEM & affiliate marketing).

Also, I am offering Corporate SEO Services in Bangladesh to the biggest Bangladeshi organizations currently in the market.
