Michael Bungay Stanier’s Summary

The two most exciting things I'm up to right now...
==> Preparing for the launch of my next book, Find Your Great Work (www.FindYourGreat Work.com)
==> Building a training cadre and community for our premier product, the Coaching for Great Work program

Meantime...

Michael is the Senior Partner of Box of Crayons.

Box of Crayons works with organizations around the world to help them do less Good Work and more Great Work. Their clients range from American Express to Xerox and across all major market sectors.

Michael has created:
- The Coaching for Great Work training program

- *Find Your Great Work: napkin-size solutions to stop the busywork and start the work that matters* (www.FindYourGreatWork.com)

- The internet movies *The Eight Irresistible Principles of Fun* (www.eightprinciples.com) and *The 5.75 Questions You've Been Avoiding* (www.fivebigquesitons.com)

- The award winning coaching tool, Get Unstuck & Get Going on the stuff that matters (www.getunstuckandgetgoing.com)

Before founding Box of Crayons, Michael held senior positions in the corporate, consultancy and agency worlds He has lived and worked in Australia, the UK, the US and Canada.

He is a Rhodes Scholar and holds an M Phil from Oxford, a law degree and a BA with highest honours from the Australian National University. He is a certified coach.

Michael Bungay Stanier’s Specialties:

*Coaching for Great Work* gives managers practical coaching skills.

*Find Your Great Work* is a book for managers and leaders. As well as 12 practical "maps", it includes free mp3 interviews with over 40 leading business thinkers and practitioners.

"Clean & Clear" supports teams to be more strategic and more creative within their organization
