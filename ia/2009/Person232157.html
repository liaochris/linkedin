Thierry Groshens’s Summary

EDOUARD FRANKLIN is a specialist Executive Search firm helping renowned institutions to identify, assess and recruit the industries’ most talented professionals and leaders. We execute retained search assignments through our offices in Luxembourg and Paris.

Our expert practice areas include: Banking Officers and Financial Officers.

The Banking Officer practice works closely with private banks, investment and wealth advisory firms as well as family offices to identify and recruit executives in senior-level positions such as directors of wealth management and private banking, desk managers, senior private bankers and relationship managers.

The Financial Officer practice helps clients from various industry sectors recruit executives with leadership skills as well as outstanding financial credentials. We recruit qualified finance professionals for positions that include: chief financial officers, finance directors, financial controller, treasury managers, tax managers, …
