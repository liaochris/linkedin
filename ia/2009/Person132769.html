Kiriaki Papadopoulou Samuelsen’s Summary

State authorized translator between norwegian and greek, both ways. Translator from english into greek. Interpretor between norwegian and greek. Have translated 3 general fiction books and 1 novel from norwegian into greek and some fiction texts from greek into norwegian. Experience as a librarian and as a teacher in english, greek, and norwegian as a second language, and as a teacher in intercultural communication.

Kiriaki Papadopoulou Samuelsen’s Specialties:

Translation of legal and economical texts, general fiction and fiction. Bibliographic work. Intercultural communication. Second language teaching. Experiential learning and co-operative learning. E-learning. Adult education.
