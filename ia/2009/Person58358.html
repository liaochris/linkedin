David Mullich’s Summary

Interactive entertainment and education professional with extensive experience developing and producing projects for PC, console, web and mobile. Produced RTS and RPG games for Activision; lead "Heroes of Might & Magic" development team for The 3DO Company; designed and produced "I Have No Mouth, and I Must Scream" with author Harlan Ellison for Cyberdreams; produced children's and action games for The Walt Disney Company; and created some of the first adventure, RPG and simulation games for the Apple II, most notably the cult classic game "The Prisoner."
