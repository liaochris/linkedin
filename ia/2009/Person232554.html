Thomas J. Hempel’s Summary

Accomplished Sales Leader with over 18 years experience building and leading sales organizations from emerging technology start-ups to notable global corporations. Currently developing and implementing new product sales positioning, revenue bundling strategies and initiatives, recruiting and supporting the NEC Sphericall platform as a Subject Matter Expert for the Regional Channel Account and Enterprise Account Mangers.
