Mitch Sowden’s Summary

Mitch is an energetic ICT professional with 11 years experience leading people & regional project teams in Africa / ME. He has a successful track record implementing and managing large-scale communications transformations, involving complex organisational change affecting people, process and technology in challenging geographies with limited budgets. He possesses an intuitive understanding of communication technologies as well as their commercial applications.

He is an enthusiastic self starter & persuasive problem solver with a broad leadership & the ability to communicate effectively with individuals at all level & cultures leveraging knowledge, talent and technology across teams. He uses his passion and experience in ICT to exploit communications technology to address complex business, organisational and technical issues for customer excellence. A versatile IT Leader capable of determining appropriate action, devising strategies and systems across a breadth of service delivery and support functions to achieve commercially focused business solutions.
