  • Partner

    C3 Holdings, LLC

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 2006 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    As the chief architect and software engineer for C3, architected and built a large-scale Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) web application suite that is currently utilized by dozens of municipalities and counties in New Jersey to enhance their communication capabilities with citizens. Citizen Communication Center (C3) empowers governments to more efficiently meet the needs of its citizens by leveraging online registration features, easy-to-use online services and many popular web 2.0 technologies.

    Solution developed enables governments to quickly publish content to various media outlets, including their website(s), Twitter, Facebook and YouTube, communicate with citizens via email, SMS (via a commercial SMS aggregator gateway) and telephone via VoIP (Asterisk), leverage GIS technologies, process online payments for licenses, permits and recreation programs and increase the efficiency of servicing its citizens via online workflow process management functionality.

    Since its initial product launch in February 2006 as an ASP.Net 2.0 web application, the application has matured into a suite of cohesive applications built using ASP.Net MVC 1.0, C# 3.5, jQuery, .Net Framework 3.5 and SQL Server 2008 that processes thousands of citizen registrations per day, serves tens of thousands of web pages per day across dozens of municipal websites and delivering hundreds of thousands of messages per month.
