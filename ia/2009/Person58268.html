David McKibbin’s Summary

A strong reputation for providing financial support to corporates around the world through the international reinsurance marketplace. Now focused on helping corporates reduce funding costs and improve their access to sustainable funding through the more effective use of credit risk data and sound credit risk management practice enabling confidence to be better maintained across trading and finance networks. This reduces the impact of sudden external shocks and ensures corporates and their networks do not have to rely on the credit assessments of third-parties alone.

Real cash value can be generated for corporate clients. The knowledge created from buyer risk data benefits existing and alternative funding sources enabling lower regulatory capital charges per transaction. This helps corporates take greater control over their funding process and improve funding terms.

The risk management issues corporates must manage to maintain confidence are growing. Insurers have a key role to play in supporting their clients business aims by providing sustainable services that support business confidence.

Insurance and reinsurance underwriting, Business and network confidence,trade receivables financing with embedded insurance, the psychology of risk, behavioral economics, increasing cash available to corporates through more effective management of risk benefiting both corporates and financial product manufacturers, credit risk mitigation, trade credit insurance, supply chains
