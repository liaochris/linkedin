Goutham Vishwanath’s Summary

Goutham is a graduate in Electronics with a Full-time MBA from ICFAI Business School, Hyderabad . He has over 7 years of experience of which he has spent 5 years in Executive Search business in Hyderabad. He has been passionate about being in search industry with specific focus in IT/ Infra/ Pharma verticals. Prior to the role of Executive Search Consultant he has worked as HR Systems Design consultant assisting companies in Performance Systems Design, Competency Mapping etc… Goutham has to his credit of closing several technically niche/ typical CXO mandates across industry verticals involving Manufacturing/ IT/ Pharma sectors. Goutham is an active participant in HR forums/ blogs wherein he has been a regular invited speaker and a columnist.

Stanton Chase Intl:

With 54 offices across 34 countries with more than 350 highly qualified fulltime executive search professionals, Stanton Chase offers a value proposition to both clients and top talent with global and local perspectives that can be matched by few
