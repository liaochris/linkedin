Jane Neumiller-Bustad’s Summary

I love bringing fresh, creative ideas to people...as a way to help them improve their businesses and how they serve their clients. With over 20 years experience as a consultant, facilitator, and training & development professional I know how to help people develop ideas that lead to business results.

Creative Mind Consulting offers two complementary approaches to developing customer-focused cultures:

■ "It's Hard to Think Outside the Box...When You Don't Have a Box!"

I create "boxes" of web tools for business professionals. Each one includes a basic web site/blog/online portfolio, a corresponding social media profile, and an online contact management tool.

Training in the use of each tool is included in the box.

Larger "boxes" include the basics, plus an additional assortment of online tools that build your business. The largest box includes a complementary business brainstorming session.

■ "Once You Have a Box, I'll Teach You to Think Outside and Beyond its Walls"

My fun and engaging business brainstorming workshops give you and your team the tools needed to develop innovative customer service attraction and retention strategies.

You can also use brainstorming sessions to help improve teamwork, develop leadership or create new marketing ideas.

I also meet individually with people to brainstorm ways they can create or build their businesses.
