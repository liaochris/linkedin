Allison M Markin’s Summary

A skilled manager, writer and creative thinker with vast skills in marketing, communications and media relations. Throw in a bit of branding and government relations, and that's Allison, owner and principal of All She Wrote Consulting.

Allison M Markin’s Specialties:

Communicating in a crisis, media relations, social media integration. Specializing in the cultural, culinary and wine tourism sectors.

Allison M Markin’s Experience

  • Principal

    All She Wrote Consulting

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 2009 — Present (4 months)

    Merging media relations, marketing and social networking to promote cultural, culinary and wine tourism.

    You know you need to be on the latest social networks. You know people are “tweeting” about you, for better or for worse. And you know you don’t have the resources to tell your story, build your brand, and express your online voice consistently.

    All She Wrote Consulting manages social media strategies, focusing on the cultural tourism sector. We merge media and marketing to integrate the most popular social media tools – Facebook, Twitter, Flickr, YouTube and blogging – into one voice that represents your brand. We can teach you how to effectively manage your social media, or we can do all that tweeting, video-sharing, and status-updating for you. We keep on top of “Web 2.0″ trends, providing our clients with the best advice on which social media tools to use to reach your audience.

    And we’ll merge these technologies with traditional media relations and marketing strategies. Having an event? Starting a winery? Announcing your upcoming festival lineup? We’ll write your news release, book your ads, create the Facebook group, handle the Twitter profile, and blog about it. Cultural tourism aims to create a sense of place. We’ll help you do that in the online world.

    That’s All She Wrote.
