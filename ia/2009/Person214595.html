Shagun Jain’s Summary

Shagun Jain has an extensive experience across banking, consulting, financial research and investments in Indian equity markets.

Shagun looked exclusively at wholesale banking sector at Mckinsey. He has helped build knowledge across the firm on SME and wholesale banking. Shagun also advised clients on entering, redefining presence in various segments within wholesale banking. Shagun has also extensively worked on the recent credit crisis and its impact on the Indian markets and economy.

Prior to this Shagun worked in various capacities at Standard Chartered Bank. He has a extensive experience in wholesale banking operations focusing on payments and cash management. He also has served the working capital needs including supply chain finance, trade finance and cash management across middle market and local corporate clients in Mumbai and Delhi.

Shagun also cofounded Fundamental Stocks India. FS India is an informal platform for placing ideas relating to investments in Indian equity markets.

Shagun received a Masters of Management Studies from Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studies in Mumbai. Shagun is a also a qualified Chartered Accountant from Institute of Chartered Accountants of India
