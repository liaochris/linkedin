Tom Presotto’s Summary

In July 2008 I started my own activity as a strategic consultant to support small/medium ISV and SI to innovate the way they build and market products to stay alive in a changing market. I named my company EVOG that means “EVOlve to Grow”. Thanks to the experiences I gained at IBM and Microsoft, and the many contacts I still have inside these two companies, I do support my customers to adopt the “service” models to create applications that can be easily adapted to follow the speed of the market. On top of this, together with a multimedia agency, I provide marketing services to produce events, references, news, adv campaigns and others. Our combines offering is based on 3 main points: quality, low-cost, speed.

In March 2007 I assumed the responability as general Manager of the Sykora-ML italian branch office. We offer a prouct/service to migrate AS/400 RPG code into Microsoft .NET, preserving the existing investments.

Freelance consultant, from November 2006 to March 2007, after 21 years with IBM and 6 with Microsoft.
Tom's main job role in Microsoft was "Program Manager", responsible to autonomously develop strategic initiatives, managing human and financial resources, aimed at presenting Microsoft as an “Enterprise Ready Company” to the Italian IT architects and CIOs.
He developed experiences in the following areas, partner – like HP, Accenture and Intel – coordination, strategic marketing, key customers scouting, business-messages creation, speaker at internal and public events, technical awareness aimed at business people, internal and external resources management.
