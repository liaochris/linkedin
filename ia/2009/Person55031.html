Dan Stoll’s Summary

Leadership and management experience starting as an infantry officer in the US Army. Transitioned into the Medical Device Manufacturing industry working in manufacturing engineering, quality engineering, manufacturing management, sales and consulting roles in companies like CoopervVision, Alcon Laboratories, Chase Medical, Marlow Industries, Kiran Consulting Group and Essilor Laboratories.
