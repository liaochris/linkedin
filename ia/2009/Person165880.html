Mirit Eder-Turley’s Summary

Mirit Eder-Turley, CMI, LMT, is a Medical Intuitive, Licensed Massage Therapist, archetype counselor, and practitioner of energy medicine. She has maintained a private practice in New York, Massachusetts, Florida and New Mexico since 1995. She has taught and lectured about the mind-body-spirit connection since 1997. She assists her students and clients in finding the clarity they need to live their potential.



Mirit uses her intuitive healing gifts and the techniques she has learned through studying directly with master teachers to assist people in every aspect of Be-ing: physical, emotional, mental and spiritual. Her clients include those displaying the dis-ease patterns of fibromyalgia, trauma, cancer, nervous system disorders, lymes disease and chronic fatigue among others.



Mirit has been certified by Dr. Norm Shealy and Caroline Myss through The Institute of Scientific Medical Intuition and is a graduate of CMED Institute. Her studies have provided Mirit with a language for the intuitive healing work that comes to her naturally. She also incorporates wisdom teachings from the many traditions she has studied and directly experienced.



For information about Mirit’s events, products, classes and private sessions, go to www.miritturley.com. Or call 1-800-418-9566.
