Steven Sacks’s Summary

OBJECTIVE:

As of June 2009 I am actively seeking both a full-time position and short-term consulting assignments. I am based in Charlotte, North Carolina but can work virtually and travel as needed.

WHAT I DO:

For a full-time position I am interested in discussing opportunities where I can use my 18 years of product management and online marketing experience to help a customer-focused company grow its business.

An example of a short term project would be to "Maximize Lead Generation from your Website," which includes an end-to-end analysis and detailed recommendations for increasing the amount and quality of leads, and for changing online and offline processes to improve conversion.

Additional short term or long term projects include building e-commerce websites, building mobile applications, and marketing using social networking sites such as Facebook and Twitter.
