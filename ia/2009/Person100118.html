Gen Ford’s Summary

Gen founded Ithaca Business in 2001. Today they operate in 3 permanent offices & 7 affiliates around the world – working with organisations to find better ways of optimising STRATEGY, STRUCTURE and PERFORMANCE.

After a successful corporate career of trouble-shooting, project management & change leadership Gen wanted to bring her particular SYSTEMS APPROACH directly to organisations needing help. To do this she developed and applied robust project management methodologies and unique mapping / improvement tools, underpinning Ithaca’s exemplary record of success.

Gen’s portfolio of tools – which are applied by all Ithaca Consultants – ensure a comprehensive picture of the ‘as-is’, then a unique ‘visioning’ process ahead of fully researched and mapped out implementation pathways for best fit solution sets. Unusually Ithaca works with clients throughout implementations – because that is the most difficult part!

Clearly the robust, holistic and INDEPENDENT approach worked – Ithaca’s clients to date include Carter Holt Harvey, CSL Pharmaceuticals, Federal and State Government Departments in Australia (Defence, Communication & IT, Innovation & Industry), NHS Trusts and Health Services plus many privately-held small and medium enterprises.

Beyond Ithaca Gen believes in sharing expertise and time with those who wish to engage in constructive debate. To this end she teaches supply chain and business strategy at post-graduate level as a visiting or Sessional lecturer in four countries. She is regularly invited to speak on leading-edge issues at conferences around the world.

Gen established Ithaca to provide a professional problem solving organisation who get a buzz from playing a significant part in the success of an enterprise by meeting its improvement, strategic and implementation challenges. Gen has strong principles – integrity matters – these values have transferred to Ithaca. Organisations they work with will recognise and value those qualities
