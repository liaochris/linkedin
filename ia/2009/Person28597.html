Bill Kesselring’s Summary

My career has been equally split between operational roles and as a general partner in venture capital firms. I enjoy developing and implementing corporate and business development strategies including; policy/objective governance management, fundraising, corporate structure, establishing long term strategic and profitable business partnerships and acquisitions.
