Sharon Hearty’s Summary

Highly experienced in spearheading consumer-centric marketing communications across a number of market sectors; retail, fmcg, agri-business, direct food industry, public sector/government, major event venues, specialist products/retailers.

Extensive experience in raising brand/business awareness; customer retention; marketing communications in recessionary times - UK based during the early '90's recession.

One of my goals is to raise my game higher in the area of Integrated Marketing Communications and integrate social media into the mix for clients. I have been bitten by the social media bug and cannot get enough insight on how to apply this new channel to the communications mix. This is going to be big in the next few years and the greater business market will want their slice of the action!
