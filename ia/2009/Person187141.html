Phil Stubbs’s Summary

Phil Stubbs produces 'The Environment Show', a leading program on the environment.

The Show is the no.1 independent environment podcast on itunes in Australia and New Zealand. It's been top 10 in the UK and top 20 in Scandinavia and Europe.

Phil also lectures at the University of Western Sydney in the areas of digital media, online journalism and advertising.

Phil has over 15 years experience in the media. He's worked as a journalist, writer, producer and ad executive. In his spare time he’s been a DJ, film & music reviewer, surf instructor and outdoor guide.

He's worked as a communication manager at leading Australian advertising agency The Campaign Palace and done a stint as marketing manager for the outdoor education organisation Outward Bound Australia.
