Michael McClure’s Summary

Entrepreneur whose primary attributes are creativity, a strong financial background (as a CPA), and an unusually relentless work ethic.

Quote: "I've been studying real estate franchises since before I entered the industy, and have contemplated the purchase of at least half of the "large name" franchises: RE/MAX, Keller Williams, GMAC, Prudential and Avalar. Along this journey, I came to the realization that none of these models has the "total package" to comprise what I believe a real estate company should be. Necessity being the mother of invention that it is, I then set out to create a better mousetrap. Along with the tireless efforts of a few other incredibly talented people, I think we've done that."
