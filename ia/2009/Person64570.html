  • Singer

    Svarun

    (Public Company; NWS; Internet industry)

    October 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Svarun is a nine-piece metal band from Novi Sad, Serbia, founded in October, 2008., in Novi Sad by Vsevolod Pacek-Velevid (drums, composition) and Stojan Sukunda (guitars), who are soon joined by Svarun Gleb Veleslav (baritone), Iskra Vozarova (alto), Strahinja Vikomor (growl, scream), Vladivek Tihomir (guitars), Bronimir Svarosta (bass guitar), Kazimir Morislav (keyboards) and Duska Miloradovic (soprano).

    Svarun was founded because of the need for the creation of a quality metal band capable of performing the music similar in style to what we usually listen to, but still close to us and the audience from these parts, dealing with the subjects concerning the common history of all Slavic nations, with a strong stress on the brotherly message and the one of peace that are always embroidered into our lyrics. Musicaly, Svarun is based on the postulates of symphonic metal genre, striving towards inovation, but at the same time trying to maintain the delicate balance that saves us from going into extremes. In short, our goal is the creation of listenable music, while still making it as inovative as possible.

    Svarun is at the moment busy with creating a strong repertoire for the first live appearance, as well as practicing that which is already written.

  • Singer

    Prometheus, Modern Music Project

    (Public Company; NWS; Internet industry)

    September 2008 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Prometheus is an art metal project started by Nikola Pacek-Vetnic, with the support of Stojan Sukunda and Djordje Markovic, after the final decision that lead to the termination of their former band, Lonesome Night.

    What singles out Prometheus from other bands? Unique and unconventional approach to music that renders the forms of genres such as rock or metal very much obsolete, reshaping the sound space usually found in the mentioned styles by the introduction of the new formal structures, instrumentation, harmony, melody, rhythm and tembre, using this peculiar sensation as the carrier for the messages of hope amidst bleakness of what is today. In short, we are trying to reinvent the music we love to listen.

    Prometheus is at the moment a project, meaning that there are no rehearsals, live gigs or appearances. First step in realization of Prometheus is the production of a demo recording, a task that requires a capable guitar player, the rest of the staff (drums, bass, two vocals and a keyboard) being available. This page also serves as an add: if you find this music to your liking, also think you are able to play the guitar tracks, please contact us via mySpace, or via mail: ylvaon@gmail.com .
