Paul Sutton’s Summary

I have twenty years of industry experience having worked for many of the large agencies throughout the Midlands and surrounding areas. During this time my experience has covered high end repro, packaging, brochures, magazines, books, catalogues, adverts, POS and just about anything else you can think of!

With a commanding knowledge of all the major applications such as QuarkXpress, InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator I pride myself on my diligence and the dedication I will bring to your projects.

Please take the time to peruse the examples of my work that can be found on my website and please feel free to use the enquiry form or the telephone number to contact me if you have any questions or enquiries that you would like to put to me.

Paul Sutton’s Specialties:

Experienced in all of the major mac apps including: Indesign, Xpress, Photoshop and Illustrator. My design and artwork experience has covered everything from books, catalogues, ads, leaflets and brochures to high end repro and packaging.
