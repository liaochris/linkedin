Phillip Chen’s Summary

What am I passionate about? Talent. Vision. Leadership. Ideation.

What drives me crazy? Organizations that don't "get" the 4 things above.

What do I do about it? Constantly re-imagine how things can be done.

At the present moment, I am creating an organization that aims to help individuals find their dream careers through an innovative methodology. The underlying premise of this venture is to debunk the myth that work can't be fun, or that it can't be rewarding in more ways than just financially. Rather, the goal is to help people realize what they're capable of doing by employing their talents in professions that suit them best...and in professions that allow them to exercise their passions and follow their values. It's only at the intersection of a person's values, passions, and talents that I truly believe they will be the most happiest when doing work for a living.

Previous to my new endeavor, my focus has always been fusing best business practices with HR/Recruiting functions in organizations through what I call Talent Research.

What is Talent Research? The creative methodology aimed at defining, discovering, attracting, and analyzing talent. Research in this realm can include (but is not limited to) identifying rock-star talent, competitive intelligence, market analysis, employer branding, or recruiting process analysis.
