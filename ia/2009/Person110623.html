Jeremy Meyers’s Summary

My passion lies in developing remarkable content for social media. I want to tell stories in a compelling, unique, personal and ultimately valuable way through the internet. I believe that creating common ground through shared experiences is at the core of making the world a better place, bridging the gap between celebrity, activist, teenager, 'Joe Sixpack', everyone. I have worked on both audio and video series around some of the most classic and influential music ever devised, attempting to help lend a deeper context to the things that play such an important part in people's daily lives (music, etc).

I am a veteran of the online and digital media world, being professionally involved since 1999 and playing my own small part in shaping the landscape for music in the online space.

I was hired by Sony Music to run their word-of-mouth department in 2004, which included monitoring message boards, and introduced the executives to a fledgling site called MySpace. This culminated in my securing the first feature for the new MySpace Music brand with a band called Midtown.

Currently, I am in charge of digital content and fan relations for Sony BMG. My job is to improve our interaction with fans of the music we're in charge of through the use of blogs, podcasts, message boards and other communication tools.
