Sean Wilder’s Summary

My goal is to be considered by my clients and peers as a trusted business advisor in the area of IP telephony and unified communication. I have worked within the telecom industry in various capacities, including business owner, for the past 15 years.

Sean Wilder’s Specialties:

I have been involved in communication system (PBX) sales and implementations for nearly every major (and some not so major) telecom platform, including Avaya, Cisco, Nortel, NEC, and Fonality. Over the past 15 years, I have developed a consultative approach in which I first seek to understand an organization's business processes, challenges, goals, and culture, then make a solution recommendation that is as close to a perfect fit as possible.
