David Wilkinson’s Summary

A first class software system architect and developer with an excellent in-depth understanding of Internet-based technologies and their application to business problems. Has long been at the forefront of the application of technologies and is constantly active in assessing the potential of new and emerging technologies in electronic commerce.

Played leading roles in several major commercial Internet-based developments and has been in the vanguard of the movement towards the use of the Internet as a serious commercial tool since long before Netscape invented the cookie.

Comprehensive knowledge of a wide range of technologies, complemented by a thorough understanding of the commercial needs of the business.

Author of "Flickr Mashups", a hands-on guide to building applications with the Flickr API. Published by Wiley under their Wrox imprint in February 2007.
ISBN-10: 0470097744
ISBN-13: 978-0470097748

Other activities:

Operations Manager for Utata (http://www.utata.org/), looking after all of the back-end systems and responsible for creating and maintaining the software that drives the site and its integration with Flickr (http://www.flickr.com/).

Regular photographer at the Loft Theatre in Leamington Spa, taking photographs for use in in programmes, front of house and marketing collateral.
