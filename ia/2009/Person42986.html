Chris Butcher’s Summary

Accomplished Sales Professional. Over 15 years of sales, management, training and coaching excellence. My wide variety of experiences includes 6+ years of high school Chemistry and Physics teaching, 15+ years of athletics coaching, and over 8 years of high activity, business-to-business, outside sales with a hunter mentality and focus.

My specialty is LISTENING. I listen to the people I interact with and I try to deliver to them what it is they are asking for, whether they are asking for it directly or indirectly. The bottom line is that I try to satisfy the needs of my clients rather than my own, because in the end, I know that is what creates a WIN for everyone involved.

Finally, I believe in treating people as I'd like to be treated. Nothing else in this world matters other than how you treat others. Period.
