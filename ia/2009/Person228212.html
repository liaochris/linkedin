Kristy Redmon’s Summary

Symphony Travel is a boutique travel consulting firm established in 2006. We specialize in creating custom itineraries to Belize and Mexico for honeymoons, destination weddings, small groups, adventure, and upscale adult travel.

Belize- We arrange jungle lodge escapes, eco-adventure vacations, Mayan ruins exploration, luxury private island honeymoons, and even barefoot beach weddings. Whether you want to dive The Blue Hole or are in search of the elusive Jaguar, it will be an experience you will never forget.

Cancun and Riviera Maya, Mexico- Riviera Maya is the perfect location for your upscale all-inclusive honeymoon or destination wedding. Cancun offers the best of both worlds, nightlife and action, culture and eco-adventure, and romance and relaxation. We provide you with the perfect Mexican experience.

We strive to exceed your expectations by providing exceptional service in every aspect of travel planning. For you, we orchestrate the extraordinary!
