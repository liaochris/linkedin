Louise Watson’s Summary

I have a valueable knowledge of the Higher Education and not for profit sectors, having worked at The Open University for the past three years within the Development Office.

I hold an honors degree in Drama & Theatre Studies with Music which I gained whilst in the wonderful city of Liverpool.

My first job fresh from University was working for Milton Keynes Theatre where I gained experience in Front of House, Backstage, Development and Marketing to finally become promoted to Deputy Sales Manager which I enjoyed until I left for a year's worth of global exploration!

During my travels I visited South America, Malaysia, and Australasia, where I settled and worked for a wonderful eight months in Wellington, New Zealand.

My current role as Development Officer at the OU is something which is really rather enriching - raising funds for such a well known and high ranking University to make education accessible to more people. I take the lead on the project management of a wide range of activities supporting our University strategic objectives. This project work ranges from printed materials such as direct mail, newsletters and legacy mailings through to telephone fundraising.

I am also responsible for writing copy for magazines, donor newsletters, alumni publications or mass emails, and am frequently asked to edit or proof other colleagues work.

More recently I have been actively involved in developing the content for the Alumni website Platform and have led the development of the fundraising news and content within our monthly enewsletter, which has had a direct impact on the number of hits on our fundraising sites.

I am confident with management information and have the ability to analyse and interpret this to demonstrate successes and failures, making recommendations for the future.
