Tony Toto’s Summary

Nouveau Riche provides world premiere real estate investment education, instructing individuals on how to build businesses and create wealth. Using the scientifically proven Instructional Systems Design (ISD) process, NR teaches the foundations of building your business, starting your investment career and expanding on both as you advance through their college-style curriculum. The founders of NR understand that the right education is the key to success and wealth building. The combination of having millionaire real estate investors as instructors and using the ISD process revolutionized the way real estate investment education is being applied!

Nouveau Riche brought in millionaire real estate investors to teach at their college. Before becoming instructors, they submitted to a rigorous FBI background check and proved through tax returns and real estate settlement statements that they achieved millionaire status. Each of them became a millionaire using the strategy they teach, and each provides real world experience and knowledge so that every student can apply what they’ve learned to become successful!

To help prepare for a constantly changing real estate market, Nouveau Riche teaches their students how to invest in both a stagnant and booming market. With over 30 classes provided, every student can choose their own Personal Preference Curriculum, applying each strategy in the current market condition of the geographical location in which they are investing.

Everyone benefits! Both beginning and seasoned investors are taking advantage of learning more in order to advance their businesses while continuing to grow their wealth. Many experienced investors have admitted discovering brand new strategies after attending NR classes.
