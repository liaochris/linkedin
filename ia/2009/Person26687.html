Benjamin Schmittzehe’s Summary

About Schmittzehe & Partners

Part of the Sustainomics Group, we are a high value-add China-focused management consultancy staffed by seasoned China business and corporate finance professionals with offices in China (Beijing, Shanghai, Hong Kong), Europe (London, Munich), and America (Sao Paulo, Chicago).
We provide the following services:
* China in & outbound Corporate Finance M&A: analysis & strategy, target identification, business due diligence, valuation & structuring
* China in & outbound Business Development: analysis & strategy, partner identification & selection, interim management
* Global Procurement: cost base & cost reduction diagnosis, global strategic sourcing
* China Intellectual Property Rights: registration, investigation, enforcement

For more information, visit our website: www.SandPconsulting.com
