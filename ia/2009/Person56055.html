Dave Macdonald, CMA’s Summary

I am an accountant by trade and a passionate student of business and systems thinking. My strengths lie in the analysis of relationships between diverse entities - both strategically and operationally.

With backgrounds in mathematics and finance, combined with general curiosity about the world and sensible business intuition, I have become very suited to projects that consider complex relationships and, eventually, simple solutions.

My first passion is martial arts and the application of such principles to business settings.

My second is evaluating strategies pertaining to positive stakeholder engagement - call it corporate responsibility, good citizenship or just something that feels good.

My favourite accomplishment to date came about in 2007. I was a key contributor to WPCG's Corporate Responsibility Program (http://www.wpcg.ca/community). We were able to incorporate many aspects of sustainability and societal involvement right from the start.

Other accomplishments:
- developed an innovative recruitment compensation package (2006)
- article on corporate responsibility published in CMA Winter Update (2007)
- performed full GL migration from Quickbooks to Dynamics NAV (2007)
- performed full disaster recovery on a failed accounting system without operational interruption (2006)
