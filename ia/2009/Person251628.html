Zlatan Dzinic’s Summary

Zlatan's primary role is an Enterprise Content Management Lead, System Architect/Lead Developer and an Enterprise Content Management/Enterprise Architecture Consultant, he also comes from an extensive Enterprise Content Management (ECM) product background - MS SharePoint 03/07, Opentext - Livelink, Identitech - FYI, Global 360, Meridio, K2.NET, Opentext - Domea (including Business Intelligence - SQL 2005), working, providing solutions and managing projects for the Public Sector (Local, Provincial and National Government) and also Corporate Sector (Mining, Retail, Telecommunications, Oil, Manufacturing, Banking and Tourism Industry) with a strong Business and Technical background. Mostly he is a Technical Lead/System Architect as well as Project Manager (PRINCE 2 with MSF) on large ECM projects. His main focus is Developing and Architecting Office Business Applications (full blown end to end enterprise industry solutions), SharePoint 2007 Enterprise Content Management, also Document and Records Management, including BPM development of Windows Workflow Foundation (WWF), achieving Enterprise Service Bus (ESB) and SOA through Windows Communication Foundation (WCF), SQL 2005/2008 BI Solutions (Project Real), Performance Point Server 2007 and MS Office System Client Development and Integration. He's a guest speaker at International and National Microsoft Conferences and he also presents at Local Community events on the topics of Enterprise Content Management, SharePoint Server and Technologies, Business Intelligence as well as Office Business Applications.
