Guy R. Martino’s Summary

I am a Sales Management Professional that consistently exceeds established targets and quotas.

My career includes positions as an Individual Contributor and as a Sales Team Leader with experience in start-up and established companies. I also managed a Distribution / Reseller Channel for a Xerox company and a Government / Education Market Channel for a Graphics Technology company

As a Senior Business Solutions Consultant with Qwest, Verizon Business and Sprint e/Solutions, I sold managed enterprise and data center hosting services to Premier, Global and Corporate clients. I was also a Director for the Strategic Account Sales Team with Exodus/Cable & Wireless and a Global Account Manager for Pilot Network Services providing the same managed colocation services.

Previously, I was a respected sales leader and a Regional Sales Manager in the prepress graphics industry. I authored a monthly column for a trade journal, managed a Xerox channel and, as the North America Sales Director, I participated in the launch of Screaming Color and Screaming Technology, the developers of RIPLINK software, which landed me on the cover of "Printing Impressions" magazine.
