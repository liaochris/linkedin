Rick Bolton’s Summary

Co-Founder of PREFIX Retail Solutions—a new task force designed to help organizations plan and execute effective retail programs, with a focus on balancing Brand and Business objectives. Immediately prior to launching PREFIX this year, I was prAna’s Chief Strategy Officer, responsible for high-growth initiatives such as Concept Retail and Brand Extensions.

My background is a strange blend of executive, consultancy and creative roles including four years as Creative Director for California-based No Fear, Inc. and EVP of Marketing for Denver-based Centrix Financial where I developed and managed B2C and B2B programs involving high-profile sponsorships and large event activations (NASCAR, Indy 500, etc.)

As a consultant and freelance creative, I’ve worked with Coca Cola, PepsiCo, MCI, Netflix, Sky Vodka, DC Shoes, Champ Car/CART (Championship Auto Racing Teams), on projects ranging from simple Brand Positioning Assignments to complete development of Print and Broadcast Creative.
