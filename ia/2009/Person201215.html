Rob Frasca’s Summary

Online pioneer, sold first Internet Company in 1995, pre-Yahoo, pre-Netscape. Sold second company to Lycos.

Built shareholder value in the context of a publicly-traded company and five times in venture backed start-ups. Seasoned in leading and managing the operations of a technology company. Demonstrated success establishing strategy, vision, and structure; and driving operational growth of organization.

Exceptional strength in consumer online services, media & advertising,and iPhone applications. Expert in online user acquisition with a deep understanding of every aspect of E-commerce, including paid search, SEM, SEO, banners, affiliates marketing, search engine optimization and social media.

High level accomplishments:

• VP and GM directly reporting to CEO at a top 3 public Internet company, managing all of the core dotcom services. 5 product marketing/management groups, engineering, operations, and online media, containing over 25 products and 350 employees; grew P&L revenue from $56 million to over $135 million.

• Chief Executive at 4 high tech venture backed start-ups. Seasoned seed, series A - B executive.

• Raised over $150 million in venture capital into 6 high tech companies.

• Pioneer and product visionary, in the online market since 1993, bringing over 125 new products to market. From the internets first financial service, online clubs, and Mp3 search engine, to virtual goods and iPhone development platforms.

• Proven ability to manage and market media and high technology products though their entire lifecycle and contribute triple digit year-over-year revenue growth.

While I am thinking of starting an new company, I am also seeking to join the right high growth opportunity. I am extremely excited about social media, alternative monetization methods and next gen iPhone mobile. I am considering working with small venture backed organizations and also established large media companies.

I am open to relocation.
