Kevin Hardern’s Summary

Kevin is a Business Change Director with a high level of experience in the conception, planning and delivery of large scale business change, including major integration of business systems in an M&A environment and the negotiation of major multi million pound contracts with suppliers.

He also has significant experience in helping companies to maximise their revenue from key clients by building strong cross company relationships, thereby moving towards trusted advisor status for their key clients.

He is an experienced leader of people, and has established his own company, focused on the IT and Financial Services sectors, to help companies;

- get the most out of their relationships with their key clients;
- organise themselves effectively for their scale of operation;
- assure the delivery of large scale change programmes to their clients.

The company was started in 2004, with the vision of creating a network of associates who provide real value to clients, based on experience. We feel that the business model of the traditional management consultants can be challenged by a loose association of experienced and energetic 'Silver Foxes'. Client list includes Logica/CMG, City Practitioners, Bank Misr (Egypt).

Kevin is increasingly active in the not for profit sector and can also be found at Ecademy, profile link(http://www.ecademy.com/user/kevinhardern)
