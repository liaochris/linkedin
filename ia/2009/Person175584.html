Nikolai Livori’s Summary

I have worked with diverse Front-end programming languages, and one of my goal is to change most of the back-end systems that i built throughout the years into a more attractive and usable font-end ones.

I see Programming as a lifestyle and not just a career, whilst I divide programmers into 2: those that do it for a living (thats how I started), and those that use if for business (that’s where I’m heading). The mass population tend to label good programmers as geeks, however this is just a stereotype. I have been doing business and working with programmers for the last few years and only met a handful of that stereotype geek (sleazy, glasses, weird, and doesnt take care of his image).

I am one of the real-breed of programmers – those with a visual eye! Yes, I.T. people tend to be divided into the logical type or the artistic type, usually with a clear bridge between them. I am an artist by nature and logistic by education so I am proud to say that I took down that bridge in my career. I urge you to do the same.

Next goal especially for 2010 is to release some Flash games, and refurbish the old ones.

Provide help to students willing to learn ActionScript programming, and graphic designers that are willing to shift their focus onto programming for better use of the Flash/Flex product. Also, build upon a student's existing knowledge by introducing more advanced topics with particular focus on development of GUI.

Hopefully, my business will be successful and I can focus on attaining customers and retain them.
