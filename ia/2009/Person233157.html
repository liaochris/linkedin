Tien Wong’s Summary

Opus8 is a Washington, DC-based private investment firm, investing in outsourcing companies in China and North America: Business Services: CRM (customer relationship management), call center, BPO (business process outsourcing), ITO, and marketing services companies.

We are "entrepreneurs investing in entrepreneurs." Our leadership team and Advisory Board members have created and built numerous successful start-ups, and we understand the challenges and what it takes to build a successful enterprise.

We have committed capital of over $100 million to acquire call center and BPO companies in North America.

We are in the process of raising $50 million for Opus8, L.P., an investment fund which will make equity investments in business services, call center, and outsourced marketing companies in China.

Our intent is to play a leading role in establishing a "next generation" customer lifecycle management (CLM) movement which will revolutionize customer care, customer service and acquisition, and customer experience as we know it.

Our specific experience includes building from scratch, CyberRep, an award-winning customer relationship management company. At the time of its sale in 2003 to Affiliated Computer Services, CyberRep was the 3rd largest privately-held call center company in North America with over 2300 employees and $80 million in annualized revenue. Today, the operation is highly profitable with over 15,000 employees and $775 million in annual revenue, making it one of the largest call center/CRM operations in the world.

Our Principals and Board Members are recognized international experts in BPO, CRM, outsourced business services, and entrepreneurial leadership. We are frequent presenters and editorial contributors to major industry events and publications in the US and China.
