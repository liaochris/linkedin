Joni Scanlon, M.C.I.S.’s Summary

Highly experienced communication expert with significant, managerial-level experience in the corporate, educational and nonprofit sectors. Transitioning from ownership of a successful, woman-owned business to join a global, full-service communication enterprise that will offer new and traditional forms of graphic design and communication strategies to our clients.

Focus on keeping abreast of new and emerging technologies and stringent budget and project-management oversight. Strong managerial skills and high rank on the emotional intelligence scale.

Joni Scanlon, M.C.I.S.’s Specialties:

My career versatility reflects an insatiable curiousity and desire to keep abreast of new and emerging technologies. Strengths include strong managerial skills and stringent budget and project management oversight. I am most proud of my enduring belief in "the Golden Rule."
