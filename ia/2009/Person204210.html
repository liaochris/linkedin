Ross Putman’s Summary

I am currently with the Walt Becker Company in West Hollywood. Focusing on comedy, The Walt Becker Company has multiple projects in active development and a production deal with Disney. In this position I read scripts, write coverage, answer the phones, and provide general help with the day-to-day operations of the company.

With Abbot Management, I provide script coverage part time on submitted spec screenplays. In addition, I have provided freelance script coverage services for TriCoast Studios.

With Washington Media Associates, I was a research intern utilizing on-and-off-line sources to fact-check and flesh out the upcoming PBS documentary "Torturing Democracy." In addition, I provided production assistant duties on two re-creation shoots.
