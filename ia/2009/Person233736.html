Tim Lale’s Summary

I teach English composition and grammar at Southern Adventist University. For 20 years (1988-2008) I worked in magazine and book editing, along with product development, product positioning, and marketing. The first five years were in copy editing, which gave me a good foundation and breadth of experience for editorial work. In the 1990s I worked on two youth magazines for the Seventh-day Adventist Church. From 1999 to 2008 I acquired books in eight categories for the Adventist bookstore market, with constant change and new projects every day. I managed projects, found and developed authors, maintained a positive working relationship with more than a hundred authors, evaluated manuscripts for content and sales potential, did developmental and copy editing, assessed potential profitability of books, analyzed and reported on past sales and profits of books, contributed titles and cover ideas, made recommendations for binding and price and cover concept, chose and placed photos in books. I co-created a project tracking program called "Publish" with an IT programmer. Created a pro forma in Excel for estimating book revenues. I can get publications produced from start to finish.
