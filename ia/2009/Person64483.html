David Bressler’s Summary

In my various roles in the Actional product team at Progress Software (PRGS), I provide customers, partners, and our global field with the expertise and experience required to deliver an enterprise service-oriented architecture (SOA) and sell Actional offerings. From my career in enterprise software and data networking, I bring to customer engagements a deep knowledge of integration and networking, blended with an ability to leverage emerging technology to create business value. I also bring a global perspective on selling software, having worked with customers in approximately 30 countries.

Before joining Actional, my fourth successful startup, I was one of the team responsible for bootstrapping Tibco's European operations. I went on to become vice president product marketing and management at Aether Systems, and then on to manage the delivery of shared middleware networks at Radianz.

I received my MBA in International Business from New York University Stern School of Business where I graduated with distinction.

I really enjoy the dynamic "think on your feet" experience of selling enterprise software into emerging technology markets.

David Bressler’s Specialties:

Excellent writing & presentation skills.

A firm grasp of diverse technologies, how they relate to business, & how to clearly explain their impact on an organization.

I understand the importance of influence, & have often managed teams that involve people that don't report directly to me, or even to my company.
