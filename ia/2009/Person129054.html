Keith Moore’s Summary

GM & SVP at Tree.com, Inc. / LendingTree, LLC leading the Emerging Businesses division and Short Form Mortgage (GetSmart.com). Promoted from GM & VP of the LendingTree Lending Exchange, which was then made up of over 450+ lending partners nationwide. Using the LendingTree and GetSmart brands, the Lending Exchange matches and connects its business partners with engaged consumers who have completed qualification forms for loans.

Since joining LendingTree, the company has consistently ranked as the top media advertiser online (According to TNS Media Intelligence & ClickZ.com).

Prior to joining LendingTree, Keith served as SVP of Marketing in the Mortgage and Insurance industries, responsible for building profitable, direct response Marketing operations that were also frequently recognized as one of the top online media advertisers, ahead of the direct competitors with much larger marketing budgets. #8 Ranking in March 2006 at http://www.clickz.com/3603121

Prior to LoanWeb & 4Insurance, Keith led the full development and marketing of the Internet's first Homeowners Insurance instant quotes and online purchase. As the VP of Marketing, Sales and Internet Operations at Western Mutual, Keith launched Western Mutual's 'Direct Sales' division, which transformed a traditional 'Agency' based Insurance Carrier into a primarily direct sales operation with both internal and external broker channels, all via Internet and Direct Mail marketing. Web based applications at WesternMutual.com, DirectBrokerLink.com, ArizonaHomeInsurance.com, NationwideAgents.com & EasyEvidence.com allowed approved homeowners and insurance brokers to generate instant quotes with online underwriting approvals with fast binding options. Western Mutual online consumers were also able to compare their online quotes to those of the top insurance companies available in their area (AllState, State Farm, Farmers), with internally developed HO3 online rating systems.
