Jared Garfield’s Summary

LION500 & GroupLinked Open to All Invitations: jtgarfieldjr@yahoo.com

Jared Garfield is the founder of Dartnell Investment Academy, a team of real estate mentors that has been helping investors for over 15 years find 20-100% ROI investment properties with significant equity. He has successfully coached over 500 clients in areas of real estate investing, financial literacy and planning, as well as small business development. He is an expert in fields of building and establishing credit, and creating wealth through partnerships.

In the past Mr. Garfield has built a real estate investment firm that developed a significant portfolio of investment properties through creative and traditional means, including; pre-foreclosures, short sales, foreclosures, land trusts, lease purchases, options, assignments and purchasing through wholesalers. He is an expert at identifying markets, timing of entrance to market, developing power teams, analyzing, negotiating, acquisition, renovation, management and sale of real estate investments.

An experienced entrepreneur, Jared has a keen understanding of business planning and development, and implementation of marketing plans. While in his early twenties he developed and grew a small real estate brokerage to four offices, recruiting and training over 42 real estate agents at two different firms.

As a past trainer and presenter for a nationally recognized financial services firm, Mr. Garfield gained an excellent understanding of the utilization of bypass trusts, LLC's, estate and financial planning strategy, as well as asset protection.

Mr. Garfield is licensed in real estate, and has studied the real estate brokers course as well as Accredited Buyers Representative and GRI real estate Courses. As a financial consultant he has received life, variable products, health, accident and sickness, and property and casualty insurance licenses.
