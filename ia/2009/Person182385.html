Patty Sommer, MSW’s Summary

I founded Essential Elements Coaching because I am passionate about supporting individuals who are pursuing their dreams, whether it is running their own business or simply attracting more peace, joy, meaning and purpose into their lives.

I work from a heart-centered approach, using my intuition, knowledge, training and experience as a mid-life woman, mother, coach and business owner to guide me. I believe that everyone can do whatever they set their mind and action to. As a coach, I partner with my clients, supporting, encouraging and holding them accountable along the way, while helping them to get in touch with who they are being in the process.

By combining my 23 years experience in sales, marketing and leadership, with my success as a three-time business owner, it enables me to understand and guide entrepreneurs as they define their vision and create the business they’ve always wanted. In addition, as a mother of 2 teenage daughters and a soon-to-be empty-nester, I love helping women and mothers re-discover who they are, explore what they truly want and take the next step toward their dreams.

I run interactive coaching groups for women in transition, women transitioning through divorce and women business owners. I also work with clients 1:1 over the phone and in person.
