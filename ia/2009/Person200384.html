Robert Fay’s Summary

I’m an experienced, Orange County, Ca.-based Internet specialist with a successful track record of writing website copy in collaboration with Search Engine Optimization (SEO) experts. In addition to being a first-rate writer, I also have a sophisticated knowledge of current SEO/SEM strategies.

I understand how to use social networks, blogs and backlinks to increase organic website traffic. I have written pages of website copy using targeted keywords, while also creating internal and external links with anchor text.

My copywriting experience includes a wide range of online and print vehicles including: direct ad pieces, newsletter articles, website copy, blogs, marketing brochures, print ads, email blasts and press releases.

My goal is secure an Internet marketing position where I can combine my love of both writing and Search Engine Optimization techniques to help increase traffic to an organization's websites.
