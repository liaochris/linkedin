Praveen Yalamanchi’s Summary

Praveen Yalamanchi is a Founding Partner and the Managing Director of Good Fortune Ventures investment team and Managing Director, India for Penfold Capital. Formerly at Dawson James Securities (top 25 investment bank in terms of deals completed in 2006), he was VP of Technology and Clean Technology where he was responsible for sourcing and conducting due diligence on banking candidates, and for providing management consulting and investment advice to CEO’s and CFO’s in preparation for IPO's, PIPES, and Capital Markets Transactions.

Before joining Dawson James, Mr. Yalamanchi was founder and CEO of TextbookOutlet.com, a textbook and telecommunications service provider. From 1996 to 1997, Praveen served as Controller at Armigeron, a web software firm, where he began his career in technology in the areas of sales and engineering for start up ventures in Silicon Valley. In 2005, Mr. Yalamanchi was able to turn a 5 figure personal trading account into a 7 figure portfolio within three years by trading stocks, options and commodities.

Praveen is able to draw upon an extensive professional network of distinguished individuals in the U.S., Canada and India which spans the industries of global finance, healthcare, automotive suppliers, real estate, broker-dealer compliance, patents, technology and clean technology.

Praveen holds two honors degrees in Finance and Computer Engineering. Additionally, he has obtained many certifications and licenses in the both the IT and finance realms.

Conjointly, Praveen has served in several leadership roles. He is active among various charitable and civic associations including: TiE (Charter Member and Executive Committee), Kappa Sigma Fraternity Alumni Association (Board of Directors), Network of Indian Professionals, Indo-US Chamber of Commerce and Habitat for Humanity.
