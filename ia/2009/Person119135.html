Jonathan Warycha’s Summary

Jonathan Warycha is the Director of National Sales at BandCon. Reporting to the Vice President, he is responsible for strategy and execution, market penetration, business development and brand recognition. He is also responsible for managing a team of seasoned professionals throughout multiple territories within the entertainment and media vertical markets.

Before joining BandCon, Mr. Warycha spent eight years forging strategic partnerships and customer relationships. Clients such as NHL, NFL, Warner Bros, Indy, AMA, F1, Sony, Activision, McGraw Hill, NeuLion. Core competencies within media, entertainment, sports and gaming.
