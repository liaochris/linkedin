Michael Fruchter’s Summary

I’m a 31 year old marketing professional focusing on social media, with over a decade of experience working on the Internet. I wear many hats both on the business and technical side. I love technology, digital media and anything that resides at a dot com address. My focus is specifically on the ways we use the Internet for consumer engagement, marketing, branding, and spreading an individual or business brand awareness. In other words, by getting the word out there positively, using any and all means necessary in social media. I also like to focus on the tools and applications,that help us effectively use and leverage our staying power, relevance, and recognition.

When I first got involved on the Web in 1997, I was heavily involved in email marketing, SEO, traffic generation and traffic brokerage. My passion is about the technologies behind social media and how business and individuals can benefit from them. I stay very active with self promotion, social bookmarking, social networking, focusing and experimenting on my own personal branding by using my blog and domain name.
