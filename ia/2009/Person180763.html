Pankaj Nakhat’s Summary

I describe myself as test obsessed, who believe in using innovation and effectiveness to change the face of software testing in the Industry.

During my professional experience of five years in the Industry, I have worked with leading organizations on various domains like Business Intelligence, Banking, Retail and CRM.

I have worked a on making Automation easier and effective for different organisations. Apart from working on tools like QTP, Selenium and Perl. I have also developed simple and business effective automation frameworks.

I have also Imparted training on Automation frameworks and Advanced QTP to corporates.
I have worked as QA Lead for a large bank, where I have managed the technical and test management responsibilities.
