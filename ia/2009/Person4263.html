Jeff Yeomans’s Summary

Jeff Yeomans has been in the internet advertising industry for 12 years beginning as an ad serving admin with Hyperbanner networks (Bpath.com). Mr. Yeomans conducted both advertising sales and business development for Bpath while at the same time managing their Canadian banner exchange network. In 1998 Mr. Yeomans became their director of business development and facilitated several partnerships and revenue generating deals.

In 2000 Mr. Yeomans implement and operated the ad serving system for Yellowpages.com. During his time with Yellowpages.com he set up and operated different ad systems until they finally settled on OAS. In addition to conducting all ad trafficking & ad reporting for Yellowpages.com he also sold yellow page advertising and internet advertising for the company.

Since leaving Yellowpages.com in 2003, Mr. Yeomans has operated an internet ad serving consulting business during which he has provided adserving system setup, ad trafficking, reporting and advertising sales support to a variety of sites including Hola Networks, Northjersey.com, Vidshadow.com and Careerbuilder.com. His experience in all aspects of internet advertising is extensive which assists him with ad serving optimization and maximizing revenue generation for all his clients.
