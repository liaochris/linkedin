Greg offers a quality photographic service and 17 years experience. Like so many photographers, Greg began his photographic career working with other leading studios in Sydney. In 1992 he gained his "Certificate in Photography" passing with Honours.

As Greg became recognised in the world of corporate and events photography he decided to launch his own business. Soon after he developed regular clients who have remained with him for many years. Not only has he photographed a large variety of business and community events, he is also a successful wedding and portrait photographer.

In fact many of his wedding clients go on to hire Greg for their trade and industry needs. He has been responsible for photographing events from as little as 10 through to corporate occasions with hundreds of guests which have included Vips, Government ministers and International dignitaries.

Whether your photographic need is for internal use, an industry specific magazine, website or a national publication, Greg will photograph with an artistic eye and a professional approach.

Greg uses the latest digital technology and editing software. He has a portable studio, including lights and backdrops and is able to visit you at your place of work, conference centre or any desired locations.
