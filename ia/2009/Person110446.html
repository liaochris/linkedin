  • Business project team member

    Deloitte

    (Partnership; Management Consulting industry)

    January 2009 — May 2009 (5 months)

    Today most of the applications running within companies are based on “ON PREMISE” principle. It represents a huge market for software vendors (Oracle, SAP,...) but also consulting companies (Deloitte, Accenture, IBM,...).
    For two years now, a new model is emerging: “ON DEMAND” with a key actor: Salesforce.com

    Key questions are:
    - Is “On Demand” going to replace “On Premise”?
    - How can we compensate the lost of fees due to “On Demand” projects?
    - What is the sustainability of the current players?
    - What will be the reaction of the traditional players (SAP, Oracle, MS,...)?
    - What are the key success factors of the consulting firms who will lead this potential new market?
