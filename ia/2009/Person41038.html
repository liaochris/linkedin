Peter Hess’s Summary

I'm transitioning from a career in Management/Organizational Consulting to one in Sustainability (Environmental, Economic, Social). Please contact me if you'd like to chat about how I could apply my experience and skills to further global sustainability.

My Background:
President and CEO of CHANGE Partners an Organization Effectiveness and Management consulting firm. Peter is an executive with 25 years of broad-based experience and a proven ability to leverage human resources in a focused, orchestrated approach to achieve business goals. He has particular skills in the areas of change management, strategy implementation and executive guidance.

Peter has assessed and coached senior executives during mergers and as part of ongoing succession planning processes, helped formulate and implement strategy & values; and managed complex systems change. He has facilitated and consulted to several hundred teams, ranging from ex-ecutives to state-wide labor-management committees.

CHANGE Partners’ clients include leading companies in the pharmaceutical, financial services and consumer packaged goods industries. Some of the organizations with which Peter has worked are Pfizer, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Bayer Corp., Sterling Winthrop, Abbott Labs, Pharma-cia Biotech, JP Morgan Chase, Goldman Sachs, MetLife, Prudential, AT&T, and Nabisco.

Peter Hess’s Specialties:

Change Management, Org. Strategy, Analysis/Problem-solving, Group/Team Facilitation, Communication (written & oral), Large Group Involvement Processes
