Richard Woolfenden’s Summary

I am a Producer, Director and Writer - in that order! I am one of 2 company directors of Xube, a production company based in East London committed to making cutting edge communications for the UK corporate market. The types of films I have worked on recently include: training films, best practice videos, observational films, promotional videos and 3D motion graphic animations. I am committed to pushing creativity to the limits within transparent and realistic budgets.

My plans for Xube are to further consolidate our position as one of the premier suppliers of video content for the UK, and European, corporate video market.
