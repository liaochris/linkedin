Deonna Barnett’s Summary

I am the Marketing Manager for INCREASE Community Development Corporation, a non-profit organization that helps individuals start and expand businesses in Central Ohio.

My responsibilites include coordinating the marketing initiatives for our business planning course, workshops, the Business Network and our annual events.

I am always looking to collaborate to provide our training students and our Business Network members with additional resources for business and professional development.

I am also a musician. I sing and play the keyboard for different groups in Columbus, OH. I enjoy giving vocal instruction to those who are passionate about singing but do not have the resources to obtain formal training.
