Tim Girvin’s Summary

I'm looking for stimulating partnerships -- for intellectual and business alliances -- for companies that are trying to enter the US markets effectively, or for organizations that are considering exploring the US, Europe or Asia. We strive, using imaginative collaborative "thinkshops", with our partners, to build the business of our clients, internationally, through balanced and thoughtful marketing / creative, coupled with empowered, collaborative design. I believe in exploring; and getting that exploration out there to my community: I tell stories here:
http://blog.girvin.com/ | http://tim.girvin.com/Entries/index.php |

Brand: the etymology, from 1000 years ago -- it's about fire and passion -- this what we ignite, brandstories® sparked from the heart of the business, to the experience of the target. We use a grouping of tools -- brand workshops and charrettes that energize teams in the exploration of brand development and positioning. Collaborative brandstorming is part of the practice that our teams manifest in visioning, strategic direction, emotional content and personality positioning and experience design development.
