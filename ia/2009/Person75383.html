Erin Brumleve, MA, LPC, ATR’s Summary

Licensed Professional Counselor (Colorado #5056) and Art Therapist Registered (08-241). Over ten years experience providing counseling, psycho education and therapeutic arts services in varied milieus, including elementary schools, residential, inpatient, and community mental health centers, as well as memory care facilities for older adults. Evidenced based therapeutic approach rooted in family systems, object relations and cognitive behavioral background/techniques. Art therapy is integrated with these approaches to bypass verbal defenses and/or communication deficits to focus on enhancing self-esteem, improving communication among group members, and helping to provide individuals and families relief from stress, anxiety, depression, and loss.

Working artist, specializing in drawing/painting.

Interested in academic opportunities, and connecting with professionals in private practice.

Erin Brumleve, MA, LPC, ATR’s Specialties:

Individuals and families and couples in the areas of Divorce/Separation, Blended Families, Grief and Loss, Anxiety and Depression, and Trauma. Helping school age children with issues related to school refusal, attention deficit disorder, and peer relationships
