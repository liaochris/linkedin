Frank Elbert’s Summary

TipSpot - your social event guide - is a new online service that makes sure you always know what's going on in your city. Via the web and your mobile phone, TipSpot offers a last-minute and personalized overview of the most relevant events; such as nice parties, concerts, exhibitions, theater shows or the sale of a cool store. You can 'tip' events yourself or follow the tips of friends and other interesting parties, like your favourite bands, DJ's, artists, magazines, clubs and shops.

Frank Elbert’s Specialties:

TipSpot gives a complete and personalized overview and is available anytime, anywhere.
