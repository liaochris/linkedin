Jessalin Lam’s Summary

As of now, I am balancing four summer internships, mentor/CEO of Baruch City Advertising Society and thinking about applying for the Director of Marketing position in Undergraduate Student Government. In addition, I am actively trying to become part of the Young Professionals Committee in the Advertising Club of New York as well as attend as many networking events that I can.

The reason for this is that I love networking, meeting new people and have discovered that I have a strong passion for marketing and advertising. Overall, my short term goal is to become an account executive at an ad agency post graduation (possibly Wunderman or one that pays me a satisfying salary) and at the same time, I would strive to develop my own business.

Feel free to contact me if you would like to hear more information or if you just want to say hi because I enjoy meeting new people anywhere as long as you are nice about it.

In the end, I always try to enjoy life and expect less to appreciate the little thing in life more.
