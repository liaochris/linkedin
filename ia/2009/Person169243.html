Mark Hewitt’s Summary

I have a rich 30+ year career in designing and operating large scale communication systems and networks, chances are that at some point in your life you will use either a network or some of the technology that I have had the opportunity to help create. Today I am focused on expanding the framework created by the MetroCore which is a business and technology protocol designed to aid in the planning, engineering, modeling, construction, and long term operations of sustainable systems. The MetroCore provides developers, engineers, architects, and planners with the tools necessary to model complex systems. Then once built the MetroCore provides systems designed to manage and operate Smart Systems.

Mark Hewitt’s Specialties:

My industry experience is primarily centered on developing large scale communications systems and products, over the course of my 30+ year career I and my product teams have won over a dozen product of the year awards from industry trade associations. My focus has been centered on convergence and scalability of products and systems.
