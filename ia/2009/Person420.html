Maryellen O'Connell’s Summary

I have worked in Search for 10+ years, the last 8+ in technical. I help mid-level/senior candidates find opportunities that are long term interests. I am recruiting at rovi working within the Embedded and Security groups placing software Engineers. Throughout my career I have placed candidates in Software Engineering, (the whole scope from developers to architects and directors), Electrical Engineering, and Project Management (software side). My niche and geography is Software Engineers in Silicon Valley and surrounding areas, lately added Southern California to my groups I support. My goals are to help my client achieve their business objectives with the most qualified employees and for people considering a move to another company to feel like they are in the hands of a consultant that cares about their well being.
