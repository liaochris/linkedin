Fred Huebner’s Summary

I am currently part of the "Webby & Freddy Show" on Chicagosportswebio.com, along with Matt Weber. CSWebio.com is the first Internet talk site dedicated to Chicago Sports. I had worked from 1999-2009 as a co-host/sidekick at the SCORE with hosts Mike Murphy and Mike North, providing sports updates as well as serving as "sounding board" for the host. Provided weekly movie reviews on the Mike North Morning Show, reviewing the latest Hollywood films and new DVD releases. I hosted when the main hosts were away and also hosted many weekend shows during my time at the station, including our NFL pre-game show for seven season working with co-hosts including Steve Silverman, Hub Arkush, and former Bears players Joe Cain, Raymont Harris and Doug Buffone.
