Joshua Gerkin’s Summary

Driven, analytical, and savvy property risk management professional with 10 years of experience providing loss control and risk transfer services to Fortune 500 organizations. Particularly adept at deploying an extensive risk assessment and fire protection engineering background to mitigate exposures associated with fire and other perils using NFPA and FM standards. Proven ability to significantly impact risk quality and implement/manage change by thoroughly understanding and effectively communicating hazards, jointly developing effective risk mitigation strategies, and obtaining broad acceptance of proposed solutions. Proven record of fostering client relationships, utilizing exposure driven engineering methods, and effectively prioritizing risks in order to develop comprehensive property risk management programs for complex commercial and industrial property risks. Strong communication, leadership, training, and consultative skills are demonstrated to accomplish results.
