Debi Silber, MS, RD, WHC "The Mojo Coach"’s Summary

Debi Silber is a Registered Dietitian with a Master’s degree in Nutrition, a Personal Trainer, Whole Health Coach, Lifestyle Expert-just for moms, speaker, President of Lifestyle Fitness, Inc. and author of The Lifestyle Fitness Program: A Six Part Plan So Every Mom Can Look, Feel and Live Her Best (recommended by Parenting Magazine) and From Mom To Wow: Your Ultimate Body, Mind and Life Makeover Guide. Debi’s branded “The Mojo Coach” because for nearly 20 years, she’s inspired unfit, overweight and overwhelmed moms to “get their mojo back” through gradual lifestyle change.

Debi’s a featured expert on numerous websites, providing information, articles and insight to thousands of struggling moms. She’s a popular radio guest, is quoted regularly in print and online magazines, was recently featured as a successful “career-at-home-mom” in a new book and serves as a student mentor for the National Institute of Whole Health; the most highly accredited and widely respected Whole Health Coaching program in the U.S.

Debi also holds two certifications in pre/post natal fitness, with specialty recognition in weight loss and weight maintenance. She’s the New York Ambassador for W.O.M.E.N. e.o (Women Own Mentor Empower Nurture Each Other. Debi’s been honored for her unique charity program (2008), is a Madison Who’s Who (2007), Notable American Woman (1992, 2000) and Outstanding Young Woman of America (1992, 1998).

Debi understands that moms needs a program which supports their goals, values their time, meets their specific challenges...and creates lasting results! She’s a role model any mom can appreciate and relate to as she tends to her business, home, husband, four children, four dogs and her own self care!
In a warm, fun and “mom friendly” way, Debi can encourage ANY mom to find the balance between motherhood and womanhood…because it doesn’t have to be a choice.

Debi Silber, MS, RD, WHC "The Mojo Coach"’s Specialties:

Weight loss, fitness, wellness, lifestyle change, weight maintenance support and coaching exclusively for moms.
