kHyal (No Last Name)’s Summary

kHyal has been in the business of communication since 1985. An AIGA award-winning graphic designer and creative director with blue chip clients from Black&Decker to MTV. She later expanded her interactive design services to include expert project management, copywriting, marketing, public relations, events and education. kHyal founded fizz Agency in 2007, a communications firm with a strong focus on social media.

In her 20-year career, kHyal has held the positions of Creative Director at Microtech International, an innovative manufacturer of Apple Computer peripherals, and Circle.com (now Euro RSCG) where she developed database-driven interactive campaigns for UPS, GAP and BMW. kHyal was a cofounder and VP of Marketing for SunStar Publishing, a consumer software publisher, and Blowtorch Studios, an interactive agency. She also served as Interim Director of Marketing and Public Relations at Stamford Hospital.

kHyal majored in Mass Communications at Emerson College in addition to studies at The School of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston; The Art Institute of Boston; Boston Film and Video Foundation; Loyola Law School and Kodak's Center for Creative Imaging.

kHyal has served on the MIT Enterprise Forum of New York City’s Marketing Committee and co-chaired the Fundraising Committee of Volunteer Lawyers for the Arts, Los Angeles. She has been a juror for the London Interactive Advertising Awards and Westchester Ad Club's Big W Awards. She is Communications Chair for the AIGA Metro-North Chapter. Her fine art and design have been exhibited in the American Visionary Art Museum, Housatonic Art Museum, New Britain Museum of American Art, Outsider Art Fair, Intuit, Cooper Union; and galleries from New York to London.
