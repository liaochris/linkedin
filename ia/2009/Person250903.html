Zach Browne’s Summary

I currently provide technical support and web design services for a portfolio of about 25 small businesses. I mainly assist companies who are too small to hire a dedicated IT professional with services ranging from installing Microsoft Office on a laptop to industry standard web design and SEO.

I have always believed in a hands on, aggressive approach throughout my career. Starting out with RightNow Technologies as a Sales Engineer I got right in the faces of CTO's with companies like Proctor & Gamble & Enron to explain the benefits of utilizing online customer support systems opposed to phone calls. I traveled across the country as a Regional Sales Manager for several years with Greyfox Systems driving home revenue and educating businesses and consumers about software and web technologies.

I jumped off the corporate band wagon in 2004 to start my own business. I started approaching small businesses and providing personal services such as technical support, web design, graphic design & SEO. Since then I've assisted over 100 companies with their technical support strategies and helped them reach out to the web as their primary way of conducting business instead of just an information tool.
