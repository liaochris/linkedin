Andrew Braun’s Summary

A summary of my career is outlined below:

• Was the innovation manager for Yellow Pages Australia (Sensis) where I developed business cases for the 3-5 year yellow online product roadmap
• Project led the implementation of a new product development (stage-gate) process at Sensis with Bain & Co
• Project led a number of $1m+ projects that covered the entire new product lifecycle from data and insight gathering to business casing through business and functional requirements, to the actual development and eventual commercialisation:
** A new online product for Yellow Pages, www.homeatyellow.com.au
** Credit risk software Optimist7 utilised by over 100 banks worldwide (Inmatrix)
** The roll out of Optimist7 to 4000 employees at Bangkok Bank (Thailand’s largest bank)
** A 3G wireless platform for a start-up company that I ran in Los Angeles (Icewrx)
** A mobile sporting portal for the NBA, 2004 EuroCup & 2004 Olympics portal for Vodafone Live! (Icewrx)
• Established distribution partnerships with global companies, including Ericsson, Vodafone, Macromedia and Hewlett Packard, whilst at Icewrx
• At the Eclipse Group (Deloitte) I was an e-business consultant (business analyst) for the implementation of complex internet solutions for a number of publicly listed Australian organisations
• At Price Waterhouse I provided Tax and business consulting advice to businesses

Andrew Braun’s Specialties:

Since completing my law degree, the past 11 years have seen me work for emerging businesses and mature organisations at a senior level in sales, business development, marketing, product strategy and development, partnership & channel development and project management.
I have broad ranging commercial, technology and entrepreneurial skills, which I believe are important in a company that supports innovation, values creative thinking, requires flexibility and plays to win.
