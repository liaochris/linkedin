Sean Ross’s Summary

My twenty (20) years experience in the fluid power industry covers the full range of technical and sales experience including;

Technical Support: training, design, and commissioning of new technology industrial hydraulic systems for a major hydraulics distributor.
International Sales: marketing, setting-up and managing distributors, direct sales to major mobile equipment OEMs, and assisting in setting-up new European manufacturing subsidiary.
Distributor Management : setting-up and leading hydraulic distributorship involved in both industrial and mobile design, sales, and services.

I have practical technical experience in virtually all major hydraulic technologies including; proportional / servo, brake systems, vane, radial, axial piston pumps and motors, and logic valve systems. I also have practical sales experience in virtually all major overseas markets including Europe, Asia, Australia, Africa and South America, both distribution and direct OEM sales.

Sean Ross’s Specialties:

Hydraulic Training/Design/Service and International OEM & Distributor Sales Management.
