Philippe Peltier’s Summary

As a manager, strong oriention towards the creation of added value for the customers, while streamlining in-house processes to improve efficiency and allow strongly improved cost-value ratios.

As an engineer, extensive experience in a wide variety of complex technologies, (e.g. high-vacuum, high-voltage, RF) related to any kind of machinery, from ship's engines to particle accelerators. Extensive knowledge of dosimetry and irradiation processes.
