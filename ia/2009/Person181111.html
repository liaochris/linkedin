mahesh bhatt’s Summary

I am a prominent nature lover, keenly interested in adventurous activities, engaged since 32 years in all kind of adventure, Nature and anything related to nature, Wildlife and its protection, Forest & its Conservation, Environmental Education and Protection with training in Rock climbing, Mountaineering, Rafting, Water and Ice Skiing, Parasailing and Swimming. Apart from three entries lead by me recorded in the Limca Book of Records, I have discharged my services for more than 50 courses as an Hon. Instructor for Gujarat State Mountaineering Institute, at their various centers and have participated about 85 adventurous events such as camps, courses and expeditions as a Leader including 8 Peak climbing expeditions into the Himalayas. Excluding my various trekking of Jungles, deserts, seashores and other ranges of mountains in India, my outstanding performance in trekking Himalayan ranges - plus 15000 kms, rafting in various rivers of India at least 1100 kms. & bike riding for over one lakh kms. in various adventurous expeditions.Since July 1990 I am an Hon.President & Founder of Parikrama Nature Adventure and Sports Institute, one of the leading and outstanding organizations engaged in adventurous activities, nature and environmental education, forest conservation and wild life protection,which is famous for its peculiar, unique and special adventure every year in the state of Gujarat. It is the only institute of India, which is recognized in LIMCA BOOK OF RECORDS for three times. It organizes camps, courses and expeditions for trekking, mountaineering, rafting water and ice skiing, parasailing / gliding and two and four-wheeler adventure with one motto - "Development of Youth". --I am Online Volunteer of Online Volunteering service provided by the United Nations Volunteers programme that connects development organizations and volunteers over the Internet to support the achievement of the Millennium Development Goals. I have created an OV Group of my organization.
