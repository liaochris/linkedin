Eric Godfrey’s Summary

We run one of Arizona's largest and most reputable DJ Services. We provide services for weddings, corporate and school events, team building, fund raising efforts, private parties covering nearly any topic. We also provide Casino party services, Team building via gameshow and trivia programs. We have one of the industries best training programs as Eric Godfrey is the West Coast Director for training for the FAME DJ School based in Florida. Eric is also the President of the American Karaoke Jockey Association and an advisory board member for the Karaoke Industry Alliance of America (KIAA) leading efforts in training and oversight on legal issues and ethical issues in the Karaoke industry.

Eric is also a long standing Executive Board member for the Phoenix Chapter of the American DJ Association and served as President for 2008. He has spoken and taught seminars at the National Mobile Beat DJ Convention in Las Vegas, where Starz Entertainment was honored as one of the top DJ company success stories in the United States. He has spoken at national DJ Events sponsored by WEDJ and also the National Association of Mobile Entertainers (NAME).

Starz Entertainment has been one of the most honored DJ companies in the United States with 12 different "best of" awards won over the last 3 years. Starz is a Better Business Bureau Member and has been chosen to do events for the Better Business Bureau in each of the last 3 years. Starz conducts over 1000 events annually and takes a role in assisting it's community through various programs including the Special Olympics, and several fund raisers for local Police and Fire Fighting organizations.
