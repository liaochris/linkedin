Nic Allum’s Summary

Nic Allum is a Production Director specialising in events and experiential marketing. Originally from the UK, Nic was a Production Manager for The Mean Fiddler Group across their major venues and landmark music festivals including Reading.

In Australia, she was a founding organiser of the Pacific Circle Music Convention, a key event for the Asia-Pacific music industry, and moving to the digital sector, established 24/7 Real Media's youth and music portfolio which included some groundbreaking digital radio and blogging sites.

Returning to the event world, Nic served as Director of Communications for the Staging Connections Group until 2007, establishing their communications department and developing end-to-end systems, processes, programs and publications for their 1200+ staff and over 16,000 clientele.

Nic was until recently Production Director at Maverick, one of Australia's most creative experiential agencies, and directed the production of a diverse array of event marketing activities including touring roadshows, major PR stunts, tailored events, sponsored experiences and live promotions.

Nic is currently looking for new opportunities.

Nic Allum’s Specialties:

Content production (on/offline), event management & production, brand innovation, marketing/communications analysis, change management communications, business writing.
