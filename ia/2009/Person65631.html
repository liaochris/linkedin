Don Coley’s Summary

Dynamic senior leader with unique depth and breadth of expertise and accomplishment in turning apparent obstacles into opportunities with breakthrough business results.

Distinguished track record of thought leadership, revenue creation and brand building in diverse environments including Bose®- a $2.5 billion global multi-channel consumer products company. Rare and unique blend of achievements encompassing product concept through full lifecycle in corporate category leadership, marketing communications, B to C and B to B business development in new and existing markets, as well as Bose owned store influence.

Significant contributions to over 40 new Bose® products and product launches including the Acoustimass®, Lifestyle®, BUILT-INvisible,® 901®, and 3 2 1® families.

Passionate and creative high-energy strategist with an earned reputation as a collaborative leader, capable of building strong relationships and results-oriented teams including all levels of colleagues and customers in large, small companies and non-profit organizations.

During more than 23 years of significant growth at Bose®, Don collaborated to champion and market many unique product concepts, successfully overcoming internal resistance. These products ultimately helped to define the Bose brand, created new categories, major revenue streams, and were widely imitated by other manufacturers.

Product Manager for Acoustimass®-5 Speakers -named one of the 10 Best Consumer Electronics products of all time in 2006.

Led company entry into home theater category.

Highly regarded for ability to function effectively as an individual contributor or as team leader -from the research labs/corporate level to e-commerce, B to B and retail. .

Skilled in creating value added innovation to deliver enhanced revenue streams from new and existing technologies, product lines and customers.

Actively seeking the right opportunity to solve problems, energize teams, and make the world better !
