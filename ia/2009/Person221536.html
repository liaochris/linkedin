Laura Khan’s Summary

My husband and I have owned and operated convenience stores for the last 28 years. We currently own a gas/convenience store in Margate, Fl.
I started selling on Ebay in August, 2003 and I now also sell on Bonanzle, ioffer, and Zazzle.com.
For the past year, I have been helping my son, Adam, aka Ville Baby, with his music career as a hip-hop performer.
We created the company, Sunshine State Records, a year ago, and Adam has other artists such as Chess, an R&B singer, recording with him.
They have performed at some local clubs and do all their own promoting by passing out t-shirts and CDs for free.
