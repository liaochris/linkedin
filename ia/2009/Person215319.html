Sharat Thakur’s Summary

Sharat Thakur is the founder/ owner of the company - Shapia Software Solutions Private Limited. Sharat is a serial entrepreneur, with a vision of creating one of the biggest conglomerate business in the world.

Sharat has background in Information Technology. He has global corporate expertise having worked for IT companies in India and USA. He was the founder member of an internet based startup venture in USA - Utopia Innovations LLC. Earlier he has worked with companies like Ness India, Ness USA, Business Objects, Wyeth Pharmaceuticals, I-Link Software Pvt. Ltd.
