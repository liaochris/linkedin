Markus Miklas’s Summary

In my professional life I maneuver in the contradictory context of market and technology. Developers, users, product managers, sales people, deciders - they all have diverse perspectives and needs, often speaking different languages. Making those worlds understand each other, enabling successful communication between them – this is my profession.

Rarely that I settle with what I have already achieved. For me it is fun to discover new worlds, may it be new technologies, new markets or new target groups. Creativity and an open mind almost always lead me to the right ideas, thorough research and analysis provide me the facts.

In all my activities I, of course, not only rely on my talent. A technical eduction and studies of communication sciences represent the basis, on which my professional experience grows and flourishes.

Currently I am developing business for mquadr.at in large parts of Europe, offering solutions for internet services providers - and, of course, their customers. Automation and simplification is the key to bring internet services to as many people as possible. For technically inexperienced persons, using computers and setting up internet connections still can be a huge challenge. Mquadr.at's solutions provide for automatic setup, comprehensive support and usability tools, as well as realtime automatic self-repair, etc.

While the customers enjoy a new internet expierience, with everything working just like it is supposed to, the number of calls to the support hotline is dramatically reduced.
