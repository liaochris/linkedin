Andres Goyanes’s Summary

Andres Goyanes is a marketing management professional based in Orlando, Florida. His work covers corporate communication, public relations and advertising management. In addition, he has extensive experience in integrated marketing strategy, direct response and online marketing.

With experience spanning more than a decade and having worked in diverse marketing competencies, Andres is a solutions-driven marketer who has successfully developed projects for lead generation, direct sales, business development, corporate communication and sales support.

The breadth of Andres' marketing skills includes web and e-mail marketing, direct mail promotions, web development management, marketing campaign planning and media buying across all media formats.

Given his understanding of Web 2.0 and how it can support promotional efforts, Andres integrates online marketing tactics to his mainstream marketing strategies, applying diverse formats and philosophies, including search engine marketing, SEO, mass email and social media.

Adding to his skills set, Andres is bilingual in business-level Spanish (oral and written communication) and has extensive world travel experience across Europe, the Caribbean and South America.

Andres Goyanes grew up in A Coruña, a city in the Celtic region of Galicia, in northern Spain. His basic-conversational knowledge of Galician, Italian and Portuguese are an added asset in his global communication skills.

Andres holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing from the University of Central Florida and is a graduate of Leadership Orlando Class 66. He also serves as president of the American Marketing Association of Central Florida for 2008-2009.

Andres Goyanes’s Specialties:

Marketing Management and Strategy, Public Relations, Corporate Communications, Online Marketing, Direct Response Marketing, Creative Development, SEM/SEO, Social Media, Advertising Campaign Management, Resort Marketing, Hospitality Marketing, Campaign Planning and Hispanic Markets.
