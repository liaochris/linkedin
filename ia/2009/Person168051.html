Idris Mootee’s Summary

An expert in strategy and Innovation, Idris partners with clients in all sectors globally to identify their highest-value innovation opportunities, address their most critical challenges, and transform their businesses with innovative thinking .

Prior to Idea Couture, Idris has held numerous global leadership roles with firm including Blast Radius (WPP), Organic (OMG), Live Lowe (IPG). CBIZ (CBIZ) and McKinsey.

Idris also provides C-level executive and board-level strategic counsel, innovation and strategic guidance for multiple industries that often involve leading growth initiatives and innovation management.

He is a frequent speaker at international conferences and executive retreats on innovation and strategy and iis the author of several books in strategy and marketing. He can be contacted at imootee(at)ideacouture.com or visit his blog www.mootee.typepad.com
