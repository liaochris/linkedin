Joan Nowak’s Summary

When working with small business owners and solo professionals, my goal is to help them create the lifestyle they want from a business that is Simple, Fun and Profitable.

My hybrid approach helps clients learn new marketing and business skills, provides them with simple, proven systems and support for easy implementation, keeps them focused on priorities and motivates them to achieve their goals -- more sales, more profit, more control, more time and more freedom.

I value results and relationships and believe both are key to success in business and in life -- so I enjoy working with business owners and professionals who also value both.
