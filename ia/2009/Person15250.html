Andrew Key’s Summary

A highly results-orientated, articulate and innovative Business Communications Expert with over nine years experience of working closely alongside major blue chip organisations within a diverse variety of sectors including pharmaceuticals, new media, petrochemicals, financial services, IT, the public sector, retail and automotive. Clients include Cisco systems, Glaxo SmithKline, Pfizer, BUPA, NHS and Shell.

Presents information clearly and concisely, easily engaging a wide audience. Decisive and intuitive, driven to deliver extraordinary results with a proven ability to work calmly and methodically under pressure. Flexible and persuasive, quickly and easily develops strong working relationships. A strategic thinker and resourceful solutions provider, displaying creativity, expertise and integrity. Enjoys working with a high level of autonomy.
