Nikolaj Gyde’s Summary

Since January 2009 I am working as Consultant at Detecon International GmbH in Bonn, Germany, which is an international consulting company and part of the Deutsche Telekom Group. I belong to the Corporate Finance Group and focus on transaction services, investment planning and financial/strategic due diligence.

From February 2007 to December 2008 I worked as Analyst at Ernst & Young Transaction Advisory Services in Copenhagen, Denmark. My focus areas has been on mergers & acquisitions, assisting buyers and sellers in handling their transactions.

I have profound analytical skills which I have gained from five years of higher level business studies at the Copenhagen Business School in Denmark. I have an international academic profile gained from the completion of two master programs; Master’s of Science in Finance and Strategic Management and the CEMS Master’s in International Management. During my studies, I have studied in Denmark, the U.S., Austria and in China.

I have organisational experience through volunteer work during my studies as part of the Line Committee in my master program as well as part of the international Student Board with CEMS.

I held a three months traineeship with Ernst & Young Hua Ming in Shanghai, China, where I gained insight into the Chinese business environment. I further completed two internships in Germany; a three-months internship with KaDeWe working with public relation activity, and; a six-months internship with Egmont Ehapa Verlag assisting the editor of two children’s magazines.

My international working experience has provided me with in-depth knowledge in how to do business in an international business environment.

Nikolaj Gyde’s Specialties:

Transaction services, mergers & acquisitions, public private partnerships, project management, strategic management, Risk Analysis, qualitative industry and market analysis, and corporate business development.

I have gained international management competencies through academic and practical experiences from abroad.

My communications- and IT-skills are strong. I speak Danish, English and German fluently.
