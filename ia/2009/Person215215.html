REAL ESTATE EXPERIENCE

A native St. Louisan, I know the metro area neighborhoods! Resident of and specialist in historic homes, renovation, rehab and construction.

Want to evaluate a home’s price? Experience with appraising, area pricing statistics, and tight comparables allows me to give you a negotiating edge. As an experienced home renovator and rehabber, I can give you excellent advice about a home’s condition.
