Teresa Wilkins’s Summary

Currently I teach two online courses that I designed. This fall, I will be working as a clinical intern at a community mental health center as part of my doctoral degree in pastoral counseling. Goals upon completion of my Ph.D. are to have my own practice and to work as a counselor educator, offering both online and face-to-face courses.

I also designed a professional development program in technology integration for teachers in a K-12 Catholic school. The "Mount Carmel Technology Certification Program" was recognized by the National Catholic Educational Association and Boston College as a Selected Program for Improving Catholic Education. That program was also recognized in spring 2005, by "Today’s Catholic Teacher" magazine, in conjunction with the NCEA and the Institute for Pastoral Initiatives at the University of Dayton. Our Lady of Mount Carmel was a winner of the "Catholic Schools for Tomorrow Award" in the category of technology integration.
