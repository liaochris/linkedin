Christopher Vetter’s Summary

Business Executive - March 1996 to present
Launched the Vetter Group, a highly successful marketing company specializing in Web development, advertising, public relations, market research, and technology consulting. Served CNN, Turner Media, Intel, AutoShopper, C2Q Staffing, DRW Networks, Etchcraft USA, Marketing Analysts, Waccents and other North American companies. Expanded in August 2008 to become Vetter Global, serving additional accounts in Canada and Europe.

Publisher - March 2003 to present
Created Inside Portland, an online magazine exploring news and trends that impact the Pacific Northwest. Increased readership 40-60% annually for five straight years. Developed relationships with journalists, publicists, business executives, newsmakers, and political leaders across the United States. Portland Tribune Columnist Phil Stanford called Inside Portland “a lively new Internet newspaper.” Willamette Week Editor Mark Zusman praised the publication for innovative design.

Political Consultant - March 1992 to April 2008
Fifteen years of political management and consulting experience in local, regional, and national races including a Kentucky Senate campaign that nearly changed the balance of power in Washington. Areas of public policy expertise include the environment, higher education, economic development, employment, and child advocacy.
