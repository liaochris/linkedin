Neil Adams’s Summary

I have been working in retail for 20 years, from working on a till, to managing in store IT, store management, business analysis, project management & programme management of retail system change with multi million pound budgets and savings realisation.

I have experience of business analysis, project management (PRINCE2 Practitioner), and programme management in the retail systems arena.

I project and user managed the 1st Self Checkout installation in the UK in 2002, the 1st Clothing Self Checkout in the UK in 2006, and carried out a successful Self Returns proof of concept in 2007 which was a global 1st. I have experience of the NCR, Fujitsu and Wincor Nixdorf self checkout solutions.

I have a very keen interest in retail systems, new emerging technology and particularly the evolution of self service and where convergence of techologies can take the consumer journey in the future.
