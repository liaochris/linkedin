Larry Salomon’s Summary

I am an extremely versatile professional who has a good view of the “business of IT” along with a rock solid technology foundation. My strong analytical and problem solving skills have made me ideal for tackling new roles, which I have done successfully many times already during my career. Collectively and together with my excellent relationship management skills, I am suitable for several types of positions and will provide value wherever my talents are aimed.

Additionally, my background gives me relevance in other areas such as business process re-engineering and rudimentary financial analysis, both of which I have done previously.

Finally, I have the endorsement of several top-tier individuals, a few of whom are listed as references. However, there are many others of similar caliber that are willing to speak on my behalf about my abilities as a professional and my character strengths as an individual.

My skills include the following:

- Strategic business development. I have successfully developed and implemented long term strategic plans by devising short term tactical plans meant to achieve specific objectives within the longer term strategy.

- Pre-sales account management. I am intimately familiar with the standard pre-sales processes used by many sales organizations.

- Post-sales account management. I have been solely responsible for the relationships with several accounts. In this capacity, I built “relationship equity” with the business sponsors at those accounts and used these relationships to uncover new sales opportunities that were transitioned to the appropriate sales team.

- Process management. I have direct and indirect experience with various IT processes in application development, support, and operations management. Coupled with my ITIL v3 certification, I have instant credibility with senior IT management.

- Communication. Lastly, my written and verbal communication skills are top-notch as are my presentation skills.
