Louis Bowers’s Summary

Lead Meteorologist for World Leading Renewable Energy provider Iberdrola Renewables East Coast pipeline. Direct experience producing wind resource and energy analysis, producing wind farm layouts, and coordinating with project managers. Expert experience using mesoscale meteorological numerical modeling for wind energy: greenfield, resource analysis, forecasting, and operational statistics.

Previous experience producing operational meteorological forecasts for a large NJ public electric and gas utility's (PSE&G) severe storm operations (4 years), operating a real-time numerical weather modeling system (5 years), meteorological consulting, marine forecasting, and applied meteorological and oceanographic research.

Louis Bowers’s Specialties:

Wind farm energy analysis and design (2 years), meteorological consulting and operational forecasting for a major electric and gas utility (4 years), mesoscale modeling using Weather Research Forecast Model (7 years), direct management experience (4 years).
