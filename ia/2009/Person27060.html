Bernell King Ingram’s Summary

Bernell K. Ingram is president, professional coach, language and intercultural trainer of Visions International, a professional coaching practice partnering with global corporations, entrepreneurs, professionals, non-profit and Christian organizations. Bernell partners with her clients to help them add strategy to their passion™ and exercise best practices of communicating and relating in an intercultural environment.

Bernell embraces her client’s vision as her own and offers proactive services customized to fit their needs. Bernell draws from her experience in business development, entrepreneurship, strategic planning, intercutural relations and non-profit and ministry program development to lead clients from discovery to living their vision. A system of accountability and encouragement ensures focus, progress, and sound judgment in decision making. Clients develop their leadership qualities, set goals, and implement strategic plans to make their vision a reality in their family, career, business, ministry and life. Services are offered through one-on-one coaching sessions, workshops, conferences, motivational speaking/teaching and print medium.

Visions International also offers corporate and private intercultural, English and Japanese training. Bernell has equipped managers and global executives of Michelin North America, JTEKT Automotive, NICCA USA, Yazaki Corporation, BMW, Spartanburg Stainless Steel and other global corporations of Mexico, South America and Germany. Bernell’s love for languages and cultures has lead her as far as Japan where she studied abroad and lived with a host family in Shizuoka, Japan – home of the beautiful Mt. Fuji, Canada and Mexico.

Bernell authored "The Visionary’s Guide to Small Business Development" and offers companion workshops to guide small business owners through each step of engineering a business vision.
