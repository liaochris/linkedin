jim schwenke’s Summary

Having spent more than 20 years in the advertising business, I’ve seen and experienced amazing changes in the industry from how we did and do our jobs, to immense changes in communication vehicles and methods. Throughout my career, I’ve made every effort to stay as close to the forefront as possible, and in numerous cases, ahead of the curve. For example, I was doing Branded Direct or Image Response advertising in the late 80s while at Chiat/Day; I created an Interactive Direct group in 1997, actually doing direct online for Charles Schwab; and I was guiding creatives to do Integrated on- and offline Campaigns in 1999 which was the driving force behind the Microsoft 2000 launch that drove customers to a microsite from print and tv. My approach to lifecycle campaign messaging was initiated prior to the internet and I’m still fine-tuning it with the added advantage of the web. I’m more passionate today about this industry and where it has the potential to go, and have been working on what I think, will be a major new approach to data-driven dialogue communications.

jim schwenke’s Specialties:

A passionate creative, always on the lookout for new and better ways to reach, attract, and win—clients, staff, and just about anybody that’ll listen to my spiel. A proven leader, your clients will have expertise unparalleled, and your account leadership will have an ally/partner. I’m about providing relevant, branded, data-driven, campaignable messaging, and results that exceed. I mentor my people with positive energy and direction, and always strive for work that entertains and sells.
