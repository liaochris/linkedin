Alexandra Salomon’s Summary

Alexandra Salomon joined Zinio mid 2008, responsible for international growth. Prior to that, she worked at Yahoo/Overture, first, leading international strategy and business development for Global Product Management, and then managing international sales development, product monetization, brand/search synergies and trade marketing events for Yahoo Global Sales.

Alexandra was with AOL International in the early internet years, where she oversaw global deal alliances, international interactive marketing strategy, training and in country development. During her 7 years there, she worked with over 100 clients and agencies including eBay, Philips, Coca-Cola, Travelocity, Monster, Schwab and Toyota.

A long time veteran in international online advertising, Alexandra grew up in Europe, speaks French, Russian and Spanish, holds a B.A. in International Affairs and an MBA in International Trade and Finance.
