Brian Bowers’s Summary

I have served many different roles, all leading back to where I began. At the end of my college career and for a short time afterward, I was involved in the financial services industry, selling life insurance and mutual funds.

I worked for Nationwide Insurance, for eight years, in various management roles. During my time with Nationwide, I learned the insurance process from the agency level all the way through upper managment. This was a great learning experience, not only professionally, but also personally.

In 1995, I was recruited by Kauth Custom Builder, Inc. to manage new home sales for the company. My thirteen years in this industry gave me a great deal of experience in small company management; customer service; negotiation; and employee relations.

XANGO was initially a single product my wife and I marketed, as a result of our own personal experiences. Gaining hands on experience with mass marketing and the technology used in today's market place has been invaluable.

Bowers Financial Solutions, LLC is the fruits of many different labors and the realization of a dream. My personal goal was to work from home for years. This business is enabling me to do just that. It also marks a full circle of learning and events that has placed me in the financial services industry once again.
