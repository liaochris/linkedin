Nathan Stephens’s Summary

I am a self-taught web designer with a background in Media Arts. I have a deep appreciation of nature; I'm a husband, a father to a beautiful daughter, and a life long home to 1 cat and 2 dogs. As a third generation Montanan, I understand the importance of small businesses and entrepreneurs as part of our state's economy. In my work with web development, I design the small business and entrepreneur's most important marketing tool: their website. My wife, Jennifer, and I co-founded the Montana Web Designers & Developers Association ( MTWDA.org) and are working to address the needs of and develop this vital sector in our state to enable web designers and developers, who create and manage many complex online systems, to best serve their clients. Thank you for your support.
