Trish Caballero’s Summary

My goal is always to INFORM, INSPIRE, EMPOWER, AND SUPPORT people and organizations to make strategic decisions and successfully implement them. As an accomplished educator, realtor, executive, and social worker, I bring a "big picture" perspective to identifying opportunities and assessing risks within the context of everchanging economic, social and market conditions to help my clients understand the context for the challenges they engage, whether they are building an organization, making a real estate transaction, or improving their personal lives.
