David Blasco’s Summary

I have lived and worked in four different leading European countries in the field of telecom (UK, France, Sweden and Spain), which, I believe, gave me the ability to broaden my view and keep me questioning. I have been professionally shaped in the telecom, media broadband internet and mobile communications. I have had the great opportunity to be involved in various leading telecom companies (Tele2, Primus, Openwave Systems, Bango and others), which showed me a particularly broad and detailed vision of this business. During these years I worked with innovative solutions, complex software, leading services, first class entertainment content, and most effective customer centric billing.

The rapid creation power of this fast moving market generates opportunities for both companies and individuals able to understand the dynamics of this, so call, new economy. I have been really lucky to work with the best of breed solution providers capable to market their offerings effectively profiting from this momentum. To better face this market challenge companies in this Telecom industry are moving towards a re-consolidation whilst the market demand the bundle of offerings under one single provider moving to convergence of services, formats, networks and protocols. Professionals in our field must now, more than ever, be able to specialised in the business dynamics of these new technologies and their interaction with the markets. Change continues to be the ony valid constant I find in my day to day work. The ability to quickly adapt to these changes and indentify business opportunities leeds to plan and materialise wins that consolidate revenue's growth. My expertise is not to have one and yet understand them all enough to turn them into profits and sustainable business development.
