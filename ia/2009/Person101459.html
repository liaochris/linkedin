  • Project Associate

    Knight Digital Media Center

    (Online Media industry)

    August 2008 — Present (1 year)

    The Knight Digital Media Center is a partnership of the Annenberg School for Communication at the University of Southern California in Los Angeles. The Center is funded by a grant from the John S. and James L. Knight Foundation and was launched in April 2006 to focus on helping journalists succeed in the rapidly changing media landscape of the 21st Century. The center provides competitive fellowships for New Media journalists seeking to improve their critical thinking and beat reporting skills as well as help for the newsroom leader managing change in a dynamic era.

    (Online Media industry)

    January 2008 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    *SAIS MOI* was founded in January 2008 as an online magazine and member of the online community Riottt, Inc. This online publication is written by and for style-makers and trend-setters. Reports range from a tribute to fallen fashion designer Yves Saint Laurent to a concert review of one of the bands Rolling Stone Magazine says to watch out for this year, The Cool Kids, to the premiere of the in-house produced official trailer for recording artist Vi's debut album SUPPOSED 2 BE. Since publication, *SAIS MOI* has become listed as one of the “most popular” blogs hosted by Riottt, Inc. According to audience feedback, *SAIS MOI* has a continuing growth in readership amongst the 18-34 urban demographic.
