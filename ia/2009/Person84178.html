Gavin Becker’s Summary

Equal parts brand marketer, futurist, business strategist and creative thinker, Gavin is a new breed of business development executive with a proven track record of securing new business and leading teams to deliver innovative, award-winning solutions for the world’s leading brands.

A digital veteran with a deep understanding of emerging technologies and social media, Gavin brings a fresh perspective to the power and possibilities for brands in this brave, new, ever-evolving interactive world.

Gavin Becker’s Specialties:

Communications, digital, brand strategy, business development, mobile and emerging technologies, social media, brand and product innovation, public speaking and presenting
