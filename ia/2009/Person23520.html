Sunil Bagai (Linus)’s Summary

Sunil Bagai is an experienced Silicon Valley executive with proven expertise in next-generation software technologies and consulting services.

A visionary, Mr. Bagai consistently recognizes technology and business trends before they happen. He is a high-tech pioneer who enjoys working with breakthrough technologies and developing emerging markets.

Skilled in market analysis, product definition, positioning and building and executing go-to-market strategies, Mr. Bagai has held key management and technical positions at Fortune 500 and startup companies such as EMC, Veritas, TimeSpring and Sanrise. Mr. Bagai is currently the Founder and VP of Product Marketing for Indosys Corporation, a company providing leading edge SAAS based talent acquisition solutions to the world’s most successful businesses, including Google, IBM, Citizens Bank, Brocade Communicaions and Medtronic.

At EMC, Mr. Bagai managed technical teams responsible for architecting and implementing storage networking solutions for enterprise clients. At Veritas Mr. Bagai managed the replication business unit and headed an effort to increase market share and establish Veritas as an industry leader. As VP of Product Marketing and Management for TimeSpring Software, a Continuous Data Protection startup, Mr. Bagai was responsible for setting product strategy, positioning and messaging, providing business direction, recruiting a world-class management team and raising venture financing.

Mr. Bagai serves as an advisory board member for several high tech startups, offering guidance on concept validation, technology roadmap definition and go-to-market initiatives. Mr. Bagai holds a Bachelors of Science Degree in Information Technology from the University of Phoenix.
