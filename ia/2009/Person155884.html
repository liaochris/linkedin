Matthew Miller’s Summary

Matthew I. Miller is the Director of National Sales/Marketing of PlanSwift, a subsidiary of Tech Unlimited, Inc., the nations fastest growing developer and distributor of digital estimating and takeoff software for the construction industry. Matthew has been with PlanSwift for close to 2 years. Prior to his current position, he served as a Senior Estimator for the 84 Lumber Company, where he developed and managed a team of estimators, with a primary focus on the industries top 20 residential builders. Raised in Washington, Pennsylvania, Matthew started his career over 14 years ago working for a local home center. Matthew spent a few of his early years in the US Navy serving two 6-month tours to the Persian Gulf during Operation Desert Thunder. Matthew served aboard the USS Supply (AOE-6) as an Engineman 3rd Class Petty Officer. Shortly after his honorable departure from the US Navy, Matthew attended college at the University Of Pittsburgh in Johnstown, PA. During his time at the university, he studied Civil Engineering. Matthew ventured once again back into the construction industry working alongside builders in his region. Currently Matthew manages a marketing team that works closely with todays top material suppliers and builders of the construction industry. His team is responsible for maintaining an elite alliance, reseller and affiliate partner programs along with other web based marketing initiatives such as today's leading Social Media Sites. For more information about Matt's company, please visit their website at http://www.planswift.com/

Social Media Marketing (SMM), Search Engine Optimization, Partner Relations, Product Management, & Advertising
