Greg Fisher’s Experience

  • Africa Director

    Hosanna/Faith Comes By Hearing

    (Non-Profit; Religious Institutions industry)

    June 2006 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    As the Africa Director of Faith Comes By Hearing, I am responsible for the FCBH program in 33 countries of sub-Sahara Africa. I develop and maintain relationships with partner organizations who depend on us for the publication of audio Scriptures in the various languages of Africa. Currently, Faith Comes By Hearing is committed to starting 43,000 audio Bible listening groups in Africa.

    I have a dedicated staff of three people who report to me at the main Hosanna office in Albuquerque, New Mexico. In addition, four field coordinators in Africa report to me as well. I manage a budget that is larger than many denominational mission budgets. In doing this I worked out the conceptual framework that has allowed Faith Comes By Hearing to drastically cut the per program cost while exponentially growing the program's size and effectiveness. Working together with my dedicated staff we have completely revolutionized the way in which Hosanna collects program data, tracks materials inventories, prepares donor reporting, and controls and accounts for project funding.

    I conduct regular continuing education programs for the field coordinators in Africa. I also visit our major partners in Africa about once each year.

    I designed and have implemented a project verification program that we call "Rice Checking". This program helps us to establish hard verifiable statistics for reporting back to our donors and to verify our data collection system. In addition, I am contact with many of our major donors to report personally on projects which they are funding.

    My position requires me to have a familiarity with African languages and to prioritize the publication of audio New Testaments in those languages. Once the audio New Testament is published, I work directly with implementing partners to launch Bible listening programs in the language that are sustainable by local initiative.
