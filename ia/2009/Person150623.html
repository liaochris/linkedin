Marilyn Fletcher’s Summary

Marilyn Fletcher is an award-winning television producer, writer, talent coordinator and journalist who has covered some of the most important news stories of our time: the death of Princess Diana, the Bill Clinton-Monical Lewinsky scandal and the tragic events of 9/11 among them.

She has also covered the world of entertainment with accuracy and flair, producing several hours on some of the players' most successful achievements as well as their less than fairy tale endings.

As a medical producer, Marilyn has also delved in the world of the country's miracle workers and helped to guide those suffering to healing in the program "Three Wishes" in which she lead a woman bound for parapalegia to a surgeon who helped her to walk again; a man in need of a new kidney to the Mayo Clniic who made the unworkable work; to helping a young college student who stuttered obtain the device that helped him to speak clearly and to fulfill his dream of running for class president.

Armed with experiences in the world of talk shows and reality, Marilyn comes arms with a Rolodex full of contacts from her experiences in broadcast and she will deliever what you require accurately and on time.
