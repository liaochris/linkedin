Octavio Hinojosa Mier’s Summary

Founding Executive Director of an expanding Congressional-sponsored 501 (C) (3) organization with proven track-record in organization management, program development and fundraising. Over seven years of Federal Government experience with the U.S. Department of State and U.S. House of Representatives. A strong team leader with positive outlook on challenges, excellent reputation for confidentiality, personal integrity and work ethic. Effective decision-making and communication skills. Active non-profit board member of various local and national organizations. Fully bilingual in both English and Spanish.

Octavio Hinojosa Mier’s Specialties:

Non-Profit Management, Fundraising, Program Development, Human Resource, Communications, Public Affairs, Public Policy, Government Affairs, International Development and Administration. United States Congress. Legislative experience. Recognized U.S. Hispanic Community Leader.
