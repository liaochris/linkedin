Joel LeBendig’s Summary

I work for Euler Hermes, a 116-year old company that can insure your accounts receivable so that in the event you don't get paid by your customer, we'll pay you on what you're owed.

It's simple. We're not a factoring company, meaning we do not buy your receivable, we simply insure it to protect you if you get slow paid, no paid, or if your customers go out of business.

Credit Insurance can truly protect you, especially in this economy by ensuring that you get paid for what you sell. Did you know that in 2008 bankruptcies were up 54%? Or that in 2009 they're predicted to go up another 45%? Whose next to file?

I know, I know...they've always paid you on time, you know them, you've worked with them for years and you even play golf with the CEO. Maybe they're a Fortune 500 Company... Well, you'll be the last to know they're going to file for bankruptcy or shut their doors. Just ask those companies that sold to Circuit City or the countless, not so well known others that suddenly shuttered their business in the last 12 months.

Credit Insurance is not new. Euler Hermes ACI, was founded in 1893, and is the oldest and largest underwriter of business credit insurance in the world. As you can imagine, the demand for credit insurance has grown substantially since the end of 2008 in order to help protect America's businesses from the greatest economic downturn since the Great Depression.

Just because your largest customer files for bankruptcy or doesn't pay you, doesn't mean that your company has to suffer. Let us protect you because we can. Stop reserving for bad debt and free up that money to improve the business. Grow sales, increase profitability, increase revenue, and most of all protect your largest company asset.

For less than half of 1% of your sales per year, you can be protected.

Contact me today to discuss whether Credit Insurance is right for your business.
