James M. La Rossa Jr.’s Education

  • Fordham University School of Law

    JD , Law , 1981 — 1984

    La Rossa was educated at Sarah Lawrence College and Fordham Law School. He resigned his Juris Doctor in 1983, following a brief clerkship in the Federal District Court, 2nd Circuit, in New York. He is a member of the New York Academy of Sciences. La Rossa is on the Board of Directors and heads the Executive Committee of the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention, a group comprised of neuroscientists and pharmaceutical industry executives dedicated to the research and prevention of suicide. La Rossa is on the President’s Advisory Council of Phoenix House, a drug and alcohol rehabilitation group for adolescents and young adults. La Rossa is a vested member of the American Civil Liberties Union, and a member of Amnesty International. From 1982-1984, La Rossa was a U.S. Merchant seaman and a member of the United States Coast Guard.

    Activities and Societies:
    James M. La Rossa, Jr. is the founder and Chief Executive Officer of MedWorks Media, LLC, a limited liability corporation based in Los Angeles, CA.. MedWorks owns and publishes seven health science journals. MedWorks’ journals and projects have a distribution of over two million printed pieces per annum.
