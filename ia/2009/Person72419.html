Manohar Reddy’s Summary

manohar@galloptech.com

Manohar Reddy has over 17 years of experience in various facets of strategic sourcing and solutions industry including sales, marketing, customer support and project management. Prior to founding Gallop, as an Account executive, he has managed several high profile Fortune 500 accounts. Manohar started his career as a research and development engineer which gives him an unique advantage at working with new technologies/products before taking them to market.

Manohar believes in building an enterprise that drives significant and consistent financial value for clients by providing quality IT solutions and services.A distinctive entrepreneurial spirit drives everything that he does - forging relationships with clients, strategic partners and more importantly the employees.

He believes in providing the opportunity to his associates to share in the wealth created through excellent compensation and incentive programs.

Manohar always believed in simple things that bring immense joy in our lives. His other interests include film making and renewable sources of energy.
