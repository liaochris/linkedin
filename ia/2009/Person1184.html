Aaron Myers’s Summary

I have worked in Community Service and Politics since 1995, when I received my first award from the NAACP of Navarro County Texas. I have worked from Boy Scouts, to the United States Army, serving as a Minister of A Church for 10 years, to Running for the office of Mayor in 2005. I have currently worked along with the Barack Obama Campaign since 2007 as volunteer, to as a current paid staff member.

In addition to this, I have had the honor of joining some of my fellow thesbians in many productions. I started doing Stand-up Comedy in Los Angeles, California at the World Famous Comedy Store, where I made many friends. I acted and directed the YAM Drama Ministry at the West Angeles Church of God in Christ, while also acting in many other productions.

For Twenty Years, I have played the Piano, opening for 15,000 people for John P Kee in 1997 at the Bronco Bowl in Dallas Texas. I have served as Minister of Music of Different churches for 11 years, staying the longest at the Mount Zion Missionary Baptist Church in Kerens, Texas. In 2005 I joined the West Angeles COGIC Music Ministry, serving as the Minister of Music for the Youth Department.

I worked as the National Director for the Global Family Program, until it dissolved, where we celebrated Global Family Day the first day of every year.

Currently, I am the Executive Director at the newly formed Non-Profit: Alliance for Education and Progress
