Phillip Julian’s Summary

I’m a Software Engineer with experience in pharmaceutical marketing and sales operations, phase II and IV clinical trials, telecommunications, and software R&D. I have expertise in SAS programming, databases, business intelligence, data warehouses, data quality, and data integration. I provide robust software solutions that produce high quality results and meet delivery deadlines. I am an internet pioneer, and created one of the first email and news networks in 1983. I wrote the version of SAS that helped make the company what it is today. I was part of the team that launched SAS on the “minicomputers”, and saved the company by helping it win a landmark software copyright case. For the past 4 years, I created Inspire’s marketing and sales reports completely from the ground up – from IMS tapes to emailed Excel reports. I migrated our SAS reports to MicroStrategy, until the project was cut from the 2009 budget. I welcome challenging opportunities where I can use my expertise, creativity, and innovation to solve complex problems that may require a variety of skills.
