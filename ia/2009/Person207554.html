Samantha Schultz’s Summary

Teaching is a career that I found extremely rewarding for the past 4 years. My passion has been teaching high school students the basics of marketing and business. Through them I discovered a love for teaching that helped me be a better instructor while at the same time culminated in creating an environment where my students were more engaged and ultimately more interested in the world of business, specifically marketing.

However, as in all things related to and about business, situations arise that are a cause for change. Marketing tends to be one of the most fluid and ever-evolving fields of study; therefore I found it essential to remain on the cutting edge of that knowledge. This is why I chose to pursue a Master’s degree in Marketing and Communication from Franklin University. In addition to this degree I proactively made a career change into a marketing position with www.RippleResults.com.

Ultimately, I am interested in networking with current and past colleagues and friends to help develop my marketing expertise and to assist with any business or marketing needs others may need.

I continue to pursue any learning opportunity related to social media, as I see this at the forefront of all marketing related efforts for businesses.
