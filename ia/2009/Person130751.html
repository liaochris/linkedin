Kevin Burden’s Summary

I am a journalist at heart, but for the last twelve years the main thrust of my work has been in helping other individuals and organisations to strengthen their journalism, production and management skills.

I design and deliver training myself; I help others to train; and I develop tools to support learning.

I am most proud of having had a role in the creation of an online learning system and content which has helped thousands of journalists in countries in crisis and transition to report fairly and accurately on the fast-changing world around them.
