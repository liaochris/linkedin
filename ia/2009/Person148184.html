Mara Sternthal’s Summary

Mara Sternthal is a senior executive with extensive experience and expertise in the entertainment and media industry. Mara is currently the SVP of Business Development at Ascent Media Group, spearheading the launch of the Global Media Exchange (GMX). Prior to joining Ascent, Mara was an independent consultant to entertainment and technology companies, including Microsoft. Previously, Mara served as SVP of Business Affairs & Development at Paramount, overseeing the full spectrum of transactions for the Home Video Group and the development of new media business worldwide. Mara led her team in closing Paramount’s first content deals with online video platforms, e.g., Amazon, Apple and CinemaNow. She was also a board representative for Movielink. Before Paramount, Mara was VP, Business Development and Pay TV at Warner Bros., where she initiated and managed relationships with international media companies, in addition to being a Warner representative on the board of HBO’s Latin American TV platform, HBO Ole. Mara also served as VP, Corporate Development of Sony Pictures Entertainment, where she was responsible for recommending investments and acquisitions to senior management. Prior to joining Sony, Mara negotiated content and equity agreements for HBO, leading her team in securing Disney as a key content partner for HBO Asia. Mara launched her entertainment career with ABC, where she was responsible for the international distribution of ABC-owned programming (including the Academy Awards and Major League Baseball) to Latin America, Asia, Middle East and Africa. She began her career as a banker on Wall Street with Irving Trust. In addition, she has been an advisor to Internews and Irex, both non-profit organizations dedicated to the development independent media worldwide. Mara is a member of the Los Angeles Committee of the Council on Foreign Relations.
