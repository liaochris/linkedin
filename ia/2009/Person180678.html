Martin Warczynski III’s Summary

For over 20 years Martin has successfully provided management and organizational support throughout the US and Southeast Asia. He has worked for industry leaders such as The Boeing Company, Pelco, US Filter Corporation and Orbital Sciences. After serving in the Pacific Fleet, aboard nuclear submarines, he consulted for Micro Focus and Telepacific Communications. He has held Officer and VP-level positions with several entrepreneurial start-ups.
As a Management Consultant he enjoys helping aspiring entrepreneurs reach their full potential.
At home, Martin is actively engaged in the community - from coaching basketball to leading a referral network for local business owners.

Panda Technology is a veteran-owned business headquartered in Maryland specializing in IT managed services, network security, and managed office protection for small and mid-sized companies.
This proactive approach allows our clients to build their relationship with Panda Technology. A scalable solution allows our clients to “pay as they grow” with affordable and reliable protection services. In addition, we have partnered with ‘best in class” providers of hardware and software products such as Cisco, Dell, Microsoft and Panda Security.

Our managed services solutions are designed to reduce IT cost and complexity and allow small-business owners to focus on business instead of technology. Panda Technology can decrease your Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) ensuring you a healthy return for your IT investment.

Panda Technology is a premier channel partner of Panda Security, a global leading security solutions provider with millions of clients in more than 200 countries and products available in 23 languages.
