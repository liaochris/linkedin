AnnMarie Rogers’s Summary

With nearly all my work experience having something to do with a non-profit it's probably not surprising that it is my passion is working with the public and working with organizations that help people reach their goals, be it physical fitness or recovering from a fire that has left them homeless.
Aside from my administrative work I am also a certified Kripalu Yoga (basic level certification) and Pre-natal yoga instructor. My specialties include working with student new to yoga or with injuries and/or disabilities that restrict movement, strength and/or flexibility. My classes are also an excellent alternative for pregnant and post pregnancy woman who are interested in continuing their Yoga practice but need a less vigorous alternative.
In 2002, while residing in Germany with the US military, I started volunteering with the American Red Cross on Ramstein Air Force Base and became a certified American Red Cross CPR/First Aid and AED instructor and eventually an employee. I have been working with the American Red Cross ever since. In 2004 we moved back to the states and I was hired as a full time employee and continued to teach CPR and First Aid as needed.
I look forward to one day opening a non-profit health and wellness center. I would like to help alternative medicine and wellness practices become a choice available to everyone regardless of income.
