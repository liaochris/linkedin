Paul Needham’s Summary

Internet industry executive with start-up experience. I started an online ad network called BidClix, which also licensed ad serving and campaign management technology to large web-based publishers such as Sympatico.MSN Canada. As CEO of BidClix I led the company through launch, 7 years of growth, and then through its acquisition by Accipiter Solutions. Accipiter was then acquired by aQuantive, which was acquired by Microsoft. My experience therefore ranges from the very small organization, to the very large. I enjoy the challenges in business planning, strategy, start-ups, product development, go-to-market, growth management, capital raising, M&A transactions, integration and more.

I am also on the board of directors of CAMFED USA (Campaign for Female Education), a not-for-profit organization that tackles poverty and HIV/AIDS by investing in the education of girls in Africa. CAMFED improves the lives of nearly half a million young people by making long term commitments to communities, schools and individuals.

I'm keen to share my experience and continue learning. I'm excited about renewable energy, social entrepreneurship, internet services, collaborative production, new business models, and generally building a better world for my kids and theirs.
