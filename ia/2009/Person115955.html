Joe Nassise’s Summary

I'm a professional writer, specializing in novels, screenplays, and comics. A good selection of my work is available at my website - www.josephnassise.com - and I invite you to stop by and take a look. I've worked with major publishers in more than seven countries and am always looking for new and interesting projects.

I'm a past president of the Horror Writers Association, the world's largest organization of professional horror writers, and a Bram Stoker Award and International Horror Guild Award nominee.
