Debra L. Bruce’s Summary

Debra is the founder of Lawyer-Coach LLC. She practiced law for 18 years, and since 2000 she has personally coached over 400 lawyers across the United States and Canada. She was the first Texas lawyer credentialed by the International Coach Federation. She is Vice Chair of the Law Practice Management Committee of the State Bar of Texas and former leader of Houston Coaches, the Houston chapter of the International Coach Federation. She is a former Chair of the Law Practice Management Section of the Houston Bar Association.

In addition to coaching lawyers, Debra has experience coaching executives of Fortune 500 companies and is a member of the elite cadre of coaches for NASA. She is trained in using numerous assessments, including DiSC Behavior Style Profiles and CPI 260 Leadership.

Since 2004 Debra has authored a monthly column for the State Bar of Texas in its newsletter, The Practice Manager. She has also been published in the Texas Bar Journal, Texas Lawyer, Colorado Lawyer, The Legal Intelligencer, National Magazine's Addendum (Canadian Bar Journal), Women Lawyers Journal, Texas Law Reporter, My Table, Texas Realtor, Women Monthly and the Orlando Sentinel.

Debra speaks frequently to and puts on programs for bar associations, law schools, law firms and legal departments. She was honored with the Standing Ovation award by TexasBarCLE in 2007.

Debra L. Bruce’s Specialties:

Lawyer-Coach LLC provides executive coaching, training and retreats customized for the needs of lawyers, law firms and legal departments on topics including leadership and management skills, communication skills, team-building and team effectiveness, client relations and business development, time management and talent retention. In the turbulent economy of 2009, Lawyer-Coach has also added outplacement coaching for lawyers.
