Catherine Walter’s Summary

Manager with 5+ years of broad industry and civic experience in the US, Ecuador, Germany and most recently in Hong Kong. Experience in CSR strategy development for local and multinational companies based in Hong Kong. This includes performing industry benchmarking and gap analysis, stakeholder engagement, policy and strategy development, report writing and communications, code of conduct, social compliance and research. Interested in working with companies who want to improve their social and environmental performance and those who want to learn more about how CSR can benefit their business.

Recent consultancy work includes the writing of the Cathay Pacific Airways CSR Report 2007 according to the GRI G3 Sustainability Reporting Guidelines; CSR strategy development for PCCW Telecom, including benchmarking, stakeholder engagement, supplier code of conduct and CSR reporting; and heading up contract research for Oxfam Hong Kong entitled “Transparency Report II: Have Hong Kong Garment Companies Improved Their Reporting on Labour Standards.”
