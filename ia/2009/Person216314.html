Shelly Gordon’s Summary

The PR landscape has changed dramatically with the advent of social media, downsizing of newspapers and magazines, & consolidation of television and radio. Throughout these changing times, Shelly Gordon has been a public relations leader, creating innovative PR and social media programs for prominent high tech, health care and professional service companies in the Bay Area and across the U.S.

With more than 20 years of experience, Gordon continues to develop local, regional and national public relations and social media programs for client companies. Whether she’s creating a blog and training client staff on the keys to building a following (writing thought-provoking posts, tracking industry blogs and posting effective comments), or “branding” a company’s CEO to change public perception about its products and services or raising the discussion level around a legacy product category that leads to an acquisition, Gordon’s clients have praised her work:

“I truly appreciate Shelly's brand of no-nonsense, results-focused PR. It not only raised the awareness of our flagship product but also educated the press about our market segment -- a significant task in and of itself -- which resulted in customer feature stories in the key trades.” Chuck Stern, Vice President of Marketing, BMC Software.

“Thanks for all the positive press you get for us. We are very happy with the great exposure.” Gary Rapaport, CEO, Sutter Delta Medical Center

“Shelly has been instrumental in gaining valuable media coverage for our programs, services and physicians. Both the newspaper editors and our doctors have been delighted with the results.” Cassandra Phelps, PR Director, Eden Medical Center

“Shelly turned PR into a valuable tool for communicating Sutter Regional Medical Foundation’s services to the community and increasing our credibility, which has made a difference in recruiting new patients.” Carolyn Appenzeller, COO, Sutter Regional Medical Foundation
