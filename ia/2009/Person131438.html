Kevin Houston’s Summary

Following a successful career spanning some 15 years in the Contact Centre / Business Processing Industry, in January 2009, I acquired an established UK & Ireland Contact Centre & Business Process Outsourcing Company, Anderson Manning Associates, in Bangor Co Down. I have assumed the role of Managing Director.

Anderson Manning Associates was established in 1993 in Bangor Co Down, Northern Ireland. The company specialises in business process outsourcing and contact centre services to a broad base of organisations throughout the UK & Ireland. AMA is an established niche operator for DRTV / Home Shopping & Charity Sectors. Services include: Fulfilment, Response Handling, Warehousing, Credit Collections, Overflow Call Handling, Back Office & Graphic Design . I'm now embarking on an aggressive growth plan over the next 3/5 years. I've done this very successfully before for 2 of NI's leading outsourcing players - Stream Global Services and HCL BPO Services. I'm now seeking new business introductions and alliances to help me on my journey to establish AMA as one of Ireland's leading outsourcing companies.
