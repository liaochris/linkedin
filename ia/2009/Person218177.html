Simon Probert’s Experience

  • Director

    Skybox Cloud Computing

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    June 2009 — Present (4 months)

    Unlike managed service providers and IT support companies, Skybox solutions are based entirely on the principles of Cloud Computing. This removes the need for infrastructure investment or any services deployed in-house.

    At the heart of our philosophy is the idea that businesses should only pay for the resources they need, but should also be able to access additional resources at the touch of a button. This notion of a truly scalable infrastructure, available on a simple, transparent, monthly subscription underpins all our offerings and allows us to provide affordable, enterprise class IT provisioning to businesses of all shapes and sizes.

    Skybox simplifies complex and often expensive IT services and makes them consumable for all types of business. We deliver the complete array of IT and communications services.
