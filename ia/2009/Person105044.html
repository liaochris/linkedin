Jason Hanna’s Summary

I am an experienced leader, technical architect, and software engineer. My new company, Coincident, is working to develop a low-cost energy management product for consumers and small businesses participating in smart grid and metering initiatives. Additionally we provide energy, utility, business process, and advanced technology consulting services through Coincident. Prior to forming my new venture I spent nearly a decade in the high technology sector – designing solutions, building teams, and creating value for my company and customers.
