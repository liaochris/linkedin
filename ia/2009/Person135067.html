    www.crozierfinearts.com

  • Advisory Board Member

    FIGMENT

    (Performing Arts industry)

    June 2007 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Figment is an annual participatory arts event held on Governors Island in New York Harbor. The mission of the event is to provide a forum for community-based participatory art and experience. FIGMENT strives to build community among artists and participants, to foster the participatory arts in New York City, and to demonstrate a vision for the future of Governors Island as an international arts destination.

    www.figmentnyc.org

  • Curatorial Director

    Pure Project

    (Fine Art industry)

    February 2007 — December 2008 (1 year 11 months)

    PUREPROJECT is an idea incubator directed by a visionary collective of creative enthusiasts whose goal is to influence social, environmental and economic sustainability through the vitality of its numerous projects and programs. Our interests lie in interdisciplinary working methods, participatory art projects, network development and working with like-minded individuals. Our intention is to develop physical & online communities that serve as nexus points to promote creative sustainable practice.

    www.thepureproject.com

  • Gallery Manager

    Openhouse Gallery

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Events Services industry)

    August 2007 — May 2008 (10 months)

    Openhouse Gallery is an exhibition gallery in Soho, New York City with a triple bottom line. Our goal is to conduct business in a socially and environmentally sustainable manner without sacrificing quality, utility, efficiency or profitability. With this in mind, we designed Openhouse Gallery to act as a specialized venue for any event, product, band, artist, or concept, that promotes sustainability - be it through direct action or charitable contribution.

    www.openhousegallery.org

  • Art Director

    Kritzinger, MacGarry & Partners

    (Design industry)

    June 2005 — January 2006 (8 months)

    Co-founder of Kritzinger, MacGarry and Partners – a strategic and creative design production team. Kritzinger and MacGarry worked together as an Art Director and Designer team with both individuals involved in strategy development, information architecture, final production and client presentations. The team works closely with web developers and printers for a full service delivery. Selected client portfolio includes: South African Broadcasting Corporation (SABC), the trinity session, IMPI publishing, DEV4 - Property Developers, and the Reaper paintball team.

  • Multi-diciplinary Practice

    Trinity Session - Art Lab

    (Fine Art industry)

    December 2003 — August 2005 (1 year 9 months)

    Trinity Session - an independent, Johannesburg-based contemporary art lab. Interdisciplinary and socially-oriented in their approach, the Trinity Session is focused on public art projects, project initiation & production, curatorial work, research and critical writing. The Trinity Session has featured at the following conferences and institutions: Ars Electronica (Linz, Austria); Transmediale.03 - play Global! (Berlin, Germany) and the International School of new media (Lubeck, Germany). Selected client portfolio includes: Redbull (music academy, mobile lounge & land-art project), UNESCO Knowledge Portal (Digi-Arts Africa network & Digi-Arts Joburg network development), Woolworths (mobile exhibitions), Cultural Arc (Johannesburg City public art program management ), Sandton Central (public art & place making program), Spier Estate (creative consultation), WITS University (Professional Practice, 3rd year fine-arts students).

    www.onair.co.za

  • Workshop Facilitator

    CICI (Creative Inner City Initiative)

    (Arts and Crafts industry)

    October 2003 — October 2004 (1 year 1 month)

    CICI (Creative Inner City Initiative) – the overarching goal of the CICI is to engage with urban renewal and human and community development through artistic and cultural activities. The CICI runs a free art school in the inner city of Johannesburg, that is geared to sharing artistic skills sets with under privileged adolescents, and to create new employment opportunities in the creative fields.
