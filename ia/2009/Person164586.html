Mike Matthes’s Summary

I'm a Senior Software and Manufacturing Engineer with over 10 years of experience. My strong understanding of software systems for use in manufacturing and test is based upon years of practice working in process engineering in manufacturing companies. The aerospace, telecommunication, and computer storage industries have provided a me with a history of working with leading edge sensor and instrumentation technologies to test and qualify cutting edge products.

I have experience creating entire manufacturing and design validation systems that collect data centrally and use web reporting systems. My experience spans from tool hardware and electrical system design, to programming to integrate the sensors, instrumentation, and unit under test, to providing analysis and reporting systems from the data collected. The data collection systems use central relational database and web technologies to provide advanced data analysis capabilities.

Mike Matthes’s Specialties:

LabVIEW and TestStand programming, system design and integration, data collection systems, process development, test development, failure analysis, data collection and analysis, cleanrooms, electrostatic sensitive devices, instrumentation, motion control, vision analysis, measurement systems, design of experiments, SQL, advance query development.
