Leslie Ng’s Summary

After graduating from U of T, entered into the hospitality industry and fell in love with industry. My entrepreneurial background includes event planning (Panic Entertainment), catering (Elixir Alcohol Catering Co.), restaurants (kubo, kubo DX, kubo Radio, Lil' Baci) and bakery (Babycake Cupcakes).

Currently, leveraging my skills and passion in a different sector of the hospitality industry by recruiting & placing great candidates in Canada and United States.

My goal is to expand current client base further in United States and enter into Asia and Europe.
