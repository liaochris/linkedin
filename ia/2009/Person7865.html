Alex Bäcker’s Summary

As an inventor and entrepreneur, I am in the business of bringing about innovation through technology. I am always looking to hire the best and brightest entrepreneurs, particularly software engineers.

As a consultant, I help businesses with innovation, search engine marketing and data mining, and help start-ups start up.

My long-term goals are to establish a laboratory that efficiently turns inventions into products and to build software as capable as the human brain.
