Gildas Deograt’s Summary

Gildas Deograt, CISSP, is an Independent Information Security Consultant. He is a contributor of Information Systems Security Management Handbook, the coordinator of Indonesia Information Security Research Group, the founder of Indonesia Information Security Community and the chairman of Information Security Awareness Day Declaration Committee. He has been working in IT field for 15 years, with a focus over the past 11 years on Information Security.
As an Independent Information Security Consultant, he is an associate consultant of various information security service provider (directly or behind the scene) to work on various information security related projects covering policy development, awareness program, architecture and strategy development, assessment, audit, incident handling and penetration test team co-ordination.
During his 3 years assignment as a corporate information security officer for Total Exploration and Production Head Quarter (the fifth largest oil and gas company in the world) in France, he was developing and implementing policies; conducting security assessment and awareness across the subsidiaries especially in North America, Europe and Asia regions; responsible for some sensitive security projects; defining architecture and processes, such as Global Intrusion Prevention System, SCADA Security and Enclave Systems to deal with high grade attacks; and was the key person in case of major information systems security incidents.
He was the Chief Information Security Officer at Total Indonesie (the biggest gas producer in Indonesia), an official (ISC)2 CISSP Common Body of Knowledge seminar instructor, one of founders and a board member of the ISSA Indonesia Chapter and also a board member of Kampus Diakoneia Modern, an Indonesian NGO to serve homeless people and street children.
