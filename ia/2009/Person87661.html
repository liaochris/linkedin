Gregory Lipman’s Summary

Gregory (Greg) Lipman began learning about the graphic arts at age 16 working for his father, a co-owner of a printing operation in New Jersey. An office runner in the summers, Greg learned how how conduct a “press-check” and wield a Pantone Book long before attending art college.

He attended Temple University’s Tyler School of Art beginning in 1990 and began his professional career in 1995. Since then he has worked for several design and public relations firms gaining experience in a vast array of business sectors in both interactive and traditional mediums. His interactive experience began at Clarke/Thompson Design in early 1995.

Currently, one of the longest serving members of Edelman Digital, Edelman Public Relations’ online division, Greg began his journey up the Edelman ladder beginning as a Senior Designer in 1999 and ending his New York chapter as their Senior Art Director during the summer of 2007 co-managing a team of five.

On Sept 1, 2007 he began a new phase in his Edelman Journey. Greg was transferred to their Chicago office as the VP, Associate Creative Director with a mandate to “stabilize, fix & grow the team”. With nearly 100% turnover in two years prior to his arrival, Greg built a team of 3 writers & 3 designers from an original staff of one and completely changed how the team operated.

Now with a new mandate to do nationally, what was done for Chicago, Greg was promoted again in October 2008 to VP, US Director of Creative Services with the mandate to “standardize, document and streamline the national operation” with a phased timeline over at least of a year with quarterly rollouts. Covering about a dozen offices, Greg conducted a Phase One all-office training tour at the end of March 2009 to rave reviews. He is also serving as VP, Interim Associate Creative Director for their West Coast Operation and fills in as Creative Director for the Chicago operation during crunch periods.

Portfolio available at http://www.gregorylipman.com.
