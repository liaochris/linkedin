Kara Miranda Lawrence’s Summary

Kara Miranda Lawrence is a Digital Animation and Visual Artist who specializes in 3d Animation, Modeling and Design, currently completing her BMA in Animation at Emily Carr University.

Kara Miranda Lawrence’s Specialties:

Advanced in Softimage XSI and Maya. Familiar with 3D Studio Max. Proficient in After Effects, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Corel PHOTO-Paint, Corel Draw, Painter, Fireworks, Magpie, Dreamweaver, FrontPage and Tablet Use. Adept in both PC and MAC. Type: 75+ WPM.
