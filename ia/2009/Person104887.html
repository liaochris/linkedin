Jason Wells’s Summary

I am the Founder and Managing Director of Scienexus a company focused on delivering leading edge solutions for Intelligence Services and products related to Operational Risk and Security environment.

Professionally I hold:
BSc (Honours) with Diplomas in Economics and Information Technology
Post Graduate Diploma in Security and Risk Management from Leicester University.
Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)
Prince2 Project and Change Management.
Military intelligence capabilities e.g. HumInt, Analysis & Assessment, Psychological Operations and Training Design & Personnel Development.

Professional Experience

My 20 year plus experiences span public and private sector organizations.

Leadership roles have included:

Corporate Security & Anti Illicit Trade Manager within the Middle East for a major multi-national. Within this role I created many best practices that were shared globally and was a member of the Strategic Working Team shaping Information Security across the company.

Head of Plans managing Intelligence covering overseas operations conducted by UK Special Forces. During this period the UK SAS conducted a highly successful rescue mission of British soldiers in Sierra Leone.

Assistant Defence Attaché for the British Embassy, Damascus, Syria during the Iraq War (2003). I provided critical leadership and support to Strategic Intelligence/Security operations and conducted diplomatic activities with the Syrian military and foreign allies

Lead Trainer for the Intelligence Division, Land Central (LANDCENT) NATO Headquarters providing strategic training for key decision makers in multinational scenarios, and the application and use of intelligence assets. My critical analysis and assessment backed key diplomatic initiatives to negate the influence of non compliant paramilitaries within the Balkans.

Other roles have included Strategic Analyst for Counter Terrorist operations and Counter Intelligence Investigator.
