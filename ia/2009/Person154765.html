Kristin Goecke’s Summary

Currently a stay at home Mom to two girls. I started MasonMetu to help other Moms and families see all that our local community has to offer. You'll find kid friendly activities, programs and events. I also have a monthly top picks page highlighting fun events for the month. There's a recipe exchange forum for Moms, a place to post your child's artwork or favorite book. Great restaurant recommendations for kids with a kids eat free listing plus also have grown-up time recommendations when you need a night out. There is a summer camp listing, where to get ice cream, and I have a blog where I write about my experiences as a Mom. Also, check out MasonMetu Marketplace for your online shopping needs. MasonMetu - Greater Cincinnati's one-stop site for family fun! Check us out and I guarantee there'll be something there to interest you!
