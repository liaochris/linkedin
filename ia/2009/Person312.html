Wayne McFarlane’s Summary

Our founder, Wayne McFarlane, has been in the transpotation industry for over a decade. In 1995 Wayne purchased his first vehicle and started his TAXI CAB services while working full time at MIT Container Ship Port as an assistant Voyage Planner in the Republic of Panama. Over the years he has developed a passion for professional chauffeur services to the general public and private sector with services that go above and beyond expectations. 1st Class Car Connexions, LLC provide airport transportation services to and from Tampa International Airport and other sorrounding airports. Our quality of service is what has set us apart from our competitors and is the reason why our clients keep returning. Wayne understood clearly that transportation without excellency in service would not help build a strong relationship with his clientel.
