Kimmo Rinta-Pollari’s Summary

Experience in 3d-modeling, texturing and animation. Have used 3d studio max from version 4-8. Nowadays using Cinema 4D for modeling and animation. Also Maxon's other great tool Bodypaint 3D for UVW-mapping and Photoshop for texturing.

Goals:

To make great looking 3d-visualisations for corporations to support their marketing efforts. Providing special skills to help customers to get best out of their projects.
To make connections with great people who manage other specific areas in 3d, multimedia and cg. Such as motion capture, flash-design and compositing/video editing.
To develop company services and get new collaborations with people who can work in larger design projects.

To make living out of all things mentioned.
