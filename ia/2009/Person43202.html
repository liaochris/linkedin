Chris Dyball’s Summary

I've been working in film and media since 1987 chasing waves from helicopters for Surfing Magazine, and escaped convicts for the Washington Post. I've traveled the low border towns of southern Kyrgyzstan with a Mig-29 fighter pilot and a UN peacekeeper, hunted kangaroos in Australia (no, none were injured), and been chased by wild dogs through the jungles of Samoa (karma for the kangaroos, I'm sure).

My greatest passion is for connecting key influencers within the nexus of art, technology and culture to craft a better world.

Chris Dyball’s Specialties:

Startups, Team Building, Everything Creative, Web 2.0
