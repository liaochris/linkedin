Brian Vandeputte’s Summary

Hi I'm Brian. I'm a Senior at Michigan State University studying Creative Advertising. According to #1 New York Times Bestselling Author Tom Rath's book "Strengths Finder 2.0", My Top Five strengths are Developer, Achiever, Positivity, Harmony and Empathy.

My Developer strength allows me to see the potential in others. No individual is fully formed, each is a work in progress, full of possibilities.

My Achiever strength explains my drive. I feel as if everyday starts at zero and I have a constant need for achievement. I must achieve something discernable to feel good about myself.

My Positivity strength means I am generous with praise, quick to smile, and always on the lookout for the positive in the situation. My enthusiasm is contagious and I can't quite escape my conviction that it is good to be alive.

My Harmony strength has me look for areas of agreement. There is little to be gained from conflict and friction. I can't believe how much time is wasted by people trying to impose their view on others. Wouldn't we all be more productive if we kept our opinions in check and instead looked for consensus and support?

My Empathy strength enables me to sense the emotions of those around me, as if their feelings are my own. I help people find the right phrases to express their feelings - to themselves as well as to others.
