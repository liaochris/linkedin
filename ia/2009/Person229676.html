Taylor Rath’s Experience

  • Vice President of Business Development

    The Strategy Network

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year)

    The Strategy Network is a full-service consulting firm that specializes in ballot measure campaigns – including ballot initiatives, referenda, constitutional amendments and bond measures. TSN produces and oversees all aspects of ballot measure campaigns, including: strategic planning, TV/radio advertising, media relations, direct mail, website design, phone banks, public opinion research, grassroots outreach, petition management and get-out-the-vote activities.

    In addition, TSN develops and implements effective public affairs measures by creating effective networks of neighbors, community organizations and governmental entities who can affect change or maintain the status quo through a strong grassroots and public relations effort or ballot measure. Our winning programs tell our clients' side of the story, build awareness to gain and mobilize public support.
