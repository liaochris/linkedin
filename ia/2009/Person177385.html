Andrew Nusca’s Summary

Editor, writer, multimedia producer.

Freelance work has appeared in The New York Times, The Huffington Post, New York Daily News, MensVogue.com, Popular Mechanics and PopularMechanics.com, MONEY, CNNMoney.com, Editor & Publisher, New York Press, Irish Voice, Irish America, DRUM!, TRAPS, Washington Square News and others.

Publisher of The Editorialiste, a media criticism blog.

Andrew Nusca’s Specialties:

Editing, writing and reporting, multimedia production, research and fact-checking, design and layout, real people stories, service pieces, product reviews, profiles, technology, business.
