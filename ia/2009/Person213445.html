David Hall’s Specialties:

I produce archival prints, in sizes up to 3 meters wide using customised profiles all of my work is printed personally and signed in editions of 15.

Face mounted Acrylic Block. This contemporary, polished-edge effect is achieved by sandwiching a print behind Perspex and dibond which, helps to protect image from UV light while adding a unique 3-dimensional effect to my images.

David Hall’s Experience

  • Visual Artist

    David Anthony Hall

    (Fine Art industry)

    January 1969 — Present (40 years 11 months)

    The work I exhibit gives me a huge sense of accomplishment and fulfilment, put simply 'This is exactly what I should be doing.' Upon finding a suitable location I will often wait days, weeks and even months for improvements in the available light, taking into account calculations of the various trajectories of the sun as the earth orbits throughout the seasons as well as the lunar tidal rhythm.

    This purposeful approach can result in planning shoots years in advance. Through my images, I seek to convey the beauty of our rich and diverse natural environment, I am forever humbled by the forces that have made it so and it’s to the frailty of human existence and just how much we are taking for granted.
