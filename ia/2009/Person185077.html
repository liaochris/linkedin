Perry Chen’s Summary

I am a 9-year-old boy in third grade at the Torrey Hills elementary school in Carmel Valley, San Diego. I have been writing movie reviews since Oct 08, and enjoy it very much!

Since then, I have become a columnist and movie critic for "We Chinese in America" weekly newspaper to review G/PG-rated family movies.

I have been featured extensively in San Diego media since March 09, including a Fox News TV interview, a feature on "San Diego News Network", a feature on La Jolla Light's four newspapers (Del Mar Times, Solana Beach Sun, Rancho Santa Fe Record, and Carmel Valley Leader).

My near-term goal is to be invited to attend the 2010 Academy Award as a young film critic! I would also be interested in writing for national media outlets to review G/PG-rated family movies. I am also interested in connecting with distinguished filmmakers of family movies.

I'm writing a movie screenplay for a fantasy animated feature, very exciting! I hope it will be made into a movie in the not too distant future.

Finally, by sharing my movie reviews, art, and experiences, I want to inspire young children, their parents, and teachers to take on their own journeys to discover and cultivate the children's natural talents and creativity!
