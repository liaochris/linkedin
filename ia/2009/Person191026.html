Regina Collins’s Summary

Ever Wonder Why?

Have you ever wondered why some people are more productive than others? Ever look at some people and wish you knew how to be that successful? After all, you’re smart and you have a passion for what you do. So what makes them more productive, more successful?

Often the difference between mediocrity and magnificence is a few skills and habits. Gifted with the ability to teach long-term skills and habits for productivity I launched Purposed Success, LLC to target individual and small businesses. There are many consulting firms that provide strategic planning for large companies but very few who will spend time with individual business owners and small businesses.

What is Purposed Success? Purpose is something that one hopes or intends to accomplish. Purpose is intention, resolution, and determination. Success is achieving a desired result, a favorable or desired outcome. To purpose success or have purposed success is therefore an intention, resolution, or determination to achieve a favorable or desired result.

The vision of Purposed Success, LLC is to provide solutions to enable you to achieve purposed success in your career or business. Click on the Contact Us tab and complete the online form for more information about achieving your own Purposed Success.
