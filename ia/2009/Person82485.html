John Furrier’s Summary

About Me - The Bottom Line:

I'm a pioneer, visionary, and implementer. I love developing startups. I've been often called crazy with ideas that have turned out very well. I'm currently focused on developing social media/online advertising products for corporations & advertisers in the Web 2.0 area.

Success areas: I've had the most success in identifying, designing, and deploying new solutions for new markets - entering new markets then handing if off to operational teams.

More Background:

I have a broad range of computer science and business expertise and experience that spans 25 years in computer systems, software development, networking, naming and search, directory services, internet infrastructure, online advertising, broadband wireless, online media, and web 2.0. I've lived through three cycles of technology innovation. I've experienced first hand how technology innovation creates disruption which in turn creates 'wealth creation'.

Examples in the past include: internet infrastructure, search marketing, web services, web 2.0, online advertising, and internet media.
