Jasmine High’s Summary

I enjoy working long hours back in the cavernous spaces of museums. With a background in collections management, I hope to take my masters degree to a smaller museum or historical house and improve the collections space by including proper integrated pest management, adequate storage and by training staff to appropriately handle objects at all times.
