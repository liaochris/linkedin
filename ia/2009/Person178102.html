Oleg Fadin’s Summary

Seven years of rich experience in Telecom and specifically VAS infrastructure and applications across all VAS segments – Voice, Data, Messaging, Content and Roaming.

Proven Track Record as Sales Manager with 4 years sales experience in reputable and market leading global FMCG companies (distribution, promotion, sales).

Unique blend of world-class technical, domain and business skills, strong thought leadership and strategic acumen coupled with focused and systematic approach to work leads to meticulous planning and result oriented execution. Proven track record of evolving product / service from idea / concept to launch covering the entire Product life-cycle from concept, Requirements & analysis, design / architecture, Proof of Concept, development, Quality Assurance, Testing, Deployment, Integration, Launch to promotions, marketing and revenue management. Expertise in technical product and vendor evaluation and commercial negotiations.

Global knowledge of telecom markets in Belarus, Russia, CIS countries, Asia, Europe and Unites States. 5+ Years of Qualitative Experience in Mobile VAS which includes VAS Marketing, Product Management, Vendor Management, Content Management, Usage & Penetration Analysis.

High level of the competence in Voice and Data wired and wireless technologies:

- Fiber-optic networks, TDM (Prepaid/IN, SS#7 and ISDN signaling), MethroEthernet, xDSL
- VSAT, Microwave, Wi-Fi, Mesh Networks, WiMAX, WLL
- Mobile networks (GSM, GPRS/EDGE, 3G UMTS HSDPA/HSUPA, LTE, CDMA, Voice&Data Roaming, Billing, SMS, MMS, USSD).
- NGN and all-IP networks (Softswitch, IMS, FMC, IP-Centrex, VoIP, IPTV, TriplePlay). Good knowledge of Cisco product portfolio.

In-depth knowledge of base ITU-T and 3GPP standards.

Excellent skills in business planning and interpersonal communication. Good command of English (advanced level, technical documentation), German (base level) and Polish (advanced level). Advanced PC user (Windows and Linux platforms).

Oleg Fadin’s Specialties:

ICT, Telecom, VAS (Value Added Services), Marketing, Branding, Sales, National Corporate and Civil Law. Especially interested in MVNE/MVNO projects.
