Marisa Moleiro’s Summary

It is my goal to assist people with their marketing and advertising needs so that they can be successful! Marketing expenses should not be wasted money, and instead should be an INVESTMENT into the future of your business in which you should expect a return. I can advise you on how to do that, and custom tailor a plan to fit your needs.

I have been working for The Real Estate Book in Martin County, the Bahamas, and Ft. Lauderdale helping Realtors since 2003. Before my position with The Real Estate Book, I ran a high profile, nationally-advertised salon and day spa on Long Island's prestigious north shore. Through my experience in the positions I've held, I have the expertise to help you make the most out of your marketing dollars and have fun doing it!
