Scott D Harris’s Summary

I have 17 years experience providing IT solutions to the manufacturing and Financial Services industry.

At Korry Electronics I was application manager responsible for telecommunications, networking and application development. I have worked at Russell Investments for the past 12 years. Initially I developed relational and DSS database systems for internal fund reporting. In my present position as product manager for one of our key analytical tools I am responsible for adoption as well as vendor and product life-cycle management.

I recently earned my MBA through the University of Washington Technology Management MBA program. While in the MBA program I am proud to have competed in the Sweet 16 round of the student business plan competition. My class team placed first in our capstone project by developing the best technology commercialization plan for an emerging technology coming out of the UW mechanical engineering department.

My goal is to use my experience and education and buy or start a small business that I can grow.
