A Key West Wedding can be romantic, simple and private or a large formal affair - that's up to you to choose.

Your wedding marks the transition from where you are now as man and woman to where you will be as husband and wife. I would be delighted to have the opportunity to be part of that new beginning with you. I know how important this day is for you and it is my job and my goal to ensure every detail is exactly what you want and how you want it to happen. Clients will get my personal attention for their wedding plans: I will work with them to ensure their destination wedding lives up to their dreams.

Choose from one of my personalized Wedding Packages, or we can work together to create a unique Wedding Affair designed especially for you; no matter how small, and within any budget. All of my Wedding Packages are based on two people, but most of them will accommodate your invited guests. I am qualified to make arrangements for your Photographer, Wedding Cake, reception, Catering, Music, flowers and transportation for your guests.
