David Siegel’s Summary

David Siegel, CAE, IOM, is the Executive Vice President of the Minnesota Restaurant, Lodging and Resort & Campground Associations and President and CEO of Hospitality Minneota, their management company. In addition, Siegel serves as President of the Hospitality Minnesota Education Foundatin.

Siegel decided on a career in association management after being hired as Communications Director for the associations in 1987. After 10 yearse in that role with ever-expanding duties, Siegel became the Executive Director of the Maine Innkeepers Association. In 2005, he accepted his current position as Chief Staff Officer of the three hospitality associations and their management company.

He is a past President of the International Society of Hotel Association Executives and the Maine Society of Association Executives. He is presently on the Board of Directors of the Council of State Restaurant Associations.
