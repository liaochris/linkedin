Mark Mitchell’s Summary

I have recently moved to become Head of Search for the OMD UK Group looking after 30+ search people and over £40 million in billings over 2008.


Previously I worked at The Search Works for over 4 years playing a key part in the operations development within our account management teams. In April last year I moved from our operations centre in Telford to join the Sales Team in our London office. My role consists of essentially a business development role whilst still supporting the sales team and working on ad hoc projects for existing clients.

I have won key brands in my time within the sales team and last year generated over £200,000 in PPC revenue from the clients I individually closed. As a team we continued to deliver for the business and represent the brand ethically in all our sales meetings. As an former account manager I see myself as a solid all rounder who can impressive within a sales meeting whilst also understanding what is achievable from an operations perspective.

I'm very passionate about Digital Marketing and feel at The Search Works are not just selling our services but the concept of Search Engine Marketing. With furher significant growth expected from the business I'm determined to help educate the market whilst delivering revenue for the business.

I'm very proud of the business and what we have achieved. From when Nick Hynes, Jim Brigden and Stephen Rust took over the business we never expected to see such huge growth and transformation. I'm honoured to be a part of that and now very excited to see the transition as we integrate with another impressive organisation being Tradedoubler.

Mark Mitchell’s Specialties:
