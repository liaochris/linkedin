Wayne Millheim’s Summary

Hi - I'm Wayne and ULTREX is in the business of providing the best solutions for our customers in the areas of document management and MFP's (multi-function devices that copy/print/scan/fax). I'm the Product Manager for our new SHARP line and am focusing on this product launch in several counties of California.

My focus is on phenomenal customer service and the solving of customers' problems - offering solutions - not just "hardware." I believe that anything less than excellence is unacceptable...and work with owners who feel the same way!

I've enjoyed working in many sales roles from account executive, sales trainer, up through sales manager - and have also filled in as a shop foreman and general manager as needed. I've also had the good fortune to work with and for some of the best management consultants in California.

Reach Wayne at: waynedude@gmail.com. I'm open to all serious business connections. And I'd be honored to have you check out my "Servant Leadership" blog.
