Chris Farrow’s Summary

With almost 20 years of experience in systems management and security, Chris has assisted many Fortune 1000 companies in securing their infrastructures. His background spans several industries, including DoD, healthcare, manufacturing, investment banking and software development.

Over his career, Chris has held positions as a sysadmin, systems engineer and product manager for several well known technology vendors such as Bindview Corporation, Intrusion.com and NetIQ. An active industry resource on the topics of compliance, security management and virtualization technologies, Chris was the founder of Configuresoft’s Center for Policy & Compliance, co-founder of the PCI Security Vendor Alliance and was the driving force behind the original CIS benchmark on virtual machine security. Chris has publicly spoken at numerous conferences including Blackhat, SANS, RSA, InfoSec, ISSA and ISACA. He is a SANS local mentor in Colorado Springs, CO, and holds various certifications from (ISC)2, ISACA, SANS, Microsoft and Novell.

Chris Farrow’s Specialties:

virtualization, computer security, regulatory compliance, vulnerability assessment, IDS, configuration management, green IT, power management
