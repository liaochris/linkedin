Margaret Szerlip’s Summary

Listing and Buyer Broker serving, Newton, Needham, Brookline, Weston, Wayland and West Roxbury. I believe I set myself apart with my negotiating skills and my abilty to incorporate what is happening in the financial markets and how that translates to real estate. I also have a very strong sense of what buyers and sellers want; mostly because I listen. My role is to make the home buying and selling process successful. In this climate there are always snags and the outcome sometimes depends on my ability to keep the process moving forward, and to know when to walk away from a deal. It is my job to educate my clients and to utilize all the information we have to make the best decision. I use technology extensively to produce reports, market data, and graphs. My Black Berry allows me to answer phone calls, e-mails and text mesages promptly.
