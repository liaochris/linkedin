Ricky Wong’s Summary

Dato’ Ricky Wong is the founder and CEO of Asia Media Sdn Bhd, a media powerhouse that reaches 1.5 million viewers daily.

Currently, Dato’ Ricky is the CEO of Asia Media Sdn Bhd, Malaysia’s largest Out-of-Home TV Network and Digital Signage operator which owns and operate more than 3000 Bus-TV known as Transnet(R).

“In less than 2 years, Dato’ Ricky has nurtured Asia Media into a media powerhouse in Malaysia by establishing a strong presence and brand name in the media and advertising.”

Dato’ Ricky acknowledges the need to forge strong governmental liaisons and is currently working with several government ministries.

Dato’ Ricky also believes in contributing to the society and as part of the company’s corporate social responsibility, Asia Media will continue to disseminate free public announcement and bulletins from the Government and NGOs, as well as work with the Royal Malaysian Police for its crime prevention programmes and community messages from societies.

With the contribution to the Government and societies, Dato’ Ricky has been conferred the Darjah Indera Mahkota Pahang (D.I.M.P) which carries the title “Dato” by His Royal Highness Sultan Pahang Sultan Haji Ahmad Shah in conjunction with HRH’s 78th birthday on 24 October 2008.

Dato’ Ricky was also recently awarded the Creative Young Entrepreneur 2008 Award by Junior Chamber International and SME Rising Star 2008 Award, whilst the company picked up the ‘Best Start-Up’ company award from MSC Malaysia APICTA (Asia Pacific ICT Awards) and also gained entry into the Malaysia Book of Records for the “Biggest Transit-TV network” with 3,175 networked screens installed at various public transportation vehicles.

Asia Media is a MSC status company that currently boasts more viewers than many other mainstream media in Malaysia. Under Dato’ Ricky’s stewardship, Asia Media’s growth has been exceptional and unparalleled in the industry.
