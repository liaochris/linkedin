Thomas M. Eppinger’s Summary

Being in business for more than 25 years, I am currently totally focused on Masterterminal:

For millions of consumers, the Internet has become an indispensable information and reference source for products and services. Nevertheless, barely half of all businesses currently have their own website, even less in the emerging markets. Conventional mercantile directories cannot solve this issue, for they neither provide extensive nor up-to-date information.

Masterterminal consists of individual websites, the so-called “MasterPages”, which share the same clearly laid out design. Each MasterPage provides comprehensive company information, including a professionally written portrait, a site plan and a route planner. Additional features are templates for brochures, business documents and business cards and a powerful solution for sending and managing data securely on the Internet. Our customers can manage the contents of their website independently and inform about all their offers and services up to the minute with an innovative and powerful content management system.

The complete solution for marketing, communications and data management captivates by simple handling despite a variety of functions.
