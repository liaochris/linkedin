John Sisler’s Summary

John Sisler is currently seeking a position as an SEO white hat architect specializing in application architectural design to support organic search result performance.

At GTP John Sisler was responsible to deliver and support an SEO strategy to turn an archaic php application into a scalable solution that included upgrading multiple platforms and managing code changes while allowing the business to operate. The strategy includes organic SEO, link building, paid search and usability.

John Sisler worked in Brulant's Search Engine Optimization practice where as a technical architect he designed SEO solutions for dozens of clients across most enterprise platforms. In 2007 Brulant was named in the top 20 SEO firms in the world and this year already has increased their visibility by accepting speaking engagements from search conferences located thoughout the country. John Sisler most recently was invited to speak at Microsoft Corporate offices on the topic of MOSS SEO.

Before that John Sisler worked on an IBM WebSphere Commerce and Web Content Management system at a large book seller in Ann Arbor, Mi. The client's goal was to bring their commerce site in-house and end their hosting relationship with Amazon which was the largest migration away from Amazon recorded. As part of this strategy Borders decided to focus their effort towards the online channel and sell of parts of the company that were not as profitable such as their international brick and mortar locations.

When John began at Brulant John worked in the Microsoft Practice. John Sisler was engaged with a large hotel chain who needed a portal based solution that would maintain corporate branding while not exceeding franchise budgets. The platform chosen was DNN which was used in a leveraged support model and is hosted by Brulant to accommodate all the requirements.
