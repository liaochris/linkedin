George Burr’s Summary

Marketing and sales professional with decades of multi-industry experience in both online and "offline" environments. The past 12 years have been spent in the Internet space, with hands-on experience in the set-up and management of online initiatives like pay-per-click search marketing, affiliate marketing, targeted/localized online promotional ad campaigns, and link soliciation efforts.

For the past 6+ years, I have also been the chief organizer and administrator of SMPnet (www.SMPnet.org), a not-for-profit 300-person sales/marketing networking group located in the Minneapolis-St. Paul metro area.

George Burr’s Specialties:

Pay-per-click search marketing, targeted promotional ad campaigns, affiliate marketing, and other kinds of lead development efforts over the Internet.
