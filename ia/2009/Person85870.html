Gerry James, GISP’s Summary

Geographic Information Systems Professional with 12+ years of experience. I have been involved in myriad types of projects from creating custom GIS applications for engineering firms to deploying enterprise/distributed GIS applications for the federal government. I have experience in both open source GIS software (e.g. Quantum GIS, GRASS GIS, FW Tools, PostGIS/PostgreSQL) and commercial GIS software (e.g. ArcGIS/ArcINFO, ArcGIS Server, ArcSDE).

Gerry James, GISP’s Specialties:

Open Source GIS architecture design, system development and deployment for enterprises. Software experience with PostGIS/PostgreSQL, GRASS GIS, GeoNetwork, UMN Mapserver as well as programming experience within the .NET framework.
