Chris Sargent’s Experience

  • Founder & Managing Director

    Sargent & Trayler Global Search

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    March 2009 — Present (6 months)

    Successful companies have one thing in common; they employ great people. Sargent & Trayler Global Search help you attract and retain great people.

    Sargent & Trayler Global Search is a technology search firm, operating across EMEA, APAC and the Americas. From our offices in London and Sydney, we offer a range of flexible, trusted recruitment and talent management solutions including Executive Search & Selection, Specialist Retained Search and Contingency Search. We believe that recruitment should not simply be viewed as a transaction. Therefore, in addition to traditional recruitment, we also provide talent-centric business consulting and leadership development services.

Our customers have been responsible for some of the most important innovations of our time and include market-leading Blue Chips as well as pioneering Start Ups. Their software, systems, solutions, services and people are advancing the capabilities of fixed & mobile carriers, internet service providers, broadcast & media companies and large corporations across the globe. Having a core focus on high technology has ensured the development of our capability, knowledge and expertise within this ever-evolving marketplace.

    Our goal is to demonstrate superiority and surpass all others who have come before. We are committed to delivering business excellence and the implementation of our Recruitment Quality Assurance process is at the heart of us being able to consistently deliver excellent results, beyond expectations. We are business partners who nurture the best relationships with our customers to ensure we anticipate and deliver outstanding service at all times.

    We have one, very simple aim; to excel. Through satisfying this obsession to excel in all areas, we will perfectly position ourselves to emerge as a leading technology search firm.
