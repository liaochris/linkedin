Reg Mywebedge’s Summary

What do I do? Well I could have you online with a whole new website by the end of the week – not just so you can have a website, but so you can get out on the web and actually pull in some new business.

What you get is a step-by-step system with a choice of designs and functions. So when you choose your design, you can add content like pre-written copy or news feeds, or use other tools like newsletters or payment systems.

But the point is, nothing’s pre-ordained - it’s whatever you need to grow your business.

So let’s say you want your website to grow your client base. You might choose to add a newsletter module, so you can build an opt-in mailing list and share your wisdom with the rest of the world. Then whenever you’re short of work, you can send out special offers to a list of people who already know and love you.

Or say you want more money from your current customers. You could add on a catalogue facility to sell all your products and services, and add to it by selling eBooks on the latest tips and tricks. No problem - you just choose the catalogue module and add a secure payments option, and by the end of it you’ve got a full e-commerce system.

And that’s it! You decide what you need to do, and the system does it for you. But the real beauty of it is that you don’t have to jump in at the deep end. Over time you can scale it up or scale it down so your website evolves along with your business.

I hope it doesn’t sound complicated, because really it’s anything but! There’s no building involved, and you don’t need to understand stuff like html codes because we’ve got a roomful of techy geeks who’ve done all that in advance!

You don’t need any technical skill to give yourself the edge.

In fact the product’s called MyWebEdge because this time week you could have a new website where all your different elements gel together to build you a seamless package. MyWebEdge gives you a serious edge over the competition!
