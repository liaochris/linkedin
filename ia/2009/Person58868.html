David Solie’s Summary

• Educator, clinician, speaker, author, and underwriting expert for life insurance.
• 25 years experience in underwriting for HNW clients who are utilizing life insurance as part of their estate planning strategy.
• Created unique representation strategies for HNW clients (Case Research Protocol) that have led to exceptional underwriting outcomes.
• Acknowledged expert in geriatric psychology whose pioneering regarding the developmental agenda of older clients has led to communication breakthroughs for countless professionals and layperson alike.
• Author of How To Say It To Seniors, a handbook of groundbreaking communication strategies for professionals who work with older clients.
• Respected speaker in the area of client communications whose training programs include The Secret Mission of Older Clients and The Secret Mission of Baby Boomers,
• Respected speaker in the area of longevity and quality of life whose training programs include The Longevity Code and Against All Odds: Preserving Quality of Life in a 24/7 World.

David Solie’s Specialties:

Risk assessment of life expectancy, medical analysis and underwriting, optimizing medical information to negotiate the most favorable life expectancy, HNW client presentations, HNW client relationship manager, lifestyle assessment and coaching, Finanical advisor presenations.
