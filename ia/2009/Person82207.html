Francisco Ríos Muñoz’s Summary

I have more than 10 years of professional experience in Research & Development (R&D) , Management of Technology (MoT), Planning & Control and Entrepreneurship in supporting to tecnological institutions and service companies involved in the mining industry.

My direct participation in the creation and planning of the Project “Technological Consortium for Development of Robotic Solutions in the Mining Industry” has been my main achievement. This was a join venture of more than 10 Millon US$, integrated by Industrial Support Co Ltd., Codelco, Kuka Roboter GmbH , Nippon Mining & Metals Co. It was sponsored by CORFO (Chilean Economic Development Agency) with 2.1 Millon US$. The technological company “MI Robotic Solutions S.A.” was born from this project and I had the opportunity to participate such as Substitute Director during the start up period.
