Mandy Priest Arnold’s Summary

As a marketing and communications professional with more than a decade of hands-on experience, Mandy manages her clients’ projects for accuracy and results.

Mandy has managed marketing, public relations and branding efforts in the healthcare, financial, building supply and CPG industries. She also offers a wealth of in-house experience allowing her to work seamlessly with any communications and marketing teams to deliver the most comprehensive results.

Beyond traditional marketing, Mandy is also the resident new media guru, with expertise in social media, search engine optimization (SEO), email marketing, website analysis and creation, social networking media, and much more. She is completely tuned into what’s new, what works and how it may or may not work for her clients.

Mandy holds a bachelor’s degree in communications, a graduate certificate strategic communications and integrated public relations and a master’s degree communications management.
