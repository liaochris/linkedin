Justin Giardina’s Summary

Throughout my professional career, I have demonstrated my leadership abilities, drive for success and passion for design.

As an employee of Wegmans Food Markets, I worked my up from a part-time department clerk, to managing corporate assignments. With these assignments, I would work with store management and corporate executives utilizing my graphic design and marketing skills to assist in the development of company-wide programs and in-store marketing materials.

In college, I studied Graphic Design, Communications and Media Arts and expanded my interests into the business world as well. With a strong interest in branding, I concentrated on advertising and marketing – developing products, packaging, environmental graphics and web designs.

As a graduate of the Rochester Institute of Technology, I have gained extensive knowledge in the foundations and esthetics of design. Combining my experience as a young adult with a keen interest in contemporary design, I’m confident I will bring a strong mix of an excellent education and broad knowledge of current social and cultural trends into the workplace.

Currently, I am employed as the Director of Art and Marketing for Urban Architectural Group located in Matthews. This job has challenged me to develop the department from the ground-up, including purchasing computer programs and hardware, the development and organization of company forms and processes as well as designs for company and client marketing and advertising materials. I have also been responsible for leading the rebranding and positioning efforts which will include a new website launch and a completed marketing plan in September 2008.
