Inger V Johannessen’s Summary

After many years in the rat race of the oil related industry, I developed my skills in IT and web design and started a new carrier in teaching / mentoring. It was during these years of teaching that I was introduced to the network marketing industry. I decided to take my leader- and mentoring skills to the next level.

Personal development is one of my major interests, and after reading Think and Grow Rich, The Secret, Rich Dad Poor Dad, I delved into Attraction Marketing. This was the right way of conducting business.

I developed Lifechanging Elements, a personal development newsletter. Here people can get inspiration and help to develop leadership and skills in Attraction Marketing or just a change in life and perspective. Whatever suits the person best.

The thoughts of Ann Sieg, Mike Dillard and other like minded people grabbed my attention.

As a teacher and eternal student, I developed my skills in Attraction Marketing and Social Marketing.

Now I was ready to help others achieve the success they wanted, giving help and support.

Timing is sometimes perfect, it was at this point a friend introduced me to a fully customizable Attraction Marketing System, no matter which MLM company you are part of. A great site. Highly recommended.

The ordinary MLM business doesn't actually give a person the security one is often seeking, as the company can go out of business or cancel your distributorship if they think you are not complying to their thoughts.

The best business practice would be to develop your own company, branding yourself, as I learned from Ann Sieg, Mike Dillard and others.

I was now introduced to the G.P.T. Business Model. I received the Coffee House Letter, and after reading this report, I found that my passion could be easier achieved. I am passionate about helping others achieve the highest level of success, and using my skills as teacher and mentor to develop leadership, this lets me help more people achieve greater success.
