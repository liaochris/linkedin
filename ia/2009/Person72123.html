Ellie Zarrabian, Ph.D.’s Summary

At Centerpeace we offer individuals the tools they need to communicate more effectively at home, at school and in the workplace, to resolve or manage conflict, develop better coping skills, and to integrate spirituality in everyday life.

The mission at Centerpeace is to help individuals achieve their goals and dreams, be more expressive and creative, improve relationships and live with emotional and spiritual integrity.

Ellie Zarrabian, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

Conflict management and resolution, effective communication skill building, cultivating and living with Emotional Intelligence, community/peace building, stress management, anxiety/depression management. Mindfulness Meditation classes and shamanic healing practices and apprenticeship.
