Keita Broadwater’s Summary

Dr. Broadwater is a business leader at Hewlett Packard with cross-functional accomplishments. He has worked in global organizations with accountability for revenue growth and expense reduction. He has achieved results in both customer-facing and back-office roles, and is comfortable working with people at all levels of an organization. His specialties are:

High Risk Product Development: Keita started out as an engineer, developing new products and technologies. After working through several product development cycles of risky products or volatile markets he understands what makes products successful, and why promising development teams stumble. He has experienced product development from all angles: R&D, operations, product marketing, and sales.

International Alliances: Dr. Broadwater has developed business relationships with partners around the globe, including Western Europe, East and South East Asia, Latin America, Africa, and North America. He understands the mechanics of business relationships: contracts, negotiation, and transactions; but he is also adept at building and maintaining the bonds that make alliances successful. Partners regard Keita as a man of high energy and integrity.

Sales and Market Development: Keita has led a global sales team, selling complex solutions in direct and indirect channels. He also has a firm grasp of the marketing lifecycle that bolsters sales.

Corporate Strategy: Dr. Broadwater has facilitated and led longterm strategic planning exercises and urgent strategic discussions. He has presented recommendations to executive and upper management, and communicated strategic decisions to the rank-and-file.

Operational Excellence: Keita understands the supply chain, transactional, and logistical underpinnings of the product lifecycle from conception to end-of-life. He has led the setup of operations for new products. He has experience in handling escalations when prompt responses matter.
