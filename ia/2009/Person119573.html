Jon Ostler’s Summary

Jon is the Founder and Managing Director of First Rate, and one of Australasia's leading authorities on online marketing. First Rate currently works with over 100 of Australia’s and New Zealand leading online brands, on the key areas of SEO, Paid Search, Email Marketing, Online Advertising and Website Conversion.

Jon sold First Rate to Q Ltd (ASX:QXQ) in 2007 and since then has been heavily involved in the development of this group of digital companies.

Jon spends his time between First Rate’s Auckland & Sydney offices managing a team of over 30 staff. First Rate has been listed as a Deloitte Fast 50 company 3 years in a row.

Jon is a regular speaker at online marketing events around Australia and New Zealand.

Jon Ostler’s Specialties:

Start-ups, Public Speaking, Technical Management, Business Consulting, Website Strategy, SEO, Search Engine Marketing, Email Marketing, Ad Networks, Website Conversion.
