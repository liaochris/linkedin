Michael Cohen’s Summary

Michael is a seasoned marketing executive with more than 15 years of hands on experience, focusing on driving direct-to-consumer acquisition, cross-sell, and retention strategies. He is an effective communicator with the ability to establish relationships with all levels of management and to coordinate efforts across business units, clients, and teams. His areas of expertise and skills include:

* Comprehensive experience in the methodologies of online advertising and marketing, direct and database marketing, email campaigns, customer relationship and life-cycle management, as well as customer Acquisition (CRM/PRM).

* Skilled in strategic planning and optimization, customer segmentation, budget management and forecasting.

* Proficient in channel management and fully knowledgeable in direct marketing regulations. Well-versed in the production and design process; familiar with database architecture and a wide variety of interactive technologies.

* Strong technical and analytical skills; experience administering online usability studies, surveys, interviews, and focus groups, as well as testing controls vs. challenger, creative and pricing.
