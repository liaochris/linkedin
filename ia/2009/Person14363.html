Andrew Horner’s Summary

A telecommunications apprenticeship, led to "end customer" technical training in the UK and overseas. On returning from overseas placement I broadened my experience into the commercial fields, concentrating upon Procurement. Marrying the technical experience with the commercial experience I then moved into Customer Relationship Management and support contract management, progressing quickly into CRM team man management. There then followed a move into the Bid / Proposal Management arena.

My aim to continue to broaden man management experience whilst delivering revenue growth via the delivery of successful tender submissions and business development opportunities together with process improvement through change management initiatives.
