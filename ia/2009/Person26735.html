Ben Stone’s Summary

Driven recruiter focused on mid to upper level IT positions in the Omaha area.

I believe in doing business in an old fashioned and organic manner. I want to earn respect and the privilege of connecting with others by hard work, honesty, and verve. I found this list on someone's LinkedIn profile and I think is summarizes my thoughts very well when it comes to a business philosophy:

1. Fact-based pragmatism - Drives decision making - Is where the organization starts when problem solving - The ego is left at the door

2. Entrepreneurial risk-taking - No afraid to take a risk - Risk takers are rewarded and given more responsibility - Constant thinking about new initiatives

3. Collegiate, collaborative, non-combative relationships - Professional culture - Fun is ‘allowed’ in the work place

4. Detailed analysis before judgment - Take the time to get the facts - Give time for the facts to present themselves

5. Totally open communication - Talk Straight - Open honest communication - Safe to speak your mind

6. Objective assessment of results - Again, no ego involved - Let the facts speak for themselves

7. Recognition for achievement, not status

8. Flat, non-hierarchical structure - Typically found in smaller companies
