Uday Shankar’s Summary

Uday Shankar is a Technology Entrepreneur interested in created business ventures using new technology. Uday is serving as CEO for a High-tech software company Ennovasys. Ennovasys provides product visibility to enterprises using GPS, Mobile, Sensors, WiFi and RFID technologies. Ennovasys was founded in November, 2007 in Chennai, India. Ennovasys is a solution provider for Indian Market.

Uday also founded Supply Insight Inc. in 2004 for creating RFID solutions for DoD market.

Uday served on the Board of Directors of privately held companies in India. Uday was one of the Directors of Engineering for a business process integration company primarily handling customer relations, services and quality assurance.

Uday has also served in industry's leading consulting companies such as Kanbay and Infosys, executing multiple projects for Fortune 100 companies.

He received a Bachelor's Degree in Engineering from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.
