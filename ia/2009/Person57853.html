David Halek’s Summary

I have a pretty diverse work history. Aside from four years in the USMC as a machinegunner and clerk, I've worked in the retail and restaurant industries mainly. I've been a receiving/warehouse manager, bar manager, bartender, and a lot of other positions in those industries.

Outside of that, I've worked in data entry, drafting, and as a lens technician (making eyeglasses).

My immediate goals is to get work as a freelance writer/ghostwriter. I plan on over-delivering on quality and amassing as much of a steady clientele as possible so that I can get back into college and pursue a copywriting degree.
