Victor Liu’s Summary

About Me:

My life is about making things happen, and for the last 7 years, I've spent my waking moments building a business. I believe work should be fun and you should enjoy what you do. At Web Advanced, I'm the sales/marketing guy. I spend most of my time working with clients on creating a successful website for their business, and by successful, I mean 'highly visited and profitable'.

I enjoy building relationships in and outside of work. It's always interesting to learn about what other people do for a living. I especially enjoy meeting other entrepreneurs, as we are in a fraternity that few can relate. Our careers can be demanding and stressful, but it also yield some of the best rewards in life.

Professional Bio:

Victor Liu is the CEO and co-founder of Web Advanced. With over 9 years of development and management experience in the high tech field, Mr. Liu has worked with companies such as Accenture, FileNet, Endeavors Technology, and many others. Mr. Liu tripled the annual revenue of his company in the first 4 years of being in business. Currently, Mr. Liu heads up all business development and sales/marketing initatives at Web Advanced, managing both customers and vendor's expectation as well as making sure both the sales and implementation teams meet their deadlines. Mr. Liu graduated from UC Irvine with a degree in Information and Computer Science and currently is on the Curriculum Advisory Board for the School of Information and Computer Science. Mr. Liu is also an active participant of both the Business Alumni Organization and the Computer Science Alumni Organization at UC Irvine. When Mr. Liu is not working 80 hour weeks, he can be found hooping it up on local basketball courts, biking to the beach, and traveling around the scenic West Coast, laptop and cellphone in tow.
