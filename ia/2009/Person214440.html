Sandy Gerber’s Summary

What do we do? We revitalize brands, especially through the power of the web!

3 Degrees West was created in 2005, when Twist Multimedia and ThreeKids Media merged forces. The two partners, Sandy Gerber and Karley Cunningham have collectively over seventeen years in advertising and marketing and specifically eleven years in web architecture, design and marketing.

"Due to our passion for our work and our honest desire to achieve effects for our clients, "competitors" are not an issue. After all, every project or program a person executes in business relies on people. At the heart of a successful project lies a team of individuals with varied skillsets and personalities. All of whom are accountable to the success of the project.

My favourite milestone in a project is not when it succeeds, as we know it will, but more importantly it is the defining moment when the connection is established between ourselves and you, our new client. It is the visible comfort and trust you express in our meeting that fosters our creativity." Sandy Gerber, 3 Degrees West

A few of our Fabulous Clients include: Appleton Estate Jamaica Rum, Scenic Tours, Grand Marnier, The Renaissance Gourmet, Shoeless Joes Restaurants, Virtus Inc, The Source Group, Donaldson Ropes, Pencilneck Software, Gilroy Stained Glass, University of British Columbia, Firefighters of York Region, Aurizon Mines, Mark Staff Photography, Peak Potentials Training, Fionn Griffiths Racing, Mills Basics, KidZone, Joxwear, Innovation Networks, White Spot and Buntain Insurance.

Sometimes all it takes is three degrees of new perspective to take you where you want to go...
