Monique Montecinos’s Summary

Monique Montecinos brings an extensive amount of experience to executives, professionals, business owners and other clients seeking interim and project assistance. Within the past nine years, she has earned a reputation for providing skilled and practical guidance as a recruiting professional. Her client engagement list includes Marathon Oil, British Gas, various Private Equity firms, ExxonMobil, Anadarko, Shell Oil, Hines Interest, and American Express.

Previous to her career in staffing, Monique received her Bachelors degree from Texas A&M University and pursued a career in social services.

Monique began her career in recruiting in April of 2000. Within her first seven months, Monique reached the Million Dollar Milestone award through her diligent work in establishing herself as a practical resource for her Downtown Houston client and candidate base. Since then, she expanded her focus to include managing a full time staff of consultants through the downturn of the economy to a growth of 60% within a year’s time. Monique has led a team which experienced an exciting year over year growth of 50% through the team’s dedication to excellence for our clients and candidates. This team’s growth placed them within the top ranks of national and international offices. Most recently, Monique generated a year over year revenue growth of 213% in twelve month’s time through identifying key areas of immediate operational and customer service improvements. Monique has been an active participant in the Petroleum Accounting Society of Houston, the American Society of Women in Accounting, American Business Women’s Association, and the Texas A&M Reveille Club.
