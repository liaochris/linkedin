Lynette Sheppard’s Summary

Writer, Photographer, Blogger and Artist are a few of my roles. I’m a Registered Nurse, but these days my job (read passion) is connecting with and disseminating the wisdom of menopausal women via my blog at www.menopausegoddessblog.org and as the Health Maven for the Menopause Community on Wellsphere.com. Six years ago, a core group of menopausal goddesses came together to make sense of the Changes assaulting us. What we learned and shared helped us beyond our wildest imaginings. Our small community has since expanded to a larger virtual community where we continue to share and grow every day. Together, we're tackling not only menopause but the challenges of midlife and creating the second half of our lives. Our new book of wit, wisdom, and whimsy is available exclusively on our website. Titled "The Big M" - it's everything you want and need to know about this transition. www.thebigmwebsite.com.

In addition to being the official 'Scribe” for the Goddesses, Lynette is an expert on the Enneagram of personality; author of The Everyday Enneagram, A Personality Map For Enhancing Your Work, Love, and Life...Everyday, She consults with myriad businesses and organizations, find out more at www.9points.com.

Lynette Sheppard’s Specialties:

Humor, heart, and help for the Menopause transition, The Enneagram of personality: corporate culture, teambuilding, leadership, growing client relationships, understanding others in the areas of family, intimate relationship, and self development.
