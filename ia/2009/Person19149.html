Adam Johnson’s Summary

Designing since the mid 90s, Adam has left his creative mark, in the form of logos, billboards and graphic design work, both locally and abroad. With projects for local companies, restaurants and civic organizations, Adam has experienced many facets of the multimedia market. After founding ARC Design in the late 90s he soon moved his thriving business from Raleigh to Fayetteville, North Carolina, where it has continued to grow.
