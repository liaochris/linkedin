Jeff Hoffman’s Summary

Jeff Hoffman has experience helping organizations transform their processes and improve the effectiveness of their finance organizations. His work has included process analysis, business process re-engineering, and system design. He also has experience optimizing finance operations in global organizations with departments and processes that are geographically dispersed in both industrialized and non-industrialized parts of the world.
