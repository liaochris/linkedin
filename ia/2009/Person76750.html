Eva Velasco’s Summary

Extensive experience in product marketing, brand strategy and key account management in the healthcare industry. Broad international experience, my assignments have taken me from my native Spain to Europe (focus on Germany and France) and the United States, where I worked during six years as Global Marketing Manager for Alcon Laboratories, Inc in Fort Worth, Texas.

I recently moved back to Spain, where I joined efforts with Annabel de Maria, also a pharma brand manager, and created the boutique marketing firm DEMARIA&VELASCO to provide strategic consultancy, training and coaching to the healthcare area, primarily in the DTC, or patient area.
