Mark Moebius’s Summary

17 Years Experience in The Radio Broadcasting Industry

In 1992 I created a Marketing Company that bridged the gap between traditional marketing (Print, Design, Direct Mail, Radio and Television) and Internet Marketing, still today we offer what most companies can't...expertise with traditional marketing while offering clients technology in house.

In 2004 MILJONAIR Development was made to offer multi-million dollar homes in the most spectacular places. Current we have 2 subdivisions in the St. Albans MO area with plans to offer old world design of homes centered around less square footage and incredible extras. Stone cottages with slate roofs and copper gutters plus interiors that are built for the way you live.

On the horizon the purchase of a Dinner Train and Local Radio Station.
