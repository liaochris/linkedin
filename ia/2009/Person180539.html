Pamela Moraga’s Summary

Ms. Moraga is a senior executive with 27 years leadership experience in diverse industries such as consulting, government, aerospace and defense, manufacturing, electronics, education, pulp and paper, and telecommunications. Other qualifications include an MBA from the University of California – Berkeley, certified systems integration consultant, and a graduate of GE’s Financial Management Program.

As a senior executive with ManTech, she leads technology programs focused on National Intelligence Agencies. Prior to that, as a Principal with Booz Allen Hamilton, she led a business that provided management consulting services focused on technology-enabled business transformation and performance improvement for government clients.

Ms. Moraga is personally known for her ability to turn around troubled projects and to manage large-scale complex consulting engagements. Those engagements have ranged from systems integration jobs and ERP, to management consulting projects involving organizational change management and business process re-engineering. Her most recent role was the Organizational Change Management and Communications Director for Virginia’s $2B IT Outsourcing Program where she managed resources and activities targeted to gain stakeholder acceptance and adoption of the new business model. The program entailed a full-scale transformation creating an enterprise shared services environment.

Ms. Moraga also served as program manager for Booz Allen's piece of a multi-year, $2B transformation program of a large national security agency (NGA). She provided executive oversight for about 150 consultants who performed systems engineering, systems integration, management consulting, and infrastructure support. Earlier, as the chief business architect and a key leader in the Program’s Transformation Office, she led business process re-engineering (BPR) and organizational change management activities.

Technology-Enabled Business Transformation, Program Management, Organizational Change Management, Systems Integration Consulting, Enterprise Resource Planning, Enterprise Application Systems Implementation, Business Process Re-engineering (BPR), Strategy
