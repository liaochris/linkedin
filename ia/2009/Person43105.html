Chris Cruz’s Summary

I have established myself as a successful sales professional and leader within the telecommunication industry. Over the last 20 years, my skills have been honed selling direct to Fortune 500 companies. The ever changing marketplace and the challenges it presents continues to fuel my desire to sell solutions to my clientele.

The goals I set personally and among my team members are to take ordinary sales results and elevate them to extraordinary by taking a very methodical, strategic selling approach to grow existing business relationships, and acquire new logos.

Ultimately, my goals are centered on family, team and personal growth.
