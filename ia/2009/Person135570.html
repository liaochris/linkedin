Adriaan Tijsseling’s Summary

On the one hand I have a PhD in cognitive science (in particular, neural network modelling) and on the other hand I'm an experienced software engineer. The last couple of years, I have been focusing on internet-related projects. I created desktop applications that interact with the internet (e.g. blogging, Flickr client, etc). I helped write Technorati's web-services API, and I do a wide range of consulting about blogging, APIs, standards and web-services.

For desktop clients, my platform of choice is OSX. Currently, I program mostly in ObjC, C++, Python, PHP, and JavaScript, but it doesn't mean I don't know any other languages (C, Perl, Ruby, etc).

You can read all about me and my work on http://infinite-sushi.com.

Adriaan Tijsseling’s Specialties:

Developing new desktop applications, web-services, APIs, and promoting standards. My orientation is broad and I have expertise in a variety of areas. I consider this one of my best aspects.
