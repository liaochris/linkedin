Gary Fitzgerald’s Summary

I specialise in Discretionary and Advisory Portfolio Management here in Goodbody Private Clients. Goodbody Stockbrokers offer a range of investment opportunities covering Managed Equity Portfolios, Property, Private Equity, Third Party Specialist funds, Retirement Planning and Alternative Investments. We offer one on one accessibility to a personal portfolio manager, as well as complete visibility over the clients investments through on-line access, contract notes and regular reporting. Clients can be as involved in the investment process as they choose to be, ranging from execution only accounts to full discretionary management.
If you think that I can be of assistance to you, please feel free to give me a call on 01-6419231 or email me at gary.a.fitzgerald@goodbody.ie
