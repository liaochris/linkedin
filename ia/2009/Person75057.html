Erika-Marie S. Geiss’s Summary

Wordsmith with over a decade of editing and publishing experience. Agented published author, working on sixth book (fourth solely-authored book).

Professional goals are to push the envelope in magazine publication for the work-at-home parenting community with theWAHMmagazine--the only content-driven digital magazine for work-at-home parents with a mission that is dedicated to validating, empowering, encouraging, educating and supporting work-at-home parents. I am also deeply committed to new media with a specific focus on digital editions and other green publishing. (I don't believe that print is dead, but that there is need and ample room for print and digital editions to coexist.) I aim to take theWAHMmagazine to the next level and have it become a key, well-known resource for current and future work-at-home parents including telecommuters, freelancers, business owners and others who work from home full time or part time.

My other goals are to continue writing books and articles and to help other individuals and professionals present their best content with effective editing from Red Pencil Editing Services.

Erika-Marie S. Geiss’s Specialties:

Writing (articles and non-fiction books). Magazine design/layout, editing, editorial decision-making for magazine production. Proofreading, copy editing, substantive editing, ghost writing, proofreader's marks and symbols, strong communications skills, teaching experience (college level), public speaking, writing (books, periodicals and blogging), AP, Chicago, MLA and other writing styles, fluent in English and Italian, proficient in Spanish, reading knowledge of French and German.
