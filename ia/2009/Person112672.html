Jill Hoffspiegel Asher’s Summary

Seasoned Human Resource Executive turned blogger/community organizer/social media strategist!

I am the Co-Founder of Silicon Valley Moms Group. Since 2006, I have recruited and grown this influential, collaborative mom blog community to over 300 contributors (mom bloggers) and 10 sites (and more to come!).

http://www.svmomsgroup.com

In addition to Silicon Valley Moms Group, I consult with companies to reach out to consumers through social media tools and bloggers (the blogosphere). This includes hiring bloggers/journalists/freelance writers/great talent to create content for corporate sites, define social media strategy and create strategic partnerships.

Specifically, I hire bloggers to write content for companies, build positive PR & Blogger relationships and ensure marketing programs and outreach are successful.

More information about Silicon Valley Moms Group.

The Silicon Valley Moms Group LLC owns and operates regional mom blogs around the United States. Our sites showcase the ups, downs, outrages, struggles, victories, and everyday humor of motherhood. Our more than 300 writers are working moms, stay at home moms, city moms, suburban moms, and rural moms—but, most of all, they are opinionated moms.
