Simon Melaniphy’s Summary

The pinacle of my Internet career, as Managing Director of Refreshed Media, has seen myself and my hand picked team go from a standing start to £1m turnover within 3 years. Playing a key role in pitching to national brands, as well as strategic and technical growth, helped significantly in us receiving and accepting an offer for a majority stake in the business in 2006. With even more rapid turnover and geographical growth now taking place under the new company structure, this challenging and fast paced role is as exciting as it is rewarding.

Prior to this role, my exposure to the internet began in 1991 and my degree was in Computer Science/Software Engineering, and my career has included sales roles and IT and network consultancy, installation and support.

Through a strong passion for music, I have also worked launching and managing a successful DJ agency, event management and promotion, A&R Licensing Manager, as well as time as a club and radio DJ, with audiences of up to 100,000 on-air in Australia and regional UK audiences to a TSA of 250,000.
