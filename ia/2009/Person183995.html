Pavel Farkas’s Summary

Pavel Farkas has been working in broadcast media for 15 years. He is a freelance journalist and documentarian involved with printed and broadcast media, interested in photography, languages, art and architecture as well as lasting and worthwhile projects. He is currently enrolled in two graduate media programs at the Charles University in Prague, Czech Republic. He travels extensively (U.S.) and is considered to be an independent, organized and creative individual who pays attention to details.
