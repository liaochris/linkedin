Ruben Toral’s Summary

Ruben Toral is a recognized expert in branding, medical tourism and healthcare globalization.

Formerly Group Marketing Director for Bumrungrad International, Ruben is widely credited for pioneering medical tourism in Asia and positioning Bumrungrad as one of the world’s leading medical tourism destination.

With over 15 years experience in healthcare marketing, Ruben has built companies, brands, physician networks and has distinguished himself as a thought leader in the area of healthcare globalization. He is the Conference Chair for The Healthcare Globalization Summit, external advisor the Joint Commission International Task Force on Globalization and president of the International Medical Travel Association.

As CEO of Mednet Asia Ltd., Ruben provides strategic consulting, branding and marketing support services to the healthcare industry at large. His clients include Apollo Hospitals, Bumrungrad International and United Health Group International. In addition to his consultancy work, Ruben is the creator of Medeguide, a web portal to connect patients searching for international doctors and treatment packages, and Executive Health Retreats, a company specializing is 3 day corporate health and wellness retreats for Asian based executives.

Ruben is an accomplished speaker and writer on the topics medical tourism, healthcare globalization and lifestyle medicine. His insights appear in business, travel and trade publications like Fast Company, Medical Travel Today, BusinessWeek and Spa Asia

Ruben Toral’s Specialties:

Healthcare marketing, branding, building physician networks, web platforms

For doctors and hospitals, Medeguide is an online platform to market their services and products to the millions who search for medical information daily. Using Medeguide, patients are able to search and find doctors globally based on location, specialty, procedures and board certification, and treatment packages based on country and procedure.
