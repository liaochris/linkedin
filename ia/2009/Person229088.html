Tanuj Rai Taneja’s Summary

After completing his Bachelors of Science degree in Computer Information Systems from The University of Tampa, Florida in 1993, USA, Tanuj has been in the software services sector for over 15 years. Out of the 15 years, he has worked for 7 years in the United States and the remaining in India.

Tanuj domain skills are in client delivery of software development/ maintenance and production support in Human Resource Batch and Online Systems, Banking and Financial Systems, Subscriptions and Fulfillment Systems, Telephony Billing, Order Entry, Inventory Management, Supply, Customer Contact for Delinquent Collections systems.

His work experience has been with top Fortune 500 companies like Time Customer Service a subsidiary of Time Warner, Inc., Verizon Data Services subsidiary of Verizon Communications, Inc and top I.T consulting companies like Keane, Inc. and Computer Associates Inc. USA.

Tanuj is currently the System Project Manager of Batch and online development Shared Services of HRMS on TBA, Production Support and Client Delivery team handling 146 employees with 7 direct reports as System Project Leaders supporting multiple clients. In August’ 08 he was awarded a Quest Award for his implementation of process improvements which saved the company over US$ 400,000. His idea has been sent to Hewitt’s global process excellence team for a nomination of the Q4 HRO Innovation Award.

He is a very mature project and delivery manager handling large complex systems with teams in Noida, Gurgaon and Chennai. He is extremely good in people and client management and has consistently received the highest CSS (Customer Satisfaction Survey) scores across all development and production support teams in FY 07 and 08.
