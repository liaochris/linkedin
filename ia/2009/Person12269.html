Amy Neumann’s Summary

Since my first job with AT&T in early 1994, when the potential of email was still a little "questionable"?, internet media and technology has been constant fascination of mine. Media, networking, search, security, advertising, design - for more than 15 years now, it's been a fun, rapidly-evolving learning experience. New technology and internet media is not just my focus, my business, my hobby, and my passion... It's also a way to help give back to others, and a way to help clients. Maybe I spend too much time researching and learning about new ideas and trends, and how they apply to fixing problems - because I LOVE it.
For the past three years, my primary focus has been consulting with F500 corporate clients. The goal is to understand and help orchestrate how they can use all these fun new tools to achieve their business goals. Trends and technology are fun, but ROI is king.

I'm also passionate about creating ways to incorporate Philanthropy into business strategies as a competitive advantage. Some of the most impressive people I've ever met spend a great deal of their non-work time helping others. What business isn't looking for those people?
