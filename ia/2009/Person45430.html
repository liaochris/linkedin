Christopher Sabec’s Summary

Christopher Sabec is an international entertainment industry executive, entrepreneur, creative thinker, and accomplished attorney with more than 20 years of combined business management, global entertainment industry, and digital media and entertainment technology experience. He is well established in the domestic and international entertainment communities, having traveled and conducted business in 30 countries on 5 continents. Christopher is currently the CEO of Plushy Feely Corp., the home of Kimochis. Christopher has served as CEO of the Jerry Garcia Estate LLC, Manager for Hanson, and Attorney for Dave Matthews and earned his J.D. from the University of Georgia School of Law and his undergraduate degree from Georgetown University School of Foreign Service. Christopher and his wife, Jacqueline, reside with their 2 daughters in Northern California.

    Feelings can be messy...Kimochis can help! Our goal is to provide helpful information and communication tools to parents, teachers and children to enable them to learn how to be better communicators and to express their feelings. Using Kimochi characters, kids can get in touch with their own emotions in a fun and comfortable way, building self-esteem. And when kids can communicate their feelings, they cultivate confidence and character.

    Kimochis™ build character and confidence one feeling at a time.
