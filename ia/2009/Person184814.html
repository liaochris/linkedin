Penelope Herbert’s Summary

I believe in following passions and mine are teaching/training and business writing. As a presenter/trainer, I engage participants to think strategically, creatively and collaboratively about their marketing (online and offline), understanding the needs of their customers and their non-manipulative sales approach. Marketing is an overall strategy and I encourage the exploration of myriad solutions not predicated on paid advertising. It's not the size of your budget, it's how you use it. Further, intelligent sales knowledge is an imperative to successful marketing.

This dovetails nicely into my passion for writing about business & media issues, and interviewing successful business people about their challenges and strategies for success. I have been Contributing Editor to 'in-business' magazine for 7 years writing features. Prior to that I was Editorial Director of a national fashion and lifestyle magazine.

My recently published book 'Underdog Marketing' is a showcase of real-life business studies and the development and implementation of creative communications, strategic marketing, profile raising public relations and globally reaching internet marketing.

My goals include writing another insanely clever marketing book, directing a movie based on my own fictional novel and growing my seminar and business writing career, with more travel included.

I am looking for opportunities to work with big and small business to present seminars, train staff and business owners, and write communications.
