Brett McDonell’s Summary

I specialize in a vast array of financial services ranging from Gemstone/Jewel Lending on loose, rough or polished stones and entire portfolio's , Acquisition & Development loans ($10M - $500M+), Mezzanine, Bridge land hard money loans. I also work directly with venture capital & private equity investors to place my clients debt/equity financing needs, structuring the purchasing or leasing of BG's, SBLC's and MTN's. I Can also provide Blocked funds for investments/ trade platforms and balance sheet enhancements up to $1 Billion, I also work directly with a private fund that will lend up to 95% face value against cash backed bank instruments such as SBLC's/BG's from any top 50 world bank!

As Senior Vice President of MG Financial Group, Mr. McDonell brings over 6 years of mortgage origination experience in both residential and commercial loans. Having held positions at Fortune 500 companies Countrywide and First Horizon Home Loans he looks to catapult MG Financial to a new level. Mr. McDonell is in charge of new business development with investors, lenders, institutions, brokers, builders and developers in an effort to continue MGF’s takeover in the private money lending arena.

Brett McDonell’s Specialties:
