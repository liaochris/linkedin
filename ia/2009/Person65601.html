Don Blais’s Summary

My entire professional career has been focused on the printing and graphic communications industry. From integrating a part-time job in outside sales for our family business while attending college, to managing and subsequently purchasing the business in 2001, I've watched the industry negotiate some dramatic transformational processes. My initial degree program when I entered college was 'programming' (i.e., Basic, COBOL, C++, etc.). This was the early days of computers, before the advent of the web and the proliferation of personal computers. It has been remarkable to watch the printing industry adapt to these digital progressions. While digital technologies have impacted every industry, I believe none more so than the printing and graphics trades. It is exciting to see a mature industry radically impacted and disrupted by these new technologies. For those of us keeping pace and leveraging these new opportunities, I believe the future of our industry is bright, and look forward to the challenges and changes ahead.
