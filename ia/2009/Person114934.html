JoAnn Magno’s Summary

JoAnn Magno specializes in consumer product licensing, sponsorships, and entertainment. She is licensed to practice law in the State of California and is admitted to the United States Supreme Court. Ms. Magno was formerly employed by Mattel, Inc. where she worked in the Consumer Products Department in the Boys Toys & Entertainment Division. Additionally, she served as a systems implementation project manager for Sony Pictures Entertainment’s Consumer Products Division. Ms. Magno received her undergraduate degree from U.C.L.A. and her J.D. from Southern Methodist University, School of Law and was recently nominated for the 2007 American Media & Entertainment Association Awards for Excellence in Branded Entertainment.
