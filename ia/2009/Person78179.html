Fatt Weng Wong’s Summary

Fatt Weng has almost 20 years of extensive brand and sales experience in fast moving consumer goods across Food and Non Food categories. He started his career as a management trainee with Unilever Malaysia. After 5-year tenure with Wall’s Ice Cream as the brand manager, upon his return from a secondment with Unilever Thailand, he moved to Quaker Oats Malaysia, with a regional marketing responsibility covering Malaysia, Singapore and Thailand. His new position as the Product Group Manager, prepared him to be a strong commercial manager, a role which also required him to manage a group of distributors in each of the market it represents.

Following a 4 years stint at Quaker Oats, Fatt Weng subsequently joined Cadbury Confectionery Malaysia as their Marketing Manager. The 7 years he spent at Cadbury was his best in his career as he was also able to develop himself in various non marketing roles such as field sales, strategy development, commercial planning, category management, consumer insights and point-of-purchase management. He had also been expatriated to Cadbury Schweppes Australia, based in Melbourne to work as a State Field Sales Manager, Victoria for over a year.

In June 2006, Fatt Weng took up the position of a Marketing Director with Johnson & Johnson Malaysia where he oversaw market leadership businesses in multiple personal care (baby care, women hygiene, skincare and oral care) and healthcare (OTC drugs) categories. Highlight of his achievements at J&J include the winning of 1st Effie’s Gold award for Johnson Baby Infant Massage campaign.

Fatt Weng joined Bates 141 Malaysia in June 2008 as the Managing Director. His joining of Bates141 represented a cornerstone of his career due to the change in the industry that he has been very familiar with all this time. At Bates141, he is looking at a career that can elevate his capabilities as a successful people manager and at the same time continue his passion in building brands that people love.
