Rob Glass’s Summary

As President of Glass & Gold, Inc. I develop and implement positioning strategy and marketing communications plans for colleges and universities. Enhancing the student educational experience is my passion.

I partner with graphic artists, writers, photographers, production artists, web designers and staff, programmers, and print production professionals to create print & electronic marketing communications programs.

Services also include communication flow strategy, project management, senior administration advising and consulting, and qualitative / quantitative research development, student orientation review and recommendation, student life experience enhancement.

With more than 25 years and 80+ clients within the higher education community, I've come to understand that long term success is achieved through collaborative partnership, not through independent effort. Effective educational marketing touches all aspects of the student experience and is a community commitment, not a campaign.
