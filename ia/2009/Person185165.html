Pete Berryman’s Summary

I am an experienced senior project manager with considerable commercial skills, a flexible and knowledgeable approach to difficult deliveries across multiple workstreams, and excellent team management credentials.

I have played a critical role in influencing the success of many high profile projects, ensuring the delivery, integration and operational aspects of complex web-based solutions that support the business flows as defined by customers across a variety of sectors.

As a successful project manager I can demonstrate the following:

• Ensured clear technical, commercial and project governance, including reviews, scope and acceptance criteria.

• Ensured best practice lifecycles and project management methodologies are followed to guarantee a consistent delivery of the project and subsequent service.

• Ensured a holistic approach balancing commercial and business benefits with technical considerations and design trade-offs.

• A good understanding of financial planning including P & L statements, balance sheets and cost/benefit models.

• Ability to delegate effectively to business, technical and operational staff, whilst maintaining full management control.

I have the following skills and experience:

• A strong and rounded technical background with many years of experience in the delivery of complex IT systems involving multiple workstreams and diverse stakeholders, involved in the design and development of solutions including integration and the full support and service lifecycle.

• Worked within a Prince2-type framework to properly define and track the project and have successfully married this to an Agile approach that works well in fast-moving environments.

• Experience of integrating software applications and hardware platforms, working in conjunction with technical teams to define and test the systems.

• Proven written & verbal communication, negotiating & presentation skills and the ability to motivate staff.
