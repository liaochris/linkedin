Abigail Lipson Mahnke’s Summary

I handle projects for businesses that they don't have the time, staff or desire to do. These are typically one-off projects that are essential for a business to get done, but do not contribute directly to the bottom line or billable hours (hence, they don't get done!). I help businesses grow while they focus on their bottom line.

I have a core set of well-developed skills that enable me to perform a range of tasks for clients, including project management, page layout, writing, editing, proofreading, QuickBooks bookkeeping, research, and more. I also work in such software as InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator.

As the former owner of Full House Property Management, I am familiar with many aspects of running a business, and love helping other businesses to perform well.

I work from my own office or my client's office, and I'm also willing to travel. (In fact, I spent 4 days in a booth at Toy Fair in NYC selling dolls this past February.)
