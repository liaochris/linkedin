David Lipschitz’s Summary

David is a software engineer with a Computer Science Honours degree and an MBA, who has been running an information technology business since November 1994. In 2004, David started researching wind turbines and in June last year he started physically implementing and testing battery based photovoltaic systems. He installed his first system in October and his second installation is almost complete. It includes a 1KW solar array and a 1KW wind turbine with a battery bank and inverter/charger for those cloudy and windless days. These two systems have been installed in his house where he can monitor and learn from them daily.

In September 2007, David and his wife, Mirjana, qualified as Reiki Masters. This opened up David's intuitive side and together with his logical mind, he had gone from strength to strength, both business wise and in the healing field.

David enjoys the Cape Town lifestyle including sailing, hiking, wine tasting and has an interest in philosophy and African Jemba Drumming.

David's goal is to create a Centre of Green Learning in Cape Town, South Africa and to live "off grids" by 2011.
