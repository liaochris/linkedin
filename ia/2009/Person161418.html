Michael Field’s Summary

Michael Field is an independent business advisor with two decades of experience in strategic marketing, needs based segmentation, e-commerce and innovation.

Michael's career began in 1989 as an advertising executive working across various publications including newspapers and business magazines. He progressed into online marketing in 1999 working on the launch of an ASX-listed Australian online business directory.

From 1999 – 2004, Michael worked in a number of senior marketing roles in listed technology companies. This experience included prospectus writing & capital raising, mergers & acquisitions and strategic alliances & partnerships with organisations such as AOL and Yahoo! Australia and New Zealand.

In 2004, Michael was engaged by a records management company to conduct a comprehensive market research and needs-based segmentation programme. The project was part of a broader objective to prepare the business for sale, which was successfully concluded in December 2005.

From 2006 – 2008, Michael was the General Manager of Marketing and Member Services for the Australian Institute of Company Directors and was responsible for implementing a comprehensive needs-based segmentation programme, managing sponsorship, publications, e-business and the ASX Top-200 programme.

Michael served as a non executive director of Save the Children Victoria, is currently on the Save the Children New South Wales council and is a former guest lecturer in marketing, small business promotional marketing and professional communication at ECU in Western Australia.

Michael is the founder and moderator of the LinkedIn group Next Director for aspiring and emerging company directors.
