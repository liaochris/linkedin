Rock Armstrong’s Summary

Proven, results-oriented marketing professional in corporate and agency marketing, advertising, public relations, and trade show planning. Has extensive experience in market research and strategic planning, and all components of marketing and communications including public and media relations, publicity, advertising creation, media buying, publications, collateral design and creation, special events and trade shows. Writes and produces television, radio and print advertising. Major strengths in Internet marketing, direct mail, long-term strategy and staff supervision. Additional skills in computer applications, budgeting, trade shows and special events planning, including branding and corporate image enhancement.
