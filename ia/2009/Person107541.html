Jean-Paul Haire’s Summary

I provide creative & user experience design direction, strategy, and leadership to business stakeholders and product development groups.

I bring 14 years of agency and corporate design team experience. I have solved complex problems and delivered beautiful results for ‘Fortune 500’ companies in the automotive, fashion, financial, music, tel-com, technology, and consumer-product industries. I have extensive experience leading teams to employ user-centered design methodologies to create, develop & deliver rich user interfaces for consumer and enterprise-level, transactional web-based applications and products.
