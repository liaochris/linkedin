Gareth Nicholas’s Summary

My name is Gareth, and I work as a Business Development and Key Account Manager for Mobiventures Ltd / Mobiventures Inc, a leading provider of digital marketing solutions. I work across four of our companies, all with a common theme - helping companies, organisations and clubs communicate with their tribes better.

Whether those tribes are clients, potential clients, fans, employees, we will be able to help.

The three companies are:
1.) Mail the Market Ltd offer full email marketing campaign management, including sourcing email data, design, broadcasting and reporting of email campaigns.
2.) Our Tribes Ltd design, build and market social networking sites
3.) BluePush Ltd offer proximity marketing campaign management using Bluetooth technology.
4.) Our Tribes Digital Ltd - a digital music label. This is a meld of the above services, and we are currently looking for band managers/artist managers (or artists themselves if self-managed) who have a relatively healthy fanbase. We have a great business proposition for them, which means that everyone is a winner!

Call me to discuss any ideas that you may have, and we can chat about how we may be able to help you. I would suggest that about 50% of my job is consultative, discussing ideas, exploring what's possible and what isnt!
