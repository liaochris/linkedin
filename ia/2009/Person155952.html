Matthew Loew’s Summary

I have experience in managing large-scale engineering projects, prospecting, technical marketing, sales, and business development activities. My engineering career has had me responsible for project planning and execution for engineering-based CAD models, advanced simulations, and strategic supplier relationships. I was previously employed in the automotive industry by a Tier 1 supplier as both an engineering analyst and a project engineer and by a small cutting-edge research and development company focused on advanced, light-weight structures and components as a project engineer. I'm now working with some of the biggest equipment on earth - surface mining equipment. Awesome!

I am a specialist in the development, deployment, and management of simulation-driven engineering tools and techniques. Managed large-scale engineering projects in automotive, defense, and heavy industries. Particular experience managing remote and distributed groups involving direct and contract employees, sub-contractors, technical partners, and consultants. I have routinely demonstrated a quick grasp of new technologies and specialties outside my original core expertise. I am a specialist in deploying solutions and solving problems. If you have a challenging product development environment, please contact me to see how I can help you.
