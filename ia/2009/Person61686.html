Dennis Hodgson, MBA’s Summary

Dennis Hodgson is the Senior Vice President and Chief Information Officer at A.C. Moore, an arts and crafts retailer. Mr. Hodgson has responsibility for hardware, software and telecom infrastructure. He also serves as the President of their e-commerce division. Prior to A.C. Moore, Mr. Hodgson served as Vice President of Information Systems at Foot Locker, Inc, a global footwear retailer with over 4,000 stores. Mr. Hodgson has over thirteen years of experience in selecting and integrating large-scale point of sale, merchandising, human resource, logistics, and financial systems. He has previously been the Chief Operating Officer of HealthQuest Inc. a division of Staff Builders Home Health Care.
