Antonio Buehler’s Summary

Former military officer with extensive leadership, turnaround and life experiences. I am the Founder and Managing Director of a private investment fund created to identify, acquire, and grow a small to mid-sized company ($2 million - $10+ million in EBITDA).

I am always available to meet like minded individuals, operators, and entrepreneurs looking for a liquidity/change of control event. Potential areas of interest include business services, education & training, franchisors, personal finance solutions, and franchise concepts.

* Stanford Graduate School of Business (MBA)
* U.S. Military Academy at West Point
* Former Army Officer; Engineer, Airborne/Ranger
* Private equity, investment banking/restructuring, investment management and IT consulting experience

======================================

It is my desire to make this world a better place for children; where they can hope, dream and grow to achieve their full potential. It is my belief that our best hope for a world with economic and social freedom comes through education and opportunity.

I have been involved in efforts to improve the lives of disadvantaged youth in the US, Germany, Bulgaria and Kosovo. For the past seven years, through Childreach/Plan USA, I have sponsored three children in Egypt, Malawi & Zimbabwe. I am currently on the board of directors for the New York based non-profit for child bereavement, A Caring Hand. I am interested in talking to people leading social or business ventures to improve the lives and create opportunity for children.
