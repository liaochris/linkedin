Chris Vertonghen’s Summary

Chris has been developing and managing web- and software-projects for over 20 years in a wide variety of industries including banking, insurance, copyrights, news media, internet, transport & shipping, metals & mining, networked printing, colour reprography, design, DTP, imaging and data warehousing.

As founder and/or manager of several ICT companies, he has been directly responsible for the development and implementation of large infrastructure- and software projects for internet, banking, finance, e-commerce, large-volume data-processing, reporting, records- and document management, library-, archiving and scanning, and learning- and productivity enhancement.

Chris has travelled and worked across Europe, Asia and The Americas. He lives in Belgium with his wife and two daughters.

Email: cv at dimolto dot com

Chris Vertonghen’s Specialties:
