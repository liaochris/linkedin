Daniel Beinart’s Summary

Registered patent agent with experience drafting provisional and utility applications, claim charts, Office Action responses, pre-appeal brief statements and appeal briefs. Skills include using various search tools (e.g, Delphion, Aureka) to search for prior art and for patents and applications relevant to clients’ product lines.

My intellectual property work draws on a broad base of engineering experience in the medical device and test and measurement industries. Experience working in small startup, mid-size, and large companies. Roles have included software development manager, project leader and individual contributor.
