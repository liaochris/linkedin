Adhi Goen’s Summary

Native Indonesian, Adhi is a long time Beijinger working his way up the ladder in the marketing field of hospitality Industry, starting from graphic design, to jump-starting public relations for a 500 units of luxurious villa and working for one the largest hotel groups in the world - IHG. While at InterContinental he worked closely with Aston Martin in their historical drive from Tokyo to London; as well as collaborated with sister properties Marketing on managing charity events with total donation of half a million CNY for educational support program in China.

Adhi’s latest project is pre-opening the first Canadian luxury hotel in Beijing, starting the work from ground up from media, to building foundation for sales and integrating corporate program into China. He is also currently heading up the hotel’s web social media marketing channel, where he successfully boosted the natural growth on one of the fastest viral growing web - Twitter (http://twitter.com/beijingfairmont) sharing green or eco talks, travel, Beijing local experiences, food, etc..

Aside from the traditional PR/Communications, he is also passionate about technology, Internet, web 2.0, web social media, PR 2.0, digital public relations, brand marketing and loves sharing a cup of Nespresso. Adhi believes in strong characterized leadership, leading by example and preparing his team members for strong future development.
