麦卡瑟 伊恩 Ian McArthur’s Summary

Ian McArthur coordinates COFA Online's Undergraduate online program and lectures in the School of Design studies at The College of Fine Art (UNSW). He is a member of the Omnium Research Group at the University of NSW in Sydney.

In 2003/2004 Ian initiated the COLLABOR8 Project with Annie Morrad to foster multidisciplinary projects online between design education programs in different institutions, and specifically across different cultures. In 2008 he presented at DesignEd Asia in Hong Kong about this research and will publish and present at the 3rd International Conference on Design Principles and Practices to be held in Berlin in February 2009. The COLLABOR8 Project 2009 will be held in Shanghai during September at Dong Hua University.

Having over 25 years experience as designer and design educationalist, Ian McArthur has also held a number of leadership roles in vocational and higher education in Australia and South East Asia. From 2001 - 2003 Ian was Program Director of Graphic Design, La Salle DHU International Design College (Dong-Hua University, Shanghai, China). He holds a Master of Design (Middlesex University, UK) and two education degrees (UTS, Sydney). Ian is currently a PhD Candidate in the School of Media Arts at COFA.

Ian is able to apply significant insight, knowledge, and skills developed in significant international experience across a range of art and design related domains including: cross cultural design management and design consultancy, branding and identity, design education and design related e-learning, web and multimedia development, fashion design, visual arts, and newspaper production.

He brings a strong emphasis on collaborative visioning, inspiring leadership, excellent interpersonal and communication skills, cross-cultural expertise, strategic planning, holistic people, financial and resource management and is able to apply these knowledge domains cogently to achieve both business and educational objectives.
