Adam Harris’s Summary

My professional experience as a webmaster and web developer includes my Bachelor's Degree in Multimedia from Bradley University. After school, I worked for a small local website company, SpyderHost.net, in Peoria, and I designed and built a dozen or so websites, most of which are still being used today. I created greatplainsortho.org, appleblossomfarm.com, and haddenbuilders.com. I also designed an e-commerce website, ultoffice.com. After about one year at SpyderHost.net, I worked for TEKsystems on site at ChoicePoint in Peoria. I successfully finished a 6 month contract developing CSS skins for ChoicePoint's DirectLink application. Just recently I started work at The Ghantous Companies as Director of Marketing, and I will be building and maintaining websites for the Ghantous Law Offices, Haymeadow Apartments, and GRE Properties. At the same time during the past 2 years, I have been developing my own website company, Adam Harris Design.
