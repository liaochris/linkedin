Judy Lea’s Summary

I am the senior graphic designer and founder of JL Digital Design. I hold a variety of certificates from Humber College’s Digital Imaging Training Centre.
With my experience in designing projects for both the web and for print, I can create the look you've been envisioning to take your business to the next level.
From logo design and corporate identity packages, to trade show posters, packaging and promotional items, I can manage your projects from start to finish so you can stay focused on what matters most...your business.

With the addition of my husband to the team at JL Digital Design, I have a renewed love for web design. I can now focus on the creation of elaborate, unique and creative designs and hand over the programming to him. His brain is wired to enjoy the coding and the entire process is not only enjoyable for him, it's what fuels him.

We are excited that our new site will be launching soon! We look forward to sharing our recent projects in our newly designed gallery as well as more wonderful testimonials from our clients.
