Anupam Agarwal’s Summary

Anupam Agarwal is an executive with 15 years of business experience including 10 years in management consulting with a particular focus in High-Tech Sales & Marketing, and strategy. During his career, Anupam has worked with several Fortune 500 companies on a variety of Sales & Marketing engagements including Go-To-Market model design, Sales Operations optimization, Sales Productivity improvement, Market Segment Strategies, Product Pricing and overall Growth Strategies. Besides High-Tech, he has worked across a broad range of industries including Telecom, Healthcare, Financial Services, and Utilities.
