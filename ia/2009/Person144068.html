Louis tutino’s Summary

I was born and raised in Pittsburgh, PA. I am a die hard Pittsburgh Sports fan. (yes, even the Pirates) After a shoulder injury in college put my baseball aspirations to rest, I went into the Transportation industry in 1986.

My father, of the same name, was the VP of Operations of Mark VII Transportation. I went to work there, starting on the ground floor auditing freight invoices and working on freight studies. Several years later, after working up the ladder, I was given the opportunity to go into sales. There I have stayed ever since.

Mark VII was sold to MSAS Global and then they merged with EXEL. I stayed on there as a 100 percent sales agent for about 7 years before leaving to join FedEx Services as an Account Executive in 2002.

FedEx promoted me and moved me to the Sarasota, FL area in 2005. I enjoyed success there being the third ranked Account Executive in the Southeast Region. I was also named the FedEx Sales MVP in FY 07.
Wanting to be promoted again, but things were not happening as quickly as promised and I was feeling the bug to go back to my 3PL roots, when an opportunity presented itself at FFE Logistics, which is a division of Frozen Food Express.

I took that position as a Station Manager and founded the third location that FFE Logistics opened. It started from nothing and has risen to be one of only 2 FFE Locations that are profitable.

While there, I was offered an opportunity to further my career and income by coming aboard Eleets Transportation.
I took the offer and that is where I am currently.

I am happily married to my wife, Patty. We have known each other since high school. We have 5 children, Sydnie (22) Living in Pittsburgh; Ericka (19) who is in the Navy as a SeaBee, Olivia (17) a junior in high school, Alex (12) he is in the 7th grade and Jack (7) who is enjoying the 2nd grade.
