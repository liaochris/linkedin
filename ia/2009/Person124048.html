Julien Ponge’s Summary

I am focusing my research activities on enterprise application integration, software distribution and deployment and next-generation version control systems. I hold a joint French/Australian PhD in computer science.

I have been an open source developer for many years. Especially, I am the creator of the software installer generation project called IzPack (see http://izpack.org/) which is part of Codehaus.

I give lectures in a French University, and I am available for consulting opportunities on deployment (not limited to IzPack) and Java EE related middleware.

Julien Ponge’s Specialties:

Emerging technologies, research and development, scientific and technical writing, giving talks, teaching, software development (Java, Ruby, Groovy, Python and C++), Java Enterprise Edition, service-oriented architectures and web services.
