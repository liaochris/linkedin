Jon Lindberg’s Summary

Currently working for Intellect, the leading trade association which serves to represent its members in the UK technology industry (represented by our 800 SME and multinational member companies), in their Transformational Government Team I have responsiblities for the Public Sector Council (includes the 20 biggest suppliers to government) and their workstreams in issues predominant in the IT industry–government relationship such as green ICT, professionalism, innovation, and procurement. I also look after Local Government and Identity and Information Management

I completed my MA in International Political Economy (with merit) at the prestigious University of Warwick in 2008 where I closely studied the interaction between the private and the public (mainly on the regional and international level). I have deep interest and comprehension of political economy, global finance, and international business. My research has, among other topics, dwelled upon the new, IT industry led capitalism of Sweden, the politics of global finance, the global and EU politics of Climate Change, and the business-government relationship in the USA.

My combined work and education experience have advanced my skills exponentially in the last six years. Receiving a diverse education in business/economics and political science/international studies have granted me a multifaceted view and knowledge of the world and its constituent parts. Equally important, however, is my work experience, which has both enabled and furthered my studies and life experience.

During these three years at CREES I attained significant improvements in my organizing, teamwork, communications, taking initiative, and leadership skills. By also gaining advanced research, writing, and analytical skills through my education I trust that I am well suited to take on any challenge thrown at me.
