Elijah May’s Summary

First and foremost, I am a relationship person.

My first order of business is to understand the people the around me and how we can work together to achieve success. From there, I focus on facilitating communication and cooperation to get things done.

My skills are best suited to project management. From commercial filming to special events, I thrive in a fast-paced, goal-oriented environment. After years of working with media, production, security, safety, facilities and transportation personnel, logistics management and inter-agency coordination have become second nature.

At the end of the day, I view success as being able to help others and make a difference. I find I am best able to achieve this by viewing other people's problems as if they were my own.
