Dick Huiras’s Summary

“Success” is a word not only attributed to Dick’s business endeavors, but is the driving force behind his transition coaching and training. Dick’s experience both in national and international business and owning seven successful businesses gives him a unique insight into guiding companies and individuals through transition. Dick’s coaching clients have experienced increases in business up to 250%, promotions from Area Manger to Corporate President, from the corporate world to starting and growing their own successful.

President of H & A International, a twenty-eight year old company assisting corporations in the United States, and Europe achieve success and growth.

Dick has been in the training for field for thirty-three years and the coaching field for over twenty years. During that time he has become a well-known and successful International speaker and coach.

Author of the internationally successful “ICS-CONNECT Program" and his latest digital book"How to Build a Bond of Mutual Trust".

While teaching at the University of Houston, Division of Continuing Education he received the Instructor of the Year award.

He has also coached many people going through transition, and families desiring to return harmony and excitement into the family unit. His “Family Unity Through Communications” has resulted in significant reductions of conflict and substance abuse, and increased school grades, positive attitudes, and understanding. Dick’s pre-marital coaching also results in less than a 10% divorce rate while the national rate is over 50%. .

Dick has a BA, Business and Psychology and a MA, Behavioral Psychology.

Six years flying F101 fighter aircraft for the United States Air Force.

A keynote speaker, and workshop presenter to the top 500 U.S. companies as well as international organizations in Ireland, Scotland, London, Cypress, Mexico, Germany and Spain.
