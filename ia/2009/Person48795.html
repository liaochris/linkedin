Connely Baldwin’s Summary

My academic research work at in hydrology, water resources and water management, including climate change and variability and seasonal water supply forecasting led me to seek practical application of these skills.

I am developing the practical application of the seasonal water supply forecasting through my consulting work at Vidya Inc.

I am performing a range of practical water management duties at PacifiCorp (Rocky Mountain Power). The work is varied; I can be managing irrigation and flood control releases one day and reviewing stream flow measurements the next.

Connely Baldwin’s Specialties:

Water management and modeling (RiverWare), hydrologic modeling, hydraulic modeling (HEC-RAS), stream flow measurement, stream gaging management, water supply forecasting, data collection system design, data logger programming, data analysis using MS Excel spreadsheets, MS Access databases and S+/R statistical programming languages, statistical analysis, computer programming using MS Visual Basic and Visual Basic for Applications for Excel.
