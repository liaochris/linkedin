Lauren White’s Summary

I graduated from UC, San Diego in December 2007 with a BA in Communication. After graduation I quickly moved up the ranks at UC San Diego from Marketing Coordinator to Program Coordinator to my current position as an Events Manager. As an Events Manager for the Office of Special Events and Protocol for UC San Diego, I am responsible for the planning and management of all events hosted by the Chancellor, as well as acting as a consultant for other events hosted by departments within External Relations.

My past experiences include working for the Graduate School of International Relations and Pacific Studies (IR/PS). My efforts include managing the Pacific Leadership Fellows Program, managing all administrative, travel and budget, and planning events. I also worked for the UCSD Alumni Association as the New Media Marketing Coordinator. My efforts include building and managing the UCSD Alumni Facebook Group, UCSD Alumni LinkedIn Group, UCSD Wikipedia page, updating the UCSD Alumni website, creating online ads for membership, and other alumni outreach via the web.

I interned for UCSD for two years and my experience from that internship includes:

Online marketing coodinator for UCSD Alumni Association; created UCSD Alumni Wikipedia page, UCSD Alumni Facebook group, UCSD LinkedIn group and researching new ways to reach out to UCSD Alumni. Planned many events including the annual Senior Saltue, Chancellor's Associates luncheons, and assisted in planning ths Hearts and Scholars and Faculty Excellence Awards for UCSD. Managed the Telephone Outreach Incentives Program and Triton Junkyard Derby Donation Drive. Written numerous "thank you" and "ask" letters to donors of UCSD. Manager Day in the Park events for BCI, a PR company in San Diego and created press kits for all BCI events.
