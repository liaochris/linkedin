Bob Birss’s Summary

Software test engineering manager with 20 years of experience, including 12 years managing groups of 6-45; experience in startups and small, medium, and large companies, with shrink-wrap, enterprise, and custom software.

• Skilled at establishing strong working relationships across organizations as well as with partners and customers
• Extensive experience in evaluation, development, and use of test automation tools, creation and management of policies and procedures, and management of software and processes to conform to international standards
• Successfully shipped consumer, enterprise, and custom software products
• Built QA groups from scratch as well as revitalized existing QA groups
• ASQ Certified Software Quality Engineer (CSQE) and Certified Quality Auditor (CQA); ASQ Senior Member

Bob Birss’s Specialties:

Test architecture, automated test technology
