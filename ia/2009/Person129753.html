Ken Colby’s Summary

An energetic, internationally-experienced executive. Highly experienced in venture-backed, early-stage companies. Over twenty four years leading and improving operations in every major region around the world. Accomplishes this through:

• A passion for delivering excellence to customers
• Setting high standards and building organizations that can exceed them
• Partnering with Sales channels to achieve aggressive revenue goals
• Delivering profit by improving processes and tough cost controls

Has built groups from the ground up; has also led organizations of more than 500 employees. Knows how to improve the bottom line and has delivered profitable revenue in excess of $100 million. Builds excellent leadership teams; can also provide hands-on leadership when required. A team player who thrives in multi-cultural organizations.
