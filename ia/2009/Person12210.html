Amy McKenzie’s Summary

An established training and instructional design professional with experience in classroom and online facilitation, reference tool development (paper and online), instructional design processes (in-class, on-line, or self-study) and training support materials. A proven project manager capable of working with business partners on all management levels and keeping target goals on task. A strong design team member that focuses on using creativity, adult learning theory, ADDIE and Kirkpatrick training and development models for proven success.

Amy McKenzie’s Specialties:

Creativity, project management, design layout, performance improvement, coaching, facilitation, support documentation, mergers and acquisition training, financial retirement training
