Dan M’s Summary

At Charisma Coaching our mission is to inspire your success in communication by learning to reveal yourself in a unique and confident way. Charisma is a skill that can be effectively developed by anyone. Human connection is at the heart of charisma and it is what we feel towards another person when we can see a bit of ourselves in them. It is what allows us to inspire and be inspired by others. We teach practical skills and provide a forum and support to manifest them in your life. Our success happens as you embody your highest potential.

Dan M’s Specialties:

Consulting in communication, conflict management, leadership, morale building, and networking
