Leigh Rowan’s Summary

For years, I've aimed to grow partner's business through intelligent and effective online marketing solutions. I've had excellent success with launching e-commerce companies, optimizing websites for Google and Yahoo, and managing brand presences online and offline.
In my current role at Moderne Communications, Inc, I am able to offer a full suite of online, offline and virtual world marketing programs to ensure that all brand marketing goals are met. From street teams and multicultural marketing initiatives to virtual world marketing to viral microsites and optimized online press releases, Moderne has the tools, personnel and capabilities to grow business across all platforms.
