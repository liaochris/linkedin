Snowden McFall’s Summary

Snowden McFall, professional speaker, trainer, and author, is the owner of 25 year old Brightwork Advertising & Training and 12 year old Fired Up. A business expert who speaks on motivation, marketing, stress management, teambuilding and communication, Snowden has authored and co-authored four books, including Fired Up!, which has now sold 60,000 + copies.

Snowden graduated in 3 years from Vassar College with honors and received her MAT from Brown University.

Snowden began speaking professionally 18 years ago and has since addressed several thousand people. Snowden is a member of both National Speakers Association and MPI.


Snowden's volunteer work has garnered her national media attention. For co-founding and running a non-profit group dedicated to helping women entrepreneurs, the Small Business Administration named her National Women in Business Advocate of the Year which led to a White House ceremony & Congressional luncheon in her honor.

Inc. Magazine later chose Snowden as finalist for “New England Entrepreneur of the Year.” Snowden has appeared on 300 radio shows, CNN Financial News, Bloomberg Television. She was recently featured in Investors Business Daily and Foxnews.com

An active community volunteer, Snowden serves on the Jacksonville chamber of Commerce as a Trustee, on the Board of Ambassadors, as Vice-Chair of Uncampaign membership Drive, as an Athena Powerlink Mentor, as a Marketing Matters Mentor and member of the Procurement Conference Committee. She is also on the Board of Directors of Women Business Owners, and has been named 2009 Small Business Leader of the Year by the Professional
Women's Council. She is honored to be part of Leadership Jacksonville, Class of2010.


Goals are to make a difference in the community, to provide top quality work for clients with integrity, to build long-term professional win-win relationships. To do more speaking internationally to empower women, especially in Africa.
