Anthony J. Darin (JDUSA)’s Summary

My goal is to be the best HR professional, coach and mentor for team members that I can be for any organization that can appreciate what I have to offer.

I am a passionate Human Resources professional with global HR experience with an extensive background as a senior human resources manager. I enjoyed over 20 years of human resource experience with the Ford Motor Company.

I have lived and worked in the U.S. A., Canada and Poland and have worked in Mexico and China. Through my work, I was able to travel to many places and be exposed to many different cultures. These travels have given me a broad world perspective and enriched and enhanced my performance as an HR professional.

I am Human Resource professional with extensive experience in customer-focused and service-oriented HR management positions at the Plant, Division and Corporate Staff levels that honed my employee relations, employee engagement, organizational management and policy development skills. I possess superior strategic aptitude and vision with a clear sense of purpose and urgency. I have a talent for making rapid assessments of diverse situational challenges and then developing and leading the resulting action plans. I also have business expansion and start up experience.

After leaving Ford Motor Company, I formed Personnel Relations Solutions, LLC to provide consulting services to assist companies in the development of solutions for complex employee relations issues by providing business coaching support and advice for individuals or groups in finding ways to improve the effectiveness of their business, to improve leadership, performance management, employee engagement, employee accountability, teamwork, goal setting and conflict management.
