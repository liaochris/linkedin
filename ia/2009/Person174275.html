Nick (Naeem) Noorani’s Summary

My background has been in advertising, working with large corporationa in India and the Middle East. My first job in Canada was with a small publishing group where I started as Account Manager and rose to VP Advertising & Marketing. I am the founder/Publisher of the Canadian Immigrant a national magazine for immigrants, bestselling author of Arrival Survival Canada and Radio host for the Link on Radio Canada International.

Nick (Naeem) Noorani’s Specialties:

I have been described as a social entrepreneur where I use entrepreneur skills to address a social cause. I focus on the subject of immigration and immigrants to Canada. I am an apolitical advocate on this subject and hold all levels of Government accountable. I also speak to corporations on the business case for diversity and bringing down barriers for immigrants. Lastly, I speak to hundreds of immigrants about succeeding and motivating them not to give up!

Nick (Naeem) Noorani’s Experience

    (Publishing industry)

    August 2003 — Present (6 years 5 months)

    In July 2003, five years after my wife and I with our two kids immigrated to Canada and a month after we bought our house, we were both laid off. The Canadian Immigrant Magazine had been a dream of mine and I started this in the basement of my home. In December 2006, we were acquired by the Toronto Star and launched a Toronto edition.

    I also am best selling author of a book for immigrants titled Arrival Survival Canada www.arrivalsurvival.com, a radio show on CBC International www.rcinet.ca and motivational speaker
