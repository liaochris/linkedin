Chris Mohritz’s Summary

Chris is a seasoned technologist with a few successful startups under his belt, he also has more than 15 years of experience designing, managing, and securing information systems. Just as his interests tend to be quite varied, Chris’ professional experience also encompass a broad range:

• Nuclear reactor operator, aboard the USS Abraham Lincoln
• Decorated instructor for the US Navy
• Patent holder / inventor
• Licensed Real Estate Broker
• Historic home renovator
• "Green" building technologist
• “Smart Home” designer

While Chris maintains a detailed knowledge base of current technologies, and is skilled in the seamless deployment of IT solutions into high-demand business environments, one can not categorize him with other Technologists. Chris is a unique blend of technology know-how coupled with creative thinking and business acumen.

Chris is also an active volunteer within a local non-profit organization that teaches kids from low-income backgrounds how to use computers. He is also an active volunteer and contributor to the Make-a-Wish Foundation. Chris lives in Chicago, where he indulges his fascination with the city’s historic architecture by periodically restoring historic homes…with a hint of high-tech. He also enjoys traveling and putting his mobility strategies to the test.
