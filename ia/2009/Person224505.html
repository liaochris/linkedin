Steven Lundin’s Summary

Since 1967, Augmentation, Inc. has been a leading provider of staffing and personnel services to Fortune 500 and Federal contractors. This experience has given us the insight into the staffing challenges facing our clients and the ability to provide solutions tailored to their individual requirements. Our network of recruiters has an extensive pool of highly qualified candidates, pre-screened and available for immediate assignment to your facilities. Our employment solutions are flexible and cost effective...from short-term contract to permanent placement, Augmentation, Inc. can assist you with all your staffing needs.

In the years since its inception, Augmentation, Inc. has come to maintain three separate and distinct divisions, each specializing in a field of expertise synonymous with the site of operation. In addition to the original Technical Services Division/Corporate Headquarters founded in 1967 (Frederick, MD), we have established the Health Care Services Division (Birmingham, AL), and the Industrial Services Division (offices in Winchester, VA and Martinsburg, WV). Each office operates with the same objective for which Augmentation, Inc. has become known over the years: quality service with a personal touch.
