OneKeyAway sets up interactive mixers.

OneKeyAway Mixers set up a cool and hip atmosphere for professionals who want to meet other professional or different people with complimentary personalities to their own in an interactive, unpressured, and face-to-face environment.

Our singles mixers utilizes a small computer gadget the size of a key chain car alarm remote. Before each mixer, our guests goes online to complete a personality questionnaire.
