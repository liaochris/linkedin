Larry Webster’s Summary

President and CEO of Media Magik Group, with over 12 years experience in overseeing the day to day operations of a multimedia entertainment firm. Management of all staff, including the film, music and new media divisions. Oversee all location operations on film shoots, post workflow and all studio operations of the music divisions.
