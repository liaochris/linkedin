Angela DeYoung’s Summary

*Specializing in residential and commercial interiors while creating a way of life and enhancing a brand. The new development of high rise living in Nashville,TN has commercial and residential international building guidelines applied to the details of the final project. The United Artist Building once a office building repurposed for corporate condos. I created the floor plan,electrical, kitchen & bath design to the fininshes for the project.

*Circa 1886 Werthan Cotton Mill Loft space went from rags to riches with the result of true industrial loft. Space planning, lighting and electrical were all challenges in this space that flowed between two stories of living. Great results capturing a welcoming and entertaining atmosphere. Construction & furnishings were a treat to place in this overall design.

*Designs by DeYoung is an instrumental value to DeYoung Investments. The knowledge of remodeling costs along with timelines to completion has been an asset to this investment firm. Interior selections elevated the values on every property that work was completed on.The average ROI was 15-20 percent.

*Completed Interior Design consulting,furniture replacement and design for the Burton Residence in Franklin,TN owners of Rivals.com

*Completing a project with Debroah and Ken Dale @ Werthan Lofts for interior design staging for resale. They have scene first hand the financial impact my designs have had and they are wanting the same return for themselves.

My goals are to balance a life of an interior design career as a soloprenuear and be the best wife and mom that my family would want to have around.

I also want to have the opportunity to have the luck of being a designer for a celebrity client in Nashville,TN or an international client in another country. Just the power of the internet can make all of these things happen. I have been fortunate enough to have friends who have tremendous wealth and warm hearts and I believe anything is possible.
