Jeffrey A. Bloom’s Summary

I have helped over 100 clients in a dozen industries exceed expectations! Since 1998 I have been focusing my management consulting activities on "Project Portfolio Management (PPM)", "New Product Development (NPD) Management" & "Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)”.

In addition to my Graduate Studies in Program/Project Management, I completed formal Program Management Office (PMO) and Project Portfolio Management (PPM) classes while a Manager in the Risk Management Practice at KPMG/BearingPoint. I have PMO Management experience at major international commercial clients including: ING Bank Amsterdam, Fortis Bank Brussels, Topdanmark Insurance Copenhagen, TIAA-CREF Retirement Svcs New York, PriceWaterhouseCoopers Toronto, DirecTV LA & Xerox Global Svcs.

I specialize in architecting & implementing PPM Solutions to increase Return On Investment (ROI), minimize risk & enhance governance effectiveness. Since 2002 I have completed 17 PPM/PMO consulting engagements in support of major clients using the "CA Clarity PPM" product family – 8 as a subcontractor to CA Services, CA's professional services organization that assesses, motivates, architects, implements and deploys CA solutions. I am fully experienced with all technical, functional, enterprise integration and solution architectural aspects of CA Clarity PPM. I am Board Secretary of the "CA Clarity Global Users Community" and Vice President of the Florida CA Clarity Users Group.

Prior to my focus in the PPM, GRC & NPD marketplaces, I provided management consulting in Information Systems Architecture, Enterprise Application Integration, IT Infrastructure Design & Analysis, Advanced Network Design & Analysis, New Product Development and Business Development. I have held positions as Chief Technology Officer in the Automotive industry, Chief Operating Officer in the Healthcare Claims Processing industry, Chief Information Officer in the Software industry & CEO in the Management Consulting and Software industries.

Jeffrey A. Bloom’s Specialties:

Corporate Governance, Risk & Compliance; Program Management, Project Management, Project Portfolio Management; Information Technology Management, New Product Development; Business Development; Marketing; Procurement; Information Technology Forensics, Expert Witness, Professional Services Management
