LTC (Ret) Frank Zipperer’s Summary

Internationally known freelance photographer with over 50 years experience in both film and digital photography. Produces fine art photographic Limited Edition prints using museum quality archival materials.

Designs attractive but simple and easy to use websites for individuals and families, service organizations, groups and clubs and small businesses.

Professional goal is to increase the exposure of my work to the public through networking and gallery exhibits. I also seek networking with other photographers to share both technical and marketing skills.

LTC (Ret) Frank Zipperer’s Specialties:

Over 50 years experience in film and digital photography and equipment, Over 10 years experience in web design, working knowledge and use of Photoshop for image files post processing and graphics.

  • Owner

    Frank Zipperer Photography & Web Designs (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Photography industry)

    February 1994 — Present (15 years)

    After many years of using film cameras, I switched to digital cameras. The Nikon digital D70 and Nikon D2x are my working cameras now. I use the Hasselblad 500 but recently upgraded to a digital back.

    I personally print each image on an Epson photo printer using fine art paper with an acid free base and bright white surface. I have the capability to print up to 13x19 prints using 2880x1440 dpi high resolution images.

    Objectives:

    (1) Make photographic images for use in my own fine art photo business.
    (2) Maintain my online gallery website posting new works when finished.
    (3) Provide photography shoots to other artists for use in their ad campaigns and
    gallery show entry requirements and other marketing tools including website design and maintenance.
    (4) Assist in supporting the growth of creative artists through exhibitions, educational programs and by educating the public to the collectible value of photography.
