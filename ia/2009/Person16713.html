Anna Gonzalez’s Summary

Three months after graduating from SMU, I was hired into a professional training program at CBS 11 News in Fort Worth, Texas. Four months into the program, I moved into the position of Assignment Editor, learning more about the inner workings of local television news. A year later, I achieved my goal of becoming a web producer.

My position allows me to express my creativity through problem solving, writing and sometimes video editing. Creating art with new technologies excites me, and I hope it will allow me to deliver messages online in a new way.
