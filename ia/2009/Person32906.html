Brett Marlow (brett.marlow@jobsincredit.com)’s Summary

www.jobsincredit.com & www.jobsinrisk.com are truly unique recruitment portals that offer a cost effective option for recruiting credit, risk or compliance staff. If your looking for a new job the sites are extremely easy to navigate and registration is free.

The sites have over 40,000 registered credit, risk and compliance candidates and receive in excess of 30,000 unique visits per month.
