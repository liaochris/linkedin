Valerie A. Cooper’s Summary

Valerie Cooper is the President of Picture That, LLC, a fine art consulting firm. Formed in 2002, Picture That supplies fine art, curates exhibitions, develops multicultural marketing materials and provides art advisory services to organizations worldwide. Her firm’s clients have included CARTUS, Credit Suisse First Boston, DIAGEO, Day Pitney LLP, Deloitte, Bank of America, GE, GENWORTH, The Goldman Sachs Group, IBM, Northeast Utilities, Pitney Bowes, The Stamford Center for the Arts, Thompson Hospitality, UBS and numerous private individuals.

Picture That, LLC is certified as a Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) by the Connecticut Minority Supplier Development Council; and as a Woman Business Enterprise (WBE) by the State of Connecticut.

Picture That, LLC is the proud recipient of the 2008 NMSDC National Supplier of the Year Award - Northern Region, a designation given to companies that exceed expectations in service, delivery, product quality and overall value. In 2006, Picture That received the CMSDC "Connecticut Supplier of the Year" Award.
