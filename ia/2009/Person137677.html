Laura McHarrie’s Summary

My career history supports my level of professional experience in the retail industry, other than a brief period which isn't listed here, where I opened a retail outlet of my own with my now ex husband. (Tells a tale in itself)!

Welcome Home made a trading profit in its first year, based on a thoroughly researched business plan and is still trading some 13 years on. I am quite proud of that. The closure of Excel Cothing in 2000 gave me a huge insight into managing trading difficulties which until then, I had no real experience of. My subsequent foray into teaching, training and freelancing has given me another level of experience. The sum of which I draw upon to give impartial advice to companies as part of my Business Link advisory role.

I am organised, have a strong tendency towards detail, consistancy and accuracy. I hate to miss a dead line. These are tempered by naturally strong interpersonal skills which seem to engage and enthuse others. All skills that are linked strongly in the retail trade.

In terms of goals! At a bit of a cross roads at the moment so will hold back on sharing what direction I am heading in until I have cleared the fog a bit.
