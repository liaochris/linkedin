Shridhar Kumar’s Summary

Handled transition of payroll operations for one of the worlds largest retailer – Tesco.& Credit reporting venture (D&B) for IBM, scaling 6 different functions, across 4 client sites in an operational role.
Improved on the Payroll Model which was traditionally employed in UK to a more efficient solution by combining Processing & Voice activities at one desk (Tesco). Worked with the Operations Enhancement teams (OE) and Transformation teams during the transition phase for D&B, in order to identify quick hits to improve productivity & efficiency and also new opportunities of functions that can be moved to IBM
Led the payroll activities for one of the world’s largest retailer for a total population of 3, 50,000 employees in a complex environment involving severe compliance standards
Created & Implemented MARS (Medco Agent Ranking System) in IBM, to provision a performance management system, which helped in improving overall delivery
Handled client communication on behalf of IBM, with the leading Credit reporting organization in the US (D&B), pre & post contract finalization. Also, was responsible for all client communication during the transition & implementation phase. These discussions involved SLA’s, Work force Management, OJT & Transition Progress check points, BCP, Billing, Infrastructure & Technology.
