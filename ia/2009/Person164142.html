Mike DeLaney’s Summary

Founder - President - Technical/Executive Recruiter
Global Network Recruiting (GNR), a hi-tech recruitment firm that specializes in contingency, engaged and retained searches for professionals and executives across North America. Since 2000, GNR has worked with organizations ranging from start-ups to Fortune 500 companies, helping them access the best and the brightest talent.

GNR can successfully identify, recruit and deliver qualified applicants in a variety of areas of engineering, operations, marketing, finance and product development.
