Karen Stewart’s Summary

Karen Stewart is an attorney who educates clients on how to avoid probate court or probate an existing will and assists them in creating a will, setting up a trust and choosing an executor and other agents.

Because Karen Stewart is a CPA, she can also advise clients as a tax attorney on trust accountings, estate tax returns and minimizing or eliminating estate taxes.

Karen is a service oriented, down to earth attorney, easy to work with and committed to helping every day people plan how they want their estates to be preserved and allocated. Karen is a friendly, caring, and trustworthy attorney for the middle class.

Karen Stewart’s Specialties:

Estate planning, trust and estate administration, probate matters, decedent′s estates, guardianship, conservatorship, trusts, wills, probate, power of attorney, patient advocate. Serving Michigan, Novi, Livonia, Farmington, Farmington Hills, Northville, Plymouth, Canton, Milford, South Lyon, Wixom, Commerce Township
