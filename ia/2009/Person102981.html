James Sandoval’s Summary

I am a buy-side digital marketing professional with 10 years of US & UK leadership experience servicing Fortune 1,000 and FTSE 100 advertisers.

Today, I am the Founder and Managing Director of Invizua, a digital marketing accountability company.

Invizua is 100% focused on supplying unparalleled technology products + consulting and data services to smart advertisers, their agencies and related organisations.

Invizua's current products and services are:

- TruEffect DirectServe™ (www.trueffect.com/products/DirectServe.aspx)

DirectServe is a game-changing, multi-channel digital ad management [aka ad serving] platform for advertisers and their agencies.

DirectServe's vast value starts with enabling advertisers to own and control their digital marketing data - as it's collected & managed via their own domains (e.g. brandx.com) vs. via a 3rd party (e.g. doubleclick.net/com).

DirectServe also enables digital marketers, for the first time, to use one of their most important business/data assets - on and offline customer intelligence - for powerful digital ad pre-targeting, re-targeting, phenomenal campaign analytics and consumer insight generation.

Expect campaign performance lifts between 20% and 200% through TruEffect's DirectServe vs. via 3rd party platforms.

Invizua very excitedly & exclusively represents TruEffect's products Europe-wide.

- Visokio's Omniscope (www.visokio.com)

Omniscope is an enormously powerful desktop application for data visualisation, distribution and collaboration.

Download and trial Omniscope 2.5 today: http://tinyurl.com/cs87ao

Omniscope is an enterprise-wide solution, not just for use within functional BI teams.

Invizua is a proud UK Value Added Reseller partner of Visokio's Omniscope.

- ICDS (Invizua Consulting & Data Services)

Supplying revenue-generating consulting and bespoke data aggregation and reporting services to digital media and marketing [services] organisations.
