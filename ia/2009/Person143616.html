Lori Gallardo’s Summary

LORIMARK CONSULTING is a certified Affiliate of "EWS" Environmental Waste Solutions.

We specialize in substantially reducing waste disposal and recycling costs for our Clients on a risk- free, results-oriented basis.

We are not a waste hauling company, but rather a waste and recycling cost-reduction company who focuses on Clients who spend $20,000 or more monthly on solid waste.

Our Clients achieve SIGNIFICANT COST SAVINGS because we:
Pinpoint where they are currently losing money.
Access technology that vendors have not made available.
Evaluate costs versus benefits for technology upgrades.
Identify cost recovery and recycling alternatives.
Forecast waste disposal costs - months, years in advance.

Our Clients pay nothing for EWS' analysis and recommendations. EWS gets paid only when we produce a direct refund, credit or savings in your waste and recycling services.

Contact us for a COST-REDUCTION waste management system that will work- GUARANTEED!
