Casey Watson’s Summary

For the last few years, I have been working in multiple software development roles, primarily in a consulting capacity, in the North Dallas area. My core focus is the .NET platform and supporting technologies. I earned my Microsoft Certified Solution Developer certification in 2006 and my Certified ScrumMaster certification in 2008. My key professional goal is to never stop learning.

I am currently with Improving Enterprises, a Microsoft Gold Partner located in the Dallas area with a strong focus on Agile practices and methodologies.

I've presented at the Dallas C# SIG, AgileDotNet 2008 (Making a Mockery of Your Unit Tests) and Dallas TechFest 2009 (C# Jeopardy).

Casey Watson’s Specialties:

CSM, MCAD, MCSD, C# 1.1/2/3.5/4, Windows Forms Development, ASP.NET, Multi-Threaded Applications, SOA, Agile Development and TDD, Financial Systems, Compact Framework Development, Language Integrated Query (LINQ), Windows Communication Foundation (WCF), Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF), Dependency Injection and Inversion of Control (Castle and Ninject), Entity Framework
