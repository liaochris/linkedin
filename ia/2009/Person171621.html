Natalie Whistler’s Summary

It is my goal to help everyone I deal with in Real Estate to learn something new out of each transaction, hopefully something helpful and good.
I started as a buyer's agent for the first three years of my career and have been a top listing and selling agent at Prudential and for a few years at Coldwell Banker. Having been in this business for over 13.5 years and selling in excess of $80 million worth of real estate, you can say I have experience. Most of my business nearly 98% of it is from referrals, past clients, friends & neighbors.
