Jon Martindale’s Summary

Jon's passions are 7 UPs, Toastmasters, Prison Ministry, Health and a Green Environment.

Telechem is a main sponsor of 7UPs. Telechem manufactures safe environmental green products for convenience store owners and operators and the quick lube industry. Jon is the Convenience Store Sales Co-ordinator with Telechem.

As a Toastmaster, Jon has provided several training workshops on Goal Setting and Time Management by Principles and Values (life is more than a checked off to-do list).

Principles and Values are part of the program Jon provides in his Prison Ministry. People need balance in 8 sections of their life. Spiritual/Physical; Metal/Emotional; Family/Society; and Financial/Career. Using Force Field Analysis as a tool and then setting goals and objectives based on principles and values allows one to be free to let his light shine.

7 UPs is a Faith and Fellowship organization that has weekly meetings to discuss the current liturgical lesson and share thoughts. Jon provides the weekly email update. This can be seen at http://7ups.org by clicking on the Penny.

Back in February 2008 Jon was hospitalized with a stroke and diagnosed with diabetes. Toastmasters has help greatly with speech recovery. And recent involvement with his HWHBiz - Nutrition program with Isagenix he has shown significant progress and now is an advocate for obesity prevention. Jon's A1c dropped from 7.9 to 6.8 after less then 45 days on the program and his weight dropped from 242 pounds down to 198 in approximately 75 days.

Jon's former Red Cross experience (30 + years) at chapter, area, division, and national headquarters as an instructor trainer and disaster director has provided him an opportunity to help others learn how to learn and to make better presentations.

Health education, nutrition education, diet vs cleansing, travel, discount membership trips, cruizes, vacations, honeymoons, ASTA member, network marketing, and more
