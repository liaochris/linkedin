Luke Ribich’s Summary

Luke Ribich is Managing Partner of the Avionics & Systems Integration Group, LLC (ASIG), where he is responsible for day-to-day operating activities, sales and marketing, program management, production, customer support and exacts a special expertise in avionics engineering, installation and certification management. Mr. Ribich has 15 years avionics and airframe related experience. Prior, Mr. Ribich managed Aircraft & Systems Modifications for Air Transport International and has held positions of increasing responsibility including avionics mechanic, avionics installer, inspector, line mechanic, lead mechanic, shift supervisor, program coordinator, and avionics manager for airline and repair station employers. Mr. Ribich has completed study requirements with Embry Riddle Aeronautical University in the university’s B.S. Management of Technical Operations program.

Luke Ribich’s Specialties:

FAA STC & PMA Certification Program Management, Government Affairs Liasion, Fabrication Inspection Systems Development, Electrical System/Component Integration and Design, Airframe & Powerplant Licensed, FCC Licensed, Management of Technical Operations, New Product Implementation and Budgeting

    (Non-Profit; 51-200 employees; Education Management industry)

    APCS is an open-enrollment charter school district located in Maumelle, Arkansas. APCS was founded in 2000 by local leaders and the City of Maumelle, who through a needs study found that it would benefit from 6-12 educational facilities. APCS now has grades K-3 and is expanding annually until K-12 grades are added. In partnership with the city, UCA and the Maumelle Public Facilities Board, APCS is pursuing facilities and curriculum development projects in order to yield a college preparatory educational alternative to public schools.

Trustees are elected by a majority or appointed at the leisure of the APCS board by the Mayor of the City of Maumelle. Mr. Ribich was elected in Sept. 2007 by a majority vote of the members and serves as the Board Treasurer and Chairperson of the Operations & Communication Subcommittee. Mr. Ribich's Term expires in Sept. 2010.
