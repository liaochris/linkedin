Christine Grogan Taylor’s Summary

A newly returned resident to Nashville, TN. She is the V/P of Social Media/Marketing for JTMarCom a full service marketing communications company. Clients included Bridgestone/Firestone USA and Canada, MCA Records and Hospital Corporation of America.

Christine brings a proven track record in sales, marketing and entrepreneur ship. She was one of Nashville's top performing promotional marketing salespersons for several years before starting her own promotional marketing firm. She counted several multi-national corporations as clients before selling her company and moving to Greensboro.

One of the keys to Christine's success in promotional marketing was her creativity, and once in Greensboro, her creative side really took over. With a father who was a commercial artist in New York City, Christine always knew that she had artistic genes. So, she decided to leave the corporate world for awhile and let her creative juices flow.

Christine spent months honing her skills in oil painting and then launched a successful career as an artist. She still shows her works at leading Greensboro galleries, hosts events at her home studio and showcases her work on her Web site. Christine is committed to always devoting a portion of her life to one of her great passions.

This coming together of marketing, sales and art experience creates a winning combination for Christine's clients.

A native of Scotland, Christine is married to a John Taylor, President of JTMarCom. They have two daughters - Alexandra ( a screen writer in L.A.) and Tori (a Sophomore at UNC-Chapel Hill).
