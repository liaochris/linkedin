Patricia Witherite’s Summary

I was hired in 1997 as a Retail Sales Associate at Rejuvenation's flagship store in Portland, Oregon.

In the past 12 years I have worked in both Customer Service and in Merchandising as an Assistant & Buyer.

As Retail Sales Associate I was required to have product knowledge of any item that was sold in the store, furniture, lighting, hardware, plumbing and salvaged house parts, and also expected to answer questions related to old house ownership.

As a Lead Sales Associate I worked with purchasing manager to create an electronic Vendor Library to replace print materials for the Retail Sales Staff. I trained new staff, was responsible for opening and closing duties including all end of the day responsibilities. I was a member of the team that provided training, support and helped open the second Rejuvenation Retail Store in Seattle, Washington.

As a Furniture Sales Specialist I was responsible for upkeep and maintenance of the sales materials, including a large collection of vendor fabrics and samples for over 8 major furniture vendors. I began a customer mailing list and sent personal notes of upcoming events in the store to more than 60 of my customers. I had the highest gross sales for several years and also executed all Customer Special Orders for over 8 major furniture vendors. I resolved customer complaints and arranged warranty repair or replacement if needed.

I have had hands on experience with at least 6 truckload sales events for a major fine furniture/rug vendor, in both Customer Service and Merchandising Assistant. I was Lead Sales Associate for several events, and as Merchandising Assistant I was responsible for all purchasing related tasks from item set up, receiving support, communicating discrepancies, and organizing a return to vendor of unsold items.

My experience in Customer Service and Merchandising for 12 years has given me the unique understanding of what is needed to deliver an exceptional customer shopping experience.
