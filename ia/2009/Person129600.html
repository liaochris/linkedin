Kelly Yocum’s Summary

Get Involved!

The GTRA Symposium is designed to provide government CXOs an executive-level forum to learn, collaborate and share best practices across the four areas paramount to executing successful IT strategies: Enterprise Architecture, Security, Green IT, and Leadership. Through forward looking presentations, break-out sessions, and cutting-edge technology demonstrations, executives will see how each of these areas are inter-related and walk away armed with the resources and contacts necessary to achieve success. The GTRA Symposium converges the four GTRA Council Meetings taking place at the same time and location for true government-wide collaboration. Each of these four individual councils will have a full program while allowing participants to network and share information across technology areas.

    Government Technology Research Alliance (GTRA)

    (Government Relations industry)

    January 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    The Government Technology Research Alliance (GTRA) brings together executive-level government technology leaders to collaborate, strategize and create innovative solutions for common challenges. By sharing best practices and lessons-learned, knowledge at the executive-level is exchanged among peers, resulting in actionable government-wide strategic plans.

    Within GTRA there are several Councils for Technology. The Councils are comprised of government executives in the fields of Technology, Security, Mobile/Wireless, Enterprise Architecture, HR, Learning, Health IT, Training, Legal and Leadership. Through Annual Symposiums, Meetings, Networking and Conferences, the Councils provide a forum for government-wide dialogue while advancing strategic technological initiatives and common goals.
