Janie Curtis’s Summary

I am currently the Chief Strategic Officer at Wildfire and enjoy being part of an established agency while at the same time being extremely entrepreneurial (which I love). In addition to heading up all the strategic work in the agency, I am also responsible for new business for both the parent agency (Wildfire) as well as our marketing to women group called Factor 9. I love new business because it stretches one to be extremely innovative in the way one reaches out to possible contacts and clients.
Prior to joining the team at Wildfire, I was the managing director at Frank About Women, where I was able to utilize my varied skills including female centric consumer insight development, the development of unique and proprietary research and branding tools and techniques and of course new business development.
I have also headed up strategic planning for major international advertising agencies including DMB&B and Euro RSCG and run my own branding consultancy in Chicago called Idea Box.
Having started life and my career in the UK, I have been fortunate enough to gain experience in different international markets including Italy, Canada and most recently the US.
My goals are to continue to be challenged every day - if I am no longer being challenged and challenging myself then I know that something is wrong. Being in an innovative and entrepreneurial environment is really important to me, as well as one where it is recognized that growth often requires challenging the status quo and looking at things from a new perspective.
