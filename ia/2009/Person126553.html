About me:

In a world of change, where I have worked intensively with people from all walks of life - from children to executives - I have come to understand how important it is to focus on and invest in the human potential, guiding individuals as well as companies onto a meaningful (!) and successful path.

Our thinking, our focus and our actions need to change in order to develop attitudes of heartfelt commitment, trust, loyalty, devotion, motivation, enjoyment, passion, inspiration and enthusiasm!

SmilingOne I founded in 2005. My commitment to make a great difference. Touching peoples lives!

My Journey has been a challenging and very rewarding one. I have gathered experiences from adventures in various countries, learned from different cultures and I have met great mentors on my path allowing me to grow and gain through their presence and sharing of knowledge.

Yes, I have both "failed" and "conquered" and accepted both equally as important learning processes! I have trusted deeply in my path and I feel fortunate. Not only has it given me great insight - it has also taken me to the home of my heart: South Africa.

The experience and knowledge I come with combined with a very special beginning in this beautiful country of different and very unique cultures have brought me to understand - with all of my being (in my heart, my mind & my soul) - the true essence of being part of something bigger, standing united and strong as a team. The more you share and give of your energy into a process the more everyone can become and the greater a difference can be made!
