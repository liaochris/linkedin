Yummy Spoonfuls wins national magazine recognition as best baby food.

Atlanta-based Yummy Spoonfuls won first place among 12 organic baby foods this month in a taste test. The taste test, conducted by Cookie magazine and a panel of 12 mothers and babies, ranked the products on flavor, texture, ease of use, and general appeal. Yummy Spoonfuls’ five star rating outranked Jack’s Harvest, Healthy Times, Tasty Baby, Happy Baby, Pomme Bebe, Plum Organics, Taste Bud, Petite Palate, Bobo Baby and Mom Made Food.
