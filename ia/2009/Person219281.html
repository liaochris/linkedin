Paul Jenkins’s Summary

Paul Jenkins is a seasoned, self-motivated Information Technology professional with over 26 year’s experience, with a unique combination of business experience and technical skills. A results focused and an effective leader, with proven ability to produce efficient business computing solutions. Talent for proactively identifying and resolving problems – proposing, architecting, developing and delivering cost effective business computing solutions

Paul successfully helped architect and lead an industry leading Microsoft .NET Point of Sale project. Paul was also instrumental in introducing relational database modeling, MS SQL and MSDE, Server 2003, XP Pro and XP Embedded, and Sonic MQ message brokering.

Paul began his working career as a retail manager. However, he realized a natural talent for software development and embarked upon a career that enabled him to combine his talents in understanding business processes and software solutions development.

Paul has held various lead and senior development positions across a diverse range of business sectors, including Retailing, Warehousing and Distribution, Manufacturing, and Banking. He excels in fast-paced, dynamic and challenging business environments, and is exceptionally comfortable operating at all levels of company management.

Paul is articulate, organized, an effective leader and mentor. He strongly promotes the belief that everyone can accomplish great achievements with the right balance of motivation and support.

Paul Jenkins’s Specialties:

IT dev. management & leadership, Software Solutions for Business, Dept. & Ent. apps development in industry sectors of Manufacturing, Retailing, Warehousing and Distribution, Banking, Inventory & Asset Management.

Adept at adopting & using dev. technologies including: Microsoft .NET, MicroFocus NetExpress, Tandem Pathway, UML for application modeling, ERStudio for data modeling, Unified Process for project framing, XML in MQ messaging & EAI.
