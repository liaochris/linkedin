Stephen Ruiz’s Summary

Stephen Ruiz is an Information Architect and User Experience expert. He has 13 years of experience in interactive technology, having served the field through positions as User Interface Designer, Interactive Producer and Independent Consultant. Over his career, Stephen has worked with a number of high-profile client companies, including many headquartered in Silicon Valley.

Stephen Ruiz’s Specialties:

Information Architecture, User Experience, Usability Testing, Heuristic Evaluations, Rapid Prototyping, System Usability, Computer Human Interaction, Taxonomy, Web Site Evaluation and Web Site Design

Stephen Ruiz’s Experience

  • Senior UX & Digital Strategy Consultant

    Agentes Consulting

    (Internet industry)

    September 2009 — Present (1 month)

    I am the founder of Agentes, and it's most Senior Consultant. I help people create better digital experiences. My primary responsibilities are to:

    - Create and manage web site wireframes, use cases, and data models by working with product managers, analysts and developers to enhance the feature set and performance of high-volume e-commerce websites.

    - Develop thorough, realistic IA plans that support business needs and objectives.

    - Understand business requirements and goals and help capture the strategy, content, and features for layout of the web sites or business logic flow.

    - Define web site architecture, navigation and site maps that serve as a blueprint of the site upon which all other aspects are built.

    - Create and manage schematics, process maps, feature lists, visual specification, data flows and other artifacts to describe the intended user experience.

    - Work with Quality Assurance to ensure that delivered features match IA-specified system functionality.

    - Participate in brainstorming sessions on functional requirements, content organization, process flow, UI specifications, and technical integration

    - Leading user research, concept testing, and usability testing
