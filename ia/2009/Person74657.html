Eric Novikoff’s Summary

My current goal is to grow ENKI, a Managed Cloud Computing services company founded both on a sound technical background of IT operations experience in both startup and enterprise environments, as well as a deep commitment to operation based on win/win relationships - relationships that mutually support my business and those of my customers and vendors from a basis of integrity.

My recent experience is in entrepreneurship: creating and managing the operations of a technology startup, including marketing, sales, finance, internal operations, and relationships. Prior to that I have extensive experience with managing technology generation projects (requirements, definition, implementation, quality, support) ranging from integrated circuit design, high speed memory and multimedia chip test, integrated circuit CAD software to automated test software and small/medium business enterprise resource planning and finance software. This experience, coupled with extensive management training and personal growth and self-realization training, has provided me with strong people-management and organization building skills as well.
