Richard K Howe’s Summary

Richard recently joined Kowabunga, a company focused on providing performance based technologies for online advertising, as President and Chief Executive Officer.

Previously, Richard was Chief Marketing Officer at the Acxiom Corporation where he had global responsibility for marketing and strategy. Rich had also run Acxioms Asia Pacific, Media, Consulting and Data Products divisions.

Rich also completed 3 acquisitions at Acxiom within email, search and banner that totaled more than $150 million. Rich also owned the companies partner programs.

Rich joined Acxiom from Fair Isaac & Company, where he served as general manager of the company’s Global Marketing Services unit, turning that unit around by growing revenue from $70 million to $100 million and improving profitability from a $12 million loss to a $20 million gain.

Before joining Fair Isaac, he founded and was the CEO of ieWild Inc., a software as a service marketing services company whose solutions were utilized by a number of the largest financial institutions in the U.S. He successfully negotiated that company’s acquisition to HNC Software where he had previously worked in both sales and product management capacities for the fraud, risk and originations products provider.

Rich has been at the forefront of software, services, data, IT and analytics technology designed to drive business intelligence for more than 15 years. He has launched more than a dozen products and been involved in more than eight M&A transactions ranging in size from $2 million to $800 million.

Rich graduated first in his class with a Bachelor’s degree in structural engineering from Concordia University, Canada, and a Master’s degree in engineering from McGill University, Canada. Rich is a patented inventor and currently sits on the board of the American Lung Association and Business for Diplomatic Action.
