Jim LeVine, Ph.D.’s Summary

Seasoned professional writer of books for business owners and others. Published writer that helps others organize, write, edit, self publish, and market manuscripts of books, memoires, websites, papers, essays about businesses, experiences and passions.

Writing instructor for private companies, training employees.

My goal is to be a writer and proponent of clear, simple, human and vital writing that can help others make their books, papers, reports, memos, web pages, correspondence, and instruction manuals more user friendly.
