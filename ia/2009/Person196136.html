Robin Wood’s Summary

I have spent 30 years working at board level with Fortune 500 and global organisations, as well as founding and leading several start-ups such as Idon, Genesys and Telesure, and co-founding the strategy and e-business practices at Ernst & Young. I was also a Managing Director at e-business creator Scient in the heady days of the internet boom.

I am now Presdient at Renaissance2- please visit us at www.renaissance2.eu

Renaissance2 is focused on shifting the mindsets of leaders who are capable of creating a new global Renaissance. To do this we apply five key principles:

1. Leadership & Transformation
• What kind of a leader are you?
• What is your Potential?
• Making it Happen

2. Sustainability & Thriving
• How Sustainable is your Lifestyle?
• Is Your Organisation/Community Sustainable?
• Creating a Sustainable, Thriving World

3. Innovation & Development
• Creating the Future
• Developing Yourself
• Developing Organisations and Communities

4. Communities in Action
• Creating and Joining Communities
• Finding Friends, Mentors and “Angels”
• Building Coalitions

5. Excellence
• Are you applying good/best practice in your field?
• Are you performing at your peak?
• What are your learning challenges personally and organisationally?

Learn more at: www.renaissance2.eu

My doctorate from the London Business School focused on organisational psychology and alignment, leading to the development of the alignment wheel which is used in conjunction with several other diagnostic techniques, including Peoplescan.

Robin Wood’s Specialties:

Personal Development, Team Development and Organisational Development applying the Strategic Alignment Wheel.
Intentional Wellbeing and Integral Leadership are two major focii of my work.
