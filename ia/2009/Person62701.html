Dez Stewart’s Summary

Business Intelligence Direct (BID) is a specialist lead and demand generation agency based in the UK, focusing on creating sales opportunity for the clients that we work for via Telemarketing.

BID generate opportunity for companies who either deliver professional services or technology led solutions, delivering complex propositions ranging from software, infrastructure, consultancy (systems integration) and managed services to management consultancy, actuarial consultancy, employee benefits (including pension strategies) business process outsourcing and transformation.

BID’s primary objective for our clients is to generate well qualified sales engagements and appointments for the sales / consultants to pursue, therefore establishing an initial relationship and sales engagement process with their prospect companies.

To support and deliver these successful campaigns, BID have developed a collaborative process to design telemarketing campaigns, bringing together the most effective sales and marketing messages with telemarketing best practice to produce a campaign framework. The process enables BID to design messaging, for even the most complex propositions , that will deliver opportunity for their clients.

This messaging is then delivered by a highly skilled and experienced account team who engage directly with the client to hand over opportunity.

You can check out our team and their backgrounds more effectively via LinkedIn, please feel free to contact us directly if you feel that we can add value to your marketing strategy

I have worked for BID for the last 8 years and can happily say that I and my colleagues are very competent at our jobs by targeting what ever decision maker level that may be required off us, all the way upto the most senior 'C' - level contacts.

If you would like to know more about me or the work I do please do not hesitate to contact me.
