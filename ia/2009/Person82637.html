Gabriel McIntyre’s Summary

Gabe McIntyre (known as GabeMac) has become an European authority on the subject of Video Online with almost 10 years of experience. He is video blogging, traveling all over the world to talk to companies, media agencies and at conferences about the subject. In the past he was host of XOLO.TV and Mobuzz.tv. Two of the largest and first professional video blogs in Europe. GabeMac also teaches at the Design for Virtual Theater and Gaming faculty at the Utrecht College of the Arts. One of the largest Art Academies in Europe. In his spare time you can always find Gabe making new viral video projects, interviewing interesting people, creating spectacular videos for clients or developing and teaching about new software and sites to help people create their own worldwide TV station for free. If you want to get your message on the web with video, Gabe Mac is the Viral Media Creative Consultant you are looking for. To keep up with GabeMac check gabemac.com.
