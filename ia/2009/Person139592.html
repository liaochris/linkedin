Len Dahlberg’s Summary

After 30 years as Executive Vice President of an International Consumer Products Company, I decided to start Dahlberg Global Enterprises to take advantage of my years of experience in sourcing products globally. I have developed key supplier relationships with companies in China, Korea, Taiwan, Japan, Indonesia, Thailand and Malaysia. I had the opportunity to travel extensively to these countries. I have also been responsible for the distribution and logistics operation which also adds to the knowledge I bring to my company to support our customers.

In addition to my experiences working with strategic suppliers in Asia, I have also established strategic relationships with legal counselors who concentrate on International Affairs, custom brokers, and freight companies.

Dahlberg Global Enterprises fills the need for many small companies that do not have the resources to research reliable suppliers, handle the logistics of importing products, and be assured their products will meet governmental safety requirements and industry standards.
No matter how small or how big your requirements are, Dahlberg Global Enterprises is capable of handling your needs. From custom imprinting to brand name products of the vendor, Dahlberg Global Enterprises has the network and expertise to find your partner in Asia and deliver your product on time, at competitive pricing and with the quality you demand.

Current products and raw materials include:

* Custom team logo Baseball Hats and Visors
* Custom team logo Baseball and Basketball Jerseys
* Team Flags and Car Flags
* Banners
* Ladies Handbags and drawstring bags
* Custom team mugs and glasses
* Team Souvenirs
* Team insulated cooler bags
* Any Custom Imprinted Items.
* Team promotional gifts.



We welcome the opportunity to be your partner in business.
