Rob Molenaar MBA’s Experience

  • Managing Director and owner

    ConceptOne (WISE-TM International B.V.)

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2005 — Present (4 years 8 months)

    ConceptOne, the tradename of WISE-TM International. B.V., develops concepts for small to large companies and develops its own concepts. The Shoppago.nl multimedia webconcept is developed for Retail to boost sales and to be able to work more closely together with e.g. manufacturers. Shoppago.nl will be introduced on the 28th of november. Contacts are currently with investors in shoppingcentres, shop chains and manufacturers/importers of retail products. The WheretoCard concept includes e-ticketing for eventlocations and the use of barcode and RFID for ticketing, e.g. using the public transport card. The WheretoCard-concept was sold to VolkerWessels in 2006.

  • Sales & Marketing Manager Entertainment & Retail

    Mobility Commerce Services (Mcom) - Volker Wessels

    (Events Services industry)

    October 2008 — May 2009 (8 months)

    Provided sales & marketing plan for Entertainment & retail to realise a growth of e-ticketing turnover for leisure contractants from 1 up to 10 % of total turnover within two years. Furthermore focus on synergy between leisure contractants and new customers and retailchains.

  • Business Development Manager

    Mobility Commerce Services (Mcom) - Volker Wessels

    (Events Services industry)

    January 2008 — October 2008 (10 months)

    Development of solutions for e-ticketing with public transport card (RFID) and combi-ticketing for events and public transport. Closed pilotcontracts with 6 medium to large retailchains for about 90 Add Value Machines (AVM's) in Amsterdam and Rotterdam. AVM's can be used by the traveller to load the public transport card with cash and also function as a pick-up device for public transport products bought on the Internet.

  • Commercial Director

    WheretoCard International B.V./VolkerWessels Telecom

    (Events Services industry)

    June 2006 — January 2008 (1 year 8 months)

    On the basis of the patents of WISE-TM, the company of Rob Molenaar, the company WheretoCard International B.V. was founded as a subsidiary of VolkerWessels Telecom to become an important e-ticketing supplier in the near future. Focus was also on the installation of access control with readers to check the e-tickets and in the future the public transport card with which tickets for the leisure market can be bought in the future. The e-ticketing system of LogicaCMG was bought and possibilities for RFID- and NFC-ticketing were implemented. WheretoCard provided her services for the 20 largest leisure events in The Netherlands and access control for Live Nation events in Belgium. WheretoCard was taken over by the new Volker Wessels company Mobility Commerce Services B.V. (Mcom).

  • Market Analist/Concept Development

    Posit, The Positioning Company

    (Market Research industry)

    February 2002 — May 2005 (3 years 4 months)

    Provided market analyses for e.g. ABN Amro, ING, Fortis, OPG, Princess, TPG, AKZO, DSM, Randstad, Schuitema, Sligro, Buhrmann, Getronics, GVB and Makro. Results were discussed with CEO's (e.g. Rijkman Groenink - ABN Amro; Van Voorst - Getronics; Blom - OPG; Brouwer - Schuitema, Farla - Randstad; Elverding - DSM; Kroon - GVB) and Directors of Strategy.

  • Managing Director

    Aldi Zaandam BV

    (Supermarkets industry)

    January 1999 — January 2002 (3 years 1 month)

    As Managing Director responsible for 57 Aldi-shops in the Randstad and a workforce of 335 people. Special focus on the efficiency of the shops and sales turnover. Management team: HRM, Distribution, Procurement & Sales.

  • Commercial Manager

    Electrolux Professional

    (Public Company; ELUX; Consumer Goods industry)

    January 1997 — December 1999 (3 years)

    Started at the beginning of the international reorganisation operation of Electrolux International and was Commercial Manager of one of the Professional companies "Foodservice Equipment" and advisor of the Managing Director for the internal efficiency operation. Electrolux Professional also was one of the cases for my MBA Dissertation about synergy between related mergers and acquisitions. Focus has been put on the dealermarket and projects were outsourced.

  • Senior Account Manager/Topverkoper

    Silcon no-break systems

    (Facilities Services industry)

    January 1989 — 1997 (8 years)

Realised important marketshares for medium and large no-break systems in the municipality market (market leadership), market of Chambers of Commerce (market leadership), Banking & Insurance (realised large projects with e.g. ABN Amro (e.g. Zuidas, Singapore and Japan), ING, Interpolis, Westland Utrecht Hypotheekbank).
