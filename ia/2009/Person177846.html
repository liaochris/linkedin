J. Glovsky’s Summary

( www.Twitter.com/OFFMar )

The large-scale commercial division of OFFMar Global Property Consultants exists to shorten the "chains" between investment object(s) and qualified investor client(s), worldwide.

With a full-service real estate brokerage offering sales and rentals in Miami, Florida, the worldwide OFFMar platform also engages 'on the ground' affiliates throughout western Europe, North Africa, the Caribbean and Latin and South America (in strategic as well as emerging markets) to provide discreet, DIRECT access to attractive and uniquely available opportunities: from existing hotels, shopping centers and office square footage, to luxury and bulk residential sales and rentals.

Additionally, through our Morocco-based OFFMar Ventures division, and strategic allliance with one of Europe's most renowned, family-owned yachting brokerages, we provide hard commodities, international product licensing and luxury lifestyle opportunities....Worldwide.
