Kevin Thomson’s Summary

Kevin Thomson FCIM ABC
Founder MCA (Acquired by WPP) and Textic.
Author Emotional Capital, The Employee Revolution, The Company Culture Cookbook.
Kevin has a background of business, brand, marketing, and change communication in the public and private sectors. Since 2004 he has been involved in developing web accessibility tools, technology and setting UK standards. He is an Honorary Professor of Marketing.
Prior to selling his business MCA in 2001, Kevin pioneered the use of internal marketing and branding inside organisations.
Clients and Awards - His clients have covered many of the worlds leading organisations including Inland Revenue, BA, Sony, Vodafone, Electrolux, Anglo American, KPMG, Barloworld, Royal and Sun Alliance. He and his team have won many awards including the New York Film Festival for non-broadcast video Gold and Silver Awards for BA Putting People First Again, IABC Gold Quill Awards and IPR Awards of Excellence

International Speaking Engagements
As President of MCA Kevin was a regular speaker at many international conferences like the Conference Board, Chartered Institute of Marketing, IABC, London Business Forum, Marcus Evans, and Marketing Week appearing in the UK, USA, Canada, Sri Lanka, Spain, South Africa.

Founder Textic – www.textic.com
In 2004, having sold his business to WPP, Kevin became an investor and innovative force in the area of web and mobile accessibility. He established Textic to develop tools to assist people with and without communication difficulties.

Leading work with UK and international charities and lead bodies. BS8878 and the DDA.
Currently Kevin represents both IMRG and the Chartered Institute of Marketing as a committee member on the new British Standards Institute, BSI standard BS8878 working with the BBC, IBM, RNID and many others. BS8878 is a standard to help organisations understand and deliver the DDA 1995/2005.
