Lori Simms’s Summary

I am embarking on a new career challenge at the State of Missouri, Office of Administration. This is the agency that is responsible for supporting all of state government through budget, policy, accounting and purchasing.

Prior to this, I worked in the travel and tourism industry for 11 years marketing the State of Missouri as a vacation destination. During those years, I have worked on various marketing and public relations campaigns.

I helped develop the Divison's ethnic marketing program which specifically targeted African-American and Hispanic travelers. I also worked with our Cultural Tourism program marketing to those who travel for history, heritage and art.
