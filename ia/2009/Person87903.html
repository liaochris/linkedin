Gail Mayhugh’s Summary

I am a Certified Interior Designer, Interior Merchandiser, Redesigner, Real Estate Stager and a Certified Interior Redesign Instructor and Certified Color Instructor, Speaker and Author.

I have been designing and operating a successful business in Las Vegas, Nevada for over 17 years, working with over 2000 clients. I have experience in Residential, Commercial and Model Home Design and have designed over 6,000,000 square feet of living and commercial space and interior merchandised over 450 model homes. I have clients in California, Arizona, Virginia, Colorado, New York, Utah, Wyoming, Texas, Pennsylvania and as far away as Russia.

A small list of the type of projects I have designed are Log & Adobe Homes to Traditional Style Homes, Single Executive Offices, 30,000 square foot buildings, Restaurants, Medical Offices, Television Set Design, Sky Boxes, Leasing Offices, Club Houses, Retail Shops and Model Homes.

Before starting my Interior Design business I was in banking for 10 years. I started at the bottom and worked up to holding positions of, Corporate Officer, Mortgage Processing Department Manager and Business and Professional Officer. My client’s appreciate my unique background, because it allows me to look at both the creative and business side of every project.

Since 2002, I have been the Design Smart Columnist for the Las Vegas Review Journal and an Adjunct Interior Design Instructor for the College of Southern Nevada.

I believe that the services of a Professional Interior Designer should be accessible to all. And that those looking to acquire education should receive more then expected. I especially pride myself in being honest, hard-working and approachable by all.

Interior Design, Color & Paint Consultations, Interior Merchandising for Model Homes, Interior & Exterior Finish Selections for homes and commercial, Speaking, Teaching Interior Redesign, Real Estate Staging and Decorating
