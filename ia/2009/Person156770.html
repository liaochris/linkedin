Matt Vincent’s Summary

After spending several years in corporate America selling solutions to fortune 500 and 1000 companies, law enforcement agencies, and education field I learned that my true calling was helping people in the job market. Since being in the recruiting field working both the temporary side and executive, the spring of 2008 I decided it was time to take my education to the next level and return to school to work on my master's in career counseling. Since starting grad school I have now taken several classes in human resources, counseling, and psychology and every class has made me a stronger recruiter and I have also learned why I have a passion for helping others.

Once I graduate in the summer of 2011 I could honestly go several different directions in my career. Most likely I will start off teaching at one of the junior colleges and will eventually start either a private practice or consulting firm helping others. Another part of me wants to get involved with working with at risk youths or adults in the correction system helping prepare them for the workforce once they have been released.
