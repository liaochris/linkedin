Cathy Ashbaugh’s Summary

For almost 20 years, I have been partnering with some of Southern California's top technical companies to assist in the recruitment of outstanding Software and IT professionals. Pacific Shore Resources, a company I founded in 1995 with an emphasis on the personal touch is focused on developing positive, long term relationships with the clients and candidates we represent. For over 11 years now, our team--comprised of family and friends who work very closely together--has been successfully recruiting Engineers, Developers, Management and Support personnel and more for many highly sought after companies, ranging from large well established firms to exciting start ups.

For career advice or information on how we can be of assistance to you and your firm in your recruitment efforts, please feel free to contact us. www.psresources.com. We are never too busy to help!
