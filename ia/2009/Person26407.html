Ben L. Campbell’s Summary

I hold a Bachelor's Degree from Northwest Missouri State University, where I double-majored in Business Management as well as Marketing. Shortly after graduating I packed up my truck, said 'Goodbye' to my friends and family back in Nebraska, and drove 1,200 miles to relocate to the Valley of the Sun.
