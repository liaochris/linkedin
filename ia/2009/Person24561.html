Gavin MacMillan’s Summary

Gavin has worked all over the world from London to Singapore, and Bangkok to Birmingham, with hundreds of restaurant and bar concepts. He has been featured in music videos, on the Travel Channel, A&E, City TV's Breakfast Television, E! Entertainment Television, The Toronto Sun, Metro News, Bar and Beverage, and many more. He can be seen on world championship competition DVD's, and he continues to train and compete with the world's finest bartenders. Gavin's monthly column "Raising the Bar" is read by more than 20,000 people every month in the Food Service News. He also co-authored Maran's Illustrated Bartending - the bestselling complete home bartenders guide available at bookstores everywhere. Gavin is happy to be working the Canadian Restaurant and FoodService Association to help shape the way the Canadian Bartending Industry approaches mixology. He has developed training programs and menus for A-list clients all over North America. Gavin is one of the founding members of BartenderOne.

My goal as a beverage educator is to have a noticeable impact on the level of bartending expertise worldwide.
