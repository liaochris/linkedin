Michael Falato’s Summary

Txtstation is a leading mobile marketing company specializing in sports and entertainment. We allow event owners, sponsors and media (TV, radio, press, Internet) to communicate with fans directly through their cell
phones, and create one-to-one ‘real time’ dialog with consumers at specific times and places such as sports events and concerts. Since 2000, we have been at the forefront of mobile interactivity designing “live” integrated programs that harness the excitement of live events while delivering high consumer response
and encouraging brand loyalty.

What We Do –

Engage and Entertain

Txtstation's complete mobile marketing service utilizes all interactive event media including cell phones, television, live event big screens and the Internet to engage and entertain your customers. We add value to

your event production and commercial activity, generating additional revenues for your properties. Your tailored campaign can include gaming and voting applications (vote for MVP or favorite song), promotional sweepstakes (meet a celebrity, win backstage passes), mobile couponing (merchandise and concession stand discounts), event-specific downloads (Java games, ringtones, wallpaper) and event information (upcoming events and promotions). Your campaign can deliver a database of qualified information to conduct ongoing dialog with your customers pre and post event.
