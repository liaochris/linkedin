Buyrite BC’s Summary

Buy Rite Business Furnishings is British Columbia's first choice for quality business interior products & services. It has one of Vancouver's largest inventories of pre-owned office furniture. They offer outstanding savings on the Office Desks, office Chairs, Filing cabinets, Office dividers and Cubicles. Their business model is built on perpetual improvement and growth. For them, it's about fair prices, quality products and most of all, serving their clients with integrity.

Their Services and Effective Office Solutions provide the following solutions:
• Office Work Space Planning
• Custom Office Furniture Design
• Office Renovations & Construction
• Office Furniture Rentals and Leasing
• Delivery and Installations
• Office Moving Services
• Furniture Repairs and Maintenance


Buy Rite Business Furnishing’s team of Business Interior Specialists maintains the highest level of customer service when providing Commercial project management.
