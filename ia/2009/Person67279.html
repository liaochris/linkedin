James L. Geiger M.S. M.D.’s Summary

Promoting Wellness through Nature is the "M.O." for the oilMD. Wellness is a global platform. As an expert in all things wellness I give presentations on gaining and regaining health.

I have applied to the Google 10 the 100th power contest with a presentation on worldwide malaria prevention co-endorsing the work of the Bill and Melinda Gates foundation, Malaria.org and the Millennium Project.

The goal is to teach farmers to grow the plants which are processed into cash crops that are distilled into essential oils which are used to to kill mosquitoes and prevent the spread of malaria by preventing mosquitoes from lighting and biting.Mosquito nets can be impregnated with natural non-neurotoxic phytomolecular compunds.

The Sweet Smell of Success:Health and Wealth Secrets is my latest book.
