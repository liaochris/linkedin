Daniel Oen’s Summary

What editors do with text, I do with images to tell the story
as clearly and concisely as possible. Instead of slicing or
adding letters, I use pixels.This usually means either taking
them out, putting them in, changing them, or it may mean
I have to illustrate what isn't there.

In the end, you should notice the image and not what I did to it.

In addition to retouching I'm trained in all aspects of DTP.
Including design, layout, production, prepress and
also digital small and large format printing.
