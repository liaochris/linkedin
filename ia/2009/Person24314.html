Andy Barrett’s Summary

I run my own wealth management practice within the St. James's Place Partnership providing bespoke financial planning solutions predominantly in London and the South East.

I work with people who are looking for answers and guidance to their individual or business financial situation, now and in the future, by providing bespoke financial advice that they can trust.

With the hectic lifestyles we lead today, fewer people have the time to manage their own financial affairs effectively. That is hardly surprising considering the financial world we now live in is both complex and fast changing. Consequently, at one time or another most people need to seek sound professional financial advice.

I help my clients see their financial future more clearly and most importantly I provide a clear plan as to how they will achieve their goals.

There are no simple off-the-peg solutions. When you need advice, you need advice you can trust that will assist you to manage your financial affairs more effectively. You also need a person you can relate to, who doesn’t talk jargon but explains concepts clearly.

Any meetings we have are entirely confidential and without obligation.
