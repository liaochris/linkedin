James Rouse’s Summary

I am a personal weight loss coach with a mission to help cure child obesity. My dream is to develop an online weight management program for children and adolescents. My focus has been on children who are obese. Finding a cure for child obesity is a daunting task. The creation of my website was to get the information across to children and families and try to get them to find an action plan.


Most of my time is spent working on the computer helping children find solutions. I also spend a great deal of time doing site promotions and building quality relationships.

I have learned through experience that the secret to network marketing is more about building quality relationships and being a solutions provider. We call it attractive marketing.
The internet has changed and to be successful online you need to be where the people are and you need to know your target audience. The answer is social networking.

Twitter, facebook, digg ,linkedin, and my space are where the people are. Social networking done the right way is a very effective way of building quality business leads. It is less about your product or service and more about being yourself. By being more conversational you position yourself as an expert by giving away free information and being active in the social scene. E-zine articles, web pages, squidoo pages, hub pages, etc. are terrific mediums for doing this. Its about pre-selling rather than selling. When you link from your articles to your webpages it builds link popularity which the search engines love because you are not hard selling. I am now finding myself coaching other network marketers by means of video and tutorials because this is where it is at. They are searching me out to learn more about this new model of marketing.
