René Gauthier’s Summary

• As WW Manager for Quality & Project Office and Shared Services, focusing on:
- Harmonization: sharing same processes, methods and tools among 3 R&D centres.
- Synergy: promoting within the 3 departments the sharing of knowledge and expertise (qualification, certification)
- Optimization: better using resources, and saving costs
- Process institutionalization for R&D activities (QA, CMM, CMMI).

• As R&D Manager:
- Coordinate Software Configuration Management and System Validation activities to capitalize and factorize developments on different products, tools, and methods.
- Estimate the level of different resources (infra-structure, equipments and manpower) in line with the global R&D budget.
- Follow-up with the teams managers the resources allocations to match required competencies and to meet milestones on projects conducted in a multi-site environment.
- Handle the logistic of the different teams, in relation with Sourcing and IT.
- Standardization of R&D activities for more efficiency and reliability: Contribute to have decisions on products development compliant with the internal project life cycle policy. Actively promoting processes focused on our business goals. Supporting use of reliable tools.

• As People Manager:
- Responsible for hiring,
- Conduct half-year evaluations and identify training needs to support R&D activities to develop skills among engineers and leaders,
- Communicate within the department and toward the other teams to reinforce consistency between the different activities (development, integration, and validation),
- Ensure on a daily base implementation of sub-contracting and off-shore developments in China, and guarantee the capability of the department to support our customers and cooperate with our partners everywhere in the World.

This position requires management and relationship skills, and a strong technical involvement in the Digital TV field and Embedded Systems developments.
