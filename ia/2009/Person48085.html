Colette Buscarini’s Summary

Having spent more than 15 years in the IT industry working for commercial and public organizations; Colette decided to transfer her wide range of skills, knowledge and experience in dealing with health and environmental issues. She therefore founded Naturä Inspired Ltd.

We test, redesign, promote and supply ecological body care products for the entire family. Not only these ecological body care products are healthier and provide an enormous relief for families at home, but they also help the region and its environment to cut down greatly on energy cost and landfill waste.

Before we distribute any new product into a new or existing market, we go through a thorough process.

Our core business components are:
-- Market Research
-- Strategy
-- User testing
-- Communication and Media
-- Sales
-- Distribution
-- Channel Management.

Colette Buscarini’s Specialties:

EU citizen, multilingual Creative Director/Interactive Designer and Senior User Experience Architect with strong general management, marketing strategy, interactive and creative design, information and site architecture, usability design, research and testing, business and IT strategy, communication and digital branding background.
