Ronnie Ilan’s Summary

I am an experienced solicitor, with a breadth of expertise of in-house legal work. I worked as a sole solicitor, a member of a team and run a department.

I welcomed challenges throughout my career, as I always sought to identify the strategic and operational requirements of the companies that I worked for. I have always taken the initiative to identify better ways of achieving the most effective outcome.

At DSG, I identified the need to better manage our intellectual property portfolio and lobbied senior management to set up a separate department to increase efficiency and reduce costs. I amalgamated fragmented domain name and trade mark portfolios across more than 30 territories into one platform provider, providing greater transparency, ease of use and cost reductions. I influenced and supported colleagues in developing marketing concepts, our OEM product portfolio and designing compliant procedures. I devised ways of protecting our intellectual properties, and creative solutions to infringement cases.

At Lledo I identified our eligibility for a Government grant and managed the application process including a site visit by the Minister for Trade and Industry. I managed our property portfolio, and initiated a review of rates which culminated in a substantial rebate. I worked with our brokers to tender our insurance and achieved better pricing by addressing concerns. As we did not have a HR department, I dealt with employment issues and one of the experiences that helped form me as a solicitor was successfully managing a company wide redundancy programme.

At Serco, I dealt with company secretarial issues and was responsible for compliance, share options, corporate governance and legal sign off of major projects. It was a fast paced environment with challenging issues, where both legal skills and creative solution delivery were highly regarded. I initially made my mark by finding a solution on a matter which two firms of advisers had failed to solve!

Ronnie Ilan’s Specialties:

I worked in a range of industries including retail, services, insurance and manufacturing -each having distinct corporate cultures.

I have been fortunate to gain diverse experience in all in house legal issues, commercial, corporate, corporate governance, company secretarial, intellectual property, disputes, employment and property.

I had great exposure to different jurisdictions, including, the US, Middle East, former Eastern European block, the Nordics and EU Member States.
