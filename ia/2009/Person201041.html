Robert Solomon’s Summary

Intellectual property advice is offered from a former CEO and technology leader. Maximize and protect the value of IP assets through effective commercialization and licensing. Patents, trademarks, copyrights and trade secrets. Assess IP assets prior to venture investments and M&A.

Previously built and led world-class technology organizations—Praxis International, GTECH, Apollo, Computervision, Datapoint.

Led software engineering programs and commercialization projects that transformed concepts to viable market products—with major market leaders, start-ups, and technology innovators (Digital Equipment Corporation, Apollo Computer, Computervision, GTECH, Datapoint, Praxis International).

Focused on culture and getting the right people into the right positions. Created innovative career-development plans and compensation plans. Developed staff who went on to management positions with EMC, Storage Networks and Hong Kong Jockey Club

Spearheaded successful turnaround initiatives for faltering organizations. Just as effectively, managed high-growth and downsizing efforts.

Significant experience managing many simultaneous projects and geographically dispersed teams

Developed and managed strategic relationships throughout career—OEM, VAR, and technology. Closed $6.8MM deal with Amdahl; closed/managed key accounts including BankBoston, Wachovia, UBS, The Gap, VISA, Applied Materials, Lucent, CSC, U.S. Army. Closed/managed $30 million (per year) Spanish National Lottery. Managed the $400M Bell Laboratories account for DEC.

Captured investor interest and millions of dollars in funding—successfully raised funds 3 times, with both angel and established investors (Goldman Sachs). Effectively represented companies and their technology concepts with investors, analysts, and M&A candidates.

Robert Solomon’s Specialties:

Accomplished in both turnaround and green-field situations; able to quickly evaluate existing teams, plans and processes, determine the holes, fix them and get things back on track; adept at putting together the integrated teams, plans and processes necessary to successfully bring new products to the marketplace. Managed P&Ls exceeding $300 million.
