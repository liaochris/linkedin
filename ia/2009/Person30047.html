Boaz Rossano’s Summary

Experienced product manager, marketing and sales manager, and business strategist with over 15 years of hands on and managerial experience. Expert branding & Marcom consultant for High-tech companies.

Founded and managed several companies in the Israeli Internet arena.

Currently serves as a consultant in 2CENTS.co.il, consulting in the fields of Product Marketing, User Experience, branding, Marcom, etc. to his clients and assisting to Netica's clients (a company that he recently sold), amongst them: Amdocs, Fundtech, Migdal, IAI, Comverse, SanDisk, and others.

Consults to the council of high education regarding Interactive media design programs in local academies.

Boaz Rossano’s Specialties:

Custom-made Web applications design and development, product management, User Experience Design (UX & GUI design for all standard platforms, inc. Web, IPTV, iTV, Mobile and PDAs), e-branding for high-tech companies, product marketing & product strategy, corporate/road-show presentations.
