Justin Kownacki’s Summary

I'm the creator / writer / producer of the web sitcom Something to Be Desired (or STBD), a Pittsburgh-based comedy about life after college. In addition, I produce custom web videos for corporate clients in Pittsburgh and beyond. I also write features for The Art Institute of Pittsburgh's alumni department.

As an ambassador for social media, I'm the co-organizer of the annual PodCamp Pittsburgh "un-conference," which combines podcasters, bloggers, artists, journalists and business owners in one multifaceted 2-day conversation. I've also spoken at several media conferences, including Video on the Net 2007 in San Jose and the Blogference (July 2007) in Tel Aviv, Israel.

My future goals include the expansion of STBD into new markets and the opportunity to create new entertainment and information experiences. (Yes, this means I enjoy working online, in TV, in film and in print. Distribution is merely a means to an end.)
