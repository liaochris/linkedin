Julie King’s Summary

Company Statement:
The goal of Greenworld Creative LLC is to provide excellence to clients on all levels of service in creating effective, visual and other communications that enhance and strengthen a client’s brand and help grow their business. The Greenworld Creative philosophy embraces the essence of “Green” to generate “Green” — think with both sides of the brain; choose and use resources wisely; develop smarter, more efficient creative solutions; and, make shrewd investments now to yield greater return in the future.

Profile Summary:
More than a decade of management experience in the creative industry. Consistently satisfy/surpass needs and goals of employers and clients to increase sales and profitability through creative materials, detailed workmanship and organizational expertise. Entrepreneurial, self-starting problem solver with a passion for melding design and practical functionality. An exquisite, symbiotic balance of right and left brain.
