Mark Rittman’s Summary

Very experienced developer and architect for business intelligence & data warehousing systems based on Oracle technology. Lead and successfully delivered projects for FTSE100 businesses in the UK together with universities, charities and public sector organisations.

Recognised expert on tools such as Oracle Warehouse Builder, Oracle Discoverer, Oracle OLAP, Oracle BI Suite EE and the BI elements of the Oracle database, with articles published in Oracle Magazine, OTN and other publications.

Speaker at events such as Oracle Open World San Francisco, UKOUG Conference, ODTUG Kaleidoscope and Collaborate on business intelligence development topics, contributor to the Oracle Press Discoverer 10g Handbook and author of the forthcoming Oracle Press book "Oracle Business Intelligence Suite Developer Guide"

Mark Rittman’s Specialties:

Deep understanding of Oracle business intelligence & data warehousing technologies, consulting, systems architecture, presales, technical writing, presenting.
