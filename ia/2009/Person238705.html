Trevor Shanski’s Summary

Trevor Shanski is the founder of eWORDofMOUTH Inc. As the former Chief Operating Officer of Warehouse One Ltd., a 100+ store national retail apparel company, Trevor held P&L responsibility for all aspects of operations. Trevor took the company from 13 local stores to 108 stores nationally and grew revenues of Warehouse One to nearly $100 million in sales. Warehouse One remains one of the most technologically advanced retail chains in Canada.

After achieving acceptance to the U.S. under a designation of an Alien of National Interest and Extraordinary Ability, Trevor sought to apply his diverse national supply chain experience and formal Strategic Management, Operations, and Information Systems education from the Wharton School of Business to a new model emerging that could be used on a mass scale, Software as a Service (SaaS). 2006 marked the beginning of the eWORDofMOUTH on demand Enterprise eMarketing system which leverages the Web 2.0 platform. eWORDofMOUTH fills the increasing demand for cost efficient, measurable, enterprise e-marketing technologies tailored for mid-market multi-location companies.
