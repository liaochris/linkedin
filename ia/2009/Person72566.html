  • Systems Analyst

    Cleveland Clinic

    (Non-Profit; Hospital & Health Care industry)

    August 2002 — August 2003 (1 year 1 month)

    Was part of the Middleware team participating as an Integration specialist. Middleware is a very important part of the Healthcare ERP. A typical Healthcare ERP consists of the ERP connected to a mainframe backend via a middleware application. The middleware application intercepts all communications between the ERP and mainframe (and vice-versa) filtering out unnecessary data and formatting data that enters from third-party (vendor) applications. The communication between ERP, Vendor, and mainframe is called a transaction (TRX) and follows HL-7 standards. HL-7 standards are very similar to EDI and X-12 that may be more familiar to SAP consultants.
