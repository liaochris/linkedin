Roger Hamm’s Summary

I have 15+ years in syndicated marketing research in the CPG arena. During that time I developed expertise in Client Service - Manufacturer, Client Service - Retail, Operations, Client Support, Technology Management, Product Management, Project Management, Application Development, Process Improvement and more.

Most recently I was a successful product manager for both web based and desktop Business Intelligence Applications. These products that were deployed across the globe. I managed numerous cross-functional, cross-border and cross-cultural teams to deliver world-class products to Fortune 100 clients to enable them to make key business decisions based on the best market intelligence available.

I managed the entire lifecycle of these products from initial customer requirements identification to defining the product vision, working closely with senior management, sales, implementation and development to exceed revenue goals and ensure client satisfaction.

★I am actively searching for other opportunities in Marketing Research, Application Product Management and/or Business Intelligence where I can continue this success and help companies grow their lines of business.
