Adam Madlin’s Summary

Adam is an entrepreneur specializing in online innovation communities and is working to reinvent the business of innovation. He is a founding executive of Jijuu™ where he leads operations for the startup business. He is an established entrepreneur and startup executive with more than 25 years of professional experience and ten years of entrepreneurial experience. He has started multiple companies as well as advised numerous entrepreneurs and inventors. Adam’s industry experience spans the customer loyalty, market research, software, Internet, enterprise software, IT, and consulting industries. Throughout his extensive career he has worked with many Fortune 100 companies advising them and implementing enterprise software systems on a global basis. His degrees from Duke University, Rochester Institute of Technology and the University of Rochester are still being put to use in his endless quest for knowledge and new challenges.

Adam Madlin’s Specialties:

Innovation, Internet, market research, entrepreneurship, world leading Net Promoter expert, customer loyalty, enterprise software, and professional services.
