Christel Steinvorth’s Summary

Over 14 years of combined experience in human and organizational development. Passionate about building common ground, bridging differences and generating new worlds (i.e. perspectives) in support of collaborative action for protecting and/or achieving life quality.

Christel Steinvorth’s Specialties:

Strategic planning facilitation, front end analysis, performance improvement interventions.

Knowledge management strategies, participatory methodologies and virtual tools.
