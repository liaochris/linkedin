Carla Dal Mas’s Summary

Carla Dal Mas has worked as a Designer, Job Captain and Urban Planner in San Francisco, California and Brazil. Her project experience includes community, retail, mixed-use, urban design, planning, housing, residential, cultural and healthcare projects. These projects have allowed Carla to work directly with local agencies, consultants and clients through all phases.

Carla is committed to extending her knowledge by attending to events by organizations such as AIA, SPUR, USGBC and SF Environment. Her next goal is to become a licensed architect and further her studies in sustainability as an asset and as a way of contributing to a better world.

Carla Dal Mas’s Specialties:

architecture, interiors, urban design, project coordination, green materials research.
