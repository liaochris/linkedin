Angie Mehsling-Gorr’s Summary

I have 8 years experience as a Business Analyst/MIO Coordinator in the retail and CPG industry. I have worked on a variety of complex projects. At Kohl's I worked on size and pre-pack optimization, allocation system design, trend/forecasting development, purchase order and distribution/receiving systems. I have mentored and trained Analysts and Planners throughout the organization on allocation and forecasting best practices.

I have built prototypes for applications. The users are able to use the prototype and give feedback, so tweaks can be made to the prototype. From there, the actual application can be designed. I found this helpful in multiple projects. The design is more likely to be accepted by the users once the application is built, and the prototype can be used to match up against during system testing.

At Connell, I was the CRM Administrator and Developer. I used Salesforce.com. I was able to streamline business processes using workflows and approvals. I developed my own tool using VBA to help catch duplicate accounts and contacts. I used Salesforce to Salesforce to securley share our data with another organization. I eliminated outside forms and spreadsheets and embedded them within the applications, further streamlining business processes and communication.

I am currently seeking employment. My company is closing their doors Dec 31st 2008. I'm looking for a career as a Business Analyst, Inventory Management, or CRM development and Design. I enjoy challenging and complex projects.
