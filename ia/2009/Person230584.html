Teri Greene’s Summary

-- I report and write feature and enterprise stories for the Montgomery Advertiser. Topics include home & garden, entertainment/arts, style/fashion, health and -- my strong point -- in-depth profiles of fascinating people.
-- I post several daily updates on our website, www.montgomeryadvertiser.com, and my stories, often accompanied by photo galleries and videos, are included on the website.
-- I am a staff writer for Elite magazine, a quarterly publication aimed at the Advertiser's high-income readership.
-- I am primary local writer for Health & Fitness, the Advertiser's popular monthly health magazine.
-- I am trained in shooting and editing video for the web and am a novice photographer always seeking instruction to improve my skills.
-- Recent freelance writing assignments include work for Pilates Style magazine
