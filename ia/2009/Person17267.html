Ann Farrell’s Summary

For over 14 years, Ann has been guiding her clients on anything and everything to do with direct mail. Whether providing independent marketing consulting for small businesses or serving on a team of niche consultants for a larger corporate initiative, Ann's professionalism, unique background and client focus are a true asset.

Most recently as Periscope’s in house Direct Response Specialist, Ann served a variety of accounts teams with DM program development. Her expertise was critical to the direct response promotions for multiple clients such as Centerpoint Energy, Cox Business Services, Miracle Ear, Buca di Beppo, Treasure Island & Arctic Cat.

She began her career in direct as VP of start up niche marketing firm, Irresistible Ink in 1994 where she established relationships with State Farm Insurance, Discover Cards & Skyline Displays to grow the unique “handwritten direct mail” company from 0 to $7MM in four short years.

Ann also served as Director of Marketing for Irresistible Ink helping to establish brand within the direct industry. This publicity attracted the attention of Kansas City based Hallmark Cards and their Loyalty Marketing Group who purchased Irresistible ink in 1998.

As Account Executive at Hallmark Loyalty, Ann continued her relationships & work with personalized direct mail programs on State Farm & Discover Cards while gaining experience with loyalty programs of Huntington Banks, Purina & Saks Fifth Avenue.

Ann spent over 3 years at PrimeNet where she rounded out her direct marketing expertise by offering a full range of direct mail and database marketing tools while directing the DM programs for local accounts including Cub Foods (Supervalu), Treasure Island, Dairy Queen, Star Tribune, Sedgwick’s, Buca, Inc. and Haskell’s.

Ann Farrell’s Specialties:

Her expertise includes: direct mail strategy, offer development and creative management, specialty print procurement, list brokerage, practical understanding of data processing and hygiene products, personalization / letter shop production techniques and postal requirements. She is also skilled at defining test metrics; database segmentation, front-end audience selection, backend response & gains needed analysis.
