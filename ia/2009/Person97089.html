Do you need Lead Generation to go the extra miles? In other words, would you appreciate Sales Leads that have been so well qualified they are much closer to done deals than ever before?

How about if you could have a 'heavyweight' software sales person on your Lead Generation, Lead Development Team, without the expensive overhead you might expect?

I offer:

- In-depth experience and successful track record taking new software technologies into new markets - "putting new software technologies and companies on the map ... "

- Market development & Sales expertise in Enterprise Software, Data Integration & Business Intelligence tools, Talent Management Systems, Biotechnology Solutions ...

- An early stage company specialist skilled in market niche discovery & development

- Finding & establishing the first waves of early adopters

- In depth understanding of complex technology from a marketing perspective

Ian Goldsmid’s Specialties:

"The expert ability to “diagnose” who (usually at senior manager/executive levels) has a complex need and what it is exactly - and how the need fits a particular software solution capability – and to predispose the prospect to taking the “remedy” - this is a function many find the hardest. Well, that is what I enjoy and really excel at."
