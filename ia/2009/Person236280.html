Tom Peroutka’s Summary

Tom has worked in the E-Commerce and Search Engine Marketing industry for the past 5 years with MonsterCommerce and Network Solutions. Tom started out as a sales representative helping over 1,000 small business succeed online to managing a sales team of 10 sales representatives that helped MonsterCommerce successfully grow to more than 6,000 customers and $20 million in annual revenue.

MonsterCommerce was recognized by the St. Louis Business Association for numerous awards and accolades during his tenure:

- Fastest Growing Private Company - St. Louis Louis Business Journal (2004)
- Best Places to Work - St. Louis Louis Business Journal (2005)
- Best E-Commerce Software - SIIA Codie Awards (2005)
- 50 Most Influential Companies - St. Louis Commerce Magazine (2005)

After Network Solutions acquired MonsterCommerce in early 2006, Tom managed a team of 20 E-Commerce and Online Marketing sales representatives that achieved over $6 million in new acquisition revenue in 2007 and $9 million in new acquisition revenue in 2008.
