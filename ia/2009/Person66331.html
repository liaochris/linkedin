Doug Daniels’s Summary

Doug Daniels is a 18-year veteran of the wireless and GIS industry with strong sales, business development, marketing and technical leadership covering software, consumer electronics and services.

While at DigitalGlobe, Doug was Director of Consumer Sales, a leading provider of high resolution satellite imagery products and services. His leadership was responsible for building multi-million dollar revenue streams from major Fortune 500 companies including Microsoft, Google, Navteq, i-cubed, Garmin and other LBS application developers.

Prior to DigitalGlobe, Doug was Vice President of Sales for deCarta, a leading geo-spatial software platform company. His sales leadership is recognized by customers such as Google Maps, Yahoo Maps, Ask.com, TeleNav, Qualcomm, Networks in Motion, Hotels.com, Zillow and dozens of other deployed mapping and navigation applications.

Before deCarta, Doug was Vice President of Sales for Cellport, a leading telematics IP licensing and technology company focused on providing embedded hands-free solutions for the automotive market. Previous to Cellport, Doug was National Sales Manager for SignalSoft (acquired by Openwave), a leading provider of location infrastructure and software for both E-911 and commercial LBS wireless location services.

Doug began his career at AT&T Wireless Services, where he held a variety of senior management positions, including Director of Sales - Wireless Data for the Western Region, where his team was consistently ranked #1 for the entire company.

Doug holds a Bachelors of Arts degree in Economics from the University of Colorado - Boulder and a Masters degree in Telecommunications from the University of Denver. Doug lives in Castle Rock, Colorado, and has 2 children age 4 and 6. In his spare time, Doug’s a CAT4 bike racer, all-mountain expert skier, and overall outdoor enthusiast.
