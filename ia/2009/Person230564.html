Teresa Scholten’s Summary

Teresa brings extensive recruiting and healthcare experience to her role as Chief Operating Officer. She initially worked as a nurse recruiter for a major medical center within the Human Resources department and then as a healthcare recruiter for a national LTAC company. Prior to co-founding HeadHunter Health Exchange, Teresa owned and operated Professional HealthCare Consultants, LLC, a contingency healthcare recruiting business that specialized in the permanent placement of healthcare professionals into leadership positions nationwide. Teresa’s unique understanding of healthcare recruitment stems from her years as a registered nurse, specializing in adult critical care. Working at both a major medical center and a county hospital setting, she served as a mentor and preceptor for new employees as well as in various leadership roles. Her extensive clinical and recruitment experience give her exceptional insight when assisting employers fill key positions and conducting searches for healthcare professionals. Teresa has a B.S. N. degree from St. Mary’s College in Notre Dame, Indiana.

Teresa Scholten’s Specialties:

Sourcing strategies, retentions programs, web based HR applications, salary and benefits programs, sales and marketing tactics.
