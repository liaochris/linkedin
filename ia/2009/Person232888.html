Thomas Young’s Summary

Thomas has been involved in many things through-out his career, from owning and operating the family billiard hall in the late 80's, coordinating software design/development, to now operating completely freelance as an Audio Recording Engineer.

Through out his career Thomas has worked with many high-end clients with high expectations and demands. Now as a owner operator of Sound Graphics Thomas continues to produce great products at a reasonable price. Currently Thomas has moved into expanding his talents in the video realm with the same dedication and pride.

Thomas recently assisted Joe Vitale with the new Crosby, Stills, & Nash CD. "Joe had just received the newly mastered version from Sterling Sound and we were comparing the tracks with the original mix's."

Thomas Young’s Specialties:

Sound Recording and Content Editing.
Video Editing and Sound Sweetening.
Programs:
Digidesign Protools, Final Cut Express, Sound Designer II, GarageBand, Apple Computers!
