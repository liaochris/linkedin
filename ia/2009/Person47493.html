Clint Kenner’s Summary

The Kenner Group is dedicated to BUILDING THE HEART OF BUSINESS™ allowing people and companies to reach their full potential.

At The Kenner Group, our search goals mirror our clients hiring goals; to not only find an excellent candidate that you are excited about the first couple of months, but more importantly 12 months later.

For that reason we are very unique in that we delay a portion of our fees for 12 months. If for some reason our placed candidate leaves your organization during the first 12 months, two critical events are triggered:

First, we permanently forfeit the remaining portion of our service fee, plus we conduct a search at our cost to replace the candidate.

The Kenner Group provides this extraordinary guarantee because we are confident that our exclusive search process will yield successful results.
