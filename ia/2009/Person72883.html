Emily Cunningham Rushing’s Summary

With a background in social sciences, law, and information sciences, I have experience in research, analysis, writing, and instruction. I look forward to developing my career as a research specialist and strategic intelligence expert.

I have worked as a librarian and researcher since 2004. I am currently the Competitive Intelligence Specialist for Haynes & Boone LLP in Dallas, TX. Previously, I was previously the market researcher and database administrator for a management consulting firm. From 2005-2007, I worked as a Research Specialist with King & Spalding LLP in Houston, TX. I graduated from the University of North Texas’s MLIS program in December of 2007 after receiving a JD from the University of Houston Law Center in May of 2004. I also have a Bachelor’s of Arts in Sociology from the University of Houston.

In addition to research, my hobbies include reading, cooking, and reading about cooking
