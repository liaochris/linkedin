Martin Stillman-Jones’s Summary

Martin has a thorough understanding of modern business management including complex strategy formulation, gained with blue chip consumer goods & consumer durables businesses, built up over the last 30 years in Commerce.

He has applied these techniques across a wide variety of sectors Nationally & Internationally, operating at Board level in small medium and large, private & public organisations.

Martin concentrates on developing strategic competencies in leaders and teams. Guiding them to find & go through important transitions; providing help in making wiser decisions in business, developing values, attitudes, skills and habits in order to make them more effective, creative and productive.

Additionally Martin Chairs Chief Executive Seminars, where he facilitates "idea sharing" between Business Leaders, in order to help one another solve complex problems, achieve better results and outperform the competition.

Martin Stillman-Jones’s Specialties:

Strategic Acquisitions & Disposals. Scar tissue masquerading as wisdom!!

Considerable & specific experience in developing and implementing creative “lateral solutions” to add shareholder value.
