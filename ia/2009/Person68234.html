Vicki Duncan’s Summary

At Duncan Business Services, Inc. we strive for excellence in every project we undertake. Whether we are assisting clients in their Direct Marketing efforts through graphic design, printing, procurement of mailing lists, mail processing, invoice and statement processing, or fulfillment, we give each aspect of the project our full attention.

On the business services side, we work closely with our clients to customize our services to their needs for off-site secretarial services. Our menu of services includes bookkeeping, typing, word processing, data entry, forms creation, medical, general, and legal transcription (both digital and tape formats), secure FTP site for file transfers, appointment setting, fax transmission and receipt, and on-demand mailing and fulfillment services.
