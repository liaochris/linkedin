Ross Cranwell’s Summary

I am currently working for Praxis Language, an innovative company that converges learning with technology. After just 3 years in operation, we have over 300,000 users users from all over the world and cooperations with some of the biggest companies and organizations worldwide including the Chinese Government's National Office for Teaching Chinese as a Foreign Language.

Chinese, English, Italian, French and Spanish are the 5 languages that we are currently concentrating on, but spurred on by their success we will be expanding into more languages, such as Arabic and Russian, to supply people around the world with fun, practical and convenient learning!

Having lived for 4 years in China, both as a student and as a working professional at the largest American chamber in Asia, I have attained language, culture and business experience that is important for helping Praxis Language succeed in China and the global market.

With Praxis Language, I am helping to promote this fantastic learning method by cooperating with organizations globally that are involved in media, education, technology and more.
