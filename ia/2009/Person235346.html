Todd Vaccaro’s Summary

Seasoned, award winning Sr. Product Team Manager responsible for the definition, strategy and execution of educational and e-commerce websites based on business requirements and user testing. Recognized as the person to solve problems, develop business strategies and ensure quality deliverables in a timely manner.

Managed overall product line, and those responsible for making individual components of the product, from inception to completion and maintenance. Investigated and communicated strategy, product details, schedule, specifications, features and usability testing. Accountable for obtaining business requirements, new content, workflow production stats, improving quality, process and technologies related to a product.

Specific strengths include:
- Product Specifications: Using a sound software development model, like rational unified process (RUP) as well as IA documentation like UML and use cases to ensure that technical specifications meet market needs.
- Project Communications: Providing documentation about budget, schedule, specifications and solutions to problems across both the executive team and the project team.
- Instructional/Information Design: Years of skill, from being a high school teacher/dean, information architect and a designer of e-commerce and instructional software, ensuring users relate to the material and learn from it in a smart, simple, interactive design.
- Content Architecture: In conjunction with the business, many years of experience developing the business strategy, technical aspects (DTDs, taxonomies), and workflow surrounding content development and management.
- Creative Manager: A project is more than just execution; creativity allows problems to be solved with a balance of resources and business requirements.
- Usability: Understanding how users truly use a piece of software through user-centered analysis and testing ensures a solid user retention and ROI strategy.
