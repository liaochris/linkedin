Jeff Richman’s Summary

Jeff has been in the world of real estate investing since 1994. Starting with the buying and rehabbing of properties, he quickly moved into other areas of property investing. To date, he has done well over 200 transactions, which include wholesaling, assignment of contracts, Subject to (the art of buying property with little or no money), lease options (the art of selling property), owner financing, pre-foreclosure, and foreclosures, short sales and more.

He started in Broward County, Florida and to date has done property deals in 9 states. He has spoken on many topics nationally from stage as well as syndicated radio and Internet shows.

Jeff began his career in Asset Recovery helping people with the facilitation and recovery of Surplus Funds (also known as Excess Proceeds and Tax Sale Overages) in November of 2008, when his partner showed him the opportunity in helping other people achieve from the unfortunate experience of foreclosure the return of money that they did not know they could receive.

Prior to his real estate investing career and surplus fund recovery, he was the Vice-President and Director of Sales for a large medical equipment company in Miami, Florida.

Jeff Richman’s Specialties:

I am an expert at recovering Surplus Funds, Excess Proceeds & Tax Overages for people who do not know that money exists.

I am an expert at negotiating judgments and liens off of a property or settling the debt for pennies on the dollar to redirect Surplus Funds, Excess Proceeds or Tax Sale Overages to an individual where, before, they would not receive any money.

Excellent at negotiating and settling defaulted paper with institutions.
