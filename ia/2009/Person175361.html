Nika Wynnyk’s Summary

My background starts with a Bachelor’s Degree in Film/Television from California State University, Long Beach '03 that led me to work full-time in Production and Casting.

-First, I began as a Casting Recruiter for Commercial Scouts working closely with directors to ensure operations were carried out efficiently and effectively.
-Then, grew my office experience as a Casting Coordinator for a Production Studio where I managed and maintained the audition process, kept a database and dealt with front of house responsibilities.
-This developed my marketing tools, which helped to launch a Visual Merchandising Company.
-There I assisted in providing executive administrative support, daily scheduling and coordination of supplies and travel, domestic and international shipping, handling phones, participating in trade shows and marketing.

I have also developed skills in fast-paced, high-end restaurants in San Francisco, which have included private catering. In the last year I have enjoyed hosting marketing research events with a high level of professionalism.

My personality includes a multitasking, enthusiastic, self-starter with a smile. My ability to handle challenges while mixed with my great communication skills makes me a good team player.
