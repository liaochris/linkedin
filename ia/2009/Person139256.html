Lee Packham’s Summary

A career of 10 years in the I.T. industry spans the gaming and medical sectors. Starting out developing systems for cancer treatment machines, I learned the skills to architect large and complex systems that are built to last and be secure to the highest standards. These skills were applied to the successful Prizefight and X-ray Gaming Platforms as well as optimising sites like Enemy Down to manage larger user bases than they were designed for. While at TNWA, under the help of Paul Sulyok and Simon Green I developed leadership skills to manage projects and teams of developers to produce the systems I architect. Looking to continue in senior management roles and apply my skills to help other companies turn projects, and teams, around.
