Jason Gonzales’s Summary

Web design and development have been integral to every aspect of my professional life. To promote my career as a musician and recording engineer I made websites, designed promotional materials and otherwise leveraged the web. In addition, I worked in public schools, teaching youth to use computers as multimodal composing tools—making digital video, audio and text. I served as the Tech Coordinator for The Arts and College Preparatory Academy high school, providing web application and database design as well as network support. Most recently, I have been developing and designing for the web as a freelancer and for RMG Advertising, serving clients that range from Weight Watchers to HP. I am currently the lead designer and developer at RMG.
