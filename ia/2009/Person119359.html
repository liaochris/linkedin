Jon Gonzales’s Summary

My career spans over 41 years a salon owner, hairdresser/stylist, seminar leader, business consultant, educator and author. I founded Hairdresser Career Development Systems because I saw a need to upgrade and modernize the salon owner and hairdresser educational system. I felt it needed to better meet the educational and business needs of my colleagues. My only business is education…my company is not affiliated with any special interest groups or industry manufacturers.

To succeed in the industry, beauty professionals need to know more than just how to cut and style hair. That’s where I come in. Through Hairdresser Career Development Systems I am providing my expertise and easy to understand, affordable programs that are helping salon owners and hairdressers achieve higher levels of excellence – personally, professionally and financially.

My ‘Business Management Seminars’ are widely acclaimed for helping salon owners & managers develop powerful management skills that are taking their businesses to a whole new level. My ‘Personal Development & Success Seminars’ are impacting thousands of hairdressers, salon owners, spa managers and beauty industry professionals throughout the United States and Canada.

I am personally committed to sharing my 41 years of real-world experience and helping my colleagues raise their standard of living and reach higher levels of excellence.

Jon Gonzales’s Specialties:

Teaching owners & managers how to: develop a business plan, improve salon profits & cash flow, practice team building strategies, develop a training program and ultimately how to increase salon/spa traffic and client retention.

Teaching hairdressers how to: develop a proven plan of action for success, identify & avoid customer loss, create value & a demand for services, gain the competitive edge, build an enthusiastic & upscale clientele and ultimately double and triple their income.
