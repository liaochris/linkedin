Nathaneal Mohr’s Experience

  • Business Development Team Leader

    GuaranteedProfit.com

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2001 — Present (9 years )

    I have been in working in the areas of lead generation, direct-marketing, one-to-one and one-to-group sales for over 11 years in 38 different industries. Not because I didn’t like any or all of them; but because as soon as the system was growing the business I quickly and smoothly got bored and moved on to another industry.


    After my daughter was born in 2007, I decided to put organization to my Entrepreneurial ADD behavior and officially build a team of very talented individuals that could provide predictable growth for companies that offer great products and services but are not sure how to exactly reach their goals. I put this team under the umbrella of GuaranteedProfit.com

    Yes, we had a lot of fun challenges to overcome starting a new company in the middle of a recession. (such as vendors going out of business after we paid for their services and so on) But all that this did was inspire us to evolve our services to be more hands on.

    Because of the exceptional results we were able to get for our clients during the recession, we now have an overwhelming demand for our services that forces use to charge a reservation fee as well as put our new clients on a waiting list.
