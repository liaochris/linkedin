Susan Garcia’s Summary

I am a 2009 graduate of the University of Minnesota with a BA in Strategic Communications. I have an emphasis in Public Relations, and a minor in Fine Arts.

I have been an intern in the marketing and communications field since 2005 and have experience in government proposals, special events, media relations, consumer relations, and non-profit work.

I am actively working toward becoming a professional working in an agency setting. I enjoy my work with social media trends and innovations but would also welcome the challenge of working in other industries. I am open to all challenges, and would be excited to move wherever my career takes me.
