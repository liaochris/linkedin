Janet M Langer’s Summary

Janet has been teaching and choreographing for over thirteen years and still remains a student yearning to evolve new styles fused of many elements. Her love and passion for teaching all ages has spread Nationally and Internationally. She is trained in Hip-Hop, Jazz, Ballet, Tap, Breakin’ and Modern, but her influences don't stop there. Janet is inspired heavily by anything and everything in her environment. Clear explanation of technique, pure expression and a lot of fun are what make Janet's classes fulfilling and memorable. Most recently Janet assisted Choreographer Teresa Espinosa on Mylie Cyrus’ new video, “Start All Over”. She also served as the Assistant Choreographer/Producer of Kevin Maher’s “STEREOTYPE”. Janet is the Original Founder of The Funkanometry Dance Company, which has branches in San Francisco and San Diego. Janet has done choreography and master classes for numerous studios, high schools, companies and universities and served on the prestigious dance faculty at The University of California, Irvine. She has also performed with the world renowned Groovaloos in their stage show, "GROOVALOO". Janet is very proud to have been a semi-finalist on Hi-Hat's new reality-TV pilot, "The Choreographer" and teaches regularly at Edge Performing Arts Center in Los Angeles. Other credits include: Monsters of Hip-Hop: The Show, Toyota, Carnival, Dance2xs Portugal/London/Chicago/UIUC, Loyola Marymount University, NIKE, Kat Jimenez, The Ellen Show, Unity Dance Ensemble. Janet dedicates gratitude towards her most influential mentors including: Kevin Maher, Teresa Espinosa, Jayson Wright, JP San Pedro, Desiree Robbins, Tabitha and Napoleon, The Jabbawockeez, Essence, and of course, Funkanometry.
