Robin Blitzblum’s Summary

(At MTVNE)

-MTV Networks Advertising.The first Ad Agency with channels of it's own!

We operate as a full-full-full-service Ad agency. We integrate TV (formats, in program integration), Advertising (all media; instore, print, premiums, packaging, you name it), Internet and Events. MTVNE Advertising produces TV billboarding, commercials, strategic platforms, full flash through the line product introductions and much more...

Our clients want to team up with our powerfull brands (Nick, MTV, TMF, Comedy Central). Or want to profit of our expertise in Kids/Youth communication. Nickelodeon is marketleader in kids entertainment. www.nickelodeon.nl is one of the strongest kids internet platforms in the Netherlands!

We don't charge anything for our strategy, creative work, account handling, traffic etc. We "live" from the exposure costs. Clients pay the production costs (we produce in a very creative, lean and mean way by the way).

But...we also work for Media and Ad agencies. When an Ad agency has a campaign running, we can enrich that campaign with our media support. We are willing to think along and come up with supporting ideas especially targeted at our media audiences.

Want to know more? See some cases?robin.blitzblum@mtvne.com


(After work)
