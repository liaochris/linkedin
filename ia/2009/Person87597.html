Glenn R. Smith’s Summary

I have been in the financial products and services industry since 1995. It has taken me several years to realize that working as an Independent Financial Strategist gives my clients and prospects the best opportunity for success. I am all about helping my clients achieve more with the resources they currently have. We use a variety of strategies and educate our clients so that they understand what we are doing and why. They understand all the various products and options that they have and then they decide what to do. We back up everything that we do from a numerical format - putting various options into a Model and testing the outcome while adjusting various variables.

We are able to do this with Individuals, Families, Businesses, and Charities (Not-for-Profit Organizations)

The end result is that our clients acheive more with their money, do it in less time, have less risk and more enjoyment of their financial resources.

In short, I guess that you could say that we help our clients make "Smart Financial Decisions".
