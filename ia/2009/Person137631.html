Laura Ballay’s Summary

My focus has largely centered on managing and building high-performing design teams. I have found that the success of a User Experience team not only includes building up the team internally and evangelizing User-Centered Design best practices, but is also largely dependent on building strong partnerships with Development, Marketing, Product Management, PMO and Senior Executives.

I enjoy spearheading creative concept development, user research, product development and design strategy. To me, a successful leader is someone who is able to bridge strategy and execution. That is, someone who can not only think fuzzy big picture, but refine it into a high-level vision, and translate this into tactical execution. So I see my role as spanning across the development cycle, and ensuring not only that the vision is solid and clear, but that the execution goes as smoothly as possible.

I've got a definite soft spot for new product development -- incubators, start-ups, innovation teams, R&D and blue sky product design always pique my interest! My entrepreneurial spirit means that I really relish unearthing potential product opportunities/gaps, digging for consumer insights, and I have a knack for ferreting out trends and sleuthing down information.

I bring a strong understanding of UI design principles, interaction design, usability and experience design, with ten years experience in interactive product design. I've worked on websites, print-to-web, handheld devices, kiosks, desktop software applications and voice UI/IVRs.

For recent work samples, please contact me.
