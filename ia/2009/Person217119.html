Shruti Shukla’s Summary

I’m a motivation-driven individual who believes in cheerful disposition under all the circumstances. I have a rich experience of over 6 years in training wherein I have undertaken training for almost all the paradigms of training, be it voice and accent, grammar, behavior, or culture. My Bachelors and Masters in English have helped me in streamlining a lot of modules on not only grammar but also on a lot of other training aspects. I’m an avid reader and have read a variety of literature. Moreover, I have written articles on current affairs for a number of English dailies in New Delhi

I am a behavioral Skills Trainer and have trained on various behavioral aspects including Possibility Thinking, Motivational Skills, Conflict Resolution, Leadership Skills, Peer Competition, Coaching and Mentoring, Team Building, Decision Making, Career Progression, Personality Development, Presentation skills for various big names like Sahara group, various State Governments , BPOs and security forces.

I have also contributed to content development on various behavioral topics. I have streamlined the English grammar manual for training for various organizations I have worked with. Currently I am actively involved in designing and implementation of the Training Manual, Coaching & Feedback on Process improvements, “Train the Trainer” programs, client presentations and development of training modules.
