Growth in the 2B's business portfolio areas remains steady. A strategy of personal and business development and networking, continues to show real returns.

Past experience and skills include:

30 years of project management/running businesses of up to 50 staff, plus sub-contractors.

Bringing together public & private sectors, as the initial interim manager for Bicester Vision. see www.bicestervison.co.uk, where my line managers were, the Chief executive of the local Business Link, & the Chief executive of Cherwell District Council
