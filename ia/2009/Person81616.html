Fred Dempster’s Summary

My career is focused on helping clients succeed. From Business Development to project execution I operate under three guiding principles:

- Collaboration: Listening vs. telling
- Leadership: Professional on-time execution
- Relationship: Being a trusted advisor

Finance Transformation covers a wide spectrum; finance lives horizontally across the entire enterprise. I am also passionate about the revenue cycle, from market offering to cash, and have assisted numerous firms with millions in direct bottom line impact and working capital improvements.

Depth of experience runs from turnaround of distressed firms to helping successful firms move to the next step towards World Class Finance, from process improvement to business process outsourcing.

Industry experience includes consumer packaged goods, manufacturing, high tech, telecommunications, financial services, and resources/energy.
