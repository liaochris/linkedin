Neil Hewitt’s Summary

Neil together with his Business Partner and Wife, Sarah, are Directors of a Management and Training Consultancy, SunFire Ltd.

Neil and Sarah are both experienced Programme and Project Managers, and Master NLP Practitioners. They combine these skills with amazing results by not only introducing new Programmes and Projects into Organisations, they also have the skills and tools to affect lasting change at the Organisational Level around the Beliefs and Values of the Teams / Departments that are affected by the Business Change.

Sarah has specialised in “Everything DiSC” (www.everythingdisc.co.uk) a profiling tool used to develop critical interpersonal business skills in the areas of:
• Sales
• Leadership
• Frontline Management
• Conflict Management
• Teambuilding
• Communication
Sarah is “Train the Trainer” certified, allowing SunFire Ltd to train key HR personnel and private individuals to deliver Everything DiSC profiling.

Neil has also developed “Breakthrough” coaching courses that stretches individuals and Teams to the limits of their own personal beliefs, leading to increased individual performance and bonding between Teams and Departments (www.FireSpirit.co.uk), enabling both individuals and Teams at all levels of the Organisation to realise their true potential.

Together Neil and Sarah have studied under Anthony Robbins, Robert Kiyosaki (Rich Dad, Poor Dad), Keith Cunningham (Keys to the Vault) and Jill Fielding (Secret Millionaire) to hone their skills to the highest standard to ensure that value together with delivery are achieved on time and to budget.
