Sahar Azarabadi’s Summary

BioSpace is globally recognized as the leading provider of web-based resources and information to the life science industry. With industry brands for 20 years BioSpace has helped to accelerate communication and discovery among business and scientific leaders in the biopharmaceutical market. Featuring a loyal online audience of over 1 million unique monthly visitors, BioSpace.com offers an unparalleled distribution channel for recruitment, investment, product, event and other life science industry messages.

BioSpace is the leading career services organization serving the Life Sciences (Pharmaceutical, Biotechnology, Medical Device and Health Industries). Products and services include: Job Boards and Resume Databases for Employers and Seekers, Career Fairs and Events for Employers and Seekers, Industry Portals and Cluster Marketing Campaigns, and News and Information for Industry Professionals.
