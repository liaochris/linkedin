John Bailey’s Summary

John’s strength is designing and implementing the organizational, operational and technological adaptations required to rapidly achieve key, strategic objectives in complex environments during times of substantial change.

Now in his 11th year as an independent consultant and interim manager, John has already been a trusted advisor to blue-chip corporate clients, privately-owned global players and successful start-ups across industry sectors.

John has worked in many parts of the world and thrives in highly dynamic, international working environments. His clients particularly appreciate his hands-on drive and commitment coupled with his humour and empathy.

An American born in 1970 and with family roots in Belgium and Germany, John moves effortlessly between cultures. He is fluent in English, German and Dutch and conversational in French.

John Bailey’s Specialties:

Crisis Management, Strategic Plan Execution, Change Management, Heavy-Duty Programme Management, Driving Organizational and Operational Improvement Initiatives, Marketing Proposition Development & Delivery, Product & Services Management, Coaching of high-talent individuals, Business Plan Review, early-stage / Business Angel investment
