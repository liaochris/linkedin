Roland Tibell’s Summary

Roland is Managing Partner at Tibell & Associates, an independent Advisory, Consulting and Interim Management for Hire firm. The business model is simple and straightforward; sharing the experience and expertise obtained from working in the intersection of technology and financial markets for over 25 years. Of these, Roland spent 16 at NASDAQ OMX and the Stockholm Stock Exchange. Working closely with more than 30 financial marketplaces globally in a period of intense industry transformation has gained Roland a deep understanding of how to adapt exchange operations and businesses to changes in the market, competitive and regulatory environment. This knowledge is gathered not only from large markets and projects but also from startups, emerging markets, and regional collaboration.

• 25 plus years of experience in information technology, organizational and operational efficiency, problem and crises management, project management, business development, and strategy work for the financial services industry globally, with particular focus on securities trading and processing at exchanges and alternative marketplaces.
• More than 16 years of experience of executive management, leadership, change management and the efficiency of large organizations with business critical systems and services.
• Over ten years of experience in negotiations, sales, design, delivery, implementation and operations of large complex system solutions for financial marketplaces on a global scale.

Roland Tibell’s Specialties:

Roland’s approach is to be extremely hands-on and produce practical results that have immediate impact. His strengths include operational efficiency, change & problem management, evaluations, negotiations, business development, and strategy work. The field in which Roland adds most value is in the “sweet-spot” of his experience; the combination of exchange business, change management and Information Technology, including both strategy and operations.
