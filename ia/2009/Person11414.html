Amir Khella (amir@fictiv.com)’s Summary

Amir is an artist, designer, scientist and engineer whose life is about designing and shipping great products. His work delivers innovative, usable and engaging user experience to the web, the desktop and mobile devices. His mission is to help companies create break-through user interfaces and interaction designs.

Amir’s unique strength lies in combining his design skills with his strong technical and academic background, and his experience managing the software life-cycle for small and large companies. This spectrum of skills enables him to wear multiple hats and communicate efficiently with various disciplines.
