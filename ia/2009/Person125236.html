Jyl Walker’s Summary

22 Years Floral Design
12 Years Jewelry Design

As a Jewelry Artist, I recognize that my childhood plays a big part in my current work. I grew up with an imbedded sense of exploration and appreciation for all things natural. Largely a self-taught artist, I am always exploring new techniques, which allows for a broad sense of style and wide range of design possibilities.

Creating fine original pieces that are easy to wear, always elegant, yet versatile enough to flatter all clothing styles! Ageless and timeless... created for comfort, constructed for durability and... heirloom quality.

Jyl Walker’s Specialties:

Elegant Custom Designed Jewelry,
Originality and attention to detail are the hallmarks of my signature designs.
