Ciaran Henry’s Summary

Business-oriented, hands on, visionary professional with 21 year track record in financial technology management. Extensive knowledge of global cash and derivatives markets and associated trading, risk and analytic systems. Strong leadership skills with experience of building and running global teams of 1200+ and cost-conscious management of budgets over $260m. Experience with a broad range of technology areas from infrastructure through to development both in-house and outsourced via off-shore and near shore.

Equally effective in hands on player-coach role as in larger organizations. Looking for intellectual challenge, size of employer firm not important.

Industry authority on markets, instruments, evolution of market structures. Familiar with all asset classes pricing, risk management and trading techniques. Exceptional ability to communicate with traders, clients and senior business management in their own language. Proven track record in Mergers and Aquisitions, including full scope of business integration as well as technology due diligence, best of breed platform selection and error-free implementation under immovable regulatory deadlines.

Sample of technologies used: RDBMS - Sybase, SQL Server, Oracle RAC. Languages - C++, Java, C, Visual Basic, Javascript, AJAX, various XML, C# and .Net. Suse and Red HAt Linux, Microsoft Server technologies, Advanced Excel and Office. Opensource platforms and tools - NeoOffice, Apple Webkit. Paradigms - structured SDLCs and agile/RAD development. Murex, Summit vendor systems. IBM Tivoli monitoring and Enigmatec auto-failover. Custom low-latency and high speed electronic pricing and algorithmic trading technologies. FIX protocol. BEA weblogic, IBM Websphere, Microsoft ASP and ASPX, Active X.
