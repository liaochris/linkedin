Wally Johnson’s Summary

High-energy entrepreneurial executive with strong record of success in transforming both large and small companies through vision, collaboration and mentoring. Mr. Johnson was one of the founding executives in the successful startup/turnaround of EPIC Technologies LLC, joining the company in 2000 as Vice President, Supply Chain Management. Working in close partnership with Jochen Lipp (then COO), they implemented a synchronous flow shop floor layout supported by kanban material replenishment which over the next two years saw inventory and lead-times reduced by more than half while at the same time customer OTD (on time delivery) moved from the mid 80 percent to virtually 100%. Mr. Johnson built a globally competitive supply chain organization which was a key foundation for growing EPIC's business from $30M to $280M over the next seven years. email address -nwallyj@roadrunner.com
