Dritan Malo’s Summary

Dritan is an Associate Broker with Century 21 Tri-Boro Terrace Realty Inc
Also with Century21 Calabrese Realty in New Jersey
He considers himself a home buyer and seller specialist. Dritan deals with corporate relocation and is quite knowledgeable in the particulars of many areas in New York City. He is a Loan officer with Guaranteed Home Mortgage Company and has been trained by the best in the industry. Dritan was born in Tirana, Albania, and has lived in several countries such as Greece, France, & Switzerland. He has been living in the U.S. for the past nine years. No matter what your needs or questions are pertaining to real estate, Dritan has extensive background and expertise needed to work on your side and find you the right home for your needs or help sell your current property.
