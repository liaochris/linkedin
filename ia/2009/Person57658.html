David Happe’s Summary

David Happe is a 20 year veteran of the retail industry, including corporate purchasing experience for Best Buy Company Inc. and Wal-Mart Stores Inc. (Sam's Club). David was the Executive Vice President of Sales and Marketing for SSC Inc., the retail marketing partner of Samsung CCTV in the United States. In addition, he created the Akai television line from concept through product development and distribution, which went from $0 to $200m in sales in 4 years. He was the lead consultant for Amazon.com when Amazon opened their consumer electronics store. He is also the founder and president of U.S. Install, which he sold controlling interest to MBND in March 2008.

David's paralegal education is from St. Cloud State University in Minnesota, where he resides with his wife Lisa and two children. In addition to pro bono legal work, David is available for industry consultation, speeches, and collegiate teaching assignments. Recent engagements include moderating a panel of experts at the International Security Conference (ISC), and serving as an industry consultant/advisor to a major investment research organization.

In early 2009, David concepted and designed a license agreement with NASCAR and JR Motorsports on behalf of Transplant GPS, Inc. out of Rochester, MN. The industry recalibrating GPS featuring Dale Earnhardt Jr. voice navigation and exclusive NASCAR content entered mass production in April 2009.
