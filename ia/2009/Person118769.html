Jonathan Campion’s Specialties:

Translations to and from the Russian language. The fields in which I have most experience are: Ukrainian legislation with regards to Oil and Gas, Land Law and Intellectual Property Protection.

Jonathan Campion’s Experience

  • Translator and English language editor

    (Legal Services industry)

    September 2007 — October 2008 (1 year 2 months)

    My main duties included: proofreading memorandums and letters written by the firm’s lawyers, translating Interfax articles on economic issues and natural resources for distribution to our foreign clients, editing articles written by the firm’s lawyers on Ukrainian legislation, and assisting with the English-language aspects of the firm’s human resources and PR departments.

    After the merger of Volkov and Partners with Sergiy Koziakov and Partners the volume of my work increased: I was responsible for translating the new firm’s website, and all press releases in connection with the merger.

Jonathan Campion’s Interests:

My interests include sport and Russian cinema. I combine three of my hobbies – travelling in the former Soviet Union, writing and photography – in a journal, at www.travelblog.org/Bloggers/Vinovat-Sudarynya. The articles from it, about my experiences of life in Russia and Ukraine, have been used on the travel websites Odyssei (odyssei.com, Poland) and Beaten Paths (beatenpaths.net, England), the football website Inside Futbol (insidefutbol.com, England) and the forum for ‘expats’ in Ukraine (expatua.com).
