Steve Remote’s Summary

Grammy winning recording engineer / producer / mixer specializes in all aspects of location recording and broadcasting plus live and studio production. He works with some of the most exciting and cutting edge artists in the entertainment industry.

Steve Remote’s Specialties:

Audio engineering and consulting services for the broadcast, music recording, film sound, teleproduction audio and live sound reinforcement industries.

Steve Remote’s Experience

  • Owner

    Our mobile units can be configured as per the engineer's request with an "a la carte" style. Our sound trucks can handle 2 to 96 track audio recording with up to 264 inputs for effective location audio recording and production.
