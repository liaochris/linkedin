Bruce Joy’s Summary

VastPark.com
VastPark enables you to build your own 3D worlds and even provide the tools that you can redistribute to your users so they can build their own worlds using your official content. VastPark was built with a vision where there are millions of interactive worlds and where users must be able to enjoy highly interactive worlds which they can influence EASILY.

VastPark focusses on connecting your community and makes the process convenient and fast. Tools include a free 3D world Creator tool and a 3D browser so people can experience the interactive 3D work.

There's many more innovations to come including a content format system that we're going to open up for others to use. Got an online community interested in richer collaboration. Whether it's for entertainment or business, our platform can help you solve the problems ELEGANTLY. Contact us: team [AT] vastpark [DOT] com.
