Dan Whiting’s Summary

I'm a Systems Engineer with expertise in the areas of technical solution development and architecture focusing on Microsoft and NetApp technologies. I have worked with customers both in the public and private sector to develop cost effective solutions for complex IT challenges. As a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE) I have extensive experience in Windows Server 2003 technologies, while specializing collaborative solutions based using Microsoft Exchange and Microsoft Office Sharepoint Server. Coupling these technologies with NetApp's storage platform and data management software, I have built customized solutions encompassing application performance, backup and recovery, as well as disaster recovery.
