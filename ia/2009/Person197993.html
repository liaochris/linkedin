Richard Simtob’s Summary

Entrepreneurship is my passion. Taking ideas and making them into income generating businesses from the age of 14 to today. Always looking for a better way to do something and putting systems in place. Looking at future trends in business. Spends time with other entrepreneurs giving advice, help or assistance when possible. Always learning from each person and experience. Looking for growing Franchise Brands and companies with profitable business models that could grow effectively with franchising.

FranchiseSforSale.com is an franchise education and listing site with detailed financial information on select growing brands. "Your Ultimate Franchise Connection!"

Past: 3 time "Inc. 500" Winner for two different franchise businesses.

Richard Simtob’s Specialties:

Franchise, real estate, development of new concepts and ideas, creating systems and improving overall business strategy. Public speaking, Franchise development consulting and finance are specialties.
