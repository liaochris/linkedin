Mike Kenny’s Summary

At KCI Business Finance we don’t pretend getting the right loan for your enterprise in any environment is easy. It requires time, effort, access to a large network of lenders and someone working on your behalf to ensure they have a deep understanding of your situation.

It’s important you have an expert on your side so that you can successfully secure a loan structured in a way that is appropriate for you. If you need to purchase new equipment, finance your accounts receivable or inventory, raise working capital, acquire or start a company, purchase or develop commercial real estate you can count on us. Make KCI Business Finance a part of your team.

MyBusinessPlans.com is a nationwide firm that is focused on providing a range of affordable business planning and consulting services to startup, small and medium sized businesses.

Our clients are evenly distributed throughout the United States, as well as in various countries around the world and range from start-ups to companies with decades of operating history.

Contact us today for quick review. We look forward to hearing from you!
