Jeri Hird Dutcher’s Summary

I am a Certified Professional Career Coach for executives and professionals in technology fields such as IT, IS, Web Development, E-commerce, and Engineering. My company offers process-oriented approaches to Goal Attainment, Company Research, and Job Search Campaign Strategy.

As a Certified Employment Interview Professional, I teach Interview Preparation and Salary Negotiations.
