maria claudia brigido’s Summary

After more than 10 years at what they still call offline, I've just taken on my hands to start up the digital department at Dentsu's branch in Brazil. That comes from my restlessness and my belief that there's no more space for advertising that does not consider online and digital.
Specially in Brazil, we are living a moment when things are happening fast: technology and resolution and details are not good enough. But this is a time for concepts, thoughts and to start acting.
And that is what I can bring from the huge experience from major agencies and accounts I've worked with. To think the online and the offline together, as one, as mkt strategy, is key to enter this new moment of "innovate or die", and "low cost or no campaign". That's the basis for good concepts and great fun ideas to survive.
But, hey, that's just me typing.
