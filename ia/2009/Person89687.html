Gregor Rohrig’s Summary

Since 2008 I have joined the Avusa Media Innovation Lab (iLab) as the New Media Specialist, working with a dynamic team, implementing, conceptualizing and project managing commercial online and mobile products.

Previously I was part of developing and implementing the first, and largest, daily news multimedia portal in South Africa in conjunction to The Times newspaper. Direct experience in multimedia production, planning, development, management.

Consulting on: web strategies, online product development, audio / visual multimedia equipment, social media integration, online media convergence, new media technologies.
