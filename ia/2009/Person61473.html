Denise Montgomery’s Summary

I am a partner in managing The Society of Decorating Professionals, which is an international organization of entrepreneurial decorators. The SDP offers certification, networking and education opportunities to our members and the public. Our certified trainers offer classes nation-wide that equip new decorators to establish independent businesses, without the expense of franchise buy-in or fees.

I am also the owner of Mais Oui! Redesign, LLC, an interior decorating firm that specializes in interior redesign (often called one day decorating), home staging and professional organizing. My goal is to bring beauty and order to my clients' homes so that my clients can say "Mais Oui! or Yes, of Course! ... This is the way I've always dreamt my house would look!"

My training division, Mais Oui! RedesignU, offers training seminars that lead to certification from the Society of Decorating Professionals in both interior redesign and home staging. Graduates of my 5-day programs leave with the confidence and skills that they need to start their own redesign and/or staging businesses.

I also offer Redesign WordCoach writing and editing services to decorators who need help finding their "written voice" to market and describe their services.

Additional consulting services include small business marketing and start up.
