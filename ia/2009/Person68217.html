Don Dunbar’s Summary

The goal of Building Material Services is to provide consulting services to the Kitchen and Bath industry, with emphasis on installing cabinet dealers.

BMS has extensive experience operating building material contracting companies. We will use that experience to complete a thorough analysis of your building supply chain business operation and quickly identify solutions and strategies that result in increased profitability.

We can assist your business with:
▪ Increased Operational Efficiency
▪ New Product Development
▪ Business Diversifications
▪ Business Start-ups
▪ Geographical Expansion
▪ Ownership Transition

Strengths:
- Development and implementation of “Powerful Propositions” that leverage business strengths into geometrically increasing profits.

- Extensive experience in sales, marketing, strategic planning, financial, operations management of multiple contracting locations and manufacturing.

Career highlights:
- Took a new cabinet business from zero annual revenue to $300M+ in 6 years.

- Played the key role in the aquisition of Gale Industries by a Masco, a Fortune 200 company.

- Developed and implemented material and labor procedures that control hundreds of contracting locations.
