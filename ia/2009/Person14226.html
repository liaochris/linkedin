Andy Kirby’s Summary

I've worked on sales, inventory, warehousing, stock control and integration projects for manufacturing, retail and distribution companies world-wide.

I've also worked in all IT project phases (business and systems analysis, design, programming, testing, implementation and support) for a range of ERP systems as well as performing project and team management duties. Most of this work has been using IBM's iSeries computer using ILE programming to enhance ERP systems and enable communication with other systems.

I think that the future of the IT industry lies in the SaaS model, which will gain wider acceptance as the cost and manpower savings this model provides businesses will become clearer to more business users over time.
