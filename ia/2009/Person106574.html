John Bliss’s Summary

In 1996, I graduated from Lehigh University with a BA (Psychology) and minors in Computer Science, Cognitive Science (A.I.), and Technical Theatre.

I returned to my home town of York, PA and was hired as Technical and Customer Support Manager for a small, local ISP: Cyberia Communications. Eventually, the ISP roll-up swept through and Cyberia was purchased by a regional ISP (D&E SuperNet) that was in turn purchased by a national ISP (OneMain.com) that was eventually purchased by EarthLink. During all of this, my job shifted toward Web development and database design and administration.

In 2000, I moved to Washington, DC to work for Doceus (www.doceus.com) developing database-driven Web applications for a diverse set of clients including non-profits, associations, emerging dot-coms, and traditional companies.

In 2005, I moved to Houston, TX to work for BMT Designers & Planners (www.dandp.com) developing database-driven Web applications for a diverse set of clients including energy-sector, US government, US military, and traditional companies. At D&P, I was in charge of I.T. in the Gulf-coast region, including business development and technical sales.

In 2008, I became Senior Project Manager and Senior Database Administrator at imwave.com, Affiliate Marketing & Pay-Per-Click Search Engine Advertising Experts. I am refining my skills every day and really love my job.
