Scott Darrow’s Summary

A highly experienced Technology Executive who has provided leadership in a broad range of roles over the past 25 years, including many years building development organisations in Southeast Asia.

Balancing technical knowledge with a strong understanding of business – both the operational requirements, and the managerial needs of business has given me a long list of success stories in various industry sectors including supply chain, distribution, manufacturing, wholesale, retail, warehousing, contract logistics, data centers, banking, food and beverage, automotive, pharmaceutical, hospitality and e-commerce.

With 17 years in BPO industry in Asia including both experience building and operating outsourced operations I am able to effectively work hand in hand with senior management, both local and international to get problems solved.

Having partnered and run my own businesses, and working both for, and with, small companies and large corporations I have borne ultimate responsibility for technology aspects of the organizations for which I have worked. My background as the author of two Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Supply Chain Management (SCM) packaged solutions gives me an extensive knowledge of Software Development life cycle and related management activities from pre-sales to customer acceptance.

In more recent times, my focus has been on deployment of high quality browser based applications, over the Internet using tools and technology to generating high performance e-commerce websites.
