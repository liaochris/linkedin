Kasper Krogh’s Summary

Focus is on client management and consultancy regarding strategy and business solutions. The services rendered mainly refer to communication in general (visual identity, branding/marketing, media and conceptualization) in an online setting, which means that a comfortable knowledge of software development aspects (both front- and backend) is also a factor.

Extensive experience with online product development, service- and application development based on approximately 15-20 VC-funded project launches through 2007-09. These services also included applications and business solutions developed for various Fortune 500's.
