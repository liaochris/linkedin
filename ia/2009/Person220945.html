Rachel Toy nee Scanlon’s Summary

Rachel has over 15 years experience in the Graphic Design industry and has been working freelance for 5 years. She works regularly with other design partners to provide strong Graphic Design projects with purpose and intent for many satisfied clients. Specialising in Corporate Identity and Branding, she will aim to ensure that all of the work her clients come to her to design, will have a unique and creative image, with a consistent look and feel across all the company's literature.

Spirit Creative's clients range from small start ups, to more established businesses, marketing agencies, web developers and larger corporate clients. Rachel can provide a full graphic design agency service to your business, or work alongside it as your freelance graphic designer.

Skillset includes: Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Fireworks, CorelDraw etc

Rachel Toy nee Scanlon’s Specialties:

Logo Design, Business Cards, Flyers, Leaflets, Brochures, Website Design, Instruction Manuals, Catalogues, Exhibition Graphics, Posters, Newsletters, E-shots, Corporate Identity, Business Stationery
