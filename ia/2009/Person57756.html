David Silverman’s Summary

dsilverman@securityfirstmf.com

My 11+ years in the mortgage industry, and 8 years of investing in residential real estate in Boston provide me a unique perspective on financing.

MORTGAGE ADVISING

My expertise is in showing you via state-of-the-art tools, the best decision for loan, rate, term, cost, and payment. I evaluate the tax consequences, and recommend clients to tax attorneys, CPAs, financial planners, and investment advisors when appropriate. I can implement techniques to improve your FICO score so you can achieve more favorable financing terms.

At Security First Mortgage Funding, our organization originates, approves, closes, and funds your loan. We have access to the top national lenders, and that satisfies all needs including A paper, Jumbo, Super Jumbo, Alt-A, Construction, Commercial, FHA, and Hard-Money loans.
