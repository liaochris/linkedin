Frank Ball’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur sharing a lifetime of successful leadership. Currently helping to shepard a highly successful manufacturing firm, Sunrise MFG, to the next level. This is an exciting long-term venture destined for prosperity even in today's economic times. This growth is due to innovative solutions which make shippers much more cost effective.

Previously a semi-retired CEO advisor, who mentors management professionals. Working with Presidents / Chief Decision Makers, teaches creative innovation techniques, frontline sales methodologies, modern marketing principles, leadership development and financial disciplines. Imparts systemization, accountability, and statistical process controls.

Building on degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Marketing Management, grew seven businesses within the retail, industrial, distribution and franchise sectors. Continues to invest in, and manage, multi- and single-family properties along with multiple business interests.

Cherishes time with family. Enjoys investing in philanthropic activities, especially those that support our community and youth. Very involved with Boys Scouts of America.

Current Concentrations: Lean Manufacturing, Alternative Energy, Food Production/Processing, Life Sciences, Technology, Transportation & Distribution, Cargo Containment & Safety, Corrugated Cardboard & Dunnage.

Goal: Create legacy of innovative companies which serve the community to produce a better quality of life.
