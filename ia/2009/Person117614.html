John Crowther’s Summary

Experienced senior executive with a global multi-function and general management background. 11+ years CIO experience with Fortune 200 and FTSE 100 companies. Significant results and experience in global business transformation and organizational change management. Executive roles in manufacturing, service, and technology companies including responsibility for global logistics, operations, distribution, supply chain, finance, marketing and information technology. Board and advisory board experience with technology and telecommunications companies.

John Crowther’s Specialties:

Global organizational transformation; change management; large scale IT outsourcing; extensive 'offshore' experience - captive and 3rd party; global ERP implementations; business process re-engineering, shared services and business process outsourcing; senior-level talent acquisition and development; developing and managing international operations - emphasis on India, China and Brazil; M&A integrations.
