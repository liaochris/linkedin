Rebekah King’s Summary

Rebekah King is what happens when an artist and a businessman marry and have kids. Growing up with the marketing flair and creative streak from her mother’s side, and taking it to job-sites with her dad as early as 8 yrs old, Rebekah's Multiple Personality Disorder has lead her down a very unique career path. From TV & Radio to E -commerce and Print media, she's worked in a variety of industries – always in a marketing role. In her consulting projects she’s seen a consistent 200% growth (over 6-12 months) via implementation of more “non-traditional” marketing programs, and now uses that to fuel the phenomenal growth of www.rebekahking.com and www.printlessmarketing.com. To date the real joy for Rebekah comes when she gets to support her customers as they work to print less, reach more
Also a firm believer in the value of community, Rebekah works with several charities in Orange County, CA. She is the founder of the Southern California Health & Beauty Association (www.myschb.com) and serves in several local association leadership roles. Rebekah is often a guest speaker at the Irvine Chamber as well as RBN, and writes a blog sharing her knowledge with the community. She hopes through this and her work with www.elihome.org to learn the skills it will take to continue her personal mission: the www.ranchworks.com dream - providing a safe place for kids to be kids while learning to deal with a myriad of physical and psychological challenges.
