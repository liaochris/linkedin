Hildi Borkowski’s Summary

Hildi Borkowski, a lifelong New Jersey resident, began taking photographs as a hobby over thirty years ago. Since then, this hobby has evolved into her passion. Having been self-taught, her photography has realized great personal satisfaction. She keeps abreast of new techniques by attending seminars and courses offered in such places as The New York Botanical Gardens and other educational institutions.

Photography has enabled her to experience life from a new perspective, realizing that peace can only come from within oneself. She has found the natural beauty of landscapes and nature to be peaceful and comforting, and to hold the most inspiration. From oceans to mountains and everything in between, she is compelled to capture those images. She has been known be intrigued by nature’s smallest creatures, from the intricate designs of spider webs, to nested hatchlings huddled together waiting for their next meal. Although inspiration comes from extensive travels, she finds most of her subjects in unexpected places close to home.

She is a regular contributor to www.Moonbaby.biz, an I-Literary Magazine, with her own column entitled “Picture This”.

Hildi Borkowski’s Specialties:

Digital photography of nature, landscapes, flowers and portraits.

Hildi Borkowski’s Experience

  • Owner

    hbphotography, L.L.C.

    (Photography industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    I started my company in 2008 to enable me to share my passion for art in nature. Anyone can take a picture, but my expertise is marrying the beauty of nature with art and emotion.

    Art is subjective, just as beauty is subjective. Each person finds art and/or beauty in different things. It is inherently different in everyone. When I walk along a pristine beach or through a shaded path, every sense works in harmony with each other. The solitude brings peace. Peace enables me to appreciate the profound beauty found only in nature. Such beauty must and does appeal to everyone on some level of consciousness.

    We are very fortunate to live in an environment that is ever changing on its own accord. No two moments are alike; each different in content and attitude. Each day is different from the one before. Each season is different from one year to the next. The basic concept of each season remains the same from year to year, yet none are identical. Nature governs these changes.

    If we recognize the beauty that surrounds us all, nature is rejuvenating. If we respect our ecosystem, nature is a healing power. If we abuse our natural world, nature can become a devastating force. Nature is a force to be respected.

    Photography has enabled me to accept and appreciate every aspect of the natural world. It has become an extension of my consciousness. Experiencing the juxtaposition of warm sunlight on a brisk autumn day while the colors of the season cast a warm glow on fading green lawns and majestic trees begs me, the photographer, to capture the serenity of that moment on film. When this occurs, a true masterpiece has been born. It is my goal to share the beauty of nature and respect for our environment through photographic images whose sole purpose is to replace confusion and disorder with tranquility and solace.
