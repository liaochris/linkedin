Daniel Earley’s Summary

I have travelled throughout Asia and Europe sourcing parts and establishing manufacturer relationships. I have a strong background in International Supply Chain, Distribution and Material Handling Operations, E-Commerce, Reverse Logistics, Start-Ups and Process/Flow improvement.

I have experience with the following:

P&L / Supply Chain / Cost Containment / Business Intelligence / ISO / Six Sigma / Training / Lean / eCommerce / International / 3PL / Inventory Productivity / Warehouse / Labor Relations

Led all aspects of operations, global supply chain management, labor management, product line rollout, pricing strategy, and manufacturer negotiations for small and large public companies. Developed multi-divisional operations and contained costs in a growing business while increasing customer satisfaction and profitability. Extensive knowlege of Asian Manufacturers.
