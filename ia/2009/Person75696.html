Ernie Ferguson’s Summary

I sell business solutions. While a review of my resume will show a variety of companies with a variety of products the reason I was successful at each firm was due to my focus on understanding how my products could help my clients solve their business problems. My ability to articulate these solutions is the reason why busy executives made time on their calendars to meet with me and wny they would later allocate millions in their budgets to acquire the products I had brought to their notice.

Executives buy solutions. They don't buy products. That is always my approach regardless of what I'm selling.

My deep and wide background in many of the core vertical industries in the world means that I can talk to almost any senior executive in their language whether their company is in telecommunications, finance, oil and gas exploration/production, or travel/hospitality.

My two Bachelor degrees including a BA with a double major in Creative Writing/Spanish and a BS in Computer Information Systems should indicate that I'm not just all about technology. I'm well rounded and have superior written and verbal communication skills.

My goal is to expand opportunities and help my organization cross over the horizon. There is always more out there than what we see today. My value is that I can drive and expand the revenues my company derives from its highest value clients.
