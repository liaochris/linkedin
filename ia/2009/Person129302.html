Ivor Kellock’s Summary

Kellock Wealth Management provides a bespoke, professional & modern approach to sustaining, growing and protecting long-term family wealth.

A Combination of business consultancy, mentoring, taxation advice*, legal services*, investment management, cash management*, raising finance*, insurance & estate planning.

We will take the time to understand how your values, aspirations, personal & business finances connect with the global economy, trends, & political sentiment.

Our relationship with you is at the heart of our belief that your personal wealth can be managed more effectively by closely working together.
