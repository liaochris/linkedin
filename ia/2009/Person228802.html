Tami Clark’s Summary

Accomplished senior brand marketing executive with successful history in driving top and bottom lines of the P & L: launching consumer brands, delivering critical revenue and profit gains, strengthening organizations and improving brand trust in customer-centric consumer and business-to-business categories. Known as a creative strategist with a proven ability to successfully identify, analyze, launch and manage brands in both domestic and international markets.
