Heidi Yeadon’s Summary

I have a B.S. in Early Childhood Education. I have worked as an owner and director of a childcare center and later opened my own photography business primarily photographing portraits on location and weddings throughout New England. I have recently become accredited through Haverhill Institute as a home stager and opened Tower Hill Interiors in May of 2009 where I can combine my love of home decorating, my
creativity as a photographer, my interest in real estate and architecture with my business, marketing and sales expertise.

Heidi Yeadon’s Specialties:

I have had commercial and outside salesexperience, marketing and business development training. The skills I have developed as a photographer and my love of architecture, interior design and decorating have afforded me the opportunity to open my own business as a home stager. .
