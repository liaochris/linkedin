Jim Sleeman’s Summary

I am an Information Technology professional with 25+ years of experience. It includes mainframe and client server application development and support, database design and support and enterprise data architecture. I am especially strong in the architecture and planning of applications and databases, problem solving and debugging.

For the last 10 years I have been working as a DBA on Sybase and Microsoft SQL Servers and Sybase Replication Server. This involved performance monitoring and tuning of the servers and stored procedures, triggers and view, server and database backups, developing, testing and documenting database disaster recovery, creation and maintenance of standby servers and the usually object and data migrations.
