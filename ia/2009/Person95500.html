Holly Storm’s Summary

VMIX provides Software as a Service (SaaS) video platform solutions for websites and publishers looking to maximize audience engagement and monetization of premium in-house produced, user-generated, and syndicated content. Through APIs, hosted templates/widgets, and a unique human review process VMIX has become the trusted partner of Tribune Company, McClatchy Company, Lee Enterprises, LIN Broadcasting, Warner Bros/Telepictures, Raycom Broadcasting and Post-Newsweek Stations.
