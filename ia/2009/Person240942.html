Valerie Lazutina’s Summary

• Goldsmithing – Excellent attention to detail, soldering techniques, symmetry and miniature work

• Jewellery Repair – Quick and precise ring sizing, reshanking, retipping, claw tightening and precision soldering

• Casting Cleanup – Fast, high quality finishing, problem solving; pit burnishing, pit filling with solder, determining whether the piece is salvageable

• Gem Setting – Proficient in channel setting, experienced in prong, bezel, princess, marquise and gypsy settings

• Forming Techniques – Considered and recognized to be exceptional in the use of fold forming techniques to create innovative process driven designs; Fold forming techniques: Rueger, score and bend fold, t-fold, rolled fold

• Decorative Techniques – Proven to create unique metal contrasts and patterns with the use of marriage of metal inlay, roller inlay, cold inlay, roller printing and twisted wire patterns

• Jewellery Design & Rendering – Competent to develop new design ideas and render them in three dimensional view

• Gemology – Knowledge of varieties of stones and their properties for repair purposes

• Professional attitude and readily accept challenges,

• Patient, hard working, persistent, problem solving, punctual, trustworthy and friendly.

• Excellent customer service skills
