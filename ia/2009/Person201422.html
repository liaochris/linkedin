Robin Kirchhoffer’s Summary

Thanks to numerous work experiences as part of international and multicultural teams (mainly in Europe, Africa and Asia), I acquired a broad vision of business as well as a true open-mindness. In the meantime, studying in 4 different countries gave me the opportunity to develop an international expertise in Marketing, Trade and Strategic Management.

After my graduation in July 2008 from both ESCE Paris and Birmingham City University, I started last September an Advanced Master in Strategy and Management of International Business (SMIB or SIAI) at ESSEC Business School. This Master, taught both in Paris (September to December) and Singapore (January to April), provides its participants with a first-class education in Strategic Management.

Starting in May 2009 (available from the 4th of May), I will carry out a final internship. I am interested in consulting agencies, especially for sustainable development, but remain attentive to all opportunities: business development, product/brand/project management, international trade, etc.
