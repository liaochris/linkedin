Eugene Frank Westall’s Summary

Before I start, feel free to email me with any questions you think I can help to answer(frankthetank@unforgivenwar.com) . Being an "old guy" I have experienced being involved in the software/internet industry for the last 26 years, all in either start up technology or marketing companies.

In the software side my focus has been on service and consumer oriented products. I previously helped to found two software publishing companies(Fifth Generation - FastBack Software and DTG Inc - System Sleuth), created a service marketing company(Strategic Alliance Partners) who's clients were major software publishing companies(e.g. Mindscape, Activision) and 2 internet companies that grew from 5K members to over 2.5 million within 18 months.

I am the founder of a small internet mmorpg network, which includes www.unforgivenwar.com, contractedassassin.com, mafiachaos.com and mmofury.com . In addition I manage a consulting, marketing and development service (Nvinium) based in California with offices in the Philippines.
