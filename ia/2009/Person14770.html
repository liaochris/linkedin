Andrew Sokol’s Summary

Andrew Sokol's international resume includes consulting for management and staff of Nike (Thailand); Yamaha (Italy); Oishii Foods (Japan); Siemens (Germany and Italy); Kinowelt Motion Pictures (Germany); Bristol/Myers (Thailand); The Crossroads School (Japan); and others.

In America, Sokol has worked on campaigns for Ninetndo, Bally's Fitness Centers, Banana Boat Skin Care Products, and a diverse range of industries including: automotive; health and fitness; hotel; government; and more.

Along the way he’s won awards and recognition for writing and creative direction.

Sokol began his career after earning a degree in Radio/TV/Film production. His skills as a writer led to his being hired as writer for several publications in Southern California. His work captured the attention of local businesses and he soon found himself with a growing list of clients.

Working with these small companies, Sokol began offering
suggestions for strategic campaigns. When his ideas proved
effective, his client list quickly grew. This led to the founding of his first advertising agency in 1982.

Though Sokol has since worked for some of the world’s largest advertising agencies including J. Walter Thompson and Chiat/Day, he now dedicates himself to his first love; helping small to mid-size businesses succeed.

“When you think about it,” he says, “huge companies can afford advertising agencies, public relations firms, even entire in-house marketing departments. But who’s there to help smaller businesses succeed? For me, after years of helping large companies get larger, I now find great personal satisfaction helping small companies grow to become the large companies of tomorrow.”

Sokol currently resides in Los Angeles where he continues his
consulting practice. He is also a writer and sought after public
speaker on the subject of business and marketing strategy. He also publishes a marketing information website at
http://www.Marketing-Resources-Center.com
