Anthony Mok’s Summary

I've 10 years of experience in organisational excellence & transformation. I'm the founder & Principal Consultant for the MINDEF Innovation & Transformation Office with the Ministry of Defence (MINDEF) from 2001 to 07, & was instrumental in creating her Innovation Framework & Pipeline, & Jumpstart, the change management programme that brought creativity, innovation & entrepreneurship to the Ministry & Singapore Armed Forces. These initiatives have attracted the interest of the National Library Board, Prime Minister Office, SPRING Singapore, Civil Service College, & ChervonTexaco.

Please visit http://www.mindef.gov.sg/innovation/ & http://xxiaohao.blogspot.com/search/label/Credentials for my contribution.

Through the years, I have developed competencies and skills in creating interventions that enable change, like the MINDEF iGame, iThink (http://www.innovationtools.com/PDF/Mass_Ideation_Process.pdf) & iDive, designing consultation & facilitation tools & processes, & delivering these as organisational interventions to obtain intended outcomes.

I hold a Bachelor Degree in Business Administration with specialisations in operational management & logistics with the National University of Singapore. Currently, I'm pursuing the Masters in Education in Leader, Policy & Change with Monash University & PhD with University of Western Australia. I'm a member of the International Association of Facilitators & preparing to be a certified professional facilitator in 2008. Besides these, I hold the Graduate Diplomas & Diplomas with the Institute of Management Consultant (Singapore), Chartered Institute of Marketing (UK), Association of Business Executives (UK), Institute of Administrative Management (UK).

Some of my clients include Roundtable of Sustainable Palm Oil, Ministry of Defence, National Health Care Group, Ministry of Education, Ministry of Law, Land and Transport Authority of Singapore, National Community Leadership Institute, & Gillette Asia Pacific.

Anthony Mok’s Specialties:

I've in depth knowledge & experience in creativity, innovation & entrepreneurship to do talks & deliver experiential based programmes for different levels of the organisation.

Besides holding a Level 3 Performance Coaching certificate with the Singapore Sports Council, I'm also an accredited trainer & administrator for DiSC, MBTI, Margerison-McCann's Team Management, Linking Skills, Team Performance, Window On Work Values, & QO2 profiles. By Jun 09, I'll be certified for ACTA.
