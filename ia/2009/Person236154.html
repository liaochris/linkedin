Tom Murphy’s Summary

Tom Murphy, President, Spenser and Associates, a company providing national consultation and coaching, has been involved in mental health, social work, human services and counseling for over 30 years. He was actively involved in all aspects of EAP client service, including national, regional and local account management.
In addition, Mr. Murphy has conducted consultation and training across the U.S. to audiences in both the public and private sector, on a wide range of topics. He has a Master's degree in Counseling and Human Development, is a Certified Employee Assistance Professional by the Employee Assistance Professionals Association, a Nationally Certified Counselor by the National Board of Clinical Counselors, a Licensed Mental Health Counselor by the State of Florida. He is a member of the Employee Assistance Professionals Association (EAPA, President, Treasurer, Chesapeake Chapter, MD), and Diplomate Staus in Professional Coaching, International Academy of Behavioral Medicine, Counseling and Psychotherapy, Inc.
