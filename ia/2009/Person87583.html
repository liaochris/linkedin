Glenn Myers’s Summary

Glenn is responsible for the day to day management of MDE, including coordination of client service, client development, support, technology and operations, and administration. He also works closely with the firm’s CEO on strategic matters. In addition, as part of his role as a member of the firm’s Investment Committee, Glenn is responsible for operational due diligence and approval of the ability for investment managers to implement strategies without error or fraud, manage growth, etc.

Glenn’s background as a financial analyst and wealth strategist, combined with his proven ability to structure and manage a technology-oriented, client-service organization help ensure that MDE’s clients receive only the highest quality services from the firm.

In addition to his other responsibilities, Glenn maintains the important responsibility of overseeing MDE’s SEC compliance as Chief Compliance Officer. In this role, he develops, implements, and monitors adherence to a series of strict policies and procedures to ensure that MDE’s services are provided within SEC regulations.

Glenn's innate view of technology, along with his intuitive understanding of finance and investment analysis, led to his role as co-creator of The Option OptimizerTM - a revolutionary tool for employee stock option education and analysis. This innovative application was honored as one of Human Resource Executive Magazine's Top 10 Products of the Year upon its launch.

Glenn received his J.D. from Rutgers School of Law and is a member of the New Jersey Bar. He graduated cum laude with a B.S. in Finance and a minor in Computer Science from Rutgers College and Rutgers School of Business where he was a member of the Golden Key National Honor Society.
