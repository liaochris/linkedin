Carra Riley, CRB, CRS, GRI’s Summary

*30 years in Real Estate Sales. Many sales awards as a top producer in Colorado, Hawaii and Arizona.

* Teaching experience with the GRI, Graduate Realtors Institute, program for the Colorado Association of Realtors.

* Speaker at State Real Estate Association Conventions in Colorado and New Mexico.

* Past small business owner of two retail stores. Colorado's Incredible Machine in Estes Park, Colorado and Tahoe's Incredible Machine in Lake Tahoe, California.

*Developer of the Golden Rule web sites for Arizona and Hawaii. www.GoldenRuleAZ.com and www.GodlenRuleMaui.com

Mission Statement:

To help people, one at a time, with heartfelt joy and humor.

Vision Statements:

My vision is to help mankind by sharing my experiences and possibly save or relieve individuals from unnecessary "pits" in the stomach. I will attempt to help stimulate behavior that makes the world a better place by understanding people are different and by walking in their shoes, expressing respect and love for who they are without being judgmental. The vision is for peace and balance within ourselves and then the world. It all starts one neighbor at a time.

Carra Riley, CRB, CRS, GRI’s Specialties:

Real Estate consulting. Carra identifies 3 agents in any State to represent a purchase or sale, client selects agent from 3 nominees. Carra stays connected as consultant through process bringing 30 years experience to the transaction making it easier & faster for both the client & Real Estate agent

Workshops to reinvent yourself, catering to small business owners and individuals. Goal Setting and balance programs in an outdoor environment including trips to Sedona and the Grand Canyon.
