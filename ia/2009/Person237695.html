Matthew Wall’s Summary

MDW Consulting Inc. is an independent consulting firm that specializes in providing transfer pricing services.

What is transfer pricing?

Transfer pricing involves setting a price for inter-company transactions between business units, divisions or entities ("related parties") within a company.

Why does it matter?

Profits are shifted if the price is too high, or too low, often resulting in a material error in the financial statements for one or more related parties.

Who cares?

Management cares about making the wrong decision based on misstated financials. This has broader implications for everyone - management, employees, directors, shareholders, creditors, tax authorities and others.

The tax authorities care if profits are shifted to related parties in different countries, which explains why transfer pricing is the single largest issue under audit by the Canada Revenue Agency and tax authorities in other countries.

Service Team

Matthew Wall is the lead practitioner, has specialized in transfer pricing since 1996, and combines his skills as a C.A. and Chartered Business Valuator to advise on related party transactions. When needed, Matthew will involve transfer pricing professionals in other countries to ensure each project balances the transfer pricing rules in Canada and all of a client's international operations.
