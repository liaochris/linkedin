Nehemia Kramer’s Summary

I have close to 15 years of experience in the fields of international finance, securities, real estate, politics and law, including six years as the founder, CEO and mortgage director of a real estate agency and development company. I enjoy and am skilled in communicating with and understanding the unique requirements of clients from many diverse cultures. My interest in real estate developed from my involvement in the purchase, renovation and sale of distressed Russian factories at the beginning of 1995 following my education in history (followed later by the study of law and finance). I recently returned to New York after living in London for 6 years.

I relate well to my clients because of my distinctive international experience. I have lived in the Middle East, Eastern & Western Europe and North America and am fluent in several languages, including Russian, Lithuanian and Ukrainian and also basic Arabic, Japanese and German. As a result, I have a vast network of international contacts that are prominent in business and government in many countries. I continue to invest personally in properties around the world and am very familiar with the unique challenges and opportunities foreign investors face. I am well versed in finding solutions to obstacles and in managing a team so that my clients can comfortably rely on my commitment to them. My family's involvement in real estate law also gives me insight into the field of commercial and residential development and financing.
