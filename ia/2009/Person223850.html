##

I joined enCircle Media in October as Sales Director/West serving as a senior level business development role but the position was cut in April. Company is based in Irvine.

Further details are in my job summary below.

I have 20+ years experience and success in sales and marketing and have sold at the local, regional, national and international level with business travel to Asia.

I started my career in Seattle at the Puget Sound Business Journal, a weekly business publication, and later returned as the Director of Sales and Marketing for five years and oversaw a staff of 12.

I have also sold and managed locally in Los Angeles and Orange County.

Sales includes eight years at USA Today selling at the national and international level out of the the Los Angeles office before my move back to Seattle.

I returned to California in 2003 and reside in Orange County.

I welcome being contacted via Linked In or through my e-mail addresses or cell phone number listed above.

Steve Fawthrop’s Specialties:
