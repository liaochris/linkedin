Jeff Raab’s Summary

My goal in life is to become a better "connector". A connector of people, resources and ideas. I'm open to any form of contact, so reach out to me if interested and I will help in any way I can.

Detroit Trading Exchange is the single largest automotive lead marketplace currently trading more than 400,000 leads every month. Joining DTX brings me back to my internet roots - focusing on lead generation/customer acquisition and being reunited with some of the smartest colleagues and friends from StoneAge.com. If you're involved in automotive lead generation (new car or finance) or you're thinking of getting involved - get in touch with me - I can really help you out.

Prior to founding SearchSandbox, I worked directly for a number of pioneering internet companies including StoneAge.com (Automotive), Car.com (Automotive), Autobytel.com (Automotive), HomeRoute.com (Real Estate), DebtXS.com (Finance) and LiveDeal.com (Classifieds).

I have had the privilege of working for a number of successful entrepreneurs, an Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Award Winner (Great Lakes Region/Technology) and two Inc.500 Companies.

I'm still actively involved in Search Marketing, both paid and organic. If you need some help - maybe a referral to someone to help with SEO - or if you're looking to get started in pay per click advertising - get in touch with me.
