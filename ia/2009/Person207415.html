Sally Smith’s Summary

I have worked in online for seven years, both client-side for a charity and agency-side for the OTHER media. I offer a unique blend of copywriting ability and information architecture skills, the two complementing each other to create more user-friendly and intuitive sites that are based on the needs of real users.

As an IA and UX I've conducted contextual user research and content audits; created user personas and scenarios; mapped user journeys through content, identified cross-linking opportuntities and created intuitive site/navigation structures that are user-friendly, accessible and flexible.

In my life as a copywriter, I have written and optimised body content and META data for large-scale websites including charity, Cats Protection; language school, International House London and theatre-ticketing agency, Abbey Box Office. My content is based on sound research, using Google Analytics to identify trends, content gaps and opportunities. I have also written Requests for Tender as a client-side project manager and responded to these documents agency-side.

Personally, I am a committed team player who is able to lead and motivate people. I write to tight deadlines and extremely high specifications; being dedicated, passionate and communicative with a skill for understanding the needs of real people. My academic record is strong and the publication of my MA Dissertation is testament to what I achieved at university.

In summary, my goal is to improve usability wherever possible and change people's mentalities towards it. I want to make the lives of users easier and as a result improve the KPIs and ROIs of companies who wisely put the needs of their users first. Too often websites frustrate and confuse users, thus having the opportunity to shape a refreshing experience online is always an exciting prospect for me as an Information Architect, UX and copywriter!
