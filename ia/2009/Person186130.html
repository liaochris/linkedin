Peter Steingröver’s Summary

QUASAR B.V. is founded in 1990.

Seamless integration of business, processes and systems were the main goals in the development of a standard but very flexible software package in the early 90's.

This approach not only resulted in satisfied customers but also in the opportunity to be successful in challenging permanent jobs in complex and fast-changing environments.

The accumulated experience, together with a strong business focus and a pragmatic approach, can be valuable in any business environment, small or complex.

“ICT is an instrument, not a target on its own”
