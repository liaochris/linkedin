Brock Butler’s Summary

“The true measure of a man is how he treats someone who can do him absolutely no harm.” – Samuel Johnson

Dual degrees in Marketing Management and Behavioral Psychology give Brock a perspective on business that is both innovative and analytical, and have contributed to a unique record of success as an IBM sales executive, software entrepreneur, business development executive, and Internet demand creation & prospect development coach.

His knowledge and insight into changing sales and marketing trends, and his passion for new ideas in Internet-based marketing and sales, are matched with an aggressive results-oriented style and a forward-thinking problem solving ability.

Mission - Brock coaches mid-tier Business Solution Providers (companies who sell complex solutions to other businesses) how to use the Internet to create demand, build relationships, and develop prospects into active sales opportunities. He delivers practical solutions to:
a. Understand changes in the market, changes in how your customer buys, and new practical Internet strategies to directly improve your sales pipeline and revenue.
b. Strategically Assess which strategies can most easily be applied to your marketing-to-sales process to yield the greatest increase in sales pipeline and revenue.
c. Execute simple, low-cost, best-practices email and Internet processes to create demand, build relationships, and develop prospects into active sales opportunities.

Values - Brock believes that the Internet is evolving into a strong social fabric that rewards honesty, transparency and respect. It is all about building relationships and giving more than you get. Brock follows these principles in all his affairs.

Brock Butler’s Specialties:

Psychology of Selling, Structured Sales Methodologies & Automation, Enterprise Resource Planning, Vertical Marketing, Customer Relationship Management, Interactive e-Marketing, Web 2.0 Social Networks and Blogs, Business Process Modeling.
