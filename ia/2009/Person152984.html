Mark Williams’s Summary

Executive Talent Network (ETN) is a recruiting and specialist recruitment training company with two key products.
* ETN Training * Specialist training for Recruitment Consultants. We deliver specialist open and in-house recruitment training courses and recruitment training workshops for all levels of recruiting professionals UK wide. Having recruited and trained many talented recruiters over the years - many from scratch as 'first jobbers' fresh from university I am now using this knowledge and experience to deliver 'leading edge' recruitment training solutions to other recruitment organisations.
* ETN Recruitment * Placing highly successful and talented Supply Chain professionals into dynamic organsiations which can match their ambitions.

I have been in the recruitment business for 19 years now and I have always recruited in specialist markets. Having met many successful professionals in that time I am now utilising this network to drive the growth and success of ETN

Previously MD of a specialist supply chain recruitment business in the UK employing 30 staff - a company I personally launched in 1997 within the RK Group.
I am keen to take my new business forward by embracing new technology and methods so that we can continue to provide a highly valuable service to clients and candidates. I firmly believe the secret to great recruitment lies in people skills, 'who you know and how well you know them' are the two most important things for recruiters and I believe LinkedIn and internet facilitated networking will become a major factor in this.
