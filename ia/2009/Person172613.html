Shahar Nechmad’s Summary

Shahar is currenlty the founder and CEO of NuConomy, a leading web analytics and advertising company.
With more than 10 years of experience, Shahar has extensive experience in delivering technological solutions. As the business development manager of Microsoft MTC in Israel, Shahar was responsible for developing company business and marketing strategy. In addition, Shahar has years of experience as a senior Microsoft Architect, helping business throughout Europe. Shahar led some of the biggest development projects in the world, assisting companies such as Cup Gemini, Accenture and Bearing Point. Shahar is also a leading speaker for Microsoft Israel and has years of lecturing experience at conferences such as MS Tech Ed and Dev Days.
Shahar is a frequent speaker in technology and advertising conferences, where he talks about the value of data and the future of digital advertising.
