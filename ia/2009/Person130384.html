Ken Wyble’s Summary

Started Design work with Kinko's in mid 90's and established myself further with mid marketing food company EMG where I was the in-house designer of private labels, point of sale materials, large booth displays and multimedia content for trade shows. I established my freelance business in 2001 where I mostly made logos and packages for small businesses. I decided to go back to school and get a degree in Graphic Design and focused on Multimedia/Interactive Design so I could offer clients a very wide range of services and even jump into the tight market of 3-D design for print and animations as an affordable option to small and medium sized companies who desire something beyond the limits of Powerpoint. I have been working at First Data Corporation as an Executive Assistant while I build up contacts and revenue with K-Rex Design. I also have become extremely active politically with the Libertarian Party of Colorado. Chair of the Arapahoe County Affiliate, chair of the Colorado Delegation for the National Convention and candidate for Senate District 28 in Colorado running against the incumbant democrat Suzanne Williams.

Ken Wyble’s Specialties:

Specialist in Multimedia design and range from branding to motion graphics for any medium. Additional specialties in 3-D modelling design for print and animated people for simulation and training courses.
