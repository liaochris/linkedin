Brett Robbins’s Summary

We believe that most homeowners—armed with knowledge of the loan modification process—can obtain a loan modification on their own without having to spend several thousand dollars for a loan modification. There are, however, circumstances in which a homeowner may need or find it beneficial to pay for professional assistance. For example, if you are facing foreclosure, have a complicated financial situation or have reason to believe you were the victim of unfair or predatory lending practices. Therefore, a great need has been created for this type of service. Unfortunately, there are many unethical companies looking to exploit vulnerable homeowners.

Our objective is to provide distressed homeowners across the country with resources, knowledge, assistance and access to a network of attorneys and professional organizations specializing in protecting homeowners. Our organization can handle any type of property nationwide, including commercial. Our vendor affiliates have gone through extensive professional ethics reviews, have track records of success, are in compliance with State and Federal guidelines regarding loan modifications, have no unresolved complaints with the Better Business Bureau, do not make guarantees and will not accept every client.

Brett Robbins’s Specialties:

I have been working in the foreclosure markets locally and nationwide for over five years in San Diego, CA. I have experience with most aspects of the foreclosure process and resolution, including listing, buying, selling, auction/courthouse steps, subject to, assignments, fixers, refinancing, short selling and postponing and stopping foreclosure. I have helped many folks and have testimonials on hand.
