Alex Zivoder’s Summary

Contact: alex.zivoder@gmail.com

Over the course of a diversified 20-year career, Alex Zivoder has held executive and senior management positions in the online and telco industries, in public and private companies, non-profit organisations and government agencies.

Zivoder is currently Managing Director Europe of viagogo, the leading online ticket exchange that allows people to buy and sell live event tickets in a safe and guaranteed way.

Prior to viagogo, Zivoder has held 2 senior leadership roles at Expedia, the worldwide leader of online travel:
- VP and MD of Expedia France, which he helped to (re)launch and establish firmly on the market;
- SVP of the 4 largest European operations (UK, Germany, Italy and France); under his leadership, the 4 points of sale were successfully integrated into one single European entity.

Prior to Expedia, Zivoder held 3 Managing Director positions in the Tele2 group:
- MD of Tango/Tele2 Luxembourg (mobile and fixed telephony); under his leadership, both brands increased their market shares and Tango was among the first mobile operators in Europe to launch UMTS;
- MD of 3C Communications (integrated credit card solutions for hotels, restaurants and parking operators);
- MD of IntelliNet (low cost fixed-line telephony services).

Between 1991 and 2000, Zivoder worked for and at Eurostat, the statistical agency of the European Commission. He was in charge of the production of Europe's statistics on foreign trade, advised the Statistical Office of Russia in managing its transition to the production of statistics based on western standards, and managed EC's Tacis Statistics and Training of European Statisticians programmes.

Alex Zivoder founded OPAL, the Luxembourg Federation of Alternative Operators.

He holds a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Nice-Sophia Antipolis (France) and an Engineer's degree in Statistics from the Ecole Nationale de la Statistique et de l'Administration Economique (Paris, France).
