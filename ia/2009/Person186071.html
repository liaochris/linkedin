Peter Sabev’s Summary

Peter Sabev interests in the IT start from the early 1989. He has grown up with computers and he has participated in many individual and team programming contests. He has won a Bronze Medal on the Balkan Olympiad in Informatics in 2000, and also participated in the International Olympiad in Informatics. Besides computers, he has prizes in other areas - Mathematics, Physics, Literature. During his university education, he has specialized in Hungary, Serbia and Macedonia and currently he is a Master of Science in Computer Science with the highest possible marks. He has been part-time university lecturer, and the captain of the university programming team. His career covers also a wide diversity of specialties - technical writing, quality assurance, software engineering, management of small team. Peter's hobbies involve writing computer-related articles on various topics for a popular magazine in Bulgaria.
