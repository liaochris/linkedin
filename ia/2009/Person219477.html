Highly skilled in driving marketing/communications activities for agency, television, music, digital, radio, and print companies. Strong ability to effectively provide senior-level management experience to operations, P&L, and business development, plus keen ability to identify new revenue generating business models.   • Creative Artists Agency

  • FeatureCast - Idealab

    (Internet industry)

Kramer orchestrated the merger and liquidation of three late stage new media companies to form one of the first syndicators of web content on the Internet. FeatureCast also built and managed the websites, brand extensions, interactivities and online assets for top media and entertainment clients.

  • BUZZ Magazine

    (Publishing industry)

    1995 — 1998 (3 years)

    Kramer held the post of President for BUZZ Inc., responsible for the day-to-day operations and management of a 75-member staff, accountable to the Board of Directors. As primary contact to the investment community, Kramer secured new investment and initiated the build-out of a multi-media organization, including online, television, book publishing and event production.

    Under his leadership, BUZZ Magazine became the fastest-growing regional magazine in the country, as well as one of the top regional magazines in revenue growth and ad pages. Kramer played an instrumental role in the development of major cross branding platforms with high end advertisers. Kramer also managed the sale of BUZZ to Fairchild Publications, the publishing division of Disney.
