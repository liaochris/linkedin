Keith Cheuk’s Summary

Keith Cheuk is the Head of Chinese Corporates Business Division at MRI China Group, MRI has started offering its unique solutions to significant Chinese companies that are experiencing the same pain felt by foreign multinational customers in the talent markets. Keith is responsible for strategic overview, localisation of the MRI China methodology and collateral, management of the division, and growing business opportunities within China's homegrown corporate customers and prospects.

Keith has been based in South China since 2005, after working in Hong Kong for 10 years. His professional experience in China includes executive search for senior positions and pre-IPO, RPO projects as well as volume recruitment in Telecom and Financial sectors.

Keith was previously Director for a significant Guangzhou based local recruitment and training firm, leading a team of 25 consultants in its Guangzhou and Shenzhen offices. In this role he was pivotal in cultivating MNC and domestic clients in the FMCG, IT and manufacturing sectors.
