Manoj Thakur’s Summary

Top performer through out the career and education, Mr. Thakur has over 11 years of successful technical and business leadership experience with startup and larger firms including Microsoft, IBM, HP, KDDI, NEC, Narus in India and also in global context.

After graduating in 1996 in Electronics & Communication from IIT Roorkee, Manoj started his professional career at IBM India. He received many accolades including a coveted “Outstanding Award” from then IBM CEO Lou Gerstner. He played different roles ranging from software engineer to technical manager in four years of his stint with IBM. He played an important role in transitioning OS2 LAN L3 Support to IBM India.

Later he graduated from IIM Bangalore in “Post Graduate Program in Software Enterprise Management” in 2004. He played a key role in development of overall strategy and consulting for FoursNSixes http://www.foursnsixes.com. As part of curriculum, he worked on a project with ICICI to improve the marketability of http://www.icicidirect.com.

He has recently joined Microsof as Senior Program Manager to stay closer to business. Prior to that for over 2 years he was Manager in OpenVMS lab of HP STSD Bangalore. Apart from engineering management, he was accountable for patent contribution, key account development, field engagement, partner relationship management and total customer experience. He was also leading various programs in business and operational nature.

Prior to joining HP he was managing Current Product Engineering group for Narus Inc. Narus has platform based products in Telecom and Security domain. He helped the organization to grow five times in this area and helped in realizing larger business deals with enterprise customers.
