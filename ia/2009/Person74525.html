Eric Lemonne’s Summary

Freelance Web Architect for the Open Source Plone and Drupal Content Management Systems. Plone 3 theming specialist.

IT Project Manager since 1998, experienced with complex CMS Websites (teams from 5 to 10 people). Designer and integrator of Zope/Plone (ZPT, CSS, skinning). Specialized in XHTML/CSS integration in the respect of the standards of accessibility.

Official Partner and Vendor of Makina Corpus products and services in Belgium.

Geologist (sedimentologist and a geochemist), data processing specialist, in the domains of geological mapping, mining and petroleum exploration worldwide (ULB, Région Wallonne, Royal Museum of Central Africa, Lhoist, Geoservices, from 1987 to 1999).
