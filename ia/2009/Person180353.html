Patricia Steele’s Summary

Patricia Steele has over twenty years experience as a communications strategist with focus in reputation management
in the media, entertainment and creative industries. Ms. Steele’s specific expertise is helping companies, brands and individuals target and articulate their strength and benefit from change.

Ms. Steele specializes in companies and individuals experiencing in transition, through cultural shifts and restructure. Ms. Steele has expertise in executive and crisis counsel, image assessment and corporate social responsibility.
