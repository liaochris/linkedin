Olivier PARRICHE’s Summary

Olivier Parriche is co-founder and leads Product Marketing at ShopInvaders, an innovative social video shopping experience.

Before, he was the Director of Yahoo! Search in France and Director of Innovation for Yahoo! Search in Europe. In this capacity he was responsible for building Yahoo!’s Search business and audience in France as well as develop innovative product ideas for Europe. During his tenure, Yahoo! Search maintained market share in a very competitive environment. He also successfully launched web 2.0 community product Yahoo! Answers in France developing an audience from 0 to 4+ million users in one year, positioning France in the top 5 countries. He introduced monetization of this product in France with well known brands. With a team of engineers he developed prototypes for a renewed search experience.

Olivier Parriche joined Yahoo! France from start-up Xyleme where he was Product Marketing Director, leading product developments in the field of the semantic web, with development teams geographically dispersed (US, India, Romania, Russia, France).

Olivier Parriche is a French native with german roots. He looks back at 14 years of experience in the High-tech and Media industries. He worked for 4 start-ups in the fields of Telecommunication Services, CRM and Search, and for 2 major corporations in the Media and Smart Cards industries, including Schlumberger Electronic Transactions, where he held the positions of Sr Product Manager and Strategic Projects Manager, leading to a small terminal embedded-Java technology industry standard.
