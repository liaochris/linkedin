Helen Rankin’s Summary

Originally a Council Audit Clerk, Helen went on to open 10 computer training centres across 5 Middle East countries by the age of 19. At 21 she was managing the Microsoft Middle East website and introduced the first partner symposium, Fusion, which ran for 10 years. By 23 she had returned to the UK and was contributing to the Goverment's 10 Year Strategy for the NHS.

During a brief career break, Helen trained as a croupier and has worked for a number of industry-leading casino operators including Royal Caribbean and Carnival cruise lines. On returning to the UK, Helen managed the marketing and customer relations for London Clubs International at their Brighton casino, including the first televised CelebPoker tournament.

Most recently, Helen supplied the marketing for the initial three tranches of Lehman Brothers' first UK structured product and managed the regional office of a corporate communications agency before opening Lyons Rankin Ltd in 2009.

Trained at Cranfield University in Strategy Development, Leadership and Management, Helen is also a member of the Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM) and currently working towards the IR Society Certificate in Investor Relations.

Helen Rankin’s Specialties:

Project management, strategic marketing, brand development, IR and corporate communications, business start-up, internal communications, information audits, risk assessment, change management, event production.
