Patrick Breen’s Summary

My experience spans over 30 years of professional creative development. My foundation is in the fine arts which then expanded into art education and eventually commercial art and small business development. The years took the usual coarse of upper education into teaching, art director, creative director, agency owner, publisher and even owner of a television cable channel.

Throughout it all - affective representation for the client with proven results is the reputation I am most proud of establishing. At this stage in life I’m looking to surround myself with personal and business relationships that bring fulfillment, joy and pleasure. Life’s too short: Vive Bene - Spesso L’amore - Di Rista Molta ( Live Well - Love Often - Laugh Much )

Patrick Breen’s Specialties:

Conceptual development and implementation. This incorporates management of a teams with multiple deadlines in all disciplines: Graphic, web, video, photography, branding and general market penetration. I enjoy and have specialties in project management, product development, strategic planning, team leader, copy writing, sales and marketing management, creative director, photography, video production and last but not least - I am a problem solver.
