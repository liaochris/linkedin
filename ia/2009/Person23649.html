Balaji Ramachandran’s Summary

Balaji Ramachandran is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of SoftClouds. Balaji is responsible for all business development, channel sales and operational aspects of the company. Balaji is holding this position since January 2005. Prior to founding SoftClouds, Balaji was a Founder and Director of eRiva Systems Inc.a System Integration Company and grew sales to several Millions / year.
With 16+ years of experience in Consulting Services, Balaji is effective and efficient in building Customer Relationship Management (CRM) solutions to Enterprise and Mid Market clients.

Vision, Management, Technical Architecture, CRM Solutions
