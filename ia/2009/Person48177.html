Edward Williams (ewilliams@coldclosers.com)’s Summary

Cold Closing is a thought process and philosophy for making meaningful connections between people; typically for sales and business related purposes. I have tagged it as, The Art of Making The Connection; (all the capitalized words are important and deserve the emphasis) which is also the title of the book to be released this year. I came up with the term "coldcloser" a handful of years ago and it has evolved into so much more than I had initially anticipated. I set out to come up with a cool, catchy name for a business and instead have started a movement or shift away from traditional thinking related to cold calling. The principles are not unique because effective (and ineffective) communication dates back to the beginning. (created with words of authority and mortalized with words of stupidity) However, I expressed those principles with my voice (we all have one; whether it speaks or not) and a few people have listened... for which, I am thankful.
