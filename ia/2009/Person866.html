Andy Affleck’s Summary

Program and Project Management & PMO: Highly accomplished senior project management officer and senior project manager, nearly twenty years of progressive experience. Successfully completed projects in Pharmaceutical, Government, Corporate, and Higher Education industries. Managed, trained, and mentored teams of project managers as a PMO and worked extensively in program health reporting. Managed projects using multiple methodologies including Waterfall, Agile, Rational Unified Process, SDLC, and Pfizer’s Enterprise LifeCycle (ELC).

Department Management & Team Building: Managed at Executive, PMO, Operations, and Project levels in both direct and matrix. Built remote team of technical support professionals from ten to sixty-five in one year resulting in excellent support and low call turnaround times for all customers during a period of explosive growth. Successful history in building and managing motivated and effective teams including developers, customer relationship managers, system administrators, designers, and business analysts.

Mentoring, Training, & Communications: Strong communicator as a manager, author, experienced teacher, trainer, mentor, technical writer, and public speaker. Trained and mentored project managers, technical support professionals, and developers. Presenter, panelist, and keynote speaker at conferences, panels, seminars, and club meetings.

New Media & Design: Expert in New Media and always on the cutting edge having published one of the first books on Podcasting, designed one of the earliest college websites at Dartmouth College, and created one of the first television websites for NBC’s Friends. Award-winning graphic and interface designer. Accessibility expert specializing in Section 508 design. Published white paper on accessibility in Government websites. Present at conferences and trade shows including the New Media Expo. Design and develop marketing materials for print, web, and animated display.
