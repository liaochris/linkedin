Kadet Kuhne’s Summary

Professionally, I am the owner of post-production sound studio for independent film, video games, interactive media, commercials and music releases. Services include Sound Supervision, Music Composition, Dialogue Editing, Sound Design, Re-Recording Mix and Mastering. Projects have screened at festivals such as Sundance, Cannes and Toronto. Select clients include Google, Electronic Arts, Disney, Konami, Adidas, Toyota, Volkswagen, CNN and Vail. Select film projects include Happy Tree Friends, Northfork, Rolling, The Joy of Life, Hardihood, A Fine Mess, Re:Vision, A Woman Reported and Naiad.

Personally, I am a media artist whose work includes video, installation and music composition. As an award-winning filmmaker I screen numerous shorts including Infinite Delay, which premiered at the Sundance Film Festival, and my most recent film, Fight or Flight, which premiered at the Armory Center for the Arts. My installations involve motion detector sensors utilizing custom software in Jitter, online virtual space, and single or multiple channel screenings. My music compositions twist signal processing, FM synthesis and neurological impulses into electronic ambiences that aim to make your cilia vibrate in curious patterns.

Select exhibitions and performances include the Museum of Art Lucerne, LACMA, Musees de Strasbourg, REDCAT, Museum of Contemporary Art LA, San Francisco Arts Commission Gallery, The LAB, The Weisman Art Museum, Highways Performance Gallery, CEAIT Festival and the New York Underground Film Festival.

Pro Tools HD, MAX/MSP/Jitter, Native Instruments Komplete 5, Reason, Live, Peak, Waves, Final Cut Pro, DVD Studio Pro, Adobe Premier, Adobe Photoshop, Excel
