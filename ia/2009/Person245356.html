Waldir Pimenta’s Summary

Having recently graduated in computer graphics and multimedia, I am now taking a master's degree in computer graphics and intelligent systems.

I am familiar various CG-related fields such as 3D animation, virtual reality, web development, photo editing, vector illustration, audio and video editing, and also general purpose programming.

I am particularly interested in the field of virtual/augmented environments and applications (VR/AR) and interactive 3D websites (3dWeb). I also am quite fond of cartography and Geographic Information Systems (GIS)

I consider myself deeply committed to open content and open software, and have been a volunteer for a few years now in several non-profit organizations, most notably the Wikimedia Foundation.
