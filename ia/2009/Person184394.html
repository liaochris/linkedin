Pierre De Nayer’s Summary

-In depth experience of General Management and Business Development in High-tech emerging companies. Consultant, lecturer and author. Recognized expert in Biotechnology and in CRM.
-Co-founded and managed a biotech company active in mammalian cell culture (4C's now acquired by Henogen) in the early nineties.
-Founded, with two partners, a company –Citobi- now recognized as leader in its segment (CRM) and currently expanding its activities across Europe.
-In Citobi: served several organizations for the design and/or implementation of their CRM projects, including at global CEO level. Worked mainly for blue chips companies. Put in place several award winning CRM schemes namely in the pharmaceutical and in the automotive industries.
-Past experience includes: Management at Johnson&Johnson, Co-Founder and General Manager of a biotech company, key account manager at P&G pharma (In charge of a CRM project), consultant at McKinsey (Expert in CRM and in Life Sciences)

Pierre De Nayer’s Specialties:

-General Management, Business Development, High-Tech Entrepreneurship, Business consulting (strategic and operational), Counseling at CEO level,
-Leadership of small and large teams, strong interest in company building, strong expertise in high technologies, especially in biotechnology and Customer Relationship Management (CRM).
