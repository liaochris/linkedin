Gerald Adler’s Summary

I lead the national & major accounts team's interactive advertising sales efforts, and enjoy working with the staff of managers and senior advertising executives to increase their digital media sales revenue.

I have over a decade of interactive marketing and advertising management experience. Business development, consultative advertising sales management, relationship development, contract negotiation, and increasing market share are strengths. I am passionate about interactive marketing and advertising - and enjoy leading and working with teams to exceed the goals of our company and clients.

Primary skill sets include taking a 30,000 foot view of client needs and evolving goals, discussing behavioral/audience-targeting objectives and success measurement, and developing a strategic plan to enhance campaign achievement. I apply the same principle to corporate growth.

I actively foster the team concept, innovation, and being a corporate ambassador.

My professional goal is to increase each client's long-term business by effectively using digital media to work for them!
