Ole Cram, MBA’s Summary

I am open to all invites. Use a common group or say you are a friend and use our generic email address MarcobeInvestmentsInc@gmail.com to invite me.

Ole Cram is president of Marcobe Investments, Inc. which researches mostly oil and gas related investments for its own portfolio. Mr. Cram receives inquiries from accredited investors interested in the oil and gas projects that Marcobe Investment, Inc. participates in and shares information about the associated developer with these investors for their consideration.

Mr. Cram has invested in oil and gas drilling ventures, real estate, stocks, options, commodities, business startups, and other ventures. Mr. Cram now shares his experiences with others on his Accredited Investor Talk blog site to help others avoid some of the lessons he has had to learn the hard way. You can view his current and past articles on the "Accredited Investor Talk" blog at http://accreditedinvestortalk.blogspot.com/ and associated oil and gas investing related articles on the Marcobe Investments, Inc. corporate website at www.MarcobeInvestmentsInc.com.

  • President

    Marcobe Investments, Inc.

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    January 2006 — Present (3 years 7 months)

    Marcobe Investments, Inc. is a corporation that directly invests in energy related projects such as oil rigs, oil/gas drilling ventures, oil/gas drilling leases, and others. Marcobe is not a registered advisor or registered agent, but is motivated to get these projects funded quickly so all participants can gain the resulting income sooner. Therefore, Marcobe also works with accredited investors, holding companies, trusts, and other investing entities interested in participating with these projects by directing them to the principles of the associated offering company.
