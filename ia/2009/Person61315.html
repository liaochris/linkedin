Mikkel deMib Svendsen’s Summary

When I was young I wanted to become a photographer. I worked in studios for a couple of years but then decided to dedicate my professional life to music

I opened my first company as soon as I could - shortly after I was 18 - a recording studio that I managed for the next 13 years. During that time I also had a production company, a record company and a publishing company. I ended my days in the pro music business with a 2 year job for Virgin Records signing and producing new talents (A&R).

In the mid 90's the music business started to suffer - and at the same time the internet was gaining momentum. I jumped on the wagon and decided to drop out of the music business and start a new career in internet.

In 1996/97 there was very little knowledge about search engines and how to market a website in them available. And in Denmark, where I live, nobody knew anything. So I decided to dedicate most of my time to study, test and learn how to manage this area to perfection.

In 1999 I wrote my first book about search engine optimization (SEO) for IDG Publishing in Denmark, in Danish. It sold approx 6.000 copies which is pretty good at a market this small!

In 1999 I also started writing more about search engine marketing on the web and begun speaking at the biggest conferences on the subject around the world.

In 2005 I joined WebmasterRadio.FM as show host on the, still active, weekly show: Strikepoint with my co-host David Naylor – one of UKs best search engine experts.

Today I am involved in an increasing number of companies – as founder, ideologist, investor and/or expert and advisor.

- My main SEO company in Denmark: deMib.com (www.demib.com) – a nice and growing 6 people agency

- Hjem.dk – One of Denmarks biggest and fastest growing online stores

- Redpitt (www.redpitt.com) – new exciting eCommerce Platform

- deMib Holdings – my investment and personal consulting company

- RedZoneGlobal Denmark (www.redzoneglobal.dk) – PPC-agency

Mikkel deMib Svendsen’s Specialties:

International recognised expert in search engine marketing. Specialty in getting large dynamic websites (such as content management (CMS) driven websites or eCommerce sites) indexed and ranked well in major search engines.
