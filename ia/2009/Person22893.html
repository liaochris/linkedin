Avram Hein’s Summary

I turn confusing jargon into understandable information for your customers by using the art of language and applying the appropriate tools. Whether it's in print, PDF, XHTML, Adobe Air, or a platform not yet developed, I make sure readers -- whether they are programmers or your mother -- understand.
