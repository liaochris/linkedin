Peter Walker’s Summary

My goal is to work with a multi-regional team to produce first class entertainment for international audiences.

As a Independent Producer/Director living in South East Asia for the past three years, I have been fortunate to connect with organizations who are willing to collaborate on diverse international projects.

These organizations include; The New Mexico Film Office (NMFilm), Bhutan Broadcasting Service (BBS), Malaysia Multi-Media Super Corridor (MSC), Asian Academy of Film and Television (AFFT), Wild Asia, Six Senses Luxury Resorts, El Nido Resorts, Ecosphere India, Ziryab Entertainment, and Salsa Cruise Asia 2009.

Whether it be a film about the Human/Wolf/Wildlife conflict in the Indian Himalayas, a "Bali Spice" Cooking Show, or a Eco-Travel Series, I can bring my ideas, networks, and hard work to make it happen.

Peter Walker’s Specialties:

eco-lifestyle shows, documentaries, adventure travel productions, building bridges between markets, idea development and execution for both film and television
