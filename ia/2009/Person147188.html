Malcolm Friedberg’s Summary

18 years of marketing, sales and business development experience in a broad range of B2B industries. Outstanding critical thinker and strategist who can take clients through a well-conceived business analysis process (having training as a lawyer helps!). Has built several national brands and staffed marketing departments from the ground up. Award-winning creative (two national awards), excellent communicator (published author) and a hard working professional with high integrity. Wrote a book (www.whywellwin.com) that explains law to laypeople, currently in stores.
