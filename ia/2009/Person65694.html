Don Golden’s Summary

Pure online professional who started his first job in the Internet straight out of college in and never looked back. I love everything that comes with working online. Non-standard hours, workplaces, fires and most of all the people and challenges. My career has taken me through many different verticals online but I found research some 6 years ago and knew I'd found my place. Online research is project based with short timelines so we're constantly on to the next thing which suits my craving for variation.

I suppose curiosity has a lot to do with it as well.

I'm constantly curious why anyone would want to survey one armed yak farmers living in Memphis and even more curious how I'm going to figure out how to meet the client's need and win the business! You wouldn't be laughing right now if you saw the ridiculously difficult requests we get; but in reality, that's the fun part.

So if you have a need to survey one armed yak farmers living in Memphis or pretty much anything else I'm looking forward to hearing from you.
