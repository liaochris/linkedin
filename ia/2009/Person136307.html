Laila Kassis’s Summary

I am currently attending the Harvard Business School and the Harvard Kennedy School working towards an MBA and a an MPA-ID (Masters in Public Administration - International Development). Most recently I was a Senior Associate at Key Venture Partners, working on investments in software, communications and technology enabled services. At Key Ventures I was responsible for conducting due diligence on potential investments, working with portfolio companies and sourcing new investment opportunities. I currently serve as a Director for Fusion Media Group and as a Board Observer for Centive, OpSource and Triactive. Prior to joining Key Ventures, I was an Associate at Spectrum Equity Investors and an Analyst at Bear Stearns in their Technology Investment Banking Group. I am looking to expand my VC network, particularly in the Boston area. I graduated from the University of Virginia with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and Foreign Affairs.
