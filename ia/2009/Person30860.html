B. Bonin Bough’s Summary

Currently, I am the Director of Social Media at PepsiCo. My focus is on helping to socialize our communication. For those of you who know me I have been an active supporter of the space and constantly strive to help grow the space.

Previously, I was the EVP and Director of Weber Shandwick's global interactive, social and emerging media practice, leading a 100 plus-person global team. I have over ten years experience in the space, ranging from digital strategic planning and project execution to leading and building global practices. I have a deep interest and experience with community building, social media, social video, and digital marketing.

In addition to my corporate career I was a professor at NYU's Center for Publishing Graduate Studies from 2000 - 2005.

During my time at WS I grew the practice from 60 – 100 people including the roll-out of EU and Asia. I led strategic programming for the agencies top clients including Federation of Colombian Coffee Growers, Electrolux, MasterCard, Ambien, MAC Aids, Honeywell, and most recently Best Buy.

Prior, I was a founding member and SVP at Ruder Finn Interactive (RFI), I ran the interactive strategy unit for 8 yrs. During which time I led strategy for clients/projects such as Société Générale Project Finance Group, Nursing and Caregiver Campaigns for Johnson and Johnson, Kellogg 100 Anniversary, iUniverse, International Center of Photography, Hunter Douglas, Schering Plough, Affymetrix, Ready.gov (Ranked by NNG beyond 508 compliance), Novartis, Elidel, Gleevec, Femara, Zometa, Xolaire, Pfizer, Berringer, Perdue and over 70 more engagements and campaigns. I also developed RFI's processes for measuring effective Web strategies, and ePR the industries early social media methodology.

Over the years my work has won numerous awards including a, Webby, Stevie, Golden Pencil, Sabre, Big Apple, AdTech, Com Arts and SxSW – Viewers Choice for www.MrPicassoHead.com.
