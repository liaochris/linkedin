Crystal Adair-Benning’s Summary

After 12 years of special event planning Crystal Adair-Benning has had her fingers on the pulse of whats hot & trendy and whats tried & true. Her own wedding broke the mold of tradition when she fused her Scottish heritage with modern elegance and charm in two very distinctive and different ceremonies on two different continents!

Not afraid to be different, Crystal strives to help her clients create weddings and events that represent their taste, their vision, and their passion! It's your day - it should be an Occasion as Distinct as You Are! So if you dream in black and white, colour, sand, sun or snow, Crystal and her team can help create your wedding or event - your way!
