Simona Melani’s Summary

I'm 22 years old, recently graduated in Comunicazione Internazionale (International Communication) at University of Palermo.
In the last few years, I have developed many skills, such as dealing with the public. Reliability and listening to people's issues has been my primary target while working as a PA at the Assemblea Regionale Siciliana.
I have gained skills of working together as part of a group thanks to the work I have done within the several associations I have worked for. I have developed good speech skills having been part of youth organizations. Having travelled around Italy, I compared myself with different points of view and adapted to different situations. I have a very good attitude towards speaking in public, even in emergency situations.
The different roles I have played at work have given me the possibility of co-ordinating groups of work, staff and crowds of people. I have organized within the university youth associations several events dedicated to social issues, as well. Playing with a non-professional theatre company gave me the chance to organize performance and events in the field of the local show business. Working as journalist and as radio speaker helped me to manage my time frame even in everyday life.
