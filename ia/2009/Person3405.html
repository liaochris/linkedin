Charles A. Prescott’s Experience

  • Editor, The Prescott Report and Principal

    Oak Knoll Limited

    (Public Policy industry)

    October 2008 — Present (11 months)

    Researching and reporting on current developments in, and public policy issues surrounding, marketing, privacy/data protection, postal and other issues which create barriers to efficient and effective cross-border business and commerce. We report these in The Prescott Report and on its website. We assist companies to develop global growth and marketing strategies and programs. We provide legal representation on matters relating to privacy and data protection, international investment, marketing, promotion, and postal matters. We continue to represent the private sector at the Universal Postal Union through chairmanship of the Consultative Committee.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 1998 — October 2008 (10 years 5 months)

    Representation of industry before US government and international agencies and bureaux including: FTC, USPS, State and Commerce Departments, Universal Postal Union, European Commission, APEC. Development of member-support and information programs including trade missions, advisory system, networking system, monthly newsletter and providing specific advice on markets, issues, services in overseas markets. Frequent author in industry publications and speaker at industry events world-wide. Specialist in privacy and data protection and interactive legal aspects of direct and interactive marketing.
    Established and maintained global network of relationships with non-US direct and interactive marketing associations and similar organizations, as well as major industry members world-wide.
