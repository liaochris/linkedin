Budget Van Lines’s Summary

When someone needs help with a problem, they usually turn to an expert. If their pipes are leaking, they call a plumber. If their lights don’t turn on, they call an electrician. So, why is it that when they decide to move, they don’t call professional movers? With rates up to 50% less than its leading competitors, and none of the hassle of self-moving services, Budget Van Lines provides the best and most cost-effective solutions for individuals looking to make a local or long-distance move.

Active across the United States, Budget Van Lines is one the country’s largest moving brokers, maintaining an extensive network of partnerships with 300 moving companies nationwide. The Budget Van Lines system is unique in that it provides benefits for moving companies, as well as customers. Because traditional movers typically only provide one-way delivery service, their trucks are empty on return trips. This drives up prices for customers, as the moving company needs to recoup its losses from unnecessary traveling expenses. In other words, the old way means nobody wins.

On the other hand, Budget Van Lines, as a fully licensed moving broker, utilizes its large network of partners and staff of highly trained relocation consultants to negotiate the use of these empty trucks at discounted rates for its customers. Through this system, not only are trucks filled, which saves moving companies money on gas and other expenses, but customers are given the most efficient service and lowest prices on the market. Furthermore, because the movers Budget Van Lines works with are experienced professionals, customers are guaranteed that only the utmost care goes into packing, loading, and unloading items.

Licensed by the U.S. Department of Transportation, Budget Van Lines constantly endeavors to improve the quality of its services. If you have any questions about Budget Van Lines, call 1-800-611-6001 for a complimentary moving estimate.
