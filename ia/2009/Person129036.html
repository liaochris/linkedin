Keith Matson’s Summary

keith@alito.com.au
I hold a Master of Business Administration & professional qualifications in Accounting, Finance, Law, Tax Law and Corporate Governance.

I specialise in providing ‘interim management’ through functional strategic analysis to facilitate business turnaround, recovery, reconstruction, mergers, takeovers, business expansion, organisational redesign and improved operations.

My focus is increased performance through developing and driving the execution of effective strategic management processes, identifying, recruiting and developing people and creating and managing the cultural change necessary to support the performance drive.

During a professional career spanning 30 years in my own accounting and consulting practices, I have refined my knowledge and skills in the areas of Mergers, Business Performance, Strategic Business Development, People Management, Conflict Resolution, Entrepreneurship, Innovation, Strategic Analysis, Business Planning, Organisational Culture, Change Management, Leadership, Negotiations, Team Building, Executive and Management Development.

I have advised Boards of Directors, CEO’s, senior management teams and small businesses across all functional areas to develop and effectively implement robust business improvement techniques.

I value considered, commercially realistic and cost effective practical solutions to business issues.

I am a contributing author to various business magazines and have taught Accounting, Tax Law, Business Planning, Entrepreneurship, Innovation and Strategic Management at University level.

I have given seminars on Strategic Analysis and Planning, Business Planning, Innovation, Conflict Resolution, Marketing, Franchising, Turnaround Management, Corporate Finance, Information Management and Negotiations.

I have provided in-house training for senior executives and have given seminars to entrepreneurs in Australia, New Zealand, Hong Kong, Singapore, America & Canada.

  • Founder, Managing Director, Lead Facilitator

    Accelerated Business Education Pty Ltd

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    August 1995 — November 1998 (3 years 4 months)

    Created & delivered Australia's most successful training program for longterm unemployed Youth - as quoted by the Minister for Youth Affairs in his report to Parliament.

    The program consisted of 30 young people (16 - 24), 8 days wilderness trekking covering 250 kms, 8 days courseroom based Life Skills training in a remote location & 6 weeks Work Skills training.

    The program differed from other such programs in that employment positions were found for participants prior to their commencement on the program - thus creating a realistic 'goal-effort' experience for participants.

    Employers were provided with a 1 day 'Mentor' Training program to support the participants as they settled in to their employment role - for most of them, their first experience of employment.

    Programs were delivered in 4 states with approximately 3000 young people graduating the program.
