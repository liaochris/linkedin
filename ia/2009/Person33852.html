Brian Robbins’s Summary

As a leader in the casual gaming space, I strive to help developers bring casual games to the world. To do this we need a strong community, that properly rewards all members of the value chain, and focuses on bringing fun into people's lives every day.

At Fuel Industries, I primarily work on ensuring we have the best creative and technical strategies to bring our Interactive Entertainment ideas to market. Both for our customers and for our internal projects.
