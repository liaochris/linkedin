Matt Perez’s Summary

Matt Pérez is COO at Nearsoft, a nearshore outsourcing company. Nearsoft provides high tech companies the benefits of software development nearshore outsourcing while minimizing the risks through 1) experienced local project management and 2) a work force in Mexico that is one time zone away, instead of twelve.

We have a successful track record with clients like KLA-Tencor, Chamberlain, TIBCO, Axolotl and others. We will help you specify your requirements and manage the project to make sure that our nearshore engineers deliver on time, on budget and with high quality.

To learn the PROs/CONs of nearshoring, watch "Nearshoring... the Movie!" at http://www.nearsoft.com/case4nearshore.php
