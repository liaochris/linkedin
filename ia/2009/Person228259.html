Paul Valentino’s Summary

Paul Valentino is a systems and network engineer with over 13 years of professional experience, and specializes in distributed systems architecture.

Furthermore, he has extensive experience with architecture, design, testing, troubleshooting, and production rollouts utilizing market leading and open source technologies.

Paul has been the architect and engineer of several distributed systems, messaging/collaboration, Active Directory, WAN, LAN, terminal services, and ERP systems for multi-national companies including Rexam Cosmetics International, Westfalia Inc., Truelove & MacLean - H&T Waterbury, and BYK-Chemie USA.
Paul also directly supported engagement of several Fortune 20 customers for OP40, Inc.

Paul Valentino’s Specialties:

Distributed Systems, Messaging & Collaboration Architecture, Exchange, Live Communications Server, MOM, SMS, Network and Systems Architecture, Network and Systems Engineering, IT Management, Systems and Network Security, IT Procurement, Professional Services and Consultation.
