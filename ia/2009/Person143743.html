Lorraine Devon Wilke’s Summary

With an eclectic resume focused predominantly in the creative businesses, Devon Wilke offers an exceptionally wide range of both left and right brain talents upon which to draw.

Presently working as Executive Assistant to legendary brandmaker and photographer, Mike Salisbury; as Project Manager/Writer for BIRDY INDUSTRIES, a web and photographic design company, as well as her freelance work with her own company, Third Girl, she brings her extensive organizational and creative skills, writing expertise, and visual talents to the table.

As Mike Salisbury's "right hand," she provides tremendous administrative, organizational and creative support to this reemerging and high-profile branding legend and designer. Mike's work can be viewed at www.mikesalisbury.net.

In her role with Birdy Industries, she works with the head designer of the company overseeing both visual and text, focused on meeting the business and marketing needs of each client with effective. artistically appealing web statements.

On her own, under the moniker Third Girl, she provides product and creative input in a wide range of projects in the arenas of theater, film, recording and live performance.

A recent venture was the development of ROCK+PAPER+MUSIC, a record label positioned to tap into the burgeoning interest in "all things boomer." With a business plan that focused specifically on the entertainment and creative value of that demographic's artists and consumers, RPM hoped to tap into the enthusiasm and consumer savvy of this group, creating an exciting and profitable venture that would play a vital role in the necessary expansion of the entertainment industry beyond the youth market. Coincidentally, as a noted singer/songwriter herself, Devon Wilke's CD, "Somewhere On the Way" was part of the Rock+Paper+Music roster and can be found currently at her website (www.lorrainedevonwilke.com) or at CDBaby.com.

Lorraine Devon Wilke’s Specialties:

Creative writing (screenplays, treatments, articles, songs, etc.), business writing (business plans, marketing and advertising copy, web text, resumes and bios, letters, etc.), product/project pitching and brainstorming, creative marketing, organizational and production skills in both creative and business arenas, staff management, artist development, record production, digital photography and photography post-production.
