Yvo Verschoor’s Summary

In the past I have been active in both purely technology oriented settings, e.g. laser technology, and more human directed settings, e.g. usability research.
In April 2008 I accepted a job at the dutch consumer association; "De Consumentenbond". It corresponds nicely with my last accomplished education: Human Technology Interaction. Knowing that a large part of the 540000 paying members will read the results of my research is a very appreciative task.
