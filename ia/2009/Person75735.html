Pierre Erol GIRAUDY - MEG-JIC’s Summary

President of the Club MOSS FRANCE (PARIS FRANCE www.clubmoss2007.org) - MUG. Webmaster’s sites and BLOG: http://clubmos2007.org - http://meg-jic.com

Co-author of 6 books: the Portal SharePoint 2007 and 2003 ; and an other one about “Guide Juridique du Portail Internet/Intranet”. (I managed a team of 10 MVP to write 4 of these 6 books). I do a new book on MOSS. Author of The LABSPS© and LABMOSS 2007© (for teaching MOSS and SPS & WSSv2 and v3 with Office 2007/2003, on a Virtual PC).

MVP SharePoint (since 8 years) MVP Project 2002 and speaker in 2006/07/08 for Microsoft on : Microsoft Office SharePoint Server 2007 - MOSS, I wrote a lot of articles.

Know-how is about: SQL & Windows Server 2003 & 2008– IIS 6.0/7.0 – SharePoint - Project, and the management of Portal. I manage the study on the implementation of MOSS 2007 and WSSv3.

Over more than 15 years of experience managing teams and developing systems on NTIC. In the past four (7) years I have deepened my theoretical and practical knowledge in project management and MOSS+WSS, working as the responsible manager for the development of projects for the GROUP FIAT - IVECO, as well as private companies (MEG-JIC), Renault-VI, VOLVO, PREVOIR. Graduate of: the University PARIS IX Dauphine (DEA 101) IESTO CNAM PARIS “MBA”- ESCP PARIS.
