Steve (Horn)’s Summary

My primary field of IT is in regards to document imaging (conversion of paper to digital image) even more specifically in the area of forms processing using OCR/ICR/OMR and Barcode technology. Document imaging and automated data entry is my first love in IT and a field in which few have the level and years I obtained.

I have designed hundreds, if not thousands, of automated forms in a OCR/ICR product currently held by Autonomy (formerly Cardiff Software) called Teleform. In this product I have acted as a reseller, certified trainer, expert form designer, technical support, systems administrator and service bureau manager. Hundreds of companies have benefited from my expert knowledge of this technology including such organizations as the US EPA, USAF, Beth Israel Hospital Boston, Baylor Medical Center, Johns Hopkins, American Red Cross National Headquarters (Disaster Welfare Inquiry System), Direct Mail Express Inc., Digital Press Inc., Wal-Mart National Headquarters, the NYPD and Catholic Charities USA to name a few.

My professional experience also includes commission sales, management, customer service, recruiting and support.

My goal is to work an honest business, pay my bills, help others pay theirs and to enjoy a fruitful life doing things I receive great joy and satisfaction from. I am working the get rich slow plan and intend to keep doing so minute by minute.

I have many contacts in the employment placement field and am happy to offer what help I can to those in need of positions. As for my needs, if you are in need of my professional services I encourage you to contact me. There is an old saying..."If you don't ask you don't get."

That being said, I ask of you...contact me if you believe I can help you or you can help me. These are hard times but the faithful and diligent survive. Continue every minute to improve your situation and do the next right thing for yourself and others.

Proud F.O.B
