David Petersen’s Summary

March 2009, and on it grows.... National gets acquired by Norel Services and what a boom to both companies. Norel gets to play in the ESD world and National gets a boost in the Service and T&I arenas. I go from having 7 direct reports to 15...Oh Boy!!! Stay tuned!!!

Oct. 2008. Back in Distribution!

I have led teams that have developed products from complex Fire Alarm Control Systems to the simple re-packaging of existing HVAC Control, Notification Appliances and Annunciators.

In a memorable team effort the Sales Manager of Mammoth Fire Alarms, his department and the boys in the Highbay area brought MFA to it's highest annual sales figures to date. I was very proud of their efforts.

I have managed employees from 32 union technicians to 6 highly professional and energized Customer Service and Tech Support personnel.
I developed and taught the complete Fire Alarm Training ciriculem for FCI's and MFA's customer support training programs.

I ran a highly successful Customer Service group for 11 years starting from scratch (only me) to a 7 person Department through the company's peak growth period.

I fully enjoy working with Cross-Functional Teams in a Multi-discliplined effort to produce and/or develop products (wow, big words to say I work well as a team leader).

Marketing and Product Management are my passions.

But above it all are my Family, my Friends and my Road King. These keep me sane....
