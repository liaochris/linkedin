Kieran J Morris’s Summary

A Montreal based (bilingual) senior sales & product marketing leader with over 12 years global experience in Internet, web technology & telecommunications services. A consistent reputation for improving teams' results through a well developed performance management style. An excellent multi-tasking communicator possessing a respected talent for effective leadership both locally and remotely of subordinate regional managers, team leaders and non-management staff. Kieran joined the Internet pioneer UUNET in Toronto in 1996, one of the first and largest global Internet service providers dedicated to business to business Internet services. Today, UUNET is an internal brand of Verizon Business.

Currently Kieran is the Director of Sales at UpClick.com, a fast growing Montreal based company offering comprehensive payment gateway and payment processing platforms that leverage cross selling synergies for online eGoods merchants.
