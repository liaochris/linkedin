Lee Bierman’s Summary

Technical architect, technical manager (on and offshore), and software developer experienced in both software product development and large-scale project based e-commerce and financial services implementations.

A principal architect of Offermatica, the leading SaaS targeting, testing, and optimization platform. Designed and built the core Offermatica platform.
