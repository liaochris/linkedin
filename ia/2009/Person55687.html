Darren Wise’s Summary

I am a Financial Services Professional as well as a Business and Life Coach with over 17 years experience. I have had experience in business management and leadership at a national level, business coaching, leadership coaching, professional and personal development coaching of Individuals and Teams. I had 9 years experience as a Financial Planner from 1992 - 2001 specialising in retirement planning and managing over $50 million of client investments. In 2001, I launched and developed as Managing Director and major shareholder the Dealer Group Financial Planning Services Australia, which ultimately grew to over 30 offices, managed over $1 billion dollars of clients funds, and ranked in the top 50 largest financial planning companies nationally. I sold my interest in FPSA in March 2008 to platform provider Netwealth. I accepted a role with Equiti Financial Services Pty Ltd in Sydney as Head of Advisory Services to assist them to expand their Financial Advisory business nationally, as well as to develop and implement financial products and services for their retail and wholesale clients.

Darren Wise’s Specialties:

I am a qualified Financial Planner and Business and Life Coach. I specialise in the areas of business coaching, business management and leadership, business marketing and development, life coaching, leadership training and personal and professional development of Individuals and Teams.

My core skills are also in the identification, implementation and development of business opportunities in the Financial Services Industry through my industry knowledge, experience and leadership skills.
