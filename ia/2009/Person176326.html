Natasha Kapij’s Summary

"We grow great by dreams. All big men are dreamers". These words of Woodrow Wilson motivate my every day. What am I dreaming about?
Globalize my real estate business Modernova Development LLC with courage and supreme persistence, long-term planning and calculated risk.
At the same time I pursue my passion for design with enthusiasm, well-developed skills, and burning desire to succeed. NK interior Design provides design and project coordination to both commercial and residential clients. NK designs in a clean and sophisticated contemporary style, with attention to the details and quality materials.
Our Modernova Group is a unique strong team of developers and investors who work together to service the needs of real estate clients operating in the current real estate environment. Our team develops, builds, leases, owns and manages real estate assets. When you think of Modernova, understand that our team is committed to honesty, integrity and the highest standards of ethical conduct concerning real estate in the world.
