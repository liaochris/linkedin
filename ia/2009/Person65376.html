Dominic Pinto’s Summary

Dominic is an experienced and successful senior manager with a depth, breadth and diversity of UK and international business experience and skills over 25 years, and now in the Not for Profit/Third Sector/Charity sector.

Recent roles and expertise include: Consulting Policy Adviser, Fundraising, Freelance and Contract Project Manager, and Interim Management.

Integration, Turn-Around & Restructuring

I'm looking for more challenges to add to this - whether by way of continuing individual independent advising, interim or short term management, or indeed a further employed position (full or part time).

The objective is: To get some real satisfaction and, in the words of a good friend in New York, who was COO of the Institute for International Education, to have a practical and appreciable effect and impact doing something either worthwhile in itself, or helping achieve something really worthwhile.

It doesn't have to be up there in big lights, although that would be OK, and it can be in any of the commercial, private, community, voluntary, or public sectors. I've shown I can do it, and rather than being a 'jack of all trades', demonstrated that I can apply the skills and experience across the sectors and in different businesses and organisations.
