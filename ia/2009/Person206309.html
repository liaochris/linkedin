Ryan Bauer’s Summary

Ryan is passionate about using marketing strategies as a solution to overcome challenging economic hurdles facing today's organizations.

Ryan has contributed his innovative marketing campaigns to several successful start-up organizations and several well known alternative brand clients. As a result, he has an extensive background in various industries; including environmental/eco-advocacy and the tech/gaming industry. Combined with his vast experience in marketing to the GLBT demographic, Ryan has the breadth of background and varied skill set that make for creative and unique campaign development. Ryan is a well known speaker on new media and a consultant to several marketing agencies and startup clients.

Ryan Bauer’s Specialties:

Experiential Marketing, Social Media, Leadership Strategies (specifically, the Kazien Method), Community Organizing, Start-ups, Online Marketing Strategy, New Media, Marketing and Communications.
