Cita Walsh’s Summary

Medical marketing executive with broad experience in strategic marketing, product life cycle management and marketing operations. Cita leverages her knowledge of customer behaviors and the selling cycle to create integrated marketing and interactive solutions that ignite sales. Areas of expertise include communications, branding, social media, practice development and consumer pull-through programs. Extensive experience leading teams in the creation of collateral, web initiatives and advertising as well as sales training, PR and direct response programs. Proven ability to work with sales, regulatory, clinical, and legal affairs to commercialize products.

Cita Walsh’s Specialties:
