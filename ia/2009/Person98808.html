Ingmar Bergmann’s Summary

Treasurer with over 18 years of Corporate Finance/Treasury experience, 5 of which as a Corporate Treasurer for global companies and 3 years as Head of Corporate Finance for a major Dutch energy utility company. Major accomplishments include tendering of a Eurobond, financing with US Private Placement, organizing a RCF and Project Financing. Specialized in closing complex financing transactions, organizing and improving quality and added value of Corporate Financing departments and determining and implementing treasury policies. Thinking ''out of the box'' and in solutions. Strong interpersonal, management and third party communication skills.

Ingmar Bergmann’s Specialties:

Clear knowledge of the Dutch Unbundling Act and the conseqeunces from a financing point of view.
Ability to negotiate transactions in challanging times.
