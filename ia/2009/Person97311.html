Ian Wyatt’s Summary

* Co-Founder & President - Business Financial Publishing
* Editor-in-Chief, Growth Report and Rising Star Stocks
* Chief Investment Strategist, SmallCapInvestor.com
* Member - Entrepreneur's Organization (EO)

Ian is a serial entrepreneur who is focused on building Business Financial Publishing into the leading provider of news, analysis, research and commentary related to small cap and micro cap investments in the U.S.

Business Financial Publishing currently reaches 600,000 individual investors each week through our free and paid subscription investment newsletter products. Current paid subscription products include Growth Report, Rising Star Stocks, and Supernova Stocks. We also publish two free weekly newsletters titled Big Idea Investor and Financially Fit, and two regular special reports through our web sites NewsletterAdvisors.com and BrokerAdviser.com.

Ian is an active member of the 6,400 member Entrepreneur’s Organization in the Washington DC chapter. In 2006 he was one of sixty entrepreneurs selected from around the world to participate in the prestigious Entrepreneurial Masters Program (formerly Birthing of Giants) at MIT’s Sloan School of business, a three-year executive development education program.

As a leading market expert, Ian has written for CBS Marketwatch, Zacks Investment Research, and Empire Magazine. He has been interviewed or cited in articles in well known publications including Kiplinger's Personal Finance Magazine, Barron’s, Forbes.com, The Dick Davis Digest, The Dick Davis Income Digest, The Wall Street Transcript, the Money Show Digest, The New Jersey Star Ledger, The Wisconsin State Journal, and the Seattle Times. Additionally, Ian is a regular speaker at Money Show investor conferences held throughout the United States, and is a regular guest on the Money Matter Financial Network radio program broadcast in the tri-state area and online.
