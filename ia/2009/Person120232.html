Jorg Meyer’s Summary

Jorg is an experienced hospitality professional, with wide-ranging expertise in the effective application of organisational processes and systems. Working internationally for many years, Jorg has been involved into start-ups as well as turnarounds of local and multinational corporations alike.

Jorg’s primary interests and expertise are in organisational development, talent management, mainly in the hospitality, leisure & retail industries. His projects have made him travel to China, Indonesia, Morocco, Oman, Philippines, Russia, Sri Lanka, Uzbekistan and Vietnam in recent years.

Previously, Jorg has gained valuable working experience with InterContinental Hotels Group, ACCOR Hotels and Jumeirah Hotels & Resorts in various executive positions across operations, human resources and development - in Europe and the Middle East.
