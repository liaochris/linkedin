Freedom Debt Relief’s Summary

Since 2002, Freedom Debt Relief has been America’s choice in helping people settle debt accrued from financial hardships, such as divorce, job loss, or medical bills. Freedom Debt Relief pioneered the debt settlement industry with its incomparable customer service, commitment to working only for its clients, and its unique Debt Reduction Program.

Working directly with clients’ creditors to negotiate low monthly payments, Freedom Debt Relief has an average settlement rate of 42.6%. Freedom Debt Relief’s clients often obtain financial freedom within 12 to 36 months. Freedom Debt Relief not only equips clients with the tools they need to achieve financial freedom, but also works to create a hassle-free environment for clients by serving as a go-between for clients and creditors.

Maintaining a consistently growing client base of 30,000, Freedom Debt Relief serves clients from a variety of backgrounds. Bucking current "outsourcing" trends, Freedom Debt Relief chooses to employ over 500 highly-trained U.S. professionals that are able provide clients with a level of satisfaction unmatched in the industry and has garnered it accolades nationwide. Freedom Debt Relief is also dedicated to upholding the debt settlement industry’s best business practices and standards through its membership in The Association of Settlement Companies (TASC).

In addition to being recognized by the San Francisco Business Times and the Phoenix Business Journal as one of the best places to work, Freedom Debt Relief ranked third on Entrepreneur Magazine’s 2008 “100 Hot Companies” list. Freedom Debt Relief has also received extensive recognition from the San Jose Business Journal, Stanford Business Journal, and the Christian Science Monitor, among others. Freedom Debt Relief is also a member of Inc. 500, “The Fastest Growing Private Companies in America.”

Freedom Debt Relief is headquartered in San Mateo, California; offices in Sacramento, California; and Phoenix, Arizona.
