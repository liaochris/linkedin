Adam Kagan’s Summary

Adam Kagan is a recording engineer, mixer and producer with almost 15 years experience in the Los Angeles music scene. At least 6 albums have been nominated and/or won Grammy Awards and several albums have been gold and platinum. Along with these album credits, Adam has been involved in several feature film and television shows as music editor, music supervisor and mixer. Recently, three artists that Adam has produced have been picked up by major labels for distribution.

Adam founded 2 Degrees Productions to facilitate artist development and to provide production and post production services to music, film, television and multimedia companies. 2 Degrees maintains a private facility in Burbank, California.

Adam is always looking for strategic partners and development opportunities in music, film, television and electronic media.

Adam Kagan’s Specialties:

Recording engineering, mixing, music production, artist development, radio promotion. Post production audio services for film, television, internet and multimedia.
