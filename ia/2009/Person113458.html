Jim Suchara’s Summary

Technology executive with over 20 years of experience, including 15 years of operational & strategic leadership in marketing agencies and Fortune 500 companies.

Have developed an extensive portfolio of digital marketing strategies & capabilities based on web, mobile, and multimedia platforms for use in global programs, campaigns, and live events for major brands such as Toyota, Ford, GM, Chrysler, Delphi, Alcoa, Pepsi, Coors, and Sony.

The wide range of applications and technologies involved includes virtual events, social media, e-learning, e-photo/video/sweepstakes, ROO / ROI measurement, content management systems (CMS), online collaborative portals, RFID, text/SMS marketing, mobile devices, touch screen kiosks, networked digital signage, and advanced audio/visual displays.

Frequently tapped by executive management to take on special corporate efforts that extend outside of technology. These initiatives have included everything from directing corporate marketing communications, to driving operational efficiencies, and project-managing entire corporate relocations.

Jim Suchara’s Specialties:

Strategic and operational management, Digital marketing strategies and technologies, Corporate/enterprise IT direction.
