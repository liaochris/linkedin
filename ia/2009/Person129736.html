I sell/ lease commercial Trucks,Vans,Suvs, and misc. equipment to customers from all kinds of business.

Plumbing companies, Air Conditioning, Electrical Contractors, Cement Contractors, Home building Contractors, Drywall contractors, Landscapers, Etc.

I also sell to individuals who are requesting
specific make or model that I need to find thru our vast dealer network!
