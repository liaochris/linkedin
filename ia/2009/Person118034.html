  • Author

    "The Legend of the Great Horse"

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    In 1996 an idea for a story about the development of horsemanship came to me, and I spent the next years doing research and learning the craft of writing. The book expanded to become a trilogy, and the first volume, "Eclipsed by Shadow," was independently published in the Summer of 2008.

    "Eclipsed by Shadow" received excellent reviews from Library Journal, the Midwest Book Review, the Historical Novel Society, and many others, and won an in the 2008 ReaderViews Literary Awards.

    The second volume of the trilogy, "The Golden Spark," will be published in the fall of 2009.
