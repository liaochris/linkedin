John Melnick’s Summary

Melsongs Publishing represents my original music and the songs of other talented writers. There many wonderful songs in my catalogue and am currently looking for artists, producers, and publishers who are in the need of new music. The songs represent timeless statements, they are melodic, and meant to last. A Christmas song, Spend Every Christmas with You, is looking for a home.
I invite the music industry to contact me. We are open for business.
One composition, Before You Go, became a theme for an audio visual presentation honoring our Veterans and has be visited online by 20 million, that's right, 20 million people in the past 3 years.
Melsongs Publishing has more music ready and able to make the same imprint. We are new, unsigned, and ready.


My other passion, golf, has led me to develop The Golf Diary Personal Scoring System which is a unique software program that gives any golfer a complete picture of their game, not just a score. It is a very useful training tool as well as a great gift for the golfers in your life.
Out of the Golf Diary comes a new company, US Golf Data. We carry data on nearly 18,000 US golf courses, including tees and yardages, and work closely with the National Golf Foundation, the travel industry as well as many web design and software development companies. Our clients include Golflink and The Golf Channel.
The Golf Diary website is currently under reconstruction. Sorry for the inconvenience. We will be up and running shortly.
