Nancy Dunne’s Summary

A national leader in the movement to transform health care through integration of all effective healing systems.

Published self help book for women suffering the metabolic syndrome known as Polycystic Ovary Syndrome and related fertility problems. Created a self hypnosis product to assist weight loss for women with PCOS. The Natural Diet Solution for PCOS and Infertility: How to Manage Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome Naturally (pcosdiet.org)

Served as President of the Montana Association of Naturopathic Physicians; on the Alternative Health Care Board for the state of Montana; Naturopathic Physicians Licensing Exam Chair for Emergency Medicine; founding member of the North American Board of Naturopathic Examiners.

Elected to the Board of Directors of the American Association of Naturopathic Physicians 1995, chairing the Professional Affairs and Ethics Committees;edited the association’s monthly newsletter.

Presented 2002 AANP President’s Award describing her contribution as having “reliably cared for and nurtured the soul of our profession.”

President of the AANP 2004-06. During her tenure as AANP President facilitated collaborative projects including contributions from MDs, RNs, psychologists, grass-roots political organizers, acupuncturists, herbalists and education leaders.

Through 2008, Chair of the Board of Directors for the Institute for Natural Medicine, a philanthropic working group that manages resources for the advancement of naturopathic philosophy, education, research and health care.

Master of Arts degree in Applied Behavioral Science in 2002.
Certified Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) therapist. Many of her counseling clients are combat veterans and their families

Nancy Dunne’s Specialties:

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome; therapeutic weight loss; psychotherapy, including trauma resolution; self hypnosis for personal goal achievement
