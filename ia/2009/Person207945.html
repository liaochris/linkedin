Sam Johnson’s Summary

My passion is solving business puzzles and finding answers, being a detective. I enjoy simplifying the complex, finding answers, and developing actionable solutions.

Every organization has similar issues because they can rarely answer the simplest of questions: What business are we in? Who is our customer? What are we offering? How can we offer it to the right customer? Who are our competitors today? Tomorrow? How will their actions affect ours? And vice versa?

Innovation is rarely about adding functionality or extending services (though that can be done, too). Innovation surprises. It surprises competitors by fooling them into complacency while surprising new or less-served customers with new, exciting, or even good enough, options.

My goals are to expand my management experience and continue to have impact for organizations willing to stretch themselves through the challenging exercises that a solid program of research, analysis, interpretation and recommendations can bring about.
