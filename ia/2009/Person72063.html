Ellen Shi Min’s Summary

After achieving master of engineering in National University of Singapore. I worked as a R&D member in Design technology Institute in the field of data mining. I've done projects of analysis manufacturing data for semiconductor company and CRM software for call centers of consumer electronics company.

During my work at the institute, we participated and won the prestigious KDD cup 2002 competition with honorable mentions, my role and responsibility in this event was a complex coordination of 3 teams from different research institutes and university.

I have also published journal and conference papers and given speeches at international conferences and fulfilled one of my dreams as being an engineer when I grew up :-)

As a native Chinese speaker from china, I always have great desire in helping people to break the Chinese language barrier, armed with my Chinese & English education and translations and interpretation skills ,I have shifted my professional route from research to technical translation and localization area.

I have gained over 10 years experience in providing services to the diverse translation, localization and globalization needs of my clients which vary from high tech, science based companies to law firm and financial magazines.

I've helped my clients with Chinese brochures and posters in exhibition, websites, marketing presentation, products catalogs, user manuals, etc. I've done various software GUI into Chinese.

I am committed to excellent translation that is both linguistically appropriate and technically accurate.

I love what I do and I do what I love :-)
