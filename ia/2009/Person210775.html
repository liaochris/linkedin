Steven Brown’s Summary

Goal-oriented engineering executive with over fourteen years engineering experience with eleven years developing diverse medical devices from concept through clinical trials and production. Developed and commercialized products which include the first successful posteriorly implanted artificial disc, posterior dynamic stabilizers, pedicle screw systems, interbody fusion devices, resorbable sports medicine implants, and various total knee systems.

Steven Brown’s Specialties:

• Product and business development leadership
• Early-stage start-ups to large companies
• Surgeon partnerships
• Managing multiple team members
• Project management and budgeting
• Clinical support and medical education
• Cadaver lab management
• Investor negotiations
• Engineering support of PMA, 510(k), CE Mark, and global regulatory submissions.
• Developing products consisting of Bioabsorbable polymers and various biocompatible metals and plastics.
