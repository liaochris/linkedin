Jeremy Likness’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur at heart. My field of expertise for the past 15 years has been technology, specifically software development, in various industries including insurance, health and wellness, supply chain management, Wi-Fi hotspots, mobile devices, and WLAN management.

I established my professional reputation as a software development expert through my role as development manager at Manhattan Associates a decade ago, working on custom code and enhancements for their flagship PkMS project with a team of developers.. As Director of IT for Dr. Tango (Hispanicare) I was able to practice my Spanish-speaking skills while I grew our technology infrastructure to support a 250% increase in staff and served as architect for the next generation platform for our multilingual diet software.

I founded my own health and wellness business in 2004 and pioneered the use of podcasts to reach a broad audience through online blogs and fitness lessons. I produced and sold several CD sets internationally and published my book, "Lose Fat, Not Faith" which has sold thousands of copies in over a dozen countries around the world.

As Director of IT for Wandering WiFi, I continue to help us grow and solidify our position as one of the leading providers of wireless infrastructure solutions with over 500 customers in the U.S., including airports, hospitals, grocery chains, hotels, retail stores and corporate offices. I primarily oversee the software development of our product portfolio that includes HotSpot solutions and AirWatch, a wireless network and mobile device management platform.

Jeremy Likness’s Specialties:

Public speaking, coaching, mentoring. Building technology infrastructure - growing small start-ups to viable businesses. ASP .NET 3.5 C# commercial enterprise applications.
