Craig Woods DVM, MS, MBA’s Summary

I have spent his career in life science research and development, licensing transactions and veterinary medicine and surgery. I have special interests in biomaterials, tissue regeneration, immunology, leukocyte biology, bioscaffolds, and new devices for veterinary medicine.

I was in veterinary practice from 1995 until 1999. In 1999, I entered industry holding various senior management positions in research and technology licensing. In 2007, Craig founded TR BioSurgical, LLC, a company developing advanced biomaterials and devices for veterinary medicine. I founded Animal Health Consulting to assist drug companies in clinical trials and entering veterinary medicine.

I have my B.S. (Biology), M.S. (Biochemistry/Anatomy), and Doctor of Veterinary Medicine (DVM) from the University of Missouri and M.B.A. from Arizona State University.
