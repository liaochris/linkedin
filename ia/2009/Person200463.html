Robert Haddock’s Summary

Technology and Business Innovator with proven experience in imagining, planning, financing and starting new ventures, and in refocusing existing ventures.

Integrative thinker, able to synthesize new solutions and bring them to profitable reality. Holder of four major patents, author/ developer of print publications that generated sales of more than 10 million copies.

Creator of the first smart phone, upon which more than 1.5 billion mobile phones are based, the first phone with a chip card for security (SIM card), the first phone with a graphical interface, the first phone with a graphical web browser and the first phone supporting Voice Over IP. First conceived of integrating a telephone with chip card, now implemented in all GSM phones worldwide. Creator of the first text browser that allowed access to millions of pages from multiple content sources worldwide, the first commercial concept-based search engine and the first news reader.

Robert Haddock’s Specialties:

Business Strategy, Strategic Marketing, Business Development, Technology Planning and Implementation

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Banking industry)
