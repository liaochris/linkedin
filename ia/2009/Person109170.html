Jen Karetnick’s Summary

*currently work as restaurant critic for MIAMI Magazine from the Modern Luxury group; features editor for Wine News; columnist for Biscayne Times; and freelancer for Onboard Media, Florida Travel & Life, and others.

*recently became the creative writing teaching for middle and high school arts students at Miami Arts Charter School, a school devoted to arts education in addition to academics (think the old Fame, not the new one)

*published in a wide range of newspapers, magazines and websites including The Atlantan, Continental Inflight, Diversion, Endless Vacations, Florida Table, Florida Travel & Life, Food.com, Gayot.com, julib.com, Miami Herald, The New York Times, Ocean Drive, Poets & Writers, Vegas

*12 years as staff restaurant critic and "Dish" columnist for Miami New Times and New Times Broward/Palm Beach before going freelance

*co-author of Raw Food/Real World (HarperCollins, summer 2005) and Born Again Vintage (Potter Craft, December 2008); author of Around Miami with Kids (Random House, 2000) and poetry collections Necessary Salt (Pudding House Publications, spring 2007), Bud Break at Mango House (Portlandia Press, fall 2008) and Eve and After (Southern Hum Press, forthcoming).

Jen Karetnick’s Specialties:

food, wine and spirits, travel, literature and poetry, health and medicine, home and garden, shopping, culture and art, and all things lifestyle
