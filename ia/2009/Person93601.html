Hedy Macferran’s Summary

Dynamic, accomplished, Harvard-educated Event Manager and Producer with a focused expertise in high-end food and beverage management and a 10+ year portfolio of success at orchestrating both small and large-scale, experiential marketing, internal brand communications, and high-end social events. Results-driven, big-picture strategist with sharp eye for detail. Reputed for producing projects with budgets up to $500K and up to 70,000 attendees. Demonstrated ability to multi-task in high-pressure work environments. Equal ability to work autonomously and as a supportive team player. Adept at mentoring and training high-performance, service-oriented event teams. Dedicated to delivering exemplary customer service.
