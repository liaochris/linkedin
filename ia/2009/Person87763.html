Gloria Garner, Au.D.’s Summary

Dedicated, results-oriented professional with a doctorate degree in audiology, coupled with more than twenty years of hands-on experience in the clinical field. Proven leadership qualities with a proactive style of management, organizational skills, and the ability to manage time efficiently.My goal is to apply my professional and clinical training as an audiologist to improve the quality of life for individuals with hearing loss through the use of leading edge technologies.
