Sandi Smith’s Summary

The mission of BrainWays® Training & Development is to help employees in organizations reach their highest potential by understanding the strengths and limitations of the human brain, and by understanding that they have more control than they ever thought possible about how best to use their own brains.

The BrainWays Tool Box consists of 40 powerful techniques that make up the BrainWays system. BrainWays instructors teach employees how and when to use these tools via small group training and one-on-one sessions. When BrainWays workshop participants apply this system of tools to their workplace and their lives, transformational results occur. The BrainWays training teaches concrete ways to improve employee productivity, performance, and engagement.
