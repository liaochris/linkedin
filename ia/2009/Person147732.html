Manjit Rana’s Summary

Manjit has over 20 years experience of supplying technology / ebusiness based solutions to the Insurance and Financial services market sectors. He has built over a dozen sales operations and launched a number of businesses.

Manjit has operated in various types of organisations in the UK, Europe and the USA ranging from complete start-ups to large multi-national corporations. He has worked at a senior level in organisations including the Misys Group, CSC, Fujitsu Consulting, Delphi Computer Services Inc, Netik plc and Focus Solutions

Manjit helps technology based companies take their solutions to the Insurance and Financial Services sectors. He is currently involved in bringng a revolutionary new web based marketing utility to market
