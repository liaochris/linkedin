  • Volunteer Chair

    UPA World Usability Day

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    June 2006 — December 2006 (7 months)

    "Why doesn't this work right? What am I supposed to do with this now?" World Usability Day is for everyone who's ever asked these questions. This Earth Day style event, focused on raising awareness and visibility of usability engineering and user centered design, is currently being organized by volunteers and local event coordinators from around the world. Whether a usability professional or just an enthusiastic (or frustrated) user, each participant is making a contribution to "making life easy".

    WUD promotes the value of usability engineering and user-centered design and the belief that every user has the responsibility to ask for things that work better. The Usability Professionals' Association is doing that by encouraging, organizing, and sponsoring 36 hours of activities at the local level around the globe, all occurring on November 14 of each year.

    Learn More ---> http://www.worldusabilityday.org
