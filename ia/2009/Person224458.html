Steven Forbes’s Summary

Currently Projects Director at DistinctMEDIA Ltd. specialising in comparison & social shopping, affiliate marketing, web publishing and improving the user experience.

Originially started out as a Web Developer & Designer at Marcat Ltd (now Vamosa) [1999 – 2002] having graudated from University with a BSc Hons in IT & Multimedia Technology.

My role included:
* Managing client relations and customer support helpdesk
* E-commerce developer [Intershop certified]
* Web design & development for numerous private and public sector clients inc. Church of Scotland, Scottish Tourist Board (now VisitScotland) and Scottish Enterprise

Following that I was Project Manager, Developer & Designer at Clearfuse Ltd (now iBooking.com) [2002 – 2008]
Project Manager on a wide variety of projects for small, medium and large organisations across Europe.

* Specialising in online booking, ticketing and e-commerce.
* Managing day to day operations and multi-functional project teams
* Client account management with private and public sector companies across Europe
* Information architecture, technical writing for bespoke applications, quality assurance and usability
* Design & Creative Direction
* E-Commerce

My next role was as a Project Manager & Designer at Clickspace Ltd [2004 – 2008]
Specialising in Paid Search & Price Comparison Shopping [UK & US].

* Jointly responsible for the overall running and growth of Clickspace
* Managing day to day operations and multi-functional project teams
* Key partnership account management with companies inc. Pricerunner, Shopping.com & Kelkoo
* Design & Creative Direction
* Technical Writing & Quality Assurance
* PPC Advertising [Google Adwords, Yahoo! Search Marketing, Microsoft AdCenter]
* Search Engine Marketing and Optimization
* Hands-on development as and when required [XHTML / CSS/Javascript/ASP]

Clickspace later merged with Forsight Ltd to become DistinctMEDIA in October 2008.
