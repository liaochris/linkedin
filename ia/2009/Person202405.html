Roelof Botha’s Summary

Partner at Sequoia Capital

I am currently closely involved with the following companies: Challenge Games, Gene Security Network, Jawbone, Mahalo, Meebo, Tokbox, and Xoom. Prior portfolio companies include YouTube (acquired for $1.65bn by Google in 2006) and Insider Pages (acquired by CitySearch in 2007).

http://www.sequoiacap.com/people/roelof-botha/

Prior to Sequoia, I was the CFO at PayPal. I helped to take the company public in February 2002, and participated in negotiations for the sale of PayPal to eBay for $1.5bn in October 2002.

Stanford Business School: MBA, 1998-2000

McKinsey & Co.: Business Analyst, 1996-1998, South Africa

Fellow of the Faculty of Actuaries, Scotland

University of Cape Town: Bachelor of Business Science, with majors in Actuarial Science, Economics, and Statistics
