Manish Singh’s Summary

Manish Singh began his career as a software freelancer while he was pursuing his Engineering degree in Information Technology.

After graduation, he joined Amdocs Inc as a Subject Matter Expert. Briefly after he left Amdocs, Manish started a software firm named WonderPoint Technologies based out of Mumbai, India. As a co-owner of the firm, his main responsibilities included business development (Marketing & Sales) and Project Management. As the firm grew he took up additional responsibilities like Recruitment/HR, Finance and Operations.

In 2008, WonderPoint Technologies underwent a restructuring of the management and shareholders. The company is now called Kudos Infomedia Pvt Ltd. Manish assumed roles primarily of Marketing, HR and Operations in this new setup. His experience includes handling marketing and sales in the IT consulting domain, lead generation using Internet Marketing and Search Engine Technologies (both Organic and PPC). He has more than four years of experience in IT Project Management handling small, medium and big project teams. He has been involved in all stages of software development including requirement gathering, design, coding, testing and delivery/deployment. He also has over 2 years of experience in IT recruitment and operations management.
