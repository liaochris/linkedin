Big U Music Sound Design’s Experience

  • Music & Audio Production

    Big U Music . Sound Design

    (Media Production industry)

    1989 — Present (20 years)

    Big-U was started in 1989 as a small company doing location recording and copying for college students and professors. During the next 5 years, Big-U continued to expand into bigger recording arenas and into producing, arranging and composing music for Radio and TV. In 1994 Big-U Music.Sound Design, Inc. was formed with the partnership of Matthew Cornwell and Sam Esparza to create one of Arizona's first commercial music companies.
