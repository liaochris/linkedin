Jeffrey Tobias’s Summary

Jeffrey Tobias’ career in the application of information technology and innovation has spanned the worlds of academia, big and small business, government and research. In each environment his leadership, clarity of thought, creativity and drive have generated cutting edge advances and successes far beyond expectation. His broad and deep experience and extraordinarily wide skill set are almost certainly unique within the region.

Dr Tobias is an accomplished strategist, with years of commercial and business experience. He is a respected team leader and manager, with a history of motivating and leading teams in many and varied disciplines. One of his many strengths is his ability to communicate with people at all levels.

Jeffrey Tobias’s Specialties:

Strong background in business and the public sector. Very strong business leadership skills, team leadership and solid business management experience. Very strong technology background, especially in vertical markets including health, education, retail and business. Skills include financial, management, innovation, leadership.

    Joined IBA as General Manager, eHealth. IBA Health provides software solutions to hospitals and health groups.   • Chairman and CEO

    Dataflow Computer Services

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1983 — May 2000 (17 years 5 months)

    Executive Chairman and Managing Director of Dataflow Computer Services from 1983-2000. Dataflow Computer Services was Australia’s and New Zealand’s premier distributor of software and peripherals. Dr Tobias grew Dataflow from $0 to a business of approx. $90million in revenue, with staff of over 120 people in six offices in Australia and New Zealand. Dataflow focused on excellent customer services, a strong internal culture and the development of long-term partnerships with companies such as Microsoft, Symantec, Apple Computer, Logitech, Macromedia and Disney. Through strong leadership and focus, Dataflow was the major supplier to approx 85% of the retail and education software market in Australia.

  • Director of Research

    Australian Atomic Energy Commission

    (Research industry)

    January 1975 — January 1980 (5 years 1 month)

    Completed Ph.D thesis at AAEC, researching and developing a multiprocessor personal computer. Designed and built a multiprocessor desktop computer using a dialect of Modula-2
    Designed and developed the only real industry strength version of Pascal that would run on an IBM mainframe. This research was carried out in conjunction with the Computer Science Department of the University of Tokyo, and with the collaboration of Niklaus Wirth (of ETH, Switzerland, the original designer of the Pascal programming language).
    Conceived the idea of holding an international conference on Language Design and Programming Methodology, and organised this conference. Niklaus Wirth flew from Switzerland specifically to attend and deliver the keynote. The Proceedings of this conference were published by Springer Verlag as Volume 79 in their Lecture Notes in Computer Science series, Jeffrey Tobias being acknowledged as Conference Chairman
