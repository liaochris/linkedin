Andrew Weyrich’s Summary

I DO NOT ACCEPT RANDOM INVITES. WE HAVE TO HAVE DONE BUSINESS OR WORKED TOGETHER. PLEASE NO RECRUITERS OR SALES PEOPLE.

At heart and passion, I am a serial entrepreneur who combines the tri-fecta of business, technical and creative skills to build companies out of opportunities that I see in the U.S. business to consumer market. I have never taken the traditional path professionally since I came out of college. Since 22 years old I have started 4 Internet companies & 3 mobile service/smartphone app. companies. Some of my biggest business failures have been my greatest learning successes.

After 5 years and a large member milestone, one of my companies, http://CatholicDaters.com was acquired by a competitor in Oct of 2007. http://www.theregister.com/2007/10/10/dating_merger/

Another company of mine(http://www.PictureZoo.com) was the first to get funded by an angel investor where I had my own staff and office.

My three newest ventures that are launching before 2010 are HailCab, Inc. (http://www.HailCab.com), a way to catch a cab with no physical effort using your cell/smartphone. FreeYourCell Inc. (http://FreeYourCell.net) which is a way to get a free cell phone plan by allowing 15 second radio ads to replace your rings callers get when you do not answer your phone. And 'Parker the Parrot' (http://www.ParkParrot.com) which helps you remember where you parked your car and can even find you an empty parking space.

I love building companies. It's my passion. Please see my ideas & companies @ my company I call WE http://WeyrichEnterprises.com

  • CEO

    HailCab Inc.: Click and Ride. Catch a Cab with no effort from your Cell Phone or the web.

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    August 2009 — Present (3 months)

    HailCab is the best way to catch a taxi cab quickly, with no physical effort from the comfort of your cellphone, smartphone or the web! HailCab is a Location-Based-Service allowing anyone to Hail a Cab to his/her location and know exactly when it is arriving to the minute--no more wasting time walking aimlessly, yelling, waving your arms, standing in the cold or remembering cab company phone numbers. You can even lock in on a cab from inside the office or home and get an instant fare estimation, credit/debit card payment, e-receipts, time of travel, share-a-ride option, current traffic conditions and even real-time drink, dining and entertainment information all around the destination that you are headed to. Anyone with a smartphone or web access can use HailCab to view a map of nearby roaming cabs (green cabs are available, red cabs are occupied) and use the GPS or text messaging functinoality on their cell phones to bring the cab right to you 24/7. HailCab has over 25 features for the Rider and Cab Driver. Sign Up Now to Click and Ride!
