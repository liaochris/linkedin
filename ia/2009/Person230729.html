Responsibilities also included direct coordination with IBM Marketing, Legal, Contracting, Accounting, and Financial Services. Given the visibility of the account, I had constant interaction with IBM executives at the America’s levels.

    • Customer responsibilities included relationship management and selling activities at all levels of the USAA organization. This included monthly meetings with the CIO and CFO, as well as on-going interaction with USAA’s Legal staff, Senior Architects, and the Vendor Management and Acquisitions organizations.
