Pravin Lawrence Rajendran’s Summary

Pravin Rajendran worked as Senior Analyst - Real Estate, Construction and Cement for GCC Markets at NCB Capital. Prior to joining NCB Capital, Pravin worked as Investment Analyst at a global real estate fund. Pravin identified and analysed investment opportunities for the firm's Global and India Thematic Real Estate Public Equity Funds. He was promoted to member of the Global Investment Committee for strong equity research performance.

Pravin has completed the CFA Program, CFA Institute USA. He has an MBA in Finance from the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore, and is an Engineer in Computer Science from the College of Engineering Guindy, Anna University. Pravin is a certified Financial Risk Manager (FRM) - Global Association of Risk Professionals. He has also completed the Chartered Alternative Investment Analyst (CAIA) Level 1.
