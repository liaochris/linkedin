David Liddell’s Summary

It's all about delivering results. I founded SKYE Business Solutions with the primary focus of helping company leaders build better and more successful organizations. Without the results, I don't get invited back.

I spend a great deal of time helping leadership teams work more effectively and in alignment with their organization's strategy - provided one exists. The intent to produce high performing results is always present but too many teams let day to day activities get in the way of building great companies. I help them unlock this way of thinking.

The rest of my efforts include coaching, facilitating, assessing, training, and consulting to help my clients and their employees achieve their objectives and desired results faster then they would on there own.

In a few words.........

SKYE provides performance and management consulting solutions to help companies solve critical business issues, meet strategic goals, and increase corporate value by significantly improving the performance of people, systems and business processes.
