    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    I work in the Studio under Dinorah Coton, preparing projects for production. The work I do includes anything printed, from signs to brochures.   • Owner

    Zerflin

    (Design industry)

    May 1999 — Present (10 years 8 months)

    I began designing in 1999, and immediately saw the potential to grow the skill into a company.

    Originally called Blue Eclipse Design, I built up a portfolio by doing massive amounts of pro-bono work.

    Originally attending college as an Engineer, I switched to Graphic Design after his 2nd year. Barely having enough time to graduate on time, I kept the business going (adopting the name Zerflin in 2002) by matching up client work with class assignments and fitting work time between studies.

    I was inspired by working for free, and vowed to create a company that would not only be haven for other freelancing designers, but also always be dedicated to providing the best quality work for the client, regardless of the price.

    I am also the artist for all the artwork you see on the site.

    I was one of two lead graphic designers at National Fatherhood Initiative, under Art Director Paul McNally.
