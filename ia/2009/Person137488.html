Laura D Lewis’s Summary

My specialization is taking existing assets, intellectual property, and re-packaging it into new revenue streams with the involvement of corporate partners. I’ve outlined the 27-strategies used in my article The 27-platforms of Revenue Generating Marketing. Creating the essential triple play win for all in involved through the platforms is achieved by matching the needs of the buyer /viewer with the needs of the underwriting company and the media I work with to produce something inexpensively that addresses those needs while delivering high margins with high quality and exceptional satisfaction. Trend analysis and trend capitalization, the ability to read the market, understand historical needs and cycles while anticipating future demands is the key to creating successful products and services. Previous partners and employers have remarked that I seem to have a unique ability to call trends before they exist. Perhaps this is due to my insatiable appetite for history and learning; though technology may change, the basic needs and desires of people do not.

Negotiating high level partnerships at a senior level by creating win-win-win situations is how I succeeded in bringing in major corporate marketers and underwriters including Xerox, AST/Samsung, Exodus and Bell South into the fold at the Los Angeles Business Journal. With other companies I’ve put together online games, online/offline marketing partnerships and other strategic partnerships focused upon channel optimization.

With nearly 20-years experience in media covering broadcast and print and 13-years experience in the internet industry, ten spent running a successful advice and entertainment web portal for people in relationships, I bring extensive knowledge in consumer behavior, psychology, online behavior and channel development and marketing.
