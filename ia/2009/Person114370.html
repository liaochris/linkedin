WHAT MOTIVATES AND INSPIRES ME, WHAT I LOVE TO DO

In much the same way as an orchestra conductor is passionate with beauty and perfection and gets the best of his musicians by personally understanding, caring and inspiring each one; in much the same way I am passionate with teamwork and all-things-engineering from nuts-and-bolts to management.

QUALITIES

Strong ethical principles and values of honesty and integrity

Strong ability to plan and organize work, coordinate and complete multiple tasks, deal with problems, manage resources, make decisions. Special intuition to anticipate potential risks and timing issues.

Experienced in supervising team members, liaise with colleagues, negotiate, lead and participate in team work. Special ability to make and maintain friendly professional and personal relationships with colleagues.

Natural ability to write complete and concise letters and reports, talk to large and small audiences.

Used to looking at things with creativity. Special ability to organize and interpret vast amounts of complex material, and grasp patterns.

Hard working, with lots of initiative, motivation and autonomy. Special ability to work on extended and difficult pieces of work, with tenacity and patience.

Flexible and multi-skilled, fast learner. Able to handle a wide range of assignments.

BELIEFS

I picture my role of project manager more like a captain of a sports team, on the field playing the game and leading by example.

Relish helping my team by inspiring, facilitating, empowering, promoting, recognizing.

Foster team relationships based on confidence, commitment and openness, with an attitude of enjoyment and gratitude to tasks.

WHAT I HAVE BEEN DOING

Senior Instrumentation & Control Engineering Professional with 16 years of experience and successful record of achievement as Site Manager, Project Manager, Instrument & DCS Engineer in large-scale greenfield and brownfield projects in refining and pulp & paper.
