Erin Johnson’s Summary

I am an Entrepreneur at heart. I have a passion for the life skills that can be learned through team sports and have grown up playing soccer. I believe that all children should have the opportunity to play soccer and therefore have created Soccer Kids of America to introduce as many at-risk children to soccer as possible and teach them valuable life skills in a fun environment.
