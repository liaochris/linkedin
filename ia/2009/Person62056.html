Derek Johnston’s Summary

Derek is an experienced project manager and has over 23 years experience in the Information Technology industry (and a number in the movie industry (SFX)), covering a wide range of clients in both the private and public sector. Derek has spent the last twelve years in management consultancy, utilising his project management, communication, analysis and change management skills to help businesses do business better, more efficiently or achieve their specific goals on time and on budget without jeopardising quality.

Derek's professional experience has seen him contributing to published articles, while his breath of work includes IS strategy, programme management, ERP and systems implementation, business analysis and change, systems specification, process reviews, systems selections, data migration and cleansing, communications strategies, event management.

Derek’s technical ability along with his ability to move from high level to detail level work with ease, allied with an appreciation of business requirements (including accounting knowledge) and effective people management skills keeps projects moving forward.

His key strengths are his adaptability, dealing with people (at all levels) and mentoring client staff through periods of change, together with his ability to translate business requirements into systems needs and strategies into tactical solutions, enabling organisations to move from where they are to where they want to be. His practical and pragmatic approach enables him to be a good project integrator and stream/ team leader, providing valuable liaison between clients and system suppliers, ensuring the requirements are clearly defined and business objectives are met.

Whilst remaining professional, focused at all times and inspiring confidence that he will deliver, he is able to balance that with a personable approach to work. Derek has repeatedly gained recognition from clients, authors and colleagues for this attitude.
