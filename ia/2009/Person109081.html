Jennifer Dalitz’s Summary

Jen Dalitz helps people to get real and get results in their business and life. As a business consultant, farmer, author and speaker on change and business management, Jen’s a rainmaker in every sense: she knows how to create something from nothing!

As the founder and SheEO™ of sphinxx – a community dedicated to advancing women as leaders – Jen is also an expert on the issues women face in forging their careers. She is the author of "Little Wins for Working Women" and a contributing author of "Ideas Volume 3 - Original perspectives on life and business from leading thinkers".

Jen’s clients have described her as practical, refreshing and informative. She bridges the divides between industries, genders, generations and cultures by sharing case studies and stories from her 15-year career in finance and consulting and the lessons she learned growing up on the land.

Having led complex business units, major projects, customer service teams and change programs in a number of Fortune 500 companies, Jen brings a depth of experience and credibility to the business topics on which she presents and consults. She is a Certified Practicing Accountant and holds a Bachelor of Arts (Accountancy) and a Master of Business Administration.
