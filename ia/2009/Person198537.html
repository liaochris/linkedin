Rick George’s Summary

Rick George has 10 years of Information Technology working in the Telecommunications Industry and 6 years working in the Medical Industry, with 11 years in Computer Operations. Rick has worked in all aspects of the software development lifecycle, most recently concentrating in software testing and software quality assurance. Specifically as IT Test Coordinator/Lead; Rick has experience in creating test plans from business, functional, and system requirements, subsequently applying the Detailed Test Conditions to Mercury Test Director 8.0. Rick’s experience also includes executing the System DTC, tracking progress, monitoring status of defects and re-testing defects.
