Paul Ramady’s Summary

I am responsible for the recruitment of both the Microsoft and SAP Recruitment Divisions at ERP Resourcing.

The Microsoft Division recruits for MS Dynamics NAV, AX, GP, CRM and RMS; MS SharePoint and .NET Professionals.

The SAP Division recruits for Professionals specialsing in CO, FI, FS, IS, HCM, HR, LE, PLM, PM, MM, QM, PP, SCM, SRM, BW, SEM, VM and SAP CRM modules as well as SAP Business One.
