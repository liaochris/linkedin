Jonathan Strong’s Summary

Extensive track record of innovation and success identifying and applying the right technologies, methodologies and management approach to solve business challenges great and small.

Senior IT Executive – climbed the ranks to become CTO for financial services firm, CIO for market data startup, VP Technology for health care services company, Director of management consulting for global firm, Regional Projects Director with P&L responsibility for $40 million budget for global consulting firm, president of consulting firm providing support to Fortune 100 and 500. Report to and/or collaborate with C-level executives in each instance.

Hands on Innovator – Bring new approaches and solutions to every challenge. Evaluated and redesigned product and enterprise architecture as well as processes and standards at virtually every job, aligning technology and business. Inventor of BI product at RCA that became a standard at GE, later spun off as Cognos/IBM product. Invented risk architecture for major bank identifying $100 million in bad debt. Designed online gaming community and user experience ranked #1 on Internet in repeat usage by Comscore Media Metrix. Consistent world class results.

Transformational Leader – Quickly assess risks and weaknesses, establishing order, rigor and sustainable success. For Telcordia, directed project “rescue” of online forms architecture and an online purchasing system, allowing it to begin processing $35 million monthly, eliminating 100 paper forms and reducing time and expense by 30%. For national student loan company, moved technology and operations from “ad hoc” to structured and repeatable (CMMi level 3) in eighteen months, allowing the company to pursue a $1 billion loan portfolio while achieving a 200% ROI on technology spend in less than 2 years. Doubled ROI on a $40 million consulting practice in 18 months.
