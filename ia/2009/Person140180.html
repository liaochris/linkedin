Leslie Forman’s Summary

I’m a San Francisco native, and I’ve lived in China for more than two years.

At Yaxley Education, I manage tailored training programs. Working closely with corporate leaders, we create courses to build the communication skills needed to succeed within each company. Our recent projects include the following:

- Training wind turbine engineers across China in industry-specific English

- Serving as a sounding board for a patent attorney preparing to speak at a conference in Europe

- Teaching reporters for an energy publication to write precisely about market fluctuations

- Tutoring an African diplomat in professional conversational English

- Providing customer service training to an international insurance company’s call center team

I enjoy this work because it gives me the opportunity to learn about many fields, in a rather personal way.

My Chinese boss once asked how I learned to write. I pointed to three phases of my formal education: as a "literary lawyer" in high school, as a student of Spanish, and as a workshop participant creating personal poetry. I think my broad education makes me a good teacher.

I'm also an environmental enthusiast. I'm particularly interested in the ways that environmental ideas spread through society, through both top-down policy and grass-roots action.

I love traveling, good food with good friends, and events that involve costumes.

If your interests intersect with mine, drop me a line and let’s connect.
