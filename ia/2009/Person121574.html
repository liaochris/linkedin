Joshua Tree Retreat Center’s Summary

The Joshua Tree Retreat Center is the oldest and largest retreat center in the Western United States; a unique architectural landmark on a 420 acre sacred site. We are a non-profit 501c(3), whose mission is to support the education and nourishment of the infinite human potential.

This land, though desert, rests upon an aquifer, a running river, producing an intriguing magnetic effect on those who enter this ancient and sacred space. Yogananda had walked this land in earlier times.

Most of the unique buildings at our site were built by world-renowned architects, Frank Lloyd Wright and his son, Lloyd Wright.

Joshua Tree Retreat Center is unlike the more commercially oriented Hotels. We have created open spaces, sacred meditation locations, and serene vistas with plenty of wonderful spots to explore...here are a just few:
* An outdoor amphitheater with earthen stage, lights and fire pit for outdoor activities and wonderful evening events under the stars.
* We have accommodations for groups of various sizes (min.15-278 max.). Each group have their own eating areas and kitchens
* Large and small meeting spaces with many breakout rooms and beautiful views. (from 20 in smallest space to 500 in our largest space
* Meditation paths, Labyrinth, Medicine Wheel, and Tai Chi circles, pool, warm pool, sauna.
* Local musicians are available for drum circles or concerts
that can be set up for your group's evening entertainment (CD samples are available upon request)
*Local meditation, yoga and tai chi instructors are also available for instruction
* Wellness Center: We offer massage services on site by some of the finest technicians in the country (preferably by appointment)
* Swimming pool, warm pool, sauna, steam room, and sand volleyball court, will be available end of 2009
* Traditional Lakota Sweat Lodge (as well as a facilitator and fire keeper available with a donation) (2 sweat lodges - 30 & 60+)

Joshua Tree Retreat Center’s Specialties:

We are located approximately 30 minutes from Palm Springs International Airport, and 1½ hours from Ontario Airport. Our high desert (3000 ft) locale boasts wonderfully cool evenings even during the warmest of seasons.

Joshua Tree Retreat Center has been a sacred vessel, a magnifier for the works of many world-renowned teachers. Our Sanctuary and Noble Hall have been visited by some of the finest authors, lecturers and organizations to date.
