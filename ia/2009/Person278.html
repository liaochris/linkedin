Steve Kaye’s Summary

My goal is to make leaders more effective.

Better meetings will gain a full day of work each week at no extra cost (because people waste 20% of their time in bad meetings).

Better presentations will inspire people to take action (instead of bore, offend, or upset them).

Better communication will facilitate excellence (and avoid most of the problems that consume a manager's time).

Since 1992 my workshops have shown leaders modern techniques that are easy to apply.

And my meeting facilitation always achieves results that others will support.

I've written three books, published over 800 hundred articles and blogs, and appeared on radio and TV.
