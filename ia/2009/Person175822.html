Nina Alag Suri’s Summary

Over 20 years of work experience. Started my career with ICIM-Fujitsu (an ICL company) in selling hardware and system integration solutions in India (Delhi). Moved on to IIS Infotech (now Xansa)as Business Manager for the Corporate Education division , based out of Delhi. Setup my own recruitment firm called Nastrac India in 1997 to provide niche recruitment solutions to the IT and Telecom industry. Expanded operations to Finland in 1999 to setup Nastrac in Finland. Setup the UK entity- Nastrac Ltd,UK in 2000. Early 2004 formed a joint venture with the London based Contact Recruitment to jointly expand business in India, APAC, UK, Europe. Now a Managing Director, Asia-Pacific and based in Singapore.

Highlights: startedup 5 operations from ground zero and made each one of them breakeven in 6 months or lesser time. Strong experience in setting up and building international business, understand Asia, Asia-Pacific, UK and Europe markets.
