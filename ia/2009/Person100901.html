Jack Grimes’s Summary

I'm a specialist .Net supplier to the Netherlands and have been for the last seven years, I have a team of seven .Net specialist consultants in Amsterdam, who are specialist in (.Net, C#, VB, Silverlight, C++, SQL, MySQL, Developers, Programmers, and Architects etc).

Based in Amsterdam, I work with many clients throughout the region that use Eurostaff to find highly skilled .Net resources (Dutch or English speakers). Our network of available local resources is impressive and we are keen to prove this to you. We know the best freelancers, and more importantly, when they are available.

We select our freelancers very carefully, making sure they are highly relevant for the role and fully committed to your projects.

Eurostaff was established in 2004 and has grown year on year to become a market leading supplier of niche skills within the IT and Finance domains. We are a Pan-European agency and focus on markets in Netherlands, Germany, Benelux, Switzerland, France and the UK. I am currently leading the .Net specialist in the Netherlands I represent highly reputable companies within the industry.

Eurostaff is divided in different teams specialised in various IT areas (CRM, EAI, Development, Architecture, Infrastructure, Project Management, Telco…). Also, I invite you to visit our website for a full breakdown of the range of services we can offer.

I would be interested to replicate the success that I have had with these companies and help you with filling any external requirements that you require now and in the future. I am confident that with our broad Pan-European network of highly skilled individuals we are well placed to service your needs.

If there is anything further that you wish to discuss or clarify, please feel free to give me a call or email at any time!
