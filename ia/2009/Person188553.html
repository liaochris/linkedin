David Coley’s Summary

Since leaving University I have worked within APEhire, and have focused primarily on the latest innovations in piling equipment. With the company focused primarily centring around small pile driving equipment, for trench sheeting and plastic piling, this has become the main focus of my work. As a consequence of this experience a new company has been created Aldridge Piling Equipment (Manufacturing) Co Limited. APEman, trades under the name of APE Plastic Piling at present and host the widest choice of plastic sheet piling from leading suppliers around the world, including Creative Pultrusion SuperLoc composites, Profextru's very novel hybrids MultiLock and ProLock, Formtech inc very innovative box pile the Truline Series 800, not to mention the high quality traditional products from Pietrucha.

Added to this range are some very novel pile driving equipment, including the new Movax Lite range from Unisto Oy in Finland. This is a new design of Movax directed towards the installation of Plastic Sheet piling.

Work also continues on the APEX, this perhaps will be seen at some point as my life's work, a unique product that impacts or vibrates. Currently with my primary focus on plastic piling and linked web promotion this development is currently resting!
