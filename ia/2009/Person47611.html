Richard Clayman’s Summary

Thirty years as producer, director, writer, and actor for all major TV networks, most major studios. My goal: position Cloudwalker Videoworks as the top supplier of network-quality, affordable, high-impact website videos, local TV ads, training films, fundraisers, and more for small/medium-sized businesses. Looking to network with companies which have a synergy with my own, such as website designers and marketing firms, to create mutually beneficial business opportunities.

Richard Clayman’s Specialties:

Directing, Producing, Writing, for TV, film, and stage.
