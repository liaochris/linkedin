Matt Simpson’s Summary

Words are my passion, but it’s my analytical mind and eye for the big picture that separate me from the pack.

My specialty is translating an organization’s unique value proposition into differentiating communications and collateral. I’m also versed in creating transactional copy that drives results in direct response campaigns. And I’ve broadened beyond simply writing—leading creative projects in a Fortune 500 environment and experimenting with social media and online marketing as founder of a startup website.

Words are my passion, but words accomplish nothing if not backed by the right strategy. I not only offer the words; I offer the strategy behind them.
