Kurt Krause’s Summary

These days, brand differentiation and brand recognition are the keys to success. If you’re running a business, all channels of communication must be considered. In today’s media savvy marketplace, how the world sees your brand or product is more important than ever.

The old adage is true: you only have one chance to make a first impression. People only trust what they understand. If the message is unclear or confusing they will not respond to you. Brand consistency – the ability to immediately recognize any marketing message as uniquely yours — is essential.

Welcome to Krause Creative Advertising + Design. We’ve built an entire business out of strengthening our customers’ brand recognition. We are a full-service design, image branding and design firm dedicated to effective, well-conceived marketing, advertising and brand development. The biggest challenge in today’s marketplace is being seen and remembered. At Krause Creative, we have years of experience in creating marketing messages that work, advertising images that impress and branding strategies that stick.

Krause Creative has been in business for eight years as an independent agency focusing on developing and executing creative visions with creative solutions. In addition, the principal, Kurt Krause has been in graphic design, art direction and creative management positions for 18 years.

His expertise is in applying concepts and branding to practices and methods of graphic design and marketing communications. The result is graphic productions, from books and brochures to websites and advertisements that reflect the client’s message, and draw the reader to respond to the message. His strong conceptual, analytical and problem solving skills are unique among graphic design firms. While our core competency is brand communication to convey a message, we realize that only a well organized and managed project will achieve the best results.
