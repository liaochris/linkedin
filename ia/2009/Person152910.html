Mark van Rossem’s Summary

I am an experienced internet entrepeneur serving large accounts with consultancy in the following areas of expertise: online concept and strategy, online branding, e-business, e-commerce, online marketing, online CRM.

I have worked for a wide variety of brands:
Amstel beer, Atos Origin, Beertender, BNR Newsradio, Brand beer, Campina, Delta Lloyd, Het Financieele Dagblad, Heineken, ING Bank, Laurus, Menzis, Nationale Nederlanden, Pepsi, Philips Corporate, Philips Lighting, SNS Reaal, Super de Boer, Vrumona, Wessanen/ Zonnatura.

Typical projects are:
Developing online concepts and strategy, Website (re)design, Development of a online community, Online loyalty programs including CRM strategy, Corporate website, Intranet, B2B portals, Online advertising campaigns.

Mark van Rossem’s Specialties:

General : brand strategy, marketing and advertising, mobile, corporate identity, account management, project management, team management, training, coaching.

Creative: Interaction-design, graphic design, webdesign.

Technical: Information architecture, functional design.
