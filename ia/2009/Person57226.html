David de Weerdt’s Summary

David de Weerdt is a business and social entrepreneur. He has a long-standing interest in strategy and team-building to achieve motivating goals in business and community. His past vocations include CEO of a rural ISP, professional sales of IT & telecommunications solutions, GM of a local economic development corporation, magazine editor and publisher, educational foundation president, commercial real estate development, in addition to numerous roles in community service.
