Varun Alagati’s Summary

As an Electronics and Telecommunications engineer from Swami Vivekananda College (VESIT, Mumbai), Varun had a successful three years stint at Wipro Technologies where he worked as Project Engineer in Telecom and Embedded Systems divisions. Varun's responsibilities included client interaction, software development and software testing. He has been awarded with “Feather In My Cap” and “Thanks A Zillion” appreciation certificates. In his tenure, he has also learnt German and Spanish. After this, he worked for a consulting firm called Asri Technology in the role of a Knowledge Consultant. His main responsibilities lay in project implementation, consulting and training for IBM platforms and tools like Rational Clearcase and ClearQuest. He loves teaching children and is a career counselor at his alma mater. He loves reading, listening to music, and performs voluntary work for Pratham.
