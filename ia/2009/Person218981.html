Richard Evans’s Summary

Skydd is a TV and Radio post production company nestled in the middle of the buzzy Soho Recording Studios off Tottenham Court Road.

Both of Skydd's studios are based around Pro Tools HD systems and have good sized voice booths.

The aim of Skydd is simple. Provide the best quality audio possible without the heart-wrenching rates of some of the other post houses in Soho.

You may have to make your own cup of tea but I promise you'll walk away happy.

For details and more examples of my work please visit www.skydd.co.uk
