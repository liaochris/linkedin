Myra Sangawong’s Summary

Myra SaNgawong, was born in Brooklyn Heights,CA. And raised in BKK.I attended school here.I like the arts, fashion and jewelry. I am currently based in BKK,THAILAND.

I also create accessories with a twist of art.I strive to be different from the rest of the beaders.I strive to put art to every piece me create.I dare my minds to think out-of-the-box and execute my designs with utmost craftmanship.

I'm love to creates wearable art pieces.And always make sure that every piece I create have aesthetic value. This is what makes our accessories different from the rest!

I started my interest in art and design at College of Fine Arts.After that in 1994, I continued studying Advertising at Saun Sunandha University.During studying in university, I also attended the contest and got the awards from organizations in Thailand.

In 1998, graduated from Saun Sunandha University and joined with Board Casting Network (Thailand )company as a Production Creative. And also set up a group to work as a freelance creating product and design works - gifts and souvenirs, and jewelry etc.products for companies.
