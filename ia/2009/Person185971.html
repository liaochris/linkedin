Peter Oberdorfer’s Summary

I manage and supervise the artists at Giant Killer Robots, a visual effects production studio that has grown over the past decade from several artists into a facility of up to 85 artists and production staff. I am the lead creative for the studio, and have participated in every aspect of the productions we have worked on; from bidding, testing, conceptual development, on set supervision, technical direction, compositing, animation, pitching creative concepts and acting as the creative liaison with our clients. I serve as the primary lead for many of the largest shows that we take on, and make sure that all the work in the studio meets a high aesthetic and creative standard.

I am interested in finding new opportunities in visual effects, animation, or design that emphasize creative and collaborative opportunities, and exploit my talents as a creative and as a supervisor.
