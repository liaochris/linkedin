John King’s Summary

I'm a composer. I write music and LOVE to do it. My goal is to write music for you. From marching band to film score, whatever you do that needs music, I want to earn that business.

I've written for many live ensembles as well as produced realistic recordings using only a fully loaded MacPro and several leading sample libraries.

I was honored to receive third place in the 2006 Turner Classic Movies Young Film Composers Competition in Los Angeles, CA. In April of 2005 I won an international orchestration competition hosted by the Vienna Symphonic Library.

I currently spend much of my day creating recordings for Marching Show Concepts and Center X Productions and writing original music for a variety of uses.

John King’s Specialties:

I specialize in creating realistic recording of orchestras using a computer. I have extensive experience working with complex audio setup on Macs. I have experience creating, editing and producing written scores.
