Jas Kaur’s Summary

Prior to joining Lavalife as a Director of Partner Relations, Ms. Kaur was responsible for meeting performance-based revenue targets for Yahoo Canada. Part of her mandate was also to manage and implement strategic partnerships for Yahoo Canada.

Ms. Kaur also spent 5 years at MSN/Microsoft Canada where she built, launched and managed the English and French online shopping channels. She consistently helped her clients achieve higher revenue year over year through effective merchandising and online promotions.

Prior to MSN, Ms. Kaur worked at eHarlequin.com, Accessory Network group and in the traditional retail sector. Her previous achievements included increasing sales by over 40% for a national retailer by optimizing performance on various commodities, creating marketing strategies, and ensuring effective working relationships with merchandisers, suppliers, and sales teams.

In her current role as Director of Partner Relationships at Lavalife, she is responsible for developing and executing an online and mobile integration strategy for each strategic partner with the primary objective of acquiring traffic/unique users and converting these users to free and paid members.
