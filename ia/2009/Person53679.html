Daniel Benitez’s Summary

For the first seven years of my career I was an accounting professional in public accounting and law firms. These were great expeiences that allowed me to work closely with people such as Mike Bloomberg and Bill Wachtel. One night while working for a public accounting firm and trying to find a client's general ledger error of $36.00, It hit me that I wanted to do something else, something more. I met with a true professional recruiter by the name of Allen Williams and I never looked back.

That was in 1996 and I can say with no question it worked out great for me. First and foremost, I love being a recruiter for Accounting and Finance professionals. As an accountant in another life I know and feel the wounds of both the job seeker and the employer. Also, I have a great ability to listen to what people are saying and help point them in the right direction.

As of 2006 I started a company with my two partners, Armando Benitez and Anthony Rodriguez called TC & Associates, Inc. I continue to do what I love, recruiting / staffing but get to be my own boss.

Daniel Benitez’s Specialties:

Consulting and training in sales, marketing, recruiting, placement, and deal closing skills for Staffing / Recruiting, Human Resources, and Hiring professionals. Strong knowledge of employee retention, screening skills and employment laws.
