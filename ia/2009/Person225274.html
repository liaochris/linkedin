My entire career has been in the "people business." My #1 objective has always revolved around recruiting and business development, regardless of the industry or location.

I am most excited about my current role with Staffing Now as it affords me the opportunity to work with an established team of colleagues and managers, while taking my previous experiences and refining them to create a win-win-win (maybe even another win!) for all involved.

While developing and maintaining relationships (previous, new, and former), I am interested in gaining new knowledge and perspective from every possible interaction.
