Ali Asghar Malik’s Summary

MS in Computer Science and also has several international certifications by Microsoft, Sun Microsystems and IBM. It includes MCP, MCAD, SCJP, SCWCD and IBM Certified OOAD

Founder & CEO of eVentureSolutions (EVS) Private Limited
Managing Trustee of Operation Badar (Trust), the largest IT educational movement in history of Pakistan.

Conducted several seminars under Operation Badar’s flag in various universities of Pakistan such as PUCIT, UET, LCW, SSUET, APIIT in different area of information technology

Conducted 200+ training courses and workshops in different areas of information technologies & technical team & project management.

Designed & supervised development of several successful mega software projects such as “RecruitmentPoint”, a web portal for consortium of employment agencies, “TeleBilling”, a whole sale online billing system for telecom sector, “ClaimsManagementSystem”, a claim management system for National Accountability Bureau, Punjab.

Envisioned, designed & supervised development of several software products e.g. “RetailManager”, an enterprise level chain of retail store management system. “AlertsManager”, software to send timely sms, email & voice alerts to subscribers, “AcademiaManager”, software to manage operations of academic institutes.
