Efrem R. Jasso’s Summary

I am the lead Sales Coach and IT Project Manager for Sparque, Inc.

Sparque is The Power Source for Revenue Generation. With a focus on front line sales managers, Sparque brings revolutionary energy and sales behavior changes to organizations who want top line revenue growth.

We think about and implement revenue generation and provide resources to business-to-business sales organizations in totally new ways.

This new methodology is not your historic classroom-type lecture, conference room meeting, or typical Friday sales meeting.

We give the Sales Manager the resources to provide productive, engaging sales meetings in a Web 2.0 world.

Sparque provides the structure and content needed to implement this approach. Sparque’s methodologies stand out and stand strong in a weak economy, and helped sellers to close over $20 million in new business in 2008 (in a down economy!) alone.

Sparque provides quick, to-the-point training materials and methodologies that will get into the field in record time. Gone are the days of endless Power Point slides, shown in dark rooms for hours on end. Sparque works best with companies who want sales behavior changes and want them now.
