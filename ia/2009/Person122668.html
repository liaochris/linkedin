Justin Stark’s Summary

For over five years, I have been instrumental in establishing Digital Artist Management and DAM Consultants (DAM) as the premium permanent and temporary contract staffing resource for companies that create digital content and technology for feature films, television, video games and online new media/web2.0. We are always looking to bring on new clients that can benefit from our robust network of talent. Additionally, DAM is constantly looking to assist artists, designers, software engineers and producers find and explore new employment opportunities within these industries.

As of Jan 2009, I've assumed responsibility over the business development efforts of DAM's sister company called Production Road. Where DAM provides creative and technical individuals for job placement, Production Road brings full development teams to the attention of games or VFX content developers looking for outsourcing assistance.

Justin Stark’s Specialties:

Games, VFX and Web 2.0 recruiting; outsourcing; project management; rapid prototyping; art asset creation; IP development
