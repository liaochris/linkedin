Dave Stacey’s Summary

Experienced Sr. Manager with focus on Rich Media & Video. Currently Managing the Video In-Stream team at Google (DoubleClick) involved with ad serving technology solutions (DART Rich Media / Motif), in-stream video, Flash-in-Flash and other emerging technologies. Responsible for day-to-day management, working with clients from start-ups to blue chips, able to motivate and oversee staff. Self Motivated, flexible in approach and able to work to tight deadlines.

Experience developing ECMAScript apps, consultancy services, custom interactive Rich Media application development, motion graphics, streaming Rich Media, and Video for digital collateral such as DVDs, desktop apps, microsites, and ads used in the development and implementation of B2B campaigns for the advertising, marketing and education industries.

Dave Stacey’s Specialties:

Flash, ActionScript, object oriented programming, designing classes & components, Rich Media applications, working with audio/video, animation, GUI design, project management, scope planning, estimating duration, scheduling, organizing resources, directing team, monitoring and controlling progress, assessing results and experiences.
