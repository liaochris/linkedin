Ian Jackman’s Summary

Ian Jackman, an Industrial Chemist, is a business development director: environment, soil & groundwater for Eurofins Environmental Services in UK and Ireland - part of Eurofins Scientific - the leading analytical laboratory services group.

The company specialises in analytical services for soil and groundwater for contaminated land site investigations and remediation, groundwater protection, effluent water discharge license compliance, sampling services - as well a drinking water, process water, microbiological testing.

Ian has a background in analytical chemistry, industrial chemical formulation for drilling fluids, cementitious and chemical grouting products and services, and for the past 10 years has gained extensive experience in sales and services for environmental sampling, testing, monitoring and chemistry.

More recently Ian has been involved in the management of CPA Laboratories - a leading asbestos and dust testing laboratory in the UK - with sampling, air monitoring services, and COSHH/EPA investigations.

A desire to forge new relationships with dynamic business people willing to explore new ideas and better ways to engage laboratory services or outsource functions that will make a significant difference to their business.

Ian Jackman’s Specialties:

Environmental chemical analysis of soil & groundwater for contaminated land - site investigation, risk assessment, due diligence, auditing, remediation, groundwater protection. Laboratory services, support, outsourcing and related advice and consultancy. Asbestos, fibres and dust analysis. Account management.
