Jojo Mathew George’s Summary

Currently, the Academic Coordinator of IBS City Campus, Hyderabad, as well as a Faculty Member in Economics. Was earlier a Senior Policy Analyst and the Coordinator of the Centre for Policy Studies at the Icfai School of Public Policy, Hyderabad, while also teaching Economics and Sustainable Development for the Master in Public Policy (MPP) as well as the pre-PhD programs of the Icfai School of Public Policy. Masters and M.Phil in Economics from the (Central) University of Hyderabad after completing undergraduate studies from the Madras Christian College, Chennai. In addition, a Post Graduate Diploma in Environmental Economics (PGDEE) from the University of Hyderabad in association with the International Institute for Industrial Environmental Economics (IIIEE), Lund University, Sweden and certified by the European Union. Areas of interest include Development Economics, Environmental Economics, Economics of Science and Technology, Law and Economics and International Economics. Qualified the National Education Test (NET) of the University Grants Commission in December 2001.

Before joining the Icfai School of Public Policy, worked as a consultant on Cost-Benefit Analysis for the Integrated Environmental Strategies (IES) – India Project on pollution control scenarios for Hyderabad, co-sponsored by the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) and the United States Environmental Protection Agency (USEPA), and coordinated by the Environmental Protection Training and Research Institute (EPTRI), Hyderabad. Was also associated with institutions like the Icfai Center for Management Research and the International Institute for Information Technology (IIIT), Hyderabad. Has co-authored a book titled 'Issues in Public Finance: The Indian Experience' published by the Icfai University Press in 2006.

Education management, Teaching and training, writing and editing, research and consultancy, Non-profits.
