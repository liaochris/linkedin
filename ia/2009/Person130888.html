Kevin Fjelsted’s Summary

Kevin Fjelsted helps business owners senior managers, and project leaders meet the challenges of today’s constantly changing technology. Kevin provides a wide range of systems consulting and programming services:

 Assists managers in understanding the role of technology in their particular business.
 Assists them in incorporating what they need for the future into what they already have.
 Assists them in migrating to new technologies that can improve their efficiency and better serve their clients.
 Then, as a strategic systems architect and hands on software developer, Kevin puts it all together, ensuring the new system and software work exactly as planned.

For the last 30 years, Kevin’s work has been on the cutting edge of systems and software development. His background includes nine years in executive management as well as solid experience in all aspects of product development: specification, estimating, programming, testing, consulting, installation, and budgeting.

This breadth of experience allows him to bring a unique perspective as well as an unmatched level of expertise to each project. Perhaps the most important point about Kevin’s background is simply this: his entire career has been focused on implementing solutions that meet the customer’s needs and requirements.

He has worked with teams composed of world-renowned masters, including Jerry Weinberg and Norm Kerth, with expertise in systems architecture, organizational systems thinking, and software engineering. These collaborations have permitted Kevin to learn, shape, and influence many aspects of the thinking that has gone into the art and science of software development and consulting. A leader in his field, he has held committee positions in the Data Processing Management Association and the Institute for Electronics and Electrical Engineering Computer Society.

Kevin Fjelsted’s Specialties:

Systems Architecture, Technology assessment, Software Engineering, Infrastructure implementation, Software Engineering Management, telecommunications systems implementation, CTI implementation, VOIPimplementation, unified messaging
