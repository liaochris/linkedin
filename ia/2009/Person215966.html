Shawn Santos’s Summary

I am interested in advancing technology and tech services, clean technologies, sustainable energy, unique applications of social media, and innovations in the US wine industry.

In my current role as Director of Programs with SSPA / Point B, inc, I am focusing on program portfolio strategy and management, product marketing, market research, enhancing communication channels particularly around Web 2.0, resource planning, and producing insightful content for the technology services industry.

As Product Marketing Manager within the test and measurement division of Agilent Technologies, I focused on positioning, pricing, promoting and placing global portfolios of tech services products.

In my role as product manager with Enologix (industry leader in wine information systems, research and consulting), I led my staff to package and “productize” wine industry information services--this software has become their core product line.

While at San Francisco-based Global Vintage, I was resonsible for the conception and launch the core product line of market research reports, as well as "Global Vintage Quarterly" and globalvintage.com a popular wine consumer magazine and website.

In addition to my background with several complex consulting engagements in the wine, technology and marketing agency industries, I have also held winemaking positions in California and France.

I have a B.S. degree in Molecular Biology from the University of California, and have extensive executive education, most recently completing the Technology Services Management Program through the Wharton School of Business.
