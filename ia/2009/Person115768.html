Joel Libava’s Summary

Joel Libava, Franchise Selection Specialists Inc. 2nd generation President, brings real world franchise industry experience to his clients, coming from a franchise management background in the automobile and restaurant/hospitality industries.

Joel works with folks in Northeast Ohio that are in "career pain," and who want to explore owning their own business. His franchise matchmaking services are FREE.

Joel continues to bring new businesses into Northeast Ohio, that translate into new jobs, which are badly needed here.

Joel is on the Key Entrepreneurial Development Center Taskforce, and an early member of The Cleveland Social Media Club. His presentations on franchising and small business throughout Northeast Ohio are energizing, and factual.

Joel Libava’s Specialties:

Advise transitioning middle managers and corporate executives who are exploring careers in franchise ownership, as opposed to more of the same in corporate America. Master consultant and franchise coach.

"The Franchise Expert In Residence" for Anita Campbell's award winning and highly influential Small Business Trends website, as well as the franchise columnist at American Express's OPEN FORUM website.
