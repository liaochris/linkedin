Steve O'Neil’s Summary

My main focus is Pre-Employment Screening & Vetting solutions operated on a Country, Regional or Global basis. Working with a wide range of market leading companies across all business sectors, I am always interested in networking with people and organisations interested in Screening, Referencing and Vetting or alternatively anyone involved in hiring, recruitment, recruitment process outsourcing (RPO), business process outsourcing (BPO) or Human Resources.

My ambition is grow with The Security Watchdog Ltd as it becomes the recognised dominant global provider of pre-employment screening & vetting solutions.
