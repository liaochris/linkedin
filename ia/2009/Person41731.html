Charly Lippoth’s Summary

C-level executive with 10 years experience in past-paced consumer software and technology environment with an impeccable track record in sales and product development. My career at Nero has taken me through just about every facet that technology companies (that quickly grow from a founder driven team of 10 to over 400) have to offer. Primarily engaged in sales, I soon interacted in support, QA, marketing, product marketing , PR, event management, product management, product design, localization, UI design, feature design in the first 2 years. My focus shifted to OEM sales (first w/w, then homing in on first tier accounts in APAC) and product management, along with being the lead product evangelist. Moved to Japan in 2004 to start Nero’s APAC office from scratch with full P/L responsibility and overlooked HR, finance, support, sales, marketing and PR. Shifted gears to a corporate role as CTO and then additionally as Chief Strategy Officer of the Nero group.

I have a stong affection for young, underdog, fast paced companies that have unique products and technologies, want to rise to the challenge and make a difference.
