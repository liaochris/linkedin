Nayan Bheda’s Summary

Nayan Bheda (NB) has been a serial entrepreneur all thru his career, pioneering some of the 1st in the industries he worked. He did his masters in Marketing & Int'l Business.

NB heads Special Projects at Suvidhaa. He is also involved in telcos in Aus & UK. NB advises to Emerging Business ventures providing a strong foundation for them to build lasting value.

NB has been invited for his talks on variety of Mgmt topics.He has been often Quoted/Interviewed/Profiled by Media. He has also published thought-provoking articles in Times of India & leading business mags. Acclaimed as Guru of Emerging Business, NB has also advised some reputed projects. Having traveled to many countries, NB has acquired much needed int'l exposure.

Starting his career in ’87 as S/W Developer, he moved up the value chain by adopting emerging technologies like Multimedia (93) & Internet (95). He is still acknowledged as pioneer who popularized these technologies in India.

In ’97 NB developed 1st touch screen kiosk in India giving way for his next venture, ESP Interactive Ltd which went on to become expert in building Self-service-TRX-Terminals. ESP become market leader pioneering many concepts in the business including country’s first bill payment m/c attracting customers like Citibank & ICICI bank. After ESP was acquired by an US based co, NB was invited to setup the kiosk division for Intellvisions S/W Ltd (ISL). Today ISL is a leader in Kiosks & acknowledges NB as Father of Interactive Kiosks in India.

Soon opportunity pulled NB into telecom indstry. He pioneered an idea of unlimited calls & talk time to India, a boon for Indians residing abroad. Today NB is on board of Telcos in UK, Aus & Ind.

Recently, NB was once again invited by ISL as visionary to head the emerging business of Digital Signage. Under the leadership of NB, Sellivision - an Out-Of-Home TV channel, has pioneered some concepts leading other players to do what it does.
