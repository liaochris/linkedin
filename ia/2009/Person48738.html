Mary Thomas’s Summary

We offer interactive, realtime IT Training across the internet to enable you to use your IT applications more effectively without leaving your office.
Relevant, bespoke training offered. Learn what you want to know not what you already know.
Training carried out using online classroom so learner can see the tutor screen to watch a demonstration and tutor can see learner screen to watch learner carry out a skill or task and answer relevant questions. Communication is real time via a headset.
Training is cost effective and can be provided either 1:1 or in a group.

Mary Thomas’s Specialties:

Online Training, Microsoft Office applications, training and assessing ITQ, distance learning, ACT!, internet applications, e- networking applications (linkedin, twitter, facebook, etc.), Open Office, Google documents, bespoke training, user guides
