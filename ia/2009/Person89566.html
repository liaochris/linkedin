Greg Lanz’s Summary

Accomplished sales and management executive with over 10 years of Internet and game industry experience. Solid track record of managing sales teams, growing revenue, and closing AAA deals. Set up advertising sales and operations for Wikia, an off-shoot of Wikipedia. Previously managed advertising sales and game sales across multiple platforms and channels. Set up affiliate distribution networks, sold DRM and e-commerce technologies, managed game publishing on Sega Dreamcast, conducted channel management and licensing reviews for consumer and enterprise software companies. Extensive Rolodex of advertisers, agencies, websites, portals, ISPs, game developers, and game publishers.

Greg Lanz’s Specialties:

Building sales teams. Sales management, training, and compensation plans. Revenue analysis and tracking. Intellectual property licensing (in-bound and out-bound). Extensive experience with Salesforce.com including set up, administration, customization, and user maintenance.
