Lara Merriam-Smith’s Summary

I created an elegant solution to an annoying problem and it started from an unlikely source, a simple paper clip. The Bra Barrette is my innovative idea to solve the age old problem of slipping bra, tank top or swimsuit straps. Conceived out of my own necessity, this delightfully simple, beautiful tool not only keeps my bra straps where they belong, but adds elegantly functional jewelry to any woman’s couture. The Bra Barrette is my invention, and it has become my passion to make sure that every woman has a Bra Barrette in her repertoire.
