Deborah McAdams’s Summary

McAdams has reported, edited, designed and managed at trade publications and daily newspapers for more than a decade. Her expertise includes telecommunications, technology, legislation, business and the digital television transition, in particular.

She's appeared on CNN and C-Span, at association conferences and executive summits. McAdams has won writing awards and recognition for her feature writing and personal columns as well as her business and technology coverage.

She’s lived in the Midwest, the Sierra Nevada, New York City, metro Washington, D.C. and Hollywood. Before attending the University of Nebraska-Lincoln as a nontraditional student, she was a farm wife.

McAdams welcomes opportunities to communicate, manage and lead.
