Bruce Silver’s Summary

Employers Rx LLC helps entrepreneurs and executives of growing companies with 5 to 500 employees to compare, select and form successful employee leasing, payroll and HR Outsourcing or Professional Employer partnerships.

Bruce has over 25 years of experience in the insurance and employee benefits industry. His accomplishments include serving as technical advisor for the Palm Beach County Community Health Purchasing Alliance (CHPA), formed by the State of Florida to provide affordable health insurance coverage for small business owners. He has provided expert testimony before the Palm Beach County Court, and has taught a variety continuing education courses for insurance agents and brokers on insurance and financial planning topics at Palm Beach Community College.
