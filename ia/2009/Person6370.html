    People Capital, Inc. (http://www.people2capital.com) was founded in 2008 by a team of world-class talent with backgrounds in student loans, consumer finance, credit ratings and new media to develop the next generation of credit risk management and funding for student financing. Our peer-to-peer (p2p) lending platform brings innovative financing solutions for students to finance their college educations by marrying together traditional FICO scores, guarantors and co-signers with cutting-edge information-based strategies from the credit card industry. Our patent-pending human capital score can underwrite students without credit history by being able to project individual income levels and ability to pay indebtedness. We incorporate merit data such as GPAs, SAT scores, internships, college and major to providing a true and unbiased, data-driven measure of economic value of an education.
