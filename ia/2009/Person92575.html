Matt Harrigan’s Summary

Matt Harrigan is a technology industry veteran, having played major roles in both developing organizations as well as fortune 500s such as Cisco Systems and SAIC.

In 1992, he founded MCR - one of the first commercial network security firms, and the first company to offer managed security services. Matt has advised venture capital and investment banking firms on their technology investments, with a specific emphasis on network security products and services.

In 2000, Matt joined Winfirst, LLC as Vice President, Internet Services where he oversought a $1.2B effort to make fiber-to-the-home services a reality for citizens in Sacramento, San Diego, Los Angeles, Dallas, Austin, and Houston.

His work has been covered by several leading industry publications and programs, including Forbes magazine, Fortune magazine, Inc. magazine, The Wall Street Journal, Client / Server magazine, Computer World, ABC News, CBC News, and The Discovery Channel.
