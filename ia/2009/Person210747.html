Scott Babcock’s Summary

Nearly 17 years of hands-on experience in all aspects of video/audio production and post-production as well as experienced in web production and technologies including HTML, CSS, ActionScript2 and Flash animation. Experience and understanding of the b2b publishing company environment. Multimedia Director, project manager and producer with a focus on client service, sponsorship model development using the latest online media technologies and platforms, developing and refining work-flows and providing a high level of quality regardless of budget.

Skilled in the roles of Director, Producer, Video Editor, Project Manager and team member and able to work hands-on each step of the way, in a team environment or alone using leading common tools and software including: Adobe Creative Suite (After Effects, Premiere, Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, Fireworks, Dreamweaver, Soundbooth, OnLocation), encoding (Abobe Media Encoder, Sorenson Squeeze, Windows Media Encoder), greenscreen compositing, online project management (Central Desktop, Basecamp), online video mangagement platforms (Vidego, Veotag, SlideShowPro, YouTube), Mac and PC, Avid, Vegas, DVD Architect, GarageBand, Google/MS apps and more...

Going forward my interest is in bringing my experience and talents to a leadership role, to help a company define and realize new goals in digital and online media and inspire and mentor those that I work with.

Scott Babcock’s Specialties:

Thorough experience and understanding of conceiving, creating and deploying digital and online media, aligning creative goals with business objectives, delivering mutual success for the company and its clients.
