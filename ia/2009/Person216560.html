Sherwin Krug 舍文’s Summary

Sherwin is now the Founder and CEO of AppointmentCity.com, a start-up venture that seeks to become the one stop platform for consumers of services to schedule all their appointments in an online real-time setting.

Previously Sherwin was the COO and SVP of MFG.com. His duties included responsibility for all day to day operations of MFG.com. Sherwin role also included Global Business Development including leading the acquisition of its European operations and helping to set up MFG.com in China. He also oversaw the creation of MFG's Textiles unit which quickly become MFG's largest new vertical launched by the company in the past three years and he also played a role in the creation of MFG.com's groundbreaking Open Source Hardware CNC Machine.

Prior to joining MFG.com, Sherwin was the Chief Financial Officer for Tectonic Network, Inc., a micro-cap public company most recently focused on 3D CAD products for the construction industry. In addition to his financial responsibilities, Sherwin oversaw many operational aspects of Tectonic. Prior to that, Sherwin served as Vice President of Finance and Chief Financial Officer for CMD Group, a worldwide publisher of construction information, through its sale to Reed Elsevier plc for $300 million. Prior to that, Sherwin was a Senior Manager with Ernst & Young in both its Johannesburg, South Africa, and Atlanta offices, where he focused on entrepreneurial growth companies.

Sherwin received a Bachelor of Commerce degree from the University of the Witwatersrand and a Bachelor of Accounting Science Honors from the University of South Africa. He is a Chartered Accountant and member of the Atlanta chapter of the Executives Roundtable
