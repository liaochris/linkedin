Fahad Khan’s Summary

Fahad Khan, 27, is an entrepreneur driven by a set of larger-than-life ambitions and goals, and an abiding desire to understand how the world works.

Fahad Khan, has more than 7 years of extraordinary and diverse multi-country technology and business management experience in telecommunication, media, and technology sectors. While he is currently involved in a number of ventures, he aspires to become a venture capitalist one day. For his day time job, he runs Social Shore—a Manhattan based pure-play social media consulting and technology company serving Fortune 500 consumer brands and Inc 500 advertising agencies. He is at helm of company’s overall strategic direction, client engagements, and product development functions.

Before founding Social Shore, Fahad Khan was heading the multi-million-dollar WiMAX Telephony product development division at Wateen Telecom—a $500+ million Abu Dhabi Group venture. He was an influential part of the core team that launched the world’s first and largest Mobile WiMAX (802.16e) network, with the cooperation of Motorola, Inc.

Prior to that, Fahad Khan co-founded an innovation management consulting firm while still a student at Chalmers. In his early career he worked for a New York based telecom startup, where he was responsible for starting, scaling, growing and managing multi-country BPO operations for telecom providers based in New York and Florida. While he managed a knowledge based workforce of 80+ employees, he was merely 21 years of age.

A scholarly expert in the area of International Entrepreneurship, Fahad Khan holds a Civilingenjörsexamen degree in Management and Economics of Innovation, from Chalmers University of Technology in Göteborg, Sweden.

Fahad speaks 5 languages with native fluency and lives in Jersey City, New Jersey, with his wife who is graduate student of dentistry at UMDNJ and a former scientist at Schering-Plough Corporation
