Pratul Kant’s Summary

Latest: Senior Manager - Information Technology at Metropolitan Transportation Commission, Oakland, CA.

Pratul is an Electrical Engineer and has 15 years of hands-on systems and network administration experience, specializing in systems management of enterprise servers, managing online java based applications, file and network services, database servers, corporate email infrastructure, high availability configurations, monitoring tools, large corporate local area networks, communication infrastructure and maintaining 7x24 production server farm and IT production operations. . He has wide IT domain knowledge and has worked with variety of industries like Internet technology start-ups (Speedera Networks, Schoolcity.com, Clickability.com), automobile manufacturing [Tata Motors], finance (World Savings Bank / Wachovia), healthcare supply chain management companies (Neoforma, CMOS Soft) and educational institutes [Stanford University, Sacred Heart Schools]. He has supported 24x7 IT operations and NOC/Help Desk support functions all along his career and currently managing a team of systems and network administrators to achieve above tasks for a government client.

Pratul Kant’s Specialties:

Managing IT functions and production operations, aligning and forming IS strategy based on organization's business strategy with a flexible and scalable infrastructure and network design, including Virtualization. Systems management and high availability of production servers, systems administration of Redhat Linux and Windows 2003 servers, performance and availability monitoring, performance tuning, Corporate Email system, data protection, security and support to finance audits.
