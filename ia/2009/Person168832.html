Mike Reyher’s Summary

After a 20 year career of building high-tech teams and dynamic companies, Mike Reyher comes home to Farstar where his passion for business, technology, and creating winning business teams is now focused on unleashing the great potential of Farstar.

Reyher started selling technology while attending Kansas State University where he earned his way through school selling and repairing computers for local shops. After college, he came to Dallas selling new phone systems to local businesses before encountering Soft Warehouse and quickly taking charge of their new computer assembly operation. Reyher quickly rose to the executive ranks as Soft Warehouse became CompUSA. As Vice President, he managed key relationships with Dell, Apple Computer and Intel while managing within the company’s meteoric growth to a successful public offering in 1992.

In 1995, Reyher’s insight gained at CompUSA led him to invest in a local IBM business partner and led the company to providing Internet strategy and application development to local enterprise customers at Workflow Designs. After a successful sale of Workflow, a former client and Yahoo! executive asked Reyher to co-found Stick Networks, a next-generation wireless platform developer. He joined DataStarUSA (another Workflow client) in 2002 as Vice President and Chief Information Officer to guide their e-business strategies. He was later promoted to Vice President of Sales and Marketing.

Reyher, a Dallas resident, is a father of two and enjoys endurance mountain-bike racing.
