Patti Keating’s Summary

Patti Keating is a Professional Coach and Success Trainer. She supports self-employed professionals and entrepreneurs in creating success, wealth, and achievement by connecting with their unique life purpose and deepest core values. Patti helps her clients fill their business with ideal customers and clients. She helps . Her clients increase their income and fulfillment, they are committed, action-oriented professionals who have a passion for life. Patti is a former marketing consultant with over 25 years experience in business development. She is a life long student of personal growth and development and is continuously reading, attending seminars and workshops, and listening to cutting edge trainings, by current thought leaders.

Patti is described by her clients as inspirational, insightful, energetic, and passionate about her work. Her client’s success can be contributed to Patti's intuative insight and experience in life and business development.

Patti graduated from the University of Oregon with a Bachelors Degree in Finance and Marketing. She began working at the age of 15 and has held numerous positions, including Membership Director for the Eugene Area Chamber of Commerce, Athletic Club Marketing Director, and Director of Communication for a Coaching and Education company. She has successfully launched 3 businesses. An intuitive entrepreneur, Patti has charted her path to success through creativity, persistence, resourcefulness and a strong desire to excel.

Along with her professional life, Patti has years of volunteer experience, which includes Fox Hollow French Immersion Site Council Chair, Evergreen Site Council Chair and Portlandia Club Mentoring Chair. She also served on the Relief Nursery Leadership Committee where she worked to raise funding for children in need. This passionate Professional Coach is also the mother of four incredible children Kim, Nic, Spencer, and Avery. She will help you live a life of intention, attention, action and joy.
