Paul Wlodarczyk’s Summary

Strategic marketing professional, expert at commercializing emerging technology and incubating IT professional services. Focused on new market entry and developing markets for technology that hasn't "crossed the chasm." Expert in valuation of businesses and R&D portfoilios. Consultant to Fortune 500 clients for strategic revenue growth, business process innovation, and technology adoption. Technology focus on all aspects of unstructured content.
