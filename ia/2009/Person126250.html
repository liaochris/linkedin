Karen Gehm’s Summary

I'm a nurse by trade, working specifically with special needs populations (blind, deaf, deaf/blind, autistic, physical handicaps, DD, DD/MH, TBI, etc.). I've been in the field for over thirty years in various capacities, and as a nurse for the past thirteen years. I belong to several nursing associations serving the Developmentally Disabled, and passed my certification exam through our national organization to become a Certified Developmental Disabilities Nurse.
