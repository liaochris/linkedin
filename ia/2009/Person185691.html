Peter H. Sliedrecht’s Summary

Newland Science and Technology Group chooses “The Netherlands” to drive its regional business expansion.

Fujian Newland Auto-ID Tech. Co., Ltd one of China’s AIDC leading companies a full daughter Fujian Newland Computer Co Ltd. has opened on October 1st 2008 a new venture and regional head office for Europe in “The Netherlands”

About: Fujian Newland Computer Co Ltd.
Public company Shenzhen Stock Exchange Ticker 000997
Group revenue 2008 approx. 340 Mil. Euro.
The group’s principal activities are manufacturing, selling and leasing of computers and peripheral equipment; provisions of computer technology and information services and designing, installing and testing of computerized highway tolls, monitors and systems. Group divisions are:
-Computer
- Auto-ID
- EFT-POS / Payment terminals
- POS & Electronic Cash Register systems
- Mobile payment, ticketing & couponing solutions
- Software
- Digital Equipment (Digital Receiver Technology)

Newland Auto-ID has developed a great products line based on 2D Imagers technology CMOS or vision-based imaging
technology primarily used for the scanning of 2D symbols such as Data Matrix, QR code, and Maxi Code

Newland Europe BV (NEU) is honoured to manage and channel Newland’s fast growing European AIDC Business.

Newland Europe BV (NEU) operation will cover all European countries including Russia and Turkey.

NEU’s operation will include sales and marketing, admin, logistics and warehousing and RMA handling and repairs.

Heading the new venture Newland Europe BV (NEU) will be Peter H. Sliedrecht who has been appointed as CEO for Newland Europe BV as well as Newland Taiwan Co. Ltd.

Before this new challenge he was the CEO for more than 8 years of one Europe’s leading retail scanner manufacturers.

He is joint by an team of AIDC specialists with many years of experience in the European AIDC community.

So Europe here we come !
