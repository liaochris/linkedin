Ralf Borchert’s Summary

I also accept connection requests at: www.HOSPITALITYLEADERS.com

Ralf’s career has revolved around causing corporate transformation projects designed to optimize the use of resources and enhance performance. In executing this work he has developed into an articulate, compassionate and inspiring Leader, an expert Trainer, a remarkable Speaker and an astute and savvy Negotiator.

He has a in-depth track record of planning implementing and leading global group-wide initiatives that deliver a quantifiable competitive advantage. His initiatives leave companies with systematic group wide processes and best practices supported by training and education systems to sustain the desired outcomes.

He possesses extensive experience in planning and managing resources and understands risk and opportunity and how to capitalize on both. He is able to confidently respond to all market conditions and create positive outcomes.

Whilst head of procurement with both the Mandarin Oriental and Peninsula Hotels, he was accountable for identifying and unlocking untapped value. His involvement with the supply market transformed how the group’s budget owners and purchasing staff conducted themselves, which lead to a fundamental shifting of mindsets and results.

He now manages Hansa Victoria Strategic. In this capacity he advises clients on staffing, hiring, performance and retention and recruits senior personnel globally.

Since 2005 Ralf has also been working with Landmark Education, a global leader in the field of training and development specializing in coaching and empowering people and businesses to achieve breakthrough results in areas important to them. Ralf produces and manages workshops and leads seminars.

He also addresses senior executives at company workdays and meetings covering topics addressing personal productivity, organizational effectiveness, leadership and performance.
