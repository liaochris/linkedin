Charles Besondy’s Summary

Chuck Besondy is recognized as a champion for B2B marketing optimization and transformation. He is also a leading authority on the topic of interim management.

His book, "Leadership On Demand", and his blog, "One Riot - One Ranger" present useful advice on how companies can drive growth by leveraging interim leadership in marketing and sales functions.

If a company suspects their marketing is under-performing or is not aligned with business goals, Chuck steps in to right the ship. He is especially skilled at solving issues in five critical areas: An ill-defined target market, a vague value proposition, dysfunctional lead management, an ineffective marketing plan, or an absence of marketing accountability.

Besondy is a senior-level “player-leader” with 7-1/2 years consulting and interim management experience. This is underpinned by 25 years of marketing leadership primarily within small and mid-market software/Web businesses that place an emphasis on action and growth.

For companies he architects, directs, and implements high value-add marketing initiatives related to product marketing, strategic marketing, and marketing communications. Having hands-on and management experience in every aspect of marketing, he has built an arsenal of battle-tested processes that can be applied to market analysis, competitive analysis, positioning, go-to-market planning, market requirement assessment, opportunity pipeline engineering, and marketing performance management.
