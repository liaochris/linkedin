Chungmei Cheng’s Summary

With Orchid of Life -LifeCoaching my goal is to coach and inspire people to experience more peace of mind, self esteem and love in their lives. I coach people on topics such as positive thinking, making the right choices, cultural differences, living an uncluttered life, love related themes and effective communication in either a business or personal setting.

The people I coach have different backgrounds: self -employed/ working for a company/student/artists, man/woman, age ranges from 20-55 and diverse nationalities.

The languages I coach in are Dutch, English and Spanish.

I have been coaching professionally for almost 6 years and my passion for coaching continues growing.

For Free LifeCoachingTips you can subscribe to the Orchid of Life's Monthly Newsletter in either Dutch, http://www.orchidoflife.nl/ or English, http://www.orchidoflife.nl/index.php?l=en on the Homepage.
