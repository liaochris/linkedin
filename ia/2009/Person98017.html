Ilan Friedman’s Summary

Ilan founded and is the President of Ncompas (www.ncompas.net), providing Israeli companies with international market development services (market research, international expantion strategy, business development/marketing/sales, executive and personnel recruitment, site location, etc.), and international companies with local support in Israel (market research, technology scouting, missions and business delegations, company visits, etc.).

While living in the US, Ilan acquired two academic degrees, worked with two multinational communication companies, and spent almost five years as an executive with the most effective bi-national trade organization focused on the Israeli market.

During this time Ilan built a reputation for working with persistence and diligence to bring together Israeli scientists and entrepreneurs with prospective partners and clients among the Southeast’s most significant corporations, as well as medium sized and small companies, and service providers. On top of building a powerful network of professional relationships based on trust and quality work, the time spent reviewing hundreds of technologies in the areas of software, communications, security, Cleantech, and life sciences helped Ilan develop an expertise in quickly understanding technologies and then positioning them appropriately within a framework of a market-entry strategy.

Ilan is completely fluent in both Hebrew and English, in terms of reading, writing, and speaking with no accent. He’s culturally adept in both the United States and Israel. He completed Undergraduate studies in Finance and International Business at the Ohio State University, and Graduate studies in International Business at Georgia State University.
