Kevin Saladyga’s Summary

After 17+ years in the Point of Purchase industry, I can honestly say its still an exciting field to be in....never a dull moment.

My passion for P.O.P. led to the creation of POPON, The Point of Purchase Online Network, which is a unique online portal that offers a variety of resources related to the Packaging, Display, Retail and Printing industries. POPON was founded with the intention of creating an environment where professionals could Connect : Communicate : Create. We believe we are achieving just that. If you are seeking a diverse Point of Purchase Marketing platform to showcase your products and services in, you've come to the right place.

Please visit www.popon.net and take advantage of the wealth of information at your fingertips.
