Crystal Bruce’s Summary

Crystal Bruce, CMP, has nearly ten years of experience in meeting/event planning and marketing. Her experience spans several industry sectors including non-profit, government, association and corporate. She has managed programs as small as a ten person executive retreat to a five-day national conference with 4,000 attendees. Prior to founding FairWind Meetings & Events, Crystal was Assistant Vice President of Meetings and Conventions for Raymond James Financial Services, where she was responsible for the planning and execution of eight annual programs.

She has been an active member of Meeting Professionals International (MPI) since 2003. Over the last few years, Crystal has volunteered to serve on several committees, as well as in leadership positions. Crystal is currently on the Board of Directors for the Tampa Bay Area Chapter as Vice President of Administration.

In 2006, Crystal earned the Certified Meeting Professional (CMP) designation. Obtaining this designation, which is administered by the Convention Industry Council, is regarded as the premier certification program in the industry.

Crystal graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Public Relations and a Minor in Business Administration.
