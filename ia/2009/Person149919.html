Maria Avgitidis’s Summary

I have more than two years of work experience, both in the public and private sector. After working in the US Embassy of Athens in the Foreign Commercial Service, I transitioned to the private sector in digital media market research for WPP company- Safecount. I am responsible for maintaining and nurturing business relationships with publishers, ad networks, social networks, and websites to promote partnerships in data and software for Fortune 500 companies.

I am also currently pursuing my M.S. in Global Affairs at NYU, with a concentration in International Business, Economics, and Development. I achieved my B.S. in International Business and European Affairs in December 2007, from the American College of Greece, graduating Magna Cum Laude. My interest in International Affairs and its collaboration with business has helped me co-found NYCIR, the digital community of New Yorkers who are passionate about international affairs and business and political science.
