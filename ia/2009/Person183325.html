Paul Lanzillotti’s Summary

Paul Lanzillotti is currently the Vice President of Operations for Veritas Prep, reporting directly to the Co-founders. Prior to joining Veritas Prep, Paul was a Senior Consultant in the financial services practice of BearingPoint (formerly KPMG Consulting). He has extensive experience in project management within IT, financial services and accounting.

Additionally, having previously worked as a Senior Consultant for a major commercial lender and as a Senior Project Manager for a niche wholesale lender, Paul's experience includes business process outsourcing initiatives, paperless processing conversions, activity based management implementations and quantitative operational studies.

Prior to these roles, Paul labored as a Manufacturing and Quality Engineer for an international Tier One automotive supplier, a Production and Maintenance Supervisor for an international consumer products company and as a Project Engineer for an international chemical manufacturer.

Paul holds a Bachelors degree in Mechanical Engineering with an emphasis on Manufacturing Process Design from Kettering University (formerly GMI Engineering & Management Institute), and completed his Masters in Business Administration at the UCLA Anderson School of Management; concentrating in Operations and Entrepreneurship.

Paul is the author of several professional and scholastic publications. His most recent publications include an Offering Memorandum/Private Debt Offering for a specialty lender in the sub prime automotive and real estate space and a year-long efficiency study entitled: “Thermodynamic Efficiencies of the BASF DPP Steam Power Plant Systems.”
