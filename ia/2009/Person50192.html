Who is CyberCoders?

CyberCoders and CyberScientific were organized in 1999 with a single purpose - to bring the best people together with the best companies. We started out with the name "CyberCoders" because in our early days we recruited software engineers. We soon expanded and hired some of the best recruiters in the country. We have branched out into all different segments of professional recruiting nationwide.

Now, CyberCoders is the premier recruiting firm in the nation. We are your source for finding experienced, qualified candidates for all of your open positions. We specialize in sales, executive, technical, financial, accounting, engineering, mortgage, construction and operational positions across all industries.

Put us to work for you -- you will get results! Find out what Wells Fargo, KPMG, Microsoft, Abbott Pharmaceuticals, Nokia, American Express, Beazer Homes and hundreds of other companies and thousands of candidates already know - CyberCoders connects exceptional people with exceptional companies.
We are designed to save your time...

What makes us different? We listen to your needs.

Recruiting the most qualified professionals to work in strong companies is a people intensive process. The Internet has revolutionized job searching, but experienced recruiters who think out-of-the-box will always do the best job of matching truly qualified individuals with the right companies.

When their expertise is teamed with our nationwide network of over 5 million candidates, and our referral fee that allows candidates to refer their friends to us and earn $1,000, the results are unmatched in the industry.
