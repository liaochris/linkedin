Ubongabasi Inyang’s Summary

Thidasp Limited is a strategy management consultancy firm that specialises in helping organisations improves their performance, identify growth areas & opportunities, envisioning and defining strategic direction, developing strategy blueprint by leveraging on existing capabilities. We also recommend building new competencies to diversify revenue streams and manage business risks based on capabilities and resources, with specific focus on implementation.

Thidasp Ltd's vision is to be the company most admired for helping businesses become the leading corporations of tomorrow.

As part of Thidasp's growing strategy, we are committed to understanding client issues and delivering customised solutions instead of fitting standardised model-based solutions to their issues.

Thidasp's team of consultants will be able to guide you in:
o Increasing your profitability
o Developing corporate strategy
o Curbing your dwindling turnover
o Effective change management
o Analysing the organisation place in the current business environment
o Introducing effective processes to maximise productivity
o Implementing technological solutions to existing systems
