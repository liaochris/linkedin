Jeremy Fox’s Summary

Having recently earned a Master's in Global Business Administration degree, coupled with years of invaluable work experience, I believe I am prepared for the next step in my corporate financial career.

Most recently I was employed as a financial analyst by Volkswagen Group U.K. to design and develop a number forecasting models which are currently being used within the company, as well as their independent consultants. My largest project was creating a model that was able to accurately forecast the various costs incurred by their 166 unique rental properties, each with varying exposure to market and rent inflationary pressures; ultimately the model will be used to help in negotiating the terms of future lease agreements.

In addition to my professional experience abroad, I was employed for three years as a QA analyst with Putnam Investments, a multi-billion dollar mutual fund company. While at Putnam I worked on numerous technical projects within their IT department. My role was to assist and work in conjunction with the lead project managers in order to research and develop new user interface for both customer and service representative use. Working within the Internal Software Department of a financial corporation afforded me the opportunity to gain considerable back office knowledge and the confidence to work independently within a team setting.

My academic background is focused on both finance and economics. This past December 2008 I earned my Global MBA with a concentration in international finance, which helped me in building a solid foundation in financial theories and industry trends. In my last semester I focused on the economic effects of environmental regulations on the automotive industry within Germany as an independent research study.

I am certain the employment experience and graduate education I have attained demonstrates my ability to easily adapt and apply my expertise in any work environment, making me a vital team asset.
