Amit Chitale’s Summary

Walking the path of Creativity-Innovation-Productivity, Amit terms it as his passion. He has exemplified this by converting capabilities, expertise into profitable and successful offerings/ products in various roles in Product Management, Product Marketing, and Business Development and into engineering centric functions such as Architecture and Engineering Management.

In his current responsibility as a Senior Product Marketing Manager at KPIT Cummins, he owns Product Strategy for AUTOSAR products delivering Auto Grade and Zero tolerance software to the customers. Critical areas of his current work assignment include vehicle networks, Operating systems and Infotainment. In his previous engagements he has successfully carried ownership of MobileMedia product at NVIDIA. He has 12+ years of experience in managing projects and people for Product Development, R&D with multi-site distributed teams. He has very creatively applied his experience in Tactical Marketing and Technical Product Management.

Amit holds Bachelors in Engineering with specialization in Electronics and Telecommunications. He is also an Evangelist, Founder, Moderator and Author of CRYPTOcrats a special interest group for Security and Cryptography.
