Tony Morris’s Summary

Recycled American entrepreneur now strategy consultant, independent Director, private investor working exclusively with technology-based growth companies.

Actively transitioning focus to green/cleantech from IT and internet. Specifically interested in opportunities where cleantech value propositions have demonstrable IP and/or are enabled or enhanced by IT.

Tony Morris’s Specialties:

Venture development - accelerating the business building activities that deliver sustainable growth and value creation in chosen markets, both existing and new.

Specialize in customer- and market-facing issues including business direction, offering definition, positioning, go-to-market strategies and execution in dynamic market ecosystems frequently involving multiple partners, channels, and tiers of distribution.
