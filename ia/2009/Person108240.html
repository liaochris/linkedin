Jeff Mehl’s Summary

My wife and I previously owned and operated a business for 22 years. During this time I experienced all of the pitfalls of hiring, training and firing staff, paying the wrong consultants, and dealing with the “stuff “ of business, including allowing my business to take over my life. I was married to my business and allowed my personal life to take a back seat for too many years. At a certain point, however, I realized that I was off-base – I discovered that business should support my lifestyle, not become my lifestyle.

As that realization took hold, I began to see that I was not alone. I found that many other small business owners were in the same boat with so many of us floating along thinking that it’s normal to lose the excitement and passion of our businesses as we give up our evenings, weekends, and family time. We struggle with the sense that there just aren’t enough hours in a day, or that we feel we have to do it all ourselves!

I've started on the road to recovery and want to bring others along with me!

Through this experience, I've become passionate about the need to use technology and outsourcing to enable small business owners to reclaim their lives and their passion while still maintaining the quality of their businesses.

As a result, my wife and I started Daybreak Virtual Office Solutions which is a provider of outsourced clerical, secretarial and administrative support for small business owners.

My wife, Valerie, and I have been married for 38 years and have lived in Esopus, NY for the past 36 years. We have 3 grown children and 3 grandchildren, and agree that grandkids really are among life’s best experiences – even better than the originals!

My favorite quote: “If you do not stand firm in your faith, you will not stand at all”

Jeff Mehl’s Specialties:

Although not a virtual assistant myself (I'm an RN by background), my company provides clients with their own virtual Administrative Assistant who works with a back office team. Once trained, this team becomes a seamless part of our client's business while serving as their remote office.

    At Daybreak Virtual Office Solutions, we differentiate ourselves from the majority of corporate and independent Virtual Assistant providers in two ways:

    1) Besides giving our clients their own Virtual Assistant, we also give them a back-office team that works under the VA's supervision.

    - This means that the client gets full virtual office support for everything from answering phones, scheduling appointments and managing to-do lists, to handling high end projects and assisting with proposals.

    2) Our second differentiation is based on relationship building. We all want to develop relationships with our clients. In our case, we continually experience the strong relationships that occur between our VA's and their clients. Loyalty develops because of the relationship, and the VA becomes the client's biggest fan and protector. We see it happening over-and-over, and It's always exciting to watch how this impacts our clients, as well as our employees' job satisfaction.

    The result is a culture within Daybreak that makes it "All About Relationships."
