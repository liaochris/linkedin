Chuck Zdrojowy’s Summary

My Profession is supply chain management consultant and project manager. I love my work.

My avocation is networker and creator of networking communities designed to bridge the internet and your local community.

Combining my vocation and my avocation have been quite rewarding.

I started networking on Linkedin and since there were so few Supply Chain types when compared to the Techies and Recruiters, I invited every person that was in the CLM, now CSCMP and WERC Data Bases. I quickly learned that the interactions provided by LinkedIn were lacking and decided to try creating my own community for SCM professionals. Over the last year that had evolved in the SCNi the Supply Chain Network International with over 3400 members. I replicated this with a group for my local area called Link_Atlanta with 2,100+ members in a little over 9 months. My latest adventure is Link_USA as the parent group for a group for each of the 50 states and the top US Metropolitan areas.

The idea is that no matter where you maintain your online professional network, you can meet those people face to face in your local area at Link_Atlanta, or Link_DFW etc LIVE event. Relationships are built face to face. From what I have heard the founders of PNOL have a similar philosophy.
I am extremely interested in how new media/ social networking et al will influence the way businesses do business in the coming decade. I see it as a HUGE! opportunity. How can we work together!?
