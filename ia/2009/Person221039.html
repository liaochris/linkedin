Jim Spraggett’s Summary

I am a Senior Consultant with twenty years of experience developing systems for Manufacturing, Retail, Pharmaceutical, Government, and Hospital organizations.

I have focused primarily on the implementation of systems based on Oracle and SqlServer.

For example,

ETL and Data Warehouse Systems - Pfizer's Global Financial Warehouse;

Financial Systems – Ecommerce for Grand&Toy, Billing for Princess Margaret Hospital, GL and AP for Stratford General Hospital, Payroll and Human Resources for Extendicare;

Government Systems - AP and a specialized Paramedic System for the Ministry of Health, Case Tracking for the Ministry of Labor, and Patient Care for Kitchener Waterloo Hospital;

I have extensive experience in the Analysis, Design, Development, and Implementation of Data Warehousing solutions using the latest ETL and Data Modeling tools. I have worked with the two leading ETL tools, Informatica and Sagent. I am currently a member of the Informatica Devnet Developers Group and have hands-on experience with Informatica 8.1.1.

I have led many projects from their initial conceptual design phase through to their successful implementation. I promote team co-operation, effective communication between team members, users and management, reusability in building blocks of codes, system ownership, and a structured approach to system design and implementation.

Jim Spraggett’s Specialties:

Data Warehousing Consultant, ETL Developer ( Informatica, Sagent, VB, PL/SQL ), E-Commerce Designer/Developer, Data Architect, System Architect, Project Manager, Oracle / SqlServer Developer, .Net Developer ( C# , VB.net )
