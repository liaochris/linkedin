Executive Summary:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Atul Shetty, a result driven Training expert who has above nine years of experience in Instructor led technical / business process training with expertise in designing training content using instructional design methodologies. He also possesses more than four years of experience in providing leadership and behavioural trainings to market leaders in Information Technology, Business Process Outsourcing and Practice Management. He has successfully developed and implemented training processes from the company’s inception stage and has shown a proven ability to deliver results to business specific needs.

His training engagement with clients like Dell and EarthLink has given him tremendous exposure in the area of adult leaning, e-learning, developing and creating innovative learning techniques, administering and implementing appropriate learning solutions and evaluating overall training effectiveness. Atul possesses a passion for his profession and that is reflected both in his creative ideas and his excellent evaluations as a training facilitator. He is skilled in bringing course material alive by encouraging interactive learning sessions and using examples relating to their needs.

Some projects conceptualized and successfully implemented by him include setting up an ‘online knowledge base’ and ‘projects to streamline reporting, documentation and records’. His indigenous initiative of designing a ‘project to improve customer experience and site matrices’ was recognized as best practice amongst all EarthLink sites worldwide.
