Or-Tal Kiriati’s Summary

New!
For the past year I have been examining an idea for a new startup relating to the internet, game development and education, while keeping alive my consultancy.

I am a seasoned business communications and marketing strategy expert, specializing in developing communications strategy for international hi-tech companies. I also offer my services in naming, branding, and web presence tactics and I am looking for opportunities to use my unique combination of talents, knowledge, experience and connections to advance my clients, most of which are start-up companies.

In the coming year I plan to take my startup to the next stage.
I may continue to take on selected projects.
I intend to continue my contribution to the Israeli hi tech entrepreneurs community through the linked in group "Israeli Hi Tech Entrepreneurs Wherever" and the Meetup/Facebook group "Tel-Aviv-Yafo Entrepreneurs Meetup Group".
I am also an active member of the PTA in the elementary school my boys attend and the hi-school my daughter goes to.
