David F Aust I’s Summary

My professional experience has primarily focused on research and development of activities related to products/services, economic/monetary systems, reducing the financial burden of government, social trends, industry/market trends, and building prosperous communities through integrating family, business, and government communities in focusing on problem resolution.

When I was a young father, I learned that each member of my family had different gifts and talents. I discovered that these gifts could be used to solve family problems. I also learned as a parent, my responsibility is to develop and promote those talents and gifts in settings that will improve their ability to solve family problems. I later discovered that the reason I go to work, is that my employer is directly or indirectly sponsoring the solutions to my family problems in exchange for my best efforts to make them profitable. I then discovered that this social structure could be implemented into how government, business, and families should solve problems. My experience and goals are in accomplishing the vision of involving the family of humanity in solving social issues.
