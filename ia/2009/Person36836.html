Cassandra Morgan’s Summary

Cassandra Morgan, Co-Founder and Director of Operations for C&M Business Solutions, brings 20+ years of experience in the Information Technology industry. Ms. Morgan graduated from Northwestern University with a BS in Electrical Engineering. Cassandra is an accomplished professional of marked distinction in various aspects of Consulting and Business Management with an extensive record of achievement. Cassandra consistently demonstrates strong leadership skills as well as solid judgment and decision-making abilities. She is an experienced Recruiting, Training, IT, and Engineering Professional with exceptional Management experience, whose varied background proves to be an asset to any project.

Cassandra Morgan is also an original co-founder of the Commemorative R.O.S.E., Inc. (CRI) organization. CRI was founded primarily to provide financial, educational and career guidance for at-risk and disadvantaged individuals. Currently Ms. Morgan is the Board Chair and has held this elected position since 1998. (http://www.commemorativerose.org/)

Accomplishments

Under Ms. Morgan's tenure as Board Chair, CRI has grown from tutoring children at the founders personal home to offering several programs in a building of their own (the Youth and Community Enrichment Center). The current programs offered include: After School Program, Summer Program, Community Outreach Initiative, Junior Toastmasters Club (Sponsored by the 310 North Toastmasters Club of Atlanta), Jr. Workforce Initiative, and Local Shuttle Service.

Future Programs include:

School suspension program
Home School Program
Youth Investment Training Club
Parenting Classes
