Dane Storrusten’s Summary

Dane has over 10 years experience in design spanning several avenues of the industry from traditional print graphic design to engaging interactive product design.

Dane has a vivid, evolving design sensibility and a wide range of both professional and technical skills. He also prefers working in passionate, high-quality, collaborative settings that offer inspiration, provide great challenges and ultimately result in success.

Dane is always looking to take the next leap into uncharted territory and prides himself on the conceptual design process as well as nurturing them into realistic, well-executed solutions. A personal mood board is available upon request.
