YD Gordon’s Summary

YD Gordon is the founder and director of THT Research, a business research company that focuses on OEM / ODM / EMS manufacturing.

Recent custom research:
Global LNB production and shipments
Global DSC production and shipments by technology, geography, and application
Global DVD shipments by company, region, technology

Recent whitepapers:
China's Solar Energy and Polysilicon Industry
China's Mobile Phone Market
IDHs and Mobile Phone ODMs in China
China's GPS market
China's Telecom Market: EDGE Services
Japan Mobile Phone Vendors in China

THT began in 2001 as a media company covering the outsourcing manufacturing relationships between Taiwan- and Greater China-based computer and components manufacturing companies and international OEMs.

In 2002, recognizing the need amongst OEM sourcing executives and market research directors for accurate, reliable, and independent information on potential ODM / EMS partners, YD opened THT Research. Relying on his network of industry contacts (particularly among Asian manufacturers), YD has built a strong and well-connected team of researchers and analysts.

THT Research produces regular reports on ODM / EMS manufacturers, maintains an Online Contract Manufacturing Database, and fulfills custom research projects for leading OEM customers.

A partial client list includes Dell, Intel, Motorola, NTT DoCoMo, Agere, A.G. Edwards & Sons, Applied Technology, Conexant, Dolby, Cypress, Thomson, National Semiconductor, Xerox, Xilinx, Philips, Flextronics, Andigilog, NDS, Sky Works, Siemens, Prismark, Israel Export Institute, TAITRA, Jenner & Block, Celestica, Sanyo, Sony, Canon, Panasonic, LG, Toshiba, and Samsung.
