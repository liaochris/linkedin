Luigi Benetton’s Summary

When it comes to computer software, I’m the complete communications package: technical writer, course designer, trainer, copywriter and journalist. For more than ten years, I’ve placed my skills at the disposal of software firms, their clients, and government. Currently, I also write for several technical publications, among other freelance activities.

My writing work continues to be bolstered by the following attributes:
• Uncompromising plain language geared towards your specific audience
• Quick turnaround
• Continued learning in the latest technology developments
