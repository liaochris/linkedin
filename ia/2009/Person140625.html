Laurence Huntley’s Summary

Available for fulltime, Board of Director and Consulting assignments.
With over 25 years international management experience in the telecommunications industry in general management, marketing, sales, customer service and outsourcing, mergers and acquisitions and consulting, Laurence also has in specific experience in:
• General management; management of P&L to $100m level, including both outsourced services and manufacture and assembly.
• Sales and global account management to businesses, via both direct and indirect channels.
• Marketing nationally and internationally to businesses and consumers for both fixed and mobile voice, data and security services and equipment with revenues ranging up to $10bn.
• Leadership in marketing and business development, growing a small data networking company (Equant) from under $50m to over $1bn over 6 year period.
• Marketing leadership in voice, data and IP based services and equipment, being first to launch new VOIP and data services locally and globally.
• Marketing Communications and Brand management on a global basis.
• Organization Development and executing major structural changes.
• Customer care management, service and call centers, continuous quality improvement processes, and project management experience internationally, with management of over 3000 globally.
• Management consulting for large companies in the USA and Europe.
• Strategy and merger planning and execution.
• Experience as part of team raising capital and taking company successfully through an IPO and two secondary offerings, on the NYSE and Paris Bourse.

Laurence has his MBA from Warwick University in the UK and is also a Sloan Fellow of the London Business School.

Laurence Huntley’s Specialties:

Strategy; Marketing; M&A; Sales;Customer Service; International People Management; Raising Capital; IPO and Secondary Offerings; Organization Design and Change Management; Large and Small company experience.
