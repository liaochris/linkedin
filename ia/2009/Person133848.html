Len Steffen’s Summary

Since 2001 I operate as an independent international consultant and interim manager. Mandates included assignments in Croatia, Slovenia, Romania, Mauritania, and Russia.

Professional knowledge of and experience in:

- Merger and turn-around management
- Governance and Organisation/HR issues
- Relevant knowledge of ICT issues and alignment of business, processes and IT
