Stuart McIntyre’s Summary

Experienced, successful and respected Collaboration Consultant, Web2.0 and Social Software Evangelist, Blogger and Lotus Software Deployment Specialist.

One of the most experienced Lotus Connections and Quickr consultants in the UK, with significant and recent experience of solution design, deployment, pilots, upgrades, consolidation and integration.

Significant experience of all Lotus technologies, specifically Notes/Domino, Sametime, Quickr and Connections, plus growing knowledge of Websphere Portal and Web Content Management. Backed up by a wide breadth of server architecture knowledge, including IBM iSeries (OS400), pSeries (AIX), Windows and Linux plus extensive exposure to client architectures such as Mac OS-X.

Evangelist for Social Networking and Collaboration technologies, author/editor of a number of blogs (Collaboration Matters, the Quickr blog, the Connections blog and the Symphony blog), and budding podcaster.

Currently owner and editor of the top-ranked 'Power of the Lotusphere community' blog for Lotusphere 2009, plus creator and manager of the LinkedIn Lotusphere group. Finally, I'm co-facilitating the 'Lotus Connections and Beyond' Birds of a Feather session at Lotusphere 2009.
