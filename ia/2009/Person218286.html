Simon Chhoeu’s Summary

Simon Chhoeu
B.Acctg (UTS), M.Tax(Hons), ASIA, CA, CPA, CFP

Partner: Wintergate Group, Wintergate Consulting, Wintergate & Associates, Wintergate Solicitors, Wintergate Assurance Division, Wintergate Financial Services, Licenced Business Agent www.wintergate.com.au, simon@wintergate.com.au, +612 9633 5677.

Simon is a principal and partner of Wintergate & Associates, a Practice which had its origins in the establishment of J.F. Smith & Co. in the 1930’s and which in 1955 became Smith Jones & Co. He still has clients dating back from the 1940’s and has honorary audits of organizations such as the NSW Sports Federation. He has been instrumental in helping clients with infrastructure projects and assisting them to establish and grow business in Australia and Overseas.

As Managing Partner, Simon is responsible for the accountancy and consulting division and for driving the future direction and vision of the Practice. He recently added a Legal division that currently comprises a number of solicitors and an immigration specialist. This will add value to the existing accounting and financial planning businesses.

He is responsible for branding Wintergate & Associates as the clients’ wealth & lifestyle improvement strategists and the adoption of the motto: "working with you to achieve the wealth and lifestyle that you want and to ensure your peace of mind using our uniquely integrated accounting, financial planning and legal services team".

Simon Chhoeu’s Specialties:

Accounting; Corporate secretarial services; Audits; Infrastructure projects; Assisting clients to establish and grow business; Immigration law; Taxation planning and financial planning and corporate restructuring; Business Financing and leasing solutions; Retirement planning and estate planning.

Our client base is predominantly made up of companies & Professionals involved in the wholesale, manufacturing, building and construction industries, retail, service business and registered clubs.
