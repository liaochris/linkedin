Anne Lake’s Summary

Responsible for the design, development, delivery, and evaluation of IT training and learning activities. Assists all employees to maintain and improve their job skills by providing instruction on how to best utilize the technologies made available to them by the organization. An emphasis is also be placed on providing instruction about how to improve efficiencies through the consistent use of systems and tools across the global organization.


Primary Duties and Responsibilities:

• Assesses IT Training needs for entire organization
• Designs, develops, organizes, and markets courses that achieves the desired learning/performance goals and meets the demands of the business.
• Creates and maintains all IT Training documentation

• Creates, schedules, and facilitates IT training sessions. This will include webinars, conference calls and just-in-time training.

• Monitors and reports on the effectiveness of IT training/learning solutions

Additional Responsibilities:

• Coordinates training and development activities with ENSR’s OD training group to ensure a consistent approach and high quality delivery of training across the organization.
• Works with ENSR IT Infrastructure staff to synchronize training program requirements with available network resources.
• Provides a gateway into AECOM’s self-paced technical training programs for third party providers.
• Participates in the development of curriculums for IT related technical training (what would be an example of this?) .
