Lisane Basquiat’s Summary

Coaching is a powerful and effective tool for empowering individuals and groups to develop themselves in a way that aligns with their goals, objectives, values and priorities. It is solution-focused, action and results oriented, and designed to maximize personal and professional performance.

Lisane Basquiat is the founder of In the Scope Life Coaching, LLC. She is a Certified Empowerment Coach, Accredited Energy Leadership Coach and a member of the International Coaching Federation. Lisane specializes in helping successful individuals, teams, small businesses, and groups bring out their personal best in all aspects of their lives - through transformational change.

She has a proven track record of success in leading workshops, working with adult individuals, business teams, and departmental organizations. She coaches them toward a clear identification of vision (where they would like to be), clarity around where they are, and supports them toward developing a clear strategy and then the implementation of the plan toward reaching their goals.

Lisane is a former Director of Staffing Process with over 20 years of corporate experience. Her area of corporate expertise is in analyzing and streamlining complex business processes. Her focus has been in the areas of enterprise-wide Project Leadership, Organizational Effectiveness, Business Process Design, Staffing Technology and Relationship Management. She has worked for several Fortune corporations inclusing UBS Warburg (formerly PaineWebber), American Express and Prudential Financial.

Lisane may be reached either by telephone at (973)378-2262 or via email at lisanecoach@inthescope.com.
