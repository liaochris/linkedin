Thom Milkovic’s Summary

Companies hire me to make Websites. I take advantage of my 17 years of branding, design, photography and technical knowledge to create compelling online experiences. Customers are more inclined to do business with my clients because I take the time to understand what they want from one another.

Small to mid-sized businesses benefit from my talents the most, although I have had the privilege to work with TRX, Lexus, SAP, Lucent, Mapquest, Holiday Inn, GTE, and Worldspan.

I have also released a book for brick-and-mortar businesses named BRANDING TO THE POWER OF 5. It outlines how small business owners can attract and retain loyal customers. Available at http://www.Branding5.com.
