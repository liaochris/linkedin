Merrie Turner Lightner’s Summary

Attorney and real estate broker with expertise in real estate, residential and commercial property management as well as real estate investment.

Served on San Francisco Rent Stabilization & Arbitration Board for 12 years, as well as active in industry organizations such as the San Francisco Apartment Association, California Apartment Association, Professional Property Managers Association of San Francisco, Coalition for Better Housing, and San Francisco Board of Realtors. Helped developed important landlord-tenant legislation during tenure on the Rent Board and with SFAA and CAA.

Current member of and affiliated with SFAA, PPMA, CAA, NAA, NARPM, MNHC, NAR, CAR, SF Bar Association, as well as other industry organizations.

Merrie Turner Lightner’s Specialties:

Experienced residential and commercial property manager. Highly experienced in residential property management with expertise in San Francisco rent control issues. Regularly serves as expert witness in property management standard of care and habitability cases.
