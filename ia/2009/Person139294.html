Lee Marshall’s Summary

I have been working in the film and television industry for over 10 years now in various positions. Most of the projects I've worked on have been low-budget, but I'm looking to break into bigger shows, and would start in an entry level position if it is necessary to do so.

I am, however, a member in good standing of IA Local 871 as a Production Coordinator and Assistant Production Coordinator.

Alternately, I am looking to take my career in a different direction than production, and am sending off resumes for office work in TV and film-related businesses. I have also been an assistant in our industry and in non-industry jobs, and am good at anticipating needs and trouble shooting.

My communication skills are excellent, both written and verbal, as are my editing skills. I am exploring employment opportunities in the magazine and publishing world. I can submit a writing sample if asked.
