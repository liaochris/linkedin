Cory Ferber’s Summary

Cory is a Senior User Experience Designer specializing in user interface design, interaction design, visual information design, and information architecture for software, web applications, web interfaces, eCommerce sites, and task oriented B2C/B2B web sites. He has produced user experience deliverables, information architecture documents, and detailed functional specification documents to communicate design and planning since 1995. He designs creative solutions that optimize the user experience to improve efficiencies, save time, and cut costs. He improves people's experience with interactive systems and information technology.

Cory has designed the user experience for over 100 projects and has been responsible for the user interface design, interaction design, visual information design, information architecture, and UCD research. He created the visual design, graphic design, and information architecture for over 40 web sites working independently. Cory has taken a diverse path through a range of job positions based on his desire to continue developing his talents. Participating in the progress of technology has allowed him to grow professionally within an ever changing environment. He has worked in San Francisco, Silicon Valley, and New York City's Silicon Alley.

Cory received a Bachelor of Science with Honors in Information Technology from the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) - College of Applied Science and Technology. He has a formal education in human-computer interaction, user interface engineering, interaction design, usability, human factors, database and knowledge management, software and web development, internet technologies, object-oriented programming, scripting languages, and user-centered design. He also completed a New Media Publishing Concentration from RIT's College of Imaging Arts and Science and received a formal education in concepts of design, typography, dynamic graphics, animation, and publishing.
