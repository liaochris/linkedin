Carla King’s Summary

Over 25 years of diversified and comprehensive experience in sales, training, relationship building and product education within the Southern California real estate industry with an emphasis on title insurance.

Currently involved with Listingbook LLC, a company partnered with SoCal MLS, that is an MLS-wide service that connects agents and their clients through an integrated platform of client management, sales productivity and direct marketing tools.

Designed to complement your MLS system, Listingbook™ automates, tracks and analyzes client activities in an agent-managed, real time environment that encourages collaboration and communication.

An Intelligent Virtual Assistant working 24/7 for agents, Listingbook™ saves agents and their clients time, makes agents more knowledgeable, speeds the sale and results in more satisfied buyers & sellers, creating clients for life.

For Buyers
Conducts instant property searches
Obtains personalized service from agents

For Sellers

For Agents
