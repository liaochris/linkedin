Kara L. Geiger’s Summary

I have 5 years experience as a professional fundraiser for both public and private non-profit organizations. I have successfully raised private funds through various special events, grants, and direct one-on-one solicitation. My strengths include building and nurturing long-term relationships with civic groups, volunteers, sponsors, and donors.

Outside of work, I serve as a Board Member for the North Dakota Humanities Council, and Social Team Co-Lead for the Bismarck-Mandan Young Professionals Network.

As a native North Dakotan and young professional, I am proud to help build the future of our state by providing local communities with the tools they need to successfully build endowment and promote philanthropy.
