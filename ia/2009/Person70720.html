Eileen Borromeo’s Summary

Eileen Borromeo works as an Account Planner for Bartle, Bogle & Hegarty where she is working on Vaseline in the Asia Pacific. She is also on Asia Pacific team for British Airways and the global team for Surf.

Prior to joining BBH, she was with Ogilvy & Mather (Singapore) Pte Ltd where she was on regional Nestlé (Milo) and Kimberly Clark (Kotex) as well as various Ogilvy Healthworld accounts.

Before moving to Singapore, Eileen was the regional planner on Kimberly Clark (Kotex) for SADEC, and has worked on FMCG clients like Unilever and Nestle in the Philippines. She has also been involved with Nike, Motorola, and GSK. She also lends her expertise to CRM and direct communications, working with clients like Unicef, Western Union, and Nestlé Nutren. Her involvement in PR has resulted in the acquisition of clients like Starbucks and Greenwich (the number one pizza chain in the Philippines). Prior to Ogilvy, where she's been with for the past four years; she was Business Unit Manager for GMA New Media, Inc., the digital arm of GMA Network Centre, the Philippines' number one television network.

Eileen Borromeo is also an award-winning planner, having a bronze from the Asian Marketing Effectiveness Awards in 2006 for her work on Gillette, and creative awards from Cannes and Clio for the Philippine Daily Inquirer.
