Greg Garner’s Summary

Professionally, I have over a decade of challenging experiences in different entrepreneur ventures in health care, real estate, online marketing / SEO, technology, e-commerce, education and property management.

Although my training and early experience was in IT, my real forte has been in creating companies, positioning them in a market and selling them. I have helped several companies take a new product to market or position themselves to be acquired.

I started my first company, an IT consulting firm, in the late '90s which grew to over 150 consultants in Texas, California and Colorado.

In 2002 I sold my second company, a online health care training company, to an investment group. The product is still in use by Scripps Network and the US Air Force.

Over the past several years I have either founded or taken an equity position in several companies in real estate, health care training, online marketing and more. I have been able to utilize my SEO/SEM knowledge to position the companies in leading positions in their respective markets.

I also do SEO/SEM for clients.

See my Past Positions for the old IT experience that no one cares about any more.
