Frederic Court’s Experience

  • Board Member

    Echovox

    (Privately Held; Telecommunications industry)

    February 2007 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    Investor and Director in Echovox, a leading mobile monetisation company based in Geneva and Palo Alto. Echovox is behind the rapidly growing ZONG (www.zong) mobile micro-payment service focused on consumer web, gaming and social media monetisation. Led Advent's growth capital investment in the company.

  • Board Member

    Fizzback

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 2006 — Present (2 years 8 months)

    Led Advent's investment in Fizzback, a SAAS provider of customer experience management solutions to the retail, financial services and telecommunications industries.

  • Board Member

    Qype

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    August 2006 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    Investor and Board Member at Qype (www.qype.com), Europe's leading local search and review site. Led Advent's seed investment and subsequent Series A and B investments with Partech International and Wellington Partners.

  • General Partner

    Advent Venture Partners

    (Privately Held; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    March 2001 — Present (8 years 4 months)

    Advent Venture Partners is one of the most experienced technology growth and venture capital firms in Europe. Established in 1981 it invests in both the Technology and Life Sciences sectors and has over $1bn under management from institutional investors across Europe and the USA. Technology investments include DailyMotion (world's largest independent UGC video site), Cartesis (now part of Business Objects/SAP), Snell & Wilcox (leading digital media infrastructure technology company), Ubiquisys (fixed-mobile convergence technology), Qype (Europe's leading local search and recommendation web site) and Echovox (Mobile Media technology company).
    For more information: www.adventventures.com

  • Investor and Board Observer

    Snell & Wilcox

    (Privately Held; Broadcast Media industry)

    May 2002 — May 2007 (5 years 1 month)

    Advent acquired a large majority stake in Snell & Wilcox (S&W) in 2002 and subsequently restructured the business, brought in new management and repositioned S&W for profitable growth. S&W is one of the leading independent providers of technology to the broadcast media industry with global presence (sales >$50m). Played key roke in due diligence, investment and business restructuring, notably on the debt front.

  • Investor and Board Observer

    Cartesis

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    May 2003 — April 2007 (4 years)

    Advent invested in the Cartesis buy-out (growth capital deal) and co-led the turnaround of the company and subsequent sale to BOBJ for $300m. Cartesis is a specialist in enterprise performance management (EPM) software with more than 1,300 customers worldwide. Cartesis provides financial reporting, consolidations, and planning capabilities mainly to multinationals with a turnover > $100m.

  • Co-Founder and Managing Director

    Etexx

    (Internet industry)

    September 1999 — January 2001 (1 year 5 months)

    Co-founded ETEXX in France in 1999, an online marketplace for the European textile industry (with premium content, community features, e-collaboration and online transaction tools). Venture capital backed (Partech International, Global Retail Partners, CIR).

  • Senior Associate

    Lazard

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Investment Banking industry)

    1994 — 1999 (5 years)

    Corporate finance - based in london, Milan and New-York over 5-year period
    Initially generalist advising FTSE100 companies on large, complex cross-border transactions (mainly M&A)
    Co-founder of Lazard Telecom and Technology group in Europe in 1997 (London)
    Based in Lazard New-York office in 1999 with focus on mobile communications and Internet transactions
