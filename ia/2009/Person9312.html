Alisa Bonsignore’s Summary

My professional experience can be summarized in four words: I clarify complex ideas.

I specialize in taking often complicated corporate messages and product information and speaking to audiences in a voice that they can understand, whether they're C-level executives, technical specialists or consumers.

My areas of expertise include medical devices and healthcare, networking and security, and healthcare IT.

I am actively looking for contract/freelance writing assignments.

Alisa Bonsignore’s Specialties:

I excel at all types of writing, from more technical documents, such as whitepapers, datasheets and clinical summaries, to more marketing-focused work in the area of brochures, web copy and promotions.
