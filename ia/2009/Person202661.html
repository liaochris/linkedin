Rohan Jacob’s Summary

Before co-founding Q5 Capital, I worked at DSP Merrill Lynch, Merrill Lynch in London and at Credit Suisse First Boston.

We provide strategic advisory services to Indian and international companies. These services include evaluation of an entry into certain businesses and industries, assistance with entering new geographic markets, strategic alliances and combinations of businesses including acquisitions and sales of strategic stakes and M&A.

We focus on large and mid-market clientele. Our clients include leading Indian and international information technology, media, insurance/financial services, hospitality, leisure and utilities companies.

Rohan Jacob’s Specialties:

We have a network of affiliates and advisers that often bring relevant expertise and ideas to bear for our clients' benefit.
We also have an alliance with a leading international real estate consultant for transactions in that sector.
A niche area of our advisory portfolio is in golf - we have done some interesting work in advising on the golf business and also have an alliance with India's most accomplished golf course designer.
