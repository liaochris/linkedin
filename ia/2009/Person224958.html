Steve Sparling’s Summary

Is President and the founder of a Career Mapping firm which help top "A" professionals in their career transition. Steve is available as a career agent/coach for a few select executives.He has a 12 step program to guide, make referrals and coach executives until they find and make a successful career change. Much of his insight comes from being a Senior Executive Search Consultant and Senior Vice President of Human Resources. For more information on his services as a Career Coach or Retained Executive Search Consultant e-mail him at ssparling@bellsouth.net.
