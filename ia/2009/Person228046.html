    PINE TREE:

    The PINE TREE project consists of building 89 windmills in the Tehachapi Mountains, building about 30 miles of difficult and treacherous access roads, and 8 miles of 230Kv transmission line. PTEI used Primavera P6 for schedule control.

    Responsibilities include:

    Establish scheduling polices for all project schedule components.

    Participate in scope definition process to ensure adequate understanding of project objectives and goals.

    Facilitate communication with the subject matter experts to define the WBS and activity list in order to complete the project scope using consistent nomenclature and activity granularity.
    Sequencing the activity by defining dependencies and identify constraints in order to develop logical relationships between activities.

    Estimates activities durations via appropriate techniques in order to schedule dates and identify critical path(s).

    Create baseline schedule through standard techniques in order to proceed with project execution.

    Establish the frequency, format, and content of status reporting from activity owners to project scheduler necessary needed to keep schedule status accurate and up-to-date.

    Incorporate the approved changes into schedule.

    Document all causes / reasons for the schedule changes.

    Update schedule activities on regular basis to include recording actual start dates, actual finish dates, activity progress, estimate to complete(ETC).
