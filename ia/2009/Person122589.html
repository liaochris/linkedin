Jay S. Lim’s Summary

An HFI Certified Usability Analyst that offers extensive strategic and hands-on experience in the fields of human factors and visual design from concept to deployment. An advocate and passionate practitioner of the User Experience (UX) methodology as a designer, analyst and architect with over 15 years of experience within the IT and design industries.

Proven ability to balance creative concepts and processes with a focus on bottom line business needs. Highly structured with excellent operational, interpersonal and communication skills and instinctively insightful of client needs. Thrives on working in fast-paced, diverse and interesting projects. Skills are easily bridged between various industries and environments.
