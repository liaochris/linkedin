Arvind Jain’s Summary

Arvind Jain is Enterprise Architect and SOA Communication Manager for Cisco IT and focuses on enabling Cisco Business capabilities using strategic use of Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) and Cloud Computing. He also leads the SOA Communication and Collaboration initiative using social media and web 2.0 approach and through the rapid adoption of in house developed SOA Platform offered as a service (PaaS) to all of Cisco IT teams.

Most recently his team was awarded "Best overall SOA implementation award" by the CIO Magazine based on a SOA Consortium Case study about Cisco’s "Commerce Transformation" initiative.

He has over 11 years of experience in business development, product management, marketing, SaaS, SOA Platform, strategy and negotiations. Most recently, he was with Oracle USA where he worked on BPM product management and "Go to Market" launch of BPEL Process Manager.

Arvind also has deep domain knowledge and hand on experience in Insurance, Investments and Real Estate industry where he co founded NRILIC eBrokers Inc., an online insurance and residential real estate brokerage service.He loves working with customers and partners, bringing innovative solutions to market, created new businesses, and managing high performance teams. He has traveled across the globe to delivered many public speaking engagements on behalf of Oracle for upcoming technologies and product launches. Arvind also held management positions as Board of Director for San Mateo County Workforce Investment Board (WIB) and Northpoint HOA.

Arvind received his B.Tech. (Honors) in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur and holds an MBA from the Carnegie Mellon Tepper School of Business where he specialized in Finance, Marketing and Strategy.
