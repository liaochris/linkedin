Phoebe Chongchua’s Summary

Phoebe Chongchua is a visionary, creator, and author who uses her 20-year journalism background to weave together savvy marketing/media campaigns, events, articles, and profile stories.

She is the Principal of Live Fit: Offering keynoting, seminar/workshop training (live and Webinars), on camera/voice over work, TV/radio hosting, fitness workouts, writing, coaching and consulting services.

Phoebe hosts a home improvement segment called, To Fix It Tuesdays. She also writes real estate columns and her writing is featured in TRUMP: The Best Real Estate Advice I Ever Received, The Complete Idiot’s Guide To Buying Foreclosures, and her own book, If the Trash Stinks: TAKE IT OUT!

Phoebe has extensive experience in the customer service industry. As the former Business Development Director for QSC she worked to create partnerships with real estate and mortgage brokerages to develop a Quality Service strategy to differentiate companies from the competition while providing measurable and independent proof of superior quality customer service to the companies' clients. Customer Satisfaction Ratings help real estate consumers select the best sales associate. Also conduct Quality Service Certification training seminars and Webinars. Help to create brand awareness in the real estate industry as well as with consumers. also holds a real estate license in CA. and has spoken to thousands of people on customer service and real estate.

A veteran TV newscaster, Phoebe, worked as an anchor, news reporter, fitness host, and community affairs specialist for the ABC Affiliate, KGTV.

Phoebe is the founder of www.LiveFitMagazine.com a resource site for busy people who want quick information on real estate, finances, overall well being (physical fitness, nutrition, and mental health) traveling, and philanthropy.
