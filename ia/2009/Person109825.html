Jennifer Saake (Camp)’s Summary

In 1994 my husband and I developed a non-profit organization designed to offer information, hope and support to families facing infertility, pregnancy loss, infant death and adoption-related challenges. I served as General Director of Hannah's Prayer Ministries from January 1995 - May 2001, remained on the governing board of directors through April 2003, and currently serve as an message board forums moderator and honorary board member, retaining the title director emeritus.

During my season of active directorship God grew our ministry from a small handful of families seeking local support in northern California to an international outreaching impacting tens of thousands of families in over 30 countries. I was primarily responsible for writing and developing all of our training and ministry materials including our leadership training handbook, a website with nearly 500 pages of content, and editing our quarterly newsletter from 1995 through 2001.

In May 2005 my first book, Hannah's Hope: Seeking God's Heart in the Midst of Infertility, Miscarriage & Adoption Loss, was published by NavPress. I am now beginning the process of writing a book on living with chronic pain and/or illness.

I am a happy homeschool mom of 3 living miracles and enjoy my work as Beaty Advisor for a quality line of mineral makeup called Affordable Mineral Makeup™ and a Fashion Consultant with Gurrlie Girl™, a Christian jewelry line. As an outgrowth of my interest in healthy, natural beauty products I offer Inner Beauty Girlz Blogs, using outward beauty to exhibit life lessons in inner beauty.
