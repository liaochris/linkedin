Valerie Rigsbee’s Summary

I am a freelance programmer, designer and webmaster who offers affordable web work for individuals and businesses using innovative concepts incorporating the latest in web technologies and trends. Experience in: HTML, xHTML, CSS, Javascript, Flash, Photoshop, Search Engine Optimization, Revenue Earning Options.

I am currently doing web work for a health insurance company based out of Bloomington, but am still available to take on additional projects. Please feel free to contact me for an estimate. Sample work is available at http://valrigsbee.com/portfolio.

My ongoing web project is The Broadway Musical Home (http://broadwaymusicalhome.com), a site I began in high school for fun, which has since grown into a revenue earning gem that sees over 190,000 hits a day. At present I am the site's only employee - managing the updates and responding to inquiries.

I am also an actress/singer/dancer in the Twin Cities, having worked with Theatre in the Round, Sandbox Theatre, Working Shadow Theater Company, Scrimshaw Productions, Theatre Unbound, ThE BRiNK and Bedlam Theatre among others. News, media and a complete acting resume is available at http://valrigsbee.com/actor.
