John Meyerson’s Summary

Follow me on Twitter! http://twitter.com/john_meyerson

I enjoy putting together research projects, identifying trends and statistical bellwethers, and figuring out where businesses and industries are headed, based on sound research and analysis, survey instruments, and interviews.

My role at Alsbridge is to keep our consultants and our clients up to date on what's happening in the outsourcing industry. Most of my work is available only to Alsbridge clients in our consulting engagements, but I also write and edit a series of articles with our consultants, and put together web seminars and white papers that are made available to professionals who are interested in advice on sourcing, outsourcing, shared services, and benchmarking.

I've been in research and analysis since college, and have an objective of launching my own consulting firm sometime in the future.
