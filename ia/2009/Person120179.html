Jorge Ojeda’s Summary

My professional experience is mainly focused in the financial market having worked with Household Finance where I received my initial trainng, and having moved and worked with various mortgage banking firms since. My background in the mortgage banking arena has focused on audit, loan shipping and MBS fulfillment, and closing to the Servicing side to encompass default management including Collections, Loss Mitigation, Bankruptcy, and Foreclosure including close coordination with REO. I have also worked in international mortgage banking with successful operation of a mortgage lending operation providing financing to US clients purchasing, constructing, and refinancing in Mexico.
