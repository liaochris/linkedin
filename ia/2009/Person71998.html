Ellen Deason’s Summary

As Director of Operations and Recruiting for Thompson Interim Professionals, I manage the operations and specialize in the recruitment and placement of high-level accounting and finance professionals to businesses on a temporary, part-time or consulting basis.
My career began in personnel services in San Diego in 1988 and quickly advanced to supervisory and management roles due to a strong business savvy and talent for building client relationships. Before joining Thompson Interim, I managed accounts for several well-established recruiting and personnel services in the region, growing and developing operations and sales.
A native of San Diego, I have witnessed firsthand its incredible economic expansion and know where and how to find the financial talent and expertise that my clients require. I have a true passion for my work and take great pride in placing the most-qualified professional with the right employer. My exemplary reputation in the recruiting field has allowed me to foster long-term relationships with clients, and candidates alike, many of whom I have been working with for over 15 years.

True to my San Diego roots, I'm a fan of the outdoors and play almost as hard as I work....Leading an active lifestyle enjoying extreme activities such as riding quads in the desert and taking long rides on the Harley Davidson. Very few people know that I'm a former World Skateboarding Champion; holding the World Freestyle title in the 1970’s actively competing throughout the U.S., Europe and Japan.
