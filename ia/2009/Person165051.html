Mike Warner’s Summary

As the COO of Egami Consulting Group, a boutique urban marketing firm, I lend my unique project management experience and out-of-the-box creative direction to Egami’s fast-growing clientele which includes Heineken, TAG Body Spray and TAG Records, Jostens, and Russell Simmons’ owned RUSH Communications. For the past 3 years I have worked closely with Teneshia Jackson, CEO/CCO of Egami Consulting in creating a unique marketing experience for our clients. As an advocate for education, I have developed an exciting program for high school students called "Class Is In" which is designed to provide young people with viable career options by allowing them access to executives in those fields.

I am also a 20-year veteran of the music industry. My unique history goes as far back as to have produced songs on demos for Hip Hop icons such as Jay Z and Nas. I hold degrees in music theory from The Royal Schools of London and taught piano while studying electrical engineering at Brooklyn Technical High School in New York City where I am from. As a song writer and producer, I have written and produced for the biggest selling Japanese artist of all time, Hikaru Utada, as well as numerous artists in many other genres of music such as Big Daddy Kane (rap), Jade (R&B), Snow (Reggae) and Seduction (dance). As a manager and producer, I currently have an artist signed to Atlantic Records named Bayje (pronounced beige) who is set to release an album in 2009. I have closed various types of deals with most of the major companies in the industry including my own music publishing deal with Sony and I've managed budgets of over $1Million. In addition I am publishing a series of books called “The Atlas Series” to provide invaluable insight and information on succeeding in the music industry and the true value of marketing your passion. The Music Producer's Atlas will be available in 2009.

Also check out Teneshia Jackson's inspiring keynote "LIFE BULB MOMENTS" at www.teneshiajackson.com.
