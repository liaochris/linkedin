Sara Emami’s Summary

I am the President and Founder of PG Enterprises (Progressive Generation) which promotes awareness of various human rights causes, campaigns and movements. My company is responsible for the maintenance of multimedia branding related to our cause, the creation of documentaries, performing research, conducting interviews, and public speaking at businesses and academic institutions. Prior to the formation of my business ventures, I worked in the law enforcement field and counter-terrorism intelligence (TXDPS, DHS, NCIC and local law enforcement) for a combined 4 years and eleven months.

I currently posses a Master of Liberal Arts degree and my future goals include the pursuance of a Doctor of Philosophy degree (PhD) in History, Literature and/or Women's Studies. In addition, I am currently a Professor of History and am eager to continue to educate the masses on the college level. Academia and research are the two driving forces that serve my future and long-term goals.

My academic research is centered on German History and Literature. Below, you will find my abstract:

Master's Abstract:

The legend of the Grail is significant in its universal contribution to the historical, literary, and political spheres. History confirms the transformation of the Grail from its original Celtic identity into a Christian symbol much like the metaphysical mutations of Christian theology. The Grail legend as illustrated in European literature influences the policies of political leaders, resulting in the shifting destiny of nations governed by these leaders. The story of the Grail as depicted in Wolfram von Eschenbach’s Parzival became central in the Nazis’ attempt to exploit the legend of the Grail to further a fascist, political scheme. Understanding how Hitler in his power misappropriated a powerful symbol like the Grail shows us how human rights abuses are justified.
