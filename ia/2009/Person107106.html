J.D. GERSHBEIN’s Summary

J.D. GERSHBEIN is the Founder & President of OWLISH COMMUNICATIONS and a one-of-a-kind Internet marketing specialist. He is a gifted wordsmith who lends a distinctive voice to LinkedIn profiles and helps Owlish clients achieve mastery of LinkedIn through focused, one-on-one training. His scholarly approach to personal branding also includes customized business cartoons, comic strips, or cartoon characters as product mascots. He is perhaps best known as the creator of THE HANDSHAKE CLUB ™, a comic strip that takes jabs at Social Media and Business Networking and features such personalities as Will Banter ™ and Brandy Share ™.

J.D. is a classic example of a successful adaptation to the changing landscape of business. Noticing the paradigm shift away from traditional Internet marketing, he became a student of Social Media ~ primarily LinkedIn ~ with the goal of helping his clients achieve greater visibility and cultivate business opportunities on the platform. According to J.D., “The road to online success goes through LinkedIn.” Today, he is a full-immersion LinkedIn trainer and in especially high demand as a LinkedIn speaker and panelist. J.D. fashions unique LinkedIn profiles and provides customized LinkedIn training for individuals and businesses. J.D. also unburdens busy entrepreneurs and C-level executives through the ongoing administration of their LinkedIn activities.

J.D. is also a Master Cartoonist whose artwork has been showcased across a wide variety of media. He can craft a customized business-themed cartoon, comic strip, character or mascot that is specific to your enterprise or industry and that will make you memorable.
