Jack Buser’s Summary

Dynamic, visionary and results-oriented technology executive with ten years developer relations, business development, licensing, marketing, and product development experience in the entertainment technology, audio/video, consumer electronics, games, and Internet entertainment industries responsible for initiatives and business segments generating up to $100 million per year.
