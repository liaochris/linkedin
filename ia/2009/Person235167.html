Todd Greco’s Summary

Interactive advertising professional with over 14 years of experience spanning financial services (iShares, Visa), consumer goods (Molson, CapnCrunch), technology (Adobe, Intel), and even industrial b2b (Agilent, Parker-Hannifin). I run departments of talented designers, interface directly with clients, and build award-winning sites and online tools. When I'm not doing all of that, I spend my free time teaching undergrads the finer points of interactive design. I never sleep.

Todd Greco’s Specialties:

Flash, teaching, mentoring, javascript, jsp, xml, css, photoshop, cs3, cs2, audio production and editing, server building, programming, graphic design, interface design, information architecture, usability, management, creative direction, code review, client-facing, project management
