Jeff Barnes’s Summary

Jeff is a Senior Technical Architect with over 15 years experience designing, developing and maintaining mission-critical IT and shrink-wrapped applications for the utilities, government and telecom industries. He is often commended by clients for his skill in problem solving, design, coding and communication. His approachability, integrity and skill in quickly understanding work and social systems are cited frequently as great assets to the projects and clients for whom he works. As someone with a "hands on" affinity, he rolls up his sleeves and gets to work, mentors junior developers, and communicates well with management.

Prior to joining the Electric Reliability Council of Texas (ERCOT), Jeff designed and developed IT applications to support Cingular/AT&T Mobile company operations. As a member of the COMET/ORBIT team, Jeff helped implement the billing and collections systems to which 23 million Cingular accounts were migrated. He also designed, implemented and rolled out solutions to support wireless networking processes, level 2 and 3 subscriber network troubleshooting tools and web based technical support operator administration.

Since then, Jeff has developed a practice in ISO Consulting at ERCOT and PJM.

Jeff is interested in application and system design and development using UML, Java, C++, Oracle, Linux, Apache Tomcat, MySQL, PHP and Perl.

He is also a very strong proponent of open source technology.

Jeff Barnes’s Specialties:

Enterprise Archtecture and Development, UML, XML, Database Design and Application Development, PL/SQL, Oracle Database Administration.\
