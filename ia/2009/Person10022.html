Allyn Armstrong’s Summary

Genius is the gold in the mine; talent is the miner who brings it out; expertise understands the market demand - Lord Blessington

ViP was founded on the premise of networking the collective synergy and intellectual assets of the very "best in breed" small and medium sized product, service, and idea leaders; desiring to leverage their core competences into the Marketplace more efficiently and effectively.

This uniquely structured business relationship creates a powerful "win-win" collaborative and globally connected "Marketplace Affinity Group" focused at wealth creation. The benefit is simple; integrating our business development initiatives, assuring depth of services delivery expertise, and leveraging our combined purchasing power.

With this business proposition, we have developed a refreshing approach to create, sell, and deliver eminence across a variety of industries and organizations.

We take advantage of acedemic research, independent service providers, business entities, business process outsourcers, and excess corporate capacities desiring to leverage their core competences into the marketplace.

Thus, creating a powerful virtual delivery network, product & service supply chain, and a global trading environment aligned to dominate our clients' respective markets.
