Mike Burns’s Summary

SENIOR LEVEL MANAGEMENT

My fifteen years of continuous growth and promotions through various segments of the real estate industry will enable me to lead your organization. The current economic climate necessitates the redistribution of assets. This redistribution presents the opportunity for me to join your organization. My articulate and personable leadership style coupled with my extensive real estate knowledge will allow us to intelligently grow your assets. I respect that we are in an economic climate that necessitates careful consideration on all business decisions. I am confident that your decision to bring me into a senior level management position in your organization will add significant value to your executive management team.

In my next senior level position, I look forward to continuing my responsibilities as a Director at Community Development Group and as a Vice President on numerous Management Associations & Metropolitan District Finance Boards;

•Creating and implementing business plans for complex real estate assets
•Advising principals on the economic and political implications of their investment decisions
•Overseeing the strategic implementation of the business plans
•Communicating issues and progress to the principals
•Articulating the project vision in contentious public environments
•Creating and maintaining relationships with the media, public officials and local constituencies
•Marketing, negotiating contracts and closing assets
•Negotiating complex entitlement and contract documents
•Managing construction contracts, budgets, schedules and cost control mechanisms
