Raymond Reid’s Summary

A passionate Internet marketing and advertising professional with 12+ years of experience working within and for organizations focused upon communicating and advertising to consumers online. Core focus upon leveraging consumer driven data to better understand and highlight patterns of behaviour, demographic insights, and media opportunities. Direct experience working within diverse industries such as telecommunications, publishing, syndicated research, loyalty/rewards, and broadcast media.

Interested in opportunities to leverage broad range of experiences in digital media across academia, broadcasting, start-ups, media and communications, as well as internationally.
