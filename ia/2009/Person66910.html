Eric Drake’s Summary

Digital Marketing and Corporate Marketing Events Planner serving San Francisco, Silicon Valley, and most recently, the Monterey Bay Area.

I am currently serving on the Board of Directors as Vice President of Events with the San Francisco chapter of the American Marketing association (SFAMA) and as the Event Director for Santa Cruz NEXT - informing, inspiring and involving the next generation of civic and political action in Santa Cruz.

Eric Drake’s Specialties:

Conceptual marketing and creative services working in the events planning and marketing events industry. I have worked primarily with corporate clients but have extensive experience in social engagements ranging from managing and marketing events for artists like the Rolling Stones, golf events at Pebble Beach and weddings for baseball super stars like Jason Giambi.
