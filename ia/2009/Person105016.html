Jason Gordon’s Summary

I currently head up development on a variety of web application projects. The most recent focus has been in the social network arena using drupal as the software framework of choice. Earlier in my professional career I focused primarily on tools for game and film production, specifically in the way of digital resource management. I am a skilled programmer with experience in most common web languages, but most experienced with PHP server-side and Javascript client side, with many years perfecting XHTML and CSS.

My goal for the future is to form a team of web developers, not all necessarily programmers, who are energetic and talented. I'd like to lead that team to create web applications that blend practicality from a business sense, with the fun of games. My vision is to create a place where passionate game developers of all ages can come together and collaborate to have fun and realize dreams.
