Brandon Tilus’s Summary

Oddly as part of the last generation to remember a time before Facebook, YouTube and the Internet, I grew up teaching my elders how to use them.

Formerly of the Southwest Marketing Advisory Center, where I served as Managing Director, I'm now with Insight Marketing Design in Sioux Falls as a Client Service Executive.

I spend my days developing marketing strategies for web, print and non-traditional uses, uncovering web trends for different demographics and answering questions like, “What’s the difference between a Zune and an iPod?”, “How do I do that on Facebook?” and “Would you reach up and change that light bulb?”
