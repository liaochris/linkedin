David Berney’s Summary

After ten years in the interactive industry, seven of which were spent at Avenue A \ Razorfish, I have joined Sapient as Vice President Experience Marketing in New York City. This exciting opportunity opens new doors to provide companies with a broader range of solutions, a proven commitment to client success, and greater capabilities in business consulting, marketing, advertising, and technology.

Prior to Sapient, David developed strategies and technology enabled programs for new global clients and leads customer relationships for companies such as Lenovo, a 5 Billion dollar Entertainment brand, Multi-Billion dollar Dubai - UAE based marketplace, US Navy/ Computer Science Corporation, CIBA Vision, Liberty Travel, Norwegian Cruise Lines, Cendant Corporation, Zyman Group, Coca Cola, BlueCross BlueShield, NetBank, and the Taj Hotels Resorts and Palaces, to name a few.

Before joining Avenue A I Razorfish, David led Delta Air Lines first incubated company, MYOBTravel, and was responsible for the eBusiness initiatives related to the Air Lines interactions with small businesses. David was also a member of the senior management team working to deliver Delta’s consumer eBusiness strategy.

Prior to Delta Air Lines, David was the Chief Marketing Officer for eHatchery, a seed-stage investment company with venture funding by Credit Suisse, UPS, IdeaLab, and Cox Enterprises. David led the marketing and brand development for eHatchery and six start-up companies under the advisement of Sergio Zyman, former Chief Marketing Officer of the Coca Cola Company.

David spent ten years working in the advertising and media business for divisions of Time Warner and American Express. He attended Phillips Academy in Andover, Mass., studied International Business and Marketing at Franklin College in Lugano, Switzerland, and received his BA in Economics from Emory University.

David Berney’s Specialties:

David brings over 18 years of global marketing knowledge in related disciplines including strategic marketing, knowledge management portals, internet/intranet strategy, ecommerce, and brand creations/awareness.
