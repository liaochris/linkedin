Nikki Sweet’s Summary

Music veteran Nikki Sweet has worn just about every hat there is in the business. Along with being a top talent buyer for the last seventeen years, she has also been a manager, show producer, charity event coordinator, promoter, and has worked in A&R, artist development, and public relations. This has given her the unique ability to deal with artists, agents, managers and record company presidents with equal aplomb resulting in an ability to create, promote, produce and publicize events that is unparalleled in the business.

The concept for ‘The SWEET Life by Nikki Sweet & Company {LifeStyle Assistance}’ started two years ago with a life-changing experience. As a result, this prompted Nikki Sweet to decide to utilize her talent and abilities as well as skill sets that she developed over 22 years in the music industry as a talent buyer/artist liaison/club manager and event coordinator to transition in to a service-oriented career.

What is The SWEET Life?

The SWEET Life by Nikki Sweet & Company {LifeStyle Assistance} enables you to keep your world running smoothly so you have more time to spend doing what is important to you. What can we do for you to make your life run more smoothly? There are often insufficient hours in the day to undertake tasks at work, home, or organizing a special event. We can manage the details, large or small and we will work with you to put together a short- or long-term personalized service plan that utilizes strategies that fulfill the individual client's particular needs and provide complete peace of mind. We place the utmost importance on creating a relationship with each client based on trust, confidentiality, and understanding. Most importantly, The SWEET Life by Nikki Sweet & Company {LifeStyle Assistance} is Entertainment-Industry friendly.
