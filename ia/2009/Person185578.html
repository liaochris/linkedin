Peter Fraser’s Summary

I'm an established freelance graphic designer / art director in Portland, with 14 years of experience doing formative work on national and regional brands; especially in sales collateral, identity work, advertising, packaging, annual reports, catalogs, trade displays, web design and point-of-purchase materials.

I often do work for companies and organizations that prefer to deal directly with someone who can art direct, design, and write copy; rather than a larger, more expensive agency whom may or may not prioritize their needs. I am also frequently contracted by larger agencies to work on their accounts as well, so I understand the pros and cons of both approaches.

In whichever case, I make my living helping clients, large and small, from consumer goods to apparel, from aquaculture to hi-tech, successfully extend their brand identities in their respective competitive marketplaces, and help them reach and serve their customers. I learn my clients' businesses, and strive to become a true partner in their endeavors.

In addition, I am reliably creative, dependable, organized and ethical; and have an extensive portfolio of quality design work. I genuinely am someone who can, and does, take a multitude of projects from start to finish, year after year; on time and on budget. I place a premium on honoring client confidentiality, and strictly avoiding conflicts of interest. And I try to build productive, long-term business relationships; with high degrees of creativity, efficiency, and satisfaction all around.

What else? Well, I love to do humor. I'm a nationally published cartoonist, and I've created a T-shirt and novelty beverage line. I expect to have my online store up and losing money (did I really say that?), in 2009.

SOFTWARE & SYSTEM:
I run a Mac-based system (OS 10.5) with Adobe Creative Suite 3 (Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Dreamweaver), as well as Office 2008 and Quark 6.5.

Peter Fraser’s Specialties:

Art direction, graphic design, copywriting, branding, sales collateral, packaging, advertising, concept work, logos, fast turnaround creative, humor.
