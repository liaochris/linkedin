Mark D Grissom’s Summary

My professional experience has included ten years of active military service, four years expeience Directing or Managing Health and Safety Operations for small and medium sized employers, and over fifteen years of professional experience in Performance Improvement, Training Development, and Training Delivery.

My professional goals include securing employement with an ethical, people focused, and innovative organization strong enough to take a look at themselves when necessary and always aggressively moving to better their performance.

My professional goals also include introducing a new school of thought to Corporate America, Cooperative Performance Improvement. This school of thought focuses on "the activities between people and the systems and structures that allow those activities to allow, prevent, or significantly influence performance and or outcomes."
