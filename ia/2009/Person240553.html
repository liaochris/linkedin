Lauri Romanzi’s Summary

Dr. Lauri Romanzi is a reconstructive pelvic surgeon and urogynecologist with 25 years of clinical experience. In addition to her surgical and clinical work at home and abroad, she founded PHIT (pelvic health integrated techniques), a uniquely feminine medical spa, in July 2008. Later that year, Beauty Call Books publishing house was launched with the book "Plumbing and Renovations". Associate Professor at Weill Cornell Medical Center/New York Presbyterian Hospital in New York City, she has served on the national and international advisory boards of many pharmaceutical and medical device corporations such as Pharmacia, Pfizer, Novartis, Yamanouchi, TENA, Procter and Gamble, Ethicon, and Boston Scientific.
Author of a number peer reviewed medical manuscripts, monographs and medical text chapters, she is on the Editorial Board of the Journal of Gender Specific Medicine and the Highlights Editorial Board of the Journal of Sexual Medicine. She is a reviewer for several medical journals, including but not limited to Urology, The Journal of Urology, The Journal of Sexual Medicine, the Journal of Gender Specific Medicine, and the International Urogynecology Journal.
International collaboration in developing nations as visiting professor and surgeon includes the International Organization of Women and Development, Harvard's Humanitarian Initiative, Surgeons OverSeas, and Weill Cornell Medical Center.
