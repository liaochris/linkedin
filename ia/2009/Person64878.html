Douglas Maxwell Smith’s Summary

As an experienced advertising strategist and manager, I have worked with all forms of online and offline strategy (corporate reputation, campaigning, e-CRM, online PR, brand engagement, brand planning and management), and with wider aspects of the marketing remit such as; partnership strategy, integrated campaign planning and brand definition & positioning.

My mission as a strategist is to reconnect organisations and brands to their customer base and to bridge the gap that is created in the way corporations communicate in the online space.

Douglas Maxwell Smith’s Specialties:
