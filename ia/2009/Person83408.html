Gareth Young’s Summary

For 15 years at BellSouth I was responsible for developing and executing business expansion and rationalization strategies through M&A, partnerships, and other corporate and commercial transactions. I helped businesses identify strategic needs, establish priorities and plans, and execute on their plans. Where alliances, acquisitions, or divestitures were required, I established search criteria, screened and selected candidates, and staffed and led teams to negotiate, execute and close transactions.

When I left BellSouth in 2006, I founded finanSight which, served small/medium businesses and startups for four years and helped them drive growth, improved financial performance, enhanced cashflow, and value creation with a variety of niche services.

My 2009 accomplishments are: publishing my first novel, The Grace of Guilt; going to South Africa on vacation with my Dad; running my business successfully and profitably through the financial storm; and joining Abraxas and the M&A and business brokerage world..
