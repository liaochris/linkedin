Gary Young’s Summary

Universal Vacations have been offering sales and rental management since 1986. The company originally opened offices in New Port Richey Florida providing property management to a number of British homeowners needing someone to look after their properties whilst they were not using them. Universal Vacations then began to market and operate offices in Sarasota/Bradenton - New Port Richey - Miami and Fort Myers which is where the companies head office and central reservations office is based. Universal Vacations have contracts to supply vacation accomadations to the Worlds leading Travel Partners and vacationers from all walks of life.

Gary Young’s Specialties:

Promoting tourism in Fort Myers - Bradenton - Miami, building relationships with many industry leaders from North America, South America and Europe. Improving and making more accessible the self-catering accommodation concept.
