Steven Swimmer’s Summary

Steven Swimmer is an innovative media professional with deep experience in the fields of Web, Digital Media and Motion Pictures. His work has spanned strategy, management, creative and technology.

Swimmer has specific experience in online strategy, product management, creative direction, technology management, production, analysis, content, information architecture and integration. His digital media experience includes work on major entertainment and museum Web destinations, digital video, imaging, museum kiosks, corporate presentations and electronic gaming.

Swimmer is a filmmaker who co-produced the hit independent feature film, Gabriela. His experience includes writing, packaging, developing and marketing feature films. He has also directed music videos, documentaries and short subjects.

In his spare time, Swimmer is a stand-up comedian, musician and still photographer.
