Erik Swanson’s Summary

Erik's goal is to help individuals and small businesses with their legal problems. He concentrates his practice in litigation, with emphasis on business-to-business disputes, collections and contract disputes, and real estate disputes, inlcuding condominium disputes and landlord-tenant cases.

Erik has handled hundreds of cases and is a regular practitioner in the Cook, DuPage, and Lake County Circuits, as well as in Illinois federal courts. As Plaintiffs' counsel, he has had a significant role in winning millions of dollars for his clients; as Defendants' counsel, he has successfully saved his clients millions of dollars by defeating Plaintiff's lawsuits at summary judgment and on Motions on the pleadings. Erik has taken and defended hundreds of depositions, handled pre-trial and arbitration conferences, and participated in trials.

He is admitted to practice in before the Illinois Supreme Court, all the Federal Courts of Illinois (North, Central, and South) and Wisconsin (East and West), as well as the Northern District of Indiana.

Erik is an active member of the American Bar Association, where he sits on the GPSolo editorial committee, the Illinois State Bar Association, and the Chicago and DuPage Bar Associations.

In addition to his professional involvement, Erik is active in his community. He serves on the Village of Morton Grove Community Relations commission, on the Morton Grove Historical Society Public Relations committee, and serves as Vice President of SkoMor Soccer club.

Erik Swanson’s Specialties:

Business & Corporate Litigation, Condominium Litigation, Collection Litigation, Real Estate Transactions & Litigation, Corporate formation; Contract drafting & review.
