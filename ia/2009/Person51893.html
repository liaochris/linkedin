Dennis Korkchi’s Summary

I have a masters degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Chalmers University of Technology. I am a skilled software developer and test designer with good leadership ability and management skills.
My preferred way of working is writing great software every time, agile, in a team oriented organisation and in a test driven design fashion, preferably in projects where open source software is considered a strength.
In late 2007 I started my own IT consulting firm, CeeMeyo. In 2008 I and a former colleage started Bloo AB, developing two unique and exciting location-based services aimed for mobile phones.

I am a social person, full of initiative, with a strong problem solving analytical mind. I am considered a fast learner with good communication skills and I am comfortable taking responsibility.
