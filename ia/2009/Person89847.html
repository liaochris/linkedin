Greg Woodward, Ph.D.’s Summary

I am a software engineer who is passionate about Ruby and agile development methodologies, including extreme programming (XP). I've got over fifteen years of commercial software development experience, and have worked in Ruby, Java, C++, and C. My background is in dynamical simulation (Ph.D. in M.E. from Stanford in 1992), so in addition to my programming skills I've got a strong math and physics background. I'm very hands-on, but have also functioned as chief architect and have managed development teams of up to 10 people.
