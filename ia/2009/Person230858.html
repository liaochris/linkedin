Terry Toomey’s Summary

Started ZCD Properties Inc. and the Zero College Debt Program to help parents and students finance college using real estate. This program shows students how to generate revenue from rents and pay-as-you go rather than building up huge debts at the end of their college experience. Also providing them with benefits programs, ongoing education and a support network. Go to http://www.zerocollegedebt.com for more information on this program.

Experienced in developing and launching national seminars. The seminar program for the Zero College Debt Program will launch in October 2008. Focus is to educate students on buying real estate, educate them about residency requirements to take advantage of in-state tuition, and develop a support network to assist them through all the phases of a first time home buyer. Did you know that if we can reach just 2% of the 17.5 million college undergraduate and graduate students, we can save them over $14 billion. Take a look at the program and pass the link on to anyone you know with college-bound children.

Member of National Association of Realtors, Texas Association of Realtors, Central Texas Commercial Association of Realtors, CCIM, Austin Board of Realtors.
