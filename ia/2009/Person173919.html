Nichole Chambers’s Summary

I have more than 10 years of marketing, public affairs, governmental affairs and non-profit management experience. Currently, I am the President/CEO of the Dana Point Chamber of Commerce, where I am responsible to the day to day operations, as well as leading growth and development by directing the activities and resources of the organization. I previously served as the Communications and Marketing Manager for the Greater Riverside Chambers of Commerce, where I was responsible for the execution of all marketing plan programs and elements, in addition to visioning and strategic planning. While the managing editor of the Riverside Chambers monthly publication, Greater Riverside Business, I won numerous awards and recognitions from the Western Association of Chamber Executives.

I am a current member of the Western Association of Chamber Executives (WACE) and the Southern California Association of Chamber of Commerce Executives(SCACCE). I often speak at both WACE and SCACCE conferences on marketing and communications issues. Additionally, I often consult with other chambers for assistance and expertise on communications and marketing. I am also a member of American Chamber of Commerce Executives, and a past member of the Southern California Marketing Directors Association. I am an alumnus of the University of California, Berkeley and recently graduated from WACE Academy.
