Anthony (Tony) Riggio’s Experience

  • President

    Coastal Security Group, LLC

    (Security and Investigations industry)

    January 2006 — Present (3 years 8 months)

    Coastal Security Group, LLC (CSG) is a company that deals with security consulting issues and private investigations. The bulk of CSG's work deals with security reviews of small to medium sized business' and large industrial facilities. CSG has specialized in restaurant reviews for numerous security issues including due diligence in the hiring process.

    CSG provides instructions on drug and alcohol abuse and provides counseling on violence in the workplace. The owner of CSG is a CFE (Certified Fraud Examiner)and has provided expert guidance on various fraud investigations. CSG has provided investigative assistance to insurance companies and banks on fraudulent loan matters and has worked with Attornies in both Georgia and Florida on various and complex civil matters, including criminal defense.

    All investigators that work with CSG are either retired FBI special agents or retirees from other Federal, Military investigative service agencies or local and State law enforcement and have extensive experience in numerous areas including crisis management, counter-terrorism, electronic technical matters, computer security, personal protection, polygraph matters, surveillance etc.
