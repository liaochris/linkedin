Peter Larsen’s Summary

I have been a Marketing and Sales Professional for over 20 years, serving in both large and small corporations and privately held companies. All my experience has been in here in the Commonwealth of Virginia and I'm proud of that.

My area of expertise is in Document Management, understanding the document lifecycle from creation to finishing. In 2008 I joined Cobb Technologies to manage their KIP and Canon Large Format product division. I was promoted January 2009 to add Production Print sales to my document management offerings. In August 2009 I added Digital MFP and Document Management Software Solutions to my sales offerings for my clients.

My goal is to earn the trust of my clients through effective follow up and providing a product of value.
