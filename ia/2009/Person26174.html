Ben Cooke’s Summary

“The best of the old school, meets the best of the new”

Several years of tutelage in the methods of “old school” traditional PR combined with a more personal tech savvy approach has allowed Ben to create his own unique approach to public relations focusing on getting the job done in new trendsetting ways while building long lasting relationships. Ben believes strongly that strong relationships, creativity, and hard work are essential to delivering client messages clearly for audiences.

Ben earned his Bachelor’s Degree in Communication from The University of Wisconsin where he honed his story creation skills at writing, directing, and producing for television and film while producing and hosting his own radio program. After moving to Los Angeles in 2000 he broke into production for television and film. In 2003, Ben made the transition to public relations representing clients in a wide array of fields including medical practitioners, product manufacturers, web based technologies, and marketing companies. Ben brings a solid understanding of what it takes to create enticing news media stories for a diverse client base. His passionate ability to work with the media to give them the stories they want has put him on speed dial with producers and journalists at such major news outlets as Fox News Channel, CNN, LA Times, The Wall Street Journal, Forbes, Entertainment Tonight, Extra, Access Hollywood, People Magazine, every television news desk in Los Angeles and the list goes on and on… One of Ben’s greatest joys of PR is getting to know a client’s business almost as well as the client does.

Ben Cooke’s Specialties:

Public Relations! Obtaining media opportunities by booking clients on local and national television programs, radio shows, interviews in magazines, newspaper, and the ever ubiquitous internet.
