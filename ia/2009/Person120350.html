Jose Antonio de la Calle’s Summary

José Antonio de la Calle leads the antitrust team of Baker & McKenzie, Madrid. His practice spans the entire breadth of EC trade and competition/antitrust law advice.

Recent instructions include high-profile merger control assignments, private antitrust cases and cartel investigations concerning the telecoms, industrial equipment, media & entertainment, agrifood and airline industries.

José Antonio is recognised as one of Spain’s leading antitrust lawyers in 'Chambers Europe: Europe's Leading Lawyers for Business'. He is also listed as an expert in his field in ‘Who’s Who in American Law’ and ‘Who’s Who in the World’.

Aside from serving as deputy chairman of the International Club of Corporate Lawyers, Madrid, he is a member of the elite Group of Competition Policy Experts and an antitrust analyst at The Gertrude Ryan Law Observatory.

A practising lawyer since 1998, he is admitted to practice in Madrid (Abogado) and Brussels (Avocat européen).

Jose Antonio de la Calle’s Specialties:

European law, antitrust/competition law, merger control, private antitrust litigation & arbitration, unfair competition, State aids, regulated industries
