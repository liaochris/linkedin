Wallace Rodrigues’s Summary

I'm a designer with strong graphic and web skills established in Oslo, Norway. I love to bend code to my will and create a relevant online experience. I have a strong background in Flash Design and have designed thousands of online ads that were featured in the most prominent Norwegian websites. I've also designed/redesigned several websites which receive thousands of visitors daily, improving their ROI (Return On Investment) and optimizing them for search engines (SEO). I'm particularly found of combining my graphic design skills in Photoshop with hand-coded/standard-compliant/semantic XHTML & CSS, though a bit of PHP and a tad of Javascript always help the recipe. Always interested in broadening my network, attractive freelance contracts and milk chocolate!
