Robert Stehwien’s Summary

Robert Stehwien has been a software developer for 12 years, working mainly as a Windows application developer with some web development in Java. His most recent work has been doing web development with C# and Adobe Flex/AIR. Robert has found he enjoys the Adobe frameworks and ActionScript but is also learning Clojure (Lisp on JVM) to expand his knowledge.

His undergraduate work is in Information Systems, he has a MBA, and taught "Specification of Software and Systems" at the University of New Mexico.

Robert lives in Albuquerque NM with his wife, grandmother, and several small furry children. His hobbies include various forms of exercise, motorcycles, roleplaying games, and someday getting another masters degree or PhD in Computer Science.
