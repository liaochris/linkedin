Will Moore’s Summary

Will has experience with creation and design of Wordpress Blogs and CMS, working with clients various sectors including creative, music and arts as well as business and corporate.

Will specialises in creating holistic web environments, focussing not just on one specific element of social media, but creating a 'web suite' for the client - incorporating a CMS/Blog, Facebook Page, Twitter Account, Flickr etc, as well as creating viral marketing ideas and email campaigns. Through his current role, Will also provides training in effective use of social media platforms.

He is married and lives in Maidstone with his wife - Lydia, border collie and cat. A keen amateur photographer and videographer, Will can often be found around the UK coastline documenting one of his loves - The Sea and UK surf Scene. He is also a keen charity runner and is currently in training for his first London Marathon in 2010.
