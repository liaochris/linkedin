Chris McLellan’s Summary

Chris is an experienced marketer with a proven track record of developing, launching and promoting profitable web, mobile and telecommunications products and services.

A graduate with an Honours BA from Bishops University, Quebec, Canada, Chris has been working in Europe and North America in both B2C and B2B environments for over 15 years.

Chris has an in-depth knowledge of the entire marketing mix with particular experience in the Communications, Social Networking, Interactive Entertainment and Premium Content sectors. His product work has spanned multiple service platforms including Web, Mobile, Switched Voice, IP/Data, Television and iTV/IPTV.

An expert in all forms of web (SEM, SEO, CPA, Email, Social) and mobile web marketing, Chris has also experience in website monetisation/ecommerce and reporting/analytics.

Since 2004 Chris has been driving the design, development and promotion of multi-platform social networking services at 4D Interactive Ltd. in London.

Chris is also a Partner and Contributor at leading music industry blog The Music Void (http://www.themusicvoid.com).
