Jane McLaughlin’s Summary

With 25 years in high-tech, I can help you position your products and services while developing your collateral. Or manage marketing projects that demand clear thinking, strong communications, and an innate sense of the critical path. Able to grapple with ambiguity, probe for details, generate ideas, and ramp quickly with very little information or guidance. My special competencies include:

• Learning about emerging technologies and business models
• Synthesizing, architecting, and demystifying complex information
• Creating benefits-oriented communications that speak to customer needs
• Fostering structure and integration among cross-functional teams
• Bridging creative execution with business strategy
• Maintaining brand integrity across projects and media

Jane McLaughlin’s Specialties:
