Sarah Brown Spoelstra’s Summary

I have been working in the industry for about nearly a decade. I started out my career at a little print shop where I mastered two color design. Then, I moved onto Computer Market Source Magazine where I was in charge of the production and design of a monthly publication, as well as, maintaining and updating the website. My next job took me to Pumpkin Masters, where I refined my design skills—creating displays, packaging, and a website. Next I worked for Scott’s Liquid Gold conceptualizing and designing packaging, point of purchase displays, logos, and more. I moved north to Boulder, and worked for for Covidien for a spell, managing FDA regulated products and production work on collateral materials for those products.

Currently, I run a sucessful freelance business working on anything from websites to packaging to marketing collateral. I also work part-time at Sashco, Inc., designing packaging, point of purchase displays, marketing collateral, logos, trade show graphics, and etc.

Sarah Brown Spoelstra’s Specialties:

Photography background, Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML, InDesign, Quark, Dreamweaver, good relationships with vendors, getting printing quotes, press checks, experience art directing photoshoots, and plays well with others.
