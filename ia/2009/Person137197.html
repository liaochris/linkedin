Lars Kjær Hansen’s Summary

As an experienced and successful project manager - with more than 4 years of team leader responsibility - it is always my goal to make a difference for the projects I am part of. All projects have had a high degree of cross-functional involvement in common. I use my strong business understanding in combination with my strategic and visionary way of thinking to develop objectives and strategies on various levels. I keep a clear overview while I create the necessary structure and achieve the wanted results. Through my ability to listen, my positive way of thinking as well as my empathy I am capable of building good relations to the people I am involved with.

I have the necessary ambitions, abilities and engagement to take the responsibility for challenges, which create value. My next challenge is within project management of internal projects with a high strategic impact in - or in close collaboration with - corporate HQ in an international company.
