Karine Lintvelt’s Summary

I AM A DESIGNER IN THE BROADEST SENSE OF THE WORD
With great enthusiasm and clear vision I specialise in the interior and exterior design of hotels, restaurants, offices, shops and homes, from small to big, from new construction to established buildings.

From simplicity to luxury, from modern to classical, from multicoloured to black and white, my designs exude a feeling of unity and tranquillity. Everything is coordinated to the finest detail. Whether it involves restyling one or more rooms or a total concept (everything you see, feel, hear, smell and taste).

I will add my endless creativity to your ideas. The final result will have your style and class.

I AM:
* a good networker * positive * enthusiastic * innovative * visionair * energetic * capable of getting things moving * inspiring * trustworthy * hard worker * I strongly believe that it's better to share 'the whole pie' rather than chasing the biggest part (see my recommendations)

INTERNATIONAL NETWORK THE D SIGNERS
I have an extensive network at my disposal for specialists, the latest design furniture, beautiful fabrics, the best antiques, old and new building materials and unique lighting, etc. I am always on the lookout for new products and applications.

MY ADVICE
Apart from designing, I also work in an advisory capacity for hotels, offices, restaurants, shops and homes. I can show you how you can use your budget to achieve optimal results in your interior and exterior.

After an introductory meeting, without any obligation, and examination of the room(s) concerned, I directly have a picture of how it can improve. After the first meeting you will receive detailed and honest advice.
