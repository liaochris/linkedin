Anna Young’s Summary

Born and raised in Southern California, I never thought that I would be living in Ohio and enjoying it - except for the snow in the winter.

Being an information junkie who loves being on the web, participating in Social Media has become an addictive habit and I have taken this enthusiasm for new media to make connections, and to pursue marketing efforts within those areas where I can get others to interact and communicate. I even got my father-in-law to blog on www.MyCelebrityTwin.com/blog.
