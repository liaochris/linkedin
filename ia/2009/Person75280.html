Erik Sand’s Summary

Background
Until 1996 I was working as a lithographer and we had a huge revolution in the early 90´s when the Mac and Photoshop turned a hole industry upside-down. I loved these new tools and all the things we could do with them, it was indeed a new world.

Moving over to the designbusiness in 1996 I started working in Digital-Hverdag, one of the first «hard core» internet agencies, founded and driven by Bente Sollid, a visionary young woman who saw large business opportunities in the new media.

In 2003 I was one of the co-founders of Fete typer, a design & communication company in Oslo, owning 25% I was also a member of the board. We had a lot of nice customers and the work was interresting enough but after 3.5 years I felt for a change again, I do admit I like changes. I started working in a company called Creuna, one of scandinavias best internet consultant companies.

Two years ago I quit my work as a seniordesigner in Creuna Oslo to start a new life as independent designer two days a week, and working three days a week as a media-teacher in college. I had a very interesting year teaching subjects like web-design, graphic design, photo, and the Adobe collection of tools of course. After a year I had do choose between teaching and my company, and here I am.

Although I am experienced, the best part of the web-media is the development that seems to be a never ending story, you can never say «I know everything there is to know about the web». I love learning, I love challenges, working with the web-media gives me an ongoing reason to expand my knowledge, and it challenges me to try to look into the future. What´s the next big thing to be hot? What comes after Twitter? How can my customers meet the challenges ahead and gain on their investments in smart net solutions?

The future is now, -and I am ready!
