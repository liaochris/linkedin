Joshua Hale’s Summary

Experienced lawyer and technologist with a high-technology telecommunications background, having worked with Fortune 500 companies, including AT&T, SBC, Pacific Century CyberWorks, Cable & Wireless, and Hong Kong Telecom. Now serving as outside general counsel to large construction firm in San Diego.

My office also houses one of the few cross-border practices which includes all law areas in Tijuana Mexico, with over ten south of the border legal professionals under his purview.

I am OPEN to all new connections, and am willing to learn more about your business and how my office might be able to help your business grow safely and naturally, please feel free to add me to your network. Please use jhale@hesocal.com to send an add request.

Employment, Criminal, DUI, Immigration, Worker's Compensation, Personal Injury, and Construction Law are areas my office handles on a regular basis. Many of these cases are international in nature, involving parties in the United States, Mexico, and El Salvador.

As a telecommunications expert, I am regularly recruited to handle large legal and technical issues such as web presence analysis, infrastructure analysis, and their legal ramifications.
