Cara Heath’s Summary

A passion for music and respect for artists is the driving force behind everything Cara Heath does.

In the beginning of February 2008, she launched With A Bullet, a brand new independent radio promotion company. Her goal was to provide a strong foundation in traditional radio promotion and add to it a modern approach that capitalizes on all the avenues of radio promotion now available in the digital landscape. With A Bullet’s focus is to solidify the relationship between radio and artists and also look for new and unique opportunities to further this rapport. Ultimately, it’s all about getting the music out to the listeners. Since launching With A Bullet, Cara has worked with the Juno nominated Ivana Santilli, In-Flight Safety, and Saint Alvia, critically acclaimed US band The Gaslight Anthem, as well as Canadian indie artists including Spiral Beach, The Priddle Concern, Library Voices, and Bad Flirt among others.

Prior to stepping out to form her own company, Cara spent three years helping build another radio promotion company, where she worked with artists including Feist, DJ Champion, and Paul Brandt. She also set up on-air promotional campaigns for clients including Eagle Rock Entertainment, Anchor Bay/Starz, and Morningstar Entertainment. Before that, Cara served for two years in BMG Canada’s radio promotion department.
