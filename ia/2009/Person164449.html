Mike Kemp, CSP’s Summary

Mike Kemp is a Senior Safety Engineer and Industrial Hygienist at Intel Corporation. From 1994 to present, he has been responsible for a broad range of EHS related initiatives from basic program & training development to complex changes such as modifying the safety cultures of entire manufacturing facilities, both at sites in the US as well as international assembly operations.

In addition, he has also been active in driving Intel to the next level of safety excellence by influencing changes to what and how indicators are used in performance tracking.

He currently serves as a delegate to the New Mexico Chapter of American Society of Safety Engineers (past president) and holds a Certified Safety Professional (CSP) title.

Mike Kemp, CSP’s Specialties:

Stakeholder engagement, fatality prevention program compliance, behavioral safety initiatives, design reviews (blueprint knowledge), coaching/mentoring ehs staff (including international teams), construction safety (industry), OSHA VPP implementation and sustainability, accident/injury investigations, and applying critical thinking to complex problem solving that provides appropriate protection for employees while not driving the company into bankruptcy!
