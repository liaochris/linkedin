  • IT Director

    Geoapikonisis S.A.

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    July 2007 — September 2008 (1 year 3 months)

    I had worked with Geoapikonisis since 2002 for various projects but July 2007 was considered as a good opportunity to tighten the cooperation and really test the waters for doing more than projects together. The work I did for Geoapikonisis mainly boils down to two very important projects and the laying of the foundation for the company to start undertaking projects that require IT skills and development of custom software solutions. The two most important projects were the work I did involving ECDIS technologies funded by two EU projects and resulting in a custom ECDIS software solution for Geoapikonisis, and the Greek Cadastre Project which involved less coding but required a complex and demanding IT infrastructure solution.

  • Senior Software Architect

    DAeM

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    October 2007 — December 2007 (3 months)

    Worked on a consulting contract with DAeM to design the new CRM of the Municipality of Athens which is to be used by 195 (the Municipal Call Center) and OAKP (a Municipal Organization for Handling Citizens' Everyday Problems). The system will be developed on Oracle's technology platform and its users will span form every municipal organization and department to every citizen and visitor of Athens.

  • Senior Consultant

    ECDC

    (Government Agency; 11-50 employees; Government Administration industry)

    May 2007 — June 2007 (2 months)

    This short term project in ECDC involved a functional and user requirement analysis for the new web portal of this important EU Agency. The project concluded in a complete UML model describing the proposed web portal and the draft terms of reference for the call for tenders that would lead to the implementation of the plan.

  • Senior Software Engineer

    DAeM

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    April 2006 — June 2007 (1 year 3 months)

    My work with DAeM revolved around Thesseus, the web based system I had developed for use by the Citizen Service Centers of the Municipality of Athens. I worked with DAeM to support and evolve the project and to transfer know how to the company for taking it over and pushing it further to its next versions incorporating it to the Municipality's CRM.

  • IT Director

    Kapa Research Group

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    March 2002 — December 2006 (4 years 10 months)

    I was drawn to Kapa Research Group after moving away from the Internet Services sector mainly because of the interest I had in exploiting the large amounts of data the company was collecting.
    Although I did quite a few things which I still consider important for Kapa Research and Emos Interactive, I think that both companies and their customers, will remember me for the creation of Thesseus, (a web based system used in the network of Citizen Service Centers of the Municipality of Athens), and the creation of the Open Online Election System (which has been used many times in internal political party election processes in Greece). Both projects were challenging, critical to both the customer and the company, successful and left me with important engineering and PM experience.

  • Managing Director

    Bee Communication S.A.

    (Internet industry)

    May 1995 — January 2002 (6 years 9 months)

    Bee Communication S.A. was one of the first Internet start-ups in Greece, even before the web really went mainstream. It started as an ISP PoP and evolved into a regional ISP and ISV providing custom solutions for e-commerce, e-learning and other web based applications. I very much value the experience I gained on the ISP market and business model while starting and running Bee Com and it’s subsides HUBnet & SatLine. I left the group in 2001 before the dot-com bubble burst, having managed to drive the company and its staff through the first web era under a strong business and technology vision but having failed to share the same vision for the future with the rest of shareholders and co-founders.
