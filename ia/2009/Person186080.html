Pete Scanlon’s Summary

For over 12 years I have worked with businesses and advertising agencies to lead cross-functional teams that develop digital branding, advertising, ecommerce, and content strategies in new and emerging environments. My focus is to build and maintain meaningful relationships with audiences by facilitating interactions across multiple channels.

My experience with brands includes work for T-Mobile, Hewlett-Packard, Saturn, and California Pizza Kitchen while at advertising agencies such as Goodby, Siverstein & Partners, Publicis West, and Hal Riney & Partners.

My experience with businesses includes Gap, Old Navy and 24 Hour Fitness while working directly for those companies. Additionally, I have served as a digital adviser to non-profit organizations that include KEXP Radio, and the National Cowboy Poetry Gathering to help increase both their online listening audience and financial support from members.

My work with creative, media, and technology teams has been awarded by shows that include: Future Marketing Awards, One Show, London International Advertising Awards, Communication Arts Interactive Annual, and International Association of Business Communicators (IABC).

I graduated with a B.A. from the University of Minnesota in Minneapolis, and currently enjoy living in the warmer weather of San Francisco.

supporter of great creative ideas; client consigliere; liaison to technology, and media teams; digital branding; online advertising; ecommerce; content strategies; web site analytics and optimization.
