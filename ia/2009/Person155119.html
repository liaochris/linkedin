  • Electroacoustic Composer

    Self-employed

    (Self-Employed; Myself Only; Music industry)

    1993 — June 2002 (9 years)

    Michael is an electroacoustic composer, software developer, and network admin. As the Systems Engineer for the New Media and Emerging Technology center at BGSU, his current research projects include audio plugins, Ambisonics, and Virtualization. His music has been performed at International Computer Music Conferences (Miami 2004, Berlin 2000, Hong Kong 1996), International Computer Music Festival (Japan 1998), KEAMS 2000 (Korea, 2000), Rein à voir (Canada, 2002, 2001) and also at concerts in Taiwan, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, United Kingdom, Northern Ireland and the United States. His music has also been presented at the Society for ElectroAcoustic Music in the United States Conferences (2003, 2002, 2000, 1998, 1996) and the Florida Electroacoustic Music Festival (2006, 2004, 2000, 1999). Michael’s awards include a 1999 Residence and 1997 Mention from the International Electracoustic Music Competition (France). His music is available on Centaur Records, CemiSonics CDCM Volume 27.
