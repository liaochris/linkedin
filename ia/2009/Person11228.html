Amelio Vázquez-Reina’s Summary

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Laboratory for Imaging Science Research (LaISR) at Tufts University. My Ph.D. advisor is Professor Eric Miller.

I am researching variational segmentation and tracking methods for biomedical imaging applications. Some of the projects I am currently involved in include the study of shape-driven deformable models for the extraction of biological neural networks

I'm also currently a researcher at the Initiative in Innovative Computing at Harvard University where I am working on The Connectome Project.

Amelio Vázquez-Reina’s Specialties:

Image segmentation, variational image processing, active contours, level set methods.
