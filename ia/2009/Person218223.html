Simon Spencer’s Summary

Simon Spencer has nearly 20 years experience in leadership roles across the Financial Services, Digital Media, Software Engineering and Telecom industries within Australia, United States and United Kingdom.

His successful experience across multiple industries has enabled Simon to blend the best of the agile, innovative and dynamic cultures of start-up environments with the attributes of complexity, maturity and scale demanded by larger enterprises.

With a broad network, Simon creates highly effective teams able to deliver cutting edge techology projects ranging from patented media services fabrics, through to high volume transaction data warehouses and new Software as a Service solutions.

As a technology executive with CIO and CTO experience in mid sized enterprises (~700 employees), Simon has consistently been able to communicate a compelling vision for his organizations and to take this all the way through to execution, moving across the job space from Investors, Board and the Senior management team, through to Product Management, Infrastructure Operations, Engineering, Creative and Support teams.

Simon has worked closely with leaders in the Angel, Venture Capital and Investment Banking sectors in Australia and the United States, including directly participating as either a founder or senior executive in the floats of two successful start-ups (NASDAQ and ASX) and in sucessfully integrating new businesses through over 30+ M & A transactions in Australia, France, South America, Asia and the United States.

Simon Spencer’s Specialties:

Financial Services, Startups, Raising Capital, M&A, Sales, Marketing, Software Engineering, Innovation, Transformation, Leadership, Thinking out of the box.
