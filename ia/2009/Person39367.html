Cathy Henesey, SPHR’s Summary

Over 20 years in recruiting and staffing industry. A graduate of Texas Tech University with a MBA in Personnel/Human Resource Management, and an undergraduate in Business Management. Expertise lies in training recruiters, developing processes, and researching and developing new recruiting strategies. Cathy has spoke at many conferences including the ITAA CEO Summit, ERE Expo conference, SHRM Regional meetings, NACCB regional and national conferences, and many local organization meetings. Represented the staffing industry in Washington, DC as a lobbyist for the Immigration Visa Quota bill. Current President of the Dallas/Ft. Worth Texas Recruiters Network. Reached the highest level with Toastmasters International titled Distinguished Toastmaster. Cathy is a Certified Internet Recruiter from AIRS and certified SPHR from HRCI/SHRM.

Products/Services: Recruiting Department Assessment, Compensation Assessment, Permanent Search, Training, Persomal Coach for Recruiters and Management
