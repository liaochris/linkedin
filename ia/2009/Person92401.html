Hari Sharma’s Summary

Hari is Technology Consultant with NIIT Technologies, Global IT Solutions Company, focuses in verticals of Transportation, Banking, Finance and Insurance and providing services in LMM, CSDM and Data warehousing.

Hari has over 8 years of experience across various business domains such as Finance, B2B, ECCM and Manufacturing (Distribution, Sales, Accounting, and Reporting).

In his current role, Hari is responsible for team management, effort estimation, and work allocation and application delivery management for current project. He is also responsible for designing and development of applications for leading Investment Management client using .NET platform. He is also contributing in the areas of Architecting and Designing Applications, Database Administration, EAI and Business Intelligence.

In his prior experience, he worked at Sr. Consultant-Technical for ECCM Practice (Zensar Technologies), was responsible for architecting, designing and developing application using ECM products such OpenText Livelink and Microsoft SharePoint Portal Server and various other MS Technologies (.NET Products and SQL Server). He was also part of pre-sales team for ECCM Practice.

Hari completed his Bachelors of Commerce Degree from University of Delhi and hold Diploma in Information System and Management from Aptech.

He has expertise on wide range of technologies such as .NET Framework 2.0(VB.NET, C#.NET, ASP.NET), SQL Server 2K/2K5, Visual Basic 6.0 with ActiveX Components, COM/DCOM, MSMQ, MTS, OpenText Livelink, Sharepoint Portal Server, BizTalk Server and data warehousing Tools such as Cognos, MS Analysis Services and SQL Server Reporting Services.
