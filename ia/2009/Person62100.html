Derek Ramos’s Summary

Derek Ramos is a consulting executive with an extensive background in the field of medical services. In the recent past he was a Long Term Care specialist, agent trainer and onsite product expert for National Health Administrators.

Prior to his position with National Health Administrators Mr. Ramos accumulated 10 years experience in client service relations as a Director of Operations at Lifespan medicine, a company with offices in Dallas, Texas and Los Angeles, California. He was responsible for both vendor and client relations, product development and quality control for individualized custom formularies geared to a roster of over 400 patients. His management contributed to the growth of Lifespan medicines in-house pharmacy and expansion to accommodate oversees clients.

As Executive Director of IBGS, Mr. Ramos was responsible for business development, initial client contact and continuous management for all services IBGS provides in the areas of employee benefits, human resources and risk management as the Small Disadvantage Business (SDB) leader in the government services industry.

Presently, as a new member to the Arcturus Team, he will utilize his business development skills to further expand Arcturus Partners presence in the Texas energy arena.
