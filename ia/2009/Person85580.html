Gerald Friedland’s Summary

My research interests focus on intelligent multimedia technology, especially to extract meaning out of data created for human sensory perception. Currently I am leading the speaker diarization research at ICSI,a non-profit lab affiliated with the University of California Berkeley. Before this I worked on interactive image segmentation. Maybe the most popular example of my work is: http://www.siox.org

Gerald Friedland’s Specialties:

Developing machine learning applications that work.
