Michelle Conceison’s Summary

BACKGROUND:
I started in brand advertising on the agency side, and since have worked in virtually all major forms of marketing - television, print, online, direct, shareholder, events, guerilla, PR, search and affiliate. I currently lead the Paid Search Marketing team in Digitas' Boston office. I have also managed and promoted internationally touring acoustic/folk musicians in my free time for 9 years.

WHAT'S THE DEAL WITH MARKET MONKEYS?
I founded Market Monkeys in 2004 to respond to marketing challenges all independent musicians face. Marketing, Promotion, and Distribution through Market Monkeys is delivered for artists on my client roster sometime on an ongoing basis, and sometimes on a project by project basis.
