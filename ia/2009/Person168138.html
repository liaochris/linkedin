Cindy Morgan-Jaffe’s Summary

As a communications, education and counseling professional for over 30 years, my focus has been on how to effectively achieve results through effective leadership and management, communication, listening, and creativity. I bring to my work a broad reach of skills and experiences – both personal and professional—that give me the confidence and ability to pursue my passion for youth, education, entreprenuership and the factors that lead us to success for ourselves and in the world around us.

I am an entrepreneur in spirit and action. I thrive on new ideas, innovative thinking and creativity. I am also a stickler for good writing, clear message and effective design. My goals are to work with a strong team, contributing what I have learned as an art director, manager, small business owner to reach goals.

Cindy Morgan-Jaffe’s Specialties:

Communications including Writing, Speaking, Website Design and Implementation, Project Management, Training. Also, Nationally Certified Counselor with Masters from George Washington University in Career Counseling for youth.
