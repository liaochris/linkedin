Tom Ball’s Summary

Quick introduction, I am a passionate innovator - I live for new ideas. I love to build companies. To change industries (aim high!). To achieve "the impossible".

I was once told that I am "helplessly in love with people". Not sure if it's a good thing - but I rather liked it, and hope it's at least partly true! I LOVE spending time with good people. To me "good" means people that are passionate about life and something that they do - you get "good" people in every walk of life - and often where you least expect it.

Cognac: the big picture company

"85% of the information in the brain is stored visually. Studies show that visuals nearly triple the communication experience. So don’t even think about presenting anything to anyone, if you’re not going to use visual aids..."

Cognac creates "big pictures", large diagrams which visually communicate your message in ten minutes (guaranteed).
