vibha verma’s Summary

Briefly Stating, I am a Sr.Graphic Designer with an 8.5 years of experience into Designing & Visualization for Print Media. Currently am enhancing as a Freelance Graphic Designer in Charleston, South Carolina and have achieved a wide level of experience in designing various forms of print jobs.

My work expertise includes Designing and Visualization of various Marketing Materials, that help to promote a Brand/Business in the Market. These Materials include-

* Corporate "Brand Identity" Designing- Logo, Business Card, Letterhead, Envelope Designing
* Corporate Brochure Designing,- Tri-fold, Bi-fold, Multiple pages
* Magazine / Newspaper Print Ads'
* Fliers / Direct Mailers Designing- like Rack Cards, Door Hangers, Hang Tags, Postcards, Self-Mailer Designing, etc
* Magazine / Newsletter Designing,
* Events / Tradeshow Designing- Vinyl Banner Artworks, Mural or Large Display Backdrops, Rollups, or Tradeshow Booth Backdrop Designing
* or any other form of Print Media designing,

All these Designs are conceptualized and designed for the Print Media Advertising and are finally formatted Ready to Print using the following graphic application software-

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Corel Draw, and Quark XPress.

My goal, throughout my Work Process, exists, to achieve the requirements of an organization by successfully meeting their deadlines and hence creating a powerful Brand Identity for them, in the Market!
