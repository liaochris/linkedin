Jim Marsh’s Summary

We live in a time in which the traditional rules of media and entertainment must be reworked in order to remain relevant and adaptable to rapidly advancing technologies and shifting consumer behavior. I have chosen to position myself at the forefront of the changing landscape to ensure that the digital space is utilized to its fullest potential.

I feel at home working for companies that share values similar to my own including innovation, hard work, strong relationships, open-mindedness, financial accountability, and respect for others. I offer a unique perspective, a strong work ethic, interpersonal abilities, a sense of humor, and most importantly, solid experience.

I have diverse experience in media, beginning with my work in ad sales for CNN International and most recently as an Account Executive at Deep Focus. While working with Deep Focus, I helped develop and implement digital strategies for brands including Comedy Central, Court TV, and Picturehouse. This included overseeing the production of cutting-edge websites, innovative banner ads, media planning, PR, viral campaigns and assisting with the development of long-term digital strategies. I also worked as an individual to promote the rock band Guyz Nite, whose song and accompanying music video "Die Hard" proved to be an integral part of 20th Century Fox's campaign for "Live Free or Die Hard".

Jim Marsh’s Specialties:

I have had the opportunity to get my hands dirty in many different areas including TV and web ad sales, account management, preparing marketing materials, interactive project management and creative direction. I have a solid understanding of new media and it's role in supporting traditional media.
