David Kerr’s Summary

A dynamic, technically and commercially aware Programme Manager, with 17+ years IT industry experience, adept at delivering large-scale business and IT change. David is both pragmatic and driven, he is a proven strategic thinker able to successfully navigate and manage high levels of complexity. Over 4 years experience in Identity Management (particularly using Sun’s Identity Suite).

He has an AMBA accredited MBA (2003), is a registered Managing Successful Programmes (MSP) Advanced Practitioner, registered Prince 2 Practitioner and registered Management of Risk (MoR) Practitioner with the APM Group. David is also an accredited NLP Master Practitioner and independent facilitator.

As well as being responsible for the successful delivery of University of Salford’s IdM Programme (a Sun Centre of Excellence for Identity Management), he has advised a number of institutions and organisations in both the public and private sector considering embarking on IdM projects and programmes. He has also presented at a number of Identity Management seminars and conferences, and was recipient of the Best Paper award at EUNIS 2007.

Industry and vertical market expertise includes higher education, banking, mobile telecoms, manufacturing, retail, property, asset appraisal, channel commissions, pharmaceuticals, training, pricing optimisation, decision support, EFT, ERP, EDI, supply chain and CRM.

Client exposure includes: University of Salford (UoS), Deutsche Bank, Vodafone, T-Mobile, Alcatel, Sainsbury’s, Unichem, Ciba Geigy, YMCA, Boosey & Hawkes, Scottish Power, Caterpillar, Puma, Specialty Minerals, Minerals Technologies, Salvation Army.
