Carle Aldrete’s Summary

I live to build relationships. Never have I been an order taker except maybe when waiting tables in college. Some of my clients have been with me for ten years or more because we work together to grow their businesses. I am honest when I think my customers are making poor choices. I don't claim to have all the answers but I'll come by them one way or another and if I think that you're spending more money than you should be, I'll tell you. Some of my best "relationships" will tell me when to shut up and that's great. I am a consultant and I want what is best for you. If you want the best, I'll get it for you.

One of our company values is to "Always Be Learning". I am reading everything my boss gives me plus a bunch that I pick up on my own. I attend corporate training as well as third party webinars but I am most excited about my current training. I just completed the Dale Carnegie Course led by Dave Wright. I was elected by my class to receive the "Highest Award For Achievement" and that has been my proudest accomplishment for a VERY long time.

The plan for me is to move into a position of sales management and business development so I went to Dallas to learn the Rockefeller Habits where the other team leaders and I prepared our organization's strategic plan. I am very excited about the direction of our company and I look forward to sharing these experiences with new customers who will evolve into part of our family.

Carle Aldrete’s Specialties:

Originally a photographer, I also dabbled in graphic design. Having worked with a variety of marketing directors, ad agencies and design firms, I have learned much about details, expectations and how to better communicate.

I know direct mail and am getting involved with variable data, one to one marketing and more importantly, learning what what works best for each individual organization.
