John Arnold’s Summary

John Arnold is a leading marketing speaker, author, and web marketing expert specializing in email marketing, web marketing, and mobile marketing strategies. Here are some facts about John's marketing expertise:

John is the author of E-Mail Marketing for Dummies and co-author of the comprehensive desk reference Web Marketing All-In-One for Dummies.

John is also a contributing author and ghost-writer who's credits include The Social Media Bible and The Constant Contact Guide to Email Marketing.

John is an engaging marketing speaker, media contributor, and small business marketing consultant. John's marketing articles and blogs are featured in many well-known publications, and John is a marketing speaker for many well-known organizations and associations.

John's marketing expertise first gained national attention when he helped to pioneer a marketing speaker and seminar series on behalf of the permission-based email marketing company Constant Contact®. Through the program, John personally shared his marketing advice with thousands of small business owners through marketing speaking engagements and training events. During John's tenure, Constant Contact® was named #166 on Inc. 500's list of the fastest growing private companies and later completed a successful Initial Public Offering.

John presently speaks, trains, and advises small business owners through Constant Contact® as the Director of Constant Contact University. He is a highly sought-after marketing keynote speaker and small business marketing trainer.

Please contact John to inquire about being a marketing speaker or writer for your organization, association, or business.
