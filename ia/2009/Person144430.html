Luca Figini’s Summary

• I’m still working in Qimonda Italy S.r.l. (Infineon Technology FLASH MEMORY GROUP) in MILAN, I moved here from Atmel on last July 2005.
• Since July, 19th till December, 17th I worked in Munich as Layout Leader on a project on 2GB Flash Memory on 70nm CMOS technology.
• Now I’m Layout Leader of a group of 4 people and I refer directly to the Layout Manager.

• Good experience with custom layout and also analog blocks of the device.
• Good experience with FLOORPLAN and with every issues.
• I’m always in touch with the technology in Dresden to solve any kind of issues concerning the critical structures.
• Good experience with all the main layout and verification tools (Virtuoso, Vituoso XL, Calibre/Assura DRC/LVS).

• I have been working in Atmel (Flash Memory Group) since April 2001 till July 2005.
• I have been working as Layout Designer on 0.18u technology (CMOS triple Metal process) and I have designed 32MB, 16MB, 8MB standard products and 64MB Burst Mode product.
• I was leader of a small group of 4 young layouters and I had the contacts with the technology in USA and with the layouters there for little parts of layout of the whole chip.


• I have been working in STMicroelectronics since February 1994.
• Initially, I was a production operator in manufacturing Fab in Agrate B.za (MI).
• In April 1996 I moved into EPROM DESIGN GROUP like Layout Engineer of Non-Volatile memory. Skilled in CMOS technology (single and double Metal processes).
• In 1998/99 I collaborated in 64MB multilevel Flash memory project with Mitsubishi Electric Co.
