Dale Pullin’s Summary

Working as a drafter for most of my career I have extensive experience using 2D/3D CAD for engineering, architectural and simulation graphics purposes.

Specialising in CADWorx for process and power plant design. Set up and customisation of CADWorx Plant Professional design suite using live linked databases and custom specifications from P&ID, 3D, Isometrics and fabrication drawings (including steel and equipment).

Current goal is to become a Senior Drafter / CAD Manager / Consultant.

Dale Pullin’s Specialties:

Process, Power, Pipework, Steel fabrication, AutoCAD, CADWorx, 3D Modeling, Graphic Design, Database.
