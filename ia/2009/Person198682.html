Rick Rockwell’s Summary

Currently, I teach multimedia skills and theoretical lecture/seminar-style courses on international communication. As part of these classes I teach traditional radio and television skills and how to apply them in traditional and emerging web environments.

I am a broadcaster with more than 25 years of experience in the field, but I also have experience across platforms: in internet and newspaper journalism too. I have experience as a writer, reporter, producer, field producer and newsroom manager.

Current projects include informally advising the Argentine government on its proposed telecommunication reform project (this work grew out of my time on Capitol Hill as a telecommunication policy advisor) and developing my next book project on politics and media in Venezuela.

I also edit the blog iVoryTowerz, which began as a blog for honors students at American University, and now includes writers who are students, alumni, and professionals. And I program the blog's ancillary podcast.

Rick Rockwell’s Specialties:

Freelancing as a broadcast, print, and internet journalist. Key areas of expertise in producing, writing, and editing on a variety of platforms and in various styles. My primary mission is teaching these skills to others.
