Jimmy Bosmans’s Summary

Strand, as your recruitment partner, offers its experience and competence to both candidates in search of an interesting, challenging new position, and clients in search of competent staff with skills optimally matching their requirements and company culture.



Towards Clients:
We commit ourselves to provide you a transparent, honest and results-oriented service. Not only a candidate's technical skill will qualify him, but it is their personality, attitude and values that will ensure their success.

Your company culture will be observed together with your specific requirements. All our candidates are interviewed face-to-face and only presented when we are sure to have a optimal fit.

Due to our dynamism and low-overhead structure we guarantee a customer-focused approach, as well as competitive prices and great flexibility.



Towards Candidates:
We are of service to ICT specialists in search of a challenging job, either permanent or on a contractor/freelance basis. Using a constantly updated professional database, we ensure we are constantly aware of the most competitive and interesting vacant positions. Based on the candidate's information, we ensure an optimal match of skills and experience with the vacancies.

We help you to not only just your next job, but a strategic lasting career move. Values like honesty, transparency and trust is what we build our reputation on and is the way we work with you to match your personality and skills with the right company, with the right job.
