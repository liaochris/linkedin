Mark W. Hudson’s Summary

Louisville CPA with over 30 years of experience as a business owner, local company CFO and financial manager for a large multinational fills the role of CFO or Controller for companies and not-for-profits who have a need for a senior level financial manager on a regular but less than full-time basis.

This service is an independent resource for CPA firms to improve the financial management of their clients and add efficiency in the tax and audit processes.

The return to the business owner is enhanced profitably, through professional financial management.

Mark W. Hudson’s Specialties:

Cash flow/profit enhancement and maximization through strategic management of revenues, expenses, accounts receivable, inventories (if applicable) and supplier relationships.

Systems implementation, internal control evaluation and risk management assessment.

Capital acquisition (equity and debt financing); cost and investment analysis, budgeting, projections, financial modeling, financial statement preparation and analysis
