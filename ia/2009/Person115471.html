    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    September 2009 — Present (2 months)

    Satguru Marketing is a single member LLC company founded in September of 2009 in Grand Forks, ND. The company was formed when a need for social marketing was identified among local businesses in the Red River Valley. I took this opportunity, combined with my expertise in this area, to form what is now called Satguru Marketing. As the sole employee, I am responsible with overseeing all aspects of the company including: Managing, marketing, designing and implementing all client work. I manage a list of clients and provide them with social marketing strategies that best fit their company’s needs. I specialize in designing websites, blogs, facebook and twitter pages as well as search engine optimization and online advertising placement. My company’s approach is to target small business owners who do not have the resources to do marketing in-house or pay high agency fees.
