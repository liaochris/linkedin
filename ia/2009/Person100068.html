Kevin Gajjar’s Summary

I borned in India but i feel myself 100% Italian due to the fact that i was adopted in Italy in 1979 (Milan) and i have done all my studies here. A wonderful place to live!

Due to my personal experiences my dream was creating a bridge between my birth country(India) and my host country (Italy).

This is why after university i open probably one of the first outsourcing consulting companies in Italy, Indianbridge, with the goal to provide affordable high skilled and talented indian human resources to italian companies whom needing high skilled IT professionals just like graphics, coders, programmers, project managers, engineers...
We was able to create team-works of indian IT high skilled resources to more then 100 companies in Italy avalaible 24h a day.

After this experience i decided to fund in Milan Startupsfactory.com with the goal to help italian startups in pursuing their plans for becoming successful startups trasforming their dream in a real internet successfull service in the new Internet 2.0 age.

With a team of more 1000 developers avalaible 24 a day in India my company helps italian companies to develop their internet or mobile sites/services/applications for affordable prices.

We have just supported more then 50 startups at the moment in launching their services at the moment making them being in touch also with investors and ventures.
