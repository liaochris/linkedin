Erwin Van Lun MBM MSc.’s Summary

August 2005 - current: Speaker, futurist, trend watcher (trend analyst), facilitator and author in Media, Marketing & Brands. Speaker on congresses, seminars and customer days; facilitator during strategy session on brand strategy; Trend watcher on www.erwinvanlun.com/www.mensmerk.nl. Creative partner by generating ideas in individual sessions.

2004 - August 2005: Wrote a visionary book on media and brands in 2015 titled 'Van Massamerk naar Mensmerk' (in Dutch). A weblog tracks the day-to-day changes of media & brands: http://www.mensmerk.nl. The book got a top-3 nomination for the marketing literature award.

April 2005 - August 2005: Strategic Planner at Ogilvy

2003-2004: Master of Brand Management, Erasmus University.

1994-2003: 10 ten years CMG (now LogicaCMG)
4 years (1999-2003): corporate communications @ international head office. Involved in all processes and primary responsible for Internet. Strongly involved in global branding project.

6 years (1994-1999): Marketing & IT Consultant. Worked for customers like Ahold, KLM, Schiphol, General Electric and Ascom.

1987-1993: Electrical Engineering at Technical University Delft. Thesis on Artificial Intelligence.
