Francisco Okecki’s Summary

Francisco was born in Buenos Aires on July 16th, 1973. He is married to Pía Segura, and has four sons. He was educated in St. Brendan´s College (Buenos Aires - Argentina) and he is a lawyer graduated from the Argentina Catholic University (1997).

Although Francisco is a practicing lawyer, he has developed an entrepreneurial profile specialized in technology businesses. At the early age of 23 years old he founded Amarata.com (sold to PSINet a couple of years later). He then helped to start the Technology Law practice in M. & M. Bomchil Law Firm.

He then becomes part of Itegra where he developed several businesses related to the Mobile Industry (Topkash, M4biz, Mundo Movil, Mobile2biz).

Lately, he left his regional position at Net People International, the top mobile integrator that merged last year with Mobile2biz, in order to take care of his duties as member of the board of several companies.

Francisco Okecki’s Specialties:

As Legal Director: General Practice, Corporate, Contractual, Deal Structuring, M & A, Intelectual Property Rights, Entertainment, Consumer´s acts, and the direction of outsourced specialized legal advice.

As entrepreneur: Find new trends and technologies. Make businesses convenience and opportunity analysis. Plan and execute strategic alliances. Set up management teams. Develop operative procedures and policies.
