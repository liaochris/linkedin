Patrick O'Loughlin’s Summary

I established, in 1990, the O’Loughlin Partnership Executive Search & Selection which has been operating for over 15 years supplying recruitment expertise to the Irish Industrial , Commercial and Public Sectors.

In 2004 established PharmaCareers Career AdvisoryTraining company focused on the Pharmaceutical, Medical Device , Healthcare and associated Service Providers. Leaders in training Good Distribution Practice, GDP, Cold Chain, Temperature Logistics. Also provide training in collobaration with major Irish Educational Institutes in assisting individuals with re-skilling to move from declining industries into Pharmaceutical sector.

PharmaExecutives Interim Technical & Management Staffing Services. This is a way of resourcing senior technical & management staff on a temporary basis.

Patrick O'Loughlin’s Specialties:

Managing partner of O'Loughlin partnership Executive Search & Selection specilising in Executive search & Selection

Director of PharmaCareers aspecialist Career Advisory and re-skilling & Training Consultancy. Also specilised in legaslative based training related to Good Distribution Practice for Wholesalers & Manufacturers and Logistics companies in Pharmaceutical Sector

    (International Trade and Development industry)

    Logistical Efficiency - promoting and providing access to the latest cost reduction and supply chain efficiency improvement techniques and opportunities.
    Material Quality Control – assisting life sciences companies and those serving the industry to keep up to speed in the latest material handling and environmental control methods and standards.
    Good Distribution Practices – Benchmarking the standard for the industry and promoting its use by all service providers regionally and internationally.
