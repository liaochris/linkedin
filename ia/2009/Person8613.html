Alexander Shumilin’s Summary

Our company offers development of software products, complex solutions, and modules for integration with other products. Our specialists are highly skilled, with wide experience in the field of development of software solutions, both at the level of the system kernel and application. Our in-house software products work deeply at the level of Microsoft Windows kernel and are based on innovative ideas and technologies.
The software our team develops is intended for:
* monitoring employees' computer and Internet activity (web traffic, e-mail, IM, etc) in order to ensure compliance with corporate security policy and protection from internal threats,
* monitoring actions of users in small working groups – small companies and home offices (SOHO sector),
* paternal control – monitoring activity of a child on a home computer and in the Internet,
* control of Acceptable Use Policy in public places – schools, Internet-cafes, libraries,
* personal chronologic control of all PC activity and behavior in the Internet of persons suspected of unlawful actions (for law enforcement authorities and private investigators),
* prompt response (using mobile communication devices) to incidents of security policy violations,
* counteracting unfair competition using proactive approach to protection from unauthorized data interception by means of spy software,
* integration of modules for proactive protection against spy software into other software and hardware products (antiviruses, firewalls, USB-flash devices et al.),
* other.
All of our projects are fulfilled using the most modern products for collaboration development and version control in order to ensure maximal efficiency and safety of the whole cycle of development, testing and support of the software products. Basic programming languages are C/C++.
