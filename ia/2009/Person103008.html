James Silverman’s Summary

Im a British Photographer based in Goteborg/Sweden.For the last 9 years I have sponsored my own shoots-supporting budgets with assistants travelling across the globe communicating with some of the best designers and architects to photograph unique architectural homes . I have involved lighting designers to spruce up exterior shots just for photography, sponsorship to refit interiors with contemporary furnishings,hired sky lifts and built scaffolding towers for viewpoints-Having help with a digital assistant with the post production. My recent adventure was in Japan shooting 8 architectural homes in 23 days driving from Tokyo to Fukuoka - East to West. In the last year I have photographed in Japan,Chili and Brazil!
