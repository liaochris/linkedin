    What is it?
    Thailand Ring Pull project is a project run by Rotary Clubs to raise funds for the production of artificial limbs in Thailand. Ring pulls from soft drink and food cans are collected and sold. The funds raised are sent to Thailand.

    Who benefits and what's Rotaract Doing to help?
    This project benefits the people of Thailand who have had amputations due to different reasons such as stepping on land mines, accidents and birth defects
