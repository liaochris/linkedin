Patrick Stansbury’s Summary

Patrick Stansbury currently serves as the Executive Vice President of Harmonix Technologies. In addition, he is also a Partner in Bodhi Investments LLC.

Mr. Stansbury began his career in 1990 in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Since then, he has held a series of leadership positions throughout his career, with fortune 500 companies; Pentair Inc, Alco Standard and Lucent Technologies. In addition, Mr. Stansbury has worked with smaller regional companies such as Norstan Communications and Computer Assets. At Norstan Communications of Minnetonka MN he served as managing director of new business development and led a national K-12 National Practice driving over 30 Million in Revenues. At Computer Assets Mr. Stansbury served as senior vice president for the Enterprise Systems-North America.. Mr. Stansbury played a key role in creating and leading vertical market teams operating across North America.

Patrick Stansbury is a graduate of The University of Wisconsin at Madison. Mr. Stansbury is in the process of completing his MBA. In recognition of his leadership role Patrick Stansbury came to Harmonix Technologies after a from a 20 year track record of consulting on a national level to fortune 500 companies, analyzing and advising clients on building effective business communication networks.
