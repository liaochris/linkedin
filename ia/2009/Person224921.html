Steve Seeger’s Summary

I am a strategic and action-oriented executive with experience running large sales organizations in Fortune 250 firms.

While serving in such positions as Vice President of Sales and Marketing, General Manager and Area Sales Manager, I have demonstrated a passion for winning and a commitment to performance excellence.

I have held responsibility for managing a department of 65, delivering $550 million in revenue, administering $80 million in inventory, opening more than 60 stores (26 in one year), increasing market share during a declining market, and identifying and training future leaders for the benefit of my employers.

I hold an MBA from the University of Phoenix, a BA in Communications from The University of West Florida in Pensacola and attended Mount Union College in Alliance, OH for two years where I earned varsity letters as a proud member of the Purple Raider Football dynasty.

As an adaptable team player, I quickly acclimate to and utilize new concepts and understand existing practices and standards. I am actively seeking an opportunity to apply my education, skills and experience on behalf of an industry leader.
