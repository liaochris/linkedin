Stephanie LH Calahan - Calahan Solutions, Inc.’s Summary

Stephanie LH Calahan is a dynamic entrepreneur, nationally known speaker, media personality, author, publisher; productivity/organization consultant, and business start-up coach. As the creator of a number of programs, including www.FindInFiveSeconds.com and many others, she focuses on helping you identify solutions for the way you think and work.

As the founder and CEO of Calahan Solutions, Inc., she and her consultants focus on helping you make the most of your time, space and information. She and her team of experts work to help you maximize every hour and every dollar you invest in your business and life... and because they are small business specialists, they are especially attuned to the skills and tools required to make smaller businesses work.

As a natural connector, I believe in a pay it forward philosophy. Sometimes I can directly help those that I connect with and other times it is better for me to connect them to another professional to help them reach their goals.

Ideal clients for me are people who want to maximize their time/space and increase productivity. So if you hear a relative, friend, colleague, or client say that they can't find something, or that they are buried under clutter (or piles of paper), send them my way! I especially want to connect with entrepreneurs that have grown to a point where they are experiencing growing pains.

We are always looking for self-motivated individuals that have great organization skills.

Visit our Media Room at http://www.CalahanSolutions.net
