Aad Van Mourik’s Summary

International economist with specialisation in European integration issues. Twenty-eight years of teaching & training experience in academic, higher education and vocational training (economics) programs in all EU, Central- and Eastern European countries, as well as the USA. Fifteen years of experience in economic policy consulting and technical assistance projects for governments of EU member states and governments in Central and Eastern Europe (travelled extensively in these countries), consultancy & training experience at the highest levels in central banks of (prospective) EMU countries, as well as design, organisation, management, and teach¬ing of European integration seminars for the most senior level civil servants and top-executives of multinationals. Management and administrative skills developed in all career stages, until recently among others as (part-time) director of a successful economic policy consultancy, dean/professor of the Cologne Business School (D) and academic director of the Maastricht Hotel Managemente School. Excellently networked in various Euro¬pean administrations (EU and non-EU member countries), as well as in various international organisations. Finally, I hold visiting positions prestigious academic institutes (KU Leuven in Belgium and the Cologne Business School in Germany) and have trained hundreds of junior & senior diplomats in the Dutch Foreign Ministry, as well as civil servants in the Dutch economic and Social Affairs Ministries on a regular basis since 1994.

Aad Van Mourik’s Specialties:

Economics & economic policy trainings for (senior) civil servants.
