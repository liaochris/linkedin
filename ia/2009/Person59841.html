David Deasy’s Summary

Hello, my name is David Deasy. I'm a graphic designer based in Greensboro, N.C. My job is to excite and motivate people. Here's how I do it. First, I look and listen for a virtue that will resonate with your audience. Then, I write, sketch, walk, talk, sip coffee, etc. to build an idea and bring it to life. Next, perhaps we'll share a drink? This simple approach has earned me awards, new business for agencies, and accolades from my clients. I hope you can see that I love what I do. Now let's talk about what is possible for you.

David Deasy’s Specialties:

New business pitches. Developing and executing concepts across all media. Assigning and art directing photography and illustration. Working seamlessly with clients who are off-site. Being versatile and adaptable in a changing and competitive environment.
