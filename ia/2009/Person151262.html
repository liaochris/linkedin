Mark Parker’s Summary

Mark Parker is currently the Chairman and Co-founder of Talkbiznow Ltd.

As well as chairing the board, Mark also co-heads corporate strategy and business development, and oversees the teams responsible for managing its operations.

Mark has held several leadership roles, most recently at Halifax Bank of Scotland Plc (HBOS) - Intelligent Finance, where he was Managing Director responsible for one of the UK's leading internet and telebanking businesses.

Prior to this, Mark held COO and CIO roles at HBOS managing five thousand people and oversaw its business and technology infrastructure operations. Prior to this, Mark held technology leadership roles at PepsiCo Inc., British Sugar plc, The Boots Company plc and J Sainsbury plc.

Mark has achieved awards for business leadership, and was one of the most featured banking leaders in the UK press during his time at Intelligent Finance. He has appeared several times on the cover of leading management magazines, and speak at business conferences around the globe.
