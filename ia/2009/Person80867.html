Franck Rousseau’s Summary

I am associate professor at Grenoble INP-Ensimag (formerly INPG), and a member of the LIG lab, in the Drakkar group.
My teaching activity is mainly in networking and distributed systems -- computer networks, security, operating systems, Java, J2EE.
My research activity is mostly focused on wireless networking -- medium access control, x-layer, mesh, ad hoc, IP and transport layer, quality of service.
