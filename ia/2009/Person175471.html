Nikitas Koutoupes’s Summary

Partner at Insight Venture Partners, a $3 billion private equity and venture capital firm focused on software and Internet investments. Co-manage Insight On-site, the firm’s team of experts who provide operational assistance and strategic advice to Insight’s portfolio companies. Worked with over 40 portfolio companies including Greenfield Online (NASDAQ: SRVY), Capella University (NASDAQ: CPLA), DivX (NASDAQ: DIVX), and GetPaid Corporation (acquired by SunGard).

Prior to Insight, co-founded and was the Chief Financial Officer of Citadon (acquired by the Sword Group), an on-demand software company that became part of Insight’s portfolio. Previously worked as an Associate with McKinsey & Company, on strategy, turnaround, M&A and corporate finance engagements across a variety of industries.

BA from Princeton University (summa cum laude and Phi Beta Kappa), MBA with high distinction from Harvard Business School.

Nikitas Koutoupes’s Specialties:

Venture capital, private equity for software and internet companies; growth strategy, lead generation, online marketing, sales and telesales optimization, M&A target identification, due diligence and post-merger integration.
