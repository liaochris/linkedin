Joanna Broussard’s Summary

Marketing-oriented PR Strategist with strong business management skills, start-up and business development experience. Worked closely with top management of Fortune 500 and privately-held companies, governmental and non-profit organizations. Experience in multiple markets in North America and London, England.

-- Strategic thinker with demonstrated experience in maximizing the PR and integrated marketing communications functions throughout overall business operations.

-- Effective in identifying and creating innovative PR and mar/comm solutions to support companies in increasing sales and productivity, building/increasing brand awareness and in positioning organizations, products, services and top executives in market leadership roles.

-- Developed and managed multiple, complex PR and mar/comm programs, multi-functional teams and budgets for customers in a variety of industries.

-- Strong skills in staff development and training, customer relationship management and teambuilding.

-- Solid research, planning, writing and computer/new technology skills.

-- Served on non-profit boards and chaired key committees for civic and cultural organizations.

Key industry experience includes: consumer packaged goods, professional services, retail, financial services & insurance, hospitality & tourism, healthcare, cultural & performing arts, industrial manufacturing, food & beverage, real estate, environmental services, utilities, education, government, economic development, non-profit & associations, among others.

Key clients include: Coca-Cola Enterprises, Coca-Cola USA, Deloitte & Touche, Waste Management, Seven-Up, The Rouse Company, DeBartolo Investments, Hines Properties, Metropolitan Life Real Estate, Sheraton Hotels, Castano National Tobacco Litigation, The Nokia Sugar Bowl, Ramsay Healthcare, Ruth’s Chris Steakhouse, New Orleans Symphony, Entergy Thermal, City of New Orleans, MetroVision Economic Development Partnership.
