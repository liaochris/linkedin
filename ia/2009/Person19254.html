Ardy Heijnekamp’s Summary

I am a strong believer in networking and meeting new people. Sharing different ideas and ways of accomplishing the goals that are ahead of us is what develops us and makes us constantly more efficient.

My primary focus is on helping my clients expand and achieve their company’s vision for continuous growth and development by using social media.

I Advise companies on how to set up and run blogs, how to reach out to and relate to bloggers, how to use social media and how to make advanced use of RSS.

I help people use new media such as social networks to achieve their business goals (e.g. e-recruitment, shortening of sales cycles,..) Helping small, medium, and large sized businesses to positively influence their brands using social media online through the utilization of widgets, videos, podcasts, and RSS feed

My focus is on long lasting relationships with customers. Therefor I prefer to work with customers where Internet is a core element to their business strategy
