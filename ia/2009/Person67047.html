Steven Daviss MD’s Summary

Here's the blurb I've used at Gerson Lehrman:
Steven Daviss, MD, DFAPA, is the Chairman of Psychiatry at Baltimore Washington Medical Center, Maryland. He works as a hospital-based psychiatrist and clinical psychopharmacologist, consulting on depression, dementia, delirium, addictions, chronic pain, geriatrics, psychosis, and bipolar and anxiety disorders in medically-admitted patients. Dr. Daviss’ interests include psychopharmacology, pharmacogenetics, health informatics, and use of HIT and social media in health care. Dr. Daviss is Co-Chair of the CCHIT Behavioral Health Work Group, Past-President of the Maryland Psychiatric Society, and is on the Health Standards Committee for URAC and on the board of the Anne Arundel Co Mental Health Agency. He is currently pursuing a career merging his clinical and administrative experience with health care policy, informatics, and health care reform.
