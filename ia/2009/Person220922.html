John Michael Spinelli’s Summary

OHIO TUBULAR RAIL

As Director of Ohio Operations for Tubular Rail Inc. of Houston, Texas, from July 2008 to the present, John has met with state, regional and local leaders to educate and inform them on the patented "trackless train technology" that is Tubular Rail.

JOURNALISM AND REPORTING

Spinelli's career in journalism started as a beat reporter covering business and government issues. In 2003 he created Profiles in Business.

He wrote the OhioNews Bureau stories for ePluribus Media.
He worked as a staff reporter for The Hannah News. one of Ohio’s premier government- and political-news reporting publications.

His business articles have run in Columbus Business First and Columbus CEO Magazine. His travel writing work has been featured as cover stories in The Chicago Daily Herald, Dreamscapes Magazine, The Columbus Dispatch, The Cincinnati Enquirer and Hiatus Magazine.

Spinelli On Assignment, his travel-writing blog created in 2006 to document his 40-day excursion through Europe, displays both his writing and photography skills.

OHIO GOVERNMENT SERVICE

Spinelli worked in the Ohio Senate and with The Ohio Public Works Commission and as Information Manager for the Office of Ohio Secretary of State.

PRIVATE SECTOR EXPERIENCE

He served for eight years in various executive leadership positions with various Ohio chambers of commerce.

He also owned and operated his own successful small business.

As a communication consultant, he provided grant writing services and fundraising strategies to various governments and non-profits.

Two of his private sector accomplishments include winning national awards while serving as the Director of Neighborhood Business Development for The Columbus Area Chamber of Commerce.

COMMUNITY ASSIGNMENTS

Spinelli was a member of The Historic Resources. chairman of the Franklinton Area Commission, The Columbus Neighborhood Design Assistance Center and director of the Southwest Ohio InfoCenter..
