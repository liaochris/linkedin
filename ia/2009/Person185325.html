Peter Ashley’s Summary

Peter Ashley has extensive entrepreneurial, operations, marketing and business development experience from start-ups and Fortune 1000 companies.

Peter currently serves as the SVP of Business Development at Opinmind, an ad targeting service that identifies "signals of purchase intent" from user generated text on social media sites. At Opinmind, Peter has also served as President, a role in which he managed the Series B fund raising process.

Prior to Opinmind, Peter was VP of BD for MyWaves, a service for sharing video on the web and mobile phone. Peter closed several major deals for MyWaves including ESPN and Nike's Brand Jordan.

Peter Ashley previously led Business Development for the PayPal Merchant Services division of eBay, responsible for extending the PayPal transaction platform beyond eBay, and was the GM or PayPal’s New Media business.

While at PayPal, Ashley built the foundation for the Merchant Services business unit, which Mary Meeker profiled in her January, 2005 analyst report on eBay, titled, “The PayPal Opportunity.” Ashley negotiated and closed the first ever online shipping aggregation deal with UPS, which provided the foundation for eBay’s Shipping Center. He closed the first deal integrating PayPal into a Payment Gateway, which was described as, “[helping PayPal] increasingly be viewed as the “Visa” of the Internet.” Peter started the Large Merchant Services (LMS) business by launching deals with Overstock.com (OSTK) and TigerDirect (SYX), (the first billion dollar retailers to integrate PayPal as a payment mark in their online stores) and he closed the deal with Apple’s iTunes, where PayPal is a payment mark for iTunes music purchases.

Peter received his MBA in 1995 from the Stanford Graduate School of Business and a BA from Tufts University in 1989. He lives in Los Altos, California with his wife and three daughters.

Peter Ashley’s Specialties:

start-up financing/fund raising, team building and people management, strategic planning and contract negotiations.
