Leslie Rossman’s Summary

For further information contact: lrossman@pacbell.net

Open Book Publicity offers traditional publicity services that include booking national and local television and radio, national and local print media, writing press releases and pitch letters, as well as handling all details of author tours. Open Book Publicity specializes in creating events tailored to each author and designed to generate extensive word-of-mouth and media coverage.

Having worked with leading publishing houses including, BantamDoubledayDell, Ballantine, Broadway Books, HarperCollins, Simon & Schuster, and Penguin Putnam, among others, the principals of Open Book Publicity, Leslie Rossman and Emily Miles Terry, have acquired years of experience and a network of solid media relationships. Authors they have represented in the past include His Holiness the Dalai Lama, Bill Moyers, Robin Cook, Ali MacGraw, Julia Cameron, Judy Collins, Mary Pipher, Douglas Rushkoff, Daniel Silva, Rebecca Walker, Dr. Richard Lavoie, David Whyte, Dr. Miriam Nelson and Tom Robbins.

They are able to create and implement innovative strategies to market not only nationally recognized authors, but also lesser known authors who have written important books deserving national exposure. Their tenacity, hard work, and follow-through result in solid bookings and appropriate exposure for your title and author.

Open Book Publicity is able to meet all of your publicity needs on time and within your budget. Open Book Publicity values professionalism and is ready to work with you to create a complete and successful publicity campaign whether your title is a work of fiction, non-fiction, history, spirituality, pop-culture, parenting, business or technology.

Leslie Rossman’s Specialties:

We offer full service publicity campaigns and specialize in high-end radio including long-format interviews, National Public Radio shows and affiliates, nationally and regionally syndicated radio shows, and book-oriented shows at independent public radio stations and other major stations.
