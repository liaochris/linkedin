Damir Ćavar’s Summary

My professional experience involves research and teaching in Germany, the U.S.A., Croatia, and Slovenia in the domain of computational linguistics and language technology, in the domain of University level education (director of the computational linguistics program at Indiana University, head of the CL group at the Institute of Croatian Language and Linguistics in Zagreb) as well as industry (project manager, consultant).

Damir Ćavar’s Specialties:

Education, consulting, natural language technologies, computational linguistics, project management for software development, languages: German, English, Croatian, Polish, Japanese, Scheme, Python, Java, C, C++, Objective-C, D, R
