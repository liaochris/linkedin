Maarten Oelering’s Summary

IT consultant with 17 years of experience in software- and systems development. Performed many roles such as software engineer, IT architect, project manager, development manager and CTO. Worked for various software and systems integration companies, from small internet startups to international enterprises (Philips and IBM). During the last 9 years, developed a unique expertise on email applications, including email marketing applications, outbound email infrastructure and deliverability.
