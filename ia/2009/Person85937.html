Gert Pauwels’s Summary

Gert Pauwels holds a MASTER in Applied Economics from the Catholic Univeristy of Leuven and a postgraduate degree in Marketing from Chicago University.

He has over 15 years' experience in the advertising industry, working his way up the ranks to his current role as Managing Partner and Head of Strategy & Creative at Proximity BBDO in Belgium. He acquired experience in media, starting out as a research analyst at OMD, Omnicom's planning and buying company.

Gert's advertising skills were sharpened at BBDO and Leo Burnett where he was active in both the creative and the client management departments. As one of the Belgian interne pioneer, he had already been involved with the web for several years when he founded Atmosphere BBDO in the magic "post crash" year of 2000. In 2007 Atmosphere BBDO merged with Proximity and Direct BBDO to form Proximity BBDO, the countries largest integrated marketing company.

Proximity BBDO holds an impressive blue chip client list including Belgacom, KBC, Daimler Chrysler, Pepsico, Smiths Food Group, Unilever, ING, Masterfoods,
P&G...

Gert was a member of the jury in the 2006 MSN Europe Awards in Londen and in the 2007 cannes Cyberjury. He also is a valued guest speaker at BBDO University in Barcelona, GRP, the Belgian Marketing Foundation and the Internet Advertising Bureau (IAB).
