Chase Johnston-lynch’s Summary

I am a music video Director with over 15 years of experience in the making of music videos and independent films. I have worked in various roles from Producing, writing, editing, and as a 1st AD. I have mainly worked in NY (In the states) but have been re-establishing my role over in Liverpool (United Kingdom) for the past 7 years.

In England, I have taken a master degree and a PGCE to establish myself as a trainer and univeristy lecturer in my chosen field of video production. I have just developed a unique course in the bachelor of sciences whereas to deconstruct the technology and methodology of music video production in to its very own film school of course modules.

My independent video company, Square One Pictures focuses on the making of music videos, promotional documentaries, and corporate videos with vision. We are working mainly on the Wirral and Liverpool but now branching out to manchester.

Chase Johnston-lynch’s Specialties:

My main specialty is the ability to adapt creatively. I have directed several short films and my first independent feature, One Way Love (See british film catalouge). I have written several scripts and co-scripted a couple of films. I am available for script editing which I have done for several years. A good percentage of videos I directed have been on broadcast. So the films we make, we have an eye on distribution and marketing.
