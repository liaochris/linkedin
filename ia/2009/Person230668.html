Terrence O. Kommal’s Summary

Terrence O. Kommal: Is a seasoned military, medical and research professional whose research interest gained great recognition by being nominated as one of the youngest ever Full Member of the South Africa Royal Society and the only SANDF Member of the Society. (Nominated by the Dean of Science-UCT and RSA Presidential Order of Mapungubwe recipient, Prof Daya Reddy, and was duly accepted by the Full Royal Society Executive Council in April 2007)

He is also a very well experienced business professional and has worked in the ICT, Media and Web arenas for the last 9 years! Some of his clients included/include: the Massmart Group, Edcon, Esquire Technologies, KZN Dept of Education (Kwa Dukuza), SABC/Lotus FM, and others.

He has also served in the SANDF for many years as a commissioned officer and his specialised training. He further specialised in International Operations via UNITAR, United Nations and was admitted to the International Roll of Honour. Dr. Kommal’s academic career started early with him pursuing parallel degrees.

He is currently the Chief Executive Officer and Editor-in-Chief of Kommal Publishing (Pty) Ltd, and the Chairperson of Kommal Holdings (Pty) Ltd. Kommal Holdings (Pty) Ltd wholly owns Kommal Publishing (Pty) Ltd (South Africa, Cape Town), SUTRA Magazine Limited (UK, London), SUTRA Magazine Limited (India, Mumbai). Kommal Publishing (Pty) Ltd, is an “AA”/ Level 3 Contributor BB-BEE company, as certified by Empowerdex.


He has a great interest in developing the capacity of non-profit organisations as well as community based organisations.He in recent years has worked in collaboration with and/or been associated with various professionals and organisations such as Gavin Rajah, the 2010 World Cup LOC, Vinny Lingham (international ICT expert), the Indian High Commission/Embassy in SA, People Interactive Inc( India, US, Dubai), JHB/DBN/CT Fashion Week, Sarva Dharma Ashram (NPO), FNB, ZEE TV, and others.
