Paul Payne’s Summary

In 2005, I cofounded One Way Resourcing. The vision was, and still is to grow our business to be the leading player within the Construction sector. How? Delivering top quality service to each candidate and client - keeping it simple.

Today, One Way is an established business - we're proud that in many ways we punch above our weight.

In terms of our client base, we have long term relationships with many of the leading Construction Companies in the UK.

Paul Payne’s Specialties:

Recruitment in the construction sector - from general site labourers through to company directors on both a temporary and permanent basis.
