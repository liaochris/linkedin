Steve Patten’s Summary

A Business Change Programme/Project manager with a solid track record for delivery and a keen commercial focus gained.over a 30 year career within a wide range of industries and business contexts.

Has delivered many spectacular turnarounds and project rescue assignments as well as significant business transformation programmes resulting from mergers takeovers and business rationalisation.

A strong people person with a mantra of "Vision into Profit through People", recognising the povotal role relationships have in determining success or failure of any initiative. A licensed and certified Practitioner of Neuro Linguistic Programming (NLP).

Steve is an accomplished workshop facilitator (vision, strategy, planning, risk management, issue resolution, conflict resolution) as well as entertaining speaker on leadership effectiveness.

A co-developer of the "Synergy Programme"suite of interventions aimed at promoting a "change able" culture within the workplace and author of "Live Younger Longer", a guide to taking control of your health and longevity.

Currently available immediately for short or long term assignments.
