Laura Grempel’s Summary

I am from a very small town in Nevada and now live in Reno with my family. I am a faithful believer in Jesus as well as the marriage and family institutions. I am currently a stay-at-home mother to our giggly little girl Madison Reese. My daughter was born last November and I foolishly thought that I would have MORE time once I was able to quit my JOB and take care of my baby – I was completely delusional! I’ve thankfully been married to my husband Clay for 6 years with many more to come.

In the middle somewhere, I traveled to Europe to perform on a concert tour in 7 countries at age 15. During a short residency in Columbia Missouri, I had the tremendous honor of recording a Christian worship CD of which I’m incredibly proud. While in Columbia I was also blessed to go to a small community outside of Biloxi Mississippi to help with a harvest party for the neighborhood kids shortly after hurricane Katrina. All of these experiences were life-changing for me.


My main work experience is in the banking and financial world. I worked for a large bank for several years in different capacities. I learned a lot about what NOT to do with money. I’ve dabbled in several network marketing opportunities but never really took any of them seriously until I found a marketing strategy that made sense to me. I took on a mentor (or 5) and haven’t looked back. I am currently learning everything I possibly can about personal development in order to make myself more valuable to others in every way.

I WILL be successful in network marketing because I have a “burning desire” to be with my family and not waste my life working for someone else giving them the lifestyle that I wish to have.
