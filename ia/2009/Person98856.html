Ingrid Muller’s Summary

I'm an award-winning graphic and publications designer with more than 20 years of experience in design, writing, copy editing and photo editing. An experienced art director, I have managed and coached fellow designers and layout editors for several years, earning international design awards from The Society for News Design, National Press Photographers Association, Associated Press and Pictures of the Year International, as well as a staff award for the Pulitzer Prize. I'm also an accomplished writer and editor, and have found that I'm uniquely talented in marrying words and visuals in a very powerful way. My goal: work that inspires and challenges me on all those levels, enabling me to create a powerful brand, identity and message for your company.

Ingrid Muller’s Specialties:

Print design, web design, art direction, desktop publishing, photography, photo editing, copy writing, copy editing, writing for the web.

Ingrid Muller’s Experience

  • Creative Director

    The Denver Post

    (Privately Held; Newspapers industry)

    2003 — 2009 (6 years )

    Directed a staff of 20 designers and layout editors in the production of The Denver Post. Worked one-on-one with designers, reporters, photographers, graphic artists and web editors to create the visual report for the daily newspaper, as well as production of free-standing special sections and magazine reports. Hands-on design of the daily and Sunday newspapers, including high-profile investigative projects and breaking news events.

  • Lead Designer

    The Hartford Courant

    (Public Company; TRB; Newspapers industry)

    1997 — 2003 (6 years )

    Designed and copy edited the front page of the Courant, a major metro
    newspaper in Connecticut. Part of a team that covered a workplace
    shooting at a government building; the report earned the staff a Pulitzer Prize for breaking news in 1999. Also earned numerous individual design and photo editing awards during my six years there.

  • Designer and copy editor

    The Saint Paul Pioneer Press

    (Public Company; KRI; Newspapers industry)

    1994 — 1997 (3 years )

    Copy-editor on the paper's universal copy desk. Also became lead
    presentation editor at the paper, designing just about every section
    of the paper during three years there.

  • Features editor, copy editor and presentation editor

    The Poughkeepsie Journal

    (Newspapers industry)

    1990 — 1994 (4 years )

    Began as a reporter covering local news; then moved to the copy desk, which also entailed work in design and graphics arts. Joined the features department as a designer, then took over as editor of the section, managing a staff of 10 reporters and editors.
