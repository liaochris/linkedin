Melissa Bader’s Summary

I am an enterprise architect with deep technical grounding in various disciplines. After earning my Masters in Computer Science from Georgia State University focused in web development, I began my career in IT consulting. While teaching graduate level classes in applied IT, I also developed a network of clients for Microsoft architecture and design work. Active Directory and Exchange migrations led to SharePoint portal, Project Server, and Live Communications Server deployments throughtout the Southeast. With Cisco certifications, 3rd party vendor certifications for high-availability, and cross-platform experience (Linux, EGenerra, WebSphere, Oracle, AIX) as well as deep application support and architecture, I have been in the largest of environments and have had global architecture responsibility.

I am currently with Turner Broadcast Systems in the Turner Technology Services group as a Subject Matter Expert on the Exchange messaging platform. I hope to transition this architecture, which spans four continents, to the latest cutting edge technology and client platform. Exciting is an understatement.

Melissa Bader’s Specialties:

I specialize on the optimization of Microsoft technologies and in delivering the latest technical integrations to further the vision of unified communications to the client platform.
I have experience supporting streaming multi-media servers, print servers, Active Directory or LDAP integration servers, network equipment including Cisco routers and switches, firewalls, PBX, email platforms, web platforms, database servers, clustering, SAN storage and architecture, among other technologies.
