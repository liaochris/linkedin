Philip (Chino)’s Summary

I have an extensive background not only in the marketing industry, but in others as well. I graduated from San Jose State University with a Bachelors in Management in 2007. With an accumulation of 9 years experience in the marketing/promotions and entertainment business I have gained a vast amount of knowledge.

Everything that I have been involved with I have exceeded the expectations of management and colleagues. So I believe that not matter the job, I should be able to succeed and make the company, I am working for, better.

I am currently the head of the San Jose Division and coordinates nightlife entertainment as well as any events/festivities that occur in San Jose, CA.
