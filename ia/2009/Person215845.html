Shawn Coughlin’s Summary

Shawn Coughlin is a Principal and the Chief Operating Officer of Capitol Health Group.

Formerly with Steelman Health Strategies and the Law Offices of Deborah Steelman, Mr. Coughlin was Senior Vice President and Health Policy Analyst, respectively.

Mr. Coughlin worked for over seven years on Capitol Hill, handling various healthcare policy needs for several Members of the Ways and Means Committee. He was a Professional Staff Member on the Ways and Means Subcommittee on Health for Chairman Bill Thomas (R-CA), Senior Health Policy Advisor for U.S. Representative Nancy L. Johnson (R-CT), and Legislative Assistant with U.S. Representative Fred L. Grandy (R-IA).

Prior to his work on Capitol Hill, Mr. Coughlin was Legislative Representative for the Society for Human Resource Management, and the American Machine Tool Distributors’ Association. He also served as a Government Relations Consultant with the National Forest Products Association, the Weyerhaeuser Company, and as an intern with Common Cause Pennsylvania.

Mr. Coughlin earned his Bachelor of Arts degree from Lafayette College in 1987 and a Masters in Public Management from the University of Maryland in 1990.

Mr. Coughlin’s professional experience on the Ways and Means Health Subcommittee has led to the development of personal relationships with all of the Members of the Health Subcommittee as well as Members of the Full Ways and Means Committee and the Commerce Committee. His work on the Leader’s Task Force on health has also helped to foster relationships with Republican Leadership.
