Ladislav Martincik’s Summary

Hi, I’m Ladislav Martincik. My origin profession is Software Engineer and I’m currently located in Ostrava, Czech Republic.

I’m big believer of Agile way of developing software and I’m always trying to learn and practice as much as possible.

Last 3,5 years I’m mostly writing Ruby (on Rails) code. I’ve also done a lot of Javascript, XHTML, CSS and lately Flex with ActionScript. Also whenever appropriate I use other languages or tools to solve the challenge.

Along with passion for Ruby I’m enthusiastic about servers and server configurations and I’m eager to do more of servers related work in future. These two passions resulted in establishing new company called SimpleClouding.com which I’ve started in December of 2008.

Things I learning and exploring include languages Erlang, Scala; OS - Open Solaris; Clustering with EC2; Advanced Linux/Unix administration and iPhone development.
