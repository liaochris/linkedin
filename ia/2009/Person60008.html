Dean Guadagni’s Summary

My goal is to be the driving force behind entrepreneurs, small businesses, corporations, and job seekers' in their quest to deliver their value message to their strategically targeted audience through the use of Web 2.0 social media sites and blogging.

As career strategists, we support job seeker's efforts to find their desired job. We have identified the coming trend in job search which emphasizes a job seeker's ability to utilize Web 2.0 and Social Media networking to access the hidden job market.

Our proactive employment campaign is based on multi channel target marketing a job seeker's message of value, expertise, and experience. The delivery system for this value message begins with the establishment of a blog. The next system is a search engine optimized Linkedin profile which acts as the job seeker's 24-7 resume, and the final piece is a strategy that incorporates social media tools like Twitter as the broadcasting system.

As Social Media strategists our goals are to help build online presence, utilize social media sites for marketing, and help create a community of enthusiastic "evangelists" for entrepreneurs, small businesses, and firms' products and services.

My work experience as a blogger, social media and blog consultant, networking evangelist, business development director, and Web 2.0 evangelist supports my vision to become the driving force behind our client's success.

I am a frequent workshop facilitator and public speaker on such topics as career development, employment campaigns, networking, the hidden job market, Web 2.0 and Social Media strategies.

I am a member of the content syndication service blogburst.com and I author a nationally recognized business self help blog as well as our Inner Architect career strategies blog. I have had 115 articles published by mass media agencies Reuters.com, FoxBusiness.com, Nielsen.com, Chicago Sun-Times, Computer Shopper, and Ziff Davis Enterprise.
