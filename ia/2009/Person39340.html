Cathy Bryant’s Summary

Most of my professional experience has been in the teaching profession. I've taught both privately and in the public schools for the past twenty-five years. But writing has always been a part of my life. When I was in second grade I was in a competition called story-telling; by the time I was in High School I had advanced to Ready Writing competitions. While in High School I worked for the town newspaper, and had several articles published. I also won several essay contests. I have started several novels during my adult life, but could never seem to get past the 30,000 word mark. But in June of 2008, I decided to give it another try. I joined American Christian Fiction Writers, and began studying the craft of writing. In October I launched my blog, WordVessel, and in November I completed the rough draft of my first (completed) novel, "These Roads Lead Home." I will begin the editing and revision process shortly.
