Steve Clayton’s Summary

PRINCE 2: trained in Project Management Methodology. Experience encompasses Programme Management, Project Co-ordination, Project Support, Project Recovery, and establishing Project Office environments.

Considerable experience in the implementation of solutions and service delivery to UK Local and Central Government including:

* Change, Project, Programme, Recovery Management
* Modernising Government Initiatives
* Software development and product introduction
* Systems integration and migrations
* Audits, security and standards, risk, contract and configuration management
* Resource management including third parties, suppliers and services

Clients include many Local and Unitary Authorities, London and Metropolitan Boroughs, The Audit Commission, Department of Health, Food Standards Agency, National Care Standards Commission, the UK Research Councils and Police Services.

(When available for a position) I welcome a challenging role/project(s) and more especially those that involve significant change with associated levels of risk or projects that, for whatever reason, require a level of recovery action.

Steve Clayton’s Specialties:

Prince 2, Programme & Project Management, managing change in complex organisations, establishing working relationships at all levels of management
