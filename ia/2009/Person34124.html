Brick Graham’s Summary

I presently sell spas at home shows and special events around the country for local dealers in their area.Previously I worked as Sales Manager for A-Tex Family Fun Center in Austin, Texas for nearly nine years beginning in September, 1998. Before working in Austin, Texas I was the Sales Manager and company trainer for Family Fun Spas in Albuquerque, New Mexico for five years. I also sold top of the line Dimension One Spas and became one of their largest volume dealers in the country. I have received sales awards from Dimension One Spas yearly since 1999 when we first took the line. In March of 2004 I was recognized as one of the top salesmen in the United States by Dimension One Spas. I am well liked by my customers and coworkers, and, my ability to encourage people is only surpassed by my unique sense of humor.

I am Private Investigator licensed in Texas and South Carolina.My company is Inlet Legal Support in Murrells Inlet, SC.
Among other things, I have been a bartender, private investigator, massage therapist, taxi driver, Sunday school teacher, sold new trucks, cook, printer, telemarketer, sold spas (still do), network marketer, pool salesman, radio comic, surfer, knife salesman, sailor in the USN, paramedic, foster parent, owner of a coffeehouse, the volunteer coordinator of a gubernatorial campaign, written a book, been in a movie, sold time shares, and lived with aborigines in Australia. I am also part of an organization called Nourish the Children that has fed millions of children worldwide for the last 8 years.
