Bill Branson’s Summary

I am founder of Whiteboards That Work! ™ which I established in 2006.

Since 1995, I have been helping project managers, business analysts, enterprise architects, sales people and all types of professionals dramatically increase their effectiveness through visual communications and visual design strategies and techniques. I have more than 30 years experience as a teacher, coach, innovator, strategist, and technologist. I’m passionate about effective communications and find great satisfaction in helping others excel.

I am also founder and owner of Strategic Business Architects which I created in order to help professionals make the most of themselves, their organizations and their relationships by helping them improve how they acquire new information (learn), form and nurture their relationships, and communicate clearly and persuasively.

I use several industry assessment tools including tools from Birkman International and Kolb and philosophies and methods from Arbinger Institute as well as my own proprietary program for success.

I also coach individuals and groups who attended an Arbinger Seminar and are interested and ready to make the principles learned a part of their daily experience. In short, I act as their personal trainer.

Bill Branson’s Specialties:

Visual Communications, Technical Marketing, Enterprise Architecture, IT architecture, IT and Business Strategy, Business Process Modeling, Birkman International Assessment, Kolb Learning Style Assessment, and Graphic Facilitation.
