Manoj Karwa’s Summary

Manoj Karwa serves as Project Manager at Auriga Research Limited, a Clinical Research Organisation based in Delhi. Provide overall management of bioavailability/bioequivalence study and clinical trials conducted at ARL. General areas of responsibility include: Sponsor liaison, project assessment and initiation, resource procurement and planning, project implementation, leading and motivating a cross-functional team, milestone planning and tracking, ensuring that projects are progressing according to contract and quality standards, SOPs, ICH-GCP and/or other guidelines to fulfill local regulations, production of key project progress reports, management of communication between Auriga project team, Sponsor, contract and financial management. Being a key member of upper management responsible for strategic planning and decisions. He is also involved in development & implementation of internal policies, Recruitment and training of clinical research personnel.


Prior to joining Auriga Research Limited, manoj worked as Research Scientist at Nicholas Piramal India Limited (R&D, Goregaon) where he performed Preclinical Pharmacodynamic and Pharmacokinetic screening of NCE in Pain and Inflammation animal models. Manoj is a also an active member of Indian Pharmacology Society and participate in IPS conference annually. He is MS pharmacology from National Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research. He is graduate from Lachoo College of Science & Technology where he studied Pharmaceutical Sciences and earned a Bachelors degree in 2003.
