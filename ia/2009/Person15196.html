Andy Halliday’s Summary

Andy’s strategic roles as Entertainment Group President for Edison Brothers Stores and as Co-Founder and President of Simon Brand Ventures, a division of Simon Property Group (the nation’s largest retail mall developer) prepared him for his executive positions with leading technology companies, first as VP GM of Excite’s Electronic Commerce Group, then CEO of Contact.com, and as the first COO of In-Q-Tel. After co-founding Open Field Software and leading business development for Spoke Software, Andy felt that technology and timing had aligned to the point where he could finally realize his vision to create the social media experiences at OurStory.com and Tribbit.com.

OurStory users collect personal and family media in a lifelong interactive timeline. At OurStory.com you showcase your photos and videos in multimedia stories about events and perspectives on your life. These are organized and shared in a lifelong timeline collection of events, people, places, and memories, becoming a reference legacy for family and friends.

Tribbit.com is "group social expression", where family and friends gather together to collaborate on a multimedia celebration of someone or something they love.

Now Andy is working on the development of semantic collective knowledge base functions in online communities, and on an industry-leading initiative in Software Asset Management for enterprise based on the emerging ISO/IEC 19770 standards.

Andy Halliday’s Specialties:

social media, semantic social computing, electronic commerce, knowledge management, start-up venture management
