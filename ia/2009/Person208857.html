Sandy Green’s Summary

I specialize in print and digital design for advertising and marketing communication campaigns. Throughout my career, I have worked in many positions within the creative departments at Advertising and Marketing agencies. In doing so, I have worked on small projects that are on page 8 of the local paper and large scale projects with national exposure that represent multi-million dollar clients and projects. I have seen the advertising and marketing design landscape change over the years from print and outdoor boards to new media campaigns designed to be delivered on hand held devices and it has been exciting to grow with the industry and technology. I would be a strong addition to an agency’s creative team or can lead an in-house creative department of any size.
