Joan M. Bundy’s Summary

I believe I have a great deal to offer my clients as well as any cooperative business venture. With nearly a decade's experience in the legal field preceded by another decade in marketing, promotion and journalism, I am a seasoned professional who prides myself in exercising integrity, standing by my beliefs and principles, being trustworthy and honest and, most of all, working hard.

In addition to paying great attention to detail, I also am a big picture thinker who is curious about the world around me, wants to know how things work, how all the pieces of the puzzle fit together, and how processes can be made to operate better and more smoothly.

The most important thing I hope to accomplish in my legal career is to make a positive impact on the lives of others. I am focused on domestic relations and how to help people in transition.
