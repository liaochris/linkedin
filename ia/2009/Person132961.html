Kishore A K’s Summary

Prior to my brush with entrepreneurship, I used to develop software at EmSyS, Samsung, TrueSpan and SiRF, mostly as a part of multi-location and cross functional product teams. I also had the opportunity to represent my company on the working groups of a few wireless standards. I have remained an open source enthusiast since my university days and have always enjoyed contributing to community development projects.

I closely follow the advancements in web, mobile, TV, and related technology spaces. I am fascinated by the possibilities of consumer internet technologies.

At Althea Systems the scope of my work involves just about anything and everything that is required to run a technology startup.

I independently consult with companies as a developer/architect/adviser. In addition, I advise early stage companies on seo, online marketing and social media strategy.
