Todd O'Donoughue’s Summary

I am a seasoned Senior QA Project Manager/Consultant with over 15 years of experience in business analysis, project management and quality assurance methodologies including Waterfall, RUP, SCRUM, CMMI and Agile. I have extensive experience in both ISO 9001:2000 manufacturing and software quality assurance (SQA) in various industries including industrial, e-commerce, health care/FDA, finance, insurance, career center management and retail. This includes Web technology management/testing, Mainframe testing as well as Client Server testing on multi-level platform environments among Windows 98-Vista, NT, Unix, Linux, Middleware, Sybase, Oracle, MS SQL and Informix Databases. I have successfully implemented quality assurance processes and procedures, including developing test strategies, test plans, test cases and QA assessments. I have acted as a liaison between end users and system engineers to define systems needs and requirements. Additionally I have been recognized for my ability to communicate effectively to both end users as well as technical professionals. For more information, please visit my web site at http://www.sqasleuth.com
