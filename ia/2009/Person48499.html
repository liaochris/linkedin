Collin Lam’s Summary

In my young years, I have afforded hands-on, multi-disciplinary sales, marketing and management experience at many levels of business and as an entrepreneur.

It has taken me abroad, spending time in the two major financial centers of Asia, Hong Kong and Singapore, as well as Western Europe. This has given me the chance to build relationships with like-minded individuals around the world.

I hope to incorporate all my experiences in a rewarding business that generates culturally relevant products, operates efficiently and achieves maximized profit potential.
