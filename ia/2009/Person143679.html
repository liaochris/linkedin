Lori Riegler’s Summary

Cultural Consultant - Artists, Brands, New Media

Work with established and emerging artists across all disciplines to create, conceptualize, and identify innovative, and credible partnerships, opportunities, and experiences that support the progression of art, music, and culture.

Curate lifestyle and culture around music, brands, and fans that fosters true artistic collaboration and discovery. Tap into and collaborate with non-traditional & alternative media outlets, reinvent standard industry marketing practices. Develop intuitive, memorable, messaging and outreach campaigns that reach people where they are. Establish mutually beneficial artist and brand partnerships with integrity and value. Create high quality, tangible, product offerings, experiences, and customized content outside of musical recordings.

Lori Riegler’s Specialties:

Music consulting, management, supervision, event & tour production, concept & strategy development, outstanding A&R abilities, extensive global network of music industry professionals spanning all disciplines - artists, record labels, booking agencies, managers, promoters, venues, media outlets, distributors. Avid researcher, theorist, and advocate for new media & digital technology.
