Rachel Brown’s Summary

Approach Sales Services Ltd was recently formed, by Managing Director Rachel Brown, to provide results-focused telemarketing and new business development services, after many years of keeping other companies in business using her Sales skills.

With over 15 years of Sales, Telesales and Telemarketing experience, Rachel has headed up business-to-business campaigns for start-ups, SME's and established organisations throughout her career.

Rachel is a Fellow of the Institute of Sales & Marketing Management and works with an associate team of expert telemarketers and telesales professionals. All of whom are equally competent new business developers with a minimum of 5 years experience; some are veterans with decades of new business campaigns under their belts.

Because we're a small business, rather than a large call-centre, we're not interested in winning clients just to "fill the seats"; this is a long term commitment and Rachel proudly advertises the mission statement at every opportunity.

"We work hard to build long-term relationships as a trusted partner for new business growth"

Rachel Brown’s Specialties:

Developing the new business campaign, data sourcing, collateral development, CRM set-up and reporting.

Lead generation and lead nurturing telemarketing campaigns focused on generating qualified sales leads.

Senior level appointment setting services to open doors and set up sales appointments with qualified decision makers.

Ongoing demand generation, lead nurturing and multi-touch campaigns to manage new business opportunities through the sales pipeline.
