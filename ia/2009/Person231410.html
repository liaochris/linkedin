Jodi Gill’s Summary

The Experts Bench is an industry leader in providing experienced marketing experts to Fortune1000 companies on a project basis The company’s focus is on specialized marketing-intensive industries, seeking a deep understanding of the clients’ environment. These skilled marketing engagements empower F1000 companies to execute superior marketing performance quickly and with expertise. The Experts Bench enables clients to manage project workloads, maximize profit and lower marketing overhead.
