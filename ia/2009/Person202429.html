Roger Lawrence’s Summary

Roger has worked in IT for 20 yrs in many areas including: Mainframe Programming; Networking & Systems Support, Implementation, & later Design; Consulting; Project Management; Delivery Management; Training; Information Security & Solutions Architecture & Strategy consulting.
More recently he managed Consultants, and then Technical Evangelists for companies such as HP and Microsoft.

Now he has started Rog42 Enterprises, an IT & Strategy Company which helps organisations solve intractable challenges through the innovative application of people, strategy or software solutions. We provide specific software development in cloud, enterprise, and mobile technologies, and our largest project is FAARMS.

Roger co-founded FAARMS as a company which aims to help solve the water crisis using innovative software. You can find out more at www.faarms.com.
