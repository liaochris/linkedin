Christine Thompson’s Summary

Senior strategy consultant and interim marketing executive, specializing in helping clients grow their business. Skilled at thinking things through and enabling clients to turn the right opportunities into smart business. Multi-year track record in strategy development and launching new companies, products and market/application categories.
