Phil Murphy’s Summary

A dynamic, technically-capable and well-respected software engineer, with a solid 16-year track record in analysis, architecture and development. My role has broadened considerably over time to encompass business analysis, user experience design, interface design, information architecture, systems architecture, pre-sales and training. I have a very broad and deep understanding of the end-to-end process of designing, developing, deploying and maintaining web applications.
