Joseph Yen’s Summary

Over 14 years of combined experience in product management, marketing and entrepreneurship. Most recently, responsible for product management for Laszlo Systems, a venture-funded Web 2.0 applications provider. Previously, responsible for the B2B Integration product portfolio at TIBCO, managed numerous site features on eBay, started two venture-funded Web services companies and held a series of positions in sales, marketing and product management at Oracle. Served on the Board of Governors for RosettaNet, a global supply chain and logistics consortium.

Demonstrated ability to successfully work with executives, board members, C-level customers, analysts and top-tier VCs. Deep understanding of business and technical issues related to consumer web, mobile applications and enterprise SOA.
