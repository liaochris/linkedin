Carlos Gil’s Summary

Consultancy for Mining World: providing HR solutions and assesment for Energy issues. Recruitment for offsite and onsite resources. Management for operative and non operative staff. Trustworthy source of information for feasibility stage, purchases and on going operations.

Head Hunting: Executive recruiting for middle management and executive positions in LatAm at a very efficient and custom made consultancy firm. My focus at my business is quick response to my clients, high confidentiality in both ways and taking care of my candidates in order to help them in their careers.
