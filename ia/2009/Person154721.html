Marian Schembari’s Summary

Over five years of professional experience in the media, from working in press departments to research and editing dissertations. I held jobs during all four years at Davidson, balancing both studying and outside work.
I also have artistic experience, teaching art to children at a summer camp, working press and marketing at the Royal Academy of Arts, designing product displays at a functional art gallery, and participating in the design of CD/DVD covers at At Peace Media.
Additionally, my studies in Sociology and Gender Studies have provided a solid background in research, editing and referencing. I have taken a research methods and statistics course. Both my studies in social sciences and my extensive travels (13 months overseas, 2007-2008) give me a unique view of the world which will most definitely benefit any company I work for.
