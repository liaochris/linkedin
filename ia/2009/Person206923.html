Saed Abu Hmud’s Summary

Saed Abu Hmud Was born in Bethlehem Palestine, He studied TV directing and audiovisual productions at the Palestine technical colleges. After graduation in 1999 he worked as a cameraman and producer with major broadcasting bodies and production companies and as an assistant director in feature films.

He participated in many filmmaking and script writing workshops and training courses arranged by the Euromed Audiovisual Project 2, the Sundance film Institute and the Script factory. His first documentary film "Nativity Church" 28min was produced in 1999. Since 2004 he is working on short documentaries which was selected to many film festivals. Recently he launched his own production company "Bethlehem Audiovisual Production Center" BAPC.
