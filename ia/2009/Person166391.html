Michael Clarkson’s Summary

Michael's background includes 20 years in Global Sales, Marketing and Finance in the technology field. Serving in his final capacity in that field as a Global Director of Sales. Michael has been the primary team lead for revenue commitments up to $70 million annually.

Michael graduated Regis University in 1988 with a B.S. in Mathematics and Economics and from Regis University with an MBA in International Business in 1993.

Michael is an actively licensed real estate managing broker in the State of Colorado and a member of the Jefferson County Association of REALTORS®, the Colorado Association of REALTORS® and the National Association of REALTORS®.

He has attained the following accreditations in the field of real estate:

Managing Broker License - 2007
Broker Associate License - 2005
GRI - Graduate REALTOR® Institute - 2007
REALTOR® Designation - 2006

Michael's success in lead generation resulted in his being featured in the National Association of REALTORS® magazine on three separate occasions. REALTOR® magazine has a circulation of 1.3 million.

Michael has been a featured guest on 850 KOA, 630 KHOW, The Radio Colorado Network, and a featured Realtor® in the Denver Business Journal, and Denver Post.

Michael has also taught seminars in the areas of demographic marketing, lead generation and lead capture.

Michael is the owner of Conejos Capital LLC, Conejos Media LLC and Mile High Home Hunter Realty and is a partner in Cash Path Real Estate LLC.

Michael Clarkson’s Specialties:
