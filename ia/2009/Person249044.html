Diane Mueller’s Summary

Founder of XBRLSpy Research Inc, I am a long-time XBRL Evangelist, and a XBRL Implementation Strategist. I currently serve on the Board of Directors of XBRL International.
I am Vice President, XBRL Development at JustSystems.

I am also a frequent commentator and lecturer on Financial Compliance, XML Standards and Semantic Web technologies.

I have been designing and implementing products and applications embedded into mission critical financial and accounting systems at F500 corporations for over 20 years.

I serves on both the XBRL International Steering Committee and the Best Practices Board, chairing the software interoperability working group and the XBRL Rendering Technical Working Group, as well as an active member of the RIXML.org working group.

In addition, I am host and editor of a half dozen other drupal-based sites that allow me to pontificate on my interests ranging from geocaching, my pugs, and the Pacific Northwest I calls home.
