Nancy Myrland’s Summary

I am a Social Media Consultant, Speaker and Trainer, as well as a Professional Marketing Advisor.

I work with firms to help them grow their business through the strategic use of social media and other marketing tactics to strengthen their relationships with their clients.

I work with executives, professionals, managing partners, COOs, CMOs, CTOs, marketing departments, management, firms' clients and others to help them understand how to use, talk about, and implement social media marketing strategies.

I offer one-on-one and group training and coaching, as well as classroom training for groups to use the appropriate tools in a hands-on coaching environment.

Firms outsource their marketing strategy to me, and bring me in as a marketing partner to work with them on an ongoing basis.

I specialize in helping companies and firms understand not only how to use Social Media tools, but also how to be wise, efficient and strategic about their use in order to maximize their effectiveness.
