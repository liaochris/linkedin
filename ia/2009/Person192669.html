Rajeev Ratra’s Summary

With about 10 years of experience as web presence strategists, we at Arnima Design have helped scores of clients establish presence on the Internet.

Web Presence encompasses website design and development, strategic and tactical planning, internet marketing and all the activities related to establishing long term presence on the Internet.

We specialize in the following areas: website design and development including web applications and e-commerce websites, internet marketing, and strategic web presence consulting

With over 200 clients in more than 25 countries, we help our clients establish themselves on the Internet.
