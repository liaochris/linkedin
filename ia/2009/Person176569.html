Noel Anschutz’s Summary

Richard Harris Law Firm proudly serves Las Vegas and the surrounding communities with quality Social Security disability representation. We agressively advocate for the rights of the injured and disabled.

You may contact Noel directly at noel@richardharrislaw.com or 702.444.4392.

Ms. Anschutz’s strong litigation background, legal aid background, and practice of Social Security disability law, defines her as an experienced and committed Professional Social Security Representative. She is a member of the National Organization of Social Security Representatives, a Board Member of the Community College of Southern Nevada Paralegal Program Advisory Board and the State Bar of Nevada Ethics Committee.
