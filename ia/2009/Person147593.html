Martin Angeletti’s Summary

Martín Angeletti is an entrepreneurial executive with operational experience who understands how to utilize technology as a competitive advantage. With over seven years of experience in the field of information technology, he has had the opportunity to deliver value in professional services, telecom, health care and manufacturing organizations.
Martín has the experience of building entire departments from scratch and turning others around. Throughout his career he has always worked for companies undergoing rapid change and has implemented and integrated ERP & CRM solutions and large-scale software development projects.
In his various roles, he has continually demonstrated an ability to interpret business and market conditions. He creates a competitive advantage for organizations and bottom line costs savings by leveraging information technology solutions while controlling and reducing IS expenses, and has a reputation as innovative and dynamic agent of change.
He also has proven to be an inspirational leader and has led staffs of more than 15 people of professionals. He has worked in various labor markets including Chile, Venezuela and Brazil.
Martín finished his Masters in 2005 (MBA – IAE Business School). Previous degree was Systems Engineering (ITBA).
