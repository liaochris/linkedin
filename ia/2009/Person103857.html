Janet Walker’s Summary

Janet Walker is the CEO/Founder of Pulse Point Productions, Inc a multi-platform entertainment company.

Her duties at Pulse Point Productions include all corporate, creative and communication decisions. Additionally, she maintains a feature beat as a New York based freelance writer.

She began her investigative news reporting career covering fiscal news and any story with a hint of controversy. She has maintained a freelance news beat, freelance editorial services agency and now a feature beat for more than ten years.

She chose to nurture her creative passions, pursuits and desires instead of a single corporate pursuit in communications. In 1998, she gained acceptance to Circle in the Square Theater School.

She received recognition with her 2007 Pulitzer nominated coverage of Victim X and the Hudson County, NJ Corruption Machine. Her features can be found on Splash Magazines Worldwide.

She has covered all things New York from iconic Donald Trump and Fashion designer Rebecca Taylor to celebrity photographer Nigel Barker, Tribeca Film Festival and Broadway. She has worked with numerous PR agencies promoting their clients.

She was invited by the Consulate General of Israel Cultural Affairs to attend Israel’s 60th Anniversary celebration. Her review of Anna Wilding's, Buddha Wild has received mention as far away as New Zealand. Her review of Federico Fellini’s, newly restored, Toby Dammit, is featured on Wikipedia. She is frequently quoted as an authority in Film and Fashion.

Ms. Walker is inviting investors to be a part of an exciting journey as Pulse Point Productions, Inc., seeks a partner, gap financing, institutional financing or private equity. Pulse Point Productions, Inc., does entertain offers of acquisition.

Pulse Point Productions first feature, The Six Sides of Truth, is a high tech contemporay thrill ride through the inner sanctums of the Pentagon and is producer ready.
