Barbara Barza’s Summary

Strategic marketing consultant with extensive experience in all aspects of launching products to market. Engineering background with marketing expertise provides integrated big picture view as well as analytic approach to managing complexities of growing businesses. Interested in new product categories, with focus on small, high growth companies or new divisions within larger companies. I am also interested in applying my expertise to the field of education to make changes that can positively impact our students and the workforce to empower them with 21st century skills of entrepreneurship, flexibility, and adaptability.

Convert marketing strategies into results by:
-Create and validate strategies, develop implementation plans, and manage the rollout of products and marketing programs
-Define and launch projects and programs on time, on budget, and with impact and results
-Provide critical input for product and marketing decisions by conducting primary, qualitative research and analyzing secondary research

Barbara Barza’s Specialties:

Product marketing, product/program launch, program/project management, research and analysis
