  • Sr. Firmware Engr.

    (Computer Software industry)

    February 2007 — January 2008 (1 year)

    Wrote Drivers for various COTS Devices under Linux.

    Architected a layered approach to writing Device Drivers that maximizes code-sharing across Linux / WINCE ports by isolating the O/S specific components from the device-specific components. A secondary benefit of this approach is that the O/S specific components tend to become somewhat similar after a while and can be "boilerplated" as new devices come on-line.
