Brad Larsen, CRPS, AIF, PRP’s Summary

Brad Larsen has 23 years of experience as an advisor specializing in marketing, managing and designing qualified and non-qualified retirement plans with extensive knowledge about institutional investment planning.

Mr. Larsen's experience spans a wide range of industries including professional service organization’s (Medical, Legal, and Accounting), manufacturing, and construction with particular emphasis on the following practice areas:

• Investment Policy Management
• Fiduciary Risk Management
• Retirement Plan Design Analysis
• Vendor Search & Selection
• Plan Merger & Acquisition Assistance
• Participant Services
• Plan Renovations

Brad has spent his career building the Sales & Marketing Divisions’ of some of the most respected qualified Retirement planning companies in the industry today. His prior experience includes working with firms such as Principal Financial Group, 401kExchange, Nationwide and RSM McGladrey. Brad now puts his expertise to use for the exclusive benefit of ERISA Fiduciary Advisors, Inc. clients.

Mr. Larsen is a frequent speaker and has hosted, presented and served as a seminar panelist on a variety of topics including qualified retirement plan design, fiduciary liability management and investment manager selection process.

Brad Larsen, CRPS, AIF, PRP’s Specialties:

Accredited Investment Fiduciary Advisor (AIF®) with training in all components of a comprehensive investment process and related fiduciary standards of care.

Chartered Retirement Plans Specialist designation (CRPS) from the American College of Financial Planning

PlanSponsor Retirement Professional (PRP) from PlanSponsor Magazine.
