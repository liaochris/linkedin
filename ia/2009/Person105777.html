Javier Agüera Reneses’s Summary

Hello, my name is Javier Agüera and I am a young enterpreneur. Some people think I am too young, I firmly disagree. I believe that the creativity of the human being is extremely bright specially on people my age, and I think that it's important to take this into account. I have a entrepreneur spirit and I usually have tons of ideas rounding my head, and when I come up with an idea I usually love to share it with others and try to take it into practice.

I created my first webpage when I was 10 years old.. I even created a 3D videogame design studio (DarkGate Softworks) with some friends at the age of 15!!

I have worked as translator for several open source communities, and some internet companies such as Wix.com. I also have teleworked as part-time for a small company called Unified Mobile Communications Co. Ltd (formerly Unified Telcom), leading the multimedia (IT) division and e-marketing section.

I am now creating my own company, which I believe will revolutionize the mobile telephony ecosystem.

¿Objectives? Tons of them. I want to get my degree in Computer Science/Electronic Engineering and study abroad my specialization (master) in leading technologies. Maybe too optimistic but... not impossible ;) As of now, i do my best to be helpful in the various projects i am involved into.

If you have something in mind... don't hesitate to contact me. I like to take new challenges, and participate in innovative projects. =)
