Scott Dodd’s Summary

Scott is an awarding-winning journalist, online editor, science writer and blogger. He reported for many years at The Charlotte Observer, and his work has appeared in The New York Times, Scientific American, The Huffington Post and others. He explores and explains social media and works as the website editorial manager at an environmental group in New York City. He also freelances for magazines, museums and nonprofit organizations.
