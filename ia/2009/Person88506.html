Gordy Pleyers’s Summary

Through a multidisciplinary background at the crossroads of marketing and cognitive science, my objective has been to develop particular and valuable areas of expertises relating to consumer psychology and to the analysis, prediction and influence of attitudes and behaviors.

After several years of scientific research, I have decided to join the private sector to implement (in concrete and pragmatic terms) innovative scientific approaches for a wide range of projects. I have notably created a “Laboratory of cognitive & sensory analysis”, which completely relies on scientific methodologies, programming and analyses. It notably enables to objectively, precisely and reliably examine all the reactions (conscious & unconscious) elicited by a brand, a company, a logo, a name, a type of packaging, an ad, a website, a music, an odor, a color, a visual,…

In addition to these activities, I teach innovative courses concerning consumer psychology and the topic I have developed.

EXPERTISES:

* Effectiveness of commercial stimuli (logos, names, packaging, ads, websites,…)
Scientific analysis and optimization of the real effectiveness according to their most fundamental efficiency criteria (depending on a large variety of factors like the kind of positioning, of product, of buying process, of target audience,…)

* Sensory marketing
Applying a scientific and global approach in the light of new advances in consumer psychology enables to question – and to go beyond – the common intuition-based (and potentially dangerous) practices in this quite complex field

* Effectiveness of promotion strategies
based on the scientific research and according to all the relevant marketing parameters

* Market studies

* Social & environmental marketing
Making the most of powerful psychological tools to generate favorable behaviors for people & society (relating to health, energy saving, recycling,…)

* Emotional marketing (see my book)

* Political marketing (see my book)
