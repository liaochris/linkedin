Wendie Owen’s Summary

Wendie Owen is a highly-skilled freelance public relations professional with over 25 years experience and demonstrated expertise in virtually all aspects of public relations work. While she is an excellent researcher and writer (including press kits, bios, backgrounders, speeches, white papers, non-peer reviewed medical writing, brochures, fact sheets, presentations and more), most of her assignments involve media relations/media placement (from defining the pitch angle, to compiling media lists, writing the pitch letters and/or press releases, to contacting and following up with the media – broadcast, print and online). Wendie is justifiably proud of her outstanding media placement record - local, regional, national and, on rare occasions, international.

With years of experience as a public relations freelancer/consultant in both the public and private sectors, Wendie Owen has a history of success in a wide range of client categories from various sources. Many of her assignments come from public relations agencies in need of immediate expert help. Other assignments she undertakes come directly from a client in need of a professional communications program. In those situations, she works with the client to define the communications needs, key message points, target audiences and public relations goals. She then creates and implements a public relations program to reach the target audience(s) with the client's message(s) in ways that achieve the client's public relations goals.
