Jon Clarke’s Summary

I am extremely passionate about ‘Digital Media’, an ideas person, an innovator of new and profitable solutions, an avid fan of new technology and how it can be utilised either B2C or B2B or P2P.

In the past I have turned around and grown digital companies into successful ones. I created SubSites and SubLines whilst at Eyeconomy and since then have gone on to create more revenue solutions. My background is many years in advertising both traditional and for the last 8 in online media.

Always interested in Web 2.0 and it's many facets I created www.MediaStarz.co.uk a leading social community for advertising people to discuss both social and business issues and meet up at the many member listed events on the packed calendar.

I'm happy most playing with technology, helping people and leading projects and so this business is perfect for me. Feel free to get in touch anytime.
