Gary Jacobs’s Summary

Top caliber business and technology consultant – delivering 15+ years of progressive consulting solutions to leaders in the media, entertainment, communications, consumer goods, distribution and manufacturing industries.

Versatile subject matter expert with best-practices knowledge in: financial planning and analysis, accounting operations, financial reporting, regulatory compliance (GAAP, SEC & SOX), supply chain management, customer relationship management, contract management, information systems management, manufacturing, material requirements planning, warehousing and logistics.

Skilled in tactical “hands-on” project management and delivering economic value from business initiatives - particularly major business transformation initiatives - including: business process and systems design and implementation, corporate restructuring, outsourcing, mergers & acquisition integration, turnarounds and bankruptcy emergence.

Intuitive thought leader and visionary with a keen sense of the psychological and economic forces that influence decisions and drive results.

Trustworthy mentor and leader – capable of developing and motivating top performing teams; as well as managing the personal, contractual and financial affairs of individuals.

Poised, clear and concise communicator to people at all levels of the organization – from board of directors to clerical staff.

Analytical, and focused problem solver - proficient in solving complex problems by breaking them down into manageable tasks.

Expert in ERP systems and their deployment to achieve business process improvement, business system automation, and cross-functional integration.

Expert in business intelligence and desktop technologies used to synthesize (collect, organize, transform and present) data as information to support alternatives, recommendations and decisions.
