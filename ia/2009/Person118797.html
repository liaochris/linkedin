Jonathan Creaghan’s Summary

For the past 20 years Jonathan has helped people and organizations successfully maneuver through change by using an approach to business, leadership and strategic thinking that is unique and powerful. He has developed a proven system of tools and support that clients have used to see their landscape reality more clearly in order to navigate their way to meeting personal and business objectives.

Jonathan's first book LEAP! A Journey to Personal Power and Possibility, an integral part of the approach, is now recognized and available across the globe in 5 countries and 4 languages.

He currently completed 5 books in his Thinking Differently Leadership series. Each book is no more than 70 pages in length and deals with the pressing issues facing leaders today: Thinking clearly in times of chaos; Creating change initiatives that stick; Getting the most out of themselves and their management team to handle the challenges of growing their businesses. There is interest from 13 countries for publication of this series.

Clients include: Family enterprises, senior executives, leadership teams, business owners, and entire employee groups going through change.

As a result of providing solutions for the human side of business:

Clients have taken their companies from small operations to sophisticated financially viable companies.
Reorganized and transformed all aspects of their businesses from the bottom up.
Clients have broken through limited thinking to create new exciting visions and strategies for their companies and families.
Adult children have resolved long-standing family differences, and reconnected with their mother creating unity which transformed mediocre business results into new financial stability.
Mentored and groomed leaders to be better thinkers, and make stronger clearer decisions that have resulted in more peace of mind and in some cases, millions of dollars in savings or revenue growth.
