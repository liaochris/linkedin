Mila Talabucon’s Summary

16 years of experience in Retail Consulting, Business Strategy, Project Management and implementation of Retail Technology (Retail Applications, Point of Sale System, Supply Chain, Traffic Conversion, Global Ecommerce and ERP). Has multi-channel expertise that covers stores, ecommerce, mail order, wholesale and B2B.

Lead efforts to establish an ecommerce business in 7 months that generated $20M sales in the 1st year of operation.

Responsible for process improvements and technology for stores for a multi-billion dollar business that resulted to significant increase in store labor productivity with impact to increased revenue, 1% increase in conversion and $2.5M cost savings.

Very strong vendor collaboration that resulted to higher compliance to Service Level, implementation of financial penalties and successfully negotiated contracts saving approximately over $1M.

Manage successful large scale aggressive project implementations on-schedule and on-budget by providing risk mitigation and technical expertise.

Strong management skills overseeing developers, support staff, sales, client executives and business analysts.

Co-founder of Jimac Incorporated a POS Software Development and Consulting Company operating in Southeast Asia. The company grew from 2 to 80 people achieving sales revenue of $5M and is considered as one of the Top 5 POS vendors in the Philippines. Has financial responsibility to operate within budget, generate sales and meet profitability.

Established business relationships with vendors/partners/clients in Asia (Japan, Singapore, Hong-Kong, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia and China).
