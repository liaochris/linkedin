Jerry Bires’s Summary

As a uniquely trained recruitment professional who gained specialized skills working in a retained RPO environment at Accolo, I can help in a number of different ways to find, attract, screen and, ultimately, deliver difference makers to hiring authorities across a breadth of skill domains.

In helping to evolve Accolo's process and technology, I created and managed some 650 independent job campaigns and secured more than 300 direct, full-time hires. I was one of the point persons who introduced new clients to the Accolo process, and was tasked with making them ongoing customers. I still consult for Accolo, but am seeking full time work.

My experience covered the recruitment lifecycle and I'm comfortable and experienced at the multiple skills this work entails. Each job campaign began by interviewing the direct hiring authority. From that input I next wrote both a compelling job description and a job-specific online application. It consisted of 15-25 weighted multiple choice questions and several behavioral-based questions. This delivered a pre-screened candidate to my client and allowed us to telephone screen the candidates most likely to succeed in the position.

My role once a job was launched was to source within Accolo's proprietary one million member network; to mine the Internet and to navigate the social networks. I was able to fully leverage my AIRS training, Boolean search, Internet research and telephone networking skills. I can build credibility and rapport with candidates, and I'm successful at eliciting referrals from, or applications by, passive job seekers.

Jerry Bires’s Specialties:

Mastering the processes and best practices used by Accolo allows me to cut through a lot of noise, and to impose structure and a sound methodology on the job creation and candidate screening process. The level of granularity I can extract from a hiring authority give me a leg up when executing on a talent acquisition strategy that will attract high-quality candidates.
