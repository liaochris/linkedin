  • Jingdezhen Ceramic Institute

    Certificate of Completion , Ceramics , 2005 — 2005

    Through a unique linkage, the Jingdezhen Ceramic Institute, and West Virginia University have teamed to offer a comprehensive study opportunity for the serious advanced or professional ceramic artist/potter. The Jingdezhen Ceramic Institute is considered to be China’s finest ceramics art and design school. This program offers 12-credits hours of advanced undergraduate, graduate or professional level studies in Ceramics, basic language and culture, and Chinese ceramic art history.
    China’s roots in ceramic art history are strong and deep. For thousands of years, Chinese artisans have developed and mastered techniques and artistry that has set the standard of excellence in ceramics throughout the world. High fired porcelains, underglaze and overglaze techniques, celadons, copper reds, temmoku, yaoware carving, ash glazes and tri-colored lead glazes are just a few of the techniques covered.

  • Nanjing Arts Institute

    Certificate of Completion , Traditional Ink Painting , 2003 — 2003

    Nanjing Arts Institute is one of the oldest art schools in China. With modern studios and facilities, and western style dormitories, it is the perfect host site for an intensive workshop studio experience. Extensive studio time is dedicated to developing technical skills and artistic vision. Members of the Nanjing Art College faculty lecture and demonstrate their skills and ideas, introducing students to traditional Chinese painting. Studio time is designed to allow students the opportunity to incorporate these Chinese techniques, materials and concepts into their own artistic expressions and intellectual concepts.
