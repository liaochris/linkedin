Gregory Luce’s Summary

I've been an attorney for more than fifteen years, first as a clerk for a Minnesota district court judge, and subsequently in private practice and as the director of a nonprofit tenant advocacy group. I am now working for the Minnesota State Bar Association, overseeing its web-related products, including www.practicelaw.org, www.mndocs.org and other publications.
