LaTanya Orr’s Summary

LaTanya Orr is an award-winning designer and seasoned marcom professional whose creative and strategic approaches to business communications have impacted corporate, academic, civic and nonprofit institutions throughout the US and abroad. LaTanya’s professional career encompasses nearly 20 years as a marketing communications specialist for the University of Michigan, director of Creative Services and Marketing for Word of Faith International Christian Center and chief steward of her own marketing and design studio. Time after time, her tenacious resolve, savvy marketing genius and innovative design skills have consistently leveraged brand value and significantly enhanced the overall image of the organizations and clients she has served.

LaTanya Orr’s Specialties:

Branding and identity; art direction and design strategy; ministry marketing; book publishing; event planning; human resources communications
