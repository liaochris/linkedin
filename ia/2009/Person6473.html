Alastair Lee’s Summary

Economics degree. Worked in strategy in a management consultancy advising broadcast clients. Moved to project management within blue chip digital agency producing interactive TV and web products for multi-nationals. Moved to BBC to edit channel site. Commissioned indies to produce original web content and microsites. Launched BBC Collective, a leading contemporary arts and culture site with online community focus. As a sideline produced sites for BBC arts shows The Culture Show and Imagine. Moved to Time Out as Online Editor for London in Dec '07. Now responsible for digital development at the company.

Alastair Lee’s Specialties:

I work with production teams, combining content and technology to deliver compelling user experiences. My areas of expertise include digital strategy, editorial, production management and leadership. Content-wise I know most about music, the arts and travel.
