Ruta Raju’s Summary

Define the behavior of products and systems that a user can interact with love, delight and WOW!

Currently Ruta works with Yahoo! Front Page & Integrated Consumer Experience Practice as Ideation and Design Evangelist. Previously Ruta Headed Yahoo! India R&D User Experience Design Group. He also setup the top notch usability lab and Research Practice in Yahoo! Bangalore.

Ruta has extensive experience in collaborating with product stakeholder to identify the right solution that defines the user and business objectives to strike the right balance.

Ruta is a active and award winning hacker exploring new boundaries and canvas in social, collaboration and monetization space.
