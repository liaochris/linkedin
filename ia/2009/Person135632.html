Kurt Galitski’s Summary

A WHOLE NEW LEVEL-Always careful to balance exceptional service with keeping up with local market trends and industry developments, Kurt formed the Kurt Real Estate Group to serve his clients on a whole new level. He employs a well-trained staff of Buyer Agents with expertise in foreclosures and distressed properties and Transaction Coordinators to assist with all aspects of buying and selling real estate. The response from his clients has been tremendous. Local residents, banks, asset managers, investors and businesspeople alike turn to Kurt and his team of real estate specialists to keep abreast of real estate trends. He offers sellers the ultimate in advertising exposure (whether it’s printed collateral or online ads, call- capture technology) and a marketing strategy designed to sell a property quickly. Likewise for clients looking to purchase a home, he’s plugged into the Orange County scene and he knows how to connect buyers with their dream property or bargain foreclosure. At every stage of a home purchase or sale, his team of Coordinators monitor the status of every transaction. For Kurt, the aim is clear—to help others achieve their slice of “the good life.”
