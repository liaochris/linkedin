Steve Bastyr’s Summary

MOGFX is the common shorthand for “Motion Graphics” and is the working alias of the established motion designer Steve Bastyr, based in Minneapolis, MN.

Steve has spent several years in Hollywood producing high-end design & motion graphics, and is now back in the Midwest offering his services as a freelance motion designer and a consultant teaching motion graphics in the Twin Cities area.

Steve has recently been hired by Rasmussen College as a consultant to teach After Effects to their instuctors, who will then go on to teach the software to students. His goal is to now offer this specialized training to the public as well, and build the motion community in the area.

Please contact stevebastyr@gmail.com for any business inquiries.
