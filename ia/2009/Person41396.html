Charles Mittelstadt, CPP, CCDI’s Summary

Founded in 1989, ISC’s professional consultants, investigators and trainers have decades of combined experience in most aspects of security and investigative matters. ISC was founded in response to an absence of professional, need specific advice, assistance and consulting within corporate and legal settings. ISC employs some of the most credentialed practitioners currently involved in the private sector today.

Fortune 100 companies, celebrities, politicians and high-profile attorneys are among the many that ISC has the privilege of calling “clients”.

Over the years, The Mittelstadt Firm, a division of ISC, has been involved in investigating sensational criminal cases of national and even international proportions. The firm actively investigates criminal and civil cases on both federal and state levels, and without geographical limitations. Whether taking the lead investigative role or in a consulting capacity to overall case, the firm has consistently proven itself to be a significant outcome contributor. Many of these cases have been profiled on Court TV, Dateline NBC, in books, as well as other print and broadcast media.

Similarly, within the security consulting and training resources areas, ISC can count an impressive list of successful assignments and training features that have benefited countless corporations and their employees throughout the U.S. and overseas, as well as increased the skills of professional practitioners within the security industry and legal community.
