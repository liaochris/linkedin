Barbara Eilert’s Summary

I believe beginning with what matters to your customer is the only sane way to market. My job is to work with you to create marketing plans that will grow your revenue and company value by first understanding what your customers want, and then connecting with them to let them know how your company delivers. We focus on the most efficient communications chains for big results through smart marketing.

Services include strategic marketing plans, online and offline marketing programs, and media relations programs.
