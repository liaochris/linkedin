Michael W. Malone’s Summary

One of the technology industry’s most successful entrepreneurs; launched several innovative companies, introduced leading-edge online applications and raised more than $50 million in a 22-year career.

Named to Sm@rt Reseller’s “Top 10 in Technology” ranking in 1999, behind only Steve Jobs at Apple and John Chambers at Cisco.
