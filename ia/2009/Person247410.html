William Haney’s Summary

I am a Capital Markets Executive with extensive fixed income and equity experience in product management; product development; product sales, trading and marketing, in product areas including swaps, derivatives, structured products and financing.

Most recently, I was an Executive Director at CIBC World Markets where I headed U.S. Sales and Marketing of Equity Structured Products. I spent the balance of my career at Morgan Stanley managing and developing fixed income products for institutional customers.

With both an undergraduate and a graduate degree in engineering, I have a reputation for quantitative and technical competence. But I am also known for my ability to communicate, to develop concepts and products and to build teams.

I am a very straightforward person, “What You See Is What You Get”. I operate with no hidden agendas and have developed a reputation for high ethical standards; treating colleagues and customers alike with fairness and respect; and doing business the right way.

I am currently seeking a senior product role with a broker/dealer, banking, asset management or wealth management firm.
