Matt Heindl’s Summary

I am an online marketing innovator with the ability to leverage alternative digital strategies to ensure maximum engagement with a keen focus on ROI… and the experience and vision to extend digital promotions offline.

Most recently I took Ripe Digital's 3 online properties (RipeTV.com, OctaneTV.com and Flow.tv) from "supporting cast" to prime time players in the male 18-34 category online (2.1 to 5 million unique visitors)... allowing RD's sales team to add themselves the upper tier of RFP lists and increase sales by 200% while decreasing the cost per visitor aquisition by 50%. I handled cross-platform marketing initiatives by brands such as Dodge, Subaru, Puma and Sony.

I have also brought Heavy.com from 80K visitor sessions per month to 12 million unique visitors per month (Nielsen NetRatings) in two years. Heavy is on the bleeding edge in the broadband video niche and it rapidly expanding into the new frontier of wireless digital entertainment. I have become a specialist in being a catalyst for quick viral growth and seeding. I have spearheaded successful online marketing campaigns for Comedy Central, AMC, Burger King, Axe and many more.

Do a search for Heavy.com in the news... you'll see for yourself.
