Brock Ray’s Summary

Welcome to my LinkedIn profile. Thanks for stopping by. If you’ll indulge me, I’d like to take a few moments to introduce myself. The first thing you should know is that I’m an accomplished marketing and design professional with a track record of delivering strategic marketing solutions for clients such as Sears, Piper Jaffray, Wilson’s Leather, ADT Home Security, 3M, and many, many more.

In addition to my award-winning design skills, I have significant business and marketing experience under my belt. I’ve conducted marketing research, written business plans, developed content and analyzed the ROI of marketing efforts. I also worked as a senior recruiter for creative talent with Celarity. In this capacity, I used my background in marketing and design to connect talented creative professionals with the employers who needed their services. In addition, I recently completed a two-year term on the board of the Minnesota chapter of AIGA, the professional association for design

The question, of course, is how do all of these disparate skills help you, your firm, or your clients? Simply put, I don’t believe that you are looking for someone with a specific collection of skills or someone who can complete a certain set of tasks. Rather, I believe that you are looking for a solution to a problem: namely, the effective communication of your company’s or your client’s marketing message. That means you need someone who knows how to get things done, not just someone who goes through a checklist. I have the experience, and — more importantly — the passion for marketing to proactively solve the complicated communications challenges faced by a successful firm like yours.

If I’ve managed to pique your interest, drop me a note, and we’ll take some time to sit down and discuss your needs in more detail. I have every confidence that I will more than exceed your expectations.
