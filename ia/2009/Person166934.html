Michele Carroll’s Summary

A marketing evangelist who 'cut her teeth' transforming marketing processes for logistics and international trade giants, Michele Carroll is now one of the leading market entry advisors and 'connectors' in the USA.

From entrepreneurial family roots, through her own beginnings in sales, Michele’s foundation is from the frontlines of business start-up, turn-around, growth and successful change. As Director of Marketing, Marketing Services, Strategic Planning & Research for SeaLand Services, American President Lines, Itel Rail and Emery Worldwide, Michele was instrumental in researching, planning and launch of logistics and technology services and subsidiaries and directed transformation to more market-driven organizations.

Her marketing teams and clients have won industry recognition for market impact and revenue growth – including the ‘Reggie’ for Sales Impact, ‘Best in the West’ for Advertising and the prestigious 'Fastest Growing in Silicon Valley' awards.

Carrollco Marketing Services provides project management, lead generation and marketing support for a broad Software, Hardware and Service Provider clientele and is the leading market entry consultant for Enterprise Ireland Americas.

Michele Carroll’s Specialties:

US Market Entry, market sizing, product definition, lead generation,and customer research, secure funding, business plans, Board/VC presentations, marketing campaigns, communications (web, email, collateral, speaking, PR), -- interim leadership, support or project management.
