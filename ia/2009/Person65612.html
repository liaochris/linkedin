Donald Browney Jr’s Summary

Donald Browney is an Electronics Engineer with over 15 years experience in the areas of both analog and digital Design, Calibration, Test, Manufacturing, Failure Analysis, and Repair.

Donald is skilled at:
Implementing process enhancements designed to reduce product costs.
Creating schematics and PCB layouts using CAD software such as Orcad (Cadence) and PCAD.
Modeling circuits using analysis software such as Simetrix Spice analysis.

Donald is a dedicated, team player that embraces the challenges in an evolving environment and offers a commitment to the production of superior results.

Donald Browney Jr’s Specialties:

Switched Mode Power Supply (SMPS) Design, FCC Compliance, Product Cost Reduction, Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Design, Analog Modem Design, Battery Charger Design, Project Management, expert in Schematic Capture and PCB Layout, generation of Bill of Materials, second sourcing of components, analysis of existing designs for potential flaws, analysis of existing designs for cost reduction, fully knowledgeable of methods to perform not intrusive Root Cause Failure Analysis (RCFA)
