Lidia Kozlowska’s Summary

Experienced marketing and sales executive and consultant, having worked on top positions for the biggest telecom operators in Poland and international. Professional manager experienced in the management of marketing, sales and customer care aspects of a telecommunications business. Has a proven track record of successful product development and management covering scope of fixed and mobile, voice, data and internet offers both for the residential and business segments, marketing communications including introduction of new brands to the market and multi-channel sales management. Member of one of the first management teams working on MVNO introduction in Poland. Having worked for small start-up organizations as well as big corporate structures in multinational environment.

Lidia Kozlowska’s Specialties:

Marketing and sales executive, experienced in product development and management, multi-channel sales management, marketing communications, customer care operations, telecommunications and ICT.
