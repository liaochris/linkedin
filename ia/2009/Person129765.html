Kendall Allen’s Summary

As an independent adviser to agencies, publishers and brands, Kendall runs her pursuits through a company she established, Influence Collective, LLC, in NYC. She recently has operated as SVP for 'mktg', driving Digital Marketing Services development. She now advises on select initiatives. Other advisory work includes special assignment for Carolyn & Co. Media, where she advises founder Carolyn Kepcher, former Trump Organization EVP on digital and media ventures.

Allen’s 16-year path has led from print to digital media, from publisher to agency-side. She first spent a few years covering emergent media and tech in the SF Bay Area. Moving from print to the interactive realm during its most formative years, Allen helped found Thrive, an AOL/Time Inc. JV, ultimately leading consumer marketing and distribution for the company. She has held top posts at USWeb/CKS and Euro RSCG Circle. Allen’s most recent post before assisting the team at ‘mktg’ was as Managing Director for NYC's Incognito Digital, LLC. Here, she spent two years building out the agency from its nascent state, expanding the client base and establishing a voice in the marketplace.

A highly regarded thought leader within media and marketing circles, she was on the founding floor of Search Marketing, as an original executive at Fathom Online, for whom she moved to New York to establish the east coast – and then regional Director of Client Services for iCrossing. She contributes heavily to media industry thought leadership, as frequent speaker on the SES circuit; regular editorial or programming contributor to Media Post and iMedia; and periodic NYU guest lecturer and author in online learning. She also sits on the Producers Guild of America, New Media Executive Council; the Association of Downloadable Media, Ad Council; and the Ad Club of New York Digital Marketing and Media Council. And she serves on the Board of Directors for 212NYC, where she oversees the Professional Development programming series.

Kendall Allen’s Specialties:

Blended management profile, with significant business background on agency- and publisher-sides of the media business. Stellar reputation with reports, clients, partners and within industry. Long-term business operations and total client engagement orientation. Avid connector with a broad and deep business universe. Converged media background. Strong, balanced integrated marketing services bent, due to progression from print to digital, in various capacities, during industry evolution.
