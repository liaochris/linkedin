Ilyce Glink’s Summary

Think Glink, Inc. is a holding company for my media work. I have been a financial columnist, radio talk show host, book author, Internet publisher, and television reporter/producer for the past 20 years.

My work appears in my nationally syndicated column (appearing in 100+ newspapers and websites), eleven books, my top-rated Sunday morning talk show on Newstalk 750 WSB, online at ThinkGlink.com, and in my online video series, "Expert Real Estate Tips."

The goal is to help consumers make smarter choices about the money and real estate options that they have.

Think Glink, Inc. also sponsors on-the-ground informational events that allow consumers the opportunity to spend a day learning how to manage their money and real estate better. Our next event, HOW TO PROFIT FROM FORECLOSURES is October 24, 2009, in Atlanta.

At Think Glink Publishing, we create strategic content for companies to help them connect better with their customers and clients. This might include custom video, custom online or offline content, books, podcasts, webinars, speeches and other interactive opportunities.

We also assist companies in creating unique media and marketing opportunities for the content, in order to maximize their ROI.

Ilyce Glink’s Specialties:

Content creation, writing, editing, video production, radio hosting and production, television reporting and production, organization, and team building and management.
