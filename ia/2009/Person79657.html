AMBROSIA FALLS, co-written with Karl Scarff
Feature length crime thriller with elements of film noir.

Ambrosia Falls, on the U.S./Canadian border, is a location ripe for crime in general and drug smuggling in particular.
Bill O'Riordan is a down on his luck, retired homicide detective, a Mickey Rourke type with a central core of decency. Toni Malone is a hip black medical examiner investigating crimes and the love interest to Bill.

Bill is haunted by his last active case. His CI is found dead leaving two daughters motherless. He retires to forget, alternating between driving the night bus in town and killing time at his local watering hole until he finds himself implicated in a string of vice/occult-related murders.
