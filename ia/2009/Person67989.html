Deepak Taneja’s Summary

A Marketing specialist with experience across various categories. Bringing in over a decade of professional experience in brand management, research & planning.

Currently working as the Head of Digital and Lifetime Relationship Marketing at Nokia, India I am responsible for Digital e-marketing, Nokia Owned Media and Relationship Marketing. My last assignment was with Times Internet , India's largest online portal (www.indiatimes.com) , where I was responsible of driving Communities and eMail channel as a Business and Product Head.

Professionally a trained marketing professional from IMT (Ghaziabad), have also specialized in Strategic Account Planning from IIMC.

Having spent time spent at Lowe Lintas & Mudra-DDB. I have been responsible for looking after brand management and planning on varied categories from Automobiles, Durables, FMCG and Technology products.

Key professional achievements: I was the head of businesses in Mudra overlooking advertising & brand planning on Samsung, Hewlett Packard, HBO, Mitsubishi Motors & Dabur.

I was responsible for driving the re launch strategy of Electrolux and Samsung Telecom.

I have been on lead roles in the launch of LG and Reliance Infocom in past.
