Christopher Robin Miller’s Summary

Christopher Robin Miller is an experienced actor with Film credits that include; 'Hatching Pete', 'Return to Halloweentown', and 'Going to the Mat' for Disney Channel,
Television credits including roles in; 'Touched by an Angel', 'Promised Land' and CBS Movie of the Week 'A Husband’s Betrayal',
and Stage roles such as; Norman Cornell in 'Star-Spangled Girl', Jesus Costazuela in 'The Odd Couple- Female Version', and Mendel in 'Fiddler on the Roof'.
His years of experience working and studying with improv troupes; The Garrens, The Skinny Lincolns, and now with ComedySportz, as well as producing, directing and writing for his own sketch comedy show Chez Comedez, lend to his expertise in bringing out the best in those whom he teaches in his acting workshops.

Christopher Miller’s voice over credits include over 2000 radio and television commercials(McDonalds, AT&T, JC Penney and Dodge to name a few),
as well as animated features including;
'The Light Before Christmas', 'The Guardian Oath', 'Tiny Heroes', 'Davie & Golimyr' and 'A Dog’s Tale' on HBO Family.

He also writes and produces local, regional and national radio commercials (i.e. Meadow Gold, Library of Congress) as well as directing dialogue for animated features for Ageless Animation and Sandman Studios. His years of experience with working in “the padded room” as well as directing actors he puts in there, lend to his expertise in bringing out the best in those whom he teaches in his Voice Over workshops.
