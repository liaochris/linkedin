Michael Erney, TopLinked.com’s Summary

Mike's wide knowledge of the construction & engineering industries enables him to quickly understand the challenges of his clients, thereby helping them solve problems, develop alternatives, and find creative ways to promote their businesses.

Mike has had success placing Construction Executives, Project Managers, Construction Superintendents, Senior Engineers, HR Professionals, and "key" Operations Personnel. He has placed candidates successfully at clients such as ESCO Turbine, Lockheed Martin, URS, Black & Veatch, AECOM, The Shaw Group, CH2M Hill, Goodyear, Sanmina-SCI, Arcadis, & Parsons to name a few.

Mike earned his Bachelor's Degree in Finance from John Carroll University. After spending 2 years as a Financial Consultant, he decided to pursue his current career in the field of human capital management.
