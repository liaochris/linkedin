Erick Carlson’s Summary

Currently VP of Sales and Marketing for Jalousie Residences Development in St. Lucia, an MGroup Company.

Successful sales professional with 18 years experience in services and solution sales in the EDA, PDM, and CAD markets both US and Scandinavia. Demonstrated ability in large account penetration, business benefit solutions and team selling coordination.

Additional 14 years experience in electrical design for factory floor automation, burner flame management and instrumentation in the Electrical Controls discipline.

Recently (2006), sold World Weatherwatch where I was president. World Weatherwatch was a 20-year-old Canadian weather forecasting company. I was responsible for it’s reorganization and finding a buyer since 2003.
