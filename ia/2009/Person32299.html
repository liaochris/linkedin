Brandy Schaffels’s Summary

I've spent more than 20 years as a journalist supporting the automotive industry, and have worked for such magazines as Car Craft and Motor Trend. I was the Project Manager for MotorTrend.com for more than six years, developing, writing, and photographing all kinds of editorial content from automotive reviews to domestic and international auto shows; I also managed their forums and e-newsletters and oversaw their Spanish sister site MotorTrendenEspanol.com. Since February 2007, I've been blogging nearly daily as a contributing editor for the female friendly automotive website AskPatty.com, am the managing editor for the online magazine AutomotiveTraveler.com, and spent six months as an automotive consultant for JMPRPublicRelations.com. I recently joined the social applications team at Zurock, where I am helping to launch their new facebook application at http://udrive.zurock.com

Brandy Schaffels’s Specialties:

Full editorial support including news and feature writing and photography for both print production and online presentation. Expert at Quark Xpress, and Photoshop. Proficient in html and css. Specialist in Web 2.0, best internet SEO practices and social media.
