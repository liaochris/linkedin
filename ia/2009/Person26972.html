Bernard Collin’s Summary

Business savvy combined with Expertise in Technology

I started my professional career with the avenue of the micro computers and have been in this industry since 1977. I created and operated my first business as a reseller of computers in Belgium, and have more than 20 years as entrepreneur in the computer industry, with the last 7 in Security.

What makes our company unique is the blend of technology understanding and business acumen. We always approach a company through a business angle before looking at security and our audit and advise is always tailored to match Business objectives with security measures, rather than pure deployment of technology.

Bernard Collin’s Specialties:

We understand the business impact of security on an organisation and we master the technology. We offer real security matching business objectives.

We have a unique approach analysing Systems, Data, Processes and Functions in order to develop an intelligent risk management strategy, compliant with security standards (BS7799)

We can review your existing security and provide you with a clear report to get you certified on security standards, to reassure you suppliers and business partners
