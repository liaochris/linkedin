Heather Lutze - Marketing Speaker’s Summary

Company Founder Heather Lutze - Marketing Speaker Specializing in Internet and Search – Heather is a nationally recognized speaker, trainer and consultant in search engine placement, cost per click models, natural search, and ad campaign tracking. She trained for Yahoo! Search Marketing for two years, and also trains for Pay Per Click Summit nationwide. Heather is a sought-after consultant, marketing speaker and instructor who conducts popular trainings and presentations in cities throughout the U.S.

Prior to forming the Findability Group (formerly Lutze Consulting), Heather had her own web design company. In fact, it was her clients’ frustration about getting traffic to their sites that was her inspiration for learning and mastering the art and science of search engine visibility. Before starting her web design firm, Heather’s notable internet experience included executing a two million dollar internet marketing strategy for major dot com, developing and architecting a $200K online Amazon.com promotion, and consolidating a $20K click budget for seven major CPC campaigns into one centralized reporting tool. Her employment background includes positions with U.S. West, Borg-Warner and TEK Consulting.
