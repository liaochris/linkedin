Dean Nelson’s Summary

I have generated over $2B in sales for companies like Comcast, Sun Microsystems and Teleglobe over a 20-year career. I've found my way into the hottest areas of technology innovation and launched dozens of services and products. I'm particularly adept at forming strategic alliances, partnerships and joint go-to-market strategies, and most recently developed commercial (revenue-generating) applications for services that the Comcast Media Center developed for its parent company - Comcast.

You will see that my network extends from executives at most of the major television networks to cable companies and new media content distributors. I like to leverage this network in developing commercial projects for clients - and can significantly shorten the time-to-market of new products and services.

Dean Nelson’s Specialties:

Business development, marketing, sales, negotiations, contracts, new media, global, production, executive management, strategic planning.

Dean Nelson’s Experience

  • President/CEO

    Clear Creek Media LLC

    (Telecommunications industry)

    March 2009 — Present (5 months)

    Clear Creek Media has three lines of business; 1) content creation, distribution and advertising/sponsorship; 2) creation of content brands and communities of interest using linear, VOD, Internet and mobile distribution; 3) development of strategic plans and business development for new technologies, including partnerships, sales, financing, mergers and acquisitions.

  • SVP of Bus. Dev.

    TV Anywhere

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    February 2009 — Present (6 months)

    TV Anywhere is a disruptive technology that integrates video, the Internet, Radio, podcasting, consumer generated content, and personal libraries of video and audio with a point and click navigation tool. It brings the most popular services to consumers using a television, MPEG4 video stream (DBS) and DSL/cable connection. It will enable small cable and telco operators to provide their clients with the best services available.

  • Sr Dir of Commercial Services

    CMC

    (Telecommunications industry)

    2002 — 2007 (5 years)

    Managed Commercial Business Development, Marketing and two Sales teams chartered with generating revenue to support both Comcast Media Center's Commercial activities and the development of services for the parent company; Comcast Cable.

    Over a period of five years, Dean assumed the responsibility for Production sales, Content Services (Programmer) sales, Affiliate (Distributor) sales and Marketing for all services.

    He also initiated the business development efforts for digital signage, and participated in the development of streaming services, VOD, advertising distribution, broadband services and the expansion of the HITS program distribution platform from 150 channels to over 260, including HD.

    He is credited with building and managing the high-performance teams that expanded the subscriber base for distribution services from 9.6M to over 17.3M.

  • GM, Enterprise Services Global Business Office

    Sun Microsystems

    (Public Company; JAVA; Information Technology and Services industry)

    June 1998 — March 2002 (3 years 10 months)

    Managed a team that negotiated Enterprise Services Global Trading Agreements with top 50 accounts. Team was located in four countries. Also managed the Passport project which managed global sales commission conflicts.

  • President

    Dean Nelson Comm. Inc.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 1991 — June 1996 (5 years 4 months)

    Managed a virtual marketing, sales consulting and event promotions agency. This agency created financing and managed sponsorships for the Grand Prix Cycliste de Montreal, The Teleglobe Triathlon Series, Canadian Television production of the Tour de France, the Second World Mountain Bike Championships, and a number of other events.

    The agency also conducted communication audits of government agencies, worked on corporate land re-zoning projects and special events supporting product launches for technology companies.

  • Sales and Advertising Manager

    Teleglobe

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; TLGB; Telecommunications industry)

    A number of positions in Public Relations, Sales and Marketing and advertising over almost 10 years.

    Managed the advertising and sales promotion functions, and working with an agency, created award winning commercials in the UK, France and the Caribbean.
