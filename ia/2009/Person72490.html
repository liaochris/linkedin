Everett Marwood’s Summary

Experienced and comfortable in most areas of corporate responsibility as a result of fifteen years of senior professional and managerial experience in Fortune 500 environments.

Extensive experience in entrepreneurial endeavors with startups and in sustaining operational enterprises, spanning a 25 year period.

The Company's business objective is to partner with motivated individual and corporate entities to structure financial success models that project these entities towards exceptional financial achievements for the future. The resources and expertise for this objective will draw on the experience and networks developed over numerous years in successful financial and entrepreneurial initiatives.

We explore the positive characteristics of an individuals conduct and choices that make them capable of exceptional achievement. This includes identifying the interests and motivations that tend to contribute to, or distract from, individual initiative and the effectiveness of that initiative. The process stresses the importance of life-skills, attitudes, and excellence in the development of success models.

Everett Marwood’s Specialties:

Profession is the application of financial and human resources to stimulate and encourage the growth of motivated and ambitious individuals and corporations.

Commitment is to engage in confidential, casual, complimentary, and non-judgmental dialog on issues of individual and corporate growth towards identified objectives.
