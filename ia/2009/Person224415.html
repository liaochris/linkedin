Steven Howard’s Summary

Based in Wellington, New Zealand, Steven Has enjoyed a career as a manager in information technology and banking spanning over 24 years, including people management, professional development, performance management, and organisational change.

Steven's company, Illumine Consulting Ltd., was founded in 2003 as a base for his personal & executive coaching business. This has now extended into working with RFP contract proposal teams as a bid coach specialising in tailoring bid proposals to the client's business goals and organisational culture. This bid coaching work includes researching, analysing and interpreting client culture and business objectives; assisting bid writers to write for the target audience; and helping client facing team members to design and deliver more effective presentations.

Steven's academic and professional interests include: leadership, personal development, coaching, organisational culture, the psychology of gender, social constructionism, post-modernism, power dynamics, career change, career development, business development, and social networks.
