Angus Phillipson’s Summary

Angus Phillipson is a founding director of eShopworks Ltd the ebusiness consultancy and web software development company behind the WORKSsitebuilder site management and ecommerce software product.

Angus has been involved in the development and promotion of successful, profitable web sites for 10 years, particularly within the Publishing sector.

Clients Include; Faircount Media Group, Business Monitor International, LexisNexis, Phaidon Press, Fleet Street Publications, International Network for Scientific Publications (INASP) etc

Angus Phillipson’s Specialties:

Angus is a business person rather than a technologist whose primary focus is attracting, converting and retaining customers online.

Areas of specialisation include; content management systems, online business development, web application development, agile development processes, user conversion strategies, online community building, online marketing (particularly search engine optimisation), KPI definition, web analytics and reporting, usability and web-site accessibility.
