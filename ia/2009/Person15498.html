Aneel Lakhani’s Summary

I have 10+ years of professional experience in tech--from support call-centers and systems administration to teaching to consulting to product development and management.

I'm a fan of community-building and participation, and frequently organize grassroots efforts to breath some life into the huge organization I work for--from blogging and podcasting to informal networking events and parties. I think everything in business should be transparent, but not overbearingly so, so that all employees can understand and perhaps throw their ideas into the mix of the strategy and direction of an organization.

I'm restless, a little academic, love to be challenged, love to find ways to make processes simpler and better, love to connect people more closely to the work they do and the impact they have.
