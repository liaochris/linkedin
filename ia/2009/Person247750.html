Will Schwartz’s Summary

Advertising ~ Marketing ~ Business Development

Will Schwartz is an accomplished sales and business development executive with a facility for bringing new products to market through creative marketing strategies and targeted advertising opportunities.

Will has an extensive background in sales and marketing for early-stage, high-growth Internet companies, having worked with several enterprises from inception, as well as more established and public companies. Will has an outgoing, poised and persuasive communication style and brings an entrepreneurial spirit and creative expertise to his work.

Will has great people skills and the proven business development acumen to help clients take advantage of cutting-edge technologies and bring products to market effectively. Will offers insightful problem solving, outside-the-box thinking, enthusiasm, and persistence to help clients enjoy building product value and creating revenue streams.

Will holds a Bachelor’s from the University of Chicago in Religion and Philosophy, which has been invaluable in helping him to think universally, network globally, and act locally from his home base in beautiful Portland, Oregon with his wife Makiko, son Izak, and daughter Sakura.
