Reza Amini’s Summary

Mechanical engineer with a PhD in the field of thermofluid science. Broad experience with the solid modeling application SolidWorks and computational fluid dynamic (CFD) software tool, Flotherm. Advanced knowledge of medical and biomedical related experiments. Proven track of record in design, development and design verification of electronic cooling solutions. Active team player as well as an individual contributor with strong problem solving skills

Reza Amini’s Specialties:

Fluid Dynamics analysis, Thermal Analysis, Respiratory System Mechanics and Modeling
