Vivian Mendoza’s Summary

Vivian has an extensive business background in selling, consulting, and managing projects which have resulted in improved business operations for her clients. Vivian influences and manages client relationships, takes personal responsibility for delivering solutions, and proactively seeks sales opportunities. Vivian has led various business development activities including pre-sales presentations, demos, responses to RFPs, and proposals. Vivian continuously acts as mentor/coach to others and builds effective teams.

Vivian has a strong functional and technical background, with over 8 years of experience implementing Customer Relationship Management processes and applications. She has broad product knowledge as well as experience in all phases of implementation, including business development, project management, fit gap analysis, business process prototype/design, development, testing, end-user training and production support. Vivian has consistently filled leadership roles and has experience managing both small and large teams.

In addition to selling and managing CRM implementations, Vivian engages with customers frequently to assist them with CRM strategy planning.
