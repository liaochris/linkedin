Robb Lewis’s Summary

Entrepreneurial-minded executive with proven hands on experience defining and driving products and programs for technology companies. I balance technical savvy and creative thinking with solid business and marketing skills to produce innovative product experiences. I'm a team player with the focus, drive and passion to deliver products customers love to buy and sales teams love to sell.

Key Experience:

• eCommerce & Merchandising –Experience with both digital media (download and streaming of games, video, audio and software) and physical goods; Performed both POV's - Front-end merchandising, behavioral mapping and back end software development of products and features such as a product recommendation engine, cross sell matching, contextual product lists, gift cards, coupons and more.

• Social Media - Introduced use of social media into the Retrevo experience. Twitter, facebook, other key SM networks as well as launching a social community on Retrevo called Friends and Fans.

• Advertising / Digital Media Monetization - Enabled new revenue streams by driving the vision and release of new and innovative new media advertising channels: In-content advertising products (In-game ads and in video / audio streaming media ads), pay per use, subscription, virtual goods, ad networks and single site advertising solutions.

• User Experience - Implemented an Apple style philosophical approach to human interface design process producing intuitive and attractive human interfaces at Retrevo that have contributed to a 30%-60% growth in PV/V with ARPU and conversion rates up by mid to high double digits in the most popular categories.

• Real-Time Based Features - Leveraged Retrevo's vertical search algorithms and real-time data to produce consumer facing products such as the Real Time Review, Value Assessment and the Product Advisor.
