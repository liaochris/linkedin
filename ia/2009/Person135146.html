K R V "Raja" Subramanian’s Summary

I co-founded Radix Learning in January 2007. Radix Learning is involved in technology-enabled learning and technology-enabled assessment. Our first program called Yogyata (www.yogyata.com), in collaboration with IIIT, Bangalore, has been launched. Yogyata is an employability enhancement program that aims to transform individuals aspiring to enter the IT industry through imbibing of strong problem-solving & technological foundations along with business communication & soft skills. This program is offered through a blended learning approach.

Earlier, I served as CEO of Ascendum Systems (formerly AnswerPal) during March 2000 and Dec 2006. I have also served as Professor of Computer Science and Dean of Distance Learning at the prestigious Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS) in Pilani, India - considered to be among the top 5 universities for higher learning in India.

I also served as Director & Secretary on the board of the International Board of Standards for Training, Performance and Instruction (www.ibstpi.org).

Do contact me on email or phone if we have some areas of common interest. And, of course, let me know if you feel that there are business opportunities that we can discuss seriously!
