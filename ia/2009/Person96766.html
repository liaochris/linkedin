Hamilton Jones’s Summary

My career over the last 14 years has been focused on excellence in client service, client development and entrepreneurial business management, in the arena of digital communications and advertising.

I am passionate about digital and the role it plays in both the consumers and brands lives and helping both meet in an engaging and beneficial way.

There is a wealth of opportunity in digital and my mix of traditional and digital marketing skills built over 14 years puts me in a unique position to offer a breadth of ability and experience to capitalise on those opportunities.
