John McMillan’s Summary

I received a degree in May of 2008 from University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I was an advertising major and worked throughout my college career and was very involved in my fraternity. I was a social chair and recruitment chair for two terms. I worked at the Daily Nebraskan selling retail ad space as well as Pioneer Coatings in the summers and Lincoln Parks and Rec.

I had two internships in and after college as well. I worked in retail sales for Suburban Newspapers and at Bozell in the media department. I love working and am very interested in media and its changing landscape. I also love sports especially Husker football, basketball, baseball, and volleyball. I enjoy playing basketball, volleyball, soccer, and golf as well. I try to get in as many leagues as I can handle.

I am outgoing and stay busy on the weekends with whatever Omaha has to offer. I have a lot of energy and would love to work in media, sales, or something that combines the both.
