Wendy Shepherd’s Summary

I have been providing trainings on stress management, parenting, child development, and balancing work/life issues for 20 years. In addition to one-hour seminars and webinars, I conduct longer workshops and have been a keynote speaker at many conferences. My background also includes work in advocacy, which has led to a long commitment to bettering the field of child care in Michigan and nationally. In recent years, I have added the application of humor and laughter into stress management and parenting. This is a wonderful blend of realities - and strategies - that reflect the needs of today's employees, managers, and families.

Wendy Shepherd’s Specialties:

Speciality areas include: stress management; parent education; humor/laughter; change management; advocacy; parental involvement and engagement

Wendy Shepherd’s Experience

    Reach Out and Read Michigan

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    July 2006 — Present (3 years 4 months)

    Reach Out and Read Michigan Coalition is part of the national Reach Out and Read nonprofit organization. ROR's mission is to make literacy promotion a standard part of pediatric primary care, so that children grow up with books and love a learning. There is a special focus on children growing up in poverty as the target population. As Outreach Manager, my responsibilities include fund-raising efforts, media outreach, event management, and technical assistance to the various Reach Out and Read sites in Michigan. For more information, please visit http://www.reachoutandreadmich.org

    ALFAB Services

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    June 1991 — Present (18 years 5 months)

    I have been providing trainings to corporate, nonprofit, community, and educational groups since 1991. I specialize in stress management, humor therapy, parent education, and advocacy.
