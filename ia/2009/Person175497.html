Nikki Hoole’s Summary

A born entrepreneur, my primary experience since graduating has been as director of Ivyleague Consulting - my first business, established rather informally in 2004 for a small and elite clientele , which has now grown to cater for every organisation from universities and NPO's to small and medium enterprises and large corporates.

My passion is enabling others to achieve excellence - my goal is to reach the younger generations through my newest venture Vogue Frosting and to take Ivyleague Consulting international - particularly further into other African countries
