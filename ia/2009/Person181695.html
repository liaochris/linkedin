Patricia Kowal’s Summary

Assisting with restoring balance and well being for a variety of disorders (gastrointestinal, OB GYN, all types of pain, etc...) using both Chinese and Japanese acupuncture styles and Chinese Herbal medicine. My goal is to offer any individual seeking treatment a chance for an extensive intake and follow up treatments tailored for their specific needs, and offering direct communication with those that use American Sign Language to communicate.

Services:
Chinese Herbal Medicine
Chinese and Japanese style acupuncture
Dietary and lifestyle guidance
Natural at home health care
Techniques for mind/body integration

Patricia Kowal’s Specialties:

Acupuncture and Chinese Herbal medicine services to those using American Sign Language to communicate. Specializing in OB/GYN issues. Many other medical issues also addressed.
