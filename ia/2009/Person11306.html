Andrea Gastaud’s Summary

I am always on the look-out for new experiences and ventures. Living proof is my THIRD development in 2008 (what a year) where I partnered up to form our own recruitment company NSU staffing solutions - leaving " No Stone Unturned".
Earlier in 2008 I had opened Travel-Window, a One-stop solution for all your travel requirements and in the beginning of 2008, Auto International, worldwide car rental solutions came to life.

NSU offers you 5134 things that everyone else offers you...and just FOUR that they don't - Find out more by contacting me!

Travel Window allows you to get instant rates, compare and book online, directly with great suppliers for best rates on flights, hotels, car rental worldwide (Auto International) and much more. We offer valuable services for the independent traveller who wants quality products, fast and knowledgeable services at the best market rate.

Throughout my professional career I have gained experience in various industries with a focus on Sales and Management. Having worked over 15 years in the Travel & Tourism industry and experience in the recruitment field, I recently started expanding my company and service offerings.

As a focused, quality and service oriented person with an analytic mind I am on track to achieve the planned results and take the necessary strategic decisions to get to the next level.

Born in Germany, I worked and lived in various countries such as France, Switzerland, Greece, Brazil, Bahamas, Kenya and now operate from South Africa. I speak English, French and German and somehow always link up with new people and opportunities. I am always open to suggestions for a new venture or an additional product to promote if it is valuable to my clients.

Making my clients and business relationships happy by exceeding their expectations is my strength and many word-of-mouth clients, job/business opportunities and repeat business speak for itself.
