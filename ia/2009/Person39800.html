Chris Atwood’s Summary

Currently, I am a digital sales specialist with The Times in Shreveport, La. I specifically work in generating additional online revenue, and developing new digital products for the paper. Since starting the position, I have generated more than $30,000 in additional revenue.

I am also the Director of Client Services and a managing partner in CC Communications, a start-up marketing communications firm with an emphasis in design and interactive media. We work with accounts in the Austin and Houston metropolitan areas, and specialize working with new clients who are interested in developing a creative, identifiable brand, and then publicizing it through the Web and other media with a cohesive marketing/advertising communications plan.

I graduated from Texas Tech University with a B.A. in Public Relations with a minor in political science. In the past I have experienced writing for a newspaper with a daily readership of about 20,000 (Daily Toreador at Texas Tech University), creating concepts for multi-faceted campaigns to include not just the public/media relations perspective, but the advertising, marketing and promotions, and computer science/Web site design/structure consultation.
