Patrick Roane’s Summary

My name is Patrick Roane and I am an account executive for Holum and Sons, Inc., manufacturer of high end presentation packaging.

A vignette of my responsibilities:

Developing new business relationships is what I enjoy the most. I like the dynamics of innovation and learning what a company does that makes their product so exciting. I am always developing answers to questions companies have about how they can improve their packaging. The way a company packages their information and or product says a lot. My mission is simple, it is to help you get what you want. Be it a sales kit, a catalog binder or something completely different .. my job is to smooth out the edges and help it really stand out in the market place.

In conclusion, creativity is at the heart of any good and lasting solution. I value team based business solutions and positive customer relationships. Rapport building is essential in today's business world where things can change at a moments notice. When the chips are down, cash is king. This said, I believe a companies ability to remain profitable is based on forging win win relationships. To borrow from Zig Ziggler's play book, "You Can Only Get What You Want, If You Help Enough Other People Get What They Want". In the end, a company's success hinges on it's ability to take risks and embrace change when and where it is needed.
