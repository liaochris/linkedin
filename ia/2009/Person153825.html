Chris Martiniak’s Summary

Product marketing and GTM lead for Yahoo! Web Analytics (www.analytics.yahoo.com). We recently launched v9.5, which provides website owners and marketers with the demographics and psychographics of their website visitors, new marketing reports, advanced data graphing, and many time-saving reporting and agency features.

Jupiter Research and CMS Watch both rate "Yahoo! Web Analytics better than Google Analytics for Enterprises"!

------------------
Experienced Product Marketer and Product Manager for Yahoo's advertising platforms and analytics products. I was the resident go-to product marketing/strategy person for the 2008 Y! Search Marketing ('Panama') roadmap prioritization. In 2007, I authored the MRD outlining the Yahoo! strategy in the analytics space and was involved in the M&A discussions that led to the acquisition of IndexTools in May 2008. Since the acquisition, I was the strategy and marketing lead for Yahoo! Web Analytics, responsible for:

+ setting corporate strategy
+ defining and prioritizing feature enhancements
+ re-branding IndexTools
+ coordinating launches and go-to-market activities

In February, Yahoo! Web Analytics caused a stir when CMS Watch finally declared "Yahoo! Web Analytics leaps ahead of Google Analytics for Enterprise Use”, fulfilling Yahoo!'s marketing strategy.

Other Yahoo! product marketing tasks have included:
+ conducting market research (i.e. social network monetization concept testing, advanced analytics, etc.)
+ developing market requirements
+ financial modeling and prioritizing product enhancements
+ conducting advertiser segmentations
+ building investment cases
+ conducting build/buy/partner recommendations
+ evaluating acquisition candidates
+ re-branding an acquired technology
+ leading go-to-market activities, such as website development, sell sheets, sales training and PR
+ developing a partner network
