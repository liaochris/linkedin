Dave Erickson Fuentes’s Summary

Dave has lived the life of internet/online marketing. He started out as a content writer in one of the largest SEO companies in the Philippines. He gained strong knowledge and sufficient skills in the field of website optimization and pursued the technical aspects of the field.

Having the right tools at hand, he joined another online media company serving clients in Canada and California both in the fields of search engine marketing and online media buying. He adopted new evolving strategies and have studied the field frequently.

Travelling to Dubai, he found himself to be a part of one of the leading web development companies in the region, H2O New Media. He developed and enhanced his skills in web development focusing on Content Management Systems such as Joomla and Wordpress. He was also involved in project management and planning at the initial stage of web development.

Currently, Dave is currently reviving Click-Dubai.Com which will be launched by March 2009. He will be talking about the basic tenets of organic search engine optimization and its importance in the Middle East & GCC markets.
