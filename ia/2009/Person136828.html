Lara Young’s Summary

I am a qualified Human Resources professional, with over 17 years experience across generalist HR, Recruitment, OD, Change Management, Learning and Development and Leadership Development. Most recently, I have been working with global remits at HR Director level. I am a Trainer of Neuro Linguistic Programming and a Trainer of Hypnosis.

In September 2006 I formed my own limited company, Somersault Consulting www.somersault-consulting.com, where I combine NLP Training and Certification, NLP Coach and Master Coach Certification, HR Consultancy, Excecutive Coaching and Facilitation of Business Strategy with my Retreats, Life Coaching and NLP training business
www.designyourfabulousfuture.com.

My career has been mainly focussed within the financial services industry, with experience also gained in the consultancy, public and entertainment sectors.
