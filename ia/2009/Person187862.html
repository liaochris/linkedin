Pieter Oosthuizen, M.Inst.D’s Summary

Pieter is a self-starter, a creative and energetic individual, has a passion for people, and is a team player. He interacts with and functions with ease at all stakeholder levels. He is performance orientated, results driven and a creative and forward thinking Entrepreneur.

Pieter is a succesful Business Management, SAP ERP and Senior Project and Programme Management Professional with hands on experience, covering:

- The set-up and management of effective and efficient re-engineering (People, Process, Technology) projects and programmes for multi-national organisations,

- The development, motivation, implementation and tracking of high value quantifiable business cases (driving large scale business change),

- The setup and management of Project and Programme Management Offices,

- The setup and management of Strategic Information & Business Intelligence competency centres,

- The setup and management of new business ventures (from business case to project to operational phases),

- The consolidation and commercialization of Corporate (Internal) Service Organisations,

- The management and expansion of Internal Service Orgnisations (Including SAP ERP services and consulting organizations),

- The deployment and management of Business Processes and SAP ERP systems to multiple locations (nationally and internationally), and

- The strategic (ICT) management (CIO) of a multinational company.

Pieter presented various papers at International Conferences including papers on E Business as a Development Issue (Commonwealth - Kuala Lampur, Malaysia) and The Role of Information Systems during Negotiated Land Reform Programmes (World Bank - Bogota, Colombia).

He has successfully seen through complex projects in various industries, including mining, beverage, leisure, property, agriculture, fishing, dairy, retail, investment, management consulting and government.
