Vijay Kapoor’s Summary

Total Call Management Solutions (TCMS) has been around for past ten years and have been providing rugged and reliable technology platforms with total of two offices & 40 + employees worldwide.

We offer business telecommunication software, e-Commerce solutions, internet marketing technologies, & related consulting.

Total Call Management Solutions is continually in steps with the emerging critical demands of today’s telecommunications market needs. Our Solutions can be implemented as premises based, hosted, (VOIP) or as hybrid solutions. Range of products we offer are telecommunications, call center software & solutions, predictive dialing, automated voice broadcasting, interactive voice response (IVR), automated call distribution (ACD), skills based routing, call recording & monitoring with other industry specific applications.

We are proudly servicing hundreds of customers with thousands of port installations. We are proud vendor for US Federal and State Governments, US District Courts, USA Navy and hundreds of other corporations and organizations spread all across globe.Our prospects are seeking to reduce operational overheads up to 80% by automating their business processes.

Total Call Management Solutions philosophy is simple – to provide turnkey solutions and services. “When it comes down to it, support and service is the foundation of Total Call Management Solutions”.
