Alexandre Mensi’s Summary

My name is Alexandre Mensi. I am a passionate web application architect with over 8 years of experience. I'm from Montreal, Canada and I currently work has an independent consultant for large corporation in Canada.

I adopted a motto from a renowned professor from who I've learned object oriented development and analysis and I try to apply it at my work on a daily basis.

This motto is very simple: Do the Right Thing, Do the Thing Right!

Finally, I have a degree in networking and telecommunication and I am completing a bachelor degree in information technology engineering at l'École de technologie supérieure(ETS) in Montreal. I'm also a member of the Agile Alliance and the International Association of Software Architects IASA).

I'm very involved in the .Net community, especially in Montreal and online. You can find me on http://asp.net under the alias "amensi". I also translate Scott Gutherie`s blog in french at http://weblogs.asp.net/scottgufrench.

Finally I’m certified ScrumMaster and I was nominated for the Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP) for ASP.NET... Still waiting!!
