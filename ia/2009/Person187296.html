Phill Robinson’s Summary

Spanning 20 years, Phill's experience in the Enterprise Software industry demonstrates an ability to identify winning companies, new technology trends, and opportunities before they emerge.

Phill has worked for some of the most successful software companies at their early stages of development in Europe, as well as working at a Strategic level in the US. He has been part of the management teams for Oracle, Sybase, Siebel and salesforce.com, where he was Chief Marketing Officer and subsequently Chief Operating Officer - Sales & Distribution based in San Francisco

Phill has performed roles that include Sales, Marketing, Engineering, Product Management, Business Development and Alliances.

Since his return from living in the US in July 2007 Phill has been operating out of the UK, as CEO of Velocix, and a Non-Exec Board Member of Tideway Systems.

In January 2009 Phill joined Iris Software, the UKs largest privately held software group, as Managing Director of APS. Iris is the market leading provider of Enterprise Application Software and Services to Professional Industrys such as ; Accountancy, Legal, Health Care, Charities and NFPs.
