William H. Wilkins III’s Summary

Born in Brookline, Massachusetts, Bill is the eldest son of the late Bill Wilkins Jr, the award winning newscast director at KGO (ABC) in San Francisco from 1968 to 1984, and Bill is a nephew of one of the founders of Syncom Funds.

In the late 80's Bill worked at KROQ in Los Angeles under the infamous programming genius Rick Carroll, then at KSRF-FM and KIEV-AM in sales, followed by New World Pictures in the foreign marketing division. After starting and selling a small radio syndication firm, Bill moved to San Francisco and entered the booming Internet industry. After working at such companies as Metrocom, Internet.com, AdKnowledge, and the iTraffic Interactive Agency, Bill founded Wilkins Media Holdings LLC, a private holding company for the initiation of integrative entrepreneurial projects in entertainment, internet, and virtual communications.

William H. Wilkins III’s Specialties:

Entrepreneurial capitalization and development of integrated Internet and virtual communications properties. Entertainment capitalization and executive production management.

( Please Note: Wilkins Media Holdings LLC, a private asset holding company, has no affiliation with Wilkins Media Company, an Atlanta based advertising agency specializing in the 'Out Of Home' segments. )
