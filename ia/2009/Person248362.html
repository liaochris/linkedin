Tara Cousineau, PhD’s Summary

Tara Cousineau, founder of Women Insight, Inc., brings together years of expertise in eHealth program development and clinical health psychology. Her expertise includes writing, research, and conceptual development for eHealth products, publications or services. Consultative services include helping businesses and organizations to identify and formulate health communication strategies to reach women and families.

Dr. Cousineau has received numerous grants over her career from the National Institutes of Health, Small Business Innovative Research program. Her work has been recognized in peer-reviewed journals and prize paper awards. Her most recent project is www.bodimojo.com, an online health destination for teens.

She received her doctorate in 1996 in clinical psychology from the Derner Institute of Advanced Psychological Studies at Adelphi University, New York. She holds a clinical appointments in psychiatry and OB/GYN at BIDMC/Harvard Medical School. She is a part-time staff psychologist at the Domar Center for Mind/Body Health, Boston IVF, Waltham, MA.

Tara Cousineau, PhD’s Specialties:

Web-based health tools and content strategy; eHealth product development and online health communications; focus groups, program planning and evaluation.
