Ryan B. King’s Summary

Everyday we are all exposed to thousands of advertisements. Seldom do we remember most of them. Even less likely we want to remember them. Through my masters degree and my thesis I've researched advertising that creates positive experiences. Consumers are more educated than ever. They are also more connected than ever before. Their ability to find and share information has become something dreamed up by science fiction.

Multi-platform campaigns have become the norm, and mobile phones are the mortar of these campaign. Campaigns with branded media are are reaching new heights while they encourage sharing and interaction.This is where I am taking my career. And, I'm currently making my way from graduate school to professional ad man. I love talking to any one who is interested in what can do for them, and I'd love to talk to you.
