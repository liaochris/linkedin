Elizabeth Brooks’s Summary

Senior-level experience in marketing, brand development and company strategy, derived from a background which includes the original Napster, management roles at startups covering consumer Internet, digital media/entertainment technologies, mobile, and ecommerce, and senior poistions in the music industry. Unusual skill set in content, digital, and brand creation
As head of marketing at Napster, participated firsthand in the digital entertainment revolution, managing the fastest-growing application of its kind in Internet history. Executed several successful rebrands for companies in varying spaces, garnered enormous positive media coverage for companies and clients, and worked with investors to develop brand awareness for fledgling technologies.
Keenly interested in technologies and ideas that change consumer behaviors and cause us to look at the world in a different way.

VP Marketing (original and only) and founding management team member at Napster; senior positions at Sony, Buy.com, Bertelsmann, GoTV. Successful campaigns developed for everyone from P&G, Nintendo, and Warner Bros. Pictures to System of a Down and independent retail brands.

Elizabeth Brooks’s Specialties:

Senior-level (EVP/GM/C-level) team manager. ROI-driven and creative. Adept at branding and developing/clarifying messaging. Excellent when interfacing with tech teams. Global work and development experience.
