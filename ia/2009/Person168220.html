Paula Morris’s Summary

I have 26 years of experience in intellectual property law. The first 13 years included intellectual property litigation and patent and trademark preparation and prosecution. The last 13 years have involved primarily patent and trademark preparation and prosecution and opinion work. More recently, I have become involved with equity licensing as an alternative to franchising.

Experience includes patent preparation and prosecution in the areas of chemical compositions and methods, materials, chemical and physical vapor deposition techniques (including fuel cell electrodes), drilling fluids, Fischer-Tropsch products and their uses, fuels, drilling equipment, catalysts, and mechanical devices, including medical devices. Creation and perfection of security interests in intellectual property, and (more recently) "equity licensing" arrangements.
