Lyn Andrews’s Summary

Lyn is an innovative, strategic and results driven executive with thirty years of increasing responsibility in sales, marketing and general management leadership positions for traditional and new media businesses. She has worked at companies which are known as the best brands in their category of business: CBS Television Network, ABC Radio Networks, WebMD, The Weather Channel, weather.com, Boomerang Mobile Media and TheHealthCentral Network.

She is experienced in meeting the strategic, operational and cultural challenges of both mature, early stage and start-up companies. She has has been successful as both an intratrepenuer and an entrepeneur. As a business leader, she has consistently increased revenues, profits, market share and organizational performance, built and repositioned brands and driven product and programming innovation. P&L responsibility for $300 million in revenue and $80 million in operating income.

During her career, Lyn has been a pioneer, a change-agent and an innovator. She has been eager to take on new challenges, create departments or companies from scratch, turn-around undervalued brands and be the voice of change when the status-quo no longer can meets the needs of a rapidly changing marketplace or new forms of competition..

She brings energy, imagination and commitment to her work and her life. She is unique in her ability to translate how new media is reshaping the world.

Lyn is intuitive, creative, articulate, inspiring and honest. She is also generous of spirit and of the highest intregity. She has been a mentor to men and woman during her entire career, created and developed strategic alliances with marketers, on-air radio talent, advertising agencies, affiliates and consumers, and been selected to serve on numerous advisory boards because of the insights and experience she can bring to different businesses.
