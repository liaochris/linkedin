Kyle Rollins’s Summary

My career in technology started while working with student's with disabilities at The University of Georgia's Adaptive Technology Lab. I was exposed to a wide range of customized pieces of hardware and software while getting my degree in Management from UGA's Terry College of Business. Upon graduation I moved to London and began work at Korn Ferry International on their helpdesk where I gained invaluable experience supporting and implementing a scalable corporate infrastructure. These experiences gave me the foundation necessary to successfully lead the core business at Conosco - delivering corporate style IT to small businesses - a sort of Geek Squad in the UK. As the head of Operations I managed three teams - Helpdesk, Engineering & Account Mgmt. I left Conosco to become Director at Liveline, a full-service interactive marketing consultancy focusing on assisting traditional media agencies who work with Fortune 500 brands and are looking to add digital services to their portfolio. I worked closely with top agency executives to develop integrated marketing plans with digital at the heart of the campaign, teaching them and their staff how to use online tools to leverage the entire campaign, giving them the experience required to develop new practices, creating a competitive advantage and sustainable revenue streams through new services their clients were already demanding.

Most recently I've started working with Atlanta-based mobile application designer Jen Gordon to advise clients on best practices using social media tools to promote the apps we create for their brand. I enjoy teaching our clients new and interesting ways to engage with their customers through mobile applications and social media sites like Facebook and LinkedIn. We are currently developing a mobile application community to explore ways to leverage these new channels for our customers. If you have any questions about new ways to engage your customers, please feel free to contact me!
