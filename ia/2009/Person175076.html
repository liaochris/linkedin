Nicole Verbeeck’s Summary

Was it Albert Einstein who said, "The true sign of intelligence is not knowledge but imagination"? I guess Pablo Picasso believed that "Everything you can imagine is real," and I partially believe what Henry Ford encouraged us to think "Anybody can do anything that he imagines."

Well, I believe imagination can take you far, in communications, or in life. So much was created in this world through imagination including what Einstein did in his lifetime or Mozart.

Knowledge is only good if you have the imagination to do something great with it!! I like to combine knowledge, experience and imagination to generate ideas and transform them into a myriad of communication solutions to create value and to support the long-term strategic goal of an organization.
