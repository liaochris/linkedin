MD Launchpad’s Summary

Medical Device Launchpad, LLC (MDL) provides consulting services to help medical device & diagnostic inventors protect, prepare & present their ideas to industry, for potential sale or license opportunities. In addition by working with MDL, you as the inventor would have an agent that also acts as a "buffer" between you (a health care provider/inventor) and the companies with whom you negotiate, thus helping you comply with all current and potentially future federal and/or state regulations regarding transparency in relationships with industry.
