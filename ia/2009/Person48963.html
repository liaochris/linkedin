Christina’s experiences have included but are not limited to supporting a wide variety of industries: healthcare, consumer durables, consumer package goods (CPG), manufacturing/industrial, financial services, high technology, professional consulting, manufacturing, logistics, insurance, accounting/consulting, advertising, building services, construction, legal, pharmaceutical, not-for-profit and search.

Christina’s human capital experiences have been heavily focused in start-up and turnaround project work. Her experiences include executive search as well as management responsibilities in the areas of staffing, Internet recruitment, process improvement/reengineering/change management, employment branding, retention, technology and HRe, diversity, university relations, policy and procedure development, employee relations, affirmative action, organizational development and management education.

Christina’s staffing experiences include recruitment of executive and staff professionals ranging in base salary from $750,000 to entry level and supplemental staff roles (c-level, executive, staff level, entry level, college recruits, supplemental staffing, etc).

Christina prides herself on her dedication to customer service, drive for results and her ability to accomplish strategic business objectives through the use of creative tactics.
