Melody Beattie’s Summary

Bestselling author drawing on personal and professional experiences has written 17 nonfiction books (self-help genre). Selling in 20 countries, my books have now sold over eight million copies. Have also worked for two daily newspapers, and optioned one teleplay. Now, I'm switching to screenplays and teleplays -- true stories to help people make sense of their lives. Sensitive to the most unusual stories; I've dealt with psychopaths, sociopaths, and middle-class "normal" people. My goals are to write prolifically the rest of my life and assist grieving people and others on my websites.

Melody Beattie’s Specialties:

Skilled with sensitive issues or unusual circumstances that might overwhelm others. Comfortable in any situation requiring therapy skills to pull out or understand story. Strong points include sense of humor, knowing structure, following editorial guidelines, writing to required length, slanting to editorial input, editing, and incorporating notes. Able to help people relax and want to tell their stories. Strong intuition, can sense lies. Can be trusted with off-the-record details.
