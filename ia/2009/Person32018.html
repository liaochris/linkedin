Brandi Ratliff’s Summary

*Corporate Recruiter for AMN Healthcare working to bring top talent to each division of our company: Platinum Select, Med Travelers, RN Demand, Staff Care and Merritt Hawkins.

I have worked as a staffing consultant/account executive/assistant branch manager over the past 9 years and am looking to share my expertise with recent college grads looking to get started with an exciting career on up to seasoned sales professionals looking to make a career change to the healthcare industry.

I have staffed through various waves in the economy. Clients ranging from .com, high tech, event planning, mortgage, corporate accounting, and healthcare.
There has been a recession every 10 years for a very long time, 1981, 1991, 2001 and another one due in about 1-2 years from now.

The three different positions that we usually hire for at AMN are as follows:

Territory Sales Representative
Recruiting Consultant
Account Representative

The Merritt Hawkins division does require 20-30% travel and will be the only division conducting a retained search for permanent placement of physicians nationwide.

Please send your resume if you are a highly motivated, aggressive sales professional looking to make a career change into the healthcare staffing industry.

I look forward to assisting you with the start of an exciting new career path!!! brandi.ratliff@mhagroup.com
