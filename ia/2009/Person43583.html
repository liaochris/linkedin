Chris Laffra’s Summary

I developed various tools to monitor and analyse performance of Eclipse applications. Java classloading techniques: I designed ways to make Eclipse start 35% faster and to reduce memory consumption by 10-25%. I co-authored the Official Eclipse 3.0 FAQs. My motto while developing software is Simplify Everything. I managed a team to improve performance of the entire product of RAD/RSA v7. I am a technology sponge. I give tips on the proper use of new technologies, such as Ajax. I currently am leading a team that adds support for web2.0, Ajax, and rich user interfaces to IBM's strategic business development language called EGL, see:
http://www.ibm.com/developerworks/rational/products/egl.

See also my home page: http://laffra.com/chris.aspx

Chris Laffra’s Specialties:

Computer language design and implementation. Compiler design. Visualization. Profilers. IDE development. Eclipse. Technical writing. Technology Evaluation. Seeing the Bigger Picture. Public Speaking. Java. Performance. EGL. Web2.0. Ajax. Javascript. Out of the Box Thinking.
