Nelson Lewis’s Summary

I am a 30-year veteran leader of innovative communications and services with global experience with principled leadership in executive-level and senior operating positions in technology and communications companies that are enabling transformation around the world. I have successfully migrated from the days of “pirate” satellite TV into the beginnings of competitive long distance to the most sophisticated cryptographically secure global IP, wireless and content driven cross-platform networks, information systems, and technology of today. I have started, grown and sold several businesses over the years. As a consultant, I have conceived and implemented business plans, solved complex problems, boosted operating performance, created collaborations and joint ventures, and maximized value for stakeholders with accountability for delivering results up and down the balance sheet, for some of the finest telecom and broadband carriers, enterprises, and Governments throughout the world, from the Silk Roads to the pre-Columbian sites of Central and South America to the jungles and mountains of Asia and the deserts of Africa. I was honoured to serve as a country representative to the ITU and Intelsat. I have experience working through the labyrinth of issues of financially distressed situations including tactical and structural cost reductions, out-of-court restructurings, insolvency management, asset valuation and disposition, complex asset misappropriation, preference and fraudulent conveyance actions, and security realization involving a multiplicity of industries and complex situations with difficult cross-border insolvency matters on behalf of debtors, creditors, strategic investors, the Courts and regulators.
