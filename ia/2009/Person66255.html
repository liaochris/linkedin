Dotty Ewing’s Summary

Accomplished and entrepreneurial executive with specific expertise and successes in new product introductions: identifying objectives, strategy, creative planning, and building teams. Background spans 15+ years in high profile consumer, business-to-business and start-up marketing. Experience includes entertainment businesses as diverse as broadband TV, cable networks, interactive TV, online marketing, website development, mobile tv, cable operations marketing, and consumer electronics, as well as packaged goods. Strengths include leadership to success, based on accurate marketplace analysis, translating objectives to profitable plans, ROI-based marketing, award-winning creative, drive to results and motivating winning teams. Energetic, enthused and focused.
