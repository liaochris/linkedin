Dane Lee’s Summary

Dane A. Lee is the Vice President, Business Segment Leader and manager of Wells Fargo’s Kansas City Corporate Trust Services office. He has been a leader in the industry since 1989. In addition to chairing the Bond Buyer’s North Central Public Finance Forum, he has also presented at numerous other industry forums and meetings. Dane is known for his innovative solutions and strong relationships with many of the region’s largest issuers of municipal debt, currently managing a portolio in excess of $8 billion. He earned his B.S. from the University of Kansas and his M.B.A. from Baker University with a published thesis concerning trustee’s environmental liability under “Superfund” legislation. While at the university of Kansas, Dane played outside linebacker on the Jayhawk football team. Currently, Dane is President of the City Council in Fairway, Kansas,. Dane has obtained the highest professional designation for trust administrators, Certified Corporate Trust Specialist, and is a two-time winner of Wells Fargo’s Management Excellence Award.
