Gino Gonzales’s Summary

For the last 9 years I have been working in the IT staffing industry and have helped individuals find permanent, contract and contract-to-hire positions across a range of roles at small, mid-sized and Fortune 500 companies.

Whether it’s an entry level technical support role, Systems/Network Administrator or Engineer, a Java/J2EE software developer or PMO Director, I try to focus on the same approach, I work closely with both the candidate and client to fully understand their needs and focus on bringing together the best fit, in turn creating a mutually beneficial working relationship.
