Doug Abbott’s Summary

Embedded software engineer with extensive experience at the operating system and hardware/software interface levels. Author of three books, "PCI Bus Demystified," "Linux for Embedded and Real-time Applications", and "Embedded Linux Development using Eclipse." I teach 3- to 5-day seminars on various aspects of Linux, as well as other operating systems, for embedded applications. These seminars are offered publicly and can be customized for in-house presentation.

Doug Abbott’s Specialties:

Extensive knowledge of both Linux and MicroC/OS.
