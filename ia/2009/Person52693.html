Dana Sol’s Summary

Head of HD Division of MiCasa Multimedia. At HDVision I helped to develope the HD work flow in 2005. Beta tested software for Cineform - Compression Codec. Beta tested Wafian - Direct Disk Recorder. Currently Producing and Editing a HD Television series "Tall Ships", a 13-episode series on American Tall Ships sailing today.

Past experience includes IT for PC computers. Recording Engineer using several DAW, including Pro tools, Nuendo, Live, Cubase. First DAW a Commodore computer & Atari using Cubase in mid 80's. Classicly trained coloratura soprano, Jazz/Blues and Gospel singer. Artist management. Organized fund raising concert events. Studied Jazz & Modern dance. Started the first break dancing group in Tucson, Az, called "Chain Reaction" Dance Director and Choreography for Arthur Murray's Dance Studio. Also an ordained minister working with non-profit organizations and hospitals providing support for people living with HIV/ AIDS-related issues.

My goal is to make a difference in what ever I do. Love myself, my family, my friends to the best of my ability. Record an album of some original music, negro spirituals, dance music. Also to create amazing content through technology that can change the world.
