Peter Forint’s Summary

Peter is a business coach and advisor serving the owners and managers of small and medium-sized companies. He works with his clients to create the business systems and company cultures that support manageable growth, leading to higher profits and lower stress.

Previous to joining Wardell he was the entrepreneurial manager of several small software companies. He has launched and managed several products and services in Canada, the United States and Europe for companies ranging in size from Fortune 500 to three-person startup. Peter started his career in major account sales at NCR and IBM.

As a strong supporter of green energy and sustainable development, he has written articles for Private Power Magazine and is a member of the Canadian Wind Energy Association where he has served on their small wind committee. He also volunteers with Big Brothers and Sisters of Ajax-Pickering, and coaches with the Pickering Baseball Association.

Peter holds a BA from University of Toronto and an MBA from the DeGroote School of Business at McMaster University.
