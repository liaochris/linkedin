Darryl Groves’s Summary

Corporate Manager for Nestle Company the biggest food company in the world for almost 30 years. Owned and operated commercial real estate for almost 15 years. Founded and operated a Bible College and Bible Institute for 14 years. Founded and Pastored a Church for 12 years. All done concurrently.

Primary skills include Action Planning, Time Management, Team Creation and Team Building, Training, Motivating, Problem solving and problem prevention. In addition have served on a number of non profit and for profit boards and planning groups. Presently continue to operate own business for more than 20 years. Now providing business consulting, career counseling and personal development coaching.

One goal is to help individuals to uncover their core passion and turn it into a money making business or venture. Another goal is to help people start and own their own business and to help existing businesses to hyper grow. Another goal is for me to provide more board of directors experience to existing companies looking for people with multi year experience with a large multi national company. Another goal is to help businesses and universities to assist their employees and clients to work in their core passion for the company and be in the right position doing the right work.
