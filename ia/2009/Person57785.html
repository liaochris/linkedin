David Ian Gray’s Summary

With a 20-year background in finance, market research, strategic planning and sustainability, David Ian Gray has worked with a variety of leading organizations to foster better decisions in times of change. Combining fact-based counsel with his unique thinking, he helps organizations become more relevant to the right target audiences and connect deeply to them.

David is also a recognized expert on shopper trends and retail strategies, producing high-impact business intelligence and strategic studies for and about the retail sector.

A thirst for knowledge drives him, as does the belief that insight is a catalyst for innovation. David's curiosity and desire to find better business practices has kept him consistently ahead of the curve on many issues.

David Ian Gray’s Specialties:

High impact strategic studies, marketing research, business intelligence, information management, workshops, facilitation, and stakeholder engagement. Recognized expertize on shopper trends, customer experience and market-facing retail strategies.
