Abigail Gray’s Summary

After leaving the Publishing Industry (end of April 2008) I decided it was time to strike out on my own! Combining my years of Event, Advertising, and Management experience; I decided to open a retail venue that is focused on the community in which I live with my family. "Olivia Browning" is named after our two daughters, Alice and Maggie. It is a retail storefront that brings a much needed element to the Charlestown Community -- and the rest of the country through the Internet site and corporate relationships.

We cater to every customer and always carry well made, high quality, well priced, diverse merchandise.Customer Service is out number one priority - everyday.

Abigail Gray’s Specialties:

Table Top, Women's and Men's Accessories, Children's toys, books, clothing, games, lots for "tweens", Stationery, Gourmet. Wedding Registry, Baby Registry. Custom Gifts Baskets, Custom Corporate Gifts (i.e. American Red Cross "Go Red" Ball), and Events (weddings, baby showers, Fundraisers, Corporate Parties, Children's Birthdays, Private Dinner Parties, etc.).
