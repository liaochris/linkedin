Bruce Holmes’s Summary

Senior executive with more than 33 years experience in the Federal government (National Aeronautics and Space Administration). Recent experience in entrepreneurial startups in air transportation innovations (DayJet Corporation) and in information technologies and services (DayJet Technologies, LLC). Leadership in national air transportation system strategies; large scale public-private partnerships in strategic aeronautics technology development; state-level air transportation strategies; and national airspace modernization. Service in advising industry, states, universities, and the federal government in technology system thinking. Long-term drive in advancing personal air mobility systems, including aircraft, airspace, and airport systems.

Bruce Holmes’s Specialties:

Aerospace, aeronautics, transportation technology, strategy, and partnerships, aerodynamics, flight test, and innovation leadership.
