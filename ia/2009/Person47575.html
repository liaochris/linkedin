Chris Jordan’s Summary

After graduating from Auburn University in 2003, I moved to Metro Atlanta and began working with Nielsen Media Research, both collecting and analyzing data. Working with the largest marketing research firm in the world gave me the skill set that allows me to excel today. The experience I gained in both understanding market research and developing innovative marketing strategies has allowed me to be successful in my own business and also assist others in their businesses.

I made the decision to leave Nielsen Media Research in 2007 to pursue new challenges as an insurance agent. I quickly found success at Liberty Mutual and in early 2009, I made the decision to start my own agency - Atlanta Insurance LIVE!

Being a business owner requires careful planning and fast decision making. My innovative approach to insurance sales has earned me recognition by Intuit and CBS Atlanta. I have also been a guest on various internet-based media outlets.

Along with running a successful insurance agency, I recently began consulting with small businesses on their internet marketing strategies - saving them time and money, along with increasing the effectiveness of their message. Print media is leaving us folks... how will you adapt?

My 5 year goal is is to grow Atlanta Insurance LIVE! into a regional agency and completely revolutionize the industry using new media technology.

My big personal goal: to own property on the island of Kauai

Chris Jordan’s Specialties:

Operations management, sales, sales training, market research, web development, efficiency, and the hustle!
