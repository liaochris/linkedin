Brian Swichkow’s Summary

A little about me, I recently graduated from Champlain College in Burlington, Vermont with a degree in Multimedia and Graphic design. All throughout college I was interested in the marketing side of design and how a brand effectively communicated with their clients/customers. It is because of this interest in the message that I continue to study and search for examples and uses of marketing to connect with consumers.

Throughout my college years it is this fascination also led me to become deeply embedded within social networks. I would not only participate in communications, but research how they are made and what tools are being used that make the message so effective.

It is because of my personality that keeps pushing my to answer the question “why?” that I have started my business. Swichkow.com offers both consulting and full-service social media services to businesses both small and large. Contact me and we can talk about developing your online personality that is being shown to consumers. Using social networks allows for your business to bring consumers to your website on their own terms. In today’s society people want to have choices and marketing that is pushed into the consumer’s lifestyle entices them to push back against you. At Swichkow.com we help people know who you are before they’ve even met you.

Another service that I offer at Swichkow.com is text message marketing. This service is completely customizable to your business and comes in many flavors such as: mobile couponing, text-to-win, and appointment reminders. Test message marketing helps to build a base of customers that are interested in a specific product or service and send them updating messages that are targeted at their interests.

Please contact me for a formal or informal business meeting where I can learn about you business, you can learn about mine, and we can learn how to help you.
