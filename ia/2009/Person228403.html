Tadd Huntington’s Summary

Hello, this is Tadd Huntington, owner of SecurityAlarmNow.com.

We are looking for a person to be part of our growing online sales and marketing company. We are ready for someone who wants to be an important part of an INNOVATIVE team that is accomplishing great things. In addition to SecurityAlarmNow.com, we will be growing additional projects. We realize to do this we will need to keep building a team of ambitious, results-oriented leaders. Every person that joins our organization has a high contributing factor to the overall success of the company.

We are not seeking someone with experience in selling alarm systems nor are we seeking a future manager to boss around a bunch of subordinates. Most companies that sell home alarm systems run their company like a "shop." We are a dynamic online marketing company that sells products entirely on the web or by telephone. We want you to be passionate about using our products. However, we want you to be even more passionate about how to educate, create value, and systematically deliver our products more effectively, which give our customers a better overall experience.

We have a motto for success that we follow: “Work smart before you work hard."

Our environment is casual yet fast paced. Apply today for our Inside Sales Representative position if you feel that you can leverage your talents in our dynamic organization.

COME JOIN OUR TEAM NOW! Send your resume to taddhuntington@securityalarmnow.com and make sure to include a cover letter explaining why you are an ideal fit for this opportunity.
