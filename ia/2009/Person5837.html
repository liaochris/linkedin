Akira Shibata’s Education

  • University of London

    Ph.D. , Experimental Particle Physics , 2004 — 2007

    Thesis: “Investigation of Electroweak Production of the Top Quark at the LHC”

    I developed computational techniques for measuring the mysterious properties of the heaviest known fundamental particle, the top quark. They were produced from high energy collisions of accelerated particles. They will teach us about the condition of the early universe, within a microsecond of its creation.

    The work is important because I applied a quantitative analysis strategy based on Monte Carlo simulation techniques to measure spin of the top quark for the first time in my collaboration. I have also developed the software tools that have drastically improved the efficiency of analyses within a large collaboration.

    The project was funded by awards (see honors and awards). [see publication "PhD Thesis"]
