Marissa Beck, MS, RD’s Summary

Marissa Beck has a passion for fitness and nutrition that is unsurpassed. She is currently the Director of Wellness at NextJump, a company that targets online advertising for corporations by linking merchants with consumers. There, she is developing the wellness program, creating nutrition tools for their Virtual Health Fair, and being a big ball of energy!

Most recently she was a nutrition educator for Harlem RBI, a unique youth development organization, where she developed an after-school nutrition education program that is currently being implemented at affiliated schools. While studying at Columbia University, she was the Editor of the nutrition newsletter, The Grapevine, where she published articles and reviewed editorial as well as and participated in social networking events with alumni and faculty. She has also done freelance writing for numerous online diet and nutrition publications.

Marissa received her BA from Tufts University in English and Art History and Master of Science in Applied Physiology and Nutrition from Columbia University. She is also a certified personal trainer and former collegiate athlete.
