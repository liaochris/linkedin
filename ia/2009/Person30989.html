Elizabeth Boone’s Summary

An experienced marketing professional who's just as excited about this business as the day she started. It's such an exciting time to be a part of this crazy business. From social media, to social responsibility, to transparency, to the economic meltdown, to consumers longing for meaningful experiences and authenticity... "Truth well told" is still a powerful guiding principle (I never worked at McCann, but always liked the line). All in all, I'm just a suit who's trying to make a difference. I'm proud of the contributions I made to help revitalize Cadillac. It was rewarding to play a role in helping Ford experience its first car market share growth in years. I enjoyed being a part of Mazda's ascent to the top as the leading automotive growth brand in 2007. Currently, I'm proud to be a part of the winning team at Hyundai. What we're doing at Hyundai proves that all it takes to succeed is a great product, a great insight, a great idea, flawless execution and a decent media budget. This business really is quite simple. After twenty years, I still have a passion for it, this craft, and I look forward to the next twenty years.

Elizabeth Boone’s Specialties:

Motivating people, inspiring creatives, building brands, harvesting/identifying consumer insights, leveraging insights to develop innovative ideas that connect with consumers and tell a brand's story. Keeping it fun.
