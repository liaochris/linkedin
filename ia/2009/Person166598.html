We have two beautiful children who keep us young and active. We both have an intimate knowledge of the valley of the sun and a passion for real estate with a strong desire to watch people fulfill their dreams of buying a home. Whatever your real estate needs are, "Put the Power of Teamwork behind you."

With us, we are two full-time REALTORS® that give more than full time effort. Welcome to your portal into an Arizona Lifestyle. As members of our local the Scottsdale Area Association of Realtors®, Arizona Assoication of Realtors®, and the National Association of Realtors®, we work closely with Buyers and Sellers serving Chandler, Gilbert, Tempe, City of Mesa, Fountain Hills, Scottsdale, Phoenix, Queen Creek, Ahwatukee, and Maricopa.

When looking to Buy or Sell a home in the Phoenix metro area, hire a Realtor®, like ourselves who want to win your business and trust.
