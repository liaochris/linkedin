Fabian Lange’s Summary

My work within such global companies like Hewlett Packard and Nokia especially supported me in gaining the required skills, hard and especially as well soft skills to effectively participate in and lead commercial software development.
During my work in Nokia I got involved in a Multisite development of a Middleware product. Using Agile processes and daily builds with automated testing and doing model driven development helped in coping with 200 developers around the globe. I do believe agile is the appropriate process model to solve todays software development issues. I am certified Scrum Master. Using state-of-the art techniques as model driven development and automatic testing enables the creation of higher quality complex software. I was responsible for defining the architecture of a Web Framework and involved in joint design and architecture of a new network management platform of Cisco Systems and Nokia Siemens Networks.

My current employer codecentric is a midsize company specialized in agile Java development and Performance consulting. It is very refreshing to work in such a small and energetic company, which values every individual with its skills and has a friendly work environment. We try to do a 20% off-work time assignment to conferences open source development and continuous learning.

I myself drive for continuous learning which reflects in my certification history: I am...
-Sun certified Java programmer
-Sun certified Java developer
-Sun certified web component developer
-Sun certified business component developer
-SpringSource certified professional for SpringFramework 2.5

I am also Zend certified Engineer for PHP5, as I also deal with projects demanding a smaller footprint than Java. In my free time I am developing and using the symfony framework to create a PHP 5 bases community site.
I am always trying to get always a grasp on new Languages and Frameworks, because you never can know when these tools might come in handy.
