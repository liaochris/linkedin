Eduardo Farinelli’s Summary

Eduardo Farinelli (BIO)


Eduardo Farinelli has 13 years of experience in developing, supporting, managing and selling IT related services & solutions for global accounts and carriers. He has a Bachelor's degree from The University of Tennessee with a major in Business Administration / Marketing and a minor in Finance and a MBA in Business Management at FGV in Sao Paulo, Brazil.

He has joined AT&T, in 2004, as a Regional Account Director in the CALA Region, responsible for developing sales of complex managed services and network outsourcing solutions for Global Accounts. In 2005 Eduardo Farinelli was promoted to AT&T Brazil Sales Director and was responsible for growing the sales team, increase market demand and wallet share for AT&T services in the Latin America region. Eduardo is currently the Sales Vice President for South America.

Prior to joining AT&T, Eduardo Farinelli worked as a Multinational Sales Manager at Lucent Technologies / Avaya Communication for 5 years, responsible for selling voice and data solutions to a group of selected global accounts located in Brazil and South America. In addition, he has worked as a Sales Director for TeleTech Brazil (TeleTech is a leading full-service provider of strategic customer care solutions), responsible for every aspect of the sales cycle. Eduardo Farinelli has also held sales and management positions at SupportComm (a Brazilian voice and data portal technology company) and Asia Express (Trading company) in New Delhi, India.

Eduardo Farinelli has acquired a fluency in English by studying, living, and working in the USA for 09 years and in India for 01 year.

Eduardo Farinelli is a 37 year old Brazilian and Italian citizen and is single.
