Anthony Etherton’s Summary

Since leaving higher education I've held management / leadership positions in several different industries including hospitality, wholesale, advertising sales management, business journalism and event management.

I re-trained as an actor to BA level and after a career of 10 years, I began combining my total experience in 2003. Develop my skills as a leadership communication facilitator / coach, my work has developed from room based 'training' to experiential leadership behaviour challenges.

The Courageous Communication Partnership creates and facilitates real world, real time 'experiences/scenarios' and the delegates take on responsibility for their own learning. You can't get this from a book... yet.

Anthony Etherton’s Specialties:

Helping leaders consciously create the 'widget moving' impact they choose.

Anthony Etherton’s Experience

  • Courageous Communication Partnership Ltd

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2008 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    CCP is an organisation of like-minded leadership coach/facilitators who've been attracted by a belief in experiential development for leaders. Classrooms, pie charts and Venn diagrams have their place and so does PowerPoint, but that place isn't CCP. When you've read all the books you can stomach and sat through all the theories you can fill you head with, come to us and we'll challenge you to turn all those words into behaviours.

  • Impact Factory

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2003 — Present (6 years 4 months)

    Impact Factory is unique in it's approach to soft skills development. It was established almost 20 years ago by an actor and psycotherapist and draws on both disciplines to inform the way we think communication happens between people. The acting discipline informs the physical aspects of communication and the physcotheraputic element is concerned with the inner 'voices' that can stop us from being as effective as we can be. My work at IF includes creating workshops for every kind of business-based interpersonal communication challenge you can think of. From Presentation skills and customer service to team development and Personal Impact.

It's prime objective was to supply actors to industry to help teams re create difficult scenarios and, working together with the actors, to explore those difficulties to create different outcomes. The business also used the its resource of 'talent' to create corporate theatrically based events. for product or new service launches.
