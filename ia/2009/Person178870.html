Onkar Sharma’s Summary

I am into the 4th semester of my graduation course and till now has a experience of 4 paper presentations at various national and international conferences. The papers are mostly concerned with Intellectual Property Laws. Also I had an experience of working with various persons from the legal industry during my vacations. I am also into writing essays and articles on current topics of legal interest and is also on the editorial board of the GNLU e journal. Presently I am a member of the internship & placement committee of my college. I am highly interested in IP laws and has also completed a course on Intellectual Property Laws offered by WIPO. In future I wish to work with a top notch Firm or a Company or Organization dealing in IP laws.


The various presentations by me in a chronological order:

1. Presented paper at “National Seminar on Redefining the role of women: challenges and remedies” organized by National Law University Jodhpur in collaboration the Indian Council of Social Science Research, New Delhi, held between 14th-15th 2007, on the topic, “Paradigm Shift: Role of Women in the changing Society”
2. Presented a paper in 2nd International Conference on Management-Globalization: Changes and opportunities, at Icfai Business School, Ahmedabad held on 27th-29th December on the topic “Trademark Protection in the Present IPR Regime”
3. Presented a paper at 31st Criminology Conference, conducted by Indian Society Of Criminology at National Law Institute University, Bhopal held to be on 18th-20th January,2008 on the Topic, “Emerging Patterns of Cyber Crime-Intellectual Property in Cyberspace-A Trademark perspective”
4. Presented a poster at National Conference on Intellectual Property Rights, conducted by Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, held between 28th February- 1st March, 2008 on the topic: “Is My Gene My Own? Gene Patenting and Enforcement Mechanisms.”

Onkar Sharma’s Specialties:

Intellectual Property Laws, Criminal Laws especially relating to Cyber Crime, Corporate laws especially relating to Public Private Partnerships and Retail Industry and Environmental Laws.
