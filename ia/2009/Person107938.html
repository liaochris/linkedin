Jefferson Spady’s Summary

Background:
Award-winning Producer/Director & Director of Photography, always looking for exciting & challenging projects. I started out directing live-to-air talk shows at Time Warner Cable TV, then directing/shooting music videos and commercials, working as a director with Dick Dale & Lemmy, and as a D.P. with Mick Jones of The Clash. After briefly directing at MTV itself, I worked as a PBS producer for several years at P.O.V./American Documentary.

Writing/Producing:
I started screenwriting in late 90s & was fortunate to have great mentors like director Mike Nichols at the New Actors Workshop. I have strong/proven story skills--adaptations from prose & three-act structure in particular. I've been hired as a "script doctor" for many writer/directors, and a short-story adaptation I worked on won the top honors at the Hypnotic Film Festival with Universal Studios in 2001. In my producing work, I'm also often asked to forge a story from existing material, whether on paper or in the edit room/studio. In 2007 I wrote/produced a documentary, "A Test of Faith" about NYC after 9/11 with this approach.

Camera:
Very experienced w/documentary, working w/director Barbara Kopple on two films ("Hamptons" 2002/ABC & "My Generation" 2000) & many other top directors for hand-held camera work/see D.P. credits. I've also worked continually as a still photographer w/many corporate, non-profit & event clients.

New Projects:
Recently as producer I've focused on earth science/education. I began teaching middle-school students part-time in NY-Metro area classrooms, w/an emphasis on climate change. I'm interested in students' learning styles and the ways in which creating media & education can intersect. In the past, I've produced educational talk shows w/political leader-hosts such as Rep. Jerrold Nadler at Time-Warner & former HHS Secretary Tommy Thompson ("Plain Talk About Health") I'm currently in development for a similar-format program w/an earth science theme in 2009.

Jefferson Spady’s Specialties:

Story structure, working w/real-life situations for camera, 16/35mm/DV, time-lapse, Avid edit, U.S. politics, education, earth science, research/fundraising
