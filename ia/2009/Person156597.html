    Phipps Realty, a family-owned boutique agency in Rhode Island, presents "The Real Estate Minute." Broadcasted locally every week to over 100,000 viewers on ABC 6 and the RI News Channel, these 60-second segments provide educational information to potential home buyers and sellers. Topics include: market updates, home renovation tips, staging ideas, reflections on real estate coverage in the media, steps of a transaction and styles of homes.
