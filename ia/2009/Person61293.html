My long term working experience has matured in a multinational environment, with daily relationships with collegues based all over the world and many international clients.

My professional experience as senior assessor relates to:
- extensive knowledge (more than 1000 mandays in assessments in EMS EMAS QMS

My last 5 years management experience relates the coordination of teams of high skilled professionals (QHSE auditors) so I hade the opportunity of developing:
