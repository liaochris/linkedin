Kirsten Pines’s Summary

Backed by more than a decade of experience, I have established myself as a proven communicator both written and oral. I have a solid background in AutoCAD, largely self-taught in 1997 whilst working for a design & construction firm full time and studying Architecture part time.

Over the years, I have demonstrated that I have more skill with the product than the ordinary CAD user and have often doubled as a CAD manager and introduced CAD standards in some of the small architectural/design firms that I have contracted for over the years.

My last position was held at KarelCAD. My position included the following duties:
· Training and implementations at KarelCAD or Client's facilities,
· Delivery of technical support assistance to customers and internal staff of KarelCADboth on-site and remotely.
· Presentations/demonstrations of software products to small or large groups.
· Installation of computer software.

Establishing myself as an individual who is not only ambitious, but also detail oriented has led to a professional reputation of integrity and loyalty. My positive and can-do attitude has been considered an asset to every organisation I have been party to.
