Gail Conn’s Summary

I am first and foremost a creative thinker, with a great ability to sell abstract concepts as well as anything I put my mind to. I have worked in retail, consulting and corporate sales. I have learned to meld many techniques and practices I have learned over the years into my present incarnation as an owner of an online market research company. My present position blends my love of sales with marketing and generating fresh ideas and practices for companies to use to develop new strategies for the 21st century. I am a junky for information and knowledge, and feel that gathering intelligence is very important from many sources.
