Marco Leal’s Summary

Environment artist with over 4 years of experience, great passion and knowledge for visual arts, and great proficiency with several software packages like 3d studio max, Mudbox, Photoshop, UnrealEd, Zbrush and several others.

Marco Leal’s Specialties:

3d modeling and texturing within current and old gen standards, great capacity for problem solving and debugging within artistic pipelines as well as a great eye for optimization of 3d models and materials for use in realtime applications.
creation of every type of models, from organic, to architectural with great understanding of edgeflow, light, color, form and anatomy.
