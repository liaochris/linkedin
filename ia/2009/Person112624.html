Jill Bremer’s Summary

Nationally-recognized trainer and coach in the areas of communication skills, presentation skills, business protocol, professional image, and civility. Successful business owner and entrepreneur for 23 years. Experienced coach known for providing supportive environments where participants feel safe to expand their skills. Talent for designing creative, interactive, and results-oriented learning experiences for professionals at all levels.

Currently serve as one of five national trainers for a long-term collaborative project led by the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and the Public Library Association, teaching advocacy, leadership, and presentation skills to librarians and library trustees in 32 states.

Teach multi-day presentation skills courses as faculty member of the American Management Association.

Published author - It’s Your Move: Dealing Yourself the Best Cards in Life and Work (Financial Times Prentice Hall, 2004)
