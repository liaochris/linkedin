David Kohl’s Summary

An Entertainment, Communications and New Media industry specialist that has advised dozens of companies in these sectors on strategic business transformation and operational performance improvement matters.

Considered a PricewaterhouseCoopers subject-matter specialist on cable MSO operations, broadcast, multi-channel and Internet advertising, audience measurement and digital content distribution. Has helped several major media companies implement game-changing revenue strategies, and is a specialist in revenue maximization for high-volume, low-dollar transaction businesses. Brings a mix of strategic and operational expertise to the Entertainment and Media sectors that have helped clients capture quantifiable year-over-year benefits in excess of $500 million.
