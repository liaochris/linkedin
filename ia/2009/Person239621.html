Mark Williams’s Summary

Executive Talent Network (ETN) is a recruiting and specialist Linkedin training company with 2 core brands.
* ETN Training * Specialist training for Recruitment Consultants. We deliver specialist open and in-house recruitment training courses and recruitment training workshops for all levels of recruiting professionals UK wide. A key part of mt training is to deliver LinkedIn for recruiters courses which are designed to help recruiters get the most out of this amazing tool.
Having recruited and trained many talented recruiters over the years - many from scratch as 'first jobbers' fresh from university I am now using this knowledge and experience to deliver 'leading edge' recruitment training solutions to other recruitment organisations.
*Executive Talent Network* Specialist LinkedIn training for professionals in the UK whether they be active or passive job seekers, business developers who want to use LinkedIn or business owners wanting to network through LinkedIn to widen their market reach.

I have been in the recruitment business for 19 years now and I have always recruited in specialist markets. Having met many successful professionals in that time I am now utilising this network to drive the growth and success of ETN

Previously MD of a specialist supply chain recruitment business in the UK employing 30 staff - a company I personally launched in 1997 within the RK Group.
I am keen to take my new business forward by embracing new technology and methods so that we can continue to provide a highly valuable service to customers. I firmly believe the secret to great recruitment lies in people skills, 'who you know and how well you know them' are the two most important things for recruiters and I believe LinkedIn and internet facilitated networking will become a major factor in this.
