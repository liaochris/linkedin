Patrick De Mazière’s Summary

I'm a postdoctoral scientific researcher at the K.U.Leuven with broad experience in biomedical and statistical signal processing, (nonparametric) statistics, neural networks, data/text mining, Machine Learning, fMRI, and computational neurosciences in general.

In addition, I'm an ICT specialist with in-depth experience regarding dynamic web sites, database design, system architecture, Linux/UNIX system admin, Linux networks (NFS/CIFS/SAMBA/...), HPC, ...

I'm always eager to broaden my horizon by discovering new technologies or exploring existing ones in depth.
