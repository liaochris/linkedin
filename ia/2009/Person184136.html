    At Amtrak, Sebastian directs eCRM for E-Commerce, an operation generating over $1B in annual revenue. Sebastian is also heading up multi-channel expansion. He's earned an MA in International Affairs and an MBA in Marketing & MIS from American University. Sebastian has nearly 15 years of sales and marketing experience. Throughout his career, Sebastian has managed CRM at virtually every position held, working over the years to shape the people, process and technology aspects of a wide array of solutions built from the ground up: from Oracle to Salesforce to cutting edge eCRM. His experience includes working for Fortune 100 companies like MCI and Sprint Nextel as well as Internet media startups backed by Young & Rubicam and Burson-Marsteller.

    Sebastian was born in Argentina and grew up in Boston, MA. He and his wife are raising a family in the suburbs of Washington, DC. Outside of teaching, Sebastian's passion is developing stories and finding ways to bridge the divide between the physical and the digital.
