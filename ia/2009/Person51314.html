Francesco Cura’s Summary

CURAphotography has established itself as one of the leaders in the Headshot and Fashion/Editorial fields in Los Angeles.
Published photographer Francesco Maria Cura' offers services to Actors, Models, Musicians, Artists, Lawyers, TV and Radio Personalities, Real Estate Brokers, Families and anyone who is in need to be immortalized.
Through the use of the "Talk Through Technique", Francesco Maria Cura' has invented a new, sophisticated approach to headshots. His unique ability to help the actor explore him/herself while being in front of the camera has given CURAphotography the reputation of a boutique Studio. A great headshot needs not only the high quality standards that CP Studio offers, but the layers of emotions that MUST be present inside the subject. That's why a CURAphotography headshot possesses the power, depth, quality and intensity that will change your career path.
If you are serious about being an actor and you want to reveal the real you on a printed photo, you found the right place.
If you are tired of hearing that you don’t look like your pictures, or that your headshots are missing “something”, then it’s time to make a decision.
Each shot must carry your qualities within. Each shot must be created with precision and detail. Each shot must be the most accurate journey inside of you. Each shot must tell us your story. THAT’S what we do at CURAphotography.

We also offer high quality fashion and editorial services. Whether you’re in need of a portfolio, a zed card or some campaign-like images, we cover all fields.
Our images have gained incredible respect and reputation status in the fashion/modeling industry.

Please, visit our website at www.curaphotography.com for more information. You will also find out more about all the other services we offer: from Commercial to Architecture.
