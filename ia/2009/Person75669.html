Ernesto Lopez’s Summary

- My current work is to assess, and help to improve national systems against money laundering and combating the financing of terrorism (AML/CFT). I coordinate and provide technical assistance to countries in Latin America and the Caribbean, by training public officials, drafting laws and regulations and providing advise to government agencies on AML/CFT issues. I also lead the CFATF evaluation teams for Central America, Venezuela and the Dominican Republic.
- Before joining the CFATF I worked on AML/CFT at the International Monetary Fund for 5 years.
- Prior experience as Compliance Officer and Director of the AML Unit of the Central Bank of Colombia, Legal Counsel to the Board of Directors of the same institution, and Law Clerk of the Constitutional Court of Colombia.
