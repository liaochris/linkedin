Habbo Beem’s Summary

* I Work with big symphonic orchestras, with small ensembles, with rock/pop, jazz and all kind of musicians and also with the latest synth- and sampling techniques. Sometimes separate, and sometimes combining everything together in my 'state of the art' digital recording studio.
* Beside my work as a composer, I worked for several years as a sound designer as well, which resulted in new and different ideas concerning music for film.
* I started composing at the age of four. However, my musical career started as a pop musician. I was a guitar player/singer in lots of bands. Later on I spend a few years as a bass player/singer and moved back to the guitar again.
* During studio sessions I played several instruments. I even did a few gigs as a drummer, not a very good one but at least I know 'how a drummer thinks'.
* This experience gave me the ability to communicate with other musicians rather effectively in order to convey musical ideas in any language.
After all music is just one great international language!

Habbo Beem’s Specialties:

Big orchestral music production. Cross overs with different kinds of music. Very versatile in musical styles.

Music production in 5.1 surround.
