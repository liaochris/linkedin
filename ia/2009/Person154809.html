Massimiliano Mazzer’s Summary

I developed my career selling and distributing products and services to medium and large traditional and On Line companies in the Information & Communication Technology and Web Media sector. I have gained experience in specific sectors: Enterprise B2B and luxury B2C.

I have an in depth knowledge of business intelligence, digital marketing communications and web advertising. I have focused my expertise on Email Marketing, SEO & SEM, Web Analytics, Semantic Analytics, Web Leads Generations, Paid Search Marketing, Online Copywriting, Web Project Management, Online PR, Accessibility, Mobile Marketing and Affiliate Marketing.

I have developed Partnership with major international ITC and Media Advertising channel.
At the same time I have developed sales agreements with the major European channels specialized in SaaS distribution to large accounts.

From 2008 until 2009 I worked for IntelligenceFocus Inc. as sales Manager Europe.
I was focalized on Web Intelligence & Analytics tools and platform.

From 2007 until 2008 I worked for the European Internet Division of Seat PG.
I reported to the Internet Vice President in order to manage SEM (PPC and SEO) development new media projects for the Seat Group middle size company as Project Manager Internet Distribution.

From 2004 until 2007 I worked for Travelex Worldwide Money as Head of Sales where I developed Corporate Foreign Exchange B.U cooperating with Retail and Cash-to-Cash B.Us.

I am the Maex founder and administrator since 2004.
Maex is a 100% private capital.
