Frank N. Vignieri’s Summary

I am a leader with 17 years of business management experience within the transportation logistics, manufacturing and airline industries. I have worked in marketing and operations management of both large and small businesses.

I enjoy taking on technically complicated or unpopular projects and building support for them with both an internal and external constituency. I enjoy collaborating with a team of exceptional and passionate individuals brought together to solve a significant challenge or problem.

In addition to my business background, I’ve been advocating for a special cause for many years now in my personal life which has taught me the importance of persistence, team building, collaboration and diversity.

My career history demonstrates continuous achievement and advancement into positions of increasing complexity and responsibility. My current position has an entrepreneurial flavor to it. I have used my talents to stabilize and grow a small transportation logistics company.

In previous positions, I have led cross-functional teams comprised of sales, marketing, engineering, operations, distribution, finance and customer support personnel to implement strategic business initiatives and develop new products and services.

My core business skills are in management, marketing, research & analysis, supply chain logistics, small business finance and administration. My written and verbal communications skills are exceptional. I’m accomplished at managing details via formal and informal process controls.

Continuing education and adapting to change has been a mantra during my career. I am passionate about learning, researching and teaching. I’m a life-long aviation buff. In my spare time, I enjoy studying the historical and technical aspects of flying. I have earned a private pilot’s license and am a member of the Experimental Aircraft Association of Oshkosh, Wisconsin.
