Nicolas Borda’s Summary

My name is Nicolas Borda, I am a multimedia producer currently working in a freelance basis in London. The balance between art and technology is key to my work as I believe that this balance influences the harmony of the final outcome. Each project I do is completely personalized to meet the customers requirement and all the material is always original and unique.

I am currently specializing in Drupal web development and making accessible, usable web applications. I am very experienced working with new wave JavaScript libraries such as jQuery and Prototype.
