Ben Sargent’s Summary

Ben Sargent has served as producer and executive producer for a number of award winning Web sites, including European and Asian language sites. With over 15 years in information technology, Ben gained substantial experience in both operational management and marketing management, held executive positions and worked as both an industry analyst and a senior consultant in e-business globalization. Ben has led publishing, technology and strategy teams serving GE, Microsoft, and many other Internet and IT companies. He has published, led workshops, addressed technology conferences in Europe and the U.S., and helped numerous Internet and Fortune 500 companies implement global Web strategies.

Ben Sargent’s Specialties:

Strategic analysis, business process re-engineering, business planning, information technology, multilingual content management, business globalization, marketing
