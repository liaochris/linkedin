Adero Allison, PhD’s Summary

Certificate in Professional Sociological Practice - extensive work with adults with disabilities, assistive technology, career and life planning.

Business Coach and Consultant since 1994, Organizational Development, Strategic planning, A.Inst. Independent Business, Small to Med. Business focus, Micro / business start-up coaching.

Certified Senior Advisor, Family Life Planning, Person-Centered Life Planning, Caregiver Training.

I help adults (over 40) with life transitions, providing the best experience for the most people possible. We remember in our work that a "failure to plan is a plan to fail" but we rarely carry this message over into our personal lives. I want to change this missing planning aspect and help people live exciting and fulfilling lives after 40. Our planning model works with older adults and their adult children who, in many cases, have become their caregivers.
