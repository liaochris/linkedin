Tom Raftery’s Summary

I fell in love with computers and set up a software company while studying for a PhD in Plant Science in 1991. I have since worked for a number of companies at Group IT Manager/CTO level and more recently I joined industry analyst firm RedMonk to head up the green energy and sustainability practice.
