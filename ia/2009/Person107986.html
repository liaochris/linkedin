Jeff Franks’s Summary

I specialize in designing, building and deploying custom business process management and enterprise application integration solutions using a variety of technologies and tools.

Foremost among those tools is Microsoft BizTalk and I have experience with each and every version of the product. I have extensive experience as a database developer with SQL Server, Oracle, Informix, Sybase and IBM DB2 and UDB. Additionally, I have experience with Maximo, Emptoris, Browz, Informatica, SeeBeyond and Mercator.

.NET developer, C# and VB.NET, and experienced with XML technologies (XPath, XSLT, XSL FO). I am an experienced web developer and adept with ASP.NET, ASP, Javascript, VBScript and ancillary technologies and languages such as PHP, CSS and MySQL. I have previous experience with Java, Korn Shell, UNIX, Linux, Informix 4GL and earlier versions of Visual Basic.

My life experiences provide a distinct advantage in building effective and highly motivated teams. I lead by example, choose not to be a manager, but instead someone that inspires performance and takes opportunities to train and mentor promising associates. In my opinion, leadership is the key to success in any and all endeavors.

I am also a professional photographer that accepts assignments that fit my schedule; normally on weekends or evenings. This keeps the other side of my brain engaged; creating unique and beautiful images is a personal passion and has proven to be a path to self-actualization and immense personal joy.
