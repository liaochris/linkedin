Jessica Mallard’s Summary

For Jessica, there is nothing like carving through a sea of glass on a wakeboard, skiing in and out of thigh-deep powder, exploring the mountains with her hubby or being happy with where you are in life...and she is happy using her experience and skills to help companies and individuals achieve their objectives.

Jessica has expertise in developing and executing PR and marketing plans in a variety of industries. As a social media enthusiast, she has had success in incorporating social media strategy into traditional marketing methods.

She has both agency and corporate experience having worked for well-known companies like Omniture and Edelman Public Relations, gaining solid experience working in the tech industry. As she began her career in PR, Jessica knew the hard-knocks life of straight media relations was not for her. She decided to take the marketing communications path and hasn’t looked back since.

Jessica attributes her affinity for cougar cubs, Polynesian dancing and men's volleyball to her time at Brigham Young University in Provo, UT where she received a BA in communications with an emphasis in PR and a minor in psychology.

Jessica Mallard’s Specialties:

Jessica has a variety of marketing experience including: writing, web site development, market research, media relations, securing speaking engagements, community relations, blogger relations, creating and executing social networking strategies, events coordination, media analysis and awards development.
