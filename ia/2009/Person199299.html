Christopher Herring’s Summary

** Manager of 1,500 LinkedIn's Real Estate Investors Group
** Manager of Delta Sigma Theta - Omega Psi Phi Group

** Finalist for the City of San Antonio's Martin Luther King, Jr. Distinguished Achievement Award--Presentation January 17, 2009

Christopher was recognized by the US JAYCEES as one of Ten Outstanding Young Americans Award (TOYA) in 2003. He joined an elite group of a little over 600 Americans to include former Presidents William Jefferson Clinton, Gerald Ford and John F. Kennedy, civil rights leader Rev. Jesse Jackson and corporate tycoons Ross Perot, Henry Heinz and Nelson Rockefeller.

Christopher Herring is an active duty officer; entrepreneur and community organizer. He is the Publisher of River Walk Magazine (serving the 7th largest US City in Texas). His company recently launched the Chamber Advance Mag. He is a Board of Director for the West San Antonio Chamber of Commerce, and Chairs the Board for Joy Keeper (Breast Cancer Patient Support).

In 2008, Chris developed a unique mortgage elimination service to help families through the recession, My Mortgage Teacher (http://www.MyMortgageTeacher.com). 100% of American homeowners qualify to reduce their interest rates, save tens of thousands of dollars paid to the banks and shorten 30-year bank mortgages by at least 8 years and up to 22 years.
