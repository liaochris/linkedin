Petra Dreiskämper’s Summary

After a short lived career as a historian and some basic-training in html and database-techniques at the Leiden University, I pretty much taught myself PHP and in 1999 started working as a freelance developer.

I did all sorts of things in the beginning but gradually became a dedicated PHP developer. These days I have (or am) my own company and work from home. Most of the time I'm developing custom made Content Management Systems (CMS) and websites for small companies or institutions, but I've also made educational cd-roms, built webservices, webshops and serverapplications.

I'm really a hands on programmer and love to build everything myself. I'm also dedicated to writing clean and efficient code which makes me very fond of the OOP possibilities of PHP5. And I'm glad to still be learning new things everyday.
