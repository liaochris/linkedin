Gonzalo Ferro’s Summary

Mr. Ferro has seven years of research and applied experience in the area of Industrial/Organizational Psychology. Currently he manages a number of projects in the areas of training development and evaluation, performance, and human capital systems. He also has experience conducting focus groups, interviewing stakeholders, and developing, conducting and analyzing surveys on a variety of organizational issues. As a consultant, Mr. Ferro develops applied customized solutions to clients, based on organizational principles and the latest research. He has presented his work in technical reports, published in peer-reviewed journals and at national conferences. He is fluent in English and Spanish.

Gonzalo Ferro’s Specialties:

Adaptability training, training program development and evaluation, work performance evaluation, needs assessments, survey development and deployment, conducting research with U.S. Army Special Operation Forces.
