Gary Stockton’s Summary

I am an eBusiness manager for CMS Products, Inc. in Costa Mesa, California. CMS specializes in storage, backup and data security products which are manufactured in our Southern California headquarters.

Our backup products include the award-winning ABSplus automatic backup system, an external storage solution that allows you to, in the event of a hard drive crash, simply boot up from our external hard drive. Our Data Security solutions are powered by our CE-Secure encryption software, providing business users with simple, effective and strong AES 256-bit encryption for their data.

I oversee our online business, including daily management of our web site, and our presence on many of the social networking web sites. My goal with Linked In is to identify potential business opportunities for my company, by introducing our world class backup and storage solutions to potential clients.
