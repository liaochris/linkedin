    Maximum Business Innovations, Inc.

    (Real Estate industry)

    May 2009 — Present (6 months)

    Maximum Business Innovations, Inc. is a forward thinking group of professionals. We have proprietary funding programs that we created for our own investment objectives. Because of this, we are able to work with select groups, and help them achieve their funding goals. We are not a lender! We are not a broker! What we are is a company looking to maximize the opportunities out there to acquire assets, and participate on projects. Our funding programs allow us to do that.

    We engineered 2 unique programs that offer a very exceptional project funding opportunity. We offer a 6-12 month term with no payments during the 6-12 month funding period. Our group funds your project using a performance bond in the form of an SBLC or BG (stand by letter of credit - Bank Guarantee). The SBLC can never be drawn on, liened, assessed or otherwise encumbered. The only condition of the SBLC is that it be placed with our investment group during the 6-12 month funding term. This is an iron clad, simple, straight forward project funding program.

    We also created the Capital Leverage Program. This program is a streamlined escrow transaction we created for any level of participation. There is not a project requirement. This program was arranged for a film client of a principle in our group. It requires liquid cash, and essentially the CLP will compensate up to 100% on seed capital inside 22 days. This is NOT an investment program! This is prepared for our groups goals. We just let groups participate..Its a win win scenario. This seems to work best for development groups or producers looking for money to fund a film. The compensation is NOT a loan - So there is nothing to repay.
