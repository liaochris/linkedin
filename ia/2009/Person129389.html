Kelly Faircloth’s Summary

Kelly Faircloth has been with Inc.com since October 2008. Kelly attended Harvard University, where she served as executive editor of the Harvard Independent, a campus alt-weekly. She interned at the Oxford American, and she honed her skills as a freelance editor working on a book of film criticism with Giorgio Bertellini, a Radcliffe Fellow. Her senior thesis in Folklore and Mythology explored shifting narratives of Sherman¹s March to the Sea, based on a summer spent interviewing residents of her native Georgia about family legends from the Civil War.
