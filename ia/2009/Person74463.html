Eric Knight’s Summary

The story begins a vision to conquer the world, well the musical world that is. Eric Knight began singing and performing at the age of 6 and writing since age 13. Since that time one of his ultimate goals has always been to write great songs period. "I've always believed in if its a great song, its a great song no matter what group or person sings it.

For more of my biography visit my website at ericknightonline.com

I have released 2 solo albums and preparing for my third "Delusions Of Grandeur" to be released in early 2009.

Notable mentions: I have opened for Aerosmith, KISS, Kid Rock & The Dave Matthews Band. Received substantial press and TV coverage.

My goal has been to diversify and expand my brand.

I am extremely excited to be a part of the new "music industry" at this time in history. I feel this has shaken the corporate dinosaurs and old ways of doing business to their core. The musicians destiny and control of our creativity is finally in our hands. See what everyone seems to forget is the simplest concept, without music and songs and the people who create them there would be no "music industry!"

  • President

    LAMX - Los Angeles Music Experiment

    (Music industry)

    February 2008 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    LAMX (Los Angeles Music Experiment) will host one of the biggest music conferences and nightly music showcases in the country. Think of SXSW or CMJ but in the heart of Hollywood, California during the peak of summer. The event, titled the Los Angeles Music Experiment, or LAMX as we like to call it will attract thousands of music industry professionals, musicians, music fans and media from around the world.

    LAMX is a global think tank and exchange of new ideas between musicians and industry alike on how to create, promote and distribute their products and services in the new music industry. LAMX will be the biggest yearly music industry showcase in the West Coast. Showcasing LA’s and the countries top unsigned bands. Indie and national acts will also play a major role in the festivals success as they will be interspersed throughout Hollywood’s hottest venues which are world renowned.
