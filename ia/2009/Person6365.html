Responsible for managing Applications Product Delivery for the Oracle, Peoplesoft, SAP, Lotus Notes and Siebel Lines of Business; as well as the Global Service Center. Primary mission is to focus on revenue and margin growth and customer satisfaction.

* With CrossCom Trading Network, I took on the challenge of creating the entire infrastructure for a new concept in online bond trading. Given a concept and a "go," I led an intensive effort to build the operation from the ground up in a year and a half-including gaining SEC and NASD regulatory approval and inking deals with strategic partners.
