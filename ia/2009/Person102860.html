James Cunningham’s Summary

Talented and versatile Information Architect / User Experience Design Lead with 14 Years’ experience creating high quality, user-centered software and interactive projects for consumers and the enterprise. Strong on strategy, concept and design execution. Adept at the full spectrum of the project life cycle, from business analysis, user analysis, process analysis to design planning, interaction design, visual design and engagement with development teams.

Summary of Qualifications:
Excellent communication and collaboration skills for working on multidisciplinary teams, leading projects from concept through production; able to coordinate multiple projects, schedules; highly organized.
Dedicated user advocate who designs with a user’s needs in mind.
Able to understand and transform business goals of projects into clear, user-centered solutions.
Passion for well conceived, innovative, easy to use, and well crafted interfaces.
Great attention to detail; trained eye for aesthetics.
Highly skilled and experienced in creating flowcharts, wireframes, storyboards, design mockups, interactive prototypes, UI specifications and visual assets.
Can work effectively within constraints of Web and software development environment.
Project Management and client relationship experience.
