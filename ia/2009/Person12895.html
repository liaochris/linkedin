Anders Birch’s Summary

I have obtained valuable experience through a variety of positions, covering a wide spectrum of consultancy and management positions and now as manager at Telenor, Denmark. The common denominator in my work has always been implementation and communication of strategic changes.

I hold a M.Sc. in Political Science with a minor in IT-management. I also hold the military rank of First Lieutenant in the Danish Army.

I am dynamic and positive by nature and feel most comfortable in large international organizations. I am determined, ambitious and enjoy challenges. I always remain positive and focused – even under pressure or stressful conditions.

Leading others has always been a natural part of my life, either as team captain, army officer or manager. I work hard to become a successful manager in the eyes of directs, peers and superiors – and not least myself. That is my mission – and my ambition is to become a senior manager at the executive level. My leadership style consists of three key components:

1) It’s all about people and relationships…
2) More communication is better…
3) Ambitious goal setting…

Anyone who wants to network, please contact me at birch.anders@mac.com or give me a call at +45 6050 6003
