Rogério Snatus’s Summary

Working for over 14 years with fundraising for projects and organizations, social, cultural, environmental, universities, municipalities, governments, researchers and businesses. I am author of the book "Advanced Strategies for Capture of Resources" and "Guide of financing for Latin America". I have a network of strategic contacts with donors, leaders and opinion formers and sectoral policies.
