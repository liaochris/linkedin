Ray Smets’s Summary

Proven professional with 20+ years of experience in forming, developing and managing high-tech businesses and teams. Established track-record of delivering on aggressive commitments while maintaining high-quality and high-morale. Passion for leading high-growth technology businesses in the IT, networking, software, telecom (PSTN, mobile & wireless), and security industries. Experienced in leading companies through both a spin-out of a large technology business with outside equity investment, as well as a public company acquisition by a larger public corporation. Personal qualities include highly creative, intelligent, innovative, personable, and team oriented personality.
