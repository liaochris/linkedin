Paul Moore’s Summary

PM Integrated Marketing was established in Toronto in 1992 and has provided outstanding sales and marketing consulting services in Toronto, Kelowna, Vancouver and Carlsbad, California.
My specialty is in high tech and manufacturing industries in small and medium sized business. My regular customers particularly value my ability to provide real life experience in all aspects of running profitable and successful businesses.

My Ideal Prospect is a product/service business owner with 15-100 employees; no or limited internal marketing support; located in the Greater Vancouver Regional District. They have typically been in business for at least 5 years. These businesses are outwardly successful but have done very little marketing.

The greater problem is that they have lost control over the various marketing initiatives and marketing materials that have been created on-the fly over the years. Internally there is no accountability and most, if not all of the marketing responsibility falls to the owner of the business. They have also found it difficult to grow their business beyond its current market share due to increasing competition or the current economic environment.

They desperately want to take their business to the next level.
