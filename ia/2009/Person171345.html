Luca Nardino’s Summary

At the beginning as freelance, then hired by Capgemini and then further by TXT E-solutions, I gained an extensive advisory experience in ICT, been involved in a number of technology solution assignments in the areas of information systems and business processes analysis for several companies in different industries: BPM, Telecom, Vodafone, Stogit (ENI Group) and Bticino. Finally, I grasped the opportunity to verticalize my expertise moving first to Monte Paschi Asset Management as Project Manager and now in B-Source as expert Business Analyst. At the moment my role includes, in addition to coding (roughly 40% of my time), functional and technical analysis, technical solutions proposal and assessment, testing and trouble-shooting, coaching and project leading.

In the last nine years, I’ve dealt with the design and data modelling, development, test, deployment, integration, tuning, operation and maintenance of web/database/eai/service oriented applications, distributed on heterogeneous environments according to high availability and tight security policies.
I’m experienced in estimating, planning, effort distribution and overseeing. I took part in drafting technical solutions and commercial proposal within a national and international context.
