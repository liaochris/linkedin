Olga Slavkina’s Summary

I am a brand strategist and founder of Schmoozy Fox, a brand strategy consultancy and blog dedicated to funky customer-centered brands.

My typical client is an innovative and original company that wants to build its brand, make it sharper, clearer, juicier and, most importantly, sought after by consumers. A great deal of my work has to do with building brands online. I work with real-life brands that seek greater online presence, as well as with online brands which want to attract real-life consumers.

I hold an MBA from the top-ranked IE Business School in Spain, where I specialized in marketing, and a Masters in International Relations from the Fletcher School of Law and Diplomacy in the US where I was a Fulbright student. I have also taken executive marketing courses at London Business School and ESSEC.

I am fluent in English (native level), Russian and Latvian, speak French well, and can get around in German, Italian, Spanish and Dutch.

I am positive, creative and sociable. I thrive on teamwork and human relationships. For me, business is fun if done in a team of interested people with passion. I believe that the process of creative production can bring most value to your company if carried out in a team. I am comfortable working across different cultural environments, persistent in fulfilling objectives and entrepreneurial. A natural networker, I can help develop your business by attracting new clients, distributors and other stakeholders key to your brand.

I am fascinated by the idea of growing a business based on superior-quality product or service, winning customers and building sustainable brand equity. If you are passionate about building a funky brand, if you value innovative ideas, flexibility, spontaneity, and put a premium on trying new approaches, chances are we could create a lot of value for our customers together.
