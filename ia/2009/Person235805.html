Thomas Dill’s Summary

Diverse experience in oil and gas E & P, from worldwide resource assessment and regional studies to exploration and well evaluation. Explored Rocky Mountain basins and thrust belt, with discoveries in Green River Basin. Expertise in source rock geochemistry, compressional structure, basin modeling, and wellsite operations. Strong expertise in well data management and databases. Interested in exploration geology, especially in plays that could utilize my data management skills.

Thomas Dill’s Specialties:

Stratigraphy, Rocky Mountain basins and plays, thrust belt and foreland structure, source rock evaluation, geochemistry, Lodgepole Waulsortian Mounds, Cretaceous Western Interior Seaway, fractured plays, database design and administration, Oracle and SQL, well data management.
