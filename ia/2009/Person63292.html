Diane Lau’s Summary

I have worked for over 15 years in marketing in the retail, industrial, legal and finance fields. My experience is broad and thorough, but I excel especially in web marketing and written communications. In addition to my experience with the employers listed here, I have operated my own small publishing company since 1998 to publish my own works of fiction and non-fiction, and conducted all aspects of that business including a broad array of web marketing techniques. Today my publishing house is profitable and growing, with ten paperback titles, ebook versions of same, and a set of trading cards. I also serve as marketing manager for my husband's residential cleaning service.
