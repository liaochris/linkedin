Terri Hayley’s Summary

Terri was the #1 Sales Counselor for one of the Top 20 Builders in the Nation and an expert in new homes. She negotiated the new home transaction between the buyer and seller in 100's of transactions for years and really excels at getting you what you want at the best price possible. So, it only makes sense that she decided in 2008 to join her husband, Robert, at the # 1 real estate sales offices in Dallas - Keller Williams Preston Road office where we closed $850 million in sales last year alone.

My husband, Robert Hayley, and I work as a team. He does more of the commercial side, but works with me on the residential side of things, too. We absolutely light up at helping you find your dream home and/or selling your home and getting into the home of your dreams. How is it different working with us? You will be hard pressed to find a team who will listen more meticulously to your needs, try harder to fulfill them, genuinely care more about you and your family's happiness, who are native Texans with a broad scope deep knowledge of the North Texas area, and will negotiate on your behalf with whatever level of ferocity the situation requires to get your needs met. One of my dearest friends calls me "nail, hammer, wall" as once I put my mind to a task (such as finding someone the home of their dreams), I pursue it relentlessly and I don't let the others involved (apprasier, title, mortgage co, etc.) fall behind on their jobs either and we get it all accomplished within the timeline we put forth from the beginning. A nice way of saying it is that I've got bulldog tenacity. ;) I've got laser like focus on my clients and my husband is awesome at doing the CMA's, crunching the numbers, pulling statistics on neighborhoods, and any other data you could need - all without a calculator!
