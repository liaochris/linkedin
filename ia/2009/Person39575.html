Christopher Bjerrisgaard’s Summary

My background is a mix of entertainment and new media marketing. Additionally I have managed my own business, had the benefit of major corporation experience at Nokia, and spent time learning and perfecting skills in new media with technology start ups.

I have taught myself a new industry at almost every position I have held through my career and now am using those skills to build my personal brand in the beer industry. Since Gary Vaynerchuk started Wine Library TV three years ago, the way people went about branding has forever changed. I bring the skills companies need in traditional marketing, event organizing, and public relations, as well as the ability to build my personal brand, as well as others, in new marketing frontiers. I plan on exploiting all avenues of new media, combining use of internet video, blogs, twitter, and other social media outlets to firmly establish a presence in this new world of branding.
