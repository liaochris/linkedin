Jonathan Spinney’s Summary

15 years combined experience specializing in enterprise IT, GIS, content, wireless network infrastructure, and handsets, and in a wide range of roles as an industry analyst, product manager, industry solutions manager, marketing director, business development VP, and VP of sales

Most recently I was Vice President of Business Development at Sonim, a manufacturer of rugged GPS-enabled mobile phones where I worked on carrier, retail, & B2B sales, partner branding, grassroots global-market insertion, enterprise and consumer applications design, and supplier agreements. Prior to joining Sonim, I was Marketing Director at Openwave positioning a Web service delivery platform in the B2B developer ecosystem with Sprint, and deploying mobile Browser, Messaging, and AJAX device software solutions with Kyocera USA, LGE, Motorola, Sagem, Samsung, and Qualcomm. Before that, I had an Industry Solutions Manager role at ESRI, where I built a large developer-partner network serving industry verticals jointly with wireless carrier-partner sales teams from Sprint, NEXTEL, and Bell Mobility. In 2004 I won ESRIs Award in Innovation. Before ESRI, I deployed the first wireless e911 products at AT&T, T-Mobile, Sprint, and Bell while at LBS pioneer SignalSoft. These systems still route half of all the 911 emergency services calls in the U.S. and Canada today. I started my wireless work as a Senior Analyst in Washington D.C. with wireless research & consulting firm The Strategis Group where I built, managed, and sold a wireless-market segmentation product that tracked FCC spectrum auction activity and coverage build-outs. My tech career began at M7 Visual Intelligence, an angel-backed remote sensing innovator funded by Crown Castle International, a leading international provider of real estate tower infrastructure.
