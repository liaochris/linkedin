DeeDee Riley’s Summary

I am a REALTOR® in El Dorado Hills, CA with Lyon Real Estate. I have lived in Northern California most of my life except for 12 years spent in Redmond, WA with my husband and children. I have a BS degree in Clothing and Textiles from CSU, Chico. When our kids came along, I decided to become a stay at home mom. I'm a firm believer in ongoing education though so took classes in whatever interested me during that time. Real estate and horticultural particularly interested me. I love networking and meeting new people. Working in real estate has let me put all my passions together in one field. New technology fascinates me and satisfies my ongoing education desire so I try and utilize it in my business as much as possible while still providing the personal service to my clients.

I am a proud and active member of the El Dorado Hills Chamber of Commerce and the El Dorado Chapter of Women's Council of Realtors and encourage you to get involved with your community as well.

DeeDee Riley’s Specialties:

* I specialize in El Dorado Hills and the surrounding areas that include, Cameron Park, Rescue, Shingle Springs, and Folsom but also work throughout the Sacramento Valley representing all buyers and sellers including first-time homebuyers, investors, and short sales.
