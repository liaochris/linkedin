Michael Hahn’s Summary

We facilitate team experiences for teams exhibiting a lack of respect, trust, accountability or constructive conflict...

We foster communication and team unity for Organizations and Departments charged with doing More with Less staff and resources!

We motivate and inspire your teams to create success!

We give Associations and its members the experience of a unique development program that they can use with their own teams...


Using our 3 Step Approach for 'High Performance Teams', we create a unique environment for your team.

Assessment
A anonymous pre-assessment allows the opportunity for the truth about the team dynamic to be exposed.

Event/Facilitation
A fun offsite adventure event allows the break down of emotional and mental barriers allowing a healthy, completely experiential facilitation fostering communication, respect and trust among team members

Accountability Program
Accountability for the agreements made on Game Day maintains the long term effects of the facilitation and keeps the teams fueled for months after the event!
