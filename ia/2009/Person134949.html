Kristi Serrano’s Summary

Kristi Serrano has been working in the Internet and marketing industry for over five years in multiple facets. From Ad Trafficking and Email Marketing to Search Engine Optimization and Social Media, she is no stranger to the dot com marketplace.

Kristi is currently the Sr. Online Marketing Manager at The Knot, Inc where she oversees all SEO and Social Media efforts across all Knot, Inc online properties. Kristi has also started her own Internet Marketing Agency, MOCALogix, in which she services an array of clients assisting with their online presence.

Prior to her recent endeavors, Kristi head up the marketing team at Prime Visibility, LLC where she oversaw and monitored the marketing tactics used to generate traffic and increase ROI for an assortment of clients. Kristi also created detailed marketing plans for clients as well as Prime Visibility that include SEO, Email Marketing, Public Relations and Lead Generation.

She was also the Director of Operations of Interactive Femme, a networking site for woman in the interactive and technology industries. Kristi has been a member of 212 – New York’s Interactive Advertising Club since 2004 and a member of SEMPO since 2005.

Kristi speaks at several local Universities on Internet Marketing, showcasing new opportunities for new graduates. Kristi also teaches an Internet Marketing course at Long Island University, C.W. Post in Brookville, New York, and was interviewed and published in the Spring 2007 issue of Search Marketing Standard Magazine in regards to her educational efforts.

Kristi currently holds a B.F.A. in Public Relations from Long Island University, C.W. Post.
