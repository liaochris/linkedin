Ramzi Faraj’s Summary

I am truly passionate about my profession, the result is that nearly 100% of my business is by referral from satisfied clients and the most experienced Realtors in Michigan. The mortgage industry has changed more in the last 12 months than any time in history. Now more than ever the choice of the right loan professional can truly make a difference in getting to the closing table. My clients only think about home financing a few times during their lives; I think about it every single day. It’s their home and their future. It’s my profession and passion. My mission is to carefully guide clients through the entire home loan process, so that they feel confident as they make choices about the many options available for their financing strategy. With many years and a wide range of experience in the mortgage industry, my team and I stand ready to assist every step of the way.

Ramzi Faraj’s Specialties:

I specialize in FHA, VA, and USDA loans that allow people to buy with as little as 0% down. My goal is to be your mortgage advisor for life. I believe that our initial closing is just the beginning of our relationship. I also am an exclusive marketing coach for Realtors. I work with Realtors to solve the marketing puzzle. I help agents develop and implement a comprehensive plan that achieves results.
