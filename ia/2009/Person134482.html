Kris Schindler’s Summary

KRIS SCHINDLER is a highly effective communications professional whose experience crosses the globe, spans more than a dozen years and includes providing marketing and management services to clients in the entertainment, arts, travel, sports, retail, religious, social service and non-profit arenas. A strategic thinker, Kris possesses a big-picture, progressive entrepreneurial mindset that has helped advance businesses from as small as sole proprietorships to as large as major television and motion picture studios. She is known for her “producer mentality,” resourcefulness and ability to identify and build synergetic relationships.

Public relations, buzz PR, influencer marketing, trend forecasting, digital media, social media campaign planning, strategic planning, media relations, writing, presentations to professional groups and university and college students.
