Manas Kanungo’s Summary

Manas Kanungo Founded IMM, a consulting firm, to help small-mid size companies develop and execute global new concepts, startups, market and product entry strategies that are differentiated, competitive, and sustainable in the medical devices, diagnostics, therapeutic, biotech, and pharma fields. He is an accomplished entrepreneurial professional with proven leadership and track records to manage domestic or international projects and programs including for launch and growth plans that deliver and exceed unmet needs; and to build, manage, train, and coach results oriented, dynamic, cross-functional, multi-cultural, global, internal and external teams. He has the ability to leverage expertise in, and bridge gaps between various segments, functional, professional, and user groups with respect to understanding customer needs, “translating” for internal use, and decision making for different diseases, diagnosis, therapies, technologies, and markets in the medical device, diagnostic, IVD, POC, biotech, therapeutic, and pharma fields. Manas has worked with Bayer, Roche, Eli Lilly, DPC (now Siemens Medical), Quidel, Ohmeda (now GE Medical), and Bio-Tek Instruments in the US, Brazil, India, China, Asia, EU, and Latin America markets. He is a citizen of US; originally from India; has traveled in over 50 countries; lived and worked in US, Brazil, and India; and is multilingual with fluent - basic abilities in English, Portuguese, Hindi, Spanish, and Bengali. He is open for consulting or full time senior level positions.

Manas will graduate in Aug'09 from the Paul Merage School of Business at UC Irvine, with a Health Care Executive MBA degree. He has a Global Executive Leadership Certificate from Thunderbird, an MS in Bioeng & MS in EE from WVU, and a BSEE from NIT in India. He has a patent pending, published in the electrophysiology area, received an Honorary Diploma in Hospital Engineering from the Association of Hospital Engineers of Spain.
