Clayce Rodamer’s Summary

Technologically-savvy operations professional offering 15+ years’ experience “getting the job done” and positioning companies to achieve record revenue growth and profitability while saving time and money. Top skills include creating systems and processes to meet tough business challenges. Advanced understanding of financial instruments, including balance sheets, cash flow, P&L, business requirements and negotiation.

Passionate about technology and known for ability to design and implement innovative software systems and Web-based applications to position companies for record growth.
