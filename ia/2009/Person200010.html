Roberto Cosenza’s Summary

I'm an application developer/designer with experience in building custom applications interfacing with legacy systems.
Theoretical approach with a large dose of pragmatism is my strength.

I enjoy challenges and I'm interested in discovering new technologies, new experiences and new people.

Design, refactoring and test driven development are some of the keywords in my professional description.

I prefer to work in an agile team with close cooperation with business stakeholders and I think that continuous building and integration are natural parts in any work environment. I take ownership and pride into everything I do.

I'm also a certified Scrum Master.

Roberto Cosenza’s Specialties:

J2EE, Spring, Hibernate, Ajax, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, Adobe Flex, ActionScript are among the technologies I use daily.

I make use of TDD in all my work, in order to guarantee the highest quality for my products.

I have a long experience with Java and I do not have problems to adapt to any programming language, as long as I feel they are appropriate for the job.

I'm specializing in Adobe Flex and I'm currently developing an enterprise application based on pureMVC/AppFuse.
