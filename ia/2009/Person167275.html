Mohamed Hashish’s Summary

Sine 2004, I had graduated from Helwan University, Faculty of Law with a general degree of good then I admitted to the Egyptian Lawyers Bar Association.

I handled legal positions at many entities and currently, I am working with Al Kamel Law Firm ‘‘one of the most prominent Law Firms in Egypt’’. At Al Kamel we are working as big team in respect of standards of the international law firms.

Recently, I have an individual participation of updating the Arab legislations database at the Programme on Governance in the Arab Region (the "POGAR") which launched by the United Nations Development Programme (the "UNDP").

My databases updated includes to the banking laws during the year 1920 until present (109 legislations), the investment laws during the year 1952 until present (160 legislations), the capital market laws during the year of 1882 until present (132 legislations), the financial oversight laws during the year of 1942 until present (49 legislations), the parliamentary laws during the year 1923 until present (78 legislations), the Egyptian Anti-corruption Laws (95 legislations), the Algerian Anti-corruption Laws (13 legislations), and the Bahraini Anti-corruption Laws (43 legislations).

Also, I am the owner of www.our-law.com. Currently, it have been under construction which includes to the biggest database on the domestic levels in more than 111 countries in the world, in particular Intellectual Property Rights, Telecommunication Laws, Banking Laws, Capital Market Laws, Investment Laws, and the others legislation concerning the financial oversight. However, I hope this will be published soon for free.
