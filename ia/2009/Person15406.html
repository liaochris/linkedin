Andy Siddiqi’s Summary

An experienced and adaptable 3rd generation Project Manager with a proven track record of successful projects achieved in a variety of industry sectors.
Delivery focussed with experience of structured project management and iterative techniques. This ranges from requirements gathering through to design & build and release into a live operations. Reporting to senior management and being able to influence corporate decisions at board level.
An excellent communicator, articulate, confident and sensitive to different cultures and customs, supported by drive and initiative and a goals-oriented approach, and is ideally seeking a role that would be focussed on IT strategy that would prove to be both a personal and professional challenge.
