    Direct Dialogue Fundraising

    (Privately Held; Fund-Raising industry)

    May 2003 — December 2003 (8 months)

    At the former Direct Dialogue Fundraising (DDF), currently known as Emolife, I have worked in 2003 as a direct dialogue recruiter for donations, team captain for Oxfam Novib. I was responsible for the recruitment of donators for Oxfam Novib, where I approached people by means of open dialogue, also known as ‘direct dialogue’. Team spirit, as well as working towards a common goal, were particularly important factors which frequently resulted in a strong bond between colleagues and an extremely pleasant working environment. For me, the foundation was laid at DDF for my future independent entrepreneurship and my activities as a fundraiser within the non-profit sector.
