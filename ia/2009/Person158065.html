William McKibbin’s Summary

I am a consulting financial engineer specializing in risk analysis, stochastic modeling, pro forma forecasting, simulation, optimization, spreadsheet modeling, auditing, algorithmic programming, statistical analysis, and litigation support. I have been in professional practice serving companies in various capacities as consultant, analyst, auditor, programmer, and trainer since 1998.
