Vickery Eckhoff’s Summary

Senior B2B/B2C marketing copywriter with expertise in writing branding, concepts, content and marketing collateral for the financial services, food/wine, healthcare, fashion/luxury, media, packaged goods, technology and travel categories.

Extensive experience working on both client and agency side handling projects ranging from conceptual/fun to super brainy (I wrote all content and promo for the first two New York Times Travel Shows, and both the policy holder and business sections of Virgin's HealthMiles Plus website).

I'm happy developing creative strategies, concepts and copy from the bottom up or refining copy you’ve already got. And I'm a total girl scout when it comes to working with clients, teams and scheduling.

Vickery Eckhoff’s Specialties:

Reaching educated consumers and executive-level business decision makers through high-quality integrated media.
