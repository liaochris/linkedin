Seema Sreenivasan’s Summary

Interior design manager with over 12 years Indian and international experience in hospitality, retail, mixed-use developments, pharmaceutical, commercial and luxury residence sectors.

Set up interior design and execution teams from boutique design houses to large hospitality and mixed-use developers.

Worked seamlessly with completely in-house teams as well as totally outsourced design resources from all over the world.

Defined design goals, shortlisted and selected designers from among the biggest firms worldwide and worked in and with hospitality industry leaders such as HBA, Wilson Associates, Aedas, WS Atkins, Trident, Sheraton & Rotana.

Have managed complete turnkey projects from design to delivery.

Significant entrepreneurial experience. Specialize in delivering projects on time and within budget, and in crisis management.

Seema Sreenivasan’s Specialties:

Design, design management, project management, vendor development, material sourcing, crisis management.

  • Al Ghurair City, Dubai, UAE

    (Privately Held; 10,001 or more employees; Commercial Real Estate industry)

    April 2006 — April 2008 (2 years 1 month)

    At AGC, I was single-handedly responsible for establishing design guidelines and processes, including group brand identity, for both the premium and the budget hospitality categories. I setup processes for defining design requirements, selecting the designer from internationally reputed design houses, managing the design process, managing the contractor and vendor selection processes, local and international procurement, project management, through handing over to the operations team and supporting them after launch.

    I handled five projects in two years, completing two on time and under budget, with the other three on schedule. I lead a twin tower mixed-use project, with 420 hotel keys, 126 apartments and three floors of high-end retail in downtown Dubai, where the operator is Rotana and the interior designer is Wilson Associates.

    In addition, I handled design and project management for all the Al Ghurair family palaces and yachts.

  • Allegrato FZ LLC, Dubai, UAE

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Design industry)

    September 2005 — March 2006 (7 months)

    I was the consulting designer for Allegrato for the relaunch of a UAE-wide furniture retail chain. The client is a large furniture retail chain operating in the unorganized retail sector, without clear branding or product design strategies. The client wanted to move into the higher-margin branded designer furniture segment, to compete with chains like One and Aati. Allegrato's scope of delivery covered the entire relaunch - from corporate identity to product design and retail strategy.

    I was part of the team that worked on the initial strategy, from corporate identity to product mix. I also developed innovative retail programmes that will serve to differentiate the chain and build a loyal customer community.

    In addition, I was responsible for designing the retail interior style and defining the corporate identity standards for interior spaces, as well as managing the entire project, including site management and co-ordination with the product design team.
