Davide d'Atri’s Summary

As main occupation Davide d'Atri is CEO and founder of BeatPick.com, a 3 years old music licensing agent showcasing over 200 artists/bands based in 40 different countries.

Davide also teaches new music strategies in 2 universities, providing insight regarding Creative Commons licensing and focusing on development of business strategies around such a licensing system as well as investigating new music strategies.

As a freelance he has worked for several private companies and associations, successfully leading short and mid term projects. He has taken care of raising funds mostly through public fundings and state aids, assembling and managing teams in order to complete short term business projects, organizing the financial aspects as well as the operative and practical aspects of such projects.

Davide has recently launched a new start up named SoundReef.com
