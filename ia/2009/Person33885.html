  • Contract Artist

    Bungie

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Computer Games industry)

    March 2000 — August 2000 (6 months)

    I was responsible for creating the many level of detail models used for characters, vehicles, weapons, and scenery objects. I also created models and textures for environment objects that are used throughout the game. To vastly improve my productivity, I created a Max Script that automates the process of calculating the target polygon counts that will be used for each LOD so I don't have to do it by hand. Without my help, some features might have been left out and Halo may not have shipped on time for the launch of the Xbox. My previous work for Bungie includes:

    Halo (Xbox)
