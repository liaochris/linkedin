Arthur Corliss’s Summary

"Adapt, improvise, and overcome." I can't entirely take credit for the amalgam of skills I've acquired over the years, since from the Marine Corps to my involvement in the Open Source and Linux communities they've taught me most of what I've needed to achieve the success I have had to date. From leveraging retired hardware and open source software to produce mission-critical services while at GCI, to engineering, deploying, developing, and maintaining systems and network infrastructure for managed IP services at AT&T Alascom, my greatest satisfaction is in creating solutions under the most adverse conditions.

In a nutshell, that's what I am: a builder. I enjoy the challenge of evaluating a problem and finding a solution for it -- even building it, if need be.
