Al Rey’s Summary

Al Rey recently worked in Corporate Solutions at LinkedIn.

Al served on the board of the National Society of Hispanic MBAs San Francisco Chapter. Al is very proud to have negotiated the first ever contract between the National Society of Hispanic MBAs and a graduate school of business in the state of California. He also assisted with the monthly editorial and corporate relations. Al received his MBA from the University of San Francisco Graduate School of Management, where he earned Dean’s List Honors. Additionally, he is the recipient of the National Society of Hispanic MBAs Scholarship. He also was the recipient of the Cardinal Health Inc. MBA Scholarship. Al also holds a BA from the University of California at Santa Barbara. Additionally, Al completed a Stanford Graduate School of Business Executive Education Program.

Prior to graduate business school, Al spent time in corporate business development roles with Morgan Stanley Online, Charles Schwab Corp and E*Trade Financial Corp. At Charles Schwab Corp, Al received two Excellence in Business Development and three Chairman's Club awards. Most recently, Al was selected as an MBA panelist speaker for the National Society of Hispanic MBAs Lead Conference, a contributor for the Hobsons' Hispanic MBA Guide and as an E-Mentor for the IBM National Talent Mentoring Program.

Al enjoys volunteering for the Make-A-Wish Foundation, Habitat for Humanity, Boys & Girls Club of America, IBM National Talent Mentoring Program and the National Society of Hispanic MBAs.

Al's goal is to continue to seek out challenging positions that will broaden his perspectives and enhance his skills.
