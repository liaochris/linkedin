Pierre Michel Combaluzier’s Summary

Pierre Michel Combaluzier is a multilingual marketing expert in smart card industry, loyalty program for Credit Card and interactive communications media with more than twenty-two years of experience in smart card applications and commercial general public launching, Marketing business intelligence and public launching of brand new or commercial electronic products. The author has proven himself to be capable of working with executive teams in new ventures. He is skilled in effectively imagining, conceptualizing, creating, assembling and integrating new marketing concepts into turnkey platforms, especially for European (UK, France, Belgium, Switzerland, Italy, Spain) and US markets. Combaluzier also speaks French, English and understands Italian, German and Spanish.

One of the passionate of the Age of napoleon since his young time , he was enthroned as a fellow member of the International Napoleonic Society in Montreal for his action in August 2001 in the legion of members chaired by Mr. Ben Weider, famous writer defending the case of the assassination of Napoleon by his goalers in Saint-Helena.

Expert Consultant for the French Government in order to create innovative collector’s items during the celebration of the 200th anniversary of the French Revolution on January 1989. He was one of the main editor/promoter in charge of the creation and development of the natural mass phenomenon for preserving prepaid phonecard collection in the philatelist‘s collector market.
