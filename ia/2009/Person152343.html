Mark Nozeman’s Summary

Mark Nozeman (1971) is based in the Netherlands (Amsterdam), Graduated BFA at the St. Joost Academy of Fine Arts Breda on photography (cum laude) and History HBO (prop) in Rotterdam. After a period working in the IT Business (Check CV) he decides to choose for creative photography related industry and became self-employed.

Main field of work is documentary photography,developing of projects & concepts and education.Former projects like Últimas Estórias Exhibition, a project on the interior of Brazil for instance, are exhibited at the National Museum of Ethnology (RMV) Leiden (NL) 2002, Tropical Museum (KIT) Amsterdam 2007 and SESC Pompeia São Paulo (BR) 2006.Publications, reviews on the internet, European papers, magazines and catalogs.

Mark Nozeman considers his self as a crossover artist/ Documentary photographer with a strong social awareness and commitment. Shaping and developing personal style & vision on photography while exploring the dialogue between arts & commercial works & editorials.Besides using the range of traditional platforms for photography,web based Multimedia is a growing creative solution as a virtual environment.

"In addition to free-lancing in the creative/education industries, I am working on long term photo projects about the relationship between individual identity and home ground in order to make a comment on the so-called shifting (European) identity of the young emerging generations in post conflict regions to disclose the ordinary and the concealed.

Limited Archival art prints are obtained for different companies and private collections.Since 2008 selected photographic works are under courtesy of the Amsterdam based Kahmann Gallery formerly know as HUP Gallery (check box websites)

Furthermore, portfolio on request and get in touch with photo agency Maartje Wildeman for assignments and editorials.
