Bernard Vanderlande’s Summary

Bernard Vanderlande leads the search and consulting teams of Tula International, an Atlanta (Georgia, U.S.A.) based firm whose clients are prominent in Renewable Energy and Sustainability Manufacturing industries.

In the firm’s Retained Executive Search practice, Vanderlande and his team help clients fill strategic management positions, such as Chief Sustainability Officers, Chief Corporate Social Responsibility leaders, Senior innovation and marketing professionals, Global procurement and supply chain executives , Technology and development leaders, Corporate environment, health and safety leaders, Educators and academic research professionals

The company’s Consulting Practice assists green start-ups meet their capitalization, planning and marketing needs.

Bernard Vanderlande’s Specialties:

Having worked in Europe, Asia, the Middle East, Africa as well at the United States, Vanderlande has a unique ability to understand his firm’s clients' critical business needs.
