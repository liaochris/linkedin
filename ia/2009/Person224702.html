Steven Waldren’s Summary

I am Board Certified in Family Medicine. I hold a Masters in Health Informatics from the Univ. of Missouri. I have helped the AAFP increase the adoption of electronic health records in their membership for 10% to 40%+ over a 3 year period. AAFP members lead all physicians in the rate of EHR adoption at over 40%. (all physicians - 10-20%).

Beside EHR adoption, I direct and manage all activities around health-IT standards development and advocacy.

Goals: (1) to drive the adoption of health-IT in physician offices to help those practices deliver the highest quality care possible; (2) to increase the patient's access to health information in a form they can use to be an active partner in their care; (3) to drive general computer industry practices and standards into health care to help the health care industry to catch up its 10 year plus lag in IT tools and services.
