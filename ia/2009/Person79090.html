Franck Giroudon’s Summary

Franck Giroudon, Chief Operating Officer of the Nexway Group and Head of the Services Division.

Franck Giroudon serves as Chief Operating Officer. He is a member of the Board and reports into Gilles Ridel, Nexway's Founder and CEO.

In his role, Franck is a Performance Leader with strong Business Development / Marketing, Sales, Operational and Analytical skills.

Prior to this, Franck was Vice President Marketing and Managing Director, responsible for the French P&L. He served one year as Marketing Director.

Before joining Nexway in 2006, he worked for 6 years at Packard Bell, in his last position as Head of the Internet Sales Business Unit.
Franck served as European Brand Manager at Infogrames (ATARI) between 1997 and 2000.

Franck is graduated from the EM Grenoble (1997).

He is married and has three children.
