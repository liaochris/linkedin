Thomas Philp’s Summary

Thomas has worked for 500 fortune companies alternating the roles of art director and graphic designer on a daily basis providing brand solutions, communication design, and expert level understanding of graphic design applications.

Thomas has an appeal for and is constantly chasing the ever changing trends of contemporary design but more importantly how to infuse future ideas of branding and marketing into his clients emotionally and with a BANG! effect. Thomas' ideal situation is where he can use all of the skills he has acquired over time as a designer and as a production artist; but he also says that knowing how to listen to clients, responding well to direction, and maintaining good relationships can create even greater situations and be ever-rewarding.”
