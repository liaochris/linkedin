John Gravely’s Summary

Entrepreneur/Leader/Marketer. In late 2001 I co-founded c360 Solutions, a Microsoft CRM-based Independent Software Vendor. Leveraging offshore resources in Bangalore India to avoid the need for outside capital we built c360 to a multi-million dollar organization in the Microsoft Dynamics ISV (Independent Software Vendor) channel. We then sold the company to Chinese software conglomerate CDC Software, a subsidiary of China.com.
