David Drake’s Summary

David Drake, founder and Chairman of LDJ Capital in New York City, has been involved in the Media, Luxury Products, Energy and Project Financing and Real Estate services and financing for over 20 years.

Mr. Drake's proclivity into Media and Luxury Products intercorrolate directly with the luxury segments of real estate investment strategies and clients of LDJ Capital.

Born and raised in Vasteras, Sweden, Mr. Drake holds Bachelor's Degrees in both Chemistry and Marketing from the American U. in Washington, D.C. Mr. Drake holds an MBA in Finance and an MA in International Law and Economics from George Washington U. where he was awarded the Wallenberg Scholarship for academic merit.

After completing his education, Mr. Drake worked on Wall Street in Institutional Sales for Alfred Berg, working in both the New York City, and Stockholm, Sweden offices.

In 1996 Mr. Drake joined the NY real estate market as a Senior Director in commercial real estate at Prudential. Today this includes ownership in firms involving: loss mitigation, non-performing notes, tapes distribution, developing properties, property management and bridge financing, hard money lending and realty financing for income producing retail, hospitality and office products.

Mr. Drake hosted the Harvard Club and Boston Symphony performances at his house and sits on the advisory board of Best Buddies Charity for autistic kids. Mr. Drake has been behind raising $250,000 in charity donations through his produced Carnegie Hall concert series.

Mr. Drake now has ten divisions housed in numerous NYC office locations with head quarters at One Penn Plaza. The investment deal flow ranges from $30 million to $2 billion. His network of accessing financing extends all continents from hedge funds, high net-worth investors, Sovereign Wealth Funds and private equity firms, family offices, money managment funds to institutional pension funds and fund-of-funds.

David Drake’s Specialties:

LDJ Capital Partners is a boutique advisory firm focused on companies and assets in the luxury, commodities, real estate, and media / entertainment industries.

Although each situation is evaluated individually by the LDJ Capital team, we are generally able to provide customized assistance with business development, board building, technology, marketing, operations, and a wide range of financing issues.
