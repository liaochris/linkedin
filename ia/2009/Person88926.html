Graham Wallington’s Summary

I have a passion for broadcasting.
I pioneered the LIVE broadcasting of wildlife in 1998 on the Internet with the formation of AfriCam.com. This was a very exciting time to be the founder of an Internet company which held the honour of being the busiest website in Africa for 6 years (read about it at www.africambook.com).
After AfriCam went insolvent (in the dot com meltdown) I created two TV channels. First was ProperTV, that showcased residential real estate opportunities and issues, and then later Business Television Network (BTN), which was an 8 hour per work day LIVE listed business information channel aimed at traders and fund managers.
I then went back into wildlife TV and produced and directed numerous documentaries, films and inserts for ORTV, National Geographic, Animal Planet, 50/50 and Carte Blanche.
I realised however, that the time had once again come to create my own brand and to follow my first love of LIVE wildlife broadcasting and so WildEarth was born.

Graham Wallington’s Specialties:

I suppose my specialties are: vision, management, motivation, problem solving and tenacity. I also like to think that I have an eye for content and can usually find animals and wildlife content when it is needed. I am inspired to lead a team of committed passionate people who don't just want a job, but rather have a burning desire to change the world.
