David KINNEAR’s Summary

+ Recognized thought-leader, ground-maker and momentum-builder in the global sourcing sector

+ C-level management strategist & adviser concerning business growth & value enhancement through global sourcing & services globalization

+ Globally-active brand-builder and rainmaker, leveraging extensive worldwide network of private & public sector relationships

+ Polished networking, relationship development & presentational skills - articulate & persuasive communicator at C-level

David is a sought-after adviser, strategist & speaker on global sourcing. He draws on 20+ years of international management experience and a wealth of relationships in both the private and governmental sectors worldwide.

David is President & CEO of Lisnagol Resources and Lisnagol Ventures. See: www.lisnagol.com / www.lisnagolventures.com. He is a strong advocate of global services sourcing in the private sector and the "win-win" socio-economic & philanthropic opportunities this can create for both clients & destination countries.

David is a Founder of The Global Sourcing Council (GSC). The GSC is a global business forum addressing both the emerging business issues and also the socio-economic implications of global sourcing. See: www.gscouncil.org

David is a Founder of The Philippines BPO Council, a voluntary, public-private collaboration. In addition, David is a Board member of the Philippine-American Chamber of Commerce (PACC) - see: www.philamchamber.org

David is the US Race Liaison Officer for the Rona Trust Atlantic Challenge (2009), helping The London Sailing Project in raising funding and logistical support for its participation in the Trans-Atlantic Tall Ships' Race in 2009 - see: www.ronatrust.com

David lives in New York with his wife Katherine Sinsabaugh who is a professional violist and music educational consultant – and their daughter.

David KINNEAR’s Specialties:

Global sourcing. International trade & development. Socio-economic impact of global trade. Onshore / Offshore Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) & multi-shore Human Capital deployment. Particular experience with global services solutions and expertise in regard to newer, emerging markets, such as the Philippines, China and South Africa.
