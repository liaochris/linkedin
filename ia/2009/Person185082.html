Instructor in machine vision technology and application. Also instruct in scuba diving, first aid, and CPR.

Perry West’s Experience

  • President

    Automated Vision Systems, Inc.

    (Industrial Automation industry)

    February 1981 — Present (28 years 8 months)

    Automated Vision Systems, Inc. (AVS), is the leading consulting company specializing in machine vision. Founded in 1981 with the birth of the machine vision industry, Automated Vision Systems was the first company dedicated to supplying services and support for machine vision activities.

    AVS helps clients identify significant opportunities and execute successful solutions. We recognize machine vision holds the key to solving many special manufacturing process and automation needs, and provide services to solve application needs, to develop business opportunities, and to transfer know-how through education and training. We work with clients to provide just the right support and solutions they need to meet their opportunities.
