Armando Liussi Depaoli’s Summary

Executive with international experience as a General Manager.

"Alive to rethink realities"

At Bridged World, S.L., International Consulting & Interim Management, we provide strategic consultancy and marketing services to domestic companies in their internationalization process and help foreign companies to become established in Spain.

As former high-ranking executives with extensive experience in international markets; we help create added value to your company, as we have done for other well known multinational companies. Thanks to our global vision of the business world, we manage to provide highly effective solutions.
We serve a wide range of sectors in various markets.

We deliver tangible, fast results and sustainable growth. We provide turn around management, new business model advisory and marketing and sales execution. We offer the knowledge and experience of those with a proven track record in providing successful, lasting competitive advantages. We also guarantee the effort of the group with an independent criterion.

As consultants, we help companies develop specific plans on critical projects, with constant assessment, identifying opportunities, building new capabilities, stretching potential, assessing initiatives, redesigning organizations and putting in place systems to follow up and monitor the execution.

As Interim Managers, we involve ourselves in the company’s daily operation, managing as another member of the organization but with a cost and flexibility not recognized in a payroll executive. From the start-up of a new business to the management of a particular transaction, we assume CEO and Managing Director positions, as well as Marketing, Communications, Sales, Operations and Country Managers. Our interim management platform offers an excellent service of high profile executives to deal with delicate transitional situations at the time you need us.

Available worldwide, we go wherever, whenever you need.
