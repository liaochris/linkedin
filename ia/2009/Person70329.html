Eelco Aartsen’s Summary

Eelco is an experienced, enthusiastic, all round IT consultant, specialized in Java and Oracle. He worked as a developer, designer, project leader and (Java) architect. He likes to program, loves to design systems, can lead a team, has a good sense of responsibility and is honest. Producing a clear and maintainable product is his main objective. He is interested in new tools, methods and techniques, and has the right experience to judge and apply them in a manageable way. He has the capability to explain complex matters to colleagues and management in an understandable way. He prefers leading technical roles, especially in combination with some hands-on work for himself. He has experience in working with multicultural teams.

Eelco is Dutch, born in 1966 and has a Masters degree in Computer Science.
