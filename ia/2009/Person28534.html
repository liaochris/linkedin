William Hesch’s Summary

Located conveniently in Oakley Square, William E. Hesch Law Firm, LLC, helps clients develop and implement viable estate plans, and advises entrepreneurs in the formation, growth, buying and selling of their businesses.

Attorney Bill Hesch’s background as a CPA and Personal Financial Specialist (PFS), former Big 4 tax partner, and former CFO of a multi-million dollar company makes him uniquely qualified to approach clients’ situations from legal, accounting, and tax perspectives– all of which are critical for maximizing tax savings and achieving financial goals.
