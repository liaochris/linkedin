Rodrigo Dauster’s Summary

Rationally intuitive VP-level executive with extensive team building, project development and delivery experience; a team player quick to spot the best growth opportunities and rigorous in making sure they are real and come to fruition.

15 years consumer-facing experience in marketing and Internet technologies from Booz Allen Hamilton, a strategy consulting firm, interactive agencies (Viant, Metrius (a KPMG company) and AGENCY.COM), independent senior advisory consultancy and senior roles at start-ups.

MBA from INSEAD, in France; Masters from Brown University, in the US, in artificial intelligence.

Strong international background – lived in and worked across Europe, in Brazil and the US; speaks fluently English, French, Portuguese and Spanish.

Rodrigo Dauster’s Specialties:

Functional expertise in business strategy, product development, sales, marketing, branding and web design. Deep understanding of current and emerging Internet-related technologies and views on the benefits and risks they present to businesses.
