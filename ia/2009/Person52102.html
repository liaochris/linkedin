Dakota Reese Brown’s Summary

Nimble UX is an outcome-based method of UX discourse that I am developing which employs an internal dialectical logic coupled with an external rhetorical presentation layer.

Nimble UX is further based five operational principles:

1. People react best to instantiations, not abstractions.

2. Transparency is the best process.

3. Prototype on hypothesis, iterate on results.

4. Unnecessary documentation wastes time, money, and social capital.

5. Speed wins: Quick Success > Quick Failure > Long Success > Long Failure
