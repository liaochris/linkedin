Terry Moshenberg’s Summary

Terry is the founder and visionary behind the League Of Rock, the only producer of International music-based team building events. The league partners with professional musicians and famous rock stars.

Terry built a dream team to deliver Music-Based Team Building Events Internationally. The team comprises famous musicians, executives from the music and prof. sports industries, event planning, marketing and finance industries.

This well-sponsored organization gives non-professional musicians the opportunity to jam, workshop with famous musicians and record in world class studios.

Programs Include:
1. Team Building & Corp. Retreats
League Of Rock produces team building events with famous rock stars. Predefined goals and metrics measure results. Employees are charged with building a band, re-writing the lyrics to famous songs, recording and performing. The emotion-packed process is captured on film and presented live on stage with a rock star hosting and providing a blow by blow description. The League Of Rock band performs and everyone enjoys life changing experiences.

2. J.A.B. Sessions (Join A Band)
Get into a band, perform and record a CD all in 10 weeks. The LOR takes care of all the gear, rehearsal space, recording studios and live venues. White glove service and attention to detail ensures all members enjoy a fabulous experience.

3. Full Day Intensive Workshops
Intermediate to advanced musicians are invited to workshop with famous musicians in a hands-on full day event. Workshops are limited to 30 people and the events are intense and packed with fun.

Terry has 15 years in the marketing/communications industry. He developes powerful strategies. He's implemented international marketing solutions, ecommerce and web solutions. The LOR is his next global roll-out.

Terry has experience, vision and deep relationships. He has tenacity and the power to execute.
