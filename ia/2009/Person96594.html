Wendy Coulter’s Summary

HUMMINGBIRD CREATIVE GROUP WON THE 2008 GREATER RALEIGH CHAMBER OF COMMERCE PINNACLE ENTREPRENEURIAL AWARD IN THE 6-10 EMPLOYEE CATEGORY!

As President of Hummingbird Creative Group, a strategic branding agency founded in 1995, I am responsible for new business development and serve as brand manager for all of Hummingbird's clients. I develop brand strategy and oversee all the agency's creative projects. I have over 20 years sales and marketing experience and over 15 years design and branding experience. I speak locally and nationally on strategic branding.
