Steven Toole’s Summary

Toole has more than 18 years of leadership experience in B2B, B2G and B2C marketing. Throughout his career, Toole has held executive level marketing positions with numerous emerging technology firms marketing to the public and private sectors, including immixGroup, INPUT, iCode (now Everest), Precise Software and W. Quinn (both Symantec acquisitions). He has worked in other marketing capacities with Axent Technologies (acquired by Symantec), PeopleSoft (now Oracle), and several other companies.

Steven Toole’s Specialties:

Strategic marketing, government sales and marketing, marketing communications, public relations, demand creation, company positioning and differentiation, Internet marketing, etc.
