Luke Kanies’s Summary

I am a Unix system administrator and developer turned entrepreneur. I have focused my administration career on server automation, and working as a consultant with the current generation of tools convinced me that things could be better.

I am the founder of Reductive Labs, which produces open source server administration tools, mostly focused on centralized, automated administration. Our first product is Puppet, which is a framework for specifying and managing site configurations, rather than focusing on individual servers.

I am actively seeking business and technical partners to help in the growth of Reductive Labs. The system management space is full of opportunity and we need to grow to meet that opportunity without it passing us by.
