Taj Tashombe’s Summary

Impresario of lifestyle marketing, creative innovation and contemporary entertainment is an emerging
|| Renaissance man ||

His ideals can be encompassed with the personal mantra & branding philosophy of:

envision | create | live

Through the onset of visualization, the creation and development of ideas, coupled with the power to utilize and live them, Taj has developed a formula that incorporates the ideal recipe for a tastemaking entrepreneur.

As founder of the sui generis group, a parent company housing a variety of intellectual property assets; Taj has developed business units consisting of lifestyle marketing, creative innovation, and entertainment .

In 2008 the sui generis group will be partnering to manage the releases of a clothing line, an online lifestyle publication, and the introduction a music production company to the general marketplace.

As a Musical Impresario (recording artist, keyboarder, producer) Taj collaborates with many new and established artist, producers, and entertainment executives on new music production, song writing, and vocal arrangements. As a recording artist he plans to release his untitled debut LP in 2009 on his independent record label tsgg entertainment.

In corporate america, Taj has worked with several fortune 100 companies in various marketing roles including Toyota, Hilton Hotels, Macy's, The Oakland Athletics, U.S. Congressional office of Barbara Lee, among others.

Civically, Taj is a board member of CLIMB Inc a community non-profit organization; he is also member of the National Academy of Recording Arts & Sciences (Grammy), ASCAP, Toastmasters, & the Clark Atlanta University Alumni Association, Los Angeles Chapter.

Taj is a graduate of Clark Atlanta University and is based in Los Angeles.
