Christian Fonnesbech’s Summary

Congin is a next generation Internet company, delivering Dramatic Communication Solutions. We make knowledge transfer into an emotional experience.

Congin works across media, primarily on the Internet, creating dramatic and interactive content for communication, learning, HR, sales and marketing.

We have a unique product. Our customers are both national and global companies and organisations - such as banks, telcos, high-tech manufacturers, insurance companies, public organisations, educational institutions, media companies and others.

Congin has a global network of highly specialized subcontractors and talents that cover every aspect of story-based cross media content production.

Dramatic content has been the killer application for all previous media markets. Congin was founded in 2003 to bring Dramatic Content Engineering to the global information society, as it matures around us.

I have 15 years of experience in the entire process of dramatic content creation, from analysis, strategy development and conceptualization to bringing together the right partners and teams, negotiating, producing, delivering and implementing. I have acted as a consultant, reviewer and speaker at conferences across the world, as well as for the EU Commission, for film and tv companies, and for the danish government.

See more at www.congin.com

Christian Fonnesbech’s Specialties:

Dramatic cross media content, delivered to the highest professional and artistic standards.

Our work is full of the spark that all content must have - and it is based on a rock hard foundation of analysis, strategic insight, audience segmentation and business model savvy. Sales pitches, conceptualisation, creative development and production management for story-based entertainment, corporate storytelling, advertising, communication, recruitment and learning across a variety of media.
