Alan Snider’s Summary

I'm a writer who grew up in and around Chicago. I've primarily worked on interactive content, but I've also got experience working on print and television. In addition, I've written about Chicago politics and had the pleasure of working with a number of our city's non-profits. And when I'm not busy with that, I've covered many of the cultural events that happen here in Chicago.

I've got a portfolio of all the different kinds of writing I've done to keep food in my mouth and the wolf from the door at http://chicagowritingservice.com/. I can be funny. I can be corporate ("client-focused!" "proactive!" "kapow!") . And I can be yours for the price of an email.

I have my Master's in English and taught for two years at NIU—so I have plenty of experience editing comma splices and complaining when someone, even me, overuses a dash. Besides that, I've been running a copywriting business for more than four years now.
