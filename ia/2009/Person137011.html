CAREER SUMMARY
Over twenty-nine years of experience in Program/Project Management, Consulting, Information System Development, Strategic and Tactical Systems Planning, System Architecture Design, and System Management in the Legal, Government and Commercial Sectors. I have provided Program and Project Management leadership for system development programs in excess of $5M and have developed e-commerce applications including Knowledge Management Systems, Intranet Portals and Extranets, Document Management Systems, Transaction Production Systems and Research Systems.
