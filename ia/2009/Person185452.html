Peter Crowe’s Summary

CEO and Co-Founder at start-up media/tech business, Life Events Media. We operate the Quotify consumer information and referral network.

Founded in early 2006 Quotify connects consumers and services providers (Pest Controllers, Carpet Cleaners, Accountants, etc) through a proprietary profiling process. With a deep understanding of the needs of both groups we not only deliver exceptional consumer benefits, through real time introductions to a pre-qualified network, we also deliver real value to our trade partners. They pay on a CPL performance basis. The Cost Per Lead (CPL) technology we have developed covers consumer acquisition, profiling, matching, messaging and ratings services.

We deeply understand that across all the customer/suppliers introductions occurring Australia wide there is significant opportunity to increase the yield for ourselves and our partners through the appropriate using of our profiling and matching technology.

Peter Crowe’s Specialties:

My experience covers company, BU and product management and well and business development and sales management with direct experience planning, developing, launching and growing a range of sites including Quotify, Web Search, Local Search (Yellow Pages), Commerce (auctions, shopping and hosted ecommerce apps) and Classifieds (Cars, careers & Real Estate).
