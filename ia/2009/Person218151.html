Simon O'Shaughnessy’s Summary

My focus is achieving personal best for individuals, and companies.
I have a wide creative skill set, gained from 35 years of entrepreneurial work, setting up and running multiple businesses.
So that's why I am an Entrepreneurial Coach; empowering people, projects or products. I've actually 'done it' many times.

My largest venture in the UK turned over approx $14M pa and specialised in design and build in spa resorts. The business changed the UK market and had subsidiaries in retail shops, cosmetic franchisee and medical eyewear.

Other businesses have included art galleries, catering and medical- I practised as a dental surgeon for 8 years.

Since being in NZ I have focused my business skills on developing companies and their personal for others, and I am a trained coach.

Assignments have included;
• CEO of a regional TV station, re staffing and rebuilding it.
• Rebuilding a Charitable Trust, refinancing and setting up educational divisions.
• Negotiations with a group of media companies and shareholders to form a single entity to take its main product to market, which is close to a successful result and US$2.5M sale to China.
• Coaching CEO’s and staff through company changes.
• Taking a company, with new technology for the home, to market in NZ.

My aim is to deliver measurable results for the business, with rapid changes in strategic and personal levels to make for success.
