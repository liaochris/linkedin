Ailing Cai’s Summary

Creative Director, Web Communications Manager.
With over 10 years experience in a Web Manager role at advertising/Marketing research/ SEO, between Shanghai, Silicon Valley - the U.S. and at Northern Italy. Thanks to this ongoing international exchanges, she has achieved a professional experience in Internet and interactive multimedia design.

In 2004 her blog (www.goodmorning-italy.com) goes on to win secondo prize in the International Blog competition, at the Sohu.com in Bejing. In 2005 she goes the ‘Excellent site’ in the Web design competition at the ‘Web è Donna 2005’ event in Viareggio, Italy (www.patt.tn.it ) and at the ‘Italia Web Awards 2005’, Italy (www.autoscuolatorrevanga.com). In 2007 goes the ‘Excellent site’ at the "Il sole 24 ore - Premio WWW, Italy" (www.goodmorning-italy.com).

In her free time she paints and writes poems and stories, participating in art exhibitions at galleries Trento, Rovereto, Bolzano and Verona. In 2007 she was invited to perform the international festival culture "Dis_Oriente" in Gorizia, Friuli Venezia Giulia Italia, and one of his stories was published in a book collective "cuori di donne" - Publisher Nicolodi Rovere, Italy. Her blog of poems and photographs in Chinese and Italian (www.goodmorning-italy.com) is visited every months from about 600 Italian and many Chinese.

She was born in Shanghai (China).
For five six years living and working in Italy.
