Ameen Aziz’s Summary

A highly motivated and ambitious recruiter and sales person, who thrive in a high paced, proactive and results-oriented environment.

I have proven ability identifying and engaging key decision makers at VP and Director level, gained over 6 years of international business-to-business sales experience in both public and privately owned enterprises.

I started my career in sales of business-to-business directory services, providing technical support and troubleshooting internet and network connectivity issues as well as assisting with IT quality assurance; I subsequently moved on to sales, staffing and recruitment process outsourcing solutions and executive search services within the Information Technology market servicing clients in India, Middle East, Eastern Europe and Southeast Asia and now Canada. My Canadian experience includes providing executive search services to both private and public companies as well as the Federal Government of Canada.
