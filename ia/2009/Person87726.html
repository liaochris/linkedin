James Filbird’s Summary

JMF International Trade Group Ltd. is a multi-disciplined Import/Export company. We provide a comprehensive range of services for medium to large-sized enterprises. Our core services include: Business Migration, Product Sourcing, QC Inspection and Assembly, Project Management, and Solutions-Oriented Business Consulting.

We are American-owned and China-based, located in Shenzhen, China with a global network of partners that give us the experience and capacity to help you get the results you’re looking for. We have in-depth knowledge and experience in sourcing a broad range of products, i.e., hi-tech fabrication, LED, software development, consumer electronics, apparel, retail packaging and promo items.

Our strong business relationships in North America and throughout Asia can help your company meet its manufacturing or technology outsourcing needs cheaply and with surprising efficiency. With over 7 years of experience working with factories and doing business in Asia, we have the capability to develop sound solutions for your overseas business requirements.

We provide an array of specialty services for inventors of new products. Whether you’re a company that already has a product and need it sourced; want to develop a new product and need it designed, tooled and prototyped; or want to introduce a new, previously unreleased product and need a “team partner” to bring it to mass market, we can help you. In short, we can arrange and manage all you need to get your product designed, prototyped, mass produced, sold and shipped while providing superior customer service, protecting your IP, and helping you maximize profit potential.

Doing business in China is very challenging because of the distance, business culture, and language barriers. This is why it is so important to work with a trusted representative and professional Western contractor to look after your own interests. Working with JMF gives you the peace of mind you need to do business in China.
