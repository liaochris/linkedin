Nico Macdonald’s Summary

Worked as an IT consultant with magazine and newspaper publishers and design studios. Learned how to create working systems that people could use based on commercially available tools.

Grew into developing publishing strategies for publishers moving online but also retaining print publications. Developed models for helping print designers learn about interaction design for the Web (and other screen-based platforms). Worked as director of digital design in a major design consutlancy, and design strategist for a major newspaper.

Developed writing careers around design and technology, with a focus on interactive media and tools, and around social and business aspects of these activities. Also developed event programming capability, and public speaking skills (including broadcast).

Built business strategy skills as a founding partner in an Internet-focused business consultancy, working with diverse clients in the UK and abroad.

Lead self-initiated research projects funded by commercial sponsors. Focused on substantial writing projects, including books and heavily researched book chapters.

Developed role in SME consulting in a semi-management role. Built skills in bringing together, managing and inspiring remote teams for Web development projects.

Goals include: creating, through research, writing, events and broadcasting, a richer understanding of innovation processes; facilitating innovation around IT in organisations; identifying barriers to innovation; creating networks to facilitate development of knowledge and improving professional practice.
