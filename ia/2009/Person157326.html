Howard Arnold’s Summary

I provide perspective and guidance to move a company to its next stage of growth. Based on my experience as an entrepreneurial visionary I identify and create business opportunities that will maxmize corporate value by maximzing revenue opportunties while streamling the company's operation to expand margins.

A proven chief executive and business strategist I was a founder of two software products and professional services companies. One resulted in an IPO and the other was sold to an intenational consulting firm. I also founded and managed a boutique investment management and strategic consulting firm focused on emerging technology companies.

Howard Arnold’s Specialties:

Corporate growth planning and execution primarily for professional services, application software development and delivery and preparation assistance for the sell side of acquistion activity.
