Carrie Masters’s Summary

I am a mixed media artist. I use photography blended with elements of digital manipulation, and digital drawing. I own the company Robot Luv. My designs have been seen on tshirts sold at Forever 21, Virgin Megastores,and Nordstorm. My tshirts have been worn by Miley Cyrus. Currently I am working with Palmer Snowboards and Shoes. Robot Luv line of 10 styles of women's shoes have been extremely well received in Japan. My goal is to offer Robot Luv's images to more top companies in Licensing deals.
