Maritza Parra’s Summary

Training and speaking about the Applying the Principles of Self-Empowerment is one of Maritza's passions. She leads several groups in San Antonio and is the founder of Our Law of Attraction Podcast mentioned during her interview with Oprah Winfrey for Oprah's XM radio Show, "Oprah & Friends: The Soul Series" in May 2007.

She's integrated these principles into her inspirational and experiential workshop, The Equine Experience. This is an equine facilitated workshop using horses. These beautiful and perceptive animals help people overcome doubt, fear, and negative emotion, the biggest barriers to personal success.

While coaching her clients, a recurring theme for every single one of them is creating more Abundance. Each one of them had specialized knowledge or a part of their business that is perfect for Online Marketing. So she began Easy Online Marketing specifically to help her students use principles of the Law of Attraction and inspired ACTION.
