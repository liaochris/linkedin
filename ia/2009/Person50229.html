Craig Snodgrass’s Summary

I have two main goals I am focusing on -

1. How to incorporate strong process management and focus within an organization so that it adds impactful business value. I am tired of process for the sake of process. I want to show how process can create a competitive advantage and impact the bottome line.

2. On a more personal but still business level, I am focusing on taking ideas to execution (i2ex). I want to learn what it takes to create a product and sell 1000 individual orders. I figure if you can learn how to sell 1000 items, you can sell 1MM. And the more practice, the more efficient and better ideas that will evolve. In the beginning, its not all about making money. It is about building my platform of knowledge so when I do recognize or have a great idea, I will know what to do.
