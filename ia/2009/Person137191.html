Lars Johansson’s Summary

Engineer/Project Manager in Telecommunication and Media, with over 11 years of industry experience.

For the last 8 years worked with IP telephony and before that with mobile telephony. Highly skilled in (global) VoIP and IPTV services, from a technical as well as commercial perspective. Previously worked mainly as software developer, but have been involved in all areas of product development.
