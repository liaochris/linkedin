Lizzie Azzolino’s Summary

A driven, enthusiastic student at the University of Georgia, I will graduate in May 2009 with an ABJ in journalism/public relations and certificates in new media and leadership advancement.

I thrive on staying busy and have been actively involved on campus, serving as president of the PRSSA, on the founding executive board for IABC, as a Grady College Ambassador, as the student representative on the Grady Alumni Board and with various leadership positions within my sorority. A number of internships in PR and marketing have provided a broad experience in traditional and social media PR writing, brainstorming, event planning and pitching.

While I enjoy the field of communications as a whole, social media has become a strong suit of mine, having taken a number of word-of-mouth elective courses and served on the planning committee of Grady College's Connect PR & Social Media conference. A self-proclaimed Twitter addict, I continue to learn online skills daily by reading and participating in the blogosphere. Most recently, I worked on a team to write a social media "field guide" for Jackson Spalding employees.

I remain dedicated to scheduling "me" time - advancing my passion for fitness, pop culture and expanding my way too large shoe collection! I would jump at any opportunity that combines these interests with PR.

Confident that Grady College, along with my leadership experience and internships, have prepared me for the realities of a career in the ever-changing, challenging field of public relations, I am eager to dive into my first job and look forward to committing myself to advancing my career. I am always interested in meeting and networking with professionals in any field of business.
