John Sopher’s Summary

I received a honorable discharge from the Navy having served four years in the submarine force as a sonar technician. After which I found a job in a calibration lab, certifying and repairing industrial test equipment. I worked in the lab for nine years while I worked on an associates in business and continued on for a bachelors in finance. Along with work and school I still managed to start a businesses in rental property. Midway in the pursuit of my Bachelors, I took a hiatus to gather my bearings, and met my wonderful fiance. My fiance and I now own a vending company together and of course I now inspect homes for you.
