Ariella Shoham’s Summary

I have over 13 years experience in international marketing positions, ranging from start-ups to public companies and escalating from marketing communications to corporate level marketing including investors relations. I have managed large scale projects, which included recruiting and managing employees and have lead all company marketing activities throughout my career. In all my positions I have worked directly under the CEO or under the CMO and usually ran the local marketing operation under US based management.
