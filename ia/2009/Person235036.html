Toby Stevens’s Summary

Toby is a Privacy expert with extensive experience in the identification and resolution of policy, technology and ethical issues arising from the management of personal information.

Toby is the Chair of the British Computer Society's Information Privacy Expert Panel. He is a Fellow of the British Computer Society (FBCS), a Chartered IT Professional (CITP), and Member of the Institute of Directors (MIoD). Toby is an expert evaluator for the European Commission's 7th Framework Programme, and a British Standards Institute Committee Member. He is also a member of the CESG Listed Advisor Scheme (CLAS consultant). He sits on the Department for Transport's Road Pricing Advisory Forum.

Toby Stevens’s Specialties:

Management of privacy, identity, data protection and information security, facilitation, presentation, public speaking, training course development and delivery, technical and management report-writing, negotiation and intermediation
