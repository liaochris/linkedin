▓ TEAM BUILDING PROGRAMS ▓

I recommend the best fit program. I will recommend your group one of the following:

◙ Video Team Building ◙
◊ Teams have to work together to create a short video
◊ Teams can write and film whatever they want, within pre-determined guidelines

◙ VolunTeamBuilding ◙
◊ Receive all the benefits of a team building program while also making a difference in the lives of others
◊ Challenges your team skills while reminding employees how it feels to be of service

◙ Scavenger Hunt ◙
◊ Teams are set loose in any city or resort location and seek to answer questions, take photographs and collect objects
◊ Checkpoint activities make our hunts similar to 'The Amazing Race'

◙ Team Olympics ◙
◊ Combination of shorter activities/challenges that are designed to be fun and challenge certain business skills
◊ Done indoors and outdoors anywhere!

◙ Paddling Adventure ◙
◊ While paddling, teams are given tasks to perform that are a challenge of their business skills, such as problem-solving, communication, leadership, and collaboration
◊ A prime learning opportunity and a memorable outdoor experience.

◊ Available in any location worldwide
◊ Suitable for any group size
◊ Fun and engaging
◊ Team oriented
◊ Challenge a group’s team skills.
◊ Designed so that participants must communicate, solve problems, strategize, collaborate, act as leaders, etc.
◊ Can be professionally facilitated
◊ Last anywhere from 2 hours to a full day
◊ Customized and themed
