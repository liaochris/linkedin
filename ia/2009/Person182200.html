Patrick Trant’s Summary

Aircraft Leasing

Specialist in aircraft lease return and delivery holding Power of Attorney, contract negotiations plus aircraft records audit and assessment, in line with EASA, FAA and national regulations. Have worked with leasing companies such as AWAS, GECAS and ILFC. I ensure that the aircraft are introduced into the fleet in an acceptable condition and likewise returned in a cost efficient manner whilst meeting budgets and expectations. Extensive experience in both ACMI and Dry lease operations. I have a resourceful can-do approach that is adaptable to any location and culture.

Project Management

I have worked as a Technical Representative for Base Maintenance inputs which involved component and logistics control plus management of budgets and analysis of critical path, from project inception to finalization. Managed the day to day workload for a fleet of aircraft which included the control of ADD/CADD/OOP's, troubleshooting, technical services, liaising with aircraft manufacturers and vendors. Respond to AOG situations in a timely and enthusiastic manner. Responsible for all aspects of an aircraft operation in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia as sole representative supplying two Boeing 737-300’s to Air Asia in an ACMI capacity. Managed the introduction of two Boeing 737-300’s to Seagle Air in Slovakia on a Dry Lease.

Certifying Aircraft Engineer / Quality Assurance

Have maintained a Line and Base facility both in the UK and overseas. Having previously performed audits to satisfy Part M for IATA and IOSA accreditation. I have held a UK issued CAA Type rated Aircraft Engineers License for the past 13 years and have been fortunate to work on many different types of aircraft all over the world.
