Jerome Pimmel’s Summary

I’m a proficient Grails developer with experience developing several live commercial Grails applications since November 2007. Have also recently obtained Scrum Master Certification.

Frameworks aside, I have a strong belief in developing high quality applications that deliver business value. The most effective way to go about this goal is, i believe, for organisations to possess a clearly stated objective of "being agile", where the organisation itself embraces changes inherent with iterating frequently, testing first and employing close collaboration within cross functional teams when developing software systems.

Prior to Grails have been a first class Java developer with 10 years experience spanning a wide spectrum of applications including autonomic distributed Grid compute applications, Swing GUI based applications as well as numerous Web/MVC type solutions.

Jerome Pimmel’s Specialties:

Agile/XP/Scrum, TDD/TFD, Grails/Groovy, Java, XML, TDD, Selenium, Webtest, FIT, Javascript / AJAX, JSUnit
