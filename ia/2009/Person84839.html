Gene Moy’s Summary

With 12 years of experience in predominantly consumer-facing e-commerce and online retailing websites, I transform insights derived from observations of consumer behaviors within their unique contexts into user experiences of remarkable value for my clients.

I seek leadership opportunities in user experience, interaction design, information architecture, information design, and usability work where my cross-disciplinary knowledge can provide the most impact where users and businesses intersect.

Gene Moy’s Specialties:

User interface design, interaction design, information architecture, branding, corporate identity design, persona design, wireframing, prototyping, server scripting, creative-strategy-technology integration, client-side programming, relational databases, usability testing, qualitative and quantitative analysis.
