Simon Cropper’s Summary

Simon is the co-founder of NoLimits Career Management, Japan. NoLimits Japan provides innovative solutions in Executive Search, Onsite Recruitment Process Management, Executive and Career Coaching, Leadership and Team Development.

Simon’s thrives in developing customer tailored solutions for major organizations to attract, recruit and retain staff. Simon’s passions are motivating people and teams to drive better results and to believe in their own abilities to achieve both personal and career goals.

Simon has extensive experience across Asia Pacific and Japan building and developing teams in multi-national organizations.
