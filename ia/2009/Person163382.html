Michie Madison’s Summary

Michie Madison Manlusoc is a professional promotional model based in Los Angeles, CA. Born in Detroit, MI, educated in Europe and New York City. Michie has studied Fashion Design, Business, and Acting.

With her 4+ years of experience, Michie has worked for a diverse range of products, events, and tradeshows including:
-CES (Las Vegas NV) for Netgear and Microsoft
-Comic Con (San Diego CA) for Zen Press, Haruhi Japanime, Criss Angel, and Tick Tock on Facebook
-ADA (San Antonio TX) for Dentsply
-Natural Products Show (Anaheim CA) for Kahiki Foods
-USGA Championship at Torrey Pines for American Express
-International Surf Competition (Huntington Beach CA) for Lost Energy Drink

Michie was also the Retail Manager for American Apparel's Hollywood Flagship store and one of their in-house ad campaign models.

In her spare time, Michie loves to travel, go hiking, practices yoga, studies acting and dance.
