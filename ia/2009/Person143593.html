Lori Byrd Buckner’s Experience

  • Independent Realtime Court Reporter

    North America, Central America and South America

    (Legal Services industry)

    October 1975 — Present (33 years 11 months)

    Available for depositions, court and jury trials, other forms of verbatim reporting of the spoken word, worldwide. I live in Costa Rica, speak Spanish (2nd language), and am available for work in Central America or South America.
    PREFER: Realtime outputs to your laptop, daily and/or expedited transcript delivery, high-volume cases. NOT afraid of digging into high tech terminology - lots of experience from business litigation to white collar criminal trials ... and all in between

  • Other Court Reporting Services

    Transcription, Scoping, Proofing of Transcripts

    (Judiciary industry)

    1993 — 2009 (16 years)

    TRANSCRIPTION: of legal proceedings such as trials, hearings, depositions, videoconferences, via most all digital media, cassette tapes, videotapes.
    C.A.R.T. : for the hard-of-hearing and deaf - instant translation from steno machine to laptop (for one or two people) or projected onto a wall (for large audiences).
    SCOPING: scope transcripts of other court reporters on an expedited basis at times when I am not reporting myself.
    CAPTIONING: trained but not presently working as a broadcast captioner or off-line captioner

  • Official Court Reporter

    North America

    (Legal Services industry)

    October 1975 — April 1989 (13 years 7 months)

    I worked with mainly one judge for 14 years, doing every aspect of a general jurisdition state circuit court (Brown County, South Dakota, along with 5 other counties that we traveled to). I was one of the first reporters in South Dakota to embrace C.A.T. (computer-aided transcription). I scoped and edited my own transcripts, for 6+-week jury and court trials. ALSO freelanced for depositions, meetings, arbitrations and other verbatim reporting jobs. I am happy to give you names of attorneys and judges that can recommend my services.
