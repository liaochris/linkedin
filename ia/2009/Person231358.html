Dan Bochichio’s Summary

I've been pursuing my career as a Freelance Web Developer and Videographer since 2004, however, I have been involved with building websites for over 10 years now. While in College, I was an intern, and later the Scheduling Manager for Purchase College Television (PTV). As a Manager it was my duty to develop and run a small but efficient team of Interns to maintain a 24/7, seven day, weekly programming schedule. We were also in charge of providing the statement with rich advertisements for campus events & organizations, as well as produce mid-show bumpers.

Since graduating Purchase College in 2008, I've been developing websites for Clients throughout the State of New York. I've made it my practice to offer quick turnaround on projects, outstanding customer service, and great communication skills. It is my goal for each and every project to ensure the final product is exactly how my clients intended it to be. I'm not satisfied unless you are!
