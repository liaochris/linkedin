Shelly DeMotte Kramer’s Summary

Freelance marketing consultant specializing in strategic planning and branding, positioning, new product launches and growing marketshare. Also extremely effective utilizing guerilla marketing and buzz-based brand building tactics. 15+years experience with a wide variety of clients, ranging from entrepreneurs and start-ups to well-established businesses. Clients have and do include fast-casual restaurants, fine dining restaurants and top-level chefs, banks, real estate developers, both commercial and residential, not-for-profit organizations, hotels, manufacturers and internet start-up businesses.
