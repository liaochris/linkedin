Jeff Chenault’s Summary

For the past 16 years, I've had the pleasure of working for some of the most creative companies in the most creative industries in the world. My wide variety of professional experience has brought me from my hometown of Kansas City to sunny Los Angeles, then here to the hustle n' bustle of New York. Throughout my career I've used my strong visual sense, creative problem-solving skills, and just plain raw passion to create unique solutions in design, photography and video.

With "eleven07" I'm finally activating my master plan and bringing it all to the masses... causing my previous freelance clients to weep with joy, and my new clients to order up a celebratory cocktail.

Join me, and let's work together to make this world way less crappy-looking...
