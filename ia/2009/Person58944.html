David Stubblefield’s Summary

I am the Vice President of Sales and Marketing for Applied Scholastics International. I have worked with the organization for the last six years in a number of areas including pilot programs, organizational administration and International expansion of Applied Scholastics.

During this time I have gained a broad understanding of Applied Scholastics Programs and training and the educators that use this study methodology and have used this to help others implement it in to schools, community groups and in the corporate world.

I am expert in the field of educational consulting in regard to the Study Technology developed by L. Ron Hubbard.

As the VP for Sales and Marketing my responsibilities include the management of sales of our training and programs as well as our books and courses. In doing this I work with a lot of different types of people including teachers, tutors, parents and business people to get them information about Study technology and how it will help them in there life or there line of work.

I also oversee Effective Education Publishing the publishing branch of Applied Scholastics International. Parts of my duties in this area are to assist and oversee the many translations projects that we have happening with our books and courses and to ensure that we have the materials for our public.

Lastly I work with others in our organization to market Applied Scholastics International.
