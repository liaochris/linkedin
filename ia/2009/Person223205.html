Stephen McDaniel has 20 years of experience as a teacher, consultant, leader, innovator and author in the fields of business intelligence and data mining. As founder of Freakalytics.com and author of "Rapid Graphs With Tableau Software: Create Intuitive, Actionable Insights in Just 8 Days" and "SAS for Dummies", he helps people understand, present and take action with their data. He has worked with over 50 organizations including: Netflix, UC- Berkeley, EBay, Yahoo!, Duke, Amgen, Fidelity, Target and Microsoft.

When I am not training or consulting with great customers on the cutting edge of analytics with Tableau and SAS, you can find me with my lovely wife on the trails hiking or snowshoeing, visiting wineries, traveling to national parks or just relaxing over a great meal with friends.
