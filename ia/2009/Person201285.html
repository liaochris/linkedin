By providing participants a positive incentive to shop with reusable bags, ReUseIt and its partners are helping protect the earth and to promote sustainable living. Participants who shop with reusable bags at participating retailers earn reward points that can be redeemed for rewards from hundreds of companies. ReUseIt is a sustainable business venture focused on earning an economic profit while helping preserve the earth and contributing positively to the communities in which we live and work.
