Tony Parsons D.Phil’s Summary

Dr Tony Parsons, formerly an Information Analyst in the NHS 18 Week Patient Pathway Programme at Kent and Canterbury Hospital and Lecturer in the Project Management Module for the Foundation Degree in Management (Greenwich University). He is internationally recognised for his high level contribution to innovation in Life Science Research Informatics. Tony has over 20 years experience in the application of information systems, processes and technology to address fundamental problems in Life Science R&D, primarily in the pharmaceutical sector. A highly respected Information professional in the Informatics community he has consistently identified innovative leading-edge solutions and successfully championed, introduced and implemented them in the R&D business process.

Updated 2nd September 2009 by Tony Parsons
