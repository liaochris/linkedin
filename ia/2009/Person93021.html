Hayo Schreijer’s Summary

I am working for government, non-profit and profit (finance) clients, mostly on internet and intranet related projects. Mostly working as project/programme manager where development of websites and knowledge management applications is just a small part of the job. I am focussed on the preparation stage of web- and intranetprojects, helping to formulate strategy, demands and select suppliers. During development I have a projectmanagement role or coach the internal projectmanagers of the customer. Building a well-oiled organistion behind the website for fullfillment, customer management and content management is my specialisation.
