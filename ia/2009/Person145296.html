Luke Bazzard’s Summary

I have consistently established strong and enduring relationships with the most senior business and IT leaders of the organisations that I have worked in. I have had great success in gaining senior executive sponsorship for firm-wide, global risk remediation projects and have a track record of delivering on time and on budget whilst continuing to manage the expectations of senior business stakeholders

• Taking the lead role in a top 5 Bank-wide 2005/08 initiative to transform the firms Operational risk profile (exposure to systemic, IT and external threats). Key aspects of this role are Crisis leadership responsibility of all significant business threatening incidents and delivery of Business sponsorship for a £250M Data centre remediation programme. This role reports to the Barclays EXCO.
• Repositioned the relationship between IT Infrastructure, IT Development and the Business through the introduction of a global relationship Management programme, dramatically improving the Total Cost of ownership of the Infrastructure estate and the predictability of project delivery.
• Provided strategic leadership to transform and improve end-user computing experiences through process re-engineering and a re-factoring of the Global IT support organisation (people, process and technology).
• Transformed underperforming global IT Infrastructure functions (Client support, Networks, Data Centre, Database, Market Data and Platform Management) through the introduction of a structured (best practices) support and delivery methodologies.
• Managed functions of up to 3000 staff.

Luke Bazzard’s Specialties:
