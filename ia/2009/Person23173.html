Ayne Taylor’s Summary

Owner of August Mercer, LLC (est. 1988)* which has two interests: healthcare facility design/development and sales of new & pre-owned cardiology devices & imaging equipment.

Everyday, we strive to do better that which we do best, helping healthcare providers and hospitals in the treatment of their patients. We go to the "heart" of the matter by continually seeking new-to-market devices: electrophysiology, diagnostic & interventional imaging (echo to nuclear), patient monitoring, & peripheral vascular, equipment.
