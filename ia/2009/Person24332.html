Barrington Booth’s Summary

Barrington Booth graduated from Northern Illinois University with honors in May of 2007. In August of 2007 he joined the Harris N.A. Business Banking Development Program which further developed his skills in both accounting and finance while strengthening his analytical and underwriting skills. Upon completion of the program Barrington became a Regional/Central Credit Associate II and most recently became a Commercial Portfolio Manager.

During 2008 Barrington enrolled at the Keller Graduate School Management where he is working towards his Masters in Accountancy and Financial Management with an emphasis towards the CFA certification. Barrington is scheduled to complete the program in December of 2010.

Barrington's future goals include completing his graduate degree coursework along with earning the CFA (I,II & III), CPA and CTP certifications. After accomplishing these goals Barrington would like to begin consulting as well as possibly become an Ad Hoc Instructor at a four year institution in the department of finance.
