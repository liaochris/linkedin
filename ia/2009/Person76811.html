Evelyn Vincent’s Summary

I am a full-time Young Living Essential Oils Distributor. I am passionate about natural living, "Green" living, eliminating chemicals from our homes, foods and environment, complimentary healing practices, creating balance of mind, body and spirit.

I love helping people empower themselves and reclaim the ability to heal our bodies naturally. From household cleaning, to air freshening, perfumes, First Aid and medicinal purposes, cooking and supplementing - essential oils have been a part of living since 4500 BC.

The very essence of a plant, the aromatic liquids distilled from the flowers, trees, roots, and seeds determines the plant’s aroma, and are vital for plants to grow, live, evolve, adapt, defense from insects, environmental conditions, and disease. Today, research shows that when used aromatically, topically, or internally essential oils can calm, energize, balance, purify, cleanse, and rejuvenate the mind, body and spirit.

Aromatherapy, the practice of using therapeutic essential oils, is one of the most powerful and effective modalities I've seen, either alone or in conjunction with other therapies. As a distributor I am a part of the largest worldwide community dedicated to achieving wellness via nature’s purest, most potent and precious gift. We are a wellness company, choosing to focus on the positive, life affirming attributes of our products.

I specialize in helping others to achieve and maintain wellness, harmony and balance in daily life through the simple practices that make their lives and homes truly "Green." As a traditional Feng Shui practitioner, Energy Healer, Labyrinth Facilitator, and native plant Landscape Designer - I bring to my Young Living business a complete sense of the cycle of life and wellness.
