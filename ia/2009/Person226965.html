Susan White’s Summary

In my current position, I manage the day-to-day marketing communications activities of Saint Joseph Hospital, a community hospital serving the Lake View and Lincoln Park neighborhoods of Chicago. I work closely with internal constituents as well as a team of marketing/communications professionals who represent various entities within the Resurrection Health Care System.

In my previous position, I was a Senior Public Relations Specialist in a 3-person PR Department for a national, 1,000 + attorney law firm. I worked with markets and industries across the firm's 19 offices and often traveled to visit attorneys in other offices to talk to them about their practices and learn about how we can best promote them to internal and external audiences.

My day-to-day was varied. I could receive a call from a reporter who would like to interview one of our attorneys or be on deadline for a press release. I could be attending a seminar or retreat or professional industry event. There were days where I coordinated with reporters and editors all day, discussing pitches and article placements. There were other days where I did mostly internal project management, which included interviewing attorneys on a recent pro bono case win or award they received or discussing ideas for a strategic media relations plan.

The nature of my work in the legal industry was creative, people-oriented, regulated and fast-paced.
