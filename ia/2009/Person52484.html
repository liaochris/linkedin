Damon Cali’s Summary

Damon Cali is a Managing Principal at Cali Realty Capital, LLC. The company is looking for commercial real estate development and/or investment opportunities in the Washington, DC and Austin, TX markets.

In 2008, Damon started Ninth Yard, LLC, a web development firm whose first product is Your Neighbor's Place, a site where users can share and discuss photos of their homes.

Previously, he spent six years on NASA's Hubble Space Telescope project as a design engineer and project manager.

Damon earned a BS in mechanical engineering from the University of Colorado and an MBA from the University of Texas at Austin.
