Chris Jones’s Summary

IT STRATEGY & DELIVERY. Experience based on 20+ years delivering technology strategies and solutions from start-ups to Fortune-500 commercial enterprises to the federal government. Competencies include organization design, software engineering, technical architecture, strategic planning, cost management, governance models, frameworks for decision making, and building effective teams.

Sourcing space: continued refinement of best practices in effective outsourcing: looking at risks as a portfolio, and balancing cost savings with the benefits of strategic partnerships.

Education space: supporting initiatives for a statewide ecosystem, creating a functional pipeline for 21c talent among academic institutions across the State; driving increased attention to commercial & economic development aspects; mentoring & speaking.

Collaboration space: driving team-based, cross-functional work methods that enable "co-creation" and cultures of innovation; fostering evolution of traditional KM practices into viable solution sets, with the help of social technologies.

Chris Jones’s Specialties:

Transformational IT, Strategic Sourcing, Collaborative Solutions (CS) & Knowledge Management (KM), Information Architecture (IA), Web Services & Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), Adaptive Governance (AG), Compliance (SOX SAS70, HIPAA, PCI), Business Intelligence (BI), Call Center Integration & Support (CRM); Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
