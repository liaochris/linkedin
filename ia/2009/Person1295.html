Aaron Schliem’s Summary

Leader of Glyph Language Services from its inception in 2001.

Glyph offers comprehensive localization services for web, software & multimedia projects. Our experienced project managers, software engineers, sound engineers and media integrators coordinate to produce seamless global audio/visual content. Clients trust Glyph to ensure flawless quality and appropriate local flavor for their e-learning, games and multimedia initiatives. Partnering with Glyph means impeccable project organization and fast service. Superior sound quality, clean engineering and accurate communication of content and tone are our hallmarks. Support for efficient subtitling and localization of DVD navigation, web deck, packaging and peripherals. Integrated services for full-scale localization, multimedia internationalization and multimedia QA testing.
