Rob Williams’s Summary

After 15+ years managing radio stations across all market sizes, most recently as President & Market Manager for Clear Channel Radio's New York City stations, I left CC in October 2008 to start a new venture as CEO of Goom Radio. Goom Radio will launch in the US later this year....please register to receive updates at www.goomradio.com, and if you're a facebook user, join our group!
