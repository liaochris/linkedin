Ken Gross’s Summary

I manage a small mapping company focused on high-quality design and aesthetics with an eye for detail, legibility, and accuracy. We manipulate and convert GIS data for design work in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign for four-color printing.

My goal is too leverage my experiences into Product Design and Development management in the Location Based Services field and to advance within this field through additional experience and education.
