Ana Fabiano’s Summary

I was the first American hired by the Embassy of Spain’s Commercial Office and became one of the three founders of Wines from Spain. There, I inaugurated many promotional programs and developed key relationships with trade professionals and wine writers at a time when the wines of Rioja, Jerez and Penedes (predominantly known for its Cavas) were the only wine regions of Spain with recognition abroad.

Following my tenure at Wines from Spain, in 1990 I founded SANA Associates, Ltd., a marketing company exclusively dedicated to education and promotion of products from Spain throughout the United States. In that same year, I instituted the first successful field program promoting Spanish wine and spirits to on and off-premise accounts in the U.S.

In 2008 I was appointed U.S. representative for the DOCa Rioja and Brand Ambassador for Vibrant Rioja. I have appeared numerous times on the TV Food Network, and on the radio and in print as an authority on Spanish cuisine and culture.

Ana Fabiano’s Specialties:

My unique training and experience equips me with a keen ability to position Rioja’s extensive portfolio of quality products with U.S. market needs and specific regional market demands.
