Biography :
Pascal Bonnet is EMEA Director of Licensing & Brand Management, at VIZ Media, the leader in the field of manga publishing, animation and entertainment licensing of Japanese content, owned by 3 of Japan's largest creators and licensors of manga and animation, Shueisha Inc., Shogakukan Inc., and Shogakukan-Shueisha Productions.
In that role, he is responsible for expanding VIZ Media character based merchandise's business and managing the brand development in the EMEA territories, negotiating pan-regional deals with leading VideoGame Publishers, Toy Companies, Collectible Publishers. He also coordinates all local licensing agents activities on key brands : Hamtaro, Naruto, Kilari, Bleach, Death Note, etc...
Prior to joining VIZ Media, Pascal was Director of Licensing, US at Ubisoft, in San Francisco where he acquired rights from major Hollywood Studios, to create groundbreaking games : P. Jackson's King Kong, J. Cameron's Avatar, CSI. He started his career in FMCG Marketing at Lactalis, Europe leading Dairy Products Company, then at Bandai France. He graduated in 1991 from EDHEC, a leading French Business School, and is fluent in Spanish, English and French.
