Kevin Goodman’s Summary

I function in the sales, marketing and business development role at BlueBridge Networks, a world class SAS 70 Type II & PCI compliant secure data center. I am a two time recipient of the SBN Pillar Award for Philanthropy. I contribute to BlueBridge Networks success, winning awards such as The COSE 10 Under 10, SBN Rising Star in Innovation Awards, NEOSA 2007 Tech Awards-Best Emerging Company and 2008 Team NEO Asset Creation Finalist.
I am involved in the Business Development and Entrepreneurial arena for over 20 years; growing companies in both the Technology and Construction Industries.
I played a role in two residential construction companies, obtaining both local and national design awards.
I am Interested in emerging e-commerce businesses.
I enjoy serving on committees and boards. Currently, I am the Chairman elect of the Red Cross (BEPA) Business Emergency Planning Association, Strategic Advisory Committee, Cleveland Business Leaders at the Union Club & ARC
