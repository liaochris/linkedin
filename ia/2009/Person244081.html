Stacy Layne Bergsma’s Summary

About Me...

I have been in the office environment since 1985. I have worked with various businesses from the Building and Design industry to Real Estate.

Many times vendors and subcontractors would ask, "Where could I find a person like you for my office?"

This question prompted me in the mid 1990's to start my own business of revamping and computerizing offices for small businesses and setting them up with personnel. While my intentions were to only setup the offices, I became the office manager for two of my clients.

In 2002, I decided to try my hand at selling Real Estate. I sold in the West Los Angeles area for approximately 4-years, before I realized that my passion was more setting up the systems, tracking the escrows and doing the computer work, then going out to sell...sell...sell!

So here I am again, but this time in the virtual office community! This concept offers a win/win situation for everyone. My clients are happy to not have to pay the extra costs involved with in-house assistance, and are pleased that I have great resources at my fingertips of other professionals in my field. And, I love the ability to have refreshing changes in my environment, from the beaches in San Diego, to the rugged beauty of the Pacific Northwest. Who could ask for a more enjoyable business?

Life is good!
