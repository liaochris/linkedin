Steve Cerilli’s Summary

Audio Post production and Sound Design for TV commercials, Documentaries, video games, mobile devices, radio campaigns and audiobooks. Steve Cerilli (owner) is a freelance post production audio engineer / sound designer with over ten years in the field.

Specializing in:
sound design, sonic branding, audio project management, custom sound effects for
broadcast , audio post production editing and mix, voice over and narration, radio and TV post production, audiobook editing / mastering
