Dayna Gonzalez’s Summary

Art has been a passion of mine since I was a wee child. I've been fortunate to follow my dreams and have over a decade of experience as a Lead Animator on productions for film, broadcast, and web, many of which are award-winning television programs. I have additional experience in traditional hand-drawn character, background, and storyboard design. I contract out my compositing skills as an After Effects Artist, and also serve as an animation consultant to various projects. I'm a web designer and seek to combine my animation skills with interactive web design.

I am available for full-time and freelance employment in animation or web design, including direction. I'm willing to consider relocation for the right opportunity.

My Character Animation Reel and Broadcast Design Reel can be viewed online in the portfolio section of my website at www.daynamation.com.
