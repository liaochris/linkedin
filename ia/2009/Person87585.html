Glenn O'Neil’s Summary

In brief; Australian-Swiss, I have managed evaluation, research and communication programs in Europe, Asia, Australia and Africa for the humanitarian and business sectors.

Working with Owl Re and Benchpoint Ltd., I am leading and managing evaluation and measurement projects in Switzerland for large companies and international organisations.

In addition, I am a professor at the International University in Geneva, where I teach courses on communication and media and I am co-editor of the Geneva Communicators Blog.

I have a bachelor of arts (social science) with honours, a masters in communication management and have recently started a PhD (part-time) in social research and evaluation at the London School of Economics.

Glenn O'Neil’s Specialties:

Research & evaluation of communication programs, web strategy and communications, online marketing & promotions, public relations for non-profit & international organisations, cross-cultural communication projects, trainer & lecturer on communication and marketing.
