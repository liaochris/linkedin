Melvin Simpson’s Summary

I'm currently working as a project manager at Ministry of Sound, managing the delivery of a new ecommerce platform and a number of related website projects.

My goal remains a full time position in eCommerce or an internet related business.

Until recently I was Head of eCommerce Operations at zavvi, where I was tasked with developing and executing eCommerce strategies to make zavvi.co.uk one of the leading online stores in UK entertainment.

Prior to that I was a consultant/project manager on various new media projects, including: acting Managing Director for a startup video archive and social networking site; Virgin Digital; Arsenal Football Club; FA Premier League; 2 entertain.

I was involved in two startup internet/technology companies, and before that I spent five years in communication agencies, founding my own in 1997. My early career was in entertainment publishing; music and video.
