Enette Pauzé’s Summary

Dedicated to empowering personal leadership.

Enette is the Executive Director of Spetha Inc., a company dedicated to helping individuals and teams empower themselves and enhance their performance in all areas of life. The Health Services division specializes in helping teams work more collaboratively, through research, education, coaching and consulting. Enette has led initiatives and training programs at community, provincial and national levels, and during the past 3 years, has co-authored and developed more than 40 educational resources for health professionals, educators, leaders and policymakers. She has taught undergraduate and graduate learners, and speaks professionally to education, government, health care and corporate audiences across North America and internationally. Among others, key topics have included: leading through change, personal leadership and empowerment, team building, and team and individual coaching.

Enette has an exceptional ability to deeply understand groups in a short amount of time, and can relate to them in a way that is highly effective and influential in calling individuals to action. Audiences appreciate her deep understanding of communication and interpersonal relationships. More importantly, audiences benefit from the way she delivers this information in a hands-on, practical way that helps groups and individuals apply unique strategies to achieve desired outcomes.
