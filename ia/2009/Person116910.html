John DiBenedetto’s Summary

Top Human Resources executive and change leader who has successfully transformed organizations in the retail/wholesale, consumer products, financial services and telecommunications industries. My background and 25+ years of HR, Operations and Sales Leadership experience with companies in multiple industries lend themselves to change leadership roles where I thrive in transforming businesses, developing talent and driving business performance.

As the attached resume outlines, I am a proven and tested leader who has been recognized with numerous industry and professional awards for:

- launching new talent management organizations for 4 of the largest companies in America; Wal-Mart Stores, Limited Brands, Wachovia and AT&T.

- transforming the HR function at General Parts International (CARQUEST/WORLDPAC), Wachovia’s global Corporate Bank and AT&T Global Markets through a complete organizational re-design, coupled with brand new talent planning/acquisition/development platforms and employee relations, compensation and performance management processes.

- driving large-scale HR change initiatives for big-box and specialty retailers, Wal-Mart Stores and Limited Brands by creating high-performance success profiles for all job families, followed by competency-based selection, development and performance improvement tools.

- growing recurring revenue 54%, improving business performance 21% and reducing turnover by 15% at Wachovia Securities by building new selection, incentive compensation and performance management systems.
