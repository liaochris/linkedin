Matt Gavigan’s Summary

My focus is helping clients locate highly skilled professionals in the areas of finance and accounting. I focus on permanent searches in the greater Boston area for mid to senior level roles.

My industry background and education affords me a unique advantage in working with clients and candidates alike.

I focus on positions such as Controller, Assistant Controller, CFO, Staff Accountant, Senior Accountant, Financial Analyst, Director of FP&A, Internal Audit, Corporate Tax, Financial Reporting, Public Accounting (both Audit and Tax), SEC and SOX roles, business analyst roles.

I focus on leveraging my personal and professional network to help clients locate talent and help candidates find their next opportunity. My network coupled with my team's network at Robert Half is very deep.

Honesty, Integrity, and Committment are the foundation of my business practice.

Please feel free to call me at 781-848-9800 or email me (matt.gavigan@roberthalf.com) at any time should you wish to discuss the job market, your search, or to address your hiring needs.
