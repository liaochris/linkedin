Eric Tompkins’s Summary

Tompkins Consulting Group is a Los Angeles based consulting firm specializing in the event management industry. With more than 15 years experience, we have helped organizations streamline their conference services/event management operations, implement technology as a cost-effective tool, develop successful marketing plans and sales strategies.

The primary goal is revenue improvement by helping organizations implement a "best practices" approach to meeting and event management.

Solutions are practical and when implemented, help our clients to achieve their organizational goals. Our experts have "on property" experience, allowing them to provide genuine solutions based on practical experience.
