Graham Honeywill’s Summary

I am an experienced leader and manager with more than 12 years experience in Nokia and many more years experience in other industries, primarily in GEC and Unilever in Australia. I am a significant agent for change with a proven track record in management, leadership, procurement and selling, both internally and externally.

I have several years experience in marketing, especially online marketing including online brand development and scenario-driven information architecture. Having worked both in Nokia and in the online channel area I can claim a strong mobile and digital pedigree especially within a telecommunications background. Prior to my Nokia career I have had a broad range of experience including selling of long-term service contracts to industrial, mining and government businesses across Australia.

Most of my professional career has been spent in Australia or in Finland. My working and social life in Australia has taught me much about multicultural environments and my transfer to Finland has allowed me to experience the opportunities and challenges first-hand.

I have experience in multiple business disciplines including sales, marketing IT, finance and process management. During the last 5 years my main focus has been Online Marketing, responsible for development and execution of the Nokia Internet strategy through the Nokia Online team until summer 2006 and since then as part of the Nokia Siemens Networks planning and management teams, responsible for leading development and execution of the Online strategy for NSN.

I am a keen user of “bleeding edge” capabilities, especially web and mobile capabilities. I possess a relatively unique capability to deliver truly creative yet simple solutions while remaining true to the pragmatic needs of a business. I am able to strongly focus on the “big picture” with a desire to achieve big results accordingly.
