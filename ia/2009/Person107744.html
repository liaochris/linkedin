Summary

15+ years of analystical Investment industry experience.

Jeff has extensive portfolio management experience in the Investment industry. His expertise includes trading and operations, research analyst, equity selection, and asset allocation.

During his career, Jeff has worked in a series of positions ranging from investment operations to Senior Portfolio Manager for individuals and institutions, to Tax Accounting, with a focus on Buy-side Portfolio Management.

From 1993 through 1998 Jeff worked for a series of Investment Advisors as an operations assistant, trader, and financial analyst.

In 1998 Jeff joined Bank of America as a Portfolio Manager, eventually managing a book of business valued at $350 million. While at Bank of America, Jeff was selected by the sales team to communicate the bank’s investment policy with prospective clients, generating more than $75 million in new assets. Jeff attained the CFA designation in September, 2000.

In 2004 Jeff joined an independent investment advisor, Rowland & Company Investment Counsel as a Senior Portfolio Manger. Jeff was also responsible for investment research and developing new business

From 2006 to present Jeff has been an independent contractor, working for Eagle Accounting doing tax preparation for individuals, LLCs, and S-Corps. In 2008, Jeff also began consulting with a new money management firm, Buckhead Asset Management.

Jeff is a great fit for an Investment, Consulting, or Financial Analyst roles. He brings a wealth of experience and a demonstrated track record of success. In addition to his strong analytical and asset management skills, he is also skilled at building client relationships.
