Maureen Thorne’s Summary

Maureen is a dynamic, seasoned marketer with more than 9 years of experience. She spent the last 4 years in an Assistant Marketing Manager role at Harlequin Books brilliantly managing campaigns from inception to completion. She was a driving force behind an innovative direct mail piece that turned doubters into believers.

Earlier in her career at AIR MILES, Maureen's hard work realized a 94% increase in coupon revenue for the MEGA MILES campaign. Maureen also has experience in the not-for-profit arena working on the Heart & Stroke Lottery campaign.

Although Maureen’s core expertise lies in direct marketing, she realigned her career path and set her sights on e-marketing. She successfully completed the Canadian Marketing Association’s e-Marketing certificate program with honours and made a major impact on the Internet team at Harlequin where her enthusiasm and hard work resulted in an 18% increase in revenue.

Although Maureen’s dream job working the red carpet at the Oscars is likely not going to materialize, she knows that her strategic thinking and spirited management style will lead to an engaging and challenging marketing management role.
