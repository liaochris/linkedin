Seth David’s Summary

I really started my career at age 21 working as a stockbroker and started to do very well. During that time the brother of my then girlfriend convinced me to go back to school and finish my degree. So I decided to get an accounting degree specifically so that I would have a better understanding of what goes into the balance sheet of a company. Ironically I really didn't learn that until much later when I was out there working and consulting with companies. I did receive my accounting degree from Pace University in 1996.

I have been an auditor with a private company contracted by HCFA (Health Care Financing Administration)
I have worked at a large publicly traded company as a senior accountant.
I have worked at a CPA firm and continue to work as a consultant with many CPA's supporting them and helping their clients get their financial accounting affairs in order.

My goal is to use technology to help make the accounting and bookkeeping process more efficient. One of the ways I do this is by using the technology to train you or your staff in QuickBooks, Microsoft Excel, and other programs that can be used to make the business run more efficiently. I have been known to show people things they can do in programs they thought they knew (like MS Outlook) to keep them much more organized and I am often told "..I had no idea you could do that!"
