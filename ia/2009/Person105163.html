jason lampshire’s Summary

At MRP Associates we specialise in recruiting for the Communications and IT Engineering industry in the Middle East, Africa and across Europe. We provide both Sales and Engineering professionals of all levels and our clients include Telco’s, Civil Engineering companies, ISP’s and SI’s, E.g. Norconsult, QTEL, Fujitsu, Thru Point, Equinox, Bull (Lebanon), Getronics (Dubai), Verizon, Orange Business Services, Vodafone Egypt, Siemens Iran,Global Crossing, Telstra, Telia Sonera, Edarat,COLT, Logicalis, BT Global Services, Dimension Data, ARUP and many more.

We have recently assisted Bond Communications in supplying them with engineering and electrical staff for the projects at Burj Dubai and the Dubai Mall.

We have had several years of successful recruitment to another VNO; Vanco and over the years have built their sales and engineering force.

For Sales recruitment we place VP’s, Directors, Managers, New Business Sales Executives, Account Directors /Managers, Channel Manager’s covering Global and local markets.

We also provide Senior Operational Candidates, Vice President’s, Director’s, Programme Manager’s, Project Manager’s, Network Designers, Project Engineers, NOC Engineers contract or permanent.

Our Banking and Finance section work with leading Banks and Financial houses all across the GCC providing staff of all levels.

The senior partners have over twenty years of experience in recruitment and we have built a comprehensive network of contacts to enable us to source high calibre professionals Globally.


We can supply local resource to any project and have a vast pool of candidates all over the Middle East and Africa.
