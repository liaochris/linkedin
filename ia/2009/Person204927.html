Robert Hallock’s Summary

I am a technical analyst, blogger, and tweeter with an established history of providing well-regarded and approachable technical content. I come to the table with more than three years of experience in delivering informative how-tos, analytical news and reasoned op-ed pieces.

In addition to my time spent covering the evolving IT landscape, I have served in research & development, customer satisfaction and technical writing capacities. These diverse and exciting opportunities have allowed me to run the dimension of business and focused my attention on accuracy, timeliness and comprehensiveness.

I aspire to apply these experiences, my qualities, and my knowledge to professional technical analysis in print or web media. Telling the world about the power of the internet, the web, and how we might leverage them in new and unique ways is my passion.
