Sreejith Kumar’s Summary

Someone who aspired to become a space physicist in his childhood. Someone who got appointment as bank staffer in his final year in college. Someone who started blogging to kill time till employment. Someone who got mails and calls from the CEO of an American e-learning company, after the latter went through his blog posts. Someone who attended tests and interviews regarding an unknown working sphere. Finally, someone who preferred software to banking after a deep thought.

That marked the start of my career, in 2005. It was a great experience learning new things everyday, at Vitalect, that e-learning company. After a year of working with Vitalect, I moved to one of the best transaction billing companies in the world, SunTec Business Solutions, as Technical Writer, at its development centre in Technopark, Trivandrum, where I am now.

Apart from work, I devote time for reading Indian history, especially the life and times of revolutionary Indian freedom fighters. The one I admire the most is Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose, the most organised of all leaders. I am also the founder member of Mission Netaji (www.MissionNetaji.org), a Delhi based national non-profit trust conducting research on Bose. In fact, I am so happy to tell you that I coined the name 'Mission Netaji' for the organisation. :)
