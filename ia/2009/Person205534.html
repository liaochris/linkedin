Russ Novy’s Summary

I'm a results-driven and creative marketer with over 20 years of proven experience in both start-up and public companies within the Internet, music, software, media, desktop publishing and consumer electronics industries. With expertise in online advertising, social networking, communities, web publishing, and traditional marketing, my core responsibilities have encompassed worldwide marketing initiatives with an emphasis on messaging and positioning, demand generation, brand awareness building, revenue-generating program development, and sales support. I excel at providing strategic direction, integrating marketing with other communication vehicles, and building and motivating top-notch teams that deliver results in the consumer, industry, and corporate marketing arenas.

A few of my career notables have been launching the web's first integrated video and photo-sharing environment on Webshots; the very first digital music subscription service with Rhapsody; the ubiquitous web publishing platform of Flash, Dreamweaver, and Fireworks by Macromedia; and the world's first 35" and 40" presentation monitors by Mitsubishi Electronics.
