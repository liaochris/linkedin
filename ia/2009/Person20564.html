Arturo Neto Jr., CFA’s Experience

  • Chief Investment Officer

    OakRun Capital

    (Investment Management industry)

    September 2009 — Present (2 months)

    OakRun Capital LLC, is a boutique investment management firm that specializes in asset management for institutional and ultra high net worth clients. OakRun offers qualified investors a range of sophisticated investment products including the flagship Income Plus Fund, a global macro fund, managed accounts and custom white label products for qualified institutions. OakRun Capital works with qualified institutional clients such as banks, insurance companies, securities firms, private banks, wealth managers, financial planners, investment advisors, corporations and fiduciaries.

    OakRun Income Plus Fund Ltd.

    Investment Strategy - The Fund is an actively managed, short duration, Receivables Refactoring Fund. The Fund
    strategy is to purchase commercial accounts receivables (obligations) of highly rated companies. The Fund was born out
    of demand for a low volatility, low risk, income generating investment with a low correlation to the capital markets. The Fund seeks to insure a high proportion of portfolio holdings and strives to maintain a short-term investment grade weighted average credit quality of A1/P1.
