Kathy Xiong’s Summary

I have over 5 years of nonprofit related work, with planning, strategizing, and organizing experience going back to middle school and onto my undergraduate career. Other interests I obtain are with grant prospecting and proposal development.

My writing and rhetorically intensive education through a degree in sociology has solidified my expository writing skills, essential for research and grant proposal development. In the research and grant proposal development phase, my strengths are also inclusive of researching information to build a strong argument to secure funding or to infer conclusions based on research.

Other than having an interest and strength in grant prospecting and proposal development, my writing skills have also been demonstrated through procedure manuals, orientation manuals, revising policies, website content revision and layout, informational materials, publishing, and program materials.
