Chris Boden’s Summary

Chris is a talented new media executive with strong entrepreneurial experience and a track record of starting up, building and managing companies with successful projects based out of China, Australia and Singapore.

Able to function at a hands-on and strategic level, Chris has a penchant for getting things done. Having started one of the earlier wireless media businesses, Chris has a thorough understanding of the connected landscape and the ability to navigate the risks and capitalize on the opportunities in new media.

Through his experience in building and running new media ventures, Chris has been involved in all aspects of M&A, on both the buy and sell side. He has handled fund-raising rounds, negotiated valuations, managed due diligence and post-acquisition integration. He is as comfortable working through business cases at board level with investors as he is managing detailed technical integration challenges with developers.

Chris has built a significant regional and global network of business partners during his professional career - in his most recent role, he was responsible for establishing partnerships with the major Asian mobile carriers, portals and handset manufacturers.

Prior to that, he built relationships with many of the major mobile content publishers around the globe, securing deals to publish their content in China. He is comfortable working internationally and has experience of negotiating and relationship building with partners from a wide array of countries, cultures and languages.
