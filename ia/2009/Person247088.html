whurley (William Hurley)’s Summary

I am currently Chief Architect for BMC Software’s Open Source Strategy and Chairman of The Open Management Consortium.

I have fifteen years' experience in the research and development of groundbreaking technology solutions, which have earned me a number of honors and awards, including IBM's Master Inventor title; an IBM Pervasive Computing Award; two 'Going for the Gold' Awards for innovation, from Apple Computer; and a 2004 Apple Design Award. I have authored numerous patents while working in the research and development labs of Fortune 500 companies including IBM, Tivoli Systems, and Apple Computer.
