Erica Holland’s Summary

Although I am an Advertising and Spanish double major, I ultimately would like to pursue a career in public relations. I enjoy all facets of public relations, whether it is media pitching, social media or drafting written materials. With my involvement in Public Relations Student Society of America (PRSSA) and other campus organizations, I strive to learn more about the field of public relations.

As a senior at the University of Georgia, I have served as a national and international ambassador for the University of Georgia as a representative of Grady College for a public relations trip to Washington, D.C. and as a study abroad participant in Buenos Aires, Argentina for a semester. In Argentina, I also had the opportunity to work as an intern for Colombo Pashkus PR agency, working with clients such as Nickelodeon and MTV.

As a self-proclaimed environmentalist, I enjoy the marriage between public affairs and environmental policy. During my internship at Waste Management, Inc., I helped with future initiatives to make the world a better place.
