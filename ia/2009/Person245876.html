Wayne Kessler’s Summary

I provide counseling, coaching, mediation and consulting for individuals and small business owners. Through individually virtual or in person meetings we create marketing and business plans to help you manage change, grow their business, make use of social media, the latest technology, as well as deal more effectively with work-related stress. I have experience and training in both business and the helping professions which, as you can imagine, is very much needed in this economy.

Wayne Kessler’s Specialties:

My specialty work includes helping my clients with internet marketing, website development, purchase and usage of electronic devices, and using social and business networking tools to grow their business. Please contact me for further details. I look forward to hearing from you!
