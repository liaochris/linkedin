Sheri Hoff’s Summary

Life Coach, author, and owns the Sheri K Hoff International Coaching Company. Author of the motivational book, Keys to Living Joyfully. Conducts individual life coaching, group coaching, and keynote speaking. Adjunct college professor in both on ground and online settings teaching classes in Ethics, Management, and Business. Currently, pursuing a PhD through Walden University in Applied Management and Decision Sciences with an emphasis in Leadership and Organizational Change. Prior industry experience in management in the areas of higher education, training, operations, sales, and human resources.

Sheri Hoff’s Specialties:

Motivational training, individual development plans, life coaching, human resources generalist, hiring, recruiting, training, sales budget management, operations management, organizational development, higher education management and program oversight.
