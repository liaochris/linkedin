Randy Miller’s Summary

In my 46 years I have done some amazing things that I am most proud of. For 12 years I was a top professional bicycle racer, went from there into 2 years of professional Bass fishing, worked 2 years fund raising for the Special Olympics and then studied to become a Botanist. Specializing in the wonderful world of Bromeliads, this now 20 year passion has afforded me to travel all throughout latin, central and south America studying and photographing these amazing plants.
I had 5 years ago joined with a long time friend to assist with the sales for HireVeterans.com and now, I must admit, am fully committed to the success of this company and to the benefit all our US Veteran Men and Women receive from our service.

Randy Miller’s Specialties:

Employer Recruitment....In other words I am committed to acquiring the jobs that you may have and to get those jobs posted to the HireVeterans.com job board for all our US Veterans to apply to.
