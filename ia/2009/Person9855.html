Bart Verhulst’s Summary

Bart Verhulst is an international business and trade development consultant.
In his free time he is a passionate admirer of seven-a-side rugby, likes to travel, meet cultures, and learn about industries.

Bart Verhulst’s Specialties:

Trade development, export marketing and management, collaboration strategies (alliances, partnerships, smart networks) for private sector development.

    Cedis International

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    August 2008 — Present (11 months)

    Consultancy and training services in trade development.Objective is to stimulate international trade, investment, technology transfer, partnering and access to high growth global markets.
    Clients are SME's worldwide, governments, departments of trade and industry, intergovernmental organisations, service providers, sector associations, and trade promotion organisations.

  • Principal

    IfED - Institute for Entrepreneurial Development

    (International Trade and Development industry)
