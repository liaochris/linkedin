Chris Coyle’s Summary

Christopher G Coyle is an industry recognized sales and marketing leader with over 20 years experience in the telecommunications industry.

Coyle is currently working for XO Communications responsible for sales into the high end enterprise marketplace.

Coyle’s area of expertise lies within successfully managing the needs of multi-location organizations with complex and varied service requirements. Coyle’s role as Senior Account Director for XO, has him manage the relationship with Fortune 100 Enterprise customers and be responsible for overall account leadership, financial and contractual negotiations and overall relationship/partnership between these top firms and XO.
Account leadership is provided by offering value-add communication services & acting as the customer advocate and ensuring customers complete satisfaction with XO.

Prior to XO Communications, Coyle led the sales engagement at Level 3 / Broadwing Communications into the top NYC metro based financial firms. Coyle increased annual billing from 1.2 million in 2003 to over 20 million in 1st Qtr 2009.

Previous to Level 3 Coyle worked as a National Account Manager and Sales Manager for MCI/WorldCom/WilTel. After Coyle’s tenure at MCI, he spent 3 years in various sales and account management roles with start-up companies GiantLoop and IDT Winstar.
Coyle has held multiple roles within the industry, including Regional Sales Manager, Branch Manager, Global Accounts Director, where he was responsible for running sales teams and sales engagements targeting the NYC Enterprise Accounts marketplace. Point of interest - As a key member of MCI’s sales force Coyle played a key role in the success and the development of MCI’s 1st Major League Baseball contract.
Coyle started his career in telecommunications working for US Sprint in 1986.
