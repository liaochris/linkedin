Ronnie O'Dell, MBA, MA’s Summary

For the last 14 years I've helped hundreds of Small Businesses owners in Los Angeles lower their risk and enable them to focus on their core business.

Today I can help owners save up to 60% on their workers compensation insurance, employee benefits, and HR/Payroll administration through an innovative Professional Employer Organization (PEO) platform.

"Big Company" Support for Employers and Employees
Today's pace-setting executives understand the importance of giving their company a competitive advantage through stellar HR processes and services. With PEO Professionals, small companies outsource their entire HR function and enjoy a comprehensive set of services that are typically only available to much larger organizations.

In addition, I can help business owners accumulate wealth on a leveraged basis, provide access to business continuation funding, fund college for their children/grandchildren, and to defer the taxes owed on the sale of their business and or commercial/residential property.

Saving folks time, money and headache on workers compensation insurance/benefits and employee administration and helping them grow their business and their personal wealth through the use of safe, proven, and "approved" financial strategies..it doesn't get any better than this.
