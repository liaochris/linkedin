David Klein’s Summary

Recently launched the Brainshark Content Network, a business content marketplace for the promotion of professional subject matter expertise. My company is Brainshark, an on-demand, professional content delivery solution used for training and marketing material. My focus is to lead this current strategic initiative and continue to evolve it alongside social media, elearning, and SaaS trends. This initiative blends well with my responsibilities on the Board of the Boston Product Management Association, as I am focused on our online community.

Previously, I was a member of the pre-launch team at Eons, an internet startup lead by Jeff Taylor, founder of Monster.com. Successfully launched an online memorial business from concept to creation. Challenged a marketplace devoted to newspaper obituaries by developing a website empowering people to connect, grieve and share life stories. Enabled a site mixing the technology of community tools, search and content.

Prior to this startup opportunity, I was working to improve Houghton Mifflin's technology presence within the College market of Educational Publishing. Established strategies for their eCommerce and supportive web product models with a goal of solidifying supplemental revenue streams.

Previously, hired as employee number 21, I contributed to Monster Worldwide becoming a profitable business. I focused 3 years on the expansion of the business model into international territories (Europe, Pac Rim, Canada), with latter 2 years based in London. My focus turned to Product Development, partnering in the establishment of Monster Worldwide within the Executive and Skilled & Hourly spaces.
