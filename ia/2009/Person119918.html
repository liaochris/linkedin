Jordan Lampert’s Summary

I am a highly entrepreneurial individual offering a unique blend of both Business and Product Development and skills. I have a history of building and selling successful businesses, products and services with extensive enterprise experience. I'm greatly respected by my peers for my collaborative management style and work ethic and I have a strong customer focus orientation.

After completing University in 96’ I started working for Merisel where I consistently sold above quota of $1M per month. Leveraging that experience, in 1999, I co-founded a SaaS company (Cowboy Corp) which provided CRM and Web solutions to dealerships across North America. I was responsible for Development, Support, Training, QA, Marketing and Administration. I led a team of over 30+ employees to produce the industry leading automotive CRM product (Prospector).

In 2003, I sold Cowboy to a large US-based software firm (The Cobalt Group). There, I directed the merger of technology, support, operations and training from Cowboy to Cobalt. I also worked directly with enterprise clients to sell the Prospector solution which was successfully endorsed by GM who rolled the product out to over 40% of their Saturn dealers nationwide. The CRM solution built by Cowboy continues to provide significant returns for Cobalt and was eventually installed in over 500+ dealerships.

During my five years at Cobalt I continued to build relationships with major clients, interpreting their needs from product inception to launch. I built comprehensive market share/profitability forecasts and financial models to determine strategic direction. I engineered many endorsements and certifications for Cobalt products and authored RFPs winning business from several OEMs.

Now, as part of the leadership team at Voice on the Go, I’m generating profitable growth in the Mobile Voice Recognition space securing new business with Global Telecommunication Carriers, Major International Retailers, OEMs, and Online Resellers.
