Brian Newbacher’s Summary

Excellent communicator and relationship builder working with the media, lawmakers and the community on behalf of AAA in four states (Ohio, Pennsylvania, West Virginia and New York) to improve traffic safety and to build the AAA brand.

Have also served the State of Ohio in leading marketing and public relations campaigns including the 200th anniversary of statehood in 2003.

Former U.S. Marine and reporter working in radio, television and print. Also served for five years as editor and publisher of The Ohio Motorist, AAA's member publication in Northeast Ohio.
