Michael Datta’s Summary

Michael Datta first started turning his passion for wine into working reality back in 1992. This is when he first started visiting the Hunter Valley at the ripe young age off 22 and fell in love with all aspects of wine; from growing the grapes to making the wine and then having to actually sell it.

In 2000, the first stage of achieving his end goal became reality. Michael left the lucrative world of consulting/recruitment and set up a small wine business consulting firm. This business simply gave boutique wineries what they wanted; which was end-to-end business services covering, vineyards, winemaking, and especially sales & marketing.

Since 2000, Michael has assisted numerous boutique wineries achieve their goals and gain a true commercial understanding of where their businesses stood. This took Michael further into working with grape growers, attaining them better prices for their grapes and also to look at the possibility of launching their own brands in the future.

With so much involvement in vineyards, it made perfect sense to Michael to take ownership and accountability in his own vineyards, while building a strong co-operative of high-end grape growers. After travelling and researching vineyards across the country, Michael new that the only place for him was Eden Valley, South Australia.

MICHAELDATTA Vineyards (MDV) is all about growing great fruit and producing high quality wines from it’s single vineyard, old vines in Eden Valley, South Australia.

MDV consists of the oldest registered Riesling vines in South Australia (over 110+ yrs old). The property also consists of:
• 40+ yr old Riesling
• 15+ yr old Cabernet Franc
• 25+ yr old Merlot
• 40+ yr old Shiraz
• 40+ yr old Cabernet Sauvignon

Additionally, MDV sources fruit from a 100+ yr old Eden Valley Grenache vineyard, while sourcing the premium Chardonnay from a mate's property next door, which is 35+ yr old and is cropped under 1 tonne to the acre.
