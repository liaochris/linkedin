Kristin Keller’s Summary

I am a Global Program/Project Manager with 13 years of experience mainly in software development. I have worked in a variety of industries including government consulting, healthcare, localization, and e-commerce. Most recently, I worked at Sun Microsystems as a Project and Process Manager where I managed three cross-functional software teams in America, England and Australia. I find that wherever I have worked, I bring three key strengths to the table: a solid program/project success record, process improvement expertise, and the ability to manage international and cross-functional teams. At this time, my future plans are to secure a Program/Project Manager or Director position within the Denver-metro area.
