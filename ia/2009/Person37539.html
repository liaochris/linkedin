Expert in building high-performance sales and market teams, forming strategic partnerships and alliances, negotiating large contracts and helping companies achieve their goals. Master of customizing solutions, launching new products, and solving real business issues involving content distribution and management (ECM), Internet technologies, new and online B2B and B2C media technology, telecommunications, IT infrastructure, hardware and software.
