Mike Hanes - IT Recruiting/Staffing Expert’s Summary

ProVisionTech is a leading IT Resource Staffing Services company which provides consultants for IT projects and candidates for Contract-to-hire and Permanent placement.

ProVisionTech is a division of RAI Consulting (RAIConsulting.com), an organization providing professional management consulting internationally for 17 years.
"Mike's extensive experience in Information Technology operations plus his leadership capabilities will be extremely valuable as we implement key strategic initiatives. He will also have an immediate impact in providing customers with excellent customer service", said Tom McCullough, President of Resource Advantage Inc (RAI Consulting).
Hanes earned an MBA in eCommerce from the Dallas Baptist University. He has over 12 years experience in Information Technology.
Prior to joining ProVisionTech, Mike was the Southwest Regional Sales Director for Infinity Consulting Group, a leading IT staffing and solutions company with its headquarters in New York City and offices in New Jersey,
Florida, and Texas offering more than 20 years of experience. Mike was in manufacturing for 22 years before moving into Information Technology Services over 12 years ago.
"I am excited to join a growing and dynamic company like ProVisionTech. My broad and diverse experience will be helpful in identifying new ways to exceed customer expectations. I am delighted to be part of ProVisionTech's
strong operations team," said Hanes.
