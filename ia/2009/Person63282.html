Customer service-driven professional with success leading and directing the full complement of HR functions and services in public, private, non-profit, and for-profit enterprises representing diverse industries. Skilled at establishing and scaling human resources programs for small to medium sized firms and working effectively in a global, matrix-managed organization. Principled decision-maker adept at developing and implementing strategic HR programs that maximize the return on human capital investments and align with corporate goals and values.
