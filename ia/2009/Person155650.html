Matt Anthes’s Experience

  • Director, Federal Affairs

    Van Scoyoc Associates

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Government Relations industry)

    October 2002 — Present (6 years 9 months)

    Matthew D. Anthes serves as Director, Federal Affairs at Van Scoyoc Associates (VSA) where he advises and assists corporate clients concerning procurement and public policy issues, as well as provides acquisition expertise and support on major procurements. Focusing on information technology, homeland security and financial services, he assists client companies in identifying, qualifying and capturing federal business (e.g. grants and contracts). With a focus on the federal government, as well as the Commonwealths of Massachusetts and Virginia, Anthes provides strategic guidance, lead generation and tactical marketing assistance for corporate clients.

    In addition, Anthes assists clients with transforming their commercial successes into federal market share. Anthes has an expertise in the General Services Administration’s (GSA) Federal Supply Schedule process (from inception to award) and has secured GSA contracts for Fortune 500 corporations as well as start-up companies.
