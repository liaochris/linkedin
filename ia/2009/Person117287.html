John Huffman IV’s Summary

John has become known as an innovative digital media & entertainment entrepreneur. Real Content Group is the third company he has had a hand in founding, and he has served as the Company’s CEO since its inception. The company focuses exclusively on providing digital (online and mobile) distribution and marketing services to an elite clientele of entertainment content owners, advertising agencies and consumer brands.

Prior to Real Content Group, John co-founded Real Hip Hop as a direct-to-consumer mobile content company with mobile industry leader m-Qube. Prior to Real Hip Hop John structured a strategic alliance with Rondor/Universal Music Group called Silverback Music Publishing. Silverback is a lyric focused music publishing company that uses the Internet and Mobile technology to acquire, develop and sell songs to new and established artists, producers and A&R executives.

Before being sold, John’s first entrepreneurial venture, Maximum Networks, spun off several online innovations including a lifestyle-specific search engine, an online e-commerce site, and an A&R Operating System that included content management, micro-market testing and work flow management.

European based “Digital Distribution In The Music Industry” voted John “30 Under 30” two years in a row. He currently serves as a United Nations Goodwill Ambassador and Head of Global Technology with a focus on Eradicating Malnutrition by the year 2015. Other career highlights include John’s co-development of the community event model for the Hip-Hop Summit Action Network lead by Russell Simmons and Dr. Ben Chavis.

In addition to leading Real Content Group, John is the founding co-Chairman of the Mobile Marketing Association's Urban Special Interest Group.
