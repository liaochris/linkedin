Damon Girbon’s Summary

Studied film, television and radio production during Media Studies degree at RMIT in Melbourne in late 80's.
Worked for various production companies as camera operator, editor and producer in Melbourne and Sydney.
Been working on feature films the last 10 years, freelancing for most of those.
Running Filmview Services with Tim Ahern the last 3.5 years in Sydney.
Recent productions inlcude The Boys Are Back, Wolverine, The Knowing, Nim's Island and Fool's Gold.

Damon Girbon’s Specialties:

Interactive motion graphic design, on set acquisition and playback (video split - film & HD), technology playback for film and television: design, sourcing, installation and integration. Content creation for screens: computer, video, internet, iphone, mp3 etc.
