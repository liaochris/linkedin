Ina Klasko’s Summary

Owner of I K Associates Computer / Technology Training & Support – The Technology Pros With Business Sense™, considers herself a Techno-Therapist, helping clients understand the mysteries, and maximize the opportunities of the computer and technology through individual and group training, web site design, custom industry specific application design, and Technology & Business consulting. Her unique approach to computer consulting combines extensive business management experience with solid computer and technology expertise. The IKA BUSINESS SPECIFIC TRAINING PROGRAM™ was designed to give entrepreneurs and businesses training in the tasks they need to effectively run and grow their businesses, equipping participants with the tools they need to compete in the business world.

A member of the New Jersey Association of Women Business Owners (NJAWBO) since 1998, served on the State Board of Trustees and local Chapters' Boards, and the Annual Conference 2009 Chair. Ina has spoken to business and social organization monthly meetings and annual conferences on utilizing software applications, technology plans, and starting a business. She has taught QuickBooks and Microsoft applications for the Dept. of Labor Self-Entrepreneurial Assistance (SEA) Program, Community Colleges of Morris, Sussex and Warren. She was the Chairman of the NJ-SBDC Regional Advisory Board (Skylands SBDC) from 2001 to 2005. I K Associates helps businesses integrate business management and technology throughout the state as well as eastern Pennsylvania, Delaware, and southern New York.

Specializing in computer software and hardware training, computer sales, technology plans, general computer and network support for small businesses and their employees. Designs custom databases according to client specifications.
