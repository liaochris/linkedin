Andy Antonelli’s Summary

-Research and development scientist holding Ph.D. in physics with eight years of work experience in the semiconductor industry in project, program, and area management roles focusing on process technology and capital equipment development.

-Appointed as adjunct assistant professor of physics at Brown University in Providence, RI.

-Novellus Systems representative to the Semiconductor Research Corporation serving on the Executive (ETAB) and Interconnect and Packaging Sciences (IPS) technical advisory boards for Global Research Collaboration and science advisory board of the Focus Center Research Programs (FCRP).

-Received one U.S. patent with multiple applications filed and pending related to semiconductor processing.

-Authored 24 publications including one book, 13 articles in peer-reviewed journals, and 10 articles in conference proceedings.

-Delivered invited lectures at universities in the U.S. and Japan and contributed multiple lectures at international conferences

Andy Antonelli’s Specialties:

thin film deposition especially plasma enhanced chemical vapor deposition (PECVD), interconnect and assembly process integration, thin film metrology, and ultrafast laser technology

    -Expertise in statistical quality control such as manual & automated SPC, monitor development, and excursion control.
