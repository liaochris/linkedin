Margaret Wallace’s Summary

Margaret Wallace is the Co-Founder and CEO of Rebel Monkey -- a New York-based entertainment company focused on next gen casual games. Before Rebel Monkey, Margaret Co-Founded and was CEO of Skunk Studios. Ms. Wallace was named in Next Generation as one of the Game Industry’s 100 Most Influential Women of 2006. She served on the Steering Committee for the International Game Developers Association (IGDA) Casual Games Special Interest Group for three years, 2005-2008. Ms. Wallace was a Co-Editor of the 2006 IGDA Casual Games White Paper and is a current member of the International Academy of Digital Arts & Sciences (IADAS). She earned a B.S. and an M.A. in Communication from Boston University and the University of Massachusetts/Amherst, respectively, and studied the intersection of popular culture and emerging technologies.
