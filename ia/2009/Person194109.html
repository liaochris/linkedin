Ratan KK’s Summary

Ratan KK is Global Vice President - People, Process, Culture at Pinstorm.
In his current role, he is responsible in building and maintaining a high performing team in all the functions  in GDC and across the global offices (People); creating systems and processes that enables the team to  delivers customer delight, on a continuous basis and that help in enhancing and replicating our success stories (Process); creating a conducive culture in the organization globally where high performance thrives and be one of the best place to work for our high performing teams (Culture).
 
Before being VP - PPC, he was Director – Delivery at Pinstorm, where he lead multiple delivery teams, which had Integrated online marketing and Branding capabilities in the areas of SEM, SMO, Display, Online Communities and Analytics . He was responsible for achieving campaigns' objectives and operational profitability for each campaign.

He is a regular speaker at Digital Marketing Conferences, and is also quoted in the press for his views on the digital medium and its applications. For more information, do visit www.pinstorm.com/team.htm.
 
Before Pinstorm, in his 11 years of corporate experience, Ratan has worked in the functions of Pre-Sales and Business Development (in IT Services) and Operations & Service Delivery (in ITES).
 
In IT services, he worked with marquee clients in Northern America and Europe providing business solutions.In ITES, he has managed large global delivery centers servicing customers of Apple Computers Inc., Microsoft Inc., and Gateway Computers Inc.
 
He has experience working with Global Standards such as CMM, COPC and BS7799 and is exposed to SOX compliances. He is a green belt in DMAIC approach for Six Sigma Methodology.
 
He is a Mechanical Engineer (Osmania) and an MBA from IIMA, India.

Ratan KK’s Specialties:

Digital Marketing, Setting up Business Process, Data Measurement & Analytics
Building an organization around a core service.
