Simon Campbell’s Summary

Simon is a multilingual entrepreneur, currently running his 5th start-up business, though ViaPost is the first that was not his idea (a chap called Ben Way came up with the concept for this one)!

Having been based in Barcelona for much of the past decade, and working predominately between Spain and the UK, Simon has established and run businesses in the IT, advertising, publishing and fashion sectors…and now post/communications.

ViaPost has invented POIP (Post Over Internet Protocol) much as Skype did in telecoms, and having raised two rounds of finance in poor market conditions, the business is set to grow significantly in 2009 from a standing start in 2008. Focus is on grabbing UK market share and international expansion.

Simon Campbell’s Specialties:

Entrepreneurship, sales, marketing, strategic alliances, brainstorming, product and brand development, ideas, increasing revenue and developing new business areas.
