Steven Moulden’s Summary

I am a US Navy Veteran of 14 years, and have been working in the IT field for 16 years now. My previous job experience has been as a database administrator at each of my military duty stations. I am currently the IT Support Specialist for the City of Eustis, Florida and am responsible for the hardware and software for approximately 150 end users, including system administration for 14 servers and over 125 workstations. I have completed several IT security related courses. I will graduate with my BS in Information Technology focused on computer forensics in August and will further pursue my education with a Master's degree in Information Technology soon after. I find the electronic discovery field fascinating, particularly when it pertains to email discovery and archiving.
