Jason Melone’s Summary

I have been involved with technology for 10+ years now. Beginning as a programmer/analyst with Accenture in 1999, I worked my way up as a consultant, to senior consultant and eventually onto leading development, business analysis and QA teams while gaining a foundation in project management. From there I joined Partsearch Technologies as a project manager and moved on to a director role where my team and I created the company's first PMO (i.e. the Project Planning dept). I'm currently a Sr-level PM at StreamingEdge, leading the creation of a new, state-of-the-art platform for our parent company, Viel-Tradition.

I'm a big believer that establishing vision, remaining agile, maintaining a lean product development approach and communicating openly and honestly gives you your best shot at producing a quality product or service.

I've had the honor and pleasure of working at amazing companies filled with the brightest of people. These experiences allowed me to be intimately involved in a number of initiatives, including the building of: telecom platforms, B2C/B2B eCommerce systems, CRM packages, a Fixed Income Electronic Exchange Platform, ERP systems, HR management software, and third-party integrations.

I look forward to future opportunities of partnering with individuals or being part of a team that can define its success and get there, is driven by technology, inspires, and values its people and their hard work.
