Steven Jeffes ’s Summary

Executive and Consultant with long and successful history in development and execution of Marketing, Sales and Business Development strategy, plans, programs, processes, metrics and technologies across multiple industries.

FORTUNE 500 employers over 20 years include PwC / IBM Global Service, General Electric, Lockheed-Martin and Accenture. Achievements include:

* Over $1 Billion in sales and management of programs to deliver products, information, and services.

* Recognized Industry Leader – Named “expert in campaign management” by Large Publishing client in 2001; Received numerous other letters of commendation from elated Fortune 200 clients over many years.

* Vision – Grew $850,000 investment to more than $11,000,000 in incremental revenue by developing new marketing, sales, business intelligence, and (CRM) products and service offerings.

* Strategic Planning – Produced $8,000,000 in annual sales by initiating global business development plans, strategies, and field programs at a Marketing Automation Software Company.

* Turnaround – Led turnaround of a $365,000,000 ship control system business at Lockheed-Martin and annual growth rate of 34% at Lockheed Martin.

Consulting clients include US Govt. (US Navy, Postal Service, FEMA), Welch Allyn Medical Systems, Wyeth, American Express, Fleet Bank, Fannie Mae, Pfizer, First Union, Vanguard, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Citibank, Reader’s Digest, AT&T, Vanguard, Macy*s, Blockbuster, Intuit.

Honors include numerous client letters of appreciation & commendation and several corporate excellence awards from Accenture (including Bill Green - CEO of Accenture), Macy*s, Vanguard, Unica, Reader's Digest, US Navy, Mutual of New York (now AXA), GE, CSC, and Lockheed-Martin.

Academic credentials include dual bachelor’s degree from Temple University (Finance and Computer Science), Master’s degree from University of Pennsylvania/Wharton, and studies at GE's Executive Education center at Crontonville.
