Long Wong’s Summary

Long is an energetic leader with executive experience at leading e-tailers such as Amazon.com, Joyo.com, PPG Direct Merchant and VANCL. He has exposure to all aspects in managing a B2C retailer and China eCommerce strategy, and is an expert in package delivery, fulfillment and supply chain. Long has excelled at all his previous roles, and has won a succession of accelerated promotions and earned a fast-tracked career. His past responsibility includes logistics, supply chain, order fulfillment, procurement, customer service, strategy planning, IT management, and consulting, with P&L responsibility.

Long looks forward to utilize his broad business experience, analytical mind-set, leadership skill and operational expertise to tackle complex business and organizational problems. He is best suited at a high energy environment and is eager to help your organization to reach aggressive goals. Long would be a great addition to an executive management team looking to add strength in execution, operations and strategy planning, or serving as senior management at a MNC with regional leadership responsibility.
