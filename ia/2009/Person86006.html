Joe Gregory’s Summary

My background is in advertising as a graphic designer and sales copywriter.

I started my first business with Debbie Jenkins in 1997 (when I was 19) specialising in website design, branding and marketing. At its peak the business employed 10 designers and techies but "being a boss" wasn't for me and we realised that getting bigger didn't necessarily mean being better. So, we changed our focus away from the big-paying corporate clients in order to focus on small /entrepreneurial businesses.

In 2003 I co-wrote "The Gorillas Want Bananas" sharing our Lean Marketing approach with small businesses.

Our success with this book led us to set up Lean Marketing Press and Bookshaker.com which is our current business.

We're aiming to publish 60 books in 2009, pay upto 40% royalties and are accepting submissions at http://www.leanmarketingpress.com
