Eric Erickson’s Summary

My position as a District Sales Manager with Colonial Life is foremost to grow a successful team of professional Voluntary Employee Benefits consultants in the Greater Boston area. To that end recruiting highly committed benefit consultants is my number one goal in 2009. Contact me if you are interested in learning about a career with Colonial Life; where you can have a positive impact by providing both business owners and their employees real benefits that are affordable as well as paid when needed most. Employers that are looking to provide more benefits to your employees without incurring more cost or paperwork contact me to learn how Colonial Life might supplement your current benefit program.
