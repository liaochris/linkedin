Edial Dekker’s Summary

Student New Media on the University of Amsterdam.

I try to blog as much as possible, on Spotlight Effect, The Next Web or elsewhere.

Among other things, I am currently working as a freelance web-editor for the city of Utrecht (http://www.utrecht.nl/). I am also co-founder of BLOG08, a conference about blogging which will take place on the 24th of October this year.

Also founder of MapTheGap, a mobile application that let's you store and retrieve ideas line no way before.
