Steve Probst’s Summary

Executive and management consultant with deep experience in the motor vehicle and transportation industries. Have consulted for more than 35 manufacturers of motorcycles, cars, trucks, agricultural equipment, construction equipment, and locomotives in North America and Europe on overall strategy, vehicle and aftersales marketing, and service and parts operations.

As an executive, I revitalized a service-parts organization and led it to substantial improvements in supply chain performance. As a consultant, I led engagements that generated $8 million NPV and 700% ROI from higher inventory turns, as well as higher fill rates, from new processes and systems; $100 million in incremental profits from improved pricing; and $10 million annual improvement in financial performance from redesigning and repositioning a transportation company's worst performing service.
