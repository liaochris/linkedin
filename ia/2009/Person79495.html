Faisal Irzal’s Summary

I gained experiences in Oil and Gas industry back then in Indonesia after finishing my bachelor degree. I joined OPPINET, the research consortium between Mathematics and Petroleum Engineering Department, with Oil and Gas Companies in Indonesia. This experience fastened my footstep to pursue a career in Oil&Gas field. To sharpen my knowledge in applied mathematics, I went to Germany and specialized myself in Computation in fluid dynamics. Soon as I finished my master in two years, I went to Netherlands to work as a trainee in Mathematic for Industry program (TOIO) at Technical University of Eindhoven. Here I deliver solutions for problems posed by the companies. The solutions are based on mathematical modeling and simulations in Matlab and C++ software.

Faisal Irzal’s Specialties:

My specialties are in mathematical modeling in fluid dynamic, elasticity phenomena, signal processing, object-oriented programming, and software architecture.

  • Technological Designer in Training
