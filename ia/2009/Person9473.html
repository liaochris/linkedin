Alison Sullivan’s Summary

Established veteran of the graphics arts. During her 15 years in the photo industry, she's held a variety of editorial, sales, creative and consultative positions. From stills to video, from fine art to advertising design, she brings a unique perspective and understanding from both the point of view of the artist and the viewer. Moreover, as an accomplished artist herself, she knows what goes into creating compelling, provocative work that inspires and persuades.

Alison's extensive training include a Master’s Degree in Media Studies from The New School for Social Research in New York City.

Alison Sullivan’s Specialties:

Project management, creative recruiting, stock photography, creating and maintaining image libraries, business development and strategic partnerships, negotiating talent contracts, copyrights and purchasing images.
