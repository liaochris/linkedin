Ashley Hamilton’s Summary

I've been working in brand marketing and special events for more than ten years, between San Francisco and Seattle. As a consultant, I help to craft unique, effective and relevant marketing programs that meet the objectives of my clients. Capitalizing on my passion and strong skill-set for strategy and execution, I work from the ideation phase to the production of any program. I dig into demographic and trend research, build core concepts and graphic themes, and then carry that through to the operations, logistics, production and assessment of targeted marketing programs, one-off events, or touring campaigns. My clients have included Visa, Levi's, Diageo, Live Nation, Electronic Arts, Starbucks, and various other consumer brands. My areas of expertise are within the music and video game industries in producing marketing, corporate hospitality or sponsorship programs.

Ashley Hamilton’s Specialties:

Entertainment marketing (music, theatre and other artistic platforms), consumer brand marketing and strategy, research and development for client demographics, Look-and-Feel analysis and refinements, proposal writing, website content coordinator, video game marketing and promotions, touring promotional programs, special events and corporate hospitality.
