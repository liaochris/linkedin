Alina Oswald’s Summary

Alina Oswald is a freelance writer and photographer residing in New York City Metropolitan area. Her subjects of interest include medical/health, science, HIV/AIDS, LGBT, arts/culture, profiles and community events. .

As a writer, Alina Oswald has been a contributor to various publications and has worked with publishing houses on a variety of math/science projects. Her new book is "Journeys Through Darkness," a biography of an award-winning, legally-blind visual artist. Other books include "The Awakening," "Backbone," and "The Best of MJ." She's currently working on a memoir.

As a photographer, Alina Oswald specializes in weddings/events, portraiture/headshots, also landscapes and fine art photography. She has contributed to various publications and has worked with artists, local businesses and business professionals, and galleries. She has shown at New York City and New York City area galleries, including Visual AIDS and Leslie-Lohman.

Alina Oswald’s Specialties:
