Ever since I started my study Computer Science & Economics I have been passionate for people, business, technology. I like to work in an open environment where we are able to collectively build and create the next stage.

As account manager at the Rotterdam University I enjoy linking students, companies, projects and research.

I'm founding partner of Rotterdam CS, a company specialized in realizing (custom) solutions for customers. Solutions focus on collaboration, knowledge management and application integration. The basics of our solution are in portal technology and language technology. Furthermore our product is Open Source, and completely build with Open Source components.

In 1999 I co-founded Motosto.com. Motosoto.com was specialized in profiling knowledge. Based on the knowledge profiles we were able to match questions (demand) on the knowledge available within the organisation.

Co-founder of Q-Bird, Q-Bird developed a tool based on CMM to get into control of the software development process.

Before my career as entrepreneur I worked for:
@Cambridge Technology Partners as business consultant and software engineer.
@Ernst & Young as EDP auditor
@Erasmus University as developer and analyst
