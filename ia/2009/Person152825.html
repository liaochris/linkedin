Markus Burgdorf (markus.burgdorf@amprice.de)’s Summary

15+ years of management experience in corporate and product related communications for the automotive industries. Global PR work for leading tire-manufacturer. Very good relationship to the automotive press.

3 years expierence as owner of full-service PR-agency ideo Kommunikationsberatung which serves companies of different industries: Telecommunications, Real Estate, High-Tech, Internet, Automotive and others. We are very successful with our offer Materndienst, where we provide editorial-offices with easy to use PR-Material. We can guarantee 12 million circulation a week for our customers. And more and more are using our services.

In 2005 I started amprice, a online-marketplace and auctionsite, which is growing very fast in Germany. Within serveral months, we got position 5 in the German market, more to follow. German Venture Capitalists said "It will not work", so I financed everything by my own. Now we are nearby break even. More to follow. It started as a vision and nobody gave us a chance against eBay, but we are getting more marketshare everyday. Recently we purchased AZUBO.de, the leading reverse-auction platform in Germany with 250.000 members.
