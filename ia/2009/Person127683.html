Kathryn Cooper’s Summary

A committed Sustainability Practitioner with a unique scientific, business and socio-ecological perspective. Over twenty years of experience in change management and strategic planning. Experienced in working with private, public and not-for-profit companies involved in navigating shifts to new operating paradigms and new business "start up" initiatives.

A strategic thinker focused on the translation of today's "sustainability imperative" into authentic value creation for all stakeholders.

Facilitation, strategic planning and management, training and development, technology transfer, writing and editing.
