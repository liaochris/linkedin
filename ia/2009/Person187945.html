Sophie Thiebaut’s Summary

Every strong brand, project, service or movement starts from a strong idea. To keep that idea alive with your public, to make it blossom, you need creativity, out-of-the-box thinking and smart communication. Pigment takes your communication project from the first strategic thoughts all up to final delivery on your desk. Results may be websites, a brochure, an event, a strategic action plan, you name it.
