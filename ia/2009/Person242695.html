Victor Howard’s Summary

Victor Howard has over 17 years experience as an innovative and versatile Marketing and Operations leader delivering revenue and profit growth in B2B and B2C markets. In industry, Victor has a proven track record of success delivering large-scale customer-centric business transformation, institutionalizing the use of analytics in decision-making, improving business with BPM and Six Sigma, and deploying best-in-class strategies and capabilities for customer loyalty marketing, demand generation, and marketing process management.

As a consultant, Victor engages with a coaching, mentoring, and hands-on collaborative approach to help clients accelerate business transformation. Most recently, he engaged with www.bptrends.com as a contributing columnist, sharing ideas on business innovation, customer experience, and business performance measurement.

Victor operates as an idea-practitioner, championing new causes and integrating new ideas to deliver change and improvement in organizations. His lead-by-example style fosters a culture of teamwork and dedication to excellence. His "ideas-to-action" leadership approach includes:

• Entrepreneurial leadership with managerial savvy to create and lead innovative, revenue-producing programs, solutions, and businesses
• Visionary leadership with marketing, operational, and process expertise to drive profitable execution
• Assertive leadership with proven motivation, team-building, and communication skills
• Analytic leadership with focus on fact-driven decision making in complex environments
• Financial leadership with solid P&L and budget management experience
