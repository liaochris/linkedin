Dr James Langridge’s Summary

Have had two decades of involvement in Directorships of companies and organisations in the private education sector, regional and economic development, and the establishment of international export markets. This broad spectrum has provided me with a wide range of industry and government contacts both nationally and internationally. My experience in offshore markets is diverse, including in particular the Middle East, South Asia and North America. Specific assignments have been in Canada, India, Kenya, Lebanon, Oman, Pakistan, Qatar, Syria, USA, United Arab Emirates, and the United Kingdom.

In January, 2009 I was appointed Chairman of the Board of Trustees, University of Wollongong in Dubai and Chairman of ITC (Emirates) Ltd.

I held the joint appointments of Vice President (International) of the University of Wollongong and CEO/Managing Director of the ITC Group of Companies for 20 years until early in 2009. The ITC Group is an Australian public unlisted company which owns and operates the University of Wollongong in Dubai (UOWD), Wollongong College Australia, Avondale Flying School and has controlling interests in the International Film School Sydney and the Australian School of European Aviation.
