Joy Milkowski’s Summary

I specialize in bringing web strategy, search engine marketing and marketing intelligence to my clients in a way they can understand and measure. With over 10 years of marketing, sales and professional copywriting experience, I have worked in a variety of areas including tech, industrial, direct to consumer sales, commercial real estate ventures, professional and collegiate athletics and Internet-based marketing.
With a background in Business Communications and News/Editorial Writing from the University of Kansas, I consult on overall sales and marketing strategy, tactical marketing programs and results-measurement for a range of companies from start-ups to established businesses. I work with companies to develop results-based search engine and Internet strategies and other marketing initiatives. Through my efforts and management, clients get surgical placement in paid search as well as actionable information from their statistics for marketing measurement and sales lead generation.

In addition, I train businesses and entrepreneurs who want to leverage the power of search engines as an advertising tool to reach their audiences. Students of my live seminars rave about myenthusiasm and spirit in addition to my knowledge of Google Adwords best practices. I hold the philosophy that "teaching small business owners to fish" instead of "giving them fish" empowers them to create results previously only enjoyed by companies with huge budgets.

Highlights of my career include
- Top Sales Professional, winner of multiple awards and distinctions
- Leader/Manager of sales teams of various sizes and skill levels
- Speaker, Trainer and Facilitator for topics such as marketing strategy and predictions, prospecting/networking, sales/presentation techniques, personal development and growth.
- Writer and copy editor for metro news publications

Joy Milkowski’s Specialties:

Training, Marketing, Web Strategy, Internet Marketing, Copywriting and Editing, Search Engine Marketing Project Management including Google Adwords, SEO, Web Design and Applications, E-mail Marketing, Case Studies and White Papers.
Among my most valuable skills are the following: procuring, maintaining and enriching long-term client relationships; facilitating win-win business transactions; verbal and written communication including presentation, copywriting and editing.
