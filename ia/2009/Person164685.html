Mike Nicholson’s Summary

I currently manage Telegraph TV, which is TMG’s video on demand offering on telegraph.co.uk - www.telegraph.co.uk/tv

My role is a diverse one, and I am involved in all aspects of running Telegraph TV from a commercial point of view.

This includes responsibilities such as:

> Building, implementing and adapting our sales strategy both internally and externally

> Liaising with editorial, marketing, client services, SEO, and traffic to ensure we are always on track to deliver the best revenue for the business

> Work with all of our commercial sales teams to update and inform about video

> Manage the revenue budget for Telegraph TV within the sales department

> Presenting our sales offering to senior personnel at agencies and clients

> Meeting with external suppliers of commercially attractive content and technology - I was instrumental in the implementation of the InSkin ad format on telegraph.co.uk

> I am the TMG representative elected to sit on the IAB Video Council

> I act as an internal resource for all things relating to video

> I build & sell attractive sponsorship opportunities for Telegraph TV shows

> Sell and project-manage branded content commercial deals with agencies and clients

> I am responsible for feeding back TTV performance to the business, and for implementing new strategies and ideas to increase revenue generation

I love this role as it enables me to perfectly blend my four years experience of working with video, with my many years experience of working in regional and national press.

Video advertising has become a passion/obsession over the past nineteen months or so and I know that I want to stay working in this exciting industry for as long as it will have me.

Over 30 senior personnel from top ad agencies acted as a reference for me when I joined TMG, and I would like to think at least the same number would do so again if I ever decided to move on.

Mike Nicholson’s Specialties:

I like to think that my communication skills are a big asset, along with my ability to deal effectively with people from all walks of life; from the most senior director, to the newest grad on the block.

I was honoured to accept a seat on the IAB Video Council; to be a keynote speaker at a recent APA conference on "The future of advertising in one afternoon" and to be quoted in several trade titles on the subject of video advertising.
