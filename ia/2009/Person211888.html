Scott Priestley’s Summary

Scott Priestley is the founder and President of Lionshare Software, an enterprise applications consulting firm located in the Raleigh-Durham area of North Carolina.

Lionshare Software helps guide companies through ERP and CRM product selection, vendor evaluation and review and provides seasoned, expert project managers to represent the interests of the customer to reduce costs and implementation time, improve quality and acceptance/adoption of ERP and CRM systems.
