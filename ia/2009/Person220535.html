Rafael Capone’s Summary

I mix pop, rock, r&b, hip-hop and dance records for both signed and unsigned artists.
What are you paying for when you hire me to mix your records?
• My years of proven experience
• My ears
• My ability, and more importantly, my unwavering commitment to consistently deliver an absolutely killer mix every time - no matter what
• My fully outfitted and acoustically tuned hi-end recording studio

I consider that last point to be the least important reason to hire me, or anyone, to mix your records. It's all about who mixes your records and how they do it. That said, my own standards require that I have a top flight studio with excellent digital DAW and outboard equipment as well as analog outboard gear. Most importantly, my studio has been completely tuned with custom professional sound treatment to create an incredibly focused, detailed, and flat listening environment that is absolutely critical for mixing top quality records.
Mixing is a highly creative process that also requires extensive engineering abilities to get the best out of what a song has to offer. I can confidently guarantee my work I never cut corners in quality or aesthetics and I always deliver at a bare minimum album, clean, a cappella, instrumental, and tv (performance) mixes no matter what. A lot of people will cut corners all over the place to knock the price down just a few bucks in order to "get the gig", but the problems that creates wind up costing the client 20 times the savings (and potentially much more!) in the end – I simply won't cut corners on your project.
I work with artists and labels not only all over the U.S., but also all over the world and I can accept files over the internet or via traditional mail.
