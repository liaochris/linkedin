Jose Kreidler’s Summary

CRESO Capital Partners is a middle-market investment banking boutique and merchant banking firm. We have two primary lines of business, (1) middle market M&A advisory services and (2) Mezzanine capital investments.

On our advisory division our mission to advise client companies in corporate finance and M&A transactions. CRESO excels in the industry through our focus on strategy formulation and solution-driven structures. With a tremendous growth rate since its inception CRESO Capital Partners continues to emerge as a leading investment banking firm for companies with revenues between $30mm to $550mm.

Through our mezzanine investment arm, we target investments between $3mm to $15mm for growth, recapitalization, and acquisitions. Despite most enterprise value lenders in the capital markets are exiting investments and putting off any new investment/lending opportunities, we remain very active and willing to invest/lend. We target growing or stable companies with EBITDA north of $2mm.

CRESO’s main focus in 2009 is on growth industries including, but not limited to:

§ Telecommunications
§ Life Sciences – medical device and later stage biotech
§ Oil & Gas (E&P and Services)
§ Manufacturing & Distribution
