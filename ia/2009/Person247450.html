William E. Prado, MBA, CGEIT, CISA’s Summary

Proven, Experienced, Certified and Degreed professional adept at conceptualizing efficient Corporate and IT Governance, Risk and Compliance strategies and leading the deployment of cost-effective and enterprise value-focused solutions to minimize the likelihood and impact of enterprise and information security risk and strengthen Internal Control environments.

Highly-skilled, organic leader, well-versed with deploying sustainable SOX 404, GLBA, HIPAA, J-SOX, Data Privacy Management, Information Security and PCI Data Security Internal Controls that impact enterprise IT, Accounting and Finance governance functions at publicly-traded, non-profit and privately-held organizations.

Strong Compliance Program/Project Management, communication and problem resolution skills are complemented with sound Accounting, Finance, IT, business operation and business process acumen and a resourceful base of practical knowledge. Adept at identifying the true nature; scope and impact of key business issues and the development and deployment of effective action plans that address identified problems.

- Control Design and Operating Effectiveness
