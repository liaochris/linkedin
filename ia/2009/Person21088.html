Ashish Bhatnagar’s Summary

Graduating from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi, Ashish got his degree in management from the Xavier Labour Relations Institute, Jamshedpur.

Ashish kicked off his career with Reliance Industries and in 1991; he joined a mid-market cable manufacturing startup, where he was the Head of Supply Chain.

Post his management, he worked with Ramco Systems as a project manager and supply chain consultant. In 1998, he joined Ernst & Young (EMEA) with their mid-market consulting practice in a leadership role.

He joined Navision A/S in 2002 when they were starting their operations in India. As Director – Technology, he was responsible for ERP revenue from the Enterprise segment; strategic alliances & partner relationships, product localization & training and product development.

After the acquisition of Navision A/S by Microsoft, he transitioned to Microsoft India and is currently the Director- Microsoft Dynamics PTU where he is focused on small & medium businesses for ERP & CRM; strategic alliances & partner relationships.
