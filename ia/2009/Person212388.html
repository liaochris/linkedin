My current business interests include, running a free Job Board and Career advice website which I own. I have recruitment experience in the Industrial, Logistics, Call & Contact Centre, Office Services, Retail and Recruitment to Recruitment Sectors.

During my career I have supplied staff on a temporary and permanent basis to a wide range of clients including DHL, DHL Express, DHL Excel, Royal Mail, Parcel force, Kwik-fit and Wincanton to smaller independent organisations with only 4 or 5 staff.
