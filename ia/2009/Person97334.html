Imran Bashir’s Summary

Imran is an entrepreneur and a visionary with a demonstrated track record of business successes and leadership. His professional career spans over 28 years. Imran is currently President & CEO of Envision Mobile and Partner in Extreme Venture Partner. Prior to Envision, Imran was the founder and COO of Octanewave Software. Octanewave specialized in Mobile Application Platform. Under Imran’s leadership, Octanewave Software grew to 50 employees and was sold to Dexterra Inc in 2006. Prior to Octanedwave, Imran founded ISG in 1994. ISG focused in Internet infrastructure. Imran grew the company to 60 people and sold it to USWEB in 1999. During the last 28 years Imran has held executive positions at Dexterra, Octanewave, excendis, marchFIRST (formerly USWeb/CKS), ISG and AT&T Canada.



Imran holds a Masters of Engineering degree in Computer Communications from the University of Toronto.
