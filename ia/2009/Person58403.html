David Nightingale’s Summary

David Nightingale is a professional graphic designer, delivering high quality, creative graphic design solutions for full colour print and web design. Creating artwork for sales, marketing and promotional material. From logo and branding, poster, brochure, leaflet and folder design, to digital and online formats such as web design, Flash, email newsletters, PDF and eBrochures. He also loves photography and photo manipulation.

With over 12 years experience in design for print, a background in repro, and a diehard Mac user since the mid 90's, he has the perfect balance of technical and creative abilities, with a big dollop of enthusiasm on the side. With an eye for detail and quality he can turn his hand to almost any digital project.

David Nightingale’s Specialties:

QuarkXpress, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Flash and Dreamweaver are my main core strengths. Brochure and folder artwork design, also creating websites, PDF documents and ebrochures, repro and pre-press work.

    (Privately Held; Outsourcing/Offshoring industry)

    (Printing industry)

    November 2002 — October 2008 (6 years)

    • Accomplished with spot UV and emboss work, and cutting formes for die cutouts.
    • Using Kodak Preps for impositions, of anything up to 300 pages.
    • Proficient with the Agfa ApogeeX PDF workflow, Enfocus PitstopPro, and Palladio Computer-to-Plate. Also experienced with INposition and Harlequin RIP software.
    • Designing and creating the Bprint website, using Flash and Dreamweaver.

    Alongside design, the workload also incorporates overseeing ‘client supplied’ artwork for print.
