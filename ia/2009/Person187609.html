Pia Klit Poulsen’s Summary

Inventor and owner of Taunaki.com. A complete resource to wellness with user reviews, articles, listings and more.

Wellness massage therapist educated in Paris. Practitioner of Massage Créatif. Focus on de-stressing and relaxation, as well as general well-being and minor muscular issues.
Writer of educative articles about wellness and massage, covering relevant topics and questions.
Continuously working to expand my knowledge within these fields.

Future goals include creating a resort for personal growth, wellness and health, as well as provide more information about massage and wellness to the populace.

My mission is to help people help themselves. To educate about wellness and give people the guidance to obtain and maintain their own. I feel it's important that people are empowered to take charge of their own wellness and thereby responsibility for their happiness.

Pia Klit Poulsen’s Specialties:

Massage of the anglophone community in the Paris area, providing deep tissue massage and complete relaxation. Wellness specialist.
