Akshay Kapur’s Summary

I am a Business Manager & Analyst on the provider side of health care. I'm constantly curious about what makes the health care system tick!

I've worked with hospitals, multi-specialty groups and private practices to develop cohesive systems that deliver better care. My current projects include managing six primary & specialty practices, financially migrating a physician's organization from an individual to a group contracting model, and conducting an IRB-approved study measuring the financial impact of an EHR in an academic medical center.
