Evan Leibovitch’s Summary

Communicator and entrepreneur who taken both for-profit and non-profit organizations from a standing start to ongoing success. Exceptional talent for problem solving and translating technobabble into plain language. Special depth in "sustainable IT" -- open standards, open source methods of production and distribution together with low-power, low-toxic "green" computing. Experienced in writing, presenting, community development and viral marketing. Works well in teams, leading teams, or off in a corner working solo. Widely published and interviewed by conventional media and peer-reviewed journals.

Always interested in new challenges in the fields below that require creative thinking, doing whatever is necessary to get the job done, and results that exceed expectations.
