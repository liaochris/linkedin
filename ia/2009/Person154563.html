Mary LeMieux-Ruibal’s Summary

Creativity and Communication are two things I like the most about being a Quality Assurance Consultant. I've been lucky enough to hold positions in a myriad of corporations, not just in one industry. I began my career as a web developer and graphic designer but discovered I had a talent at seeing not only decorative patterns, but could manage business patterns and software trends.

I've been working in Software Quality Assurance now for more than 10 years and am CSTE certified, and am a trained internal CMMI auditor. My specialty is establishing templates and standards for project management across corporate departments. My most recent projects have focused on international communications management and process management as well as quality assurance.
