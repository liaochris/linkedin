Schelli Whitehouse’s Summary

I spent 15 years in the study of human behavior as a professional actor in Atlanta, New York and Raleigh. (1985-2000).

The winds of change re-located my family to a remote area of the country where I was able to reconnect with my life long love of horses. Riding horses in NOT like remembering how to ride a bicycle! With a bruised body and humbled ego I went in search of help with my woefully lacking horse-man-ship skills.

I became fascinated with the study of horse behavior and the need to develop the leader in myself in order to become the leader of my herd.

So my professional experience is my life experience. When it came time to step into the next highest version of who I really am, my two passions; horse and human behavior stood front and center. This led me to the following studies and certification courses.

Professional and Life Coach Training Through the Way of the Horse (http://www.EquineAlchemy.com)

EAGALA Level 1 (http://www.eagala.org)

Liberty and Horse Behavior (http://www.parelli.com)

certified Money Marketing and Soul Coach (http://moneymarketingandsoul.com)

Schelli Whitehouse’s Specialties:
