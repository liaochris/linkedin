Victoria Ronco’s Summary

With experience in working in everything from Communications to Finance; Publishing to Photography, Victoria comes with an energetic creative streak and a love for making ideas into reality. She has had her writing and photography published in various magazines, has been involved in marketing campaigns for international household brands, and has become fascinated with all things to do with brand management and public relations. After finishing her degree in English Literature and Philosophy, Victoria traveled the world before returning to Vancouver, where she grew up, to focus on her career. With a natural skill for communications and a deep love for the internet, she has quickly established herself as a Social Media expert. She is drawn to companies that strive to create a positive impact, and loves that when she wakes up in the morning, she can smile about what she does for a living.
