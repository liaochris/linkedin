Christian Anstice’s Summary

I’m a conceptual creative with over 12 years digital experience. The level of my experience allows me to work effectively and flexibly through the impact of various technical solutions. In addition, I am a seasoned manager and have a track record in delivering Pan-European online creative solutions for Global Brands. I can motivate, coordinate and inspire the various creative skill-sets required to build effective marketing campaigns in diverse markets. A solid understanding of user needs when creating interactive experiences is now second nature. I’ve also gained valuable exposure to user research techniques and processes to drive solutions that are effective from a marketing perspective. In short, my uncompromised creative approach is eclectic, and that’s good for collaboration and business.

At home, when finances allow, I make a paltry attempt to collect art. Otherwise I spend my time happily entertaining my wife and children.
