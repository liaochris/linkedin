Christian Schaal’s Summary

I am selling my book, Life in Poetry and Prose (normally $16.95) on my website only, for $2.00 (e-book version).

www.christianschaal.com

For more than 20 years I ran businesses, serving as a change agent and development expert. During that time, and since i am able to remember, I carried within me a creative root. My creativity grew as the years passed and was quickly coupled with a growing desire to move others to a higher place in their own lives. My working career, as deep and strong as it was, served as an end to a means. My compensation was used in large part to fund my work for others. I have written several plays, each designed to touch and inspire others. These ventures were very successful.

Here is a brief description of my corporate career:

1978 - 1992: Manager RSE-Sierra
Designer and Company Manager
Developed Personnel, Designed and Engineered Electrical Distribution Equipment, New Product Development

1992 - 1998: Cross Manufacturing
Manger three manufacturing facilities
Strong in human development
Ran and responisble for Operations

1998 - 2002 - Business Owner
Consultation
Provided outsourcing for Human Resources, Quality Systems Management (ISO, ASQ, QS specialties)

2002 - 2005: Business Owner
WEb site development, Internet Marketing
Developed local portals in several cities and states which found a high level of success.


I have one book published and currently on the market - Life in Poetry and Prose (again designed to bring growth in a unique way). I have a fictional novel, Choose You Must, which was developed from a play I wrote fo teh same name, due out this year (2009). And a nonfiction work, Lessons I've Learned, due out in late 2009 or early 2010.

I have heard many great things about this site, and am hopeful of making some good connections and better friends.
