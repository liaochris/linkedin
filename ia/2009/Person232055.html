Michelle Burns’s Summary

"The difference between the right word and the almost right word is the difference between lightning and the lightning bug."

~ Mark Twain

Creating the right words and the perfect message for my clients is what I do best. It's my job, my love, and my daily mission as a full-time freelance writer and owner of The Word Zone LLC.

The combination of my Marketing and IT experience allows me to bring a unique mix of creative and technical skills to each project. It has proven to be a winning combination for my clients because I can handle technical projects with intelligence, but I am also creative and detailed.

Building lasting relationships with my clients has been the best part of my writing career. I've been fortunate to work with some amazing and generous people across several countries and industries.
