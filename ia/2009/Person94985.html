Hin-Yan Wong’s Summary

Digital Strategist with over 10 years of experience in helping organisations using digital channels effectively. Familiar with the modus operandi of commercial, public sector and third sector clients. Extensive experience in achieving senior stakeholders and decision-maker buy in.

Commercial clients: AXA, Boots, BT (various divisions), Credit Suisse, Diageo, O2, QinetiQ, Reuters, Shell (various divisions), Sovereign Asset Management, UBS, Vodafone

Public sector clients: Acas, BBC, British Army, British Council, COI, Department of Health, Education UK, Prime Minister's Office, Qualification and Curriculum Authority, Royal Mail (various divisions), Royal Navy, UK Trade & Investment

Third sector clients: Lloyd's Register, London Philharmonic Orchestra, Wellcome Trust

Hin-Yan Wong’s Specialties:

Industries: Defence, Petrochemical, Public Sector, Telecommunication

Expertise: Strategy and long term planning,digital media and marketing, market research (commissioning), operational research (Linear Programming), Problem / Issue Structuring Methods (Critical Systems Heuristics, Strategic Choice, Robustness Analysis, Soft Systems Methodology)
