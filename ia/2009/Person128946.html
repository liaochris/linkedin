Keith Cooper’s Summary

Accomplished attorney specializing in business transactions, primarily in entertainment and real estate.
Serve as management consultant to film productions and business start-ups in such areas as efficient use of resources, marketing, branding, and operations issues.
Key strengths in contract negotiation, drafting, research, analysis, litigation strategy, and settlement negotiation. Achievements and core competencies include:
* Extensive background in motion picture production, budgeting, finance, and distribution;
* Expertise in copyright, trademark, and other intellectual property rights and licensing;
* Expertise in business strategy, management, and marketing;
* Production counsel to feature motion pictures (for partial list, see http://us.imdb.com/name/nm0178181/)
* Expertise in real estate purchase, leasing, and management;
* Notary public.

Keith Cooper’s Specialties:

Copyright and trademark, production counsel for motion pictures, corporate formation and maintenance, branding and identity, real estate investments.
