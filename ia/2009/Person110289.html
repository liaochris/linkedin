Jen Thomson’s Summary

I specialise in tasty, aesthetically pleasing but functional websites which comply to the latest web standards.

But thats not all I do! I design and build e-marketing campaigns, flash animations & presentations, interfaces for software & applications, illustrations, logos, graphics and all kinds of digital & printed stuff.

I often collaborate with back-end programmers to supply a knock-em-dead balance of looks & functionality and I can advise you on usability and accessibility issues and help with Search Engine Optimization.

I’m super passionate about what I do, I've been doing it for 9 years!
