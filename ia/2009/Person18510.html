Anupam Saraph’s Summary

Dr Anupam Saraph works with top leaders across Asia, Europe and the Americas to build better organizations. As an out-of-the-box innovator, a lateral and systems thinker, Dr Saraph helps leaders and teams to reengineer remarkable performance, envision and create enduring value. Dr Saraph also serves as an Adviser and Independent Director on various organizations, helping them to bring focus and clarity in their pursuit of missions.

Dr Saraph also serves as the CIO to the Pune City and has received the CIO 100 Award for 2008.

He is known for having created Giki, the first world-wide governance wiki. He has designed and implemented the use of Web 2.0 and Web 3.0 Information Systems for several large-scale organizations including for the City of Pune.

Dr Saraph is also a life student of the roles of people and systems in driving change and a ‘street-artist’ at capturing mental models of change for design, strategy and building enduring value.

A global citizen, he has lived and worked in Asia, Europe and the Americas. He loves traveling and enjoying different cultures and people across the world.

Anupam Saraph’s Specialties:

Out-of-the-box strategy, reengineering, Web 2.0 and 3.0 based IS design, business and economic analysis, scenario based planning, large-scale change management, mentoring and building teams, visioning and evangelism, product and service redesign, keynote speeches.
