To-Nya Ton-Nu’s Summary

To-Nya Ton-Nu was born in Paris from Vietnamese parents. She soon moved to Montreal (Quebec), where she grew up in a close-knitted family of 6 children. To-Nya, a Paris native, designed her first dress at age 9. By age 11, To-Nya had accumulated a compilation of her own designs. Her formal training was held at the International Academy of Design in addition to her college degree held in creative arts. Upon graduation, To-Nya was soon recruited by Y.O.Y.O. Jeans Canada, where she led the design team with her creative flair and incredible sense of style. To-Nya Ton-Nu impacted and perfected her craft at the house of Simon Chang and Femme de Carriere. This ambitious designer has accumulated many credits and achievements winning the excellence award at the International Academy of Design, and appearing in various Canadian magazines such as Clin d'Oeil, Filles, Jasmine, 24 hours. She was rewarded the Windsor paper dress contest; Landing the front cover of the National Post during Toronto Fashion Week.

In 2006, To-Nya and her twin To-Tam made the drastic change of moving to New York to pursue their dreams. As cocktail waitresses and real estate agents (Citi Habitats) working in the hustle and bustle of the big apple, the twin designers were inspired to create a brand to answer the clothing needs of the active woman. Staying trendy, sophisticated, and comfortable while constantly on the move was a challenge- a challenge that sparked an idea… and in a short time, SACHIKA was born.

To-Tam and To-Nya Ton-Nu are parallel reflections, fashion’s yin and yang; each sister possessing qualities that compliment the other. To-Tam aka “the righty", handles the marketing and sales division of SACHIKA. While To-Nya, aka “the Lefty, is the more artistic of the two. Her creative decisions and original designs are what shine on the fashion forefront. They are two sides of the same coin.

SACHIKA in Japanese means: “more wishes, more fortune, and more happiness.”

To-Nya Ton-Nu’s Specialties:

The SACHIKA brand is tailored from the finest fabrics & cut to clothe even the most distinct feminine shapes & silhouettes. SACHIKA dresses are made to be versatile; many can be adjusted and converted to fit all occasions. SACHIKA specializes in classy yet flirty, & flattering feminine dresses. The label effortlessly takes the woman from day to evening saving her the hassle of going home and changing after a busy day’s work.
