Kenneth M. Valdiserri’s Summary

Twenty-five years working experience in the professional sports arena with expertise in branding, marketing, sales, operations, ticketing, public relations and community outreach. Have served in key executive roles with successful pro sports franchises including the 1985 Super Bowl winning Chicago Bears and the 1984 Bears team that played in the Conference Championship game. Also worked for the 1983 American League Championship series participant - Chicago White Sox. Professional sports marketing, administration and operations experience has enabled a deep understanding of the cohesive relationships necessary between league, team, media and the corporate world to drive a team's brand awareness, build a fan base and grow franchaise revenue. I have compiled an extensive data base of C-Level corporate contacts and personal relationships over a 20+ year period of selling sponsorships, TV, radio, internet and print advertising. Over ten years of front office experience in developing business & marketing plans, managing budgets, delivering P&L's and hiring coaching and front office staff. I have a deep understanding of the conviction necessary to move a 'start-up' venture forward with first hand experiences in the XFL (Chicago Enforcers), Devine Racing and Power Plate, NA.
