Frank Wagner’s Summary

Entrepreneur and executive in the digital media and marketing space with 10 years of experience in management and a focus on international business and corporate development, sales and marketing. Founder and managing director at nugg.ad AG, a Berlin based provider of targeting technologies for interactive advertising with 30+ employees and business across Europe. Passionate about game changing technical innovations in media consumption and the advertising industry.

Frank Wagner’s Specialties:

Areas of functional expertise include general strategy, international expansion, Venture Capital fundraising, strategic alliances and partnerships, marketing and sales operations. Industry experience includes cross platform media, advertising technology, market and media research, data driven brand management and communication, social networking and personalization.
