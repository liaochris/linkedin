Steve Winters’s Summary

Accomplished Executive Manager with 20 years experience and proven ability to strategically meet bottom line results. Qualifications include: improving performance and productivity of declining operations, implementing strategies and visionary leadership to drive performance through quality and continuous improvement.

Led international outsourcing opportunities for Service and Repair of manufactured products and worked directly on Key Customer Accounts to ensure service objectives were met. Managed budgets for both domestic and overseas operations in excess of $20M.
