Tim Bunce’s Summary

Tim is a highly competent application, middleware, and database architect, implementer and trouble shooter. His creative solutions are well engineered to balance immediate needs and longer term goals.

His understanding of data processing issues, particularly performance, management, and reporting of large data volumes, are at the heart of his international consultancy business.

He has a clear understanding of business risks and the importance of business processes and change management.

Self-motivated and self-directed with a proven ability to work remotely. An effective communicator at all levels who is always keen to share knowledge and ideas. He regularly presents tutorials and sessions at the Open Source Convention and other conferences and workshops.

His Open Source Perl DBI software is used by hundreds of thousands of developers worldwide.

Tim Bunce’s Specialties:

High performance data processing and database middleware. Software systems design and implementation. Performance optimization at all levels. Training. Perl. Perl DBI. C. RDBMS. MySQL and DBD::mysql. Oracle and DBD::Oracle. Database schema design and tuning. Horizontal scaling. Server architecture. Open Source. Unix.
