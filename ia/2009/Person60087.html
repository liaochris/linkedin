Deanna Lang’s Summary

Deanna has 16 years of professional experience in the IT and Professional Services industry. Deanna specializes in transformation and change management of technology sourcing and implementation decisions for organizations. Besides her core skills, Deanna brings extensive international experience through her work past work in the US, Asia and Australia, spanning across several industries, including, Financial Services, Government and Manufacturing.

Deanna is an analytical, results-oriented professional and uses extensive planning skills to effectively translate strategic business needs into tactical goals and programs. She is highly adept at evaluating and streamlining operations and business process design, identifying and resolving problems. Deanna is a dynamic relationship builder and flexible team-player.

Deanna has a Bachelors Degree in Business and Accounting from the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology, in Melbourne Victoria.
