Tony Wilson’s Summary

Tony Wilson has a strong sales and marketing background as well as public speaking experience. Tony began his outside sales experience selling office equipment for Minolta Business Systems, he then went on to sell printing services for CopyMax, a division of OfficeMax where he obtained the position of Interim Regional Sales Manager for Southern California. He left and became sales manager and then Director of Operations for a large print brokerage in Torrance. After 3 years, Tony started THINK Communications. He currently works with organizations such as EPSON America, Long Beach Community College District and The Aquarium of the Pacific, assisting them in the development of marketing and visual communications pieces. Serving as a consultant to the California Conservation Corp, State of California, Tony provides sales training and presentation development training to the Southern Districts’ nine development directors. In 2003, Tony was approached by the Long Beach Unified School District and asked to develop an after-school program for middle school gifted and talented students not performing to potential.
Tony teaches 6th, 7th and 8th grade students the financial benefits of their education, using THE ECONOMICS of STAYING IN SCHOOL curriculum. The program is in its fourth year and has served 200 students. Tony also serves as a California State Commissioner. Tony was also recently appointed to the Los Angeles Harbor College Foundation Board!
Tony, a constant networker, is known as being outgoing, outspoken and funny. Never afraid to engage anyone, His desire is to “connect people”. Tony has training and experience in online and email marketing, as well as visual communications development, corporate sales, print project management, trade show and corporate marketing collateral buying.

Tony Wilson’s Specialties:

Printing, print project management, promotional products, ad specialty, marketing projects, special projects, small and medium business consulting, full color printing, brochures, flyers, postcards, business cards
