Lisa Saline’s Summary

After spending 22 years in the Insurance industry a change was definitely needed. I did something that the average person would never consider doing to get out of a rut. After spending several thousands of dollars on Internet Marketing Training, Network Marketing Training and many hours of self study I found my niche. The Internet provides an incredible road to connecting with positive people and people who have a dream. Whether you are a Plumber, Yoga instructor, Life Coach, or Real Estate professional it feels great to know that I can assist them to grow their business by providing a road map on "How to Get Online" and then show them what to do when they are online.

I am passionate about assisting others by being a friend, a coach and a team member. I have a desire to fully fund a resort for families who have children with all abilities and continue to support the wonderful organization of Pacer Center (www.Pacer.org).
