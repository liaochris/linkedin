Stan Spotts’s Summary

Hands-on architect primarily focused on Microsoft technologies. Strong background in .NET, SQL Server, BizTalk, SharePoint. Started in mainframe development (CDC Cyber!), PICK OS's, UNIX, even OS/2 (see "OS/2 Unleashed"), moved to working with Microsoft tools and servers around 1994.

Very interested in new and upcoming products and how they fit into the big picture, but still a geek at heart, so I also want to get my hands dirty figuring it out at the low level.

Background in multiple development frameworks and methodologies, and software project management. Have implemented MSF, TDD, RUP, Scrum, etc., and have advised and given webinars on them.
