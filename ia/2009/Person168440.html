Mou Mukherjee’s Summary

I'm a marketing manager at Tucows Inc, a wholesale Internet services provider. Previously I worked for a VAR (value-added reseller), marketing security, networking, and business software and service. Before that I was a unix administrator at Inquent Technologies – a startup webhosting provider at the time. I started my professional career as a sales/marketing assistant for a Commercial Illustration Agency.

I'm enthusiastic about technology, social media, marketing and sales, writing, reading, and researching business ideas and perspectives. I like best practices and classic resources (that are still relevant today), but also enjoy digging into new ideas and technologies. I enjoy finding tools and resources that help me work better and smarter, and sharing those with other people.

Some of my favourite thinkers and leaders include Seth Godin, Guy Kawasaki, Tom Peters, Zig Ziglar, Seymour Schulich, Peter Drucker, Jiddu Krishnamurti, and David Suzuki.
