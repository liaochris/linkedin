David Dutch’s Summary

Over the past 20 years I have played many roles in the management of software companies. I am as comfortable in the boardroom and I am sitting with developers, architects, and systems engineers. In the past 8 years I have grown organizations from 150 people to 800 people and taken them back to 100 people. My strong technical background and experience in every aspect of the software development lifecycle ensure that I can review and manage scope and implementation of software and hardware projects.
Perhaps my greatest strength lays in my ability to recruit, motivate, and manage strong technical resources. While strong on execution and definition of vision, I understand the organizational requirements to ensure success. From the early days of developing and selling my own software product (HyperAuthor) as a one man show, to the formation of The RAD Group and RAD International Ltd (consulting companies), to my current position as EVP of Technology at ReserveAmerica, I’ve always driven for success while putting people first. As such I have gained the trust and respect of senior executives, managers, architects, developers, and operations staff that I have had the privilege to work with.
My earlier experience providing technical sales have provided me the opportunity to deal with many of the top technology companies in Canada and the US.

David Dutch’s Specialties:

Technology Management, Software Architecture, Fault tolerant systems design, Financial analysis, Organizational Efficiencies, Slaughtering sacred cows :)
