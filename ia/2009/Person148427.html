Marcello Lagosh’s Summary

Video Game Marketer and a passionate Hardcore Gamer who is experienced in marketing and social research, community involvement, event coordination, and developing programs tailored for specific communities based on their needs and interests. Passionate about working with Gaming Communities and enthusiast press with extensive experience with PlayStation specific communities.

Through hard work and intense determination, earned a position in SCEA's Marketing department, a goal professional since 2006. Has gained valuable industry experience at SCEA through working with many departments including Product Marketing, Public Relations, PlayStation Home, Promotions, 3rd Party Publisher Relations, FPQA, Creative Services, Creative Assets, Video Services, Channel Marketing and Events.

While at Hudson Entertainment, wrote and published feature articles for DECA SPORTS 2, developed a community game night on the PlayStation Network for Military Madness and created a marketing research project using NPD data.

Professional goals include the following: become established in a marketing department for a video game publisher, gain more experience with community management and product marketing and learn more about business to business relationship building.

Marcello Lagosh’s Specialties:

Software: File Maker, MS Word, Excel, Power Point, Outlook, Lotus Notes, SPSS statistical software, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premier, and CMS (Content Management System)

Hardware: PlayStation 3 Dev. Kit/Test Unit/Retail unit, PlayStation Home Admin. and Developer clients, XBox 360 Retail unit, Wii Retail unit.
