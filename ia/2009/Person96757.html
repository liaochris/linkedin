Hemant Varma’s Summary

Informatics architect & strategist with more than twelve years of experience in designing & developing solutions for life science research organizations.
Expertise in enterprise data and application architectures for life science research.
Specializing in applications for data management, data warehousing, knowledge management and data mining for a variety of data sources in biotechnology research.
Expertise in management and analyses of genomics, proteomics, metabolomics, gene expression and pathway data sources, including next generation sequencing technologies.
Excellent communication and strong leadership skills applied towards effective coordination between research and IT organizations and effective project execution.
