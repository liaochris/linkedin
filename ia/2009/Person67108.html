David Reche’s Summary

I have more than ten years developing information systems using a big set of programming languages and technologies, mainly object-oriented programming. In the last years I have used mainly Java and .NET frameworks (C++, C# and Visual BASIC). I have used most of the existing operating systems from UNIX and Linux systems to Windows of course. Therefore, I have a great knowledge in programming tasks and I have very good skills in the use of several frameworks and languages.

I have passed the last five years developing healthcare information systems. And I have wide experience in this sector, since my obligations not only have been centered in the development but also in the definition of the systems, working close of the customers, making analysis of the requirements and establishing the architecture and the design of the final system.

I have always worked in research and development and this has presented me the opportunity to regularly go to congresses and different events and to know from first hand the technological advances in this sector. Additionally also it has allowed me to publish several papers and to expose its in conferences so that I have facility in the exhibition to an audience. These activities allow me to maintain a link with academic institutions and for that reason I continue collaborating with research groups of different Spanish universities, mainly the University of Malaga.

One of the areas in which I feel more comfortable and in that I have much experience is in the integration of healthcare information systems, normally by means of HL7 messages and in some cases using integration engines.

The most satisfactory for me is to help my customers to get success. In fact, I actively participate in the definition and technical coordination of new products that satisfy their needs and in the writing of bids and offers that provide the guides for the implementation of the final systems by different suppliers.
