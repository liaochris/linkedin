George E. Thomas, Jr.’s Experience

  • Partner

    Scura, Rise & Partners, LLC

    (Privately Held; Investment Banking industry)

    April 2009 — Present (4 months)

    Scura, Rise & Partners, LLC (SRP) is a private investment banking firm located in Rockefeller Center in New York City. We provide financial advisory and capital raising services to our clients and invest our own capital in selective situations. SRP’s nine partners have an average of twenty-five years of experience in a broad array of industries providing nearly every investment banking product and service to their clients.
    Our experience is particularly deep in the following areas:
    * Raising debt and equity capital, privately and publicly
    * Providing advice in mergers and acquisitions, divestitures, valuations, going private transactions, and fairness opinions
    * Advising companies in restructurings and securities holders in troubled and bankrupt situations
    * Assisting companies in the renewable energy industry raise capital through project financings, venture funds and mergers
    * Advising in structured financings
    SRP’s partners have been directly involved in transactions which aggregate over $100 billion in value. The hallmarks of our business are the high levels of senior attention we pay to every assignment and the thoughtfulness and creativity we bring to our clients.
    SRP is a registered broker dealer and regulated by the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA).
