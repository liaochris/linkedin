Cédric Génin’s Summary

An MBA graduate with more than 11 years of experience in the interactive entertainment industry (mobile, video games and internet). Content / Marketing specialist, with proven track record in the Mobile and Games industry at both a national and international level. Due to the rapid development of the mobile content industry, from the early adopters stage to an almost mature market, I have gained expertise in marketing topics that most industries take years to cover.

As Head of Games for SFR, I am responsible for a €28M content service managing all aspects of the mobile games business, including business strategy, managing and selecting content and partners, business development, marketing and product management, platform and service management. I've led SFR’s gaming business to become a significant revenue contributor, a leader in the French market and now a leader across all European carriers.

Prior to joining SFR in 2001, I held numerous positions in the video games industry. My key competencies in this industry are a deep knowledge of games publishing, product development, marketing strategy and execution. I have worked on globally renowned video games and accrued a total of 30 published games.

I began my career creating a start-up in the interactive software industry (internet, IVR...) in 1995.

Personal skills: passionate, committed, dependable, result-orientated, organising skills, able to take charge and get the job done.
