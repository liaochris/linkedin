Greg Litvin’s Summary

The majority of my experience is in the pharmaceutical sales industry and more specifically the "Women's Health" market promoting my products. However, my degree was in English Composition. I have written 3 book-length fictional manuscripts, and as a result of making aliyah to Israel in July '09 I am in the process of "reinventing" myself.
