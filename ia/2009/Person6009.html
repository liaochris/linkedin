Alain Alvarez’s Summary

I have been employed in the IT field for about 10 years now working in a variety of roles that have utilized my technical, analytical and business skills. My technical background has ranged from desktop troubleshooting and network management to programming and database administration. For the last 6 years my focus has switched more to the business side of IT involving roles more geared toward work that translates into end user analysis of systems and data.

I have been in client facing roles through project implementations, product analysis, change requests, process reengineering as both an analyst and a consultant.

I have worked for some prominent government organizations, educational institutions and health care corporations, such as the FBI, CMS, OIG, Veterans Affairs, Scripps, UC, involving high profile projects with extremely high financial budget dollars. Having worked for these organizations has exposed me to a number of key legacy systems.

My current role as a consultant and project manager focuses primarily on aligning a company's IT strategy with their overall business goals. I guide them in making better informed decisions and usage of their present and future IT investments providing that constant missing link between Corporate and IT Strategy. In all my years of experience in IT I can honestly say that this has always proven to be one of the biggest challenges facing the majority of today's organizations.
