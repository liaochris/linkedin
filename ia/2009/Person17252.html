Anne Waskul’s Summary

Born into a family of Emmy Award-Winning creative moguls, Ms. Waskul’s passion for music and entertainment was passed down through many generations and continued to grow within her from a very young age. Listening to everything from the Police to Marvin Gaye to Elton John on her father’s 8-track, she began to understand and appreciate the creation of music and how powerful it could be. Anne realized that many of these artists were in desperate need of a voice – some one who could take their careers to another level. With her father’s valuable experience in the PR world combined with Ms. Waskul’s knowledge of the Entertainment Industry, they opened up Waskul Worldwide Communications, a Public Relations Firm specializing in everything from healthcare to entertainment.

Within a year, they were known throughout Los Angeles as the #1 Small Advertising Agency, and had the awards to prove it. They landed a 3-year deal with The Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences to help create a new image for the Academy, as well as working up-close and personal with the creative team for the Grammys and First Annual Latin Grammys. Following the 3 years of work with the Academy, Anne and her father decided to part ways so she could follow her dreams of having an agency dedicated to Music and Entertainment.

In 2004, Ms. Waskul opened Waskul Worldwide Entertainment, a PR Firm that caters to the worlds of Music and Entertainment. Hosting a variety of Grammy Award-Winning and multi-platinum selling artists on her client roster, Anne has been blessed to work with the best of the best and give them a very precious gift – A new voice. Her work speaks for itself, gracing covers like SCRATCH, VIBE, and Rolling Stone Magazines, and she has encompassed “Worldwide” through international tours, shows and commercials. Ms. Waskul feels blessed every day to wake up and do what she loves. She believes in her clients and works very hard for their success.
