Iose' Magno’s Summary

Iosè Magno is the founder of Blucape: a new groundbreaking Communication Service that finally lets people produce and share meaningful information through social interactions. Thanks to our disruptive approach to communication, information is no more disconnected and fragmented into multiple formats and people communicate more easily, more powerfully and more effectively.

Iosè devoted his career to design new technologies let everyday users overcome every restrictions and barriers in the communication field.

When he is not working on some innovative idea to improve technology, Iosè spends his time thinking to God, walking, playing his guitar, reading sacred scriptures, having fun with friends and geek gadgets, trying to play sports.

He is happily engaged.
