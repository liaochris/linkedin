Jonathan Perks’s Summary

Jonathan is Managing Director of Boardroom & Executive Coaching at Penna, and focuses on high performance leaders and teams. He is a Board Level Leadership Coach and inspirational speaker and is known as a leader who grows other leaders. Jonathan's life purpose is to help other leaders achieve inspiring performance in their organisations. He is a fully accredited coach with both the International Coaching Federation (ICF) and Association for Coaching (AC). In recognition of his success in the leadership field, he was recently invited to become a Fellow of the RSA.

His client work on leadership, emotional intelligence, executive coaching and facilitation is particularly successful. As an entrepreneur, motivational speaker and passionate leader, he is sought after for comments in publications such as the FT, Director, CIO, Executive Grapevine and Times. He is a PLC executive board member and leads an ever growing global team of 130 executive coaches in 24 countries.

HM The Queen presented Jonathan with the MBE in 2000 for his leadership achievements. He is a Certified Management Consultant with an MA, MBA, and a wealth of practical coaching and assessment qualifications. Jonathan was Assistant to the Head of the British Army, Chief of Staff of the Army’s largest Brigade, Commanded his Company on 3 operational tours. He therefore encourages coaching clients in all organisations to also lead by example and draws out lessons learnt from challenging experiences as a front line leader.

Jonathan Perks’s Specialties:

23 years experience in leadership and coaching roles in Government & Private Sector from his time in PwC Consulting, IBM and as an Army Officer.
