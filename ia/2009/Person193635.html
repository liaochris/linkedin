Randy Blaser’s Summary

Award-winning editor and writer with experience in managing small local and larger regional editorial staffs. Proficient with deadline editing and with directing long-term, on-going projects.

Directed companywide special projects and investigative reporting on topics ranging from flooding and pollution, suburban growth, immigration, welfare reform, charter schools, campaign spending and the impact of No Child Left Behind.

Directed companywide election coverage since 1998, including endorsements.

Randy Blaser’s Specialties:

Investigative and in-depth reporting; editorial and opinion writing; weekly column writing; blogging; news management; editorial staff management; teaching.
