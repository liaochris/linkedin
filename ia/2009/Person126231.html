  • President

    (Internet industry)

    September 1994 — Present (14 years 11 months)

    Based in Paris and Washington, DC.

    Overseeing English language content web site for Francophiles. Working with writers and creating a community. Generating content plus writing a weekly column.

    Bonjour Paris is not just a tourist site but one for intelligent people and first rate writers.


    France is not the sole location ... content focuses on travel and anything French ....

    Bonjour Paris.com covers everything from cultural differences, life in France, Expatriate issues and needless to say -- wine and food, travel destinations, how to's and which ways to maximize your travels.

    Even if you're not France-bound, Bonjour Paris is a good read.

  • Freelance journalist

    USA Today International

    (Public Company; Publishing industry)

    February 1989 — June 1998 (9 years 5 months)

    Contributed articles for USA Today's International edition "Expats Abroad" on a frequent and ongoing basis.

  • Director of Public Affairs and Marketing

    Economic Development & Industrial Corp. Boston

    (Government Agency; 51-200 employees; Government Relations industry)

    April 1986 — December 1987 (1 year 9 months)

    Responsible for promoting blue collar jobs for the City of Boston. Oversaw all publications, setting up programs, a day care center plus attending public hearings throughout Boston. Worked with the press in creating visibility for the city's economic development office and its bond issuing.

    Responsible for conceiving and running seminars and conferences for Boston's business community. Worked directly with the Mayor's Office.

    Member of the Boston Women's Club.

  • Founder
