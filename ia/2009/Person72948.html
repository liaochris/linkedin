Edwin Jamora, MBA’s Summary

Solid experience in corporate finance, accounting, government, non-profit and real estate development dealing with tax credits and equity investments. My background is public accounting and financial auditing, broadcast media accounting & finance, BMI/ASCAP/Sesac & Arbitron experiences.

Previously managed government contracts worth over $135 million and 16 limited partnerships with assets over $300 million and 3 corporations encompassing all aspects of accounting, auditing, planning & budgeting, asset management, financial management, HOPE VI reporting and compliance requirements as well as compliances with SOX, PHFA, HUD, investors and banks.
