Pamela Meding’s Summary

New homes sales and marketing expert with more than two decades of experience working with multiple homebuilders and developers. Consultant for The Bozzuto Group for 10 years, as well as for other builders and developers, including national award-winning urban development Silo Point in Baltimore, Maryland.

MD Real Estate Broker 2009
Licensed MD Real Estate agent since 1989
CMP (Certified Marketing Professional) designation, National Association of Homebuilders (NAHB) in 1997
CAASH (Certified Active Adult Specialist in Housing) designation, National Association of Homebuilders (NAHB) in 2007
Member of NAHB's National Sales and Marketing Council (NSMC)
Member of the NVBIA/MNCBIA Monument Awards committee for nine years

Public speaking engagements to various homebuilder associations for the past 15 years include topics such as “Traditional Neighborhood Design” (TND), “Selling to the Active Adult”, and “Introduction to Sales and Marketing of New Homes.” Presented a case study titled "Creating the Community" to the 1995 Multi Housing World conference.

Pamela Meding’s Specialties:

Sales and marketing strategy, target-driven branding, community engagement, quantified analytics/tracking, site assessments, sales training, product evaluation
