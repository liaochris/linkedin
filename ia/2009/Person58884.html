David Sowerby’s Summary

I've worked around the Internet and Technologies since the late 90's initially with large scale implementations but more recently as we rebuild Shado as a Cloud based platform making it easy for companies to expand their global footprint.

I am a Director (and investor) in Straker and Founder (Investor) in Sportsys. Both companies are focused heavily around the Internet so technology plays a big part on my day to day work.

Aside from work I am an avid rugby and cycling fan, and since I no longer have to worry about sharks (basking sharks don't really count) or the cold (just bought a wetsuit) i've been doing a few 5km ocean swims of late and building up to a big one later in the year.
