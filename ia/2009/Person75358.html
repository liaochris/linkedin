Erik Winkler’s Summary

I have been recruiting for the past 10 years and joined NEAR & Associates in March 2009 as a Senior Account Executive. My focus area is centered around the recruitment of top notch talent in the CPG space. Most recent searches have been conducted within the Food & Beverage arena.

My team also has the bandwidth to handle full cycle recruitment in the Charlotte Metro area. Recent searches have been Mid to Senior level roles in Accounting, Finance, HR, Sourcing, and Sales.

Prior to NEAR & Associates, a large part of my recruiting career was spent as a member of the MRI Baltimore/Timonium office specializing in the fields of Administration, Operations, IT, Human Resources and Accounting.

Throughout my career I have enjoyed award-winning success because of my unwavering commitment to my clients and candidates, making the right match to build long-lasting relationships. My dedication, thoroughness and commitment to excellence are directly reflected in the high quality work I perform daily for my clients and candidates.
