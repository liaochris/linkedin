Simon Serrano’s Summary

SEM and SEO expert with years of experience on the Agency side managing mostly Fortune 500 companies including American Airlines, ExxonMobil, Dyson Vacuums, Cal Tech, LensCrafters, Pepsi Cola North America, Join (Red), Nokia USA, Midas, and more. Experience with both small and large scale SEM budgets, managing thousands of keywords and ads with Branding, ROI, and CPA based accounts. Link building using natural linking techniques, viral marketing techniques, directory submissions, and paid links.
