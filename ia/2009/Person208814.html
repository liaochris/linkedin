David Sandusky’s Summary

David Sandusky loves building and promoting personal brand and career strategies for business owners, gainfully employed and those seeking new challenges.

CONNECT: What do you need?: david@yourbrandplan.com 303.325.3225
YOUR BRAND RADIO: want to be on? www.PersonalBoardofAdvisors.com
SKYPE: david.sandusky
Twitter: DavidSandusky

David Sandusky founded Your Brand, LLC (1/05) based on experiences as a top producing executive recruiter and business leader. The company, range of products, services and events support personal brand strategies applied to career, business and community. Experience and passion for people and strategy drive the company, public speaking, Internet radio show and small business support. He is deeply involved with his wife's business creating custom products for social events and proud to be involved with other organizations and people with impacting ideas as a board member or advisory board member.

David also builds awe-inspiring teams around people making a difference utilizing prior experience in retained executive search. He was recognized as a top producer during rapid growth years, has interviewed hundreds of executives, managed client engagements, built research teams and ran western region operations for a premier retained executive search firm. David's client work ranged from venture backed start-ups to some of the best known brands in the world. He also advises entrepreneurial ventures and start-up organizations.

David is a Colorado native along with his wife, Julie (who is also a local business owner). David earned his Marketing degree from MSCD where he was recognized as alumni of the year - giving back '08. He is proud board member for the new Center for Innovation in Denver and board president of Camp Fire USA - Central Rockies among other volunteer and leadership roles in the Denver community.
