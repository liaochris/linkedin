Shawn Seager’s Summary

My company Ettain Group specializes in IT staffing within the DC area. We have other offices in Jacksonville, Atlanta, Raliegh, and our based out of Charlotte.

I am a Recruiter for Ettain Group. It's my job and passion to meet new people and try to help them get where they want to go in their career. I help create opportunities for them whether it be, revising their resume, interview coaching, or just offering another avenue that they didn't think of before.
