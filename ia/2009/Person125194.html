Jonny Wood’s Summary

J.Wood Creative.
Design. For print, for web.

–––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––––

J.Wood Creative is, essentially me, Jonny Wood, freelance graphic designer, freelance website designer and web developer based in Manchester, UK. With nine years industry experience I set up as a freelance designer in 2006 with a focus on complete creative solutions in print and online.

I don’t always fly solo, most of my best projects have been in collaboration with other freelance designers, creative agencies and design studios. Working this way allows my clients to benefit from top end creative web and graphic design work at freelance design rates – in case you’re wondering, that means competitive prices for quality design work!
