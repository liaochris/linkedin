Nipun Dave’s Summary

Graduate student at Stanford University, CA, USA.
IIT Bombay, B.Tech + M.Tech. in Electrical Engineering alumnus. Worked on an algorithm to process stereo audio to obtain surround sound for headphones.
Cofounder of a startup called MoFirst Pvt. Ltd. which provides the technology for conducting all kinds of financial transactions over the mobile phone itself.     (Banking industry)

    January 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

  • Student Researcher

    Nokia

    (Consumer Electronics industry)

    May 2008 — June 2009 (1 year 2 months)

    I had worked on a project funded by the Nokia Research Center, Bangalore. The objective was to develop an algorithm for processing and down-mixing of multichannel audio to 2-channel audio suitable for rendition over headphones while retaining the spatial perception of a realistic sound field.

  • Summer Intern

    EPFL

    (Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; EPFL; Research industry)

    May 2007 — July 2007 (3 months)

    I achieved my objective of enhancing the 3DOF absolute position sensor’s resolution to 0.2 µm from the initial 4 µm by using an innovative yet simple algorithm. The sensor has been designed to detect the location and orientation of a target. It a part of the MICRON project involving many European universities. I concurrently reduced the processing time from 80ms to 15ms. I have also published 2 papers based on my research conducted in the 3 months in an IEEE's DTIS and SPIE's ISOT conferences.
