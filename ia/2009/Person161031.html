Mike Craddock’s Summary

I am an accomplished graphic designer with over 20 years commercial experience. During this time, I have spent 12 years, developing multimedia and internet-based solutions for a wide range of clients, and in the later 8 years I have also been responsible for supervising small creative teams operating in a highly pressurised agency environment.

I consider myself a highly experienced, talented and adaptable creative web designer that has always strived to identify and embrace new interactive and internet-based technologies and techniques to which I can apply my creative and communication skills. This has enabled me to offer clients the most effective and enduring solutions I can within budget and time constraints.
