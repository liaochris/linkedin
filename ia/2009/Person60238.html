Debbie Feldstein’s Summary

I'm a copywriter veteran in NYC, one of the most demanding and competitive marketplaces on earth. I'm known for my unique voice and writing style, and the ability to distill complex information down to its essence. While you may not know my name, if you shop on the Internet, you've undoubtedly seen my sales copy or the library of ghostwritten how-to books that are my strength. As a marketing coach, I teach clients how to identify the benefits they have to offer, the target audience that needs their services/products, and how to reach them with irresistible sales copy that focuses on every consumer's favorite question: "What's in it for ME?"
