Tony Griffiths’s Summary

Career spans twelve years in the oil and gas industry as a process engineer based in London (2 years), Aberdeen (5 years), Perth WA (3 years) and Oman (2 years), plus a year onsite as a student engineer prior to graduation.

A broad range of study, consultancy, engineering project work and operations support roles, for onshore and offshore (fixed & floating) facilities. Experience includes: Production and treatment of light and heavy oils, natural gas, LNG and produced water; utility systems including steam, water, hot oil, chemicals, drains, flares.

Experience in all phases of project lifecycle including feasibility, concept design, basis of design, FEED, detailed design and operations/ facilities support.

Tony Griffiths’s Specialties:

* Produced water treatment design & troubleshooting
* Water supply/ re-use/ disposal options and legal/ environmental issues.
* Economic evaluation (cost benchmarking, lifecycle indicators).
* Safety issues esp. in utility systems (flares, vents, drains).
* Total process surveillance & troubleshooting.
* Production chemistry issues in water systems.
* Process control & instrumentation.
