Erick Mott’s Summary

Based in the San Francisco / Silicon Valley area with 20 years of results in product, service, and solution marketing, communications and innovation (B2B and B2C) at agency, startup, medium and Fortune 1000 organizations.

Hands-on, teaming and leadership experience includes: entrepreneurial achievements in new products, go-to-market and revenue creation; strategic planning; corporate marketing, messaging and collateral creation; integrated programs development and execution; Web operations and email marketing campaigns; Internet services and online community innovation and deployment (Web 2.0); team building and cross-functional collaboration; product marketing, channels and sales support; PR and AR strategy and programs management.

Past employers include Habeas (acquired by Return Path), Nokia, MarkMonitor, GlobalFluency, Creator Connection, Cisco Systems, Sun Microsystems, Philips NV, Elm Products and CBS TV.

Erick Mott’s Specialties:

Community building and revenue creation from marketing, communications, collaboration and innovation. Ability to overcome significant business challenges in any economy.
