Dr. Ahmed Saber, PhD’s Summary

Dr. Ahmed Saber is a professional Business consultant for many well known local, regional and International organizations, helping them in the areas of Marketing, New Media, Human Resources (HR) and Business Communication. He offers a broad portfolio of Marketing, New Media and Business Communication solutions to help worldwide clients to improve their business performance and efficiency. Saber's core portfolio comprises E-Marketing, iPR and New Media professional services, as well as Creative Content Creation services.

As leading step, Saber has established the International E-Marketing Association (IEA) to support worldwide businesses and relevant agencies and organizations to follow the E-Marketing standards as to assure the best quality for deliverables and E-Marketing deployment in different fields and businesses.

Saber has been nominated during the year 2007 for "Queen Victoria Commemorative Medal" by the Europe Business Assembly asthe only selected entrepreneur from the Middle East in the field of "E-Business" for his achievements. Dr. Saber is offering wide-set of Marketing intelligence and research services through his consultancy firm "Saber Consultancy"

Saber began his career as a technical editor within major computer and information technology magazines in the Middle East . Saber used to enrich Arab wide readers for the best products' guide through his performed tests and evaluations at the editorial labs.
