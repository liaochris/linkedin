Michael Watkins’s Summary

I'm the author of The First 90 Days and Shaping the Game and co-author of The First 90 Days in Government and Right From The Start. The First 90 Days has sold a half-million copies since publication and has been translated into 27 languages.The Economist described it as "the on-boarding bible."

I give speeches on leadership, coach CEOs and GMs entering new roles, and consult on organizational transformation efforts. I'm particularly interested in restructurings and post-acquisition integration. I also design and facilitate executive team and senior leader meetings.

My firm, Genesis Advisers, helps leaders make successful transitions into challenging new roles, especially on-boarding and promotion transitions. We offer a full range of First 90 Days(TM) coaching, program, webinar, and e-learning solutions for individuals and companies that want to speed up important transitions, including promotions and international moves. We also support the implementation of major change efforts through the cascading of First 90 Days implementations.

My favorite work is coaching and leading senior teams in structured dialogues about strategy, organizational design and culture.

Michael Watkins’s Specialties:

* Speeding up implementation of organizational restructuring and post-acquisition integration
* Designing and facilitating senior leader meetings
* Coaching senior executives in transition
* Acceleration Coaching(TM) for executives entering new roles
* Senior executive team workshops
* Development of Future Enterprise Leaders through custom management development programs including case study discussion, business simulation, action learning, and individual coaching
