Marcelo Lewin’s Summary

I have been designing; developing and managing large interactive media projects since 1995 and have been producing, shooting and editing videos and podcasts since 2005.

In January 2008, I created a popular new media network called Pixel Heads Network, where we create shows focused strictly on new media / digital media creators. You can learn more about it at http://www.PixelHeadsNetwork.com/. On December 16, 2008, ProMAX Systems acquired Pixel Heads Network and we are now part of the ProMAX family.

I've done it all (and still love to do it all). From web design and development, to producing and shooting video, to hosting podcasts shows, to SEO and eMarketing. Whatever it takes to get the job done, I'll do it. New media is the future and I'm on the bleeding edge of it. I highly believe in the new mediums and believe that "traditional" media is a thing of the past, as it merges with new media and becomes the "new" traditional media.

The companies I have worked for have recognized me as a leader in my field and an entrepreneur at heart, ready and willing to take on any facet of the job to make sure it is completed on time and on budget. I'm also known for excellent client-facing skills, I have participated in many presentations not only as a technical representative, but also as a creative and business unit representative able to interface with all levels of people in an organization.

I’m bilingual (English / Spanish), married and have 2 sons. My hobbies include photography, video, movies, and learning about technology.
