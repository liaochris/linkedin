Sanjay Sethi ’s Summary

I am a food and agribusiness professional with 17 years experience in various parts of the food value chain. I have worked in variety sectors of food industry like fresh produce, edible oils & oilseeds, grains, spices, other agricultural commodities, animal feed supplements, agricultural implements & inputs and consumer products. I have represented small and medium enterprises for their growth and cooperation. As Co Chairman of FICCI's Task Force for Quality in Agriculture, I represent the contemporary face of Indian Food and Agricultural Industry. As a protege of Dr. V. Kurien, The Father of Milk Revolution in India, I seek to improve the quality of life of Indian farmers.

Sanjay Sethi ’s Specialties:

Supply Chain Management, Procurement, General Management, Brand Management, Sales, Exports, Imports, Training, Human Resources Development, Strategic Planning and Project Management.
