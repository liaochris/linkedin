Belen Pardo’s Summary

Over the past two years I´ve been preparing as a fashion stylist, working as a freelance in addition to my work in digital marketing and communication.
In June 2004, I finished a Master in Marketing and Commercial Management in ESIC. A year before, in June 2003, I finished my studies in San Pablo CEU University, obtaining a BSc in Business Administration.

In the past five years (in Grupo Recoletos -Madrid- Media Planning Group –Madrid- and Prisacom (Grupo Prisa) -Madrid-), I have improved my analytical, creativity, communication, flexibility, teamwork, organization and leadership skills.

My passion for styling and fashion have led me to create my own company in 2008, Your Personal Shopper Madrid, a Madrid based personal shopping consulting firm.
We apply fresh and modern principles to all aspects of consultation: private clients styling services, personal shopping, wardrobe planning, special occasion shopping, gift buying...
YPSM distinguishes itself from the rest by paying integral attention to our client´s unique characteristics; the basis for a successful, lasting image.

It is my goal at YPSM to help our client´s fine tune their personal and professional style, and save time and money in the process.
