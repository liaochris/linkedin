Linda Stanley’s Summary

Linda's mission is to help small businesses tap the potential of the internet.

With 10+ years of experience in the software development field, Linda is skilled at understanding and translating the needs of clients into dynamic and effective web sites, graphics, and other marketing needs.

Working with clients to design an effective web presence requires:

1. Accuracy - Each site is individually tailored to ensure the website is an effective extension of their business.

2. Easy to Find - Every site is optimized to increase search engine results and improve ranking.

3. User Friendly - Once visitors arrive to your site, they can expect to find what they need quickly and efficiently.

4. Affordable - Because clients are delivered a CMS web site, they can maintain their own content. Anyone familiar with standard computer software, such as Microsoft Word, has the expertise to roll up their sleeves and update their websites, easily and affordably!

Is your web presence ready to step up? Call me!
