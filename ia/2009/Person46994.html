Claudia Medina’s Summary

• Bilingual Trainer, Coach, Mentor, for Women’s Initiative for Self Employment (WISE).
My responsibilities include supporting women in writing business plans; analyze their market competition, serve as a guide and coach for marketing and business development strategies, understand break even analysis, as well as establish coping skills for time and stress management. I am highly sensitive to their financial goals and the support both technical and emotional they need to succeed. I have graduated over 100 women with practical business plans and the re-awakened self-confidence needed to pursue their journey into financial sufficiency and success.

• Trilingual personal and business Coach, Peak Potentials Training International (PEAKS).
I work with Clients in Europe, Canada, Latin America, and USA. I support my clients in clearing out the issues that hold them back from playing a bigger game--poor relationships with their families, past resentments, lack of hope, and issues that hold back their energy to try new things that can bring them new opportunities and financial rewards. My business background supports them with systems, marketing strategy, and in building long term relationships. I particularly work with them to update their relationship with themselves and money. Which is fundamental to feeling worthy of doing what they love and being paid for it.

• Owner, Creativity à la Carte, Marketing Consulting, Piedmont, California (founder).
I work closely with clients who had an entrepreneurial seizure and thought they could run a business. They have no marketing or business experience but a dream more powerful than their limitations. I work with the strengths and focus on your personal growth as it becomes a reflection in the business. I take clients from their comfort zones to the unknown where they reinvent their vision, upgrade their relationships, and take guilt-free pre-paid vacations.
