Merly Stonier (LION)’s Summary

Merly began her career in the recruitment industry, working with a number of difference sectors which include sales, marketing, communications, business support information technology and finance, has developed a her skills in executive research, talent acquisition/management and recruitment services.

Currently studying a Bachelor Degree in Internet Studies which include the study of socio-technology and e-commerce, Merly has implemented networking strategies and resourcing capabilities at Hamilton James & Bruce such as Twitter, Linkedin and Linkme. A proactive and outcome driven individual, she excels in meeting her KPI’s contributing 78% of the total revenue for the Sales, Marketing & Communications Team.

Looking to build upon her recruitment experience, Merly has moved into the Employment Services Industry to further develop her skills in counseling, assisting candidates to overcome their vocation and non-vocation barriers to find sustainable employment.

Merly has recently enrolled in an Advance Diploma in Western Herbal Medicine and Aromatherapy with the hope to offer a holistic approach to workplace management.

Merly remains committed to helping people find sustainable employment as well as maintaining workplace wellbeing for all candidates.
