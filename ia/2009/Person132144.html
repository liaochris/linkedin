Kimberly Kimmel’s Summary

Over fifteen years experience as a staff writer and freelance journalist. I’ve interviewed celebrities and personalities geared mostly toward the teen market for various magazines and newspapers. Have written features for local businesses for newspapers. Former story editor for That’s Country magazine with experience in writing bios for press kits for musicians and other artists. Have written for over 15 publications and have had over two hundred articles, features, profiles, and short stories published. Have been published in the Chicken Soup for the Working Mom's Soul.

Kimberly Kimmel’s Specialties:

Cover features and profiles for magazines, newspaper features, copywriting, proofreading, interviewing, and writing bios for press kits. Have written CD reviews, movie reviews and ad copy. A detailed organizer with great communication skills.
