Jean-Claude Chidiac’s Summary

Mr. Chidiac’s goal is to translate client's business requirements into specific system, application or process designs for very large, complex and leading-edge solutions. This includes working with client personnel and executives to identify functional requirements and subsequently working with and leading others in the identification, justification and design of the client's solution. His responsibilities include the formation of partnerships among customer, vendor and subcontractor organizations and the negotiation of lasting agreements regarding goals and approaches. Also responsible for a wide range of design activities from requirements analysis through systems, application and process design specification. His position depicts significant knowledge across multiple platforms, processes or architectures and will include directing the design efforts of less experienced architects. Also, has an extensive understanding of industry and technology trends and to apply that understanding to articulate a strategic vision which influences or guides business area, country or international strategic directions.
With his over 14 years of experience in the IT industry, Mr. Chidiac can bring his expertise to the table, sharing his knowledge with other members and lead a team of experts in order to provide the best solutions that fit and respond to clients needs and budgets.
