Christos Constandinou’s Summary

I have been employed in a variety of contract jobs in the UK for the last 4 years including Associated Newspapers, IPC Media, Framfab, the BBC and Deutsche Bank, to name a few. I was also employed as the lead front end developer for 6 years with Australia's largest general insurer, Insurance Australia (IAG).

During this time I have been involved in a plethora of challenging projects ranging from the front end design of internet applications and web content, to the design, development and maintenance of enterprise content management solutions using Interwoven TeamSite as well as building bespoke content managment user interfaces.

I am more than proficient in X/HTML, CSS, DOM/JavaScript, AJAX methodologies and SOA. I have a good understanding XML/ XSLT and have worked using AGILE project methodologies like SCRUM as well as waterfall methodologies like PRINCE 2.

I study usability guidelines in my own time and have an in-depth understanding of the subject. My approach to building a web application or site goes beyond most of the, "just get it working" attitude of a lot of other developers I have seen.

I believe in scalability allowing for a clients application/ site to grow as their business requirements and user base do. This is the inevitable constant of the web, change; and I believe planning for this, from the inception of a project, is a key way to keeping clients' applications/ sites up to speed with their ever increasing and changing requirements.

However I do not just possess excellent technical skills. I also bring a firm understanding of how to work with business clients in gathering requirements and liaising with them to design solutions that work for them.
