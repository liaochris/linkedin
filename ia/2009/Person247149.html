Wietse Veenstra’s Summary

At the moment my main focus is on graduation for my study Communication and Multimedia Design at the Noordelijke Hogeschool university in Leeuwarden and on further improving my skills with Flash, Flex, ActionScript 3.0 and exploring all other cool RIA techniques. I am very curious to see (and hopefully actively develop) what the future of the internet has to offer.

I’m also very eager to become even better in user interface design and all about interaction and experience design. I am trying to combine the last two with mind mapping, which significantly improves the learning experience.

Another thing of great interest to me is every aspect of personal development, trying to improve your live by changing little things at a time.
