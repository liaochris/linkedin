Karen Vieira’s Summary

I specialize in writing and consulting in the food and supplement industries. I write about health, nutrition, fitness for a general audience as well as more specialized manuscripts, courses and textbooks for either a student or medical professional audience.

As a scientist at Kraft Foods, started a comprehensive cell culture research project, a first of its kind there, discovering and prioritizing novel and emerging food ingredients for health and wellness using cell culture studies.

Undergraduate and graduate projects in the area of sickle cell research, including both clinical and laboratory experience.

I invest in real estate, continue learning about alternative/complementary medicine and start new businesses as hobbies. Always looking for business ventures in the Caribbean since I spend a lot of time there.
