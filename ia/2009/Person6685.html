Alberto Mantovani’s Summary

I am a serial entrepreneur with more than 20 years of expertise in the high tech industry with extensive background in product development, marketing, business development, sales and corporate governance.

I moved from Italy to US (California) in 1997 to work for Rockwell/Conexant (where I became Executive Director of Strategic Planning) with the goal to start - one day - my own company ....... and so I did ....

In October 2002 I started RFDomus, a fabless semiconductor company specialized in the design of GPS components ...... everyone was telling me that I was crazy to start a company in 2002 .... economy was sooooo bad ...

In June 2006 RFDomus merged with the GPS business unit of CEVA to form Glonav while in the process raising ~$16M from Atlantic Bridge Ventures.
In January 2008 Glonav was acquired by NXP in a transaction valued $110M.
On April 1st 2008 I told my wife I was retiring .... she said "yeah ... for a day".

So I started another company: DiveNav that is developing an innovative service for the marine recreational market. everyone is telling me that I am crazy to start a company in 2008 .... economy is sooooo bad ...

I am also a member of the Tech Coast Angeles (TCA) an organization of individual angel investors operating throughout Southern California; since 1997, TCA members have invested approx $70 million in more than 100 Southern California Companies, together with 10x from venture capital co-investors.

I am a certified SCUBA Instructor and I have been diving since 1999.
I have 3 motorcycles and I strive to ride almost every day .... one of the perks of living in Southern California.
