  • Audit Specialist

    AVC Corporation

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    July 2007 — August 2008 (1 year 2 months)

    AVC is an operational expense management firm providing service throughout Southern California.

    As an Audit Specialist, my duties requiered me to contact decision makers on an executive scale ranging from "mom and pop shops" to major corporations.

    After determining whether our services could possibly benefit their company financially, I would set up an appoint to meet with the executive along with myself and my Project Manager.

    Upon the meeting we would determine where our services would best benefit them, if they would. If so, I would conduct an audit of their operating expences on all their output devices (i.e. copiers, fax machines, scanners, printers, etc.).

    I would then put together a spreadsheet showing the overview of their expences for 90 days, one year, and 5 years if they continued using their devices the way they currently did. We would then show our solution that would often reduce overhead costs, provide them with updated equipment and maintainence, and eliminate any redundant devices that were ultimately costing unnecessary expences.

    Often the savings would range anywhere from in the low thousands, to hundreds of thousands of dollars, depending on the machinery and volume of output.
