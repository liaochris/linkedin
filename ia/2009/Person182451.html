Paula Casey’s Summary

My goal is to save large commercial properties a substantial amount of money through tax incentives and reducing energy costs by installing Solar Control Window Film. The return on investment for commercial buildings is between 1 to 3 years. We can provide an Energy Audit to support the savings and ROI.

Window Film is not just for large buildings. We provide residential homes with relief from heat loss, heat gain, fading interiors, glare and safety and security as well. There are many options in colors, performance and designs. Call me for a consultation at 452-1700.
