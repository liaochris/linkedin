    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    June 1992 — Present (17 years 3 months)

    The fraternity of Alpha Kappa Psi Xi Sigma Chapter is both a local chapter at Florida International University and a part of an international fraternal network of thousands of business leaders around the globe.

    Alpha Kappa Psi is a globally recognized as an organization of executive, business, and professional leaders working together to improve the prosperity of business in communities throughout the world. Our goal here at Florida International University is helping the business minded become the principled business leaders in their industries and prepare them for wherever life may take them. By progressing the professional lives of students at FIU, we reaffirm our goal, and that is what Alpha Kappa Psi Xi Sigma is about.

    Alpha Kappa Psi Xi Sigma Chapter connects students to this international fraternal network that includes chapters in the United Kingdom, Canada, and the United States. Members from all walks of life have learned to appriciate and understand the value of friendship and service that are the principles that bring us together here at FIU.

    Our slogan “Shaping People, Shaping Business”, means that we are devoted to helping others reach there business aspirations at the same time developing the Miami-Dade community as our Alumni go out into the workforce and use the teachings of our fraternity to become better business leaders.

    On June 27th, 1992, the Xi Sigma Chapter of Alpha Kappa Psi was established at Florida International University and the tradition of fostering knowledge of business to students continues today as we continue to develop our fraternity in our ever changing business world.
