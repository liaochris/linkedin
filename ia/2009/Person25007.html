Birger Baylund’s Summary

To add a startup experience to my professional background breaking RKI's 33 year monopoly within consumer credit. Even 5 Years after it feels Great!

Birger is a European consumer credit expert. He started Debitor Registret to break RKI Kredit Information's (now own by Experian) monopoly within consumer credit in Denmark. Birger analyzes the consumer credit throughout Europe and can provide his insight on the trends in the industry, risks, and the potential of a credit crisis in Europe. Debitor registret is growing at a 20% rate, while local competion's growth rate has been flat. Prior to this position the consultant was the Sales Director of the Nordic Region for D&B (Dun&Bradstreet).
Biography by DeMatteo Monness LLC, New York
