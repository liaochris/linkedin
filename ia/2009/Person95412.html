Hollis Thomases’s Summary

After working in communications, marketing & sales for over 10 years, I founded Web Ad.vantage (d/b/a WebAdvantage.net) in 1998. I recognized the need for Internet businesses to market themselves just as off-line businesses do.

Along the way, as I developed my expertise, I shared it with others in the B2B sector as a public speaker and author of dozens of published articles. Currently, I'm a contributing columnist for ClickZ and somehow was fortunate enough to be named the 2007 SBA Maryland Small Business Person of the Year, a 2008 Enterprising Woman of the Year by Enterprising Women magazine, and my latest award is being named to the Baltimore Business Journals List of Baltimore's Top 50 Women-Owned Businesses. Pretty cool!

Also in 2007 my company was named a "Small Giant" in recognition that companies don't have to be large to be great. I'm really proud of this honor.

Hollis Thomases’s Specialties:

Internet marketing, search engine optimization, pay-per-click (PPC) keyword campaign management, online media planning and buying, social media and online public relations.

    April 1998 — Present (11 years 9 months)

    I founded Web Ad.vantage in 1998 with the original idea of producing co-op *print* ads to promote web sites for businesses that couldn't otherwise be able to afford to advertise them in print. After receiving many requests to provide online marketing services, all of which I initially rejected, I realized there might be something to these requests! I took a sabbatical from selling my original concept and went online to learn. Back then, that's pretty much how we had to learn!

    I used my own web site as my guinea pig, and after about 3 months attracted the interest of my very first online marketing client. Viola! Web Ad.vantage was re-born, and the rest, as they say, is history!
