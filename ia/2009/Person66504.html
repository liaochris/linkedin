Doug Hutchinson’s Summary

I've served as Mayor of Fort Collins, Colorado, since April 2005. It has been very gratifying to give back to the City that I love, and primarily to make a difference in our City's economic health (e.g., totally changed the budget process to focus on services to citizens, not bureacuracy--formed public-private partnerships for the economy/jobs). I'm running for election (April 7, 2009) for a third term.

    October 2001 — November 2004 (3 years 2 months)

    I was delighted to write a monthly column to put the light of day on political issues in Fort Collins, and urge people to get engaged in local issues--and vote! I attended nearly every Fort Collins City Council meeting for 3 1/2 years (including retreats and work sessions) so I was fully informed on the issues and Council dynamics. This experience made me a very concerned citizen--especially about our economic health--and I ran for mayor in 2005 and won election against three competitors.
