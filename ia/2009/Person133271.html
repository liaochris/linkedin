Klaus Duetoft’s Summary

Klaus Duetoft - currently leads Human Resources for eBay Asia Pacific - he has a team of HR professionals in Australia, Singapore, India, Korea and Greater China. In this dynamic, exciting & challenging industry his focus is on ensuring the Organisation commercially performs through 'down to earth, pragmatic HR business partnering solutions that are both progressive and innovative. Previously he was the founder and principal of Irrelach Consulting Pty Limited, a consulting organisation focused on supporting organisations deal with two key human resources challenges. Challenge 1 - Building your Organisational Capability, through the development and implementation of strategic planning processes, leadership & workplace management development and capability mapping & succession planning. Generally targeting medium to large enterprises. The 2nd Challenge - Managing your Organisational Resources, Irrelach Consulting does this through the design and implementation of Outsourced Human Resources Solutions. Generally targeted at small to medium enterprises, who want a cost effective and pragmatic approach to managing their people effectively. Klaus brings over 16 years of human resource generalist experience, ranging from tactical through to strategic human resources experience. Klaus has gained his extensive human resources experience in industry sectors including the pharmaceutical, fast moving consumer goods, high technology, eCommerce and mobile telecommunications sectors. Additionally he has had experience delivering human resources solutions throughout countries including Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, China, United States of America, United Kingdom and South Africa. He brings his commercially orientated workplace based human resources experience to good effect in diagnosing, developing, delivering and measuring human resources solutions, which are ultimately designed to deliver enhanced value and performance to businesses.

Klaus Duetoft’s Specialties:

Strategy development, Leadership development, Workplace Management development, Outsourced Human Resources Solutions, Capability Management and Talent & Succession Planning.
