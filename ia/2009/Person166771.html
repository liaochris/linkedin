Dr M L Goyal’s Experience

  • Director

    Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Rohini, New Delhi

    (Higher Education industry)

    September 2008 — Present (9 months)

    I have joined Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Rohini, New Delhi (An affiliated College of GGS Indraprastha University, Delhi) as its Director. It runs 4 year BE courses in Computer Science & Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering and Mechanical & Automation Engineering.

  • General Manager

    CMC Limited

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 1977 — September 2008 (31 years 1 month)

    I joined Computer Maintenance Corporation Limited, a Govt. of India Enterprise in September 1977 as Systems Engineer. I superannuated in September 2008 as General Manage, from CMC Limited, a Tata Enterprise. During these 31 years, I had been associated with the management of software development & implementation, systems support, consultancy, quality processes, education & training, business development, business excellence and general management activities.

  • Adviser

    Central Informatics Bureau, Govt. of Mauritius, Port Louis

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    December 1991 — April 1993 (1 year 5 months)

    I went on deputation from CMC Limited to Govt. of Mauritius. There I headed Central Informatics Bureau (CIB). The main function of the CIB was to instal IT Infrastructure and plan & coordinate Computerization in Civil Service. During this period, I was also a Director of State Informatics Limited, a leading IT Service Provider Company owned by Govt. of Mauritius.

  • Faculty Member

    Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, India

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    April 1973 — August 1975 (2 years 5 months)

    During this period, I was a Faculty member at the Department of Computer Science and taught Computer Science subjects for Graduate and Under graduate students. There, we started M. Sc. Tech. (Computer Science) Course, in 1973, first time in India.
