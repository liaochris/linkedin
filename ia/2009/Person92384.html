Harinder Singh’s Summary

Innovative, creative and result-oriented professional with over 20 years of experience in managing a wide spectrum of operations – profit centre operations, sales/marketing (including marcomm), corp-comm, client servicing, projects, operations, HR and Recruitments, among others. Skilled labour, business comercials and order commercials negotiator, outstanding presenter and lecturer. Strong motivator and task- (as well as resource-) oriented leader. Knows how to deliver top and bottom line.

Demonstrated ability in developing business plans (which have yielded results as per expectations); branding strategy for optimal product positioning as well as managing & operating process-driven functions, budgeting revenue targets and ensuring adherence.

However, if you peel all the layers that I have accumulated over the past 2 decades, you will find a creative writer, who has written novels, humour and review columns in various newspapers; and has a great passion for writing for kids. Writing poems and satire are great stress busters for me.
