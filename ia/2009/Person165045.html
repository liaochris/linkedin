Mike Wallace’s Summary

Almost 20-years experience delivering strategic advisory services to corporations, government agencies and non-governmental organizations (NGOs) on sustainability strategies. Specialist in creating business opportunities from the sustainability trends that are emerging in the global financial markets. Expertise in identifying environmental, social & governance risks and opportunities and providing strategic guidance on measuring, managing and reporting performance. Clients have included:
