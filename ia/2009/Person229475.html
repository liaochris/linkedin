Tasha Lee’s Summary

Tasha Lee is a creator, an artist, a graphic designer. She works closely with her clients to identify their vision and create a graphical representation. Tasha Lee is a graphic designer with six plus years of professional experience. She's educated in the field of Arts Technology and began her experience in the field in 2001, when hired as an Assistant Graphic Designer for a local newspaper near her hometown of Roscoe, IL. Today, she works successfully as an entrepreneur and graphic design specialist catering to individuals and small businesses.

Whether you are a new company in search of an identity, a well-seasoned one looking for a bolder image or an individual looking to capture a strong customer base, then Tasha Lee has the design experience to help promote your business.
