Danny Hollinger’s Summary

PRESIDENT and PRINCIPAL CONSULTANT, HOLLINGER INTERNATIONAL

EDUCATION and LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT CONSULTING

Danny Hollinger is President and Principal Consultant at Hollinger International, an international education and management consulting firm serving independent, international and charter schools, nonprofit organizations and education companies. Hollinger International’s mission is to provide tailored, strategic assistance to schools, nonprofit organizations and corporations to improve education and leadership. Competencies and areas of expertise include:

• Visionary Leadership
• School Startup & Improvement
• International Baccalaureate Programmes
• Board Governance
• Strategic Planning
• Leadership Development
• Executive Coaching & Searches
• Crises & Change Management
• Organization & Faculty Culture Transformation
• Team Building
• Teacher Training
• Faculty & Staff Evaluation & Compensation
• Marketing & Branding
• Fund Raising

Dr. Hollinger is a seasoned and visionary education leader with 21 years of experience as head of school. He chairs and develops boards of schools and nonprofit organizations. He builds and leads collaborative, high-performing teams. Dr. Hollinger develops and implements strategic plans, marketing and branding initiatives, organizational climate and faculty culture assessments and interventions, and fund raising campaigns. He coaches school and nonprofit leaders and business executives.

Dr. Hollinger founded, built and led international schools, transitioning them from start-up to full-scale operations. He developed pioneering dual-language immersion, International Baccalaureate Programmes in Arabic, English, French, Greek, Mandarin and Spanish to educate highly diverse populations of students to excel academically, intellectually and socially. Dr. Hollinger established partnerships with corporations, foundations, governments, schools and universities worldwide. He acquired and developed five campuses.
