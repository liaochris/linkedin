CHARLES R. HOGGE, Jr.’s Summary

My practice includes mediating a wide varity of disputes. I am featuring Marital Mediation as a way for couples to preserve and improve their marriage. The approach is quite similar to assisting business partners to improve their working relationship.

My Mission: To work with individuals, couples, families, business partners and corporations toward resolving conflicts quickly and peacefully.

Mediation offers many advantages over litigation:

* Mediation can provide the means for resolving issues quickly and peacefully!
* Expenses are far less.
* The proceedings are completely confidential.
* Nothing that takes place during a mediation can be used by any party to their advantage if settlement is not reached and the dispute ends up in court.I
* All disputing parties maintain maximum control over the outcome. What they decide is final. They avoid the risk and unpredictability of a judge or jury.
* Mediated settlements often contain creative solutions that would never have come out of a court proceeding.

If you are interested in seeking a peaceful resolution of a dispute, please feel free to contact me for an initial consultation.
