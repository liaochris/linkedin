Alison Doyle’s Summary

I am a job search expert with many years of experience in human resources, career development, and job searching, with a focus on online job searching and job search technology.

I've been the Job Search Guide for About.com, a New York Times Company 1998, and have been quoted extensively in major online and print publications including The New York Times, Newsday, NY Daily News, Los Angeles Times, Philadelphia Inquirer, MSNBC.com, ABCNews.com, Kiplinger.com, LinkedIn, Mashable.com, TypePad, WashingtonPost.com, Consumer Reports, and the nationally syndicated Careers Now column.

I have been featured on the radio shows InfoTRAK, Blogtalkradio, Career Success, Women Aloud, Something You Should Know, and The Job Search Solution, as well as many local radio stations.

In addition, I'm the author of Internet Your Way to a New Job: How to Really Find a Job Online (2009) and the About.com Guide to Job Searching (2006).
