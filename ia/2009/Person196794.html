Rob LeRoy’s Summary

I write about and sell real estate. I work primarily with home-buyers in the Bellingham and Seattle metro areas. I write about real estate and life in the Northwest, posting primarily to blog sites like therealestatenovelist.com, activerain.com and realestate.bellingham.net

I'm a big fan of live music and micro-brews, both of which appear quite often in my writing. I take a laid-back, practical approach to my work, my writing and my life. It has always been my philosophy that life is best when you can find the humor in it all. Whatever doesn't kill me makes me stronger, and the bastards never drag me down.

I spend quite a bit of time coaching other real estate agents, artists, and small business owners on modern, social-media-based marketing opportunities. In particular, I help them to integrate sites like Facebook and Twitter into their marketing plans, and to understand the power of personal branding.

For side-projects, I do event and party planning. Booking venues, bands, organizing themes and guest lists, etc. I've got about a dozen significant projects under my belt, usually birthday parties and small corporate events.

In my free time I play my guitar, hike in the hills, and spend a lot of time at the gym- mainly to offset my micro-brew hobby. I travel whenever and wherever possible, as I have seen a pitifully small percentage of the world, thus far.

Rob LeRoy’s Specialties:

Real estate sales, marketing and negotiations. I write numerous articles/blogs and specialize in educating and representing home buyers. I also know a lot about beer.
