John Fischer’s Summary

Marketer:
Companies must constantly find new and innovative ways to promote their products and services. Afterall, if people don't know about their product or services, they can't buy them. That's where I come in. I find outside-the-box ways for companies to find and target their niche to achieve maximum sales potential.

Communicator:
Communications is the meat and potatos of marketing. Companies must control the message of their brand. If that message is lost in translation, the consumer will lose interest and shop elsewhere. I make sure companies communicate effectively what they have to offer their customers.

Networker:
I enjoy meeting new people. I also passionately enjoy helping others achieve goals, because I hope they will want to help me achieve goals. Quality relationships is what allows business people to thrive.

Social Media:
"Word of mouth" marketing is the most effective and affordable type of marketing. Social media is the main force between this trend and companies who have social media strategies will be way ahead of the competition.
