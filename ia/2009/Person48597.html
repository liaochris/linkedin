Eduardo Comella’s Summary

Seasoned professional with more than 30 years in the field of Global Executive Search/Recruiting and Human Resources management.

As Partner & Managing Director of PROLAURUM INTERNATIONAL, leads the Global practice of the Firm for the Financial Services industry. Share with other Partners of the Firm multi-industries and multi-geographies consulting practice.

As Managing Partner and Founder of COMELLA CONSULTING GROUP, have placed General Managers in top Financial Institutions in the USA, Europe, the Far East, the Middle East, Central America and South America.

20 years at Citigroup as VicePresident and Managing Director Human Resources Latin America (Corporate and Consumer Businesses)

10 years at Schering-Plough Corporation as Director of Human Resources Latin America and The Far East.

Eduardo Comella’s Specialties:

Consultant to Corporations in global market search to identify,
evaluate and attract top professionals in a wide variety of industries and geographies. Specialized in the Financial Services Industry (Corporate Bank, Retail Bank, Credit Cards and Investment Bank).


Management assessment and organizational due diligence in Human Resources for Mergers & Acquisitions..

Organization and Management Talent asessments based on 360 methodology.
