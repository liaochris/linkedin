Douglas Koerich’s Summary

I am a 16-years experienced professional in Software Engineering for the automation and telecommunications industry. I have a very deep knowledge and experience in C/C++ programming, object-oriented analysis & design (OOAD) and programming (OOP). I have been developing embedded and desktop computer software for proprietary OS, Windows and Linux/UNIX. For some projects in the past I also developed in Visual Basic with support of SQL.
Due to my knowledge, commitment and pragmatism I always have occupied key positions in the teams I joined, and I am usually a referred person for technical and non-technical discussions.
The involvement with coordination tasks in the past made me motivated to get a pos-graduation degree in Project Management based on PMI concepts and practices.
Since a few years, I am sharing my knowledge and experience with the academic community by giving classes for the first grades of Computing Science and Electrical Engineering.

Douglas Koerich’s Specialties:
