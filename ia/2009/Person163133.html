Michelle Parent’s Summary

Michelle is a recently graduated student from the Masters of Digital Media program in Vancouver, BC. Prior to completing her degree and founding BHg, Michelle earned a bachelors of visual fine arts from Emily Carr Institute of Art and Design. Michelle is co-founder of a rapid game prototyping studio that supplies interactive presentation materials and games to traditional animation houses and other non-digital sectors.
