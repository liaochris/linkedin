Mark Bense’s Summary

- Currently Chief Financial Officer of Display Boys with a focus on improving internal controls and adding structure to company operations as well as overseeing the complete treasury and financial reporting. Also responsible for banking relations, tax preparation, contract negotiations and human resources.

- Previously, as CFO at Stussy, spearheaded the beta development of a new integrated apparel software and ERP solution called Full Circle®, which transformed the company from an outdated environment to a state-of-the-art desktop environment. Full Circle® is today the most prolific ERP solution on the West Coast in the action sports industry.

- Over 20 years of experience in accounting and finance.

- Received an accounting degree from Natal University in Durban, South Africa and holds an MBA degree from the University of California, Irvine.
