Ingrid Wiese’s Summary

Ingrid Wiese is a marketing and public relations specialist with fourteen years of experience executing communication strategies, building partnerships, developing sales promotions, and strengthening brand identities and awareness. Former clients include, the United Nations Foundation, United Nations World Food Programme, American Red Cross, Wildlife Conservation Society, Office of Security and Cooperation in Europe, New York City Opera, Pearl Theatre, The Franklin, Porsche Designs, The Buying Office, SavvyShopper.com and Nordstrom.

Ingrid is a talented interactive guerilla marketer, skilled in growing on-line communities and using social media tools to cultivate conversation. Performing consistently amongst the top 5% of her peers, she is a dynamic sales woman with a strong entrepreneurial drive.
