William Kapfer, Ph.D.’s Summary

Bill is always looking for new friends in the New York area and abroad. Bill Kapfer is a media professional with a Ph.D. in Media Studies. He served as the Merchandising Director for both Travel + Leisure Magazines and at Harper’s Bazaar—and most recently was the Publisher of GENRE Magazine & the Co-President of Window Media. He continues to consult for the travel industry. He moved to NYC from Seattle, where he was the Marketing & Corporate Relations Director for the Seattle International Film Festival. He began his media career as a publicist for Miramax films and in the newsroom at the MacNeil/Lehrer NewsHour.

William Kapfer, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

William started Kapfer Group over 15 years ago. The company is a full-service marketing and public relations provider—working with clients to define, clarify, and achieve their marketing and communications goals.
William's current and former clients include small businesses, non-profit organizations, film, arts, entertainment, print & online media organizations.
