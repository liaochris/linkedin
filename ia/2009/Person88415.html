Goran Šikić’s Summary

Worked as a journalist from 1994 for radio, tv, newspapers, magazines and online media. Graduated journalism at University in Zagreb, which gave me acces to broad
range of social interests - goverment issues, law, economics, information society, culture... Worked for a six years as editor-in-chief of daily news-portal www.monitor.hr.
Also, I was editor-in-chief of two magazines (cultural magazine 'Godine' and film magazine 'Total Film'), as well as editor and publisher of two books ('Lift', short-stories collection of various authors and '365', collection of columns by Ante Perković, published at Monitor).
Since 2006 I'm working for highly circulated weekly 'Privredni vjesnik' and writing about publishing and marketing industry, about music and film industry, as well as ICT industry. Since October 2008. I'm back in new media game, working at monitor.hr as editor-in-chief.
My goal: To be involved in all fascinating and world-changing things that future brings.
