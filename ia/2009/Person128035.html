Katie Morales’s Summary

I have been a Registered Nurse since 1985. My extensive nursing experience has focused on acute care nursing. I have served as a nursing instructor, written Policy and Procedure, and served as an item writer for the NCLEX exam. I am certified in Basic Disaster Life Support, Advance Disaster Life Support, Basic Cardiac Life Support, Advance Cardiac Life Support and adult medical/surgical nursing by the American Nurses Credentialing Center.
I am Licensed in Georgia and Tennessee

My practice settings have included hospitals, physician offices, diabetes education, pharmacy, and nursing instructor. I have worked in both rural and metropolitan settings and remain active and current at a small rural hospital.
My professional objective is to provide safe, therapeutic, and competent service to clients.
In 2006 I received my Bachelor of Science in Nursing from University of Phoenix. I received my Associates Degree in 1985 from Columbus State University in Columbus Ga.
I have been a legal nurse consultant since 2005 and have reviewed and screened in excess of 40 medical malpractice and personal injury cases and located expert witnesses. I am a member of the National Alliance of Certified Legal Nurse Consultants, American Nurses Association, and Sigma Theta Tau Honor Society. I serve on the board as the 2009 president of the American Association of Legal Nurse Consultants (Atlanta chapter). I am an author for the third edition of Legal Nurse Consulting: Principles & Practice, reviewer of Developing an Independent Legal Nurse Consulting Practice, and contributor to Emergency Nursing Bible. I volunteer with RN Response Network and serve on the Atlanta Bar Association Disaster Response Committee and Outreach Sub Committee. I also serve on the GNA ad hoc committee for Nursing Practice and reference committee and ad hoc legislative committee. I formed the Georgia Legal Nurse Consultant Society, organizing legal nurse consultants from across the state quarterly.

Katie Morales’s Specialties:

My current employers are Union General Hospital in Blairsville, Ga. where I have worked since February 2007 in the ICU. In 2005 I founded DisceRNment, LLC, in Ellijay, Ga. where I serve as a legal nurse consultant.
I have served as an independent contractor for Vaccination Services of America/Health Fairs of America in Omaha Nebraska since 2000.
