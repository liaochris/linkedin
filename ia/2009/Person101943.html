Jake Kent’s Summary

A US Navy Veteran and a certified Project Management Professional® (Project Management Institute®), Jake is President of K2C Real Estate Solutions. His business approach has contributed to the development of K2C Real Estate Solutions’ solid foundation that yields repeatable quality and client satisfaction. Jake specializes in advising and consulting clients in streamlining strategies, processes, culture, staffing models and optimizing information technology systems that yield maximum results. Along with his keen business acumen, he is a veteran investor, collaborating with prominent investors and elite clientele to acquire and sell investment properties and luxury homes. Jake has strong contract negotiation and risk mitigation skills that allow him to obtain the most lucrative and near risk-free deals possible for his clients.

Jake has enjoyed a successful career in building, managing and selling profitable businesses. In addition to running the day-to-day operations of K2C, Jake is an organizer and actively involved as a Director of Carolina Premier Bank, South Charlotte’s only community-minded and business-oriented bank and a charter member of the Matthew-Mint Hill Optimist Club.
