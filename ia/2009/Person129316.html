Kelly A. Meerbott’s Summary

My mission is simple (adopted from my mentor Michael Mendelssohn): combine evocative creative, persuasive powerful strategy, motivation, inspiration, and strategic thinking into a comprehensive marketing plan of action. I focus on making sure your internal workings are sound and complete prior to launching external marketing campaigns. I coordinate that strategy and put it in terms that is manageable, measurable and most of all profitable. So often, business owners need a key partner working "on" their business while they work "in" it. This key role is executed by me and my team.

We Are Partners tied to your business development and growth. Our clients may receive the following from their partnership with us:
-Strategic Development
-Creative Development
-Media Consultation and Relations
-Market Research
-Client Meeting/s
-Public Relations
-Promotional Strategy
-Implementation of internal and/or external marketing activities
