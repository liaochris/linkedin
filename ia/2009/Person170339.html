Myke Miller’s Summary

We are launching a compliance software tool to reduce the time, cost and risk of helping medical device companies’ mange the regulated obstacle course from concept to product development through market release and beyond to post market compliance management. We are providing a simple to start, simple to use, integrated solution designed specifically for small to mid-size companies, giving them dashboards, metrics and templates. Delivered using an inexpensive software-as-a-service model, we offer a nimble, yet comprehensive alternative to expensive, generic enterprise solutions.
We are having a great time using SaaS both as a product foundation to facilitate our own collaboration. We passionate about change the compliance dynamic so medtech innovators can focus on their product and not on reams of paper work.
