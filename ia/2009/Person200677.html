Robert Cohen, BBA, MBA, DBA’s Summary

In 1980, at 24 years old, I became the youngest full professor in Canada and founded Integrated mar.com: a channel focused marketing company that has helped 350 IT companies penetrate the SMB market through the IT channel.

In 1993 we expanded into publishing and launched eChannelLine in 1997...currently the world’s leading channel eNewsletter, providing 200,000 channel members with a daily synopsis of the news. In 2003 we launched ConnectIT – the only daily online B2B channel advocate pub. I personally act as the editor-in-chief of:
-Trusted Business Advisor, a weekly pub focusing on providing channel members with actionable business advice that will help them to successfully grow their business.
-Channel Advisor, providing hardware vendors, software developers & service providers with marketing and sales information.

In 1993 we founded the ChannelLine Advisory Council, a high-level industry thought leadership group comprised of 1,000 leading channel people.

In 2009 we launched Trusted Business Advisor, consisting of:
• Association:
i) Provides rules of engagement and peer-to-peer networking for the VAR community.
ii) Through the Vendor Channel Marketing Alliance (The Dream Team) we provide Vendors with information on best practices and develop/impliment programs for reaching SMBs via Channel programs.
• W3 VAR Enablement Tools: makes it easy for Vendors and Distributors to work effectively VARs to cost efficiently reach the SMB marketplace. The program provides easy to use, automated, feature and content rich tools.
• Business Education & Certification: allows business savvy VARs to easily differentiate themselves from other VARs and helps them provide better IT-centric business solutions. It’s tied into a channel advocacy and lead generation program.

What I do best is help IT companies successfully go-to-market. If you want to grow you business, please contact me.
