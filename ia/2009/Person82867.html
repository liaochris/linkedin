Gabriel Ramalho’s Summary

Advertising Professional / Graphic & UX Designer / Web Media Planner / Focus on new media and marketing trends.

Cases I'm proud of taking part in:

* Development of visual identity and signing for corporate seminars promoted by BNB and other huge entities, such as Brazil's Ministry for Agrarian Development, Brazil's Ministry for National Integration, Brazil's Ministry of Energy, Brazil's Communications Secretary and BID (Inter-American Development Bank), that took place at BNB's conventions' center;

* Development of a multimedia presentation for the Brazilian's Presidency of the Republic, presented on Tucuman's Regional Development's Seminar (Argentina, '07) by Minister Walfrido dos Mares Guia;

* UX Design and graphic interface to the BNB's mobile banking application and accessibility studies in order to allow the best service delivery to its users.

* Adaptation of the BNB's Portal to make it accessible to visually handicapped people, being consultant on a multidisciplinary team.

* Development of analysis and measurements studies on the BNB's Portal statistics, usability and users' habits, allowing a redesign study which culminated on increasing in 300% the monthly visits, consolidating the Portal as a business tool and being awarded with the Regional Aberje Award on Corporative Communications, in 2006.

* Member of the workgroup responsible for the Project of the BNB's Internal Corporate Portal, based on a corporate desktop interface with resources focusing on collaboration and knowledge management.

* Development of cross-media internal communications campaigns with creative use of supportive tools as hot-sites and advergames.

* Development of strategies focusing on new marketing trends, increasing market share and optimization of ad vehiculation costs.

* Planning of social media actions to BNB and CCBNB, developing guidelines and coordinating processes and strategies, such as to the Forum de Comunicação do Governo Federal no Nordeste.

Gabriel Ramalho’s Specialties:

Altough being a designer, today I'm more focused on planning and analysis on social media and corporate communications. Other specialties: Consulting on Usability and accessibility, UX interface design, Graphic Projects, Visual Identity to corporate seminars, Adwords campaigns setup and management, Cross-media campaigns, Analysis and Measurement of results on web media campaigns.
