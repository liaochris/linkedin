Tim Molendijk’s Summary

Intrigued by the power of computers since the age of eight, when my parents bought our family a personal computer. Wrote my first little computer programs at age 12 and when the internet arrived in our home a few years later I knew I would be spending my life working (or better: living) with it. Notwithstanding the impressive developments that we have seen so far, I am a firm believer in vast unexplored terrain being still ahead of us. In my desire to be part of this future I decided to startup a business and finish my studies in my spare time. I hope this opportunity will enable me to operate in the front-line of all that is yet to come for the rest of my professional career.
