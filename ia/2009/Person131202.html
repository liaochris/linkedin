Kevin Napper’s Summary

Energetic and highly accomplished Facility Management decision-maker with extensive Project Management and Facilities Management success for a Fortune 500 retail drug store with over 523 retail sites in five Western States and Hawaii. I have a comprehensive understanding of facilities management, building operations, energy management, safety, regulatory compliance, and vendor management. I am now looking for an opportunity to bring my experience and expertise to an organization capable of using me as a building block for their future growth.

Accomplishments:

Managed many different aspects of retail facilities including, HVAC, Lighting, Fire Protection, Pest Control, Trash, Project Manager and Energy for all Longs Drugs sites, including 523+ retail sites.

Provided leadership direction for over 70 different facilities related vendors to ensure accurate and timely completion of store repairs and maintenance.

Effective team player that worked as a liaison between Longs Drugs Store Managers, District Managers, Group VP’s and vendors for facility related issues.

Earned a reputation for ability to multitask and implementing decisions on facilities management, energy cost reduction, time management and customer service issues.

Developed and administered a capital improvement budget of over $3 million a year, utilizing fiscal analysis, budgetary planning and cost control.

Managed a $5.4 million Energy Management System (EMS) web-base retrofit with demand response capability for over 220 retail stores. Projected annual energy savings of over $1.5 million a year. Worked with utilities such as PG&E, SCE, SDG&E on customized energy rebates that will total over $1.1 million.

Responsible for more than $2 million in utility rebates for various energy efficiency upgrades

Developed and implemented an energy reduction program in 2001 to 2003 that saved Longs Drugs over $4.5 million.

Identified over $500,000 in billing errors over the last 5 years.

Kevin Napper’s Specialties:

Knowledgeable with various different Energy Management Systems (EMS), including Trane Tracer, Trane Tracker, Comtrol, and Johnson Metasys.

Member of the Energy Committee for (CRA) California Retailers Association.

Chain Store Age, Store Planning, Equipment, Construction Services (SPECS) Advisory Board Member
