  • anchor, reporter, producer, director

    Number of the Russian TV-channels, production houses.

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    September 1991 — May 2005 (13 years 9 months)

    Started at the age of 14 as a trainee at the MTK channel (local Moscow channel). During the 1991-2005 period was a reporter, anchor, director, producer. Reported for well-known Russian television programmes like «Namedni» (NTV channel, 1994-95) and «Vremya» (ORT channel, 1999-2000). Presented 9 programmes, mostly entertainment ones (can provide the videos). Produced two of them and also directed one of them. The programme «Muzykalny prospekt» (The Music highway) was the most successful. I co-produced it together with Yuri Borisov (CEO, Yauza TV-production Limited) and presented the show. It ran from 1996 to 1998 at the STS channel at prime-time and gained the second highest rating score among the channel`s programmes (after the "Chas Sovy" (Night Hour) talk show).
    Was also a staff screenwriter for the ORT`s Popular science films department (2003-2004).
    Made political commercials as a creator and a director of photography during the regional elections in Russia (2002, 2003) and the parlament elections in Ukraine (2002).
    Worked in the field of distribution: collaborated with Oleg Blinov, deputy GM of the Videofilm distribution company in 2005.
