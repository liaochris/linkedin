Chris Schrade’s Summary

Calasanz Physical Arts is the most sophisticated martial arts and fitness training on the planet. The training is a fine blend of ancient wisdom and modern sports conditioning set in a 1 on 1 environment and therefore is customizable to any person, any age, size, shape, athletic ability, and martial arts experience.

We focus on holistic conditioning, meaning strength, endurance, flexibility, coordination, balance, muscle unity, agility, reactions, & speed, using various martial arts, the Calasanz System Exercises, conventional bodyweight exercises, and unique functional exercises with odd objects.

The 1 on 1 environment allows us to customized a program based on your desires and abilities and have experience working with gifted athletes, the physically challenged and everyone in between.
