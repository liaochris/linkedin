Karen Highland’s Summary

After many years as a teacher, including full-time, part-time and tutoring, I got my real estate license and joined my husband in the business. He has been an agent for 16 years, and we are building a Team, The Highland Real Estate Group.

My role on the Team is website management, lead generation, and contact management. I share the office management with Chris and the interaction with buyers and sellers. He is a great negotiator and handles the initial sale or buy, and I handle contract to close issues.

Together, we make one great realtor!
