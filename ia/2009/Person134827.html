Kristin Conrad’s Interests:

I am fascinated on both a personal and professional level in design that makes people want to interact with something. Whether this is a website, a poster, a computer screen, a mobile device or a piece of furniture, the underlying principles are the same. Great interaction design drives which technologies and skills I seek out and aquire (and this acquisition is endless!). On a personal level I enjoy art from the 1900's to the 1960s, music with a strong sense of rhythm including jazz, being outdoors, and understanding the "frameworks" that people and cultures structure their lives around.
