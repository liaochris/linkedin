David Blackwood’s Summary

DAVID M. BLACKWOOD

General Counsel - McGladrey Capital Markets
Assistant General Counsel - RSM McGladrey

David Blackwood was appointed General Counsel of McGladrey Capital Markets (formerly RSM EquiCo Capital Markets) in April 2001 and Assistant General Counsel of RSM McGladrey in September 2007. McGladrey Capital Markets is a global investment banking firm specializing in middle-market M&A, corporate finance and fairness opinions. RSM McGladrey (together with McGladrey & Pullen) is the fifth largest provider of accounting, tax and business consulting services in the United States.

Before joining McGladrey Capital Markets and RSM McGladrey, Mr. Blackwood was Executive Vice President and General Counsel for Canon Computer Systems a $1.4B computer peripheral systems company. Prior to that, he practiced law at several firms in New York City, primarily in the corporate transaction field: Dorsey & Whitney, Squire Sanders & Dempsey, and Thacher Proffitt & Wood.

Mr. Blackwood holds a B.A. degree from Cornell University, and a J.D. degree (with Specialization in International Law) from Cornell Law School. He is active in national and local bar and legal industry activities, having served on the Board of the Southern California Chapter of the Association of Corporate Counsel and as Chairman of the Orange County Bar Association’s Corporate Counsel Section.
