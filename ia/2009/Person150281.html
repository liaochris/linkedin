Maria Piscopo’s Summary

Marketing seminars and workshops for artists, graphic designers,photographers and other creative professionals. Author of The Photographer's Guide to Marketing & Self-Promotion, 3rd Edition and The Graphic Designer and Illustrator's Guide to Marketing and Promotion both published by Allworth Press. Twenty five years experience teaching at art and design colleges such as Art Center College of Design in Pasadena, Laguna College of Art & Design and for Dynamic Graphics Training as well as local colleges. Speaker at industry conferences and writer for industry magazines such as Dynamic Graphics Magazine, Graphic Artists Guild newsletter, Adbase Newsletter CAPIC newsletter, Rangefinder, Step inside design, Shutterbug magazine and Communication Arts magazine.
