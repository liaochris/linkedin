Noelle Hunt Bennett’s Summary

"I'm a great believer in luck, and I find the harder I work the more I have of it." Thomas Jefferson

I help take creative projects and products to delivery by partnering with decision makers and vendors, finding the right talent, creating and sticking to a plan that leads to success for growing companies. Fast and accurate ramping inside budgetary guidelines is my specialty. I am focused on Casual Games, Mobile Entertainment and handheld games products.

I completely enjoy the casual/online games and digital entertainment world and have a high level of respect and admiration for the amazing talent that keeps this industry constantly innovating and creating exciting products in positive environments. A good philosophy for me is to stay aligned with the project needs, keep current in the field, and offer fresh perspectives. I advocate for top talent, and use instinct, feedback and input to pick creative and business superstars, while protecting and building the company’s reputation for making the best products on the market.

I am committed to the to the activities and progress of the organization Women in Games International, and am currently serving as the Secretary of the Board

A humanitarian above everything else, I founded CambodiaHope.org, a private organization dedicated to stemming the human trafficking problem in developing Asian countries, and KenyanHope.org, providing educational and health sustainability opportunities for 31 amazing young ladies in Kenya.

As the worlds of global progress and human rights issues collide, my advocacy becomes more focused and I find many opportunities to combine my passions.

More information on my current client, Big Fish Games, Inc. can be found on the website: www.bigfishgames.com or by emailing me directly anytime. nhunt@nhnorthwest.com

More information about my human rights endeavor can be found on www.cambodiahope.org and www.kenyanhope.org. Partnerships in these efforts are always appreciated!
