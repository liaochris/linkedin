John Holstein’s Summary

GeoSpatial Metrics (GSM) remains an international company specialized in GeoSpatial aerial applications and spatial data cubes. To put it simply we count things, repurpose data and add geospatial meaning to create actionable business intelligence and/or resalable data. While we are great integrators’ of GIS mobile data collection sources, we shine at turning data into spatial data cubes, mining the data and creating innovative new revenue sources. We license and build custom solutions in the aerospace (UAV- unmanned aerial vehicles), homeland security, healthcare and biohazard industries in both the private and public sectors. We are strategic partners of Top Eye View (www.topeyeview.com) and VueLogic. (www.vuelogic.com)

Conversational in Russian with proficient reading and writing skills • Basic knowledge of Spanish and German • Experienced with multi-national business environments and cultures; successful business engagements in Canada, Israel, Russia and United States • Nuclear Regulatory Commission Authorized User • Licensed Nuclear Pharmacist • Registered Pharmacist • Senior Conflict Resolution Facilitator
