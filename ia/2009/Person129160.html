Keith Warrick’s Summary

I am an IT Spark Plug.

I use my passion as a compelling communicator, penchant for building strong team relationships and high task focus to act as an agent of change that keeps software process improvement and reengineering projects on track to improve companies’ operational efficiencies and bottom lines.

My expertise and skills are in full life cycle software development activities demonstrates a proven track record in the areas of business analysis, quality assurance analysis, technical writing and project management spanning the telecom, financial services, insurance and information technology services industries.

My experience includes CMM software development environments and software migrations/implementations.

My focus has been on custom software development initiatives as a product manager, senior business analyst/business analyst as an expert in requirements planning and execution in client server and web development areas that use Oracle, JAVA, .Net, Citrix and MS-SQL Server architecture.

• Exemplary Communication Skills
• Relationship Builder
• Strong customer focus in work style.

My professional image has always been characterized by managers and peers alike as being dedicated, thorough and dependable.
