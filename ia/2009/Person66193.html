Dorrit Gruijters’s Summary

Coincide means “to occupy the same relative position” or “to happen at the same time” or “to agree exactly, as in opinion”. Coincide BV brings people together in time, place and opinion. We organize network trips and events, mostly within the healthcare and new media markets.

Since 2003 I have been a Partner of InterimIC. InterimIC is a virtual network of entrepreneurs, consultants and business developers. Our core strengths are innovation, inspiration and implementation. InterimIC is established in 2001 and is known for its passion, involvement, knowledge and results.

My 2009 projects will include:
* Digital Perestroika 2.0 - June 2009 - A new media business trip to Sint Petersburg and Moscow. Participants are innovators, entrepreneurs or otherwise inspiring new media people.
* Long-term Care Study trip - fall
* Singapore Healthcare Innovation Trip - January 2010
* Veelbelovend Vastgelopen - Een studie naar waarom innovaties in de zorg mislukken. In opdracht van Ministerie van VWS.
* Nationale Congres- en Vergaderclassificatie. Inspecties van congresaccommodaties.

Dorrit Gruijters’s Specialties:
