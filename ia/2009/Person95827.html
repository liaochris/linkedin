Chris Hough’s Summary

I began to work in the IT sector whilst studying Electrical Engineering at the University of Liverpool. By working as a freelance web designer I built and maintained many sites for small local businesses. Having graduated with honours I expanded my fledging small business and began to work with local marketing companies as well as local SMEs. Having run my own business for a little over a year I decided that I was too young to be a sole trader and so began employment as a developer at a large financial website in order to gain further experience. Within twelve months I had been promoted to team leader of the development team and, six months after that, was successfully promoted again to the team leader of a different department; Search Engine Optimisation.

Having run the SEO department for a little over twelve months I moved to a different company in Manchester so that I could further develop my SEO skills and serve a range of clients. Despite being promoted to SEO Team Leader within the first three months I was unable to stay at this position for very long due to the arduous commute I faced each day. And so, within twelve months of having left Chester for Manchester I was back in Chester, this time working for a large Travel Company as the Affiliate Marketing Executive.

Having worked in a number of different IT departments and putting what I’d learnt into practice I left the Travel Company in 2007 to once again focus on my own business; to design, create, optimise and maintain a number of websites for external clients and for myself.

I am passionate about user experience, social interaction, web optimisation and usability.
