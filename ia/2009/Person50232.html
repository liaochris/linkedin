Craig Stanley, CFA’s Summary

Senior investment management professional with extensive domestic and international experience in both alternative (hedge funds, fund of funds and venture capital) and tradtional (fixed income and global equity) asset management. Uniquely skilled in capital development, but also experienced in the research, compliance and admininstration areas.

Considerable experience restructuring boutique investment houses, turning around poorly performing firms and seeding new funds.

Distinctive and highly developed philosophy of conveying the unique attributes of an asset management firm and building trust in prospective investors.

Significant international work experience in Europe, Australia, Japan, Brazil and Argentina.
