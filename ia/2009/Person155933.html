Matthew Krzysztofik’s Summary

Internet entrepreneur with MBA and over a decade of experience working abroad primarily in Poland. I am Polish American, born in Bridgeport, Connecticut. My friends in Poland refer to me by my Polish name Maciej. (Yes, Maciej in English is Matthew, although it does not translate as smoothly the other way around).

Linked In Open Networker. Networkers and fellow libertines welcomed. I accept all invites. My email: linkedin@ jobs.pl.

Seeking international contacts with employers and recruiters requiring Polish staff to optimize their businesses, providing clients with highly competent staff in various sectors such as Construction and Care including both managerial and specialist positions. In spite of the financial turmoil we continue to search for ambitious sales staff for one of Poland's largest job sites. Seeking partners in Europe, Poland and abroad. Our job site is seeking to partner with companies that provide HR solutions, in particular applicant management software.
