Eric Cornavaca’s Summary

Partner and co-Founder of Buffalo BioSciences, LLC since late 2005. Mr Cornavaca brings over 20 years of experience in Life Sciences in the areas of product development, intellectual property, licensing, international marketing operations and strategic business development necessary to commercialize innovative discoveries.

Mr. Cornavaca received a dual Bachelors of Science degree from the University of Massachusetts in Chemistry and Economics and holds a Masters of Science degree in Biophysical Chemistry from the University of Denver. He also has completed advanced executive management coursework at Penn State University.

Mr. Cornavaca’s career spans various business and technical leadership roles at Invitrogen Corporation including Director of Corporate Development for both GIBCO and BioReliance business units as well as Business Director of Gibco Research. Prior to Invitrogen, he participated in International Marketing activities at Life Technologies and advanced process sciences research & development at Life Technologies, Genentech and Centocor.

Fluent in Spanish and proficient in Portuguese, Mr. Cornavaca has traveled extensively abroad and worked where he developed a unique perspective of offshore business customs and a strong network of contacts to affect business deals in these markets.

Mr. Cornavaca is a member of several Life Science boards such as the Industrial Advisory Board at the University at Buffalo Center for Advanced Biomedical and Bioengineering Technology (UB CAT), the Niagara University’s Business Advisory Board, and the Foundation for Healthy Living (FHL) and Biomed Upstate Medical Conferences advisory boards for Upstate New York.

Mr. Cornavaca also volunteers his time to support Life Science commercialization activities as a coach at both Buffalo’s Panasci Competitions (MBA Business Plans) and UB Center of Excellence’s Annual Pre Seed Workshops (PSW’s) for high technology start-up companies.
