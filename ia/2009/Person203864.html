rosa maria sarri’s Summary

music therapist and music art therapist with extensive work experience.
composer, guitarist, play various instruments (bass, double bass, piano....) with many active bands and many projects on the assets side (BazArt, Elektrolaiv, Melma Calma,…).
graduated at the conservatory " cherubini" (FI) in theory and solfeggio.
graduated at the university of music art therapy in the globality of the languages.
given a degree in musicoterapia, she has achieved a specialization in "music therapy of the developmental's age".
*from 13 years she carries out her activity of freelance in the field of music therapy.
*realizes sound dubbings, live electronics and musical drifts for many occasions (performing arts, interactive pathways, short films, images,…). also deals with sound designing environments and sets internal and external environment.
*plays his trade in schools (all levels), clinics, hospitals and hospice (where takes care of people with terminal ill and coma, in pediatric oncohaematology and other occasions) and in centers for the rehabilitation, for "specific diseases" (anorexia, bulimia, depression) and for the handicap.
*carries on her business in parallel opportunities as centers for holistic health, planning of routes and courses (burn out, sublimation and metamorphosis of conflict;...).
*involved in training and in-training for music therapy, doctors, nurses, psychotherapists, educators, and almost all the professions that are active in social work.
*performs qualitative and quantitative research in the field.

*techniques and methods:
The Humanistic Music Therapy (C. Rogers), the Anthroposophical Music Therapy (Steiner,...); the clown therapy with all its things (juggling, mime,...); Music Art Therapy in the Globality of Languages and any form of discipline or thought she has learned, to improve the Quality of Life, the humoral and emotional tone and viable State.
