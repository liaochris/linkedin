Doron Tal’s Summary

PDF - "Living" this format since 2001. Specialist in the format itself, plug-ins for Adobe Acrobat and other applications based on Adobe technologies.
The core technology of my company is a self and independed PDF engine, which I wrote myself, that allows to create, modify and manipulate PDF files for different needs such as: Output solutions and Digital Signatures.
The combination of my experties in that field and creative thinking has made very interesting digital document solutions for projects in Israel such as: ISA (Israel Securities Authority - Magna project), The Israeli Court of Justice systems, Orange Israel (Cellular).

BiDi (bi-directional) support for unsupported RTL applications - This was my first famous project - EasyHEB & EasyHEB for Web which was the first applications for converting LTR to RTL and vice versa.
Netvision, the biggest ISP in Israel, has sponsored EasyHeb for web. EasyHEB became a huge success at the time, giving the best solution for the Hebrew language problem in an unsupported Hebrew applications such as Freehand, Photoshop, Premiere, Corel Draw, and Visual html web pages. (1998).

After havy research of that field in Microsoft products, I wrote a BiDi algorithm to convert Hebrew and Arabic LTR to RTL paragraphs.
Since then I'm continue developing that algoritm. I have made a Hebrew application support for Adobe GoLive (2001), a CAD application and a BiDi support for PDF format.

Developer - I have started developing in 1995 and still does (just love it). I don't like to call myself a developer, but an artist. For me, coding is way to make my thought and ideas run in the computer.
As a technology artist and expert, I always learn the latest trends and technologies.
I develope in: .NET c++, vb and c#; php; java; delphi, magic.
Other fields of experties: XML, XHTML, HTML, CSS, XPATH, XSL, XSLT, AJAX, SOAP/WSDL (Web Services), SOA, SQL, MySQL, MSSQL.
