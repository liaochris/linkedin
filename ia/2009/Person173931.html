Nichole Parker’s Summary

Drawing on my international experience, tax and intercultural know-how and extensive professional network, I partner with expatriates and employers to overcome the tax and cultural complexities of assignments abroad by providing intelligent solutions that demystify international taxes.

With a successful international tax practice focusing on both Italian and U.S. taxes for internationally mobile individuals, I am a trusted advisor to expatriates and employers from all corners of the globe. By sharing with clients my technical expertise and passion for the international lifestyle, I enable my clients to pay less taxes legally, plan for their financial futures and avoid the stress and confusion that so often accompany living and working abroad. Through my global network of tax consultants, I can assist my clients wherever they may be.

My corporate clients include companies with anywhere from 1 to 500 expatriates in varied industries including pharmaceuticals, energy, kitchen appliances and automobiles. Given my keen understanding of the tax, social security and administrative challenges of running an expatriate program, I often work in-house to better align expatriate programs to meet company goals. My individual clients are expatriates who hail from all over the world and pursue careers in fields as varied as engineering, cinema, make up artistry, finance and publishing. What unites my varied clients is the need for my experience and guidance through the a complex field of expatriate taxation.

My credentials include:

 honor degrees from Georgetown University and Tulane Law School;
 member of the New York and New Jersey bar associations;
 worked for AndersenLegal, Deloitte, Camozzi & Bonissoni, Studio di Consulenza Giuridico-Tributaria (current);
 member of National Association of Tax Professionals.

In my free time, I like to read, swim, practice yoga, travel and explore Rome.
