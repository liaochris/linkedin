Nakiah Cherry Chinchilla’s Summary

I am currently the Director, Public Relations & Corporate Communications for Oberon Media, the world's leading multi-platform casual games company. I oversee the Public Relations & Corporate Communications department and am the sole spokesperson. I am responsible for the development, execution and management of press strategy and group-wide marketing and communications efforts; both internal and external. I also develop and maintain a strategic perspective-based on marketplace and consituent needs and satisfaction-in organizational direction, program and services, and decision making.

Previously, I was the Manager, Corporate Communications, Digital Media & Consumer Products at MTVN/Nickelodeon Kids & Family Group. While there I managed publicity for $6 billion-dollar global Nickelodeon & Viacom Consumer Products business including publicizing all lines of business such as apparel, packaged goods, toys, electronics, games, home entertainment, music and more. I also partnered to develop, execute and manage all press strategy for all digital lines of business such as the extensive virtual world and gaming portfolio of websites and communities, download-to-own programs, mobile, broadband, etc.

I’ve had a successful career in the media for more than ten years. I have used my knowledge and interest in digital media to generate ideas and implement business strategies that have helped to position the companies/brands that I've worked with as leading digital media companies. I’ve prepped many talent and executives for interviews and done media training when needed. I have a wealth of knowledge in the digital and multi-platform arenas and have a keen eye for the future of technology and how it affects publicity and brand strategy.
