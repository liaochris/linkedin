Using eBay as an Add-On Business Model

Business owners are trained with an easy to use business solution to enhance revenues by using eBay to expand their markets beyond the local consumer to the 181 million registered eBay users. Onsite Training with Follow Up Support: Onsite and Online Support & teleconferencing

SCOPE OF SERVICES- Onsite Instruction and Training:
a. Setting up and using an eBay account.
b. Marketing on eBay (your company and your products)
c. How to use eBay effectively to sell excess or slow moving inventory,
product returns, unneeded assets, end of life products & more.
d. Researching and creating listings that will attract buyers.
e. Enhance listings with better descriptions and photography
f. Open and use a PayPal account to simplify getting paid.
g. Monitor sales to know exactly what is working and what is not.
