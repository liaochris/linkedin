Ron Sparks’s Summary

I have been doing web design as a freelancer for the past almost 6 years now, with a focus in html/css and flash websites.


As a whole I focus now on web design and how to make design and function work together to get a great working and looking user interfaces, that markets through image and action

For 2 years I lived in Florida and worked for a company as a marketing assistant and in-house designer. Learning a lot about the marketing and business side of design.

I moved to Seattle, WA in early 2008 because I love the northwest and I really enjoy the culture of art and design in the area. Sense I have been in Seattle I have freelanced on projects for HP, Windows Mobile, Dreamworks, a number of smaller and and larger agencies including VML, DraftFCB, Hadely Green and spend 5 and half months working at Zillow as part of there UX team.

I have started my own company Stratosphere Design, and have a number of clients. I am not sure what the future is weather I go on to work with one of the great companies here in the northwest or continue freelancing or maybe some of both.

I have:
Designed 30 plus websites, Design 8 full flash websites, Developed 4 Online Stores, Art Directored for Product Photo Shoot, Branding Work for INC 500 Company, Manage and maintain nation music artist websites, worked on national magazine ads, and I have designed multiple artist album art projects.
