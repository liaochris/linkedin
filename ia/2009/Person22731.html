Avery Moon’s Summary

15 years experience building and delivering mission-critical software, while growing passionate world-class teams (both local and distributed / international).

Demonstrated track record across diverse organization sizes (Fortune 500 to early-stage) with varying roles that blend architecture, distributed technical engineering management (on-shore, contractor, and offshore), and hands-on engineering. Proven success using methodologies ranging from agile to TDD, in development cycles ranging from bi-weekly to quarterly. Experience building and supporting software for both high-volume consumer Internet and enterprise markets, including all delivery models: browser (SaaS), appliance, packaged server-side, and thick client.

As a venture-funded founder, possess deep understanding of how successful commercialization depends upon effective interface of engineering execution with business strategy, product management, and sales.

Avery Moon’s Specialties:

Leadership, management, architecture, and dev of web apps and server-side systems of all flavors. Building and leading distributed teams, including dual-shoring, spanning the entire development lifecycle. Java server stack, core / POJO and J2EE / lightweight (Spring / Hibernate). Diverse domain model experience from security and workflow to numerous vertical-specific. Deep experience integrating disparate enterprise apps using diverse flavors of integration, most recently SOA, WS-*, and XML.
