Gerson Cabrera’s Summary

Gerson is a private contractor and founder of Cabrera & Associates, Inc. He has several years of experience in business development, real estate investing, commercial investments, fulfillment operations, logistics, customer support, administration, and security operations. He is continuing his education in Marketing Management and International Business at Florida Atlantic University and he is a member of the Professional Business Fraternity of Delta Sigma Pi, Zeta Phi Chapter. He is also a Gunnery Sergeant in the United States Marine Corps with 12 years of service in Military Occupation Specialties of Amphibious Reconnaissance, Machinegun Infantry and Heavy Equipment Engineering. He deployed to Iraq in 2005 in support of OIF III and 2008-2009 in support of Department of Defense Security Operations. Currently possess DOD secret clearance.
