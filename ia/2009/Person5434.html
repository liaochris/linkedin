Ajay Kukreja [ajay@outofbox.org] Toplinked.Com’s Summary

Specialists in Recruitment & Manpower Consulting, Top Management Executive Search, Board Consulting. Strong presence in emerging markets of Asia which specifically includes India & markets of Singapore, Malaysia, Hong-Kong and Middle East.

For years, Out-Of-Box Consulting has been a strategic resource and leader in executive recruitment, conducting senior-level searches for Global & Multinational Corporations across Asian Markets with strong access to close-knit top management executive-networks. Today, Out-Of-Box Consulting is the premier provider of executive human capital solutions, with services ranging from Executive Search, CEO recruitment, Board Consulting and Leadership Development. We have achieved a growing reputation for success in difficult or sensitive searches.

Our team works closely with clients and candidates to craft successful human capital strategies and solutions. Our partnerships with Top B-Schools, proprietary databases, seamless network built over the years, time-proven search process and broad industry and regional expertise provide the competitive advantage necessary to recruit and develop world-class leadership teams. We work closely with the Chairman, Director, VP, creating a collaborative work environment and assist them in recruiting top levels for their organization.

STRONG HOLD IN ASIA
After working with diverse International Business Communities and Practice Groups, our team has accumulated wealth of talent and expertise on how markets work globally. Strong understanding of Global Market dynamics, coupled with our in-dept expertise within the Emerging Asian Markets, gives us an edge to act as globally-local partners to our clients in helping them attract the best talent. Our team has extensive professional experience in the Emerging Markets of Asia that specifically includes India, Singapore, Hong Kong, Malaysia and the Middle East.
