Derek Hatchett’s Summary

Experienced entrepreneur and team leader. Creative problem solver and flexible team player. Over thirteen years experience leading high tech development projects for the consumer, medical and industrial markets. Have taken numerous products from cradle to grave with outstanding financial results. Have been involved with over a dozen start-up companies (venture and non-venture backed) either as a full-time employee, consultant or partner. Have been solely responsible for or have been a key team member in over fifty product development efforts. Significant background in strategic marketing (branding/positioning).

Derek Hatchett’s Specialties:

Start-up companies, building and leading teams, product design and development, project planning and execution, design for manufacturing and assembly, process and factory design, domestic and overseas supplier/partner relationships.

    (Design industry)

Primary duties of project leader included: crafting new project proposals, interfacing with key client personnel, resource scheduling and allocation, designing products using Pro/E, coordinating efforts between industrial design, engineering and production, prototyping models, creating detailed drawings for tooling and assembly, creating bills-of-materials, and qualifying vendors for production and assembly.
