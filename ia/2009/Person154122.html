Martin van Heun’s Summary

I have 20 years experience in designing the instrumentation and process control part of advanced energy and process systems, like: process pilot plants, industrial cogeneration and (combined cycle) waste heat boilers. My aim is to deliver the client indepth knowledge how to develop the instrumentation and industrial automation part of process pilot plants and energy systems.This will result in setting up P&ID's , functional descriptions and further basic engineering documents. Good knowledge of ATEX and SIL functional safety guidelines can be incorporated.

Martin van Heun’s Specialties:

Instrumentation and process control expertise for energy systems and process systems. From basic design to installation and commissioning support.
