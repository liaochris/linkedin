Jim Foley’s Summary

My experience has mostly been in management positions, rising from a Sales Manager in Chicago to various Branch Manager positions in Ohio, South Carolina, and Illinois, and now finally as the Director of Business Development in Chicago. In July, my position was eliminated as a result of the Morgan Stanley Smith Barney Joint Venture.
