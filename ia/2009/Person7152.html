Alessandra Luzzi’s Summary

Alessandra has 10 years experience in Group Facilitation and Organizational Change.
She has a rich educational background in Literature, Drama, Sociology and Arts.
She’s been working as a Senior consultant with Infinity, Creative Institute Of Experiential Learning and Organizational Development, since 1999.
Both as Development Specialist and as Research & Development Specialist, she has gained experience with a wide variety of facilitation techniques and strategic planning processes.
She has worked with main multinational companies and follows some of them personally as key account.
