Brendan Smith brendan@motiveinteractive.com’s Summary

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

    April 1996 — May 1999 (3 years 2 months)

    Founded and operated a full-service interactive marketing agency. This agency built seven distinct web based businesses all reaching profitability within less than four months of launch. Core focuses: Email Marketing, Viral Marketing, List Management, Consumer Surveys, IT training, Travel, Sweepstakes, and Real Estate. Market Ready web properties generated a user membership of over 2 million users. Market Ready Inc created, owned, and maintained 100% of its software and data base solutions. At its pinnacle Market Ready Inc properties were listed as some of the most visited websites online by Nielsen//NetRatings and content from our travel properties have been cited on many national and regional publication such as newyorkmetro.com, The Village Voice, The New York Times, The Washington Post, Travel And Leisure and more...
