  • KPMG Corporate Finance LLC

    KPMG

    (Partnership; Accounting industry)

    January 2001 — May 2002 (1 year 5 months)

    I served as the Midwest Regional Senior Marketing Manager for KPMG's Corporate Finance LLC investment banking practice. The practice served Fortune 500 and middle-market companies that were seeking financial advisory services. The practice served a diverse range of industry groups. Corporate Finance services include: mergers & acquisitions, sales and divestitures, debt advisory services, leveraged and management buyouts, and pricing analysis and fairness opinions. The practice was relatively new and I directed all marketing and communications initiatives on both a regional and national level that were designed to enhance name recognition, build brand awareness and communicate the practice’s products and services and unique value propositions to our target market. These efforts often provided new business opportunities to KPMG Partners and Managing Directors. I also have worked closely with our global headquarters in the UK on developing U.S. branding and messaging

  • National Proposal Manager

    (Legal Services industry)

    January 1998 — December 1999 (2 years)

I worked closely with industry partners, senior managers and consultants in developing unique and compelling documents to help KPMG Consulting win business. I helped write non-technical sections, provided strategic direction while working closely with SWAT Teams and technical Subject Matter Experts (SME) to develop content, messaging, and value-driven solutions. I also created high level solution-driven PowerPoint presentations for multi-million dollar client proposals directing both the messaging and creative content.

  • Marketing Manager

    (Legal Services industry)

Other key initiatives included developing and maintaining the content for the SSA web page, interacting with clients for the development of case studies and developing strategic internal and external communication campaigns.
