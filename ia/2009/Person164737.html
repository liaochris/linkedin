Mike Peredo’s Summary

3D Technical Art Director with 15+ years of experience in computer graphics.

Adaptable, flexible and MacGyver-like in my ability to build creative and effective solutions with a minimum of resources. I wear whatever hat is needed to make things work.

I specialize in designing and implementing solutions for high level problems. I speak and translate the needs of artists and designers to create effective production tools and pipelines. I manage people and resources to make things happen. I script, mockup, design, and outsource. I support artists in production both directly and through the creation of training and resource materials.

I have taught and developed 3D modeling and animation courses at a number of educational institutions and at Radical Entertainment.

I enjoy innovative projects with small teams in an environment of mutual respect where anything is possible. I am self managed and work best with a mandate and resources. I am rooted in Vancouver and only interested in local (or virtual) projects.

Mike Peredo’s Specialties:

Maya, CG effects, mel scripting, tool development, teaching, course development, 3D modeling, texturing, lighting, project management.
