Melissa McVeigh’s Summary

I am a senior digital leader with 12 years demonstrable accomplishments across strategy, creative, operations and delivery. I have also worked in the publishing industry for over 10 years in Australia.

Currently I am Digital Director at The Team. The digital practice at the Team is 25+ staff and I am responsible for the client services team, new business & strategy. This includes driving business growth while delivering tangible benefits to my clients. I am also on the digital management board supporting the MD Digital on managing day to day activities of the business.

I have a proven ability to quickly analyse business drivers and develop strategies and products to build customer engagement and grow the bottom line. My entrepreneurial spirit is combined with strong business acumen. It is a combination of wanting to develop innovative ideas into products utilising my excellent management skills in operations, finance and business development mixed with my ability to motivate, inspire and mentor multi-disciplinary teams.
