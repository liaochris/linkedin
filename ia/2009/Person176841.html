Norm Alger’s Summary

Prior to joining Digitas Health, Norm has steered creative for pharmaceutical companies such as Amgen, AstraZeneca, Bayer, GlaxoSmithKline, Johnson & Johnson, Lilly, Novartis, Ortho-McNeil, Par Pharmaceutical, Pfizer, Sanofi-Aventis, Wyeth, and ZLB Behring.

With 20 years of experience in the creative field, over 10 years of design and web development experience; he brings a deep understanding of audience behaviors and needs where creative is concerned. He has developed creative for touch points including branded and unbranded web sites, e-detailing (online and kiosk), adherence/compliance programs, online advertising, video (online, kiosk, convention, tutorial) as well as offline print initiatives.

Norm spent ten years with SSI (creative systems integrator) - Qwest Interactive as a Design Studio Manager with a migration to Director of Creative Services at Cadient Group after a management buyout for nearly five years and as a Creative Director for the Philadelphia office of Razorfish for two years. Norm is an award-winning Creative Director who is published and speaks often at Delaware Valley creative events. He is a graduate of the Art Institute of Philadelphia and holds an Associate Degree in Advertising Design.

Norm Alger’s Specialties:

Leveraging experience to arrive at creative solutions for clients based on business objectives which are driven by collaboration and results-oriented design.
