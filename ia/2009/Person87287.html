Graeme Jack’s Summary

Graeme is a product developer, inventor, master of creating logos, branding and identity guru and loves designing unique and interesting packaging.

As an Art Director at Wasserman + Partners Advertising in 2007, he worked on their largest campaign to date, spearheading the 2007 Drinking Driving Counterattack campaign.

In 2008, he again jumped forward to become the lead Art Director in rebranding one of Canada's largest corporations. The 3 billion dollar 5000 employee corporation ICBC, which was also Wasserman + Partners largest rebranding project ever.

On a daily basis Graeme is continually working on new projects and expanding his experience towards product development, web marketing and design. He also operates a highly popular blog for gadgets and inventions named OneMoreGadget.

Graeme is also a published children's book author and illustrator, part-time oil painter of statues in landscapes and enjoys a good frozen yogurt from the fruz.
