Kimberly Cecere’s Summary

Corporate Communicator. Journalist. PR Professional.

This has been my professional path for more than 15 years in both agency and corporate environments. Along the way, I’ve gained experience in multiple industries and developed a 360-degree view of client service. This is the driving force behind my business, ON Point Communications.

Established in 2002, ON Point serves corporate clients and small businesses in need of creative communications strategy, PR programming and professional writing services. In addition, On Point serves PR agencies looking to augment their service offerings.

Kimberly Cecere’s Specialties:

Customized PR program development and execution; media relations; social media strategy; issues management/preemptive communications; employee communications (publications, intranet); executive speech writing; corporate collateral (annual/charitable giving reports, corporate identity/standards manuals, HR/Organizational Development materials); brand communications (positioning/messaging); content strategy; marketing collateral (brochures, case studies, white papers, email marketing campaigns).
