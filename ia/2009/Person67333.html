Dr. Irene T. Boland’s Summary

Professional eLearning Strategist with diverse experience providing performance improvement through effective, engaging learning solutions.

I provide expert consulting to my clients in the fields of: instructional design, interface design, accessibility and usability development and testing, compliance, and technology translation for high-stakes communication. I can easily speak to the issues important to management, developers, creative talent, customers and stakeholders of all sorts—helping all parties understand and collaborate with each other successfully. My thorough background in the science of learning enables me to employ the best practices in applying learning theory to instructional design.
