Bidemi Ladipo’s Summary

I have nearly 7 years of experience in Nigerian Telecomms Sector at Starcomms (awarded Best Telecomms Company of the Year 2007 & Best Product of the Year 2008) and a pioneer staff who has grown the company and has grown with the company from a customer base of about 2000 customers to the current base of about 2 million customers,coming from the behind several larger operators to be the No. 1 CDMA operator today.
I have held key positions in handling pre and post core network implementation & migration issues when the company changed her core network from QUALCOMM to Ericsson and finally Huawei & have also been exposed to various billing/ provisioning/CRM applications, I trained several times abroad on the full multimedia call/contact centre applications from suppliers in South Africa & recently United Kingdom (UK).
I have managed various projects for the company like the National Contact/Call Centre Project- which involved sourcing of the property, technological set-up & staffing & finally overseeing day to day operations of the contact/call centre. I was appointed the head of the committee that rolled out the first set of service centres (Starcomms shops) & more recently, the Commercial Project Manager for the ABUJA rollout projects between Dec & Mar 2006.
I was (in Jan 2007) appointed Product Manager in the marketing dept with specialty in managing our highest revenue generating Product Segments.
I was awarded Best Marketing Staff of the Year 2007.My products are now launched as full brands.
My Product Segment iZAP 3G Broadband Product was awarded Best Product at the recently concluded Nigerian Telecomm Awards 2008.
