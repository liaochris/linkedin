Eric J. Silverman’s Summary

I welcome and want to work with companies that support change via social ventures

20 years of establishing value-add relationships as a sales director and founder of a b-to-b direct marketing firm.

My true calling is broking - helping to aggregate and stimulate communities of organizations. A CSR-focused executive, my focus is on ROI-driven companies, and social stewardship.

I absolutely believe in the means of cooperation, rather than self-limiting profiteering at a cost to our world. Customers are evolving and are demanding products and services with a green storyline. I help develop, market and sell sustainable products for small to mid-sized companies that wish to build brand and do CR-building for corporations, as well.

Thanks to those marketing and advertising specialists who taught me how best to start and succeed in running my firm.

One former manager describes me as "... Tireless and focused, who will have your employees' back and keep your firm’s initiative on track."

"Eric is a persistent, conscientious and down-to-earth sales professional. I’m glad our professional paths have crossed a couple of times, and would look forward to working with him again. He is a winner!"
