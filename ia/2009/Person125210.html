Jan Willem van Gijzen’s Summary

My career has taken me from being an (air) freight forwarding agent to managing a bonded warehouse; from managing the planning & admin department of an EDC to managing their intl. mail house; from consulting and implementing logistics (customs) software solutions to implementing/selling these solutions as a project/account manager. Now, I am going to being a consultant again and I’m ready to use this huge pack of experience in helping out companies in improving their Supply Chain achievements and/or helping with their interim projects and/or change programs.

Obviously, my field of expertise lies within the Customs domain, but is certainly not limited to it.

As per 1 February, TruEconomy will my new challenge! (www.trueconomy.com) and I challenge you to check it out!
