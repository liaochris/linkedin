Liviu Gheorghe Vanau’s Summary

I'm a journalist and media business developer since 1990, and I held top management positions in newspapers, radio, press agency, TV and Internet companies.
My clients and employers were either large media groups such as Media Pro or Realitatea-Catavencu, Ringier Romania, TVR: the Romanian National Television or companies such as British American Tobacco, Renault-Dacia, the Coca-Cola Company and Beiersdorf.

I am conducting research and development in some various social facets of Internet, Computer and Communication security & counter-terrorism issues.

I'm interested in inter-religious conflicts, and I currently do research for a book about the Zealot movement of the Holy Mountain Athos in Greece.

    This listing is but a reference to the exceptional work of William Blacker in Romania and Transylvania, work that brought the momentum that consolidated today's MET.
