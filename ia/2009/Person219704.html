Michael C. Cordell’s Summary

Michael C. Cordell is an author and screenwriter living in Los Angeles. Among his credits, Michael has written several short stories, essays, poems and screenplays as well as a number of articles relating to technology in the legal industry.

A collection of Michael's short works,"In The Foothills," was published in July 2008 by Foothills Villa Publishing and can be found on Amazon.com, BN.com and other fine booksellers' websites.

Michael C. Cordell’s Specialties:

Writing fiction, non-fiction, poetry, screenplays, stage plays
