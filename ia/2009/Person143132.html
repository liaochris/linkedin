Logan Henriquez’s Summary

Creative, customer focused product manager with a track record of building profitable products and effective teams. Planned and launched three products that earned over $100M in their first year, one of which was an entirely new technology and product category. Conceived and developed two innovative web sites in the visual search and social news categories.

As Director of Product Management, grew a division’s revenues from millions to billions, won a “Best Product” award, created the product and business plan that sold a startup for $102 Million, and was the marketing lead in raising a $5 Million A round for an early stage mobile venture.

Some products I have developed are
* newsforwhatyoudo.com - a social news site targeted at news that's relevant to your profession or hobby.
* www.searchartgalleries.com - created the first visual search engine for art from conception through software architecture and development.
* McDATA i10K director - lead the specification and launch of this product which won InfoWorld's 2006 "Best Director Switch" award and adoption by all major storage OEMs.
* Compaq's Proliant Fibre Channel Storage System Family - lead packaging and launch of this product which earned over 140 million dollars in its first year and defined a new market for the company.
* Software Engineer on Intuit's Quicken 2.0 - the leading checking software.

Interested in startups and established companies with strong teams and innovative ideas.
