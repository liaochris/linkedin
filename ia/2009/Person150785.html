I am especially interested in planning and executing sports for the benefit of others. Whether that is for youth to develop their confidence and self-esteem or for underdeveloped countries to live a better life.

I have excellent communication skills in which I acquired through many years of traveling around the world while growing up. As a result, this has helped me not only appreciate communication but the ability to reach out to more people through my language abilities (Fluent in Japanese, English and German with basic knowledge of Chinese and French).

Amazing teamwork skills in which I acquired through being an athlete (Basketball, Softball, Track& Field and Swimming) through out high school and college along with my participation on campus events and clubs (International Club, Student to Student Mentoring Program, American Marketing Association and Public Relation Student Society of America) during my college years.

I have learned how to play an important role as well as multitask and work under great time pressure while I enjoyed my experience at the Beijing 2008 Olympic Games.
