Michael L. Mattos’s Summary

First and foremost I enjoy consulting work within the payment processing industry - both from an acquiring and issuing side. I will consider serious opportunities where my experience creates additional revenue sources as a consultant, board member or even investor within the company.

Mr. Mattos brings more than fifteen (15) years extensive experience in sales management, marketing and business development. Twelve (12) of these years have specifically been serving the card processing industry - initially on the acquiring end of merchant processing and most recently with issuing cards to cardholders. During his tenure within the industry, Mr. Mattos experienced two large acquisitions/mergers, which ultimately helped with starting and running other companies. He currently owns three successful corporations and serves as the President/CEO and co-founder of each one.

Mr. Mattos’s entrepreneurial career has taken many “start up” ideas to development, to strategic positioning and ultimately to profitable success. His most recent accomplishment was forming a company called EduCard, LLC serving the HigherEd market to issue reloadable VISA Prepaid Debit cards for financial aid disbursement and selling the company after only 2 ½ years of operation. Mr. Mattos's commitment to integrity, honor and enthusiasm for life has helped others succeed in each of their own personal professions.

As the President/CEO of enCards, Mr. Mattos encourages direct access with every client for additional support, day-to-day management or simply to say hello.
