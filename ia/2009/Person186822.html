I started out my career as an engineer, but realized fairly soon that I liked getting results through working with people much better than I liked working directly with technology.

I've been a recruiter for over 20 years, and I still love introducing good people to each other and making matches that benefit everyone involved. I also love building a recruiting company that hires only outstanding people who feel the same way.

Please contact me if I can help you find great talent for your company, or if you'd like my help in finding your next job.
