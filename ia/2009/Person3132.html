Adam Rishe’s Summary

As director of internal audit at Bravo I am working to improve the control environment and enhance risk management. My position here will include guiding the company towards compliance with the NAIC Model Audit Rule (MAR) and bringing an enterprise-wide risk management program to Bravo.

I bring ten years of experience in internal audit oversight and execution for major companies across the country. I have also gained significant data analytic skills while serving as the Director of Financial Solutions at VitalSpring technologies.

Highlights:
• Assisting teams around the country to service multibillion dollar managed care clients in designing and appropriately focusing their engagements. Clients included: United Health Group, Kaiser Permanente, CareFirst BlueCross BlueSheild of Maryland, Healthnet, HealthNow, Coventry Health, and Magellan Behavioral Health.
• Designing, directing and executing internal audits in many key operational and financial areas within managed care companies.
• Revamping the internal audit function and methodology at the University of Maryland Medical System (UMMS) ($3.5 billion revenues).
• Outsourcing the internal audit function at LifeBridge Health ($1.2 billion revenues).
• Executing a significant portion of the Internal Audit plan for MedStar Health ($3.5 billion revenues).
• Identifying risks and controls at Kaiser Permanente in connection with Sarbanes-Oxley compliance, focusing on revenue cycle and other critical areas.
• Performing control rationalization with Kaiser management.
• Formulating a risk model and risk universe for the Provider Care industry with a national team of other E&Y professionals.
• Analyzing claims data for self insuring companies to identify potential savings or areas of control weakness. Developed methodologies and SQL routines to isolate exceptions (duplicate claims, ineligible claims, unidentified members for whom payment was made, etc.) in large amounts of claims data.
