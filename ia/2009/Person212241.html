Stuart DePina’s Summary

Seasoned professional with over 23 years experience ranging from early stage companies to large multi-national corporations with responsibilities for the management of multiple functions of operations stemming from tenures as CEO, COO, CFO as well as a Partner in a Big 4 firm. Managed two successful IPO's, over 25 acquisitions and 5 sales of businesses while operating as an Executive and consulted with numerous clients on public offerings and M&A activity while at KPMG.

Stuart DePina’s Specialties:

Proven leader with extensive experience building teams, developing and executing strategic plans, business development, raising capital in both private and public markets including IPO’s, SEC filings, M&A, divestitures and corporate reorganizations.
