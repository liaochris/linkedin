Eric Buryn’s Summary

I have been in the electronics and telecommunications industry for 14 years and have worked in many different technologies, from managing a Military Intelligence Electronics shop repairing radar and remote sensors on the Korean demilitarized zone, Special Operations radio and optics repair in Ranger Battalion to providing top notch technical and engineering services to three wireless carriers.
I am energetic and enthusiastic about taking on the difficult issues and working them until a solution is found. I am multi-faceted and am as comfortable and competent at managing a technical shop or working on a bench as I am designing sites and providing real time analysis of system issues and their solutions.

My professional goal is to branch out from consumer wireless and take on a larger role within an technical organization. My true passion is Research and Development and Project Management. I thrive on blazing new trails and trying to make the impossible, possible. I am open to contracting or full-time positions.
