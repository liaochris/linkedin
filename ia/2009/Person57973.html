DAVID KIRKUP: B2B CFO® Partner
INDUSTRIES: Services, Hi-Tech, Green, Manufacture, Medical
LOCATION: Atlanta, Georgia


I am an Atlanta based partner with B2B CFO® - the USA’s largest CFO firm focused on emerging and mid-market companies. We are a 20 year old firm serving owners of companies with revenues up to $75 million who want to increase cash, profitability, sales and company value. We now have over 100 partners and 500+ clients in 42 states; and over 2,500 years of collective expertise to share with our clients.

I have over 25 years experience working with companies in senior financial roles and you can see my full bio at B2B CFO® . My strengths lie in my wide experience as a hands-on accounting, financial, and operations manager, as well as my knowledge of big picture issues like strategy, financing, growth, and company sale. As an Englishman with over 25 years in the USA I am uniquely positioned to advise Euro businesses targeting the US market.

Why I am here on LinkedIn:

TO NETWORK with other B2B networkers.

TO FIND BUSINESS ADVISORS (Law Firms, Insurance Agencies, Bankers, CPA Firms, Financial Planning firms) who may have a client who needs CFO help, or may be focused on similar markets, or may be able to service my clients.

TO MEET POTENTIAL CLIENTS with sales of $2 million to $75 million who need help organizing their financial infrastructure, or wish to discuss Finding the Exit.

TO MEET ANGEL INVESTORS, AND M&A FIRMS looking for investment opportunities, or for help with existing clients.

TO FIND consulting and speaking opportunities.

TO MEET CFOs who are tired of W2 employment and would like to experience a different way to earn a living.

In Atlanta, I meet business owners at weekly PowerCore networking meetings and attend B2B and Tech networking events such as TAG, BABG, CEO Summit and Chambers.
