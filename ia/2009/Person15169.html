Andy Gilpin’s Summary

More than ever, there is a real need for companies in all environments to exploit information technology in order to get the most out of their business. My role at the Business Gateway in West Lothian is to help support companies that have the potential to take advantage of all elements of e-business to help them grow and compete.

Although my main focus is on supporting companies in the West Lothian, I am looking to use Linkedin as a networking hub to enable West Lothian businesses to build a community that connects them with other networks and people that will help them to drive their business forward.
