Geoffroy Gueulette’s Summary

After a first experience in people management (public service : Team of 5 people), I've worked 5 years in a financial/business analyst position with Sodie for ArcelorMittal (www.sodie.be). Thaler is installed in more than 40 banks in 15 countries.
