Elaine Hsieh’s Summary

Business savvy “green” building consultant whose professional activities focus on energy and sustainability, and include work with the leading non-profit organization promoting environmentally, socially and fiscally responsible building practices. Experienced business, team, and project manager with expertise in operations, marketing, and business development. Solid technical background with a broad understanding of the construction, biotechnology, education, retail, manufacturing, and finance industries. Keen ability to effectively communicate difficult concepts to people in diverse age groups, cultures, and business sectors.

Elaine Hsieh’s Specialties:

sustainable building technologies (indoor environmental quality, landscaping/site/community, material and resouce selection, mechanical and plumbing systems, overall water and energy efficient strategies); "green building" rating system development for all types of buildings; experienced with all LEED and GreenPoint Rated systems; building design and construction experience; facilitation of integrated building design teams; eco-charrettes; extremely experienced trainer and event speaker.
