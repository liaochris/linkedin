Nicole Haibach’s Summary

With 16 years in Marketing and Sales, 6 1/2 years of licensed experience in the Real Estate Industry, Center for Executive Coaching Charter Graduate, and a BA in Business Administration, Nicole Haibach focuses on business and personal development through Core Values.

Nicole was fortunate to have amazing mentors at a very young age and utilizes this experience through her results company, Authentic Holdings. The company provides workshops, individual results meetings, and speaking events to young adults, professionals, and businesses in regards to personal, business, and leadership development through her Core Values Model, Real Life Living Model, and ethics training.

Nicole provides marketing support and book publishing for entrepreneurs that want to transition, reintroduce, develop awareness or grow their business within their target markets.

Nicole represents buyers and sellers of residential homes at Coldwell Banker Select, Realtors and maintains the security of the land the billboards are on for LAMAR Advertising. As a real estate professional, Nicole has been recently featured in a Real Estate Series for first-time home buyers and sellers in a downloadable MP3 format.

Nicole is on the Board of Directors for the Erie Arts Opportunity to support efforts in finding and empowering new talent among the youth in Erie City Schools through the Arts including music and dance.
