Carol Ong’s Summary

Carol Ong is consistently listed as one of the Hottest Creatives in Asia by Campaign Brief Asia. She has handled a diverse set of accounts from beauty to youth brands to food to babies. Some of them include Johnnie Walker, Pepsi, Mentos, Pizza Hut and FedEx.

Her proudest work to date is launching and developing a groundbreaking mobile app for WWF China, "Fate's in your hands", where a virtual bear interacts with your real environment, anywhere you point your camera phone at. The app also allow users to immediately sign up or spread the advocacy with a few clicks on the phone. To bring the idea to life, she helped develop the tech "Shijie Lens Technology" and now finding ways to push mobile marketing further.

Earlier in 2009, her WWF Panda Series Poster and Print Campaigns have won a Silver Clio, a in-book at D&AD and a few metals at Asian Ad Fest. She's also part of the team that won the Sprite global pitch in 2009.

In 2008, her Mentos Gum launch TV was listed in Gunn Report's 100 most awarded ads worldwide. In China, it swept the awards shows including winning the Best in Show at the prestigious China 4As Golden Seal Awards.

Prior to BBH, Carol has worked in BBDO, Saatchi & Saatchi, and Publicis-Basic in Manila. Her work on FedEx was one of the most recalled TV ads in Singapore in 2005. The soap brand she launched in the Philippines, Biolink, became No. 1 in its category in less than a year and surpassed its sales forecast by 200%. The fastfood she relaunched, Chowking, grew from a small chain to a fastfood giant.

Carol’s campaigns were recognized in awards shows such as Clio, D&AD, Cannes, One Show, Media Spikes, China 4As Golden Seal Awards, China Advertising and Brand Conference, Philippine Ad Congress, and Creative Guild of the Philippines. You can find her work in advertising journals such as The Work and Luerzer’s Archive. You can also find her in a bar sipping Amaretto Sour.
