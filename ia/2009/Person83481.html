Garrett Browning’s Summary

I am a career recruiter (nearly 20 years of recruiting experience), passionate about my profession and committed to providing a full range of tactical & strategic recruiting solutions to Employers & Candidates. My experience includes 13 years of success in the AEC and Manufacturing CAD/CAM industries, focusing for the last few years on BIM and Digital Prototyping.

►EMPLOYERS: My mission is to partner with you to recruit the most talented people who best match your jobs and organizations in a timely fashion, so that you can take full advantage of your business opportunities and maximize your recruiting ROI. To that end I deliver:

● Results that exceed expectations;
● Excellent ROI and low cost-per-hire;
● Skilled full-cycle recruiting services & guidance;
● Quick ramp-up;
● Unmatched attention & service;
● Effective communications & collaboration;
● Leadership and personal integrity.

►CANDIDATES: My mission is to apply my extensive recruiting and counseling experience in helping you to gain competitive advantage and a successful career move.

►BACKGROUND: My education and early experience in counseling and team-building provide a strong foundation for my recruiting: (A) valuable insight into people, jobs and organizations, and how to match them, including style and personalities; and, (B) strong interpersonal and communication skills with a collaborative style and authenticity.

My background lends itself to my work and passion in Building Information Modeling because BIM is not about plug-'n-play technology and headcount. It is a game-changing business model and it is about vision, leadership and collaboration along with technical expertise. Success depends on effectively matching those elements with the particular dynamics of an organization, its staff and business.

My services are highly scalable and I can engage talented associates as necessary. I am available to work onsite in the SF Bay Area or virtually for clients anywhere.
