Jennifer A. Leake’s Summary

CONSULTANTS GOLD:
I have a PASSION TO HELP those considering consulting know more about what it takes to be successful, and to help those struggling to survive and flourish as existing consultants. Therefore, I have gathered resources and talent that I have found valuable so you can spend less time searching and more time working.

ConsultantsGold.com is where consultants go to CONNECT with resources to GROW their business and IMPLEMENT actions to get more clients and make more money.

ASSESSMENT PROS -
As a consultant, I work to help businesses put people in the best "job fit" making them more satisfied and productive, so their businesses run smoother and more profitably. Our goal is to help reduce PEOPLE PROBLEMS and:
1. Improve how you hire people so they perform and stay with you;
2. Build better work groups by using team strengths and improving communication;
3. Grow your leadership and bench strength by answering the questions, “Who?" "What to develop?” and “How?”.

Jennifer A. Leake’s Specialties:

Building the business of Solopreneur service providers by offering ideas and resources to better know how get more clients and make more money.
Building workplace performance and business profitability by attracting, hiring and retaining employees so your business runs the way you want it to.
