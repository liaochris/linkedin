  • Corporate Senior Account Manager

    Siebel Systems

    (Public Company; SEBL; Computer Software industry)

    February 2000 — November 2000 (10 months)

    I worked as a Corporate Senior Account Manager for Siebel Systems in Salt Lake City when Siebel first opened its inside sales group in Utah.

    My territory covered Ohio. I was given a $330K quota to hit my first quarter, which by the time we actually completed our training, I had 45 days to his. I closed $345K my first quarter and hit my commit. I was the only inside sales rep to achieve that commitment. Within 6 months, I had already achieved my yearly quota of $1,250,000. My largest sale was just under $800K.

    I left Siebel on good terms when I accepted my position as the Vice President of Business Development for Connaissance Consulting, a Siebel VAR and Premier Implementation Partner.
