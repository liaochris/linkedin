James Byrne’s Education

  • University of Limerick

    PhD , Engineering , 2004 — 2008

    Development of a decision-support / e-learning application giving companies the capability to run supply chain simulations over the web, allowing the user to experiment with different supply chain configurations and analyse risk when entering into and costing a contract. The tool integrates with request for quotation (RFQ) data from the buyer side of a contract, and abstracts the user from the complexities of simulation, greatly reducing simulation model build and experimentation time. Developed using Microsoft .NET and Adobe Flash technologies.
