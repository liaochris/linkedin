Edward van Eckert’s Summary

A results-oriented global marketing communications professional with experience in public relations and promotional initiatives, Edward has been successful in the management of multiple campaigns focused on thought leadership to create and raise levels of brand awareness.
With deep experience in interactive brand management, new product development, sales and business development all within the new media and interactive arenas, major strengths include building multiple brands through integrated business development.

During his career, he has been responsible for marketing, public relations, corporate and employee communications. He has led teams in event/conference planning, print and electronic brand development, content management and advertising -- all with a track record of client satisfaction.

With skills garnered through these diverse roles, Edward's industry experience runs the gamut from financial services to consumer products, from financial and human resources consulting, to high-tech manufacturing.

As a director for PricewaterhouseCoopers and the Mellon Financial Corporation, he created unique promotional programs with long-term impact.

At Deloitte Financial Advisory Services, he successfully launched the Deloitte Forensic Center, a think tank unique to the marketplace.
