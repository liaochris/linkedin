David Friedman’s Summary

I have achieved one of my goals being Chief Marketing Officer for technology-based and service companies.

My experience includes:

> Strategic marketing with P&L
> Blending technical expertise with business acumen to grow revenue by thinking strategically and executing tactically
> Business and application development through third parties providing content and applications
> Comfortable in large corporations or early-stage companies
> Background in the tech fields of telecom, internet, software, wireless, broadband, telematics, and satellite services.
> International experience particularly building distribution sales partnerships.

My passion is commercializing technology and making technology relevant to customers by studying customers and the drivers of their purchasing behavior. I have been fortunate to have many "firsts" in commercializing technology ranging from the first commercialization of wifi on airplanes to the first VSAT data service.

I recently completed a tenure as EVP and Chief Marketing Officer for ATX Group which provides telematics and information services to global automobile manufacturers..

Prior to this position, I was VP, Marketing and Direct Sales for Connexion by Boeing, which provided internet and entertainment services to passengers and airlines.

My career spans a who's who of top technology, software, and service companies, having served as an executive US Cellular, Internap, Ameritech, Covia Technologies and the Federal Government.

I hold an MBA (Economics) from George Washington University; a MSEE from Columbia University; and a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from City College of New York graduating Summa Cum Laude.

I am a charter member of the Marketing Leadership Council of the Conference Board, a prior VP, Conferences and officer of the international Product Development and Management Association, and a founding member of the Message Oriented Middlware Association.
