Brian Freedman’s Summary

In addition to the positions listed above, I am also a trained sommelier--I've passed my first exam with the Court of Master Sommeliers--and have hosted numerous wine dinners at restaurants, clubs, and private residences throughout the Philadelphia area. My work has been published both nationally and locally, and I've made several live television appearances on both Fox 29's "Good Day Philadelphia" and Comcast's "Your Morning on CN8." I have also been interviewed for articles in a number of publications, including USAirways Magazine and The Philadelphia Inquirer, among others. I am also a member of the Wine Media Guild.

In August 2008, I hosted the Chef's Panel at the National Association of Catering Executives conference in Philadelphia for an audience of more than 500 people, and in February 2009, I will lecture on wine and beverages and host a number of cooking events at the Catersource / Event Solutions Conference and Trade Show in Las Vegas.

Brian Freedman’s Specialties:

I bring a sense of liveliness to my writing and teaching, but never at the expense of solid information and rigorous education. I also specialize in pairing food with beer and wine.
