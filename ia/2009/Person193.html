Joke Terlaak Poot’s Summary

I want to stimulate young - sometimes too naive - , but intelligent, passionated and potential young people towards a challenging, pleasant and responsible position in life, especially in the real estate profession.

The world famous play 'Dangerous Liaisons' made me aware of the fact how adults can play with the feelings, the emotions and uncertainties of young and adolescent people. Life can be such a mean play, if you want to play it mean. Do you want to play it mean? I guess (and hope) not!

It is up to your own personal standard, to your own 'morale' whether or not you want to play the game of life with honour or false. Be always aware of the impact of your personal mental powers, both positive and negative, in everyday life.

  • The one and only, lonely member of the Lonelyh Poeths

    The Lonelyh Poeth

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    September 1967 — Present (42 years 1 month)

    How to describe a lonely poet? . Everyone of us, is somehow, living and behaving like the lonely poet. That's the reason why I want to share the next poem ( a Japanese Haiku in Dutch) with you.

    A Haiku represents a moment, a feeling, free from the loaded Past and the unsure Future, just related to Here and Now. No notions about the unchangeable Past, no notions about the unknown Future, only The Moment counts, because you can only can feel, breathe, smell, taste, hear and see This Moment in Your Life...

    A Haiku seeks to catch The Moment...

    mijn Geisha schenkt thee
    haar roodzijden kimono
    ruist als rijstpapier

    (read this and listen to the sound of the words used; Haiku made by Tjo-ke)


    I consider love and humor as a precious gift from many gods whenever I talk, read or write poetry. So be my guest and share your thoughts with me in regard to poetry and literature.
