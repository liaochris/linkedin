Philip Durnford’s Summary

I catalyse courage, learning, action and play in individuals and organisations. To work with me is to take bold steps towards claiming your true value, towards taking up the space that is rightfully yours.

These days I am most excited by a unique coaching business of which I have been an instrumental part, youPlus, Inc. We are in the process of attracting 1m USD capital - join the fun!

Here's why we are unique:
* We offer provide high-quality, provable personal coaching through our youPlus Coaches and our coaching Products AND
* We provide an army of champions in our clients' cause through live support from our Customer Connection Team AND
* We guarantee the quality and impact of our coaching on our clients' lives, "or your money back" AND
* We guarantee continuity of powerful coaching in the unlikely event of a coach unable to attend a call AND
* We have personally assessed all of our coaches to ensure they are leaders as well as great coaches AND
* We have a large stable of coaches, trained and certified by the most prestigious coaching school in the world, The Coaches Training Institute AND
* We actively enagage our clients and our coaches in feedback to ensure our coaches stay great and our clients stay amazed at themselves AND
* We have the Coaches Quality Model to live up to AND
* Our management team uses our own coaches and our own coaching profducts for their own personal development AND
* Our Customer Connection Team are also customers of our own coaching products, using our own coaches AND
* Our employees and our contractor coaches are all part owners of the business AND
