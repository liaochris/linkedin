Christopher Morlan’s Summary

Twelve years of print/digital design, art and creative direction for projects spanning the gamut of a monthly music magazine to industrial and e-commerce web sites. The coordination, conceptualization and execution of point-of-sale packaging, corporate and product identity, business-to-business and business-to-consumer marketing materials, print and digital advertisements, photo shoots, press checks, training and guidance of junior designers, and direct client presentation and consultation are par for the course.

Christopher Morlan’s Specialties:

Graphic design, branding and identity design, print collateral, packaging, editorial design, marketing, advertising, creative solutions
