Tony Castranova’s Summary

Principal Engineer with extensive experience in the operation, design, construction and testing of satellite
earth stations. Possess extensive knowledge in the fields of radio frequency and digital electronic systems with 20 years of experience in program management and product development. Designing and building earth station facilities has permitted me to work in various disciplines ranging from fiber optic cable to emergency power and remote computer control.

Tony Castranova’s Specialties:

• Technical training
• Coaxial, waveguide and fiber lines
• Emergency power systems
• RF systems design and test
• Earth station commissioning
• Interference mitigation

Tony Castranova’s Experience

  • Principle Member Engineering Staff

    SES Engineering

    (Public Company; Telecommunications industry)

    May 1988 — December 2008 (20 years 8 months)

    SES Americom, Inc. (formerly GE Americom, Inc.), Princeton, NJ 1989-2009
    Principal Member Engineering Staff, Terrestrial Systems Engineering

    • Managed and supervised vendors and contractors during the construction phase of earth station facilities
    • Developed and implemented training classes for Americom University, a corporate training facility, including Satellites 101 & 102, Basic Earth Station Antennas and ITAR compliance
    • Designed, and supervised the installation and testing of numerous satellite communications earth stations
    • Installed and commissioned an 11-meter, transportable, C-band satellite earth station for temporary use at the Keflavik Naval Station, Iceland in support of the U.S. Navy during the winter months on time and on budget
    • Designed and managed the installation of a 13-meter TT&C earth station in Australia to support the SES Americom Asia Pacific satellite
    • Upgraded the C-band high power amplifiers at the SES earth station at Thule Air Force Base, Greenland
    • Developed design specifications for earth station hardware for both internal and external customers
    • Supported sales by providing detailed circuit and system designs and cost proposals to meet customer requirements
    • Supported spacecraft operations by designing and installing Telemetry Tracking & Control (TT&C) earth station facilities and upgrading existing facilities to permit control of orbiting spacecraft
    • Supervised the installation and testing of new communications earth station systems.
    • Designed and installed earth stations utilizing antennas ranging from 75-centemeters to 21-meters with associated communications equipment and infrastructure facilities
    • Evaluated proposals and selected vendors and contractors based on technical merit and cost comparison analysis
