Ashkan Afkhami’s Summary

Ashkan Afkhami has been working within the technology and finance industry for the past four years; majored in Management Information Systems and Finance with a minor in Technological Entrepreneurship at Northeastern University.

Graduated with high honors and the top of his class while actively being involved with:
- College of Business Administration Alumni Advisory Committee (currently active as well)
- Peer Mentoring program
- ResLife; serving as a Resident Assistant
- Management Information System Club
- Finance and Accounting Club
- Entrepreneurship Club
- Northeastern's Mens Club Soccer Team
- Northeastern's Freshman Crew Team

Ashkan was recognized as a exceptional leader amongst his peers and requested to attend the 10th annual Undergraduate Business School Leadership Conference at Emery College in Atlanta, Georgia.

Additionally, Ashkan was on the Dean's List for all of his undergraduate years and was honored with 2009 Most Influential Senior Award for his active involvement within the community.

Currently, Ashkan is employed at Hercules Technology Growth Capital a publicly traded VC firm providing venture debt and equity to venture capital and private equity backed technology and life science companies.

Ashkan has also founded a student ran initiative called Inter-Disciplinary Entrepreneurship Accelerator (IDEA) at Northeastern University aimed at providing tailored services for entrepreneurs, designed to accelerate the development of their business ideas.

Ashkan is extremely passionate about product development and is eager to work with start up companies to provide guidance and resources.
