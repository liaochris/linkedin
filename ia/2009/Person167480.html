About Netbiscuits Inc.:
----------------------------------
Netbiscuits operates the world’s leading B2B web software platform for development, publishing and advertising in the Mobile Internet. The platform serves the global Mobile Internet programs of premium web brands such as eBay, Yahoo!, Half.com, LandRover.mobi, Visa.mobi, AOL, BILD, Spiegel, RTL, Wallstreet:Online or Sixt.

Globally the Netbiscuits platform delivers more than 1.5 Billion optimized page impressions and content items each month to more than 5000 mobile devices in 40 countries across the globe.

In Germany Netbiscuits is market leader, serving 80 % of all off-deck Mobile Internet traffic generated by the major web brands.

As part of the Netbiscuits Partner Network globally leading agencies such as Avenue A Razorfish, AKQA, Ogilvy or companies of Omnicom Group use the platform to provide worldwide Mobile Internet campaigns to major consumer brand customer such as Coca Cola, McDonalds or BMW.
