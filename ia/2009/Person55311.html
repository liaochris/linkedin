Darin Andersen’s Summary

Senior Executive, Organizational Development, Sales, Marketing, Operations, Legal/External Affairs and International Leadership experience. Cyber security and cybercrime expert. Chief Operating Officer of ESET, LLC, an international software security company with global sales of $200M in 160 countries. ESET earned 3 Inc. 500 Awards (2007, 2008 & 2009) and Deloitte's 2008 Technology Fast 1000, growing 667% over the last 5 years.

Darin has served as CEO, COO or C-level executive for several companies including ESET, Tulip Biomed, lynda.com and Total Training. Prior to this he was a Director at Red Hat.

Andersen founded and led two companies as CEO; Ready Set Net, an Internet consultancy and Content Management System (CMS) development company and Biz Dev Direct, a management consultancy focusing on leadership and organizational development and business operations. Clients included Adobe Systems, Merrill Lynch, UNOCAL and California Pizza Kitchen.

Andersen has been a presenter, panelist, or program committee member for the New York State Annual Cyber Security Conference, US Chamber “Securing the Perimeter: a Public/Private Partnership Roundtable”, Daily Transcript’s Cyber Security Roundtable, the C4ISR, Cybersecurity, Robot Platforms & Sensors Conference and Steering Committee member of NCSA/APWG's "Unified Messaging Working Group". He helped create ESET’s “Securing Our eCity” initiative; an international Cyber Security awareness and education program.

Darin has also been a speaker for TechAmerica, UCSD Extension, San Diego Software Industry Council, the US, San Diego, Hollywood and Los Angeles Chambers of Commerce, the LA Trade Commission and Claremont McKenna College. Interviews include the Wall Street Journal, PC Magazine, PC World, Burton Group, San Diego Business Journal, The Daily Transcript, bizSanDiego Magazine, Austin Sentinel, Atlanta Constitution, Monster.com, Computer Reseller News (CRN), eWeek, Information Week and Linux Today.

Darin Andersen’s Specialties:

Expert in strategic planning and organizational development, international distribution, sales (online & channel), business development, marketing and product development for high tech and biotech companies. Business systems expert in process modeling, reengineering, Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), infrastructure, and IT hardware and software. International experience including ESET (Americas, EMEA, APAC), Tulip Biomed (APAC and Latin America) and Cushman & Wakefield (EMEA and APAC).
