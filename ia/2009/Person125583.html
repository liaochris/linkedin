Chilobe Kalambo, CISA, CPA, MBA’s Summary

Chilobe is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), a Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), and also has his MBA in Finance. He serves on the local chapter boards of the National Black MBA Association as well as the National Association of Black Accountants. In addition, Chilobe is an active participant of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants, Information Systems Audit and Control Association, and Big Brothers Big Sisters Foundation.

Chilobe has a combined 7 years experience in Industry, Government, Technology Risk, External Consulting, and Internal Audit services. Prior to his current position, Chilobe worked for a Big 4 Accounting firm, leading consulting firm, multinational company as well as government. Chilobe has worked in the United States and overseas in various capacities encompassing the areas of Accounting, Finance, and Information Technology.

Chilobe Kalambo, CISA, CPA, MBA’s Specialties:

Chilobe's core expertise include Transaction Advisory Services; Business Risk Services; Banking & Insurance Consultation Services; Financial, Managerial & Tax Accounting; Technology & Security Risk Advisory Services; as well as Internal & External Audit and Control.
