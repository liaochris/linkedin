Patricia Pang’s Summary

Patricia founded Yang Kang Tang TCM Practice in 2005 after graduating from the full-time 5-year degree course in Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) at Middlesex University. Patricia's godfather is a TCM practitioner in Hong Kong so she has been interested in TCM from a young age. Patricia grew up in Australia and England and speaks fluent English therefore minimising the language problems common with practitioners employed from China. She can also read & speak fluent Chinese, allowing her to continually improve her understanding of TCM by reading TCM materials that have not been translated into English.

The 5-year degree course Patricia graduated from is based on the TCM training at the Beijing University of Chinese Medicine and included 5 months full time clinical practice under the most prominent TCM doctors in 2 of the largest hospitals in Beijing. Graduates also recieved a Bachelor of Medicine qualification from the university in Beijing. Patricia completed her UK clinical placement at the Asante Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine, Haverstock Hill Clinic, Whittington Hospital Pain Clinic and the Chinese National Healthy Living / NHS Walk-in Centre (Soho), where she treated many patients under the supervision of some of the leading TCM practitioners in the UK. In recognition of her achievements in the study and practice of TCM, Patricia was awarded the "Prize for Excellence in TCM Studies 2005" by the Association of Traditional Chinese Medicine (ATCM).

Patricia is a full member of the ATCM, the largest professional body for TCM in the UK which is also actively involved in the current process of statutory regulation of TCM in the UK. Only practitioners with at least 4 years of full-time training are allowed to join the ATCM so you can be confident with the services that Patricia has to offer. All treatments are tailor-made to suit individual needs with no hard-sale.
