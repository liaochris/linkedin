  • Network Rollout Manager

    Wataniya Télécom Algérie S.P.A (Qtel subsidary)

    (Telecommunications industry)

    March 2004 — March 2006 (2 years 1 month)

    First in the field in the city of Oran, as Rollout Manager for Western Algeria, I set up the local organisation for the third operator Wataniya Télécom Algérie (WTA). I’ve recruited all the local staff and established both the temporary and permanent office locations. In less then four months time, the network consisting of a MSC, a BSC and 60 BTS-sites using Siemens equipment was successfully launched. I’ve managed all the start-up activities such as radio and transmission planning; site acquisition and permitting; civil engineering and construction; installation, testing and commissioning; warehousing and logistics; integration and optimisation and core network realisation. I maintained the relations with the local authorities and was responsible for the local management of subcontractors and suppliers.

  • National Network Rollout Manager (Indonesia)

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    August 2002 — November 2003 (1 year 4 months)

    Together with the future project director, I set up a full scale project management company and network rollout organisation in Indonesia. Initial setup consisted in founding the company, acquiring the necessary office space in 5 different cities spread out over Java and Sumatra and in acquiring the necessary human resources (both local and expat). I acted as the Reime’s National Network Rollout Manager for all of Ericsson’s network rollout activities in Sumatra. Scope of work consisted of all activities going from site acq; permitting; civil, mechanical and electrical works; BTS and BSC installation, testing, commissioning and integration; LoS-surveys and logistics. The major end clients were Telkomsel Indonesia, IM3, Satelindo, Excelcom and Siemens. I was responsible for the full operational organisation consisting out of more then 60 employees; subcont selection and management; planning, forecasting and progress control of over 700 sites (incl. backlog sites taken over from client).
