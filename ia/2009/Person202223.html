Rodney Sampson (Connect with me at efactor.com)’s Summary

Rodney Sampson is a serial social entrepreneur, investor, thought leader, author, motivator and global influencer. Building and implementing successful business models and movements from the ground up is his primary core competency. At 35, he simultaneously reaches into the worlds of business, foreign relations and faith. He regularly advises Heads of State, Global 2000 companies and Bishops that lead millions of constituents throughout the planet. Over the last 15 years, he has been involved with ventures that have gone on to generate more than $1B US in revenue worldwide.

His legacy purpose and passion is to mentor one million entrepreneurs to generate at least one million dollars, thus creating a new trillion dollar economy of people that will live by the mantra, “make money, have fun and do well”. E.Factor is the catalyst for this passion.

Rodney is the Chairman of The Direction Movement, a General Overseer in Full Gospel and a trainer with Dr. John Maxwell’s EQUIP.

Rodney has a Bachelor of Science degree in Psychology from Tulane University, a MBA from Keller Business School, and an honorary Doctorate of Ministry degree from The International Gospel Fellowship Theological Seminary in Greenville, SC. He also attended the Pennsylvania State University College of Medicine.

Rodney is the author of “Your Manifest Destiny: 7.5 Words to Transform Your Future” and “Trillion Dollar Universe”.

A strong believer in global reinvestment, Sampson has been a friend of Africa, particularly Namibia for more than three years. He has invested hundreds of thousands of Namibian Dollars in training thousands of Namibians in leadership, entrepreneurship and legacy.

For his contributions to humanity, Sampson was awarded The Phoenix Award - the City of Atlanta’s highest honor - by Mayor Shirley Franklin in 2004.
