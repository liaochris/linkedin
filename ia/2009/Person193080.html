Ralph Perkins’s Summary

Headquartered in Miami, Florida, Potamkin Automotive is one of the largest automotive dealer groups in the United States. Potamkin Automotive’s significant growth is attributable to our dealership acquisition & consolidation model, as well as our decentralized "dealer-centric" strategy that emphasize geographic and brand diversity, localized operations and "as needed" re-investment of capital. We call our philosophy of dealer management "The Power of Partnership."
