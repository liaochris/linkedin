Chris Lunn’s Summary

I have worked with a primary focus on online entities since 2001. Whilst Online Marketing Manager at www.eteach.com I managed to establish this Education Job board amongst the top 10 most visited recruitment websites in the UK which was a great achievement for a niche job site.

In December 2007 I moved to 50connect, which is an editorial-led portal aimed at the over 50's market. Here I have assumed the role of Operations Manager and have responsibilities for the continued development of the website and effective running of advertising campaigns for our high profile clients. The market along with our service, continues to grow and provide a great challenge and opportunity to our organisation.
