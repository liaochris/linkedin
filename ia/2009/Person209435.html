Sara Brummel’s Summary

Stemming from eight years in the property management field as a National Sales Training Director, Sara Brummel transitioned into recruiting in 1998 as The Recruiting Director for Southern California’s largest property management firm. Since that time she has been entrenched in the executive search arena for over a decade specializing in industries ranging from Sales to Biotech; with her core competency heavily weighted in Accounting and Finance.

Merging her two areas of expertise, Sara started her own consulting practice in February of 2007 providing Human Capital Consulting and training services where she partners with organizations in order to ensure that their recruiting and training efforts are positioned to increase retention as well as bottom-line impact.

Sara Brummel’s Specialties:

Sara works in concert with executive management to drive the creation and implementation of talent enhancement processes that will ultimately increase client services quality and organizational profitability. Her expertise lies in improving the quality of personnel through effective talent acquisition efforts ranging from individual searches to recruiting strategy implementation coupled with valuable industry, sales, leadership, professional growth, management, and client services training.
