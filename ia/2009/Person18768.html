Andrea (Anders) Pistaceci’s Summary

Andrea Pistaceci has been training and speaking professionally throughout the world since 1999 and have spoken in more than 50 countries.

Formerly Self Employed, he is now a member of Guidance.be the training center of Devoteam Belgium. In his role at Guidance his primary job involves training and speaking at Microsoft partner gigs and conferences.

He is Frequently involved in helping at Microsoft Events. He has been a Technical Learning Guide, an MCT ambassador, an ILL guide and have spoken at Microsoft TechEd Europe.

His Speciality involve the Evangelisation on Management and Virtualization, especially on the System Center and Hyper-V Products. He has been also involved in Sales and Presales training across Europe to increase awareness of IO and Dynamic system.

He fluently speaks 4 languages (Italian, French, English, Spanish), and can present a session in any of those.

He also enjoys participating in free training for NGOs thru organizations like Nethope.

He loves supporting the community with his User Group and is a current active member of Culminis EMEA Board of Directors.

Please feel free to contact him for any Conference and Training engagement, and if you want help building a local IT-PRO community.
