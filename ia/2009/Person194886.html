Rodney Caston’s Summary

I have over ten years experience in the areas of Linux and Windows systems administration and engineering. My primary focus has been the management of internet accessible production environments which are publicly visible and require high levels of availability. I have extensive experience as a senior engineer with internet software relating to web, email, and DNS services.

My accomplishments include having built network operations centers from the ground up at multiple ISPs, having built up email infrastructure handling millions of user emails daily, monitoring infrastructures using both commercial and open source NMS tools and handling the administration and capacity planning of major internet websites with peak traffic in the 350 Mb/s range. I have also worked as a writer and designer in the past, having co-created the MegaTokyo™ intellectual properties along with the systems infrastructure used to deliver the web based content.

I am currently involved with projects ranging from fault management, performance management, and converting production environments from physical to virtual for reasons relating to cost, disaster recovery, and high availability.
