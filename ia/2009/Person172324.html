Neil Walker’s Summary

With 22 years of commercial information technology experience in a range of roles, companies & technologies, from a background in IT management consultancy systems development, I moved into the software industry focusing on post-sales service delivery (consultancy, support, training, hosting & related software services), and operational management (product management, product development, IT, pre-sales, partnerships/alliances). I followed this with a move into the retail industry and I am now the head of IT infrastructure, development and architecture for a UK-based retailer.

I have also been marketing director for a consulting company launching a software product.

My consulting experience includes design, development & implementation of private and public sector systems in commercial enterprises and government agencies such as the NHS.

Neil Walker’s Specialties:

Retail, software services delivery (support, professional services/consulting, education), IT infrastructure management.
