Jon Stoddard’s Summary

At Intuit, I was responsible for developing partnerships with Internet Search Engines, local directories and Internet Yellow Pages for Intuit’s WebListings offer---which allow small to medium sized businesses a way to get their business listed and found on sites like Google, Yellowpages.com, Yahoo and Superpages.com. I also explore business development opportunities to expand Intuit’s SMB eco-system. Before this position, I helped launch the Point of Sale reseller program for Intuit.

Prior to Intuit, I was co-founder and VP of Marketing and Sales at Turbo Squid, Inc. and oversaw the launch of the Marketing and Sales department for the company.

Before launching Turbo Squid, I was Director of Business Development for Digimation, Inc. Here I was able to expand partnerships and increase sales of the company by 30% in the first year.
