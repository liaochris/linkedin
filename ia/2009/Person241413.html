Vanessa Fincher’s Summary

I am a Sustainable Business Management Consultant whose present enterprise is enhanced by my past experience in the Media and Education Industries.
I have13 years of combined professional experience in higher education, management, entertainment media, and marketing which informs my current endeavor.
As an educator, I was the Director of the Film and TV Department at Merkantilt Institute of Norway, where I designed the curriculum, launched the program and taught the core classes.
I also taught Film and Television courses at The University of Bergen, Norway and at Columbia College, Chicago. As founder and CEO of TigerLily Productions,
a media company in Chicago that specialized in titles that promoted social change, I wrote, directed and produced features films, plays, PSA’s, promos, and documentaries.
Currently, having co-founded The Idea Hive, a sustainable business management consulting company in San Francisco, I am using systemic models and the creative power of story to design and inform business strategy and operations.
In the last three years, I have developed, marketing and social media strategies, alternative transportation, waste management and long term sustainability plans for media businesses and municipalities.

I have BA in Media Communication and Theater from DePaul University in Chicago, Illinois and an MBA in Sustainable Enterprise from Dominican University in San Rafael, California.
