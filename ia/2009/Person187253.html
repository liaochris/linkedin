Phillip Medina’s Summary

As General Sales Manager @ Aria Medical, my roles were many. Most of my responsibilities were in the sales department, but I also had responsibilities in the warehouse, customer service, accounting, and IT/web departments.

My primary duty was to recruit, train, and develop the sales team. The sales team grew three fold, from my initial promotion to departure, bringing with it multiple months of record sales. Overseeing the team meant everything from working on proposals, refining approach strategies for customers, diffusing conflicts, training & development, and most importantly developing vendor/customer relationships.

I functioned as a liaison between other departments and promoted change and a team atmosphere; working with customer service team members regarding overall customer satisfaction, warehouse team members regarding inventory flow and procedures, and IT/web team members regarding tracking product sales and internet application development.

This was truly a growing experience for me; this job very quickly became the epitome of multi-tasking. The knowledge and experience that I've walked away with from this position will remain with me forever.

I also own and operate my own freelance media productions company. Apollo Media Productions is a niche solution company for small businesses needing audio/video production or graphic design. I have worked with a range of clients from small business owners to national chains in local markets.


I like to think of my interests as a buffet. I'm a driven, business oriented individual. I enjoy training and developing employees and creating a great workplace. On the leisure side, I've involved myself in music for the last 17 years and music production for the last 12 years. Graphic design is my other vice, dabbling at first and eventually producing client quality work for 12 years or so.
