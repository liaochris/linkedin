Laurence Rothman’s Summary

I look for challenging jobs where I get to use my experience in marketing communications, internet marketing, and digital dialogue to create a comprehensive world-class online experience. This includes managing the customer from organic or paid search, through online engagement with your website, continuing to activate products online, and then establish a continuing and profitable online relationship with them. All facets of the customer "inbox" should be managed, from email, RSS, mobile devices, IM/chat, social networking sites and so on to deepen the relationship, creating a happy and well informed customer. This online experience, and the measurement around it, is something that can add real profit to your business. My history of implementation of electronic channels, the measurement of them, and establishing customer dialogues is something modern companies cannot afford to do without.
