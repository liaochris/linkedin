Christine Stoddard’s Summary

I am a professional writer with interest and experience in other fields of the arts. In the past, I have held multiple simultaneous jobs and internships that often involved working at least partially from home. I am, however, familiar with the typical 40-hour work week in an office environment.

Right now, I investing my time and effort in a wide range of different endeavors, with a concentration in writing and editing, with the same fervor as I've had since high school. This semester, I'm taking a full classload, interning 10 hours a week, and taking on freelance projects. My freelance projects range, again, mostly involve writing (from non-fiction to fiction) but I am also acting for pay on occasion. Every now and then, I model mostly to improve my acting but I hope to get paid for it soon. I also dabble in the visual arts, and get paid to take photos and edit them. My collages---which feature a combination of my drawing, photography, and sometimes writing---and 'zines have been popular since high school, so I sell those, as well. In my spare time, At this time, I am building up an independent artists' group, Simply Lark Press, with Daly Martinez, Cheyenne Glenn, and Callison Slater.

I plan to intern at a media company every summer until I graduate from college and ultimately become a full-time writer and actress. So far, I've been rather successful in achieving my goals and I believe that if I continue to work passionately and diligently, many of my dreams will come true---and that philosophy makes me one of the very few optimistic artists I know!
