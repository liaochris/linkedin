    About BURP at KU:
    Planning is an ongoing process that concerns both physical and socio-economic issues. As resources are limited and global events affect the future of each community, planning must be done with a framework of sustainable development. From this point of view University of Khulna introduced an unique discipline of education, Urban and Rural Planning, to meet the bare necessity of professional Planners in each and every development sector of the country. The discipline introduced, for the first time in Bangladesh, an undergraduate course in Planning education. Here the young Planners evaluate and seize opportunities to make fundamental improvements in human society as well as to solve social, economic and physical problems in an organized manner.
