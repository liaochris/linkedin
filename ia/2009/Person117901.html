John Pirillo’s Summary

Accomplished, results oriented sales executive who will take ownership and responsibility for the sales function of technology based companies. Hands on management style includes carrying a bag and working the trenches as a fully contributing member of a sales organization while providing leadership to the team, fostering a positive and energetic work atmosphere and staying focused on producing results.

Extensive experience at helping early stage companies establish “Go to Market” strategies to ensure maximum customer penetration and rapid capture of market share. Considerable experience at helping companies identify, close and manage revenue opportunities in an unpredictable business environment. Considerable experience in managing revenue responsibilities across all segments of network service providers worldwide including ILEC’s, IOC’s, IXC’s, PTT’s, MSO’s, Federal Government and ISP’s.

Extremely active and contributing member of an organization’s executive management team in helping to set both long and short term strategic direction. Considerable experience at speaking and presenting to all levels of organizations including customers, employees and potential/current investors.
