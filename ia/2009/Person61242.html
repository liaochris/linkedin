Delphine Queme’s Summary

After getting an advanced degree in Strategic Management & Marketing at Dauphine University, I have worked for 7 years in the fragrances & luxury products industries up to being in charge of a 50 millions euros international business.

At 29, I decided to work in the music business and after a two-year stay in London, i have managed a successful french electronic music record label.
I have started my own record label in 2004.

I am currently looking for a position in an innovative company where i will be able to make the most of :
- my knowledge in marketing and business development
- my abilities acquired as an "entrepreneur"
- my artistic general knowledge and insight (see my blog)
