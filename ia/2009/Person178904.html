Joe Cronin’s Summary

Entrepreneur in the education sector specializing in college marketing, internet marketing, e-learning, student loans, financial services, lead generation and direct to consumer sales. Our company, Edvisors, manages a network of websites, forums and blogs reaching 1 million college students every month.

Specializing in SEO, New Media Marketing, Social Networking and Email Marketing, we have unique access to a broad spectrum of 18 - 30 year old customers looking to further their education.

We have been successful marketing a wide variety of products ranging from student credit cards, savings and checking accounts, online degree programs, career services and other related products and services. If you are looking to promote / market your product or service to college students, visit: www.Edvisors.com/about

Joe Cronin’s Specialties:

Edvisors manages education related websites serving college and graduate students with specific products and services including college admission advice, online degree search, student loans, scholarships, health and travel insurance and adult and continuing education programs.
