Paul Segreto’s Summary

Highly experienced franchise industry executive with over 20 years of experience in senior-level roles including industry consultant, corporate franchise executive, multi-unit franchisee, and area developer.

Recognized franchise industry expert frequently serving as a guest speaker/topic leader for focus groups, strategy and sales planning meetings, training sessions and industry panels.

Frequently called upon to utilize expertise in the development of articles for industry newsletters and blogs, and training programs for companies and organizations within the industry.

Franchise Development Services

Assisting franchisors, area developers and master franchisees in all aspects of franchise development including franchise sales management and candidate marketing. Services provided to startups as well as emerging and mature franchise concepts. Special projects include mergers & acquisitions, business dispositions, conversions and franchising as a business model.

Franchise Marketing Services

Developing eMarketing strategies to complement traditional marketing that are essential in attracting the next wave of franchise candidates for franchisors and future customers for franchisees. Specifically, strategies centered around new technology and Web 2.0 concepts including social networking, video sharing and blogs as well as non-web applications including email, text messaging and mobile advertising.

Coaching and Consulting Services

Providing services to franchisors, industry executives, franchisees, affiliate companies and franchise candidates. Special programs and services offered for master franchisees, area developers and multi-unit franchisees. Extensive experience with family business ownership, business partnerships and minority business ownership.

Paul Segreto’s Specialties:

Revenue and profit generating solutions in franchise marketing and development.
