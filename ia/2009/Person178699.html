Ömer Durmaz’s Summary

Ömer Durmaz is a graphic designer. He was born in 1974. He started his graphic design career in 1993 and began to work as a full time designer in 1997. He graduated from Dokuz Eylul University (Faculty of Fine Arts Graphic Design Department) in 1999. He has been a full-time lecturer at the same university since 2002. He is the author of several articles about graphic design and is a member of the Turkish Graphic Design Association (GMK).

Ömer Durmaz’s Specialties:

branding, packaging design, typography
