John Rothstein’s Summary

John Rothstein has been involved in communications and marketing since his childhood writing plays for his camp color war teams. The times, the technology and the topics have changed, but the goal remains the same: get the message across.

John received a B.S. at Plymouth State University, Plymouth, NH and a J.D. from New England Law - Boston, Boston, MA. He is a member of the Massachusetts and New Jersey Bars (but don't hold that against him).

Effectively communicating a message has always been a large part of his life. In college it was communicating ways to improve his school. He won election to the Student Senate and the Vice-Presidency of his class. In law school, his communication skills were used in business where John founded and marketed a company that provided temporary paralegal help to small law firms.

Recognizing the need and service gap that exists in the fields of data and message integration and dissemination, John formed White Space Marketing.

Before founding White Space, John was President of Next Generation Printing, a commercial printing business on the forefront of digital production technology. John spent 10 years as the Managing Partner of the NJ law firm he founded in 1995, where his marketing skills helped to create a successful, multi-branch law firm. In 1996 John received a Certificate in Entrepreneurship from Farleigh Dickinson University, where he also served as an Adjunct Professor of Communications. In 2001, John won the Small Business Person of the Year Award from the Morris County New Jersey Chamber of Commerce.

Today, John is focused on helping White Space customers communicate more effectively and cost effectively. With tools such as Transpromotional Messaging, variable data personalization, e-presentment, invoice and statement redesign, third-party messaging and more, it is an exciting time to be involved in the marketing and communication fields.

John Rothstein’s Specialties:

Helping customers use their existing data and transactional document white space to create great looking, easily understood, personalized statements, invoices and other documents and save them money by utilizing green e-presentment technologies.
