Owen Bach [owen@rhl.co.uk]’s Summary

Owen Bach is a technical recruitment professional with experience in the clinical, pharmaceutical, biotechnology, medical device, chemical, consumer goods, cosmetic, and FMCG healthcare sectors. He is experienced in winning and growing high value contracts, as well as recruiting staff of all levels to multinational organizations.

Since joining RHL Owen Bach has developed a standalone Cosmetics and Consumer Goods desk specialising in the recruitment of technical, marketing, and supply-chain professionals of all levels to the Cosmetics and Consumer Goods industries. He is an expert in recruiting staff to work with all product types within this sector including skincare, hair care, toiletries, colour cosmetics, personal care, air care, household care, fabric care, surface care, perfumes/fragrances, and depilatories.

Some of the key roles Owen recruits are:

Cosmetic Chemists, Formulators, R&D Managers, Product Development Managers, Claim Support Scientists, Sensory Scientists, Toxicologists, Regulatory Affairs Managers, Quality Assurance Managers, QA Technicians, Laboratory Technicians, QC Chemists, Graduate Chemists, R&D Directors, Production Managers, Manufacturing Managers, Operations Managers, Operations Directors, Engineers, Technicians, Marketing Managers, Brand Managers, Product Managers, Category Managers, Supply Chain Managers, Packaging Technologists and Packaging Engineers.
