Dev Tandon’s Summary

Dev Tandon runs Tangerine Tribe, a boutique digital studio that provides consulting services and operates web properties including Trunkt and Foliomo.

As a consultant, Dev has designed and developed digital products and strategies for clients including Remy Cointreau, Charles Schwab, Harris Direct, Coca-Cola, Shecky's Media, National Geographic and many start-ups.

Prior to consulting, Dev was the VP of Digital Products at Hallmark Channel where he led development of set-top box applications featuring the vast Hallmark film and television library.

Dev graduated with distinction from Colby College with a degree in American Studies and earned an MBA in a joint program with NYU Stern and NYU Tisch.

Prior to graduate school, Dev was a brand strategist at world-class ad agencies including BBDO, Saatchi, Swirl and Carmichael Lynch. He worked with clients including Apple, Disney, Sony, Hewlett Packard, CNet, Pentax, Skyy Vodka, Kahlua, Fetzer and Levi Strauss.

Dev lives in New York City with his wife Ayesha Ahmad and likes taking photographs around the world.
