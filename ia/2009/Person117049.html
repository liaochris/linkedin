John Fazzio’s Experience

  • Corporate and Tax Associate

    Herten, Burstein, Sheridan, Cevasco, Bottinelli, Litt & Harz, LLC

    (Law Practice industry)

    March 2009 — Present (5 months)

    I am a business lawyer with expertise in the tax and intellectual property fields. I advise companies on mergers and acquisitions, divestitures, spin-offs, financing transactions, joint ventures and strategic alliances, equity and debt issues, and general corporate matters. I utilize my multidisciplinary tax knowledge to achieve the most tax-efficient structure for a transaction. I advise companies on the federal income tax consequences of transactions. I regularly advise technology companies on license agreements, co-promotion and marketing agreements, R&D agreements, and other technology-driven transactions.

    (Law Practice industry)

I work on matters involving complex commercial transactions, mergers and accquistions, business restructurings and commercial finance, business formations, the purchase and sale of businesses, and various commercial real estate matters. I also work on a variety of estate planning matters and intellectual property matters.
