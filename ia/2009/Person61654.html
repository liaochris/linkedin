Dennis Cuneo’s Summary

Dennis Cuneo is counsel with the Washington, DC law firm of Arent Fox, where he provides strategic advice to corporations, trade associations and organizations on a broad range of issues. He was formerly Senior Vice President of Toyota Motor North America where he played a key role in the launch of Toyota's manufacturing organization in North America. He is the former Chair of the Cincinnati Branch of the Federal Reserve and is on the Boards of AK Steel, the National Stock Exchange and several universities. He has lectured at MIT's International Motor Vehicle Programs in Berlin and Beijing, and has given more than 100 speeches on topics ranging from economic development to improving America's manufacturing base.
