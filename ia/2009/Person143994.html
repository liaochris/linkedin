Louis Louna’s Summary

Solutions-driven business professional with strong performance in Brand Communication Management for consumer goods, capitalizing on several years of experience in developing communication tools to increase product performance and profitability.

Louis Louna has recently worked for multi-brand organizations as Nestlé and Adams-Cadbury among others. Over the past years, he also has collaborated to create powerful communication tools for brands like Hershey's, L'Oreal, ADT-Tyco, Cartoon Network, Bimbo Group and Visa and was involved in all aspects of the initiatives branding efforts.

Until now, his work has been edited in several publications like Periódico Reforma, Milenio Diario and a! Diseño and Expansión magazines. Same way, his portfolio has been displayed at diverse collective exhibitions.
