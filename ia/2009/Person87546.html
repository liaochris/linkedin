Glenn Garnes’s Summary

I am a retired attorney who spent approximately 20 years handling complex commercial litigation for my law clients. I have always loved technology and people, and I have found a way to pursue both of those passions through the development of niche affinity groups that rely on a social networking platform as their base.

My projects include the Relationship Marketing Center where I teach business professionals how to generate more business through referrals, and two social networks (ConsumersWin.com, and OptimumHealthClub.com) that get merchants and services providers excellent access to their target markets.

My goal is help business professionals find the resources and education to excel at their highest potential.

Glenn Garnes’s Specialties:

Building communities of people who come together for a common purpose, whether it be business or personal is my focus. I have expertise with referral and relationship marketing, as well as social networking technologies and multi-media production and marketing tools.
