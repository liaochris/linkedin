Dale Oestreich’s Summary

28 year entrepreneur. Founded American Delivery Service (ADS) in 1979 to be named by Inc 500 in 1988 as #226 of the fastest growing closely held corporations in U.S. Recipient of the United States Chamber of Commerce Blue Chip Award and named Small Business Person of the year by SBA in 1994.

Established Office Supplies, etc., in 1985, a business supply and printing company. Company sold and merged into Finley's Office Supplies, Etc.

Developed ADS Logistics - A Warehouse Distribution, and Third Party Logistics business and sold in 2006.

Established Franchise Business Services, Inc in 2006 to provide a sales and marketing resource for small to medium size franchisors.

Dale Oestreich’s Specialties:

Developing and growing businesses. Building organizations and Human resource management.
