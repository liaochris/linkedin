Erik Bo Sørensen’s Summary

More than 25 years of professional experience with Information Technology and Telecommunication from a variety of angles. This experience has been put to work in ebs.dk a/s - a Business Intelligence and Portal solutions provider.
I have a Masters Degree in Public Administration supplemented by profound technical insight and many years of experience on the basis of successful management of a wide diversity of IT/Telecommunication.
This background and experience gives me a business-oriented approach to IT/Telecommunication planning and management combined with deep knowledge of IT/Telecommunication technologies, markets, trends, methods and nomenclature etc.
My experience includes a good understanding of the conditions for politically high profiled organisations.
To sum up, I am:
• Visionary – for the whole business as well as for the IT of the business
• Result oriented – strategy and visions are of no value, if not converted to actual, implementable and value adding solutions
