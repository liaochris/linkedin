Philip Calvert’s Summary

Hello

I'm the founder of IFA Life - the social networking website for Financial Planners, IFAs, Financial Life Planners, Mortgage Brokers and Wealth Managers worldwide. Successful financial advisers join IFA Life to network, share best practice, debate industry issues, raise their profile online and to provide help and support to one another.

I speak at conferences worldwide, teaching financial services brands how to combine social media and traditional marketing techniques to enhance their reputation, get closer to customers and to increase sales.

If you're a financial services conference organiser looking for a proven and entertaining speaker and expert interviewee on sales, social media marketing and online communication skills, please contact me through LinkedIn or to philip@ifalife.com.
