Aaron Magruder’s Summary

Aaron Magruder is the owner of NonStop Networks, LLC, a Cisco Select Partner, and primary network, security and unified communications architect. Since 1999, he has pursued continue growth in the areas of information technology including network, security, wireless and voice by obtaining the Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) in May 2000, the Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) in June 2001 and the most respected network certification in the world, Cisco Certified Internetworking Expert #21942 (CCIE) in September 2008.

Experienced in multi-tiered network environments responsible for research, testing, design, documentation, configuring, installing, securing and troubleshooting of voice and data networks.

Software: NetFlow, Microsoft Windows 95-Vista, Cisco ACS: Radius and TACACS+, CiscoWorks 2000 and LMS, HP OpenView, OpNet ITGuru and ACE, Visio, Microsoft Office Suite, Remedy, Magic, Peregrine Service Center, Citrix, Edge and Sterling EDI software, Unix, FreeBSD, Novell Netware 3.11 – 5, Lotus Notes, Network Sniffers, Solar Winds Orion and Cirrus, MRTG, VMWare, DNS, DHCP, WINS, Voyence

Hardware: Cisco Router (675-7500), Catalyst Switches (1900-6500), Cisco ASA (5505-5520), Cisco PIX (501-525), Cisco CSS 11503, Cisco XML Gateway, Cisco CE 565, Cisco VPN 3000 Concentrator, Blue Coat SG Proxy, Symantec Enterprise Raptor Firewall, F5 Big-IP 520-3400 and 3DNS, F5 Firepass, Expand Network Accelerators (1810-6810), Peribit Accelerators, Cisco Aironet 350 and 1200 AP's, Cisco IGX, Terminal servers,, Motorola 6560 and 6520 routers
