Drew Popple’s Summary

Drew is a digital expert with an extremely broad range of experience gained after less than 5 years in the industry. During this time he has worked with a large number of blue-chip, multinational clients on a variety of projects and initiatives in both a digital and integrated environment.

While primarily specialising in marcomms strategy, Drew spent six months building a content production division within an established London agency, producing cutting-edge digital content. He has also worked on a number of UGC and social media projects.

Further to this Drew has developed both creative and technical skills and know-how on a number of personal ventures and projects, further developing his understanding of the digital medium.

Drew speaks Spanish fluently and has a good knowledge of French, Italian and Portuguese.

He currently resides in London.

Strategy, consultancy, digital content production, eCRM, social media, UGC, branding, future trend tracking, foreign languages
