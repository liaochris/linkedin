Peter McCarthy’s Summary

A dynamic and energetic organizational leader with multi-national experience in a complex multi-disciplinary environment. Highly effective at leading teams to utilize and integrate their expertise to fulfill strategic objective. Accomplished at creating and managing a portfolio of drug projects with consideration of medical, commercial, regulatory, scientific and financial requirements. Additionally experienced in supervising and developing people, influencing broadly within an organization, and detecting and adapting to changes in the internal and external environment.

• 20 years experience in drug discovery.
• 12 years experience in drug development (preclinical thru Phase II).
• Therapeutic area leadership experience in obesity, diabetes, osteoporosis, atherosclerosis, cardiovascular, frailty, inflammation, immunology, oncology, antibacterials, and respiratory diseases.
• Experience in discovery and development of small molecules, antibodies, and protein therapeutics.
• Well-versed in all allied disciplines including chemistry, pharmacology, molecular genetics, drug metabolism, pharmaceutical sciences, safety evaluation, regulatory affairs, and clinical sciences.
• 17 publications, 13 patents and 10 scientific presentations.
• Extensive involvement with IND submissions and regulatory interactions.
• Extensive leadership, management and supervisory experience.

The corporation endorsed the option to shift from a pure obesity focus to a diabetes with weight loss focus, and I led this transition in development.
    • Supervised 3-4 Clinicians and 1 Project Manager.
