Joseph Isaacs’s Summary

I am the Founder/CEO of ISG-Telecom Consultants, a telecommunications regulatory consulting and legal firm. I am responsible for the day-to-day operations including legal, sales & marketing, contracts, project managers, strategic alliances, partnerships and public relations. I have formulated strategic partnerships which have generated over $3 Billion in telecommunications deals since 1996.

Additionally, I sit on the Board of Directors and Advisory Councils of over a dozen regional and local telecommunications companies doing business throughout the USA and Canada. I have negotiated thousands of telecommunications agreements and have done thousands of Federal and State CLEC/IXC certification filings. I have been involved in securing billions of dollars of telecommunications related deals.

My company is an authorized dealer, with the nations top carriers including, Verizon, AT&T, Sprint, Nextel & T-Mobile, Dish Networks, GE Security systems and VoIP suppliers.

I am also in business with some very influential people. Here is a link to that release. This is a must see:
https://www.fortunesocial.com/component/content/article/11-myblog/85.html

In 2005 I was responsible for the formation of two Denovo banks in Florida of which I remain a stockholder. Additionally in 2005, I founded Planet One Holdings, a marketing organization. I am the managing member of Hitchhiker Media which produces the world's only 3D calendars. I currently has equity positions in multiple telecommunications company that specialize in long distance, VoIP and local service in 16 states.

I have been licensed in Real Estate in Florida since 1976 and have transacted millions in residential investment property.
