Syed Khalid’s Summary

International Ford Fellow,pursuing Masters in Communication and Development Studies,Ohio University ,Athens,United States.

Communication and Development professional with interest and experience of social entrepreneurship, broadcast, print and social media.

Worked on grassroots-projects in rural and urban India on development communications and micro-finance,research on new work culture,teaching experience in post-graduate media institute and media-production experience with a 24 hours bilingual television news channel .

Also worked on community outreach, community building and social media integration with online fund-raising experience in the US.

Presently engaged in developing a master's project in Ohio University to introduce Social entrepreneurship into the academic curriculum through a graduate course and build a new Center for Social Entrepreneurship as a collaboration between the Appalachian community and the university.

Interested in media relations,social entrepreneurial research, media advocacy,communication for social change and participatory development positions.
