Jill Corbin’s Summary

Corbin Consulting specializes in providing accounting, bookkeeping and business consulting services to small to mid-sized businesses and high net worth individuals. Our trained professionals provide quality service at costs lower than the cost of employing a full time bookkeeper. By outsourcing bookkeeping functions business owners are able to concentrate on their core functions instead of getting weighed down by paper work.

Additionally, Corbin Consulting offers controllership services including financial statement interpretation and trend analysis, budgeting, and forecasting. We work closely with you, the business owner, to interpret and understand this information.
