Fabio Monteiro, MBA (fabio.monteiro@ig.com.br)’s Summary

NET cable TV – I participated in the co-administration of the project Phoenix that sought a reduction in 50% of the time of revenue, collection, and generation of managerial information for the taxes system and monthly balance system of Billing. Being the first project of convergence in the Brazilian market (triple play) that makes possible the release of the product VoIP together with EMBRATEL.

Camasmie – Management of implantation of ERP ABC71 and the restructuring of the company, which involved the whole control of customers, register, plans of sales, finite production, organization interns and it manufactures, seeking the increase of the production, profitability, and differentiation in the textile market.

Grupo Unidos – I participated in the planning, development and administration of the project of development of the new collection system & unification via web, implanted in the 50 dispersed branches in Brazil.

Bank Pactual (UBS) – I was responsible for the area of operational back office and for the network project. In addition, I did all viability analysis of businesses and system for the new division of private pension.
