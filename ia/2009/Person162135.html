Michael Schwandt’s Summary

Michael Schwandt is one of the most dynamic visionaries for choreography and movement today with an extensive body of work in commercials, corporate industrials, television, award shows, special events and various recording artist projects, while crossing all genres of dance. As creative consultant, Michael contributes to the overall look of a project influencing style, costume, lighting and set design as well as musical arrangement. Michael is also the Founder and Creative Director of FUSION ent, an LA-based company that provides dancers and cirque-style specialty acts for live events.

Michael has worked as a choreographer/dancer/consultant with Aerosmith, Jennifer Lopez, Britney Spears, Craig David, Mis-Teeq, the Beach Boys, the Latin Grammy Awards, MGM Mirage and Vh1. He has choreographed live industrials for PUMA, Bravo, Jafra Cosmetics, Charles Schwab and has commercials currently airing for Palm Treo, Land Rover and Mitsubishi. Recent stage works include the Music Video Prod. Awards, Kristine W’s promo tour, Norwegian Cruise Lines and “Jambalaya” at the Caesar’s Nassau Resort & Casino. Michael is currently choreographing Brian Anthony’s video for “What’s It Gonna Be” from Fantastic 4-Rise of the Silver Surfer, Universal recording artist Taxi Doll’s video “Be With You,” UK sensation Jamelia’s Battersea Arena show in London and directing performers for Sheryl Crow’s one-night concert at MGM Las Vegas. Look for his work in 2008 in the Championship Bowl Game Halftime Show on FOX, for Skechers, on the TV show Big Shot Live and as the new staging director & choreographer for Miss USA.

Michael continues to give back to the dance community by nurturing and educating aspiring dancers through guest master classes all over the world. Key credits include the EDGE in Hollywood, Tremaine & Hoctors Dance Convention Tours, and guest spots at Sydney Dance Co and Broadway Dance Center Tokyo.
