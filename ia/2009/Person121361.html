  • Co-Founder

    PF Records

    (Music industry)

    1994 — 2000 (6 years )

    Independent record label which specialized in punk, ska, alternative and Latin artists. First home to such diverse acts as Latch Key Kids (punk), Los Skarnales (latin ska), Southern Backtones (rockabilly), Thanx But No Thanx (punk) and Vatos Locos (Chicano punk/rock). Released series of music compilations and was part of LatinoVision, one of the first online music portals specializing in alterlatino music. In conjunction with Aztlan Records (West Coast) and Grita! Records (East Coast), I participated in cross promotion of album releases and artists regardless of label home. My primary territory was the Southwest United States and Chicago, Ill.

    During my involvement with the label I created grassroots marketing strategies, street team programs and online viral solutions that were very successful and brought a high level of return on investment. The marketing processes I developed would be continuously improved on and later would be implemented as part of my consulting and contract services to many major label groups.

    My knowledge and ability to embrace the internet early on secured PF Records' a digital download agreement in the US with online distributor E-Music - one of the first ever in the industry. Additionally, I secured physical distribution deals in Argentina, Colombia and most notably in Mexico with Mexican based music group Opcion Sonica.

    Internationalized label bands; Latch Key Kids in Canada and Los Skarnales in Latin America and created friendships and business relationships with professionals in the music industry that are still active to this day.

    1987 — 1992 (5 years )

The knowledge i gained allowed me to create online marketing campaigns during the day and into the mid 90's after my employment.
