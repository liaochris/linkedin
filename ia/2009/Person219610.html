Simon Newman’s Summary

I currently am a Team Leader/Client Services Manager for Symphony Services. In addition to managing a small support team, I also have primary Account Management responsibilities for some of Symphony's most complex and demanding customers, including Verizon Business.

I recently worked for Digital Citizen (a joint venture between PassAlong Networks and Zango), which was dissolved due to funding. My role in this joint venture was project managing the development of an advertising-supported online music service, project managing and running the operations of the company, and acting as a reporting analyst.

Prior to being spun out to the joint venture, I was leading sales activities with promotions/advertising agencies.

Prior to Nashville, I lived in San Antonio where I worked for Rackspace Managed Hosting, a technology company that has experienced explosive growth. While at Rackspace, I learned the value of doing whatever it takes for the customer, and the importance of an organization to be both customer and employee focused. I was able to move up and have many different experiences at Rackspace, including managing one of our customer care teams for the Enterprise Division, and being an Account Manager leading the relationship with some of the company's largest and most complex accounts. While at Rackspace, I won their "Fanatic" award for exemplary performance, and I also got mentioned in an article in "FastCompany" magazine (http://www.fastcompany.com/magazine/83/open_cuckoo.html).

I attended The University of North Carolina - Chapel Hill, where I got a BSBA at the Kenan Flagler Business School, a top 10 undergraduate program.

I also worked for 1.5 years with Healthstream (www.healthstream.com), a leading healthcare e-learning company in Nashville. While there, I managed relationships with some of their largest, most complicated accounts, including Tenet, Sutter, Baxter, Roche, Cardinal Health, and Medtronic.
