Janardhanan Gangathulasi’s Summary

Jana is having six years of teaching and research experience in geotechnical and geoenvironmental engineering. He obtained his doctoral degree in the area of bioreactor landfills and developed an operational procedure for accelerating the degradation of MSW with the correlations to interpret their engineering properties. He was also involved in the geophysical investigations on landfills to assess the efficacy of leachate recirculation.

In addition to his research, he was involved in the consultancy testing undertaken by the Geotechnical laboratory of UIC. He has worked on more than 100 projects which involves laboratory testing on the field soil samples as per ASTM procedures. Some of the consultancy projects were done for Argonne National Laboratory, STAT analysis, CREED etc. He has a good working knowledge of geotechnical software tools such as SLIDE, FLAC and also underwent training for PLAXIS.

As a faculty, he implemented practical oriented teaching module, mandatory field/industrial trip for the courses taught. He authored a paper emphasizing the need for knowledge network in geotechnical engineering education. He delivered lectures with video presentation to motivate undergraduate students to pursue career in geotechnical engineering field.

During his earlier career as a project associate in Center for Disaster Mitigation and Management involved in major projects Seismic Microzonation and Vulnerability Landslide Atlas of India. He outlined procedure for liquefaction susceptibility assessment based on field testing. He co-authored the training manual "SEISMIC MICROZONATION – A GEOTECHNICAL APPROACH", publication sponsored by the Ministry of Science and Technology, Government of India. He also developed serviceability equations for the maintenance of low volume roads for his master thesis. EIT certified professional and also holds FEMA ISC certification. He is recipient of ASCE(IL) Geotechnical Student Award and SWISS Confederation fellowship.

Janardhanan Gangathulasi’s Specialties:

Laboratory testing of soils as per ASTM, BIS and BS procedures, Geoenvironmental Engineering, Environmental Risk Assessment (USEPA & RCRA Tier 1 & 2), Slope stability analysis, Liquefaction susceptibility assessment, Solid waste engineering, Soil mechanics, Disaster mitigation and Management,
