Chris Moander’s Summary

Problems come up in life and in business. The key is to anticipate the problems and work to minimize the problem's effect on the individual or the company and that is where my skills come into play. My passion is to work with you to develop preventative measures for problems I anticipate. The result? Less stress and less cost for you. Stated another way, I focus on proactive, not reactive, strategies on your behalf.

Because of my pre-law work history and having run my own firm in the past, I have a passion to serve small businesses and entrepreneurs. I know the entrepreneurial mindset and all the sacrifices required of those who take such risks. I want to be there to support and ultimately witness the success of the small business owner and his or her employees.

I also work with individuals in planning estates or drafting wills. My goal is to ensure that each person has end-of-life matters properly structured so they can rest assured that his or her family will be cared for after death.
