Nancye Bailey’s Summary

Founder and owner of Carolina Event Consultants, an event and meeting planning firm based in South Carolina. CEC specalizes in planning events using creative and innovative designs for a variety of functions from the largest of corporate events to the most intimate of gatherings. When you only want the best, I can provide customized experiences for sporting events, luxurious destination travel, entertainment, concierge services, and special treatment for VIPs.

Along with an eye for detail and a committment to provide excellent customer service, I also bring to the table more than 10 years of planning experience. In addition, my staff has a combined 25 years experience in the events industry and is poised to assist you with all of your event and meeting planning needs.
