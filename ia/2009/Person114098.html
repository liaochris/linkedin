Jason Lind’s Summary

Jason Lind is an expert at making the impossible happen. With a knowledge of technology that is both wide and deep, Jason makes an excellent edition to any Microsoft team. He has been working in .NET since the beginning, primarily on the ASP.NET/C# side, as well as SQL Server 200x, W3C Standards, Windows Forms and Silverlight. Few people can match his ability to rapidly design and deploy custom business applications on the .NET platform.

Jason is looking for Innovative positions that leverage both his extensive technology and leadership skills, primarily focusing on the titles of Solutions Architect and Team Lead.
