Trevor Brody’s Summary

As a Coe Financial Group affiliate, in all situations Trevor strives to make recommendations with the clients best interest in mind.

Trevor takes the time to get to know you and your preferences. He can help you anticipate and prepare for the best and worst scenarios. The relationship established with you enables him to focus on your needs, the needs of your family and/or your business.

Outside of Coe Financial Group, Trevor serves on the Board of Directors of Special Kids Network, an organization that was created to provide funding for programs that help children with special needs; In addition he serves on the Board of Trustees of Helping from Heaven, an organization dedicated to improving the comfort and quality of life for children with special needs.

Prior to joining Coe Financial Group in 2003, Trevor obtained his Bachelor of Business degree in Computer Information Systems from Western Michigan University.

Trevor Brody is a Series 6 & 63 offering securities through First Allied Securities. In addition he is licensed in Accident, Health, Casualty, Fire and Life.
