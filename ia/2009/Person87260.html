Giuseppe Russo’s Summary

Throughout my education and career to date, I have been involved in many areas of industrial design. I have worked most recently as a consumer product designer but a consistent part of my early experience also comes from yacht and interior design, design research and concept design roles.

Concept creation:
Conceptual studies to define new mass-market products/product families (based on trends, technologies, user aspirations, user segments);
Development and presentation of fresh designs and design languages for products to fit into the company’s portfolio (understanding the Company’s vision/strategy, opportunities/risks, organization/timeframe).

Industrial design:
Creation and follow-up of the ID for products in the Company’s current roadmap (dealing mostly with Mechanical Engineering Teams and ODMs)
Represent the ID Team in meetings with Program Managers, Mechanical Engineering Teams, UI Teams, Human Factors Teams, etc. and reporting back to the Design Managers.
I have been acknowledged for consistently delivering fresh ideas and concepts. My work has been appreciated for its design quality and value, it has been commended when reviewed by Senior Global Leadership, and has contributed towards defining the Motorola’s current portfolio.
