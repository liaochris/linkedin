Sami Isoaho’s Summary

I have been entrepreneur (running my own business) since 1994. At ICT-business I have been since 1991.
Tietoexpertit is my company, founded in 1995. "Tieto" is a Finnish word and means "knowledge" and "expertit" is the Finnish plural form of 'the expert' (you know, we finns have very strange way to twist english words ;).

Shortly, Tietoexpertit sells our team (me and my colleagues) knowledge.

Previously (-2007) I have acted as a member of the board at Go Finland Oy (www.gofinland.fi) and the chairman of the board at Tietotalo Infocenter Oy (www.tietotalo.fi).

Totally I have been involved with more than 10 startup's.

Sami Isoaho’s Specialties:

At business side I have quite strong experience about mergers, buy-outs and acquisition. Strong growth and growth management (control) are also well-known.

By productional / technical side I have quite good knowledge of project management (I have some diplomas too), professional software development leading and digital media consept design.
