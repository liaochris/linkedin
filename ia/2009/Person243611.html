Vincent Rais’s Summary

Working in the telecom and internet sectors since 1996, I've moved gradually from fixed voice networks (pricing, billing and product management) to ISPs (product management, marketing, sales). I have a broad view of both telecom (fixed and mobile) and internet (access and content providers, as well as community models, online marketing, sales & advertising). I enjoy building revenue increasing solutions together with customers/partners, and keeping things sweet and simple as much as possible.

Vincent Rais’s Specialties:

marketing, sales and product development on fixed line, mobile and internet. Convergence and how to best deploy the available media to come to a multi-channel solution for the customer's marketing or contact needs.

I have experience with IP networks, BGP4, NNTP, hosting, Windows Server 2003, online collaboration solutions like (hosted) SharePoint/Exchange/OCS, service marketing, (complex) solution sales, provisioning, billing, marketing strategies, niche marketing and sales.
