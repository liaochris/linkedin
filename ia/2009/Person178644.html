Omar Khan’s Summary

Clients who have had the pleasure to work with Omar truly understand the care and patience he displays to assure that their project/campaign gets completed in the best possible way. In essence, he is on top of all aspects of the work process, possessing an uncanny ability to predict situations and offer up solutions to keep projects on track. His exacting professionalism stems from his overall philosophy of life, which seeks a definitive balance between mind, body and soul.

Omar has been working on Fortune 100 interactive engagements for over 9 years. After completing his MBA, Omar joined a San Diego based Internet Start-Up to develop and run an offshore development facility in the Sub-Continent. Omar continued his interactive agency career in North America working with the enormously talented Sonic Boom Creative Media, Blast Radius, Organic and Grip Limited. Omar currently works for Wunderman (the world's largest marketing services network - Advertising Age 2008) to manage their key Telecom and Automotive clients on the interactive side.

Omar has worked with numerous global brands/clients including: Rogers, Ford, Sirius Satellite Radio, Sprint PCS, Chrysler LLC, BMW & Mini, Cushman & Wakefield, Procter & Gamble, Honda & Acura, Whirlpool Canada, Citibank Canada, Harry Rosen, Aeroplan, SEI Investments and National Cancer Institute of Canada to name a few.

Omar has also worked in partnership with some of Canada's leading Advertising, Media and Interactive agencies including BBDO, Cundari, The Media Company, PHD and Teehan+Lax.

Omar Khan’s Specialties:

Account Management, Project & Program Management, Business Development, Relationship Builder, Tactical and Strategic Negotiator, Positive Cultural Change Agent, Demonstrated success in Empathy based Management
