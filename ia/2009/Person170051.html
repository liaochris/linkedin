peter jin hong’s Summary

Perhaps this is the secret formula for UX innovation insights:

meaningful innovation = context ( just enough ) + relevance ( just enough )

Inspired by Milton Glaser's life lesson #5, which beautifully offers that Less is not More, and More is not More, rather... "Just enough is More".

How great is that—the right amount of what really matters so that the experience is wonderfully satisfying. Even if life doesn't have a lot of simple solutions, it has lots of dots... and together, we can get better at both finding and connecting them.
