Wayne Kairdolf’s Summary

Highly Creative, Market oriented, Sales Engineer, with over 10 years of experience in telecommunications. Created and headed a new business area through strategic plan involving management. Effectively led cross-functional teams to successfully deliver state-of-the-art systems. Demonstrated success driving growth in targeted markets through implementation of key projects. Solid leadership skills; able to build and guide top-performing teams. Adept at communicating with management, vendors, and internal departments to coordinate overall sales efforts.

Experience in all levels of networking, data communications, and wireless internet service provisioning. Highly skilled implementation of large scale Wide Area Network project deployment. As a Cisco, Microsoft, and Wireless Network Engineer, working across multiple platforms has become integral. Significant experience in engineering and managing data networks and RF systems. Team Leader of most communication projects while working for multiple ILECs and CLECs. Utilization of Wireless Ethernet, Local Loops, and optimization of ISM 900, ISM 2.4 GHz, ISM 5.8 GHz, UNII, licensed FCC and ETSI Band Networks.

Proficiencies in all aspects of site planning, analysis, acquisition, surveys, construction, and tower erection. Adept on building permits and applications, site selection, selection of manufacturer, style and pricing, soil sampling, surveying, layouts. I also possess the skills necessary to build turnkey tower sites, including knowledge of communication regulations.

Thorough in Data and RF system design. Responsibilities also have included link budget, use of propagation tools, antenna diversity implementation, terrain analysis, spectrum analysis, system tests and network feasibility verification for wireless internet systems. Also versed in most aspects of communications management by bid response, marketing, and proposing, said projects for enterprise LANs, large scale WANs.
