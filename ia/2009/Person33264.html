Wrote programs for various tasks including an audit program tracking shipyard versus Navy records of expenditures for all AEGIS cruiser and Burke Class Destroyer contracts. Collateral duty: DISCO Security Officer eight years; rewrote company security manual and incorporated employee badge system. I retired from JJMA in May 1995 and went fishing! In December 2002, JJMA became part of employee-owned Alion Science and Technology.

  • Missile Technician Chief Petty Officer

    U.S.

    (Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    February 1956 — June 1979 (23 years 5 months)

    A few days after 17th birthday in February 1956, entered U.S. Navy Reserve, Surface Div. 1-29, Augusta, ME as Electronics Field Seaman Apprentice. Joined regular Navy Sept.9, 1957. After Fire Control Technician (FT) training (5" guns), went to first ship, USS POWER DD-839, Bath, ME built! After three years and promotion to FTG2, volunteered for submarines.
