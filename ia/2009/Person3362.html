Adarsh Kulshrestha’s Summary

Interview: Red Herring Top 100
http://www.brightcove.tv/title.jsp?title=1352464904&channel=1263945361

As Regalix' Chief Financial Officer and Head of Strategic Business Development, Adarsh has executive responsibility for Finance, Sales, Business Development and G&A activities. In addition, Adarsh heads strategic new business initiatives. Adarsh brings a strong background in corporate finance, strategy and technology business development.

Before joining Regalix, Adarsh was with Alcatel USA's $2 billion Broadband Division where he led strategic business development, financial analysis and planning, M&A, analyst and press relations, eBusiness and outsourcing activities. Adarsh was closely involved in Alcatel's $1.5 Billion acquisition of GenesysLabs, a Nasdaq-listed company in San Francisco, CA.

Earlier, as a Corporate Finance Investment Banker, he successfully executed multiple IPOs, private equity and debt placements and M&A transactions.

Adarsh holds a Wharton MBA, a University of Michigan MS and an IIT-Delhi B.Tech.
