Brian Riley’s Summary

Over 9 years experience architecting, leading, developing, and delivering rich internet application based solutions within the financial services and retail sectors in the Fortune 100. I work directly with technical architects and user experience directors to coalesce the technical requirements with the creative vision; I offer strategic consulting including technical assessments and informational sessions to clients diving in to the RIA space.

I have expertise in Flex, Flash, AIR, ActionScript 2 & 3, LCDS, BlazeDS, WebORB, Ajax, (including Dojo and other popular toolkits and frameworks), and J2EE development.

I've also led several RIA technical educational conferences, informational sessions, and workshops to IBM's global business services division and its clients.

Started working with the Flash Platform with Flash 3 (TellTarget anyone?) and worked directly with Macromedia to vet out Flex during it's Alpha and Beta releases as Royale (compiling Flex on the sever ONLY...old-school Flex).
