Haitske van de Linde’s Summary

I am nosy by nature and therefore interested in a broad spectrum of subjects. I am also told that I am very stubborn, but I like to sell myself as being tenacious. :-)
I love using different media to tell a story or get a message across. I am very much at ease with speaking in public and highly enjoy doing so.
One of my favourite job-related activities is coming up with new ideas for stories, websites or tv-shows.
