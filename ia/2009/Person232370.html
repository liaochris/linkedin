Thomas Carroll’s Summary

My career started in 1990 at Tracor System Technologies where I transitioned from Computer Aided Design for the Department of Defense to the development of security applications based on visual basic and the emerging internet. 1998 found me at American Express where I led a design team that developed an ASP website allowing the Financial Advisors to manage client accounts. Next I took a permanent position with iManage, a developer of online collaboration applications, where I honed my Interaction Design skills creating the interfaces for their suite of products. A move to California led me to a contract position creating and building a web-based class scheduling application for Minnix, Inc. Later I provided all of the Interaction Design and front-end ASP programming for inSite’s SAGE tool, a web-based meeting scheduling application. Previously I was engaged in building a web-based mortgage application and portal for Ameriquest Mortgage Company by providing the interaction design and leading the design team in prototyping/wireframing as well as contributing to the business analysis process. Most recently I have been involved with Move as a Senior Interaction Designer as part of their User Experience group.

I want to lead a design group with a focus on interaction design. Key strengths include innovation, creativity and an understanding of end-user expectations. I am able to inject new and existing products with cutting-edge, user-friendly interfaces.
