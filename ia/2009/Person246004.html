Wayne Wilson’s Summary

I have over 19 years of experience in IT consulting and management using a wide range of technologies. After my start developing workgroup applications using a 4GL called 4th Dimension, I became an early adopter of Java (JDK 1.0.8 in 1997), concentrating on distributed web applications in the SFA/CRM and e-commerce markets. Later I expanded my skillset to include Microsoft technologies, successfully delivering many .NET-based projects. My experience on the two major development platforms has allowed me to be a technology agnostic architect, choosing the right solution for the job based on the requirements and existing environment.

For all my career I have been involved in the full lifecycle of a project, from project definition and proposal, to requirements gathering and analysis, to design, implementation, deployment and maintenance. I am a "hands-on" architect also known for my client interaction skills: being able to really understand what a client wants, translate that into a solution and fully communicate my vision of that solution to the client.

For most of the past 12 years I have been in a technical management position from a team lead to the director of engineering at two companies, managing up to ten engineers and each company's infrastructure.

Wayne Wilson’s Specialties:

Software architecture &development in both Java & .NET, database design & implementation, technical leadership, client interaction.
