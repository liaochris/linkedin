Rob Newport’s Summary

Weburban provides design, animation and software development services for the web. We specializes in web design, Flash animation, and software engineering for Windows, Linux, and Macintosh systems. Our Portfolio includes projects for Cingular Wireless, Nissan Motorsports, LeoVince Exhaust Systems, Country Wide Home Loans, Scorpion Helmets, Yokohama, Honda, and many more. With over 12 years in the internet development business, Weburban has proven to be one of the most sustainable companies in the industry with a solid reputation and proven financials. Our record of excellence is partly due to our understanding of the design and software planning process. All of our proposals and quotes come with our own unique insight into a project.
