Badri Raghavan’s Summary

CEO of Prediction Machines. Prediction Machines Inc. (www.predictionmachines.net) is a startup focused on providing advanced Analytics based business solutions for the Smart Grid. Our proprietary algorithms analyze vast amounts of data that is now becoming available in the generation, demand and management of energy to help our customers identify, optimize and predict patterns of demand and supply.

Co-Chair of the Clean Tech Special Interst Group (SIG) of TiE Bangalore. TiE (The Indus Entrepreneurs) is arguably the largest and most influential global non profit organization aimed at fostering entrepreneurship. The Clean Tech SIG aims to create an ecosystem focused on entrepreneurial opportunities in the clean technology space.

In the past, a senior leader in Business Analytics applications with extensive experience in designing, deploying and maintaining advanced analytic decisioning software in Payment Cards, Merchant Acquirers, e-Commerce, Telecom, and Banking Compliance. Successfully started new international office in Bangalore, India, and led several strategic initiatives in Risk, Fraud, Marketing, and Analytic consulting.

Badri Raghavan’s Specialties:

Clean technology, energy efficiency and optimization, demand forecasting and optimization. Analytic decision support systems, offshore-onsite global workflow, program management, risk management in retail banking customer relationship lifecycle, fraud detection and prevention, competitive landscape in outsourcing.
