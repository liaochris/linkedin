John Rosser’s Summary

Hire InTuition puts tomorrow’s leaders to work for you, today. We do this by linking college students with businesses to complete part-time work, related to the students’ majors.

Hire InTuition offers companies a low cost, high quality solution to their human resource needs. At the same time, we supply students with part-time work that enhances their professional experiences, provides competitive wages, and molds around their class schedules.
