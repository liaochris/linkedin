Abhishek Sharma’s Summary

I have 6.5 years of in-depth experience in IT & Finance with 4 years of excellent track record in Treasury & Risk management. Currently, I am with Thomson Reuters actualizing Treasury Product Implementations (functionally & technically) from specification to post go live support.

Functional Experience: Worked on 'As Is' 'To Be' study, detailed functional / interface / report specifications, CR / SoW management, end user training, data migration, nostro / intersystem reconciliation, verification of pricing, valuation and trial balance. Setup of organization structure, counterparties, securities, SSIs, accounting entries and Deal / GL revaluation.

Technical Experience: Test, review and develop test cases, MIS / Statutory reports, Settlement messages such as SWIFT / RTGS / IFN, Confirmation formats such as long form / short form, Batch jobs such as fixing / maturity / inventory queuing and netting, interfaces with other systems such as CBS / risk management and provide UAT /Live support till stabilization
