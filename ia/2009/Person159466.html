Melissa Chase’s Summary

Ms. Chase is an experienced immigration lawyer with a true passion for immigration. For seven years Ms. Chase has practiced solely immigration where she has represented employers, employees, families and individuals in accessing and fulfilling the American dream.

Ms. Chase is admitted to practice before the Oklahoma Supreme Court, the Federal District Court for the Western and the Northern Districts of Oklahoma, the 10th Circuit Court of Appeals and the Federal District Court for the Southern District of Texas. Ms. Chase is also a certified advocate for the Sexual Assault Victims Advocacy Service in Prince William County.

Currently, Ms. Chase is a member of the American Immigration Lawyers Association and ZONTA and is the past-President of the South Oklahoma City Lawyers Association.

Ms. Chase has been a featured speaker on topics such as Work-site enforcement, Employer Compliance and Non-Immigrant Visa Categories.
