Deanne Verrall’s Summary

Imagine You Have a Highly Skilled Executive Assistant …

… your Right-Hand Person who makes your business run smoothly … organising your diary, confirming appointments, typing up notes, transcribing tapes, running meetings, buying office supplies, screening your calls and emails, organising events, managing your customer database, writing reports and a host of other little jobs.

What could you do with your spare time? Get home on time, spend time with your family, have lunch with colleagues, grow your business?

Would You Like a Highly Skilled Executive Assistant from as little as $40?

HOW?

I provide “virtual” administrative support to small to medium businesses – businesses just like yours who may either have too much to do and not enough hours in the day, or who need a reliable extra pair of hands and who don’t want the hassle and the cost of hiring extra staff or a temp.

With the advances of modern technology, you don’t need to have your Executive Assistant physically sitting in your office … costing you money even when you don’t have work for them to do, using expensive equipment and creating administrative overheads. I work from my home, using my equipment … only when you need me.

Hiring a Virtual Assistant means you only pay for the services and time you actually use – I am more economical than a temp (no agency fees or paying for breaks) and because you get the same person each time, I get to know your business and its needs. You get the benefits of a top-flight executive assistant, at only a fraction of the cost.
