Sean Correia’s Summary

Sean Correia is a Senior Partner for Phalanx Ventures, Inc where he is responsible for the business execution strategy and working with clients to mentor and assist with funding, execution, organizational structures and other aspects to ensure they execute successfully, are prepared to launch and well positioned to potential investors and strategic partners.

Correia also provides specialized sales, marketing and business development guidance to CEO’s at early stage high-tech companies, since traditional sales and marketing techniques for mainstream products do not work effectively for early stage companies.

Before joining Phalanx Ventures Correia co-founded EarlyStageProducts, LLC where he was responsible for advising and enabling client companies to capitalize on future growth opportunities.

Prior to EarlyStageProducts Correia was the Business Development Manager for the NA Inside Sales Organization of Gartner. Sean's focus was to build and grow strategic relationships with C-level accounts. In 3 years at Gartner Sean has won the prestigious Eagle award for sales excellence as well as Q3 2007 MVP and number 1 sales executive NA inside Sales.

Before Gartner Correia was a business analyst for 2 years with RSM Equico, Inc.

Previous to RSM Correia served as President and CEO of Learn2 Distribution, Inc., which was recognized as the first eLearning company to be ranked in Entrepreneur Magazine’s “TOP 100” in June 2001.

Before founding Learn2 Distribution Correia served as Vice President of Sales and Marketing for Omni Diagnostics, Inc, where he drove all activities related to sales, support and marketing. Sean developed a partnership alliances program which resulted in a 1,200% increase in sales in just two years. The key ingredient to the partnership alliance success was Sean’s dynamic sales training program.
