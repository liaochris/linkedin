Gabor Talan’s Summary

Parallel Partners is an executive search firm that offers recruitment services for direct hire positions and contract assignments for information technology professionals.

President Saven Bellarmino founded Parallel Partners in 1995 to offer high-end technical recruitment services to Chicago's top companies. Since then, Parallel Partners has expanded its services nationwide. Prior to founding Parallel Partners, Saven Bellarmino was the Corporate Vice President of Operations for a National Staffing Company.

Parallel Partners success is driven by know-how, employee retention and extreme customer care. Clients range from the Fortune 500 to emerging technology firms.
