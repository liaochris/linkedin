Leesa McKeon’s Summary

Amazing Affairs and Events is an affordable event, wedding and marketing company serving Western North Carolina and specializing in creative theme decor with 30 years experience. We are known in our community for our easy going spirit, meticulous detailing and reliability. Amazing Affairs and Events combines talents in creativity, style, decor, reliability, organization skills, and budget planning to assist you with your Corporate Event, Special Event, Wedding, or Non-Profit/Fundraising Events.
Leesa McKeon, owner of Amazing Affairs and Events, has over fourteen years experience in the interior design and space planning field, and 30 years experience in various venues of event, wedding planning and marketing. Her eye for painting a mental picture and then creating it is amazing.

Creative problem solving, providing innovative solutions that work well, look attractive, yet managing "the budget" are welcome challenges.
