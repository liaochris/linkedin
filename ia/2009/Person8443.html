Alex Mumo’s Summary

I am a telecommunications experienced risk and fraud analyst, with over 3 years solid experience .
I aspire to be a market leader in Revenue Assurance and Fraud management, effectively controlling revenue loss and fraud to optimize company revenues and eventually manage a Revenue Assurance and Fraud department.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS
-Between Jan –Mar 2009, saved $2,000,000 of Interconnect revenues from Sim-box fraud.
-Reduced Grey traffic in the Network to less than 2%.
-Since November 2008 to March 2009, reduced SMS Fraud scam in the network by 90%.
-January 2008, identified EIR (Equipment Identity Register) anomaly after network swap.
-Conducting Risk Impact Assessments for all promotion events and products.
-Timely analysis of HUR and NRTRDE alerts.
-Carried out effective ZAP (Zain Money Transfer) tests leading to a smooth launch.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
-Monitoring and control of Sim-Box fraud to prevent revenue leakage from network bypass.
-Validation of complete Interconnect CDRs for billing partners per route.
-Analysis of Interconnect traffic for completeness of reporting.
-Validation of Interconnect invoices sent to and received from Interconnect partners to ensure accuracy and completeness.
-Resolution of any Interconnect disputes.
-Validating Distributors remuneration, that is commissions and payments.
-Analysis of fraud alerts from the fraud Management System to monitor and identify any fraud including; Call selling, Network hacking, Sim-cloning, PRS, Call back,Subscription and PABX frauds.
-High user roaming (HUR) and NRTRDE monitoring to identify and prevent roaming fraud.
-Conducting Internal and External Investigations.
-Access control, review and analysis of Audit logs to ensure no unauthorized access to systems.
-Tariff/Short code Tests and Test SIM analysis to ensure accurate billing and minimizing misuse of test Sims.
