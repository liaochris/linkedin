Johan van Mil’s Summary

Johan is managing partner of investment holding Dialogue Company and promoter and partner of Peak Capital which invests in starting and growing companies in TMC (Technology, Media & Communications). In 2005 Johan wrote the book "Entrepreneurship: Just do it!” and he is a member of the Education Council of the University of Amsterdam.

Johan is currently also vice-chairman and responsible for Interactive Marketing within the DDMA (Dutch Dialogue Marketing Association). Besides this he is als board member of the board of advice of IPAN (Interactive Professionals Netherlands) were he acted as a chairman in the period 2001-2004. Furthermore Johan is jury member of the "DM Man of the Year" and of the Dialogue Marketing Challenge.

Prior to this Johan van Mil founded Mailmedia in 2002, which was sold in 2009 to OMG (Online Marketing Group). Johan worked as a Commercial Director at BNR Nieuws Radio and held various sales, marketing and management positions at different publishers.
