Mark Torng’s Summary

Mark Torng, is a senior hands-on consultant with over 11 years of industry experience. Mark enjoys developing business platforms, specializing in system architecture, high availability, deployment and design.

After completing his education, Mark explored future options in mechanics and electronics, developing an extensive hardware and network knowledge.

After getting bored with the transistor, Mark enjoyed building complex network environments and later became a Network Architect and has been extremely successful in finding solutions to complex business problems, specializing in network infrastructure troubleshooting, design, and exploring business needs.

In his personal life Mark enjoys exploring anything else he can do with his hands, from robotics to carpentry or whatever else has caught his attention for the weekend.

You can always track Mark down on his blog at http:/blog.vistanetworks.ca where he provides a brain dump, or you can e-mail him directly mark@vistanetworks.ca
