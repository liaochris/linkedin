Lonny Gulden’s Summary

After a 33 year career in sales I recently launched my own consulting and recruiting firm here in the Twin Cities. I recruit nationally in the areas of information technology, technology sales and marketing, electronic engineering and manufacturing. On a more personal note, I have been active in aiding individuals in career transition for over 15 years as an avocation. My new career allows me to align my professional activities with my passion for helping others.
