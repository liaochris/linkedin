Chad Falkenberg’s Summary

Our company, Soilworks® provides engineered solutions to the broad needs of the dust control and soil stabilization industry. Whether your market is commercial, industrial, military or residential, we have the innovative tools, unmatched technical support, and environmentally-friendly technologies to fulfill your specific requirements. Soilworks® performance-driven portfolio of industry-leading products include the Patented U.S. Dept. of Defense Surtac®, Soiltac®, Powdered Soiltac®, Gorilla-Snot®, Durasoil® and the newly patented water proofing Dryloc®. Our international sales network, global distribution centers, and integrated customer service provide an ideal platform to satisfy your needs. Get to know us better at www.soilworks.com or call 1-800-545-5420.

  • Regional & International Sales Manager

    Vermillion's Environmental Products & Applications, Inc. (EP&A)

    (Chemicals industry)

    March 2001 — March 2003 (2 years 1 month)

    I sold and applied Envirotac II Acrylic Copolymer (AKA Rhino-Snot). Created and maintained www.envirotac.com. Established standard application and dilution rates. Created the logo, brochures and literature. Established and developed international sales including military sales in Iraq. I discovered and experienced the limitations of NOT having a formula and relying on non-prime polymer emulsions. These were traditionally disposed of as waste including concentrated flush water, ultra-filtrate, by-products, off-grades, down-grades, off-spec, aged, distressed, spoiled, bacterial infested, reworked, destabilized, waste polymer emulsions. Unfortunately, these were the acceptable standards for dust control product companies at the time.

    I realized that the success and integrity I sought could not be achieved at EP&A with Envirotac II. It was time to leave EP&A, start Soilworks and formulate an engineered prime copolymer emulsion that could provide repeatable and predictable results which soon became the world’s soil stabilizer of choice, Soiltac®.
