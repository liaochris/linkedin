Sandy Asch’s Summary

Sandy Asch is the principal of Alliance for Organizational Excellence LLC, a San Diego based consulting company. She is the author of Excellence at Work: The Six Keys to Inspire Passion in the Workplace, a nationally recognized speaker and regular columnist for the San Diego Union Tribune. Sandy was a top rated speaker at the SHRM 2008 and 2009 annual conferences.

Sandy's passion is to make the workplace exciting and joyful - a place where people experience pride, satisfaction and fulfillment. She consults with companies globally to achieve employer-of-choice status and improve bottom line results through increased ability to attract, optimize and retain top talent. Fifteen thousand individuals from 400+ companies on four continents have participated in her programs.

Modern Postcard, Asch’s client, achieved unprecedented growth and increased revenue in 2005, decreasing turnaround time by 64%, and increasing customer service satisfaction to 92%. Modern Postcard won the 2005 Workplace Excellence Award.

In 2007, REMEC Defense & Space, increased employee retention by 20+% and improved quality by more than 14%. REMEC was recognized as a finalist in San Diego’s 2007 Best Places to Work and won the Marble Award for Workplace Excellence in 2008 demonstrating exceptional business results.

Sandy has eighteen years success consulting, training and coaching with leaders on five continents, and combines real-world leadership and management experience with a strong academic background. The result: real-world application that improves the bottom line through increased morale and performance.

She holds a Masters Degree in Organizational Management and has taught Graduate and Undergraduate classes in Management, Organizational Behavior and Human Resources Management. She is a frequent presenter to human resource groups and an approved speaker for Vistage International, where she presents to CEO groups internationally.

Sandy Asch’s Specialties:

Employer of choice strategy, employment branding, high-touch recruitment videos, Excellence programs, leadership development and inspiring keynotes.
