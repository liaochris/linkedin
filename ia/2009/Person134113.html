  • ERP Project Manager

    Caterpillar

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; CAT; Machinery industry)

    March 2000 — August 2005 (5 years 6 months)

    ERP Project Manager with Caterpillar Tosno, responsible for ERP class system (JDEdwards) implementation and reengineering of planning and control functions of the factory after Class “A” Oliver Wight standards. In autumn of 2005 the plant was awarded Class “A” certificate of operational excellence from Oliver Wight (first in CIS). The major achievements included: from 50% customer service level to consistently higher then 98%; from 20% production on time to consistently higher then 95%; from 5% shop floor stock inventory accuracy to consistently higher then 95%. This level of operations control resulted in 20% shop floor efficiency improvement, 25% inventory level reduction and 2005 being the first profitable year since the start of CAT Tosno in Russia. Definitely these achievements are attributed to the whole team of CAT Tosno people I used to work with and I am proud of being a part and one of the leaders of this excellence team.
