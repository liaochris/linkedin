Shelly Begarowicz’s Summary

My story's similar to many, maybe even YOURS. Before I started internet marketing, I was the shipping manager for a large retail company. It was a good solid job. I enjoyed it, the pay was good and I could stay home and homeschool my daughter.

Then... as happens to all too many women, my husband left. Suddenly, I no longer had an income, a place to live or transportation. I was left with a decision...

•Shove my daughter into school and try to find a JOB that would support us in the manner my daughter and I were accustomed to after 10 years of unemployment.

•Try to keep what little stability was left in our life and start a business for myself. One that I could work from home and not disrupt my daughter’s life any more than it was.

I started out knowing nothing about the internet. I could surf and read email. That was it!

The internet's income potential fascinated me. I knew there had to be a way to make a good living online.

I read till my eyes crossed. Listened to speaker after speaker... I build a website and achieved moderate success. But the residual income the BIG money I knew was out there still eluded me.

Then I Found Renegade University!

Here I found internet attraction marketing that is paced for beginners, so NO ONE get's left behind. Using step by step video tutorials Renegade University shows anyone who's willing to put forth the effort how to build their business online.

The rest, as they say, is history... Now I'm a successful internet marketing consultant teaching new and struggling business owners to build a thriving business online using the latest web 2.0 tools and attraction marketing techniques.

The internet is in a growth phase and you couldn't be starting out at a better time.

Attraction marketing WORKS! And it works BIG. If you'd like to learn how to make it work for you NOW is the time to take action. Let my experience shave years of trail and error off your internet business journey.
