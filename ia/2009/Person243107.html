Vijay Subramanian’s Summary

A renowned expert on materials, corrosion, coatings, surface science, adhesion and tribology.

One of the few experts on organofuncational silane technology in the world

Currently Product Development Manager at Federal Mogul Friction Products, responsible for Original Equipment and Aftermarket brake pad formulation for LV market in North America. Responsibilities cover Non Asbestos Organic (NAO) and Semimetallic brake pads.
