Linda Wittig’s Summary

As Social Media is my current passion, I am working with Alex Zagoumenov to develop a series of Social Media seminars and workshops.We are very excited to be in a delivery position this fall. I continue to blog at Get the Dirt (www.creativechickie.com) about Social Media, Internet Marketing, Writing and Video production, in an effort to provide the knowledge and skills others need to succed in this exciting area.

I recently completed a three-month contract with an Internet Marketing company coordinating a Social Media campaign. I created and managed multiple account set-ups, content development and page population. I worked with client on copy approach, event ideas & implementation strategy. Facilitated account updates and ensured each vehicle was tied to the other. I also invited followers through Twellow to join specific Social Media sites for maximum exposure.

My Media Production experience as a Project Manager, Writer/Producer/Director has been invaluable. I've learned the importance of strategic time lines and goal setting, clear messaging, the subtleties of teamwork and strong direction. It's been a very interesting ride to where I am now.

I have ran a Creative Department twice, hired and managed a creative team of five in a Media Production and Broadcast environment, written corporate and creative communications for the Municipal Government, taught writing at the College level and been the Project Manager on many, many creative productions.

Linda Wittig’s Specialties:

I'm in my element when looking at the big picture. I like to look at challenges from every angle, then provide creative solutions that fit the clients communication challenges. I am energized when researching, strategizing and presenting to clients. In my view, there are never problems, only solutions.
