Arne Bergmann’s Summary

Mr Bergmann is GROUP Technologies' Director International Sales, whose responsibilities include, international development, marketing and sales.

During the course of 10 years of being involved with high technology, Bergmann has formed a diverse background spanning companies ranging in size from startup-phase through to large multi-national organizations.

He is experienced in an array of high technology arenas including; telecommunication, software management, e-mail security, organisation, management and compliance. Bergmann´s specialty is in forming partnerships and in managing strategic top customers.

Prior to joining GROUP in 2002, Mr Bergmann was a solution sales specialist at asknet AG, a Germany based company which developed software management portals and marketed complete solutions for software inventory, deployment, metering and auditing.
