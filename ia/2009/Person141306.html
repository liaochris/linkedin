Linda Marcus’s Summary

Public relations and marketing communications consultant with over 30 years experience in the high-tech, business-to-business and industrial-processing industries. Every business needs to clearly and concisely communicate its benefits to target markets. I take an organic approach to marketing that naturally builds credibility with your target audiences as we define and communicate your organization's advantages with insight and transparency. My capabilities include long-term strategic communications planning, writing, brochure and collateral development, online marketing, direct response, trade shows and seminars.

Linda Marcus’s Specialties:

Providing strategic marketing communications including program development, concept and writing, public relations, trade show marketing and program management:

* Strategic Messaging, Integrated Campaigns
* Long Term or Project PR, Launches, Media Relations, Press Materials, Article Writing & Placement
* Writing Capability & Product Brochures, Newsletters, Data Sheets
* Email Campaigns, Web Site Content, Online Newsletters
* Trade Show PR & Marketing Support, Seminar Promotion

Linda Marcus’s Experience

  • Principal

    Astra Communications (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    September 1997 — Present (12 years 2 months)

    I develop marketing programs to meet clients' goals and build lasting brands of integrity. Work includes business-to-business product marketing, trade associations promoting standards and best practices, and consumer marketing. Grounded in the field of ideas and innovation, I began working in the high-tech industry a year before IBM introduced the PC and have developed communications and PR programs for corporations, small businesses, entrepreneurs and nonprofits. Client and corporate experience includes the Messaging Anti-Abuse Working Group (MAAWG) and its global anti-spam efforts, the Internet Streaming Media Alliance (ISMA) and its IPTV open standards, security software at Hughes NetLock, healthcare consumer products, the launch of the industry’s first Web-enabled smart phone for CIDCO, Xerox Corp. printing and computing divisions and Peter Norton Computing, now known as Symantec. I have also worked in the process and environmental monitoring industries.
