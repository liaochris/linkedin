  • Professional Civil Engineer

    Caltrans

    (Government Agency; Civil Engineering industry)

    September 2000 — March 2001 (7 months)

    My work at CalTrans also included representing the State in the Inter-Agency Pre-Pass program, which is a program designed to aid the trucking industry in saving time at weigh stations throughout the US by utilizing transceivers inside cabs of approved trucks. The transceivers emit a signal to the station in advance of the trucks' arrival at the station. If the truck has a history of compliance with weight regulations, the truck is cleared to pass the station without stopping.

    The inter-agency program included representatives from CalTrans, California Highway Patrol, Board of Equalization, Department of Motor Vehicles, Federal Highways Association, and national trucking industry representatives.
