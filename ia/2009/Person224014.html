Steve Hyde’s Summary

Today my business focus is to help people to be the best that they can be.

After many years in various media communication and strategy roles, using these skills with people on a one on one basis is both challenging and rewarding. I run a group of companies specialising in media and publishing recruitment and business management that seek to get close to the personal and business brand spirit and to the opportunity potential.

I began my career in full-service advertising agencies, initially at Foote Cone & Belding in 1981 and then Saatchi & Saatchi in 1984. In 1988 was one of the founder members of the newly launched Zenith Media in positions ranging from Broadcast Director to Head of Radio to Head of Strategic Planning on the Executive Board.
I have worked with Clients from nearly every sector including Newspapers, Hamburgers Planes and Cars and just about every chocolate product ever made! Having bought a significant proportion of Christopher Hamblyn Associates Ltd. in 1999 we founded Hamblyn Selection at that time. In 2007 Steppingstone Associates was set up prior to float on the AIM market in Jan 08 after which the group name was changed to 1700 Group. My role is now Chief Executive of 1700 Group Plc. and Director of the 4 trading companies within the group.

Headhunt/Recruitment, Communications and Media strategy, personnel development, Management Consultancy, team organisation, exit counsel and management, psychometrics, marketing
