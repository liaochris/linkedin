Dr. Tony Alessandra - The Platinum Rule’s Summary

Dr. Tony Alessandra has a street-wise, college-smart perspective on business, having been raised in the housing projects of NYC to eventually realizing success as a graduate professor of marketing, entrepreneur, business author, and hall-of-fame keynote speaker. He earned his PhD in marketing from Georgia State University in 1976.

He is president of AssessmentBusinessCenter.com, a company that offers online 360º assessments and chairman of BrainX.com, a company that created the first Online Learning Mastery System™. He is also a founding partner in The Cyrano Group and Platinum Rule Group--companies which have successfully combined cutting-edge technology and proven psychology to give salespeople the ability to build and maintain positive relationships with hundreds of clients and prospects.

Dr. Alessandra is a prolific author with 18 books translated into 49 foreign language editions, including the newly revised, best selling The NEW Art of Managing People; Charisma; The Platinum Rule; Collaborative Selling; and Communicating at Work. He is featured in over 50 audio/video programs and films, including Relationship Strategies; The Dynamics of Effective Listening; and Non-Manipulative Selling. He is also the originator of the internationally-recognized behavioral style assessment tool - The Platinum Rule®.

Recognized by Meetings & Conventions Magazine as "one of America's most electrifying speakers," Dr. Alessandra was inducted into the Speakers Hall of Fame in 1985.

    AssessmentBusinessCenter.com

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; E-Learning industry)

    July 1996 — Present (13 years 1 month)

    AssessmentBusinessCenter.com builds totally automated, online 360, or multi-rater, assessments. A 360-assessment gathers information from several different groups of people about an employee’s effectiveness - managers might seek feedback from the people that manager reports to, peers and people who report to that manager; salespeople might solicit feedback from the sales manager, fellow salespeople and customers of that salesperson; students might seek feedback from teachers, parents and fellow classmates. Each individual has the unique opportunity to see themselves more objectively through the eyes of others.

    Our online 360-assessments are the most robust and user friendly on the market. Employers who use our assessments recognize that they are powerful resources; and when used as part of a training and development program, they enhance employees’ skills related to communicating effectively. The benefits from using assessments can be profound.
