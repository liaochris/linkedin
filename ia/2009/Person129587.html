Kelly Walters’s Summary

Investment banker to corporate clients commercializing emerging technologies in alternative energy, clean/environmental technology, and advanced materials.

Twelve years corporate finance experience including:
- 9 years in chemicals/materials/cleantech investment banking executing transactions for public and private clients ranging from technology start-ups to Fortune 100 including mergers & acquisitions, equity financings (venture capital, public, private equity), debt financings, credit analysis and advisory, restructurings, and merchant banking.
- 4 years experience at two Fortune 500 companies executing critical projects relative to corporate strategy, business development, and cost reduction initiatives including cross-border partnerships and alliances, outsourcing, and offshoring as well as extensive financial management/reporting experience.

- MA International Affairs and International Finance
- MBA Finance and Financial Insitutions Management
- BA Economics with published applied economic research
- Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) charterholder
- Certified Management Accountant (CMA)
- Certification in Corporate Financial Management (CFM)
- Series 7, 63 securities licenses
- Series 24 supervisory licensure in progress

Kelly Walters’s Specialties:

Interests: advanced technologies for clean energy & power generation, energy storage technologies, bioenergy/biomass, advanced alternative fuels, CTG/CTL/GTL, sustainable chemicals, advanced materials solutions for cleantech/efficiency/environmental/water applications.
