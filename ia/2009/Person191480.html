Joe V Rodriguez’s Summary

As a manufacturers representative, R2E Associates Inc. is committed to providing architects & designers with cutting edge furniture design to realize their vision for virtually any interior space.

Joe Rodriguez brings over fifteen (15) years of experience successfully managing large furniture projects interacting with C level executives.
"Our approach for every commercial project is to focus on the aesthetic, as we refine functionality and streamline cost".

With a proven track record for customer satisfaction, Joe’s hands-on approach includes project management from inception to completion of projects; staff assignments, directing specification, directing Sales & project management teams, estimating, coordinating delivery & installation team's on a national basis; collaborating with interior designers, architects and general contractors to ensure complete customer satisfaction.

Our goal is to achieve excellence in our approach for every project which refines our philosophy...
