Sunny Sharma’s Summary

Most recently been working with Michael Page International as a strong leading Project Manager on a global business process re-engineering and systems integration programme - overhauling 80% of the worldwide systems. From programme mobilisation, analysis, design, development, test and implementation; each phase has had a plethora of challenges to see through a disparate systems architecture to a global standardised suite of applications.

Managing the integration of applications that will bring process and operational efficiencies on an international scale is a challenge, with a strong Business Analysis and Technical Architecture team this has been made possible. Strategic planning for project delivery with demanding programme timescales has been challenging and has required innovative risk mitigation techniques and delivery strategies to achieve this.

A first class honours degree in Computer Science has been extremely beneficial as a key to unlocking opportunities at FTSE 250 companies such as British Telecom where I was a consultant as part of a new Business Venture to bring to market the first Broadcast Mobile TV solution.
