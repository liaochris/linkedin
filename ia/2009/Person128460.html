Kay van de Linde’s Summary

Communications strategist with more than 25 years experience in the United States and Europe. Learned the craft in the trenches from world renowned media maven, David Garth. Worked on the political campaigns of Ed Koch, Rudy Giuliani, Arlen Specter and Mario Cuomo. Shifted to corporate consulting after returning to Europe in 2000. For more information: www.press-strategies.nl.

Kay van de Linde’s Specialties:

- Press strategy
- Crisis communications
- Positioning of people
- Reputation management
- Issue management
- Political campaigns
- Workshops (on all of the above)
