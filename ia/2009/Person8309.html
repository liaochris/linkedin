Alex Keller’s Summary

From music production to game audio to teaching, Alex Keller's career has spanned a wide variety of disciplines. The work that he's done has shown an obsessive attention to quality, detail, creativity and timeliness.

Alex has been an active media professional since 1995. His work has been in the areas of:
-sound design for games, film and theater
-music production
-radio production
-teaching

In addition to having run his own studio, Singular Audio, for four years, Alex has worked for Edge of Reality, Midway Studios, the Connecticut School of Broadcasting, the Art Institutes of Seattle and Austin, and Shoreline Community College. He interned at Experimental Sound Studio in Chicago, and holds a Master's Degree from St. Edward's University in Austin and a Bachelor's degree from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago.

Alex doesn't like talking about himself in the third person but sometimes it's a necessary evil.
