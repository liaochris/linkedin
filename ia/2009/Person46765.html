Cathy Lamothe’s Summary

My whole working career has been devoted to insurance. Primarily, I've had a workers compensation background. Currently, I'm in training and responsible for training on products (policy coverages, proprietary endorsements) to the independent agents doing business with Liberty Northwest as well as for Liberty's internal employees.

I have a "passion for education" and believe it's the key to advancement in the insurance industry. In additon to my college degree (which I earned at the age of 50), I have attained the following designations:

Chartered Property Casualty Underwriter - CPCU
Associate in Underwriting - AU
Associate in Personal Insurance - API
Associate in Information Technology - AIT
Associate in Insurance Services - AIS
Certified Professional Insurance Women - CPIW
Diversified Advanced Education - DAE
Certified Toastmaster - CTM

I am a member of the National Association of Insurance Women (NAIW) and belong to a local chapter, the Insurance Professionals of Portland. The last two designations shown above are NAIW designations.
