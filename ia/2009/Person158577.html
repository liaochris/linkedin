Haiko Meelis’s Summary

Management professional with over 10 years of experience as management consultant in helping businesses to translate their strategy or goals into a concrete plan and help to implement it. So: action and results. Worked mainly in telecoms and internet environments. Industry experience in telecoms, media, entertainment, electronics and banking. Direct experience ranging from start-up companies to corporate environments.

Particularly interested in the definition and implementation of new concepts, products, services and processes that use today's technology to realize previously unthought-of opportunities.

Currently living in Leiden, Netherlands, age 36, married and 1 daughter. Holding a MSc in Computer Science of the University of Leiden, a MSc in Business of the Nyenrode Business University. Currently studying Philosophy at Birkbeck College, University of London. Knowledgeable person, professional attitude, very driven, pragmatic and entrepreneurial.

Accredited associate of the Institute for Independent Business (IIB), which gives access to an international network of over 5000 business executives in every industry imaginable, who can provide me with their specific expertise when needed.
