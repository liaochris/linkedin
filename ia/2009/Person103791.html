Janet Lane’s Summary

Multi-published freelance non-fiction writer and multi-published fiction novelist with solid, award-winning experience writing advertising and promotional copy and full multi-media campaigns.

My projects have included promo campaigns for television, radio, newspaper, outdoor, brochure, specialty advertising and targeted website content writing

I'm ready to help with your part-time or temporary writing and editing needs in any of the areas listed above.

    Janet Lane, Author

    November 1997 — Present (11 years 6 months)

    Multi-published author at Five Star Publishing, Inc. (Thorndike) and with RMFW Press. Titles include my fifteenth century historical novels set in England (Tabor's Trinket and Emerald Silk) and a contemporary short story in the 2007 anthology, Tales from Mistwillow.
