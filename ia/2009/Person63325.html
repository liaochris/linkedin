Diane Southwick’s Summary

Over 20 years direct experience in Supply Chain and Logistics, including working within Export and Import functions. Set up and ran a highly successful recruitment business specialising in placing senior and professional people within a diverse range of client companies. Has advised clients on specific staff requirements relating to Import and Customs functions and has worked on assignments in Paris, Moscow, Hong Kong, Japan and the UAE. These have included Board Directors, senior Sales and Marketing personnel, Export Sales, Finance Directors, Export Compliance Specialists, Global Tax Specialists, Senior Procurement etc.

Working through a wide area network of contacts and a search approach, I have a very wide and thorough understanding of the market place.
Degree in Business Studies with International Transport, CMILT, MIEx and MInstFF (Graduate), I am interested in hearing from clients who have a need to recruit senior professionals within my areas of expertise and candidates who would like me to represent them in their search for a new opportunity. Through my company PGC Ltd., I can also provide a full range of HR and staff assessment services.

PGC can provide highly experience executives on a permanent, interim, project or consultancy basis.

Diane Southwick’s Specialties:

Senior Manager and board level recruitment and HR issues relating in particular to Supply Chain and Logistics, Freight Forwarding, Finance, Manufacturing, pharmaceuticals and customs compliance.
