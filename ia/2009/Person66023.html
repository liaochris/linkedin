Don Vickers’s Summary

Do you want to increase your business and personal cash flow?

I find and unlock cash in business and individual assets that are not apparent. This creates liquid resources to enable owners to meet their objectives and sometimes change their strategies as they see new perspectives.

Thus, we offer the following:

CASH FLOW CONSULTING SERVICES FOR BUSINESSES AND INDIVIDUALS

Increase your income.

Review existing sales model and utilization of customer lists.

Marketing techniques to increase sales income and cash flow using new business models.

Lifetime analyses of cash flow of potential customers using the Strategy of Preeminence.

Reduce your expenses.

Strategically evaluate vendors and suppliers.

Rent or lease versus buying: real estate, vehicles and equipment (business or personal).

Protect your investment and cash accounts from loss by using proprietary fiduciary guidelines.

Pension protection in bear markets.

Avoiding the number one cause of business failure by proper cash flow management.

Debt reduction and management.

Tax strategies to optimize your cash flow.

Customized consulting on issues we become aware of during the engagement.
