Randy Vogenberg’s Summary

Dr. Randy Vogenberg is a nationally known expert on managed care, the health care services marketplace, its evolution and future movement towards value based strategies. His broad managed care expertise includes health care financing, training and benefit management.

He is a Senior Fellow at the Jefferson School of Population Health in addition to Adjunct Faculty at the University of Rhode Island College of Pharmacy.

He is a highly requested Trainer and an Author of programs and books on managed health care, pharmacy benefit management, and marketing channel strategy in the U.S.

Randy Vogenberg’s Specialties:

Managed Care, Managed Care Training, Health care benefits, insurance, finance; hospital economics and finace; health care and pharmaceutical economics; health policy; manufacturers of pharmaceuticals, biologics, diagnostics, devices, and medical equipment; and strategic business perspectives on the future of health care.
