    Connect Through Art

    (Mental Health Care industry)

    July 2006 — Present (3 years 1 month)

    CONNECT THROUGH ART

    I have researched, developed and marketed an innovative therapeutic programme fusing art therapy practice and
    community arts, to be delivered as a `package' for organisations working with people who have complex emotional,
    psychological and behavioural problems. I formed a working partnership with Full Potential Arts, an established
    Birmingham-based arts organisation with over ten year's experience of delivering arts activities in mental health
    environments. I gained the involvement of the Early Intervention Service within Birmingham and Solihull Mental Health
    Trust to deliver the test-pilot, and the organisation will support the programme by providing the service user group, staff,
    venue, some materials and monitoring / evaluation. The project has gained funding from the Heart of Birmingham
    initiative Health Exchange through The Digbeth Trust to secure the role of an art therapist during the project. A bid for the
    rest of the finance required to deliver the test-pilot is currently being considered by Arts Council England. Once funding is
    secured, the three month test-pilot will be delivered 2008 / 2009.
