Melinda Sensabaugh’s Summary

I have 12 years combined experience as a Telecommunication Manager and Technician, relative to design and maintenance. With extensive expertise, training, and certification on Nortel products and systems. Responsibilities included managing the infrastructure for wired and wireless communications systems, including new installs, upgrades, and maintenance, MAC, renovations and new construction. Such as successfully leading the team which designed and developed the WAN, servicing 26 buildings for the City of Dearborn, via integrated, CAT5E, Fiber, and Wireless solutions. Most recently I managed a budget fluctuating between 450K- $850K and 4 team members supporting the voice and data cable infrastructure, individual extensions, trunks, pay phones, automatic call distribution, voice mail, messaging, call accounting and emergency services for 63 Divisions.
