Gill Mills’s Summary

Retail/fmcg specialist, having worked internationally with several of the world's largest retailers, including Carrefour, Coles/Myer and the Burton Group.

For past 10 years, have worked in the recruitment field, handling assignments for international brands at senior management level. Most recent focus has been on luxury brands and new media (ecommerce and multi-channel retailing).

Now specialise in overseas appointments, across the board, including major projects within civil engineering and energy.
