Andy Gelernter’s Summary

I am an UltiPro consultant interested in helping companies support their UltiPro Product Suite. With over 6 years of UltiPro experience and more than 20 years of experience in a variety of technology fields and a proven record of accomplishments, I believe I am qualified to help your company grow and continue to be successful.

I have worn a variety of different hats in working with UltiPro including:

• Development of project data gathering tools, client training materials, and internal tracking procedures
• Been Project Manager for a significant number of UltiPro implementations
• Worked as the System Consultant setting up business rules for clients
• Worked as a Technical Consultant importing and manipulating data with SQL and other tools
• Developed and delivered UltiPro training to client’s end-users
• Significant experience with importing data into UltiPro, data mapping, data cleansing, SQL queries, moving data between source and destination SQL tables, and exporting data out of UltiPro.

My background enables me to help companies with UltiPro on both the front end with your business rules and behind the scenes with their data including the following areas:

• Business Rules Setup
• Procedure Documentation / Creation
• Implementation
• Imports / Exports
• Benefit Provider Interfaces
• COGNOS &/or SQL Report Development
• Job Aides
• Historical Data Imports
• End-User Training
• ACCESS databases
• UltiPro Web & Site Administration
• Creation of Stored Procedures, Triggers, and Time-Based Events
• Data transfers between UltiPro and other HR applications
