Mariela Tinoco (www.career4change.com)’s Summary

Mariela Tinoco-Aramburu is an Appreciative Career Coach and HR Freelance Consultant with extensive international experience, whose goal is to facilitate her clients to reach their dreams, by switching their minds from the negatives and the biases to the possibilities of discovering and designing their desired path towards their potential future.
Her current work facilitates the connection between professional immigrants and the Global Corporate Culture by an appreciative cross-cultural-inclusiveness process.
She is also a HR Freelance Consultant with a vast experience in Recruitment, Personal Development, Cross Cultural Diversity and Inclusion workshops , Team facilitation and Focus Groups under the Appreciative Inquiry methodology, Virtual Team Building and Salary Administration.
