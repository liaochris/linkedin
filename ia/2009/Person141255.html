Linda Ireland’s Summary

I'm co-owner and partner of Aveus LLC, a global strategy and operational change firm that helps leaders find money in the business performance chain while improving customer experiences.

As author of Domino: How to Use Customer Experience to Tip Everything in Your Business toward Better Financial Performance, I built on work done at Aveus and aimed to deliver real-life, actionable, how-to help for leaders of any organization.

My passion for emerging growth and turnaround situations shows up in my experience in a wide variety of industries – automotive, multi-channel retail, technology and health services, and in roles that encompass operations, marketing, corporate development, and governance. Prior to Aveus I was CEO of FORWARD I, a strategy and marketing consulting firm. I led strategic change through IPO, new economy, and down economy shifts while in executive roles at Wilsons Leather, Genesis Direct, Digital River and Deluxe Corporation.

I have served on the boards of organizations in health services, the arts and leadership development. I am an active angel investor in woman-owned businesses.

In 2009, I created Customer Experience for Profit (www.ceforprofit.com) with Aveus. The site aims to dispel the myth that customer experience is a tradeoff to profitability, and to connect a community of leaders putting this lesson to work.

I have two Bachelor of Arts degrees from the University of Minnesota, and an MBA from the University of St. Thomas in St. Paul - where I teach in the graduate school.

Away from it all, I am a global wanderer. 30 countries and six continents and counting - I am energized by the deeper perspective that comes with a change of people and place.

Linda Ireland’s Specialties:

Business, brand and product strategy. Leading change. Business evaluation. Market assessment. Operationalizing change. Customer experience: expert at using customer experience as an operating strategy to get tangible performance payoff.
