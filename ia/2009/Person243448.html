Vince Hee’s Summary

After working as a craftsman on literally thousands of rooftops over the course of 13 years as a roofing mechanic, thousands of rooftops I have directly managed as a project manager(8 more years) for a quality roofing contractor and the thousands i have managed through others as their sales manager(5 more years), I feel I have been training up to this point to serve my next customers needs. I seek to offer my services and those of the craftsman who have put their trust in me, as the best value for a growing referral network, based on met and exceeded commitments, throughout the Philadelphia and sorrounding areas..

Vince Hee’s Specialties:

All types of residential roofing maintenance and replacement, siding, ventilation, windows, gutters, skylights. Clear communication of commitments and follow through on those commitments.
