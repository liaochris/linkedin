Petre Marcu’s Summary

After a seaman activity of 15 years, I decided to continue my career in shipbuilding industry, working as paint inspector at the beginning. I gained experience during 15 years of activity and now I am in charged with all the activities regarding shipbuilding, Hull, Machinery, Piping, Outfitting, Painting and Plan approval.

During this time, i worked in several countries like China in Zhejiang Shipyard; Yichang Shipyard; Hudong Shipyard - Shanghai; Chengxi Shipyard.

The Netherlands in Harlingen - Volharding Shipyard; Hoogezand - Volharding Shipyard; Royal Niestern Sander Shipyard; Delfzijl - Royal Niestern Sander Shipyard.

In Korea: Daewoo Shipbuilding & Marine Engineering- Okpo Shipyard,
South – Korea.

In Romania: Daewoo Mangalia Heavy Industries, Braila shipyard, Tulcea Shipyard
Constantza Shipyard.

I am constantly looking shipowners who are in search of Newbuilding Supervision services, that i can provide with.
