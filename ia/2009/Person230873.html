Terry Maxwell’s Summary

Bilingual professional, college educated business manager/analyst with extensive experience from the factory floor to senior management in manufacturing, retail and wholesale distribution and in the automotive industry. In the course of my career, have put to good use my skills in budgeting, forecasting, P&L preparation and detailed cost analysis; continuous improvement and lean manufacturing; production scheduling and strategic planning; customer relations and public speaking; employee leadership, motivation and coaching, helping several diverse companies attain their goals of increasing profitability, decreasing costs and improving employee morale and team spirit. A decisive and persevering manager, with great integrity and communication skills, adapts easily to different environments.
