Albert Brown’s Summary

My backgrounds are in education and computer science. In education, I am a California licensed adult teacher, with over ten years of total teaching experience. I have experience teaching adults in classroom environments, teaching computer subjects in the corporate environment and serving overseas as a Chief Technical Advisor for the United Nations, where I led a team of American professionals in a transfer of technology project, to develop regional computer centers.

In the field of computer science, I have over 15 years experience in all phases of computer science. I learned computers from operations, programming, artificial intelligence, systems analysis, to database management. I have held positions as project manager, product manager, lead engineer, programming manager.I have demonstrated that I understand the latest technology and how to apply this technology in practice.

I would like a career position that allows me to combine all of my background. One position would be as a school adminisrator for an American International School. Another possible career position would be as a training manager for a multi-national company. I would like to travel and work overseas. I have over nine years experience working and living overseas. I would not mind working for an international organizaton, since I have previously worked for the United Nations.

I do not believe in retirement (unless one is unable to work), and I beliee in lifelong learning and teaching.
