Stein Rogan + Partners’s Summary

The Agency's core deliverables are brand/business strategy, outstanding creativity and innovation delivered across an integrated, multi-channel marketing communications mix – underscored by a focus on achieving the metrics that move our clients’ businesses.

2009 Brand and Demand Study:

Stein Rogan + Partners, in conjunction with Forbes.com and Ziff Davis Enterprise, conducted a survey among advertisers and business-to-business and consumer marketers in an effort to categorize and codify the direction marketing organizations are taking to balance investments in brand-centric and demand-centric initiatives.

The study uncovers the view of the interrelationship of brand-centric and demand-centric marketing among marketers and advertisers during times of economic instability. Furthermore, the study offers insight into upcoming tactical trends within the space.

The 2009 Brand/Demand Study (B/D09) is the first such study of marketers’ and agencies’ views of the interrelationship of brand-centric and demand-centric marketing.
