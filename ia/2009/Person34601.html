Jeff Brown’s Summary

Celebrating 21 years in broadcasting this year, 20 of which have been spent in Christian radio, with a four year detour in the Christian music business between 1996 and 2000. The last eight have been spent at 88.7 WAY-FM in Nashville, Tennessee, including 6 years on the morning show and over three years now as Operations Director.

In July 2008 I stepped down from the morning show in order to concentrate on my duties as OD.

I now am heavily involved in engaging listeners on an even more intimate level utilizing various social media platforms (blogs, social networking, twitter, streaming video, etc.).

Jeff Brown’s Specialties:

Branding, viral marketing, persuasive communication, voice overs, production, talent coaching, consulting.
