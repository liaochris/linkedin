Pete Madigan’s Summary

During my time as an Account Manager with digital agency Bloom Media, I developed a keen interest in websites and online media. The next 2 years of my career have been spent working in IT recruitment at Elan IT, which is owned by Manpower, where I specialised in placing senior candidates. Working on a daily basis with highly skilled, senior IT professionals helped improve my knowledge of web techniques and innovative, online advertising strategies which has really aided me in creating my own website.

Having always had a passion for music, videos and short movies, the concept of UGroove.tv was born. The music, video-sharing site with full social networking capabilities is currently being redeveloped and will be relaunched in early 2009.

I am now working for Vapour, who are a collective of bright, young things, based in Leeds. We provide our clients with services ranging from Brand Design and Development through to Digital Design, PR and Marketing and pretty much everything else that falls in between.

If you are considering a new image/feel for your site, company or brand, drop me a line and we'll see how we can be of assistance. Below are a selection of pdf's containing examples of our some of our recent works.
