Claire Giordano’s Summary

I am a product management and product marketing leader, both analytical and creative, with 19 years of hi-tech experience in marketing and engineering. I excel at roles that require me to operate at the intersection between product management, user experience, engineering, and marketing. I set high standards for the usability of products, and fit well with teams who share those values.

I bring an intense customer focus to my work, as well as energy and enthusiasm. I deliver results. I thrive when managing high caliber teams, and have experience managing effectively across geographies and time zones. I excel in communications: evangelizing, presenting, and writing; with presentations, I believe in the ‘presentation zen’ approach rather than powerpoint bullets. Finally, I’m effective at partnering with people across the company, and outside the company.
