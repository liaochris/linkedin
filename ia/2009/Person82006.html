Frédéric Vandendris’s Summary

Active in Online Marketing since 1997, I have driven during 10 years the end-to-end creation and growth of what is today a local search engine to a top-10 usage and a steady top-3 revenue position on its market (>60 M€).
By relying on creative pragmatism and smart levering of the company's and brand's assets we have always been able to manage a well-performing P&L while continuously developing. In doing so we have brought the Belgian version of the Yellow Pages on-line concept to one of the strongest of his international peers on many KPI's.
My personal career has also lead me to very active involvement in strategically and operationally driving other domains such as DBM and especially sales approach and segmentation.
Today, my heart still clearly lies in the online sphere in which I keep finding the same excitement in looking at new initiatives coming from all sides, interacting with the social web, and discovering the rise of new technologies and the user and business opportunities they create.
