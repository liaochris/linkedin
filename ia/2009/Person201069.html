Robert Stribling’s Summary

Robert is a lifelong entrepreneur with a passion for invention. Taking ideas from concept to reality and a viable business model is Robert's strong suit.

One current project is based around a "No-Spill' straw for the aseptic beverage industry, Juice Boxes & Pouches. This is a huge undertaking even though it may seem rather simple to the average consumer. The project is global in reach and requires a wide range of tasks to include; Functional Design and Testing, Intellectual Property Coverage around the world, material selection and testing, consumer research, traditional and digital marketing, etc.

Team building is vital to the success of this project and Robert has amassed the "best in class" to participate and contribute. As project manager and CEO of The Last Straw LLC, Robert is involved in every aspect listed above.

Robert is also the President & CEO of Stribling Systems, Inc. a unique waste/recycling business based around one of his earlier inventions.
