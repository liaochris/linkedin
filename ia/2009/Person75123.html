Erik Eisel’s Summary

Erik Eisel grew up in Huntington Beach, California (45 minutes south of Los Angeles), travelled the world, attended graduate school and returned eight years ago to work in the robust Orange County software industry.

As a software salesman since 1999, he has worked closely with software developers such as Intuit, Microsoft and SAP. More recently, he has delivered software solutions to top tier hardware developers in the storage industry, including Linksys, HP, Toshiba and Freecom Europe.

Today, as a director of business development, Erik manages the Irvine office for a global provider of International Communications Services to legal, financial and high-tech companies. Erik's customers serve a diverse U.S. audience or hope to expand their international reach.

Having received his Ph.D. in Comparative Literature from UCLA in 1997, he offers a unique perspective on the entire sales process, having developed research, writing, public speaking skills and foreign language skills at the graduate level before choosing the sales profession.

Erik is well-rounded, possessing international experience and an engaging communication style.
