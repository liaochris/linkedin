Kevin Hunter’s Summary

Extensive experience in the analysis, design and development of 2-tier and 3-tier client-server architecture systems. Extremely strong in Java technologies.

Mobile development experience in the iPhone, J2ME, Symbian, Windows Mobile and Palm environments, including tie-in of mobile systems into Internet-based infrastructure systems.

Extensive experience in barcoding, both in the development of systems that use barcoding, and in software that encodes and decodes barcodes.

Strong working knowledge of the patent system, and in the evaluation of competitive patents. Holder or co-holder of over 45 US and International patents.

Kevin Hunter’s Specialties:

Software development: Windows (C++), Java (J2ME, J2SE and J2EE environments), Symbian (Nokia Series 60 and UIQ), Windows Mobile, Palm, iPhone
