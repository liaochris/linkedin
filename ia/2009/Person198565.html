Rick Stephens’s Summary

I am very process driven and believe that clearly defined, documented manufacturing business processes, are the cornerstone of a company’s success. My management style utilizes straightforward communication, organization, attention to detail and a controlled sense of urgency. I strongly embrace continuous improvement and teamwork. My experience in manufacturing operations management has given me excellent communication and time management skills. Proficiency in spreadsheets, project analysis, and presentations are second nature. With over 35 years of working in the manufacturing environment, I have experience in numerous aspects of the production business. I have had a very diverse and successful career and have always pursued new opportunities and challenges. This is evidenced by promotions with increasing responsibility over the years.
