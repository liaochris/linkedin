Jim Deerman’s Summary

Extensive experience in network architecture, design and implementation. Including experience in network/system security, VoIP network design and product architecture. I'm available for direct employment or consulting in the areas of design, implementation and operations for network/system security, enterprise data networks, and VoIP networks. I'm also available for system architecture evaluation and design.

Experience includes:
- Extensive experience with start-up companies in the role of system architect.
- Network security related network design and implementation.
- Product definition and design for VoIP networks and VoIP related products.
- Enterprise and large scale network design, including ISP and CLEC networks.

Jim Deerman’s Specialties:

I have been working in network design and architecture for over 20 years. I have extensive experience in the areas of data network technologies, security (both host based and network based), Ethernet switching, routing, and Voice over IP. I have done, not only network design and architecture, but also network product architecture, system architecture and system software design and development. I have also work in Product Management as the technical liaison between Marketing and Development.
