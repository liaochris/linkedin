Eric Wilson’s Summary

Eric Wilson has more than 17 years of experience in engineering, engineering management and leadership. Eric has spent the last 12 years involved with some of the industry’s most successful and fast moving startups. Most recently, Eric was CTO of GoTV Networks, the first made-for-mobile video company. He was responsible for all technology and product distribution efforts. Eric was also a member of the executive team that provided corporate strategy and fund raising activities.
Previously, Eric was a founding member and VP of Infrastructure at JAMDAT Mobile (NASDAQ: ERTS), now EA Mobile, where he was responsible for the development of the company’s gaming network services, data mining and SMS capabilities. While at JAMDAT, he co-authored patent-pending mobile data technologies and was a contributing member of the expert panel for JSR-185 (Java Technology for the Wireless Industry; JTWI). Prior to JAMDAT, Eric co-founded and led the engineering efforts for an Idealab e-commerce venture that was purchased for $45M. Eric was a founding engineer at Citysearch (NASDAQ: IACI), currently a division of IAC/Interactive Corp, and was responsible for the architecture and development of all the corporate and distributed sales force automation systems. He has architected cutting edge imaging solutions for Union Bank of California (NYSE: UB) and built real time avionic systems at Computer Sciences Corporation (NYSE: CSC). Eric has a deep understanding of how to create successful engineering organizations and how to turn ideas into products.
