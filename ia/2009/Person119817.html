Joost Wouters’s Summary

Personal and Professional Overview
---------------------------------------------------
Joost Wouters' drive is to help individuals and organizations realizing their passions and dreams.

He started his professional career at Procter & Gamble in Sales & Marketing where he mastered the skills of sales. Five years later he joined the Executive Team of Pepsi Cola where he was responsible for the Sales of PepsiCo beverages in the Benelux.

When his wife Frieda became pregnant with Ruben, their first son, Joost decided to quit his job and start his own firm in Organizational Development. Since the very first days of his company, he has worked with clients such as Unilever, Heineken, Royal Dutch Telecom, LG.Philips, Dutch Railway, Delta Lloyd, Duyvis, Allied Domecq, Campina, Uniekaas, The Colomer Group, Vodafone, Sara Lee, Yess, P&G, Ohra and Nestlé across Europe, and in South Africa, Russia, USA and South America.

From mid 2005 Joost and his family traveled throughout the USA for a year in a motor home. Since their return they moved to Spain, where they’ve built a house on the Mediterranean.

The most important reason why CEOs and team leaders want to work with Joost is that he’s a living example of the programs he facilitates. He makes things happen. If they see what’s possible on a ‘personal’ level, they get very excited about the idea what he can bring for their organizations. Or, as one client stated: "he is Mr Results".

You can contact Joost directly at joost.wouters@actimpact.com.
