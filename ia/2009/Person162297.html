Michael Todd Smith’s Summary

Michael Todd Smith is an American fashion designer and entrepreneur. An experienced apparel industry executive and designer with over 18 years in the luxury and fashion industry, he is CEO & Creative Director of Michael Todd Holdings. Known for his Italian take on American style, his collections have garnered an international following for their strong emphasis on artisan couture quality and attention to detail.

Previously Mr. Smith was Co-Designer and CEO of Atelier Tracy & Michael, where he was responsible for, within three years, taking the company from inception to being featured in multiple departments in 35 Neiman Marcus stores, 24 Saks Fifth Avenue stores, Nordstrom, Holt Renfrew, Wilkes Bashford and 175 top tier specialty stores in North America, the UK and Japan.
From his initial collection being listed in Women's Wear Daily as one of the top selling dress lines for Neiman Marcus, his collections have been regularly featured in national and international press including WWD, Forbes, Vogue, Elle, InStyle, Glamour, MarieClaire and Lucky, among others and can regularly be seen on award winning television shows such as Desperate Housewives, in film and on the red carpet.

Prior to Tracy & Michael, Mr. Smith was Managing Director of John Ludlam Fine Stationer, one of the most respected stationers in North America, where he worked with heads of state, national and international socialites and heads of Fortune 500 companies.
