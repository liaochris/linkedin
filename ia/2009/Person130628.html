  • Founder

    Askja Energy Holdings

    (Renewables & Environment industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year)

    Askja Energy Holdings has its roots as private law firm, established in Iceland decade ago. Originally the firm specialized in Law on Utilization of Natural Resources and International Environmental Law - including the Legal Framework of Climate Change, the Kyoto Protocol, and EU Law on Carbon Trading.

    With increased focus on consultancy and investments in the energy sector, the company name became Askja Energy Holdings in early 2008. The main investment areas of Askja Energy Holdings are commodities and the renewable energy industry. The company also offers consultancy services regarding the European Energy Industry.

    -------------------------------------------
    Askja is a volcanic area in NW-Iceland that Neil Armstrong and his crew of Apollo 11 visited to experience a lunar-like landscape, before their successful mission to the Moon. Askja is also an Icelandic word over a box where one keeps valuables.
