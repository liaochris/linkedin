Peter Porteous’s Interests:

My passions are the environment and outdoor recreation. Convergence occurs through my involvement in The Meduxnekeag River Association and our creation of the Meduxnekeag Valley Nature Preserve. Over the past 11 years our volunteer based group has protected 360+ acres of some of the richest remaining Appalachin Hardwood Forest in the Province of NB. Additionally we have developed over 10km of walking trails, bring 1000 students per year to the Preserve for an outdoor classroom session and have pioneered an annual Environmental Showcase program. Recently grad students have found on the preserve several plant species previously thought to be extinct in New Brunswick and 13 species of insects never previously documented on the planet. In conjunctions with 3 other partners we are in the process of constructing a "green" commercial mall in Woodstock, NB. We are striving for LEED Gold certification and believe this to be the first project of its kind in Atlantic Canada.
