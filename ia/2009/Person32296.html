Brandy Posey’s Summary

Brandy Posey is currently acquiring her Bachelor of Science degree in Graphic Design at the Art Institute of Pittsburgh Online Division. She is in her fourth academic year and set to graduate spring of 2010. Brandy’s appreciation and passion for design is demonstrated in how she thoroughly approaches each project. She is highly creative and multitalented. Passionate for print design and developing multiple creative options for any project, as well as accustomed to multi-tasking assignments from her educational experience. Brandy has the ability to effectively integrate photographs, illustrations, display, and type using page composition software.

Brandy also has experience with local businesses in designing logos, websites, and brochures for their advertising needs. She has also dabbled in photography and illustration and enjoys learning different ways to communicate her creativity through many mediums.

Brandy Posey currently resides in Columbus, Georgia.

Brandy Posey’s Specialties:

Creative experiences in print designs, digital manipulation, photograph restoration, visualizing initial ideas, typography, packaging design, and layout styles in web design. Knowledge of the design cycle, specifically, problem identification, analysis, brainstorming, and idea refinement.
