Josh Wimmer’s Summary

I'm a freelance writer with a lot of experience covering retail business (particularly jewelry stores) and restaurants, although I can and will write about nearly anything. Presently I'm interested in doing more marketing and promotional work for small and large businesses. I've written some pretty funny humor pieces, too.

And I'm a longtime copy editor who's spent many years hunting down typos for newspapers, magazines, and advertising and marketing firms.
