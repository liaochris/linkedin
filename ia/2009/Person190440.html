John Simos’s Summary

Promopays is a Canadian leader in the promotional products industry. Specializing in the Imprinting, Visual Media,
Creative Thinking, Branding, Market Research, and Advertising Services of Archetypes in Advertising and Marketing.

Promopays' Ability: Is simple, a definition that creativity is the ability to imagine or invent something new. Promopays' creativity is not the ability to create out of nothing, it has the ability to generate new ideas by, changing, or reapplying existing ideas. Promopays was founded on these thoughts and beliefs: "Curiosity".

Promopays wants to create, it wants to know things,--all kinds of things-- just so we can know them. Our knowledge of things does not need a reason nor require any reason. Most people question us on this, "Asking why we are always trying a different approach, or why do you need to know that?" Which is strange to us, the creative thinker, our response is simple, "Because we want to know the answer." Our light of Knowledge is enjoyable and is the reason why we are who we are today.

We are a useful tool in our strange ways and unexpected ways. "We are a Creative Thinking Promotional hands-on company".
