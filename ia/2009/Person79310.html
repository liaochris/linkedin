Sherlyn Fox, AICI FLC’s Summary

Owner and President
Fox Image & Makeup Intl. &
Foxy Faces Makeup Collection &
The Stylish Male

With many certifications, Sherlyn Fox, AICI FLC President of F.I.M. is an expert in Makeup, Image Consulting, and Color. Her original training was conducted in L.A., NYC, and Nashville, TN.

Some of her training includes many Image and Makeup seminars throughout the country. She also attended Nashville’s Master’s Barber School and received a certificate as a Barber Tech.

Sherlyn has been trained and mentored by the leaders in the industry such as Image Master, Dr. Joyce Knudsen, PhD., AICI CIM and Bernice Kentner, the founder the Educational Color Corp.in addition has been trained in color theory for Munsell and Pantone. She also graduated Cum Laude from Bethel College in McKenzie, TN with a Bachelors Degree in Business Management.

She has been featured in local magazines such as Nashville Lifestyles Magazine and the Tennessean and currently the Cumberland Scene. She is also one of Manchester’s Who’s Who of American Professionals and a FLC certified member of AICI. She has also judged national beauty pageants and local cable talent competitions and has been featured on Sky Radio.

Sherlyn has worked with make-up and hair for many years. She has worked with celebrities, several musicians, multiple CMA events, campaigns that can be seen in local establishments etc. She has also worked closely with Red Ridge Entertainment and American Television Distribution as well.

Sherlyn is also a Media Makeup & Airbrush Artist and Color Analysis Trainer and Mentor as well as the primary regional Airbrush Makeup Trainer to other industry professionals. She also has her own Private Label Makeup Collection "Foxy Faces Makeup Collection".

You can read Sherlyn's "Style Scene" Column in the Cumberland Scene Magazine beginning with the April 2009 issue. The magazine is distributed throughout the Southeast KY region and can also be seen at www.cumberlandscene.com
