Lisaann Dupont’s Summary

Lisaann Dupont is a marketing dynamo with more than 16 years of diverse experience. She spent 8 years in the music industry with luminaries like RCA Records and BMG Music and 6 years in film with big names such as Klasky Csupo, Paramount Pictures, and Sony Pictures. She joined Philips Electronics in 2004, where she worked in consumer marketing and then brand programs/corporate communications/corporate sponsorships. When she left Philips in December 2007, she made the leap to full-time consulting and relocated from New York City to a 6 acre mini-farm west of Nashville, TN.

As a proficient public relations specialist, Dupont has consulted with myriad entertainment industry entrepreneurs, securing high-profile media placements from a cover story in the Sunday Business section of The Tennessean to features in Nashville Lifestyles, Nashville Business Journal and INC. Additionally, she has obtained speaking engagements for clients at The Clive Davis School of Recorded Music at NYU and the CMJ Music Marathon conferences.

As for events, she has managed projects including:

The Sundance Film Festival
Live Earth
Philips Simplicity Event
Stop Global Warming Tour with Sheryl Crow
The W Hotel Hamptons Summer House
New York City Marathon
Kips Bay Showhouse
The Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas
2007 World Cup
Tim McGraw/Kingsford Charcoal
John Mayer/BlackBerry which is nominated for a 2008 Billboard Touring Award.

Dupont is also the manager of the Music Industry Forum group on LinkedIn and the author of “Marketing Beyond MySpace: A Practical Guide On How To Enhance and Manage Your Internet Presence” an ebook geared towards musicians [available soon!]
