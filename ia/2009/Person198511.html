Rick Erling’s Summary

I am the President and CEO of a firm that specializes in business performance improvement by integrating sales process, marketing methodology, corporate strategies and financial management into one outbound revenue capture program.

Much like business forensic analysts, we first look at your entire customer facing communication and assess the value your customers see.

Next, we take a step inside the four walls of your company and compare your customer facing value, to the value you think you have, and then measure the "Value Variance" gap between the two.

Once we've completed our analysis and identified the "Value Variance," we write strategic and tactical recommendations on your corporate strategy and marketing programs and work in tandem with you to close those gaps.

Then, using a custom fit program, we train your sales team to sell to management more effectively using techniques that are linked to our recommendations.

I am also the publisher of The CxO Group e-Newsletter, a national best practices thought leader publication focused on increased revenue.

Before joining The CxO Group, I spent over 20 years in senior corporate leadership positions. In the 1990's, my professional reputation was first validated by becoming nationally recognized as a manufacturing evangelist at Textron and Intergraph Corporation. Following into the 2000's, I was a increasing revenue at Siemens PLM (UGS) , and HP Enterprise Services (EDS). More recently, at Cincom Systems and at Tecnomatix I reported to the President of North America Operations, and had responsibility for setting the strategy, and driving execution in establishing new selling, and distribution channels.

Collectively, these experiences have created a full circle of experience in sales, marketing, business strategy and operations that provide a shop-floor to top-floor balanced experienced exposure to revenue capture strategy and business perspectives.
