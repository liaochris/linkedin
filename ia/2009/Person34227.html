Brig Lamoreaux’s Summary

Throughout his career, Brig has been identified by coworkers and management as an extremely talented software developer and technologist. One who possesses the core traits needing to be a successful software developer. Brig has stepped into leadership roles of designing and developing software for many different industries and company sizes.

He is currently a Senior Software Engineer for the Apollo Group and leads a team designing community based applications that are used by hundreds of thousands of users.

Brig holds a bachelor of science degree in information technology and is currently pursuing a MBA. He supplements his education with opencoursware from MIT and Stanford.

When he is not programming or reading something new, you can find him on the slopes following his true passion of skiing.
