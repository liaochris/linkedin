David R. Crais’s Summary

Interdisciplinary manager and advisor with national and international experience with medical technology startups, corporate management, digital media, healthcare administration and consulting, business development, and capital markets and investment advisory work. Top performing leader, sometime 'agent of change', with passion for leading an enterprise or campaign to achieve aspirational goals with integrity, intellect, and drive.
Described often as a "panoramic thinker", Crais has a recognized ability to problem solve and deduce the simple from the complex. This has been recognized by his appointments while still in his 20's to senior public sector economic development and trade finance positions, promotions up through the ranks in sales and general management with leading companies like Quest Diagnostics, Roche Diagnostics, Careside, and Merrill Lynch, along with his role in several startups and early growth companies in management and consulting, and with his creation of entrepreneurial ventures with others in medical, digital media and arts, and real estate industries. He also held a position as an asset manager under contract with Resolution Trust Corporation managing distressed assets from failed S&L's. Experience that gives him insight into today's capital and property markets.
Licensed in securities and capital markets, insurance, real estate, mortgage finance, and contractor management; and certified in project management, healthcare administration, marketing technology, business process management (BPM), and negotiation skillls: he is well versed in the training and professional development necessary to drive change, face complex and challenging issues, and know how to get things done - and do it. Not just in theory, but in practice.
