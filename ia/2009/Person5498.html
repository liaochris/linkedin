My Life-Drive

Both in my professional career as in personal life, I have always strived to search out the limit of my abilities, break boundaries and show myself and the world that with a right attitude and a pure intention anything can be achieved.

My Passions
I feel very strongly about physical and mental health and the role nutrition plays in it. SPORTS! Aside from regular fitness workouts, I play tennis, football, and basketball; and have started paragliding and kite-surfing. BOOKS on physics, philosophy, and personal development are another passion. I am also a big MOVIE buff. Finally, I believe that our greatest joy is always with other people and try to therefore create true, honest, and deep RELATIONSHIPS.

My GOALS
About a year ago I have made a switch to offering my services as an independent contractor. Recently I have also focused on becoming a Motivational Trainer. My vision:
1. Offer professional analysis and consulting services independently.
2. Create a motivational training and coaching business teaching skills for success.
3. Shift my life more towards Slovenia at certain point. Already having a broad professional network in Slovenia, create a business there, based around health-consciousness and skills for success in life, with a not-for-profit section that will focus on teaching these skills to children and young adults.
4. Eventually also study philosophy and physics, and of course keep challenging myself in forms of regular and extreme sports.

GIVE ME A CALL IF I CAN HELP YOU WITH THE BELOW!
All my professional experience has been in dynamic change-environments. From quick and flexible MS Access/Excel reporting solutions to complex business analysis and process-redesign projects in large organizations, I have the ability to work under time pressure, put things into action and create results fast.
