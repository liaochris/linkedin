Frank McCann’s Summary

Engineer specializing in network design and construction. I work on our corporate microwave network, stretching from New York to Texas. I also lead our Disaster Recovery team. I have designed and built several Cell On Light Trucks (COLTs) for microwave communications. Combined with other aspects of our Emergency Preparedness / Disaster Recovery efforts we can restore communications to anywhere within a 100 mile radius of a Mobile Switching Center. I have also had the responsibility of overseeing Telco POP (Point Of Presence) rooms in several national network buildings.
