Gary Deines’s Summary

At NoosMedia we study how the human mind works at the pre-synaptic level. This means we examine how boutons (pre-synaptic buttons) and axon terminal junctions operate during the (1) sensation and (2) thinking process.

Boutons naturally ideate for life.

From this basic research, insights have emerged, which will allow people to directly improve their basic mental processing.

This Mind Science insight is embedded into NoosMedia training courses, web software products, and personnel services.

On average, people are productively using 10% to 20% of their mental abilities. This is a serious waste of life opportunity. Our goal is to help people use 70% to 80% of their boutons properly. In doing, we show people how to get more for less.

I develop Mind Science insights and share these with people, in a commercial setting.
