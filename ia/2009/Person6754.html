Albert Sandoval’s Summary

Mr. Albert Sandoval is a seasoned marketing communications and brand development expert with over 15 years experience. Part businessman, part innovator, Albert draws from a variety of talents to create smart, strategically focused brand platforms while creating award-winning marketing campaigns. His no-nonsense approach to marketing has earned him respect within the creative and business communities, while attracting blue-chip clients and talent-rich employees. Albert understands the process of building businesses from the ground up, from developing business models to branding, marketing, strategic alliances and promotions. In 1997 Albert started an advertising agency on his kitchen table in Los Feliz, CA, and within 18 months the company had reached $5 Million in annual billings. When the company was sold in late 2005, the agency had over 25 employees with offices in Santa Monica, CA and New York City and annual billings exceeding $25 Million. Albert is a recognized leader in the marketing world, having successfully developed and executed many notable campaigns in various categories of business including entertainment and gaming, consumer electronics, packaged goods, food and beverage and automotive. He has dedicated himself to the emerging-media world where technology and entertainment collide and has since been a part of numerous emerging technology and entertainment companies. His accomplishments have allowed him to build and keep strong relationships with key people in the marketing and national brand communities. Albert has been featured in Adweek, Brandweek, Los Angeles Business Journal and AdAge magazine.

Albert Sandoval’s Specialties:

Pop-Culture Branding, Integrated Marketing Communications, Emerging Media, Business Development, Strategic Planning, Creative and Identity Development, Digital Marketing and Social Media.
