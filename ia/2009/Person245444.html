Walter Bratkowski’s Summary

Highly skilled (MBA/CPA) financial and strategic senior executive with demonstrated results in developing and executing plans that resulted in the creation of significant shareholder value in both existing and emerging markets. Developed highly sophisticated analytical tools that: (1) Value target companies based on a triangulation process (DCF, comparable companies, comparable transactions, etc.) including and excluding synergies, (2) Calculate EPS impact based on various financial projections and deal structures (debt vs. equity), and (3) Prepare consolidating and consolidated financial statements and relevant covenant ratio compliance calculations. Lead negotiator on many transactions including the acquisition of a $1 billion U.K.-based firm saving the company $20 million on the transaction. Negotiated the selection of the appropriate investment bank and fee structure. Prepared and presented analyses and transactions to the Board of Directors and rating agencies. Played an integral part in maintaining banking relationship. Interfaced very successfully with the legal, accounting, tax, human resources, and treasury departments. Reputation for improving employee loyalty and being a team builder with highly effective communication, motivational, mentoring and management skills. Recognized as a successful change agent who develops plans securing management and employee buy-in. Additional large transactions have included CBS ($5.4 billion), Infinity Broadcasting ($5.0 billion), and the divestment of specific Westinghouse Business Units ($5.2 billion). LBO transactions included General Nutrition, and Kemet Electronics.
