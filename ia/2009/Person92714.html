Harry Tormey’s Summary

Harry Tormey is a co-founder of the Peer to Peer Research Institute and also organizes the monthly PyGameSF mulimedia programming meetup. He has worked as a software engineer for security giant Cybertrust (now owned by verizon) and currently works in the pro-audio industry for Digidesign (a subsidiary of Avid).

Harry loves cool technology and programming languages from low level microcode right up to high level programming languages such as C++ and Python.
