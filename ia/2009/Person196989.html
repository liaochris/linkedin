Rhiannon Ubelhor’s Summary

Prior to joining Pointroll, Rhiannon moved to the sales from a background in media to Conde Nast’s Brides.com where she worked as an Account Manager on top accounts such as Bloomingdales, Scott Kay and Watters & Watters as well as growing the southern territory.

Rhiannon started her career in online advertising 6 years ago working at Deutsch LA on the Mitsubishi account as well as Coors Brewing Co and TGI Fridays. She then moved on to TeamOne Advertising working on the Lexus account and focusing on the Launch of the redesigned 2006 Lexus IS. Her career also includes working as a Media Supervisor at Carat Fusion LA on the Kia Motors account. She then moved to NY as a Media Supervisor on the Johnson & Johnson account and worked on the pitch that led to McCann winning the entire Johnson & Johnson US account. Before making the move to sales she worked as the Associate Media Director in NY on the Chrysler, Dodge and Jeep account where she managed of all digital media components of the CLLC national and dealer business and worked strategically to increase the tier 2 dealer budgets from 4% to 10%.

Rhiannon Ubelhor’s Specialties:

Trained in online and offline research, planning and budgeting and strategic sales tools including: MRI, IMS, DDS/iDesk, Microsoft Office, Nielsen/ComScore, Atlas, DART/MediaVisor, MediaPlex, Rich Media venders, Advertising Database and PeopleSoft
