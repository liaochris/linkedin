Allan Sommerville’s Summary

I have over 9 years experience as a graphic designer, starting with the design of academic and educational books for Cambridge University Press. This was followed by a career in illustrated trade publishing at Duncan Baird Publishers, where subject areas ranged from historical and cultural reference to cookery. My current employment is with Smith&Gilmour, a design studio which specializes in print and publishing. In this role I work with many clients, including Penguin, Ebury and Harper Collins on a varied list of projects.
