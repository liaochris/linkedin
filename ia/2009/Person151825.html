Mark Frieser’s Summary

Mark Frieser is a seasoned media executive with 20 years experience in founding, building and managing traditional, digital and mobile companies and products for consumer and business markets.

Current Projects:
Music Management - more than 15 artists including Midnight Youth.
iPhone/Smartphone applications and sites based on music/models/licensed content
Executive Event: Content and Cash - November 3, 2009 in Beverly Hills

Past highlights:

1988-1990: Co-founded first company, PM Productions - agency for tours of top Soviet musical and theatrical talent.

1990-1991: Offices of Senator Daniel Patrick Moynihan (D-NY) and Rep. Eliot Engel (D-NY), Asst. Campaign Mgnr. of NYS Supreme Court Judge Edgar Walker.

1990-1995: Senior Analyst, Jupiter Communications.

1991-1993: Editor, Consultant, A.Jour (Paris, France)

1995-1996: News Corporation. Interactive services manager, on-air talent liaison.

1996-1997: About.com. Community/editorial.

1997-1998: iVillage.com. PR; Director of Community.

1998-1999: MY-CD (part of Bandai), BD

1999: Womenjapan.com (Tokyo, Japan). Strategic/editoral consultant.

2000-2005: Founded Consect, leading mobile market research firm.

>Events: MobileMusiCon, Global Wireless Summit, Mobile and Money.

>Newsletters, reports

>Consulting with Lagardere, Index Holdings, Orange, France Telecom, among others, strategy, content dev, BD, management.

>Co-development (with Billboard) of the official US ringtone charts.

2005-2007: Development of mobile entertainment offerings, including Text2Win, Jenna Jameson, Melyssa Ford and Carmen Electra.

2007-2008: SVP, Marketing/Content/BD at http://zedge.net. Grew membership 5 million to 12 million, co-managed website re-design and content network development, signing major studios, musical artists. Managed viral marketing, business development, SMS development.

Mark Frieser’s Specialties:

Entertainment, market research, strategy, analysis, events development and marketing, music, mobile, touring, film, TV, digital, social networks, design, adult, international business development.
