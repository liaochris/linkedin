Deborah Moses’s Summary

President of Veris Associates, Inc., an IT Consulting, Staffing, and Training company, specializing in IT Service Management, the process of IT.

Speaker on multiple topics and Author of Change Your Life! The CORE Approach® to Creating the Life You Want. Co-author of Conversations on Success, Volume Five.

Deborah Moses’s Specialties:

IT Professional, IT Service Management (ITIL) Subject Matter Expert, Speaker and Author. Expertise in leadership, managerial learning, project management, business operations optimization, teambuilding, coaching, personal development.
