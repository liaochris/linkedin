Andrew Masters’s Summary

Bachelor of Arts in Performing Arts Management
Minor: Business Administration, focus on advertising/communications

Andrew Masters has forever had an immense attraction to and presence in all aspects of the music and entertainment industry. His involvement in music began as a drummer and soon got him involved in live promotion and ultimately led him to the Hartt School of Music to study the business of music.

Andrew has spent his time in a wide range of industry areas including direct involvement with a New York City record label Roadrunner Records and Massachusetts Independent Illumina Records. He has also gained invaluable experience in the nonprofit sector while interning with the world renowned Hartford Stage Company in creating concrete annual fund campaigns, as well as assisting in the development of special fundraising events, and seeking out new corporate donors.

Andrews’s true passion lies in the live entertainment sector both as a performer and event coordinator. Here, he has spent time working with the international Emergenza Music Festival. His group performed in the Boston, MA dates of the festival at The Middle East and Paradise Rock Clubs as well as served as stage manager for the Hartford, CT dates of the festival.
Most recently he has become involved with GMR Marketing where he assists in creating positive bonds between consumers and brands at an intimate face-to-face level. Brands include the Marlboro Copperlabel music promotion, live artist promotions at venues across the Boston, MA market, and numerous other products.

In addition, he has recently become involved in an longtime passion of his, graphic design, with the hopes of helping artists across the country in developing valuable marketing tools to progress their careers.

Andrew possesses a broad range of skills to act as an asset to any organization with the desire to learn all he can and the determination to execute and produce a winning outcome!
