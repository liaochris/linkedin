Robert Bousquet’s Summary

Robert Bousquet is an award-winning web developer with an eye for design. His projects have been recognized internationally by the American Design Awards, Rails Day and most recently RailsRumble ‘07. Robert’s projects currently serve millions of people everyday.

Robert’s seminars on web application programming have helped faculty and staff at the University of Southern California, and internal web teams at CBS Television integrate cutting-edge web technologies into their infrastructure.

As lead developer for CollectiveX, Robert led the development of a web-based software platform that helps thousands of people setup or participate in websites that enable collaboration within their organization.

Robert is a founding member and integral part of the New Leaders team.
