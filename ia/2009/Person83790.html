Gary Long’s Summary

My clients have a desire to learn and achieve a greater understanding of how their mortgage relates to their financial goals. Many people have professionals to help them manage the asset side of their balance sheet, but few people have financial professionals that help them with the debt / liability side of their balance sheet. How an individual manages credit and liabilities can often have more impact on their economic health, than how they manage their liabilities. Knowledge is power and my clients are powerful.

I am an experienced people manager with excellent process, analytical, and people skills. With over 25 years of supervisory experience, combined with a law degree I would really like to use my abilities as a hands on leader to connect with people at all levels and lead them to exceed their goals, while driving growth and profitability for the company.

A quick thinker with strong analytical and problem solving skills, that excels at relationship building. A focused, dependable manager with the ability to get the most out of my team, by daily tasking to meet and exceed weekly, monthly, and annual production goals. Key to my contribution is the innate ability to learn and apply complex rules and objectives to both internal processes and external customer needs.
