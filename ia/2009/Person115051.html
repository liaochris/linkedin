Joaquin Gomez Moya MBA’s Summary

I hail from Ciudad Real, in Spain, but I'm in love with Japan and so many other places I use to travel to. Friend for my friends, I'm very lucky for working in what I like, namely business administration and international sales, travelling all over the world half of my time and dealing with many persons, completely dissimilar individuals and professionals, in different cultural environments.
Tenacious, I assume calculated risks, being rather analytical and conceptual, and possessing (I like to think of myself) some capacity to develop strategic vision . And, I finish, I've grown fond of two... say obsessions: on one hand, to respond to and to take care of my clients well; on the other hand, to help my team’s persons to success, by means of being taught self-exigency.
