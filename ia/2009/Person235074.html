Todd Beaupre’s Summary

Seasoned product director who lives for personalization, consumer behavioral data, and the opportunity to truly engage hundreds of millions of users based on their individual interests.

Todd Beaupre’s Specialties:

Todd Beaupre’s Experience

  • Director, Product Management, Content Optimization and Personalization

    Yahoo!

    (Public Company; YHOO; Internet industry)

    October 2007 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    I'm Director of Product Management for Content Optimization. My team manages a platform that feeds real-time user engagement data and user profiles into a modeling engine which determines the best content to show each user on sites such as the Yahoo! homepage (yahoo.com).

  • Director, Product Management, Music Personalization

    Yahoo!

    (Public Company; YHOO; Internet industry)

    January 2004 — October 2007 (3 years 10 months)

    I led the effort to extend music personalization beyond radio into recommendations of songs, albums, artists, music videos, and music fans. I also drove implementation of Yahoo! Music's web services. These products elicited over 10 billion explicit ratings of music from end users.

  • Director, Product Management, Radio

    Yahoo!

    (Public Company; YHOO; Internet industry)

    October 2001 — December 2003 (2 years 3 months)

    I was product director for LAUNCHcast Radio, with key involvement in product design, user experience, and personalization algorithms. Projects included various player redesigns, and integration of radio into Yahoo! Messenger, for which I hold patents.

    LAUNCH
