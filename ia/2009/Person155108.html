Mathijs van Zutphen’s Summary

My name is Mathijs van Zutphen, I live in Amsterdam, a truely global village. I’m a philosopher, writer , consultant and artist.

Creativity and innovation are crucial for the sustainable success of any business, and yet many companies struggle with making the best use of their creative potential. At Crossing Signals we developed a great way to get teams to collaborate, innovate and create value more effectively.

We help businesses get the most from their people and ideas. Working with a team of world renowned experts and academicians, we focus on the innnovation process as a team effort. We have the tools to bring people together, create trust and synergy, and create breakthrough solutions.
