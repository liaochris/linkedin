Kirk Lowe’s Summary

Anointed SuQu Sherpa at SuccessQuest on March 6, 2009. Also founding partner of SuccesQuest and SuQu Mapping.

As the SuQu Sherpa, Kirk is responsible for guiding SuccessQuest and SuQu Mapping as the standard for achieving success. SuccessQuest was built by coaches and consultants, for coaches and consultants. It's a powerful tool that adds significant tangible value to the coach-client relationship, value never before seen.

Aspiring business owners, sales professionals and executives who seek a simple yet profound solution for greater success should look no further than a SuQu Certified Coach. SuQu Coaches are simply the coaches & consultants who use SuQu.

SuccessQuest helps professional business coaches facilitate the creation and execution of a concise business plan.

SuccessQuest has beautifully simplified the process of concise business planning - we call it SuQu Mapping. Traditional business planning is a comprehensive and cumbersome process that most often doesn't meet the needs of business owners and sales professionals. Comprehensive business planning rarely fulfills the need for entrepreneurs to find clarity, sustain focus and execute masterfully. SuQu Mapping does all that and more.
