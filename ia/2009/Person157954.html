Clara Carter’s Summary

MCCSN’s commitment to excellence has forged loyal relationships among its clients. Whether your event needs the best hotel and venue at the best rate, a unique venue, or guests would like to experience off-site attractions and San Diego culture, Clara and her team will be happy to make it happen for you. MCCSN—Meetings made easy.

MCCSN markets and promotes the city of San Diego to national Multicultural Associations and Companies, and to Leisure Travelers. Our primary target audiences are convention and meeting planners in the Hispanic, Asian, and African American business and ethnic Leisure Travel markets. We hire diverse professional consultants to assist in market research & strategic planning, public relations & marketing, and in sales & advertising.

Clara Carter’s Experience

  • Owner

    Multicultural Convention Services Network

    (Hospitality industry)

    June 2004 — Present (5 years 3 months)

    The Multi-Cultural Convention Services Network (MCCSN) is a San- Diego based for-profit tourism sales and marketing corporation, serving the San Diego hospitality industry as the vital link to the Multicultural Convention & Tourism market. MCCSN matches the needs of national multicultural associations and companies, as well as, leisure travelers with it’s network of dedicated civic leaders, hospitality partners and supporters, and proven service providers.
    “Our goal is to ensure that our visitors have the triple “E” effect— Experiences that Exceed their Expectations.”

Clara Carter’s Honors:

Ms. Carter has received several awards and has been recognized by local leaders, national organizations, political constituents, and the media for her contributions to the San Diego community and the hospitality industry. In 2008, received proclamations from Mayor Jerry Sanders, Congressman Bob Filner and Councilmember Tony Young for producing the 2nd Annual Multicultural FAM Tour in San Diego, 2008, received the “Self Determination” Award” at the 16th Annual Kuumba Fest, San Diego, and was filmed and recognized during the month of February as one of Black History Month’s Honorees by NBC TV, Channel 7/39. Most recently,awarded the “Phenomenal Woman Award” by Bethel AME Church. In 2007 awarded the "Meeting Planner of the Year" Award from the International Association of Hispanic Meeting Professionals, 2007 named the Citizen of the Month, by Congressman Bob Filner, 2007 Mayor Jerry Sanders proclaimed September 13-16, as Clara Carter day, produced 1st Multicultural FAM Tour for San Diego.
