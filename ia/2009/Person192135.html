Rafael Montenegro’s Summary

User Experience Expert and Manager with more than 10 years of versatile experience, gained in corporate and IT consulting varied work environments. Extensive qualifications in project management, UX, brand, and design. Thinks both analytically and creatively about software, web applications, user centered design and communication. Takes charge of creating cross-functional teams with customers to identify, prioritize, and design solutions. Creates visual prototypes, concept maps, and effective written requirements that define a product vision and enable to design customized UX methodologies. Has demonstrated ability to interact with both internal and external key stakeholders. And enjoys doing it.
