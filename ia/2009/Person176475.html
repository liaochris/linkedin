Noah Bryant’s Summary

I graduated from UMaine, Orono with a degree in Chemical Engineering. I took a Process Engineer job with Sappi, a paper mill in Westbrook, Maine--the mill that I had been interning at for the previous five years. I love my job as it entails the perfect combination of data analysis, leadership development, and technical problem solving.
