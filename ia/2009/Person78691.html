felix bonnier’s Summary

.d.o.p. was created by five international architects in 2008 in Shanghai as a collaborative effort between DP WT and i.a.d. of Hong Kong.

.d.o.p. (divium.orbis.pyro) is a plural-process, design-oriented collaborative within the domain of architecture, urbanism and design. Its collective team of architects, designers, and specialized consultants, seek the creative synergy between architecture, environment, landscape and
design through a clearly defined conceptual process. To create a unique, cost-effective total solution tailored to the client’s needs.

.d.o.p. spans beyond geographical boundaries. Current projects extend from Beijing and Shenzhen, China to Stockholm, Sweden; from Faro, Portugal to the Dominican Republic. Its base of operation will be situated in Shanghai with Parent offices in Beijing, Hong Kong and Paris.

.d.o.p. steps away from the traditional architectural studio as a service oriented company. Instead, it fosters a professional consortium of Architects, Local Design Institutes, Interior Designers and Decorators. An open network collaboration between all actors that play a role in the creation of the environment. Its projects entail not a product nor space, but a concrete conceptual communication of its ideas. It lies on the perimeter of architecture, design and cultural identity.

.d.o.p. believes that to foster a successful project there has to be thourough communication and clear understanding of the client and end-user’s needs. Comprehensive quality services integrating professionalism, design and project management. And hands-on supervision on site during the implementation stage.

felix bonnier’s Specialties:

.d.o.p. explores the very fabric of our society to transform sustainable consepts into functioning projects. Using the very essence of our environment as the base for its sustainable ideas.
