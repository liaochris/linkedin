Murat Eskiyerli’s Summary

Experienced RF/analog integrated circuit and system design engineer.
Ph.D. from Imperial College, London on Analog Integrated Circuit Design. Worked in US and UK for more than 9 years including in Sony, National Semiconductor, Analog Devices and Cambridge Silicon Radio. Designed high-volume products for customers including Quantum, Hewlett-Packard and Nokia.

Murat Eskiyerli’s Specialties:

CMOS/BiCMOS RF and optical communications integrated circuit design: LNA, Mixer, VGA, Limiting Amplifiers, RF buffers, hard-disk preamplifiers, Clock and Data Recovery (CDR) circuits, high-speed output drivers.
