Giulio Coraggio’s Summary

Giulio was awarded with a law degree at University Federico II of Naples. Before joining Lovells, he worked in London, focusing on privacy and data protection issues, where he received an LL.M. in Intellectual Property, Information Technology, Internet and Telecommunications law at the Queen Mary College.

Giulio is a Senior Associate at Lovells, Italy and has a strong expetise in advicing clients on information technology and e-commerce issues, including the recovery of domain names and drafting of software licence agreements and outsourcing and procurement agreements. Also he advises companies on betting and gambling activities performed online and offline and on data protection issues under Italian and European privacy law.

Likewise he has reached a considerabe experience in drafting and negotiating international commercial contracts, including sponsorship agreements and product and service supply and distribution agreements, also dealing with competition issues arising out of vertical agreements.
