Vikas Shah’s Summary

A passionate business development leader with sales and management experience in financial services, hi-tech, media and telecommunications industry. Strong sales track record of independently winning multi-million dollar contracts and consistently exceeding annual revenue and gross margin targets. Ability to engage and negotiate strategic deals with senior executives leveraging consultative and solution selling techniques. Employing the use of intutive business concepts to influence stakeholders to implement key transformation initiatives thereby gaining credibility as a trusted advisor. Acting as a management consultant to position project, program and portfolio management services helping organization restructure and optimize their business and IT operations. Ability to analyze industry trends to segment markets and create differentiated value propositions. Diverse experience in multi-shore strategic outsourcing of information technology and business process services. Expertise in financial modeling, market competitive research, quantitative analysis and investor-grade presentations. Experience with global distributed outsourcing of Information Technology (IT) and Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) services in U.S., Philippines, India, Canada and Europe.
