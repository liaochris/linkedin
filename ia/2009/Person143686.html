Lori Saitz’s Experience

  • Chief Executive Rabbit

    Zen Rabbit Baking Company

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 2003 — Present (6 years 6 months)

    Zen Rabbit makes an unbelievably addictive gourmet cookie from a unique family recipe. These cookies have a wonderful crunchiness and a delicate balance between the butter, sugar and rest of the short list of other, all-natural ingredients. We call them Gratitude Cookies(TM) because we encourage you to think of something you are grateful for as you eat each cookie.

    Packaging can be customized for corporate and business clients with their logo and message.

    Need to say thank you to your clients, referral sources or associates? Need to stand out and make a fantastic impression on an important contact?

    NEW CONCEPT - The Gratitude Cookie (TM) package holds one dozen cookies PLUS a Gratuities Card with an inspirational saying on one side and on the back, the question "What are you grateful for?" along with space to write in an answer. Now available at gourmet markets and tea and coffee houses nationwide!
