Jason Holland’s Summary

I love negotiation strategy and creative application as it applies to sales and business development. At 25 (within a 14 month span), I effectively and solely negotiated ad sales agreements in excess of $3M with VP level marketing execs at Fortune 1000's including Maytag, DirecTV (OMD-NY), Brink's Home Security (DDB), Sam's Club, Pitney Bowes, Gateway, and Sage Software. These deals were worked client direct, in conjunction with their digital / print specific media buying agencies, had an avarage close value of $310k per deal, and accounted for 60% of the boutique firm's second year revenues.

Today, at 29, I head up International Business Development for Gorilla Nation. GN is the world's largest online ad sales rep firm and an all around incredible company. My competencies here include contract negotiation, content licensing / distribution, deal origination, and M&A, as well as teaching the art of these negotiations to our teams (an important asset). I've run point on contract executions including Starwars.com, Marvel.com, King.com, Gawker Media, and Reuters.com... all marquee websites in my company's representation arsenal.
