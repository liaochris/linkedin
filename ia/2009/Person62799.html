Richard D Gladfelter III’s Summary

Leading a peripatetic existence within Timmons Group, Donnie has been an employee of Timmons for nearly five years. Getting his start at Timmons in a design role, he has since excelled to his current position as Design Systems Specialist. In that role his personal mantra is quite simply to “empower CAD designers” by rendering an unparalleled commitment to each and every user. Such a commitment parallels itself in establishing a more knowledgeable staff who consequently can work more efficiently.

Backed by more than a decade of experience, Donnie has established himself as a proven communicator both written and oral. As a public speaker he has been privileged enough to engage audiences ranging from only a few people to an arena of more than 60,000, and will be a speaker at the upcoming Autodesk University. As a columnist for AUGIWorld magazine, and blogger at The CAD Geek Blog, Donnie’s writing has reached hundreds of thousands of CAD users throughout the world.

Donnie’s professional reputation has been founded upon both integrity and loyalty. These ideals are illustrated by his passion for empowering CAD professionals not only within his own firm, but throughout the industry as a whole. Proof his peripatetic existence extends well beyond the walls of Timmons Group.
