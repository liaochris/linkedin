Robert Thacher’s Summary

I am a skilled professional with more than fifteen years of solid Information technology experience. As Chief Technology Officer, I oversaw Global Wireless Data's product development and technical operations and expand their technology platform to meet escalating customer needs. My responsibilities included product engineering, network operations, data center operations, IT and customer support. I have strong leadership, communication and organizational skills and a proven ability to implement and manage technology-based solutions which produce a cost reduction and improved efficiencies.

Prior to joining Global Wireless Data, I served as President, CEO and CIO for Karbon Systems, a nationwide distributor of high end wireless devices and managed wireless services. I was responsible for product strategy, project management and engineering management for the company's software and sales teams nationwide. I also executed a successful acquisition of Karbon Systems in December 2006.

Before founding Karbon Systems, I served as the Director of Professional Services for the WolfeTech Corporation, the first provider of wireless Internet access solutions for two-way pagers and devices. While at Wolfetech, I founded Wolfetech's Professional Services division in 1999, which became WolfeTech's largest revenue generator. I also co-managed the acquisition of InfoBeam, a competing wireless Internet access solutions provider for two-way pagers and managed a network-wide software application preload on the Skytel and Weblink Wireless networks.

I am the co-creator of U.S Patent # 20030196084 for secure wireless communications using PKI
