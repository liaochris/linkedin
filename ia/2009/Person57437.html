David Ezell’s Summary

David Ezell is the Founder of Better Than Ever Support Groups & Coaching as well as the author of the health blog, InSide Effects.

David is a therapist, life coach, activist and avid blogger. He combines the techniques of CBT and REBT, along with a dash of history and sociology, to help his clients help themselves. His eclectic approach has allowed his diverse clientele--business leaders, artists, actors, athletes and solo entrepreneurs--take positive steps in their lives.

He loves his gym time, reading his Kindle and great conversation.

David Ezell’s Specialties:

Post-relationship trauma, life transitions, gender identity issues, Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD), depression, anger, anxiety, fitness and cognitive restructuring
