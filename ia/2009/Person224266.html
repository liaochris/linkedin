Steven Bonner’s Summary

Also Known As was conceived in early 2009 after I left Baseline to pursue a freelancer's life. I've always been inspired by creative people and I wanted to get out and meet as many of those people as possible, which is what made freelancing so attractive.

My aims for AKA are to create creative work with a touch of pragmatism to ensure my clients get work that not only looks the business, but does the business too.

I want to build contacts in primarily the Scottish design community, but also further afield and also have clients in England, the US, and Brazil. So far its been hard work, but a lot of fun so long may it continue.
