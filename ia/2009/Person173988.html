Nick Black’s Summary

Vice President of Strategic Insight at Concerto Marketing, Nick Black is North America’s only expert in Morphological Research, Brand Strategy and Innovation. With experience spanning Australia, Asia and North America, he has delivered deep insight and strategy for clients including Coca-Cola, Dell, Foster’s, George Weston Foods, Intel, Yellow Pages and 20th Century Fox.

Nick holds a Bachelor of Business and a Master of Arts. He's taught at Monash University and has been a guest lecturer at Melbourne University and Simon Fraser University. Nick is a frequent speaker and writer in the areas of market research, strategy and innovation.

Nick Black’s Specialties:

- Research: Morphological research, in-depth interviews, focus groups
- Strategy: Brand strategy, category & market management, innovation
- Industries: Consumer Merchandise, Energy & Utilities, Entertainment, FMCG & CPG, Financial Services & Insurance, Grocery & Retail, Information & Directories, Property, Social Policy, Technology
