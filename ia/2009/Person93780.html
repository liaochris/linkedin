Hélder Falcão’s Summary

Hello!
Feel free in the end of reading my profile to send me an email directly to helder@bni.pt

Thanks for visiting my profile.
Business Networking is my full time job.
I am currently in charge of BNI in Portugal and Brazil, as well as leading Ecademy in Portugal.

BNI is the World's largest Referral Organization with over 100.00 members that meet every week to share ideas, contacts and business referrals.
BNI in Portugal arrived in October 2005 and since then, we have exchanged thousands of referrals, that already generated over 13 million Euros worth of Business.

Brazil is the country where I have just bought the Master Franchise and am developping a team of entrepreneurs that will implement BNI Groups in their local communities.
São Paulo, Vitória are the two first States to welcome BNI.

Regarding Ecademy, it's the largest business and social networking platform in the World with over 130.000 users.
I am in charge of the strategic alliance between BNI and Ecademy and run a Business Club with almost Two Thousand members around the World.

Ecademy in Portugal is organically growing at a rate of 3 or 4 new members every single day.
XING is the platform I have been invited to be the Embassateur in Portugal.
We hold monthly events around the country and do large networking events with the Chambers of Commerce, BNI and Ecademy all together!
Just as what networking its all about. Give without remembering...Receive without forgetting.
