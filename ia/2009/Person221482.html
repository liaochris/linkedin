Salim Sheikh’s Summary

An independent consultant, entrepreneur and thought-leader with a refreshing flamboyant and trusted reputation for delivering high-quality advice and Business/IT solutions across several Industry sectors over the past 13 years.

A Chief Strategy Officer for a growing consultancy located across UK, US and India who excel at delivering Business & IT integration and Enterprise Architecture strategies and solutions.

A well-grounded professional with a strong engineering backed up by an impressive academic record that includes BSc (Hons), MSc & PhD qualifications.

Currently writing a series of books on "Holistic Enterprise Architecture" and a series of white papers and articles on mixed topics around EA, Strategy and Governance for the Open Group and several Business/IT magazines & journals.

Key skills & areas of technical expertise include:

- IT Strategy
- Enterprise Architecture
- Service Oriented Architecture (SOA)
- Enterprise Application Integration (EAI)
- Enterprise Information Integration (EII) & Business Intelligence (BI)
- LEAN/Six Sigma & Enterprise Business Modelling

Public & Private Industry sector experience:

Financial Services, Telcos, Utilities, Insurance, Local Government (e-Government), Healthcare and Automotive.

Strategy: Lead architecture strategy definition and provide architecture and programme management where necessary.
