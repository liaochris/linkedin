Michael Neill’s Summary

Michael Neill is the bestselling author of "You Can Have What You Want" and “Feel Happy Now”, published by Hay House and translated into 5 languages. He is an internationally renowned success coach, a media commentator and advisor to movie stars, music moguls, business leaders and Royals.

Michael has written over 400 coaching articles in the areas of success, money, relationships, health, happiness, well-being, and spirituality. His weekly coaching column has been reprinted in newspapers and magazines throughout the world, and the web-based version currently has over 15000 regular subscribers in over 90 countries around the world.
Michael is a licensed Master Trainer of NLP and regularly co-presents the UK's largest and most successful personal development seminars with TV hypnotist Paul McKenna and Dr. Richard Bandler - the inventor of NLP - working as part of an elite leadership group in personal development, NLP, Coaching and Mentoring.
His live call-in radio show, ‘You Can Have What You Want’ , is broadcast around the world on Hay House Radio and the podcast edition is downloaded by over 50,000 people each month.
