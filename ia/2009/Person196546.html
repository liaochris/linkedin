Colleen Wright’s Summary

Online Marketing Specialist with over 10 years of online marketing experience and 15 years of BtoB marketing experience. My focus is on leveraging the Internet to help you increase leads, sales and brand awareness implementing search engine marketing (SEM) to include search engine optimization (SEO) and pay per click (PPC) advertising. I also help craft email campaigns and design online display ads that target your customers and increase sales. My goal is to help grow small and medium size business through creative online strategies and training.
