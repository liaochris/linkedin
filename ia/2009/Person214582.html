Shafik Mansur’s Summary

Shafik Mansur is the co-owner and co-founder of Redberry Support Services,LLC. Shafik brings his telecommunications technology consulting expertise and project management experience along with his luxury and resort hospitality industry background to provide a unique insight into building and provisioning robust, affordable and convenient entertainment and telecommunications solutions for his clients. From his early years in hotel management and operations with both Starwood Hotels and Resorts and Marriott International to his more recent hospitality telecommunications project manager and consultant positions, Shafik is a dedicated, customer centric and innovative resource for not just hotels and resort properties, but also brings his keen understanding of social media networks to providing effective web design, branding and organic SEO practices for small and solo-entrepreneur operators who realize that they cannot reach their goals without a strong support team.

Shafik and his business partner, Jannice, created a specialized firm, Redberry Support Services, LLC in January of 2009 in order to assist entrepreneurs such as speakers, trainers, authors and life coaches to focus on doing what they do best. The Redberry Support Services, LLC team takes on the vital work of creating coherent branding, website and sales page design, social media networking, customer care, administrative services, marketing and research, desktop ezine and brochure publishing and positioning of their clients' businesses for easy findability and high search rankings. In addition, corporate concierge and event management services are provided by Shafik and Jannice and their team of professionals at Redberry Support Services, LLC.
