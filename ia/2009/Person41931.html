jason brumm’s Summary

For the last 16 years, I have worked in every aspect of food preparation and creation. During this period I have developed a philosophy about food and management that guides my daily work. Simplicity. A few flavorful ingredients combined in a creative way and artfully presented. I have expanded my capabilities beyond the kitchen and now am an experienced owner/partner in an upscale restaurant venture. I have consulted with a number of restaurants to improve their operations, develop innovative menus, and re-launch their concepts. One of my greatest strengths is developing staff. Most of my staff has come to me with minimal skills and over time each has been able to move up to the next level of responsibility. I have developed a reputation for outstanding food, innovation and quality and been featured in numerous local & national publications.
