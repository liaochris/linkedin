Richard ZHAO’s Summary

Dr.Richard Zhao Liang (赵粮) obtained his PhD degree at 1997 from Peking University, majored fiber-optic communications, followed by over 9 years of professional experience on telecommmunications and security with certificates of CISSP, ITIL, BS7799.
