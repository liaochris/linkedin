Tom Speeckaert’s Summary

Due to intense trainings and projects/products experience over the past years I have a good knowledge of several home and building automation systems.

Trying to find the right products into the jungle of suppliers and make them communicate together is one of my challenges. Under the saying “one for all and all for one” I manage to engage several players in security, home & building automation, audio/video automation to connect.

Thanks to my good communication skills I have the abilities to translate the technical part into an understandable concept for the client.
My enthusiasm into the matter helps me to motivate co workers and contractors to give the best of them.

In private times I love to spend time with my family, watch a good movie and cherish my passion for boats.
