Ryan Morgan’s Summary

Diverse ministry background with an emphasis on reaching emerging generations and providing creative infrastructures to support long-term goals.

I place a high emphasis on the discovery and application of "liveable theology", and am currently completing a B.Th at Master's College & Seminary in Toronto.

I've written extensively on the impact of postmodernity on the way we "do church" and live our lives, and taught a seminar for Parents along these lines entitled, "Understanding the World Your Teen Lives In."

I plan on pursuing an M.T.S degree following my completion of the B.Th program, in preparation for doctoral studies.
