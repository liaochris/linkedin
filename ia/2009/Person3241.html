Adam Taggart’s Summary

I'm a Silicon Valley executive with 15+ years experience in the Consumer Internet, Mobile and Telecom industries. I enjoy bringing to market solutions that enrich the lives of millions of people around the world.

In addition to my day job, I'm passionate about personal finance and the economy - as I believe the vast majority of people are unprepared for the severe repercussions in store after several decades of unsustainable financial and monetary policy practiced by most governments. If you know me, there's a good chance we've had at least one talk about the wisdom of owning gold & silver.

If you're curious to know what interests me career-wise, it's roles that:
• Advance the well-being of all stakeholders (customers, employees, investors, community, nation)
• Address an important & sustainable need in the marketplace
• Do not require me to sacrifice my integrity (e.g., does not result in undue environmental harm)
• Offer work-life balance (so I have the time my family needs from me, as well as to pursue interests outside of work)
• Offer material financial remuneration
• Interest me
• Provide opportunities for continuous professional growth/development
• Make me (and my kids) proud of what I do for a living
• Let me make and/or shape decisions at the highest levels
• Allow me to live in an area that’s good for my family
• Build skills that will be in long-term demand by the market
• Can be a lifelong career
• Offer the chance to work closely with senior mentor(s)
