Smokie Sizemore, CPCC’s Summary

-Founder of the Smart Woman's Club, a Richmond, VA based coaching-training firm focused on professional women

-Executive coach, keynote speaker, and innovator of intellectual capital retention process

-Designed and led world-class coaching segment for Emerging Leader Program of United Way of Greater Richmond and Petersburg

-Extensive experience creating debrief process for 24 hour Endurance Race Car Driver Training

-Founder of second decorative flag company in US

-Created new products impacting international industry

-Designed and led custom communication training for Fortune 100 company

-Produced WOW! and ICF-Richmond Chapter conferences

-Active member of professional organizations including National Speakers Association (NSA), richtech.com

-Board member of United Way of Greater Richmond and Petersburg Advisory Board

-Former board member of National Association National Association of Women Business Owners (NAWBO), American Business Women's Association (ABWA), International Coach Federation (ICF) Richmond Chapter, the Shoppes at Patterson and Libbie Merchants Association, Rotary Club of Richmond

-Founder of International Coach Federation (ICF) Richmond Chapter, the Shoppes at Patterson and Libbie Merchants Association

-Featured in a variety of publications including Virginia Business, the Richmond Times-Dispatch, Express Network Newsletter, the Virginia-Pilot
