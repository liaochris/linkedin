Rob Reid’s Summary

My primary role is as a Director and part Owner of Cybercom, Ireland's leading Digital Marketing agency.

Cybercom's clients include AIB, Coca-Cola, Diageo, RBS, Aer Lingus, Vodafone, Open University and Jurys Inns.

We employ 32 full-time staff in Dublin and also work closely with a number of design and production freelancers throughout the world. We also have a sales office and do considerable business in London.

My principal responsibilities include guiding the strategic direction of the business and managing the commercial output of the Client Service and Media departments.

I also sit on the board and have a shareholding in the (recently beta launched) Roomopia.com, an online platform allowing consumers to book worldwide hotel accommodation in seconds.

I've been an "on / off" affiliate for over 5 years - most recently through an Irish specific vehicle at allAbout.ie - I sold my stake in the operation in 2008.

I spent two years on the board of the Irish Direct Marketing Association (IDMA) as Head of the Digital Committee.

I regularly contribute to broadsheet media and trade publications, speak at digital marketing events and blog at Cybercom.ie

Rob Reid’s Specialties:

Search Marketing - 8 years experience in devising multi-lingual PPC and SEO strategies.

Affiliate Marketing - experienced Irish affiliate and also regularly deliver leading programmes for Cybercom clients.

Social Media - advise some of the world's largest brands on widgets, blogs, social networks, wikis etc.

Online PR - experienced consultant and practioner including SEO-PR, review site moderation strategies, RSS and user generated content.
