Kim Brennan’s Summary

My expertise is in teaching, planning curriculum for art classes for all ages from 6- adult and what it takes to run a non-profit Commuity Arts Center. Currently I am developing two new classes for Kennebec Valley Community College, Ceramics and Drawing as an adjunct professor. Since August of 2005 I have been able to be part of the start up process of a non-profit Arts Center in Waterville, Maine which has given me experience with fundraising, grant writing, and the hiring of new teachers. I believe my strength is in my ability to relate to people of all ages and walks of life and my passion about teaching the arts.

Kim Brennan’s Specialties:

curriculum development, parts of running a non-profit arts center (grant writing, fundraising, ect), running of a ceramics studio and expertise in non-traditional firing techniques.
