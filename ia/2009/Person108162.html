Jeffrey Lemkin’s Summary

Simplify. Clarify. Connect. Profit.

Today, companies need to rise above the noise of competing marketing messages and make stronger connections with their customers. We're passionate about first understanding your value proposition, then making it persuasive, easy to understand and easy to remember.

Relevant Content helps you engage more closely with your customers and create more profitable connections.

We believe in the power of story and we help tell your story through:

*White papers and editorial content for thought leadership, brand reinforcement and high-return campaign offers.
*Customer evidence - evidentiary and brand case studies that reinforce the unique business value of your products and services and reinforce sales' efforts.
*Copywriting that drives customer action.
*Presentation scripts and executive bylines for clear, consistent messaging - internal and external
*Positioning and messaging frameworks to help explain and differentiate your business while highlighting the unique value of your brand.
*Action oriented Web content that engages the "scanners" and gets results.

Other professional interests:

*Proposal writing: Extensive experience crafting winning RFP responses for enterprise software solutions.
*Screenwriting: Currently working as script consultant with Lucid Dreams, Inc.
