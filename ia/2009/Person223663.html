Steve Beatty’s Summary

Have been a financial advisor since 1989. Spent five years as an agent at Equitable Financial, five years as a financial advisor and sales manager at Mass Mutual. Have had my own independent practice since 1998. Have been a member of the Million Dollar Roundtable 15 times and Top of the Table four of the last five years. Hold the Accredited Investment Fiduciary (AIF) designation.

Steve Beatty’s Specialties:

Work with business owners who are 40-60, worth $2-100 million, care about their familes and care about their communities. Specialize in strategic tax planning, exit planning, and business planning. Also own 401k Administrators,a third party administration firm that provides edu-tainment for participants and and compliance and fiduciary solutions for plan sponsors of 401(k) plans and defined benefit plans.
