Scott Bretschneider 619-465-9628 scottb@ispards.com’s Summary

Scott has seventeen years of international executive search experience. As a Senior Search Consultant for International Search Partners he manages executive searches in the Educational Gaming, Video Game Publisher, Developer, MMOG, Entertainment, and Consumer Electronics/Goods industries. Previously he founded HeadMatcher.com, an international executive search firm in San Francisco. He was formerly an Associate Partner with Bialla & Associates a retained executive search firm in San Francisco. Scott was Vice President of Veritas International and an Executive Search Consultant with TMT, Technics In Management Transfer, Inc., both in Tokyo, Japan. Scott recruited bilingual, vice president- to chief executive-level executives for the Tokyo, Japan subsidiary offices of multinational companies.
