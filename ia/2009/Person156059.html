Matthew Roberts’s Summary

My experience includes over 12 years of professional consulting and development of elegant software solutions to complex business problems in varied industries including healthcare, automotive, entertainment, document processing, and group collaboration.

My goals are to build my business through both short-term consulting and long-term contracting, as well as architecting commercial-quality tools and SDKs to help other developers be more productive and follow best-practice procedures.

I cater to both large and small businesses, but have a special place in my heart for small businesses that need professional services but cannot afford higher-priced consulting firms. Further, my company is gay owned and operated and provides services for GLBT charitable organizations at a reduced rate.
