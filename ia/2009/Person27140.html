Bertalan Meskó’s Summary

I'm a medical student and a medical blogger with more than 1.1 million readers in one year's time. I plan to pledge my life to personalized genetics, an emerging field between preventive medicine and genomics. I also give slideshows about web 2.0's impact on medical education and healthcare. I try to ease the work of physicians and scientists by recommending useful tools and sites to them and by presenting them the new world of web 2.0.

I've given lectures at several clinics and departments at the Faculty of Medicine of the University of Debrecen. I gave a presentation at the Medicine Meets Virtual Reality Conference (Long Beach, CA in 2008); at the University of Yale, School of Medicine and at the clinics of Greenwich.

Bertalan Meskó’s Specialties:

Creating web 2.0 based medical/scientific sites, blogging, organizing information, communication and e-education.
