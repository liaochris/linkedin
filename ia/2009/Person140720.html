Libby Duane-Adams’s Summary

Libby is the EVP of marketing and founding partner of SRC a leading developer and provider of geographic business intelligence technology solutions.

Along with co-founders, Libby knew she could make a difference in the spatial business intelligence industry by combining software and data to match business requirements with easier to use, faster to deploy and more affordable Geographic Business Intelligence.

Libby has been SRC's lead salesperson, closing 23% of revenue since its founding in 1997. She has helped Ford Motor, Comcast, AT&T Mobility, NSA Media and Clear Channel Outdoor, design and deploy full-scale business intelligence solutions.

Prior to SRC, Libby was a leading sales representative for the media, advertising, telecommunications and automotive industries at Strategic Mapping, a provider of spatial analytics and mapping software. She sold early renditions of desktop-based geographic business intelligence solutions before the Web emerged as the most effective tool for deployment.

Libby also served as an account manager at Donnelley Marketing Information Services in Stamford, CT where she was responsible for customer acquisition in the advertising, media and telecommunications industries. In this role, she was responsible for defining both the company's national sales growth and vertical-specific product development.

Prior to Donnelley, Libby was a client service associate within the IMS division of VNU, which was the 2nd largest information resources holding company in the media and publishing industries in the U.S. At VNU, she was responsible for supporting the company's user base regarding the use of the product and interpretation of the key media information resources used in the development of marketing and media strategy planning.

Libby earned her BA from Castleton State College in Vermont. She owns a custom-painted 2006 Harley-Davidson Road King and enjoys attending motorcycle rallies and taking road trips across the country.
