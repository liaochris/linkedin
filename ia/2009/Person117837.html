John Pask’s Summary

John Pask is currently Managing Member of MotionHR and CEO of HRnexus.

He is a former Principal (2003-2006) of Towers Perrin SAP HR practice lead and Sales Director of the ERP Practice including SAP and Oracle Enterprise/PeopleSoft. He founded Delphi Consultants, LLC in 1994 and developed it into one of the largest and most respected SAP HR consulting firms in North America. Towers Perrin acquired Delphi in 2003.

John has been engagement partner on over 40 SAP HR implementations including: Starwood Hotels, Burger King, Bristol-Meyers Squibb, BHP Billiton, Hunt Consolidated, Ogilvy & Mather, Lufthansa Airlines, Westlake Chemicals, Ulta Cosmetics & Salons, Dover Elevator, County of Sacramento and Chevron Phillips Chemical. John has extensive experience in the governance and delivery of large HR implementation projects including MSA, Genesys and SAP Human Capital Management. He has dedicated almost 20 years working with clients on the design and implementation of human resource systems.

Before founding Delphi, John worked for major two consulting firms where he worked on HRIS implementations as well as directing sales and marketing efforts. For Coopers & Lybrand (PWC), he served as a manager for the project and outsourcing solutions practice in Dallas. At EDS, John served as a HRIS systems engineer on the planning and implementation team for a new human resource information system. Additionally, John was a programmer/analyst at the Dallas Independent School District.

Mr. Pask earned a bachelor’s degree in business administration with a specialization in management information systems from Texas A&M University in 1984. In addition to his managerial duties, Mr. Pask has served on numerous civic and professional organizations including SAP America National Partner Advisory Council, American SAP Users Group and Richardson Chamber of Commerce. John lives in Dallas with his wife, daughter and son.
