Andrew Mooers’s Summary

Have listed, marketed and sold real estate for nearly 30 years. Goals to keep promoting property and putting Houlton Maine / Southern Aroostook on the map in blogs, websites, videos, podcasts and quality performance. Raising family in a friendly small town community is something I am grateful for. Mainers are hardworking, respectful, helpful. To make Maine grow and a better place to live, work and play, it takes networking with leaders and movers/shakers. Anxious to link up and do business or promotion with others with "Fire in the their belly" that are passionate, excited about Maine...the way life should be.

Andrew Mooers’s Specialties:

Especially enjoy listing and selling farms and land. Grew up on a large farm and know the lifestyle. Have helped first time home buyers, lakeshore clients, land developers, small business owners and all age buyers purchase and market Maine property. Past president of Rotary, Aroostook County Realtor Council, Local Chamber of Commerce, Growth Councils, and a Paul Harris Fellow. Past Zoning Board Chairman and served on local hospital boards. Member of Elks.
