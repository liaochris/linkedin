Chad Haas’s Summary

PROFILE

Senior sales & marketing executive with proven track record driving market share, revenue & profit growth, combined with an innate ability to build and maintain strong client relationships. Highly effective at leading change and creating a sense of urgency to build high-performance teams in competitive business environments.

My unique talent is my keen ability to create new markets, find better solutions to complex problems and stay ahead of the curve to seize what others might consider underdeveloped or unconfirmed opportunities & exploiting them.

Proven history of marketing new products & brands to position them strategically for maximum profitability and visibility (appearences on MSNBC, Forbes, Robb Report, and Smart Money are among some of the national media coverage received).

HIGHLIGHTS INCLUDE:

1. Business Turnaround & Culture Change - Led turnaround of several organizations that had not met expectations. Overcame strong resistance from employees comfortable with the status quo by establishing a clear vision/strategy to lead a complete turnaround in both business results & culture.

2. Revenue and Profit Growth - Transformed commodity-based sales organizations into high performance, margin driven, customer-orientated teams to drive sales – from $450MM to $493MM.

3. Business Development & Sales Leadership – Personally closed contracts up to $15MM with The Home Depot, Lowes, Masco, PetSmart & other Fortune 500 companies where I built and maintained outstanding client relationships.

4. Transforming Customer Service: Resurrected strained relationships with customers and transformed commodity-based “order-taking” organizations into customer-orientated teams by focusing on delivering exceptional and consistent service.

5. Creating Innovative Products & Solutions - Inventive and resourceful at finding a better way to achieve competitive differentiation, accelerate revenue, drive profitability, and avoid costly problems.

Chad Haas’s Specialties:

Marketing, Public Relations, Product Development, Branding, Media, Strategic Alliances, and Sales [Chief Marketing Officer and Chief Sales Officer activities].
