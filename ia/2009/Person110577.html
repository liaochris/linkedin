Jeremy Knight’s Summary

20 years in publishing and events primarily focused on the private equity, venture capital, corporate finance, technology and owner managed sectors developing brands and titles including FutureFest, a festival of the future and Real Deals, the award winning private equity magazine. I established the UK Private Equity Awards with the BVCA, the European Private Equity Awards with the EVCA and developed a suite of events and online information tools for the private equity industry. I built the most profitable division of Caspian Publishing and was highly commended for the PPA Publisher of the Year Award in 2003. I speak fluent French and sold over a quarter of a million records during a music career in France spanning the 1980’s.

Jeremy Knight’s Specialties:

B2B Magazine Publishing, Conference/Awards/Event management, Financial management, Sponsorship/Advertising/Subscription Sales, Online and offline Marketing, Project management, Recruitment, Partnering, Purchasing
