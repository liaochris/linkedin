Jennifer Norman’s Summary

Jennifer Norman brings over 15 years of marketing management and sales experience from the consumer products industry. She has launched powerful brands and led comprehensive marketing initiatives for L’Oreal, Victoria’s Secret, Neutrogena, and most recently BORBA.

Jennifer’s management experience is broad, ranging from health & beauty to IT, small entrepreneurships and turnaround situations to mature, established enterprises. Having had direct P&L responsibility for up to $178 million in sales, Jennifer has a solid track record of developing & executing winning ideas, leading cross-functional teams and achieving financial targets. She has experience managing distribution in multiple channels: mass retail, prestige department store, specialty retail and direct sales through QVC, infomercial DRTV and e-commerce. Additionally, Jennifer has managed numerous marketing teams, restructured & led a prestige retail sales force, directed PR, customer service, web, creative, packaging, market research and regulatory teams, developed winning TV, print, online & DRTV ad campaigns, organized multinational supply chains, managed short & long term forecasts and developed distribution in Canada & Europe. Jennifer also has experience as licensor in the beverage industry.

Ms. Norman’s work experience includes Vice President of Marketing & Sales at BORBA, Inc., Product Director at Neutrogena Corporation, Marketing Manager at Victoria’s Secret Catalogue, Marketing Manager at L’Oreal Paris, Sales Manager at Weadon Printing & Design and Marketing Manager at Advanced Communication Systems.
