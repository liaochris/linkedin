Avery Fisher’s Summary

Generalist marketing and technology consultant specializing in creative and technical project management.

Many organizations suffer execution lags because of break downs between the creative and technical sides of their organization. I often act as a conduit between the client, the programmers, and the creatives to streamline execution.

This is one of many roles I play for my clients in the United States, Canada, Europe, and Asia. Sometimes I get hands-on, processing analytics to develop demographic profiles or designing the front-end for an e-commerce initiative, while other times, I may reach out to my network of talented professionals to solve a variety of technical and creative challenges that can't be handled by a client organization in-house.
