MIA YOUNG’s Summary

MIA YOUNG is the Owner & President of MB Personnel & Screening, Ltd., who draws upon twenty years of experience in Human Resources consulting, recruiting, staffing and placement to find the best possible fit between job offering and job candidate.

Mia is acutely aware of what makes people unique and underscores their qualifications and attributes with accurate, emphatic recommendations. On the employer side, Mia smooths out what can be a very rigorous process, saving companies hundreds of ours in time in arriving at the highest caliber of individual for consideration. To make the case for hiring, she presents a thorough analysis with recommendations on each candidate's skill sets, work ethic, and cumulative experience. She hunts down references and does background checks to take any potential liability out of the equation. The goal is to present the most qualified and talented professional and position him/her for immediate success.

On the candidate level, her approach is disarming, nurturing, and interview-based. The nature of the industry dictates incredible selectivity in the screening process and Mia is highly accomplished in the process of isolating the most qualified individuals. Her true gift is in talent evaluation and recognizing that one sparkling resume. From that point, the wheels are set in motion.

Mia is a true advocate of those involved in the hiring dynamic, and people in general, who draws on her superior powers of observation and capability to match talent with need.
