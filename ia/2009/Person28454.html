William A. ("Bill") Lederer’s Summary

Successful C-Level digital media executive with extensive build-up and turnaround expertise in both entrepreneurial and established companies in Internet, TV, video, mobile, advertising/marketing, and information services seeks accountable opportunity to profitably grow data intensive, technology-enabled business. Possesses unique ability to think and act strategically in emerging technologies and markets while implementing tactically with urgency by creating high performance teams and partnerships, solving problems, communicating persuasively, and implementing viable operational and financial solutions to create shareholder value. Former board member for public and private companies and non-profits, both foreign and US. Extensive work in Americas, Europe, India and Israel.

Work best and gain the most satisfaction when building teams to accomplish challenging goals borne of smart strategies that give competitors difficulty sleeping at night. Used to working under constrained circumstances. Greatest success and personal pay-off for those with whom I have worked have always come from opportunities that require vision, passion, leadership, exceptionally creative sales and marketing, strong financial skills, and unremitting, disciplined execution.

My experience is in: leadership, general management, operations, finance, capital allocation, analysis, negotiation, business development, corporate development, strategic planning, partnering, budgeting, sales management, marketing management, technology, media and marketing research, human resources and recruitment, equity and debt financing, mergers and acquisitions, integration, and management consulting including extensive US and international turnaround and build-up experience.
