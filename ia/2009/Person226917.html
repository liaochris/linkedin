Susan Cato’s Summary

Web, new media and information strategy professional with 20 years of experience working in communications, publishing, and information management. I am an innovative yet practical web and information management leader with extensive experience integrating organizational strategy, business needs, technology, content, and conversation. It is my belief that we all have it in us to be creative, innovative, and incredibly resourceful if we focus on 'doing great things' for our communities and each other. My responsibilities have included visionary leadership of internet strategies, management of web application development efforts, oversight of corporate infrastructure and capital assets, strategic management of information and knowledge assets, and publishing operations management. I have an excellent track record of leading teams of technology and other professionals with diverse backgrounds and talents to achieve amazing results - and have fun while doing it!
