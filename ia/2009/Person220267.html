Sonya Beam’s Summary

The P.O.P. Shop and it's team of marketing superheroes provides creative printing and unique promotional products to build brands. The superhero-themed promotions firm blends creative printing, packaging, promotions and branded products to build respected brands. The company can design and develop powerful direct mail, sales kits, point-of-purchase programs, promotions or internal branding. A single source allows clients to get back to marketing and growing their brand rather than managing multiple vendors. The P.O.P. Shop’s creative marketing expertise is where clients will find the most added value, and view the team of superheroes as an extension of their marketing department.
