Marc Charlier’s Summary

After starting at BCT in november 1990 as after sales, I changed in 1996 to customer marketeer and in 2000 to product manager. In the start in 2000 I focused on 1 product; HotDocs documentmanagement. This softwaretool for documentmanagement aimed for secretaries. After a very succesfull introduction, we moved HotDocs in 2004 to our 'sistercompany' Pro/Future. I then started a broader product management in our company. Since the first of January my job title has become marketing manager.
My speciality is networking and give presentations to (large) groups. My goal is to get BCT from a European player in the ECM market to a global player in the ECM market.
