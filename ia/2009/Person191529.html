Ted Hunter’s Summary

The goal is to find contract employment promoting goods and services through association with sanctioned drag racing while utilizing vast industry experience in the high tech industry.

Experience includes project management experience in the construction industry which includes being responsible for the timely and profitable completion of major construction projects with skills including CPM, Project Management, HR, PR, Purchasing, Contract Negotiations, Change orders, Invoicing and Payroll.

Experience in IT field includes founding the Network Plus Certification for CompTIA, Hardware and Software best practices, HR, PR, Marketing, Partnering, Creation of Channel and Direct Programs, Technical Writing, working with the IT Media, establishing manufacturing relationships, and Product Management and Placement.
