Matt Hackett’s Summary

Affinity Business Referral Association! (ABRA)

ABRA generates quality sales referrals for business owners and sales professionals from various industries with innovative technology and traditional word-of-mouth networking events in local communities.

Each local chapter allows only one member, per professional industry to join. This helps eliminate internal competition and fosters loyal and lasting business to business relationships. In addition to the dynamic 'members only' website, members benefit by attending 90 minute networking events held every other week.

Contact me today to learn more about the benefits of membership, starting a chapter in your community or joining our growing team! http://www.goabra.com
