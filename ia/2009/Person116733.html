John Caracciolo’s Summary

I am the President of a business that owns and operates radio stations on Long Island. Our unique organization employs over 50 people and has been doing business for over 20 years. Our goal is to provide effective and customized marketing and advertising services to companies throughout Long Island via our media outlets. We have many valuable partners, clients, stakeholders and employees and enjoy doing business on Long Island. Our goal is to provide exceptional service and results for our clients, make an impact on our audience with thought provoking and entertaining media services and to hopefully provide and Island where our children can live and prosper.

John Caracciolo’s Specialties:

Branding, marketing and producing non traditional revenue events that help promote business on Long Island

John Caracciolo’s Experience

  • Chairman

    Suffolk County Planning Commission

    (Government Agency; 11-50 employees; Government Administration industry)

    January 2002 — Present (7 years 5 months)

    Each town and village in Suffolk County that has the jurisdiction to adopt or amend zoning regulations, issue special permits and variances, or approve subdivision plats and condominiums must refer to the Planning Commission any municipal zoning/subdivision action that would affect real property lying within one mile of an airport or a nuclear power plant or within five hundred feet from a number of other set boundaries.
    The Planning Commission's primary interest in conducting its review is to protect the public interest and investment. It also provides local officials with the benefits of the planning expertise of the County Planning Department staff and the views of the County Planning Commission on inter-municipal and area-wide impacts of proposed municipal actions.
