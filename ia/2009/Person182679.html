Paul Bertrand’s Summary

After having worked in a governmental research lab in underwater acoustic during 5 years, I have created my first company in 1990 to develop realtime acoustic processing for the French navy.

In 1998 I created Watteco where has been invented the WPC concept that allows low power communication through power line.

Name inventor on 5 patents, I have more than 15 publications at my name. Experience on a variety of French and international projects.

Long experience of management and coordination of research and development projects dealing with energy control and efficiency, ICTs and services, and involving international and multidisciplinary teams.

Financial exec with broad experience in all aspects of accounting, auditing and financial management.
Direct experience with venture capital. Interested in early-stage startups as well as companies “on the bubble.”

As a CEO, I have all the necessary expertise and skills to carry out efficiently global coordination tasks, costs and results follow-up.

I speak French and i am fluent in English and Spanish
