Andrew Clark’s Summary

My two passions fit into these two companies. I enjoy working and developing relationships with people, helping them assess their circumstances, whether real estate or not-for-profit. My strength of being able to develop a plan and strategy for peoples' needs give me great satisfaction and drive to deliver results that are rewarding, impactful, and rich.
