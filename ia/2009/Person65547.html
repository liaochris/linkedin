Don Schmidt’s Summary

Twenty-five years in the publishing industry with an emphasis on operations, departmental management, database formulation, customer service and using an array of software programs to achieve production/manufacturing expectations on time and under budget while exceeding quality goals.

Don Schmidt’s Specialties:

My experience with purchasing type, printing/binding from domestic and overseas vendors (4/color and 1/color), inventory management, design and all facets of vendor contracting and negotiation. With experience encompassing 24 years in the publishing industry, as well as the founding and operating of my own book manufacturing consulting firm, I am well versed in operations management, customer service, and achieving goals on time, under budget and with the highest quality.
