Dick Hoffmann’s Summary

I help technology, healthcare and pharmaceutical organizations deliver performance, growth, differentiation and innovation through the disciplines of business development, alliance performance management, team collaboration, channel development and workforce development.

In addition to my business collaboration expertise, my technical specialties include 16 years of enterprise solution marketing, sales and delivery spanning business intelligence, data warehousing, enterprise data integration, Web-based collaboration, document management and knowledge management.

I served from 2001 through 2004 on the global Board of Directors of the Association of Strategic Alliance Professionals (ASAP) as vice chairman of the board responsible for strategic planning. I founded the Silicon Valley Chapter of ASAP in March 2000 and served as the Chapter president through September 2001.

In 2008, I was appointed to a two-year term on the Capital Area Workforce Development Board serving Wake and Johnston counties in North Carolina.

Dick Hoffmann’s Specialties:

Business Integration – pharmaceutical, biotech and technology alliances, M&A, supply / demand chain optimization and related business performance management

Product / Solution Commercialization – design, development, commercialization, implementation and integration of technology-based solutions and science-based products

Business Development – building and executing business development strategies and leading teams through complex marketing and sales initiatives
