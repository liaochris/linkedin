Ross Franklin’s Summary

Ross Franklin has consistently built on the notable success he has achieved in the field of Fitness Club Management over the past decade. Mr. Franklin’s current position with the new Gold's Gym in Old Bridge New Jersey has grown directly from his previous experience with four of the top players in the highly competitive New York/ New Jersey health club market: Club H Fitness Centers, Equinox Fitness, New York Sports Clubs, and Chelsea Piers.

Since 2007, Mr. Franklin has served as General Manager and Director of New Club Openings with Club H, where he already successfully opened two locations with record-breaking presale revenue, overseeing operations from construction through organizing and hosting the grand opening celebration event. His excellent management skills allowed him to direct his sales staff to accomplish impressive results, attracting over 1700 new members in midtown Manhattan and 3000 in Jersey City, where break-even was reached within 3 months of opening.

Prior to that, at Equinox Fitness where he served as General Manager of the trendy Union Square and Upper East Side locations, he was lauded for his extraordinary club manage­ment ability, creating a positive atmosphere for people to work or to workout! Staff, trainers, vendors, members and guests all responded to his upbeat management style, and he fostered tremendous goodwill between the various departments including Fitness, Personal Training, Group Fitness classes, Spa, and Pro-shop. Mr. Franklin was responsible for all club operations, overseeing 150+ employees and man­aging annual rev­enue over $13 million.
