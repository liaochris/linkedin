Amy Herrick’s Summary

Amy Herrick performs emerging market investment research for Freedom Capital Partners. She also has experience managing global projects related to asset rationalization. Prior to that, Ms. Herrick worked abroad at Advention Business Partners, a boutique management consulting firm in Paris where she performed due diligence and market sizing on a wide range of European companies. She also has extensive professional research and analytical experience in subject areas ranging from the Gulf State economies to West African cotton subsidy programs to the French media from previous work with think tanks (CSIS) and other non-governmental organizations (IRG). During her Masters degree she led an international research team to Hong Kong for the World Trade Organization to gather research for an article that was later published.

Ms. Herrick has overseas experience living, studying, and working in Europe, the Middle East and Asia.

Academic background: Concentrations in Conflict and Security Policy, Political Economy, French Studies.

Ms. Herrick is fluent in French and has spent 2 years studying Arabic, notably at the Universite Saint Joseph in Beirut, Lebanon.
