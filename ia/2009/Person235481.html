Tom Andel’s Summary

An editorial content creator and manager with more than 25 years of experience. Career focus on putting management best-practices into relevant context for all audiences. Proven reputation as skilled communicator, both for online and in-print media, as well as for in-person events such as live conferences and webcasts. My goal is to continue focusing on generating content for professional development and business improvement.

Tom Andel’s Specialties:

Logistics and manufacturing technology, warehousing and distribution center management, supply chain software, industrial packaging, environmental sustainability.

Tom Andel’s Experience

  • Modern Materials Handling

    (Publishing industry)

    July 2007 — January 2009 (1 year 7 months)
