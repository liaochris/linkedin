Glen Smith’s Summary

At Gyro I work with the creation of visual strategies for our stable of international clients such as ITT, GE Healthcare and Novartis. I also work closely with our in-house digital department, conceptualising, designing and building digital products for our clients, which include Panasonic, Google, WM-Data, Adobe, IDG and Sun Microsystems.

Gyro Scandinavia is part of the Gyro HSR organisation, the UK's largest B2B agency and operational in 11 countries around the world.

Previously I was co-founder of Summer Design, and prior to that AD at Icon Medialab in Stockholm and London.

Glen Smith’s Specialties:

Strong conceptual skills and the ability to translate them to powerful campaigns which are on brand, on time and on budget.
