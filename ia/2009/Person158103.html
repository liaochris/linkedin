Mike McMeans’s Summary

My clients typically have one of four major concerns:

1. Preparation for Retirement - So many people fund their retirement at work or on their own and struggle to know whether they are saving enough to meet their goals.
2. Living Effectively in Retirement - Even for those already retired, there is still much to be done to ensure that your money outlives you and that you have a tax sensitive wealth transfer plan for your children and beneficiaries.
3. Tax Planning - I specialize in tax planning, not tax preparation. Keeping Uncle Sam at bay requires good planning ahead of time.
4. Education Savings - From college education for children and grandchildren to creating family scholarship programs, providing education subsidies for those close to you can be a great way to help encourage success.

Which one of these concerns resonates most with you and your family?
