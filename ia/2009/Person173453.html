Newton Campos’s Summary

Newton is a Brazilian/Portuguese accountant who works with International Marketing, Business Development and Technology since he was 17 years old. After a brief experience at PricewaterhouseCoopers, he lived in Australia (Sydney) to study English and after some more years working in Brazil (São Paulo) with technology for the Publishing Industry, he went to Spain (Madrid) and India (Kolkata) to pursue a world top full-time MBA program at the IE Business School together with the IIM Indian Institute of Management.

He then worked as Marketing Expert at the headquarters of the Telefónica Publishing company in Madrid (Spain) for a year and a half and after a brief moment living in France (Nice), opened the Brazilian office of IE in 2003. Since then, he is in charge of IE’s activities in Brazil. In 2006, Newton became a Ph.D. student at FGV-SP, a Brazilian top Business School and an active member of the Brazilian Green Party (Partido Verde). He also supports the development of a software company for the Publishing Industry and is the co-founder of a restaurant called Aïoli Bistro at Itaim in São Paulo.
