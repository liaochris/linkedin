Ed Buelna’s Summary

Twenty plus years of nursing experience. Focusing, primarily on Urgent/Emergent Care settings.

Management in an acute Urgent Care clinic. Integrating emerging health technology into clinical setting to improve patient throughput and outcomes.

Coordinating development of EHR/PHR system for beta testing in 4Q '09.

Developing a centralized psychiatric access network to facilitate patient referral to behavioral services offered both in & out of their insurance networks.

Working with primary care departments to streamline the treatment of all PAMF patients. Seeking to improve the patient experience, experience of work for the clinical staff and the provider experience of treatment. Beginning a Quality Measures study to accurately measure the diagnosis of depression in primary care patients. Hopeful to establish a committee to advocate for behavioral health professionals within primary care.
