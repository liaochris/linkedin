Andrew Grill’s Summary

Linkedin member since February 2004.

Join my network at http://linkedin.andrewgrill.com

*** Meet me at Mobile World Congress, Barcelona Feb 16 -19 ***

Based in London, Andrew is a mobile advertising evangelist and author of top 50 UK blog London Calling on mobile advertising.

He is also an experienced Senior Telecoms Executive, Business Developer and Marketer with particular expertise in the mobile operator space across Europe with a specific focus on mobile advertising, location and social networking.

He brings to any role a unique mix of sales director, entrepreneur, marketer, rainmaker, mobile evangelist and company spokesperson.

Andrew's career spans over 20 years working in senior positions across the entire value chain and has a deep understanding of the Telco market from vendor, operator, analyst, consultant and media viewpoints.

From November 2004 - July 2008, he was General Manager, Sales and Marketing of leading location provider, Seeker Wireless. Andrew was quickly able to launch Seeker onto the world stage, attracting the attention of the world’s largest mobile operators in the UK, Europe and Asia-Pacific which led to the signing of a number of contracts with mobile operators including Vodafone. Based in London, Andrew was also a regular conference presenter on topics related to location and mobile advertising, and was also the company spokesperson.

Previously, he held senior marketing and management positions with Australian operators Telstra, Optus and mobile messaging experts Legion Interactive. He is also experienced in the online space having developed and launched PropertyLook, the leading Australian commercial property website.

Andrew is currently consulting in the mobile advertising space, connecting companies with smart mobile advertising solutions with brands, agencies, operators and end users to derive the best mobile advertising experience.

Contact Andrew via http://contact.andrewgrill.com
