    Freelance Photographer

    (Photography industry)

    January 2006 — Present (4 years )

    Martine Cotton relocated her photography business to Port Douglas in early 2009 from Tokyo, Japan, where she worked as a photographer and event organiser, staging five exhibitions and taking commissions from a number of different organisations and individual clients.


    Working in the Cairns, Northern Beaches, Port Douglas and Daintree areas, Martine offers photographic and post-production photo services for portraits, weddings, party/conventions, commercial products, real estate, travel, editorial, fashion and beauty, bands and live performances.

     Martine’s clients include Vogue Australia Entertaining and Food magazine, the Port Douglas and Mossman Gazette newspaper, Events NQ, Managing Australian Destinations, Port Douglas Carnivale 2009, The Courier Mail, Sassi Balé, Ulysses Getaways,  www.portdouglasrealestate.com, Metropolis Magazine (Tokyo), Snip Magazine (Tokyo), The Hiragana Times (Tokyo), Ping Magazine (Tokyo), www.drsketchystokyo.com, ‘Big in Japan: A Ghost Story’ (Chin Music Press), Audi Car Magazine (Germany) and dozens of model/actor/comedian/musician/band/burlesque performers.

     Martine approaches her subjects in a warm, documentary style, which is rich with emotion and alive with vibrant colour and light and her online portfolio can be visited at martinecotton.com. She offers her clients a range of affordable and versatile photography packages and is proud of her reputation as a professional, reliable, creative and passionate photographer.
