Will Dearman’s Summary

With experience in both banking and entrepreneurship I have a broad perspective of business and finance:

As an entrepreneur, I evaluated hundreds of firms for potential acquisitions, eventually developing an investor group to purchase a firm and make significant investments in two others. I conducted full due diligence on all of the firms with a focus on recurring cash flow and potential for margin enhancement by management.

As an analyst and later a credit products underwriter in Bank of America’s middle market 'General Industries' segment of the Global Bank Debt group, I gained experience with a diverse portfolio of businesses. Covered sectors include commercial distributors, semiconductor manufacturing and development, insurance, not for profits, retail, and energy generation. Of note, our team was the lead bank in a $600MM syndicated finance facility.

Please feel free to contact me directly with any opportunities or questions: linkedin@dearmanventures.com .
