Timothy Donahue’s Summary

Tim Donahue is a trainer and consultant. He brings a combination of academic and practical business experience to your organization. As the Former State Director for the Minnesota Small Business Development Centers (SBDC), he has worked with the profit and non-profit sectors providing consulting, training, and research in the areas of strategic management, marketing, customer service, sale’s training, human relations, and business development.

He has done training and consulting on Marketing and Entrepreneurship for the Veronozh Region in the Russian Federation.

Donahue has owned and operated his own small businesses and knows first hand the difference between educational and hands on business experience. He has received 5 district, 3 regional and 1 national award for outstanding consulting from the Small Business Administration. In addition, the Independent Business Association of Minnesota awarded him the “Small Business Champion” .

As an educator, he has taught a variety of business courses at the graduate and undergraduate level since 1978. His courses are practical. He blends real world applications with solid academic principles to make sure students are equipped with the tools necessary to compete in business.

Donahue’s ability to apply conceptual theories with real world experience makes his training sessions exciting and fun learning experiences. His ability to identify problems quickly enables him to assist client’s work through problems and focus on alternatives that will fit with the client’s unique business culture and personality.
