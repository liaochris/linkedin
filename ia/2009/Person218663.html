Sarah Collins’s Summary

I established my web consultancy business in November 2008 after spending the previous 10 years working across numerous online roles. Roles included web management where I was responsible for setting and implementing online strategy, to project management, where I have managed large scale online projects from specification to go live. My experience has also included managing web teams where I have also been able to take hands on role.
