Steve Curtin’s Summary

Experienced content development executive: Over 15 year track record in creating and working with successful P&Ls, teams and programs for world-class training businesses and content development organizations. Expertise in key training-related domains; instructor-led, eLearning, virtual classrooms and related content to build effective and profitable blended training solutions for customers and partners. Significant experience with growing online communities, technical resource web sites, knowledgebases, certification programs, technology book programs, and sales training programs for companies of all sizes. Strong communications background and leadership abilities. Adept at working with cross-functional teams to solve business problems and achieve corporate goals, including the achievement of revenue and margin targets.

Steve Curtin’s Specialties:

Building and managing teams involved in content development and related programs for technology companies: curriculum development, online communities, eLearning, documentation, certification, developer resource websites, retail technology books.
