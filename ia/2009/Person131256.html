Kevin Pollard’s Summary

I am a seasoned travel professional, having traveled extensively throughout the world both privately as a backpacker and adventure traveler, and professionally as a tour guide with a leading adventure travel company.

I have traveled to over 90 countries and I'm on my third 60 page Australian passport!

I am currently establishing my own adventure travel company - Babel Travel.

My personal experience and achievements outlined below:

• 2 years of Operational Experience while I held the position of Assistant Operations Manager of India, Middle East and Australasia for Intrepid Travel.

• 2 and half years of group leading experience for Intrepid Travel in over 20 countries in the Middle East, Europe and Asia.

• Responsible for managing 25 staff and co managing over 60 during my managerial experience.

• Responsible for designing, pricing and marketing over 25 trips for Intrepid Travel in Asia, Europe and the Middle East.

• Responsible for setting up operations in 12 countries in Europe and the Middle East.

• Over 9 years of backpacking experience to over 90 countries worldwide.

• Almost 2 years of managerial experience at a sports bar in central London.

• 6 months experience as a tour guide on the Greek Island of Rhodes.
