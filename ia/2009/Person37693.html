Carlos Condesso’s Summary

Carlos Condesso is currently the Product Owner (worldwide) of Reporting solutions at Nokia Siemens Networks. He is responsible for business model, product strategy, roadmaps, product positioning and product definition in the area of OSS - Performance Management & Reporting solutions. His leadership has lead to great sales volume growing (40% CAGR), tens of million euros in last fiscal year.

He has several years experience in Telecommunications and he has worked in the field of Performance Management & Reporting for the past ten years, looking firstly at fixed and broadband networks and more latterly into the mobile and access networks. Prior to Nokia Siemens Networks, he occupied various roles in Siemens and Alcatel in the development of OSS applications. He has a degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Technical University of Lisbon.

Carlos Condesso has good knowledge of Telecommunication Networks (Mobile, Wired and Access Technologies), and excellent knowledge of OSS, Network and Service Management platforms. He has a proven track record in business development, business partnerships and product development. He has excellent presentation and communication skills. He is well-organised, analytical and strategic thinker. He is able to manage complex projects and large teams. He is fluent English, Spanish and Portuguese.

Business Development, Business Partnerships, Product Management, Product Life Cycle, Project Management, OSS, COTS, Network Management, Service Management, Performance Management, SQM, Network Optimization, Mobile Networks, Wired Networks, Access Networks, TDM, NGN, xDSL, VoIP, GSM, GPRS, EDGE, 3G, UMTS, IP, ATM.
