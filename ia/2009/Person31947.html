Bram Souffreau’s Summary

¨* Concentra: Metropool-section Gazet van Antwerpen *

From 2006 till 2008 I was a web editor for Concentra, a Belgian publisher.

The last half year of 2008 I worked for the online Metropool-section of the Gazet van Antwerpen. I worked together with the editors of the newspaper and managed the collaboration between print and web.

* Concentra: ActuaNet *

Till September 2008 I was the co-ordinator of Actuanet.

I thought-out the mission of Actuanet and wrote out the strategic plan. I selected the journalists and gave them a training. Actuanet collects news via diverse channels and put it online through www.gva.be and www.hbvl.be.

* Concentra: Gazet van Antwerpen *

From 2006 till December 2007, I was an on line editor for the regional newspaper Gazet van Antwerpen.

* Metro *

I worked for a daily newspaper, Metro, from 2000 till 2006. I wrote articles and edited wire texts for the business section. I was responsible for the weekly technology and multimedia section.

* Objectives *

It's my main goal to write clear, objective and accurate material in order that the reader has all information on a event in a comfortable way.

I work in a very structured way and try to implement the basics of project management on my work. I am able to work in an ad hoc situation. I'm a quick learner and can provide solutions in a flexible manner.

* Journalism *

As a journalist, I did a great variety of tasks: gathering news and information, interviews, writing features, making a selection of news items and reporting on day-to-day news events.

Although I am a 'generalist' and able to cover almost any topic of intrest, I have a special intrest in globalization, Belgian and European politics, telecom, technology and multimedia. I try to follow new trends in and discussions about journalism and new media, too.

I participated in debates about online journalism.

* Writing for the web *

I give regularly courses about writing for the web.
