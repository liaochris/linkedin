Paul Booth’s Summary

An innovative technologist with a strong background working with multiple diverse teams across multiple departments and organizations globally. Possessing strong entrepreneurial skills with experience in organizations from start-ups to academia to Fortune-500. Proven track record of product launches, on time, to scope and cost. Launched large scale global projects and small community projects. Proven time-management skills with ability to work well under pressure and meet tight deadlines. Possessing a solid academic grounding in Computer Science and Software Engineering, with hands-on experience of Product Management, Business and Marketing. Published in over 22 national and international conferences and professional bodies’ proceedings. Internet evangelist and specialist since 1993.
