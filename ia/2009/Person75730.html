  • Graduate Research Assistant

    University of Texas at Austin

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    August 2005 — Present (4 years 4 months)

    ** Ge‐SixGe1‐x core‐shell nanowire tunneling FET (TFET)

    1. Investigate design considerations which are junction doping abruptness, gate overlap & underlap, drain/source doping imbalance, and its effect on ambipolar characteristics by 3D device simulations.

    2. Develop the detailed process for implant energy, implant dose, annealing conditions in order to optimize the doping properties for TFET.

    ** Multiscale modeling of dopant profiles with improved defects and dopant dynamics models (SRC Project)

    1. Perform first principles diffusion modeling with density functional theory (DFT) to understand the fundamental behaviors and properties of defect and dopant complexes in Si and SiGe.

    2. Implement DFT‐based diffusion models of defect and dopant for kinetic Monte Carlo (KMC) simulation and validated the results with the design of experiments.

  • Graduate Research Intern

    SEMATECH

    (Non-Profit; Semiconductors industry)

    June 2007 — July 2009 (2 years 2 months)

    1. Characterize process integration issues of HK/MG SiGe/Ge n‐ and pMOSFETs by electrical measurement and TCAD simulation/modeling, especially for interface traps, parasitic series resistance, strained SiGe/Si heterojunctions, and reliabilities (HCI and NBTI).

    2. Investigate dopant diffusion & activation, strain characteristics in SiGe/Si heterojunction, and novel annealing technique in order to optimize the parasitic series resistance in Si/SiGe/Si heterojunction channel pMOSFETs by experimental characterization and kinetic Monte Carlo (KMC) process simulation/modeling.

    3. Investigate an inverse modeling in order to predict 2D dopant profiles with electrical characterization and TCAD simulation in HK/MG Si CMOS.

    4. Optimize strain‐induced dopant diffusion and effective channel stress in dual stressor liner (DSL) CMOS by TCAD simulation.

    5. Characterize dopant diffusion and activation in order to optimize underlap LDD and epitaxially grown S/D in FinFETs by 3D process and device simulation.
