Huub Waterval’s Groups:

Co-founder of PleinU. Platform electronic employment of temporary staff. The Platform electronic employment of temporary staff (in short: ‘PleinU’) can be described as a ‘place of gathering’ of staffing agencies and companies they work for. Goal of PleinU is to stimulate the use of international open XML standards for the electronic communication between parties in the temporary staffing industry.

PleinU was founded on April 20, 2004 in a meeting organized by ECP.NL. Participants of the platform are large staffing customers and temporary staffing agencies, a limited number of consultants (who have already proven their use in the field of HR-XML / SIDES and ebXML) and ECP.NL who facilitates the platform.

The customer mentioned was Essent, the Temporary staffing agencies were Adecco & Vedior and the consultants were from Capgemini who all worked together on the Essent/Adecco/Vedior Pilot. Since the implementation more then 150.000 electronic time cards have been processed.
