Therefore, we are sending letters to the appropriate District Attorneys, CA Attorney General Candidates, Governor Schwarzenegger, and other Government Officials regarding the establishment of a legal medical marijuana business. We will also ask that these matters be resolved immediately, as given recent trends, we believe that unlicensed dispensaries and cartels will continue to serve the needs of medical marijuana patients if legal establishments do not.
