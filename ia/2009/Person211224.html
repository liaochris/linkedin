Scott Bloom’s Summary

The three most used words by all the firms at my past reviews I’ve worked for during my career were dependability, attitude & organized. I feel these are three traits that are my strengths and that would work well in the office place.

Dependability, when someone gives me a deadline for a project they know from my character traits that I am someone they can depend on to get the job done, get it right and most importantly get it done on time.

Attitude, when asked to work on several jobs, change deadlines in midstream, help out another team with their deadline and all the scenarios that come up in an architectural firm I always do this with no complaining.

Organized, my primary strength is organization. This trait has lent itself extremely well to my previous firms when developing Construction Administration tracking methods along with the formation of office standards from filing measures, computer-directory organization, and AutoCAD/Microstation procedures.

That’s the highlight of a few of the traits that make me a serious candidate for a position at your firm.
