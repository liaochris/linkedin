Kent Speakman’s Summary

Kent Speakman is a film maker as well as award-winning online strategist, social media consultant, blogger, and speaker.

He has helped fast-growing companies effectively navigate the new world of online media, websites, blogs, podcasts, widgets, online communities, social networks and developed branded entertainment content for the companies he consults with.

Kent works on a variety of film and television projects with his entertainment consulting company, Speakman Entertainment, he started in the late 90's. Kents company produces television and film projects as well as assists select public figures with their online identity management & publicity.

With a decade's experience in strategic marketing and communications combined with a hand on background in operations and management, he is happy to be working within his core skill sets and passions.

Kent understands it is paramount to understand a customer and create an experience that delivers a strong ROI. He has the opportunity to work with clients in multiple industries and learn the interworking of their organizations. He engages new clients/partners on both national and international levels and leads collaboration to formulate a strategy to reach organizational objectives.. He has worked with a wide range of clients in the Entertainment, Finance, Tourism & Hospitality, Luxury, Energy/Oil & Gas and Non profit sectors. Kent has developed digital media plans for over a dozen TV properties and films.

Speakman also acts as an Ambassador for the Greenlight Community. As an advocate and friend to Keith Ferrazzi, the author of New York Times Bestsellers “Never Eat Alone” and “Who’s Got Your Back”, he assists the online community members in developing relationships, and helps to host networking events across Canada and the USA.
