Tito Melega’s Summary

Tito is an award-wining advertising Creative Director, Art Director, Director and
Creative Consultant.

Tito’s experience runs the gamut in almost every brand category. Throughout his career, he has worked on the world’s most recognizable brands some of which include: Lexus, Acura, Toyota, Pepsi, United Airlines, Cisco Systems,
DirecTV, Miller Lite, Holiday Inn, Clairol, Kodak, and Beringer Wines.

In his position as Creative Director, Tito consistently helps brands reach their targets in meaningful ways and has a keen understanding of what’s relevant to today’s audiences. Utmost is Tito’s strong dedication to his clients by delivering online content that is entertaining, engaging and memorable.

He most recently created an innovative national advertising campaign for Toyota’s 3rd Generation 2010 Prius. Tito’s launch work for the completed redesigned flagship vehicle continues to receive accolades from both the viewing public and trades.

Examples of Tito's work can be viewed at titomelega.com.

Tito currently lives in Los Angeles, California
