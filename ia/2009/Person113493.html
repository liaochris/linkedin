Jim Veilleux’s Summary

VirtualLogger LLC is the first and only significant "software as a service" play in the call recording and quality monitoring space. We provide high-powered solutions for contact centers using a pay-as-you-go model that lets contact centers leverage their existing technology to the maximum, while minimizing their capital investment and the risk of changing technology.

Our approach to call recording, screen capture, quality monitoring and agent coaching is a "plays well with others" SOA-oriented design that makes us a good fit for both the very smallest call centers, and also huge, multi-location call center operations.

Jim is the CEO and company founder. Over 20 years in telecommunications and Internet business. Strong sales and marketing background, with ability to translate technology into marketable products and services.
