Scott Stroud’s Summary

I've been in new home sales, sales management, and sales training since 1978, and worked in new home construction before that. I was VP of Sales & Marketing for a home producer for 10 years and director of marketing for another for 2 years. I now work as an associate with J. Rouleau & Associates LLC as a consultant to new home sales organizations, as well as serve as program director for The BuilderRadio Media Network, and as Co-Host of Selling New Homes: The Monday Morning Sales Meeting. Current projects include a group coaching program offered to home producers who sell their products through builder/dealers. My goals are to remain active with my current partners and to grow both the BuilderRadio network and the coaching program, and to enjoy time with my beautiful wife of 30 years, Melanie.

Scott Stroud’s Specialties:

Builder/Dealer coaching; sales training; seminar leader on new home sales topics; internet marketing, blogging and podcasting; social marketing.
