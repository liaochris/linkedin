Daniel O'Neil’s Summary

I have forged strong working teams and directed projects for more than 20 years in the Americas, Africa and Asia, including ten years split between Haiti and the Dominican Republic. I have led projects that improved the dynamic of the Haitian-Dominican border, established the community-based disaster system now used by all NGOs in Haiti, developed detailed training programs, developed numerous proposals, implemented dozens of rural infrastructure projects, and had a great time doing it.

Directed projects funded through USAID, World Bank and directly by host countries. Worked in the Dominican Republic, Haiti, Vietnam, Ethiopia, Guinea-Conakry, Guinea-Bissau, Sierra Leone, and Togo.

Daniel O'Neil’s Specialties:

Project Management, Social Media, Disaster Preparedness and Response, Institutional Strengthening, Cross-Border Relations, Public Outreach, Rural Infrastructure, Project Conception and Start-up, USAID Regulations, French, Creole, Spanish
