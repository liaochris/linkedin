Lindsey Kracum’s Summary

I am currently the interactive media buyer for Intelligent Beauty. I do the planning, negotiating, and optimizing for their 4 core brands: iQDerma, RAWNaturals, Sensa Weight Loss and Kronos Hair Care. All products utilize DM techniques with order conversions as the end goal. Optimization is based on meeting monthly CPA goals by product.

Prior to Intelligent Beauty, I was working part-time as a marketing coordinator for BLITZ Interactive Agency. I work on the FX and Naked Juice accounts. I also help with strategy and market research for potential new clients.

I began my professional experience at Team One advertising. I was with the company for 2 and a half years, starting as an assistant. I was promoted to a planner after 10 months. Ten months later, I was given the opportunity to work on the digital side as an interactive media planner for Lexus.
