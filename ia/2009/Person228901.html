Tammy Scholtes’s Summary

I help plan high-impact business events, from exclusive invitation-only gatherings to international conferences for thousands by recruiting the world's most influential thought leaders for public lectures and speaking engagements.

From concept through completion, I can help plan your event as well as help manage delegate recruitment to populate the event with the ideal attendees. My 15+ years experience includes overseeing every aspect of an event including: budgeting, scouting the right venue, developing the agenda, recruiting speakers, coordinating front of house, security, sound and lighting, live television productions, sponsorships, catering and publicity for your event.

I am a high energy individual who's organized and detail-oriented at multi-tasking several on-going events at once. I deliver polished events on time and under budget and can put you in touch with influential contacts, provide you with sales meetings, hold them on your behalf if required, market your product and use PR and trade press contacts to assist in promoting your event.

I have influential contacts with senior level executives from global corporations and strongly believe relationship-building is the key to everyone's success which is why I devote time each week to networking.
