Richard H. Tholen MD’s Summary

Over 20 years in private practice at Minneapolis Plastic Surgery, Ltd., focusing on cosmetic surgery of the breast, face, and body. National and international lecturer and faculty on topics in cosmetic surgery and laser techniques in surgery. One of the first private cosmetic surgery practices in the state to offer a complete accredited in-office surgical facilitiy with full anesthesia services. Virtually all of the cosmetic surgeries we perform are done safely and comfortably with state-of-the-art anesthesia techniques that allow outpatient care of our patients, reducing not only costs, but exposure to hospital infections. One of the first cosmetic surgery practices to own and operate a skin and body care clinic (Carillon Clinic, Ltd.) which offers laser hair removal, Botox and other injectable skin fillers, facial peels, microdermabrasion, Endermologie, spider vein removal, and cosmetic/pharmaceutical skin care products in a spa-like setting staffed by trained and experienced nurses and estheticians--over ten years of excellence in 2 locations.
