Dave Garrett’s Summary

Co-Founder and CEO of gantthead.com, a well known and profitable on-line publication since early 2000. Gantthead.com is the world's largest dedicated community of IT Project Managers. We are growing profitably by 30% per year through partnerships, acquisitions, and natural extensions of our brand.

Dave Garrett’s Specialties:

Significant expertise in online publishing, project management, and application development best practices.

Related expertise: business development, strategic marketing, partnership development, product development, business planning, and technology consulting.
