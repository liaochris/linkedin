Gabriel Tezier’s Summary

Gabriel is a nomad character who travelled and worked in the 5 continents since the early 80’s.

He studied sciences and engineering but his love for nature and freedom took him to the remote mountains and seas of this world and in 1987, he became a guide in the desert of West Africa and lead rock climbing and trekking expeditions.

Realizing the precarious situation of populations across the planet, he will dedicate next 10 years to karma yoga. In 1997, he discovered the practice of Aikido and realized that it was much more than a martial art.

In 2001 he moves to India and discovers Indian philosophy and Yoga and in 2006 decides to dedicate himself entirely to Yoga, Aikido and Meditation.

Certified teacher from Yogalife and from Integrated South Pacific Yoga Academy, Gabriel continues to lead a nomadic life but dedicate most of his time to the travel within and walking the difficult path that leads to the discovery of the self.

His yoga can be energetic or peaceful. He draws simultaneously from years of intense sport practice, his experience as a trainer and a guide in multicultural setting and the Peace that he has gathered amidst the turbulence that life took him through.
