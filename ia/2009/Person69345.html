Ed Cholfin’s Summary

We understand the value of your investment and the importance of a major decision such as a home renovation. With an outstanding team of project managers, designers, and administrative staff, and a core group of trade professionals, we’ve built a solid reputation in a competitive market, with the majority of our clients resulting from referrals. We work as a single team with a single goal - our client's complete satisfaction!
We’ve also been recognized as an industry expert, having been featured in Today’s Custom Home, Kitchen & Bath Design News, Northside News, Atlanta INTown Paper, WC Magazine, North Atlanta Living and TrendIdeas.com. Our design professionals are sought after by major industry publications looking to feature their work and wanting to get their input on market and design trends! When you're ready to start your project - go straight to the source of the trends - AK!

AK Complete Home Renovations can make your dream home a reality with our superior turnkey services. We offer complete home renovations, our extensive portfolio also includes decks, sunrooms, basements and additions while our specialties remain kitchens and baths. AK team members understand that in order for a home renovation to be successful from all perspectives, we must provide you with all the options to make the best decisions. The result is an enhanced, personalized experience for you and your family.
Our award-winning commitment facilitates a distinctive and straight forward renovation experience. From the company president to the design professional to the project manager, we maintain an open line of communication with you using online tools, face-to-face meetings and telephone conferences, always aware of what best suits your schedule and preferences. At the end of the day, our work is 100% guaranteed because we are truly vested in the integrity of your home renovation project.
