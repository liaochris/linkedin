Jim Browell’s Summary

Masters in Mechanical Engineering with extensive project management experience in product design, development, and production support of rubber and plastic components; Expertise in the use of Computer Aided Engineering (CAE/FEA) to characterize materials, optimize design (DFSS and DOE), support testing, and improve reliability; Expertise in metal, plastic, and rubber product durability prediction and validation including LCF, HCF, and aging; Expertise in minimizing NVH; Ability to minimize resource expenditures while meeting engineering objectives and quality system standards; Provide technical support and presentations for sales and management reviews; Skilled at aligning strategic planning and resource portfolio with business objectives.

Jim Browell’s Specialties:

• Designing thermoplastic and rubber material components utilizing complex assembly and deformation predictions, including effects due to geometric instabilities, cycling, and aging.
• Correlating FEA and numerical method prediction with testing and field experience, and using the results to develop design guidelines.
• Utilizing Design for Six Sigma (DFSS) and Design of Experiments (DoE) for design optimization.
