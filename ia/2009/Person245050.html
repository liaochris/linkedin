Venkata Sivashankar’s Summary

Venkata Sivashankar is currently a senior architect at Interwoven. He has been involved in the development efforts of both the WCM (TeamSite/LiveSite) wing and the CDM (WorkSiteMP/WorkSiteNT) wing of Interwoven product suites.

Sivashankar received his M.S. degree in Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering from University of Colorado at Boulder, USA, M.Tech degree in Optoelectronics and Optical Communications from Indian Institute of Technology, New Delhi, India, and B.Sc./M.Sc. degrees in Physics from Madurai Kamaraj University in Madurai, India.

He is been developing web based application software since Feb 1996. He has good experience in OO design and anlysis, reverse engineering and complex enterprise system analysis and modelling. His skills include statistical analysis and graphical report generation with R. He has published a chapter in Guided Wave Optical Components and Devices, Academic Press (Coauthored) and at the international conference on optoelectronics, fiber optics and Photonics (Coauthored) . He is a Sun Certified Programmer for the Java2 Platform 1.4 and Sun Certified Web Component Developer for the Java2 Platform, Enterprise Edition 1.4.
