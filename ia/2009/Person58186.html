David MacGregor’s Summary

I have been a marketing communications professional since the early 1980s. I have been involved in both international and local brands.

Created and launched Family Health Diary, one of New Zealand's most successful media properties in the consumer health sector and, in doing so created a viable model and business system that I later sold to my business partners. FHD is now a multi-million dollar venture and one of the largest advertisers in New Zealand.

Explored the world of interactive communications (brands, loyalty, experiences) as Creative Director for an e-marketing business owned by Lion Nathan, one of the world's most succcessful brewing giants - working to assist in the development of strategies that would intimately connect many of the world's most famous beer, wine and spritis brands with their most loyal customers.

Co-founded Idealog magazine HB Media. Idealog (idealog.co.nz) is the world's only magazine dedicated to the creative economy. It is New Zealand's largest business magazine and won the Magazine Publishers Association awards 2007 and 2008 for best business magazine and magazine launch of the year.

I have taught at Massey University School of Design in Auckland (Marketing, Advertising and Design Research) since 2004 and am preparing a masters thesis
about nation branding and social media).

I recently rejoined the company I co-founded in 1996 - BrandWorld as creative director to develop new media properties for New Zealand and international use.

David MacGregor’s Specialties:

Creative marketing communications strategies, creative direction, creative coaching, campaign design and implementation, publishing, product concept development - prototyping and implememtation.
