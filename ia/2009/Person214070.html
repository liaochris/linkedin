Seroj de Graaf’s Summary

Dutch product/interior designer. Driven to create solutions by design. By giving shape to innovative materials and technology we create the beauty of tomorrow.

After his studie at the ArtEZ institute of arts and design in Arnhem and an internship at the world famous Marcel Wanders studio, Seroj launched an exclusive interior collection together with fellow designer Bo Reudler during the international furniture fair in Milan. Named the Asylum Collection, it is now sold worldwide exclusively through a selected group of galleries such as Tools Galerie in Paris.

In the same year Seroj started his design studio called 'Chocolate and Foam'. With the philosophy that design is the key to create clear solutions and beautiful experiences, the studio focus on concept and design projects in the field of identity, product and interior. By giving shape to innovation and communication design can serve and connect consumer markets and industries.

In addition, Seroj works on design projects from his own initiative and sets up collaborations with other designers, engineers and companies to push innovation more. This resulted in an inspiring collaboration with tooling specialist Wisent. This company is known for its smart and often surprisingly simple but effective solutions.

Following one of his other passions Seroj has initiated a new company under the brand Loggia Import. Loggia imports premium quality Prosecco from Italy. In this way Seroj expands his entrepreneurial activities in other fields and sectors.

Seroj de Graaf’s Specialties:

Design, Innovation, Communication, Product Design, Product Development, Furniture Design. Innovative Materials en Technology. Production. Design and Production in EPS Foam.
