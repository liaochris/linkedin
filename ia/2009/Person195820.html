Reidar Wasenius’s Summary

Reidar is a trilingual communications specialist in the broadest sense of the word. He has accumulated extensive experience in the fields of public speaking & acting since 1981, training & education since 1984, reporting & journalism since 1989 and PR & marketing since 1991. He has a proven track record of motivating people to do their best and develop as professionals in the areas of convergence between technology and media.

Currently, he is heading the start-up Prometium Inc. His main focus is on the development of the BRIIM system - a set of unique training and coaching methods for individuals and organizations in development and use since 1989. Novel products and services based on the BRIIM system include a comprehensive personal development facility - the Brain Fitness Center - with its Gym, Aivobic hall, Language Spa and other unique services.

Reidar Wasenius’s Specialties:

* Skill development of individuals and organizations

* Heading and developing media, communications and computer science education

* Teaching media literacy, computer science, natural sciences and languages

* Editor-in-chief for print media, Producing for radio and interactive media, Reporting for radio, TV, print media

* Public relations, B2B and B2C marketing, branding - especially for technology products

* Mobile telecommunications business development
