Richard Cutting’s Summary

It's all about the internet - Utility computing, Video, SaaS etc. Love the internet. Love it! The boundaries it brings down, raising human consciousness along the way.

The last 9 years have been in IT sales management. Specifically within the managed service providers and content management arenas. I have built many successful sales team for those sectors.

Started Over Achievers Ltd. A specialist recruitment company placing quality sales, pre-sales and technical personnel into managed service providers, CDN specialists and software companies.

Prior to IT I spent 18 months as the Public Relations Manager of the largest generic pharmaceutical company in central Europe, Zentiva.

Before pharmaceuticals I was Director of Studies at an American Business School lecturing in business communications. I taught MBA students and managed a department of 10 teachers.

My goal is to build a first class recruitment company. I have dealt with many recruitment companies over the years and have recruited literally 100's of people for organisations. We're on our way. Please feel free to link up with me richard.cutting@overachievers.co.uk.
