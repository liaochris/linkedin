Eyal Reshef’s Summary

Worked for 4 years in management consultancy, learn how to analyze various market situations and identify the key drivers of a business. Creating change process in the market and inside the company. Had the amazing experience of launching a mobile network in Orange Israel, being the 3rd player in the market, moving in a few years into a leadership position, through building a very attractive brand, differentiating the value offer, and deploying innovative marketing strategies. Gained a lot of sales experience and working with various sales channels, direct, consumer stores, dealers, system integrators, learning how to motivate them and maximize their yield. Worked for 4 years with in 2 startups, learn a lot from doing mistakes, and having different management on board.

At present I am managing a non for profit organization which I founded and turned into an serious force in the industry. Including over 90 companies and active worldwide in promoting Israeli companies in mobile communication market.

My goals is to form partnerships that will help my portfolio companies enhance their value offering and a better chance to achieve sales in the market. We are very active in Europe and have a very strong ambition to increase our activities in Asia Pacific.
