Jérémie Tronet’s Summary

I'm a professional kiteboarder specialized in freeriding, promoting my sponsors and traveling the world bringing photos, videos from all over the globe.

This gave me the chance to become one of the most published kitesurfer worldwide.
In fact the 2008 season ( September 07 to September 08) I -
-have had the chance to be on over 100 full pages worldwide in kitesurf magazines.

-This past season ( September 08 to September 09) I have had 7 magazines covers and over 100 magazines full pages in the US, France, Greece, Spain, Germany, Belgium, Italy, etc etc ....

I travel together with my girlfriend Linn Svendsen from Norway,
we work as a team and share the exposure and the sponsors for twice more exposure for them.

Video wise, this season only ( September 08 to September 09) I filmed and edited 2 kitesurf DVDs, one have been produced at over 120 000 copies worldwide and became the most published kitesurf DVD, an online version is also available. The 2nd one came out in France and online with over 40 000 views. In those DVDs and movies I promote my travels and my sponsors in many different ways.

I also use internet a lot with weekly updates sent to the biggest kitesurf website and surf website.

My website: http://www.jeremietronet.com get an average of 2500 different visitors / month ( over 5000 visits per months) and keep growing in the kiteboarding comunity.

One of my goal for the future seasons is to expend those coverage to bigger magazines and TV programs and not only specialised kitesurf medias. I already had coverage with national geographic adventure magazine and I'm planing on expending this.


I speak: French, English Spanish, Portuguese and Creole
