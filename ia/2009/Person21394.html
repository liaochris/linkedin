Ashok D Rathod’s Specialties:

Architecture and Design is our passion. We don’t just produce beautiful CAD drawings; we design, create beautiful and practical buildings to your needs, which we aim to achieve as our prime objective. We are also committed to the design, implementation and applications of renewable energies for ‘Green Buildings’. For example: use of solar energy for heating and hot water, wind energy for electricity, bio-fuel (alternative to gas) and recycle rainwater for home and garden use.
