Jim Pack’s Summary

Mr. Pack has been involved in many successful technology ventures during his career. Most recently, as President and CEO of AdvancedMD, he has directed the efforts of the leading web-based and hosted medical practice management system – AdvancedMD. This system addresses the needs of small/medium sized medical office and 3rd party billing service industries. Under his direction, AdvancedMD has been recognized on the Inc. 500 fastest growing companies in the US, as one of the “Elite 15” fastest growing companies in Utah and one of Utah Business’s “Best Places to Work in Utah”. This was accomplished by driving the national use of hosted solutions by development and marketing of quality managed services in the Healthcare marketplace. AdvancedMD was aquired by Francisco Partners in 2008.

Prior to working with AdvancedMD, Mr. Pack was President of Easy Dental Systems and a principle of Dentrix Dental Systems. Both companies are known as successful vendors of healthcare software and services and have been recognized among the Inc. 500 fastest growing companies in the United States, and with many varied awards for quality and service.

Mr. Pack has been involved in many technology revolutions. In addition to the current movement toward more cost effective and operationally efficient hosted solutions for the medical industry, he was involved in bringing the first Microsoft Windows based Dental practice management and clinical systems to the market. Today, those systems comprise over half of all systems installed in Dental offices today.

As Vice President of Marketing and Sales for SilverPlatter, Inc., Mr. Pack introduced CD-ROM technology to the library industry, and in the mid 1980’s while at Sterling Wentworth Corp. he was involved with the introduction of the first Artificial Intelligence/Expert Systems technologies to the Financial Planning industry.

He has a BS in Economics from Brigham Young University and an MBA from the University of Michigan.
