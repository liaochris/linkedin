Alison Green’s Summary

I have worked on virtually every aspect of the web design process, and have highly developed skills in website scoping, information architecture, design, writing, HTML/CSS development and testing, based on over 12 years' experience. I adhere to e-government guidelines and W3C accessibility recommendations and utilise validation tools.

I have designed, developed and/or maintained over 150 websites - some for design companies including Shift, Origin Design, Base Two, CWA New Media, Mission Hall and Oryx Technologies, and others working directly with clients. I specialise in pure CSS sites.

My extensive web development experience includes sites for the Historic Places Trust, NIWA, Salvation Army, NZWood, Weta Digital, Tourism NZ, Education NZ, Shift, WWF-NZ, National Library, LINZ, Contact Energy, Transpower, Meadow Fresh and Icebreaker.

Alison Green’s Specialties:

I design and develop multi-template websites and am an expert coder in HTML, XHTML, DHTML, CSS and JavaScript libraries for a wide range of Content Management Systems.

I manage projects and timelines effectively, and proof-read and fully test my websites on a wide range of browsers. I deliver on time and within budget. As an experienced designer/developer I can quickly grasp the requirements of any web design project and work as part of any team, requiring minimal supervision or instruction.
