Diane Hamilton, Ph.D.’s Summary

My experience includes working in several industries including pharmaceuticals, banking and real estate. I have trained corporations in areas such as time management, emotional intelligence and Myers Briggs. I currently work as an online professor for 6 different universities. I teach mostly business-related courses to bachelor, master and doctoral level students as well as mentors doctoral learners. I am a contributing writer to sites such as Investopedia.com, Youngmoney.com, AcademicInfo.net and MyTwoDollars.com to name a few. I am in the process of writing a personal finance book for the young adult as well as a book on utilizing personality assessments training to be effective at work.

Diane Hamilton, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

I specialize in the following areas: Personal Finance, Management, Sales, Real Estate, Banking, Personality Assessments Including Myers Briggs and Emotional Intelligence, Pharmaceuticals, Marketing, and Life Skills for the Young Adult.
