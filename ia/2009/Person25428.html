Damon Young’s Summary

My feature screenplay, "My Own Lily", was a quarterfinalist in the Nicholl Fellowship in Screenwriting competition and a semifinalist in the the Chesterfield Film Company's Writers Film Program. While developing my own independent feature film and web-based media projects, I'm always open and available to both screenwriting and directing opportunities.

Damon Young’s Specialties:

I'm primarily a screenwriter and director, although I have on-set experience as both a 1st AD and a script supervisor.
