The following benefits were achieved with the original implementation and continued enhancement of this platform:

    - Increased partner satisfaction and visibility to future NPI activity by enhancing the get product catalog web service.
    - Decreased turnaround time and complexity in managing product availability exceptions enabling organization to increase its performance of on-time delivery commitments to end-consumers.
