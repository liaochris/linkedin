Lara Vander Ploeg’s Summary

Lara brings a rich and diverse background to all of her work. She has worked and advised numerous non-profits in development, public relations, and marketing endeavors for nearly fifteen years. Combining her degrees in Psychology, Theology, and Fine Art, she is always excited to use her skills to creatively inspire individuals and teams to 'live out loud' with passion and purpose.

After studying with the Institute for Life Coach Training, Lara left her job as a development director to pursue her dream to start the life-inspiring company, birdseyeview. Now, Lara coaches clients from all over the globe. She also creates life-changing workshops and seminars and speaks for groups and organizations all over the country.

A single mom with an indomitable spirit, she is known for walking the talk -- banishing fear, illuminating hope and encouraging bold action wherever she goes!

Lara Vander Ploeg’s Specialties:

Individual and group coaching; Brand Identity; Marketing and PR for the Small Business Owner; Building Your Coaching Practice; Keynote Speaking; Coaching for Creatives
