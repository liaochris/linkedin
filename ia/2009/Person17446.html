Ann Oliver’s Summary

Ann has more than 15 years of hands-on, high-impact marketing communications experience. She has managed global clients at advertising agencies in Cleveland and Chicago and has extensive background in consumer products, travel and tourism, and business-to-business. Ann is responsible for leading account strategy, program management and measurement for several of the agency’s top accounts.

Ann advocates for the "power of the purse" and is developing an agency practice area focused on marketing to women and marketing to moms. She has developed integrated marketing programs with best-in-class "women–focused” media partners such as Disney/Family Fun, Meredith/Midwest Living/BH&G, Nickelodeon, iVillage, Scholastic Parents Media, and PBS Kids.

Her clients have been recognized with industry awards including several Best of Show honors from both the AAF Addy and PRSA PRism competitions. Ann joined Fahlgren in 1997 from Doner in Cleveland. She has worked with a wide range of brands and companies including Balloon Time, the Ohio Division of Travel and Tourism, Motorola, Cooper Tire, Cub Cadet/YardMan, and the Make-a-Wish Foundation.
