Huu Nghia Nguyen’s Summary

Huu-Nghia Nguyen received the Engineer of Information Technology from Hanoi University of Technology (HUT), Vietnam, in 2002. From 2003 to 2005 he continued his study at the Francophone Institute for Computer Science (IFI) for the Master of Communication System. From March to October 2005, he joined EURECOM, France, for his master internship, on the 3GPP Multimedia Broadcast Multicast Service (MBMS), within the scope of the European FP6 DAIDALOS project.

In January 2006, he started his Doctoral thesis at EURECOM, in the Mobile Communications Department, under the supervision of Prof. Christian Bonnet. He joined in activities of different European and French projects: FP6 MULTINET, RNRT AIRNET, FP7 CHORIST, MAXSSIMM.

Huu Nghia Nguyen’s Specialties:

IPv6 Mobility Management, Multi-homing, 3G, 4G/LTE
