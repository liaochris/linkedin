Jason Muhlstein’s Summary

I am a marketing executive who thinks through ideas and strategies as well as developing new ones to help clients achieve measurable results. I do this by implementing best “practical” database marketing and analytics practices. I have over 11 years of business consulting experience managing engagements with a particular emphasis on business to business direct marketing and sales in the healthcare, insurance and manufacturing industries.

My clients would say I improve the effectiveness of their direct marketing campaigns by applying my extensive experience in annual planning, lead generation and nurturing, customer growth strategy, best customer profiling and segmentation, campaign automation and databases, operations including campaign execution processes from planning to measurement, vendor evaluation and selection.

Past companies include Arthur Andersen, drkoop.com and Zamba Solutions where I gained a deep understanding of financial analysis, e-commerce and marketing technologies developing a well rounded business perspective to rely on when making client recommendations that achieve results.

When not working I am active in the local community as the head coach of the St. Paul Freestyle Wrestling Club and a member of the Wishes and More Marketing & Fundraising Committee.
