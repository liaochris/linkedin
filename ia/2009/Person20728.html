Arun Tyagi’s Summary

- Marketing strategy and brand positioning
- Supporting IndiaMART.com's growth and revenue generating efforts through effective and result focused online and offline marketing campaigns
- Working with cross functional teams to drive marketing and communication initiatives
- Marketing alliances to grow IndiaMART.com's presence and market share
- Supporting IndiaMART.com's client acquisition/sales team through marketing collaterals, product brochures, proposals, presentations and case studies
- Conceptualizing and executing new ideas for internal communication through internal web portal and other employee engagement/promotional campaigns

- Media planning and buying
- Liasioning with creative, media, pr and event management agencies
- Client relationship development
