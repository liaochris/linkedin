Benjamin Tseng’s Summary

I graduated from Harvard in 2007 with an A.B. in Biochemical Sciences and a Secondary Field certificate in Mathematical Sciences. While at Harvard, I developed a fascination for using computational and mathematical approaches in conjunction with traditional laboratory experiments to understand biological systems. This particular background has translated into a keen interest and knowledge base in business and research relating to biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, the Web, and software.

Currently, I am working for management consulting firm Bain & Company in the lovely San Francisco Bay Area.
