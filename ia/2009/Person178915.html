Hein van der Wielen’s Summary

My name is Hein and I'm working as a Sr. Online Marketing Specialist. I'm an all round online marketer (1974) with a passion for e-commerce, online display advertising, analytics and search. I have been active on the internet since the early days of the '90s. Having weaknesses for media, computers and new technologies you can imagine I was hooked the minute I explored the Internet for the first time.

I started my first company named Van der Wielen Productions in 1998. At first active in several fields: Internet, web/IT development and media/music. Today the field of online advertising and online sales & marketing is my only business.

I geared up on my internet activities since the beginning of this millennium. During these years I became a specialist in the field of online advertising, SEM, media buying, analytics and e-commerce. Combining a straight forward hands on mentality with entrepreneurship.

Together with about 40 colleagues at Blue Mango Interactive (Eindhoven, NL) we offer top shelf online marketing knowledge and work force for some of the largest (e-commerce) companies looking for online successes.

Don't expect the above average full service online marketing agency. Blue Mango Interactive is different. Think about us as 40 online marketing specialists with the highest level of service available in this industry. Focussed on your online sales and branding campaigns, 24/7. We manage some of the largest advertising budgets available in The Netherlands. Blue Mango Interactive offers its service to a very select group of premium brands such as Vodafone, Ditzo (Fortis) and The Phone House (The Carphone Warehouse/BestBuy.com).

We are working in extreme close cooperation with our clients. Together we are taking care of our clients’ online activities making sure they are always way ahead of the competition. Actually, I don't consider our clients as clients. We look at them as colleagues. Did I mention entrepreneurship before ;)?
