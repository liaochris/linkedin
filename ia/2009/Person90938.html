Sumit Gupta’s Summary

I am a technologist and strategist, I love evangelizing new solutions and putting myself in the shoes of customers. I have over 12 years of experience in products and solutions, architecture, sales operations and strategy for internet based applications including enterprise and Saas. My technology experience ranges from deep understanding of web consumer behavior patterns to web analytics, internet fraud and security, internet applications for financials, order management, supply chain and CRM.

I am cofounder of BitRhymes, a brain trust comprising IITians and entrepreneurs embarking on several web application initiatives. I am also an advisor to Pacific Tech which provides outsourcing solutions to mostly US based customers.

At Entrust, I am responsible for sales operations/strategy and solution architecture for their Transaction Guard division, originally called Business Signatures before acquisition by Entrust. I was founding team member of Business Signatures where I played a key role in every aspect of the business from product definition, hiring the team, solution architecture, sales strategy and operations eventually to a successful acquisition by Entrust in 2006. Business Signatures provided real time fraud detection and prevention capabilities to internet banking using patented activity corelation, behavior analysis and access pattern analysis on HTTP data stream.

Prior to Business Signatures, I was an early employee at Escalate, which provided order management and supply chain visibility solutions to retailers such as Home Depot, Williams Sonoma. I was involved in multiple groups at Escalate ranging from infrastructure, engineering, sales and SaaS operations. The company grew to 200+ employees at its peak with more than 40 customers before succumbing to dot com crash and acquisition by GERS. Prior to that, I was one of the early engineers in Oracle CRM Marketing application division at a time when the division was just getting started.
