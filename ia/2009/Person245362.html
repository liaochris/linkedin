Waleed ALKalash, MBA’s Summary

I started working in the banking field in 1996 after graduating with a BA in Business Administration. My last position was as a Senior Manager in the Corporate Finance department. In 2006, I received a full scholarship from my government, Saudi Arabia, to study a Master’s degree in Business Administration, MBA, in the U.S.A. In fact, I believe it is really more beneficial to study MBA after having experience in a managerial position than study the MBA without having such working experience. The reason is that one can feel and experience the learning in the classroom and applying this knowledge into the real world. Thus, in many occasions and case studies that I had, I applied what I faced as a manager (my experience) to those incidents and case studies; it worked very well and it was very interesting for me to manage both sides.

In the beginning of June, 2009, I was appointed as the Vice President of Beta Gamma Sigma- San Diego Area Alumni Chapter. I believe it will be interesting experience. As officers in this Chapter, we will work hard and effectively to encourage and honor high academic achievement by students of business schools that accredited by AACSB International. In addition, we are committed to promote the society’s values: honor and integrity, pursuit of wisdom, and earnestness. We will support advancement of business, and cultivate the reputation of Beta Gamma Sigma.
