Dario Tainer’s Summary

Dario Tainer is the President of Tainer Associates Ltd. an award winning multi disciplined design firm headquartered in Chicago, IL. Mr. Tainer’s project experience and expertise range from long range urban and master planning studies through all phases of design and project management for companies and corporate entities whose image is equally as important as their programmatic requirements.

He has held college teaching positions and has participated in lectures, juries and exhibitions at various institutions. He has received numerous awards for his design work and has been published internationally in addition to having been featured on two television (HGTV) programs.

Mr. Tainer is not your typical architect expanding his creative talents into the marketing and image development arenas. His generalist attitude towards integrated multi disciplined design solutions and his ability to convey architectural concepts and ideas through illustrations has prompted many of his peers to solicit his assistance on their own projects.

Born in Torino, Italy , Mr. Tainer is a graduate of the University of Toronto with a Masters Degree in Urban Planning. He received a Bachelor of Architecture degree in design from the University of Illinois at Chicago and was part of an overseas exchange program with the Ecole’ des Beaux Arts in Versailles, France. Work Experience includes positions with small and medium size firms as well as with one of the best and largest, (SOM) Skidmore, Owings and Merrill of Chicago.

He is a registered architect and has been a member of the board of directors and eventually president of several international professional and trade organizations. Work over the past 30 years has been within all aspects of real estate, office furniture and fashion sectors for U.S., and European Clients.

A US collegiate Decathlete and record holder, Mr. Tainer currently holds a USA Cycling racing licence and coaches high school Track and Field.
