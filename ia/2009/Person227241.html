PROFILE
Dedicated, organized, and process-oriented professional with the expertise to coordinate projects, tasks, events, schedules, and all other aspects of the engineering project life cycle. Currently supporting the world wide engineering organization which is comprised of many various cultures located in a number of countries and locations. I assist project teams with planning, tracking, reports, financials, logistics and processes, and keep the project stakeholders and contributors informed at all times while keeping the team focused on the goal at hand. I work with the engineering organization for continuous process improvement, in developing tools and in training team members.
