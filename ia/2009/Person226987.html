Susan Gosselin’s Summary

Gosselin is a public relations practitioner with more than 20 years experience in the industry. As Director of P.R. at Vest Advertising, she handles all p.r./communications work for the agency's clients and provides strategic counsel.

Susan Gosselin’s Specialties:

Social Media execution and strategy, traditional and social media p.r., internal communications,communications planning, crisis communications,investor relations, all manner of writing for advertising and p.r., video scripts, media training, as well as straight journalism assigments for publication.

Susan Gosselin’s Experience

  • Director of PR

    Vest Advertising

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    May 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Handle all public relations work for Vest Advertising, a full service marketing, p.r. and multimedia production company in Louisville, KY.

  • President

    Gosselin Communications, Inc.

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    January 2003 — April 2008 (5 years 4 months)

    Worked full time as a self employed communications consultant for international, regional and local clients. Clients included General Electric, Schmitt Sohne Wines, Decora Cabinets, Christian Care Communities, The Kentucky Derby Museum, The Red Cross and many more. Conducted everything from advanced media tours, to trade and consumer publicity work, to corporate communications strategy, crisis communications, writing for the web, brochure and advertising writing. Also wrote for publication, with regular assignments from The Lane Report, Prep Magazine and Business First of Louisville.

  • Manager of Communications

    Lightyear Communications (formerly UniDial)

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    November 1997 — May 2000 (2 years 7 months)

    During Gosselin's tenure, Lightyear, located in Louisville, KY, was known as UniDial Communications, and was one of the largest and fastest growing Telecom Resellers in the nation. The company tripled its size during this period, adding approximately 15 national offices and expanding its workforce to more than 800 employees. It sold a very large array of telecom networking and connectivity products to businesses and individuals nationwide. Gosselin served as their one person p.r. department, handling communications for employees, more than 500 independent agents, potential investors, the telecom trade, trade and local press and potential customers. It was exhausting, but great fun.

  • Public Relations Manager

    Brown-Forman, North American Spirits Division

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    May 1996 — November 1997 (1 year 7 months)

    Located in Louisville, KY, Brown-Forman is one of the world's largest spirits manufacturers. In this position, Gosselin worked in the communications department, assigned to the Spirits Division, which handles the company's flagship brands such as Jack Daniel's, Canadian Mist, Woodford Reserve, Old Forester, Finlandia Vodka and many, many more. Here, she worked on corporate media relations projects such as the opening of the Woodford Reserve Tourism facility, wrote executive speeches, helped plan internal events, managed the division's magazine The Sovereign, and produced other needed printed materials.

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    The Buntin Group is Tennesee's largest full service advertising agency. As an account executive in the public relations division, Gosselin served as editor and manager of the 300K plus circulation magazine, Senior Friends, for Columbia/HCA Healthcare. She also managed a large daily onslaught of print and media relations projects for Baptist Hospital in Nashville, as well as working for clients such as Ruby Tuesday, JC Bradford, Silver Spoon Cafe, Morrison's Cafeterias, L&N Seafood and many more.

  • Publications Manager

    Ericson Marketing Communications

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

In this position Gosselin managed all the long format publications produced for the agency, which included national specialty magazines, newsletters, long-format brochures and reports, advertorials and the like. She did all the research and writing, interfaced with the client, managed the design and production team and managed the project budgets on all these projects.
