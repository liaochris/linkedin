Vijaya Sagar Vinnakota’s Summary

I am a computer science engineer with 12 years of experience in systems software. I have worked with both large scale and embedded systems, at Wipro, inSilica, TrueSpan and SiRF during these years. My technical interests include computer architecture, programming languages and operating systems.

I, along with two of my friends, started Althea Systems to change the world with our revolutionary ideas ;^)
