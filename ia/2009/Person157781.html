Mike Branch’s Summary

♦ Publishing │ Marketing │ Copywriting and Editing | Digital Communications

My professional experience includes nearly three decades of communicating information and ideas in the form of words and graphics in print and online. I have also experienced the joy of building collaborative communication and ownership among project stakeholders, seeing the work as a whole surpass each person’s individual abilities.

Along the way, I have found hands-on opportunities in:
• Copywriting, editing, and designing for print and Web.

• Leadership in team-building and vision-setting.
• Management of products and employees.

Mike Branch’s Experience

  • Proprietor

    KnowSowGrow

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    KnowSowGrow cultivates growth in companies and people.

    Experience and observation tells me that we are often a product of our information and insights (what we know), our ability to use these insights to pursue new opportunities (what we sow), and the fruit that develops from what we plant (what we grow).   • Partner

    Branch-Smith Printing

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Printing industry)

    January 1980 — Present (29 years 8 months)

    Branch-Smith Printing has been family owned and operated since 1910. A national leader in the printing industry, Branch-Smith Printing specializes in printing publications — from design to printing and distribution.

    For more: www.BranchSmith.com

  • President

    Branch-Smith Publishing

    (Publishing industry)

    January 1980 — August 2008 (28 years 8 months)

    Since 1910, the company has provided the professional horticulture community with industry-specific information. In my tenure at the company, Branch-Smith Publishing expanded from one weekly magazine and semi-annual buyers’ guide to: 4 focused monthlies, industry’s first web site, industry’s first weekly news e-mails, industry’s first interactive blogs. The company was acquired in 2008 by GIE Media, a leading publisher of business-to-business publications.

    More: www.KnowSowGrow.com

  • Writer / Artist

    Wichita Falls Times & Record News

    (Newspapers industry)

    June 1978 — December 1979 (1 year 7 months)

    The Wichita Falls (Texas) Times and Record News provided writing, design and illustration assignments when daily newspapers were first adopting widespread use of artwork in their pages. I introduced such design into their features section, including a front-page map that tracked a mile-wide tornado’s sweep through town in 1979.

    More: www.KnowSowGrow.com
