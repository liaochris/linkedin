Mark Tomich, AICP’s Summary

Planning professional experienced in leading teams to accomplish diverse planning objectives. Broad experience ranging from long-range planning, current planning/land use entitlement, environmental planning (CEQA and NEPA), housing and redevelopment. Extensive experience with California's legal and regulatory environment.

Mark Tomich, AICP’s Specialties:

Planning management, planning department budgeting and strategic planning, land use entitlement and regulatory permitting, long-range policy development, housing element updates, annexations and municipal service reviews, natural resource conservation, zoning and specific plans, redevelopment, and military base reuse planning.
