Steven Fisher’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur and currently Managing Principal of AppSolve, an award-winning firm that specializes in user experience design, enterprise web development and online community development.

Been actively writing about small business and innovation since the late 90s on how small business could leverage the Web and stellar customer experiences to innovate their business and compete with the largest of competitors. Among the early thought leaders on the intersection of user experience, customer service and innovation.

Published several e-books on Product Management and Marketing, Small Business Management, User Experience, Online Marketing and Innovation. Currently, working on my first book, “101 Rules for Entrepreneurs” slated for a Spring 2010 release.

Co-founder of Big Damn Fan Films, Inc. a non-profit organization dedicated to "increase the awareness of non-profit charities through fan made films”. He is co-creator and producer of “Browncoats: Redemption”, a film set in Joss Whedon's Firefly/Serenity universe.

Holds a Bachelor of Science in Business from University of Baltimore and on a personal note he is a private pilot, musician and concert photographer. Currently resides in Columbia, MD, USA.
