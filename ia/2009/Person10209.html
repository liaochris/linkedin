Matt Booth’s Summary

My veterinary duties have included consulting with referring veterinarians and clients, managing and educating staff technicians, and cooperating with internal medicine, surgery, radiology and cardiology board-certified specialists. I have managed many acute decompensations of chronic disease and many surgical cases including hemoabdomen, urethral tears, gastric dilatation volvulus, gall bladder mucocoele, gastrointestinal foreign body, dystocia, septic abdomen, sub-total colectomy, cholecystoduodenostomy, thoracotomy, PRAA correction and amputation. Medical cases I have managed have included DKA, renal failure, systemic hypertension, pulmonary hypertension, DIC, pancreatitis and congestive heart failure. I regularly utilize diagnostics such as CBC, differentials, chemistry panels, PT/PTT, FDP, lactate, acid base, blood gas analysis, cPL, radiology, ultrasonography, cytology, indirect blood pressure, EKG, pulse oximetry, and CVP. Preventative preanesthetic bloodwork was routine. Client education regarding incidental findings such as cardiac murmur, gingivitis and undefined masses was also common.
