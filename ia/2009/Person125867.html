Kane Wai, MBA, PMP’s Summary

Kane is an accomplished manager with more than 13 years of consulting and management experience and a proven ability to communicate and deliver solutions, lead cross-functional teams to resolve problems and bring complex projects to success. He is a resourceful, dependable, and hands-on professional with exceptional technical and managerial capabilities. Kane is a proven leader and motivator who delivers exceptional results to his clients. He is capable, honest, committed, well-organized with strong analytical, interpersonal, and communication skills.

As part of the Business Intelligence and Performance Management (BIPM) practice at Hitachi Consulting, Kane is responsible for helping clients define and design BI road maps, develop BI and data warehousing solutions, and provide "center of excellence" leadership. Kane is also leading the Portfolio Management practice which helps clients align technology and business solutions and projects with corporate strategies, improve business processes, standardize project management methodology, and establish benefit management and reporting, including scorecards and dashboards, for the purpose of advanced business planning and executive decision-making.
