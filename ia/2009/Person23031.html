Chinmoy Sarkar’s Summary

My aim is to build a critical mass of support for a living concept in order to establish it as a framework in the professional world. Education space in its current format is limited as it is linked with achievements and performance in the corporate world. Education, instead has to adopt a joyous flow that will take everyone through a journey into 'real him".

I came out of the corporate world and joined the academic space to work as an Evangelist. I am performing this journey devoid of any achievement whatsoever. I want to share my understanding with everyone of "something intriguing" that I came across during my journey in last few years in the corporate arena. I am doing my research on my model AXELL which can infleunece the business transformation and re-engineering exercises. Knowledge Economy has truely given muscles to all of us to achieve evrything that we seek.

I have witnessed drastic and radical change in me as I pursued my interest in this 'unexplored' domain of intangibles. I discovered the dominance of some 'unknowns' in our living, not with my known mind but with the mind that gets naturally revealed when experience and knowledge stop influencing. I have seen the tremendous impact of those 'unknowns' on the maturity of the corporate professionals to understand creativity and innovations. All of us know about our obsession for growth and expansion. If we can give ourselves a pair of wings, we would have learnt to fly. I have started believing that the ‘spirit of expansion’ and the urge for unknowns is the presumed path to success in human form. After all, we are 'human being'; 'human' as a form and 'being' as formless. I am on the move with AXELL, a model that inspires everyone to understand the feelings of inherent possibilities in our journey towards excellence. I want so much to be the love that is ME that I am going to find a way at looking at this apart from the conditions surrounding me.

    Business space needs innovative and creative mind not disconnected from the reality. We need massive transformation to rebuild our strategy, culture, process and technology. I have captured my understanding of truth in living in AXELL, a model that I developed for human transformation in business space. My model gives every professional a 'feeling of inherent possibilities' to address this change scenario. Forty nine components of AXELL will help them to trace their journey path to the real 'unknowns' without the influence of 'knowns'. Each component is made up of SPARK that ignites the fire of imagination. S for Seeking (Strategy), P for Promised (Resources), A for Allowing (Capabilities), R for Receiving (Goals) and K for Key for natural revelation (Integration).
