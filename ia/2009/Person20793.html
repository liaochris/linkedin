Arvind Kulkarni’s Summary

Professional Experiences:
(1) A year of working as a pure Electrical Engineer with NELCO, testing Thyristorized AC Drives. I left this wonderful job to get into an MBA school (NMIMS).
(2) Over 4 years in Philips India working in various capacities (planning manager for the Pimpri factory, sales manager for the Western Region for my Division, JD Edwards implementation specialist for Logistics for my Division, Product Manager for PA/Congress products), each of which gave me the requisite background to eventually bring me into the world of ERP.

(4) 9+ years in the US working for a company that went through several acquisitions and name changes. I moved from an SD consultant to a Logistics consultant to a Retail consultant.

Notice that the term 'manager' has never entered this description, primarily due to my enchantment with the technical nature of my work, I guess. Someday...
