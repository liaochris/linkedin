Deep Thapliyal’s Summary

In the last few years Deep has built couple of businesses in the Executive search space and Health and Wellness space. He now runs an executive search company in the IT and BPO space catering to the Sales and P&L Leader positions. His Executive search endeavors have built a reputation in the industry with his network and reach in building winning teams for his customers.

Prior to this Deep had over 17 years of outsourcing industry experience with an exclusive focus on high 'Quality of Revenue'. He places high Premium on Relationship building abilities and Long term relationships. Has an uncanny knack to seek out and close large deals with corporations.

Experience in multiple geographies including India, Asia Pacific and in the Americas.

A successful track record of turning around operations.

A natural leader.
