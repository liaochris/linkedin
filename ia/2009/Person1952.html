Abigail Harrison’s Summary

thebluedoor is a specialist PR agency delivering strategic communications to businesses that deliver results.

We excel at integrating online digital and off-line traditional channels in order to maximise results for our clients. A member of the PRCA, thebluedoor is a founder member of its Digital Committee. We have also been awarded the PRCA’s Consultancy Management Standard, the internationally recognised gold-standard for industry excellence. We are active member of the Surrey Chambers of Commerce.

We offer the following services: Strategy development, Media relations, Online PR / SEO, Sticky content, Training, Crisis communications, Product / campaign launches, and Design. Our robust and analytical understanding of the technical aspects of the web, as well as its latest developments, enable us to strengthen our clients’ online presence, drive their lead generation, as well as support their SEO activities.

Our customers include the following businesses and organisations: South East England Partnership Board, Combined Insurance, Mars Drinks, KLIX, FLAVIA, Farrington’s Oils, Metro Guides, Borderlinx and Telegraph Media Group.

We operate in the following sectors: Business to Business, FMCG, Food and Drink, Publishing and Finance.

Abigail Harrison’s Specialties:

Media relations, web 2.0 training, web content, creative strategies/implementation, business development, sales force / marketing collateral, crisis communication.
