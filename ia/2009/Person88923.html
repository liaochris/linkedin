Graham Thornton’s Summary

Broad based IT expert with experience ranging from software development to sales, from small start ups to multi-nationals, from Europe to the United States.

Graham comes from the developer background and has kept a strong ability with Oracle database technology, but never fails to recognize that the technology exists to support the organization, not the other way around.

His experience has given him the ability to communicate across the experience, technology and cultural spectrum.
