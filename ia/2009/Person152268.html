Mark Middlebrook’s Summary

Mark Middlebrook has been in the banking industry for 19 years. He is senior vice president and client experience manager for City National Bank where he manages companywide programs to improve the client experience through all product and service delivery channels and increase client loyalty.

Mr. Middlebrook is also the manager of the bank’s eCommerce Group. With 12 years specializing in eCommerce, he directs online strategy and interactive marketing efforts to support the company's online revenue goals. He is responsible for the bank's public Web sites, cnb.com and a private extranet site, and the intranet, the firm's primary colleague communication portal.

Before joining City National, Mr. Middlebrook was assistant vice president and AOL channel manager at Union Bank of California, where he was responsible for managing all products on the bank's AOL Web site, BankNOW™, and strategic alliances. Before that, Mr. Middlebrook worked at Home Savings of America, where he managed and launched Internet funds transfer and bill pay services.

Mr. Middlebrook earned an MBA from the University of LaVerne, with an emphasis on information technology, and a bachelor's degree in marketing management from California State Polytechnic University of Pomona. He also earned a certificate Proactive Leadership from eCornell and a Successfully Managing a Technology and Product Strategy certificate from MIT Sloan.

Active in the community, Mr. Middlebrook is a member of the Board for Junior Achievement of Southern California. He also serves on the University of La Verne's Business Advisory Board and the AYSO Region 32 (Upland) Board.

Mark Middlebrook’s Specialties:

Manage and lead a team of web usability and content management experts, supporting the bank’s public web site (providing leads/revenue), Corporate Intranet/Portal, Wireless Remote Worker web site, Interactive Marketing (including ATM screens, Direct E-mail, RSS Feeds and Podcasting and online advertising), and much more.

Excel at developing and aligning the most strategic thoughts and concepts into immediate tactical implementation within a team environment.
