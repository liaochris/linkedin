John Cass’s Summary

20 years of marketing experience. Ten years in online marketing, and the last five years in social media marketing. Consulting or in-house online marketing director for online marketing strategies and campaigns including: search engine marketing, customer loyalty, product management, pay per click (PPC), webinars, social media marketing, brand monitoring and corporate blogging.

Author of "Strategies and Tools for Corporate Blogging," published by Elsevier an imprint of Butterworth-Heinemann, a book devoted to giving executives the strategies and tools necessary to run a successful Corporate Blogging effort.

2005/6 President of the Boston Chapter of the American Marketing Association. (http://www.amaboston.org)

Founding Fellow and Advisory Board Member for the Society of New Communications Research. (http://www.sncr.org)

Live in Arlington, Massachusetts with my wife Karin, and son Bram.

John Cass’s Specialties:

Marketing, marketing management, branding, blogging, corporate blogging, community manager, social media, search engine optimization, SEO, PPC, SEM, online PR, blogger relations. Search engine optimization of news releases. Building customer networks for product development. Natural language processing, online media measurement, online brand management.
