Gaida Zirkelbach’s Summary

I am a business, technology and Internet attorney, providing strategic counseling and negotiating transactions in the areas of technology, Internet, interactive marketing, mergers and acquisitions, and privacy and security. I advise large companies and start-ups and have vast experience in negotiating deals against technology and Internet industry leaders, including the largest online media properties and Fortune 500 companies.

Super Lawyers, Florida Trend Legal Elite and the South Florida Legal Guide have recognized me as an "Up & Comer," and publications such as the National Law Journal, Florida Trend magazine, the South Florida Business Journal, and the Miami Herald have quoted me on technology, Internet and marketing issues.
