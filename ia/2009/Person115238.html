Jodi Gaylord’s Summary

City Escapes Nature Photography is a small, web-based business specializing in wildlife and nature photography. Begun in 2007, the company offers both traditional and unique ways of seeing the natural world. We like to present our clientele with images that will make them laugh, ponder, or simply sigh at the beauty captured therein.

We believe very strongly in the preservation of the world's wildernesses, and as such, we donate 1% of our gross sales annually to organizations, such as The Nature Conservancy, that help to achieve that goal.
