KATE COBB’s Summary

I'm passionate about providing practical, just-in-time learning solutions which support both individual and organisational objectives.

I develop ranges of learning interventions to support (or replace) traditional face-to-face training, including:

• self study modules
• interactive learning packs
• distance learning
• teleclasses
• one-to-one coaching
• ANother

I provide my service at a distance – coaching by telephone or VOIP, training design by email, conference calls instead of face-to-face meetings – so I’m living the ‘virtual’ life which so many business people now live. And how many executives these days have time to set aside for a two or three day training course? They want to learn but when it’s suitable for them. It suits corporations too and forward thinking multinationals are adopting this approach!

My clients include KPMG, Dell, BTGS and ISP globally, as well as local and central government departments, business institutes and SMEs.

It’s a great way to share my twenty years plus experience as a trainer/coach! I’ve had my own learning and development company since 1986, consulted in many different sectors, private, public and not for profit, in a variety of countries in Europe and the Middle East, providing training in areas of management, leadership and communications. I have also worked coaching teams and individuals, helping them to resolve challenges and move forward. I’m a published author of training books for use by managers, trainers and coaches in different countries and write about business topics for online magazines.
