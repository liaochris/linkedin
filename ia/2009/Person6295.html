Alan Lyes’s Summary

I am an experienced, adaptable, client focused communication strategist, internet technologist, creative director and designer.

My focus is on innovative communications solutions for brand and internal communications and training for both UK and Global pharmaceutical and health care industries.

My current goal is to pioneer Web 2.0, web-video conferencing and Virtual World technologies and their use in the pharmaceutical and health care sectors.

I can be found in Second Life as AlanTVF Proto.
