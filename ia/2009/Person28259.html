Bikas Mishra’s Summary

Bikas is the founder and editor of DearCinema.com, a leading Indian web-magazine primarily focused on "world cinema". DearCinema works closely with European Union cine portal cineuropa.org.

DearCinema.com has had a successful collaboration with leading Indian broadcaster CNBC TV18 for their short film/documentary show "Through The Looking Glass".

Bikas was a part of the selection committee of the 10th Mumbai International Film Festival, organized by the Mumbai Academy of Moving Image (MAMI), a body comprising of representatives of Mumbai Film Industry led by noted filmmaker Shyam Benegal.

He is also a part of the selection committee of the 7th Third Eye Festival of Asian Cinema, organized by Asian Film Foundation, Mumbai.

Bikas has interviewed renowned international directors such as Márta Mészáros, Carlos Saura, Shyam Benegal and Mehreen Jabbar to name a few.

He contributes film reviews and articles to leading Indian publications such as Mint-The Wall Street Journal and NDTV.com.

DearCinema.com is currently working on a film appreciation module in consultation with the British Film Institute

DearCinema.com, in partnership with Enlighten Film Society, Mumbai also plans to organize film screenings, discussions and film appreciation workshops.

Bikas is a member of Fédération Internationale de la Presse Cinématographique (FIPRESCI) also known as The International Federation of Film Critics.

Bikas Mishra’s Specialties:

Film critique, festival programming, writing, film appreciation, film research, scripting and direction.
