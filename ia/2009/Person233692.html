Tim Mitchell’s Summary

I am an experienced and broadly skilled internet and digital media expert with a passion for innovation. I have 12 years of experience at fast-paced and cutting-edge internet and mobile and digital media companies. I have held high level positions in large and small organizations, and I’ve managed several large cross-functional teams. I possess a high level of creativity backed by strong business acumen and solid quantitative and analytical skills.

Career Highlights

• Significantly contributed to the rapid growth and success of IODA, the Independent Online Distribution Alliance, holding 2 executive roles, including a dual executive role with a wide range of management and strategic responsibilities. In 2005, the company was voted one of Fortune Magazines’ “Top 25 Breakout Companies”, and recently was named a “Deloitte Silicon Valley Technology Fast 50 Rising Star” based on three-year revenue growth of 1,258% from 2005-2007.
• Re-launched the MP3.com music destination as the Site Director at CNET Networks. Director level position with General Management level responsibilities.
• Developed and launched Lycos TV, an innovative and profitable streaming video product, in 2001. Delivered an innovative streaming video web application to serve as broadband portal and platform for all regional and country business units and content verticals.
• Well regarded as industry expert in convergence of music, media and technology. Quoted in publications such as Red Herring, Billboard, Digital Music News, eCommerce Times, Wired.com, Villagevoice.com, and others. Continually sought for speaking engagements at various conferences, including Jupiter Media, CTIA Wireless, SXSW, CMJ, Portable Media Expo, Music 2.0, London Calling, Mobile Content World, Popkomm, NARM and others.
