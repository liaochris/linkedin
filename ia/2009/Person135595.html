Faruk Ates’s Summary

Faruk Ateş is a creative design & web development professional living in San Francisco. He writes and speaks about making great websites and other things he considers important.

A long-time enthusiast for all things Web, Faruk has held positions over the past nine years ranging from design lead, back-end infrastructure architect, front-end developer & web accessibility consultant.

After a three year tour of duty at Apple, Inc., Faruk is now in search of new challenges in the world of (UX) Design and Development and plans to actively contribute to the Web Standards & Web Accessibility movements once again.
