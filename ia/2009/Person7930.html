Alexandra Brown’s Summary

A senior executive with extensive leadership and cross-functional experience in the digital, broadcasting, e-commerce, social media and communications sectors. A reputation for success at the operational, corporate and board levels in both public and private corporations. Achieves best results in a dynamic, entrepreneurial environment, and has proven expertise in high-profile roles where a seasoned voice is needed. Recognized as a respected leader, motivator and coach.

Currently, Alex operates Alex B. & Associates, and provides strategic, business development, and marketing/ communications advice to businesses in the online content, media and digital sectors, and those wishing to incorporate social media into their overall marketing plans.

Prior to this, Alex was at eBay Canada as a director of communications and public affairs, and a member of the senior management team, responsible for growing the online community of eBay users. She joined eBay as one of the first employees in 2002, and led the award-winning communications launch of the eBay Canada French-language site in 2006.

Prior to eBay, Alex spent a year as an independent consultant. She acted as an advisor to the Craig Broadcast Systems "Toronto One" television channel application, and as the Project Director for CanWest Global's launch of its six digital specialty channels in September 2001.

Before this, Alex spent eight years Alliance Atlantis Communications. She was the first employee hired in the Atlantis Broadcasting Group and eventually became Sr. Vice President of that division. At Alliance Atlantis, she was the Sr. Vice President of Corporate Communications, responsible for the company's international corporate reputation, brand management, industry and government relations, corporate and television marketing, crisis and issues management, and philanthropic policy.

Alex started her career working at TVOntario as a switchboard operator.

Alexandra Brown’s Specialties:

Senior executive with a specialty in communications, marketing, public relations, media relations, social media, internet, television channel and start-up launches, e-commerce, digital, project management, employee mentoring, non-profit, fundraising and charity. Known for insightful strategic planning and flawless execution.
