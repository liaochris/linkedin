Sandra DeChant’s Summary

My company is a boutique, Talent Consulting Group. Insight ITC provides search & recruitment at the Professional & Executive level. We also serve clients by providing companies a highly customized, flexible workforce deployment & strategies as well as outplacement services. We reduce the cost of workforce engagement by eliminating the need to engage multiple staffing vendors. Insight ITC was founded on the premise that we help business solve problems by finding them the ideal talent to do so. I started building a 17 year track record of having placed key talent for my clients. Insight ITC was certified as a Women Business Enterprise in 2008 and is a member of NAPS.
