Eamonn Clarke’s Summary

Always prepared to discuss promotional opportunities for fashion retailers and publishers across social media.

Schway is an interactive and engaging application where users create outfits on a mannequin. People experiment with and coordinate styles. Users get easy access to buy their favourite ensembles or outfits can be sent to friends across networks.

Schway retains the online visitor by entertaining them with the possibilities of fashion whilst at the same time, proliferating womenswear, menswear, teen and childrenswear brands across social media.

Our goal is to work with fashion retailers looking to maximise sales opportunities, expanding online both domestically and internationally.

Schway will gain maximum exposure for products and brands across social media, engaging new customers and driving traffic to increase conversions.

    Schway Ltd offers the proprietary software, Schway®, a business to business creative service issued under licence to online retailers in the apparel and footwear markets.
