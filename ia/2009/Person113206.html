Jim McManus’s Experience

  • Director of Public Health

    Birmingham City Council

    (Government Agency; Government Administration industry)

    November 2008 — Present (7 months)

    My role is to ensure that the City Council responds to health inequalities and to lead a team of staff working to provide public health information and evidence. I work with Directors of Public Health in the Birmingham NHS Primary Care Trust to develop an effective public health function for our city. My current main projects include the Joint Strategic Needs Assessments, Needs assessments and equity audits, health inequalities projects, being the lead Director of Public Health on child public health, prediction and prevention of need in older adults, supporting people developing public health skills and being the lead Director of Public Health on end of Life Care.

    (Religious Institutions industry)
