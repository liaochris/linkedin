Richard Santiago’s Summary

I am currently a Senior Consultant/Team Manager with IT:Rec Ltd. I provide a specialist recruitment service within the IT industry, servicing leading Telecoms, Banking/Finance clients and also Global Consultancies/IT Service providers. I specialise in delivering a full life cycle recruitment/HR consulting service to established clients across numerous verticals. This includes working with functional and technical business teams to accurately define requirements, sourcing suitable candidates, managing the full recruitment life cycle and recommending efficient and cost effective solutions. Liaising daily with internal and external HR/Recruitment teams, including outsourcing agencies and managed service consultancies, working to predefined SLA’s and KPI’s to provide suitable candidates for both technical and non technical roles up to Director and Board level.

Whether you are a candidate looking for the right job or an employer looking to fill a specific vacancy I can offer a successful recruitment solution for you.
