Ariens AMP’s Summary

New Eco Friendly Riding Electric Mower


Ariens, the company customers have trusted for rugged outdoor power equipment for more than 75 years recently introduced an all electric mower, the AMP™ Rider electric mower.

The new riding electric mower is eco friendly, clean, easy and powerful.

Eco Friendly

The AMPTM Rider electric mower has no gas engine emissions and is 50% quieter than gas engine mowers.

Clean

The electric mower requires no gasoline or oil to manage. The electric mower eliminates fumes, spills, mess, fire hazards, annual maintenance and trips to the gas station.

Easy

The AMPTM Rider electric mower is easier than driving a car. Unplug the charger, turn the key to "on," and you're off. When you're finished, plug the AMPTM Rider electric mower back into the charger and forget it. The electric mower charger fully charges the batteries in 16 hours and then hibernates when no charge is required. For the off-season, simply leave the electric mower plugged into the charger. The charger ensures the batteries remain fully charged, extending the life of the batteries.

Powerful

The AMPTM Rider electric mower delivers as much drive and cutting power as a conventional rear engine rider mower. The AMPTM Rider's cutting time averages up to 75 minutes.

The AMPTM Rider electric mower will be available Spring 2009 in select The Home Depot stores and Ariens Certified AMPTM Dealers.
