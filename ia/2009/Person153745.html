Martin Fawley’s Summary

Martin is an experienced project manager having worked on a variety of projects in the Aerospace, Defence, Naval, Health and Nuclear industries. He has a strong delivery focus, having successfully delivered several key projects involving the management of teams up to 20 staff. Martin attained his APMP qualification in June 2006 and is also a PRINCE2 Registered Practitioner.

Martin has recently joined Purple Secure Systems where he will be working as a Project Manager on a large UAV related development project.

During his time at Praxis, Martin was the Operations Manager for the company where he was responsible for assuring delivery on all projects within the company and managing company risk. This involved day-to-day interfacing with Project Managers as well as regular formal reviews. Martin was also the champion for the company’s Project/Programme Management discipline. This included rolling Earned Value Management out across the business.

Martin’s skills have developed from technical roots in software engineering. His work has focussed on the development and analysis of software applications, usually working closely with clients, and requiring an understanding of business as well as technical requirements
