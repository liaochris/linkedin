Claudia Resta’s Summary

Journalist, TV author, anchor and community manager with a broad experience in International Journalism, specialized in Travel, Entertainment, Women and Food. Several years of experience in web content and community management, five years working for major Italian broadcasting groups (TheBlogTV SpA, SitCom S.p.A. - both Sky Group). Freelance by one the biggest publishers in Italy (Arnoldo Mondadori Editore S.p.A. - Panorama Travel).

Six years in professional TV documentary journalism corresponding from Italy and USA for satellite channels Alice TV, Danish Broadcast and Made in Italy Channel.

Eight years in professional radio journalism.

Expert in web creativity, online community generation and in the integration of the Internet with others media.

Trilingual: Italian, English and French. Highly motivated and focused, always interested in exploring new challenges in the means of communication.

Specialties:
TV, Online, Radio and traditional Journalism, Web Content & Community Management, HTML, Tv production, Photo editing (Photoshop), MAC OS 9 and X, Ms Office, Translation from English and Italian, Work optimization, Speaker and Dubber.
