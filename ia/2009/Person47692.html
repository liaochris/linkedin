CA Manish Iyer’s Summary

My vision is to create an indian brand globally recognised for its audit and assurance, financial advisory, leagal advisory, investment advisory, insurance advisory and management consulting including HR services.

I am the pioneer of the column on IFRSs in Ahmedabad Chartered Accountants Association Journal. I have delivered many presentations and conducted training sessions for corporate in India on Indian GAAP and IFRS. I have written articles on IFRS application in The Chartered Accountant Journal of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. I have handled IFRS conversion and consulting assignments. With IFRS becoming the financial reporting framework in the developed and emerging markets, I am looking to increase my network and open to invitations and assignments on IFRS in any such market.
