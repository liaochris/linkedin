Rob Burns’s Summary

Most people have got marketing entirely wrong.

The result is they spend fortunes making things 'look pretty' believing that alone will create profitable customers: throw enough mud at a wall, and eventually something will stick, right? It won't.

Or that brand is more important than service and substance and a great product. It isn't.

And oppositely, the belief that "if you build a better mousetrap, the world will beat a path to your door". It's frustrating but true, there are people with terrible products who are doing much better than you ( and the best satisfaction is beating them).

In my book, marketing exists for one reason and one reason only: proa-actively creating PROFITABLE relationships with customers.

You don't need more ideas, you need better, more profitable strategies and marketing funnels - which really make a difference you can measure.

Strategies which respect your precious time, cashflow, and the scale of your business.

My approach is unconventional. My help rarely coalesces into some kind of 'leaflet' or 'brochure'. It's all about converting customers. And for that reason, I charge for my knowledge and expertise.

If you think you could do with me on your side, I'd be happy to talk to you and discuss your needs.
