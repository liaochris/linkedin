Quentin de Molliens’s Summary

I have a Masters in Environmental Management from the University of Rennes 2 (France), Haute Bretagne. I previously studied for my undergraduate degree in Geography at the University of Nantes, where I had a particular focus on coastal and marine environments. My interest in the political side of environmental issues stems in part from my involvement in clean-up operations after the French Oils spills.

I came to E3G Third Generation Environmentalism in London between March 2007 and September 2007 with the support of the EU Leonardo da Vinci programme, and follows time spent working on a project investigating ways to assist the ecological recovery of the river Loire.

I worked for this NGO as a freelance editor position at the end of my internship until December 2007.

I am Back in France now and working currently for a young NGO le Centre de la Consommation Durable as a project manager.

I also have my own blog TheOriginalgardener (www.univert.wordpress.com), and I enjoy taking part in the green blogosphere. I am a keen surfer, a fan of rugby, and have a passion for DJing.
