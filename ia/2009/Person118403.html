John M. Whitney, Ph.D.’s Summary

I'm a proponent for the systematic use of digital chemical information and tools to accelerate chemical R&D. To meet this goal, I employ statistics, molecular modeling, predictive algorithms, chemical databases, and semantic search technology. I always say, "A day in the library saves a month in the lab".

I'm also a strong believer in corporate citizenship. I strive to exceed my job description and use my talents to help others outside the chemical R&D environment.

In my spare time, I like to volunteer my programming/website skills for the community. In particular, I'm a proponent for SharePoint technologies, and actively contribute on CodePlex. Visit my two websites to see more! I'm also in the process of reading the top 100 science fiction novels of all time.

My current goal is to achieve principal chemist status in the next few years.
