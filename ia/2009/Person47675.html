  • Job-search help

    Human Transitions

    (Privately Held; Human Resources industry)

    March 2009 — Present (9 months)

    We provide unemployed, underemployed, and at risk employees a chance to attend a one or two day session that will help them jumpstart a search for their next career.

    While there is terrific support available from companies that provide in depth services, at a cost of somtimes thousands of dollars per worker, we provide the alternative of a quick response, in a much shorter timeframe, for much lower fees.

    In our sessions we review, but are not limited to, the following:
    - psychological issues of job loss
    - resume prep
    - interview skills, techniques, role plays
    - job boards
    - recruiting agencies
    - networking
    - common sense job search reviews
    - perspective from people that have gone through it
    - dos and don'ts
    - personal approach tailored to the group

    We provide our service to church groups in short, mostly night or weekend sessions, and to companies and municipalities, who seek to help their laid off workers become efficient more quickly in a new job search.

    The benefits for the companies and municipalities that participate?
    1) good will from employees
    2) possible reduced unemployment costs for workers who get work
    3) much more economical than 3-month programs

    We don't pretend to be psychologists or sociologists, but simply people who have gone through the process, have empathy for others going through it, and have practical experience in executing a search. We know what works and we provide sessions to get that across quickly. And, we have a passion to help people in this tough economic climate.

    We keep our groups to 24, or less, so that as we lead the group, the group can lead us into specific areas where they need more focus. We offer a personal touch, and will spend some extra time, as required by our groups. We don't rush anyone. We offer to provide a free professional email address for our job seekers, and we're available by phone for our groups, even after the group sessions are completed.
