Jim Grady’s Summary

I am a 17-year veteran of the global mobile/wireless/Internet business. My first 8 years were spent on the carrier/operator side of the business at AirTouch/PrimeCo (now Verizon Wireless/Vodafone), where I worked in marketing & sales before I became a VP/General Manager. For the last 9 years, I have worked in general management, marketing, product management and sales on the vendor side of the business. I have helped build several telecom software and equipment start-ups from the ground up, scaled revenue up to $100M, and built teams around the world.

Before AirTouch, I worked in consulting at The Boston Consulting Group (BCG).

My formal name is James H. Grady
