Jeffrey Cohen’s Summary

Seasoned management consultant with emphasis in executive coaching, team building, training, career counseling, OD/change management, talent management and psychological assessment. I've been an executive coach at the "C" level for over 20 years and have written over 1500 psychological assessments. Have expertise in and broad exposure with compensation issues, especially incentive compensation. Senior-level leadership roles at Revlon's International Division and Hertz combined with advanced degrees and multi-faceted consulting, have provided practical experience and state-of-the art knowledge.

Design and distribute a periodic newsletter on talent management, OD, coaching and other topics that interest me.

Some well-known clients: AXA Equitable, Chanel, Chase Bank, City College of NY, Colgate, Deutsche Bank, Hermes, Homecare Industies, IKON, ITT, Johnson & Johnson, KPMG Peat, Malcolm Pirnie, Met Life, New York Life, New York Stock Exchange, NYU, Pepsi, Philips Electronics, SBLI, TIAA-CREF and Wintrop Hospital. Clients have also included smaller companies, not-for-profits and governmental agencies.

Recently served two terms as the Co-President of the NY Human Resources Planning Society (HRPS) and was a Board member of the NY chapter of Society for Human Resources Management (SHRM).

"BS" from Penn State in psychology; "MBA" in Industrial/Organizational (I/O) psychology from Baruch College (CUNY); "ABD" in I/O psychology from Akron University.

Advanced Certification in Myers-Briggs (MBTI), CEI (emotional intelligence) and other instruments.

Jeffrey Cohen’s Specialties:

A 20+ years as an executive coach with an emphasis in helping senior execs solve knotty "business" problems. Also, provide career counseling, team-building, psychological assessment, training, design of compensation plans, incentive and performance and talent management systems and organizational change initiatives.
