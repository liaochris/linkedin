Massimiliano Schiraldi’s Summary

Assistant Professor in "Industrial Plants" (Scientific Sector ING-IND/17), he teaches courses of Operations Management since 2001: since 2001 is co-lecturer of the course of "Industrial Plants Management"; since 2003 is the lecturer of the course of "Production Management" and since 2004 of the course of "Industrial Plant 1" at the Faculty of Engineering. Since 2006 he is on tenure-track.
Since 2002 he had directly organized or participated in the organization of more than 20 research projects, either at national or international level, including several FP6 projects. He is the author of more than 20 scientific publications related to Operations Management, and he supervised more than 60 students in their thesis work among the Faculties in Rome and in Naples.

Massimiliano Schiraldi’s Specialties:

Production Systems Design and Operations Management; Lean production; Logistics; Production & Inventory Planning; Stock Reduction; Process Analysis & Optimization; Supply Chain Management; Maintenance & Quality Management; Transportation Systems Management; Project Management; Decision Support Systems Design.
