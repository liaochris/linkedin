Kelly McCarthy’s Summary

Kelly McCarthy helps Bite Communications define a growth strategy for its U.S. operations and is responsible for identifying business development opportunities on the East Coast. The focus of these efforts is to form partnerships and client relationships which will continue to compliment Bite’s ‘fearless & boundless’ approach to communications. More of a matchmaker than salesperson, Kelly concentrates on finding the right partnerships for Bite; helping brands focus on the strength of message and ensure their story resonates with the audience and is consistent with their business objectives. In addition to a business development role, Kelly works with clients to outline the overall strategy of their communications campaigns through a closer examination of sales channels/targets, external and internal stakeholders, socio-economic trends and competitive analysis during the account planning process. Prior to Bite, Kelly worked within a handful of agencies in the San Francisco and Denver areas in the consumer, technology, and healthcare sectors. Past client relationships have spanned the B2B and B2C sectors and have included such names as: Philips, Zinio, Inamed, Xenogen, Xdx, BiogenIdec, ProLogis, Chipotle, Red Robin, Coors, and Hydrogen Works. In addition, Kelly has donated her time and services to a number of non-profit groups, including the George Mark Children’s House in San Leandro, CA.
