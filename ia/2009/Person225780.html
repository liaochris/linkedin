Subash BGK’s Summary

At present, I am busy in creating N-Axis, a company based out of Hyderabad and has interests in Business IT Consulting Services, Software Infrastructure & Development Services and Corporate Training Services.

I worked with SREI Sahaj E-Village Ltd., a subsidiary of SREI Infrastructure Finance Ltd. as Vice President - IT in Kolkata. Sahaj is the SPV for implementing Common Service Centers (CSCs) across India. It is one of the largest greenfield IT projects and one of the mission mode projects of DIT (India). It has Wipro, HP, Intel, Oracle, Redhat, Hughes, Bharthi, TataNet etc. as technology partners with a goal to establish 50000 CSCs in rural India across many states in partnership with the Government on PPP and BOO models.

I enjoyed the role and created the IT platform from scratch, which involve rural connectivity across different states, data centers, operations in different states, application platform, integration platform for services aggregation and the kiosk infrastructure. The project started running from day one and I played the role of an architect and creator in an absolute agile and learning environment. I am proud of this engagement as it has brought out the best in me not only in technology but also in bringing out the business clarity.

Prior to Sahaj, I worked in Wipro as a Solution Architect and later promoted as Senior Client Solutions Manager. I worked on variety of large projects in the Indian Government space (Central & State).

Before joining Wipro, for almost ten years, I worked with Start-Ups and Small-to-Medium sized companies like Indialeads (My Own), Compulearn, IKF and CES (Computech) as an enterpreneur in Mumbai and Hyderabad. I handled positions like CEO, CTO, Project Manager, IT Head etc.
