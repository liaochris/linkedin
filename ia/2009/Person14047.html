Andrew Bashfield’s Summary

Focussing on businesses engaged in geospatial and asset information management and design in the telco, water, power, civil infrastructure and government industry sectors, Andrew provides marketing and sales operations services on a part time or consultancy basis.

Drawing on his wealth of sales, marketing, project management and training experience with major vendors, local consultancies and professional associations, Andrew can design and execute a strategic program to grow a business's profile to be recognised as the leader in its field by customers and prospects.

To complement an effective thought leadership program, Andrew can structure a formal sales prospecting, pipeline management and forecasting process to ensure that sales activities can be planned, tracked and evaluated.

Andrew Bashfield’s Specialties:

He has extensive experience in product and services sales and marketing management, project and technical staff management, application training, budgeting, strategic planning, presenting at board and user levels, publications, conferences and media.
