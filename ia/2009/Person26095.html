Ben Humphry’s Summary

An experienced and well known member of the digital advertising industry, specialising in complex organisational and workflow challenges, having run MSN's International Sales Operations for several years.

I've recently introduced Adtoma Fusion in the UK market. Fusion is a next-generation solution for digital media owners; a fully integrated combination of CRM, workflow and ad server behind a slick, powerful interface.

Fusion represents a revolution; one platform for all advertising sales and operations activities, allowing proposals to be put together in seconds, using life data and pricing.

Previously, I created a compelling online vision for Vio and numerous others in a consulting capacity, while at Microsoft/MSN I managed the global transition of multiple independent sales teams into a single streamlined sales force with common processes, goals and objectives.

I have a depth of experience acting as principal consultant on numerous strategy, operations, workflow and organisation projects. My client list is varied, and includes many large media owners such as MSN, AOL, BSkyB, Vodafone.
