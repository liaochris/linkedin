Michael Anthony’s Summary

I am the vice president of Paradise Profits, and editor for a new book just released titled: Real Estate Investing Guide--Costa Rica: Fundamentals for Profit in Paradise, by Jeffrey P. Hickcox with a Foreword by Than Merrill. This book offers strategies for sound investing in Costa Rica real estate by providing access to a network of trusted professionals on the ground in Costa Rica. Topics covered include real estate law in Costa Rica, building spec homes, remodeling homes, raw land development, passive strategies and "green" techniques. This book offers a great balance of material for the beginning or advanced investor.

With my other company, Ameurope Global Services, I am a managing partner. Our company offers Costa Rica relocation services, real estate listings in Escazu, Costa Rica, investment opportunities, tourism services, as well as professional services in editing, writing, and public relations.
