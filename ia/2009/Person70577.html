Evan Stein’s Summary

After completing the Medical Scientist Training Program (MD/PhD) at NYU School of Medicine in 2004, I spent one year as a general medicine intern at NYU, and then began four years of training in general radiology at Montefiore Medical Center. In 2009, I will be a Neuroradiology fellow at Mt. Sinai Hospital in New York City.
