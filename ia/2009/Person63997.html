Dajana Dimovska’s Summary

I am a game developer looking for new challenges and opportunities to express my creativity and skills in game development and research.

My interest in games is personal and professional, which is why I am educated in Media Technology and Games and I hold a master degree in Game Technology and Development.
The past 2 years I have been actively working on designing and developing small indie experimental games (some of them award winning and can be found on www.copenhagengamecollective.com). My professional knowledge varies from solid design to solid programming skills as well as strong team work and management skills.

Currently, I am a game producer on an indie Wii game project, which is a new and great challenge for me that I enjoy solving.

My professional motivation and ambition is developing innovative games that provide game experience that can change or improve some aspects of the human everyday life.

Please contact me if you find my profile interesting for possible career opportunities or networking.
