Tom Lopilato’s Summary

I'm looking to build NewFiction.com into a premier web destination.

We are an innovative platform that delivers new fiction in a digital audio format. This is not Books-on-Tapes. We are more like a modern version of the old-time radio show.

Subscribers can receive a variety of dramatized fiction via the web, podcast, cell phone, iPod or email. We also have a social media component where readers can discuss the various stories.

I am looking for help to build our membership as well as our library. I'm interested in talking with publishers, authors, e-marketers, web innovators, social media experts, movie production companies, agents, and venture capitalists.

Please take a minute to visit us at www.newfiction.com.
