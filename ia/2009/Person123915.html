Julie Murphy’s Summary

I am a public health/drug and alcohol librarian working in a research institute devoted to prevention of drug,alcohol,tobacco and other public health problems. I have extensive experience managing both a one person library and providing virtual library services to our Centers across the country.

Over the last twenty years I have worked in legislative, academic, public, school, and special libraries, including the United States Senate Library. I have also worked as a consultant to school libraries regarding retrospective conversion of catalog records, and created web site mock-ups for a dot.com.

My professional interests include medical and public health librarianship, and keeping abreast of the Open Access Movement to make scholarly publications more freely available to researchers and the public.

Julie Murphy’s Specialties:

Negotiating with library and database vendors; searching social science and medical databases for public health literature; collection development of specialized materials.
