Alistair McFarlane’s Summary

Key experiences:-

Relevant Experience:-

Throughout his career, Alistair has gained experience within both the Certification arena and Research & Development fields, enabling him to support a strong industry balance when addressing emerging certification schemes.

His exposure to Telecoms commenced with the approval of TACS, E-TACS and GSM devices under the old FTA ‘Full Type Approval’ scheme. Whilst supporting the role of a conformance engineer, within an UCAS accredited test facility, Alistair also supported the validation of specific certification criteria on ATE platforms prior to their inclusion into approval schemes, providing him with solid foundations within the Cellular Telecoms industry he works within.

Alistair’s further experiences include the management of Global Field Trial Teams, NO-IOT and NV-IOT focused teams, and the management of a Conformance Facility (Manufacturers Internal Test Laboratory) focusing on the execution of the certification criteria to support GCF declarations, as well as ‘pre compliance’ PTCRB test execution, and the compliance management of R&TTE applications using both internal and external Test facilities.

Alistair has also supported, Issue responsible, attendance at numerous industry forums such as the GCF-SG, GCF-AG, GCF-FTAG, GCF-SWG, EICTA MTG and NVIOT Forum, presenting technical papers and proposals, defending requirements addressing certification development, and has long running experience of Multi site Chairmanship of business focused project meetings.

Alistair has been responsible for the Implementation of a manufacturer’s ‘Assessment Compliant’ and ‘Quality Competent’ status, allowing the organisation represented, to become an active member of the GCF and self declare terminals compliant with the GCF Certification Scheme.
