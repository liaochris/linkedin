Marta Segura, MPH’s Summary

Marta Segura M.P.H. is a highly sought after leadership coach, speaker, trainer, facilitatator and writer who works passionately to inspire and develop Multiucultural Leaders and youth to live and work at their full potential. She engages everyone she meets to find their sweet spot, turn their lives in to their dreams and their talents into their treasures. In mainstream this is referred to as personal development or professional growth. Cater our workshops to leaders, professionals, business teams and organizations ready to shift into action. Wrked extensivley in the Environmental Sector, a Masters Degree in Environmental Sci, capably crosses over leadership skills to intersect with Green Expertise. Focus on Multicultural Leadership also includes sessions on professional development, Conflict Transformation, Board development and Fundraising Planning. Has 15 yrs experience in the non profit, philanthropic, academic and private sectors combined. She is the owner of The Inspired Planet for Leadership Consulting. It's an integral leadership development approach that has been successful in diverse work places. Also successful in fundraising planning, grantmaking, proposal writing and program design. Additional expertise in capacity building, strengthening leadership, strategic planning, fundraising systems analysis and organizational development assessments to create sustainable, abundant and thriving communities. 15 years of leadership experience and breaking glass ceilings have led to the development of an arsenal of tools in the philanthropic, non profit, Academic and private sectors. Marta is also fluent in Spanish and has worked with clients in Latin America. Marta has had great success with transition periods and implementing several tools to address moving forward with excellence. Marta’s interest in creating leaders for the future, has drawn her to present at Youth Conferences and workshops for several Non Profits. To serve is to lead.
