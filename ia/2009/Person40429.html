Cristina Favreau’s Summary

I specialize in helping passionate & motivated professionals in the service industry who love what they do, but struggle with running & marketing their business. Discover simple ways to gain visibility, credibility & get more clients. Within 3 hours, I’ll help you identify where you’re stuck in the marketing process & come up with an action plan to gain immediate momentum.

If you find my no-nonsense, plain language approach appealing, you’re invited to subscribe to my monthly newsletter, The Journey is the Point, on http://www.cristinafavreau.com/. You’ll get my no-charge guidebook, “Designing a Compelling 30-second Intro;” each month, you’ll receive straightforward and simple tips, suggestions and advice for starting, running & marketing your service-based business. Join me on my blog, The Savvy Entrepreneur, as I call things the way I see them at http://blog.cristinafavreau.com.

Some of my skills: Focused on your needs and goals, Results-oriented, Good listener, Finely tuned communication skills, High responsiveness, Honesty, Natural troubleshooter, Insightful, Methodical (not mechanical), Passionate, Resourceful, Determined, Energetic, Bilingual (French/English).

Cristina Favreau’s Specialties:

First, I help you identify where in the marketing cycle you're stuck. Then I help you come up with strategies to get you "unstuck". I break down the steps into manageable, realistic, bite-size pieces so that you can get started right away reaching your smaller and/or short-term goals. I found that this process works best to give you the momentum needed to reach your bigger and/or long-term goals.
