Katie Curran’s Summary

Katie is a Talent Acquisition/Recruiting and Talent Management leader with 12 years of experience with a boutique healthcare consulting firm, IBM, and national retained search firms. Her background includes a proven track record of developing recruiting departments as well as developing, implementing and administering cost-effective, full lifecycle recruitment programs and strategies. Her accomplishments include:

• Creating the firm’s first Recruiting department and corresponding strategies to support the achievement of business goals and objectives by partnering with senior business leaders to identify and fill talent gaps in the organization.
• Effectively managing the full lifecycle recruiting function, vendor relationships, workforce planning, metrics, and compensation guidelines as well as numerous other initiatives.
• Successfully leading the vendor evaluation and selection of the firm’s first Applicant Tracking System (ATS) including needs assessments and current and future state analysis.
• Functioning as a sourcing tool (AIRS) Super User and training fellow teammates.
