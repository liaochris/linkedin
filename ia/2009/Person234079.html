Tim Poston’s Summary

I have published with co-authors ranging from archaeologist to brain surgeon, in journals from psychology to mathematics to geography: my two books (on catastrophe/singularity theory, and on differential geometry and relativity) are still in print after a third of a century. My professional goal is to make software -- whether managing documents or manipulating 3D objects and worlds -- as natural and obvious to use as cutlery.

Tim Poston’s Specialties:

Singularity theory, cognitive ergonomics, geometry, algorithm design, patent writing.
