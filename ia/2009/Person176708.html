Nona Jordan’s Summary

Martha Beck Trained Coach, educator, speaker, and Yoga Instructor, Nona Jordan guides women and men experiencing health challenges and life changes to connect to their innate happiness, peace, and purpose through mind-body coaching.

Pain, both physical and emotional, provides amazing opportunities to become the best version of who we are meant to be. Being coached by Nona provides a safe place and proven methodolgy to move through the pain and embrace the gift. Clients often discover renewed purpose and meaning, while connecting with deep peace and happiness.
