George Lambelle’s Summary

BBC Producer/director for more than thirty years, winning five awards involving documentaries, outside broadcasts and music programming, as well as minor awards in outside broadcasting and other public participation events. Experienced in live programme production, edited or "built" programmes off tape, radio and television direction. Correspondent/editor in sport, politics, music, news reporting and news editing, programme and budget control, interviewing (evderyone from Margaret Thatcher and Tony Blair/ Rolling Stones and the Beatles as well as leaders of regional and local government, industrial experts and general reporting/interviewing involving members of the public.
Obsidian makes use of all these developed techniques and through broadcasting contacts over three decades can employ broadcast professionals to fulfil any specific needs in support of the company's director.

George Lambelle’s Specialties:

Broadcast staff management (especially journalists working to news desk under my control; and general programme producers requiring use of my budget etc etc), fee negotiation, broadcasting and journalistic legalities (from early training as a journalist under the Natoinal Concil for the Training of Jourhalists plus BBC specialist training over many years, seminars, specialist courses and lectures; also handled "hands on" staff training and mentoring of new staff)
