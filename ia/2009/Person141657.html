Ron McManmon’s Summary

ronm@linkedcv.com

"Follow the passion, and you will be successful in every way!"

Ron is the visionary and Architect of the "Career Marketing Program" (CMP). A successful sales and marketing process that has been developed and integrated into a powerful technical step by step solution and the objective is to help people with their careers (for both long and short term goals). Ron has been coaching people on all levels for close to a decade and this is his passion.

Ron's past experience includes but is not limited to:

May 2001- Ranked the #1 MRI Recruiter (out of 5000 recruiters) in the Pacific Region within 18 months from start and maintained a Top 5% status, nationwide.

Ron's career sales exceed $300 million. While the majority of these sales were 'Grandfathered-In" Ron's achievements in this sector of his career was providing tireless Sales and Marketing support and Training to thousands of customers. This said, Ron is a hunter but lives by the saying,"anyone can sell product in, but the art is selling it through"!

FOR FUN: Ron searched for the Santiago El Grande (a Spanish galleon) on the Bahamas Banks for over six months and prepared a complete business prospectus, on-site evaluation and operations process for the archaeological excavation of King Louis XIV’s Royal fleet, which sank off the coast of Venezuela in 1678. The excavation was coordinated in conjunction with the government of Venezuela.
