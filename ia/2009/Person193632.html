Randy Bernard’s Summary

Sales & Recruiting professional who is interested in speaking with people in the Healthcare and IT industries regarding positions for which they are looking, and clients interested in filling positions with qualified candidates. I have a no-nonsense approach to recruiting which includes sourcing, qualifying, relationship building, and having fun throughout the process. My biggest satisfaction and main goal in my profession is to find people jobs and help them become successful.

I am interested in speaking with people who like to play in the arena of ideas with similar interests. Contact me if you would like to be part of my network.

Randy Bernard’s Specialties:

Sales, contract negotiation, recruiting, client relationships, and think tanking and ideation of products or service flow. The majority of my career has been to take the challenge of an under-performing branch or region and turn it to profitability through process flow analysis and improvement.
