Selena Chan’s Summary

I am interested in opportunities with the consumer lifestyle, tech and entertainment industry as well as film and broadcasting.

My background includes marketing program development and execution, strategy, online and high impact PR launch events.

- Over six years marketing, communications and PR experience with global consumer brands including the Marriott, Canon Europa and Porsche;

- Worked from local office and EMEA headquarters developing and executing the marketing, online and PR strategy, the evaluation, best case practices and budget. Ensured consistency in branding, promotions, messaging, creative and localisation of global toolkits;

- Project managed multimedia launch packages for local markets, used for dealer, trade and press purposes, including executive briefing documents, managing fashion photography, videos and event guidelines;

- Managed an interactive agency to drive SEO and online campaigns. Worked with interactive online tools to promote the Curacao Marriott such as the ‘Virtual Trip Kit’ including creating content for online videos, virtual experiences, social technologies, highly interactive and customized content;

- Worked closely with internal business units to integrate campaigns across all channels of the business and ensure legal product compliance. Worked with external parties including agencies, talent and partners to leverage promotional and business opportunities;

- Winner of PR Week Awards for Best Public Relations Campaign in Europe for the EOS 350D Safari, Chartered Institute of PR.
