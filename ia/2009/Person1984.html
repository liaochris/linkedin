Abiud Diaz Amaro’s Summary

Hi, I'm Abiud Amaro Diaz and I help businesses realize their potential.

The best way to introduce my services is with this phrase: "Waste not. Want not." What it means is that I help you use all your resources effectively so that you don't pay a penny more than what you have to in order to get the services that you need. I work with many business consultants and experts that specialize in creating value and finding more efficient ways to operate. This is a short list of the services I can help you with:

1. Waste disposal review
2. Utility review (telephone, DSL, etc.)
3. Energy efficiency tax credit review
4. Cost segregation review
5. Research and development tax credit
6. Workers Compensation Audit

BUT IF YOU DON'T SEE IT DON'T ASSUME I DON'T HAVE IT. I have more services available. I can also connect you with marketing experts and personnel consultants. For example, if you need some organization in your life I can connect with several personal organizers that can help you be more effective with the use of your time and your space.

Contact me with your problem and let me help you solve it.

To discuss how I might help you please give me a call at (510) 910-6707.

I am open to invitations to connect for business networking. I am specially interested in hearing from CMU and Notre Dame alumni.
