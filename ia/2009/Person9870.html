Alli Joseph’s Summary

WWW.ALLIJOSEPH.COM

WWW.SEVENTHGENERATIONSTORIES.COM

Alli Joseph is an author, TV host/producer, media consultant and personal historian. The recent death of her mother and coinciding birth of her daughter effected her profoundly; so much so that she decided to shift her focus from entertaiment production to a more human, service-based business. She founded Seventh Generation Stories to help Baby Boomers, their adult children, older folks and young couples just starting out by capturig their personal and family histories across a variety of media products and services.

Alli uses the latest storytelling technologies, and comes out of a 14-year background hosting and producing interactive media projects and shows for companies like NBC, USA Networks, CBS News, VH1, TNT, and many more.

Joseph developed her ability to spot hot trends and pop culture branding opportunities for clients over many years working as a creative for large media, advertising, marketing and Internet companies like Grey Worldwide, Margeotes Fertita Partners, Viacom, Cablevision, NBC, Hotjobs.com/Yahoo, AOL and Hachette Filipacchi.

Alli's writing has appeared in numerous publications, including People Magazine, Premiere, George, Maxim, Playboy, Hamptons, Gotham, the Miami Herald and the New York Post. She is also the author of a sports/self-help book for Harper Collins called "The Shaolin Way: Ten Modern Secrets of Survival from a Shaolin Grandmaster".

Joseph is a certified Emergency Medical Technician, sometime dolphin cognition researcher, and former model. She also has produced several documentary films. A native New Yorker, she loves to drive fast and lives for six-speed transmissions and putting her foot on the floor...mostly in other peoples' very expensive cars.

Alli Joseph’s Specialties:

Professional historian/oral history storyteller, on-air host/reporter,senior-level producer with top-market experience, high-end digital strategy/development consultant, published writer and author, multi and traditional media production (digital media specialty), writing and producing for television, documentary film and print (entertainment and news genres, features), media relations and public speaking, autosport marketing.
