Emilio Pedral’s Summary

I am a passionate, optimistic and motivated sales and marketing manager with international business experience. I know that the most important part of the sales process is to understand the customer. Hence, I speak four languages, English, Spanish, Italian and German.

I know that it’s quite possible to work without results, but that there will never be results without work. Therefore, my formula is to perform sales based on the value that the product delivers and not only its manufacturing cost. I will always maximize the profit generation and will consider that every part of the strategy has to create return on the investment.

Challenges are always welcome and I enjoy winning, but I like it better when it comes as team effort and with the customer's satisfaction and loyalty.

This is my passion... Got Sales?
