Adam Flavell’s Summary

I am a senior eCommerce professional with nine years experience with large-scale on-line retailers. My experience spans all aspects of eCommerce from customer services, supply chain and logistics to software development and infrastructure management. I specialise in Oracle systems integration, data management and fulfilment systems, specifically from a multi-channel and drop-ship perspective. I enjoy delivering fast, flexible and dynamic technical solutions to complex eCommerce problems.

Technical
Programming: Up to seven years experience with SQL, PL/SQL, Java, Application Express (APEX 1.6 – 3.2), VB, VBA, PHP, MS Access as a database and Oracle front-end, VBA applications (MS Excel, Access and Outlook).
Tuning & troubleshooting: Oracle systems (8i,-11g).
Systems integration: Six years experience integrating Oracle with core retail systems (JDA, SAP, Manahatten), POS, 3rd party data (EUK, Bertrams, Dawsons, Party Delights etc).
Systems administration: Apache, OHS web servers, ATG Dynamo, OC4J and Tomcat app servers, Oracle Application Express (APEX 1.6 – 3.2), Oracle 8i-11g and MySQL 4/5 db servers, Verity and Fredhopper search servers.
Operating systems: Windows (98 and NT through to 2008 server and Windows 7), Linux (RHEL 4/5, Ubuntu 8/9 and Fedora 7/8/9) and Unix (Solaris 7/8/9).
I have a keen interest in new and high performance computing and networking technologies.

Leadership, Management and team building
I have recruited and built a successful team of IT professionals and managed temporary staff. I lead by example; I am hard working, support & coach team members and encourage good communications. I have project-managed large-scale systems development and integration projects.

Customer Focus
Having worked both for retailers and service providers I understand the need for responsive and diligent customer service. I am able to understand business requirements quickly and translate them into pragmatic or strategic technical solutions.
