Bernadette Martin’s Summary

Bernadette Martin, Founder of Visibility Branding, LLC is a Career Transition Brand Strategist where she helps individuals develop an online and offline strategy to build and express their professional and personal brand. She delivers career development workshops to groups in corporate and academic environments as well as 1:1 sessions in virtual and on-site locations.Clients include professionals across any industry sector, entrepreneurs, MBA graduates, coaches and consultants. As host of the Visibility Branding Webinar Series she interviews authors and experts on topics related to career development, personal branding, social networking and 2.0 technology. She is the upcoming author of "I Need a Branded Bio, Now What?" Messaging Your Story Online and Offline.

A 15 year career in brand management took her to Asia, Europe and the U.S., holding senior level positions in corporate, agency and academic environments. In 2002, she transitioned from working with product and service brands to "people" brands and founded her company Visibility Branding.

Bernadette holds an MBA from Thunderbird, School of Global Management and certification as a Reach Personal Brand Strategist. She is President of the Thunderbird Alumni Association, Vice President of the American University Clubs of France and member of the European Professional Women's Network

Brands she has worked with, either in her private practice or corporate career, include Procter & Gamble, Ford, Saatchi & Saatchi, Chanel, Nortel, IBM, Boston Scientific, Cisco, Goldman Sachs, American Express, Citibank, Merck, DHL, Jaguar, Nestle, Hennessey, Jacobs Suchard, Tauck World Discovery and GES Exposition Services.

Academic institutions and associations where she has delivered workshops include: HEC Executive MBA, American Chamber of Commerce, AMA - American Management Association/MCE Management Center Europe, IABC, Schiller University, ParisTech, AUP, ISM, ABS, and European Professional Women's Network.

Bernadette Martin’s Specialties:

Personal Branding for Individuals and Entrepreneurs, Online Identity, Strategic Offline and Online Networking, Coaching and Consulting for Individuals and Entrepreneurs, Resumes, CV, Bio, Online Professional Portfolio, Aligning Online and Offline Brand Messaging, Marketing and Branding for Entrepreneurs, Solopreneurs, Consultants, Coaches
