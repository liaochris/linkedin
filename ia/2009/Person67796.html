Don Sanders’s Summary

For nearly fifteen years, I guess you could say I’ve been an executive for hire. I built my professional management chops in what used to be called the Bell System, including a part in breaking it up, starting in technical management and ending in corporate development. Since then, I’ve started a few companies, sold a couple, been interim CEO, COO and CFO, managed major projects for large public companies, and mostly looked out for people who needed help.

I am broadly experienced in business development, operations and finance, with proven ability to lead professional teams in a variety of business situations. I’m a strong team player, analytical and creative in my problem solving abilities, and have a record of hands-on success in process improvement, project management, and budgeting, finance and administration. What I really enjoy is to help identify an organization's critical business issues, assess the deficiencies and opportunities, and execute on ideas for enhancing competitiveness, increasing margins, and improving customer service.

Don Sanders’s Specialties:

Teamwork, collaboration, leadership, project management. Strategy, planning, process improvement. Group and interpersonal communication. P&L, business operations, finance, administration, controls and reporting.
