Eric Krock’s Summary

• Product management and marketing leader with 17 years of experience defining and selling products and managing customer relationships for large enterprises and media companies in North America, Europe, and Japan.
• Experienced with web application, web services, application service provider (ASP), and enterprise licensed software models and Agile, waterfall, and hybrid development methodologies.
• Proven track record of success repeatedly delivering products on time, to specification, within budget.
• Has managed Linux and Windows servers and web, Windows, and Macintosh client user experiences.
• Has repeatedly recruited and led outstanding teams of product managers, program managers, and marketing staff.
• Successfully promoted adoption of JavaScript on the Internet and delivered first release of the Gecko browser engine that now powers Firefox.
• Defined and delivered the Kontiki Delivery Management System, the leading solution for secure, commercial peer-to-peer content delivery inside the enterprise and on the Internet.
• Passionate about making the best use possible of available time and resources. Believes that the prerequisite for repeatable success is a repeatable process.
• Special expertise in the use and promotion of Internet, open source, and peer-to-peer (P2P) technologies.
• Fluent in Japanese.
