Paul Reimer’s Summary

We specialize in electronic payments technologies commonly deployed by retailers, card issuers and acquirers, and payments processing suppliers.

We perform per engagement based projects for companies in the electronic payments industry, rendering professional services for organizations for which credit and debit cards based payments are mission critical.

We are unique in that we offer not only competent business analysis services on par with competitors, but a true specialization in the systems, infrastructure and technology aspect of the electronic payments industry, possession ample intellectual property in:

(1) the electronic payments supplier community
(2) best practices for implementing retail payment solutions
(3) as a project resource that provides for a working sounding board of implementation best practices

We offer specialized background and expertise in Information and Data Security (PCI DSS), PIN Security, Electronic Payments Technology and Products.

Paul Reimer’s Specialties:

Specializing in the electronic payments industry, providing technology architecture, strategy, analysis, audits and implementation support for issuers, acquirers, retailers, financial institutions, government agencies, and payments networks.
