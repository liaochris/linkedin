Joe Vaughn’s Summary

Joseph Vaughn encapsulates a diverse and eclectic skill set with a strong emphasis on entrepreneurship, leadership, and sales.

Joe has comprehensive sales experience in both the traditional and non-traditional realms. He has held sales positions in manufacturing, retail consumer goods, wholesale consumer goods, and is currently Director of Sales for the worlds largest Social Media Advertising Network.

Joe’s entrepreneurial work ethic and significant contributions has helped two different start-ups become huge successes, one of which he still advises. Joe comes from a long history of family entrepreneurs and always incorporates this spirit, forming intreprenuership. Additionally, he is a public speaking champion and an inter-collegiate business plan competition champion, as he was won multiple awards at the undergraduate, graduate, and PHD level.

Not only has Joe lead specific regions to exponential growth but he has also developed teams to maximize productivity. Through his leadership style, Joe fosters an environment conducive to growth, flexibility, and effectiveness.
