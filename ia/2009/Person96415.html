Hugh Finnan’s Summary

I moved to the US in 2001 after working my way up to senior management for Microsoft Ireland. I joined MS Corp as Worldwide Test Manager for .Net Enterprise Servers with teams in Europe and E.Asia. This role covered such products as SQL Server, Exchange Server, Biztalk Server and Commerce Server. Once my goals were met in this position I was approached by the Exchange Server team to take the role of Lead IPM for the 2003 release. Once this product shipped I got an opportunity to join my passsion for aviation with my love for software development by becoming the multiplayer core feature PM for Flight Simulator X.

After completing the functionality for FSX and 10 years at MS, I was approached by Amazon.com to come build a team and help them launch their M@ business in Europe and Japan. I built the strongest team of Technical Program Managers at Amazon who continue to do great things for Amazon today. I was promoted to Director of Technical Program Management at Amazon where I ran the corporate planning process for the company and executed on the agreed large cross functional programs for the Ecommerce Platform at Amazon. In January 2009 I moved to the Digital organization to take on a new challenge building a world class technical program management team for Amazon Kindle.
