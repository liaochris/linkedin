James Ketchell’s Summary

Starting in 1997 as the co-founder of Web Host Directory, www.webhostdir.com, I was in the privileged position to see the final throws of the "first" internet bubble as it went out with a bang. In reality our world was actually pretty much unaffected by the rapid decline in headlines and IPO's. At the time we really didn't appreciate just how fortunate we had been in choosing the web hosting market as the home for our first internet venture.

Over the coming years we have expanded both our site content and global reach. Opening sites in the UK, Australia and Canada, to compliment our US operations.

Serchen Interactive, was born out of the need to improve campaign management and communication across multiple advertising platforms.

Our combined network has emerged as the recognized leader in matching buyers and sellers of internet services. Almost 11 years on, we now successfully operate 8 sites in the USA, UK, Canada and Australia, providing buyer information on over 11,000 individual service providers around the globe. Our continued goal is to develop the worlds largest marketplace for internet services, through a combination of innovative marketing products, and a dedication to the highest standards in data aggregation.
