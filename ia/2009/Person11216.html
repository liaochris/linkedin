Amelia Whitfield’s Summary

My goal is to introduce people to the beauty, wonder and splendor that is embedded in the very fabric of our being and within the environment that surrounds each and everyone of us. I find beauty and art in the little things that are so often overlooked due to the seemingly insignificance and or the lack of obvious detail within an object. I believe that everything has a purpose and a beauty lying within that purpose. It is my job to capture these things in such a way that it demands a second look and hopefully an appreciation of the pure essence of it's state of being.
