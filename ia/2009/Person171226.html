Nani Saffer’s Summary

Born and raised on the island of Kaua'i, but currently living in the San Francisco Bay Area. Since I can remember, have always had a passion for sales and the art of negotiation. I am fortunate to work for Tracker Corp in the the heart of San Francisco's financial district.

My goal is to always have a goal. The biggest room in the world is the room for self improvement. I am a big advocate for on-going education. Coming from a long line of teachers, I believe that education comes in many forms. The most powerful in my opinion is experience. Either by experiencing it yourself or being mentored by someone who has. The education and mentorship I received DeVry University has given me a solid foundation to set out and accomplish my carrer goals, but had also given me a positive outlook on continuing my education wherever my journey takes me.

Every challenge is an opportunity and every opportunity should be seized. You'll never know unless you try.
