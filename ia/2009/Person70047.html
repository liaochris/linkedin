Edward Altman’s Summary

After graduating from the University of Michigan, I moved to LA and have spent my entire 30-year career in Southern California. While I began my career in the Aerospace industry, the bulk of the first half of my career was in financial services - first joining IBM's LA Banking office, and then spent over (10) years with First Interstate Bank and then Security Pacific Bank.

The last half of my career has been in Media & Entertainment having worked at 20th Century Fox, Paramount Pictures, and then became the CIO at MGM. As CIO, I rebuilt MGM's IT organization which then developed and implemented a critical corporate-wide system that resulted in a nomination for a Computerworld/Smithsonian Award - a first for any Hollywood Studio.

Since leaving MGM I have been on the Systems Integration/vendor side of the business starting both TCS' (TATA Consultancy Services) Global M&E Practice and then HCL Technologies' Global M&E Practice. For many years I was the only Global Practice Head in TCS not based in India. When I started at TCS in 2002, we had (3) M&E clients generating over $12MM in revenue/year. Within (2) years - every major Hollywood Studio was a client. When I left in 2008, TCS had (35) clients generating over $120MM in revenue/year across (7) industry sub-segments/micro-verticals (Hollywood, Music, Publishing, Broadcast Network, Cable Network, Business Information Services, and New Media).

While focusing on the full breadth of technology offerings for the M&E industry at both TCS and HCL - my passion and in-depth expertise is in the area of Digital/Online Media and in particular in establishing a wide eco-system of partners and industry thought leaders to develop and provide solutions to help content companies establish digital media business models that scale and are as frictionless/cost effective as possible.

Edward Altman’s Specialties:

Global I/T and Media Executive with deep skills in all aspects of I/T from both the industry side (former M&E CIO) and the Services/Consulting/Outsourcinig side (Global M&E Practice/Solutions Head), strong skill set in Business & Technology Transformation, I/T Governance, Sourcing strategies and models, and deep expertise in online media - business models and digital infrastructure, excellent personal network that cuts across industry executives, suppliers, start-ups and thought leaders
