Laura Bacchus’s Summary

Bold, innovative, adaptable, driven and results oriented (ROI) “out of the box” communications professional with over 15 years of experience working domestically and internationally (Bangkok, Thailand, China, etc…) in business development, strategic planning, contract development and negotiation, investment banking,capital growth strategies/corporate financial structuring, strategic partnerships and marketing/PR/media relations. Having spent many years in diverse organizational settings (for profit and not for profit) and helping individuals and teams define, develop and implement their strategic vision and growth objectives by leveraging organizational strengths and current capital structure to achieve maximum performance.

A born promoter, Mrs. Bacchus is creatively talented with regards to promotion, marketing, business development and quickly discerning clients needs with the expertise and training to understand the financial bottom line of any business and how the overarching communication goals promote and support the bottom line. Having worked both internationally and domestically with senior executives, Mrs. Bacchus is as adept a focusing on the finer details of a situation as seeing the big picture and always focused on the bottom line ROI.
