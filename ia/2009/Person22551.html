Austin Meyermann’s Summary

Austin Meyermann is the founder and current President of Job Tumbler LLC, a web development company that specializes in creating career center resources for niche industries, such as:

StormwaterJobs.com
WaterandWastewaterJobs.com
allWasteJobs.com
InfrastructureJobsUSA.com
EnvironmentalHealthandSafetyJobs.com
ArchitectandPlannerJobs.com

Austin is also the founder and current President of SideDoor Strategies LLC (www.sidedoorstrategies.com), a company dedicated to developing and distributing high quality educational resources that help students and their families overcome financial and admission hurdles that may be standing in the way of obtaining a degree from a top tier university.

Austin is also the General Manager of Environmental Site Solutions, LLC. ESS specializes in providing comprehensive water quality solutions to site and facility owners/operators throughout the world. From concept to construction, ESS can provide the right solution for clients facing tough problems.

Previously, Austin led Corporate Development for AbTech Industries and is the former Chief Operating Officer of BaySaver Technologies. Both companies are multimillion dollar developer of proprietary water treatment technologies.

Austin holds a bachelors degree in Agricultural and Biological Engineering from Cornell University.
