Paul Sherman’s Summary

You've probably heard that hiring user experience practitioners such as usability engineers, interaction designers, user researchers, etc. will help you to break down the barriers between your organization's business stakeholders, your technologists, and the customers. It's true. I can show you how.

I am a user experience practitioner and strategist. I work at both the tactical and strategic levels, designing product and services as well as long-range customer experience strategies.

I ensure that your organization's products and services are designed to be usable, useful and delightful. I do this by conducting foundational, contextual research with your target customers, design and iterate solutions to your customers' pain points, and validate the solutions via usability assessment.

I also help businesses hire and build in-house user experience talent, as well as help organizations create, implement and assess their customer experience strategies.

Paul Sherman’s Specialties:

Methods:
Usability engineering. Customer/user research. Interaction design, including rapid iterative prototyping and rapid contextual innovation. Systems development life cycle (SDLC) process design and evaluation.

Domains:
Ecommerce/retail web sites. Telecommunications management systems, including voice & data, optical transport, & last-mile access technologies. Financial, tax, accounting & business management applications. Mobile applications. Voice user interfaces.
