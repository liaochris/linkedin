Smith Harrison’s Summary

Voice Over Actor, Producer, Editor.

Fascinated with radio and broadcasting from a young age, I went on to study drama, journalism and English. After college radio, I found work at public and commercial stations, which eventually led to another occupation — doing voice-overs for television and video. I studied under some brilliant voice actors and worked in Los Angeles for four years. Hear some of my varied work in the box below (if connected) or go to my "Websites" section and click through to my demos (if viewing the public profile).

Feel free to contact me about my voice-over services. With a home studio, I have quick project turnarounds — no matter where you are.
