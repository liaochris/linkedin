Jon Wilcox’s Summary

While my main interests are illustration, comics and screen printing, I also have a diverse creative background including endeavors such as design for print and the web, flash development, motion graphics, photography and more.

I am interested in introducing a hand-made quality to things that I design and to create objects that are fun, interesting and eye-catching while maintaining a sense of integrity and sophistication.

Jon Wilcox’s Specialties:

Digital illustration, traditional illustration, comic art, cartoon art, screen printing, web design, adobe flash, html, css, wordpress, digital photography, graphic design, logo and ID development, motion graphics
