dori gillman’s Summary

For over 20 years, I worked in politics. I worked five years on the hill on both House and Senate side. I worked 15 years as a lobbyist. Eleven years for small banks, a few years for education and arts issues and two years for legal services.

I have also worked as a political consultant doing research on various congressional campaigns and on a presidential campaign as a writer when I was first beginning in politics.

I would now like to become a freelance writer. I excel in writing first person articles but I am also proficient in many other types of writing and editing.
