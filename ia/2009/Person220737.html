Spasen Tsenov’s Summary

When I was 10 I participated in programming competition, where the first stage was “BASIC” and the second one Pascal. Thinking about this reminds me the way I started getting into the IT business.

In my early youths - 2003 I have been interested in design, creativity, innovative and business ideas. My age didn’t let me find some big job so I started as an advertisement agent in 2003. Till 2005 I got into web design, web technologies and publishing. For these two years I had done more than 15 books published (poetry, prose, city guides), over 10 web projects (including portal web sites) and a big number of flyers, posters and graphical concepts.

Parallel with this I graduated high school of computer science and Cisco Academy (CCNA). Since 2005 I have redirected myself to advertisement and innovative web and mobile technologies. During 2006 I have worked with Technical University’s research and development team, where I enlarged my experience enormously. In 2007 I became a member of The Olympians EU team (originally from st. Louis, USA) as a marketing and development consultant. Outsourcing to USA helped me develop good relations and gain huge knowledge about marketing and management.

In the late 2007 I established own company - Geen Innovations // Web technologies for the masses.

During my whole life up to now, going through difficulties and successes I’ve always followed my life goals:
• Go deeper into the “market’s organism”.
• Work with the biggest companies in the IT sector
• Become part of the world's “innovative wave”
• One day = one good idea. No good idea today = one lost day.
• Smile everyday
• Make people (company managers :) happy
