Kevin Noonan’s Summary

Kevin is responsible for the design and delivery of Intermedium’s consulting services. His particular area of specialisation is Federal Government ICT where he is sought after as a senior analyst, facilitator and presenter.

Over the last two years, his advice has been sought by more than half of the Top 100 ICT companies, as well as a variety of government agencies. In addition, Kevin has provided mentoring and coaching for managers of more than a dozen small-to-medium enterprises in developing government business.

Kevin has more than 28 years experience in government ICT, including ten years as a government senior executive and Chief Information Officer. Over this time he has held positions responsible for almost every aspect of ICT, ranging from technical infrastructure and major purchasing, through to policy and business change.

Drawing on his management experience in more than six government agencies, Kevin brings a broad understanding of the needs of government, as well as a deep background in many ICT disciplines.

Kevin Noonan’s Specialties:

Government and ICT strategy, procurement, ICT governance, government market analysis, contract engagement, issues management, mergers & acquisitions, facilitation, media commentary
