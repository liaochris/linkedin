Yvonne Levy’s Summary

Bilingual, AFAA Certified Personal Trainer, group fitness instructor and AFPA Sports Nutrition Counselor with 10 years professional fitness experience in the United States. Professional experience includes Group Exercise Manager at Reebok Sports Club/NY, Group Fitness Manager at University of Miami Medical Wellness Center and Director of Personal Training and Group Exercise for Gold’s Gym. Teaching experience includes instructor at Crunch Fitness, Equinox Fitness and Reebok Sports Club/NY. Featured presenter at the Miami Beach Sports and Fitness Festival (2002 - 2005.) Excellent people and communication skills in English and Spanish. Current American Safety & Health Institute CPR certification.

Yvonne Levy’s Specialties:

Management of group exercise programs. Extensive experience in group exercise training as well as one-on-one personal training. Also, very experienced presenting on stage in front of a live audience. Some experience in modeling, acting, radio and TV broadcasting as well.
