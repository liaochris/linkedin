Jacky Misson’s Summary

Founder & Director of Innovation

I set up Nido Marketing in 2003 after being made redundant twice in 8 months and thinking I may as well have a go at running a company myself. 5 years later we employ 13 fantasic, creative full time people with a further 4 in an advisory capacity - so I guess we're doing something right.
After a 10 year career in sales and marketing (starting in door to door kirby vacume cleaner sales and progressing through to recruitment and estate agency) I figured telemarketing was the next 'underdog' industry for me to get my teeth stuck in to!! I truly believed there was (and is) a shortage of reliable, professional, results driven telemarketing companies that genuinely want to do a good job for their clients and ultimately help increase their business.

It is our people at Nido who really set us apart so a big part of my job is making sure they are motivated and interested in their work. I also run the Training division of Nido and enjoy sharing the knowledge and experience we have built up over the years with other companies who realise the importance of a good, polite phone manner - still so important in these days of increasing online communication.

My ultimate goal is to create a company where people genuinely enjoy coming to work and like working with their clients. This in turn leads to great employee and client retention so makes good business sense as well as creating an enjoyable working environment.
Our team have twice voted us Best Place to Work at the local Brighton and Hove Business Awards (Winners 2006, Finalists 2008) and Best Employer at the Sussex Business Awards 2008/09 so I think we are well on our way - which is nice.
