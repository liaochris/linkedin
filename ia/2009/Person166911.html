Manosh Markos’s Summary

Manosh is a result oriented generalist with over 10 years of experience in strategic management, marketing and consulting. His expertise covers business development, marketing analytics, P&L management, product management, project & team management. He has an undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering, with an MBA and a Masters in Information Systems Management. His work experience ranges from start-up to Fortune 500 companies in the Retail, Technology and Advertising sectors. He is presently with Harrah's Entertainment as Corporate Director HR Services & International Mobility.

Manosh brings to the table a diverse background, broad skills, education and experience in several roles. He has traveled extensively in Asia and the United States, and has worked in the US, Hongkong, Singapore and India. He is fluent in a number of South Asian languages including Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, Urdu, Marathi Punjabi & in basic French.
