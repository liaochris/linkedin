Bhavin Desai’s Summary

Bhavin is an experienced Information Security Consultant with an extensive range of skills covering evaluation, consultancy, team leading, and project management. His professional career spans more than 18 years, of which the last 11 years have involved the theory and practice of Information Security.

Bhavin has excellent written and verbal communication skills, together with good negotiating and influencing skills. His work is systematic, logical, and precise, with a passion for attention to detail. He works very closely with every client to ensure mutual satisfaction with all aspects of their project.
