Shyam Srinivas’s Summary

I am currently the Environmental Manager at RSC Equipment Rental, Inc. (NYSE: RRR)

My experience of over 15 years extends to a variety of projects directly related to international operations, International sales, marketing, environmental engineering and management and project management. I planned, organized and executed the implementation of quite a few of the projects from design through startup, including development of Marketing Plans for various companies. I have been very effective in comprehending customer needs, and arriving at simple and cost effective solutions to complex problems. Based on my performance alone I was able to corral over three million dollars in new sales from my customers over a 3-year period. I brought in projects from top companies and opened doors to future business in other areas of those companies. The list of clients includes Kraft Foods (Philip Morris Group of Companies, now Altria), Procter & Gamble Company, Metropolitan St. Louis Sewer District, Fleischmann’s Yeast and Ralcorp Holdings, Inc. I have also developed a new market (International) for a lubricants company and also portfolio companies for a private equity group.
