Mike Herman’s Summary

16 years in the mobile telecom & financial services industries. Mainly specialising in mobile payments and commerce for both profit and not for profit organisations. Last 6 years running a consultancy Stitching Fog supporting early stage activities for large and small companies such as PayPal Mobile, RBS, Brookfield Europe, Simpay, Jersey & Saudi Telecom, Upaid, CLP....

Currently assisting RBS (UK’s second largest global bank) to form their mobile strategy and build the new group mobile team. Working on a number of areas including the value chain and business models in areas such as MMT (mobile money transfer) both domestic and cross border.

Previously business development success at Director level with 40 European mobile operators. Experience ranges from proposition service design and launch to R&D, manufacturing and Infrastructure supply.

Now seeking consulting roles to all players in the mobile marketplace that require concept crystallisation, scoping, definition, delivery or sales.
