Mark Mückenheim’s Summary

urban environments architects is an architectural
design and research network which covers a broad
range of work in architecture, urban planning,
interiors, furniture-, product and media-design.

founded by mark mückenheim in 2001, the office is
utilizing an ever growing network of committed
professionals such as architects, engineers, landscape
architects, designers and artists. some recent affiliations
are with club L94 - landscape architects, greenbox -
landscape architects, owe arup düsseldorf - structural
engineers, fkj - structural engineers, peutz - acoustical
engineers, integralingenieure, winter - ingenieure
and a cooperation with the artist stefan sous.

the work of the office received international recognition
through different publications in architectural design
magazines, such as l’arca, italy; a+ united arab emirates;
beaux arts magazine, france; deutsches architektenblatt –
germany as well as in book publications such as
“contemporary prefab houses”, “1000x European
Architecture” or “taschen's 1000 favourite websites”.

urban environments architects has also gained reputation
through a number of exhibitions in Germany and abroad.

Mark Mückenheim’s Specialties:

urban environments architects
experience is ranging from
