Jon Hedemann’s Summary

If asked to describe myself, I would say that I’m an outgoing, presentable and passionate male with a positive effect on my surroundings. I’m highly motivated, structured, focused on detail and creative whenever needed. I enjoy working in a high-paced and international environment where I can interact with motivated and professional colleagues.

I have project management experience with focus on documentation projects, coordination, production and distribution of sales/marketing material. Other expertise lies within overall text/layout QA and coordination of translation of brochures, booklets and user guides.

I have worked as a technical writer, writing and translating technical documentation in(to) English and Danish. I am also experienced in working with internal communication platforms, primarily Intranet development/management, newsletters and events, and Content Management Systems. I am well-versed in various marketing disciplines, incl. writing web text/press releases/newsletters, website/Intranet management and development, and handling market analysis and campaigns.

Earlier in my career, I have worked both as a full-time translator in England (translating mainly documentation/user guides) and as a Microsoft documentation translator and reviewer.
