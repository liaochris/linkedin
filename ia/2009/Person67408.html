Dr Vijay Venkatraman Janarthanan’s Summary

I am a medical doctor specialized in Diabetology. I was part of Accenture's Global Pharmacovigilance & Epidemiology wing as a Medical Review Safety Physician (Drug Safety Physician / Drug Regulatory Doctor) for a year.

After joining ICON Clinical Research in May 2008, I have been handling the tasks of a Medical Monitor as well as those of a Project Manager, along with the responsibilities of a Pharmacovigilance Physician.

As an initiative to develop people using the knowledge I possess, I give guest lectures to students pursuing the 'Post Graduate Certificate Program in Clinical Trials Management' course offered by Anna University – K B Chandrasekhar (AU-KBC) Research Centre, Chennai.

I look forward to synergize my broad-based experience in Medicine (Diabetes), Pharmacovigilance & Project Management towards an illustrious leadership role in the Drug Safety vertical.
