TAYLOR CASWELL’s Summary

A senior executive and former White House appointee with over fifteen years of experience in strategic planning, policy leadership, general management, and external relations. A diverse skill set developed through extensive professional background in both public and private sectors. Solid reputation as innovative and energetic leader as well as a fair and team-oriented manager.

PUBLIC SECTOR EXPERIENCE SUMMARY:
- Regional Director, U.S. Department of Housing & Urban Development (HUD)
- Senior staff, U.S. Congress
- Chair, New England Interagency Council on Homelessness
- Member, Greater Boston Federal Executive Board


PRIVATE SECTOR EXPERIENCE SUMMARY:
- Founded a new firm to help privately held and nonprofit organizations evolve their missions in the housing and energy sectors.
- Led Washington DC presence for the third largest personal lines insurer in country.
- Directed government relations for the largest supplier of natural gas in northeastern U.S., including management of external relations for multimillion-dollar energy development project with international partners.
