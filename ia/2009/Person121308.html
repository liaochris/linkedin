Josh Stutt’s Summary

Josh Stutt is Imagery Creative's Director of Strategy and has been working with global business leaders for nearly a decade as a strategic consultant and entrepreneur. He is responsible for conducting analyses and developing plans to help shape the overall directional strategy for Imagery's clients as well analyzing key measures for existing businesses, researching trends in the offline and online media world, and recommending activities to allow Imagery to capitalize on marketplace opportunities. Josh's forward thinking mind-set and new media mastery have allowed Imagery's clients to take full advantage of the changing media landscape and helped keep Imagery on the cutting edge of conversation creation with our audiences.

Josh received a Masters in International Business from the University of Florida. Before joining Imagery he ventured from Tokyo to Detroit, and just about everywhere in between, as a Sales Director for a world leading business meeting organization working with Global 2000 companies to bolster their strategic sales development. From assisting NTT Docomo's introduction of 3G to the world to working with ING to develop exposure to CFOs throughout the EU, he has worked alongside some of the world's most influential business leaders to help them plot out and execute their sales and marketing strategies for both established and emerging markets.

Josh brings both Imagery and its clients a global strategic perspective and a passion for being on top of the latest trends in markets, products, and technologies. His future focus has lead him to found two internet companies including the first social network for Baby Boomers and a ground-breaking social music community. Brooklyn born and South Florida raised, Josh can commonly be found looking for or developing "the next big thing" when not researching basketball statistics or his next travel adventure.
