Mark Ippolito’s Summary

My work focuses on building and growing great companies in digital medial, online advertising and e-commerce with a proven track record in selling innovative technology products and services. During his 20+ year career in sales and marketing, Ippolito has held senior leadership positions with Digital Railroad, Zango, Getty Images, Corbis, PhotoDisc, and Comstock.

Mark has successfully helped all of the companies he has worked with in winning significant new business and driving profitable partnerships with leading companies across a variety of industry sectors, including: Advertising Agencies (creative and media), Media & Publishing, Corporate and Retail.

Results Oriented Leader: Under his leadership DRR acquired over 50,000 registered buyers and attracted more than 2,700 paid monthly subscribers to the platform growing revenues from $0- 2.5MM in the first full 12 months of launching the product. At Zango he was responsible for growing online advertising revenues from $36MM to $80MM and diversifying revenue through parallel expansion of sales channels including direct, agents, resellers and international. At Getty Images, Ippolito was responsible for revenues of $210MM leading a sales organization of 250+ sales professionals. In 2004 Ippolito was recruited as SVP Sales & Marketing for OnRequest Images where he helped the founding team secure $5MM in venture financing, launched the company’s on-demand digital photography service and drove adoption of this new offer with over 250 top advertising agencies in North America in less than 12 months.
