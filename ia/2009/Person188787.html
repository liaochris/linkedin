Paul Morris’s Summary

Twenty years solutions based business experience including: technical and financial software and consulting. The last ten years have been selling solutions to Financial and Insurance industry focusing on Core Banking, Market Data, Securities and Investment Banking Solutions. Sales experience outside of Canada includes the US, Europe, Middle East and Africa.

Experienced in management of integrated solutions specifically related to ERP, e-Commerce, B2Bi/C, Business Process Management, Business Activity Monitoring, messaging and application level integration. Most importantly I have the proven ability to provide results.

My experience has afforded me the opportunity to build strong and lasting relationships with senior business and IT executives in Financial, Insurance Services and Manufacturing markets. I have managed the sales lifecycle on large projects from start to finish and have been responsible for organizing services and partnerships with third party organizations.
