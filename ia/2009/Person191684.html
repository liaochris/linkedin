Rachel Lawlan’s Summary

I'm a Planner. That means I work with many diverse businesses/brands to help them identify and develop the most effective means of achieving their objectives. Simple as that.

Except, as we all know, it's not that simple.

So you want to grow sales by 3%/5%/10% by this time next year (or defend your SOM, or enter a new market, or develop a new product...)? And, let me guess, you've got a limited budget?

There are hundreds of things you could and should consider: grow penetration? frequency? drive loyalty? influence your key influencers? attack or undercut the competition?

And once you've decided that, how are you going to go about it: a major ATL or TTL campaign? a website redevelopment? better SEO, better eCRM? experiential or 2.0 tactics?

And whichever of these or the many other options you may consider, how do you prove to the people who count (shareholders, employees, bosses) that any of it's actually been effective?

That's where your Planner comes in handy, if you've got a good one. I work with my Clients to help identify the best way to meet their goals. I then work with creative people - that could be Art Directors, Copywriters, Designers, Interface Developers, UX experts, Content Developers, Social Media/Seeding/PR experts, Media/Engagement Planners, Data Strategists, Back-end Developers, POS experts, Event/Interaction Designers - to inspire them to bring that strategy to life and make it work as hard as possible to reach or surpass your objectives.

And then I help report on how effective whatever it is we've decided to do together has been - so I'm accountable, you're accountable, and we all learn things that we could improve upon next time round - because there's always stuff you can learn, no matter how long you've been whatever business it is you're in, or whatever business you might be planning on getting into next.

Planners are handy people. Get in touch if you'd like to know more!
