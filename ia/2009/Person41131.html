Chardy Wang’s Summary

Rubify is software consultation house company based in Singapore.

Our services include building applications and systems on mobile platforms, desktop widgets development, Google Maps, Adobe AIR applications and iPhone applications and Web 2.0 applications development.

We understand the idea of sharing and contributing to the community with the purpose of bringing about possibilities to greater heights. Therefore, we are currently running two online communities. Usingmac.com is a blog that discusses about interesting Mac applications and miscellaneous online findings. Blendfu.com is a database of design resources that are contributed by designers from all over the globe.

The team at Rubify is made up of Rails developers who started Web development and consulting company to cater to startups as well as progressive companies in Singapore. We are passionate about the work we do as we constantly seek for creativity and improvement in our work to provide the best for our clients.
