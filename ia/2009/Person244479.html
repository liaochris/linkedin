Vivian Wagner’s Summary

My writing and photography have appeared in American Profile, Bluegrass Unlimited, Clean Eating, ECT News Network, Geek Monthly, LinuxInsider, Relish, TechNewsWorld, VegNews, Vegetarian Times, and many other publications. My book, FIDDLE: ONE WOMAN'S SEARCH FOR TUNES, GRIT, AND AUTHENTICITY, is forthcoming from Citadel Press. I teach journalism, advise the student newspaper, and direct the journalism program at Muskingum College in New Concord, Ohio, and I also serve as news editor for InTheFray Magazine.
