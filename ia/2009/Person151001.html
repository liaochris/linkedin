Marisa Powell-Schoen’s Summary

I've produced for 15 years. I've always enjoyed the challenge of figuring out something new. I find that working with great people motivates me to do the best job that I can. Yep, I'm a people pleaser. My goal for 2009 is to return to the core values that inspired me to join this business in the first place: Fresh ideas, inventive solutions, and the perpetual opportunity to learn something new.
