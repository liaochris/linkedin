Sam Peterson’s Summary

Telecommunications engineer with a strong background in satellite systems. Experienced in all aspects of satellite communications including link engineering, systems control, telemetry monitoring, network management, circuit operation and maintenance, fault isolation, troubleshooting, and customer service.

Sam Peterson’s Specialties:

- Satellite equipment including high power amplifiers (HPA), low noise amplifiers (LNA), modulators, frequency converters, timing modules, waveguide systems, and antenna tracking systems
- Baseband equipment including multiplexers, encryption devices, routers, and switches (IP and ATM)
- Electronics test equipment including spectrum analyzers, Fireberd test sets, signal generators, network analyzers, power meters, and oscilloscopes
- Computer software including Microsoft, UNIX, and VMS
