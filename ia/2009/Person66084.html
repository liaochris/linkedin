Dorian Tireli’s Summary

Dorian Tireli is user experience consultant, digital media specialist and designer with over thirteen year experience in the Internet, Media and Telecommunications sectors.

Dorian Tireli’s Specialties:

User experience, graphic design, interaction design, information architecture, product management, usability
