Steve Hemenway’s Summary

National Sales Manager currently working with public transportation agencies for on-board video surveillance hardware and software solutions that provide passenger/driver safety, operational risk management and increased agency security.

Drives initiatives to develop revenues through direct sales, channel partners and solution partners. Knowledgeable of vehicle-based applications using wireless communications, video analytics, and related back-office applications.

Steve is a proactive manager, outspoken industry ambassador and relentless competitor. Experienced in creating and developing strategic nationwide channel partnerships to win regional business opportunities. Develops sales plans, market requirements, and works with internal project teams for successful customer fulfillment and retention.

Additional professional experience includes business consulting & training, broadcast communications management, network security marketing and advertising sales management.
