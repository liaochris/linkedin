Bernardo van de Schepop’s Summary

Interaction Designer focused on concept development where prototypes are validated throughout a iterative contact with real people.

Highly interested on context-aware and adaptive interfaces coupling physical and virtual interactions within one product. Fond of creative teams and environments. Enthusiastic about designing toys and games.

The professional and academic experiences encompass technical skills ranging from Graphic Design, Interface Design and Information Architecture to programming of physical interfaces and hacking electronics.

Bernardo van de Schepop’s Specialties:

People-centred mind-set. Interaction design plan, specification and validation. Experience and proficiency with information architecture and interface design.
