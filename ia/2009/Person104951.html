Jason Roth’s Summary

So who is Jason Roth?

Well to sum it up professionally, I am a "Web Guy". What does being a "Web Guy" entail? To me, it means living and breathing everything on the WWW (thanks again Al Gore for your great invention!).

Developing full web marketing strategies, from conception to launch, including branding, email marketing, SEM/SEO, social media, and affiliate marketing, as well as hands on experience designing and building websites, UI design and animated marketing pieces all centered around a consistent brand image and message, with of course the skills and experience to analyze the data at the end, are just some of the things I have been working on for the last 5 years. With companies ranging from a 10 person software firm to a Fortune 500 giant, Intuit (TurboTax).

I am passionate about understanding who the customer is and their needs, and I focus on technology as the vehicle to create the best and most beneficial experience possible.
