Paulo Perez’s Summary

In 2004, i began my own career as interactive designer. My first job was at Thug Interactive, a design studio with focus on interactive working for clients like 20th Century Fox, Unilever and CSU.

Seven months later, i went to 10'minutos interactive, an interactive ad agency. This change gave me the opportunity to get more focus on interactive advertising, creating campaings, hotsites and Flash Animations. I used to manage the of 20th Century Fox, Globo.com, Africa, Tam Airlines, ABN AMRO BANK, Young & Rubican and Coca-Cola.

In 2005, I accepted a new great professional challenge . i started working at DM9DDB as Art Director both for Online and Offline advertisers such as Itaú, Honda, Telefonica, Guaraná Antarctica, AMBEV, Blockbuster, Philips, Terra, Johnson & Johnson, Consul and Brastemp, and stayed there until 2007.

Things went quite well and in 2007 I got a amazing opportunity to move forward on my career and change my life style: I moved to Mexico to lead a creative team at Grupo W in jobs for Nike, Hersheys, Rexona and One Show Festival.

When I got the offer from Gringo in 2008, I decided that it was time to get back home. And here I am as a Creative Director.
