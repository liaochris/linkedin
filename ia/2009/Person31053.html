Bo Rinaldi’s Summary

Bo has pioneered Scalable Staffing Solutions and RPO Strategies at ORACLE, Hewlett-Packard, Apple, Lucent and Stanford University. Bo distinguishes himself as an industry leader who staffs consulting and contracting projects for Biopharmaceutical, CRO, Medical Device and Diagnostics Companies around the world. Previously, he joined Jim Trattner as a founder of the prestigious Trattner Network, a $60M Silicon Valley high tech professional services firm, which sold in 2001 to Spherion.
