Joan E. McCarthy’s Summary

A results-oriented writer with a talent for crafting creative, compelling communications and thoughtful content strategies based on customer insights, I’m passionate in my attention to detail and my commitment to the quality, integrity and excellence of the user experience.

My background in Internet and print publishing allows me to offer a unique combination of innovation, analytical thinking and deadline-driven performance. And as a hands-on team leader, I contribute organizational and planning skills for project management and web content development.

I’m also exceptionally proficient in optimizing web copy for natural search (organic website SEO).
