Joshua Schipper’s Summary

Joshua is a multi-platform software developer with a strong track record of successful full life cycle development. His experience spans over a decade of both the planning, analysis and development of over 100 creative business software solutions, as well as expertise in initiating improvements in technical architectures and rapid development procedures geared to maximize application performance and business efficiency.

As an Enterprise Application Integration (EAI) Specialist, Joshua has migrated dozens of legacy applications from platforms such as ASP and MS Access to .NET and SQL Server implementations. In doing so, Joshua's innovations in coding optimization and system architectures have gone beyond simple updates to current standards to include improved performance capabilities, extensibility, and enhanced user experience.

Joshua has worked in positions directing the design and deployment of large scale applications, as well as positions requiring the concurrent management of dozens of enterprise-wide smaller applications. He has capably directed the efforts of junior developers, has successfully worked with executive and user-level client representatives, and has spearheaded the establishment of policies and procedures to ensure quality solutions in direct support of business objectives.
