Jeff Eriks’s Summary

Cambridge is a full service design~build construction firm. Since our inception, we have worked on various different projects across the country. Our most recent focus has been on the waste industry where we have completed over 100 projects over the last 14 years. Outside of the waste industry, we have completed projects for financial institutions, restaurants, churches, industrial buildings, professional offices and daycare centers.

We believe, "The bitterness of poor quality remains long after the sweetness of low price is forgotten." At the end of the day, we want to provide our customers the best facility possible for a fair price. Cutting corners and providing inferior facilities is not our practice.

I have been working for Cambridge Companies, Inc. since Oct. of 2000. I have worn many hats since I started here in order to learn all sides of the business. I started out as a contract administrator, then went on to field supervision briefly before I went on to estimating and small project management. I carried most of the the estimating duties for about 4 years creating everything from conceptual budgets to final construction budgets. My project management started out as small projects in 2002 (200k to $3m) and carried into the most recent, an $8.9 million dollar facility outside Chicago completed in Dec. of 2008.

My position evolved even more in 2007 as I started to work with current customers to develop conceptual projects into real projects. I then also took on the managing of the design department in 2007 which has since merged with an architectural firm and I now manage that company of 6 employees on a day-to-day basis as well as maintain my project management and project development roles.

I am also responsible for obtaining out of state general contractors licenses. I currently hold a general contractors license in: Alabama, Florida, Louisiana, Tennessee and Virginia.
