Debbie Mainquist’s Summary

Over 10+ years of pharmaceutical/vaccine sales experience with Merck and sold over 50 million dollars in product. Promoted to Executive Sales Representative after three consecutive years of sales being in the top tier out of 250 sales representatives. Won many awards including the Vice President's Club in 2000 and 2004 and the Director's Club Award in 2005. Opened 75 accounts in less than 6 months with primary care physicians selling Gardasil, Zostavax and Pneumovax. Increased use of Pneumovax and Zostavax in hospitals. Helped a major hospital increase their use of Pneumovax by 30% and resulted in the hospital retain their government funding. Created Zostavax vaccine clinics in community pharmacies to increase sales and my plan was used in other territories to increase sales for the entire region.
Extensive background includes sales in 13 unique therapeutic categories and over 15 medical specialties. My goal is to return to medical sales either specialty pharmaceutical or medical device sales. Known as a hunter for new business to either open new accounts or to increase sales in existing accounts.
