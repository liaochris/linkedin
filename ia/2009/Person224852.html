Steve Remington’s Summary

President of SEE Development, Inc, a festival and events management and consulting company. Recently finished contract work as Assistant Director of Special Events in the University of Oregon’s Office of Advancement; serve as Adjunct Faculty member in the UO Arts and Administration program and the Arizona State University Festival & Events Management Program.

Twenty-five years experience in the events industry including Tempe Festival of the Arts, Minnesota’s St. Paul Winter Carnival, da Vinci Days, Portland Waterfront Blues Festival, Eugene Celebration and the Oregon Truffle Festival.

Steve Remington’s Specialties:

Festival and community development, tourism marketing, sponsorship development, board leadership.
