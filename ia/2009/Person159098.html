Melanie Francis’s Summary

Development of custom Word, Excel and PowerPoint templates for blue chip companies, government departments and SMEs. Creating letters, reports and document templates in company style with automated procedures to increase efficiency, accuracy and uniformity.

Development of Access databases for job tracking, time management, order processing, stock control and sales.
