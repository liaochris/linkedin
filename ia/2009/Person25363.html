Béatrice Judel’s Summary

I joined Les Taxis Bleus in December 07 to take in charge the Marketing and Commercial Department. Les Taxis Bleus is a radio company helping Parisien customers to find a taxi. This company offers to its customers a lot of services to make the taxi experience easier, thanks to subscriptions or contracts. I was in charge of all these products, more generally of business development and external communication.

Previously I worked in an international company where I was in charge of world key accounts's development. I had to manage international relationships internally and with customers to increase business (with AF and KLM for example) thanks to a team of business development managers.

I had been before Sales Director with experience of strategy, commercial policy, budgets, management of sales forces and an external distribution network, management of customer relationship, direct and operational marketing, negociations with corporate, during 6 years for the daily newspaper LE PARISIEN.

I 've worked 10 years for AIR FRANCE/AIR INTER at Paris Orly Airport managing commercial and transport staff on several positions.
