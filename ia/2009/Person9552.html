Alister Robbie’s Summary

Alister is an Editor and Post Production Specialist, with 15 years experience working in and around the Broadcast Industry. He has been working actively with Final Cut Pro as an editor, trainer, systems designer and workflow consultant since it was released in 1999.

Alister editing screen credits include international release documentaries, TV series, TV commercials, music videos and corporate work. In the midst of several other projects, he is currently working as an editor on the Australian feature film "The Joe Manifesto".

Alister Robbie’s Specialties:

Long Form Editor (Documentary / Drama), Online Editor, Video Compression & Streaming, DVD Authoring, Post Production Consulting, Video System Architecture & Workflow Design.
Extensive experience working in High Definition using HD-CAM SR, Varicam, P2 (DVCPRO HD, AVC-Intra) & XDCAM EX
Currently delving into 4K Digital Cinema workflows using Red, Crimson, RedCine, RedRushes and Final Cut Pro.
Final Cut Pro / Apple Systems specialist;
- covering system design, integration, training and support.
