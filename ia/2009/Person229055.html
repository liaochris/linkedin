Tanner Williams’s Summary

I hope to find some work in either Intellectual Property Law, Political or Election Law, Entertainment law, or International Business or Abitration. I also hope to eventually work in either the Bay Area, Los Angeles or Orange County, Washington D.C., or abroad in Singapore or perhaps Hong Kong

Entertainment Law, I believe, would also be an excellent fit as the IP classes I enjoy would also combine with the contracts as well as business classes. Finally, I have lived abroad in Indonesia and traveled throughout the country as well as to Singapore and my time spent abroad has given my a greater cultural, political, and economic understanding of certain foreign regions.
