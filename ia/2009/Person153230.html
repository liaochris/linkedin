Marsha Baker’s Summary

I established Marsha Baker Communications, LLC (MBC) in January 2003 as a copywriting, marketing and public relations consulting firm. I offer my clients the benefits of 35 years of communication, marketing and public relations experience in both the private and public sectors, having served as an employee and a consultant to private companies, non-profit organizations and government agencies. Prior to forming MBC, I served as Public Relations Director for Gordley Design Group, Inc. (advertising agency), Director of Marketing for Sun Tran (public transit), Community Relations for Pima County Department of Transportation and Flood Control, and Museum Coordinator for The Amerind Foundation (American Indian museum and research foundation).

Marsha Baker’s Specialties:

Copywriting (Web, print, TV/radio scripting, surveys), strategic marketing, branding, market research, public/media relations and community outreach. As a former ad agency director and copywriter, my experience spans a broad range of industries.
