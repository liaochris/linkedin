    M. Tech, ICT, 2005 — 2007

    M.Tech. Thesis : Quality of Service (QoS) in Wireless Network
    This work proposes a downlink packet scheduling approach to QoS provisioning in High Speed Downlink Packet Access based UMTS networks. In UMTS a single logical channel is shared among multiple contending users. Besides better link utilization, scheduling seek to achieve fair allocation of shared resource. However, these two criterions can potentially be in conflict. Proposed algorithm, “Wireless Fair High Speed Scheduling”, addresses the abovementioned conflict.
