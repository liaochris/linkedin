Daniel Halberg’s Summary

Professional and Educational Background:

I have a Ph.D. in Pharmacy Health Care Administration from the University of Florida where I specialized in biostatistics, psychometrics, simulation modelling, and pharmacoeconomics. My dissertation work used operations research models to optimize capacity relative to perceptions of service quality.

Post-graduation in 2007, I was an Assistant Professor at the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences in Little Rock where I taught Biostatistics, Health Economics, Health Outcomes, Evidence Based Medicine, and Management. During that time I was awarded the distinction of being a UAMS Teaching Scholar.

I started with AstraZeneca in November 2000 as a Senior Regional Scientific Manager. I lead many initiatives in addition to my regular customer activities, including training and metrics development teams. In 2005, I accepted the role of Regional Director Scientific Affairs where I currently manage a team of field scientists. I have been honored with many awards for excellence, including the Soaring Eagle Award and MA Leadership Award.

Avocations:

Currently, I am working on two exciting projects in my "spare time".

The first is through my corporation with Gary Allen, Allen & Halberg Publishing, Inc. We are the developer of one of the top ranked guitar web sites on the internet called GuitarGearHeads.com. I started playing guitar when I was 31, and fell in love with the instrument. I can't say I'm high on talent, but I am certainly high on interest!

The second is through my affiliation with Kyle Hollaway and our project titled Faithful Dad Ministries that is aimed at helping men of faith understand and reach their full potential.

Personally:

I have a wonderful, amazing wife and two great kids. Having a family has changed my outlook on life. They keep me grounded and help me understand how truly blessed I am. In the end, I just want to be a great dad and give my kids a positive example.
