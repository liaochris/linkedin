Eric Lewerenz’s Summary

Eric Lewerenz, M.S., is currently the Director of Information Quality at My InnerView. Over the past 6 years, in this role he has helped build a company that now holds the largest privately-held database of satisfaction survey data in the senior care industry.

He has been a SAS programmer for 9 years, and this year is an invited speaker at the 20th Annual Midwest SAS Users Group (MWSUG) conference in Cleveland, OH. He also has several years of experience with different facets of web development (PHP/MySQL coding, QA/testing of user interfaces/workflow, usability, and database design).

He is a member of the International Association for Information and Data Quality (IAIDQ). His interests include database design, data modeling and usability, lean and Total Information Quality Management (TIQM), GIS (geographic information systems), and improving business effectiveness with high quality data and information.

He earned his Master’s degree in Health Services Research, Policy and Administration, with an emphasis in Health Information Management, from the University of Minnesota’s School of Public Health.

Eric is known for his work ethic, integrity, insightful inquiry, good-natured personality, and unique ability to find anomalous data and trends.
