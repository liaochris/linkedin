Christian Prusia’s Summary

Experienced technology entrepreneur possessing a wide range of strategic planning, marketing, international sales, business development and P&L management insights. I most enjoy creative, fast-paced consumer technology companies. Early stage start-ups in need of fund raising, strategic account/channel development within highly competitive, international markets a plus.

Christian Prusia’s Specialties:

Private company financing (venture and debt), mergers and acquisition, contract negotiation, business development, sales, and cash flow/financial management within both the software-as-a-service (SaaS) and semiconductor industries.
