Link Stevens’s Summary

App Publishing, LLC has developed mobile-commerce and mobile-marketing applications unlike any currently available.

Our web-based applications, developed to capitalize on the enormous opportunities and as yet untapped revenue in mobile commerce, are available now.

Most companies that supply services and resources to an online membership base are already discussing, or developing tools to monetize the explosive growth of the mobile platform and mobile-commerce.

Don’t wait. "White label" versions of our tools have been developed, tested and are ready to be integrated into any subscriber or membership-based online network immediately.

1. Mobile Store Maker (www.mobilestoremaker.com) is an intuitive, easy-to-use, web-based tool that builds “mobile-friendly” web stores. This is perfect for creating mobile-formatted versions of existing eCommerce websites, or to turn "brochure” websites into commerce sites.

2. Mobile Invoicer (www.mobileinvoicer.com) is an easy-to-use, web-based tool that turns any browser-enabled phone into a credit card processing unit and also sends branded invoices with an imbedded “pay now” link. This is the perfect tool "add-on" for brochure websites e.g. plumbers, electricians, attorneys, home inspectors, artists, etc. to immediately begin to generating revenue.

3. MOWBI (www.mowbi.com) is a “soon to be released”, ground-breaking, mobile marketing tool, expected to revolutionize social marketing on mobile devices.
