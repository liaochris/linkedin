Khadevis Robinson’s Summary

Having served as a representative of the United States for nearly a decade in international sports competition, US Olympian Khadevis Robinson has had the opportunity to serve as a public speaker, coach to children through his non-profit coaching organization, an educator at Santa Monica Malibu Unified School District, and a Masters of Science recipient in Public Administration from California State Los Angeles.

    With his victories in the 2007 AT&T USA Outdoor Championships and in 2006 at the USA Indoor and Outdoor Championships, US Olympian Khadevis Robinson left little doubt that he is the premier U.S. men’s 800m runner, and for the first time in his career he was ranked in the top ten in the world by Track & Field News (#7). Robinson was so dominant in the U.S. in 2006 that he posted 11 of the top 12 outdoor times by an American that season. Robinson placed third in his opening round heat at the Olympic Games in Athens.

    Robinson earned his undergraduate degree from TCU in social work and Masters from Cal State. ‘I love working with people’…nickname is ‘KD’…was a football standout in high school…broke his wrist in football his junior year…felt he needed to have another sport to list on his college application, so he tried track…loves to play chess…made 2001 World Outdoor Championships team by having “A” standard, when higher finishers at U.S. Nationals did not have “A” standard.
