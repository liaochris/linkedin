Brenda Braden’s Summary

Currently I am a publicist for authors. I book radio shows, speaking engagements, interviews and book launches. I enjoy assisting in the promotions of the author not just the book.

In me you will find a versatile professional with my primary focus on living life to its fullest while maintaining balance. My career skill-set is comprised of leadership, sales, training, process development and program/project management.

Learning is a passion form me; exploring all I can on a myriad of subjects whether it is self improvement, environmental, engineering, medical it doesn’t matter; I thirst for knowledge and going the extra mile. Networking is a passion for me, it is always exciting to meet new people and observe diverse viewpoints. In my free time I attend plays, sporting events, volunteer, attend self development workshops and enjoy being outdoors taking in all that nature has given us. Of course much of this is done with my family.

My goal is to continue living a balanced life full of harmony and abundance. Abundance is not only monetary it is health, relationships, spiritual, and financial. 2008 and much of 2009 has been a year of transition and while it has not been easy it has allowed me to get back in touch with so much that had been lost due to imbalance and skew of focus in my life. I am finding public speaking and exploration of my spiritual connection is bringing much joy to me these days.
