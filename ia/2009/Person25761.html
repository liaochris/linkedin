Brian Elliott’s Summary

Leader of growth strategies and operations for online businesses. Over 15 years experience in strategy development and execution, driving sales and marketing, engineering and operations for technology-related consumer goods and retail.

President & CEO at Alibris, Inc. a leading media exchange for thousands of professional sellers of books, music and movies globally and an Internet Retailer "top 100" retailer, purchased in mid-2006 by private equity firm. Deep experience in online marketing, technology management and partnership development.
