Rebecca Levi’s Summary

Realizing a lifelong passion for career development, Rebecca joined the Markinekt recruiting team in 2007. At Markinekt, she specializes in digital placements, with a focus on Producers/Project Managers, Information Architects, Creatives, Strategists, and Technology Managers. She can be contacted at rebecca.levi@markinekt.com for recruiting and staffing services.

Placements with health- and pharma-related agencies are an additional focus.

Rebecca has over 10 years of project management leadership in interactive and multimedia at agencies and independents, as well as a background in small business management.

In addition her recruiting career at Markinekt, Rebecca is an independent Career Coach. She can be contacted at reblev@gmail.com for career coaching services.

Rebecca has a Professional Certificate in Career Planning and Development from NYU, a degree in English Literature from McGill University and is a proud alumnus of New York’s own Stuyvesant High School. She is also a fine artist whose work has been shown in San Francisco, Toronto, Provincetown, and Williamsburg.
