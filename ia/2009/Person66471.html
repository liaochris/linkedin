Douglas Smith’s Summary

WD Partners is an international, integrated design and architecture firm that specializes in multi-unit design and development.

We are a single source for powerful, adaptable store solutions. The world’s leading retail and restaurant brands—from fashion to fuel, fast casual to financial—rely on WD Partners to deliver comprehensive, in-house Design and Architectural services.

My primary responsibilities are to the Design & Branding Stuido and building processes around this group leveraging greater internal efficiencies to provide a higher value to our clients.

Outside of WD Partners, I provide financial counseling to those that are struggling with debt. This part-time ministry started in 2006 with the goal of providing sound, Biblically based strategies to help people understand how to manage their money and pay down debt. Topics covered include budgeting, cash flow, debt reduction plans, emergency funds, as well as providing spiritual support to help people understand what God's purpose is with their (His) finances.

Other interests include regional planning, regional transportation, retail development, urban development, construction, and current events.
