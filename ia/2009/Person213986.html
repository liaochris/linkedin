Sergio Palumberi’s Summary

I am the ECLSS Engineer of the Engineering Support Team for the european module Columbus of the International Space Station. My task is to suppport the ISS flight controllers from the engineering point of view, in real time troubleshooting and engineering analyses.

In parallel I am working in the new Italian Bioregenerative Life Support Program (CAB), developing a system that will allow the regeneration of resources and the production of food for astronauts' life support in long duration Moon/Mars missions.

I am also involved in several Thales Alenia Space projects, providing support for the Environmental Control and Life Support Subsystem in the design and definition of future mission architectures for the Exploration of Space.
