Kevin Lawver’s Summary

I've worked at AOL for almost 13 years, six years of that time working on AOL's flagship search product as the lead UI developer. I spent two years doing consulting, training and acting as the "web standards guy" for one of the largest development organizations at AOL. I then worked on a large social networking project, AIM Pages, and am now the architect on several large social media projects in the company.

I spend a lot of time working web standards and development best practices within the company, speaking at conferences, mentoring other employees, and advocating transparency and forward thinking in product design and development.

And playing video games. I play a lot of video games.
