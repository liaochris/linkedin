Elizna Engelbrecht’s Summary

Graduating in 2006 at the University of Stellenbosch with a degree in Marketing I got into the industry straight away by getting chosen out of multiple candidates for an internship at one of the world's biggest ad agencies - Ogilvy. After being there for a month I was offered a position as Account Manager. I quickly proved my ability to handle clients and project manage campaigns under pressure. Exceeding the expectations of both my clients and the agency it was not long before various projects were placed in my care to run on my own. I quickly had to learn the ins and outs of in particular the online medium. Realising that my heart had always lied with marketing and not so much advertising I made a move to a smaller company where I was given the opportunity to manage all marketing activities (Ops24 Internet Services - currently a client of mine). After working at Ops24 for a little over 6 months I started getting requests from other small business owners to help with marketing on their brands. I went on my own and are now handling various clients successfully.
