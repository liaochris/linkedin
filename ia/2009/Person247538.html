Bill Maxson’s Summary

I am skilled in project management, product engineering, and technical problem solving with many years of experience in automotive, defense, and aerospace industries. With the ability to solve very difficult reliability and quality problems for manufacturing and engineering , I can generate significant savings for any manufacture in any industry.

As a program manager, I led a team of engineers responsible for product development, testing, and installing manufacturing lines, which included automated assembly, molding, and testing equipment. I was responsible for developing budgets, timing, and keeping senior management and the customer informed through reports and presentations.

Working as a consultant, I embedded effective and efficient problem solving programs at a variety of manufacturing sites and engineering technical centers for clients such as: John Deere, Daimler, Continental Teves, Honda, GM, Kavlico, Teledyne MEC, NAL, Unifrax, Delco Electronics, Systron Donner, BEI Sensors, and Delphi. I routinely cross-trained Six Sigma and Lean professionals in Shainin Red X techniques complimenting and enhancing their existing skills.

My product engineering experience includes product development, testing, and ensuring the program was on time and within budget. I used Microsoft Project, APQP, PPAP, FMEAs, MSA, and other quality tools and concepts to launch several electromechanical devices. I have two U.S. Patents and several technical publications.
