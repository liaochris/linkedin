Marcus Flemming’s Summary

Marcus Flemming grew up in Colorado before moving to California where he established his first business on his way to amassing more than 12 years of experience in Internet related businesses. His expertise is in areas ranging from innovative business planning, effective business aministration and business development to creative hands-on UI design, development and a strong background in search engine marketing (SEM & SEO) of web site destinations.

He has founded and/or been involved in 9 successful Internet start-ups including MyFitnessQuest.com, Psyphire, Localprofit.com, and Savings.com that have combined for several million in annual revenues.

He possess outstanding communication and presentation abilities. He is recognized by clients and colleagues as a consummate professional with a high degree of personal integrity. Marcus is known for a contagious passion for excellence, a talent for resourceful business solutions, and a capacity for motivational leadership.

His experience includes business strategy/development/management, project leadership, Internet marketing strategies, SEO (since 1995), SEM (PPC Advertising), web analytics, SMO (Social Media Optimization), interface design, graphic design, web design (project management, planning, design, architecture of more than 150 web sites), web development (Javascript, PHP, Perl, HTML, XHTML, CSS, XML, mySQL), web site management (FTP, SSH), network admin (Linux, Apache, Bind, Sendmail, IMAP), security, technical/creative writing, and directing/editing for video/film production.
