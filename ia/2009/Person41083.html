Chantel Brannon’s Summary

Contact thedirecthirepro@yahoo.com

Chantel is the Founder/CEO of Direct Hire Resources, LLC. DHR provides consulting, clinical staff for direct hire opportunities to hospitals and associations throughout the United States. Chantel brings almost 20 years of healthcare consulting, marketing, and business development experience to DHR and is responsible for overseeing all operations.

Chantel is also currently a licensed Respiratory Practitioner and works as the Marketing Director of a well known respiratory company located in Phoenix Arizona, in which she oversees business development and is responsible for setting the strategic direction for the company and for overseeing the operations.

Prior to founding DHR, Chantel served as the Division Director for a Healthcare firm in Arizona. Being highly successful in the Healthcare Staffing and Recruiting Industry she decided to start her own business with the motto of "“leading by example” and has the ability to be the nations largest direct hire company that strives for quality that is unheard of in this industry today.
