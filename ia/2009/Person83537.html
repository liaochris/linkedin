Garry Kwan’s Summary

As a Investment Advisor and Financial Planner, I offer my clients professional advice and services in managing their investments and providing financial advice.

My professional capabilities and experiences include investment management of personal, corporate, estate, trust, and registered accounts, as well as banking and lending services (eg. mortgages, loans, PLCs). Complementing these abilities are my professional education and accreditation in financial planning, taxation, insurance, and investment management.

By combining a wealth of practical financial skills with a sound academic foundation, I'm able to offer my clients a unique experience from which they are able to benefit. I provide my clients with personalized service, professional advice and access to the extensive financial services and products available through BMO Nesbitt Burns and the BMO Financial Group.

My clientele includes executives and managers, interested in building and protecting their family's assets to the retired, interested in protecting their capital and receiving tax advantaged income.
