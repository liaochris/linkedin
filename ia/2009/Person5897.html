Alex Kravets’s Summary

I have 14 years of hands-on experience in conceiving, designing and building complex software architectures since graduating with a Cum Laude Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics from UCLA in 1995. I have always prided myself on possessing both the deep understanding of fundamentals of Computer Science as well as pragmatism and know-how necessary to be the technical leader and mentor of development teams working in either corporate or start-up environments, including 8 years of experience as a Chief Architect at iRise where I led a team developing a large suite of software from a proof-of-concept prototype to an enterprise product purchased by over 100 Fortune 500 customers.

Technical Career Highlights:

• Invented Suggestive Search used by Google while at Overture.
• Invented the Visual Modeling Language and its Web Interpreter at iRise.
• Developed a Distributed ODBMS and Message Broker at iRise.
• Architected the website and Data Warehouse back end at FirstLook.com.
• Led the team which developed the First Banking Website at Citigroup.
