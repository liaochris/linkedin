Robert Corcoran’s Summary

THE NEED: In today’s technical, labor saving, gadget driven world, there is no single device that can serve as a Master Command Center of your personal universe.
THE SOLUTION: The LAMBDA LCE is the solution to this need. It allows you to control your entire electronic environment, either locally or remotely, with a single device.

The fact is, even according to Bill Gates, the problem of connecting all of the diverse technologies is the last of the digital promises that still hasn’t been delivered. And this is the promise that LAMBDA TD Software fulfills. We do this by using the Internet to deliver our patented open system software to private households and commercial enterprises via our own web server. The LCE is an open source, floating platform that your personal electronics, communications, security and comfort controls, lighting, and all your electrical appliances can connect to. The platform is then controlled through any of our LCE Touch Panels which commands everything on your property, from inside the home, or remote from anywhere in the world.

The thing that makes the LCE unique from our competitors is in its ability to connect entire communities. Sure, our competition has the similar ability to put together a single-family automated home. But because of their proprietary systems, they are unable to connect one home to another. The LCE can connect entire communities, and even offer connected households an automated concierge service. And even still, the LCE could even turn out to be a BLUE PRINT of a connected future.

The LCE is the first patented open system, open architecture remote control system designed for homes, businesses, schools, hospitals, and municipalities. We call our unique and “complete domotics system” the LAMBDA CONTROL ENVIRONMENT- or LCE.
