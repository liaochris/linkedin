Rod Fermin’s Summary

Please come join our "Global Sources of Conventional and Alternative Funding for Real Estate Forum" group. Our forum brings real estate moguls and capital providers together to close real estate development projects.

I am a principal partner with a real estate development company seeking venture capital, private equity, offshore funding, humanitarian funding, credit enhancements, structured investment vehicles, sovereign wealth funds, alternative-centric financing, collateral funding (MTNs, SBLCs, BGs, Bonds, CDs, Life Insurance Wraps, CMOs) and trade platforms with proven capital groups, European, Middle East, Asia (particularly China and Japan), South American and US based traders, investment bank groups, hedge funds, capital providers, and top 50 world largest banking partners for several real estate development projects.

Our organziation is a full service real estate development and homebuilding company managing the entire process from design to completion including land acquisition, planning & design, entitlement processing, improvement of infrastructure, construction of product mix, sales & marketing and customer service. Our partners are deep with industry experience and have built production housing, semi-custom and custom single family dwellings as well as commercial projects.
