Ves Dimov, M.D.’s Summary

Dr. Dimov is an Allergy and Immunology Fellow at Creighton University, a Member of the New England Journal of Medicine (NEJM) Advisory Board for Students and Residents, and a former Clinical Assistant Professor of Medicine at Cleveland Clinic.

He is the Editor-in-Chief of AllergyCases.org, a case-based curriculum of Allergy and Immunology, endorsed by both the American Academy of Allergy Asthma and Immunology (AAAAI) and the American College of Allergy, Asthma and Immunology (ACAAI).

Dr. Dimov is the author of 21 articles and 76 abstracts, book chapters, published interviews and letters to the editor. He is the founder of ClinicalCases.org, the most popular online case-based curriculum of clinical medicine, which has had more than 3 million page views since 2005. The project was featured in several high-impact scientific journals is hyperlinked in the web sites of 29 medical schools.

As an experienced website editor and EMR expert, Dr. Dimov can help your practice with website development, social media tools (RSS, Twitter, blog, podcast, video) and EMR/EHR development.
