Alistair Houston’s Summary

Core Consulting are a trusted recruitment partner to some of the UK’s best known companies. Our clients range from global conglomerates to small niche manufacturers.

We specialise in the provision of recruitment services in the more technical markets where speed, innovation and market understanding are so important.

Our strengths are in delivering middle to senior management assignments where our clients benefit from full retained search and selection assignments or results-only placements.

We are experiencing sustained growth but we remain flexible and responsive to your needs. Each industry is handled by specialist teams; therefore you will always deal with someone who is an expert in your field.
