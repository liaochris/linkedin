Evan Paul’s Summary

Evan is currently twenty years old and the founder of eGamePlace.com. Evan was just fifteen years old when he founded eGamePlace, LLC in 2004, and he manages the company today as its Chief Executive Officer. A dedicated gaming enthusiast for thirteen years, Evan has a comprehensive understanding of the gaming industry. eGamePlace is Evan's third company, as he previously founded other companies with other members of the management team. eGamePlace's has currently been valued at over 35 million. Evan has been covered in over one hundred major media publications.

Evan was diagnosed with Dyslexia at the end of eighth grade; which most say is "very late". Evan struggled in school where teachers and students would call him "stupid and the slow kid". Evan had no idea why he was unable to keep up no matter how hard he ended up working! During this time in Evan's life he was very depressed and went though serious mental pain. Evan's goal is to insure that never again does a student with Dyslexia ever have to suffer the fate he did until being diagnosed!

Mr. Paul now feels it is time to give back to the community in as many ways possible. Evan has started to go on speaking circuits to raise awareness about Dyslexia and to inspire students who have Dyslexia to "never give up". Evan finds himself donating his time and money to insure that never again will a student with Dyslexia be left behind.
