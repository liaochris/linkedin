Erwin H.J. Elands’s Summary

After having more than 18 years of Sales experience I think Sales is the most beautiful job I can imagine. My strength is to penetrate new markets or introducing new products or services in existing markets. I am a real hunter and like to make a short "lean & mean plan" before I start and execute the plan by achieving quick wins and target goals. Especially my combination of high Quality & Quantity in Sales processes is the foundation of my success. It is my goal to gain the trust of customers and colleagues by being honest and deliver on my promiss so the foundation can be made for longterm relationships.

The title will be: 'BestSeller'!
