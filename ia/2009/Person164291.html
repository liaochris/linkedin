Mike Goetz’s Summary

Development manager with several years experience in web development. Capable of managing small and large teams, with particular concentration in agile development using Scrum. Organized, pragmatic, conscientious, disciplined.

I believe in an 'empowerment' style of management, encouraging and equipping those around me. I like doing the little things; I'm not afraid to get my hands dirty. I care more about who I work with than what I'm doing. I learn best when discussing and collaborating with others. I value disciplined thought over current fads, consistent action over motivational talk, relationships over self-promotion.

My experience is backed up with a BSc in Computing Science (Co-op) from SFU and an MSc in Computing Science from the University of East Anglia in England.
