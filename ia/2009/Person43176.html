Chris Downey’s Summary

Visit me at http://www.chrisdowney.name/

I am designing and constructing a web-based inventory management, asset control, performance metrics system for a 10 year, > $200M project. The system has hundreds of users worldwide and contains 20,000 items worth more than $30M. I am also the database administrator, responsible for running complex audit reports and protecting the value-added data of a large government organization.

I also serve as a part-time IT Director for a small non-profit organization. I collaborate with their Director of Patient Safety to produce data intensive statistical analysis of vaccine-related adverse events from a public database, help design process improvements, and design incremental IT architecture enhancements within tight fiscal constraints.

One of my passions is figuring out the best combination of technologies to help people be more effective and to find greater enjoyment with their software/hardware tools. For example, at the non-profit, I'm encouraging the use of MacBooks within a Microsoft network environment because the Macs are such a joy to use and have an incredibly powerful search feature called Spotlight that after I tried just once I could never live without.
