Kien Tong Chua’s Summary

Kien is a Manager at Disney's Sourcing and Procurement organization. He specializes in media computing, information technology and IT outsourcing deals. He has successfully negotiated deals with major technology companies like Apple, Adobe, Google, Microsoft, HP, IBM, EMC, as well as with startups providing leading edge solutions for video streaming and asset management for Disney's digital convergence initiatives.

Prior to joining Disney, Kien worked for leading management consulting firms - PRTM and Accenture - providing in-depth knowledge in global supply chain and international expansion strategies to Global 50 and Fortune 100 companies. Originally from Singapore, he has traveled more than 20 countries and worked in Asia, North America and Latin America.

Kien graduated with highest distinction (1st-class honors) in Mechanical Engineering from UK's top engineering university, Imperial College (London), and also received his M.B.A. from UCLA Anderson School of Management.

Kien Tong Chua’s Specialties:

Business case, feasibility study and ROI analysis for digital content distribution, media asset management, video streaming, IT outsourcing, application development, servers and storage technologies.
