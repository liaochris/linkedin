David Gross’s Summary

I spent 20 years making records, touring with various bands, writing instructional music books and videos and traveling around the world as a bass player.
I now own 144 Music and Arts Inc which is an outsourced Arts education company that brings professional musicians, artists, dancers, and actors into the NY City school system as Artists in Residence.
I am looking to turn this into a franchise-able business throughout the US

I have created "Jazzin' in the Key of Sea," "Ludwig Van's Baithaven and Craberet,"Catfish on a Hot Fin Roof," all children's edutainment programs revolving around music.

I am looking to find CEO's and high level executives who play musical instruments and are in bands for a new TV venture.

For the past 6 years I have been a member of Dan Kennedy's Inner Circle and I have been a long time practitioner of his brilliant marketing concepts.

I currently write two blogs, http://thedcgblog.wordpress.com/ devoted to finding ways for CEO's and Entrepreneurs to work with students and http://accordingtointuition.wordpress.com/ which I created to showcaseand review books, CD's, and DVD's that have inspired me and helped me grow.

David Gross’s Specialties:
