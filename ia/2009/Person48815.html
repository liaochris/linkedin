Connie Fry, MSW’s Summary

I have been in the creative field for over 30 years. cfk creative now combines my love of graphic design, marketing, branding and advertising with an intent to give back to the community and others. I started cfk creative to serve many different industries, but my passion and expertise lies in the senior living industry/retirement living. I have been doing marketing strategies for senior living/retirement communities for the last 5 years and with my master's in social work, the combination of marketing and social work has been realized.

My goal and the goal of cfk is to give back to others with community service, plus provide effective, creative, affordable design and communication strategy to our clients. We also take note of our seniors and elders, that deserve a life of abundance and joy. We hope we make it better for them!
