Matt Enders’s Summary

My entire professional career has been a progression towards founding and growing mgecom, inc., a professional outsourced affiliate management firm.

Beginning with an internship during college, I was directly responsible for a large affiliate program and PPC campaign.

Following my internship I worked for a large internet marketing firm where I was responsible for the affiliate programs, PPC campaigns, portal shopping, and comparative shopping engines for a variety of large online retailers. Clients I worked with ranged from home improvement, to sporting goods, to dress shoes.

Next I worked as an independent contractor / consultant managing only affiliate programs for a wide range of clients in numerous shopping verticals. This role included both pay per sale and pay per lead affiliate programs. I successfully implemented and launched new affiliate programs as well as successfully taking over the full management of existing programs. I have worked with many affiliate management platforms including CJ, Pepperjam, Linkshare, ShareASale, Google/Performics, MyAP, DirectTrack.

Finally my career path led me to form mgecom, inc. We are a fast growing and very nimble company providing our clients with personalized affiliate management services. We do not offer one-size-fits-all solutions. Each affiliate program presents a new set of challenges and opportunities which allows us to customize our solutions to generate maximum growth. We are constantly looking for new online retailers to partner with whose affiliate programs are either non-existent or currently not performing to your standards. mgecom, inc. exists for the sole purpose of delivering results via affiliate marketing.
