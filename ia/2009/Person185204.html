Peter Forsyth’s Summary

I help small businesses, nonprofit organizations, and political campaigns overcome communications and technological challenges.

I have been in business under the name Puddletown Consulting since October 2004. I work with a wide variety of clients (see http://www.puddletowntech.com/links.html ) developing written materials (web sites, business plans, etc.), designing and executing Internet and media campaigns.

I volunteer for a wide variety of civic and charitable organizations. I have written and edited for Wikipedia for four years, and have worked to develop a strong community of Wikipedia editors in Oregon. I also work with OnwardOregon.org, The Giving Tree, and a number of other organizations.

Samples of my writing may be found at http://peteforsyth.com/writing .
