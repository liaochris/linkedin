Gavin Keeley’s Summary

A committed change agent, in the 13 years prior to joining Norwich Union, I founded and established a successful IT Consulting company, after an initial period learning my trade at a ‘big four’ consultancy and gaining both IT and Business diplomas. The company I founded became renowned, through white papers, trade articles and as a conference speaker, for pioneering, with emerging technologies, for a portfolio of blue chip companies. This was in fields such as Decision Support and Executive Information in the 80’s and internet and advanced networks in the 90’s, including a number of ‘world first’ client implementations for, amongst others, Oracle and Microsoft.
I have always been interested in how technology disrupts existing business models. I have recently been researching and sponsoring innovation projects which are leading the adoption of consumerization and web 2.0, and sharing this learning with a cross industry working group of peers from other organizations. I am especially vocal on the specific impacts of these two emerging trends on large corporations, and in recent conference sessions I have shared my opinions on the changes that are starting to emerge.
