Joel Kaczor’s Summary

Joel has over 7 years experience in the mortgage industry beginning his career as a loan officer with a small regional mortgage broker.  Within a year, he had successfully opened his own branch office, eventually becoming a partner in the business.  This led to his latest role as an advisory board member with a branching company that produced over 3 billion in loans in 2007, and was one of the fastest growing mortgage brokers in the nation.
With all of his experience, Joel has an in depth knowledge of the mortgage process and handles his customers with special one on one attention.

Joel Kaczor’s Specialties:

Home loans for purchasing or refinancing your home. My philosophy is to give my customers my best deal up front and offer lower rates and fees.
