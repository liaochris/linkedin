Annemarie van Campen’s Experience

  • Founder

    Overheidswidgets.nl

    (Internet industry)

    March 2009 — Present (9 months)

    Overheidswidgets.nl is a foundation/platform run by and for governmental organisations to develop widgets using professional tools. All developed widgets are available to all member organisations. This way, each member profits from its co-members, while the technological infrastructure is shared. Secondly, all governmental widgets are available to the public, to use in their own websites.
    Overheidswidgets.nl is an idea of Verse Overheid, to stimulate the development of Open Government ('Open Overheid').

  • Founder, Owner

    Verse Overheid

    (Internet industry)

    January 2009 — Present (11 months)

    Verse Overheid provides advice and information to governmental organisations about government 2.0. We want to contribute to the development of government 2.0 by sharing knowledge and information, support and start innovative ideas and assist organisations during their projects. Together with the organisation, we determine how they can profit from all the options government 2.0 offers, which options are usefull and how they can effectively realise this ambition.

  • Internship

    Netherlands Atlantic Association

    (International Affairs industry)

    September 2007 — January 2008 (5 months)

    The Netherlands Atlantic Association has been providing information on transatlantic security issues and promoting the study of issues such as relations between Europe and the United States, NATO, and European Security. As a trainee, I provided content for the monthly magazine, organised informational meetings and handled the communication with members and other contacts.

  • Implementation, Sales, marketing, communication

    Chatfone

    (Internet industry)

    December 2005 — June 2007 (1 year 7 months)

    Chatfone has developed an online communication tool for governmental organisations. Through instant messaging, website visitors can be helped with their search for information and services. An unique aspect of the tool is the option to transfer conversations both internally to a colleague and externally to another organisation. Working in a small team, for an innovative company was a great opportunity to experience different aspects of a company, from sales meetings to co-designing the website to organising conferences. Being involved from the start of the idea to the realisation of the company enabled me to gather valuable knowledge and experience.
