Ann Kimura’s Summary

I am a freelance interactive Art Director/Designer, endlessly enthusiastic about creative collaboration and learning organizations. I embrace an interdisciplinary approach to projects, researching associations and inspirations from a gamut of resources. This flexible focus allows for an indulgence in many side projects, from organizing designer markets and exhibitions to a plethora of sewing projects.

Ann Kimura’s Specialties:

Interactive campaign concepts and design, concept and narrative creation, creative collaboration and ideation, network organization and gatherings (in both the design arena and the DIY craft world), brand building, and affable games.
