Grishma Kavi’s Summary

I have over 8.5 years of experience in the online domain. My skills range from the creation of detailed strategic roadmaps or business planning for e-commerce and m-commerce strategies, through to the presentation to senior stakeholders covering emerging global themes, such as the social web and future interactive mediums. I have a background with a sharp focus on enhancing the user experience through technological diversification and I have wide range of experience of usability strategies and interface design. I have worked on projects for some of the largest global web-based companies in the world covering a range of projects including UI frameworks, Site Optimisation, Competitor Research and Ethnographic research and experience modelling.
