Rob Shore’s Summary

Over the course of a 30 year career in corporate America, Rob Shore has been a leader and producer who has been known for sales success, innovation, creativity ….and memorability.

His career has been dedicated to Financial Services in positions ranging from entry level bank teller to President of a broker dealer. He has built organizations from the ground up and led firms to number one marketshare positions.

Also during the course of those 30 years he has managed through challenges that resemble today’s difficult business environment. He has come to understand that while the headlines in any given day might be large and global in scope, it is the personal challenges of professional adversity that we need to navigate through.

Whether in personal production, or leading teams to great success, the same underlying principal always need to be operating: the ability to be memorable.

That is why in 2007 Rob began to ask audiences across the country What’s Your MQ - Memorability Quotient®?

Today as CEO and founder of shorespeak®, Rob consults with sales organizations on strategic direction, coaches leaders on career development and growth, and speaks to audiences large and small.

The common thread of those engagements is the same. How can your firm, your team, or you stand out from the sea of sameness? What will you do in your business that achieves lasting memorability with clients, employees and colleagues?

More than many other professionals that serve the financial services community, Rob’s depth and breadth of industry experience immediately connects with firms that engage his services.

A leader who has been tested, both through experience and through time, Rob Shore looks forward to bringing you his style of new thought leadership.
