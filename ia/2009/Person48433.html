Colleen Georges’s Summary

High-energy, dynamic Business Owner/Psychology Educator/Higher Education Administrator/Licensed Counselor with 11 years of experience developing résumé writing and career coaching services, instructional methodologies, and educational materials that facilitate the success of a diverse population. Active member of the educational community, developing and maintaining rapport with students, staff, and faculty.
