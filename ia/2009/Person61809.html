Dennis Volz’s Summary

For years I've been in love with the 'idea' of Network Marketing. While my previous adventures in this business model yielded some modest success, I never quite got a handle on the real secret of finding the right people and impacting their lives in a positive and constructive way. I was busy chasing friends and family, making my 'warm list' and chasing people away from a very solid opportunity.

I never gave up though. I kept researching and learning and failing (which is just discovering additional ways that it WOULDN'T work) LOL

Didn't really have an Aha! moment, but over the course of a few months the answers slowly came into focus. I needed to be about educating people rather than convincing them. That's when I started MLM-Toolkit.com. It's the gateway to an online training system that takes all the frustration out of the 'business' of Network Marketing. I endeavor to freely pass these "secrets" along fellow entrepreneurs.

So Take Heart -- The dream IS STILL ALIVE.
