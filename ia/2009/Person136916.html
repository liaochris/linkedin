Larry Ausley, CPM’s Summary

I am a retired water quality biologist with a B.S. degree in Marine Biology and 30 years of service to the NC Division of Water Quality. Involved in state and federal water quality protection through my own organization as well as working with the USEPA and the Society of Environmental Toxicology and Chemistry (SETAC) I served as president of the Carolinas Chapter of SETAC and on the steering committee of SETAC's Whole Effluent Toxicity Experts Advisory Panels. I managed water quality programs including aquatic toxicology, ambient water quality monitoring, phytoplankton monitoring, special surveys, microbiology and inorganic chemical analyses. Credentialed as a Certified Public Manager, I was inducted to North Carolina's Order of the Long Leaf Pine for service to the citizens of NC. I am currently a Notary Public in Wake County, NC. Achieved certifications in cave diving, divemaster and EPA/NOAA working diver. I served as president of the Carolina Cane Club for 2006 and 2007. In 2007 I was certified by the American Canoe Association as a River and Whitewater Kayak Instructoras an advanced whitewater kayak Instructor in 2008 and a level 3 coastal kayaking instructor in 2009. Elected to the Board of Directors of the American Canoe Association in January 2008, ACA Secretary in 2009 and currently serving as the chair of ACA’s Recreation Committee and as Board liaison for ACA’s Safety Education and Instruction Council. Beginning in 2008 I developed Triangle Kayak as a focal point of my kayak teaching activities with Paddle Creek in Wake Forest, NC.

Larry Ausley, CPM’s Specialties:

Aquatic Toxicology, Whole Effluent Toxicity (WET) testing, whitewater kayak instruction
