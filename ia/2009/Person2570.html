Allan Cuttle’s Summary

Allan is a Managing Director of Sales and Business Development within BPS’ (Business Propulsion Solutions) GRC Technology Solutions Practice. Allan is responsible for direct sales, creating channel partnerships, furthering overall new business development and creating new sources of revenue for the company

Prior to joining BPS, Allan was a Managing Director in PWC's Advisory Practice in NY where he provided Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) consulting services to clients. Allan's extensive background and expertise is in Enterprise Risk Management (ERM), Operational Risk Management (ORM), Sarbanes-Oxley and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Compliance. Allan has over 20 years of experience as a leading industry practitioner and consultant.

Prior to joining PWC, Allan was the founding partner of Operational Risk Enterprise, Inc. (ORE), a boutique Technology Solutions and Management Consulting Company specializing in ERM, ORM and Sarbanes-Oxley Compliance.

Prior to founding ORE, Allan was the Deputy Chief Operational Risk Officer (CRO OR) for Deutsche Bank (DB), where he directed the global implementation of the DB Operational Risk (OR) Framework within all DB businesses and jurisdictions worldwide. Before his role as Deputy Chief Operational Risk Officer, Allan headed up Operational Risk Management for the Private Client Asset Management (PCAM) division of Deutsche Bank (DB) which included the Private Wealth Management, Asset Management and the Retail Banking businesses globally. Allan’s previous experience includes supervising Operational Risk Management for the Global Private Bank within Deutsche Bank Group and Bankers Trust Co. (BT). In that role, he was also responsible for the Anti-Money Laundering (AML), Know-Your-Client (KYC), Anti-Fraud and BCP (Business Continuity Planning) programs. I

Allan is also a frequent public speaker, trainer and author on the subject of Operational Risk Management, Enterprise Risk Management, and GRC frameworks.
