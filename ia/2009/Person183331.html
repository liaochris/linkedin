Paul Layfield’s Summary

Vertically integrated experience spanning diagnostic catheters, PTA and PTCA balloons and catheters, steerable teflon catheters, ureteral stents and catheters, urological implants, and SDS.
Experienced in customizing PTFE, FEP and Polyolefin heat shrink to meet various requirements.
Experienced in solving difficult extrusion problem involving multi-lumen PTFE, FEP, PEEK and many other materials.
Strong analytical, organizational, and interpersonal skills. Goal oriented and team player with a record of releasing new products and meeting or exceeding deadlines.
Experienced in validation, PFMEA, MVP, TMV and all associated documentation for 510k submissions as well as ISO/GMP requirements.
Over 17 years experience in the biomedical engineering field with a strong background in new product introduction and research and development. Able to take an idea from concept through to manufacturing.

Paul Layfield’s Specialties:

Experienced in all extrusion processes and their development. Fluent in all aspects of balloon and catheter development. Development of various devices requiring hydrophilic coatings, laser welding, plasma treatment, ultrasonic welding, extrusion, centerless grinding, reflowing and modification of plastic properties.
