Mark Prasatik’s Summary

Mark Prasatik is the global director of credit risk training for Triple Point Technology Inc. a leading provider of enterprise risk management solutions to global trading corporations. Since 2007 he has led and developed a thriving for profit training business using a unique blend of instructor led and technology based training methods. He was previously the corporate training manager for Aegis Mortgage deploying training technology and programs to 5 subsidiaries in the prime and sub prime mortgage industry. He joined Aegis in 2004 to implement its learning management system which evolved into the Aegis Sharing Knowledge network. In 1990 Mark joined Academy Sports and Outdoors in retail operations management and in 1999 moved to IT to architect its first e-learning platform which is still in use today. Since 2007 Mark has served on the board of ASTD Houston, a 501c3 organization serving the training and development profession, and will serve as its president in 2010. Mark is a frequent national speaker on training and enterprise 2.0 technology and has a BS in Information Technology.
