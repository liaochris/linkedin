Alex Snelling (asnelling@dgrh.loreal.com)’s Summary

Having started my career in FMCG marketing I have worked for the past 7 years within Human Resources, developing expertise in the recruitment, development and management of talent. This has included 3 years spent as a solo graduate recruiter for L'Oréal in the United Kingdom as well as 2 years working in training & development in Paris, France. Following my last role as Recruitment Director for L'Oreal UK developing innovative direct hiring strategies, I am now creating a European platform for talent acquisition based in Paris, France. During my career I have consistently pursued voluntary activities leading a Hospital radio station in the UK and SOS Help, a suicide listening help line in Paris. My long term goal is to develop into a strategic HR leadership position with an international scope.
