Laurie Orlov’s Summary

Laurie is an industry thought leader on the technology for aging in place, and the owner of a website and blog: www.ageinplacetech.com to research and write about technology for aging in place, a new market area that will target the 'senior value chain'.

Experienced consultant to senior IT executives; CIO Coach and guide; hiring CIO; Industry Analyst; Progressive 24-year career in IT, leading to VP and CIO prior to Forrester. Nine years of industry analyst role and research director, including B2B ecommerce, ERP and eProcurement applications, and IT leadership.

As vice president and principal analyst at Forrester, Laurie provided thought leadership for CIOs on the CIO-business relationship. She is widely recognized for her guidance and research on how CEOs view IT, IT communication and marketing, and IT's role in business innovation. Laurie was one of the key drivers of Forrester's definitions about IT maturity, IT archetypes, and how to communicate about IT with business execs and the board of directors. During her nine years at Forrester, she has led Business Applications, Information Delivery, and IT Leadership practices.

Her research focused on IT-business alignment, communicating and marketing the value and benefit of IT, characteristics of leading IT organizations, and how IT can boost its role and reputation in an enterprise.
