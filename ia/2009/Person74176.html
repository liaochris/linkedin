Eric Edelson’s Summary

I am passionate about helping others and finding unique solutions to really big problems in sustainability, "green" technology, and healthcare.

I recently graduated from the Stanford Graduate School of Business ('07) and am committed to finding interesting opportunities the cleantech / sustainability / medical device / health and wellness / consumer healthcare industry. I am most passionate about entrepreneurship, either with raw start-ups or entrepreneurial environments within larger corporations.

Before school I worked hard in the investing world while at Lehman Brothers. I specialized in biopharmaceutical and medtech investing in both the private and public sectors. While investing is a fantastic profession, in the midst of the job I realized that my true passion was working on entrepreneurial ideas in growing companies helping bring new, innovative technologies into the lives of people who need them most.

I almost forgot, I love to run and am always up for training and competing in crazy races. My most recent race was the Oyster Urban Adventure Race in San Francisco and I am currently training for the San Diego Marathon where I hope to break 3 hours for the first time. One day I hope to get a lottery spot in the Western States 100.
