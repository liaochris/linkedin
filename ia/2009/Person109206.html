Jen Millard’s Summary

Jen is the EVP of Retail Client Services and Real Estate Strategy for Zoom Systems. Zoom Systems is the pioneer of the automated retail channel.

Prior to joining Zoom Systems, Jen was a former Director at McMillan|Doolittle and lead the retail operations practice. Jen specializes in the retail operations and consumer experience areas. Her background includes 20 years of experience as a retail operations executive with a focus on new concept strategy and execution. She is acknowledged by the industry as an expert in operation design and execution and takes pride in assisting clients in creating and managing profitable retail environments. Key clients have included an impressive mix of both large and small retailers, manufacturers, and service providers such as T-Mobile, Garmin, Caterpillar Harpo Studios, Nickelodeon, and The Mall of America. She also has done retail growth and positioning work for retailers in Canada, Korea, Japan, and India.
Prior to joining McMillan|Doolittle in 2006, Jen held senior executive positions in store operations with Nambe LLC, Saks Inc., Brown Shoe Group, Bed Bath and Beyond, and Sears. Major projects have included, new concept positioning and start-up development, strategic assessments, selling culture performance and measurement; in-store operational process improvement; retail design/construction and planning, as well as research for the investment community.
Jen is an active member of the Retail Council of Advisors, The National Retail Federation, The International Council of Shopping Centers; and the Greater North Michigan Avenue Association. Jen was selected for the 2007 Chain Store Age,Rising Stars- 40 under 40 and is frequently asked to comment on the retail industry by leading news organizations such as the Chicago Tribune .

Jen is an honors graduate from Colby College in Waterville, ME and lives with her family in San Francisco, CA.

Jen Millard’s Specialties:

Retail Operations, Strategy, New Concept Development, Start-up, automated retail stores, vending, airport retail, channel management, Turnaround Strategy, Store Operations, Brand Management, Brand Positioning, International growth strategies and execution.
