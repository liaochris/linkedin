Craig Crane’s Summary

Jim Henson trained Puppeteer, 10 years at Nickelodeon puppeteering their characters, digital Puppeteer animating CG characters in realtime for both Medialab Paris and Dreamteam Israel. Even spent a good few years performing George from Rainbow for the ITV kids show of the same name. My early involvement with CG performance animation was also my first introduction to Motion capture in 1994, with the use of the Polyhemus and Ascension Motion Capture systems.

Along the way, I re-imagined Jeff Wayne's vision for Jeff Wayne's The War Of The Worlds, redesigning all the martian craft, as well as animation for the UK live tour.

2 years as a Generalist and Mocap lead at DNEG led me to Centroid 3D, a Motion Capture facility based on the Pinewood studio lot. From there, its back into Soho, previously working as a VFX artist for The Peerless Camera Company followed by some Mocap / crowd wrangling for Double Negative.

My main interests in CG are Motion Capture, crowd co-ordination and behaviorial simulation such as Natural Motions Endorphin. However, I welcome any new challenge sent my way.

Craig Crane’s Specialties:

CG Generalist (Autodesk Maya), ability to lead small teams (DNEG, Peerless), Motion Capture specialist / consultant, Puppeteering, Lip Sync, Client interaction, creative writing.
