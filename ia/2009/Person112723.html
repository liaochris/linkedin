Jill Nicholson’s Summary

Jill Nicholson is an enthusiastic, entrepreneurial, innovative thinker with a results-driven style and an ability to successfully manage and motivate individuals and teams. Jill is experienced in developing strategy and the execution of public relations, marketing and communications programs (including leveraging social media) leading to successful new business development.

Jill has proven success in building strategic relationships with new business partners, developing and promoting new business initiatives and developing strong community partnerships. She has the ability to successfully operate at a strategic and operational level while mindfully tracking the details. She has strong business management and communication skills along with a successful record of managing budgets and project management.

Jill creates innovative concepts when launching new products/services/programs ensuring brand continuity and "pull marketing".
