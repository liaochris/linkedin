Kate Bellingan’s Summary

As a marketer for SkillPod Media, I am passionate about the casual games market, and highly recommend skillpod.com games to any gamer interested in relaxing and taking a quick and highly addictive break from our extremely stressful days.

I promote SkillPod mainly via network marketing, and you can view SkillPod's profiles on the following sites: Zoopy, Viddler, Dailymotion, Flickr, Photobucket, Metacafe, Viadeo, Yahoo Videos, Mister-Wong, Bebo, Twitter, Facebook SkillPod Games Application, Delicious.

I also write press releases which can be viewed on SkillPod's profiles on Wikio, Digg and Stumble Upon.

I manage the tournaments and prizes on SkillPod's and SkillPod's partners various sites.

Promotion of the new and exciting features on Skillpod's site - see under SkillPod's Gaming News: http://www.skillpod.com/news.

Work hard, and play even harder! Game, because time won't kill itself :)
