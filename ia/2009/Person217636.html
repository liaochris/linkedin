Silvia Goh’s Summary

Silvia Goh is one of the few who have crossed between New and Traditional Media, her competence lies in Creative Management, especially in Integrated Marketing, Events and Promotion with Broadcast Media. Silvia's forte includes startups, where she spent 16 years in North Asia - Macau, Hong Kong and Beijing. She is based in Singapore currently.

Silvia Goh’s Specialties:

Branding, Creative and Design, Integrated Solutions, Digital and New Media, Traditional Media, Management
