Amber Williams’s Summary

Express Anonymously with BitchOGram

Bitch’OGram is an innovative online venture that lets its users send anonymous mail. The site acknowledges a human need to communicate secretively at times. It tries to address the typical situations when a user might like to complain or comment or bitch or simply admire. The site allows its users to send a secret mail or text message over phone or a USPS postcard to anyone they wish to communicate with anonymously.

Extending freedom to express without inhibitions is the mainstay of the site. It attempts to provide its users a unique platform where they can voice their opinion to a particular person or group or the public at large, without being identified by anyone.

The mission of the site is to help people let out and let go. The site does not restrict the nature of the message for delivery in any manner. However, it explicitly states in its elaborate terms of services that it would restrict any users found guilty of posting illegal, offensive, pornographic, obscene, threatening and/ or harassing content through the site. The purview of objectionable content material includes viruses and other disruptive or destructive material. The service costs $0.99 per message as transmission charges, excluding postage.
