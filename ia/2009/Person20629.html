Arunav Banerjee’s Summary

Hey there,

My name is Arunav Banerjee. I have recently graduated in 2009 with a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering from one of the premier Engineering schools in India and have joined Zyoin Web Inc., a startup doctom entrepreneurial venture in India's Silicon Valley city, Bangalore. You can call me a Web Strategist or a Marketing Strategist or a Marketing Research Analyst or even a Technology Evangelist, or whatever related nicknames you can think of.

We are doing some great work in changing the way the recruitment concept works in modern times. Believe me, we have some really smart guys working in here. And i believe, i am one of them. :-) The concept of Zyoin basically involves the employer and the head-hunter as the oxygen of the recruiting supply chain. Employer posts job requirements on Zyoin's database and headhunters find suitable candidates to refer them to the employers. In the end, if these candidates are hired by the employer, the headhunter is rewarded in a handsome fashion. We even cater to freelance recruiters.

We are soon launching our Zyoin Complete Recruitment Solution Software (CRS) which we soon hope to provide to interested vendors in SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) format. We are also launching our Applicant Tracking System (ATS) Software which can be either bought from us in licensed format or can be availed free of cost with our CRS Software. We are also in the process of collaboration with other organizations to start recruitment certification programs. Our website is also undergoing massive modification.

As a Marketing and Web Strategist, my work is to research, analyze and implement different marketing methodologies to make Zyoin a brand name in the recruitment marketplace. I am mostly using Search Engine Optimization and Marketing techniques to achieve this goal.

If you think that you too can contribute with your novel ideas to our team, then please feel free to contact me.

Cheers!
Arunav Banerjee
