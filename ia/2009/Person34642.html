Bruce Blythe’s Summary

Skilled, accomplished journalist with almost two decades as a reporter and editor with global, real-time media organizations including Bloomberg News, Reuters and Dow Jones Newswires. Expertise includes energy, agriculture, finance, futures exchanges, government, sports, the Federal Reserve and the economy.

I’m a proven newsroom leader adept at covering spot news in highly-competitive environments, building wide-ranging source networks from scratch, juggling the multiple tasks of editorial management and writing and editing under daily deadline pressure where both speed and accuracy are at a premium.

I've gone just about anywhere to get the story: Chicago's futures trading floors, sweltering corn fields of Downstate Illinois, Iowa's hog farms, sweaty, post-game locker rooms at the World Series, middle-of-nowhere Kansas for 8-man high school football, offshore oil-drilling rigs 150 miles into the Gulf of Mexico, just to name a few. There's no 'news' inside a newsroom, as the saying goes. Scroll down to "Bruce Blythe's files" for some story samples.

I'm seeking full-time opportunities in writing, editing, corporate communications, investor relations, equity/fixed-income/commodity research or new media.

I'm also available for freelance assignments of all varieties.

Bruce Blythe’s Specialties:

Business and financial writing, editing and research, stock and balance sheet analysis, sports coverage and personality profiles, editorial and project management, including story, photo and graphics assignment. I'm also proficient with the Bloomberg terminal.
