Patti Becker’s Summary

BECKER ASSOCIATES LLC creates, develops, sources and licenses children's consumer products and promotional items to major manufacturers around the world. Many of our products have sold millions of pieces!

We're a full-service agency for new product development, sourcing, licensing and marketing consulting, for manufacturers, inventors, entrepreneurs, trade organizations, and financial companies.

We pride ourselves on our evergreen products and long-term relationships. Othello, Gumby, Pokey, Barrel of Monkeys, the Disappearing Magic Baby Bottle, Battle Dome, Nerf Ping Pong, Baby Get Well, TMNT Goops, Sliders and Shirt Pocket Games are a few of the hundreds of products we've developed or licensed, many of which have become familiar household names.

With licensees in more than 100 countries, we partner with industry leaders including Mattel, Hasbro, Bandai, and Fisher-Price.

Our proprietary children's products and properties connect kids with their favorite licensed characters around the world! Many of our products have been co-branded with: Disney, TMNT, Simpsons, Spiderman, Superman, Batman, Barbie, Pokemon, Hello Kitty and Angel Cat Sugar.

Our innovative product range includes toys, games, consumer electronics, premiums, private-label, home video, interactive, bed and bath furnishings, publishing, accessories, gifts and other consumer packaged goods, sold through every channel of retail distribution, from mass retailers to specialty and chain stores, including Wal-Mart, Target, Toys R Us, Borders.

Our consulting clients include: Ascend Capital, Capital Consulting, Clovis Capital, Diamondback Capital, Eminence Capital, Grand Toys, Toy Industry Association, Vista Research, Society of Industry Leaders, A Standard & Poor’s Business, Women in Toys and more.

Visit our website www.BeckerAssoc.com to see a few of the products and properties we've licensed or email PBecker@BeckerAssoc.com for an appointment.
