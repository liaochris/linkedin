Nicole Davis’s Summary

National Recognition Corporation serves to provide recognition of outstanding students on a national level. Through our annual publication, we bring attention to those students in America who are exceeding expectations and paving the way to a bright future for both themselves and our country!

Who’s Who Among America’s Middle School Students offers a customized way for students to showcase more about themselves individually, more than any traditional local yearbook. Our continued recognition of high performance students has become a strong motivator and compliment to America’s youth.
