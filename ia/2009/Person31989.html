Martin Johnson’s Summary

Over 13 years' experience in the high tech industry creating powerful brand identies, marketing strategies, integrated marketing programs and demand gen campaigns, with a proven track record of successfully building global brands and driving lead generation. I have also spent the past 5 years leading large marketing teams in the internet security industry, focusing on building awareness and demand among Small and Medium Businesses and large Enterprises in the finance, medical, telecom, manufacturing and govt. sectors.

I have a passion for social marketing, online marketing, ecommerce and web design and ensuring that all of these activities are tracked, measured, and aligned with overall brand, campaign and demand gen goals. Having started as both a technical/marketing writer and a web/graphic designer, I bring a creative, aggressive, hands-on approach to marketing leadership.
