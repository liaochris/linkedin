Nigel George’s Summary

As a self-confessed technology junkie, I am really interested in helping businesses leverage modern technologies - especially distributed technologies like the internet - to build more innovative, more efficient and ultimately more sustainable business practices.

Along with a passion for working at the leading edge of change, I have extensive experience in business management, standards based integrated management systems, IT and infrastructure development, software programming and auditing.

I am particularly interested R&D and project management in renewable energy.

Currently, I have a project with Newcastle Uni looking into citizen science input into environmental monitoring and management - focusing on water issues in Australia. I am developing a range of electronic monitoring devices and an Internet website to facilitate data collection and mapping.

Nigel George’s Specialties:

ISO9001:2000 Quality Management Systems, ISO14001:2004 Environmental Management Systems, internal and external auditing, project management, change management and R&D in high technology fields.
