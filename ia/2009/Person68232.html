Duncan Brooks’s Summary

Duncan is currently at American Transmission Company (ATC) located in Waukesha, Wisconsin, as Senior Financial Analyst where he has been challenged to help lead ATC's Asset Management and Maintenance functions to world-class business, operational, and financial practices.

You’ll find Duncan to be a versatile and organized finance and accounting professional with extensive and progressive experience in financial systems, analysis, reporting, service center support, and accounting. He has demonstrated ability to effectively administer reporting applications. Furthermore, he brings a strong focus on logical and organized design and implementation of financial systems. You will further notice he is very cooperative in his sharing and exchanging of self-taught and job acquired skills and experiences. Duncan enjoys the challenges of high technology, manufacturing, and corporate environments and thrives in an atmosphere of cross-functional and operational teams.

Note that Duncan has extensive exposure to such applications as: Hyperion financial applications (Enterprise, Pillar, & Essbase), Oracle, Cognos, JD Edwards, Infinium, and SAP R/3 v4.6C (implementation and configuration of the finance (FI), and control (CO) modules).

The firms with whom Duncan has held permanent positions include: American Transmission Company, eFunds Corporation, Compaq, and Digital Equipment Corporation. Complimenting this are his several long-term assignments, via national recognized staffing agencies, where he consulted on finance, accounting, and HR matters with such enterprises as: Pneumatech Inc, Johnson Controls Inc, Bombardier Recreational Products (BRP), Harley-Davidson Inc, ProHealth Care, the F. Dohmen Company, and JohnsonDiversey Inc.

Duncan holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Accountancy from Bentley College (with a minor concentration in Public Administration); he has also studied at Cape Cod Community College and Southeastern Massachusetts University.
