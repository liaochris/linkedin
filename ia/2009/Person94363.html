Henry Schneider’s Summary

Henry is an active SEI-certified CMMI Lead Appraiser, SEI-certified High Maturity Lead Appraiser, and SEI-authorized CMMI instructor for the CMMI-DEV, CMMI-ACQ, and soon the CMMI-SVC. Henry is President and Senior Principal Consultant at Process and Product Quality Consulting. Henry has over 30 years experience in software and systems development as a systems engineer, project manager, developer, trainer, consultant, and assessor (ISO 9000/TickIT and SW-CMM). His background includes more than 15 years in project management, software quality, and process improvement (Quality Circles, TQM, ISO 9000, and CMM). He is an SEI-authorized Lead Assessor for SW-CMM, an SEI-authorized SCAMPI Lead Appraiser for CMMI, and is an SEI-authorized instructor for the Introduction to CMMI course. Henry’s software consulting experience encompasses commercial, government, aerospace, product software, finance, insurance, health care, data communications, and telecommunications organizations. He has also trained software professionals nationwide on how to conduct software quality assurance and testing activities.

Henry has participated on or led more than 130 appraisals, encompassing appraisals in more than 60 companies, four countries, three continents, and many appraisals at Maturity Levels 4 and 5.
