Alexander Thijs’s Summary

Alexander Thijs is a dentist who loves to practice the combination of general dentistry and orthodontics.
He studied at the University of Hasselt (Belgium) for one year after which he went to the University of Nijmegen (Netherlands) where he graduated in Dentistry (2004, cum laude). He studied orthodontics through a training programme of three years (2008).
In addition to this, he has built a great experience in 19th Century Master Paintings for research and business purposes. He fell in love with the beauty of the Promenade des Anglais in Nice, where he owns a 3 bedroom apartment which he rents out on his website (see website links) supplying his guests a wonderful holiday / vacation rental.
