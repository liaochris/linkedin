Arun Manansingh’s Summary

Career Summary:
Business oriented Information Technology Executive with a passion for creating demonstrable business value through technology enablement with over twelve years of progressive IT management experience in international finance, leasing, and banking.

SMBC Leasing and Finance, Inc., a subsidiary of the Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation Group, is a leading financial firm providing tax and non-tax oriented leases and financial products to the global marketplace.

Selected Accomplishments:
1. Building and managing an IT department in New York and London.
2. Implemented systems capable of supporting 24x7 operations designed for redundancy, scalability, and business continuity resulting in a 99% uptime for the environment and eliminating costs due to outages.
3. Delivered savings of $60K by improving service levels and reducing hardware/software complexity by standardizing products.
4. Managed reorganization and consolidation of datacenters in the northeast for Sumitomo Mitsui’s Americas Division headquarters reducing overall operational costs by $1M.

Career Goals and Objectives:
1. To become a senior executive where I can contribute to the corporate vision and responsible for leading a group of people to develop innovative ways to build and implement the technology solutions in alignment with the corporate vision to maximize profitability and, preferably as a Chief Information Officer or Chief Technical Officer.

2. I would be interested in securing a position at a startup or an established company requiring technical direction in the New York/Stamford/Westchester area.
