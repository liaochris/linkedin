Ron Boat’s Summary

Working with quality people as well as meeting and serving quality clients is a passion. One reason I never held a regular job? I started PS/A in '71 and been moving forward ever since serving some of the largest as well as newest companies on the planet.

Over the years I've learned many regimens and applied them to my work. From script writing and planning to production project management, camera operator, editor and sound engineer. My background in music having scholarships to one college and two universities and time with the Naval School of Music & Army Band brings a sense of continuity and fluidity to my work. I love what I do as do our people.

I love this biz because it allows me to be in a nuclear plant one day, on a golf course with Jack Nicklaus the next and participating in drug enforcement training the next.

Our experience began with the Production Team on the first Fiesta Bowl '71.

On location recording groups such as Stephen Stills, Jackson Browne and The Group, Ronee Blakley, Todd Rundgren, Richie Beirach, Dave Liebman and Paul Winter Consort.

Marketing Promoter for Ramada Inns, Royal Inns, DoubleTree Inns using music programs with artists such as Oscar Peterson, Kenny Burrell, Buddy Collette, Bud Shank & others.

Concert Producer for Andy Kaufman (Phoenix / San Diego), The Graduates, Wayland & Madame.

Producer/Director for Jack Nicklaus and Tom Weiskopf marketing programs for the Troon and Desert Highlands resorts.

Broadcast Director and Technical Director for Carnie WIlson, Naomi Judd, Jimmy Newquist broadcasts and events.

Technical Producer/Director for Boys & Girls Clubs annual Black Tie events.

Producer/Director for nuclear training and marketing programs for Areva / France.

Production Project Manager, Producer/Director for hundreds of commercials, corporate, marketing, promotional and live events and broadcasts over the past 38 years.

Past Advertising Agency for Arron Rents Furniture's national marketing.

Ron Boat’s Specialties:

Project Manager for all areas of marketing and production. Includes: project planning, script writing, music selections and editing, talent selections, audio production, video shooting and editing, graphic element oversight, strategic marketing and advertising, still photography shoots, directing and producing live events and broadcasts, creative concept director for websites and multimedia.
