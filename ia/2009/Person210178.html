Sari Follansbee’s Summary

EdTechDesign is a forward-looking educational product development, research, and business strategy company. We work with K-12 publishers, developers, and schools as they face the imperatives and challenges of using digital capacities to transform their products, services, and most importantly teaching and learning.

Sari Follansbee’s Experience

  • President

    EdTech Design Associates

    (Internet industry)

    2005 — Present (4 years)

    Focusing on Web 2.0 and 3.0 capacities, EdTech Design works with those who want to use disruptive technologies to transform teaching and learning. With literacy at the core, we bridge the traditional in publishing and schools with the new in online read/write tools and social networking environments.

    EdTech Design helps educational publishers and developers, schools, and community/corporate educational initiatives build collaboration and differentiation to extend reach and impact. We inform business and product development with schools' needs, market analysis, pedagogy, instructional design, and "what works" research. We do the product architecture, requirements, implementation, and evaluation as needed.

    Areas of expertise include: teaching and learning reform with multiple media and delivery vehicles, subject taxonomies, content chunking and tagging, search/browse user interfaces, customization, literacy development, special needs, Universal Design for Learning...
