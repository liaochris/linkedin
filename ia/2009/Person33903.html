Brian Hayes’s Summary

I have held a variety of leadership and management positions over the last 20 years. From managing a division of a major southern California pest control company to owning and running my own business. I have seen and done a lot over the years. I love the experience of learning new things and helping others succeed. Helping others has helped me to personally develop a "Pay it Forward" mentality that guides me each day.

Working with different companies and people over the years has taught me to always look for ways of improving the things I do. I have learned a lot and love to share the things I learn with others. Finding an efficient, cost effective way to do something is fun, exciting, and motivating for me.

At this point, I have recently started my own business and love the challenge. The opportunity to work with new people everyday and solve new problems is what keeps me going.

One of my greatest joys is being married to my beautiful wife Lisa. She is the mother of our five children and keeps the house running while I am working at my "job." We have been married over ten years and she still says she loves me, but doesn't always like me! I can live with that and look forward to the next ten+ years.

If I can do anything to help anyone, please do not hesitate to ask. I am willing to do what I can and admit what I cannot do. I will happily refer you to someone if it is something I am no able to do. I believe that is part of success, focusing on what we are good at and letting other do what they do well. Each of us has a talent and it is important to develop those talents and help others to find and develop theirs too.

Brian Hayes’s Specialties:

I primarily work with small businesses that are starting out or need help with their accounting or other business needs, such as; payroll, taxes, business entities, or registered agent services.

I also help families with income tax preparation, IRS problems, personal budgeting, and loan modifications for mortgages.

I am a Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor and a member of the Utah Association of CPA's and on the ProNet Council. I am also a member of the AICPA.
