Derek Mansfield’s Summary

The (London) Times described me as "sometimes outrageous, but always amusing"

I decided that when I pop my clogs I'd like that written on my headstone.

The Times wrote the piece when I wrote a best selling book on landscape design. The trade press wrote about me ".. A true visionary... will do more to change the face of landscaping in Britain than Repton". It was true, because for better or worse this book, and the company I ran at that time, established the instant makeover garden concept complete with 3D CAD.

........ a couple of decades speed past......

For the last 10 years I have been commuting between Kiev, Ukraine and London, England running a small group of expert and successful companies; the companies specialise in software and web development, design, online and search marketing.

The link between landscaping and online marketing?

Vision, and a passion to excel.

And I still only work with people I like. Ask them.
