Peter Kaye’s Summary

Breakthrough consumer brand marketer, general manager, and strategist. Success launching new products and reinvigorating existing brands in the U.S. and globally. Classic consumer packaged goods marketing training, top-tier general management education, documented success in building brands under the most challenging conditions through innovative integrated marketing plans across traditional and digital media.

Experience: food & beverage, personal & household care, media, and “green” products.

Peter Kaye’s Specialties:

brand strategy development, consumer research and insights, insight-driven marketing plans, multi-media brand-building programs, traditional and digital media, brand-building capability development, general management perspective, US and global experience
