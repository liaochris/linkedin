Amanda Davie’s Summary

Amanda is a Search and Digital Marketing Consultant, based in London.

With over ten years' digital comms experience on the agency side, Amanda has spent eight years in Head of Search roles at Outrider (WPP) and more recently at i-level, working with clients such as Procter and Gamble, Orange, Direct Line, BT, Sky, The AA, and the COI, to help them to develop marketing leading search and integrated digital strategies and to maximise results from digital channels.

In 2008 Amanda founded Boolian, a digital and search marketing consultancy which provides training, education, support, process and strategy (operational and marketing) for clients, agencies and trade associations.

Amanda is a regular contributor to Search-related national and trade press, and has spoken at European industry conferences such as World Telemedia, Internet World, WorldVision, Search Engine Strategies, IAB Engage and IAA / IAB Europe European Online Advertising Summit. Amanda is passionate about agency best practice and supports industry bodies to help to educate and train the media industry on the value and importance of Search, not just as a marketing channel but as a consumer insight funnel.

Amanda is passionate about best practice, particularly agency best practice, and in 2007 she chaired the IPA Search Steering Group (now the IPA Search Group) and played an active role on the IAB Search Council. Similarly, Amanda has also contributed towards training programmes for the IAB and the APA.
