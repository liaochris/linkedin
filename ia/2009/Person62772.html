Gord Mackay’s Summary

A veteran of Demand Generation Process, I have been creating sales-ready qualified opportunities in the B2B High-Tech Sector for over ten years, through effective marketing campaigns, proactive engagement techniques, and methodical process.

I have written an eBook on the "Ten Steps to Effective Demand generation", which is currently available on my website, www.dgauthority.com.

Demand Generation is a Science, constantly evolving and requiring creative, out-of-the-box techniques to prepare raw marketing leads for the Sales Team. The way people purchase in the B2B marketplace has dramatically changed. Prospective Customers are more informed, and will only respond to offers relevant to their interests. Demand Generation empowers you to understand their online behavior, and react accordingly.

I can help you:

Build a Demand Generation process, from start to finish
Create and deploy effective marketing campaigns
Write compelling copy for all types of marketing (email, website, brochures)
Optimize your database and CRM to be most effective and efficient
Build a team of Engagement Professionals to bridge the gap between marketing and Sales
Train, coach and mentor your existing teams on Best Practice Demand Generation techniques

Gord Mackay’s Specialties:

I specialize in Demand Generation in B2B High Tech and Business Services organizations.

The majority of my experience has been at the Engagement Step, building, coaching and training teams to generate qualified sales leads using effective marketing campaigns, analysis of prospect behaviour and conversation techniques.
