Ralph Stalter, Jr.’s Summary

After my first career in management within the not-for-profit professional theater, I “switched gears” and have spent over 18 years as a workplace learning and performance improvement consultant and administrator. Now, in my 3rd career stage, I am looking for new challenges and opportunities that combine personal meaning and social impact!

I would like to apply my business acumen to initiating and deepening international and intercultural programs. As a lifelong learner myself, managing blended learning programs for businesses and individuals that need to reach their organizational or personal goals continues to be of interest as well.

I have also worked successfully on projects with young people and teachers, to stimulate creativity and expand their learning horizons.

My volunteer activities include working with Minds Matter, a not-for-profit organization that brings high-achieving and motivated high schools students from low-income families together with dedicated mentors to achieve academic excellence and expand educational horizons.

Please do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions, or simply want to brain-sail. I am always happy to collaborate and share my expertise. I would welcome the occasion to speak with you further regarding any consulting opportunities that you may have available.
