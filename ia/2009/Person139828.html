Leonardo Clemente, MSc, PMP’s Summary

Leonardo is an executive with about 10 years of broad experience in project management, mostly in energy industry, involving complex and multidisciplinary projects (technical, institutional, economic, financial, environmental and strategic).

During his career he was involved with project management in other industries, as water resources, sanitation, telecommunications and IT.

He is a PMI certified Project Management Professional (PMP), has a bachelor degree in Civil Engineering, a Master degree in Hydraulic Engineering and a Specialist certificate in energy planning, operation and trading. all of them from UFPR. He has also presented technical papers including many themes in energy industry.

At Petrobras he is working with renewable energy (hydro, biomass, wind, photovoltaic and solar thermal), mostly with small and medium hydro power plants. His core business is develop new partnerships and existing projects in the portfolio. In this work he has to contact with many stakeholders, internal and external, as entrepreneurs, regulatory agencies and public/private companies.

Leonardo has a natural leadership, entrepreneurship and is committed with results. In 2005 he had introduced to Human Dynamics, a science that studies fundamental distinctions in peoples functioning as whole systems. His system is Physical-Mental.
