Carmen Parnos’s Summary

* Founder of Super Leaders of Australia and
Australian Coaching Academy
* Australia's Personal Branding Expert
* Leadership and Performance Coach
* Trainer, Facilitator, Public Speaker
* Author: Super Leaders of Australia Vol 1 and
Apprentice to Business Ace - your inside-out guide to personal branding

Carmen has over 25 years of corporate experience.. She is an entrepreneur by nature and is the founder of two companies; The Australian Coaching Academy that focuses on leadership development and workplace coaching and the Super Leaders of Australia initiative that pubishes an annual book that showcases Australia’s most powerful leaders at an international level and Business Briefing Events with the purpose of aiding in the relief of poverty in developing countries.

She is one of Australia's most recognised personal branding experts,accredited coach and public speaker and a published author. (Apprentice to Business ACE - your inside out guide to prsonal branding, Super Leaders of Australia Vol 1) She is also a member of the International Coaching Federation, American Chamber of Commerce in Australia ( Committee member) and Women on Boards.
