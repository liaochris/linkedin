Enrique Carrillo’s Summary

Web Portfolio Link: http://www.mycareerhighlights.com/enriquecarrillo

Senior Management Executive
Supply Chain, Strategy, Sales, Marketing, Finance, Human Resources
International Businesses - High-Growth & Startup Companies

Enrique Carrillo is a Senior Partner and Director at Azurian SCM which specializes in delivering Value Chain Management consulting services to Latin American companies. During his career he has excelled at being a catalyst in developing and implementing progressive business strategies leveraging IT intelligence to gain competitive dominance, raising the standards of organizational leadership and executive management through high level professionalism and focus on results.

Among his previous responsibilities, Enrique was world wide Leader for Workforce Supply Chain at IBM, in which he oversaw the supply side of a new labor resource management system to manage employee and contractor resources similar to the way a supply chain tracks and forecasts demand and inventory. This substantially improved workforce management and skill matching, resulting in savings in excess of $925 Million.

Prior to that he was an Associate Partner in the Supply Chain Management Practice of IBM, with overall responsibility for Supply Chain Management consulting services. When Enrique first joined IBM he created the Global Supply Chain (SCOR) Assessments group which pioneered and established the divisions’ supply chain improvement assessments, industry benchmarks for performance measures, and identified opportunities for reengineering. During his leadership the group successfully identified and achieved savings of $230 Million across the supply chain. Prior to IBM he was a Senior Manager for Nortel Networks’ Wireless Latin America region responsible for $660 Million worth of revenue, as well as former manufacturing manager for Johnson & Johnson.
