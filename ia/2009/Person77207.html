Ranjit Singh Dhaliwal, MD’s Summary

RANJIT S. DHALIWAL, MD, DOMS, is a renowned exponent, and one of the pioneers of Manual SICS in North India.

He obtained his Masters in Ophthalmology from Government Medical College, Patiala, India.

He is practising Ophthalmology at EYE INFIRMARY, NABHA for the last 18 years and has the proud privilege of running a modern eye set-up in a rural area. He is a pioneer in using Computerisation & Micro Eye Surgery to the advantage of his patients in Nabha.

He is the Founder Editor of the Video Journal of Punjab Ophthalmological Society and is an Associate Editor of the Journal of ISMSICS and Tropical Ophthalmology.

He is the Coordinator and Moderator of the SICS Club on the internet.

He has more than 60 publications, presentations, instruction courses and workshops to his credit.

He can be reached at Eye Infirmary, Nabha – 147201, Punjab, India.
