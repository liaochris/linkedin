Timothy Nurre’s Summary

Experience: I have over 14 years of successfully selling IT services, a total of 10 years in management, and 6 years of nuclear engineering experience. I have several entrepreneurial projects under my belt, although running my own company hasn't been my primary focus. I'm also a technical writer, having completed several successful projects for a small client.

Goals: I'm working with Southway Systems and NetStar Telecom in both business development and sales roles, utilizing a combination of channel and direct sales. As sister companies, Southway works with clients on the customer premises side of the switch, and NetStar as a telecom company, works on the cloud side.

My goals are two-fold:
1. Build an active and successful referral partner network in the following verticals: Corporate Real Estate Brokers/Leasing Agents (most people purchase IT consulting and/or telecom when they move), General Contractors (new buildings need IT infrastructure), Technology Vendors (non-competing partners having visibility into client telecom/IT infrastructure needs).
2. Build and active and successful customer network, focusing on disaster recovery/business continuity in the Houston and Galveston regions.
