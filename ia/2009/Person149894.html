marguerite manteau-rao’s Summary

Don't try to squeeze me into a box. I won't fit.

I am a woman with multiple experiences, interests, and skills. An industrial engineer, with a Master's degree from Ecole Centrale de Paris, in France. A marketer and advertiser with an MBA from University of Chicago, and years of CPG experience, last as VP with JWT ad agency. A licensed clinical social worker, with an MSW from Loyola University of Chicago, and Art Therapist credential, who led over 2,000 psychotherapy groups. An artist, with jewelry sculptures featured in several prominent art publications, and numerous national shows in galleries and museums. An environmental activist, whose blog La Marguerite, was one of Top 10 Eco-Blogs for Earth Day 2008, by Times Online. A blogger for the Huffington Post, where I am a social media and sustainability voice. A founding member of Green Moms, a group of women and environmental activists, that won the 2008 Twitter Shorty Awards for Green category. A social entrepreneur, about to launch GroceryChick, an organizing community for health conscious food shoppers. Also co-founder of Terraqualo, a new venture in ag-water information software.
