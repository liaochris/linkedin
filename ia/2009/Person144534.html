C language and Software Engineering.

Operating System specialist, capable to develop any kind of kernel and ecosystem, for most of the modern embedded or huge applications. Good knownledge about the most popular operating systems and its kernel/ecosystem: AmigaOS, Mach, TOS, MinTOS, MacOS Classic, PalmOS, NeXTStep, uClinux, Linux, MINIX, 386BSD, MacBSD and NetBSD.

Good experience w/ Windows NT, Win32 API and DirectX from 8.1 to 10.

Structured Architecture of Computers - Digital Logic level, Microarchitecture level, Instruction Set Architecture level, Parallel Machines Architecture, etc. Processors, Controllers and DSPs.

Digital Security, conceptual experience and pratice of classic problems in software development: bug hunting, exploration through advanced exploitation techniques (even in code-execution-protected scenarios) and wide mastering of buffer overflows (format string, heap overflow, null pointer dereference, etc). Microcontrollers based in cores like 6800, PIC and 8051, and the MC56000 family of Digital Signal Processors (DSP).

Game development using OpenGL, DirectX, Psy-Q and Pro-DG - Meshes, Collision detection, Gravity simulations, etc. Game design, Music composing, Roadmap and Team management.

Development of games for the most popular video games, including XBOX360, PSX, PS2, PSP, Nintendo DS, MegaDrive, MasterSystem, Super Nintendo, NeoGeo and Amiga -- Using proprietary tools (contemplated from reverse engineering) and officials (XBOX360, PSX, Amiga and MegaDrive).

Virtuous, creative and highly social!
