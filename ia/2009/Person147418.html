Fernando Terry’s Summary

T N T Management Group LLC is an independent firm of experienced Real Estate Professionals dedicated to help you capitalize on the current market. Our philosophy today is the same as when
T N T Management was founded; Professionalism in property management, high quality standards and timely and accurate accounting and reporting. These principles have served as the cornerstones for the company's operations.

Our management and sales team are highly qualified individuals who have achieved success in academics and in their business lifes. They are experts in their field with highly developed analytical skills and profound sales techniques. They also have the personal qualities which ensure meticulous attention to client's problems and good working relationships with tenants and unit owners.

Our company is a growing full service property management firm servicing hundreds of satisfied customers. Providing a full compliment of property management that includes maintenance and repairs, renovation services, rent collection, leasing and Sales for residential properties.

T N T Management Group LLC. has grown to become the standard of excellence in our industry. Within that industry in South Florida, the demands for a higher level of service at even more competitive prices grows and effect sales campaigns, T N T Management Group LLC. combines a proficient computer organization with a professional Sales and Management staff knowledgeable with local building codes, standards and sales niches and techniques.

Our years of experience, combined with our reputation for quality service and proven reliability can not be surpassed. Our devotion to our customers has contributed significantly to our success. The company has expanded to its present size through offering superior service at competitive pricing to our clients. From the beginning our goal has remained the same: an unrelenting commitment to exceptional customer satisfaction.
