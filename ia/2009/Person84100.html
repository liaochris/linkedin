Gaurav Sud’s Summary

Gaurav Sud - Vice President - Corporate Strategy at Asian Oilfield Services

Born in Shimla, he did his schooling from there. After obtaining his BTech degree from IIT Delhi, he went on to do his MBA from IIM Calcutta. He started his career with KPMG Corporate finance and since then has worked at Wipro BPO, Keane Consulting, & WNS Global Services. He has over eleven years of experience in M&A, investment banking, financial consulting, corporate planning and strategy. Over the years he has lead engagements for company acquisitions, business strategy, corporate restructuring, due diligence, business planning, financial engineering & merger advisory.

Currently he is heading the corporate strategy division at Asian Oil and helping the company scale up to capitalise on the boom in the oil & gas exploration space. He is leading the new business initiatives and is also handling the Investor Relations for the company (Listed on Bombay Stock Exchange with a Market Cap of Over Rs 300 Cr (USD 75 Million). He has lead the company negotiations with Private Equity firms raising over Rs 80 Cr captial since June 2007.

Gaurav is married and has one son. He is an avid trekker and landscape photographer. He also is a keen value investor and whenever he finds the time organises investor club meetings. He is currently settled in Gurgaon.
