Thiago Ferraz’s Summary

I am an electrical engineer with seven years of experience on software development, three of them working as project manager.

I speak English in an intermediary level. The majority of experience was obtained on the job in an international project that I worked for more than two years and that everyday I had to report and discuss in English with project stakeholders.

I had opportunity to work on all engineering areas improving my systemic view about projects needs.

In two projects I lead teams of almost thirty professionals and the majority of them were more technical experienced than me.

On software development projects, I commonly use to improve my productivity IDEs like Eclipse and Netbeans for Java projects, Source Insight and Microsoft Visual for C projects, LabVIEW to work with GOO programming and to make easy to perform PC and data acquisitions communications. Finally, I really like scripts languages like Pearl to perform a variety of tasks in a fast way.

On project management, I use PMBoK techniques to help me to be handful in almost every project segment. Also, I like the Extreme Programming ideas and the feelings of project control that some basic statistical techniques give me to deal with aggressive deadlines.
