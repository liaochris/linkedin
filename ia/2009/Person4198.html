Adrien Marseille’s Summary

"After 25 years in kitchens on every continent, one thing I know for sure is that the best way to understand a culture is the way in which they celebrate the basic human need for food." - Adrien Marseille

Like all true bon vivants, Marseille makes a sense of epicurean discovery a priority wherever he goes—which explains his extensive expertise on global cuisine and an innovative flare for indigenous tastes and textures available only to those who have visited and experienced regional fare first-hand.
