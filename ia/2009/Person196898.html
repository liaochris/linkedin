Ryan Griffin’s Summary

Ryan currently oversees all brands within the Mars Snack/Candy business, responsible for their complete strategic representation and tactical rendering within all digital environs (traditional online, search, video, content distribution, digital OOH, mobile, etc.).

Prior to his current post, Griffin served in various capacities at Starcom Mediavest Group, mOne Worldwide (now Neo@OgilvyOne) and MPG Media Contacts. His career client list is marked by a number of blue-chip global brands, inclusive of Mars, American Express, Coca-Cola, IBM, Cisco, and ING.

Griffin has been named one of the industry's Rising Stars by both OMMA and Media Magazine, with his work winning recognition from the OMMA Awards for Online Advertising Creativity, IAB's MIXX awards, and Media Magazine's Creative Media Awards. Past honors include a "Media Plan of the Year" award by Adweek, a shortlisting at Cannes, and a "Best in Show" designation at ad:tech.

Outside of work, Ryan's passions include: taking full advantage of the NY music scene, embracing the new via travel, and making time to make things.

(He also thinks that the latter should not be so difficult...)
