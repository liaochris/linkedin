Robert Hudson’s Summary

Accomplished professional with over 20 years of experience in product line management, emerging market assessment, offer strategy development, user-centered design research, and online/offline marketing resulting in award-winning, market-leading offers.

Unique, extensive expertise in user-centered design spans hardware, software and Internet services for emerging markets providing strategic advantage for companies pursuing innovative market opportunities.

Proven leadership in high profile projects at large multi-nationals, a high growth, mid-sized market leader, and an award-winning start up venture, provides a solid managerial base to build and inspire new teams or grow existing ones.

Robert Hudson’s Specialties:

online media & marketing, user-centered design, marketing, public relations, branding, product management, planning, management, team building, early stage product/services, product line management
