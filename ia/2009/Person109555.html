Jennifer Graf’s Summary

For the past nine years, I have been working in the fashion industry as a designer and have also had the opportunity to use my creative talent in other areas; such as photo styling, retail window/visual displays, jewelry design and costume design but focus predominantly in designing for the youthful and trendy fashion market.

After graduating Magna Cum Laude from the Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT) in 2000, I embarked on my career as an apparel designer. I have worked for companies like, Timberland, Pony, Rocawear, G-Unit and 8732, and many of my designs were featured on television, music videos and magazine editorials. While designing for Rocawear, I was honored when the collection was awarded "Best Athletic clothing line" by Sportswear International.

While continuing my career in the fashion design world, I have been given numerous opportunities to branch my creative skills to other facets; such as costume design, photo styling, working "behind the scenes" for several fashion shows and jewelry design. One exciting opportunity I had was to be part of the costume design team for an Off Broadway play, "Marriage of a Figaro". The New York Times review stated "… festive costumes alone are enough to make you smile" and The New Yorker, "elegant costumes set the tone for the production right away".

I recently moved back to the USA from London, where I look forward to experiencing new and exciting challenges. Please feel free to contact me with career or freelance opportunities.
