Sudhir Sohoni’s Summary

Over the past 29 years I have worked across Strategic, Task and Operational Levels in FMCG, Consumer Durables, Widely Diversified Group and now in Energy & Environment. Of the 29 years, I have spent almost equal number of years in IR, HR & General Management.

In the course of my journey have restructured Manufacturing Facilities, extensive downsizing, concluded productivity linked wage settlements in multi union multi location scenarios, evolved Culturebased HR Strategy, extensive work in Change Management using OB & OD Interventions and Operational Excellence.

I have enjoyed every moment of my work life and never ever experienced any boredom todate. Also in all my assignments have eventually developed a very competent team of professionals with whom I still have contact at personal and professional level.

My Goal is to create an organization where every individual employee can reach his / her full potential who continously contribute to the business through quantifiable outcomes.

Sudhir Sohoni’s Specialties:

My current passion is to drive the Culturebased HR Strategy in Thermax and contribute to through Operational Excellence to the 16 different businesses so as to create a high performance culture which is sustainable and robust whcih can withstand all types of business cycles.
