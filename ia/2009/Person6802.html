Michael Alcock’s Summary

An executive with more than 10 years experience in entrepreneurial environments for Entertainment, Ecommerce and Education. Expertise in new product ventures, strategic business development and leading-edge applications architecture, including experience designing and launching the first e-commerce sites for Disney.com and most previously as VP Technology & Operations for a 70 sized team at a $150 million division of Laureate Education. My strengths are as a communicator and as a product developer for new markets.
