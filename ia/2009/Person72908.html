Emily Stoddard Furrow’s Summary

As a partner and communications consultant at DVQ Studio, I help organizations learn, create, and connect. I began writing for a media start-up as a teenager and have worked in communications ever since, serving each sector and organizations large and small.

My love of communications does not come from the work itself – it comes from the power communications can have. I believe strategic communications can improve how people and organizations work, deepen organizational knowledge and networks, and lead to real community or industry change.

For these reasons, I help organizations effectively use communication to:

- Learn about ideas within and outside their organization.
- Create things that help opportunities be understood and pursued.
- Connect people to advance their work and make meaningful change.

In addition to my work, I support the following organizations as a volunteer: League of Women Voters, Michigan Women's Commission, Blandford Nature Center, and Michigan Give Camp.
