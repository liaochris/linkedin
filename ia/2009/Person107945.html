Jeffery E. Lay, CMT’s Interests:

Family, golf, aviation, & cutting edge technologies. As a survivor of Hodgkin's Lymphoma, I am passionate about cancer, health and wellness issues. Diagnosed in 1998 at the age of 33, I was told I'd never fly again and not father children. Opting for an experimental treatment, my faith was rewarded - I returned to the cockpit a record 9 months later, and we welcomed my daughter's birth the following summer. I have dedicated my life to creating The Children of Aviation Foundation. Through our commitment to building into the lives of others, we will create flying squadrons in major cities throughout the United States. We will provide a cutting edge learning environment manned with aerobatic aircraft, pilots and mentors of the highest caliber. We will give wings to the dreams of economically disadvantaged children so that they may achieve flight and higher education. Contact me personally to learn how you can make a difference in the lives of others.
