Meghan Murphy’s Summary

My work experience has given me a valuable and varied skill set which I continue to develop and grow. From public relations, marketing, planning and execution of game launch, event planning, convention logistics, online media planning and buying, advertising sales and business development.

My experience is mainly within the videogame industry, but I have previously dabbled in professional services, tech and non-profit areas. I currently work for the most cutting-edge Themis Media, publisher of the videogame industry and culture hotbed The Escapist (www.escapistmagazine.com).

I previous utilized viral marketing and social networks to promote game launch and expand online presence for clients. I am a avid Facebook and Twitter user, finding the most successful ways to grow a community through those mediums.
