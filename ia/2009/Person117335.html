John Geoghegan’s Summary

John Geoghegan is a Partner with Bialla & Associates, a Bay Area-based search firm specializing in retained search for executives at the VP and C-suite level. Some of the placements John has handled in the past 24 months include:

VP, Marketing for LeapFrog, a publicaly held, educational toy company with $500mm in annual revenue

President + Director of National Sales for Reactrix, a Mobius and Thomas Weisel-backed company in the out of home, interactive media space

Head of Strategy for AKQA, the leading interactive agency in the US

VP, Marketing for Take Two Interactive's 2K Sports, a video game publisher

CMO + VP, Business Development for Flock.com, the preferred browser for the social networking space

VP, Store Development + VP, Store Operations for QuickHealth, a Draper Fisher-backed company in the healthcare/retail space

Media Director for WestWayne, an advertising agency

Prior to joining Bialla, John served as VP, Global Sales & Marketing for LucasArts, the video game division of LucasFilm and a top 10 video game publisher, where he helped the company achieve record revenue and profits as well as launch four of the top 10 best selling video games of 2005.

John also served as CEO of CareGuide, a venture-backed, Internet, start-up in the heathcare space, which he helped position and build out including raising $20mm in two rounds of funding. CareGuide was acquired by a disease management company in 2002.

From 1989-1999 John managed offices for professional marketing service companies including WPP, IPG and Saatchi both in the US and abroad.

Specifically, John was GM at J. Walter Thompson’s flagship San Francisco office from 1994 to 1999 and President of Hill Holliday’s San Francisco office from 2002 to 2004. Additionally, John helped Saatchi with its international office expansion in Asia where he oversaw their Korean and Thailand offices as well as headed up their Procter & Gamble business in Japan.
