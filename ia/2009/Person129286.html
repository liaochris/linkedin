Kelli McLaughlin’s Summary

If you are looking for an experienced quality sales manager -you have come to the right place!

The sales function can be the most crucial asset to an organization. Let's think about that for a moment.... When a member of your sales organization engages a prospect or customer, they are representing your brand and providing an identity to your company. Face it-people buy from people.

Throughout my career, the teams I have had the privilege to be a part of have built solid relationships with clients on behalf of the organizations we have represented. We have done this through taking a truly customer focused approach to marketing programs – not just focused on the client, but focused on the client’s customer.

Advertising programs designed to drive demand are programs that get results. The more results we get for our advertisers, the closer the relationship becomes.

My management style is leadership based with a focus on accountability. I lead by example and always do what I say I will do. I am careful about the promises I make until I am certain they are promises that can be delivered.

If your organization can benefit from a collaborative, consultative sales and management approach, I am looking forward to hearing from you.
