Davis Janowski’s Summary

I'm currently the technology reporter for InvestmentNews, a national publication that's part of the Crain Communications Inc. business publishing empire.

Prior to joining Crain in July of 2007 I spent almost a decade covering many facets of technology for the nation's leading computer magazine. In my last position at PC Magazine I served as Lead Analyst for Web services and software and had the pleasure and daily challenge of keeping up with and reviewing the online services produced by the likes of Google, Microsoft, Skype, Yahoo! and many others. My experience over the years goes beyond this to include computer networking at both the consumer and business level, GPS technology, Voice over IP technology, and peer-to-peer technologies among others.

In addition to PC Magazine, my work has appeared in Digital Life, e-Business Essentials, Extreme Tech Magazine, the Pensacola News Journal, Rodale's Scuba Diving Magazine, the Syracuse Newspapers, and Tech Almanac 2004.

I’ve been a frequent contributor to Good Day New York, Gearlog Radio, and PC Mag Radio; and interviewed as an expert on BBC World News, Business Week TV, CNN International, Fox 5 News at 6 and 11, and NY1.

Prior to PC Magazine I was a medical editor and writer covering the rather technical field of Epidemiology and infectious diseases; my work was disseminated to both a professional and lay audience. During that time I received training at the Centers for Disease Control in Atlanta and was invited to make a presentation at the International Conference on Emerging Infectious Diseases, and was co-producer of a documentary on the history of Public Health in Florida.

Davis Janowski’s Interests:

I've spent 20 years as a dual NAUI-YMCA-certified scuba diver. During that time I've dived extensively in the Gulf of Mexico off the Florida Panhandle, as well as many of the rivers and springs in the region. I've served as a volunteer on the Emanuel Point shipwreck excavation and conservation project, as a volunteer supply diver on the Woodville Karst Plain Project, and even had the opportunity to visit and write about a week spent on the clear blue Aegean at the Bozburun shipwreck excavation in southwestern Turkey. I've also enjoyed paddling my Folbot sea kayak along the Hudson River, many of the lakes and rivers in the Adirondacks, the Gulf of Mexico, and off the coast of Southern California. I've solo backpacked extensively in the Adirondack High Peaks region, the southern Adirondacks as well as many week-long jaunts along the Appalachian Trail with a close friend. I'm a bushwhacker, GPS and geocaching enthusiast, orienteering enthusiast, canoeist, and Eagle Scout as well.
