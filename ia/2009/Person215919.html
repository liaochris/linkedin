Shawn McCammon’s Summary

Former In-house corporate attorney, now solo practitioner, focused on providing clients with practical and effective legal advice and business counseling through custom tailored pricing models.

One of the core competencies offered by Liberty Law is the outsourced general counsel service, which is typically offered on a flat fee monthly retainer basis. This pricing model allows clients to more accurately budget their monthly legal bills, and keeps the client in regular contact with their attorney for day-to-day advice, employment counseling and other compliance guidance without worrying about generating legal bills when they contact their attorney.

Liberty Law also maintains an affiliation with a well respected Sacramento law firm, which allows Liberty Law to provide big firm support for client's larger projects or litigation matters. This relationship also provides Liberty's clients with access to reduced fees not available to the general public in Sacramento.

Shawn McCammon spends time working on the following matters: Contract review and drafting, employment advice and counseling, regulatory guidance, lease review and negotiation, litigation counsel management, corporate governance, entity formation, common interest development law (HOA law), elder abuse, estate planning, and other related matters.

Shawn McCammon’s Experience

  • Owner

    Liberty Law

    (Privately Held; Law Practice industry)

    August 2009 — Present (2 months)

    Liberty Law's mission is to provide its customers with practical and effective legal counseling in a manner that delivers real value in today's tough economy. For example, the firm's flat fee outsourced general counsel service, which is designed to deliver day-to-day legal advice, employment counseling, and other services normally provided by a full time staff attorney, while removing the fear of unknown billable hours at the end of the month often seen in more traditional firms. This way, the customer never worries about calling the attorney and generating additional billable hours. To further assist our customer's in stabilizing their monthly budgets, the firm also offers project and budget based services to limit your exposure to out of control legal bills. All of our custom tailored approaches are designed to help you maintain regulatory compliance and avoiding costly litigation.

    Another distinguishing characteristic of Liberty Law is its approach to dealing with incidental costs incurred in delivering legal services. Unlike many other firms, Liberty Law does not use ordinary copy charges, facsimile costs, or excessive mileage reimbursements as firm profit centers; instead, the company views these items as overhead in delivering quality legal services. We work to avoid the nickel-and-dime approach to recovering standard expenses.

    Finally, Liberty Law takes a new approach to technology. New technology now allows you to do things like share documents and collaborate with your attorney through an online client-connect feature that is built in to our unique case management system. You can also visit the firm's legal blog to keep up to date on the ever changing legal landscape. You can even pay your electronically delivered invoice through Paypal®.
