Angus Cormie’s Summary

A senior Internet and mobile telecoms executive, with a unique knowledge base crossing web and mobile digital media and convergence environments.

Currently manages consumer customer online experience and content at Dell Online - as Consumer Site Manager for EMEA.

Previous experience includes Managing Director of www.thedoghouse.co.uk, and boysstuff.co.uk, an online gift reminder and gift etailer, and an online gadget retailer.

Career developed in various strategic and operational focused roles, becoming part of ‘Leadership Team’ in O2. Management of O2’s web presence – a top 10 UK transaction-based web service – stitching together the digital media requirements for the various operational parts of O2 into a single consistent, coordinated approach. Specific experience in e-care, e-marketing, e-commerce, consumer web / intranet support management.

Identified opportunities across digital internet and new media services – and developed them in line with core company strategic objectives.

Style consists of entrepreneurial and creative / innovative spirit and maintaining high levels of passion and enthusiasm for initiatives.
