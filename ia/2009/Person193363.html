Ramon Chen’s Summary

Ramon has 20+ years experience designing, developing, managing and marketing technology products.

He has provided strong managerial leadership running marketing teams at large successful corporations as well as early stage startups. A former engineer with expertise in: RDBMs, knowledge management, data modeling, data integration, data management, data replication, archiving, application development and integration, with working practical knowledge of Web 2.0, SaaS, Cloud Computing, Security, Mobile Applications, Interactive Television, Business Service Management, Product Portfolio Management and more. Using an extensive marketing and technical background involving roles in product management, product marketing and corporate marketing, he has successfully defined, launched & grown market share for numerous enterprise software products ranging from development tools to application software. He prides himself on being a clear communicator & motivator and believes in empowering talented individuals while simultaneously fostering collaboration and team spirit. He has been a consistent hands on individual contributor through authoring of MRDs, PRDs, solution and product marketing white papers and web content. He has created & delivered presentations to a wide range of audiences including C-level prospects, analysts and press as well as at major industry events. He leverages a wide range of tools and utilities ranging from SaaS applications such as RightNow Technolgies, salesforce.com, MindManager and more, and is an expert in all MS Office suite tools.

He views marketing as an investment and as such should be held accountable for producing quantifiable, demonstrable results while supporting the business goals of the company. He holds himself to the highest ethics & accountability while working on behalf of investors and employees.
