Stephen Saunders’s Summary

PROFILE: Retail Leader and Marketing Strategist

General Manager of a leading international loyalty marketing company.


Business leader with strong strategic skills, 24 years’ management experience. Experience of business start-ups, growth and establishment of strategic relationships.

General management positions in retailing, franchising and management consulting. Previously General Manager: Books & More New Zealand Post, a leading NZ franchisor. Conducted feasibility study, was asked to set up and run the company. Grew it from zero to 19 stores, and profit for shareholders in less than 12 months. Shareholders’ loans repaid by month 18. Network had grown to 36 stores at end of three-year management contract.

Corporate strategist for two large South African retailing groups, both multi-billion dollar department store companies, Edgars and Woolworths (South Africa) and has also held bottom-line responsibility for a chain of fashion stores, and franchise companies.

Wide experience of retail through consulting to leading NZ retailers on business strategies, growth plans, start-ups and operational improvements. Knowledge of many categories: pharmacy, apparel, cosmetics, books and stationery, housewares, sporting goods, appliances, consumer electronics.

Hands-on retail leadership experience: run a chain of fashion stores ($28m turnover, 120 staff) and a franchised chain of 36 integrated book, stationery and postal stores (turnover $ 40m+ significant postal revenues, 15 staff, 36 franchise partners).

List of NZ retail consulting clients has included: Briscoe Group (Briscoes and Rebel Sport), Life Pharmacy, Pharmacy Brands (Unichem Pharmacy), Whitcoulls, London Bookshops, New Zealand Post, the former Pacific Retail Group (Noel Leeming, Bond & Bond), Progressive Enterprises, Crane Distribution (Mico Plumbing, Mastertrade, Corys)

Doctor of Commerce degree specializing in Industrial Psychology, Consumer Behaviour.
