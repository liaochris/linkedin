Greg Cole’s Summary

Motivational, yet pragmatic leader and entrepreneur with 16 years of exceptional management experience. Strategist and leader in the transformation of a church struggling for survival to a dynamic, thriving organization with a well-deserved reputation for community outreach, a young appeal, a first-class school, and a global perspective.

Unique combination of experience in the profit sector, establishing and growing a start-up business. A proven record of accomplishment in mission fulfillment, entrepreneurial creativity, strategic thinking, organizational development, fund raising, and program execution — planning, developing, and leading programs and organizations to measurable success.

Exceptional communicator, with the ability to create a unifying vision from diverse perspectives, functional groups, socio-economic communities, and personalities.

Greg Cole’s Experience

  • Rector - Executive Director

    Emmanuel Church, Newport, RI

    (Non-Profit; Religious Institutions industry)

    October 1995 — May 2008 (12 years 8 months)

    Led operations, programs, and facilities as executive director, development officer, and spiritual leader of church, pre-k to kindergarten day school, and outreach ministries. Developed and directed team of 10 full and part-time employees, while providing leadership to 400 (approximate) church members and broader community.

    Directed combined operating and capital budgets of $575,000, $1 million endowment, and fundraising and pledge programs supporting historic facility (built 1902, National Register of Historic Places) and total operations.
    • Accomplished institutional growth in resource-scarce environment, driving 200+% increase in operating budget. Increased annual member giving by average 6%, while identifying and instituting alternative income sources that ultimately provided 20% of total income.
    • Successfully implemented financial development plan, including soliciting and receiving numerous gifts in mid-to-high five figures from individuals, foundations, corporations.

  • Co-Owner, Operations Director

    Cole Apparel Group, Ltd

    (Retail industry)

    2004 — April 2008 (4 years)

    Established women’s specialty boutique business in Nantucket to exploit opportunities selling “better-to-bridge” clothing lines and accessories in popular tourist destination. Charted and steered start-up strategy and infrastructure, driving sustainable growth through long-term strategic planning and tactical operations management. Developed and directed all financial and operations systems and procedures including budget preparation and control, forecasting, reporting, cash management, payroll, inventory, purchasing, point of sales systems, shipping, and state and municipal licensing.

    • Piloted business through challenging start-up years, driving steady growth and increasing revenues by 140% — to $854,000 in 2007.
    • Launched second location in Barrington, Rhode Island. Achieved first year revenue of $359,000 funneling business model expertise gained in start-up to second location operations.
    • Planned, instituted, and manage an efficient computer / network infrastructure

  • Associate Rector

    (Religious Institutions industry)

    June 1992 — October 1995 (3 years 5 months)

    Directed educational, outreach, spiritual, and general operations programs for one of the largest Episcopal congregations in New Jersey and the Diocese of Newark. Served a diverse membership and community of varied ages and socio-economic backgrounds.
