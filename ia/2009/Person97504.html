Jeroen Schouten’s Summary

I am an ICT Lawyer with 11 years of experience in a renowned Dutch ICT law firm (Kennedy Van der Laan), an Internet Service Provider and KPN (The Dutch national telecommunications company). I am currently the sole legal counsel for Google in The Benelux. My main expertise is in e-commerce, consumer protection legislation, privacy, legal interception, computer crimes, content related legislation, website development and exploitation and ISP liability.

The main point through which I am able to differentiate myself is my ability to easily understand the technical and business aspects of new products and services and to translate these aspects to any applicable legal requirements. My legal advice will always contain any practical steps my inhouse customer will need to take to ensure that his new product or service is compliant with applicable legal requirements.

I am a lawyer who likes new technology and the potential it has for society. One of my main challenges is to recon new and undiscovered legal grounds. My aim is therefore to continue to work in the ICT industry.

Jeroen Schouten’s Specialties:

My specialties include privacy law, ISP liability, e-commerce and telecommunications law.
