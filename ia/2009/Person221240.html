Serge Rielau’s Summary

Serge Rielau got interred into IBM's Toronto penal colony from the University of Kaiserslautern/Germany (aka K-Town) 12 years ago, having previously served a year's time at the Almaden Research Lab where he got into DB2 and his life took a turn for the worse (see "OO what have they done to DB2?!").
Over the years Serge has been guilty of many crimes including SQL Functions, generated columns and what's come to be known as the "new SQL": select from insert, update and delete!
No bad deed goes unpunished though. He is now serving time as DB2 for LUW's SQL Architect and labeled an STSM.
Not having learned a thing and obviously having become delusional he now seems to liken himself to a modern day Robin Hood.
In a truly despicable act he has not shied from masquerading DB2 as Oracle. Having sunk that low he is launching raids across the the seven seas stealing from the rich buccaneer Larry E. and giving to the poor Sam P.
His hideout is rumored to be somewhere in usenet, possibly comp.databases.ibm-db2.
