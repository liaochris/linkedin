Jonathan Doyle’s Summary

dbox’s varied expertise allows an informed perspective into each aspect of a project. dbox was founded by architects who understand architecture. Over the last fifteen years, we have seamlessly introduced art, branding, photography, advertising, illustration, animation and graphic design into the studio. dbox has studios in New York, Amsterdam and Taipei.
