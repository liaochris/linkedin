Robson Mendonça’s Summary

I'm a Computer Science Student at Unisinos - Rio Grande do Sul - Brazil, and work since 2001 at LEC (Cognitive Studies Lab) under Professor Léa Fagundes orientation. I started work at LEC during my High School course, when I was studied Industrial Computer Technics at Senai(National Industrial Learning Service). In my time working at LEC, it was possible to learn many technologies and much about Free Software development, witch I adopted as a life philosophy.

As a LEC member, I had to learn different things as educational computing, and how to develop self-guided applications, that bring a better experience of use to people, in addition to working as tutor in some cases of use. These experiences bring me a better “how to get a point” understanding. What became me a better mentor and team manager and guide me to CTO position.

I'm a big enthusiastic of AJAX and Web 2.0 development. I have professional experience with Django since end of 2007, building the new version of AMADIS 2.0.
Actually I became an active member in the Brazilian Django community, soon, International too. For now I'm contributing with translation of the Django documentation to Brazilian portuguese.

Know more about me in my website: http://www.myweboncreate.com

Robson Mendonça’s Specialties:

My main development knowhow involve some technologies like PHP5, Python, Django, Ruby on Rails, SQL, XHTML, CSS, JavaScript and Flash(ActionScript), UI design (Gimp or Photoshop) and Database Design.

My career is divided in two parts: working directly with code, mainly with websites construction and Drupal customization (themes and modules), and project management. But, particularly I like more of development, so, I always worked as a programmer and manager at same time.
