Daniel MacKenzie’s Summary

Global executive and published marketeer with leadership experience in interactive media, subscription television, telecom, cable and network television operations, luxury consumer goods and international trade and export. Employs a "Concierge" approach to the business practice and specializes in tying the consumer's experience to the brand. Highly effective in B2B representation, politically savvy and able to advocate at the highest levels. Thorough understanding of the end-to-end distribution process including supply chain management and developing sync'd after-market solutions. Works in multiple industries assisting organizations seeking a proven record of success and substantive outcomes in the establishment and development of a North American business presence and expansion globally.

Three time award recipient, acknowledged for excellence in marketing and communication planning. Background includes multi-lingual proficiency and contribution in 17 countries and cultures with a specialized expertise in North American, Western, Central and Mediterranean European Markets, Australia and the Southern Caribbean Basin.

Adept at understanding and integrating the politics of business. Extensive experience in client relations and affiliate management. Maintains senior level global contacts within several governmental agencies in trade and export. Proven success in opening new territories, growing existing markets, and maximizing ROI in fluid domestic and international environments.
