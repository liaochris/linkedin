Kin Ho’s Summary

Started in recruitment upon graduation recruiting for SME's in the UK Engineering and technical field. Was then moved to help spearhead a specialist recruitment division working with the UK rail industry to recruit engineers. This role evolved into Account Management with a number of MNC's.
Moved to London to an executive search firm working in the rail and construction industry before moving sideways into recruiting specialist staff for the UK public sector where I helped set up and develop a new team of recruiters in London.

Moved to Hong Kong in 2007 and joined Hudson to develop the Industrial and Operations team in Hong Kong and South China focussing on Senior Level recruitment including Executive Search.

Kin Ho’s Specialties:

Permanant and contract recruitment in the following industries, construction, railways, public sector, manufacturing, logistics and supply chain, pharmaceutcal.
