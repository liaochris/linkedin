Mona Thorpe’s Summary

As the founder and CEO of a company that assists small business owners, I conduct and facilitate seminars, conferences and expos geared for mastery in entrepreneurial endeavors. For more than twenty years my company has produced programs and events that empower people to start-up, grow and develop businesses beyond their expectations.
I am a contributing author of a book on success strategies, 'Conversations on Success'. Volume 6. I co-authored the book with Brian Tracy and 15 other success strategists to provide information to people seeking to become more effective in achieving their goals.

My company has a powerful event coming up in the fall of this year geared for small business owners. It is the 2nd semi-annual SBM Small Business Conference and Expo targeted to be held at the Hilton Hotel in Stamford, Connecticut. We have sponsorship availabilities and vendor positions with many levels of participation to choose from. These sponsorship positions offer great exposure to many companies looking for services and products to build their businesses. Sponsors will be included in our major print, cable TV, and radio advertising, email and web campaigns. Contact us to secure a position in the event.

I have been facilitating events and seminars for more than twenty years. You can view video testimonials at www.smartbusinessmoves.us/video.php. Feel free to contact my company with any question about enrollment in our seminars or other training and development programs, sponsorship positions, membership, web or email advertising
campaigns or just to network!
