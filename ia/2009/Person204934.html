Ryan Thousand’s Summary

Has successfully built, tested and implemented multiple clients on FirstNet, PowerNote ED and PrescriptionWriter solutions, while also learning to key integration points between all the solutions.

Successfully headed a Pilot to introduce and demo PowerNote *2G and PCEV to a providers in multiple clinic locations and multiple specialties.

Completed hardware testing and recommendations from a clinic standpoint with PowerNote integration. Worked to measure our overall changes in template usage with tablets and went forward with info for funding for more hardware.

Solution knowledge in PowerOrders, PowerNote, CareNet, CORE Security, SQL, and Millennium table structures
