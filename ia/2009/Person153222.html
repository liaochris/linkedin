Michael Arrivas’s Summary

I currently supervise a dynamic and fast moving engineering group at Cox Communications - Las Vegas, responsible for integrating and supporting Video on Demand systems into the Hotels & Casinos. Additionally, we deploy data services at some sites where the system is cable modem based. My group also provides technical support to our outlying markets in Phoenix, Indiana, Mississippi, Atlantic City, and Oklahoma.

My position utilizes skills honed over a span of 18 years; Semiconductor, Telecom, & Audio/Video. They consist of test & measurement, scripting, project management, research & analysis, formal training, technical sales, and leadership.

At Cox, the 3rd largest MSO (behind Comcast and Time Warner), I am fortunate to be able to work with so many different technologies and have the ability to wear many hats. Our group strives in being the best, rightly so in a town that never sleeps and our systems must remain operational 24x7.
