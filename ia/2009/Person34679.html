Bruce Fieggen’s Summary

I'm a Project Management guru. I take people's complex ideas and resolve them into a set of finite tasks that can be sequenced, assigned to the right people and accomplished with minimum frustration. In that way I can take something as complex as bringing a new drug or medical device from conception to the marketplace while staying on schedule and within budget.

I also enjoy teaching these techniques to people and have had great success with my 3 day Cadence Project Management Training course.
