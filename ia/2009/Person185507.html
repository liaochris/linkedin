Peter Donohue’s Summary

Marketing professional with broad tactical and strategic experience in marketing for both start-ups and for Fortune 500 companies.

Currently running Expand Abroad Consulting, a marketing and business management consultancy specializing in:
- Start-up Development - we help an entrepreneur take something from an idea to becoming a viable company. Business formation, strategy, how to market, IP protection, etc. are all activities we can be involved with.
- New Country Entry - whether you are an American firm looking to expand into a new country, or a foreign firm looking to enter the United States, we can help. Setting the strategy on how best to enter the market, finding local partners (if needed), marketing, etc. are all part of what we can do for you. Focus recently has been with Japanese companies entering the US.

Previously, founded helpUhire Solutions (a resume pre-screening company) and was marketing manager/product manager with Tyco Thermal Controls. I also have taught global management classes for the MBA program of University of Phoenix.

Personal web page and Blog is at http://www.singlespeed.org.
