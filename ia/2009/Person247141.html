Dave Wieneke’s Summary

Dynamic marketing executive, thought leader, speaker, writer, teacher and recognized expert on online marketing, branding and promotions. Reputation for merging vision, technical expertise and creativity with project management strengths. Change agent, leader and team builder skilled in gaining consensus across all levels including C-level, executive boards and other stakeholders.
