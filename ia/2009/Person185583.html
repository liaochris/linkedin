Peter Fulda’s Summary

A project engineer with 20 years of automotive experience developing mechanical systems and components in switches and electronics modules. Knowledgeable in all aspects of taking programs from concept/quote phase to production launch. A highly responsible and service oriented team player with the ability to quickly acquire new skills and knowledge.

Fluent in German, limited conversational in Spanish and French.

Presently searching for a new challenge engaged in product development, continuous improvement, cost reduction and problem resolution activities.
