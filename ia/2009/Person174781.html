Nicolas Snieg’s Summary

Currently I am a LevelDesigner at Ankama Games.

I am a French student coming from SupInfoGame (a school of Gamedesign and control of production based at Valenciennes, France).

I was Gamedesigner, Leveldesigner and Lead artist on our school-leaving project: an RTS in a Shoot'them Up universe, on Nintendo DS.

I have worked on the sequel of "The Saga of Ryzom" : "The ring", during an internship of 4 month in Nevrax.

I have worked on DarkMessiah Xbox360 at ubisoft Annecy.

In addition, I create and build some maps on Electron, for NeverWinter Nights 2.
I have also worked on a mmorpg (amateur), for 2 years. I have designed full documentation of the GameDesign, and the beginning of the LevelDesign.

My goals are to bring something into the games, and to do good work.

For my resume, my portfolio and some screenshots, see http://cerberazur.googlepages.com/
