David Park’s Summary

A web and mobile executive with more than 20 years of experience in the technology industry working for global companies including Netscape, Visa, AOL, Coopers & Lybrand, and United States Postal Service.

- Experience working across highly matrixed organizations with diverse employees.

- Broad background in business development, product management, mobile & online product strategy, systems integration, and management consulting.
