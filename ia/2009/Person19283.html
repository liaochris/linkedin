Martin Arendts’s Summary

ARENDTS ANWÄLTE offers comprehensive legal counselling in the field of sports betting and gaming law.
Our clients include many foreign companies who are already active or are trying to become active in the German gambling market. Attending to our clients on this difficult path, we develop entry strategies to the German gambling market.
Sound knowledge of this extremely dynamic field of law, which unites Community law, civil law, criminal law, trademark law and administrative law, forms the basis for our effective legal counselling and, where necessary, subsequent court trial.
Amongst others we fought for the so called bet-at-home-decision of the District Court of Munich I, which was delivered some days before the ECJ's famous Gambelli Decision.
