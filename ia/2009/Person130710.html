Kevin Bates’s Summary

Hello, my name is Kevin Bates. I have many years experience in Web Analytics, SEO, SEM, Pay Per Click, and Email Marketing. I have a solid history of building relationships and taking care of my customers, as well as prospecting for new ones.

Throughout all the positions I've held in the Internet, Software, and Animation industries they've always had a common responsibility; taking extremely technical situations and explaining them to customers in a way that made sense and helped them accomplish their goals.

Applications these days can be extremely difficult to setup, optimize, and use efficiently. I'm the guy who can translate the technicalities into what needs to happen to solve a problem, be that training, proper setup, or fixing a bug in the code.
