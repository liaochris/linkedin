Tom Kiernan’s Summary

After a tour in Vietnam in 1969-1970 ,Tom joined Citibank in New York, got transferred to Venezuela where he ran the asset based financing activities throughout northern South America and the Caribbean. He left in 1977 to run a local sales finance company for 2 years, and departed that position to enter his own businesses as an entrepreneur until returning to the USA in 1985. Tucson became home as he purchased several local businesses over the next 8 years, enjoying the buying and selling more than the operating. In 1993 he obtained an Arizona real estate license and began the present career of business brokerage, having sold over 200 businesses of sizes from $100k to several million dollars in value.

Tom Kiernan’s Specialties:

Assisting buyers and sellers of businesses to accomplish their goals quickly and confidentially.
