Edward Sliwinski’s Summary

The key goal of Edward Sliwinski is to contribute on a strategic level to the medical nutrition business by enabling the launch of unique innovations that really make a difference in the lives of our patients.
Next desired career step is a position as program manager which will enable him to further expand his responsibilities following on the development as a project manager and to be the true counterpart from R&D with marketing.
Edward Sliwinski has a background in Physical Chemistry of foods and Dairy technology.

Edward Sliwinski’s Specialties:

Management of innovation projects from idea to launch having the quality of life of our patients and the business perspective in mind continously and thus working in close cooperation with marketing.
Main areas of expertise are: dysphagia, powders, condensed sip feeds.
