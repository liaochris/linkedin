Ann Steckel’s Summary

Faculty Training, Support, & Consulting:
Provide faculty training, technical computer support and instructional design consulting. Assist faculty in developing and integrating applications into their courses and the curriculum using the Web, multimedia, and Blackboard VISTA learning management system.

Distance Learning Support:
Consult faculty teaching students online in best practices for online learning materials. Assist faculty in preparing their course materials for Vista and HorizonWimba environment. Collaborate with other units (DLT, STCP, RCE) in supporting distance learning students and operations.

Teamwork and Special Projects
Collaborate as a team member with Academic Technologies and campus staff supporting and integrating technology wisely into course curriculum and university projects, including distributed learning, Web services, classroom technologies, media production, and graphics.

Previously employed for eight years as the District Technology Coordinator for Warren County Technical School and Educational Technologist for WARRENnet, a consortium spearheaded by Warren County Technical School that provides Internet access, technical support and services, staff training, and E-mail to 20 educational institutions and 11 educational, municipal governments / departments and non-profit organizations located throughout New Jersey and Pennsylvania
