Camille Cerria’s Summary

Assist clients with the planning of parties & events aboard yachts that sail from NJ, NY, & CT.

With over 35 vessels in our fleet, there's something for everyone who is seeking a venue that is unique, festive, and memorable.

Clients include NJ State Troopers, Tyco, Bayer, Bovis Lend Lease, Merrill Lynch, BNP Paribas, Zenith, American Express, Columbia University, Cornell College, NJAWBO, as well as celebrity events.

Dining, dancing, & sightseeing while sailing is a novel way to celebrate!

Camille Cerria’s Specialties:

What I specialize in depends on your point of view.
- Companies believe I specialize in corporate events.
- Non-profits believe I specialize in fundraisers.
- Schools believe I specialize in reunions, project graduations, and proms.
- Brides believe I specialize in weddings, showers, and rehearsal dinners.
- Others believe I specialize in anniversaries, birthdays, retirement parties, sweet 16s, and bar/bat mitzvahs.
