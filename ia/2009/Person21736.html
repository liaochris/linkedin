Asokan Thiyagarajan’s Summary

Ashok has over 19 years of senior technology management experience in brand name high-tech companies like Motorola, Nokia, Ericsson and Compaq (HP). Ashok has worked for start-ups as well. He has also been an entrepreneur and co-founded of a technology/services company. He presently also serves on the board of directors/advisors of some technology start-ups.

Ashok currently serves as a technology evangelist at a major mobile phone manufacturer responsible for the developer ecosystem. He is a respected speaker at international conferences on technology. He authors technology and visionary articles for leading magazines around the world. As an evangelist, he is responsible for recommending strategies to address market and technology trends as well as strategic alliances with key developers.

Ashok has skills blended in technology, business, engineering and management, and he is very effective in “Technology Management”, in addition to understanding and deploying the technology trends to suit business needs. Ashok has been involved in technology planning and driving of new strategic initiatives in multiple organisations. He is uniquely equipped with blended knowledge needed to lead companies to create company strategies and achieving their vision. He has many years of experience in International markets and travels around the globe.
