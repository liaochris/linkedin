Akash Trivedi’s Summary

Akash Trivedi is an aspiring entrepreneur who graduated with a dual degree in Finance and Management from The Wharton School in 2007. To date, his work experiences have centered primarily around cash flow management and customer acquisition. First as a summer analyst at MarchEx and Johnson & Johnson in 2005 and 2006 respectively, and more recently as the co-founder of "The Freight Exchange" and the business controller of a DTC genetic-testing venture called, "Smart Genetics."

Having gained an intuition for managing cash flow, Akash is now hoping to take the next logical step and find a position that enables him to develop and implement winning digital marketing strategies: either as a jack of all trades at another start up, an analyst at a consulting firm that specializes in high-growth companies, or an associate at a digital ad shop.
