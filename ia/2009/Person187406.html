Phil Tolliday’s Education

  • The University of Birmingham

    BA Hons , Media, Culture and Society , 2001 — 2004

    Specialised exploration of cultural media issues within contemporary society including their historical formation and future development. These include 'mass' media and other forms of cultural production. The degree also looked at social groups and social divisions, the cultural and political forms which they generate; and the ways we form meanings, prejudices, stereotypes, values and identities around and about them. An examination of the role of cultural institutions (for example, the BBC); various forms of culture, for example, high culture, mass culture, popular culture, women's culture, black culture; youth and other subcultures (e.g Club Culture) and cultural production, acknowledging the role of both media producers, media owners and cultural industries.

    Modules included:
