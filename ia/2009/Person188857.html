Andrea Mitchell’s Summary

A veteran of the entertainment industry, Andrea’s experience is second to none when it comes to content creation and production execution. Starting first as a production assistant in Boston in 1994, she arrived in Hollywood in 95 and worked for 7 years as an assistant director on many top feature films and Academy Award winning films with companies such as Miramax, Paramount, DreamWorks, Warner Brothers Sony, and Lions Gate Films. The roster of all-star directors she has worked with includes James Cameron, David Fincher, and James Mangold. In 1997, Andrea participated in Dot Com roundtables and brainstorms with young talents who were on the brink of Internet streaming video. Her vision: The Internet will be the next cable television! – and, then there was the 2000 crash.

In 2000, Andrea left LA for a series of producing jobs in NYC. She has resided in NYC ever since. It was in The Big Apple where the culmination of her experiences started to form plans for a new venture in video branded online and mobile podcasting. Then, when on a trip to Cambodia in 2005, she witnessed a monk captivated by his cell phone; and, upon questioning his intensity for his phone, she was informed that he was, “Getting all his news and information”. Eureka! That was it – the formation of a company that blends compelling information in an entertaining fashion, with ubiquitous reach, and delivery of the “infotainment” worldwide via an online and mobile platform. Andrea took this from idea to execution, and from this podcastGO was born.

3 ½ years later, the company continues to grow exponentially while remaining true to the mission of delivering easy accessibility to infotainment and experts. Currently, Andrea serves as Founder /Executive Content Creator of podcastGO. Andrea currently serves as the active CEO of the company
