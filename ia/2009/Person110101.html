JennyLee Herrera’s Summary

Currently, I work at Burson-Marsteller supporting the Miami Public Affairs practice and servicing our clients. I am the executive assistant to Mr. Ramiro Prudencio, Managing Director and Market Leader as well as Mr. Santiago Fittipaldi, Director.

Formerly, I worked with Toyota Tsusho America, coordinating corporate events for the Caribbean and Latin American markets and supporting the Toyota regional office.

Born in Miami, Florida by way of Cuban parents, I take pride in my community by becoming involved in through volunteer opportunities and supporting local emerging talent.

In 2002, I held the Miami-Dade County Organizer position for the Rock the Vote! initiative, I have also participated in Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce’s leadership training program, Leadership Miami, and have volunteered extensively through Hands on Miami and the National Multiple Sclerosis Society.

I have also worked with Univision’s Premios Lo Nuestro as a stage assistant and talent relations and with MTV Video Music Awards (Miami) coordinating casted audience participants. Currently, I juggle other projects ranging from promoting Miami local artists and coordinating the Miami annual poetry event/art exhibition, SWAN (Support Women Artists Now) Showcase.
