Andrea Dale’s Summary

Are you a business, life or executive coach, or a fitness trainer who is struggling or overwhelmed by marketing and sales? If so, let me help you create and implement authentic, effective *and enjoyable* ways to recruit new clients.

I specialize in helping personal development sole proprietors and businesses with their marketing. This includes fitness trainers and fitness facility owners, and all types of coaches including business, life, executive, cross cultural, etc.

I have more than 15 years of marketing experience in a wide range of industries including business coaching, textbook publishing, and CPA services. I am also a professional singer/songwriter (with two CDs). As such I bring a high level of creativity and focus to your web sites, marketing messages and copy.

Save your precious time and hard-earned money. Avoid endless frustration. Turn what is often a chore (your sales efforts), into powerful processes and actions resonating with who you are.

* Gain clients & increase referrals by discovering your ideal client.
* Save time and get results with decisive, effective marketing decisionmaking.
* Collaborate with me to create web site content and marketing strategies targeting who you most want as clients, that also personify who you are, and the results you offer.
