Boris Rudnik’s Summary

I’m a user experience (UX) specialist with solid academic background in Behavioral Sciences (B.A.) and Human Factors Engineering (M.Sc. toward its completion), combined with the substantial hands-on experience in human-computer interaction design (IxD) and user interface (UI) prototyping. I’m a strategic thinker, high premium on providing effective solutions that balance technological constraints, market demands, consumer organization business needs and product user requirements. I aim to ensure pleasant, productive, trustworthy and safe user experience of a brand by active involvement in various phases of a product lifecycle, from high-level requirements gathering and analysis, to interaction and visual design, to graphic design and branding, to mocking up design solutions, to implementation, and finally to quality assurance and usability evaluation. Strong communication skills enable me to effectively negotiate UX issues among multiple inbound stakeholders.

Boris Rudnik’s Specialties:

Requirements analysis, information architecture through cards sort technique, interaction design (IxD), user interface (UI) prototyping, heuristic usability evaluation, web and software product accessibility, internationalization and localization, usability standard ISO-9241, semantic web principles, w3c recommendations, and more...
