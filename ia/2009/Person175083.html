Nicole Williams, PHR’s Summary

Resourceful, goal-oriented HR professional with 8 years generalist experience including process improvement, business partner relationships, administration, and programs in union and non-union environments. Nicole is a detail- oriented planner who leads with a flexible management style that contains integrity, fairness, work/life balance, and results. Dynamic trainer with excellent internal/external relationship building and coaching skills. Nicole solves problems using creative, innovative, and diverse thinking that is in alignment with associate development as well as company goals.

Nicole has a Master in Business Administration in General Studies from Winthrop University in South Carolina, a Bachelor of Science in Recording Industry from Middle Tennessee State University in Tennessee, and Associate of Science in Broadcasting and is PHR certified.
