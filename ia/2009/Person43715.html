Chris McTiernan’s Summary

Chris McTiernan is a product development and business strategy professional with 12 years experience helping premier brands adapt to and compete on the web. He has led teams of designers, engineers and business stakeholders to plan and develop solutions for many clients, including eBay, Toyota, Visa, Verizon, Juicy Couture, Orbitz, and Wells Fargo.

- Successful record leading teams to create web-based technology solutions, including commerce sites, marketing sites, corporate intranets, customer portals and content management systems.

- Substantial experience in managing premier client relationships.

- Experience across multiple verticals: high tech, retail, automotive, healthcare, entertainment, financial services.
