Bilal M. Husain’s Summary

Bilal Husain has recently joined Specialized European Executive Management Advisers (SEEMA) in the capacity of Chief Commercial Officer (CCO) company wide and Country Manager for its operations in Saudi Arabia. Prior of taking this position, Bilal was the Director of eServices Projects for the Saudi eGovernment Program. Assuming this position in 2007, he represents Yesser at all government agencies, evaluating and approving funding requests, discussing and consulting with public agencies regarding their master IT and eGovernment action plans and strategies. He is also a key member in all steering committees for public agencies implementing eGovernment projects. At the forefront of ground-breaking technologies as well as innovative thinking, Mr. Husain is responsible for enabling all eGovernment projects related to transforming a public agency into an electronic agency by providing most of their services to consumers by electronic means.Performing duties as necessary to ensure successful implementation of e-Government Program. He was also responsible for e-Services Project's administrative tasks and supervises the work of program consultants. Providing strategic, visionary leadership and management, his aim is to achieve the main objectives of e-government: "providing better services". A very dynamic and forward-thinking professional. He embraces the tremendous challenges his position brings, particularly in implementing IS management, obtaining sufficient and relevant resources, communicating and collaborating internally and externally and managing change management. He is open-minded, innovative and a forward-thinker with highly developed diplomatic, verbal and negotiation skills. Committed to continuous improvement and fine tuning operations, Mr. Husain, passionate and determined, is confident that he has the right strategies, the resources and the infrastructure in place to efficiently and effective achieve the Government's eServices goals and objectives.
