Steve Hinde’s Summary

Resumé
I work as an Innovation Consultant within the Digital Arts sector , I also work as a trainer. After a successful track record for twenty-five years as a manager, researcher and innovation consultant within top international technology companies I started an exciting and successful second career as a freelance consultant two years a go. I would be delighted to bring my skills to your company or organization if you have an interesting problem that you need solving.



Recent consultancy experience


.• I am a ‘Lead Advisor’, advising on Organisational Development for the Arts Council (ACE). I am currently completing a successful ACE project with several Bristol based organizations including: the Watershed in Bristol, iShed The Architecture Centre, Spike Island, Picturethis and Arnolofini.
• I am offering consultancy to the Watershed together with Demos on developing a presence in the public space in Bristol.
• I run Innovation and Creativity workshops for New Media, Arts and Technology companies. The workshops are typically from half a day to a few days duration. I have recently run these workshops at: BT, Bristol City Council, Cornish Film, Channel 4, Aardman, 3CR, SW Screen and others.
• I offer core skills training to Bristol University students as part of their Student Development Unit, the courses I run include team building, presentation skills and creativity.
• I have worked as an associate at South-West Screen one of the regional screen agencies specialising in Innovation and New Media.
• I am an accredited de Bono trainer.
• I am an Open Space Technology workshop practitioner
• I have worked as an innovation advisor to Cornish Film
• I am a board member of the Encounters film festival, Bristol's international short film festival
