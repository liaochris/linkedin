    (Alternative Medicine industry)

GUSD incorporates stress control, an easy-to-follow exercise and nutrition program, and a daily dietary supplement. The GUSD program has been presented at 8 of the top nutrition science research conferences in the world – including the American College of Sports Medicine, Experimental Biology, the American College of Nutrition, and The Obesity Society.

    Wicked Fast Sports Nutrition

    (Alternative Medicine industry)

    January 2005 — Present (4 years 6 months)

Our principals bring together a wide range of experience, including a leading PhD-level lifestyle expert, a best-selling author, an Ironman triathlete, an elite-level rower, a University professor, and a team of leading business professionals. The Wicked Fast Team is focused on developing and delivering the most cutting-edge, highest quality, and efficacious natural products to consumers around the world.
