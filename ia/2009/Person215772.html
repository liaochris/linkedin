Scott "Shavoni" Parker’s Summary

Scott “Shavoni” Parker
For years, Scott “Shavoni” Parker has styled hits that have consistently sat atop the Pop, R&B, and CCM charts. In the past, Shavoni has been recognized by the entertainment industry as a pioneer of music, receiving numerous accolades, namely, “Producer of the Year,” and produced albums that have won NAACP image awards on six separate occasions, and the coveted Grammy Award for three different albums. Backed by record sales nearing 20 million units sold, Shavoni is focusing on creating, developing and aggregating various forms of content across the entertainment spectrum for broadcasting, streaming, and downloading to multiple formats.
