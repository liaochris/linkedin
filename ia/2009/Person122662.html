Joseph Stage’s Summary

Mr.Stage joined SyferLock Technology Corp. in May 2007 and is responsible for developing and managing SyferLock’s Sales, Channel Development and Parnerships/Alliances.

For the last 17 years, his experience has included IT consulting, IT sales management, direct sales, business strategy development, alliance development and new product development. Mr. Stage is a member of the Cambridge Whose Who in IT for 2007.

Prior to his current role, Mr. Stage was a Vice President of Gartner Consulting’s IT Management Market Solutions group, where he was responsible for scoping and crafting IT related solutions for client needs by applying methodologies and approaches in the areas of IT Optimization and Maximization, Performance Management and Business Value of IT. Many of his current engagements were in the area of security, risk and compliance and he worked mainly with Wall Street Financial Services firms on their current state readiness and helped to align them to their future state visions through a comprehensive IT strategy roadmap. He was also responsible for WW Alliances in Consulting, where he structured third party relationships with companies for additional solution value-add, brand reach, channel extension or product penetration into existing or new client accounts. Mr. Stage joined Gartner in 1995 through his departure in May 2007.

Prior to the Consulting role, Mr. Stage was a senior director in Gartner’s Corporate Business Development group. There, he was responsible for identifying investments in companies through mergers and acquisitions (M&A) or partnerships, as well as handling the due diligence related to the process from lead to close. In addition to M&A activity, he focused much of his time on alliances at a corporate level across all business units within Gartner.

He lives in Shelton, CT with his wife and 3 daughters and is a referee at both the college & high school levels for basketball and has a passion for the game of golf.
