David DiAntonio’s Summary

I have over 19 years of experience as a true supply chain professional with end to end supply chain experience in Warehousing and Logistics, Factory Planning, Production Management and Global Supply Chain Management, utilizing the Sales and Operations Planning process to increase revenue, customer service, forecast accuracy and reduce obsolescence for consumer product and publishing companies.

At Houghton Mifflin Harcourt, I have developed and executed a strategic business plan for supply chain including forecasting, project management, supply planning, sourcing and procurement. This year my team was able to successfully reduce customer backorders by over 60%, resulting in customer service improvements, revenue recognition, and an improved overall end to end supply chain process. Also, my team was able to reduce purchasing spend by 27%, in 2008, in the face of a 7% increase in material and transportation costs.

At Gillette, I was involved in a variety of roles/projects, including Assistant Manager of Warehousing and Logistics, Manager of Factory Planning and Logistics for North America and Manager of Oral B Global Supply Planning for North/South America. I have experience that includes supplier negotiations, working with union logistic providers, and leading large scale ERP implementations. My accomplishments have resulted in measurable results including inventory reductions and improved customer service levels.

Also at Gillette, I was one of two team leads that was charged with finding synergies between Oral-B and Crest when P&G purchased Gillette. My team was tasked with identifying and implementing cost savings, driving change, and focusing on network optimization after the purchase. I am well versed in planning, inventory, customer service, and is someone who is very driven and focused on results.

David DiAntonio’s Specialties:

A true leader in the Sales & Operations Planning process who understands the 5 critical elements - Demand Consensus Meeting, Forecast Pass, Supply and Procurement Planning Phase, Pre-S&OP Reviews and then the Executive Review. Also, experience in "Total Network Optimization" from suppliers to plant batching/production operations to global distribution centers to logistic solutions on through customer distribution networks utilizing software packages such as Optiant and LogicTools.
