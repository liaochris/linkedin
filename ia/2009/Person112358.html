Jon Hall’s Summary

Pragmatic, hands-on, delivery focused Enterprise Developer/Architect with a proven track record in a broad range of FTSE100 and NASDAQ companies.

Excellent communicator - regularly liaised with internal users and external clients.

Solid understanding of n-tier/client server (14 yrs): leading to robust, scalable and maintainable solutions.

Full life cycle: requirements, analysis, design, code, testing & deployment (good operational awareness).

Implemented numerous (35-40) projects (15 Java based) and many prototypes/POCs. Projects typically Greenfield. Very experienced in setting up new projects, mentoring, defining standards and introducing best practices.

Long-time proponent and user of Agile Developer best practices within agile and non-agile process/project environments.

Very good understanding of the challenges that scale-out architectures (traditional J2EE, stateless Spring POJO services and Compute Grids) place on back-end resources, such as databases and how they can be alleviated with products like Oracle Coherence (formerly Tangosol Coherence).
