Will Duggan’s Summary

Going green ain't going far or fast enough.

I provide clients with ways to anticipate and claim authentic sustainability leadership that translates into quantifiable improvements for the bottom line, the brand's reputation and society at large.

22 years in public affairs media design for clients who include American public broadcasting (PBS), Fortune 500 corporations, entertainment companies (e.g. Discovery, Turner), and strategic communications firms.

With Better Days, a 501(c)(3) organization, we create and facilitate the creation of media that inspire future-oriented thinking and large scale social change. With our diverse partners and projects, we help increase mainstream America's random collisions with better ways forward.

My colleagues and I are emissaries who work within the sustainability movement. We talk to and learn about a lot of organizations and their work, and we make connections among people who are thinking the same way. We suggest and nurture systemic coalitions — ways for groups to work effectively at the leverage points of cultural influence (advertising and marketing, news, entertainment, events.).

We work extensively with young leaders, believing that under-35s have the moral authority and cultural, economic, and political clout to affect great change and rebuild America's standing in the world.

Will Duggan’s Specialties:

Civic engagement & brand management in a world of regenerative capitalism, Environment, Sustainability Governance, Corporate Social Engagement, Millennial Development Goals, Global Reporting Initiative, Extensible Business Reporting Language, Cultural Simulation Modeling, Community Sustainability Indicators and hyperlocal news coverage.
