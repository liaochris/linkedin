Dr. Juan Manuel Roca [800+] juanmanuelroca@hotmail.com’s Summary

My name is Juan Manuel Roca and so far I've combined my professional career with my academic and research career.
On the one hand, I'm Ph.D. in Communication from University of Navarra and Master in Media Management from U. Navarra-IESE, as well as visiting research fellow at Oxford and INSEAD. On the other hand, that is, from a professional perspective, I was the editor of Sports in the El Salvador daily LA PRENSA GRAFICA (2000-2001). Then I came back to Spain and I worked for the dailies LA VOZ DE GALICIA (2002) and EL CORREO GALLEGO (2005), in which I was the development coordinator of the online division. Finally, I’ve been working for three years (2005-2008) as editor of management and business education in the Spanish financial daily LA GACETA. Now I start a new step in my career, first as freelance collaborating for Spanish, British and American dailies and magazines, but my hope is to enter the managerial world as director of communication of a company. I would be delighted to be the head of communication of a business school. But, apart from and due to my background in both communication and corporate reputation, I want to enter consulting. In fact, many professors at INSEAD, for instance, always say to me that I am strategic consulting minded. My e-mail is juanmanuelroca@hotmail.com.
