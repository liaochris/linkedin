Ivan Illan, CFS’s Summary

For more than two decades, Ivan M. Illan has pursued his enthusiasm and vision for careful ways of investing. Beginning at the age of 12, when he asked his father to turn over his college fund, and then developed his first investment plan. Mr. Illan was one of the first individual investors to utilize the internet for trade execution in 1989.

Mr. Illan's professional career began in 1996 with Nuveen Investments at their headquarters in Chicago, Illinois, where he learned how Wall Street's product development and distribution machine works. Working mainly with client firms Merrill Lynch, UBS, Morgan Stanley, and Wachovia, he pioneered relationship reporting systems. In 1998, Nuveen transferred him to their Southern California office, where his efforts were highlighted by a record-breaking NYSE listing that still stands today.

In 2000, Mr. Illan founded MingConsulting, an investment banking consultancy formed to serve the distribution goals of investment management firms in the U.S. and Canada. During this time, he had successfully introduced one of the first Canadian Royalty Trusts and Covered Call Writing ETF's to the U.S. financial advisor market.

In 2003, Mr. Illan decided to shift his knowledge and experience to work with individual investors. He understood that there are unique investment plan challenges, specifically for those who have investable assets from $500,000 to $3,000,000.

Mr. Illan served as a Vice President at Fisher Investments, a $50 billion wealth manager run by Forbes columnist, Kenneth L. Fisher. Seeking to embolden his clients with a more complete estate and retirement income strategy, he joined Michel Financial Group, as Director of Financial Planning and Registered Principal, in Los Angeles in January 2005.

Ivan Illan, CFS’s Specialties:

Mr. Illan has a B.S. in Finance and a B.A. in Philosophy from Boston College. He holds FINRA Licenses 7, 63, 65, 24, & 53 (General Securities Principal & Municipal Securities Principal), and CA Insurance License #0E72008. He lives and works in Los Angeles (Century City), California, where is also philanthropically involved in the support of contemporary art.
