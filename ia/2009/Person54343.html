Dan Mitchell’s Summary

In my career, almost all of the positions I have held focused on either B2C or B2B sales of various goods and services. I had taken the first steps into management with my previous employer, handling all aspects of the day-to-day operations of six car rental locations, including, but not limited to: personnel decisions, scheduling, marketing/market research and analysis, planning, and setting goals.

For most of the last five years, I have been working for CDW, a Fortune 500 provider of computer hardware, software, and services to businesses as well as government and academic institutions. I have worked closely with all three verticals, and have, for the last three and a half years, specialized in taking complex abstracts such as software and its myriad volume licensing options and making them easier to understand, steering customers to the software and licensing options that best suit both their present and future needs.

I am a highly adaptable, career-minded, goal-oriented Individual looking for a position that will take advantage of all that I have learned up to now, challenge me every day, while allowing me to learn and add even more experiences, and rewarding me for achieving the goals set forth for me. I like the enviornment at CDW, and can definitely see why CDW is consistently one of the 100 Best Companies to work for, and one of the best places to work in Chicago, I'm just looking for that "next step".
