Mark Strehlow’s Summary

Accomplished creative executive, strategist, designer and product manager with over 11 years of experience in rich-media applications and convergent interactive products. Specific expertise in concept ideation, product strategy, product management, information architecture, interaction design, user-centered design process and end-to-end product development across web, client, mobile and iTV platforms.

Clients include: Yahoo! Inc., Discovery Communications Inc., The Coca-Cola Company, Wrigley Jr Co., Nikon Corp., Hitachi Ltd., DaimlerChrysler AG, Honeywell Inc., Loews Corp., JetBlue Airways Corp., Tiny Pictures Inc., Microsoft Corp., 3Com Corp., The Charles Schwab Corp., Sovereign Bancorp, Financial Times Group, Whitney Museum.

Passionate about interactive technology, social media and video convergence. Always looking for the opportunity to develop new products and services that change the way consumers experience and interactive with media.

Mark Strehlow’s Specialties:

Product strategy, brand strategy, user research, usability, user-centered design process, design direction, information architecture, interaction design, product management, rich internet applications, mobile services, social software, interactive television and convergent product development.
