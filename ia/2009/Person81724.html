Frederick Rudolph’s Summary

Aviation Human Factors
Extensive human factors design and evaluation experience in the aviation and information technology domains. Design experience includes human factors system evaluation and design in support of military fighter and transport aircraft. Advanced technology evaluations of aircrew support systems including synthetic vision systems, HUD systems, and in-flight decision aiding systems. Training in aviation/aicraft accident and incident investigation including safety management systems. Professional experience in line operations as an airline pilot with extensive human factors/human performance assessments of aircrew performance.

Information Technology / Usability
Experienced as a usability architect in User-Centered Design, Human System Integration, Usability Evaluations, and Human Performance Evaluations, and HCI project management.

Frederick Rudolph’s Specialties:

Aviation human factors and crew systems design and assessment; task analytic techniques including cognitive task, task, function flow and allocation, workload and situational awareness analyses; user-centered automation and interaction design, usability testing including heuristic evaluation, use case development, cogntive task and work analyses, and rapid prototyping.
