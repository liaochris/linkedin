Phil Ryan’s Summary

A variety of Leadership positions in the Financial Services Industry including as Head of the Branch Network in a Regional Building Society and in Retail, Wholesale, Regional and Strategic roles for a Major Clearing Bank have honed my skills and allowed me to consistently achieve targets and goals in a motivational way.

My experience includes turning around under-performance and leading complex change programs. I have repeatedly been recognized as a member of various talent streams.

My experience includes sales-management of large teams (Regulated and Non-Regulated) complemented by a series of roles overseeing new initiatives and re-structures.

To support my business experience, I completed my MBA, part-time with merit and distinction level passes. In addition, I enjoyed a 10 year teaching career for World-wide skill development provider Dale Carnegie© & assoc which has given me a broad skill-set particularly as a leader and as a professional communicator.
