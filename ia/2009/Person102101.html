Jose Allan Tan’s Summary

I help conceptualize new websites for Questex Asia based on stated business goals by the Publishers of the company. Part of this responsibility includes liaising with the Web development team on key operational issues, working with the editorial team to understand their requirements, and working with publishers on developing new opportunities for the company. I regularly conduct regional electronic surveys, speak at forums, organize and moderate panel or roundtable discussions, and conduct workshops on marketing and PR for both vendors and PR agencies.

My passion is taking concepts and developing these into new products. I also look for new technologies or new processes from partners and localize these to meet our business needs and operational limitations.

Jose Allan Tan’s Specialties:

My various experiences as an IT end user, IT manager, sales and marketing executive, teacher, PR executive at an agency, analyst and regional marketer at a major, global vendor combined gives me the unique perspective of understanding the different needs of the various stakeholders in the IT ecosystem. I have empathy for everyone's needs and am able to translate this into meaningful programs.
