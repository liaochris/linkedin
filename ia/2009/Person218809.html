Jason Ice’s Summary

I have been working in the Fashion industry for just about 9 years now. Specifically in the Accessories areas. I have been designing watches for the past 8 years, and was involved with the graphics group and leathers departments my first year at Fossil. For the past 4 years I have been Sr. Product Designer over the Diesel License. I would like to stay in fashion somewhat, specifically being able to work on a number of different types of products. For example, it would be very cool to draw graphics for shirts, skateboards, stickers, etc... and then also design watches, jewelry, and other consumer goods that all speak a very tight similar language. I am at a point in my career, that I feel that I should be working on other projects than just watch design. I show art at a gallery in downtown Dallas called Studio Fling. I do a lot of other art events, and charities as well. I enjoy having other outlets to feed my creative energy, but I think I would like to try multiple product lines professionally and really round out what I have been doing for the past few years. Eventually as an artist I will want to change and take on new goals, styles, and continue to grow, absorb, observe, and expand my skill set. That is natural...

Jason Ice’s Specialties:

Conceptual development, Asian factory communication, hand skills, illustration, graphic design, product design, installation design. Total design package - Form, color, texture, material, from setting the direction and trend forecasting, conceptual development, technical development, to the store shelf regarding consumer goods.
