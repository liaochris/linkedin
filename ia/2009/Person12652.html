Anand Sanwal’s Summary

Finance and strategy specialist with significant expertise in corporate innovation and corporate portfolio management (also referred to as enterprise portfolio management, product portfolio management, resource allocation, investment optimization).

I've built the corrporate portfolio management effort at American Express. It is widely recognized as the leading practice in corporate portfolio management. I also recently managed the company's inaugural Chairman's Innovation Fund with a focus on identifying and seeding the best disruptive and emerging ideas for the organization.

I'm the author of a book entitled "Optimizing Corporate Portfolio Management" which features a foreword by Gary Crittenden, the current CFO/COO of Citigroup and former CFO of American Express. I am also a frequent speaker on Corporate Portfolio Management speaking to organizations like the CFO Executive Board, Gartner, Beyond Budgeting Roundtable, CFO Magazine and to many corporate management teams.
