Bob Patrick’s Summary

We are revolutionizing the Real Estate and Mortgage Markets. Our service marked Mortgage Training Program, Real Estate Trade-In Program, and Worry Free-Guaranteed Homes provide innovative ways for the mortgage and real estate markets flourish.
The Mortgage Training Program provides under qualified home buyers the means in which they can become a home owner. “Become a homeowner through financial literacy.” We will train and coach you to be financially literate while you live in your future home providing proof that you can handle THE MORTGAGE and everything that comes with homeownership.
Our Real Estate Trade-In Program was developed as a marketing tool for new home builders similar to the auto industry we take the buyers existing home in trade. Clients receive a quick and easy offer eliminating all the concerns a new home buyer faces: when to list, how much will the existing home sell for, what is the true completion date of the new home. New home buyers can move-on knowing their existing home is sold and its value while eliminating the coordination game.
All homes we purchase are inspected and repaired allowing us to offer them as Worry Free-Guaranteed Homes.
