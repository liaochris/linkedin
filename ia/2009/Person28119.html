George Bickerstaff’s Summary

George is a board member, investor, partner and trustee for various public and private businesses and philanthropic organizations.

He has held senior business and finance leadership positions with some of the largest and most respected companies in the world, including Dun & Bradstreet, General Electric and Novartis.

George has been on the board of directors of more than a dozen international companies and philanthropic organizations.

He has been instrumental in establishing the strategic, operating and financial direction for numerous private and public companies, and provided financial leadership in mergers valued at more than $50 billion during his career.

He has expertise in investment banking, venture capital, private equity, healthcare, emerging markets, corporate governance, and philanthropy.

He has been a guest speaker on global finance at various international industry associations and graduate business schools, including the Harvard Business School.

George has lived on four continents and currently resides in Connecticut.

George Bickerstaff’s Specialties:

I enjoy advising and helping people with projects in my areas of interest which include education, emerging markets, financial services, healthcare and developing private and public global partnerships.

My non-profit and philanthropic work focuses on helping disadvantaged children from the poorest countries in the world.

I prefer hands on board roles working with innovative and team oriented entrepreneurs, business people and public leaders.

    (Partnership; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    October 2008 — Present (11 months)

    The GAVI Alliance is one of the largest and most impactful public and private partnerships in the world focused on improving global healthcare.

    Our mission is to immunize children from the most basic diseases in the poorest countries of the world. We are headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland. Our website is www.gavialliance.org.

    The World Health Organization estimates that the GAVI Alliance has saved the lives of more than 5 million children since its founding in 2000.

    The GAVI Alliance partners include The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, The World Bank, The World Health Organization, UNICEF, the United Nations, and the vaccine and pharmaceutical industries. Our extended partners include universities, the world banking industry, NGOs, news and reporting organizations, and key opinion leaders.

    The GAVI Alliance has committed more than $5 billion to immunize children and improve related global healthcare systems. We have developed innovative financing and advanced market commitment programs to develop new vaccines and immunization processes. We actively seek and encourage new ideas and participation from all concerned people and organizations.

    Some of the people who have shown their support for the GAVI Alliance by committing their time and energies to our current and past Board of Directors are:

    CRT Investment Banking believes that public companies, privately owned businesses and entrepreneurs in the middle market require personalized, high-quality investment banking expertise. Most of the large Wall Street firms, including the bulge-bracket investment banks, depend on large clients and transactions to support high-cost infrastructures and cannot afford to invest the time and skilled professionals required to service the middle market. Middle-market boutique advisory firms, on the other hand, often lack the investment banking experience required to guide their clients through complex situations and transactions - precisely the time when their clients need them most.

    Our strategy is to fill this gap. We stand ready to assist middle-market clients in all situations, whether they need advice in completing a merger or acquisition transaction or assistance raising financing on attractive terms. CRT investment bankers have the flexibility to seek and nurture long-term relationships with clients in situations where we can help them create value.

    Our professionals' first-rate investment banking skills, gained advising and raising money for clients over decades at world-class investment banking firms, makes us more valuable to our clients as the complexity of their needs increases.
