Shirish Mehta’s Experience

  • Optimization Engineer (Gas Processing)

    eSimulation, Inc.

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    April 2007 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    • Modeling of midstream gas processing plants for online optimization; the plants modeled includes Cryogenic plants, Dew point plants, J-T plants, Refrigeration units, Liquids fractionation units.
    • Rigorous equation based online optimization which includes model predictive of the process control system.
    • Good knowledge of process units like exchangers, separators, reciprocating and centrifugal compressors. Representation of compressor performance curves in the form of regressed surge, head and efficiency co-relations.
    • Modeling includes plant economics in the form of contract terms, shrink, fuel, fees and product margins.
    • Optimizer commissioning includes working with the operators making optimization moves on the plant and performing a detailed value analysis on the moves made.
    • Engineering consulting in the form of value capture program explaining the optimization moves to clients, discuss strategies to improve value.
