Eric Meyer’s Summary

EXECUTIVE TECHNOLOGY MANAGEMENT

Visionary and creative executive with exceptional management skills and a strong record of building and leading high-performing professional technology teams and operations in industries including social networking (Zurock, Be Jane), e-commerce (Netflix, LowerMyBills), technology (KPMG), and retail (Harry’s Farmers Market). Highly successful start-up experience. Built two teams that contributed to successful IPOs. World-class technology expertise in the development of Social Networks, Internet, Web, e-commerce, business analytics and IT solutions. Strategic planner with a focus on simplicity and efficiency and the imagination, focus and leadership to accomplish challenging goals against formidable odds. Fluent in English and French.
