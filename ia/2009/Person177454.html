Nicola Woods’s Summary

Nicola has extensive experience working in HR, Recruitment, at both a strategic & operational level, and Project Management within a variety of organisations covering Financial Services, Public Sector, Film and most recently within demanding Technology start-ups. She is delivery focussed, thrives in demanding and challenging environments and has delivered proven results in all her roles. Nicola is thorough, responsive, possesses excellent communication and listening skills, is pragmatic and flexible. She is currently in the process of completing the International Coach Federation accredited Professional Certificate in Leadership Coaching.

Nicola Woods’s Specialties:

Project management, coaching, mentoring, facilitation, organisational development, restructuring, employee relations, managing redundancy, redeployment, change, recruitment (SW engineering, knowledge engineering, product management, project management, commercial, UI design, financial services), employer branding, assessment centres, performance management, comp & benefits, HR operations, expatriation and repatriation.
