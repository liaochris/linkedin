Angie Allred’s Summary

I have been involved in clinical research for more than 20 years and have held almost every position at the clinical research site level, from my first data entry job in 1990 to the Director of Clinical Operations at a Phase I research facility. My varied background in clinical research has given me a comprehensive knowledge in the conduct of human subjects trials. I have used this experience to train and mentor many novice clinical research professionals and to make countless contacts in the clinical research industry. I have utilized my skills and contacts to start my own clinical research company and plan to expand with a Phase I facility within 5 years.
