Gary Mountain’s Summary

Gary founded the first ever school to train loan agents/ officers in 1984. The Royal American School of Mortgage Banking in 1980. More than 4,000 people paid $ 3,500 each to attend a one-month training class for 30 hours per week. Mr. Mountain trained a team of 22 trainers and set up facilities that trained the Loan Agents in seven counties in California and in six additional states. Mr. Mountain sold the training materials to GE Mortgage Insurance and he wrote the Community Home Buying Class Program from the materials which became a national program sponsored by FHLMC.

Gary has processed, underwritten and funded real estate loans since 1966. Authored the first Adjustable Rate Mortgage (not a Government loan product) with an interest rate and a payment cap in 1981. In 1983 Mr. Mountain wrote the first three year fixed ARM with caps on the rate and period of change. In 1986 Mr. Mountain authored the first Option ARM loan Product and completed a $ 300MM first ever Mortgage Backed Security on Wall Street at a price of 107.

Gary is Founder of California Star Properties, Inc., which conducts the following real estate based business: sales, loans and investments. Mr. Mountain has sold over 3,000 properties (one at a time). He has closed $1.4 billion in loans for residential development, refinances and purchase money transactions.

Gary buys real estate properties, creates an avenue for investors to purchase a percentage of each property. Mr. Mountain manages the entire project from its beginning to completion. He buys the property and then when he sells the investment he stays in the transaction to assure its success. He has sub-divided raw land, designed single-family homes, and managed the General Contractors and all engineering disciplines' of the entire project.

Contact Gary today if you have any inquiries related to real estate.
