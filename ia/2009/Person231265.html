Nicole Bienfang’s Summary

Nicole Bienfang, former part time Marketing Manager for both Custo Barcelona and Tous Jewelry brands for Chicago locations only, has made a successful launch pad out of her failed website based entrepreneurial endeavors. Nicole is often asked for help or suggestions involving businesses in the local area, she happily obliges rather for payment, barter, or trade. She is a Platinum member of GenArt Chicago. It is her mantra that “When you focus on your customers the bottom line happens.”

"You must be the change you wish to see in the world." -- Mahatma Gandhi

So, often it is much easier to blame others and our surroundings for what is personally happening in our lives and our world. Nicole believes that the only thing that stands between yourself and a better day, a bigger profit, and a richer life is YOU! Taking accountability for oneself and your actions is the first step, on the road, to success and making things happen! Business is not just business, for Nicole most of her good friends are people who got to know her first through business relations.

She seeks to make a difference in the work she does and the people she does projects with. Often chosen as leader in groups Nicole has no problem working in a team as long as everyone involved is focused and out to get the job done as efficiently as possible.

Always learning, whether reading a new business book or magazine or in the classroom, Nicole is currently studying for a Bachelors in Marketing Management from The Illinois Institute of Art in Chicago. She is also conducting research in the field of women and their romantic relationships for a possible book.

Nicole Bienfang’s Specialties:

Nicole is apt to take initiative and is often seen as a fire starter or one who takes an old idea and tweaks it into becoming something great.

Consulting businesses on creative advertising, new product launches, and attending conventions in tandem to cut the clutter and find the gems is what adds spice to her life.

She has a focus on customer service and creating “Raving Fans”. Whether you need to work with someone laid back or someone strictly professional she’s your woman for the job!
