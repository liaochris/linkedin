Calvin Fraites’s Summary

Here is the area I am supposed to describe my views and opinions on what I version “Calvin” I am running…. If you did not get the joke you must not be to familiar with computers. My brain often works most efficiently when sitting in front of large LCD screens. Late nights in the solitude of my “Cave” either at home or work, may it be Mac, Pc, or even Red Hat I am the guy to make it work like it was designed to.

For the past 15 years my work circle has been an aviation themed one, it could be in simulation or training and I could say a thing or two about the subject.

I am not sure there is a "Goal" in the aviation world, rather a stream of keep trucking. Technology has been a big ticket item these past 4 years or so in the General Aviation arena, so my goal with ELITE Simulations would be to see our trainers online and being utilized to its full capability in every flight school, academy, and university across the country.
