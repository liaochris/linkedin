Nathan King’s Summary

After graduating from Pratt Institute in 2001 with a BFA in Communications Design, I started up a home based graphic design studio under my name. In the five years that my business was operational I worked with clients such as award winner perfumer Christopher Brosius, actor Alan Cumming, Details Magazine and more. The majority of my clients were small/startups who were in need of corporate identity and brochures.

After serving over 90 clients with various projects, I felt that my career was at a stand-still. In August of 2006, I began working with Austin & Williams, an advertising agency located on Long Island. The projects I handle at the agency include logo design, brochure/collateral design, advertising design and more. Even more important, I get to work with a team of talented designers with a wide range of skills. In the last year or so, I have produced some of my best work and continue to grow as a designer.
