Amanda Brzoska’s Summary

Hello. My name is Amanda Brzoska, and I am the managing member of My Small Business Assistant, LLC. My goal is to help entrepreneurs navigate through the tedious process of establishing a new business as well as to help existing small business owners in areas where it is more cost effective to outsource such as bookkeeping and web design.

As a small business owner and office manager of ten years, I understand your needs. I realize that you are on a budget, and your time is valuable. I also understand the need for accuracy, integrity, and confidentiality when it comes to handling sensitive business information. It is my mission to provide honest and accurate information, useful tools, valuable resources, and above all, excellent customer service. Let me help you to develop your small business. Contact me today!
