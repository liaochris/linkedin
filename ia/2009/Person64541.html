Dj J-Rok’s Summary

There are a few people in this world that set out to be not your average, Dj/Producer J-Rok is one of them. For J-Rok, life as a Dj began at the age of 12. Instead of hanging out with mates and chasing girls he would come home and spend every minute out of school mastering his skills. With support from his family and close friends, there has been no stopping in J-Rok from dominating the whole circuit.

Today J-Rok is one of the most sought after and captivating dj’s in Australia with his unique mixing style and technical skills on the turntable. Working with a wide range of contemporary music genres including Bollywood, Hip Hop, R&B, Neo Soul Electronic and Dancehall, He also holds a degree in audio production under his belt. From incorporating live instruments to digital sounds, he produces his exclusive remixes from start to finish by himself.

Dj J-Rok has played at a number of Australia's largest nightclubs. Whether it’s the Gold Coast, Melbourne, Perth or at home in Sydney, He has also had the chance to support local and international artists such as lil jon, Ricki Lee, Chingy, Montel Jordan, UB40, LL Cool J, Israel, DMX, Joe, Eve, RDB, Jay Dhabi, Stan Bravo, Bally Sagoo, Fatman Scoop, 112, KC & Jojo, Sean Kingston, Boys II Men and more.

With 6 to 8 club gigs a week, J-Rok likes to stay on top of his game by practicing his routines at a minimum of 4 hours a day and also keeping up to date with the best of the best party tracks for his rocking club sets.
