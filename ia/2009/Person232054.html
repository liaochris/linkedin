Nikki Cooke’s Summary

I am a professional freelance copywriter, specialised in helping small to medium sized businesses promote themselves. To me, business words must work as hard as any other employee - they are the voice and face of a company and need to perform!

Having spent 16 years in the Education sector teaching English to students at all levels and managing newly-qualified teachers, I decided on a career change. After attaining a Copywriting Diploma with distinction, I became a member of The Institute of Copywriting and freelanced for several marketing agencies.

The drive to be my own boss inspired me to start a copywriting, web-design and Internet marketing company, The Word Well. We currently provide professional copy and web- design to a host of UK SMEs, industries and organisations. Our mission is to put works to work and businesses on the map!

I also write a copywriting blog - Copy Break - through which I give advice on how to write effective sales copy. When I'm not working, I publish online articles, write poetry and tutor students.
