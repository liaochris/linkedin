Scott Thornton’s Summary

Scott Thornton is the Owner and Principal Designer for Blenderhouse Creative, a Graphic Design Studio that specializes in Web Design, Print Design, Brand Identity and Custom Illustration. He has seven years experience producing strategically driven design solutions for a diverse clientèle. He has helped large corporations such as Universal Pictures as well as small local entrepreneurs just starting out.

Scott prides himself on maintaining long lasting relationships with his clients by providing high quality work and service. He is a life long student of business and design and uses that passion to help his clients succeed.

Scott has earned a BFA in Graphic Design from Shepherd College and is also an accomplished illustrator. Scott is a member of the Berkeley County Chamber of Commerce.

Scott Thornton’s Specialties:

Brand Identity: Logos, Business Cards and Stationary Systems.

Web Design: Design, Development, Web Hosting, Programming, Online Marketing, SEO, E-newsletters and Blogs.

Print Design: Brochures, Newsletters, Direct Mail, Magazine Layout, Posters and Ad Design.
