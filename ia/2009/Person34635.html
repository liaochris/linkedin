Bruce Barber’s Summary

I host and produce "The WNPR Health Forum" for Connecticut Public Radio, and blog at "The Real Life Survival Guide", an online collection of practical advice and "Tips for Living".

I also help bloggers and small business owners understand the power of social media.

Bruce Barber’s Specialties:

Professional broadcasting, independent radio production, creative web development, blogging, vlogging, consulting, speaking, and podcasting.
