Van Overbay’s Summary

Originally from Kingsport, Tennessee, Van formed The Galactic Attic LLC in 1997. During his agency life, he headed studios for advertising firms in Atlanta, Chattanooga, and Colorado. Van currently enjoys being the Creative Director for North Bridge Studios and Game Designer for History In Action Games.

North Bridge Studios, a division of The Galactic Attic, is an award-winning communication design firm located on scenic Signal Mountain in Tennessee. The company is headed by founder Van Overbay and serves local and national clients ranging from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies.

Van Overbay’s Specialties:

• Innovative game design, research and development.

• Logo, brand and corporate identity design.

• Print and graphic design services including: annual reports,
business collateral, POP, packaging and high-res digital art.

• Website, user-interface and interactive design including:
programming, hosting, SEO, and site management.

• Photography and fine art including: original art, limited
edition prints, corporate art and image licensing.
