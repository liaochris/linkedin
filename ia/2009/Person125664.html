Kalpesh Desai’s Summary

20+ years experience as a techno-commercial executive having held multiple roles in product development, operations management, sales and marketing management and general management.

Till recently, the Chief Operating Officer at 3i Infotech’s Europe, Middle East & African operations, and a member of 3i Infotech’s Operating Board, reporting into the Deputy Managing Director. Known for ability to convert corporate vision into strategic intent and then move teams to embrace it. Attract and retain talented people and allow teams to take chances and innovate by creating the space of an entrepreneurial-corporate environment. Drive self and teams hard, but manage with honesty and respect at all times while fostering an unparalleled loyalty that delivers results. Managed the growth of 3i Infotech’s EMEA organization at an average of 85% Year on Year, meeting financial objectives in expenses, cash and productivity, core management team activities, consistent initiatives towards enablement of new markets and new channels, product and market direction, direct engagement, where required, in the large account acquisition and management process.

Built and energized sales channels while creating new revenues and clients from widening business lines. Make marketing and product divisions client-focused and accountable, enforce the corporate vision, and then reengineer processes to become more nimble and responsive. Successfully merged disparate acquisition cultures, strategically funnelling activities to create new revenue streams.
