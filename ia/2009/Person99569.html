Irina Grozdeva’s Summary

Coaching executives in their process of clarification and execution of corporate goals to achieve results by:

1. Leading by example:
Support her executive clients to communicate authentically;
2. Leading towards the company direction and results: Involvement or identification of the people in the team wih the strategic direction (vision, mission, USP, values) through communication;
3. Aligning the team:
Supporting her executive clients to align the team with the strategic direction and accelerating the process of execution of goals; CEOs achieve results through people - by inspiring them.
4. Clear, simple and centered communication to customers and the market:
Supporting the CEO to authentically communicate to the market in a centered, simple and memorable way.

Irina Grozdeva’s Specialties:

Authentic communication underpins Irina's work with her executive clients.
Why authenticity? It "the fast track of communication" in the team as it:
1. Provides fast and simple communication in the team;
2. Accelerates times the achievement of results;
3. The customers and partners hear and easily recognize the message from the company;
4. The people in the team are inspired and are ready to give the best of themselves.
