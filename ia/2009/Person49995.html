Craig "Big-T" Thompson’s Summary

Craig Thompson, Dallas-based entrepreneur, is making headlines. While forays into the insurance field netted him great success (a team of 200+ agents) he has most recently crafted his Internet Protocol Television expertise into two new ventures: the Weight Loss Surgery Channel and The Plastic Surgery Channel. Both offer consumers relevant, unbiased edutainment as well as a community of support. These channels have made a splash with consumers and reputable surgeons alike. As a side note, Craig is a singer of some renown; he is Big-T of Big-T and the Bada-Bings—which blends a unique mixture of Blues, Swing, Big Band and Classic Rock guaranteed to deliver a good time. Ask the locals, or ask Queen Elizabeth; Big T sang at her Golden Jubilee Celebration.
