Chris Unwin’s Summary

I'm a driven media executive and brand strategist, with a track record of two successful brand launches, and countless client campaigns involving television, web, print, mobile, and live events.

Currently, I take care of Brand Partnerships, Strategy, and Business Development at the Much MTV Group (MuchMusic, MTV Canada). The role involves crafting integrated media campaigns with clients such as Rogers, Virgin Mobile, Universal Music; strategic partnerships with the likes of Apple and MasterCard; and development of our audience's television and web experience. Prior to that, I contributed to MTV Canada's successful launch strategy as a member of the Consumer Marketing team.

Before joining Much MTV, I managed marketing strategy and helped to launch Dose, a multi-platform media company spanning print, web and mobile.

Above all else, I am most comfortable on the leading edge of new media development and strategy.
