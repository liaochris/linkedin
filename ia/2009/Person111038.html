Jerome Sutter’s Summary

Founder and CEO of SonidoLocal.com, I created this company in Bogota (Colombia) to use my knowledge in e-business and to focus on digital music opportunities.

Before coming to Colombia, I was in charge of the European Development of RideSpirit.com, Mailorder company (website and paper catalogue) specialized in extreme sports hardgoods, wear and shoes (snowboarding, surfing, skateboarding).
After having participated in its creation in 2000 and set-up its Spanish subsidiairy until 2004, I was in charge of the international business development on the English, German, Spanish, Swiss and Belgian markets through website and catalogues. In 2006 RideSpirit was leader on the French, Belgian, Swiss and Spanish markets and considered as one of the main European retailers.

After having completed my mission and lived 6 great years of entrepreunarial experience from Scratch to Europe, I met my Colombian wife and decided to leave the company and live new business experiences in Colombia.

I launched SonidoLocal.com in April 2007 as a provider of digital marketing and social networking strategies focused on music to biggest marketing agencies in Colombia and Latin America (Havas Digital, Publicis Starcom, Leo Burnett...) and strong brands & companies (Nokia, Coca Cola, Pepsi Co, Reckitt Benckiser, Procter & Gamble...).
As an example, we realized on the Colombian market the first marketing campaign in Andean Region that provided free, legal and DRM-free Mp3s from local and international EMI Music artists to Veet customers (Reckitt Benckiser group).

Since 1st of october 2008 and the lauch of its new version, SonidoLocal.com is the first Streaming on Demand website in Latin America in association with Universal Music, regional companies and local indies bands.
The service is currently available in Colombia and I am planning to make it available in the whole Andean Region, Argentina and Chile by the end of 2009.
