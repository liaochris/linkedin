Mark Tosczak’s Summary

I am a senior account executive at RLF Communications, a public relations and marketing agency in Greensboro, N.C., where I have provided counsel to clients in health care, financial services and real estate, among other industries. I have also provided public relations, communications and marketing services to nonprofits, universities and businesses. I spent about a decade working in journalism as an award-winning writer and editor. And I have been blogging off and on since 2000.
