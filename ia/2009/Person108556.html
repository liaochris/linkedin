Jeffrey O'Brien’s Summary

I've been writing and editing long-form magazine features since 1991. Several of my pieces at both Fortune and Wired have been reprinted in various "Best of..." anthologies. I received the Jesse H. Neal Award as editor of the best single issue of a business magazine in 1998, and was a 2006 Templeton-Cambridge fellow in science & religion at the University of Cambridge (UK).

Jeffrey O'Brien’s Specialties:

science, adventure, business stories
