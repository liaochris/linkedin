Suspended Animation Media’s Summary

Suspended Animation Media (SAM)

To those who haven't encountered our humble abode, allow us to fill you in.
We are Suspended Animation Media (SAM)

Suspended Animation Media is a team of Filipino artists and one Latino.

It is a studio dedicated to doing pre-production and concept design art for Film, 2d/3d animation, Cartoons, video games, comics, web design and more.

Empowered by our skills and talent, and fueled by determination, the team of SAM aim to bring the most experienced and skilled talent in the country, and together we will develop and execute the optimum solutions in Pre-production.

SAM is a place where imagination, creativity, expression and innovation is encouraged, nurtured and expected.
Our policies are focused on truth, justice, honesty and trust.
And our value only regard that artists are treated right, respected and welcomed.
