Drenen Tucker’s Summary

A Proven, High-Level Leader, who had to learn that you LEAD people and MANAGE the process, I accept the challenges, the responsibilty, and little, if any of the success that my teams have accomplished. I realized that to maintain a consistant level of energy, enthusiasm, and patience, a person must be passionate about their life's work. After 8+ years in the mortgage industry, I realized I no longer had that passion. Sports and athletics have been at the foundation of my life since my first baseball game in 1976. In completing this Bachelor of Science degree in Sport Management, I will have the opportunity to continue on the path that was started while I was the Director of Sales and Marketing with ESPN Radio in Spartanburg, SC. My day-to-day interaction with the Wofford College Athletic Department (Members of the Southern Conference), The University of South Carolina - Upstate (Members of the Atlantic Sun Conference), The Greenville Drive (Class A Affiliate of the Boston Red Sox), The Spartanburg Stingers (Coastal Plains League, a Collegiate Summer Baseball Team), as well as our relationship with the Carolina Panthers (NFL), Charlotte Bobcats (NBA), and the Atlanta Braves (MLB), all allowed me to be a part of each organization and a 'behind-the-scenes' look at the inner workings of different levels of amateur and professional sports.
Finding business partners within the community to rebrand our station with was a tremendous experience as well. With the relationships we built within the community, we implented several campaigns that directly increased our visibility within the community. We were able to gain valuable relationships with Team Spartanburg, the Spartanburg Touchdown-Tip Off Club, and worked closely with the City of Spartanburg and the Carolina Panthers to enhance the Training Camp experience for the Fans. For the first time in over 30 years, ESPN1400 is now a rated radio station in the Greenville-Spartanburg-Anderson Market.
