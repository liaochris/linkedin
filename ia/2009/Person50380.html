Helene Lerner’s Summary

For more than two decades, New York based Helene Lerner has been addressing the concerns of contemporary women. As a prolific author, independent public-television host, Emmy Award-winning executive producer, and Fortune 500 workplace consultant, she covers a wide array of women’s issues—from work-life balance, to health and wellness, to relationships and building self-esteem. With more than 20 televised specials, nine books, and one of the premier websites for professional women www.womenworking.com that has some seven-million hits annually, describes herself as an empowerment advocate who helps women from all walks of life actualize their potential.
