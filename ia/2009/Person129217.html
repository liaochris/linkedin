Kelle Sparta’s Summary

Professional Speaker, Trainer and Coach focused on helping small business owners create lives and businesses they can love. I do this by helping people grow their business from the inside out - starting with the owner. We look at the marketing and branding of the company, we look at how well the company marketing materials bring forward the vision and personality of the owner and we look at how the owner may be sabotaging the business and how we can fix that. For more information about me and business consulting, visit www.kellesparta.com.

I also have a specialty in the real estate industry. I teach real estate agents how to coach their clients not only through the transaction itself, but also through whatever major life transition caused those clients to move in the first place. By doing this, agents create stronger, deeper, longer-lasting bonds with their clients, creating more repeat and referral business. Because the agents are invested in their clients, the clients also invest in them. This allows agents to have a higher quality of life with more time off and higher-trust, lower-stress relationships with their clients. Author of The Consultative Real Estate Agent, C2006, AMACOM Publishing. You can find more details on me and my real estate company at www.spartasuccess.com
