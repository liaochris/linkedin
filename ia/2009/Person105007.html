Jason Gobeyn’s Summary

This all started with my Grandfather who started in this industry now over 72 years ago. My father was also in the exhibit manufacturing industry for 40 years of his life retiring 12 years ago.

I personally started in this industry at the age of 15 as a carpenter working at McCormick place taking a 6 year vacation in the Marine Corps at the age of 17.

Since my discharge from the Marine's I have done nothing else but trade shows. I have now been in this industry over 20 years handling some of the largest exhibit programs in the United States. Some with over 250 shows per year.

LAB Exhibits, Inc. is a full turn key operation which allows us to be there for our customers every step of the way if they want us to.

LAB Exhibits, Inc. has it's own in-house designers, in-house graphic production, in-house fabric print and production, in-house carpenter shop, in-house metal shop, in-house carpet department, in-house project management department, in-house AV department, in-house IT management staff which assists customers at showsite as well, and in-house account management department.

LAB also has contracts with unions in now 33 major cities and over 300 suburban cities allowing us to install your exhibit in any trade show facility accross the United States. Offering you direct prices, unlike most of our competitors that must sub out this work to various labor companies which is much more costly for the exhibitor.

We offer very competitive pricing with amazing design, just call us and see 630-896-2500 x 33
