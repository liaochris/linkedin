Rodney Overton’s Summary

I have the unique experience of working for websites for both leading newspapers and TV stations, with a key understanding of the strengths and weaknesses of both.
I can help each media become more like the other -- newspapers to learn TV skills and TV stations who need to learn to become more like newspapers.
Obviously, this cross-over information is extremely relevant as both sets of media seek to reinvent themselves and grow their audiences on the web.
My experience can help propel a newspaper forward incredibly quickly -- and can do the same for a TV station.
The question is: are those organizations ready for real change?
