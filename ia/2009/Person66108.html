Debbie Origho’s Summary

Hello and thank you for stopping by my LinkedIn Page.

I am Multimedia Communications Specialist. I specialize in every form and platform of multimedia communication and production. My main areas of focus include:

1.) Media Sales/ Advertising

2.) Public Relations

3.) Marketing

4.) Writing (Print, Web, TV & Radio)

5.) Freelance Video Production (Print, Web, TV & Radio)

I conduct business with the utmost degree of integrity and professionalism while using current technological outlets to advance all projects, programs, companies, and organizations that I may support. I have a wealth of professional experience from TV (NBC, FOX, Nat'l Geo Ch), Print (Westside Gazette, District Chronicles) and even online media (Award- Winning Producer for Eyes of the Prize New Media Project http://www.nbpc.tv/hbcu).

My time in corporate America has taught me to follow my heart and work through my faith. As a result, I use my positive work ethic and uplifting personality to empower, inspire and inform the world about the gospel of Christ.

Presently, I am focusing on Christian Media and Ownership in hopes of owning my own Contemporary Ministry Network one day. I would like to provide my generation and others with an outlet in which they can be entertained without compromising their values or beliefs.
