    isNSFW

    (Computer Software industry)

    May 2009 — Present (3 months)

    isNSFW is the Safe For Work way to share Not Safe For Work links. When you click a shortened URL from other services, you are automatically redirected to the site. The shortened URL offers you no information about where it's going to take you to.

    isNSFW is different. When you shorten a URL you specify why something might not be safe to open. When someone clicks your link, they're taken to a landing page with information about the link and they are given the opportunity to either follow the link or to pass on it for their own reasons.
