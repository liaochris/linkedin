Rishi Narang’s Summary

Researcher with interests & hands-on experience in Vulnerability Management, UTM, Network Security, Binary reversing, Zero Days Exploits and PoC, responsible bug disclosures, implementing physical and virtual networks, virtualization concepts etc.
Eagerly interested in new ideas related to security from brim to bottom. Always ready to explore new ventures, creative ideas and deep insights into the network packets and implemented protocols.
Specializing in Vulnerability Management Consulting, Information Security Advisories and Research. Regularly updated with ongoing progress in the field of security and it's darker counterparts. Love to read blogs, security and information feeds, mailing lists, author articles in magazines as well as white papers. Enjoy contributing to information security forums, learning and sharing knowledge and building networks - human as well as machine.

Public Profile:
Rishi Narang is presently working as Security Researcher with Trend Micro. He has 4+ years of hands-on experience in information security which includes research on vulnerabilities, malware, protocol analysis, evolving attack vectors and signature development for network & host based security products. He holds a B.Tech degree in Information Technology, CCNA certification and has been a part of SuSE, Red Hat & Ethical Hacking trainings in the past. He is an active member of Indian Honeynet Project and has been closely associated with various security advisory publications & disclosures. He was the first to report a bug & demo it’s proof of concept for Google Chrome Browser and his work has been quoted in ZDNet, CNET, Hindu Business Line, SCMagazine etc. He is an advisor for International Edition of Hakin9 Magazine and has been a speaker at OWASP (Mumbai Chapter) & eSurakshIT 2009.
In his free time he prefers reading blogs and writing articles at his personal site.
