Daniel Hower’s Summary

Daniel Hower has delivered successful results to numerous agencies and businesses in the Internet industry. He has twelve years of professional experience in web interface design.

Dan is an exceptional web graphic designer and a certified developer in HTML, xHTML and CSS. He utilizes JavaScript such as JQuery, Moo.FX and sIFR to enrich web sites and applications and builds dynamic pages with PHP. Dan's expertise also includes information design, user experience and Section 508. He has top-notch interpersonal skills, an extensive background in the fine arts, and an interest in business development.

These characteristics allow Dan to handle diverse responsibilities and makes him a significant asset to any web business-driven company. His goal is to provide outstanding value as an experienced, highly talented employee on a creative or marketing team.

Daniel Hower’s Specialties:

Hand coded validated xHTML, advanced CSS, Section 508, JavaScript and PHP. Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver and Visio. Strong interpersonal, presentation, communication and organizational skills. Competent on PC and Mac. Experience in Java and PHP environments.
