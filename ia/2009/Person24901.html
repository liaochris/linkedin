Bas van Koll’s Summary

Educated as an Industrial Designer, Bas moved into New Media after graduating in 1996 from the prestigious Design Academy in Eindhoven. Realising desktop computers would become an essential future design tool, his self-taught knowledge inspired him to explore new media and motion graphics, which came together in his interactive work. Bas has a strong drive to push the creative boundaries within concepts and strives to constantly explore new technologies and applications. During his time at LostBoys, working closely with computer programmers and directing a design team, Bas’ curiosity, perfectionism and eagerness to take on full creative responsibility, resulted in a variety of successful and pioneering cross media projects for a wide range of clients.

By 2002, having founded his own crossmedia design company Stoot, Bas accepted the position of Creative Director Film and Video at Nike Brand Design EMEA working on Brand Initiatives and Sales Projects. Over the next 5 years, Bas worked closely with directors, design agencies, post production houses worldwide as well as directing films himself.Credited with upgrading the Film & Video content coming out of Nike EMEA, Bas was given a wider responsibility within the Brand Design department, which included managing designers and working on marketing and retail marketing conceptual design solutions for the Brand.

Bas has the rare talent of having both creative and organizational skills. And from a creative point of view, he knows what he wants, always comes well prepared and is clear and straight when choices need to be made.
