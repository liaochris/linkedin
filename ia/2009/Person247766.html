Will Sturgeon’s Summary

Experienced UK journalist turned public relations consultant, working predominantly on campaigns and clients across the technology sector.

Through my experience, contacts and understanding of how the media works I am able to work closely with clients and advise and coach them on the most effective way to handle the media and get key messages across, as well as building stronger media-facing relationships. I also offer a high level of creative input on campaign planning, messaging and drafting content.

In terms of internal agency development I work closely across all teams coaching at all levels on subject matter and media strategy as well presentation skills and media relations. Also planning, resourcing and hiring.

I also play a senior role in the agency's new business activity.

I bring 10 years of media experience to my role at LEWIS PR, including a senior editor position at silicon.com, part of CNET Networks and freelance writing for the Sunday Times.

Will Sturgeon’s Specialties:

Media relations consultancy, creative campaigns, content generation, public speaking, presentation and media training.
