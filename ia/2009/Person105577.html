Jason Whipple’s Summary

A hard-working and innovative thinker with a dynamic professional background including 8+ years of writing and editing experience, 4+ years as a producer and director of marketing videos and websites, and 5+ years as a community organizer and social worker with a focus on empowering low-income families, delinquent youth, and children with unique behavioral and emotional challenges. An organized and thoughtful team-player with a vivid imagination and an unforgettable sense of humor.
