Erik Bowman’s Summary

Erik Bowman is a senior technology executive with over 20 years of experience in the industry. As a serial entrepreneur, Erik is a results-oriented, strategic thinker with proactive, creative and analytical abilities. He works successfully with all functions and levels of management.

Erik is an industry veteran, having founded and developed iMessenger.com, one of the first 'social networking' sites, in 1997.

Over the past 11 years he has gone on to found a number of multimedia and communications companies including Talent-Exchange.com a co-branded multimedia talent portfolio service launched in 2001, VerticalAnswer.com a Hosted PBX and Telephony service provider launched in 2003, ProviderOffice.com an appointment scheduling and call center solution for healthcare providers launched in 2003, PublicityEngine.com an XML/RSS based press release service that featured syndication in most popular feed formats and PeopleScreening.com video resume hosting service for individuals with private-labeled online career center for businesses and recruiters launched in 2006.
