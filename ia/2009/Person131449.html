Kevin Wing’s Summary

Kevin has a full range of experience in television and broadcast journalism. He is a news writer and producer at NBC Bay Area (KNTV/NBC Universal). He is also an occasional field producer for ABC News in Northern California. Since the late 1980s, he has worked as a news and feature reporter, anchor, news producer, assignment manager/editor, special projects producer, segment and field producer and news writer. During his career, he has worked in his native California, most notably in San Francisco. He has also worked in San Diego and Eureka. Kevin was with KTVU in Oakland for 11 years as an assignment editor and reporter. He has also worked at KRON and KGO-TV, both in San Francisco, and KNTV in San Jose. He was the No. 1-rated anchor at KIEM-TV in Eureka. His first full-time TV job was as an assignment editor for KFMB-TV in San Diego. Kevin is also executive producer and host of a TV and Podcast travel series, "Catch a Wave" (2010). He writes "Beach Trip" and "Suite Surrender", two travel columns for AARisings.com, a site showcasing up-and-coming Asian Americans, and is also a contributing writer of Silver Circle and Gold Circle profiles for the San Francisco/Northern California Chapter of the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences. Outside of broadcasting, Kevin has four years' experience as a public relations consultant. He's also authoring a children's book.

Kevin Wing’s Specialties:

Television news reporting, producing, writing, field producing, special projects producing, segment producing and anchoring. Also: public relations and voiceover experience.
