Amanda C. Peterson’s Summary

I believe that ideas can make a difference - with people and with profitability. Words are the cheapest, easiest way I know to get ideas from inside the brains within companies to inside the hearts of consumers. And that's the only way to truly accomplish something worth doing.

I've created advertising, naming, tag lines, nomenclature, brand strategy, brand voice strategies, messaging hierarchies, communications audits, marketing materials, packaging writing, websites, HTML code, corporate training and a coloring book to convince drunk college students they wanted ice cream after bar time.

Amanda C. Peterson’s Specialties:

I'm good at:
* Using words for brand development, brand repositioning, connecting with people and making communication work as hard as possible
* Presenting, lecturing, teaching and pitching – although you can always tell how odd I feel wearing a suit
* Conceptual and strategic development
* Wading through technical jargon, marketing buzzwords and tired cliches to find things actually worth saying
