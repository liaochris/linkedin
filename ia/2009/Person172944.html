Neil O'Keefe’s Summary

I began my career in Direct Marketing over 15 years ago after completing my MBA at Northeastern. I have experience with 3 companies that have a deep heritage rooted in direct marketing and catalog marketing. In each of these companies I have developed extensive marketing programs that have focused on loyalty, retail credit, and membership marketing. Each of these programs were fully integrated into the Internet platform and provide tremendous marketing opportunities.
