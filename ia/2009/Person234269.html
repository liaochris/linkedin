Tim Woda’s Summary

With 20 years of experience in direct, channel and call-center sales, sales management, customer retention, marketing and collaborative negotiation, Tim brings to buySAFE, Inc. a refined ability to implement and execute best-practice sales and business processes. Tim has been a leader on the buySAFE team since its official founding in 2003, and he currently serves as Vice President of Business Development & Sales. He has a successful track record of developing a deep understanding of a partner's business, their strategy and key initiatives and clearly identifying the business benefits of a proposed relationship.

Prior to buySAFE, Tim was the founder and managing member of The Telecom Recruiter, LLC, an executive search firm specializing in the telecommunications industry. He also held several sales and sales management positions with MCI Communications including Southeast Area Director where he managed several district sales teams and was inducted into the MCI Small Business Hall of Fame.

Tim is a member of The National Association of Sales Professionals and a Certified Professional Sales Person (CPSP).
