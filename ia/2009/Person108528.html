Jeffrey Leo’s Summary

LBS Financial Services is a multi-faceted independent world wide broker dealer. Our Main focus is to assist high net worth and corporate executive clients in diversifying their investment portfolios. We are constantly striving to locate quality diverse not only Financial Market Instruments but also a well diversified menu of Alternative Investments for our clients such as Middle Eastern real estate Investment Opportunities. At LBS our goal is to develop financial plans* to help accumulate, preserve and transfer wealth for our clients.

Working together with our clients, we develop clear, in-depth financial plans to help them work toward their financial goals. This involves understanding your goals and objectives, risk tolerance, investment time horizon and individual situations. After we have completed your financial plan, we can work with you to adjust your asset allocation or rebalance your portfolios according to market moves and your personal needs.

We offer financial planning services as an advisory service in our capacity as investment advisers. After the delivery of the financial plan, we can help implement your investment strategy in our capacity as investment advisors or broker-dealer, depending on your needs and the services selected
