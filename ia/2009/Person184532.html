Pedro A. Palicio, MBA, Ph.D.’s Summary

The mission of the International Division of Universal Wealth Managers LLC is to provide insurance brokerage and financial advisory services of the highest quality — mainly to its clients in Latin America and the Caribbean — based on its basic tenets of experience, trust, integrity and objectivity.
Our company has the best financial instruments in existence to bring our clients the impenetrable protection shield that all of us need. Health insurance and travel insurance protect us physically and economically from sudden and unexpected injuries and serious illnesses. Kidnap, ransom and extortion insurance protect us from very real and growing threats in today’s world. Life insurance protects our family from the devastating financial impact of a premature death. Investment plans allow us to pay for our children’s education, distribute our estate in a tax efficient manner and guarantee that we are not going to go through our old age without economic resources. Life settlement investments provide access to a highly-prized asset class which is the perfect alternative to stock market volatility and risk while providing very substantial earnings potential.
You will find our website www.insurance-investments-uwm.com as an excellent source of financial advice that you can carefully read in the privacy of your home or office, so you can determine whether or not the described financial products and services satisfy your financial goals, and if these solutions are within your budget.
If you have any question, or need clarification on any topic in our website, contact us by email at pedro@uwmanagers.com, by telephone at +1-305-665-4508 or via Skype at pedro.palicio and we guarantee that if we are not available at that time, you are going to hear from us within 24 working hours. We know that the success of our firm is in direct relation to the satisfaction that you experience from our products and services.

Pedro A. Palicio, MBA, Ph.D.’s Experience

  • President & CEO

    Universal Wealth Managers LLC

    (Financial Services industry)

    October 2005 — Present (3 years 9 months)

    Pedro graduated from prestigious Columbia University, in New York City, with Bachelor’s, Master’s and Doctoral degrees in Engineering and Applied Mathematics, and with an MBA with emphasis in Strategic and Financial Planning, obtaining all his degrees with the highest honors.
    Upon graduation, Pedro held executive positions of increasing responsibilities in the areas of Strategic and Financial Planning with major international corporations including IBM in Wall Street and in the headquarters of Ford and Ryder. Concurrently with the above positions, he served as Adjunct Professor in the faculties of Columbia University, in the City of New York, the University of Maryland and Barry University.
    In 1994, Pedro decided to apply his knowledge of Financial Planning and entered the financial services industry. From 1994 to 2005, he was one of the highest producers at the national and international levels, and was Regional Vice President for two of the major financial institutions of the world, working in their offices in the city of Miami.
    In 2005, Pedro established his own company, Universal Wealth Managers LLC in the city of Coral Gables. The purpose of its International Division is to advise its clients in Latin America and the Caribbean. The mission of the firm is to provide insurance brokerage and financial advisory services of the highest quality based on its basic tenets of experience, trust, integrity and objectivity.
    Pedro is fully bilingual in Spanish and English.
