    Nowhere Like Home

    (Hospital & Health Care industry)

    August 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Driven by the desire to assist seniors to live independently and safely in their own homes, as opposed to communal institutions, Nowhere Like Home was launched in December 2008.
