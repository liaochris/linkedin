David Carroll’s Summary

I've spent the last decade consulting as a professional software architect and developer, specializing in designing and implementing various enterprise solutions.

My latest passion is driving new entrepreneurial innovation in real estate technology. We've launched a new Web 2.0 based service partnering with real estate professionals to deliver a fresh new user experience that will captivate home buyers and reinforce their professional value. Search Connect is our flagship product providing an IDX property search engine designed to help agents promote themselves and their web sites with the appeal found in many of the large national property search engines and best of all – it's FREE.

Our team includes talented and sleep deprived code junkies, business guru's, savvy investors, all fueled with an endless supply of caffeine.
