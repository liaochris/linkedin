Steve Bradley’s Summary

Over 25 years working as a professional in the Cable Telecommunications Industry. A detailed individual with a keen understanding of taking ‘Big Picture’ projects from concept to fruition. I take a very business conscience approach to every project. I have a passion for very technical and complex projects and take pride in teamwork. I can bring new resources to your business and technologically harness your organization’s creative and intellectual capital. Harvesting, sharing and evaluating ideas throughout the enterprise. A successful project can become a windfall of competitive advantage that provides new revenue sources or efficiencies through more innovative products, services and strategies, continuously improved processes, reduced costs, and increased employee motivation.

Experience:
•Management – Experienced in Facilitating Strict Management of Capital and Operating Budgets, Very organized with excellent written and verbal skills.
•Digital Cable – Solid Background with Design and Operation of Digital Headends, Addressability and Billing system interfaces, Encoding, Multiplexing and Transport Architecture. Updated skills include SDV, Digital Simulcast, Ad-Splicing, VOD, HDTV, IP TV.
•HSD and VoIP – Experience with launching and overseeing operations of VoIP and High Speed Data services, multiple CMTS platforms, VoIP Soft Switches, Area Networks, Circuits. Capacity Management.
•Headend/Hub – Have Extensive Skills in Network Operations, Designing, Building, Signal Processing Centers, Electrical systems, HVAC. Digital Advertising, DOCSIS, Lasers, Combining. all aspects to the operation of a Headend.
•Network/Plant – Experience with Transport Systems, Design, Activation, Construction, Repair, GO-95, Fiber, Passive Optical Networks (PON).
•Computer Skills – Proficient with Controller Systems (multiple platforms such as S.A. and Motorola), billing systems, Windows, Unix, Excel, Access, Visio, Word.
