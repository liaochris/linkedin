Deborah Rath Howell’s Summary

I'm currently an on air host at 94.7 The Wave in Los Angeles; I also lend my voice to various commercials, promos and imaging for this smooth jazz station. I'm also hosting a 1 hour guest-heavy internet radio talk show for www.healthradio.net; interviewing physicians and health professionals and taking listener calls on a wide variety of health-related issues.

Owner/operator of Topanga Sound Studios (www.topangasoundstudios.com) in Malibu, California. Produce cost-saving voice over and audio projects for companies and individuals, narrate audio books and industrial scripts, make "Memory Movies" for individuals, and "Company Movies" for small businesses, host national and international radio shows, and voice promos for Radio & TV. For voice samples and demos, please visit:
http://voice123.com/Deborahhowell

Very happy hosting shows on 94.7 The Wave and HealthRadio.net while increasing my list of freelance voice over clients. Still doing two live shows on Tokyo FM every week like I've been doing since 1991!

I'm also doing lots of travel writing for www.tradetotravel.com --click on "The Voice of Rath" to see the articles, and am engaged in public speaking events on the topics of travel, voice overs and the state of Radio.

Deborah Rath Howell’s Specialties:

Excellent narrator, award-winning major market on-air radio personality and talk show host, published writer, in-demand public speaker, skilled maker of small movies for personal clients and small businesses. Examples of my writing and voice work can be accessed at www.topangasoundstudios.com in the links on the "about us" page.
