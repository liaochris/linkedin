Strategic marketer for smart energy products and services.

* Lead efforts to define markets, partners, competitors, policy instruments and social value chain partners.

* Craft and deliver company and product messaging as well as value chain and consumer education and outreach.

Climate change solutions require a building of political will, but they are ultimately technical. Providers will be entrepreneurial companies small and large, though the value chain will include foundations, non-profits, governments at every level and, at the base, citizen activism.
