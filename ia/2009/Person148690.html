Marcia Eichenauer’s Summary

King of Kings Streaming Media Network will address the root causes of the abandonment, abuse and sexual trafficking of children ~ including street children, with creative programming and mental healthcare and as a tool for new market entry. I am working cooperatively with the State Department, (including former managers), Helsinki Commission and faith based organizations.

I am building on all of my previous and now newly created networks. Tv Markiza, the TV Station I consulted to launch, helped to bring democracy to Slovakia. The State Dept. Program I generated, first of it's kind ever, has brought $14 million into Russia; children receive healthcare in their homes rather than being institutionalized. See Assistance to Russian Orphans info below.

Unusually creative and high level background in global public relations, following a very successful track record in communications and for the media, New York based. My strong passion and commitment is for the safety of the most vulnerable children, globally. I am using my high level background and skill in media/networking, globally, to bring creative solutions to this urgent, growing crisis.

Only with the most creative, committed people in many different industries and the grace of God is this possible. Please only contact me if you share this vision and passion, as well as FAITH.
