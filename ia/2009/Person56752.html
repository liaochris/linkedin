David Singh’s Summary

The Backbase Next-Generation Portal delivers unparalleled levels of customer personalization in unprecedented timeframes.

There is no One-Size-Fits-All solution for eBusiness applications – different customers have different needs, and they all expect individualized, personalized service.

Even though most organizations know valuable information and data about their customers, they are unable to leverage and translate that data into personalized user experiences. Customers who expect individualized services are disappointed by the impersonal experiences offered to them. Companies who do not deliver personalized experiences are unable to differentiate themselves from the competition.

The Backbase Next-Generation Portal enables organizations to offer highly-personalized solutions that delight customers with individualized user experiences. Profile-based personalization can be utilized to target branding, content, applications, and tools to each individual customer. Tailored guidance and expertise can be pushed to customers to build customer loyalty and be a differentiator from the competition. Customers can even personalize their own experience based upon specific needs and preferences.

Backbase Next Generation Portal can easily integrate with or replace existing systems. Existing, new, or third-party applications and content can be incorporated within the portal in minutes. Aggressive online marketing and eBusiness initiatives can be delivered in unprecedented timeframes. Entire portals can be implemented in weeks, not months.

With Backbase Next-Generation Portal, you are no longer forced to offer One-Size-Fits-All. Instead, you can provide My-Size-Fits-Me.

Global 2000 eCommerce and Online Self-Service applications built with Backbase technology currently serve millions of users. Customers include organizations such as ABN AMRO, Barclays, CIBC, General Motors, ING, Motorola, Vanguard, and Visa.
