Pedro Campos’s Summary

In the years immediately after I graduated from University, I worked in three consulting companies and gained experience with a wide range of industries and business challenges. I have since focused my interests on sales strategy for large consumer and services companies. I have worked in many countries with some of the largest consumer goods, information and processing companies in the world, and now my long-term aspiration is to become partner at a consulting firm and at the same time share my experience in Executive Boards of start up companies.
