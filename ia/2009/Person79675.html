David Mannl’s Summary

David Mannl is Head of Creative and co-founder of Mippin, which brings the web's best content to mobile devices.

Previously, David was a Senior Multimedia Designer at T-Mobile International in London. From 2002 he worked with Siemens Mobile and Macromedia, specializing in Flash for mobile devices.

David loves mobile design and technology, and takes pleasure in creating a great user experience on the small screen.

He is a Macromedia Max award winner, Nokia Developer of the Year and Forum Nokia Champion and Nokia Innovator of the Year.
