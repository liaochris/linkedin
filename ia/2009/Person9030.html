Alice Prabitz’s Summary

Do you manage individuals and wish to raise their capability?
Do you want to lead and/or negotiate more effectively?

Do you feel stuck and don´t know anymore what you want or who you want to be? Do you feel it´s time for some change?
Do you wish to move forward towards your goals NOW?

Then Alice Prabitz works with you or your company, in person or by telephone, in English and German,

helping you to move on and increase your energy, to take decisions and control of yourself and your work. Thus coaching takes you faster to where you want to be - with extraordinary results.

How does she do this? With gentleness and intuition, in a light and soft, and sometimes playful way, where surprises are normal. Alice has a great sensitivity to what people bring to her and maintains her respect of them.

She loves to work with all kinds of people, focussing on transition and life changes, career and leadership development (i.e. MARS, SAP, European Commission, Vanlanschot Bankiers).

Alice trained with Coaching Development, now preparing for accreditation by the International Coach Federation (ICF), and is in ongoing professional supervision.
