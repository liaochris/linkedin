Pam Older’s Summary

After 25 years in management at Esquire Magazine, Children's Television Workshop, The Dial Magazine, Time Magazine, and The New Yorker, I started something new- a design company specializing in private label and limited edition, handmade jewelry sold in boutiques, jewelry shops, museum shops and galleries. My line is carried in over 50 stores and boutiques across the U.S. and online at www.pamolderdesigns.com.

ABC's Desperate Housewives wore our jewelry and I was profiled in "O" Oprah Magazine in a feature article about how entrepreneurs realize their passion. My jewelry has been featured in Westchester Magazine, The Bridal Guide, Westchester Weddings and In The Country and Abroad.

The company's goal is to increase sales into larger stores, design under private label for catalog companies and to keep the creative process strong in my life.

Pam Older’s Specialties:

Pam Older Designs produces limited edition handmade artisan jewelry designed for jewelry stores, boutiques and museum shops.
