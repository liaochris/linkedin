Angela Jossy’s Summary

I am a cutting edge designer, finger-on-the-pulse-of-independent-music writer, passionate supporter of the arts and artists, singer/songwriter, guitar and piano player, community builder, master networker and extremely valuable business asset.

I have over seven years of experience with writing and graphic design for print and web. As an active community volunteer I've also gained valuable skills with organizing and promoting large events and festivals. Specific skills include news and feature writing, graphic design, website and newspaper design, public relations and entertainment booking.

Current goals include:
* Supporting my community
* Collecting new experiences
* Expressing my creativity
* Creating affluence
* Making a difference
* Inspiring others
* Creating a legacy of quality products and services

Angela Jossy’s Specialties:

Publications my work has appeared in include: Weekly Volcano, Puyallup Herald, Peninsula Gateway, Rockrgrl Magazine, Kaffeine Buzz Magazine, Victory Review Magazine, TacomaScene.com and Fort Lewis Ranger.

My leadership skills are demonstrated by my work with the online organization I founded seven years ago called Happy Friday Club - a vital resource for musicians, nightclubs, booking agents and all other music business workers in the Seattle, Tacoma and Olympia area of Washington State.
