    CNAM-Paris

    (Higher Education industry)

    November 2008 — December 2008 (2 months)

    CNAM-Paris offers Professional Experience Accreditation. This system enables candidates to validate a whole degree offered by CNAM-Paris, or one part of it, thanks to their professional experience.

    Candidates have to fill in a specific application form and describe one or several professional activities related to their Master’s programme. Candidates will then have to defend their work in front of a committee made up of professionals and academics.

    The Professional Experience Validation process has four steps:
    1. First contact with CNAM-Paris, meeting, sharing of information
    2. First application form, pre-decision
    3. Help/ coaching to complete the final application form
    4. Interview with the PEV committee
    5. Decision of the jury. The jury can decide to validate the whole degree or just one part of it. In this case, the applicant can be asked to write an additional piece of work such as a report, or to enrol in a specific training.
