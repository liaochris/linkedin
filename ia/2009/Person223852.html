Steve Felson’s Summary

I founded Empowered Hospitality Solutions, Inc. to make a difference in the lives of those who have chosen hospitality as their profession. Not a difference like a slogan or an idea, but a significant, lasting difference in people's lives and businesses.

What do we do?

Simply, Empowered Hospitality Solutions, Inc. helps puts passion and profit back into people's business. By implementing well crafted systems and long range plans that meet both growth and profitability needs, we empower fledgling companies to be successful and transform successful businesses into wildly successful organizations.

How do we do it?

First, we get to know you and your business...why you chose to open the restaurant; what your passion is; who you want to be in the business community. Next, we craft a personalized plan based on your individual needs (no cookie cutter programs used over and over). Then, we work hand in hand with your people to develop and empower them to achieve long range results. Our goal is for you NOT to need us anymore.

Best of all, the work we do is always GUARANTEED. We're not done until you say we're done. There's no extra charge to get it right. We keep working with you until YOU say we have delivered on the promise of our work.

We have a full range of services to meet the needs of your organization:

*Profitability Analysis
*System Creation and Implementation
*People Systems (Onboarding, training, development)
*Marketing Plans that increase market share, not just sales
*Stand alone referral marketing program
*Compliance Audits and/or Secret Shopper Programs
*Motivational speaker for your next national conference or local manager or staff meeting
*Many other custom solutions to meet the needs of your business

We look forward to assisting you in realizing outstanding results in your business and living the life you always dreamed of. It's our privilege to be your partner.
