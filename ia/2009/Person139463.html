Leilani Shimoda’s Summary

I am taking on various freelance projects while interviewing and trying to find a full-time position. I enjoy designing for both men and women and love to be involved in the initial research and design concept and all aspects of design from creative to technical.

For the past 5 years, I have been designing for both men and women within all categories. From creative concept and research to the technical details of making a garment into a product, I hand sketch and CAD, tech pack, spec, develop graphics, and have strong knowledge of garment construction as well as MAC and PC computer experience.

Most recently, I designed and tech packed an entire collection of men’s and women’s contemporary outerwear from start to finish. Other then designing, my responsibilities have included conducting fittings, providing specs, participating in fabric meetings, communicating with factories overseas and working closely with the production manager for a new label.

Leilani Shimoda’s Specialties:

Illustrator, Photoshop, MAC/PC, technical and hand drawn flats and Illustration, import/domestic manufactuing, specs, tech packs, graphic design, storyboards, trend reports.
