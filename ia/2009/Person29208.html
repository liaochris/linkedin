B. Janell Grenier’s Summary

An employee benefits and ERISA attorney with 20+ years of experience assisting employers of all sizes with their benefits needs, including a state government with 40,000 employees, hospitals, multi-national and publicly-held corporations, as well as tax-exempt organizations.

B. Janell Grenier’s Specialties:

Retirement plans, including 401(k), defined benefit, defined contribution, cash balance, and 403(b), Also, government and health care organization plans, including 457(b) and 457(f) plans. Health plans (both fully insured and self-insured), retiree medical plans, VEBAs, MEWAs, flexible compensation programs including 125 Plans, HSAs, HRAs, consumer-directed health plans. Experience in assisting ERISA fiduciaries in plan governance. Mergers and acquisitions.
