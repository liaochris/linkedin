Joe Montanez’s Summary

17 years of software engineering experience using leading-edge technologies and programming languages to develop government and commercial enterprise-class systems. Extensive experience developing and deploying critical client-server and multi-tier solutions for Windows, UNIX and Linux platforms for very large user populations. Proven development experience in all tiers of enterprise-class systems including device drivers, persistence, business logic, and presentation.

Emphasis on delivering use-case-driven, pattern-oriented, best-practice, and reusable solutions that address architectural concerns of scalability, failover, extensibility, maintainability and performance. Responsible and results-oriented technical leadership experience with various team sizes (ranging from 5 – 12) and various team configurations (local and distributed team members) using various development methodologies (RAD, Rational Unified Process, and Extreme Programming). Excellent people, communication and mentoring skills.
