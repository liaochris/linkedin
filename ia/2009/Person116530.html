John Agno’s Summary

As a former corporate executive, business owner and recovering management consultant, Agno understands that in the business world, we don't speak much about the heart. Yet, since all businesses are ultimately people serving people, our life's work should come from the heart. Long after your products or services have been delivered, the feelings and knowledge shared during the business relationship remain.

Coach Agno believes that people need connection, belonging and meaningful contribution. They need to know that their life and work matter. Many people find that personal or career transformation is an excellent time to better understand their life purpose and direction. Coming from the heart makes sense in life and in business.

He holds the designation of Certified Executive Coach (CEC) by the Worldwide Association of Business Coaches (the highest designation available to business coaches) and is a Certified Business Coach (CBC) by the International Consortia of Business Coaches.

A past member of the International Coach Federation (ICF), member of the National Association of Business Coaches and a founding member of CoachVille, he is also the founder of the Coach to Coach Network, a virtual community of over 1,300 personal and business coaches from around the world. He enjoys working with coaches, executives, professionals and management teams to help them flourish in their professional and personal lives.

John Agno’s Specialties:

John Agno coaches executives and business owners on how to build their leadership capability. As a certified executive and business coach, he works with men and women who are disappointed with their leadership development, who struggle with energizing, exciting and coaching their employees, who are worried about increasing corporate productivity and frustrated with the complexity of doing business in today's global economy.
