Lisa M. Englert’s Summary

As a Virtual Assistant, I reduce tasks for entrepreneurs, business owners and independent professionals so they can focus on their core business. With 20+ years of experience as an administrative professional, I provide outsourced administrative/business support services -- with expertise in public relations, marketing and communications -- without needing to be physically present in my clients' offices. Because I work with my clients via phone, Internet, fax and e-mail, they can be across the street or across the globe.

My client base is small, which allows us to work in long-term, collaborative partnerships. And since I'm not on my clients' payroll, they don't have to provide me with office space, purchase extra equipment, or pay employee benefits or taxes. My fees are arranged exclusively on a retainer basis. For their investment, my clients receive 100% productive time and all of my vast talents and skills acquired from being in "Corporate America."
