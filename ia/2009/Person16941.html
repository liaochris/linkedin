Ann Cummings’s Summary

Having been a full-time professional REALTOR for over 25 years, I've certainly gained priceless experience working with both buyers and sellers here in the coastal areas of New Hampshire and Maine. I'm a firm believer in utilizing today's technology to make buying and selling real estate as smooth and efficient as the process can be, and I also believe in having fun and enjoying working together as well. If you can't enjoy what you're doing, why bother?!?

I have a proven track record of success helping many clients buy and sell real estate here in the Portsmouth area, and I'd love to help you out with your real estate needs as well. Be sure to check out my websites and my blog for real estate for sale in both NH and Maine, as well as great local area information, market information, and more. My blog is updated regularly with local information, so I hope you'll enjoy reading that - subscribe to it or bookmark it!

Looking forward to helping you reach your real estate goals!

Ann Cummings’s Specialties:

Residential real estate - homes, condos, and multi-family properties. Relocation for both buyers and sellers, as well as corporate clients. Land sales and some commercial real estate as well. I love utilizing today's technology to make real estate sales more efficient and to make marketing much more effective for broader exposure.
