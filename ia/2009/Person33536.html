Brian Klecker’s Summary

Welcoming the changes in the different seasons of our lives. Married to my best friend. We met Ballroom Dancing of all things in Charleston SC. That chance incounter changed my life.

Tracy and I've started several businesses, sold a few of them, house flipped for a while while the flipping was good. Now we're settling down and begining to enjoy the empty nesting years. A good game of Chicken Foot with our closest friends from our Church. Or having the grandkids up for a week or two during the summer to swim off the dock, do a bit of wave running or pulling them on Big Mabel. Summer is our favorite time of year.

Lately, Tracy and I have been getting back to the ballroom dancing once or twice a week for lessons or the dance party. Now we've got 10 of or friends going with us and we are all having a blast. It's also not a bad way to get a bit of exercise.

We also love our walks with our Boarder Collies, both Black & Whites, named Joleen and Savanna. I guess the empty nesting causes you to think of your dogs as kids but we really love 'em.

This year is my big expansion year for my main business, www.KidMusic.com. With several new music collections in development (a new Christian CD and a Christian Lullaby CD), they're looking better than any of our current collections. We're also adding some other CD collections from our partners at Disney. Plus were adding a personalized sippy cup line and a personalized drink bottle line. My prayer is that the Lord blesses me with wise decisions that will allow our business to be a blessing to many children.

Pervious Degree is a BS in Mechanical Engineering from Tri-State University. I spent many years as a Design Engineer but now I focus mostly on Professional Business Development and Web Development
