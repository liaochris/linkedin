John Donaldson’s Summary

33 years at Teradyne. The last years were mostly in Program and Project management. For many years I was a Memory Test Applications Engineer, and I also spent many years in Software Engineering writing mostly GUI SW. I developed at least one successful major software utility and one successful major hardware instrument while at Teradyne.

At the moment I am semi retired and enjoying it. I am working part time work at BancBridge. I am also running two small projects at my church and expanding my activity in that area.

I am helping to organize customer and corporate data at BancBridge. This company is largely staffed by software engineers in India. Communications and easy access to company data is important as the business is growing rapidly.

John Donaldson’s Specialties:

Program Management seems to be my strength. I have accreditation in the Program Management Institute as a Program Management Professional. I am skilled in organizing groups of engineers at the beginning phase of projects. I have extensive experience in planning and executing multi-site multi-discipline projects. I grasp concepts quickly and my experience need not be limited to the semiconductor test fields.
