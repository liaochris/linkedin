Allison Jones’s Summary

I am a passionate young professional committed to educational equality. From years of working in a variety of educational settings--from classrooms in charter schools to correctional programs and South African townships--my professional experience includes educational project design and implementation, curriculum development, fundraising, and program management.

I am also dedicated to strengthening the nonprofit sector by not only learning more about the challenges the field faces, but also by providing solutions. I am actively engaging the next generation of non profit leaders through my blog, Entry Level Living while working closely with current leaders on better shaping the image of nonprofits.

I am eager to connect with people who share my passion for educational equality and my desire to acquire and sharpen the skills necessary to thrive in the nonprofit sector.

Allison Jones’s Specialties:

curriculum development, community liaison, blogging, writing at the secondary, collegiate, and professional levels, program outreach, program assessment
