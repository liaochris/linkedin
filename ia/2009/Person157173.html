Michael Andrew Wilson’s Summary

Before establishing Paris-based Wilson & Associates in 1995, Michael Wilson’s career covered a wide range of entrepreneurial activities.

Moving to Paris in 1989, he worked, for five years as an Interior Design Project Manager for Polo Ralph Lauren where he helped to plan and open more than 60 Polo stores and showrooms across Europe. This background gave him a unique experience purchasing art and antiques in every European market. With these qualifications, he founded Wilson & Associates to provide exclusive services for people looking to buy antiques, art, decorative furnishings, contemporary design and other rare objects.

Known for his broad knowledge in Decorative Arts and impeccable sense of style, he attracted a prestigious international clientele of noted business and entertainment figures as well as some of the word’s most important collectors. In addition to working with individual clients, Michael Wilson has worked on design projects for companies including: Hermes and Van Cleef & Arpels.

Through its extensive repertoire of resources and industry contacts the company, which offers professional art and antiques broker services, personal buying guides and exclusive VIP services, has earned a solid reputation with clients and dealers for expertise, professionalism and discretion.

Michael Wilson’s on-going research on the history and development of style formed the basis for his course, "Furniture Design and Style" at the IESA (Institute Arts Superior) in Paris and his photo archive, consisting of over 70,000 photographs is a resource for scholars and students. He also continues to expand his video archive of over 160 hours of the major European art and antiques fairs including: Paris’s Biennale des Antiquaires , Maastricht’s TAAF. In addition to providing his clients with expert services for these events, he specializes in visiting the secret locations, warehouses, and private collections not open to the public.

Michael Andrew Wilson’s Specialties:

Searching for, negotiating and purchasing European art, antiques and 20th century design. Our goal is to help our clients make informed purchase decisions, while also offering personalized services that include: personally guided buying trips throughout the markets of Europe, sourcing, expert appraisals, restoration, and shipping and delivery coordination. In short, everything our clients need to be assured of the most knowledgeable, reliable, and pleasurable collecting experience.
