    Leviton

    (Privately Held; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    September 2004 — March 2009 (4 years 7 months)

    Leviton is an international leader in electrical components and systems. The Lighting Management Systems business unit provides performance lighting control and fixtures for use in theatre and television. It also provides relay, occupancy sensor and control systems intended for energy management in architectural and commercial applications.

    DESCRIPTION OF POSITION
    Logistics coordinator of product and system quotations for four major product lines, order processing, coordination with production and shipping, completion of customs documentation, compliance with regulations in both U.S. and receiving countries, negotiation with sales developers, agents and distribution network in all foreign markets.
    • Streamlined and standardized international order intake, product fabrication and staging, as well as shipping processes over 3 month period, improving staff efficiency by 50%.

    • Produced 4 separate ISO 9001 Work Instructions for position, standardizing procedure for both internal and external procedures, improving staff efficiency by more than 25%.

    • Created over a 3-month period and then maintained ISO 9001 controlled document templates, improving staff efficiency by nearly 25%.

    • Created step-by-step description of process for overseas distributors, eliminating confusion and reducing customer complaints by 60% and product returns by over 30%.

    • Worked with Director of International Business Development and Vice President of International Sales to develop a consistent international sales program.

    • Developed training program and centralized archiving.

    Notable project: National Printing Bureau, Quebec, Canada
    • Broad use of graphic user interface (GUI) control of 3 floors and basement in a large application requiring over 2000 relays in more than 80 cabinets.

    • Coordination and proactive effort to deploy needed equipment to jobsite in under 60 days, less than 50% of standard lead time.
