Greg Barman’s Summary

I'm a Technical Recruiter ("headhunter" is okay). I find great, talented tech people and have done so for a variety of industries such as Civil/Environmental Engineering, Information Technology/software, Telecom, Broadcasting, Aviation and others. Most recently I recruited in Hydropower/dams, renewable energy. I'm a longtime member of the Colorado Technical Recruiters Network (CTRN), the nation's largest statewide tech recruiting organization, was recently on the Board of Directors and PR/Spokesman for the group. Previously I had a successful career in the Radio-TV and news media business where I was actively involved as a Journalist, on-air Newscaster, Reporter and Editor. And I started as a disc jockey! imagine that...

Greg Barman’s Specialties:

Full lifecycle Recruiting, Sourcing/Headhunting; Information Technology, Telecom, Radio-TV-Satellite Broadcasting, Aviation, civil/environmental engineering, Hydropower/dams.
