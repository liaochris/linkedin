Suraj Hattangadi’s Summary

Suraj Hattangadi has 16 years of work experience. Of this, the last 12 years have been invested in the Internet related space and prior to that, in the advertising business as a copywriter and visualizer.

As a Certified Usability Analyst (CUA) and owner of FIRSTusable, Suraj's usability consulting expertise sees him consult for clients both, in India as well as across the globe.

Suraj's usability firm FIRSTusable (http://www.FIRSTusable.com) services the usability requirements of many firms large and small, including Microsoft, MSN, Akamai, Yahoo!, Google, SAP Labs and many other SME IT and non IT firms.

Suraj Hattangadi’s Specialties:

Heuristic Evaluations; Information architecture, GUI (Graphical User Interface) design, usability, branding, online strategy, web design, e-media; product innovation and ideation.
