Mark Hedley’s Summary

Mark is a global nomad (aka geo-neutral). He is internationally educated having studied in the UK, Australia and the USA and is an MBA graduate from a leading US business school.

Mark has lived and worked for extended periods in Europe and North America, but calls the Asia Pacific region home; from both a physical and cultural perspective. He truly believes that this is the Asian Century, and is doing his best to make sure he plays a major part in it.

Mark has significant cross-functional experience in complex, dynamic and often extremely challenging environments (think first expatriate in the relocation industry in India in the 1990s). He performs best when time is short, and the challenge is great, and dealing with ambiguity and complex relationships is just part of what makes his life interesting.

He likes to think he is altruistic, but in reality he is a big believer in karma. He enjoys helping connect people and work with them to solve problems simply because he derives enormous satisfaction from moving himself, and others forward.
