Frank Glaug’s Summary

Championing product implementation (from concept to commercialization), intellectual properties, creating innovation roadmap (new & improved products), identifying new business opportunities for organic growth, product evolution through creativity and patent estate. Led product development for Retail Incontinence (1996 - 2003), which grew from $50 to $200 MM and included a "private label" Protective Underwear product line (ASSURANCE) that gained more market share than the leading national brand at Wal*Mart. Also, led product development for Clinical Incontinence (1998 - 2003), which had sales over $200 MM and was the first to introduce the Hook & Loop Cloth-like Adult Brief (Wings HL) in the US.

Frank Glaug’s Specialties:

Creative Leadership (55 issued patents), Innovative Product Development (customized & unique product designs), Legal Representation (deposed twice and winning both cases), Track-Record of New Product Introductions (Protective Underwear, Male Guards, Supreme Adult Briefs, Bladder Control Pads, Millennium Diapers, Pantyliners, Thin Maxipads, Training Pants, etc.). Also devised "next generation" product designs (higher efficiency & optimized executions) for market leadership.
