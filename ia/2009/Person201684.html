Rob Nesvacil’s Summary

Graphic Designer with 13 years' experience in concept development, project management, production and execution, plus internal and client-related communication and reporting for both print and electronic subjects. Direct engagement in multi-channel B2B marketing strategies for a diverse range of industries including pharmaceutical, manufacturing, high tech, real estate, financial, educational, and not-for-profit, among others.

Rob Nesvacil’s Specialties:

Branding and Identity, Graphic Design, Print Collateral, Online Media, E-mail Marketing, B2B Marketing, Adobe Creative Suite (Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign), QuarkXPress, Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Powerpoint, Excel)
