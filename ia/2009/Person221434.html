Shankar Saikia’s Summary

I am an enterprise applications software solution architect. My key skills are the ability to:
(1) understand needs of specific industries in the areas of customer acquisition and retention (customer relationship management or CRM) and customer demand planning and fulfillment (supply chain management or SCM)
(2) explain complex topics such as financial budgeting, social media marketing, demand/sales forecasting and supply chain management
(3) convince businesses to invest in enterprise software

Over the last 10 years I have developed relationships with over 150 US-based customers in the following industries:
- high technology (semiconductors, computer hardware, consumer electronics)
- consumer (CPG/FMCG, retail, specialty retail)
- life sciences (pharmaceuticals, biotech, medical devices).

I have worked in a pre-sales (sales consultant) capacity for two (2) early stage companies (Extricity, Icarian) and for a major enterprise software vendor (Oracle). I have co-founded two companies (The Baxley Group and Highlander Systems).

At present I am a sales and marketing consultant to US-based tech companies in India.
