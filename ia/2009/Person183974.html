Pavan Kumar Madamsetty’s Summary

Pavan Madamsetty has over four years of business experience in the consumer goods industry, including a role on the corporate marketing team of South India's leading retailer. During his tenure in this position, he collaborated with leading consumer electronics companies such as LG, Samsung, Sony, and Whirlpool to develop marketing strategies and sales promotions aimed at increasing revenues and the customer base. His areas of expertise include industry and market analysis, brand building, product pricing and management, and sales promotions.

At Thunderbird, he is the Vice President of Marketing for the Thunderbird Toastmasters Club. Under his leadership the club achieved a distinguished status from Toastmasters International. He is also an active member of Thunderbird Marketing Association, Brand Management Club and THINC (Thunderbird Innovators Circle).

Currently interning with Aginsky Consulting Group, a mid-sized management consulting firm based in Portland, Oregon, he works on projects involving new market entry and global business development for mid-sized companies.

Pavan is currently seeking a full-time position in marketing strategy for new market entry and global business development.
