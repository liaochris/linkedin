Mitchell Elutovich’s Summary

Mitchell had 5 years experience with IBM in various roles: Key Architect, Project Leader, Designer, Coder, Instructor, Technical Consultant, Tool Architect.

His experience includes designing and implementing solutions using C++ and Java, plus many additional languages. He is recognized for his advanced problem solving and debugging abilities.

He recently assisted a company convert an excel document of products into a Java based web store.

He is currently refreshing his skills and self-learning more about Java EE, JSP, PHP, Spring(IoC&DI, AOP, MVC) , Hibernate, iBATIS, Eclipse, Agile(Test-Driven Development(TDD), Refractoring), Design Patterns, OOD, C++, and Efficent software application development.

Additional, he can be beneficial for a company in a consulting, architect, and/or designer role. For examples, helping a company escape from monstrous programs which are hard to maintain and evolve, companies' which need to integrate multiple software systems together, revising systems which were produced with a poor design.
