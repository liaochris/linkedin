Anna Anisin’s Experience

  • Founder

    Baseball Beauties, Inc.

    (Internet industry)

    March 2008 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    Baseball Beauties is your ticket to brains, beauty, and baseball -- year-round! This fan-based social networking website, encourages women, who enjoy and/or want to learn more about local and national games to create profiles, join other members in their local area to attend games, learn more about the sport, track their favorite teams, and receive team news, scores, and events tailored to their own fandom. Incorporating user-generated content, the website allows members to blog about their baseball-related adventures, provide carpooling and other game-related assistance, create special events and invite other members, share photos, shop at the Baseball Beauties online store, and access great resources and books about America's favorite past-time, beyond the regular season. With baseball's rich history, Baseball Beauties will also honor the great icons, like Babe Ruth and Joe DiMaggio, in cultural references to films, books, and art events, and feature creative member participation in 1950s pin-up style photos of our member-beauties.
