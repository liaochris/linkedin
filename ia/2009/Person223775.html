Stephen Roberts, MD’s Summary

Recent founder of Protanza Corporation, a marketing-driven consumer healthcare company. Protanza’s novel OTC medication safely and rapidly eliminates the pain and inflammation associated with many chronic and acute ailments - including migraine, arthritis and other musculoskeletal disorders. We hope to improve life for millions.

Recent founder of c/it/2/believe/it - a direct-to-consumer marketing and product development company with an emphasis on infomercials. Previous founder, chairman and CEO of GelStat Corporation.

Physician turned investment banker turned entrepreneur. Substantial experience in management, operations, corporate governance, new product development, consumer packaged goods, marketing, retail and finance. Inventor & creative thinker.

Substantial past involvement and continued interest in finance, especially as regards new ventures.
