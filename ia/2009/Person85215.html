George Miller’s Summary

A strategic and creative business developer who operates across B2B, B2C & Public Sector; extensive international experience; possesses strong management, commercial and communication abilities; a proven track record of delivering innovative business development programmes targeted at board level management; experience gained through senior team leadership positions in highly regarded IT services, strategy consulting and advertising firms.

Currently leading a collaborative business development programme which includes BT's strategic systems integration, applications and technology partners.

The focus of this group is to support BT's top Private & Public Sector clients as seek more efficient & effective ways to do business, through enabling their migration to virtual business infrastructure & cloud computing.
