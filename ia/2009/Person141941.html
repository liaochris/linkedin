Lisa Cooper’s Summary

Owner/Partner of new visionary Art Gallery dedicated to finding and sharing amazing art from around the world.

I am taking over 24+ years of advertising, interactive marketing, direct/CRM marketing and public relations experience to promote an area that I love and that can help make this a better world.

We believe that art can help heal and transform lives by interacting with it, living with it and creating it. We also believe in giving back and donate 5% of all our sales back to charities that help the underserved communities - children and their families - heal through art.
