Brian Smith’s Summary

Th(INC), LLC --- The Innovation Company, is a multi-disciplinary professional services firm, working with Nonprofits and NGOs, Chambers of Commerce and Economic Development agencies, Small Businesses and Entrepreneurs.

Trained in organizational assessment by Peter F. Drucker Foundation; economic & community development at the Penn Center, SC; entrepreneurial education and business planning by the Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation.

Brian Smith’s Specialties:

Fundraising; strategic business planning; financing and capitalization for new ventures; marketing; writing; organizational assessment; community development; staff and board training.
