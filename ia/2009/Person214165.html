Seth Myers, MA, LPC’s Summary

Seth E. Myers, MA, LPC is a specialized clinician and accomplished business professional. He is a private practitioner and arbiter of clinical programs. Mr. Myers' work as a psychotherapist has allowed him to partner with physicians and establish a positive reputation in the medical community. His focus as a provider is based on a medically-managed treatment model for addiction and chronic pain. Mr. Myers provides consultative and emergency psychological services to medical staff in hospitals and adjunct facilities throughout North Texas.
