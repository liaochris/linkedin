Per J. Håkansson’s Summary

Per Håkansson is a seasoned web executive and entrepreneur; currently advising several consumer web startups on business and product strategy.

Previously, he lead the product development at the internet darling Blurb; spend 6+ years at Yahoo! on product management for the media, communications and e-commerce products across the world (UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, India, China, Taiwan, South Korea, Japan, Brazil and Australia); started the internet publishing subsidiary at Icon Medialab and, started and lead interactive media at Universum (worldwide leader in employer branding).

He has also worked for the United Nations on projects in Zambia and as a stringer for business magazines in Europe. He is educated in Sweden, Spain, Switzerland and the US with a focus on business, economics, journalism and technology, and speaks several languages.

At Yahoo! he was nominated for the Superstar Award two years in a row and won the Webby award while at Blurb. Per was profiled in Fast Company in 1997.
