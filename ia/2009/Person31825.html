Brad Burns’s Summary

I am currently the business development division of Ittner Architects. I completed my Executive Master's of International Business at Saint Louis University (May 2009). I graduated from the University of Missouri – Columbia in 2002 with an Interdisciplinary Studies Bachelors Degree, emphasizing Marketing, Psychology and Sociology. Prior to my current position at Ittner I worked in commercial real estate finance for a nationwide firm. I have also owned and operated a small residential finance firm and currently own a commercial real estate holdings company.
