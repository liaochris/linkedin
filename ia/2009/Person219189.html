Scott & Laurie Prindle’s Summary

We are Scott & Laurie Prindle and live in the beautiful Jackson Hole area (Yellowstone and Teton National Parks).

Scott is our online voice, but we are very much a couple who loves being together.

We pastor together, work together and play together. We live in a place geared to the outdoor lifestyle, so the freedom to take advantage of all that is offered is one of our core values.

That freedom is also vital to our church ministry. As pastors we want to be available when needed.

Our business is for people who like helping people and is also another avenue of ministry for us. We call what we do in business "Marketplace Ministry". It is the reality that God uses businesses to reach and serve people.

We distribute patented, life changing nutriceuticals worldwide and show others how to do the same. We have helped others earn hundreds of thousands of dollars in income by showing them how to help others.

When not working or traveling, we are playing in the mountains. We love to ski, bike, hike, sunbathe, jog or whatever else we can do in the Tetons. Laurie is a musician who enjoys worship.

We have raised three awesome boys and are now "empty nesters". We feel it's time to invest in other people and help them get what they want in life. We know what we want will take care of itself.
