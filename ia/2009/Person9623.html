Al Krueger’s Summary

Comet Branding is passion, creativity, strategy, execution and value fused to create bright work, great results, high returns per marketing dollar and above all — happier clients.

Comet is a transparent, professional, driven, responsive, hard working, open-minded, results-oriented and talented branding + public relations agency that serves our clients with the utmost integrity and respect and treats their business and success as our own. We believe client-agency relationships should be easy for the client.

This agency has been launched because of a desire to create inspired work and treat clients the way they deserve to be treated — no spin, no bs, no ego.

By working with Comet Branding companies will win bigger and ﬁnd greater successes.
