Grahame Maher’s Summary

* Origional background as an Entrepeneur in a range of business ventures in Aust
* Sold several of the communications busineses to various parties including Vodafone
* Joined Vodafone in Australia to help Restructure the Australian MVNO operations and operated as CEO of Vodacall in Aust for 3 years
* Was part of the Vodafopne team that negotiated to buy Vodafopnes first International business in New Zealand and became the CEO of NZwhere we grew fom a 17% marketshare to 51% share over 3 years.
* Then returned to Australia to lead the turnaround of the struggling Aust business to avoid it being sold. As CEO Aust and COO Asia Pacific, achieved first ever cash positive result in Aust, made major reduction in costs and improved growth, resulting in a 300% profit improvement
* Went to Sweden to help answer the future for our struggling business and negotiated the first ever sale of a Vodafone OPCO at 13 times EBITDA
* Moved to Czech Republic to integrate Oskar into Vodafone as CEO of Vodafone Czech and became the best Number 3 operator in the Vodafone group and had the best profit growth globally.
* Then took the challenge to set up our first GCC based business in Qatar that was also out first start up business in 10 years as we build a Middle East footprint.
