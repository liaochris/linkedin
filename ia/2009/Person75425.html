Erin Fitzgerald’s Summary

Erin is always looking for animation cohorts. She loves getting to use her full vocal range over the length of a project. She has voiced lead roles as well as hundreds of additional voices to animated series, anime, and video games. She loves developing a character from the very beginning of a project like she's getting to do with Andy's Airplanes and she enjoys the spontenaiety of coming up with something wild right on the spot. She is a consumate professional and her positive attitude and energy is a pleasure to work with. She has a knack for ADR and admits the excitement of nailing it in the first pass, keeps her going back for more.

and thanks to my mom for writing this. lol!
