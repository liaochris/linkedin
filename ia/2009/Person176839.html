Norma Jean Crippen’s Summary

Diverse professional with more than 15 years recruiting and human resources work in the game, technology and media industries. A seasoned recruiter, experienced at effectively recruiting at all levels. I have successfully closed the right candidate in a vast array of positions, including information technology, accounting, finance, operations, marketing and entertainment software. My hands-on experience includes benefits administration, employee relations, new hire orientation, performance evaluation and career guidance. However my passion and expertise is in Recruiting. I got my start as a game industry Recruiter at Origin Systems in Austin. After six years in the games industry I jumped into a completely different culture and joined Dell Financial Services for another six years before going independent and full-circle right back into games, joining my old Origin colleagues Mary-Margaret Walker and Robin McShaffry at Mary-Margaret Network.
