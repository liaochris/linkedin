Kris Hardy’s Summary

I am the President and Application Integration Evangelist at Submerged Solutions, LLC. We are an Agile Software Development and Enterprise Application Integration firm, focusing on the insurance, health care, manufacturing, and e-retail industries.

We understand the dynamic nature of business, and that software requires constant change to keep up-to-date with your business. We are experienced at managing change, dealing with unclear requirements, and working closely with our clients and customers to deliver software and solutions that fit their businesses.

We believe that today's businesses must be agile in order to succeed, and ours is no different. We use Agile development processes (including XP, Test-Driven Development, Iterative and Incremental Development, and Scrum where appropriate) to deliver solutions on time and on budget, providing the greatest value as quickly as possible.

I am a career Project Manager with 8 years experience in project management, military acquisitions, software development and chemical research and development, working with Fortune 500 firms throughout the United States.
