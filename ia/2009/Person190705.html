Peter Stam’s Summary

During the last 7 years I treated and collected over 900 cases in my private practice and incompany. The written experience-based evidence (VAS-pain scales on a daily basis) with photomaterial, made before and after treatment, formed the basis for a unique treatment concept, Caretaping. Almost 400 cases were directly reported to the medical officer in charge. These cases where at the end of treatment evaluated and reported with a 20-question evaluation.
The outcome of my therapeutic interventions had an tremendous positive effect on the morbidity and reintegration of workers within the company. Extending my consulting hours also in the factory made an early diagnostic process and early treatment possible.

I strongly belief that working incompany as a clinical physiotherapist gives me the opportunity to be closest to the experience of my patients (work-related) and to expect the unexpected. For me the body (and its mind) is the greatest work of art. Just learn to listen carefully to its messages is a unique tool to accelerate recovery, in my studies 3 times faster than most regular medical (therapeutic) interventions offer. It's the story behind the story that makes you want to listen, offering support on a daily basis with Caretaping.
