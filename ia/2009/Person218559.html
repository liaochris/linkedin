Stephen Scott’s Summary

A proven Internet entrepreneur with over 25 years experience in high technology sales and business development.

Since 1996, when he build his first website hosting company from the ground up on a few PC’s in his home office, Stephen has focused his entrepreneurial efforts on the Internet as a technology developer and business executive. By the way, that first company grew to over 2,800 hosted website accounts and 22,000 annual domain name registrations.

Utilizing his expert programming, website design, online marketing and database design/management skills, Stephen later went on to develop InterNetSites.com; a multi-channel Publisher network that represented over 200 online merchants. Collectively, the publisher network generated annual affiliate revenues in excess of $15 million among 12 market verticals.

Over the years many of Stephen’s friends, family members and colleagues have asked him for advice on how to improve their website performance. As a result, Stephen conceived and developed SiteAchieve.com, a new and innovative resource that provides free expert advice on how to attract more visitors to your web site.

While SiteAchieve.com is a non-commercial site (you won’t find any advertisements on the site), Stephen couldn’t resist his desire to build a truly unique and useful resource that promotes ‘community’ among website owners. To that end he used his extensive programming expertise to add peer-to-peer website review and link exchange programs.

Stephen not only understands the business aspects of emerging Internet technologies and the synergies between them, but he also maintains a deep understanding of the underlying technology platforms upon which they are built. It is this unique combination of business and technology acumen that gives Stephen the ability to effectively ‘connect the dots’ to drive innovation and revenue growth.
