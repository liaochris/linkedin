Trevor Smith’s Summary

Have you experienced the ULTIMATE LEAD GENERATOR? Well if not, how would you like to use an inexpensive, scalable lead-generation solution for your company, which can provide you with unlimited inbound calls directly to your sales floor?

With years of experience offering Voice Broadcast technologies for the mortgage, auto, insurance, debt and other “lead driven” industries, we have a proven track record. We are confident our campaign will work for you.

Using a professional, pre-recorded, emotionally charged message to potential clients, when they pick up their phone, they hear and like the offer, they Press 1 on their telephone and are transferred directly to your sales floor. Your sales force can then gather the pertinent information and proceed with your sales process. We also provide the option to drive the call to our call center. We will pre-screen the call before our marketers transfer the call directly to your sales floor! You will help our call center design a script for your pre-screening. Once we determine the call meets your guidelines it will be transferred to your sales floor.
