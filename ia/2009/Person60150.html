Dean Whitney’s Summary

An early pioneer in interactive media, Dean Whitney is an inbound marketing and social media expert that has worked with some of the world’s most successful brands including Cisco, Fidelity, General Motors, Microsoft, Pfizer and collaborated with top agencies such as Leo Burnett, Saatchi & Saatchi and Starcom.

Dean Whitney’s Specialties:

Organizational leadership, start-up/turn-around strategies, multi-disciplinary team management, project/portfolio management (PPM), technology process (scrum, agile, RUP, BRM), emerging technologies (web 2.0, SOA, ajax, internet video, mobile, openID, semantic web), speaking, blogging/writing, VC/funding strategies.
