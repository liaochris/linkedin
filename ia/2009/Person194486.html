Raymond Jean’s Summary

Independent consultant with numerous contracts servicing state and local government agencies anf fortune 500 companies. Prior to going independent I was Vice President of Consulting Services at RiskWatch, Inc. I have unique background combining management, project management, hands-on consulting and sales. Established numerous consulting methodologies for IBM and created a profitable consulting practice for RiskWatch, Inc. from the ground up. Outstanding record of selling, hands-on consulting and project leadership for hundreds of risk, business continuity, disaster recovery and IT technical consulting engagements from SMB to international companies worldwide. Trained, mentored and led consulting teams throughout the world. Began career as a Computer Operator, System Programmer and Manager for a large international insurance company.

Project Manager of numerous engagements with various ranges in complexity from 12 departments to over 100 departments on a single engagement. Integrated business requirements into IT strategies for companies throughout the world.
