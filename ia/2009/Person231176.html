DJ Jones’s Summary

Product-oriented and multi-faceted game developer with both design and programming experience. Loves to take part in collaborative creative efforts, responsive to a high-energy environment. Experience in both large and small development teams with varying levels of organization and structure. Enjoys working on innovative games for "non-gamers" as well as bringing aspects of broad market appeal to games of more traditional genres. Life-long gamer and funseeker.

DJ Jones’s Specialties:

Experience with: Unreal 3 Editor, Kismet script editor, Visual Studio/Visual Assist X, CodeWarrior, C/C++, Maya and MEL scripting, Win32 libraries, MFC, DirectX, OpenGL, shader programming with GLSL and Nvidia Cg, Nintendo DS and Microsoft Xbox 360 development kits, PC / Nintendo DS / some Nintendo GBA programming, Microsoft Office apps including Word, Outlook, Excel, Project, strong in linear algebra & calculus
