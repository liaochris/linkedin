Roberta Friedman’s Summary

Provide science and medical reporting and writing for a wide variety of audiences from public to professional.

Past clients include WebMD, BioWorld, BioCentury, Natural History, California Wild, The Scientist, Psychology Today, Genetic Engineering News, Longevity, the science section of the San Francisco Examiner, Doctors Guide, BioMedNet, Reuters Health, and the NASA Astrobiology web site. Wrote on drug abuse for the Gale Group, and on the solar system for Time/Life books. Ghost authored a book on circumcision for a urologist.

Roberta Friedman’s Specialties:

Breaking medical and science news, backgrounders, features and targeted writing.
