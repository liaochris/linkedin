Mitchell Polley’s Summary

I am a dynamic and versatile thinker with a thirst for knowledge and new experiences.

My passions of chemistry and computers became fused during my undergraduate years and I drove myself into molecular modelling and just about all aspects of computational chemistry and computational biology.

I have recently recovered from a life-threatening illness and I am very keen to re-enter the workforce in research, software development, project management, customer support... whatever sounds interesting. Willing to relocate between continents.
