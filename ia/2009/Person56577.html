Dave Van Stijn’s Experience

  • Founder

    Scuba Signs Foundation

    (Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year)

    We contribute in the education of local people in undeveloped areas that want to become dive professionals or owners of a dive center. On the local level we want to create a strong economical interest in keeping marine life healty.

  • CEO and owner

    Read & View B.V

    (Internet industry)

    August 2005 — Present (3 years 11 months)

    Read & View is a fast growing online retail organisation currently active on the Dutch market.

    Amongst our shops are 123tijdschrift.nl (online subscriptions), Nationale Tijdschriftenbon (National magazine voucher), 123luisterboek (audiobooks), GSMPlaza.nl (mobile communication) as well as a large network of whitelabel variations of our shops. Our strong brands can count on the enthusiasm of a 400.000+ buying client population in 2008.

    We seek further growth by utilising our online sales/marketing knowledge, sales network and large clientbase (both consumer and b2b) as a trampoline for online concepts that are both developed within Read & View as well as taken onboard by acquisition/ participation.

    Besides servicing the online consumer market, our products are in great demand with companies that use them to deliver an original gift to their relations and/or staff or as a marketing tool.

  • Founder & owner

    Studio JA

    (Design industry)

    2004 — Present (5 years)

    Designs wonderfull things ranging from corporate identities to advertising materials & websites as wel as the full production of books and magazines. Going strong and recently 'the' Gerrit Jan Deunk joined our studio as a partner together with Dolf Heebing who has been with us for many years

  • Shareholder

    Upmedia B.V.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2000 — Present (9 years 6 months)

    Upmedia helps publishers to get maximum turnover from their publications. This is done by the creation of specials and inserts, internet cross overs etc. Besides Upmedia supports publishers on permanent or temporary sales initiatives.
    Sold majority stake in 2009 to Gebr. Keeven B.V.

  • Founder

    Twofoldmedia B.V.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2002 — 2004 (2 years)

    First last minute marketplace for print advertisements. Joint Venture with Rethinq. Unfortunately no big succes, many interest from the publishing industry, but anticipation from advertising industry took to long for Twofoldmedia B.V to bloom

  • Director & shareholder

    Centurion Media Services B.V.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1998 — 2003 (5 years)

    Delivering sales support to publishers. Part of centurion press ltd. Responsible for the acquisition of PP/Partners. Centurion Media Services was sold to Communisis PLC as part of the Centurion Press Group in 2003 and later bought back after which it was incorporated in Upmedia b.v.

  • Manager

    Straatkrant / homeless newspaper

    (Non-Profit; 201-500 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    January 1995 — November 1998 (3 years 11 months)

Besides the newspaper (later magazine) which was sold by the homeless, we launched many unique projects amongst which a telemarketing agency where only homeless people worked.
