John R. Clement’s Summary

Licensed Psychological Associate (NC), Nationally Certified Psychologist, President of Asheville Area Psychological Services, PLLC. Provide onsite psychodiagnostic testing and psychoeducational evaluations and consultations.

John R. Clement’s Specialties:

Psychodiagnostic evaluation of psychiatric disorders in children, adolescents and adults including forensic evaluations; psychoeducational evaluations of children, adolescents and adults with learning disabilities and ADHD.
