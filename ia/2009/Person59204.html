David Yates’s Summary

I'm an interaction designer, application developer and sometimes artist based in New York City. With a multi-disciplinary background I focus on concepting, designing and developing products, services and applications for mobile and emerging platforms, web, and physical installations. It's my passion to extend and translate human experience, emotion, and community into the digital world. I've worked for the past 10 years with brands including Nokia, Nike, Verizon, IBM, Sharp, Reuters, Charles Schwab, Delta Airlines, Sunglass Hut, Cablevision and Liz Claiborne, among others.
