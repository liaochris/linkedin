Ben Brenner’s Summary

After an extensive experience in Travel Business, with a strong interaction with people, technologies, solutions and visions, there has been a drastic change of direction.
Entered PSINet, a strong Internet Super Carrier in the Tech world. Success in sales helped obtaining the responsability for the subsidiary office in Southern Switzerland, Lugano.
After consolidating presence on the field and after a short experience in a Lausanne-based IT company (COMMSwitch SA), I opened my own company active in the IT, Networking, Security field (Netability SA).
Assistive Technology was the next step. Decision to change was because of personal belief in that specific market and the fact that I could make people's life better.
In 2006 an important new challenge which allows me to increase my knowledge in the telecommunications business: Chorus Call, covering the position of Sales & Operations Manager in the Video-Conferencing area.
