J Philip Faranda’s Summary

I am the broker and owner of a boutique-style real estate firm located in central Westchester County. I was the number one ranked agent for single family home sales for 2007 in the Westchester-Putnam MLS. I remain in the top 10 out of 7000 with over 100 closings in 24 months. Some of the communities the company serves are Briarcliff Manor, Ossining, Croton on Hudson, Pleasantville, Sleepy Hollow, Peekskill, Tarrytown, White Plains, Yonkers and Scarsdale.
