Douglas Benson’s Summary

Over 28 years of experience in using computer systems to financially change, improve, and enhance business operations. Early experince focused on large IBM mainframe systems programmed in COBOL utilizing CICS with later focus on business process re-engineering using SQL on Oracle RDBMS to integrate business processes, downsize hardware and software expenses replacing mainframes with UNIX servers on distributed networks.

My previous business analysis experience has been helpful in organizations in their development of new business strategies and software compliance measurements.

My roles in these efforts have included Programmer/Analyst, System Analyst, Project Leader, Instructor, Data Modeller, Data Base and Enterprise Architect, Consultant, Member of the Board of Directors of non-profit corporations.

Specific courses that I taught as a professional trainer for Oracle Corporation included Relational and Object Data Modeling using Oracle Designer (CASE) software. Deliverables from this coursework included Entity/Relationship diagrams, Process and Data Flow diagrams and physical data base structures to reflect newly defined business processes and flows.

My Consulting experience has been in numerious companies and industries with specific expertise and exposure in the Airline, Upstream (Exploration and Production) Oil and Gas industry.

Author and presenter at numerous conferences.

The last couple of years I have focused my energy in my real estate investment property as an investor and owner. I currently have an inventory of 3 single family residental investment properties and a 11-unit apartment complex in Houston, Texas.
