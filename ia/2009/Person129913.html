Ken Johnson’s Summary

Ken is a trusted European marketing & product management leader, with proven blue-chip experience in the Converged Solutions, VoIP, Mobile and Fixed Data Product space. He is a direct manager, who builds high performance relationships both within his team, and with his major stakeholders. He has consistently demonstrated his ability to deliver significant positive change through leadership of the product management and marketing functions, across a European footprint. He has delivered numerous major high impact product launches in the B2B space, and has a particular competence in high tech Telecoms service launches including VoIP collaboration & data products. His commercial acumen and numeracy skills are extremely honed, as is his ability to educate and inspire a business in pursuit of success in a new market.
