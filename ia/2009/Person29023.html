Bipin Singh’s Summary

I currently run a startup called mobikwik which is creating a new web/mobile platform for telecom service delivery to mobile users. We are currently looking to hire a J2EE developer with 2-5 years of experience. Location : Delhi NCR

In the past , I have worked as a system architect concentrating on camera,display, multimedia acceleration and Flash technologies for handheld devices. I have architected SATA and RAID6 controllers and tuned the benchmark performance of a high end chipset including an industry leading memory controller. Spent 3.5 years at Intel designing the cache coherence engine of a high speed server microprocessor.

I have deep exposure to the PC as well as Mobile phone system technologies including a taste for what is latest and the hardware software complexity involved.

I am a keen sportsperson with fanatical interest in playing and watching football. I have also been a lead trainer,motivator and content contributor in Janaagraha's flagship training program called CAP - Citizen Awareness Program. I have conducted CAP for employees in Dell and other multinationals as well as for MBA students in Bangalore.
