Nikki Scaplen’s Summary

Designer, marketer, copy writer, visionary...these are the elements of success for any good creative director. I have pushed boundaries and changed the ways things look, and the way we experience them, for over fifteen years. I design strategically because good advertising and marketing is not successful unless it accomplishes set goals at the end of the day. I'm not about just making things pretty. I'm about making things 'happen.'

My hands-on approach to design and creative management, combined with my experience in marketing communication, brand management and business management sets me apart from the typical creative.

Projects and range of experience extend to branding, corporate identity, trade advertising, marketing collateral, direct mail campaigns, exhibit graphics, and web design/development with knowledge of SEO. My work has appeared both nationally and internationally in a variety of industries. Key clients have included The Church of Christ, Oxygen Snowboards, Atomic Ski, Teletrol Systems, Mother Caroline Academy and Education Center, and Spruce Floral, Boston.

    (Graphic Design industry)

    2002 — Present (7 years)

    Bark! Creative Solutions is an independent creative consulting and graphic design studio that helps businesses succeed by maximizing their brand and marketing potential by providing strategic, innovative creative design.

    I do the business of graphic design and creative solutions differently. When small businesses, non-profits and other organizations need help bringing their marketing materials to life and need to do so economically, Bark can help. I help sort out what they need from what they don't, identify their message and make it into something tangible, and work as their personal creative and marketing department without the overhead and expense.

    Bark Creative Solutions is just that - creative SOLUTIONS. Whether it be a new logo; branding; print collateral such as brochures, newsletters, direct mail or postcards; advertising and marketing campaigns; event materials; web design or email marketing, Bark offers it all at competitive rates that provide value for money to all our clients. I believe a small, compact team with a face to face approach benefits both the client and ourselves, enabling us to work together to produce the most personal solution for their success.
