Karen Rohaly’s Summary

Karen Rohaly is an executive recruiter with the Highland Consulting Group. She has been employed in the banking and investment services field for 17 years. She has held positions in operations and sales within Regional and Community sized banks and has been a Series 6 and 63 FINRA registered sales assistant for several financial firms.

Karen's recruiting philosophy is simple. Listen, listen, listen to the candidates and the clients closely. It is important to understand the candidates and clients needs and help both parties make the most comfortable decision that they can.

Karen Rohaly’s Specialties:

Karen recruits the following type of individuals within the banking community: Small Business Lenders, Commercial Lenders, Corporate Lenders, ABL Lenders, SBA Lenders, Private Bankers, Commercial Real Estate Lenders, Cash Management, Treasury Services, Credit Underwriters, etc. Any banker that works on the commercial side of the house is what she specializes in.
