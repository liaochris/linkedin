Marcella Collins’s Summary

Marcella Collins is the marketing director and new home sales manager for Watt Communities, a premium quality home builder that has been developing new home neighborhoods throughout California for 60 years.

She earned her Bachelors of Business Administration from East Tennessee State University and is also a California licensed Real Estate Broker. She earned two distinguished professional designations by the National Sales and Marketing Council of the Institute of Residential Marketing - Certified Sales Professional and Certified Marketing Professional.

Marcella has worked for developers and sold hundreds of homes in both Santa Barbara and Los Angeles counties, many to repeat buyers. She enjoys networking with other real estate professionals to help buyers achieve the dream of owning a home, and prides herself on top level customer service. She also assists her company in land acquisitions and forward planning for future home sites throughout California.

Marcella Collins’s Specialties:

Broker/Realtor outreach, branding and identity, purchase/sale negotiations, identifying target markets, competive market analysis, sales coaching, model home design and staging
