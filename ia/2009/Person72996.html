Emma Lindahl’s Summary

I am a highly motivated PhD candidate in the field of peptide hormones where I in particular study interactions of proinsulin C-peptide.

I also together with the Unit for Bioentrepreneurship and KI Career Service at Karolinska Institutet organize a workshop series on entrepreneurship and career tools.
With a high degree of engagement I am involved in several activities at Karolinska Institutet, where I serve on the Graduate Students' Association board as well as coordinate the Career working group. I also organize the annual KI PhD party.

Emma Lindahl’s Specialties:

Scientific skills*, presentation skills, organizing skills, leadership and project management, innovation and problemsolving.

*Mass spectrometry, protein interactions, method development.
