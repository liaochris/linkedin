Denis Karpicek’s Summary

I am a senior financial analyst (specializing in business valuation) at the international level.
I am an experienced professional whose international career has included operations in France and North America, particularly in Canada.

I am also a registered consultant with the Business Development Bank of Canada (BDC), and act within the scope of mandates involving business transfers for clients of the bank. The many mandates I have already carried out have mainly been on behalf of SMEs with turnovers of between $2M and $80M. These mandates have typically been related to business transfers (financial set-up, sale or acquisition of companies, financial evaluation and simulation, share buy-backs, …)

Strategic expertise

My field of expertise encompasses corporate transition, as much within the scope of family successions as external transitions (management buy-outs or strategic sales). My well-developed network of consultants gives me the ability to find solutions for a wide range of issues.

My highly developed expertise in the field of corporate financing, particularly in the area of financial evaluation, allows me to optimize financial structures in terms of strategic positioning in order to find the right balance for my clients.

I am a certified expert on Excel, and have built a number of financial models in order to maximize wealth creation. In order to meet the needs of corporate clients in the most optimal manner, I have created a dynamic financial evaluation model adapted to SMEs. This advanced tool is used to measure wealth creation on an ongoing basis, and the software is delivered as part of the mandate.

My vision, together with an ability to understand the needs and issues of companies rapidly, allows me to provide highly effective strategic solutions. In addition, my capacity for analysis combined with my technical know-how allows me to put forward solid arguments in support of winning solutions.
