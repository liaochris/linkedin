Kelly Rued’s Summary

For over 5 years, Kelly has designed, developed, and managed online entertainment products. Her special areas of interest are social, casual, role-play, and virtual world experiences for niche and non-traditional gamer markets.

Currently, Kelly manages adult entertainment studio Black Love Interactive LLC. Her passion projects include a growing portfolio of diverse adult entertainment sites, and an MMOEG called RaptureOnline. Black Love Interactive's latest release is the x-rated lolporn parody site ICanHasFreePorn.com.

In her recent past, she published The Sex Ed Game by ISER Games--an interactive trivia game that acted as a teaching aid for parents and teachers to encourage a healthy dialog about teen sexual health and wellness while supporting a range of ethical and pedagogical approaches.

Kelly participated as a panel speaker at FuturePlay 2005, spoke on two panels, and moderated a third panel at the first Sex in Video Games conference (2006). She also moderated a Sex in MMOGs roundtable at the first annual IMGDC 2007. Kelly spoke about Tikiwiki CMS/Groupware for Game Dev Teams at the April '06 Twin Cities IGDA chapter meeting, and about her work with ISER Games at the University of Minnesota's Games as Learning Tools course in July 2005 and again in July 2006.

Kelly continues to pitch in on a variety of creative projects outside her primary domain of sex in games. She also returned to college in 2009 to study business management. Her personal site, www.kellyrued.net, spills all her beans.
