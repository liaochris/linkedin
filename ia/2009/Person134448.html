Kris Marlein’s Summary

Since the liberalization of the energy market in Belgium and The Netherlands, companies have been forced to reorganize in order to survive in a high competitive environment. These changes made energy companies rethink their processes and supporting IT systems. As a consultant I worked at several utility companies implementing a market best-practice solution based on Microsoft Dynamics AX. Soon I understood the success of an IT project was not only driven by the quality of the IT solution, but even more important the alignment between business and IT. During the years my project focus changed from a functional/analytical to a process and business role. Understanding what your customers want and advising them how to do things better and smarter can make projects a real success.

Kris Marlein’s Specialties:

Extensive knowledge of the energy market regulation and law in Belgium and the Netherlands, billing processes and revenue management, value chain of an energy company and Integrated Production-Grid-Supply companies (e.g. water), functional analysis, Microsoft Dynamics AX, Prince2 (Foundation an Practitioner Certified), Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), Business Process Analysis, quality management.
