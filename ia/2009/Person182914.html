Paul Todd’s Summary

Over the past 30 plus years Paul’s career experience has been helping people solve issues. It has brought great satisfaction to his life so far. These many experiences has enabled him to meet many individuals from all walks of life, several of which Paul has developed long term relationships with. The future is bright with many more exciting individual to meet and work with.
Paul’s first experience in the financial services industry was before he graduated from college. Paul started selling life insurance and annuities for a fraternal insurance company. This position led to several other opportunities in the insurance and financial services field.
In the early 90s, Paul help start a National Financial Services Company that combined insurance, mortgages and debt reduction to help enable families better experience the American dream. This opportunity enabled him to train and lead seminars throughout the United States about Financial Services.
Paul took this experience and specialized in mortgages, credit and debt when he became a partner/owner of EquiPartners Financial Services.
Paul’s experience in the Financial Services field gave him opportunities for local and national radio shows on financial matters. It also enabled him to be used in court cases as an expert, write articles and e-books.
Paul and his wife, Carolyn manage their vacation rental business in Mentone, Alabama where Paul continues to work in the financial services field.
