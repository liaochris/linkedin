Bob Carrasca’s Summary

Energetic and compassionate program manager with 13 years of experience in developing complex programs with domestic and overseas suppliers. I have 10 years of experience in executing projects in Domestically as well as South East Asia and Europe.

My work history shows that I thrive in challenging, active and diverse work environments. My depth of knowledge as it relates to corporate values is unique since I have the experience of program managing major projects with million dollar budgets. I have worked with Fortune 500 companies as well as small start-ups. Time and time again, upper management teams have bestowed on me the accountability and responsibility of some of the most critical projects that they want to go to market with. I have the experience, expertise, ability and where with all to form unique teams to meet these monumental challenges.

My strengths are my ability to communicate with a variety of technical individuals and mediate between different diverse groups within a company. Co-workers have said that I am very good at mining people’s core skills sets. They also know that I am a manager who stays involved from start to finish. I thrive on understanding what individuals do and what their core skill sets are. I understand how to take complex problems and break them into small digestible pieces and create and action plan to solve the problems. I work with core groups to gain consensus on what variables need to be addressed and how to navigate a situation. I am a team player and enjoy being part of such a synergetic process.

Experience has trained me how to properly create time and cost effective processes to meet upper management’s goals. I have the ability to look at a project holistically and determine where the risks are and how to execute to maximize positive gains.

Bob Carrasca’s Specialties:

My personal specialties includes: program managment, product development, Plastics engineering, DFM (develop for manufacturing) engineering, digital game development, 3D modeling, surfacing, rendering, vendor & supplier analysis and qualification, managing MRP systems, Rapid prototyping, Close Cycle Product Development, Advance Materials and Composites, FEA testing tools and analysis.
