Claire Koelsch Sand’s Summary

Dr. Claire Sand is a strategic thinker with over 20 years experience in Packaging and Food Science and Food Packagiing

Packaging
-technical areas of: basic research, packaging materials, package development, sustainable package development, and packaging market research
- strategic areas of: competitive positioning, market sector analysis, value chain opportunities analysis, and leveraging packages for retail markets

Food science and food packaging
- technical areas of: ingredient analysis, food product development, food engineering, processing, and preservation methods
- strategic areas in designing market competitive foods and food packaging

Dr. Sand applies this unique background as an international consultant. She has interest in project work on a variety of international packaging and food science initiatives. She is President of Ameripak, an Adjunct professor in Michigan State University's School of Packaging Masters degree program, and a PTIS Associate.

Dr Sand has a consistent track record of developing refined viable solutions for various clients to meet their market needs. She communicates technical subjects and strategic initiatives clearly in an approachable manner.

Dr. Sand prior experience has been as a Category Manager at Dominick’s, Principal at Total Quality Marketing, Technical Business Manager at Kraft Foods, Scientist at Grand Metropolitan (now General Mills), and Packaging Engineer at Gerber Products as well as in overseas assignments in Germany, Colombia, and Thailand . She has been recognized for her abilities in research, teaching and service.

Dr. Sand’s professional involvement includes: editorial boards, authorship of books, chapters, and articles and as a presenter on a full range of packaging, food science and strategic topics. Dr. Sand holds a Ph.D. in Food Science from the University of Minnesota and MS and BS degrees in Packaging from Michigan State University.
