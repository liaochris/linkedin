Stephen Pineda’s Summary

The most common question I get, even from long-time friends and relatives is, "Stephen, what exactly do you DO for a living"? With over 9 years of experience in the merchant services operations area and 6 years in sales, I know how to close the deal, but most importantly, service it. While being employed as an RM for a public payment processing company, I felt a serious lack in this industry of interchange knowledge and customer service and decided to devote more time to sales.

My background is serving resorts and hotel chains with very specialized chargeback modules, instant notifications and interchange qualifications monitoring. I also assist restaurants with complex gift and loyalty programs. In addition to the hospitality industry, I collaborate with retail, emerging markets, auto dealers, B2B and others in lowering their processing fees and minimizing losses due to fraud. I also have helped several large clients that are considered high risk or are having challenges with domestic and international solutions.

Not only has my experience allowed me to stand out in a crowd of sales brokers, but my reputation as a trustworthy associate has helped me earn plenty of new relationships. My customers are my best referral sources as I provide the very best in service, along with cutting edge products and innovative technology to provide the very best solution to you from ONE vendor. I hope to be that ONE vendor for you and your company!
