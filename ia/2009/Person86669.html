Gillian Nalletamby’s Summary

Look up in the sky …. Is it a bird …. Is it a plane?

I can type faster than a speeding bullet, write copy more powerful than a locomotive and I’m able to leap photocopy machines in a single bound! About the only thing I can’t do is catch a bullet between my teeth! Well, I’m not quite Superman but to be perfectly honest, did the underpants over the tights look really ever catch on?

I am a freelance writer in my 30's who enjoys writing about women’s issues, dating, fashion, exercise and fitness, trying to make sense of a world obsessed by couples, calories and celebrities. I have worked in media and entertainment for over five years and write engaging and compelling copy including web copy, feature articles, media releases etc. I am currently employed as a SEO Copywriter at SEO Works.
