Kelly Hartigan - kellyhartigan@gmail.com

My career objective is to develop award winning digital solutions at one of the country’s leading digital agencies in a senior role. And in the process, work to keep them at the forefront of a rapidly evolving industry that is growing globally at an unprecedented rate.

I am a highly motivated, independent and outgoing person who thrives upon the challenges of advertising brands online and developing effective, vibrant, unique communications solutions tailored to the needs of each client.

With over 10-years experience working in Australia and the UK I’ve built a solid skill-set that covers all aspects of the digital mix from account, project and team management to new business, digital strategy and marketing. The clients with whom I have worked have come from many different market sectors including FMCG, music, automotive, retail, healthcare, financial services and telecommunications.

I have extensive experience and knowledge of the agency environment and the skill and accuracy required within it to deliver a high standard of client service. I believe this to be just as important as having the nation’s top creative and imperative to building strong, long lasting client relationships.

I work well with all personalities vital to the agency’s efforts and have developed a good understanding of each person’s role whether they are the Managing Director, Creative Director, Copywriter, Designer or Programmer. I understand that strong relationships here help us to deliver the best work even when under pressure.

I love advertising! I love brands! I love being involved in bringing them to life and the stimulating, rewarding, challenging yet fun environment of agency life!
