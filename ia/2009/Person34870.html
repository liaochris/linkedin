Bruno Abate, CISA’s Summary

I have a total of 13 years of work experience. My main area of experience is providing consultancy services both on administering/tuning/troubleshooting the Oracle RDBMS, and helping organizations achieve IT security governance. I’ve been hired as an independent systems’ auditor, sysadmin, netadmin, webmaster and Oracle University instructor many times, including conducting security assessments in the president's central government offices.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the responsibility of making hardware purchasing decisions, building data centres from the ground up, and leading small technical teams. I regularly answer questions on the Server performance forum on Oracle Corp. support website, where you can also find one of my technical articles on SQL tuning. I have a current CISA certification, Master in Computer Engineering and Corporate Management University degrees, and communicate in English, Spanish and Portuguese.
