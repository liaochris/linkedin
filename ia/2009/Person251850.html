Zulfiqar Deo’s Summary

Zulfiqar specialises in the SME sector. I draw out tacit concerns of the business and help by adding my skills and expertise to the business for the duration of our relationship. I have experience of setting up my own companies and have been through the process right up to the VC stage.

I specialise in ensuring the companies I work with make the most of their existing capacities and understand how to build their businesses sustainably. My MBA in International Business Management has a SME focus and has ensured I have covered concerns ranging from Communications to Strategy Implementation. I have previous experience of analysing markets, geo-political and market entry risks. I have recently spent over two years in the voluntary sector working for the Big Issue and Fair Trade Foundations. I also have consulting and business mentoring experience.

My goals are to help people build their own businesses sustainably based on the Internet.

Zulfiqar Deo’s Specialties:

Advising and mentoring SME's on how to build sustainable businesses based on the Internet, encourage greater use of Information Management Systems and ensure an indepth understanding of Strategy Implementation concerns.
