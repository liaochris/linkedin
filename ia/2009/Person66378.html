Doug Harman’s Summary

• Left school into the graphics art industry, attained a diploma in lithographic printing at Hiedelberg, Germany.
• Moved into journalism in 1990/91, retrained and joined IPC and Amateur Photographer magazine as a technical writer. Moved through the ranks (deputy technical editor, technical editor and web editor).
• Redundancy in 2001 meant a move to S2 Creative Solutions as technical editor of What Digital Camera magazine.
• Moved to Total Digital Photography (in same role) magazine when WDC went "in house".
• Redundancy when TDP "went in house" meant branching out on my own, setting up US3 Media Ltd, writing my first photography book, The Digital Photography Handbook and producing my first photography DVD, Doug Harman's Guide to Digital Photography.
•  Now pursuing my media business aims, writing more books (just finished a collaboration as Technical Advisor for John Freeman's latest book: The Digital SLR Handbook) and scipting the next in my series of DVD photography shows.
• Have now launched Best4Reviews.com, a new technology reviews web site (under the US3 Media Ltd banner) which is now live.
• As well as writing for my own new website (Best4Reviews.com) I also still write for various photo titles including Professional Photographer magazine, What Digital Camera magazine and web sites including pocketlint.co.uk and productsifter.com. And I still do photography – by commission.

Doug Harman’s Specialties:

Photography, Image retouching (Photoshop etc.), technical writing, print production, advertising and marketing.
