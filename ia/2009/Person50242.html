Craig Allan Teich’s Summary

As an executive, consultant, coach, advisor, board member, investor and entrepreneur, my career has been colored by the opportunity to see deep inside many different industries, from financial management to bagel making, from home improvement to retail, from high tech to university administration, from real estate development to internet marketing, and from publishing to the wellness industry.

The common thread binding these varied business adventures is a love of innovation, creativity, quality and helping others.

I'm passionate about several things: my wife, my two daughters, growing businesses, creating mind-blowing marketing campaigns that grow sales and profits while building brand equity, extreme fitness and nutrition, all outdoor sports that include some speed and some danger, such as cycling, kite surfing, rock climbing and shark rodeos. (I made that last one up.)

> Writing copy that makes men trade their girlfriends for a used pair of socks
> Brand building strategies that lead to priests and nuns wearing your logo
> Brokering joint venture partnerships so compelling that the entire Middle East would be one big lovefest by next week if they just asked me to help
> Coaching executives & entrepreneurs on how to accomplish 5x more in 1/2 the time...that's Quantum Leverage
