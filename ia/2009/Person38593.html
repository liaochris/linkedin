Alberto Carrero’s Summary

Alberto delivers a challenging and supportive coaching approach that facilitates personal development in an effective and sustainable manner. He supports individuals and teams unleash their potential by helping them: decide on the choices and changes they want to make to achieve their goals, develop their emotional intelligence and discover their authentic self. His coaching is focused on results, leading to clear and measurable improvements in the performance of the individual and the business which are reflected in the bottom-line.

His coaching style is complemented by over 20 years of experience in leadership positions and management board roles. His corporate background includes having been Head Director at Red Bull Energy Drink where he, together with Founder & CEO Dietrich Mateschitz, played a key role in laying the global foundation and creating a multi-billion-Euro company. He has also served as Managing Director, Marketing Director and in management positions in top international companies (i.e. Procter & Gamble, Kimberly Clark and the Danone Group). He brings a proven track record of building up teams and creating businesses across ten countries in three continents by leading and coaching executives in new start-ups, high growth and mature operations. Having worked in diverse cultures and organizations, gives him a multifaceted international outlook with a deep understanding of people, their surroundings and motivators that empower them to achieve and exceed goals.

He coaches internationally and his clients include successful entrepreneurs, board of director members, senior level executives, directors, high potential talents and leadership teams in leading global companies.

Alberto’s commitment and effective partnership with clients ensures a journey to achieving personal peak performance.
