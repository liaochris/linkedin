Dan London’s Summary

Dan London graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. His professional career after graduation includes a stint as an online marketing associate for LastAvailable.com during the wrong side of the Dot Com explosion in the early 2000s. He spent time with local Durham ad agency Clean Design as a copywriter and wrote web and print copy for numerous companies such as IBM, John Deere and Red Hat. After a short time as a 6th grade teacher, Dan joined search marketing firm Websourced.com as an SEO Copywriter and quickly began taking on top clients such as Lowe’s, Black and Decker and Citi Financial. He advanced to the Paid Search team and began handling some of the high-profile accounts such as Motorola and Interstate Battery.

In 2006 Dan joined the world's leading self-publisher Lulu.com as the Director of Search Engine Marketing. In this role Dan has taken on both natural and paid search as well as banner advertising. Dan has been able to increase the traffic to Lulu nearly 500% in his time at Lulu and helped increase revenue significantly each year. He was a featured Speaker at Google AdWords Client Forum October 2008.

Outside of professional interests, he is an avid reader, writes short stories, plays and coaches hockey, and enjoys living in Raleigh with his family, cats and dog.
