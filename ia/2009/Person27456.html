The old cliché ‘it’s lonely at the top’ is something most business leaders know to be true. But now, you don’t have to feel like you’re ‘in this alone’. Beth partners with business owners and managers - one-on-one and in teams - to see themselves more clearly and learn skills to optimize the impact of people on their bottom line.

With more than 20 years in an organizational effectiveness and coaching role within Fortune 100 companies like Hewlett-Packard and Sun Microsystems, she knows the pain and pleasure of working with people. She also knows what it takes to bring people together to work as an A-Team.

Here’s what Beth’s clients say they value most about her.
• “She holds me accountable.” - CEO
• “Beth brought our leadership team together . . .” - VP of Operations
• “Helped me focus on the right things, instead of every thing. “ - Operations manager
• "Beth makes even the most difficult activities fun and positive" - Director of Public Affairs
• “Whether I like it or not, she holds up the mirror and shows me my blind spots.” - Controller
• “She never gives up on me.” - President/CEO

Beth started her company, Unlimited Potential, in 2004 and uses a combination of coaching and consulting to help her clients reach higher performance, have more time and increase profits.

Beth has a passion for life and a desire to help others. She lives with her four 4-legged kids (2 golden retrievers and 2 horses) in gorgeous Colorado and wants all she meets to live the life of their dreams!
