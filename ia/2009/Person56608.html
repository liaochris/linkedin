Dave Winslow’s Summary

Dave has been active in the online marketing industry for over a decade specializing in the areas of online business consulting, search marketing, testing and optimization, and web analytics. He has worked as Interactive Director for an creative agency, Director of Sales for an Internet start-up and founded a couple of companies along the way.

Currently as CEO of EpikOne, a strategic analytics consultancy, Dave is responsible for the vision of the company including marketing, innovation and leadership. EpikOne offer's online marketing solutions, training and software to enable organizations to grow their business online. They are a Google Analytics and Website Optimizer Authorized Consultant, Google Enterprise Partner and a Google AdWords Qualified Company, and have worked with a diverse mix of clients around the world ranging from Fortune 500 organizations to e-commerce growth companies.

As an industry thought leader, Dave incubated and serves as the Executive Director of OM Boot Camp, a four-day training summit in Burlington, Vermont. He also speaks regularly at conferences such as Search Engine Strategies, eMetrics Marketing Optimization Summit and Digital Asset Management. Dave also instructs courses on Web Analytics at Champlain College and serve on the Advisory Board for the colleges BYOBiz entrepreneurial program. Additionally he founded and manage Urban Style Direct (www.urbanstyledirect.com) - an e-tailer that sells urban-inspired fashion globally.
