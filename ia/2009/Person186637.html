Phaedra Boinodiris’s Summary

Currently working at IBM as their Serious Games Producer developing a series of games for corporate training, point of sale, and extending brand reach. Just launched second game in the INNOV8 game series teaching Business Process Management and Services Oriented Architecure in a Sim-style game. My first 3D serious game for academic use that I helped produce for IBM is a BPM simulation tool built on an FPS engine.
