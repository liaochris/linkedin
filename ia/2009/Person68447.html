Dustin Senos’s Summary

Dustin Senos is a Software Engineer for Disney Online Studios Canada, a part of the Walt Disney Company. While most of his responsibilities are technical: software architecting, unit testing, and socket to client communication, his task is to ensure the site is always smoothly run, frequently updated, and, of course, fun.

In the past, Dustin has worked as a Freelance Graphic Artist, Senior Implementer, Media Developer, and Software Salesman for a large, unnamed company. Outside of work, Dustin enjoys fixed-gear bikes, photography, and music. He loves spending time with his family and friends.
