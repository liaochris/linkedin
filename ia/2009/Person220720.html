Krystal Hosmer’s Summary

Although I have no formal design education, I've been actively involved in design in Las Vegas for the past 12 years. I service my own clients, help out other designers in the community and come to the rescue with on-site fill-in or an extra pair of qualified hands for many of the small and mid-sized agencies in town.

At some point or other, I have done design work for just about every major casino on the Las Vegas strip and several out of state. You could say that I am an expert when it's comes to graphic design for the gaming industry.

An active member of the design community, I've sat on the AIGA Board for the Las Vegas Chapter for the past 4 years. I currently serve as Membership Director.

I share my love of Adobe Photoshop through UNLV's Education Outreach Division by teaching the Photoshop Level I and Level II classes, year round. I will be teaching classes on additional topics this fall. I also offer private instruction in Photoshop.

In addition, I serve as the Co-Manager of The Vegas Adobe Photoshop and Print Users Group. Please check us out online at http://www.vegasPUG.org.
