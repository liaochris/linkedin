Michael Reilly’s Summary

Select Resorts is a villa holiday and property sales specialist working in resort destinations such as the Algarve, La Manga Club Spain, Tuscany, Barbados, and Tenerife.

We are currently working on the lauch of the next phase of the Oceans Edge development in beautiful St. Kitts.

Have more recently been involved in the development of www.selectexchange.co.uk - a home exchange site aimed specifically at the vacation home / second home market. The scheme is designed to create a community of like minded owners who seek to explore the full value of their second home investments by using it as a tool to finance other holidays.


Prior to this I worked as a professional recruiter focussing on recruitment of sales professionals across a variety of industry sectors including HVAC, Construction, Building Services, IT, Electronics and many more......

Specialising in the placement of sales people ranging from Sale Executive up to CEO or Global VP. All candidates are interviewed in depth analysing KPI’s, achievements, components of sales quota, career highlights/ wins / clients, target markets/ sectors / verticals and overall credentials.
