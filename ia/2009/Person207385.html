Sally Hope’s Summary

Sally joined the PiR Group in 2000 and has been with PiR’s Interim practice since its launch in 2005. Sally focuses on managing client and candidate relationships for quality, manufacturing and operational roles in pharmaceutical and other life science sectors. Her experience of building the Practice and understanding expectations of key stakeholders who work as or engage the services of interims is invaluable to developing effective relationships.

Previously, Sally has held a variety of support roles which have involved her in many areas of PiR's business. These have included research, operations, administration and finance.

In her earlier career, post-graduation, she worked in support roles within the education and insurance sectors.
