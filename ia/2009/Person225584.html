Stuart McClure’s Summary

My real joy is online marketing, although I have a wealth of experience in both traditional and online marketing - at small and large companies.

In terms of online, my experience extends to PPC, SEO, project management, web development, blogs, forums, content management, e-mail, affiliate and much more. I have worked with companies of varying sizes, ranging from one man bands right up to those with a membership of over a 1,000,000 people (at The Caravan Club).

My offline experience includes brochure and catalogue production, direct mail, inserts, trade exhibitions and shows, magazine publication and a host of other things. Again, I have worked with numerous companies of numerous sizes.

I also have experience in standard marketing disciplines that transcend the divide between on and offline marketing - such as brand, agency management and selection, brief preparation and so on.

I am always keen to hear from people who've got a challenge on their hands which they think I could help with, so feel free to give me a shout.
