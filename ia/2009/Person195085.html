Global Employment Holdings, Inc., through its subsidiaries, provides a range of human capital solutions to business, industrial, and professional enterprises in the United States. The company operates in two segments, Staffing Services and Professional Employer Organizations Services. The Staffing Services segment provides direct placement services; and temporary staffing... see more services that consist of on-demand or short-term staffing assignments, contract staffing, on-site management, and human resource administration. This segment offers services in various areas, such as light industrial, clerical, information technology, engineering, accounting and finance, call center, and logistics. The Professional Employer Organizations Services segment offers human resource management services, including payroll, payroll taxes, employee benefits, health insurance, workers’ compensation coverage, workplace safety programs, compliance with federal and state employment laws, labor and workplace regulatory requirements, and related administrative responsibilities. The company is headquartered in Lone Tree, Colorado
