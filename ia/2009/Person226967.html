Susan Farlow’s Summary

A freelance travel writer, Susan Farlow covers the world, especially the more exotic parts of it, looking for the off-beat and the unusual. A member of SATW since 1984, she specializes in cruises, foreign destinations, food & drink, culture. She particularly enjoys travel which involves animals. She has roamed the Sahara by camel, toured the Alps by mule, and interviewed a Komodo Dragon on the Indonesian island of Komodo.

Susan has been a freelancer for 30 years. Her articles have been published in major newspapers and glossy magazines.

Also, a photographer, Susan's photos almost always accompany her written words. She is based in Maine.
