Marciela Harris’s Summary

Marciela Harris, a native of Inkster, MI, received her BS from Kettering University in Flint MI. She worked in various marketing roles for Buick Motor Division from 1994 – 2002 before leaving as a Brand Analyst to attend Carnegie Mellon Tepper School of Business.

She received her MBA with a concentration in strategy, marketing and finance from Tepper as a Consortium Alum in 2004. Immediately upon graduation, Ms. Harris joined Air Products and Chemicals in various marketing positions before transitioning to the company’s Home Health Care division as a Brand Manager. Marciela joined Target in October 2007 and served as a Product Manager in Target Sourcing Services, managing core camping items to ensure that Target sources the trend right, high quality items at the best value guests.
