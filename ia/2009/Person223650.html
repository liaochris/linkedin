Steve Barcik’s Summary

28+ years in engineering, have spent all of those years in various High Tech Industries, Solid State Lighting, Industrial Equipment, Semiconductor and Semiconductor manufacturing equipment, with extensive experience in electrical, electronic, lighting, chemical, medical, green energy, energy reduction and solar. Steve has started 6 companies to date. HTDS provides engineering evaluation support, design criteria, and design evaluation to enable companies to rapidly market their new technology.
HTDS supplies design evaluation to enable faster market acceptance for lower total cost.
