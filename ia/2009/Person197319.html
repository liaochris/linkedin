Riccardo Perotti’s Summary

Composer, arranger, producer and musical director with vast experience in artistic and commercial production, musical shows and music for film and TV. Singer and pianist-keyboardist with solid history of studio work and live performance. Audio Engineer qualified for recording studio and live sound.

Reliable and resourceful in challenging or stressful projects, used to working with short deadlines and in fast paced environments. A team player with strong leadership skills, very effective with technology, in love with sound checks and a strong believer in rehearsals.

Riccardo Perotti’s Specialties:

* Instruments: lead and background vocals, piano, keyboards, acoustic and electric guitars.

* Author, Composer, Arranger, Producer and Music Director.

* Qualified Audio Engineer fluent in Digital Audio Technologies. Expert MIDI Programmer.

* Fluent English and Spanish (singing, speaking, writing, reading).

* Fluent in Perl, HTML, CSS, Javascript, mySQL and Apache.

* Apple MacOSX pro user, comfortable in Linux, willing to survive Windoze.
