Dr. Praveen Kumar L.’s Summary

- Ph.D. with 10+ years of academic & professional experience in the areas of Research, Quality Assurance and Regulatory Compliance.
- Currently playing a key role in commissioning, qualification & validation of USFDA / EMEA cGMP complaint biopharmaceutical contract manufacturing facility at Penang Science Park.
- Earlier played a key role in designing and commissioning of USFDA / EMEA cGMP complaint biopharmaceutical manufacturing facility for Avesta Biotherapeutics & Research Pvt. Ltd. (ABRPL) – a JV between Avesthagen and Cipla.
- Successfully accomplished ISO 14001:2004 environmental management system certification, NABL ISO 17025:2005 accreditation and DSIR recognition as in-house R&D unit for Avesthagen Limited.
- Established Quality Management Systems for Biologics complying with Indian (Schedule M), USFDA 21CFR 210 & 211, 600, 601, 610, 820, 58, EU standards at Avesthagen Limited and Avesta Biotherapeutics & Research Pvt. Ltd.
- Was member of Avesthagen-Cipla delegation to China for assessment of various mammalian cell culture manufacturing facilities for scale-up and due diligence and integration team in acquisition of Siegfried Biologics GmBh, Germany.
- Possessing in-depth understanding of relevant cGMP and GLP regulations applicable to biopharmaceutical products and strong laboratory skills.
