Ty Wolfe-Jones’s Summary

Efficiency is something I am always striving for, with a constant focus on the Brand and what the Consumer sees. All things Operational effect the customer as well as the bottom line, so that is the fine line that I live on.

My creative side revels in packaging and developing products. My reading list is articles, magazines, and websites on ingredients and my trip through the grocery store is constantly interrupted by new, interesting, and even horrible packaging.

I eat and breath what and how Brands do what they do. I never say that I know how to do it all because I am constantly learning. I have a decent rolodex of people that I rely on to stay connected, as well as get things done.

My larger Contracts are mentioned below, but I have also worked with Cha Dao Tea, Unique E Vitamin company, Caregifting.com, Pink Godzilla Games.
