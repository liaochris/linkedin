Dana Cowie’s Summary

I am a fine artist specializing in drawing and painting. My paintings are displayed in group and solo exhibitions regularly in galleries around Ontario.


My skills and experience include:

- Commissioned artworks for clients with specific needs.
- Mural and decorative painting for department store windows such as Holt Renfrew and Sporting Life Inc.
- Concept Illustrations for advertising agencies and design firms as well as editorial publications.
- Fashion Styling for department stores and photo shoots.
- Prop building for department stores and set design.
- Creating paintings for galleries.

My goal is to continue working as a multi-talented freelance artist, providing my skills to companies for whatever their projects need.

You can visit my website at www.dcowie.com

Dana Cowie’s Specialties:

concept illustration, fashion illustration, mural painting, commissioned artwork, building props, fashion stylist, creating fashion accessories.
