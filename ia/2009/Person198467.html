Rick Carlson’s Summary

Rick Carlson- BIO

With over 15 years as a Human Resources Professional he has been responsible for developing and maintaining client relationships as well as creating marketing, networking and recruitment strategies.

A Certified Personnel Consultant (CPC) with an education focused on HR Management & Labor Relations, his success comes from building relationships with key operational leaders and by delivering quality services in a cost effective manner. Fueled by the desire to understand and identify what a company's needs are and by providing them with solutions, Rick believes the true reward of the process is seeing a project through to completion and exceeding the expectations of the client. His direct style, enthusiasm, passion for excellence, and ability to work with teams across all levels that achieves results are a few reasons why you should utilize his expertise.

Rick Carlson’s Specialties:

Human Resources, Client/Customer Relations, Networking, Sourcing, Business Development, RPO, Talent Acquisition, Human Capital, Recruitment and Consulting. Well seasoned with TRM's (Talent Relationship Management) and ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)...AIRS, Taleo, Authoria, Hodes, etc.

With a positive, optimistic, can-do attitude!!
