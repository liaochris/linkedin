Joeri van den Steenhoven’s Summary

I'm intrigued by the transformation towards a knowledge-driven economy and the way society responds to this. A transformation that has started decades ago and will continue in the decades to come, but that provides many challenges to people, institutions and society at large. To find answer to these challenges, I started Kennisland | Knowledgeland, a Dutch thinktank that wants to make society smarter. For that purpose, we design innovative interventions through research, consulting, projects and events.

Currently I'm also involved in the Lisbon Council, a Brussels based European thinktank on the Lisbon Agenda, and the Kafkabrigade, a research team that helps governments to combat red tape.
