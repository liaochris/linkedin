Bob Dececco, CPA’s Experience

  • Co-Founder, President and COO

    Typed-In LLC

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    July 2007 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Typed-In is an internet media company focused on helping businesses of all size increase sales by taking advantage of the shift in users approach from print media to local online search. Through the acquisition and development of its geo-targeted domain network, these domains, once acquired by Typed-In, are subsequently monetized by bridging the advertiser to the local consumer.

    A geo-targeted domain name answers the question of what (product/service) & where (location); for example, consumers searching on the internet for a particular product or service in their local market may type the following in their browser; LAWYERPORTLAND.COM, DIVORCEATTORNEYBOSTON.COM, PLUMBERLASVEGAS.COM, DOCTORSANFRANCISCO.COM, etc.

    Consumers searching locally are a very high quality lead for a business, typically converting 250% more often than conventional leads for a fraction of the cost of traditional advertising.
