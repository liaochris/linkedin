Joanna Ricco’s Interests:

I am motivated by culturally aware web content and wish to make a mark as a dynamic editorial force with a distinct voice. With a background in the relationship between gendered media and pop culture, I wish to produce work that challenges the status quo of gender identity and culturally imposed roles, while keeping a sharp eye on shifting market climates. My interests, editorially, also branch into the creative development of branding, voice, web content, and customer facing marketing collateral.
