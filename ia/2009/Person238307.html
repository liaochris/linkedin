Trisha Torrey’s Summary

As Every Patient's Advocate, I am a newspaper columnist, radio talk show host, speaker, author and busy blogger.

I empower patients to take control of their healthcare and decision-making.

I teach health professionals how improved communication and partnership with patients can make their practices more efficient and improve their patients' outcomes.

My career as a patient advocate began after a heinous run-in with the dysfunction of the healthcare system. I was diagnosed with a rare and fatal lymphoma and figured out on my own that I had been misdiagnosed. So I took my previous experiences, both as a marketing professional and a teacher, and turned them into my new mission in life -- helping others navigate their healthcare more successfully, too.

Learn more about these patient empowerment activities here: http://www.everypatientsadvocate.com/trisha.htm

Or read about my misdiagnosis odyssey here:
http://patients.about.com/od/misdiagnosis/a/misdiagnosis.htm

Or check out my personal blog here: http://everypatientsadvocate.com/blog

I'm always available to speak to your group -- one of my passions. With more than ten years experience doing keynotes and presentations, I can tailor the message to your group's needs.

Follow me on Twitter @TrishaTorrey
