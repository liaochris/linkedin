Mark Posen’s Summary

I am the Managing Director and Principal Consultant of RPC Telecommunications Ltd. in the UK and Director and Principal Consultant of RPC Telecommunications Asia Pacific Pty. Ltd. in Australia.

I have substantial professional experience in the satellite telecommunications industry since 1983. Prior to going into the private consulting business, I was an engineering manager with British Telecommunications PLC where I held a number of posts including Head of Satellite Systems Consulting Group and Satellites and Lines Development Advisor.

I am very experienced in the spectrum and interference management, international and national regulation and frequency coordination of satellite telecommunications networks. I am an expert on the Radio Regulations and associated procedures of the International Telecommunications Union (ITU) and, on behalf of a number of national telecommunications administrations and satellite operators, have been responsible for making and coordinating ITU satellite filings and providing representation at an international level.

As Managing Director of RPC Telecommunications Limited, I have overseen the ongoing growth of the company since its formation in 1993 and have managed projects for a wide range of clients, contributing to RPC Telecommunications' international reputation in its field. Through RPC Telecom Asia Pacific this expertise and knowledge is now being brought closer to clients in that part of the World too.

I am a Chartered Engineer, listed on the professional register of the UK Engineering Council and a Corporate Member of the Institute of Engineering and Technology.

Mark Posen’s Specialties:

satellite systems engineering, radiocommunications engineering, frequency coordination, ITU Radio Regulations, radio interference analysis, satellite and radio communications training, satellite and radio communications software
