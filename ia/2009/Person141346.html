Linda Taylor’s Summary

I have spent over 35 years helping individuals achieve their dreams through education and training.

After 15 years in the oil industry with Chevron USA and 5 in motorsports as the Marketing VP at Taylor Marketing & Management, I became one of the Principals at College Funding Network (CFN).

For the last 14 years, CFN has helped small business owners, home-based business owners and entrepreneurs rescue their family's dreams from the confusing and stressful education "system". We create COLLEGE PLANNING BLUEPRINTS that combines the expert input from a nationwide team of college, financial, tax and home school professionals who specialize in testing, selecting, applying, and paying for education. CFN provides the right information at the right time so families can regain control of the process. http://www.CollegeFundingNetwork.com

The newest CFN program is CAHSEE - "Creating Access to Higher Scores and the Education Edge". The first initiative is 30 MINUTE A - a digital tutor for mastering the "3 R's" of math, English Language Arts and vocabulary. To learn more http://www.30MinuteA.com

I remain the Owner & a Principal at TM&M coordinating a team of experts that help small business owners and entrepreneurs implement the strategies recommended by CFN. TM&M specializes in marketing because without customers, there is no business! We further specialize in using Web 2.0 Social Media, Social Networks, Special Networking tools like Facebook, blogs and Twitter. http://www.TaylorMadeSecrets.com

Linda Taylor’s Specialties:

Strategies for entrepreneurs, small and home-based businesses to pay for education and training. Client and prospect analysis for marketing plan development. Web 2.0, Social Media, Social Networks, Social Networking tools like Facebook, blogs and Twitter.
