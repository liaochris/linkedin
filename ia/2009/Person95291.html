Mike Williams’s Summary

Ubermore are digital uber-specialists. Mike writes and Kathrin draws. I started out making interactive videos. She trained as a graphic designer. We met at the Royal College of Art in 1993 doing Europe’s first post-graduate new media course.

Our digital work includes advertising, websites, installations and all things interactive. We’ve had various solo careers but, inspired by Take That, recently decided to reform as ubermore. We are a 100% pure digital team with a bunch of awards. We do creative strategy, pitch work and campaigns for digital marketing.

We're already helping Heineken, Xbox, Unilever Axe and BT. If you'd like to chat about us helping you too, then give us a call on +44 (0)207 9934256
