Michael Birch’s Summary

I have over fifteen year's of highly successful experience in Information Technology team management, technology acquisition (RFI/P/Q), contract preparation and negotiation, software development, data center management, and project delivery. Most recently as a Director responsible for an IT requirements team with United Services Automobile Association (USAA) in San Antonio, Texas. My team were responsible for facilitating IT requirements, conducting evaluations, planning 3rd party implementations, contracting, and ensuring success with suppliers providing multi-million dollar business and information technology products and services.

I thrive on working with people, developing teams and individuals, and providing solutions for complex and critical needs. I have a unique ability to communicate to all corporate levels, motivate staff and colleagues, develop and tune processes, and deliver success regardless of the challenge.

My diverse background provides me the ability to take creative approaches to strategic planning, tactical implementation, problem analysis and solution, process analysis and development, project management, and employee development to ensure that needs and budgets are met.
