    (Human Resources industry)

    June 2009 — Present (2 months)

    Fulcrum Employment Solutions is a new concept within the Recruitment Industry; one where the focus in on the economy. A partnership where the key link is Clients (Companies & Employment Agencies) to Candidates; not primarily focused on bottom-line but servicing the economy. Thus, we have initiated a two point business model: 1.) the Employment Seminar Series where we educated jobseekers on how best to construct resumes and target potential employers, and 2.) on providing recruitment partnerships with new start-up, small to large businesses - relationships where bottom line does not dictate service, but where “service” is paramount and fee is individualized.
