Julie Schramm Dartt’s Summary

I started my career in printing in high school in a graphic arts course. I then went on for 2 AAB's in printing: flexo and litho. I completed my BS in Printing Management from RIT while working 3 jobs to make it through. My full-time job was with the General Services Administration, mainly acting as the computer technician for three field offices.

I worked extensively in electronic prepress and have the "old-school" conventional background as well. I've worked in both packaging art and commercial print, doing college magazines, catalog work and print promotional materials as well as packaging for cartons, labels, flexible packaging, shrink films, rigid containers, corrugate, and just about everything else.

After seeing the condition of the artwork coming in, I realized there was a lot to be gained on helping the design side become more efficient (therefore, saving money) for the clients.

I then moved over to the design side for 7 years, doing anything from QC/regulatory checks to sourcing vendors for major clients. This included finding container structure suppliers, pigment suppliers, printers, converters, contract packagers and more. I worked with global offices to mainstream a proprietary color library, consolidate global artwork, manage production art and more.

I'm now in business for myself (but also open to job offers, as the economy is slow right now). I'm working on my Regulatory Affairs Certification and working on going back to school for my Pharm D as time allows.

Major clients who's projects I've worked on over the years include: AT&T, P&G, Kimberly-Clark, Delta Faucets, Clopay, Xavier University, See's Candy, Comco, Campbell-Hausfeld, Iams/Eukanuba, Frito-Lay, Dean's Foods, Birdseye, John Morrell, Kenner, Frontgate, Kraft Foods, Publix, Kroger, Coca-Cola, Edward's Pies, Farm Rich/SeaPak, Sunny D, Wrigley's, KAO Brands and Colgate-Palmolive.

Julie Schramm Dartt’s Specialties:

Legal/Regulatory labeling for OTC, CPG and food product packaging, Project Management, Print Feasibility, Design Implementation (packaging artwork), Quality Control/Assurance.
