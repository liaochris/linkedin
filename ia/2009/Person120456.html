Jose Guerra’s Summary

Jose is President and founder of L5 Source, an Information Technology firm providing software development services to US based companies through strategic outsourcing strategies.

He has been an IT executive for 26 years, leading large-scale implementation of e-business, Customer Relationship Management, Supply Chain and Business Intelligence solutions. His experience also includes managing computing and networking infrastructure environments for Fortune 500 companies. Jose has had extensive experience leading global and domestic IT organizations within diverse industries including Consumer Packaged Goods, Telecommunications, Retail and Energy. His positions at Procter & Gamble, Convergys, Nine West Group, and Cinergy Corp have allowed him to help these companies streamline their supply chains, grow market share, and strengthen customer-supplier relationships.

Jose volunteers his time to Hispanic issues and serves on the boards of the Cincinnati Chapter of the National Society of Hispanic MBAs (NSHMBA), the Hispanic Chamber Cincinnati USA, Hispanics Avanzando Hispanics, and the Hispanic IT Executive Council (HITEC).

Jose did his undergraduate work at the Universidad Iberoamericana in Mexico City and obtained his MBA from Xavier University in Cincinnati, graduating Beta Gamma Sigma.

Jose Guerra’s Specialties:

Assessing organizational needs and developing integrated IT approaches to meet changing national and international business environments such as M&A, divestitures, market expansion, turnarounds, and deregulation.

Expertise in weaving and aligning business and IT strategies together across the entire IT value chain, including business applications and infrastructure management. Skilled negotiator with track record for developing strategic collaborative partnerships with third parties.
