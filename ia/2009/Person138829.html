Leah Burdick’s Summary

We provide marketing services, products and education to help entrepreneurs achieve their vision! Entrepreneurs typically fall into one of three buckets:

(1) Need clarity on brand, market positioning, competitive differentiation, external messaging and a plan to tell the TARGET market about it. If you aren't clear on these elements, your marketing dollars on all the rest are wasted. Here we offer marketing plans, brand/competitive differentiation strategy reports, naming services, logos and business card design.

(2) Need tools to communicate your message. If you fall in this category, we'll provide website strategy, web design/development, landing pages, blogs, company brochures, sell sheets and the like.

(3) Need to set it and forget it. We work with our clients to provide AUTOMATED tools to distribute your message. This includes online and offline campaigns including email autoresponder campaigns, printed card campaigns, social media marketing services and various sales programs.
