Larry Heller’s Summary

Larry has been helping small business owners for over thirty years. Having been in both the corporate world and the world of small business he brings a unique blend of experiences and proven business skills to his clients. Larry has shared his sales and leadership experience with scores of business owners coast to coast changing lives and making friends along the way.

Larry approaches the challenges of today’s business with the confidence and passion for performance that invigorates his clients and motivates their employees.

Larry is a licensed business coach living in Atlanta Georgia with his wife Catherine. Larry graduated from Western Michigan University in 1972 with a B.S. in Engineering. He is a graduate of the Volvo Executive Leadership and past member of the Audi National Marketing Counsel.

Larry Heller’s Specialties:

Cash Management. Exit &Succession Strategy, Sales and Marketing, Breakeven Modeling, Employee Accountability, Leadership Training, Functional Organization Structuring, Utilizing Financial Intelligence, Benchmarking Performance, Loan Risk Assessment.
