Will Campbell’s Summary

Willie Campbell brings over 11 years of past and present leadership experience in Information Technology (I.T.). Willie began his I.T. career serving in the US Air Force (USAF). He served five years in the USAF, where he performed multiple lead roles such as database tech lead, communications center and help desk floor lead, and was a supervisor at the 27th Intel squadron. Will received USAF Leadership Training and he was directly responsible for ensuring team objectives were met to support the USAF mission. In addition to leadership roles, Will gained technical experience working with multiple technologies (Microsoft, UNIX, Sybase, and Solaris) and also became Microsoft certified in 2000.

After completing his Air Force military commitment, Will begun working a tech lead role at Northrop Grumman. At Northrop Grumman, Will was responsible for leading a team of five system administrators to setup a Network Operations Systems Center (NOSC) in Hampton, VA. Some of the task performed in his tech lead role at Northrop Grumman consisted of:

- Establishing team direction and defining the scope for setting up the NOSC
- Leading technical meetings to discuss progress on NOSC implementation
- Creating a standard Image and Ghosting processes for the XP systems sent to Iraq
- Designing and implementing the Active Directory infrastructure
- Setting up and configuring Symantec and ISA firewalls
- Serving as Security Guru and Lead

Willie's next step in his career landed him at at Microsoft in November 2004.

Willie constantly strives to broaden his knowledge in the I.T. field. He has obtained multiple certifications working as a full-time I.T. professional for the past 11 years and has found time to complete higher educational goals. Willie has an Associates Degree in Information Systems (I.S.), a Bachelors Degree in Computer Networking with a minor in Computer I.S., and a Masters Degree in Management I.S.

IT Management, IT Consultation, Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist in Active Directory and Networking, Telecommuting best practices, Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer, Microsoft Certified System Administrator, Cisco Certified Network Associate, Advanced Networking planning, Windows Server 2003, Windows Server 2008, Network Security, Virtualization, ITIL Certified
