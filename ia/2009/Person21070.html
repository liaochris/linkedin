Kevin Tuohy’s Summary

My background is diverse and varied; from construction, demolition and military security to customer service, hospitality and special events.

My partners and I are celebrating 13 years at A Shine & Co., a socially responsible business owned and operated by recovered alcoholics.
Currently we have five shoeshine stands with shoe repair service as well as concessions with the San Francisco Giants, and jetBlue Airways at JFK airport in New York .

We also provide entertaining shoeshines at events for Cisco Systems, Johnnie Walker, GQ Magazine, Crown Royal, Macy's and the San Francisco Chamber of Commerce to name a few.

My personal goal is:
To serve both staff and client with respect and provide a superior product;
To reflect my gratitude as much as possible in all my affairs;
To shine the world!
