Shuchi Kalra’s Summary

I am a freelance academic and creative writer, web content developer and a contributing writer for leading national and international magazines, websites and portals.
I enjoy writing on a variety of subjects focusing mainly on lifestyle,food and wine, travel, health, environment/ wildlife, pet-care and general-interest articles.
