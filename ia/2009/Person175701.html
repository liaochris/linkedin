Nilda Velez’s Summary

Nilda Velez is an integrated communications professional whose career has been spent effectively marketing Fortune 500 products and services to Hispanics both in the US and abroad on both the Agency & Client side.

Today, Nilda is the Managing Director of the New York offices of The Bravo Group, Young & Rubicam's pioneer division specialized in Hispanic marketing who after 28+ years continues to be a leader among Hispanic integrated communications agency groups in the country. Young & Rubicam is one of the world’s most successful advertising agencies, owned by the second largest communications and advertising group worldwide, WPP.

As the Managing Director of the New York office, Nilda is responsible for day-to-day management of Agency and of maintaining solid P&L performance, the establishment of high standards of quality in agency product from strategy through implementation and nurturing and growing existing Client partnerships as well as lead new business acquisition. Nilda also collaborates and contributes to WPP network local, regional and global initiatives.

Nilda Velez’s Specialties:

Hispanic Marketing, Promotions, Grassroots events, Consumer Packaged Goods
