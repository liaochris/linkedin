Charles Bronson’s Summary

My passion is equipping the organization's sales force with the capabilities to expand wallet share with the existing customer base. My goal is to then teach the sales force how to create new customers by co-developing the customer's value proposition.

As senior sales executive, I have implemented these processes, disciplines, and governance systems which result in sales growth at a rate faster than industry average. As a consultant in the field of sales efficiency, I diagnosed and addressed what elite, world-class sales organizations are doing to meet sales challenges which impede success.

In sales management roles, I have coached and mentored sales professionals to reach their highest levels of effectiveness, using tools to develop sales competencies. Bringing science to the field of sales management, the vice president of sales or the director of sales can produce forecasts which can be sustained, no matter the state of the global economy.
