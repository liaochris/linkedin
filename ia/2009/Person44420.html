Christian Oliver’s Summary

Interested in leveraging digital media to optimize marketing strategies, multiple-media publishing, product development and innovation in media.
I believe in media-agnostic content production and building relationships with the audience throwout all viable media outlets.
With more than 13 years of experience in New Media, I'm one of the pioneers in on-line newspapers in Latin American, leading the development of eluniversal.com in Venezuela since 1995.
My experience is also extensive in Strategic Planning, private equity and on-line marketing.
I keep track of the latest developments in media, personal technology, Web 2.0 and mobile applications in order to identify opportunities for media organizations, particularly for content generators.
I've been a speaker in several high profile conferences and seminars on topics such as the Newsroom of the Future and Trends in Digital Media.

Christian Oliver’s Specialties:

Digital Media, New Media, Marketing, Strategic Planning, Investment and Valuation, Organizational Design, Product Development, Innovation, Change Management, Project Management, Corporate Communications, Print Media, Newspapers, Partnerships.
