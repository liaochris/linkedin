Bengt Hamsten’s Experience

  • Professor

    Chongqing University, China

    (Education Management industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    Product Development Process, Project Management, Commercial Vehicle Technology, Management Consulting, Technology Management.

  • CEO, MAN Truck and Bus, China

    MAN Nutzfahrzeuge

    (Automotive industry)

    April 2004 — April 2007 (3 years 1 month)

    The task was to form of a new company as a legal entity, MAN Truck and Bus (China) Ltd, the base for further development of the MAN commercial vehicle business in China.
    One idea was to start up a MAN heavy truck engine factory in China, to find cooperation with truck customers and to continue the development of bus activities in China. However, in 2006 the decision was made not to pursue any large projects in favor of new developments in India.

  • CEO and VP Engineering

    NEOMAN GmbH

    (Automotive industry)

    2001 — 2004 (3 years)

    The bus and coach company of MAN AG in Munich
    The assignment was to form the NEOMAN Bus company, merging two bus companies into one new entity. After three years we renewed the product program, rearranged the production plants (Turkey and Poland), reorganized the company and had brought a massive loss situation into profit.

  • Vice President R&D

    EvoBus

    (Public Company; Automotive industry)

    1990 — 2001 (11 years)

    I started as Technical VP (Strategy, R&D, Purchasing, Production) in the Kässbohrer Bus Company, which was aquired by Daimler in 1995 and fusioned with the Mercedes Bus Company into EvoBus.
    I was responsible for development and technology of all bus products called Mercedes-Benz and Setra worldwide.

  • Vice President R&D

    Volvo Bus Corporation

    (Public Company; Automotive industry)

    1985 — 1990 (5 years)

    Head of Engineering, member of the management team.
    I reorganized the R&D function and introduced modern tools for the product development process, CAD, FEM, FMEA, etc. Development of new products for example the B7R which is still very successful in Asia.

  • Chief Engineer Interior Equipment

    Volvo Car Corporation

    (Public Company; F; Automotive industry)

    1971 — 1985 (14 years)

    Starting as a test engineer over project management to packaging and technical coordination and ending as a department manager.
    I had the fortune of working in the center of the development and industrialization of a new car family from the first styling ideas to full series production of the model range. After that I was responsible for the development of complete special vehicles and all car accessories.

  • Project engineer

    Götaverken

    (Shipbuilding industry)

    1969 — 1971 (2 years)

    Mostly technical calculations. Working with the complete drive line of large ships. The interaction of engine, propeller and hull design and securing the contracted trial trip speed.

  • Sergeant

    Swedish Army

    (Government Agency; Military industry)

    1964 — 1965 (1 year)

    Military education / infantry officer
