Sharon Hiebing, Send Out Cards’s Summary

Sharon is a Manager for Send Out Cards, a web-based relationship marketing strategy system that allows you to send REAL greeting cards and gifts from your computer that are printed, stuffed, addressed, stamped, and mailed for you through the postal system (not e-cards) for a fraction of the time and cost.

You can create your own cards from photos and artwork, and write the cards in your own handwriting with your own signature (or multiple signatures), and all cards are under $1.

This system is instrumental in keeping customer by allowing you to receive email reminders for birthdays and anniversaries being automated to send multi-card direct mail campaigns. Business owners need to ask themselves what is a customer lifetime value? Sharon can help you answer that question and develop a marketing plan that meets your goals.

Send Out Cards is a network marketing (mlm) company and has many distributors like Sharon earning income from home on a very part-time basis. Earning lifetime residual income by caring and connecting with others is something she is passionate about and loves teaching others how to do it. She is always looking for entrepreneurs who would like to start their own home based business.
