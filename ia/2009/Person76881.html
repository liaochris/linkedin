Abbey LeVine’s Summary

Event Producer passionate about helping clients telling stories through food, music, texture and interactive events. Private Parties, Corporate Events and some Weddings... design and production. I can serve up the entire production or come to the rescue last minute. It is imperative that my clients trust me so they can depend on me. I'd rather create long term relationships than spread myself too thin.

Every Party Tells A Story examines some of the psychology behind events and works VERY closely with clients to deliver fresh ideas, dependable vendors and endless energy! We believe its about not just the elements, but the people, as well.

Abbey LeVine’s Specialties:

Skilled at Live Production coordination, Managing many moving parts, Asking the Right Questions, Food and Wine selection, Looking at a blank room and creating a vision, Helping Clients juggle events and other professional and personal responsibilities.
