Natasha Woodford’s Summary

I am an Executive Search consultant proactive in the recruitment of mid to executive positions internationally. I work with companies, assisting in the strategic growth of their businesses, in sourcing key personnel for senior commerical and technical roles throughout the world.

Recent projects succesfully undertaken have been in Austria, Italy, Russia, Poland, Spain, Netherlands, Sweden, Germany, Africa, Middle East and UK. Many of my clients have been high tech companies focused on the Telecoms or Financial Services industry. Ranging from established international manufacturers, solutions vendors, systems integrators and service providers, through to small but growing entrepreneurial (startup) high tech companies. In addition, I have worked with venture capital firms and their Financial Service investments

My network of contacts include the CxO level and are decision makers in business - I am always keen to further my network of contacts of decision makers internationally.
