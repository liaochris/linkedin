Bruce Bley’s Experience

  • Founder and Owner

    Environmental Product Solutions (EPS)

    (Environmental Services industry)

    December 2003 — Present (5 years 3 months)

    EPS is a GREEN technologies provider for residential and commercial clients. EPS products can purify the air, treat the water, protect the electrical system and save clients money on their electrical bill. Our air purifiers can kill germs from across the room safely, effectively, all day, every day.

    Mr. Bley is a qualified Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) consultant with manufacturer certifications to evaluate IAQ problems and custom design systems using proprietary PCO products for residential, commercial, institutional and light industrial applications.

    Mr. Bley is also qualified to evaluate single-phase and three-phase electrical systems in residences and businesses and provide systems that protect against harmful power surges and spikes, provide electronic noise and harmonic distortion suppression and reduce electrical consumption by improving the efficiency and extending the useful life of motors on a system.

    These products can qualify for USGBC LEED points in many applications.

Many of the articles are picked up by in-house publications and used in various industry and special interests newsletters.

    Bruce frequently speaks to local school, civic and professional groups about technology solutions available to solve many of today’s indoor environmental problems. He explains how they can have fresh air, clean water and a toxin-free environment.
