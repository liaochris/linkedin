Jeetendra Joshi’s Summary

I take this opportunity to introduce myself as a seasoned e-commerce / Marketing professional with over a decade’s rich corporate experience within Indian Luxury industry.

I hold the distinction of initiating and developing the E-Commerce channel for Diamond & jewellery Industry in India. I am also distinguished for conceptualising the new launches for lifestyle Jewellery stores on internet. I have the record of customising need based products as per the market for greater value addition and performance.

The most important among all was my association with ebay.com (India) Pvt. Ltd. I got the opportunity to venture into the field of e-commerce when the concept was in its infant stage. I joined the organization in July 2003 and learnt the basics of Business–to-consumer model. I was managing Jewelry and Watches category in ebay.in formerly Baazee.com, world's leading marketplace.

The latest company in my Career Portfolio is B. Vijaykumar & Comapany. I am executing the responsibilities of Chief Executive Officer of B.Vijay Internet Initiatives Pvt.Ltd. Which have launched www.trijewels.com India's first jewelry portal.

The aforementioned experiences and attributes have not only helped me grow professionally but also have complemented the academe. I am a Graduate of Parle College, Mumbai University with Statistics as my special Paper. I have also completed a Diploma course in Import & Export Management from Indian Merchant Chamber in 1995. Additionally, I have done a Certificate Course in Web Designing alongwith attending a Professional Program in Retail Management from Wellingkar’s Institute of Management to sharpen my professional skills. Currently doing specialized course in serach engine marketing from worlds leading Search engine education institute Marketing experiements Labs, USA.
