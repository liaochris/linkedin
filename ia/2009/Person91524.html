    (Military industry)

    January 2008 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    ES provides innovative Military Solutions in System and Software Engineering .The company has a high prospect of penetrating the international market in more than one manner and in new fields, such as media, industry, transportation and medicine.
    ES has a deep knowledge of operative communication protocols/standards in "civilian world", Military networks require more complex architecture than "civilian" networks. The following factors contribute to this complexity:
    - Unstable end-to-end connectivity between source and destination
    - Limited bandwidth allowance per source and/or destination
    - Strict prioritization requirements
    - Hard real-time requirements
    - Traffic and protocols restrictions because of special military network devices (gateways, encoders, firewalls, etc)
    - ES "adapts" civilian standards to military communication applications. It allows to meet strong military requirements and still to be fully interoperable with existing civilian standards/protocols.
