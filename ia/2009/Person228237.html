Don Synstelien’s Summary

Strategist for social networks -- expert knowledgeable in the development of large scale, high-use platforms and products, advertiser metrics, monetization, widgets, apps, mobile, brand advertising and games in relation to social networks.

I've successfully launched several products for companies like Myspace, Fox Sports and The Weather Channel among many others. Most recently, the team I led redesigned all MySpace mobile products, including iPhone, Blackberry, Android and m.myspace.com

Other products of note: I founded SpringWidgets.com, Initiated and led the creation of two analytics/metrics products. I created prototypes for, conceptualized or spearheaded several major initiatives at FIM for the first two years, including such diverse projects as a self serve ad system, FIM's first developer network and Several internal redesigns of MySpace products for senior management.

I've worked on more top-secret projects than public ones and led a team who's products influenced many tens of millions in sales across Myspace and other various FIM properties.

I'm looking for a position that will allow me to supersede everything that I have done before and have fun while doing it.
