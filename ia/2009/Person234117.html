Tim Salaver, MBA, PMP, CSSMBB’s Summary

Tim Salaver is Principal of Cornerstone Systems Solutions as a strategy and technology consultant. He has more than 20 years experience in leadership positions in small- and large-sized organizations in operations, enterprise, and supply chain management. He is the President of the Golden Gate Chapter of APICS The Association for Operations Management. He is also a Founder and Chief Strategy Officer for the Bio Supply Management Alliance. He was Vice-President, Operations and General Manager, for Alphaville Design where he led product and operations efficiency initiatives. He was Executive Vice-President, Products for Dana Software where he led strategy and product planning. He was a Director of Information Systems for terraspring which was acquired by Sun Microsystems. He has held management positions at Barton Healthcare, Innoventry, National Insurance Group, Lawson Software, and Data Design Associates.

Tim uses the building villages concept in creating business and project communities. He loves mentoring and guiding his teams for business success and individual achievement. Through extensive professional development and team-building, Tim inspires his teams to exceed their own expectations.

His leadership of a Healthcare ERP project received the 2006 VHA Leadership Award for Supply Chain Management Improvement. He is a highly sought speaker at user groups, international conferences, and higher education institutions throughout the world giving his remarkable perspective on individual and corporate development, operations and supply chain management, and innovative technological solutions. He is the author of the white paper “Inventory Management for Small and Medium-sized Businesses” which was released by Cisco’s BizWiseTV and the Innovators Forum. In 2000, Tim was inducted into the International Who’s Who of Information Technology.
