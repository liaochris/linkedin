Karen Swaeby’s Summary

Create!form applications generally require little administration or maintenance once they are cleanly implemented. With a new project it can be a challenge to find resources to get modules implemented quickly. For existing installations it can be difficult to find personnel with the knowledge (or time) required to make the changes needed (upgrades, new modules, additional forms, etc.).

If Create!form projects remain unresolved due to a lack of experience or priority in the organization, I can help move those forward.

Karen Swaeby’s Specialties:

My expertise includes forms design and development as well as implementation, upgrades and training for all Create!form software modules.
