Mary Kay Parce’s Summary

As a personal trainer, motivational speaker, and life coach in the health and fitness industry, I continue to develop programs to support men, women, and children in maintaining personal fitness of the body and mind. The Yoga Pilates Center provides students the opportunity to expand their knowledge of yoga, nutrition, pilates, and fitness as well as learn to live within a community to share their personal life experiences with other members. The power to come together as a community is the key to healthly living. Now more than ever it is time to "dream.create.educate" our world ... my goal is to begin with the Yoga Pilates Center and to expand the business nationwide.

Mary Kay Parce’s Specialties:

Specialty classes include vinyasa yoga, pilates mat and reformer, core training, indoor bootcamp and restorative yoga. Teaching meditation, daily journaling, and dream boards are dynamic tools to enhance mindful living. I use my skills as a spiritual life coach to support individuals seeking clarity in their life.
