Michael Kelly’s Summary

ClickMail Marketing is an Email Solutions Provider, and offers the nation's largest selection of Email Service Providers (ESPs). We specialize in optimizing the ROI for email marketing initiatives. We take a holistic approach in identifying and coordinating all the moving parts that determine the level of success of an email campaign.

We've partnered with appr 2 dozen top-tier Email Service Providers (ESPs) which allows us to be objective and platform-neutral. We work under the premise that there is no one 'best' ESP, but depending on the particular email/emarketing initiatives - there will be a short-list of ESPs that are 'best-suited' for the project. We help to identify and select from that short list.

Our clients have the option to use us as an extension of their marketing team (i.e. service bureau and fulfillment), or operate autonomously once we've helped them select/deploy the ideal delivery engine for their objectives.

We keep up with the moving target that is email best practices, and share that information with our clients. We also provide additional consultative services as needed, along with sharing our repository of white papers, articles, case studies, and reports - based on topics such as deliverability, email optimization, legislation, and many others.

Michael Kelly’s Specialties:

Helping companies select the best-suited ESP for their email marketing programs; Optimizing content for email deliverability; managing/optimizing tactical execution for highest ROI; integrating ESPs to virtually any 3rd party (via APIs to CRM, Web Analytics, etc...); custom applications/development specializing in Flash (dashboards, 'reportals', and custom personalized flash production emails/webcontent (yes, insertable merge data into flash content)
