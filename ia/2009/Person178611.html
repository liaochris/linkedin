Omar Augusto Pinto’s Summary

Prior to Green Energy Solutions Management (GESM) as a General Partner & EVP Omar was Vice President of Global Consulting and Business Development at VH Clean Environment LLC. (VHCE), an Environmental Science & Energy Conservation Management Consulting firm associated with Subject Matter Experts (SME's) in various areas related to environment, energy and building/plant management with performing projects located in various North, Central & South American cities. In this position responsible for the origination and delivery of consulting projects Omar and his partners managed Alliances with local consulting/technical experts billing fees that generated exceptional revenue and profit from each project to the organization.

Experience as a leader include organizations as GESM, VHCE, SAI, Bitco Enterprises/Gateway Capital Corp/ITSS, DSS, and MultiCorp. Achieving Growth, Revenue and Profit targets in each.

Formal education in International Business & Management Information Systems and State of Virginia issued certifications for Sales/Insurance/Finance Licenses, complement over 25 years of combined experience in International Growth Initiatives, Strategic Business Development & Sales to Federal & Local Government clients, Project/Contract/Program Management, Company/Program/Product Branding & Identity campaigns, Joint Ventures, Partnering Agreements/Management, International/Offshore Outsourcing, PR and Relationship Management in Americas, India and Greater China (GC) projects.
