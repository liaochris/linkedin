Dan McKenzie’s Summary

I have over 10 years experience in design and technology specializing in online branding, visual design, design management and interaction design. I am currently working at 17FEET, an interactive agency in San Francisco, as the lead user experience designer. 17FEET specializes in product development for the digital medium and works on a variety of projects ranging from web sites to applications for mobile devices.

Before joining 17FEET, I worked several years as Creative Director at JiWire, a tech start-up focused on Wi-Fi and advertising for mobile devices. At JiWire, I had the opportunity to build the company’s brand from the ground-up and design for some big partners including, Microsoft, Intel, Sony, Marriott, AT&T and others.

I am an advocate of user-focused design and enjoy building experiences that matter. Through research, investigation and an iterative design process, I believe great experiences can be built on purpose. Early in my career I was a musician, artist, teacher and traveler, living abroad for some years.

For more information please visit http://www.danielmckenzie.com

To read my journal on a variety of design issues, visit http://www.danielmckenzie.com/blog
