Norm Yustin’s Summary

Living on 3 continents, working in 20 countries, and building over 100 brands – more than 25 of them #1 in their category – has turned Norm into a 24/7 executive marketer. Succeeding in C-level positions within a large multi-billion dollar client organization and a billion dollar advertising agency, has given him a well-rounded view from both sides of the fence.
 
Whether building brands on the client or agency side – for mega brands such as Macy’s, Procter & Gamble, Estée Lauder, Mercedes Benz, Ikea, Polo Ralph Lauren, Johnnie Walker, Martha Stewart, Nintendo, Activision and Tommy Hilfiger to mention a few – Norm is driven to express his business acumen and creativity in unexpected ways to get results.

Dynamic change in the marketplace requires dynamic change in business thinking. And this is his specialty: bringing new ideas, structures, and ways of doing business, then operationalizing them within large organizations. Currently, Norm is working with brands, organizations and celebrities to help re-invent their businesses and marketing models for maximum growth potential. This includes developing digital expertise with a specialization in the future of mobile business and marketing.
 
During his career, eight marketing campaigns have been awarded Effie’s. Fourteen have been acknowledged at Cannes, including a Gold Lion for media innovation. Other awards and acknowledgments have been at the Marketing Awards, Kelly Awards, Clio Awards, and the One Show. 
 

Norm Yustin’s Specialties:

Re-inventing businesses, iPhone apps and all digital and mobile marketing applications, branding and identity, retail marketing, training and development.
