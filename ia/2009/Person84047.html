  • Member Technical Staff

    Cadence Design Systems India Pvt Ltd

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; Computer Software industry)

    2004 — 2006 (2 years)

    In Cadence, I used to work in development team of BG (BuildGates) and RC (RTL Compiler) products. Both these products are Cadence's serving in front end area of chip designing tool, also known as EDA tools. In Cadence I got an opportunity to work with few of the brilliant minds in EDA industry. Both these tools are basically compilers, which convert the given chip design in HDL (Hardware Design Language), also knon as RTL (Register Transfer Language), to the logic gate level chip design, known as netlist. I was part of the team which was responsible for developing the frontend of the tool that used to read and interpret the given HDL chip design and used to create the Control and Data Flow Graphs. Further down the flow, these graphs are converted into generic netlist by these EDA tools. From frontend perspective, EDA tools were almost same as any compiler, as most of the compiler development concepts and fundamentals are used in the development of frontend of EDA tools.
