Carl Hansen’s Summary

Carl Hansen is a local market expert based in San Francisco. He is also an integral part of a highly productive corporate services team as a transaction specialist. Mr. Hansen began his real estate career at CB Richard Ellis in the Global Corporate Services division in 2002, assisting in a variety of corporate consulting assignments. After having completed CBRE University training in brokerage, client service, financial analysis, and account management, Mr. Hansen joined the San Francisco transaction management team where he is focused exclusively on serving the real estate transaction needs of corporate occupiers.
