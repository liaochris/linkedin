Andy Polaine’s Summary

SO, WHAT DO YOU DO?
One of the problems with Linked In is that it only gives you the choice of one primary area of expertise. What do I do? Mainly interaction design, research, writing and lecturing with a smattering of service design (of which I'd like to do more).

WHAT DO I WANT TO DO?
Put together my skills boil down to anything that allows me to:

- mentor and lead a creative team (pleasantly for all involved usually)
- develop engaging interaction design and service design projects
- research and draw disparate spheres of thought together and synthesise these into compelling words
- speak easily and off-the-cuff in public about a wide range of areas
- do something useful for the planet

Andy Polaine’s Specialties:

Interactive media, emerging technologies, research, education, journalism, writing, service design
