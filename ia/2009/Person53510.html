Daniele Pesaresi’s Summary

I started my career as a technician developer for car components, and quickly became a team leader. I then switched to Car Design, after a master in automotive design; car interiors at first, with a focus on ergonomics, materials and finishings. Moved to exteriors, focusing on purity definition, proportions, advanced design, dream creation. After that I spent 10 months developing my artistic skills focusing on 3D and 2D illustration, rendering, animation, storyboarding. I'm now proud to be the senior designer of one of the coolest Design Studios on the scene. We have numerous important custumers and different kinds of projects, my work ranges from brainstorming to market research, to advanced design, sketching, project managing, engineering, finished elements, cost analysis, prototyping and - last but not least - managing the crew of the junior team. I’m responsable for the whole project, from the concept to the delivery of the industrialized product to the customers, which is amazing. I’m planning to move in the next few years to the managerial side of strategic design, for which I'm currently studying. I love languages, my mother tongue is Italian but I am fluent in English, Spanish and French and have a basic knowledge of German. I aim to be a complete strategic-design-engineer-manager-available worldwide. Oh, i am handsome, already 8-)
