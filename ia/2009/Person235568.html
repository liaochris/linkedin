Tom Steele - tsteele@jobs2web.com’s Summary

Follow me on Twitter http://twitter.com/tomassteele

As a passionate Web 2.0 guru in recruiting, I assist employers in attracting talent through the Jobs2Web Recruitment Marketing Platform (RMP). The RMP allows our clients to building their own proprietary Talent Community so they won't have to ever rely on the candidate community of a job board. The Jobs2Web RMP automatically markets employers jobs on the web thru the search engines like Google, job aggregators and by deploying Social Media Integration strategies. Our Web 2.0 centric approach allows employers to keep on top of and utilize the latest in Web 2.0 tools to attract talent. This increases recruiter efficiency and allows our clients to reduce their overall recruiting budgets!
