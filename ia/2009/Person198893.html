Riemer Thalen’s Summary

Sherpa's are guides and bearers at the same time. That's what I do. I guide clients when they are getting lost in unknown territory. And if need be, I do the hard work when the going gets tough.
Having run an ad agency for altmost 20 years, I know how to set goals and how to achieve them.

Currently, I am hired as a parttime interim manager at Sanelco (medical do-it-yourself tests).

My other activities:
* Consulting in marketing and communication; execution and project management of promotional campaigns, mostly on a project basis.
* Writing scripts (video), websites, advertisements and direct mail. (No, I do not write copy for brochures, folders, editorials and so on!)
* Google AdWords campaigns. Trained as a direct marketeer and a direct response copywriter, I think AdWords is the ultimate DM tool. Many people have wasted money on poorly set-up AdWords campaigns, because they do not know how to test, how to optimize (writing ads and landing pages is a skill), how to reduce costs and how to improve their revenues. I do.

Riemer Thalen’s Specialties:

direct marketing, AdWords campaigns, business-to-business marketing, script writing, creating names for companies and products.

  • owner / partner

    Thalen - reclame & direct marketing

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    July 1998 — July 2006 (8 years 1 month)

    Thalen bv was een voortzetting van Janssen & Thalen - marketingcommunicatie. Het bureau deed alles wat reclamebureaus plegen te doen: van advies tot uitvoering, van huisstijl tot themacampagne. Het specialisme was direct marketing, vooral vanwege mijn eigen affiniteit met ICT en respons-gestuurde communicatie en vanwege mijn achtergrond als copywriter.
    Opdrachtgevers kwamen vooral uit de BtoB-sector, de non-profit markt (overheden) en de database-driven consumentenmarketing.
    Thalen stond bekend als strategisch sterk en copy-georiënteerd. Het was vroeg actief met het gebruik van Internet, toen nog vooral websites. Er werkten 12 mensen.
    In 2004 is het bureau overgenomen door TBWA. Het bestaat niet meer in de oorspronkelijke samenstelling en cultuur.

  • founder / partner

    Janssen & Thalen - marketingcommunicatie

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 1986 — July 1998 (12 years 7 months)

    Janssen & Thalen was founded as a creative hotshop by art director Rob Janssen and copywriter Riemer Thalen. Within two years, the hotshop developed into a full-service ad agency, employing 8 - 10 personnel.
    In the wake of the ICT and Internet revolution, Rob and I turned out to have different views. Rob started JanssenVos communicatie, I continued the agency renaming it Thalen.
