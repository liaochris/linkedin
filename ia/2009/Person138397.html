Laurie Pehar Borsh’s Summary

As a personal publicist and representative, I produce and manage personal brands and publicity campaigns for professionals who are looking for opportunity placement and for those who are looking to achieve consistent professional exposure (personal publicity) for expansion or movement purposes in the markets on an ongoing/long term basis.

www.corpshorts.com/lauriepehar.htm or www.personalprproductions.com for information.

Maintaining a well written, well placed and consistent Personal Brand is so important to have in place these days in to achieve greater visibility in the markets and to be and stay competitive in a challenging business environment.

However, most professionals (even the most successful executives in business today) just do not have the time, ability or desire to “self produce” and literally manage a personal publicity campaign (and it does not matter how easy the tools are to use).

My service model is a cost effective outsource personal publicity/advertising production and management solution for busy professionals (LinkedIn or not).
