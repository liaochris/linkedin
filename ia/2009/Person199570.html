Robert K. Lear’s Summary

Robert Kyle Lear is a consultant, an author, a direct response marketer and entrepreneur who is focused on using his forward thinking and energy to collaborate with other entrepreneurs and organizations to form strategic alliances that enhance their visibility in the marketplace.
