John Suter’s Summary

I have extensive experience in web development, administration, and management. Most recently, the relaunching of the Al & Ed's Autosound e-commerce site. I have successfully managed information systems migrations and integrations, as well as several office relocations. For Al & Ed's Autosound, I managed the conversion from Point of Sale dumb terminals connecting over leased lines to Windows PC communicating securely with VPN over DSL, saving thousands of dollars a month.

I was a member of a four person management team that achieved the only profitable month outside of a holiday selling season at that specific Good Guys location.

As the youngest store manager for Al & Ed's Autosound, I was the only store lead to be both Installer First Class and Product Sales Specialist certified by the MECP (a division of the Consumer Electronics Association). I also ran a store that was consistently in the top 3 of 40 in both sales volume and gross profit.

For the past several years, I have attended California State Polytechnic University, Pomona in pursuit of a Bachelors of Science degree in Business Administration focusing on Computer Information Systems. In the future, I plan to pursue a MBA.
