Clancy Cross’s Experience

  • Partner

    Development Beyond Learning -- USA

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    December 2008 — Present (3 months)

    Development Beyond Learning (DBL) was founded to solve a problem – training OFTEN doesn't work! Consequently, nothing changes. The DBL philosophy is, "change occurs when beliefs are impacted."

    Ordinary people just need a big enough reason "why" and an opportunity to do extraordinary things. The DBL approach helps our clients:

    – discover their "why"
    – build competency and confidence with information and experience
    – become empowered with tools, techniques, processes, methodologies AND encouragement

    The best part? DBL helps ensure the momentum continues beyond the learning event into the daily lives of every participant. We call this "Enlighten, Engage, Empower!"

    As our client, you can choose a standard or a customized program. Either way, DBL will create a personalized experience that is sure to increase the success of your organization.

    DBL has clients throughout the Pacific Rim and will be opening its North America Division in 2009.
