John Hering’s Summary

An Adaptable Visionary, I believe in social and environmental progress through personal investment in community. I have experience in teaching, writing, editing, research, event coordination, small to mid-sized group management, light general administration and web-site design and maintenance; I easily learn new concepts and get along well with people; and I am relatively computer literate in both software and hardware. I have been active in the theatrical, musical and literary arts.

While my long-term goal is to further develop and apply my skills to enrich the communities of which I'm a member, my more immediate aim is to contribute substantively to the sustenance of my family.
