  • National Association of REALTORS®

    GRI Designation, Real Estate Practice and Theory

    GRI (Graduate, Realtor® Institute) Designation holders have completed a predetermined course of study and curriculum that requires a minimum of 90 hours of specialized Real Estate coursework.

    These specialized studies focus on a body of knowledge for progressive real estate professionals. REALTORS® who have earned the GRI designation have received enhanced training in many areas of real estate that equips the REALTOR® to better serve and protect their clients.

    GRI Designation holders also subscribes to the Code of Ethics of the NATIONAL ASSOCIATION OF REALTORS®.

    Holders of the CSP Desgination (Certified New Home Sales Professional) are required to complete a broad-spectrum, predetermined course of study.

    The CSP was designed for specialists in new home sales to enhance their professional image, increase their marketability in the home building industry and to enhance the Designation holder's skill set in order to sell more homes!
