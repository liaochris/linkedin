George Grellas’s Summary

Startup business legal specialist, having represented thousands of founders and entrepreneurs - and the companies they serve - from within Silicon Valley since 1984.

George Grellas’s Specialties:

Company formation, founder arrangements, IP protection, licensing and distribution, bridge and preferred stock funding, acquisitions - representing primarily independent-minded entrepreneurs and their companies.

George Grellas’s Experience

  • Founder and Principal

    George Grellas & Associates

    (Law Practice industry)

    1984 — Present (25 years)

    Legal counsel, strategic adviser, and head of boutique firm specializing in Silicon Valley startups, focusing primarily on independent-minded entrepreneurs with early-stage startup companies.

    We handle the range of startup matters from company formation to exit, including founder arrangements, equity incentive plans, ISOs, NQOs, restricted stock, IP protection, technology licensing and distribution, strategic alliances, OEM deals, bridge funding, preferred stock financing, key employment contracts (CEOs, etc.), trade secret and unfair competition issues, and mergers, acquisitions, and reorganizations.

    I have been working with startups almost exclusively for over two decades from within Silicon Valley and have represented many successful companies, entrepreneurs, and executives within the startup field.

    Our approach to startups is distinctive in that we represent only founders and entrepreneurs and their startup business ventures. We respect VCs but we do not represent them or hold interests in them that can potentially conflict with those of our client companies.

  • Associate Attorney

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Law Practice industry)

    1980 — 1984 (4 years)

    Initially trained as a commercial litigator working for a premier San Francisco law firm specializing in commercial litigation and Fortune 500 commercial representation.
