Carina Nørklit’s Summary

I have completed an international MBA in France in July 2008.

I am presently working on the start up of http://www.ATAWAS.com - a community based online service exchange platform. I am leading the development and implementation of the e-business- and marketing strategy.

ATAWAS is a website where individuals and businesses can advertise their service offer and connect directly to individuals or businesses that are in need of their service. In Parallel, individuals and businesses that are in need of or have an interest in a specific service can advertise their need and connect directly to individuals and businesses that offer this service. There is theoretically no limit on the type of service that can be advertised or traded through ATAWAS. ATAWAS can be used for free by anyone.

There are two main aspects to the ATAWAS concept: The pragmatic business aspect, and the social and sense-making aspect:

ATAWAS is a web platform where individuals and professionals can buy, sell and trade skills/talents and services locally – and for free! ATAWAS will enable anyone to offer expertise and talent. ATAWAS will empower anyone to find, purchase and trade expertise and talent. This is the pragmatic business aspect of ATAWAS.

But there is more to this concept. ATAWAS will encourage, motivate and teach people about following their dream, doing what they do best - and even starting their own business. ATAWAS will therefore provide and develop a learning-and-teaching section of the platform – we call it ATAWISDOM – and this is also for free! Here ATAWAS will seek to inspire and share experiences and insights with the community online – and soon the community will be able to share their experiences and dreams also online. This is the social and sense-making aspect of ATAWAS.

I decided to try out the dream - why not? I want to see where it takes me - and I want to learn more, test my own abilities - and inspire others to do the same. That is why I believe in ATAWAS.
