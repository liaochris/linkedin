Shaun Milne’s Summary

Journalist and media consultant based in the UK with more than 15 years trade and industry experience.

Worked in top flight journalism for the national press including foreign assignments, running news-desks and other senior management roles, the majority at Trinity Mirror.

Writing experience includes reporting from war in Kosovo for which I was shortlisted for an award, and the civil unrest in Sierra Leone.

Capable of adopting hard news, features and corporate writing styles.

Most recently founding director of award winning design, media and publishing company Planet Ink Limited, specialising in digital page turn publications.

Elected as Fellow to the Royal Society for the encouragement of Arts, Manufactures and Commerce (RSA) in 2008.

Active blogger for a number of sites, seasoned ghost-writer for newspaper columns and guest speaker for accredited journalism university courses.

Shaun Milne’s Specialties:

Digital newspaper and magazine production, reporting skils, management experience, media law, newspaper production, contract negotiations, copywriting, newsgathering.
