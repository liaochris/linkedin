Victor Vega’s Summary

If you are looking for a theoretical thinker that can deliver results, look for a theorist that can cross domestic and foreign environments while promoting acquisition policies to government, start up and fortune 500 corporations.

Victor Vega has the technology and “can do” attitude with more than 12 years of experience delivering acquisition strategies to start up and transnational corporations. Victor started selling Kirby® vacuum cleaners at the age of 15 and has worked his way from the ground up.

Victor Vega first started in the financial industry by selling jewelry boxes on 47th street to the mass jewelry enterprise. “The financial markets are always of interest to me, I like the color of gold and I was astounded that a metal had so much value.”

Victor Vega then joined the precious metal commodities industry where global trade was an every day issue. “Traveling to Latin America and dealing with governments along with 20 plus industry verticals was a bit difficult.”

Moving forward Victor Vega decided to start becoming more fluent in the public eye by joining IndexTools as a sales and new business consultant to the North and South American markets. Achieving more than average amount of business, Victor Vega decided to also fund a self reliable .com while maintaining a serious posture in the commodities, internet and ecommerce markets.

Victor Vega now is part of the web analytics association, admiral’s circle in the Sandler Sales Institute and is developing an international business theory. Victor now networks with industry leaders to make intuitive strategy decisions which will pave success in any vertical once applied.

“In a competitive environment where reality is immanent, philosophy and strategy guides ones will to optimize while capitalizing on success.”
