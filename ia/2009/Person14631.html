Andrew Ortyn’s Summary

High impact business leader with consistent track record of seeking, setting, and driving top-line and bottom-line improvements.

Expertise: Strategic Planning, Team Building / Culture Building, P&L (Profit and Cash-flow Improvement), Business Expansion, Market Positioning, Partnership / Alliances, Sales Force Development, Turnarounds, Brand Management, New Product Development, Market Commercialization, Global Operations.

Industry Experience: Medical Products / Devices, Paper Products, Heavy Equipment, Chemicals, Pharmaceuticals, Financial Services, Automotive, Food, Durable Consumer Products, and Automation.

Creator of the Tools for Profit™ and Creating Alignment™, proprietary methodologies that galvanizes cross-functional leaders to a common business vision linked to measurable improvements in business performance.

Previous line management experience include the following:

Directed with full P&L, a $265MM core business category (B2B) for a Fortune 500 company. Achieved +69.7% year-to-year profit growth, +13.7% revenue growth; +6.7% volume growth - while concurrently reducing number of product SKUs by 85%.

Repositioned commodity chemicals business to branded focus; achieved +85% year-to-year profit growth in core markets; +32% profit growth for the company - overall.

P&L responsibility for diverse portfolio of food products (B2C) for Fortune 500 company; exceeded all profitability targets.

Successfully launched multiple new products / product lines in both consumer and business-to-business market segments for both Fortune 500 and middle market businesses.

Author / Speaker / Coach on profitability, pricing, performance, market positioning, and sales effectiveness.

Andrew Ortyn’s Specialties:
