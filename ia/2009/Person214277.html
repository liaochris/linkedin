Tony Drootin’s Summary

My partner and I left Sony/BMG to start a new entertainment company with the help of overseas investors. This company, SevenSeas Entertainment Inc., has three divisions. SevenSeas Media Management handles a number of Grammy winning recording and mixing engineers, as well as songwriter/producers and artists. SevenSeas Music, our record company, just signed the very talented and busy band The Jazzkamikazes and is negotiating with other artists and ventures. SevenSeas Studios has 4 rooms on 51st Street in Manhattan. Three of these rooms are for digitizing analog assets. We currently have accounts with The Metropolitan Opera, and the US Air Force. The 4th room is a recording and mixing room. SSS is also building a 17,500 sq ft. recording facility in Chelsea due to be open this coming spring.

These three business groups compliment each other and allow us to provide a diverse level of services to our clients. That and the years of experience we provide have proved to be a successful combination.
