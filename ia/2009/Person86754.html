Gina Chen’s Summary

I have 19 years experience reporting and editing for newspapers. I've covered everything from city government to federal courts to state government. I've overseen coverage of religion, higher education and police and courts coverage. I spent five years running a satellite bureau for a daily newspaper and producing a weekly tabloid on local news. I've also produced annual special sections on how to choose a college. In my current position, I write about parenting, children and balancing work and family and I blog about my life as a mom and wife.
