Josh Thompson’s Summary

I'm a Nashville native that special­izes in large-venue installed sound and digital media production and delivery systems. I am fortunate to have gathered a broad range of experience, with notable projects in Las Vegas, Northern California, Nashville, DC, Miami and points between.

I currently serve as a Vice President with Polysonics, a DC area acoustics and technology firm, hold a seat on the Advisory Board for Dispersive Networks and write the occasional feature article for Live Sound International magazine. I am a Kentucky Colonel, hold a Commercial Pilot certificte with multi-engine, instrument airplane privileges, and am currently a member of AES, NSCA, InfoCOMM, International Code Council, NFPA, and hold sustaining membership in SAFMA and IMAT.

Josh Thompson’s Specialties:

Digital audio and streaming media distribution systems, unified communications systems, concert audio systems, sensitive compartmented information facilities design, planning and program management, system commissioning, feature writing.
