Jeffrey S. Frichner’s Summary

Good News Capital Services

Finding “hidden treasure” in overlooked emerging markets and public companies.

Goodnewscapital.com features market intelligence designed to inform, educate and mine for undiscovered investment opportunities.

Good News Capital, LLC is dedicated to the individual investor and their personal investment success. It features on-going news briefs, market analysis and educational commentary as well as discovering special situations and profiles on emerging public companies. We are an investor intelligence and awareness firm that features emerging markets and companies that warrant further due diligence by investors looking for potentially explosive investment opportunities.

The news on thousands of public companies is followed by millions of investors globally each day. Unfortunately, only the largest companies are covered by the investment press and news wires. This situation leaves a large number of publicly-traded companies without a voice.

Goodnewscapital.com enables public companies to reach thousands of qualified investment bankers, hedge fund managers, stockbrokers and investors through a comprehensive marketing strategy.

Jeffrey S. Frichner’s Specialties:

* Architect successful capital raising campaign.

* Function as the listed investor relations contact on press releases for public companies.

*Email newsletter to proprietary investor database.

* Post cards campaign to current shareholders and/or opt-list of prospective investors with an Investor Hotline to receive current public information about the company.
