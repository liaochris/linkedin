Raimo Van der Klein’s Summary

After working in Sales & Marketing for more than 6 years at Nokia (networks and mobile phones) and working in 2006/2007 as Principal Innovation Manager at the leading Dutch (mobile) operator KPN, Raimo (1973) started in June 2007 as freelancer in the Internet & Mobile domain. As a freelancer Raimo worked the last year for e.g. NOS, Vodafone, Logica CMG, Univé and Senternovem.

Next to this Raimo is founder of Mobile Monday Amsterdam (www.mobilemonday.nl), speaks at events about The Mobile Life and writes for the leading marketingblog in The Netherlands called Marketingfacts (www.marketingfacts.nl).

From May 2008 Raimo is co-founder and partner at SPRXmobile. SPRXmobile is mobile agency focusing on strategy & concept development and experiences(workshops, events, trips)

Current interests involve: Value Creation, Innovation, Concept Development, Mobile Marketing, Augmented Reality, Internet of Things, Social Networking, Social Sharing, Social Search, RFID, 2D Barcodes, Co-Creation, Crowdsourcing, Context aware applications/services, Gadgets, Sustainability, Strategic CSR.
