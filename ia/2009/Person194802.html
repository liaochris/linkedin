Ron Blum’s Summary

Responsible for the planning, implementation, and execution of targeted multi-channel campaigns using database marketing techniques. Also responsible for list management and address hygiene, as well as CAN-SPAM and GLBA compliance. Manage team of developer analysts and quality assurance engineers.
