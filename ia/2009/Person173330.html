Jérôme Neuvéglise’s Summary

Beginning in 2008, pocketmovies.net is my main and only project I am working on. Stay tuned for more information very soon !

My latest experience was COO and Delegate CEO of Mobivillage, a Buongiorno subsidiary, specialised in mobile content delivery and services. Mobivillage operates in B2B, providing mobile careers innovative and robust enablers for mobile content & service delivery.

Before, I have been working in SFR for 7 years on mobile services.
I first started as system and support engineer on the value added services team in the O&M team on various systems : SMSC, email2SMS gateway, HDML/WAP gateway
Then I worked for the DSL project as Radius architect for 6 months.
Then I became project manager for Mobile Video Streaming for 2 years.
Then I was responsible for the Value Added Services administration for one year.
