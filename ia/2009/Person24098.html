Barbara Saunders’s Summary

I am passionate about putting people and organizations in touch with liberating stories and ideas that help them achieve their potential. As a writer and researcher, I specialize in technical/scientific communications and cultural observation. As an editor, I help individual authors and organizations clarify their messages, organize their work process, and complete high-quality, courageous, and polished work. As a teacher and facilitator, I guide self-directed learners and learning organizations in achieving goals they choose for themselves.

I have worked with Fortune 500 companies, start-ups, nonprofits, and individuals from 5 to 90 years of age, in forums ranging from campfire gatherings to online social networks.
