Gérard Dupin’s Summary

I have 30 years of experience in the telecommunication field :
With France Telecom (FT) :
• in operational departments in the area of switching and subsequently access and transmission networks,
• within FT R&D where I was responsible for the design of the FT synchronisation network, and for the acceptance and network integration of signal processing transmission equipment, and related quality issues within the domestic and international networks,
• in the Submarine Cable Department, responsible for the Department of Engineering : have been involved for almost ten years in the engineering, design and implementation of the various FT submarine projects (Channel, Mediterranean Sea, Atlantic & Pacific Oceans). I also have an extensive experience in the area of route design and cable protection, and has been Chairman of the International Cable Protection Committee (ICPC).

I subsequently joined Global Crossing as General Manager for France where for over three years I was in charge of setting up the local organisation and roll out of the network, including the implementation of the submarine sections of the PEC network.

Since completion of this network, I provide consultancy services in the area of submarine systems, and strategy and business development for various telecom companies.

In addition, I am actively involved in the commercialisation of technology for several companies.
