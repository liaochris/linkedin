Fred Khachi’s Summary

Fred Khachi is founder, President and CEO of JK Consultants. He has 25 years of experience in working with clients ranging from startups to Fortune 100 companies in the areas of recruitment, management, organizational change and performance management.

Mr. Khachi created JK Consultants to bring outstanding leadership talent to organizations and help them strengthen their management teams to realize increased long-term profitability and growth. The firm has an elite network of senior executives that provide a pool of highly qualified candidates to fill top-level positions. The ongoing success of JK Consultants is demonstrated by the repeat business as well as candidate longevity and the impact that the executives have made on the firms in which they have been placed.

Before founding JK Consultants, Mr. Khachi was the Vice President of Sales for a $500 million international manufacturing company. At that company he developed a world-class division with strong recruiting, training and compensation programs and created an environment that ensured the retention of high-performers. He ensured long term success because he developed a practice of thoroughly analyzing the needs of the company and then effectively meeting those needs.

Mr. Khachi always applies his strategic and financial planning skills as well as his business acumen to his client relationships. Fred begins each executive search by learning all he can about the members of the existing executive team as well as the corporate culture and objectives so that he recruits an executive for long-term success.

Mr. Khachi is a member of numerous professional organizations including the National Association of Personnel Services and Vistage, an exclusive international organization of CEOs and presidents. His professional network gives him rapid access to high-caliber executives globally.
