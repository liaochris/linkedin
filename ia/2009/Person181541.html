Pat McGraw’s Summary

Helping innovative firms differentiate themselves from the competition in the hearts and minds of the target audience for more than 25 years so that revenues, profits and market share are increased.

My work has helped start-ups, small and medium-sized enterprises as well as leading Fortune 100 corporations across multiple industries. Employers and clients include IBM, Microsoft, Sears, Kmart and ExxonMobil - industries include retail, high tech, telecommunications, tourism, hospitality, education, manufacturing, business services and financial services.

I am currently responsible for marketing at Walden University (www.waldenu.edu), a subsidiary of Laureate Education (www.laureate-inc.com). In addition to my work with Walden, I am also an adjunct faculty member at Towson University (www.towson.edu), Axia College of University of Phoenix (www.axia.phoenix.edu) and University of Maryland University College (www.umuc.edu).

Prior to joining Laureate and Walden, my experience has included senior-level positions with University of Maryland University College (www.umuc.edu), Blau Direct Marketing (currently Euro RSCG 4D, a division of Havas) and Frequency Marketing, a division of Alliance Data. I have also launched and operated a highly successful consulting and services firm, McGraw Marketing (www.mcgrawmarketing.com) and Cirino McGraw Inc., a Phoenix-based direct marketing firm.

I earned my B.S. in business administration from Arizona State University and my M.S. in marketing from The Johns Hopkins University. I am an avid writer and frequent speaker at conferences addressing topics such as "How to Develop a Successful Marketing Plan", "Loyalty Marketing", "Online Customer Acquisition" and more.
