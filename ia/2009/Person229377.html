Tariq Al-Hasan’s Summary

Thirteen years of full-time work experience mostly in Finance, Audit & Compliance with professionally managed Multi National and Local Banks (AlRajhi Bank, Banque Saudi Fransi (a susbs. of Calyon), SAMBA Financial Group (ex subs. citibank)).
