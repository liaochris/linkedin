Pacific NorthWest Computers’s Summary

We at PNWComputers strive to assist you so that you can more clearly understand your computer problems, computer issues and computer errors. Our goal is to help you to understand your computer so that any future computer problems can be minimized or avoided!

It is important for consumers to stay informed of all current internet security risks. Therefore, we want to work with you personally to provide you with pertinent security information and to provide you with those important answers to your questions about developing safer internet surfing habits!

Many computer stores have a strict "my way or the highway" service structure that is often non-negotiable. We at PNWComputers believe that the programs we use are the most effective. However, if you have a program(s) that you feel more comfortable using or if you are required to use Norton Corporate by your place of employment then we will strive to accomodate your preferences and your computer needs!
