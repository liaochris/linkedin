Christian Dalberg’s Summary

Throughout my career I have found the greatest satisfaction in aiding the companies that I have worked for in building and sustaining outstanding performance. I believe that in order to create and maintain this kind high performance, one needs to fully understand each client, the clients competitors, suppliers and customers. But in order to sustain an outstanding performance, I have always believed that communication, innovation and motivation are the three critical factors.   

Profile: More than 10 years experience in the field of marketing, sales, project management, new business development, advertising and PR. With a background in the advertising industry I have planned, managed and implemented successful advertising campaigns - both on a national and an international level, mainly in the European market. Furthermore I have experience with corporate sales in a multinational corporation in Taiwan developing local (Taiwan) and regional (Asia Pacific) contracts with Citibank, DaimlerChrysler and ASML.

Education: MBA and a Bachelor degree in Marketing Management - both obtained at leading UK universities as well as a degree in Communications.
