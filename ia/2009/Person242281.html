Veronique Martin-Place’s Summary

I am a freelance writer, translator, editor and proofreader. I mostly write, edit and proofread in French. I really enjoy to influence positively people through my writing. Moreover I have a strong experience in research and fact checking.

I am interested in any opportunities within this field.

Please, do not hesitate to contact me if you need to write a document in French, translate one from English to French or edit and proofread your work in French.

My goals are to write and to translate as much as possible in order to grow as a writer. I wish to get my children's book published within two years.

I have an extensive experience of living and working abroad (Norway, Sri Lanka, France and the US).

I have a good team spirit. I am flexible and able to multi-task.
