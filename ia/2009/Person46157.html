Cindy Andreatos’s Groups:

The Jewish Connection (TJC) network is an online community where spirituality and Judaism is shared. The Internet is vital part of the global success of TJC, bringing Jews together from around the world. Palm Beach County resident, Cindy Grundman Andreatos, founded the network in March 2004.

With the expansion of the Internet population and the ability for global reach, Andreatos wanted to make a connection. The Connection allows for the opportunity for Jews everywhere to bond and share beliefs with those of diverse cultural background. Since March, the response has been embraced by many and reaches to far away lands such as Thailand.

Through the power of the Internet, TJC transcends borders, distances and time zones. It brings Jews together from all continents to exchange traditional beliefs, life, recipes, Torah studies etc. It's just about life, the Jewish life. Membership to the TJC is free.
