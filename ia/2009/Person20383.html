Arthur Kosten’s Summary

Chief Marketing Officer at Booking.com

Booking.com is Europe’s leading online hotel reservations company, with over 19 million room nights sold in 2007. Booking.com achieved $1 billion in gross travel bookings for the 2008 Q1, an increase of 100% over the prior year.

Booking.com is part of priceline.com (Nasdaq: PCLN). It offers over 45,000 hotels in more than 10,000 destinations. Booking.com attracts over 20 million unique visitors each month from both leisure and business markets worldwide.

The company has offices in Amsterdam, Athens, Barcelona, Berlin, Cambridge, Cape Town, Dubai, Dublin, London, Loulé, Lyon, Munich, New York, Paris, Rome, San Francisco, Singapore, Stockholm, Vienna and Warsaw.

For further information, see: www.booking.com.
