Rodney Wallace’s Summary

Working capital, cash generation & crisis stabilisation specialist with over 15 years International experience, including five as "Head of Delivery" for PricewaterhouseCoopers Business Recovery Services ("BRS"), London.

Extensive and Global "C" level experience in the provision of strategic and "hands-on" working capital, operational restructuring, performance improvement, crisis management and business recovery services.

Delivered some of the largest turnaround & cash generation programmes in Europe, delivering £multi-million savings for corporate organisations either experiencing crisis or striving to improve operational performance.

Main industry focus: Manufacturing, FMCG / Retail, Distribution & Government.

Clients include: US Steel, EMTEC Media, Gillette, Genie Industries, Millennium Dome.

Rodney Wallace’s Specialties:

Working Capital management, Business Recovery / Turnaround, Crisis Management, Cost reduction, Cash Generation, Operational Restructuring, Change management.
