Julie Lam’s Summary

The beauty of creativity lies in words, hidden in brush strokes, buried in pixels. Work is art. And art is work. It takes hard work and teamwork to create something grand. I believe a brilliant idea can come from anyone. It doesn’t matter. The most important thing is to have everyone believing in it and working together to make the best of it. People who know me know I am a true passionate. I never give up on a good idea. That’s why I never stop having fun doing what I do everyday.

Currently, I am working on an Advertising-based reality show, writing two novels, painting, teaching art and taking care of my son and my plants.

And I am still collecting yellow VW toy beetles.

Julie Lam’s Specialties:

It all boils down to conceptual thinking and that's what I am trained for. Creative solution for the right time and place.
