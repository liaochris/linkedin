Brian Smith’s Summary

WHAT I DO FOR REAL ESTATE AGENTS

I help real estate agents perform a marketing makeover that converts more prospects into clients. With today’s innovative marketing methods, agents can produce more clients, spend fewer dollars and enjoy greater consistency of referrals.

Albeit I’m a mortgage guy, this is what separates me from my competition. Certainly, I offer similar loan programs and rates as every other loan officer in Greater Atlanta. But that won’t mean much to you unless there’s a long line of new and repeat customers knocking at your door.

If you want to get your own marketing makeover (a makeover that doesn’t require a budget the size of Coca Cola’s, but yet helps you produce more business in a predictable manner) and you’ve been a full-time agent for 3 years or longer - I want to help you. To learn more, take the next step and go to http://www.MyLoyalClients.com


WHAT I DO FOR HOME BUYERS

I help individuals and families qualify for, and own the home of their dreams with little money down, and even with less than perfect credit. Many of my clients are first time home buyers. Some are upgrading to their next home.

As a mortgage loan officer, what separates me from my competition is:

1. Upfront, total understanding so that I can assist you best
2. Approval within hours
3. Close in days not weeks
4. View your settlement statement BEFORE you get to the closing table
5. Streamlined, easy process
6. Personal service

If you have questions, want to see how much home you qualify for, or would like to receive more information, just contact me or visit www.bsmith.loan-blogger.com
