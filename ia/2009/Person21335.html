Ashley Root’s Summary

I have been a caregiver since I was 14 years old. I have taken care of children and adults with disabilities, and seniors. Now, I try to build relationships with the community in an effort to meet seniors in need of day-to-day care and be able to meet those needs with all the resources available to them. I work with seniors everyday. These are the people who helped build our country, establishing its heart and soul. They are the ones with something to learn from, they remember the history of this country and who God is. They are worth it, and they should not be forgotten. I hope to grow our company into a leading, trusted home care agency in Jacksonville Florida.
