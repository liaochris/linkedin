Nathan Ruegger’s Summary

Nathan Ruegger is an award-winning director and writer of thrillers featuring strong female leads.

His passion for filmmaking began at a young age as a voice actor on the TV series, "Steven Spielberg presents Animaniacs." He went to Dartmouth College, where he graduated with High Honors in Film Studies and received the Dartmouth Film Award. Now in his last year at the USC School of Cinematic Arts, he has made over twenty films, whose credits include producing the award-winning documentary "Carry Me Home" and the Coca-Cola finalist film, "Moonlight Theater."

Nathan recently finished writing his action feature screenplay, "Furious Angels." He is currently rewriting his horror feature screenplay, "Red Snow," and is in post-production on his short drama, "Another Life."

As of Spring 2010, Nathan will be submitting "Another Life" to film festivals around the world and seeking employment in feature film development.
