Mari Anne Vanella-Wright’s Summary

* Founder | CEO of highly successful Silicon Valley Sales Development Firms delivering High Touch / High Quality lead generation for Technology, Financial Services, and Fortune 500-1000 Accounts for over 15 years.

* Designer of Telesales 2.0(TM) formula for identifying and engaging with active sales cycles for enterprise technology solutions and services

* Leading Expert in Lead Generation for High Tech Companies Nationwide with a client list that includes SAP, HP, Sun, Hitachi, Borland, and many others.

* Successful track record in Executive Management positions with mature mainstream companies 500M+, early and late stage start-ups, and services firms.
