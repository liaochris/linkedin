Peter Thomas’s Summary

Peter has extensive management experience in the insurance and software development industries. He focuses on forming a deep appreciation of business / customer needs; developing pragmatic strategies to address these; having a passion for high-quality execution; and understanding of how to enact cultural and organisational change. His recent areas of activity include business intelligence and financial systems.

In 2007, Peter’s skills in cross-functional and cross-regional implementations led to him being appointed VP of Management Information for the International Division of The Chubb Group of Insurance Companies. Chubb provide property and casualty insurance for personal and commercial customers worldwide. The International Division has premium income of $2.8 bn (23% of the total) and consists of Chubb’s subsidiaries in Asia Pacific, Canada, Europe and Latin America.

For 7 years before this, Peter was head of Enterprise IT for Chubb Europe. He led the Management Information, Financial Systems, Marketing Systems, HR Systems and Application Integration groups and was accountable for European IT Governance and Budgetary issues. He was also a member of Chubb’s Senior Leadership Development programme.

Peter originated the transformational concept of a business intelligence system for Chubb Europe. He was tasked with developing a strategy to create greater transparency for the region’s performance metrics and then with executing this programme of work. The resulting EMIR system is quoted by Chubb managers as a key factor in turning around their results and achieving record profits in 5 of the following 6 years.

EMIR has won two industry awards and been the subject of many case studies in the European and US press. Peter has spoken about EMIR's at seminars across Europe and North America.

At Chubb, Peter nurtured a strong team of managers who have since moved on to more senior roles within the company, a tribute to his people development skills.
