Krista Moon’s Summary

My goal is to help companies use the most current online technologies and strategies to help them get found by people already learning about and shopping in their industry and increase brand loyalty by developing long-standing relationships with their ideal customer types.

1) Draw more people in: Increase traffic to your website

2) Increase leads: Increase the number of ideal customer types interested in your company at any one time.

3) Increase sales: Higher conversion to purchase rates and more repeat sales by giving customers a more positive experience at your website and by using online communities to increase brand affinity and loyalty.

4) Reduce operating costs: reduce the cost-per-lead and increase efficiency of key business processes.

5) Ensure future growth: Consistent evaluation and measurement of the strategies implemented to ensure they are providing the desired results and to plan for future growth.

Krista Moon’s Specialties:

Web design and training so you can easily update your website yourself, develop and implement inbound marketing strategies to drive business growth at lower costs, and training seminars to help customers stay educated.
