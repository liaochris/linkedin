Kevin Buckner’s Summary

* Game Industry experience dating back to the early 1980's
* I have worked at a select group of companies including: Microprose, US & UK, Electronic Arts and Hasbro Interactive.
* I have held a wide range of positions including: Programmer, AI Programmer, Producer, Lead Game Designer, Executive Producer, Development Director, Studio owner, Design Consultant & Head of Studio.
* I have designed, produced or coded some great games including: The original FIFA Soccer, Gunship 2000, The original Rugby World Cup, Risk I & II, Monopoly, Frogger, Monopoly Tycoon, Ghost Master, Syndicate, Theme Park, Trivial Pursuit.
* I have worked for developers & publishers, in the UK, the US, Eastern Europe, Australia and Singapore - I have set up and run a development studio, an international publisher and a design consultancy, I have programmed, designed, managed and raised finance.
* I have designed games, created games and launched games, often against difficult schedules.
* I have seen and experienced most aspects of video game development, and yet I still love making games. Clinical insanity remains a possibility.
