Dan Meyer’s Summary

DAN MEYER is one of the last of the few sword swallowers left in the world today.

Described as a "Christian David Copperfield" who combines real feats with a powerful message instead of tricks and illusions, Dan eats fire and swallows swords, blades, sabers, and other objects from 15 to 30 inches long and up to 8 swords at once!

As President of the Sword Swallowers Assoc Int’l and winner of the 2007 Ig Nobel Prize in Medicine at Harvard for his medical research study published in the British Medical Journal, Dan is recognized as the foremost authority in the world on the ancient art of sword swallowing.

One of only a few dozen sword swallowers left in the entire world, a multiple Guinness World Record holder and Ripley’s Believe It or Not performer, Dan is in demand as one of the top sword swallowers in the world today. Featured on America's Got Talent, Today Show, CNN, MTV, ESPN, Discovery Channel, Discovery Health, Learning Channel, Travel Channel, USA Network, Food Network, CNN Health, ABC, CBS, FOX, NBC, MSN, MSNBC, PBS, CMT, ET, 48 Hours, 20/20, NPR, BBC, CBC, CTV, NTV Russia, WGN, Jimmy Kimmel, Mancow, Rick & Bubba, USA Today, Newsweek, National Geographic, Forbes, British Medical Journal, Scientific American, Science Daily, Nature, NY Post, NY Times, London Times, Christian Activities, and hundreds of other interviews around the world. Dan has performed on national tour with Brooks & Dunn's Neon Circus, on international tour with the Celtic Christian band Ceili Rain, at Ripley’s Believe It or Not attractions, colleges, universities, corporate and church events, and Upward awards programs around the world.
