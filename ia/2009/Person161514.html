Michael Kimball’s Summary

Experienced attorney specializing in licensing transactions, corporate formation and financing and a variety of commercial and corporate matters, with an emphasis on emerging growth companies and internet related transactions.

I recently spent 6 years as a senior member of the legal team at Yahoo! Inc, where I specialized in the area of intellectual property licensing transactions. I supported several business units, including Yahoo! Small Business, SDS, Engineering (Geo-location team), IT and Y!PNO. In the past I have also supported Yahoo! Enterprise Services, Yahoo! Real Estate, Advertising Sales Operations, and YSM Yield Management. I have played a key role in negotiating many of Yahoo!'s technology deals and strategic alliances for a variety of business units in multiple arenas, including transactions with SAP, Microsoft, eBay, Apple, Amazon, PayPal, Google, Oracle, AOL, IBM, Reuters, Aetna, SUN, Lucent, among others.

Before joining Yahoo!, I served as senior licensing attorney, supporting worldwide sales and business development, at Commerce One, where I helped seal key strategic alliances and supported the company's sales and marketing strategy. I was instrumental in closing transactions with Deutsche Telecom, Asia Trade Alliance, SAP AG, SAP Markets, Compaq, Accenture, PWC, Sun Microsystems, Siemens SPLS and several key founding marketplaces of the Global Trading Web Association. I also helped grow revenue from approximately $16 million in 1999 to more than $400 million in 2000, after Commerce One's initial public offering in 1999.

My past legal positions include Pacific Bell, AT&T Capital, and private practice.

Prior to my legal career, I served as a naval officer on the nuclear attack submarine USS William H. Bates (SSN-680).

I hold a B.A. degree in Chemistry from Claremont McKenna College (1981), an M.A. degree in Government from Claremont Graduate School (1983) and a J.D. degree from the Southwestern University School of Law (1995).

Michael Kimball’s Specialties:

Intellectual Property Licensing, internet related matters, corporate formations, early stage financing, emerging growth companies, copyrights, patents, trade secrets, negotiation, strategic alliances, broadband and mobile technologies, internet advertising, content, hardware and software licensing.

My regulatory expertise includes: HIPAA, Gramm-Leach Bliley, Privacy, CAN-SPAM, FTC and FCC matters.
