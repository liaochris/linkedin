Tina Smith’s Summary

Strong design professional with a unique mix of creative vision and business expertise. 25 years of art direction and design experience, focusing on business challenges and expertly creating effective design solutions.

Have a range of clients and am also a strategic creative partner for agencies. Some of my clients include an animal health products distributor, an area Chamber of Commerce, a credit union co-op, a vision care center and a specialist in web development and search engine marketing.
