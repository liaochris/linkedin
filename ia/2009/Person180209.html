Doug Johnston’s Summary

I have spent my working life in the packaging and printing industries, predominantly in the corrrugate, label and carton sectors in commercial, sales and general management roles. Through gaining a post-graduate qualification in Packaging Technology, I have sought to match this commercial background with a strong technical knowledge.

After running a succcesful corrugated sheet plant for a number of years, the opportunity to become part of an independent consultancy presented itself. This brings with it the ability to offer clients a wide range of services across the entire packaging supply chain.

Particular areas of expertise include cost-down, tender management, packaging development, product and innovation sourcing, supplier auditing and sales/technical training.

I also have developed a strong portfolio in Interim Management at senior level in companies undergoing change or requiring assistance in seeing through specific projects to completion.

Doug Johnston’s Specialties:

Site management, sales and commercial management, interim management, product development, tender management, industry specific knowledge of print, corrugated packaging, carton packaging, labels.
