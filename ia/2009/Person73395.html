Rick Carter’s Summary

I have a lengthy background in technology and marketing with a passion for sports and fitness. My business experience is also very diverse, having worked for a variety major national and international companies. I have lived a long and full life with many years of Vitality ahead of me.

Ten years ago I turned my attention to sports marketing, working with some of the biggest names in sport (inc World Champion Dmitry Markov, AFL Legend Wayne Carey, Olympic Medalist Tatiana Grigorieva) and entertainment/media (inc Derryn Hinch).

More recently I have focused on Media/PR/Marketing Communications, building brands for companies in the Wellness/Wellbeing/Vitality/Sustainability Industries.

I founded eNova Communications to provide specialist PR/Media services to the industry and JustForMyWellbeing, to specialise in sales and direct marketing for our clients products .

My aim is to assist emerging brands to understand and utillise the power of Social Media and Social Networks combined with traditional Media/Marketing and PR to build, with the support of strong communities, "tribes".
