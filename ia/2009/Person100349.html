Ivan Gallo’s Summary

After having obtained my engineering degree, with a specialization in the area of computer technology, I began working in a number of top-notch companies such as Ericsson and then Nokia, where I am currently employed.
My current position involves leading the business requirement definition process with the customer and translate these requests into technical requirements as input for the development process. Then I follow up the development process in tight connection with the project managers and programme manager, assuring both requirement fulfilment and quality and eventually following the acceptance and change delivery phases. Regular reporting of work progress and getting customer feedback is also part of my routine activities.
I applied my knowledge and skills in several fields, including real time software for telecom market, GSM and multimedia traffic handling, provisioning, billing, system integration projects and internet related projects
