Monica Dodi’s Summary

As founder and chief executive of several successful new media ventures, including MTV Europe, Disney Europe, AOL’s Entertainment Asylum and Softbank VC EIR, Monica has earned the reputation for building highly effective and profitable marketing strategies for global media brands. Known for surpassing corporate objectives through strong executive leadership, organizational skills, and negotiating acumen, she is a luminary in the field of new media.
