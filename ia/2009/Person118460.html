Jo Hughes’s Summary

Whether you are a busy professional, run a small business, or represent a large corporation, my aim is to provide you with flexible and cost-efficient virtual support whenever you need it.

If you need someone to...
...type your dictations or dissertations;
...write original factual or fictional content in your name;
...provide translations of your publications;
...perform administrative tasks for you;
...produce any kind of document or publication for you;
...organize your event;
...coordinate your project;
...help manage your work/life balance;
or if you require any other kind of personal or business support, I can offer you this through a wide and flexible range of virtual services.

At all times I work in complete confidence and with discretion, never disclosing any information relating to my clients unless it is with their explicit permission or at their personal recommendation/choice/initiative.
