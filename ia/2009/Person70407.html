Efia Moore’s Summary

In her current position with Hilton Hotels Efia manages many different areas of public relations, including providing supplemental administrative and public relations support to the Hilton Hotels Western Regional Director of Communications. In her duties, she helps organize regional monthly reports, track and gather data, and executes corporate initiatives. Efia also manages media and community relations for the Arizona Hilton Resorts, which include Hilton Sedona Resort and Spa, Hilton El Conquistador Golf and Tennis Resort, and the two Pointe Hilton Resorts in Phoenix. She facilitates media requests, coordinates community activities such as the annual Water Safety Day event and television appearances, and plans and manages campaigns. Efia was also responsible for all the media and event support for the opening of Tocasierra Spa at the Pointe Hilton Squaw Peak.

In her past role as Account Coordinator at Mindspace, Efia Moore had the opportunity to help manage a variety of clients and subsequently gain experience in consumer products, hospitality, music, food and beverage and community relations. Her responsibilities at Mindspace included account management, event planning, and media relations. Efia also oversaw the creation of monthly recap reports, publicity clip books, return on investment analyses, and media list development.

As a budding freelance writer, Efia regularly contributes to “The Night Owl” column in the state’s only full-color weekly publication, AZ Weekly Magazine. Before joining Mindspace, Efia held communications positions with the Tempe School District and Gitenstein & Assadi Public Relations. She has a journalism degree with an emphasis on public relations from Arizona State University’s Walter Cronkite School of Journalism and Mass Communication.
