Bob Griswold’s Summary

Spent 7 years at the Boston Consulting Group - 4 in Chicago, 3 in Tokyo. Speak/read/write Japanese fluently. Managed M&A/partnerships at Sun & BEA, led the Crossgain acquisition & Intel partnership at BEA, and led the acquisition of Appeal Virtual Machines in Feb. 2002. General Manager of the JVM business, overseeing acquisition integration, engineering management, building the team, shipping product, business development & product management, etc. for 3 years. Senior Vice President at Terracotta in San Francisco. CEO at Provment, Inc. - leader in IT resource metering and chargeback systems. VP of Strategic Services at Cisco Systems.

Bob Griswold’s Specialties:

Java Virtual Machines, Java scalability, caching & clustering, grid & utility computing, OA&M, mergers/acquisitions/partnerships, general strategy
