Andrew Smith’s Summary

Please visit our website at:

http://www.TaxQueries.com

The idea behind TaxQueries.com is that you ask questions and receive answers from other tax professionals who might know. It's entirely community driven. There is no single "authority" who has all of the answers. Everyone pools their knowledge into answering questions. We feel that the design we've put together and the way we've organized the information (in many cases) can much more straightforward than browsing forums or wading through database sites to get an answer.

Reputation (ie. points) and badges are earned for interesting questions and correct answers.

That's it in a nutshell... please check out the site and let us know what you think.
