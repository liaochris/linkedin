Rebecca Dittmar’s Summary

I have worked in a few different industries in the past 8 years, but in each of them I gained valuable customer service experience. I learned that I work well under the pressure of a deadline and that I'm especially good at coordinating the work of others.
In my time at Forum Publishing Group, a subsidiary of the Sun-Sentinel and Tribune Company, I learned that my passion is for media and communications - primarily writing.
Though I left FPG for an opportunity to live abroad for 6 months, I enjoyed my time there and continue to write for their publications as a freelancer.
After spending several years in the school of life, I have decided to return to university and finish my much needed English degree. While I am studying, I am looking for a flexible full-time or part-time position or freelance work. I am available to write, read/edit copy, blog, write/compile/edit listings and calendar items, etc.

  • Editorial Assistant

    Forum Publishing Group

    (Newspapers industry)

    February 2005 — January 2009 (4 years)

    I worked at Forum Publishing Group for four years. My position was Editorial Assistant but during my time at FPG I had a variety of responsibilities. From writing stories to building pages, I have been involved in, or helped with, nearly every aspect of the paper.
    As Editorial Assistant I was responsible for the workflow of the newsroom, including press releases, freelance invoicing and other financial/administrative tasks. I coordinated work with editors, designers, reporters and the other editorial assistants. I would field dozens of calls, requests and complaints from readers and customers every day.
    I have written for nearly all of our publications at one point or another, especially our monthlies. But for the last three years I wrote a weekly column, Shelf Life, which I continue to write as a freelancer. It is about the local libraries, books, reading, writing and life in general. It runs in all of our weekly Broward County papers. It is probably the assignment I enjoyed most during my tenure at FPG – and, I hope, the one I excelled at most.
    I also wrote the Home Feature for one of our monthly papers, the Oakland Park Gazette and I contributed regularly to Shalom, our monthly Jewish publication that runs as an insert in the Sun-Sentinel.
    Through my work at FPG I have had some, though very minimal, exposure to HTML and to page design programs.
