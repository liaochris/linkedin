Darren Lancaster’s Summary

Local community networking guru striving to reduce the chaos associated with leading and participating in local-level, family-oriented group, such as schools PTA/PTO's, clubs, and non-profits. Seeking innovative ways for these groups to monetize the value of their community by fostering tighter relationships with local businesses.

Darren Lancaster’s Experience

  • Co-Founder & President

    The Groupery

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    February 2006 — Present (4 years )

    We help volunteers within local schools and groups to dramatically reduce the tedium and grunt-work associated with the organization and coordination of their communities. The resulting solution also provides families with an effective online family bulletin board that reduces the chaos associated with a day in the life of a typical family, such as: figuring out where the kids need to be today, how are they getting there, and what are they supposed to bring, wear, pay for, and drop off at the office? In parallel, we facilitate a local ecosystem that connects local families, schools, and groups with merchants for mutual benefit.
