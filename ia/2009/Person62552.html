Devin LaCrosse’s Summary

I began my professional career as an intern/ assistant producer for the #1 rated morning show in Detroit radio. I quickly progressed to on air talent positions with 98.3 WTWR, 104.3 WOMC and 95.5 WKQI. After the initial effects of becoming a minor local celebrity wore off, I decided to take a career path that offered more potential growth and opportunity.

For the next 8 years, I concentrated on sales/business development in several different verticals. I held positions as the top national sales associate for two different IT hardware/software providers and gained a wealth of experience in all aspects of account management and customer relationships.

Because I truly enjoy tackling complicated business challenges, I then became a sales process and growth consultant to small/mid-sized businesses. Over the course of my consulting career, I was fortunate enough to secure deals between my clients and such companies as UMI Racing/UMI Racing Technology, Ford Motor Company, Federal Mogul, Johnson Controls, Arvin Meritor, Honigman Miller Schwartz, The Farbman Group, Real Estate One, AON, Noble International, and many others. I specifically worked with top executives on branding, advertising and marketing campaigns, product launch and rollouts, strategy, team building and preparing for merger/acquisition.

I currently work as a business development associate for an exciting automotive startup called TrueCar. TrueCar's goal is to commoditize car buying and to help establish consumer confidence during the car buying process through full pricing transparency. I truly believe that we will not only change the way people buy their cars, but also dissolve much of the pain and fear involved in visiting a car dealership. It's my hope that the effort I put into making TrueCar successful will have an impact on the automotive industry as a whole and in some way begin to heal my stuggling hometown of Detroit, MI.

Devin LaCrosse’s Specialties:

Replationship building, purchasing and product sourcing, business development, strategy, sales and business process implementation, keeping business fun, making people laugh.
