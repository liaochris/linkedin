Jeff Kramer’s Summary

Accomplished human resources professional with extensive experience in providing strategic leadership to world-class financial services, technology and biotechnology organizations. Proven track record of developing innovative high impact human resources programs that are aligned with business strategy and values. Skilled at rapidly designing and implementing programs to address mission critical business issues.

Jeff Kramer’s Experience

  • Management Effectiveness Consultant

    Fidelity Investments

    (Privately Held; Financial Services industry)

    2007 — 2009 (2 years )

    Hired to provide expert consulting in leadership development, talent management, succession planning, executive coaching and change management to the senior executives of the Fidelity Technology Group Dallas Data Center and the Enterprise Solutions Dallas and Salt Lake City Software Development teams. Created and facilitated a Talent Review process that resulted in the development of succession and individual development plans for all positions at Vice President and above. Lead a cross-functional team in designing a curriculum to provide advanced training in Change Management consulting and coaching skills and tools for all Human Resources Directors and Vice Presidents. Member of Fidelity’s Southwest Region Technology Board consisting of the region’s most senior technology executives. Played a leadership role in the recent rightsizing of the Technology Group.

  • Vice President Human Resources

    GAB Robins

    (Privately Held; Insurance industry)

    2003 — 2007 (4 years )

    Recruited as part of new executive team charged with transforming this 122 year-old “bricks and mortar” financial services business into a nimble entrepreneurial enterprise after it was acquired by a private equity group. Lead the rightsizing of the entire North American operation resulting in annual savings of 15 million dollars. Engineered conversion of health insurance benefits program to a Consumer Directed Health Plan (CDHP) resulting in savings of over 1 million dollars in year one and subsequently holding health insurance costs flat for three consecutive years. Established orientation, performance management, rewards and succession planning programs that are aligned with new business strategy. Deployed state of the art distance learning technology to deliver technical and management training to employees based throughout the country.
