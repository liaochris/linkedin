Alejandro Pisanty’s Summary

Well-versed in matters related to IT, the Internet, and the Information Society, with cross-layer knowledge and experience. Alejandro Pisanty brings together technology, policy, strategy, and education at the local, national and international level. A good public speaker and a team player
