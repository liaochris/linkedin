Boaz Babai’s Summary

Boaz Babai is the Founder & CEO of ARPU~UP, a Web Marketing Strategy & Search Engine Optimization consulting firm. ARPU~UP is providing award-winning Internet Marketing and Digital Identity Creation Strategies to innovative business startup companies in Israel, Europe and US.

ARPU~UP is currently providing internet marketing consulting services to leading business companies such as:
> ControlGuard - Leading provider of Endpoint Security and USB Encryption Solutions.
> DeveloPrint - TransPromo campaign management consulting company.
> Aerotel Medical Systems - leading suppliers of advanced remote monitoring solutions for personal telehealth & telecare.
> innerActive Smart Media - Ad-funded mobile advertising solution provider.
> JagagMobile - Innovative provider of Mobile Greeting tool.

In the last two years ARPU~UP successfully completed online marketing projects for those innovative startups:
> Mobixell Networks - Leading provider of advanced mobile multimedia solutions.
> Sportingo - User-Generetad Content sports community website.
> MetaCafe - Web 2.0 video sharing startup company.
> SupportSpace - Innovative online tech support stratup.
> BeInSync - File sharing & online backup startup that recently acquired by Phoenix Technologies.
> EPOS - Leading provider of advanced digital positioning technology.
> Come2Play - a provider of White Label multiplayer casual flash games platform.

As a leading social marketing strategist in Israel, Babai is a senior lecturer about Web 2.0 marketing in the MBA marketing courses at the Interdisciplinary Center (IDC), Bar-Ilan University and the Collage of Management.

Babai holds MA degree in International Relationship from the Hebrew University, Jerusalem, and MBA in Finance from IDC, Herzeliya.

Boaz Babai’s Specialties:

Web Marketing Strategy | Web Analytics | Search Engine Marketing (SEM) | Natural Search Engine Optimization (SEO) | Content Management | Link Strategy | Social Media Optimization (SMO) | PPC Campaigns Management | Corporate Blog Marketing | Interactive Word of Mouth Marketing (WoMM) | Press Release (PR) Optimization and Internet Delivery | Social Networking Marketing (Twitter, Facebook & LinkedIn) | Smart Online Guerrilla Marketing services (Digg, Yahoo Answer, Propeller).
