Gahlord Dewald’s Summary

As Director of Web Strategy at Union Street Media, my objective is to develop and execute online marketing programs on behalf of our clients and for the company. This work involves client consultation engagements, development of business intelligence assets, coordination of the internet marketing team and specification of marketing features for Union Street Media's software offerings.

The mix of direct client consultation, team and tool building and software development allows me to examine data, human behavior and context in a constant and consistent environment for the industries with which we work. I use these experiences to develop strategic advantages for my clients in content, social media, SEO/SEM, paid advertising and other online initiatives.

Eight years of solo entrepreneurship prior to my current position provided me with an intimate understanding of the needs of businesses and organizations as well as rounding out a variety of software and content creation skills.

I'm a frequent speaker on web development and marketing issues for audiences consisting of business owners and/or small business development programs. Most recently including BloggerConnect and InmanConnect in San Francisco, the Women's Small Business Development Program in Burlington, Vermont and the Vermont 3.0 Tech Jam and Conference also in Burlington.

Outside of work, I am active in Burlington's cultural life as arranger and musical director for a puppet opera company, college-level animation teacher, and bassist in a wide variety of situations ranging from community orchestras to improvisatory rock ensembles.

I am most motivated by projects which allow me to make use of data and information to assemble meaning and useful action.
