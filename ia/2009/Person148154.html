    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    December 2007 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    Adversity is an organization primarily focused on identifying and addressing issues of diversity within the creative advertising industry. Adversity proposes a multi-level approach with a core objective, to establish outreach programs for students who are both unaware of the advertising/design/branding creative fields and culturally underrepresented in the advertising industry. Programs established by Adversity will educate students pursuing advertising, design and branding creative fields, by providing professional guidance to all students regardless of race, religion, sex, color or economic status. With academia as our focus Adversity hopes to provide a solution for the advertising industry to ethically adapt to the consumer markets they serve, by increasing the knowledge and qualification of minority “creative” students.
