Timothy Manning’s Summary

Timothy is a senior in the Fraud Investigation & Dispute Services group at Ernst & Young. He has acquired a range of experience in terms of clients and project types. Timothy has been involved in government pricing and price reporting, diagnostic audits for pharmaceutical manufacturers, fraud investigation, forensic accounting, dispute resolution, restatement work, and financial modeling. Timothy has experience regarding government pricing for pharmaceutical manufacturers and compliance with programs such as, the Medicaid Drug Rebate Program (MDRP), the Deficit Reduction Act of 2005 (DRA), and the Public Health Services 340B Program (PHS).
