Murad Ismailov’s Summary

Dr. Murad M. Ismailov, an American permanent resident, is a
graduate of the Moscow State Technical University [Baumann], BS
and MS degrees in Aerospace Engineering. He has a PhD degree – a
cooling system for liquid rocket engines.

He is an internationally recognized professional on injection and
combustion technologies as well as precision instrumentation and
control systems through his various project-leader jobs for the
automotive and aerospace industries carried out in Russia, Germany,
Japan and the USA.

In the USA Dr. Ismailov has lead new development projects
mostly related to diesel engine diagnostics and control, a common rail
accurate multiple injection system, a multi-strike injection flow meter
and a molecular tagging velocimetry system to quantify air intake, all carried out
for American and European automotive industries and research
institutions.

He is the author of more than 63 technical papers and participant in a great
number of international conference proceedings. He also holds 9 US patents as
a sole inventor.
