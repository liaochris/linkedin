FRED VAN BREE’s Summary

Founder of FourWings B.V., a company specialised in Coaching, HR Consulting and Interim Management
A senior executive with a 30-year successful career in Human Resources management positions in U.S. and European multinational corporations I bring an extensive experience in developing and implementing global and local human resources strategies, projects, tools and instruments. Projects include global incentive, compensation and benefits plans, merging and separation of organisations; post-merging integration of HR strategies, complex reorganisations, Union and Works Council negotiations, competencies, talent development and succession planning. I have effectively worked and lived in different cultures: three years in Saudi Arabia, one year in Argentina and the last eleven years in Germany.
As a coach I work with (Senior) Executives, with talents, entering the management profession and with seasoned managers in transition.
After having finished a radical restructuring at the Foxboro Company, I joined Corrugated Europe, a Joint Venture of the later KNPBT as HR Director. After Corrugated Europe became a division of the newly formed KNPBT, I left the company after a few years and started my own HR Interim Management company. Four interim assignments have been successfully accomplished during a two year period. Leica Microsystems however offered a new challenge as VP Global HR, providing leadership to twenty HR Directors/Managers. During more than ten years in Leica Microsystems, I developed processes and lead projects on talent development and post merger integration. At the end of 2007, I decided to go back to coaching and HR consulting and interim management, as independent consultant, specialising in: - Multilingual coaching (Dutch, German and English),
- International cross border HR solutions in particular between Germany and The Netherlands, - NGO HR Consulting and Recruitment
