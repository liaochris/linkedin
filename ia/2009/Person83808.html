Gary Meeks’s Summary

A great deal of experience in operations both in the trenches and management. My passion is with operations and processes with a great deal of focus on customer relations and creating innovative synergies. In my world, nothing is impossible or too complex. In fact, the more complex the project is the more likely I take it as a challenge either to find simpler solutions or adapt the processes to the complexities. One of my goals is to be in a position to direct individuals at the operational level and provide them with the means to adopt and adapt these teachings into their daily operations.
