Mike Biggs’s Summary

My objective is to bridge sustainability with business. My technical background, management experience, and business training put me in a unique position to demonstrate to organizations how they can be environmental stewards, increase social responsibility, and maximize their profits by doing so.

I have extensive experience in project management, department management and product development. I am currently working on my MBA in sustainability management and entrepreneurship. As Director of Sustainability Programs at iCAST (International Center for Appropriate and Sustainable Technologies), a non-profit organization focused on providing appropriate technologies and business solutions for community development, I managed a team of five project managers and provided technical management of the various programs in renewable and sustainability technologies.
