Adnan Pathan’s Summary

I am a part-time MBA student at Babson College and I am expected to graduate in summer of 2009. In 2004, I earned my Bachelors of Science in Finance from University of Massachusetts, Amherst. I joined Liberty Mutual Insurance Group in July of 2004 as a Commercial Casualty Underwriter. I earned my Chartered Property & Casualty Underwriter designation in March of 2007.

Commercial Casualty Underwriting Job Description:
• Underwrite three separate and distinct commercial casualty insurance products with a portfolio of $25 million.
• Quantitative analysis of historical losses and premium using actuarial loss forecasting and pricing models.
• Qualitative analysis based on prospect’s annual financial statements, 10Ks, loss prevention reports, company’s safety programs, and comparable company’s underwriting analysis.
• Present findings in written reports explaining the opportunity, analyzing risks, and recommendations to stakeholders
• Premium projections by applying multiple pricing methodologies; dynamically weighted to contemplate qualitative data results.
• Communicate underwriting fundamental and tactical ideas to senior management, brokers and clients.
• Lead underwriter for Liberty’s National Market’s Merger & Acquisition group. Meet with brokers face to face to enhance relationships, help broker understand new manuscript insurance products which reuses existing insurance products of M&A clients.
• Develop strategic plans to retain existing customers. Offer hybrid insurance program structures in combination with different collaterals (LOC, Surety Bonds instead of cash, escrow account) to avoid collateral cost to the customer, reduce overall premium and add value by offering loss prevention services.
• Developed a loss stratification and loss distribution excel program for loss forecasting and loss-ratio based premium projections.
