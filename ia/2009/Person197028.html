Rhonda Bulmer’s Summary

Building Information Modelling (BIM) is the future of the construction industry. (Please be aware that Building takes on the term of all construction from sub terrain through to above ground structures.) The premise offers vastly reduced errors on site caused by coordination issues in documentation, the ability to analyse the civil/construction/structures real world performance over its life cycle, visualisation, quick and simple cost estimating, and post occupancy maintenance, ultimately reducing construction & documentation costs as well as time frames.

By working with consultants ie Civil, Structural, Mechanical, Electrical and Hydraulic Engineers, a complete and resolved model is created where much of the co-ordination is automated and errors are identified very early so they can be addressed at a minimum cost. The same model that’s used for CONSTRUCTION can also be used for photorealistic and stylised marketing.
Typically it uses three-dimensional, real-time, dynamic building modelling software to increase productivity in building design and construction. The process produces the Building Information Model, which encompasses spatial relationships, geographic information, building geometry, and quantities and properties of construction components.
•BIM is moving away from the traditional lines and layers systems and replacing 2D symbols with building elements (objects), capable of representing and understanding the relationships of common civil and building components.
•This way the project is realised before it is constructed, issues are identified earlier and systems can be designed in the most efficient way possible and above all increase productivity and improve quality.

Let Cadway partner with your company and through a dynamic approach of training, development and implementation of project capabilities, we can make sure you get up to speed without all the guess work that Cadway has had to do over the past few years to be successful.
