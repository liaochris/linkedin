Thomas Denegre, MBA’s Summary

Seeking Marketing/Sales Leadership position in business technology solutions, advertising, publishing, and business services.

As an Award winning marketing and sales leader I use the eight step process of value added marketing and selling solutions to generate sustainable revenue growth.

It’s all about being authentic, transparent, and altruistic.

It’s about the right value proposition, core competencies, messaging, relationships, trust, processes, execution and setting goals that can be measured and managed.

Simply speaking it’s the process of building awareness, engagement, capturing, converting, and generating good word of mouth.

Exceptional planning, strategic market planning , market expansion, C-Level relationship selling, organizational leadership, revenue and profit maximization, team building and superior leadership skills to produce consistently strong revenue.

The ability to adapt and win in a fast changing environment developing long-term relationships built on superb service, and coaching high performance teams.

Managing Interactive Internet and Social Media Marketing. Well versed in SEO (paid and organic) strategies, email marketing, PPC, landing pages,website utilization, and internet inbound marketing strategies.

Value added marketing and sales is an eight step process of understanding the true value your product/service brings to the market. Customers buy benefits, not features. Customers want to know what makes you different and how your solutions are going to have a significant impact on their lives and business.

Value marketing will know the customers pains, their needs, their requirements, and their wants. Excellent value marketing will generate interest and compel the prospect to take action. The prospect will perceive the value difference thereby setting up a profitable sale for your organization.
