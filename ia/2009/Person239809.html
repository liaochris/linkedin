Una Medina, PhD.’s Summary

Experienced startup CEO and consistent high performer in startup campaigns for investment services, IT, manufacturing, solar electric, retail travel, publishing, and social marketing indusries. Marketing through implementation of behavioral change strategies. Ten years experience outperforming quarterly revenue goals. Consistently generated $200K to $300K sales annually selling concepts, services, and investment assets to CEOs of Fortune 500 companies with top Dunn and Bradstreet ratings. Conceptualized and designed innovative product and service campaigns tailored to client goals, needs, experiences. Complex systems modeler, manager, communication and database expert. Graduate Certificate in computer modeling. National and regional communication awards. Publications in management, public administration and innovation journals. Invited presenter at conferences. Ph.D. in persuasive communication. Adjunct Professor Persuasive Behavior Change, UNM. Behavioral Scientist UNM CASAA. Six years experience as a social scientist researcher for National Institutes of Health, University of Southern California Medical School, UNM School of Public Health, UNM Research Service Learning Program, and University of New Mexico Hospital Pediatric Heart Departmet, with a focus on persuasive strategies. University instructor of statistics, labs and workshops for graduate students. Statistician, federal grant evaluator for the National Institutes of Health.

Una Medina, PhD.’s Specialties:

Persuasive strategies to precipitate behavioral/ideological change in individuals, markets, and social systems. Computer modeling of complex social systems, agent-based models, discrete process models, and systems dynamics. Patenting algorithms.
