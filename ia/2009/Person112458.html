Joshua Miller’s Summary

The purpose of a coaching partnership is to work together to accomplish well thought-out objectives, increased self-confidence and awareness that that produce significant results in enhanced personal and professional satisfaction, and rewarding relationships.

Joshua Miller works with both individuals and organizations to define their key goals, working at an accelerated pace to accomplish them successfully. The process begins with a thorough evaluation of professional and personal challenges and their impact on ones life. Joshua’s ability to clarify and motivate, together with his life and business experience and his intuitive skills enable him to transform unfulfilled hopes and ambitions, even dreams, into solid realities. Once defined, obstacles hindering progress are identified and removed, breakthroughs are realized and new possibilities are embraced.

Joshua is completely results-oriented. In his notable career as senior creative marketing executive, he was responsible for developing the marketing and advertising for Fortune 100 companies and wide range of global brands. He organized and motivated teams and supervised strategic development.

As an executive and personal life coach, he has an enviable track record of helping others achieve their potential and find solutions to what had seemed to be insurmountable problems.

For a complimentary in confidence one to one coaching session, please contact me at (917) 548-6377 or Josh@JHMlifecoaching.com so we can schedule a time to speak.
