Russell Williams’s Summary

I have nearly 19 years experience in the Telecoms sector with BT and Cable&Wireless, working in all aspects of voice and transmission networks culminating in management of a global network operations centre.

For the last 6 years I've specialied in all aspects Business Continuity, Disaster Recovery and Crisis Management and have wide global experience having worked extensively in the UK, Europe, Asia and South America. I have been a member of the Business Continuity Institute for 3 years and am ITIL v3 Service Management/IT Service Continuity certified.

In Jan 2008 I joined Fujitsu Service, the third largest IT company in the world, working on major private and public sector managed service contracts.

I started a new role as Business Continuity Manager with HomeServe PLC in October 2008. This position emcompasses the whole BCM lifecycle from programme creation, goverance, implementation and testing.
