Demetrius (Demi) Minos, PMP’s Summary

I am a certified project management professional with a lot of experience in IT projects. Most recently I worked at the Walt Disney Company providing oversight of vendor-led IT projects. Prior to that I participated in, and led, the execution of complex ERP and business process re-engineering projects.

I have a passion for understanding the cause-and-effect relationships that govern performance, behavior and systems. I am driven to gain this understanding because I have observed all decisions (decisions, systems, business processes, etc.) are dependent upon a solid understanding of the underlying relationships at play. If our understanding of the underlying relationships is flawed, then the decisions and models we create as a result will also be flawed.

I am also driven to continuous improvement, both personally and professionally - I always want to be better. Professionally speaking this means using relationship management, process engineering, and lessons learned to improve the project management value chain and project ROI.

I find that wherever I have worked, I bring three key strengths to the table: quickly identifying key cause-and-effect relationships, and their drivers; simplifying those relationships for others, and leveraging those relationships to create process improvements and cost-savings benefits.

Why be on LinkedIn?

» First, I use it because it also allows me to network with other like-minded individuals.
» Second, because I believe if you can help someone, then you should. LinkedIn allows me to help more people than I would be able without it.
