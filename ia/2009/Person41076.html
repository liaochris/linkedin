Chantal Mora’s Summary

Chantal is pursuing creative and conceptual challenges in interaction, interface and visual design.

Chantal has worked as a designer since 1999, helping pitch ad campaigns for brands such as Kodak and Citibank. She went on to further develop her digital interests at Parsons School of Design. She explored database, interaction and visual design; and developed a digital clothing tracker as her master's thesis, which was widely publicized and emulated. She graduated with an MFA in Design and Technology from Parsons in 2005. Currently Chantal works as a Art Director at AKQA on Nike and General Mills.

Chantal Mora’s Specialties:

Interaction design, art direction, user interface, flash design, print and packaging design.
