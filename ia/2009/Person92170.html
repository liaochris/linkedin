Hans Peter Brøndmo’s Summary

Hans Peter has spent his career at the intersection of technology innovation and empowering consumers. He is a successful serial entrepreneur who is at it again. Plum, his latest company is making it one-click-easy for people to save the things they find online for themselves and others.

Hans Peter’s last venture was Post Communications, a pioneering email marketing company he founded in 1996 as an entrepreneur in residence at Mohr Davidow Ventures. Post was acquired by Netcentives in 2000. Before Post he co-founded DiVA, a developer of consumer video editing software, acquired by Avid Technology in 1993. In the mid 80's Brøndmo was a co-founder of an expert systems firm funded by Apple Computer. He has also worked at Apple Japan, McKinsey and Company and at CERN (the center for European Nuclear Research).

Hans Peter has addressed more than 50 conferences in the past several years on topics ranging from the new models of sharing and social collaboration on the Internet, to privacy, to entrepreneurship, to the broader impact and influence of technology on individuals. Hans Peter is often featured in national media, and has testified at U.S. Senate and FCC hearings on Internet privacy and spam.
