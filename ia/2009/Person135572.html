Garry Lewis’s Summary

I began building sports stadiums in 3D for Heinlein Schrock Architects fulltime during internship. After graduation I taught 3D and worked in Film as a 3d artist. Visual effects in broadcast and corporate video fell in line next as life would have it. After a good deal of hours later I found it was time for a short break. I turned my attention to teaching and writing a book. Film work presented itself once more and that led to gaming and simulation for military training. I feel fortunate to have been able to work in so many areas of the 3D industry. 3D modeling and animation is just the medium after all. I still find design, and the intitial creation of a new idea to be such a pleasure.
