Sherry Martin’s Summary

Fresh Thinking Ink Advertising & Design provides big agency thinking in a small agency structure. We're different because we’re only as big as our clients require. We draw from a pool of internationally awarded senior talent like Copy Writers, Art Directors, Designers, Web Masters, Producers and Planners to tailor make your creative team.

You pay for the creative product - not process, layers or overhead. When working with Fresh Thinking Ink, you'll notice efficiency, speed and a healthier bottom line. Our clients report they get better ads with more control while having more fun along the way.

As the Creative Director of Fresh Thinking Ink, our Founder, Sherry Martin, brings over fourteen years of internationally awarded experience to the table. She has won an entrepreneur of the year award and created sales for such clients as Molson's, GM, The Toronto Zoo and Shopper's Drug Mart to name but a few. She’s also a professor of Advertising at the prestigious OCAD (Ontario College of Art and Design) and currently writing a book called "What's an Idea and how the heck do I get one".

We’re looking for mid-size companies that don’t have an internal marketing department, new business start-ups that will grow quickly or motivated Marketing Executives.

So who do you know wants original, innovative and compelling ideas that connect a brand to its target and get results? Please give them our web address.

To see some of our work, please check out our website at www.FreshThinkingInk.com

Fresh Thinking Ink – ideas that motivate.

Sherry Martin’s Specialties:

Since April 2001, we’ve been building brands through Marketing Strategies, Corporate Identities and Multi-Media Advertising Campaigns. We provide Logos, positioning lines, stationary sets, websites, ads and promotional materials for both new business start-ups and mid size Nationals like Waterford Wedgwood, Childish Notions and The Arthritis Society of Canada.
