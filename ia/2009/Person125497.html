Kai Liang’s Summary

Building on from 8 years of management consulting experience with a range of significant projects with clients like SinoPec, Vodafone, BT, Deutsch Bank, Nationwide, Tesco, China Netcom etc, Kai started as a full time entreprenuer with a highly committed and skillful team working on a specilist wireless broadband telecom start-up, Coordinate Technologies.

Founded in 2006 with seed capital from three major founders, Coordinate is now clear market leader in providing Wi-Fi and WiMAX roaming clearing services between Chinese operators and 20+ global partners including iPASS, BOINGO, T-Mobile, PCCW, Comfone, NTT, Orange FT. In addition, Coordinate also offers a range of wireless ISP and value adding service based on the Wireless Broadband tecnology and market assets we can drive. Into 2009, Wi-Fi is now a strategic service provided by leading Chinese operators especially China Telecom and China Mobile. This would have very positive impact of the global market targeting at Wi-Fi and WiMAX services.

Coordinate has successfully completed Angle financing round in 2008. We are now fully focused on growing our services and revenue, building on from our experience in China to extend our strength internationally. We will be ready for the first round of Venture Captical funding around the end of 2009.

Kai's other interests including golf, table tennis and helping British Government on relevant policies and research projects to help Chinese entreprenuers in UK.
