Mary L Cottingham’s Summary

M.L. Cottingham is a Senior Consultant at ENVIRON International Corporation, specializing in data management. She has over 15 years of experience in the design, implementation, management and support of database systems for assessing environmental risks. At ENVIRON, M.L. Cottingham has provided ongoing database support to several concurrent projects. In addition, she spearheads ENVIRON's implementation of a centralized data repository (i.e., data warehouse). Before joining ENVIRON, M.L. Cottingham worked as a Business Systems Analyst for M.F. Smith & Associates in Morristown, New Jersey. She has a B.S. in Computer Science from Lehigh University.
