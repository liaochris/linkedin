Robby White’s Summary

I am a passionate Sales Executive for ONEsite.com.

I have a proven track record of being successful in Internet software sales and management. I started out as a communications and customer service guy, learned about Web technology and hosting, combined that with social networking and Web 2.0 - now I let my skills help ONEsite grow its business.

ONEsite is a leading market and technology provider of online community and social media solutions with clients that include Panasonic, American Idol, BeliefNet, Capcom, World Wrestling Entertainment, Clear Channel Radio, Univision Communications, eBay, Cleveland Cavaliers, News International, The Telegraph, AllHipHop.com, and Marvel Entertainment.

We deliver the core technologies and expertise to create social applications that monetize our customers’ content and audiences. We work collaboratively with each client to understand their audience, and implement the customized mix of features and functionality that will drive their social strategy.

ONEsite’s diversity of deployments and customer-driven roadmap have led to the creation of the most scalable, flexible, and extensible platform in the industry. Beyond its technology, ONEsite has set itself apart by creating a unique, collaborative approach.

ONEsite is as much a services company as a technology one, and the result has been highly customized sites that are tailored to our clients’ audiences and ultimately more successful – whether it is driving sales, advertising revenue, or simply brand excitement and involvement.

I enjoy traveling and seeing the world, and with our company growing as rapidly as it is, and our footprint extending around the globe, I will enjoy the exciting opportunties that lie ahead. You can email me Robby @ ONEsite.com or call 405-753-9300 x150 for more information.
