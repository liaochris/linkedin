Ivan Pellegrin’s Summary

Ivan has dedicated his career to bridge the gap between business and I.T., and translate the opportunities and constraints of information technology into relevant and cost-effective solutions.

Ivan has applied himself to the area of data warehousing, business intelligence, and data quality since the mid 80’s before the acronyms BI, DWH and DQ were commonly used.

For more than 20 years Ivan has served an extensive array of clients. His roles span across:
- Data Warehousing and Business Intelligence Architect
- Information Requirements Analyst
- Data Quality Engineer
- Data and Information Analyst
- Database Manager
- ETL Developer
- Data Warehouse Performance Engineer
- Project Manager and Team Leader
- Trusted Advisor

Despite his relative notoriety in his areas of expertise, Ivan is passionate about keeping his feet well grounded into a solid technological knowledge and experience and he displays a long and growing array of technical qualifications accumulated throughout his career.

By living in Italy, the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany, the Netherlands, and finally Belgium, Ivan has developed a degree of savoir-faire when communicating with people coming from a different background. He can play a role of facilitator in re-aligning conflicting parts of an organisation to a shared information strategy.

Ivan is bi-lingual in English and Italian and displays a set of increasing language skills in Dutch, French, German, Spanish, and Portuguese.

Capable of an infectious enthusiasm, Ivan conveys a degree of confidence and applies Optimal Thinking to all aspects of his professional and private life. For more information about Ivan, please visit his blog at http://www.ivanpellegrin.net .
