Hariesh Manaadiar’s Summary

I have been in the shipping industry for 20 years and have worked in India, UAE and currently in South Africa..

During these years i have worked predominantly within container shipping, but have also worked with breakbulk and bulk cargo/vessels.. I have been involved primarily in operations, documentation, ships planning etc, but have since branched off into commercial and sales activities..

One of my goals is to contribute to the industry and help newcomers to the shipping industry to understand, learn and blend into the industry as smoothly as possible..

Hence i started this shipping blog (http://shippinginsouthafrica.wordpress.com) so that it can be used as a reference material for people new to shipping and already in shipping..
