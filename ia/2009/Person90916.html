Bharat Gupta’s Summary

Bharat Gupta, a Senior Manager with Alvarez & Marsal brings more than 11 years of work experience in operational performance improvement, post merger integration, M&A, financial and operational due diligence, financial planning & analysis and controllership. Bharat has gained this experience with blue chip organisations in diverse international environments such as India, Africa, UK and the Middle East.

Bharat has earned a Masters Degree in Business Administration from London Business School and is a qualified Chartered Accountant.

Bharat Gupta’s Specialties:

Strategy - Developing an executable long term strategy aimed to achieve investor expectations. Well versed in standard strategy tools.

Operations - Working Capital Optimisation, FMCG and Retail Market Execution Excellence, New Product Introduction, Channel and Product Profitability analysis, Post Merger Integration

Finance - M&A, Due Diligence, Financial Planning & Analysis, Strengthening Internal Controls and Processes
