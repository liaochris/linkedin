Mitì Vigliero’s Summary

I am a journalist and writer.

Born in Turin, Italy, I live in Genoa since 1980.

My areas of experience include society, folklore, history of the Italian language and custom.

I wrote some books roughly categorizable under humor, poetry, fiction and essays (published by Idea Libri, Sansoni, Marsilio, Rizzoli, Mondadori).

I also wrote for the Italian newspapers Il Giornale, la Repubblica, Libero and magazines Cosmopolitan, Anna and Ticino 7.
