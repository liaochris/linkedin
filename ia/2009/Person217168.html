Shukran Alakbarov, CMA’s Summary

Certified Management Accountant (CMA) is a designation conferred by the US Institute of Certified Management Accountants, and is "the Gold Standard" for finance professionals that are experienced and possess expertise in strategic planning, budgeting, forecasting, controlling, accounting, reporting, working capital management, long-term investment appraisal, and financial statement analysis.

Certified Public Accountant (CPA) is a credential licensed by the governing State Board of Accountancy that allows the holder to practice in the State as an independent accounting practitioner. The role of a CPA is to perform independent financial statement audits, to prepare tax returns, consult on tax planning matters, and to perform various attestation engagements.
