Parameswaran Venkataraman’s Summary

Over the last 12 years, Parameswaran Venkataraman (Param) has worn various hats — Ethnographer, Innovation Consultant, User Researcher, Design Manager, User Interface Designer – in companies like Mindscape (Technopak), GE Healthcare, Sapient Corporation and Planetasia.com, working on projects for India / USA / UK geographies. He started his career in Interaction Design for software & application design, and then specialized in Ethnography & Contextual Studies for New Product / Innovation initiatives.

Param is currently Associate Vice President at Mindscape, the consumer insights division of Technopak – one of India’s leading management consulting firms. In this role, he leads the ‘Innovation & Design Insights’ practice, helping organizations with innovation in their products / services / business for emerging markets like India.

Key Accomplishments:
• Started up and ran a user research & innovation consulting firm, Onward Research + Innovation Pvt. Ltd. between June 2007 and Nov 2008
• Has recently been invited to join a panel of multi-disciplinary experts, as part of ‘The Centre for Emerging Market Solutions’ at the Indian School of Business (ISB), Hyderabad. This initiative will focus on Urbanization for emerging markets like India
• While at GE Healthcare earlier, generated intellectual property (2 defensive publications / patents) based on contextual research done at several hospitals in the United States

Speaking / teaching engagements:
• Speaking and teaching engagements at Indian School of Business (Hyderabad), IIIT-Bangalore, MindTree Consulting, World Usability Day (2006 and 2007), DCamp (Bangalore 2008)

Parameswaran Venkataraman’s Specialties:

Innovation consulting, New Product Innovation, User research, Ethnographic research, Human-Computer Interaction; Design Management
