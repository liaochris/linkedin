Cynthia Shannon’s Summary

Cynthia completed the International Baccalaureate from the Frankfurt International School in Germany, and then graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Journalism from New York University. She suddenly realized that newspapers weren’t her thing, and decided to take a job in the publishing industry, where she started at the small, independent publisher Other Press, LLC as a Publicity Assistant. She then joined John Wiley and Sons as an Associate Publicist, and was promoted to Publicist for the Jossey-Bass imprint in June 2008.

For this promotion, Cynthia relocated to San Francisco in July 2008. In her spare time, Cynthia volunteers with Litquake and 826 Valencia. In New York, Cynthia had been a tutor for Literacy Partners for a year, teaching reading and math to adults. Cynthia also volunteered at the Center for Independent Publishing, where she assisted with coordinating the Writers Conference and Book Fair.

Cynthia plans on staying in publishing forever, or at least until she buys a vineyard in Italy.
