Luke Gordon’s Summary

Being born at the end of the Baby boomer era, I was brought up with the paradigm which was dished out to me by my parents - get good marks at school, go to university and persue a profession that will deliver a decent standard of living and perhaps a dollop of prestige.

I was fairly good at Maths and Science and I noticed that our family GP never seemed to be short of work so I decided to do medicine. Once I was studying, I decided to specialize in plastic surgery not realizing that cosmetic surgery would be a major industry as baby boomers moved into retirement age.

The frustration I experienced was that as a one man practice, I had very little time to pursue other pastimes and was somewhat bewildered at having to work long and hard hours.

I discovered Michael Gerber's The E-Myth revisited where he states that in order to attain freedom, you have to have a systems orientation to your business. Work ON your business and not IN it and put systems in place. People run the systems and the systems run the business. Think like an entrepreneur and not a technician.

Having built a clinic, I was still frustrated at not having the time and realized that there are huge opportunities in the massive anti-aging megatrend as I was all too aware of the lengths people would go to to stave off getting old.

I have discovered what I believe to be a major break through in the area of anti-aging and I am looking to partner with go-getters who also see the massive potential in anti-aging.
