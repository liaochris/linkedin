Christopher Lees’s Summary

Results-oriented professional with 17 years experience in law enforcement, loss prevention, and security management. Served in the U.S. Air Force for two tours as a Security Police Officer, deploying to Desert Storm and supporting several other mission critical endeavors. I worked at Nordstrom for 5 years as Regional Investigator, Loss Prevention Manager, and Area Manager before moving on to The Home Depot where I spent 5 years as Loss Prevention Manager, District Asset Protection Manager, and Corporate Security Divisional Investigator. Most recently I am with The Walt Disney Company as Regional Loss Prevention Manager. I enjoy learning, as I have my California POST, Bachelor of Science in Organizational Security and Management, and finishing my Masters in Administration of Justice and Security with hopes of teaching some day.
