Peter Liao’s Summary

Peter Liao is currently a senior concentrating in Communication Studies at the University of Michigan and will be graduating on the 1st of May. Throughout his college career, Peter has distinguished himself by excelling in his coursework, while taking on leadership roles in organizing and promoting successful fundraising and publicity events for various non-profit organizations and fashion magazines. Additionally, he has acted as a consultant for Motivation--the largest urban lifestyle retailer in Ann Arbor.

Professionally, he has managed to earn the confidence and respect of his former employers and co-workers by being a self-starter, a frequent contributor, and an outstanding team member. Moreover, his conspicuous motivation, thirst for knowledge, and swift and efficient style of working in everything that he does, allowed him to stand out among his colleagues during his internships at EMI and Universal Music Group.

Not only does he possess excellent communication skills, but he also works well under pressure, is very keen to details, and is able to adapt to unforeseen changes in any situation. Furthermore, he has an affinity for new technology and is very quick to learn and master new programs and applications.

While he is particulary interested in marketing, event management, and/or product development within the entertainment industry, he is always open to exploring new career territories; he welcomes any opportunity to learn and grow.
