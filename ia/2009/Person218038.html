Simon Harvey’s Summary

Simon predominantly architects and implements Security solutions for large and small organisations, at the business and technical levels.

Simon has specialised in Identity & Access Management, Risk Assessment and Security Architecture projects at both architect/technical and at Project Management levels. Simon has worked within multi-disciplinary and multi-national teams in regions as diverse as the UK, mainland Europe, the Middle East, South-East Asia and Australia.

Simon has worked as a Security Consultant in Government, Financial Services, Resources and Communications industries and also previously as an academic researcher.
