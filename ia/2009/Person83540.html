Garry McCoppin’s Summary

After 10 yrs Sales/Marketing, Product Management, and Branch Management experience with Chrysler Corporation, Airtemp Division, I completed my M.S. in Management Science & Finance, and led growth initiatives in marketing/sales and product development for 3 organizations and general management/COO/President positions in 4 organizations. I now specialize as interim COO/CEO or business consultant in $5-$35 MM companies with fewer than 125 employees, usually privately held manufacturers/distributors, with founder or younger professional management.

Industries include HVAC (heating, ventilation, air conditioning), swimming pool equipment (pumps, filters, heaters, valves, lights, controls) residential/commercial heating products, commercial fans, balancing valves & coil piping packages, industrial pumps, medical machines, composite parts for aerospace and aviation, skylight installations, and pad & screen printing.

Garry McCoppin’s Specialties:

Product development Real-Win-Worth - launches, Organizational team building, Open-book management, Restructure manufacturing accounting, Due diligence for M&A, Turn-around management, Sales force development, NGT-continuous improvement
