Roger Jackson’s Summary

Highly skilled Data Analyst, Data Warehouse Architect and Cognos Specialist with 25 years experience in Commercial Systems Analysis and Data Modeling. Principal BI Consultant with Cognos for many years.

Heavy emphasis on independent Relational and Dimensional Modeling using ERwin and Cognos tools. Full life-cycle experience. Seeking opportunities to coach and mentor IT individuals wanting to rapidly extend their skills in Data Warehouse design and implementation.

Our aim at 3BI is to make Business Intelligence simple and scalable.

if you are a potential client that requires an Architect/Designer with more than 10 years of experience of building data warehouse applications then please contact me. I am probably the most experienced person in the UK in using Cognos DataManager and I have built enterprise data warehouses using Oracle PLSQL; I have worked with Informatica ETL being developed by TATA and I have experience of SQL Server 6.5/7/2008 & Oracle 7/8/9/10 in conjunction with Oracle OeBiz and SAP enterprise applications as well as many years of experience working with financial organisations such as Schroeders, Merrill Lynch, Deutsche Bank, Barclays, BZW, Bank of Canada, and others.

My concern with so many data warehouse developer roles on offer is that [data warehouse] applications are much too complex to be managed by a single person and now I concentrate on using my experience to mitigate the many risks of failure of projects. This is in preference to trying combine the diverse skills of analysis, user-liaison, design, project-management, programming ETL, programming Reports, operations job-control, release-control and politics. I would expect to be working as part of a team and the "one-man-band" approach should be considered a quick-win with no expectation of scalability or maintenance in the production environment for a corporation.
