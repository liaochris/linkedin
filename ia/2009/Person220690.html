Sam Palazzolo, CPLP’s Summary

Pathos Leadership Group is the "Home of the Influential Edge!" Most leadership development programs are "pie-in-the-sky" or polyannaish at best with "21 Irrefutable Laws" and "360* Leader" titles. It leaves people grasping for a road-map to implementation! We discovered a little known study by social psychologists regarding influence and persuasion that allows leadership development outcomes of hard, implementable road-maps for implementation. Participants in our sessions burn rubber!

I provide Leadership Coaching (Certified Coach and member of the University of Texas "Executive Coaching Program"), Organizational Development (Based on my book "The Influential Leader), Keynote Addresses, and an Affiliate Program.

The Affiliate Program is unique to the Coaching, Training and Speaking industry. Most new entrepreneurs head out into their new venture with a few "tools in the toolbox" so to speak. With our Affiliate Program, we "bridge the gap" for these new start-ups by providing our extensive training programs. We work with them to help establish our programs in the organizations they'd like to work with by doing all of the marketing/enrolling for the events with our customized marketing process. It's been an awesome undertaking and we've seen first-hand how it's helped people just starting-up leverage their talents right from the start!

In October I received notification from ASTD (The American Society of Training & Development) that I have successfully completed their grueling CPLP (Certified Professional in Learning & Performance)!!! Why did I get this designation? Anyone can throw-out a shingle and call themselves a trainer. I wanted the only professional designation that indicates that I know the in's/out's of professionally training individuals with impact towards a business’s bottom line.

That's what Pathos is all about...The Home of the Influential Edge!
