Linda Burlison’s Summary

Linda began her career at one of Canada’s early interactive agencies, Caught in the Web/Personus.

For 10+ years, she has worked with leaders such as Mercedes, Nestle, Coke, ZipCar, Circuit City, CIBC, Nintendo, Labatt, Olympic Bid, Vonage, Beer.com, Organic, BrandWorks and others, helping organizations achieve their business objectives online.

Currently she is responsible for Dell Canada Online Demand Gen, (managing Affiliate, SEO, PPC, Display, Social Media, Email and Shopping Feed channels).

Prior to Dell, Linda was the Director of Digital Media & Analytics at Canada's most awarded interactive agency where she turned an unprofitable media business into the agency's fasted growing division.

With P&L responsibility, Linda built a team of professionals in results-oriented online media, paid search, organic search, social/emerging media and analytics, and built the client list from two to ten.

Division clients included Nestle Canada, Maple Leaf, Dr. Oetker, Capital One, Mercedes-Benz, Molson, Nintendo, Circuit City, Lainey Gossip, Primus, CTV/Canadian Idol, Ontario Securities Commission, and CAA.

Previously Linda grew a Search Marketing practice within a traditional agency (Brandworks); launching Vonage and Nikon's first Canadian PPC campaigns.

At the same time Linda ran a Search Marketing consultancy, working with clients including National Geographic, DaimlerChrysler, Delvinia Interactive and BBDO.

Linda is a respected speaker, with credits including: Toronto SES 2007; Sheridan College; Canadian Marketing Association’s 2005/6/7/8 Digital Marketing Conference; and the CMA's Digital Marketing course.

Linda was one of the first 100 people worldwide to become a qualified Google Adwords Professional.

She speaks conversational Japanese and holds an MBA from McMaster University.

Linda was recently adopted by a lovely 5-year old Bernese Mountain Dog named 'Mama' from the SPCA.
