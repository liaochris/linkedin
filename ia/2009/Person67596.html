Dr Pankaj Gupta’s Summary

I come with a rich combination of strong management skills, software experience and medical background. For over fourteen years I have been working in eHealth business and Healthcare process standardization. This includes experience of starting and leading Healthcare Consulting and Healthcare-IT units @ Infosys, FCG, Satyam and Cerner. I have managed IT Departments of Hospitals in US and delivered business transformation by process standardization. I am a Healthcare-IT person having specialized in General Management from Indian Institute of Management Bangalore.

I have wide experience in Healthcare standards and processes consulting such as HL7, CCR, CDA, CPOE, EMR, ISO 13485, 21 CFR part 820, and JCAHO. I have applied these standards in various projects and provided value to the clients.

I am also an offshore-outsourcing expert. I have served as a consultant to setup offshore India centre for Healthcare-IT companies to reduce cost and increase productivity by using the 24/7 working model. I have done Organization change management, Business transformation, Knowledge management and Transition management on globalsourcing model.

My experience spans Healthcare-IT Consulting, Business analysis, Solution design, Software design and Project Management for North-American Hospitals, Lifesciences and Pharma companies. I have specialized in General Management from Indian Institute of Management Bangalore. Due to my background and experience I am interested in Digital Hospitals, Pharma R&D Labs, Medical devices or next generation Healthcare Software development and implementation.

I have demonstrated ability to start and run business units from scratch. For Healthcare projects I have effectively applied PMBOK and CMMi Project Management standards.
