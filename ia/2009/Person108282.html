Jeff Nugent’s Summary

"Did you know on average Contractors or Project Firm Resources account for more than 20% of most large organizations workforces and account for the largest single line item expense?"

Contingent Workforce Solutions Inc specializes in developing and implementing best-of-class contingent workforce solutions (people, processes & technology) that help its clients reach its contractor and contract staffing vendor management program goals.

As a truly Vendor Neutral Partner we Provide:
- Advisory and Program Consulting Expertise
- Managed Program Outsourcing Services
- Third Party Contractor Payrolling and Administration Service

With close to 25 Years of Experience in Contractor, Temp staffing and Vendor Management Solutions, CWS is a partner that you can trust.

Jeff Nugent’s Specialties:

Consulting Expertise in Contingent Workforce Management, Risk Management, Rate Management, Recruitment and Staffing, Vendor Management Solutions, Business Process Management.
