Brian Minards’s Summary

International, award-winning strategic design, corporate and brand strategy, communication, and management experience with clients in the public and private sectors, operating with companies which include the World's largest marketing communications group and a UK top-5 brand consultancy.

Brian Minards’s Specialties:

Strong design management, organisational, communication and presentation skills, and the experience to orchestrate and inspire successful, cross-disciplinary teams to deliver the right solutions.
