Tanmoy Saha’s Summary

Experienced consulting professional and aspiring business leader with more than 7 years of experience in IT Consulting, Project Management and Program Management in the areas of business and technology risk management, identity management, application security and payment security in large banks and manufacturing companies in India and Australia.

Currently providing leadership in setting up businesses for new service lines in the healthcare (21 CFR Part 11) and payment security (PCI DSS) space as part of regulatory and compliance areas.

I am also part of the quantum innovation program in Wipro, focusing on the launching of new services for banking and defense verticals.


Key highlights of my career:


- Experienced in project management and program management using industry best practices involving scoping, requirements gathering and analysis, project planning and monitoring, coordinating with stakeholders, people management and client relationship management

- Expert in designing and implementing risk management systems involving risk assessment of information systems and business processes, identity management solutions, payment security solutions, review of IT and business process controls, designing solutions for regulatory compliance and security in application development & deployment using globally accepted best practices.

- Involved in conceptualization of new service lines and business opportunities, which included identifying market potential, research on industry trends and global standards, and creation of practice offerings.

- Hands on experience in designing technology-based solutions involving network analysis and design, security architecture design and implementation, identity management solution design and deployment, application security, web services security and payment security.
