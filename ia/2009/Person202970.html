F. Romall Smalls’s Summary

F. Romall Smalls has more than 15 years of journalism and media relations experience and has a longstanding commitment to insightful media development and impactful news reporting.
Currently Mr. Smalls is the Associate Editor of Scholastic News Magazine 5/6, one of the nation’s leading weekly news publications for students and teachers.
His award-winning work as a journalist has appeared in The New York Times, Black Enterprise, Gannett Newspapers, The Sphinx, The Source, Africana.com, blackenterprise.com and Scribner’s Encyclopedias.
He is the former Director of Public Relations for the City of Mount Vernon, NY, the seventh largest municipality in New York. He is also the founder and CEO of the Mallio Media Group, a full-service media consulting firm.
Past clients include GMAD Regional Conference at Brown University, Providence, RI, featuring the Rev. Jesse Jackson and Congresswoman Donna Christian-Christensen; The Frederick Douglass Creative Arts Center’s 26th Annual Black Roots Festival at Symphony Space, featuring Quincy Troupe and Saul Williams, New York, NY.
