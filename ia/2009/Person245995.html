Willard C. Morgan III’s Summary

Willard C. Morgan III ("Bill") is a senior-level finance and accounting professional with an impressive 20-year career. Bill delivers strong leadership, strategy formulation, and business management savvy to achieve corporate goals.

Bill is skilled in building talented teams and is highly focused on getting things done. He is experienced in managing day-to-day accounting operations; financial reporting; project management; process improvements; and effective communications.

Bill began his career as an auditor in public accounting for six years. He was then an accounting manager for several years at two large companies, ValueOptions and USA Today. For the past 8 years, Bill has been successful as Controller, VP Finance, and CFO for several small and medium-sized private companies.

RECENTLY...

Accent Energy LLC repositioned to face fundamental changes in the retail energy marketplace. This repositioning resulted in a significant downsizing. Bill's role as Controller was among those positions eliminated.

JOB SEARCH...

Bill is seeking a new opportunity. He is open to a wide variety of challenges, but areas of special interest include: business/professional services; healthcare; energy; communications and technology; construction; and government contractors. Challenging and rewarding roles include senior accountant, accounting manager, financial analyst, finance manager, and similar positions. However, Bill's ideal opportunity would be Chief Financial Officer or Controller for a small or medium-sized private business.
