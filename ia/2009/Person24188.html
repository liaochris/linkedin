Stephen (Guim) Barbour’s Summary

I was a co-founder of Medical Funding Services, a healthcare finance company that provides enhanced cash flow services to hospitals and other medical providers. While there, I helped build and grow the business from concept to 40+ employees and a $50 million annual run rate.

Since leaving Medical Funding Services, I have been providing consulting services for Ozmosis, Inc, an early stage company that empowers physicians to share knowledge, build and nurture professional relationships, and collaborate in real-time through a Web 2.0 platform. My areas of focus for the company have included competitive strategy and marketing activities to build the Ozmosis online physician community.

I am a 2005 MBA graduate of the Darden School (University of Virginia), and an American/German dual-citizen. I graduated from Georgetown University in May 1998 with both a Finance and an International Business degree.

While I have extensive experience managing strategy consulting projects for Fortune 500 clients across multiple industries, my career goals focus on helping build and grow businesses internally.
