Jane Davison’s Summary

Communications professional experienced in design, copywriting, and marketing for small to mid-sized businesses and non-profits. The bulk of my experience is in healthcare, education, and enterprises seeking to improve the quality of life for children, families and seniors.

Jane Davison’s Specialties:

Branding and identity, small web sites, comprehensive marketing collateral from concept to completion, copywriting, environmental design, internal and external communications (from analysis to crisis planning and newsletters). Also available for voice work, script development and digital photography/portraiture.
