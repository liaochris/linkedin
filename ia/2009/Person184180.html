Payson Cooper’s Summary

I create unique jewelry from my own designs, and work with customers to create custom pieces. I also create and manufacture lines for companies that wish to add a signature piece or pieces to their other offerings. Recently I designed a piece to be part of a book launch and I am working on an entire line for a large seminar company. I have 9 years of experience in a variety of jewelry techniques and love creating pieces using all of them, particularly 3D digital/CAD creation.

Prior to working full time in the jewelry industry I worked as a systems designer for the City of New York Human Resources Department. I was part of a team that created, developed and rolled out both the Paperless Office System and the Model Office to Job Centers throughout the 5 boroughs of NYC.
