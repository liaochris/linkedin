Jonathan Smith’s Summary

I am an enthusiastic, curious, forward thinking designer with five years of product design/development experience. I do what it takes to get the job done right the first time and I leave the ego at home. I am a Senior level Industrial Designer with consultancy and corporate experience.

I have experience working with overseas vendors in China and Israel. I have lead and worked with team members to develop cost effective and aesthetically pleasing solutions. I have lead cost cutting measures to garner greater profits and make potentially viable product releases possible.

I have worked directly with ethnographic and generative researchers in the field to gain my greatest insights into customer needs.

My breadth of experiences has instilled in me a great understanding of the many facets of industrial design. I look forward to learning more about product development.

I learn a lot from my mistakes and I am not afraid of making them. I focus on the users needs and consumer experience as a defining basis for design solutions.

Market research, generative and observational, are key factors in identifying the consumers underlying wants and needs.
