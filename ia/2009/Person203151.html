Ronald Achs’s Summary

Dynamic, engaging and proven professional with 25 years of executive leadership and senior management experience in entrepreneurial and emerging commodity-based companies. Participated in successful start-ups, raised over $20MM in venture capital, lead all significant contract negotiations for strategic partnerships, key customers, and mergers and acquisitions. Hand-picked and successfully recruited management teams and multinational BODs, and obtained government and political endorsements and assistance. Superior leadership and management skills with an emphasis on execution, strategic planning, managing profit and loss, mitigating commodity and cash flow volatility, brand creation, winning new business, and adjusting to market conditions leading to establishing trusting business relationships and employee loyalty.
