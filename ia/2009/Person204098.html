Ross Ashton’s Summary

Creative Designer and Consultant in large scale projection.
Video Projection Design.
Slide Projection Design.
Projection Art

I work in video and slide projection.
I normally work on a large scale and am known for my architectural projection work. However, I also work in theatre and rock and roll as well as smaller events and artistic installations. As well as designing original pieces and “son et lumieres”, I work as a consultant on the design of installation for events of all kinds.

My portfolio includes:
‘Cambridge 800’: the celebration of the 800th anniversary of the University of Cambridge. This piece was taken to China early in 2009 and will be performed in New York in December 2009.
‘Rockestra’: a ‘classical rock’ concert fundraiser for the Malta Community Chest Fund.
‘Palimpsest’: an artwork commissioned by the Pittsburgh Festival of Light 2009.
‘The Mayor of Londons New Years Eve Celebrations’: working as a consultant on the installation from 2005 and as designer of the content for 2008.
‘The Edinburgh Military Tattoo’: 2005 to the present. Creative and designer of the installation and the content.
