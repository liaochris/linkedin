Frank Settineri’s Summary

VeraCorp LLC provides pharmaceutical, biotech, medical device and startup companies with the essential advice they need to keep their business running efficiently and within regulatory guidelines.

Our specialties include GMP compliance, validation planning, outsourcing, microbiology, sterile and non-sterile manufacturing issues and addressing FDA Warning Letters and 483s. We focus on three main areas, Science, Compliance and Business.

In the Science area we rectify microbiology excursions and conduct employee training. In the Compliance area we lift FDA warning letters and answer 483s. In the Business area we build virtual companies, find and retain the best individuals.

Frank Settineri, Founder, spent his career setting up and running laboratories; auditing, validating, and outsourcing supplier and vendors; and building a start-up pharmaceutical company and its infrastructure. He has helped clients ranging from start ups to fortune 500s; from laboratories to manufacturing facilities; from cosmetics to medical devices.

Veracorp's relentless commitment to our clients and our common sense approach to their needs helps them solve their most difficult problems readily and cost effectively.
