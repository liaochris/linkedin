Issam Saby’s Summary

I worked in a New York based Agency for a year as an Online Media Buyer for affiliate accounts. I was put in charge of the company's publishers accounts on various networks such as Tradedoubler and Linkshare. My job consisted in acquiring targeted traffic for affiliate programs through multiples media channels in particular: PPC and Contextual networks. Through research I gained a good understanding of the affiliate marketing value chain, keyword research and campaign optimization techniques. Those helped me to raise the turnover of the campaigns I was responsible of by 27% in 7 months. I attended industry's events such as Ad Tech New York and San Francisco where I did some networking, traffic provider research and met partners we already worked with.

When I left TheMedias I went back to France and did Online Media Buying for my own account while I was finishing my Business school curriculum. I succeeded as a freelance Media Buyer and developed one to one relations with affiliate programs managers. These relations helped me to understand the issues associated with affiliate account management.

Recently, I've had an interest in affiliate website management. I took an exclusive on-line course by affiliate marketer Jeremy Palmer which has helped me to learn a lot of technical and practical knowledge in a short amount of time. Particularly on: Dynamic landing pages, php server side includes, data feeds use and integration as well as broad affiliate marketing knowledge: google quality score, market research, etc…

This course led me to create the website LocationDeDvdEnLigne.com which I promote mainly through Adwords and monetize with affiliate links. I built this website entirely using Dreamweaver, Photoshop and the content is integrated with Wordpress. I use Optimize My Site in conjunction with Google analytics to track my PPC costs and affiliate commissions.

I'm fully bilingual in French and English
