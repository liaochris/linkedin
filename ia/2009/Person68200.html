Tibor Duliskovich’s Summary

I am a medical doctor and board certified (in Hungary) clinical radiologist with 16 years of experience in all modalities of diagnostic radiology using both conventional and digital equipment, and with outstanding technical and physics background and deep involvement in hardware and software development. I do not shy away from tough situations, tight deadlines and high responsibility. I am self-motivated; enjoy working both individually and as a team member. I have demonstrated the ability to change jobs, locations and industries and produce results in new environments.
