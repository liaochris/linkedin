William M. Thom - MISM, PMP’s Summary

Seasoned and energetic Project Management Professional that successfully builds information systems solutions that support business goals. Uphold excellent leadership skills that support diversified and cross functional project teams, with a track record of success in the most challenging of circumstances. Incorporation of sound and proven principles from PMBOK, ITIL, CMMI and others that lead to successful project implementation and high customer satisfaction.

I have fifteen years of expertise directly related to the Project Management of integrating business processes with Information Technologies.

You can find me listed in the PMBOK Guide - Fourth Edition as a Contributor and Reviewer ... pg. 392 in Appendix C

William M. Thom - MISM, PMP’s Specialties:
