Dominick Crincoli’s Summary

Corporate Communication Professional with expertise in internal and external communications, sales, marketing and human resources (HR). Tech-savvy communication strategist with strong marketing and business background, writing experience with The NY Times and demonstrated expertise in portal communication, including Web 2.0. Results-driven innovator, skilled negotiator/consensus-builder and proven project manager with experience executing complex communication campaigns including planning, budgeting and segmented messaging to a geographically and functionally dispersed clientele.

Keen understanding of how communication can establish linkage between employee engagement and a company’s financial performance.
