In 1985, Adam Levine qualified and obtained two security licenses, series 7 & 63. Adam Levine began market making and trading stocks for Domestic Arbitrage Group, a NASD securities dealer in Pompton Plains, NJ. Within a year, Bear Sterns and Co. recruited Adam, and offered him a position as a retail broker. Adam Levine left Bear for a more lucrative position with Charles Schwab & Co. Adam’s experience in securities included arbitrage, research, equities, options, LPs and fixed income.

He has worked for some many of the mortgage industry leaders including Household Finance (now HSBC), Long Beach Bank, New Century Financial, Bear Sterns, Charles Schwab & Co., and WMC (Weyerhaeuser Mortgage). His experiences have included successfully managing over one hundred people both at WMC, Zone Funding and American Business Financial Services.

Adam Levine has served as a corporate officer for two prominent mortgage-banking operations; as President of Zone Funding, a Mortgage Corp. of America Company, and as CFO/CMO of Rekaren Incorporated. During his twenty-three year financial career, his responsibilities included secondary marketing, diligence, senior underwriting, finance, and sales and executive management, including investor delegation on transactions up to two million dollars. My underwriting authority was granted by Deutsche Bank; Bear Sterns; and Credit Suisse.

Currently, Adam Levine is working with other risk managers performing diligence services for clients, and representing buyers and sellers of non-performing assets including NPN's and distressed real estate.
