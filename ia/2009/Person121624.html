Josh Zaretsky’s Summary

Josh Zaretsky has served as Director of Product Marketing at Meraki since October 2008.

From 2006-2008, Josh served as Senior Manager, Small Business Strategy for Cisco Systems. In that role he drove development of Cisco’s SMB strategy across multiple product lines and channels, assessed Cisco's opportunities in adjacent markets, developed marketing initiatives and pilots, and shaped Cisco's customer segmentation of small businesses.

From 2003-2006, Mr. Zaretsky served as a Director with Gartner’s Market and Business Strategy consulting practice. There he sold and led business strategy engagements for the world’s leading technology companies. Specifically, Mr. Zaretsky served as Gartner’s lead at Cisco Systems, earning over $2M in revenue in just two years. Other clients included: Avaya, SBC, Samsung, NTT, Alcatel, HP, and Sun Microsystems. He also assisted a number of venture capital firms evaluate investment opportunities.

Prior to Gartner, Mr. Zaretsky spent three years with the Alliance Consulting Group, a spin-off of the Boston Consulting Group. At Alliance he assisted clients build business, marketing and e-business strategies, and his clients included: Office Depot, PricewaterhouseCoopers, and AT&T.

Mr. Zaretsky also spent two years at Info-Prod Research (Middle East) Ltd, a consulting firm in Tel Aviv, Israel, assisting Global 500 firms develop market entry strategies throughout the Middle East and North Africa. His projects took him to Israel, Jordan, Turkey and the Persian Gulf.

Mr. Zaretsky received bachelor’s degrees in economics and political science from the University of California at Davis and an MBA from Cornell University.
