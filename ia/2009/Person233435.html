Tim Bianchi’s Summary

I am in the “People” business. I enjoy meeting people and learning about their goals, desires and passions. The most rewarding experience I have ever had is helping a person find a career they are passionate about.

My approach with people is to get beyond their resume to see who they are inside and then work to help align their talents with a career that is truly a fit. My background is very diverse, which has given me the opportunity to gain a wealth of experience and knowledge that is very useful in serving others. I have also come to believe “People don’t quit jobs they leave relationships”. I have observed this throughout my career with people in many different situations and industries. What we do is important but who we do it for (the company or immediate supervisor) is more important! If we don’t feel the value returned for our efforts we quickly become discouraged and leave.

My career has exposed me to recruiting and staffing, HR policies and procedures with large and small companies. One of my favorite roles has been in the training arena. I have had the privilege of creating and delivering training programs as well as being a facilitator for Franklin Covey’s “7 Habits of Highly Effective People”.

That’s why, when I had the opportunity to start my own company, it had to be in the people business. I have taken my 20 plus years of business and life experience and combined it with my passion for helping people. This led me to adding Success Coaching to the list of services I offer.
