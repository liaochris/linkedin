Carles Salvany’s Summary

I have a great passion for videogames, cinema and everything that involves creativity.
After graduating from an Msc in Videogame Creation, I have been working as an animator in a Barcelona-based videogame studio. This, added to my experience in the development of the award-winning independent videogame “Number 6”, has given me a valuable insight in the videogame development process.
