Elliott Pesut’s Summary

Elliott Z. Pesut graduated from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University's Prescott, Arizona campus with a Bachelors Degree in Aeronautical Science (2007). He is experienced in the design and development of contextually-based aviation curriculum learning experiences. Mr. Pesut is currently a Courseware Developer for Alaska Airlines and is responsible for developing Computer Based Learning Experiences for the pilots that fly the Boeing 737. Elliott is a consultant and knowledge broker. He consults with companies and educational institutions about future applications of emerging technology platforms that support the design and development of teaching/learning products and services.

Elliott Pesut’s Specialties:

Microsoft Office, Adobe Creative Suite, Final Cut, HTML, Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), Web Design and numerous social media / marketing tools (i.e. blogs, RSS, wikis, etc) Logo/Identity Development & Design, Print Design, Knowledge of Numerous Web Applications/Hosted Software.
