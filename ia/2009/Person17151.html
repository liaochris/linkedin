Anne Newell’s Summary

Anne Newell is a Business Consultant and Executive Coach with over 25 years experience working with Fortune 500 companies, non-profit organizations, small businesses, and entrepreneurs in the areas of Organizational Effectiveness; Performance Improvement; Training design, development, and delivery; Coaching; Strategy; Change Management; and Measurement.

Anne is also the founder of Chicks with Checks, a social network and giving circle comprised of local chapters and an online community. Chicks with Checks supports low profile, high impact local non-profits through monthly Parties with a Purpose.

As an author and speaker, Anne has written articles on a variety of performance-related topics. Her newest book, The MYTH of Time Management, was released in 2008.

Anne is a graduate of the Honor College at the University of South Carolina and of the National Institute for Teaching Excellence at Cambridge.

Anne Newell’s Specialties:

Organizational Effectiveness; Performance Improvement; Training Design, Development, and Delivery; Executive and Entrepreneur Coaching; Strategy; Change Management; and Measurement.
