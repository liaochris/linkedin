Bruce Friedman’s Summary

CTO at Movêro Technology, developing Managed Wireless Solutions for the enterprise and helping corporate customers create "support ecosystems" for mobile and wireless systems. As CTO I am responsible for identifying new service initiatives consistent with Movêro’s strategic plans, developing key opportunities, managing intellectual property development and advancing ongoing operations initiatives.

Before Movero, I was vice president of Sprint’s Mobile Computing Services Group responsible for delivering a suite of market facing services including 3G messaging, mobile applications development and custom integrated solutions. I directed several of Sprint’s successful initiatives in mobility, ecommerce, IT consulting and managed services, helping to transform Sprint from a long distance telecommunication provider into a next-generation wireless Internet infrastructure company.

Before joining Sprint, I was the executive director of the energy technology practice at MCI Systemhouse, vice president of sales for Mission Critical Software, and spent six years with EDS in its business systems consulting and implementation division working with a variety of business sectors.

I am a frequent and enthusiastic speaker about the emerging M-Economy as well as how leadership and business governance in mobility is a model for creating new opportunities and process transformation. Through my career, I have served in various advisory and board roles for several successful technology companies in the telecommunications, financial, health care, media and manufacturing markets.

I have been recognized as a leading wireless executives and widely quoted by The Wall Street Journal, MSNBC.com, InfoWorld, PC World, InformationWeek and many other national and international publications. I have also been honored speak at CTIA, HIMSS, COMDEX, IEEE and as a keynote at IM Planet among many others.

Bruce Friedman’s Specialties:

managed mobility, advanced wireless services, wireless technology, mobile applications, instant messaging technologies, carrier contract negotiations, business development for companies targeting the telecom industry, venture funding
