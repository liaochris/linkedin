Steinar Brenden’s Summary

Over the last years I have been working with my own investments and developing the business as an entrepreneur. My main focus from 2004 has been biotech. After my first job in the oil business, it was mostly about IT and telecom except for 8 years in management consulting with lots of interesting and demanding projects.
Has been the main owner of Infostream AS since 2002 which is rooted in the first Norwegian internet-company, but later has sold out all of its business.
Before I began focusing on my own business development and investments, I was a senior partner in the management consultancy Semco AS. In Semco I had the priveledge of being in charge of several large scale change programs through-out the 1990-ies. Before Semco I had my own software company developing banking system from 1986 to 1991 when I sold the software. My first job just after business school (Handelshøyskolen BI) was in the oil industry in the group treasury department of Geco AS and later as Financial Director in one of its subsidiaries.
