David Swain’s Summary

Retired from the UK's Royal Navy after nearly 34 years service worldwide. A naval aviator by trade and a deep specialist in Anti-Submarine Warfare, unusually dual-qualifying both as Maritime Helicopter Observer and as a surface warfare specialist (PWO(U)). I have had two NATO appointments: one in Naples, Italy, coordinating maritime interdiction operations in the Adriatic during the Bosnia crisis and the other in Norfolk, Virginia as the head of intelligence for NATO's North Atlantic Command area as well as overseeing the introduction of NATO's Open Source Intelligence capability. I also completed two tours in the Permanent Joint HQ, planning and running many of the UK's overseas military operations. Always in the wrong place at the wrong time I have seen active duty in many parts of the world, including the Falklands, Grenada, Northern Ireland and the Arabian Gulf. A graduate of the CASS Business School, City University, London, I am now well into my second career as the Chief Executive of a Private Members Club in central London.
