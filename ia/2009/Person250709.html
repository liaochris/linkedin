Yves Lehmann’s Summary

Financial Engineering, structuring and business development. Currently in charge of Variable Annuity (VA) and GMxB solutions. Participated in the development of the Lyxor hedge fund platform, the launch of hidden assets (correlation swaps, investable credit and volatility indices) and ALM solutions for financial institutions. Educational partnerships with EDHEC, University of St Gallen and Hong Kong University of Science & Technology.

Interests include new developments in areas of pensions, sustainable development and new frontiers in financial technologies.

Yves Lehmann’s Specialties:

Capital markets, derivatives, Variable Annuities & GMxB, insurance and pension ALM, financial engineering, stationery & office automation.
