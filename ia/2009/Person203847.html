Run Your Business Like A Fortune 100

Editorial Reviews

Great businesses start small and achieve success with the vision, courage and tenacity of the entrepreneurs who conceive and build them. Turning your business into a first-rate, reliable generator of profitable growth year after year takes creative positioning, winning tactics and effective tools.

When you’re an entrepreneur,nearly every decision you make can lead to major financial and operational consequences. The risks you take have to be smart ones. Begin today to Run Your Business Like a Fortune 100! Implement proven principles to get and stay on the track to peak profitability, while avoiding all the traps that can drain the life out of your growing company.

Rosalie Lober, Ph.D. (Greenwich, CT) is the CEO of PROFITS Principles, Turning Profitable Businesses Into Great Ones, by simplifying and adapting best practices of Fortune 100 companies with CEOs of small and medium sized businesses and with intrapreneurs of larger companies.

As a problem solver and turnaround specialist, her focus is enhancing strategic and operational processes, developing people, boosting results and increasing profitability.

For 15 years Rosalie led management teams in roles including: Global Head of Learning and Development at Bloomberg Financial Markets/Media; Vice President of Learning, Organizational Development, Communication at Venator Group (Footlocker); Vice President of Performance Management, Training and Organizational Development at Citibank.

Consulting includes: NASDAQ, New York Times, Countrywide Financial, FDIC, UBS, Citigroup, WPP, FootLocker, Combustion Engineering, Polo Ralph Lauren, AIG, Clairol, Philip Morris, Federal Reserve Bank, LinensN'Things, Success-Force - applying best practices to small and medium-sized companies.
