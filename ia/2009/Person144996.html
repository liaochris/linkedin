Luis Ampuero’s Summary

Dynamic Spanish national, with an international carrier and specialized in all related to the "Global Leather Industry" and it's anexe industries. Having worked for several tanneries during 8 years and leading chemical multinationals for 15 years, providing leather technical & marketing support to customers “tanners” in Latin America, Africa, Europe and Asia.

Lately involved in the ultra competitive automotive market at Faurecia since more than 3 years. With the strategic role of bringing innovative and cost effective ideas to improve margins in our leather seating offer to OEMs.

I believe that my experience and personal skills, could match the requirements for a leading management position in purchasing, focused in the achievement of a successful procurement strategy, particularly in those aspects concerned with technical know how of leather making, innovations, trends, costs reduction and it’s best sourcing solutions.

I offer a suitable combination of knowledge and experience:

* Familiar with multinational management and their strategies.

* Extensive technical & practical industrial leather processing experience, by organizing working teams within different multicultural backgrounds, in several countries.

* Proven record of successful implementation of partnerships, key account management, cost effective business processes and innovations projects in previous assignments.

* Fluent in several languages (Spanish, English, French, German) with Mandarin, Italian and Portuguese knowledge. Very familiar with Chinese cutlture after living 7 years there.

* Excellent organizing and presentation skills, acquired during more than 27 years of international exposure.
