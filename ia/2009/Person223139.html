Stephen Chen’s Summary

Stephen Chen is a conscientious, well educated, purpose driven, risk taking, problem solving, highly motivated, disciplined, optimistic truth seeker. He values integrity, tact, intelligence, and persistence. Stephen is an extroverted introvert who’s insatiably sense of curiosity leads to new adventures and brilliant friends in his personal and professional life.

Stephen has been building companies that deliver value since the 3rd grade when he started distributing wholesale magazines. In the 17 years that have passed, he has faced some successes, many failures, and developed a clear sense of purpose in developing companies and attracting people that deliver outsized value.

He is currently the cofounder of GreenSoul, a branded social venture capital company, with its first portfolio company: GreenSoul Shoes. (www.greensoulshoes.org)

Stephen is the former Vice President of Business Strategy & Portfolio Analytics at Bear Stearns, where he helped launched and operated BearXplorer, an award-winning international hedge fund software company. They reached $6m in 16 months of operation with their dedicated team of investment professionals.

He holds a degree in Biology from Brown University.

His favorite quote?
Every morning in Africa, a gazelle wakes up.
It knows it must run faster than the fastest lion or it will be killed.
Every morning a lion wakes up.
It knows it must outrun the slowest gazelle or it will starve to death.
It doesn’t matter if you are a lion or a gazelle.
When the sun comes up, you better be running.
