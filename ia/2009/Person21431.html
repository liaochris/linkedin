About me:

I’ve been in Sales & Marketing for around 15 years with diversified industries ranging from Oil Marketing to FMCGs and from Hospitality to Telecom. During my career with Caltex Oil, Tapal Tea, Hashoo Group (Pearl Continental & Marriott Hotels) and Worldcall Group, I’ve gone through B2B selling, relationship management, sales promotions, trade marketing, retailing, merchandising, appointment of dealers, distributors and franchisees, product launches, sales planning, distribution, brand activation, trouble-shootings, town-storming activities, lucky draws, hiring of staff, trainings, motivational sessions and appraisals etc.

Why hire me as a trainer?

I’ve gone through all aspects of sales & marketing in my career, so I know where the shoe pinches and how to fix it.
I’m neither an entertainer nor a performer. I simply transfer skills that I learnt in my sales & marketing career.
