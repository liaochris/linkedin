  • CEO

    Popular Ford Sales Inc

    (Automotive industry)

    1983 — 2005 (22 years)

    Prior to joining Sayville Ford, Steve owned and operated Popular Ford sales & Bayridge Ford, in the New York metro area. As the Dealer he increased sales by 140% and became one of the largest dealers in the New York Region. Popular Ford also led the NYC zone for 16 straight years in CSI Scores winning multiple Distinguished Achievement Awards for Customer Satisfaction. In 2000 Popular Ford won Fords highest honor, The Presidents Award, which recognizes the top 10% of dealers nationwide that lead in CSI, market share and profitability. Popular was also recognized by Crains New York Business Magazine as one of NYC's top privately owned companys. Steve sold the dealerships in 2005.
