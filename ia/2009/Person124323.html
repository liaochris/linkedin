Jeff Stepanik’s Summary

In my 18 years in direct response, both on the client and agency sides of the business, I know how hard it can be to find the right hire with the right marketing experience – especially direct marketing experience. And, I've been frustrated in the past to not find just the right fit to fill a position, or wished I could hire two staffers, but only had the budget for one. jumpSTART fills that gap.

Jeff Stepanik’s Specialties:

jumpStart specializes in multi-channel, direct response media planning and placement thru: National, regional and local print media (magazine, card deck, FSI); direct mail; remnant cable and spot market TV; national, regional and local remnant radio; national and regional remnant newspaper; paid search; online site, affiliate, email and network media; alternate media (co-ops, inserts, shared mail, etc.)
