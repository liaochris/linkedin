About Greg Clowminzer
For the past 23 years Greg has been both a successful entrepreneur and passionate truth seeker. Today he dedicates his life to helping business owners grow their business, increase profits and most importantly have a rewarding personal life with lots of freedom.

Greg generally helps businesses with 5 or less employees get crystal clear about what needs to happen next in order to grow their business, increase profits and most importantly have a rewarding personal life with lots of freedom.

Greg Clowminzer also known as Zen Coach is a self-realized spiritual teacher, life success coach and publisher of Daily Wisdom online coaching tips. He’s been studying small business success, personal leadership, marketing, business development along with martial arts, meditation. shamanic healing arts, humanistic psychology, yoga, life coaching, and other spiritual and esoteric traditions from around the world for the last 20+ years — and by now should know to keep his mouth shut — but has too much information he wants to share with you in order to transform you into a total master of living a life of SUCCESS and TRUE HAPPINESS – one that you can operate effectively in a cool and sexy way in today’s modern world.

Greg is most passionate about leading Enlightenment Intensives a Zen-like meditation retreat where he teaches people the lost art of true-self reflection and the uncommon practice of authentic communication.

For speaking and coaching inquiries please call 760-930-9604.
