Mitchell Ashley’s Summary

My passions are creating businesses and innovative products, for customers and markets needing a unique approach to solving their business problems, all with highly talented people who are passionate about what they do. I've brought to market multiple new security, networking, multimedia and Internet products throughout my career for Fortune 500, SME, B2B and consumer markets.

My technology and market focus includes networking, security, virtualization, mobility, SaaS and user-centered product design. I've developed a reputation for effectively understanding customer and market needs, translating those into successful products, and delivering software product releases on time with high quality as part of an overall effective product organization.

I love to evangelize this work with analysts, media and customers, and I am fortunate to have appeared in industry publications including USAToday, NetworkWorld, ComputerWorld, InfoWorld, InformationWeek, CRN and others.

Previously I was CTO at StillSecure where I led the successful creation and market launch of multiple industry award winning security products. I also co-founded and led BoldTech Systems, and designed and implemented a national data broadband network at Jato Comm.

Through my consulting company, Converging Network LLC, I work with clients to develop successful product and strategies, create great products, and promotion through traditional and social media. I also served as interim CTO at a leading On Demand application performance management SaaS in Boulder, CO.

I regularly blog about my activities and topics of interest at The Converging Network, http://theconvergingnetwork.com, the Network World Microsoft Subnet blog, http://www.networkworld.com/community/ashley, and I co-host the highly subscribed to podcast "StillSecure After All These Years", http://www.ssaatypodcast.com, and the Network World Converging On Microsoft Podcast, http://www.networkworld.com/podcasts/com.
