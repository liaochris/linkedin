Sharon Palatnik Simoncini’s Summary

Sharon Palatnik Simoncini is an award-winning graphic designer with 25 years experience in creative visual communication for business.

She has designed and managed projects from concept through production for a wide range of clients. Projects include web and new media design, brochures, logos and corporate identity, press kits, advertising, invitations, forms, manuals and direct mail.

For the past 10 years, she has been the Chief Idea Officer for ZigZag Design, a NJ-based firm specializing in corporate identity and branding, collateral, print and web design for real estate, hospitality and business to business clients.
