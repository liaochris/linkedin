Belma Michael Johnson’s Summary

Belma Michael Johnson is a content creator for television, books, and new media, with a long history in the traditional media (newspapers, magazines, radio and other print media.)

He launched several social-networking ventures on Jan. 3, 2008, within his new, official site, www.BelmaMichael.com. The main site features the following social networks:

* the WorldWideWritingWiki -- a place where writers, illustrators and other artists collaborate to create books and other content online as a wiki community.

* Hollywood 2.0 -- where every major aspect of the entertainment industry is re-examined and re-imagined on a weekly in light of constantly emerging Web 2.0 applications and hardware.

* EasyGreenBook.com -- where other people concerned about the earth contribute ideas to a series of books about easy ways we can all do our part to preserve our planet.

Belma Michael Johnson's latest book, "Easy Green: The Absolute Easiest Ways to Preserve Your Earth" also debuts Jan. 3, 2008. His two-year promotional tour launched Jan. 5, 2008.

Belma Michael Johnson's next major goals include growing his social networking sites internationally and producing major motion pictures.
