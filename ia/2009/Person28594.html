Demonstrated abilities in planning, directing, developing, and implementing software and hardware systems to provide innovative information technology solutions. An effective team player with proven success in establishing strong links between diverse business and technology professionals in complex, global organizations.

Marketing and Product Planning:
Hands on project management skills including: tactical and strategic planning, system architecture design, engineering and testing for systems software development, internet messaging, database, mobile and web applications; strong cross-functional experience in production deployment, change management and quality assurance; practiced in budget planning, administration and resource management.

Product Management and Execution:
