Adam Casas’s Summary

My current focus is to seek a professional position to utilize my past experiences and high energy level to gain new knowledge in all fields of employment. My extreme motivation and drive reach and exceed expectations.

My experiences at REVShare include maintaining existing business relationships and developing new relationships that turned into profits for local and broadcast television stations, national networks, and syndicators across the United States, Canada, Puerto Rico, and the Virgin Islands. Utilizing the Cost-Per-Action method of business, we were able to supply our clients with short-form advertising and long-form programming used to fill un-sold inventory. Continuously building relationships with over 250 clients, I surpassed my sales goals by 50% percent from the previous year’s earnings and closed my first annual sales just over $1.2 million. I was also one of two representatives that managed 50% percent of all Spanish networks at REVShare and helped contribute to their revenue gains of over $2.8 million by monetizing their unsold inventory in less than 2 years.

Prior to REVShare, I was a part of a team that developed new business and maintained a profile of client prospects desiring to become home owners or wanted to refinance their homes. I assisted in over 200 client transactions for new home loans and refinancing loans. I worked with over 50 different lender affiliates and assisted homeowners in choosing the best option available for them.

In 2003, I was deployed with the United States Marine Corps to the Al Anbar Province, Iraq to fight in Operation Enduring Freedom and Operation Iraqi Freedom. I was part of a team of experts that delivered and maintained ammunition for ground forces and completed every mission with success. I completed my active contract with the Marine Corps in 2006 and currently serve in the Marine Corps Reserves.
