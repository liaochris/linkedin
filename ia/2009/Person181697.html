Patricia Kutza’s Summary

During the last decade this veteran writer has reported on the news, trends and personalities that populate the tech sector worldwide. Recently expanded her scope to include writing about the convergence of technology and hospitality sectors. Major clients include East Bay Agency for Children, University of California Haas School of Business, Comstock, Prosper, Acura, San Joaquin, Bay Area Kids, Valley Lifestyle and Solano magazines, Office.com, Upside Magazine, Tech Republic, ZDNetUK and Australia, Business Communications Co, Emerging Business Magazine, California Technology Magazine, Executive Traveler, ,and Journeys Magazines, Tourism-Review and TravelTalkRADIO.com.

Scope of writing genre includes: Features, white papers, news and views, company profiles, case histories, ghostwriting, copywriting, online media kits, advertorials and product reviews.
