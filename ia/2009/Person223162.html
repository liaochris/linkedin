Stephen Crocker’s Summary

LaBroche, Arzak, Bouley, Obauer’s, Tantris, Zinc, Cafe Pierre represent the best of food, wine and service. I have spent time in Crete, Tuscany, Austria, Germany, Basque country, Barcelona, Madrid, Southern France and Paris. I work or have worked in The Peninsula New York, The Pierre Hotel in Manhattan and The Occidental Miguel Angel Hotel in Madrid, premier destinations for the most discerning guests. I have profitably managed revenue up to 70 million dollars. I have worked and directed members of the most powerful labor union in the country, Local 6. I have a career of continued successes from position and rank to profit and loss to quality and style. Twelve years ago, my first culinary review earned three and a half out of five stars; five years ago I turned around a corporate restaurant to “Seafood Restaurant of the Year” and a place at the Taste of Washington D.C. with a signature dish of Seared Scallop and Sweetbreads.

Stephen Crocker’s Specialties:
