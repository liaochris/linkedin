Courtney Banks’s Summary

I am currently an undergraduate student at The Ohio State University studying Strategic Communications. I had absolutely no idea what I wanted to pursue with this degree until I started getting involved on campus. After organizing and executing the 2007 Homecoming Pep Rally I realized I really love the fast-paced environment of event planning. I then applied for the position of Concert Chair with The Ohio Union Activities Board. OUAB for short, is a board that uses student dollars to bring entertaining and informative artists, comedians and lecturers to Ohio State's campus. I have ABSOLUTELY no experience in the field on concerts but somehow I got the position and fell even more in love with planning events and marketing.

Why Do I Enjoy Event Planning:

Events are like puzzle pieces. You have things your client want, things you want and then all of the things in the middle that you need for your event to work. I like fitting these pieces together and seeing the end result, no matter how hectic that day may be.

What Do I Want To Do After College?

I really have no idea but at least my confusion is focused in the areas of event planning, public relations, image consulting and marketing. There are so many fields and careers that aren't taught in college that I want to learn about and explore. I really am up to try anything that touches on my passions and I am ready for a challenge in the working world.

My dream event would be to plan an award show, specifically the MTV Video Music Awards.
