Leo De Bock’s Summary

* 2008 - ...: Spokesman of the Minister of Justice (till the end of 2008: Jo Vandeurzen, CD&V, Christian Democrats, since then Stefaan De Clerck, CD&V). The Ministry of Justice is a Federal Administration.
* 2007: TV-host at Vitaya, hosting TV-debates about criminals, criminality, law and law inforcement issues
• 2005-2007 : Head of TV-Department Sylvester Productions (international tv production company)
• 2003-2005: Managing Director Kanakna Productions (international tv production company)
• 2002-2003: Head of the TV-News Department of VRT (Flemish Public Broadcaster); Member of the iDTV Research Group
• 2000-2002: Head of the second channel of VRT, Canvas, (profile comparable to BBC2) and Children’s Channel Ketnet
• 1999-2000: Assistant Managing Editor of Humo (number one weekly in Flanders, published by Mediaxis, now Sanoma)
• 1997-1999: Manager of the Documentary Department VRT
• 1987-1997: Scenario writer, reporter, documentary film maker (author and director), editor-in-chief of documentary programmes

Leo De Bock’s Specialties:

media consultancy, communication (acting as spokesperson), media HR, negotiation, purchasing, government liaison, branding and identity, journalism (print & tv)
