Chaz Berman’s Summary

Mr. Berman has conceived and built several successful consumer companies over the past three decades. Mr. Berman has been appointed to a board position with Atlantis Cyberspace a company in the virtual reality sector and as a result stepped down from his position on the Board of Directors of Optimate Networks, Inc a San Francisco Online Media company that revolutionized video advertising. He is concurrently the Manager at Nutak Consulting where he is forming new ventures. He was the Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer of United Consumer Benefits Network, Inc a membership and direct response loyalty marketing company, an internet loyalty rewards program. In addition he was the Founder & Chairman of UniPrivacy Inc dba DeleteNow.

Prior to his role at UCBN, Inc and previous to his founding UniPrivacy dba DeleteNow, Mr. Berman was Chief Operating Officer (COO) of MyPoints.com, Inc., where he was responsible for the Company's domestic operations. As COO, Mr. Berman initiated a major profit-focused restructuring of the Company ahead of its acquisition by United Airlines. From 1998 through 2000, Mr. Berman held a variety of senior executive posts of increasing responsibility. Mr. Berman built the Company's sales and client resources operations (comprising 135 professionals), growing annual revenue to $64 million in 2000 from zero two years earlier.

Before joining MyPoints.com, Mr. Berman was president of three high-growth consumer companies: Goodstuffs, Footprint Marketing and WineWrights. Earlier, Mr. Berman was EVP of Sales for Willitts Designs, a merchandising company acquired by Hallmark, Inc. At Willitts, Mr. Berman profitably grew annual sales to $32 million from $1.5 million and managed more than 200 sales professionals.
