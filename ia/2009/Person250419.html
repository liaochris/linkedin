Yves Peynaud’s Summary

Yves Peynaud, 50, has 20 years of expertise in Strategic Marketing, Product Marketing and International Business Development of technological companies. He helps entrepreneurs in developing and financing new businesses from innovative technologies and ideas.

Engineer graduated, Yves initially spent 5 years at hardware and software design and project management in the fields of real time industrial imaging, vectorial processing, and graphical and networks computers. Since 1988, he has taken various managing positions in product marketing, strategic marketing and international business development in French and Franco-American technology companies, as well as in the International World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) and in the INRIA Industrial Relations Department. Since end 1999, he has been working as a Consultant at helping entrepreneurs to rapidly get from technology-centric vision to market success.

Yves has developed over years a strong expertise and efficient methodology in new business planning and development, proven over a wide range of business models and industries, including: Internet, Software and Hardware design, Consumer Electronics, Telecommunications, Robotics and others.

So far, he has successfully accompanied 70+ companies in their market positioning, product marketing, goto market strategy, business planning and fund raising.

Yves Peynaud’s Specialties:

Yves has been a early adopter of Internet technologies as an email daily user in business practice since early 1986, and as responsible for European business development of the World Wide Web Consortium since 1995. He has developed in-depth business expertise and understanding in the Internet and Web industries.
