Mal Walker’s Summary

GreyHair Alchemy brings an exciting method of sourcing executive talent utilising interim managers.

An Interim manager is defined as a highly skilled, seasoned manager who is available for assignments of any duration, to either lend specialised expertise to a strategic project, or to fill a mission-critical skills gap.

As at January 2009, GreyHair has 800 interim managers ready for deployment throughout Australia and New Zealand and this is growing at around 25 a month.

We charge a flat fee to introduce the right person to meet your needs. This fee remains fixed, irrespective of the duration of the assignment or the level of the executive, from Project Manager to CEO or a Board position.

Many of our placements stay on as permanent members of the client's executive team.
