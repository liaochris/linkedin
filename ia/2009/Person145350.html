Luke Harvey-Palmer’s Summary

Luke Harvey-Palmer is a Corporate Trainer, Motivational Speaker and Coach to those who want to be rich and famous.

Luke has worked in Financial Services, Technology and Consulting - developing some of the strongest brands in the country, and using his own personal brand to network and develop his career. Luke has never applied for a job! As a husband and father of two; Luke’s commitment to health, exercise and the outdoors is an inspiration to his family and friends.

He is an enthusiastic authority on Personal Branding; particularly for those who depend on their reputation and profile for their success - this includes independent financial advisors, real estate sales professionals, business owners, consultants, recruitment professionals, and corporate sales teams. Anyone who sees themselves as the MD of ME can benefit from Luke’s expertise.

If you are sensitive to your target audience, and how they perceive you, professionally or personally, then Luke will help you to manage this perception, develop your key brand attributes, and make sure when your target audience thinks of you; they think powerful, clear and positive thoughts about you.

Luke is currently writing a book called "You're GREAT....but are you popular!. Staunch advocate of it is better to be known for who you are than what you are!.

"Regardless of age, regardless of position, regardless of the business we happen to be in, all of us need to understand the importance of branding. We are CEOs of our own companies: Me Inc. To be in business today, our most important job is to be head marketer for the brand called YOU! (Tom Peters)
