Chris J. Guerra’s Summary

Chris J. Guerra is the founder of Brand Orders. The core vision is to increase the efficiency of wholesale buying. It is an extremely antiquated process & involves too much paper, notes, faxing & frustrations.

Previously, Mr. Guerra served as the Vice-President of Business Development & Strategic Initiatives for Entertainment Media Works. Chris created and managed EMW's retail distribution channel and managed multiple aspects of StarStyle.com, a solution to buy the product featured in television, music and film. In addition Chris played key roles for 3 web based product launches:

TheLookBook- A b2b shopping destination for stylists & brands to connect.

StyleLogue- A social shopping community.

Plink- A tool to ask & identify the items within photos.

Prior to joining EMW,Chris opened four family retail locations In South Florida. He managed two stores and gained extensive knowledge in buying, merchandising, operations, and financial planning. Chris also held management roles at three Tommy Hilfiger stores.

Chris grew up across the country, exposed to the retail industry through family involvement. He holds a Bachelor in Business Administration and Marketing from Florida Atlantic University.
