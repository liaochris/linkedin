Pat Palleschi’s Summary

A Human Resources Executive who partners with Line Executives to acquire, develop and retain the talent needed for the business to be a success.

An accomplished manager of people and strong team player with other executives who report to the President of the Business.

Able to implement large HR system changes such as downsizing from 140,000 to 100,000 people( in one of the largest bank mergers in history) and rapid growth 12,000 - 15,000 with yearly hiring rate of 5,000 (entertainment).

Worked with the CEO and Presidents of the The Walt Disney Company to identify the 100 top high potential executives in this Company of 100,000 people.
Chaired the Department of Speech Communication at Loyola Marymount University.

Managed one of the first successful internal Organization Development Departments which charged internal clients for service at a top 10 bank.

Managed Budgets of $2 million (at Corporate Staff Level) to $25 million (during merger) and 10-250 staff, giving me flexibility and knowlege of how to get things done in a "guerrilla" or a highly polished corporate fashion.

Pat Palleschi’s Specialties:

Building & Implementing Streamlined and Effective HR systems to enable Business Succession areas such as Talent Acquisition, Talent Development, Executive Coaching;
Managing both small teams at the Corporate Level and Large Business Unit Teams in the Banking and Entertainment Industries; Mergers and Acquisitions; Planning and execution of Downsizing (Banking) and Rapid Growth (Entertainment); Dealing with colleagues across units; Building effective high level teams; Organization Development.
