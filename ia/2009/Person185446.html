Peter Cottingham’s Summary

With over 10 years Middle East experience I have acted as a lead designer and project manager on a wide range of International project types and my extensive experience encompasses Master planning, project management, urban design and landscape architecture.

Previously I have held the position of Associate Principle of
TVS International, Inc. acting as Director of Masterplanning and Urban Design for the Middle East Region. I was also formerly a Senior Associate with Cracknell Landscape Architects and have wealth of practical experience working on large scale mixed use, residential, commercial and leisure developments throughout the Middle East, Asia and Europe.

My primary strengths are landscape concept design
and vision masterplanning and I have developed a holistic approach to design that considers sustainability and responds to the constraints imposed by budget, constructability and timeframes.

I have considerable experience with developing landscape for podium structures and heritage projects and have worked closely with many large real estate developers designing new urban and commercial settlements for the UAE, Middle East and Far East.

Peter Cottingham’s Specialties:

Master planning, project management, urban design and landscape architecture, Sustainable development, value engineering, design and graphics.
