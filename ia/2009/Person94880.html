Hillary Bond’s Summary

I'm a recent graduate of Belmont University with a background in public relations and communications.

Diverse professional experiences have afforded me valuable communication and organizational skills. As the public relations assistant for Crye-Leike, Realtors, I created written content for the company’s internal newsletter, available to more than 3,000 employees.
