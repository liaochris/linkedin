Ricardo Viana Vargas, MSc, IPMA-B, PMP’s Summary

Ricardo Vargas is a project management specialist. He was responsible, in the past ten years, for more than 20 billion US dollars in areas as telecom, finance and energy.

He is a partner of Macrosolutions, a consulting company specialized in project, portfolio and risk management.

Ricardo is the author of nine books and one DVD. He has been published in Brazil, USA, UK and other coutries, with more than 125,000 books sold.

In 2005, Vargas was honored with the PMI Distinguished Award and also the PMI Product of the Year Award for his PMDome Workshop, considered the best solution for project management learning.

He is a PMI certified Project Management Professional (PMP), International Project Management Association Certified as IPMA-B. Vargas has a bachelor degree in Chemical Engineering and a Master degree in Production Engineering from UFMG. He has also a Master certificate in Project Management from George Washington University.

He was the first professional worldwide to be a Microsoft Certified Office User Specialist in MS Project 2002 and is an acknowledged reviser of the most important reference in the world concerning project management, the PMBOK Guide.

He is a professor in many MBA and graduation courses in Brazilian and American schools. He has presented several technical papers in Brazil, in the USA, in Europe and in Asia.

He is a member of the Project Management Institute (PMI) Board of Directors. He was elected in 2008 by the board members to serve as PMI Chair in 2009.

He is also member of the Association for Advancement of Cost Engineering (AACE), of the American Management Association (AMA), of the International Project Management Association (IPMA), of Global Association for Risk Professionals (GARP), of the Intitute for Global Ethics (IGE) and the Professional Risk Management Int. Association (PRMIA).

  • PMDome.com

    (Privately Held; Education Management industry)

    May 2006 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    The PMDome® was designed to meet all challenges faced by project management and leadership students. The workshop were developed by José Finnocchio, Danubio Borba and Ricardo Vargas. The main objective is to prove that we can do complex projects making use of simple things and best practices.

    The PMDome® workshop is "hands on" training of Project Management where a group of participants (12 to 44 students) are divided in three teams that compete to each other to demonstrate superior performance in Project Management Skills in accordance with requirements stated in SOW provided by a fictitious customer.

    With more than 1,500 students, PMDome is the state-of-art in Project Management training, being honored with the PMI Award in 2005 as the best training solution worldwide for project management learning.

    Ricardo Vargas deliveries PMDome worhshop worldwide for diferent kind of customers and educational organizations. For more information about PMDome visit www.pmdome.com.
