MANAGER SERVICES INTERNATIONAL is a combination of M4M International and Manager Services.

Meetings are organised in each city location where we're active. These off-line meetings offers business people to build new business contacts during the M4M "Greet & Meet" formula.
Recently w're using 4 online networkplatforms to give our members the change to keep in touch after a meeting.
