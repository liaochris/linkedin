Andrew Scott’s Summary

Andrew is currently CEO of Rummble, a mobile technology company. Rummble is like a travel guide that all your friends write for you. Rummble works anywhere.

Rummble makes it easy to find personalized trusted content relevant to your location, at home or on the move.

A serial entrepreneur, Andrew ran a successful web development company through the dot com years serving blue chip clients, which sold to CMG plc in 2001 where he developed the world’s first online digital video news archive (BritishPathe) with over 3500 hours of video and 13 million+ images. Andrew later founded a ground breaking interactive personal fitness website (InTraining) with Olympian Dave Morgan, build the UK’s first interactive SMS flirting service (DinnerDateAuctions) and pioneered Europe’s first location based social software (playtxt).

Andrew also sits on the board of UnLtdWorld.com, an online platform which connects and empowers social entrepreneurs.

As an authority in the field of mobile and online communities, Andrew speaks at industry events and contributes to trade press.

Andrew Scott’s Specialties:

Entrepreneur, social media, web 2.0, web 3.0, business development, strategy, mobile technologies, social networks (MOSOSO), location based services (lbs), marketing, concept architecture, system architecture, usability, UI design, UGC, BPR, CRM.
