David James’s Summary

Recently chosen by Barron's (February, 2009) as one of the nation's top wealth advisors, David has 20 years + experience in all aspects of wealth management.

His private wealth advisory team (The Paragon Group of Oppenheimer) provides comprehensive, tax efficient, multi-generational wealth planning services for a limited number of clients. His clients are highly successful individuals, often entrepreneurs who have experienced a liquidity event, usually through M&A activity or a private sale.

Backed by a 25 person due diligence group providing research in New York, David's clients have access to a full array of world class investment strategies, including top hedge fund and private equity managers.
