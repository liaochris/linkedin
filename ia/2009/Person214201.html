Seth Miller’s Summary

I am a specialist in managing consumer service and product businesses. I have managed turnarounds, large scale process reengineering projects and taught companies to compete with speed.

I have helped grow companies through both organic growth and through acquisition/roll-up. I have unwound and repositioned companies whose growth strategies or mergers have gone bad. In the process, I have become very familiar with what works, and what doesn't.

I have been deeply involved in the software, marketing, operations, information systems, real estate, inventory and finance functions for businesses in the internet, catalog and retail sales channels.

I like to manage businesses where high quality and fast delivery significantly impact the company's value proposition. I am deeply committed to delivering the best products and service my company can muster. That makes me a demanding leader...always seeking to raise the bar with new ideas. But, I also believe in turning the spotlight on my people to help celebrate their successes.

By personality, I am an arranger, a connector, futuristic and visionary, a learner and analytic. I love managing all of the variables in complex situations, figuring out how all the pieces fit together, and determining the most productive configuration possible. I also enjoy connecting people and ideas to create win/win situations. I love to learn and am drawn to the process of learning. I like to spend time understanding unrelated businesses or ideas because their study might come in handy later in a seemingly unrelated business.

My past employers have represented such complex multi-unit brands as Blockbuster Video, Casual Corner, LensCrafters, Easy Spirit, Castle Dental, Boston Proper, Charles Keath and Mark, Fore & Strike.

My education includes a BA from Tufts University and an MBA with concentration in Consumer Marketing and Service Management from Harvard Business School.
