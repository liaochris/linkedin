Mark Coble’s Summary

I'm a real estate investor/entrepreneur and work with a network of investors. we buy homes from people just like you that are experiencing challenges with their homes.

My company is the perfect solution for people that are dealing with job loss, behind on payments, divorce, making 2 mortgage payments, facing foreclosure, tired of being landlords.

If you or someone that you know is experiencing any real estate problem or NEED to sell you home, we can help. Our real estate group has closed over 450 transactions and negotiate short sales for Realtors & Lenders to help them turn their dead leads into profits!.

Our goal is to help as many homeowners solve the current mortgage issues facing millions of families in the United States and to help them have peace once again.

Mark Coble’s Specialties:

We have a business model that allows for our company to make a CASH OFFER directly to the owner's lender and negotiate and market the property using 3 different exit strategies. This is absolutely the BEST option for people that need to sell their home quickly because they not only have an offer to purchase from us, but we'll sell it down the road to a new buyer looking for a great home!
