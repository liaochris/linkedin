Ken May’s Summary

I've been in the financial planning industry for 30 years and still enjoy a good challenge. I've built and sold a successful accounting firm and am in the process of expanding the services of my financial planning company, The Small Business Doctor, Inc.

Primary services include:

* College Planning - Working with families to make college funding more affordable

* Financial Planning for businesses and individuals

* Retirement Planning and IRA Rollovers

* Investments - Securities licensed - Securities and Investment advice offered through Capital Financial Services, Inc. Broker/Dealer, Investment Advisor, Member SIPC/FINRA

* Long-Term Care Planning

* Insurance - Health, Life, Disability & Long Term Care

On a more personal note, I enjoy meeting new people and building long-term relationships based on truth and honesty. I work with clients from all walks of life; using layman's terms so we can effectively work together based on each person's level of financial understanding.

Blue collar or White collar, nothing pleases me more than helping someone work their way out of a tough situation. I create solutions that help people put their financial house in order. To me, that seems like a worthwhile thing to do, and I've dedicated my professional life to that single concept.
