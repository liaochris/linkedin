Peter Scaturro’s Summary

I am an independent music and audio producer with more than 20 years of experience in a variety of audio production roles. As the owner of The Sweet Spot, a post production audio studio and music resource in Venice Beach, I am the lead creative. I also oversee all aspects of production, project management, and day-to-day business operations. I provide audio content for local and national advertising companies, marketing and television production companies. I have created original scores and sound design for television shows, commercials, business communication projects, film trailers and production libraries. My current clients include several companies in the video game industry including Microsoft, Sony, Electronic Arts, Maxis, Pandemic, Bioware, and Mythic.

Recent Work:

Managed and supplied audio packages for several high profile presentations at E3 2009 for Xbox and Electronics Arts.

Co-wrote two songs with Guns N' Roses for the album Chinese Democracy. One of those songs, Shackler’s Revenge, is featured in the hit video game Rock Band 2.

Collaborated with Bootsy Collins on the "Woods Of Wisdom" ad campaign for Gatorade featuring Tiger Woods and Samuel L. Jackson.

Provided audio processing and implementation for the video game Red Alert 3 and the expansion pack RA3:Uprising.

Peter Scaturro’s Specialties:

Working with creative people and coming through in the clutch.
