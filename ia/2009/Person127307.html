Kate McMillan’s Summary

I specialize in unique, clean, goal-oriented user-experiences, website designs and visual identities.

• As a user experience designer, I create strategies and innovative solutions that delight users.

• As a web designer I build custom sites that both look and work great. They are optimized for search engines, and designed specifically to make each web presence meet its goals.

• As a visual designer, I create assets that clearly communicate visual identity & brand, and help launch new ideas.

***

I've been in the business of design for 10 years. These days it's harder than ever to find good, personal service. I strive to maintain a level of care, timeliness and communication that is unsurpassed. My goal is to be the kind of designer that you would gladly refer to your friends.
