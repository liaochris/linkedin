Christopher Stimpson’s Summary

Results oriented and profitability minded efforts underscore my broad and deep experience in project management, project controls, and systems implementations for very large transportation and industrial projects or programs ranging from $160 Million to over $4 Billion, saving customers millions of dollars and up to 23% shorter project life cycles.

Customers/employers have received creative and solid solutions to their project and program needs. My customers are Fortune 500 companies, state agencies, and smaller specialty shops. Significant savings were delivered through process improvement, streamlined systems, effective controls methods, persuasion and mentoring.

I provide enthusiasm and leadership for others to engage and take ownership in their work. All of my projects, programs, and customers have attained improvements and success in this regard. Public speaking, cost management, performance measurement and accountability have always been my strengths.
