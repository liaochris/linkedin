Robert Goforth’s Summary

I have managed large sales organizations and sold staffing and health care services. I managed hotels and a large athletic club. I developed web sites and sold IT services. I also designed and developed compliance software for the health care industry and helped design, patent and market two products.

I have developed and implemented promotions for large corporations and small organizations. I have designed marketing materials and produced television commercials. I started two companies and worked with several start-ups.

I am currently a Marketing Consultant with Accolades Public Relations. I also work with other organizations to build brand loyalty, generate sales and when possible give back to the community.

It is a personal goal to provide assistance to the men and women that sacrifice so much to provide and protect the freedom and quality of life many of us take for granted. When possible I incorporate support for non-profit organizations into our marketing campaigns.

Military personnel, teachers, law enforcement officers, fire fighters and ordinary citizens that make an effort to make a difference are the real heroes among us; the Great Americans that deserve our attention and appreciation.

If you or your company would like to get involved please send your comments to rgoforth@AmericansCare.us.
