Ian Turk’s Summary

I am a marketing professional with 13 years experience. During this time I have gained active knowledge of all aspects of marketing including strategy, research, analysis, pricing, product and brand management, communications, and web marketing. I have a track record of successful planning and executing marketing campaigns and activities on often a very limited budget. I has been awarded the Chartered Institute of Marketing Postgraduate Diploma in Marketing and I am an Economics graduate.

I started marketing difference because I believe the difference between a successful, dynamic business and your competitors, is good marketing. Effective marketing does not need to cost a fortune and I can advise on many low cost solutions that can have a dramatic impact on sales and profits.

Ian Turk’s Specialties:

Product life cycle management; Brand management; Pricing Strategy; Analysis using SQL and Excel; Marketing Strategy; Marketing communications including direct mail, email marketing, advertising, brochures, catalogues, events & PR. New product development with gap analysis and market research. New product launch including presentations and training.
