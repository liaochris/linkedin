Aaron Cannata’s Summary

Aaron Cannata brings over a decade of experience in broadcast, commercials and live television.

He is an accomplished producer, director and camera operator. Aaron won a national 2006 Sports Emmy Award for his camera work on a game show that aired on Fox Sports Net.

Full-time employment includes CBS, Viacom, Clear Channel Television, Westinghouse, United Television and Landmark Communications.

As an independent/freelancer he has worked for NBC, ESPN, Fox Sports, Versus (née OLN), Speed Channel, A&E Network, specializing in live telecasts of entertainment, sports, motor racing, music concerts, reality, episodic and game shows.

As a producer, Aaron has written, directed and shot hundreds of commercials. Aaron lived for two years in Guatemala, is fluent in Spanish and has traveled extensively in Europe. With a current passport, he is available for travel.

Aaron has a yellow card with IATSE Local 720 and can be letter-of-requested.

Aaron received his Bachelor of Arts in Communications with an emphasis in Broadcast Television from Brigham Young University.
