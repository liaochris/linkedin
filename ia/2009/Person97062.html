Ian Duggan’s Summary

The Covestic mission is to ‘provide practical services and solutions that optimize the value that technology can bring to business. Our people are business savvy, technologically informed, and operationally disciplined. We excel at what we do, and are vested in the success of our clients and employees.’

In today's fast-paced environment, optimizing business and operational efficiency is essential to remain competitive. That's why we're delivering practical services and solutions that optimize the value that technology brings to business—blending business and technology consulting with day-to-day operations smarts. Our people put their business savvy, technology knowledge, and operational excellence to work for you.

We only succeed when you succeed. So, in every engagement, we dig in and invest the time to clearly understand your business objectives and metrics for success. Together, we work with you to implement solutions that achieve those objectives and maximize return on investment. We "co-invest" with you, creating a true partnership that allows you to realize higher levels of service and stronger results than with other consulting companies.

At the engagement level, we employ a step-by-step approach beginning with a detailed assessment of your business challenge. Structured solutions are then provided with well-defined deliverables and a clear roadmap of activities to achieve desired results. Our commitment to your success extends beyond project completion. We provide a smooth process for transition and regular check-ups to ensure that the solution continues to deliver value for your business over time.

We are fully vested in building long-term, successful relationships with our clients and our employees. To this end, we are committed to setting a standard of excellence that is second to none. That's what keeps our clients coming back, and keeps our team together long-term.
