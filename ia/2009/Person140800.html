Lidiya Kaplun’s Summary

Since starting her tenure in Information & Technology Consulting and Recruitment in 2005, Lidiya has gained the ability to find a niche area of the industry that is making a massive change Nationally and on the Global scale in the areas of Business Intelligence & Data Warehousing. In a time where the economy is shifting based on international trends, Lidiya has identifed that Business Intelligence and Data Warehouse functions will only grow stronger and increase in demand within an Organisation with a drive for Management to make better business decisions.

Lidiya's specialist areas of recruitment range from Information Analysts, Management Information Systems Specialists, Business Systems Analysts, ETL Developers, Production and Development Database Administrators, Data Migration specialists right through to Senior Level Appointments around Consulting Pre and Post Sales, Solutions Architects, Technical Systems Architects and Business Intelligence and Data Warehouse Managers, to name a few. Lidiya also has a keen interest in the developing areas of Anti Money Laundering (AML) and Actuarial/ Statisticians. The toolsets that Lidiya has recruited for and has an indepth functional understanding of ranges anything from Cognos, Business Objects, Crystal, Hyperion, Oracle Warehouse Builder (OWB), SAS, Brio, Microsoft BI Reporting, Analysis and Integration Services.

Lidiya has an indepth understanding of specfic market trends specifically in Queensland (QLD) and Brisbane more so and is keen to keep up to date with current and upcoming projects in the area. Lidiya is always willing to give her feedback and updates to all who are interested to learn more about what is happening where and aims to keep ahead of the market and share her knowledge with industry experts.

Lidiya also has a keen interest in helping those who are looking to relocate interstate or from overseas and continues to build on a strong network of resources in BI / DW across the Country.
