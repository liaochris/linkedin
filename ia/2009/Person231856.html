Theresa Jennings (Mesa)’s Summary

"Opportunity paged me, beeped me, linked me, e-mailed me, faxed me, and spammed me. But I was expecting it to knock." From a Toastmaster magazine cartoon.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Experienced graphic designer and fine artist for over 35 years. Production artist, typesetter, indexer, proofreader/copyeditor, book design and formatting, illustrator.

Fine Arts: watercolor portraiture. Work has been in national juried art shows and has won some awards. Considering I haven't entered that many pieces, this is quite gratifying.

I'd like to teach and am working on completing my degree in art. I'd also like to do more illustrating, and have stopped taking on as much web design work in order to concentrate on print and illustration.

Theresa Jennings (Mesa)’s Specialties:

Graphic design with a marketing mindset. Logo design, letterhead systems, brochure, annual report, POP, programs, advertisements, branding and identity.
