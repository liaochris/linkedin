peter allan mariano’s Summary

I am a Manufacturing Engineer by profession but I also function as a Technical and Creative Writer, a Graphic Artist and a Technical Designer. I do have a passion for writing and graphical design, which I developed from my school days as a certified writer and editor for my school’s official publications. I have applied these skills in making excellent Technical Reports, Position Papers, White Papers and Project Proposals, which solicited a lot of praises from management and customers alike. In parallel with my technical job, I am currently working as a Freelance Writer for several clients and have written articles on several non-related subjects according to the requirements of my customer. In addition, I have written other articles and compilations, screenplays (one of which was short listed by a major film outfit here in the Philippines), an unpublished novel, poems and songs. I am continuing my journey into the world of Internet writing and am making my online presence felt through blogs, articles as well as writing for online companies.

My more than 15 years experience in the Semiconductor, Computer and Electronics manufacturing industries as a Senior Engineer and Manager have provided me with an extensive exposure and training to relate and deal with the various segments of the workforce from top to bottom. This process taught me the value of DIVERSITY and the importance of TEAM EFFORT.

Utilizing diversity and using team effort to push towards our goal is very effective, not only in completing our tasks with excellence, but in developing and molding each individual in the team towards the best that they can be.
