Annie Murrell’s Summary

Therapist: Licensed Professional Counselor for adults, teens, and couples. Ongoing interest in academic work, writing and research. CEU courses on the business of private practice and on Mindfulness-based Cognitive Behavioral therapies. Licensed LPC Supervisor.

Consultant: Since 2002, "Business Therapy" consultancy for small businesses, churches, and non-profits. Marketing and development consultant for individual private practitioners.

Community Organizer: In 2007 organized Tulsa think-tank style conference on issues of ecology/the environment, spirituality, inclusion, sacred activism, politics, social and civic issues. 750 attendees. Keynote: Robert F. Kennedy, Jr.

Certified Rolfer (TM) (retired): Rolf Structural Integration is a manual processing of the fascial layer within the human body. Rolfing(R) has echoes of Osteopathy, massage, cranial-sacral, and energetics yet is an organized taxonomy of its own, based on the theory research of Ida P. Rolf, PhD. Also used with equines, felines and canines.

Annie Murrell’s Specialties:

Psychology: Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Positive Psychology, Buddhist Psychotherapy, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Mindfulness, Somatic, Integral, Trauma, Addictions, Spirituality/Religion. LGBTQ-affirmative.

Business: "Business Therapy", Personnel Management, Project Management, Operational and Organizational Improvements, Eco-office Solutions/Sustainability. Special interest in greening the workplace and greening our events.
