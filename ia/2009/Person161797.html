Michael Melnick’s Summary

I am a creator of brand identities.

I have over nine years of experience in the branding Industry, designing packaging for consumer goods, creating brand and corporate identity systems, building online interactive experiences and marketing communication on multiple media platforms.

My portfolio includes creative planning and design for clients such as Bed Bath & Beyond, Payless Shoe Source, Jordache, IMG Fashionweek, Curvation, SkinMedica, Maoz Vegetarian, IRO, and non-profit organizations such as The Israel Film Festival, Peace Now and the International Institute for Counter-Terrorism.

I graduated from the Design Management masters program at Pratt Institute with highest academic distinction. In addition, I have a BFA in graphic design from Vital - Tel Aviv Center for Design Studies (Israel); and a bachelor’s degree in behavioral sciences from Ben Gurion University (Israel).

As a professional designer who understands the complexities of business strategy and human behavior, I believe in a multidisciplinary approach to problem solving that aims at balancing the human needs, the business objectives, and marketplace constraints.

Michael Melnick’s Specialties:
