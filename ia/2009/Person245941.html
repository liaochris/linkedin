Wayne Weil’s Summary

Wayne is a Strategic Sourcing consultant and experienced executive with expertise in sourcing strategy; IT sourcing (enterprise software licensing, implementation consulting, IT outsourcing, application development/application maintenance (AD/AM) outsourcing, Software as a Service); business process outsourcing (BPO); and offshoring.

Wayne has a demonstrated track record of delivering value to leading financial services firms, both as a consultant and executive. He has led large, complex sourcing engagements and managed project teams encompassing multiple stakeholders (front office, operations, IT, legal). An experienced contract negotiator with strong financial analysis skills, Wayne prides himself on crafting win-win agreements with innovative deal structures that control costs, mitigate risk and exceed client expectations.

A thought leader and speaker on sourcing/outsouring topics, Wayne applies leading-edge technologies, thorough analysis and rigorous process to a wide variety of sourcing challenges.

Wayne Weil’s Specialties:

Strategic sourcing, outsourcing, offshoring, enterprise software licensing, contract negotiation, strategic planning, financial analysis, professional services sourcing.
