Lynn Williams’s Experience

  • Regional Grassroots Organizer

    National Association of Community Health Centers

    (Political Organization industry)

    February 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    I work with state Primary Care Associations, their members and local Board and staff members at Community Health Centers, developing interest and involvement in the federal issues that affect their business. My hope is to empower them to communicate effectively with their elected officials and increase advocacy in the eight SE states I cover (TN, NC, SC, FL, GA, AL, MS, KY). I provide training in media and voter registration outreach at health centers, their national and state conferences, and via www.

  • Owner and business consultant

    Lynn Williams Communications

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    1987 — Present (22 years)

    Marketing and media consultant, educational trainer and facilitator for municipal, university, non-profit and small business clients. Expertise in message development and targeted delivery. Award-winning journalist, former radio sales manager, talk show producer and host.

  • District 34 Council Representative

    Metro Nashville/Davidson County Government

    (Government Administration industry)

    August 1999 — August 2007 (8 years 1 month)

    One of 40 members of Nashville's governing body elected to represent 18,000 constituents, I served two terms until term limited in the largest non-partisan city council in the country. I sponsored legislation creating Educational Access Television in our city, amended zoning regulations to restrict higher density construction more than any other district in the city, and preserved one historical neighborhood with a protective overlay in spite of contentious special interest opposition.
