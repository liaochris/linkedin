Miguel Briceno’s Summary

Equipped with an M.B.A. and over 20 years of progressively responsible experience in the areas of procurement, strategic planning, project leadership, and technical/operations management, I am well suited to join your organization in a related role. Throughout my career, I have displayed a firm technical skill set and have specialized in leveraging business strengths, discovering new sourcing opportunities, and spearheading successful negotiations for multi-national corporations. Furthermore, I have shown the ability to forge strong and trustworthy relationships worldwide via excellent communication, interpersonal, and bilingual skills. Moreover, I have exhibited proficiency in such areas as budgeting, automation, team facilitation, financial analysis, and sourcing methodology.



During my extensive tenure with Cemex Inc., one of the three largest building materials companies in the world, I was a key player in supporting company growth from $6B to $22B and held such titles as Senior Negotiator-Global Sourcing; Operations Planning Director; Plants Project Manager; Systems Project Manager; and Distribution Planning Engineer. Selected highlights included the following:



• Conducted global agreements with annual spend of $320M, resulting in average annual savings of $32M in diverse product categories, which included refractories, grinding media, international logistics, IT hardware, IT software, telecommunications, courier services, travel expenses, pumping equipment, and trucks.

• Pioneered the utilization of advanced sourcing tools, which increased savings from 9% of total spend to 17%, in complex logistics negotiations.

• Developed optimization models for the annual replacement of mobile equipment worldwide, resulting in accurate forecasts of business needs that reduced delivery times 10%. Achieved 20% savings by implementing testing trials of materials from low-cost countries.
