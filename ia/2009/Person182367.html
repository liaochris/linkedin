Patty Gale’s Summary

Hi! My name is Patty Gale and welcome to my Linkedin part of the web.

I am a 40-something baby boomer who turned in my heels, suits and briefcase back in 2001 to start my own business from the 'corner office' in my home. (It really is in the corner of the house, too!)

I come from a very large New York Italian family and while we are very passionate about our food, we are also passionate about our relationships.

I think this is why I have embraced the internet and most recently, social networking.

My favorite motto is "Let go of the past and keep moving forward." Seriously, we all have 24 hours a day and 7 days in a week. How are you spending your time?

You know, regardless of what industry you're in or what you do to pay the bills, when it's all said and done, the only thing that really matters is the difference we make in the lives of others.

I truly believe that when you embrace the potential of the Internet combined with the power of appreciation, you create a dynamic combination in which your business will soar and will make a real difference in the lives of your customers and everyone you meet.

I am married to my best friend, Doug, and I am the proud mom of three great kids and a golden retriever who is 2 vocal chords shy of actually talking.

Sean and Matt are proudly serving our country in the U.S. Army, and Emily is mostly into Hannah Montana and Veggie Tales.

I love partnering with businesses and people who 'get it.'

If you'd like to connect, just drop me a note and let me know!
