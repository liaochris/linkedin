  • Editor-in-Chief, Airsoft USA Magazine

    Global Entity Media, Inc.

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    January 2007 — July 2008 (1 year 7 months)

    Chosen to help get a new Airsoft-based publication off the ground from the people behind one of the country's top Paintball mags, Paintball Sports Magazine (PSM). Airsoft USA Magazine (AUSAM) is the country's first legitimately nationally published magazine based solely on Airsoft.

    Airsoft is a tactical, team-based sport, similar to Paintball, but with a higher level of realism-- from authentic looking weaponry and uniforms, to play styles and scenarios reminiscent of Black Hawk Down and the like. It's barely 20 years old, came from Asia, moved in a wave from the West to East coast.

    Involved a large amount of team building and management, in addition to advertiser/client relationship management when it came to big names in the rapidly growing Airsoft and Paintball industry. The editorial side was chosing what to feature in the magazine and editing the content as it comes to me, but getting the articles from idea to hard copy was the challenge, and an enjoyable one at that.
