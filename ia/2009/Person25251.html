Bryan deSilva’s Summary

Senior Consultant with Improvizations will bring strong communication, project management and implementation background to a project with a forward-looking company. Skilled at building strong team environments, with a passion for doing the right thing while getting things done. The perfect scenario would include the project management of a large, global ERP implementation with difficult to achieve deadlines and complex data and systems requirements. The perfect client is not just interested in tomorrow or next week, but one that nurtures its teams to work outside the box, using tools and training to achieve the seemingly unachievable.

Twenty years of project management, systems and applications development experience with current focus on PeopleSoft, SAP, Oracle, EV3, Kronos & eTime systems implementation. Solid functional and technical skills in all areas of ERP including significant education and work in Human Resources, Payroll, Finance, and Benefits Administration. A large base of high profile, extremely satisfied clients.
