Wade Eck’s Summary

In 1999 I incorporated my own company, offering scaleable, efficient e-business environments, customer service applications and dynamic marketing analytics. My credentials include developing complete online solutions and MS SQL databases for the City of Los Angeles, Lieberman Research Worldwide (www.LRWonline.com), The L.A. Office (www.LAoffice.com), Lavazza Coffee (www.AromaCafeCulture.com), CBS' & Showtime's anchor / reporter: Karyn Bryant (www.KarynBryant.com), NBC's American Gladiator - Panther: Corinne van Ryck de Groot (www.SexyPanther.com) and Tadashi (www.TadashiCollection.com), just to name a few.

After earning an architectural engineering degree, I began my career working several years for Prudential Lighting, a prominent lighting manufacturer. I provided optical and aesthetic design for their premium luminaires, many of which are used in the J. Paul Getty Museum in Los Angeles. In addition I programmed custom software applications and sales tools, as well as conducted technical training seminars for all of the sales personnel.

Please review some of my work, I think you'll see I have an adept understanding of numerous programming languages, Flash animation, online video and 3D modeling. I believe this skill set has lead to my continued success and achievements using new-media.

If you're in need of an online solution, please give me a call. I can answer your questions, provide an estimate, or just point you in the right direction.
