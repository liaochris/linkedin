Fred Thiel’s Summary

Fred Thiel has over 20 years experience managing software and technology companies for rapid growth in both revenue and shareholder value. Prior to joining Triton Pacific, he was CEO of several companies including GameSpy Industries, a leader in on-line media where he dramatically grew revenues and then sold the company to a strategic acquirer at industry leading multiples in under twelve months and Lantronix, a leader in device networking and systems management technology where he repositioned the company, tripled revenues in four years, acquired and integrated four companies and successfully raised over $120 million through an IPO and follow-on secondary offering. Fred attended the Stockholm School of Economics and is fluent in English, Spanish, Swedish and French.

Fred sits on a number of private and not-for-profit boards of directors and is a current member and chapter chairman of the YPO (Young Presidents Organisation) California Coast chapter.

Follow me on Twitter: http://twitter.com/fgthiel

Fred Thiel’s Specialties:

Mergers and acquisitons (M&A), leveraged buy-outs (LBO), managment buy-outs (MBO), growth capital investments, start-ups and turn-arounds, all stages of funding, outsourced product development, corporate strategy, software as a service, business process outsourcing, board of directors advisory, exit strategies, product line rationalization, building management teams, and product marketing strategy.
