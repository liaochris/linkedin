    Our Story!
    Let’s Do Tea was conceived by Meredith Tieszen in November, 2002 while flying for a major airline. After talking the idea over with her mom and partner, Jerry Moore, they decided this was where God was leading them. Let’s Do Tea helps open doors for hospitality and sharing health facts while enjoying a cup of tea.
    The past few years have been blessed with on going growth and friendships that would not have been possible without God’s touch. He has brought us more then we could have hoped for and continues to bless us every day. We feel fortunate to work with such wonderful people and hear their stories.

    Thank you Lord, for your amazing gift and grace!

    Let's Do Tea Mission Statement:

    Our goal is to bring good quality tea and tea related products to the home through an atmosphere that creates new and lasting friendships. In a society filled with chaos and uncertainty, LDT strives to encourage people to share hospitality and kindness with each other so that they may be fulfilled in life's everyday routines. Bringing people together to savor a warm cup of tea, to bond in kindred spirits, to feel the comfort of the things God provides...this is our ultimate mission.

    1 Peter 4:9 "Use hospitality one to another without grudging."
