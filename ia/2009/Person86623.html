Gilles Clavel’s Summary

I have a proven record of success in structuring businesses and developing startup companies.
Since 1967, I have created, organized and managed various organizations in several countries.
I am not only familiar with small business operations, but also have an excellent professional track record within large companies (e.g. IBM, Siemens and Deutsche Telekom).
I speak French, English, German and Tunisian Arabic.
Through my present activity at a.g.Cube, I have a good understanding of the US business environment. Thanks to my previous experience, I also have a strong knowledge of the business environment and the social background in North Africa (Algeria, Tunisia, and Morocco) and more generally in the Arab world.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry)

  • Director, e-technologies Business Unit

    T-Systems, France

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; DT; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1998 — September 2002 (4 years 9 months)

    I directed the Competence Centre for e-technologies (object oriented software engineering and web-based information systems).
    From January 1998 to September 2002, the number of staff in my department (consultants, sales persons) increased from 25 to 100, and sales rose from €2.5 million to €11.2 million.

  • Founder and CEO

    IMA-informatique

    (Computer Software industry)

    January 1995 — December 1997 (3 years)

    Creation and development of IMA–informatique, a company that specialized in object-oriented software engineering and Internet technologies. IMA–informatique was a company whose economic model was based on profitable growth. In 3 years, staff numbers grew from 4 to 25, turnover increased from 0.3 to 1.5 M€ and each trading year produced a positive result.
    IMA–informatique had significant partnerships with IBM, Sun Microsystems and Borland and was one of the leading expert companies on object-oriented software engineering and web based systems. The success of the business model led to the acquisition of IMA–informatique by T-Systems, one of the companies of the Deutsche Telekom Group. IMA–informatique thus became the Competence Center for new technologies of T-Systems France.
