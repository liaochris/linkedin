Tina Kisela’s Summary

General Manager of The Promenade Shops at Saucon Valley – a 475,000 square foot outdoor lifestyle shopping center located in the Lehigh Valley, PA market just off I-78 between Allentown and Bethlehem. This shopping center, which features over 50 stores, 10 distinctive restaurants and a state of the art movie theatre - was developed and is managed by Poag & McEwen Lifestyle Centers LLC of Memphis TN. Responsible for the Grand Opening and operations since The Promenade Shops opened for business on October 27, 2006.

Responsibilites include overseeing day-to-day property management including overall operations, maintenance, security, specialty leasing, marketing and merchant relations.

Has an extensive background in the shopping center industry and experience working for two privately held management companies and two publicly-traded Real Estate Investment Trust companies. Prior to the appointment at The Promenade Shops, spent 22 years at several malls in the Lehigh Valley region including Lehigh Valley Mall, South Mall and Phillipsburg Mall.

Holds a bachelor's degree with a dual major in business administration and accounting from Muhlenberg College and is a PA-licensed Real Estate Sales Person. Also holds Certified Shopping Center Management and Certified Marketing Director designations through the International Council of Shopping Centers (ICSC). Serves as a faculty member for ICSC's school for professional development.
