linda somma’s Summary

IRONIC is a free-lance consultancy offering TV/film/events production management services in all areas of the entertainment industry. In addition, IRONIC provides ancillary communications, promotions & marketing services.

IRONIC was founded in Rome, Italy in 2003 by Linda M. Somma, a professional with numerous years of experience in the promotion and management of artistic and cultural projects in the entertainment industry. In December 2007, IRONIC “relocated” to the Boston area.

Linda M. Somma, born and educated in the USA, has a degree in Marketing & International Public Relations, with a concentration in Italian & Spanish. Over the past 20 years she has developed extensive experience in the communication & marketing of artists (architects, industrial designers, graphic designers, actors, directors, etc.). In 1992, after having worked for 6 years in the US, she moved to Milan, Italy and upon her arrival, worked in various professional design studios for over 7 years as a Strategic Marketing Consultant. In addition she taught "Professional Organization" courses in several design schools. In 1999 she moved to Rome where she initially collaborated for 2 years with a multi-national start-up as a Corporate Communications Manager, directly managing all communications activities in 3 countries. From 2002 she began her career in the entertainment industry working for 1 year as a talent agent. In 2003 she began IRONIC, a free-lance consultancy which operates in the entertainment industry. In addition she translates theatrical and film scripts and often acts as an interpreter. Her native language is English and she has native fluency in Italian, speaks very good Spanish and conversational French.

pre-production research (scouting/location manager, local English/Italian speaking crew, logistics, local authorities, permits, transportation, contract writing/negotiation in Italian & English, catering, accomodations); field production manager, translator/interpreter, events organization/production; networking
