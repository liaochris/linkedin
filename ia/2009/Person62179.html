Derrel Fincher’s Summary

Derrel Fincher has international experience in engineering, project management, IT management, and education; a bachelors and masters in engineering; and a masters in educational technology. He is currently a doctoral student in Educational Technology and Leadership at Pepperdine University and is also the ICT Director at Graded--The American School of São Paulo.

His experience in teaching, engineering, and management provides a broad background for collaborating with teachers and students to help them create engaging, rich, and meaningful experiences. His experience in IT and IT management allows him to work in the central administration team to develop systems and infrastructure that support the needs of running a school system both as a business and as a premier school. His overall background provides him the flexibility and creativity to implement innovative solutions in business and education environments.
