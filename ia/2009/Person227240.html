Susan Tate’s Summary

One of the finest recommendations I ever received came from Vicki Cooper with whom I worked at Newman Graphics and Susan Tate Design. Vicki was an outstanding Production Manager and not only did I have the pleasure of working with her, we are still great friends. Eventually Vicki helped me with my business, becoming an indispensable part of Susan Tate Design. {The following is Vicki's recommendation.}

"I have known and worked with Susan since 1989. I can honestly say Susan is one of the finest designers I have ever worked with. Her creativity far outshines other designers in my association. In 1993, I started working for Susan as her assistant in her business, Susan Tate Design. Her relationship with her customers was outstanding. Susan is a devoted person, to her work, her clients and to her friends.

Susan is one of the kindest individuals I have had the pleasure of knowing. And that's the highest recommendation I can offer!"

Sincerely,
Vicki L. Cooper
Production Manager, Newman Graphics Corp
Assistant, Susan Tate Design

One area of design I particularly love is "Western" design. The opportunity to use the "cowboy" theme often presented itself during my time at the Gaylord Texan. I've found researching the subject matter interesting...almost as much as executing the design to fit current trends. What a kick!
