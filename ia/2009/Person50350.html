Asim I. Janjua’s Summary

I have been working in the creative industry now for over 11 years within a number of different disciplines such as web, rich media & multimedia, GUI, print and 2d/3d animation.

Presently, I'm freelancing for various agencies in London Senior Digital Art Director.

Past few years I have worked on accounts such as P&O Ferries, Zurich Insurance, Hewlett Packard, ITV, Panasonic IMAX, Siemens, ABB, Friends Reunited, British Airways, 7 Eleven, Das Air and First Choice Holidays. Presently I have produced work for, Toshiba, Desperados Beer and MFI. My roles on the account ranges from concepting to evolving the final look and feel, managing and developing junior staff (as designers and conceptors) and to help with scoping with project managers.

Even though I am well versed in all forms of communication my passion lies with the web and interactive communications, as this is more and more the way society and the industry is moving.
