Mary Petto’s Summary

While I created the Page in Progress Box for Cropperware LLC in 2006 and sit quietly waiting for the patent to be awarded, my true love is all things marketing communications--social networking, advertising, marketing materials development, blogging, and a little fiction writing, too. (Think of me when you need help with your social media campaigns. Check out www.marypetto.com.) I also adore working with talented designers to create logos and print materials for small businesses and volunteer my skills for Hadassah International.
I'm always available for freelance work, from soup-to-nuts marketing plans, to social media virtual assitance to emergency proofreading.
