David Alexander’s Summary

With broad and deep experience in technology and in business operations, Opal Computing focuses in the 21st century on socially beneficial causes such as environmentalism, healthcare, and not-for-profit efforts.

After experience as a CTO for the NY Software Industry Association, and as a senior consultant managing development and deployment at one of the largest Web sites in the world (Toysrus.com), I now prefer to use my experience to work with smaller organizations with a true mission, whether that is helping people directly, or improving the environment, or improving the quality of healthcare services.

David Alexander’s Specialties:

Opal Computing provides custom software development and Internet marketing services with a specialty in healthcare, environment, and not-for-profit organizations.

Our Opal Summit service offers business-building through Web site construction, Internet marketing, e-commerce, and newsletter and email list management services. We have hosted e-commerce, event management, and newsletter and email list services available at a reasonable cost, and operating since 2003.
