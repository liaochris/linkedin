Ronnie Parsons’s Summary

Ronnie is a Partner and Designer at Studio Mode, a design, research, and consultation practice located in New York City. He holds both a Bachelor and a Master of Science in Architecture and has studied abroad at The Architectural Association, London, The Institute for Experimental Architecture, Innsbruck, and The University of Applied Arts in Vienna. Ronnie has taught at Stevens Institute of Technology as well as the Pratt Institute and has assisted in design studios at Ohio State University Knowlton School of Architecture and Princeton University School of Architecture.

Ronnie Parsons’s Specialties:

architectural design, Interface design, digital fabrication, code-based procedures, parametric modeling
