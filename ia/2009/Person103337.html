Jamie Powell’s Summary

As a recruitment consultant I specialize in software engineering and systems engineering fields that align to my own interests in elegant and effective solutions to technical, business, social and environmental problems. I principally like to recruit capable people to solve new and challenging problems, work in interesting teams, build solutions that work and scale, and to be the brains behind real innovations.

Problem Domains (in no particular order):
Finance (trading systems, portfolio management, insurance and underwriting, smart card systems), real-time systems, engineering and control systems, audiovisual and embedded software, Internet and telecommunications infrastructure, distributed and web-delivered applications, machine learning, search, pattern matching, language technology, image processing, scientific computation, commercial and academic research, and anything attracting algorithmic complexity, non-trivial correctness issues or generally interesting design issues.
