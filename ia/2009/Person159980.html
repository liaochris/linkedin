Merlyn Gordon’s Summary

Merlyn is a digital marketing professional with more than a decade of success working in B2B and B2C marketing organizations. He specializes in developing integrated digital strategies, managing website production programs, and developing tools to support the sales cycle.

Merlyn is results-oriented, and has an established track record building teams and managing cross-departmental initiatives.

Merlyn holds dual citizenship with the United Kingdom and the United States.

  • Boatswain Mate

    (Maritime industry)

    Merlyn took time off in order to pursue his dream of becoming a merchant mariner. Maritime experience includes over 800 documented sea-days on Federal research ships, and over 1,000 logged hours as Coxswain on survey launches, dive tenders and rigid hull inflatable boats.

    Merlyn studied for and earned a USCG Master 100-ton/Mate 200-ton Near Coastal license (STCW), as well as NOAA Working Diver certification.
