Chris Reed’s Summary

I have over 20 years of B2B and B2G sales experience. This includes account and relationship management and customer service. My experience has been honed through the wireless industry starting in the paging business with over 5 years with BellSouth Paging then with over 5 years of experience with Cellular One (BellSouth Cellular) that changed to Cingular Wireless then to AT&T. My Last position was for Indiana Paging Network where I headed up a start up division for the Parent call IPN Data Solutions. Currently I am the the owner of CREED Improvements a general contracting firm that helps home owners turn their houses into their dream home. You can check out our website at www.creedimprovements.com and email me at chris@creedimprovements.com. We welcome all inquiries and stand ready to help you with your next small project to the next major renovation, room addition, basement finish or Man Cave design.
