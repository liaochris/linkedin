Brad Swanson’s Summary

Brad Swanson is Principal Consultant and an Agile Coach / Scrum Coach at Propero Solutions. He is a veteran of the software industry with 15 years of experience in executive management, project and program leadership, product management, and software development in both start-ups and large companies. He has led successful agile projects with teams distributed between the US, India, China, and Russia. He has deep experience with eXtreme Programming (XP) starting as a developer in 1999, Scrum since 2004, and more recently Lean & Kanban techniques. Brad is a Certified Scrum Coach, Certified Scrum Practitioner and Certified Scrum Master. He is very active in the Agile and Scrum communities as director of Agile Denver and a speaker at the Software Quality Association of Denver (SQuAD), Agile Denver, and the International Association of Software Architects (IASA).

In addition to his deep experience with agile software methods, Brad has a long history with software process improvement initiatives. He has led ISO 9001 and CMM initiatives for a 2000-person engineering organization. Brad previously held the positions of Director, Engineering Manager, Senior Consultant, Software Performance Engineering Lead, Technical Lead, and Senior Software Engineer.
