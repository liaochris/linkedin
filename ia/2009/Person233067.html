Tia Dobi’s Summary

Dear Business Builder,

It takes a certain talent to get someone to respond directly to your sales proposition or company newsletter.

That's where I can help.

As a hybrid writer (news/copy) I bring to the table almost 10 years of good thinking expressed as cleanly as possible.

Writing that's clear, concise and conversational.

Questions that uncover your needs the first time.

Fixing why your website, brochure, press release, resume, sales script, PPC/SEO and Super Bowl TV ads don't work.

So you can sleep soundly at night knowing your content / marketing $$$ are well spent ...and making more cash.

Former production manager of TV commercials, corporate and music videos making pink elephants fly backwards (getting all the people, places, contracts and stuff in front of--and behind--the camera) while bringing in projects on dime, in time (up to $2M for a :60 spot).

For great people at National Geographic, MTV, VH-1, HBO, Budweiser, Coca-Cola, Fox, Players Casinos, ad agencies and car companies like Nissan, Ford, Nascar and N.H.R.A. plus Cal Worthington Chevrolet.

Transitioning from the daily grind of production to staring at blank sheets of paper till beads of blood form on the forehead, I had the pleasure of operating the tele-promptor for Walter Cronkite, Dan Rather, journalists from '60 Minutes', Conan O'Brien, and Geraldo Rivera.

(That's live national TV...100% error-free.)

Some say they let me stick around because I'm tenacious and good with down-to-the-DNA details.

That could be true.

Secretly, I like to believe I meet David Oglivy's recipe for success. "A good writer must be well-rounded."

Here's what else you might need to know: I suck at lunch.

So call anytime if you want an ad message, email, newsletter, press release, PPC, white paper, business card, resume, U.S.P., blog, article or love note that gets opened, read and acted on.

Full-time, part-time or freelance, ring me right up at (310) 839-2468.

Tia D.
