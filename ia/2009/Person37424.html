Carl Bedwell, CCIM’s Summary

I am a commercial mortgage banker with over 15 years of commercial real estate experience. I am experienced in all facets of commercial real estate finance, including the structure and negotiation of competitive real estate capital solutions to practically any form of investment grade asset. I truly enjoy teaming with others in my field to find DELIVERABLE solutions to our clients' needs, and pride myself in obtaining the best terms the market has to offer on a given investment.

I have formed numerous and significant long term relationships with a number of institutional and private investors, as well as other service providers in the field, which typically translates into a level of flexibility others may not be able to obtain.

My goal is to continue to grow our office in Nashville as the premier commercial mortgage banking operation in the region.
