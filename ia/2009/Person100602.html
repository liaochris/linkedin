Ian Wells’s Summary

Having started with a Bsc in Computer Science @ the University of Hertfordshire, I spent 5 years in a variety of roles at National Westminster Bank as an implementer of strategic IT projects.

This was followed by an 8 year tenure at Attachmate, where from my beginnings as an SE I progressed to be the UK MD, a position I held for the last 3 years of my time with them.

I then spent 2 years with Mediapps, an Enterprise Portal provider, for whom I established & grew their UK operation.

From there I joined Lizardtech at the request of the CEO where I was responsible for all the companies operations in EMEA. Lizardtech is a specialist provider of Image Compression technology.

Briefly I was the UK Sales Manager for Neon Systems. Neon are a leader in the Mainframe integration market with comprehensive solutions for the inclusion of the mainframe in either a Service Oriented or Event Driven architecture.

I have been with EMC since end 2004 prinicipally selling the Documentum Enterprise Content Management platform to public sector customers across Central Governement, Local Government, Criminal Justice and Education.

Resulting from success in this area, I have been promoted internally within EMC to District Sales Manager with responsibility for leading a team of 9 sales professionals selling the EMC range of Backup, Recovery and Archive Solutions to all customers across the UK and Ireland.
