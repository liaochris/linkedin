Terence Bentley’s Summary

Strategic Development executive who can envision a strategy, implement the tactics, and deliver results.

Led international and domestic strategic projects affecting $8B in revenues via successful change initiatives.

Expert at the entire spectrum of a P&L-driven strategy process, with a strong focus on key profitability and product issues; identifying high-growth target markets and technologies; and delivering successful transformational actions.

Key points:

Managed and negotiated the Strategic Development cycle from strategic identification, target identification and management of diligence/valuation/contract matters, to final transaction structuring and integration.

39 key and completed transactions ranging up to $3B in individual value.

Cross border experience (UK, Norway, Germany, Israel, France, Canada, Brazil).

Direct management of acquisition and divestiture negotiations.

Member of executive team, responsible for transaction P&L decisions.

Managed intricate multiple-level, cross-functional teams from line management as well as legal, financial, tax and human resources groups.

Very strong history in technology transactions (software, IT services, databases, web applications companies, aerospace, network systems, semiconductors)

Terence Bentley’s Specialties:

Transactions include large public companies, private companies, VC investments, technology alliances and divestitures.

Communications skills include panels at the Conference Board Corporate Development 2008 meeting; ACG seminars and panels; Incremental Advantage Diligence conferences; Wharton School MBA and Stern School MBA guest lectures. Published in Corporate Dealmaker magazine.

    Lucent Technologies
