Jose Mena Raposo’s Summary

My profile combines technical aspects because of my engineering education basis, with other influences based on my experience as a business consultant and postgraduate studies in a business school.

My career began in a Spin-Off of the University of Dresden, where I spent 5 years working as a DSP engineer. When in early 2003, it was acquired by Philips, a life-cycle was completed and I returned to Seville to work for SADIEL. The first years I was working on European R & D and international projects of the company, doing consulting work, commercial, project management, etc..

In 2005 I started working on projects in the areas closest to the innovation and telecommunications. I participated as a consultant in the Strategic Plan for the Information Society and I collaborated in the design and development of new systems for managing the Corporate Network of the Andalusian Government.

In January 2007 I started working on a Public Enterprise, where I started a CIO and Business Relations Manager, and ended as Deputy Manager.

In 2007 I completed the Executive MBA of the Instituto Internacional San Telmo Business School, and now I'm in the training process to be a teacher of this school.

The final distinguishing factor in my profile is my extensive international experience and knowledge of languages, having worked in Germany for five years and having been always linked to the field of international relations (European Union, Multilateral institutions, prestigious Universities, etc.).
