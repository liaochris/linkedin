Sherry Mattos’s Summary

I have over 25 years of experience working with children and families. Along with immense counseling experience is vast knowledge and career experience with The Department of Children and Families, Department of Juvenile Justice, the public educational system and area service providers. My continued relationships and familiarity with these area contributors thru consulting with case managers, probation officers, investigators, administrators, guidance counselors, SAFE coordinators, and working closely with teachers in their classrooms, as well as within the residential settings of a local psychiatric hospital for adolescents, allows for holistic continuity of care. Assistance with those systems can also be beneficial to the overall growth of the family. Working a number of years with families in their home environment and when necessary assisting with the facilitation of residential care allows for all aspects of the families and treatment process to be considered.
