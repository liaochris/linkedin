Rok Zavrtanik’s Summary

Founder, director and editor of publishing house SANJE (since 1997). Sanje (in English: a dream) has started with a single book of poetry and succesfully expanded its catalogue to more than 250 titles of quality fiction, non-fiction, children books, music albums and audio books. Its list includes acclaimed international authors, such as Orhan Pamuk, Kurt Vonnegut, Barack Obama, Philip Roth, Carla Del Ponte ... Today Sanje represents finest and bestselling Slovene writers worldwide.

Main projects in the past:

- FREELANCE PROFESSIONAL WRITER
Activity period: 1987-1991

- EMZIN – magazin fort arts & culture
Activity period: 1991-1997 (editor, director)
In 1991 he joined M'zin, a fanzin of Metelkova network. In few years M'zin has developed to one of key magazines of culture and graphic design in Slovenia. Now it enjoys status of one of best magazines in Europe.

- PHOTOGRAPHY OF THE YEAR (from 1993)
In 1993 has initiated annual exhibition project »Photography of the Year«, which has soon developed to the national extent – with an international jury and awards for best cycles of photography. Now is being considered number one event in field of photography in Slovenia.

- FESTIVAL SANJE (in Slovene = dreams): festival of imagination and creativity. He founded this annual, one Month, open-air, non-proft festival of music and literature in 2002. The festival expands every year - in Slovenia and internationaly.
Festival creates, expands and supports the free exchange of creativity and imagination to unlimited cultures: in the music from classic to hip-hop, in the literature from the sonnet to the unbunded verse in science over the edge of common sense, in sports from Tai Chi to flying the kites.

Rok Zavrtanik’s Specialties:

Tactics and strategies on "how to survive or even expand business on one of the smallest and toughest markets in Europe."
