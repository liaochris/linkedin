Azita Fazli Obregon’s Summary

Experienced Internet marketer, business strategist and certified Google Adwords Professional with 9 years experience as a consultant. Specializing in various realms of eMarketing including search engine optimization, pay-per-click advertising, web analytics, copywriting, eMarketing strategies and email marketing.

Over the years I have helped several well known local and national brands establish a presence online and most specifically in search engines. In my previous positions, I worked with FedEx Canada, Harmony Airways, Coast Hotels & Resorts, CMHC, Granville Island Brewing, Purdy's Chocolates, Coast Capital Savings Credit Union, Prospera Credit Union, Rainier Pacific Bank, Ubiquity Bank, BC Housing, Ballard Power, Copper Kettle Lodge, Integrated Paving Concepts, Kryton Chemicals, Club Modele, West 49 Clothing, Flight Centre, and several other established Canadian and international brands.
