Phil Nieman’s Summary

I have spent the last several years doing political organizing and consulting work in the Chicagoland area. My political work was based primarily in the field, where I had the privilege of working with some of the best "old school" grassroots organizers in Chicago. I have experience in planning and managing campaigns including direct voter contact through phone, door to door, and mail.

I am currently the head of marketing for GasPedal, LLC a Word of Mouth marketing training firm. At GasPedal my daily responsibilities include word of mouth marketing campaigns, email marketing, promotional placements, and relationship marketing. I'm also the companies salesforce.com administration.

One of our divisions at GasPedal is the Blog Council. The Blog Council is a community of senior executives involved in blogging and social media at major global corporations. It's an honor to have the ability to be a part of private group discussions that include some of the smartest marketers in the world.
