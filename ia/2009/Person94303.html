Henry Chen’s Summary

I have over 8 years of research and design in the mobile handheld device industry. I currently work in a multidisciplinary organization that is responsible for the hardware and software experiences of the BlackBerry mobile device. I have experience in a wide range of user research and design thinking methods to aid in uncovering user needs and generating useful, usable and delightful design solutions. I have managed research projects independently as well as collaboratively across disciplines, teams, and geographies. I enjoy working with people of different backgrounds and perspectives and working with customers to understand their wants and needs.

Henry Chen’s Specialties:

 Research methods: interviewing, field observation, surveys, panels, focus groups, heuristic evaluation, expert review, cognitive walkthrough, think-aloud usability lab studies, eye-tracking, benchmark, competitor studies, OOB studies, and analysis of software quality metrics
 Research design and statistical analysis (SPSS, Tableau)
 Persona and user scenarios development
 Prototype creation: Paper, PowerPoint, HTML, Visual Basic, Flash
 UI design flows: Visio and InDesign
