Jeannie Drake’s Summary

In the service of my clients, I’ve invented pre-Columbian myths for Central American resorts; crafted the stories of Hawaiian kahus (shepherds) and kupunas (elders); chronicled the renaissance of Rockefeller’s Caribbean legacy; revealed the mystique of Moroccan style; and shared the spirit of La Vita Toscana. I’m passionate about travel, culture and the art of storytelling. I have a flair for long-format writing that resonates with the sophisticated market, yet I’m equally adept at short, compelling copy tailored to diverse audiences.

Key clients, past and present, include: Calistoga Ranch, An Auberge Resort; Castiglion del Bosco; Costa Smeralda; Dorado Beach (former RockResort); Four Seasons Resort Hualalai; Four Seasons Resort Punta Mita; Kapalua Resort; Montage Beverly Hills; Montage Deer Valley; Montage Laguna Beach; Peninsula Papagayo/Four Seasons Resort Costa Rica; and West Caicos Reserve/Ritz-Carlton Molasses Reef.

Jeannie Drake’s Specialties:

Brand storytelling. Engaging web content. Long-format writing. Connecting with buyers' emotions. Editorial accuracy. Client relations. Strategic thinking. Immersion in the product. Articulating a consistent brand voice and style across multiple mediums.
