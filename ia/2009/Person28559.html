Billie Jenkin’s Summary

SELF-ACCEPTANCE GURU, Public Speaker and Presenter:
Empowering others for change in their attitudes and lives is my passion. Effective change comes when individuals learn that they, too, can use their perceived shortcomings to advantage.

With attitudinal transformation, individuals and groups experience growth: they stop trying and start allowing.
Some may learn to appreciate their own greatness, reframe the idea of struggling with A.D.D. (and feeling dumb) or deal with approaching death of a loved one. The joy comes in finding the gift beneath its wrapping.

Customized programs can be created for schools, churches, social organizations, and businesses. Self-acceptance and diversity training are areas I particularly excel.
Specialties:

AUTHOR, "The Knock-Kneed Cowboy":
I wrote and illustrated this short chapter book for the purpose of raising self acceptance of kids (and the "kid" inside us all). In an adventurous tale of a tall pre-teen girl who wishes she were short and a knock-kneed cowboy who wants to be bow-legged, readers learn they have the power to choose what they believe about themselves.

The book is designed to be effective in classrooms, in home schooling, or by independent readers grades 4 - 6 or above.


CO-AUTHOR:
Co-author of two best-sellers (Wake-Up Moments & Wake Up Women):
In these two books, I wrote of true experiences that taught me to look at life's problems in a light that transformed them from stumbling blocks to stepping stones.


EDUCATOR:
Decades of teaching children (K-12) and adults has shown need for "emotional literacy." Teaching incarcerated adult males demonstrated that poor decisions stemmed from poor opinions of themselves... and, instead, looking to others for acceptance. Such awareness led to my writing and speaking.

Billie Jenkin’s Specialties:

My expertise lies in empowering the transformation of self-limiting, destructive attitudes into those which propel individuals and groups to achieve their desires.
