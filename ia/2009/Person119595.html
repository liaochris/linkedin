Jon P. Vague’s Summary

Multilingual International Energy Business Consultant and Attorney with significant Fortune 100 corporate energy commercial experience in the US, Brazil, and several Latin American countries, including a US$ 1 billion international project closing, currently working as international business lawyer and Houston representative of 80-attorney Brazilian oil and gas and corporate law firm and Principal of Houston-based Navarro Lucas Vague, LLC International Business Consultants.

Ten-year experience conducting business structuring, negotiations and project management of international projects and operations for US energy companies doing business in Latin America, including Duke Energy and El Paso Energy, actively utilizing English, Portuguese, Spanish and French languages and project leadership skills.
• Due Diligence, structuring and negotiations of project bids, partnerships and M&A transactions
• Analysis of regulatory and government affairs issues with host governments
• Foreign subsidiary transition and integration management
• Market research, identification and evaluation of greenfield, M&A and other investment opportunities

Jon P. Vague’s Specialties:

Oil, natural gas, and electric power project development and negotiations in Latin America and in Portuguese, Spanish or French-speaking countries; international energy company mergers and acquisitions; international energy company operations; foreign government and regulatory affairs; post-acquisition foreign subsidiary corporate transition and integration management.
