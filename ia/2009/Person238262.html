Tripp Babbitt’s Summary

October 15th - Live Webinar: "Achieving Customer Intimacy through a Brain Enema" . . . sign up here: http://bit.ly/Vd3OK

Long time advocate of the work of W. Edwards Deming. "Reformed" Lean Six Sigma MBB. I have worked with service industry for 15+ years of my career. I recently started working with Vanguard Consulting Ltd. to promote "systems thinking" in the US. This method combines Deming, Taiichi Ohno and intervention theory to improve service and reduce costs, by moving from a command and control mentality to a systems thinking one. A free download (Understanding Your Organization as a System) from my website is available to anyone wanting to learn more (160 pages of exercises and content). Only for those serious about service improvement.

Exposing the ills of command and control thinking. Call centers (good place to start to understand customer demand in service industry); working with core processes in both the public and private sector service organizations and the service side of manufacturing.
