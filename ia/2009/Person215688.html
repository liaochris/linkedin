Shaun Barnes’s Summary

Over 10 years of experience designing websites, one of my first was featured in Computer Music magazine back in 1998!

Designing professionally for 2 years after leaving dull office job with NHS Business Services. During that time I have worked on projects for Business Link, Altec Lansing, Sarah Beeny (C4 Property Ladder) as well as many local clients. Completing a wide range of jobs from full website designs to managing PPC campaigns to SEO tweaks.

My goals are to keep producing high quality websites and build up my client base. I believe in working closely with clients and forming long term relationships.
