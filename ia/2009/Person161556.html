Michael Kauffman’s Summary

Sales and Marketing exec and team player with two decades of experience in music and entertainment and with a love for the colorful spectrum of pop culture. Particularly interested in bringing the compelling story, the memorable song, and a million remarkable ideas to the marketplace.

Michael Kauffman’s Specialties:

Sales, marketing, and distribution strategy, social media and community marketing, project and conversation management, brand and corporate presentations, cultural recommendations.
