Fiona Young-Brown’s Summary

I am a Renaissance Soul, a teacher, counselor, researcher, writer, businesswoman, traveler, lover of life. Do you have the right opportunity to interest me?

- Qualified and experienced coach. I can take you beyond fear to discover your true calling.
- Writer for multiple publications including EBSCO, Colorado Daily, Kentucky Monthly, Bluegrass Dog and others
- Graduate research in the infertility experience and fertility treatments.
- Experienced teacher. Three years teaching at junior high school level in Japan, four years teaching at the Universities of Iowa and Kentucky, plus further freelance tutoring experience with adults.
- Experienced in small business development and management as owner of Kentish Maid Candles.
- Freelance writer for various online and hard-copy publications.
- Nonprofit and board management. Served as Program Director for a Federal Program with the Homeless and Housing Association of Kentucky. Former board member of the Emma Goldman Clinic in Iowa.
I am seeking to further develop my coaching clientele and contacts. I coach primarily by telephone and thus can work with clients nationwide. I also offer workshops and group coaching on a variety of work and personal topics.

Fiona Young-Brown’s Specialties:

I coach:
- Women and couples who are dealing with infertility. As you navigate the wide array of emotions, treatments and options, my background in counseling and coaching allows me to offer sympathetic yet objective support.
- Anyone who wants to move beyond fear. Fear holds so many of us back - move past it.
- The true Renaissance Soul
