Ami Readdy’s Summary

Ami provides leadership, management and consultation services to individual and organizational clients in the areas of prospecting and outreach, business development, change management, facilitation, public participation, organizational governance, communications training, complex problem solving, group decision-making, and interest-based negotiation. She treats all staff and clients as collaborators on a shared path to great results!

As a manager, Ami has achieved outstanding success at meeting and exceeding metrics, focused on clear goals and objectives. As her prime skill set indicates, she excels at training teams to delve into innovation, advanced problem solving techniques, and implement effective and continuous improvement along the way.

As a professional coach and facilitator, Ami has assisted business owners, NGO's and community groups in conflict management and business strategizing, assisting groups in developing tangible outcomes, implementation and action plans with clearly defined objectives, milestones, and schedules.

She has been the facilitator for corporate retreats and off-site strategic planning sessions, team trainings, and is skilled at tapping creativity and innovation helping to transform groups of individuals into high functioning teams.
