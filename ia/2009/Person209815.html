Sarah Michaud’s Summary

Sarah Michaud has three years of experience in public relations, and more specifically in providing PR support to BtoB and BtoC companies.

For Ballou PR, Sarah develops and manages projects in e-commerce, web 2.0 and enterprise solutions fields. She works and have worked for Facebook, Crystal Content, DaWanda.com, Advestigo, Autonomy, BonArtisan.com, Seedcamp, Netlog, coComment and dismoioù. Sarah also brings to her clients her expertise on new media such as blogs and social networks to optimize their communication strategy.

Prior to joining Ballou PR, Sarah worked for the Paris-based Ketchum agency, the media relation and influence strategy division of DDB group. Being part of the high-tech department, her role was to run, alone and in team, PR activities for a few clients, from strategy setting to operational execution. Daily contact towards the clients and media, she was also responsible for the redaction of the communication documents. By developing expertness in DotComs companies’ support, she participated to the PR launching of Maxicours.com, Entreparticuliers.com, EverestPoker.net and AutoScout24.fr. She also took part of First Data, Meetic, and Forrester Research media relations.

Other professional experience includes For Him Magazine, where she was responsible for the « Hotline » column, which aim is to get unusual objects and original events for readers. She also wrote several articles for other columns of the magazine, as well as for Madmoizelle.com, a feminine website.
