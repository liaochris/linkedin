George Magklaras’s Summary

An experienced Systems and Networks administrator with substantial research and development experience in the fields of data storage, high performance computing, and intrusion detection (host and network based) and substantial consultancy experience on large systems administration issues, especially on the UNIX (Solaris, AIX)/Linux world.

My interests lie in the area of large infrastructure development (HPC and storage), and I have derived computing solutions for bioinformatics, life science as well as Computation Fluid Dynamics areas, especially on the storage arena (parallel and cluster filesystems, PVFS, GFS).

Finally, I have strong interest in pattern matching algoriths, intrusion detection/prevention (IDS/IPS) and domain specific languages (DSL),
