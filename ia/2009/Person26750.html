Bent Christensen’s Summary

M.Sc. in Chemistry including inorganic, organic and technical chemistry, metallurgy, silicate chemistry, pharmaceuticals and enviromental chemistry.

More than 30 years of experience within IPR consultancy. This includes development and implementation of IP strategies, identification of strategic IP key parameters, selection of inventions, drafting and prosecution of patent applications, drafting of license contracts, handling of oppositions and appeals, performing IP due diligence and freedom to operate, counselling in patent law suits etc.


European Patent Attorney (EPA) since 1990.

Bent Christensen’s Specialties:

Danish, European (EPO) and US patent case law. Member of the Association of Danish Patent Agents, FIR (Danish Industrial Rights Association), FICPI (Fédération Internationale des Conseils en Proprieté Industrielle), AIPPI (Association Internationale pour la Protection de la Proprieté Industrielle), LES (Licensing Executives Society) Scandinavia and EPI (European Patent Institute).

Languages: Danish, English, German and French.
