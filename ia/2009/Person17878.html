Anthony Noel’s Summary

Graphic designer and art director, articulate, analytical, visually and verbally confidant, good ability to discuss and describe ideas, provide insight and tangential thinking.

Strong photographic background, and I particularly enjoy interpretative work that requires close engagement with the subject.

Good experience establishing brands and design systems, particularly style guides.

Writing and consultancy on design and business practice are developing interestings. I would like to get into mentoring in particular, and social enterprise.

Anthony Noel’s Specialties:

Print design, layout & management; posters, flyers, magazines, zines, books, manuals, catalogues. Photo, illustration and content direction and specification. Information design systems and analysis.

Anthony Noel’s Interests:

I divide my free time between film, music, photography, books and some sport. I tend to like arty, indy films, and consider a couple of hours spent in the cinema some of the best, most selfish and self-indulgent time I can waste. I would say that I like all kinds of music, but so does almost everyone else, so I’ll nail my colours to the mast and say that most of my musical tastes orbit around jazz and its derivatives. Writing and photography are both pastimes that have grown out of professional activities, and I’d like both to be developed further. I enjoy watching cricket and rugby, but a kind of in-built antipathy to exercise consigns most attempts at physical activity to two or three month stints in the gym before twice that away from the treadmill. I’m no slob, however, and aswell as being an early riser, I like to walk places, use public transport and generally get out and about. I’ve dabbled in yoga and running, but never manage to be consistent in sticking with a regime.
