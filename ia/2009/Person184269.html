Paul Brugger’s Summary

CityLocal Cork offers low cost Web adverts, optimised to ensure that your business can be found on the first pages of Google.

You don't have to have a website, our Web adverts will ensure that your business gets noticed.
If you have a website, Fantastic: increase traffic to it with a link from an advert in Cork's Business Directory.
Our aim is to get you up and running with a minimum of fuss. We have a simple, low cost pricing structure with no hidden charges or surprises.

CityLocal Cork also provide a local portal for local people packed with Cork News, Cork Events, Cork Cinema listings, Cork Weather and fun pages for all ages - all updated daily.
So whether you are looking for a Cork Restaurant, Cork Accommodation or a selection of Cork Hotels you'll "Find it" on CityLocal.
