Patrick Lauzon’s Summary

Responsible for Product Marketing & Strategies, Communications Marketing and Sales for the online products at Quebecor Media . The products include :
Canoe.ca portal ( French & English ), Jobboom.com ( careers classifieds), Micasa.ca ( realestate classifieds), Autonet.ca ( automotive classifieds), ClassifiedExtra/vitevitevite.ca ( general classifieds ) , EspaceCanoe/Canoe Space ( social platform to interact with Quebecors offline content ), Reseau Contact ( personals site ), TVA sites ( sites related to the TVA network Canada's largest french TV network ), SUN online properties ( www.torontosun.com and 20+ other daily newspaper sites, Bowes and Hebdo Quebec online strategy ( 250 + weekly newspapers online strategy, and TVA publication online properties ( online property and strategy for aprox 35+ magazines ) .

We are looking to make Canoe.ca and the specialized verticals the top Canadian destinations where Canadians can find the best Canadian content & services .
