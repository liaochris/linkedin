Nick Tomlinson’s Summary

Long-time Resourcer / Consultant at HTS, a fantastic, ethical recruitment boutique. Author of the best recruitment blog (I've) ever written.

Particularly interested in software and hardware development, Dynamics Nav, fleet management solutions, biotech/microbiology, industrial inkjet, renewables, semiconductors.

Novel 'Saint Valentine' published by Transworld (Black Swan and Bantam). Critics loved it, nobody bought it. I blame choice of release-date (after Valentine's Day).

Nice words about my writing include:
Dame Jacqueline Wilson on 'Saint Valentine' protagonist Neil Flack: 'A delightful literary hero. I wish I'd invented him.'

Ireland on Sunday: 'Quite superb... Brilliantly observed and wonderfully piquant. Savour it.'
The Times: '...summons up an instantly recognisable world: the very early teenage and its exquisite embarrassment over trivia.'

Former Academic Learning Mentor - it's like a school counsellor, but the only advice you can give is to do more homework.

Was a supply teacher. No way to earn a living. Contact me for free survival tips.

Working on another book. Then again, who isn't working on a book?

Was awarded karate black belt in ridiculous circumstances.

Trying to make up for lost time discovering the New Social Media.
