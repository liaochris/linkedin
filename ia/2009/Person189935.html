Paul Smith’s Summary

Thumbnail sketch:

I'm a sustainable business consultant with an MBA in Sustainable Management, a degree in Advertising, diverse experience at a green products provider, 8 years experience founding and running an association, 10 years running and managing an on site corporate massage provider business.

Extended version:

I help forge truly sustainable businesses at GreenSmith Consulting. I am a "green Swiss army knife", with a range of skills available to support the needs of my clients. For both existing companies wanting to push the needle greener, and startups needing help effectively and authentically getting the message out and navigating the considerations involved, we can help.

Getting an MBA in Sustainable Management from Presidio considerably strengthened my business skills, helping make me attuned to how to do business in a way that's more profitable, well managed, and ecologically sound. I see possibilities where others don't. I make connections that others haven't yet. I help people to see them, and know how to best take advantage of them.

My overarching talent is "bottom lining" complex ideas, in a way that is understandable and accessible to a variety of audiences, internal and external to a company. After all, if you have the greatest idea/product/service ever, and your employees or customers don't see the relevance to them, it doesn't really matter, does it?

Bolstering the human capital part of the equation is 10 years that I ran Traveling Massage, a successful private and corporate on site business. In doing so, I've interacted with 1000s of people from all walks of life, which developed keen insight on what makes people tick, and how to bring out their potential, whether an employee or a customer.

I also managed the near impossible, creating a successful fulcrum point for Bay Area holistic practitioners, Bliss Association. From this, I developed a knack for creating, organizing, and inspiring a disperate group of stakeholders.

Paul Smith’s Specialties:

copywriting, business plan creation/review, connecting people to resources, branding, sustainability implementation and management, bottom lining complex subjects, facilitating collaborative partnerships, and creating community (with the public and within organizational structures)
