Vinay Varma’s Summary

I was a multi-disciplinary scholar when I went to work with dreams of an academic career. In ten years I made three career shifts - from research and teaching to design to business solutions and management, all of them successfully, however modest the success may have been.

My consistent strengths have been a rapid ability to learn and master a new area; an inter-connected, n-step analysis of everything; an uncompromising approach to 'honor'; a die-hard, focus-driven pursuit of an objective in my mind; an ability to drastically change course when external influences seem to push me in a direction away from what is required for my long-term goals; and an ability to completely transform myself quite invisibly. These represent the common bedrock of all my career shifts and whatever modest success I have achieved.

My short-term career aims include helping companies develop innovative solutions to their internal needs and client needs, and handling challenges of increasing scale and complexity in order to master them, besides making good money in the process to sustain my long-term goals.

My long term aims in life include writing books in a range of subjects and starting/running a company in the area of book publishing/distribution/retailing - largely with the aim of distributing knowledge far and wide. I also have an active interest in politics and consider national building serious business for all citizens.
