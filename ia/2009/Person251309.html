Zeger De Spiegelaere’s Summary

For the past 4 years, I explored b2b relations with focus on long term relationships, customer satisfaction and budget on a high tech background. With this acquired customer orientation, as head of team (18 pers.), we're reorganizing internal supply chain (lean management, autonomous teams, ISO 9001 organization). My goal is to enable my customers (internal/external) to focus on their core business and added value activities. On a short term I'm expanding my operational and people skills while I seek to explore the terrain of general management on the mid term (1-2 years). Within 3 to 5 years, I aim to move up to management level or to lead a business unit in an operational environment.
