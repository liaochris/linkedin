Betsy Curtin’s Summary

As a recruiter for the sport and outdoor industries, I have successfully worked with the following clients: C9/Hanes Brands, NIKE, nau, adidas, lucy, Umbro, New Balance, Brooks, Moving Comfort, KEEN, Horny Toad, Yakima Products, Timberland, Reebok, Oakley...

I maintain an extensive community of corporate brand management, retail marketing management, advertising management and product development talent, as well as apparel, footwear, graphic and brand designers. If you'd like to become a part of my community, please send me an email [betsy(at)thinkplum.net]

I was just elected to the Board of the Outdoor Industries Women’s Coalition where I will Chair the the Professional Development Committee.
One of our new initiatives is to promote talent from within the industry as well as develop talent from outside the industry - to learn more visit www.OIWC.org and look for the OIWC Guide to Turning your Passion into your Profession.
