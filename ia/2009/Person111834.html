Jessica Wolfson’s Summary

I am a film, video and photography freelance producer. I have produced several critically acclaimed feature length documentary films as well as series for television and for the web. I have worked in many roles on projects from line producer to supervising/managing producer to post production supervisor. I have overseen the conception to completion of my projects, from working closely with clients and networks to script writing to managing budgets, schedules, crew, cast, locations and sets. I have directed editors, designers, animators to form the creative content of a project and have worked extensively in the post production online and sound mix.

Jessica Wolfson’s Specialties:

Documentary Films, Reality Televison, Videos for the web focusing on a variety of subjects from Book Videos and Trailers to Fashion Photography to Educational Videos and Short Films.
