Matthew O'Riordan’s Summary

Have come from a technical programming background whilst always being involved in media, advertising & marketing. Worked as Technical Director for a number of agencies before starting Lemon Foundation in 2003, which is now operating as a digital production house under the Lemon Digital brand.

I am currently the Digital Services Director of Aqueduct, which is an integrated brand, marketing and communications agency. The direct client team from Lemon Foundation merged with Aqueduct in 2008 enabling both companies to offer an integrated service from a combined team of roughly 40 people.

I am also the co-founder of E-consultancy and the founder of Lemon Studios which is a digital work space / serviced office business currently in Clerkenwell, London.
