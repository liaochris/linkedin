Andrew Matkovic’s Summary

Andrew’s main goal at The Carmon Group is to make sure that his clients get the most efficient, seamless service possible all the while making it a memorable experience.

Andrew has a proven track record in the Search Industry, with many successful placements with Major General Contractors, Construction Management and Utility Companies. His niche focus is in Energy Infrastructure, Renewable Fuels, Renewable Energy and Alternative Energy Companies.

Andrew has a BA from Baldwin-Wallace College. He graduated in 1998 and shortly there after got into the search industry placing Technical and Mid - Level Management positions such as Engineers, Plant Managers and Vice Presidents. His role gradually became more niche focused over the years to eventually evolve into his market practice at the Carmon Group.
