Sam Evans’s Summary

***Specialist in providing TIVOLI, WEBSPHERE, LOTUS DOMINO and CLOUD COMPUTING Consultants***

Established Senior Recruitment & Service Delivery Consultant with a strong background in Information Technology and a solid understanding of both public and private sectors.

Eight years experience within the IT industry; three years working in a technical capacity followed by five years recruiting technical consultants. I have recruited all walks of life from developers, right the way through to technical programme managers.

Whilst I have supplied many companies with various skill sets during my career, there have actually been two main areas in which I have specialised, and ironically these two specialisms have now merged.

I began supplying messaging consultants into hosting companies such as Cobweb Solutions. This shortly moved into supplying virtualisation consultants and now more recently cloud computing specialists.

My first ever placement in recruitment was a Tivoli Storage Manager Consultant; I had not heard of Tivoli at this point so I made it my goal to build a solid network of Tivoli specialists and obtain a firm grip on this niche market. I did this succesfully resulting in Flex supplying major financial organisations, leading IBM Business Partners and also IBM direct in certain countries.

Outside of Europe, major achievements include supplying Tivoli Consultants into leading companies both in the US and Africa.

How do the specialisms merge? Well, take a look a IBM's cloud and you will notice that Tivoli will be integral to it's success.......more to follow.
