Julie-lynn Belon’s Summary

Julie-lynn Belon is a high energy Event Manager and has over 15 years of event management experience in industries such as IT, Computers, Energy, Medical Devices, Pharmaceutical, Retail (Footwear and Outdoor Sports) Healthcare, Government -MIT Lincoln Labs, Hospitality, the Tree Care Industry and most currently sports productions.

She has managed, marketed, promoted and executed to date over 1,700 events worldwide including experiential marking, mobile tours, special events, educational events, tradeshows, promotional, events, conferences, road shows, executive briefings, CME meetings, Medical CEU meetings sales/user meetings, symposiums, webinars, seminars, training meetings press conferences and charity events.

She has expertise in Event Management, Tradeshows, Marketing and Promotions. She has been fortunate enough to travel domestically and internationally to Asia, Europe, and Australia managing some of her events.

She has worked with such prestigious companies as Digital Equipment Corp. (HP), Cisco Systems, Radionics (TycoHealthcare) Timberland, Ingersoll Rand, SigSauer (Sigarms) and Kittery Trading Post.

She has worked with such prestigious groups such as American Red Cross, Camp Sunshine, Child Advocacy Center, Coleman, Colorado Ski, FOTR (Friends of Tuckerman Ravine), Go Daddy, Life is Good, Maine Dept of Inland Fisheries, NH Fish and Game, National Geographic, NRA, Smith and Wesson, Sunday River, Sugarloaf/USA, Toyota and The Meathouse to name a few; the list is long.
