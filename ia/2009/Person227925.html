Stuart Hillston’s Summary

Stuart spent over twenty years in the IT industry in the UK and USA. He started his first start-up in 1994 and has been directly involved in early stage companies ever since.

He has managed investment in a London based family office worked in a FSA regulated brokerage & fund management business investing in pre-IPO opportunities, particularly AIM.

Most recently, Stuart founded an advisory company for early stage technology businesses to enable them to grow and achieve their potential through great management, a focussed go-to-market strategy and sufficient funding to achieve their goals.

Stuart and his wife live in North Buckinghamshire with two teenage boys and other livestock.
