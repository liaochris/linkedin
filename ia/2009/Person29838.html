    Blue Room FX

    (Privately Held; Broadcast Media industry)

    1998 — Present (11 years)

    Blueroom provids the motion picture and television industry with premium post production services:

    • Editing
    • Finishing / Online
    • Visual effects
    • ADR / Foley / narration recording
    • DVD and Blu-ray authoring

    Blueroom is based on the Raleigh Studios lot in Manhattan Beach with satellite offices in Louisiana and Hollywood.

    Blueroom is the leading full-service post production facility in the South Bay, our flexibility and focus allows us to tailor our services to specifically serve our clients needs. Our Hollywood office located on the Raleigh Studios Hollywood lot provides ADR recording and audio services for television and feature film productions. In Louisiana, Blueroom provides film productions with experienced personnel, AVID rentals and cutting rooms, production offices, ADR and foley stages, screening room and DI theater for color grading.

    Blueroom DVD & Blu-ray Services

    (Entertainment industry)

    1998 — Present (11 years)

    Blueroom has a rich history and expertise in DVD production under the Directorsite brand since 1998. Now as Blueroom, Directorsite clients (film distributors and studios) have complete Blu-Ray encoding, authoring and design capabilities at their disposal.

    Blueroom creates Blu-ray discs for consumer distribution. Blueroom has the capability to do multi-pass hardware encoding for DVD and Blu-Ray, our encoders create MPEG 2 and AVC files of the highest quality acceptable for Studio motion picture consumer releases. We extensively QC masters for proper replication.

    Blueroom menu design and graphics department has the capability to create everything from beautifully animated menus to the most simple images that perfectly execute the marketing concept of the film.

    DVD Commentary recording can be done in our Manhattan Beach, Hollywood or Baton Rouge, Louisiana recording stages. All three locations feature high definition projected image and comfortable atmosphre.
