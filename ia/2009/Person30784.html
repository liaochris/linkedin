Michael Payne’s Summary

Michael has worked in the advertising and marketing field for over 12 years. During this time he has gained experience in the studio, marketing, and print production departments working on a wide range of print, online, interactive, and broadcast projects. He has gained valuable project management experience within marketing and agency environments at Hummingbird, Sharpe Blackmore, and most recently, as a core member in a cross-functional team at CSI. He has developed marketing communications plans for a variety of projects in small, medium and large corporate environments. Michael is at home in both the print and online worlds. Projects have included direct mail, books, marketing collateral, POP, OOH, digital signage, digital video, display advertising, online advertising and a variety of related projects. His computer experience includes a sound working knowledge of industry standard graphic design and web applications. All this has allowed him to develop unique and valuable skill sets. He has used this knowledge in various capacities to implement effective strategies that bring projects to successful completion. A creative, strategic thinker with an entrepreneurial mindset, he is able to quickly identify and understand issues and opportunities. He has been able to call upon a depth and breadth of his design, communication and organizational skills to consistently deliver beyond expectations. Michael’s ability to multi-task, organize and effectively delegate allows him to bring order to chaos. A people person with effective communication skills and an ability to garner respect and cooperation, Michael has consistently built strong business relationships. One reason for this … he has maturity, is honest and hard working, yet light hearted.

Project management, print production, advertising, studio management, creative project management, marketing communications plans, writing, branding and identity, graphic design, print design, corporate standards and guidelines.
