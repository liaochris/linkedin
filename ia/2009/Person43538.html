Chris Kilkes’s Summary

I've been online media long enough to remember running 468x60 banners keyed to search terms on Hotbot; using an early adserved called AdKnowledge; or mourned the demise of the Webby’s in San Francisco because the awards show at SF City Hall in 2000 was just so obnoxiously fun. A moment in life that can be fondly bid adieu to with both sadness and relief.

Fundamentally I specialize in providing not just digital solutions, but integrated, customized and exclusive marketing opportunities for clients that transcend the various tactical buckets that media is typically sorted into. So that could be print, TV, display, search, social media, etc. all rolled up into one multitouch, integrated program.

Having started out in analytics, leveraging this key strength of online marketing remains vitally important to me (and as it should) since it can literally determine the life and death of any media strategy / tactic. Media plans benefit from the wisdom of hindsight and no analyst truly understands how difficult it can be to truly “innovate” in just the specific way you want.

In my current role I manage overall media planning & buying operations on the West Coast for primarily consumer clients focusing on brand and direct response depending on client need.

Chris Kilkes’s Specialties:

Interactive media planning & buying, integrated custom programs, direct response email & postal, outdoor, print, social media, viral marketing, online video and other emerging media types.
