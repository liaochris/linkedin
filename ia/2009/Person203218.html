Ronald Meeus’s Summary

Started up a video game production company. Hippo Point Interactive is a business that has grown from a set of scribblings on the back of a beer coaster to an active entity in the summer of 2008. Working on 'Out of Bullets', our first game, and a piece of in-game conversation technology we call the 'Emo Gauge'. We (myself and associate Jan Put) have some great technological, gameplay-related and narrative ideas for the game industry, ones we finally want to put to work.

My background, however, is in journalism. I was a staff writer at the Belgian national daily De Morgen for seven years, and will continue working for this publication in a freelance capacity. During previous bouts in which I worked as a freelancer, I wrote for such publications as Netwerk, Menzo and Dag Allemaal. Currently, I am taking the freelance writing path once again, writing for publications such as De Morgen, GUNK, Data News and Clickx.
