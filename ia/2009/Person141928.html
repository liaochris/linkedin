Lisa Chiv (Lisa@FerreriSearch.com)’s Summary

I’ve been a proven financial recruiter for 12 years. Prior to recruiting, I started my career in public and private accounting. The competitive advantage I can offer candidates and clients stems from my combination of experience in accounting, coupled with extensive corporate finance and accounting recruitment experience.

Currently, I am a Partner with Ferreri Search, a specialized firm focused exclusively on providing recruitment services in the accounting & finance and information technology sectors within the Tampa Bay area to employers and individuals. Our three practices are Executive Search, Permanent Placement, and Contract/Project Staffing.

Prior to Ferreri Search, I held several roles within a national staffing firm: Recruiter for finance and accounting professionals seeking contract positions (primarily upper level versus clerical), Market Manager for their San Francisco Flex Division (top 5 producing office firm-wide) with up to four direct reports, and Executive Search Recruiter where I placed candidates in permanent positions. My recruiting & accounting career started in the San Francisco Bay Area where I spent 12 years and then transferred to the Tampa Bay area where I have been for close to 3 years.

I have consistently won Top Producer awards company-wide 10 years in a row and earned "Platinum" status five times which is awarded to the top 5% of recruiters within the firm as well as "Gold" status three times, which is awarded to the top 10% of recruiters within the national staffing firm.

I'm passionate about what I do and the service I provide to my clients and candidates. I adhere to strict ethical standards in my practice. Candidates can remain confident that I will never represent them for an opportunity without their consent and clients will be presented with candidates only after they have been met and gone through a rigorous screening process.
