errol lawrence’s Summary

Moved to Washington DC from Los Angeles in 2003 to launch www.itshospitality.com and open OYA and SEI restaurant and lounge. They are both located in the Penn Quarter in downtown DC. Prior to moving to DC i owned and operated 6 restaurants in Los Angeles from 1990 to 2003.

Just recently launch www.itshospitality.com. It's a networking, job, marketing and search site for the hospitality industry. its my effort to creating a single community for all of hospitality and the other industries that support us so we can easily connect, share and conduct business.

The participants include job seekers, individuals, travel, gaming, restaurants, hotels, vendors, service providers and organizations. we are all connected in someway but yet we are spread out in almost 300 different hospitality groups on different networking sites.
