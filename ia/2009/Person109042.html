Jenard Stokes’s Summary

I want to personally thank all of you that have come to this site for the 1st time. My name is Jenard Stokes and I'm an internet network marketer and a work at home professional. I truly believe that most people who have failed in present-day network marketing have done so due to the old outdated traditional methods of marketing that are taught.

Now don't get me wrong..certain basic principles and fundamentals still apply such as utilizing phone techniques. However, the MARKETING that is being taught just isn't as effective anymore.

My goal is to help as many network marketers as I can to succeed by teaching the use of more relevant, up-to-date strategies that are more in-line with today's day and age of the internet and technology.

From the very start of my Career, it has been my mission, my life passion and purpose to create millionaires with my companies.I shifted from Real Estate Investing to owning a company that still allows me to make my dreams come true. In 2004, I was blessed to partner up with a mentor that put me well on the road to accomplishing my goals. Over the last couple of years things really started to take off and go places I've never imagined.

The bottom line is this… I can now see my Vision to create millionaires and make my dreams become reality.

So, after taking a look at an industry that had been around for years, it changed my life instantly. After taking a deeper look at everything that was going on with the economy, I made the Decision to make a new move into Network Marketing. I decided to publicly announce to the world that I was moving on to expand on my vision and to reach my objective of…

Creating Millionaires Within The Industry

It will not be easy. But I truly believe and see that I made the right decision!

Of course if there is anything I can do to assist you achieve GREATER RESULTS in your business, just give me a shout!
