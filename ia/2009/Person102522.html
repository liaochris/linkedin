Dr James Green’s Summary

I was born in Glasgow, Scotland, in 1949

Following schooling at the John Neilson Institution in Paisley I studied Western European literature in Portugal (Coimbra) before training as a tax accountant.

After a spell working with offshore trust administration I spent two years completing a PhD thesis on Fernando Pessoa and working as a freelance presenter/producer for BBC Radio.

I spent the next 25 years in the publishing, printing and bookselling industry as a director and editor. I also wrote, edited or translated over 60 books. Search Amazon for J.C.R. Green for details of available titles. These include business, commerce, poetry, fiction, criticism, biography etc.

Following a takeover of my company in the late 1980’s I changed direction and set up a corporate services company in London, later moving it and my family to Shropshire and the Isle of Man and with an office in Geneva. We provided UK and offshore formation and administration services for clients of accountants and solicitors.

Having exited this business I have for the past few years undertaken consultancy work advising companies on international taxation, strategic development, corporate law, and change management.

I am a member of the International Tax Planning Association and am a non executive director of companies, both listed and unlisted, in several European and Carribean jurisdictions.

Professional Qualifications
Chartered Director (CDir)
Member of the Chartered Institute of Management (MCMI)
Associate of the Institute of Financial Accountants (AFA)
Fellow of the Institute of Directors (FIOD)
Holder of the IoD Diploma in Company Direction (Salford University and the Isle of Man International Business School).

I have wide experience at board level in large and small companies and was a senior elected member of a large UK local authority. I have been a member of a number of NGO boards currently including the EU Objective 2 and Objective 3 Program Monitoring Committees.

Dr James Green’s Specialties:

I have a wide experience in business and finance and a well-developed network of contacts. I have experience in report writing and communications. As a Chartered Director I believe that my professional skills as a director covering effective marketing strategies, leading strategic change, HR issues, strategic business direction, finance, company law, and board development is an asset to any type of company.

Dr James Green’s Experience

  • James Green & Co

    (Financial Services industry)

    June 2005 — Present (3 years 11 months)

    James Green & Co offer international corporate support services and international tax planning for large and small businesses. The business is under my direct control and as a Chartered Director clients know that I have proven my knowledge and experience at the highest level.

    My skills as a Chartered Director are not industry specific and a typical project sees me reviewing the strategy and corporate governance of a company and advising on measures that should be taken to improve the make-up of the board and the structures that are needed in order that the board can fulfill its statutory role in setting strategy and monitoring outcomes.

    At that point I may introduce associates to act as non executives or consultants to actually take things forward.

    Recent and current clients include companies in the property, training and professional services sectors and several hedge fund managers.
