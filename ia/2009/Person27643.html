Beverly Harvey’s Summary

Beverly Harvey has been coaching senior managers and C-level executives in job search, career transition and career marketing strategies for 16 years.

Founder of HarveyCareers.com, Beverly is passionate about identifying her clients’ unique talents, crystallizing their brand, articulating their value proposition, and creating dynamic marketing materials and job search strategies to achieve a successful landing. She personally works with each client in all aspects of their career transition.

Beverly holds the industry's most esteemed credentials:
*Master Resume Writer
*Credentialed Career Manager
*Certified Career Management Coach
*Certified Job & Career Transition Coach
*Certified Professional Resume Writer
*Certified Personal Branding Strategist
*Certified Leadership & Talent Management Coach

Beverly is the author of a monthly newsletter, Career News & Trends, where she writes about job search strategies and tactics, career planning, and career management. To subscribe and immediately receive 7 feature articles, click here: http://www.harveycareers.com/subscribe.html
