Joe Berkowitz’s Summary

Veteran online and mobile media executive with an established entrepreneurial track record of business results including raising capital, building a business, and M&A transactions. A unique combination of business, product, and technical expertise.

These are interesting times for the media business with old
models no longer relevant given changing media consumption habits. As such, I Co-Founded and currently run a digital strategy and incubation business (Interactive Moxie.) In addition to building our own portfolio of digital businesses, we help partners make sense of the evolving landscape and surface the most viable strategic options.

Joe Berkowitz’s Specialties:

Growing online and wireless media businesses. Key areas of focus include overall business growth strategy, raising capital, mergers and acquisitions, technology strategy, product development/marketing/management, business development, and building result-driven teams.
