About Ross Allred

Ross Allred is Coldwater Creek’s Email Marketing Manager responsible for leading the company’s strategy, deployment and analysis of all promotional, transactional, and triggered email messages with its corresponding email deliverability. In over the first year, Ross helped increase the email marketing revenue by more than 30% with an overall Email Deliverability of 97% in the inbox. Ross has further developed behavioral based trigger emails with deeper segmentation resulting in a $30 million lift in annualized sales.

Prior to joining Coldwater Creek, Ross was contracted as an Email Marketing Manager for United Online (parent company of NetZero, Classmates.com, MySite) where he managed all email communications and email deliverability for MySite.com, FreeServers.com, 50megs.com and many others. Ross also lead the due diligence and acquisition of the email software, Lyris ListManager and developed and improved the complete email life cycle, including trigger campaigns, billing and transactional, promotional, and event-driven emails.

Before joining United Online, Ross managed MyFamily.com's (parent company of Ancestry.com and Genealogy.com) Internet Commerce Division responsible for Cross Selling products through the email, call center and online ad channels. Ross also developed email marketing strategies to promote online subscriptions, memberships, and commerce-related products (books, CDs, software, maps, etc.) for three different business units showing performance, testing results, and overall strategy while handling 2.5 head counts' responsibilities but still over-performing on each sales goal.
