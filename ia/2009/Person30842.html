Guy HR Bondonneau’s Summary

I run a web development agency, Bondonneau eSolutions (http://www.bondonneau-esolutions.com)

Our mission is to empower small and medium size enterprise (SME) grow with the Internet.

• Web site design and build
• E-commerce solutions
• Search Engine Optimisation – SEO
• Web site maintenance
• Hosting - Domain name registration

Some of the recent projects include:

• Lycée Français Charles de Gaulle, Londres (http://www.lyceefrancais.org.uk/)
• Fédération des Associations Françaises de Grande-Bretagne (http://www.fafgb.org/)
• CityScope Developments (http://www.cityscopedevelopments.com/)

All the members of my Team are top international professionals, specialist in each of the disciplines required to achieve the best possible solution to meet our client’s objectives.

• Councillor of the French Chamber of Commerce in Great-Britain
• Member of the E-business Program Professional Committee at Advancia-Negocia - Business School of the Paris Chamber of Commerce and Industry
• Member of the steering committee of The LEEG (The London Expatriate Entrepreneurs Group) the hub for all expatriate entrepreneurs in or starting a business in the UK

I was born and grew up in a multilingual and multicultural environment.

I have over 28 years experience in interior and furniture design. During that time I headed the design department of a top interior design house and leading groups, I consulted on major property development and looked after an international clientèle.

Prior to that, after studying civil engineering in Zürich and a stint as editor and in PR in London, I followed a career in banking with major institutions in the City of London followed by running a purchasing agency for the supply and installation to important development projects in the Middle-East.

In 2003 I turned to the Net as the field of activity of the future in which to apply my talents and started Bondonneau Design, now Bondonneau eSolutions.
