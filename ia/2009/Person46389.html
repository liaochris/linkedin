    January 2006 — Present (3 years 3 months)

    Carlos I. Rodriquez, Jr. was born and raised in Rochester, N.Y.

    I began dancing at the age of 15 in the ethnic salsa style known as Cuban. I then continued my studies in Modern Rhythmic Ballroom Dancing that originated in Latin America. I am an experienced Ballroom and Latin Dance Instructor for students in the Rochester area and have previously taught at prestigious local dance schools.

    My teaching experience includes Ballroom posture technique combined with Cuban/Latin American style.

    Additionally, I have recently become a certified Zumba Fitness instructor. Zumba is a Latin-inspired, dance-fitness program that incorporates Latin and international music.
