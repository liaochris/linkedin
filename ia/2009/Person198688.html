Rick Row’s Summary

In the face of the rapidly emerging twin global problems of global warming and conventional oil and gas production nearing a plateau, a key question for a business or local government is how to recognize these factors in its strategic plan.

Response to these issues can be strengthened by partnering with other organizations. My personal goal is to continue to contribute through facilitating private/private and private/public partnerships that create and exploit sustainable solutions. Collaboration will accelerate the diffusion of sustainable technologies and management practices as well as reduce non-intrinsic barriers to implementing change.

My professional experience includes 12 years in forming valuable partnerships for organizations, most recently for a non-profit dedicated to improving the sustainability and economic health of Silicon Valley and its companies and local governments. In this work, I have drawn on my extensive knowledge of energy technology, financial and securities analysis, management and marketing practices, public policy, innovation management, and human behavior; as well as my work in the mining, engineering, electric utility, semiconductor and software industries.

Rick Row’s Specialties:

Business development, analysis of industry-specific global environmental regulations impacts, relationship building, innovation and change management, organizing an environmental, health and safety voluntary industry initiative, win-win negotiation, management with sustainability in mind
