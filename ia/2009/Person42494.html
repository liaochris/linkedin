Lorna Selig’s Summary

The Children’s Link Society offers resources and support links to parents and families who have children with special needs. We assist parents to advocate on behalf of and in partnership with their special needs children (birth to 24years), we facilitate parent to parent contact and support, and we share a comprehensive listing of resources and service agencies for children with special needs and complex medical issues.

The Children’s Link Society is staffed by parents and family members who have a child with a disability and therefore have personal and hands on experience with systems and support needs. We promote unbiased information to encourage parents to make their own unique choices for support needs for their family. We offer support to multicultural families who are navigating systems and experience complications due to limited understanding of English. Our transition service assists youth and young adults who are in the process of shifting from children’s services to adult services.

We are a non-profit agency that is funded by two main core funders and several other smaller entities. We are always searching for ongoing sustainable funding.

Mission:
The Children's Link Society provides information and support links to families who have children and young adults with special needs. The society acts in liaison with communities, professionals and agencies that support people who have disabilities.

Check out our free Resource Documents for people who support children and young adults with disabilities on our website. There is some general information available and other more specific documents for the Calgary, Alberta area.
