Jeanne Hartman’s Summary

Preparation of professional entertainers, actors, corporate public speakers, writers who want to pitch, and people who want to use professional actor techniques to help them communicate better in their professional and private life.
Analysis of techniques for communication. Improvement of abilities to maintain better communication with co-workers and employees.
What I do best: I can find out why you REALLY don't communicate what you want to say. I can help you find out the real reasons you have trouble with nervousness, bad communication or just not reaching the level of performance you want to attain.
I love working with people preparing speeches, reports or any level of public speaking and acting performance.

Jeanne Hartman’s Specialties:

Training in communication, acting performances, public speaking preparation, analysis of scripts and characters, preparing for job interviews, auditions, negotiations. Teaching you how to research , prepare and practice in a way that supports your goals.
