Jake Calabrese, CBAP, CSM’s Summary

My expertise and experience is in improving businesses by optimizing business processes, leveraging intelligent technology investments, and ensuring that at the end of the day the most important needs are met – the needs of the stakeholders – customers, users, leaders, and the organization.

Solid solutions start with communicating with customers to understand their needs. Whether working directly with customers or leading a team, getting the most from a project requires a clear understanding of the value it will provide. What will success look like? Does the project align with organizational goals?

■ A bit about “magic technology beans” – throwing technology at a problem NEVER makes sense. Obvious perhaps, but it is done far too often.
■ Straightforward communication is always the best approach – sometimes the news is not ”perfect” – but at least you know where you stand and can take the appropriate actions!
