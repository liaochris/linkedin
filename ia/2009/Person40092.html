Antigen Realty’s Summary

Antigen Realty has established itself as the Sports and Entertainment world's leading provider of premium real estate services.

Headquartered in Miami's star-studded South Beach, in the heart of South Florida (the sports and entertainment capital of the world), Antigen Realty is a full service real estate brokerage firm and relocation company, which specializes in assisting high profile and high net worth individuals in their real estate transactions (buying, renting, selling, and investing), from first-time home buyers to savvy luxury property investors, throughout the United States and Canada, as well as around the world, including:

Professional Athletes; Actors & Actresses; Agents, Advisors & Business Managers; Coaches & Managers; Musicians; Media Personalities; Sports Executives; Rock, Country Music & Rap Stars; Celebrities; Team Owners; Film, TV & Music Executives; and Other high net worth individuals & VIPs.

By delivering a stellar combination of extensive experience; comprehensive market knowledge; discreet, confidential, and exceptional personal service; and superior negotiation skills, Antigen Realty and its exclusive global network of the best real estate developers, brokers, REALTORS®, and estate agents (the Antigen Realty Network™) have teamed together to build an unparalleled roster of celebrity clientele, comprised of more than 2,000 of the biggest names in sports and entertainment worldwide.

Don't wait until you are about to move. Call or e-mail now for more information, and enjoy the premium real estate services from which so many of sport's and entertainment's most famous and accomplished have benefited.
