Mark Walker’s Summary

I have very good experience within the Automation Industry, both Industrial Automation and Home Automation

Industrial Automation covers a wide range of Industries including Pharmaceutical, Biopharma, Brewery, Automotive, Water Treatment, Airport Baggage tracking conveyor system and other manufacturing plants.
I am used to working in all areas of the project including, System Design, Software design and Coding, Commissioning and fault finding. This also includes the various levels of documentation and validation.
I have good security clearance and work within the usual qualification procedures such as GMP
I have in depth experience of DCS, PLC and SCADA systems as well as various simulators and other PC based applications

Home Automation
I am qualified C-bus Integrator and have completed several C-bus installations, from the design stage through to Commissioning.
I have also worked on other home automation systems, including X10, Russound, Universal Remotes, Sonos whole house Audio to name a few.

Property Investment
We are now starting to ramp up the Property investment side of things.
We currently have property in the UK, Poland and India. We are now concentrating on the UK side of things and also looking at doing some refurbs
