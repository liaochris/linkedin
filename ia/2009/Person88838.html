Graham Brenna’s Summary

I am the Technical Arts Director at Our Saviour’s Lutheran Church where I encourage the use of communications technology and social media in particular, as a tool to be embraced in the workplace and beyond.

I joined the team at Our Saviour’s in 2003 upon graduating from high school. While growing what started as a small part time position into a full time career, I attended North Central College and earned a B.A. in Broadcast Communications.

I enjoy skiing in Colorado and really anywhere where there’s a ski slope, road biking along Lake Michigan in Chicago and hanging out with friends. If I can hang out with friends and do the other two… so much the better!
