Brice Dunwoodie’s Summary

Brice is a technologist focused on electronic content management, knowledge and collaboration management and public-facing web applications. Specializations include Microsoft .NET architectures and integration; marketing-driven initiatives such as community building, user experience optimization, content optimization and strategic analytics.

He currently manages Cylogy, Inc. -- an international technical consulting firm, and Simpler Media Group. Simpler Media publishes CMSWire.com, a web magazine covering the content management and publishing sector.

Brice Dunwoodie’s Specialties:

technical content management, collaboration and publishing strategies, strategic marketing technologies, outsourced application development
