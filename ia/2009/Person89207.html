Julie Giles’s Summary

Julie is the Chief Madhatter at GreenHAT Digital and an experienced marketer, publicist, and producer. She has successfully channeled her creativity and curiosity into the wide, wide world of digital marketing where she has focused on delivering simple yet effective solutions that help people and companies get found.

Julie began her professional career in the arts & entertainment sector, where she promoted hundreds of live events, including ground-breaking tours by The Rolling Stones, David Bowie and others.

Online since 1994, Julie was one of the first Canadian publicists to use Websites, forums, message boards and other pre-social media applications to quickly build and grow a strong fan base for her music, film and broadcasting clients.

Julie has a BA in Drama from Brock University (St. Catharines) and a Certificate in Performing Arts Management from Confederation College (Thunder Bay) and is a member of Interactive Ontario and Women in Film & Television (Toronto).

In her spare time, she makes documentary films with her brother, Jim. Their first feature, La Dany (http://ladany.com), will hit the festival circuit this fall.
