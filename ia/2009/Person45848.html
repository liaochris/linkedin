Chuck Camp’s Summary

Chuck is an Operations Executive and Change Agent with more than 25 years of international experience in strategic planning, business restructuring, significant process improvements, and turnarounds of Class I, II, and III medical device and medical product businesses using Lean, Lean Office, and Six Sigma. He has held leadership positions and managed multiple facilities on a global basis in companies ranging in size from small to Fortune 100. His background includes positions as Vice President of Global Operations, Director of Manufacturing Engineering Development, Director of Manufacturing, Director of Materials & Logistics, R&D Supervisor, Plant Manager and Acting General Manager. He has proven abilities in creating and translating Corporate Vision, Mission, and Strategic Direction into an Operational Reality through: reengineering of product lines, production processes, and facilities; operational restructuring; improving productivity; increasing shareholder value; and creating a positive and progressive employee work environments.

Chuck has a BSEE (3.45 GPA) from the Univ. of TN at Chattanooga and completed coursework on an International MBA (4.0 GPA) at the Univ. of St. Thomas in St. Paul, MN.

Chuck Camp’s Specialties:
