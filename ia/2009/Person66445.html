Douglas A. Bard’s Summary

I graduated from Bentley College in 1994 with a degree in Marketing and over the last 15 years, have built a very successful career in sales and marketing. From selling enterprise-wide IT solutions, software and hardware, to online advertising sales with ad-serving pioneer DoubleClick and media giant CBS Sportsline, I have consistently been a top-performer selling mostly into C-level individuals. My work experience has helped me to build a fantastic professional network and has also enabled me to develop a comprehensive grasp of the technologies relevant in today's business world. Simply, I am a person who enjoys and thrives on working for innovative and forward-thinking organizations. Most recently, I have been immersed in the process of developing and marketing a newly patented device. STOP-A-SPILL™ (www.stopaspill.com), the name by which the device is trademarked, is a new home and office product that prevents desktop spills while doubling as a powerful promotional tool and has applicability on every desktop, tabletop and work-surface around the world.
