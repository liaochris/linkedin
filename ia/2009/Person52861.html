Dan Collins’s Summary

Brevity being the key...even more succinct profile here now:

An excellent combination of marketing knowledge from the client-side, large global network marketing agencies & Big 4 consulting prowess -- based around the digital & CRM spheres.

Developed global strategies for a number of Fortune 500 companies -- and then actioned those strategies to result in positive results through a number of successful projects. Have a wide variety of tested & true strategic frameworks to help achieve excellent results within a actionable structure in a short period of time (without wasting time & effort in useless "ramp up time.").

I'm great at:

-- Working on international clients across a multitude of global regions (US/LATAM/EMEA/APAC) in a variety of verticals (FMCG, technology, telecom, automotive, entertainment & media, etc.)

-- Understanding not only the marketing aspects of the company, but the business implications of proposed changes and how to maximize the ability to both make money and save operational costs -- critical requirements in these days of the credit crunch.

-- I specialize in CRM & digital -- helping large companies connect to their customers (to find out how to sell to them, when they want to buy, what they want in the product, what price they are willing to pay, what they are looking for, how to sell to them again in the future, how to keep them happy, etc.)

My current clients here in London range from international videogame companies to global FMCG to telecom to financial services.

Former clients include Microsoft, Hewlett-Packard, P&G, Fiat, Diageo, McDonald's, Ubisoft, Samsung, Kraft, Coca-Cola, Warner Brothers, AOL, BellSouth, Deloitte Consulting, MSN, American Express, Virgin Music, The Cartoon Network, etc.

Drop me a line and let's discuss how I can help your company achieve better results at a fraction of the price -- making your budget more effective.
