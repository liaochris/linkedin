Peter Eisenman’s Summary

Peter Eisenman currently works in the marketing and promotions group at Turner Sports and Entertainment Digital. Specifically, he is responsible for developing custom advertising programs on Turner’s Golf properties, which include PGATour.com, PGA.com and Yahoo!Golf. He also creates sales material for the sales teams to use in their pitches to advertising agencies.

Prior to joining Turner, he was responsible for supervising the digital media strategies for Allstate’s direct response campaigns at Neo@Ogilvy, Ogilvy’s digital media division. Peter has worked on Sears, Six Flags, Kodak and Allstate auto insurance. He worked on a multitude of new business pitches, which include; CEC, METLife, Nestle and Kaplan. As part of Neo’s social media expert group and Task Force he has shown a strong background in social media and new technologies and how they can be applicable to his clients.

Peter lives New York City. While at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, he received his honors degree in Journalism and Mass Communications.
