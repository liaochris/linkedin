Christopher Whittington MBA PMP’s Summary

Experienced IT Product and Services practitioner providing Enterprise Information Technology solutions that provide reliability, actionable insight and organizational agility. Chris brings deep insight and broad understanding to the application and management of information technology within the modern enterprise. He has delivered several strategic process and technology programs that align operational capability, cost management and revenue enhancement through a deep understanding of issues and opportunities.
