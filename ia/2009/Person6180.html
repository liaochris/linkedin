Alan Dunn-Birch’s Summary

An influential and experienced Service, Operations and Supply Chain Director with an outstanding track record of delivering profit improvement in blue chip D2C and B2B businesses. Experience includes large-scale operations, supply chain and logistics, call-centre management, project management, acquisition and post-acquisition management, process re-engineering and change management. Experienced in the retail, media, insurance and service sectors.
