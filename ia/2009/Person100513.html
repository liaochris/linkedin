    One Video Platform, Three Video Experiences

    IVideo Corporation is the industry leader in advanced compression technology, with broad applications for cellphone video, laptop video and IPTV. Because IVideo's bitrate is less than half of old technologies, video delivery and customer reach have become profitable for the first time in the industry. Built into advanced IPTV and streaming media solutions, IVideo's KS.530 codec outperforms H.264 by well over 100%.

    IVideo's compression solutions enable owners and distributors of video to deliver high definition video at 3 Mbps peak bitrate and standard definition video at 750kbps peak bitrate. The same technology delivers cellphone solutions at 110kbps and full-screen streaming applications at or below 650kbps.

    IVideo KS.530 compression technology is integrated into realtime encoders, upgradeable set-top boxes and applications for streaming media to the cellphone and laptop. The KS.530 compression technology offers solutions for delivering interactive digital television and on demand video utilizing private networks for IPTV or the public internet for Over the Top Video applications.
