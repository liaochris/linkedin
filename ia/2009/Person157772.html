Michele Boyko’s Summary

Experienced in developing and leading results-oriented marketing, communications and training departments at major financial services, telecommunications and high tech companies. Strong company representative and trusted advisor to senior management.

Marketing and Communications achievements over the past 15 plus years encompasses:

 Established new marketing and communications programs to reflect changes following a major acquisition. Target objectives included new branding, group identity, product redesign, culture shift and to build community awareness. Resulted in increased attention in industry publications, media coverage and customer awareness. Increased market share for year over year sales growth.

 Strategic development and management of firm program marketing plans to increase market share and drive sales in a volatile market. Created sales themes and focus campaigns to target results for specific products. Reduced printing and mailing costs by 40% in 2008 by converting to email, internet and intranet communications.

 Key liaison for major sports sponsorships. Developed promotional campaigns, advertisements, and sales incentives to leverage events. On-site management to ensure event resulted in positive national and local exposure to increase consumer awareness. Event proceeds benefited charities.

 Concept/content developer and ghostwriter for company president and senior leaders on speeches, articles, PowerPoint presentations, customer and employee communications. Served as primary proofreader/editor of marketing and communication materials.
