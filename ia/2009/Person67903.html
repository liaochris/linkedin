Digvijay Lamba’s Summary

My experience is in designing algorithms at various levels.

Currently I lead the categorization team at Kosmix and am working on algorithms related with search and information technologies like query categorization, document categorization ,search quality, intelligent query parsing, statistical classification/clustering techniques, automated taxonomy and ontology building, indexing etc.

I have earlier worked with disk drivers, large arrays, multipathing accross disks, load balancing, volume management and related problems.

I have also trained in and worked with industry standardization efforts including SNIA in CIM WBEM technologies and in development of standardized APIs and Models for wider use. Development of XML API's for inter company communication is one of my current roles
