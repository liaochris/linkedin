George Main’s Summary

Founded and participating in local community organizations in the immigration, health care, and political action areas.

Law practice experience on all phases of immigration law; filed petitions and applications with DOL ETA, INS, and CIS; filed appeals for clients before EOIR, BIA, and AAU.

Handled immigrant and nonimmigrant administrative filings from inception to decision; managed all phases of hearings before EOIR, from pleas to the NTA through appeals to the BIA; briefed immigrant and nonimmigrant appellate cases.

Supervised junior associates and paralegals, counseled and advocated for litigant groups, and settled a high-profile denaturalization case; managed immigration cases for Intel Corporation, Samsung America, and Matsushita group corporations.
