Giselle Conyette’s Summary

As a technical communicator, I help clients produce excellent content that is easy to read, understand and use while facing challenges such as:

- Their target audience is frustrated with the content because it's difficult to read and understand.

- They are in a last minute panic to get the documentation done to meet a looming deadline.

- Their content is all over the place and they need to pull it together in a nice, neat package.

- They are stressed out because they have no content at all, and they just don’t have the time to produce it.

If any of your colleagues or clients need to produce excellent content while facing these challenges, feel free to forward my profile to them.
