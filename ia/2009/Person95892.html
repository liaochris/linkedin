    Howard G. Frederick is a partner of Silbowitz, Garafola, Silbowitz, Schatz & Frederick, L.L.P. and is a seasoned trial attorney who has achieved million dollar verdicts and settlements on cases severely undervalued by insurance carriers and defense attorneys. He is the consummate Plaintiff's trial attorney who is committed to his client's causes and is tireless in seeking awards that truly represent the kinds of significant recoveries to which they are entitled. His signature results have been featured in The New York Law Journal, Newsday, and the Poughkeepsie Journal.
