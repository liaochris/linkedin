Ramin Ron Hariri’s Summary

For past six years I have been focusing on running a private investment and holding corporation I founded. Operating in private equity investments in real estate and mortgage instruments, developed proprietary business process software, leading business functions in marketing, sales, operations and finance, with particular attention to client management functions of the business. Produced consistent double digit revenue growth rate year after year.
