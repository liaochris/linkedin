Kevin Tan’s Summary

As with many people looking to control their financial future, I FAILED constantly at building my network marketing business. I was completely shut down by friends and family and wasted countless hours following "successful" network marketers looking to take my money.

This trend continued until I finally learned how to provide the value people needed and closed the door on "sales pitches". I realized that I was simply networking with people and not network marketing with people.

Once I began to set myself apart from the mainstream of marketers and brand myself as a person of value, my business instantly picked up momentum and hasn't stopped since. I have opened the doors to BizSwagger.com to share my tactics and aid struggling marketers in their journey to success.
