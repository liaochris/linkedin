Sharon Doherty’s Summary

I have over 18 years in blue chip organisations with experience of working in Europe, America, Asia and the Middle East in senior people and change roles. I am currently the Group HR and Organisational Effectiveness Director for Laing O'Rourke, an international engineering solutions provider.

I have enjoyed moving industries retail, financial services, professional services, construction and aviation.

My career has allowed me to have exposure early on to operational management moving then into senior HR generalist roles, some exposure to consultancy and project management and in the last five years.

In addition to my Corporate career I've had the opportunity to get involved in a Non-Executive role with Regents College, write a book about "Heathrow's Terminal 5' and work with colleagues to start a people and change consultancy, 'ThinQ Differently' and an internet media company, 'ThinQ Digital Media'.
