Rabia Rahimbayeva’s Summary

As I'm structurally a person always focused on success, diversity and leadership, I chose Public Administration to study at University.
Giving private English lessons to secondary and high school students and working at different job-positions in tourism sector every summer during my student years, I tried to develop my personal skills, be independent financially and improve my language&communication skills.
Making studies on USA's Greater Middle East Project for my Masters Thesis, I focused specially on economic, political, cultural and social relations between Turkey and Turkic Countries. After finishing M.A. Program I've been working as Educator at private courses for open Univ. and Sivil Servants Students for 2 Years; I took Writer mission in a Publishing Commitee of one of the most well-known Publishing Houses of Educational Matherials between 2006-07.
In Jan 2007, I've started for my new work at "Export Sales" area which was my career goal and area to develop and improve myself. I started working for "Klimasan A.S." a part of Senocak Holding.
After work as Central Asia Area Sales Representative for 15 months, I've been promoted as an Export Sales Director and I'm still working on this position. During my 2,5 year work for Klimasan A.S. I've visited several actual and potential customers in different countries, managed processes as increasing-activation of sales, creation of distributing channels, strengthening after-sales services, following tender process of such global brands as Coca-Cola, Pepsi, Inbev, Heineken, Miller, Baltika, Nestle, Unilever etc. and after contracting, defining and developing the product, providing production and delivery of goods at needed time to needed place.
Partially with impact of sales of Control Packet of Klimasan A.S. Shares to Brasilian "Metalfrio Solutions S/A" in April 2008, start of reorganizational process and changes in company's strategies, now I'm open to new offers...
