Ronn Schrader’s Summary

•Quality - Accomplished background in planning and executing QMS plans. Highly developed skills and experience in cultivating quality initiatives. Equally strong in the development of COQ programs, Six Sigma Lean applications, and continuous improvement projects.

•ISO - Advanced knowledge of implementation and the auditing skills required to achieve and maintain ISO certifications. Certified auditor and champion of ISO programs.
