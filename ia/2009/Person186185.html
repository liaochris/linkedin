Peter Tyrrell’s Summary

> 30 years experience in the Information Technology industry, particularly in the Financial Services sector.

Excellent communication skills allow him to work with people to understand their objectives and formulate an appropriate business solution to achieve these objectives.

"Can do" attitude and vast experience, Peter provides a much sought-after combination of business, technical and people skills. Comfortable working alone, in a team or as a manager, he offers his employer a flexible, valuable resource.

AMP (1990-1998) he held several architectural roles, culminating in his appointment to Corporate Architect – responsible for leading AMP’s architects across the various projects being developed.

General Manager, Asia Pacific - CommerceQuest 1999-2001, established the Asia Pacific branch from the ground up and, when he left CommerceQuest in March 2001, it was turning a multi-million dollar profit. CommerceQuest’s revenue was mainly derived from their pre-eminent position in Australia in the provision of Middleware consulting services. Recognised by IBM as their “go to” company in this space.

Accepted a consulting role with AMP UK (2001-2002) leading the architecture team through a number of important projects.

KAZ Group (2002-2006) as a Client Services Manager. Promoted to Business Unit Manager, Commercial at KAZ Technology Services where he was responsible for managing a team of Client Service Managers and to achieve the team’s target revenue.

January 2005, Peter was asked by Peter Kazacos (Executive Director of KAZ Group) to consider moving into the Solutions & Innovations area of KAZ Group to take on the role of General Manager.

With a re-organisation within KAZ in October 2005, Peter was moved into the role of Business Partner Manager. This role is responsible for working closely with KAZ’s major strategic partners to determine yearly targets, inter-company ventures and specific campaigns, certifications, etc.
