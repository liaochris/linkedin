Derek Murphy’s Summary

Accomplished media executive with over fifteen years of experience developing and implementing traditional and digital media partnerships in the financial services, entertainment, sports, news and information markets.

Recognized for leading creative advertising and media agencies and cross functional teams to develop compelling and sustainable go to market strategies and innovative campaigns. Action oriented team player with strong operational and problem solving skills. Solid track record of integrating technology, content, media and marketing to develop innovative client solutions.
