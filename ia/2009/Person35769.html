Burcu Dogan’s Summary

I am a change oriented engineer who are seeking for technical challenge to change this world to the better. I'm mainly interested in small start-ups who wants to make impact on daily human life.

I'm looking for "challenge", excited engineers with strong computer science background, people who believe they can change the world, global concepts & products and people who care for UX.
