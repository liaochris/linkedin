Angela Cruz’s Summary

As a Marketing Consultant, I champion The Business Store's philosophy of zero-based marketing, providing innovative solutions which leverage existing assets and relationships to help clients achieve the best out of every marketing dollar.

I am interested in working with ambitious professionals who are looking for marketing solutions to take their business to the next level.

My commitment to excellence is reflected in a strong academic background, which includes a Master of Commerce (First Class Honours) in Marketing and a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Auckland. Graduated top of the class in both Marketing and Film, Television, and Media Studies and is a member of the International Honor Society, Beta Gamma Sigma. My work has appeared in top publications including Advances in Consumer Research and the European Journal of Marketing.
