Nuno Matos’s Summary

Relevant wide experience auditing life and P&C insurance companies, pension funds and investment banks. Specialist in IFRS/IAS and US GAAP. Also strong knowledge about Portuguese, Spanish, UK, French and Angolan GAAPs. Knowledgeable about the regulations issued by: ISP (Portuguese Insurance Regulator), DGSyFP (Spanish Insurance Regulator), FSA (British Financial Services Regulator) and ISS (Angolan Insurance Regulator). Also great experience in M&A, IPO and financial due diligences. Also expert in capital markets, structured products, derivatives and actuarial subjects (namely EEV/MCEV reporting and ALM). Also relevant experience in Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) projects, with particular focus on Solvency II. Has also solid experience in SARBOX and forensic projects. Frequent speaker at various seminars both for staff and for clients. Former professor of advanced accounting, corporate finance, auditing and taxation (at Lusíada, Internacional and Lusófona Universities, all in Lisbon, Portugal). In addition, also responsible for a number of publications on insurance accounting and risk management issues in Portugal and Spain. Holds the ACA/CPA qualification (ROC in Portugal).
Native in Portuguese. Fluent in English and Spanish, written and spoken.
