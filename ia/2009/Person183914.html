Paul Winer’s Summary

Paul is an operational and information technology leader for the consumer products supply chain. He has nearly two decades of success driving technical and non-technical people and resources together to create dramatic results with start-ups and fortune 50 companies. Paul is recognized for building team consensus while driving for deadlines and high quality. He effectively leads in multiple industries and technologies from consumer products retailing and manufacturing, software and hardware products to re-engineering processes in warehouses, assembly lines, merchandising, product development and labor improvement. He has lead electronic commerce, EDI, warehouse logistics, forecasting, replenishment, sales tracking and merchandise planning projects. Paul is also recognized as an engaging public speaker at multiple national and local conferences.

Paul Winer’s Specialties:

Consumer products supply chain, process re-engineering, warehousing, assembly lines, retail technology, logistics, small businesses, start-ups, technology implementation.
