Robert Bear’s Summary

I am a Property and Casualty Consulting Actuary, Reinsurance Consultant and Insurance Arbitrator in the actuarial consulting firm that I have established, RAB Actuarial Solutions LLC. I am a Fellow of the Casualty Actuarial Society, an ARIAS-U.S. certified insurance and reinsurance arbitrator, a Chartered Property Casualty Underwriter, a member of the American Academy of Actuaries, and a Fellow in the Conference of Consulting Actuaries. I have earned MS degrees in both theoretical and applied mathematics, as well as in economic systems.

I am a Property and Casualty Actuarial Consultant with over 33 years of insurance industry experience, including 20 years managing reinsurance actuarial services. In my actuarial consulting practice, I have performed loss reserve studies and reinsurance pricing analyses, served as an insurance arbitrator and an actuarial expert witness in insurance and reinsurance arbitrations, and provided litigation and investor support.

I previously served as Senior Vice President and Chief Actuary of PXRE Group, where I managed the Actuarial Department and served as the Appointed Actuary for the companies within the group. I was responsible for loss reserving functions and pricing model development, along with related corporate modeling. I began my career at Insurance Services Office and subsequently served as an actuarial manager at Prudential Reinsurance, Signet Star Reinsurance and SCOR Reinsurance Company.

My service to the actuarial profession has included terms as Chairperson of the RAA Actuarial Committee and as President of Casualty Actuaries in Reinsurance. I currently serve as co-chairperson of the CAS Loss Simulation Model Working Party and Vice Chairperson of the CAS Dynamic Risk Modeling Committee.

Email me at rabsolutions@gmail.com or call 1-973-229-4465.

Robert Bear’s Specialties:

RAB Actuarial Solutions LLC is a Property and Casualty actuarial and reinsurance consulting firm based in New Jersey that is focused on providing quality loss reserving, actuarial and reinsurance expert witness, arbitration, excess and reinsurance pricing, reinsurance commutation and risk modeling services. The firm supports insurers, self-insurers, reinsurers, brokers and investors.
