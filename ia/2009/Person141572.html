Lindsey Pollak’s Summary

Hi! I am a bestselling author, speaker and blogger specializing in Gen Y career and workplace issues.

I recently wrote a book called "Getting from College to Career: 90 Things to Do Before You Join the Real World" (http://tinyurl.com/5bloav), which is the guidebook I wish I'd had when I graduated from college. I also write a top-rated blog, the Lindsey Pollak Career Blog (http://www.lindseypollak.com/blog). Please subscribe for ongoing career advice, job listings and resources!

In December 2008, I became the Career Contributor for ABC News on Campus (http://www.abcnewsoncampus.com), where I write career advice articles and answer viewer/reader questions.

My advice and opinions have appeared in such media outlets as The New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Washington Post, Boston Globe, CNN.com, National Public Radio and ABC News America This Morning.

I'm also professional speaker (http://www.lindseypollak.com/speaking), delivering keynote speeches and workshop presentations at universities, conferences and corporations around the country. My audiences have included Yale, Harvard, Columbia, Penn, Amherst, Duke, MIT, Smith, University of Michigan, University of Texas, USC, Bliss, Fidelity Investments, Merrill Lynch, MTV Networks, Time Inc. and many others.

I graduated from Yale University in 1996 and received a Rotary Ambassadorial Scholarship to Melbourne, Australia, where I completed a master’s degree in women’s studies.

I look forward to connecting!
