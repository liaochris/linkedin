Fabiola Fleuranvil, MBA’s Summary

As CEO of two marketing & public relations firms, N.Y.L.A. Entertainment Group & Blueprint Creative Group, Fabiola Fleuranvil is an entrepreneurial mogul in the making. Both agencies provide marketing, PR, project management, and business consulting services. N.Y.L.A. Entertainment Group serves the fashion, music, and entertainment sectors while Blueprint Creative Group serves non profits, consumer brands, small businesses, and real estate developers.

Her expertise lends itself in creating strategic marketing plans focused on strengthening a brand's positioning. Her business formula is vital to the successful implementation of a strong communications plan & relies on a spiral approach using various media platforms to complete the entire branding landscape. At the end of the day, her passion, diligence, and resourcefulness is her claim to fame, and her clients, colleagues, and business partners can attest to that.

Besides the two agencies, her entrepreneurial successes are even more evident in her other businesses. She currently serves as a Realtor with Re/Max Marketplace (Atlanta) with specialties in the foreclosure and investor markets. In Spring 2004, she established an online women's shoe store, Fabulous Feet, with a new branding relaunch set for Fall 2008. Most recently, she's started a print brokerage business to serve governmental print needs.

She's a recipient of a Master's of Business Administration Degree (MBA) in Marketing from Clark Atlanta University and a B.S. in Business Administration from Florida A&M University.

Despite these incredible credentials from a woman still under the age of 30, there's a lot more in the universe for her. There's still more business ideas circulating thru her head, more people to serve, and a community that needs her passion and intelligence.

"Entrepreneurship is living a few years of your life like most people won't, so that you can spend the rest of your life like most people can't."
