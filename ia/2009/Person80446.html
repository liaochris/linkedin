Frank Pazera, CPA, MBA’s Summary

Frank is an experienced business executive with more than twenty-five years of financial and operational management expertise in a wide array of sectors. He has a strong background in back office operations, mergers and acquisitions, venture capital transactions, SEC reporting, SOX compliance, financial reporting and managing troubled companies.

Frank’s industry experience includes media, music, entertainment, healthcare, publishing, direct marketing, mobile marketing, wireless communications, data, telecom & voip services, real estate, office furniture, retail, business process outsourcing, software development, manufacturing and professional consulting services.

Frank’s previous roles include CEO, COO, CFO, Director of Business Development, Controller and other Senior Management positions.

As a TechCFO Partner, he serves as Chief Financial Officer for a number of privately funded technology companies, providing on-demand expert strategy and guidance to help successfully transform these clients with precision and speed.

He is an active member of several professional organizations including the Nashville Technology Council (NTC), American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA), Association for Corporate Growth (ACG), Chattanooga Technology Council (CTC) and the Technology Association of Georgia (TAG) and National Association of Realtors (NAR).

His community and non-profit involvement includes the Chattanooga Community Kitchen, March of Dimes, Junior Achievement, former board member of the Signal Centers and former local Chairman of the Life Skills Training Center for the Saint Vincent DePaul Society.

Frank earned his MBA from the Goizueta Business School at Emory University with a concentration on Finance and Entrepreneurial Studies, and his BBA degree is in Accounting from the University of Wisconsin, Milwaukee.
