Kim DeCoste’s Summary

DeCoste & Associates is a Professional Development Consultancy as well as a Technical and Excecutive Search Firm. We work with clients to help create and deploy professional development services and, if needed, can help with recruitment and placement. The company has a back-to-basics and highly personalized approach to each project. The company's emphasis is shifting from traditional technology to clean technology as we find more opportunities in the sustainable arena. We are focused in such areas as e-learning/training, and some of the newer sustainable energy technologies, where technology is transforming the way work is done. If you have skills in these areas, please get in touch with us to see how we can help you move your career or your business forward.

Previously I worked for Jones Knowledge as a creator and the implementor of the JIU Total Professional, the University's Career Service offerings. I have a solid understanding of how technology can be leveraged for highly individualized professional development.I also earned an MBA at JIU in 2005 in e-commerce

Previously I was a Senior Recruiter in Information Technology in Denver and in the Los Angeles area.

My long-term professional interests remain focused on professional development, professional services, strategic management and professional networking.
