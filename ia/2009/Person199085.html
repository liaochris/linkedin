Paul Wesselmann’s Summary

I live in Madison, Wisconsin and travel around the U.S. to hang out with all kinds of organizations to help energize individuals and teams.

I also nurture The Ripples Project, an experiment in encouraging kindness that involves over 19,000 members who receive an inspirational email each Monday morning (the most enriching part of my life is creating Ripples!!) One of my primary goals here at LinkedIn is to spread the word: http://TheRipplesProject.org

My other adventures include having been a wellness center coordinator, support group facilitator, residence hall manager, volunteer coordinator, radio talk show host, and a college mascot.
