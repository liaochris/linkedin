SURESH PATEL’s Summary

Suresh is a British Citizen and was born in London, UK.

Educated in London and moved to Central Europe (Munich/Vienna/Warsaw) shortly after the Berlin wall came down and the reunification in 1990. Suresh works throughout Europe and frequently travels back to London at weekends.

With a unique ability to understand complex Business, Technology, Business Development and Private Equity markets Suresh has worked for leaders such as Apple, Lotus (IBM), Netscape (SUN & AOL) and Compaq/Digital/Tandem (HP). In addition Suresh started ventures in the UK, Germany and Poland.

Suresh enjoys international business challenges and travel to new and old markets for business development.
