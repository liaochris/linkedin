John Marshall’s Summary

I am an artist, designer and curator with a background that encompasses industrial design and manufacture, architectural collaborations and fine art practice.

John Marshall’s Specialties:

CAD/CAM, rapid prototyping, 3D modelling/visualization, artist/architect collaborations, curation.
