  • Group Head of Finance

    Channelfly plc now Mama group plc

    (Entertainment industry)

    October 2003 — September 2004 (1 year)

    Channelfly PLC group’s principal activity is the promotion of new music through a variety of media platforms and talent management. It also has interests in a number of areas of the music industry including magazine publishing, radio programming, producer and artist management and recordings. The Group's businesses include Barfly Club, The Fly Magazine, Radio Operations and Promotions. The Barfly Club network, operates live music venues across the UK which also include the Hammersmith Apollo, Jazz Café, Forum and Borderline with interest in the Lovebox, Great Escape, Global gathering festivals. The Management and artist services division, manages over 100 record producers, programmers, songwriters, engineers and mixers with investments in recording businesses. Channelfly is currently under the MAMA Group’s stable of assets which include Music and Media solutions responsible for all brand partnership activity and recently acquired Nettwerk Music Group and Angel Music Group.
