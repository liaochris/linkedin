Tad Jones’s Summary

My wife, Beth and I co-founded Aunahil, LLC in 1995 out of desire to support and promote moral and ethical values through various forms of entertainment. Aunahil has produced, written, and/or directed over 75 custom designed theatrical productions, fund raising events, and educational conferences. We have worked with high profile business and international government leaders, educators, celebrity entertainers, orchestras, musicians, choreographers, and thousands of American youths. Venues include: Kennedy Center, Constitution Hall, Lincoln Center, multiple NYC theatres, Orchestra Hall Chicago.

Lawrence on Arabia (LOA) - is an innovative way to bridge the gap to a better understanding of current East / West cultures that incorporates history, adventure, and entertainment while teaching valuable lessons. LOA involves an 1,100 mile trek partner Miles Spencer and I took from Jeddah to Damascus that follows the footsteps of Arab independence as told by T. E. Lawrence (of Arabia) in his “Seven Pillars of Wisdom”. LOA II 8 man multi-national team is sched for spring 2009.

West End/Broadway Production: LOA dosen't stop in Damascus. . . we are bringing Lawrence of Arabia back to life in a new play entitled, “A Line in the Sand” with Winston Churchill, Gertrude Bell, Emir Faisal bin Hussein and others who drew the lines in the sand following WWI 1918-1921 that created today’s Middle East. Playwrights are acclaimed writer/director Mick Gordon and philosopher/writer A.C. Grayling. Our producing team is led by multi-Tony award producer Nelle Nugent. London opening 2008/09 season.
