    me-mo is a mobile content delivery platform that enables the delivery/sharing of any content (photos, videos, music) from PC or Web to any mobile device on any network. me-mo optimises the content on the fly to ensure the best consumer experience regardless of the phone or network used by the consumer.

    Our platform will do the following for your website:

    - Drive a new revenue stream by enabling you to monetise content from your site that is optimised and sent to a mobile phone.

    - Drive a new advertising revenue stream by allowing for the insertion of relevant targeted advertising (banners, pre and post-roll messages, watermarking) for more sophisticated operations.

    - Enhance the user experience by adding the ability for users to share photos, video and music in real time from your website to their own or their friends' mobile devices.

    As we operate a revenue share model with our partner sites, the integration process is very simple and investment overhead is very low. More often than not, this would involve simply tagging selected or all photo, videos and music on your site which then enables the user to send content to mobile straight from your website.
