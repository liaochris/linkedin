Dr Kem Thompson’s Summary

Dr Kem Thompson is a Success Coach for Women. She helps women create better, balanced and fulfilling lives for themselves. She is a qualified Performance Coach , who also teaches strategies and techniques that lead to Peak Performance in the Personal (including Health), Professional and Business arena.
She is the author of 'Personal Success Blueprints for Women', 'Business Success Blueprints for Women' and 'Seven Beliefs of Highly Successful Women'. Visit www.totalsuccessforwomen.com for details of these products.


Dr Thompson is also a Professional Health & Wellness Coach (as part of the Personal Development program), working in partnership with one of the leading Nutriceutical companies in the world (USANA Health Sciences) to help clients maintain lifestyle habits that lead to true health. She is especially passionate about helping children cultivate healthy lifestyle habits and runs free Wellness-For-Kids seminars to promote that message.


She is an accomplished author, having achieved Platinum Expert Author status in Ezine Articles.com as well as similar accolades in various other online venues.


Dr Thompson is a passionate speaker and trainer who delivers inspirational, motivating talks both live and virtually (teleconference, web conference) .


She lives in the United Kingdom with her family and is a medical doctor working in London.
