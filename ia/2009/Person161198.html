MICHAEL E. PARKER, Author of "Who Said So"’s Summary

Previously, I worked for Toyota, implementing Lean principles throughout the company’s North American operations. I frequently deliver seminars on Lean management to companies outside of the manufacturing arena and I am the founder of the Value-Centered Management Institute.

While working at Toyota Motor Manufacturing and General Motors, I was handpicked by Akio Toyoda the grandson of Toyota’s founder to receive specialized training on the Toyota Production System and Lean Management Philosophy from experts of TMC and OMCD in Japan, Canada, and throughout the United States.

I have successfully implemented lean solutions into service-oriented businesses and evolved to create a new management philosophy, Value-Centered Management™ which I introduced to the world through my book, “Who Said So? The Questions Revolutionary Businesses Ask That Make Them Successful”.
I am also an instructor with the University’s Continuing Education Program teaching classes in Just-in-Time systems.

    (Management Consulting industry)

    November 2004 — Present (5 years 1 month)

    Value-Centered Management is is a business management philosophy designed to help businesses focus all efforts on what their customers’ Value and then provide that value seamlessly through smooth flowing operations and a team member friendly Culture. Michael E. Parker created Value-Centered Management Institute (VCMI) as an educational vehicle that would give other businesses and entrepreneurs an opportunity to learn the benefits of the Value-Centered Management approach to doing business. VCMI is dedicated to the delivery of value-centered solutions and tools to allow our clients to gain insight on methodologies that will ultimately help decrease cost, increase productivity, improve business culture, and manage organizational change. Our mission is to provide knowledge and solutions through innovative technology, products and services, and a relentless commitment to coaching businesses into implementing Value-Centered Management in diverse working environments. Comprised of scores of highly motivated professionals, VCMI brings cutting edge business and life management information and coaching that will help to improve any business and the lives of those who work in them.
