Eric Druckenmiller’s Summary

About Eric:

* has over ten years of digital marketing experience working on some of the most well-known and valued brands in the world

* is respected for his understanding of communications strategy, marketing, digital media and what’s around the bend

Currently Eric helps manage PHD’s east coast digital portfolio, and is responsible for helping drive the strategic vision and direction for the department.

Prior to joining PHD, Eric worked as VP, Media at Deep Focus, having spent three years building the media discipline there and overseeing digital solutions for entertainment and lifestyle brands. Here he developed a track record for expanding existing business and helping win new clients. In 2008, Eric’s efforts contributed to the win of OMMA’s Best Media Planning and Buying Agency of the Year, as well as several Webby awards in 2007 and 2008.

Eric also holds a Masters from New York University in Environmental Conservation with an emphasis in journalism and philosophy, and continues to pursue ways to infuse a more pluralistic approach to marketing and communications by looking outside the industry for inspiration and insight.
