Eyal Lior’s Summary

I have BSc from Mercy college NY in Computer Sciences, 2 years of academic studies of Electricity Engineering in college and geeky experience since 1996, professionally. My technical orientation started developing as a teenager, through learning of graphic software (Autodesk Animator, later adobe) and hacking tools. In 96' I was building websites non-stop for various personal, cultural and then commercial parties. One thing lead to another and I got to learn Perl as first real-life-job programming language.

I have wrote many front end software in JavaScript / Flash (and tons more) with rich Client-Server functionality.
Threads, Scheduling and Transactions are no biggie for me, as I have written a complete Jabber based Instant Messenger and File Sharing application for the Gnutella network.

I am the king of web design and web entrepreneurship. Since I took every little job along the way of planning through refining a software product in my own hands, I have pretty good experience in managing large scale projects by knowing all aspects to their details, pitfalls and benefits.
