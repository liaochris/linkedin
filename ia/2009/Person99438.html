Ira Becker’s Summary

Ira Becker, as Senior Consultant of Inland Real Estate Auctions, Inc. is a bilingual real estate professional with 22-years in the industry. He has extensive Project Management, Sales Management and Sales experience for national condo conversion companies, developers and brokerages in Chicago and South Florida. His clients include banks, developers, investors, corporations and end-users specializing in the sale of commercial, residential and industrial properties.

As Project Manager, he managed the operations of three condominium conversion projects in South Florida consisting of 558 units that earned over $25,000,000 in funds from operations (FFO). In his Sales Management capacity, Mr. Becker managed a 570- unit condominium conversion project and also sold over $16,000,000 of properties in this Chicago project in one year alone.

He is a licensed Illinois and Florida Auctioneer and Broker as well as a licensed Florida Mortgage Broker. Mr. Becker is a certified auctioneer and graduate of the Missouri Auction School in Kansas City. He holds an MBA degree and earned his Diploma in Spanish as a Foreign Language from the Instituto Cervantes. In addition, he is a member of the National Auctioneers Association (NAA), Illinois Auctioneers Association (IAA), Florida Auctioneers Association (FAA), Northern Illinois Commercial Association of Realtors (NICAR), National Association of Realtors (NAR) and Illinois Association of Realtors (IAR).
