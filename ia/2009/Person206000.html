Ryan Colditz’s Summary

I have a creative mind. I am a team player.Combining practical observation and past experience, I am able to conceptualize and execute solutions to both graphic design and marketing objectives. I have a very critical eye and accept nothing but the best possible solution. With these skills in hand I have helped raise the bar of excellence at LA WEEKLY, and previously at IKEA. Taking educated chances that have the opportunity to elevate the image of a project is one thing I pride myself on. I have executed projects that others never considered, and the final results have been exceptionally positive, furthering brand strength and raising the bar above the competition.

Ryan Colditz’s Specialties:

Print design, web promotions, anaglyph 3D design, corporate identity, marketing campaigns, event graphics, experimental typography, 3D photography
