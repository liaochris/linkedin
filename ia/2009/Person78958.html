Fernando "3D" Sosa’s Summary

My name is fernando and my passion lays on 3d animation and game development and everything that comes with it.

i attended school as a traditional Art major and then moved to 3d animation and graduated with a 3d animation major with a minor in Art.

I'm very proud of my work experience since i been working at a proffessional level almost right after i got into college. 1st as a freelance web developer and then as a 3d modeler for a small video game.

Right after i got hired as a lead 3d modeler for a educational video game my knowledge of work pipeline and my leadership landed me a production management job in the same company.

After that project i landed a job as a production assistant for a leading architectural renderings company named renderings.com.... i worked there for about two years

renderings.com is partners with g&g advertising who after learning of my many skills added me as an illustrator and a web developer. Juggling these positions sometimes proved to be a major task but i enjoyed the challenge.

In order to suplement my income i still do some freelance on the side. This picked up after redesigning my new website. (www.amznfx.com) latest update is some screenshots and videos of a video game getting designed as we speak.

After G&G and renderings.com i landed a position as a web and video developer using flash and after effects for a company named Tourico Holidays as a multimedia specialist.

In this company i did a lot of different things and my tittle says it all "Multimedia Specialist". I do stuff like booth designs, avi presentations, web development, flash presenations, email campaigns, and even 3d modeling.

Now i'm working as a UI designer for Madden 2010.. hopefully this position will help me get a position in VFX or character modeling and animation
