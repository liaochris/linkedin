J. Ambrose Little’s Summary

Computers and programming were a hobby of mine as a kid, and although I took a degree in history and philosophy, my professional work was for several years as a software developer and architect.

I still keep up with my dev and architecture skills through writing, speaking, and professional assocations, but in the last few years I've shifted my primary focus from software architecture and technology to human-centered design. I find all facets of software design fascinating, and my goal now is simply to create awesome software for people.

J. Ambrose Little’s Specialties:
