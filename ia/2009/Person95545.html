Home Creations Inc.’s Summary

Home Creations builds new homes in Oklahoma City, Norman, Moore, Midwest City, Del City, Yukon, Edmond, Broken Arrow, Kiefer, Verdigris, and Lawton. No other local new home builder (we started building homes in Norman in 1981) offers the number of new home communities we do. We're passionate about offering something for almost everyone. We'd like nothing more than for every Oklahoman to own a new home.

Home Creations Inc.’s Specialties:

All of our new homes are ENERGY STAR qualified, saving you money on heating and cooling costs. All of our new homes are also built with three tornado safety features to help keep your new home in tact during high winds. These features are included at no additional cost.

When buyers use our participating lender for their new home financing, Home Creations pays their basic closing costs. This saves buyers between $2,000-$2,500 in fees charged by the mortgage company and title/closing company.

Home Creations Inc.’s Education

  • University of Oklahoma

    Bachelors and Masters Degrees , School of Architecture , 1978 — 1984

    Brothers Mohammad and Jalal Farzaneh migrated to Oklahoma in 1978 to attend the University of Oklahoma in Norman. After receiving their bachelors' degrees in 1981 from OU's School of Architecture, Mohammad and Jalal, in pursuing their father's trade as master builder, started their careers as Norman home builders.

    While building homes, Mohammad and Jalal received their masters' degrees in architecture from OU in 1984. In the decade since they started, Mohammad and Jalal have helped several hundred home owners experience the art of easy-living design and top-notch service.

    The Farzaneh's established Home Creations to expand their services as a leader in Oklahoma's home-building industry. Home Creations' goal is to provide home owners more locations, more quality, more flexibility, more features and ultimately more value while having fun in creating their dream home.

Home Creations Inc.’s Interests:

A company dedicated to providing the best product in Oklahoma’s new home real estate market, Home Creations also believes in giving back to the community. The company generously donates to non-profit organizations like United Way, Big Brothers Big Sisters, Cleveland County’s Christmas in April, American Cancer Society, American Heart Association, Fraternal Order of Police, Tinker Air Force Base and numerous schools, sports teams and religious groups. Furthermore, Home Creations is proud of its participation in the Central Oklahoma Habitat for Humanity Building Blitz on June 5 – 9, 2006 when the company joined nine other local businesses to construct 10 houses for 10 families in five days.
