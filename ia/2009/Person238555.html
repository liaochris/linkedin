Troy Sullivan’s Summary

I am always looking to expand my network, so feel free to add me to your contact list: troy.sullivan@matrikonopc.com

Since the early 1990's, MatrikonOPC has developed a collection of more than 300 software products for industrial data connectivity solutions using an open standards based specification called OPC.

MatrikonOPC allows hardware vendors, industrial systems integrators, and end-users a simple, and effective way of connecting proprietary hardware and software systems together to exchange data using a common interface. With MatrikonOPC software, data which was previously inaccessible, or accessible by proprietary means, is now available to exchange with any software system with an OPC interface. This allows disparate islands of data throughout a facility to be consolidated and used to monitor efficiency, increase performance, leading to increased revenue generation, and cost savings.

With over 100,000 installations worldwide, MatrikonOPC is the world's largest and most experienced industrial data connectivity company. Being a charter member of the OPC Foundation with employees on the OPC Technical Steering committee, MatrikonOPC is dedicated to promoting OPC data connectivity as the de-facto standard for open connectivity world-wide.

With best-in-class OPC data connectivity products, services, training and support, MatrikonOPC's customer base includes a wide-range of companies across the globe spanning commercial, military, and government clients. With offices throughout North America, Australia, Europe and the Middle East, MatrikonOPC's reach is truly global.
