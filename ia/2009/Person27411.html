Beth Graham’s Summary

I love food, wine and travel. So I made a career out of these things I love! Clever Endeavors is a small boutique public relations firm specializing in strategic and tactical-based initiatives. I'm a PR traditionalist so my philosophy is that the root of all marketing and PR has to begin with the target audience - drilling down to the specifics of the demo-, psycho- and socio-graphics. Then, it's all about reaching them with the most creative approaches in the most creative mediums.

In my 25-year career, I've worked across numerous industries, from Fortune 50 companies to independent entrepreneurs, in ad agencies and in corporate marketing departments. But my success always comes back to working with the products I love - food, wine, travel.
