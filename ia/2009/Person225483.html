Michel Stroom’s Summary

Profile: Senior Consultant Michel Stroom.

Michel is a professional consultant with a solid technical ICT knowledge. He has worked in the ICT industry for 20 years in Asia, USA and Europe. In the role of entrepreneur he has managed, sold and developed several systems.

He works directly with people who are responsible to deliver a new product or implement a system. His motto is: "Together we will finish the job within budget and time."

Michel is a creative problem solver and knows how to motivate people. He has a good sense of humor and delivers a change of working mentality that has been beneficial to many organizations.

Michel can be contracted in the role of manager, business consultant or ICT architect, because he has proven in the past he is capable of doing any job in the ICT industry. He has met and exceeded expectations by delivering products to many organizations in several industries. The company who wants to contract him will decide which project role will be to their benefit.

Contact Michel if your company needs help to cut down (ICT) cost, advice to develop a new software application or to implement a system.

Michel Stroom’s Specialties:

• Deliver ICT projects within time and budget
• Motivate and coach project members / staff
• Design and implement (new) ICT architecture
• Cut down ICT Costs
