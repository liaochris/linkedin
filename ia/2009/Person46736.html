Claire Ridgway’s Summary

I am a professional freelance writer living the life of my dreams. To me, writing is a vocation, not just a job. Yes, it pays the bills, but writing is my life. I enjoy my work and it shows!

At the age of 11, as part of a Geography project at school, I was asked to write a story. I let my imagination run away with me and wrote 24 pages about a secret island and pirates. I was truly inspired and just couldn’t stop writing. My story was published in the school magazine and my career as a writer started!

Writing has always been a major part of my life and when I was a school teacher, I enjoyed inspiring my class to be creative in their writing.

Since March 2008, I have been working full-time as a writer and have already established an excellent reputation on Elance as a writer who constantly overdelivers, writes top quality content, sticks to deadlines and brings a project to life. My 100% feedback, glowing reviews and project experience have allowed me to become an Elance Premium Provider.

Recent projects that I have completed for employers include:-

* Blog posts for travel, real estate and relocation sites

* Newsletters on real estate and investments

* Online magazine travel articles

* Web content for personal injury compensation website, supplement site, real estate site and many more.

* E-books on relocating to a new country

* E-book on stockmarket trading techinques with a long copy sales letter

* Keyword rich articles on a wide range of subjects

* Creating a work book

I am happy to write on most subjects and can write in many different styles. I like my writing to SPEAK to readers, to engage their interest and to bring the subject to life – allow me to do that for your business!

I believe in using my skills to help make this world a better place. 10% of the money I make from my writing goes to charity. My preferred charity is ROPE - www.rope.org.uk
