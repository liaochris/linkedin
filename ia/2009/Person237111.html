Known as results driven and able to lead cross-functional teams to drive revenue, customer experience, and cost management. Background of senior roles with market leading B2B Brands and record of successful business venture launches, revenue growth, and Brand equity enhancement (ADP, FedEx, IBM, Sharp, D&B).

20+ years of experience in Product & Solution Marketing, primarily in information intensive business services markets. Experience spans both SMB and Enterprise tiers.

Recognized and promoted for success in leadership of diverse teams of development, marketing, sales, finance, service, and fulfillment professionals. Expert in navigating complex global matrix environments to create value and deliver to customers through multiple channels yet with consistent experiences.
