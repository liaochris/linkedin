Jim Gernetzke’s Summary

EMPLOYERS: Search & Research

CANDIDATES: Job Search Coaching (See Experience: J Gernetzke & Associates, Inc., below)

J Gernetzke & Associates, Inc. was founded in 1989 as a traditional executive search firm. We are quality conscious and provide a highly individualized level of service to our clients. Since our beginning, we have been involved in recruiting management personnel at all levels – manager, director and executive level – and across all functional organizational lines. Our clients are in a variety of industries and have operations locally, regionally and all across the United States.

In 1996, we introduced recruitment research as another option for our clients. There are times when a full search fee may not be an option, for budget or other reasons. Research provides pro-active access to the market - search methodology - without full traditional search fees.

Whether engaged on a traditional search or research basis, our emphasis is on providing the highest level of value possible. We have successfully completed assignments at the manager, director and executive management levels in a variety of industries

Jim Gernetzke’s Specialties:

We have successfully completed assignments at the manager, director and executive management levels, primarily in: Retailing, 3rd Party Logistics, and Banking

We recruit nationally and have completed field and corporate assignments across all functional lines including: Operations, Marketing/Advertising, Merchandising/Merchandise Planning, Physical Distribution/Logistics/Transportation/Engineering, Finance/Accounting, Real Estate, Loss Prevention, Human Resources, Information Systems
