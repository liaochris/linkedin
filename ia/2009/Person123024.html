Juan Diego Mercader’s Summary

Juan Mercader has experience in Information Security developed over more than 9 years, acquired through his participation in Penetration Test based on OSSTM methodology & OWASP, Network and Systems auditing, Assistance in Implementing perimeter security (Firewalls - IDS - IPS), Security audit in Web applications: e-commerce solutions and e-banking. Review of wireless infrastructure in various organizations of Argentina and Latin America with various network topology (WLSE, ACS - Radius, PKI, etc.) and performing Security Awareness.
He developed consulting work for the implementation of the circulars of the BCRA "4609", "3198" and "4793" Review of process flows, controls, security policy and security of the systems for data classification and to analyze the situation of a company with respect to the standards: CobIT framework, BS 7799, ISO-17799 and ISO 2700 and performing the operational and implementation solution of COBIT control objectives, Business Impact Analysis, Business Continuity Planning.
He has participated in several projects related to the enforcement of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act in US public companies subsidiaries and Argentine companies governed by the SEC, SAS 70 audits, and assistance in the implementation of CobiT control objectives are also part of their experience.
Also has participated in projects for the Evaluation of Security in Early Alert for major financial institutions, in projects of assistance to Internal Auditing Systems, designing standards and procedures, and global plans of systems and Information security, Development and defence of proposals related to ISMS and PDS (Director Plan of Security) and Collaboration on the Project Risk Analysis for Information Security.

Additionally has been active in penetration testing and review of security standards, data classification, and management of incidents and vulnerabilities in industries such as Multimedia, Oil companies, Banks and Insurers

Juan Diego Mercader’s Specialties:

He has conducted projects for the following industries: Financial Services, Oil & Gas, Manufacturing, Retail, Telecommunications, Transportation and Utilities; IT Services and Technologies, and for important national and international governmental entities.
He has conducted penetration tests in industries such as entertainment production, oil & gas, banks and insurance companies.
