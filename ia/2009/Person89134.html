Gray McCarty’s Summary

I'm currently the Vice President of Communications for the School of Business & Economics Students' Society at Wilfrid Laurier University. In this role I'm responsible for creative marketing strategies, public relations, online presence, the monthly newspaper, clothing and merchandising business, advertising for a portfolio of events and services, graphic design, communications, and promotions. I also initiated a re-branding for both our main organization (SBESS) as well as our portfolio of services to increase our presence within the school and to convey a more professional look. I am also currently involved with the re-branding of the School of Business & Economics at Laurier, in co-ordination with Quarry Communications. In the inaugural year of the Communications Team I also formed the SBE Street Team to help with guerilla marketing techniques. As the VP of Communications I manage a team of over 18 individuals dedicated to marketing & communications.

Recently I have also been a part of the Toronto-based toy / children's entertainment company Spin Master Ltd. where I interned in the Public Relations department. During this internship I had my hand in media monitoring, event planning, research & special projects, website development and media relations.

Previously I was the Business Director for the CyberARTS Summer Camp where I grew the camp from a non-profitable state to a point where it was the most successful camp of its kind in Canada.

My goals are to be a part of a creative, thriving organization where variety is the norm, and ultimately having a hand in the overall vision and direction of a team or aspect of the organization. Specifically, I am seeking a position in the creative marketing / advertising sector.
