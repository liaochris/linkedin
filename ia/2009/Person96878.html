Iain Lanivich’s Summary

My primary responsibility is the lead digital creative director for the Alltel Wireless, U.S. Navy, and Carhartt accounts (as well as some others), directing all phases of creative work from concept development through production.

This includes the creative strategy, concepting and execution of various forms of online and integrated advertising, which may involve: cross-media campaigns, websites, rich and standard OLM, buzz and viral, WOM, social networking, web video production, advergaming, guerrilla, street, music, etc.
