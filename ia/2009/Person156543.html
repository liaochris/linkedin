Matthew Nelson’s Summary

I am the Director of Market Intelligence for Omgeo LLC, a leading provider of straight through processing solutions for the global securities industry. I monitor trends and events in the financial services industry and assess their impact on Omgeo, our clients and our competitors.

Prior to joining Omgeo in 2007, I was a senior analyst in the Securities and Investments research service at TowerGroup, where I examined technology trends impacting the investment management industry. During my tenure, I published over 30 research notes, advised executives at leading institutions and software providers and spoke at numerous industry conferences around the world. I was also regularly quoted in national and industry publications including The Wall Street Journal, The Financial Times, MarketWatch and Wall Street & Technology.

Before joining TowerGroup, I spent 10 years in the financial services industry holding a variety of positions with Fidelity Investments, Interactive Data Corp. and Forum Financial Group. I received a B.A. in Economics from the University of California, Los Angeles.
