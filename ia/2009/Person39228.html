Catherine Malicki’s Summary

Marketing executive with broad experience including strategic planning, start-up and existing offices, corporate and recruitment branding, identity, public relations, on-line marketing programs, M&A due diligence and integration, team building and training, program management and execution, CRM system development and integration, and client service programs.
