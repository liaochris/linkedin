Christy Finley’s Summary

I have had over 14 years in the design industry beginning with web site design. We specialized in data base driven "dynamic" sites that could be updated using your browser very early on. I was the sales person and brought in over 200 accounts - some of them very large and needing several months of my small companies time to develop.

This branched into customers asking if we could develop designs for printed materials as well. Naturally we stepped up to the plate and I became senior designer on top of my other responsibilities. While running this business, which was a dba of a corporation owned by my family, I still had corporate responsibilities. I was in charge of all marketing materials, trade show planning, travel coordination (at times), helped with new product development as well as keeping an eye on the books. Not to mention inside sales, business development and customer service.

Marketing and Design have always been my first love and now is my focus.

Business Development and Sales: increasing inside sales, representation at trade shows, new product development, promototions, project managment.

Web Site Design: HTML certified, some PHP and CSS.
