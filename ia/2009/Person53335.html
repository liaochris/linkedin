  • president

    n2 (pronounced n-squared) is a digital media solutions company based in Atlanta, GA dedicated to providing clients with cutting edge planning and ad-placement services utilizing numerous digital signage platforms and mobile phone marketing applications on a national level.

  • Chief Operating Officer

    Wilkins Media Company

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    July 2000 — Present (9 years 1 month)

    Headquartered in Atlanta, GA, Wilkins Media Company simplifies multiple market Out-of-Home Media buys for both agencies and advertisers. Based on the requested markets, we will work with the agency and/or client to design an Out-of-Home Media program that best suits their needs and budget. Whether a campaign includes billboard or transit advertising, Wild Postings, or some of the newest media types, Wilkins Media Company will provide the most competitive rates and incomparable expertise.

    We do not own any billboards, trucks, posters, buses, or any other kind of media inventory. We maintain close relationships with Out-of-Home Media companies that allow us to be aware of competitive situations in each market. Due to the large volume of business Wilkins Media Company places, we are able to secure the best rates and locations in the industry and pass those advantages along to our clients.
