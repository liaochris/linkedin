Cesar Harada’s Summary

My family was in constructions and crafts, my father is a monumental sculptor on granite. I have been studying crafts, design and the arts. I have been also practicing martials arts and travelling alone since I am a kid. I love working on creative and innovative design, art, performing, organisation projects. I can work very hard and independently but I also can work well in teams, I have lot of positive energy and drive, I'm good at managing people and plan with budget and strategy goals on short and long term. I have worked in various positions : international interaction design workshop organiser, assistant traditional carpenter in Japanese mountains, teaching graphic design in architecture school, animation film production assistant, exhibition curator, translator, film-maker screened in festivals, Graphic designer for corporate identites, webdesigner for fashion designers and charities, advisors in commercial strategy for the art business, art direction and building manager for national political events in France ...
I'm interested in radical creative design, especillay when it includes technology.
I'm fluent in french and english, good spanish and japanese oral, understand portuguese. I'm very serious about work, social and flexible.
