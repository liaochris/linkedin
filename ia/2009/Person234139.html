Tim Selders’s Summary

Tim Selders is one of the founding partners and director of PARK, a well established European consultancy in the area of design management with 12 years of experience.
Together with his business partner and co-founder Frans Joziasse and the PARK team he has worked with clients like LEGO, Roca, Johnson Controls, BMW, Vaillant and Airbus.
During the last 12 years he has lectured, gave workshops and wrote articles on design management for several audiences.
He holds a Master in Industrial Design Engineering from the Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands.

Tim Selders’s Specialties:

(1) design strategy, (2) design organizations & (3) design processes & tools
