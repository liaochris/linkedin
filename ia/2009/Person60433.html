Deborah Armenante’s Summary

My objective is to pursue a career in technical writing as I enjoy research, writing and being creative. I love creating documentation as it uses all the skills I enjoy and have learnt during my working life.

I am currently completing a Graduate Certificate of Social Science in Technical Communication. This is a 1 year part-time course (online) which provides a formal qualification for technical writers.

I have completed the ITIL V3 Foundations (Online Training Course) which is preparation for completing the ITIL Service Management Framework exam.

Writing technical and end user documentation for the IT industry. My Strengths lie in my ability to analyse IT Systems and document them in a clear and concise manner.
