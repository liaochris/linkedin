Sangam Gupta’s Summary

Co-Founder and Owner of Trine Entertainment Ltd. I built up Trine from ground up in the Indian Industry which was struggling to develop even small Casual Games. Trine Games in a span of 2 years became the largest and most reputed Developer in the country . Trine has so far released over 5 AAA games for PC and Consoles.

Trine today operates 2 Game Development studios in Mumbai, India and New Delhi, India with over 300 fulltime employees on the payroll.

With the evergrowing opportunities, I will be positioning Trine Entertainment Ltd. in India as a strong Entertainment Brand with ventures into Video Game Publishing, Mobile Entertainment, Movie Production and as a Record Label in the country.
