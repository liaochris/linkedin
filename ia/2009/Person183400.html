Paul Becker, PHR’s Summary

WHAT CAN I SAY……I LOVE HUMAN RESOURCES……I LOVE WHAT I DO!!!

I enjoy being a part of people's lives. I take pleasure in helping others and being a part of their growth. I also love being the legal spokesperson for the corporation and thinking in legal terms of risk and corporate liability often when others do not.

I am an energetic and career focused Human Resources Professional having worked directly with Business Owners and Chief Executive Officers. My experience over the years covers construction, manufacturing, retail, and government sectors.

I have experience in the following areas:

- MULTI SITE, MULTI STATE OPERATIONS

- Training & Development
- Staffing & Recruitment
- Human Resource Information Systems (HRIS)
- Workplace Safety Compliance
- Workers Compensation
- Job Analysis & Job Descriptions
- Coaching & Leadership Development
- Human Resource Audits
- Performance Evaluation Systems
- Occupational Health & Safety
- Corporate Security Design/Management

**Professional in Human Resources Certification (PHR)**

I play a very active part in my local Human Resources community as I am now the Past President of the Board of Directors for the Inland Empire Society For Human Resource Management (IESHRM).
