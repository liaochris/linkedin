Bob Weber’s Summary

Robert Weber is a successful management consultant, serial entrepreneur, senior executive, and inventor. In his role as Vice President, Corporate Development and Licensing (part time), Open Security Solutions, LLC, Mr. Weber was instrumental in licensing a patent portfolio relating to information security to a well-known patent licensing and assertion company. Earlier, Weber was Co-founder and Senior Vice President of Corporate & Market Development at Evergreen Open Broadband, a wireless Internet services startup acquired by Mobilepro Corp. in June, 2005. Previously, he was SVP Business and Technology Strategy, InterTrust Technologies Corporation, where he made significant contributions to the company's intellectual property.

Weber has also been a Principal at Northeast Consulting Resources, Inc. (NCRI, prior to its acquisition by NerveWire). At NCRI, his consulting practice focused on the intersection of business and information technology strategy with a particular emphasis on Information Commerce, publishing, Internet-based services, and technology-based companies.

Weber has been a member of the IEEE Boston Entrepreneurs' Network and served on its Advisory Board as Vice Chairman, Programs, 2005-2007.

Weber is an inventor with 20 United States issued patents and a number of foreign issued counterpart patents.

Bob Weber’s Specialties:

Projects and activities that entail one or more of entrepreneurship, intellectual property, business strategy, and computing and telecom technologies. Weber blogs on the copyright industries and Digital Rights Management at www.managingrights.com and occasionally on Wicked Problems and Scenario Planning at www.strategykinetics.com. Weber also provides IP strategy, evaluation, and brokering services at www.patentkinetics.com.
