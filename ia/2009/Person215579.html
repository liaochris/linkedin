Steven Harper’s Summary

We are excited to announce the Salesforce.com version of Territory Plan Pro! It is a Salesforce.com Certified Application for the App Exchange, that facilitates the Territory Sales Planning while integrating with the Native Salesforce CRM.

My career has included:

- WW Vice President of Sales
- Regional Vice President of Sales
- Regional Director of Sales
- Sales Manager - Channels, The Americas
- Sales Rep at every level

I started P2W in 2006. Plan2Win was started because I saw a gap in the training and skill levels of today's sales teams. Real territory strategy and key account planning were not being taught, and there were no tools to facilitate the process. That is why P2W was started. The goal of P2W is to create sales tools that enhance the sales person’s activities, not impede them.

P2Ws software, consulting, and sales training push sales people to think analytically, methodically and strategically about their industries. P2W will get them to think about their selling techniques and their territories. We will help them create a sales plan or Account plan that will help them sell. More importantly, we make them think in new ways.

The sales applications walk the user through a series of questions that are fundamental to any sales person's successful Territory Plan or Strategic Account Plan. The software asks questions about contacts, customers, competitors and geography. And it asks questions we know most sales people have little time to consider, even if they know that should be asking them. By working through the answers, sales reps gain valuable insight into the way they do business, and the way they should do business.
