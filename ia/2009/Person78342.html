Craig Schweitzer CNIM’s Summary

I've been working in research and clinical neurophysiology since 1983. I'm experienced in IOM, EEG, Sleep, EPs, and Cognitive testing. I have worked in several capacities in this exciting field: research assistant, clinical instructor, clinical testing, surgical monitoring, medical-legal testing (for personal injury and criminal cases), and extensive medical billing for neurophysiology.

I'm primarily interested in providing web-based services that facilitate secure diagnostic telemedicine: physician supervision, limited medical management, post-hoc interpretations, and medical billing.

My emerging personal interest is in the application of telemedicine in the exploding trend of Outbound Medical Tourism among U.S. consumers that are seeking safe and less costly health care abroad.

Craig Schweitzer CNIM’s Specialties:

Certified Neurophysiologic Intraoperative Monitoring (CNIM), Bachelor's degree in Biological Sciences, some graduate coursework in Neuroscience.
