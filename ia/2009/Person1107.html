Aaron L. Gilbert’s Experience

  • Arcana Productions Inc.

    (Publishing industry)

    August 2008 — Present (7 months)

    Arcana Productions Inc. is a joint venture between Arcana Studios, Inc. (“Arcana”), a top independent comic book publisher and new media developer, and Bron Management Ltd. (“Bron”), a media production, finance and consultancy company. API was established to develop and produce an ongoing slate of live-action and animated theatrical feature films, TV movies and television series, based on the comic book characters owned or controlled by Arcana.

    Current projects in post-production, production, or heading into pre-production include: PARADOX, CHOPPER, MIGHTY MIGHTY MONSTERS, EVE: VAMPIRE DIVA, and SUNDOWN.

  • Bron Management Ltd.

    (Entertainment industry)

    July 1999 — Present (9 years 8 months)

    Over the last decade Gilbert has functioned as a finance executive, facilitator and consulting producer to more than 40 companies who have operated primarily in the media, finance, entertainment, Internet and telecommunications industries in North America and across Asia. As Bron Management Ltd., Gilbert has provided consultation and facilitation services to content providers, distribution outlets, investment groups, and technology companies that support digital distribution and marketing of programming worldwide.

    Bron offers access to finance and financial structures for film and media. Outside entertainment business, Bron has a relationship with a refinery and trader with access to sizable buyers and sellers of physical commodities world wide. (D2, Qatar Marine Fuel, JP-54 (jet fuel) and Bonny Light Crude, diamonds, gold, and MSO2)

  • Rainmaker Entertainment

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; RMKR; Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    July 2007 — August 2008 (1 year 2 months)

    After spending a year as Rainmaker Entertainment's Vice President, leading business affairs on many of the studios film, DVD and television productions, Gilbert transitioned his role to that of Executive Producer, involved on several film projects he set up for the studio, including Stan Lee's "Legion of 5".

  • MediaMen LLC

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry)

    November 2004 — August 2007 (2 years 10 months)

    Bron Management was a partner in MediaMen LLC (Vancouver/Bangkok/Dubai) from 2004 to 2007. The Company was sold to Dubai based Media Gateway.With this acquisition completed, Media Gateway now represents more than 300 channels, which it targets toward Broadcast, Triple Play, IPTV, PC Broadband (streaming), Video on Demand, Mobile Telephony, PDAs and Airline platforms worldwide.

  • Mainframe Entertainment

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; MFE; Animation industry)

    March 2001 — May 2003 (2 years 3 months)

    Through Bron Management, Gilbert functioned as a business development and finance consultant / facilitator to the Mainframe senior executives, reporting directly to the CEO and CFO of the company. Mainframe is a leading producer of CG animation, best known for such productions as ReBoot, Barbie, Beast Machines, Beast Wars, and Max Steel.

  • BOXX Entertainment (International) Ltd.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Music industry)

    March 1994 — June 1999 (5 years 4 months)

    BOXX was a music publishing and talent management group based in Vancouver, with satellite offices in Los Angeles, London, and Toronto. Gilbert was a co-founder of the company, focused on both the international licensing and the film/television music supervision initiatives. The Company was disbanded in early 1999 as each of the principals wanted to pursue separate interests in the media / motion picture industries.
