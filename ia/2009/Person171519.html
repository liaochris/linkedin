Write poetry and paint.

Among themes, which do not mostly well known and recognized in Russia, are essence of national problems and simply history of small nations, especially in vast spaces of huge Siberia.
So one of the lines of our work is describing life of these forks and drawing attention of public to preservation of culture, language and folklore as one of the impact of human civilization.
Living in the Soviet Union I could permit me to study only. Now the necessity to study is more violent. Reading of people who want their cups full? I think that the global society needs us to be more supportive and less selfish. We need not be like great Mother Theresa. Our impact could be smaller. I want to be useful in this life with all my heart. There are funds which are necessary for every action, so I study to be a networker, to develop my personality and knowledge.
