Franciszek Migaszewski (fmigaszewski at gmail.com)’s Experience

  • Sales Director

    Agmar SA

    (Packaging and Containers industry)

    November 2006 — March 2009 (2 years 5 months)

    Agmar (www.agmar.com.pl) is a medium-sized company (230 employees, 20 mln PLN of annual turnover) based in Mielec, active in area of metal enclosures for telecommunication, energy sector, IT and industry.

    I was responsible for four areas of activity:
    * Sales management
    * Strategy planning
    * Marketing
    * Product development (including cost calculation)

    Beginning my job in Agmar I defined three objectives:
    1. Market diversification -> it was crucial due to company’s 80% dependence on investments in telecommunication infrastructure which means in Polish conditions dependence on investment plans of three companies (TPSA and two other mobile phone operators)
    2. Regaining ability to gain new clients -> within few years before 2006 company did not gain any new important clients
    3. Restructuring product offer – 99% of turnover came from highly customized products with very limited chances of sales to the other customers.

    In over two years in Agmar I reached all the objectives. I created from scratch effective and motivated sales team. Company’s sales structure is diversified (telecommunication, energy and industry) and half of its biggest customers were gained within last year. In spite of global crisis Agmar income in I quarter of 2009 will exceed substantially its income in 2008.

    Product development process has been deeply modified towards systemic solutions. Moreover, it has gained previously lacking marketing back-up.

    I often carried out myself effective negotiations with key customers and on key contracts.
