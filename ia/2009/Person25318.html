Lindsay Collins’s Summary

I am highly competitive, a self starter who is organized and is goal oriented.
I am a self taught jewelry designer utilizing my creativity and talents to design statement pieces.
Some Friends and I started a finely crafted, custom, handmade, one of a kind jewelry business. Its Called Beach Glam.
Beach Glam is not even a year old yet and we are already selling at Farmers Markets and online at http://www.etsy.com/profile.php?user_id=7997931
We want to take Beach Glam to the top. We would love to sell our jewelry at Boutiques and one day have our own store in our home town Long Beach Ca. At Beach Glam we dream big, we want the " A listers " to be wearing our Jewelry. We are not just stopping at jewelry making. We will be making hand bags, fans, candles, and scarfs. Beach Glam will have something for everyone.
