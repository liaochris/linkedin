The Dance of Life Coaching
-----------------------------------------
Teaching you to gracefully navigate the dark & sunny days of life, rather than getting easily thrown off course.

Do you understand your Values, & the values of the people around you?
Do you have Financial blocks that you are struggling to breakthrough?
Are there old Pains/hurts from your past that affect your present?
Are you totally happy with your Relationship?
Do you feel that something is Missing in your life, or Fear Losing something?

I teach you how to:
- Keep what you have
- Make the most of it
- & Get what you would love

In any area of life: Home, Relationships, Health, Work, Money or Personal development.
Through workshops or 1to1's (face to face or via skype).

I am the first certified UK Demartini Facilitator & Coach
Check out my Dance of Life site: http://www.DofL.co.uk

The Mummy Whisperer
----------------------------------
Empowering Mums to Increase the Contentment & Sparkle in their lives

Feeling Overwhelmed/Stressed?
Not Managing to Juggle all the Balls?
Confused by Baby Training Systems?
Swamped by Parenting Techniques?

After 6 years of being a Demartini coach (& many more in other areas of business/healing/therapy) and then miraculously falling pregnant at 36, I realised that there are many Baby Training Systems out there, and Parenting Techniques, but nothing purely for the Mum. I realised that I could create a program for Mums that would clear their minds of the brain noise from all the worries, guilts, conflicting advice & lack of confidence, that would empower them to listen to their own inner wisdom, because the Mum knows, she just doesn't know she knows!

We start with my workshop (1 day or 4x1.5hrs) & then can move on to specific problems 1to1 either face to face or via skype.

Check out my 'Mummy Whisperer' site: http://www.Mummywhisperer.com

Lisa Pearson’s Specialties:
