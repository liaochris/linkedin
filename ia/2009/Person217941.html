Simone Dadsetan’s Summary

I have been involved in real estate for over seven years. My background is retail and wholesale mortgage lending. That coupled with my knowledge of the orange county coastal real estate market gives me a powerful advantage. I have attended school here and lived in the area all my life, therefore, I have my "finger on the pulse" for the type of lifestyle home buyers seek. Whether you're selling a home, moving up or downsizing I have all the tools and knowledge to assist you with your decision.

I'm very proud to be an agent for Prudential California Realty. Prudential is a Berkshire Hathaway affiliate and one can't ask for a more solid reputation and brand recognition. In fact, the Prudential rock is the most recognized brand in the world. There are over 1.7 million unique visitors from round the world that visit www.prudentialcal.com. That's brand recognition! With the California coast being one of the most desirable places to live, the odds are in favor of my clients.

I pride myself in providing an unsurpassed level of service. Being that selling or purchasing a home is the biggest financial decision a family will make, I ensure that my clients experience is as seamless and worry free a transaction of this magnitude can get. Having worked on the lending side of the business I'm able to be steps ahead of the transaction therefore, I can set my clients expectations, avoid unnecessary delays and frustration. Please contact me regarding any real estate related services or questions.
