Winston Hooker’s Summary

Winston Hooker is one this country’s most successful healthcare executive recruiters with particular expertise in the realm of oncology. With an already established successful background in healthcare executive recruitment he is able to quickly give his clients and candidates the outcomes they expect and need in today’s demanding job market. His professional relationships with hospital administration from the C-level down to the manager level afford him the rare ability to fully comprehend the complex reporting structures of varied hospital settings and service lines resulting in timely introductions of appropriately experienced candidates with his highly valued clients.
Winston has been fortunate to be able to work with some of the most advanced cancer programs in the nation, and has experience working on the following positions: Multi System/Corporate Oncology Service Line Director, VP of Oncology Services, Oncology Service Line Director, Cancer Center Administrator/Director, Clinical Oncology Director/ Manager, Clinical Med/Surg Director/Manager, Radiation Oncology Director/Manager, Bone Marrow Transplant Director/Manager, Nurse Practitioner, Physician Assistants, Clinical Nurse Specialist, Oncology Research Director/Manager/Coordinator, Patient Navigator, Stem Cell Manager/Coordinator, Neurosciences Service Line Director, Radiology Director, and Vice Presidents of Operations.
Winston is now Vice President & Managing Partner at JW Friday, Hooker & Associates. His background includes six years in sales and healthcare recruitment. Winston currently holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration Management from Saint Louis University.
