Brian Johnson’s Summary

ClicksThatCall.com get's customers and clients (who don't know you) calling by positioning your business in front of people online who are ready to buy.

We use a proven 3 step process

1. Get You Found! Google, Yahoo, Ask & Bing and more.

2. Right Message, Right buyer, Right Moment

3. Track, Report & Maximize. - We don't care if people find you. We only care if they call you and buy. We track which keywords and web traffic generates phone calls and emails.
We then track which places are generating you the lowest cost per call.

It's the big fish in a small pond theory. Let your competitor waste their dollars on ego advertising while you get more leads and make more money per dollar invested. It's advertising that works smart not hard.
