Rob Caulfield’s Summary

As founder and chief executive officer of TrustCommerce, Rob Caulfield is responsible for the strategic vision, growth and business operations of the Company.
An authority on e-Commerce and data security, the Industry Standard named Mr. Caulfield a, “Grandfather of e-Commerce.” He has testified as an expert witness in front of the U.S. Senate.

With nearly 20 years of Information Technology, Security, e-Commerce and payments experience, Mr. Caulfield has provided Internet-based solutions for hundreds of Fortune 500 organizations. His experience focuses on payment processing, back-end database development and management, operations, Internet and data security, web site development and management and more. Mr. Caulfield designed and implemented the first city in the United States’ e-Commerce platform for the City of Los Angeles.

Mr. Caulfield is viewed as an industry expert on data security and has served as a key presenter at the following conferences - International e-Commerce Summit, Internet World, Association of Internet Professionals and the 10,000 Women’s League of Business. He is actively involved in the Electronic Transaction Association (ETA) and serves on the Emerging Markets committee.
