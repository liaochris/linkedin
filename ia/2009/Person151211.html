Mark D Harris’s Summary

I'm actively looking for a new Marketing role. I'll consider part-time or contract work as it gives us both a chance to see how we work together.

I always view businesses from the outside inwards and this inevitably leads to me helping you influence your customer's journey, be that in-store or online.

Whilst I search, I'm spending time helping local businesses and charities to promote themselves to the right customers and grow profitably.

Until June 2009 I was Consumer Partnerships Director at SpinVox, delivering consumer propositions for Oxfam, MagicFM, Ministry of Sound and Radio WNYC in US.

In my early years at SpinVox I held a number of other positions managing Consumer products, Retail and online channels and Enterprise solutions.

Prior to SpinVox, I worked for 6 years in Mobile Value Added Services at O2 and Zed UK. In both companies I marketed and managed value added services such as Roaming, Entertainment, Community, Ringtones, Chat, Football alerts and other content services.
