Frank Richards’s Summary

Frank is a serial entrepreneur having successfully founded three companies in the Silicon Valley. He brings over 16+ years of experience in building market leading companies in the areas of technology, marketing and real estate.

Frank is responsible for leading the overall management of Smartzip's business and its strategy, with a focus on driving growth, product innovation and expansion of the product suite into other verticals. He founded SmartZip in January 2008 with the goal of developing a revolutionary online real estate application and the world's first rating engine for residential real estate based on 50+ years of combined real estate experience, innovative methodologies and ground-breaking algorithms. He has assembled a world class team and raised millions of dollars in funding for SmartZip from leading venture capital firms in the Silicon Valley.

Prior to SmartZip, Frank founded The NorthPoint Group and continues to serve as its Chairman and CEO. The NorthPoint Group is a unique full service residential real estate firm offering end-to-end suite of high-touch residential real estate investment services, including proprietary research, investment strategy, out-of-state property acquisition, property management, and long-term portfolio optimization.

Earlier in his career, Frank founded Protocall, a technology driven direct marketing services firm, which Frank led through its acquisition by Intek in 1997 and IPO in 2000. Frank has a Bachelor’s degree in Political Science from the University of California, Davis and continued his post graduate education at The University of Essex, England. Frank is a professional real estate investor and owns a large portfolio of residential investment properties around the country.
