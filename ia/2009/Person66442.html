Douglas Anweiler’s Summary

More than eight years of experience as Vice President of Corporate Marketing and Communications at the Team Reliance Group of Companies and over twenty years senior leadership experience in both the private and public sectors. Responsible for developing and communicating the Team Reliance brand and strategy throughout the Team Reliance companies and key aviation and aerospace markets.

Team Reliance Companies include:

Reliance Aerotech Services Inc. www.relianceaerotech.com/services
Navhouse Corporation www.navhouse.com
Reliance Aerotech Canada Inc. www.teamreliance.com

Focus is on creating growth by implementing positioning, branding, advertising, media and social media programs.

Responsible for developing and implementing the government affairs agenda for Team Reliance Group, working with federal, provincial, state and municipal governments to advance programs of mutual benefit for the aerospace industry.

Also provide advisory services for aerospace and technology clients including strategic diagnostics, corporate strategy, business development and marketing.
