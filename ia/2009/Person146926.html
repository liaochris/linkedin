Maia Beatty’s Summary

I help professional women discover and develop their powerful presence.

Your presence is your impact, your expression, your behavior. Although it comes from inside you, it can only be experienced when you interact with someone else.

Everyone has a presence—it’s up to you to make it powerful.

I show you how to discover and develop your Powerful Presence, whether you are:

•Speaking, or simply need to get your ideas across to others—in person or on camera.
•In a challenging relationship that matters to you—whether professional or personal.
•In the process of “becoming,” as you face a major transition—whether professional or personal.

The strategies for powerful presence are consistent, whether you are speaking to an audience of any size or to a camera, relating to someone who is challenging for you in business or personally, or working through a major transition in your life.

When you work with me, you will be able to approach each of these situations with more:

•Clarity
•Confidence
•Resourcefulness
•Authenticity
•Results

The results of developing Powerful Presence are:

•You will feel confident and engaged so you can express your message to any audience.
•You stay focused in every conversation and don't get derailed by anything the other person says or does.
•You stay focused on the present moment. You find it easier to adapt to change, make the most of your inner resources, and you experience less pain.

You will find that the process of Discovering Your Powerful Presence is simple. You can choose the way we work together, because the focus is always on YOU. You'll find more details at www.maiabeatty.com.
