Jim Tuchler’s Summary

ECommerce expert with experience managing multiple direct-to-customer channels, including Internet, call center, direct mail and catalog.

Marketing focus includes Search Engine Optimization (SEO and SEM), paid search, email marketing and affiliate marketing.

Experienced in integrating direct market channels with retail outlets. Focused on growing sales, reducing cost and improving service.

Jim holds several patents for online innovations, and he sits on the advisory board of several of his current and former clients.
