Bruce Carlos’s Summary

A "transformational CIO" with an innovative and dynamic approach, with a national security clearance, I have 17 years of successful IT industry experience including 12 years in senior technology management appointments involving strategic contribution to a wide range ICT and business programs.

I have managed and supported the implementation of complex business, technology, and security programs ranging from $30k to $100m with success in strong people leadership. Experience extends from working in a range of diverse business and cross-cultural environments at national and international levels. Established global ICT operations, data centres and as well support existing (& matured) businesses focussed on high level ICT strategy, architecture, standards, governance, operations, service delivery (ITIL), security advise, policy development, coordination and management. I have sound knowledge of security standards including ISO 27001, 27002, 17799, CLEDS (Police) and Protective Security Manual (PSM) and ASCI33 (ISM) (national security).

Broad range of qualifications, training, experience and competence underpins an ability to deal effectively with a wide range of business technology scenarios and opportunities, both in public (State & Federal level) and world class global private sectors. Good knowledge of supply chain and logistics (MRP, PLM, PDM). Certified Six Sigma Specialist and CMMI qualifications.

A pragmatic executive, who has the ability to communicate at all levels of an organisation. I have a service orientated approach, to deliver value in a changing environment.

My strengths include: strategic and operational ICT planning, strategic program/project management (Prince2, PIMBOK, IPDS), ICT governance (COBIT), technical expertise, operational performance excellence (ITIL, CMMI), and successful leadership of productive teams in private and public sectors. I have a passion for driving quality and aligning ICT to business growth and value.
