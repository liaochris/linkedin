Eric Stockton’s Summary

An experienced executive and serial entrepreneur with proven leadership in the areas of Internet marketing, ecommerce, publishing and online media. Demonstrated ability to quickly and consistently identify prime movers of a business, and provide astute strategic planning and the practical implementation of initiatives for direct and indirect distribution channels.
