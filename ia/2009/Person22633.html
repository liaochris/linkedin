Autumn Hurd’s Summary

I have had the opportunity to work with a variety of clients, from aerospace to computers to landscaping; from print to web design; strict graphic design to concept design to structure design to user interface design. I am basically a jill-of-all-trades within the Mac world. I am an expert with Adobe programs (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, and Acrobat), and can easily find my way around most all other programs in the Mac platform. I recently have started using Strata 3D to help conceptualize various structures with art applied, before taking it to final art, production and manufacturing. I am actively learning about corrugated, semi-permanent and permanent retail displays. I am always looking for better and faster ways to get things accomplished.
