Ranbir Singh’s Summary

Expert at Silicon Carbide semiconductors for power, detector, sensor and high frequency applications. Published over 96 papers in industry leading conferences and journals, awarded over 24 patents and written 1 book on these subjects. Experienced in proposing and winning government and industry research contracts and delivering results that matter. Delivered Solutions to Industry that were unique and relevant.Leadership shown in the design, fabrication, characterization of SiCdevices.
