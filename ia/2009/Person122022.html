  • EVP Business Development

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    March 2000 — July 2007 (7 years 5 months)

    Peer Impact was a full service digital media marketplace where members could not only buy their favorite titles, but SELL them as well...think iTunes meets eBay...

    Unfortunately we didn't have the same size checkbook as those guys...

    Wurld Media was sold to ROO Media in 2007
