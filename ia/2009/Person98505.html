Peter Bain’s Summary

Peter Bain, principal of Incipit, is a designer whose practice is built upon letters. He works with clients including publishers, brand and identity consultants, advertising agencies, cultural organizations, marketers and others to make communication visually powerful.

Bain has lectured widely; published on typography and lettering; and has been on the faculty of Parsons/The New School for Design and Pratt Institute in New York. He was Type Director at Saatchi & Saatchi Advertising in New York for more than a decade and emphasizes the links between careful analysis and successful, creative, typographic design.

Peter Bain’s Specialties:

Typeface design, logotypes, typography, custom lettering
