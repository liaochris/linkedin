Varun Chhonkar’s Summary

Managing and founded intellectual property business consultancy firm specializing in the areas of patents, intellectual property licensing and transactions. Involved in high-end intellectual property asset management (IPAM) support like invention lifecycle management, R&D (IP) strategy, IP due diligence, business intelligence so on. Actively involved in patent-regulatory support for pharma business in the development of ANDA, 505 (b) (2) and paragraph IV products for the United States. Aim to provide clients’ intellectual investment an IP thrust for maximum value and returns.

Involved in counseling technology startups in structuring/framing policies and protocols (SOPs) to index, protect and position their intellectual property to maximize their corporate value and leverage market competitiveness. Also involved in commoditize business investment into IP-driven Business Model through proper blend of business and legal strategy. Provide support to optimize R&D productivity and product market feasibility by avoiding infringing and designing around the valid IP claims and constantly screening the changing IP landscape.

Involved in providing quality patent services customized to meet challenging requirements of R&D units, technology startups, SMEs, IP law firms, Corporations, venture capitalists, inventors and entrepreneurs. Services include like patentability search/opinion, patent clearance (FTO search), invalidity/validity search, claim interpretation and analysis, patent analytics, patent landscape, white-space mining, etc. Strong expertise and experience in conducting structure/sub-structure searches for NCE and agro-chemical compounds using high-end STN database files (CAPLUS, CASLINK, and MARPAT).

Challenges are what we interested in and excellence what we are committed to deliver. You may reach us at varun@ipfeathers.com.

Varun Chhonkar’s Specialties:

Patent law aspects of India, US and Europe. Actively involved in patent support for paragraph IV products, patent clearance, non-infringement analysis, and licensing agreements. Also involved in patent awareness programs and workshops involving advanced patent searching and claim writing. Conversant working on various high-end patent and non-patent databases like STN Express, Micropatent, PatBase, SciFinder, Dialog, Delphion, ScienceDirect, DWPI, IMS patents, West law, LexisNexis, PubMed etc.
