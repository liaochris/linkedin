Samantha Payn’s Summary

I am a British national with eighteen years experience of working with the Russian insurance industry both in the UK and Russia. As a translator I offer a full understanding of the specifics of the international needs of this unique market. Working in the sphere of insurance enabled me to gain experience of a wide variety of business sectors with the emphasis always on strictly accurate transmission of information.
I have held a wide range of customer service, sales and marketing posts throughout my career. Additionally I can offer project management to both one-off, short-term events and for longer term commitments on a self-employed part-time basis.
