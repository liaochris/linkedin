bob hovey’s Summary

Management Recruiters of the North Fork, (MR North Fork) based on Eastern Long Island (NY) focuses exclusively upon recruiting candidates in the renewable energy and Cleantech sectors. MR NorthFork’s ever expanding role in the development of renewable energy technologies is measured by its many successful technical, managerial and executive placements in leading renewable energy companies worldwide.

bob hovey’s Specialties:

MR North Fork specializes in both permanent and contract placements within the Renewable Energy and Cleantech industries: wind, solar, geothermal, waste to energy, and wave technology. We place skilled and talented professionals in Engineering, IT, HVAC, R&D, EHS, Consulting, Sales & Marketing, Business Development, Project Management and Construction Management.
