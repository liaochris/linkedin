Nichola Burton’s Summary

I began my professional career at the Conservatorium of Music studying piano and opera training to be a music teacher. I was bored and spent most of my time in the recording studio recording my first album. There's the first clue - I need to be busy and be creative! Since then I have morphed between Rock Chick Singer, Recording and Touring Artist, Artist Management, Producer, Vocal Arranger, Composer to Entertainment Agent, Front Line Business Manager, Systems Software Designer, Workplace Trainer and Assessor, to Event Manager, Publicist, Brand Consultant and certified Personal Development Facilitator. It's been quite a journey where each step has naturally moved into the next.

I work with clients in diverse markets on a variety of projects. To work consistently in the Music Industry for the past 30 years and own and manage an Entertainment Agency in partnership with my husband for the past 20 years has been a major achievement. Survival in this industry requires dedicated, commitment and a willingness to explore chaos. I own all three of those traits!!

I am currently writing a number of books and my goal is to finish - stop rewriting - actually close the file and send it off to the publisher. :-) Then release the training programs that support these books while continuing to work in the chaotic business that I love while being incredibly privileged to remain in such a crazy creative industry.
