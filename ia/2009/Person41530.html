Charles Blackham’s Summary

• Chartered Engineer with over 15 years experience of gaining product approvals to National and Internional requirements throughout Europe, North America, Australia and Japan.
• Identified applicable requirements and directives; written test plans, performed testing, written test reports, technical justification documents;
• Delivered the required EMC, safety, RF, Machinery and Environmental approvals to meet project deadlines and allow CE marking
• Tested products against EU, North American and Japanese Radio and EMC standards
• Implemented design changes to solve EMC emissions, immunity and receiver SNR problems.
• Increased product reliability using HALT and HASS environmental test techniques
• Written Test Plans and tested product against MIL-STD-461E and MIL-STD-801F
• SC cleared by UK DVA
