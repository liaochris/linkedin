Jeremy Brett’s Summary

Experienced CFO with considerable experience of high growth IT environments. Experience includes VC funding and Board level financial reporting, trade sales, broader business skills (including legal & HR) and strategic business support.

Qualified as a chartered accountant (ICAEW, 1988) at Price Waterhouse; followed by positions at organisations in a variety of environments from start-up to plc.
