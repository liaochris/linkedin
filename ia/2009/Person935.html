Aamir Rasool’s Summary

Starting my career in 2001 as a quality officer in a confectionery manufacturing company and gaining expertise in food safety, quality and environmental management system, helped me reach a point, at which my company arranged for me to attend a “certified quality professional diploma”, which further motivated me take up MBA studies. (And now further CMQ/QE)
This progressive trend of experience and advanced studies in Hygiene and HACCP helped me in assuming the role of HACCP Coordinator at Tiffany Foods (IFFCO Group), in UAE managing QC activities, providing technical support, training of personnel and most important HACCP development, implementation and certification.
Successful completion of this project provided me the confidence and a chance to take up a new challenging role in hospitality food safety management with “Rotana Group” based in Dubai, and thus I am a “food safety and quality management practitioner” as a part of which I provide trainings.
Above set of skills is backing me to further my career to be a a food safety, hygiene and quality assurance expert to utilize my knowledge, skills and experiences to add value in organizational process.
