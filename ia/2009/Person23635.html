Balaji Jagannathan’s Summary

After my 15 years of experience in leading teams at TOP MNCs in India, We in 2008, launched our latest venture, Workplace Nxt – a WorkPlace Transformation Company, wherein the charter is to create & promote the People Innovation Ecosystem in organizations.

Workplace Nxt would work towards promoting & employing best people practices in workplaces that help ignite passion and unleash the talent of people.

Prior to this, I headed Sybase from 2003 as the Country Manager for Sales and was responsible in rebuilding the image of Sybase in India and revive Country Sales figures. Under my leadership, Sybase India had taken the lead position in a maturing and evolving IT marketplace in India, focusing on its core strengths in Information management and Information mobility for the enterprise. For turning around Sybase in India, I had been awarded as the Best Country Director for Sybase worldwide for two consecutive years

I also worked with Computer Associates India as the National Manager for Information management solutions for India & SAARC. Prior to that I worked with companies like SGI, heading their Business Intelligence line of business & SAS India, heading the MFG, Retail & FMCG industry vertical for the India & SAARC Region respectively.
