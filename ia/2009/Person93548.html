Hector Botero’s Summary

Hector Botero leads Marketwire’s development, growth strategy and operations in the Asia-Pacific region, Latin America, India, Russia and the Middle East, cultivating communication networks in emerging global markets to serve the organization’s growing international capabilities. Hector came to Marketwire through its acquisition of International Media Services (IMS) Companies, a leading emerging markets news distribution and media management organization; he was IMS' founder and president as well as the former president of Business Wire Latin America and Business Wire China.

Hector has more than 20 years' experience directing international commerce activities in emerging markets and prior to his role at IMS Companies, he served as president of Conservation and Management International, a not-for-profit government organization developing media strategies and communications programs for the United Nations Environment Programme – Regional Office for Latin America and the Caribbean (ROLAC). He is currently the Managing Director of Hong Kong based Marketwire China Holdings, Ltd. and the Chief Representative of the Marketwire Representative Office (RO) in Shanghai, China.

Mr. Botero oversees other Marketwire companies and investments including NoticiasFinancieras, Latin America’s largest news syndication service which includes over 50 of the region’s largest and most influential newspaper publishing groups and Rio de Janeiro and Sao Paulo based Comunique-se Comunicacion Corporativa, the largest workflow solution, media contacts database and press release distribution service in Latin America.

Hector Botero’s Experience

  • Vice President, Emerging Markets

    Marketwire

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Information Services industry)

    January 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    Responsible for development and implementation of a growth strategy in the Asia-Pacific, Latin America, India, Russia and Middle East regions for Marketwire, the only fully integrated North America-based global newswire. Marketwire delivers its clients' news to the world's media and financial communities, fulfilling disclosure requirements in North America in compliance with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and the Toronto Stock Exchange (TSX). Having merged companies (Market Wire and CCNMatthews) in April 2006, and enjoying a combined history of 25 years of service, Marketwire is now majority-owned by OMERS Capital Partners, the private equity arm of one of Canada's largest pension funds. Marketwire distributes the majority of press releases issued by publicly traded companies in Canada and serves more than 8,000 clients worldwide through 17 offices on four continents. For more information, visit us at www.marketwire.com

  • President + CEO

    IMS Companies, LLC.

    (Information Services industry)

    November 1996 — January 2008 (11 years 3 months)

    IMS Companies was started in 1996 through an alliance with Inter Press Service (IPS), a 50 plus year old non-governmental organization news agency headquartered in Rome, Italy, as part of the United Nations Environment Programme project Tierramerica. In its inception, IMS launched a region wide clipping service using IPS offices and correspondents throughout Latin America to read and clip hard copy articles from newspapers and magazines. Having consolidated a regional operations base in partnership with IPS, IMS next moved to create newswire operations to offer press release distribution services. Mr. Botero was instrumental in building Iberonews, the Iberia peninsula {Spain and Portugal}, Business Wire Latin America and Business Wire China – Mr. Botero was president of both until December 2007. Business Wire was acquired by Berkshire Hathaway in 2006.

    Other IMS investments and projects include NoticiasFinancieras and Comunique-se in Brazil.
