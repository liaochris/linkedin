Lori Kasbeer’s Summary

I graduated from the University of Arizona in 1994 with a B.A. in Communication. Throughout college and into my work life I have over 8 years experience writing reports, journals, office correspondence, and blogs.

I am also the book reviewer for Christian Women Online. I'm joined at CWO by writers such as actress Candace Cameron Bure from TV's "Full House," author's Shannon Woodward, Allison Bottke, and Bonnie Bruno. I have also had the awesome opportunity to personally interview Christian speaker Angela Thomas, actress Lisa Welchel, Grammy winner Rebecca St. James, along with many more.

I am also a contributor on MrsDealFinder's Blog (http://mrsdealfinder.com/author/laurel520)

And beginning a Beauty Review Blog along with my sister who is a registered Estition. If you are a company that would like their products reviewed, feel free to contact me. (http://laurelwreathsreflections.com/productreviews/)

I have been married to my best friend for 15 years, I live in a house full of boys; three to be exact. However, if you add the cat and the dog, testosterone runs rapid. I look forward to working with you. You can also find me at my personal blog, Lori's Reflections to learn more about me.
