Nuria Sadurni’s Summary

I define myself by nature curious. I am a creative thinker with real passion for change and innovation.

Although I started as a civil engineer my professional career, after I completed Marketing Studies enrolled in an Executive Master for two years in ESADE Business School, I decided to focus on marketing.

Since then I have developed most of my professional career undertaking marketing roles with primarily focus on analysis. With international exposure as a key contributor in strategic planning, I have been working in different marketing roles across multiple sectors in both client and agency side. From industrial to later on FMCG markets, and lately being involved in new technologies, online, social media, e-commerce and search.

During all this time I have had exposure managing varied projects, from qualitative and quantitative research studies, branding , forecasting trends, competitive analysis, assessing business opportunities and working with marketing strategies providing analysis, insight and giving recommendations to different organizations and multinationals.
