Daniel McGinn’s Summary

Creative and energetic reporter and writer with deep expertise in business. Author of "HOUSE LUST: America's Obsession with our Homes," and biweekly real estate columnist for Newsweek.com. Recently assumed editing responsibility for Project Green, Newsweek's coverage of energy and the environment. Successful freelance writer whose stories have run in Wired, Inc., Fast Company, Boston Magazine, the Boston Globe Magazine, and other publications.
