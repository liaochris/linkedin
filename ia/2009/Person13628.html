Andrea Walsh, PhD, JD’s Summary

Skilled patent attorney having over 15 years of progressive experience in strategic global patent procurement, enforcement and evaluation within corporate and private practices. Extensive expertise in patent searching, drafting and prosecuting global patent applications, litigation management, formulating validity, clearance, infringement, and patentability opinions, negotiating and drafting agreement and licenses, and counseling researchers and high-level management on IP concerns, including issues related to branded and generic drugs and Hatch Waxman legislation. Excellent analytical, communication, and organizational skills with strong international experience. Dynamic leader with extensive knowledge in multiple fields, encompassing biochemistry, molecular and cellular biology, chemistry, and engineering. Experienced in patent litigation electronic document review and coding. Over 20 years experience in patent searching..
