Susan Fitzgerald Reichert, MNA’s Summary

Susan Fitzgerald Reichert, MNA is the founder and CEO of Strategic Nonprofit Solutions (SNS). SNS is a consulting company which helps cooperating associations and nonprofit organizations in growth strategies including organizational assessments, strategic planning, financial planning, fundraising and program development, board and staff leadership development, nonprofit retail operations, collaboration, and general capacity building utilizing facilitation and training.

Susan has her Master degree in Nonprofit Administration, has over 20 years in nonprofit and business management.

Susan served as Executive Director of Sierra State Parks Foundation (SSPF) for 5 years; SSPF is the non-profit cooperating association 501(c)(3) for the following CA State Parks: Donner Memorial State Park, Tahoe Recreation Area, DL Bliss State Park, Emerald Bay State Park, Sugar Pine Point State Park, Grover Hot Springs State Park, Bodie State Historic Park and Mono Lake Natural Tufa Reserve. She grew SSPF to become the fifth largest cooperating association affiliated with California State Parks – out of 85 associations.

She has brought her expertise to the rest of the cooperating associations throughout the state by conducting seminars and workshops at the annual CALPA conference and as an instructor at the CA State Parks Training Center in Asilomar.
