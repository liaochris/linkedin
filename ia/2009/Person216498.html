Sherri Raftery, M. Ed.’s Summary

I am a Public Speaker, Toastmaster and Performer.
Topics I speak on are Domestic Violence, Foster Care and growing up as a resident and client of The Home for Little Wanderers. www.thehome.org

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Performing Arts and Communications from Emerson College and a Master's Degree in Education with a Concentration in Arts and Learning from Endicott College.

My poetry submissions have been published locally and I am a member of the Theatre Co. of Saugus.

Please visit my website: www.findyourplatform.com

I was honored by The Home for Little Wanderers on May 28, 2008. www.thehome.org
