Ritwik Dey’s Summary

I started my design career with print design and advertising. Since my days in engineering college, I have been trying to combine design and technology to create meaningful communication pieces and giving life to conceptual projects.

I've taught design and information architecture, developed visual representations of complex data sets, created code-based visualization projects and designed online interaction environments to meet specific strategic and design goals.

I'm actively involved in several non-profit ventures for social causes, espcecially in India. I intend to use my expertise in design and technology to execute projects that will impact environmental/nature conservation efforts.
