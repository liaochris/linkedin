Himani M’s Summary

My professional experience varies from working with a jewelry export house- insight into the nitigrities of overseas markets, to being a faculty in my alma mater, to working at the extreme grassroot level in Indian handicrafts industry- where the finer nuances of design bring about huge difference in many lives, to working in the most high-end luxury market- designing directly or indirectly for various brands of Richemont Group, SA.
Having worked in diverse environments endorsing ‘design’, I have a comprehensive understanding of design application & process, its peripheries & related domains. I understand the responsibility of design & its power to create value in daily life.

Himani M’s Specialties:

Lifestyle products, Product development & animation of Line, Opportunity mapping, Trends & Forecasting, Luxury consumers, Brand & Identity, Visual merchandising & story telling

    The design studio deals with the corporates, manufacturing & export houses and various NGOs dealing in handicraft products, offering them client-specific solutions in product design, styling, trend forecasting, visual merchandising, etc.

    Chicory Chai takes the onus of protecting design in spirit, through action & by Law.
