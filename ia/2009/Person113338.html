Jim Ordway’s Summary

Budget getting tight or even tighter? I specialize in identifying areas of opportunity to increase efficiency within Facility Operations while reducing costs. Raise the level of quality provided by your maintenance staff within your Education Facilities or Business, utilizing technologies and processes designed to deliver results while lowering your operating costs.

- Baseline Audits
- Identification of areas of strength and weakness
- Strategic planning to move operations from where they are to where they need to be
- Plan implementation strategies
- Measurements to ensure success

Reduce costs, increase effectiveness and deliver results that deliver a healthy building. Your building can’t just appear maintained anymore, there has to be processes in place to ensure the building is healthy and safe for all occupants, especially students.

I am a results-oriented professional with proven abilities in improving efficiencies of operations, managing projects, team building and planning. Able to identify areas of strength and weakness and implement policies, standards, changes in operations, and systems that optimize productivity and fiscal responsibility. Demonstrated ability to motivate staff to maximum productivity and control costs through the most efficient use of manpower and available resources.
