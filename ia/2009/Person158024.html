Choirul Amri’s Summary

Senior Trainer on various Microsoft development technologies, specializing on ASP.NET, SharePoint platform, SQL Server, and business intelligence. Strong technical background as a network administrator, DBA, and developer.

Choirul Amri was a Co-founder of IlmuKomputer.Com, which is the largest elearning portal in Indonesia. Actively writes tutorials, articles, books, and speaking on various seminars and community workshops. He manages SQL Server user group in Indonesia (sqlserver-indo@yahoogroups.com) a PASS Chapter and Culminis member.

Received Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP) award in ASP.NET on 2005, 2006, and 2007. He was also named as an IT Hero on Microsoft Partner Summit Singapore, together with the Windows Server 2008 Launch Wave.
