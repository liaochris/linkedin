Sam Shoulders’s Summary

I have returned to the Bay Area, and I am actively seeking employment as a Interaction Designer. I have recently completed a Masters Certificate in Emergency Management and ICT, with an emphasis on interaction design and new technologies to support first-responders, from The George Washington University. I received a M.S. in Human Computer Interaction Design (HCID) from Indiana University, Bloomington's School of Informatics in 2008.

I want employ novel approaches to exploring how people use tools and technologies in everyday action in order to better inform the design of interactive technologies. To this end, I rely upon my interdisciplinary skill sets, that I have developed from both work and education.
