Ray Horn’s Summary

I am working to make history by creating Web 4.0 before anyone else gets there.

I have created a product called Vyper-CMS(tm).

Vyper-CMS(tm) is a fast powerful low-memory data-driven Content Management System built to serve content in the 180-380 requests per second range using low-cost low-end Pentium-D servers using very little RAM (typically less than 100 MB) using next-generation techniques.

You can see a sample Vyper-CMS(tm) site online now at http://www.vyperlogix.com.

What makes Vyper-CMS(tm) different than every other CMS you might be aware of ?

Vyper-CMS(tm) is different because it can adapt to whatever Application one may want it to serve and manage content for. Need Vyper-CMS(tm) to serve Wiki content ? No problem, just feed Vyper-CMS(tm) a template for the Wiki you want to handle and Vyper-CMS(tm) automatically handles your Wiki content. Need a Blog ? Just feed Vyper-CMS(tm) a template for a Blog and you've got a Blog. Need to deploy your own FaceBook or MySpace ? No problem, just feed the templates to Vyper-CMS(tm) and watch it serve the same functionality as FaceBook or MySpace.

Like I said, you can see a sample Vyper-CMS(tm) site online now at http://www.vyperlogix.com and it is only going to improve with time. I am adding Google inspired static content handling to Vyper-CMS(tm) to allow any site built using Vyper-CMS(tm) to load faster than is now possible with the version 1.0 release.
