Jon Michaeli’s Summary

Jon helps companies grow by developing and implementing winning marketing strategies. His particular focus is companies that rely on the Internet and e-commerce as key marketing and sales channels. His industry expertise includes travel, social media, online software, and mobile applications. Jon has a strong record of increasing core metrics such as sales, subscriptions, acquisitions, conversions, upgrades, and cost savings.

Jon is known for his drive to help companies succeed and thrive. He leverages his comprehensive marketing skill set to consistently produce top- and bottom-line results. People Jon has worked with describe him as very smart, analytical, dedicated, results-driven, a visionary, and possessing “astounding marketing prowess” and strong leadership skills.

Additionally, Jon knows how to leverage limited resources to achieve aggressive goals, a skill which, in recessionary and/or startup environments, is invaluable. In terms of people management, Jon is adept at building, managing, and motivating U.S. and international teams. He has managed teams as large as 12 people and has been accountable for yearly marketing budgets as high as $3 million with full P&L responsibility. He also has successfully built relationships with many “big brand” partners and sponsors. Jon is bilingual (English/Spanish) and manages effectively in multicultural contexts.

Jon Michaeli’s Specialties:

branding, online marketing (search, affiliate, social media/networking) and merchandising, product marketing, big brand partner marketing and management, viral and WOM (word-of-mouth) marketing, SEM (search engine marketing), SEO (search engine optimization), strategic planning, market research (primary and secondary, qualitative and quantitative), financial modeling and forecasting, email and direct mail, P&L Management, project management and implementation, product launches
