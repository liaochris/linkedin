Ross Freedman’s Summary

Ross is a Co-Founder of RightPoint Consulting. Ross is an entrepreneur and a recognized leader in the Information Technology field. Ross' career has been focused on helping clients increase revenue, reduce cost and gain competitive advantage using a combination of technology, process improvement and industry expertise. Throughout his career, Ross has consulted with more than 100 different clients ranging from large Fortune 500 companies to middle market firms and small closely held partnerships to improve their business operations.

Ross has extensive experience in developing and executing IT roadmaps, advising clients on customer relationship management issues, implementing sales, marketing and service best practices, managing complex user-centered website and Intranet initiatives and improving operational efficiency by leveraging technology.

Due to Ross' extensive IT and business experience, he is often involved in merger & acquisition transactions - helping private equity and venture capital firms assess target companies. Ross also speaks regularly on how companies can leverage technology to drive profitable growth.

Ross Freedman’s Specialties:

Offering a rare blend of business and technology competencies, Ross is recognized for his success in growing his clients’ sales and profits.
