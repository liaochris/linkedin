Jennifer Trelewicz’s Summary

I am CTO of Google, Russian Federation and visiting professor for the Dept. of Computing, Mathematics, and Physics at Waterford Institute of Technology (Ireland). I am making a lecture series at Novosibirsk State University (Novosibirsk, RF), and have lectured at State University of Management (Moscow, RF), Moscow State University (RF), in Plovdiv, Bulgaria, Colorado State University, University of Maryland, Baltimore County, NASA Goddard Space Flight Center.

I am fluent in English and Russian, written and spoken.
