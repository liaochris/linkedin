Kurt Karlenzig’s Summary

I help people, organizations and companies envision the future and activate strategies to get them there.

About Me:
I'm an experienced interactive professional who has built a career at the intersection of creativity and technology. As a passionate advocate for better experiences, I've set the vision and led the growth of 3 successful interactive groups – delivering both outstanding creative and solid business results in the process. Over the past 15+ years , I have been fortunate to lead some exceptional teams and work with some world’s most admired brands. My hybrid roles in the business have had me directly involved in the strategy, creative and development of numerous digital initiatives for notable brands such as adidas, Gatorade, P&G, US Army and many others.
