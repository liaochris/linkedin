Tom Sato’s Summary

Business Development in Japan
Technology sourcing, alliance partnership, Japanese marker entry, distribution deals. Project start with understanding your business model and identifying the right potential partners, then meeting with the key person in that company to get the business going. Then, follow through with negotiation, agreement documentations, making sure that two parties understand each others strength and issues, and closing the deal. Once the deal is signed the groundwork of business development, localization, technology transfer, sales development and finally getting your products to the customers.

Having done new business development for Microsoft with Windows launch back in 80ies and having build two businesses, one in Silicon Valley (E-commerce business with $Ms in sales) and Tokyo (Financial Information portal) I understand exactly what is needed to start up a business from ground up.

I current specialize in all area of clean technology and IT sector.

And I am very reasonable. Don’t spend millions on expensive global consulting company, just email me.
