Flynn Ravner’s Summary

I am a professional with twenty years of experience in the design, development, implementation, and project management of information systems, in the insurance, investments, financial services, and agri-business industries.

I am always interested in finding and developing a position that will allow me to apply my strengths and talents in an organization that will be both professionally stimulating and enjoyable, and provide opportunity for growth and advancement. This position does not necessarily have to be in technology or project management.

I am focused on the delivery of solutions through the use of sound methodology, planning, risk management, business analysis, requirements gathering, and a high level of quality assurance to ensure that business strategies and objectives are achieved. I seek the pragmatic balance between agility and control that I believe every organization has for having solutions delivered.

The combination of my consulting and industry experience has made me very adaptable to any environment, and allowed me to effectively assume any role.
