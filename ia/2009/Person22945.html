Andrew Wilson ’s Summary

Andrew is an experience design innovator who knows how to parlay his spectrum of talents into progressive visual concepts. His dynamic portfolio reveals the versatility and depth of a genuine creative professional. With his command of the entire creative process—sketching, wireframing, UI design, styleguide creation, interface development and concept visualization / implementation—Andrew has produced a stunning array of highly original work including illustration, web and Flash design, online marketing campaigns, print advertising, manual design, prototyping, branding and game animation.

Past client work includes:
AOL, Deloitte & Touche, Disney, eFax, Fox Interactive, Intel, Microsoft, Nestlé, Toshiba, Toyota, Zenith Insurance
