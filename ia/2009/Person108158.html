Jeff Lawniczak’s Summary

Jeff has been a successful Recruiter/Staffing Director in video games for over 18 years. He’s helped to staff several of the industry’s most talented and memorable teams. Working closely with renowned game producer, Mark Cerny, Jeff helped to build the original Crystal Dynamics and Naughty Dog teams who went on to create innovative games such as Crash Bandicoot. Jeff was also instrumental in growing the staff at Insomniac Games when they were launching the highly successful Spyro the Dragon series.

Jeff is selective with his clients, preferring to work only for top tier teams working on new and innovative AAA titles. He is currently the Staffing Manager at Red 5 Studios, a new MMO startup founded by key World of Warcraft developers including Mark Kern, former World of Warcraft Team Lead, and William Petras, former Art Director on World of Warcraft.
