Brian Walker’s Summary

Brian Walker
Principal & Founder
The Retail Doctor® Group Pty Ltd

As the principal and founder of The Retail Doctor Group in the Australasian and Asian market place, Brian brings considerable senior experience gained in the specialty retail, franchising and shopping centre sectors.

In addition, Brian has both operated in line management across various senior leadership roles and has successfully built his current consulting profile within the retail, franchise and service sectors.

The Retail Doctor specialises in the development and transformation of retail strategy into increased asset performance, increasing client sustainable differentiation models underpinned with independent systems and frameworks increasing revenue and profitability. These focus areas are delivered through The Retail Doctor ‘Business Fitness’ programs.
Awarded a BRW fast starter award 2009 and a member if the ebeltoft global retail consulting alliance (www.ebeltoftgroup.com)
