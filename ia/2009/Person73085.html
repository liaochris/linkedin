Emma Pirie’s Summary

Currently studying for a PgC in Career & Talent Management through the CIPD, Emma has a keen interest in the role HR and L&D policies play on the careers of senior executives.

With excellent organisational, client liasion and research skills, Emma brings an extra dimension to the team. Able to think laterally, she has highly developed problem solving skills. Emma is friendly and approachable, and is driven by a desire to exceed clients expectations through fabulous customer service.

Joining from a specialist search and selection business, having previously run her own successful event management company, Emma has significant experience in dealing with companies and high-net-worth individuals, at potentially stressful times, and always in the strictest confidence.

Previously Emma has worked as a Project Manager in the Web and Print industries, and before that as a Graphic Designer, having won international awards for her work.

Emma Pirie’s Specialties:

New business development, design & marketing, process development, sales, business networking, information and typographic design, usability and inclusive design.
