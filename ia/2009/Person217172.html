Shulah Jones’s Summary

Was Campaign Manager for the highly successful 'England's Golf Coast' initiative and engaged with national and international audiences to position the Northwest of England as 'the' English golfing destination. Also ensured the effective delivery of 2 very successful Open Championships at Royal Liverpool and Royal Birkdale Golf Clubs along with a number of funding partners.

Worked with NWDA and Culture Northwest to deliver the Industrial Powerhouse heritage tourism project and engage stakeholders in this brand-building initiative through partnership liaisons and relationship management delivery across Cheshire and Merseyside.

Managed day visitor marketing and PR for the Liverpool City Region resulting in over £2million of added value PR in the run up to and during Liverpool 08.

Successfully managed and delivered Liverpool City Region's travel trade activity from 2005-2008 and worked domestically and overseas to engage tour operators, booking agents etc in making Liverpool City Region their destination of choice.

Has worked overseas in Mexico, Japan, Europe and the United States to promote the Liverpool City Region and Britain's Heritage Cities to overseas tour operators and agents.

Developed a brand identity and then subsequently delivered the launch campaign for World Museum Liverpool in 2005 (the city's most popular visitor attraction).
