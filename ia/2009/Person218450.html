  • Business Development Manager, Upper Midwest

    Xerox

    (Public Company; XRX; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2006 — April 2009 (3 years 4 months)

    Manager North Central Division, Xerox Corporation Sini Ross joined Xerox Corporation in 2000 and has been a consistent top sales performer as a Graphic Communications Solutions Executive in the Central United States. Today, Sini is responsible for helping iGen3 customers successfully launch their investment into the exciting new frontier of digital printing. Sini holds a Bachelor of Architecture from the University of Minnesota, Institute of Technology, and an MBA in New Venture Management at The University of St. Thomas. Sini stumbled upon her passion for new ventures and marketing thanks to the combination of bad economic times and a positive attitude. Upon completing her degree in Architecture, she moved to Sweden and practiced Architecture for 3 years until a major economic shift (Prime lending rate increase 5x) caused Swedish Building Market to collapse. The Swedish unemployment rate was at an unprecedented 27 percent, architectural jobs were scarce and Sini’s mastery of the Swedish language was similar to the likes of Tarzan. In a time of crisis, what did she do? She founded Cowboys Western Store AB, a company that imported Western Clothing, Boots and Tack. The company grew to support two retail stores and had a strong mail-order customer base in Norway, Denmark, Finland and Sweden. The company was sold in 1998 so Sini could move back to the United States. The lessons that Sini has learned from her direct mail marketing business has been a real asset in helping the Graphic Communications Industry see the potential of 1 to 1 Marketing and “The New Business of Printing”. One of the key fundamental lessons that she teaches is that “Crisis is equal to Opportunity as long as you add a little Creativity”.
