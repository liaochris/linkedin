Robert Brack’s Summary

My goal at this point is simply to help Lead Research Group grow to its potential. I work with two of the most gifted and polished salesman and marketers that I have ever had the pleasure being around.

I have a more important goal of rebounding and re-establishing myself after a long bout of unemployment. I am thankful every day for the position I am in and hope to look back in a couple of years after excelling with LRG and laugh at the brief moment of confusion, uncertainty and fear for the future. They say that timing is everything and I am grateful that despite my troubles, timing put me infront of the group of winners that make up LRG.

Furthermore, I would like to help the businesses I serve grow long term and thrive even in a down economy. I want to be looked at with respect and acknowledged for knowing how to help businesses enhance sales and grow their businesses.

All of my financial and career related goals as it relates to my current position will come to fruition if I simply do this:

1) Go Out Of My Way To Service Clients
2) Enjoy My Work
3) Work Tirelessly For Customers
4) Keep My Competive Spirit
5) Continuously Learn Products And The Trade

I would say this about my goals: As a former sales mentor once said to me as it relates to talking to customers about services and products "Shouldn't Be A Problem". It will be done!!!!
