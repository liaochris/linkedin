Paul Anagnostopoulos’s Summary

I have a computer science degree from Brown University and have been working in the computer field ever since. My work is primarily in systems programming and programming language development. In 1988 I wrote a book for Digital Press, which led me into the book production business. Since then we have produced over 200 books in ZzTeX and LaTeX.

Paul Anagnostopoulos’s Specialties:

Technical book production for publishers and self-published authors, using TeX.

Computer language assemblers, disassemblers, compilers, "little language" translators, and text processors.

Languages: assembler, AWK, C, C++, Fortran, Gossip, Java, Lisp, PL/I, RPG, TeX
