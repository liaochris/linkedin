Brian Moss’s Summary

ILS Technologies, LLC (ILS) is a minority, service disabled-veteran owned full service Information Technology consulting company based in Maryland, with satellite offices in Washington, DC, Virginia and Missouri. We offer ground-breaking, state of the art and goal-oriented services. ILS employs highly dedicated professionals in the planning, development, and implementation of progressive and practical solutions for our clients. Our ability to combine business-driven objectives with advanced technology enables us to create key and well planned solutions requiring minimum investment. Our customer first focus has earned ILS a reputation for integrity, commitment, and quality for our customers.

Utilizing a wealth of industry knowledge and skills we have successfully completed projects in the following areas:

3. Systems Administration
4. Website Development and Maintenance
5. SSL & IPSec VPN Support and Maintenance
6. Symantec EndPoint Support and Maintenance including new deployments
7. Exchange Administration including deployment and management of new email server farm
8. WIFI Access Point deployment and management
