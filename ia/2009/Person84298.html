Gayle Rodgers’s Summary

From a junior high teacher to stay-at-home Mom to owner of a manufacturing company, my life has been fun, hectic, and varied. Now I am helping individuals and businesses stay connected and save money with my SendOutCard business. I help friends look, feel, and perform better everyday with my Univera LifeSciences business. Both of these businesses are a way to create wealth on a part-time basis or full-time.

My talks focus on Personality, Positivity, Passion and Play. Some topics I cover are finding your passion and purpose; goal setting; how to reduce stress and stay healthy; rediscover the child within; and how to lead a more joyful life. I love to work with teens and put on classes about making choices and self-esteem. My tag line is BE ALIVE AND THRIVE.
