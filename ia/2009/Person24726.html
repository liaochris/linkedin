  • Channel Partner Manager

    Reed Elsevier

    (Public Company; REL; Publishing industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Since July 2008 I'm the dedicated online professional for the largest media agency networks in The Netherlands. These are Group M, OMD, Aegis Media, Universal Media, Zeddigital, Digilogue, SVBmedia, Twist (Kobalt), Starcom and Mediaxplain. Thereby I cover all the main media agencies in The Netherlands.
