Andrew Wilmot’s Summary

I am a project manager by profession and a coach by choice ... in the fields of land development, nutritional health and some sports.

As a chartered engineer with 15 years experience and a qualified project manager of 10 years standing, I have worked mainly on infrastrastructure development projects in the UK and Asia for large multi-disciplinary consulting organisations.

As an accredited health blogger, team leader and club leader, I encourage networking and sharing of helpful resources ... and through ethical enterprise, I am building a dynamic global organisation.

I use a powerful business model as a way to teach others entrepeneurship and to allow people to sustain and improve lives like I do by doing what they are good at.

Andrew Wilmot’s Specialties:

Topics: Nutritional health, fitness and land development.

Projects: business case development, project scope definition, project collaboration and project assurance - including programme management.

People: mentoring and coaching.
