Tom Cheevers’s Summary

For two decades I’ve crafted compelling, emotional communications for all kinds of clients–from regional brands like Alaska Airlines and the Seattle Mariners to national campaigns for Kraft, Best Buy, Microsoft, and Disney.

Today, I’m media agnostic, and particularly interested in creating solutions that cross mediums and connect with customers on multiple levels and in multiple ways.

Tom Cheevers’s Specialties:

Hands-on creative and digital direction for brands as diverse as Best Buy to the Neighborhood Electric Vehicle Company. 25 years of proven brand success in major and small markets. Voiceover talent for diverse clientele.
