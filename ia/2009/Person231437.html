Ron Nash’s Summary

The Nash Group started in 1996. It was the brainchild of our illustrious founder, Ron Nash. Since then our company has grown its clients and services.

Something new and exciting! April 1, 2008! Due to an overwhelming success with candidate coaching and interview preparation, we’ve launch a new business called "The Friend Zone.TV". A video based, interactive training tool, that focuses on job interviewing, personal development, resume preparation, and a host of other topics. Using video to expose you to world class coaches who teach the segments, these tools will change how a candidate interviews forever. This is the first online job seeker course ever developed. During a recession job seekers can't afford pass these types of resources; Go to www.TheFriendzone.tv and check it out!
