Tony Rizzo’s Summary

A seasoned professional with 20 years of experience in consulting and services delivery leadership in the Enterprise Performance Management, Business Intelligence, and Analytics space. Tony is a creative and flexible self-starter with a proven track record of building and leading high performance teams, assimilating new technologies, and defining strategic vision. With a relentless focus on delivering customer value and satisfaction, Tony has had the opportunity to help some of the world’s leading companies take advantage of software and technology to achieve business transformation through successful implementations of Enterprise Performance Management solutions. He has developed creative and innovative implementation methodologies to improve time to value enabling customers to maximize the ROI from their software & technology investments. Tony is a dedicated leader who not only understands detailed business requirements and complex technical concepts but can also lead meaningful discussions at the CxO level. Tony has been a key contributor to virtual account teams throughout the entire customer engagement lifecycle consistently contributing to both software license and services sales growth. Tony is committed to driving financial results while maintaining the highest levels of customer satisfaction.

Tony was most recently a SVP at HCL Axon where he established a new practice focused on SAP’s Office of the CFO product suite (Business Planning & Consolidation, Strategy Management, Profitability & Cost Management, GRC, and BusinessObjects). Tony was a co-founder of the OutlookSoft Corporation, a recognized innovator for unified planning & consolidation software, acquired by SAP in June 2007. Tony spent a year at SAP as the VP of Field Services for the Business Planning & Consolidation product. Tony spent six years as a consultant at Hyperion and implemented hundreds of successful planning, consolidation, and reporting solutions.

Tony Rizzo’s Specialties:

Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) * Business Intelligence (BI) * Analytics * OLAP * Services Sales and Delivery * EPM strategy definition * EPM solution design * Business Process Improvement * Rapid Implementation Methodologies.
