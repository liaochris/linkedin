Taylor Snyder’s Summary

I am an Art Director graduate from the Advertising Design program at MCAD (Minneapolis College of Art and Design) under the tutorship of Nancy Rice (co-founder of Fallon Worldwide). I am also sustainable design certified. I have appeared in "The Show" for a Silver in Art Direction of Print, Applied Arts Magazine for an awarded "win" in student print and in the October 2007 issue of HOW Design Magazine. I also have shown in the prestigious Art Director's Club Student Portfolio reviews in New York this past year (attended both the Design and Ad reviews).

Taylor Snyder’s Specialties:

Award Winning Designer, Certified in Sustainable Design, Well-Versed in Package Design, Branding, & Product Design, Fine Art Printer (screen printing & photo-polymer plates)
