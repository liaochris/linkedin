I have over seventeen years of experience in the healthcare consulting arena, including significant experience in implementations of clinical and financial applications at both small and large multi-specialty centers. It’s because of this background, that I am currently working as the Vice President for Profero Solutions.

Profero Solutions is dedicated to delivering customized implementation strategies to healthcare facilities to maximize the ROI on their EMR investment. This investment, in many cases, is one of the most significant in terms of dollars and process change.

One of more innovative programs at Profero Solutions is utilizing clinical resources to help with "at the elbow" support during the go-live process of clinical applications. This model has proven to save clients tens of thousands of dollars in the past few years.

As one of the initial Profero Solutions employees, I have been involved in all of the EMR client projects.

Recent Profero Project oversight include: University of Maryland Medical Center, Baylor College of Medicine, Parkland Hospital, Dallas Children's Hospital, Omaha Children's Hospital, Cleveland Clinic Foundation, Cleveland Clinic - Weston, University of Florida Medical Center

Being a part of the growth of Profero has been rewarding and exciting. Our flexibility in the way we approach each implementation has proven successful in every type of healthcare environment. I’m particularly pleased to be an integral part of EMR becoming the standard in the US and the positive results that these implementations will have on patient care. If you have any questions or would just like to say ‘hello’ please message me!

Warm regards, 

Barry.
