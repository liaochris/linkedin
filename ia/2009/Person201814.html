Rob Coats’s Summary

I would say I am a economic opportunist, outspoken non-conformist, and groundbreaking philanthropist. I have over 8+ years in Marketing and Recruiting Experience.I genuinely love networking and "connecting" with like minded people. If there is anyway I can add value to whatever you are inspired to do please let me know. I am one the most down to earth and loving individuals you can can ever meet.

My passion is using the power of social media to develop awareness and relationships around businesses, ideas and issues. Network Your Life connects innovative entrepreneurs and professionals who are committed to cultivating and exchanging information and opportunities while still giving back to community at the same time. Every month NYL sponsors a different non profit or charity.

Public Speaking:
I have gone through extensive public speaking training through Toastmasters International and Les Brown Speakers Training Courses. I love speaking and inspiring young minds of the future. I make it my goal to speak to atleast 10-15 schools and colleges each year. I speak on various things like thinking out of the box, turning passion to profit, branding yourself on the internet, and turning what people thought was nothing to something.

I am Founder of The Non Profit "The One Dollar Dream", an online donor site that give full scholarships to college students that are on the deans list that furthering their business education to studying abroad. We sent our first candidate to Europe for three and a half weeks all expenses paid (Flight, Food, Hotel etc) to study under the Top Companies and Business Leaders in the world. This trip was special because we only had 5 weeks to raise $8,000. We worked extremely hard and before we knew it we had raised what he needed to go and a extra $2,000 so he could have a great start for the next quarter. Helping students realize anything is possible as long as you believe is the motto I live by.
