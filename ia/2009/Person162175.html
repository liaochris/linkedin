Michael Shirley’s Summary

Pharmacologist with discovery and development experience, specializing in drug metabolism, pharmacokinetics, PK/PD and small molecule bioanalysis. Provider of technical and strategic input for accelerating product candidate selections. Major contributor toward entry of 6 compounds into the clinic, with two drug approvals.

Michael Shirley’s Specialties:

Drug discovery, quantification, assay validation, liquid chromatography-mass spectrometry, drug metabolism, structure elucidation, pharmacokinetics-pharmacodynamics (PK/PD), in vitro-in vivo extrapolation, allometric scaling, formulation, microdialysis, outsourcing, regulatory documentation
