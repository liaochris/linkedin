Ed Eliot’s Summary

Developer and technical manager with 10 years experience in web development.

Strong web development knowledge with over 3 years specialising in client side technologies. Experience working on high traffic sites including the Yahoo! home page.

Management of functional and cross discipline teams of up to 10 developers. Part of management team for wider group of 50 client side developers at Yahoo!.

Helped to build a world class web development team at Yahoo! with in depth involvement at all stages of recruitment process.

Experience of waterfall and agile development methodologies.

Ed Eliot’s Specialties:

Management of technical teams, (X)HTML, CSS, JavaScript (with an emphasis on Progressive Enhancement), PHP, MySql, Web Standards, Accessibility, SEO. Familiarity with Django and Python. Previously worked with TCL, ASP, Sql Server and other proprietary technologies.
