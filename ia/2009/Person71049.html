Elaine Silva Flesch’s Summary

My past experience includes engineering and project management work in real-time, telecommunications systems for wireless and IP networks, data-centered applications.

Building on knowledge acquired in these positions I am currently a Product Line Manager (business-to-business) to a network vendor to tier one and tier two telecommunication carriers working with different teams across multiple departments.

Known for my ability to quickly learn new technologies and products, I also offer solid experience in:
- Understanding complexities of efficient, effective data/database management,
- Process improvement (with a focus on high-quality and low cost), and
- Team development as both a team leader and mentor

Finally, I continue to develop skills as an accomplished Toastmaster (Distinguished Toastmaster) and member of the National Society of Hispanic MBAs (NSHMBA).

My technical background includes
- UTRAN/UMTS/GPRS networks
- CDMA 2000/EVDO networks
- Mobile IP networks
- IP application protocols and IP network troubleshooting
- other wide-ranging experiences in operating systems, programming languages, architectures, and technologies
