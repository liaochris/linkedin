Adesh Singh’s Summary

I currently work with Yahoo! search and display advertising user experience group.

I have around five years of industry experience in designing business applications for medium and large scale enterprises. I started my career as a Usability Engineer with PeopleSoft and later moved to Oracle Applications User Experience group as an Interaction Design lead. I have worked across solutions for various business domains including CRM, SCM, Manufacturing, US Govt. Contracts, OnDemand solutions for various mainstream products from PeopleSoft Enterprise, JD Edwards EnterpriseOne, Siebel CRM, Oracle Fusion etc. Recently, I worked as the lead designer for Oracle's first ever Fusion Applications for Sales, which were showcased in Oracle Openworld 2007 (www.sales.com).

In my spare time, I prefer to get involved with HCI community together through conferences, connecting with academic institutions and student community from various design schools in India. I am also an active member of ACM SIGCHI, UPA, IxDA and Computer Society of India, and visited various HCI conferences including CHI 2006 at Montreal Canada.

I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur with Master’s degree in design. My academic research was focused on designing interactions for touch screen interfaces. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science & Engineering with majors in Software Engineering and Communication Systems.

Adesh Singh’s Specialties:

User Centered Design, Interaction Design, Software Usability Analysis, User Interface Design, Leadership in Design Implementation, Developing Design Standards Guidelines and Patterns, Consensus Building, Project Planning and Management, Project Negotiations, Design Communication, Usability Evaluations, Usability Testing
