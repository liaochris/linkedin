Patrick Boot’s Summary

I have almost 15 years of experience in the dynamic world of mobile communication. I worked building up departments on Product Marketing, Purchasing, Service and Repair. I’ve worked for a mobile handset distributor, a mobile network operator, a mobile handsets service and repair company and an OEM manufacture of mobile phones and CE products.
