Linda Stetson, MLS’s Summary

* Library Director for over 13 years
* Librarian for over 20 years.
* Experienced "change agent" for libraries in distress.
* State library departmental management.
* Interest and expertise in library service to people with disabilities.
* Experience in working with diverse populations.
* Guest lecturer at Simmons College, Boston, for special education teachers working on their master's degrees in special ed., and in the Graduate School of Library and Information Science.
* Presenter of workshops on "Disability Awareness & Etiquette" for librarians and teachers.
* Wish to continue and expand my services as a consultant for disability awareness workshops for librarians and teachers.
* Ran as a candidate for the Wyoming House of Representatives while living in Cheyenne, in 1994. Would be interested in running for public office in the future.
