Lisa Myers’s Summary

Lisa Myers has eight years' experience in Marketing of which 4 in Search. After working for Base One as Head of Search for 4 years she decided to set up her own search venture. Lisa founded her own company, Verve Search in January 2009. Verve Search offers strategic search consulting as well as implementation of SEO, PPC and Social Media Services.

Lisa won the "B2B Newcomer of the Year" award at the B2B Marketing Awards 2007. And in 2008 Lisa won "Best Use of Technology by Under 30 Year Old Woman" at the Blackberry Women & Technology Awards. This year (2009) Lisa made the '35 women under 35' High Achievers list by Management Today (and published by The Times).

She has authored a chapter on search techniques in the book B2B Marketing Experts (published in 2007), written a chapter on SEO in the "B2B Marketing - Best Practice Search Marketing Report (2008)", and writes a regular 'Search Column' for B2B Marketing Magazine.

Lisa is heavily involved with the global SEO community and founded the blog SEO-Chicks (www.seo-chicks.com) in June 2007. Lisa also blogs for the European Search Blog; Search Cowboys www.searchcowboys.com

Lisa is very passionate and driven about everything search, if you want to talk search Lisa is always keen to listen and input.

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO), Pay Per Click (PPC), Social Media Optimisation (SMO), Blogging, Web Analytics, Online Marketing, Online Strategy
