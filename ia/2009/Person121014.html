Josh Bourne’s Summary

As an online branding strategist and policy advocate, I have devoted my career to helping brand owners promote and protect their trademarks on the Web. Along with fellow Internet industry veteran Phil Lodico, I am the co-founder and managing partner of FairWinds Partners LLC, a full-service Internet consulting firm. We advise Global 500 and other leading brands on how to navigate the domain name system, Internet traffic and marketing strategies, and corporate online risk management.

The FairWinds team delivers solutions and offers advocacy on matters involving domain names, online trademark enforcement and direct navigation (a common practice in which online users directly type URLs into their browsers). We first analyze each client’s domain environment, then implement plans to minimize unnecessary expenditures, optimize online marketing efforts, prevent cyber fraud, and increase online revenues.

Recognizing that the most effective way to fight online trademark infringement is through legislative policy, I also serve as the president of The Coalition Against Domain Name Abuse, Inc. (CADNA). This nonprofit association builds awareness about and advocates policies to stop criminal infringement of brands and trademarks online. To further this mission, I am a member of the International Trademark Association’s Internet Committee and speak frequently at major industry events.

On behalf of the FairWinds team, we look forward to helping you achieve your online business goals.

Josh Bourne’s Specialties:

International management consulting, branding and identity, intellectual property protection, online business strategies to drive measurably improved Internet traffic.
