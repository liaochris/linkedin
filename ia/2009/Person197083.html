Ryan Huff’s Summary

Ryan is a seasoned technology professional with over 10 years of experience implementing strategic technical projects for Fortune 500 corporations and small-to-medium business alike. With deep experience in technology consulting, project and product management, SDLC processes, and business process optimization, Ryan works closely with clients to help them get a jump-start implementing Software-as-a-Service solutions leveraging Salesforce.com and Google technologies.

Ryan brings enthusiasm, professionalism, creativity, and high standards to the organizations he works with.

Ryan holds an MBA in Marketing and Business Strategy from the University of California, Irvine.
