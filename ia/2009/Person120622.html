Joe Birner’s Summary

I am always looking to advance my pknowledge to improve my proffesional careerr if possible.

I enjoy learning more about the Solidworks api model thru self taught methods and education. This can be used for the purpose of standarizing and customizing the software to be company specific. It can also integrate forms into it to help consolidate calculation tools and more easily access design information, as well as accelerate the design process.

These skills and interests can also help introduce a paperless enviroment into the engineering dept. through programming methods and intelligent forms. Overall it helps with standardizing and organizing the flow of information through the engineering dept.

I am fairly proficient with Solidworks, as a modeler, creating details, drawings or anything cad related. I also tend to excell at fixing complex models (Parts and assemblies) that may have been created incorectly in the past.

I've had experience designing parts, assemblies and sub assemblies, floor layouts, test fixtures, holding fixtures and plant layouts. I have had experience with parts made from plastic molds, metal castings, sand castings, tubing, plate and rapid prototyping.
