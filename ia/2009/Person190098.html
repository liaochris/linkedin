Priscilla Li’s Summary

Priscilla's experience in developing business strategy and commercialising new ideas draws upon an entrepreneurial mindset and tenacity for achieving excellence from a small start-up of 50 employees to a large enterprise of 50,000.

At a leading think tank, Priscilla started her career writing case studies for companies such as Motorola, GM, HP and Cisco providing insight into the impact of networked economies at the height of the Internet phenomenon.

After her consulting experience, she joined the consumer Internet services team at Bell Canada, managing the communication strategy for a portfolio of products including VoIP, online music store and IPTV. Priscilla later led the delivery of a new Internet product-line, pricing and branding strategy to support the largest capital investment of Canada's first fibre optic service.

After completing her Cambridge MBA, Priscilla continues to pursue her passion in business strategy and technology, providing differentiation and thought leadership in an evolving marketplace.

Priscilla Li’s Specialties:

Extensive experience in the technology Industry, leading multidisciplinary teams to deliver new products and services, redesign and upgrade old processes. Core competencies include team management, delivering business strategies and achieving operational excellence.
