niki gomez’s Summary

Nine years experience developing strategy for a broad range of creative industries (digital media, film and music) in Europe; specializing in increasing individuals’ engagement in media technologies and strengthening social communities. Founded two creative media companies; identified and funded by UK government as entrepreneur to develop and lead start-up; recognized as a Technology Star by The Sunday Times of London. Innovative, strong global vision, highly personable, effective manager of large scale, high-profile international project teams.
