Jim Macer’s Summary

Jim Macer is a Systems Engineering Consultant staff member at Enabling Technologies, Inc. (a privately-held, Texas-based Corporation). Enabling Technologies, Inc., formed in 1994 as a company developing and marketing PC-based business software, initially transitioned into a broader software and systems engineering consulting firm and is now expanding into products and services associated with "new media" (web-based digital audio and video).

Jim Macer is available for software engineering and systems engineering consulting/contracting roles that can capitalize on 15+ years of solid hands-on experience and leverage a broad range of core competencies.

Core Competencies:
* Web-enabled Audio and Video Production: planning, production, post-production for delivery to web, mobile and hand-held devices, DVD; web-based marketing & sales of digital products; screencasts, training material, webinars, web-based workshops.

* Project Management: planning/replanning, scheduling, budgets, staffing analysis, metrics gathering & analysis, proposals, ECPs.

* Project Requirements Analysis: statements-of-work, concepts-of-operation, business process improvement, system-level requirements generation, requirements decomposition, design contracts, interface control documents, ROI analysis.

* Contract Deliverables Preparation: engineering design review material for SRR, PDR, CDR; test plans & procedures; "as-built" design documentation; system administration and end-user documentation.

Jim Macer’s Specialties:

Experience with clients from start-ups to Fortune 500 companies. Can successfully interact with diverse teams from management to end-user.

* Willing to support a wide range of Software and Systems Engineering roles and responsibilities.
* Strong writing and presentation skills.
* Strong organizing and documentation skills.
* 15+ years of hands-on consulting, of which 10 years is highlighted in the "Experience" section below.
