Suzy Vance’s Summary

Trained as a lawyer, today I use my skills as a Strategic Coach - a professional positive presence when disruption or challenging situations occur:
- career transition coaching
- presentation/public speaking coaching
- change in ownership of a small business
- family-owned business coaching of on-going relationships
- unique and unusual team-building workshops, corporate and not for profit
- unusual exercises designed to reveal untapped skills and assets among staff members
- mediation in conflict situations - family, work, colleagues
- mentoring programs: launching, tweaking and evaluation

My career began as a divorce and labor lawyer representing clients in their fight to win battles resulting from conflict at home and at work.

As Chair of the Employment Litigation Department at Arnstein& Lehr, I represented individual and organizational clients successfully in class actions and individual litigation. I presented and prevailed in an argument before the United States Supreme Court. I also established state and national precedents.

Today, in an environment often calling for quick and astute perception skills and strategic awareness, I work with individuals of all ages as they make decisions, implement them and move forward in their lives. I help them discover, build on and use what they are good at to their advantage.

I also support K-12 and post-secondary mentoring programs:
developed and implemented mentoring programs including Hours For Ours Mentoring Program for elementary and middle school students of the Michigan City Area Schools, the STRIVE Mentoring Program, a collaboration between Rotary Club of Michigan City, the Success Through Education Program of Purdue University North Central and the Michigan City High School, focusing on first-generation-college-bound students.

I also have worked with high school and college young people as they clarify goals and objectives and make decisions based on their strengths.

Suzy Vance’s Specialties:

Coaching for individuals and small groups. Consultation for creation and enhancement of mentoring programs K-12 and post-secondary.
