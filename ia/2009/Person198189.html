Rich DelCore’s Summary

During my 34 years at P&G - I have worked in operations (Manufacturing), Finance (Business CFO, Accounts Recivable, Strategic Planning), Human Resources, and now Global Marketing and Brand Building.

In all of these positions I have had the opportunity to lead significant change processes - by utilizing my understanding of processes, people systems and the economics of change.

I hope to work another 3-5 years for P&G to finish the largest change process I have led - Marketing Agency Compensation Reinvention and the Re-invention of Branded Entertainment and then hope to use these experiences either in consulting or community service.
