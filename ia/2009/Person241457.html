Vanessa Polendo’s Summary

We are a skillful, down-to-earth and determined design boutique located in Austin, Texas with our eyes set to the sky. Our vision and our mission are the same: to utilize our skills & creativity to create the best images and web content possible. Our sincere and unrestrained passion for excellence drives our design. We live for coming up with neat color schemes, ingenious layouts and interest-catching web pages.

Graphic(At-X) firmly believes that an image is worth a thousand words and we hope we know the content and images we create speak volumes. We love our work and we put our all in everything that we do.
