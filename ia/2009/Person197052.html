Rhonda Porter’s Summary

Rhonda Porter is a top producer at Mortgage Master Service Corporation. She began her career in real estate in back in 1986 in the title and escrow industry on the ground floor as a doc puller. She worked in a variety of roles in the title unit, sales, and eventually as a branch manager. Her background in the title and escrow industry has given Rhonda an invaluable perspective. In escrow, Rhonda would know just by who the loan officer was, if the client was likely to be frustrated or relaxed for their signing appointment. This has impacted Rhonda’s business tremendously. It is crucial for her clients to be as happy with her from start to finish and to always understand what is happening at every step of the transaction. Since 2000, Rhonda has greatly enjoyed serving her Realtor and consumer clients as their Mortgage Planner and Consultant.

Rhonda has been recognized by the Seattle King County Association of Realtors as a nominee for Affiliate of the Year in 1997. R TEAM, a network group she founded over 10 years ago, was designed to help Real Estate Agents stay on top of current topics and to promote their listings and buyers needs. Rhonda is proud to be one of the few Loan Originators to have her Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist™ designation. Less than one quarter of one percent of Loan Officers today have taken the steps necessary have earned the CMPS™ title. In addition, Rhonda is a member of the Financial Planners Association of Puget Sound.

Her business is 100% by referral, due to the strong relationships she has grown and maintained with her business partners and clients.
