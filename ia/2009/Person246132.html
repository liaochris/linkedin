    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    May 1998 — February 2005 (6 years 10 months)

    Self-taught in the field of IT. In 1997, I created the first Internet site that combined restaurants in Belgium together. Resto.be then listed about 1,200 restaurants and saw immediate major success given its functions whereby Internet surfers were able to interact with the restaurants. Eight years later, Resto.be shows annual turnover of almost two million euros and is consulted by more than 15,000 visitors every day. It is the foremost dining site in Belgium. In parallel with Resto.be, I was responsible for the graphical and commercial development of Resto.lu, Luxembourg's equivalent of the Belgian site, Resto.fr for sites in northern France and Paris, and Resto.nl for the Netherlands. Restaurants.be was created shortly afterwards to offer Internet surfers a solution based primarily on the visual of the restaurants. 1999 saw the birth of hotels.be, a site for hotels in Belgium, recently extended to the rest of the world in cooperation with the firm Bookings.org.
