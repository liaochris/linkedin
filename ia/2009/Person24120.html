Barbara Talisman’s Summary

Passionate About Excellence in Non-Profit Management

Talisman Associates, Inc. is a consulting firm working with public benefit corporations in the areas of fund development, leadership training and executive coaching.

For 20+ years, I have committed myself to inspiring and educating donors, staff and volunteers in the non-profit arena. My passion for and experience in development results in boosted awareness and commitment within the community for my clients and a measurable increase in donor giving -- empowering my clients to advance their mission and achieve their goals.

Embracing a philosophy of excellence in development for non-profits, I apply a no-nonsense, streamlined and efficient approach to driving development efforts. No cookie cutters here, I design proposals and plans and strategies that are unique to each organization’s needs. With that, my clients are set up to build a strong future for themselves and their community.

When you select Talisman as your consultant and trusted advisor, you get me, not a junior consultant or less experienced professional, working on your behalf. I work with my clients one on one and share my network fully with each client.

Dedicated at all times, I live my clients' mission and considers myself a member of the staff who shares in the passion for the goal -- not merely as a “consultant” who shows up on occasion.

I define success in terms of setting my clients up to be independent of my help by building their capacity to create a significant community return on investment (C-ROI).

Most importantly, I work with a limited number of organizations at any one time. I offer each client my time, attention and an unwavering commitment to their goals.
