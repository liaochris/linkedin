Joel Vincent’s Summary

I am a partner and one of the founders of VinTank - a Digital Think Tank for the Wine Industry. I am in charge of Marketing Strategy both for the firm itself and for our clients. We are a specialized development firm helping companies get into and manage online wine business and ecommerce.

I am also the Founder and Executive Director of the OpenWine Consortium (the OWC), a global trade association whose mission is to partner technology with the wine industry to open new avenues of business and networking in the wine world. With over 2300 members and businesses among its membership, the OWC runs education and networking events around the globe and maintains the largest online social network dedicated to the wine industry.

Prior to OWC, Joel led product marketing and management teams at Cisco, Meru Networks, Netgear, and Lucent where he has successfully brought over 65 products and services to market worldwide, garnering awards ranging from Comdex best-in-show to a nomination for a ComputerWorld Smithsonian award.

He combines over 15 years of experience in the high-technology industry and a lifelong passion for wine with his degree from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) to provide a visionary voice to the wine and technology industries.

Joel Vincent’s Specialties:

Product marketing for high technology companies. I focus purely on what it takes to move from a robust, extensible technology to high-value, business-changing solution. Smart people make great technology, I make technology great!
