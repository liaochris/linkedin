Christoph Moellers’s Summary

A realistic and objectively critical 41-year-old professional with fifteen years of specialist and generalist management experience in both SMEs and MNCs. Dual nationality (Swiss and German). Currently Managing Director of Holcim Management Services (China) Ltd in Beijing.

(Holcim is one of the world's leading suppliers of cement, aggregates, concrete and asphalt. The Group holds majority and minority interests in more than 70 countries on all continents and employs approximately 90,000 people.)

High degree of flexibility and mobility, with strengths in leadership, negotiation skills and people development. Myers-Briggs type ESTJ.

My goals are to continue my development in satisfying and stimulating environments of high ethical standards; to enhance my work- and life-experiences personally, professionally and geographically; and to always retain my integrity and independence

Christoph Moellers’s Specialties:

Extensive experience in project management, business development, procurement consulting and general management with repeated exposure to start-up situations. Profound understanding of cultural sensitivities as a result of having lived in Switzerland, South Africa, Germany, Denmark, Singapore and China, with project assignments to various other countries across Asia-Pacific as well as to the US.

Christoph Moellers’s Education

  • IMD (International Institute for Management Development) - Business Programs

    IMD Holcim Senior Leadership Program 2007 — 2007

    IMD facilitates the Holcim Senior Leadership Program, a course for top and senior management of the Holcim Group that is built around Holcim's 14 leadership competencies with a strong focus both the individual as well as the organizational aspects of leadership.

    The key objectives of this course are

    - to enhance the quality of leadership in Holcim today, leaving the participants better equipped to face their challenges in strategy execution

    - to enhance the leaders’ willingness and ability to continue to develop themselves over time

    - to ensure leaders understand the importance of developing others and have the necessary skills to do so

    The program comprises a self-preparation phase, a six-day residential module and an individual coaching program for the following nine months.

  • IMD (International Institute for Management Development) - Business Programs

    IMD Holcim Senior Management Program 2005 — 2005

    IMD facilitates the Holcim Senior Management Program, a general management program that focuses on the enhancement of knowledge and skills for effective strategic execution at Group Company level. It emphasises the practice of an integrated management approach at this level comprising strategy, leadership, finance, marketing and manufacturing. Particular attention is also paid to people development and sustainable development.

    The program comprises a self-preparation phase, an eight-day residential module and a learning transfer module.

    The SMP is case study based and is designed to stimulate active participation and interaction. Generic cases are used to frame issues and to develop related concepts and frameworks.

    The transfer to Holcim realities is distilled in an active dialogue between IMD faculty, Holcim Senior Management and participants.
