John Tracy’s Interests:

There are three things that mean the most to me and this site unashamedly portrays those priorities: God, Family, and Country. All three of those are constantly being attacked in 21st Century America. It is my relentless pursuit to hold high the flag for these basic, fundamental values of our historical American culture, and to rally those who would strive to do the same.

John Tracy’s Groups:

WHO AM I? I am a CHRISTIAN first. I believe that Jesus Christ is God’s Son, and that He is the Savior of the world. Next, I am committed to my FAMILY. I am married to Kimberly Rose, and am the Father of two sons, Luke and Bryce. My vocational work has nearly always involved being a MUSICIAN. I love to perform, conduct, record, teach, and compose music. I am a PREACHER of the Word of God. I believe that the Bible holds the answer to every one of life’s problems. Finally, I have a passion for America to return to her roots that made her so great, established as “one nation under God” by our founding Fathers. My patriotic ferver has inevitably thrust me into the world of POLITICS. It is through these pursuits listed above that I strive to serve and make a difference in the world.
