    New Medium Enterprises

    (Media Production industry)

    2007 — June 2008 (1 year)

    Producing a range of content for the VMD high definition disc video format using Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, Apple Final Cut Studio and NME’s bespoke authoring software.

    I captured high definition video using Apple Final Cut Pro in different international standards (24P, 25P / 50i, 30P / 60i). This footage was then transcoded into either MPEG-2 or VC-1 using Rhozet Carbon Coder and Sonic Cinevision respectively. Audio streams where captured and encoded into AC3 format audio.

    Motion menus where created in Adobe After Effects using video elements from Final Cut Pro and static elements created in Adobe PhotoShop.

    Once I had compiled all the source material for the title and the client had approved the menus, the disk was then authored using bespoke HD VMD software.

    Clients included: BAC Films, EROS, ICON.

    Titles authored include: Chalte Chalte, Dor, Koyla, I See You, Miss Potter, Paheli, Sahara, Saawan, Spy Kids, Stormbreaker, Wild at Heart
