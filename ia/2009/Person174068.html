Nick Edelstein’s Summary

I have performed in many different ensembles: symphony orchestras, bands (Rock, Country, Progressive, and R&B bands), solo Jazz piano concerts, and everything in-between - even recorded lead guitar on two Rap albums. I've toured North and Southeast U.S. and entertained audiences from 10 to +100,000. I released two full-length albums: a studio CD-format in 2006 and a DPD-format (digital) in 2007. I'm endorsed by Holland Guitars and KOA, licensed via BMI, published by Sibelius Music. Most of my songs are available on iTunes, Amazon, and about 40 other download sites. ~NE

Nick Edelstein’s Specialties:

Live and studio performance, improvisation, composition;
