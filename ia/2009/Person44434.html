Christian Posse’s Summary

Fifteen years of expertise in the research, analysis, design, development, and delivery of cost-effective, high-performance technology solutions to meet challenging business demands in the area of predictive analytics, visual analytics, statistics, machine learning, data and text mining, knowledge management, information retrieval and social networks. Extensive qualifications in all aspects of project lifecycle development – from initial feasibility analysis and conceptual design through documentation, implementation, quality assurance testing, and user training/enhancement. Equally effective organizational leadership, team-building, and project management experience that produce award-winning solutions.
