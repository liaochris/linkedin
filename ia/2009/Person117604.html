John McLearran’s Summary

Jack-of-all Trades.

I have worked in many fields throughout my life as I try to better myself. I have always been driven and when the opportunity presented itself for me to move on to something better - I did. I have managed a successful hotel and was an Assistant Manager for a nationwide retail company.

Ever since I was a teenager I have done graphic / web design and the occasional pc repair or upgrade for myself and family members. It didn't take long for me to realize my love for technology and helping others.

I spent approximately 5 years of my life doing all I could to help those in need, working as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) where I continued my education and began working towards my Paramedic License. It wasn't long before my son was born and I made a career change. Working 24 hour shifts and in a job that puts you in some very "unstable" situations made me realize that I wanted to be around for my son. That's when I went into Nuclear Construction where I worked as a Journeyman. Basically it boiled down (no pun intended... well ok, so maybe there was) to me putting up scaffolding and then taking it down. To date it was the best paying job I have ever had. The only problem there was the work is seasonal (at best) and to keep a steady supply of money rolling in you have to travel. Once again a newborn baby doesn't allow for such behavior and it only lasted a little over a year.

My relationship with my son's mother became strained when she became addicted to prescription meds and she lost her job as well as put our family in jeopardy. So my son and I moved back to my hometown and I was able to get a job as an Assistant Manager for a nationwide retail company. I worked there several months before applying to Automated License Systems, Inc (Active Outdoors) where I now hang my shingle. I couldn't have asked for a more perfect fit. The company is perhaps the best company I have ever worked for and the people are of as high a caliber.
