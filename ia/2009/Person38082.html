Carmen Zella Nunez’s Summary

Over the past 10 years I have worked in video as an editor/producer for commercial companies, network companies, freelance, and as an experimental artist. In addition, I have developed graphic designs for several start-up companies including logos, website designs and print material.

My skills include working in large creative teams, as well as one-on-one with clients. I am adept in both supportive and leading roles, and enjoy increasing the quality of projects by implementing my technical, and creative skills.

My passion is working with other creative and focused individuals, to develop extraordinary, imaginative and innovative videos/design.
