Lisa Colasurdo’s Summary

Lisa Colasurdo has been an advocate of healthy eating and lifestyles for many years. Her company, Fruits of Life, is all about offering services that promote healthy living.

As a Personal Chef for several families, her goal has always been to offer her clients fresh, innovative meals while meeting their exact nutritional needs.

As a certified Nutritional Educator through the Global College of Natural Medicine, she has assisted clients in improving their health through dietary and lifestyle changes.

An educator at heart, Lisa has taught cooking classes that address specific health issues through the Northampton Community College Adult Education Program located in Pennsylvania. Her classes include “Life Without Wheat,” “Improving our Health through the Foods We Eat,” and “Healing Foods.”

Lisa has appeared as a guest chef for both PBS 39 and WFMZ-TV Channel 69.

In a desire to broaden the number of people that can share in her love of cooking and healthy living, her company’s main focus is currently producing professional cooking videos.
