Paul Treffers’s Summary

Global IT Manager responsible for Global Systems Management. Manages a global team in offices in 12 countries.

15 years experience in IT in various roles.

Pro-active, very pragmatic and self-starting. Capable of developing and leading high performing teams and teams from different cultural backgrounds. Adaptive and flexible approach towards technology and problems. This enables me quickly to respond to the needs of the business, not by point solutions but by embedding the solutions in a mid- or long term ICT strategy.

Good people management skills, combined with a strong technical background, capable of successfully design infrastructures and lead local or global projects involving Microsoft Windows Server infrastructures, Active Directory, Citrix, Terminal Server, collaborative systems like Exchange 2007, Sharepoint and other products based on the Microsoft Windows Server platform. VMware is an important part of the strategy going forward.
