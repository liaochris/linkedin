Ann Clarke’s Summary

Ann began her career in 1978 as a widely published writer and photographer. Hundreds of her pictures and articles have appeared in a variety of national magazines. As a marketing professional, her clients have included Fortune 500 companies as well as a variety of non-profits, small businesses, professional speakers, and top producer real estate agents. Ann became a Professional Member of the National Speakers Association in 1998 and continues to present workshops, keynotes to audiences across the country. Today Ann enjoys putting other people in the spotlight by sponsoring business conferences and workshops.

Nationally recognized as THE expert in "personal promotion marketing" Ann says, "You do business with people you know. It's all about relationships and building trust. Your marketing can set the stage to make it easier to build the connections you need to build a successful business. It's wonderful when people you have never met come up to you and say I feel as if I know you..."

For four years Ann was an Executive Managing Director with eWomenNetwork, bringing these talents to her Northern Front Range Chapter by creating events which help eWomenNetwork members build the connections and relationships with potential customers & clients.

For over 10 years Ann has sponsored workshops, seminars and conferences packed with GR8 ideas from successful business men and women who can't wait to share their knowledge and expertise with you. Ann continues to live her life-long mission to help women of all ages become more financially successful so they have better choices by bringing them the resources they need. "Take good notes," says Ann of her conferences,"because these speakers will shorten your learning curve to a successful business!"

Ann Clarke’s Specialties:

Professional speaker, writer & photographer. Specialist in relationship marketing, women in business, networking, small business, entrepreneurism, young entrepreneurs. Joined the National Speakers Association in 1998 and become a Professional member in 2000. Conference and event planner since 1998. Personal Promotion Marketing Specialist since 1978.

  • Sponsor, Facilitator & Event Coordinator

    The Outrageous You Conference

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    April 2008 — September 2008 (6 months)

    The Outrageous You Conference for inventors of new products or services was held in Ft. Collins Colorado on September 19, 2008 at the Ft. Collins Country Club. The theme was "From dream to reality: all you need to know to get your great idea out of your head and into the hands of consumers."

    The six topics were Create Your Team: Who should you line up before you begin? Protect Your Dream: Contracts, trademarks, copyrights and patents. Promote Your Dream: Marketing, public relations and promotions. Launch Your Dream: Licensing, fulfillment & distribution.

    Cost of the Conference was $118.85. The first 100 people registered received a VIP Guest Bag.

    10% of the net proceeds were donated to The Tickled Pink Foundation.

    The conference was such a success that future in-depth workshops are being planned to elaborate on the six topics.

  • Owner

    (Photography industry)

    March 1978 — December 1995 (17 years 10 months)

    As a professional sports photographer in the equine market, Ann specialized in "peak of action" shots. Her photos appeared in calendars and on covers of every major equine publication in the U.S. She photographed all horse sports, from rodeo to dressage, and every breed from Miniature Horses to Fresians. Ann, her Nikon cameras and her motor home traveled the Colorado/Wyoming/New Mexico horseshow circuit, and her show ring and win pictures were in high demand. Her stallion pictures, portraits and conformation shots were featured in hundreds of advertisements in breed magazines, but she was best known for her action shots. Reiners, dressage riders, hunter-jumpers, cutters and "can-chasers" appreciated Ann's ability to catch them at their best. Ann retired in 1995 when she sold her business.She still has her Nikon cameras...

Ann Clarke’s Interests:

Ann Clarke’s Honors:
