Ava Chisling’s Summary

As a content provider, Ava has more than 20 years experience working with national and international companies, including Air Canada, Quebecor, Standard Broadcasting, CFCF, CBC-TV, Just for Laughs, Spafax Publishing, among others. She has worked in film, tv, radio, print, internet content, and most other forms of media in Canada, Switzerland, the UK and Japan. Ava has won multiple awards for her work, including the 2004 Leon Levinson Prize from McGill’s Faculty of Law and a Canada Council Award for writing. She articled at Borden Ladner Gervais.
