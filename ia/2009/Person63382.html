Peter B. Diaz, CPA’s Summary

Peter is a Bay Area Native raised in Menlo Park. He is a licensed Certified Public Accountant with over 23 years of Tax Experience. His Tax Practice caters to businesses of all sizes and individual taxpayers. He holds a Masters in Taxation Degree From Golden Gate University and Undergraduate Degree in Accounting & Business From San Diego State University. He’s served over 10 years as Corporate Tax Director for two global publicly traded corporations and started his career with the Accounting Firm of Deloitte & Touche in Their San Jose Hi-Tech practice office. He has quantified over 100 million in tax savings over his career. Peter has instructed a series of free small business tax workshops for The IRS, The SBA, Canada Community College’s Small Business Development Center and other community centers. His favorite part of his job is meeting people.
