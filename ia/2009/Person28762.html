Bill Stankiewicz.’s Summary

At the current time I am working as Vice President & General Manager for Shippers Warehouse. I am also the Principal & Managing Director for Atlanta Supply Chain with offices based out of South Georgia. www.shipperswarehousega.com .

Projects have included supporting the FEMA efforts on inventory control, transportation & logistics for: Fort Gillem Army Base Forest Park Georgia, Maxwell Air Force Base-Montgomery Alabama, Camp Beauregard-Pineville,LA., and Fort Worth Texas FEMA Logistics Center at 501 West Felix Street, US Department of Homeland Security. Current projects include LTL/TL, Ground Expedite, Ocean rate negotiations for several US domestic customers. Working with MBE-Minority Based Enterprise companies with distribution and fullfillment projects for Coca-Cola Company, Honda, Toyota, etc.

Bill Stankiewicz worked as the Corporate Transportation Executive for Con-Way AIR in a strategic position. He is responsible for negotiations of all Corporate Airline, Line-Haul and Cartage Service and Pricing Contracts of major Partners, & assisting the Regional Field Service Centers. This also includes working with contract negotiations for sales organization with new customers. Con-Way Air Quality Assurance, C-TPAT Training/Certification & Pricing Departments report to Bill also. He is based in Atlanta, Georgia.

Prior to Con-Way AIR Stankiewicz worked as the Corporate Director of Transportation and Logistics for Eagle Global Logistics. He served in a strategic management position in helping to expand logistics supplier partnerships in the current account base through technology and supply chain management.

In addition I have expertise in: Air, Barge, Dedicated Contract Carriage, Distribution, Expedited, Freight Forwarding, Intermodal, Leasing Rental, Logistics Providers (3PL 4PL), Maritime Seaports, Moving, Storage Home Delivery, Package Express, Postal Services, Rail, Supply Chain Management, Trucking (TL LTL), Third Party Logistics, and Warehousing
