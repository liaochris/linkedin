Arjan van der Meer’s Summary

Founding a startup and bringing it towards one of Netherland's marketleaders in online collaboration software, or enabling a team of travel enthusiasts to create one of Asia's leading e-commerce travel brands.

At the crossroads of Internet, rapid growth and management is where I excel, putting my wide interests and my deep knowledge of IT to work by creating fascinating Internet related products, working with other professionals to realize those products, nurture and grow them towards successful brands, and in the meantime being very lucky to enjoy my hobbies as my profession.

Having lived and worked in my home country (The Netherlands), in Finland and now in Thailand, I like to boost my international experiences. Add to that travel leisures that brought me from skiing at the arctic circle to snorkling at the equator, from cruising the wineries of Nappa Valley to climbing the Chinese wall, from exploring World War I trenches towards the top of the worlds highest skyscrapers.

Throw a technical problem at me, and you'll get a solid technical advice. However, be prepared to receive unrequested recommendations about the operations, organization, accountancy, legal risks and anything else I might think of. By no means do I claim to be a "know it all", but for sure I know enough of enough to give the other experts something to think about, and to work with them to mutual benefit.

And that's what I enjoy most.
