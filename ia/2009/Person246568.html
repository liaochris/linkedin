Wendy Folse’s Summary

In 1997, I worked for the US Army in Europe giving me the opportunity to work with a multinational design team specializing in the creation of large medical exhibits, presentations, lectures, posters, and training material. One of my last projects was the creation and compilation of the new "Commander's Guide to Physical Fitness," a US Army publication.

In 1998, I was granted two consecutive, 3 year internships under the ORISE program, where my primary research focused on bridging the gap between the scientific and artistic staffs working together on the creation of highly technical presentations and exhibits.

While in Europe, I worked for Gen. Kevin C. Kiley , formerly the Surgeon General of the Army. It was my responsibility to plan, coordinate and create training and briefing materials for the professionals of LRMC.

I have over 32 published articles and have written product and book reviews for companies such as PhotoFlex, KeyPorter Books, In Focus Productions of NY, Traveler's Tales Inc., Westcliffe Publishers, Allworth Press and Focal Press.

I served as the briefing support specialist for the Commander of OPS Group with Cubic Applications, Inc. at JRTC. I was responsible for 3 command conference rooms and graphic coordinator for all command level briefings, training and support. One of my last major projects was working with the planners, project managers, Pentagon, and Executive staff members on the Stryker Brigade Combat Team as part of the new Army Transformation.
