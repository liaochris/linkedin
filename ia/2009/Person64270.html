Dirk Loop’s Summary

Dirk Loop identifies, analyzes and facilitates the launch of new business initiatives within the field of Intellectual Property and Innovation (Process) Management. Typical goals include developing business plans, general research, data gathering, market and business analysis, planning, project team management, stakeholder relations and marketing. He works with management to identify, scope and execute new business initiatives through their early launch phases. Loop offers fundamental knowledge, trusted relations and more than eight years of relevant working experience.
