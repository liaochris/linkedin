Greg Linch’s Summary

I’m a storyteller who uses several platforms. With a background in writing and reporting, I’m as comfortable composing a 1,000-word in-depth story for print as I am shooting and editing a three-minute video for the Web. Or a 140-character update on Twitter.

I also have experience with audio, photography, Web design, content management, online community management, newspaper layout and copyediting.

When reporting I don’t think, “How can I write a great story?” but instead, “How can I tell a great story?”

I am currently editor at large for online and multimedia at The Miami Hurricane. I previously served as editor in chief during the 2007-2008 school year.

My goal is a work as a multimedia reporter after I graduate in May 2009, combining my writing, video, blogging and other online reporting skills.

In summer 2009, I will work as an online intern at The Dallas Morning News. My role will include writing stories, reporting for the breaking news desk and shooting video.

I am always looking for freelance opportunities to write or shoot videos.
