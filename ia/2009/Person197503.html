Richard Cole’s Summary

As we continue down a path of economic uncertainty, I am able to leverage the knowledge and management experience gained during the late 70's and early 80's, the last time our country was experiencing anything similar.

My management experience began at a time when the economy had gotten so bad that our nation had misery index. High unemployment,high interest rates with mortgage rates often exceeding 18% was causing the worst economy since the Great Depression..

With hard work and creativity, I became part of a management team that began breaking numerous industry records. Operational models developed and deployed during this time period are still used by the industry around the globe.

I describe myself as a Business and Technology Specialist with a concentration in Business Process Optimization,Virtual Workforce, Unified Communications and Storage and Security.

In addition I maintain extensive experience in Threat and Risk Management Professional specializing in high risk environments (HREs) including physical and information technology security solutions and intelligence gathering for multi-national corporations and governments both foreign and domestic.

As a technology professional I assist senior management in identifying advanced technologies that allow companies to establish competitive advantages, increase operational efficiencies, maximize profitability, geographical barrier removal, improved communications, competitive intelligence and protection of valuable proprietary data.

My network consists of highly talented individuals that include technology specialists, former local and federal law enforcement officers, specials forces, etc capable of providing security programs that address all aspects of business operations. Business protection includes physical plant/facility, mobility (vehicle, aviation, vessel, etc), technology, legal support and knowledge transfer.
