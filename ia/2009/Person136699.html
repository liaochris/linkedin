Laney Chavis’s Summary

An intrapreneur in an entrepreneurs world, I worked for 12 years inside a small law firm, working side-by-side with the attorney to grow and manage the business. As an "intrapreneur" I learned to think like a business owner, understand the big picture for the company, and my role in it. I shared these skills with legal assistants and law firms across the country I trained. Since I have co-created two companies to allow law firms to benefit from the skills I mastered. The Ultimate Smart Solution, LLC provides team training to transform the employee into a dream team. Your Outsource Solution, LLC provides back office trust drafting, funding, trust administration support and bookkeeping.
