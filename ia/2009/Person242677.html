Victor Ella’s Summary

Dr. Ella has had more than 26 years of teaching, research and extension experience in the field of soil and water resources engineering. He also had more than 3 years of administrative experience as Dean of the College of Engineering and Agro-industrial Technology, University of the Philippines Los Banos. He has taught numerous courses at the graduate and undergraduate level including hydrology, irrigation and drainage engineering, tropical water quality management, water quality control engineering, sanitary engineering, erosion and sediment transport, open channel hydraulics, water control structures, fluid mechanics, groundwater hydrology, advanced water resources planning, soil engineering, mathematical methods in engineering among others. He has done research on numerical groundwater modeling, hydrologic and soil erosion modeling, contaminant transport in soils, groundwater quality, drip irrigation among others. He has served as either coordinator or resource person in various short-term training courses on irrigation system planning, irrigation water management, hydrology among others. He continues to serve as faculty member of the College of Engineering and Agro-Industrial Technology, University of the Philippines Los Banos, teaching graduate and undergraduate courses, advising thesis students and advancing knowledge in the various fields of soil and water resources engineering.

Victor Ella’s Specialties:

Simulation Modeling, Surface and Groundwater Hydrology, Irrigation and Drainage Engineering, Water Quality and Wastewater Engineering, Contaminant Transport in Soils, Soil Physics, Spatial Analysis (Geostatistics), Hydraulic Engineering
