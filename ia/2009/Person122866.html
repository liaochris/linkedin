John Taylor’s Summary

I am a 25-year marketing communications veteran who has opened my own firm in Nashville, JTMarCom. Our expertise is melding traditional marketing principles with the ever-changing and exciting world of social media marketing for the benefit of our clients. We're developing a service model that is ideal for small to mid-size companies focusing on local markets and vertical industries. I provide more of the traditional brand marketing experience after working with such clients as Michelin and Volvo. My wife, Christine, specializes in Social Media, particularly Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn.

During my career, I've established a proven track record in delivering bottom-line results. For instance, I played an instrumental role in creating and implementing a grassroots marketing program for Uniroyal Tire dealers involving youth soccer. This program led to increased sales, dealer advocacy for the brand, and increased purchase intent among soccer families.

As PR manager at Bridgestone/Firestone, I conducted some of the most successful PR campaigns for product launches (i.e, Blizzak snow tires) in the industry.

From establishing a pro-active consumer news bureau for a grocery store chain that netted millions of positive consumer impressions to helping a Mexican retailer reach the Hispanic community in L.A, all my work is characterized by sound strategic planning, thorough execution and delivering on key metrics.
