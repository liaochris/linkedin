Objective: To teach and enrich students through the technical and creative aspects of music.

Teaching Experience:

2003-2006
Founded Project Rock. A program developed to teach Rock and Pop music in a band setting. Students write, perform and record original songs.
2003-Present
Artist in residence at the Riverside School, Greenwich CT, performing lunchtime concerts and teaching the music improvisation class ("Jammin' with Vinny) on the annual Arts Field Day.
2003-2005
Musical Munchkins. A parent child singing class sponsere focusing on traditional children’s songs to help develop pitch and rhythm.
