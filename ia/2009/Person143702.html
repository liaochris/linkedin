Loris Stavrinides’s Summary

I’m a very passionate and highly motivated individual who is always looking for that creative spark on any project I’ll be given. I always try to stay ahead of the game by learning new trades and experimenting with various ways to keep my self motivated and inspired, as well as the people around me. Coming from an extensive traditional Art background and 6 years of Art & Design education, I now have over 4 years of professional experience in the digital industry and have worked for a wide range of European clients, many being high profile.

I always try to deliver unique and creative solutions for any brief and the same time trying to push any idea and design style even further. The last 2 years I have also developed my leadership & management skills at high standards in order to be able to successfully handle the direction of a production team on big projects.

The type of work that excites me working with is mainly projects for the Entertainment & Game industry, because of their creative potentials and creative assets. On my personal time I still keep my self occupied with creative work, that being either fantasy Art & Illustration or personal art projects.
