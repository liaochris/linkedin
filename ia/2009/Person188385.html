PJ Stafford’s Summary

Rosamaria and I founded Green Irene LLC in the Fall of 2007 to help homeowners make green lifestyle changes “without excuses” and to enable "ecopreneurs" to have a personally satisfying and financially rewarding, part time, consulting practce as a local Green Irene Eco-Consultant. We added the Green Office Makeover service for small business in January 2009.

Green Irene is on a mission to “Green Our World, One Home at a Time.” It's a very satisfying mission and perhaps the one I can connect with the most over all the companies I have started or worked for. Green Irene trains local Eco-Consultants to assist in IMPLEMENTING proven green solutions. With the vast amount of information available in books, magazines, web sites and blogs, many families (and small businesses) want to GO GREEN but don’t want to make a hobby out of going green. That’s where local “Green Irene” Eco-Consultants come in. Local Green Irenes work with you in person to identify and IMPLEMENT changes that can be made now for a healthier, green lifestyle for your family or small office.

For more information, visit us at www.greenirene.com.
