Edward Arroyo’s Summary

Edward Arroyo has worked in the motion picture & TV Industry for over 25 yrs, including Universal, Warner Bros., Disney, Sony.

He's developed a unique TV & web cross-media computer animation project called, “Out Of Our Minds” – a show about the imagination and the quest for the next-gen digital storytellers.

Positioned as TV's first Premiere CG Animation Variety Show, OOOM will have a fresh design style set in a CG world and offer a ground-breaking marketing and promotion platform for CG animators.

Edward founded MakeBelieveStudios initially to R&D various CG projects,including OOOM. He serves as producer/designer. MBS is also developing “iPOP,” an interactive point-of-purchase character-based order-exchange system.

An engineer-turn-philosophy graduate from U.C.L.A., Edward began his career in set decoration at Universal Studios. In 1982 he started Applied Entertainment Technologies, at George Lucas' Egg Company, to pursue developments in TV, film, computer graphics, and new media. AET developed WIS (Wideband Imaging System), a wide-aspect video system alternative to analog HD, for electronic cinema/home entertainment. He consulted with the IMAX company to design AnOmniTheater, an innovative cinema design concept for film and electronic event exhibition.

In 1984, Edward was presented an Emmy Award of Merit from the Academy of Arts & Sciences in Art/Set Decoration for work done on “The Duck Factory,” an ABC series sitcom, set in an animation studio, that launched Jim Carrey's foray into TV.

He's worked with independent companies, with Jon Peters and Peter Guber, and was associate producer on “Computer Television,” a series pilot for Discovery Computer Network (1984).

Ed Arroyo has been an ACM/SIGGRAPH member since 1981. He helped produce the Electronic Theater at Art Center College of Design; the 1983 ET at CalTech Pasadena; ACM/SIGGRAPH'S 1995 ET committee, and worked on SIGGRAPH's HD production “The Story of Computer Graphics.”

Edward Arroyo’s Specialties:

Edward is a entertainment media expert and cross-media strategist, including broadcast television, film, internet, & mobile technologies.

He has over 20 years experience in Set Decoration/Art Direction in TV/Motion Pictures.

He is well-versed with CG applications such as 3D Studio Max, After Effects, Photoshop.
