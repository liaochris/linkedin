Joshua Pang’s Summary

Mr. Pang, Managing Director of StarElite Capital Group, started his career with an European Bank US-Treasuries team. Held CEO/MD positions in multinational media/informediary companies in Asia. Ex-CEO of a holding company under the Singapore Trade Board. A Venture Capitalist and Catalyst with an European bank covering Greater China and North Asia. Shareholders & Board of Director of various companies.

StarElite Capital Group is a privately held investment and advisory corporation. We specialize in financing via L/C, Performance Bond (PB), Bank Guarantee (BG) & Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC) without the need for business owner to dilute both equity and management control.

At StarElite we put high priority in the value of customized financial solutions. StarElite has an unique approach to both short & long-term liquidity. We partner with 30 banks worldwide.
