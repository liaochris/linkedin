Brian Salazar’s Summary

Following a successful position as Design Project Manager with Liberty Mutual's Corporate Real Estate department, Brian has focused his efforts on his passion for real estate and the environment. With fellow Babson MBA classmate, Michael Manzo, Brian has recently established Entegra Development & Investment, LLC. Entegra's mission is to successfully complete the first LEED certified residential subdivision in New England, and expand its development and investment portfolio by 100% from 2007 to 2008.
