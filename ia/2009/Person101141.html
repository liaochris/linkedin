Jack Sallay’s Summary

Vook is an enhanced digital book publisher creating a new innovation in reading that blends a well-written book, high-quality video and the power of the Internet into a single, complete story. There’s nothing else like it available on the market.

At Brightcove, the leading online video platform, as a member of the Product Marketing group, I made the overall business strategy and marketing recommendations for products - including market targeting and segmentation, pricing, positioning, broad market requirements, and go to market strategy.

At Simon & Schuster I helped devise and implement publishing campaigns for many high profile projects including New York Times bestsellers by Hillary Clinton, Bob Woodward, David McCullough, and Mary Higgins Clark. As a junior editor I negotiated contracts, acquired, edited and managed new hardcover titles. Given responsibility for supervising Simon & Schuster Paperbacks, I conducted market research and spearheaded the reprint and reissue of many revised backlist paperback books.
