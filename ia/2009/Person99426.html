Venkat Adhikari’s Summary

Venkat Adhikari is an experienced IP Consultant and IP Strategist associating with IP activities including USPTO, EPO, WIPO and IPO or the like. He specializes in performing prior art searches, assessing the merits of latest inventions, providing support and advice on patent matters for product development groups, preparing responses to office actions, reviewing patents to determine if appropriate coverage has been obtained, reviewing third-party patents to determine scope and validity, work closely with senior patent attorneys and software developers to help build patent portfolio and assist in providing advice regarding a variety of patent related matters including transactions and disputes. In addition, he further specializes in stimulating the innovation process, and the development and strategic management of patent portfolios in technical areas that include Software, Hardware, eCommerce, electrical, electronics and communications. Moreover, he has experience with all phases of portfolio building and management, including identifying the strategic business goals that support development of a patent program, determining the appropriate level of resources to support the program, strategically focused patent prosecution and portfolio development, portfolio evaluation for acquisition, sale, or licensing, and patent litigation.Additionally, his technical background includes undergraduate and graduate coursework in software, hardware and engineering experience in software development. In similarity, his legal experience includes law firm and in-house corporate positions. In relevancy, his experience with patent prosecution, patent application drafting, patent mining, patent litigation support, patent portfolio evaluation and patent licensing.

Venkat Adhikari’s Specialties:

Venkat Adhikari has rich experiences in intellectual property research, litigation and licensing activities, M&A support, SWOT analysis,competitors market or focus, responses to office actions, patent mining, patent searches, IP analysis, patent drafting, landscape analysis, IP trainings, IP awareness, innovation management, and idea management.
