David Dowies’s Summary

Real Estate Broker focusing on residential real estate services in Northern Virginia and Washington D.C.

REO Default Certified Professional (RDCPro) and Accredited Buyer Representative (ABR) specializing in foreclosure homes, REO portfolio management and disposition, real estate investment, acquisitions, marketing and new construction home sales.

Extensive experience, coupled with a technology background allows us to provide streamlined systems with high-impact marketing strategies for our Clients. We are a single source solution for complete default services, providing seamless transitions for all phases of the REO process.

David Dowies’s Specialties:

Residential real estate marketing and acquisition services; Corporate REO asset management, preservation and disposition; Nationwide relocation services; property management; new construction; distressed property sales; contract negotiation; business development; short sale and foreclosure consulting; real estate investment strategy; Internet marketing, branding and SEO
