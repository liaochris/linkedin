Sam Day’s Summary

I accept all invitations. Lets get Linked!

Wedding Paintings:
I paint an oil painting, LIVE, at the reception. This unique, high end service is perfect not only for weddings, but also for any significant event worthy of long remembrance. The guests enjoy the performance as well. The result is impressionistic, with rapid brushstrokes, but with sufficient detail to render the bride and groom recognizably, and to capture the look and feel of the occasion.

Illustration:
I began illustrating my father's articles for newspapers at the age of eleven. Now published on four continents, my clients are Fortune 500 companies, as well as major magazines and publishers, and governmental institutions.

Storyboards and conceptual drawings:
Your idea, my rapid and cogent depiction.

Sam Day’s Specialties:

Storyboards, humorous & whimsical illustration, cartooning, conceptual and visual support for ideation sessions, realistic oil painting and illustration, impressionistic wedding paintings
