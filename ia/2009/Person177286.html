Juan Nuñez’s Summary

Juan Nuñez has a unique quality in that he knows what he wants and what he wants to become. Even before his graduation, Juan has shown his certainty in working as an Account Executive and has strategically built his experience around this profession. Juan first began working as an Assistant Account Executive for a multicultural advertising agency; after working at this agency for over a year, he then left to pursue a summer internship at Ogilvy & Mather. During Juan's internship at Ogilvy, he was able to meet and work with top talent in Account Management. After the summer internship at Ogilvy, Juan was appointed as the team leader of an Integrated Marketing Communications Campaign where he handled responsibilities that coincided with duties of Account Executives.

After graduation, Juan plans to continue building his expertise by getting Marketing/Advertising certificates at NYU's SCPS Program and working for an innovative agency.

Juan's ultimate goal is to start an online business that relates to advertising and contributes to the industry's success.
