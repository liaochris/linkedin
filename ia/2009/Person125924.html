Kanya Yoshihiro’s Summary

I have a background in media production, marketing, over-the-top-research, acting, employee motivation and customer satisfaction; all allowing for a unique perspective on the marketing and customer networking that is needed for the entertainment and advertising industry.

With an extensive background in working in front of and behind the camera for nearly 10 years in Promotion, Marketing and Film, I have experience in print and film marketing from conceptual design to creative completion. Print media design goes hand-in-hand with the advertising, filming and marketing evolution and I have a great understanding of it, as a both the buyer and consumer.
