Steve Yamaguma’s Summary

Developed marketing and promotional campaigns for a wide variety of corporations including semiconductor and board level technology companies, networking, software, as well as government, education and service businesses.

Was part of the original Intel Inside Team (1990), US Japan trade mission, American Airlines Inaugural flight, consultant to JETRO, Splice International.
