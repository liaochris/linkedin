Kid Richmond’s Summary

Professional Stunt Coordinator/ Performer for the film, television and commercial markets with a 100 percent safety record. Well experienced and trained in designing and delivering safe and entertaining stunts whether the budget is ultra-low or full scale.
Formerly sponsored by Safety Strategies Inc. Aided in researching and field testing fire safety products used in the racing, military and steel industry.
Performed full body fire burns lasting longer than 2.5 minutes. Coordinated Car crashes, near misses, fight choreography, squib hits and falls. Advanced knowledge in special FX use and design.
Kid Richmond is also founder of Devil Skin Stunt Team. Devil Skin Stunt Team is a group of well credited and qualified stunt performers, stunt coordinators and stunt riggers. They have an excellent source of readily available resources ranging from stunt pads to cars to flip.
