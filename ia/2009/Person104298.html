Jan van der Walt’s Summary

My interest lies in the disciplines and industries of corporate renewal, turnaround management, business rescue, business transformation and management consulting.

I regularly publish, lecture and present talks and papers on these topics, as well as webmastering websites, blogs and internet discussion groups.

Professionally, I am a turnaround practitioner, and heads Corporate Renewal Solutions as its CEO.

Presently I am serving as the Chief Executive Officer of the Turnaround Management Association - Southern Africa, as CEO of its Turnaround Governance Board, as a member of its Academic Subcommittee and Chair of its Standards Subcommiteee. I am also a member of the Institute of Directors.

Lastly, I am a member of the Business Rescue Subcommittee of the King III Commission on Corporate Governance.

Jan van der Walt’s Specialties:

Turnaround management, business transformation, business rescue, general management consulting, strategy consulting.
