Rebecca Kraus’s Summary

CREATIVE CONSULTANT
I've recently provided creative solutions to an energy firm, invention studio, online games developers, book publisher, educational center, printing company and mobile content provider.

ONLINE CREATIVE MANAGER
As one of the leads of the Creative Services group at Classmates.com, I oversaw the team's efforts to enhance the site by tapping into the nostalgic and fun-loving spirits of over 50 million curious members.

GAMES COPYWRITER
As the Copy Curator at Cranium, I led the editorial re-branding of the product packaging. Prior to that, I was the senior writer for Mattel Games Design in L.A. I helped create and develop brands from research and brainstorming to play testing and final execution of the content, packaging and rules. I worked on developmental games for young children, licensed products for kids and social interactive games for adults.

MAGAZINE EDITOR
Before being a gamer, I was the Managing Editor for “Entertainment@Home” magazine, a national publication that explored the convergence of media in the digital era. I was one of the principals that helped launch this magazine. In addition to being a regular contributor to its film section, I was also the Music Editor for the weekly “Entertainment Today” magazine.

FREELANCE WRITER
I've contributed stories of pop culture to publications including “Premiere,” “Rolling Stone,” and “Request.” I’ve also written about travel--from the tale of a Bollywood film star in “Soma” magazine--to my experience at a Croatian peace camp in the book, “Europe from a Backpack.”

CHILDREN'S ADVOCATE & TEACHER
I served on the Board of Directors for the non-profit, the Global Children’s Organization. In addition to fundraising and re-designing the website, I volunteered as a camp counselor globally. As a teacher and guest lecturer, I espoused the virtues of volunteering, travel writing, word play and games.

ENJOY! www.rebeccakraus.com

Rebecca Kraus’s Specialties:

Web design, quality copy, game development, marketing strategy, product packaging and entertainment journalism. For over 15 years, I’ve employed my sharp intuition for branding to devise successful design and editorial solutions across several industries.
