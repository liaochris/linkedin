Dale Gibbons’s Summary

It's rarely a straight path on the journey to our most fulfilling work.

Most of my career has been in marketing and sales with Fortune 500 companies. 16 years and 4 countries later, I joined a small startup in a marketing role in 2003.

I acquired the struggling business from the owner and, after reinventing, repositioning and re-staffing the company, quadrupled sales and it is now nicely profitable.

Having promoted a very talented and trustworthy no. 2 to president, I'm developing my second business, The Continuous Improvement Center. Find more and join in the conversation at www.LeanBusinessNews.com
