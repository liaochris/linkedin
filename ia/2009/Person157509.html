Martin Mayne’s Summary

You are in litigation and have relevant information in email and other electronic media. I help you get that relevant infomation ready for trial.

With a broad knowledge base of discovery issues as they relate to the identification, preservation, collection and processing of relevant electronically stored information, together we will get you through the meet and confer process, IT interviews, 30(b)(6) depositions and the rest of the discovery process including review and document productions.

You need to use the discovery process to gain or maintain advantage in litigation, and I can help you do that.

Significant experience creating and maintaining effective document review databases. Recognized leader in document review techniques. Excellent manager who can attract and retain qualified document review personnel.
