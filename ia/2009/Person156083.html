Matthew Rushton’s Summary

Matthew is the GM/Sales Director for Fujitsu’s Northern Territory region and has over 16 years delivery & commercial experience within the ICT sector. Matthew has extensive experience working across Australian and Global markets for both pre-IPO technology start-ups as well as established international market leaders.

Matthew has extensive experience in marketing, sales, pre-sales and service delivery. Prior to joining Fujitsu, Matthew was a sales & marketing director, launching 4 technology companies across international markets.

Matthew’s previous role at Fujitsu involved running Sales & Service Delivery for the Managed Services team, and has enjoyed unprecedented success in winning several new high-profile clients whilst increasing customer service across the customer base.

Matthew is a member of the Fujitsu Managed Services management team. He is committed to driving constant improvement and customer satisfaction across the IT industry and his customer base. Matthew has a passion for building businesses and is a keen cricketer and ultralight pilot (flying an Aerochute which is a form of powered parachutes). In February, Matthew relocated his family from Melbourne to Darwin.

Matthew Rushton’s Specialties:

Extensive international experience in UK, European, Emerging Territories (Eastern Europe and The Middle East), USA and ANZ markets.

Launching and accelerating technology (software, hardware and services) companies.
