Robert Guerra’s Summary

I am of the founding directors of Privaterra - an ongoing project of the SAGE Charitable foundation that works with Human Rights NGOs to assist them with issues of data privacy, secure communications and information security.

As well, I set on the board of Computer Professionals for Social Responsibility (CPSR), and have been actively involved in the preparatory process of the UN World Summit on the Information Society, including as a panelist at the Pan European and Latin American Regional Meeting, an NGO advisor to the Canadian delegation, and the focal point for CPSR. I also sit on the advisory board of several non-profits, including Taking IT Global, DiploFoundation's Internet Governance and Policy Capacity Building Programme, and The Internet Corporations for Assigned Names and Numbers's At-Large Advisory Committee (ALAC).
