Zane Appel’s Summary

More than 15 years of experience with Databases. First as a Database Programmer / Analyst and for the last five and a half years as a Database Engineer. Successful history of supporting mission critical enterprise class databases that require high availability and performance. Known for strong analytical and problem-solving skills, and a professional attitude. Reputation as a master of problem management concepts and root cause analysis. Zane Appel’s Experience

  • Database Engineer

    SanDisk

    (Public Company; SNDK; Semiconductors industry)

    March 2004 — September 2009 (5 years 7 months)

    • Worked as part of Global Infrastructure Services team in a large enterprise with approximately 85 databases totaling more than 5 TB spread across multiple vendors and operating systems.
    • Designed and implemented snapshot backups for Production databases on NetApp NAS storage which reduced backup time from 6 hours to 10 minutes.
    • Designed and implemented automated database cloning for QA and Development environment refreshes which reduced refresh time from 12 hours to 20 minutes.
    • Acted as performance liaison for all of infrastructure services which allowed us to solve performance problems that couldn’t be solved previously.
    • Developed corporate standards for database implementation based on best practices which simplified database implementation and maintenance.
    • Helped to develop and followed testing and change control processes for modifications to Production databases which reduced errors and assisted in root cause analysis.
    • Implemented Oracle Enterprise Manager (OEM) which gave us a comprehensive view of our databases and supporting infrastructure.
    • Implemented a program of regular database restores to validate backup processes which found a number of problems with our existing backups.
    • Engineered solutions to meet company requirements for availability, performance, and cost.
    • Mastered ITSM concepts including Incident Management, Problem Management and Root Cause Analysis.
    • Transitioned database operations to offshore team and managed that relationship to create a team environment which improved database availability and customer service.
