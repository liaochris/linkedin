Vaughn Hovey’s Summary

I've been fortunate to have had a number of worthwhile corporate positions including Eastman Kodak Company , IBM , Bank One and Nationwide Insurance after I graduated from the Ohio State University with a degree in Production Management I also attended the Duke Advanced Management Program and participated in the IBM PE, Experienced PE programs and the IBM Executive Insights program.

I was a CPM-certified member of the NAPM and was also certified as a production and inventory control manager from APICS. I'm member of the National Institute of Government Purchasing (NIGP). I was a former member of the Rochester BBB and Business Advisory Council for Data Processing for Disabled Persons. I was the first co-chairman of the ISSC Customer Council and was Vice President of the IITUG and a representative to the SIG and the Outsourcing Institute. I was a finalist in the 1999 “World Outsourcing Achievement Award” and am a frequent speaker at National and International Conferences addressing effective management of IT alliances, managed services, procurement and strategic sourcing
