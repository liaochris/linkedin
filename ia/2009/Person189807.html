Eric V. Allen’s Summary

PRECISION FINANCE & REALTY PARTNERS is a full service commercial real estate consulting and brokerage firm.
We offer various forms of Commercial Funding Solutions, Commercial RE Sales, Marketing and Buyer Representation, Real Estate Development and Project Consulting, Real Estate and Business Recapitalization Solutions.

The firm is made up of several partners from different fields in the Real Estate Industry, Capital Markets, Commercial Mortgage and Business Funding, Construction and Sales and boasts a combined professional expertise of 50 years.

Precision Finance and Realty Partners serves the entire commercial real estate market, with a strong focus on the Hospitality Industry, Long Term Care Industry, Mixed Use Developments, Gas/Service Station market, and Income producing property.

Our current lender pipeline has over 150 direct lending sources, with 50 we consider the top rated in both progams, experience and customer service, ranging from retail and wholesale lending institutions to various types of managed funds and private equity firms.

Our current real estate pipeline ranges in size and scope of off-market property types.

Eric V. Allen’s Specialties:

Commercial Funding: Traditional Institutional & Wholesale Mortgage, Small Business, Mezz, Debt/Equity, Participation & Private Equity Capital.
Commercial Real Estate: All types of properties qualified as commercial use & projects qualified as commercial in nature.
Other services: Due Dilligence, Contract Negotiatons, Project Development, Business & Financial Plan Development and Recapitalization/Investment Studies.
