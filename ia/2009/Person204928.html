Robert Harris’s Summary

Entrepreneurial, user-focused, managing engineer with sixteen years of leadership in the planning, development and incorporation of usability into B2C and B2B software applications.

Highly experienced with client-server, desktop and thin-client user interface design, information management and web applications. Seasoned leader in the areas of user advocacy, user research methods/methodologies, product/project management, business development, vision and strategy, quality process, team building and personnel management.

I am a usability evangelist who not only possesses extensive experience with numerous software development lifecycle (SDLC) processes (including Agile, SCRUM, RUP, Spiral and Waterfall) but also incorporating UX into these processes as a core function through:
o Establishing Identity
o Developing the Infrastructure
o Defining the Process

Highly experienced with off-shoring, outsourcing, working with and managing remote personnel and geographically diverse teams as well as operating within highly process oriented development houses. I've excelled in organizations possessing Capability Maturity Model (CMM) Level 5 and International Organization for Standardization (ISO) 9001:2000 Quality Management System (QMS) certifications for both Systems and Software Engineering as well as less mature models without issue.
