Greg Pence’s Summary

I am a leader of finance, strategy and analytics having delivered an average annual increase of $25,000,000 in gross margin by:
- Driving up financial performance and operational efficiency
- Leveraging technical resources to produce better technology with less
- Launching higher margin products using more timely and useful analytics

My goal is to steer strategic success by:
- Bridging execution gaps between business and IT
- Growing analytic competence
- Optimizing financial performance

I bring valuable leadership skills by by initiating and sustaining organizational change around the use of financial, analytical and technical resources. I deploy these resource to evaluate better how human behavior changes with economic and financial events, and therefore, how customers drive business strategy.

In support of my goals and core competence, I have developed Lean Analytics, August 2008, which is a methodology to meet critical strategy needs cost effectively and rapidly.

I have been a senior leader for over 15 years. I have led individual contributors, department managers and executives, through a communication approach based on mutual respect, meaning and purpose. I effectively drive consensus and commitment to action through ongoing dialogue and feedback. And I build strong relationships with all stakeholders to resolve conflict, set priorities, guide planning, establish performance criteria and leverage the right resources.

I have an advanced education and over 20 years in financial engineering and actuarial science. The coursework and experience requirements leading to my professional credentials are equivalent to a masters in finance or economics.
