Kim Kobza’s Summary

I have been CEO of Neighborhood America since 1999. I have had a varied career grounded in many experiences including business, law, technology, and leadership.

At Neighborhood America we have developed a simple solution to a universal problem shared by all organizations - how to provide engagement solutions that create value for the enterprise. We have become well known for our work across virtually all industries including business, media and government.

My goal is to establish Neighborhood America as a leader for responsible change in business and government as organizations learn how to leverage the collective intelligence and relationship networks with their customers, partners, and employees.
