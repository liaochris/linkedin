Leah Donti IFRS trainer’s Summary

I operate my own private seminar practice since 1991 offering seminars to prepare candidates for national accounting and finance examinations (CPA, CMA/CFM, CGA, and CFA).

In addition, I conduct in-house seminars for companies and associations across North America. Clients include Microsoft, GE, and many CPA firms. Topics include IFRS, compilations and reviews, auditing, stock options, business combinations, variable interests, revenue recognition, fair value accounting, business valuation, FASB update and MBA in a day.

I also do public speaking for Surgent and Western CPE.

Prior to beginning my seminar business in 1991, I worked for DMR, a major information management consulting organization, where I provided training to staff in offices across North America. I also worked with an accounting software firm, offering accounting education courses to clients on site. In addition, I held positions in internal audit and cost accounting with Canadian Pacific, the largest company in Canada and traveled all over the world.
