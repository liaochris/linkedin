Michael Aubrecht’s Summary

My only job is to help individuals and companies identify the right opportunities (jobs/careers/candidates/contracts) throughout the renewable energy, battery, fuel cell, power, portable device, charger and related industries. I have over 18 years experience finding that "perfect" employee for my clients. I also pride myself in the ability to match candidates and clients into win/win situations for the long term. If you're looking for that life changing job, or if your looking to hire the one individual who "really makes a difference" I will be there to help you all the way through the process.
