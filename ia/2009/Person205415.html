Russell Bielby’s Summary

Graduating with honours in Computer Science at Leicester in the mid 90s, Russell began his career with CBL Technology Ltd. as a training designer and content developer using tools such as Authorware.

Following success as a multimedia development manager, Russell was appointed to establish a Virtual Reality capability which led to lead roles on innovative projects with clients involving Rolls Royce, Hewlett Packard, IVECO, Powergen, CALGroup, Hasbro Interactive, Thorntons, LEGO and more. During his time as VR Manager at VEGA Group Plc, he led a team that became recognised as one of the UK's leading desktop VR innovators, successfully producing the first real-time 3D interior design software for the UK consumer market. Having later worked as a key member of the senior management team of VEGA’s Commerce Division, Russell left in 2000 to focus on establishing REALSENSE as one of the UK's leading suppliers of next generation software.

Russell was the pioneer behind the hugely successful and innovative Hornby Virtual Railway product - distributing over 120,000 units world-wide. This has led to more recent developments including multi-lingual instructional software for Hornby's award winning LIVE STEAM range.

Russell has worked with clients in North America and across Europe and in the late 90's was invited by industry 3D specialists Superscape Plc to deliver virtual reality training, development and consultancy to NASA for their International Space Station Programme.

Over recent years, working with international clients has given Russell a first hand understanding of global business issues and practical experience in the provision of learning, marketing and e-business solutions in numerous languages. Russell's background provides a sound foundation for the successful provision of high quality tailored software and media solutions across business areas and sectors including Education, Financial Services, Government, HR and Health & Safety and Retail.
