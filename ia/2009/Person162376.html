Michael Bay’s Summary

I excel at producing video productions, bringing together a dynamic skill list that combines planning, production, and editing with the latest in graphics, music and sound production technology.

For the last two years, my professional focus has been on science and technology programming, and I bring to that a broad interest and understanding of a range of fields.

In addition, I've worked in my spare time to develop writing, directing and producing skills in narrative video.

As a producer, I am highly skilled at anticipating and quickly overcoming challenges as they arise. I have exceptional communication skills, helping the people I work with achieve their best.

My goal is to work in a dynamic creative environment where I can best use my skills to create compelling, modern video programming for a variety of platforms.

Michael Bay’s Specialties:

Creative problem solving, writing, video production, video editing, 2d graphics, 3d graphics, promotion, marketing.

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; TWX; Broadcast Media industry)

    January 2006 — February 2008 (2 years 2 months)

    I wrote and produced CNN International's Future Summit, an hour long program that examined some of the challenges facing our world in the coming century, and how science and technology may help overcome those challenges. The show featured some of the most creative minds from around the globe, both on our panel discussion and in interviews.
