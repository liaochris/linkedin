Yannis Triantafyllou’s Summary

With more than 20 years of working experience in various fields, in parallel to several related studies, I am active in the industrial investment sector of the graphic industry since 1994, having occupied ever since several managerial positions in marketing and sales.

Being a multilingual person (I speak Greek, English & French fluently, Italian at a good level and have basic knowledge of Turkish & Romanian), since 2003 I have established and have been managing the network of international subsidiaries & partners Linomedia & Punch Graphix Hellas were maintaining in Turkey and the Balkan countries.

Also, over the years, I have participated in specialized consulting teams (inhouse or independent) that prepared and implemented more than 50 investment-aid feasibility studies, including prepress, offset printing and digital printing equipment, for some of the most prominent graphic industry companies in Greece.

Apart from these, my sales records include several milestones: the first digital label press in Greece (2001), the first integrated black&white and color production digital system in South East Mediterranean region (2001), the first digital press in northern Greece, in Cyprus and the first CTP installation in FYROM etc.

In recent years my activities focus around digital printing and other related trendsetting technologies for Print-On-Demand, Industrial Printing, Large Format Printing and Publishing, where i also have acted as a consultant to customers of the companies i worked for, introducing and educating them especially in color digital printing processes, being an excellent speaker with deep knowledge of the graphic industry.

Being dedicated, with analytical mind and with the proven ability to organize, train and lead an international team with diversified functions and roles and develop the business by establishing and achieving targets, i am always focused on problem solving and results.

Yannis Triantafyllou’s Specialties:

Organisational Skills on corporate, team and project level, Marketing & Advertising Skills in fields of Concept creation/description and copywritting, excellent speaker, deep knowledge of the graphic industry, proven professional training/coaching skills, negotiation skills on industrial sales of equipment & services.
