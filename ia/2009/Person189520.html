Pranav Tyagi’s Summary

Pranav Tyagi is a Co-Founder of Tango Management Consulting, and serves as its Executive Vice President and COO. Mr. Tyagi has over 11 years of consulting experience with a wide range of clients across Retail, Restaurants, REITs, Investment Funds and Banking. Prior to TMC, Mr. Tyagi started and co-led the Retail Real Estate practice at BearingPoint, and helped develop Retail and Corporate Real Estate Consulting practices at Ernst & Young and Deloitte Consulting. Mr. Tyagi has led several industry benchmarking studies for Corporate and Retail Real Estate, and has worked with over 60 retail and restaurant companies in improving their store development organizations, processes and tools. He has also worked with over 25 Corporate and Commercial Real Estate Clients in the area of Real Estate Strategy, Process Improvement and Technology. Mr. Tyagi holds an MBA in Strategy and Marketing Research from The University of Texas at Dallas, a Master of Science in Information Systems, and a Bachelors degree in Mechanical / Industrial Engineering.
