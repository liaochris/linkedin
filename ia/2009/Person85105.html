Geoff Tyson’s Summary

Director, Corporate Strategy, Business Development and Technology for DV8 Mobile S.R.O / Bubble Mobile

Geoff Tyson founded DV8 Mobile in 2006 in response to the turmoil of today’s recording industry business. Focused around proprietary technology invented by Geoff, he leads the tightly focused programming and developing group as they develop new and emerging technologies for mobile content distribution.

Tyson formerly owned and operated MOPLH Media where he was the Director and head of the Global Media and Entertainment division. In that position his responsibilities included global oversight of all media-related projects and development of business strategies for new technology ventures such as portable music devices and online music services. He managed all aspects of new media business, including content, sales, PR, marketing, business and technology development, and distribution. He is a key player in the sale of millions of CDs, DVDs, digital downloads, and clothing accessory items worldwide netting tens of millions of dollars in revenue. His accomplishments include movie soundtracks for Disney and Touchstone Pictures, TV and radio commercials for Altell Wireless and I Zod, popular music CDs and DVDs, MTV music videos, and clothing and fashion accessories.

Prior to MOPLH Media, Tyson worked extensively as a record producer and performer. Credits, projects, productions and tours include some of the biggest names in popular music such as Duran Duran, Joe Satriani, The Go Go’s, Ugly Kidd Joe, Queens Of The Stone Age, A Perfect Circle, and many others. He has worked extensively in collaboration with some of the world’s top selling record producers such as David Kahne, Melman, Mike Shipley, Jason Corsaro and Eric Valentine.

Tyson received his E.M.B.A. from the Anderson School Of Business at UCLA in 2006




*Featured extensively in music and lifestyle magazines worldwide.
