Kurt Haller’s Summary

Strong technical background, semiconductor fab and product development experience. Proven leader of cross-functional engineering, marketing, and operations teams, exerting influence to get results without direct reports within a matrix management structure. Establishes relationships with customers in all geographic regions through travel, direct support, joint development projects, beta and head-to-head competitive evaluations. Excellent writer and speaker, comfortable in front of all levels of my own and a customer's organization.

MS-Office; VBA programming; Some experience with Matlab, some Mathematica, enterprise databases and statistical analyses with SAS. Hands-on operation of commercial semiconductor inspection, metrology, and process equipment. Experience designing and building highly-instrumented, computer-interfaced research apparatus. Adaptable and willing to learn: statistics, yield modeling, statistical process control, queueing theory acquired by self-study and continuing education.
