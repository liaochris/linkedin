Martijn Snels’s Summary

Martijn is an expert on printed, new and social media. Within a short time Martijn learned himself a wide area of knowledge to create both strategic and creative concepts on marketing and communications. He learned this skills by setting up clear goals which guided Martijn to both small and international companies. There he expanded his point of view on the market and created his own thoughts on marketing and communication.

After and while Martijn was learning those skills he founded two companies. The first company, mac (formally known as mac·creations) is specialized in printed, new and social media development on all segments. From projects management to development and production all the aspects of creating this piece of media is token by mac to offer clients a full spectrum of services.
After four years within mac, Martijn founded, together with his father Vincent H.C.H. Snels, the company recens. This company is based on the concept, also created by both Martijn and Vincent. Via this fundament for there company, Martijn and Vincent are achieving the global society to go beyond the current way of life and take the next step to an open, sustainable and healthy society.

After telling you this mac is normally asking you "Who are you?". Martijn loves to have a chat with you about you and your work or company. Please invite yourself to introduce yourself and make a appointment with Martijn.

More information about Martijn's thoughts, experience and blogposts are available on Martijn's personal weblog at www.martijnsnels.nl.

Martijn Snels’s Specialties:

Martijn specialized himself in printed, new and social media and from this he is managing marketing and communication processes for both clients (mac) as for one of his companies (recens). By using his technical, graphic and marketing skills Martijn is creating groundbreaking concepts.

Based on these three fundaments Martijn is coach and strategic thinker on Personal Branding Martijn is also a passionated public speaker on Personal Branding and Social Media.
