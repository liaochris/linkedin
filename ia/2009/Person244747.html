Vladimir Nagin’s Summary

I have been working in telecom industry since 2000:started my career as software engineer and worked in several companies on different positions since then.

Currently I am responsible as CEO for Cellnetrix, a young R&D company focused on the development of software which makes wireless mobility and Internet services more reliable and secure.

The company implements software on different embedded platforms, first of all, for universal subscriber identity modules (USIM) for various mobile networks such as GSM, UMTS or CDMA, as well as WLAN and Internet, provides secure value-added mobile applications and full-scale Over-the-Air post-issuance management services for (U)SIM and R-UIM card based on a proprietary OTA platform.

Embedded software solutions delivered by the company are based on the de facto industry standards maintained by Open Mobile Alliance, 3GPP, ETSI and Global Platform and use widely adopted Sun's Java programming language

Development environments:Microsoft Visual Studio, Eclipse, Delphi, Jbuilder, Matlab.

Programming languages:C/C++, Java, Jscript, assembler.

Operating systems: Windows NT/2000/XP/2003 server as developer, Linux/FreeBSD as user.

Software:MS Office, MS Project/Project Server, CASE-tools, source control (CVS, SVN, VSS), bug tracking (JIRA, bugzilla).

Technologies:embedded programming for smart cards (native, java card), SIM-toolkit app. development, handset app. development (Symbian), SIM browsing, OTA (SIM and device remote management), networks (GPRS/GSM/UMTS, TCP/IP, win sockets, IPX/SPX), .NET (COM, COM+), UML, J2ME, J2SE, J2EE, Crypto (DES, PKI), WAP, SQL, WEB, XML,XSLT.

Standards:GSM and 3GPP standards esp. transports SMS-PP, SMS-CB, GPRS, USSD, MMS, SMPP, base knowledge of SS7 signaling and GSM/UMTS networks, good knowledge of smartcard standards (ISO, ETSI, VOP, JavaCard), OMA (DRM , Terminal management).
