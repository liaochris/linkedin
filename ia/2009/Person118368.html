John W. Beck’s Summary

Combining private sector, military, and government service experience, John brings over 40 years of leadership skill, diverse acquisition workforce knowledge, and T&E management experience to contribute toward the success of any assignment.

John graduated from the Defense Acquisition University’s Advanced Program Management course and has earned Army Acquisition Corps level III certifications in Test and Evaluation and Program Management. During his almost 30 years of government service, John led T&E teams comprised of as few as two employees to organizations numbering more than 350 employees. He managed test budgets from several thousand dollars to over 2 billion dollars.

John's management style and philosophy: "You never quit learning. Change is opportunity. Work smarter rather than harder and emain flexible, Form high performance teams. Work to build consensus and secure a win-win end state. Surround yourself with dedicated, high-performance people and trust in them."
