Daniel Cunningham’s Summary

Daniel Cunningham has a rare blend of 15 years of experience in asset management, construction project management and real estate development that gives him tremendous depth of field as a real estate executive and makes him particularly adept in value-added repositioning of multi-family or commercial assets. From design and construction execution to lease-up and operations, Daniel has senior experience in all facets critical to realizing the upside in such value-added opportunities.

In his multi-family asset management role, Daniel consistently demonstrates an ability to improve NOI by focusing on streamlining operations and reducing expenses, as well as strategically deploying marketing resources and drilling down on details that maximize leasing velocity. He has a demonstrated track record of successfully turning portfolios at one time considered too crippled to recover into profitable investments. This expertise proves particularly useful during acquisitions by helping to identify poorly-managed deals with real potential.

Daniel’s experience also extends to the marketing and lease negotiation of commercial assets in his portfolios ranging from office to retail space and is proficient in the oversight and coordination of tenant improvements.

And although Daniel has performed well within large companies such as AIMCO, he is also an accomplished entrepreneur and co-founder of LivingHomes, LLC www.livinghomes.net which also highlights his continuing interest in minimizing our ecological footprint as we produce housing for future generations. He is therefore uniquely adept at managing a team in a large corporate setting, or wearing five different hats in a start-up environment.

Daniel Cunningham’s Specialties:

Multi-family and commercial asset management, financial analysis, modular housing, green & sustainable building practices, construction project management, chili preparation (of negligible value in real estate, to be sure, but a skill is a skill.) Start-ups and entrepreneurship.
