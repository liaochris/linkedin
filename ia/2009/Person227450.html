Suzanne Carawan’s Summary

Suzanne Carawan has over 15 years of experience working with non-profits from both a vendor, volunteer, and member perspective. While her career has been spent in for-profit software and consulting companies, Suzanne has always served the non-profit market through working for companies that either serve the non-profit market directly, or through working on pro bono projects. Her lifelong pursuit is to find ways to use technology to improve the welfare of populations in developing countries. Prior to Higher Logic, Suzanne held a variety of product and channel marketing roles at Parallels, JDSU, Advanced Solutions International and Active Matter. Suzanne serves as member of the Executive Committee for the Social Networking and Media Association and is a member of Women in Technology, the American Marketing Association, ASAE, the National Association of Professional Women, and several alumni organizations. She is also a student of Tae Kwon Do under the renowned Grand Master Eung Gil Choi at U.S.Tae Kwon Do Martial Arts Academy. Suzanne holds a Master of Business Administration in marketing from American University’s Kogod School of Business, a Master of Public Health in social marketing from Tulane University, and a Bachelor of Arts in philosophy from the University of Maryland.
