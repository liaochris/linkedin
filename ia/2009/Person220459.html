Soren Thompson’s Summary

As a champion for Business Continuity and Change Management, I leverage my formal education, training, and ten-plus-years in the Information Technology field to identify potential business impacts and risks associated with projects and changes.

Highly regarded for my "get it done" attitude, self-motivation, and accountability, I am always willing to take ownership of a situation. I believe in learning something every day and view each new challenge as an opportunity.

I am well-versed in creating process documents and training materials, ensuring they are comprehensive yet easily understood by all prospective audiences. My attention to detail and excellent communication skills have allowed me to excel as a business analyst capable of presenting to individuals across all levels of the organization with confidence, poise, and professionalism.
