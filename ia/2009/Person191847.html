Roy Reyer’s Summary

In 1998 I retired from the Maricopa County Sheriff’s Office as a Lieutenant after twenty years of public service. During my law enforcement career I was assigned to traffic enforcement and Special Investigations involved in narcotic enforcement, white collar crime and computer crimes.

During my law enforcement career I founded a public service organization which I am still involved with called Cops Who Care. Cops Who Care is nationally recognized program assisting at risk children and in 1999 I was featured by Family Circle Magazine as their “Top Ten People Who Care and Make a Difference”.

After my retirement in 1998 I began a web development company called Wickenburg Web.

After it became clear to me that the clients that I was making more money on their websites then I was making them, I decided to take inventory and develop my own e-commerce presence. Pulling out a yellow pad I wrote down everything I had a passion for during my law enforcement career; handcuffing bad guys, guns and writing tickets….

I was certified as a traffic radar instructor and felt I could develop a website that would teach people how to avoid traffic tickets and do reviews of speed counter measure devices such as radar detectors and jammers. That night “Radar Roy” and RadarBusters.com was born.

In 2007 one of the fiercest competitors on the Internet, Netshops, acquired my company for over seven figures and hiring me on as a consultant.

After the sale of Radarbusters I had numerous associates and companies contact me who wanted me to either teach or do search engine optimization or Internet marketing for them. As I no longer needed or desired to have a job, but interested in keeping my skills sharp, I began coaching five clients.

Three of these clients also have become "Internet Millionaires" Through their online business and my coaching, so in 2009 I've decided to launch my new business, SEOtrainingSW.com
