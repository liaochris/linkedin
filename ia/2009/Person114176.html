Jose Luis Vallejo’s Summary

Combining my base academic background, my interest for new technologies, and my enthusiasm when you can work closed to smart and up-to-date people; finally I became an entrepreneur in Media Net Software and other ventures. Constantly I’m looking for the professional relationships (with customers, providers, partners, colleagues, or employees) for sharing a stable and healthy long vision for agreements.
What means long-term vision for professional agreements? I suggest we must use to think about 100 -120 years for life expectancy for next decades, according with last medicals reports and for developed economies, and so, we have time to change our companies business-models and relationships tenths o twenty times yet… :-)
Several times you see someone becoming from being a client, after a competitor, then an employee, or a provider, perhaps a partner and again a client. I am only concern to find the right equilibrium on each step and achieve, each time, a more reliable and trusted cooperation.
That’s what I think have made Media Net Software ‘s Culture a favorable environment for attracting talent and new and exciting projects. People know that we are here with long-term goals and it what inspires confidence for new employees and of course for our customers.
One of the things I’m proud is how we can combine a collaborative and compromised environment with people that share new ideas for developing inside the company.
No matter the size of the company we will reach on future, but we must keep our “soul” while we are growing with better people every year. That’s what inspires me and also my main challenge every Monday morning.
Something about my hobbies not included above? Simply be at home and follow, as I can, all my wife and three children desires ;-)
