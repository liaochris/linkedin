Becky Vieira’s Summary

With more than a decade of consumer PR experience, ranging from large agencies to in-house positions, I have started my own company to help educate clients on the practice of PR.

PR You (www.PRYouInc.com) offers a cost-effective solution to traditional public relations. We are a boutique public relations firm specializing in educational PR consulting. No publicist. No firm. Essentially saving your business hundreds of thousands of dollars in the critical start-up phase by effectively educating your team on how to create and maintain a fully operational in-house PR program, no prior experience necessary.

My personal practice areas practice areas include entertainment integration, strategic planning, crisis communication, event production, and national and global media outreach. Before launching PR You, I was a director in global PR firm Ketchum's entertainment marketing practice, where she represented top brands such as ABSOLUT and Estee Lauder, orchestrating multi-million dollar events and leading all entertainment outreach and celebrity relationships.

Prior to Ketchum, I served as the global communications director for denim brand Chip & Pepper. I am proud to have launched the new brand in 13 global markets, while coordinating celebrity relations, events, media and crisis communications, and business media outreach, which included a front-page feature on the then little-known brand in the Wall Street Journal. I also worked at Bragman Nyman Cafarelli, where I cultivated my entertainment PR and event expertise working on brands including Sony PlayStation and Proactiv Solution.

I began my career at Ketchum, where I oversaw Mattel's flagship Barbie brand account. I hold a degree in journalism with an emphasis in public relations from California State University, Chico.

Becky Vieira’s Experience

  • Co-Founder

    PR You

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    August 2008 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Educational PR consulting services that teach small companies and start-ups how to do their own PR before they require the assistance of a publicist or agency.

    In today's marketplace public relations is the cornerstone of any solid business. The public's perception can make or break your brand, and in this Internet age favorable (and frowned upon) news travels fast.

    For start-ups, PR is a must-have element. With marketing and advertising budgets being out of reach for most first-time entrepreneurs, PR is the most cost-effective means to build your brand. The field is a foreign word to most, who consider PR to come in the form of a fast-talking publicist equipped with an ever-present cell phone. The truth is, the best ambassador for your brand is the person with the most passion – you.

    Large companies and developed brands will always need a PR division, in-house publicist or PR firm. But for the smaller companies and start-ups, educating an individual or select group on the basics of PR and how to implement a strategic campaign makes the most sense financially.

    And that is where we come in.

    We are educational PR consultants. We work with companies over a period of time to teach an individual or team how to launch a successful PR campaign. What's more, we give you the tools to maintain and expand the outreach to create an ongoing and sustainable program. And, down the road, when the time comes to hire a full-time publicist or PR firm we will advise you on that as well. We work with each client on an individual basis to determine specific market needs and goals, and put together a program that we will teach the appointed team member to run.

    For a small company, a publicist or PR firm can cost upwards of $100,000 annually. For a fraction of that cost, we will teach the skills to maintain your own PR and keep that budget available to grow your business in other ways.
