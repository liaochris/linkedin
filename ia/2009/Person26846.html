Benjamin Yiu’s Summary

Dream Cortex is a full service company specializes in game and 3D animation production, headquartered at the Hong Kong Cyberport complex. Here are the business models:

1. Co-Investment: Dream Cortex evaluates the possibility of co-investing in the game development process. In exchange, a share percentage over the game revenue as ROI.

2. Work-For-Hire: Dream Cortex works in a traditional outsourcing business model, developing full projects or part of them.

3. IP Brands: Dream Cortex has access through its JV partners for some of the world widely popular character IPs such as Hello Kitty, Ben10 and Power Puff Girls.

4. IP Technologies: Currently Dream Cortex is working with MXRi for Augmented Reality (AR) projects. AR can be applied to marketing, games or consumer products.
