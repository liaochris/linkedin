Quynh Nguyen’s Summary

I value diverse experiences and have many different interests that center around my sense of caring for people, my interest in technological nnovation, and my intent to make a positive difference in the world.

My newest endeavor is to learn about sustainability and apply these concepts in all aspects of my life. To accomplish this, I have enrolled in the MBA for Sustainable Management program at Presidio School of Management. Learning about business concepts with an emphasis for how to run a business in a way promotes financial and environmental sustainability has been an extremely valuable experience for me. I'm in school part time, so I continously apply what I learn from my MBA program to my professional and personal endeavors.

I have lead and managed qualitative research projects around product innovation, new user markets, and product definition for Adobe Systems, Autodesk, and PG& E( as contractor). I also have lead and managed research around user experience and UI design and development efforts for shrink wrap software as well as websites and web applications.

I'm also leading a volunteer based green team at Adobe engaging with Adobe around sustainability projects and ideas. I started a green speaker series and an employee distribution email list where we discuss sustainability practices. I also authored an eco-wiki for sharing green ideas.
