Kirtland Snyder’s Summary

I am a highly accomplished award-winning Senior Executive with 24 years of experience as a Creative Director and Senior Writer in advertising, direct marketing and corporate communications. A results-oriented, decisive leader, I have a consistent record of success in both consumer and business-to-business marketing. My knowledge of industry sectors is wide and varied, and includes healthcare, financial services, gaming, hospitality, information technology, higher education, the arts, consumer electronics, telecommunications and banking.
