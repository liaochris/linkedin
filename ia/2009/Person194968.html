Robert Cole’s Summary

Robert Cole is the founder of RockCheetah. RockCheetah is focused on developing next generation eBusiness processes, integrated marketing, seamless distribution, Web 3.0 content, and management advisory services for the travel industry. Robert played an integral role in the development and launch of SideStep’s meta-search packaging product.

Robert was previously Vice President, Destination Experience for Mark Travel where he oversaw account management and operational support for destinations, hotel groups, car rental companies, and attractions for all vacation brands, including United, Southwest, Spirit, Midwest, Continental (UK), Funjet, Fabugo and Mark International.

Before joining Mark Travel, Robert was Vice President, Hotel & Car for Neat Group, a subsidiary of Cendant Travel Distribution Services. Neat developed and patented dynamic packaging technologies for the travel industry.

Prior to joining Neat, Robert was responsible for electronic distribution at Budget Group where he managed all global distribution systems and internet channels. He created direct interfaces with priceline.com, Southwest Airlines and EuropCar.

Previously, Robert also operated the hotel line of business for Sabre Group – including global account management and product development, achieving $2.7 billion in booking volume, strategic joint ventures with JAL/AXESS and ABACUS for the hotel vertical, and creating the Hoteligence product.

Before Sabre, Robert was Vice President, Business Development for Anasazi / Travel Resources; served as Corporate Director of Marketing for Journey’s End; and Corporate Director of Marketing Planning for Four Seasons Hotels.

Robert has served on the HEDNA board of directors, the Open Travel interoperability committee, the HITIS advisory committee, and as a partner representative for the Cornell Center for Hospitality Research. He has presented at travel conferences and Universities including Cornell, Houston, Queens, and Ottawa.
