Fred Pietzsch’s Summary

Evangalized VoLAN as toll bypass for MSO's and energy offset technology for Utilities back in '93. Moved on to Asterisk with XMPP in private lab with Aastra, Polycom, Snom, and other IP phones. Enjoyed VoIP sales applications engineering Broadsoft Broadworks and Clearspan systems for Aastra. Currently studying social implications of ubiquitous access. Goal is to remain on the leading edge of VoIP development in a customer facing positon, helping people find solutions.

Fred Pietzsch’s Specialties:

VoIP & Telecommunications Sales Engineer with deep experience presenting technical solutions to a broad audience in a meaningful way. Able to design integrated systems and provide VoIP pre-sales support for Broadsoft BroadWorks, Clearspan Extreme Enterprise, and open standards VoIP solutions of 2,000 to 200,000 (up to 2,000,000) lines. Rich history providing TDM, "voice over", and blended solutions from traditional voice and data vendors in line sizes from 10 to 100,000 end users.
