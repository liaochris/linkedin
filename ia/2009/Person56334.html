Dave Mayer’s Summary

“Nothing in this world can take the place of persistence. Talent will not; nothing is more common than unsuccessful people with talent. Genius will not; unrewarded genius is almost a proverb. Education will not; the world is full of educated derelicts. Persistence and determination alone are omnipotent." - Calvin Coolidge


12+ years of successful, relationship-based consultative selling. Creative approach to helping businesses decide which technical solution is best for their short and long term needs and exceeding quotas. Focused on highly specialized sales in the IT, Engineering, Scientific and R&D realms.

Acted as a conduit to new IT solutions, new opportunities and new connections. It's a passion of mine to help people create strategic business alliances, and help others find new careers. It is gratifying to help a client make new contacts that they otherwise wouldnt have had- been able to compete for projects that were not in their expertise previously, and potentially find a team/candidate/consultant who can make an immediate impact in their organization.

I have been successful over the years by staying in touch with the same people and helping them reach their goals- as a result, I have a very large network of friends and colleagues around the country who come to me for advice and solutions. Referrals come in regularly for people looking for new services or people who might benefit from our technical placement services. I would be happy to be of assistance to you in your searches - whatever they may be- and please keep us in mind as you continue on through your business travels- our ability to make your needs a priority is second-to-none.
