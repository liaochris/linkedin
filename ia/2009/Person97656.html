Ibrahim Elbouchikhi’s Experience

  • Co-Founder and CEO

    Dig 'N' Swap

    (Consumer Services industry)

    July 2009 — Present (4 months)

    Dig N' Swap is the easiest most fun way to swap fashionable clothing and accessories with people who share your passion for fashion. The idea for Dig N' Swap was born out of the lack of a sensible and environment-friendly way to deal with our ever expanding wardrobes. We love fashion, but we also consider that swapping clothes puts less of a strain on the resources of the planet by allowing an item to go through several lives. Dig N' Swap takes clothes-swapping parties to the next level by giving you access to items listed by other fashionistas from all over the country, and allowing you to update your wardrobe while protecting your saving account and the environment.

    Within 10 months of its launch, Dig N Swap is now a vibrant community with thousands of users and more importantly items, the model upon which DnS is based has been proven fully and we are now slowly transitioning into the second phase of adoption: Early Adopter.

    I am continuously looking for ways to expand/develop partnerships, enter new markets, and of course market the heck out of DnS, please reach out to me if you can help in any way or see an opportunity for me to assist you in some way.

  • Co-Founder

    Authentic Morocco

    (Import and Export industry)

    January 2000 — Present (9 years 10 months)

    “Authentic Morocco” was born in 2000 out of a combination of idea and ideal. We were inspired by a number of fair trade initiatives to set up an entity that would allow Moroccan artisans’ products to reach new markets, as well as to improve the quality of life of the artisans and their families.

    We source our products through a network of artisans-partners scattered throughout Morocco. We develop a personal relationship with each of them, working to improve their quality of life through fair retribution, design assistance, sustainable development training, and assistance in the schooling of their children.

    “Authentic Morocco” is a purely e-commerce player. We distribute our products exclusively online. This organization allows us to maintain a minimal cost structure. We have exported to more than a dozen countries including the USA, Canada, UK, Denmark, Sweden, Germany, France, Spain, and Australia.
