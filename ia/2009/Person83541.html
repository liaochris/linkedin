Garry Pastore’s Summary

I have over 25 years of both front/behind camera experience. I have co starrred in 10 feature films and guest appeared in 15 televison programs. I have worked as a propman since 1996, with a lot of time invested on big budget film sets. Since the early nineties I have also completed a dozen screenplays . I have optioned two for sale and have been a hired gun for television and film rewrites. I am a published author of a book that sold over 10,000 copies in its first run. I am now running a company that will target the teen and young adult audiences with great independant feature films and create compelling televison dramas and talk shows.
