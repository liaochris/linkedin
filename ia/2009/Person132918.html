Kirstin Hagelskjær Petersen’s Summary

Former student of Modern Artificial Intelligence at the Mærsk McKinney Møller Institute in Odense. Completed a master's project in the area of autonomous construction of temporary human habitats.

Former bachelor of Electro-technology at Odense University College of Engineering.
During my education I spent half a year internshipping at Jet Propulsion Laboratory, a sub-department of NASA, in California, USA. I also did my bachelor's project with the Planetary Robotics Group at JPL.
I have a large experience in system design, including mechanical and software aspects, but with a special focus on circuit design. This experience was developed through a large number of free-lance projects done for various companies in Denmark, USA and Finland. Furthermore I have some experience in presenting material, having been a tutor for courses on the Technical Faculty of Engineering in Odense and a substitute teacher at a private school in Odense several times.
Language skills include fluent English and Danish, good German, Norwegian and Swedish and some knowledge of French.
