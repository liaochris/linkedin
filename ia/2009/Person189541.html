R. PRASAD GULLAPALLI’s Summary

Successfully developed and characterized several clinical products and currently marketed products for Small Molecules (immediate and controlled release products - Tablets, Capsules, and Softgels) using patentable technologies. Experienced in preparing CMC-CTD documentation for regulatory submission. Regulatory Affairs Certified (RAC-US).

R. PRASAD GULLAPALLI’s Specialties:

Development of small molecules from Research leads to manufacturing Phase 1-3 clinical supplies in-house and at external CMOs.

Preparation of CMC regulatory documentation.
