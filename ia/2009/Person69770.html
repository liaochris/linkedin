Ed Olson’s Summary

An experienced, artist with established credentials and a successful track record in the production of art for film, television, book, print, online and interactive projects. Proficient with Flash and Photoshop using the Cintiq, plus training in Maya character animation. Toon-Boom tutorial. PC & Mac - based.

Head Concept Development Artist for team of fifty producers and artists, staffed and managed group of twelve artists, Feature and T.V. Storyboard Artist, Storyboard Supervisor, Animation Director, developed projects and managed freelance artists while providing services to multiple media clients.

Ed Olson’s Specialties:

concept development, flash animation, maya animation, multimedia production, storyboards, character design
