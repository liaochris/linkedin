Robert Michaels’s Summary

On the one hand, I am director for Global Tribe, founded by Anthony Walton in '92 and adopted by Newsboys, to address social and spiritual poverty through various means, including building homes for the poor in Baja Mexico, as well as REACH evangelistic events. I also work with Newsboys developing strategic partnerships.

On the other hand, this year I founded Positively Sports to promote the positive side of sports, character, honor, and integrity among athletes, and working with selected individuals to help educate athletes on specific issues like character, financial management, etc.

To be certain not to waste time, I am a volunteer chaplain for FOP Lodge 41 / Tennessee, where I am an active member.I also manage the career of author / speaker Robert Davis, a former New Orleans Police Officer.

My professional focus is as an Independent practitioner for author / speaker development, emphasizing brand development and management, strategic planning, product development, enhancing market visibility, and exploitation of every opportunity for building the clients network of relationships, influence, and productivity.

I am a dreamer who sees what can be and works tenatiously to make that dream reality.
