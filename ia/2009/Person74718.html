Eric Powell’s Summary

I have been involved in interactive entertainment, design, advertising and marketing for over a decade working with both small and large-scale advertising agencies, interactive shops, and in-house teams like TBWA\Chiat\Day, Avenue A/Razorfish, Rare Medium, Virgin Entertainment, and DNA Studios. I specialize in hands-on Art Direction and Flash Design for online media and have been responsible for the concept, design, direction, and deployment of projects for clients such as Coors, Best Buy, Molson, Nintendo, Dreamworks, Néstle, Lucas Arts, Sony, Microsoft, Nissan, Levi's, Apple and more.

In addition to my online experience I have been a signed recording artist and performer with a popular underground band since 1991 where I have released 6 full-length albums on major and independent labels as well as touring extensively in North America.

I am also an accomplished composer having scored movies for Lionsgate, MGM, and Open Sky Entertainment, provided music licenses for Microsoft, Speed TV, CBS, MTV, Sony Computer Entertainment and more.

Eric Powell’s Specialties:

Interactive Art Direction and Design for online entertainment, advertising and marketing including experience design, usability and animation. Music production and composition.
