Jim Brazell’s Summary

Jim Brazell is a consultant, technology forecaster, strategist and speaker focusing on 21st century issues including community competitiveness, educational innovation, emerging technology and jobs.

Jim’s clients include think tanks, companies, universities, community colleges, workforce boards, economic development organizations and K-12 schools.

Jim's mentor is Dr. Francis X. Kane, a principal member of "Project Forecast", one of the most comprehensive long-range assessments of the military science and technology undertaken just after the launch of Sputnik.

Since 2003, Jim has participated in international technology commercialization assessment and matchmaking (Portugal-US); emerging technology forecasts and briefs for the State of Texas; designing Massive Multi-User Online Games (MMOG); CTE-STEM Education Advocacy across industry, education, workforce and economic development.

Projects include: University Technology Enterprise Network (Portugal-US); Texas Workforce Commission, Get There Texas, spaceTEAMS and Whyville Jobs for K-12 STEM Academic-Career Planning and Exploration; Alamo Community College spaceTEAMS & Information Technology Security Academy (ITSA), Digital Convergence Initiative (DCI); Digital Warrior - Fort Hood Battle Command Training Center; Joe Medic Fort Sam Houston NCO Academy and the NASA MMOG RFI.

Reports for TSTC Emerging Technologies and IC2.org include: Mechatronics, Digital Convergence, Video Games, Machine-to-Machine Computing, and Mechatronics. Free downloads at www.ventureramp.com (PUBS).

Jim's intellectual influences include George Gilder, Dr. George Kozmetsky, Dr. David Thornburg, Dr. Leonardo Salamini, Dr.Marshall McLuhan and scholars involved in the philosophy, history and sociology of science and media. Jim is a Summa Cum Laude graduate of Bradley University and George Gilder Fellow in High Technology and Public Policy.

Jim Brazell’s Specialties:

Industry, Workforce, Education, Economic Development Research, Strategy, Collaboration, Teaming, Advocacy, and Training. Regionalization, Technology-based Economic Development, Technology Commercialization Strategy, Assessment & Matchmaking (Nano-Bio-Info-Media-Telecom-Health-Aero). Seeking pre patent I.P. from Universities for regional job and wealth creation.
