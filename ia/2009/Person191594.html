Rachel Caplan’s Summary

I have twelve years experience as a film exhibition and distribution executive, with a background in film marketing and publicity. For the past three years I have also been providing services to Bay Area non-profit organizations in all aspects of special event production.

I am interested in developing skills and sharing experience of film exhibition and distribution. I’m passionate about the film industry and have a desire to support filmmakers' careers within it, by enabling personal development and connecting people.

Rachel Caplan’s Specialties:

Organization development, strategic planning, and program management for film festivals and non-profit organizations. Encompasses programming, 2.0 marketing, operations, and fundraising.
