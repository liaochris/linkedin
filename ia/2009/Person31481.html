Brad Collar’s Summary

With over 18 years experience in the entertainment industry, Brad is deeply involved in the development of new media technologies, production techniques and processes.

For the past several years Brad has worked on the development of the next generation of DVD products, namely Blu-ray.

Having received his MBA in June, 2007, Brad was promoted to Vice President , Technology, where his roles have expanded to include digital distribution, 3D to the home, and other new media formats.

Brad Collar’s Specialties:

Audio/video compression technology, DVD/Blu-ray design and authoring, video on demand and digital distribution systems, 3D content production, and content protection technology.
