Gail Klanchesser’s Summary

I am an experienced BLS CPR & First Aid Instructor with over 8 years experience providing both public and private instruction. I am an AHA Training Center Faculty Member. I truly enjoy teaching the skills that can make a difference! Some of the greatest compliments that I have received are from former students who have successfully used the skills they learned in my classes.

In addition to my business I am very involved in my community. I am a former chair of a local school non-profit organization. A Former Finance Manager for National non-profit organization and a member of several local municipal and regional boards. I believe our involvement in our communities is what makes them thrive, grow and be places that we are proud to say we belong to. I also volunteer my time for American Heart Association fundraisers, projects with our local school, and maintain two websites for our School Supporters and their primary fundraiser, the Fox Point Sunset 5 Mile Road Race.
