Lee Kantor’s Summary

I have been involved in internet radio, podcasting and blogging for quite some time now. (Dr Fitness and the Fat Guy, my first show, began in the summer of 2005). I am constantly amazed at the caliber of the people that I get to meet and get to know through the various radio shows that I am involved with.

Since I began, I have interviewed well over 1000 entrepreneurs, business owners, authors, celebrities, sales and marketing gurus and just all around great men and women.

Now I spend each day helping entrepreneurs and business owners in Atlanta, GA see how hosting their own radio show and podcasting it on the internet can help them dramatically grow their businesses.
