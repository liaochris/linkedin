    What is SalesProfile.com?

    Selling Power publisher Gerhard Gschwandtner says, "SalesProfile™ is a great destination for accelerating the search for sales talent."

    SalesProfile™ is a new innovative job website for sales professionals where salespeople can effectively showcase their true talents online. Candidates can create a multimedia profile, deliver a 30-second "elevator pitch" video and complete an online behavioral sales assessment. Employer can post jobs, conduct pre-employment assessments, and upload their company video. This content-rich site will give employers the opportunity to quickly find the best candidates that match their search criteria.
