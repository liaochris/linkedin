Neal Capone’s Summary

I am a teacher and leader at heart. I enjoyed nine years in the classroom teaching middle and secondary mathematics in a high performing suburban school district. Desiring to learn more, refine my craft, and take on more of a leadership role, I have spent the 3 years working with "at-risk" populations as a teacher trainer. This position alloed me to get the finest professional development and then turn around and provide embedded professional development and coaching for administrators and classroom teachers that have the task of working with high needs students. From curriculum design and budget management to data analysis and mentoring relationships my experiences have increased my ability to positively influence more educators. With this goal in mind, I completed my certification in Educational Administration and accepted a postition as a District Data Coordinator at the Central New York Regional Information Center. Assisting districts with data analysis and data verification I have developed a completely new skill set. While becoming much more proficient with tools such as Access and Excel I have also refined my skill as a facilitator of data driven dialogue. There have been many data sources that have been available to districts but few districts that have used these data successfully to make decisions. I truly enjoy helping districts develop and design procedures and processes for making this a reality.
