Cris Popenoe’s Summary

Cris is renowned for her impressive breadth of business relationships in the media and entertainment world, and amongst marketers, technologists, venture capitalists, and entrepreneurs. She’s been a digital media maven for as long as there’s been digital media.

She uses technology as an enabler to access and organize information and narratives. Throughout her 30 years of experience, Cris has innovatively combined content, commerce, and social engagement into media, creating compelling interactive experiences. She is a creative executive and entrepreneur with a proven track record. Cris has packaged and produced numerous award-winning digital media projects, including online videos, games, and applications and widgets. She excels at being part of the creation and growth of both new products and at leveraging and extending existing brands and products.

Cris has an expertise is in the intersection of online video and social media promotional tools to drive revenue through syndication and engagement vehicles with a company, brand, or product. The work that she's done ranges from the basics of employing video to strategic recommendations on where best to deploy and why, negotiating and executing the partner deals with consumer and premium video sites, managing the relationship, doing the deployments, and online video advertising. She's also acquired enthusiast programming from both TV and online resources and curated programming channels.

Her professional and personal passions include the body-mind-spirit realm, wellness, green initiatives, and East Asian culture. If you asked many people what word comes to mind when they think of Cris, it may be her energy or passion, but it is likely to be her ability as a connector and her desire to use these connections to make the world a better place, and our individual lives more authentic and honorable. That’s what she values above all in herself and in others. She’s connected. She’s effective. She cares.

Cris Popenoe’s Specialties:

Online video strategy and monetization; content strategy and programming; social media; video platform enabling technology and ad networks; web audience engagement, social media and personal networks; Web 2.0 tools, widgets, and applications; leisure and casual games; program acquisition and production; deal making and representation, partnering and connecting; business launches.
