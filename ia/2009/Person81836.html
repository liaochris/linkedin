Career Summary

I'm an IT manager that likes to implement new technology and processes, drawing on more than 20 years of experience with large corporate IT environments and with significant technology vendors.

I aim for straight forward processes, trained people and solid tools in a secure architecture designed with enough flexibility to meet current and not yet anticipated requirements.

I have led or contributed on a multitude of committees, teams, industry panels, cross functional technical teams, task forces and swat teams, where I worked with my own people or colleagues from other departments and peers across the automotive and IT industries.

I also have worked with the major technology vendors, collaborating on strategies, partnering on projects, negotiating costs and facilitating services and support agreements.
