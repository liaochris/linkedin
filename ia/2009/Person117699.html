John M. Wills’s Summary

Freelance Writer/Speaker. Published dozens of articles on officer survival, training, and ethics on Officer.com and LawOfficer.com. as well as several print magazines. Short story published in Lt. Randy Sutton's book, True Blue, To Protect and To Serve (available on NLEOM.org and Amazon.com). Just released my first novel: "Chicago Warriors, Midnight Battles in The Windy City." Available now on Amazon.com and BarnesandNoble.com. My second novel, "Gripped By Fear," is set to be released in the Fall '09.

Member PSWA (Public Safety Writers Association), ILEETA (International Law Enforcement Educators and Trainers Association), FBINAA 175th Session, The Virginia Writers Club, and The Riverside Writers, International Thriller Writers, American Christian Fiction Writers.
