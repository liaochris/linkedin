Emma Wortt’s Summary

Hello and thank you for taking the time to read about me.

My business Em-powering Executives expertly coaches and trains business leaders. I specialise in, and am passionate about, the creation of outstanding leaders who exude calm, confidence and vision. I empower leaders to achieve potential for themselves, their staff and their business and still have a life.

Em-powering Executives ultimate goal is the creation of empowering organisational cultures in which staff are happy, loyal and promotable and where phenomenal personal and business success can be achieved.

I'm on Linked In to achieve connections with leaders to whom I can make a difference. If that's you, send me a message and connect with me. I'm looking forward to 'talking' with you.

Em
