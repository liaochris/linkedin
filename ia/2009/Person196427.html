Reno Macri’s Summary

Enigma has a wealth of experience in designing, building and project managing all sizes of custom build and Modular stands.

At Enigma, we understand the importance of providing cost effective solutions, be it a 6sqm or 300sqm stand. With dynamic and inspiring stands, we ensure the creation of a magnetic and inspirational enviroment that will allow you to draw in your target audience, and generate maximum results. Drawing on the expertise within Enigma, our exibitions team creates compelling and exciting enviroments with stand activities that maximise the advertising of your brand and key message uptake.
Big ideas need big solutions, Enigma offers a comprehensive solution for all your needs.

We have worked with many major brans accross the entire globe, including BP, Morgan Stanley, British Airways, Ferrari, Honda, Tesco, Vodafone, T Mobile, Samsung to nname a few.
