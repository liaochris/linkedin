Sai Sundar’s Summary

Consultant with experence in software and application development. OO modeling, analysis and design, Requirements management, Change management, Project planning and project management skills.

Emphasis on requirements capturing/gathering through research, formal-informal sessions and JAD sessions with SMEs and end users. Adept at understanding and modeling Business process and proficient in preparing Artifacts, including Use Case Diagrams, User Stories, Use Case realization, Word Documentation etc.

Ability to gain the confidence of the business community and ensure their functional and non-functional requirements for any business change are captured, agreed and prioritized.Very good listener, empathizing with the speaker and communicate clearly both verbally and non-verbally.

Self-starter and team player with excellent analytical, problem solving, learning, interpersonal skills and the ability to deliver solutions under high stress environment. Steep learning curve for new technologies.

Very strong people skills, leadership skills, strong time management and project coordination skills. Excellent verbal and written communication skills. Highly proactive, likes to take initiative. Once given general assignments, determines priorities and defines what should be done and how to do it.
