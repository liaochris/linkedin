John Hyde’s Summary

Owners and senior managers of print and graphic communications companies come to me when they are at a cross roads in making critical decisions that will affect their business. Typically they are looking for insights and industry specific expertise based on "best practices", and they expect to gain my perspective so they can make ownership decisions faster, with less risk, and with greater confidence.

John Hyde’s Specialties:

My consulting services include: business valuations; mergers and acquisitions; sale of business; debt restructuring; partnership agreements; succession planning; and turnaround management.

I am a lawyer by background, having practiced law in the late 80s at two large law firms and then starting my own consulting practice in 1990.
