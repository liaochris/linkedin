Julie Pentis, CPC’s Summary

I have been with Aureus Group since 2006, after graduating from Creighton University with a BSBA in Human Resources and Spanish. As an IT Recruiter I work with Account Managers and Recruiters in overall hiring procedures including sourcing, screening and interviewing candidates; reference verification; and matching candidates with job opportunities. In 2007 I earned the designation of Certified Personnel Consultant, and I will be finished with my Master's degree in Management in November 2009. I currently serve as Vice President of Membership for a local Toastmaster's group and will soon assume the position of Co-Chair for HRAM's Workforce Readiness Committee. I am looking to expand my network and get connected with talented IT/IS professionals who stand out among their peers and are interested in pursuing either Contract or Full Time positions.

Julie Pentis, CPC’s Specialties:

Some of the technologies and positions I recruit for include: Application Developers (.NET, C#, VB.NET, PeopleSoft, Oracle, SQL), Web Developers (ASP.NET, Java, Flash, Flex, ColdFusion), Systems Administrators (Unix, Linux, Windows, VMware, Citrix, Active Directory, Exchange), Network Administrators, Network Engineers (Cisco), Database Administrators (SQL DBA, Oracle DBA), Website Administrators (IIS), PC Technicians, Technical Support and Help Desk, Project Managers, and Business Analysts.
