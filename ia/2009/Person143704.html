Lori Stoddard’s Summary

My eclectic background as a writer and producer spans three decades of creative, award winning television and video project management, dovetailed with clear, focused marketing and advertising savvy. I have excellent client relations, impeccable people, writing and production skills, and an “out of the box” approach that is always on time, on budget, and results oriented.

I've successfully managed people and projects for decades, serving as Executive Producer, Producer and Manager for Television, Video and Cable News segments, Newscasts, Feature segments, Magazine shows, Documentaries, Specials, Events and Non-Broadcast videos. I've also managed the Promotion Department for a major television network affiliate, and broke new ground and created new business as the Homeview Program/Production Manager for Cox Media, Orange County.

Oh, yeah. I'm terrific at print pieces as well. Corporate image, brochures, advertisements, magazine articles, newspaper articles, press releases. Name something and I've probably written it.

Lori Stoddard’s Specialties:

Non-Broadcast corporate image, training and informational videos; Client-based broadcast and non-broadcast programming; Streaming video for websites; Print collateral; Radio production.
