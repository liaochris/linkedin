GO! Network’s Summary

OUR MISSION

GO! Network, Growth and Opportunities for Professionals, is a community initiative that empowers personal and professional growth through life, education and career opportunities.

OUR WORK

GO! Network has been launched to serve the growing needs of individuals whose employment has been affected by the current economic crisis. Our goal is to encourage, inspire, motivate and connect people to potential employment and entrepreneurial opportunities.

The program initiative consists of a series of free professional presentations, employer-driven seminars and workshops, networking meetings and job fairs.

LOCATION AND AVAILABILITY

GO! Network events are hosted in a new, state-of-the-art conference center located in the Partnership Center at 800 N. Tucker. Public parking is available across the street and within easy walking distance to the Conference Center. The GO! Network web site (www.gonetworkstl.com) was created to provide information, resources, event registration and feedback opportunities for participants.

COMMUNITY PARTNERSHIP

GO! Network is a community partnership between the United Way of Greater St. Louis, Anheuser-Busch InBev, World Wide Technology, Right Management, Paramount Planning of St. Louis and Celtic Creative. By teaming up with St. Louis corporate and nonprofit sponsors, GO! Network is establishing a partnership with individuals in our community to help achieve their career transition goals and personal results.

SEMINAR SERIES

Weekly seminars are based on topics that provide building blocks for finding employment, such as:

• Personal Assessment
• Creating a Resume
• Interview Skills
• Networking Your Plan
• Taking Your Message to the Market
• Make the Web Work for You

The topics are designed to prepare participants to find a job that matches their skills.

A career fair will be held to bring participants and employers together.
