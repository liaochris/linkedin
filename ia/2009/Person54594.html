Dan March’s Summary

With over 20 successful years of sales & marketing experience, Dan's background has spanned from technology and services to marketing and new media. Recently, Dan lead the creation and strategy component of LPI, a Web 2.0 consultancy launching a new viral marketing strategy to the Financial Services space at a top media company. Before that Dan served as Strategic Director for Aerospace & Defense with one of the world’s top Software companies, where he was a top sales Director and strategist. At Vignette and Nexgenix, Dan worked with the world’s largest Financial, Media & Entertainment companies providing technologies and services to run their portal, internet and content management initiatives enterprise wide. He is the founder of advertising agency 1:1 Media, LLC. and the founder of event promotions company Artopia.

Dan March’s Specialties:

Strategy, Marketing & Communications, Events, Customer Relationship Marketing, Viral Marketing, Social Media, New Technology trends
