Mohammed Khan’s Summary

Biography:

Mohammed Khan is an RUA (Rich Unified Applications) evangelist who specializes in design and implementation of enterprise grade applications. As Project Director of Enterprise Systems at Esberi Technologies Pvt. Ltd., he and his team designed and build enterprise applications using technologies like Adobe Flex and Adobe LiveCycle for MNCs. He is an active blogger and maintains a blog called The Pixel Code, where he shares his experience on UI design to data driven application development. Also he is the Google Tech User Group Manager (GTUG – Hyderabad) and regularly conducts workshops and speaks at Adobe User Group meetings in India. Off late he has been working on to develop the Rich Unified Application theory, and believes that in coming years it will be all about Unified Application Development. For more you can visit his blog, http://www.thepixelcode.com

Autobiography:

Currently serving as Project Director at Esberi, where I balance out my time between solution design and business strategy development. Primarily at Esberi, my focus is to share values with clients, partners and vendors, thus making a people-centric organization. Also off late I have been engaged in product development, where I have invested time and energy to design high-level and low-level architecture. With rapid shift in ubiquitous web technologies, its always necessary to keep with the pace, and same at Esberi I have tried to initiate and control change management.

Since 2006, when Esberi got founded, then a three member team and now a 30 member team (still growing) what I have learned and evolved is, working with creative and critical solutions involves one to bridge at very close professional level with its stakeholders. Thus this helps to deliver engaging and reliable applications.
