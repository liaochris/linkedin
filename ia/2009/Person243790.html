Jason Morgan’s Summary

In 1998, Jason co-founded Innersync with a solid balance of art and technology knowledge. At Innersync, Jason helps govern and refine the unique production process developed by Innersync. “Everyone knows process is key, and over the last 10 years, we’ve refined a process that uses business-intelligence and cutting edge tools to capture client knowledge, research the competitive market, and deliver a cohesive, intentional solution.”

The culmination of these efforts is called Campusuite CMS, a web content management system utilizing web 2.0 technology and methods.

With clients ranging from Yahoo!, International Paper, Delta AirElite, and many collegiate institutions, the focus of Innersync is to deliver powerful, process-changing technologies via the web.

Beyond a full understanding and advocacy of the progressive technologies that drive success for Innersync and its clients, Jason’s personal style in working with clients, vendors and staff is refreshingly blunt and insightful. He is the technical lead and oversees the entire production process on all Innersync accounts, and shares his partner Steve’s knack of making complex issues easy to understand.
