Ashwinikumar Patil’s Summary

After finishing MBA from IIMB in 2008, I joined TAS (formerly Tata Administrative Service), a managerial talent pool in the Tata Group, one of India’s largest business conglomerate.

Before joining IIMB, I was exposed to Sales & Marketing (specifically, branding and pricing) activities as an apprentice in my father's ventures & as a young entrepreneur. I put the learning to use as the Convener of the Annual Cultural Festival at IITG. I was also exposed to organizational and people management issues in this role.

I acquired in-depth knowledge of service operations and the origination processes in Commercial Leasing as well as the IT industry, while working with TCS-GE Commercial Finance relationship. At IIMB, I gained valuable experience managing retail operations (purchasing, shelf-space & inventory) & finance (working-capital) as part of MARS store on campus.

The summer internship with Tata Motors (deputed from TAS), needed me formulate strategy independently. It required me to understand relations between a new vehicle's launch and existing regulations, branding, sales teams, dealer-relationships, customer, product-life cycle and devise a launch strategy.

I did a post-graduate internship with Quetzal Verify Pvt Ltd, in the sell side of Human Resources services, specifically Training and Background Verification Services. The entrepreneurial nature of Quetzal allowed me to sharpen my business acumen, by engaging in strategic and day-to-day tactical issues.

In my first rotation with Tata Motors as part of TAS, I am engaged to develop a HR Roadmap for Nano project.

Currently, on my second rotation with Tata Power in Strategy engagement, I am expected to structure and close some acquisition deals.

I hope to add Structured Finance to my portfolio of functional experiences by the end of the year.
