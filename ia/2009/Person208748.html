Sandra Tillman’s Summary

Sandra Tillman, Senior Vice President of Child Health Corporation of America, leads a multi-faceted division of CHCA focusing on the delivery of executive programming, strategic planning, education and development, conference planning, and corporate communication.

The Leadership and Learning Strategies division is responsible for CHCA’s Executive Institute and Strategy/Research and Development Committee of the Board of Directors. These two mission critical programs of CHCA seek to ensure that CHCA continues to be CEO-driven by engaging Owner Hospital CEO’s in strategic and business issue analysis. Sandra has also cultivated CHCA’s CHEX Knowledge Exchange program into the service it is today, fostering the development of award-winning course content and an educational library of more than 200 resources delivered to 100,000 children’s hospital employees across the nation.

Prior to assuming her current position, Sandra directed CHCA’s business development and investment management functions. She conceptualized and developed a number of CHCA’s long-standing Forum groups as well as other program initiatives. As the managing director for the Child Health Investment Corporation, she was responsible for cultivating venture industry partnerships and overseeing the placement of over $10 Million in investment funding into venture capital funds and investment opportunities in the field of pediatric health care.

Sandra has been with CHCA for 14 years and has a background in managed care and health care management. Prior to arriving at CHCA, she worked in managed care and pharmacy benefit product development for Business Men’s Assurance Corporation, a Kansas City based private insurer. She holds a Master of Health Services Administration degree from the University of Kansas and a Bachelor of Science in Pharmacy from the University of Missouri-Kansas City.
