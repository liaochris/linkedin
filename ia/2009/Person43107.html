Chris Cucchiara’s Summary

A dedicated entrepreneur for 30 years, a former world-class body builder, and nationally recognized expert in both the fitness and nutrition industries, Chris has been coaching and mentoring people in the areas of business development, leadership, lifestyle management, fitness, nutrition, and weight management. Chris was introduced to network marketing in 1995. Because of his burning desire to succeed, on a part-time basis, Chris became one of the fastest growing distributors and income earners in his first company. Due to his success, Chris became a fulltime Network Marketer in 2002.

He comes to EIRO Research as a nationally recognized leader in the industry having built large organizations and reaching the top pay plan in his previous companies. He has enjoyed seeing thousands of people reach entirely new levels of personal satisfaction, growth, and financial success.

    Eiro Research Signs Master Distributors

    Dallas, Texas - October 27, 2008 - Eiro Research, a Dallas-based nutritional company, announced today that Bo Short, Ty Tribble, and Chris Cucchiara joined the direct selling company as founding partners and master distributors.

    “Eiro Research is agile, dynamic, and well-capitalized – all the elements necessary to build a profitable, stable, rapid-growth company are in place,” said Bo Short. “The industry needs a new hero, and Ty, Chris, and I are confident Eiro is it.”
