Matthew Knisely’s Summary

Matt Knisely is an innovator who specializes in the field of developing and unleashing personal and organizational creativity, uniqueness, innovation and diversity. In other words, he gets bored really easily.

He is an enthusiastic, creative and inspirational leader with excellent team-building and leadership skills who has committed himself to the church and Non-Profit marketplace. Matt's background includes corporate marketing and communications, extensive TV and small business management experience, PR and media relations and expertise in producing compelling visual stories. With extensive media and leadership experience Matt helps Non-Profits develop Media & Communications technologies to reach emerging generations. To put it simply he is a catalyst for change and a different way of thinking.

Matt has been described by The National Press Photographers Association (NPPA) as "perhaps the most versatile videojournalist working today," and is a recognized name in visual storytelling and team building. He is known within the industry for his ability to produce technically and logistically complex assignments with expert use of color and light, and has national reputation for his unique and teachable approach into TV Photojournalism and Visual Storytelling. Matt has recruited, managed and coached some of the most talented visual storytellers in the country. He thrives on creating environments that expand imagination, unleash creativity, and maximize the creative potential of those around him. In 2006 his team earned runner-up for the best Television Station for Photography in the United States as awarded by the National Press Photographers Association and Poynter and additionally in 2007 and 2008 when his station became one of the most successful FOX Affiliates in the country.
