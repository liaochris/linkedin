Gaurav Mishra’s Summary

After doing my MBA from Indian Institute of Management Bangalore in 2002, I joined Tata Administrative Service (TAS), a fast track cadre in the Tata Group, India’s largest business conglomerate.

After doing cost rationalization projects from the client side at Tata Chemicals (with McKinsey) and Tata Communications (with BCG) and brand management projects at Tata Tea and Tanishq, I joined Tata Motors, where I handled the roles of Regional Product Manager (East), Regional Product Manager (West) and National Brand Head (Indica).

At present, I’m on a sabbatical from the Tata Group to serve as the Yahoo! Fellow in International Values, Communications, Technology, and Global Internet for 2008-09 at the Institute for the Study of Diplomacy at Georgetown University.
