  • CFO and Managing CPA

    Precision Financial Accounting (PFA)

    (Accounting industry)

    February 2009 — Present (9 months)

    We offer the following in corporate and personal financial services:

    1. CFO Outsourcing Services:

    These services are essential for publicly traded company working on a start up budget or a privately held company with revenues of $6million or more.

    PFA will be your ally and alleviate the tension many management’s feel when they scale up their operations or when they sign that audit engagement letter. We can support the planning for either for a bond, financing requirement, or planning to go publicly traded. These services for a company that does not have the resources to hire a sophisticated accounting team full time, and/or their controller or CFO is inundated with the day to day affairs of the company.

    2. Financial Support Services :

    PFA will help you through the struggles of starting, maintaining and eventually exiting from your business.

    Through a complete walkthrough of your business we will analyze and discuss a clients economic situation and advise on different affects of moving forward with the various strategies.

    We alleviate the frustration many business owners have by assisting in the preparation of their tax disclosures and their financial statements and a full set of books and records to assure their corporation or LLC are compliant.


    3. Compilation and Review Services:

    At PFA we alleviate your compliance worries and accounting costs because we won’t recommend services you don’t need. Many times cash basis presentation financial statements without footnotes are all that are necessary and sometimes the more rigid financial statements in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) with full disclosures are required.

    We also do special procedures, such as forensic work and fraud detection, which can be way more cost effective, efficient and less intrusive than a full audit.
