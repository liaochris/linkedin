Ian Mecklenburgh’s Summary

Ian brings over 20 years experience in the communications, IT and media sectors. He has specialised in defining, developing and delivering real businesses for Internet, IPTV, interactive TV and broadband services - for global blue chip organisations and startups.

Ian has a visionary and forward thinking approach to technology and strategy which has enabled him to grow and manage global organisations - delivering substantial new business and technical solutions in to the broadcast, media, telecommunications and distribution sectors.

In addition to strategy and development, Ian has a strong background in delivery and real world execution managing P&L and large global teams in addition to strategy and development teams.

Ian Mecklenburgh’s Specialties:

In depth media, broadcast, interactive and telecommunications experience in a number of 'convergence' areas including Broadband, IPTV, digital and interactive television (iTV). Development and delivery of commercial and technology strategy. International depth and experience. Change management and training/education in new technology and services. Good communicator.
