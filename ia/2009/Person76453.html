Eugenio Marín’s Summary

Eugenio Marín is the Business Development Director at TechBA-Montreal, in this position he develops business intelligence systems and strengthen TechBA’s network to support technology-based companies within the program (Technology Business Accelerator); he was chosen for this position due to his previous work and knowledge about technology enterprises development, program coordination in Mexico and outstanding results in all his initiatives. In this position, his primarily focus are these tech sectors: Aerospace, IT, New Media, and Biotech.

He has been an associate consultant for FUMEC (The United States-Mexico Foundation for Science) since 2006, providing advice on topics of innovation, technology commercialization, value proposition analysis, and corporate diagnostics methodologies. He was the Pre-Acceleration Program Manager for TechBA in Mexico.

Mr. Marín co-founded in 2004 a branch start-up of a global corporation, headquartered in Germany, dedicated to the design and virtual integration of cabin & cockpit systems for the Aerospace sector in the European market, his position: CTO for the Americas.

A graduate from the Engineering Faculty of UNAM (Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México) and with a degree in Mechanical Engineering, Mr. Marín was founder president of the Mechatronics Society of the Engineering School; he has remained active in his Micro-systems and Micro-technology research, as well as on Virtual Reality Systems Integration and Aeronautical Business Strategy.

Eugenio Marín’s Specialties:

Business Development: Aerospace, Multimedia, IT, Biotech.
Research Interests: Aerospace, Microengineering, Mechatronics, Virtual Reality, Engineering Design, Management.
