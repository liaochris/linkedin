Gene Korolev’s Summary

Specializing in piano rebuilding and restoration of Steinway & Sons pianos and other brands. Expert in custom piano design. Offering services in all aspects of piano services.

We implement our own invented devices and use old-world craftsmanship to maintain superior quality.

We sell exclusive line of our own restored Steinway & Sons pianos.


Our services include, but not limited to:
* Soundboard Replacement or Repair
* Pin block Duplication
* Bridge Recapping or Repair
* Harp Re-bronzing
* Complete Restringing
* Action restoration, Regulation and Voicing
* Total Action Rebuilding (New Parts)
* Action Mechanism, Keybed and Pedal System
* Ivory Keyboard Restoration or New Ivory Keyboard
* Tuning
* Custom Piano Rebuilding by Request
