___ Goals ___

My first goal is, well, to be the Björk of the video game world, incorporating ferocious individuality and cosmopolitan savvy into an unmistakable aesthetic. I seek achievement as a game designer, creative director, and all-around "innovation consultant." (Who'd've thought I would ever find it legitimate to study "business?")

My second goal is to continue to pursue study of video games at the stuffiest, most academic and pedantic level possible, spread discursively across disciplines. I intend to continue this thrust through the post-doctorate level and eventually attain deanship. Meanwhile, I enjoy corporate training gigs; teaching is a rush.

Eventually, perhaps while serving as an adjunct professor, I would like to develop my own espresso bar/cocktail bar/arcade/game shop/contemporary media gallery.

Someday I will release my nascent music material and perform to a sold out show. Just you wait.
