Mark Straathof’s Summary

Business developer with more than 17 years of experience in consultancy and IT. Academic degree in business economics. Industry knowledge of Professional Services, Telecom, Capital Goods, and Chemicals. More than 8 years of people management responsibility, low staff turnover, current headcount = 80. Has experience in both building an organisation from scratch as well as turnaround management.

His current success is 8 years of self-founded, growing and profitable business. P&L and cash responsibility. Regional responsibilities since 2005.

Mark is married to Suzanne Straathof-Kocken, Sales & Marketing Director of KONE BV Elevators and Escalators in the Netherlands. Mark and Suzanne have 2 daughters and live in The Hague (Den Haag).
