Susan Brauer, CME’s Summary

Marketing consultant and strategist who cultivates effective face-to-face marketing opportunities for companies - especially those exhibiting at trade shows - by helping them set and obtain measurable objectives to continuously improve results over time. Over 20 years experience in the trade show/event industry in setting strategic, quantifiable objectives, promoting and strengthening key corporate messages and demonstrating return on investment results.

Susan Brauer, CME’s Specialties:

Strategic goal setting, hands-on, interactive workshops and seminars, measurement programs for face-to-face marketing events, booth staff training
