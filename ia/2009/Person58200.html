David Mandar’s Summary

Utilizing almost 20 years experience within the Information Technology field, David has worked in many roles including Programmer, Project Manager, Account Manager and 14 years as a Recruiter.

David started recruiting with CNC Global where he spent 4 successful years honing his recruiting skills. He worked for several small firms before going to CGI, a large Canadian consulting firm where he hired several hundred resources over 3 years. He spent 1 year with MFX, a subsidiary of Fairfax as the Recruitment Manager establishing hiring processes, managing agency contracts and improving employee retention before moving to Oracle. David spent almost 4 years with Oracle supporting their NA Consulting Group in Canada. David analyzed the hiring needs of managers and filled these requirements across all ERP and CRM practice areas.

David is currently the Engagement Manager for Momentum's OnDemand Consulting Group. OnDemand provides high quality, experienced resources for project and operational based activities precisely when they are needed. This allows organizations the option of keeping projects "in-house", maintaining project control, utilizing their own resources, and minimizing the potential risk of outsourcing projects.

OnDemand Consulting ensures the just-in-time delivery of I.T expertise through a model that validates the resource requirement, the actual skill level of each resource and then integrates, supports, and manages the resource throughout each engagement.

OnDemand Consulting has the real life I.T. acumen and hands-on experience necessary to provide a resource solution that consistently delivers value and tangible results. OnDemand Consulting has a resource network comprised of fulltime employees from OnX and Momentum as well as our technology partners. OnDemand Consulting also has a diverse team of independent contractors that have a proven history of working with OnX on past engagements.
