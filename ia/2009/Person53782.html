Daniel Latorre’s Summary

I'm currently consulting and looking for Social Media product management work in either the commercial or non-profit space.

I have a strong design and product centered management practice from 14 years of experience in online interactive media managing teams of highly talented Visual Designers, Interaction Designers, Information Architects, Writers, Interface and Backend Developers.

The majority of my experience is in producing the concepting & design of web experiences, but also includes some console and mobile video game production design work, and Interactive TV projects.

Daniel Latorre’s Specialties:

Social Media Strategy, Innovation methods, Product Management, Visual & Interaction Design Management, Design Process Planning, Design Research, Rich Media Design Management, Creative Producing, Cultural Research, Mentoring
