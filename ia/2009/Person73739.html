Eran Savir’s Summary

Mr. Savir has more than 8 years experience in Business Development, Product Management and Software Development. Prior to establishing Kampyle, Mr. Savir worked as a Product Manager at SAP and managed a product mutually developed by SAP and Microsoft. Prior to that, Mr. Savir held a development managerial position at SAP and served as the Development Project leader for one of SAP’s most strategic products. Before joining SAP, Mr. Savir worked as an Instructor at the Sivan High-Tech Institution Center. Mr. Savir has great experience in Enterprise applications and has specialized in end-user productivity applications, such as Outlook and Enterprise portals. Mr. Savir holds a bachelor's degree in Law and LLB from Sha'arei Mishpat College.
