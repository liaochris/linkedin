Tord Selmer-Nedrelid’s Summary

Extensive work in the fields of online journalism, online research and in how journalism is affected by media convergence.
Experienced speaker and teacher/journalism trainer. Author of a handbook in online research for journalists. Several years experience as online/radio/tv reporter and assignment editor in online media/radio/tv.

Tord Selmer-Nedrelid’s Specialties:
