David Lippman’s Summary

A 26 year collection industry background and a desire to become an attorney took this collection veteran back to school starting in 1998. Starting part time at Northeastern University in Boston, graduating summa cum laude with a degree in electronic commerce.

Suffolk Law School Boston Mass 2001 - 2004

Moved back to Arizona and passed the bar. Started working at The Hameroff Law Firm a week after taking the bar. I transformed the law firm from a small 2 person collection operation bringing in about 50k a month in collections and grew it over a 2 year period to a fairly large operation grossing over half a million per month in collections.

August 2006 started my own firm, Lippman Law Group, P.C.

January 2007, merged my practice with Phoenix attorney George Griffeth to form Lippman Griffeth & Associates, P.C. focusing on collecting in a respectful manner.

December 2008 George exits the collection practice and the firm reverts back to previous name Lippman Law Group, P.C.

The firm now has over 30 employees including 3 attorneys, over 15 collectors and support staff, the firm's goal is to be the premier collection law firm in the southwest.

David Lippman’s Specialties:

While there is no certification for debt collection attorneys, I am probably one of the few attorneys practicing in this field that actually knows how to collect a debt. I've worked all stages of debt collection. Many attorneys enter the collection industry by accident. At Lippman Griffeth, we have the experience and know how to collect your past due accounts, treating your customers as customers and allowing them to repay their debts in a respectful manner.
