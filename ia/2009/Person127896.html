Kathy Sweeney, NCRW, CPRW, CEIC, CCM’s Summary

Kathy Sweeney, Nationally Certified Resume Writer, Certified Professional Resume Writer, Certified Employment Interview Coach, and Credentialed Career Manager started The Write Resume in 1987. She has helped thousands of clients secure interviews through effective preparation of targeted resumes and career search documents. She spent six years as a Hiring Manager with two Fortune 500 corporations prior to owning her business.

Ms. Sweeney holds four credentials which show her expertise as a resume writer, and career management / interview coach.

Nationally Certified Resume Writer - 1 of 26 resume writers world-wide to hold this designation.

Certified Professional Resume Writer - Awarded in 1995; one of the first to hold this designation.

Certified Employment Interview Consultant - 1 of 5 career services practitioners in the world to hold this important credential.

Credentialed Career Master - 1 of 45 Career Services Professionals world-wide to hold this prestigious credential.

  • President

    National Resume Writers Assocation

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    August 2006 — December 2007 (1 year 5 months)

    As the 2006-2007 president of the National Resume Writers' Association, Kathy Sweeney led a strategic campaign to build the NRWA brand, heighten the organization's visibility to consumers, and enhance the value of membership through innovative training and conference programs. During her tenure, she implemented automated financial control systems, a full slate of educational programs for resume writers, and a weekly newsletter, in addition to hiring staff members and mapping out the re-design of the association's website.

    Further, she selected the programs and guided the marketing efforts for two national conferences for the association, which are focused on resume writers improving their craft. Under her direction, the association grew by 135 members in 18 months, and maintained an impressive 98% retention ratio.

    In addition, she doubled the attendance of resume writers at the annual conference to the highest number of participants the association had ever experienced.
