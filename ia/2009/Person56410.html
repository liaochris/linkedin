Dave Ozment’s Summary

After 12.5 years, January 12, 2009 marked my last day with Accenture. We were offered an attractive buyout and I decided to jump... now my task is to stick the landing.

I'd say that my time working in the Healthcare Senior Products space has been my most rewarding years. I was part of 3 Part D projects - including a lead role in an intensive go-live effort for a large regional Blues Plan, and I've spent most of the last 18 months leading 2 CMS readiness assessments - one for a leading national senior products provider and one for an outsourcing arrangement sponsored by Accenture.

I have thoroughly enjoyed my time with Accenture and feel that we parted on the best of terms... but after so many years on the road, it just 'felt right' to take the buyout and look for something closer to home with less travel requirements.

I believe that I am a determined, results focused leader/team member with a successful record of building, developing, and leading teams in delivering complex projects and managing complex operations. I am detail oriented and methodical in my approach - it drives my wife crazy sometimes. I enjoy working as a team to resolve issues and deliver quality and impactful results.

I have been successful in Accenture's fast-paced, collaborative, team-first, project-oriented, client-value and delivery-focued environment for years and fully believe that I can translate these skills and experience into my next opportunity with similar success.

Feel free to contact me and I'll be happy to provide you with a current copy of my resume - DaveOzment@gmail.com
