Melissa Zimberg’s Summary

My professional background encompasses fine and applied art, as well as broad creative and administrative responsibilities. As a professor, my pedagogy is derived from my broad experience as a creative professional and reflects my belief that all aspects of art are interrelated and derive from a shared point of cultural inflection; therefore, they should be taught with an interdisciplinary approach to art-making. My approach to teaching is comprised of three key content areas: skill-building, creative problem-solving and critical analysis; and reflects my belief that while studio practice is derived from a core of problem solving, critical analysis can only be undertaken when framed in a particular cultural moment.
