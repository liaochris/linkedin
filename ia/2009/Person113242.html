Jimmy Brown, Ph.D.’s Summary

Jimmy Brown is a seasoned management consultant specializing in business strategy, performance measurement, and organizational effectiveness. He has more than thirteen years of experience managing projects and leading teams to develop and implement practical strategies for business improvement. During his career, Jimmy has held senior level consulting positions at leading firms such as Booz-Allen & Hamilton and Accenture, and was most recently a member of the Strategy Practice Leadership Team for a boutique consulting firm focused on customer facing strategies and advanced business analytics. In these roles, he has often been called upon to consult with senior and C-level executives on the best ways to leverage data to drive business performance. Jimmy’s industry experience includes life sciences (provider, payer and pharmaceutical), federal government (civilian and DoD), not-for-profit, manufacturing and retail.

Jimmy is a frequent author and conference speaker on the topics of business strategy and organizational change, including delivery of the key note address at the 2006 Global Business and Finance Research Conference in London. He has severed as an adjunct instructor at several universities, and is regularly sought out for his insights on how to apply cutting edge theory to real world applications. Jimmy holds a Ph.D. in Organization Development and Master’s degree in Industrial and Organizational Psychology.
