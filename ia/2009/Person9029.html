Alice Peterson’s Summary

Alice Peterson is the Founder and President of Syrus Global, a leading provider of ethics and compliance solutions* headquartered in Chicago. She is a member of the board of directors, and chairman of the audit committee, of Hanesbrands Inc. (NYSE:HBI), which was spun off from Sara Lee in August 2006.

Ms. Peterson held executive positions at Sears, Kraft and PepsiCo prior to founding Syrus Global in 2002. At Sears, Roebuck and Co., she launched and ran Sears Online, where she oversaw significant growth in e-commerce revenues between 1998 and 2000. Ms. Peterson was the Vice President and Treasurer of Sears from 1993 through 1998, during which time she had a leadership role in the company’s major transformation efforts, including the IPOs and spin-offs of Allstate and Dean Witter Discover. She previously held strategy, corporate finance and treasury positions at PepsiCo and Kraft.

She also serves on the boards of the Ravinia Festival (summer home of the Chicago Symphony Orchestra) and the Music Institute of Chicago. She participates in a number of other civic and professional organizations in and around Chicago.

Ms. Peterson holds an M.B.A. from Vanderbilt University’s Owen Graduate School of Management, and a B.A. from the University of Louisville.

*Syrus Global’s mission is to enable a culture of principled decision-making by providing proactive ethics, compliance and workplace culture solutions. Using the company’s Listen Up™ ethics reporting and helpline programs, employees at Syrus’s client organizations may confidentially report wrongdoing and other values gaps, and seek/receive guidance on ethical dilemmas. Syrus’s clients use the highly acclaimed Listen Up case management system to handle submissions, track investigations, and measure results. These and the company’s other services assure the strongest level of governance and they create winning workplaces.
