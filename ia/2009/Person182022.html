Patrick Layhee’s Summary

Senior Recruiter in the broad discipline of civil engineering.

Career placement services for these positions: Practice Manager, Project Manager, Project Engineer, Civil Engineer, Environmental Engineer, Design Engineer, Licensed Professional Engineer (PE), Engineer-in-Training (EIT), Registered Professional Land Surveyor (RPLS), Surveyor-in-Training (SIT), GIS Analyst, Drafter, Designer, and CAD Technician.

All candidate inquiries are treated as private and confidential. GANE's services are free to the job candidate.

Patrick Layhee’s Specialties:

Technical specializations include: public works, municipal, land development, commercial site development, transportation, traffic, roadways, TxDOT, bridges, wastewater, water distribution, water resources, hydraulics and hydrology, structural, architectural, environmental, utilities, geographical information systems (GIS), geotechnical, surveying, forensics, project management, construction management and design (AutoCAD, MicroStation, GEOPAK).

Geographical focus: Houston and across Texas

Patrick Layhee’s Experience

  • President & Founder

    GANE Technology, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    2000 — Present (9 years)

    I place technical professionals in new jobs in Houston and across Texas. My recruiting expertise is in the broad discipline of civil engineering which includes: Graduate Civil Engineers, Surveying, Environmental Sciences, GIS Technology, Architectural Services, CADD and Technical Support.

    GANE has established recruiting relationships with the decision makers at the best engineering, consulting and design firms in Texas.

  • Vice President Search Services

    Norrell

    (Public Company; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    1997 — 1999 (2 years)

    Managed, trained and developed up to 45 professional recruiters in 13 offices in major cities across the USA for Norrell Financial Staffing. Handled recruiting operations, client development, sales, planning, P&L and financial reporting. Promoted from Regional Manager in Texas to national VP. Norrell was acquired by Spherion in 1999.

  • President & CEO

    M. David Lowe Staffing

    (Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    1987 — 1997 (10 years)

    General management responsibility for two permanent placement divisions (professional and administrative), a contract staffing division with 900-1000 temporaries working each business day, a computer learning center and multiple branch offices. Experience includes P&L, customer service, sales, marketing, training and planning. During my tenure MDL was ranked by the "Houston Business Journal" as the largest staffing firm in Houston. Started as a Recruiter Associate and was promoted to Department Manager, and then VP before becoming President and CEO. MDL was acquired by Norrell Corporation in 1997.
