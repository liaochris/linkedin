Joe J Thomas’s Summary

Voice Actor:

My name is Joe J Thomas, a VO in LA - Aftra, Sag, and Fi-Core. I have been acting in theatre, radio, television and film for more than 30 years. My voice is capable of a wide array of styles, characters, accents and impressions. I have my own in-home studio capable of producing high-quality audio in a variety of formats.

Some of my recent roles include:
* MGM Grand at Foxwoods - announcer, tv and radio spots
* Lex Luthor in the new Mortal Kombat video game
* L.A. Philharmonic radio spots - singer
* Sawatari and other characters in "Bleach", anime on Cartoon Network
* Voices in EA Games Sim Animals and MySims Kingdom
* Radio and TV spots for Round Table Pizza and Little Caesars
* Video game roles for Blizzard in "World of Warcraft", and "Diablo III"
