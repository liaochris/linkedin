ABOUT BUSCH INTERNATIONAL (the stuffy version):

Busch International is an executive search firm serving the High Technology & Venture community. Since 1985 we have collaborated with semiconductor, communication, networking and computer systems companies to attract and hire key individuals to lead their expansion. Our focus is to use our long-term relationships and partnerships to effectively locate and hire executive talent. We focus our services on CEO, Board Member and VP level positions. Busch International has understood GREEN is more than a color - we have successfully conducted searches in energy generation, storage (batteries), infrastructure and efficiency since our first with SunPower twelve years ago.

ABOUT BUSCH INTERNATIONAL (my own words):

I love my job and I love what I do. I enjoy coaching candidates on how to use LinkedIn to help them find their next opportunity. I've also been teaching candidates about using the internet to locate opportunities and managing their online identities. Social networking has drastically changed how executives conduct job searching. While my first priority now is to locate new business - the most personally rewarding part of my job is to coach candidates. Candidates are clients and clients become candidates. I am here to help. If you are a C-Level executive just starting a job search - ping me with your resume - let's see what I can do to make your search for a new opportunity as effective and productive as possible! I'm pretty sure I can give you a few tips and hints to help you. olga@buschint.com
