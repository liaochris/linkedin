Laura Lessa’s Summary

I have been working as an UX Architect since 2000. During this time, I have mentored and managed people, acquired a good knowledge of Information Architecture, Usability methods, Interaction Design and CMS. Most important, learned a lot from my peers with all kinds of backgrounds.

I have worked for Globo.com, the Internet branch of Globo media group (which owns newspapers, TV channels, radio stations and magazines in Brazil), helping them to deliver consistent and easy to use cross-media products. I was also responsible for defining the user research strategy for Globo's products, analysing and reporting the findings for the board of directors.

I am a passionate and hard-working practitioner who enjoys creating high-quality user experiences and truly believes in team work. I like managing people and I believe I have good skills for that, but I also enjoy hands-on working - especially complex projects that need strategic thinking.
