David Corman’s Summary

As manager of J-Town Production’s marketing activities, I promote websites in search engines, social networks and on other sites. My responsibilities include Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Pay-Per-Click Internet Advertising (PPC and SEM), Social Media Marketing and Optimization (SMM and SMO) and more.

Having worked in copywriting, research, public relations, and fundraising, I combine my varied experience with a broad knowledge base.

Graduating cum laude with a degree in History and Political Science from Rutgers University, I was both a college activist and student leader. I served as the President of the Israel Action Committee as well as the Mesorah (Orthodox) Community at Rutgers Hillel, and was editor of a campus literary journal. Since moving to Israel upon graduation, I studied at the David Shapell College of Jewish Studies as well as the Machon Meir Institute. Before J-Town, I worked at illuminea Marketing and Media as a Copywriter and Web Community Manager. Among other projects, I researched and wrote all content for NatanSharansky.org as well as managed Natan Sharansky’s online persona.
