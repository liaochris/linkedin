Caroline Short’s Summary

I began my career as a web editor for Amazing Grades, a secondary education portal focused on 14-19 education. Since then I have worked both in web and print across the education field, encompassing everything from early years and primary in my current role to higher education at Northumbria University and postgraduate research at Hero Ltd. My experience covers both project management of publications and feature research and writing. I have also worked in marketing and branding, and written brochures and advertorials.

Although my work is flexible, my specialist areas are education (primary and secondary) and children's literature.

I also write a personal blog to indulge my passions: fashion, food, and social media. I'm particularly interested in vintage and historical fashions, the way vintage styles are interpreted on the modern catwalk and the history of design.

You can find a slide show of my online content/social media profiles at http://carolineshort.extendr.com/
