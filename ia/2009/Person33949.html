Practical, Customized SALT Solutions:

I provide state and local tax (SALT) Solutions (controversy representation, planning, and compliance management) to businesses and individuals across the country primarily in the areas of income tax, gross receipts taxes, sales and use tax, credits and incentives and real estate transfer taxes.

My Mission:
To be the most accessible and responsive SALT consultant, providing customized SALT solutions at FIXED-FEES, and extraordinary project management and execution, resulting in deep and lasting relationships with clients.

Client Focus:
I serve a wide range of clients, but my primary focus is to provide practical, customized SALT solutions and representation to small businesses, middle-market companies, and accounting firms and law firms in need of SALT expertise.

I also provide SALT National Tax Office (NTO) Technical Solutions to accounting and law firms of all sizes who have or do not have in-house SALT expertise; and to SALT boutique firms, "Big 4" accounting firms and "Big Law" firms as an independent contractor.
