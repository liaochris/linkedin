David Furnari’s Summary

David Furnari has been working in IT field since 1998 when he was a PL/SQL developer using Oracle Developer/2000 and Oracle 7.3 database.

His interests immediately tend towards SQL statements performance tuning and Oracle database optimization.

As an Oracle DBA (UNIX and Windows), he improved his knowledge both in operating systems and databases up to Oracle 10g RAC.

He joined Realtech Italy in 2000 where he successfully supported and assisted colleagues in Oracle topics related to SAP R/3 systems. Then he was member of SAP Enterprise Portal group where he began to work with Mercury LoadRunner 7.x product.

He joined Neptuny in 2006 as Senior Performance Consultant. Currently, he leads and drives several performance projects ensuring customer satisfaction. He is the Business Unit Focal Point for “Load Testing & Tuning” . He is counselor and coach for younger team members.
