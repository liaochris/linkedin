Johan Albrektson’s Summary

Founder and C.E.O of Systems Go (2002-2010) with experience in business management and development, focus in Technical Support and Graphics/Interactive Developement.

Teacher/Technical Consultant at Futuregames Academy (2009), helping 35 students develop four complete games in 8 weeks to great sucess.

Plans to start a dedicated Interactive Development-studio in 2010/2011 with focus on advanced graphics and post-production (2010) as well as games development (2011).
