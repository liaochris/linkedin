Lee Greenspoon’s Summary

I have multi-vertical, international experience and have been directly involved in the marketing and strategic development of several unique businesses that optimize consumer behavior.

At Novator, I am responsible for business development, account management, marketing, strategic alliances and assuring that diverse client requirements are infused into the continuous evolution of our Hyper-Channel E-Commerce strategy. My priorities are focused on client acquisition, growth and ensuring that clients are leveraging Novator’s unique technology and service offering to profitably grow their online business.

Prior to Novator, I was a co-founder of a Business Intelligence software company; targeting retailers and focused on optimizing the consumer in-store experience using video analytics.

Prior to this, I was VP Corporate Development and a Partner at Twenty-Ten, Inc., a CRM analytics company that uniquely linked customer research with databases.

I launched my career at the international consulting firm, Bariston, Inc. working with organizations seeking global export/expansion opportunities in Latin America, Central Europe and Asia.

After this, I joined Hepworth & Company where I worked with client organizations to build competitive advantages in their CRM processes by understanding the elements of the cycle-of-service that lead to customer dissatisfaction and defection.

During the dot.com era, I co-founded e-Tech Direct Inc., a company providing fulfillment, CRM and multi-lingual e-commerce services to a range of U.S. retailers.
