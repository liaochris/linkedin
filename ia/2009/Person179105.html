Dan Abrams’s Summary

Recently I moderated an A-List Entertainment Industry Panel for UMEC (University of Michigan Entertainment Coalition).

http://umec.la/umec-spring-entertainment-panel/panel-guests/

Most of my professional experience has been in television production but I also work on a myriad of independent ventures.

Creator of OfficiallyDating.com (couples announcement & networking website).

Founder & Inventor of Optevi.com (personalized search engine).
