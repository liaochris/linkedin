Marko Wolf-Pany’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL OBJECTIVE
Seeking management challenges capitalizing on my extensive knowledge and experience in Program Management, Project Management, and Process Improvement Management in a progressive, results-oriented public or private sector organization.

Passionate about this domain. Welcome opportunity to discuss with Senior Management possible opportunities in Program / Project / Process Improvement Management (defining and institutionalizing Best Practices) to meet the needs of Organizations & Clients, thereby providing a competitive edge.

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE
Over 20 years of experience in providing technical direction and expert strategic, tactical, and operational advice and coaching on “state-of-the-practice” Program Management, Project Management, and Process Improvement Management “best practices” to private and public sector, national and international organizations.

Systems Engineer by training, Software Engineer by practice. Best known for helping organizations & individuals adopt “state-of-the-practice” Program, Project, & Process Improvement Management “best practices”, based on international frameworks / standards, in order to deliver reliable, maintainable, and portable systems, in scope, on time, and within budget.

As Director of Software Engineering Management, for a global provider of information technology and e-business solutions, developed and managed the Strategic Plan for implementing industry “best practices”. This enabled a new business unit to grow from nothing to over 800 people and from $0 to over $50 million / year in revenue in three years.

Based on strategic leadership provided:
1. Software Engineering Centres in Moncton, NB, and Toronto, ON achieved SEI SW-CMM Level 3;
2. Company received Baldrige Quality Program Bronze Award - honours organizations that make significant progress in quality and productivity.

Marko Wolf-Pany’s Specialties:

Thoroughly familiar with the application of international systems / software engineering frameworks / standards, (SEI CMMs, PMI PMBoK, and IEEE), to software development and maintenance, quality assurance, systems engineering management, and Business Continuity Planning (BCP) / Disaster Recovery Planning (DRP).
