Roland Benson’s Summary

Founded in 1985, Benson Design Associates is a San Antonio based firm that specializes in message-driven marketing and design. BDA has an unique talent for expressing a company’s image and focus. BDA works with a wide range of companies. Our mission is to help your company grow. That is our number one reason for being in business. We achieve this by looking at your product and services the way your customers do, seeking an understanding of your business as it is seen by the people that you are trying to reach. Armed with this knowledge we begin creating ideas, markets and new business. For more information on our company and services visit, www.bensondesign.com. We look forward to working on future projects with you. Thank you for taking time to read our profile.

Roland Benson’s Education

  • School of Experience

    Graphic Design 1985 — 2009

    I founded BDA in 1985 as a graphic design firm. I started the company because a new addition to my family life made me feel the time was right. When my wife, Tami, brought our first son home from the hospital, I thought it was a good time to start the company. Like most entrepreneurs, I paid my dues in an effort to make our business a success. Besides using the family's one-bedroom efficiency apartment as my office, I also took a night job refinishing furniture.

    Not long after I started my business, a member of the church gave me a four-color advertisement he needed in a few days. I didn't know anything about color separation so went to some print shops for help. I continued to build the business and in 1989 moved into a larger office with two artists. One of the artists bought a small Macintosh computer and we began using it for all of our work. We pretty much learned as we went along. We produced brochures, logos and magazine ads. That's how it all started.
