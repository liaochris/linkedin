Bret Cohen’s Summary

My goal is to take my background in philosophy, computer programming studies and languages, add more computer science and linguistics and eventually become a computational linguist. Toward this end, I am now taking courses to fill in gaps in my computer science background.

I have served in very varied capacities, including: as a tutor (ESL, writing, math), translator (from Spanish and Portuguese), software tester, technical writer...Originally with an MA in philosophy, I later got a technical degree in computer information systems and then did a specialization in Java programming, including a final software development project. I see my greatest strengths to be in the areas of language, writing, research and analysis. I hope to eventually get involved in R & D in cognitive science/artificial intelligence/computational linguistics, and am prepared to return to further graduate studies in these areas.

Bret Cohen’s Specialties:

I am trilingual (English, Spanish, Portuguese), and can help with translation (preferably into English) and interpretation. I also have a sufficient background in software development, particularly in Java to do some programming and to serve as a technical writer, or to do web site translation. I could quickly pick up other software localization skills as well. I also enjoy tutoring English, philosophy and other subjects.
