Mustafa Miah’s Summary

My career goal is to help recruit for the best companies in the SAP industry who are looking to find solutions to their staffing needs as well as assist freelance SAP consultants in choosing the right clients to service their skills, experience & knowledge.

Having come from a customer service/sales background I know firsthand that providing an excellent and quality-assured service is the upmost of importance to both customers and clients in any field because creating and leaving a lasting impression to your consumer goes a long way.

The overall outlook to my career is not to have a "make money fast and run" mentality, (and there are a lot of "cowboys" in the recruitment industry with that mindset) but to make, build, develop and expand business relationships, whilst enjoying every aspect of the entire recruitment process.

I feel that if you do not have this emphasis in mind, then you really shouldn't be in this industry, as I am a big believer in that people "buy into" people and not just the service that you are delivering.
