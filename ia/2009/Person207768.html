Sameer Ramchandani’s Summary

Sameer Ramchandani is a senior technology executive with nearly two decades of experience solving complex business problems. Sameer has successfully delivered cost-effective business solutions both in the private and public sectors and across several U.S. Federal Government agencies and Non-Profit Enterprises. His focus is on providing end-user centric solutions to enhance better information collaboration, social networking and making relevant data and information available ubiquitously to the end user.

Sameer Ramchandani’s Specialties:

Web 2.0 Technologies as well as extensive experience implementing Best Practices for the Governance of complex enterprise platforms and technology solutions.

Mobile 2.0 Technologies, including mobile social media and networking solutions, mobile web design and mobile internet solutions.

Project and Program Management. Implementation of enterprise PMOs. Extensive Financial Services industry, Federal Government and Private Sector consulting experience.
