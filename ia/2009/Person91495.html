Jeff Collins’s Summary

As your Haberdasher, Jeff Collins comes to you to save you time and money. A thirty minute appointment at your home or office can eliminate 6 to 12 months of shopping in stores.

You, as a business leader, may be an expert in business, but Jeff Collins is an expert on professional business dress.

Jeff Collins' consulting services will help you and you wardrobe stay at peak performance by:
* Organizing and categorizing your wardrobe
* Eliminating clothing that does not fit or is out of style
* Using existing wardrobe pieces to create new outfits
* Suggesting new items to provide a robust wardrobe for the up coming season
* Creating a consistent closet flow by helping organize your closet
* Training for business and personal travel packing
* Developing a wardrobe plan to help you get the most out of what you already own and make sure every piece counts

MyHaberdasher is dedicated to work with you to create a manageable wardrobe that always makes you appear your best for any occasion, effortlessly!

MyHaberdasher fits into your schedule, at a location convenient for you. Convenience and personal service at prices comparable to major men's retailers. ...without the parking and the crowds! Your Haberdasher is available at your home or office, wherever you are most comfortable.

MyHaberdasher saves valuable time by providing you professional, personal assistance in creating a wardrobe that makes your own unique statement.
- 10 years experience
- Up to date on clothing styles and design from contemporary to traditional
- Handles wide range of clothing, conservative, fashion forward or somewhere inbetween
- Fully versed in custom as well as ready made clothing

Jeff Collins’s Specialties:

Personal service, specializing in coming directly to my customers in their office or home.

- Clients throughout the US, with a focus on the West Coast.
