Holly Anthony’s Summary

With fourteen years experience as an executive/administrative assistant, I started my own business as a Virtual Assistant (VA). My goal as a VA is to collaborate with business owners and analyze their administrative support needs. Based on those needs and with the help of modern technology, I am able to provide professional and efficient administrative support to clients from my office. A collaborative partnership relieves business owners of their agonizing administrative responsibilities and eliminates the expense and hassles of hiring and maintaining office staff.

As a Virtual Assistant, my responsibilities consist of generating professional correspondence, including business letters, newsletters, and presentations while meeting the deadlines established by my clients. In addition, I provide services such as calendar management, database management, Internet research, invoicing, travel planning, and desktop publishing. Since I assist small business owners from my office, effective communication skills, attention to detail, and self-discipline play a pivotal role in establishing and maintaining successful partnerships.
