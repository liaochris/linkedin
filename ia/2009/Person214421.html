Anders Karlsson’s Summary

I started programming back in 1977 and funny enough still enjoy it very much, its awesome to have an occupation were you never really will know everything there is to know, there is always something new to learn.

My specialty is technical related (MS) Windows programming (C++) which I started with sometime in the middle of the 90s. I've done/worked on hundreds of programs throughout the years both customer specific projects and part of larger products. I have also experience in working with distributed / outsourced development.

My strength is in hands-on positions which involve both team leading and doing development work since I find it is important not to loose touch of the technology while leading other developers.

I am used to a fast-paced multi-cultural environment and enjoy working in a group or independent.
