Bert Plat’s Summary

I play two games in this life: that of GTD Trainer & Coach, and that of IT Architect. Sometimes the one or the other takes precedence, but on the whole they balance each other out.

GTD Trainer & Coach: Peace of Mind. Traction in Life.

Productivity is directly related to your ability to relax. And, conversely, your ability to relax is related to how productive you think you are. The Getting Things Done methodology can help you do both.

I offer courses (or rather, group coaching) in Dutch and English in The Netherlands to help you master the GTD methodology. I also offer coaching and telecoaching for individuals.

For more information and articles, see www.bertplat.net



IT architect:
Experienced IT architect, specialised in IT infra but with a broad knowledge outside that field. Excellent at building bridges between individuals, organisations and IT. Analytical, engaged, responsible, inventive. Passionate about attaining goals, but level-headed and pragmatical about the path and method.

Key phrases: Architect is a verb -- All progress depends on the unreasonable man -- Reason within reason.

Looking for: roles and jobs within the triangle of humans, organisations and IT, where the challenge is to bring these together to their mutual benefit, within organisations and projects where quality is more than a buzz word.

For more information see www.bertplat.net/it
