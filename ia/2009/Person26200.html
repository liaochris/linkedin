Benjamin Fine’s Summary

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of California, Berkeley and my research adviser is Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka. My major field of study is controls with minors in dynamics and digital systems. I chose these minors because I am very interested in mechatronic systems and want to have the capability to work with both the mechanical and electrical systems. I have research experience in system modeling, control design, hardware design (especially digital hardware), and implementation.
