Steven Lurie’s Summary

Currently, I'm CEO of Advice Company, a leader in providing information and services over the Internet since 1995 (through sites that include www.freeadvice.com, www.attorneypages.com and www.expertpages.com).

Previously, I was an Entrepreneur in Residence at a venture capital firm in San Francisco (MHS Capital). In that role, I helped a range of companies and evaluated opportunities.

My background is in high-tech venture backed start-ups (6+ yrs in several management roles in product and business development) and at Microsoft (6+ years before then).

At Ingenio, I led a variety of teams including product and operations. Ingenio was a venture-backed start-up that was extremely profitable and growing rapidly. In 2007, AT&T's YellowPages.com acquired Ingenio. Ingenio's suite of solutions, including the industry-leading Pay Per Call® Advertising Network, Ingenio Live!Advice, Keen and Ether seamlessly integrate the Web and the phone to help businesses large and small acquire new customers and grow revenue.

At Microsoft, I drove product marketing for a variety of products including the first versions of Site Server and MSN.

I have a BA from Stanford and a JD from Harvard.

Steven Lurie’s Specialties:

Marketing, product management, team building and general management
