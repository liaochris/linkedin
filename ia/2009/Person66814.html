David Portabella Clotet’s Summary

DB4ALL is a Start-Up at the Database Laboratory at EPFL funded by Innogrant. We are experts on decision support systems and database collection, integration and cleaning.

Our objective is to promote the use of datasets to users and companies for making more informed decisions. By datasets we refer to the content (list of books, movies, restaurants, jobs, biochemical products, zip codes...).

Here there are some news from DB4ALL:

Web information extraction service for enterprises:
We extract information from the website you need, and we put it in a spreadsheet or database.
http://db4all.com/services.php
http://db4all.com/databases/
do you know some company that could be interested? we offer you a commission!! :)

Webminer open-source, closed-beta:
Webminer is the tool we use to provide the extraction service.
It's open source with a dual license model. If you or your IT team would like to participate to the closed-beta, that would be great!
you'd need to download the software, read the tutorial (1-2 hour), extract data from your favorite website,
and the most important: ask questions in the forum. (we really need to have some activity in the forums before opening to the public)

Training events:
Several training events are organized, both big picture and technical levels.
http://db4all.com/events/

Otherwise, just take a look at our website: http://db4all.com/
Your feedback is welcome!
