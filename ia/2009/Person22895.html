Avram Primack’s Summary

Broadly trained environmental scientist and GIS analyst with experience in botany, wetlands, hydrology, stream and marine invertebrate zoology, education, ecology, watershed management. Experience teaching at undergraduate and graduate level in environmental science, GIS, ecology. Experience as a consultant for state and government agencies in land use and land cover change, human impact analysis. GIS experience in mapping, managing large datasets, landscape analysis. Overseas experience in Haiti and the Philippines (as a Peace Corps volunteer). Goals: field science or project management using GIS as an analytic tool in a domestic or overseas situation.
