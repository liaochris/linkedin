Ken Ghata ken.ghata@terrapinn.com’s Summary

Ken Ghata is CEO of Terrapinn Financial Training

Ken joined Terrapinn way back in 1991 after beginning his career in financial markets. After a couple of years in Sydney, he helped launch the UK office and produced Terrapinn's first conference in London.

He moved to Asia later that year, initially to Singapore and then to Hong Kong for a further 8 years. Up until 1998, Ken served as Director of Euromoney Institutional Investor's Asia-Pacific training and conference businesses, based in Hong Kong and Singapore. Euromoney was a major shareholder of Terrapinn at this time.

Having caught the entrepreneurial bug in HK, Ken founded Wall Street Consulting and then merged it with Terrapinn Financial Training in mid 2004, starting all over again in Singapore!

Ken is currently based in London where he has been since mid 2006.
