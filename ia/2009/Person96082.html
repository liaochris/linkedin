James Sims, MBA SPHR’s Summary

I believe that providing human resources solutions through consistency and effectiveness earns the respect of your peers while building a stronger understanding of the business. I am working toward the VP/Director level in Human Resources by following these "rules". Helping to develop, motivate, and maximize the abilities of human capital is a growing endeavor for most successful companies. These three areas are most of what I focus my resources towards. I am basically your proactive, energetic, driven HR person. I am also an employee advocate until it is detrimental to the business.
