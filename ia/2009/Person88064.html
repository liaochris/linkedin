Gohar Sargsyan’s Summary

Dr Gohar Sargsyan is an experienced programme manager and business management consultant in international multidiscipline environments both in public as well as private sectors. She has a background in change management, international cooperation, governance, risk, compliance (GRC) and financial control. She holds an MBA degree and an MS degree in Computer Information Sciences from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), she also accopmlished her PhD in Informatics.

Started from 2002 Gohar is an EU appointed expert for European Commission (EC) programmes. She carried out consulting, coordination, technical, managerial activities at Management Support Unit at EC DG INFSO in 2004-2005. The last few years she was a programme director of the EU projects, in some of these projects having coordination role.
