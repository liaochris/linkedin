Joe Burns’s Summary

Joe Burns is a Principal of Sanford Rose Associates – Philadelphia. Founded in 1959, Sanford Rose Associates serves the needs of corporate and institutional clients from over sixty offices in North America, Europe and Asia. Rather than recruiting generalists, each Sanford Rose office specializes in a particular industry or profession and conducts its searches on a national and international basis.

In addition to his search experience, Joe has 20 years direct experience in the Chemical industry.

Joe is an active member in the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE), NPCA, SOCMA, and other professional organizations.

I am an open networker on LinkedIn. Please send me an invite at jfburns@sanfordrose.com
