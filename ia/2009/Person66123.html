Doris J. Gilman’s Summary

I am a Open Networker and willing to share contacts and ideas. Contact me to discuss business opportunities at:

bseenbheard@live.com

Through Be Seen. Be Heard., I help healthcare thought leaders express and find channels for their ideas, core capabilities, and solutions.

My thought leadership practice in healthcare specializes in the use of one-to-one media: corporate speakers programs for customer outreach, Internet and intranet external and internal communications; trade shows, seminars/Webinars, podcasting, blogs, and branded events. I have a broad, multi-industry background, with the last 15 years serving U. S. healthcare businesses in various industry segments: manufacturers, providers, and payers.

I’ve advised businesses in medical advertising and public relations, healthcare information, medical devices, not-for-profits, and provider organizations specializing in oncology, substance abuse rehabilitation, and behavioral health. I’ve worked with scores of industry and professional associations to make “win-win” speaker placements or alliances on behalf of private clients.

People with whom I am interesting in connecting:

Business and professional decision makers (CEOs, CXOs, heads of marketing and communications, medical and scientific leaders) from the life sciences (pharma, biotech, medical devices), as well as healthcare provider organizations, industry associations, professional societies, and advocacy groups looking to maximize their own or their organization's thought leadership.

What I can do for you:

• Build speaker programs and bureaus.

• Conduct speaker placements.

• Energize conference, tradeshow and exhibit participation.

• Reflect thought leadership on your Website, through blogging, podcasting.

• Connect with employees on your intranet.

• Distribute communications through systematic, opt-in ways.

Doris J. Gilman’s Specialties:

• Total project management, from formulating strategic and creative concepts through distribution and tracking results.
