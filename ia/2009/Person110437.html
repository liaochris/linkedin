Jeremy Broekman’s Summary

I founded BROEKMAN communications as a vehicle to professionalize companies with savvy studio branding tactics, corporate marketing & pr initiatives, visionary creative services, and solid business development tools for companies looking to go to the next level.

With a foundation in sales and marketing from Ben & Jerrys, Xerox and Universal Studios Television, I managed a successful integration of full service PR agency services into my repertoire. Since 2003, I built a successful track record of entertainment & corporate publicity, media relations, talent management and PR with such clients as Storyopolis, the Screen Actors Guild Foundation, Columbia Pictures, DreamWorks Animation, Universal Pictures, J. Paul Getty Center, ABC Television, First Americans of the Arts, International Student Film Festival, and the North Hollywood Theatre & Arts Festival.

Over the last four years, I've been hired as either a (*1) chief marketing officer, (*2) marketing director, (*3) creative director, (*4) PR director, (*5) web developer, or (*6) graphic designer to build successful brands, to package/relaunch/advertise companies, to market products, to publicize/promote events, and to make web 2.0 / technology / online marketing a reality for those on a shoestring budget. Bottom line, I am a connector and a bridgemaker and, we at BROEKMAN communications make things happen. cell 818-212-9201 or email jeremy@broekmancomm.com

Jeremy Broekman’s Specialties:

brand management & marketing, advertising & promotions, corporate communications, public & media relations, identity/anniversary campaigns, event production & corporate sponsorship, start up consulting, business plan development, strategic planning, creative services - design for print & web, print production, web development, email/viral campaigns, search engine optimization, social media optimization, web 2.0
