    (Internet industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Povo offers local businesses a one-stop shop to manage, monitor, and improve their on-line presence. Most local businesses find that managing their online business profile takes too much time, that few can find their company on Google, that there is limited info about their business on the web, and that they need help marketing online. Povo uses a variety of tools and techniques to demystify and simplify "getting found" on-line. Povo sells subscription based management and monitoring tools, and an array of VAS.

    For consumers, povo.com is a collaborative online guide that offers Consumers, Service Providers and Advertisers a more reliable, comprehensive, and well organized source of street level local information.
