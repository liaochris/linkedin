Sridhar Muppidi’s Experience

  • CEO & Founder

    PurpleTalk, Inc

    (Telecommunications industry)

    April 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    My newest exciting venture is Purple Talk (www.purpletalk.com). It is conceived as a single provider of all major Apple’s App Store solutions for both iPhone users and developers.

    To that end, we provide a cross promotion engine with advanced analytics and advertising options. We also have an exclusive-to-iPhone gaming network open for all developers. The service, first of its kind for iPhone, is free, and helps iPhone developers promote their apps while increasing the shelf life of their apps & games.

    Our iPhone app development strategy is three fold: We release paid casual games every month, which sees high downloads rate thanks to some consistencies in implementation and of course these games are really fun to play.

    On the apps side we have been working on a few stealth mode application shells due to be released with iPhone OS 3.0, these apps will allow individuals and organizations to publish content through us and we sell their content using in-app purchases and share royalty with them, watch this space for more information on them soon.

    We also release free standalone apps regularly for the iPhone, which see high repeat usage and are supported by ads.

    Apart from that we provide software & game development consulting for the iPhone platform (www.xcubelabs.com). We work with iPhone developers as well as people entirely new to the platform. Our expertise in usability allows us to help clients bring their ideas to iPhone in the best possible way.

  • Chairman & Founder

    Education for Free Research Center

    (E-Learning industry)

    June 2007 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    We started Education For Free (www.educationforfree.org) to solve some of the problems faced by public schools in India. Using our Virtual Classroom technology, educators can teach in schools from anywhere in the world, thus enabling children to learn from the best. We also provide other facilities like a platform for collaboration among teachers, learning and teaching resource repository, trainings and much more. It is a nonprofit charitable organization based in Hyderabad, India. www.educationforfree.org would have more information on the schools we are deployed in so far.

  • CTO & Co-Founder

    PanTerra Networks

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    September 2001 — April 2007 (5 years 8 months)

    We started PanTerra Networks (www.panterranetworks.com) to create a unique technology that would address the entire range of communications needs of a small or medium sized business. For instance, advanced telephony and collaboration features are not within the reach of SMBs and PanTerra’s mission was to create a platform that seamlessly integrates all communication aspects for a small business conveniently and affordably. Today, PanTerra is an established on-demand, unified communications service provider, making a difference for a large number of SMBs.
