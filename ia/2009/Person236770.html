Tony Eaton’s Summary

I am a seasoned professional with over 11 years experience in print, web design and interactive advertising. I have lead campaigns for diverse clients such as General Mills, British Airways, The Discovery Channel, Magic Johnson Enterprises/Aetna, Subaru and 3M. I enjoy leading the creative and strategic direction of multiple projects for various clients. I also take great pleasure in being a part of my team's professional development.
