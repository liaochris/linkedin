Wayne L Symons’s Summary

Project Manager, who is SC cleared and a Prince2 Practitioner, having delivered large scale, high profile and complex applications development solutions, to every stage on both internal and client facing capacities, across the Public Health, Pharmaceutical, Financial Services and Manufacturing sectors, within a number of multi-cultural environments in the UK, Central Europe and the United States.

Has worked with disparate and disciplined teams which have included offshore and 3rd party suppliers. Performed stakeholder management duties both internally and externally whilst being the sole point of contact and responsibility up to CxO level.

Experiences span midrange and open source technologies, utilising formal project management methodologies.
