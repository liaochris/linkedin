Joe Lorenz RN’s Summary

I'm a seasoned RN that has kept my California license current and up to date. I have had both a passion for being a nurse as well as being a fitness trainer. Both jobs are so rewarding and similar because in both you help people achieve a goal. In nursing the goal is health. In personal training the goal is fitness.

I own and run a personal training business and have loved every moment of it but at this juncture in my life I want to get back to my nursing career and either resume some of my previous specialties or I'm open the door to learning and exploring new directions.

I own a home here in San Diego so I'm not looking to relocate but I am open to a job that might involve some travel.
