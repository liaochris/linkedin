Erica Root Cikanek’s Summary

Strategic Marketing Consultant with years of executive marketing experience in General Management and Director Roles for Fortune 400 to small company ($25MM to $250MM in revenue). My clients value increased speed to market for new product or new market entry and resolution of profitability issues due to product, price or other marketing and sales concerns. Developed and proven new channels, implemented new marketing and sales management strategies and systems, and commercialized over $45MM in new product sales. Applying key financial assessments prove effective in nearly every marketing situation.
