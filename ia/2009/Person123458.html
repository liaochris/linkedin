Julia Kline’s Summary

My company, MakeARealOffer.com, matches buyers and sellers of real estate online. We design online advertisements that are like nothing you've ever seen before! Trust us. You've NEVER seen a piece of real estate advertised the way we do it. Plus, they're not just ads. They actually SELL the real estate online!

As a seller, you start by placing a basic ad on our site for a very low fee. To increase your property's saleability, you can then add various elements to your listing - videos, more photos, articles about the latest real estate news in your area. Other people can also make comments on your listing - letting people know they've seen the house, or they know something about the area. Then you post the price - 99 cents!!

When buyers come to the site, they search listings and when they see something that looks interesting they Make A Real Offer. They fill out a simple form, including the price they would be willing to pay. All these offers are then automatically posted on the listing, so that future buyers can see what offer they have to beat in order to get the Seller interested.

Our system is NOT an auction. Neither the buyers nor the sellers are legally obligated by anything that happens online. Instead, they're free to start a conversation with each other, and see what happens ... We like to say it's a dating website for real estate!
