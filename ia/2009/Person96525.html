    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    In the last one hundred years a great deal of research has been done on the internal drivers and experiences of human beings. JOB Consultancy has translated the outcome of this work into practical psychometric tools, such as CADT®, for the commercial environment. JOB Consultancy provides comprehensive training and ongoing support in the use of the CADT® tool.

    JOB Consultancy focuses specifically on the delivery of courses, training and interventions. The characteristic approach is from an experience psychology perspective, a practice that is distinguished by offering experience and reflection, within a context that refers to what the analytical psychology offers us. Based on the notions of CG Jung and other inspirational thinkers and innovators, JOB Consultancy offers support and direction to people on their path to self discovery.
