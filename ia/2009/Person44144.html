Christal Pomeroy’s Summary

I have been an Information Architect in IBM Canada for the Rational Software Architect and Rational Software Modeler desktop products, and later the Rational Systems Developer. Recently, I have become the Information Architect for Rational governance products, such as Rational Portfolio Manager, Requisite Pro and upcoming products.

My key interests today are improving cross-product solutions-level documentation and navigation and in encouraging improved information architecture through the improvement and promotion of our tooling.

I also participate in IBM Rational brand-level initiatives and IBM corporate workgroups and initiatives to promote improvements in usability, solutions documentation, and standards and tools development.

Christal Pomeroy’s Specialties:

Eclipse help system, Eclipse welcome, DITA, on-line help design, technical writing, technical writing guidelines and process, Task Modeler.

Christal Pomeroy’s Experience

  • Information Architect

    IBM Canada

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Computer Software industry)

    December 2003 — Present (5 years 6 months)

    I was the IA for the Rational Software Architect, Rational Software Modeler, and Rational Systems Developer products from December 2003 until December 2006. In this role I not only designed the online help systems and welcome experience, but also developed the high-level plans for tutorials, samples, and developed the contents for the welcome experience and the infrastructure and product overview topics for the help system, which was completely in DITA. These were some of the first products in IBM to adopt DITA and IBM tooling for DITA. At this time, I also helped to develop user assistance strategy and lay down templates and examples that drove other IBM Rational product information architecture development.

    Between product releases, I consulted on behalf of IBM with a large external corporation on setting up early adoption teams for DITA, DITA tooling, and IA strategy.

    I have now moved into the role of IA for IBM governance and enterprise portfolio management tooling.
