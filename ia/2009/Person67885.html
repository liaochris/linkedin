  • President & Co-founder

    Prajha Consulting

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    July 2008 — Present (10 months)

    Prajha Consulting is a global services firm with a single goal: to partner with companies in a spirit of co-creation and collaboration to help them evolve and grow in today's digital world. We help companies build great products and brands by creating engaging experiences for customers and users wherever they live in the digital world. Our two service groups, consulting and marketing agency services, help advance companies in the mission critical areas of product development and customer acquisition.

    We have global offices in three great cities - Delhi, Mumbai and San Francisco. This allows us to take advantage of offshore centers of excellence to lower the product development and customer acquisition costs for companies while maintaining the highest standards of product quality and delivery so that our clients are able to achieve sustainable results.
