Sunny Shum’s Summary

I am currently enjoying a professional career as a Footwear Buyer for a major National Canadian Footwear retailer, Sterling Shoes LP. There is no doubt my immense love for shoes and passion for the fashion industry has guided me through my successful and diverse career path.

My foray into the fashion industry began when I got a jump start to my career by working for Aritzia, a National Canadian Fashion Retailer, as a Visual Merchandiser. After 4 years, this launched me into my next opportunity, managing prestigious European denim brands such as Miss Sixty, Energie, Killah and Sixty for Western Canada and also working with footwear brands like, Diesel, Ed Hardy, Faryl Robin and Le Coq Sportif.

While continuing my full time career gigs, I concurrently made the time to produce fashion shows, provide marketing services for fashion related events as well as managing and contributing to my on-line magazine, partlySunny.ca. I quickly became the “go to” person for my network of peers and colleagues for industry insider’s tips on anything to do with fashion and lifestyle.

I'm a well rounded, mature individual that has had the opportunity to work at different levels and facets of the apparel and footwear business.
