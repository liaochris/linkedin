Jennifer Gold’s Summary

Entrepreneurial, dynamic and creative individual with 14 years of hands-on and management experience in new media, e-commerce, travel and publishing industries. Strong track record of joining innovative companies and identifying opportunities for using technology and pioneering strategies to meet company-wide goals.

Areas of expertise include: business development and implementation of new media strategies and projects for all kinds of companies; website development; digital content conversion and licensing; project management; creation of tools and systems to manage and distribute all forms of digital media; online marketing and sales.

Seeking opportunities to help companies formulate and implement new media strategies and projects to help grow their business and brand.
