Kenton Williston’s Summary

Kenton Williston is that rarest of creatures: an electrical engineer who loves to write! He has a decade of experience covering DSPs, FPGAs, ARM microcontrollers, and embedded Intel processors. His experience ranges from breaking semiconductor news stories to technical analysis of processor architecture.

Kenton has served as editor of numerous publications including the DSP DesignLine web site, Intel's Embedded Innovator magazine, and BDTI's "Buyer's Guide to DSP Processors." Kenton is also editor of the book "Digital Signal Processing: World Class Designs."

Kenton's areas of expertise include telecommunications, mobile handsets, base stations, VoIP, audio, video, and surveillance. He also has a strong interest in green technology.
