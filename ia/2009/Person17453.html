Ann Phelan’s Summary

My professional life is as an educational counselor. I have been a special education chair, have written curriculum on diversity and tolerance and have received national attention for my work on abduction prevention (featured on 48 Hours). I have had many positions in social work and counseling since 1983.

Academically I seek a consultant position for instruction in the schools around abduction and abuse prevention. I would like to continue Tech Prep Support in Cape Cod to schools.

As an event promoter I have run 8 professional sporting events. My role was marketing and PR, Event planning and sponsorship. I brought the first professional windsurfing event to the Caribbean Island of Bonaire in 2002. Currently I handle PR and hospitality for IFCA Bonaire Slalom Worlds and Pro Kids.

In 1988 I started Antigua Small Properties, a US based villa rental company. It grew to Caribbean Wind & Sun Vacations in the early 90's specializing in leisure travel. I have run travel groups specializing including location management (Grey Advertising/Antigua shoot), eco tours (Antigua/Bonaire/Montserrat) as well as corporate travel. Each year I am the travel specialist for the Canadian Medical Group that attends the March ABK Windsurf Clinic in Bonaire. I run an annual Singles Month for divers and windsurfers and Women's Windsurf Clinics. My services include Caribbean Destination Management and Concierge.

My travel goals are to continuing running events in the Caribbean and US. I seek a company that I may represent for online reservations and PR.
