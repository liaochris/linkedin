Blake Stephenson MSI’s Summary

After being called to the English Bar in 2007 I have specialised in financial regulatory work. To date I have developed skills in compliance monitoring, trade surveillance and policy work in wholesale banking and European equity exchange environments.

Blake Stephenson MSI’s Specialties:

Knowledge of European cash equity market and the prevailing market abuse regime; providing interpretations of various FSA regulations in relation to exchanges and equity markets; and experience from Bar School of legal drafting, negotiating, and conducting client meetings.

    January 2008 — May 2009 (1 year 5 months)

    Monitoring trading activity and relating it to current market events, practices and trends to identify possible incidences of market abuse, market manipulation, insider dealing, and exchange rule breaches conducted on, or in relation to the exchange and draft notifications to the FSA.
