Karen Roberts’s Summary

I am currently Head of Department within T-Mobile UK & have broad senior management experience. I am a results driven leader, with the capacity to set medium-long term strategy & embed business plans, whilst maintaining an eye for detail when required. As well as holding various management positions, I enjoy project work; most recently playing a key part in the off shoring of various finance functions to India. I take an active interest in the wider company & sit on numerous senior management steering groups including Corporate Responsibility.

During my most recent role I was responsible for merging 3 depts across Finance & Procurement into one Procurement Operations function; providing operational support to the delivery of the procurement cycle. Managing 20 staff & owning the S-Ox Purchasing stream across T-Mobile. Leading & supporting large companywide projects e.g. finance off shoring. Achievements: gained sourcing category focus (by implementing a Strategic Category Review programme), implemented a competency-based tool to drive Procurement development & streamline the number of Procurement roles, ensured S-Ox compliance across the Purchasing stream & removed all system role conflicts across T-Mobile, claimed £11m prompt payment discount. Played a key part in a site-share project with H3G & currently playing an active role to offshore all Finance back-office activity to India (a key member of the vendor evaluation team, including site visits to India - currently leading the inbound due diligence phase) - project due for completion Q2 2009.

Prior to this I achieved:- reduced headcount by 30%, retrieved £1.5m from AP audit, set Working Capital strategy, raised profile of the dept, delivered against challenging KPI’s, extended e-procurement strategy, set & implemented e-invoicing strategy, significantly improved against all KPI’s.
