Alok Garg’s Summary

Alok Garg is an executive with proven success and entrepreneurial leadership in growing businesses for information technology and solutions in the Enterprise Software and ICT Industries.

As a business leader with British Telecom since May'07, Alok has been responsible for change management and bringing focus on large transformation engagements, taking full advantage of BT's best practices.

Alok incubated healthcare and mobile VAS ventures while collaborating in parallel with several ventures capital and Private Equity firms in India and US.

Previously, during his long international career with Hewlett-Packard, he has held responsibilities in marketing, consulting & integration, off-shore technology development and strategy. He started his career in 1989 in India as an r&d engineer. Garg relocated to US in 1994, where he worked as Sr. Consultant for Telecom clients with Cap Gemini. He then joined Verifone as an Architect for e-commerce solutions for financial and retail customers and held business and product management roles, which included strategic alliances, technology availability on multiple platforms and profit and loss management.

After Verifone was acquired by HP in 1997, Alok held various global business management roles at HP, which included leading strategy development for enterprise business, new business and solution incubation in telecom sector, integration and leading strategic consulting practice globally and in a region.

Alok was the Chief Architect for HP's Adaptive Enterprise strategy and responsible for managing portfolio of solutions and incubating new offerings.

His latest appointment in HP was Managing Principal of Strategic Consulting Practice in Asia Pacific and Japan.

Garg contributed to the integration of the merger between HP and Compaq, during 2003. He led the integration of all technology assets from HP and Compaq specific to the IT Infrastructure Solutions into one cohesive strategy and portfolio of offerings.

Alok Garg’s Specialties:

Alok Garg has been successful in growing business and developing IT and network solutions for the Communication and Media industry and IT Transformation domain. He held responsibilities in marketing, consulting & integration, business development, off-shore technology development and strategy creation. Garg is experienced in large scale mergers and acquisitions having contributed to Verifone, Bluestone and Compaq integrations with hp.
