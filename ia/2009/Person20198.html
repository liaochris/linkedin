Sarah Arrow’s Summary

To grow Arrow Light Haulage into a leading UK ethical courier company, we are well on the way!

Arrow Light Haulage is a professional Essex same day courier and delivery company. We collect and deliver time critical goods and deliver them anywhere in the UK. We are part the UKs biggest transport network, so even though we are based in Essex we have the resources to collect from anywhere within 60 minutes or 30 minutes (premium service). Our drivers are polite and well trained, they know your reputation is in their hands.

We do the job right the first time, so the need for re-deliveries at your expense comes with using a cowboy not a professional courier. You get what you pay for, next time you get a cheap quote for a courier you need to ask why its so cheap...do they pay their drivers at the least minimum wage? Are they insured? Do they have the correct equipment to complete your delivery? Are their drivers helpful, clean and polite? Quite often you won't find out until something has gone wrong and then its too late.

Call us on 0844 884 3331 or email sarah@arrowlighthaulage.co.uk

Arrow Light Haulage are especially pleased to be involved with Business Scene in Essex. We will be looking to promote events across the region, and encourage and grow business to business/customer networking.

If you are a networker, or new to networking in Essex, take a look at www.Business-Scene.com and drop me a message to tell me what you think.
