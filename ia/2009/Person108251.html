Jeff Miller’s Summary

With over 15 years working in the music business, Jeff brings a vast array of talents to the table. Jeff’s varied roles have included merchandising sales for Widespread Panic, managing over 90 art and food vendors for the Austin City Limits Music Festival, and he is also a highly sought after concert poster artist. His fanatical love for live music and energetic work ethic are well known by anyone who has worked with him. His drive for a successful event is evident by the countless hours of thoughtful planning and insight that can only come from someone with his extensive experience. Jeff’ other projects include Event Production Services (Owner & Manager), Cryptical Development Graphic Art & Design (Owner & Artist), and the Austin City Limits Music Festival (Art & Food Manager.
