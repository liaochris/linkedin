Laurence Greenberg’s Summary

Laurence P. Greenberg, President and CEO of Jefferson National Life Insurance Company, is a seasoned executive in financial services and retail banking. Throughout his career, he has made a commitment to creating greater consumer value to help Americans save more. Under his leadership, Jefferson National is now widely recognized for launching Monument Advisor, the first VA with a flat-insurance fee.

Previously, Mr. Greenberg served as First VP of Consumer Banking at Merrill Lynch. Prior to that, Mr. Greenberg served as COO of TeleBank, now E*Trade Bank, where he oversaw all marketing and operations and was instrumental in driving the bank's deposit growth from $200 million in 1994 to more than $8 billion in 2000.

As a thought-leader and authority on retirement investing, Mr. Greenberg has authored numerous articles on the subject, is frequently called upon by the media, and has been quoted in the press by Reuters, the Wall Street Journal and the Washington Post, as well as a wide range of financial publications including Financial Planning, Financial Advisor, On Wall Street, Registered Rep Magazine, Retirement Income Reporter and Senior Market Advisor.

Jefferson National is a leading innovator of retirement products for fee-based and fee-only advisors and the clients they serve. We believe that simple, low-cost variable annuities should be considered as a part of every retirement portfolio. We’ve made it our mission to help Americans save more.

Jefferson National Life Insurance Company serves 50,000 customers nationwide, and is domiciled in Dallas, Texas with authority in 49 states and the District of Columbia. To learn more, please visit www.jeffnat.com.
