Tanya Korpi’s Summary

Positioning, segmentation and targeting are the terms that I think in most of the time; what's the best way to motivate this target into that action? Every action has a reaction, what do you want it to be?

Marketing, advertising, public relations -- all nature of communications -- can and should be described in these simple terms. It's as complicated as you make it.

Know what you want, know who can help you, know what motivates them.

Then make it happen.
