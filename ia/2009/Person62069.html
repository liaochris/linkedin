Derek Manuel’s Summary

Substantial senior-level leadership and international human resource management experience. Track record of managing growth, reorganisations and restructurings, acquisitions and disposals, ownership and culture change with focus on talent acquisition and development, and creating organisation bench-strength, including a period of residence in North America. Commercial sector experience in financial services and high technology. Currently main-board director for Save the Children, a global non-governmental organisation providing development and humanitarian relief to children worldwide.

After a business degree Derek started his career in human resources in the financial services industry with Lloyds Bank. Subsequent roles in the engineering and high technology sectors gave a move in 1984 into the international arena, and since then he has worked for British, Canadian, American and Japanese organisations. His experience includes business acquisitions and disposals, mergers and joint ventures involving working in over 50 countries, and living in Canada for 2 years. After leaving Misys plc where he was Director of Human Resources for the global banking division, he joined the Board of Save the Children in the autumn of 2003 as Global Human Resources Director, based in London
