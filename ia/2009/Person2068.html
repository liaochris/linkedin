Albert Van Niekerk’s Summary

Developed a keen interest in 1984 in strategic planning and operational planning, in execution, and in management by objectives, while in the diplomatic service. Applied a private sector business approach in the public sector and achieve great results with motivated teams in Pretoria and abroad although no other unit in the Department of Foreign Affairs followed suite. (Said it could not be done.)

Left the diplomatic service in 1995 and pursued a new career as a business consultant with emphasis on providing total solutions to successful small and medium-sized businesses. I specialize in strategic planning and execution. My totally understand Strategy Maps, the Balanced Scorecard, and in installing Kaplan and Norton's six-stage Closed-Loop Management Systems of planning, executing, reviewing and assessing and replanning..

Through the years my focus has shifted to getting people within organisations to understand their personal missions and roles in life as they first have to find their own voices before they are truly able to assist their organisations to learn and grow, to develop strategies and to execute.

I maintain a steady portfolio of about 20 clients, many over three, four or more years.

Goals: Assisting owners of successful but under-performing organisations to move their enterprises to higher levels of effectiveness and profits, to overcome cash-flow problems, to create great working environments and to enjoy their work.

I offer a turn-key programme over 12 months, adapted to the client's specific circumstances, with the aimed-for result of increasing a company's bottom line by 20%. The programme is results-driven and progress is reviewed each month. The client is not locked in and can cancel at any time. However, any client does require time to produce internal changes and to obtain the full benefits of this programme.

Albert Van Niekerk’s Specialties:

Balanced Scorecard (Kaplan & Norton), strategy workshops of all sizes, a wide variety of clients in services and manufacturing,, parastatals, non-profits, universities, marketing, Web site content, coaching.
