Beverly Nation’s Summary

Advertising professional specializing in product branding through the power of Hollywood. Focused on helping brands and their agencies plan and execute cinema media campaigns nationwide. Expertise in digital cinema spots, lobby promotions, concession branding, box-office promotions and interactive spots utilizing SMS technology.

Beverly Nation’s Specialties:

Product branding, new product launch, entertainment marketing, movie theater advertising and promotions, digital spot creation
