    HyLo

    (Partnership; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2009 — Present (1 year )

    Based in Phoenix, Arizona, our concept and company grew from many conversations shared with business owners (like you) and media companies over the years. It became increasingly obvious that information (whether it was a deal, coupon, event or other content) is transitioning to the mobile markets and the need to get that information to individuals based on their location was key. Our goal was to remove the cost, time and complexity that so many other mobile marketing firms had created when trying to build for hyper-local environments.

    Ultimately, you will use HyLo to drive foot traffic when you need it, fill empty tables, move large quantities of inventory, or for whatever use suits your current need. HyLo is the platform that can be molded to meet the needs of your business, no matter what that business may be.
