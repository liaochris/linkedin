Marjorie Clark’s Summary

Our web sites are as diverse as the businesses and clients they promote, each crafted to meet specific needs and tastes.

Whether you are a sole proprietor or a large corporation, we guide you through the design and promotion process of your Web site, helping you to become a more Web savvy entrepreneur.

Marjorie Clark’s Experience

  • Owner

    Little Fish Studios (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Internet industry)

    April 1998 — Present (11 years 4 months)

    Little Fish Studios started as a favor to my father-in-law. As the Web developed in the last ten years, so has our skill level and portfolio.

    Simplicity guided our early designs because we (and the Internet) lacked the technical knowledge and bandwidth to make sites more complicated. However, "Don't Make Me Think," Steven Krug's book on usabilty, convinced us we were on the right track. If a search engine can't find a web site's content, what's the point. If people can't easily use a website they won't come back.

    So we still keep web sites simple, but with ten years of design experience and technical knowledge, that give them accessibility and sophistication.
