Peter Emmanuel Juan’s Summary

I have just recently left the corporate world to focus on my start up companies. One of which is a consultancy firm that leverages my expertise in web marketing and online community building as well as the talent and experience of my partners to offer comprehensive and effective new media strategies for small to medium businesses. The other venture is a web application which we believe is a refreshing take on an otherwise everyday item that focuses equally on being fun as it does on being functional.

During my time as the product manager of I.PH blogs, I've learned that a genuine understanding of people is one of the best tools anyone in the field of marketing can have. I take pride in my ability to make a connection with the people who are our target market, figuring out what's essential and compelling to them and being able to translate that into specific goals and guidelines for our development team. This results in a service that is not only attractive to the customer but is also finely tuned to their wants and needs.
