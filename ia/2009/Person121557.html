Joshua Spaulding’s Summary

For over 8 years, I have worked at increasing levels responsibility in political organizing, producing many successful outcomes. In my current and previous positions, I have worked with a wide variety of groups including LGBT and civil rights organizations, labor unions, state and local democratic party organizations, elected officials, religious organizations and many others to work together on legislative and electoral campaigns.

Throughout my political career, I have developed communication skills essential to any organization. I have communicated with local party organizations to engage their activists to volunteer, and, during the Iowa Caucuses, to persuade them to support Governor Dean’s presidential candidacy. My organizing experience has helped me develop the skill to translate a message into resonant person-to-person communication.

Additionally, I have developed and implemented intensive voter contact programs, including an activist-based rural and urban outreach program to communicate our message and locate likely supporters.

I am continously looking towards expanding my skills in getting activists and potential activists involved in progressive and democratic politics.
