Courtnay Guimaraes’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur, executive, writer and strategy evangelist. I've been working at the ITC corporate market for the last 22 years, in the areas of Information Architecture, Corporate Applications Development, ERP/CRM/SCM Implementation, Web Marketing, Business Processes and Mobile Applications, for various kinds of computing environments. I am also a specialist in Market Strategy and Innovation Management. At i9 Consulting Group, I've supported companies with strategy consulting in Market Development and Strategic Management of Innovation.

Now, I'm in charge of Financial Services accounts at Oracle. I have taken also on managerial role at Plusoft, Link Consulting, Vivo, Yavox, Vesta, Fuego Argentina, and BRQ, as well as leading projects for large-scale companies such as Compaq, Banco Itaú, Danone, SERPRO (the Brazilian federal data processing company), Cargill, Unibanco, Telesp and Fiat. I was also the man behind the development of Vesta Technologies´s opportunities and marketing efforts for Mobile Internet, Sales Force Automation, Embedded Systems and Pervasive Computing, building international alliances with OpenWave, Hitachi Japan and Gemplus.

I also love working with the lectures industry in order to educate middle management in Brazil about possibilities of exporting software. As such, I have become one of the most requested speakers in the tech lecture circuits. I am also a board advisor at Advanstar´s Telexpo and Telexpo Wireless, IBC´s Telecommunications Division, InnovaComm and others.

I majored in Economic Sciences and hold an Executive MBA in Marketing at ESPM/SP. Now, I'm preparing a dissertation about Innovation Cycles in emerging markets and intend to attain a PhD on this topic within the next 5 years. Also, I am a guest speaker and professor at the MBA course at IBMEC/MG, on the subject of Telecommunications and at EDP programs BSP/SP, for Technology Innovation.
