Eric Lin’s Summary

As a seasoned film production and distribution manager, content developer and research analyst for various media, I have seven years of related working experience in Hong Kong and two years of film co-production experience in Beijing. My unique mix of skills and resume has proved to be a valuable asset in the current Asian film market, with growing attention towards co-productions with China and related legal and revenue models.

Eric Lin’s Specialties:

Film distribution, Market intelligence report, market research, competitive analysis, box office analysis, legal document coordination, content development, copywriting, Chinese and English translation, proposal drafting, Powerpoint presentation
