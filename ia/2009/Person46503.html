Cj Jayamaha’s Summary

As a technical architect and a web producer for a web agency, I define the appropriate technical architecture suitable for deployment of the web solutions within customer specific engagements and requirements. Making sure the solutions are within the budget and the time frame defined is essential. Many of my project solutions are predominantly based upon the above 2 factors. Furthermore striking the most optimum business goals whilst intricately balancing the time frame, budget, customer requirements, website performance and usability is my main purpose at this role.

A savvy business acumen is a vital quality that I demonstrate when providing assistance and positive stimulation to customers on their business needs. Understanding what a business requires sooner rather than later saves my employer and the clients from heavy financial setbacks.

• To act as technical authority for major technical projects assessing and approving technical proposals, and reviewing progress to ensure they meet business needs. This will include ensuring that the project meet’s business & technical requirements and is within the agreed budget and timescale. Projects may be internally resourced and via third parties.

• To specify the requirements for the technical architecture and work with suppliers to ensure they meet the project needs for efficient and robust delivery of the Internet Services to help maintain its leadership in delivering innovation.

• To ensure that the infrastructure is developed in a manner that supports delivery of services to multiple platforms, resilience, performance, accommodates external suppliers.

• To establish and maintain effective working relationships with all stakeholders. This will include technical, operational and editorial staff, internal and external staff, departments and divisions, as well as third parties clients and business managers.

• To provide technical consultancy and advice to the technical and management teams as required
