Barbara Kohn’s Summary

Barbara Kohn provides marketing and communications services to help established and emerging companies. Kohn spent many years in management positions in leading Silicon Valley public relations firms before launching her own practice in 2000

Kohn works closely with company management to help them assess the competitive landscape and develop the messaging and marketing programs to reach target constituencies. On the PR front, she has a track record for building the needed relationships among key press and analysts to help secure industry positioning. A strong writer, Kohn applies her skills to developing industry articles, web content, speeches, white papers and marketing collateral.

In addition to her industry credentials, Kohn has been called on to share her professional experience with future communications professionals. She served as an adjunct professor of public relations at San Jose State University and was a lecturer at several community colleges.
