Inge Derks’s Summary

My key driver is improving business by innovation. Innovation in products and services, marketing approach and business processes. Step by step innovation aligned with the speed of adoption of new technologies. The way our work and live changes by the internet these days is fascinating. Internet therefore plays an important part in most of my job assigments so far.

In times of pressure organizations tend to focus on their financial figures. You have to keep eyes on costs, but I truly believe a close look at our customers is the best chance for sustainable profit. So I like to work in the customer-side of the organization. My experience is in both business development and marketing.

June 2009 I started my own company. Creating products, services and websites in a way that customers experience good value is a never ending challenge, great fun and thankful to do.
