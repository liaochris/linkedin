Jonathan Rutman’s Summary

Since joining CBRE’s downtown Chicago office, Jonathan Rutman has achieved great success in representing corporate interests in complex real estate transactions. He is involved in many aspects of CBRE’s brokerage process, but has developed significant experience by advocating on behalf of office tenants.

His extensive local market knowledge, background in finance, and background in Process and Strategy consulting, gained while working for Anderson Consulting prior to CBRE, places his clients in a unique position of leverage in lease transactions and most importantly allows them to make more informed business decisions.

Last year he successfully represented 25 clients, in both national and local markets, in diverse industries, such as Finance, Law, Insurance Professional Service, and Not-For-Profits, on matters ranging from expansion and renegotiation to relocation and disposition. It is that ability of being able to translate the complex nature of a corporation’s real estate needs that secured him CBRE’s prestigious “Associate of the Year” early in his career.

Along with his national successes in the corporate world, Jonathan remains connected with the local philanthropic community, by assuming significant leadership positions with the Jewish United Fund of Metropolitan Chicago, which provides critical resources to people of all faiths in Chicago and around the world, and the Illinois Chamber of Commerce, which provides business advocacy and works to insure the economic development of the State of Illinois.

When he is not in the office or attending a community board meeting, Jonathan enjoys spending time with his wife and daughter in their home in Lincoln Park.
