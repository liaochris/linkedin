Doug Ross’s Summary

Accomplished commercial photographer with over thirty years of professional experience providing dynamic imagery for the cosmetic, corporate and scientific industries. A passionate artisan versed in traditional photography and digital capture. I bring to every assignment a superb design sensibly, rooted in a fine art background.

Doug Ross’s Specialties:

Photographic talents used in, but not limited to, advertising, trade, marketing, sales, public relations, web and package design. Noted as a proactive team player and collaborates effortlessly across the entire corporate organization. Managed and staffed in-house photo studios and fulfilled all internal corporate photographic requirements along with maintaining outside supplier relationships.
