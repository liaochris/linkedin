Leo Leung’s Summary

I help shape EMC's Cloud strategy and drive the first EMC Cloud Service built from the ground up: Atmos onLine.

My passion is seeking, defining and executing on new market opportunities. I have three years of experience in Web application and Cloud service product management; three years in strategic planning; six years in marketing communications and branding; and an MBA concentration in new product design and marketing.

My goal is to start a company focused on user-centric design and products. Let's make things easier to use!
