Brian Ussery’s Summary

Brian E. Ussery is Director of SEO at Search Discovery, a full service search engine marketing, optimization and analytics agency in Atlanta, GA.

Recognized by Google and Adobe as an authority on issues related to SEO for Flash, Brian is considered a leading expert in the search engine optimization community. His beu blog stories have been featured on National Public Radio, C|NET, Wired, Valleywag, Search Engine Watch, Search Engine Land, Blogoscoped.com, SERoundtable, SEOBook.com and MediaPost Pulications just to name a few.

In addition to his own beu blog, Brian writes for Google Blogoscoped.com, the "editorial board and echo chamber for all things Google" according to David Vise's national bestselling book, The Google Story. Brian also moderates "Google Issues" discussions at Search Engine Watch Forums, the oldest and most respected resource site for SEO online. Further, Brian manages the "Search Engine Watch Forums" and "Search Engine Strategies Conference" groups on Facebook.com and LinkedIn.com.

A lecturer and speaker on search engine marketing for the Terry College of Business at the University of Georgia, Goizueta Business School at Emory University, Search Engine Strategies Conference and eMarketing Association Executive Conference, Brian has consistently combined his entrepreneurial spirit with his passion for search engine marketing and optimization.

With 10 years of proven SEO expertise, Brian is a member of the Web Analytics Association, Information Architecture Institute and a Founding Member of SEMPO Canada.

Away from work, Brian is an Official Fine Artist for the University of Georgia. He is authorized by Nike and the Collegiate Licensing Company, a consortium which consists of more than 200 colleges and universities, bowl games, conferences, The NCAA, Final Four, College World Series and Heisman Trophy Award.
