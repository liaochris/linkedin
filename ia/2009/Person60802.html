Dee Davey’s Summary

www.creativeideasmarketing.com

Dee Davey, though her business Creative Ideas Marketing helps clients be more successful by delivering strategic market planning, service product development and business improvement projects primarily for companies who offer technology or business services to their customers. Dee helps companies adapt and extend service offers, find new sources of revenue and bring “ideas” for new services to life - helping Michigan transition to a knowledge-based economy. Creative Ideas Marketing helps businesses who want to take the next step but lack the resource or time to pull it all together.

An Executive Temp Marketing Professional with over 18 years marketing experience. Dee delivers strategic planning / implementation of marketing, product development and business improvement projects to companies who offer technology or business services to their customers. Dee helps Clients refresh / redefine their direction (find new markets, new customers, and new services), provides coverage for maternity leave, or steps in when an organization has a one-off project that doesn't warrant the investment of a full-time marketing or product development resource. Client benefits delivered:

- Strategic Market Planning / Implementation – Focus company resource to achieve business goals

- Service Product Development: Develop new Client services - Increase sales

- Service Operations Improvement – Improve use of resource; Increase customer satisfaction

- Communicate Client Services - Customers understand, value and will buy Client services

An international marketer resident in England for 30 years, Dee has a unique ability to view and understand global marketing issues from a different perspective. Former Clients have included AT&T/NCR, Siemens, Vodafone, StairMaster and Nautilus. Dee is looking for contract assignments in the B2B market primarily in technology, telecommunications or business services sectors.
