Walter Halvorsen’s Summary

I have over 20 years experience in Healthcare Information Systems with extensive experience in technology infrastructure and process design, vendor relations, automation, capacity planning, performance analysis, database, O/S, SAN and technical support management. I have managed a variety of teams including O/S and database support as well as software development. At Partners HealthCare System, I am responsible for the up-time, performance, support and capacity planning for a very large distributed ECP Caché configuration running Partners enterprise clinical applications including LMR, our flagship electronic medical record application.
