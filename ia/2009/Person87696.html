Dr. Deborah McGill’s Summary

This level of expanding human potential takes on a multi-level dimension that incorporates health and wellness on all levels of consciousness. Dr. McGill integrates cleansing and detoxifying the body while working with client's emotional health and wellness for more vitality. clarity, and fullfillment in everyday life.
