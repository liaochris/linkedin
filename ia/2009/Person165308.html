Miles Austin’s Summary

Technology sales veteran that displays passion for understanding and serving his customers every day. Being a student of his profession and a 'gadget-guy" at heart, he has created a platform of constant innovation and learning how to leverage technology to enhance his customer relationships. Miles enjoys his well-earned reputation as an expert in acquiring new clients in significant numbers, and has accomplished this during both strong and weak economic conditions and shares his experienes in his popular blog entitled fillthefunnel.com. He has delivered superior results in sales and sales executive positions from Fortune 100 companies to startups. Understanding his customer's challenges, developing a plan and successfully executing the plan to solve those challenges are the hallmark of his success.

A lifelong learner, Miles has expanded his experience by consulting with sales organizations and teaching salesmakers the valuable lessons that he has learned over the years.
