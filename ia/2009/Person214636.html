shahid satti’s Summary

I came to Germany in search of an excellent master program for communications system studies and no doubt the course work at TU Munich was really up to date. After my bachelors I worked as a research assistant in University of Engg & Tech, Taxila in Digital Communication department. There I was responsible for Design and VLSI implementation of OFDM modem. This work actually motivated me towards my masters. During my masters I worked in German Aero-Space Centre (DLR) to comply with the compulsory internship requirement of the university. It was really nice to get a short term work experience in signal processing in a dynamic research organization. As a communication systems student I took several courses in wireless and mobile communication. My interest in new generation mobile telecommunication systems pushed me to find a part time job in Comneon (Infenion) as 2G/3G protocol stack verification and test engineer. There I experienced working with RRC, RLC, MAC and PDCP modules of UMTS system. With a good theoretical understanding of mobile communication due to my studies, this job provided me very good insight of practical mobile communication systems and standards.

To excel in next-generation mobile telecommunication systems is a prime carrier objective for me. My previous education and research exposure has made this very clear for me. I also think that a start from a well known organization would be really beneficial for my professional career.
