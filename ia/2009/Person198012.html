Richard Spalding’s Summary

Richard Spalding’s Specialties:

A very strong understanding and experience of Engagement (Viral) marketing, a necessary alternative to traditional media tactics that can create high impact but also very high-level engagement with the consumer. With consumers’ mass migration on-line and the daily distractions of so many different mediums, brands need to make individual connections with people, treating them not as demographics, but as who they are, what they do, what they like and a relevance to where they are.
