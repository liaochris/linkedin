Diana Burrell-Shipton’s Summary

I am trying to live as organic and natural a life as possible.
I write about and teach organic gardening and natural living in several places, most notably at Squidoo.
Here is just one of my many pages there :
http://www.squidoo.com/AllNaturalPerfume

I help the people who want the all natural products to understand just what that means and how to read the labels on products to be able to tell the difference.

I make my own line of all natural products, but cannot supply everyone's needs, so I thought it was important to teach others how to find what they want - even if it isn't mine.

I also love to sew by hand and design home spa items like eye masks, neck pillows and travel pillows... that are one-of-a-kind. Many of them are made from vintage fabrics
