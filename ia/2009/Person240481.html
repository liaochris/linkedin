Tim Sweeney’s Summary

For over a dozen years Tim has helped shape the modern practice of innovation in the field of product & service design. The results of his work have helped organizations penetrate markets, create new markets, inform scenario planning, concepts and models; develop new products, product categories and pipelines; drive roadmap development as well as create and communicate vision.

Tim guest lectures at a number of business and design schools throughout the US and has held variety of leadership positions in the Industrial Designer's Society of America.
