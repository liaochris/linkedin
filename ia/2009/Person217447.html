Profile Summary

The consultant works at key positions as an external subject matter expert for Clients in the end-to-end design of Siebel applications. Playing a techno-functional role, the consultant actively contributes towards:

 Setting up new practices
▪ Build, Operate and Transfer model
▪ Defining project execution methodology across teams
▪ Developing engagement model for onsite-offshore or pure offshore assignments
▪ Explore joint implementation opportunities with other system integrators
▪ Staffing, structuring team and roles
▪ Supporting development team (Siebel) in:
 Detailed application design
 Technical/functional gap analysis
 Prototyping for proof of concept
 Performance enhancement
 Validation of deliverables
▪ Liaison with local training and recruitment agencies

 Providing Siebel expertise for implementations
▪ Prototyping for proof of concept as part of presales activities
▪ Requirement analysis, gap identification and scope definition
▪ High level Application and architecture design
▪ Detailed module design and effort planning
▪ Continuous Review of codes for quality deliverables
▪ Impact analysis and estimation of change requests
▪ Client buy-ins for deviation from approved designs
▪ Recommending business processes to clients based on best practices or to accommodate changes
▪ Support development team on technical matters
▪ Synchronize development, data migration and testing teams on requirements and design

The consultant owns/participates in the activities listed above after approval from client.

The consultant has earlier worked extensively on offshore-onsite delivery model based engagements in varying capacities including development, solution design, requirement analysis, change requests estimation, managing client expectations, etc. He regularly provides need based expertise to clients across geographies.
