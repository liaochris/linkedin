Rod Hunt’s Summary

Rod Hunt is a London based Illustrator who has built a reputation for retro tinged Illustrations & detailed character filled landscapes with UK & international clients in publishing, design, advertising & new media, for everything from book covers to advertising campaigns, theme park maps & even the odd large scale installation too!

Rod is also the Illustrator behind Where's Stig? the new book from Top Gear published September 2009 by BBC Books.

Rod Hunt’s Selected Client List; Alliance & Leicester, Arena, Barclays, BBC, British Airways, British Council, BT, Calor, Computer Arts Magazine, Daily Telegraph, Delicious Magazine, Dorling Kindersley, Financial Times, The Economist, FHM, IKEA, John Brown Publishing, MacUser, Marshall Cavendish, Maxim, The Observer, Reuters, Oxford University Press, Orange, Redwood Publishing, Sci-fi Channel, Sunday Times, Tatler, Tescos, Top Gear, Time Inc, Vodafone, We Are What We Do

A few examples of projects I've worked on

•2m x 5m Interactive Environmental Wall Display for the award winning Lightbox museum & gallery in Woking.

•Illustrating the cover for bestselling book “Change The World 9 To 5”, the follow up to We Are What We Do’s hugely successful (600,000 sold worldwide) “Change The World For A Fiver”. I’ve also recently completed the cover for the Irish edition & pull out poster for the German Edition. The cover was showed as a 3m x 5m wall installation at Dermalogica’s flagship London Kensington store.

• London cityscape Illustration for Vodafone advertising campaign

•Illustration for Village of the Year Award 2016 report for Calor Gas, looking at potential future sustainability delvelopments, commissioned by Forum for the Future.

•Twenty icons for an Orange business brochure

•Illustration for the Cabinet Office for the Government announcement on the Third Sector Policy Review.

• Interactive Virtual Tour for 1st Byte digital printers.
