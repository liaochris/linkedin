Lorenzo Boffelli’s Summary

After the Master Degree in Electronic Engineer at Politecnico of Milan and a three years experience as Hardware and Software engineer I joined the VoIP and Triple Play world since 2001.
Currently I am a System Test and Test Automation Group Leader with a seven years experience in telecommunication, IP networks, VoIP and NGN Network. This experience is grown in Allied Telesis Labs (Italian R&D Center of Allied Telesis Group) where I’ve executed several activities on Residential e Business Gateway, at the beginning as Test Engineer, later as VoIP Test Group Leader, System Test and Test Automation Group Leader.
In this position I coordinate the System Test Group and the Test Automation activity, I design system tests and tests on single software module, I design and implemented test tools, I am involved in pre and post sales support activity for important customer and I design new feature analyzing customer’s requirements and what already exists in the market. I also had an experience as Test Engineer in Cisco System where I was involved in the Test of Network Management System and Graphical Test Automation.
Working in an international R&D center, located in Milan and Raleigh (U.S.), allowed me to have a two years experience as a pre-sales engineer for important customers in U.S.A. and Europe.
I think I can provide an added value for the company because of my experience and knowledge, my capacity to work in group, my attitude to face new challenges and the desire to increase my professionalism.
