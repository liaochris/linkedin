Craig Leonard’s Summary

More than 75% of all U.S. executives want to be in control of their own destiny and run their own business. PBA has developed, and successfully executed, a unique business model to help executives explore these alternatives. We have built a veritable “supermarket” of business opportunities as one of the resources for executives seeking alternatives to traditional employment. Additionally, we proactively seek opportunities that best fit the needs of our executive clients. Many executives choose to pursue these alternatives. They realize the difficulties in replacing their current position and income while being engaged by the chance to pursue their dreams. We help our executive clients on a no-fee basis.
