Nicole France’s Summary

Nicole France is the Founder & CEO of Social Capital Advantage, a premier social media development firm. She is also the President of Nicole France Consulting, which provides marketing and administrative services to small businesses and individuals.

Nicole has spent over 10 years working with companies to develop strategies to increase revenue and reduce costs by utilizing the latest technologies and networking programs. Nicole has a passion for marketing, training and developing programs that will generate traffic, create brand identity and customer loyalty.

Nicole has reorganized departments, increasing company productivity and expanding sales results. She has developed training programs and worked with companies to properly educate and train their staff on how to use current technologies and programs specific to their industry.

Nicole has worked with entrepreneurs, authors, small and large businesses, organizations and associations in many different industries such as; real estate, marketing, advertising, politics and non-profit .

Nicole also served as the 2009 Executive Director for The Sacramento Network of Business Women and current Director of Northern California Social Media Society
