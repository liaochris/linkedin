Desiree Dudley’s Summary

Desiree is a multi-talented marketing strategist, project manager and professional relations specialist who is passionate about understanding and effectively capitalizing on the intricacies of human needs, behaviors and relationships in the global marketplace.

Desiree has experience and comfort working for larger industry leaders such as The Princeton Review and Stanford University, as well as wearing a variety of hats at exciting, smaller 'startup' ventures such as Caresoft Inc. and Innovations Academy. She makes a point to choose her work based on the unique quality of products, services, and passion in people she's come across at visionary organizations because she knows that when exceptional people and exceptional products come together, fantastic things happen.

Desiree loves the energy and thrill of understanding and effectively targeting markets through strategic analysis, innovative design/development and the cultivation of win-win relationships. She strongly believes that focused energy, creative adaptability, and a strong commitment to authentic communication, quality relationships, and personal integrity are absolutely crucial to sustaining long-term business.
