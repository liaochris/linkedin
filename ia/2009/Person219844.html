József Tamás Herczeg’s Summary

Having a middle course state exam of English language (passed in Hungary) I founded my firm in 2001. During these years I gained many experiences in English-Hungarian software localization and translation. My friends, being computer fans without English knowledge inspired me to start translating software.

In 2001-2002 I published several articles in the Hungarian CHIP Magazine about my localization experiences.

Being an Open Source enthusiast, I am the co-founder and board member of Joomla! User Association Hungary and the coordinator of the Hungarian translation team.

I would be glad to contribute to your project.
