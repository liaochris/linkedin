Yannick Boucher’s Summary

4+ years of experience in the video games industry, with a focus on mobile games at Gameloft, the world's biggest mobile game developer/publisher. 6+ years experience in digital media overall.

Worked on over 30 critically acclaimed and commercially successful games and franchises, including Prince of Persia, Splinter Cell, Rayman, Ghost Recon, Midnight Pool, Asphalt, and more.

Well-rounded experience in QA (in-house and 3rd party certification), digital distribution and wireless sales (WAP, carrier partnerships, etc.), technical audio production and management. Strong knowledge of wireless handset specs across all global markets, including Japan and South Korea, and production methods across a wide range of manufacturers and platforms (J2ME, BREW, WIPI, DOJA).

Experienced in putting together and building up cohesive production teams and processes from scratch across the asian region, especially Shanghai, China, which is rapidly becoming one of the leading game production hubs in Asia.
