Arvind Rajan’s Summary

I am a management generalist, and over the years I've worn just about every c-level hat at a company, in industries ranging from hybrid/electric transportation to wireless data networks to Internet startups. I currently serve the Vice President of People Operations at LinkedIn, the world's largest professional network.

Prior to joining LinkedIn, I spent five years as President and CEO of Grassroots Enterprise, a technology and services firm that builds online grassroots marketing and influencer programs for companies, products and causes. I continue to serve as Chairman of the company.
