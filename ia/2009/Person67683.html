Stephen Pratt’s Summary

Having held senior positions within academia, as well as the public and private sectors, I have seen the impact that successful management can have on an organisation's operation. Conversely, I have witnessed the effects that poor management, particularly at a senior level, can have. Effective management, particularly within executive teams, is crucial to the successful operation and management of any organisation. This involves the management of technology and the assurance that it remains a business enabler and not a millstone around the corporate neck.
My approach is to deploy an effective strategy through pragmatism. Coming up with a fancy strategy is one thing, making it happen successfully is another.
In addition to my academic awards I am a FBCS, CEng, and CITP.

Stephen Pratt’s Specialties:

Technology governance, technical architecture, shared service centres, impact of globalisation.
For recent publications please visit my website.
