  • Chief Computation Officer

    Strategic Medicine Inc.

    (Biotechnology industry)

    2009 — 2009 (less than a year)

    Strategic Medicine Inc.’s mission is to improve clinical outcomes through better patient and disease stratification. The company addresses a fundamental problem in medicine that is increasingly problematic for patients, healthcare providers, and payers alike. The old diagnostic categories are becoming more inadequate by the day, and one-size-fits all drug therapies lead to costly late stage failures and withdrawals. Attempts to solve these issues by providing so-called personalized medicine are unrealistic in the marketplace and misguided in their scientific underpinnings.

    SMI is offering disease stratification products and services driven by clinical need and supported by its rapidly expanding knowledge base and improved implementation of diagnostic technology. These products sit on top of and interact directly will existing electronic medical records. They can also function as stand-alone systems.
