Rose Waltz’s Summary

Career Objective:

Human Resources Benefits Manager for innovative company that values new ideas; Bank Vice President Retirement Plan Services; Conversions and Implementations Manager for customer focused employee benefits service provider.

Qualification Summary:

As a Benefits Manager and licensed Kansas Attorney with 16 years of experience working with defined contribution retirement plans, I’m the experienced manager you are seeking. For the past 9 years I have worked for the number one provider of corporate retirement plans, Fidelity Investments. During my tenure there I held three different positions, each with increasing levels of responsibility, working exclusively with retirement plans.

In the course of my career I have gained expertise in many facets of the retirement plan business including plan administration, employee education, plan design, ERISA compliance, contracts, implementation, sales and investments.

My previous experience as an attorney in a law firm taught me to administer retirement plans with an eye towards mitigating risk. This is what distinguishes my services from those of other retirement plan administrators.
