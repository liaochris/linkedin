Roger Noia’s Summary

Experienced Internet marketer that's been online since before the web was hip. I'm the essential Marketing person you need in your company. I've been called "the jack of all trades" and "the glue that binds groups together". I've also got extensive people management experience, from managing web producers to technical support engineers.

I have extensive product management, product marketing and marcom experience that I've gained from working at very small startups to successful corporations such as Microsoft.

I've got extensive Internet marketing experience: web advertising, Google Adwords, email marketing, B2B websites, search engine marketing (SEM) and optimization (SEO). I've even managed web production and online ads with a $1 Million budget at Microsoft.

I have superior technical knowledge for a marketing person: Photoshop, Flash, PHP, PERL, SQL, Pivot tables, salesforce.com administration, databases, RSS, APIs, XML and web design.

I specialize in making complex systems and processes work better for internal organizations by listening to the needs of individuals and understanding how technologies can streamline marketing and sales processes. I manage lead handling processes to make sure customers get what they need and so that salespeople don't waste their time. I optimize opportunity pipelines so that salespeople and management get the information they need, particularly with Salesforce.com .

I can bridge the gap between your company's engineers and your customers. Unlike many, I know the difference between a feature and a benefit and how to write them correctly for the appropriate audience.

I'm the branding person - I manage and create executive level presentations, sales presentations and corporat demos. I've even done a few VC presentations to help our company get funding (it worked).

I'm the go-to person when you need to get your sales and marketing programs working, together and effectively.
