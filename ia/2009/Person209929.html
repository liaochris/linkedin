sarah taylor’s Summary

Sarah Taylor is an international executive specializing in marketing and sales. Her clients include organizations wanting to grow global brands, align culture, brand and customers, create high performance sales teams, and individuals working to develop or strengthen their personal brand.

She has an intimate knowledge of the application of commercial practices to intellectual property (products and solutions), gained in a range of environments including internet start-up's, professional service firms, and Australia/USA/Asia's leading companies.
Sarah has consulted extensively in programmes delivering market acquisition and growth, brand repositioning, brand extension, strategic alliances, product development, product roll-out, business transition, team development, culture change, and operational performance improvement.

Her profound understanding of the impact of communication leads individuals, teams and organizations to a continuous process of improvement in client relations. Sarah's key strength is her ability to guide her clients through a creative process of synthesising perspectives, data, and apparently disparate needs into coherent and practical strategies that create market growth.
