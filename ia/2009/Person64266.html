Dirk Koester’s Summary

My main interest is in the mental lexicon, ie the vocabulary we carry around in our brains; for one language or for more. These lexical processes in the brain can be investigated using electrophysiological recordings and the measurement local blood flow changes. With a special emphasis on compound words I inquired the lexical processes when understanding (hearing) and producing (speaking) words.

If that's all Greek, this is what we want to understand:
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=POjTfSF-Qmk

Furthermore, I have an interest in visual perception. Using an artificial grammar, we have scrutinised how people perceive (abstract) events that follow such a grammar.
