Carole Brow’s Summary

I own and operate Travel Experience Consulting, a home-based travel agency doing customized plans and arrangements for special occasion travel, such as destination weddings, honeymoons, anniversary trips, romantic getaways, holiday travel, and reunions for families & friends.

My agency has two brands:

(1) Tropical Traveler for dive trips, cruises, and tropical vacations to the Caribbean, Hawaii, the South Pacific, and Latin America.

(2) Europe Traveler for travel to Western Europe (especially to Britain, Ireland, France, Italy, Portugal, & Spain), to Greece, and to the Holy Land.

Carole Brow’s Specialties:

I am the creator of Cultural Immersion Travel© for independent travelers who really want to experience the places they visit.

I am certified by the Cruise Lines International Association as a Master Cruise Counsellor & am a Certified Scuba Diver. I hold the following destination specialist certificates: Aussie; Kiwi (NZ); Tahiti Tiare; Fiji Matai; Oahu; Caribbean; British Virgin Islands; Mexico; Cancun; Louisiana; Quebec; BritAgent; Ireland (Shamrock); France; Portugal; Rome.
