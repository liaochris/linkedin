Dave Gorham’s Summary

With a strong entrepreneurial background, David focuses on investing in and selling real estate. He manages one of the offices for the #1 Coldwell Banker affiliates in New Jersey as his primary focus, while still maximizing his investment opportunities, and maintaining a fervent mandate for personal growth, and an unrelenting quest to make sure that he is offering the best possible leadership to each and every agent in the office.

David feels very strongly that his position should be focused on sales leadership and training. He considers his participation in outside coaching programs as a way to broaden the vision and deepen the value of the daily approach to agents. He believes that not only does it help him, but that it will afford the real estate agents of Coldwell Banker Elite, Realtors an opportunity to further their personal development as well through his mentorship. This is truly the mindset of a great leader.

Dave Gorham is a coach for Frame of Mind Coaching. He credits his own success to taking a personal interest in client satisfaction and of growth of each agent in his office. In his spare time, Dave enjoys spending time in Ocean City, driving his red convertible and being with his family and friends.
