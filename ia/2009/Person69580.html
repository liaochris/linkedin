Ed Hamilton Jr.’s Summary

I have had many great career experiences within The Dow Chemical Company and am continuing to seek additional career broadening experiences. I am interested in leadership positions to further refine my functional and global competencies while contributing to the success of the business. I have experience working is several businesses in a variety of positions and have a proven track record of motivating people to achieve unit and company goals. Working to achieve a goal of “best in class” is enjoyable to me. With my background and solid management style, I believe I would be a positive influence to many organizations.

While in Michigan Operations, I was the team leader for the Site Conversion Cost Team. The site MI objective for this team is for at least 50% of the plants within MiOps to be cost-advantaged versus both internal and external competition by 2010. Working with the team, we developed site conversion cost measurement creating a “green/yellow/red” light system to identify the competitive status of MiOps businesses.

In order to provide myself a more rounded business viewpoint, I went back to school to achieve a master in business administration degree. I found this learning experience enjoyable and challenging. My goal is to utilize this knowledge within Dow to help make better decisions in a leadership role. I look forward to using this knowledge as a foundation to building upon.
