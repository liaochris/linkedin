John Webb’s Summary

Marketing Director with 14 years experience gained in both traditional and digital marketing within the Games, Online, Mobile and FMCG sectors. An outstanding track record of delivering marketing campaigns growing brand and enterprise value. An accomplished leader who excels at building, melding and motivating teams. A big picture thinker with the ability to focus in to get the right things done to meet business goals.

John Webb’s Specialties:

> Using analysis of customer behaviour to build growth-oriented commercial strategies and plans
> Influencing stakeholders with conviction and sound strategic thinking

> Business and financial modelling, forecasting and definition of KPIs to drive focus and delivery
