Gabi Conti’s Summary

Gabi Conti graduated cum laude from Emerson College with a double major in Broadcast Journalism and Writing for Television and Film.

While at Emerson, Gabi won an AP Award for executive producing and hosting Emerson’s 2008 Oscar coverage. Gabi hosted Emerson’s first ever 2008 Emmy Experience on the red carpet in Hollywood; where she chatted up celebrities including Vanessa Williams, John Krasinski, Jeremy Piven, Glenn Close, and Larry David, among others. Gabi was host of Emerson’s prestigious 2008 and 2009 EVVY Awards at the Cutler Majestic Theater in Boston.

Gabi recently was the creator, co-executive producer and head writer for a single camera sitcom pilot, Ground Floor under the guidance of Emerson alumni Kevin Bright (executive producer of Friends).

Currently Gabi performs stand-up comedy all over Los Angeles and has been studying improv with The Upright Citizen's Brigade
