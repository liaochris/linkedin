Thomas Bosilevac’s Summary

For over 10 years Thomas has been involved in the sales, management, marketing or analysis of Fortune 500 interactive projects. At WebTrends, Thomas is in charge of assuring the satisfaction of WebTrends most strategic clients such as: Mattel, MetLife, Orbitz.com, NBC Olympics, Realogy, Microsoft, and Monster.com. Thomas has achieved a 100% client renewal rate by assuring that clients are enabled with the insights to make data driven decisions. Thomas does not stop at assuring the monitoring of data but builds the process to assure opportunities are discovered and acted upon. Thomas has particular strengths in offline lead capture conversion models, online brand awareness monitoring and SEO/SEM.

Before WebTrends, Thomas managed the in-house implementation of WebTrends Analytics at Caterpillar, while increasing the adoption of web analytics for all public facing websites.

From 2000-2001, Thomas managed the product development for WHN 2.0, the e-commerce platform standard within the network and cable television industry. Clients included NBC, ABC, FOX, MTV, Comedy Central and Playboy. From 2001- 2004, Thomas worked independently managing project development teams for companies such as Avid, The Black Dog, Virtumundo and more.
