Brenda Be’s Summary

Providing interior design for renovations and beyond, with total project management from concept to completion. Helping clients, including homeowners, architects, and builders, with projects of all sizes, from a kitchen or bath, to a total renovation. Working with clients at all budget levels to maximize the impact of their investment. Also providing other traditional interior design services such as furniture/finishings, space planning, color and art consultation, etc. Working with clients in homes and tastes in all styles, from eclectic artistic spaces to more conservative, quiet decor.

Brenda Be’s Specialties:

Specializing in highly unique and customized spaces with an artist's touch. Bringing an approach of Green Design to all projects, which can be integrated into any style and any budget. Also specializing in vintage housing: anticipating and avoiding problems with such renovations, and preserving architectural integrity while designing for the way clients live today.
