mike geiger’s Summary

Mike has worked on over 20 broadcast properties from “The Ren and Stimpy show” to series’ such as “The Very Good Adventure of Yamroll in Happy Kingdom” and “Total Drama Island“.

He has also produced various independent work such as the short "Transistor Sect" with artist Ed Templeton, a film in conjunction with the N.F.B. called "When Things Were a Little Backwards", his short "Viewing Abstract", and "Cuddle Sticks" which has been screened at various animation and comedy festivals and is now on tour with the 2008 Spike and Mike's festival of animation.

He has most recently been in production on his web series called “County Ghost” with the first two episode now available online.
