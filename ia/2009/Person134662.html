Kristen Throop’s Summary

I help clients promote their businesses with my intelligence, professionalism and experience. I’m thorough in my research, practical in my approach. My clients range from large to small.

Kristen Throop’s Specialties:

Wine packaging (labels, capsules, cartons), wine collateral, annual reports, concert programs, catalogs, newsletters, magazines, book covers, identity systems (business cards, letterhead, etc.), brochures, postcards, signs, logos, advertising and illustration.
