Christina Jee, RN, MSN, NP-C’s Summary

AANP Board Certified Family Nurse Practitioner with an objective to provide quality and evidence-based care in a team-oriented practice serving to vulnerable populations across the age spectrum.

Christina’s passion to work with the under-served has taken her to rural Guatemala and across the States to Fort Wayne, Indiana as a volunteer clinician.
