Tony Romanelli’s Summary

I have a strong background in Online communications, as well as online management. I have worked with large companies that cater to child user bases, as well as dealing with online child education. I have worked in Customer Service and support fields for some time now, as well as community managment and moderation.

One example of this was while I was working with Metric-Island LLC. I worked as the Human Resource Manager, as well as moderation/Customer Support manager. I handled the hiring of volunteer moderators for the virtual world. My duties ranged from management, to virtual payroll, as well as team building, and training exercises.

I worked as Customer Support Director for Kurbed LTD, where I hired and fired Customer Support Reps/Managers. I created extensive handbooks for training, policies, and other documents needed to run a successful online community. I first hand helped draft and publish the support system, and back admin panels used for the administration of the site.

Being I have had a small project in the works, that I have closed called Hydro Island, I managed a small team of coders and community reps. I built the project from the ground up, and created a fully working isometric online chatting community. I handled paying the coders and other various staff, as well as building a good user base from the ground up. I created manuals, as well as documentation for investment offers.
