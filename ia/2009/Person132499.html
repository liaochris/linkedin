Kim Seeling Smith’s Summary

Passionate about helping others reach their full potential and providing the best customer service on the planet!

I do this through my "day job" at Aequalis Consulting as well as my own coaching, training, speaking and writing business.

Former Big 4 CPA from the US, I became a recruitment consultant in 1994 and have built several successful businesses in 5 cities within 3 countries.

I provide a high touch, high quality service and am very relationship driven. I take a long term view and would gladly forgo a particular fee to cement a relationship and can provide testimonials of same.

I have been fascinated by human potential and peak performance all of my life and am currently working to become a Tony Robbins trainer as well as developing my own training / coaching and speaking business designed to help others attain their goals and realise their own potential.
