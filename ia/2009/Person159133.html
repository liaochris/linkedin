Melanie Lazarus’s Summary

My role as Director of Marketing for Archimage, Inc. and its subsidiary, Playnormous, LLC, is to foster business development and public relations by acting as a liaison between the worlds of healthcare and design. I am also the project director and producer for several serious game projects (also known as computer-based behavioral interventions). Much of my time is spent creating presentations, giving lectures, and writing--writing grants, marketing materials, press releases, blog posts and lots of emails.
