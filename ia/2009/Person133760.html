I hope this introduction provides some insight into my professional experience and my personal brand. Enjoy!
How would I describe myself in 10 words or less? I would have to say, “A commercially astute, yet personable social media and digital copywriting enthusiast”.


I'm the type of marketing professional that loves engaging brands with people and telling them a good story while I'm at it.

You've probably heard of the terms B2C & B2B marketing. If you have, forget them now! Social media has changed has forever changed the way brands engage with their customers. Which is why I'm an advocate of B2P (Brand 2 People). It's not important what form the customer takes (business entity or consumer), they are all people. People engage and buy from other people. Food for thought!

I’m totally house trained and can be left unsupervised in front of clients. I can build instant rapport with people at any level. Whether it is the CEO, CMO, or the receptionist who greets me when I arrive for a pitch. I like to leave them with the feeling, that their encounter with me (aka your agency/brand) has been positive and something they wish to engage with further.

Another skill of mine is the ability to empathise with a client and act as a “tech translator” explaining complex digital/social marketing strategies in terms they understand and can see the value in.
