Debbi Mack’s Summary

I'm a versatile writer and researcher who's written articles, marketing and promotional materials, Web content and fundraising material. I also offer ghostwriting and editing services.

I've worked in various formats, including print, online and video. I have particular expertise in legal and environmental topics. I also do legal, current awareness and competitive intelligence research.

I'm a published crime fiction author, with one novel ("Identity Crisis") and a short story ("Deadly Detour" in the "Chesapeake Crimes" anthology). I will have another short story published in the "Chesapeake Crimes 4" anthology, to be released by Wildside Press in March 2010.

The Book Grrl at http://thebookgrrl.blogspot.com/
Green Reality Check at http://greenrealitycheck.blogspot.com/
