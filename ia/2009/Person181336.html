Pascal Opitz’s Summary

I am a highly proficient web developer based in London, with more than 7 years of commercial experience and a good understanding of the full lifecycle of a web application.
The main focus of my work is PHP5 (OOP), MySQL and User Interface development, utilizing a variety of technologies such as JavaScript, AJAX, various JS libraries, XSL, MVC Frameworks like Zend or CakePHP and LINUX system tools.

Previous projects include UKTV, Tesco, The Economist, BP Gas Operations, Sodexho, Robert Bosch GmbH, Unicef, PriceWaterhouseCoopers, Eurohypo, Mainova and many more.

Through my company, ILikeCode Ltd, I am mainly interested in contracting work, but am also offering technical web development ranging from bespoke back end systems and accessible UI development to template development and back end customization for CMS and e-commerce platforms.

Pascal Opitz’s Specialties:

PHP5, MySQL, MVC Frameworks, JavaScript, AJAX, JS libraries (mainly jQuery, but also Mootools, YUI, Prototype), (x)HTML, CSS, Subversion, Content management, Accessibility, XSL transformations, E-commerce
