Kathy Worrell’s Summary

With many years experience in recreation programming, I have worked in almost every department, but my passion is with Special Events. When I was little I wanted to be in the Macy's Day Parade, now I want to plan it!

I am very happy with my current position in Fountain Hills, but I am always looking for ways to expand and grow professionally. I volunteer with a lot of professional organizations that have helped mold me into the professional I am today. If I could describe my planning style: safety first, organized and resourceful.

My ultimate goal is to get my MPA and my Event Certificate from ASU, and if they will have me – become a professor in Recreation and Tourism at ASU.

Kathy Worrell’s Specialties:

Planning special events and public speaking.
