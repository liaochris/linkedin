Who Is Procom Services?

For over 30 years, Procom Services has been matching people with companies, for jobs that are a perfect fit—contract, full-time, or part-time. We partner with the best companies—large and small—across North America, including some of the best known enterprises in the world, and provide them with a range of services, training, tools, and, most importantly, the ideal candidates, pre-screened and ready to roll.

We are a leading provider of professional technology experts to businesses and governments in North America and have expanded to other professional fields, as well. What we're known for is going beyond mere staffing to look, in a meaningful and strategic way, at the processes and people that will truly help a company, department, venture—and individual—succeed.
