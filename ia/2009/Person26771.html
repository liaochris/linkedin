Bentleys NEPA’s Interests:

Bentley’s boasts a palatial like setting with intricate work of hardwood, stone, and marble, presented with true splendid grandeur, while offering a scenic view of the Wyoming Valley. Bentley’s is able to accommodate intimate dinner parties, communion, bar/bat mitzvah’s, graduation, corporate dinners, office parties, bridal showers or rehearsal dinners, including space large enough for a grand wedding reception. Bentley’s has the capability of seating a minimum of twenty and a maximum of three-hundred people, with dancing. We are fully capable to suit your guest accommodation needs. The professional staff at Bentley’s possesses the experience to address even the smallest of touches for the detailed-oriented to exclusive flourishes for those who prefer grand gesture. Our expert culinary staff, led by Executive Chef John Hudak, will custom-design a menu sure to satisfy every palate and your utmost satisfaction. We look forward to the opportunity to surpass your every expectation.
