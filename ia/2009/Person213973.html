Sergio Millian’s Experience

  • Official Representative of Belarus Chamber of Commerce and Industry in the United States

    Belarus Chamber of Commerce and Industry

    (International Trade and Development industry)

    August 2008 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    For successful penetration into Belarusian market, profitable investments of capital it is necessary that some intermediary organization would contribute to this aim, render necessary organizational, consulting and informational assistance to a foreign partner. Such organization is the Belarusian Chamber of Commerce and Industry - the oldest foreign economic structure of the Republic.

    The members of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry represent over 1,400 enterprises, organizations and firms and joint-stock companies of the state and private sector of economy.

    The Chamber of Commerce and Industry promotes establishment and development of business relations between the Belarusian and foreign businessmen, invites and receives in Belarus the foreign trade and economic delegations, individual representatives of the foreign firms. For all this the corresponding divisions of the Chamber provide full package of services, including visa support, meeting, accommodation, transportation, interpreting services, location of the Belarusian partners, arrangement of meetings and negotiations.

    The Chamber of Commerce and Industry provides consulting, informational and mediatory services, renders assistance to the Belarusian and foreign clients in location of partners for trade and cooperation, including establishment of direct contracts, production cooperation, creation of joint ventures and enterprises with 100% foreign capital.

    The Chamber issues the certificates of origin of goods exported from Belarus.

    The Chamber has the International Court attached to it for objective resolving of the disputes, which sometimes might occur between the partners.

    All in all, 600 full-time employees works for the Chamber. They are highly-qualified experts of appropriate training and practical experience.
