Rob Hubert’s Summary

Rob Hubert, Chief Information Officer for Elite IT Services, has a strong background in the business of IT Solutions Management. Rob has developed and implemented Elite’s Information Technology Strategy, Engagement Delivery Process and Talent Management Process that includes the careful selection of the best consultants for our clients projects. Rob’s success is attributed to his business acumen, intuitive listening skills and coaching skills.

Rob is an expert in Software as a Service (SaaS) delivery models and remains current on technical innovations and trends. He has a unique background and level of expertise in service oriented architecture, application integration technologies and enterprise information technology architecture. Rob’s technical expertise is attributed to his prior 12-year tenure at A.G. Edwards, Inc., where he implemented many new technologies and managed a team of highly skilled technical resources responsible for creating infrastructure and applications to support the firm’s business operations. As his career progressed at AGE, he became the Manager of the Integration Services team responsible for Enterprise Application Integration and Middleware Technologies across all platforms.

Rob started his IT career in 1986 at May Department stores as a Computer Operator. He worked his way through the IT Enterprise, from Application Developer to System Programmer, onto Manager of the Integration Services team at A.G. Edwards. His hard work and technical expertise paid off as he was promoted to Associate Vice President in 1996 and continually held positions of increasing responsibility. Rob left AGE., to pursue his entrepreneurial interests as co-founder of Elite IT Services, an Information Technology Consulting firm specializing in Elite directed consulting and client directed consulting.
