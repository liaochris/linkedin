Hannah Chamberlain’s Summary

I’m a film maker working in corporate communications for businesses, charities and the public sector. I have 11 years experience writing, producing, directing and editing for broadcast, webcast and DVD. My films have been screened at Cannes, Raindance and the BBC Short Film Festival.

For ten years I worked freelance for clients including The Community Channel, Channel 4, Five, and corporate clients including London Business TV, The Mental Health Foundation, Natwest, Citibank and The British Library. My training was in editing and I spent four years working as an assistant editor for Nick Shaw Post Production in Soho.

Today, I run Barrage Media Ltd, a new company set up in July 08, which has already acquired clients such as John Laing PLC, South London and Maudsley Trust, Together and the Overseas Mission Fellowship, producing video for DVD duplication, authoring complex DVD’s and shooting interviews and events. I work closely with a team of professional associates to deliver highly tailored solutions to client needs.

For more information about Barrage Media please visit www.barragemedia.com.
