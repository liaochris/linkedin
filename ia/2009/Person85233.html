George Forsyth’s Summary

I have been working in UK Government IT since 1997. Prior to that I worked in other areas of government administration working in revenue services, court process admin, statistics, policy development. I came to IT through Project Management but have since worked in Strategy Development, Contract Negotiation, Technical Design and Assurance. I specialize in VFM assessments which I introduced to the technical area.

I am currently promoting Innovation in the Ministry of Justice which is great because I get to meet lots of really smart people working at the cutting edge of IT and it's my job to see if their products can actually solve a real life problem or if they are just a bit flash and whizzy. It's never dull and always fun and I'm the least smart person I have to deal with..

Outside of my full time job I have always, and I mean always, had another string to my bow. In my younger years I played badly in bands like everyone else did at the time, and then went into local politics for a bit, spending four years as a London Borough Councillor in the early 90's.

The first time I saw the web in about 1997 I thought "I'm sure you can make money with this thing". I wasn't wrong as lots of other people of proved since. Me? I'm still working on it.

I have been working online, developing internet sites for the last 10 years. I currently manage three sites:

Global Affiliate Programs, one of the first UK based affiliate directories;
Sleep Easy Loans, a meta search engine specializing in financial products, and Cathie's Cars which is the web site of the cab company owned by my friend Cathie.

I would like to make contact with others operating in the affiliate marketing space.
