Eric Lituchy’s Summary

DELIGHTFUL DELIVERIES, Port Washington, New York • 1998-Present
National gift basket and gourmet food retailer, with 2007 revenue of $5.5M.

Founder/CEO
Direct operations, managing team of eight employees, outsourced call center, and consultants. Lead advertising and marketing initiatives, as well as customer service, vendor relations, and financial (P&L/accounting) activities. Select/integrate ecommerce software and develop website appearance and focus. Spearhead online advertising efforts, including use of SEM, SEO, affiliate, email, and comparison sites.

Major Achievements:
• Grew company, with no outside financing, from $0 to $5.5M – and earned recognition as the largest pure-play gift basket company online.
• Propelled company to achieve outstanding growth: 5472% from 1998-2001, and 140% growth from 2003-2006, earning a spot in the top half of Inc. Magazine’s 5000 list.
• Provided services in 2007 to 80% of the Fortune 100 companies – and all of the top 10, including American Express, Target, Pfizer and Microsoft.
• Partnered with public relations team to secure invaluable media mentions on top TV programs including “Good Morning America” and “Today.”
• Developed integral vendor relationships with top-tier products from companies including Mrs. Fields, Wine.com, Dale & Thomas, Godiva, and Starbucks.
• Optimized print advertising during peak holiday seasons to develop profitable corporate gifting business.


ALE IN THE MAIL, INC., Plainview, New York • 1994-1998
Beer of the Month club business, offering American-made micro-brewed beers by mail.

Founder/CEO
Created and directed company – prior to start-up, performed six months of competitive analysis, research of advertising vehicles, and probability forecasting. Activities included sourcing beer from across the U.S., managing inbound/outbound freight, warehousing and fulfillment, direct response advertising and building website that offered product content and fax order form.
