Chad Sollis’s Summary

For 10 years I have been involved with Online Marketing, Demand Marketing, Operations, Lead Generation, New Customer Acquisition, Web Analytics, and Web Development for Medium and Enterprise level companies.

I am intimately familiar with marketing/sales measurement across multiple channels and tactics. I also have strong operational background from a marketing, sales, and corporation prospective.

I've had the opportunity to speak at several Industry and partner events, some of which are the largest gatherings of professionals for Software as a Service (SAAS) like Salesforce.com Dreamforce and Omniture Summit.

I had the opportunity to co-scope and build patent pending technology for Omniture called Closed Loop Marketing for Salesforce, the first of its kind in the industry, and continue to innovate new methodologies and techniques for improving online marketing and measurement.

I have also scoped and built enterprise grade (web) Content Management Solutions that scale with locale (language) and traffic volume. These solutions enable groups in the organization to manage elements of the web properties with ease and agility.

Additionally, as a senior level positions, I have experienced two industry leader's, one online retail, one B2B, through very successful IPOs.
