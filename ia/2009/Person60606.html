Debra Costner’s Experience

  • CEO & Publisher

    Bay Area Business Woman

    (Newspapers industry)

    July 2007 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    Bay Area Business Woman newspaper has been publishing for more than seventeen years. I purchased the company in July 2007 from the former owner and founder. We redesigned the publication, and focus editorial on inspirational stories of successful female executives and entrepreneurs, as well as regular business advice columns on success strategies, sales & marketing, technology and financial advice. In our efforts to expand and be green, in October 2008 we began publishing the full newspaper online. We are the only newspaper reaching the women's business community and reporting exclusively on women's success in the San Francisco Bay Area - with more than 50,000 readers. We also conduct a successful series of monthly business seminars and networking events for our community. Check out our website for our upcoming events as well as a complete calendar of all women's networking events in the Bay Area. We have over 1,000 articles online that will inspire and give you essential tools for building your business.

  • CEO

    Business Woman, LLC

    (Financial Services industry)

    2007 — Present (2 years)

    The Business Woman company provides financial and business consulting, as well as writing and editing services. Business consulting specialties include in-depth financial statement and budget planning and analysis, implementation of cost-saving measures, business plan writing, creating sales & marketing plans, and overall strategic planning. Writing and editing services include business advice columns, financial advice, company profiles, interviews and people profiles, marketing copywriting and blog writing. Contact us for your business consulting needs!

  • Finance & Operations Director

    Yoga Journal

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    April 1999 — January 2007 (7 years 10 months)

    Was hired by a new owner who took the publication from a non-profit to a for profit business. We built it from 70,000 circulation to 350,000 in six years, producing 3 successful yoga conferences each year, and marketing numerous products including videos and calendar. The business was marketed via M&A company in New York, where I produced all due diligence and met with 8 potential buyers. We sold to Active Interest Media in Sept. 2006.

  • General Manager

    Bay Times

    (Publishing industry)

    August 1997 — April 1999 (1 year 9 months)

    As GM my responsibilities included hands-on management of advertising sales, oversight of distribution, office management and production. Negotiated sponsorship deals with numerous groups (including Frameline film festival) to increase community outreach.

  • Finance Director

    San Francisco Bay Guardian

    (Privately Held; Newspapers industry)

    1992 — 1997 (5 years)

    Supervised the accounting, office management, IT dept. and H.R. for the company during a period of rapid growth - over 300% during my 6 years there. Implemented cost savings of over $500,000 in one year without any staff reduction.

  • CFO

    Russell Miller Inc.

    (Investment Banking industry)

    1989 — 1992 (3 years)

    Employed as CFO and a crucial part of the executive team of this investment banking company which does M&A work in the insurance industry. In addition to financial statements prep, budgeting and cash flow projections, I managed SEC licensing process and worked in compliance with all rules & regulations.

  • Accounting Manager

    F.C. Hock Properties

    (Real Estate industry)
