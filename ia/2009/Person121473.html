Joshua Mack’s Summary

Digital product creator and strategist with over 13 years working at major media companies and start-ups creating excellent products and online businesses. Working in the space between media and technology to:

- Create products from good ideas
- Evolve brands online
- Produce compelling editorial and marketing offerings
- Translate ideas into products, tools, and marketing campaigns
- Transform technology into products and usable tools
- Work with social media and communities
- Drive more traffic from widgets and other viral media
- Find technology partnerships and solutions
- Manage development teams
- Push the envelope in meaningful ways

Joshua Mack’s Specialties:

Social media product creation; business and strategic planning; product development; editorial programming; online marketing, technical, editorial, and creative production management.
