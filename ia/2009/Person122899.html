juan candela’s Summary

Juan Antonio Candela López, Valencia 1970. En la actualidad es Director Gerente de Las Provincias Televisión, cadena que emite en TDT en la Comunitat Valenciana y perteneciente al grupo de comunicación Las Provincias (Vocento). Su carrera profesional siempre ha estado ligada a los medios de comunicación digitales y audiovisuales.

Juan Candela currently holds the position of Managing Director at Las Provincias Television SA. Reporting to the General Manager of the group, with responsibility for the strategic direction and operational management of the business. He´s in charge of the strategic execution of maximizing market share opportunities and ratings growth. Well-rounded background in launching, growing and managing television stations / online newspapers in regional markets. Strong strategic and analytical skills, and proven ability as a team builder/leader to be able to provide structure and oversight in the overall operations of the station.
