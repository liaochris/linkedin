Raul Popa’s Summary

Over the years I have worked and been involved in different activities around computer software and internet industries. As a self-taught designer and programmer, I've started to make my first money in 1999 during my second year of study at "Babes-Bolyai" University of Cluj-Napoca, finished with a major in Sociology. I continued working in the Flash development business and build my education attending several post-graduate courses in communication, media, marketing and IT.

After finishing my education I have started to work as a lead Flash developer, and soon after it I have started to make my way to a product manager position.

During this time I have extended my knowledge and abilities for information and usability architecture, interface design, pricing strategies, branding and brand communication, support management, online marketing, partnership and affiliate marketing and generally speaking everything that has to do with software product management.

As for future goals I want to bring the products that I lead to a higher level and have a few plans about re-inventing the wheel in interactive user experience.

My secret weapon is statistics.
