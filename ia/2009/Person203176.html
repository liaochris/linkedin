Ronald Fleurbaaij’s Summary

Working as an independent photographer based in The Hague, Netherlands, I'm covering news, sports, portraits, corporate and travel photography. After finishing my last practical at the Amsterdam newspaper Het Parool and graduating my study photography at the Technical Collage Ede, I started my own photo agency.

Fleurbaaij Photography is an independent full service photo agency based in The Hague, The Netherlands. Fleurbaaij Photography is the photo agency for all your photography. News, sports, editorial or commercial photography, Fleurbaaij Photography is the place to be. Fleurbaaij Photography delivers craftwork, very fast, in every kind of way. Digital or traditional photography, no problem whatsoever. With Fleurbaaij Photography you’re always getting guaranteed quality.

Fleurbaaij Photography works on a daily basis for various media and business organizations and it's work is published in/by:
Insurance company AEGON
Dutch newspaper AD and AD Sportwereld
Dutch national press agency ANP
International press agency Reuters
Soccer photo agency Pics United
and many others.

Ronald Fleurbaaij’s Specialties:

News, sports, editorial, portraits, politics, travel, features, stock, corporated, photography, photojournalism
