Jonathan Swanson’s Summary

For the last few years I have been producing records and performing as a professional pianist. My goal is to collaborate with more talented musicians as an accompanist, arranger, producer, or recording engineer. As a pianist, I perform jazz, salsa, and classical styles. I have been hired to perform at weddings, church services, wineries, and private parties. My main goal in piano performance is to expand my classical and jazz repertoire. As a recording engineer, I hope to expand my live recording business to record more live concerts. I would also like to do more post production mixing in 5.1 surround.
