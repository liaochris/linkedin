Jerome Sierra’s Summary

French speaking Private Mortgage Banker and Certified Mortgage Planner CMPS® focused on providing personalized plan, consulting and financing services for International executives who want to purchase properties in California. My practice is built on integrity, honesty, and “down to earth” advice. 100% referral based practice.

Unlike other mortgage originators, I do NOT:
+ Get you pre-approved for the maximum purchase price without considering your lifestyle, personal budget, employment and financial situation.
+ Recommend quick and dirty financing fixes that end-up being costly propositions over the long-term.
+ Isolate your financing solution as a standalone operation without considering its impacts on your overall financial plan.

Instead in my practice I FOCUS on:
+ Helping you plan early so you can be ready and prepared.
+ Getting you pre-approved for the financing that you can truly afford.
+ Aligning your mortgage strategy with your overall financial plan.
+ Showing you how to use your mortgage as a financial instrument to build your net worth.

As part of this process, my team provides mortgage planning services by working in elements of tax planning, financial planning and estate planning to deliver a strategic mortgage solution.
