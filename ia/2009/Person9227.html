Alina Brown’s Summary

The Murphy-Goode Movement is an interactive adventure to help Alina get a really good job as Murphy-Goode’s new Wine Country Lifestyle Correspondent. Nothing serious, just a healthy tablespoon of fun combined with a dash of driving, a handful of video and a large bottle of wine.

What does this mean? Alina is going on a mobile job quest that begins in Bellingham, Washington, and ends at the Murphy-Goode Tasting Room in Healdsburg, California. She wants to do such a good job documenting her journey through video, photo, blogging and getting people involved that Murphy-Goode has no choice but to hire her.

Alina hopes that friends, family, wine-lovers, fellow job seekers and those with a spirit of adventure will join her. Post comments, send tweets, be a friend or meet up in person - there are many ways to join the movement!
