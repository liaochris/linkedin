Rob Schmansky’s Summary

Goals:
-Client-centered advice and solutions
-Conflict-free financial advice to help you make responsible financial choices

Professional Experience:
Committed to giving individuals and families the information they need to make appropriate financial choices, Rob practices a holistic approach to financial planning.

A lifelong student and teacher of personal finance and economics, Rob’s goal is to educate individuals to make better financial decisions when it comes to investing and their retirement.

Being able to draw on industry experience in many areas of financial services, Rob has a unique view of the often conflicting goals of families and the service providers they go to for financial products and advice.

Rob is regularly quoted in The Journal of Financial Planning, MarketWatch and Robert Powell’s Retirement Weekly, Investment News, Financial Planning Magazine, On Wall Street, Financial Advisor Magazine, and many other industry and consumer publications.

Rob Schmansky’s Specialties:

Comprehensive financial counseling; independent second opinions; investment, insurance and tax planning.
