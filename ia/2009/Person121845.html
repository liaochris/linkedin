Joy Rasmussen’s Summary

Listed as one of the top 5% producing realtors in Steamboat Springs, Colorado. Specializes in mixed use and multi-family dwellings, both commercial and residential. Listing Broker for the following developments both past and present in Steamboat: The Porches (64 unit townhomes), Howelsen Place (37 condominiums with commercial space), Alpen Glow (14 condominums with commercial space), and EcoCorral (28 sustainable, green townhomes) of Steamboat. Instrumental in developing marketing plans and launches for multi-million dollar developments, and training staff to assist in the sales effort of residential townhome and condominium properties.
