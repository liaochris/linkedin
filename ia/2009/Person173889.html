Nicholas Parker’s Summary

Nicholas Parker co-founded the Cleantech Group, introducing the cleantech concept to the investment and business community in 2002. Previously Nicholas accumulated over 15 years experience starting and investing venture funds worldwide through limited partnerships, family offices, corporate funds and endowments. During this time, he pioneered the first "sustainability" driven private equity funds and participated in one of the first solar IPOs. In the 1990s, he also founded, built and sold an environmental finance firm. He has served as an advisor to multilateral agencies and major corporations.

Publications Include:

Managing Editor, Cleantech Venture Monitor (including writing quarterly articles on trends in energy and environmental investment) 2002 - current

“Venture Capital for Clean Technology Commercialization”, Climate Change – Mobilizing Global Effort, OECD, 1997.

Financing Cleaner Production - A Guide, United Nations Environment Programme (Industry Office) 1995.

"Financing the Transfer of Environmentally Sound Technology", UN Department for Policy Coordination and Sustainable Development, New York, January 1995.

"Financing Clean Energy Development - the Need for Innovation", World Energy Council Journal, July 1994

"Managing the Search for Partners", Collaboration Management: New Project & Partnering Techniques, J. Wiley & Sons, London 1994

Investing in Emerging Economies, Economist Intelligence Unit. London 1993

More than 150 presentations to conferences on technology, sustainability and investment.

Nicholas Parker’s Experience

  • Founder & Executive Chairman

    The Cleantech Group

    (Environmental Services industry)

    January 2002 — Present (7 years 11 months)

    Co-founded with private investors a market facilitation service for investors, entrepreneurs and service providers. Cleantech (www.cleantech.com) organizes venture forums, publishes the Venture Monitor and provides online member benefits. In the first two years of activity, Cleantech has held three highly successful events, tracked and published information on over US $2.4 billion of transactions, recruited over 300 members, sourced nearly 450 deals and achieved an operating profit.

  • Chairman

    Parker Venture Management Inc.

    (Privately Held; Environmental Services industry)

    January 2002 — Present (7 years 11 months)

    Parker Venture’s mission is to provide high value venture services to investors seeking superior returns from emerging clean technology enterprises. Currently advising on the deployment of over C$400M in assets for a corporate venture fund, a government-supported endowment, a major North American bank and a family-controlled investment group.

  • Co-founder & Principal

    SAM Equity Partners Ltd

    (Privately Held; Environmental Services industry)

    January 1999 — December 2001 (3 years )

    Recruited by Sustainable Asset Management (www.sam-group.com) to establish a transatlantic venture capital program focused on clean energy and resource productivity - the first of its kind, and to lead investment activities in North America. Accomplishments include:

    • Raising capital for two limited partnerships – SAM Sustainability Private Equity LP and SAM Energy Private Equity Fund LP – with combined capital commitments in excess of €90.0 million, including €38.5 million from North American institutions.
    • Completing 5 transactions (solar, fuel cells), including 1 IPO: sourcing over 350 potential investments
    • Building an investment management team: designing investment strategy, operational procedures and a web-based communications plan
    • Establishing a network of business, research and investment partners in Asia, Europe and North America
    • Establishing a Boston office and related infrastructure

  • Senior Vice-President

    Technology Development Corporation

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1996 — August 1998 (2 years 8 months)

    TDC (formerly Environmental Capital Corporation) is a private investment company specializing in the commercialization of “platform” technologies. TDC's directors, including majority shareholder Maurice Strong, are world leaders in the fields of business and sustainable development. While serving as “deputy CEO”, my achievements included:

    • Formulation of strategy and preparation of documentation for a successful US$15.0 million leveraged capitalization
    • Leading the execution and monitoring of investments in advanced materials, biotechnology and software companies
    • Creating partnerships with leading institutions such as MIT to facilitate technology transfer and commercialization.
    • Conducting due diligence and, where appropriate, negotiations on over 70 technology related investment opportunities

  • President/Chairman & Managing Director

    The Delphi Group/Delphi International Ltd.

    (Management Consulting industry)

    January 1988 — December 1995 (8 years )

    Served as founding President of The Delphi Group (www.delphi.ca) – a Canadian environmental consulting services firm, and then as Chairman and Managing Director of Delphi International, a UK based affiliate. DI is a pioneer in investment strategy and corporate finance for energy and environment-related technology commercialization (www.delphi-international.co.uk). Profitably sold my interest in both units in 1996.

    From 1993 to 1995, was seconded part-time to the (World) Business Council for Sustainable Development (www.wbcsd.com) where I devised technology cooperation and public-private partnership initiatives, as well as advising the Asian Development Bank on the creation of a US$150 million sustainable technologies fund

    From 1991 to 1993, was a Managing Director of SB Capital International, a Delphi joint venture with what was one of Canada's best-known investment banking and venture capital companies. During this period, originated and lead the development of the first Indian private equity fund targeting technology-based joint ventures between local and international companies.
