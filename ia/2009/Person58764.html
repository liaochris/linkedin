David Scheffler’s Summary

As the Director of Recruitment Compliance and Process Engineering, I lead the recruitment compliance practice at HRworks that consults with clients on how to interpret the new OFCCP regulations and what impact it has on their recruitment department. Where HRworks is different than other Affirmative Action vendors, is that we don’t stop at providing a summary of where the problem areas exist, we partner with our clients to create a strategy to maximize their internal recruitment team and technology while complying with the latest OFCCP regulations. The recruitment compliance department also provides consulting on recruitment process engineering, applicant tracking system selection assistance, recruitment/OFCCP compliance training and recruitment compliance audits. In addition to heading up the recruitment compliance division of HRworks, I also lead the process engineer group which partners with business development to map out the recruitment process and define the success metrics for each project. After the requirements for the project have been gathered the process engineers assist the recruitment team in creating the training material that outlines how the client’s applicant tracking system will be integrated into the overall recruitment process.

I have worked with many Fortune 500 companies in my career which a small sample would include TI, SunTrust, Jacobs Engineering, Waste Management, CSX, Nokia, HP, Cingular, IBM, Countrywide, The Home Depot, ADP, and Starbucks. In the past I have led sales engineering teams and sales teams that provide professional services as well as software.
