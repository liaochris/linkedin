Reto Stöcklin’s Summary

Reto Stöcklin is an entrepreneur. He is the owner and head of Atheris Laboratories, a research driven company he founded in Geneva in 1995 (http://www.atheris.com). Today, Atheris is a dynamic biotechnology company with a staff of 20, highly qualified scientists. It is based on a strong technology platform, proprietary databases and a unique expertise in developing MS-based metabolic studies of peptides and in exploiting bioactive molecules from venomous animals and other natural sources. Over the years, Atheris has developed a unique discovery platform that allows for extremely fast and targeted drug discovery projects. Atheris also initiated and hosts the “P4M” proteomics for myelin platform for biomarker discovery. More recently, Atheris has launched Melusine, natural libraries of venom components on microplates, readymade for high throughput screening (http://www.melusine.com).

Dr Stöcklin is also founder and president of “FunZyme BioTechnologies S.A.”, an innovative biotech company exploiting original enzymes of fungal origins (http://www.funzyme.com). He is co-inventor of an automated high throughput screening robot for ion channels and trans-membrane proteins, which led to the creation of HiQScreen SàRL, of which he is one of the four founders (http://www.hiqscreen.com). In 2006, he co-founded the Toxinomics Foundation in which he acts as General Secretary (http://www.toxinomics.org), and he co-founded the Swiss Proteomics Society back in 2000. He is the coordinator of the “CONCO cone snail genome project for health” project (http://www.conco.eu) and was elected in an advisory board of the French Institute of Research for the Development (IRD). Among other assignments, Reto Stöcklin has teaching functions in Geneva and Paris and is organiser of the “Natural Peptides to Drugs - NP2D” international congress in Zermatt (http://www.np2d.com). He is also highly active as a consultant and advisor for several international institutions and companies.
