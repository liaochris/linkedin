Alexandra Weber Morales’s Summary

Content strategist and social media maven with over 15 years’ experience entertaining diverse communities via blogging, journalism, editing, website development, multimedia and live events. Proven creative leadership and skills (including sponsored posters, webcasts, industry award programs, events, podcasts, blogs, e-newsletters, show dailies and reader games). Seasoned public speaker.
