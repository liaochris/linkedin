Gaia Morelli’s Summary

I am currently working as a consultant to Vice-Director Roberto Reale of the all-news satellite channel Rainews24 within RAI, the Italian national broadcasting company. My work is to act as interpreter of news in english language via the Internet, to widen access to first hand information on a variety of news topics. These are subject of an in-depth analysis on a programme aired weekly, built exclusively on video contents available on the Internet.

Previously I was assistant to the editor in chief in the same channel, specifically appointed to produce and overview part of the content of the afternoon magazines. I was supervising the editing of 25-minute long pieces aired in the 24hrs. I also assisted journalists with documentation, in particular internet research, given my strong background with an Internet Service Provider at young age.

Before that I worked with a small independent film production group in Italy (www.spaceoff.it). There I was also involved, together with my design company (www.21lab.eu), in the production of a 12-episode sci-fi mini-series, aired on FOX (Sky). At the same time, I was freelancing as a web designer (HTML) and graphic designer.

I am used to work cross-platforms and have a good sense of timing and timeline management. I am familiar with the editing and cutting of content and adaptation to satisfy a given concept or line of production guideline. As an art and design graduate I am familiar with the creative process and are prepared to apply it to any multimedia situation. As a natural mediator I like to see many different identities come together and act for a common goal.
