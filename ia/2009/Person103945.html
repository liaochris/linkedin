Janice B. Gonzalez’s Summary

Since 1985, I have worked in every aspect of marketing including planning, public relations, production, copy writing, graphic design, radio and television production, speech writing, crisis management, SEO, marketing plans and strategic planning for various clients.

My experience gives me a broad range of understanding that allows for proper advice, management, and strategic planning for clients. This experience ensures that all planning is done effectively, timely, with confidence, and in a cost effective manner – qualities that clients seek when hiring a professional marketer.

Over the years I have developed close ties with the business and legal community in Florida that have allowed me to develop relationships with the Florida Bar, State & Federal judges, various local bar organizations and related journalists, editors and producers. These relationships have proven to be valuable and have earned me recognition as an effective strategic planner and publicist within the legal profession and organizations that cater to that niche.

Additionally my experience in strategic marketing has been called upon by many industries, such as real estate, during difficult “marketing times” to help develop targeted marketing, advertising & public relation plans to help increase sales. My success has earned me the reputation of an effective “crisis” marketer.

As a publicist, I have obtained interviews for my clients in national media that include: 20/20; Larry King Live; ABC News; NBC Nightly News; The Today Show – NBC; CBS News; Hoy en El Mundo Telemundo National News; Sin Fronteras Spanish Magazine Show for Telemundo; Cristina – Spanish Talk Show Univision; Occurio Haci – Spanish Magazine Show; De Mañanitas – National Morning News/Interview Show Telemundo; People Magazine; The New York Times; The Wall Street Journal; The Washington Post; Los Angeles Times; National Law Journal; National Public Radio; and a variety of local print, radio and television interviews.

Janice B. Gonzalez’s Specialties:

I specialize in creating strategic marketing plans that provide the map for the public relations, media and advertising campaigns that ultimately lead to growth - whether it be sales or business developement.

Often I am called upon to "fix" the marketing of a company and create an in-depth marketing plan that includes budgets, plans and forecasts. This plan is the core of what makes the PR and/or advertising successful. Without it, your just guessing.
