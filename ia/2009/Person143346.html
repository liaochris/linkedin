Lonnie Marts Jr’s Summary

Lonnie Marts spent eleven years in the National Football League (NFL) playing for four different franchises in his career. He was a hard hitting Linebacker that finished his career with 518 tackles, 17 sacks, 16 forced fumbles, and 5 interceptions. Lonnie also excelled at special teams. He once registered 15 tackles in one game. He began his career with the Kansas City Chiefs and then moved on to the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, Tennessee Titans and finished his career with the Jacksonville Jaguars. He is currently the owner of GodSpeed Sports Performance and resides in Jacksonville, Florida with his wife Gionne and five children Gilon, Lonnie III, Gavin, Moriah and Taylor. Lonnie entered the NFL as an undrafted rookie and lived his football dream. With his professional training you can be on the right track to living yours!

Lonnie Marts Jr’s Specialties:

Lonnie is Jacksonville's only trainer invited to Nike's Football Training Camp Tour (NFTC). This elite list of trainers develop the nation's top athletes from the USA. Lonnie Marts Jr’s Experience

  • Owner

    Godspeed Sports Performance

    (Sports industry)

    August 2005 — Present (4 years 3 months)

    Godspeed Sports Performance is a Faith based company dedicated to mentoring and training young athletes to rise above mediocrity both spiritually and physically. Godspeed offers sports specific speed and agility training for boys and girls. Instruction is broken into three tiers. Tier one is our KIDZSPEED program for ages 7-13. This is a fun focused athletic enhancement training program for young athletes through the use of “play” based drills and games. Tier two is our PROSPEED program for young men and women ages 15 and Up. PROSPEED provides professional training for male and female middle and high school athletes. This program develops linear and lateral speed utilizing drills that enhance running mechanics, power and sport specific movement patterns. Athletes must be dedicated! The third tier program is our ONESPEED program. This program provides private, one-on-one instruction for ages 12 and up. ONE SPEED is highly individualized, sports specific training that evaluates strengths and weaknesses and enhances both. Coach, parent and athlete collaborate to assess needs and set goals. Godspeed has been given a charge to "Impact the lives of the next generation of athletes." With this in mind, our goals are to offer safe and practical training and to develop well-ballanced athletes who are equipped and empowered with life skills on and off the field.
