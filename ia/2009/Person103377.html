Jamie Stone’s Summary

My experience in Industrial Design has come largely in the FMCG market, and as such, my main skill base lies in the design and management of projects that aim to get products to the market with speed, whilst having the knowledge base to understand the technical, economic and geographical constraints that comes with designing for global mass production and consumption.
My role The Brewery is as head of design, providing leadership, creative direction and strategic thinking to the teams on all of the studio projects.
