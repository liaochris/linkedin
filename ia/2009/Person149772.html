Margaret Dixon’s Summary

Margaret Dixon is passionate about facilitating successful business and leadership transformations with CEOs and leaders who run companies. Her 30-year career includes starting companies and growing them to extraordinary success. Her most recent company achieved over one billion in annual sales four years after start-up. She is a visionary thinker and dynamic leader dedicated to serving others. Margaret has the ability to communicate clear vision and purpose combined with excellence and core values. She is committed to helping successful leaders achieve breakthrough results for themselves, their teams, and their organizations.

Margaret Dixon is dedicated to giving back and has been recognized for her philanthropic endeavors.
