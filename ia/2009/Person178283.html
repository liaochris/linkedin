Oliver Hewitt’s Summary

After graduating from Manchester Metropolitan University in 2002 with a degree in Marketing Management. He was selected for the graduate programme at MBNA Bank, where he enjoyed two successful years performing managerial roles in telemarketing and relationship marketing.

Oliver began his career in recruitment with Michael Page Marketing, where he specialised in the IT / Telecoms industry within the North West. On the basis of his success in this market, he was approached by Better Placed Recruitment in order to promote their presence in that industry, and also play a key role in the growth and development of the Manchester office. Oliver joined Better Placed in March 2007, working with a large number of high profile national and international technical organisations across the UK.

Oliver most recently has joined FPSG to bulid the Sales and Marketing function for the North and the North West.

Oliver Hewitt’s Specialties:

Recruitment within the IT / Telecommunications and consumer durables industry at all levels
