Rick Armbrust’s Summary

Very experienced in online media and advertising space with experiences across traditional IT and media. I have functional experience in marketing, engineering, and strategy at Microsoft, Tribune Corp, and Northwestern University. I have championed unknown business opportunities, managed a worldwide product launch, successfully navigated controversial projects, and became a thought leader in highly visible strategic businesses.
