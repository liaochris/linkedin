Kat Schneider Fotheringham’s Summary

The Corporate Housing Network (www.TCHNetwork.com) has changed the way the short-term, furnished housing industry does business. TCHNetwork.com offers real time availability options for those in need of temporary housing solutions. Our international network of temporary corporate housing providers offer furnished accommodations -- from apartments and condos, to single family homes and vacation rentals -- all over the world.

Our extensive network -- including only the most respected corp. housing companies and private owners, worldwide -- ensures that renters consistently receive the comforts and conveniences they expect, while remaining within their budget.

www.TCHNetwork.com maintains a 1st page, Organic, Search Result ranking for major industry search terms, and has for over two (2) years.
