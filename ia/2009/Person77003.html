Eric Wiesen’s Summary

Eric is a Principal with RRE Ventures, a leading IT-focused venture capital fund in New York City.

Earlier in his career, Eric was a two-time technology entrepreneur and a Silicon Valley lawyer at the law firm of Fenwick & West, where he focused on both startup companies as well as the transactions of more mature technology firms. Eric brings his operating and transactional experience to his work as an investor.

Eric holds a BA from the University of California at Berkeley, a JD from the University of Michigan, and an MBA from Columbia University. He is a member of the Kauffman Fellows program.
