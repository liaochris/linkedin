Teresa Wu’s Summary

I’m a University of California San Diego Communication major with a passion for social media and online communities. My diverse media background includes internships in broadcasting at an international news station, in editorial at Glamour magazine, and in online media at mediabistro.com. I’m a strong oral and written communicator who's knowledgeable about emerging web 2.0 technology and online media trends.

Currently, I'm managing editor and columnist at the UCSD Guardian, my university's official newspaper, and a social media intern at Unigo.com. I've also contributed to CollegeOTR.com, Asiance Magazine, and Glamour.com.

I’m excited that nine years of blogging and spending my life on the internet are turning out to be relevant to my career!

I would love to connect with you. Feel free to email me at teresawu(at)gmail.com — let's find out how we can help each other!
