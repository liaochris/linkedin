Mark Heller’s Summary

Over 13 years of online advertising sales and marketing experience. Trusted advisor to both advertising agencies and brand marketers. Has worked for and consulted with large publicly traded companies and small privately held start ups on how to effectively develop and present advertising solutions to agencies and brands. Currently consults on cost effective marketing solutions for marketers of all kinds and has established personal relationships with many of the top buyers with the largest online ad agencies across the world. Mark is efficient in establishing campaigns for many and has had deep involvement on both branding and roi campaigns.

Mark prides himself on his relationships and currently works with and for organizations that have employed him dating back to 1995. "I like the fact that I can pick up a phone call and have a conversation with someone I have done business with in the past. Relationships are key in this industry."

Mark Heller takes personal joy in making an unknown to a known and has many resources to bring a web property to the forefront of the marketers mind by utilizing old school ways of business in a new school environment.
