Cody Bateman’s Summary

For the past two decades, Cody Bateman has served as an advisor to corporations and individuals around the world. A recognized authority on operational improvement, business intelligence, and customer relationship management, he has been honored consistently for both his strategic intellect and humanitarian endeavors. Cody has worked directly with over one hundred corporations and non-profits and impacted thousands more with his articles, multimedia, public speaking engagements, and live events. His client experience spans many industries including financial services, energy, healthcare, technology, logistics, telecommunications, retail and governmental services.

Cody's operations strategy expertise was built from over 20 years progressive experience leading large scale performance improvement deployments for multinational product and service companies. He has experience leading operational assessments and linking corporate strategy to focused improvement work for key client value streams. His deep expertise and engaging style have galvanized client leadership teams to challenge the status quo and drive substantive operational changes.

The Kinetic Incremental Warehouse Implementations (KIWI) management technique and methodology he created in May 1994 has been accepted worldwide as the optimal approach to building enterprise data warehouses. His Service-Oriented Organizational Design Approach (SOODA), developed in June 1996, continues to provide executives with multi-dimensional views of their organization and provides a foundation for organizational redesign for optimal performance.

His nonprofit, Kaleidacare, provides both financial and management assistance to numerous non-profits primarily focused on caring for children who are victims of neglect and abuse or have been institutionalized. Cody is also a board member and regular speaker for Philanthropy and Volunteerism in Education (PAVE).
