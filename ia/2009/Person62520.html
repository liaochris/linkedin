Devin Balkind’s Summary

My area of expertise is web tool sets and online news and information services. I spent the last few summers working at a private equity firm in New York that specializes in new media. I also study the subject tangentially at Northwestern and have advised a few companies in the field outside of my private equity gig.

I've started, along with a few friends and a $7,000 grant from Kellogg Business School, an online fund raising tools company called BeExtraordinary.org.

BeExtraordinary.org creates online tools that make fund raising for non-profits more effective, more fun and more social. Users create a 'challenge for charity' on our site (ex. I'll shave my head if my friends raise $1000 for CARE International) and we integrate that challenge into their social web (facebook, myspace, etc). We also provide a variety of services for corporations and businesses that want to get the exposure they deserve for their generous charitable contributions.
I spend a few hours everyday reading about trends in web powered new media and really love doing it. I don't know why... I guess I'm a little weird.
