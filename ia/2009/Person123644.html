Julia Ponce’s Summary

Julia Ponce is an International Development, Labor and Corporate Social Responsibility specialist with 8 years experience in the public, private and non-governmental sectors; currently working to promote social dialogue and labor rights in Central America. She coordinated the revision of the SA8000 standard for workplaces, a global benchmark based on international human rights norms and the conventions of the ILO, in use by 1582 company facilities in 62 countries and industries to measure their performance. As Program Associate of the USAID funded project Continuous Improvement for the Central America Workplace (CIMCAW), Julia Ponce has been working in close coordination with the Labor Ministries of El Salvador, Honduras, Nicaragua, Guatemala and the Dominican Republic to provide technical assistance to labor inspectors. She is responsible for the general oversight of Social Accountability International’s compliance with USDOL contract regulations.

Previously, Julia Ponce was responsible for providing alternative dispute resolution services to the residents of Nassau County, as Case Coordinator for New York State’s, Mediation Alternative Project (MAP) where she oversaw program management and quality, provided advocacy services, legal assistance, trained and evaluated a multidisciplinary team of 60 volunteer mediation professionals, and expanded MAP’s capacity to serve Spanish-speakers. Her private sector experience includes inventory administration and the development of inventory management system procedures for Stanton Carpet Corp., a US leader in broadloom manufacturing. She managed and oversaw US HAZMAT and BATF compliance for Island Pyrochemical Industries (IPI), a global specialty chemical supplier. She has trained staff in HAZMAT procedures and coordinated IPI’s procurement and shipping logistics of hazardous materials, chemicals, and explosives, from Asia, North Africa, Europe and Latin America.

Julia Ponce’s Specialties:

* SA8000 standard and social auditing training for labor inspectors with specialization in the labor codes of El Salvador, Dominican Republic and Guatemala.
* Arbitrator and Mediator specialized in facilitative and transformative mediation.

* Native Spanish speaker proficient in Spanish-English and English-Spanish translation and interpretation.
