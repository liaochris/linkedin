Paul Guckian’s Summary

Paul specialises in reviewing and implementing risk and control frameworks including governance forums, process design, risk assessments and improving management reporting.

Paul has developed ClockworkGRC (www.clockworkgrc.com), an integrated workflow and dashboard solution for implementing and managing IT risk and control frameworks. Together with the team at Delaney, Paul focuses on delivering practical and cost effective solutions to improve risk management, IT risk profiles and management reporting.

Paul has over 10 years experience in design and implementation roles for technology and information security specialising in UK financial services and public sector. Experience of working nationally and internationally with major clients like ABN Amro, RBS, HBOS, SwissRe, General Motors, Dunfermline Building Society, Grampian Country Foods, ABN Amro and other local government clients.
