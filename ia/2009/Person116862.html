John Dash’s Summary

People say I’m enthusiastic, friendly, creative, organised and focused.

I’ve worked at all levels in commercial radio, from running the programme department at one of the UK’s largest stations, Piccadilly Radio to being part of the team at one of the UK’s freshest new brands, Galaxy.

From Programme Director at Wave 105, the ntl Station Of The Year 2005 to Group Programme Director at UTV Radio directly responsible for the output of 17 very varied radio stations.

I’ve been involved at every level in marketing, promotions, sponsorship, research, news, sport, pr, regulation, music scheduling, HR, contractual negotiation, on-air presentation, event and programme management.

I’ve set up my own internal and external research projects, including on-line audience tracking, auditorium testing, phone-out research and focus groups as well as providing detailed RAJAR analysis. The outside companies I’ve worked with include BPR, Broadcast Architecture, EMR (SongPeople), XTra Research, Dipstick Surveys, Questionaire, Hallett-Arendt and OLE (Other Lines Of Enquiry).

In a social capacity, I’ve also held the position of chairman of the Variety Club Children’s Charity in South Wales taking a leading role as organiser of fundraising events.

I'm a keen contributor to industry events, including fronting presentations at Radio Active and Music Radio. I'm also delighted be a judge for the 2009 Sony Awards.

John Dash’s Specialties:

Programming, marketing, research, coaching and development in radio.
