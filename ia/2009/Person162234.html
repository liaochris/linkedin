Michael Stewman’s Summary

9+ years in the industry doing digital design and animation. Initially, I started as a flash animator and worked for a couple years doing mostly web sites and demos. Soon afterwards I started my own freelance business with a partner, specializing in web development while also working part time at a video editing company. I've now been in San Francisco for the last 6 years working mostly in advertising and marketing. I had the amazing opportunity in 07 to be the 6th employee of an industry leader in mobile marketing. As the principal creative lead, I was in charge of: brand identity, print materials, mobile ad creation, wap site development, software and ui design as well as execution of all online collateral/demos. Finally, In late 07, I started my own business - Santa Cruz Studios, a full service creative shop.
