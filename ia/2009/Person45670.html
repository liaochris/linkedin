Chris Uzzo’s Summary

I am a search engine marketing (SEO/SEM) specialist with a broad range of web design & marketing talents. My focus is to achieve the highest return on investment for our clients through strategic multi-channel marketing (online & offline).

I first started my career in web design. I am fluent in Photoshop, Dreamweaver, and many other design programs. I am also fluent in programming languages such as HTML, ASP, PERL, PHP, VBScript, CSS, C++, SQL, and more. My skills in web design have prepared me for search marketing because I am now able to dissect a web site to see if it is search engine friendly by looking at the code, and from a marketing standpoint by looking at the design. It is important to find the right balance of both aesthetics and effective code on any site that we are performing search marketing.

We go to conferences on search marketing and internet marketing several times per year. These conferences keep us on top of all of the latest trends and tools in the industry. We also participate in several different professional groups to share ideas on internet marketing and to foster new relationships in the industry. Search marketing is ever changing. Tools and strategies from five or ten years ago may no longer be effective. Social Media and Social Networks are emerging as the wave of the future. We plan to stay ahead of the curve by understanding these concepts and using now, before most traditional marketing firms.
