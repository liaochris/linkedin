Charlie Ragland’s Summary

Teaching: Professionally qualified faculty at the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga College of Business in Management, Marketing, and Entrepreneurship. Courses included Strategic Management, New Venture Creation, Small Business Entrepreneurship (MBA), International Management (MBA), Business-to-Business Marketing, Selling & Sales Management, Business Communications, and Basic Marketing.

Consulting: Sales, marketing, and commercialization activities with a range of companies and individuals. Clients include GE Plastics, BASF, Saint-Gobain Vetrotex America, Owens-Corning, Avery Dennison, Polymer Group, Microwave Synergy, and Robert Webb & Associates.

Training & Development: Business Development and Commercialization, Market Opportunity Assessment, Developing Winning Business Plans, Communicating to Win, and Strategic Planning Process.

Published articles: Jumpstart Your Plan for Business Performance, Driving Growth in Today’s Business Climate, Navigating the Financial Jungle, Developing and Managing Your Banking Relationship, Chapter 11 Bankruptcy: Impact, Implications, and Strategy.

Entrepreneurial activity: Board of Directors at the Chattanooga Technology Council and steering committee for the Capital Connection.

Charlie Ragland’s Specialties:

Primary market research in chemical and plastics industry, strategic planning, business plans, B2B sales & market strategy, new product development and commercialization strategy.
