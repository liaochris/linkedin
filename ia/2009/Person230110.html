Ted Canaday’s Summary

I believe in the power of imagination, and its limitless ability to creatively address ANY challenge.

I help clients achieve creative breakthroughs in their strategy, marketing & operations.

What does a "creative breakthrough" look like?

• helping SalesPartners-Troy develop & implement a social network web platform as a value-added benefit for their exclusive clients
• helping the Charles H. Wright Museum of African American History adopt & implement and online project management system to increase collaboration within & across departments.
• helping launch, run & lead new media marketing company Elemental Detroit, which generated $3 million in revenue with no start-up capital, landed national clients like Compuware and Deloitte & Touche, won Crain's Small Business Award & was named one of Michigan's Best Places to Work by Corp! Magazine.

I’m an 8-time Detroit Music Award winner and member of Crain's 40 Under 40 Class of 2008 whose creative & business experience helps clients not only think outside of the box, but crush it to a pulp, kick it to the curb & relegate it to the recycling bin where it belongs.

Creativity is a virtue. Let me help inspire your imagination.

Ted Canaday’s Specialties:

Creative development & direction, strategic planning, audio production & post, original music composition, musical performance, video direction, copy writing, operational assessment, organizational planning & management, social media, change agency, culture, team building, facilitation & mediation, project management, speaking, presenting, training
