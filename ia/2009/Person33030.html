Brian Allan’s Summary

Brian has been involved in emerging technology for over twenty years. Throughout his career he has been a proven business builder and market rainmaker in the Software as a Service (SaaS), Web 2.0, and mobile technology space. He has held executive roles including General Manager, Chief Marketing Officer, VP Sales, and President at several top technology companies including P&L responsibilities to $100M. Brian has significant experience leading high technology companies from start-up through sustainable growth including fund-raising, operational planning, negotiation, business development and sales execution. He has deep domain knowledge of ‘on-demand’ business models including digital marketing best practices, closed-loop systems, and SaaS organizational structures.
