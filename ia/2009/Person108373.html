Jeff Lewis’s Summary

Jeff Lewis has over 20 years experience in corporate finance and project management. Upon earning his MBA in 1988, Jeff has worked with technology companies thoughout his career; bringing an efficiency-through-technology mentality to his endeavors. His approach, however, is to stick with fundamental business principles as the basis from which to truly build value. At 1st Discovery, Jeff maintains that short-cuts and quick-fixes tear down business value and is, therefore, insistent upon accepting business that is clearly win-win for both 1st Discovery and its clients. Technology is applied where appropriate; it is never imposed on projects for the sake of 'dressing up' the perceived value of 1st Discovery offerings.
