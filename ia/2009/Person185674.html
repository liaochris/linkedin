Peter Himler’s Summary

Peter is founder and principal of Flatiron Communications LLC, a PR/media consulting firm in New York. He most recently served as Chief Media Officer for Edelman Worldwide, following 11 years with Burson-Marsteller as head of the agency's U.S. corporate media team and its worldwide spokesperson. Prior to B-M, Peter was media director for six years at Cohn & Wolfe, which followed five years at Hill and Knowlton where he led H&K's broadcast media relations group. He began his career with three years at the NYC entertainment PR firm of Robert Zarem Inc.

Peter Himler’s Specialties:

Traditional and social media strategy, public relations, crisis management, communications training, speechwriting, digital video production, SEM/SEO, blogger engagement...
