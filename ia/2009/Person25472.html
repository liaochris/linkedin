Becki Rowe’s Summary

A confident, highly motivated and creative Senior Sales and Marketing Manager working in retail for over 10 years. In the last 5 years I have helped two retailers by opening 29 stores in total, launching a new route to market, rebranding a retail channel and building sales and marketing teams from scratch.

I have strong organisational, leadership and planning skills and a proven ability to drive sales. I am described by colleagues as tenacious, analytical, dynamic, and entrepreneurial.

I can demonstrate solid experience and significant achievements in all areas of retail sales, operations and marketing across the fashion, beauty and premium confectionary sectors.

Known for my drive, determination and willingness to challenge entrenched practices and provide creative solutions, I operate daily from shop floor through to director level.

As a consultant, I am looking to bring my considerable skills and experience to retailers where I have the opportunity to make a pronounced contribution to the success of their brand.
