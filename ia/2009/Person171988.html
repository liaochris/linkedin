Nathaniel Montgomery’s Summary

Nathaniel has several years experience in both “Make a Wish” and “AmeriCorps” organizations. Through his past experiences working with nonprofits it has lead to the development of skills in Project Management, Volunteer Recruitment, Facilitation, and Programs Development resulting in projects designed to empower individuals through education and fostering of their own leadership skills. Nathaniel has also independently started a health-based 501(c)3 nonprofit called “A Single Light” (www.asinglelight.org) designed to supply information to cancer patients.
