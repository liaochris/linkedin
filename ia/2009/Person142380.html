Lisa Wood’s Summary

1) Turn-key custom publisher of BUSINESSWEEK special ad sections that focus on EFCA, cloud computing, biotechnology, alternative energy, cleantech, nanotechnology, corporate social responsibility and other topics of interest to business decision-makers.

2) Animations and imagery. FVG's production cycles are accelerated and client costs are reduced as compared to traditional labor intensive CGI animation studios.

3) Analysis and design of molecules of any size and complexity. Such extreme resolution is unprecedented in [all of] the life sciences.

In addition to 14 years of serious sweat equity, considerable revenue from Lisa's endeavors with BusinessWeek (and throughout the 90's with Fortune) have served to fund the R&D, validation programs, and commercialization of patent-pending algorithms which apply a natural law discovery (of the semiclassical intersection of quantum mechanics and classical mechanics). This breakthrough simplifies how the conformational dynamics and particle energetics of biological molecules and small molecule therapeutics are understood.
