Paula Asinof’s Summary

I am the founder of Yellow Brick Path, a resume expert, and an authority on essential career documents. As a skilled career coach, my resume services help executives create professional resumes that "speak" to their target audiences. These resumes are designed using leading-edge and proprietary resume processes to develop resumes with a clear career focus. These resume services specialize in building resumes that address positioning and personal branding and handle Internet and special resume formats. My clients are fully engaged in the resume development process so they can modify their own resumes for specific jobs or as the market changes. They also validate their resumes during the resume development process using a structured market test. Then, I work with clients to use their completed resumes to build bios and drive interview strategy. My resume services are available in-person in the Dallas area as well as by phone throughout the U.S. My background includes corporate leadership roles in IT and finance underlying extensive experience in executive search, career services, and career coaching. I have a Wharton MBA and expect to receive the Credentialed Career Manager (CCM) this year – a credential exclusively for career professionals who are leaders in the industry. My clients have often had bad experiences with resume writers or other resume services. I bring deep expertise to the resume development process and a methodology for resumes that sets my resume services part. The resume services I provide help clients create exceptional resumes and also provide a vehicle for engaged, interactive discovery about their careers, their futures, and themselves. Completing a resume or a set of career documents using my resume services is quick, affordable, and efficient. It is one of the best resume development values around.
