Manuj Bharti’s Summary

My name is Manuj Bharti and I am a young serial entrepreneur with specific interest in the Internet, Marketing, Networking, Web 2.0 and Generation ‘Y’. I enjoy focusing and working on business strategy and development, start-up growth, entrepreneur motivation and development, business management and marketing strategy.

Starting my first IT business at the age of 14, named it ‘techfundoo’ aka tech geek, was (infact still) too obsessed with the .com boom, I quickly experienced the excitement of the entrepreneurial world. The skills gained through founding and managing a business at such a young age enabled me to establish myself as a young entrepreneur.

Since then I have started an online electronics store, apparel store, and now working on three different projects, which should be live hopefully by this year, I have been consulting about Generation ‘Y’ and Web 2.0.
I love to work with Generation ‘Y’ entrepreneurs to turn ideas into reality.

I have a strong desire to continue building the business community with the use of my entrepreneurial spark. I am very keen to meet up with likeminded people.

My business partners complain my enthusiasm for the Internet and online business is infectious.
