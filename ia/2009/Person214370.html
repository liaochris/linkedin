Shawn Flanagan’s Summary

Shawn is a sales and marketing professional with experience in B2B and B2C sales in the areas of Hardware, Online Media, Health & Fitness, and Financial Services; He received his B.S. in Business Studies at Southern New Hampshire University and is currently an MBA candidate (expected 2010) at Babson College F.W. Olin Graduate School of Business.

As a business owner and entrepreneur, Shawn understands the needs of the small business owner. He has a reputation for honesty, commitment and dedication to his clients and candidates. Having the opportunity to positively impact the quality of someone’s life is his driving factor for top performance.

As an executive recruiter, Shawn differentiates himself by recruiting for the best possible candidate, not just the best available candidate. He looks forward to the opportunity to positively impact your business or professional career goals. He can be easily reached via phone at 603.669.9800 x 2118 or via email at sflanno@gmail.com .
