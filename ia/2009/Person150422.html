Marie Jeanne Chiriac’s Summary

I currently work as editor at Hotcity.ro, a great feminine urban culture website. I try to bring my experience as a women magazine editor as well as semi-socialite :-) to create a powerful community around Hotcity.
Because of the great passion i have for travel, I try not to lose my connection to the wonderful world of global traveling, keeping myself up-to-date with every movement in the industry. I also keep in touch with every field i have ever crossed paths with, from television to advertising. One of my strongest beliefs is that apart from the people who manage, market and sell, the weight of each industry lies on the shoulders of those who bang their heads against the desk in the torment of creation.
I try to never stop developing personal projects, some of them useful, some of them fun, as long as for me they are both. I am also a passionate blogger and a believer in the power of blogs.
You might have seen some of my work. If you never have, this is a great place to start.
