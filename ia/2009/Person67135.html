Dr. Everton Walters’s Summary

Dr. Everton Walters has over 29 years experience in Management, Project Management, and Consulting in the fields of solar systems, quality assurance/ quality control management, process control, electrical engineering, safety system, and communication systems.

He has demonstrated expertise in the ISO certification process, quality assurance, process control, solar systems design and installation, communication network design, computer networks, power system control, instrumentation, artificial intelligence, control system engineering, safety, and electrical design, as well as research and development.

He owned and operated an electrical engineering consulting firm where he designed systems and consulted on several projects.

He has proven ability to build and lead teams, work with customers, and effectively communicate technical concepts
