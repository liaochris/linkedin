Ian Sterritt’s Summary

Expert Innovation Mentor with extensive knowledge, capabilities and networks of contacts. Translating ideas into commercial reality, decreasing risk and increasing success rates. Using a proven, outcome focussed approach to innovation applicable across a broad spectrum of industries.

A unique perspective developing solutions that are tailored to your business’s specific requirements.

It’s not just what you know, but who you know. With a team of affiliated specialists, I can provide the right expertise for any particular project. Giving you access to the right innovation resources ranging from a business angel to a patent agent or a consumer behaviour expert.

Strategic business planning specialist, guest lecturer on the Doctorate of Innovation at Warwick University, registered mentor for Connect Midlands www.connectmidlands.org and The Princes Trust.
With extensive key account management experience, Chartered Marketer status and a first degree in Engineering Ian’s career has involved him in the UK launch of satellite navigation, the start-up of one of the first location-based mobile phone services, geographical information systems and software for emergency services. He has first hand understanding of the creation and building of innovation driven ventures.
