John Kaufman’s Summary

Extensive international wireless telecom performance management and strategy consulting experience with startups and large corporations.

John Kaufman’s Experience

  • Director of Operational Reviews

    Zain

    (Public Company; Telecommunications industry)

    December 2007 — January 2009 (1 year 2 months)

    This Group manages the financial performance of 15 sub-Saharan wireless telecom carriers with combined annual revenues of $6 billion and more than 30 million customers:
    • Negotiated cost structures for a new joint venture company which is creating a Pan African cell site sharing company with another major operator
    • Led the financial and operational performance measurement and analysis, budget and business planning reviews for 15 operating companies
    • Led team evaluating 3rd Generation (3G) and 4th Generation (WIMAX) network capital investment proposals
    • Co-project managed a one year cost benchmarking exercise of all the operations

  • Principal

    Redline Consulting

    (Management Consulting industry)

    January 2002 — December 2007 (6 years)

    Specializing in business development, new market entrant and market assessment:
    • Created and launched the US sales and distribution arm for a Spanish base station antenna vendor into national and regional wireless operators with sales in excess of $4 million in 1st year
    • Prepared a US antenna market assessment that resulted in the client starting operations.
    • Created strategic pricing models and promotions solutions to a Belgian broadband ISP
    • Using well established relationships, sold a wireless network optimization solution to European and American operators

  • Head of Mobility

    British Telecom Mobility, Japan

    (Wireless industry)

    September 1999 — September 2001 (2 years 1 month)

    BT investment vehicle in Japanese cellular market that was sold to Vodafone two years later resulting in a $3.5 billion profit:
    • Led the Japanese team that provided business planning and financial valuation expertise that secured the Board of Director’s initial investment in J Phone Communications
    • Led the team that prepared due diligence of J-Phone 5 year Business Plan
    • Member of negotiating team that reduced 3G network infrastructure costs from Ericsson and Nokia
    • Directed and facilitated knowledge and product transfer programs with J-Phone, hardware vendors and wireless internet service providers including early-phase Web 2.0 services

  • VP of Cellular Practice

    Kalba International

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    September 1996 — August 1999 (3 years)

    Provided strategic advice to operators, new market entrants and vendors in the wireless telecom space. Areas of expertise included business planning, valuations, due diligence, sales and marketing strategies, forecasting, market assessments and research, licensing, pricing and bidding. As team leader, managed groups of 1 to 7 consultants per project. Projects undertaken in the US, UK, Brazil, the Philippines, Vietnam.

  • Senior Associate

    Coopers and Lybrand

    (Partnership; 10,001 or more employees; Management Consulting industry)

    July 1994 — August 1996 (2 years 2 months)

    As a member of the Strategic Telecom Group in London, did valuations and due diligence of wireless and satellite operators, wireless license applications and bids, regulatory advice and business planning. Worked in USA, U.K., Germany, the Netherlands, Hungary, Italy, Brazil, Indonesia and Hong Kong.
