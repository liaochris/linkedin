Vesna Leonard’s Summary

I have had a passion for fitness for nearly 30 years. Recent life events have inspired me to move into a career in not only health and fitness, but life and goal planning. I am very interested in corporate health and fitness, as well as families and individuals. Health and fitness doesn't have to mean diet plans or gym memberships, but it should involve all aspects of life @work and @home, and be tailored to the individuals needs. I am incredibly excited about this new venture and am thoroughly enjoying working with and helping people on a daily basis.

I am also a Software Quality Assurance tester with focus on implementing, organizing and streamlining processes. Particularly interested in starting and building SQA processes and teams for start up or small to medium software companies. Specialty also in creating custom SQA solutions for developers who need testing, but do not know where or how to start. Highly interested in exploratory testing, as well as test automation, and classic testing practices (test cases/plans, etc.).

Vesna Leonard’s Specialties:

Health and Fitness: Fitness Plans, Goal Setting and Attainment, Life Planning and Balance....and everything in between!

Software: Process creation and implementation, classic testing using test cases/plans, SQA tools and administration of those tools to streamline processes.
