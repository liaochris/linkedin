Peter Svenneby’s Summary

My personal background is a broad combination of engineering, software engineering, product marketing, sales engineering, sales and sales management.

My current passion is to help both small and large companies improve their sales cultures and their sales effectiveness at all levels and throughout all departments that touch their customer - sales, product development, customer support, executive team, etc.

Professionally, my time is spent in pursuit of continuous improvement and growth in our effectiveness as a sales training organization, and in helping our valued clients realize their own visions of excellence.

Peter Svenneby’s Specialties:
