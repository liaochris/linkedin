David S. Waller’s Summary

David S. Waller is the Founder and CEO of Storeboard, LLC - an internet site that connects businesses and consumers faster and more efficiently. The site will launch in 2009. In this capacity, he benefits from more than 20 years of legal, business, investment, and real estate experience.

Formerly, David S. Waller was a real estate entrepreneur with a track record of developing distressed multi-family properties and turning them into highly profitable real estate investments. He was actively engaged in real estate rehabilitation for more than ten years. During that time, he repositioned more than 90 multi-family properties. He has extensive experience overseeing the construction and lease up of multi-family properties as well as condominium conversions and their subsequent sell out.

David S. Waller’s Specialties:

Management, Construction, Lease Up, Acquistions, Dispositions, Legal Oversight, Fundraising, Finance
