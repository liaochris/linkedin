Anthony Mongeluzo’s Summary

My background is in Information Systems, I have a degree from Rowan in Business Administration and I specialized in Management Information Systems. I also earned the MCP, MCSA (2000 and 2003), MCSE (2000 and 2003), MOS, A+, Net+, Server+, and Security+ certifications. My skills have been mostly self-taught.

I started Pro Computer Service in 2002 and we have been expanding ever since. We have a great team of people and our goal is to be the recognized #1 provider of IT Support and Web Services on the east coast. We have expanded to DE and as always are continuing to grow and look for more opportunities. Most recently, we have just expanded our Medford location nearly doubling in size and have moved our web team from Collingswood, NJ to our HQ in Medford, NJ. Our web team due to its success has now branched into a new company which I started in January 2009 called pilotLight Studios. Additionally, we added a new office in Buena, NJ in Feburary 2009.

With 8 years in the business and providing service to every industry imaginable, we have developed quite a good reputation in the area. Our reputation is so good that FOX and ABC news will reach out when they have computer stories to report on. We have taken on projects as diverse as managing companies with offices in multiple states, working with the State of NJ, and helping businesses with 5 or less employees achieve their goals. Our clients range from small businesses to companies with hundreds/thousands of employees.
