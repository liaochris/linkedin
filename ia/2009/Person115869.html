Joel Worst’s Summary

After Graduating from ODU in Environmental Science & Health I spent 2 more years working in the Golf Industry for KSL Fairways moving to 2 more Golf & Country Clubs within the companies east coast operations (Walden Lakes-Tampa, FL and Countryside-Roanoke, VA). I then discovered an opportunity (that my college degree actually prepared me for!) with an Environmental firm that provided transportation, storage, and disposal of industrial hazardous & non-hazardous waste to proper disposal via the EPA, VDoT, and State Water Control Board requirements. Then in February 1998 I began my IT career. I performed business development, marketing, and sales activities at the Department of the Army (PEO-STAMIS, now EIS), NIH, and some others. I did this for 7 years at UHD as the Director of the Integrated Solutions Division. In 2005 I accepted a position at a GDIT owned company performing IT consulting, delivery, & programmatics within PEO-EIS. At the beginning of 2007 I accepted an offer for marketing and business development at Capgemini, an industry cited (Gartner, IDC, etc) global leader in business transformation consulting & implementation. I was hired to develop business through Capgemini's eventual (early 2008) PEO-EIS PMSS2 contract award. Prior to Capgemini being awarded a PMSS2 Contract Vehicle I am tasked with knowing Capgemini's market differentiators and offerings for my transition back to developing business with the U.S. Army. I am currently marketing to Civilian Federal Agencies where neither Capgemini, nor myself, have relationships or name recognition.
