Shelley Gierman’s Summary

With more than 12 years of professional experience providing creative solutions for a variety of projects and clients, I specialize in organizing information into visually appealing, easy-to-read communications. Understanding my client's business objectives is always step one of my design process. I will then conceptualize, design and see the project through to production. I will also manage the printing, data merge and mailing processes if needed.

My experience with corporate branding initiatives includes logo development and creating extensive visual language standards to support a new brand. Or I can take your existing brand to the next level by implementing standards and incorporating brand elements across original photography, templates, newsletters and all other communication materials.

I work with a range of organizations from Fortune 500 companies to independently owned businesses. Having experience as an in-house graphic designer and art director for major corporations gives me an in-depth understanding of the marketing process from my clients' point of view. I am located in the Northeast Ohio area, however I work with clients across the country.

My style is straightforward and practical. I take pride in providing clients with an uncomplicated process for completing design projects in a cost-efficient and time-saving manner while still providing the best design solution for their needs.
