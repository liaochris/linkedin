Debbie Hill’s Summary

Debbie Hill has consulted start-up, medium and world class businesses since 1999 on effective Internet and search marketing approaches with a focus on increasing web property and digital asset value for the long-term by leveraging smart creative tactics that match budget allocations.

Project highlights include:
SEO training for GE Consumer & Industrial management, IT and marketing staff with a focus on GEAppliances.com, to increase organic exposure for broad generic product searches.

SEM/SEO strategic direction for RentalHouses.com growing monthly visits to 1 million, prior to acquisition in 2007 by a leading competitor owned by Primedia.

SEM/SEO campaigns with approximate traffic increases of 40% - 22,000% and conversion improvement for internet businesses and services, healthcare staffing, pharmaceutical products, specialty products, niche consumer products, property management, real estate, manufacturing and local service businesses.

Debbie Hill’s Specialties:

Debbie Hill has had tremendous success with search engine marketing (SEM), primarily in the area of leveraging search engine optimization (SEO) to build long-term traffic and lasting website value. Optimization is now expanding to include not only websites and social media but all digital assets, providing even more opportunities for online visibility.
