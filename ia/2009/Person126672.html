Kari J Young: www.kariyoung.com’s Interests:

In the last two years Kari discovered encaustic paints and fell head over heels in love! Encaustic paints are a mixture of bees wax, resin and pigment applied to wood boards with a heated iron, torch and brushes. "I have never felt so in touch with life's energy and our connection to it as I do now painting with this medium." "When I'm painting and experiencing 'Creative Flow' I connect to shapes that have to do with internal struggle and processing the events that happen in the world around us. I am drawn to natural forms like water, space, trees and I love the discovery of little creatures that pop up when the pieces have dried." "It is my hope that people will connect with my pieces in a way that brings them joy, healing and truth." All of Kari's paintings originate from her life experience, feelings, dreams, memories and imagination. She allows for the creative process to flow through her as she looks for narrative and deeper meaning in each piece she produces.
