Ernie Gilbert’s Summary

Moved to California in '92 to attend two years at CalArts. Since then have worked as an Animator, Character Designer, Design Supervisor, Visual Development Artist, Flash Animator, Assistant Director, and Art Director. Studios, include Nickelodeon, Cartoon Network, Warner Bros., Sony, American greetings, Ka-Chew!, Creative Capers, Entertainment...

Shows include the Fairly Oddparents, El Tigre, Foster's Home For Imaginary friends, Dexter's lab, Powerpuff Girls...

Film Credits include The Tigger Movie, My Brother The Pig, Mr. Magoo, The Very First Noel (character voices)...

I also enjoy doing theatre (when possible), singing, & playing ukulele.

Ernie Gilbert’s Specialties:

Singing, Voice-over, Ukulele, Juggling, Dialects (using & teaching)
