Tim Kallok’s Summary

Since I was young, I have always wanted to work as an animator. Later, when I found out that I could do animation as a career, I have worked very hard to learn as much as I could about animation and the process. Through my education, I have learned a lot about the principles and what makes good animation. I want to continue my education and make it a life-long learning experience. My goal is to work as a character animator in the entertainment industry and to someday teach the next generation of animators, so I can give back to the animation community.

Tim Kallok’s Specialties:

Character Animation, Modeling, Rigging, Dynamics, Texturing, Lighting, and Rendering.
