Erik Bauer’s Experience

  • Independent Film Producer

    Erik Bauer Productions (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Entertainment industry)

    January 2002 — Present (7 years 2 months)

    Erik is the producer of the feature film HARD SCRAMBLED (staring Kurtwood Smith, Richard Edson, and Eyal Podell), the film debut of Chicago playwright David Scott Hay. He is actively developing several future projects with Mr. Hay and other writer/directors (including Marty Martin, one of the contestants on the Fox TV reality show ON THE LOT).

  • Publisher

    CS Publications, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Publishing industry)

    January 1993 — April 2007 (14 years 4 months)

    Erik founded CREATIVE SCREENWRITING magazine in 1994 and remained its publisher until selling his business earlier this year. Creative Screenwriting continues to be one of the most widely distributed film periodicals in the United States.

    A writer and producer, Erik has garnered invaluable film industry contacts and experience in his fourteen years publishing Creative Screenwriting, developing several film industry products (including WriteWare, a CD-ROM of resources for screenwriters), more than 50 instructional DVDs on Screenwriting and Filmmaking, and the SCREENWRITING EXPO, the world’s largest trade show for writers (more than 4,000 attendees last year).
