Trina Alvero Iijima’s Summary

Originally from rural southwest Minnesota, Trina began her practice of law in Duluth, where she handled general litigation and insurance defense. Prior to her legal career, she worked for seven years in the financial sector, including work as an ASPA certified ERISA plan administrator and as a Series 7-licensed brokerage representative. Her longstanding commitment to community service has given her a broad range of experience, including local government work, strategic entrepreneurship initiatives, high school student mentorship, and the promotion of the arts.
