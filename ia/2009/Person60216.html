Debbie Burke’s Summary

I have lived in NW Indiana for 10 years and 9.5 of those years have been with Merrion & Associates Realtors! I got my realtor license in 1999 and my Brokers in 2006. In 2003 I was offered the opportunity to buy the business from the family - it took me about 3 weeks to make myself say yes and boy am I glad I did! I love it - even in todays market - all the unknowns are even more unknown.

As a small office in our community, 24 years, we give back as much as possible - we mentor school students, we help with meals on wheels, we work with various "homeless" groups, we enjoy helping out with "boys and girls club", we have helped Habitat for Humanity build a home and the Michigan City Chamber is also a favorite!

We moved our downtown office to Karwick Plaza, the beach area of Michigan City, in 2007 and have been extremely happy with our new digs!!!

The agents in our office are a team - they work with each other and they play well with agents of other offices. The agents in our office are best of the best in LaPorte County making our office a "A Place 4 U"!
