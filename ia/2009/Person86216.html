Bob Kerstetter’s Summary

As a ghostwriter I help you publish by engaging your audience without seeking any fame by means of your story. There is no "[Your Name] with [My Name]" credit line on your article, report or book. It's your content and your creation. My assistance is behind-the-scenes—working with you—collecting information, organizing, writing and publishing.

As a publishing consultant I help you produce beautiful published materials by using an cross-platform, open source typesetting system with a learning curve similar to Word, but with excellent typographic controls.
