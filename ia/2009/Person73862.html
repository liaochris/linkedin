Erica Duran’s Summary

A veteran entrepreneur, I have over 18 years in coordinating hotel and theme parks' business systems, hotel grand openings, and large networking and social events.

I then went on to obtain my licenses in life insurance, investment securities, mortgage, and real estate.

For the past four years, I have been consulting and creating several corporations and start-ups including my own financial services company with an emphasis on nation-wide short sale negotiations, venture capital and credit repair.

I am currently involved in two companies. MBH Holdings and Saving the American Dream, Inc.

At MBH Holdings we issue & deliver bank debt instruments which can be used as default collateral to support credit lines, loans and transactions. We offer CDs, DPLC, BGs, and SBLCs. Instruments are available from $10 Million to $1 Billion. We are the only company that can issue these instruments without a bank undertaking. Our instruments are cash-backed by the top 25 banks in the world. What makes our collateral so strong is that the instrument will be in the name of the Beneficiary, and they are fully lienable, collateralizable, callable, transferable & assignable. To see more info on these instruments go to www.debtcollateralfinancialinstruments.com

At Saving the American Dream we audit a homeowner's loan documents to find State and Federal violations. We then turn the findings over to a team of expert attorneys. The results of the audit give legal professionals the leverage they need to negotiate a possible reduction in PRINCIPAL AND PAYMENT and /or INTEREST RATE. In some cases a homeowner may get the entire loan RESCINDED!!
We are NOT a short sale or loan modification company!!

From hospitality to finance I have an eclectic background with a knack for seeking out authenticity and efficiency.

I live in Newport Beach CA with my three Pembroke Welsh Corgis: Starbucks, Kona, and Latte.

Please let me know how I can help you with your success!
