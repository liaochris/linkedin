Martin Lenclos’s Summary

Martin Lenclos is a partner, director of projects and 3D information architect at BOX Creative. As managing partner and project director, he developed a unique cross-disciplinary approach with a specialty in 3D website production and installation, as well as the development of custom e-marketing strategies. He has spent the last two years working with clients such as American Media, Inc., Magnum Photos, MoMA, Nova.Corp and PepsiCo, developing customized digital brand strategies and interactive web environments, with exceptional Internet and design technology.

With a decade of experience in technology and communication, Martin has worked as lead research director of emerging web applications for Dassault Systemes, applied media director at the acclaimed Jean-Paul Viguier Architecture Agency, as well as lead consultant for French promoters such as Altarea and Unibail-Rodamco. Through art, design, and modern ideas, Martin’s goal is to develop online success for clients and create new marketing ventures through innovation and original media outlets.

Since 2006, Martin has been developing the concept of a new form of communication called datamentary™. It is a unique media that presents information through a spatial environment that enhances one’s ability to understand and absorb web content. Recently, he has been working with BOX Creative on new forms of 3D journalism coverage (collecting, editing, and presenting material for Web diffusion). Coverage3d.com, his brain child, is a collaborative effort between teams of photo-journalists, 3D artists, and interactive designers. The teams attend various events and participate by taking photos, videos, and interviewing the attendees. Once this information is compiled, it is transported and processed into a 3D environment for rapid audience access. It allows the viewer to take part in the event through an interactive re-mastered 3D setting.

  • Co-founder/Creative Director

    Coverage3d

    (Internet industry)

    April 2008 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Coverage3d is multimedia event coverage diffused online. It not only presents information and provides user interface for access, it also creates a spatial relationship that enhances the viewer’s ability to understand and absorb the totality of the event.

    A collaborative union of documentary filmmakers, photo-journalists, 3D artists, and interactive designers, our team attends all types of events and documents participation, taking photos, videos, and interviewing attendees. Once this information is compiled, it is transported and processed into a 3D environment, designed and built around the specified theme of the event. Anthologized as a datascape, Coverage3d allows for rapid and customizable access to its online users.
