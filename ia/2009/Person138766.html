Lauren Donovan’s Summary

My experience at MPG Arnold built a strong and experienced base for the beginning of my career. I was able to learn media inside out - whether it be television, radio, print, online, or outdoor advertising. I was able to "wet my feet" dealing with negotiations, client relationships, data research and analysis, and corporate presentations. My short but thorough time at MPG prepared me for the opportunity I still enjoy today at Comcast SportsNet.

Working in Affiliate Relations - I help manage the relationships between all satellite and cable operators through the New England footprint. Day to day tasks are always changing from managing contract renewals, technology concerns or launches for our station, subscriber data, department budgets and forecasts for the business. Even after the 5 o'clock bell we continue to work on marketing events, promotions, and game related experiences for our clientèle.

My challenges are always evolving at Comcast SportsNet - and this is one of the reasons why I thrive at and enjoy my career here.
