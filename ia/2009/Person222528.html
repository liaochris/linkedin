    (Government Agency; 5001-10,000 employees; Government Relations industry)

    May 2008 — Present (10 months)

    I have accepted an invitation to join British Council in it's European project "Challenge Europe" that will run in 15 counties for three years.

    I will be 1 of 12 so called Climate Advocates in the Danish part of the project for the next year.

    The Climate Advocates works to unearth new ways to reduce carbon use or utilise methods already found but not yet properly exploited. The key thing is to create ideas that has real potential to bring about a Low Carbon Future through changes to public policy, business practice or public behaviour. The outcome of the project will be a network of about two hundred bold and young influencers working together to develop forty two tangible ideas.

    The philosophy of the campaign is simple: to create momentum through collaboration, innovation, energy, drive, passion, understanding and knowledge-sharing.
