  • President & Editor

    Self Directed Investor, Inc

    (Investment Management industry)

    March 2009 — Present (4 months)

    "Empowering investors with ideas and education."

    Self Directed Investor, Inc. provides financial education and investment information for individual investors. Our website (www.selfdirectedinvestor.com) offers timely financial data, analysis and commentary for investors at all levels of experience.

    After the market crash of 2008, many investors discovered they could produce returns as well as or better than their advisors. As a result, investors are increasingly taking control of their own investments. Others wonder whether their advisors are making good decisions for them, but don't have the background to ask their advisor appropriate questions and completely understand the answers. Whether managing your own funds or entrusting them to a financial advisor, you need to take control of your investment decisions. Through education and ideas, www.selfdirectedinvestor.com is designed to help you to gain more control of your financial future.

    Remember, no one cares more about your money than you do.
