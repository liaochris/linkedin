Kevin Ramsdell’s Summary

Kevin began his career as an intern at Dean Witter Reynolds in Boston, MA as an assistant to the Senior Vice President of Equity Investments. At this time he was completing his undergraduate degree in Finance. Upon course completion, he established himself as a Financial Services Professional specializing in Business and Estate Planning for his clients in the greater Boston area. After 7 years of success, he persued his interest in the technology side of the business and attended Graduate School.

He obtained his Master's Degree in Information Technology knowing his focus would be in the Investment Banking and Financial Services sectors. Shortly after graduation he moved to New York City and was hired by the Bank of New York as an Assistant Vive President to support their Structured Finance/Fixed Income transactions. He managed a team responsible for $5.6 billion in Assets and oversaw all Trade Executions, Compliance and Corporate Reporting.

Kevin then began his own consulting company. He has acted in a variety of roles including Buisness Advisor, Project Manager, Financial Analyst, Programmer, and Business Analyst. Throughout his career he has worked closely with firms including:

• Goldman Sachs
• Morgan Stanley
• JPMorgan/Chase
• ING
• Prudential
• Bank of America
• Citigroup
• Oppenheimer Funds
• Travelers
• Jefferies & Co

Kevin is now a Managing Director at Software by Design, a Software Solutions Provider specializing in Business Intelligence and Corporate Performance Management. He manages their South Central Division based out of Dallas, TX. His firm is authorized to resell and customize products from companies like IBM/Cognos, Microstrategy, SAP/Business Objects, Microsoft, & Oracle and as well as build entirely new applications to meet client demands. Business Intelligence is one of the fastest growing and important sectors in technology today.
