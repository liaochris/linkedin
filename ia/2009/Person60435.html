Deborah Chan’s Summary

With extensive communications, research and branding experience across a variety of industry, I combine my international experience to develop brand and communications strategies that can target and develop international markets and niche segments.

Since communications strategies are at the heart of internal and external branding, I have spent extensive time and effort advancing Web, newsletter and other media efforts. I consistently ensure that all brand messages are embedded into every stakeholder communication. My philosophy in good writing lies in ground-breaking research to ensure that the message lives up to the brand promise.

My background in training and skills development, especially for the retail and banking industries, enable companies to deliver customer value and help employees become more efficient and productive.

Moving onto another budding project, I'm now using my skills and experience in social media marketing, which is a core element of the next generation in branding. I am knowledgeable about such social communities as Facebook and MySpace, and knows how to put the power of blogs and other consumer-generated media to work for brands.
