Ehud Mendelson’s Summary

I lead a research company in indoor navigation and local based services.
we developed a set of breakthrough algorithms for intelligently routing and delivering content directly to the “user” cell phone — without relying on GPS, internet, or even cellular communication. Our unique method provides the cellular user with direct local content according to the user’s preference and a full suite of applications designed especially for indoor pedestrians from indoor navigation to emergency notification without compromising the user’s privacy. At the same time our sales sense method provides the local business/mall/store with much needed business tools to help direct sales, specials, coupons and targeted advertising.
Our intellectual property include 6 patents pending going back to 2005. Covering from serach4parking and return2parking to a method of update waypoints via Bluetooth and RDS to indoor navigation method and application and the sales sense algorithms and nav4emergency method.
