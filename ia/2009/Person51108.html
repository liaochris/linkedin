Christopher Sopko’s Summary

I have recently accepted a position as President of Dantec Dynamics Inc. This New Jersey organization is the US headquarters of Dantec Dynamics A/S based out of Copenhagen, Denmark.

Dantec Dynamics' main area of activity is development and sales of integrated measurement systems for diagnostics and research into fluid mechanics, solid mechanics, microfluidics, spray analysis and combustion technology.

Our systems are used to obtain measurement data of physical properties in air, gases, liquids and solid materials.

Quantitative measurement data include velocity, turbulence, particle size, concentration, temperature, combustion species, strain/stress and vibration.

As president of Struers Inc., I was pleased to lead a solid team of managers and sales professionals who sold laboratory equipment and software solutions to a variety of industries including the steel, automotive, aerospace, aircraft, electronic, ceramic, etc., in the United States and Canada.

Located in Cleveland, OH we were the US branch of an international organization also based in Copenhagen, Denmark called Struers A/S.

At Struers Inc., I started as an Account Manager and was promoted to Marketing Manager in less than a year. Within five years I was promoted to Marketing Director and given additional responsibilities for our IT department, Inside Sales group, and Customer Service team.

I have a very strong background in CRM, ERP and e-learning systems.

Christopher Sopko’s Specialties:

My background covers many industries, including nano technology and Silicon wafer development. Additionally, I have experience in dealing with governmental agencies, including the US defense department. We were the only vendor in our market to be GSA listed which paid great dividends to our organization.
