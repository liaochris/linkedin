Ardi Kolah’s Summary

Ardi has been one of the most influential voices in global sales, marketing and communication for the past decade and is a prolific conference speaker/chairman and author on all aspects of sales and marketing.

In 2001, he was chosen by the Lord Mayor of London Alderman Clive Martin to give the keynote address at the Britain Creates luncheon at Mansion House.

In 2003 he was independently ranked by the Chartered Institute of Marketing as one of the top 50 gurus in the world on marketing (www.cim.co.uk/Shapetheagenda) alongside Philip Kotler, Seth Godin, Malcolm Gladwell, Martin Lindstrom, Don Peppers and Sergio Zyman.

In 2004, Ardi appeared in the 40th anniversary special edition of Admap as one of 40 marketing gurus to share their vision of the future of communications, with contributions from Sir Martin Sorrell and Clive Humby.

In 2005, Ardi was a Finalist in the Chartered Institute of Public Relations Excellence Awards for Outstanding Contribution to Education & Training.

Ardi holds a post-graduate LLM international law degree from King’s College London & University College London and joined the BBC as a producer/reporter on flagship news and current affairs programmes including R4's Today Programme.

Following a successful career as a BBC journalist, he spent the next 20 years building a career in sales & marketing within organisations such as Andersen Consulting, CMG plc, Imperial Cancer, WPP plc and the Defence Academy of the UK.

With David Bowler, he launched Guru in a Bottle in 2008, which has become the first approved Global Marketing Network Professional Development Centre.

All sales, marketing and communication workshops qualify for Continuing Professional Development (CPD) hours awarded by Global Marketing Network in support of its GMN CPD Scheme.

Ardi Kolah’s Specialties:

Sales, marketing and communication training for public and private sector clients.
