Cherri Pitts’s Summary

I make sure the project stays on budget and on time. I work with our clients from the time the contract is signed until the site is launched and beyond. We work together on the timeline, content, SEO, and Social Networking.

The relationships may start with a web site or other online project, but it is my goal to make it continue from there.
I want to be a proactive agent for the client to keep them informed of all things technical that are important to their web site presence. I hope I can be a part of the team that keeps the client loyal and trusting of the service given to them.

I don't see problems, I see issues that need a way to be solved. With the internet industry that is changing and shifting so quickly, we have to adjust our processes as well. Look for more creative ways to reach our goals.

Communication is the key to any project, going from upper management through me and to the client and then back. It must be fluid and true or the project will not work as it should.

I feel this position has taught me so much about customer service as well as myself. It is always interesting and I am continuously learning something new. My clients teach me by demanding the best from me and my team. It just makes us stronger and smarter at the end of the project.
