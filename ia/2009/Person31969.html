Stephen Brand’s Summary

Helping organizations and companies uncover unrealized revenue potential through a process of inventorying a wide range of physical and intangible assets in the context of what their market wants and needs and building marketing and operational systems that allow the organizations to achieve their business objectives and visions.

Stephen Brand’s Specialties:

Innovation, new venture creation, creativity, strategy, invention, education, non profit non-traditional revenue streams, business model innovation
