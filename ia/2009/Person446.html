Rita Hovakimian’s Summary

Rita Hovakimian has been a business consultant, leadership development specialist and seminar leader for individuals and businesses since 1989. She has worked with thousands of people in the United States and Canada in discovering their unique contribution, their authenticity and their power. Rita has coached over 400 entrepreneurs including executives in major corporations in being successful and achieving their visions, both professionally and personally. Her methodology is action-oriented and results-focused. Rita brings a holistic perspective to her clients. She supports people in designing practices to live lives that are balanced spiritually, emotionally, physically and mentally. She is an advocate of attaining all of this in an elegant and effortless manner.

Rita is distinct and effective as a consultant from having managed her own successful entrepreneurial business for 16 years. She empowers people to trust themselves and their intuition, enabling them to take their leadership to new levels of effectiveness. Among Rita’s abilities are her compassionate listening skills, her focus on acknowledging and validating each person’s opinion with respect, her extremely intuitive sensibilities, and her dedicated and unwavering stand for each client to achieve success and produce extraordinary results. Rita holds a Bachelor's Degree in Physical Education with an emphasis in Exercise Physiology. She is certified as a Professional Coach through New Ventures West. Rita also is a member with the Professional Coaches and Mentors Association where she actively participates on the Membership Experience and Programs Committees . She lives in San Francisco with her husband, Robert Weinberg and their two cats, Sam and Simone.

Rita Hovakimian’s Specialties:

My speciality is working with entrepreneurs, leaders and professionals in transforming their business results, effectiveness and confidence as leaders.
