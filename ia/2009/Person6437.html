Alan Wunsche’s Summary

I am a business performance management specialist with extensive experience implementing technologies and organizational change. I help companies leverage financial and non-information to improve their analytic capabilities, and their planning, budgeting and forecasting processes to realize their strategic objectives.

I am a Senior Manager in the Strategy & Operations / Finance and Performance Management (F&PM) management consulting practice in the Toronto office of Deloitte. I focus on helping organizations create shareholder value through financial transformation, including: 1) business performance management technologies, 2) process transformation, and 3) organizational change management.

I have over 17 years experience in finance, innovation management and commercialization, organizational restructuring, value-based management, corporate strategy, business process design, change management and executive facilitation. At another global management consultancy, I led the Value-Based Management Centre of Excellence, managed Financial Management Benchmarking and Best Practices studies and led the e-Business practice and specialized in organizational change group. My broad industry experience includes oil and gas, consumer business, financial services, technology and transportation.

I am a Chartered Accountant (Ontario, Canada) and earned my MBA at the Richard Ivey School of Business. At Ivey, I focused on both Finance and Organizational Change.
