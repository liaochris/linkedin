Ola Ayeni, DVM’s Summary

Ola Ayeni, DVM
Chief Idea Officer/President
Mobile Dialog
www.m-dialog.com

Mobile-Dialog is a leader in integrated mobile marketing and social media marketing solutions. We help companies identify how to add mobile marketing to all existing channels without any extra work but increase response rate and results.

What we provide: We have 2 sets of services we provide to companies; 1) Help our clients develop and execute a mobile marketing strategy 2) Provide the tools to execute by us or using a DIY platform like our M-dialog messaging platform.

Clients and partners include Great Clips, Harrolds Chicken, Navister,Chicago International Trucks , Walter Payton Roundhouse, Quizno, Hotel Savings Directory, Access Development, Verizon Wireless, JaJah and Wadja.

What I do: As a mobile marketing expert and consultant, I help companies implement mobile marketing strategies that increase their bottom line, design integrated strategies that work with existing media channels and provide mobile marketing tools. My expertise focuses on an holistic mobile marketing solution that offers a unique blend of high caliber business consulting, flawless creatives, business analytics and proven implementation. .

Before starting Mobile Dialog in 2006, I was responsible for implementing pharmaceutical sales strategies and new product launch at Bristol Myers Squibb Co—a $25 billion global organization.

In 2006, I invented a proximity awareness technology ( Web2mobile platform) that delivers coupons and deals based on consumers location, interest and preferences . An ideal product for brick and mortar retailers and businesses. A year after I invented another patent pending system for delivering digital coupons wirelessly.
