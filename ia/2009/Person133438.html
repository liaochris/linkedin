Kevin Cabral’s Summary

Marketing and technology management expertise and enthusiasm for emerging digital technology businesses. I especially love to help old-line industries, such as financial services, retail and media, to delight their customers and employees with technology.

I've been "dialed-in" to the digital world since 1992, which has allowed me to be an active part of the lifecycle of many technologies. In 1998, I founded a major Internet community while in college which I developed until its sale in 2002. After finishing my undergraduate degrees, I worked at a start-up developing an online information service akin to "Bloomberg for retail bankers" then became a management consultant where I developed marketing analytics and new consulting practices around them.

I recently graduated with Honors from the full-time MBA program at the University of Chicago where I studied a range of subjects from marketing to economics and entrepreneurial finance. Post-graduation, I am exploring opportunities to develop innovative new products and emerging businesses.
