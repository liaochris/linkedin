Raffi Patatian’s Summary

I am an internationally seasoned, multilingual marketing executive with 12+ years’ experience working with boards of directors and senior leaders in aerospace and financial services to implement strategic initiatives in marketing and business development.

Recently, I helped start up an investment firm in Kuwait, where I worked with the board of directors to establish the firm’s strategy, and led a team of international designers to develop the firm’s branding and marketing campaign. Prior to this role, I managed product lines ranging from $40-$130M, and directed business development activity in the EMEA region for a division of a US-based aerospace firm.

I am passionate about international business, have lived and worked extensively in the US, Europe, and the Middle East, and speak four languages (English, Arabic, French, and Armenian). I also hold an MBA from Southern Methodist University and an undergraduate degree in Operations Management from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University.
