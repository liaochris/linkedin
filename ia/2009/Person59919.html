Joshua R. Beal’s Summary

Investment Advisor providing fee-based discretionary investment management services for affluent deaf and hard of hearing individuals. My firm's consumer niche market is targeted by using the Internet, American Sign Language (ASL) and Videophone to provide services nationwide.

Responsible for all Schwarz Financial Services LLC marketing efforts along with 1st year development and launch of www.DeafTax.com in 2006. I developed and mplemented marketing communications in ASL via Internet video to the U.S. deaf consumer demographic.

Now coordinating the DeafTax.com VITA Pilot in cooperation with National Disability Institute and the Real Economic Impact Tour (www.reitour.org), which will serve low income deaf taxpayers nationwide during the 2008 tax season.
