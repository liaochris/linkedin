Jim Canto’s Summary

I am the owner and founder of medXcentral (.com) and two subsidiary brands; "TheCandidateNetwork.com" and "GoHCRO.com". They are three separate sites, all within the medical and health care spectrum, with three separate missions. My income model is located at the intersection of all three missions.

Here's a brief explanation of each site.

medxcentral.com - Centralizing the Medical and Health Care Online Community. Centralize and be found. We allow commercial promotion of your blog, forum, product, service, niche community and/or individual career. My goal is to have this community function as a continuous online medical and health care trade show and convention center. Feel free to link out to your external sites and RSS in and out of our community. We are an open network architecture.

thecandidatenetwork.com - home of the MPOPS program. MPOPS stands for Medical Professional Online Privacy Service. MPOPS is a web 2.0 toolkit for medical professionals. Consider how few medical professionals are actually online. Then ask yourself; Why? We aim to alter that stat by giving medical professionals a strategy online.

gohcro.com - HCRO = Health Care Recruiters Online where we offer the Medical Staffing Exchange. As part of our MPOPS program, we offer medical professionals a Safe Job Search Strategy where medical professionals can control their privacy and connect with medical recruiters on their terms. In so much as those medical professionals wish to be found, and recruiters desire to find them, we facilitate that action at GoHCRO.com. Searching our database is free and posting medical jobs in the system is free. Now.. here's where it gets interesting....

We then share referral fees with the medical professional who took the job and with the party or entity who referred them.

In the interest of transparency, please note, I am a one man show and a bootstrapped organization. Though stretched thin, I'm always listening for opportunity. Let's collaborate.
