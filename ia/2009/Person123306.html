Juergen Harter’s Summary

Experienced Business & Product Developer in the life science and biotech industries, leader of high-performance global scientific teams, Chemistry Analyst, Information manager, and Project Manager in a variety of pharmaceutical knowledge management and business workflow applications. Latest focus is on expert systems and business intelligence within the antibody industries. Good range of experience in automatic building, creation and visualisation of intelligence networks, knowledge maps, ontologies and taxonomies. Particularly interested in knowledge modelling, knowledge capture and semantic web technologies, and any techniques and methods to handle and make sense of the ever growing "information mountain". Always interested in challenging system integration projects and knowledge-driven business development opportunities, particularly in the pharmaceutical, biotech and healthcare industries. Good expertise within the proteomics and genomics fields. Big interest in semantic systems biology, in combination with grid/cloud computing.
