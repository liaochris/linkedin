Frank Dorrin’s Summary

As Director, I am responsible for the planning, design, implementation, operation and maintenance of information technology (hardware, systems, and software) to support the business purposes of Conectiv Energy.

Certified Application Developer (MCSD), and Project Manager of a wide variety of business applications.
