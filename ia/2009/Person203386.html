Ron Durant’s Summary

My background includes 25 years of providing leadership in Sales, Management and Business Development to small and family run businesses, principally in the Travel & Entertainment, Financial Services and Consumer Goods market sectors.

As owner and chief "Marketing Strategist" at e-NERTIA Marketing, my focus is on working with other small business owners to improve their ROI through the proper utilization of online marketing tools, including social media. Online Marketing works fast, it's economical and the results are easy to track. Properly utilized online marketing concepts will;

• Attract and convert new customers and clients online
• Turn Search Engines into an online sales force
• Grow your client base and reduce the cost of acquisition
• Enhance your business relationships and improve customer loyalty.
• Utilize Social Media to monitor the competition, manage company and executive reputations and tap into new markets

My clients work directly with the owner, and experience the personal touch of a small company while benefiting from the experience, expertise and resources of a large corporation.

At e-NERTIA, we have unique access to a network of highly skilled online marketing professionals throughout the United States. We are always ready to deliver the very best website and internet based marketing services available.
