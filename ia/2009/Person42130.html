Ricardo Valenzuela’s Summary

Extensive experience in areas of commercial, merchant, investment and international banking as former CEO of a large agribusiness bank in Sonora, Mexico; As former CEO of a national bank in Guadalajara, Mexico. As CEO of an offshore investment bank headquarter in Arizona conducting business with Latin America.

Fluent in Spanish and English with extensive knowledge of international financial and capital markets, the agriculture markets. An expert in the world"s macroeconomic issues and Latin American political and economic issues. Recruited by the government of Sonora, Mexico to serve in a new position as representative of the governor in the USA.

For the last 20 years I have been acting as a bridge promoting business and better relationships between Mexico and the USA and it has not been easy. But my belief that the future is where our countries meet in that region call the border, give me the strength to keep going trying to grasp my vision and my dream.
