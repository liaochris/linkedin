Andrea Smith’s Summary

Graphic Designer with broad experience in print based and web based projects.

I believe great design should be available to all. My goal is to help improve humanity through design. I do not believe in a right or wrong style necessarily because I treat each project as a unique problem to solve. Design is about problem solving and innovation. It is my pleasure to be able to provide my skills to those who have a design need in the hopes that we can come up with the ultimate solution for your project(s).
