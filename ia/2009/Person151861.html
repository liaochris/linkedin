Mark Gilbertson’s Summary

A commercial HR and Resourcing professional with 15 years consulting and general management experience across Resourcing & Talent Management. FCIPD qualified.

An advocate that organisations, large or small, can implement 'transformational change' across their recruitment and talent pipelines leading to a competetive advantage in the recruitment marketplace.
Now works across a large client base to deliver consultancy, interim, design and implementation expertise across the resourcing and talent pipeline.

Recent projects have included:
a. development and implementation of a centralised recruitment function, web based Applicant Tracking System and behavioural screening tools for a Global Pharma/Health & Beauty firm.
b. creation of an in-house Exec search capability/team
c. Interim Head of Talent Aquisition
d. in-source of a recruitment function for a Global Telco
e. review and audit of a talent resource function

Interested in working with clients or RPOs in any geography, either on a consultancy or interim basis. Tel 0044 (0)7967 946563
