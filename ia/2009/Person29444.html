Nate Ludens’s Summary

I've been growing in the commercial creative industry professionally for over 9 years, with the last seven years in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Currently, I'm the President of Black Diamond Digital Imaging and BDDI, LLC. where we specialize in what we call FREESTYLE DESIGN & PHOTOGRAPHY. Defined, it's the ability we carry to MIX IT UP. Simply because on Monday we're shooting a new restaurant or condo, does not mean on Tuesday we can't develop a solid brand identity for a funky new startup. Get it? Yeah, we do too.

Recently, I was honored to receive an invitation to be on the Advisory Board for the Las Vegas Chapter of the AIGA (American Institution of Graphic Artists). The Advisory Board - the way I like to see it - is the link between the local AIGA creatives and the business community who has accepted that design is crucial to contemporary commerce.

Nate Ludens’s Specialties:

Results-driven, down to earth marketing specalist using a finely mixed cocktail of branding, design, photography, strategy and relationships.

I can fix a large format printer. I can design a matchbook and a building wrap. I can shoot a 500 sq. ft. condo and make it look like a palace. I can hit your budget, your deadline and your target demographic.
