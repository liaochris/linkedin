    CINC offers its international clientele a variety of business and operational services: private office rent, virtual offices, shared office/coworking, a comfortable rooms and events, business consultancy, and more services to companies.

    CINC allows anyone in need of a flexible place to work or meet to have an organized deskspace, make phone calls, meet with clients, and receive packages to an address that doesn’t include an apartment number. CINC offers several levels of memberships that range from daytime drop-ins to full-time, 24/hr access memberships
