Bruce McCullough, MBA, ITIL’s Summary

"Leads Customer Focused IT Organizations to Service Excellence"
I build and lead my organizations to be fully focused on serving our customers needs. My Service Management strategies are developed to deliver solutions and services that will help customers solve business problems and achieve their objectives. My organizations are all about the customer!

I’m an accomplished Technology Leader with 20+ years experience. I have successfully spearheaded strategic and large-scale initiatives for startups and Global 100 corporations. My strengths include Strategy, Service Management/Operations, Architecture, Infrastructure, and Customer Support. I am a strong visionary in anticipating future IT direction based on business needs and able to communicate that vision to executive management, customers, and business partners. I'm a highly intelligent, results oriented, change agent who develops creative solutions to complex problems. I lead, inspire, and motivates teams to their highest achievement possible. My industry experience includes Manufacturing, Telecommunications, Utilities, and Financial Services industries. I hold an MBA and ITIL certification.
