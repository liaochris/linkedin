Arthur Tschopp’s Summary

Seasoned expert in luxury goods marketing, specifically within the fine jewelry industry. From high-end retail sales and gemological consulting to strategic marketing plans, and tactical execution, I have experienced every "facet" of the jewelry industry.

Being a Graduate Gemologist (GIA), and holding a Bachelor of Fine Art degree with concentration in Visual Communications and Digital Design allows me to maintain an authoritative perspective on all distribution channels of the B2C and B2B jewelry industry.
