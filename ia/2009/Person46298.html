Cindy W Morrison’s Summary

I'm a Journalist, Author, Speaker, TV Personality and Mom.

Basically, I've spent 20 years in TV news... covering everything from the Waco standoff in '93 to the Oklahoma City bombing in '95.

Most of of the last two decades I've been out in the field either as a general assignment reporter or consumer reporter and eventually earned my stripes to do investigative work. I have been lucky enough to win an Emmy & Gracie for my investigative reporting and a Peabody for over-all coverage.

While reporting is a place where I feel I can make a difference, I have loved my time at the anchor desk. For the last 11 years, I've been KTUL-TV's 5pm anchor. I started anchoring In OKC on Saturday Morning News before co-anchoring the morning and noon newscasts @ KOCO-TV.
