Jorge Pereira’s Summary

I am the CEO and co-owner of Seegno, a very dynamic startup with primary activity in developing online systems that cross boundaries into the real world. Seegno is the result of years of accumulated experience and the coming together of people with different backgrounds and skills, but with a shared vision.

On an individual level, I am a systems engineer with large experience in application design, development, integration and project and team management. My focus is generally in creating software that meets people's needs, improving methods, processes and reaching technically sound solutions.
I have strong experience in Knowledge Management, ERP, CRM systems and the retail industry. I have experience in e-Learning both as a developer, course manager and tutor, and am very interested in the dynamics of collaboration and social networks.
I am equally at ease architecting a new project, programming in multiple languages or sharing ideas with people from various backgrounds. In fact, I cherish the demanding context switching of co-running a dynamic company such as ours.
I enjoy thinking outside the box and honestly love what I do. :)
