My goal is to help other entrepreneurs along their journey!

My path has allowed me to combine my experiences in technology/digital/mobile with sports.

Another passion is helping the next generation of entrepreneurs. I’m a Senior Adjunct Professor at St. Louis University where I teach Undergrads and Masters students…And now I’m helping to start a new Sports Business program within the Business School.
