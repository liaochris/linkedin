Rob Lucas’s Summary

I have over 13 years of experience in software development. The majority of my work has been with user interfaces including web interfaces. I've also developed backend systems and database access. I have experience with multi-threaded and distributed systems. I’ve worked on various size teams including large ones with developers at multiple locations. I have been the technical lead on projects.

I also maintain a website that is about my ultra cycling and it has a search for cycling and outdoor gear. In December 2008 there were around 80,000 unique visitors and 135,000 page views. The gear search uses datafeeds from over 60 online merchants. There are over 200,000 product entries in the datafeeds. I use this website to do search engine optimization experiments.
