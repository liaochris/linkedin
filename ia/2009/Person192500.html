Diane Abramson’s Summary

"The Ultimate Rainmaker" for Executive Search -
As a Partner with Rainmaker Consultants, Diane maintains a premier reputation for her expertise in sourcing and recruiting "world class" human capital and talent encompassing Vice President through "C" Level roles, leading and supporting Marketing, Operations, Analytics, Strategy and Business Development functions.
