Robertson Price’s Summary

Generated over $100MM in revenue for employers and shareholders in the past 6 years. Highly successful business development executive offering unique product development expertise spanning the development life cycle (product innovation, design, build, testing, optimization, distribution and monetization). Demonstrated ability to succeed with internal teams, third party venders, consultants, partners, investors, etc… Most recent experience includes deep dives into social media as SVP Business Development of a social network that previously took in over $60MM in VC funding. Expertise and extensive contacts span venture and angel investors, major advertisers, leading tech CEOs, top ad network executives, and talented engineers.
