Juliano Alves’s Summary

Degree in Social Science by the Universidade Estadual de Londrina (2002), Executive MBA in Business Management, at ISAE / FGV Institute of Administration and Economics at Getúlio Vargas Foundation - Londrina (2003) and Master in Business Administration from Universidade Estadual de Londrina. He is currently a Managing Director and Sales of the company Oniria (LDGames Production of Software Ltda). He has 03 years of academic experience as a university professor and 10 years of professional experience in the area of administration and Computer Science, with emphasis on Project Management, working mainly in the following areas: software for entertainment (computer games), information systems, entrepreneurship and internet.

He seeks to develop research and projects involving the mind and human behavior, interactivity and technology.
