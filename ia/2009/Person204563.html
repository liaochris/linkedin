Roy W. MacNaughton’s Summary

Roy W. MacNaughton has more than thirty years of international hospitality industry experience. He has lived and worked in Canada, the USA, the UK, The Bahamas, Trinidad & Tobago, and Australia.

He graduated from Toronto’s Ryerson University in Hotel, Restaurant and Tourism Management.

Roy returned to school at age 29, choosing the Ivey School of Business at the University of Western Ontario where he earned his MBA.

Within one year, Roy went on to establish his own firm, Hospitality Marketing, with a Ryerson classmate. Together, they provided marketing research, planning and advertising services to well-known firms including McDonalds, American Express, Four Seasons Hotels, Hilton International, KFC, Wendy’s, H.J. Heinz, Johnson & Johnson, and many others.

Within six years, he was hired away by one of his own clients, (the second largest restaurant company in Canada), as the Executive Vice-President of Marketing. After two years in this position, Roy was transferred to the U.S., as President and Chief Operating Officer of their fledgling subsidiary. Roy grew that division from $3.2 million to $72 million.

After a few years, the call of the consulting business brought him back to owning and operating his own consulting firm.

Roy loves the excitement of seeing people learn and businesses grow. As a part-time, adjunct professor, he has taught university MBAs and undergraduate hospitality students in the U.S., Canada and the West Indies, including at Johnson & Wales in Miami.

He writes weekly newspaper columns on marketing and has been a Contributing Editor for Today's Restaurant News for the last 11 years. Roy writes online for many hospitality industry publications, blogs and sites, including the new social networking site, www.FohBoh.com, the "Voice of the Global Restaurant Industry".
