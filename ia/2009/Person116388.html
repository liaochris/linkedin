Johanna Meyer’s Summary

Johanna Meyer is a GIS specialist with over 10 years of experience. She built an ArcGIS Server system from the data development of over 15,000 critical infrastructure points in a Spatial Database Engine/SQL, displayed them on an ArcGIS Server site with integrated real time weather data from NWS and other ArcIMS sites. She developed a prototype spatial temporal model for disease incidence and spread with two years research on human vulnerability. Ms. Meyer’s used her models to co-teach graduate and undergraduate classes.
