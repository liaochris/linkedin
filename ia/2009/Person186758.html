Phil Byrne’s Summary

While I am engaged with a company I always bring the benefit of my experience in Business Planning and Marketing, encouraging client companies to:

o Be the best at what they do.
o Take full control of the direction that their business takes.
o Develop company culture so that there is a true team spirit.
o Ensure their Sales & Marketing Strategy is well targeted.
o Remove all barriers to achieving better returns on their investments !

Welcome !

For many years now, I have been involved in Business Planning, with a strong emphasis on Marketing. My main area of expertise is derived from the Cleantech, Construction and Related Sectors, promoting Products and Services, to all players in those markets.

In recent years, I have worked with Enterprise Boards, Manufacturers, Distributors, Architects, Planners and Construction Companies, developing the route to market for many new products. Also experiencing how a product might perform compared to its competition and how a particular product or service may excel due to accurate, well targeted marketing.

I firmly believe that “Cold Calling IS Dead” and I promote Networking and Refined Marketing strategies as the way forward to raise the profile of companies that I work with. I have a particular interest in Start Up companies, developing Business Plans & Route to Market.

Currently, I am working with Erin Energy Ltd., an Enterprise Ireland “Incubation” company, developing Solar Panel technology for the Merchant/D.I.Y. Market. This is a company encapsulating the best thinking in the Renewable & Natural Energies Sector. I am available by e-mail and at any time by phone and I would be delighted to discuss the best way forward for your company.
