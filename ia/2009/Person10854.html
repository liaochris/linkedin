Amanda Vandervort’s Summary

Amanda Vandervort is the Web Coordinator at Women's Professional Soccer (WPS). Since December of 2008, Amanda has been working to develop and implement the new media and social media strategies at WPS. She has a keen interest in growing organic traffic and developing a comprehensive web presence that touches multiple outlets.

These strategies, combined with her unique background as a soccer coach and interest in developing the world's game, have helped WPS to extend its brand messaging across the globe.

Prior to her experience at the league office, Amanda was the Director of Online Marketing, Website and Camps at the Chicago Red Stars, one of the league's founding franchises.

Amanda Vandervort’s Specialties:

Soccer Coach, Social Media Maven, Technology Education, Website Design and Management, Online Marketing, Search Engine Optimization
