Brian Pendarvis’s Summary

Started in family business (Pendarvis Manufacturing) full-time the summer of 1985. Apprentice / OJT for the first 4 - 5 years. Learning all the aspects of the machining, welding and fabrication industry. Slowly added employees. By 1999 Pendarvis Manufacturing employed 11 people.

In 1995 founded AES. AES designed, built and install custom solutions for the industrial wastewater industry. AES was in business for 4+ years. We installed 14 "Package" plants from Hawaii to Clearwater, Florida. Provided seminar at annual industry trade show on the merits of a specific wastewater treatment technology. The family business was thriving and we made the choice to cease operations of AES.

Late 1999 I started transitioning into the daily managment of the family business.

Around 2002 we started adding more sophisticated manufacturing technologies to the company.

Since 2005 we have invested over $500K in new equipment. During that same time we have also increased our staff level to 20+ people on the production floor of the company.

Our machining and fabrication know-how has increased tremendously the last 5+ years. Through our sales and marketing efforts we are able to meet new people and explore new opportunites... on a daily basis.
