Rogier van Wagtendonk’s Summary

I started Rogier Webdesign three and a half years ago, focusing on developing custom built websites for my clients.

With my range of interests and experience growing, I founded Stripe in 2007 with my business partner Stijn Zeeman.
Our innovative, low cost websites were finding considerable popularity, our unique development concept allowing us to turn projects around in a relatively short time. We also diversified beyond construction, providing well received workshops on how to manage your own content and website appearance.

In addition to offering these workshops, we’re now also providing our clients with training in Joomla CMS, a leading program for the control and management of their website(s). Based in Breda (previously Amsterdam) and in the near future Utrecht, these highly successful seminars continue to grow in reach and popularity.

Following the "Never eat alone" ideal, we started a network event called the OpenCoffee. Open Coffee events are regular informal meet-ups for entrepreneurs, investors and people interested in entrepreneurship. Main focus: the web. It is a great opportunity to meet other like minded people and to discuss/share ideas. We also joined a network club for young entrepreneurs called Bizzion. My partner Stijn is now running the branch in our town (Breda) in the Netherlands. One of the great things about Bizzion is that it's a club for young businessmen and women aged under 28. It’s such a great vehicle for development and motivation. We organise meetings where a successful (young) businessman or woman presents their business and talks about their experiences- the road they travelled and obstacles they were able to overcome. Its an inspiring atmosphere, a fantastic opportunity to learn from our peers and a real means to meet people with shared interests.

My Goal in life is to "think out of the box"..... To be creative, to respect others and their work, and to keep on improving, both personally and professionally!
