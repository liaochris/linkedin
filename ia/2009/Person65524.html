Donald Larson’s Summary

IT executive with more than 25 years of leadership experience driving successful delivery of IT solutions to multibillion-dollar global organizations. Professionally focused on architecture, technology infrastructure and program management as well as organizational development.

Noted Accomplishments

Member of the IT leadership team that led CNA through the consolidation of IT resources and technology which resulted in savings of over $30 million in initial operating efficiency

Accountable for leading engineering teams in Europe and the US to deliver critical services to thousands of partners and employees throughout the world.

Established new information architecture function and contributed to the creation of an enterprise architecture team at CNA including policies and governance strategies, definition of technology platforms, education and communications practices, metrics and life cycle management.

Responsible for developing and delivering key technology strategies, including all areas of infrastructure, in diverse industries from financial service to consumer goods on a global basis.

Experienced in the support of consolidating and divesting information systems in support of M&A activities as well as the due diligence process. Consolidated the infrastructure of three multi-billion dollar companies into a single environment.
