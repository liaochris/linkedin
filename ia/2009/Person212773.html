Sean Volpe’s Summary

Freelance Data Management Consultant since 2006. Currently focused on transitioning into a permanent role. Have worked as a Data Management specialist helping Sponsors, CROs and Corlabs meet the deliverables of their Device and Drug trials over the last 8 years. Experience includes:

- Being a Lead Data Manager
- Study Start-Up, Interim and Close Data Management activities
- Managing efforts in cross functional environments
- Working on PK, PD, Phase I-IV and PMA studies
- Vendor Selection and Management

- Training junior Clinical Data Management staff
- Writing Data Validation Checks and related documents
- Data Validation, Data Review, and Query Management
- Reconciling SAE study data
- Auditing, Locking & Unlocking study Databases
- Transfer, receipt and integration of Electronic study Data
- Ad-Hoc report generation via SQL Brio, Resolve & Crystal Reports
- Designing & Building Databases and tools
