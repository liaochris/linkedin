David Rowlison’s Summary

An accomplished Manager in cultivating and nurturing executive relationships and driving business development (taking the last firm from ~$1.2M/yr to over $27M/yr in the Canadian marketplace in less than 7 years), I'm seeking that "next level" of responsibility that will provide a suitable challenge both from a personal and professional standpoint.

ULTIMATE OPPORTUNITY >> Would very much like to follow my passion. I'm seeking senior business opportunities in the alternative energy (waste to energy, wind, solar and/or vehicular) fields through leveraging on my strong technology, C level relationship abilities and corporate business development and program management background.

Many years in wireless telecom on several fronts, including site acquisition and development, business development, contract expertise and negotiation as far as telecom repair & supply services, along with responsibility for facilities and real estate round out this second to none background. Most recently, acted as the primary relationship owner with Bell Canada, TELUS, Allstream, Rogers, SaskTel, Shaw and others, including OEMS such as Cisco and Motorola. Key activities most recently entailed the negotiation, implementation and management of contracts for customer supply, in addition to identifying and resolving supply & repair turnaround inefficiencies and the management of new business initiatives, driving revenues to year over year increases.

Proven abilities to identify and drive new market segment growth, utilizing strategic thinking, while leveraging corporate relationships to attain new lines of business, has resulted in great personal growth. Additionally, well balanced experience over multiple years in industrial-commercial development, municipal business & economic development and government liaison, concentrating on technology firms for one of Canada’s leading cities, round out my private and public sector depth of knowledge.
