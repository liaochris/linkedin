Eleanore Willis’s Summary

Our Programming
Dive Travel TV, a travel series . Our Host travels to dive destinations showing the non-diving companions it's not just for divers by checking out what else there is to see and do in that destination.

Our first episode received a Telly award for travel programming. The episode was The Ultimate Live a Board. We took a cruise to four great dive destinations on Carnival Cruise Lines. Our divers enjoyed diving in Belize, Costa Maya, Roatan and Grand Cayman while our non-divers went on canopy tours, hikes, to the beach, turtle farm, river tubing and of course the greatest part was the pool, constant activities, spa treatments and nightly entertainment aboard the ship. Everyone had an amazing time and arrangements couldn't have been easier.

Since then we have visited several Caribbean islands as well as dive destinations right here in the states. Looking to the future we will venture to additional Caribbean islands but we'll be adding the South Pacific.

Stage My House enjoyed a popular start. Sponsored by the Pocono Mts Assoc of Realtors the program was cut due to the dropping housing market. In this program we supplied a Professional Home Stager, $ 2000. in product and contractors to carry out the renovation necessary to stage a home listed on the market for sale. Of the four homes we staged, 1 remained on the market, 1 was taken off the market as our staging renewed interest in the home and the other 2 were sold shortly after work was completed. With renewed hope in the air we are currently looking to secure funding to bring back Stage My House.

We are currently in production of a new program, Going Greener in Pa. we will bring information to homeowners and business to taking steps to live sustainably. We will answer those unanswered question in an effort to help many get started. Another important part of the program will be decifering where to go and how to obtain the incentive funding that is now available to make that change.
