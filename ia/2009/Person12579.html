ANANDA ROY’s Summary

Ananda has 14 years of progressively increasing responsibility in developing and executing marketing strategy for iconic consumer brands. In his current role as Consultant Director of (Marketing) Strategy for British American Tobacco, he works with executive board directors and heads of business units on a portfolio of global consumer brands (Dunhill, Kent, Lucky Strike and others) commanding over £9 Bn in operating revenues and £2 Bn in profit in 2007.

His projects routinely involve strategic market and consumer opportunity analysis, consumer insight and segmentation studies contributing to the development of compelling marketing propositions, brand portfolio strategy and deployment, consumer-engagement programmes and the management of strategic innovations. Combining robust analytical techniques and a creative flair, Ananda has been able to contribute to thought-leadership projects that will determine BAT’s response to economic downturn, create sustainable premium brands, and deepen its engagement with adult consumers in strategic channels.

Ananda received his MBA with high distinction in 2006 from the Warwick Business School. He added further Executive Education specialisation in Strategy and Marketing from the London Business School in 2007.
