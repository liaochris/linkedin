Richard J. Krasney, CFP®’s Summary

TWITTER: http://twitter.com/PhilanthropyCFP
I ACCEPT ALL INVITATIONS TO CONNECT RKRASNEY@RJKWEALTH.COM

RJK Wealth Management provides wealth and investment management for successful business owners and their families and customized philanthropic advice for people wanting a partner in making a difference. As one of the leading niche wealth management firms in the New York Area, we help extracate business owners from their businesses, think through, steward, and coordinate the process of financial transition, manage the family investments, and coordinate the professional team. RJK Wealth Management helps a limited number of clients uncover what's important to them, their passions, values, and desires, then translate them into the foundation of their legacy. RJK Wealth specializes in guiding clients through major financial transitions such as business sales, retirement, or succession planning, and assists in the assembling and coordination of a client’s entire financial, tax, legal and professional team. We help successful business owners and their families achieve what's important to them, we make social entrepreneurs, we make a difference for our clients and the world. We are looking to explore a limited number of new opportunities with the right clients. Please contact me for more information.

Richard is Executive Producer of “A Work In Progress”, a documentary about how one person can make a difference. The story follows philanthropists, celebrities, and ordinary citizens who unite behind a big cause proving anything is possible and that there is not such thing as impossible.

Richard is author of the forthcoming book: “Crossroads Ahead: Proceed with Passion”, a retirement, self-help book for the retiring Baby Boomer

Personal Chief Financial Officer, Wealth Manager, Philanthropic Advisor, Author, Public Speaker, Documentary Executive Producer, Personal Chief Financial Officer for successful business owners and entrepreneurs.
