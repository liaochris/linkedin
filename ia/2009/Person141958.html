Lisa Dennys’s Summary

Lisa Dennys is an expert at helping people deeply reconnect with themselves, bringing self-acceptance, peace of mind and clarity to their life direction. Her life’s work evolved from years of seeking deeper truths about how we make ourselves sick and exhausted by losing touch with, and failing to humbly accept and embrace, the core of what makes us tick.

Lisa brings plenty of varied perspectives to her current work as a Life Coach. From her experiences as an Occupational Therapist, to Doctor of Chinese Medicine & Acupuncture, to Medical Intuitive and Spirit Guide Channeler, Lisa uniquely blends Western and Eastern thinking, science and intuition in her client offerings. Preceding each of her career changes was a frustrating time of recognizing that there must be more to life and more to health than most of us realize. An avid learner and master of asking good questions, Lisa vowed to figure out how we can all live our truth to create a more satisfying life in our external world that honestly reflects who we are at our core. Her beloved channeled Spirit Guide, Sky Pony, describes his partnership work with Lisa as “bringing people home to themselves”. As a certified Power Coach®, she shares her compassionate presence with international clients and groups, providing a range of individual and group life coaching, workshops and teleclasses.
