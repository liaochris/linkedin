Peter Quinn’s Summary

Partner Alliance Strategist / Business Development Manager / Business Consultant.

As Senior Vice President of Strategic Alliances at The First Marblehead Corporation, ranked the #2 public company in Massachusetts by The Boston Globe, Pete provided leadership for optimizing partner relationships. Pete previously served roles as Senior Vice President of Corporate Strategy, and Vice President of Business Development, where he was chosen to lead a major initiative to redesign the consumer experience.

As a management consultant, Pete served as Chief Operating Officer and Director of Business Development with a company in k-12 education. With another organization, Pete assessed the effectiveness of the company’s acquisition integration plan, identified and drove solutions to increase ROI.

Before working as a management consultant, Pete was Director of Partner Alliances for a start-up organization where, within nine months, Pete initiated and closed 27 of 30 deals, representing access to over 35 million individuals.

After graduating from business school, Pete spent eleven years at PricewaterhouseCoopers (formerly Coopers & Lybrand). As Senior Manager, Pete was responsible for business development and delivery of consulting services. Recognized for completing projects profitably, on-time, with high levels of client satisfaction, Pete played a key role in developing the course that became the blueprint for the Firm's approach to recruiting, coaching and career development.

Pete's experience includes work as a Research Analyst at Boston International Advisors, an international asset management firm, and teaching mathematics and coaching ski racing at the Waterville Valley Academy.

Pete earned a Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics from the University of New Hampshire, where he was a member of the Division 1 Alpine Ski Racing Team, and an M.B.A. from the Whittemore School of Business and Economics at the University of New Hampshire.
