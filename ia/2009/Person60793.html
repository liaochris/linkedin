Dee-Ann LeBlanc’s Summary

Over nearly twenty years I have created and presented content of one sort or another. For example, I've:

* Written over 300 articles
* Written 14 computer books
* Earned an RHCE (Red Hat Certified Engineer)
* Written over 20 courses
* Presented many talks and workshops both online and at conferences
* Taught many courses online, on-site, and within training facilities
* Created exams for determining if a potential hire knows their subject matter
* Been on the editorial staff for a few Web sites and print magazines
* Done technical and developmental editing for computer book publishers
* Performed as an editorial consultant for computer book publishers (review line of Linux books and make recommendations, do the same for specific books, including comparative analysis with competition)
* Reviewed book proposals for computer book publishers
* Won awards for both books and articles
* Edited a weekly tech newsletter
* Appeared on radio and television as a Linux expert

As you can see have made a career out of translating Geek to English and distilling complex technical information from many sources into an easily-digestible format. I take great pride in using this ability with audience levels from "Dummies" to heavily technical--there is a definite pleasure in watching people suddenly understand something that has been frustrating them.

I've also written and presented non-technical material.
