Raza Ahmad’s Summary

Economist and governance expert with nearly 16 years of work experience in Asia-Pacific and the Balkans. In-depth knowledge of South Asian political economy and governance issues. Areas of interest include: public administration, political economy, anti-corruption, decentralization and institutional change .Author of several research papers published in international journals and edited volumes. Expertise in legal and institutional reforms and fluency in drafting national/sub-national laws, furnishing advice on policy process and public resource management.
