Thomas John Darcy’s Summary

As the CEO of the US subsidiary of Mangold International, the world leader in software solutions in the area of qualitative and quantitative data analysis, Thomas is setting the groundworks for the future success of Mangold International in the North American growth market for human factor, usability and behavior and protocol analysis tools used in science, industry and government.

Thomas is an innovative and results oriented senior-level sales and marketing executive with international and consultative selling experience within software, media, online marketing, high-tech, and professional service segments. Combine strong business and technology acumen with multi-industry experience in CRM, business intelligence, internet technology opportunities, creating new revenue streams, while growing value and leverage through emerging technology solutions and diverse partnerships.

Thomas John Darcy currently serves on the Board of Directors of Mangold International Inc. Atlanta and North American Technology Partners LLC.
