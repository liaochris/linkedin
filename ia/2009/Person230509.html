Teresa Canna’s Summary

As a versatile Interior Design professional with many years of successfully designing interiors and notable publications, Teresa brings an understanding for the purpose and impact of Green Design. She supports designs that promote and advance sustainable homes, workplaces and communities to enhance the quality of urban life, green technology and green building. One of Teresa's passions is to encourage the preservation of Chicago Architectural historic buildings and is in pursuit to assist with the process of restoration and expansion of those buildings.

Teresa has an extensive background in sales and marketing of consumer goods and services. With demonstrated skills in organizing, planning meetings and special events that help companies and organizations create and cultivate relationships.

Volunteer activities for Field Museum, Chicago Gourmet, Entrepreneurial Women’s Conference, Green City Market Chefs’ Festival, Lincoln Park Festival, Old Town Art Fair, Cocina de Coprodeli USA, LIFT a glass, Leading India's Future Today, Group renovation for Chicago's Women's Shelter, Blue Ribbon Ball, Chicago Sustainable Business Alliance.
