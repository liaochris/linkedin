Greg Mesaros’s Summary

Greg Mesaros is the Founder & CEO of eWinWin, Incorporated. Formed in 1999, the company has appeared in multiple media outlets and has won awards for its innovative technology.

In 2009, eWinWin was named by Inc Magazine as
"One of the Fastest Growing Private Companies" in America.

Mesaros is the inventor of five patents with an additional 19 patents pending in the areas of social pricing, group buying, and e-commerce.

The company has commercialized social pricing solutions in such diverse industries as health and beauty and nutraceuticals, to plastics and nutritional supplements.

Mesaros graduated Beta Gamma Sigma with an MBA in Finance from Loyola Marymount University in Los Angeles, California, and a BSBA from BGSU in Bowling Green, Ohio.
