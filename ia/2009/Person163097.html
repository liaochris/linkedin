Michelle Mercier’s Summary

I'm an accomplished international B2B marketing professional who’s studied and worked in Canada and Europe. I excel in integrated marketing communications, branding, product marketing and channel marketing and I'm a web analytics and social media enthusiast. I hold a BComm, an International MBA and soon an Award of Achievement in Web Analytics.

I initiated a breakthrough branding program for Creo’s thermal imaging technology, leveraging global business partnerships and sales efforts to prevent early commoditization. I also raised the bar in my industry by introducing the use of integrated campaigns that consistently outshined competitors and engaged audiences.

With my multi-language skills and fundamental acceptance of cultural differences, I have the proven ability to produce results with business partners and colleagues around the globe. My superior ability to learn, drive to excel and dynamic disposition have earned me the reputation of top performer.

I am interested in consulting contracts or an in-company marketing role that will integrate my traditional marketing background with my new digital marketing capabilities.
