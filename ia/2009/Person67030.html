Darren Kelly’s Summary

I apply Unified Modeling Language, Systems Modeling Language, Java, XML Schema, model-driven engineering, symbolic algebra, patch-based signal processing, and real-time synthesis technologies to tasks in software engineering, science, systems engineering, and the arts.

I originally trained and worked as a mathematician and physicist, and I have applied computers to analysis, modelling, and simulation of many systems including: scientific instruments such as radio-telescopes, particle accelerators, and neutron beam instruments; astrophysical systems such as supernovae; scientific data analysis; biological population dynamics; secure internet architectures and middleware; metamodelling; cognitive science.

I have made original contributions to science and technology including:

- development of the accelerometer-driven “air instrument” Drancing, which synthesises music and visuals from dance.

- the UML Parsing Analysis recipe for relating technical texts to UML model elements.

- port-based UML and SysML models of neutron beam instruments and radio-telescopes.

- Java bindings and a generative XML Schema for the NeXus scattering sciences data format.

I command a wide range of forward-engineering technologies, including many UML tools, MDA, the Eclipse Modeling Framework, Eclipse XSD, and Webel's custom Java-driven forward-engineering.

I led re-development of a SysML tool, and I have worked extensively with UML tool developers. I am familiar with the OMG's UML and SysML specifications and metamodels, and I have participated actively in the SysML Revision Task Force.

I have promoted UML and SysML education through development of courses and workshops, through foundation of The MagicDraw UML eSchool, and through educational examples on www.webel.com.au.

I offer advanced UML and SysML training courses for scientists, engineers, and IT developers through Webel.

Darren Kelly’s Specialties:

UML, SysML, simulation, modeling, analysis, design, control, software architecture, Java, C++, XML Schema, design patterns, UML-based re-factoring of software systems, Drupal CMS web sites, scientific instruments, beam instruments, data analysis, real-time audio and video synthesis, accelerometer music.
