David R. Shultz’s Summary

I have been involved in sales and marketing management for the past 20 years. During that time I recruited, trained, and motivated hundreds of direct sales personnel. I am captivated by the human experience that comes with direct selling. The interaction between the sales consultant and the customer. The psychology of the sales process. The fragility between getting the sale and losing the sale. The philosophy that ties together sales, life and a higher consciousness we all have within our reach.
I am lucky to have mentors that taught me how to reach for and achieve the next level in my development. I am also extremely fortunate to have been able to express the significance of self-development, enhancing sales skills and sales psychology to all my colleagues in a powerful and sometimes sage training and motivational message. Today I look for avenues to share my message that is so important in helping sales staffs achieve their personal and professional goals.
