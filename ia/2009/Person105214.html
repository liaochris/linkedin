    Audited fair value adjustments to each asset/liability affected in a $2.1 billion acquisition of a Texas bank. Audited goodwill on subsidiary’s books as a result of push-down accounting.

    Interacted with client personnel at various levels, from CEO, CFO, directors, and managers to clerks and other accountants. Supervised new associates and interns on engagements.

    Highly-rated for individual quality work and performance among all associates in Dallas, Fort Worth and Little Rock. Significant contributor in campus recruiting activities.

    Completed three busy seasons including an internship. Audit experience in domestic banking, real estate venture capital, and retail industries. Clients included AmeriCredit, RadioShack, BBVA, Southside Bancshares, RG Financial, and PNL Companies.
