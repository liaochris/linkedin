Dahna Chandler [LION]’s Summary

I'm an award-winning, internationally published former business journalist with over a decade of business writing experience for recognized and respected national publications who has 'retired' from journalism to purse my other avocation--media relations.

A professional publicity agent and media relations strategist since 1993, I'm passionate about my clients' work and, an expert at building audiences and attracting fans; I'm passionate about getting them publicity. I couple my media savvy with my outstanding, award-winning journalistic skills to develop creative, innovative publicity campaigns that support your overall marketing objectives as well as your immediate need for increased press visibility.

During over a decade of experience as a national journalist who has been syndicated in news media in nearly every major city--including Washington DC metro, Boston, New York City, New York State, Dallas, Los Angeles, Miami and Atlanta, I have established credibility with journalists and their organizations by understanding what they want, how and when.

Accordingly, because I know and honor what publications want for their readers, I only pitch them relevant stories, not spam them with "canned" pitches. By understanding the needs of busy journalists, editors and producers, I connect easily and build rapport with decision-makers in the top-tier print and broadcast press to get your stories press.

In cases where I write news content for you, by employing current press writing practices and crafting the press materials and pitches that provide editors and producers what they demand--interesting, newsy, content relevant to their audience--I can maximize the media attention you receive.

Let me maximize my media savvy, whether within your major US metropolitan area or nationally, to successfully execute major media campaigns on behalf of your organization, client, event or venue, to get you press.
