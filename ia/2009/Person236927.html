CAREER FOCUS
A strategic leadership role with an organization experiencing organizational change that will capitalize on my abilities to:

 Communicate effectively to ensure that complex situations are clarified and explained at all levels leading to increased executive support;

 Solve problems to identify issues, establish action plans, and provide the tactical leadership that organizes teams quickly and optimizes resources;

 Lead strategically to design inventive programs that allow companies to expand product offerings and penetrate new markets;

 Organize teams to create an infrastructure that addresses changing environments and establishes protocols to achieve corporate goals;

 Facilitate communication between business and technology groups leading to optimal solutions to key business issues.

SUMMARY OF QUALIFICATIONS
 More than 15 years experience in strategic leadership, crisis management, and executive administration. Advanced in responsibility from analyst positions through various management roles to Director of Business Architecture of a major global benefits consulting organization.

 Involved in all facets of strategic planning including M&A integration, new venture proposal, product design, road mapping, “solutioning,” ROI analysis, and budget planning.

 Specific strengths in crisis management

 Exceptional communication skills allow progress in uncertain environments by breaking down issues in ways that are easily understood by others.

 A particular aptitude in understanding complex business environments has facilitated troubleshooting and analyzing business issues to affect resolutions, including business process redesign.

 Contagious enthusiasm, flexibility, creativity and objectivity have allowed successful execution of initiatives in intricate and constantly changing business climates.

 A pragmatic innovator who is focused on growth through continuous improvement and is unafraid of change.
