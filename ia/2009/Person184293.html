Peter Carillo’s Summary

Since joining Eastern Consolidated in 2002, I have negotiated over $500 million in sales transactions, on behalf of both buyers and sellers, for a wide variety of property types including office, multifamily, retail, and development sites.

I have a clear understanding and approach to the New York market, consequently that has give me access to a vast network of experts in all aspects of real estate including attorneys, tax specialists, environmental experts, and estate planners allowing to conclude some noteworthy transactions

As a noted accomplishment to date was my involvement in the high-profile $225 million sale of a 48-building portfolio of properties located in East Harlem to a UK Real Estate Investment Group; he represented the seller and the buyer in this transaction getting a benchmark price for this portfolio and at the same time helping the purchaser enter the New York Real Estate market arena.

In addition other some noteworthy transactions are: a construction loan for 330 East 57th Street, helping the overseas developer secured a loan that permitted him build the 14-story residential condominium, also a back-to-back "1031 Exchange” transactions that involved the sale of 940 St. Nicholas Avenue and 220 East 52nd Street for $6.8Million and $6.05 Million, respectively.
