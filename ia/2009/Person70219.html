Ed Wilson’s Summary

ED WILSON

SENIOR INTEGRATED CIRCUIT LAYOUT DESIGN ENGINEER

● Design ● Analysis ● Debug ● PDK-Qual ● Training

Position Statement

I am a Senior Integrated Circuit Layout Design Engineer with strong experience in cell level thru chip level mask design. I have expertise in ‘top-down’ design using Cadence® Virtuoso® flow, including assisted layout, cell and chip level analog auto routers. I have been the Virtuoso® flow, technical use and training resource for a design group of 14 designers. My experience has been with three of the top ten U.S. integrated circuit companies. In addition to my layout skills, I have expertise in design-tool/PDK evaluation and implementation. I am known for my problem analysis skills and ‘get the job done’ attitude. My most recent experience is in deep sub micron (90, 65 and 45 nm) analog circuit layout design and debug.

Key Competencies include:
Layout Design: Amplifier, RX-TX, Up Mixer/Level Shifter, Analog/HV Logic, VCO, DAC/ADC, MUX/DEMUX, PLL, DECODER, Synth., RF Codec,
Power Management.

Tools and Process Analysis: 65nm PDK Roll out and debug
45nm PDK Roll out and debug
Layout/Design Verification Tools
Layout liaison w/Process and Verification Staff

Ed Wilson’s Specialties:

Virtuoso® Top Down Design Flow:

Analog layout tools are moving toward automation. Cadence® Virtuoso® flow offers several levels of analog layout automation. A proven flow is TOP DOWN DESIGN. A Chip is broken into top level ‘super blocks’, shown as rectangles & signal pins at top level; so on down through the hierarchy. Virtuoso® provides quite reasonable size estimates & interconnect information, very early in the design. Layout rework & design cycle
time are greatly with this methodology
