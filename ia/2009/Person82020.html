Fred Zeth’s Summary

I have five years of bench experience as a synthetic researcher utilizing such instrumentation as LC, GC, GCMS and ICP on a regular basis.

I left the lab to pursue a career in sales and marketing. I have sold instrumentation ranging from $10K to $1M per instrument. This has included analytical equipment such as GC, LC, GCMS, LCMS, OES, ICP, AA, Spectrophotometers, TOC, Thermal Analysis, FTIR, XRF, Microscopy, NMR and Animal Imaging Systems. I have sold to various industries such as pharmaceuticals, industrial, government, as well as academia.

I have excelled at successfully achieving high level selling to directors and management at all levels. This has included Global Applications for both Pharma, and Industrial accounts. I have also dealt with contract negotiation and technical product marketing and launching.
