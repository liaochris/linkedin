John Wavle’s Summary

John brings 25 years of experience working with and for technology companies to PCS. Companies ranging in size from multibillion dollar companies such as Unisys to much smaller early stage companies such as Cerebellum Software and RedSiren have profited by his experience in sales and marketing, senior management, operations, customer service and technical support. One of John's strengths has been in building successful sales teams for local companies. John is a graduate of Westminster College with a degree in Business Administration. Over the years, he has remained active with his college fraternity board and received national recognition for his volunteer work with the fraternity.

John specializes in helping Pittsburgh companies grow on the local and national level. If you have a need for qualified professionals, then he has the resources that are capable of providing you with candidates that will fit your criteria.
