Jonathan Wells’s Summary

All of my professional activities fit into the category of Personal Development Author and Mentor. My focus is on writing and creating practical programs for personal growth. My background is actually in the area of health and wellness. Nutritional counseling put me in constant contact with people looking to make significant personal changes.

About twenty years ago I began learning about pain and pleasure paradigms and the effects of emotional anchoring, mental imagery, learned behavior and established belief patterns. When I applied this knowledge to help overweight clients change their emotional association with food, the results were phenomenal.

From that point on my focus was on helping others to get in touch with their own beliefs and emotional anchors as a first step. Over time I developed a series of questions and written exercises designed to help you discover your deepest beliefs about yourself as a person, expose areas of emotional conflict, and establish internal harmony. This is now the basis of my work.
