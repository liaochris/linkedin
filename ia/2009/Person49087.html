David Cummings’s Summary

Cool Life Systems is a provider of trusted, on-demand services that provide business solutions in database management, e marketing, and CRM. They serve vertical markets including media corporations, associations, professional practices, small businesses, and Matchmaking firms.

A system consists of a single hosted environment that manages every aspect of an organizations database, administration, marketing, and web presence. They are accessed by a secure log-in, where every user has access to the functions relative to their duties and responsibilities.

Cool Life Systems offers unique features with dynamic functionality crafted to meet your business processes. We offer several different software-systems, depending on the industry and size of the organization. Each suite gives clients a distinct and trusted web presence that is essential to compete in today’s technology driven markets.

Cool Life Systems proudly delivers our clients the cutting-edge advantage that overwhelming sets us both apart from our respective competition. They have access to quality information in a dynamic environment with real-time data and interactive communications. The products are intuitive, easy to manipulate and reasonably priced.

"Our focus and commitment is in educating, administrating and servicing every client; and to become a valuable resource for each client’s business." - David Cummings, CEO
