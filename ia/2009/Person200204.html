Robert Bosman’s Summary

Strategic innovator, entrepreneur, author, strategy consultant, CEO and non executive board member, having over 30 years of experience. Specialized in Web Enabled Collaboration, holding 2 patents in that field (a.o. URM - Universal Relation Manager).

Founder and CEO of '2BeWise | Strategic Innovation' (founded in 1978) and co-founder of 'Youniverz | Changing the way we work together' (founded in 2007).

Developer of Practosophy® , (Practical Philosophy) an inspiring blend of economics, computer technology, psychology and philosophy, that boosts creativity, cooperation, communication and productivity in organizations and optimizes the life/work balance. Author of 4 books on this field.

Robert Bosman’s Specialties:

- Specializes in Web 2.0 and beyond.
- Educated as a business economist and CPA; autodidact psychologist and philosopher / practosopher.
-Former partner of Deloitte, The Netherlands. Leader of several innovative international projects.
- Wrote 4 books about ‘Practosophy' & the art of life and leadership.
- Became a high reputation strategic consultant for major national and international organizations, a frequently asked speaker and an inspiring guest lector at several universities.

  • Founding Partner

    Youniverz | changing the way we work together

    (Internet industry)

    December 2007 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    The web is rapidly changing from a search based information tool, into a relation based platform for global collaboration.

    Youniverz' mission: To boost global collaboration by offering Interconnected Worknets. A Worknet is a safe and dedicated webspace where people and organizations of any kind and any size can easily connect and collaborate with common tools.

    Youniverz' Worknets use YouniKeys to optimize collaboration, organization and data exchange on three levels: personal, organizational and in alliances.

    Both Worknets and Younikeys are based on URM - the Universal Relation Manager as made by 2BeWise (patented - PCT pending).
