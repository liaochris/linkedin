Guus van den Brekel’s Summary

Coordinator of Electronic Services of the Central Medical Library advices the head of the Library about new developments in library technology and services. Innovation projects on improving library services, enlarging library presence in user's workflow, web 2.0 - based tools, and exploring virtual (and other social) environments.
