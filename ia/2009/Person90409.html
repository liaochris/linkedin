In order to ease the workload I began automating the use of signal generators (Gigatronics) and Signal Analyzers (HP) using computer routines custom written by myself. This usually elimated 60-70% of the personnel previously needed to be dispatched to a sub in need of repairs.

I used these same techniques to automate several key bottlenecks in a modern aerospace component production facility. My first task was automating a nearly $1 million RF/Microwave Test Lab. Taking testing of parts down from 2 minutes each to under 7 seconds each. Archiving data led to less engineers needed to trace problems in production to their source. CD ROM replaced stacks of paper test results.

Next automated was the Environmental Test Lab and Engineering. After 3 years of work I decided to pursue automation on my own using the new abilites of the internet.
