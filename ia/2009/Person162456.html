Michael Zari’s Summary

Michael Zari leads a 30,000 + real estate investment group that is centered around www.NoBullRE.com, www.GoZoneOnLine.com, and www.GetPreConstructionDeals.com.

An Engineer by degree (MS in Electrical Engineering from University of Alabama in Huntsville in 1990, BS in Engineering Physics from Stevens Institute of Technology in 1986) and investor by desire, Michael now dedicates himself completely to bringing out "Turn Key" high-quality real estate opportunities for his clients and online communities. Being an investor himself, he fully understands the importance of education and opportunity for being successful as an investor.

A native of Alabama for over 23 years, Mr. Zari is an entrepreneur who has founded several successful companies, is an international speaker/presenter, active in his community, and is also a licensed real estate Broker.

Michael Zari’s Specialties:

I specialize in developing Turn Key real estate investment opportunities for individual investors, especially in the residential markets.
