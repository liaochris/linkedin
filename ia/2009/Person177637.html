Dan Marchant’s Summary

Video game industry Producer and Business Development consultant.

Over 20 years in the industry as a Producer, Development Director and Acquisitions at companies including SCi, Virgin and Ocean Inforgrammes.

I currently consult for game developers, helping them to understand publisher-speak.

Current role:
Business Development Consultant - The Obscure Consultancy - (www.obscure.co.uk)

20+ Years industry experience at both developers and publishers as Producer, Senior Producer, Development Manager, Development Director, Product Acquisition. Additional non-development experience includes Marketing & PR.
More than 23 shipped titles across 84 SKUs as a Producer (plus others in a management capacity).
