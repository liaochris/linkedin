Nathan Walker’s Summary

I am an award-winning design professional with ten years of experience in publishing and communications. My skills have resulted in seven state and national awards for my work. I also am an accomplished photographer who has exhibited work in galleries throughout California and Colorado. In addition, I have taught workshops in photography and design to adults and teenagers.

Nathan Walker’s Specialties:

Graphic design, art direction, information architecture, photography, illustration, writing, production, and web development.
