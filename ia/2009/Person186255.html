Peter de Vries’s Summary

My field of expertise concern the social-psychological aspects of behaviour in technological contexts. The experimental research that I have conducted in the past includes automaticity in traffic mode choice, its effects on the execution of behavioural intentions in the context of light regulation, and the effect of interactive feedback in household appliances on energy saving. My doctoral research focused on the use of information in the formation of trust in interactions between users and technology, and the effect of trust on technology use.
