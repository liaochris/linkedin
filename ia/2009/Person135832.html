Kevin Wheeler’s Summary

Entrepreneur, CEO and founder of Global Learning Resources, Inc., The Future of Talent Consortium, and the Australasian Talent Conference.

Author of hundreds of articles and books on recruiting, staffing, corporate learning, corporate universities and an expert in the architecture and development of corporate learning strategies and structures.

Public speaker, Keynoter at major global events. Topics include Talent Management, Talent Acquisition, Talent Development, Corporate Universities, Workforce planning, Global Trends and Human Resources, Talent Acquisition and Development in Asia.

Lecturer and Teacher on Business Strategy, Human Resources, Talent Development and Acquisition at San Francisco State University, University of San Francisco and San Jose State University.

Former corporate executive at several major corporations with extensive global experience in recruiting, staffing, leadership and executive development.

Kevin Wheeler’s Specialties:

Staffing and recruiting strategy development, recruiting process improvement, benchmarking and best practice research, corporate university development, executive development and coaching.
