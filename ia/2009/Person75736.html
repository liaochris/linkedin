Erol Seker’s Summary

My research is focused on catalytic material synthesis for energy conversion and environmental protection applications:
1. Catalytic energy conversion systems (fuel cells and
catalytic combustors)
2. Renewable and alternative fuels (hydrogen, syn gas,
bio-fuels and bio-diesel)
3. Catalytic pollution control (outdoor and indoor air
pollution control)
4. Biological and chemical sensors
5. Photo-catalytic applications
6. Development of micro-reactors and catalysts coated substrates

Erol Seker’s Specialties:

1. Catalyst synthesis and testing
2. Catalyst characterization
3. Analytical techniques
4. Design & Construction of micro-reactor systems for catalyst screening
