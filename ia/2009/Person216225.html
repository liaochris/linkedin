Sheldon Campbell’s Summary

With over twenty years working and living in Latin America, and helping a number of companies start, manage or relocate operations into and out of Mexico, I decided to put my talents to good use, as a consultant to firms wishing to globalize their operations in Latin America.

I have established various manufacturing facilities in Mexico, designed and managed construction of complete plants, as well as expansion/refurbishment projects, and managed operations here for over twenty years. I can perform turnkey start-ups, or simply consult on critical aspects, as needed.

I have worked at every level of manufacturing operations, from maintenance mechanic up to vice-president. My knowledge of Mexican import/export, labor and tax laws, and operations management, coupled with my fluent Spanish and innate leadership skills, enables me to optimize operations for foreign companies wishing to locate themselves in Mexico.

Sheldon Campbell’s Specialties:

Establish new international entity, in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations; identify optimum locations; aid in site selection and plant design; manage bidding and contracting phase of construction or modification; assist in satisfying workforce and staffing needs; setup of appropriate import/export procedures; conduct training of staff; performance and compliance auditing, and more. My highly developed leadership and mentoring skills help ensure the success of your operation.
