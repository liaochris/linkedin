Mike Jefferies’s Summary

Red Urban is Omnicom's newest international digital network with offices in Amsterdam, Dallas, London, Munich and New York. Red Urban is a digital network that creates communication ideas that live in both the on and off line world that build consumer brand experiences.

Clients include Diageo, Microsoft and Vodafone.

Mike Jefferies’s Specialties:

Mike has worked across Omnicom's leading advertising, brand consulting, direct/CRM, PR and digital agencies and has a rare insight and experience in how to build communication campaigns that work well across multiple channels.
