Bob Reissiger’s Summary

I am a trained and certified executive coach helping leaders grow and develop to increase results and opportunities for advancement and achieve a higher quality of life. My ideal clients are current leaders, leaders in new roles, leaders wanting high performing teams and high potential associates.

During my career as a regional operations executive in two industry-leading financial institutions, Legacy Wachovia and Bank of America I mobilized and inspired my teams to achieve high performance and directed change in an organized and structured way. My teams worked collaboratively with all constituencies achieving high performance in quality, productivity, unit cost and associate/ client satisfaction.

I also managed or directed numerous projects - several Six Sigma based - related to outsourcing, consolidations, mergers, new product development and systems conversions. In my roles I showed strong performance development, the ability to operate in ambiguous situations under aggressive and demanding time lines, the ability to translate strategies/situations into tactical and achievable plans, the ability to react swiftly to consistent change and encourage and motivate others.

I am recognized as results oriented and strong developer of leaders. During my career, I helped to develop leaders that now occupy significant positions of responsibility with large banks and other companies and industries. These senior leaders have responsibility in a variety of functions including sales, operations, human resources, risk management and project management.

I graduated from Georgia State University with a degree in Marketing. I attended the LSU School of Banking of the South, the Wachovia/Duke University Executive Education School and the Wachovia Leadership Development Program.

A native of Santiago, Chile, I immigrated to the United States in 1964 and I am conversant in Spanish.

Bob Reissiger’s Specialties:

I am an executive coach with over 25 experience in leadership development, talent management, and financial services. My ideal clients are current leaders, leaders in new roles, leaders wanting high performing teams and high potential associates.

I received Coach Training and Certification through The Coaches Training Institute. I am also certified by, and a member of, the International Coach Federation. I also serve on the board of the Georgia Coach Association.
