Patric Salomon’s Summary

Patric Salomon has an engineering degree from TU Berlin and is currently the Managing Partner of 4M2C PATRIC SALOMON GmbH, the German partner of enablingMNT Group. In addition to working on consultancy projects with industry, he has been involved in research projects focussing on the transfer of R&D results into industry, business models for R&D services, roadmapping, and on marketing/dissemination for engineering/manufacturing services.

Within the European Commission (EC) funded “Design for Micro & Nano Manufacture (PATENT-DfMM)” network and the Europractice initiative, Patric has been responsible for dissemination and also supported the exploitation of research into commercial service offers. Patric has also built extensive links into the Middle and East European countries and Overseas.
Patric Salomon is currently the Vice-Chairman of NEXUS, the European Microsystems Association. He has been involved in Technology Roadmapping in Micro Nano technologies: NEXUS Microsystems Roadmap, PATENT-DfMM Packaging Roadmap, PATENT-DfMM Roadmap on MNT Design Technologies and the Micro-nano Manufacturing Roadmap of MINAM, the European Technology Platform under the MANUFUTURE umbrella.

He is a contributing editor of MST News, the European Microsystems magazine and MicroNano, a leading US-based newsletter – now part of R&D Magazine. Patric has supported governments such as DIIRD Victoria/Australia in its nano programme exploitation strategies and he has been a referee for the European Commission in both areas MicroNanoSystems (ICT) and NanoManufacturing (NMP).

He works with industry on supporting their IPR/marketing strategies. He runs media/press relations for SMEs (press releases and articles) with am emphasis on efficient distribution through the use of existing publicity channels. Other offers to customers are advice on business strategies, strategic partnerships, identification of funding opportunities and consultancy on topics all along the MNT supply chain.

Business Strategy Consulting, Project Management, Strategic Marketing, Marketing Communications, Sales/Partner Network Development, Market Research, and Publication of Industry Reports (www.enablingmnt.com).
