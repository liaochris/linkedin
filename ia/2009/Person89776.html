Gregory Lee’s Summary

I am an American multilingual entrepreneur leading new ventures for over 16 years in Europe and China. I also work as an independent board director / advisor, and privately invest in companies related to:

(1) Clean Tech and Alternative Energy;
(2) Chinese ventures expanding into Europe; and
(3) Digital Media Web 2.0.

Gregory Lee’s Specialties:

I have nine years professional experience living in Japan and China; and another nine years in Europe (Spain, France, Holland). My work has been in the Cleantech, software, electronics and digital media industries.
I have acquired deep knowledge in industrial marketing, finance, and business processes on a global scale - both as a director in multinational companies and as an entrepreneur. I am business fluent in Spanish, and conversant in French, Japanese, and Mandarin.

Gregory Lee’s Experience

  • Diamond Wood China

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Renewables & Environment industry)

    2007 — Present (2 years)

    Diamond Wood China is a pioneer in helping Chinese manufacturers and construction firms transition away from the use of endandgered tropical hardwood species to a new environmentally-friendly species, called Accoya™ (www.accoya.info).

    Accoya™ is a novel form of technically-modified softwood with performance characteristics that surpass even the best hardwood species. Accoya™ has proven to be an effective substitute for external applications for which expensive tropical hardwood, uPVC and aluminium are currently favoured. These applications include external doors, windows, decks, cladding (siding), flooring and furniture.
