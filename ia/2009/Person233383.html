Tilottama Mukherjee’s Summary

Certified Project Management Professional (PMP®) with over nine years experience as delivery manager, business analyst and team lead.

Proficient in program management; process development and implementation; vendor management; solutions design & development; people care for E-learning and Education domains.

Experienced in working with clients across the globe from diverse verticals including Financial Services, Consumer Goods, Technology, Automotive, Entertainment, Healthcare, Hospitality and Education most recently.

Recently shifted gears to enter a new domain - Education. My current engagement with iDiscoveri Education is both professionally satisfying and personally fulfilling as it is a knowledge area close to my heart.
