Jon Pratlett’s Summary

For 17 years Jon has provided strategic and tactical support, training & facilitation in the development of leader and team member capability at many of Australia's top companies from AMP, Commonwealth Bank, CSL and Westpac, to National Australia Bank, CSR, Foxtel, IBM, Macquarie Bank, to name a few.

A 2 time Hawaii Ironman Triathlon finisher, and 5 time Australian Ironman finisher, Jon has the endurance, discipline and resilience to get you to your next finish line.

"I have worked with many facilitators during my career and I have no hesitation in saying that Jon is one of the best. He is well organised and well researched but more importantly he has the mental agility to deal with vastly different group dynamics and challenges posed by the idiosyncrasies of some very strong egos.
Chris Skilton. Chief Executive Officer. Suncorp Metway.
