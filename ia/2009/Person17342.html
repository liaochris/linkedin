Annie RAMAHATRA’s Summary

Holder of a PhD in organic chemistry, especially on medicinal plants chemistry, I carried out in the same time a career of academic and a career of person in charge in Drug companies where I occupied positions in R & D, in Quality control and in production. Thereafter, I devoted myself mainly to industry, in particular in Quality relating to the Production then in Quality concerning Research. To bring success to the missions, which were assigned to me, I developed capacities which permit me to determine and apprehend the stakes, in order to be force of proposal to work out the action plans to be implemented. I wish to invest myself in Quality in general in Drug Company.
