Kris Hertoghe’s Summary

The DenK iP group is a patent attorney organisation that aims at fulfilling the need for a patent organization that combines high quality intellectual property work at competitive prices with personal assistance and satisfying customer services. It consists of two members, iP HEAD, run by Kris Hertoghe and having its office in Merelbeke near Gent, and Wauters Intellectual Property, run by Davy Wauters and having its office in Zemst near Mechelen. Davy and Kris both are qualified patent attorneys with a technical background knowledge in physics, optics, mechanics, electronics and similar fields.

Kris Hertoghe’s Specialties:

Protection of intellectual property, in particular patent drafting and patent prosecution in the field of electronics, semiconductors, mechanics, physics, engineering, computer-related inventions and similar fields.
