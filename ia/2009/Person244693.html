Vitaliy Levit (v.levit@recessapp.com)’s Experience

  • Co-Founder

    Recess Mobile, Inc

    (Wireless industry)

    April 2009 — Present (8 months)

    Recess is a simple waitlist management and two-way text message paging system.

    With our text-message paging system, we reduce the frustration of long wait times and give vendors a means of strengthening relationships with their customers through targeted mobile communication, while providing a lower-cost, more effective paging solution.

    Recess is ideal for restaurants, doctor's offices, pharmacies, government agencies, and anywhere else customers queue up.

    Unlike traditional paging systems, guests don’t need to carry around cumbersome, unsanitary pagers, and Recess notifications work at any range. The system also offers significant cost savings for business owners. A complete package starts at less than $250, compared to upwards of $5k for traditional systems, not including pager replacement expenses of up to $1k/month for busy establishments.

    Recess allows guests to join an establishment’s marketing list. Owners can then send promotional text messages using a campaign management web interface. Every ‘page’ becomes a promotional tool when an offer is appended to the bottom of the message. In a down economy, this can be used for discreet discounting and price discrimination, for reallocating demand to slow periods, and as part of a loyalty program.
