Anurag Shrivastava’s Summary

Executive Search and Consulting ( Companies and individuals )

It is said that rising tide lifts all boats. Everything you touched turned into gold. The 8% + growth rate produced an unprecedented growth in our careers and in our earning. Things never looked so good.

Its all changed now. Economic growth has stalled. Business plans have been shelved due to lack of demand and capital. Companies are being merged, sold and shut down. A reality check is in order.

At a personal level – fear and uncertainty is pervasive. The questions are

1) Will I grow within the company?

2) Will I get the long awaited promotion? and salary increase. Do I deserve it?

3) Do I have the networking skills to reach out to people in times of need? How do I leverage my contacts? What skills and techniques do I need to get better at that.

4) Am I getting along well with my boss? What does he think of me? How do I find out and do better? And how do I improve relationships with my peers and associates?

5) What really makes me happy and productive? Am I doing that? If not then what should I do?

6) Have I answered all the tough questions about myself? Am I still in a state of denial? Has someone asked me those questions?

And many more

As an experienced HR professional who has worked in several world class HR oriented organizations and managed careers of middle and senior management professionals, I feel there is a very strong need for individuals to approach professionals who can help them meander through the minefield and approach the future with confidence. This is where professional, personalized coaching helps you quickly move into a productive mode.

HRNEXT is pleased to announce our new offering to professionals at mid and senior management level “STEPUP” to provide personalized executive coaching to enable you to maximize your potential. The details of the program can be downloaded from the site www.hrnext.in .
