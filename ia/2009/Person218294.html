Simon Zutshi’s Summary

I have been investing in Property since 1995. In 2003 I set up the property investors network to provide a supportive environment to help people become successful property investors.

We run monthly evening property networking events in London, Birmingham, Manhcester, Leeds, Cardiff and Bristol.

We run One day seminars which show you exactly how to ethically buy property below market value from motivated seellers.

I also set up the the Property Mastermind Programme which is a 12 month support programme for serious investors which can help you to build a £1 million property portfolio and a £50,000 income in just 12 months.

One of my passions is professional speaking. My main topic is property investing, but I also speak on wealth creation, passive income, business grwoth and internet marketing. I am a member of the Professional Speakers Association PSA and National Speakers Association NSA.
