Dave Derington’s Summary

Throughout my life I've had the most passion for working on challenging problems. Ever since early childhood I've been surrounded by computers and have naturally gravitated to my work with Internet technologies and Video Games.

Although my background and experience is very technical, I easily wear many hats and navigate through the business world. Via my work experiences both via work and extracurricular activities, I've found my true passion in Project and Event Management. Warfactory is my avocation, and features consulting services and events for the competitive video game industry. What pays the bills is work as a Project Manager or Consultant.

My goals are two-fold. For my career I aim to work for an organization which needs a strong technical leader with business savvy. My personal ambition is to continue to develop Warfactory in my free time to serve as a networking agent and consulting service to the E-Sports market.
