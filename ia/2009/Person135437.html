Michael Kuhbock’s Summary

Empowering Stakeholders to Enhance Value

Michael engineers corporate development for growing businesses.

Enabling A Capacity for Change

Creates dynamic corporate growth strategies that are the leading edge catalyst for success. By building a culture that enables a capacity for change through leveraging corporate assets; creatively, functionally and financially, Michael positions organizations to lead in their respective markets.

Track Record of Success

Working with start ups, global non profits and fortune 1000 companies, Michael has a 25 year history of empowering teams, divisions and organizations.
He enables organizations to realize their vision and facilitates their leadership in the marketplace.

Successfully led start up organizations and Fortune 1000 companies through record growth and operational profitability having an extensive track record of creating new opportunities throughout North America by moving quickly into regional geographies to establish immediate organizational leadership and corporate presence.

As a diversely qualified executive, Michael has over twenty years of progressive capabilities in; strategy enhancement, business development, operation fulfillment, sales leadership, program & project management, consultative marketing, start-up development and corporate reorganizations.

Professional Experience

The creation and implementation of award winning strategic and tactical international business development initiatives spanning numerous lines of business and global cultures.

Developed growth strategies resulting in over 2 billion dollars in capitalized valuation.

The creation, launching and leading two international industry associations developing over 250 partnerships, alliances and members spanning 6 continents in a 4 year timeframe.

Michael Kuhbock’s Specialties:

Broad experience in the Service, Franchise, Oil & Gas, Technology, Health Care, Commercial Real Estate and Development industries with national and global companies.

Successfully led Information Systems thought leadership for start up organizations, international standards bodies and Fortune 1000 companies with a focus on enterprise business integration, alignment strategy and web 2.0 business collaboration.
