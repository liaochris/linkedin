Shay Pierce’s Summary

I'm an experienced and versatile programmer with a passion for not only implementing great game designs, but contributing ideas to make those designs even better!

After graduating with a BSCS in 2003, I was quickly hired by the Houston-based game developer TimeGate Studios, where I proved myself as an intern and was brought on as a Tools Programmer.

In 2005, my breadth of technical knowledge led to my moving to a programming position on Midway's Core Technology Group. Though I again excelled at tool development and working on a modified UE3 engine, my passion for making great games led me to transfer within Midway's Austin studio to an unannounced game team. I worked there on the Gameplay Programming team as the "point man" for implementing in-game user interface elements, working primarily in Flash/Scaleform.

In August 2008 I was among the many laid off when Midway canceled our still-unannounced project and scaled back the Austin studio. This, however, turned out to be a blessing in disguise, as I soon moved to Irvine, CA to take an exciting position at Blizzard Entertainment working on an unannounced project!
