Mike Bowles’s Summary

Experienced with quantitative finance and fully automated quantitative trading. Formal education in signal processing and business from top universities. Several decades of research and development experience as entrepreneur, manager and sole contributor. Excellent communications skills.

Mike Bowles’s Specialties:

• Research and employ modern mathematical statistical methods – expert systems, neural networks, singular spectrum analysis, regime switching, Kalman filtering etc.
• Understand performance bias in back-testing, how to estimate its magnitude and how to minimize it.
• Current programming skills – C++, C, Fortran, Perl, MS Visual Studio, R, Numerical Recipes, Excel, Matlab.
• PhD thesis at MIT - numerical propagation of Bayesian posterior density function to extract signal from noise.
