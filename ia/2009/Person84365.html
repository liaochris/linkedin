Gary Bernier’s Summary

Gary Bernier works with successful people who are often frustrated because they cannot repeat their success as often as they would like. Gary coaches them on how to discover their expertise, their source of talent. He teaches them how to use it, protect it and build a team to foster it; so that success will come easier and more often to them.
