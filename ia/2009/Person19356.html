Ariane Benefit’s Summary

My mission in life is to empower people to succeed! I help people get unstuck from the beliefs, behaviors and habits that aren't serving them and break through the challenges holding them back!

If you struggle with time management, procrastination, perfectionism, disorganization, financial disorganization, and ADD or ADD-like traits, chances are you not only need new skills and habits, you also need to rebuild self-confidence and learn how to "inspire yourself into action naturally." That's my specialty.

Many of my clients have worked with coaches and therapists in the past, but needed that extra something that comes from working with someone who has been where they are and help them get INSPIRED into action.

Having grown up on welfare and learned to manage my own ADD, high creativity and disorganization, I became a master of learning, evolving and reinventing myself to succeed. I became an executive in 1991, started a successful consulting business in 1993, and launched my coaching career in 2005. These experiences uniquely qualify me to help people reinvent themselves and create radical change in their lives, such as becoming successfully self-employed.

I help you see your blind spots in a non-critical way, clarify your true purpose and get energized and inspired to take ACTION to achieve it! I provide the emotional support, feedback, and skills you need to manifest the better life you seek.

I use my 30 years of business experience to not only help you make effective decisions about which ideas to put into action, but also to implement projects in a cost and time effective manner. I can even help you create a google-friendly, highly ranked website that really gets you work.

If you want to simplify your life and have a life coach, organizing coach, and business development consultant all rolled into one uniquely qualified person, learn more about how can I help you succeed at:
www.neatandsimple.com/coaching-and-consulting.html
