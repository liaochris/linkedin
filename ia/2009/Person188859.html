Gary Gil’s Summary

I'm a serial entrepreneur who has launched 4 successful companies in the past 15 years. I have extensive background in all aspects of direct-response marketing (on and offline), customer acquisition and retention. I have strong leadership and team-building skills and I love to lead my teams to success.

I enjoy experimenting with innovative new technologies, products, and new ways to market products and services. I'm very results-oriented, however, the only results I trust are the ones the market votes on, whether the market is right or not (Windows vs. Mac OS).

My current company is Podclass.com. Podclass is an innovative way to teach, learn and access online courses from inside of Facebook. Podclass was a recent recipient of the Facebook fbFund, which has opened up many exciting opportunities for the company.

With Podclass, we hope to transform education and enable both students and teachers to access educational material from ANYWHERE they choose, whether that is from inside of Facebook or from an iPhone.

There is nothing more important to the long-term success of a business than its people. So I am actively looking for a "rock star" VP of Engineering/CTO to join the Podclass team as soon as possible.

To carry out my vision for Podclass, I need to have a highly talented team to join me along the way, starting with a CTO or VP of Engineering.

If you know of a highly talented (A-players only) VP of Engineering or CTO who is available, please contact me. Although Podclass is currently based out of San Diego, there is a possibility we will be relocating to Silicon Valley or San Francisco in the near future.
