Michael Torano’s Experience

  • President

    Sevens Full

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    October 2006 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    I have sold most of my interest in PhiMedia (now known as DCSI) and now consult, interact, research and network with DOOH professionals. Sevens Full firmly believes that DOOH content and programming should look nothing like traditional TV nor should advertising be "repurposed and edited" :30 or :60 spots. The OOH environment has unique audience initiatives based on criteria such as dwell time, frequency, unique visitors, endemic products/services as well as traditional demographic profiles (zip, age, gender, cultural, HHI, etc.). These initiatives drive content, programming, layout/design and advertising messaging.
