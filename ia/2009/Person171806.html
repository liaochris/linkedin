Nate Riggs | Social Network Lead Generation’s Summary

Nate Riggs is an entrepreneur with over 10 years of experience in sales, internet marketing, and business development. He is the principal and chief strategist at Social Business Strategies, LLC a new media communication company focused on helping B-to-B companies generate leads, relationships and sales on the social web.

LEAD GENERATION STRATEGY | THE SOCIAL MEDIA GUERRILLA MARKETPLACE
Rooted in classic communication theories, Nate's methods help companies develop visible corporate brand armies of small mobilized teams guerrilla social networkers. These teams leverage both their company and personal brands to communicate across a dispersed media landscape and achieve tactical sales, business development and marketing objectives.

By searching the market place on micro blogs like Twitter, using keyword research and SEO techniques on profile-based social networks like LinkedIn and Facebook, and leveraging content creation and rich media on blogs, niche-targeted social networks and websites, Nate trains teams of professionals on how they can gain visibility to the social media marketplace, find and connect with their target audience, and develop meaningful offline relationships that lead to business opportunities.

PERSONAL SOCIAL BRAND DEVELOPMENT | GAIN VISIBILITY TO BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES
Nate Riggs also coaches solo-entrepreneurs, small business owners, sales and marketing professionals and executive leaders on developing strong personal social media brands that can be used to gain professional credibility and greater visibility for their businesses online.

Nate blogs at www.nateriggs.com where he writes about entrepreneurs and social media business strategies. He has reviewed dozens of new internet services and social networks and regularly speaks to local, regional and national audiences to share his ideas.

For more information on Nate's speaking topics, please visit https://speakersite.com/proflie/nateriggs
