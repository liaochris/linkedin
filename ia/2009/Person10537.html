Alyssa Dver’s Summary

Mint Green Marketing (www.mintgreenmktg.com) provides affordable expert marketing help. Our clients either need a temporary Chief Marketer to develop and manage a strategic plan or they need specific marketing programs developed and implemented. In addition to overall marketing, we also have expertise in corporate social responsibility (CSR) and product management. Projects have included new company or product names, brand, website, collateral, events, etc. Our unique value is speed and ease that we get top quality marketing done.

My latest book is "No Time Marketing: small business-sized steps in 30 minutes or less." It is designed for entrepreneurs and small businesses. It is also very good for larger companies wanting to use the book as a promotion item for attracting small business leads. My older book, "Software Product Management Essentials", continues keeping me busy doing all types of live and web-based trainings.

I often write as a freelancer and my work has been published in Forbes, BusinessWeek, Entrepreneur, Reader's Digest, Promo, MarketingProfs and Software Magazine. I have written on a variety of topics including technology, marketing, corporate social responsibility, and lost child prevention. In Feb. 07, I was thrilled to be recognized by BusinessWeek.com one of 8 female entrepreneurs to watch and in Dec '09, American Express OPEN featured me as their member entrepreneur.

For "fun", I am the Executive Producer and Host of Mom Matters WebTV (www.mom-matters.com). The show features 10 minute videos that provide practical tips for more productive parenting. Amazingly, we have had over 1 million views of our continuously growing archive of shows.

Lastly my personal cause: lost child prevention. I founded Wander Wear Inc. (www.wander-wear.com) and The Center to Prevent Lost Children (www.PreventingLostChildren.org) because of my personal and parental passion to keep kids safe in public places.
