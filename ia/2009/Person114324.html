Jean-Michel de JAEGER, EMBA, PMP’s Summary

Jean-Michel intervenes in a large range of firms as Consultant, Coach and Trainer. He has a strong consultancy skill in Directing Program & Projects Management in various public and private sectors mainly in services industries and has a track record in working at all levels in Corporate organizations from coaching individuals or top Management teams to projects’ groups training and steering large programs with Executive Board level reporting.

After 10 years in Management consulting industry, as Director of 123@Business, a Management Consulting and Training firm, Jean-Michel develop Business Advices in partnership with worldwide consulting Network to build Strategic Program & Change Office for corporate group to Manage Operations of Strategy implementation throughout Corporate Group.

Actually, Jean-Michel share his time between education, consulting and business development activities in Projects and Programs Management topics. As a support to Management Training, he translated into French the Top Management Portal http://www.12manage.com and will promote & develop business related in French speaking countries. He intervenes as education trainer in some large companies in France and oversea, as for one of the world's biggest Petroleum company who set an education program for Engineers to obtain Project Management Certification. He is the owner of the International consultancy in Management Consulting & Training Brand name: 123@Business.

Before that, Jean-Michel worked during 6 years as a Senior Consultant within Coteba Conseil, a French Management Consulting company, since 1998, where he provides organisation and management consulting services to government agencies and Fortune 500 companies with a focus on large project management, strategic planning, and organisational design.

Previously to this role, he has been working for 20 years as Project Manager in IT Engineering companies worldwide.

Jean-Michel de JAEGER, EMBA, PMP’s Specialties:

A Portfolio & Programmes Management skill, an IT Master degree with 20 years experience and an Executive MBA to assist Top Management of Corporate Groups, in todays challenges of Business & Management change Programs, by building a Programs Management Office, including all dimensions of Leadership and HRM, Development, Investment and finance, Marketing and sales, Operations Management, etc...
