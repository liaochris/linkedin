Hoang Le’s Summary

Mr. Le is the Marketing Manager at vabisgroup of companies (VGC). Operating in Vietnam since 1992, VGC has grown to 25 companies and operates in industry sectors such as: property development and construction, education, minerals, IT, networks and events, gaming and racing, and other forms of investment.

Mr. Le is also the University Associate at Curtin Business School.

Prior to joining the VGC, Mr. Le worked as one of the research analysts in Tractus Asia's HCMC, Vietnam office, with primary areas of expertise on industrial projects and market research, particularly within the metalworking and logistics industries as well as the fast-moving consumer goods' sectors.

Mr. Le holds a master degree in international business from Curtin Business School in Australia.

Hoang Le’s Specialties:

Business development, marketing strategy, teaching in management and marketing
