Australia China Alumni Association’s Summary

The ACAA is an umbrella association and welcomes China-based alumni of all Australian universities. The ACAA aims to connect alumni with their university and with fellow alumni in China. We're a not-for-profit organisation supported by the Australian government and 17 'partner universities' and we're proud to have Australia's Ambassador to China Dr Geoff Raby as our patron.

We run regular events in Beijing and Shanghai.
Register now on our website: www.AustChinaAlumni.org
to receive regular event updates, special offers and job alerts.

If you register on the website and include your mailing address you'll also receive a FREE subscription to our media partner's business magazine 'Australia China Connections'.

We also provide services to our university funding partners, and if you've connected with us on linkedin and you attended one of our partner institutions we'll pass your contact details back to them so they can get in touch with you about special university-specific news and events in China and elsewhere.

We hope to see you at an ACAA event soon!

Our Gold Funding Partners (7) are: The University of Newcastle, Central Queensland University, La Trobe University, the University of Technology Sydney, Griffith University, Deakin University, and the University of South Australia.

Our Silver Funding Partners (10) are: Murdoch University, Southern Cross University, Queensland University of Technology (QUT), Curtin University of Technology, the University of Western Australia, RMIT University, Swinburne University, University of Western Sydney, Edith Cowan University and Charles Sturt university.

Australia China Alumni Association’s Specialties:

Alumni Networking, Alumni Social Events, BBQs, Speaker Function Events, Career Days, Hiking Trips, Ski Trips, etc.
