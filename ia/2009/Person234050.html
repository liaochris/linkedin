Tim Owen’s Summary

As product manager, I enjoy finding new opportunities to use technology to solve difficult business problems. That includes market segmentation and sizing analysis, market problem identification, product solution definition, marketing message creation, sales tool creation, and meeting with customers and prospects to drive our products so they provide maximum value.

I plan to continue to implement new methods to improve product management's ability to drive our revenue while decreasing costs, or creating other intangible benefits for our target market customers. Mid-term goal is to gain experience and skills needed for executive level positions for a mid-size organization or a similar sized division of a larger company.

Tim Owen’s Specialties:

Deep understanding of insurance licensing regulation, Internet technology, software product development / management. Have worked with over 100 of the top insurance carriers and agencies/brokerages in the US. Have also been trained in and applied several leading sales methodologies including CustomerCentric Selling, Process Selling, Solution Selling, Sandler and others. Pragmatic Marketing certified for Product Management.
