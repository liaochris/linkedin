Paul Plotkin’s Summary

Paul has been in the broadcast video industry for over 20 years. He has the pleasure of and continues to service such clients as Viacom Networks, HBO, Showtime, Turner Broadcasting, ESPN, Plymouth Rock Studios and many others. Some of his corporate clients include Foxwoods Resort & Casino, MIT Lincoln Labs and Morgan Stanley.
Paul is also a freelance cameraman who has work for various production companies. He is a staple at the yearly Yale Commencement Exercises, and has had the pleasure of shooting such luminaries as Tony Blair, Anderson Cooper and Sir Paul McCartney.
Paul was first camera for Mayor Vincent "Buddy" Cianci, First Night Providence.
