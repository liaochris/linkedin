Graham Riley’s Summary

I provide career consulting and job search support services to clients and I also offer presentations and workshops based on "Maximizing the Effectiveness of Your Job Search Process" to networking, job support or job transition groups.

The workshop or presentation is designed to help individuals articulate, present and market the “value” of their services to hiring managers and organizations, thereby maximizing the effectiveness of their job search process.

Attendees would benefit from this workshop if they:

* Have not conducted a job search in the new economy
* Are unfamiliar with today’s job market
* Are unclear on how to start your search
* Have no resume
* Are not happy with your current resume
* Have multiple versions of your resume
* Are frustrated with the progress you have made
* Are getting multiple interviews but no job offers

I have been helping people secure new job and career opportunities for over seven years by offering career coaching, resume guidance and personal introductions to a network of over 70 recruiters throughout the US and Europe.

Over the years, I have established a five step methodology that guides individuals through the job hunting process in a structured, informed, efficient and logical manner.

The five steps include:

1. Establishing your value
2. Creating marketing materials: Resume and cover letter
3. Making personal introductions: Resume referral
4. Networking: Selling your services
5. Closing the deal: Interview preparation
