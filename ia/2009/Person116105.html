    February 2009 — Present (3 months)

    Gateway is a speaking, training and consulting firm located in Charlottetown, PEI, Canada focused on developing talent in the Maritime Provinces. As well, developing the talent base for all professionals who work for the Government of PEI. This way they will be prepared to take on the challenges of whatever the future will bring after all this economic uncertainty settles.

    The question on which we focus is; “how can PEI thrive in the transformation to an economy based on peoples creativity and knowledge rather than just relying on physical resources and hourly labour?” The Island economy should be shifting away from jobs based on physical skill and repetitive task to one that encompasses analytical skills, judgment, and creating value for the rest of the country.
