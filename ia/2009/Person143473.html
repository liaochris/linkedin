Loren Fogelman’s Summary

I am Loren Fogelman, a masters level therapist and Emotional Freedom Techniques advanced practitioner. My business is Mindset for Marketing Success specializing in mastering the marketing mindset to grow your business. My focus is to transform the head games you play that keep you from putting yourself out there in a big way. When you are excited about marketing your business, you attract clients to you.

I have worked with thousands of professionals in demanding careers who are really good at what they do but feel uncomfortable having to sell themselves. The goal is to get past the discomfort of marketing. Working together we identify the core issues quickly and use a results based strategy to help you step into your greatness.

When I made the decision to step out of the office and into my own business, I expected clients to find me. I began to wonder why I wasn’t busy. The thought of promoting my business made me cringe. I would have an aerobic heartbeat just thinking about speaking in front of others.

The time had come to face my fears. I was ready to do what was uncomfortable. My success depended on it. Immediately, I enrolled in Toastmasters and went to every meeting, taking each opportunity to work through my fear of public speaking.

I was determined to get past my own comfort zone and break limiting self imposed barriers. The motto I embraced was that “if it is uncomfortable, then I ought to be doing it.”

I have made a commitment to helping other self-employed, sole practitioners transform their barriers to taking the action steps necessary for a successful business.

When working with me, I help to take your sense of hope and make it a reality.

Receive my free e-book Tapping Into Emotional Freedom, with that you will also receive notices of upcoming events and articles to help you master your mindset for success.

Contact me for a free 15 min get acquainted session to see if I can help you to step into your greatness.

Loren Fogelman’s Specialties:

In 2006, Loren became a certified advanced Emotional Freedom Techniques Practitioner. People are aware of their problems and what they are not doing well. Traditional therapy primarily focuses on the problem and how to get away from it. Her approach is to focus on moving toward the solution. Combining traditional therapy with energy work allows you to release your barriers to taking action; thereby, attaining your peak potential.
