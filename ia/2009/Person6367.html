Alan Rutter’s Summary

Following 11 years in HM Royal Navy, I Started commercial life as Service Manager for Omicron Electronics then moved into flight training and simulation as both an On Site Engineer and later as Regional Customer Liaison Manager. I then became a Project Manager - Civil Aircraft with British Aerospace Ltd. The next 16 years were in Aviation information management, OEM & aftermarket spares and procurement with the top three aviation B2B services, ILS, Cordiem & Partsbase looking after the EMEA region. I am an experienced business development, sales and marketing professional with a good understanding of engineering principles and IT infrastructures.
Currently Account Director for Superstructure Group AQD. AQD is a complete safety and quality management system and the first in the world to combine quality assurance audits with flight, cabin, maintenance and ground safety occurrence reporting. AQD meets the needs of international and regional airlines as well as cargo, corporate fleet, helicopter operators, airports, air traffic service providers, maintenance organisations and the military.
If you feel that there is synergy between your organisation and my latest role, I would be pleased to discuss the possibilities further.
