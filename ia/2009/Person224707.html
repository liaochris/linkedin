Steve Watson’s Summary

A highly experienced Digital Media Executive with operating responsibility extending from Sales, Product, Strategy, Marketing, General Management through to Director roles. A passionate, inspirational leader with a consistent record of delivering results across both Online and Mobile media. Known for being an innovative self-starter with strong vision and solid partner relations Steve is highly regarded within the digital Industry for getting the job done. In addition Steve brings an amazingly diverse network of International contacts across most facets of the media Industry.

A media Industry veteran with over 20 years experience, spanning print, online and mobile. Steve now facilitates strategic alliances, whilst maintaining a keen interest in advertising and mobile marketing opportunities. He works with a number of leading brands and companies in developing, identifying and securing talent individuals to build out teams.

For the past two years Steve has managed the Media and Digital recruitment practice within Mitchellake Consultancy indentifying and sourcing general managment, executive team members, senior sales personnel, product managers, prohject managers, client services, SEO and SEM, ad operations, through to planning and buying with both media owners and agencies throughout Australia and the Asia Pacific.
