Andrea Francioni’s Summary

I was born in Rome, Italy in 1979. I received the "laurea" degree in Telecommunications Engineering (summa cum laude) in Remote Sensing from the University of Rome "La Sapienza".
After the degree I worked for about 7 months for SIPAL (Società di Ingegneria per l'Assistenza Logistica) as Integrated Logistic Support Technician, employed in the preparation of manuals and courses for defence systems.
Since January 2005 I have been working in Thales Alenia Space as End to End System Engineer, inserted in the system division, employed in the COSMO-SkyMed, HUDHUD, MUSIS, and GokTurk Systems and in the BIOMASS and CoReH2O mission in the role of mission resposible.
