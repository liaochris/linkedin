Joseph Lombardo’s Summary

Nearly 30 years experience in international development, including 22 with the U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID), and 10 years in senior-level positions. Expertise in strategic and operational planning; project development, design, implementation, and monitoring and evaluation; performance management systems; operational and program management assessments. Skilled in leading and managing large decentralized organizations through periods of growth and contraction, and in overseeing management of all major operational functions: technical, program development, budget, financial, procurement, and administrative. Experience in policy, strategy, project development, management, monitoring and evaluation, and public-private partnerships in following program areas: rural and agricultural development; infrastructure, such as roads, energy, water/sanitation; housing, and urban and regional development; decentralization/local government management; health and education; municipal infrastructure finance; anti-corruption, transparency, and accountability; and public finance for social sector and economic development, including micro-finance, agricultural cooperatives, privatization, and private provision of social services. Experience working in conflict and post-conflict countries, and in applying development principles to relief and reconstruction efforts. Experienced serving on school and community boards of directors in U.S. and overseas. Work experience in over 20 countries in Asia, Europe, Africa, and Latin America and Caribbean. Speak and write Spanish fluently. See www.jfl-consulting.com for more information.

Joseph Lombardo’s Specialties:

General management, strategic planning, operations and performance management, project development, donor negotiations, public-private partnerships, crisis stabilization operations, post-conflict and post-disaster relief and reconstruction, monitoring & evaluation, democracy and governance, housing and urban development, regional development, training.
