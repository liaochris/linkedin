Roger Warwick’s Summary

Thirty years experience in corporate security and investigations assisting many Fortune 100 international corporations. Owner and MD of Pyramid International, a consultancy based in Italy. BSI qualifiedLead auditor for ISO27001 and a contributor to the development of new international security standards.
Certified, RABQSA, Lead Auditor Security Management Systems. ISO 28000.
A regular speaker at international symposiums on security and economic crime. Regional Secretary for the British Chamber of Commerce for Italy.
Nominated "Expert" by UNI (The Italian National Standardization Organization) for the development of ISO and CEN security standards.
Member and project coordinator ESRIF (European Security, Research and Innovation Forum). Member of the Standards & Guidelines Commission ASIS International.

Roger Warwick’s Specialties:

Business intelligence, due diligence, security procedures and policies. In depth local knowledge of southern Europe. vast international network of security and investigative professionals. Standards development, training and auditing.
