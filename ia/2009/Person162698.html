Michele Ide-Smith’s Summary

I have been working in the web industry since 1997 for blue chip companies, charities, internet start-ups, educational institutions and local government. I have worked in roles combining project management, user experience, information architecture, web strategy, consultancy and social media . I have a strong focus on user-centred design and am currently completing as thesis for a part-time MSc in Human Computer Interaction with Ergonomics, at University College London.

Michele Ide-Smith’s Specialties:
