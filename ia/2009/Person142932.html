Lisa Pere’s Summary

Create content that your clients want to read. Provide clarity. And do it on time and within budget.

I provide a fast, efficient edit of existing material. You choose the level of edit you want—copy edit or deep dive. If you’re starting from scratch, I have experience writing for both print publications and Web sites. I'm also available to manage projects on a contract basis, bringing experience with projects ranging from Environmental Impact Statements to Quark whitepapers and Microsoft deployment guides.

Why settle for something your clients *can* (or worse--can't) read? I’ll help you write something they *want* to read. The results: Winning proposals, educated end users, increased readerships, satisfied clients.

When you want clarity, contact me: llpere AT fullstopeditorial DOT com.
