Lynn Richardson’s Summary

With over 20 years experience in advertising & marketing, getting the right photo for your event, advertising or trade show is just what we do!

We combine composition and creativity to deliver just what you need for visual impact and great memories.

We also are building a great stock photo catalog for businesses and professionals that won't break the bank. These are photos that you can use just about anywhere for a small fee rather than a sliding scale. www.buyphotosof.com

And when you have the perfect picture, we can also create the materials or strategy you need to make your business successful through our marketing division, M.A.C. Marketing, llc. It is my personal goal to help businesses make the most of their budget and provide superior services.

Our regional clients include: North American Millwright Services, Cornerstone Builders of Maryland, The COUNT Foundation, Vigilant Federal, Renewing Touch, Signature Inspection Services, Perry Hall/White Marsh Business Assn, McCready's Catering, Chef Johnny Mo, Bubbles the Clown, Raffy's Restaurant, Plumbers & Steamfitters Training School, Backcourt Foundation, UMM Neuroprotection Group

Our national clients include: Power Purchase (AZ), SSS International (MN), PlanetSafe Products (Canada), Tripp Lite (IL), Khazana Trading (VA), and Spartan Stores (MI).

We believe in close, personal attention and building long-term relationships with our clients. When you call, you are the #1 priority to the team. Let us work with you.

Digital photography and web movies for all businesses. We can also provide project management, writing, strategic marketing planning and design. Cost-effective web packages are also available.
