Paulo Pessanha’s Summary

Paulo Pessanha has been building his knowledge of the Internet since 1999 combining expertise in all expects of web site development life-cycles, from analysis to specifications, web design and development to content writing, coding, testing and SEO.

He has been the leading innovator of customized internet marketing solutions and strategies, conceptualizing innumerous methodologies to better serve clients regardless of their size, structure or niche.

Highly respected and successful within the Search Engine Optimization communities Paulo participates in many industry forums and conferences. Thriving to stay abreast with the latest trends of the Internet he delivers consecutive top ranking results on the main search engines for all websites he oversees.

He was part of Source Interlink Media SEO team working on 63 premier online automotive brands and websites reaching over 15 million unique visitors per month. His company SEOHits.com continues to deliver the most consistent SEO results to his clientele. Now he's embarking on a new adventure as VP of Search for the MoreFocus Group Inc.
