Kyle Samuel Brooks’s Summary

Originally from a small town in Maine, Kyle now resides in Nashville, TN and makes his living as a full time songwriter. Since 1981, Kyle has been sharing wisdom and life stories through his honest, heartfelt songs. He also helps others tell their life changing stories through his custom songwriting business.

Kyle can be found performing at some of the top songwriter venues around metro Nashville. Although he claims not to be a guitar player (an award winning drummer & percussionist), his soothing, finger picking style tells a different story. Kyle goes deep with memorable melodies while sharing stories about challenges he has overcome and the future he is creating. If Kyle's in the room, you will feel the energy of his contageous smile and witness his selfless support of those around him.

Kyle has studied with some of the top pro writers in Nashville and is now receiving regular requests to cowrite with established writers across the country. Kyle has been interviewed by on-line radio stations and has gotten air play on the world famous WSM radio. Having received honorable mention at the 2009 Tennessee State Fair, his fans, along with artists and industry professionals are saying, "make a record already!". Join his mailing list so you don't miss the release of his debut CD, in 2010!

Kyle’s message to you, "My intention is to inspire you to do what you love; what you feel passionate about. Bring people into your life that can see and support your dream. And remember, we all win when we help each other"!
