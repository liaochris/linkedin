Jessica J. Henning’s Summary

I grew up in Glen Ellyn, IL and moved to Las Vegas, NV to attend college. At the University of Nevada, I majored in business/marketing/finance and graduated Summa Cum Laude in 2004. Out of college, I was hired by Pfizer Pharmaceuticals, but didn't like working for someone else. I moved to La Jolla, CA where I started a few businesses, sold a few, and eventually found my true passion - real estate. Today, I buy and sell commercial and luxury residential real estate. I love working with investors and helping clients with private placements and syndicated real estate opportunities. I'm also a car nut, and love to help clients obtain their dream cars. I enjoy volunteering and would like to one day establish a charitable foundation to benefit the homeless.
