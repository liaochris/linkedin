Frank Alfieri’s Summary

Extensive background in sales management, process, operations, training, quality initiatives, and sales performance.

I am currently running the Local Advertising Sales business for WorldNow. Primary business focus is helping media partners drive new local advertising revenue by selling their online and on-air inventory.

WorldNow provides the most comprehensive and successful suite of online products, consulting services and revenue programs enabling local media companies to build new businesses on the Internet.

WorldNow empowers local television, newspapers and cable systems to develop new distribution channels and revenue streams for their content while utilizing new and emerging technologies and platforms. WorldNow is proud to have a long list of top broadcasting partners including: Raycom, Gray, Young, Cox, Dispatch, Gray, Journal, Landmark, Media General, News Press & Gazette and many others.

I have held strategic sales and management positions for Fortune Global 500 Companies including Citigroup, Canon, and Fujitsu, as well as for Mortenson Broadcasting Company, Babylon and Bayshore Broadcasting Company, and Radio Marketing Company.

I dedicate spare time giving back to the community and was recently a recipient of The President's Volunteer Service Award from the President of the United States for my work with charitable organizations.
