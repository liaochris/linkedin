Pierre-Andre Berthault-Meca’s Summary

PROFILE

• Over 6 years experience as a Film Composer / Sound Designer.
• Orchestrated on major motion pictures including X-Men 2.
• Directed and edited music documentaries (The Simpsons).
• Proven track record as an accurate, reliable artist who delivers on time.
• At ease with both modern, technological solutions as well as with old-school techniques; gifted with a strong sense of music literature.
• Exceptional oral and written communication skills.
• Talented Industry Journalist / Writer with over 18 years of experience.

INDUSTRY RELATED EXPERIENCE

Film Scoring
• Orchestrated sequences from X-Men 2 (John Ottman, 20th Century Fox).
• Orchestrated a concert suite from Incognito (John Ottman, Morgan Creek).
• Composed for European production partners Moors Pictures (Belgium), Lympia Productions (France) and for various U.S. independent pictures.
• Scored segments from the award winning movie Baraka (Magidson Films).
• Assisted film composers (incl. Cliff Eidelman) in archiving their catalogs.
• Created sound design for shorts & websites.
• Wrote concert pieces performed in the United States & Japan.

Post-Production
• Wrote, produced and directed “The Music of The Simpsons”.
• Produced & Edited local interviews of Film Composers (incl. Lalo Schifrin) for Lympia Productions (France) and other production partners.
• Produced & Edited DVD releases for The French Piano Academy.
• Created final packaging for numerous Soundtrack CD releases.

Journalism
• First articles published at the age of 15 (Micro News).
• Entertainment Industry writer since 1996 (SFX Magazine).
• Major technical and historical Articles published.
• Has interviewed more than 60 film composers.
• Wrote over 100 film music reviews.
• Published own periodical (Click-Track Magazine).
• Praised in French trade publications (Musique Info Hebdo, 2000).
