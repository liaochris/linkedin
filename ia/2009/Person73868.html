Erica Finley’s Summary

Experienced journalist with emphasis in copy editing and page design. Five years of reporting, copy editing and page design experience. Experience in deadlines related to daily, weekly and special publications. Adept at writing headlines and cutlines on and off deadline. Goal is to be a positive, successful influence in any environment I work in, and constantly learn from those around me.

Erica Finley’s Specialties:

Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint, Macintosh and PC systems, News Edit Pro, Quark XPress, Adobe InDesign and Photoshop, Paint Shop Pro, Associated Press style, Web publishing, familiar with blogging, search engine optimization and social media
