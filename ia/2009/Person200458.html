Robert Griffiths’s Summary

A strategic P&L sales & marketing led director with hands-on execution success in the highly competitive B2B technology, software and telecoms sectors selling across many vertical SME, Public Sector and Corporate accounts.

Expertise in qualifying the value proposition and routes to market of complex products and services; building the marketing tools and integrated marketing & sales process to align the sales team to a consultative selling approach engaging C-level decision makers.

Extensive work with early growth venture capital funded businesses, driving the company to repeatable sales - typically in 90-days, and working with established teams that require sales and marketing turnaround to improve sales results - quickly.

Results focused, with sales planning, operational reporting skills and a proven management ability to build or realign teams and partners to deliver repeatable sales performance.
