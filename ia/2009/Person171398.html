  • Leadership Team

    Kettering Executive Network (KEN)

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    April 2007 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    The Kettering Executive Network (www.ketteringexecutivenetwork.org) is a private, senior level network in the Atlanta area. Members of the Kettering Executive Network typically are at the Vice President / Director level or above in Fortune 500 companies or "C" level in other companies. They typically have seven or more years of management experience, have managed at least 25 people, have managed at least a $10 million budget or P&L, and have had compensation above $150k. They have all been sponsored by another member of the network. Kettering Executive Network has over 825 members.

    Our membership process requires a candidate to be sponsored for membership by an existing member who emails the nomination and resume to me; the candidate must attend two regular meetings as a guest first; then an interview by an existing member is scheduled; followed by one week of "vetting" by posting the nomination documents to the KEN yahoo!group for members to provide feedback, before admitting the candidate as a member. The interviewer will try to ensure that the candidate has held "executive" level position in the past, by having a job that has/had impacted significantly the direction of a decent sized company (or a decent sized division of a larger corporation). The sponsorship is put in place to ensure that the candidate believes in "KEN creed" of paying-it-forward networking.

    Though most new members who join KEN are in career transition, most KEN members are gainfully employed, of course. Helping executives during career transistion is certainly a key value that KEN provides, through peer support. But, we are a peer group of executives.
