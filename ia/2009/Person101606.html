Jordan Digby’s Summary

Architectural and high level consulting working with a variety of large and small internet travel companies, airlines, hotel chains and GDS's. Currently Chair of the Travel Integration Workgroup in the Open Travel Alliance (OTA: http://www.opentravel.org/), interested in travel systems, open systems and their design and architecure.
