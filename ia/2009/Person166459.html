Mike McNamara’s Summary

I have more than 25 years experience in IT with a variety of software and hardware vendors including over 15 years providing SGML/XML Content Management and Publishing production solutions to a wide variety of high profile UK and European Commercial and Aviation Publishers. I have also created a series of Disaster Recovery Plans for a number of companies as part of their Business Continuity Planning. I have also worked with a number of small software companies helping them better understand how to successfully penetrate various target markets.

Mike McNamara’s Specialties:

XML Content Management Systems (CMS) needs analysis, CMS product evaluations and recommendations. XML Content and Publishing Production workflow analysis. XML CMS Project delivery management. Business Continuity Planning, Disaster Recovery plan analysis and creation.
