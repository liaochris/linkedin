David Albertini’s Summary

Welcome to my LinkedIn profile.

After beginning my career in corporate sales, it dawned on me that being another cog in the wheel for a multi-billion dollar organization was the not the career path I sought for myself.

This lead me to my new venture as a entrepreneurial addict. Starting Defined Presence was the best thing I ever did for myself. Now my career goals centre around helping small and medium sized businesses achieve the growth and market presence they only dreamed of having.

The success of Defined Presence has now lead me down a new and exciting path of developing web entities for both Privitta (My new business) and also those new entrepreneurs hoping to start their own business and escape the 9-5 grind.
