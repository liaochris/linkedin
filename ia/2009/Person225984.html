Sudip Bhattacharya’s Summary

A noted Business analyst and domain specialist in the Insurance Sector, he changed his Avatar in 2008 when he became a Strategy Professor in a top Business school and later founded his own small but serious Institute focussed on the education and research on Strategy. Presently he is also the chief mentor and the Chief Speaker of BodhiTree Enlightenment solutions which has an acknowledged mandate of transfporming the lives of the young people and students through Attitude management with a difference. He also dreams of creating the world's best school, for nearly two decades.

He is a hardworking, open, friendly and honest guy who values relationships more than anything else and is loyal to family and friends. He is married to Samita for the last 15 years and has a little devil for a daughter Monami, 13.
