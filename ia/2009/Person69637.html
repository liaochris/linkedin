Kate Kennedy’s Summary

Communications professional with experience in media from every angle — directing awareness campaigns, creating internal and external communications, analyzing media coverage and writing and editing news. Possesses an understanding of how to communicate effectively after working on both sides of messages, as creator and as recipient.

Kate Kennedy’s Specialties:
