André Haardt’s Summary

Entrepreneur with a passion to take educational video games to the next level.

Founded FutureWhiz Media, a dedicated publishing house for "next generation" educational video games. We are interested in meeting:

-owners of cutting edge educational games who require access to the mass market channel and/or educational channel
-innovative game developers/publishers who are looking for a venture partner in the development phase of an educational game
-educational publishers who are interested in (online) gaming as an additional business model with which to monetise their content

Previous experience was as Benelux responsible manager for €3.6 billion Italian media group De Agostini. Initiated and launched some 15 innovative edutainment concepts, aimed at target groups from pre-school to seniors. Was responsible for moving De Agostini's strategy from print-based to a cross media strategy. Built a new organisation with relevant capabilities to deliver leading-edge concepts for the Benelux and global markets.

Other experience was in senior commercial roles across a range of industries, gained both in start-up and multinational environments. Key focus has been on entering and conquering new markets. Successfully accomplished this for:

-VC backed healthcare internet start-up Dentiva
-Royal Philips Electronics (built the super premium segment for electric toothbrushes and the additive steam ironing segment in a strategic alliance with Unilever)
