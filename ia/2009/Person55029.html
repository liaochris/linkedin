Daniel Steiny’s Summary

Dan Steiny has a 15 year record of leading and executing successful Digital Media projects and initiatives. Highlights include managing the software integration and licensing for the Award Winning VIAO computer line, Directing Product Management and Business Development for digital music pioneer Liquid Audio's Liquid Music Network (LMN) and, more recently, leading partner relations for digital media distributor MusicNet's key clients including MTV, AOL and IMesh. (MusicNet was voted the #1 fastest growing technology company in New York; as recognized by Deloitte & Touche USA LLP In September, 2006). Other positions include Director of Content Acquisitions for Michael Nesmith’s PBS Home Video and consulting SVP for PushButton Music.
