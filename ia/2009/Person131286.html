Kevin Reese’s Summary

For the past 25 years I have been focused on helping companies improve their profits through increased leads and sales. I am a senior-level digital marketing and communications expert and the President of Mango Media Corporation in Lower Downtown Denver, Colorado USA.

Mango Media is an internet marketing, web design and website development agency located in the heart of the snow sports industry. At Mango, we help manage the conversations that emerging and existing brands have with their customers through customized digital marketing that garner results.

My personal experience involves an integration of marketing agency management, marketing strategy, public relations, interactive planning, event activation, corporate sponsorships, video strategy, media, mobile marketing and celebrity entertainment. Over the years I have started, grown and sold multiple agencies and .com’s. Through the evolution of my career I moved from the East Coast to the West Coast, ultimately landing me here in Colorado. I have now been in Colorado for over 13 years.

I am extremely passionate and active in the snow sports industry and am a certified Professional Ski Instructor of America, a USSA Ski Racing Coach, and a Board of Director for the United States Ski and Snowboard Association/Rocky Mountain Division.

In addition, my passion and dedication to health, wellness and fitness has encouraged me to become actively involved in a variety of nonprofit groups and foundations. I serve on the Board of Advisors of the Metro Denver Health & Wellness Commission and serve on as a Board of Director for the Colorado Fusion Soccer Club.

I’m married, live in the Bonnie Brae neighborhood of metro Denver, and I have two sons who are actively involved in competitive soccer, ski racing, golf, tennis and BMX Racing.

    Vail Devo Alpine Racing: Team members who select this team have the goal of developing and improving alpine racing skills. As with all of Vail Youth Development Team the primary emphasis will be on improving all around skiing skills.
