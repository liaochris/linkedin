Rebecca Corliss’s Summary

I am the newest member of the HubSpot marketing team, specializing in creative content development, including viral music videos. I am also a co-producer of HubSpot TV.

My first music video received 40K views in the first week. To view it, visit: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4-lGe5MnBlY

I'm also very active in the Boston social media scene, frequently attending events such as PodCamp and Social Media Breakfast. I've also helped organize professional gatherings for individuals such as Jeff Pulver and BusinessWeek's Sarah Lacey. Currently, I am the volunteer public relations adviser for Social Media for Social Change, a brand new fundraiser event series that utilizes social media tools to rally support for a common cause.

In my free time, I'm founding my own a cappella singing ensemble called Common Sound. I also write for RebeccaCorliss.com, a blog that celebrates social media and music.
