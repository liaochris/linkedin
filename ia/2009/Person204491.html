Roy Joseph Mampilly <roy@merqurimedica.com>’s Summary

Founded Merquri Medica to improve medical safety conditions in India. We sell a world class first-aid kit called Emer-aid and a portable defibrillator that "reboots" the heart in case of a sudden cardiac arrest.

YOU can save lives by training yourself on CPR, basic life support and basic first aid. We conduct regular public workshops in India.

Entrepreneur by choice. Co founded Zion Guest House, Merquri Work & Play & Sparshh Retail. 19 years of professional experience in Human Resources and Administration. Served in companies like ITC, VST, Virtusa, Idea Cellular. Passionate about travel.

Passionate about contributing to society through the Nightingale Trust for First Aid Education. NTFE conducts first aid training across India for companies, societies, associations, schools, colleges and government.
