Juuso Myllyrinne’s Summary

Juuso Myllyrinne has made a career in creating conversations and things worth talking about. Currently he is transitioning into TBWA\Digital Arts in New York. In his previous role he was the lead client for Nokia's digital agencies in the creative functions and headed a cross organizational team of digital marketeers.

Myllyrinne has lead numerous campaigns that have received recognitions in the field of digital, viral and mobile marketing and in this respect he's one of the most awarded people in the telecomms industry on the client side.

Myllyrinne is a native of Finland's capital - Helsinki. His academic background is from Helsinki School of Economics majoring in Marketing and Information Systems and in Helsinki University studying Computer Sciences.

During his studies he had a successful career as a journalist in the Finnish media. He received the honour of Radio Presenter of the Year in 2003 in the Finnish industry awards voted amongst peers. He has the experience of approximately 1000 radio broadcasts and over 100 live tv-shows.

His personal interest are a fine balance of nerding it out with friends on Xbox (Gamertag: useO), long distance running (First marathon 4:42), recording bad rap music (http://www.facebook.com/pages/SDM-Crew/8488503670) and being too much on-line and "totally ruining" romantic dinners by tweeting about the first course (@juuuso).
