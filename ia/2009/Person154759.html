David Mason’s Summary

After 20+ years as a TV exec, I've just taken a year out to do an MBA at Cambridge - and now, after a stint as Managing Director of Lebanon's national broadcaster, I'm back in Sydney creating more television.

Over the years I've created, produced and licensed dozens of formats and productions; I've produced TV throughout the US, Asia, Europe and Australasia; I've launched and led a highly successful and award winning production company; I've led teams of 1100+ people; I've managed budgets of $450m+; I've run departments within TV Networks; and I've even been credited with getting Sydney the 2000 Olympic Games (and helping London get the 2012 Games).

And now I have an MBA from a global top-ten business school which probably makes me weirdly unique and, with luck, unbelievably useful.

Whether it's for short-term consultancy projects or longer term employment contracts, please feel free to get in touch.

David Mason’s Specialties:

Managing collaborative teams; strategic analysis; creating truly excellent TV; growing profitable businesses.
