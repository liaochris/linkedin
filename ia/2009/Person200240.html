    * Estimated ROI / Operational cost savings with Proposed System: $100,000 per year

    * 90% User Satisfaction with Proposed System, preferred new over existing due to its usability and intuitivity.

    * Defined Usability Engineering Artifacts for reuse to build future applications: User Profiles, Personas, and Task Definitions.
