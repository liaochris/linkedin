Kim Reichert’s Summary

I have nearly 20 years experience in providing workforce design solutions to clients in the healthcare, financial services, manufacturing, insurance and corporate sectors. Throughout my tenure with Volt, I have managed large national and regional accounts, including several on-site managed programs. My primary goal is to develop long term partnerships with my clients and candidates.

I specialize in placing professionals in all levels of Manufacturing, Engineering, IT, Accounting and Administrative positions offering contingency, direct hire and executive search.
