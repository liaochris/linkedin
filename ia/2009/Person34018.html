Brian Vasquez’s Summary

Brian is the Producer and Creative Imaging Director for Radio Perez (Perez Hilton) which airs nationwide and throughout Canada and Australia.

Currently Brian is a DJ/Producer/Remixer at 106.7 KROQ-FM (Los Angeles). He is the official voice and producer for commericials on-air for the past 5 years. KROQ has been the top billing station in the country during his tenure. He is also a featured DJ on KROQ doing specialty shows mixing rock music and electronic beats along with remixing tracks on the fly.

Artists he's worked and produced include the Beastie Boys, Audioslave, 311, Godsmack, Team Sleep, Mars Volta, Money Mark, Green Day, Foo Fighters, Jay-Z, Jack Johnson, The Dandy Warhols, The Sounds, System of a Down, Jane's Addiction, The Arcade Fire, She Wants Revenge, Ok Go, LL Cool J, Porno For Pyros, Puddle of Mudd, Travis Barker, Coldyplay, Pennywise, Bad Religion, The Cult, Stabbing Westward, N.E.R.D., The Dwarves, Mondo Generator, The Apex Theory, Incubus, Cypress Hill, Nine Black Alps, Wolfmother, Woven, Zack Hexum, Submit and A.i.

Brian's remix of Godsmack's "Serenity" climbed the Billboard charts as the number 4 most requested mainstream rock song in the U.S. for 2003. It's huge success prompted it to be released by Godsmack as a bonus track to the Import version of the album "Faceless." Comissioned by Universal Music Group, Brian did remix's for Gary Jules' hit "Mad World" and The Mars Volta "Televators".

His remix of the Beastie Boys "Ch-Check It Out" was number 1 on the furious 5@9 on KROQ and led him to work on the Solid Gold Mix promoting their "Solid Gold Hits" record. The mix was serviced by Capitol Records and distrubuted throughout the globe.
