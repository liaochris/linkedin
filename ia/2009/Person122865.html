Johan Grobler’s Summary

The future of computing is in the hands of companies that can process and analyze vast amounts of data, fast, efficiently and in context. NeuralNet Technologies focus on research and development of new ways to use ideas from the past to design the future. Neural networks have been around for many years, but will only see its full potential with the advances we see day by day in quantum physics and especially around the Holy Grail – the Quantum Computer. The more processing we can do the better the algorithms and predictive analysis.

NeuralNet Technologies focuses on this trend to design the self learning, thinking software of the future to be prepared for the next wave once the computing power and multi-processing technologies catches up with the predictions and ideas of the past.

The Future's Envisioned in the Past, Implemented Today and Realized Tomorrow!

  • Enterprise Architect

    UTi

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    October 2007 — May 2009 (1 year 8 months)

    My main role within the company was to consolidate various companies within the group (Sun Couriers, Freight Forwarding, Material Handling, PLC and OCS) to use one centralized knowledge management solution within the South African context called myUTi Africa.

    This solution was rolled out using the Microsoft SharePoint 2007 and Microsft Search Server 2008 technologies. It replaced all the various intranets within South Africa and formed a successful central point for communication, collaboration and knowledge sharing. The system was designed, implemented, rolled out and live within 6 months of the engagement replacing all other intranets in these divisions.

    The success of any intranet is the usage and that was achieved with using the brilliant minds within the company itself (business) to drive the portal and not techies. We ensured from day one that business was involved as the owner and not IT. This was the number one success factor of this implementation.

    I also later assisted UTi as one of the transition managers with a full outsource to an Indian based company as well as consulting around the world wide intranet and other architectural decisions.
