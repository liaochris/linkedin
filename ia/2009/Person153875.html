Martin Kuipers’s Summary

I've been working with web- and creative technology for 10 years now. After I finished my education I had a few chances to try my ideas in real life. But soon after that I found myself back in an educational institute, but now on the other side of the line, being a lecturer and a coach to students.

I feel I learn more and more from my students every day, they marvel me with great ideas and no restrictions on their ambitions. Their questions force me to think hard and be creative in finding solutions. This inspired me to start a company with 2 students and a former student (now all dear friends :-)), called Lable.org. We all felt the same unease when working in a hirearchy. Our minds connected and we took the challenge to rethink everything we don't like about the world.

Now we all have a clear vision on the unbalance between the digital an analogue world, and we know how to change it. Slowly but deliberately we're finding and creating more 2.0 brainers to reach our ultimate goal, world 2.0. A world that is honest, transparent, creative and sustainable for everyone.

On various occasions I've told the story of world 2.0 to people, and I'm happy to see that people are getting the picture. So if your interested in the story, drop me a message and maybe we'll meet soon!
