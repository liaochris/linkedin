Markus May’s Summary

Markus helps people operate, buy, sell, and start businesses. This includes negotiating and drafting business documents related to mergers and acquisitions as well as shareholder agreements, limited liability company operating agreements, consulting agreements, independent contractor agreements, employment agreements, commercial leases, confidentiality agreements, and other business contracts. Corporate governance including drafting by-laws and minutes is performed for clients on a regular basis.

Markus May’s Specialties:

Concentrating law practice in the following areas: Business Law, Mergers and Acquisitions, Contracts, Company Governance for Corporations and Limited Liability Companies, and Business Formations.
