Chase Breland’s Summary

I was born in Houston, Texas on April 22, 1982. My family and I moved to Nashville five years later and I have lived here ever since. After graduating high school from Montgomery Bell Academy, I went to Belmont University to earn a bachelor's degree in Business Managment. While in college, I was very involved with Phi Delta Theta fraternity and now I serve as the Chapter Advisor and Chairman of the Alumni Board. Joining in 2006, I am currently a Board member of the Nashville Junior Chamber . With a business degree, I have worked for a few companies here in town gaining more business knowledge, sales and marketing experience and connections each day. I am currently working for The Breland Group as a project manager/business developer. The Breland Group is an environmental engineering company that mainly perferms Environmental Assessment Reports (Phase 1 reports) and Building Condition Reports. I am always interested in furthering my career in sales/business development or project management/analyst opportunity with a company that has a good presence in Middle TN.
