Hong Tong Wu 吳漢堂’s Summary

Hong Tong Wu works as interim actuarial consultant for life insurers and pension funds. Services include among others:
- state and check collective contracts
- change pension schemes
- perform actuarial and financial calculations
- state and check annual accounts
- set up and check administrations

Hong Tong gives lectures in risk and portfolio management at Europort Business School and European Actuarial Academy.
