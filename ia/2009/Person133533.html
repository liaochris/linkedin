Keith Wilson’s Summary

Throughout my career in the IT Industry I have held many diverse positions, each of which have required developing strategic alliances with business partners and go to market strategies. In 2006 I branched out on my own to create SMB Insights, Inc., and provide market intelligence and e-business expertise to small and midsized businesses. As of August 2007 I have been working full time with CNET Networks as the Director of Web Services as well as continuing to bill some hours through SMB Insights. All of my positions require strong project management, technical, leadership, consultative sales and communication skills. Some of the key highlights of my experience include:

• Led the development and implementation multiple online solutions
• Designed functional and technical specifications for various online properties including e-commerce, electronic storefronts, community portals, and a government/education applications.
• Over 9 years of competitive electronic marketing and advertising campaign experience
• Created IT channel sales programs with partner evaluation and selection that opened new markets such as ecommerce and managed services to solution providers
• Design and implement process improvement and revenue generating opportunities
• Actively increased sales revenue through sales account management roles
