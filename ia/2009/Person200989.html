Robert Sansom’s Summary

Senior Web Solutions Engineer with over a decade of experience in multimedia design, development, usability, and information architecture. Working closely with Marketing, IT, Operations, Tech Ops, and Business Improvement, I manage a global team of UI developers in delivering integrated web solutions to our clients. It is my role to provide leadership in the evolution of an organization's web presence and capabilities.

Responsibilities include:
- converging marketing initiatives with internal systems and applications
- building functional requirements and implementation plans
- analyzing online users’ activity and understanding their needs to develop best-in-class user experience
- documenting and presenting web capabilities to internal and external stakeholders.

Robert Sansom’s Specialties:

Effective liaison between Sales, Marketing, Service Delivery, IT.

Translate functional requirements into detailed technical specification and high-level presentations.

Brand strategy and development, Web accessibility standards, Usability best practices, Search engine optimization

Fully educated on the tools and technologies used to build and support web applications, including: HTML / XHTML, JavaScript / VBScript, CSS, ASP, JSP, SQL, XML. Familiar with J2EE, Struts, PHP.
