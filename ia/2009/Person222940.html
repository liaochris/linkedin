Stephanie Reinking’s Summary

I am recent graduate of Fairleigh Dickinson University, with a double major in Fine Arts and Electronic Filmmaking, with concentrations in Animation and Graphic Design. While at school I have internshiped at:

The Driver Group located in Chester NJ, and at Magi FilmWorks located in Belleville, NJ.

At The Driver Group I was a Graphic Design Intern, who designed proof advertisements for companies, worked with web design and created and built the website for Tricopoly. As an intern at The Driver Group, I was exposed to the discipline of graphic design and received feedback from clients about my artwork.

At Magi FilmWorks I was a Multimedia Intern, who took on many different roles. I was in charge of utilizing both graphics and animation to create commercials for clients and products which have media exposure. I Incorporated music and media to create successful marketing tools for clients. Lastly, I preformed as a production team and took on many different roles, on film shoots. As an intern at Magi FilmWorks I was exposed to the production and development of commercials, and was exposed to After Effects CS3 and Maya 2008.

My goal is to be hired as a Graphic Designer, and I would like to work with a company where my artwork has exposure and can be seen by others.
