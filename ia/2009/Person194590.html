Raymond Wolf’s Summary

Strategic leader that enables companies to expand into services and successfully leverage their core product strengths in innovative, revenue-producing new ways resulting in accelerated growth and global market expansion.

20+ year record of delivering results through building highly scalable global businesses focused on the end-to-end customer experience and fast paced execution of integrated product and service strategies that position companies to capitalize on new opportunities and exceed “return on investment” expectations for profit and non-profit customers. Successfully started seven service businesses globally and across three Industry sectors.

Recognized for developing and implementing best practices in the areas of customer experience, strategic business planning, operations & performance management, P&L accountability, acquisitions, sales, and human capital strategies for technology-oriented companies. Executed businesses globally and has resided in the United States, Europe, Middle East, and Asia.
