Mike Bothwell’s Summary

Business-oriented technology leader with 25+ years of success delivering exceptional business value through technology. Successfully engages senior business and technology leaders in strategic initiative planning to prioritize the high impact technology investments. Excels at quantifying business benefits and associated technology expenses to determine the financial return on technology investments. Demonstrates thought leadership and passion for improving business and technology processes to achieve more with less. Achieves powerful, consistent results through higher productivity, higher morale and a stronger culture focused on professional growth, engagement, quality, service, collaboration and flexible discipline.
