Ron Black’s Summary

As the founder of several businesses, a four-time turnaround executive, and Fortune 500 marketing division VP, Ron knows what it takes to successfully lead organizations through growth, turbulence, and change.

He’s consulted to over 200 start-up businesses, authored two books on project management including the popular The Complete Idiot’s Guide to Project Management…, created the DVD program Acrobatics for Overachievers – Control-Point Time Management for Today’s Ultra-Busy Professional, and spoken to over 1100 groups in 47 states, Canada, Australia, and Russia.

Ron’s programs help business leaders and managers deal with complexity and change, focus on imperatives, and lead effective collaborative teams: transforming intentions into tangible results.
