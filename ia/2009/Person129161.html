Keith Bloom’s Summary

Principal and lead creative executive of award-winning interactive design and advertising agency for 10+ years. Exceptional strategic planning, digital asset integration, corporate communication, and creative copy writing skills. Routinely solve complex creative and technical problems with simple and dynamic solutions. Technogenic. Current in best-practices new media strategies and dynamic architectures, SEO/ SEM and variable content mixed media outreach. Additional expertise in enterprise-level direct marketing/CRM systems and variable content print. Equally comfortable collaborating with a team of content editors, art directors, and web developers as providing thought leadership to corporate officers and account managers.
