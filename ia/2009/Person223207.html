Stephen Ezell’s Summary

My past experience includes approximately 8 years with KPMG, several years with Citibank in NYC and Asia and a number of years with Pharmaceutical Partners, LLC in NYC.

Of course, I have a great deal of experience in the Attorney Support Industry having been a co-owner of E-Z Messenger Attorney Service.

Now, I am focused on building Executive Process in Texas. Executive Process, LLC is a leader in the legal services industry, operating with a client-first philosophy at our core. We cater to the Texas market by offering technologically advanced infrastructure and client tools, such as real-time tracking on every order, and we back up these resources with prompt, professional, and reliable service at value pricing. By offering traditional legal services with client-oriented products such as on-demand delivery, courier services, filings and document procurement, as well as a capable default services division, Executive Process is well positioned to satisfy the needs of one of the largest markets in the United States.

At Executive Process, we hold our clients in the highest regard and seek to exceed their expectations. From start to finish, our clients are guaranteed seamless integration of information and personalized support. While we utilize and value the efficiency that technology affords, we recognize that technology is a simply tool rather than a total solution. By combining state-of-the-art methods with a capable and experienced staff, Executive Process can offer unparalleled service and results. Our commitment to professionalism in all aspects of our work assures our clients that they will receive the individualized attention and care they deserve.

Stephen Ezell’s Specialties:

Executive Process is committed to complete client satisfaction and we are always ready to accommodate any request, from the ordinary to extraordinary. Utilizing the most advanced technology in the industry, our clients can enter service requests anytime, anywhere, from any computer. Additionally, we provide our clients with real time tracking and updates on all jobs, as well as digital proofs of service available immediately after jobs are completed.
