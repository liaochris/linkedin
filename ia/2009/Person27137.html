Bob Berstein’s Summary

Bob has more than ten years of investment banking and venture capital experience, including public and private financings and M&A across many sectors.

Through Berstein Capital LLC, Bob provides emerging growth companies with M&A advisory as well as corporate and business development services.

Prior, Bob was a Vice President at BMO Capital Markets investment banking, with a focus on Internet marketing services and for-profit education. Prior to BMO, Bob was a Vice President at Needham and Company focusing on technology-enabled services. He was a Vice President at Advanta Partners LP, Advanta Corporation’s private equity affiliate focusing on business services. While at Advanta, Bob co-founded and served as Chairman of the New York Private Equity Network (NYPEN). Bob also worked in the investment banking divisions at Jefferies and Merrill Lynch.

Bob has an A.B. in Classics from Cornell University, where he graduated magna cum laude. He currently serves on the Cornell University Council.
