Srikant Sharma’s Summary

Srikant Sharma brings a 15 year track record of turning marketing into a powerful revenue engine for the enterprise – across multiple business and product/service offerings. He has been recognized as particularly effective in strategic corporate and product marketing, and demand generation. Particularly skilled at communicating with the key constituencies of the enterprise - media and analysts, customers and the market, and senior executives. His recent focus has been the use of the social media paradigm to power the enterprise.

Srikant hold an MBA from Cornell University and a B.Tech from IIT Bombay (Mumbai, India).
