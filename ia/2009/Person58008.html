David Kreymer’s Summary

Over twenty years of versatile Silicon Valley experience, including all aspects of software architecture, design, implementation, and productizing, with emphasis on creating customer-driven, state of the art user interfaces. Twelve years of experience in Java and Qt, including mobile, server and client-side development. Eight years of experience with developing sophisticated RIAs. Ten years of leading successful large-scale (~1M lines) software development. Outstanding skill in software architecture, scalable, robust and rapid application development, usability improvement, team leadership, mentoring, and entrepreneurship. Driven achiever, incisive thinker, and deeply creative problem solver.   • Co-Founder

    LLaminim Company

    (Computer Software industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

  • Founder

    LookWow.com

    (Computer Software industry)

    November 2000 — Present (9 years 3 months)

    Founder and creator of the first web-based photo enhancement software. The LookWow service is a feature-rich offering that includes basic editing functions, such as Crop Photo, Resize, Clarify, Remove Red-Eye and Sharpen Photo. It employs a number of unique and powerful one-click enhancements, such as One-Step Enhance, Even & Smooth Skin Tone, Elegant Look and Suntan Effect, along with a series of special effects including Candlelight, Black & White, Elegant and Classic Look, and Glamorize Effect. LookWow has a myriad of additional features that include Brighten Smile, Enhance Lips, Enhance Color, Add Border and Caption to your photo. The service can be used for online dating, social networking, portfolios, blogging, websites and webpages, sharing photos with friends and family, corporate photos and more. Using LookWow, anyone can instantly revitalize digital photos and bring out their best qualities, while creating a better image. LookWow is used by thousands of users daily worldwide. Alexa traffic rank puts LookWow in the top 7% of all websites.

    According to CNET, “This focused application packs an enormous number of photo-enhancement features into a small footprint. You can apply all kinds of effects on the Revitalize tab, including Artistic, Stylish, and Natural effects... Effects such as Suntan and Enhance lip underscore the program's focus on headshots. The Editing tab lets you Add Borders, Captions, and Colors, as well as adjust Brightness, Rotation, and Cropping. The Creative Effects tab includes fun effects such as Night vision. Our favorite feature is Review Progress, which shows each change you've made and allows you to choose any stage to start over. Anyone who prepares headshots, especially for online purposes, might really enjoy LookWow.”

    Key implementation technologies: Java SE/EE/ME, AWT/Swing/SwingX, Java Applets/FX, AJAX/GWT/JavaScript, PHP, CGI, XML/XSLT, HTML/CSS, HTTP, MySQL, JSP/Servlet, Java Comm, JCA, Ant, Eclipse, GWT, Android SDK.
