Duane Floyd’s Summary

Experienced quality assurance leader with extensive leadership and technical skills utilized manufacturing, consulting and financial services.

Duane has gained this experience in a variety of functional areas, including product design, logistics, sales, financial analysis, regulatory and compliance, technology, enterprise initiatives, mergers (transitions), commercial and consumer banking, cosmetics, packaging and power tools.

Duane's experience also includes the education of others in the use of these tools. He has trained hundreds of Green Belts, Black Belts and Master Black Belts. Additionally, he has spent numerous hours educating others via teaching quality, business, statistics, project management and other disciplines in several colleges and universities.

Duane's experience and leadership has led to some important accomplishments, the greatest of which is the associate satisfaction that his teams' have felt. The last three times that Duane has been evaluated on associate sat, his scores were 93% top two box (100% participation, 10 point scale, 87 associates), 100% top two box (100% participation, 10 point scale, 12 associates) and 97% top box (100% participation, 5 point scale, 231 associates).
