Gail Sussman Miller’s Summary

I teach solopreneur women and women-owned small businesses and corporate teams how to love business obstacles, like networking, speaking and selling, and overcome them with ease. The outcome is people move from fear to action through greater self- and other-acceptance. They learn to promote and operate their businesses as a natural extension of who they are and what they love to do.

I have a history of making a difference as a speaker and trainer teaching individuals and teams to:
* increase networking effectiveness for diversity group members in corporations
* expand employee engagement through more meaningful goals and initiative
* increase job retention through empowered internal networking
* reduce anxiety about sales and selling by having consultive conversations
* create more opportunity by developing more meaningful relationships
* increase sales by overcoming fear and engaging public speaking audiences
* define and refine their WHO and WHAT (who they serve and what they do for them)

I use a 4-step approach to help people accomplish positive change by:
~~ Getting really clear about what they truly want
~~ Overcoming "yes, but" obstacle thinking and identifying solid options
~~ Making inspired choices, and
~~ Taking action through small steps.

OFFERING:
* For women solopreneurs who are serious about creating positive change and overcoming business obstacles to grow their business, I am offering a 45-minute complimentary "Obstacle-Busting Session" to help you get more clear about where you are, where you'd like to be, and what steps you can take to get there.
* Introductions to open doors within my network

SEEKING warm introductions to;
* Leaders or business owners in women's organizations, and networking groups interested in a dynamic, inspirational speaker
* Conference planners who want their participants to get the most out of their conference
* Managers of diversity employees to learn empowered networking skills

Gail Sussman Miller’s Specialties:
