Marianne Richmond’s Summary

Marianne Richmond, founded the Resonance Partnership, LLC a social media consultancy specializing in word of mouth marketing. We offer integrated multi-channel media programs...blogs, podcasts, video, mobile, and social networks. We work with Fortune 500 Corporations, non-profits, professional service providers and small businesses.

Services include blogger relations, online and offline marketing strategy and plan develop, implementation, and measurement. We offer Account Planning, Branding, Competitive Analysis and Consumer Insight.

Other services include facillitation, new product development, training, and organizational and individual assessment.
