Kelly Yip - CIPP, CAMS, AMLP’s Summary

In today's climate, financial institutions need to be proactive. The compliance and business sides have to be cooperative with one another. The latter is crucial in enabling a firm to meet its regulatory obligations. If it were up to me, I would advise all financial institutions to encourage their departments to work together and to emphasize the various risks associated in not sharing essential information, especially the reputational risk that comes with a big fine. I would, also, remind upper management that business can only be enhanced by clients and potential clients having the confidence that the firms are properly meeting their regulatory requirements.
