Erik Bayfield’s Summary

Erik began his career in engineering. Having gained experience working on some of the world's most challenging megaprojects, he transitioned to systems and process optimization. Having delivered a raft of cost-cutting projects, he is broadening his skills-set by completing the Sustainability Management course offered by University of British Columbia.

Adding green to projects infuses cost-cutting with an inspiring message that few companies can ignore in these economically challenging times.

Erik has the breadth of industry experience, deep understanding of organizational priorities, adaptability, big-picture thinking, leadership, and 'green' skills to deliver solutions that provide multiple benefits to the larger community, benefits to the bottom line and to the environment.

• Educated at University of British Columbia in Sustainability Management.

• Negotiated with clients and vendors to define requirements, secure discounts and set service level agreements.
