Dr. Mommy -’s Summary

For as long as I can remember I wanted to help people and so it was only natural for me to pursue a health related profession. I went to many of years of schooling before I decided which route to take and then decided to pursue Chiropractic.

The field of Chiropractic was perfect for me and my beliefs...while in Chiropractic school I met my soul-mate who is also a Chiropractor today:) After finishing my career we decided to have a family and then after much thought and prayers decided it would be best for me to home school our children. I have been homeschooling for over 10 years and love every minute of it.. I continue to be a Health/Wellness Advocate and am a speaker and writer on the subject. I have a website dedicated to teaching the public on health, nutrition and wellness called Dr. Mommy Health Tips (http://drmommyhealthtips.com) My goal is to teach the public to live happier and healthier lives.

I am a freelance and content writer and have been writing for as long as I can remember. Because of my l love of writing I started a writing business called Dr. Mommy Writes, where I am the freelance writer and will write on different topics for websites, magazines, and newsletters. I can add the needed content you are looking for to make your website more attractive. My topics range from health, nutrition, fitness, blogging, home schooling, work at home, going green and much more. Please be sure to visit my site for more information and rates at http://drmommywrites.com
