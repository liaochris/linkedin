Dirk Janssens’s Summary

The combination of both a Master degree in Industrial Design, an MBA in Marketing Management and being selected for a trainingship by the King Baudouin Foundation at Samsonite Europe NV, gave me the opportunity to gain experience as Product Planner, Product Manager (2 years), Marketing Manager (3 years) and Brand Manager at the world market leader in luggage.

Since july 2006 I invested as Founder and Co-owner in ONSEA HOUSE Tanzania Ltd., a Boutique Hotel concept which might be further developped and implemented at other locations in the upcomming years.

Welcome for a business meeting or conference in combination with a Safari, Kilimanjaro climb and/or Zanzibar beach holiday extension from our home base in Arusha!

Dirk Janssens’s Specialties:

Design & Product Planning & Management, Marketing Management, Brand Development, Concept Development, General Management. Market research and GFK analysis, negotiation and presentation skills. Fluent in Dutch, English and French and good knowledge of German.
