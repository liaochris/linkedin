Albert P. dela Cruz’s Summary

Albert Pangilinan de la Cruz is currently the Managing Director of ZylogiX Systems Incorporated.

He was Platform Strategy Manager for Microsoft Philippines from August 1, 2006 to June 27, 2009. Abet worked with the Open Source community driving Microsoft's Interoperability Initiative culminating with the Opening of the First ever 'Open Source and Interoperability Lab' in Asia.

He was also CTO of Level-Up! Games, Incorporated, The Country’s first Game publisher of Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Games (MMORPG) overseeing the day-to-day technical operation of the country's largest Server Farm (1000+) as well as the Applications Development, Management Information Systems, Service Delivery and Information Security of the company.

“Abet” has worked in ICT for 19+ years in various firms which includes ADCOMS, Cybernet, Bay Networks, co-founder and Vice-President of Trends & Technologies, Inc., President/Founder of NetX Solutions, President/Founder of WolfPac Communications, and Founder/CTO of Data Networks & Defense, Inc.

He has a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the University of the East and has attended trainings and conventions abroad relating to Information Security and LAN/WAN Internetworking .

He is a Director of the Philippine Computer Emergency Response Team (PHCERT), a member of the Supreme Court special Sub-committee on e-Commerce which created the first ever court rules for electronic evidence. Most recently, Abet’s work on a Draft Anti-Cybercrime Bill done with members of the former ITECC has been filed in both houses of Congress.

Honored by the Philippine Metropolitan Computer Times as one of the 18 Top I.T. Luminaries and Advocates of 2002 and is an alumni of US Department of State's International Visitors Leadership program for eCommerce and the World Economy 2005. Abet has ISC2-CISSP and GSEC-GIAC Certifications.
