Vinay Kalyan Parakala’s Summary

As the GM and Head of Software Services I am responsible for the P&L of the Software Services Line of Business, globally including Sales and Operations. As a member of the Senior Management Team at IBS Software, I drive the sales, packaging and innovation strategy for the services business across all domains.

Prior to this, I have been a revenue generator with 15 years of core IT services sales, account management, advertising and marketing experience and professional qualifications in management, business development and IT in the Banking & Financial services, Travel & Transportation, Insurance, Facilities management, Manufacturing, Distribution, Technology and Electronics verticals.

I have a specialist focus on Outsourcing, Off-shoring and Transition and have assisted many institutions to successfully create and execute an outsourcing strategy for Software Services. I have sold to & account managed numerous multinational clients from UK, Europe, US and Australasia, making international sales my forte.
