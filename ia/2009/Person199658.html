Mark Gibson’s Summary

27 years in sales, sales management, business development, marketing, consulting and sales training in the technology business in Australia, EMEA and 12 years in Silicon Valley.

Established sales and marketing consultancy business in UK in 2004 to help companies achieve four major objectives;

1. Clarity in messaging through alignment of sales and marketing messaging around product usage.

2. Precision in qualification through training customer-facing teams in advanced consultative selling techniques.

3. Process for consistent sales performance and higher win-rates.

4. Results sustained over time through closed-loop performance support.

Mark Gibson’s Specialties:

Recent consulting and training engagements in developing Customer Messaging Architecture and Buyer Relevant Messaging; consultative sales training and coaching in Software companies in Healthcare, Capital Markets, Telco, Internet infrastructure, Engineering design, Digital out-of-home Media, Wirelss Messaging, Computer Hardware and Telesales
