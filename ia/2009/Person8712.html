Alex Wallace AIRP’s Summary

A proud affiliate member of the Institute of Recruitment Professionals (AIRP). As such I am bound to a strict code of ethics and professionalism, which you can access via the link above ("IRP Code of Ethics").

Prior to becoming a Recruitment Consultant, I gained valuable experience working within the corporate world as a Senior Buyer at organisations such as Haymarket Publishing, British Oxygen Company and British Airways.

Commencing my recruitment career, I was able to utilise my past experience to understand the requirements and intricacies involved with external services. I managed a team of specialist recruiters and provided services for clients across the United Kingdom, Europe, USA, Canada, China and Australia.

I joined RSP in Sydney, Australia, to gain experience working abroad and embrace the challenge of working in a new market. I developed and expanded my skill-set to offer a truly consultative approach to my clients and network of job seekers. I rapidly gained a reputation for my professionalism and ability to deliver a highly valued level of service.

- Consulting and working with job seekers, providing guidance and support through the recruitment process. Equipping job seekers with valuable skills such as: preparing CVs, resumes and covering letters, looking for and discovering meaningful career opportunities, preparing for interviews, adjusting to new jobs and working environments

- Consulting and working with clients, evaluating needs, both stated and hidden, to create assignment briefs: recruitment strategy, time plan, position requirements and interview process. Identifying and building relationships with operational, tactical and strategic contacts within a client organisation

Alex Wallace AIRP’s Specialties:

Training, interviewing, needs analysis, creation of assignment brief, networking, search and selection, advert writing.
