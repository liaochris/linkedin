Pete Leibman’s Summary

My lifelong passion for healthy living and personal development stems from my firsthand experience in witnessing the powerful transformations that are possible when we challenge ourselves to be better.

My 2 companies, BetterFitness BetterHealth and Idealize Enterprises, share a common mission: both businesses help our clients develop better habits so that they can live better lives!

I am a huge proponent of LinkedIn because personal and professional relationships are essential to true success, and I believe we can all teach and learn from each other.


ACADEMIC AND PROFESSIONAL HIGHLIGHTS INCLUDE:

-Inducted into the prestigious Phi Beta Kappa Society after graduating from the Johns Hopkins University. Only 1% of the top students nationwide receive this honor each year for outstanding academic achievements.

-3-time #1 Salesperson of the Year for the Washington Wizards/ Washington Sports & Entertainment.

-2-time Recipient of the Abe Pollin Award from the Washington Wizards for dedication to the company and its clients.

-Active leadership volunteer for non-profits, including Thurgood Marshall Academy in Washington DC, Springfield Youth Club in Northern Virginia, and the Alumni Association for the Johns Hopkins University.

-Candidate for membership in the National Speakers Association as an expert on healthy living and personal development; featured speaker to a wide variety of student and young professional groups, the largest one being comprised of over 4,000 students at the Verizon Center in 2007.


ACTIVE FITNESS/HEALTH CERTIFICATIONS:
-Certified Personal Trainer through the American Council on Exercise (ACE).
-Certified Nutrition Coach and Certified Lifestyle and Weight Management Specialist through the National Exercise and Sports Trainers Association (NESTA)
-Certified Group Exercise Instructor through the Aerobics and Fitness Association of America (AFAA).

Pete Leibman’s Specialties:

(1) I teach students and young professionals how to develop more effective personal and professional habits so that they can achieve true success in the "real world."

(2) I also teach adults of all ages how they can develop more effective fitness, health, nutrition, and lifestyle habits so that they can live better, longer lives.
