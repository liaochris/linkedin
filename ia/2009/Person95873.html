Howard Levy’s Summary

Brand Strategist with 17 years experience, specializing in nonprofits

--------------------------------------

Red Rooster Group is a strategic marketing design firm that helps nonprofits and businesses to improve their brand, create cost-effective publication and email systems, reach donors efficiently and promote fundraising events.

> Specializing in creating compelling messages from complex issues with 17 years of experience promoting organizations, businesses and causes that are improving the world.

> We help launch earned income ventures and promote social enterprises with strong brands and creative design that cuts through the clutter and captures buyers' interests.
