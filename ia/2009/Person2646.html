    48th Highlanders of Canada

    (Military industry)

    February 2004 — June 2009 (5 years 5 months)

    Designed more than 3 newsletters a year since the spring of 2004 for the 48th Highlanders of Canada Reserve Regiment. Thanks to the many contributors of articles and photos, this newsletter is also a wealth of information regarding Regimental history, especially World Wars 1 and 2.
