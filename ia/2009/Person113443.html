Jim Staylor’s Summary

Over 20 years professional communications experience. A background in TV News, PR, speaking and training. After producing TV shows and working for various production companies for nearly a decade, I founded Staylor-Made Communications in 1992 to serve the creative, production, and project management needs of corporate clients, non-profit organizations, other producers, advertising/PR firms and multimedia developers. Please visit http://www.staylor-made.com

Industries served: high-tech, healthcare, hospitality, defense, restaurant, and retail. Clients: Sony, HP, JVC, HealthCare Insights, ResMed, Sharp Healthcare, Garden Fresh Restaurant Corp, Outback Steakhouse, DARPA, BAE Systems, Rhino Linings, SABIA, and many more.

MOVING MESSAGES, BRILLIANT RESULTS
We create moving messages and produce brilliant results for people who want to educate, entertain, influence or inspire on TV, video, film, DVD, the web, or at live events.

HIGHLIGHTS
• Developed "How to Keep Your DO-IT-YOURSELF VIDEO From Looking Like You Did It Yourself" Instructional DVD (see Amazon.com)
• Produced and directed several video games
• Created many entertaining and inspirational event videos
• Won numerous awards for scriptwriting and producing
• Served as voice talent on games, commercials, videos, etc.
• Spoken or presented at conferences and conventions
• Travel for clients nationally & internationally (Toronto, Tijuana)
• Attended courses thru UCLA, USC Film School, NATAS, etc.

GOALS
Help clients find solutions, tell stories, and move audiences through high-quality video. Share my knowledge and expertise so others can use the tools of media to communicate more effectively. Expand opportunities for my associates to stay busy making money doing what we all love.

Jim Staylor’s Specialties:

Corporate video production, project management, scriptwriting, directing, editing, instructional design
