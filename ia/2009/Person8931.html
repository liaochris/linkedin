Al Hanzal’s Summary

Today's customers have shifted from retail street businesses to web based purchases. With this shift, small business owners must adapt their marketing practices to match customer's new buying habits.

Today's small businesses need more than a website. Small businesses need an ongoing strategy of web design tools to effectively attract and convert customers on their websites. Successful Marketing Tools provides the web design tools that help small business owners achieve these results with limited time and money budgets.

Al Hanzal’s Specialties:
