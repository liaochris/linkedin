Davin Ogden’s Summary

My knowledge in this area first began with Holly Mann and the teachings in her book Honest Riches. Later on, I became a member of Affilorama, which was a great experience for me at the time, as it explained by video some of the road blocks that I'd been running into and it helped me to cross over those barriers.

I've never really stopped learning about this magnificent field and have always worked on improving my knowledge. I've also always worked on helping other people entering the online arena with the knowledge I've always acquired.

Becoming friends with some of the people I'd started learning from has always meant a great deal to me. I enjoy helping Holly with her forum, and it sort of went hand in hand for me because I'd already been helping many people in there to improve their knowledge or to help get them past some type of barrier they may have been having. Holly's a great person to know, and a true friend, which is hard to find sometimes these days.

A while after I got to know Tim Brocklehurst, and he became a big influence as well. I help Tim out with a few things upon request to from time to time. As well, Tim is a fantastic person with a big heart. It's great to know him. Since then, I've gotten to know quite a few more marketers and it's always cool gaining new friends and influences in the business.

As far as my goals go, I will continue to build on my business which has become known as Groovytastic Empire. I'll be incorporating more sites and revenue streams to this entity over the course of time.

I've got a couple of different projects in the wings right now with other people, one of which I'm very excited about, but is to soon to mention it here.
