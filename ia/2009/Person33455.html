Brian Cosgrove’s Summary

Currently, I'm a professional Web Analytics consultant specializing in implementation, configuration, remediation, vendor selection, training, and strategy. I work with a great group of web analytics professionals at Razorfish who ensure that clients get the most out of their Web Analytics platforms. We're vendor-agnostic to ensure that our clients get the best service regardless of platform. I specialize in WebTrends and Google Analytics but I am studying Unica, CoreMetrics, Omniture, IndexTools, Woopra, Hava a Mint, Fireclick, ClickTracks, "Project Gatineau", and similar platforms. I also work in building tools to streamline the Web Analytics configuration process, as well as building partnerships with the various vendors to ensure that we are able to fully support every aspect of the platforms.

I co-organize web analytics gatherings in the Philadelphia area so if you're interested, let me know.

Previously, I focused on technical and strategic aspects of Search Engine Optimization (SEO). I still participate in SEO projects but to a lesser role. Some specialties include link-building strategy, search reputation management, web server settings and redirection, content development strategy, IA and internal linking strategy, and technical problem-solving related to search.

I've recently began speaking at industry events such as Search Engine Strategies (SES), Search Marketing Expo (SMX), and Search Camp so look me up if you're attending these functions.

I also have experience with software development and systems engineering related to resource allocation and functional availability modeling and optimization.
