Aaron Davies’s Summary

I am a determined and professional recruiter - my approach is a consultative one and I place high value on building long term relationships with my clients. I gain great satisfaction from dispelling the traditional image of rogue recruiters by providing an attentive and quality service every time.

Throughout my career, I have consistently achieved results beyond expectations and was proud to be selected for a sales management position at the age of 23.

My primary area of focus is on both the SaaS and Cloud Computing marketspace - I have an extensive network of candidates looking for new opportunities in this area. Please contact me for further details should you have a specific requirement within your business and I will be pleased to assist.

As of January 2010, I have assumed full responsibility for our space recruitment division in addition to my existing IT clients.

OCC has been involved in recruiting specialist engineers and technicians for the European space sector for over 38 years.

Our clients in this area span from European level supranational institutions related to ESA to prime vendors and contractors operating within the European Union.

OCC manage recruitment campaigns for space industry clients in many areas, including AOCS, spacecraft operations, spacecraft simulations, thermodynamics, satellite engineering, payload instrumentation, remote sensing, flight dynamics, ground station control and related areas such as astrophysics, and astronomical research.

Give me a call and try me out. I am confident that I will exceed your expectations in what is a highly competitive industry.
