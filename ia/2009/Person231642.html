Greg Parrish’s Summary

My purpose is to provide my Realtor ® clients with excellence in all aspects of the mortgage financing experience. The desired result is that my clients will close 5 or more buyer side transactions monthly. I will earn the trust of my clients by providing uncompromising service to their buyers by underwriting their loan file up front and immediately notifying them if issues arise. I will apply the following ideals to each client I work with: honesty; integrity; sincerity; professionalism and product knowledge

I will use my sales and marketing skills to assist my business referral sources (Realtors ®) in building their contact base to increase their number of closings.

I will maintain clear, concise, and open lines of
communications based on my clients specific needs. I take full responsibility for my client’s satisfaction with their mortgage financing experience, thereby earning a steady flow of referral business.

I have been working in the mortgage banking industry for over eleven years helping people obtain the best financing products available.

Integrity is the cornerstone of my business. I believe a mortgage loan should not be a mere transaction. My focus is on building relationships. As a Bank of America mortgage loan officer, I take a consultative approach to the home loan process by customizing each home financing program to help my clients achieve their overall financial goals. Many loan officers can originate a loan. My mission is to get the right loan for each of my clients.

I’m a Navy veteran. I was owner/president of a building maintenance firm for nearly ten years before entering the home lending industry. I have extensive real world experience in real estate financing to get the right loan program in place for my clients.
