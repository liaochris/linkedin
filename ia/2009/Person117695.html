John Mullen’s Summary

Mr. Mullen's background includes company startup phase, mezzanine growth and exit strategies such as joint ventures, merger and public offering. He has particular experience in IT project management and product development of global systems for military, government or commercial organizations including energy.

Mr. Mullen's technical background includes Software Systems Engineering with concentration in Data Communications Security and Network Performance. He is proficient with either PMI or SEMP management tools, as well as CMM configuration management, DIACAP and Common Crtieria Security Accreditation methodologies. He has engineering experience with distributed object models in different technologies.

Mr. Mullen's experience in team development extends from small groups to multi-level matrixed organizations, developing managers in areas ranging from Product Design, Consulting, Engineering, Manufacturing, QA, Sales, Marketing and Contracts Administration.

Promia Incorporated develops and sells security appliances targeted to finance, military and energy. The company's devices are currently protecting global networks in the US Navy.

John Mullen’s Specialties:

Mr. Mullen is experienced in identifying market needs and market timing, as well as building proposals, gaining funding and managing project execution to complete on schedule and budget. He also has experience in building sales teams to grow business. He is knowledable about audit and operational policies and procedures in the commercial sector, government and military sectors of industry.
