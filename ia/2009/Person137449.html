Laura Carmichael’s Summary

I love starting up small businesses. Over the years, I’ve done lots of trading and investing in stocks, options, and commodities. My latest business venture combines my core passion – helping others – and marketing. As many know, I have always enjoyed getting to know folks and helping them meet their needs. Sometimes, that meant lending a shoulder for them to cry on. Other times, that meant mentoring them on various aspects of their professional and personal life. And, in some circumstances, it meant finding them products and services that could assist them. With my latest venture, I research products and services that may be interesting and useful to people. Then, I try them out for myself. If I like them, I spread the word about them. I have been able to help people reduce or completely eliminate their debt (by referring them to highly effective debt negotiators), find ways to bring in extra income in their spare time, find personal wellness coaches, find business mentors, find investment mentors, find fund raising vehicles, find real estate mentors, amongst other things.

Laura Carmichael’s Specialties:

Leading network marketers to the tools, resources, and training needed to build their own business. I mentor those that are 100% serious about growing their business.
