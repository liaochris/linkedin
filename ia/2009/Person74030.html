Eric Blessing’s Summary

Eric is a technology professional with over 8 years experience in analysis, design, and implementation. He primarily specializes in PeopleTools design and development of customizations using PeopleCode and PeopleSoft Internet Architecture. He has technical and functional experience with ELM, CRM, ESA, Portal, Student Administration, and HCM in the PeopleSoft Enterprise ERP suite. Eric has had experience in project management at a large multi-national software company. His experience includes developing and implementing the communication plans and project plans as well as training initiatives for company wide software rollouts. In addition to this, his past roles have included Technical Lead and Functional Lead positions.

Eric holds a PeopleSoft 8 Technical Certification.
