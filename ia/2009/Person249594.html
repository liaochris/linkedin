Paul Yarin’s Summary

Developing innovative and useful interactive products is my passion. This has included medical, automotive, and research systems as well as interactive installations and exhibits. I enjoy interdisciplinary challenges and work well under pressure.

Depending on the project scale, I can work as a team leader, as a collaborator, or independently.

Paul Yarin’s Specialties:

Electronic design, mechanical design, 2D and 3D CAD, rapid prototyping, laser fabrication. Ideation, problem solving, research, patents. Fieldwork, testing, analysis, installation.
