Erwin van Schaik’s Summary

Erwin van Schaik (1968) is an outstanding process coach. From a psychological and sociological background he involved for over two decades now as a management consultant at several national and international consultancy firms. Erwin combines a sharp analytic view with an intuitive feeling for inter human relations within organisations. Quick and effectively he gives certain balance into the complex interaction between business needs, process improvement and the motivation of the people that have to ‘do’ it. Always with a caring eye for details, but without loosing the bigger picture.
His skills and knowledge were obtained as a project leader and process coach in engagements focusing on strategic reorientations, outsourcing, process definition, project recovery, gaming and simulation, and workshop facilitation. The following industry sectors were addressed: social security and labour market, consumer packaged goods, ICT, high-tech electronics, chemical industry, life sciences, social housing and central & local governments.

Erwin van Schaik’s Specialties:

Management Consulting, Corporate Performance Management, Change Management, Service Level Management, Project Recovery, Gaming & Simulation, Workshop Facilitation
