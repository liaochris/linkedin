Laurent Herbillon’s Summary

I am a recognized professional in the Telecom-Internet-Media market for the past 8 years. I co-led one of the most successful launch of i-mode in Europe. i-mode was widely recognized as one of the best mobile multimedia services in the world with a rate of active users of more than 50%. Part of this success was based on the setting up of a win-win ecosystem with content providers, handset manufacturers and technology providers. Thanks to a radical new approach to content, initiated in april 2002, Bouygues Telecom was able to gather in its portal all the big Internet brands : Yahoo!, Google, Microsoft, etc. Thanks to this partnership Bouygues Telecom was the first operator to launch Live Messenger on an i-mode phone. In 2006 I initiated the plan which allowed Bouygues Telecom to extend i-mode to all handsets and then increased dramatically data usage and revenues. In 2005 I started a new business by selling adverting spaces in our portal and then developed innovative commercial approaches combining content providers inventory with our owns. As «Kiosk» BU manager I was able to deliver a 15% average revenue growth over the past 4 years with premium SMS and wholesale. In the NFC field, I was one of the early promoter of an interoperable strategy with all the MNOs as well as a win-win partnership approach with services providers. This led to the creation of the French Mobile Contactless Services Association. Before that I held various positions at Bouygues, particularly as Strategic Planning Director at the Group Level. This position gave me a deep understanding of the various challenges faced by a diversified industrial group. I have spent several years in the US where I acquired a strong background in doing business with US companies and competences in reengineering businesses in a difficult market. I have a strong sense of leadership and managed up to 50 people of various competences. I am objective driven and always looking for out of the box solutions and ideas.
