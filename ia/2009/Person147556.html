Marie Kazadi’s Summary

I am the type of person who is never satisfied with settling. Always striving for that one career that makes me happy, I have finally found it in hair design! The best part about designing hair is not only the creativity, the innovation, and the accolades from my peers, but the reaction from my clients when they look in the mirror for the first time after a session with me and can't stop looking at themselves. How lucky am I that I get to make people feel beautiful everyday I go to work! As I embark on this new career, my goal is to be educated in all areas of my industry, in particular coloring, color correction, and blondes. However, I am versed in all areas of hair design.
