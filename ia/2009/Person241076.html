Valerie Cudnik’s Summary

I entered the graphic design field just after the first Macs came out (1987). I've maintained my business since that time, moving from Sunnyvale, California to San Jose, San Diego and finally to Chesapeake, Virginia. I love working with small businesses to get their marketing rolling and the cash flowing into their business. And I'm good at it. There's certainly no shortage of customers. Every business has to use *some* marketing materials: ads, letterhead, business cards, brochures, web sites, signs, etc. I've been about evenly balanced between print and web stuff since I left WAVY-TV to work on my own again.

Valerie Cudnik’s Specialties:

I try to help my customers focus their advertising on what benefits the customer receives vs. what the company does.
