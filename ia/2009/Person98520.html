Chirag Ahmedabadi’s Summary

Co-founder of IndaPoint Technologies, Ahmedabadi began his career in 1997 as a software programmer. In growing his career, he held leading roles at IT companies like IBM. He holds several degrees, including bachelors and master's degrees in computer engineering and an MBA from Newport University, California.

Ahmedabadi has contributed to many open source solutions and he is also a chief architect of 'Akaar' a PHP-based robust architecture. He has also authored articles about PHP for PHP Architect, a leading PHP magazine. (http://www.phparch.com/c/magazine/author/91) His experience and expertise with open source technology and his unique ability to think 'out of box' makes his contribution invaluable to the company.

Chirag Ahmedabadi is always happy to talk with our clients, and is always available via telephone, e-mail and all instant messengers.
