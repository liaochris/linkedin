Roland Smart’s Summary

I'm a marketing guy with experience working in both product and service verticals. I've spent several years working as a consultant, which has allowed me to develop a wide range of skills and get first hand experience implementing programs across a variety of media and channels. I believe this offers me a unique perspective because I know what it takes to make marketing initiatives a reality. I've been at this long enough to experience many successes and learn from failures, and I've developed a talent for estimating the amount of resources required to get a project off the ground and maintained over time.

I've been fortunate to join some of the companies that I've consulted to, including Adaptive Path and Adina For Life, Inc. In recent years, I've been able to increasingly tie my tactical experience into marketing strategies, product and service development roadmaps, and marketing budgets. This is in part because I've generally worked with the management team at the organizations that have hired me.

I've been a good fit at small startups and medium sized firms that are growing because I offer a wide range of experience. Many of the companies I've worked with can't afford to have more than four or five people on their marketing team, so they require someone who can handle a diverse set of challenges ranging from a company website, to online communications, collateral, events, product and service development, market research, managing creative teams, strategy, budget management and more. I'm the guy to lead these kinds of teams.

I'd love to share more of my experience with you, so don't hesitate to send me a note. In the meantime, I invite you to check out my blog about marketing, culture, and smart ideas at http://www.rolandsmart.com.

Best.

Roland
