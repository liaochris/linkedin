Israel Kehat’s Summary

I often become an integral part of new products or projects, from very large-scale projects of prominent companies all the way to startups.

My expertise lies at the point where technology meets business, and I assist in the entire range from forming the product specifications, through the various activities such as architecting the solution and establishing the engineering team. For this purpose, I retain various high-end consulting and implementation teams, a high-tech placement agency, etc.

The technology I most often use is Microsoft based, from low-level system development, applicative and infrastructure .NET, web and information systems.

I have interests and experience in many different fields, including Internet properties, entertainment, education, medical, defense, industrial and other.

In recent years I have provided consulting, contracting, training and mentoring to a large number of companies, including Microsoft (strategic, software), Intel (architecture, semiconductors), Rafael (contracting, defense), Creo (mentoring, printing), Orange/Partner (training, mobile), Ki-Bi (implementation, mobile, startup recently public), and similar.

Israel Kehat’s Specialties:

Product positioning, competitive analysis, technology architecture, overall catalyst from idea inception to actual customer delivery
