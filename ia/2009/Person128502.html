Ken "K-Bar" Barker’s Summary

• Twenty four years of military and business experience focusing on nuclear missile operations, communications, command and control, air operations center operations, training, education, project management, international relations, homeland security, and business development.
• Experienced business developer with a vast personal network of business contacts in the defense industry, IT industry, and others.
• Extensive international experience including operational combat experience (Bosnia, Kosovo, Macedonia, Afghanistan, Iraq) and living and working assignments in Japan, Kuwait, Italy, Belgium, Greece, and Qatar. Vast resource of international contacts with NATO countries as well as nations of the Middle East and Pacific.
• Exceptional communicator, experienced instructor and lecturer able to present varied information to a myriad of military, civilian, diplomatic corps, and governmental audiences.
• Author of two books on Operations IRAQI FREEDOM and ENDURING FREEDOM as well as dozens of professional papers on various military operations in the last quarter century. Member of review committee for the National Geospatial Intelligence Foundation conducted at the National Academy of Sciences in Washington D.C. Currently writing a screenplay based on one of the books on Operation ENDURING FREEDOM.
• Creator of ‘The K-Bar List Veterans’ Network;’ over 200 companies with access to nearly 700 veteran resumes and connected to over 100 other veteran networks encompassing more than 10,000 veterans around the world…all free…just a hobby that has helped hundreds of veterans get new careers.
