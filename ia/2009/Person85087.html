Geoffroy de Drouas’s Summary

Looking for suitable distributors in leather goods or in the fashion retail industry in Europe, Africa, Middle East (except Saudi Arabia and Syria), Oceania, Latin America and Travel Retail worldwide.

BONIA's forte is into ladies' handbags mainly made with Italian leather in Malacca (Malaysia). We also have shoes (gents & ladies) and men's ready-to-wear (Spring Summer only). Time pieces and eyewear are manufactured under license.

In addition to BONIA, we have two other lines, Sembonia and Carlo Rino, that are trendier and appealing to a younger clientele.

Please foward your enquiries to geoffroy@bonia.com
