Jonathan Davey’s Summary

Latest adventure is
http://www.onlinemarketingmagic.co.uk

When you are looking for online business lead generation then that’s when we can help.

We’ve been involved in online marketing since 1999, creating LiaiseOnline Limited in 2001.

You can review the timeline of our evolution here:
http://www.liaiseonline.co.uk/news193.html

Our creation of a website that allowed other websites to be aggregated started an evolution that has given us a platform for online marketing and search engine optimisation that is top of the class.

We’re not arrogant enough to think it is the most sophisticated solution out there! It doesn’t have all the bells and whistles, the foundations were created 4 years ago which not only means it has credibility with the search engines, but also makes it simple to use if you are comfortable using online content management systems like Linked In.

In 2008 we had over 1,000,000 visitors to our various micro sites (our term for the websites our system creates). These micro sites sit under different urls, many which have been selected as they are logical search strings used by the target audience to find more information on a topic.

2009 and we are taking our service out to a wider audience with two new standard template formats to add to our current Business in Berkshire offering. These will be under Business Lead Generation & Business Windows, the former for B2B sales environments and the latter for those focussed on B2C.

If you’d like some help generating business leads using online marketing then contact us on 01753 852904 or send us a message through Linked In. Thank you.
