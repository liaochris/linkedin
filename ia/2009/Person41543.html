Charlie Davenport’s Summary

A successful career in Professional Recruiting (over 25 years) was interrupted by an opportunity of a lifetime; working in what had theretofore been a passionate hobby.

My last position as Director of Membership for the 28,000 member, 820 chapter international Barbershop Harmony Society followed over 15 years of leadership roles as a volunteer. Through the years, as a volunteer and then a Staff member, had been in many high profile positions including President of a 4500 member, 100 chapter District. These positions gave me experience leading and directing teams with national and international impact; in making presentations to large groups of people; oversight of over 33% of the organizations income budget; motivation and training of staff and volunteers at all levels.

The restructuring of Society Headquarters required elimination of three directorship positions, including mine. Thus, I am focused on returning to what I had done in my prior career, Professional Recruiting. Experience there has been in retained and contingency search environments for VP through entry level positions. Greatest familiarity with the areas of IT recruiting (having served concurrently are a Systems Admin) and Accounting/Finance.

What sets me apart? I have done what most others can only say they can do. I can see through the fluff and have an innate sense ability to quickly get the real story from the candidate.

Charlie Davenport’s Specialties:

Recruiting personnel in the areas of Information Technology, all levels of Accounting, Mid to upper level manufacturing and Operations Management, and the especially hard to find personnel.
If you can convey to me what you seek, I can find the person.
