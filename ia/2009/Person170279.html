Mike Laub’s Summary

I am a Sr. Electrical Engineer, with a PE license, and exposure to the many aspects of electrical engineering that allow me to be a leading member of an electrical engineering team. My assoc. degree in mechanical engineering, bachelors in electrical engineering and PE license are indications of my well grounded understanding of the electrical engineering discipline.

Also, as a student I worked many years as a CAD draftsman, and I have a very solid understanding of AutoCAD, Micro station, and other drafting tools.

I am familiar with typical procedures associated with controlled engineering document management process.

I am competent in the many tools that engineers use to complete their design including the use of SKM electrical system analysis, and Visual lighting software.

My construction documents conform to local, national, and international codes, as well as professional and industry standards such as the US NEC, the IBC, CEC, Life Safety Codes, professional standards such as the IEE “Color Books”, and industry standards from the API and AGA.

I have worked for a variety of power and energy companies including Suncor, BP, Enbridge.

I have completed work for the US Air Force, and Fish and Wildlife Service. I have also completed electrical designs for commercial clients such as McDonald’s USA LLC, and institutional facilities such as Hospitals.

I have completed electrical distribution designs for industrial applications such as oil extraction, processing, and natural gas and oil pipeline facilities.

I have worked in many stages of the construction process including conducting business cases, handling of relationships with approved manufacturers, evaluation of approved products, conceptual design, permitting, purchasing, and construction support. As part of my construction support, I helped resolve permitting issues across the country, conducting short circuit, load, emergency lighting, and energy code calculations.
