Allen Crane’s Summary

Bio:

Allen has more than 13 years experience in eBusiness, web analytics, acquisition and retention marketing, profitability analysis, segmentation, and process improvement. After co-developing one of the world’s first web mining platforms at IBM, Allen went to Sabre, Inc., where his work led to profitability improvements for customers including American Airlines. He led the first of several web analytics consulting teams at Dell, focused on providing analytical intelligence to drive growth and profitability across multiple online business units. He developed the first-ever business process improvement program for pricing, marketing, and reverse-logistics teams and has spoken at several conferences on the subject. Allen expanded his analytical reach as Principal Consultant with Visual Sciences (now Omniture), a leading data analysis and visualization platform. With expertise in developing controlled experimentation strategies, his clients included Fortune 50 and start-ups such as Wells Fargo, AT&T, Dell, and Louis Vuitton. He currently serves as Executive Director of Market Intelligence and Business Analytics at USAA, where he is responsible for measuring the overall performance of Member Analytics, Campaign Analytics, Web and Channel Analytics, and the Statistical Models that support them. USAA is a leading financial services company that has been voted Business Week's "#1 Customer Service Champ" for the second year in a row.

Allen has authored articles and papers on web analytics and optimizing online marketing investments. He has been a guest lecturer at MIT Sloan School of Business, the University of Texas at Austin, and he is a sought-after speaker on Six Sigma. Allen holds a M.S. in Operations Research from Southern Methodist University, and a B.S. in Information Systems from the University of Texas at Arlington.

A self-described analytics junkie, his blog can be found at allencrane.typepad.com
