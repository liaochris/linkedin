Peter Kentie’s Summary

The connection in my career is a strong love for design and technology and a strong will to share and publish my knowledge and experiences.

I work as marketing and media manager for PSV Eindhoven. With a strong emphasis on value creation through broad (cross)media reach and emphasis on conversion of PSV related products and services. Innovative new media development is a key focus area, especially in the domain of mobile services and TV & Internet conversion.

Public speeching and giving presentations are a great way to promote my innovative personal marketing and crossmedia beliefs. You can always contact me for a speaking engagement.

I have published two books about Desktop Publishing and Web Design for Addison Wesley, Pearson Education. The books have been translated in English, German, Spanish and Korean and over 65.000 copies were printed and sold.

As a hobby I have interest in wireless internet and founded Hotspot and the Hotspot Event (www.hotspot.nl) to blog about the Wi-Fi and WiMax business and it's development.
Current interest areas are rapid prototyping and electricity based mobility for which I'm setting up a new blogging project.

Culturally I'm active as co-founder of the STRP art & technology festival (www.strp.nl) and with board positions at the Dutch Design Week (www.ddw.nl) and the Baltan medialab. For Brainport I initiated the Wireless Eindhoven project together with Joep Brouwer.

Follow my links and learn to know me well: www.kentie.com
