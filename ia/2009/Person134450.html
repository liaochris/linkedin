Kris Mcknight’s Summary

Design Director at KMD, a London-based visual communication agency. Worked in Chicago, Sydney and London on a wide range of projects throughout the past 8 years including branding, packaging, web design, advertising and print.

From sophisticated and suave to bold and contemporary, my work has one thing in common despite the stark contrasts in style...Every piece conveys the right message in a way that people notice and respond to. Messages that are easily understood and delivered in the most direct way possible. I believe this is what constitutes good design and I believe in making brands work harder for what they represent.

Kris Mcknight’s Specialties:

Graphic design; advertising, packaging, corporate identity, brand guidelines, direct mail, promotional material, web design, annual reports, stationery, brochures, magazines, posters, corporate collateral, signage and more.
