Andrew Janssen’s Summary

Most web contractors suck. It's true. I talk with more people who are dissatisfied (disgusted?) by their experience with contractors than I care to count. At the end of the day, and especially the beginning, what makes a solution successful is an active partnership with a listening developer who responds to your needs in real-time. The focus of Factory 44 (my freelance company) is you, not my next five clients.

I'm an active believer in Getting Things Done and Agile Development. I believe that discussing ideas with a pen and napkin is usually more effective than volleying specification drafts over emails (and weeks).

Factory 44 doesn't create systems that require my ongoing presence (or invoice) to maintain. When I solve your problem, I end your problem. Eternal project maintenance is boring and nobody wants to do it. Not even me.

I target small businesses with clear-thinking management. I'd like to hear about your interesting and meaningful project.

Andrew Janssen’s Specialties:

End-to-end web site production: design, development, testing and tuning, deployment, and maintenance. I also do: 1) Graphic and web design 2) Web publicity and advertising, E-commerce 3) Cross-platform client-side development. Database, game, and graphics programming.

Specifically: PHP and Ruby on Rails. Drupal and Alfresco for content management solutions.
