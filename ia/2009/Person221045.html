Samuel Prasad, Ph.D.’s Summary

Dr. Prasad is a successful technology executive who has worked with many companies in China, India, Europe and the U.S. He is a strategic thinker, innovator and a visonary who has had a successful history in developing technical solutions that are aligned with customer needs and business goals.

He is an expert on scalable networks, data encryption, digital media and mobile technologies. He consults in many areas including electronic payment processing, mobile payments at the POS using RFID and visual tags, online casual gaming and OOH (out-of-home) marketing using digital media. Dr. Prasad has more than 20 years experience in designing large-scale computing systems and multi-dimensional data warehouses. Sam has a Ph.D. in Robotics & Computer Science from the Stevens Institute of Technology, and a master's degree in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Technology.

Sam is a member of the Project Management Institute and a certified project management professional (PMP). He is also a member of the American Society of Quality and a certified software quality engineer (CSQE).
