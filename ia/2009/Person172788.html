Neha Asthana’s Summary

An individual who has always had a zest for the Human Resource, be it sourcing it, retaining it, training it or empowering it, Neha has always been at her buoyant best in and around people. An aggressive networker and a passionate human being when it comes to doing things with a difference or just par excellance, she has successfully built her very own company at the age of 25 years & prides in it being a place where employees are treated as family and the work atmosphere is charged with enthusiasm.
