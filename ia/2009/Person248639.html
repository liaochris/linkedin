Ray Stone’s Summary

My skills and qualifications have been developed over 19 years of experience and across a wide variety of network platforms. I have been involved with and have worked with various Microsoft operating systems and other products since the late 80s, allowing me to develop strong troubleshooting skills with respect to a wide array of Microsoft products.
