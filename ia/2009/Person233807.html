Tim Meinhardt’s Summary

As a proven senior executive with over two and a half decades of expertise within the software, technology services, and banking communities.

My unique experiences provide an opportunity for companies looking for new leadership and a new direction.

My winning results are forged through strategic based action plans, proven leadership techniques, a motivating work ethic, and an entrepreneurial dedication to success.

In my career I have exceeded expectations in every aspect of business. Starting as a recognized top producer, becoming a leader among sales and executive management, and ultimately, overall operations and ownership.

I gained invaluable experience building an unfunded technology services start-up Internet Information Services, Inc. (IIS) into the 11th fastest growing company as recognized by the Washington Business Journal in the Washington D.C. area in 1999. Ultimately successfully selling the company in March of 2000.

As a successful former owner, I understand the absolute importance of the owner’s goals, the significance of shareholder value, and the requirement for action over talk.

I take the duty of responsibility and leadership with the care of an owner and dedicate myself to the task. I have a complete understanding of the importance of financial performance and it’s absolute relentless conviction.

My unique career experiences provide the ability to recognize those special challenges facing today’s businesses and why they may not be achieving their potential shareholder goals.

During the last several years I has provided fulltime and interim executive guidance, hands–on daily operational change management, and sales expertise to software and technology services companies in the Washington D. C. metro area.
