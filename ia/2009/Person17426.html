Ann Marie Glaviano’s Summary

My goal is to grow my organization into a fully funded non for profit that can help seriously ill patient maintain their pets while they recover. My personal experience is on my website at www.petsnpatients.com.

Organizer of PetsNPatients Not For Profit with the support of Community Economic Development Law Project CED Law Program of the Chicago Lawyers' Committee for Civ and h the “pro bono” support of the national law firm Jenner and Block LLP 2006-2007.

I believe in the Power of One and I am seeking your help to build a NATIONAL outreach in every pet resuce across the USA to help gathering interested persons and groups to create Progam Partnership in your own community

While our economy is felt by a broader band width of people in the past year, for newly disabled patients, there needs to be a greater awareness of the positive healing nature of companion animals for patients is needed, this is not pet therapy as we know it. Our goal is to network of care options to assist patients with short term for their companion pets so they have a chance to maintain their pet while they recover. www.petsnpatients.com

I am also seeking corporate sponsors, grant writers to join my effort to help get much needed operational funding in place to help us establish a bedrock facility in Chicagoland and then have team in each major city in the USA help our program partners subsidize thier local programs.

My expertise is interwoven with executive level task based project management,administration, "rolled up sleeves" action to get plans into place. I work well with business owners, CFO's, CPA's and team building groups all levels of business operations.

    We are a 501c3 IL tax exempt charity. Our grassroots mission is to generate a greater national awareness of the positive healing nature of companion animals for patients enduring health crises. We are an internet referral service growing our base of program partners to start a chapter in every pet rescue to consider the needs of patients to avert needless re-homing of pets or having pets destroyed on account of owner's illness.

    We seek to network options to maintain relationships between patients and their pets during the patient health crise. Disability is not a choice! The chain of events surrounding new and total disabilty are no less catastropic than the destruction of a personal "Katrina" and many pets are displaced. I am seeking volunteer pet respite homes with pet rescues, boarding centers, pet sitters and other responsible parties to help patients facing the most severe health crises.
    Patients are invited to write their stories of companion pet love on our website and upload their home photos to uplift patients where ever they may be. We select stories to become PetsNPatients Ambassador team to participate in our "Year of Celebration" calanders and Journaling books, etc on our print on demand services. Fund raised on line help provide operational funds for our mission. We are in need of support from many organizations that will dovetail into our mission, which is P.P.A.W.S.S., Postive Pet Action With Social Service.

    "It's all about love, laughter and healing, one tail at a time."
