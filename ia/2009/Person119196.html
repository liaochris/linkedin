Jon Beverley’s Summary

Jon (regularly mis-spelled as John Beverly!) is an entrepreneur and general manager who genuinely cares about mobile interactivity. Particular expertise in the use of the mobile channel for operational CRM applications in retail and financial services.

• Experienced entrepreneurial business leader and general manager, with skills in M&A, corporate finance, business development, project management, team building, change management and investor relations.
• 14 years start-up experience, including 4 in a VC-funded mobile software company. Specialist in business-building from concept to execution, with over $15MM raised.
• Sold an incubated business to a US VC, realising 4.8x profits on initial outlay.
• International experience including senior roles in London, New York, New Zealand & Geneva, and international sales across the globe.

Most recently Jon relocated to NYC, where he continues to consult to a variety of digital and mobile firms. Prior to this, Jon spent 2008 with the UK's leading mobile solutions and marketing provider, Sponge, as their Commercial Director, having returned to the UK from NZ through his role as International VP of Strategy at mobile banking firm Fronde Anywhere. He also saw action as CEO of a mobile marketing firm during his 3 years in NZ.

Prior to this, Jon co-founded London-based mobile commerce firm Scan Mobile Ltd, in 1999, and was instrumental in securing financing of USD $10MM from idealab, Investor Growth Capital and Primedia Ventures. As COO for 4 years, Jon personally contracts from clients such as Orange, Vodafone, Argos and HSBC. Other achievements included overseeing investor relations, running an acquisition, directly managing Scan US, and finally negotiating the merger with competitor Digital Rum Ltd. in 2003.

Before this, Jon built and ran his own new media consultancy, which he sold in April 1999, and wrote extensively on new media topics for Financial Times Media & Telecoms.
