Prithwi Thakuria’s Summary

I dream in digital and fluent in the technologies that define our world and passionate about the way they’re shaping our future. I am a communicator who understands how technology connects us, and want to shape that conversation. I am a restless innovator who is not only waiting for the next big idea to happen, I want to make it happen.

My 18 year flourishing career has been a wonderful blend of experiences from startup ventures, consulting firms and large enterprises. I have worked in the financial services, big 5 consulting, retail, health care, genomics and energy sector and have progressed through the ranks of developer, DBA, enterprise architect to senior management.

I have conceived championed, architected and deployed ideas and solutions involving bleeding edge technologies in the US and Europe that includes, but not limited to, multi-terabyte Data Warehouses/BI/ERM/CRM/B2B/B2C systems, client facing high-traffic enterprise solutions and web-centric applications. I have also managed large programs and engagements with on-shore and off-shore resources.

Currently, I am leading the overall solution architecture, delivery and “go-to-market” strategy of a mobile-centric search solution that uses semantic search, Web 2.0, LAMP, Business Intelligence, high-end data mining, data retrieval and pattern recognition for a start-up venture.

I am very entrepreneurial at heart, a passion for learning new things, driven to succeed, willing to take risks, not afraid to fail, and a real asset when there are changes to be made.

Regarded as the “go-to-person” by fellow senior management for advice on the business implications of emerging technologies and alternative IT organizational structures, with a proven track record for directing high-performance teams through critical startup and restructuring, I am also told that my principal strengths are: superb at making connections between ideas that normally don’t seem to exist and making things simple.

Prithwi Thakuria’s Experience

  • CEO, Director Product & Strategy

    HAM-IT Inc.

    (Internet industry)

    December 2008 — Present (6 months)

    HAM-IT is a Boston-based company targeting the $15.4b and growing “Paid Local Search” advertisement market and the planning needs of approximately 1.9b global mobile and internet user base with a new approach.

    The company provides an innovative service using patent pending technologies, ubiquitously accessible via the worldwide web and mobile devices, with three core components:
    (a) single stop shop to globally connect and match consumers with local providers of “day-to-day” consumer services with capability to collaborate
    (b) deliver and schedule highly targeted leads to providers real time to their mobile and web interfaces for selection and approval
    (c) allow providers and consumers alike to publish web ready content from mobile as well as web interfaces.

    Our vision is to eventually extend the solution, where anyone anywhere can broadcast themselves to the world about their wants, interests, opinions and offerings and the world responds appropriately.
