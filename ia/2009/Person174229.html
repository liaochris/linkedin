Nick Marks’s Summary

I started out working in the print industry working for Kinko's in 1998. Starting out as a Machine Operator. After one year was promoted to Manager. Three years after that I was promoted to Senior Customer Consultant. The main responsibilities of this position were to provide consultation to customers with large and or complex orders, inside and some outside sales and project bidding.

Now, almost ten years later, I work for the American Screen Art in Knoxville, Tennessee, the largest printing company in a two hundred mile radius. Along the way working in the sign industry for more than four years on projects for some of the top Fortune 500 Companies.

In 2007 I performed a business experiment to determine how much money a person could make with zero investment. After about a month I was able to obtain everything necessary to run a small sign business. With the help of friends, colleagues, acquaintances and others I secure, equipment, materials and clients. After about nine months I sold all the equipment and materials. In those nine months I made over $5,000 with only time invested. This amount includes the money gained my selling all of the equipment and materials that I obtained for free. I consider this experiment a success and plan on try another experiment in the near future.
