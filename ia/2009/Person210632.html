Sav Banerjee’s Summary

I am an advertising and digital media marketing professional in New York. I co-founded Vercai Inc with Adam Leining in 2005. I have significant experience in creating marketing strategies for digital platforms, focusing primarily on social networks. My passion is the world wide web & social media/web 2.0 widgets, and I constantly work on improving user interaction with brands (right now - exclusively fashion retail) over the web. I use valuable customer insight research and follow in-depth analysis of emerging social & cultural trends. I also enjoy my client facing role, effectively communicating the client's needs to the internal creative and production teams - and following up on projects from concept to execution phases. Currently, I'm building a unique platform connecting social network audience to fashion & lifestyle brands on the internet by developing interactive and experiential campaigns.

My other interests include user interface design to enhance user experience on interactive modules and account planning & strategy development for traditional brand campaigns - forming an effective integrated campaign. I also do a lot of number crunching and reporting modules to measure effectiveness of campaigns.

I also recently completed a graduate program in database and digital marketing.
