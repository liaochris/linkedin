Leila Forsythe’s Summary

In my position at Covario, Inc. I was a Search Marketing Specialist on the Intel account, providing support in various capacities. I was responsible for data entry, database maintenance, note-taking, regular performance reports for numerous geographical regions, and overall account performance reporting on a weekly basis. In this position I became familiar with the Google AdWords platform. I also performed administrative duties for the account; keeping detailed meeting minutes and project notes.

As a Special Events Coordinator for the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, I was responsible for planning and execution for numerous fundraising events as well as all administrative duties. I helped to maintain an office with millions of dollars in revenue, and provided support to execute more than a dozen events each year, ranging from grassroots recreational events to prestigious black-tie dinners and corporate golf tournaments with local sports celebrities. I assisted with corporate sponsorships, individual donors, grant requirements, and the board of directors.

I have a strong and diverse education background in Communications, with my Bachelor of Arts degree earned at Chapman University in 2005, with honors. While an undergraduate, I held office in a number of extra-curricular activities and community events, and worked in several customer service positions.

I am proficient in Microsoft Office programs, having used Word, Excel, Publisher and PowerPoint on a regular basis for creating event invitations, programs and presentations, and marketing and sponsorship collateral. I have used industry-specific software in each position I have held, including Google AdWords and the fundraising software PledgeMaker and Maestro.

I am an extremely hardworking and enthusiastic individual, and I approach opportunities in my life with an optimistic attitude. I have particular desire to work in the sports industry and/or the non-profit sector.
