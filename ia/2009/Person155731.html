Matthew Corona’s Summary

Matthew is a Manager in Accenture’s Strategy practice in Chicago. His areas of expertise include corporate strategic planning, operating model design, portfolio management, new product development, and financial modeling.

Prior to joining Accenture, Matthew spent five years at Hewitt Associates as an Operations Manager in benefits consulting and administration. While earning his MBA from Duke University, he founded a non-profit start-up in partnership with the Emily Krzyzewski Family LIFE Center.
