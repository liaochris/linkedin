In September, 2008, Kevin Waller joined the Federal Highway Administration as the new Manager of the Administrative Service Team (AST) - Atlanta, a field office under Federal Highway Administration’s (FHWA) Headquarters. His 16 person team is responsible for the Human Resources, Procurement and Administrative services provided to twenty-nine FHWA Division and Federal Lands offices located across 28 states.

He is also the CEO of Ubiquity Enterprises, a Limited Liability Company established to promote and distribute cutting edge, all-natural Health and Wellness products throughout domestic and international markets. The principles guiding Ubiquity Enterprises, LLC are leadership and vision. Inseparably intertwined within the foundation of their diverse markets, venues & endeavors are leadership development & the expansion of individual and collective vision. "Leader of one, leader of many, if you can't lead one, you can't lead any." Nobody can expand their own vision. Ubiquity's success is dependent upon helping others to achieve their objectives. To that end, prosperity and success are both team efforts

Prior to his selection for his position with the FHWA, Mr. Waller’s career included 21 years as a U.S. Army Finance Corps Officer, where he retired at the rank of Lieutenant Colonel, and 3 years at the U.S. Army Forces Command Headquarters in Atlanta (Ft. McPherson), where he served as the Chief of Managerial Accounting.
