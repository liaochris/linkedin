Homy Maerefat, Ph.D., P.E.’s Summary

Chemical Engineer with in-depth experience in thermodynamics, process simulation, and separation processes, including distillation and liquid-liquid extraction in chemical, petrochemical, and petroleum processes. Expertise in development of highly tuned thermodynamic models to be utilized in ASPEN PLUS, PRO/II and HYSYS process simulators using experimental data. Recognized as in-house technical expert for consultation and resolution of all process related technical data issues impacting a process such as component freeze-out, hydrate formation, trace component distribution and many more problems facing process engineers on daily basis.

Development and tuning of thermodynamic models
Process simulation(Aspen Plus, Hysys, Pro/II)
