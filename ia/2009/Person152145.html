Mark Langford’s Summary

Online Digital Supply Chain expert focused on New Media delivery such as VoD, Interactive TV, Electronic Sell-Through, network based Disc Initiated Services (TM) and Electronic Content Delivery. Helping to develop the content delivery transition from physical media delivery to electronic delivery of film & TV, music, games, and software. Experience and expertise in designing and developing product and service lines, marketing these lines and developing new business. Huge network of international contacts within the electronic delivery space, including Hollywood Studios, IPTV providers, MSO's, CDN's and Service Providers.

Mark Langford’s Specialties:

Strategic marketing, service design, go-to-market planning, marketing communications, new product launches, full P&L and all aspects of classic Marketing for digital supply chain (streaming, downloading and Blu-ray), as well as IPTV, iTV, DTV, DRM, VoD, STB, streaming media, broadband entertainment, content acquisition & mgmt, software development. Delivery: broadband, broadcast, wireless and wireline telecoms, MSO's, and terrestrial networks.
