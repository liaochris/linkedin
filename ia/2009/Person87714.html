Steve Curtis’s Summary

With his strong background in sales and partner management Steve is responsible for identifying and developing strategic partnership and affiliate relationships that generate new business and increased brand exposure for Global and its operating subsidiaries, One World Yellow Pages™ and The Export Yellow Pages™.

Steve actively manages the marketing and affiliate partnership programs for the company's operating units, as well as develops integrated marketing partnerships with new and existing clients such as government agencies and trade associations, as well as industry partners.

Steve brings with him over 15 years of sales and business development experience to Global Publishers, focusing on the telecommunications and technology industries. Steve joined Global in 2004 and has played several roles during his tenure with the company. Most recently, he held the position of Director of Sales. Steve has played an integral role in the development of strategic relationships with the U.S. Department of Commerce and the U.S. Export-Import Bank.

Prior to joining Global, Steve was part of Genuity where he was responsible for wholesale access sales of Genuity's 20,000 mile Tier One fiber optic network, known as AS-1, to ISPs and government agencies. Among his notable achievements was the successful acquisition of a multi-year, multi-million dollar contract with the State of Illinois (the Illinois Century Network). Steve has successfully completed many such complex long-cycle negotiations in his career.

Steve Curtis’s Specialties:

Negotiation of strategic partnerships in the private and government sectors.
