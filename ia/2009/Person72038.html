Ellen Lytle, M.A., M.Des.’s Summary

I am the administrator of a new think tank and institute for collaboration in Atlanta. I do strategic planning, fundraising, programming, events, budgeting, and communications. The Medici Center will offer continuing education courses, panel discussions, conferences, and cultural events.

I am the best-selling author of "Click, The Best & Brightest in Computer-generated Art & Design", a former editor for Northlight Books, and a reviewer for John Wiley & Sons.
