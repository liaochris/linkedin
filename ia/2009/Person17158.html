Anne Perret’s Summary

Anne is a pychologist and marketing communications professional who specialises in helping organisations through competitive tendering projects. Her clients include organisations ranging from multinational IT and pharmaceuticals companies to construction consultants and marketing organisations.

She has helped her clients in bids worth from £2k to €3bn. Anne is a director of Calyx Communications Limited, and has won business in the UK and across Europe from Aberdeen to Warsaw and Helsinki to Madrid.

Calyx uses a blend of marketing, writing, psychology and NLP (neuro linguistic programming) to provide a highly-tailored and personal service to its clients.

Anne Perret’s Specialties:

Calyx helps companies and organisations win profitable business through competitive tenders:
Training
Live consultancy, where we help bid teams to create a bid strategy and turn it into persuasive proposals, tenders, presentations and key meetings
Mentoring of individuals and small teams so that they learn on the job
Preparation for key events such as Yellowpad sessions
Writing parts of the document for our clients - for example the executive summary.
