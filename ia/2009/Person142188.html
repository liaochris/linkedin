Lisa Morgan, CeM, J.D.’s Summary

I provide management and marketing consulting services to leading and emerging corporations, vendors, service providers, agencies, event producers and industry associations. Over the past 19 years I have worked with numerous pre-venture, pre-IPO, private and public clients on short-term and long-term strategic projects occasionally serving as VP or SVP.

In addition to working with clients, I have contributed, often on a regular basis as a feature writer or columnist, to Software Development Times, Systems Management News, E-Commerce Business, The Washington Post, InternetWeek, Planet IT, Wireless Developer, Network World, InformationWeek, Internet World, and Mobility. I am the author of How to Expedite Your Career Through Publishing, (IntelliPress Publishing, 1999) and co-author of Implementing Internet Security (New Riders, 1995) and The Internet Security Handbook (IDG/Mecklermedia, 1995). Most recently, I was a key contributor and an editor of The E-Marketplace: Strategies for Succeeding in B2B E-Commerce published by McGraw-Hill.

Since 1994 I have also served as an industry speaker and/or awards judge at executive, literary, and high technology events or for associations in the U.S. and internationally. Past and present engagements include Online Market World, the Professional eBay Sellers Alliance Spring Summit, Networld+Interop, Internet World, The Waterside Conference, Internet Alliance, Ishikawa Alliance, and the Software Information Industry Association Codie Awards and the Business.com "What Works for Business" awards. I was also recently asked to judge Stanford University's New Venture Startup course student projects.

My 14-year consulting practice has merged with Strategic Rainmakers, LLC. The expanded focus reflects relevant changes in our industry.
