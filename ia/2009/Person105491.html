Jason Sutherland’s Summary

I'm a good creative thinker who enjoys connecting the dots and distilling complex concepts and ideas into simple expressions. Not coincidentally, this works out quite nicely for marketing. Here's the caveat: I want to use these powers for good. Which means I'm interested in working with cool companies that live what they do, and do it in a way that's respectful to the world.

Jason Sutherland’s Specialties:

Developing great creative solutions, building believable brands and expressing complex strategies in the fewest and most connective words possible. Plus, I can fix your bike so it'll ride better than it ever has.
