Wendy Yurick Wing’s Summary

Wing Group, LLC is an award-winning documentation services company based in Nashua, NH.

We provide turn-key, in-house documentation services that include technical writing, editing, and illustrating.

We also provide contract technical writers, editors, illustrators, and other documentation professionals for short-term and long-term projects for both the commercial and military industries.

For more information about our services, visit us at www.wing-group.com or
call us at 603-791-4363.
