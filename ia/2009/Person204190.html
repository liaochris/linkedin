Rosslyn Tebbutt’s Summary

ABOUT ME

Passion & design is what drives me and learning new things every single day keeps me alive. I grew up painting every spare moment and reading any other moment. I have extensive experience in the interior design field as well as various other creative outlets such as digital photography, magazine journalism, painting, website design & web development.

Following my studies at Cape Peninsula University of Technology, I worked in my home town, Cape Town, at a big architectural firm in the custom made furniture department, exploring furniture & retail, high end clients and good taste. My design curiosity led me to London and subsequently I’ve worked as an interior designer in London. I’ve travelled extensively in my time in Great Britain and have had amazing experiences including trekking mountain gorillas in the DRC Congo, sleeping in the Serengeti, skiing in Poland and jumping out of planes.
On my return home I studied Digital Photography and Magazine Journalism looking for a new venture.

Collectively, all my experiences in my past, however brief, has given me the tools to create SBO and my heart feels content in knowing this is the future for the interior design industry as we know it today.

ABOUT SAMPLE BOARD ONLINE

Sample Board Online™, SBO for short, is a free online service designed to create sample boards quickly and effectively, and for suppliers who would like to see their products incorporated in the real-time world of design. SBO is a new generation networking tool for the creative industries.
SBO is a platform for quick and easy pulling together of design trends, colour schemes, textures and products either in our library or from images uploaded onto our system under your own personal profile.

Rosslyn Tebbutt’s Specialties:

Global marketing opportunity for interior design suppliers to showcase products directly to the design market.

Sample Board Online offers an unprecedented level of direct engagement with real products and brands, while its social features make it a powerful platform for the creation and distribution of user-generated advertising.
