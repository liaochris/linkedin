Adrian Bravo’s Summary

Adrian Bravo CEO, Ogilvy Interactive, He is Mexican and joined OgilvyOne Mexico as a Senior Partner in April of 2004. Prior to joining Ogilvy, Adrian was senior manager at Oracle Corporation, Epiphany Inc and Microsoft where he specialized in technology and business consulting for different operations in Latin America, heading up top financial and telecom accounts such as Banamex/Citibank, Scotiabank, HSBC, American Express, Telmex, and others. Adrian holds a degree in Computer Science, an MBA from one of the top business schools in Latin America and completed a master degree in Enterprise Systems from the Ecole Nationale Superieure des Telecommunicatons in Rennes, France. As of today Adrian runs all the Digital work including Dataservices for most of the group clients, he is also involved in CRM and communications planning within OgilvyOne. In his free time Adrian likes to practice Motocross and Tennis.
