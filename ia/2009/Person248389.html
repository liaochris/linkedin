Onn Chee Wong’s Summary

Onn Chee is currently working as the Chief Technology Officer in Resolvo Systems, a
leading information leakage expert in Asia. He has led numerous large-scale projects,
primarily in the government and defence sectors. His areas of expertise include
information leakage protection, web security and security strategy.

Onn Chee is a founding member and the first Vice-President of the Information Systems Security Association (ISSA), Singapore Chapter (http://www.issa.org.sg), the largest international, not-for-profit association for security professionals. He was also a former member of the Center of Internet Security (US) (http://www.cisecurity.org) which provides well-recognised security benchmarks for various systems which are commonly used by US Federal Government and private organisations.
Onn Chee is also the current Singapore chapter lead of Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) (http://www.owasp.org/) which publishes the widely respected OWASP Top 10 web vulnerabilities.
Onn Chee also organises the Security Meetup Group in Singapore and has been frequently invited to speak in information security conferences here in Singapore. Onn Chee also contributes to articles to ISSA Journal which is circulated globally.

Other than being a information security professional, Onn Chee is also trained in BS 7799/ISO 17799, ISO 9000 and ITIL. He is also a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) and certified PRINCE2 Practitioner.

In 2007, Onn Chee was appointed as the President of International Association of Software Architect (IASA), Singapore Chapter.
