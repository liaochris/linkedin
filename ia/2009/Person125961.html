Kapil Nagpal’s Summary

Nagarro provides outsourced software development services to companies ranging from early stage startups to Global 50 market leaders, helping them achieve their strategic goals. For ISVs, Nagarro offers full product lifecycle services, from architecture, design, development and integration to implementation and support. Nagarro’s offshore programming services combine specialized technical skills with Projistics™, a proprietary project management tool, to ensure quality, security and control in distributed development environments. Our technical teams in the US, Europe and India have significant experience in both open source (including Java and LAMP development) and proprietary (including Microsoft .NET and SAP NetWeaver) platforms.

Nagarro also provides onsite, hybrid and offshore software development services for a variety of industries. As a Microsoft Gold Certified Partner for Custom Development Solutions, and SOA and Business Process, our qualified .NET development engineers develop specialized applications that often integrate with enterprise systems to meet our clients’ business requirements. As an SAP NetWeaver partner, our SAP consultants develop flexible collaborative applications to meet the unique business needs of our clients worldwide. Strong domain expertise, combined with more than a decade of software development experience, has helped Nagarro to become a strategic development partner for our clients. Nagarro develops custom software solutions for R&D, operations, sales, marketing and finance that increase collaboration and efficiency and provide improved ROI
