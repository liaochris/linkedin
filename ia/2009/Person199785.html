Ron Nickelson CPC/CTS,CERS’s Summary

Helping build first-rate management teams is a key commitment GRN Houston makes to the companies we work with. We’ve learned that finding great people, more than any other factor, determines the degree of success a business ultimately achieves. Having the right person in the right job is especially important in early stage ventures, where missteps can have especially harmful consequences and hiring successes can jump-start progress.

GRN Houston take an active role in helping portfolio companies assess and fill their senior-level staffing needs. Our partners’ extensive experience -- both in managing business operations and in helping startup companies make the transition from initial concept to full-fledged operations – gives them hands-on expertise in this critical task.

Global Recruiters of Houston offers client companies and candidates integrity, personal attention, confidentiality and professional standards. We attract and present qualified and interested candidates in record time because of our unique team approach. We welcome the opportunity to present to you the most highly qualified talent in the industries we serve.

Our mission: to be the premier leader in the placement world. We will achieve this goal by providing:

•Continuous quality training
•Outstanding field support
•Advanced technology
•The highest standards of integrity and ethics in our practices at all times

Ron Nickelson CPC/CTS,CERS’s Honors:

Executive search firm Addison Professional Search, has appointed Ron L Nickelson Jr. global leader of its natural resources and energy practice. Mr. Nickelson, based in Houston, will oversee the practice, focused on a broad spectrum within the energy sector including E & P, oilfield service and merchant energy, refining, marketing companies and utility companies. He has successfully assisted leading global energy corporations both public and private.

Before joining Addison Professional Search, Mr. Nickelson was an international executive search firm, where he led the Upstream Americas energy practice.

He received a BBA in marketing from Texas Christian University (TCU). He is an active member of the Independent Petroleum Association (IPAA), Houston Producers Forum, Geophysical Society of Houston (GHS), Houston Geological Society (HGS), Houston Association of Professional Landmen (HAPL) and American Association of Professional Landmen (AAPL) organizations.
