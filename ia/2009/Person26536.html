Benoit Ams’s Summary

Benoit Ams is partner of New Step International Limited, a boutique consulting firm based in Hong-Kong (SAR) since 2002. New Step International helps both European and Chinese customers with qualitative and quantitative market research, market entry strategy, partner selection, merger and acquisition deals, commercial negotiation, marketing and branding strategy in Chinese characters and company set-up.
