Roger Miller (rmiller431@aol.com)’s Summary

Roger is an executive general manager with a proven record of increasing value of organizations by balancing growth and efficiency. He has a unique blend of consulting and operating experience to help him identify the underlying causes of substandard performance. He has a process to discover the remedy, plan the solution and motivate individuals and organizations to support the action plan; leading to smooth execution. Roger inspires organizations to achieve success and sustain peak performance.

The organizations Roger has positively impacted cross the spectrum and includes consumer products and industrial products. His broad experience helped him develop outstanding leadership skills which are the centerpiece of his process and his success. He is a hands-on manager with powerful skills in coaching, mentoring and influencing other executives to guarantee his organization’s success.

Roger has Board of Director experience in the UK and Asia and he has operating experience in Europe, the Middle East and Asia. Roger and his family are globally mobile.
