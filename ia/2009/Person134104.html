Alex Kovalenko’s Summary

Kovasys Inc. is an IT headhunting technology firm. Basically it is our job to find top candidates for our clients, and great jobs for our candidates.

CANDIDATES:
Are you tired of your current job? The commute is too long? Feel under appreciated? Give us a call or email for a confidential assessment of your salary as well as list of job opportunities we are working on. We are always on look out for .NET C# VB.NET, C++, Java / J2EE / J2SE / J2ME, PHP, VoIP, Powerbuilder, GIS, Project Manager, Network / Systems Administrators, Business Analyst, Actionscript, Flex in Montreal, Toronto, Ottawa, Calgary or Edmonton.

Most of our work comes from personal and direct relationships with hiring managers as well as suppliers arrangements with the federal and provincial government departments.

You can always see our jobs: http://jobs.kovasys.com

Are you looking for that HARD TO FIND SUPERSTAR? We have the largest pool of passive candidates - who are top players but unsatisfied with their employment. These are NOT the candidates you find on job boards. These are TOP NOTCH players ready to take your company to NEXT LEVEL.
