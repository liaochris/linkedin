Angela Lovett’s Summary

ThingsOrdinary Studios is proficient in all areas of graphic design, marketing, and website design. With a background in Fine Arts and Design and over 7 years of experience, Angela Lovett brings a unique vision to the company by combining creative vision with proven marketing concepts. Her designs convey her education in art history, painting, color theory and her ability to understand space and typography.

Angela Lovett’s Specialties:

ThingsOrdinary Studios specializes in all areas of graphic design, web design and layout of printed documents including publication layout, bookcover design, logo design, business card layout, brochure layout, annual reports and poster and flyer layout.
