John Brancaccio’s Summary

John Brancaccio is a Media/Internet Entrepreneur working to create meaningful companies that positively impact the world.

John is one of the founders of FindaDoc.com and the acting CEO. FindaDoc is a healthcare community for patients, doctors and dentists that rates over 1 million Doctors and Dentists on their Training, Expertise, and Patient Opinion. Findadoc empowers patients to make an informed choice in regards to their healthcare.

http://www.findadoc.com

John is also one of the founders of Lifefilm Productions, a media company that produces TV/Film quality personal documentaries for individuals and institutions.

http://www.lifefilm.com

John also owns Park Bench Media, a media company that creates original content for TV, Film and the Internet. John has created shows for MTV, ESPN, and Bravo.

http://www.parkbenchmedia.com
