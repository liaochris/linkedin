Martin Percival’s Summary

Director of Product management at Oracle after the BEA acquisition. Responsible for outbound middleware messaging to both Oracle and BEA customers at Oracle events across EMEA. Delivered internal training to Sales and Marketing staff on BEA technology and concepts.

Previously, Technical Evangelist for BEA across EMEA, responsible for delivery of key messages regarding product and strategy to analysts, press and BEA customers. Deep experience of the technical sales arena and enterprise customer support.

Interested in more of the same. My talents lie in explaining the reasons behind technology, (as well as the technical details themselves), to all levels of an organisation. Over time I am looking to move towards a CTO role probably via the CTO office of a company.
