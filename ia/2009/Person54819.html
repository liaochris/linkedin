Danny Shapiro’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur and a New Yorker, passionate about:

• building mission-driven for-profit and non-profit organizations

• improving city life with web and mobile technologies

• building community among people with shared values and interests, and connecting people to the physical places and people around them

• making local government more accessible and accountable, and increasing civic engagement to strengthen the public discourse

• making NYC an affordable 21st century city


BACKGROUND:

My unique background includes digital media deal-making at America Online, economic development in NYC government, community and coalition-building at Hillel, and marketing consulting at Infinia Group.

Across the public and private sectors, I've launched new ventures, led cross-functional teams, navigated complex bureaucracies and organizational politics, negotiated strategic partnerships, generated new business and developed new opportunities.

I received my BA in Economics from Harvard College, an MBA and MPA from Harvard Business School and the Harvard Kennedy School of Government, and completed the Coro Leadership New York Fellowship program.

I also serve on the Bronx High School of Science Alumni Association Board of Trustees and the Harvard Alumni Association Board of Directors, and am active in many civic and community organizations.

I'm interested in re-connecting with old friends, acquaintances and colleagues, and making new connections with people who share my goals and interests. Please send all inquires to shapiro@post.harvard.edu.
