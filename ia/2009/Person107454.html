Jeanne Berry, CPA’s Summary

My name is Jeanne Berry, and I am the founder of Let's Learn Bookkeeping. I have been in the accounting industry for over 30 years, and over the course of those years, I realized how difficult it was, and still is, for businesses to find qualified bookkeepers. This became an even bigger issue when accounting programs, like Quickbooks, was introduced to the world. Suddenly, many people falsely believed that since they have these programs that there was no need for a bookkeeper. I thought that if I could provide small businesses a way to have access to qualified bookkeepers that it could help these businesses stay up to date with their financials. But the bigger reason was a response to the current economic climate. Many people are out of work and in need of learning a new trade that can be profitable. Let's Learn Bookkeeping is the answer. We are an affordable alternative to trade schools and colleges that may be offering bookkeeping classes. The class is being taught by a Certified Public Accountant who has years of experience and knowledge. The class is being taught in both a live classroom setting and a web-based live setting. So come see what Let's Learn Bookkeeping can do for you!
