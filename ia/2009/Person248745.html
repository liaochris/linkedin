Stephen Harvey-Franklin’s Summary

Steve is an Internet Consultant and Business Owner.

Is committed to growing his customers' businesses and developing the business of his clients, using the internet.

Right now Social Media or Interactive Marketing is the big thing, We get results within hours, experts in Social Media have but a few months experience at the moment. It is an excellent way to talk with your customers, develop your services and get above your competitors.

Steve is looking for opportunities to act as an Internet Consultant, interim/part time/Non Exec Director with an internet/ e-commerce remit, where he can bring detailed and stratgeic expertise and resources to companies wanting to catch up, punch above their weight or get ahead online.

Steve has been in e-commerce since 1996, following an early career in Publishing and Management Consultancy.

Firstly as Managing Director of a number of leading online photo and media libraries. During this time he took direct responsibility for the website development and oversaw tremendous growth often in excess of 100% pa and was presented with the Queens Award for Export Achievement.
Secondly as managing Director of the UK's leading independent online seller of digital cameras, when Stephen left to set up WSI in Yorkshire, the company had grown by 70% during the year and was turning over £20m outselling Amazon, e-trader and Dabs, in fact outselling everyone except Dixons & Jessops.

We are now actively seeking to expand by offering Franchise Opportunities to anyone looking to take control of their career in a new growth sector.
