Mark Devlin’s Summary

After studying Production Engineering & Management at Strathclyde university in Scotland I moved to Japan in 1989 where I worked in a variety of positions before starting Crisscross with my partner Mary. Crisscross publishes Metropolis, Japan's No 1 English magazine, and Japan Today, the world's leading source of Japan news and information.

In September 2007 we sold our publishing businesses and moved to Sarasota, Florida. In November 2008 we opened Kroaky's Karaoke, a private karaoke room business.
