Chuck Job’s Summary

Chuck is an Information Architect/ User Experience Lead at Maxmedia Design. He has over 8 years experience in interactive media, information architecture, kiosk design and mobile and online experiences for clients such as Dell, UPS, Alcatel-Lucent, AT&T, CNN, and many others. Chuck has a passion for interface design and conducting research into methods for improving usability.

Before joining MaxMedia, Chuck served as Art Director for Moxie Interactive, where he led the initial redesign & architecture of the Verizon Wireless site. Prior to Moxie Interactive, he worked with weather.com as lead interactive designer.

Chuck is a member of the American Institute of Graphic Arts (AIGA) and the The Information Architecture Institute (IAI).

Chuck Job’s Specialties:

User Experience, Information Architecture, Information Design, Card Sorting, Focus Groups, Heuristics, Usability, Personas, Scenarios, Task Analysis, Wireframes, Feature Matrix, Use-cases, User Testing, Interface Design, Interaction Design, Prototyping, SEO, AJAX, Flash, HTML, OmniGraffle, CSS, Flex, Silverlight, Adobe Suite, Axure
