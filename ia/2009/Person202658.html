Rohan Deogaonkar’s Summary

I am a young professional looking to establish a career in the pharmaceutical or medical devices industry. My experience working in both the innovator and generic pharma sectors has provided me with a well-rounded view of the industry and helped develop educated opinions on hot topics of debate in the arena of healthcare.

Currently, I am working to secure regulatory approval in the US for several generic drugs on behalf of Cobalt Pharmaceuticals. My position and training in this field puts me at the nexus of product lifelines- dealing with drug development, intellectual property, clinical studies, and post-marketing considerations. In the future, I hope to utilise my skills & experience in the areas of predicting health outcomes, modelling, and health technology assessment.

Striving to stay abreast of the latest issues and developments in healthcare & life sciences, I always welcome the opportunity to have informed discussions and gain varied insights encompassing different perspectives.
