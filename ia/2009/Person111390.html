Jesse S. Hsu (徐顯光)’s Summary

Jesse serves global company clients in Asia as a bi-lingual Executive Coach and Consultant. He facilitates customized leadership programs for corporations such as Johnson & Johnson, L'Oreal, Michelin, Bayer, Mitsubishi Electric, Wall Street English, and Kimberly Clark... etc. Their growing demand to groom top talent in China offers Jesse a unique capacity to serve both expat and local managers.

With increasing loads and faster transitions, managers in Asia face high challenges with slowing capacities to enrich and to envision. Career aspiration can become flat and dry. Over time, some become overachieving with short-term vision and disengagement.

Jesse works closely with senior GM and HR executives to co-develop Talent Management strategies in sync with business initiatives. To stay ahead in succession planning, Jesse helps leaders to enrich their pipelines and enact OD changes. The immediate result is measurable by individual performance breakthroughs, and by retention statistics.

Executive coaching can yield great result when cultural understanding and personality difference is honored.

Jesse's Career Path:
Tech start-up experience during the 80's introduced him to Asia's rising economies. Kellogg MBA opened up his career path with global companies.

Procter & Gamble and McKinsey & Co. ignited his pursuit for leadership excellence and best management practices.

Focus On the Family allowed him to lead non-profit causes, family counseling, JV with political-savvy board-level partners.

Various consulting experiences took Jesse to China since 1995 with skills in board advisory and in conflict mediation.

Now Jesse serves with executives to lead smarter, to live fuller and to learn faster from life's changes. He helps leaders to stay at ease in accelerating those transitions, and enjoy the process.
