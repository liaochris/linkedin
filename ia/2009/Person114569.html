  • Controls Team

    Hybrid Electric Vehicle Team of Virginia Tech

    (Automotive industry)

    August 2006 — May 2007 (10 months)

    As part of my senior design project at Virginia Tech, I was a part of HEVT which is a team that competed in GM's Challenge X. The objective was to take a Chevrolet Equinox and turn it into an alternative energy vehicle. HEVT accomplished this by giving it a hybrid powertrain including a NiMH battery and flex fuel engine. As part of the team, I worked on the control strategy modelling in order to optimize it for fuel economy. I also created the Design Failure Modes Effects and Analysis (DFMEA) for the vehicle, and developed a way for the car to report breakdowns to the driver. The team overall took 3rd place in the national competition
