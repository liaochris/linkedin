Suzanne McQueen’s Summary

Besides owning businesses in the salon/spa field for 26 years, my experience in knowing women comes from a life journey of supporting and nurturing them on some level. From having to figure most things out on my own due to an unavailable mother as a kid (thank goodness for my sister), to a career entrepreneur, I have been a mentor, employer, educator, consultant, and confidante to women of all ages, be it family, friends, clients, associates, or community.

Mantra that helps me stay on task: "Knowing is not enough; we must apply. Willing is not enough; we must do."
Johann Wolfgang von Goethe

Suzanne McQueen’s Education

  • California State University-Fullerton

    , , 1975 — 1978

    I was a college drop-out after 2.5 years (Cal State Fullerton and SD State), because I was too antsy to be in school long enough to get a PHD in psychology, which is the only thing that I loved at the time. So went to hair school in Monterey while living in Big Sur to have something to "trade" while I traveled and while I figured out what I wanted to do when I grew up. Wouldn't you know I ended up loving it. It was artistic and personable, and I got to listen to great music all day long, Once I figured out I could trade haircuts for massage, well, you can imagine. That was enough to keep me entrenched for just about ever. And then I got hooked on the business of it all, as well as teaching and guest lecturing.
