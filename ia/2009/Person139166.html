Lee Goddard’s Summary

Internet-centred analysis, design and development of bespoke information systems, e-commerce, RDMS, and CMS. Interest in Kohonen networks, RDF, computer-composed music, cutting-edge server-side systems, photo processing and serving.

Began programming on a Vic-20 in the early '80s, then a BBC B, then an Enterprise. Dropped and restarted again in '96 with my first website and, in 1998, my first DIY WIKI for Sussex Students' Union.

Lee Goddard’s Specialties:

Analysis and design in a variety of modes and patterns, with extreme programming, spiral development, user- and process-centred design.

Not a believer in buzzwords, but rather in the birth of the trends they belatedly reflect.
