James Kung’s Summary

I am currently a developer / architect at Barnes & Noble.com, as part of the application development team for the nook. After graduating from Stanford University in 2005, I worked at Asurion Mobile Applications for 3+ years. At Asurion, our application, an open mobile address book for the Android platform (link here: http://www.getaddressbook.com), currently has over 10000 downloads and made New York Times' App of the Week in November 2009.

As a developer, I am at my best when I can understand the full picture of a project. I enjoy the challenge of integrating the separate components of a project into a single, powerful, user-friendly product, as well as harnessing the various talents of developers to work as one cohesive team. I am always open to opportunities in which I can put my development, communication, and leadership skills to their fullest extent.
