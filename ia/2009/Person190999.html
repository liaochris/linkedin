Linda Girard’s Summary

I am the Co-Founder and Visionary of Pure Visibility. Pure Visibility helps our clients navigate the possibilities in internet marketing, from online strategy to tactical implementation such as social media projects, paid search/pay per click marketing, search engine optimization, and usability/conversion rate optimization.

As PV's Visionary, I am futuristic, focused on ideas, and intent on growth. I play in the realm of possibilities for growing our clients businesses, and we employ a crack team of analysts to implement and expand upon the plans for success we hatch with our clients. I also have a strong urge to grow our own team and expertise at Pure Visibility, together with my business partner, Catherine Juon. I love that I'm always learning.

I am a self proclaimed " Internet geek". Prior to founding Pure Visibility, I ran a search engine optimization business and retained my clientele as the business changed and broadened. In 2008, I was recognized by the National Association of Women Business Owners as one of the "Top 10 Women in Business in Michigan". I am the former president of the Ann Arbor Ad Club, where I currently serve as education chair. In addition, I serve on the Ann Arbor Chamber Board of Directors, on Eastern Michigan University's CIS Advisory Board, and on Michigan Innovators' Board of Directors. I am also a judge for the annual international WebAward competition for web site development.

My goal: Simply, to improve the quality of life and abundance in Michigan and the world!
