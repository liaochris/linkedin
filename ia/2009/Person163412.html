Mickael Masset’s Summary

I started my professionnal career as an apprentice in new technologies of communications into a small company, I immediately saw
that commercial, marketing & communication aspects were the best way to do profitable business. Without these aspects, you cannot expect something better for your firm...

In 2003, after 2 years of university of technologies, specialized in communications & network services, I took the decision to do a business & management school, ESM-A. Why, in order to improve the negative points of my knowledge : marketing & management skills.
During these latest 3 years, I learned a lot about how to take responsabilites, deploy marketing plan, improve turnover by increasing communication aspect and improving customers loyalty by using marketing.

My first real job was at FCI, into the Strategic Business & Technology Development department, with extremly ambitious people . I was firstly in charge of the e-marketing & e-business development (exposition network, referencing strategy, online branding, website management : increasing by 2 the number of monthly visitors) during 1 year & a half.
During the latest year, I was in charge of the CRM at FCI and was, in brief, the initiator of this dissertation : "How to improve the CRM at FCI"...
It was a very impressive and exciting adventure, build around international and local people. FCI is a talented firm, built by talented people.

In March 2008, I decided to change my way, by joining SOPRA GROUP into the Business consulting departement, composed by senior consultants and junior consultants.
We focus on counselling and help our customers to increase their profitability by using information systems.

Now, I build my knowledge around 2 main skills :
- CRM, marketing, communication and projects management
- Application (Siebel...) conception, development and integration

Willing to expand my consulting skills at the international level, I will certainly earn an executive MBA degree...
