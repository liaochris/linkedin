Roy J. Tanner’s Summary

On public policy, Roy Tanner has served as part of the Taxpayer Budget Review Board and as a Governing Member for the National Federation of Independent Business. He also led as National Chair for American Citizen Alliance, a conservative non-partisan advocacy group. And in 2006, he ran as an independent candidate for the U.S. Senate (FL).

In the private sector, Mr. Tanner is a business analyst, a Gerson Lehrman Group Council Adviser, and the managing member of Alliance Professional Services, a business development agency that facilitates nationwide access to consulting, capital and capability for private enterprise. Mr. Tanner also founded ResourceLink Technology, an information technology professional services company which supported Fortune 500 firms through affiliate providers with core competencies in software engineering, information security, and process improvement.

For over 25-years, Mr. Tanner's career has been devoted to promoting services and solutions that foster greater productivity and cultures of excellence.

Roy J. Tanner’s Specialties:
