Chet Van Wert’s Summary

Consumer marketer with deep experience meeting ambitious growth goals by developing customer relationships and increasing their value. Combining the rigorous ROI discipline of a traditional direct-response marketer with the strategic and customer-centric focus required to establish new businesses and ignite growth in existing businesses. Expertise using and innovating subscription, membership, and relationship-based business models.

Chet Van Wert’s Specialties:

Customer acquisition, relationship marketing, online marketing, e-commerce, database marketing, loyalty programs, partnership marketing, competitive assessment and positioning, business planning and analysis.
