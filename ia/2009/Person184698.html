Peggy Moulton-Abbott’s Summary

I delight in using creative observational techniques to uncover the true motivators of human behavior which allows me to guide clients to actionable marketing strategies.

I am currently seeking full time employment as a qualitative marketing research consultant, but in the short-term I am offering my services on a freelance basis through my consultancy, Newfound Insights, LLC.

I am a RIVA-trained moderator and an active member of marketing research trade associations including: Qualitative Marketing Research Association (QRCA), Marketing Research Association (MRA) and the American Marketing Association (AMA). I hold my Professional Researcher Certification (PRC) from the MRA at the expert level.

Peggy Moulton-Abbott’s Specialties:

Qualitative marketing research, moderating, backroom management techniques, effective qualitative data analysis methods, ethnography, online qualitative methods, creating exciting reports and presentations of findings, finding actionable insights clients can put into action immediately
