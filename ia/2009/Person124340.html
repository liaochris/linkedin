June Campbell’s Summary

I have been a self employed writer since 1996. Although my client list represents an array of industries, I gravitate towards technology companies. Many of my clients are from the US, although I have also written for companies in Malaysia, Thailand, Canada and South America.

Typical projects include writing business proposals or joint venture proposals, writing or editing web site content and writing sales letters, newsletters or articles. I am also experienced writing for Squidoo Lenses, HubPages and for ezinearticles.com.

In a recent training course offered through StomperNet (www.stompernet.com), I was shown how to write the script for a video sales letter. I have had a little
experience writing scripts since then and would also welcome opportunities in this area.

I have some experience with technical writing, although I am not trained as a technical writer and would not promote myself as such.

I also write and market info products online. My web site niches are weight management, metaphysical (meditation, energy medicine, etc.), scrapbooking, altered books and other paper crafts, and business related info products.
