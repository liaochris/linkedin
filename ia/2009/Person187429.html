Phil Wratten’s Summary

My Background:
Have been involved with the power industry in steam fired and hydro power stations and in high voltage transmission. Focus has been to provide unparalleled customer service. Have used persistence and tenacity to achieve positive results. Am a strong negotiator and have the ability to think logically. Broad based knowledge. Communicate easily with people from all walks of life.

Leadership and Management Style:
Loyalty has always been to my employer to achieve the desired results. Lead in an uncomplicated manner. Believe a smooth simple approach to management is the path to success and my approach is one of fairness and transparency. Do not expect others to do what I will not do myself, as ultimately the buck stops with me. Get the job done being assertive where and when required.

Career Highlight:
Believe my career highlight spanned 3 solid years of addressing endemic problems inherited from previous management of a large Hydro power system. During this time showed extreme diplomacy with two major companies. Had to overcome differences between the two organizations as well as address labour force issues for both companies. Further, assisted in reducing main plant outage times, by improving communications between operations and maintenance staff ensuring main plant was returned to service on time. As a result of proper management, the region was the most efficient and cost effective area of 4 similar areas in the Snowy scheme. From this success, was seconded to a Corporate Training position.

Why would you Hire Me?:
Diverse background and exposure in industry from the ground floor as a tradesman, to operations, to management, and onto corporate training has given me a very broad based knowledge. Couple this background with cultural diversity I can take any future employer's company to new output levels while at the same time reducing costs.

Phil Wratten’s Specialties:

Identifying costly inefficiencies - staff and plant, manpower/staff negotiations, labour force management, energy management, analysis
