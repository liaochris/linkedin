C. Wes Cross’s Summary

Career Goal is to pursue vested interests in Westinghouse Electric Company as a professional welding engineer and possible management opportunities.

Weld experience and weld certifications with the following processes in numerous positions with various metals: SAW, GMAW, SMAW, GTAW, FCAW, HFRW, EBW and LW.
Weld Shop supervision experience.
Technician lab experience with a variety of metallographic and mechanical destructive examination equipment.
Working knowledge of various commercial and military fabrication codes (relating to both materials and welding) such as: ASME, AWS, ASTM, API, SAE, MIL, ABS and NAVSEA shipbuilding and aerospace.
Working experience with a variety of metals: carbon steels, low alloy steels, stainless steels, cast irons, aluminum alloys, Inconel and titanium.
Knowledgeable with hands-on experience in a variety of welding, brazing and cutting processes, fixturing and heat treatment methods.
Experienced knowledge of Quality NDE methods of RT, UT, VT, MT and PT.
Provide both weld and material inspection services within a Quality Control environment (previously certified by the American Welding Society as a Certified Welding Inspector - CWI - certification # 98020551).
Experienced instructor on welding principles and symbols.
Practical and working knowledge of GD & T.
Proficient in 5S principles and lean manufacturing tools and metrics. Kaizen Team Leader, Participant and Trainer.
Proficient in Microsoft Office/Project and AutoCAD.
Kanban setup and implementation.
Team player and leader in production problem resolution.
Preparation and issue of standard work and shop routings.
Production robot programming and setup with Kuka, Motoman & Fanuc systems.
Able to resolve production problems relating to design and performance.
Numerous positions held within local American Welding Society chapters.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills - both personable and discerning.

C. Wes Cross’s Specialties:

Weld processes - both ferrous and non-ferrous materials joining, welding controls, distortion control, and metric data collection.
