Roger Atkins’s Summary

I enjoy the crazy journey that is life by being involved with interesting, challenging, and occasionally mad people and projects. Ranging from blue chip maunfacturers Audi, Mercedes, Volkswagen and Volvo - to working with Richard Branson at Virgin, Alan Gibbs and Neil Jenkins at Gibbs Technologies, and now Jamie Borwick and the team at Modec! I guess my main contribution to all of the above is that by sheer force of personality and enthusiasm I can reach parts other people cannot reach. I enjoy being passionate about my work and have delivered on big projects on time and on budget. I have a strong creative streak that has enabled me to put colour into what is often seemingly black and white.My main goal is to do what I do best everyday!
From Mr Obama down the world has finally started spending serious money on potentially the most serious issue humanity has ever faced. The global recession has ironically put the focus on green tech as the area for intelligent investment to create both solutions and jobs. Modec is currently finalising it's JV with Navistar International Corporation. This builds a vital bridge into the North American market for the product, and will deliver major benefits to both companies and also our customers.
