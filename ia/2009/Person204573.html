Saulo Rozendo’s Summary

For example, in the metropolitan area of Recife, a $4 billion urban development was redesigned with 30+ social and environmental programs, addressing fair trade, carbon offsets and professional education to 6,000+ families in local villages.

As a lead consultant for more than 50 projects, I could strengthen loyalty and recognition of my clients’ brand names while pushing their companies towards sustainability stewardship.
