Doug Davison’s Summary

Doug's desire to help other business owners led him to launch Luster Software, Inc. in 2002. He graduated from the University of Kentucky with a B.S. in Computer Science, specializing in Software Engineering, with minor in Mathematics (1998). Doug and his family have lived in Champaign since 2000 and are looking forward to re-locating to Louisville, KY to expand the business into that market for 2009. With strong connections in both communities, he has a desire to provide practical software solutions that improve the operations of local and regional businesses.
