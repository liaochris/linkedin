Alex Orzulak’s Summary

I am a adaptive hands-on professional experienced in animation, video editing, motion design, narrative projects for game design, and visual storytelling. Specialties: titles, displacement mapping, 3D camera space, fluid dynamics and VFX. As a render administrator for a major VFX studio, I ensured flawless dailies workflow for feature films including The Host, Blade Runner: Final Cut, Pirates of the Carribbean: At World’s End, The Last Mimzy, Battle of Red Cliff, and Iron Man.

I am seeking contract work, paid internships, and F/T benefited career opportunities. My passions include After Effects, creative team projects, audio and video post production.

My 2006 Animation degree from San Francisco State gave me the foundation for all aspects of visual creativity, from 16mm film to exposure to international animators.

I received FUSE certification for advanced media training at Bay Area Video Coalition, February 2009.

Alex Orzulak’s Specialties:

After Effects, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Creative Suite, Sound Forge, Audacity, visual storytelling, creative collaboration, team problem solving, narrative fiction, futurism. I am familiar with basic modeling and lighting techiniques in Maya, and fundamentals of Apple Color.
