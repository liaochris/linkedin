Rick Krementz’s Summary

I thrive in "interesting times", and love the challenges of solving client's technical problems. I have created value in many industries, from iron mining, refractive surgery, international trade, and every facet of jewelry.

Highly-motivated senior executive with extensive experience in product development, operations, technology, and international trade. Extremely quick learner and innovative manager who is never satisfied with “we've always done it this way.” A Harvard MBA with diverse experiences who develops compelling solutions for both short-term tactical advantage and long-term strategy, and builds the team to make it happen.

Rick Krementz’s Specialties:

Beyond-the-box ideas, striking deals, re-purposing and re-engineering technologies and companies, international opportunities, new markets and suppliers. The unconventional, yet implementable.
