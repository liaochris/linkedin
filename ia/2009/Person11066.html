Amber Koelzer’s Summary

I am a design purest first. I gained my love working with color, typography, texture, photography, and illustration in print design. Things evolve and change, now my paper is "on screen" and you can interact with it. It is my generation's medium and it is an exciting field to work in. I can never stop learning.

I work to make the user's experience on the web memorable.
A visit to a web space should be more than just an informative experience. Whether it is an website, a banner, or an email- It should leave an impression on your audience. You know that "WOW" feeling when you land on something new? You know the one you want to tell your friends about? That is my goal with each project. Interactivity that creates memories about YOUR brand. Memories stand out, we do not forget our favorites. :)
