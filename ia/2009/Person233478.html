Tim Carroll’s Summary

Tim Carroll is a communications professional with more than a decade’s experience in consumer and business-to-business marketing driven public relations. He has a unique ability to understand varying stakeholder perspectives and how to design strategic communications programs to meet each audience’s needs.

Currently serving as a public relations manager for Pure Brand Communications, Carroll is responsible for communications council and management of a number of account teams while also aiding the principles in setting the strategic direction of the firm. He has designed and managed both media and policy communications programs targeting stakeholders ranging from consumers and businesses to government officials and employees.

Carroll’s communication expertise in media relations, community relations, executive visibility and issues management has benefited a number of respected clients and brands such as, Commerce City Colorado, Home Depot, Wal-Mart, United Airlines, Staples, First Data, Western Union and Waterpik to name a few.

Ambitious, hard working and a team motivator, Carroll has consistently overcome adversity to reach individual and group goals. He is an active supporter and vocal participant in his community through a number of professional and civic memberships.

Tim Carroll’s Specialties:

Strategic communications, media relations, team management and coaching, marketing communications, executive visibility, community relations
