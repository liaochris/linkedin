Iggy Pintado’s Summary

Iggy Pintado is an accomplished and experienced business leader, executive, coach and mentor. His key competencies are:

• Consulting - Customer and Market Focus
• Networking and Teamwork
• Coaching and Mentoring
• Communication and Innovation
• Results and People Orientation
• Passion for Business Success

His key achievements during his 22 year corporate career are:
• Transformed Telstra Business online site from electronic catalogue to fully functional, 24 x 7 self-service and lead generating sales/support channel
• IBM Hundred Percent Club: 1988, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1997, 2003, 2004, 2005
• IBM received 20 Australian Marketing Institute Awards between 2002-2004
• IBM Australia ranked #1 IT Marketing Employer of the Year in 2004
• Certified Practicing Marketer Award, Australian Marketing Institute in 2004.

Over the last 22 years, he has held professional, management and executive positions in marketing, sales, channels, operations and online management at both IBM Australia (1986-2006) and Telstra.

He is a ‘super-connected’ networker and is in the Top 30 most connected business networkers in Australia on Linkedin with currently over 3800 direct connections and 13.7 million secondary/tertiary connections.

In 2008, he co-founded a business networking consulting, marketing, coaching and mentoring business called ConnectGen, where he is CEO and CNO (Chief Networking Officer).

He is passionate about passing on his knowledge, learnings and insights to large and small organisations alike. He is regularly invited to present at business conferences, tertiary institutions and leadership seminars.

In summary, Iggy is a passionate, motivated and experienced leader - and people-person - who has brought his wealth of experience and knowledge into the 21st century business online world. He is ready, willing and able to provide this acquired expertise to any organisation, group or audience.

Iggy Pintado’s Specialties:
