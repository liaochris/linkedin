    What is paysafecard?

    In brief, paysafecard provides cash-to-internet transaction services through a network of 230,000 physical retail locations http://www.paysafecard.com/paysafecard-uk/personal/where-can-you-use-paysafecard/ . We have excellent coverage of more than 20 European countries (Germany, UK, France, Spain, Austria, Greece, Netherlands, Belgium, Switzerland, Portugal Denmark, Ireland, Poland, Slovakia, Czech Republic, Slovenia, Luxembourg, Italy, Sweden, Cyprus and Romania, also Argentina in South America; Mexico and other Latin American countries will follow soon) and are extending our reach in Europe and South America daily. With our tight distribution network in these countries paysafecard is the clear prepaid leader in Europe and the only globally integrated prepaid operator.

    paysafecard is a prepaid card comparable to cards known in the mobile phone sector and has been successful on the European market since November 2000. With 330 million Europeans without credit cards, and many of those reluctant to use them online, we are told by our largest European merchants that we account for up to 35% of their payment volumes - and for some, we are the number 1 preferred payment method, over credit, debit and e-wallets.

    Especially Gaming is one of our key markets where we’re already working together with major brands such EA (new: "Battleforge"/"Battlefield Heroes"), Frogster (new: "Runes of Magic"), Acclaim, Aeria Games, Ankama, Bigpoint, Gala-Inc, Gameforge, K2 Network, NHN USA, NC Soft, Nexon and many more.
