Efrain Mendicuti’s Summary

A successful and expercienced Interactive Marketing Communications professional, I'm taking my experience and knowledge to the field of training and talent development by collaborating with different organizations as trainer, speaker and consultant.

I have 13+ years of experience in the Marketing Communications industry, working for top global companies where I have been responsible for strategic planning, creative development and implementation of integrated marketing communications campaigns through different disciplines such as advertising, relationship marketing, promotions and trade marketing but always with a very strong focus on interactive / digital marketing.

Besides writing and editing my blogs: www.delocotidiano.blogspot.com (in Spanish) and http://thedailyandthenotso.blogspot.com (its English version), I'm a guest blogger / writter at marketing blog www.mekate.com and www.lagente.com (from Shore consultants).

I'm also a collaborator/co-author of the book Age of Conversation 2: why don't they get it and I co-host of the on-line radio show Neurona Digital Radio that airs on quiteloudfm.com

As a speaker, independent trainer and consultant I've delivered workshops and key notes for different companies like The Walt Disney Co. México, Cohn & Wolfe México, Marcus Evans and Idea Visual and top Universities like Universidad Anahuac, Universidad La Salle and ITESM better known as Tec de Monterrey.
