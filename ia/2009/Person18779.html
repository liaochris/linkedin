Alice Liou’s Summary

DVM/PhD student at the University of California, Davis.

Licensed to practice in both California and Maryland with experience in small animal general wellness, spay/neuter surgery, avian/exotic medicine, laboratory animal medicine, and pathology.

Currently performing research on the regulation of satiety hormone secretion in response to dietary nutrients.

I am interested in further training in neuroendocrinology and 'gastro-endocrinology' as it is related to energy metabolism, food intake, and obesity. I would like to accomplish this either with a fellowship or post-doctoral position or with internship/residency training in either veterinary internal medicine or pathology with an emphasis on endocrinology.
