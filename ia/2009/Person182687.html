Paul Billinge’s Summary

Industry qualified, Technical Project Manager with a 5 year track record of complex project delivery in the asbestos services industry. And twenty years project management is the shopfitting and construction industry.
All areas of Project Management.

Attributes; Fresh ideas with the ability to translate them into the art of the practical, deliver and complete. Experienced both supplier and buyer sides of the building industry and felt the challenges at the customer end. The ability to listen. Innovative.
