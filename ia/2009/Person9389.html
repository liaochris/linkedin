Alison Daley’s Summary

I am an executive coach with 15 years board level experience, specialising in supporting senior executives and teams working on projects of major upheaval such as downsizing and restructuring programmes. I also provide executive outplacement/career transition programmes.

Most recently I worked with the turnaround team at private equity firm Terra Firma to deliver the global restructuring of the EMI Music business, talent assessment & performance improvement projects.
