Debra Drummond’s Summary

A former U.S. Marine, I'm an Associate Broker with RE/MAX on the trail in Downtown Pymouth, Michigan.

I specialize in First Time Homebuyers, Military Veterans & internet-savvy Buyers & Sellers.

Additionally, I'm an Independent Consultant for Rodan + Fields Dermatologists. Dr. Katie Rodan and Dr. Kathy Fields, the doctors who created Proactiv Solutions, have developed skin care products to combat aging & sun damage. I'm excited to represent them in the consumer sales arena.

Rodan + Fields Anti-Aging, Reverse, Sooth & Unblemish regimes are dermatology-based, clinically proven products backed by a 100% guarantee.
