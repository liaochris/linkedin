Sheila Vaughn’s Summary

As a marketing consultant, I have had the opportunity to work with many mediums. Starting my career in Radio years ago gave me a competitive edge in media sales without a doubt. I then ventured into Television, and last but not least had the opportunity to sell for a wonderful publication "Phoenix Home & Garden Magazine".

My experience also lead me into consulting clients on a free-lance basis, creating turn-key programs for them and increasing their bottom line.

Right now, I have thrown myself into the digital age of the 21st century! As Sales Manager with DCSi Media, very soon you will notice 42" LCD screens in your favorite convenience, grocery, workout facility, and fast food restaurants. Reaching the highly mobile, super consumer is what DCSi is all about. It is my job to marry the best advertisers, and potential consumer on these screens! Please email me if you are interested in finding out more of what DCSi is all about! We have successfully launched 50 screens throughout Phoenix Metro convenience stores reaching 1.4 million mobile consumers monthly!!! Look now ahead to the technologies that compliment this medium. Text messaging and interactive 3D technologies are just a start!
