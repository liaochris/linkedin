Chris Robinson’s Summary

Currently, I am in the role of Human Resources Generalist for the Sales & Support Teams at TESSCO. In this position, I am responsible for managing TESSCO's Sales and Support Teams in the areas of Training, Career Development, Recruiting as well as Performance Management.

Previously, I was in an Account Managentment position at TESSCO in the Government Market. In this role, I was responsible for working close with the FBI, FEMA, Secret Service, CIA and The White House. During the 9/11 attacks and 2002 Olympics, I helped in mainstreaming products to these agencies and brought into TESSCO of over $1 million.

Previous to TESSCO, I worked for Provident Bank of Maryland. I started in the Baltimore Corporate downtown office as a Customer Service Rep and was eventually promoted to Assistant Manager. I moved from the downtown office to the Perring Parkway location to assist with the launch of "In-Store Banking".
