  • Owner

    Moving Pictures Post

    (Media Production industry)

    September 1996 — Present (13 years 2 months)

    Production and Post Production services. Film, Video and HD.

    Ken has personally directed over 500 television commercials including a 25 spot campaign for the Pentagon and has produced or directed for film and television in 34 countries worldwide and 28 states.

    Post Production projects have included Network Television Specials, several very profitable Infomercial projects (over 1 Billion in sales), widely distributed entertainment DVD's, and highly successful promo DVD's for Antiques Television Workshop.

    Besides Editorial and Motion Graphics, Ken has also run units on major motion pictures, produced a series on the National Parks that aired on the Discovery Channel, and has worked on many documentaries which included interviewing Mother Terresa in Calcutta.

  • Producer & Manager/Technology & Creative

    Video-It Post

    (Media Production industry)

    1990 — 1995 (5 years)

    Produced several projects for clients including: designed a 60 monitor video wall for Showscan Entertainment, Celebrity Cruises on board entertainment, the Emmy Awards graphics package and many commercial projects.

    Managed the creative staff as well as researched and implemented production technologies. Designed and installed edit bays including the first Avid system at Columbia Tristar.

  • Producer, Director

    Western America Films Inc.

    (Media Production industry)
