  • Webmaster

    Microsoft

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; MSFT; Computer Software industry)

    1998 — 1999 (1 year)

    In the years 1997-1999 it was my job to keep Microsoft's Danish web pages up to date. That is, all the files you find at http://www.microsoft.dk and below, including graphics and everything else (the Danish pages counted for about 15.000 web pages of the worldwide total of about 500.000, the fourth-largest in the world - of course today these numbers are very different). I worked as a member of the Microsoft Nordic Web Team, but I worked alone on the Danish site. Running www.microsoft.dk has given me opportunity to learn a lot about many different tasks in a marketing department, and it was stimulating and fun. Through this job, I have also gained a lot of expertise in web technologies and managing and implementing projects on the web.

  • Show Team

    1995 — 1998 (3 years)

Microsoft ShowTeam turned out to be a great success, and other subsidiaries of Microsoft have been establishing ShowTeam activities based on the Danish model. The ShowTeam has completed product road shows on several occasions. Apart from road shows, uncountable product presentations and seminars have been held all around the country on just about every topic, product and technology.
