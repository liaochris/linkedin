Garima Dutt’s Summary

I have worked with Indiatimes Voice (TIL), ibnlive.com and NDTV as a new media journalist till recently. I am interested in mobile content, internet, writing and editing, theatre and acting and media production.

•I am basically a compelling performer in front of the camera and behind the microphone. Most of my work has involved audio and video anchoring and production. This include voice-overs, TV anchoring and internet audio-visual programming.
•However, I am also an efficient content manager and coordinator with a keen eye for processes and scheduling. I write and edit with competence, particularly articles on movies, lifestyle subjects and soft business stories, developing specific communication content and strategies.
•Developing viable concepts and ideating on roll-out plans has always stimulated me. I have contributed substantially to new strategies and ideas for New Media products and Internet-TV convergence shows since I have a fairly good understanding of New Media trends (IPTV, VAS - voice and SMS, Radio).
•My strengths lie in media production operations (script writing, voicing, sound and video editing) and packaging any kind of content (stories/articles, voice, broadband).
•My domain knowledge is specific to Lifestyle – current affairs, theatre, entertainment, fashion and films.
•My experience in theatre has led me to work with people of varied backgrounds to help them raise voice on various issues. Street theatre and campaigning was a medium to advocate women’s issues, AIDS, trafficking, literacy etc. and that has given me a fairly good knowledge of promoting these issues.
