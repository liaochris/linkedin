George Bischof’s Summary

I am an attorney in midtown in New York City. My firm helps individuals and families prepare wills, health-care proxies, and powers of attorney; we also assist with estates in probate or intestacy. Other work includes planning for same-sex couples, life insurance trusts, pet trusts, and special needs trusts.

George Bischof’s Specialties:

wills, trusts, and estates; probate and intestate administration; advising will and trust beneficiaries; advising trustees
