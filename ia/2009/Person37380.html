Carla Johnson’s Summary

Carla Johnson is a seasoned marketing, communications and business development professional who focuses on business-to-business projects for national and international clients. As a freelance copywriter, she's received numerous awards for her projects from organizations such as the Business Marketing Association (BMA), International Association of Business Communicators (IABC) and the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA).

Carla has provided writing, project management, media relations and strategic consulting to clients such as Hewlett Packard, Western Union, Kiewit Corporation, American Express and Sybase, to name just a few.

She is an active member of the Colorado Chapters of BMA, Society for Marketing Professional Services (SMPS) and the National Trust for Historic Preservation. In addition, Carla has served as a guest speaker for classes on writing at Colorado State University and Regis University as well as developing customized training programs for her clients.

Carla Johnson’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, brand awareness, corporate messaging, business development, employee communications, marketing communications, public relations and project management.
