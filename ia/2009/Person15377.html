Andy Rice’s Summary

Andy has a strong background in sales and marketing, product innovation and business development. Excellent client relationship building and the professional presentation of creative marketing solutions are the foundations of his business development credentials. He is a natural multi-tasker, a proven marketeer, and a dynamic team leader. Andy was the power behind SportBusiness Campus, Britsport, iGaming Business, Actionsport Source and a key part of the team that turned a loss making magazine into the international brand it is today.
