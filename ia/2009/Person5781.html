Altan Khendup’s Summary

I am a seasoned professional that is well versed in technology, business alignment, large-scale project management, IT strategy and IT turnarounds. I learn technology quickly to shape and deliver solid and proven solutions with innovative technologies that deliver optimum value. I relish interactions with various groups both business and technical to solve nagging, persistent, emergent or opportunity-based problems. I am driven to learn and master multiple technical, business, management, personnel and operational dimensions in a short period of time to build consensus, solution and team work in delivering a proven solution to a business imperative.

People who have met and worked with me describe me as not only extremely competent and experienced in almost all facets of technology and highly effective in overcoming challenges but as someone willing to help anyone with any challenges that they have, who goes beyond what is expected at any time, who places less value in monetary reward as opposed to promoting the greater good, and someone who helps others understand complex ideas.

Throughout my career I have had the opportunity to work with an abundance technologies in a variety of ways. From hands-on development all the way to architecture ranging from languages and scripting languages such as Java, C++, Python, PHP and Objective C for example, to databases such as Oracle, MySQL, DB2, Informix, Microsoft SQL Server. I frequently work with various operating systems from Windows, Unix platforms such as Solaris and AIX, to Linux distributions such as Redhat, Fedora and Ubuntu. Many more instances are evident in my professional experience.

I am interested in doing cutting-edge, visionary projects with companies that have passionate and creative folks that are tackling difficult problems.
