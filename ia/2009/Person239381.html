Tyrone Van Heerden’s Summary

Tyrone joined the CityVarsity Marketing Department in 2008 bringing with him broad experience in Public Relations and Strategic Communications.

Apart from handling the internal communications portfolio at one of the largest University’s in South Africa, Tyrone has worked on various projects for charity and community up- liftment organizations as well as freelancing for several prominent Cape Town PR agencies.

His expertise along with his passions includes, Strategic Communications, Brand and Reputation Management, Online PR/ Marketing, New and Social Media and Media Liaison.

Tyrone has a National Diploma and BTech Degree in Public Relations Management from the Cape Peninsula University of Technology.
