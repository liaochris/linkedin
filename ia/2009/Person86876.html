WHO DO YOU TURN TO?
Who do you turn to when you need a Resume and/or Career Coach? There are many bright and talented people offering a variety of services so I can only tell you why you would want to consider my services.

My approach is Caring, Personal and Strategic. The work I do is tailored for you and comes from 20 years in the Silicon Valley Job Market. My in-depth HR, Staffing and Recruiting experience allows me to help you bring out your very best both in your resume and during coaching sessions that prepare you for interviews and/or strategies for addressing other employment related issues.
Coaching services are available for both those not working as well as those who are working who want to be pro-active in their career strategies.

COST EFFECTIVE OUTPLACEMENT SOLUTIONS
No Employer wants to leave their laid off worker without any resources in this economy. As an alternative to more costly solutions, I have designed and developed Cost Effective Outplacement Solutions for the small to mid-size employer.
Our services are the handshake between termination and an accelerated job search, contact me for more information.
