Kristoffer Nelson’s Summary

In our current economy, recession spiraling downward towards greater challenges, it is more necessary than ever not to lose a long term vision and strategy, while adapting to the current market and environment. Krama Consulting & Development addresses both short term and long term needs.

We provide integral business consulting: incorporating holistic philosophies to nurture personal and economic success for all echelons of business. Current clients include organizations in the wellness and media industries, labor and non-profit organizations, and individual executives within these organizations.

Kristoffer Nelson’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, vision and mission refinement, leadership development, communication refinement, integral 360 degree assessment, executive coaching, system development, branding and identity, communications and marketing, and social media platforms.
