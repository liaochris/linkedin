Peter van den Berg’s Summary

As Business Development manager at HRsoft I am responsible for setting up and maintaining solid relationships with existing and new clients.
By working together we will identify fitting solutions to solve the challenges found in the field. To achieve this objective we will share knowledge. Combining field experience, technical expertise and product capabilities will allow us to determine a pragmatic, proven, easy to use solution, that can be quickly implemented.

Exploring strategic cooperation with business partners and value added resellers is within my work portfolio. The objective is to add shared value to delivered services and future concepts.
