Robert Puerzer’s Summary

2+ years business development and financial planning for The Children’s Place Retail Stores, Inc. a specialty retailer of children’s merchandise. Led and managed the Store Development Financial Planning & Analysis team; focusing on real estate strategies, financial analysis, performa volume and the contribution of individual stores within geographic regions.

Partnered with Corporate Finance and Legal to establish and rollout The Children’s Place/Disney Store non-merchandise (SOX compliant) procurement policy within a six month timeframe. Implemented departmental training programs; supporting the sourcing processes while identifying company-wide spend consolidation opportunities. Furnished clear contract thresholds, provided assistance with statements of work and oversaw the management of the RFx process.

7-years Leading major sourcing initiatives for Gap Inc., across $300M in annual spend for Store Development products and services. Categories included construction, floor fixtures, exterior signage, marketing and visual programs. Managed several strategic sourcing professionals, along with larger, multi-functional teams. Led communications with multiple executive stakeholders and business partners. Achieved $36M in recognized cost avoidance over a two year time period.

16-years Asset Management experience with Wells Fargo Bank Corporate Properties Group, delivering high value-added, strategic solutions to middle management and senior executives. Proven engagement with leadership and analytically-based solutions.

3-years Key Brand projects. Led cross-functional partnerships to identify actionable trends in Levi Strauss consumers, the category, the competitors and general marketing principals for the western United States. Managed Levi’s co-op advertising program. Member of the winter Olympics strategy team; opening and closing ceremonies.
