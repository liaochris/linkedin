Bart Ratliff’s Summary

My name is Bart Ratliff and I’ve been a sales and marketing professional for over 20 years. I realized that in order to build a successful business, it was critical to build strong relationships and to develop a solid contact sphere. This was something that I learned at a very young age from reading books by Napolean Hill, Dale Carnegie, Brian Tracy and Tom Hopkins.

I want to share with you a few statistics on why it’s so important to stay in touch with your contacts. Did you realize that for every month you don’t contact or communicate with your clients or contact sphere, you lose 10% of your influence. On average, a business loses 50% of its clients every five years; and the cost to replace them, six to seven times more expensive. Now here’s one that I’m very familiar with, as far as generating new business, 80% of all sales are made between the 5th and 12th contact.

I’ve always been told that the fortune is in the follow up, and I believe it. However, I have come to believe in one more principal: ‘The fortune is in the education.’ At SlightEdge24-7, I will not only provide you the most cutting edge marketing and communication tools, but I will give you the resources and continuing education that will help you understand how to utilize and implement these tools and strategies within your own business. My friend Brian Tracy says, “The greatest success principle in history is ‘Learn from the experts’, because you’ll never live long enough to learn it all yourself.”

With that in mind, I would like to invite you to become a member of Slight Edge 24-7 so I can enhance your knowledge of building relationships with my daily video blogs and weekly newsletters. I’ll also be conducting monthly video casts and webinars with some of the top leaders and trainers in business today. An additional bonus you’ll receive by becoming a member is seeing how I practice what I preach by utilizing all the tools and principals you’ll discover at BartRatliff.com.
