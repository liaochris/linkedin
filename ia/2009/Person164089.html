Mike Colesworthy’s Summary

With an inherent passion for quality and a strong work ethic, I believe in giving clients/employers options - not only to offer flexibility, but to express the range of styles and methods I'm capable of executing. My influences are heavily based in commercial art and mainstream media, and I try to exercise this appeal in all of my work.
