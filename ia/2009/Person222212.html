Stefanie M. Monge’s Summary

Business journalist with a background in public relations and advertising and a strong interest in social media. I work with entrepreneurs, innovators, start-ups and small businesses. I also cover green technologies. I created the entrepreneurship beat and the Small Business Makeover series for the World-Herald. I write primarily for the Money section. My goal is to help the many talented entrepreneurs in Omaha increase their visibility in the community and to promote entrepreneurship as a viable career option for people of all ages and backgrounds.
