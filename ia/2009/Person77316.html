Fabiane Swanston’s Summary

Born in Rio de Janeiro in 1977, Fabiane Swanston is passionate about printed and digital media. In 1998 she graduated in Media / Communications with an address in publishing at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro.

In 1999 she moved to Milan to undertake an M.A. in fashion photography & styling which she graduated with honors. In 2001 she won a photography award: “Visionary Beauty - The Image Of Today’s Women”, presented by Italian Glamour in association with Elena Rubinstein.

She then moved to London where she lived until 2007. There she worked for Loaded Fashion Magazine, as Junior Fashion Editor. In 2002 she started working for Spoon Magazine, as Fashion & Promotions Editor, working between their Paris and London offices.

Additionally, she also lectured fashion at Istituto Marangoni in London for over 3 years, sharing her passion with students from all over the world. From 2003 to 2007 she worked for renowned photographer Simon Emmett as fashion forecaster and image consultant.

Over the last 10 years she has had the opportunity to work with highly regarded professionals in her field throughout Rio de Janeiro, Milan, Paris and London.

She now lives in Sydney where she continuous to pursue her passion for the media and visual communication industry works as a consultant on her business FAB Creative Solutions.

Fabiane Swanston’s Specialties:

Logos, webdesign, branding and corporate identities resources, publishing concepts, promotional solutions, production, graphic design.
