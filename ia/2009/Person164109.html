Mike Crosby’s Summary

20 plus years of experience in the computer industry with a proven track record of building and developing successful businesses from entrepreneurial start-ups to Fortune 500 companies. Brings strong, trusted relationships with top partners and manufacturers industry wide.

Expertise spans a variety of functional areas including sales, marketing, business development and general business management. In depth experience and knowledge in consumer electronics as well as vertically positioned commercial solutions. Extensive knowledge and experience across multiple channels including distribution, retail, ecommerce, VAR, DMR, government and education.

Prior to launching Concilitec, held executive positions with leading technology and consumer product companies, most recently as Vice President of Sales and Marketing at 3dconnexion, a Logitech Company. Prior to 3dconnexion, held the position of Director Channel Sales, Americas at Logitech, Director Distribution Channel Sales, Americas for Fujitsu, as well as sales management positions at AST Research and Forecast Systems, Inc.

Now leading a professional team of consultants with executive level experience in sales, marketing and general management. Each of Concilitec's consultants are former consumer electronics and technology executives with a minimum of 20 years experience in their related fields.
