David Anderson’s Summary

For the past 25 plus years I have lead companies in a variety of industries to achieve superior results by delivering effective technology solutions to their business needs. I lead the creation and deployment of a suite of web–based solutions for an internet cruise company that became the industry standard. At Spirit Airlines we grew the web from a simple site to one that was able to handle every customer interaction and be the source of 70% of all sales. We also put in place an infrastructure that kept the airline running continuously thru 6 hurricanes. At Grand Circle we built a data-warehouse that now drives the analytic engines to support this very successful direct marketer. As COO of Synadyne we developed a full suite of web delivered HR management and services for 10,000 employees.
