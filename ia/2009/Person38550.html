Carolyn Tracy’s Summary

As Director of Interior Design and Customer Relations with a prominent mixed-use real estate developer in Chicago, IL., I created all of the standard specifications and upgrades for their current and future developments – both condominium and multi-family housing community. I put together a “Standard of Customer Relations Policy” and personally oversaw the intake, processing and final move-in of over 150 separate buyers.

I coordinated team communications between the architect, builder, finance department and real estate agents. I conducted daily site visits with weekly deliverables to the managing partner with reports on construction issues, upgrade sales figures, client move-in and overall status of the projects. I created and defined the Interior Design and Customer Relations Division. My sale of upgrades was unprecedented in the industry, the buildings and buyers both benefited from this insight by having an above average reputation on the real estate market.

As I became more involved in the conceptual phase of the projects, I worked closely with the team to create a target market strategy and then monitor and adjust as needed . I reviewed construction budgets and scheduling. I researched the competition and recommended changes to our strategy as opportunities arrived.

In the long run, I want to design self sustaninable green communities and villages but am hoping for a position as a project manager with a developer and mentor that I can grow with.

Most importantly, I am searching for an extended family. People who are excited about living each day to it's fullest with laughter and exciting new ideas.

I want to create and live in communities where people are open to new resources and ideas, where the environment is constantly changing with new ideas and better ways of living. I want the communities I design to be living organisms that constantly renew themselves.

Carolyn Tracy’s Specialties:

I am a process oriented visionary...an idea person...ability to think outside the box....ability to see whole projects from multiple aspects of architecture, space planning, interior design, real estate sales, marketing, landscaping, community interaction both spatially and psychologically.
