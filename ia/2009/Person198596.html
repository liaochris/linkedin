Rick Lance’s Summary

A working voice talent since about 1993. Entered field from working in Nashville's music business as a Singer/Songwriter/Musician. Gradual transition from local radio, TV and promotions to present day work.
My voice has been called AMERICANA.
Have voiced hundreds of radio, TV, documentaries, corporate narrations, live shows, industrials and other projects in North America and abroad.
My most popular character voice is Cowboy/Western which has been performed for many different types of TV, audio book and corporate narrations and broadcast series projects. Audio samples are available at www.RickLanceStudio.com/voice
I am also an On-camera talent and an accomplished commercial photographer.

I intend to continue to reach out worldwide to help corporations promote their products and services. And to continue to provide entertainment while sharpening my skills forever growing as a first rate talent.
