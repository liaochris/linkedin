J. Mike Smith’s Summary

Expertise, wisdom and a proven track record at the junction of people and performance enhancement.

Visit my blog site to learn more about services such as spot coaching, traditional exec coaching, and my work with teams and groups.

I am an executive and team coach with a consulting practice working with individuals, teams, and organizations. I bring to my work over 25 years of business experience and a work background that spans industries including financial and professional services, high tech, biotech, retail and wholesale distribution. While I know a lot of theory, I also know a lot through the school of experience and practice. I haven’t been just a “tourist” in the corporate workplace - I’ve lived there and been a citizen.

When I work with individuals and teams it’s often the about terrain and environment I know well since I’ve held jobs based from cubicles to the executive suite - all the while having coaching as a key component of my work. My consulting practice - Back West, Inc. - is focused in three areas - coaching with individuals, coaching work with teams such as team start-up and team building, and group facilitation and off-site design.

Consulting work has included organizations ranging from virtual start-ups such as Orexigen, Inc. and Calixa Theraupeutics, Inc. to mid-size companies such as Rigel Pharmaceuticals, Asia Alternatives, LLC and Cypress Semiconductor and Fortune 500 companies such as Genentech, Gilead Sciences, AT&T, and Visa. I’ve also been been fortunate to work with non-profit organizations such as the Robert Toigo Foundation and the Gordon and Betty Moore Foundation.

More information, including contact details, can be found at my consulting practice web site http://www.backwest.com. The name comes from growing up in the Pacific West, and living out in the Midwest: calling home “back west” seemed to be the logical orientation rather than the usual “out west.”
