Laura Guthrie’s Summary

I have been focused on marketing for Nicely Done Industries since 2008 and in the process have learned the ins and outs of government contracting from the standpoint of a Small, Woman-Owned Minority Business in Springfield, Virginia. I have mastered Google AdWords and can say that I have successfully helped bring in customers to our business during a time of economic hardships for many others.

My past experience has helped me because for five years I worked as a lifeguard on a Naval Warfare Base in Dahlgren, Virginia. The last two years there I was a Head Guard, which involved me taking a much more active role in the decision making, procedures, and management that came with working at an on-base facility. As a civilian working at a Naval Base, I learned a lot about the red tape associated with getting changes/contracts completed.

My goal is to promote the growth of small businesses in government contracting through the use of our program Gateway to Government. I am deeply invested in utilizing all the tools available to our company to ensure that small businesses throughout the country can take action on the opportunities the government provides.
