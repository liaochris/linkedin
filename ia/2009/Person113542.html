Jim Ziegler, CSP, HSG’s Summary

James A. Ziegler, CSP, HSG is a world-class professional speaker and president of three corporations. An internationally recognized expert in Sales, Marketing and Advertising. Jim is also considered one of the foremost authorities and experts in the retail automobile industry worldwide.


When James Ziegler speaks, no matter what industry, his Sales Management and Marketing speeches and seminars are in high-demand. Always among the most highly rated speakers at every conference, convention and event. Speaking fee, $10,000 plus first-class travel. His humor, stage presense, and intellectual dynamics deliver expertise to your audience in a complete package, creating a memorable and effective event. They'll be discussing and debating 'Ziegler' in the hallways long after he's delivered the speech. Jim makes meaningful changes happen.

Call our offices at (800) 726-0510 to inquire about booking or visit http://www.ZieglerDynamics.com

The most 'read' and 'quoted' columnist in the car business. Ziegler is the featured award-winning columnist with his controversial article, The Dealer Advocate, featured in DEALER Magazine. Jim Ziegler is recognized as the most accurate industry forecaster and futurist in the history of the business.

In high demand for keynote speeches, and seminar events in Sales and Management; Jim Ziegler is nationally recognized in his field as 'The Best in the Business.'

Considered an Internet marketing expert, Jim is often interviewed and quoted in national media such as Entrepreneur, USA Today, Detroit News, Automotive News, BusinessWeek, RV Executive, Automotive Executive, and Dealer magazines. He has appeared on numerous radio talk forums as wel as television appearances, CNN and Sixty-Minutes.

You want a high-powered keynote for your next business meeting, sales meeting or national convention...'high-content, humor and thought-provoking...Think Ziegler'.
