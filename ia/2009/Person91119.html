Gustavo Sonoda’s Summary

After completing my BA graduation on Advertising and Marketing in São Paulo (ESPM), having also acquired professional experience in the creative field, I moved to Italy, Milan in Sept.2003.

I started working as a Motion Designer, while attending the Art Direction course in Oct.2003. After 9 months I was promoted to Art Director, coordinating a team of 21 professionals, and being in charge of all communication planning and strategic client management. Amongst the jobs developed, there are Network Brand Identity, Promos, Main Titles, Set Design and Video Expositions for Studio Universal, MTV, Nickelodeon, Mediaset Group, Sky Sport and Sky Cinema.

Gustavo Sonoda’s Specialties:

Communication planning and Art Direction skills developed working with the top media companies in the European market and the constant research and development of new visual styles & treatment.
