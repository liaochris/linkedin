Charlotte Burke’s Summary

High energy executive with extensive experience in building and operating profitable technology businesses in the Internet, mobile and computer industries. She is recognized as a strategic thinker with solid financial acumen, technological vision and the marketing prowess to consistently deliver best in class performance. A trail blazer who led Bell Canada through three major technology transformations: the launch of digital cellular, wireless data services, and the roll out of high speed Internet, she has a proven track record building new businesses. A leader who attracts and develops high performance teams who consistently deliver solid financial results. Often sought after to speak at leadership and industry events on harnessing talent, technology and timing to build new businesses.
