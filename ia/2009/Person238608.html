Annika Ek’s Summary

Annika has intuitively allowed herself to be guided on a path that at times seemed to make little sense. She began her career with a degree in business and economics, and work in that field followed, but before long she got restless and pursued studies in computer technology. She was still not satisfied and kept looking for deeper meaning in her work and had a great desire to touch the lives of others in a way that truly mattered…

As the result of a health challenge more than ten years ago, she discovered the power of natural health. Annika took charge of her own health and life, and embarked on a path as a healer with diplomas in several modalities in natural health, including Herbalist, Nutritionist, Homeopath and Hand Analyst. By trusting her strong intuition and guidance, she has evolved her practice from “physical” health to the “soul spirit level”, connecting her clients with their divine Life Purpose.

To complete the expression of her own Life Purpose, Annika now combines soul spiritual and mindset healer ship with her experience in matching the purpose to a market, and shows her clients how they can become “Purpose Entrepreneurs”. Visit her website http://www.TrulyU.com and get free Purpose2Business Tools!
