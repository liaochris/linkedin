Garry Terhune’s Summary

Garry offers over 25 years of corporate training and coaching experience. He has loved every minute of it.

Garry’s vast consulting experience spans the insurance, pharmaceutical, manufacturing, advertising, educational, financial services, government, and telecommunications industries.

His areas of training expertise include presentation / communication skills, sales training, software training, train-the-trainer, live-online training, and executive coaching. He designs eLearning programs offering students self-paced instruction. Embedded as part of the eLearning experience he has produced and directed instructional videos. He trains people around the world via various web conferencing programs. Some of his software training expertise includes CRM, various financial management programs, FDA Document Authoring and Submission, Procurement, and Sarbanes – Oxley (SOX) financial compliance reporting.

Garry delivers a wide range of his personally designed Maximum Positive Impact® train-the-trainer / train-the-coach / train-the-workforce programs and workshops.

With his extensive sales training background, much of his time is spent training clients on sales-focused offerings.

Garry spent the early part of his career honing his skills by selling software to the financial industry. Calling on upper level management he used his persuasive skills to successfully convince them to try the new concept of transferring funds between their corporate customers and their institution via electronic communications.

***

Overall, Garry’s ability to communicate effectively and to explain sometimes complex material in a clear, concise, and easy to understand manner directly results in student success. Motivating and coaching his students in a firm, encouraging, and enthusiastic manner are among his major strengths.
