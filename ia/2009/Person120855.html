Joseph Sacco’s Summary

From my experiences, I have concluded that there's only three major things a business owner or President could ever want.

1. More Time.- away from the business
2. More Control - more predictable performance
3. More Profits - risk/reward payback

One want usually dominates the owner's desires. Which is most important to you now?

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

OUR TWO STEP PROCESS PROACTIVELY ADDRESSES PROBLEMS YOU ARE EXPERIENCING WITHIN YOUR COMPANY THAT ARE KEEPING YOU FROM ACHIEVING THE ABOVE

Step 1 - Our initial management analysis intensely studies your management systems and processes to determine what is wrong, why is it wrong, what is the operational and financial cost to the company, and what can be done to correct the problems.

Our management analysis also concludes with very specific proposals for improving your organizational structure, efficiency, and profits.

"Optional" Step 2 - We become actively engaged in "implementing" the corrective action process while teaching and training all personnel on the new procedures.. Additionally we become your "business guardian angels" with periodic follow-up to keep you on track to address any other "growing pains" you may experience as a result of your company's developmental process from infancy to maturity.

*Important note: It is imperative to understand that our objective here is not to simply enable the organization to "get by" or "just get better at what you do", but to perform within the top 5-10% of the industry you are in. Not only is this desirable to attain, but in todays business environment, it is crucial in order for you to meet profit objectives and survive.
