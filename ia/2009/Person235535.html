Tomas Malmsten’s Summary

Personal Profile
I am a developer and architect with over ten years experience from numerous technologies. I have a passion for well written test driven code that does pretty much what you would expect. This combined with an aim to create the simplest possible solution to satisfy the need of the customer creates maintainable code that is easy to modify.


I take pride in being a good communicator in both written and spoken language: whether it's the code I write or my communication with team members, management and customers.

Key Technologies
My main focus is Java and related technologies but I have also worked with Grovy, Javascript, Lotus Notes and Domino, Oracle Forms, PHP and Microsoft .Net.

I have worked with all tiers of enterprise applications, from RDBMS and other data stores through most major servers to front ends like JSP/Servlets, Portlets and Swing. On the server side I am mainly experienced in Tomcat, JBoss and IBM Websphere and I have mostly worked with Oracles and Microsfots RDBMSes.

Key Processes
I have worked in both Agile and waterfall teams with different success rates. In my current role I have had the opportunity to lead the development of a process that works well with the team and customers. The process lends most of its inspiration from Domain Driven Design and Scrum and all development is test driven.
