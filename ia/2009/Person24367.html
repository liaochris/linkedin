Barry Chandler’s Summary

I have more than 15 years experience in the Hospitality Management field, 10 of those at a management level. My goal is to create communities of like minded individuals online and to connect with those of similar goals. I moved to the US in 2007 after meeting an American girl! The rest is history.

How I Help Bar Owners & Bartenders:

I work with Bar Owners in Ireland, UK and USA.
I assist them in reducing their costs, and identifying new ways to make money in their business. I provide Bartenders access to online tools that allow them to train in How-To Manage or Open a Bar.


Feel free to contact me anytime at barry [at ] manageyourbar.com or by phone anytime on 614.940.4895.

Visit http://www.ManageYourBar.com for more information!

Barry Chandler’s Experience

  • Founder of Online Resource for Opening & Managing a Bar

    ManageyourBar.com

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2008 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    ManageYourBar.com is the online resource for the American Bar & Club Industry and ideal for anyone opening a bar or planning on opening a bar in the future. With hundreds of templates, forms and downloads instantly available, owners can create training manuals, policies and procedures while keeping up to date on best practice in the industry. Essential for any bar owner serious about managing their bar business effectively.

  • Co-Founder of UK Bartender's and Bar Owners Resource Website

    Barkeeper.co.uk

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2005 — Present (4 years 9 months)

    Barkeeper.co.uk was founded to assist UK bars and pubs streamline their businesses with helpful tools and articles to drive profits and reduce costs and to allow bartenders access to the tools to manage a bar in the future.

  • Co-Founder of the largest resource for Irish Bar Owners

    Barkeeper.ie

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2004 — Present (5 years 9 months)

    I co-founded Barkeeper.ie in 2004 as a result of demand from food, beverage and hospitality businesses in Ireland for an online resource where tools, downloads, articles and forms could be downloaded to assist in the effective management of the business. Barkeeper.ie is the ideal resource for opening or managing an Irish bar.

  • Co-Founder

    Stocktaking.ie

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    August 2004 — February 2008 (3 years 7 months)

    Stocktaking.ie is Ireland's only dedicated Food, Beverage & Retail Inventory Control company specialising in delivering on-site inventory level reports and profit analysis for bars, restaurants, hotels and retail stores.

  • Food & Beverage Controller

    Seabourn Cruise Line

    (Privately Held; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    June 2001 — June 2003 (2 years 1 month)

    I was responsible for the food, beverage and consumable item stores aboard three different cruise ships. My tasks were to forecast anticipated usage of stock, place all shoreside orders, receive stock aboard the ships and control the issuing of stock to the various onboard departments.

  • Asst. Provision Master

    Cunard Line

    (Public Company; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    December 1999 — June 2001 (1 year 7 months)

    I trained in all areas of Food & Beverage Control and purchasing, assisting in the purchase of stock and controlling the onboard management of food, beverage and consumable tems for a ship with 600 passengers and 400 crew.
