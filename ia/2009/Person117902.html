John Pisciotta’s Summary

John Pisciotta, a principal at Ripcord Entertainment, producer,
business owner, & author. Propelling entertainment content, copyright creation, brand ignition and joint venture partnerships forward. A creative catalyst, who has helped to produce, create, position, reposition or launch hundreds of artists, bands, songwriters, authors, and thinkers. John and his team create and launch the careers of others, or creat new products and launch them for his own companies. Recent song of the year, video of the year nomination, and America Music Award win.


Ripcord is a brand ignition creative catalyst company specializing in brand asset management. An integrated brand activation music and media entertainment agency, we utilize and combine all available communication channels (old and new) to reach and engage consumers while creating multiple layers of income for clients. We are unlike anything you've known, and we like that.

Ripcord's mission is to multiply and monetize talent, in production, music publishing, literary, and branded events. Ripcord specializes in creating the correct world for artists to thrive in. Ripcord is headquartered in Nashville TN.
