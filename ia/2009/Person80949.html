Francois Mazoudier’s Experience

  • CEO

    GoHello

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2006 — Present (3 years 7 months)

    GoHello is an ALLmobile telephony system for business. It provides all the features of a traditional, hardware-based phone system but does away with the expense, technical complexity and financial commitment of a hardware system in the office(s). It works with any mobile phone, requires under 15 minutes to set up and is sold as a monthly subscription per active user.

    Being 100% mobile, the system provides features and benefits not available on desk phone systems, and significantly increase office worker productivity. All calls are handled on mobile phones (virtual extensions, independent of the employee's actual location) and receptionists handle calls using a web based switchboard.


    Similarly to mobile voicemail or hosted email, the entire system runs in dedicated, carrier grade data centres, and is fully compatible with any mobile network.

    With its international, proven, user base of around 20,000 active, fee paying users, GoHello is challenging the industry convention that "telecoms hardware is better" by providing a better, easier and cheaper solution to businesses of all sizes.

    GoHello has been nominated and awarded by the world's most prestigious firms (incl. Techworld Awards, VON Innovator Award, Red Herring 100 Europe, Gartner’s Cool Vendor accreditation and the Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Award.) and is the leader in the new ALLmobile category. Established in Scandinavia in 2001, the company has offices in London, Copenhagen and Prague.
