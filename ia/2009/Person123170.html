Judith Lewis’s Summary

I am a Digital Specialist who has been working actively in the online environment since 1996. My expertise spans SEO, SMO, PPC and other digital channels. I have written chapters on: analytics and SEO, measuring social media and general SEO for various industry publications. I write a semi-regular column on search and social media, have written guest spots on various marketing websites and am an in-demand speaker at conferences around the world.

I have managed an SEO hub where I taught SEO/M and helped develop peoples skills and abilities. I have spoken at several Marketing and SEO/M conferences including A4U, SMX and SES.

After being first introduced to the internet over 20 years ago during a programming course, I went on to study social interactions via live online systems eventually moving in to the IT industry full time after relocating from Canada to the UK in 1996. I worked within SEO/M in e-commerce as well as publishing before entering my current job.
