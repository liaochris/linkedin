Sandi Bachom’s Summary

My new social network, NING community to share your memories of Manny's the greatest music store in the world...., "The Manny's Music Virtual Wall Project" can be seen at www.mannysmusic.ning.com

I have created, as cinematographer and editor, over 100 award winning short digital films and Electronic Press Kits for Grammy winners, Diana Ross, Patti Austin, Elaine Stritch, Steve Tyrell and Dr John and many many more.

My films have won The My Hero Short Film Festival, AOIF Film Festival, Two "Pods", Current TV viewer voted #1, recently the 'most viewed' in the Reel13 Short Film Competition on PBS and been screened at the Cannes Short Film Corner. My YouTube channel has received over 175,000 views! http://www.youtube.com/profile?user=Schlockumentaries&view=videos&sort=d

Tweeting @ SandiBachom
NYC Digital Activist, Fearless Filmmaker, Author 3 books & 78 Schlockumentaries, YouTube Queen, New Media Maven on Social Security

I've written 3 best selling books published by Hazelden selling 50,000 copies, "Denial Is No A River In Egypt", "The Wrath of Grapes", "Hell in the Hallway"

I've produced thousands of commercials at countless agencies from BBDO, to Mad Dogs & Englishmen, where I was "Queen of Production", with a ubiquitous freelance career in between. Spots I've produced have won Gold, Silver, Bronze Cannes Lions, One Show, Clios, The O'Toole Award, and that winged naked man thing. One softdrink spot is in Ad Age's 50 best commercials of all time. I even produced the History of the Television Commercial for the 4AAAA's
