Dr. Richard Hoyer’s Summary

Over 30 years of diverse extensive experience in adult education management, law enforcement, fire, hazardous materials emergency response, emergency medical and disaster management planning. Expertise in education administration, psychology professor, environmental, occupational safety and health and engineering professor. Educational curriculum design and development for five universities and postsecondary educational administration consultant. Forensic client behavioral health management; managed the human resources, cafeteria and kitchen operations, housekeeping and maintenance, 14 retail stores and a warehouse for an adult rehabilitation center for alcohol and substance abuse for the Federal Bureau of Prisons as their Director of Operations. An ability to solve concerns before they become problems. Proven ability to manage complex programs in multi-tasking environment.
