Rocky Kahn’s Summary

After filing twenty patents (many self-drafted) at HP Labs and small startups, I started TeamPatent to address the inefficiencies, miscommunications, and long delays prevalent when pursuing a patent.

TeamPatent will become the preferred tool to draft and submit patent applications. Thomson, Lexis, and a host of smaller IP firms will integrate their products into TeamPatent, providing in-context access to their content and services. The USPTO will license TeamPatent and extend it on government servers to receive application submissions, facilitate examination/office actions, and enhance prior art searches.

In other realms, TeamPatent will be adapted by third-party developers to other document types, creating a constellation of purpose-suited document editors.
