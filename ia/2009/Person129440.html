Kelly Konis’s Summary

With 20 years in the ad business, my independent Brand Strategy practice draws on my experience in marketing strategy, research, and media planning to build communication recommendations particulally for technology clients and those embracing more sustainable practices.

I believe marketing communications can have a positive impact by introducing innovative new ideas, and new products & services by inspiring people and connecting with their values. I run a "green" business and build recommendations with green solutions. Ask me how.

I am always open to a conversation about new ideas and new opportunities to do more through communications than "advertise". Let's talk about ways to connect more deeply with your customers, further differentiate your brand, and build a stronger competitive edge in the market.
