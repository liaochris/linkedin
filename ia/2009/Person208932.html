Sang Ahn’s Summary

I enjoy working with smart, honest, and hard working people. As both investor and manager I've had the privilege of working with amazing individuals and together creating innovative and market-leading companies. I'm particularly interested in businesses that are capable of rapid growth enabled by some element of technology.

Formerly venture capital investor at a $300mm fund based in New York making first round investments in software, internet, and digital media. Experience with companies at every stage of growth from startup to profitability. Served on the Board of Directors of companies that have obtained leadership positions in their respective markets.

Early investor and Board Director of:
* HNW - leader interactive marketing for high net worth institutions & luxury brands- NY.
* Tutor.com - largest on-demand one-to-one education company in the world - NY.
* Xora - market leader for on-demand mobile applications for business - CA.
* Greenplum - next generation database software - CA.

Early investor and Board Observer of
* DeepNines - security software with significant security patent - TX.
* Peer39 - semantic advertising technology that understands and matches demand from user generated content - Israel, NY.

Currently working in either a business or advisory capacity with a number of promising companies in the New York region including:
* Zuguide - an advanced javascript technology platform for video advertising.
* Altruik - a fully automated SEO technology (Search Engine Optimization).
