Subbu Jois’s Summary

Subramanya R Jois aka Subbu Jois
Prefessionally offer Web 2.0 Strategy Consulting. Currently working on Social Computing Strategy and Monetization for Enterprises. Run a Software Solutions business in Bangalore, India, developing on .NET 3.5 & DotNetNuke

Other Offerings include
- Enterprise 2.0 portals with Ecommerce, CRM and Content Management
- E-Learning and LMS Portals leading to Enterprise Social Software
- Web 2.0 features like SOA, Collaboration ( Blogs, Wikis, Forums, etc) Mapping, Networking Portals, Image and Video Sharing
- Enterprise Content Management
- Implementation on .NET and DNN
I completed my engineering from SIT, Tumkur, under Bangalore University in 1989, in Instrumentation Technology. I started Compusol, in 1995.
Our clients include Microsoft and Infosys. Among the other accomplishments that have dotted my software career are:
- Adopted, developed and deployed the first village computerization in India – Bellandur Gram Panchayath -1999. Have computerized over 10 villages since.
- Won recognition from UN-ESCAP for the same
- Won the certificate for best papers presented in International Conference for E-Governance 2003
- Presented papers on e-governance in different fora and conferences
- Trained of 100 SQA Managers in the industry
- Member-IEEE Standards working group for 828, 830 and 1175
I became a member of Round Table India in Nov 2004 by joining Bangalore North RT 25.
Posts held :
Chairman, BNRT 25 - 2006-07.
National Corporate Alliances Convener, RTI - 2006-07
I have since been responsible of a host of the initiatives, the most notable being “Freedom Through Education Plus” – e-learning implementation in 1200 schools under FTE program of RTI, which won the BEst Service Project Award at the Round Table International AGM 2007

Collaboration, Enterprise Content Management and E-Learning solutions
