David Connor’s Summary

My personal goal through Coethica is to raise awareness and motivate businesses of all sizes and development to fully explore the commercial opportunities of being a 'responsible' organisation.

David Connor’s Specialties:

Our services include, strategic planning & implementation, benchmarking, stakeholder engagement, environmental management systems (ISO 14001, BS855 etc), carbon footprint calculation, marketing & communications (award applications), assurance (AA1000), non-financial report writing, Investors in People, flexible working, sustainable procurement (supply chain management) charitable relationship management, training and partner brokerage.

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    The Liverpool (one of 3 in UK) project provides homes in a deprived area of the city by offering preferential mortgages to selected individuals, who in return are required to provide 500 'sweat equity' hours actually on site helping with their or others homes.
