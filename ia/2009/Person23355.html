John Rasco (www.refreshweb.com)’s Summary

RefreshWeb is on the first page of Google, Yahoo, MSN and Ask for dozens of terms that people use to search for search marketing services. That's what we do: get you in front of your best prospects, the ones searching for a solution. We do both search engine marketing (natural optimization and pay-per-click) and sales flow optimization. We believe this is the smartest marketing investment you can make--getting your site to the top of the search engines, and converting your click-throughs into customers.

With over 20 years of marketing management experience with a wide range of technology-based companies, we know how to think like a prospect. That means when they find your site as a relevant result for their search, they also find that you know what they are looking for...from testimonials to case studies, data sheets to a quick chat with a sales engineer, they want to qualify your offer and make a decision. That's what we mean by sales flow optimization, and why RefreshWeb is uniquely qualified as a search marketing partner.
