Perry Hooks Jr’s Summary

Following my initial academic experience (which also included a variety of jobs in high school, college and law school), the first fourteen years of my professional life was dedicated to the practice of law. I immediately followed my legal practice with fifteen years of sucessful business leadership experience. That experience included strategic, operational, and other leadership responsibilities. Because I enjoy the practice of law, I have been fortunate that much of my work continued to require interaction in both legal and regulatory forums.

In my next professional step, I wish to continue my leadership responsibilities, regardless whether that is part of a business or legal role. I have thrived and been successful in addressing critical business issues and would be very valuable to an enterprise in that role. I also wish to continue leading and developing personnel. I have led and managed people most of my career. I believe that this has been a positive experience for all with whom I have worked, as well as for myself.

This summer I have had the positive experience of honing my entreprenurial (and also, my intraprenurial) skills by working with my wife in her smaller business. I believe that some of these skills are transferable to a larger enterprise and I intend to continue my professional growth through this experience.

Finally, I have been a tutor for students ranging from elementary school through law school. I have taught Sunday School classes and Junior Achievement (JA) classes. In 2009, in addition to tutoring and teaching JA, I have been a guest speaker on the subject of Black History, have lectured on Political Science, anid I have been a presenter on Telecommunications Law (for Continuing Legal Education credit requirements for members of the Illinois and Chicago Bar Associations). I grow and learn from these experiences and wish to continue this means of personal growth.
