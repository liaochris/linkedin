Szymon Zabystrzan’s Summary

I am currently actively seeking a position within an organisation with a culture of encouraging an open minded approach to change in pursuit of continuous improvement. • Tailor and implement management accounting and project controls processes and procedures to pursue the objectives of the company and individual projects.
• Experience with various manufacturing processes
• Design and implementation of internal management and control processes to ensure accurate performance measurement and effective management of incentive schemes.

Broadly speaking I have significant management, project controls and management accounting background. My experience has led me to adopt a holistic approach based on the use of tailored and efficient processes combined with the extended use of IT to optimise the contribution of the management information systems (MIS) to the success of the company.
