Private practice with cognitiv psychotherapy and coaching

Courses and lectures about:

I make courses and coach private firms and companies in how to solve stress, anxiety, depressions, abuse at work or programs and teaching in communication-skills and conflict solving. My background for this is the best documented and best evidence based psychotherapy method: Cognitive Therapy.
