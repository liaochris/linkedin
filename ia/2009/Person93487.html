Heather Townsend’s Summary

Heather Townsend, founder of the Efficiency Coach, has spent the last eight years helping business owners and management, achieve more for their time, effort and money. She has worked for blue chips (Tesco, Procter & Gamble) and small and medium-sized enterprises. A lifelong passion for challenge and variety has led her to explore a variety of industries—from manufacturing to retail to financial services.

Heather’s expertise lies in being able to quickly connect and understand the people she is working alongside. She is commercially minded, pragmatic and an agile thinker, always getting to the nub of an issue quickly. She achieves tremendous satisfaction from enabling others to overcome challenges and achieve their goals.
