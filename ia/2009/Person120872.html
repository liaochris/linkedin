Joseph R. Slonka’s Summary

Hello! I am Joseph Slonka and I specialize in all things written from the legal point of view (contracts, complaints, wills, news articles,etc.) I currently hold a JD degree, a BBA with major in Finance, an AAS with major in Business, and a license in Post-Secondary Adult Education.

I have been an attorney for nine years and practice mainly in the areas of Bankruptcy, Small Businesses, Contracts / Transactions, Civil Litigation, Estate Planning, M&A, with some Family Law and Debt Collections. I practiced under my rules of 1. NEVER overcharge the client (no minimums or estimated charges), and 2. the client is ALWAYS right. In my years of practice I have never had one client even threaten a grievance or complaint against me, something I am very proud of.

Ethics and integrity are paramount in everything I do.

I learned the practice of law working for a municipal judge, who then had me train and supervise his staff. His mentoring led me to pursue a part-time job as an adjunct instructor in an ABA-approved Paralegal curriculum, where I have taught since 2005 AND ABSOLUTELY LOVE IT! I have taught Civil Litigation, Bankruptcy, and Estate Planning.

I currently work with the State of Tennessee on a temporary basis as a Contract Attorney in their DHS Conciliation Unit. I also offer custom stone / masonry services for select customers through my company, Stoneworks & Designs. I create and build fireplaces, wine cellars, patios, and other high-end projects and applications as my (limited) free time allows.

Although I am busy, I am constantly on the lookout for the right professional position which I can commit all of my knowledge and experience to. I hope to meet and speak with many of you, until then, please be safe and have a great day!
