Steve Morin’s Summary

I have been working in the web industry for approximately 10 years. After spending five years in the Navy working as a journalist, photographer and public relations specialist, I transitioned into the corporate world as a graphic designer. Building on my experience in page layout and design with QuarkXpress and image cleanup in Photoshop, I started working for a small group of folks in Newton, Mass under the title Image Specialist. Basically, I was preparing slideshows and presentations for large companies. But while I was there I picked the brains of the web designers, illustrators that were employed there, and since then it has been an ongoing learning process as times, technologies and trends have moved forward.

In 2001, I was hired at bGG advertising as the Director of Interactive Services and haven't looked back. Coding, implementing, SEO, SEM, E-mail campaigns, you name it, I have helped build it and do it. With our focus on advertising, I have been able to fall back on my public relations training from the Navy and the consistent and constant learning to give our clients the best possible answers to their online advertising questions and needs.
