Dave Tidwell’s Summary

Over 20 Years sales, marketing and business development experience in advanced Telecommunications and IT systems for self-service automation, customer care and network integration. This includes Speech Recognition Technology, text-to-speech, applications, voice self service, IVR, voice portal, voice platforms, human factors, dialogue design, implementation of solutions and products on a wide range of platforms in legacy PSTN and the newer next-generation of IP based services across both legacy (traditional - old world) and modern next generation self service platforms.

I'm now well and truly entrenched in the advanced/next-generation speech self services branch of the telecommunications industry with it having been my primary focus area since in the 1990's. I have held numerous senior level sales, channel and business development roles in this field, and will likely continue to do so until I'm rich enough to retire, or expire in my pursuit of the dream! Perhaps its time to start thinking about going into consultancy in this field, and start charging people a lot of money to use their watch to tell them the time! :-)

For fun, I am a trained and fully qualified aviator, and hold a Joint Aviation Authority Flight Crew Permit for single engined land based aircraft (any size). I also have a Canadian PPL and a US FAA/PPL with night ratings. I have written several articles on general aviation and life as a Pilot which have been printed in popular press / magazines. I run and own a popular Flying Club at www.thepilotclub.com that has over 650 members - this is turning into more than a hobby pursuit.

My other non-professional interest is Astronomy and Cosmology - and I handle Public Relations on the Board of Directors of the Astronomical Society of Central Florida.

For post regular academic fun, I've educated myself to beyond degree levels in Astronomy, AstoPhysics, Cosmology, and grand unification theories (String Theory, M-Theory and others....
