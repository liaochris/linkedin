Melissa Corley’s Summary

I have a strong research background with degrees in journalism and library science. I concentrated on reference services, archives and preservation for my MLS degree. I am a volunteer reference librarian for the Internet Public Library, where I have answered questions ranging from family histories to the religious leanings of past U.S. presidents to 1950s railway motorcars. I've also performed research for the Caroline County (Md.) Historical Society and the Easton (Md.) 300th Anniversary planning committee.

Recently, I joined Creative Memories as a consultant. Creative Memories provides attractive, photo-safe materials to display your photos and stories. Contact me if you are interested in learning more about their scrapbooking and other products. In addition, you can earn free products and other rewards by hosting events and/or by joining my team.

I am also an experienced communications professional. I have been working with universities in a public relations role for nine years. I have published bylines in publications including The Washington Post, the Baltimore Sun and The Virginian-Pilot.

Melissa Corley’s Specialties:

Historical and genealogical research, archival scrapbooking, writing, media tracking, press releases, copy editing, AP style, general reference, alumni and prospect research, distilling highly technical information, public relations, marketing, e-newsletter production
