I believe it is vital to occupy a profession that gives you a sense of purpose and accomplishment. If you have meaning in your career, then you'll achieve fulfillment in all areas of your life. So what are you passionate about? What is your place in this world? How are you making a difference? I recently completed the Texas Teachers Alternative Certification Program & am presently searching for an Elem. Special Education teacher position throughout S.A., TX. Becoming a Sp. Ed. teacher & School Counselor will afford me the opportunity to achieve my ultimate goal of guiding & supporting all students to lead successful and fulfilling lives. I believe all children can learn & do miraculous things when being taught by an educator who is committed to finding & establishing ways for every child to succeed. I understand that this means HARD WORK, patience, & dedication, which I am eagerly ready to do. I want my kids to believe in themselves, love learning, & attain the ability to practice self-discipline so they can grow up to become joyful, productive members of society.
