Marc-Oliver Gern’s Summary

Marc-Oliver is a Digital Brand Strategist specializing in planning, experience design, art direction and social media strategy. His current work focuses on crowdsourcing applications, play-based facilitation and business transformation through ubiquitous information models. Marc-Oliver holds a Bachelor of Communication Design from Merz Akademie Stuttgart and a Masters in Digital Media from University of Portsmouth. He also holds a mentorship at SIAT; School of Interaction Arts & Technology at Simon Fraser University Vancouver, BC, Canada.

Marc-Oliver Gern’s Specialties:

Digital Branding, Web User Experience Design, Social Media Concepts and Content Promotion, Web Site Optimization and eCommerce. Mobile Application Design. Monitoring Brands Online.


Expertise of user-centric design principles, web standards, usability and current trends in web technology. Played important role in creating use cases, process flows, sitemaps and wireframes.
