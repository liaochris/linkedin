Joyce Tan’s Summary

I was formerly an Industrial Engineer by profession, having 8 years of working experience in electronic and semiconductor industry. Being an Industrial Engineer, I served as a "bridge" between the production shop floor and the management. While it is commonly known that there are always two opposite or conflict view between the production shop floor and management, my tasks were to come up with the plan that is both feasible to production shop floor and fair to the top management. I conducted my observation on the production process, investigated variance, made a suggestion and communicated back to the management with consideration of the culture and challenges that either party may face. The main areas which I involved were capacity and direct manpower planning, productivity, preliminary feasiblity study on new product or new process and the related financial impact, and training and coaching new engineers.

At the end of year 2007, I took the courage to venture into business in health and wellness industry with zero knowledge in sales and marketing. I am currently an independant business owner dealing with health. I have learnt a lot in health and nutrition, sales and marketing strategies and it has broaden my view about business. I am learning and implementing online marketing strategies through facebook, twitter, social bookmarking, market and keyword research, online link building techniques, and web copywriting to generate more revenue on my first website www.mangosteen-natural-remedies.com.

If you see anything in my profile that might help you, I would love to help.
