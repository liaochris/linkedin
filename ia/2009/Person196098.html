Rob Mapstead’s Summary

The software I've written discovers the "Big Trader (aka Shark)" trades and like the remora I tag along...

I have had success on my own and am perfectly willing to keep running this for myself, but am interested in connecting with investors, venture capitalists, and angel investors who would help me take this business to the next level.
