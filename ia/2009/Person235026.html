Toby Marshall’s Summary

Based in Sydney but working nationally, Abacus Recruitment is a recruiting company that specialises in the financial services industry.

Our main objective is to create a transparent link between our clients and potential employees. Abacus Recruitment seeks clients who are in the financial services industry and are suffering from a scarcity of talent.

Abacus works differently to many recruitment agencies in terms of it’s solutions, for this reason many of its client are companies who are willing to implement solutions in order to expand.

Not only does Abacus Recruitment assist in the recruiting process, they educate their clients on financial recruitment in order for them to understand exactly what is needed.


The recruitment industry is not working well and major changes are needed. Through the media and being represented at conferences, Abacus is working to change how recruiting is practised in Australia: Abacus strives to consolidate the industry and make it one that they are proud to work in and not have to constantly defend.

Abacus believes the structure of the industry, such as the way people are remunerated and managed drives unethical and unproductive behaviour. To learn more: www.abacusrecruit.com.au or the debate on my blog: www.tobymarshall.com. Or www.recruitershamefile.com for more detail.

Toby Marshall’s Specialties:

Abacus has a different approach to recruiting solutions & is constantly think outside the square in order to achieve high results.

Working with other recruitment firms and doing on-field research, this is an expertise Abacus has spent 9 years developing. A core part of this is our membership of the NPA - the global network of 380 recruitment firms (http://www.npaworldwide.com). This connection provides Abacus and its clients REACH - whether geographically or by specialisation.
