Abhinav Agarwal’s Summary

Senior Principal (aka Consulting Product Manager) in Oracle's BI/EPM division (part of Oracle Fusion Middleware) responsible for multiple products and product areas in BI Enterprise Edition, focus on bringing BI to (hundreds of thousands) of end-users.
Manage BI PM at Oracle IDC.

Track record of innovative thinking and focused execution, delivering on products from concept to shipping release and beyond.

Excellence in all areas of product management - competitive analysis, market-research & prediction, product definition, working with engineering, usability engineering, QA, documentation, release management, legal, pre-sales, field, support, and customers (pre-release to beta to production). Extensive experience working with sales and pre-sales teams across three continents in helping close deals. Adept at working on multiple releases of the same product.

Prior experience includes working for a startup (highly dynamic, non-hierarchical environment), product development company, consulting company (rapid implementation of projects with large amounts of uncertainty and requirements volatility), and sub-contracting (working with multiple hierarchies) firms in three countries.

First product manager at Oracle India. First product manager to be promoted to Sr Principal (aka Consulting Product Manager, or level 5) in Oracle's Fusion Middleware & Server Technologies division 14 year history at IDC.

Technical editor of the Oracle Discoverer 10g Handbook, McGraw Hill Osborne (2006).

First Oracle employee to start a BI blog (http://oraclebi.blogspot.com/), that attracted 750k page views from 100+ countries in less than two years.

Gold medalist - MBA (PGD SEM), class of 2006, Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore.
