Handan Ozkaymak’s Summary

As a SD and ABAP consultant she has certified in SD 4.6 and have joined many Implementation,Upgrade ,Support and Rollout Template R/3 projects. She has more than twenty project implementation experience. She had also joined global project "Unity" in Unilever in three countries Arabia, Israel and Turkey.

As a SD Consultant main responsibilities are Implementation of Sales & Distribution, Logistic Execution System, Logistic Information System optimisation according to client requirements and customize the system and design new developments. She has a deep knowledge of SAP SD(Order management& billing, complex pricing, ATP check) ,LES (delivery and shipping processing, batch determination,shipping cost calculation), LIS(Information system), thorough understanding of integration with other SAP R/3 Modules & IM/WM(Warehouse Management), APO(TP/VS,GATP) , CRM . She has also project implementation experience in SAP SD Revenue Recognition, good skills in master data preparation, cleansing and mapping, developing enhancements, user exits, routines, IDOC & EDI processing, developing queries according to client requirements and designed & tested several custom developed programs and reports.

As an Abap Consultant, She has been developed SAP Script, interactive programming, dialogue programming, interfaces, RFC's, BAPI, Badi, User-exits and Batch-inputs with modules SD MM FI QM. She has also lots of experience in debugging and integrating SAP with non-SAP systems as an Abap and SD consultant both in development and design aspects.

As a Project Management at user side, She has responsibilities of Organising of all SAP Methodology steps such as Organising of Blue print workshops , system entegration tests. Also responsible to guide the training of users , the preparing of all master data and preparation for go live.
