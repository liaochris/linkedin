Tim Empringham, MBA’s Summary

Execution oriented, innovative leadership professional with extensive and proven results in team development, project delivery, process redesign, and strategic development. My varied industry experience and dynamic leadership style coupled with my MBA and professional growth strategy allow me to deliver extraordinary results in competitive markets.

My drive for continuous improvement, collaborative innovation, simplification of complex processes, and my ability to recognize and adapt to fast moving market conditions allows me to consistently identify and exploit new growth opportunities early to generate significant early mover advantages.

I am dedicated to continued personal development through reflective thinking, communication with the people around me, and through an insatiable appetite for reading. I am committed to developing the teams around me in the same way by recognizing their unique strengths and limitations and then engaging their personal drive to allow them to flourish in their professional life.

I expect to continue my professional development in progressively more senior management roles with the ultimate goal of leading a dynamic, responsive and innovative SMB/SME technology solutions company as president and CEO within the next 5-6 years.
