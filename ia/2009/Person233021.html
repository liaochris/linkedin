Tim Huebsch’s Summary

I graduated from Minnesota State University, Mankato in 2002 with a degree in Computer Science - Management Information Systems and minors in Business Administration and Management and finished my MBA at the University of Minnesota’s Carlson School of Management in May 2007. I served as a Humphrey Institute Policy Fellow during the 2007/2008 academic year and am a past participant of the Leadership Twin Cities program. I currently am employed by General Mills as a Project Manager on our Consumer Internet Team. I have had multiple roles both within Information Systems and Supply Chain Management and co-lead Information Systems recruiting at Minnesota State University, Mankato for General Mills. Outside of General Mills I serve on the Board of Directors for the Minnesota State University Mankato Alumni Association, Leadership Giving Advisory Board for the Greater Twin Cities United Way and DRIVE Emerging Leaders Board for the Minneapolis Regional Chamber of Commerce. I enjoy volunteering with many community groups including the Greater Twin Cities United Way, Citizens League, Community 2.0, and the Digital Divide Initiative.
