Yvonne Adele’s Summary

Yvonne Adele is the Director of Ideas Culture, www.ideasculture.com :: 1300 734 904

Founder of:: Ideas While You Sleep - a virtual brainstorming service for business people who need a bundle of ideas quickly.

Imagine you're a PR, marketing or communcations account manager and you've got a presentation with a client at 3pm tomorrow afternoon where you're expected to table a bunch of fresh ideas to meet their brief.

>> Submit the brief to us online by 4pm and we'll have around 100 ideas (in some cases up to 300) back to you by 10am <<

IDEAS WHILE YOU SLEEP. What a great idea!

What else do we do? We assist business people to find clever solutions to challenges.

We can do it FOR you (Ideas While You Sleep),
WITH you (a facilitated brainstorm with your team at your office); or
VIA you where we act as your secret weapon behind the scenes, providing you with activities YOU can run with your team.

Yvonne runs creative thinking sessions at conferences as either a keynote address, plenary session, or breakout.

** Show us how soon you can get your team into a room for 2 hours and we'll show them how to pop with fresh ideas anytime to solve any business challenge. **

(we even have a list of great rooms to hire in all major capital cities, so there is no excuse!)

Yvonne has achieved a designation held by around 7% of Professional Speakers worldwide - CSP (Certified Speaking Professional) with the National Speakers Association of Australia.

What is Creative Thinking? It's a technique you use for getting fresh ideas. Innovation is the implementation of those fresh ideas.

Back in the day, Yvonne started her professional career with Microsoft, then invented the media personality Ms Megabyte as a way of getting technology advice and know-how across to the general public. At the height of the brand profile, Ms Megabyte was recognised by 1 in 5 Australians and had a best selling book: Conquer Your Computer.
