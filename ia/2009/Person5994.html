Aladin Gohar’s Summary

Business and technology transformation leader – building frameworks and realigning business technologies to deliver profitable growth.

Expertise in application/data integration across business functions and acquisitions

An accomplished enterprise architect skilled at applying technology strategy with business directives incorporating comprehensive IT governance techniques.
