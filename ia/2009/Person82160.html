Freya Mishra’s Summary

Sports, adventure, creativity, living life differently, reading about people who have but taken the unusual path to lead the way is what makes life interesting for me. I guess that is the reason why I defied the rules and when people were taking up Science as a subject, I went ahead with Commerce and while most were opting for a MBA, I opted for a Masters in Communication Studies. I have never regretted any of my decisions, not even working in a News Channel like AajTak, even though it left me a tad bit more cynical towards the entire system.

Work for me is synonymous with passion, growth, and having a lot of fun. That aptly defines my position in infoAnalytica- Project Manager Social Media Marketing; yeah it is a lot of fun. Currently working on Reach 2.0, a fabulous project that leads me around the concept of Web 2.0, which is growing everyday.

Reach 2.0 incorporates all that and is infoAnalytica’s solution for creating new dimensions in the growth and innovation charts of an alternate marketing strategy. The fun does not end here; it extends to the work environment that is a pivotal part of my organization. We have a good mix of people, coming from all sections of the country, and we all get together for a game of golf, NASA competition, Scavenger Hunt, Visits to NGO’s and so on- it is our way of being progressive not only in our work but in our overall personality.
