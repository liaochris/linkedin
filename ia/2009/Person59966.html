Dean Carpenter’s Summary

My professional experience has encompassed 3D character animation, animated film production, character design/illustration, composition, story, drawing, and painting. My foremost goal is to continue my career as an Animator / Artist by lending my top skills to all aspects of any team and production. My standards of work ethics are extremely high and I am committed and dedicated to producing the best product possible under any deadline. I welcome and embrace any creative challenge with any project and/or tasks at hand, while consistently remaining a dynamic team member.
