Tiger Beaudoin’s Experience

  • Founder, Greencentives Division

    BI

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2009 — Present (less than a year)

    Greencentives is to BI what "ecomagination" is to GE: a collection of the best platforms, tools and strategies to help customers tackle today's pressing environmental problems.

    Our flagship coalition loyalty program, www.ecobonus.org, promotes environmentally preferable products and rewards eco-conscious consumer choices with credits that may be donated to environmental charities or accumulated for green rewards and ecotour holidays.

    BI’s unique reward-code technology adds a new twist to these "collect 'n earn" programs by tracking product purchases, giving manufacturers unprecedented visibility to their green-leaning consumers plus the tools to interact with them promotionally via the web, email, text and twitter.

  • Vice President, Coalition Marketing

    BI

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    July 2003 — Present (6 years 5 months)

    Busy developing the $400 billion foodservice industry's largest loyalty program, Foodservice Rewards.

    Like a "Nielsen or IRI for foodservice," Foodservice Rewards provides brands such as General Mills, Nestle, P&G, Unilever, and Sara Lee mission-critical information about their customers and purchasing habits. It also provides direct marketing tools producing measurable results, finally bringing data-driven marketing to Foodservice.

    www.foodservicerewards.com is now the most visited website in the foodservice industry with over 1,500,000 page views per month. Its blog, www.fightprivatelabel.com details brands' newfound ability to fight private label competition without resorting to discounting.
