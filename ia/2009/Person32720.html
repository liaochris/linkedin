Brent Otterlei’s Summary

I'm a UI/UX designer/developer with experience in a wide variety of projects. I currently specialize in creating, implementing and leading the UI/UX efforts for emerging start-ups. I have a lot of experience working with and bridging the gap between product teams and engineering teams in taking UI/UX's from concept sketches and wireframes all the way through implementation. I thrive in fast paced collaborative environments and I love getting my hands dirty.
