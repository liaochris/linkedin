Jason De Mamiel’s Summary

With over 20 years of IT industry experience Jason has been involved in improving the IT infrastructure of a number of businesses.

From small additions to major projects including ERP, CRM, EDI implementations to improve the over all running of business and in some cases make a saving on the bottom line.
