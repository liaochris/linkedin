Rick O'Connor (Twitter: rickoco)’s Summary

Through True Reckoning, a privately held consultancy practice which Rick founded, Rick is engaged in providing Executive leadership to initiate and grow new business opportunities within existing organizations and within startup environments.

A proven industry executive with 23+ years of experience Rick has been recognized as one of Canada’s Top 40 Under 40(tm) business leaders. Rick's work has been focused on leading-edge product & business development with emphasis in the areas of strategy formulation and execution, marketing and business development, new product development and introduction, strategic relationship management and driving M&A activity.
