Jeffrey Ghannam’s Summary

Served as Senior Media Development Expert in an evaluation of the USAID/Egypt Media Development Program, spring 2009.

Lead multimedia and strategic communications efforts to build outreach capacities and reach audiences in the context of international development. Advised in protecting intellectual property, brand identity and managing corporate reputational risk. Directed a corporate intranet, outreach materials, media policy, and trained Chiefs of Party in media preparedness. Directed efforts to document USAID success stories on video for disemmination on YouTube and in other innovative formats.

Veteran reporter, writer, and editor with many years of experience handling major breaking news; national and foreign reporting with expertise in legal affairs and the Middle East. Former staff writer and editor at the Detroit Free Press; former legal affairs writer at the American Bar Association Journal, Chicago; contributor to The Boston Globe from Detroit, The New York Times in Washington, D.C., Time magazine in Cairo, Egypt, UPI Detroit and Nice-Matin in Nice, France, among others.

Additional Media Development Experience:

Awarded a James S. and John L. Knight Foundation International Press Fellowship to advance media training programs in the Middle East and North Africa.

Served as an editor/trainer for an investigative reporting project in Beirut, Lebanon for the U.S. Agency for International Development's Office of Transition Initiatives, and led training activities for the National Democratic Institute in Jordan and Morocco.

Analysis of legal protections and challenges confronting independent media and program design carried out in Morocco, Tunisia, Egypt, Jordan, and Lebanon for the Knight program.

Analysis and recommendations to reinvent the news operation of Qatar Broadcasting Corp. in Qatar undertaken for the Voice of America.

Jeffrey Ghannam’s Specialties:

Lawyer, media development and strategic communications expert, writer, editor, educator and trainer. Media law, international law, intellectual property, marketing, brand identities. Field experience in the Middle East, North Africa and Gulf regions identifying business trends and opportunities. Effective team builder and change agent. Public speaker and TV commentator.
