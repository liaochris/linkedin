John Babb’s Summary

Mr. Babb has over 15 years of experience in internet related information technology and has held key positions in government, private and venture-stage companies. Prior to incorporating idimension in 1998, Mr. Babb was Director of Media Management Solutions with Blackhawk Color Corporation, Director of New Media Development with TeleVisual Communications, and an active duty Field Artillery Officer in the United States Army.

While an officer in the Army, Mr. Babb graduated with honors from the Field Artillery Officer Basic Course, the Nuclear, Biological & Chemical Weapons Course, and achieved both Air Assuault (rappelling from helicopters) and Airborne (parachuting from fixed wing aircraft) qualified status.

Mr. Babb received his commission in the US Army after graduating in 1991 from the United States Military Academy at West Point, New York with a BS in Systems Engineering and Management. While at West Point Mr. Babb played two years of basketball, two years of volleyball, and graduated on the Dean's List. After an honorable discharge from the US Army, Mr. Babb was awarded an MBA from the Georgia Institute of Technology in Atlanta, Georgia with concentration in Information Technology & Finance.

Since incorporating in 1998, Mr. Babb has led idimension in leveraging network connected software to help businesses profitably market their products & services. He has served companies across the globe in a breadth of industries, including Bic, Catalina Marketing, ClosetMaid, Coast Dental, In-Sink-Erator, Monster.com, Moffitt Cancer Center, Pinch A Penny, Seacoast National Bank, Syniverse Technologies, and many others. idimension’s corporate headquarters is in the Florida Capital Bank building in St. Petersburg, Florida. As owner, CEO & President, he employs a staff of 9 including three MBA’s as well as software engineers, search marketing specialists, rich media developers, and quality assurance engineers.
