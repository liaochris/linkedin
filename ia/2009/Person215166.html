Shannon McWilliams’s Summary

Marketing and Product Development executive with 20 years of expereince driving growth through new products, new programs and account plans. Most recent position was Vice President of Marketing for Petmate where new products and marketing efforts positioned Petmate as the industry leader for innovation and creativity.

Marketing and product development is my passion. I have invented and participated in the development of true new products that have generated millions in revenue. The key is understanding the user, solving their problems and then building and leading a team of talented employees that have the same passion.

In addition to growth oriented accomplishments, financial analysis, metrics and an understanding of manufacturing technologies and processes have helped me succeed. My background also includes a broad understanding of legal issues such as trademarks, patents, license agreements, royalty agreements and litigation.

I have been to Asia 30 times to develop new products, low cost manufacturing, low cost engineering and low cost industrial design capabilities.
