Troy Dreier’s Summary

I'm a freelance writer specializing in consumer technology, and I enjoy covering a huge range of products including audio and video hardware and software, desktops, laptops, handheld devices, smartphones, cell phones, games, GPS devices, and more. I've been fortunate to write for many of the biggest names in tech journalism, including CNET, PC Magazine, Laptop Magazine, Computer Shopper, and PDAStreet.

Troy Dreier’s Specialties:

Much of my work now is in the rapidly growing area of digital video. I'm the sole writer for Web Video Universe (www.webvideouniverse.com), a site devoted to online video for business, and my first book, "CNET Do-It-Yourself Home Video Projects: 24 Cool Things You Didn't Know You Could Do," was just published by McGraw-Hill.
