John Balodis’s Summary

I am an accomplished professional with over 20 years experience sourcing and selling large-scale and custom-engineered equipment, machinery, and full-scale manufacturing solutions. With experience across a broad range of industries – flexible & rigid packaging, plastics processing, automation, food processing and automotive– I have distinguished myself as an expert in the specialized machinery and equipment manufacturing sector.

In my current role, I am the Founder and President of Procurement Management Group, an outsourced procurement management company helping small to mid-sized manufacturers make informed decisions regarding the sourcing, evaluation, and purchase of capital equipment and machinery. With over 20 years experience on the vendor side, I know what to look for in a vendor, how to evaluate the right equipment and options based on capability and suitability, know how to negotiate both cost and service considerations, know what procurement pitfalls to avoid, and have the expertise to offer unbiased independent counsel on the overall viability of a project.

Since the mid 1980’s I have been involved in selling machinery systems for several recognized market leaders in the conveyor and plastic extrusion machinery markets, specializing in high value, complex projects ranging in value up to $3.5 Million. I have used my superior analytical and communications skills to fully understand my customer’s needs and match them to my company’s capabilities. This resulted in many long-term relationships supplying equipment to companies across a broad spectrum of industries such as flexible packaging for food and other applications, electrical cables for utilities, large diameter water pipes, footwear and many others.

With my extensive experience in the manufacturing sector I am able to provide my clients with the advice and information they need to make informed decisions about their equipment requirements.

John Balodis’s Specialties:

I offer expertise across the full life-cycle of equipment procurement and am particularly effective in supporting organizations in equipment needs assesment, specification development, global vendor sourcing, vendor evaluation, quote evaluation & revision, contract negotiation, project management and installation and acceptance services.
