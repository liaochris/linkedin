John Chadbourne’s Summary

Dr Chadbourne has extensive experience in the application of environmental engineering to industrial emissions and atmospheric chemistry. He authored the chapter on beneficial use of hazardous wastes in the cement pyroprocess in McGraw-Hill Standard Handbook of Hazardous Waste Treatment and Disposal. He has over 20 years direct experience in the portland cement manufacturing industry. He is well known for his work on detached plumes resulting from ammonia and acid gas emissions. He has testified in legal proceedings numerous times communicating complex technical issues to non-technical people. Teaching full time as a Professor of Environmental Engineering for over four years significantly improved his communication skills.
