Megan Barber’s Summary

Barber Virtual Assisting Solutions is owned and operated by Megan Barber. Megan has worked "behind the scenes" of the real estate industry for over 10 years. Before opening BVAS for business in January 2005 Megan had held positions as a real estate office manager, in-house Realtor assistant, and an advertising coordinator/marketing assistant in the marketing department in Oregon's largest real estate company, RE/MAX Equity Group. Megan is a licensed Real Estate Sales Person in the state of New Jersey.

Megan is a Professional Real Estate Virtual Assistant (PREVA) is an Ethics Checked VA, and is a Certified Real Estate Support Specialist (CRESS), by the International Virtual Assistants Association (IVAA).
Megan constantly keeps up with the ever changing trends & technology that impacts the real estate industry. She continually receives training and information on the latest real estate technology products. She has extensive computer experience, and the following are a sampling of the programs she is familiar:
MS Office Applications, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Acrobat,
FNIS MLS, Rappatoni, MRMLS, Top Producer 7i, Relay Transaction Management, Planet RE, Rainmaker E-Central, House Values, Imprev, Expresscopy.com, Realtor.com, Advanced Access, Point2 Agent, Cevado Technologies, Z57, Homes.com, and many more.

Real Estate Virtual Assistant, Real Estate Marketing, Listing Coordination, marketing, desktop publishing, word processing, direct mail, lead follow-up, website maintenance, administrative assistance
