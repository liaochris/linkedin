Michael Palmer’s Experience

  • Principal Consultant

    Extendwerks, LLC

    (Computer Software industry)

    April 2008 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Perform product development, consulting and market research for this startup company with focus on the Oracle market. Created multiple Oracle development environments including Oracle E-Business Suite R12 running on Linux (Cent OS 5.X) and VMWare to support software development activities.   • Product Architect and Lead Developer

    SigmaTrak, Inc.

    (Computer Software industry)

    June 2002 — March 2008 (5 years 10 months)

    SigmaTrak marketed an innovative network appliance based solution for supplier and distributor collaboration.
    As a co-founder, served as product architect and lead developer of the SigmaTrak Integration Router (SIR). The SIR was designed to dramatically simplify B2B integration via printing. The SIR was deployed on low cost network appliances and built with the Linux/Apache/MySQL/PHP (LAMP) software stack. The SIR was sold to over 150 suppliers and distributors worldwide over a 4 year period. SigmaTrak was acquired by E2open in April of 2008.

  • Director of Orion Group

    ClearOrbit

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    December 2000 — June 2002 (1 year 7 months)

    Led the “Orion Group” within ClearOrbit as a customer focused team tasked with new product development. Worked directly with a large customer to solve a major supplier claims problem by deploying an internet based supplier collaboration portal. “Online PO” was a direct extension of Oracle Purchasing. Suppliers printed bar-coded package tracking labels at the point of shipment which were utilized for automated receipts. OPO was rolled out to over 170 suppliers in 6 months and supported up to 5000 users. The solution completely eliminated the supplier claims problem in its first year of operation. The solution evolved into ClearOrbit’s current PO Collaborator product.

  • Product Architect and Director of Development

    BPA Systems

    (Computer Software industry)

    June 1995 — December 2000 (5 years 7 months)

    Developed BP*LINK – an Oracle based bar coding solution for Oracle that allowed RF bar coding applications to be developed using PL/SQL. Expanded BP*LINK in 1996 to include pre-packed bar coding applications for Oracle Purchasing, Receiving, Inventory and WIP. BP*LINK and BP*Link/Apps became the dominant bar coding solutions for Oracle Applications in the late 1990s. It was deployed at over 250 customers such as Cisco, GE, Motorola, Alcoa, Eaton, Dana and many other large manufacturers.
