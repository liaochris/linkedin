Russell Smith’s Summary

With over a decade of experience in copywriting, researching, and editing Web content, marketing writing, brochures, e-mail and direct mail pieces I've helped a wide range of companies in Seattle, the West Coast, and across the country.

I've helped clients in the areas of:

• Web content writing and editing
• Keyword search content
• Direct mail and e-mail marketing
• Tag lines and headlines
• Corporate marketing communications
• Editing blog posts

From creating compelling website content, to applying a clear and concise tone to corporate communications, I've helped people achieve a greater presence online and in print.

Writing experience includes: Created and edited website content for a variety of web design firms, created e-mail marketing and direct mail pieces for Corbis, Microsoft, Rauxa, and many other national and international companies.

Every business needs to get their story out into the world.

People need the online story of their business told in a clear and direct way.

The right words can tell visitors to your website exactly who you are, what you have to offer, and how you will benefit them.

Every company and organization needs to get their story out into the world. Large companies and small businesses need to connect with a clear message directly to customers.

We are living in the age of scan, rather than read.

You want visitors to read your content not just scan it. You want potential customers to linger a while and learn about what you can offer, how you can help them.

People read messages that are put across with clarity.

Connection. Clarity. Communication.

What's your story?
