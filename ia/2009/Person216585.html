Sheryl Theisen’s Summary

Sheryl Theisen is a comprehensive public relations specialist who has taken the traditional PR role and stretched it outside of the box. By focusing her efforts in PRINT COLLATERAL, SOCIAL NETWORKING, and INTERNET OPTIMIZATION she builds a powerful brand promotion campaign. Sheryl works very closely with each client to learn what sets them apart in their industry and then pushes that concept beyond the limits.

Sheryl's approach is to build your brand in a different way than your competition, "I create marketing strategies and branding solutions for individuals looking to be a leader in their industry."

Sheryl has a network of business alliances that work together to build a solid solution-based process that ensures your BRAND is RECOGNIZED and REMEMBERED.
