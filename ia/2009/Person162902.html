Michelle Casanova’s Summary

A Creative Director (Art Director) with extensive advertising, branding and communications experience in the automotive, technology and telecommunications industries. Expertise includes management, design, art direction, copywriting, project management and strategic and tactical planning.

Formulated and implemented creative strategies and campaigns for some of the most successful global brands including Coca-Cola, Toyota, First National Bank, BMW, Vodafone and AT&T.

Exceptional creative talent and through-the-line experience in all forms of traditional and non-traditional media as demonstrated in being awarded the Grand Prix in the Sunday Times Marketing Excellence Awards in 2006 and 2007.

Provided outstanding leadership as proven in consistently directing and motivating creative teams towards communications solutions that achieve both client and agency business objectives.
