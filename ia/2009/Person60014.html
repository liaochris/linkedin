Dean Healy’s Summary

Ideas man. Go-to-guy. Anchor to sanity when all else fails. i.e. Product Manager (well, at least that is what I am currently). In my current state, being a Product/Brand Manager is like running your own business - you should be the custodian of your product from cradle to grave. That means knowledge of the selling, marketing, finance, logistics, strategy and overall management aspects are essential.

5+ years in IT, mainly sales and marketing experience with a dash of technical know-how. Channel focused relationships, so I am used to dealing with Vendors, Resellers and End-Users in the Enterprise, SMB and Retail spaces. I'm an analytical thinker that is always ready to go against the grain in endeavouring to make everything I do/use/interact with more efficient.

I either want to leave my mark on the world, or be part of a company (think Google, Blizzard, 3M, Nintendo, Virgin, etc.) that does just that through its people, its passion and its innovation.

That's pretty much it, I don't want to overdo it - according to Baz Luhrmann, the most interesting 40yr olds he knows don't know what to do with their lives!
