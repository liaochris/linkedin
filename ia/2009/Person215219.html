Shannon Yelland’s Summary

Over ten years web marketing and design experience. Shannon is currently the Online Marketing Manager for Sitemasher Corporation. She has been accredited as a Google Adwords Professional and also shares her knowledge with online marketing in her "Seed The Web" blog (www.seedtheweb.com). Search Engine Optimization and Analytics has always been on the forefront of Shannon's investment of time. She attends various conferences and seminars in North America throughout the year to keep up with the trends, strategies and best practices of online marketing.

Successful projects within Shannon's portfolio cover such initiatives:

- Online marketing consultations/strategy and implementation
- E-mail marketing consultations, management and implementation
- Online store design (B-to-C) and management
- Social media and custom weblogs for traffic generation and conversion of online sales
- Pay per click strategies and implementation
- Search engine optimization and advanced web analytics

Before Sitemasher, during her involvement with the Internet, she developed her own business - Mystic Marketing and has built the company utilizing her diverse portfolio of expertise in online marketing, web development, multi-media, e-mail marketing and branding. Shannon has been working with clients from the concept through to the implementation stage of their online marketing strategies to increase their sales and revenues.

Before Mystic Marketing, Shannon also worked as a Marketing Specialist at Fusepoint Managed Services, she successfully lead the company’s national re-branding project and coordinated all internal and external communications to achieve the company national market share growth.

Shannon’s ability to understand Internet technologies in great depth brings tremendous value to the marketing team.
