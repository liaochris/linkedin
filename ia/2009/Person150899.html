Marion Martinelli’s Summary

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Entrepreneurial leader with vision and a proven track record as a Senior Sales Executive, adept at driving profitability and reducing costs through the implementation of strategic and innovative business development strategies.
Extensive experience managing sales and marketing initiatives while developing strategic alliance and partner programs with major corporations such as The Boeing Company and Chevron USA, Inc.

Advanced leadership, collaboration, and communication skills, coupled with industry insight into emerging opportunities, trends, obstacles and challenges led to providing deliverables on time and within budget during a dynamic and highly competitive market.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Currently, Marion holds an Associate of Science in Computer Business Administration, a Bachelor of Science in Business Management and a Master of Science in Human Resources and Organization Development with emphasis in Organization Development.

In addition to Marion's educational accomplishments, she has a track record of achieving results in the area of enterprise account penetration, relationship development, employee motivation and sales performance. Her strengths are in organizational change thru leadership and functions in a participative, consultative, task-focused style, as well as having a high level of business acumen and strategic vision.

If you are looking for a customer focused, motivated hunter, team-player/leader and closer who can drive teams to project completion, has excellent public speaking skills and can travel extensively, Marion is your woman!
