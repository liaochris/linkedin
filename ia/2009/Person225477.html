Pete Strom’s Summary

The Strom Law Firm LLC was founded in 1996 by the former U.S. Attorney for South Carolina and current President of the South Carolina Association for Justice, J. Preston "Pete" Strom, Jr., Strom Law Firm, L.L.C., www.stromlaw.com is a leader in providing solutions to complex legal problems.

As a firm, we represent clients throughout South Carolina and the Southeastern United States in all aspects of state and federal criminal defense and in civil matters such as personal injury, motor vehicle accidents, workers' compensation, product liability, unfair trade practices, business disputes, bad faith insurance, nursing home negligence, and wrongful death.

We handle mass tort and class action cases involving issues such as whistle blower claims, environmental pollution, insurance fraud, and consumer protection on a nationwide basis.
