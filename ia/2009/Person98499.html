Gus Poskus’s Summary

For 30 years now, Incentive House continues giving 'Inspiration to those with Aspirations'.

As the name implies, Incentive House has an extensive range of unique reward, recognition products and services.......the latest innovation, the Reward Card launched, October '08.

The Reward Card is an exceptional alternative to the ever popular single store gift cards. See www.rewardyourself.com.au, currently adding approximately 1,000 stores a month.

Global Incentive houses have embraced the card as a simple redemption solution for their Australian incentive and loyalty programs.

Ideal for HR Managers and Corporates to use as corporate gifts, sales rewards, in loyalty programs, anniversary of service awards etc. dramatically reducing costs and administration time.

In tough economic times, Incentive House (born in the height of the '78 recession) continues to innovate and consolidate for the benefit of its loyal client base.
