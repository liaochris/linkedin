Kent Mulkey’s Summary

Enterprising, competitive and strategic health care operations and business development leader with 25 years experience and a proven record of accomplishment in operational start up and turnaround, strong financial performance, market analysis, business development, sales production and customer satisfaction.

An engaging and resourceful staff developer and team builder who generates sustained financial growth and corporate success. Creates an environment for open communication among cross-functional teams, fosters innovative ideas and procedures, ensuring an environment of accountability for the accomplishment of corporate goals and directives.

KEY CAREER ACCOMPLISHMENTS

• Directed the start up of a highly successful upscale assisted living and dementia care community, reaching stabilized occupancy of 95% in just eight months. Operating margin of 38% attained within two years.
• Accomplished “People First” Evangelist, Public Speaker, Humorist and Seminar Leader with presentations to over 750 audiences.
• Writer of magazine articles, newsletters, funding proposals and blog publisher.
• Noted for operational turnaround of underperforming entities in nonprofit and for profit arenas.
• 30+ years experience in counseling, mentoring and coaching others to growth and success (in excess of 15,000 hours).
• Expertise in brand rollout, sales metrics, hiring and employee retention, regulatory management and employee training.
