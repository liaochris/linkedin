Dylan Ross, CFP®’s Summary

Swan Financial Planning provides an easy, affordable way for individuals and families to get answers to their financial questions. Personal financial planning and advice is provided for an hourly fee, as needed and without the need to buy something or open an account.

I founded Swan Financial Planning, a New Jersey Registered Investment Adviser firm, in October 2005 to address the need to separate professional financial advice from product sales due to the inherent conflict of interest. Swan Financial Planning is a fee-only practice with no minimum asset or net worth requirements. Fee-only means no commissions or third-party compensation is ever accepted and no products are for sale.

Financial planning and advice should be accessible to everyone that needs it, thus clients of Swan Financial Planning come from all walks of life. Some are do-it-yourselfers while others are do-it-with-helpers. Services are customized to meet individual needs, so clients only pay for what is necessary.

“Swan” stands for “Sleep well at night.”
