Benedicte von Brasch Lystad’s Summary

Working for Techno House as a full-time 3D designer. Previously 2 years as a freelancer in computer animation and design. Graduated from Portsmouth University in 2005 with a BA (Hons) in Computer Animation following a 5 year Masters degree (with Distinction) in 3D design from ESAT in Paris.

Strong graphical creation and composition skills but equally happy working from a paper or digital concept base.
Fluent in the use of multiple media in a single project and competent in the assimilation of several disparate artistic visions in the creation of a unique output.

“Outgoing but at the same time, works well under pressure.”
Objective is to make a positive contribution to a company using extensive graphical and animation skills and a good deal of ambition.
