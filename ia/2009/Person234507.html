Tish Tisherman’s Summary

I’m the Pragmatic Career and Lifestyle expert who helps people navigate their business lives to actually coincide with their true passions because, frankly, life is just too short and too precious to wake up one day and wonder – what have I done with my life?!

I owned and operated a staffing and executive placement firm for over 25 years. I coached so many people struggling to find their place – make enough money, enjoy their daily work-life, find time for family. And although I was an entrepreneur and outside of the corporate box, I found myself being influenced by that mindset through serving it – 110%; which is how I serve anything I’m involved with – from boards to business to best buddies.

I’m in the Personal Development Industry now, and instead of placing executives in corporations I’m helping people develop from leaders to entrepreneurs. The best part of this phase of my life is helping others while working on being my best, actively working on myself on a daily basis. I’m vibrant and I’m passionate. I’m evolving with new thoughts, new processes, new ways of communication, all of which benefit my clients while, at the same time, guiding me towards both personal and professional development to live my life at this amazing level of joy!

My associates are folks looking for a kind of personal as well as financial freedom. They are tired of working too many hours and not making what they’re worth. They want control over their lives. They want more time with their family, or to travel, or just to pursue their dreams, whatever they may be. They want to hear that it is possible – even in today’s climate. And it truly is possible – as a pragmatist, I do screen my clients for attributes of coach ability, commitment, conviction and persistence; a mindset that Failure is not an option.

With that, we can make the rest come true.
