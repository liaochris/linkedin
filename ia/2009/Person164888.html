Mike Shelah’s Summary

Since 1999, I have been helping people with their business telecom needs. I have experience in Nortel & Avaya Equipment and can facilitate any telephone service order your need ( POTS, T-1, PRI, MPLS, DSL, cable and analog lines).
I also have 5 years experience designing and implementing structured cabling solutions from Cat3-Cat6 up to Fiber.

"The phone on the desk, the cables in the wall and the bill you get in the mail, I can help your business."

If your business is preparing to move, expand or open a new location in the next 6 months, lets talk about all the necessary telecom decisions you will need to make. By doing so now, you can make an informed decision that will save you time and money plus give you peace of mind that you did not rush into a bad business decision.
