Harry Rich’s Summary

Chief Executive of Enterprise Insight, the organisation that runs Make Your Mark, the national campaign to create an enterprise culture among young people in the UK. The campaign is best known for its flagship activity, Enterprise Week, which has seen over one million participants attend 6,000 events since it began in 2004.

Previously Deputy Chief Executive of the Design Council leading on services to support the understanding and use of design to drive economic growth; programme development; policy and government relations; and design knowledge, methods and research.

Pioneered the Design Council’s world-leading evidence base demonstrating the economic impact of design and its application to business transformation, including the creation of the UK-wide Designing Demand business programme.

Serves on the Press Complaint Commission’s Charter Compliance Panel and the advisory board of the US-based Design Management Institute. Joined the Design Council as a director in 1999 after a business career in industrial distribution, retailing and publishing. He was a non-executive director of the Advertising Standards Authority, a Justice of the Peace and has practised as a commercial lawyer.

Harry Rich’s Specialties:

Developed, reinvigorated and grown a range of commercial and not-for-profit organisations. Experience encompasses complex change processes requiring rapid insight, sensitivity, judgement, determination, strong communication, team building and political nous. An unusual mix of commercial and public sector experience that balances strategic vision and operational rigour.
