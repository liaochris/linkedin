Darshan .M’s Summary

Deccan Chronicle is the fourth largest english daily in the country. The group publishes Deccan Chronicle out of 9 centers, Asian Age across 4 locations, Financial Chronicle across 4 locations & Andhra Bhoomi in AP.

I currently oversee all editions of Deccan Chronicle.

My short term goal is to make us the first choice of readers & advertisers across South India before taking the leap across the Vindhyas..

My role is quite exciting as steering a 75 year old institution into the next phase of growth, especially in the internet & mobile age.

The global slow down is making things more interesting. I personally feel this is the most exciting phase for us. We will grow as well as consolidate our leadership positions.



Apart from the above mentioned responsibility I also handle the exciting business of Cricket for the group.Deccan Chargers is the IPL cricket team based out of Hyderabad India. I head the commercial side of the team and the business.

My personal aim is to make Deccan Chargers one of the worlds's best sporting team. I have done some exciting new work on the team's look, feel & packaging for season 2. Our new logo & uniform should be well appreciated. Our innovations on the ticketing would also turn some heads.
Till then Go Chargers!
