Adail Muniz Retamal’s Summary

I have a passion for research, practical application of what I've learnt and, of course, teaching others. My main interest today is on Eli Goldratt's Theory of Constraints (TOC), particularly with its applications to software development, project management and our personal lives.

I'm the founder and moderator of TOC-Brasil group (http://br.groups.yahoo.com/group/toc-brasil). Among the several TOC applications, I'm very skilled at the Thinking Process and Critical Chain Project Management (CCPM), which I combine with Agile PM and Lean PM in my Project Management practice.

In the Agile arena I'm the main promoter of FDD (Feature-Driven Development) in Brazil, an excelent agile development and management methodology, deserving its own group (http://br.groups.yahoo.com/group/gufdd). I'm also a CSM (Certified Scrum Master) and use Lean/Kanban concepts as well, in a balanced approach.

I'm also specialist on process mapping and modeling (ARIS, UML, traditional notations) and measurement using PSM (Practical Software and Systems Measurement) and GQM (Goal-Question-Metric), which I've applied to both software and non-software organizations.

One of my technical passions is Software Engineering as a whole and, particularly, Object technology (Object-Oriented Analysis, Design, Programming and Testing).
