Greg Kenfield’s Summary

In 2007, Greg Kenfield founded DataPeer Solutions (DataPeer), an independent consulting firm, responding to the growing technological changes and misrepresentation in the telecom industry. Kenfield, a fifteen-year telecom veteran, has led DataPeer in becoming a trusted adviser, helping businesses sort through the confusion and select products and services that most efficiently and effectively meet their needs.

DataPeer provides a wide range of telecommunications management services to SMB, Mid-Market & Enterprise clients across the nation. Unique in the marketplace, DataPeer leverages applied technical knowledge and experience offering telemanagement, bill auditing, carrier assessment & selection, and procurement. These four services encompass every phase of a company’s telecommunications lifecycle: initial purchase recommendations, implementation, ongoing management, and auditing of services. DataPeer typically saves clients 20-40% in telecom expenses through carrier negotiations, recovering retroactive credits & erroneous charges, and optimizing telecommunication products & services.
