Laura Williams’s Summary

I am a contracts, transactions and operations attorney with over 10 years of experience as in-house counsel, primarily in the cable television (including data) and software solutions industries. I am very active in two organizations, the Association of Corporate Counsel (ACC) and the International Association for Contract and Commercial Management (IACCM). With the ACC, I was Chair of the Law Department Management Committee for almost two years, and currently the LDMC's Vice Chair, Communications. Both ACC and IACCM have great synergies with state-of-the-art tools to help in-house counsel maximize efficiencies and better manage their contracting processes and law departments. Liberty Mutual Group did not acquire our legal team (the Strategic Transactions Group) as part of its acquisition of my former employer, Safeco Corporation, so I am enjoying the opportunity to explore various career opportunities to determine my next best move. I was fortunate to attend the IACCM Americas conference in April in Orlando (a "must attend") and the ACC NIHI in May in Atlanta. Both were excellent for education and networking.
