John Hood’s Summary

John Hood serves as Vice President and General Manager of Hewlett Packard's US Small and Medum Business Exchange organization.

Prior to joing Hewlett Packard, John ran Dell’s Small & Medium Business segment for North Asia. In this capacity he is ultimately responsible for achieving revenue, operating income, and unit share targets set forth annually. Additional responsibilities include positioning and driving the Dell Vostro brand within those markets, and acting as the Executive sponsor of Dell’s worldwide Partner initiative in North Asia.

He has also served as Vice President & General Manager of the Dell America’s Panama Customer Center (2003-2007), and was responsible for the start up, management and operations of the 2000 employee regional sales & support team. This team provided key sales and support for Dell’s U.S., Canadian, and Latin American business units in four languages.

Before his position as General Manager of the Panama center, he served as National Director, Sales, for Dell's US Consumer group. In this position, he was responsible for all Consumer Sales conducted through Dell’s Sales Contact Centers in Round Rock, Texas, Nashville, Tennessee, Waco, Texas, and Roseburg, Oregon.

He was also responsible for Sales through Dell’s “Direct Store” Kiosks, located in 57 locations throughout the United States, and the start up of US Consumer’s first international outsourced sales support activities in India and the Philippines.

In 1991 John earned his Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Texas at Austin and has continued his studies with several Executive courses from the University of Texas, and the Center for Creative Leadership.

John lives in Albuquerque with his wife and their two sons.
