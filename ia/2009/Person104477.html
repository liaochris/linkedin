Jared B. Reimer’s Summary

Jared Reimer is the founder, CEO, and technical visionary for Cascadeo Corporation. Jared founded the company in 2006 to address the rapidly growing demand for highly skilled technical operations personnel and fully managed application hosting. Jared has 15+ years experience in the Internet and telecommunications industries, beginning with pre-Internet-era bulletin board system operation and the creation of leading Seattle-based Internet service provider oz.net. During his time in the competitive telecommunications industry, Jared acquired and sold numerous companies, and personally lead the turn-around of The River Internet Access Company, a large independent ISP headquartered in Arizona. In addition to his work experience, Jared also serves on the advisory board of the University of Washington distance learning programs in web technologies, has taught for the University, and is also one of the founding board members of the Seattle Internet Exchange -- one of the world's largest open IP peering facilities. He graduated from the University of Washington with a degree in Computer Science, and subsequently earned a Masters Degree in Computer Science and Engineering. In his personal time, Jared enjoys world travel, SCUBA diving, skiing, fine wines, dining out, home remodeling, and is a FAA-licensed private pilot, flying the Cessna 172 and Diamond DA-20 when time and budget permit.
