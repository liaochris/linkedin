Corrie Adams’s Summary

After graduating from Texas State University - San Marcos with a BBA in Accounting and a Masters in Accountancy, I began my career with Novogradac & Company LLP in Austin, TX 2005. During this time, I performed assurance services for the low income housing industry as a Staff Accountant. In 2006, I transitioned to Deloitte & Touche LLP in Austin, TX as a Audit Associate. While in Austin, I performed assurance services for a variety of clients within the financial services and oil and gas industries. In 2007, I transferred to Deloitte & Touche’s Denver, CO office where I was promoted to an Audit In-Charge. During this time, I primarily served on Great West Life & Annuity. This opportunity led to my experience in working on SEC reporting and Sarbanes Oxley controls testing. I also provided services to the public sector industry. In late 2008, I returned to Austin, TX to join Clifton Gunderson LLP as a Senior Associate. I am currently providing assurance services for the public sector as well as financial institutions.
