Serhat Gürcü’s Summary

Youth Republic is the leading Youth Marketing agency in Turkey (and soon to be in neighbouring countries). We focus on youth, aged between 12 - 35 - of which there are 40m people in Turkey ;) We work with the biggest national and multi-national brands in our market.

We use new generation communication tools such as guerilla marketing, viral marketing, event marketing, WoM marketing, sampling etc. to create communication strategies for brands who target youth. Also, we provide consultancy services to brands that aim to get connected to the young consumers.

Our color is orange, to reflect our energy. We are 40 people at the head quarters in Istanbul, and we manage a huge network of university and high school students, 15- 25 years old, nationwide. This network gives us the capability to design the concepts and the campaigns hand-in-hand with the target audience. The same team also executes the projects out in the field.

I'm a founding partner and the general manager of Youth Republic.
