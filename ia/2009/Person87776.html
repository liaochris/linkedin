Gloria Nelson, CSEP’s Summary

Starting in an industry before it was recognized as an executive secretary back in the 70's, meetings and events came under the auspices of "additional duties and responsibilities". Since then it's become a whole new world with a vast expanse of goods, products and services to integrate into the marketing mix of strategically designed events.

Experience translating from various industry experiences including event management, public relations, magazine writing and now moving into the "experience economy" has given professionals much greater freedom of creativity, all with attention to the "bottom line" with measurable ROI and ROE.
