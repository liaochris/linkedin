Andre Havro’s Summary

Marketing Coordinator for Northeast Region in Brazil at Sony Electronics - Consumer Division, advertiser and architect with specialization in Marketing and courses at Boston University and Massachusetts College of Art in Boston USA.

MAIN SKILLS

− More than 10 years of experience in marketing planning, trade marketing and communication projects;
− Strong experience in managing teams;
− Good interpersonal communication;
− Strong experience in integration projects of marketing and communication, management advertising agencies and strategic analysis;
− Design and execute marketing events, including planning and management budget. Formulate strategic recommendations around key design factors for each event including strategic event plan, demand generation, site selection, registration process, event flow, space requirements, catering, and production needs.

Havro has ministered lectures about Marketing, Trade Marketing, Communications and Interactive Medias in colleges and events geared for businessmen, professionals and students. Was a predecessor in the creation of an interactive communication agency in the Northeast in 1996, and in the development of viral marketing in 1999. Was also one of the first to use the internet as a relationship tool (e-CRM) and founder of one of the first companies focused in using e-mail marketing in 2000.

Currently maintains the blog Obsonatus (andrehavro.com) about marketing, trade marketing, communication and interactivity.

Andre Havro’s Specialties:

Has been active for over 10 years in the areas of Marketing, Trade Marketing and Communication, developing plans for marketing, communications and promotion actions, performs market analysis for the development of marketing actions in different segments; develops incentive campaigns for sales teams; develops advertising pieces; does trade marketing, as well as coordinating merchandising promotion teams; implements e-commerce and interactive campaigns.
