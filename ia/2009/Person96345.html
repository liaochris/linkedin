Hubert. Dekock’s Summary

Welcome to my Linkedin Profile. I don’t often talk about the past, as only the future really matters, isn’t it ? But I will make an exception as I’m supposed to introduce myself in this summary:

In 1998, I have turned a passion (Stock Market) into a full-time job. Indeed, after several years as President and Treasurer of Investment Clubs during my college years, I started my career at BBL Asset Management.

Initially managing country funds, I then became responsible for some thematic funds. These were the days when most Investment Managers were “generalists” (multi-sectors). I had some preference for Technology and Telecoms already. I was also responsible for all derivative trades (options and futures) into the equity products. After a few years, I specialized into the Telecom sector and the management of a small team of Equity analysts covering Telecoms, Media, Utilities and Energy.

In 2004, we integrated the Brussels and Den Hagen offices to become ING Investment Management Europe. I became responsible for the Telecom & Media sectors on a global basis. I kept managing all the Telecom sector funds but also advised (Analyst role) the generalist Portfolio Managers across the globe for ING.

Essentially, during the last 5 years, I travelled the world to meet the companies under coverage both at investor relations and management level (CEO and CFO). My Telecom trips took me from Latin America (Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Chile) to Asia (India, Indonesia, South Korea, HK, Japan). I’ve always been a big believer in the emerging markets wireless opportunity. Micro-payments, and machine-to-machine wireless technologies are two other huge under-penetrated markets in my view…

I have now a broad knowledge and experience in Telecoms (companies, regulators, and technology providers) and I have been investing successfully in the stock market since I am 17 years old (that’s 18 years of experience for third party & own money, with 2 major bull & bear markets).
