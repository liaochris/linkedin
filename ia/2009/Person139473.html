Leili Khalessi’s Summary

As an HR Coordinator, Leili brings a client-oriented focus to HR to Go through her attention to detail and superior service. Leili has hands-on management experience in areas including employee training and development, performance management, candidate assessment and selection, succession planning, and payroll management. Her past experience includes administrative support for educational leaders in the Sacramento area. Her strengths are in visual design, communication, presentations, and project management. Leili specializes in occupational safety and health, including regulatory compliance, incident analysis, and training. She holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration and Human Resources from California State University, Sacramento.
