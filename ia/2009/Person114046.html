J Lee Harshbarger’s Summary

Business people needing improvement in their English language ability and cultural knowledge will find that my extensive experience in both the corporate business world and in English as a second language teaching will provide them with an inside perspective of business English not often found with ESL instructors.

My clients have included engineers and managers from the major automotive corporations and their suppliers, post-doctoral research scholars, and people from a variety of other backgrounds.

I seek to assist those needing improvement on an individual basis as well as businesses that wish to provide classes for their employees at the company site.
