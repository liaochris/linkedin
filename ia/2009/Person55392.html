Dariusz Lelek’s Summary

I started my career with programming as a teenager - wrote my first software for real estate agencies when was 18 - it has been selling in my country over next few years. Since then walked through all programming levels. Starting from very low-level programming in assembler and finishing on 3rd level platform-independent languages. In addition, since 5 years work on projects involving networking starting from planning: LAN, WAN, Wifi, VPN, measuring and testing (using such tool like Wireshark) on network security finishing.

During the time of dotcom-bubble was working on one of the biggest projects of this kind for commercial real estate market in Poland - real-time property market news platform with commercial properties listing. Entire system was build-up based on password-protected panels for journalists which have been preparing daily abstracts on recent events. pay-per-view content was accessed by visitors(companies) through pasword-protected panels

Apart from programming and networking I'm also interested in Operationg systems structure, their security, intrusion detection systems and internals. Thanks to low-level programming better understand how internal processes work.

Still broaden my knowledge in the areas of: networking, network security, communication protocols (including mobile telephony - GSM, GSMoB - gsm on board, satellite communication). Parallely to mentioned above continuously deepen my knowledge on Project management in IT. Thanks to my job in EMC have gained SixSigma Yellow Belt. Have also gained an insight into storage technology(SAN/NAS), available storage solutions based on FC, iSCSI protocols and many, many case studies.

Ultimately I'm planning to start Exec MBA in IT which will make fulfilment for my previous experiance and projects I have done

Member of: ISACA and ISSA
