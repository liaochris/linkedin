Ricardo Anderáos’s Summary

In the 80´s I worked as reporter, international correpondent and editor at Folha de S. Paulo newspaper.

In the early 90´s I have migrated to the interactive multimedia business, and in 1994 I devised and launched the startup NEO Interactive Magazine, the first Brazilian CD-ROM. In december 1995 I created the website design and consultancy company Latitude 0º.

Between 1996 and 1997 I was Senior Webmaster at Universo Online, the biggest Latin American ISP and online content provider. At Itaú Cultural Institute I coordinated the New Media Laboratory, developing virtual reality and web art projects. In 1999 I was Web Art Curator at the 24th Sao Paulo International Biennale. As a partner at Embrion Business Incubator, I worked as hi-tech advisor. From 1995 to 2004 I worked as internet and technology contributing editor at Carta Capital Magazine.

Between 2004 and 2006 I worked as Executive Editor at estadao.com.br intrnet portal, Technology Editor at O Estado de S. Paulo, Jornal da Tarde newspapers and Eldorado radio station.

Between 2007 and 2008 I worked as Publishing Director at Metro free newspaper in São Paulo.

Today I am Online Products Director at Band, technology editor columnist at BandNews FM, Metro and Homem Vogue magazine and CEO and Information Architect at Latitude 0º.

As environmentalist, I run the NGO www.ilhabela.org.

Ph.D. in Social History of Art from University of Sao Paulo (Brazil)

Journalism, technology, information architecture, interactive content producing and editing, multimedia authoring, Internet marketing, strategic planning, project management, team building and management, product development.
