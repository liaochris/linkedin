Kate Turner’s Summary

My professional sales background began in London in 2006. I was responsible for facilitating the placement of specialty products in VIP gift bags for events such as The Oscars, the BAFTAs (British Oscars) and the Cannes Film festival. When I came back to the States I began a year of rigorous sales trainin based on the spin selling technique.
In 2007 I was one of two students chosen to represent Florida State University in the National Collegiate Sales Competition where I placed in the quarter finals. Upon graduation, my ultimate goal was to have a career that utilized my talents and presented me with a wide variety of experiences. I began working as an Account Executive for KTVK-3tv in Phoenix Arizona.
