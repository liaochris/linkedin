Alagu Balaraman’s Summary

My work keeps moving across the areas of process, information technology and business strategy. In most cases, it has been working in the interfaces of two or more of these areas.

One area of fascination has been understanding more of the business-technology interface and bringing in alignment between business strategy and IT. The IT part includes both development of a technology strategy as well as designing delivery systems.

There have been repeated excursions into the area of business strategy, with an emphasis on how technology can be used to alter capabilities and create new opportunities.
