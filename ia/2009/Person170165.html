Michael Wangbickler’s Summary

As a wine marketing/public relations professional, my goal is to bring the world of wine just a little bit closer to the average consumer. To accomplish this, I promote the wines of a number of different wine companies from around the world to journalists, trade, and consumers. I also teach about wine and judge wine competitions whenever I get the chance.
