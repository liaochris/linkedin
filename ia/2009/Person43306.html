Chris Glynne’s Summary

A natural leader and experienced Director within IT, with a track record of visionary planning, business focus and a passion for added value results.

Particular vision for technology streamlined workflows but with the logical, scientific mindset to broach and improve any quarter of technology utilisation. Experienced and knowledgeable across all levels of technology architecture,development and implementation with immense business benefit focus and attention to detail and delivery.

A solid personal ethic of honesty, integrity, courage and hard work with an inquisitive mind and rapid, 'immersive' learning ability. A strong innovator, decision maker, presenter and enabler with well rounded people skills and enthusiasm to continually inspire teams and drive progress. Above all a strong breadth and depth of understanding in technology coupled with a firm grasp of commercial business practices and needs.
