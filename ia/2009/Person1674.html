Abelien Visser’s Summary

Art Director, Artist and Graphic Designer with a strong international experience. As Art Director, Abelien has a focus on Africa and as an Artist her work has been sold throughout the world (Australia, Singapore, Indonesia, Argentina, Switzerland, Netherlands and the USA).
Among her corporate clients, she has renowned companies like Deloitte and KPN.

Abelien Visser’s Experience

  • Owner & Art Director

    African Art Products

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Fine Art industry)

    May 2004 — Present (5 years 7 months)

    Promotion of African artists
    African Art Products is dedicated to promoting the work of African artists by bringing quality products based on art created by these African artists describing who they are, where they live and why they choose to become artists.

    African Art Products works with artists from countries in and outside Africa and promotes, to the best of our possibilities, the entire range of visual arts such as paintings, drawings, photography as well as textile art and other decorative arts.

    African Art Products is dedicated to fair and equal trade with the artists and artisans we work with and we respect the established fair trade principles such as long term trade relationships, paying fair licensing fees and providing financial and technical assistance wherever possible.
