Brittany Thompson’s Summary

- Designer and social media consultant with understanding of search engine optimization and social media strategy looking to share new trends and fresh energy. Self-starter with broad experience in the internet industry, designing for a wide variety of companies and holding numerous management positions. Clients have included Microsoft, HSBC, Wells Fargo, and WebProNews. Capable of designing raster or vector images for use on apparel, print media, advertisements, websites, identity, and more.

- Forum Administrator with experience moderating multiple sites with thousands of members. Understanding of phpBB and the time and skills required to successfully manage a forum.

- Writer for multiple high-profile e-Business websites and newsletters including: WebProWorld, WebProNews, MarketingPilgrim, PowerLinks (LinkMaster), SEOForumWatch, and iHigh. As newsletter designer, editor and reporter, followed news and trends in the fields of search engine optimization, marketing, design and internet technology. A book about web marketing and social media strategy is in the works...
