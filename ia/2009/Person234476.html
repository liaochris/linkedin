William Garner’s Summary

William Garner is a ten year certified eLearning specialist and an award-winning web designer, programmer, 3D MOA animator, Flash developer, virtual world designer, classically trained fine artist, and team leader. As a founding member of Tipping Point Media, Bill instinctively combines the artistic creativity with technological innovative. His renaissance ability has won him industry acclaim and his designs have been honored with dozens of awards for creativity, technical savvy, user ability, simplicity, and originality.

Bill’s natural ability to diagnose technical and creative training and marketing needs, then design unrivaled custom solutions that garner retention and define ROI, place TPM at the head of the elearning evolution. His full understanding of the technical mediums and hands-on approach make him the perfect liaison between the (in-house) technical developers, creative teams, PMs, IDs, writers, and the client. Bill is also currently pursuing an M.A. in Instructional Design at Pennsylvania State University.
