Tom Millitzer’s Summary

Tom Millitzer is President NCC - Founded in 1994. NCC is a leading M&A company in the Internet industry. NCC has completed over 300 assignments.

NCC - over 15 years in the Internet and Hosting sector. Strategic M&A assignments working with the owners of firms in the sale of their business. We provide to notch representation and know how to present your company iin the best light.

Frequent Speaker at Industry events including HostingCon.

Prior to forming NCC he was President and co-founder of Cincinnati based Charter Cable Systems, Inc. His background includes director of rates and franchises for GroupW Broadcasting & Cable.

We are merchant bankers specializing in representing owners of companies in the sale of their business - we do deals.

Millitzer also is M&A blogger for the - The WHIR.

Tom Millitzer’s Specialties:

A business broker providing M&A services to Internet related firms including Web Hosts, Data Centers, WISPs, Broadband and like firms.
