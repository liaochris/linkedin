Stewart Bryson’s Summary

Stewart has 14 years of experience designing, building and supporting complex database systems and data warehouses. Currently he is the Technical Director for US markets at Rittman Mead, an Oracle Business Intelligence-focused consultancy offering consulting, training and license sales.

He began his professional services career with Informix Software working primarily in the Data Warehouse and E-Commerce practices. The majority of his time was spent in full-lifecycle implementations, but he also participated in pre-sales and post-sales activities for large clients. Following Informix's spin-off of Ascential Software and the sale of the core database business to IBM, Stewart joined Electronic Data Systems as an Infrastructure Delivery Specialist for multi-million dollar projects.

He founded Transcendent Data, Inc. in 2004 to build a consulting practice around his considerable knowledge of best-practices and the Oracle Database Server.
