Elizabeth Browde’s Summary

Elizabeth is currently the Senior Director of Content & Partnerships for Travel Channel Media. In this position, Elizabeth is responsible for developing and launching new prime time programming as well as finding and fostering new talent. She also heads up the network’s fast growing partnership initiative, bringing in new partners, sponsorships and creating custom content across all platforms. Recently she launched the first ever in-room network, "Travel Channel on the Road" for Starwood properties.

While at Travel Channel and TLC, Elizabeth launched series including "Take Home Chef", "Tuckerville", "My First Home", "Model Life", "1000 Places to See Before You Die", "Untold Stories of the ER", "Little People Big World" and executive produced the long running "What Not to Wear".

Prior to joining Discovery Networks, Elizabeth created several original series and was the executive producer for 100s of hours of programming including, A&E's "Biography", Lifetime's "Intimate Portrait", Bravo’s “Profiles”, Court TV’s “Crime Diaries” "The Daisy Fuentes Talk Show", Discovery's "New Dectectives" and dozens of music and variety specials. She has worked with dozens of celebrities including, Diana Ross, Brian McKnight, P Diddy, Donald Trump, Nick Lachey, Drew Lachey, and Queen Latifah
