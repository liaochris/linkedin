Andreas Dimitriadis’s Summary

Dr Andreas Dimitriadis is an influential professional in the field of supply chain planning and optimization. He is the founder, co-owner and Technology Director of OptimalCore.

In the last 6.5 years he was responsible for the design and implementation of an end-to-end supply chain planning solution for a major steel manufacturer in Germany.

Previously, he was with i2’s EMEA consulting organization for 3.5 years. In various capacities, he implemented i2’s Supply Chain Management products at blue chip EMEA customers in a variety of industries.

He holds a Ph.D. from the Centre for Process Systems Engineering, Imperial College, London. During that period, participation in technology transfer projects gave him the opportunity to appreciate the issues involved in the introduction of innovative technologies in commercial environments.

With more than 12 years experience in consulting and software development, he has a strong understanding of the characteristics of the specific problems that industries face in the Supply Chain Management area and the software tools that are available to help solving these problems. He is also acutely aware of the issues involved in running large scale software implementations and, more generally, IT and process improvement projects.
