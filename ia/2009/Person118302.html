John Tree’s Summary

General management and CPG marketing executive with big company success at Procter & Gamble, Kellogg’s, and the U.S. Air Force. $400 million in annual P&L responsibility. International experience across the globe.

Created an entire B2B and B2C startup in the direct sales industry, leveraging internet/online strategies. 20 years in consumer packaged goods, pharmaceuticals, and the military, along with small company experience as both a President and General Manager – proven strategic thinker and strong tactical execution skills.

Knows how to lead people, solve problems, and make things happen – change agent with entrepreneurial passion. A new product development superstar with proven track record. Develops people – teaches by example and continuously leads teams for high performance. Global perspective with fluency in multiple languages and cultures – gains trust, overcomes obstacles, and forges agreements.

Masterful communication skills – can present/discuss issues with any audience with great facility, from suppliers, vendors, partners, and agencies, to internal stakeholders at all levels. Analytical, innovative, and organized – with both MBA in Marketing and Masters in Economics.
