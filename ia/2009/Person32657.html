Brent Goers’s Summary

I'm a Regional VP for Principal Annuities & Mutual Funds in the Financial Institution Division. I cover key bank relationships in the Mid South region (KY, TN, MS, AL & FL panhandle).

I strive to add value to bank management, financial advisors & licensed bank employees through one-on-one meetings, seminars, conferences & training sessions. This includes both product-specific & non-product strategies to provide quality investment solutions, as well as excellent service.

My daily professional life is guided by the US Marine Corps' Core Values - Honor, Courage & Commitment.

• FINRA Series 6, 63, 7 & 66 Certifications
• Life/Health Insurance & Variable Products Licenses
