Jon White’s Summary

Multifaceted, pragmatic professional with entrepreneurial drive and successful +25-year track record as an Owner of several Real Estate and Property Management companies and a Banking Services Executive. A creative problem-solver with the ability to drive revenue growth, build productive teams, improve workplace and output quality, and consistently exceed profit goals.
