Amar Ghatpande’s Summary

Amar in a nut-shell: Father. Friend. All-round genuine human being. Acute observer of people and sensitive to the psychological quirks that drive preference. And an industry-recognized creative professional to boot.

Cut my Advertising teeth as a visualiser in 1986 and rose to Creative Director Level while accumulating tons of brand building savvy involving nearly 30-35 national and international brands over a period of more than 16 eventful years.

I have imbibed the creative cultures of a number of well known Ad Agencies like Sista’s -Saatchi & Saatchi, Quadrant Communications - Lowe Lintas, iB&W communications and many other business houses for whom contributed as a freelancer, consultant or critique. Which has helped me evolve further.

Have the requisite skills for evolving effective and unique value propositions for brands at various stages of the product life cycle. Do the significant contributions to the marketing mix for successful Brand Building as per its requirements.

I am a lateral thinker with an ability to create relevant and memorable brand communication that Builds and Sells.

Worked on 23 national and international brands and counting, along the way building and managing 15 teams - that's 75 highly talented individuals - with measurable results, while providing my services to 5 of the best of the top 10 Indian Advertising Agencies. Have contributed millions & millions in India Inc while building successfully many National and International Brands. Dose not participates in any awards coz believes I am the better judge of the happiness and satisfaction that my work and contributions brings to me. Also the Brands, Products or Services have been enjoying and reaping the rewards of the work I have done for them.

Loves: Reading, Writing, Movies, Visit places, Nurture talents. Ideating, Brainstorming, Strategic insights, Build Organizations & Teams, Inspire, Criticize, Educate and Keep learning.
