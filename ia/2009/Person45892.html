Chuck Harold’s Summary

Guard Smart, LLC has joined McRoberts Protective Agency, Inc. the oldest established security firm in America, operating continuously since 1876.

Meredith McRoberts, the Chief Executive Officer, is the fifth generation family member to head the business. Now in its third century, McRoberts is a full‑service security provider with operations from coast to coast.

McRoberts has been certified as a Women Business Enterprise (WBE) by the Women Presidents Educational Organization (WPEO), a regional partner of the Women’s Business Enterprise National Council (WBENC).

McRoberts is the 26th largest security agency in America, providing approximately two million hours of uniformed guard service annually to clients in fourteen geographic markets and ten vertical markets, including special events.

McRoberts also operates Prosec Protection Systems, Inc. of Lakewood, New Jersey which has been installing patient protection systems for the healthcare industry since 1992. Prosec provides end-to-end solutions, including wireless tagging systems, and complementary customer support programs to over 400 hospitals across North America. www.prosecsystems.info.
