Sarah Gormley’s Summary

Sarah Gormley is Head of Marketing and PR at Made in Manchester. She is an experienced marketing and PR manager who has managed the international press relations, sales and marketing strategy of a number of well known brands as diverse as IBM and Vauxhall motors.

Sarah graduated in 1999 from Manchester Metropolitan University with an honours degree in International Business, specialising in Marketing. Assisting with the organisation of Manchester's Irish Festival and supporting events such as Manchester’s International Festival has enabled her to gain valuable skills and experience. She has expertise in brand strategy both for new and established companies/products.

She has worked as part of Made in Manchester’s team since July 2007 helping to deliver the PR and Communications strategy for the award winning 9th FINA World Swimming Championships 2008. Now a partner in Made in Manchester she has assisted in winning new clients for the firm, including the PR contract for international airline Finnair and Manchester’s 24:7 Theatre Festival. She has developed close working relationships with commercial organisations such as HSBC and UKTI. She has strong relationships with international media and valuable experience of working with different cultures. She compliments James Hickman’s extensive knowledge of sport with her passion for international marketing and communications.
