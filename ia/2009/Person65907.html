Donna Vetters’s Summary

I am an experienced theatre professional moving into the realm of Voice Overs. I have been involved in broadcasting since the early 80s and in theatre much longer than that! I currently have my own home studio and I am working with many clients on their various projects. I also perform with a Baltimore based improv troupe known as "Drop Three". We perform improv games as well as original sketch comedy. Many of the sketches we have done as a group have been my orignal writing. Please visit my website www.DonnasVersatileVoice.com to see how well I can "Capture the Essence" for your next project.

Donna Vetters’s Specialties:

Characterizations. Accents and regional dialects such as Irish, French, Russian, Italian, Spanish, New England, New Yorker, Midwestern, and Southern.
