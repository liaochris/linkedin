Nayeem Chowdhury (+1200 Connections)’s Summary

Nayeem Chowdhury is Bangladeshi/Muslim-American, and the Recruiting Manager of (GE) General Electric's Oil & Gas division of the Americas based in the Houston, TX NW office.

His responsibilities include driving recruiting strategy, execution, and quality for the Oil & Gas P&Ls in the Americas (US & Canada). He overseas recruitment of Subsea Engineers,
Mechanical-Product Design Engineers, Manufacturing Engineers and Turbomachinary professionals.

Nayeem has approximately 8 years of experience in recruiting, mostly in the petrochemical industry but, has done recruiting in government & infrastructure, IT (Oracle, SAP, CISCO), and freight forwarding

Since joining GE in March of 2008, Nayeem has worked as a Manager

As manager at GE, he is responsible for supervising a team of dedicated recruiters and recruiting coordinators.

Prior to joining GE Oil & Gas, Nayeem worked for Australian EPC firm WorleyParsons as a Senior Human Resource Representative based in Houston. Nayeem headed up the college recruitment program and worked very closley with the (GDO) Graduate Development Organization. He traveled across the US to get the best young talent in the design engineering business. He also managed very successful engineering job fairs and hiring blitzes.

Nayeem has vast international HR and recruitment experience when working with KBR, giving support and logistics to the United States military and coalition forces, by supplying them with an abundance of civilian contractors (both expat & foreign nationals) . Nayeem got the opportunity to work in Dhaka, Bangladesh & London, UK with Bax Global (Now Shencker Logistics) as an HR Management Trainee learning international HR policy and procedure from the ground up.

Nayeem graduated from University of Houston Downtown with a bachelor’s degree in Political Science (International Relations). He also completed Rice University’s HR Management course and received a certificate of achievement.
