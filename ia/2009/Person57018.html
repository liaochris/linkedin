David Caruso’s Summary

Making Google PPC (Pay Per Click)
Pay for your business.

How I increased revenue by $2400 in my business during the month of February for the cost of $5.85 of Google Advertising - (& that was just in one product line – I have x200+ lines)

In a crowded advertising world how does small business compete with large business in getting there message heard? Many small business owners today believe this feat is getting harder and harder.

In fact new technology has enabled small business to compete on equal grounds as big business – if you know how!

Those who are embracing new technology are now reaping the immediate rewards – those who aren’t are quickly falling behind.

Google Pay Per Click (PPC) advertising brings immediacy, specific targeting, and monitored results. Most people have heard of, or a doing Google PPC campaigns, either managed by a third party or self managed.

For many who are doing PPC campaigns – that is all that they doing. PPC is only one third of the overall equation in a successful online marketing equation.

Learn what the other secret 2/3 of the equation is!

If I only relied on my PPC campaign I would not have earned $2400 in February with an investment of $5.85.

In our session:

• We will uncover the other 2/3 of a successful PPC campaign

• We will learn the 12 second – 2 click rule

• We will see why 99% of your competitors are spending too much and not getting a suitable return on investment on their advertising $$

• Why implementing this simple strategy will increase your current ROI by 100%
