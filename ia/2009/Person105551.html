Jason Voiovich’s Summary

I help businesses and organizations build brands, launch new products and services, create memorable messages, and enhance stakeholder value. I have helped over 85 entrepreneurs, new ventures, and established organizations bring their concepts to market. My clients include organizations of all sizes – from national healthcare providers, to major manufacturers, to top-tier retail, to agribusiness.

Recent project expertise includes revolutionary online community development in the consumer-driven healthcare sector and the application of non-linear mathematics principles to the collection, analysis, and management of communication data.

In each case, I bring fresh and non-conventional thinking to old-line problems.

Public and private organizations rely on me to use some of the latest research in real-time, non-linear systems analysis to better collect relevant data from their environment, interpret that data quickly, formulate action plans, and return the resulting data back into an effective feedback loop. I am no stranger to detailed reports and thorny questions – that is the prime reason clients tap my expertise – but my true skill is the distillation of that data into meaningful, understandable, and actionable intelligence.

I am an instructor, business case writer, advisor, and academic collaborator with the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire Entrepreneurship Program. My research and writing have appeared in the scholarly journals for the Speech Communication Association (SCA), North American Management Society (NAMS), the Business Journal for Entrepreneurs, and business publications UpSize Minnesota Magazine and Twin Cities Business (where I am a regular contributor).

Most regularly, I am the author of The State of the Brand, a syndicated weekly column reviewing and analyzing current branding topics.
