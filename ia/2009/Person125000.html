Jason VanBorssum’s Summary

Jason VanBorssum is an entrepreneurial and strategic media executive with a diverse professional background. His experience includes digital content strategy expertise, creative development, business development, business affairs, animation production, leading and mentoring teams, and taking organizations to “the next level.”

With a strong background in business development, strategic relationship building and content strategy, particularly in the digital content space, Jason has successfully led new ventures and multi-disciplinary teams around common strategic objectives. Adept at identifying compelling content to drive and engage audiences, constructing and managing multimedia partnerships involving equity investment, joint marketing programs and confluence of media and digital technology.
