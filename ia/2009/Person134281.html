Kresten Buch’s Summary

Kresten Buch has twelve years of experience and an extensive network within the sports betting industry. He successfully introduced the Australian bookmaker Centrebet.com in 1996-2000 into the Scandinavian market. When he left the operation 75% of Centrebet.com's gross profit came out of Scandinavia, turnover was close to 134 mln EUR and the client base was above 50.000 clients.

He has worked as a consultant for major international bookmakers such as Ladbrokes, Victor Chandler and Sportingbet, and participated in betting software development for companies such as Multibet and Centrebet.

In 2002 he founded the sports statistical company Statman that sold statistics to Denmark's biggest tabloid paper Ekstra Bladet and a number of Danish Superliga clubs. This company was sold to Betgenius Ltd. in December 2006.

In 2005 Kresten Buch, together with a former business associate, bought Denmark's largest football website Bold.dk from the Danish League. The site had lost 120.000 EUR on a turnover of 335.000 EUR in 2005. He successfully made a turnaround that made the company profitable in 2006. In 2007 he became the majority shareholder.

In late 2006 he signed a two year contract as a managing director to build a large statistical operation in Tallinn, Estonia for Betgenius Ltd., a leading UK outsourcing company for the global sports betting industry. This setup is nearly complete currently employing over eighty employees.
