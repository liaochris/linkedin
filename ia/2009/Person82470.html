Fura Johannesdottir’s Summary

Fura Osp Johannesdottir is an information designer, information architect and film producer, born in Reykjavik, Iceland 1975.

Fura has worked within the interactive industry since 2000 when she started as a web producer/consultant at the Icelandic Web Agency. In 2003 she moved to New York City to further develop her interest in New Media at Parsons School of Design. At Parsons she explored interactive video and physical installations to extend her knowledge of the interactive world. She graduated with a MFA degree from the Design and Technology program at Parsons School of Design in 2005. Currently Fura works as an interaction design director at R/GA.
