Brent Jordan’s Summary

Brent Jordan is an automotive specialist with over 15 years experience providing advanced driving safety, marketing and educational services to the automotive industry, teens, parents and driving enthusiasts. He is the founder of DriveMark, an organization dedicated to advanced driver education for motorists across the United States. DriveMark has also represented top-tier manufacturers Mercedes, Volvo, Ford, Chevrolet, Toyota, Mazda, Hyundai, Buick, Pontiac Honda, Hummer and Scion focused on sales training, safety technology and performance.

Utilizing his high-performance driving skills, he also dedicates his time as a Senior Instructor at ProFormance Racing School where he teaches street survival skills, advanced performance driving, and racing skills to racers as well as the general public. Founded in 1987, ProFormance has become a leader in the driving industry with clients ranging from Microsoft and Cisco Systems, to Ferrari of Seattle, the BMW Car Club of America and MTV Road Rules.

Brent Jordan is an expert in new active safety technologies, including ABS, Traction Control, Stability Control, Roll Stability Control and Adaptive Cruise Control and has developed both street and closed courses to help students experience and understand each technology. Brent is also experienced in all areas of four wheel drive and all wheel drive vehicles and has facilitated educational, real World experiences in the areas of off-road, towing and hauling for owners and dealers.

Formerly Brent has more than 20 years experience in sales management, marketing and customer service for companies that include AT&T, MCI, GTE, Tandy and Firestone. He has spoken in front of large audiences and live television and radio on subjects including public safety technology, child Internet safety, automobile safety and automobile technology.

Brent advocates carbon-neutral technologies and providing the safest possible place for our young drivers to evolve!
