Paul Mozell’s Summary

My goal is to find a position where I can utilize the marketing skills I developed during the course of my sales and photography careers. This includes writing for trade and consumer magazines, white papers, press releases, web and blog copy, design and management of web sites, management of vendors and print production, orchestration of trade show exhibits and training seminars.

I am also available for photography assignments and projects including: weddings, executive and family portraits, architecture, and editorial.

Paul Mozell’s Specialties:

Within the photo and imaging industries I have expertise in stock photography, digital minilabs, on-line imaging software, color management, scanning, and wide format printing solutions. I have sold market research on the industry, as well as imaging services, software, and hardware.
