Greg Reyneke’s Summary

Greg is a seasoned leader who can direct any organization in the visioning of a marketing strategy, development of an economic business case and execution of aggressive project plans. Skilled in effective sales and marketing communications, business partnership development, team building and mentoring. Expert in helping start-up companies reach their full potential while maximizing ROI.

Greg has practical long-term experience in ion exchange (SBA,WBA,SAC,WAC) and membrane separation technologies (RO, NF, & UF) from residential to industrial-scale applications.

Greg is a gifted consultant who is able to target the perfect solution to the problem, doing it right the first time - every time.
