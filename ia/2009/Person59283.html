Davi Squaiella’s Summary

I'm senior engineer with large experience in the design of medium / large electrical machines, especially, generators in hydro power plants business.

I started my career at Siemens in 1999, as an electrical / electromagnetic designer of medium size hydro power plants. After one and a half year I moved to Bandeirante Energia that was (at that time) the fourth major Brazilian transmission and distribution Company. Within the Planning Department I was able to develop software to evaluate the necessary investments in the low voltage distribution network. The theory and software led to the development of my Master Degree that was finished in 2004.

After three years at Bandeirante Energia it was given me the opportunity to return to the power plants business, in the joint ventured Voith-Siemens Hydro Power Generation. The new challenge was to design and specify all components at the proposal department with focus on the American and East European markets, where I worked for two and a half years, when a new opportunity appeared, that time in Germany where there was a need not only for a electromagnetic designer but also the mechanical one. The new experience gave me the chance to understand the complete technical business behind electrical machines. After two and a half years in Europe, gathering a lot of overseas experience, the opportunity to return to Brazil appeared through WEG Equipamentos Elétricos that is a major player in the Brazilian electrical machines and automation segments as also a Company that is now being known worldwide with major business in all continents.

Finally, after a ten years experience in the design of electrical machines a new professional challenge appeared through GE Motors / GEVISA (a GE Energy company), this time as a DMP Leader (Direct Material Productivity Leader). This new challenge consists on costs savings and new developments on electrical machines, keeping above all, GE's integrity and its products quality.

Davi Squaiella’s Specialties:

medium / large machines, electromagnetic and mechanical designs, vertical machines, windings, insulation systems.
