Pete Flint’s Summary

Co-Founder and CEO of Trulia.com - a real estate search engine. Trulia.com is one of the most popular real estate sites in the US and the fastest growing. We've got some big ideas, a great team and very excited about our company.

We are looking for great people to join us - please feel free to get in touch if your interesting in learning more or more at http://www.trulia.com/jobs .

Previously spent 5 years as a founding team member and Head of Global Business Development at lastminute.com. We built Europe's largest online travel company with over 2000 employees, over $1bn transactions and operations in 12 countries. Acquired in May 2005 by Travelocity (Sabre Holdings) for $1.1bn in cash.

Stanford MBA, Class of 2005. Undergraduate and Masters degree in Physics from Oxford (Magdalen College).

e-commerce, vertical search, real estate, venture capital, marketing, business development and entrepreneurship
