Simon White’s Summary

A professional with a breadth of national strategic, operational and technical experience working with education, government and private sectors. Proven ability to lead, recruit, manage and develop a diverse team of specialists in a rapidly changing policy environment. Flexible, proactive and a creative thinker with an eye for detail, seeking new challenges and opportunities.

KEY SKILLS
• Effective partnership working with a wide range of technical and executive contacts at national and local levels; including senior civil servants, private sector representatives and senior managers in education and local government.
• Implementing start-up teams and innovative projects. Programme and project management experience across the whole lifecycle, from proposal to delivery and review.
• Strategic planning and operational delivery of technical and policy development projects.
• Extensive understanding of policy in the education and wider public sector: points of influence, effective instruments and the role of stakeholder networks in creating change.
• Understanding complex and unbounded environments and making creative decisions.

ACHIEVEMENTS
• Recruited, established and developed the performance of a multi-disciplinary team to deliver a new project in education technology change.
• Created, specified and successfully bid for money from Defra which engaged young people with Climate Change issues through Web 2.0 technology.
• Delivered strategic and technical advice in support of broadband network connections for schools as part of a government national content delivery strategy.

ADDITIONAL SKILLS
Completed a PRINCE2 Foundation course and experienced in managing projects in formal and semi-formal environments.
Advanced IT/Technology knowledge. Covering specific implementations, business improvements and benefits and the emerging technology agenda.
