Chuck Rylant, MBA, CFP®’s Summary

Chuck Rylant specializes in fee-only, comprehensive personal financial planning and tax preparation to upper middle income families in Santa Maria, California. Many of Chuck Rylant's clients are public safety employees such as police officers and firefighters.

Chuck Rylant's clients have a wide range of needs that include: retirement planning and projections, small business planning, college funding, tax preparation and planning, estate planning and insurance needs.

Chuck Rylant's firm is unique because they do not charge commissions or have sales quotas. They only work on a retainer basis so clients are assured they receive objective advice that is in their best interest.
