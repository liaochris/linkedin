Jacqueline Stone’s Summary

I create tools for personal transformation, including self inquiry guides, mental and emotional monitoring guides, and transformative flash presentations.

My personal experience with violent and sexual abuse, and healing from it, has made me a valuable resource to those who are ready to leave victim-hood behind. My Recovering from Abuse blog and Healing from Abuse Squidoo group showcase my personal tools for empowerment.

I am a Reiki Master, in the Usui Shiki Ryoho tradition. Remote and hands-on Reiki treatments are available upon request.

I write about my spiritual and personal journies to assist others who may be on a similar path. In addition, I have a vast collection of resources to which I can refer others.

My goal is to continue the work I am currently doing while completing several hard-cover books that document my work with a personal approach.
