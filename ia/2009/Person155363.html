Matt Chisholm’s Summary

Matt has 10 years engineering experience in web-based technologies. Primary experience has been consulting with clients on project delivery (requirements gathering, product evaluation and recommendation, project planning, leading technical teams, designing and developing systems for clients) and supporting business development with proposal generation, client prospecting meetings, project definition and estimation.

Consulting projects have focused on implementing applications such as: social networks, sales force automation systems, product databases, learning management systems, retail eCommerce systems, content taxonomies, content management/publication systems, portal applications, and financial broker extranets.
