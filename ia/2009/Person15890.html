Antonio Saillant’s Summary

“A VISION QUEST,” “EXPERIENCE A NEW LIGHT IN FILM.”

PRODUCER - Antonio Saillant

Raised in New York City, and once a semi pro baseball player and a leading engineering professional, Saillant has lived and worked in New Jersey, New York,
Italy, the Dominican Republic but currently spends his time between New York and
Santa Monica, California. In 2006 he opened his own production company, Angel
Light Pictures, LLC, located in the heart of NYC theater district.

A long-time collaboration with his mentor, Ted Kotcheff, led to the idea of one day working together in producing upcoming film projects. Kotcheff, who directed many other films throughout the 1970s and 80s, most in the United States, with perhaps the best-known was being the Sylvester Stallone feature First Blood in 1982. In the 1990s Kotcheff returned to directing for television, working on various American series such as The Red Shoe Diaries and now Law & Order: Special Victims Unit as the director and executive producer. Saillant took his mentors advice and decided to change the motto to his company, Angel Light Pictures, "VISION QUEST,' EXPERIENCE A NEW LIGHT IN FILM," that gives inspired film makers the chance to make there dreams into reality. Entertainment has a new light, "Angel Light."

In 2008, Saillant partnered with Red Hot Entertainment, Stock's Eye and FMC Productions to package and co-produce films using European film funds.

In Addition, Angel Light Pictures, LLC goal is too become the leading multi- media entertainment company targeting the English-language Hispanic market. In partnership with top Latino directors and talent, Angel Light Pictures will produce in partnership with other production companies twelve to fifteen films over the next three years, establishing Angel Light Pictures as the hub for Latino diversity film in Hollywood.

Antonio Saillant’s Specialties:

Business Description

Angel Light Pictures, L.L.C. is an independent film production company. The company specializes in developing and producing quality motion pictures, documentaries, Internet media and television/cable industries for worldwide audiences.

Saillant has recently completed work on two SCI-FI's, Angel's Blade, followed by the sequel, Angel's Blade: The Ascension starring Corbin Bernson.

Antonio Saillant’s Education

    Masters and Bachelors of Science, Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering

    Antonio's realization of painful family struggles and personal confusion, led him to embrace the entertainment industry. He evolved through the varied characters he portrayed, which helped him to develop a more fulfilling and emotionally healthy life for himself. He found passion in the entertainment industry which led him away from engineering and carried him into motion pictures.
    While relishing his role both behind and in front of the camera, Antonio met Robert Stock, owner of Stock's Eye Productions, where he began his producing career with the Indie feature "Angel's Blade." Antonio utilized his business acumen to form Angel Light Pictures, LLC which would produce low cost/high quality Indie features. Antonio is on a mission to bring a new light to Indie films. His current project includes being executive producer in the horror thriller with Stock's Eye Production, Angel's Blade: The Ascension, starring Corbin Bernsen.

    Activities and Societies:
    Antonio is a proud defender of clean air technologies. He is a graduate of the New York Institute of Technology with a degree in Aerospace/Mechanical Engineering. He was a top Management Executive for over 15 years in the energy field, managing over $1 billion in both the private and public sector. Antonio's specialties included energy conservation, energy supply management and energy infrastructure.
