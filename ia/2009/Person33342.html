Brian Geyser’s Summary

Brian is a clinician, consultant, educator, blogger, and serial entrepreneur with over 20 years experience in health care and psychiatry. Currently, Brian runs Carenetworks.com, a social technology company that offers online community and social media solutions to the senior living and eldercare industries. Brian founded Carenetworks in September of 2008.

Carenetworks enables senior living, eldercare service providers, and other care-related organizations to create a public or private branded online community for patients, residents, care givers, family members, staff, referral sources, and business partners; allowing them to interact via member profiles, blogs, forums, event calendars, private messages and other tools. Members can share and aggregate care-related news and information, stories, tips, products, and multimedia content from across the Web.

Carenetworks provides companies with a simple and cost-effective solution to the complexities of online communities, interactive marketing, real-time Websites, and social media; and provides a comprehensive way to organize and manage a company's online presence across multiple channels like Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin and others. It's the easiest way for organizations to leverage the power of the social Web to engage customers and prospects and provide them with 24-hour access to helpful information and support.

In addition to running Carenetworks, Brian lectures around the country on a variety of topics and practices emergency psychiatry at Yale-New Haven Hospital.
