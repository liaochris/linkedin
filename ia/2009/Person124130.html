Julie Sharpe’s Summary

Sharpe Recruitment is a North East based consultancy with offices in Middlesbrough, dedicated to placing professionals in rewarding jobs in Newcastle, Durham, Sunderland, Middlesbrough and beyond.

We cover a variety of disciplines including Business Management, Digital Media, Creative, PR, Marketing and IT.

Run by mother and daughter team Julie and Laura Sharpe, we aim to provide an efficient, reliable and trustworthy service.

We make it our goal everyday to always maintain high levels of customer service and make a real difference to our Candidates careers and our Client's businesses.

Julie Sharpe’s Specialties:

Turning Recruitment on it's Head!
I am not only passionate about the business of finding candidates the best jobs, but passionate about dealing with clients and candidates in a positive and professional way to build and maintain relationships within the creative industry here in the North East
