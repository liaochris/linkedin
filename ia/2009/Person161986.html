Michael Preiss’s Summary

A seasoned professional in enterprise business architecture, business simplification, business process design and business process improvement consulting in several industries, Michael has two professional passions. The first is improving business performance by analyzing, addressing, and rectifying issues surrounding organizational structure, business processes, and technologies deployed. The second is innovation - establishing it and fostering it within companies large and small.

Michael Preiss’s Specialties:

Enterprise Business Simplification; Enterprise Business Process Design; Enterprise Business Process Improvement; Enterprise Business Process Internationalization; Formalized process mapping and modeling methodologies; Workshop Facilitation.
