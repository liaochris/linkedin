Mercedes Cordero, Ph.D.’s Summary

A seasoned professional in the education industry, Mercedes has broad experience in all aspects of operations and sales. As an Area Manager, Mercedes managed a team of up to eight field consultants across a nine-state territory. In addition to management skills and responsibilities, consultative areas of expertise included school improvement, professional development, and school /program evaluation. Within a sales role, Mercedes has consistently proven herself to be a top performer, meeting or exceeding sales quotas. As a Regional Director, Executive Director and Regional VP of Sales, Mercedes has had primary responsibility for opening up new markets and covered large, regional territories. In her current role at CaseNEX, a dynamic online learning company, Mercedes is responsible for expanding CaseNEX's partnership base across the southern United States. Mercedes has succeeded in orchestrating innovative online and blended professional development solutions with state, district, and university partners. She is committed to positioning CaseNEX as THE premier online professional development company, bringing the benefits of CaseNEX's high quality, case-based learning to many more teachers and administrators nationwide.
