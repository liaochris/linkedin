Atif Ashraf’s Summary

A certified Physician & Diabetologist with strong Clinical, Analytical, Administrative, Managerial, and IT Expertise. For the last eight years, I have led various ER departments, and Family Clinics, providing patient satisfaction, hospital benefits in terms of repute & standing in the society, & ease to the senior staff faculty. I have demonstrated success in managing patients at my own whether in the ER, OPD, or ICU.

My skills include strong hands-on technological use in medicine, the basic & advanced clinical assessment skills, lab & radiology interpretation, clinical methods, emergency and elective procedures like ABGs and LP, and patient record maintenance specifically for health maintenance of my patients. I am also highly skilled in medical record application development and enhancements.

Furthermore, I have proven the ability to manage patients in disaster and emergency situations like earthquake, and bomb blasts. I possess a solid track record for leading and running my team within the department, satisfying patients to have the best of compliance, and bringing benefit to the institution I have worked with.

Highly Interested in Vascular Medicine covering Cardiology, Obesity, & Diabetes as the major spectrum; looking after the complications with an attempt to preventive measures; dealing with acute emergencies; sustaining critical care patients, & health maintenance. Also pursuing the establishment of the Health Records, both for In-patients and Out-Patients, and developing policies that would bring benefit to the patients, doctors, hospitals, and the society in general.
