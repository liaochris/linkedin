David Crislip’s Summary

- Translated scripts and related text for dozens of popular games including Dead Rising, Okami, and entries in popular franchises such as Resident Evil, Street Fighter, and Mega Man.
- Extensive experience translating game design documents, business contracts, and other internal documentation.
- Extensive experience interpreting and facilitating at both external and internal meetings (both J-E and E-J).
- Extensive experience interpreting at media interviews, press events, and other external functions.
- Currently working as an Assistant Producer in Capcom's Online R&D Division in Tokyo and affiliated with 3 high profile titles in varying capacities as well as producing dozens of smaller online distributed titles in conjunction with first parties.
- Appeared in an interview published in a December, 2007 issue of Weekly Famitsu magazine, Japan's premier video game news source, in an article about the blurring line between Western and Japanese games.
- Was interviewed by 1UP.com in 2006 regarding the localization of the then impending release of the critically acclaimed Clover Studio developed title, Okami.
