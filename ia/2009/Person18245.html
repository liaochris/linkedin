Antonio Luise’s Summary

Currently Managing Director of Sagres Advisory SA, advisor of five funds of hedge funds, among them one of the first ever China focused Fund of Hedge Funds. Responsibilities include managing and expanding the business, managing existing clients.
Also Chairman and founder of Brown Editore Spa, editor of www.finanza.com and www.finanzaonline.com, among the leaders in the online financial information arena. In 2001 the focus has been switched to content providing for banks and editors.
Former journalist at Bloomberg Investimenti.
Writes articles for Il Corriere della Sera on Hedge Funds
