Mike Guilbault, MPA, APPO’s Summary

Accredited by the Professional Photographers of Canada for Commercial photography, Portrait & Wedding photography. Awarded the Master of Photographic Arts and recipient of numerous Best in Class awards for Architecture Photography and other categories.

Director of PhotographyWorkshops.ca and instructor/educator of photography workshops, seminars and courses.

Consultant to corporations requiring assistance in setting up their own photography department, or to educate staff on photographic technique and processes.
