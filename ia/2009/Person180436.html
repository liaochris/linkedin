Per Palmkvist Knudsen’s Summary

Per Palmkvist Knudsen, MSc, is CIO at JP/Politikens Hus. During the last 25 years, he has worked with all aspect of IT. Through his career, managing change has been the common denominator.

Per Palmkvist Knudsen’s Specialties:

Managing change, Strategic partnerships, Creating business results

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Newspapers industry)

    August 2007 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    JP/Politikens Hus is one of Denmark’s leading media corporations.

    Among it's publications are three national newspapers: Ekstra Bladet, Jyllands-Posten and Politiken.

    The corporation’s undertakings also include book publishing, printing, local newspapers in Denmark and Sweden, the free morning paper 24timer, TV-production as well as multimedia activities.

    Every year JP/Politikens Hus prints 150 million newspapers, 120 million free morning papers and it's newspaper websites have 250 million hits. Also, 55 million local newspapers are printed in Denmark and Sweden, and the corporation’s publishing houses issue 1.7 million books.

    JP/Politikens Hus A/S was founded January 1st 2003 as a result of the merger of Politikens Hus and Jyllands-Posten.
