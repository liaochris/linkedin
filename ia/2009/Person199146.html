Christina Cheung, CIC, CRM, SPHR’s Summary

My past training and experience have uniquely prepared me to be an expert in the field of risk management and transfer.

I began my career in the field of Human Resources (HR). Early on my career, I completed the “HR Management” certification from UC Berkeley and finished the “Training and Human Resources Development” certification from UC Santa Cruz. In 1993, I became certified by Society for Human Resouces Management as Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR). One of my jobs involved in supporting a fast growing company from 50 to 500 employees. On the HR side, I was part of the senior management team in developing corporate strategies and trainings. I developed and implemented HR policies to facilitate organizational growth. Moreover, I arranged outside speakers and also conducted management trainings and team building workshops. On the risk transfer side, I introduced and implemented partial self-funding contracts to lower the high cost of insurance.

My career path switched to insurance work in 1994. I have equipped myself continually through professional continuous education and learning from mentors. I now hold licenses in Property & Casualty, Life insurance, and Security and have a designation of Certified Insurance Counselor (CIC) and Certified Risk Manager (CRM). I have expertise in the evaluation of insurance contracts and transference. I have extensive experience working in high growth environment. Under my care, three successful mergers and acquisitions took place. I also have extensive experience working with global companies. I was instrumental in settlement of an Amsterdam claim for close to $1M.

In addition, to the aforementioned credentials, I also have extensive experience working with non-profits and tech industry. I am confident that I could be your choice strategic partner in managing business risks, including but not limited to transfer of risks through insurance
