Jim Fitzpatrick (Jim@FitzpatrickCareers.com)’s Summary

Sr. Technical Recruiter with 20+ years experience in the Wireless & Wireline, Voice & Data Communications, Semiconductor, Connector & Power Electronics industries. Primarily recruit within the US, but also conduct international searches as well.

Areas of specialization include, but are not limited to:

Hardware Design, Test and Verification Engineering: High Speed Design, Signal Integrity, EMI/EMC, ASIC, SoC, ASSP, SRAM, DRAM, ROM, Flash, NVM, Memory I/O, Microprocessor, various Analog IC (PLL, OpAmp, BandGap, Amps, ADC, DAC, Filters, Comparators, etc.), RFIC, Mixed Signal IC, Custom Circuit, SerDes, PCIe, TxRx, DFT, DSP, Power IC, Motor Control, Motor Design, Patent Engineering, IP, Technical Sales/Marketing, Applications Engineering, etc.

Software Design: Embedded Real Time C, Assembly, C++, RTOS, VxWorks, pSOS, Windows, Linux, UNIX, TCP/IP, VoIP, SNMP, Protocols, Device Drivers, etc.

Mechanical Design: Chip Packaging, Consumer/Commercial Electro-Mechanical Product Design (ProE), Plastics, Sheet Metal, Castings, High Speed Interconnect, Connectors, Backplanes, Handsets, Cabinets, Housings, etc.

Wireless: Cellular, 3G, 4G, WiFi, WiMax, OFDM, MIMO, Antenna, 802.11x, 802.16x, DSP, etc.

Intellectual Property: Portfolio Managers, Licensing Analysts, Staff Engineers, Patent Engineers, Patent Attorneys, Patent Agents, etc.
