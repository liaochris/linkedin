Robert Zorich’s Summary

Accomplished Operations, Quality New Process and Product/Engineering Manager and Manufacturing/Process Engineer with a broad base of experience in lean manufacturing, marketing, research and operations. Six Sigma Blackbelt specializing in New Product Development, patents and IP law. Decisive leader who has brought many products to market from concept to release. Experience in bringing companies to ISO-9000 certification, using Lean, 5S and similar techniques. Drove business unit from $2.8MM to $25MM business over 5 years. Professionally trained in leadership by the Summit Organization and the Center for Creative Leadership. Responsibilities included planning, budgeting, resource allocation, scheduling, Sr. Management Reviews, driving toxicology studies on new chemistries, performing HazOp, FMEA reviews. Meets or exceeds time, capacity and quality targets. Encourages a culture of excellence with enthusiasm and direct no-nonsense approach.

Experience in many fields including MEMS, OEM equipment, Sensors, Chemical manufacturing, digital and analog IC manufacturing.
