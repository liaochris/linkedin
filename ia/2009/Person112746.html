Jill Lemon’s Summary

I am a self-proclaimed connector-- I get pure satisfaction aligning myself and my community with other great contacts, client and products. In my six years of sales and recruiting, I've grown to appreciate the term "six degrees of separation" and exactly how small Richmond, VA (and the world!) really is. I am a recruiter by nature but, really, I enjoy selling people, products and services that I'm passionate about. More importantly, I love affiliating myself with companies that I believe in. My professional experience expands beyond recruiting to include career consulting, public speaking, and relationship building to communication liasion (aka I like to talk a lot), networker, volunteer and Mom!
