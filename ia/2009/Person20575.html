Artur Severo’s Summary

During the last 10 years I had the opportunity to work with different initiatives focused on communications market

I hold a M.Sc. degree in Electronic Engineer and Computer Science from Aeronautics Technological Institute (ITA). I also hold a B.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineer received from Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS). My research and professional interests are communications over packets networks, with focus on VoIP, and multimedia communications.
