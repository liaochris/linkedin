Steve Shepherd’s Summary

Steve Shepherd, originally from Jacksonville, Florida, has maintained his current residence in Gainesville, Florida since May of 1990. He has been heavily involved in the North Central Florida, Gainesville, Florida area business and university communities throughout most of his professional career.

Steve has over twenty years of experience working exclusively in the printing and publishing industries. He graduated from the University of Florida College of Fine Arts, School of Art + Art History in 1984 with a degree in graphic design. Steve has worked over the years as a: graphic designer, photographer, art director, print production director, printing consultant, principle in a design agency, and as a publisher. Steve became the Director of Sales & Marketing for Alta Systems, Inc., a North Central Florida printing and mailing facility, in July of 2002. This latest opportunity, at Alta Systems, Inc., followed a two-year stint as a Publisher for Naylor Publications, Inc.

From 1991 to 2003, Steve taught senior-level college courses, as an adjunct professor, in graphic design, printing methods, visual communications and printing/publishing professional practice at Flagler College in St. Augustine, Florida, Santa Fe Community College and the University of Florida in Gainesville, Florida.

Aside from his printing and publishing ties, Steve continues to work diligently within the North Central Florida area, serving on several university, association and community boards. Steve is a current member of the Florida Public Relations Association—Gainesville, Florida Chapter, the City of Gainesville Office of Marketing and Communications Task Force—Executive Committee, Danny Wuerffel’s Gators for Kids Sake golf tournament—Board, the Postal Customer Council Executive Board—Industry Chair, Friends of the University Gallery—Chair, the Gainesville Advertising Federation, the Rotary Club of Gainesville, and the Oak Hall Boosters Club—Vice President, to name a few.
