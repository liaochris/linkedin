Jeanne Fu’s Summary

I want to be a part of a firm where curiosity, innovation, and engagement are not only encouraged, but required. My solid management experiences make me an ideal Interactive Marketing Manager.

As International Director of Marketing at Distance Education Co., I was responsible for all marketing strategy, planning, and buying for the company's brands (New York Institute of Photography and Sheffield School of Interior Design) and managed the Internet Search campaign for the parent company's Canadian subsidiary (PCDI Canada). I supervised employees in other departments (Production, IT, and Operations) who reported to me concerning the logistics, tracking, delivery, and creative involved in all of our advertising and promotional activities. On the strategy side, I determined the profitability and risk of new revenue-building and marketing initiatives by building financial models based on the continuous tracking of the performance of our various marketing campaigns. On the planning side, I generated the company's budget for the fiscal year and was responsible for forecasting and delivering revenue, sales, and profit targets for all products.

As Marketing Manager, I expanded the company's brand visibility and market presence through the development of a number of viral initiatives such as an Online Photo Contest and YouTube production which garnered 2.2m views.

My academic endeavors were fueled by a passion for learning, subsequently motivating me to begin language studies in French, German, Spanish and Ancient Greek. I have the same ambition and drive with respect to a career as a Marketing Executive in the education industry.
