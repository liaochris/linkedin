Sanjay Goel’s Summary

Serial Entrepreneur by Nature.

As a co-founder and COO of the company. Sanjay brings together over 10 years of business acumen to the company. After completion of his post-graduate techno-management course from NIFT, he ventured into entrepreneurship by setting up his own export business by name of M/s Ethnic India. During his aegis M/s Ethnic India got national award for the highest growth from Government of India. During 8 years of his entrepreneurship, he has widely traveled across the globe to understand and gain the rich global culture and created contacts with numerous international clients in over 20 countries . Finally sold off complete business to the Top Competitor (company still running successfully among top five in India in tis domain) few years back to pursue his love for technology business.

Then , he co-founded Srishti TechNet Private Limited , with simple objective to develop such software 'product' that should simplify today's prosumers life and simplify their lifestyle.

Today his main role in ‘Srishti TechNet’ is to look after the Marketing and selling arrangements. He has also been involved in setting up the objectives of company to selection of human resources; to make and execute product strategy; to ensure optimum utilization of resources and to devise long-term implementation planning for the firm.

He is presently responsible for the development and execution of organization’s business strategies, market positioning, branding and client relationship management.
