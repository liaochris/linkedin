Gail Hurt’s Summary

Gail founded Living Authentically to deliver career management services to professionals who want more success and authenticity in their careers and lives. Gail is designated as a Professional Career Architect (PCA) by the National Society of Career Management (http://nsocm.com).This designation is given to
people who have business and career management expertise and completed extensive training and practice in career development and management.

Her approach to career management and development is based not only on her training and education, but more importantly by significant hands on business experience.

As a Project Management Professional (PMP), her expertise in producing consistent results managing complex technology programs and projects informs her philosophy in career advancement and management - successful career management is a series of projects or assignments that produce recognized value. Her education and experience, gives her a formal body of knowledge and an elegant system for delivering career services.

Gail is a certified LikeWorkAgain coach. http://likeworkagain.com/

Prior to starting her own company, Gail had a thirty year career in business, successfully managing and mentoring people while managing large complex technology projects - a career that provided the opportunities to constantly learn and evolve.

Her passion is helping people ignite and achieve their potential and organizations reach their goals. She is adept at helping cancer survivors with their career goals and issues - being a 14 year survivor of inflammatory breast cancer.
