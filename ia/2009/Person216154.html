Sheila Farragher-Gemma’s Summary

A graduate of Trinity College Dublin, Sheila Farragher-Gemma moved to the states in 1989 to pursue a corporate position. Her entrepreneurial nature soon took over and she founded and profitably sold several businesses before settling into the world of real estate where she and her business partner Jeremy Shapiro launched a multi-million dollar company helping investors find out about and purchase niche properties in addition to acquiring multi-family investment properties in North American and European emerging markets.

Sheila Farragher-Gemma’s Specialties:

As one of the most recognized authorities on the foreclosure market, Sheila has routinely counseled professionals on the ins-and-outs of the foreclosure process and how to succeed in real estate. Her role as an industry expert has been highlighted by her appearances on CBS Nightly News, FOX 25, New England Cable News, WGBH TV and WBUR radio.
