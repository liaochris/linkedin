Ed Tatum’s Summary

With an extensive background in Merchant Banking, Commercial Finance, Residential Finance and Insurance, Ed believes that the bigger the challenge, the bigger the reward. Having over 12 years in Consultative Sales and Business Development has given him the innate ability to recognize potential in the business world.

Being a recognized leader with major organizations such as First Data Corporation, CENTEX, Francorp and Wells Fargo Bank has helped him to develop this ability. As an entrepreneur, he has also built a successful Mortgage and Insurance Brokerage that has helped countless families attain and secure their dream home or business.

His goal is to continue to give sound professional advice and continue to grow his network of clients by being a trusted advisor.

Ed Tatum’s Specialties:

Residential Mortgages, Commercial Finance, Commercial and Residential Insurance Products, Franchise Consultation, Debt Settlement, Residential Loan Modification
