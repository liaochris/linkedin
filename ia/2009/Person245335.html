Kent Wakeford’s Summary

Over 12 years of entertainment, media and interactive experience, including running a digital media company, leading sales / business development teams, general counsel at leading social media company, and attorney at national law firm. Expertise in structuring and negotiating all aspects of digital media partnerships, advising on digital and mobile distribution strategies, developing and facilitating revenue generating relationships, venture financing, and acquisitions of digital / social media companies and assets.

Developed over four hundred relationships in the digital and social media space with leading media companies, sports leagues, social networks, blogging platforms, search engines, widget developers, application providers, video aggregators, online music sites, ecommerce sites, travel booking engines, video advertising networks, white label social networking platforms, photo sharing sites, bookmarking services, and payment platforms.

Negotiated investments and acquisitions of digital media companies and websites. Completed equity investments and/or warrant issuance in fourteen digital media companies. Sourced and negotiated the acquisition of four community-based online media properties. Helped negotiate and facilitate venture financing including angel investments, Series A and Series C investments. Lead legal negotiator for the acquisition of the leading online bookmarking platform.

Kent Wakeford’s Specialties:

Developing digital media strategies and partnerships with emphasis on online content syndication, cross-platform marketing relationships, revenue generating advertising and ecommerce partnerships, remnant advertising solutions, mobile distribution, and affiliate agreements. Sourcing, evaluating and structuring the sale and acquisition of digital media assets and companies.
