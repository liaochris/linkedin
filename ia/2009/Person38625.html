Carrie Goldstein’s Summary

Carrie Goldstein is President of Impact Image, a boutique public relations firm that focuses on healthcare, physician and non-profit public relations campaigns. Carrie’s experience stems from working with top public relations’ agencies on national pharmaceutical and consumer healthcare campaigns. Her expertise includes overseeing the execution of comprehensive programs including providing strategic counsel, day-to-day project management, media relations, campaign material development, event planning and managing advocacy and spokesperson relationships.

Carrie is available for integrated project management into existing PR teams, editorial services and campaigns for mid-size businesses, healthcare, medical offices and non-profit organizations.

Carrie Goldstein’s Specialties:

Carrie works on healthcare PR campaigns including from consumer-focused campaigns to hospital-based diseases, FDA approvals and pre-launch campaigns, Rx to OTC switches, national and local media tours, building advocacy relationships and celebrity awareness campaigns.
