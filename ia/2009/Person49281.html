Corey Wales’s Summary

Leaders with the ability to see what needs to be done and the strength to act on their vision are hard to find. I am that type of leader. I worked in the Hickman Mills School District from 2001 through July 2008 and served as the Director of Technology for the last 3 years. Under my leadership as the Director of Technology the District:

• Converted to a new web based student information system
• Placed a computer on every teacher’s desk
• Automated data transfer between District data systems
• Reorganized the Technology Department to increase efficiency
• Requested and received the largest technology budget in the history of Hickman Mills
• Implemented a new technology plan
• Lit Fiber to all schools
• Installed the District’s first storage area network and centralized servers
• Installed a new surveillance system
• Improved communication with Blackberries for Administrators and Parent Portal for parents.

The accomplishments listed above were not achieved without a great deal of cooperation, communication and vision. The ability to reach consensus and a shared vision with customers/colleagues along with ‘big picture’ thinking are strengths that I bring to the organization.
