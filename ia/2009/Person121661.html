Jos Keuter’s Summary

Enterpreneur and software architect in the area of Operations Management.

Our innovative and powerfull framework, the Dynarics Foundation, empowers our application modules that can improve and support your business processes on many areas.

New! Dynarics Outsourcing: faster state of the art results and sharp pricing. We combine our own technical enabler (the Dynarics Foundation which guarantees the quality and stability of results) with the sharp rates of our highly qualified and certified outsourcing partners. Dynarics manages project related and architectural aspects and handles communication with developers from our partners onsite and/or abroad.

Communication is the critical success factor for what concerns outsourcing abroad. Our experience with this and the fact that we have a technical framework which reduces most common complexities (and thus communication complexities) makes Dynarics the ultimate partner for your outsourcing activities.

Feel free to contact us at info@dynarics.com. More details: www.dynarics.com (dutch only at the moment but available in more languages soon).
