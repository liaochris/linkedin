Gary Borelli’s Summary

Galaxy press is a full service commercial printer with both conventional and digital printing with variable data capabilities, in-house fulfillment and mailing expertise and the first Certified Green Printer in the S.F. East Bay.

Tom Meyer - President
Tom has been printing since he was a freshman in high school (1974), and is the driving force behind the company. If it’s not good enough for Tom, it’s not good enough for you.

Bob Meyer - Vice President
Bob has been printing since he was a sophomore in high school (1969). He received a degree in computer electronics in 1976 and has earned the reputation in electronic pre-press of being the guy to call when the going gets tough and the solution needs to be fast and right.

Gary Borelli - Production Manager/Esitmator
Gary has over 20 years of experience ranging from carton manufacturing to fine art reproduction. He finds the most economical way to get a quality job done on time and within budget.
