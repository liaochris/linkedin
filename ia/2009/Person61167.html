DeLegal Group’s Summary

DeLegal Group, LLC was founded in 1997 by Linda DeLegal as sole proprietor and operator. We have quickly grown to a full service team of virtual bookkeepers and office support professionals. We are located in Port Charlotte, Florida.

Outsourcing is a cost savings path that many successful and established companies are now following. The scope of our business has expanded to provide outsourced bookkeeping and office support services. Our combined years in the industry have given us a broad range of accounting experience with various business sectors including attorneys, manufacturers, restaurants, condominium associations, contractors, retail shops, real estate, and more.

DeLegal Group’s Specialties:

Accounts payable, accounts receivable, statement reconciliation, payroll, financial statements, account set-up, spreadsheet design and maintenance, word processing, bulk mail, database research and set up, business card and marketing materials design, event planning, much more.
