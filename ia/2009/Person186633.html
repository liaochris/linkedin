Demonstrates extremely strong strategic and analytical skills with a proven track record in business start-ups, the ability to enter new markets, build relationships, motivate teams, develop business and exceed goals. Exceptional P&L and cost control expertise. A leader with a passion for identifying strategic alliances to build and convert market opportunity into new business revenue streams.

• Exceptional sales, marketing, customer service and decision-making skills
• Ability to enter new markets and make an impact
• Experience with entrepreneurial business start-up
• Experience working for Fortune 1000 companies
• Extremely strong B2B and B2C experience
• Ability to develop both long and short-term growth strategies
• Results-oriented, decisive, can-do attitude
