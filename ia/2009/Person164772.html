Mike Rampling’s Summary

I am a very experienced and delivery-focused Project Manager. I have worked in IT for many years at all levels and in many different roles. I have solid early Consultancy experience, followed by a freelance career in which I have worked for many major companies, primarily in Project Management and Project Leading; I also have strong exposure to the “hands-on” side including Data Analysis and Modeling, Business Analysis, and Development.
I have well-established skills in stakeholder management, communication, requirements gathering, case-building and planning and control. I am committed to early establishment of project scope and objectives, to ensure that everyone involved understands the project aims and rationale. I am strong in the areas of Risk, Issue and Change Management. I am very quality and delivery-focused, and experienced with PRINCE2, and with other methodologies. I have delivered on assignments with clients such as Dell Computer and RBS, so I have shown my ability to get the job done under pressure. I have managed concurrent projects on several occasions.
Personally, I am practical and pragmatic, pro-active, persistent and patient. I am comfortable dealing with people at all levels of the organisation, and value communication highly. I am very experienced in running project meetings at all levels.
I have followed PRINCE2 principles in all my PM assignments, and am PRINCE2-certified. As a full member of the Association for Project Management I also have the Body of Knowledge (PMBoK) in my toolset.
My knowledge and experience qualifies me for Project Assurance and PMO roles also.

Mike Rampling’s Specialties:

Risk and Issue Management, Project Planning Monitoring and Control, Stakeholder Management, Change Management, Data Analysis and Modelling, Business and Process Analysis.
