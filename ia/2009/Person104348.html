Jan Wognum’s Summary

I believe in the combination of economics & business science with psychology. Ratio cannot do without gut feeling. IQ without EQ is poor. Facts & figures are worth more when combined with intuition and experience. Leadership needs knowledge of human behaviour. Action needs reflection and growth is always accompanied by resistance.
I'm good in the combination of this all and help managers to build their team and develop the leadership skills of the manager at the same time (coaching on the job).

As a professional certified coach (NOBCO / EMCC), mediator (NMI), changemanager, trainer, facilitator and good in building teams, I work mostly in change- and leadership programs.
Besides my passion is 1:1 coaching, group coaching and I like to facilitate conflicts and poor communication in groups so they learn how they can do it themselves.

In the future I want to write at least 2 books
