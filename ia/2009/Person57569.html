David Girolami’s Summary

David Girolami brings an extensive media background to all his endeavors. His experience began with an education in Media Studies specializing in Radio Broadcasting News and Production at the dawn of analog to digital and never looked back. David's professional experience includes positions in radio production at Sound Source Networks (a division of Standard Broadcasting), production coordination and project management at Livewire Communications and the creation of interactive campaigns with TBWA Advertising. Dave's extensive background in new media production, planning and management enables him to provide tremendous value through leadership and guidance to all initiatives. In addition, David was a part-time Faculty instructor for 2 years at the Art institute of Toronto were he gave back to the community by instructing the finer issues of Law in the Graphic Arts and Professional Practices.
