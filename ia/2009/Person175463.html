Nikiki Bogle’s Summary

Entertainment law, corporate immigration, international, nonprofit formation and criminal appeals attorney with substantial negotiation and trial expertise. Conducted over 100 trials as lead counsel.

Focus on music contracts, music publishing, literary contracts, copyright, trademark, researching and drafting federal and state criminal appeals and negotiating successful deals for clients.

Nikiki Bogle’s Specialties:

Entertainment Law (music, literary, film, multimedia), Corporate Immigration, National Interest Waivers, Family Immigration, Violence Against Women Act (VAWA) Petitions and Deportation Defense, International, Nonprofit Formation and Governance (including 501(c)(3) application), Criminal Trials and Criminal Appeals.
