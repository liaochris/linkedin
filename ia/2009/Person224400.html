Steve Cooper’s Summary

Steve is a highly decorated 18-year veteran (active and reserve) of the United States Army where he served as an Infantryman and Military Police Officer. Some highlights of his distinguished military career are: he developed the first Department of Army police academy shortly after 9-11 that currently serves as the Department of Defense model; taught and established the first military police school on the west coast which received the highest possible accreditation; during his last assignment, he taught and managed several leadership and instructor training courses at a leadership academy, all of which he lead through full accreditation, unprecedented growth and innovation; and, he developed a degree completion program for Army instructors based on prior learning and training. Mr. Cooper also enjoyed a prolific academic career where he served in various roles such as professor, director of education as well as university president and chief executive officer. He has worked with the best teams to build for-profit, online universities with a combined market cap greater than one billion dollars. Mr. Cooper has edited two books on criminology.
