Hilary Weber’s Summary

I am fortunate to have a fulfilling strategic marketing career, ranging from traditional brand marketing to online marketing, including cutting-edge social media, with experience in industry-leading organizations across health care, publishing, and high tech. My deepest expertise includes integrated online/offline brand marketing; high-response direct marketing and sales lead generation; large-scale, segmented email programs; robust paid search (SEM) and organic search (SEO) programs; marketing communications; and new and emerging e-marketing and social media technologies.

My undergraduate fine arts degree has added a rich source of creativity to my graduate work (MBA from the Haas School of Business, U.C. Berkeley), fostering an advantageous “whole brain” approach to business challenges, with outcomes that consistently exceed stakeholders' expectations. I credit a large part of my ongoing success to this unique right brain/left brain blended approach.

My passion centers on making a significant, positive difference in the world, and I focus my energy and skills on this goal not only in my professional domain, but in all aspects of my life, whenever possible.

Effective team management is one of my key strengths; highly productive teams can attain substantial, sustainable results which benefit both the organization and the team members. I am a respected thought leader among my peers and associates, and my consultative approach to strategic innovation in marketing has had a measurable impact on the organizations I have served.

I enjoy all aspects of communication, from internal exchanges of new ideas to sharing best practices with colleagues across industries. I have written articles and guest blog posts for several trade publications, and have spoken at numerous conferences and industry events. I enjoy words themselves: written, spoken and sung – personal blogging, reading and music are three of my favorite diversions.
