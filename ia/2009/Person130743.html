Kevin Brooks’s Summary

Someone who makes things happen right out of the gate. Created an entirely new product category, added more than $25 million to an enterprise sales pipeline and unified a corporate brand across international regions following a series of mergers and acquisitions. Cut teeth in analyst relations managing the successful turnaround of a high profile negative report affecting both valuation and sales. Early adopter and proponent of social networking for business benefit.
