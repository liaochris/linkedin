Proven ability to work collaboratively within a team; sensitivity to local needs and cultural norms; demonstrated relational and problem-solving skills applied in a multi-cultural environment and an ability to communicate effectively, both verbally and in writing, with all levels. Fully acquainted with EC procedures.
