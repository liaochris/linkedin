Karen Bechtel’s Summary

Karen Bechtel is a graphic specialist who has owned and operated her own business for 26 years. She has recently located to Ohio from California and has clients throughout the United States.

Karen specializes in Graphic Design and has been involved with the industry since 1981. Karen has worked with professional clients from large corporations and small business owners alike, to further their business goals.

In 1989 Karen became fascinated with the effects of naturalistic healing on the body. She believes in healing emotional and physical issues through holistic channels as much as possible while still relying on standard medicine whenever indicated. She is largely influenced by her parents who both graduated from Johns Hopkins – her mother, a dedicated nurse and her father who became one of the most renowned Anesthesiologists of his time. Her family’s medical background represents a thread she feels is “in her blood.”

She is extremely passionate about helping other people learn to take care of themselves.

Karen has affected thousands of people throughout the U.S. by teaching personal growth courses. Now, she is turning her focus toward the physical wellness arena.

She is also focused on magnifying her design opportunities and reaching out to a more personal clientele.

Karen regards your goals as very important. She'll do all it takes to support them. And she surrounds herself with like minded and supportive individuals in every field!

Karen Bechtel’s Specialties:

Website development, Corporate Identities, Digital Imaging, Print and Online Marketing. Personal and Emotional Encouragement, Dream Building. Applications Specialties: Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver.
