Layla Abramowitz’s Summary

Creative fashion designer with international professional background, Experience with branding and art direction, technically knowledgeable, hands on, focusing on women's wear apparel design with experience in lingerie, sweaters, cut&sew knits, couture, corsets, contemporary, bridge, and luxury collections.

*Knitwear training in England
*Couture training from Paris and NY
*Artistic training in NY and Florence
*Factory experience in China

Layla Abramowitz’s Specialties:

Expertise in sweater design: knowledge of v-bed, dubied, circular, fully fashioned, jacquard, intarsia, whole-garment, seamless, & gauge-less technology; Knowledge of stitches, yarns, layouts, and graphs.

*Wovens: pattern development, draping, construction, sewing, couture techniques.

*Computer skills: Photoshop, Illustrator, Excel, Powerpoint, Quicken.

*Strong in illustration, hand sketching, tech packs, and specs.

*Fluent in French, Italian; proficient in Mandarin Chinese.
