Jane Jerome’s Summary

Feather your nest, sustainably.

Green Wren is building choices for a sustainable world.

The company offers a range of green building materials to architects, builders, contractors and other specifiers in the Silicon Valley Region doing new construction or a remodeling/retrofit project, whether commercial or residential.

Green Wren currently distributes Eco Stucco, ELT Living Wall and Roof products, and Rainscreen FSC cladding systems. Additional green product lines may be added soon.

Jane Jerome’s Specialties:

Broad and deep knowledge of green building industry, solar power, and environmental issues. Effective knowledge of product procurement, analysis of cost/benefits, construction industry information, governmental impacts.
