Berrie Pelser’s Summary

After I finished school in 1990 I had to go in the army where I trained to work in a field hospital. I started working with ITS (Immuno Technology Services) in 1992 where I was responsible for the Packaging and Materials department. I completed several Management and Computer studies that time. ITS became Nichols Institute Diagnostics where I worked as a materials manager. I decided to take a job at US Bioscience in Nijmegen where I was hired to build a European Distribution Center. I worked at clean room level.

In 1996 I received an offer from Amgen Europe B.V. in Breda. I decided to take the offer and started at Amgen. At Amgen I build a European Package Development department from scratch, I traveled to the several European countries but especially to the headquarters of Amgen Inc in CA USA to study al the necessary software, hardware and infrastructure and to learn all about the Package Development Department. I took the knowledge with me to the Netherlands and build the department. During that time I did several different (Software, DTP, WebDesign and QA and GMP) courses and seminars all over the world.

In 1998 I started Ber|Art Visual Design (www.ber-art.nl) and made designs and developed websites. Soon my customers wanted Hosting as well so I became a hosting reseller next to the design work. I hired a designer so I could learn more about hosting, DNS etc. etc. At this moment we host and manage our own cabinets with collocated, (PCI compliant) dedicated and application servers. We host ± 2500 domain names and we deliver and develop specialized application hosting like Magento, Wordpress and Typo3.

Berrie Pelser’s Specialties:

Running, building and managing my Internet Service Provider (ISP) Web Hosting and Web Design company Ber|Art Visual Design (ber-art.nl) , Webwinkelhost (webwinkelhost.nl) and WordPresshost (wordpresshost.nl) specialized in Managed Dedicated Secure PCI Compliant Linux Plesk Servers and Shared Plesk Hosting or application (WordPres, Magento and Typo3) hosting.

Admin and Moderator on the forums WHT (webhostingtalk.nl), WDT (webdesigntalk.nl) and WMT (webmastertalk.nl) and on the Magento forum.
