James Peys’s Summary

After graduating from law school in 1986, I have been active in the real estate industry in several different areas. I have extensive experience in buying and selling foreclosure and REO properties, construction (ran a nationwide construction company for 16 years), marketing, sales, financing, real estate development and investments. Although I currently focus on the residential markets, I have also worked in the commercial market sectors as well.

Commitment to our clients is at the core of everything we do. My goal is to provide exceptional service that is unique, comprehensive and filled with value. A commitment to a win-win attitude assists our clients in achieving their goals and investment objectives. From a mindset of contribution, my diverse education and professional experience sets me apart and helps deliver the results my clients seek from a realtor. In addition, I have built a full service team of professionals so that my clients get the biggest bang for their investment dollar - so whether the situation calls for a lender, contractor, accountant, insurance, etc., we have the solution.

To find out more follow us at: http://twitter.com/JTPAssociates or visit our blog @ http://coastalcommunityhomes.blogspot.com/.

You can also find us on Facebook @ www.facebook.com/jim.peys.
