Thomas Recke’s Summary

Pillar To Post is Aurora’s ONLY FULL SERVICE inspection firm offering a complete line of residential and commercial building inspections, pre-listing inspections for sellers, EIFS/Stucco inspections, radon gas testing, water quality testing, EDR Neighborhood Environmental Reports, Indoor Air Quality/mold testing, Manufactured Home Foundation Inspections (throughout Hayman Residential Engineering Services), lead paint testing, and asbestos testing.

As the owner and only inspector for Pillar To Post Aurora I strive to stay on top of the latest and greatest training, technology, and techniques to ensure you receive the best possible home inspection that money can buy. With no home inspector licensing in Colorado, professional affiliation and the proper insurance coverage is imperative to ensure your assets are protected. I am a member of ASHI (the American Society of Home Inspectors) and I am fully insured. I am also a member of the Denver Better Business Bureau, the Exterior Design Institute, the Aurora Chamber of Commerce, the Aurora Association of Realtors, and last but not least the Metro Denver Realtor Foundation.

Top notch customer service is what sets Pillar To Post apart from other local inspectors – I am always available to answer your questions before and after the inspection and if a roof is covered with snow I won’t charge you to come back and inspect it when the weather clears up. Outstanding customer service is just part of the standard full service package – which is anything but standard this day-in-age! You have a LOT on your plate – you don’t need to worry about babysitting your home inspector. You want someone with impeccable attention to detail and integrity that will lay all of the cards on the table, you want Pillar To Post representing you in your real estate transaction!

I look forward to earning your business and adding you to the long list of my very satisfied clients!
