Bret Smith’s Summary

A highly-experienced principal in WebAttract, LLC (along with Mike Agron), having extensive Global Sales, Alliances & BizDev background.

I work directly with clients to conceive rich content, live case stories and white-gold papers, then present to sizeable audiences attracted by e-prospecting to a well-targeted, demographically-matched contact group.

I've developed bleeding-edge best practices in e-prospecting practices, leveraging extensive and exceptionally accurate contact databases, derived from the convergence of traditional data sources and social media.

This proprietary prospecting methodology, combined with compelling messaging, professional e-event production, and industrial-strength platforms for both delivery of messaging and the webinar itself, makes WebAttract the perfect partner for you to grow your business.

End2end Solution for Webinar Demand Creation … from conception to measurable value. Value in the form of full contact data for all registrants and attendes. Extended value in the form of post-event lead nurturing and enrichment towards the goal of 1:1 meetings for you.

Fresh new sales leads, increased brand awareness and enhanced thought leadership, delivered on a “turn key” basis, but “custom for every customer”, engaging you in a “hands-on” and personalized experience including presentation mentoring and coaching.
