Scott Fivash’s Summary

Scott is an experienced executive with significant expertise in the areas of Sales, Marketing, Advertising, Publishing and Content Development. He has experience managing startup operations, sales organizations, turn-around situations, acquisitions, and is a connected and creative business leader.

Scott Fivash’s Experience

  • VP Service Pro Network/ Business Development

    QualitySmith

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 2008 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Scott has been responsible for three key roles at QualitySmith. The first has been for Business Development; bringing on key lead partners in all of the current trades and monetizing all leads. Scott was able to accomplish this in the first four months at QualitySmith.

    The second role Scott has played at QualitySmith has been to develop partnerships with national appointment partners in major trades. The success in this effort has changed the way QualitySmith recruits new advertisers.

    The third role Scott has played is in managing the turnaround of a team of eight sales professionals responsible for recruiting home improvement advertisers. In this role Scott was able to meet semi weekly with each recruiter to focus on Sales, Service and Collection results. He built a professional sales program with a seven-step process, developed sales collateral to support the selling process and trained each of the recruiters to use the program. Scott developed a specific advertiser target and he set up a sales funnel to track results.

    In an eight-month period of time the team was able to activate 1,167 contractor advertisers, increasing the overall advertiser count by 41%, and activated a 56% increase in contractor advertiser count in the top trade.
