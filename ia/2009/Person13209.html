Andrea Fjeld’s Summary

Given a medium that is constantly evolving, I don't believe there is any such thing as a "social media expert." So I can't market myself as that; however, I'm an enthusiastic user. Let's discuss it more over a drink. (Double bonus if you need a freelance writer.) I find advertising exciting.
