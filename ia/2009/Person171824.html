Nate Whitehill’s Summary

When most people unfamiliar to blogging are asked to define what a blog is, their definition usually revolves around a personal journal or a social networking tool mostly used by young adults and teens. Anyone familiar to blogging knows that blogging is NOT just a personal journal, in fact, far from it.

A blog is a powerful marketing tool which both businesses and individuals are using to establish brand recognition and connect more intimately with a super-targeted group of clients, consumers or customers.

The number of blogs are increasing at an earth-shattering rate!  There are 120,000 blogs started everyday and more people are continually finding that they can market their personal brand or businesses exclusively through blogging, thereby saving thousands of dollars on traditional marketing costs.

The business of Unique Blog Designs living proof to that - in our first three months of business, we worked with over 70 unique clients to help them establish blogs for their personal brands and/or businesses; and we did not spend any money on marketing to attract these targeted consumers.

Even though you may write high quality content, the visual impression your blog makes is the first impression you make. Of the 75 million or so blogs currently online, the greatest majority of them are variations on one of the many free themes available.

Let Unique Blog Designs help you differentiate your blog from the crowd, thereby raising brand awareness and improving your lead and sale conversion.

With clients such as John Chow and Shoemoney, Unique Blog Designs has already established itself as the premier blog design firm.
