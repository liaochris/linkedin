JW Marriott Resorts’s Summary

A brief journey...

Camelback Inn - www.camelbackinn.com
A legendary resort to take your breath away. Against a desert landscape, traditional southwestern decor and modern structure go hand in hand.

Starr Pass - www.jwmarriottstarrpass.com
From its mountainside perch high above Tucson, this resort weaves understated luxury into the rich tapestry of southern Arizona.

Desert Springs - www.desertspringsresort.com
We’ve taken California’s hottest, coolest desert resort and pushed it over the edge. Live life large with golf, tennis, spa and 210,000 sq ft of meeting space.

Desert Ridge - www.jwdesertridgeresort.com
This Phoenix resort celebrated its 5th anniversary with the opening of a newly constructed 24,420 sq ft Ballroom, bringing the function space to 230,000 square feet.

Las Vegas - www.jwlasvegasresort.com
Set apart from the stir of The Strip, you’ll find a dozen dining options, splendid golf, Aquae Sulis Spa, a casino and over 100,000 sq ft of function space. This is Las Vegas un-stripped.

Grande Lakes - www.grandelakes.com
In Orlando, Grande Lakes reigns supreme. The resort is the centerpiece of 500 lush acres at the headwaters of the Everglades.

Cancun - www.jwmarriottcancun.com
Here you’ll find endless beaches, countless amenities and the warm hospitality of the JW Marriott Cancun. As we like to say, “recepcion al paraiso”. Welcome to paradise.

Ihilani - www.ihilani.com
On Oahu’s west shore, you’ll find white sand lagoons, Hawaiian elegance, and the island’s most complete experience. The 18,000 sq ft outdoor Pavilion is ideal for events.

L.A. LIVE - www.lalivemarriott.com
Opening Spring 2010. L.A. LIVE is the entertainment campus presenting sports, theatre, dining, music and cultural experiences like none other on the planet.

San Antonio Hill Country - www.jwsanantonio.com
In March 2010 Texas will welcome a bright new star. Minutes from the airport, it will provide a wealth of amenities for its eager guests.
