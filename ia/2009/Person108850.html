Jeff Wallace’s Summary

Jeff has 20+ years of global experience as a successful founder and hands-on senior executive, business development and marketing professional with broad-based business skills including leadership, strategy, technology, financial and operational management.

He has served as a consultant and executive for several organizations. His experiences stem from technology and entrepreneurship with specific expertise in bridging technology and business needs. He serves both small and large [global] companies in these capacities and he is widely published and recognized as an expert on emerging technologies.

Most recently, he served as executive in charge of creating a new media group within a large, well-established global marketing and promotions agency serving global clientèle. He worked both internally to structure and grow this group as well as with the company's global clients to develop and implement innovative new media solutions in conjunction with the agency's traditional marketing activities. Though formerly non-existent, in short order and with much competition, he got this new group off the ground and generated millions of dollars in revenue, not to mention having created new paths for consumers to engage with the clients' brands, producing significant brand exposure.

Prior to this role, Jeff served as interim CEO for a real estate development company designing, developing and operating luxury oceanfront properties in northern Mexico generating $100+ million in revenues. Prior to this, he served as an executive, functioning in strategic, business development and operational leadership positions for two software development companies. During this period, he was awarded the prestigious BridgeGate 20 Award and recognized as one of S. California's top 20 business leaders and "difference makers" in the IT and new media community. He started private consulting after several years as a management consultant for CSC Index, a top-tier consultancy.
