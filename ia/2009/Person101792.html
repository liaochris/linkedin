Jaime Raskulinecz, CPM’s Summary

We provide comprehensive account administration and transaction support services for retirement plans enabling investors to harness their tax deferred and tax free assets to purchase a wide array of nontraditional investments. Our plans differ in that they allow for our clients to invest in non-traditional assets within their plans.

Our target customers are anyone with a current retirement plan or someone who should have a retirement plan that is interested in taking full advantage of the entire range of investments the IRS allows.

Jaime strives to empower clients with the knowledge needed to proactively shape their financial future and is a frequent speaker on non-traditional investments within retirement plans. She delivers regular presentations and workshops to investors, advisors, realtors and other professionals throughout the tri-state area to help individuals better understand their investment options and the requirements involved in self-directed accounts and transactions.

Ms. Raskulinecz has more than a decade of experience within the real estate industry and has served on the executive board of the Institute of Real Estate Management, Chapter 1 in New Jersey. Ms. Raskulinecz has been frequently interviewed and asked to contribute to articles about self-directed investing which have appeared in The Wall Street Journal, BusinessWeek, InvestmentNews, Financial Advisor Magazine, Investment Advisor Magazine, FoxBusiness.com, NJBIZ, The Record, RIS Media and Real Estate Weekly. She has recently been recognized by Real Estate New Jersey as one of their “50 Women of Influence” (2207 & 2008) and by the New Jersey Association of Women Business Owners (NJAWBO) as one of the “30 Most Successful New Jersey Women Business Owners.” Entrust Northeast was also selected as a “Best Practices In Marketing” finalist by The New York Enterprise Report for the firm’s education outreach to the public regarding real estate investing in self-directed accounts.
