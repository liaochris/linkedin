Udayan Mukherjee’s Summary

Mission - To excel as an Individual and as a Team to make IT work for Business with quality solutions embedding powerful functionalities for improved operational excellence and to offer benefits to business.

The Products designed were packed with powerful features offering desired flexibilities catering to evolving needs of the management and business users.
