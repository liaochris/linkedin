Constantin Bucur’s Summary

I am qualified mechanical engineer having over seven years experience in various roles involving mechanical design of customised products using 3D CAD and FEA software, manufacturing drawings, production supervision, project management, and staff training.

I am also recognized as a Professional Engineer (Mechanical Engineer – ASCO Code 2126-11) by Engineers Australia.

As part of the project management I often had to source materials and equipments and to estimate the final price, also taking into account the hidden costs, and meet deadlines.

In all the projects I was involved, I have always been fully responsible for making decisions, without any required supervision.

As a Product Manager I was directly involved in medical staff training including organising of training facilities and supervising of the entire event.

Constantin Bucur’s Specialties:

• 3D design using SolidWorks, SolidEdge, Catia V5, Autodesk Inventor and AutoCAD;
• calculus and graphs using Mathcad, Microsoft Excel;
• mechanism simulation using CosmosMotion;
• stress analysis using FEA software (CosmosWorks and Ansys Workbench);
• office applications – Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint;
• thorough knowledge of hardware and software for IBM or IBM compatible PC.
