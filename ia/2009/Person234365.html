Tina McAllister’s Summary

Hi - thanks for visiting my LinkedIn profile - I appreciate it!

I am an ebook coach, ghostwriter and editor. I've been writing for years - typically books (electronic and print) - for busy professionals. But people kept asking me questions and many people wanted to write their own books...so the coaching came into play!

As I re-engineered my business to incorporate the coaching aspect, I came to realize that I love working with parents. As a mother of a young boy, I've done the full-time outside of the home working, the part-time, the work at home gig...I understand the desire to leave the grind behind and want to earn a decent living while making time for family. So helping other parents leave the grind behind (whether FT or PT) is a passion of mine!
