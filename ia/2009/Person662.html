Gregory Kim’s Summary

I create software solutions that satisfy business needs.

I bring to the table an understanding of a broad range of software technologies. I believe in continuous learning and have decades of experience across many different platforms and systems.

I see projects from the perspectives of many different stakeholders. I have worked in positions from entry level to president and worked with people from many companies within many departments.

I have designed, implemented and maintained several long lived systems. This gives me insight into building reliable, extendable and scalable systems. One of my systems has been in use for over ten years and grown from 13,000 customers to over 11,000,000 customers.
