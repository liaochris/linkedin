Terry Maloney’s Summary

Sales professional with 30 years executive and sales management experience and a consistent track record of success in managing sales teams, large accounts and high value sales programmes in IT / Telecoms Markets.

Has lived in France, worked extensively in Europe for the last 20 years and, since founding euro51, has worked world-wide - building a reputation as a business consultant, executive coach and sales coach / consultant that delivers results.

An accredited executive coach with one of the world’s leading suppliers of individual, group and organisational performance improvement instruments - all of which have been extensively researched and field tested.

Invests in his own professional development to ensure all programmes delivered by Euro51 are relevant to the challenges faced by clients in today's rapidly changing business environment.

Key attributes:

Effective communication and presentation skills, high levels of integrity, energy and enthusiasm.

Builds strong relationships with clients, taking time to understand their business requirements in developing tailored performance improvement programmes that deliver tangible business results.

Experienced executive coach with well developed people management / coaching skills - quickly establishes rapport with people, providing a confidential, supportive and challenging coaching environment where people can explore their strengths / weaknesses honestly to identify areas for improvement that have sustained personal and organisational benefits.

Skilled facilitator and team coach who specialises in strategic planning and teamwork coaching at all levels in an organisation - improving group processes, team dynamics, performance outcomes and commitment to executing agreed plans efficiently / effectively

Provides sales training / coaching which is relevant to sales people with a wide range of skills and experience using real situations faced by participants to deliver tangible business benefit.

Terry Maloney’s Specialties:

Executive Coaching - Developing effective leadership / management / personal styles

Team Coaching - Developing high performance teams that communicate and collaborate effectively - with a specific focus on sales / sales support that improves Territory / Account / Opportunitiy Management and resource utilisation.

Developing high performance organisations - Strategic Planning Facilitation / Organisational Development Consultancy.
