Frank Knippenberg’s Summary

With more than 10 years of success in sports-related business and product development, working for emerging companies, Frank Knippenberg has now turned his attention to RealEstate with the launch of Boardwalk Capital, LLC. It is while working for Red Bull North America Knippenberg established a reputation as a visionary marketing strategist and project management specialist. Knippenberg played a major role in leading Red Bull's emergence as the leading single-serve beverage nationwide.

Prior to his tenure at Red Bull, Knippenberg forged an impressive record of achievement and advancement in public relations and retail, establishing a reputation as an expert in turnarounds and start-ups. He has a proven record of success in transforming fledgling businesses and launching successful startups to exceed expectations in sales,
merchandising, and customer relations.

Knippenberg has also founded and continues to participate in the management of KMK Landscape Design & Installation, New England Golfscapes, Social Boston Sports, and the
University of Hartford Rugby Alumni Association.

A Boston resident, Knippenberg majored in marketing and graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration from the University of Hartford in Connecticut.
Knippenberg is also a Massachusetts licensed Realtor.
