Mehmet Unsoy’s Summary

I am a telecom market and technology expert and visionary, with over 30 years of data, IP and wireless communication experience, beginning with Arpanet in the early 1970’s. I have extensive global market experience and reach, working with wireless and wireline operators, manufacturers and industry players in North America, Europe and Asia/Pacific, while being based in Ottawa, Tokyo, London and Dallas, during my career.

Currently, I am a Partner at Cartagena Capital GmbH, an international technology corporate finance firm with a special focus on the wireless industry. Cartagena Capital, which is head-quartered in Munich, Germany, provides services in the fields of Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A), fundraising and strategic advice. http://www.cartagena-capital.com.

I am also a Founder and Managing Partner of TIPRA, a global network of senior business and technology executives; see. http://www.tipra.com

Since Sep 2002, I have served as Advisor to several Boston based large VC firms, such as St. Paul/Vesbridge, Bessemer, Highland, Atlas, Flagship, and Kodiak, for their investments in the wireless industry. I have also served on the Board and Advisory Boards of several wireless and broadband related technology companies, in the U.S., Canada and Europe, such as Aepona, Bitfone, Bridgeport, Handmark, mBlox, Nextone, Roundbox, Mobile Wisdom, Kajeet, Teltier, Red Bend, Chantry, Incucomm, Visage, Taral/Airwide, Megisto.

Prior to that, for 3 years, I was based in London, U.K., as CTO / VP Technology and Chief Architect with British Telecom (BT), BT Wireless and O2.
Prior to BT, I was with Nortel Networks and Bell-Northern Research for 17 years, with senior management responsibilities in technology, product development, marketing and business development roles, in Nortel's enterprise, wireline and wireless divisions. Prior to that, I was with Bell Canada for 5 years, helping to build Canada's first public packet switched network, called Datapac.
