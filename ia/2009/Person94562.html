Donald Alvarez’s Summary

Entrepreneurial, medical imaging, sales and marketing executive, now based at German headquarters, with broad experience in multiple modalities, IT and software-only solutions with consistent, top-level achievements in OEM and channel management, direct sales, marketing, product definition, technology application and PMA-level regulatory approvals.

Donald Alvarez’s Specialties:

PACS, CAD, 3D, Telerad, CR, DICOM, IHE, Mammography, Surgical C-Arms, Uro Diagnostics, Lithotripsy, Mobile X-Ray, Virtual Colonoscopy

Donald Alvarez’s Experience

  • Director, Regional Sales - Americas

    Siemens AG Healthcare Sector, Germany

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; SI; Medical Devices industry)

    January 2003 — Present (6 years 2 months)

    Primary,entreprenuiral link for sales support, customer presentations, business relationships and negotiations between headquarters in Germany and field staff in the Americas (USA, Canada, Latin America) selling surgical C-arms, full-field digital and analog mammography systems, mobile x-ray, lithotripsy and urological tables. My position also supports development of budgets and forecasts and works to ensure all are achieved by regional teams. I help to define then maintains oversight of any and all special projects, discounts or programs required to achieve agreed-upon targets. I was promoted from my previous role as Director, Women’s Health in USA.

  • Consultant

    Medical Imaging Consultant

    (Medical Devices industry)

    July 1998 — January 2003 (4 years 7 months)

    Business consultant specializing in support of high-tech start-ups and new product introductions. Clients included the following companies:

    Lumisys - Hired to develop strategies and train sales channels for successful launch of world's first desktop computed radiography system.

    Voxar - Helped young team redefine their market focus and re-develop their libraries into world class 3D applications for medical imaging including volume rendering of MR and CT on desktop PCs along with virtual colonoscopy and calcium scoring applications. Established new sales channels, set up technical support systems and facilitated OEM integration contracts.

    Deus Technologies - Hired to help company exploit its notoriety as the world’s first FDA approved application for Computer Aided Detection of lung cancer. Helped to guide them to a digital, DICOM-based, thin client version of their product which led to company's first OEM contract with a major imaging vendor.

  • Director of Sales, Marketing and Product Development

    Raytheon Systems (EMED)

    (Medical Devices industry)

    December 1989 — July 1998 (8 years 8 months)

    Managed a direct team involved in the sales, marketing and product development of teleradiology and mini-PACS solutions with worldwide distribution via independent dealer teams. Company began as Image Data Corp., sold to E-Systems (EMED), merged with Raytheon Systems and then sold to Access Medical which retained the name eMed.
