Kipp Krukowski’s Experience

  • Owner, Certified Exit Planning Advisor

    Value Link Advisors

    (Investment Banking industry)

    July 2009 — Present (6 months)

    Value Link Advisors, a subsidiary of Confidential Business Sale, Inc., specializes in business exit planning services. We have developed a model to better prepare business owners to maximize the value of their business and provide them options for the transition / succession planning and wealth preservation. All of these are important issues that business owners will eventually have to face. Our mission is to have a positive effect on our client’s lives by guiding them through the complex, high stakes, and emotional process of transitioning out of their business. We have also learned that transitioning a business is a process, not an event. The process takes time, expertise, coordination and commitment by many parties. That’s why we collaborate with other professionals such as Attorneys, CPA’s, Estate Planners, Financial Advisors and Insurance Specialists in order to deliver superior results to our clients. The earlier this process can begin the more effective the results will be.
