Susan Placek’s Summary

My company specializes in unique greetings, souvenirs, gifts, favors, fundraising and customized promotional marketing items - containing shelf stable gourmet foods as well as a line of themed and unique quality mouse pads.

We take pride in our beautiful and fun designs and we love to be creative in each and every way. Customer service is very important for us and we bend backwards to come up with the product for our clients, that's just right for their needs.

We sell stock items to wholesalers and retailers, such as gift shops, card shops, florists, gift basket businesses and souvenir shops.

Both our fundraising program and Pink Ribbon Calendar Popcorn Greetings are unique and attractive additions to any fundraiser campaign or charity. We offer practical and fun items for school groups, sport groups, church groups, cancer groups, military care packages and U.S. Troops fundraising groups.

We also customize for private and corporate customers and our products are well liked as inexpensive and unique promotional marketing items and customer appreciation gifts.
