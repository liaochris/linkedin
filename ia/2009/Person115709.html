Joel Foner’s Summary

Joel is an experienced project and product manager, with an uncommon array of skills in multiple areas of business and technology, spanning business management, organizational development, technical sales support, project management with geographically distributed teams, product vision, software and systems development, and the creation of engaged, supportive customer communities.
