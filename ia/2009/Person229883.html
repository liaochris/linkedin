Tom Dailey’s Summary

I am the former senior executive of Discover Card’s merchant network In this capacity I was in charge of $2+ billion in revenues, 2000+ employees, $250 million+ expenses, and 22 facilities ( including sales, operations, POS terminal and collateral fulfillment centers) . My responsibilities included the Discover merchant network, bank/processor relations, operations, finance, planning/strategic development, marketing,, information technology and sales. I was a direct-report to the Chairman/COO of Discover and a member of Discover’s Management Committee and of parent Morgan Stanley’s senior management group. More recently have been a consultant to the financial services industry. Some of my clients have included Citigroup, Morgan Stanley, Bain, McKinsey, Goldman Sachs, J.P. Morgan, Credit Suisse and Citigroup. I am a member of the Board of Directors of Merchant e-Solutions (a payments processor) and a Board Advisor to Progress Financial Corporation (a Hispanic-oriented lender). I have provided key testimony in several high-profile card industry litigation cases and do expert witness work. I am president of Valtegrics LLC, an organization-development consultancy focusing on values, integrity and ethics. I have been a contributor to trade publications and have served as an industry expert in print, radio and television media. See my website at www.tomdaileyconsulting.com for further information and a detailed client list. I am currently engaged in consulting work but am open to interim, full-time and/or board positions.

Tom Dailey’s Specialties:

Expert services related to the card/payments industry: processing, process/procedure reviews, competitive landscape, pricing, negotiation, vendor selection, settlement/chargebacks, issuing, design and operations. I have managed operations, call centers, sales/marketing, finance, technology, strategy and admin functions. Board member of a payment processing institution, board advisor to a lender, and media contributor. Expert witness. I am also founder of an organization development consultancy.
