Cherisse Watkins’s Summary

For the past few years, I have specialized in full cycle recruiting at a national level for Scientists and Engineers, for contract, contract to hire, and direct hire needs. I also provide HR Outsourcing services to small business owners. I have a scientific background so that I can better understand the technical needs of my clients, and I also enjoy working with people and helping them advance their career, so scientific recruiting really has been a joy and passion for me. I have my Bachelor’s in Animal Science and Pre-Veterinary Medicine, with a Minor in Nutritional Science from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo. While I am fascinated by science and its applications, I find everyday as a recruiter to be rewarding since I can couple my passion for science with my love of working with people to advance their company and career. I work for an 8a status recruiting firm, providing both government and commercial clients with staffing solutions to run their businesses more efficiently.
