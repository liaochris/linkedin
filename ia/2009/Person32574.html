Brenden "Bo" Wright, CIR’s Summary

Follow me on twitter @ http://twitter.com/bmwright

It takes talent to find talent.

Brenden is a career talent acquisition leader with an expertise in the area of pharmaceuticals, food additives, dietary supplements, and biotechnology research and development. He supports senior leaders across multiple functional areas with talent acquisition, leadership, and management strategies. Brenden has exemplary skills in isolating, identifying, and attracting top-talent for critical executive and staff positions, creating value in the employment opportunity, and completing extremely difficult projects faster and more efficiently than other search options.

Prior to entering the field of talent acquisition, management, and development, Brenden served his country in the United States Marine Corps in the area of nuclear, biological, and chemical defense. During this time, he was promoted meritoriously twice - a rare achievement for even the best of Marines.

Brenden possesses a proven track record of consistent success and, after more than 11 years of talent development and many success stories within industry, he is fortunate to have a deep and broad network of contacts that enable him to uncover the true high-performers that are often hidden from traditional recruitment methodologies.

Finally, Brenden has driven success in both for-profit and non-profit organizations and has worked in both agency and corporate environment providing staffing, consulting, contingency, and retained search services. He holds a B.S. in Management and is currently pursuing his MBA.
