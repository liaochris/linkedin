David Ting’s Summary

I am working at IGN Entertainment, managing the engineering team that develops the ign.com site. Gaming has always been one of my favorite activities, and now I can help shape IGN into the best overall destination for gaming and entertainment.

I was working at Yahoo managing the engineering team that makes Yahoo! Music one of the most popular internet destinations. We have fun building a great online Music experience for millions of end users, and bring in significant revenue to Yahoo!'s bottom line.

Prior to joining Yahoo in 2007, I was the Senior Director of Engineering for Entriq. I was responsible for the development of the security, billing, care and content publishing product lines at Entriq. I have helped to company to establish a new Digital Stores product line and strategy, which brought in over 70% of the company's revenue in 2005 and 2006.

Prior to joining Entriq in 2004, I served as the Director of Engineering at Movielink for over 2 years, and was responsible the development of the Movielink site, ecommerce engine, Content Management System, Content Distribution Network and the Digital Rights Management technology. I helped grow the Movielink service from inception to becoming one of the top video-on-demand services on the internet.

Previously, I was the Senior Director of Engineering for BayLogics, which was one of the leading providers of commercial real estate management software for Fortune 500 companies. I also worked as the Director of Engineering at AltaVista, where I managed the development of a popular ecommerce search and shopping network, shopping.com, which experienced over six million page views a day. Previous experience also includes IBM, WiseConnect and Zip2. During my career, I have received two Outstanding Technical Achievement Awards at IBM, and earned AltaVista’s Employee of the Year honor in 1999.
