Don Shirley’s Summary

Executive Leadership at the Juncture of Technology and Marketing

Don Shirley is an accomplished marketing executive with more than two decades of experience in technology and telecommunications. His entrepreneurial drive and creativity have propelled him throughout his 24-year-career – in bringing new products to market, building high-performing teams for Fortune 500 companies, and in founding and leading a successful venture-backed startup.

Most recently, as VP Market Development for UniRush, a financial services company, Don has introduced insurance offerings and related consumer services, critical to everyday living, that are expected to double the company's revenues.

As director of strategic alliances for the North American operations of SpinVox, a technology solutions provider offering voice-to-text services, Don secured $150 million in agreements with Fortune 500 technology partners and spearheaded the development of new enterprise-specific services.

Previously, Don has served in senior marketing roles with telecom companies including Ericsson, Cincinnati Bell, PowerNet Global Communications, and PageNet. At Ericsson, Don hired and led a dedicated team that built, packaged and sold Mobile Internet solutions to wireless carriers across the Americas.

In 2000, with backing from Ericsson, Don founded and served as CEO for the Mobile Internet startup Brightpod, where he raised $3 million in capital and built a 25-person organization before orchestrating the venture’s successful sale to a public company.

Don began his career with the U.S. Army, where he served in the U.S. Military Joint Special Operations Command. He graduated with a bachelor’s degree in political science from The Citadel.
