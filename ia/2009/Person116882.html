John Delaney’s Summary

I have quite a bit of experience in full-cycle recruiting within a variety of approaches (internal, external, RPO, etc.). I can fill positions directly or act as a player coach to manage recruiting teams. My strength lies in technical recruiting, but I've also filled positions across a spectrum of other industries (and have never experienced any issues ramping up on new searches).

I also have an extensive sales background, having hire/trained 100+ sales people and driving $25M+ revenue throughout my career.

John Delaney’s Specialties:

High Tech recruiting, sales, marketing, business development, contract negotiations, closing deals, building/managing/developing teams.
