John Groarke’s Summary

I work with small, medium and large professional services organisations on top-line and bottom-line improvement initiatives across the six primary business drivers - strategy, marketing, selling, delivery, operations, talent - and/or the 17 core processes, resolving issues / problems / challenges and realising opportunities along the way.

In short, I help my clients to develop more efficient, more effective, more innovative and ultimately more profitable professional services businesses. If you know your strategic direction then I will help you to operationalise and deliver your chosen strategy. If you don't know your strategic direction then I can work with you as a strategy 'shaper and setter' first and then help to deliver! Either way, I know "how to make a quid" as we say 'down-under' ... and my clients now know that too!

For large professional services organisations, consulting firms, boutique consultants and services-centric enterprises, I have a range of diagnostic tools that identify and resolve the pain and the gain and then we implement.

For the sole practitioner I have a range of off-the-shelf service offers
• Pragmatic help for your consultancy business - that results in the implementation of operational improvements
• You pull .. We push, consulting assistance - the answers to everything! - what are you having a problem with? what would we like to tell you about?

And I also offer a one-on-one mentoring service to sole practitioners, that has been very effective.

If you are just starting out as a management consultant, then my 'Setting up as a management consultant' service offer is especially for you.

Mine is a unique business that is very well-founded with excellent credentials ... try me!

John Groarke’s Specialties:

JEGMC Strategy Consulting is a management consulting firm based in Sydney, Australia, serving clients throughout the world with three distinctive, yet highly complementary, services:

1. Operational strategies for professional services organisations, and other services-centric enterprises
2. Management strategies for information technology
3. Developing a career and building a business in management consultancy.
