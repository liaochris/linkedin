Diane Nudelman’s Summary

Swag Connection began in 2002 as a one person, home-based business (called Advance Promotions until we rebranded in 2006). Since then we've grown to become one of the Top 50 Women-Owned Businesses in Portland with annual revenue approaching $1MM.

We offer custom-imprinted promotional products, apparel, awards, business gifts and presentation items. "Swag" is a slang term that encompasses the entire universe of logo branded giveaway items (and it's much easier to say and spell than "custom promotional products and apparel"). It's also an acronym for Stuff We All Get.

From our office in Portland OR, my staff and I work with local companies and many more across the US who find us through my blog and online.

Our ideal clients are busy marketing professionals who are results-oriented. They need top-notch, on-target swag ideas that fit with their messaging, brand image and budget. They typically don't have the time or desire to search through catalogs or surf the internet looking for products - they just call us when they have an event coming up and enlist our help. They have high expectations for quality and appreciate our quick response to their needs and deadlines.

Our clients also understand that everything they put their logo on is an extension of their brand image. Whether it's inexpensive goodies for a trade show, shirts for a company event or a completely custom item they want to design from scratch, they trust us to make sure it's good quality, looks consistent with their overall branding and that their logo is represented correctly.

If this sounds good to you, I invite you to contact me & experience the Swag Connection difference!
