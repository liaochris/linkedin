Steve Vaile’s Summary

Steve is a seasoned Telecoms, Media & Technology VP that has been involved in developing complex telecoms infrastructure businesses through partner channels and direct sales initiatives to key customers for over 15 years.

He has been responsible for shaping new business start ups and implementing restructuring plans for existing telecommunications software companies.

He provides consultancy services to a number of Private Equity companies seeking to restructure their portfolio or increase equity though rapid international expansion. His contacts include C level contacts within the majority of the EMEA telecommunications companies.

Steve Vaile’s Specialties:
