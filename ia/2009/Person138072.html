Lauren Mang’s Summary

I have three years of experience writing about retail, visual merchandising and store design as the assistant editor for Visual Merchandising and Store Design magazine. I was promoted to associate editor in November 2008. I love fashion and design and find it fascinating how each drives the overall store experience.

In addition to my magazine publishing experience, I'm proficient in the online environment. At VMSD, I was the managing editor of www.vmsd.com, which entailed posting, writing and assigning web copy and writing the newsletter.

Lauren Mang’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, freelance, retail design, visual merchandising, fashion, mannequins. My articles have also appeared in Cincy magazine (formerly CincyBusiness). Additionally, I've written for five visual merchandising and design books published by VMSD's parent company, ST Media Group Intl.
