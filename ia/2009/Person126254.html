Karen Golob’s Summary

In an age of media overload and the lack of effective skills to monitor and manage stress and anger, it is necessary that new resources of information be available to avoid resultant anger and frustration that we see occurring in our daily lives. Anger Management Service's goal is to assist inviduals in learning skills in order to open the door to new ways of problem solving while remaining non-aggressive.

Karen Golob's broad experience in the health care field enhances her ability to work with clients who are self referred, adolescents, Superior Court mandated referrals, as well as professionals from the corporate and health care fields.

Karen Golob, Owner and Director of Anger Management Services, has been Certified as an Anger Management professional and Executive Coach. Our curriculum is based on the Anderson & Anderson model which assists individuals in identifying and developing strengths in the areas of Emotional Intelligence, Communication, Stress and Anger Management. Private sessions provide confidentiality, individual attention and anonymity.

Karen Golob’s Specialties:

In-service seminars, workplace presentations and private Executive Coaching sessions assist individuals in all aspects of their lives.
