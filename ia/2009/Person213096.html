Sébastien Sacard’s Summary

As an Agile product designer and/or project manager consultant, I work for a small and selective portfolio of innovative projects. I help my clients to analyze and size their projects, identify the goals that need to be achieved by the users, build the roadmap and empower the team to deliver it.

Able to embrace the unique culture of each project, and share it with integrity and passion. Pragmatic approach, premium on taking great attention to clients needs and on providing time-to-market, cost-effective solutions. Focus on communicating the goals to all the team members, in order to get the best from each individuals. Great technical expertise allowing to challenge technical decisions.
