Our Executive Client Services:
PBA specializes in assisting C-Level Executives, VPs and other Senior Level Business Professionals in fulfilling their personal desires to own and operate their own business. Thus freeing them from the uncertainty of the corporate environment and giving them back control over their direction and ensuring that their work compliments their desired life style.

Are you an entrepreneur, a C-level executive or VP that is underemployed, unemployed or not happy with you current employment position or would like to add new revenue streams to your existing activities then we can assist you. We match your competencies and passions to existing businesses at no charge or obligations to you. We offer you the following 6 major business options:
Business Acquisitions; Hybrid Model; - Regional, Master, Unit Franchises; License Opportunities;Personal Business Advisors (You can join our PBA team)

Our Business Client Service:
