David Rawe’s Summary

Provide personal and small group coaching for strategic planning and goal attainment for career, business, and life 'planning'. I have created a coaching program called Think, Plan, and Expand (T.P.E.) that guides people using a strategic approach that relies on the individual strengths and looks for hidden strengths (not weaknesses) to be brought to the front for development. This is coupled with an organic model of TIPU AKE (from New Zealand) to enable a holistic program that will enhance goal setting and achievement.

Over 15 years of strategic capabilities and business analysis skills for telecommunications, logistics, research, defense, and intelligence organizations. Developed sound business process improvement for advanced management center and logistics applications while advocating strategies combining new technologies with sound mission plans to reduce costs and provide timely information for operations support. Provide information on current and possible business development opportunities and strategies for innovative solutions that result in successful program acquisition.
