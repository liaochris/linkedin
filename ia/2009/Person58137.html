David Locke’s Summary

David has spent the past 15 years in the sports media. David is currently working with the Utah Jazz. As part of the the Fan Sports Network, David hosts a daily talk show from 3 to 6 pm, studio hosts for the Utah Jazz games and is an active part of the TV show Powerhouse.

Prior to returning to Utah David spent 10 years in the Pacific Northwest. Spending the 2006-07 NBA Season as the radio play by play announcer for the Seattle Supersonics. In addition, David was the program director for the Seattle Supersonics Radio network. For the seven seasons prior, David was the pre-game, half time and post-game host of the Sonics broadcast as well as the WNBA Seattle Storm play by play announcer. David hosted the a sports talk show on 950, KJR and wrote a weekly column for the Seattle PI.

David also spent a season as Fox Sports Northwest’s play by play announcer for University of Washington Football and Basketball.

Prior to arriving in Seattle, David built 1320, K-FAN, Salt Lake City,into the highest rated sports station in the country. As program director of the station he brought the station to an unprecedented 10 share (Men 25-54) in primary demographics. David’s talk show went to #1 in both the demographics Men 25-54 and People 25-54.

While in Salt Lake, David wrote for the Standard-Examiner in Ogden and the Desert News in Salt Lake City. David also called play by play for the WNBA Utah Starzz and was a part of the Utah State Aggies TV broadcast.

Throughout, his career David has been on the cutting edge of the technology. At 950, KJR he lead the charge of integrating blogging and his own website into convergence with his talk show.

For a sample of David’s on air work and newspaper work go to www.davidlocke.com.
