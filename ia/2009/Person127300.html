Kate Masin <> Healthcare Exec’s Summary

STAFFING AND RECRUITMENT EXECUTIVE

Kate Masin is a Healthcare Solutions Executive with areas of expertise in Allied Health and Nurse recruitment, placement and retention. Kate Masin has been in the healthcare arena for 10 years which has provided her with the opportunity to develop an extensive professional network. Having a degree in Healthcare Administration and on the job experience of working inside hospitals and physician offices, Kate has been able to align herself with many organizations and groups in the medical field.

FROM THE SPACE NEEDLE TO THE ROCK N ROLL HALL OF FAME ...

Kate Masin started her staffing career working for a healthcare staffing firm based out of Seattle, Washington. Kate developed solid relationships with the healthcare community and the candidates that Seattle had to offer. Her relationships were built upon the foundation of what she calls the 'Chuck Woolery' effect. If you recall the TV show 'The Dating Game', you will know what she is referring to. There are always two parts of a relationship and both parties need to want to make it work for a relationship to be successful. With that theme in mind, Kate is always striving for that marriage between employee and employer. The 'Chuck Woolery' effect was easily transitioned into the Cleveland healthcare market when Kate relocated back to her roots in 2003. Since then, Kate has been extremely successful in assisting healthcare professionals in transition to find that 'perfect place' and that 'new home' they are always searching for. Over the years, Kate has established herself as a trusted partner who will do whatever it takes to help healthcare professionals in any way she can. Reach out to her to see how she can help you!
