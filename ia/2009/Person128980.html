Keith Harmeyer’s Summary

Innovation is dependent on ideas - and the ability to persuasively communicate those ideas to others. That's the business I'm in, and I'm always interested in building new relationships with individuals and organizations who share this focus.


Professional Experience:

Prior to joining SmartStorming ®, Keith’s professional background included over 25 years in advertising and strategic marketing; sales and business coaching; and advanced presentation and communication skills training – including the development of his proprietary system for highly persuasive presentation and interpersonal communication, The SuperSkill ® .

As a marketing and creative executive at agencies in the Omnicom and Publicis networks, as well as founder and principal of his own marketing communications firm, The ZAK Agency, Keith created countless successful brand marketing programs and business presentations for many of the world’s best known and most successful companies, such as American Express, JPMorgan Chase, Sony, Time Warner, ABC, Disney, Philips, Fujifilm, Conde Nast, Sports Illustrated, GlaxoSmithKline, Roche, McDonald’s, Footlocker and many others. He has also coached and trained numerous business leaders on their sales and presentation techniques, utilizing his proprietary system for persuasive communication.

Keith is a graduate of Loyola University and Tulane University, both in New Orleans, LA, and of Coach U, the leading training organization for professional coaches. Formerly, he was the Executive Vice President, Marketing & Creative Services, at C2 Creative in New York City.

Keith is married, with three children, and live in New York City and Hollywood Beach, Florida.
