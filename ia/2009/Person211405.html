Scott Davies’s Summary

- October, 2008. Authored 'The V1 Concept of Air Transportation Management' - a research proposal investigating the consumption of safe-separation airspace capacity and runway environment capacity as ubiquitous utilities and tradable commodities. The concept is intended to provide economically sustainable and operationally efficient air transportation as a fundamental enabler of U.S. GDP expansion.

- June, 2008. Founded Velocity 1 LLC; an aviation economics research enterprise which advocates the free-market provision and consumption of aviation infrastructure.

- May, 2008. Completed the Villanova University Executive MBA Program. Added a rigorous 21-month academic program in global commerce to a military career of documented leadership and mission performance.

- 2004. Retired from the U.S. Navy. Twenty-one years of active and reserve service. Officer-in-Charge of numerous overseas operational detachments. Department head, pilot training officer, and instructor pilot.

- Current Flying Status. Twelve years experience as an FAA licensed Airline Transport Pilot.
