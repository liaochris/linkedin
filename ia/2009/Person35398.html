Dr Brahm S Bhandari’s Summary

Extensive experience on studies of fission barrier shapes of heavy nuclei, tunneling through multi-humped fission barrier shapes, resonant tunneling and its applications,etc.
Current goals are to work on energy systems, their relative suitabilities and sustainable options in given geographical areas with corresponding resources and demands.

Dr Brahm S Bhandari’s Specialties:

Quantum Tunneling, Fission Barrier Shapes of Heavy Nuclei,Enery Systems and Sustainable Options.
Past research flavour can be obtained through Google Search for "B.S.Bhandari",fission and/or "B.S.Bhandari",physics
