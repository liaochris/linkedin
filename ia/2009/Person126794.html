Karl Raats’s Summary

Tell me. What are you looking for here? Tidbits about my past and present? And would that give you an answer to your search?

When people skim and scan each other’s profile, they will find three things:

1) That person’s accomplishments, often frosted and spinkled with colourful toppings.
2) That person’s unique qualities, sound and trust worthy like grandmother’s wisdom.
3) That person’s added value, like the joyous find of that one missing peace of the puzzle.

But why are you reading up on some one else’s garnished past, when what you’re really looking for, is a glimpse at your own future?
Profile summaries shouldn’t reflect a stranger’s heroic yesterday, they should be a sneak preview of your own spectacular tomorrow.

Now, that would be a profile worthy of your time and energy!

I do not know what your future profile looks like. That’s impossible. It’s your future and yours alone. But if that really is your quest, to find a companion over there, where ever that is. If that is the real reason of your much appreciated visit here, then maybe - just maybe - we could meet along the way.
