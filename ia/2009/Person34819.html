Bruce Tolbert’s Summary

Registered Architect with 24 years of experience specializing in community and campus-oriented projects and desire to develop and improve communities, towns and cities through quality urban planning and design. Skills and experience include:

• Designing complex, architecturally unified campus projects with budgets ranging from $2 million to $7 million
• Managing both project design and production of construction drawings for community and campus projects ranging in size from 22,000 SF to 100,000 SF
• Analyzing and synthesizing complex, multifunction design criteria to provide site and building designs that are sensitive to existing conditions and unify communities and campuses
• Unifying clients, governing agencies and multidisciplinary consultants by creating a positive, collaborative team environment resulting in mutually agreeable solutions
• Leading design teams by promoting communication and cooperation, producing designs that meet an organization’s quality standards
• Resolving design and construction problems ranging from complex site designs to owner-directed changes during construction in a practical and efficient manner to meet client goals, schedules and budgets
• Developing, researching and presenting design options for client input and approval in order to fully utilize site and provide physical improvements that enable client to expand its mission and service to the community
• Managing projects with multiphase construction schedules with durations up to two years

Computer Skills:
AutoCAD and Architectural Desktop, drawing experience with projects ranging from $13 to $27 million

COMCheck, Energy Code compliance software
PowerPoint, including budget and design status presentations
