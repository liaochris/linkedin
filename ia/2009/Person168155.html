Morgan Rees’s Summary

Marketing expert with 3 publicly traded companies involving Philips Electronics, Norelco, Marantz, Magnavox, Citrix Online, NetGear and Honeywell. Includes global marketing responsibilities and full profit/loss accountability. Responsibilities involved global brand development, channel, public relations, e-commerce and marketing campaigns involving broadcast, print, online/offline advertising, direct/email programs, public relations, executive level seminars and high visibility retail promotions. Built world class marketing programs involving SaaS, consumer electronics, computing, technology, ecommerce, retail, agency and B2C/B2B. Honors graduate of Florida International University and a published author involving numerous marketing articles in trade magazines.

BRANDING: Launched Philips brand in the U.S., creating consumer branding market awareness where none existed. Developed and implemented brand strategies for Citrix Online, Philips, Norelco, Marantz, Magnavox, Netgear, Honeywell, Oldsmobile, Toyota, Swim'n Sport and Red Lobster.

LAUNCHED: Spearheaded global product launches involving GoToMyPC, GoToWebinar, GoToMeeting, GoToAssist, WiFi, GPS, CD-R/RW, DVD, Plasma Flat TV, LCD Display, MP3, Cellular, USB and TiVo.

PARTNERSHIPS: AT&T, WorldCom, Sprint, Deutsche Telekom, British Telecom, French Telecom, Verizon, Peachtree, Commission Junction, McAfee, Dell, Gateway, Best Buy, Turner/Time-Warner, H/P, Creative Labs, PolyGram Records (Motown, Island, Mercury, A&M), NASCAR, Saks 5th Ave

TURNAROUNDS: Improved both the top- and bottom-lines, increased revenue 71% from $112M to $192M. Reversed $6.5 million annual loss to $60 million (gross) profit on sales of $192 million in 14 months.
Led efforts that doubled revenues of two Honeywell business units.

STARTUPS: Helped grow Philips Components from startup to $500 million in revenue and grew a Honeywell start-up initiative to $100 million in revenue in 3 years. Array, raised $24-million from VCs.
