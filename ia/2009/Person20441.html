  • Savoir Vivre dba Lampwork and Beads

    (Design industry)

    August 2007 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    Savoir Vivre ~ gracious living ~ began life in the summer of 2007 and Lampwork and Beads is its 1st e-shoppe. At Lampwork and Beads I design and manufacture wearable art, glass bead artisan jewellery, to adorn the self assured woman.

    Lampwork and Beads uses generic glass beads and local artists' lampwork beads, glass beads whose personality is developed under fire.
    I am the chief cook and bottle washer in this business which is operated from the den of my home: an area lovingly known as 'The PigPen.' The PigPen is my office and my studio.
    I wear all the hats in Lampwork and Beads: I am the bookkeeper, the purchaser, the designer and manufacturer, the warehouseman, the ... you get the picture.
    I write articles about artisan jewellery. I have a monthly newsletter, Glass Beads News, and several blogs and groups for which I also write.
