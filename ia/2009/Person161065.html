Michael Cohen, Ph.D.’s Summary

Michael D. Cohen, Ph.D. helps corporations, associations, government agencies, and political candidates achieve their goals by identifying timely and relevant information, translating it into strategy, and getting results. Cohen Research Group is a worldwide market research and polling firm dedicated to helping its clients think smarter and move faster.

Dr. Cohen has worked with some of the world’s leading brands, organizations, and government agencies including Hard Rock Cafe, Discovery Communications, Network Solutions, National Federation of Independent Businesses, National Taxpayers Union, Air Transport Association, United States Department of Defense, United States Department of Energy, State of New York Department of Health, and state tobacco control programs. He has polled for local, state, and national campaign organizations.

He has been interviewed by national and worldwide media including CBS News, CNN, Fox News, New York Times, Washington Post and Washington Times. He has presented his work at several academic and professional conferences and published articles on public opinion research and design, political communication, and campaigning. He was highlighted as a "Mover & Shaker" by Campaigns & Elections magazine.

Michael Cohen, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

Decision-making intelligence for top companies specializing in branding, new markets, messaging, customer loyalty, and public opinion. Cohen Research Group uses general population and customer surveys, interactive-voice-response surveys, focus groups, face-to-face interviews, and Internet research. I am particularly interested in working with growing and transitioning organizations.
