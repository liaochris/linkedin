Lisa Lugo’s Summary

Senior IT executive with excellent leadership, management and communication skills. Over 20 years experience in the technology industry specializing in IT strategy, organization and change management. Expertise includes implementing technology to support business goals, portfolio management, project management, IT architecture, and vendor management. Developed the New York City Technology Plan - See "PlanIT - Better Government Through Customer Service" http://www.nyc.gov/html/doitt/html/home/home.shtml. Currently responsible for setting operational strategies for NYC EDC.

Lisa Lugo’s Specialties:

IT strategy, organizational change management, governance, portfolio management, project management, vendor management.
