Adriana Petersen’s Summary

I have enjoyed over 24 years of building long-term relationships and successfully partnering with leading organizations around the world for the search, recruitment and placement of upper-level management and leadership in the physical and life sciences.

A WORD ABOUT TALENT...

A successful organization understands that the key to its success is not just based on the products or services that it develops or promotes, but the leadership that drives that organization.

The core of leadership is an individual’s innate ability to engage, motivate, move and influence others. Great leaders makes things happen, they shape the world, they shift paradigms and some have even altered the course of history. Great leadership requires great talent.

Identifying and recruiting great talent and leaders is what we do so well at NEXUSBioMed.

A WORD ABOUT NEXUSBioMed...

At NEXUSBioMed we are committed to positively impacting our client companies through the talent we bring to their organizations, as well as improving the lives and careers of those professionals with whom we have the privilege of working with.

Through a strategic alliance with HUMANeX Ventures, all of our candidates are assessed and profiled for leadership and talent. We represent only the best, most successful executives in our industry.

NEXUSBioMed utilizes HUMANeX Ventures as a third-party, independent organization that provides objective talent assessment with unbiased results, as well as comprehensive leadership and talent consulting. Such methodology has assisted our clients with increasing their productivity and revenues. Numerous $Million organizations have grown into $Billion ones by following these methodologies, while becoming successful talent-driven, world-class organizations.

If interested in discussing how we can assist you or your organization, please contact me at a.petersen@nbmsearch.com or (949) 338-2474.
