David Y. Todd’s Summary

I consult and write for solo practitioners, nonprofits, educational institutions and government agencies. Speechwriting, print publication, Web content, media relations, editing. From spot projects (the same-day copyedit) to long hauls (books, reports, sites).
Clients range from a prominent college president to a well-produced screenwriter to a local gallery artist.
Have spoken on behalf of myself and others in the Boston Globe, Christian Science Monitor, Washington Post, Yale Review, Georgetown Law magazine, local TV stations, Web sites, blogs, other venues.

David Y. Todd’s Specialties:

Public education. Higher education. Law (particularly civil litigation). Economics. Arts. Literature. Mental health, brain research, psychology.
