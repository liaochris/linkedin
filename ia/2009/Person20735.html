Arun Zachariah’s Summary

Arun is a highly skilled architect and software delivery process mentor, with a background in Information Systems (IS) architecture, and is also capable in the Information and Infrastructure architecture aspects of Enterprise Architecture.

Arun is a very experienced process mentor, specialising in the tailoring of process frameworks such as RUP for larger projects and OpenUP and Scrum for smaller projects. Arun is a contributor to the content of OpenUP (primarily around the Architecture practice).

Arun has also presented repeatedly on delivery process frameworks such as RUP and OpenUP at international conferences, including the IBM Rational Software Conference.
