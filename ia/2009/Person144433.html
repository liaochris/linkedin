Luca Franchi’s Summary

Started my career on Merrill Lynch's Asia Pacific trading floor in Hong Kong working for the Fixed Income Sales desk: lived and breathed through the 1997 Hang Seng stock market crash and the territory's handover to China.

Since Hong Kong I've been involved in some fascinating, creative, and innovative start-ups from Boston to NYC, Las Vegas, Tokyo, and London. Worked on and launched some of the world's first mobile multimedia services: Warner Music's ListenNow audio streaming service and RealNetwork's Mobile One audiovisual news service for Sprint PCS, Telefonica's (Spain's) first mobile video download service, and Disney's Mobile Greetings technology platform to mention a few. I enjoy creating and making things happen.

On a personal note, born in Italy, graduated from Babson College (Mass, USA) with a degree in Strategy / Management.

Luca Franchi’s Specialties:

Emerging technologies, product positioning / marketing, market demand creation / stimulation, international business development, mobile multimedia expertise, wireless telecom focus, start-up consulting, strategy development, mobile commerce, mobile delivery platforms
