Scott Seaborn’s Summary

Scott is a graduate with 15 years experience in technology for marketing.

Initially working in broadcast media, then through digital and mobile, Scott has ridden the wave of new marketing technologies.

Recently, Mike Dodds (CEO, Ogilvy One Worldwide) was quoted: “Few people in the UK know as much about the potential of mobile marketing: he was one of its pioneers. Not only does he understand the possibilities and limitations of the technology, he also understands how it meshes with great creative ideas to provide truly engaging and useful applications.”

For case studies of Scott's work see: www.realworldconnection.blogspot.com

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    Ogilvy Group UK comprises of 12 companies covering eight marketing and advertising disciplines. Each is a world leader.

    The wireless device is a glue that connects these marketing and advertising disciplines. Also, it’s a glue that connects brands, consumers and retailers - in real time. Wireless technology connects the physical and digital worlds in ways that no other media can.

    Brain Fetherstonhaugh (Chairman & CEO Ogilvy One Worldwide) said recently:
    “Marketing has moved away from the 4p’s (of product, price, promotion and place) and has instead evolved to the 4 E’s including experience, everyplace, exchange and evangelism.”

    If used correctly, mobile can deliver the 4 E’s and at the same time create a fresh dialogue of brand engagements with consumers. This dialogue with what Scott calls ‘branded mobile clubs’ will form the future of all marketing communications.
