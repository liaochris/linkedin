Ronan Mooney’s Summary

I am part of a seven desk sales division within CPL Resources specialising in providing recruitment solutions to numerous markets including Financial, Marketing, I.T., Engineering, Medical, FMCG, Logistics, Hotel & Catering, Media & Services. I setup, run & actively grow the Telecoms Sales desk.

I work with every major Telecommunications organisation in the Irish Market including the major blue chips in Mobile Communications and those dominating the Business-to-Business telecoms market.

I have successfully placed candidates in both junior and senior, account management and business development, direct sales and sales support roles in some the leading organisations in the telecoms market.

To clients I offer high end account management and recruitment solutions through sourcing & selection expertise, interview setup/screening, reference checks and advertising. To candidates I provide consultancy, mentoring, coaching & networking expertise while also being a key player in career progression.

Overall to both client and candidate alike I offer a 360 degree service in career & recruitment solutions.
