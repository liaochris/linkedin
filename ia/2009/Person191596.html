Rachel Cary’s Summary

Elevata Incorporated is a cross-disciplinary firm specializing in design and technology for branding. We help companies to improve their branding and business processes from the outside in, and the inside out. Our business model is virtual, allowing us to customize our project teams for each project. Project team members are creative, skilled professionals with extensive experience in a wide variety of industries. Typical projects include: Corporate Identity, Web Site Design and Development, Product Logos, Packaging, Print Collateral, Print Advertising, Copywriting (including Naming/Taglines), Print Production Management and Branding Strategy, Database Design and Development, Custom Software Development, Oracle consulting and Systems Integration.
