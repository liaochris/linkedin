Craig Wilson’s Summary

I help existing companies and start ups integrate their branding, marketing and selling in multi-channel environments.

The goal = revenue generation.

Whether it's a wholesale business moving into direct, or a multi-channel business in need of integration and optimization. Strategic or tactical, the result is a better functioning business driving more revenue.

Other projects and businesses include: creating a training and nutrition program for triathletes; coaching an NCAA Sailing Team to two National Championships; creating and launching an online coaching program for competitive sailors, creating and founding a skills-training business based on a program emphasizing relationship and team building; teaching classes on Online Marketing and Branding at both UCSB and USC, and continuing to be a sought after speaker on interactive marketing and strategy.
