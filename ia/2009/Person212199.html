Stacie Dagres’s Summary

Stacie Dagres, creates strategic publicity campaigns, which communicate a client's competitive advantage to achieve greater visibility for their products and services in the marketplace.

At this time she is very interested in working with companies, organizations and government agencies who wish to add "green publicity" to their marketing mix. She is also seeking part time or contract over flow projects with PR agencies, marketing or environmental firms.

Stacie has over 6 years experience in creating and developing innovative public relations and promotional campaigns. She is a former City Planner turned Publicist.

Her professional background is derived from government- 4.5 years as a Community Planner in Seattle, Washington, business- 2 years marketing coordination with a regional environmental consulting firm in Bellevue, Washington and over 3 years in administration and grant writing for non-government organizations.

PR services include:

General Public Relations- for businesses, government agencies and non-profit organizations

Green Teaming- align your company with a compatible non- government organization or environmental cause

Market Research

Plan, Research and Develop Strategic Promotional Campaigns

Media Relations- Press Release Preparation and Distribution

Public Affairs

B2G Relations

Conference Planning/Trade Show Promotions

Special Events Planning & Coordination

Graphic Arts Coordination
