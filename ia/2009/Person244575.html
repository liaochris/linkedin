Vladimir Campos’s Summary

My interests in technology started since very young with an Apple II. But only in 1999 when I got involved with Palm devices I started a company to develop mobile solutions and also keep a website about Palm platform with tutorials, downloads, tips, articles and a huge amount of information for end users. A partnership with Zip.Net (later acquired by UOL.com.br) brought me some revenue from advertisement and the website popularity growth helped selling more PDA solution for the corporate market.

Since I was the responsible for writing all the website content I also started writing for several American and Brazilian specialized magazines like PocketPCMag.com, ComputingUnplugged.com, Webinsider.com.br and others. The next step was covering other platforms such as Windows Mobile and Symbian. And after that we started also covering and developing corporate solutions for mobile phones.

With the increase of the demand for mobile corporate solutions, I became Director of Business Development in Brazil for LatinGo, an AvantGo partner for Latin America. This move helped my company develop better solutions and also reach the market of bigger companies.

Imagining the mobile phone market would grow fester then the PDA market I started working in the sales department of Claro, a Brazilian GSM carrier. Because of my skills and knowledge I was promoted and became Consultant for Products & Services, the same position I latter accepted in Brasil Telecom GSM, a GSM start up. In both companies I worked with product development, sales consulting and sales force training. I also worked for GVT a Brazilian landline telecom company well known for its innovative VoIP products. And nowadays I work for TIM.

After all theses year working with mobile solutions I became a specialist with a great market knowledge and key contacts on several telecom companies. Therefore, now I'm looking to work with consulting, training and projects that demand a person with my skills and experience.
