Joshua Feinberg’s Summary

Through Joshua Feinberg's current position as author and editorial director of the Computer Consulting Kit Home Study Course and executive director of the Computer Consulting Kit Club, Joshua helps computer consulting firms, value added resellers (VARs), systems/network integrator, IT solutions providers, and managed services providers (MSP's) increase their small business client base with more of the best, STEADY, high-paying clients. Joshua Feinberg's field-tested, proven tools, resources, and strategies are now in use by thousands of consultants, integrators, VARs, and SP's, all over the USA, Canada, UK, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Ireland, and many, many other nations around the Globe.

Joshua Feinberg’s Specialties:

Sales, Marketing, and Business Development for Computer Consulting Firms, Value Added Resellers (VARs), Systems/Network Integrator, IT Solutions Providers, Managed Services Providers (MSP's), Retail Computer Stores, Computer Franchise Owners, and PC Repair Companies.
