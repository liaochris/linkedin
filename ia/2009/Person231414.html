Brandon Stewart’s Summary

I have been working in Design since 2004 and have gained great experience from working both in New Zealand and the UK.

I have been fortunate enough to work with some of the best web design agencies in London and a great Interactive design agency in Newcaste upon Tyne.

Having gained this great foundation in design, I am now working for myself. I am currently working with connected designers from the UK and NZ on projects mostly attained in NZ.

I have always wanted to work for myself and now that I have some solid experience in design, I am achieving my goal.

Brandon Stewart’s Specialties:

I specialise in web design with paticular attention to typography and usability. I didn't originally study Information Architecture but I have a lot of practical experience in the field.

After working in Senior design roles I have confidently been the lead designer in many web design projects. Working collaboratively with fellow designers, project/account managers and web developers to achive a high level of quality and efficiency during website production.
