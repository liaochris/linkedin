Jens Raun’s Summary

With a background in Telco, E-commerce, Real Estate, Information Technology as well as general Business Development, Sales & Marketing on an international scale I am today successfully running Nuar Pty Ltd trading as place2live, an independent, internet only based real estate agency servicing all of Queensland (and with plans to expand into New South Wales). place2live is taking full advantage of the fact that 9 out of 10 potential property buyers find the property on the internet before they contact the listing agent. The company is offering real estate agency services to Queensland clients at very competitive rates.
For more info about the place2live commission structure please visit the place2live web site www.place2live.com.au.

If you are interested in becoming a partner and shareholder in place2live - a thriving future proof real estate agency business - and you are currently holding a Real Estate Agent licence (not only a Real Estate Salesperson licence), are a highly successful, seasoned, internet and IT savvy real estate agent, then I would love to hear from you. You can start the process by sending me a short e-mail with your CV (or link to you LinkedIn profile). I expect that you are currently working for one of the big franchise real estate agencies anywhere in Queensland or New South Wales. You will initially be working as a sub contractor to place2live, being paid 80% of the commission you generate (and paying no "Desk Fee"). If everything works out between us, then you might be one of my future 4 partners in the business.
I am also very much interested in being contacted by Australian and international property investors and developers. I am not only seeing this as an opportunity for me to assist you in identifying and securing properties as a real estate agency, but I would also like to explore the opportunities for a partnership in a property development JV (residential or commercial).
Drop me an e-mail at jens.raun@place2live.com.au!
