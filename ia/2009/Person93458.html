Heather Robertson’s Summary

I manage Boat for Sale as a small business within Trader Corporation and therefore I have become a generalist manager - with a diverse skill set both strategic and objective in nature. Two themes that seem to prevail are: project management (each magazine is a mini-project with it's own specifics and work flows) AND business analyst (as the company grew and changed, idenitifying needs and translating them into tangibles.)

Future goal is to extend my publishing skills to websites by adopting new Web 2.0 online strategies including social media. I bring to the table the ability to sell, lead and manage.

I bring a good business acumen. A clear understanding of publishing principles and the internet.
