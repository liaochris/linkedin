Marcel Cramer’s Summary

Born in May, 1972.

Got hooked on internet somewhere in 1995, almost instantaneously. Wishing that I could make a living somewhere between marketing & communications, digital media and of course internet. And so...

I started my first Internet related job at Sony Netherlands in 1997. Now, many years later, I run InCircle, an agency specializing in online media and interactive marketing & communications, based in Utrecht.

Strengths are innovative thinking, being able to bridge disciplines, strong in consultancy, analytical and clear about processes and how to get where you want to go. Marketing, communications, new concepts and inter(net)activity are my favorite subjects.

I look forward to get to know like minded spirits who are inventive, hard working, honest and fun!
