Dusan Miletich’s Summary

Real Estate Opportunity Fund

Arenda Capital Management is an Absolute Return fund management company with real estate operating expertise. What this means is that we are in the business of preserving capital as our primary investment discipline and that Relative Returns - how we compare to the REIT index or the S&P 500 index - do not shape our investment process. We are looking to invest in capital dislocating events that allow for capital to be deployed on an opportunistic basis – we look for attractive returns using minimal leverage. We source, capitalize, engineer, and operate real estate investments which meet this strict criteria of risk relative to return.

Arenda Capital Management was formed as a Joint Venture of Rhyno Capital Management and Arenda Capital with the expressed purpose of capitalizing on the fundamental dislocation in the real estate capital markets that began in the summer of 2007 and is continuing in earnest. When the future feels bleak and fear is at the forefront of the collective consciousness, the wise men buy great assets at fair and reasonable prices. We may see three such buying events in the next 50 years. Today, such an opportunity is unfolding before us.

Arenda Capital Management is based in Southern California and seeks to acquire industrial, retail, and residential real estate assets in the Western United States.

Dusan Miletich’s Specialties:

We specialize in Institutional JV Distressed Real Estate Opportunites of $10M-$100M - many are stabilized assets purchased from distressed sellers and others may require renovation, management, or repositioning components; Economic strategy, investment underwriting, legal structuring, deal capitalization, property construction, asset management, and dispositions - we have expertise in the entire lifecycle of multifamily, industrial, and retail real estate assets.
