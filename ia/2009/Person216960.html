Shonodeep Modak’s Summary

Respected for his innovative, data-driven thinking and analytical expertise, Shonodeep Modak has led teams in developing and advanced countries to create winning growth strategies that drive value in multi-million dollar investments. His exceptional ability to adapt marketing strategy to changing market conditions and then successfully selling these ideas and products to local and global customers enabled former employer, ExxonMobil, to sustain double digit growth in their consumer products division.

In 2008 Shonodeep joined the GE executive leadership program to develop new growth markets for their global energy business.
