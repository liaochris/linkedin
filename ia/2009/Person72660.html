Emilie Gobin’s Summary

Emilie is a student at HEC Paris, the leading European business school, according to the Financial Times.

After her second year at HEC, she took a year off in order to gain professional experience.She first went to work at Probis, a Software company, where she did marketing research and worked on opening a Romanian subsidiary. She then did a 5-month internship at Deloitte consulting, in order to develop her modeling and analyticals skills. She was then in charge of the International Business Development at the ad sales house Hi-Midia in Brazil. During 6 months, she managed relationships with some of Hi-Midia's competitors - in order to explore potential partnerships with them - as well as with the CEO of some non-Brazilian websites that she helped entering the Brazilian Market. She was also analysing new opportunities for Hi-Midia.

She is graduating at HEC in a Master degree in "Entrepreneurship ". This program is aimed at training graduates to be immediately operational entrepreneurs, capable of taking over a company or of starting a new firm.
