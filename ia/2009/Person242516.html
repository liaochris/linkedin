Vickie Newman’s Summary

Owner of Personal Assistant & Lifestyle Services, Inc, which is an errand & concierge business serving the Greater Huntington, WV area. One call does it all!

Employed at Dirtbusters Janitorial Services for 8 years. Was mostly John Perry II assistant. My last position there was Assistant Operations Mgr. I helped with employee hiring, training, scheduling. Handling of customer complaints and communications. Inspired a strategy for developing a new company culture. I was the cheerleader, motivator and negotiator.

Owner of Victoria's Tans-N-More, a tanning & video rental store in Proctorvile, OH 3yrs. Served on the board of small business owners at Collins Career Center, and Small Business Association of Lawrence County OH.

Self taught in everything I know. No college, went to the school of hard kocks. I know I have a lot more to learn. I am planing to quickly grow the finest concierge service in my area. I am a believer of employee growth - both personal and career.
I want to specialize in the greatest, specialized customer service care with strict confidentiallity for all of our clients.

The first name on everyones mind will be "CALL PALS!"

Vickie Newman’s Specialties:

PALS offers customized concierge services for individuals and companies. We enjoy planning small parties & special events. We complete your errands, tasks, chores, clean & organize. I use a variety of trusted vendors in the local Tri-State area. I want to save my clients time so they can be more productive at work and have time for friends, family and themselves.

PALS will become a great employer, to help employees grow, learn, blossom and have fun!
