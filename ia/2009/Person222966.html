Stephanie Silverman’s Summary

In addition to being a public speaker and speaker trainer, Stephanie has worked with executives in health care, finance, pharmaceuticals, marketing, automotive technology, sales, design and advertising as well as actors on stage and in film.
As a voiceover artist, Stephanie's voice can be heard in numerous audio books and educational materials (credited as "Stephanie Wolfe") as well as internal business audio and phone mail systems.

PRESENTATION AND SPEAKING
Stephanie combines skills and techniques developed over years of training and professional experience in the performing arts as well as with corporate presentations and public speaking.
The result is a method that allows individual strengths to emerge while addressing and diminishing personal obstacles to successful self-presentation.

ACCENT REDUCTION
The method used to diminish foreign accents is similar to that used in cultivating them for the stage or screen. The phonetic alphabet and the physiology and mechanics of sound production come together in interactive work, exercises and audio materials to produce new awareness and change in habits.
Each client receives customized “homework” to complement the work done in session. Integrated into this process are general presentation skills to help the individual successfully improve communication at work and elsewhere.

COMMUNICATION STYLE DEVELOPMENT
Understanding how we currently communicate is the first step to positive change. Through detailed observation and constructive feedback, behavior may be improved to better reflect the client's intentions and increase the probability of a positive outcome.
Each client receives their own communication style assessment to help determine the image and intentions currently being conveyed. The focus is then shifted to examine the goals of good communication and the skills and techniques that must be developed to reach these goals. This new insight and behavior will lead to greater confidence and success.
