Roberto Pedini’s Summary

Roberto joined Wyeth in May 1999 as Aprilia plant Project Manager for ERP implementation.
Since February 2007 after taking increasing responsibilities within Wyeth in the areas of Project, Compliance and Materials management he is Senior Director External Supply Network Consumer Europe Middle East Africa with the mission to consolidate the number of third parties in the region and to build a robust organization able to manage those contractors.
During his 10 years prior to joining Wyeth, he had several succesful experiences always in pharmaceutical companies like Janssen-Cilag, Sanofi Aventis, IDI in which he held positions starting from Purchasing, passing through Planning, Logistics, Project Management until Materials Management.

Roberto earned a bachelor of business and economics degree from Università La Sapienza in Rome, Italy. He is divorced and has three children.
