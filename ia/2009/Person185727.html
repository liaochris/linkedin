Peter Jarunek’s Summary

I went through a variety of IT project roles and many different IT technologies and environments. Nowadays I'm specialized in SAP XI (Exchange infrastructure) / PI (Process Integration) consulting, which I started in 2005 with. My project role experience comprise development, consulting and team-leading too. As a professional developer I was working mainly with Oracle PL/SQL and Java, but my experience is also in other languages - VBA, PHP, XML, HTML, CSS, Pascal, Object Pascal (for Delphi) and of course ABAP. My consultancy experience started as an ERP consultant and later I became a Business Intelligence consultant, so I had an overview and experience with corporate IT complexity. Later, with this experience I became an EAI consultant, developing and maintaining EAI solutions developed in J2EE environment and a few month later I started to develop and implement solutions based on SAP XI (SAP Exchange Infrastructure) / SAP PI (SAP Process Intagration) which I do until today. I have a lot of experience with SAP XI (SAP Exchange Infrastructure) / SAP PI (SAP Project Integration) implementation and went through a lot of full life-cycle projects starting with analysis of customer needs, designing the best solution, developing it and finally implementing & maintaining it. I have not only experience with A2A integration but also with B2B integration using SAP XI (SAP Exchange Infrastructure) / SAP PI (Process Integration) and I've implemented it with almost all types of possible technologies. In addition, I have implemented also B2B integration of 2 big corporations using "non-standard" Seeburger AS2 adapter. With this experience I am able to work as a developer or lead any stage of an integration project and bring it to a successful end.
