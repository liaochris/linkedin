Willie Brake (callwillie.com)’s Summary

Willie Brake, a certified computer professional with advanced degrees in Technology Management, will lead the company as it establishes itself as a high quality full service technology firm in the Atlanta and Detroit Metropolitian areas. Prior to the creation of All About Technology, Willie spent more than 12 years holding increasingly responsible positions in the automobile, consulting, education and manufacturing industries in locations around the world. He is currently the Executive Director for the Center For Creative Arts, Inc. and a faculty member at The University of Phoenix and Wayne State University as well as a speaker for various organizations. Willie is also a member of various civic, community and philanthropy groups which includes Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, United Way and Service Corps of Retired Executives (SCORE).

Constant evolving technologies offer small businesses an opportunity to work smarter. Effective use of information technology is crucial for sustained success in this modern business world of downsizing, reegineering, outsourcing, global competition, and ever changing technologies. Constant change places enormous demand on business and inforamtion technology managers. Our business solutions help our clients get control of and use information technlogy to maximize profitability.

In the end, All About Technology's strategy is not about products, services, or distribution models. It is about customer satisfaction. That is our top corporate priority, and everything we do - from how we design our services to how we do business with you - is focused on enhancing our ability to respond to your needs, and to meet and exceed your expectations. We look forward to the opportunity to demonstrate why All About Technology should be your IT partner of choice.
