Karen Perry, MSW’s Summary

Karen Perry is founder and president of Event Journal, Inc. The company launched in 2002 with the introduction of the first web-based alternative to paper ad journals typically found at dinner galas and major fundraising events.

EVENTjournal.com maximizes potential for fundraising events by unleashing the power of the Web and its online capabilities to add value on all fronts. EVENTjournal.com is a full-service event marketing system that transforms traditional ad journals into a tool that maximizes support with increased event publicity, more value to sponsors and outsourcing to a professional team that frees fundraisers to raise more money. Clients include universities, hospitals, charities, business groups, religious organizations and more that cite Event Journal as a source of increased revenues and decreased headaches associated with the event.

Karen Perry received her B.A. from The Pennsylvania State University School of Communications and has worked at several of NY's top advertising agencies. Her roles have included strategic planning, media buying, art direction, account management and business development. Ms. Perry's media experience includes stints in newspapers, television, web development and multi-media.

Additionally, Ms. Perry holds a Master's in Social Work from Adelphi University. Her professional background includes advocacy, development, casework and outreach positions with a variety of agencies.

Karen Perry has served on boards and committees of non-profit, business and community organizations over the years, holding numerous leadership positions. She has completed many professional training, personal development and continuing education programs and is active in a wide range of professional and community organizations and activities.
