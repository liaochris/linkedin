Aaron Nabus’s Summary

Management level Graphic Designer with a well balanced mixture of corporate and freelance experience. Whether I'm doing work for a Fortune 500 company or a local start-up, I can combine my creativity and project management skills to efficiently create a "Signature Image," that can be used to brand a company at a tradeshow, online, or when meeting a potential client for the first time...where the first impression really counts!

Currently, I manage a group of Graphic Designers that specialize in the creation and maintenance of retention products (direct mail, email templates, and personal webpages) for OneCommand.

For samples of my work please view my website at: www.mavencreative.com.
