Cole Harris’s Summary

My specific interest is in the development & application of computational intelligence algorithms & statistical techniques for identifying patterns in real world data.

Cole Harris’s Experience

  • Sr VP Discovery & Co-Founder

    Exagen Diagnostics

    (Privately Held; Biotechnology industry)

    January 2002 — Present (8 years 1 month)

    Exagen was spun out of Quasar to further develop and apply Quasar's pattern recognition engine in the medical/genomics field.

    In my current position, I am responsible for Exagen's biomarker discovery efforts. This entails both development of computational techniques for analyzing genomics data, as well as the application of these techniques to identify clinically viable biomarkers.

    Thus far we have discovered several putative biomarkers in cancer, infectious disease, gastrointestinal disease, and neurodevelopmental disorders. Some of these discoveries have been taken through clinical trials and those have been validated. To date, three US patents have been awarded.
