Aabid A. K. Dhamani’s Summary

I have a strong liking for 3D Character Animation and I want to show the world what I can do. Presently, I am working as a Freelance 3D Character Animator for Maxsell Communications (Pakistan). I believe I can contribute my share of creativity to your company.

My goal is to become great in character animation and create my own piece of short movie.

My journey starts from the 2D software, Adobe Photoshop and moved on to 3D software, Autodesk Maya in 2005. I had done my certification course in 2005, named as Dot Media Effects – Post Production, in which I got to know the 3D world of Maya. I get my first job in 2006 as a 3D Character Animator and later promoted to Lead in 2008, where I managed and supervised commercials and commissioned films.

Currently I am working as a freelancer, where I get to deliver the project from start to end, supervise the whole team and working along it.
