David Marcus’s Summary

Founded GTN telecom, the 1st alternative telecommunications operator in Switzerland in 1996. Raised over USD 17 million in VC funding and managed the exponential growth of the operations to become Switzerland's #3 alternative carrier. Sold the company to World Access in 2000.

Founded Echovox in 2000 and developed the company's activities in over 15 countries. Development of the company includes signing partnership agreements with over 50 mobile operators for mobile content delivery and monetization and setup of one of the largest mobile content billing and delivery network in Europe and the US.

Founded VOX telecom in 2003, a wholesale voice carrier with interconnections throughout the Americas (North, South and Latin America), Europe, Middle East and Asia. VOX telecom now serves over 60% of the incumbent operators across the globe.

In 2006, co-founded echo6, a Joint Venture between Echovox and French media group M6 (Metropole Television), active in mobile media entertainment and TV-Mobile interactivity and convergence.

David Marcus’s Specialties:

Inception and management of fast-growing companies with a strong focus in mobile/online entertainment and web services. Strong technology, Internet and telecom background and global large scale deployments experience.
