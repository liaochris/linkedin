Anne Marie Malfi’s Summary

I started my "marketing" virtual assistance company because over the years I have watched many real estate professionals get overwhelmed with handling many of the day to day marketing and administrative tasks that were keeping them behind their desks rather than where they should be - out in their markets with their clientse. The key to working with a "Malfi Marketing Solutions" is the relationship that will be built - a partnership - that will allow me to get to know you and your business so that together we can effectively collaborate and discover ways to increase both your online and offline presence.

If it's not evident to you by now, consumers are very knowledgeable and computer savvy and are using the internet to not only search for homes but for agents and real estate professionals who they feel are like minded and can lead them to the results they are looking for.

Working with Malfi Marketing Solutions will give you the advantage of having a partner in your business who will compliment your business goals by providing you with focus and support on all your marketing efforts so that your business takes its rightful position in your market.

I spent 11 years with one company in the Temporary Staffing industry in which I was responsilbe for an area that included 3 branch offices and 2 onsite customer locations. I consistently sold over 3 million dollars in services each year to local clients and was responsible for all my own marketing, networking, sales, and overseeing operations, staff training and mentoring. I'm telling you all of this because I have walked in similiar shoes - I know what it takes to build your presence both online and offline. I know what it takes to be in sales. Allow me if you will to demonstrate even if on a small scale how my knowledge and experience can help you take your business to the next level. I promise - you won't be disappointed and you'll only be wondering - why didn't I do this sooner.
