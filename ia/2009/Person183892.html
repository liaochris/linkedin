Paul Wehage’s Summary

As a performing soloist and musicologist, I saw that there was a need for documenting and promoting music by both living and historical composers whose work was directly important to my career. Musik Fabrik began in 1997 as a means of accomplishing this goal, and ten years later, we have published 1000 works by composers from the US, Europe, the Middle East and Asia. With such composers as Germaine Tailleferre, Jacques Leguerney, Antoine Tisné, Gian Paolo Chiti, and important emerging composers such as Carson Cooman, Jean-Yves Malmasson, Jean-Thierry Boisseau, Moyuru Maeda and others, we feel that the importance of our work speaks for itself.

Through our budding series of recordings, the large number of concerts organised every year involving our composers and our ever-growing list of retail distribution partners, we feel confident that our actions will succeed in documenting these important musical works for generations to come.
