Keith Hanna’s Summary

I am in charge of serving the Communications Industry for TeamQuest Corporation. I help IT organizations to easily manage their heterogeneous server farms in large scale data centers with a single tool, the TeamQuest Performance Software suite. In addition, we help IT to accurately predict the capacity requirements of their servers to ensure service availability of their applications. Here's where the rubber hits the road...

IT Operations can save $ millions in IT budget by right-sizing their physical & virtual server purchases, consolidations, DR sizing, etc.

TeamQuest is the global leader in IT Service Optimization (ITSO). Specializing in software that helps IT organizations consistently meet service levels at a minimum cost, TeamQuest provides quick and accurate capacity modeling and performance management solutions. Companies around the world trust TeamQuest to help improve IT service delivery while mitigating risks, allowing them to proactively optimize IT resources while minimizing infrastructure costs.

Feel free to visit www.TeamQuest.com to learn more about state-of-the-art Capacity Management software solutions and best practices.
