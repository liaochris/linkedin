Dave Raval’s Summary

My professional expertise is in commercialising new technology developments - through identifying opportunities, developing business plans, and finding partners and finance.

I have also acted as an interim manager, with roles including leading a start-up internet security company for a spin-out from a FTSE 250 company, and heading up research and development activities for five lines of London Underground.

I am also involved in politics, as the Liberal Democrat Parliamentary Candidate for Hackney South and Shoreditch - a seat where the Lib Dems are second behind Labour - see www.daveraval.com.

Dave Raval’s Specialties:

Market areas of expertise: energy/renewables/clean technology/energy efficiency, transportation and nanotechnology.

Experience of FTSE100 companies through to start-ups and the public sector.

Has lived and worked in the UK, Germany and USA.
