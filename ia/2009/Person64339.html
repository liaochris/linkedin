Disha Vyas’s Summary

Feel free to contact me if you are looking for an ad agency to help you manage any marketing and communication needs. Please check out our portfolio at www.thebodisgency.com.

We work well with start up companies and become an extension of their marketing department. For some, we take on the role as the marketing department, developing and implementing strategy, while they grow their business to the point where they can support marketing initiatives internally.

For others, we support the internal team helping to develop and define the brand and execute specific brand communications for initial launch or to broaden awareness.

Regardless, we partner closely and collaboratively and certainly can help your start-up out.

Once you've had a chance to review our work, if you feel we may be a good fit, I’ll be happy to discuss your company's specific needs in more detail.

ABOUT ME:

With over 5 years of multi-channel marketing and interactive experience in addition to strategic planning, analytical, and creative development expertise, I have earned respect in both agency and client settings and have worked with companies such as Bayer, Holiday Inn, Choice Hotel, Royal Canadian Mint, Easter Seals Society, Canadian Cancer Society.

Supported clients in developing and integrating online and offline marketing programs focused on consumer healthcare and service industry. Also managed the strategic development of corporate websites and migrated traditional DM efforts online to increase ROI for Clients such as RCM, Lifesciences, Holiday Inn etc.

Currently my position includes develop leads and establish new business relations, spearheading all aspects of Account Management and Client Servicing division, structuring, organizing, staffing, setting the strategy.
