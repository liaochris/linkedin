Patrick Speice’s Summary

White collar criminal defense experience includes representing both individual and corporate clients in Foreign Corrupt Practices Act matters and securities investigations, as well as providing advice to clients regarding implementation of corporate compliance programs. International trade regulation experience includes advising clients regarding compliance with economic sanctions laws, export controls, and foreign investment regulations. General litigation experience includes representation of clients at all stages of litigation in both federal and state courts, including discovery, motions practice, and preparation for trial.
