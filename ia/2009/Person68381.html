Dustin Engel’s Summary

A proven leader in online media and strategic marketing, Dustin Engel joined Range Online Media with more than 10 years of experience with both consumer and business products and services. He has led strategic marketing and designed media campaigns for top-tier brands such as Match.com and American Greetings Interactive. He served in the role of director of online marketing for Stamps.com and was a key member of the launch team of Blockbuster Online. In this role, he was instrumental in the online marketing efforts that allowed Blockbuster Online to be named the #1 online retailer in year-over-year growth of unique visitors by comScore Media Metrix in 2004 as well as achieve 1M active subscribers within 10 months of launch.

Engel is currently the Vice President of Strategy and Media at Range Online Media, which was named in 2007 and 2008 as a top 25 search marketing agency by AdAge and to Inc. Magazine’s 2007 Inc. 5,000 list of the fastest growing private companies in America. Range was acquired in September 2008 by iProspect, the leading search engine marketing firm as evidenced by the 2008 Forrester Wave Report.

Engel is a graduate of Seattle University with a BA in Business Administration and a major in Marketing. A well-recognized industry expert, he is a regular contributor to highly regarded industry publications such as MediaPost, iMedia Connection and DM News as well as a frequent speaker at conferences including Digital Hollywood and CitiFinancial’s Entertainment, Media and Technology Conference.
