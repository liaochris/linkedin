Scott Eagan’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL OBJECTIVE:
To move into a career that allows me to tap into my experience in education, training, curriculum development and publishing. Relocation is certainly an option but would also be interested in tele-commuting.

K-12 & UNIVERSITY CLASSROOM EXPERIENCE:
Highly motivated and award winning classroom instructor with a proven track record for providing unique classroom experiences to enhance learning opportunities.

11+ years of college and university teaching experience. Work has included instruction in English, Reading and Study Skills. Work as also included serving as a field supervisor providing clinical supervision to student teaching interns.

Extensive background in working with students of all ages, academic levels and in diverse socio-economic settings.

CURRICULUM AND INSTRUCTION DESIGN
Extensive portfolio of successful currciulum assessment and design projects including work that aligns curriculum across grade levels and content areas.

Background in cross curricular studies.

Successful experience teaching inservice and continuing education courses for colleagues in terms of implementation of new instructional models.

Over 10 years of development of new programs within the K-12 and collegiate levels incorporating technology and innovative students based methods.

PUBLISHING AND WRITING
5 years in the publishing industry serving as the owner and agent of Greyhaus Literary Agency. As an agent, I provide agent representation to writers working for major world-wide publishers.

Editorial work with current client list to develop projects that will be marketable.

Extensive background in training and presentation on both the writing process and the publishing industry. This has also included serving on numerous editor and agent panels. Most recently, I was asked to speak that the International Women's Fiction Festival in Matera, Italy.

Scott Eagan’s Specialties:

Contract Negotiation, Literary Acquisitions, Curriculum and Instruction Development, Instructional Experience, Classroom Experience, Editing and writing instruction
