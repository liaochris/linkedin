About Martin:
=======================================
With formal qualifications in software engineering, Martin has over nine years experience in the design and management of complex enterprise-wide business systems. In the past, he has lectured and tutored at universities, presented at international conferences and has even won various scholarships.

Interests in interactive online technologies, Web2.0 and social networks saw Martin also complete a Master’s research degree in the area of "knowledge networking." The research examined the behaviour and development of social networks to uncover ways of commercialising and leveraging the "knowledge within."

Martin has helped many businesses effectively engage, communicate and manage their online presence to create more profits with less work.
