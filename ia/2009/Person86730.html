Gil Yehuda’s Summary

Industry analyst, researching and advising on the Enterprise Web 2.0 collaboration industry. I have a background in the technologies, techniques, and behavioral implications of Web 2.0 for enterprise environments. I was also an Enterprise Architect with a track record of successful management of large scale technical and strategic programs. I am an experienced speaker and teacher, motivator and mentor.

  • Senior Analyst

    Forrester Research

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; FORR; Market Research industry)

    May 2008 — Present (9 months)

    I serve Information and Knowledge Management professionals by 1. writing research on topics that affect their work and inform their choices, 2. by providing consulting services to my customers and 3. by providing lectures and workshops at events. I write about how Enterprise Web 2.0 collaboration technologies and practices fit into broader workplace strategies focused on workforce productivity.

    My research areas cover: Applying Web 2.0 to the enterprise in the areas of collaboration and intranet content. Leveraging the democratization of technology and content to help companies grow knowledge beyond the confines of subject-matter experts. Where do employees put stuff? Where to they find stuff? How do you ensure intranet content is correct and of high quality? Who is the information steward? Who is the information architect?

    If you find this interesting, then read my research at Forrester.com or contact Forrester to get more information about how I can help you out.
