Steve Green’s Summary

Leveraging a career in networking and relationship building by utilizing and training other business professionals in LinkedIn for business development and career advancement.

An accomplished Human Resources professional with strengths in the full range of HR disciplines coupled with a specialization in Talent Acquisition and Talent Management to meet strategic business objectives. Successfully supported organizations with ongoing high demand as well as those going through phenomenal growth, acquiring new businesses, entering new regions or deploying new technologies. Built and managed dynamic, pro-active HR and recruiting teams to meet demand. Utilize metrics to continually monitor the HR organization to ensure it meets all service level agreements, management satisfaction and compliance. Industries served have included Professional Services, Management Consulting, Financial Services, Insurance, Publishing and International Government.

Steve Green’s Specialties:

Dynamic trainer on LinkedIn & Social Networking.

Previously on the “Capture Team” that identified acquisitions & sold IT, BPO, HRO & RPO engagements to Fortune 500 corporations. Conducted due diligence & priced the engagements from an HR perspective. Worked on the marketing strategy & presented the HR solution to clients. Managed the transition and integration of employees including compensation & benefits equalization, employee relations, orientation to new benefits, procedures & culture.
