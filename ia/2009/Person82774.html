Gabriel Gervais’s Summary

Growing up, I was fortunate enough to have seen my father build a regional political organization from the ground up. My childhood was spent witnessing the impact of people’s involvement in the community through determination and hard work. At the age of 17, while studying in college and during my summer vacation, I started my very first company, employing two friends, and recruiting others around me. After travelling throughout Europe for a year and during my university studies in Political Science, I became interested in Web Based Applications and the new possibilities presented in helping communities become organized and more efficient in order to resolve complex social needs. This interest lead me to build a successful eCommerce business that was able to help lower income families find housing, while providing high end services to those more fortunate. My passion for innovative, effective and truly helpful organizations has been a key element in shaping my career path.

In my current role at IsaiX, I am accountable for our business development in the Province of Quebec. To be successful, our consulting team has to provide long-term solutions to complex problems that our customers are facing, by conceptualizing innovative Organizational Development and Learning strategies. Our consultants provide measurable, sustainable, and effective solutions.
