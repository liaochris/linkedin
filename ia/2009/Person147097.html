Sherrin Ross Ingram, JD’s Summary

Consistently making “profitable” decisions is key to authentic growth and sustainability. As a strategic thinking specialist, I help clients improve the quality of their decisions by helping them develop and consciously use strategic thinking skills. At the International Center for Strategic Planning, I (with the help of a great team) provide structured assistance with one or all phases of the strategic planning and implementation process. I provide confidential advisory services to board members and coach executives who want to develop strategic plans for their careers, gain insight into professional challenges, and enhance their strategic thinking skills to improve performance.

I regularly speak to audiences on the topics of strategic planning, strategic thinking, and holistic wealth creation. My most requested presentations are:

- Creating Strategic Advantage
- How to Develop (or Enhance) Strategic Thinking Skills
- Strategic Thinking and Intuition as a Strategic Advantage
- How To Make Insightful Decisions
- How to Develop a Wealth Mentality®

Other “hats” I wear include being an autism advocate, author, attorney, and exercise enthusiast.

PERSONAL NOTE about the Foundation for Real Nutrition and Sustainable Living:

This is an organization I founded after witnessing the positive impact a traditional diet was having on my son’s recovery from autism. I believe diet was foundational for bringing my son out of an autistic fog. I was compelled to create a resource and social forum to raise the nutritional consciousness of our communities and allow others to witness nourishing principles in action so that they could develop the confidence to apply them in their daily lives. I am proud to say we are a very active, vibrant grass roots movement making a real difference.

If there is anything you can do to support our mission or spread the word, please contact me.
