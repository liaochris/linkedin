Tom Mallory’s Summary

12 years in executive search and 15 years in job transition consulting. On the executive search side, I place executives at small, technology enabled service companies. Many of these companies have been online marketplaces. On the job transition side, I help business people from a wide range of industries.

Prior experience was in marketing and sales positions at 3 start-ups (Futurestep, 1-800-DATABASE, and Nustar International) and 2 multinationals (UPS, Sea-Land Services). Former U.S. Army Captain and Paratrooper.

My goal is to help people in their career decisions and to assist companies find and retain executives who fit best into their organization. Outside of work and beyond my family (Eleanor, Daniel, Miles, and Bliss), my goal is to help children develop leadership skills by working with 4 organizations (Boy Scouts, Outward Bound, Boys & Girls Clubs, and Autism Society of America).

Tom Mallory’s Specialties:

For Executive Search: Small technology enabled service companies, in particular green technology, online marketplaces, family owned companies.

For Job Transition: Helping individuals optimize their mind, clarify their direction, and synchronize their marketing.
