Fabrice PEREZ’s Summary

A highly experienced business process and applications consultant, I have more than 15 years of experience in helping renowned companies across the world achieve better business performance with technology.
Expert in Siemens Product Lifecycle Management Software and Microsoft Dynamics Enterprise Resource Planning, I have a wealth of knowledge in the enterprise systems space, ranging from enterprise resource planning, supply chain management through to customer relationship management systems.
Prior to my engagement as a Solutions Consultant & Pre Sales, I have worked extensively in Europe and the Asia Pacific region, holding appointments as Regional Financial Analyst and Regional Financial Controller for global companies such as Wincor Nixdorf, Siemens and Schneider Electric. Following which I was hired by Microsoft before moving on to a career in consulting and training.
I bring the necessary valuable expertise from various industries (ranging from manufacturing to distribution, research and development, engineering and logistics to retail) to help organizations better analyze processes and profitability and to leverage on technology to make sales, production and operating processes more efficient.
