Karen Park’s Summary

Training project manager responsible for developing and teaching programs for online and newspaper staff members. Classes include: facilitation skills, change management, train the trainer, Search Engine Optimization, Social Media, Social Networking, writing tight on deadline.

I am also the newsroom recruiter, which includes managing the summer internship program. I also direct newsroom contest entries.

Previous experience includes leading development and production of award-winning consumer health and nutrition section “Better Heatlh” online and in the newspaper; supervising staff of more than 20 sports staffers who edited and designed on tight nightly deadlines.
