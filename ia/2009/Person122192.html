Joseph Wood’s Summary

I work with companies to increase their profitability through better utilization of their Human Resource strategies.

I have worked with all levels of managers from management to the Executive suite to identify personnel needs, job specifications, job duties, qualifications and skills. Used various methods to search for candidates including referral networking, schools, associations, job fairs and internet based resources. Developed internal job requisition procedure and forms. Developed plan with HR and managers to prioritize and quickly fill open positions. Assisted managers in writing and editing job descriptions. Maintained applicant tracking system as well as manpower planning/forecasting matrix.

Joseph Wood’s Specialties:

Business Partner– Demonstrated strength in building relationships with hiring managers, including defining organization needs, open position specifications, recruiting strategy, as well as the overall recruiting process.

Candidate Sourcing – Extensive experience initiating contact with possible qualified applicants for specific job openings including posting and sourcing via the internet and a variety of other sources, to include cold calling from a targeted list, and from network sources.
