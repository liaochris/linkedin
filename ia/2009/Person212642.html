Sean Nelson’s Summary

Hello, my name is Sean Nelson and since 2001 I've helped 1,000's of Individuals, the Self-Employed and Small Businesses in Atlanta save up to 60% on their health insurance and employee benefits.

How would you like to save up to 60% on your health insurance?

As an independent broker I work with the top companies to find you the plan the best meets your needs and budget. If you've

◊ Recently received a rate increase
◊ Have been on Cobra for longer than 9 months
◊ Been on the same plan longer than 1 year

I can likely help you save money.

There's no secret to what I do and no "One" health plan that works for everyone. I simply ask the right questions to determine what you are looking for and then I research all of the available companies to see who has the best fit and price.

Just as important as finding the best price, is explaining the differences in coverage in a way that the average person can understand. Then each year at renewal we do it all over.
