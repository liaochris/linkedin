Mustapha Derras’s Summary

A carrier dedicated to technology business development and management for major worldwide industry actors. A success track record in 6 different industries starting from the basics in systems and software design and development, embedded development solutions, hardware and software analysis and conceptions to grow up till high level management in different positions as R&D director, engineering director, product marketing manager, customer support director, executive director and member of board of directors.

During the last 20 years I addressed deeply domains like (but not only) market analysis, business development analysis for products and services, market strategy development, guidance and adoption plans accelerators, R&D management, software products developments and dissemination, large and small project management including distributed teams, nearshoring and outsourcing, business analysis and risks assessments, large participation to collaborative networking and knwoledge sharing as a researcher, engineer and manager, project selection, tracking and monitoring with strong capacity to manage from 10 to +300 peoples, merge and acquisition schemas, deep ICT technology knowledge.
