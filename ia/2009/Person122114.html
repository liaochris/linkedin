  • Board Member

    GRCSI

    (Non-Profit; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    September 2009 — Present (3 months)

    Governance Risk Compliance Security International

    GRCSI is an international organization comprised of individuals and organizations related to one or more of the fields of governance, risk management, compliance, and security whose goals are to promote best practices in these fields though education, certification, and accreditation.

    GRCSI was founded in early 2009 by professionals and practitioners across the globe that identified the need for an authoritative body in the realm of risk management. The GRCSI founders have an extensive background in one of the concentration areas (finance, information, and operations), and have pooled their knowledge to create the Certified Risk Management Professional (CRMP) certfication program.

    GRCSI offers a series of professional certifications to accredit the knowledge and experience of its members, and will be offering training programs, conferences, and seminars in the future. GRCSI offers an associate level designation and a base credential that are available now. Three different areas of specialization and an expert credential will be available in the second half of 2010. Full details of the designation and certifications are available at: http://www.grcsi.org/certification.html

    More information about GRCSI and the CRMP certification can be found at http://www.grcsi.org
