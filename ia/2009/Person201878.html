Rob Walker’s Summary

Rob Walker, recognized as one of Idaho's "Top 40 Under 40" business leaders, is a seasoned music, marketing, and business expert who offers a wide-base of marketing expertise. He is a leading expert in Social Media applications for business and serves as a keynote speaker on the subject.

Consulting Practices

Rob works with companies and government agencies to formulate marketing plans that integrate Social Media as one component of the overall marketing strategy. Walker serves as a Social Media Trainer with LeapFox Learning where he specializes in corporate training.

Walker holds an MBA from Duke University in Finance/ Marketing as well as a B.A. from New York University.

Educational Roles

He is Program Head for Marketing Management at the College of Western Idaho. Walker has been on the University of Phoenix faculty since 2001 where he has facilitated over 150 courses in Economic Theory, Marketing, Public Relations, and Operations Management. Previously, Walker taught sales management and marketing at Boise State University.

In the community, Walker serves on the Meridian Arts Commission providing leadership to arts programs in the region.
