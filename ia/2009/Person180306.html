Paige Johnson’s Summary

I am a senior US Department of Defense Systems Engineer, Program Manager, and Test and Evaluation Engineer with a current active Top Secret/SCI security clearance. I have 39 years experience: 15 years with the Missile Defense Agency (MDA) in weapon system acquisition (systems engineering, program management, program protection, site selection, site development, environmental compliance, test, and evaluation); 17 years with the Army Corps of Engineers in military and civilian air base design, evaluation, research, development, Army policy, construction, environmental compliance, contracting, and pricing; as well as 7 years with private industry in transportation design and site development.

Under the Base Realignment and Closure initiatives, my current position is transferring to Huntsville, AL in the October-December 2008. However, my family and I desire to remain in the National Capitol Area. Therefore, I am seeking a Senior Engineer or Program Manager position in: Southern Maryland (Calvert, Charles, Prince George’s, and St. Mary’s Counties); as well as the Virginia Area of Dahlgren, King George, and Fredericksburg; or telecommuting from my home office, which can be certified for Top Secret Collateral operations and comfortably accommodate 10 to 12 clients in a senior executive setting.
