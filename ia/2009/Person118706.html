Jonathan Arriaza’s Summary

You may be wondering, what exactly is a Creative Solutionist? Well, I am someone who specializes is finding the most effective way to creatively achieve objectives. With intense research backed by a Marketing Management background, I strive to execute original solutions aimed at positive results.

I have been hired by a variety of companies and individuals in the Entertainment, Fashion, and even Non-Profit industries. Whether the goal is to create a new Brand Image or increase Brand Awareness by redefining existing identities, I come at each client with a fresh slate full of original ideas.

Once we determine what the client's goals are and identify their target market, we then decide which medium is best fit to communicate their intended message. Because I constantly strive to push my creative boundaries by learning new methods of creating, whether it be Print Design, Web Design, Photography, Motion Graphics, or Video, limitations are rare.

It is my goal to realize your goal.
