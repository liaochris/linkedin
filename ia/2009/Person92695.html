Harry Roisman’s Summary

We purchase apartment complexes...minimum 100 units preferably in emerging markets with minimum 20% vacancy. We look for properties with ineffective management. We will consider anywhere in the US if it is possible to complete a workout, i.e., fill the vacancies to achieve a reasonable cash flow within 6-9-12 months. We purchase REO properties or properties in probate or bankruptcy. With apartment complexes, we increase net operating income (NOI) by providing quality affordable housing to average people affected by today's economy & individuals with disabilities or handicaps. Therefore, jobs are provided which results in increases to the tax base.

Some locations we presently prefer are Florida, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Virginia, Texas, Oklahoma,Kansas, & Missouri. We intend to convert some of these properties to "Green" if federal grant money is available. Properties are refinanced after significant increases in net operating income.

Our residential division brokers CUSTOM BANK OWNED portfolios of S/F homes any where in the USA. They are originally purchased for PENNIES on the dollar. Additional optional services are available through independent professionals. Examples of these services are correcting title issues, supplying pictures of the properties, re-selling the properties using a LLC or land trust using an agreement for deed. The new occupant is paying less money than renting in that same area. We then offer to help them improve their credit & then re-finance.

Our services are offered using websites of our independent professionals. Our investment opportunities are changing very rapidly.
