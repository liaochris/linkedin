Tad Benson’s Summary

I am a marketing executive with over 17 years of direct and online marketing experience. I am looking to continue my career in an executive level marketing role where I can run marketing for either a startup or for a larger company’s online business unit or channel.

I led the Internet channel marketing for 21st Century Insurance (AIG) and demonstrated top and bottom-line success by increasing monthly online leads by 772% and monthly online sales by 1,097%. I did that while simultaneously reducing the channel’s cost per sale by 88%. Most recently, I ran a software company’s re-branding campaign that generated well over 50 million free press impressions and increased traffic to company’s Web site by over 2,000%. I have run everything from multi-million-dollar integrated on and off-line campaigns to free viral social media campaigns. I was also the 38th employee at GoTo.com where I ran online marketing for the company that literally invented paid search (SEM).
