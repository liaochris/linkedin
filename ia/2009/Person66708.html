Dov Nelkin’s Summary

I am a confident public speaker with a talent for identifying key issues and clarifying complex matters -- abilities honed through several years in secondary and higher education and media training.

I bring to each position analytic ability sharpened through graduate schooling in philosophy and applied ethics (primarily health-care ethics, but also business ethics).

I also have extensive experience writing and editing for both academic and popular journals and newspapers.

Conflict management and people management are additional skills that I have developed as an educator/adviser.
