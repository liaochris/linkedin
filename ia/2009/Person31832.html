Brad Whitchurch’s Summary

CEO, SEAL SHIELD
Our company manufactures the world's only dishwasher safe keyboards and mice. Our primary market is healthcare infection control. 2 Million people get a hospital acquired infection annually in this country. Of those, close to 100,000 die each year, making hospital acquired infections a TOP10 KILLER of Americans.

Independent tests have shown that computer keyboards are among the dirtiest surfaces in hospitals and the workplace with over 400 times more bacteria than the average toilet.

On average, 25% of hospital keyboards harbor the superbug, MRSA. MRSA is a staph infection which is resistant to antibiotics and is deadly.

Hospitals are implementing Seal Shield's solutions because they are more economical and preferable to a keyboard with cover solution.

Brad Whitchurch’s Specialties:

Seal Shield has recently developed the Silver Seal line of Antimicrobial products. The Silver Seal keyboard and Mouse is not only washable, it is antimicrobial. We work with other manufacturers to incorporate our Silver Seal Antimicrobial technology in to their products as well.
