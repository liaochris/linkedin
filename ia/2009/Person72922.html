Emily Woody Bibens’s Summary

Woody Bibens and Associates is a progressive management, design, and consulting firm. One of our clients is Midland Steel Company, a steel fabricator near Kansas City who we are helping to enter the wind industry supply chain.

As management consultants, we are the people small and mid-size business owners turn to when they need advice and recommendations, projects managed, web sites and graphics enhanced, an outside opinion, or someone to help guide the company through a period of change. They turn to us because we question the status quo, seek always to improve, tackle each problem or project with a “figure it out” attitude, focus on the entire company and its long-term strategy, and maintain integrity and confidentiality.

Our sustainability services division specializes in consulting for manufacturing firms within the sustainable energy industry. We advise and can project manage branding efforts, such as a Web site and graphics work, and business structure and process changes needed to ensure that the company is a serious competitor in the sustainable energy industry.

Our management services division specializes in working to make your organization operate more effectively, efficiently, and successfully, helping to position your company to compete successfully in the future. We provide the impartial advice and recommendations necessary to improve business processes and business structure. We look at trade-offs, what needs to be cut back or eliminated to free the resources necessary for what needs to be improved and implemented. We enhance and implement Web sites, Web 2.0 technologies, graphics design, and branding strategies to mutually complement business processes, structure, and company culture. We can advise but more importantly, we can project manage these changes.
