Maria Elena Arias’s Summary

Maria Elena Arias is the CEO of Colonial Guaranty & Title Inc. Colonial Guaranty & Title Inc was established in 1995. They deliver 24/7 ultimate title services on line anytime, anywhere.

Maria Elena Arias was born in Havana, Cuba and migrated with her parents in June of 1962 seeking political asylum. She grew up in Miami and graduated from Notre Dame Academy and sought a Paralegal Certificate from Florida International University.
Maria Elena was the Head Paralegal in charge of the Real Estate Department for a large law firm in Miami for ten years before she decided to open her first title company with Builders, HG Land Development in 1988 and then expanded in 1995 with her own title company, Colonial Guaranty & Title Inc. They are a statewide and national title insurance company.

Maria Elena holds a Real Estate Broker’s License. She owned and operated Century 21 Capital Realty in Miami and had over 40 real estate agents in 1998. She sold that company in 1998 to her partners and has since then dedicated herself to her Title Insurance business. Her experience as a real estate broker has allowed her to truly understand the Real Estate Brokers needs to recruit, motivate and empower real estate professionals in the industry. Educating real estate professionals has become her passion. She is involved with many different organizations and enjoys giving of her time to empower the real estate professionals through multiple industry related seminars. Through the dedicated efforts of her team, they are successfully facilitating the short sale negotiations and closing on the the transactions.
