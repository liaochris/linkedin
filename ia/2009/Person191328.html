Devon Crowel’s Summary

Devon began his career working with salespeople at the age of 20 while working with his father in their family-owned real estate and financial planning business. Within four years he was managing a large, corporate real estate firm in Southern California. There, he was introduced to his first life coach who influenced him enough to begin studying the works of some of the world’s greatest motivators and trainers, business leaders and spiritual/personal development teachers.

For over 25 years he has personally worked with, counseled and motivated hundreds to achieve the results in life that they had only dreamed of before. This ability to bring profound change can only come through really caring about people, together with a whole lot of practical experience and education.

Devon has served as an Elected Official, held numerous chairmanships in Government, Civic and Business organizations and as a past Director on the Georgia State Chamber of Commerce.
