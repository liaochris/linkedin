Dan Falkenberg’s Summary

I obtained a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Exercise Science from Asbury College. I'm an ACE and NASM-PES certified personal trainer, an 800 meter All-American in track and field, and a past collegiate athlete in cross country and soccer. Before starting Your Live Trainers (YourLiveTrainers.com), a personal training company based out of Columbus, Ohio, I accumulated personal training experience with Gold's Gym, Lifestyle Family Fitness, and Nationwide Insurance.

I'm the feature fitness writer for ColumbusSports.com, I have my own fitness blog (DanFalkenberg.com), and I've been a feature fitness professional in the Columbus Dispatch, the Cincinnati Enquirer, Central Ohio Health magazine, at fitness conferences, and on 10TV (Columbus' CBS affiliate). My wife, Amber, and I currently live in Columbus, Ohio.
