Deo Landicho’s Summary

DOMINGO EMMANUEL O. LANDICHO


Deo is a firm believer of the new media and is very visible in the social networking sites like Facebook, Friendster and Multiply. He has been in the internet since 1998, building websites and now harnessing the popularity of this new media. He also is a great believer in everything digital. Integration of mobile, online and other digital touch points is his preference when it comes to implementing and recommending marketing campaigns. He values creativity and thinks that online advertising is the perfect new playground for creative marketers. Working as Sales Director for the official Sales House of Microsoft Online, he believes that the online experience can help build a stronger brand image for the modern global Filipino.

Prior to Microsoft Online, Mr. Landicho has a total accumulated 14+ years of advertising experience and has worked with established multinational ad agencies like J Walter Thompson Manila, Doyle Dane and Bernbach (DDB) Philippines as well as big local agencies Adformatix and MGM advertising. Deo started as a creative copywriter and worked his way up as Creative Director before landing the Managing Director position at Great Wall. His role in Microsoft online opens up wondrous possibilities in helping clients in brand-building. Mr. Landicho received his BA from the University of the Philippines in Diliman.

Deo Landicho’s Specialties:

Extensive network relations in the online (and off-line) advertising industry, mind-blowing ideas, cost-effective solutions and undeniable perseverance to succeed. Very well versed in the technical aspect of online. Passionate champion of this new media.
