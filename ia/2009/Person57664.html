David Harlow’s Summary

I am a health care lawyer and consultant with twenty years' public and private sector experience, which has given me a unique perspective on legal, policy and business issues facing the health care community. Health care providers, vendors and payors of all shapes and sizes rely on me to help them navigate the maze of regulatory and business issues facing them on a daily basis.

In addition to my practice at The Harlow Group, I am engaged as a subject matter expert in the health care consulting practice at Navigant Consulting, Inc.

I am also available as a neutral in mediation and arbitration of disputes involving health care providers, vendors and payors.

Please check out my blog, HealthBlawg, at http://healthblawg.typepad.com, for the latest on health care law and policy plus my observations and analysis.

David Harlow’s Specialties:

Stark, Anti-Kickback, Diagnostic Imaging, Long-Term Care, Physician-Hospital Relationships, "Virtual" Joint Ventures, Emergency Medical Services, HIPAA, EHRs and PHRs, Health 2.0. Managed Care, ADR, Mediation, Arbitration
