Douglas Clayton’s Summary

Investment manager with over 23 years experience in emerging Asia. Currently investing in private equity, venture capital, and real estate investments through Leopard Cambodia Fund, Cambodia's first multi-sector investment fund. Preparing to launch Leopard Sri Lanka Fund in early 2010.

Douglas Clayton’s Specialties:

Deal sourcing, business development, working with entrepreneurs, general management
