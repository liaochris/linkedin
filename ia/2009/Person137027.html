Larry Koestler’s Summary

Veteran communications professional with social media brand development expertise and corporate experience. Proactive, motivated, organized, self-disciplined, flexible and creative thinker with outstanding verbal and written communications skills.

I am very interested in joining an organization as the Director of Social Media, with responsibility for brand building, name recognition and the viral dissemination of new company activity.

As an early adopter of and daily contributor to various social media outlets—including blogging (via Wordpress, Movable Type, Tumblr), micro-blogging and social networking—I have an intrinsic understanding of the way unique marketing strategies in that space can significantly enhance an entity’s global profile through the viral dissemination of new company activity, engender brand loyalty and connect users across borders and boundaries.

I'm on Twitter all day and post through a multitude of platforms—including Tweetdeck, TwitterBerry and bit.ly—while generating multiple new followers every day; I bolster my brand through cross-posting via Facebook and Linked In; and develop and converse with multiple new contacts daily via the aforementioned social networking sites.

I'm also an experienced blogger currently posting multiple times a day to Yankeeist.com, a blog about the New York Yankees. I quintupled traffic within a week of the site's hard launch through strategic and targeted linking from websites with similar content and tens of thousands of daily visitors.

I'm also the founder of This Is What We Do Now, a New York-centric humor blog that I launched in 2004, right before the personal blogging explosion. That site saw 5,000 unique visitors per day at its peak and was linked to by media heavyweights such as Gawker, New York Magazine, Gothamist and Curbed on a weekly basis.
