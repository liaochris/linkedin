Beth Campbell’s Summary

As a progressive and innovative news/editorial director, I thrive within collaborative, entrepreneurial environments and offer proven abilities in solving problems, handling crises and motivating large teams to meet deadlines. My background encompasses 12 years in both print and web-based financial news, data and analytics, including 11 years with Reuters and 3 years as a consultant, editorial strategist and freelance content developer for diverse clients. Notable highlights over the course of my career include:

• Consistently regarded as one of Wall Street’s top credit and M&A reporters for 10+ years.
• Became Reuters’ youngest-ever VP and SVP based on talent for creating cutting-edge editorial products.
• Chosen to support the integration of a 110-person, $21M, 20-year-old startup company into publicly held Reuters.
• Directed 40 employees in four news bureaus: New York, London, Hong Kong and Singapore.
• Managed and ensured the news quality of 12 global publications, ranging from dailies to a 144-page glossy annual.
• Recognized as an “intrapreneur” for ability to identify opportunities to improve company value and differentiation.
• Moderated a “must attend,” $2 trillion commercial lending industry conference for CFOs, treasurers, bankers and traders.
• Trained writers and editors to enhance their skills, minimize turnover, enhance product quality and reduce costs.
• Provided strong team leadership to expand real-time story volume from 20/day to 120/day.
• Editorial direct reports quoted in Financial Times, New York Times and Wall Street Journal on numerous occasions.
