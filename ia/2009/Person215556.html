Sharon Stasney’s Summary

I currently run Self Encounter, LLC. Self Encounter explores how life stories shape lives. Through a blog, writing workshops, and classes on the construction of personal myths, I invite students and readers to author life stories that make for a meaningful, enriching life. My next available course is entitled "Your Story Makes It Real," through the University of Utah's LifeLong Learning program. The course explores recent discoveries in brain research and how much perception and emotion play a role in constructing our life stories. You can enroll through the University of Utah's Livelong Learning continuing education program.

My research interests and academic studies include narrative psychology, neuropsychology, evolutionary theory, dynamic systems theory, rhetoric, literary theory, and marketing. I am the author of seven books and write the Self Encounter blog at http://sharonstasney.blogspot.com.
