Michael McClenaghan’s Experience

  • Agile Edmonton

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 2008 — Present (6 months)

    Agile Edmonton is a User Group run by volunteers with the goal of helping Edmonton become more Agile. During the summer of 2008, the group was down to a few posts per month on a discussion board and had no regular meetings. I started hosting small informal meetings and then, based on some connections that I was able to make at the Agile 2008 conference, started hosting regular meetings. Our first meeting in September 2008 had 45 people and the group has averaged between 45 and 50 attendees at the monthly meetings since that time.

    I have recruited 3 other committed volunteers that now make up the Agile Edmonton executive. Together, we have built a strong group and extended our reach by building a group website along with creating LinkedIn and Facebook groups. Our membership has grown each month as we continue to expand beyond the group's core network.

    Join Agile Edmonton today at http://www.agileedmonton.org.
