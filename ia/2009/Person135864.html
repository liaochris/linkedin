Sparky Powell (LION)’s Summary

Sparky Powell is the founder and President of K.W. Powell & Associates, a B-to-B Marketing Communications firm, and is the Creator of the R:center Dealer Marketing Software and KW360 Web Software. He has 20 years of experience in the analysis, design and implementation of Corporate and Dealer/ Distributor / Agent Sales and Marketing Solutions.

Quickly into his career, Sparky realized the inefficiencies of traditional marketing efforts, and began reshaping the old MarCom model. He began to move clients in the direction of a large number of smaller, highly targeted and personal marketing offers, implemented regionally throughout the entire sales channel. These changes resulted in increased response and conversion rates, incremental profit and increased return-on-investment for marketing expenditures.

The need for ease of deployment, greater accountability and scalability led to Sparky's passion for the use of technology as a sales and marketing weapon. He began to create technology solutions for common customer problems (customized target marketing, lead-time tools, inventory exchange programs, automated lead qualification, lead distribution, lead tracking, etc.). This passion ultimately led to the creation of R:center and KW360.

Sparky is an agent of change, a visionary in sales and marketing technology and is emerging as the expert in sales channel marketing. He clearly demonstrates the ambition, drive and passion of a top-shelf leader.

Sparky is an expert who coaches, trains and speaks to corporations and associations on effective integration of “Sales 2.0 and Web 2.0” marketing strategies, branding and positioning, social media, professional selling skills and lead generation.

spowell@kwpowell.com.
