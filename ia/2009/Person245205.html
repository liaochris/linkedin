Carmen Arnevik’s Summary

Freelance Marketing Consultant offering marketing services to small businesses, corporate clients and agency clients.

Key Services / Skills:

- Strategy and Planning: product development and brand strategy, online/eCommerce strategy, direct marketing strategy, marketing communications strategy and campaign planning
- Program Management: media buying, paid search management, email deployment, social media management, blog writing, direct mail management and press release writing and distribution
- Copywriting & Creative Direction: working with freelance web designers, graphic designers, developers and agencies

Senior marketing professional with over twenty years B2B and B2C experience across major industries including; software, packaged goods, retail / e-Commerce, tourism and advertising:

• Strong skills, proficiency in analytical thinking and problem solving and development of marketing strategies and programs to grow revenue, brand preference and customer loyalty.
• Demonstrated success in market analysis, customer research, competitive reviews, product and brand development, pricing and packaging strategies and launching new products.
• Strong experience in e-Commerce and online marketing including search (SEM / SEO), email, banners, online events, social media, web and landing page optimization and tracking. Experience using the following software: Google Analytics, WebTrends, FireClick, Google Adwords, MSN AdCenter, Yahoo Panama, Hitwise, Response, View Central, Live Meeting, basic HTML and FrontPage.
• Solid understanding of direct marketing and catalog marketing including customer segmentation, list management, offer development, creative development and production, mailing and tracking.
• Creative thinker with excellent understanding of the design and production processes and proven success working with in-house design teams and managing outside agencies / resources.
