Edward Neary’s Summary

Specialize in Wedding and event videography in New York, New Jersey, Connecticut as well as destination weddings. Also, have experience filming day camps, live concerts, musical performances, corporate interviews or anything requiring emotion.

This is life, not staged, contrived or created.......it just happens.  It's what motivates me to create films that tell a story that will last a lifetime and cherished for generations.  I believe that video is one of the most effective storytelling mediums ever created and I Love being a part of it all.
