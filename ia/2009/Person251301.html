"Z" Zazhinne’s Summary

I’m a “miracle” who BEAT “permanent” Brain Injury resulting in 5.5 years of Blindness, & Disability--by practicing what I’d spent a lifetime learning and teaching: an effective Body-Mind-Spirit Wellness Lifestyle combined with cutting-edge Science and Alternative Health treatments.
Wrote the book on HOW.
Now seeking corp sponsors for a program to help Brain-Injured vet's come back--like I did.

Trained from early childhood in excellent SELF-Care for Peak Performance by a medical professor father in how the body works and world-class ballet dancers in how to care for and work the body.

Lifelong creative and performance artist, involved in the arts and public service since childhood. My 30 years+ experience utilizing Dance, Music, Art, Body-Mind-Spirit work, and Humanistic and Transformational Psychologies include work in residential treatment centers, workshop settings small and large, and my own private practice.

Training: Ira Progoff in Intensive Journaling technique, Psychodrama at Institute for Psychodrama and Sociometry and Dr Ferruccio DiCorri, Guided Imagery in Music with Helen Bonny, Creative Movement therapies with Poldi Orlando, Humanistic and Transformational Psychologies through Association for Humanistic Psychology, and related organizations. Taught at Reiki Center Los Angeles, Ca. Certified as Hypnotist, Master Hypnotist, and Clinical Hypnotherapist (Hypnosis Motivation Institute, Los Angeles, Ca.)

More than 25 years experience in private Peak Performance & Stress management practice (clients range from artists, athletes, other creative peak performers to the corporate & law enforcement fields,) & my own hard-won success over Brain Injury, Blindness and Disability, I now teach Wellness online at zeeva.net.
