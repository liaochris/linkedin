Srikant Sastri’s Summary

Srikant Sastri has successfully founded and built market leadership for two companies in India. In addition, he is now focused on drawing attention to development issues in Indian society through an intitiative called "GROWTH-FOR-ALL".
The two companies founded by Srikant are :SOLUTIONS-DIGITAS( South Asia's #1 marketing services company, and now part of the global Publicis Groupe) & Team4U( India's leading contract salesforce staffing company with over 20,000 associates, and now a Randstad company)
.
Srikant's primary focus is on providing strategic leadership & organisational vision at Solutions-Digitas, as Managing Director. Outside of work, a lot of his energies are devoted to creating a movement called "GROWTH-FOR-ALL" that brings together individuals, corporations & NGOs in focusing on development issues in Indian society. Srikant also plays an active role in the CSR initiatives of CII, besides being a Charter Member of TiE.

Srikant has had a long and distinguished career. Prior to founding SOLUTIONS, he was CEO and Founder, RESULT-McCann, where he created the concept of marketing services, and became its earliest practitioner in India. He also worked at Pond’s(Unilever) where the strong marketing foundations were laid. While at Pond’s, he was credited with several innovations in sales & distribution management.

Srikant is an engineer from IIT, Kanpur and an MBA from IIM, Calcutta.

While his interests include current affairs, contemporary history, and economic development, he is really passionate about his belief that India is destined to be a global power in this century. In the short-term, this belief is being reflected in two firm commitments: first, to build SOLUTIONS as a strong industry leader across Asia-Pacific; and the next, to ensure that his personal skills & network are deployed on social projects that facilitate inclusive economic development.

Srikant Sastri’s Specialties:

a) Help global corporations do business in India & help Indian MNCs do business overseas. Through comprehensive marketing & sales-related support services.
b) Help individuals & organisations collaborate on projects focused on development issues pertaining to India.
