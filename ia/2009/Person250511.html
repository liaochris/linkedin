Yu-kai Chou’s Summary

Yu-kai is an Entrepreneur, Social Media Advisor, and aspiring Power Coach. He is the Founder/CEO of Viralogy.com and FD Career, as well as various other web properties. Yu-kai has been running successful social media campaigns for companies to reach their target market in a lean and efficient manner. He is also a speaker on Social Media and Real Life Gaming at various entities including Google, Venture Capital Groups, Entrepreneur Organizations and Universities.

Yu-kai is the 1st place winner at the CINACON 2009 VC Pitch Competition and was selected as one of the 100 young leaders of California to attend the Governor's Global Climate Summit. He is also a board advisor for a number of organizations and startup companies throughout California. Yu-kai graduated with a degree in International Economics from UCLA, specializing in China's Green Economy.

Yu-kai Chou’s Experience

  • Founder, CEO

    Future Delivery (Viralogy.com)

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    Viralogy is a social media rank that incorporates many social media metrics such as Blogs, Votes, Twitter, Facebook/LinkedIn, Youtube, Pagerank and more to map out how influential a person is on the internet.

    With data gathered from the internet and from blogs that have integrated our Viralogy Script, we aggregate all the social media metrics into an algorithm that determines the VScore – similar to a Google Pagerank for a person’s online influence.

    Viralogy allows marketers to

    1. Find the top thousand influencers in their niche

    2. Do analytics research on these influencers for high ROI

    3. Get in touch with the influencers with a high response rate

    Conceptually, Viralogy is the "Alexa for People."

    1st place winners to the CINACON 2009 VC Pitch Competition and 2nd place at the Showcase Competition
