Gabriel Flores’s Summary

I empower people to make their dreams happen & organisations to fulfil their purpose. I am committed that all people live a life of abundance, freedom & love, and that each person expresses fully their unique contribution to the world.

[Reader Notice: Written mostly to tag searches and keep within character limits]
For those familiar with Wealth Dynamics, my primary wealth profile is Supporter [Dynamo: 8%, Blaze: 52%, Tempo: 32%, Steel: 8%].

Originally from San Antonio, Texas, I graduated from Robert E. Lee High School Magna Cum Laude. I participated in Army Junior Reserve Officers Training Corps (JROTC), the National Honor Society (NHS), and Choir.

I went to University at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering with High Distinction in 1999, with a minor in Music. I participated in a variety of activities including the Glee Club, Alpha Phi Omega (APO), and Crew (Rowing).

In 1998, I started a non-profit organisation, Flying Eagles International, with which I produced a CD of my original music, "Prelude to a Dream", whose profits went to benefit the Native American tribes of the four corners region of the USA.

In my near decade-long career at Accenture (formerly Andersen Consulting) I worked in a variety of industries including Comms & High-Tech, Pharmaceuticals, Retail, and Financial Services in both USA and Europe. My experience there helped me build my deep business, leadership, and management skills.

I now lead a group of three companies that work with individuals to fulfil their dreams and companies to fulfil their purpose and produce extreme results with the fun and spirit of adventure.

Outside my job, I write music and offer training in Reiki (I am a Reiki level III). I have also led seminars at Landmark Education. Some of the projects have been acknowledged by international leaders, such as Desmond Tutu, and have been seen on television and newspapers globally.
