Ryan Wynia’s Summary

Author of www.BeYOB.com, [Be] Your Own Brand, where my guests and I discuss the art of creating brand alignment and the science of delivering its promise.

Strategic brand architect. Culture connoisseur. Anthropologist.

Currently the Virtuoso Of Visioneering at a brand consultancy called CHANGEffect located just outside Chicago. I’m a Certified Human Behavior Consultant, a designation that affords me insight into the way an organization’s personalities, strengths and character qualities shape a brand’s ethos and culture. A degree in communication arts with a unique emphasis in communication rhetoric serves as a foundation for my passion to create meaningful brands—helping corporations and social organizations build long-term strategic branding objectives and implement short-term marketing initiatives.
