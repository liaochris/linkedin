    My background is in nursing. I received my Diploma of Nursing at St. Luke’s School of Nursing in San Francisco, California in 1979. After working a couple of years I took time off to travel Europe, came back & again while working got my Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Sonoma State University in Rhonert Park, California in 1976. I then received my Master of Science in Nursing from the University of California, San Francisco, California in 1986.

    During my profession as a nurse I’ve published research articles, and was part of a team of colleagues who formed the Sigma Theta Tau International, Xi Eta, Honor Society of Nursing at the University of British Columbia, Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.

    I have extensive background in Therapeutic Touch and have studied under Delores Kreuger and Dora Kunes.

    I started my own business Health & Wellness Enterprises due to downsizing in 1994. I have done both Therapeutic Touch and Nikken since then. My major focus is Nikken so I can make the biggest difference in people’s lives both for their health and their financial health, which couldn’t be more timely than now!
