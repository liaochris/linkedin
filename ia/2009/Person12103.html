Amy Franko’s Summary

I'm the owner and principal learning designer of Amy Franko Consulting. Much of my experience over the past 12 years has been in sales, marketing, and project management.

My love of learning and professional development is what propelled me to jump into the world of self-employment and launch my own training and development company.

As a learning designer and facilitator, I understand that training programs must energize the learner, provide knowledge and tools that can be used immediately, and when necessary, provide a reliable revenue stream.

In addition, I'm a certified Book Yourself Solid ™ coach. The group I'm most passionate about working with is women who are solo service professionals. I help them bring more ideal clients into their business, even if marketing and selling isn't something they like to do. I follow a simple system of protocols specifically designed for the solo service professional.

My training design background complements my coaching practice, especially for those service professionals who offer workshops, teleseminars, e-courses, and information products to their clients.

I have a Bachelor's degree in English from the University of Dayton. I'm a member of American Society for Training and Development and currently oversee the Programs and Professional Development offerings for the Central Ohio chapter.

Amy Franko’s Specialties:

Specialties: designing facilitator/student guides, live workshops. Areas of interest: soft skills courses; courses for women in business and women business owners; sales courses
