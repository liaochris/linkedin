Ajitha Menon’s Summary

I have worked as a journalist for almost 20 years now. The experiences have been exhilarating, tedious, tragic, comic, informative, educative, trying, tiring, inspiring, depressing, but I wouldn't have missed any of it for the world.

I am currently focusing on developmental issues, particularly those related to women, and more specifically women in conflict zones. I have also developed an interest in Media Rights, Gender Sensitive Reporting and Media Ethics. My articles can be read on www.wfs.org and www.thehoot.org. Googling Ajitha Menon, WFS/ Ajitha Menon, ANI will also give access to my various articles on different issues written over a period of time.

Ajitha Menon’s Specialties:

Specializing in women developmental issues, research, survey and print and audio-video documentation.

Ajitha Menon’s Experience

  • Journalist (Chief of Bureau), Kolkata

    Asian News International

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    August 1993 — Present (16 years 4 months)

    I am in charge of all news coverage for ANI - be it broadcast, print or internet, from West Bengal. I also make forages into the neighbouring states like Orissa, the seven North-Eastern states and Sikkim and neighbouring countries like Bangladesh and Bhutan for news coverage.

  • Sub-Editor

    Press Trust of India

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    1991 — 1993 (2 years )

    It was a great learning experience at the Press Trust of India. Besides my duties as sub-editor, I also wrote several feature articles during my tenure with the company. While my stints as probationary journalist with Business Standard and Economic Times earlier had given me an insight into business reporting, Press Trust of India gave me an all round opportunity. From politics to business, sports to culture, entertainment to incidents, accidents or natural calamities, I got to edit copies from the entire north-eastern region on all topics during my tenure with PTI.
