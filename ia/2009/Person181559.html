Pat Obuchowski’s Summary

I am currently the CEO (Chief Empowerment Officer) of inVisionaria, a leadership and business coaching organization. I help my clients become even more successful by sharpening their leadership, interpersonal, and organizational skills which help them achieve their vision on a professional and personal level.
I coach for results.
Most recently, I served as a Lead Leadership Coach in Corporate Services with Pacific Gas & Electric Company. In this capacity, I coached corporate leaders to be more impactful in their work and in the world and helped them play their Bigger Games.
My special talent includes accelerating the learning and integration of organizational leaders into new roles brought about by transitions, such as promotion, transfer, growth, mergers or acquisitions. I help leaders build strong and results oriented teams by mastering their strengths, managing their weaknesses, and becoming skillful in coaching others.
What differentiates me from others in my field is my operational experience in working at all levels and in all aspects of business. This provides me with a 360 degree understanding of how businesses operate and what’s needed to ‘fill in’ the gaps. I help people create plans that inspire them and then coach them in the implementation of those plans.
And then we party!
