Ashley Keegan’s Summary

An experienced Senior Recruitment Consultant, specialising in the Finance & Accountancy market, across all commercial industry sectors (SME to FTSE 100 blue chips) and public/not for profit/charity organisations.

Ashley has a proven track record of advising clients on best practice for recruitment of specific positions as well as general strategy. Successfully completed recruitment drives for positions such as Financial Accountants, Management Accountants, Business/Financial Analysts, Internal Auditors, Project Accountants (Engineering & Construction), Finance Managers/Financial Controllers, Commercial/FP&A Directors and Finance Directors.

Solid background in business development and strong business partnering with both clients and candidates alike. A trustworthy and professional recruiter with high moral and ethical working standards. Good technical understanding and awareness, with great inventiveness and tenacity for difficult-to-fill positions.

Ashley Keegan’s Specialties:

Specialist consultant in mid-to-senior level Finance & Accountancy recruitment in Sussex, Surrey, Kent, Hampshire & London.
Experienced in competency based interviewing, salary negotiation, career advice, contingency searches, executive head hunt campaigns, advertised selections, and business development.
