Gino Federici’s Summary

Independent Publisher, Author/Speaker/Singer-Songwriter/Recording Artist and Executive Producer/Chef at: http://www.TheItalianSingingGourmet.com.

Graduate of the Bill Gove International Association of Corporate Speakers. (IACS).

Professionally involved in the performing arts since 1971 on an international scale. Spent 23 years performing in Las Vegas.


At the younger age of 67 I decided it was time for a change and left Las Vegas for Michigan for a two year sabbatical, now in its 27th month.

I was looking for a new direction in life that would excite me and energize me for the next 50 years.

After losing two wives to cancer in the span of 5 years - the second wife in 2002 - my soul seemed to be yearning for renewal.

Believing that renewal was possible and I would be shown my new "meaning." I left Las Vegas in July of 2007 and moved to the Michigan countryside with a new lady who became my wife and guru.

The daily tasks of studying and learning has led me to a journey of discovery. The "knowing" I was seeking identified itself.

I have now, in October of 2009, resurfaced with a new direction and a message of possibility I hope will help others along my path. I want to share that message with the younger audiences in colleges around the world where "The Power of Purpose" needs to be fostered.

I have writtent a book "Get Over Your Anxiety. Paint Your Life!" and, together with my new bride, co-authored a series of booklets we call "The ABC's of Life™"

These are pocket size booklets where each letter of the Alphabet presents a value or a principle for a life of leadership and fulfillment. They are truly: "Wisdom In Your Pocket™"

Wisdom In Your Pocket Press was formed to handle the marketing. We seek representatives worldwide on a 50/50 partnership basis with no upfront capital.

Life is as exciting journey!
