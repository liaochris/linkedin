John Ostrem’s Summary

Dr. Ostrem has over 25 years experience developing new technology, bringing products to market, and founding new companies. He founded China MobileSoft in 2001, a company that developed Linux-based telecommunications software for the Chinese and International markets, and at various times served as Chairman, CEO, and CTO. China MobileSoft was acquired by PalmSource in early 2005 and subsequently Dr. Ostrem served as Lead Scientist at PalmSource. Currently he is CTO of Nexage, a company that provides mobile Ad optimization and brokering services, and a member of the Board of Directors of Genyous Biomed (www.genyous.com). Previously he has served on the Board of Directors of the Linux Phone Standards Forum, and as Chairman of the Steering Committee of OSDL’s Mobile Linux Initiative. Dr. Ostrem began his career as a scientist at SRI International (the former Stanford Research Institute), and developed the initial technology behind CIC, one of the first technology companies to be spun off from SRI. He has a Ph.D. from the University of California.
