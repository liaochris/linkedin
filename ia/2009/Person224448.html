Steven J. Wagner’s Summary

Marketers face unique challenges: provide high quality leads, grow brand awareness, manage multiple channel campaigns, invest in new technologies, and produce greater return on investment (ROI).

We work with Marketing Executives to quantify and improve their return on marketing investments.

Successful marketers are mastering seven competencies. We define these as the “kudzu factor.” The kudzu plant grows by ascending everything in its environment.

Create an executable strategy- with succinct steps and precise goals that builds long term value.

Focus on ROI –develop a system to measure all campaigns using ROI and constantly refine the metrics.

Synchronize marketing campaigns – by simultaneously planning offline and online campaigns together for broader reach and improved ROI with the same budget.

Enhance marketing with automation –to manage the entire marketing mix, provide real time analytics, integrate with sales and provide accurate measurement of ROI.

Control the “top of the sales funnel” –with lead scoring and other multi-step conversion tactics to provide sales with high quality leads.

Make your website the hub –by directing traffic, controlling communications and building accurate analytics to win the battle for mindshare and greater brand awareness.

Implement with intent –to achieve strategic goals and return on investment targets.

We work with your team, agencies and dealers to put these practices and processes in place to grow brand awareness and leads.

We provide the experience and guidance to successfully develop and execute original methods that achieve your business goals.
