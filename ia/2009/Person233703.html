Tim Justice’s Summary

InSite Dynamics develops process-oriented web-based applications that are designed to integrate seamlessly with your website's layout — adding simple content management functionality to key website sections that require frequent updates and administration.

Based in Portland, Oregon, InSite Dynamics’ modular content management tools provide a common admin interface accessed through any web browser — streamlining a normally cumbersome process of adding, editing, and managing website content... be it product cataloging and promotion, store or dealer locations, project profiling and photo galleries, press release, events and news coverage, job listings and resume collection, event registration, and more.

"Empower Your Web Site. Empower Your Business."

On a personal level, I've worked my way into progressive management-level positions with experience in marketing communications, electronic marketing, product management, and public relations. I've worked for Corrigo Inc., Extensis, CAMAX Manufacturing Technologies, Initial Plant Services, Predator Manufacturing Software, and Bioject Medical Technologies.

I hold a BS in Technical Journalism and a Minor in Business Administration from Oregon State University. (And I'm not a bad home brewer either.)
