Scott Shaub’s Summary

My goal is to continue to work in heavy industry, commercial facilities or heavy construction while promoting the well being of the individual and the environment. I have been avidly involved with contractor safety and as an industrial electrician in a heavy manufacturing, 1.3 million square-foot plant aluminum sheet and cast plate facility.
