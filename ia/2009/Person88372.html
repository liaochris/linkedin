Krish (Gopi Krishnan)’s Summary

Successful management professional with proven expertise in defining, implementing & running Program Management, Business Assurance and IT Governance practices for fortune 500’s. Have high track record in spearheading process changes; running high quality delivery centers and managing strategic multi locale / team programs. Motivated achiever with strong People Management Skills, who has constantly exceeded expectations, has been promoted regularly and has garnered numerous performance awards. Professional qualifications include CGEIT (Certified in Governance of Enterprise IT), CISA (Certified Information Security Auditor) & CIPM (Certified International Project Manager).

Currently working as Head of EPMO & Program Director-Replatforming in BMI Bank, Bahrain. Responsible for end to end implementation of any and every significant change including technical; strategic and business initiatives in the bank. Some of the key business initiatives includes New product implementation in Retail, Corporate and Islamic Business units, International & retail expansions, Enterprise BCP, Enterprise Records management, EMV enabled Debit & Credit cards, Integration of Diners business into BMI Bank, Assets management, Central process repository, Business Assurance Test practice management etc

As Program Director of IT Replatforming, responsible for replacing systems, processes related to current legacy platform with a world class IT hub and spoke platform. Scope includes applications including not limited to – T24 Corebanking, Oracle Siebel, Oracle EBS (HRMS, Payroll, SCM), Hyperion, Fusion, Data warehousing, UCM, Nucleus LOS & Collection, Internet Banking, 4tress authentication, NOC etc

Key result areas include Strategic alignment; Enterprise Program & Portfolio management; Process Governance; Implementation & Delivery of IT replaforming; Capacity baselining and Performance measurements; Business Assurance and People Management.
