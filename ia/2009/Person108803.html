Jeff Tan’s Summary

Jeff Tan has 25 years of experience in teaching, development and management in the IT industry. He has also developed software applications for the cleaning and air-conditioning industries. Today, he brings his IT industry knowledge and contacts into executive search.

Jeff Tan’s Specialties:

Executive search, headhunting, placement, contracting and computer application development.

  • Director

    Bowne Global Solutions (Singapore) Pte Ltd [now known as LionBridge Singapore Pte Ltd]

    (Translation and Localization industry)

    December 1998 — March 2005 (6 years 4 months)

    Jeff managed the Singapore office of Bowne Global Solutions Pte Ltd - formerly the world's largest translation and localisation company. Its clients included many Multi-National Companies that had global presence and extensive language requirements for their products.

    Jeff was introduced to the translation and localization domain when he joined ASLC Pte Ltd as Principal Software Consultant. The company subsequently became part of Bowne Global Solutions and he was promoted to to Director. His achievements include turning the business around within 24 months.

  • Multimedia Designer

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    1996 — 1998 (2 years)

    Jeff's accomplishments include developing the courseware to train technicians to respond to a host of technical problems in the 24-hour wafer-fabrication environment, much to the satisfaction of Company.

    From Singapore Polytechnic, Jeff joined a new startup, ASLC Pte Ltd, at the height of the dot-com era.
