Michael Fiorelli’s Summary

DIRECT AND DATABASE MARKETING RECRUITMENT
At Mangieri Solutions, we have been in business since 1994, and we are headquartered in Sandy Hook, CT. We work on a national basis, and can handle many levels within a DM Organization.
Our specialization with the Direct Marketing industry allows us the opportunity to not only understand your hiring need, but also the ability to attract the personnel that can help solve your business problem. A job description will provide a snapshot of skills that a client would like to see from a candidate, but that does not necessarily mean that someone with that skill set would be a match for you. We dig deeper, we probe to gather information, and we provide the detail that will allow you to make a quality hire. We will help to find out what you are looking to try and accomplish, and what this role will require immediately, as well as in the future. We not only will find the candidate that is a match on paper, but one that has the demonstrated ability to handle your unique business situation. Every search draws on the experience built over ten years of satisfying the needs of Direct Marketing businesses. We believe our single-minded focus gives us, and you, a competitive advantage in quickly finding candidates that are right for your job needs.
We do not advertise job openings. Our searches are solely conducted on a referral and recruitment basis. Using the most modern search tools available, including databases, computer and electronic communications, along with the old-fashioned tools of relationships built and nurtured over the years, we find candidates that are good at what they do, and stand out amongst their peers.
After finding you the best possible candidates, we continue to work with you. We’ll help you better understand the motivations of the candidates and how best to attract them to your organization. And we’ll help them understand the benefits of your job opportunity and your company.
