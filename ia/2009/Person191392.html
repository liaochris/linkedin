  • Contractor

    Intuit

    (Public Company; INTU; Computer Software industry)

    February 2004 — Present (5 years 6 months)

    I began working with Intuit in 2004 (contractor) as the author and lead instructor for Intuit's first Annual Update Tour for accounting professionals. I continue to write and/or present on this tour each year. I also provide instruction for Intuit as a contractor in a wide range of venues, including delivering keynotes at accounting technology shows, co-presenting with Intuit executives, representing Intuit at various CPA society shows, and others. I have worked (and continue to work) with Intuit on a wide range of pilot products (mostly around training for accounting professionals). Over the past 3 years I have developed curriculum and provided instruction for Intuit’s annual Enterprise Solutions User Conferences – working with the mid-market group. Right now I’m working on several products with Intuit. I can disclose that one contract is a writing project on “Environmentally Friendly QuickBooks Use,” though I’m not sure that will be the final title.

  • Owner

    Creative Financial Software

    (Accounting industry)

    December 2003 — Present (5 years 8 months)

    I founded Creative Financial Software to provide QuickBooks consulting services to Atlanta area businesses. After only 2 months, I secured my first national training and writing contract with Intuit, allowing me to grow my company on two fronts - national training to other Intuit Advisors and consulting on QuickBooks in my local market. With the national audience of other advisors my practice quickly expanded nationally - with clients spanning coast to coast. In due time we became a multi-professional firm - and I hope to expand the practice to include at least 2 new professionals over the next 12 months. This year, I will host an advanced QuickBooks conference for Intuit Solution Providers and QuickBooks ProAdvisors called "Scaling New Heights."

  • Vice President of Instruction

    The Sleeter Group

    (Privately Held; E-Learning industry)

    September 2000 — November 2003 (3 years 3 months)

    I served as the Vice President of Instruction. In this capacity I spoke at most Sleeter Group seminars and conferences in the East and Midwest states as well as some events in the mountain and pacific states. Joe expanded/updated numerous Sleeter Group curriculums that are still in use today, including The Sleeter Group's Consultants Reference Guide and The Sleeter Group's Complete QuickBooks Textbook. In 2002 Joe designed a curriculum on building and managing a QuickBooks consulting practice for The Sleeter Group that Intuit endorsed for a seminar series that same year. While at The Sleeter Group, Joe worked with Doug Sleeter to pilot The Sleeter Group's Certified Consultant's Network - a networking organization that is still thriving today.
