Marina Paganucci’s Summary

Marina Paganucci is a Senior Recruitment Consultant and HR Professional with over 18 years experience in executive recruitment. She is currently Executive Search Consultant at The Culver Group based in Los Angeles recruiting mid to senior level sales & marketing & supply chain executives for the digital media, entertainment, consumer, retail, healthcare and technology industries. Marina has extensive international recruitment experience, having recruited in the UK, Europe, Asia Pacific, Middle East and the US.

Prior to joining The Culver Group, she has recruited across various disciplines both nationally and internationally, including Consumer, Supply Chain, Consulting, IT/Technology,Sales & Marketing and Advertising, focussing on mid to senior level roles. She has recruited predominantly director level candidates up to CEOs. Marina has managed clients and candidates from broadcasting, studios, advertising,digital media, professional services, brand consultancies, music and entertainment, consumer, manufacturing, healthcare and technology companies.

Marina also has a background in HR management. She ran her own HR consultancy business providing training & development, executive coaching, organisational analysis, talent management and executive search to her clients.

Marina Paganucci’s Specialties:

Placement of mid to senior level candidates in the digital media, film , tv, print, broadcasting, outdoor media, consumer, retail, promotions and advertising industries. Also experienced in placing IT/Technology, Supply Chain and Sales & Marketing candidates. International Recruitment expertise as Marina has recruited mid to senior level candidates across Europe, UK, Asia Pacific, Middle East and the US. She also speaks Cantonese and Bahasa Malaysia fluently.
