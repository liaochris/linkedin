Mark McLaughlin’s Summary

Mark McLaughlin is expert at developing business solutions that start with the consumer. He has more than 20 years experience in media, advertising and digital strategies. Working with dozens of Fortune 500 companies in every major field from politics to packaged goods, Mark consistently delivers clear strategic roadmaps and actionable ideas.

Mark’s career developed at ad agencies where he focused on media strategies while always choosing work environments where consumer research, creative development and media strategy were under one roof. He is recognized as being among the very first senior agency executives to re-think strategy in the context of digital consumer behavior. This led to roles as a founder of one of the first companies to focus on “rich media” and to positions as the top executive running digital agencies. Before starting his own consultancy, Mark spent 4 years at Yahoo! focusing on value creation in the broadband era.

To learn more about Mark's company, visit www.MCSTRAT.com

Mark McLaughlin’s Specialties:

Marketing solutions, consumer behavior, advertising, branding, media strategy, media negotiations, mobile, Internet, digital media, media research
