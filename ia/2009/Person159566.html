Melissa Zabel’s Summary

My goal as a usability and communications professional is to effectively communicate across media types to deliver targeted messages to targeted audiences.

Whether the purpose of the communication piece is to train, sell, or inform--it is my job to ensure that the end-user receives the intended message quickly and easily. This is accomplished through careful project scoping and analysis of requirements, clearly understanding the audience, detailed information architecture, iterative testing, and refining.

Melissa Zabel’s Specialties:

Project scoping and management, user-centered design, information architecture usability assessment and testing, writing and editing for the end-user, marketing and media campaigns
