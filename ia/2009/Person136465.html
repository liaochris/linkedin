Lamar Wilson’s Summary

I am a very motivated individual who excels at sales and business development. Currently I am in business development for a telecommunications optimization & auditing firm. My experience has always been in sales and business development in one facet or another, but the last 8 years I've been playing in the telecommunications arena. I am very involved in the community, and feel that honesty and integrity are the foundation for the relationships I've built, as well as the trust & respect I earn from my clients. My past history involved show business for half of my life. That part of my life taught me to be self reliant, motivated, and how to think outside of the box in order to be successful. That's probably why I do well at business development now - all of the years having to schmooze casting directors, producers, directors, writers, and agents - now THAT's a tough sell!
