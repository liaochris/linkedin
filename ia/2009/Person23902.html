Barak Paz Tal’s Summary

Barak has over 11 years experience in the Internet and Mobile industries in Mainland China and Israel. He Co-founded Meijob.com, the leading job search engine in China and jipingmi.com, the leading property search engine in China, and currently holds the position of Co-CEO in Meijob.com, and servers in the board of directors of both companies. After less than a year of operation,Meijob.com is ranked the 1st of the vertical job search engines in China. Barak's main expertise is General Management, Marketing, Investor relations, and Online Product General Management.
Prior to Meijob, Barak was Customers marketing Manager in Orange Israel (PTNR). Barak lead the company to reduce its churn rate from 16% to 7% and lead the company to increase its mobile internet revenues from 2M$ to 23M$ within 2 years. Barak holds an MBA in Marketing and Finance and BA in Economics and Management from Tel Aviv University
