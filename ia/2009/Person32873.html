Brett Houchin’s Summary

I spent the first portion of my career at Zappos using my personality and skills to supervise the image processing department in Nevada, in which thousands of images were added to the website every day. Along the way, I helped to build the innovations in the photography department that have become the standard for E-Commerce product presentation.
As the supervisor of the Blogs/Video Team, I took on a project that was a completely new idea: Building an Outward Facing culture piece for the outside world to understand what it's like to work at such a unique company, and to do it 2-3 times a day, every day of the week. Over the course of 1.5 years, our Blogs,Facebook and Twitter presence grew into something that I am quite proud of, and has been widely discussed in the media.

I have acted, written and produced hundreds of comedic pieces for Zappos, Zappos TV as well as hosted Zappos Events in New York's Grand Central Station, Kentucky and Las Vegas.
