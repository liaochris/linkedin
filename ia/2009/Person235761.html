Thomas Crawford’s Summary

Tom Crawford is a trusted executive search and consulting partner to growth stage technology companies and venture capital. For the past 20+ years, Tom has been involved in launching and growing successful venture backed and privately funded technology companies in Silicon Valley. His efforts have been instrumental in bringing multiple technology innovations to market, including industry’s first web-based and mobile email products as well as ground-breaking anti-spam and anti-phishing SaaS solutions. Tom’s operational career includes multiple successful exits and funding events, including three acquisitions in addition to XOOM.com, which sold 4 million shares on the first day of it's IPO, filed a $125 million secondary offering and was ultimately acquired by NBC/General Electric [NASDAQ: NBCI].

Thomas Crawford’s Specialties:

OTG Partners specializes in early-to-mid stage companies with an emphasis on the consumer Internet, media and entertainment, mobile and communications technology, software and SaaS markets.
