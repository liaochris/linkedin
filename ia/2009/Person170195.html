Mary Ann Chick Whiteside’s Summary

Experienced media executive who looks for new ways to use old tools and new tools for the old ways of communication.

Experience in Internet sales and content through newspapers, non-profit organizations, small businesses, and community computer network.

Mary Ann Chick Whiteside’s Specialties:

Experience includes market and audience research, product development, news editing and writing, recruitment, information architecture for search engine optimization and web editing.

Past includes producing radio show, festivals and special events; developing and delivering methods to increase skills of staff and volunteers on computers, managing and succeeding as an artist.

    1992 — Present (17 years)
