Matt Wolfe’s Summary

Currently, I'm working as the Music Director / Morning Show Producer at WBAB-FM on Long Island..somewhere I also get the time to go on-air for weekends, and maintain voice-over work for local and national clients!

Remember THAT kid who ran around with an old school cassette recorder and a microphone at family events, parties and other social functions..and thought he was Dan Rather interviewing The Pope? Yeah, well every family had one I suppose..

Since then, my career has been nothing less than an exciting one, having been lucky enough to work in the entertainment industry with a primary focus on radio..having held positions at ABC NEWSRADIO, CBS RADIO, VH-1, SIRIUS SATELLITE RADIO, and The Word-Famous WLIR-FM and most recently a stint at Sammy Hagar's CABOWABORADIO...

Matt Wolfe’s Specialties:

Skilled in various audio-editing programs including Adobe Audtion and Netia systems. Experience with radio programming and automation software including Selector, ENCO, MusicMaster, Dalet, Prophet, Digital Jukebox and Maestro systems.
