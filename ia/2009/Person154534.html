Mary Kay O'Connor’s Summary

StartingPoint believes that profit comes through customers, that smart companies focus on customers first and build their business from there. The company’s approach sounds deceptively simple -- listen to customer problems and then work with them to implement profitable solutions. Yet understanding customer buying decisions and translating that insight into effective change is complicated. StartingPoint has a unique methodology for identifying & prioritizing customer opportunities. The company’s specialties are Customer Validation—using customer feedback to evaluate the effectiveness of client strategy and Customer Solutions -- simple, practical changes that increase satisfaction, generate new business and improve customer profitability.

StartingPoint works with senior executives who are ready to do what it takes to improve financial results -- newly appointed leaders tasked with a turnaround, companies losing customers, executives struggling to hit financial targets or identify business development barriers.
Mary Kay O’Connor founded StartingPoint in 1994 to improve financial results for clients. She has a unique but powerful set of skills -- B2B sales, sales management, marketing, & change management. Her background includes extensive work in optimizing sales force impact & key account growth.

Her marketing career began with Quaker Oats. From 1985 to 1994 Mary Kay worked for The NutraSweet Company implementing a "branded ingredient strategy" in Canada and European markets. Subsequently Mary Kay managed strategic accounts and was twice named Monsanto’s "Salesperson of the Year". As VP of Food Sales during patent expiration, Mary Kay used customer insight to restructure the business and achieve aggressive financial goals.

Before joining Quaker Oats, Mary Kay received an MBA from the University of Rochester in Marketing & IT. She is a trained negotiator and published author on sales best practices. She lives with her family in the Kansas City area.
