Carla White, CUA’s Summary

I'm a seasoned manager of both people and products as well as a certified usability expert specializing in agile development. My unique combination of experiences contribute to my strong business, technical, communication and creative skills.

I have been managing complex web development projects since 1997 and most recently have a successful line of iPhone apps. During my career, I have expanded business operations internationally, launched my own web production company, and now the Project Manager and User Experience Director for NASA's complex solutions that will hopefully help us fight global warming. During this period, my greatest successes come from focusing on ultimate customer satisfaction - whether that's the consumer or a colleague, they both deserve the same high level of service.

I'm a fierce advocate for Web users everywhere. I love to gets inside the user’s head to ensure site designs really work. I combine psychology and ergonomics to understand what makes a site engage and inform.

In a nutshell, I'm an experienced, multi-lingual, highly creative and innovative business executive with over a decade of international experience in communications and web industry. A few of my specialties include human-computer interaction, business start-ups, new media production, creative design and content, marketing, as well as large project management. Company director and manager, I have a unique combination of international experience, strong business, technical, communication and creative skills.

I’m authorized to live and work in the United States and European Union, and have traveled these regions extensively allowing me to grow a wide personal contact network. I've directed business launches, acquisitions and mergers and have exceptional interpersonal, communication and business skills.
