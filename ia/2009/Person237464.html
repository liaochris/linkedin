Tracie Wells’s Summary

Tracie Wells, President & CEO, Tracie Wells & Company. Creator and principal of TvWebCity.com. Wells is responsible for the overall vision, business development and management, programming content and strategic partnerships. Wells is an award-winning, Emmy winning contributor and Emmy-nominated television journalist who worked in broadcast network for 15 years. Wells is also honored by several independent, civic organizations for her public outreach and legislative activism on behalf of children, families and the developmentally disabled. She is no stranger to starting a TV news program from scratch. Wells served as anchor and on the editorial and management teams of four successful start-up or expansion television newscasts at four stateside network affiliates. In 1997 a national television consulting firm recruited her to serve as main anchor of a multimillion dollar TV news startup in Lansing, Michigan, one of the most successful local FOX news startup operations to date. She has covered major events from Bosnia, Germany, Ground Zero, and Washington, D.C., and most recently served at the FOX affiliate in Indianapolis. Her investigative reports, legislative testimonies and community outreach have prompted changes in state laws in several states. Wells is an associate faculty instructor of public relations at IUPUI, the co-founder and vice-president of a nonprofit children’s and family research and advocacy group. She serves on statewide public policy and legislative study committees regarding children’s and social issues. Wells is a certified fundraiser and a board member of several nonprofit and community organizations.
