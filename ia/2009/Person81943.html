Fredrik Kallioniemi’s Summary

Involved with Oricane since late 2006 as consultant from my own company, became employee and partner at Oricane AB in November 2007.

Founded award winning web consultant company in 2005. Fifteen years at Sweden’s largest telecom company TeliaSonera in different positions within engineering (1990-2005). Highly experienced with large Enterprise projects in operating systems, databases, directory services, storage and communication platforms. I also have core knowledge in web statistics, search engines, strategy and analysis.

I was for 8 years part of technical core teams planning, designing and implementing new technology in TeliaSonera internal infrastructures and platforms.

After 19 years in the industry my area of expertise can be summed with a firm understanding of software and underlying technology and an ability to grasp abstract concepts and models.
