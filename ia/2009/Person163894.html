Mike Hyman’s Experience

  • ePublishing Consultant & Special Projects Director at Headfast / Oxmill / BooksoniX

    Head Software International

    (Publishing industry)

    March 1981 — Present (28 years 3 months)

    I've long specialised in ePublishing - as a developer, system supplier & consultant. From using computers to select & format publications; to directories, databases & full texts on CD-ROM & online; & now eBooks.

    The company has always majored on services for publishers, booksellers, libraries & information services. It has 3 divisions:

    Headfast - packaged & bespoke solutions based on its own structured text database engine. Applications include online & CD-ROM searchings, e-publishing, abstracting & indexing, scientific & technical publishing, libraries etc.

    Oxmill - runs 2 subscription-based information services: CommunityWISE & EarthScienceWISE.

    BooksoniX - web-based booklist management service built around the ONIX standard. Publishers can enter, edit and disseminate bibliographic data to a variety of industry organisations. It also provides capabilities for generating printed & electronic marketing materials plus workflow management for publishers.
