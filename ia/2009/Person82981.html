Gagan Biyani’s Summary

Having grown up in Silicon Valley, my primary experience and interest is in the High Tech industry. Particularly, I am interested in the Internet, Software and Consumer Electronics industries.
In my 3 years as an Economics student at UC Berkeley, I spent much of my time gaining work experience. I was fortunate to work at two Fortune 1000 corporations, Microsoft and Robert Half International. I also worked part-time at a startup, Equilar, Inc. I graduated in August 2008 with a BA in Economics.
Prior to college, I started a successful summer speech and debate camp at my high school, Mission San Jose. I was able to experience starting a small project and growing it into a major success. By the last camp I ran, we had serviced over 300 students and raised more than $42,000 in revenues for the local speech and debate team.
My experiences have led me to realize that I love two things:
Technology and Business Strategy/Development.
Thus, I joined Accenture out of college and have been working with them since. I love solving problems and providing advice to other companies and government agencies.
