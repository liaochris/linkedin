Jeff Werner MBA, JD, PMP, PSP, CCM, CFCC’s Summary

Mr. Werner has thirty years of experience in program management/construction management, project controls and construction claims analysis/dispute resolution. Mr. Werner’s project experience includes water/wastewater, wind energy, nuclear power, fossil fuel, light rail, k-12, airports, industrial processing plants, hospitality, wireless and fiber build-out, highways, bridges and dams. Titles have included; Project Manager, Program Manager, Project Controls Manager, Construction Manager, Assistant Vice President and Director/VP.

Mr. Werner is currently Owner of Werner Consulting, LLC a project management and dispute resolution services company for the construction industry. Mr. Werner represents/assists Owners/Developers, Contractors, Surety Insurance Companies and Law Firms with complex construction delay damage issues.

Most recently Mr. Werner was Commercial Director for AllCapital Wind Energy Management U.S. (Developer), and was responsible for the commercial and risk management during the Development/Construction Phase of the $3B 1500MW Tehachpi Wind Energy Project.

Mr. Werner’s education credentials include a JD from Concord Law School, MBA from the University of Texas, Dallas, and a Masters Certificate in Project Management from UTD. Mr. Werner is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) and a certified Planning and Scheduling Professional (PSP) a Certified Forensic Claims Consultant (CFCC) and a Certified Construction Manager (CCM).
