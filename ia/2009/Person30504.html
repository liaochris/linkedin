Robert Rosell’s Summary

WHO I AM

Quality Assurance Professional improving business operations, mitigating risk, and ensuring compliance. Expertise optimizing Quality and IT incentives, negotiation, and using focused change management techniques. Ensuring the execution of support activities including coaching and counseling, and performance management.

WHAT I DO

Expertise in setting up and continuously improving Quality and IT environments using focused change management techniques. Companies who typically seek my expertise have:
• Newly formed large organizations that need processes and structure to support change caused by complex matrix teams.
• To deal with contingencies with customers or supporting businesses.
• Opportunities to improve customer revenue and/or reduce expenses.
• The need for compliance, expense, and/or efficiency improvements.
• To align leaders to respond to the changing business needs.

HOW I ADD VALUE

Ensuring the execution of support activities including coaching and counseling, compliance, performance management, recruitment, and organizational development and training.
By using my knowledge, analytical and leadership skills I am able to:
• Secure operational readiness by understanding and mitigating risk and developing resilient business practices and related systems.
• Implement processes and provide the structure to produce efficiency gains and reduce expenses.
• Lead business process projects with key components that are completed on time, under budget, and meet organizational objectives.
• Energize and influence the organizational culture and climate by creating a work environment in which employees are motivated and productive.
