Nuno Alves da Silva’s Summary

Nuno graduated from Instituto Superior Técnico in Electronics and Computer Engineering (1992/97, top 1%) and graduated from Sociedade Nacional de Belas Artes with a bachelor of Fine Arts (1998/01).
Nuno won the European Union Contest for Young Scientists (Luxembourg 1994) and the North American Science and Engineering Fair (Canada 1995). He published scientific papers in the World Congress on Computational Intelligence (Alaska 1998) and Artificial Intelligence Conference (Australia 1997).
He worked for Procter & Gamble Portugal and Spain (1996/01) and achieved European records in fast moving consumer goods for Iberia.
Nuno has also performed as Marketing and Communication Manager for OniWay Mobile Telecommunications (2001/02).
As Retail Marketing Director of CTT Correios de Portugal (2003/05), Nuno invented Correio Verde, a convenient size based mail, winner of the World Mail Award 2005 and led the rebranding of Post Offices. Later on he was invited for Pitney & Bowes Leadership Roundtable and Employee of the Year in CTT Correios de Portugal.
As New Business and Client Service Director of Young & Rubicam Brands (2006/08), Nuno contributed to get the Great Efficacy Award 2007 for Young & Rubicam and Compal and gained new business worth 16% of total income.
Nuno is currently Central Marketng Director at Microsoft.
He was identified as one of the Top 25 new generation of leading managers in Portugal by Leader Quest 2008 promoted by the Harvard Clube de Portugal.
He organized trekking expeditions to Mount Everest base camp (Nepal), Torres del Paine and Easter Island (Chile).
Nuno has 3 children.
