Bill Kitchen’s Summary

Real and Virtual World Branding, Content Development, Naming Strategy, Trend Anthropology and Social Networking/Community Building Expert.

Award winning history as marketer and producer of over 3,000 events and concerts including the 52nd and 53rd Presidential Inaugurals, RIAA Diamond Awards, The Presidents' Summit For America's Future and the G8.

Clients have included Lionsgate Entertainment, E*Trade, American Express, L'Oreal, RJ Reynolds, Bacardi-Martini, Heineken, Ocean Drive Eyewear, Hachette-Filipacchi Media, Sephora, volvic, Brooklyn Brewery, UnsignedArtists.com, ArtistDirect.com, MassachusettsRealEstate.com, JWT, Agent16, Blockbuster, DestinyUSA and Damiani Jewelers, and The New York Football Giants.

Awards include Brandweek Guerilla Marketer of The Year, Advertising Age Marketing 100, Pollstar Magazine Talent Buyer of The Year. Graduate, Leadership Greater Washington, Class of '90.

"Create Excitement. Change Perception. Shift Behavior."

Bill Kitchen’s Specialties:
