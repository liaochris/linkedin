Roxanne Emmerich’s Summary

Roxanne Emmerich has consulted with the top financial institutions, and hundreds of other industry leaders. Her book, Profit-Growth Banking, has been called “the Bible of successful business” in that industry. Sales and Marketing Management magazine named her one of the nation’s 12 most requested speakers, and she is a three-time recipient of the Entrepreneur of the Year Award.

A certified management consultant, Roxanne has proven time and again that the ultimate success or failure of a company begins on the inside. Her new book Thank God It’s Monday!: How to Create a Workplace You and Your Customers Love outlines a system for bringing more fun – and consequently more money – to businesses. She conducts workshops across the country for leaders and employees on how to create a TGIM workplace with employees on fire and the bottom line to prove it. She successfully employs her renowned Kick-Butt Kick-Off® strategy to create immediate culture shifts within their company and achieve tangible results often in less than 30-days.

A National Speaker Hall of Fame inductee, she is noted by Sales and Marketing Management Magazine as one of the most requested speakers in the nation for her ability to transform negative workplace performance and environments into “bring it on” result-oriented cultures.

She has published articles in publications ranging from The Wall Street Journal to Woman’s Day Magazine, and is a frequently interviewed on CNN, CBS, and radio and TV stations throughout the country for her business insights.

Roxanne served as a key advisor to former Wisconsin Governor Tommy Thompson, and was the Editor-In-Chief of Extraordinary Banker magazine. She is also the Founder of Permission to Be Extraordinary™ Summit, an unprecedented and exclusive CEO executive breakthrough program.
