Nora Nanayakkara’s Summary

I have worked in the online industry for over five years and as Director of Business Development for UK and Ireland, I established Sedo's UK presence in November 2007. Since then, I've been responsible for new client acquisitions, effective business development strategy and external communication through media/public relations.

My tasks include delivering impactful, clear and innovative messages to support our sales efforts and marketing campaigns through multiple channels including public speaking opportunities and media relations via relevant titles such as The Times, The Observer, The Telegraph, Web User, Brand Republic etc.

Prior to my current role, I was Sedo's International Operations Director at the German headquarters, where I managed the domain monetisation (PPC) teams for Sedo's English-speaking global market (EMEA, UK/Ireland, Australia).

I speak fluent German and hold a Masters in Business Administration (MBA) as well as a Joint BA in European Studies and German. I am an active member of Nominet's Policy Advisory Body (PAB), helping to guide future policy for .uk domain holders and channel feedback from its 2,800 members. In addition, I have recently become a member of Pilotlight, which brings together the passion and expertise of the voluntary sector with corporate business skills to help ensure the sustainability and effectiveness of charitable organizations .
