Paul Anthony Stevens’s Summary

Paul is an experienced Head of Internal Audit / Chief Audit Executive with more than 21 years internal audit, enterprise-wide risk management, internal control, and corporate governance expertise. In addition, Paul served on the Internal Audit Task Team (2008/2009) of the King 3 Committee on Corporate Governance in South Africa.

Paul has extensive specialised knowledge and skill in:
* setting-up of internal audit units;
* realignment of internal audit functions to a modern risk based approach and methodology;
* facilitating and embedding effective enterprise wide risk management structures, systems and processes;
* effective marketing and communication of internal audit/enterprise-wide risk management capabilities and related performance results;
* facilitating control self-assessment across the high risk business areas;
* development of company wide fraud risk profiles;
* facilitating the establishment of effective corporate governance self-assessment processes;
* development of a comprehensive Internal Audit Manual (including IA organization, audit methodology, leadership and management, performance measurement, communication and reporting and fraud awareness) for Corporate Internal Assurance Function;
* development and delivery of internal audit methodology and related training to a global team.

Paul has extensive experience in co-developing management expectations of internal audit, performing strategic and comprehensive business risk assessments, developing risk based internal audit plans, executing risk based internal audits, ensuring effective quality assurance over audit work completed, and providing on-the-job training.

In addition, Paul is a seasoned communicator of internal audit and risk management results to Executive Management, and to the Board's Audit Committee and Risk Committee.

Paul Anthony Stevens’s Specialties:

Paul has more than 11 years leadership experience with extensive internal audit, enterprise-wide risk management, fraud & forensics, SOX's, control self-assessment, and corporate governance knowledge and expertise in the manufacturing, steel & iron, medical & health care, mining, rail transport, FMCG, academic institutions, financial services, and public sector industries.
