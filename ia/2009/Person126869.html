Karolina Sky Rafalski’s Summary

Karolina Sky Rafalski has been a known public figure since her year in Miss South Africa, she was top 5 in the Miss South Africa Pageant, won the Miss Veet Legs South Africa title and has been the face of many international modelling campaigns such as Veet, Revlon Divine Perfume, South Africa’s official Lara Croft Model, has been a lead actress in top Music Videos for Mark Beiling, EVOLVER ,Mandoza and Adam Davis to mention a few. Karolina’s experience and contacts in the entertainment industry in conjunction with her Communications degree with specialization in Journalism and Psychology have assisted her in running her own Promotions and Events Company Runway Projects. With events such as Miss SA, interviews with radio and T.V programmes (as well as presenting them for Cedar Square Shopping centre and being featured on Channel Go).
