Samuel Bath’s Summary

My experience as a business/real estate transactional attorney has provided me with a strong foundation in business evaluation and analysis, tax, accounting, and legal issue recognition, and due diligence navigation. Additionally, I've been exposed to creative and flexible solutions to deal closings still unfamiliar to the general business brokerage industry.

Prior to working at The Independence Group, I represented major construction and shopping mall developers in the Inland Empire in addition to a Fortune 5 corporation as part of its real estate acquisition team tackling issues including: the drafting and negotiation of purchase/sale and lease agreements; counseling clients with respect to CCRs, easements and due diligence; preparing HUD-1 escrow statements and negotiating escrow fees; and assisting clients with acquisition, construction, and take-out loans from commercial lenders and insurance companies.

I've worked with small business owners to incorporate and organize their businesses into corporations and LLCs, obtain small businesses financing guaranteed by the SBA, prepare such businesses for maximum leverage in anticipation of sale, prepare all necessary documentation for asset and stock sales, counsel clients through the due diligence process, and monitor post-closing matters such as consulting agreements, non-competition agreements, and outstanding seller-financing.
