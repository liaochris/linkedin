Robin Reed’s Summary

Robin launched her retained executive search practice to serve founders and investors in Silicon Valley. Her goal was to provide them with a search partner who uniquely understood technical product development, platforms, applications, networks, software engineering and technical operations. Two decades later, Robin has been part of recruiting technology leaders and executives for over 250 start-ups. Along the way, many of her placements helped to make history.

Her firm has been the go to recruiter for the most successful venture capital firms, where she is often a Limited Parnter in their investments. She has the distinction of recruiting the first CIO, CTO and/or VPE executive team members for Ebay, Amazon.com, Yahoo, Apple, Cisco, Google, Facebook, Lucasfilm Ltd., ILM, and many others.
