Jahnelle Pittman’s Summary

I've been a writer more years than I can count, ranging from writing for pleasure to writing as a profession. Just in the past three years, I have proofread and edited hundreds of stories, novels, poems, articles and blogs.

At the beginning of 2008, I found SEO and haven't looked back. In the past year, I have written well over 1,000 pieces: blogs, company profiles, website content, articles, press releases, landing pages and much more.

Currently, I hold the position of Senior Project Manager/Copywriter with Level343.com. At this time, I manage around 10 writers, although we are looking to expand our workforce.

I am also the website administrator/ Editor in Chief and all around "go to" with BestSEOCopywriter.com, the sister company and writing department of Level343.com.

My goals ultimately center around Level343.com, BestSEOCopywriter.com and MedlinkSolutions.com. Within the next five years, I hope to see all three of these sites doing well and running smoothly.

As BestSEOCopywriter.com expands and grows, I plan to make it as client-focused as possible. I want every client to know that as soon as they set their project in our hands it will be handled with the utmost professionalism and interest. From the time we start the project to the time it's finished, we become a part of the team on that project - which means we are as interested in the final results as the client. This is the idea I'd like to get across - this is my ultimate goal.
