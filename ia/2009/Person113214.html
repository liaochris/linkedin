Jim Merrick’s Summary

Senior executive with experience in software and high technology development, as well as international consumer marketing. Proven leadership skills and management expertise working with widely diversified teams with a consistent track record of delivering high-quality products and services. Pragmatic, visionary leader with a results driven, customer-centric background. Hands-on experience managing and growing some of the world’s biggest brands in electronic entertainment. Strong media, analyst and consumer relations experience.

Jim Merrick’s Specialties:

International consumer, brand and technology marketing. Media, consumer and analyst relations. Technology management, software development, network and user interface design.
