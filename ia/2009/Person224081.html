Steve Koerber’s Summary

Steve Koerber achieved recognition as one of Auckland’s most respected agents after appearances on the popular TV1 documentary “Location Location Location” in 1999 and again in 2000. It was no coincidence that the two half hour episodes showed Steve involved in four separate sales for some very happy vendors and purchasers. Whether it is closing a great property deal or his quirky sense of humour, the vendors and purchasers of this dynamic salesperson always seem to be smiling!

Prior to Real Estate Steve served 14 years as an officer in the Royal Australian and Royal New Zealand Navies. He commenced his successful Real Estate career on Auckland’s North Shore in the mid 1990’s and moved to Remuera in 1998. Since then Steve has firmly established himself as one of the area’s top real estate salespeople.

Professional development is important to Steve and he has received tuition from a plethora of internationally acclaimed trainers. He has spoken about how to succeed in real estate to 1000 plus agents at recent Barfoot & Thompson and Real Estate Institute of New Zealand sponsored conventions. Steve also enjoys mentoring selected salespeople who are new to the real estate industry.

Steve has ranked consistently in the top 3% of Barfoot & Thompson salespeople since joining the company. This arguably ranks Steve amongst the top 10% of Real Estate salespeople nationwide.

Steve provides support to his local Community and Schools. He contributes on a regular basis to various Auckland charities including the Child Cancer Foundation – Northern Region. He is also Past President of the Newmarket Chapter of the world’s largest business referral organisation; Business Network International (BNI).

Steve Koerber is a Real Estate salesperson with exceptional inter-personal skills and incredible passion. He sells, on average, one home per week and he would be grateful for the opportunity to sell yours.
