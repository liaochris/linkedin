Greg Carroll’s Summary

Objective
A senior management role in a market driven organization, where the opportunity to drive strategic direction is combined with the need for strong team leadership and direction. Through the application of extensive skills and experience, to deliver profitable revenue, enhanced customer satisfaction and competitive advantage.

Selected Achievements
As Head of Network Operations for Maxis Communications of Malaysia achieved significant improvements in contractual terms with infrastructure suppliers (Motorola and Siemens) for a major GSM network expansion valued at over $100 million, having utilized knowledge of the competitive environment and suppliers' performances to extract maximum benefit from this large investment.

As Head of Network Operations lead a department of 500 field maintenance, network monitoring, vendor management, customer relations, logistical and administrative staff stationed in several locations across Malaysia. Established new operational procedures that delivered 10% improvement in customer satisfaction scores, reduced GSM service downtime by 30% and maximized staff efficiency by 30%.

While consultant to O2 Airwave identified the requirement for enhanced resiliency for emergency services communications post 9/11 that was not being addressed. Developed unique network architecture and worked closely with UK Home Office senior management and technical staff, modified the design which met operational and budgetary constraints. The value of the contract exceeded £300 million and this major investment by the Home Office was a major factor in Airwave's success in winning contracts of £640 million to supply communications for the Fire and Ambulance Services.

Also for O2 Airwave directed a team of in-house and vendor engineers in the design of a secure communications system for UK Fire Services. Took the lead role in negotiating with the Home Office and vendors on technical and cost issues. Awarded a contract vale of £350 million.
