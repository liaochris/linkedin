Sunde J. Sastre’s Summary

I like to create concepts from a 360º view. I do believe there is always one creative solution for each marketing problem, but it is NOT always in the same cocktail. Few times you actually need the whole portfolio and ingredients, the 360º (except probably digital media, a must). But, as Anne Bologne from Toy NY says there is always a 36º solution inside de 360º.

My career is unusual, but not fanciful. I learnt the basics for 7 years in a small full-services agency managed by experienced professionals. Then the Internet scene started in Spain and I wanted to be part of it. After some energy-consuming startups, I look for a consolidated but encouraging project. I found it at DDB (merging Direct and Interactive). That was an opportunity to prove myself "the concept is what really matters" and to collaborate in integrated campaigns. Then I looked for an old-school agency with strong roots on concept (that was Contrapunto BBDO). Nowadays I manage a departament that mixes all disciplines without any problem at Altavia, specialized in retail and focused in promotional and retail marketing. Yes, it is unusual.
