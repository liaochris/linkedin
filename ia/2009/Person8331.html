Alex Kong’s Summary

I majored in Computer Management Systems at Instituto Tecnológico y de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey.

During this time I took the topic of 3D Animation and made an intensive summer course at CSU, Chico CA in Computer Animation as well.

On my return I got a job as Character Animator at www.DigitalMinds.com.mx where I worked for 1 year on the production of a comercial short film and other smaller projects.

I left this job to take the Advanced Character Animation Course from www.AnimationMentor.com

During my time at AnimationMentor I had the chance to take Keith Lango's Animation Personal Training. Mr. Lango is a very well known professional animator in the industry www.KeithLango.com

Then I was very fortunate to start my career as a Professional Animator at www.ClusterStudios.com where I worked and learned, for 1 year, from hyper talented people.

And after that I spent 6 months at the incredible and magical land of www.Organika.tv

And now I´m working as a headhunter and have the assignment of putting together the most amazing 3D animation team Mexico has ever seen!!!
