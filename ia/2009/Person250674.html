Yuzdi N. Badhniwalla’s Summary

My Goal is to pursue a career with a nimble, new-media organisation. Where I can leverage my experience in exploiting nascent opportunities in the internet/media space by building teams that scale into stable businesses. I have augmented this with a deeper understanding of business valuation and venture capital/private equity gained at the Indian School of Business, Hyderabad, something I believe is acutely required especially in the internet space in order to unlock value for stake holders.

My career so far has been marked with successfully taking on challenging projects that most people shy away from.

My current assignment with People Infocom involves understanding the MVAS consumer and formulating strategies to establish mauj.com as a successful brand in the mobile internet.

My last assignment with Rediff.com involved taking up a project which wasn't living up to management's expectations. I took on this challenge and not only salvaged the project, but converted it into a celebrated star project for rediff.com. Prior to that, I was brought in to stabilize the operations of a team that faced a spate of resignations. I applied the principles of theory of constraints to put in place operations that coped with explosive growth, nimble decision making by field operatives and provided visibility to managers for achieving sales goals.

My work with The Times Group in Delhi and Mumbai involved pioneering projects, with no prior template of success to follow. We had to write and re-write the rules, to establish successful businesses.

I am extremely comfortable in situations mired in ambiguity and uncertainty, I find, I am able to understand the business, see patterns in chaos and establish the order necessary to turn a business around.

After having completed about 10 years working in the largest markets in India and then growing and managing a business at the national level, I would like to work with global brands.
