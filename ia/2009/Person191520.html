Rabiul Huq’s Summary

A PRINCE 2 Practitioner qualified Project Manager and former Senior Consultant at IBM/PwC Consulting, with seven years experience in large scale Datawarehouse, Business Intelligence (BI) and MI projects, and two years experience in System Integration projects. Has been engaged across a range of industries including Retail, Broadcast, Banking and Aviation.

Has managed a number of projects, with team sizes between 6 to 20+ project staff. Project values have ranged from $1.5M to $5M+ (AUS). Projects have been from 6 months to 1.5 years in duration.

Deep expertise in all aspects of Datawarehousing / BI, including project management, solution and technical architecture, information management strategy, team leadership and business analysis.

Strong leadership skills and ability to work with all levels of an organisation – from ‘C’ level management, to end users and developers. Adaptable style, with a strong focus on delivering results and achieving a positive outcome.
