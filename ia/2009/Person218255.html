Simon Trudelle’s Summary

Software Marketing Executive – SaaS/Cloud Computing, Internet/Webware, Enterprise Software, Digital Television

I am a product marketing professional with 12 years of European and international business experience, the last 3 of which have been focused on Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) and Web 2.0 enterprise software solutions for the Collaborative Project and Portfolio Management (PPM) market.

My other areas of expertise include: Internet Software & Cloud Computing, Webware, IT management software, digital interactive television and IPTV, e-commerce, Web marketing, Internet services, e-books and print-on-demand.

I have strong capabilities in identifying new strategic business development opportunities, defining new product requirements and launching software products and services to take advantage of these opportunities. Thorough market analysis and a deep understanding of both customer needs and the business potential of technology are central to my approach to success.

Business-focused product vision, company commitment and personal motivation have earned me the trust and support of engineering and sales teams, senior executives and VCs/boards of directors, as well as customers and suppliers. This has proved invaluable in building sustainable products and solutions that deliver business value over time.
