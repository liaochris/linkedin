Jeremiah Delgado’s Summary

As an Executive Recruiter, my specialty is creating a "meeting of the minds."

I direct a team that recruits nationally within in several specialist practices in Online Advertising, Marketing and Media. I have hand selected and personally trained each and every one of them to identify and assist in meeting the needs of companies and candidates alike.

The MRI/Search Max Team operates in several specialist practices:

Internet Marketing, Interactive Marketing, Online Marketing, Mobile Marketing, SEO / SEM, Advertising, Marketing, Media, Rich Media, Social Media, Digital Media, PPC Marketing, Search Engine Marketing, Search Engine Optimization, Media Planning, Media Buying, Consulting, Direct Reponse, Affliliate and Performance Marketing.

MRI’s proven approach to the recruitment process delivers a unique value proposition - Specialist market knowledge, global reach with over 1,100 offices worldwide and an unparalleled network.

I welcome submissions for candidates looking to hear about any roles in the above defined practices

I have formed lasting relationships with key decision makers in companies across the country because I partner with them in building their organization.

I will make the necessary connections on your behalf, or use my existing connections in order to help meet your needs. Allow me to show you what I can do!

Please send resumes to: interactivejobs@mrisearchmax.com

Whether you are a company with an urgent need, or a candidate who has an open ear to new opportunities, contact me to see the value that I bring to your doorstep.
