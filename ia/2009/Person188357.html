PJ LeDorze’s Summary

My experience covers almost 10 years of working as a specialized and skilled Recruiting consultant. I started in the industry back in 1999 during the high-tech boom. Like most, the market was very generous to me. With all the ups and downs it was the best learning experience of my life. After the downturn I took a year off to help start a wilderness studies program for troubled high school kids. Once the program was up and running I jumped back into my real passion...Recruiting. After stints as a Regional Recruiting Manager and Division Manager in the Healthcare industry, I switched back to IT/Software technical recruiting. I couldn’t be happier and to this day love what I do!

Specialties:

Marketing/Sales Side: Business Development, Media/Advertising, Online Marketing, Public Relations, Business Analyst, Product Management/Planning, Copywriting, and Ethnography

Technical/Design: Graphic/Web Design, Usability Design, UX Design, Software Development/Engineering/Architecture (C++, C# (.NET), SQL, Oracle, Java, AJAX, Java Scripting), Quality Assurance (QA), Software Testing, Configuration Management, Network Administration(Network Admin) and System Operations (System Ops)
