Beth Tobin’s Summary

STARPACK is your full service source for custom logo promotional products, apparel and corporate gifts for business and industry. We provide custom printed and stock packaging for retail stores as well as the hospitality industry. STARPACK offers competitive pricing, quality products, fast delivery and the best customer service available.

STARPACK is committed to the environment. In response to your requests for eco- friendly products, we are proud to present recyclable retail packaging and sustainable logo promotional products that are also beautifully designed and encourage an economically prosperous environment.

Let STARPACK help you increase awareness and name branding for your business to potential clients. Logo advertising and repeat name exposure are so beneficial. Creative and quality packaging, promotional gifts, and apparel will increase your visibility and repeat business, while reaching new clients.

STARPACK has access to major manufacturers worldwide. We are owner-operated with a total personal commitment to serving your business since 1995.

Beth Tobin’s Specialties:

Finding and marketing creative products from all over the world. Custom promotional products, gift packaging, business gifts, and logo apparel.
