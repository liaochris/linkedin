Alan Osman’s Summary

My specialty is making complex enterprise software systems easy to use. I ensure that a top-notch current design is coupled with great usability. I have managed design projects for software systems in several verticals including; Financial, Healthcare, High Tech, Consumer, Genomics, CRM, SFA, Social Professional Networks, Collaboration, Messaging, Document Management, Ad Networks, Video, UCG , IT and VOIP.

My engineering background keeps me firmly in touch with acceptable interface controls and standards. I have over 21 years of product development and management experience with consumer and enterprise, both small and large.

As a designer, I have designed some of the most compelling front ends for companies such as Thomson Financial, eRoom, Sermo, Kadient, EnPocket, Gypsii, Bungeelabs, Go2, Knome, Vivox, Awareness Networks, Currensee, Akorri, and Apptix.

As an engineer I have written software for Windows, Web, and Flash platforms using some of the most cutting edge CSS and AJAX techniques. I design interfaces using tools such as Illustrator, Photoshop, Visio, PowerPoint, Flash, Visual Studio and Dreamweaver. I am well acquainted with are: C#, C++, C, ActionScript 2, ASP.NET, CSS 2, Java, JavaScript and have worked extensively with structured databases.
