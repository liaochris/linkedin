Jenny Floravita’s Summary

I am a full-time artist living in California. My oil and watercolor paintings have been in galleries across the country and I regularly exhibit at some of the finest festivals in the greater San Francisco Bay Area.

I also spent several years as a graphic designer in which I created logos, product packages, data sheets, trade show booths, etc... My work as designer gave me a strong foundation in preparation for the many painting commissions that I create each year.

My current goal is to share my new line of glass, hand-painted chandeliers and exhibit them in more galleries and high end art festivals. If you know of a gallery or show that you would recommend my work to, please advise me.

A picture is worth a thousand words so if you would like to see my oil and watercolor paintings and my exotic hand painted glass chandeliers, please visit my website.

http://www.floravita.com/

Jenny Floravita’s Specialties:

My painting technique for my reverse painted glass chandeliers, in particular, is very unique. This is an art form that I am very proud of and have worked hard to find an artist voice that is unusual in the art and design world.
