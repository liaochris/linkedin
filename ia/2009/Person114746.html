Joan Jesion, Life Coach/Compass Leader’s Summary

In my private coaching practice I work with clients one to one or small groups in the area of work/life balance, personal foundation, self esteem, time and energy management.

As a Compass Success Coach and Leader, I work with groups of women both by phone and in person meeting to work on creating the life they want to be living.

I work with and mentor other qualified coaches to be a part of the Compass team.

Joan Jesion, Life Coach/Compass Leader’s Specialties:

Over 28 years as my own boss in the direct selling field and other self-employed jobs has given me a solid background in the pluses and pitfalls of working from home, self direction, self promotion and work/life balance.

Coached and worked with sales teams in two different organizations.

Award winning humorous speaker with expertise in work/life balance, joy and happiness, self esteem and life perspectives.
