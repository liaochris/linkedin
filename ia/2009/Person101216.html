Jack Whipple’s Summary

I've been managing finances and developing mini-ERP database solutions for small privately-held companies for over 20 years. Since 1995 I consolidated operations as the CFO/Controller at Atlanta Metropolitan Publishing so that Edit, Production, Marketing and Sales departments can access one integrated customer/billing/subscriber database, enabling the firm's digital as well as print division campaigns.

Before working at AMP, I was Controller/GM of an Atlanta midtown software training company, and before that, two land-use consulting firms in Los Angeles. At the latter I designed custom time & billing applications to better capture billable hours and increase revenues.
