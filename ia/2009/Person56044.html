David Cheong’s Summary

I am currently a Senior Java Internet Solutions Engineer with 10+ years of IT and consulting experience. I specialise mainly at developing web enterprise systems based on Java (J2EE), Ajax Web 2.0 and Relational Database technologies. I have successfully architected and implemented major systems for clients in the Telecommunications, Financial Services, Government, Tourism and Entertainment industries in both Australia and New Zealand.

I am also an aspiring Entrepreneur and spend much of my free time looking for new business opportunities. Additionally, I run a Personal Development and Business blog titled "Dave Cheong | Engineer to Entrepreneur". The url is:
