Lou Berceli’s Summary

Proven to deliver growth and profitable results within print operations management, selling print communication solutions and business development sales for a global high-tech digital printing press company.

1983 to 2003 instrumental in Acme Printing evolving from a Boston area printer into a nationally recognized leader serving the Fortune 500 marketplace.

In 2000 Quebecor World purchased Acme Printing, growing our 275 employees to 43,000 with160 printing plants.

2004 re-focused within the Digital Print marketplace.

Extensive print industry background helped Xeikon - Punch Graphix to develop new digital color printing press business.

In 2008 joined a Xeikon customer, Expresscopy.com, an infoUSA company. A pioneer with database driven digital printed direct mail via Web-to-Print portals delivering 100% in-line finished, addressed and mailed postcards with infoUSA targeted mail lists.
