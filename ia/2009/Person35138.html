Bryan Jenkins’s Summary

More than eighteen years of experience in academic, cable, and broadcast television production; as well as studio ENG, and EFP experience. Experience as a technical manager, production manager, director, technical director, videographer, video & tape operator, audio technician, gaffer, and engineer daily with Betacam SP, MII, D-3, S-VHS, Digi-Beta, Beta-SX, several DV formats, as well as familiarity with High Definition. Likewise, I have experience with/in video compression for DVD, web, podcasting and other nonlinear media.
