Amrit Singh Grewal’s Summary

Amrit started his career early while attending college at UTA. As a full-time junior, he managed to engage in a summer co-op opportunity at Dallas Semiconductor. Here, through careful study, he helped avoid a major customer disruption ($2M) by discovering anomalies in a product test design.

To diversify his experience while in college, he accepted another co-op position at Nokia's Alliance Manufacturing facility in Fort-Worth. Here, he worked full-time for 4 months while taking a full load of classes at college. The following summer he moved on to Nokia's Irving R&D facility as a Baseband Hardware Intern. He was awarded with 'Nokia's Best Intern Award' and an academic scholarship during this term.

After graduating from UTA in May 2002 with a BS in Electrical Engineering, and amidst a US economy lull, he managed to land a job at memory-maker Micron's Test Department in Boise, Idaho. After a short 1-year stint in Micron, he was drawn back to Texas where he was employed by leading chipmaker Texas Instruments.

He has since been working at T.I.'s various facilities in USA and Asia. Between 2005 and 2007, he helped develop and manage a highly critical quality project in TI's factories at Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia and Taipei, Taiwan.

His home office is Houston, USA. He’s single and likes to spend most of his leisure time in outdoor activities. He’s currently on a six-month assignment in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
