Post University's Official Site’s Experience

  • Official Profile

    Post University

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    January 2009 — Present (7 months)

    At Post University, everything we do is designed to help students develop the knowledge, personal skills and experience they need to compete in today’s and tomorrow’s job market. Students can choose to attend our traditional campus in Waterbury, Connecticut, attend one of our regional locations or complete their degree completely online. Either way, they'll earn a degree from an affordable and fully-accredited "Student-Focused, Career-Driven" university.

Post University's Official Site’s Education

  • Post University

    A lot has changed since Post opened its doors in 1890. Back then, people with a college degree were the exception, not the rule. Today, many companies won't even consider hiring employees without a college education. And it's no longer enough to simply get your degree. Graduates have to have a vision and a plan for managing their careers. They need to have leadership skills and the ability to apply what they've learned in class to a fast-paced, highly competitive global marketplace.

    Today's businesses are looking for graduates who can apply and adapt what they've learned to fit the ever-changing demands of the market. That's why we focus on preparing you to make the connection between scholarship and leadership. Post University students are not just "book smart" when they graduate, they're "career smart".
