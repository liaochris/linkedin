Angela MacKenzie’s Summary

Kendle is one of the world’s leading clinical research organisations, delivering innovative and robust clinical development solutions to biopharmaceutical companies. We provide services in more than 100 countries, spanning 6 continents, and have experience in every major therapeutic area.

Working as part of the Global Recruitment Team at Kendle, it is my responsibility to recruit clinical, regulatory affairs, biometrics and support professionals into the business.

As one of the fastest-growing global providers of Phase I-IV services we are always on the look out for people with the skill, initiative and leadership qualities to drive our business forward.

If you are interested in having a confidential discussion about career opportunities at Kendle, please contact me at mackenzie.angela@kendle.com or through our website www.kendle.com.
