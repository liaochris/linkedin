Marc Ingram’s Summary

The Orbis Group is a very successful and thriving international recruitment business, specialising in sourcing quality candidates for our business partners.

One of the many things that differentiates us is that we have a "will do" attitude, not a "can do" attitude.

Our client base is truly international and includes some of the largest companies in the world. As we respect their confidentiality I will not disclose who they are, but they have come to realise that not all recruitment companies are the same.

Our speciality is a very simple one. We source the candidates that others struggle to find. Our methodology is a closely guarded asset and our reputation is firecely protected.

If you are a potential client who is looking for a recruitment partner to relieve you of the burden of recruiting both temporary and permanent staff, then please get in touch.

Whether it's Saudi Arabia or Canada; engineers or accountants, Germany or Dubai; architects or geologists, we are here to help.

Marc
