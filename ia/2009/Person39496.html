Kevin Smith’s Summary

Kevin Smith is CEO, Managing Partner of Cayenne Global where he brings twenty years of strategic consumer insights and marketing experience from both the client and consulting side primarily with Fortune 100 technology and CPG companies.

Kevin has a successful track record of managing global marketing projects and strategic customer insights initiatives through strong implementation skills and clear communication and change management strategies. Kevin has an ideal blend of multicultural and general market customer insights fact-base development and global marketing experience, culminating in his ability to combine and link directly to marketing initiatives for greater business alignment and impact. Kevin Specializes in state of the art marketing deployment and brand management models as well as the latest social networking and viral communication techniques. He has an advanced market simulation and modeling background, with in-depth experience in advanced analytics and its integration into marketing program planning and tracking.

Kevin’s insight experience runs the entire spectrum of customer types, having shared equal time in the development of fact-bases and marketing programs for both end-use consumer segments and business customers.

Prior to starting Cayenne Global, Kevin was the Global Marketing Transformation Lead at Chevron Corporation where he led a four year global marketing strategy and voice-of-the-consumer transformation initiative. Kevin has also served as VP of Strategic Planning at McCann Erickson World Wide where he was responsible for global CRM and customer insights strategy for Microsoft. Kevin has a strong brand marketing background having served as Senior Brand Manager for both Levi Strauss and The Clorox Company and as VP Marketing at Bizfinity Inc.

Kevin has also provided marketing strategy and insights consulting services to Pfizer, Bose, Mercedes-Benz, Clorox, Disney, Lego and Johnson & Johnson to name a few.
