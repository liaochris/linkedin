Jodie Thill, ASID, LEED AP’s Summary

Strategic and high energy Senior Commercial Interior Designer with a reputation for designing space that creates an emotional impact. Diverse experience includes public libraries, higher education, corporate, retail and healthcare. Superior architectural aptitude, with a strong understanding of codes, construction standards, documentation and detailing practices. Resourcefully applies energy-efficient and sustainable design principles to design practice. Proficiency in green building general concepts, the LEED Rating System and the commercial green building industry. Exceptional ability to build trust and rapport with client thus enhancing the consulting relationship.
