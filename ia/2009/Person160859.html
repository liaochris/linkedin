Michael Blackstone M.D.’s Summary

My focus is on making healthcare work for both patients and physicians using information technology at its finest and making the practice of medicine more efficient with hopes to curb the rising costs of healthcare while enabling a better experience for both providers and patients. Furthermore, I am working to bridge the medical gap between providers and patients around the world, notably between the under-served and well-served populations, by creating access to healthcare never before available to rural physicians and their patients.
