Aspiring Writer/Novelist: Avid reader and lover of fantasy/sci-fi genres working on an epic story set in a medieval world with swords and sorcery. Major recent influences being Robert Jordan and George R. R. Martin. Also working on a short story that is part of the "history" of the world, to work on my expression of battles and give time to further refine the fantasy world.
