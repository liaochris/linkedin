John Everson’s Summary

I have been serving customers for over 23 years.

My areas of experience are sales, sales management, and product & inventory management.

My only goal is to ensure that each of my customers are using the technology available today to enhance their customer's experience. Each phone call received by a company is crucial, and can either create more business, or haunt them by bad word-of-mouth, for many years to come.

Mobility, flexibility and the ability to adapt to a changing market are all areas that can be enhanced by the right phone system and phone service.

I offer a FREE consultation, and make recommendations on your current phone system, call flow (the way calls are handled when they are answered), phone service and data service (T1, MPLS, et cetera)

With three different phone systems, and as an agent for FIVE different service providers, I work to tailor a system and service package that truly fits your needs.

Call today, and I will schedule a free review of your business. Also included will be a free telco review, which will analyze your current service.

Many of my customers are either paying too much, have fraudulent charges on their bill, or could save money (and increase functionality) by upgrading their phone service to digital.
