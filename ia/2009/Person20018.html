Arno Kapteyn’s Summary

Arno Kapteyn is Managing Consultant IT Governance. He is a leading expert in the field of IT Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) and how to integrate them with IT Security and IT Service Management. His field of expertise is the design and implementation of IT Organizations according to the available open and industry standards

He has an analytical mind and is taking up challenges in the crossing-border area of security, governance and architecture, where new solutions are required. He develops long-term strategic visions and plans the delivery of those visions. Furthermore he is pragmatic and flexible in the execution of these plans. Arno has an excellent team spirit and an open attitude towards different perspectives for instance those resulting from (international) cultural differences.
