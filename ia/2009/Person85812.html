©hr®is Trademarkblogger’s Summary

After having seen the business world from various sides, including as a solo practitioner acting for international clients and as in-house counsel for an international company I decided to learn more about the business side of things and joined an internationally operating Internet company as their business developer. As I started missing the law (believe it or not!) I decided to set up my blog www.german-trademark-blog.com.

This blog is a result of my keen interest and passion for this are of the law. It is intended to facilitate understanding of German trademark law, copyright law and intellectual property. The blog is mainly aimed at lawyers and business people from outside of Germany who might be less familiar with the German Jurisdiction.

However it is not meant to provide legal services or advice of any kind and does not establish a client attorney relationship.
