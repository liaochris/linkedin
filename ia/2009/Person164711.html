Mike Orren’s Summary

Battle-tested media executive and entrepreneur who has successfully led startups and divisions of large corporations. At best when stakes are high, resources scarce and failure not an option. Strategic leader who has to have his hands dirty. New media junkie who started his career laying out pages with an Exacto and a gluestick. Proactive leader, creative marketer and communicator.

Mike Orren’s Specialties:

All aspects of print and online media: Editorial, production, advertising, general management, sales, contract negotiations, P&L management, consulting.

Mike Orren’s Experience

  • President

    Panlocal LLC / Pegasus News (Fisher Communications)

    (Partnership; 11-50 employees; Online Media industry)

    April 2005 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    Conceived and led launch of largest single-market pureplay local news and information site in the US, featuring proprietary behavioral targeting mechanism (The Daily You™). Raised angel capital and negotiated sale of company to NASDAQ-traded broadcast company. Led post-sale growth and launched nation’s first neighborhood-level location-aware mobile advertising model.

  • Publisher

    American Lawyer Media

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Publishing industry)

    August 2001 — April 2005 (3 years 9 months)

    Revitalized $4.5 million revenue enterprise with a weekly newspaper, website, twelve book titles and custom seminars. Doubled profits and increased long-stagnant revenues during overall industry downturn. Opened new line of business with products geared towards corporate attorneys. Created seminar business to offset declining classifieds business. Created targeted email marketing program that drove sales across all divisions. Participated in local and national web relaunch. Managed key accounts.

  • President

    Cliffmedia, LLC

    (Publishing industry)

    September 1999 — August 2001 (2 years)

    Operated media consultancy focused on local and niche print and online media while executing diligence on regional magazine launch. Re-engineered operations of and created ancillary revenue streams for Business NC, Carolinas Publishing Associates, Cox Interactive Media and The Leader. Created web products for all clients. Created profitable “emergency” special edition for political coalition advocating a bond proposition.

  • Associate Publisher / General Manager

    D Magazine

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry)

    January 1995 — September 1999 (4 years 9 months)

    Key team leader in relaunch of one of country’s most successful regional titles. Managed all aspects of business, including sales, cashflow, content, marketing, circulation, manufacturing and distribution. Led company to profitability in 18 months. Launched nation’s first city magazine website. Other positions held: Production director; Editor, Business Dallas; Circulation director.

  • Editor/Art Director/Production Manager

    MARCOA Publishing

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry)

    January 1994 — January 1995 (1 year 1 month)

    Managed 15 annual and semi-annual titles for economic development clients and launched a local business magazine patterned on Harvard Business Review.

  • Project Manager

    Village Publishing

    (Publishing industry)

    July 1993 — January 1994 (7 months)

    Edited and managed six titles for custom publishing division of major directory publisher.
