Polly Parsons Howard’s Summary

I have worked in development for almost four years. My primary focus is fundraising for the Booth College of Business and Professional Studies and the Intercultural and International Center (IIC) at Northwest Missouri State University. In this capacity, I am responsible for contacting alumni who are graduates of the Booth College or who have been involved with the IIC, as well as connect with friends of the university who are interested in supporting Northwest.

I am always seeking connections to individuals who are interested in helping to move Northwest to the next level as a higher education institution. I welcome any feedback or contacts that anyone would like to share of potential funders or partners for Northwest.

Prior to being employed with my alma mater, I worked in television news as an anchor/reporter/producer in Des Moines, Iowa and St. Joseph, Missouri.
