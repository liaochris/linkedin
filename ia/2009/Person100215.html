Itzik Levy’s Summary

I really enjoy designing websites while trying to put a different and unique spin into them so they stand out from the average site. I love playing around with Photoshop and Illustrator and making interesting images with a combination of colors and effects. Doing this tends to inspire me to make a new website which implements that image into the design. I can't recall how often I change the layout to my website - it's been way too many times.

Aside from that, I love taking pictures with the massive SLR cameras. My favorite kind of pictures are macro shots where I zoom right into the subject and take the picture. Doing this makes really cool pattern-like textures. Another great effect I like using is to go outside at night and take pictures of the lights, creating a lightscape. The final picture usually always has stunning results.

My real passion though is for video production. It's an art which allows every person on set to tell their own version of a story, which when combined together should impact the viewing audience. The man holding the lights can set the mood, the editor can set the pace, and cinematographer can capture moments... every different person with a combined effort of importance makes the final product an art.
