V. Michelle Stephenson’s Summary

I am a Independent PR Consultant who has an extensive background in public relations, marketing, newspaper and magazine advertising, sales and human resources. I am the President and CEO of VMS Communications, a boutique PR and Diversity Marketing company. My clients are primarily authors, motivational speakers, non-profit organizations and small to mid-size businesses.

I have just recently starting a national online speakers' directory called The National Speakers' Network, which consists of profiles of authors, motivational speakers, consultants and experts from a wide range of industries posted on my website, http://www.vmscommunications.net/. Contact me to find out how to become a member or to book a speaker.
