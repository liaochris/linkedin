Melanie Clark’s Summary

16 years of media experience, with the last 10 years in online.

After the usual Uni grad introduction at VNU I moved into online ad sales in the dotcom boom of 1999, and spent the next couple of years riding the wave as it ebbed & flowed.

In 2006 I moved from sales into online agency planning & buying, and have really enjoyed using the knowledge I've gleaned to help to choose between the myriad of options available (and know a good deal when I hear one)!
