Prasad Kunisetty’s Summary

I lead a group of businesses in Media, IT and Real Estate in US and in India.

On the IT side, Effexoft has multiple products with more than 300 customers and offers Consulting and Placement Services in USA. US operations are headquartered at Princeton, NJ. Effexoft has a 50+ member development, design and support team located at Hyderabad, India. Effexoft specialised in Educational Software (http://resultica.effexoft.com), Web Design and Application Development and IT Support Services. Its products include Content Management Systems and Corporate Intranet modules.

On the Media side, TeluguPeople.com (TP) is a 10 million page view portal with half a million registered users. TP has a print magazine as well in United States (http://usmag.telugupeople.com). There are more than 10 more interactive / social networking / global sites in our group and we have great technical platform and CMS systems built for the media domain. We provide media solutions to some of India's largest publishing houses. We are looking forward to expand our reach in the Media industry.

TP Realtors is our group's Real Estate company in India and we have multi-million dollar projects on hand.

We are looking for growth capital and looking forward to mergers and acquisitions. Currently in the process of acquiring a US based IT Company. We have various investment opportunities - project based investments to equity.

On the community side, I am a member of TANA, ATA, TiE and several other Indian American Associations.

Coming to the social responsibility, I actively support Child Aid Foundation, Telugu Foundation and Kinnera Memorial Foundation.
