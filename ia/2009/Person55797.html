Daryl H. Bryant’s Summary

Daryl H. Bryant, founder, President and CEO of Hudson Horizons, has spent the last ten years working in technology communities and Internet-related industries. With a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics, Mr. Bryant brings great proficiency to his business ventures. He possesses expert-level knowledge and understanding of website development, social networking and search engine optimization.

Mr. Bryant also has extensive comprehension of technical sales and management. His post-college career began at Knoa Corporation, a software company that develops a proprietary user-sensing technology. That technology provided training and statistical analysis for large-scale corporate applications such as PeopleSoft, IBM, SAP, and Kraft. He began as a Solution Developer but quickly advanced in his position, taking on managerial and technical sales responsibilities.

In the short span of a year, Mr. Bryant was promoted to Quality Assurance Manager and thereafter to Lead Sales Engineer. In that top position, he was responsible for providing outside technical sales to the Knoa sales team. He also prepared and delivered corporate presentations, technical demonstrations and product training to an international audience of prospective and preexisting clients.

During the burst of the “Internet Bubble” in 2002, Mr. Bryant left Knoa Corporation and established Hudson Horizons, a professional Website Development and Internet Marketing company.

At Hudson Horizons, Mr. Bryant manages all marketing, sales, and client communications within the business. With his experience and expertise, he establishes close working relationships with all clients. By visiting their offices, facilities and/or warehouses he is able to gain a true understanding of their corporate structure and business model.

Mr. Bryant genuinely fulfills the meaning of entrepreneurship, progressively striving towards his goal to build Hudson Horizons into the leading Web Development Company of the future.
