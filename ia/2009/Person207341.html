Jennifer Salisbury Rucks’s Experience

  • Owner

    Salisbury Vineyards

    (Farming industry)

    December 2002 — Present (6 years 8 months)

    Salisbury Vineyards is a family-run company and we have put a lot of love and time into developing it into something wonderful. Our wines are carefully crafted from grapes grown by our family in Avila Valley and Paso Robles. Each varietal has its own identity as we design all of our labels in-house. Our inspiration comes from many places, but mostly from our family's rich agricultural history in California. Please visit us on your next trip to Avila Beach - in the old Schoolhouse in Avila Valley (6985 Ontario Road, the frontage road to Hwy. 101 between the Avila Beach exits, 11 - 6 pm Daily - 805-595-WINE).
