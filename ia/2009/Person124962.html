Gerald Sudduth’s Summary

Gerald Sudduth currently resides in Dallas, TX. He grew up in Texas, Washington State, Vermont and went to college at West Virginia University. After obtaining his BFA in Graphic Design with minors in Business Communications, Photography and Sculpture in 2001, Gerald began working throughout his collegiate career first as a Graphic Designer and later as the Assistant Manager of Production for The Daily Athenaeum, West Virginia Universities student newspaper and started his freelance design company digiphics,inc, where he continues to design for a variety of clients throughout the United States and Europe.

Upon graduation, in 2001, Gerald decided to move to Orlando Florida, to pursue an Associates degree in Computer Animation at Full Sail University. While at Full Sail University, Gerald held the position of Statistician for NBC for the Arena Football League.

Upon graduation in 2003 from Full Sail University, Gerald accepted the Sales/Marketing Coordinator position at Smith Manufacturing in Pompano Beach Florida, enabling him to increase revenue by 3% within 3 months and continued to improve the companies marketing campaigns and revenue in the industry. Towards the end of 2007 and after a hurricane destroyed not only his residence, but also his employer, decided to return back to his roots in Dallas.

Shortly after, joined O'Boyle Properties/ARAUSA, a top-10 real estate brokerage firm in Dallas as a Graphic Designer in May 2007 and then later to Earthvision, LLC in 2008 as Creative Director and continues to design at digiphics, inc.

Specialties: Graphic Design, Illustration, Concept Development, Animation, Story Boarding, Logo Development, Marketing Collateral, Photo-Manipulation, Web Development, Business Communications, Photography, Sculpture.
