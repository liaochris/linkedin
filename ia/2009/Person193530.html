Randall Crocker’s Summary

Senior marketing/sales executive with over 20 years experience in building brand and demand with technology products and services, with an expertise in B2B e-business/direct marketing.

Aggressive leader in website development and search engine marketing (SEM), including SEO & PPC.

Savvy in determining market potential of products/services and multi-channel marketing strategies.

Proven ability in establishing effective and loyal customer, partner, and channel relationships.

Successful experience in positioning and preparing companies to be acquired or to go public.

Demonstrated skill in negotiating extremely favorable contracts, in excess of $450M, with major direct and computer industries manufacturers and suppliers, including Compaq, HP, IBM, NCR, Oracle, Microsoft, and Toshiba.
