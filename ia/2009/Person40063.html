Howie Schnee’s Summary

Creative Ent Group has nearly 15 years of music industry experience offering mgmt, booking, production, and promo services to corporations, venues and musical acts.

They have produced & promoted concerts with numerous artists including Brandi Carlile, Brazilian Girls, Dashboard Confessional, Derek Trucks, Disco Biscuits, G. Love & Special Sauce, Galactic, Grace Potter, Goldrush, Guster, moe., Matt Pond PA, New Found Glory, N. Miss. Allstars, Phil Lesh, Robert Randolph, The Slip, Soulive, String Cheese Incident, Tea Leaf Green, Toubab Krewe, Trey Anastasio, Umphrey’s McGee, Voxtrot, etc. at such venues as Irving Plaza, BB Kings, Highline Ballroom, Knitting Fact, Canal Rm, House of Blues (Los Angeles). They exclusively book and produce all events at the 345 cap Lion’s Den in NYC.

CEG produced their first outdoor fest in 2007 at 3000 cap ampitheatre Indian Ranch in W. Mass, w/ Boston indie promoters Rock On Prod. The day-long Garden Grove Fest, feat Badfish, drew over 1700 fans. Plans are in the works for a '08 Garden Grove Fest, and other CEG/Rock On-produced events.

CEG has managed or done artist development work with acts such as Billy Mann, Nine Days, & Antigone Rising working with such record labels as Lava, Atlantic, Epic and A&M. Today, CEG exclusively manages Badfish & Project/Object.

CEG is involved with sponsorship, marketing and event planning as well. Corp. clients have included Pernod Ricard, Jameson Irish Whiskey, Spin Mag, Flair Comm., Shooting Gallery, IDG, etc.

CEG has demonstrated savvy by promoting artists and events in innovative ways. They have various niche-oriented websites and have developed email lists of 20K+ targeted music fans. They have developed a promotional route of over 200 stores in Manhattan and Hoboken. They manage street teams of music fans. CEG provides promo services for artists, promoters, record labels, mktg co.'s & venues such as AEG, Manhattan Ctr, Media Star, Nokia Theater, Town Hall and Webster Hall.

Howie Schnee’s Specialties:

Club and concert promotion, artist management and development, event production, venue negotiation, third-party marketing and promotions, online and offline grassroots promotion.
