Angela Best’s Summary

MarketingTourGuide.com is aimed toward small and medium tourism and hospitality business owners and managers, businesses that typically don't have marketing departments and if they do it's one person trying to do it all... alone.

It doesn't matter whether you operate a hotel, inn, villa, B&B, tourism activity, attraction or are responsible for promoting a destination, MarketingTourGuide.com covers a broad range of topics from offline to online including the most important subjects in today's challenging marketing environment.

Topics include social media marketing, how to build on-line communities, developing and distributing e-newsletters and much, much more. Information is delivered through how to articles, templates, video tutorials and advice on other web services and interviews with experts in their field.

Anahere Inc - With extensive background in managing the North American marketing activity for international tourism organizations Angela expanded her company, Anahere Inc,Tourism Marketing Group to include the self-help site MarketingTourGuide.com and TravelLeadConcepts.com, to promote ideas on generating leads in the tourism and hospitality industry. Anahere, Inc. continues to operate as tourism marketing consultancy and coaching operation providing services which include: Marketing Analysis, Marketing Coach, Virtual Marketing Director, E-commerce Evaluation and Customer Service Check up.
