Elviss Zants’s Summary

since a young age I've been into creative means - graphic design, art, music, video, animation; i've been working as an Art Director and Copywriter in concert and event promotion companies and as a Creative Director in two of the biggest network advertising agencies in Riga - the DDB and the TBWA\ (the last of which is my current position); i'm also the owner and director of the META-KAFE - a restaurant-cafe-bar-art and culture space-music venue-catering service-media and event promoter at Spīķeri - the up and coming creative industries are of Riga city...

back in the beginning of the decade together with DJ, producer and composer KRII (Kristaps Krievs) we created the free hip-hop music, wordsmith and visual arts collective called Ritmiņš Unltd.; the crew was lucky to present this fusion of art forms as a support act the likes of RZA of Wu-Tang Clan and DJ Ease of Nightmares on Wax; ever since the crew dismantled for it's members were then young people with lots of individuality and later wanted to express themselves separately the duo kripoo un elvi (Kristaps and me) have been pushing the limits of music production and performance - together we've done a 10-hour live electronic music marathon at a rave called PHAT2 in Riga and worked on music for "underground" film; as Elvi the Tech-Sapien I perform also as a fully live improvisational techno-music act the premiere of which was experienced upon the closing event of the contemporary art and video festival Waterpieces in Riga, 2007; together with KRII as a guest star to this concept we've done a show also at the Baltic's biggest and developed dance music venues that is the club Essential upon the occasion of the event Kwadrat Concept; I also hold down the fort of disc jocking as Elvi Soulsystems - ever since my first residency that was the club/restaurant Casablanca in the old town of Riga I've been out and about playing all kinds of music with the sole principle that is has to have... soul!
