Collin Summers’s Summary

Collin has a terrific ability to understand a business and weave an easy to follow narrative about where the company is heading and how IT can help successfully deliver on a company's vision.

He has proven successful in taking the existing technologies, talent, and vision of a company meld it with new technology options and sculpt it into a document which the entire company can understand and execute.

Collin's experience in a diverse range of industries and technologies, coupled with his international experience can be utilized to help drive innovation and optimization for a variety of organizations.

He has been successful in leading IT during integration of businesses into a corporation as well as the sale and divestiture of business.

-Optimization of budgets and vendor relationships to drive down costs and improve operations.
-Experience writing and speaking on technical subject matter to both technical and non-technical audiences.
-Proficiency in many programming languages, operating systems, and database technologies.
-Focus on IT/IS as a customer support team.
-Significant experience in leadership and management of international teams.
