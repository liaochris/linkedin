Dal Watson’s Summary

Dal currently serves in the capacity of Vice President at Allied North America Insurance Brokerage of Texas, LLC overseeing the sales and retention of commercial property & casualty, employee benefits, surety, and corporate & personal planning accounts. Dal has over nineteen (19) years of insurance industry experience and works with employers all across Texas and New Mexico.

Allied North America of Texas* offers a very experienced roster of professionals in insurance, risk management, and financial services. As an independent insurance agency, Allied is dedicated to helping customers protect their bottom lines by identifying personal and business-specific risks, and finding the best protection at the lowest possible cost.

With Texas offices in Dallas and Midland, Allied North America of Texas is supported by Allied Group Holdings, which is one of the largest independent insurance agencies in the nation.** This means greater negotiating power as well as direct access to senior management of the major U.S. insurance companies. In addition, Allied has it own Claims and Loss Control staffs to act as your advocate to the insurance industry.
