Martha Bollinger, SPHR, CAFM’s Summary

Results-oriented Human Resource Generalist with 13 years HR management experience in corporate, division, and international HR positions, plus six years financial management responsibility for 1500+ vehicles in U.S., Canada, Puerto Rico, and Mexico as a Fleet Manager.

As a self-motivated, customer service focused manager and team player with excellent analytical and communication skills, a large part of my career has been devoted to assuming challenging positions requiring my expertise to improve bottom line results. I have demonstrated capabilities in motivating diverse organizations to achieve common goals and translating strategy into action.
