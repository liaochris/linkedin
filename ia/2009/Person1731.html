Abhay Bhargava (bhargava.abhay@gmail.com)’s Summary

Abhay holds over 9 years of experience, specializing in streamlining business processes, new business evaluation and structuring strategic partnerships - a majority of it derived across a multi-cultural, global platform. Abhay has gained over 4 years of rich experience within, and detailed insight of, the Energy industry, through active business development and industry participation across a number of countries, including the Americas, Middle East, South East Asia and the Indian sub-continent.

A start-up specialist, Abhay is adept in taking new businesses/ ideas from conception to revenue & profitability in short periods of time.

Abhay is well networked across the entire Energy industry value chain, including National Oil Companies, Engineering and Design firms, EPC contractors and Service/ Material/ Equipment providers.

Abhay has led many assignments with geographically dispersed teams, and has successfully ensured timely delivery through his project management skills.

In his previous role, Abhay was responsible for consultative selling in the GCC region for an organization focused exclusively on the Oil and Gas sector. His success was reflected through new client acquisition, identifying and roping in partners, and strategizing new growth avenues for the business.

Previously handled Northern India Sales for Philips India Limited, for their range of business oriented display products. Led a turnaround for them for this product line specifically, by expanding sales in the North India region, enhancing the distribution channel and increasing direct sales.

Abhay holds an MBA in Marketing & Systems, and a Diploma in Information Technology - further, he constantly stays updated on trends in the Energy and Power industry verticals globally.
