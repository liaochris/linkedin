John Rothstein’s Summary

I work with busy business owners to solve that problem through SendOutCards. We provide an automated, yet highly personalized card system for customer follow up, thank yous, birthdays, product or event announcements, even for generating referrals.

With SendOutCards you send your customers highly personalized cards through the mail using our unique website technology without ever leaving your office! So using our product does NOT add to your busy day, yet it gives you an easy way to have that personal customer contact. You could send 5 or 500 cards in 5 minutes, in your own handwriting, and even upload a picture or logo into the card.

Our motto: People will forget what you said, they will forget what you did, but they will never forget how you make them feel. For about $5 per year per customer you can make your customers feel special and appreciated, and they will never forget you.
