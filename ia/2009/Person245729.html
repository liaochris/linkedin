  • Project Leader – Global Manufacturing

    Volvo Trucks

    (Public Company; Automotive industry)

    September 1999 — June 2002 (2 years 10 months)

    Global Manufacturing sets the manufacturing strategy for the Volvo Truck group, and ensures that these strategies are reflected in the product design process. It also coordinates the introduction of product and process projects throughout the group.

    My role as a Project Leader was to work in the design project groups and the various factories to ensure that the projects and strategies were implimented. I was responsible for 71 projects, of which 34 projects moved through the gates and were introduced. The Manufacturing budget for these projects range from SEK 75,000 up to SEK 5,000,000 (AUD 15,000 to 1,000,000).

    I also ensured all current projects were co-ordinated with a major model change. Over 300 smaller projects were affected when the coordination work began, only 6 open items remained at production start with no effect on production.

    My favorite projects were:
    Electronically controlled hydraulic steering,
    Euro 3 engine installation,
    Upgrade of the Vickers hydraulic PTO pump,
    The Vertical Muffler installation,
    Swap body superstructure,
    New B-Ride suspension.
