Brian Smith’s Summary

Brian earned his undergraduate degree in International Affairs from The George Washington University in Washington, D.C. His start in the hospitality industry came as a Quality Intern, and later Lobby Greeter, at The Ritz-Carlton (Washington D.C. and Tysons Corner).

After going abroad to study in Tokyo, Japan, Brian returned to the United States and began work as the Lobby Manager at Walt Disney World's flagship property, Disney's Grand Floridian Resort & Spa. He moved on to be Front Desk Manager at Disney's Coronado Springs Resort & Convention Center before joining Fairmont Hotels and Resorts as a Front Desk Manager/Night Manager at The Plaza in New York City.

Currently, he is a Property Manager for REDAC Inc's New York City properties, managing various investments for mostly Japanese investors.

Brian Smith’s Specialties:

Guest recognition, arrival experience, green lodging, team building, employee recognition
