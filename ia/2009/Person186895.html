Phil Hill’s Summary

Over 12 years extensive entrepreneurial experience building technology companies from start-up through exit.

Biggest value adds - achieving a lot with little resource. Doing it in ways no one else would have thought of. Being able to implement as well as plan.

Early stage - created a successful market presence for multiple companies and products in US and Europe

Strategy product development - created market leading technology that became the de facto standard for downloading media on the Internet.

Negotiation & business development - established profitable revenue generating relationships with marquee accounts including, Netscape, AOL, Coca Cola and Pepsi.

Customer acquisition - generated a worldwide customer base of 15 million users for a software start-up with $0 initial budget.

Innovation – Created one of the leading software-as-a-service phone companies targeting micro enterprises; developed & deployed early stage internet music subscription model; pioneered early online advertising vehicles, used by Microsoft,AOL, and Yahoo.
