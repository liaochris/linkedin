Allison Abizaid’s Summary

Through Allison's 30 years of practice in health and wellness and counseling she has learned that for most of us the very purpose in life is to seek happiness and is found through a balance of a healthy mind, body and spirit. Combining her early training as a professional dancer, continued education and advanced training in water exercise, rehabilitation, personal / strength training, body building and conditioning, Allison shares her knowledge with hundreds of women and men. Seen and respected in the field of wellness, she continues to broaden her practice with 2 masters degrees in sport (health) psychology, clinical psychology, individual, family and couples counseling. Her quarterly marriage education classes and couples counseling practice is supported through PAIRS a proven world renowned program used in saving and enhancing thousands of couples worldwide. Many women single and married, dating and not dating, have benefited from her groups learning; a new understanding of men as well as discovering for ourselves as women; insights to our feminine qualities, recognizing healthy men, how to celebrate men and at the same time get our needs met.

Allison moved to Reston, VA from California in 2006 to be closer to family. Allison combines her talents and years of hard work in to one location, addressing individuals and corporate environments interested in improving quality of life issues addressing specific circumstances, lifestyle management, medical and health conditions. She provides programs for individuals to take care of themselves emotionally and physically, resulting in decreased stress and disease development; improving their lifestyle moving toward a state of health as they relate to physical health, emotional wellbeing and healthy sense of self. Along with her practice, Allison holds classes and workshops through various gym locations, the Reston Community Center and writes monthly health articles for Your health magazine.
