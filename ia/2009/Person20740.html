Arup Soans’s Summary

Nothing in life is constant but change; holding this as my motto, I have learned to mould life’s challenges to my advantage.

I always put my mind to every task I take up, be it elocution, dramatics, sports, work related projects, etc. I have represented my school playing soccer at State, District and other cluster levels. I have won laurels at elocution and also been a part of the festival of school choirs for five years. My experience in organizing inter-college events at Wilson College, Mumbai helped me immensely to add value to the organizations I worked with, post my graduation. Having proved myself time and again I know that even though I have all the qualities required to be a great leader, I am a fair team player which pushes me to give and take my best efforts. As General Secretary and Sports Secretary of my college hostel, I refined my ability to coalesce, manage and lead people of diverse cultures and very different mindsets to work towards a common goal and achieve it. My active participation in all team events has helped bring about positive and innovative ideas. Being the Executive Editor and Editor-in-Chief of my college newspaper for two years, I have seen it’s circulation hit record highs.

During my tenure at Sleepins Apparels Pvt. Ltd. and Pragmites Internet Consulting Pvt. Ltd., I led teams of 20 plus people and was able to motivate and drive them to bring out the best in their ability. I took up the Commonwealth Youth Games Pune 2008 for a period of four months to give back to the city that I have resided in for almost two decades as well as satisfy my inclination towards the world of sport. I believe that broadening ones knowledge in various fields is a must, but finding ones niche is the most critical for success.

To sum myself up I would like to say that I follow the principle, ‘thinking is a poor relative of acting’. You are what you do, not what you say you want to do.
