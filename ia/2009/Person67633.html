Dr. Robert D House, PhD, CWPP, CAPP, CIRAS’s Summary

Until now, there has been NO PLACE the public could turn to determine if an advisor meets a reasonable standard of care when giving asset protection advice.

Bob House trains CPA’s, Attorney’s, Real Estate Agents as well as other Advisor’s and Insurance Agent’s in the strategies of wealth preservation that are detailed in his book. But, Bob is neither a CPA or Attorney.

Why do these CPA’s and other Advisor’s listen so intently? They know that Bob has obtained over his career knowledge they require to successful serve their affluent clients.

Bob’s initial training, a PhD in finance, has helped him gain his national reputation. He uses his academic perspective to bore down into the intricacies of the US tax code. As he states in his seminars, “Don’t be afraid of the tax code. It is just another form of “asset protection.” Many of the regulations were actually written with the intent to minimize the taxes of the affluent. I’m here to help you embrace it on behalf of your clients.”

Bob practices what he preaches. Bob has over 25 years of experience in advising affluent clients on how to implement the strategies found in this book. And over the years he has, unfortunately, seen the disastrous aftermath when people don’t consciously protect their wealth.

The down-sizing of corporate America has brought an influx of new clients to Bob’s office. He calls these apprehensive executives, his “corporate casualties.” They realize that they need to transfer millions of dollars from their corporate pensions to self-directed retirement plans. He has a unique way to “asset protect” one of their most valuable vehicles. Bob is their advisor of choice. He is their specialist, their “IRA \ 401(k) asset protection Doctor.”
