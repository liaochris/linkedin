Stefania Passamonte’s Summary

“Stefania is a wonderfully passionate musician who is highly involved in what she plays...and she brings to her playing a refreshing honesty and an appealing enthusiasm”.
London based artist, Stefania is an Italian pianist from Modena with a wide international experience, from concert performing to solo recording.”An unusually gifted young musician..”, Stefania has given recitals all around Europe and was selected to perform on the stage of the most famous International Piano Competitions as the Chopin Competition of Warsaw (in television, radio and internet broadcasting), the “Maria Canals” of Barcelona and the Valencia Competitions, the Porto Competition or the Busoni Competition . Stefania won several acknowledgments and scholarships, among which the prestigious “Premio Ghirlandina” given by the Rotary Club in Modena to an artist “distinguished in the international panorama for special merits and success”.
The Concert Recital Diploma with famous music critic Piero Rattalino at the “St. Cecilia” International Academy in Portogruaro (Venice) opened her interest in researching the different styles and traditions of the piano tecniques and interpretations from the very source. She then refined her Rachmaninov and Scriabin repertoire with Anton Naumov, her Spanish repertoire with Joaquin Soriano and Emmanuelle Carra, Isacco Rinaldi (the only assistant of Arturo Benedetti Michelangeli), Emmanuelle Krasovskij. Stefania gained as well the Postgraduate Diploma in Piano at the Royal Academy of Music of London with Ian Fountain and Christopher Elton. She then completed her studies with Jacques Lagarde at the Ecole Nornale de Musique de Paris “A. Cortot” for refining her Chopin and French repertoire.
“Impressed by her fine and original talent...Her playing is charatcterized by a powerful ability to communicate with the audience...sense of drama and genuine spontaneity”
