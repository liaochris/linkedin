Diego Simon’s Summary

Diego Simon has a degree in Business Administration from the top business school in Brazil, EAESP-FGV, with post-graduate studies specializing in small to medium sized company management from the same university. Diego worked for 5 years as the General Manager of WR Engenharia, a construction company in Minas Gerais. He was responsible for B2B sales, human resources, finance, quality control and project management. He is experienced in selecting, hiring and managing teams having hired over 400 employees during his tenure. Under his management the company grew from 20 to more than 200 employees between 2004 and 2008, multiplying 12 times its revenues in the period. He is a qualified ISO 9001 consultant and auditor, with experience in the creation of a complete quality system.

At the beginning of 2009 he joined the VivaReal Network to spearhead its operations in Brazil as the country director. Diego has experience living abroad, studying and working in Argentina and Spain. He is fluent in Portuguese, Spanish and English.
