Dan Grosh’s Summary

With more than a decade of executive search expertise, Dan remains a trusted advisor to past clients and placements often contributing to decisions around strategy as well as ongoing Board and executive leadership selection and development. Dan is a Partner & Managing Director at Vantage, leading the Mid-Market Practice initiative for the firm as well. He has built numerous senior management teams, placing executives in functions spanning leadership, engineering, sales, marketing, operations and quality.

Prior to joining Vantage, Dan was a partner with Nosal Partners, a mid-size global executive search firm where he led the Emerging Growth Companies Practice and also focused on Fortune 1000 senior-level assignments in the advanced technology, consumer and professional services industries. Previously, Dan served as President of Grosh Associates for several years - a highly-successful boutique placement firm that he founded and began his career with Northwestern Mutual Life.

Dan holds an MBA from Golden Gate University and a BA in Communications from the University of Minnesota. He resides in the East Bay with his wife, and enjoys golf, tennis, skiing, travel, and music. Dan also holds leadership roles within a number of local charity and community groups, as well as professional associations.

Dan Grosh’s Specialties:

"C", VP and Division GM / Director level search for private & public Technology and Service companies. Work closely with top tier venture and private equity firms.
