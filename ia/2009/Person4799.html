AgorA Entertainment Partners’s Summary

In ancient Greece the term “Agora” was used to describe a particular public assembly forum for all ideas and markets. Agora Entertainment Partners LP embraces the philosophy of merging all media and their markets by way of cutting edge video integration. We do this with trendsetting HD technology, multi-market savvy, zestful and creative conceptual development, and strong client relations. Our palette ranges from feature film and television, to commercials and industrials, music videos, and documentaries, to web development and graphic design. Agora Entertainment not only aims to be the premier assembly place for art and commerce, but to become the leading media management group in the Southwest United States and beyond.

AGORA INTERNSHIP PROGRAM

Agora Entertainment is pleased to announce we are now accepting submissions for the 2009 Fall Internship Program. For more information please visit our Internship Program Page at agoraentertainment.com. You can also send your application and resume to andrea@agoraentertainment.com


AGORA ENTERTAINMENT TO DISTRIBUTE FILMS

Agora Entertainment is proud to announce the fall launch of their film distribution division. Headed up by seasoned professionals, including Brad Keller, Agora Entertainment will focus on self distribution as well as buying outstanding independent projects. Utilizing the latest in technology and inventive marketing, Agora will insure the financial success of it's projects.


BIG BLACK AND AGORA ENTERTAINMENT JOIN FORCES FOR A NEW REALITY SHOW!

Dallas Video and Film Production, Agora Entertainment and Big Black (from the MTV hit reality show ROB and BIG) have joined forces to produce the reality TV concept "Big Tyme Talent." The show will feature Big Black as a budding talent agent. "Big Tyme Talent" will introduce Stevey T as his sidekick and business partner, along with BAM from Rob and Big. The show will showcase the trio as they discover talent from around the great southwest.
