Brett Morrison’s Summary

I am responsible for the IT budget, project portfolio, project management, and general IT service to five business units at ConocoPhillips with a total annual information systems budget responsibility of approximately $2.5 million. Additionally, I regularly work on information systems integration projects such as recently ensuring that all tax and accounting systems needs were met on a $33 billion acquisition.

In a prior position with ConocoPhillips, I was responsible for change control and functional integration testing on a 30K+ user global SAP installation. We received Efficient and Effective (highest SOx audit rating) for three years in a row on change control system and processes.

Prior to ConocoPhillips, I worked at Williams Energy Marketing and Trading where I was responsible for quantitative financial model documentation and the code revision control system. I have also worked in Network Operations Accounting at two telecommunications companies (McLeod USA and WorldCom).

I worked for two years as a business development volunteer with the Peace Corps from '97-'99 in Rostov-on-Don, Russia where I taught business and English and wrote grants for Rostov State University.

My activities include being involved in the United Way Campaign in Bartlesville and serving as the president of the Tulsa Zelenograd Association (TZA) which manages Tulsa's sister city relationship with Zeleongrad Russia. TZA is a part of the Tulsa Global Alliance.
