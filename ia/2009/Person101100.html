Jack McCullough’s Summary

Jack is the business development officer for the CCR LLP, the largest independent CPA and business advisory firm in New England. He was previously the business development officer for New England's leading outsourced CFO group, where he led the company to be recognized by Inc. Magazine as one of the fastest growing companies in the country. Prior to his career in business development, he worked as a permanent or interim Chief Financial officer for more than ten companies, where has raised over $200 million in private equity from several investors. A certified public accountant, Jack spent several years at the multi-national accounting firm KPMG Peat Marwick, where his client base consisted largely of early-stage technology companies. Prior to his career in finance, Jack was a professional boxer. However, after he lost all his first seven fights by knock-out, he decided to pursue a safer career path and went into accounting.

Jack is frequently contacted by members of the media for his insights into business and strategic issues, and has been quoted by the Wall Street Journal, CFO Magazine, Treasury and Risk Management, the Boston Business Journal, and Business Finance Magazine. He has also been interviewed by Bloomberg, CNN, and Fox Business Network.
