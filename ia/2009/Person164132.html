Mike DeBever’s Summary

I have extensive experience in selling Information Technology (IT) solutions of hardware, software and consulting services to the Commercial Sector, Federal, Department of Defense (DOD) and State and Local Government. My approach has been to sell high in the organization, CEO, CIO and CFO levels as well as to other key stake holders. Recognized for exceeding all performance quotas, growing new and existing accounts.

My software background extends to selling middle ware applications, system and network management, security, ERP, CRM, EAI and engineering.

My hardware experience goes from the desktop to data center and the networks in between. Consulting goes from implementation of hardware and software, ITIL methodology, network plan and design and training.

* Tactical and Strategic Sales Planning and Execution
