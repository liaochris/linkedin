Chris Malpass’s Summary

After starting to study Information Technology and Computer Science during high school through the Governor's School for Math and Science and Microsoft Academy, a drive to excel in the field was firmly in place. After graduation I proceeded to Old Dominion University, enrolled full-time, while developing my IT skills at the City of Norfolk Service and Support Department as well as enhancing my web development skills with Old Dominion University's OCCS department.

In the summer of 2006 I participated in a month long student exchange program to The College of Physical Education in Beijing, China as well as Yeungnam University in Daegu, Korea. The experience impacted me so much that In early 2007 I returned to Seoul, Korea to study for a full semester. After learning Korean, Political Science, and International Business I returned to the US a changed person!

Late 2007 brought on new challenges as I moved on and have since been employed as a web developer, systems analyst, travel writer, and IT support for a web-based business located in Williamsburg, Virginia.

I plan to attend Virginia Tech in the fall of 2009 in pursuit of a Master's of Information Technology degree. With my excellent experience and business-mindedness, I hope to develop personal ventures as well as contribute to the continual growth of the business I currently support.
