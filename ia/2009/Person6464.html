Alastair Campbell’s Summary

Principal shareholder of UK Data Ltd which operates www.ukdata.com and www.formacompany.com

ukdata.com sells financial information comprising of Company Credit Reports, the full range of Companies House data including filed company accounts, director and shareholder data and value added services such as the provision of County Court Judgement (CCJ) data.

The business also provides similar information for European and other international jurisdictions, although this forms a smaller part of the business.

UK Data Ltd was incorporated in 1992 and is the UK’s longest established credit reporting provider operating globally and exclusively via the internet.

formacompany.com offers company formations in 32 jurisdictions which are managed from our offices in the UK, Ireland and Spain.
