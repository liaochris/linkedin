Jan Zoet’s Summary

Since 1999 managing director at the Rotterdamse Schouwburg, the Rotterdam City Theatre, that presents more than 500 performances of theatre, opera, musictheatre, dance and interdisciplinary works every year. De Rotterdamse Schouwburg also presents The Internationale Keuze van de Rotterdamse Schouwburg, the international theatre festival of Rotterdam. Embedded in the theatre is Productiehuis Rotterdam, that produces performances and projects of international and local performing artists.
