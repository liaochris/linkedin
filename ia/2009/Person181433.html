Patricia Biswanger’s Summary

I am finally back to work, in a part-time position as Assistant County Solicitor for Delaware County. Back in February 2006 I had a spinal cord stroke (med mal; long story!) that left my upper arms paralyzed and my lower arms weakened by diminished nerve conductivity, as well as tendonitis in my wrists and neuropathy in both hands. I can't drive, carry much, reach forward, or lift anything, and if I type for extended periods I am in trouble, but I am still getting around! I really glad to get back to work. I love practicing law and I really miss it!

As for what I did, and will do again someday: employment and commercial litigator with practice before all tribunals, including state and federal courts, EEOC, PHRC, etc.; local counsel practice before all courts in Southeastern Pennsylvania
