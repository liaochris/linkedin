Patrick Cotting’s Summary

Marketing professional, having started in the consumer goods industry, then getting experience in the medical area, the financial services industry and in one of the biggest media groups in Europe. Consulting multinational companies from all industries on developing and implementing international marketing campaigns, using TV, internet, outdoor and mobile communication channels. Seeks to combine experience from best practice with evidence from research. Lecturer and faculty member at different Universities for executive training in the marketing and communications field. Negotiation level in english, german and french.

Patrick Cotting’s Specialties:

Development and implementation of integrated Marketing & Advertising strategies, mainly for the European markets.
Evaluating, negotiation and implementation of Sponsorships and Partnerships; from the perspective of the sponsor and the sponsee.
Marketing & Communication Strategy Assessments.
Development of Marketing Research & social empirical research projects.
