Luis Joel Lopez’s Summary

I currently am the Managing Partner at Juxtance Ltd. Co. My primary duties at this company are working with the web design, development, marketing & S.E.O. I also deal with public relations through the Radio Nation link and Independent Artists.

I have worked with all of the Adobe Creative Suite Program and also keep myself up to date with updates and new features in new versions of said software.

I started working with Web Sites, Web Graphic, and Design since 2000

I worked with marketing at a Travel Agency on which I utilized graphic design to create brochures, postcards, etc.

I have worked with non-profit organizations such as: TICH (Taller Intercultural Hispanoamericano) and PYHOT (Paint Your Heart Out) with works in graphic design and motion graphics.

I mostly work with freelancing, but among clients these include: Supermercados USA (Newspaper distributed to supermarket business owners for both Hispanic and American), D.Diego Auto Credit (Car dealership in Puerto Rico), and Tampa Bay Storms.

I was also involved in an event in the Art Institute of Tampa named Camotion (Computer Art Motion Show) on which I was featured as an artist, but also I helped with the creation of motion graphics to the event.

My work ethic is simple: "There is always a way."
