  • Founder-VP Business Development

    The CPA & Legal Network, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Commercial Real Estate industry)

    February 2008 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    The CPA & Legal Network, Inc. (www.thecpaandlegalnetwork.com) markets the engineering process known as the Cost Segregation Study. This process, fully recognized by the IRS as a legitimate and powerful way to return significant tax benefits to the for profit, commercial property owner (individual, corporate) has been "on the books" for 20 years but seldom used outside of the "The Big 4" and only on behalf of only their highest paying clients. In fact, out of the estimated 81MM commercial buildings in the U.S. in private hands, only an estimated 20,000 cost segregation studies were performed in 2006. This means that there are approximately $400B in untapped tax benefits, which could be in the hands of the property owners. The CPA & Legal Network, Inc, has created a seamless method to free up these tax benefits to interested commercial property owners at a fraction of the cost that the "Big 4" would charge for the exact same service. Our engineers are all "Big 4" trained experts.
