Lucas Allen Buick’s Summary

Lucas Allen Buick is co-founder and strategic partner at Synthetic Infatuation, a creative factory based in Minneapolis and Chicago, USA.

Lucas Allen Buick’s Specialties:

I build brands that are smart, clever, and make people think. I challenge my clients to be better than their competitors. I expect my clients to be as fearless as we are when developing a brand or campaign. Safe is always the wrong direction, because when nobody hates you, nobody loves you either. If a company can't clearly state why they are better or different than others in their industry, how are customers going to spread the message. I believe conversation is important to a brand.

Lucas Allen Buick’s Honors:

My studio and I have won our share of awards. We have done lectures and workshops to share our thoughts with others. We have been published in the annuals, blogs, and eye candy books. However, the truth is none of that matters. If what we do doesn't produce results for our clients, we have failed, no matter how many of our colleagues say otherwise. So I have stopped entering work in shows -- I have no use for gold lions, pencils, or light bulbs. Give them to someone who needs to justify their work to someone other than the client.
