Carie Strahorn’s Summary

Carie joined Boly:Welch in 2005, bringing over 30 years of leadership experience within business, on volunteer boards and in community service organizations to the company. She jump-started her career at locally owned ESCO Corporation in Human Resources where she worked five years before moving to Boise Cascade Paper, logging 15 years in HR, Marketing and Sales.

Carie's current role balances HR recruitment, outsourcing and consulting with oversight for company messaging and community partnerships. Strahorn drives client and candidate outreach programs and develops strategic and tactical direction for growth across all six business units.

Deep connections within the Portland Metro community bring strong knowledge of the HR discipline and a network of connections with local HR professionals. Searches have included Directors of HR, Human Resource Managers, Benefit Specialists, Director of Compensation, Recruiters, HRIS professionals, Employee Relations and more.

Representative clients include Bullivant Houser Bailey, SP Newsprint, Morrison Child Services, Portland Development Commission, Artists Repertory Theater, Jacobs Engineering, Business Valuation Resources, Lightspeed Technologies,.
