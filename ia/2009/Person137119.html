Larry Arnold’s Summary

Larry Arnold is manager of Garmin International’s Consumer Technical Publications (CTP), with 21 members in three departments under Arnold.

An award-winning writer, Arnold’s works include the user manual for Gateway’s laptop Solo 5100 which features his skills both as a writer and as a hand model for the digital photographs used in the manual.

Larry Arnold’s Specialties:

Globalization, technical writing, project management.
