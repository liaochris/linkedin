Brian Tonner’s Summary

Brian Tonner is a full time graduate student in the Master of Accountancy Program at Western Carolina University in Cullowhee, NC. Mr. Tonner holds a Bachelor of Arts degree from Kean University in Union, NJ. His professional experience began with Macy*s Department stores where he worked as a Civil Recovery and Restitution Manager in New York, NY. Mr. Tonner also worked for Steve & Barry’s University Sportswear as a Regional Loss Prevention Auditor for the southeast region. He has several years of experience working as a police officer and police training officer for the Town of Fletcher and the City of Asheville, NC. Mr. Tonner is a member of the North Carolina Police Benevolent Association and a student member of the North Carolina Association of Certified Public Accountants.
Brian Tonner lives in Buncombe County North Carolina with his wife and three children. He enjoys homeschooling his children and flying small airplanes with his private pilot’s license. Mr. Tonner is a Reserve Sheriff’s Deputy for the Buncombe County Sheriff’s Office. He is also an active member of his church community. In addition to serving on the parish finance council, he is an active member of the Knights of Columbus.
Mr. Tonner is looking forward to CPA certification and aspires to work in public accounting and eventually specialize in fraud investigations and forensic accounting.
