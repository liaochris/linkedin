Keith D. Taylor’s Summary

CFO for Exigen Capital, a Private Equity Fund focused on enterprise software investments, with eight portfolio companies launched to-date.
Also CFO and Board Member for the San Francisco Shakespeare Festival (www.sfshakes.org), and Board Member of ThunderRoad Investments, LLC (private high tech investment fund).

Just prior, I was Interim CFO for Magellan Navigation, Inc., $700M GPS Mfg (while a Partner at Tatum Partners, LLC), and CFO for Encover, Inc., (Service Contract Revenue Management Software solutions, funded by Baird, Doll Capital, and Sigma Partners).

I spent 5 years as VP Finance, Division CFO for Adecco (global staffing/HR Consulting company - $20B worldwide), almost 10 years at Apple Computer, and started my career at Ernst & Young LLP, earning my CPA and CISA designations. I have my MBA for San Jose State University, and my BSBA from Cal Poly State University, San Luis Obispo.

As consultant to top-tier VC firms , I have assisted as CFO consultant for Topio (Sequoia Capital/Sigma Partners software technology company, acquired by Network Appliance). Page Systems (FiberOptic Mfg Company funded by Bear Gold and later acquired by HonHai/Taiwan), BrassRing Inc., (Internet and Software company funded by Washington Post, Chicago Tribune, Gannett News, and Accel Partners, later acquired by Kanexa), and CareerCentral (Internet ecruitment company, funded by Questmark, Softbank, Arthur Rock, TCV, acquired by Spencer Stuart).

I have had extensive experience in all aspects of financial operations, accounting and business development, leading teams from two to 150+, pre-IPO start-up funding (assisting startups raise over $100M), mergers and acquisitions, and all facets of corporate operations.
