Jim McConnell’s Summary

Feel free to connect to me (Jim McConnell) at newwesttv@comcast.net. I am looking for networking partners and anyone interested in video production. I'm also willing to help other organizations implement video into new media technology.

My firm, New West Media specializes in video and digital media production for Fortune 500 companies as well as mom-and-pop shops. We use a virtual agency approach that allows us to scale projects easily while also delivering professional video production values. Our newest service is called vNews, an online product that allows our clients to produce their own professional video at a fraction of what it would normally cost them. We enjoy our work and believe we should have fun while we push down the digital frontier.

In the past I have been a director of video services for a large international insurance brokerage firm where I created and developed their video production division, providing content for training, communications, and marketing for a worldwide audience.

Jim McConnell’s Specialties:
