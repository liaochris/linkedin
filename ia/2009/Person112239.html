Joshua Garrett’s Summary

I am 23 living in Brooklyn, NY . I am currently the Creative Director of the “The Ultimate High School Sports Guide”, a Florida based magazine. I received a Bachelors of Science in Graphic Design & Studio Art with a minor in Art history from Florida State University’s Department of Fine Arts.
I began as a traditional artist working mainly in graphite, charcoal and pen. I knew I wasn’t going to be the next Salvador Dali or M.C. Escher but I knew I wanted to do something creative. Graphic Design was that opportunity.
I strive to reach perfection in everything I do. I will do whatever it takes to get a job done. I love to learn and I am not afraid to try my hand at something new.

Joshua Garrett’s Specialties:

page layout, branding & identity, advertising, photography, and illustrative work, complete working knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Dreamweaver, Flash, CSS, HTML, MS office, Mac and Windows
