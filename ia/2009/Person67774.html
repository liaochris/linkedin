Adrian Ed Lee’s Summary

I am a freelance Houdini VFX TD and artist, and have worked for both commercial, game and film houses.

I started off in the industry as a render wrangler and data i/o for Cinesite, which gave me the opportunity to see how production works from the inside, and get used to working in in a darkened room for long periods of time.

I've also worked as a concierge in a 5* hotels, curated my own art gallery and worked for way too many call centres, bars and burger joints.

The best thing about working in the FX for the film/games/tv industry has let me do things I've wanted to do since I was a little boy. Talk to monsters, blow things up and make it all look mighty pretty.

Adrian Ed Lee’s Specialties:

Production experience of Houdini. Experience of Maya and XSI. Experience of Nuke, Shake and Final Cut. Some light experience of scripting (bash). Digital filming experience including location and green screen and setup for post DFX work as well as set surveying.
