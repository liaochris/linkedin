Mark Williams’s Summary

If you are looking for some help and direction in how to get the most out of Social Networking – speak to me.

A UK based Social Media Expert and Trainer I have trained hundreds of people throughout the UK this year alone. My workshops are a practical and non technical explanation of the best way to get this amazing free tool to work for you and your business.

Too many users are not making full use of online networking and it is my mission to educate and inspire them to do something about it!
LinkedIn offers you so much more than you think;
►Market your business – reach an audience of millions
►Recruitment – find the best passive job seekers & research their credentials
►Market intelligence – research your customers and competitors
►Access to great contacts – find out who your connections know and get introduced.
►Get answers from industry experts & demonstrate your knowledge through ‘Answers’
►Build great connections to help you in future job searches.

I could go on but maybe you should come to one of my workshops for that!

Being a LinkedIn user with a basic profile, a few connections and little activity on the site is such a wasted opportunity – it’s like going to a networking meeting and sitting in the corner to avoid speaking to anyone!
Social networking is revolutionizing the way we conduct business – don’t get left behind!
