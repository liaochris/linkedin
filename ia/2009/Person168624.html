Mike Alexander’s Summary

With over twenty years experience in technology, systems and product development, I have undertaken varied senior roles incorporating consultancy, solution design, system architecture design, software development, project and programme management, technical infrastructure management, people management, technical pre-sales support, account management, product management and marketing. All of these roles have combined my broad technical expertise and analytical approach with a commercial and customer-orientated focus to deliver innovative, high-quality and profitable results.

As a person I am creative, self-motivated, goal-driven, tenacious, committed and highly versatile. I possess sound commercial acumen, strategic and tactical management skills, and am a natural leader, able to develop and motivate a team to achieve the necessary results. I have a strong commitment to personal development and actively seek opportunities to refine and augment existing skills and experience. Prepared to travel as required, I have operated extensively within the UK, mainland Europe, North America and Asia.

With well-developed communication skills, I have the competence and experience to communicate effectively with people at all levels, both technical and non-technical. I am an energetic, confident and articulate speaker and have successfully presented in varied high-profile situations, including international industry events and board-level presentations. These skills have been invaluable in establishing and managing effective working relationships with colleagues, business partners, clients and other third parties. During my career I have built up a broad network of contacts across all of the sectors in which I’ve been involved.

Mike Alexander’s Specialties:

Overall, my key strengths are technical insight, the ability to understand and articulate business requirements and, combining both of these, architecting and delivering innovative solutions. In particular, I have a passion for understanding existing and emerging technologies and utilising them to their full potential and maximum commercial benefit.
