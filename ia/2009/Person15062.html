Andy Botha’s Summary

A commercially driven CFO with a proven track record in improving company performance and increasing shareholder value, in both growing and turn-around businesses. Strength in building highly responsive finance teams and introducing controls to deliver business objectives. Broader leadership and operational skills gained as a COO heading up a 600‐strong operations function.

Effective as key member of an executive team developing & implementing business strategy, with ten years senior management experience in both UK and international businesses.

Andy Botha’s Specialties:

Experience of business restructure, change management, acquisitions, Sarbanes Oxley and SAS 70, Six Sigma process improvement, in addition to financial performance management, finance systems implementation, working capital management, treasury, acquisitions, and integration.

Most recently worked in online and offline retail, leisure, travel & tourism and e‐gaming.
