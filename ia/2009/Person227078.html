Susan Manghelli, CKD/CBD’s Summary

Susan Manghelli, CKD/CBD draws on over 15 years of professional experience in residential remodeling with a special emphasis in kitchen and bath design. As a Certified Kitchen & Bath designer, Sue provides clients with the latest trends and innovations to best meet the needs of their projects.

A Business graduate from the California State University at Los Angeles, Sue added another three years of professional Interior Design education from Learning Tree University followed by five more years specializing in Kitchen & Bath Design; she is certified by the National Kitchen and Bath Association in both kitchens and baths, and served as both the Treasurer and Vice President/programs for the Southern California Chapter of the NKBA.
