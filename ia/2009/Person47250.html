CleanEdison LLC’s Summary

CleanEdison(www.cleanedison.com) is a highly effective, award-winning and competitively priced provider of green education services and sustainability consulting and helps industry professionals obtain necessary skills and accreditation, including Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED).

CleanEdison offers LEED exam preparation courses, energy audit and solar training and sustainability consulting nationwide. CleanEdison is committed to education and training and offers comprehensive resources to its students, including detailed study guides, online practice exams and help desk access.

CleanEdison LLC’s Honors:

Recently, CleanEdison received 2009 Green Excellence Seal for Green Education award for its training courses for Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) and Building Performance Institute (BPI) certification.

CleanEdison offers:

* 90% LEED AP pass rate: The certification is unquestionably desirable, but the exam is very difficult, with a pass rate of 34% for first time takers.

* No-fail guarantee*: Students who do not pass the LEED or BPI exam initially may retake the LEED or BPI preparation course for free*.

* Small class size: Maximum class size is 20 people while our nearest competitor may have 40 individuals.

* Valued services: CleanEdison charges less per course than its competition.

Completion of CleanEdison courses and training satisfies Continuing Education Credit (CEU) requirements for major industry associations, including AIA, BOMA and USGBC.
