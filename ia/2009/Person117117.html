John Garrett’s Summary

Over the past decade I've been a student of business, living and working on both coasts, in northern and southern states, having offices in NJ, NY, FL, CA and AL.

My first love is sales. In a world that complains about what's fair, its a profession that allows you to control your destiny.

As I excelled in sales and built a network of colleagues, I was given opportunities to lead, create and implement. These became my second, third and fourth loves.

All these "loves" created a passion (and a profession) for building small businesses. I tell people all the time that I don't work for a living, I retired at 28.

John Garrett’s Specialties:

Extensive experience in B2C and B2B sales, CRM implementation, Leadership & Sales Training as well as One on One Mentoring.

I have a passion for taking existing companies and making them a bit more productive, a bit more profitable and a whole lot more fun to work at.
