Diane Webster’s Summary

Having worked within the real estate industry as an entrepreneur on both sides of the Atlantic for the last 20 + years. My expertise, if I might say so myself is the ability to recognize a growing need in the marketplace and develop solutions to fill that need. From an International Relocation company based out of London that offered an American standard of service for relocating exeuctives and their familes, to a Lead Generation service for the mortgage banking industry in the States, back in the 90's. My current venture for the past 9 years is a 24/7 online, unique and alternative method of Real Estate Brokerage that offers tremendous cash savings for consumers, operating in Washington and Oregon.

Today's real estate industry is going through a tsunami of change. We have always been a little ahead of the crowd with our innovations in service. The future looks bright for us with our new buyer Self Rep Kit and other new services. Check us out at http://www.america-at-home.com and see for yourself how much you can save on a real estate transaction. Look for our new e-book, out in January 2009 on how to represent yourself on a residential real estate transaction and keep the commission! called "The Real Estate Revolution". We know that once consumers fully understand how to conduct their own transaction and learn the secrets on legally getting the full commission they will never again feel stuck in terms of having to need a real estate agent. We love Full Service Agents, we offer that service for those who need it also. The difference here is "Having" the need to use one and "Wanting" to use one. We offer our clients and customers a choice.
