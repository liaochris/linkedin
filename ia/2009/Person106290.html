Jay Palace’s Summary

"Leadership Training Expert and Execution-Focused Business Development Executive Available for Consulting Engagements"

Via my training company, Group Experiential Learning, I help organizations:

- Develop and focus high performance teams
- Implement effective leadership development experiences
- Create high impact client relationship events

Via my consulting practice, Jay M. Palace and Associates, I help organizations with marketing strategy and business development initiatives:

Engagements typically start with the development or refinement of a marketing strategy for entry or distribution within a market segment.

Clients then leverage a world-class network, broad industry experience and proven closing skills to super charge business development and partnership results, as wells as grow and train business development teams. I have a passion for and truly enjoy helping portfolio start-ups gain traction, establish charter customers and create distribution relationships.

Core beliefs:
- Speed to market is a competitive advantage.
- Identifying ideal customers and partners is critical to success.
- Ideas without pre-formulated implementation plans close slowly and often fail to deliver ROI.
- Great execution requires collaboration across marketing, sales, and especially, engineering organizations.

Areas of focus:
- Early Stage Companies
- Clean Tech & New Technologies
- Strategic Planning
- Building & Training Business Development, Sales & Marketing Teams

Jay Palace’s Specialties:

Sales, alliance development, strategic business development in the start-up environment, contract negotiations, communication skills, leadership and management training, sports and sponsorship marketing
