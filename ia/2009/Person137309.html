Laszlo Szalvay (laszlo@danube.com)’s Summary

For companies who invest in complex software development and are committed to agile development, ScrumWorks Pro is the only commercial project management solution that is designed exclusively for Scrum lifecycle management.

ScrumWorks Pro streamlines agile software development by increasing project transparency and visibility and reducing project management overhead. It empowers agile development teams to actively manage release cycles and sprints resulting in higher product quality, lower project risk and ultimately lower development cost.

ScrumWorks Pro is a collaborative system that allows teams, product owners, and stakeholders to manage the Scrum lifecycle at the program, product, release, and sprint levels. ScrumWorks Pro offers role-based project tracking, reporting and product requirements traceability features that support the ScrumMaster, Scrum Team, the Stakeholder, and Product Owner by encouraging Scrum best practices.
