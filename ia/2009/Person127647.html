Kathleen Townsend’s Summary

After graduating from Stanford University in 2007 with a B.S. degree in Product Design I have found a home in Online Marketing.

At a glance, product design and marketing may seem dissimilar but in practice have much in common. At their cores, both professions are about understanding people, their needs, tastes, and desires. Obtaining this understanding, I believe, is the key to both designing and selling products.

So far my education in product design has brought me to:

* Gomi Style, an online television about green DIY

* Hive7, a social networking application developer

* Ubisoft, one of the largest game developers in the world.
