Daniel Talavera’s Summary

Graphic Design is not an art, but a communication tool. My task as a designer isn't to create the prettiest design/layout, but the best and professional creative solution for your communication needs. This may vary from a premium corporate identity, to graphic design for brochures, catalogues and print work, or to some online exposure for your company through a web site, landing pages, and web banners.

The unique mix of attitude, creativity and hard work, all focused to provide good quality design, both, printed and digital is my commitment.
