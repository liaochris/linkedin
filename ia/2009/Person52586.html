Dan Agostino’s Summary

I help my clients to utilize digital video technologies to enable new businesses around content distribution. My experience began with learning from traditional media and futuristic thinkers, and has moved into the newest frontiers of digital media delivery.

Dan Agostino’s Specialties:

Digital video workflows (capture, conform, compress, deliver, and archive), video content quality assurance, analyzing audience perception to content value, radio announcing, videographer, video editor, sound recording/editing, website editor, server administration, network administration, team conflict management, and writing for business / technical / marketing audiences and translating between them, HTML editing, and digital document workflows.
