Brent Campbell’s Summary

As the world of technology continues to expand into every aspect of day-to-day life, I have found myself both intrigued by the range of possible technology advantages available to myself and companies I am in contact with on a regular basis, and compelled to delve further into the industries that support these technologies.

With over 25 years of sales experience, it was therefore only natural that I expand my skill sets into the realm of personal and business technology. I chose to focus on Audio and Video technology in the public sector, and I expanded into the area of networking and communications technology as these are ever more frequently intertwined in the business community today.

I have become expert in the utilization and implementation of these technologies in the business environment. This includes, as an example, familiarity in POS systems, video display and distributive audio for the restaurant industry, network deployment and business machine implementation in the business office environment, telephony and security applications in companies as diverse as retail and government research, and I have implemented systems in each of these cases.

I am characterized first and foremost by my insatiable desire to understand how and why things work, and how this can be utilized in the real world. I am DRIVEN to learn, and I crave the opportunity to know more. This can be vitally useful to companies who need me to explore new possibilities or understand how existing paradigms work.
