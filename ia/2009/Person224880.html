Steve Rogers’s Summary

Summary

Steve is a sales professional who has represented the IBM Corporation in various marketing, sales, and management positions since 1984. Currently Steve serves as an IBM Client Executive working out of the IBM.com sales center. His territory includes many of the public sector (Gov't & Education) accounts across Florida. Steve possesses strong executive communication skills and has worked extensively with Public Sector Agency executives and senior management, as well as Legislative members and staff.

Steve works out of his home office in Tallahassee, FL and can be reached via e-mail at srogers@us.ibm.com or via cell phone at 850-510-3572.

Steve Rogers’s Specialties:

Selling IT products and services that allow public sector organizations (Gov't & Education) to be more effective and efficient as they deliver value to their members, constituents, and clients.
