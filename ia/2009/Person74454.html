Eric King’s Summary

Co-founder of boutique investment bank focused on middle market companies at a key inflection point in their life cycle. We provide leadership capital and transaction advisory services to companies with $3MM - $30MM in revenues.

Co-founder of online lead aggregator for mortgage and education markets. Built company to over $6MM first year sales and sold in less than two years with over 3x return on invested equity.
