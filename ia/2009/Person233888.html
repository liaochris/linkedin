Timothée Néron-Bancel’s Summary

After a quick stint in Washington D.C. to work with two organizations in the field of Renewable Energy, I moved back upstate to the Capital District to work as the Operations Manager for the North American Board of Certified Energy Practitioners. In this position since January 2009, I am now involved in all of NABCEP's program and activities and will continue to be as we grow the organization in Clifton Park, NY. As a young professional, driven and ambitious, with knowledge of business, renewable technologies, policy and sustainability, NABCEP has tapped into my strengths, fostered my professional development and given me great leadership, project management and non-profit experience.

My immediate goal right now is working within NABCEP to develop all the necessary programs and certifications to meet the demand for qualified renewable energy practitioners and ensure that NABCEP can continue to provide value to it's diverse group of stakeholders.

In the future, I plan on pursuing a joint MBA/MEM at a top-tier school when necessary and a return to Europe for personal and professional reasons.
