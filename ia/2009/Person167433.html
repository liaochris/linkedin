Mo Hussein’s Summary

Involved in various businesses since the age of 15 and a moderately successful entrepreneur.

As can be discerned from my employment history; I have always found it difficult working for others. I realized early on in life that the only solution for me would be to work for myself.

In every position I've ever taken on at a company I received distinction for innovative methods and for providing unorthodox solutions that were efficient, productive and successful. This led to recognition from my superiors, fast-tracked promotions and more importantly; respect from my colleagues..

Out of sheer boredom and greater ambition I would move on in hopes of finding something a little more challenging. I am now fortunate enough to work for myself and look forward to adding to my success and establishing and expanding the Sixth Sense empire.
