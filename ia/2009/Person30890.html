Bonnie Burton’s Summary

Seasoned editor, book author, journalist and blogging expert with a rich background in print and online media available for freelance projects, contract assignments or media commentary. My expertise addresses the growing market of women between the ages of 18-30 and teens, as well as transmedia storytelling and viral marketing using blogs, forums and social networking.

I founded one of the first women's pop culture web sites and blogs called Grrl.com and have been featured in Entertainment Weekly, Tech TV, CNN Headline News, E! Network, Online Journalism Review, Print, Fast Company, BBC, and Yahoo! Underground.

Bonnie Burton’s Specialties:

teen marketing, kid marketing, viral marketing, brand content development, online community building, blog marketing, branding and identity, entertainment journalism, online culture journalism, transmedia storytelling
