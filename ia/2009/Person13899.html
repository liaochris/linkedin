André Clements’s Summary

I enjoy collaborative work aimed at improving the way processes and systems work with specific focus on online media, tracking and process automation in various contexts, both corporate and independently.

Coming from a graphic/information design background I have migrated towards a more technical and development oriented role that is off-set, I hope :-), by communication and people skills developed through various instruction and training facilitation roles.

My preferred development medium is Appache MySQL & PHP (AMP) on which I can make the WordPress Semantic publishing platform do amazing things - from being a self-contained highly customized online catalog & shopping cart and highly structured directory engine to full blown Content Management System (CMS).

My studies were originally focused on through-the-line advertising but I chose to avoid the advertising/agency industry in the early years of my career due to ethical, moral and specifically aesthetic reasons. I have recently found that the measure-ability of ROI, the focus on accountability and scope for dynamic personalization and relevance afforded by Internet marketing has lured me back into the marketing world - in fact has me rather excited about what is becoming possible, even necessary - for sustainable competitive business.
