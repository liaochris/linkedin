Mike Nevin’s Summary

I am keen to contact individuals looking to diagnose the health and effectiveness of existing or potential strategic relationships.

Successful candidates will become part of a large database of alliance best practice knowledge.

I run my own alliance research consultancy but in addition I am the founding Chairman of the Association of Strategic Alliance Professionals in Europe.

I am actively looking for individuals who have access to (or represent) blue chip European multi national corporations who are looking for strategic relationships to develop business in Europe.

I have over 150 such companies looking for potential partners now. These companies are in a range of sectors eg (IT Hardware, IT Software, Systems Integration, Pharmaceutical and Biotech, Financial Services, Manufacturing, Household Goods) and many others.

Mike Nevin’s Specialties:

Strategic relationship management expertise, brokering high value alliances and partnerships for clients with strict confidentiality.
