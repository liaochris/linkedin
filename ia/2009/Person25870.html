Ben Burkhart’s Summary

Industrial Designer with 8 years experience in product design and development for the manufacturing industry. Expertise in industrial design and product development, brand strategy, advanced surface modeling, design for manufacturing, rapid visualization prototyping, innovation and conceptual design work. Experienced with Alias Studiotools, Pro Engineer, and Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator.
