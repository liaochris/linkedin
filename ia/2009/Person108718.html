Jeff Sauer’s Summary

I created my first web site in 8th grade and haven't looked back since. In the early 2000's I created a web site for a business associate that I thought looked really cool. He said to me "sure, it looks cool, but how do I get people to go to it?"

I didn't know what to tell him, so I started researching. Once again, I haven't stopped since. Originally, I thought that the only way to get people to your site was through Organic SEO. While this is a great method for acquiring traffic, it's only a piece of the puzzle. PPC is another huge piece of the puzzle, as well as other methods beyond search.

Once I mastered the process of acquiring traffic to web sites, the next question was asked of my by the owners of Three Deep "Sure, you can get traffic to web sites, but what does that traffic do?"

Once again, I was stumped. I felt like I needed to prove something once again, so I started expanding my reading regimen outside of SEO/PPC and started learning about all facets of online marketing. Web Analytics, Online Reputation Management, E-Commerce, Competitive Analysis, Online Surveys, Offline Marketing, Email Marketing, Lead Generation, are all niches that I track on a daily basis.

In between all of these readings, I have been able to carve out some time to put my learning's into action. I have achieved the #1 search ranking for several highly searched terms, and many of my sites still sit at their #1 throne. I will manage over $1 million in online advertising spend in 2009. I serve as e-commerce advisor to one of the biggest companies in the world and I handle web analytics for over 30 companies.
