Filip Coulianos’s Experience

  • Environment artist / Level designer

    Decadence Mod

    (Computer Games industry)

    2007 — October 2009 (2 years)

During this period I had the opportunity to follow a product in all stages from the very first idea to putting the finishing touches and release of a game.

    (Privately Held; Computer Games industry)

  • Level designer

    Project 25

    (Computer Games industry)

    February 2009 — May 2009 (4 months)

    Project 25 is a singleplayer mod I developed for Half-Life 2 with about 30 minutes of gameplay. Since I did the project by myself I was solely responsible for project planning, level design, game writing, voice actor coaching and level beautification. The project proved very successful with over 2000 downloads and was featured in the Belgian magazine PCgameplay.

    February 2008 — February 2008 (1 month)

    During Swedish Game Awards 2008 I had an opportunity to contribute with a lecture regarding making mods to the Source engine. The lecture served as an introduction to the tools provided, as well as a list of pros and cons when chosing the source engine.

  • Editor in chief

    Leveldesign.nu

    (Computer Games industry)

    August 2005 — February 2008 (2 years 7 months)
