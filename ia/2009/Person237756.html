Bernard Metzger’s Summary

Bernard Metzger is a communication and data management specialist in the travel and tourism sectors with over twenty five years of experience. His past experience includes consultant for the IFC - International Finance Corporation (World Bank Group), a directorship and senior management position in a world-renown travel guide publishing company, chairman of a non-governmental organization and several pioneering realizations in online content and reservation systems development.

Bernard Metzger’s Specialties:

Data distribution in the travel and tourism sectors, Data Structuring, Online Marketing, Online Distribution, Conceptualize Marketing Strategies
