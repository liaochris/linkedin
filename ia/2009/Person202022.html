Mary Agnes Antonopoulos’s Summary

Dynamic, beautiful freelance writer for twenty-two years, currently specializing in advertising and web copy. I have a unique gift for culling out the core of a client’s message and building a creative dialogue around that theme.

Web copy must grab the reader within the first four sentences, so I write each page as if it were a thoroughbred breaking out of the gate.

I have written professionally for CEO’s and fortune 500 companies all over the country and have penned extensive short bio’s, resumes and CV’s. I’ve also completed thousands of research projects. I specialize in PowerPoint presentations and spreadsheets. I am currently writing my first full-length book.
