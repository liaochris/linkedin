Paul Leacock’s Summary

Having worked in the recruitment industry for nearly a decade, I have experienced an increasing level of dissatisfaction with the lack of innovation and genuine commitment to making a difference in client businesses.

Blacksmith was created as a result of this dissatisfaction and our vision is to support our clients to not only attract & select the best talent, but also to integrate that talent into their business creating an environment of success and performance.
