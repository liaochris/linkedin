Scott Beebe’s Summary

Scott has fourteen years of experience in software development, implementation, and customization including 10 years of JD Edwards OneWorld/EnterpriseOne Development.

Experience includes software design and development, database design, and customizing and troubleshooting of J.D. Edwards OneWorld software. Additional J.D. Edwards experience includes post implementation support, system design and analysis, enhancements, conversions, modifications, retrofits, implementation, user support and training, technical troubleshooting, delivered portal components, developed JAVA components to plug into the portal, and systems management.

· Scott has managed and developed many enhancements and complete custom solutions for 30+ clients. This includes financial and general report writing, applications, and interfaces with 3rd party applications. Working with the clients he has the experience to create efficient and user friendly solutions to fulfill the clients requirements.

· Scott has been involved in many clients that included data conversion activities for legacy data during new installs and retrofitting activities that are normally part of an upgrade.

· Scott has implemented major modifications to standard and custom modules, including automating many steps in manufacturing processes involving sales orders and shipping. He has designed and performed highly complicated modifications to E1 applications including how the core C Business functions operate.

· He has published 25 white papers related to E1 development that have been published by Quest Q&A and JDETips. He is a regular presenter at the Quest Collaborate conferences.

See full resume and experience at http://www.mywwsoft.com/
