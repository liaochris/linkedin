Scott Spicer’s Summary

My academic background is in anthropology and global health. I have been involved in non-profit and community work for about 7 years, and have had a wide range of experience in education, community development, leadership, youth development and alcohol prevention. My primary goals are to work with and for communities to empower them to be strong, safe and healthy.

Scott Spicer’s Specialties:

My most recent professional experience has been in working with youth and community leaders to advocate for alcohol policies to prevent and reduce underage drinking, youth access to alcohol and community alcohol problems. I have been doing this under the frameworks of youth development, environmental prevention, community organizing and evidence based prevention models.
