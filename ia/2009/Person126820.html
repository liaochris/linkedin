Karl Varley’s Summary

In my current position I am privileged to be exposed to world-class research and best practice thinking influenced by global leaders in strategic thinking: Porter, Drucker, Collins, Ries, Hamel, Prahalad etc – This has culminated in a unique planning methodology that emphasises the creation of strategic plans with a practical “execution” focus.

I can demonstrate extensive experience in business & strategy roles across a range of industry sectors. Simplifying a Strategic Plan so that is easily articulated to all divisions and people within those teams is important to me. To be able to put a strategic plan a single piece of paper has proved to be an incredible driver for business to achieve greater success. As Jack Welch emphasis having a great Vision is only 1% of the success strategy the other 99% is having all your people aligned and executing upon that vision.

An important part of my role does requires that I am informed of market conditions and understanding opportunities that are available in the current economy. I also have a close working relationship with local Members of Parliament for the National Party which at times has proved insightful.

I am confident that my knowledge of best practice in delivering successful outcomes in strategy and planning processes is as good as you will see. As mentioned earlier I am exposed to world’s best practice everyday and use those tools to effectively drive execution with my own team.
