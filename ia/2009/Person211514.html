Scott Godes’s Summary

I am a lawyer specializing in insurance recovery and advice for corporate policyholders. When insurance companies refuse to cover claims, I assist my clients in recovering the insurance that they are due. I also advise clients regarding the purchase and renewal of insurance policies and coverage. I have tried and settled cases for clients ranging from Fortune 5 through small privately held companies, and have helped policyholders recover over $900 million for insurance claims. I have true hands-on litigation experience, from managing e-discovery through serving as trial counsel. My insurance coverage practice spans a wide range of issues, such as data breaches; property damage; crime claims; business interruption claims; directors and officers, securities, and derivatives claims; malpractice claims; computer data, hardware, software, and online services; product liability claims; sexual abuse claims; asbestos; environmental damage and CERCLA; flood and hurricane claims; alcohol advertising injury claims; class action claims; and advising on and negotiating insurance renewals.
