It was an incredible chance and also a kind of a miracle to see how a small idea can turn into a breakthrough. I learned amazing stories about people and places, about world. It was undoubtedly the greatest miracle of all to discover myself while discovering the world.
So... here I am:

Oana Turcu’s Experience

  • TV Producer

    TVR

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    January 2005 — Present (4 years 11 months)

    Since January 1, 2005, I’m a full-time employee at Romanian National Television (TVR), although my collaboration with TVR started in October 1998.
    My greatest professional achievement so far was to produce the "GOLDEN STAG" music festival, the second largest music festival in Europe, after Eurovision. As the Contest Unit Coordinator of the 40th anniversary of the "Golden Stag", also assistant project manager of the event and coordinator of the social program and promotional activities for competitors, I coordinated and supervised around 300 people: more than 230 artists on stage, 33 guides, 3 reporting teams of 3 people each, 1 live band of 7 soloists, 5 presenters, 3 editors, 1 press officer and a technical team of around 20 people. It was a great responsibility, but also a great satisfaction to be part of such event. I loved every single second spent in organizing that event, even though sometimes it felt like purgatory. It took professionalism, good communication skills and nevertheless a great sense of humor to cope with and to succeed!
    Between 2004 and 2008 I had the chance to get in touch with a lot of managers, agents, artists and most of celebrities who performed on stage at Golden Stag music festival: Joe Cocker, Pink, Natalie Imbruglia, Thomas Anders, Nino D'Angelo.
    As part of my job, I coordinated the Romanian Television's stand at MIDEM 2008 and I had the chance to meet and talk to celebrities like James Blunt, Paul McGuinness and Peter Gabriel.
    Last, but not least, I'm honored to have met Mr. Ray Neapolitan and
    Mrs. Lisa Johnson, two role models professionals, two of the greatest people in the world!
