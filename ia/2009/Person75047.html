erika rand’s Summary

Erika Rand specializes in filmmaking, branding, and photography for green and social profit businesses.

Clients and collaborators include: Alliance for Climate Education, Andante Records, Apple, Banana Republic, Biochar Energy, Brazilian Girls, Clothing of the American Mind, Deepak Chopra, Dreamworks Pictures, Elie Wiesel, Evolver Magazine, evo.com, Free Range Studios, Guerilla News Network, Guayaki, Hairspray (on Broadway), Harmony Festival, Ian Inaba, Jack Morton Worldwide, Jane Fonda, JCrew, Jim Fournier, Joel Grey, John Trudell, Jonathan Cramer, Martha Stewart Living, Metropolitan Museum of Art, Michael Christian, Moby, MTV, National Organization of Women, Pangea Day, Participant Productions, Pushpin Group, Rene Risque & the Art Lovers, Restoration Hardware, Rounder Records, Snowshow, St. Martin’s Press, Scholastic Books, SmartMoney, Tiffany & Co, Town & Country, Tribeca Film Festival, Wicked (on Broadway), and Woody Allen.

NGO / Community work includes: Alliance for a New Humanity, Amnesty International, American Anthropology Association, Casa Guatemala, Color of Change, CONAI (Costa Rican Indigenous Rights), Guerilla News Network, Hubert H. Humphrey Institute, Indigenous Peoples Task Force, International Special Olympics, Konojel Junam, Loaves & Fishes, The National Organization of Women, Pedro Albizu Campos High School, the Puerto Rican Cultural Center, Video the Vote, Women's Earth Alliance. Recipient of the Rose Rees Peace Award.
