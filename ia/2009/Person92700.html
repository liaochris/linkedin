    (Public Company; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1999 — 2002 (3 years)

    Semaphore Partners (formerly NOVO), a wholly-owned interactive marketing division of Publicis Groupe SA (NYSE: PUB).

Along with the company’s founder, I was integral to successfully negotiating all strategic, operational, and financial terms of the acquisition agreement. Bcom3 subsequently merged with Publicis Groupe in 2002.
