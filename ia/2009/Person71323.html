Eleonora Faraone’s Experience

  • Senior Test Analyst/Test lead

    Goldman Sachs JBWere

    (Privately Held; Financial Services industry)

    October 2008 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    As the lead tester on the EDGE project, my primary responsibilities included creating test plans, developing and managing test cases based on requirements, planning of test cycles and execution of test cases, reporting and management of defects and the reporting of testing progress to the project manager, lead architect and the program test manager. The project followed the Agile methodolody and the test management tool used was Quality Centre.

    The EDGE project involved ensuring that equity orders could be routed to either the direct market access system, the strategy order system or the trader order pad depending on the content of the message. This main objective was achieved by creating a system in a "plug & play" type structure which was determined by client configuration. As well as being able to route orders for various external clients, we were able to develop other features such as checking client trading status and limits, validating short sell orders, validating symbols and security ID's, assigning Warrant and Exchange Traded Fund orders to different exchanges, assigning discretionary accounts to client orders. These messages were based on Financial Information eXchange (FIX) Protocol. The testing conducted utilsed the FIX automation tool Verifix and using this, an extensive automation suite was developed to assist with the testing required. An achievement of the automation test suite structure was the ability to easily group test cases into feature groups which aligned to the configuration of clients. This enabled a signficantly reduced amount of effort in test planning and preparation and an increased level of reuse across the automated test scenarios.

    Other tasks undertaken in my role were to provide administrative services and training of Quality Centre to the IT Securities team, providing guidance for automation strategies across other projects as well for the use of various automation tools including Verifix and QuickTestPro.

This involved enabling the team to develop and execute test scenarios covering customer creation, agreement creation, product creation, billing, transporting of product XML’s to the downstream systems and liaising with the downstream test teams to ensure that advertisements were properly displayed online. Other responsibilities included ensuring that all change requests were developed into test cases and subsequently executed as well as detailed reporting of test execution and defect impact on a daily basis.
