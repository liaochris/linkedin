Jane Andersen’s Summary

Jane and her husband, Al, founded Compass Consulting Team in 2009. After a successful career in corporate america, managing her own consulting company, traveling around North America as a senior consultant and account executive for a global training company, and achieving great success, she went back to her passion and desire to coach, mentor and train people and businesses in the "Art of Being Remarkable". She and her husband, Al, are helping people and businesses become Purple Cows. Compass Consulting Team workshops are designed experientially. Each workshop quickly immerses the participant in an engaging activity in which they can reflect on, process and choose for themselves, what changes to make in their behaviors, attitudes, or approach to their work. "It's all about doing "great work", and minimizing the "good work". "People get caught up in thinking just because it's good, that it will get noticed, talked about, and sales will increase. Not so, that work is expected, not great! You have to find the "free prize" in your product or service that will be remarked about and transform you and your business into the Great Work Zone. It's knowing that you have to go to the edges. This is what we coach people and businesses on, when they come to our Purple Cow Training." Besides being a business executive, speaker, program developer, facilitator, and writer, Jane hosts, with her husband, Al, a "live" internet radio show, "THE PURPLE COWch with Jane and Al" every Monday morning at 8:00 a.m. Phoenix time, on the Modavox Variety Channel. They bring business owners, executives, best-selling authors, and professionals together on their show each week to talk about the issues of owning, running, and excelling in business today. They hope to get companies mooooooving in the right direction, and be the catalyst for this generation of Purple Cows.

Jane Andersen’s Specialties:

Facilitating Workshops: Making Purple Cows, Developing Tribal Leadership, Helping Others Do GREAT WORK, Igniting Team Power. Hosting Pinkedin Job Networking Parties. Managing an Internet Radio Show, "Career Launch with Jane and Al".
