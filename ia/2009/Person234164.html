Tim Stahl’s Summary

My experience can be boiled down to one thing. It's a quote that I like to keep handy. (and I keep a lot of them around) "If you don't have time to do it right, when will you have time to do it again?" I'm pretty sure it was John Wooden, but my memory isn't as sharp as it used to be. You'd have to ask Lara, my very first design teacher. She could tell you. I believe that we all have something to do and there is no Command Z. Get it right, then move on. Even if it's wrong, do your best and be prepared to fail, that's the only way you will get anywhere.

That brings me to a few more quotes...I'll share.

"To live a creative life, we must first lose our fear of being wrong." – Joseph Clinton Pearce

"Success isn't a result of spontaneous combustion. You must first set yourself on fire." – Arnold Glasow

"Education is an admirable thing, but it is well to remember from time to time that nothing worth knowing can be taught." – Oscar Wilde

"Advertising is designed by creative people who don't appreciate business, approved by business people who don't appreciate creative, and aimed at ordinary people who don't appreciate advertising." – Jonathan Harries

I like quotes because they act as a compass provided by people who had an intelligent moment. That's right, a moment.

I won't pretend to have all the answers, just an open mind.
