Ellen DePasquale - The Software Revitalist™

Let me bring my 20 years of computer experience into your office and breathe new life into your software. From my work with clients to software reviews in national publications to my speaking engagements addressing both small business and IT professionals, I have been preaching the need for more efficient computing and easy report generation.

I am currently certified in Peachtree by Sage and SageCRM. I am a Microsoft Partner specializing in their end-user software, such as MS Office. I have previously been Timeslips by Sage certified and a QuickBooks ProAdvisor.

My articles have been published in PC Magazine, Crain's New York Business, Worth Magazine, Accounting Technology, Self-Employed Professional, and Inc. Magazine.

Most recently I moderated a session at the Sage Software reseller conference, and I have also addressed small business audiences from New York City to San Diego. And yes, all of the topics were based on how to get the most from your software.
