Greg Pitstick’s Summary

I spent 16 years as a professional consultant helping companies to make more money by combining the latest technology with leading business practices. After working with fantastic companies from across several continents, I retired in the beginning of 2004. Never being one to slow down, in 2005 I built a construction company. In 2008, I sold the company to my business partner.

In 2007 I formed LGM3 with another retired consultant - Bill Brown. LGM3 is a firm dedicated to helping business professionals become the sought after experts by leveraging internet technology, social networking and the best marketing. We are currently working on “Digital Domination - Turning Professionals Into Sought After Experts”.

Digital Domination is a coordinated internet strategy for business professionals such as consultants, real estate agents and insurance agents. Using Digital Domination, a business professional can become THE sought after expert through blogging, web sites, social networking, articles & online/offline media.


Using our strategies almost any professional can quickly become known as an expert, reach new clients and exponentially grow their business.

Our research and development into Digital Domination will be published in a book later in 2009.
