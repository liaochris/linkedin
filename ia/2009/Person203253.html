Ronald Urlus’s Summary

Mediation activities, specialized in labour conlicts/relations since 1992 in and outside (expatriates) the Netherlands. New alternative working methods introduced opening up monopoly/competing with traditional law practice/approach (no cure no pay, fixed price/result approach, unilateral assignment for either employer or employee). Proven track record of 3000+ succesfull mediations. 93% succes rate with mediation. Office consist of: 2 partners, 5 mediators, law practice with 1 legal counsellor and 1 back office. Strategic goal: continue growth rate for coming 5-10 years, building professional relationships internationally, exploration and opening up international position (globally) for result oriented mediation working methods in complex legal environments.

Ronald Urlus’s Specialties:

Exceptional combined experience, specialization in working relationships and the solving of conflicts within these. Conflict mediation in labour relations. Broad involvement in Projectmanagement and expertise to execute (collective) dismissal/redundancy projects. SURPLUS distinguishes itself by working on either a no cure no pay, or a fixed price project delivery basis. Organisation consists of a select group of consultants/business experienced professionals.
