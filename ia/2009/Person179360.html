Oriol Miralbell’s Summary

Oriol Miralbell has been involved in tourism since 1973, working in the industry and also in the government.
He has been coordinator of the network of tourism boards of catalonia from 1989 until 2005.
During the same period hi was in charge for the tourism information services and the e-tourism projects of the Catalan Government, designing also and managing the official tyourist information portal of catalonia.
He has been participating in several European, aswell as Spanish e-tourism projects, like TIM (Tourism Information and Marketing) or TURINTER.
He has been teaching since 1999 at the EUTDH at the Universitat Autonoma de Barcelona, and has teached also in several Master Programs at CETT at the Universitat de Barcelona, EUTDH and European University of Barcelona.
He's lecturer at the Universitat Oberta de catalunya in tne Tourism Program and at the Informacion Science program.
