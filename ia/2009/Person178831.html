Jason Smith’s Experience

  • President, Panel Division

    Vision Critical

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Market Research industry)

    November 2004 — Present (4 years 5 months)

Key products are Sparq, a platform to manage customer advisory panels, and Fusion, a complimentary set of rich media and 3D visualization tools. Responsible for global sales, marketing, partnership, services and product development. Clients include Staples, Bose, Gap, Time Warner, Bloomingdales, NFL, MLB, NBA, NHL, Bell, BusinessWeek, TiVo, Conde Nast, RIM Blackberry.   • VP Marketing (currently serving on the Board)

The company is heavily tied to the Internet. Continue to serve as an active Director.

Tertiary roles include acquisition diligence and broadband Internet gaming strategy. Reported directly to President, Worldwide Studios. EA is the world's largest interactive entertainment software company with revenues of $3B+ .

  • Co-founder, Director, EVP Internet Services, EVP Marketing

    Columbus Group Communications

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    April 1995 — June 1999 (4 years 3 months)

Primary roles were EVP Internet Services, responsible for all customer facing Web site consulting and development projects and EVP Marketing, responsible for defining the brand and marketing across all channels. Clients included Kraft, Westcoast Energy, TELUS, Canfor, Citizens Bank and MTS. Peak size of 70 people in Vancouver, Toronto & Seattle, acquired by TELUS (Canada's 2nd largest telco) in early 2001.
