Brian Pate’s Summary

I have been in the real estate business since 1993 as an agent, instructor, manager, trainer and coach. I am currently the manager of the Youngsville Office at Fonville Morisey, a Long and Foster Company in Raleigh, North Carolina. We have over 600 agents locally (over 16,000 nationally) and offices throughout the Raleigh, Durham and Chapel Hill area.

If you are looking for a career in real estate, please contact me today at 919-229-2010.

In addition, I am the owner of Brian Pate Entertainment, a company providing entertainment for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events along with event management.

Brian Pate’s Specialties:

My expertise is in the real estate training field. I have been a pre-license, broker and continuing education instructor, certified by the North Carolina Real Estate Commission since 1996.
