Van Carpenter’s Summary

My initial career experience has been in Corporate communications, marketing communications and public relations for all sized companies from corporations down to small start-up companies. Have held positions as manager and director of communication, global marketing manager, public relations account manager and counselor, manager of marketing communications and community relations, creative director and strategic alliance manager. Accredited as a public relations counselor, APR.

Most recently I have established Biz Doctor, a small business advice and consulting firm, working with small and medium business owners to increase their performance and profits. I advise, educate and market clients in 7 key areas of personal leadership, planning, people and team management, promotion (marketing), prospecting (sales), performance (systems) and PROFIT (financial management).

Also I have extensive experience in corporate training and education. Have developed and taught courses in interpersonal and group communications, leadership development, marketing communications creative idea generation and innovation. Have served as an adjunct professor at several local universities teaching subjects in advertising, public relations, script and copy writing and video production. My aim is to provide companies with "edutainment" so that learning can be fun and profitable.
