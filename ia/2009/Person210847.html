Sascha Bosetzky’s Summary

I am a cross-culturalist by trade and work as a professional cross-cultural trainer, coach and consultant. Having studied sociology at the Free University of Berlin I got my M.A. there, after which I underwent an one-year vocational training as cross-cultural trainer and coach.

Together with my business partner Kerstin Schaber we have founded Mindset India in Berlin and in Mumbai. We are specialized on Indo-German business relations and have gathered extensive experiences over the last years concerning the opportunities and challenges of various Indo-German cooperations.

Our services are high-quality HR development programs designed to increase the productivity and efficiency of international companies. We take cross-cultural training and coaching very serious -- from a business perspective and a scientific perspective. A cross-cultural training is not just about learning the habits and norms that are relevant in a foreign country. Culture influences the way we think, the way we solve problems, the way we communicate, socialize, express appreciation and dislike, mold the corporate culture of our company, structure the company and reward our employees.

We take these factors into account when we consult Indian or German companies that are planning to acquire companies in the respective other country, that want to merge with a company in the other country or want to develop new business areas there. We love to prepare managers and employees before they are exposed to the other culture. If the business relationship has gone sour due to corporate cultures that were not streamlined beforehand it takes an extraordinary effort to make change happen. We could give you a lot of examples...

We can do a lot for you years after you merged with or acquired a company in India or Germany, as well. It is never too late to improve the quality of the cooperation or communication and it is definitely worth the effort.
