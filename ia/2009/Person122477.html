Jeff Schox’s Summary

I founded the Schox Patent Group to build patent portfolios for startup ventures that enable them to build value and attract funding. Over the last five years, I have represented over 50 startup ventures. Many of these clients have attracted venture funding from top firms, such as Kleiner Perkins, Greylock, Flagship, and DFJ.

Drawing on my experience of over ten years in patent law and my degrees in both mechanical and electrical engineering, I have filed over 200 patent applications in a broad range of cutting-edge technologies. As a Consulting Professor, I teach patent law in both the engineering school and the law school at Stanford University.

Jeff Schox’s Specialties:

patent strategy, patent prosecution, vehicle systems, medical devices, wireless communications, computer software, clean technologies
