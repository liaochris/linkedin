Kristine Schachinger’s Summary

A little about myself - Currently residing in Las Vegas, I have eleven years experience in the creation, development, implementation & maintenance of websites in all areas including government, academia, entertainment & ecommerce with a focus on usability, architecture, human factors & W3C & 508/WCAG accessibility compliance. I have designed & implemented websites for companies & people such as AOL, Department of Homeland Security, Reba McEntire & Ulla Popken.

My most recent responsibilities include the full life cycle design & implementation of the website; FindAnyFloor.com. Responsibilities include, but were not limited to the site look & feel, site architecture, usability, * accessibility & standards compliance, (X)HTML/CSS implementations, code review & standards documentation.

I was also responsible for the management of outside resources, third party implementations, project requirements documentation, business development solutions & development of all search engine optimization (SEO) strategies on & off-site.

Outside of FindAnyFloor.com, my core competencies include, but are not limited to the following technologies: site architecture, web usability, WCAG accessibility, human factors engineering, UI/UX, website design, code implementation & search engine optimization (SEO). I also have experience with the following programming languages (X)HTML, CSS, Javascript, .Net & ASP as well as all the essentials of dynamic site creation & full life cycle developement

Extras: My (x)html/design work won a previous employer Webby Award Recognition for setting standards on the internet & have had the good fortune to have been trained in accessibility by Jim Thatcher.

Note: I am a PhD candidate at UNLV in Las Vegas
