Gopala Krishnan’s Summary

GK is an entrepreneur, digital lifestyle visionary and futurist with 20 years of professional experience in running start up & early stage venture funded companies in India and China.

He has founded companies with an International footprint that have a base in China and/or India.

He speaks often in various forums on business issues relevant to the China-India corridor. He has raised Venture Capital from renowned global VCs 5 times in his career and has seen one company through a successful exit by sale to a global media giant. He has deep experience in Interactive Media, Interactive Marketing, Mobile/wireless services and several aspects of related technologies.

In these contexts, he has often been invited to speak at national and International conferences and also by leading global business schools including INSEAD. His work is the subject of a case study at Stanford University and The National University of Singapore.

As a domain expert on his virtual platform HanIn, GK is involved with companies in the area of Indian local language technologies, digital radio/video and digital advertising.

He was founder and CEO of Mobile2win in Shanghai and Mumbai. GK lived in China for 4 years. Mobile2win China was sold in 2006 to the Walt Disney Company. Since March 2007, after attracting new leadership to Mobile2win, he helped ensure a smooth succession through June 2008. Since then, he has setup a virtual platform for leading domain experts to help companies find the right partners in India and China. HanIn. http://www.haninglobal.com.

Before this, GK founded India's first online careers company which he sold to eVentures India, a fund promoted by Softbank, Newscorp and Mittals of Ispat. He then cofounded and headed technology as CTO and then business operations as COO for a well known Internet company in India.
