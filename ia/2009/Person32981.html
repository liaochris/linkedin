Bret Zahn’s Summary

I am a results-driven senior business and technology professional with 19 years of international multidisciplinary experience in commercial and government/DoD industries, including 12 years in executive management advising BOD's, C-level staff, investors, and analysts. I have direct experience with start-ups, LBO's, M&A's, IPO's, turnarounds and company integrations along with Angel, Venture Capital, and Private Equity fundraising.

I possess a comprehensive breadth of business and technology leadership experience in zero revenue start-ups to multi-billion dollar international enterprises managing P&L, technical sales, new and strategic business development, product marketing, software and hardware development, along with manufacturing/assembly and supply chain operations encompassing planning, procurement, logistics, and repair depot operations.

• Executed a $120M cash acquisition of a competitor company supported by Francisco Partners Private Equity and directed the successful integration of the people, processes, and systems in 2008

• Executed a $1.6B stock-for-stock merger supported by financial advisers Credit Suisse First Boston and Morgan Stanley and directed the successful integration of the people, processes, and systems in 2004

• Delivered the corporate vision to over 100 investors and analysts as a member of an executive road show team resulting in a $120M IPO underwritten by Credit Suisse First Boston in 2001

• Executed a $500M LBO with the support of Bain Capital Investors and Citicorp Venture Capital in 2000

• Directed activities as a founding member of a high technology microelectronics assembly and test services start-up firm in 1998
