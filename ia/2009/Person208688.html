Sandra Linville-Thomas’s Summary

Senior writer/editor with more than 20 years experience in writing, editing and desktop publishing for a broad range of communications projects targeted to a wide variety of external and internal audiences.

Demonstrated success in writing and editing newsletters, magazines, press releases, annual reports, catalog sell copy, marketing collateral material, manuals, speeches, Web site content, brochures, white papers, consumer information content, feature articles, and advertising copy such as tag lines and advertisement copy for both print and electronic format. Experience in interpreting specialized material, including technical, from a wide variety of sources and translating complex information into clear, concise communications content—from emotional calls to action to simple, yet creative, delivery of information. Wrote in a range of voices and styles for varied projects with diverse audiences. Proven mastery in research, fact checking, analysis, planning, and project management; and identifying and executing communication strategies to best accomplish organizational goals.

Extensive experience with both Macintosh and PC computers; Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint; QuarkXPress; and InDesign CS3; and familiarity with HTML, Photoshop and Illustrator. Has extensive knowledge of the AP Stylebook.

Comfortable in a deadline-driven environment, excellent active listener and a strong collaborative team player. Proven ability to partner with art directors, designers and account executives to ensure that copy tone and style are consistent with visual tone and style. Relentless and versatile learner with keen enthusiasm for studying Internet trends and technologies including learning more about communicating in the era of Web 2.0. Capable of monitoring leading communications and demographic trends to track the best way to communicate—keeping up with trends in usability and online marketing, and traditional communications outlets.
