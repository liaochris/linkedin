Maniesha "Shay" Lowe shaylowe@gmail.com’s Summary

I choose to build my business the old fashion way through lifelong relationships, “word of mouth”, great customer service, and individuals that know they can refer me to their friends and family with the knowledge they will be well cared for. I believe, value is most important, whether it is me, valuing my clients, what they need, want or can afford, and their market knowledge and my clients, valuing me, my time, expertise, honesty, and advice.
Real Estate is individual specific right down to the neighborhood you want to know about. When asked “How’s the market?” I must determine what information you are looking for, is it the national level, state level or neighborhood level you want to know about, because it makes a big difference.
My goals are to become a resource, when you need a plumber, yard guy, mechanic, anything you or anyone you know may need or want, I am the phone call to get that information. My business will grow through personal relationships, genuine care for others, and time.
