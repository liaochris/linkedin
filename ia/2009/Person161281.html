Michael Fury’s Summary

Materials and process development manager with a broad background in introducing new production technologies and equipment into mainstream electronics manufacturing. Specific expertise areas include CMP slurries, pads, equipment and metrology; post-CMP cleaning chemistries, brushes, equipment and metrology; wet process chemicals, equipment and metrology for general wafer cleaning, photoresist stripping and post-etch residue removal; spin-on low-k dielectric materials, processes and equipment; CVD and plasma etch equipment.

Goals include new business development around nascent materials and process technologies including categories now identified as "nanotechnology" (which those of us in semiconductors have been doing for years already...). Novel applications of these technologies hold a host of opportunities for innovation and wealth creation.

Michael Fury’s Specialties:

Semiconductor process development; electronic materials & process development; intellectual property portfolio management
