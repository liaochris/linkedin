Yvonne Roberts’s Summary

A Procurement Professional with leanings towards Strategy in Supply Chain whilst being part of IBM's Integrated Supply Chain Division more specifically Global Procurement & Services Group. Currently in the enviable position of having a ring side view to trends in the global "hyper dynamic" economy by virtue of my position as Global Program Manager for Services Market Intelligence.
