Manuel Vergara Jr.’s Summary

I am a financial manager / accountant with over ten years experience in business partnering and reporting systems. My strengths include financial reporting and analysis, book close as well as systems process development and implementation for the SAP and JDEdwards ERP environment.

As a lead finance person, I succeeded by guiding, leading and teaching others and by being a team player that works well with others. My experience has been in a wide range of financial services including Sales and Marketing support, Real Estate, Treasury, A/R, A/P, Fixed Assets, Corporate Internal/External reporting as well as SOX controls.
