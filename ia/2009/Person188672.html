Phil Lundberg’s Summary

Innovative, results oriented manager with expertise in organization and business development. Successfully grown businesses internationally and in the United States, in such diverse industries as high tech, education, and healthcare. Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, insight, analysis, and creative problem solving to optimize outcomes, build effective teams and help individuals grow. Proven expertise in managing start up and turn-around situations, marketing and business development, organizational development, training, education, and international operations.

Phil Lundberg’s Specialties:

My passion is helping individuals, teams, and organizations achieve high performance. Throughout my career I have done that through building strong relationships internally and with customers, using those relationships to build agreement about the direction that should be pursued, and then help create energy in achieving the agreed upon goals. I am fascinated by the challenges of creating high performing teams in organizations that work in multiple cultures.
