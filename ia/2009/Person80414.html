Andrea Fox’s Experience

  • Presenter and Newsreader

    Juice 107.2 Radio

    (Media Production industry)

    October 2004 — Present (4 years 10 months)

    Having progressed from weekend shows to Drivetime 3-7pm, I now currently co-present the Breakfast Show.
    I was on Drive with Guy and Andrea for 6 months before we were moved to Breakfast Show on June 2007.
    As well as joint responsibility for show prep, content and booking guests I also conduct interviews with target artists for our station such as Duffy, James Morrison and Girls Aloud. I edit these interviews using Adobe Audition, though I can use other programs.
    I am responsible for five Breakfast news bulletins between 7am and 11am a role I enjoy and have learnt on the job.
    At 11am I present solo for an hour until midday. I present solo when my co-host is off as well as covering other shows on the station on my own when required.
