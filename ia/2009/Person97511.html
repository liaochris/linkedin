Ian Mackenzie’s Summary

I am an articulate and confident but personable business developer and manager for who understanding the bigger picture is important. I am very committed and although I'm generally about the long run and building the relationships and strategies to get there, I'm also results driven and understand the less glamorous side of reality and survival! I'd describe myself as a progressive / creative thinker but I believe in the old 'fashioned ethics' of hard work, integrity and respect.

I am also a risk taker and although I have (happily, purposefully!) moved back in to the ranks of employment from being an employer, think this shows through in joining what is for me a new industry. I hope to apply my business experience & UK market experience (especially B2B services from Poland) to an industry that although is based on fun seems to have aspirations to include some more more serious aspects of business. from passionate hobbyists. I like to buck trends so the plan is to contribute just as much to this industry as someone with an established track record in it and the passionate hobbyists :-)
