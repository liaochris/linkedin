Irshad Reyaz’s Summary

Well, I'm Irshad. Or, call me Irsh. Philosophy apart, I've been working in the Web Applications Development for over 3 years now. At present I'm working as the Innovation Manager at SkoolsOnline Technologies.

SkoolsOnline Technologies is a Web 2.0 software services company provides online branding and web 2.0 applications development services for start-ups and creating solutions for internet education.

Basically, We develop tailor-made web applications viz. portals, e-commerce system, social networking system and also planning to provide SAAS products along with the online branding and social media marketing of these products.

We are based out of New Delhi with other offices at Los Angeles, Maryland and Elk Grove. Our offshore development centre is based in New Delhi. In fact, it is our main production centre and the offices in US are overseeing our Business Development.

A new company, Landshark Inc is on the way to be launched. I will definitely update once it gets up and running.
