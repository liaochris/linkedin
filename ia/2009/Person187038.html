Hello,

What the chronology above does not indicate is that I am an entrepreneur at heart, and over the years, I have been able to create a unique mix of technical and business skills. This background, my extensive network of professional friends throughout the ecosystem and finally my passion for consumer electronics have always helped me stay on the cutting edge of the industry to the benefit of my employers and clients.

I joined Telegent Systems as Director of Business Development in 2007. Telegent is a semiconductor company at the heart of mobile TV providing single-chip solutions enabling worldwide mobile TV in handsets, PMDs and consumer electronics. The company is incredibly successful in Asia, Latin America, the Middle East and Africa with 10 million TV-enabled handsets sold in less than a year.

As Director of Marketing and Business development at PortalPlayer and NVIDIA, I worked on various assignments ranging from product management and marketing strategy for product specific platforms to leading and directing the work of marketing, business development, marketing communications, design and development resources across global regions.

I kicked off my career in DSP firmware engineering and management at Digital Video Systems and C-Cube. DVS, my first startup, introduced digital video to China and went public to perform the best IPO of 1996. C-Cube pioneered the digital video revolution by introducing the first successful MPEG decoders for the DVD-player industry.

I was born in Brittany, France – I consider myself almost a native Californian after living in the Bay Area for the 15 years. Email me at phil@philippemora.net or invite me to link with you.
