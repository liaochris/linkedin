Steve Ruszkai, AIA, LEED AP’s Summary

- I have 18 years of experience working in the architectural profession with experience as project manager and project architect. I have experience in retail, industrial, daycare, medical, educational (from Kindergarten to University level), institutional, residential, military design.
- I am a licensed architect in Maine. I have been licensed in Indiana. I keep current with my NCARB resigstration and can easily obtain my license in any other state as required. I am an active member in my local chapter of the AIA. I am also a LEED accredited professional since 2001. I am an associate member and helped found the Maine USGBC Chapter with Alan Kuniholm at PDT Architects in Portland, Maine.
- I have done post-graduate studies at the Boston Architectural Center (BAC). I have a Master of Architecture degree, Bachelor of Architecture, and Baschelor of Science degree in Environmental Design.
- I have experience as the office IT manager, and managed CAD standards. I have experience AutoCAD, Architectural Desktop, 3D Studio Max and VIZ, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat Distiller, and Premeire. I also have experience in website design. Please visit my professional website for my resume, both web based and electronic downloadable portfolio at http://home.roadrunner.com/~steve (no www prefix).

Steve Ruszkai, AIA, LEED AP’s Specialties:

I have particular interest and a high level of experience in 3D rendering (computer, hand, and hybrid), architectural photography, timber framing, environmental design. I also have years of experience in specification writing, and building code and material technology research.
