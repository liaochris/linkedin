Ryan Prazen’s Summary

Mr. Prazen has over 5 years of experience in REO sales, BPO services, and all aspects of running a real estate brokerage. Current and past clients include Wells Fargo, Accredited, Countrywide, Premiere Asset Services (PAS), Ocwen Loan Services, SingleSource Property Solutions,
Integrated Asset Services (IAS), Wilshire Credit Corporation (WCC), Keystone Asset Management (KAM), Goodman Dean, ETC REO, New Vista Asset Management, Phoenix Asset Management, AssetLink, Coldwell Banker REO, Chrisley Asset Management, National Default Servicing, Old Republic, IndyMac, USRES, EMC, GMAC, American Home Mortgage, Option One, American Home Mortgage, PMH, Downey, and many others.

SDREOS is capable of handling 200+ new REO Property Listings and 1000+ new BPOs per month. Currently a staff of 30 licensed agents and 7 licensed brokers work with Mr. Prazen. SDREOS has access to over 30 licensed service providers including contractors, landscapers, plumbers, electricians, locksmiths, debris removal companies, house cleaning services, trash out companies, real estate & eviction attorney services, auction companies, and many others. SDREOS & their service providers have a comprehensive list of services which include: Same-day BPOs, Comparative Price Analysis, Occupancy Verification, Loss Mitigation, Cash For Keys, Re-keying, Property Management, Trash Out, Written Repair Estimates, Utility Turn-on and off, Property Preservation, Digital before/after Photos & Videos, Weekly Activity Reports, Comprehensive Marketing to sell quickly, Escrow Coordination, and Closing Services.

Mr. Prazen’s background in banking, mortgage, investments and technology is founded on the best expertise in the industry. Mr. Prazen has managed bank branch operations and sales for Washington Mutual and North Island Credit Union. Mr. Prazen has mortgage origination experience with numerous Prime & Alt-A mortgage products with One Mortgage Network, Inc.
