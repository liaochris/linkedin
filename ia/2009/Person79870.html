Florentijn Diepeveen’s Summary

Hello, my name is Florentijn Diepeveen and I am an Art director/Creative. My interest for design, art and digital media have taken me around the world to meet and work with some of the most interesting and leading figures in the industry.

Throughout my career I have build up experience in design & concept creation, art directed various video/photo shoots and created global websites using the latest technologies. My strategic and managerial skills enable me to work as an essential part of any creative team. Provocation in the sense of surprising clients and consumers by push the expected somewhere new is in my nature. Achieving this with a team or on my own is my passion.

Florentijn Diepeveen’s Specialties:

Projects realised for national and international clients, including: Diesel, The Beatles (Apple Corps), Sony, Citroën, Playstation, Red Bull, Ballantine's, Channel 4, The Victorian & Albert Museum, Master foods, Virgin, Nike, Peroni, TNT, Premsela foundation Holland, Dutch Police force, KPN Telecom, Marlies Dekkers, Van Den Ende Musical productions and many more...
