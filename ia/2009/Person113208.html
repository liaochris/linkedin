Jim McNiel’s Summary

I bring fresh ideas and perspective to each new challenge with a track record of proven success in creating products, markets, companies, IPOs, and venture investments.

With an innate understanding of what differentiates good from great companies, I move enterprises from mediocrity to success. Whether operating as a chief executive, board director or investment professional I always bring vision, integrity and leadership.

Currently at 5G, in addition to CEO responsibilities, I acted as primary design force behind Qonfer meeting management software and co-designer of Zude, a drag and drop social network. In less than ninety days raised 5 million dollars in the company’s first Series A stock sale and 1 year later raised an additional 5.25m in series B funding.

As general partner at Pequot Capital, I Identified investment opportunities, invested in and managed successful technology companies such as Netegrity, NetGear and Outlooksoft.

As founding team member, I grew Cheyenne Software into a billion dollar industry leader in 5 years as a pioneering server-based backup and network storage management company, generating $138M in annual revenues. I was directly responsible for developing a commanding market share that led to the company’s acquisition by Computer Associates for $1.2 billion in cash.

My earlier experience as Senior Director Advanced Products, AST Computer, and Software Engineer/Team Leader at LucasFilm Ltd./Convergence Corp enabled me to build a unique and solid technical base working on break-through products and designs.

I believe in giving back and paying forward through my board participation for the Foundation Fighting Blindness (FFB) and the National Neurovision Research Institute (NNRI). Please contact me if you are interested in participating or contributing in any way to these worthwhile organizations jamespmcniel@gmail.com.
