HC Saustrup’s Summary

Seasoned LAMP (Linux/Apache/MySQL/PHP) developer and UNIX System Administrator.

My goal in any situation is to develop long lasting software and hardware solutions that require a minimum of maintenance with a very high level of security and usability.

As a software developer with more than 20 years of experience, I know that software development sometimes requires a pragmatic approach, but without sacrificing good design and security.

My experience in system administration is anything but skin deep - I've followed the development and rise of the GNU/Linux operating system since the mid 90s, and have thorough knowledge of the systems inner workings. I enjoy working as a system administrator as much as a software developer, and I firmly believe that it's a good thing for software people to fiddle with hardware from time to time.
