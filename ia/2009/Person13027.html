Andi Roberts MBA’s Summary

Andi Roberts has over 14 years of experience in training and organisational development working with people of all levels in an organisation from Shop floor through to the boardroom.

With Spain as his home base for the last 12 years, Andi has extensive multicultural experience, and has worked in over 20 countries.

Originally from an outdoor management development background and grounding in outdoor activities instructor he has been involved in some of Europe’s leading cultural change programmes that have used challenge courses and experiential learning as the backbone for supporting the business’ required change.

As well as providing facilitation, Andi also supports his client’s organisations from the role of a coach working with individuals and teams too boost performance and also as a consultant in the areas of process improvement, change management and knowledge management.

Andi’s key areas of expertise are:

* Senior management development.
* Creating a coaching culture and coaching skill development.
* Customer service processes and programmes.
* Multicultural organisations and change implementation within them.
* Sales methodologies and effective sales development.
* Business performance improvement processes.
