fernando salvetti’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur, a philosopher and an anthropologist.

With my wife Barbara, in 1996 we set up LKN. Our corporate mission is the personal and professional development in Europe and all around the world.

Our roots: Turin and Ivrea (Piedmont, Italy). Our headquarters: Lugano, Geneva and Zurich (Switzerland). Our main networking hubs: Berlin, Boston, Brussels, Helsinki, Hong-Kong, London, Los Angeles, Milan, New York, Paris, Sydney and Turin. Our first personal address is Paris. The second is Caluso, Canavese (Piedmont). A long time is devoted to Switzerland.

LKN is a family business, set up in Turin thanks to the help of some colleagues and friends: Eugenio Calvi, a founder of the Italian Psychologists' Association, psychoterapyst and lawyer. Giorgio Girard, psychologist and economist, and Clara Capello, psychotherapyst, both full professors at the Psychology Faculty. Marco Siniscalco, lawyer, and Enrico di Robilant, epistemologist and practical philosopher, both full professors at the Law Faculty. Giacomo Bottino, theatre director and organizer, as well as manager at the University of Turin School of management.

In my earlier (and quite rich) professional life, among other things I was a young researcher working at the boundaries between epistemology, anthropology, organisational and behavioural sciences, ethics and jurisprudence (mainly with Norberto Bobbio, Enrico di Robilant, Gustavo Zagrebelsky, Marco Siniscalco, Marcello Gallo, Enzo Spaltro, Clara Capello, Giorgio Girard, Friedrich August von Hayek).

From 1996 my job is top management advising, education, personal training and consulting on self-empowerment, anthropology and knowledge governance. My biggest dream is improving self-empowerment and human sciences’ education around the world. When I live and work, I'm looking for many "ways of world-making": do you remember Nelson Goodman, the philosopher and manager of an art gallery? But above all, I have a wonderful daughter: Sophie.
