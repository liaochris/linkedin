Anil Jain’s Summary

Anil is a founder and Managing Partner of Endeavor Innovation Partners, a venture development and strategic advisory firm focused on working with entrepreneurs and companies that are heading towards an inflection point in their businesses. Endeavor's clients are typically seeking to start new ventures, launch new products, open new markets, innovate their business models, develop strategies and plans, and raise capital. Endeavor works with companies of all sizes, but has an emphasis on projects and ventures where the greatest amount of creative energy is required.

Anil is an entrepreneur and technology business executive whose career has been focused on the development of innovative technology products and services to address emerging market opportunities. He brings extensive experience in strategic marketing, technology development, product management, business development and new venture creation to the entrepreneurs, organizations and companies he works with. Having held leadership or senior advisory roles with a number of ventures and organizations, Anil’s experience spans a range of technologies and industries inclusive of software and information technology, semiconductors and solid state electronics, advanced materials and nanotechnology, communications and biotechnology (materials, informatics and medical devices).

Anil was a founding executive and the Vice President of Technology Ventures for Arizona Technology Enterprises (AzTE), an early stage technology commercialization company. At AzTE, Anil was responsible for developing and managing a portfolio of inventions, intellectual property and new technology spinout companies from Arizona State University and Northern Arizona University.

Anil is a Charter Member and founding Board Executive for the Arizona chapter of TiE (The Indus Entrepreneurs), has served as a past President (2003-2004) and will be the President again starting in October 2007.
