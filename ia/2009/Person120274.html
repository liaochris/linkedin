Joris Toonders’s Summary

" It's all about CONVERSION! "

Started my career on the age of thirteen with affiliate marketing activities for third parties like international corporates. During last years Yonego was involved in setting up the strategy for internet marketing campaigns and search marketing for several medium sized and large international companies generating millions in revenue for these clients.

Yonego is now a well respected international internet marketing agency with a strong focus on ROI. Yonego operates in 11 different countries with a result driven team of Senior Internet Marketing Managers.

Due to its unique technology and experience Yonego is growing fast, in line with the generated online revenue for its fast growing number of clients. Yonego is a Google AdWords Qualified Company, a Microsoft adExcellence Member and an active member of IPAN, IAB Taskforce Search / Affiliate Marketing and the Web Analytics Association.
