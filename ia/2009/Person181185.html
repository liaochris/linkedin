Parmesh Shahani’s Summary

I am a media generalist and author, currently based between Philadelphia and Bombay. I am studying at the Annenberg School for communication at UPenn and also serve as the Editorial-at-Large for Verve magazine, India. In addition, I am also affiliated with MIT's C3 consortium - an academic-corporate initiative that aims to understand the complex world of media convergence from a social and cultural perspective. My first book, Gay Bombay: Globalization, Love and (Be)Longing in Contemporary India (New Delhi, London, Los Angeles, Singapore: Sage Publications) was released in April 2008.

My prior work experiences include working on new media, venture capital and innovation for Mahindra & Mahindra. founding and managing India's first youth expression website, business development for Sony's Indian television channel operations, writing and editing copy for Elle magazine and the Times of India group, helping make a low-budget feature film and teaching as a visiting faculty member at a Bombay college.
