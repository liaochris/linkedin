Tim LeClaire, CEO - Lynxster.com’s Summary

I have 15 years of results-oriented leadership across a variety of business functions, including operations, finance, strategy, and human resources. I am currently serving in two roles: as CEO and Managing Partner at Lynxster Group (www.lynxster.com), and as a Human Resources Manager at Target. I have a strong track record of exceeding expected results at all levels. I specialize in turnaround situations, creating operational stability, delivering exceptional results, and developing the leaders and teams I work with.

At the Lynxster Group, I have worked with my partner to form and build the company, which will officially launch on May 1st, 2009 to the public. It's primary offering is an employment portal which will facilitate the connection between job seekers and hiring professionals on a platform that is more cost effective and easier to use for all parties involved.

During the previous 20 months, concurrently with my position at Target, I entered the full-time Executive MBA program at the Saunders College of Business at the RIT. I graduated from the program in November 2007 ranked first in my class with a 4.0 GPA.

For the last six months of the EMBA program, I worked on a consulting project for Excellus Blue Cross Blue Shield, which was focused on new product development. An outstanding final report was delivered to Excellus executives, who are continuing on with the project for launch in late 2008 or early 2009.

I am currently not on any company boards, but look forward to the opportunity to do so, to help impact a company’s strategic growth, while broadening my own leadership skills and abilities.
