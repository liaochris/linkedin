Janine Baillie-Stewart’s Summary

A committed, professional Human Resources executive with extensive experience at a senior leadership level. Accomplished in all areas of the HR value chain with a passion for organizational development. A key strength is the ability to work and influence across complex, matrix environments in a business partner role.

A combined 17 years of Human Resources and organizational development experience in retail and manufacturing industries.

Specific expertise in creating step change, system wide business improvement through culture transformation initiatives.

A recently completed Masters degree has nurtured a strong passion for futures work, strategy formulation, scenario planning, foresight and environmental scanning processes. The sustainability of global practices and the state of the future is a key area of interest!

Janine's goal is to develop conscious leadership competencies and deep democracy in organizations.
