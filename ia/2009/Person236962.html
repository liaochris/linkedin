Tony A. Romani’s Summary

<<M&A Advisory for Financial Institutions>>
Freeman & Co.'s M&A professionals have over 200 years of industry experience working with financial institutions of all sizes. Its list of clients includes investment banks, broker-dealers, alternative and traditional asset managers, insurance companies. Freeman & Co.'s advisory services include mergers and acquisitions advice, fairness opinions, capital raising, private placements and restructuring advice.

As an independent investment banking boutique with no proprietary capital markets business, Freeman & Co. is not conflicted by its own capital markets initiatives.

<<League Tables>>
Freeman & Co. has developed a proprietary algorithm that estimates fees earned by investment banks, which has long been the industry standard for benchmarking investment banking fees. The algorithm has been widely used by Thomson Reuters since 2005 to jointly produce investment banking analysis and league table reports for Thomson ONE Investment Banking clients.
Freeman & Co. provides consulting services and data analytic solutions to business group heads, strategic planners and top management at financial institutions.
