What I had been up to till now...

Working with a local company in Japan to establish Japan's first SWsoft reselling business provided a stepping-stone for SWsoft into the Japanese market, while at the same time providing a valuable localisation headstart and knowledge about working with Japanese businesses. SWsoft has been re-named, "Parallels".

Parallels decided to launch fully into the Japanese market in 2004 and I was brought on to establish the Japanese Branch and pipeline.

In early 2005 Parallels Japan transformed from a Branch entity to a corporation installing Nagaoka-san as CEO working closely with a major ISP in a JV.

Coming into 2006, Parallels needed to further grow from a JV to a fully-owned and managed corporation and set up strong support and sales as 100% Parallels.

There are a number of opportunities to work with Parallels as either Hardware, Software or Service Partners. Working with Parallels, Software Partners can make Modules, Plug-ins and ASPs that can be launched to millions of existing users.

After establishing Parallels corporation and bringing on a number of employees, I have decided to move onto my own business, Saloob, Inc. adding value with Solutions to XSPs globally..
