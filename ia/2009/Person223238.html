Steve Geldmacher’s Summary

26 Years...management and leadership success in executive levels with Telecom carriers, manufacturers, agents, dealers, resellers.
Achievement Summary:
Intrinsically motivated; as key executive and spokesperson, fostered growth and expansion of startup to fastest growing company to $10 billion in annual revenue in U.S. history (Sprint) including developing strategy, marketing, sales, staffing and training, and implementing systems.
Powerful advocate delivering commanding presence; delivered presentations to motivate and educate groups up to 3,000 professionals; successfully directed up to 4,000 employees, 250 retail stores, and 17,000+ national, regional, and local distribution doors producing 6.5 million customers annually; exceeded gross customer activations by 102% of goal.
Created and implemented successful multi-million dollar marketing plans and sales proposals from concept through fulfillment; identified and developed market penetration; forecasted field sales and marketing; defined and set product pricing; created successful advertising and promotions.
Cultivated, solicited, secured, and managed major 3rd party accounts ranging from $100,000 to $500,000 and national and local distribution agreements ranging from $100,000 to over $24 million with Best Buy, Radio Shack, Circuit City, Office Depot, Office Max, and many others.
Raised $1.5M in angel funding for development of world’s first portable, universal wireless communications and security hub. Recognized for advanced accomplishments; someone who “makes things happen” through commitment, drive, collaboration, and a passion to develop broad, deep, and lasting relationships.
