Rita Falcão Monteiro’s Summary

http://twitter.com/ritafalcao
http://ritafalcao.carbonmade.com/

A University of Oporto Multimedia Graduate, Rita Falcão is well-versed in multiple aspects of multimedia design and production such as graphic design, typography, web development and design, photography, video editing, UI design, multimedia app design, among others. Rita longs for new and unexpected brand challenges in multidisciplinary teams dedicated to harvesting creativity which would suit her ambitions of lifelong learning and blazing new frontiers in the craft of establishing corporate identity on the graphics design front. Curiosity is in her nature; being thorough and methodical is a longtime necessity – none of which stops her from being upbeat as she strives to become acquainted with as many disparate disciplinary fields within design and advertising and become more and more wired to communication and media. A Portugal Northeast native, she currently resides in Oporto and is 24 years old.

Rita Falcão Monteiro’s Specialties:

Corporate identity and image, graphic design, webdesign, multimedia design, packaging, photography.
