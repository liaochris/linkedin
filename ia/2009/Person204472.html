Royelen Lee Boykie’s Summary

My first job in online content programming gave me the opportunity to contribute to a consumer tool getting 44 million page views a month. What an extraordinary introduction into the potential of online community it was. Nine years, much learning, change and exploration later, I now put my passion and experience to work in pursuit of social justice.

As an experienced digital strategist leading and success-monitoring exciting community-focused campaigns, my emphasis is on engaging and activating the public in participatory democracy and corporate advocacy.

All aboard the movement! Defend our rights to clean, safe, affordable food and water -- join us at www.foodandwaterwatch.org.
