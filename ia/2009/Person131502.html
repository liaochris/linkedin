Jennifer Harris’s Summary

I specialize in Graphic Design for small business. Using Adobe Creative Suite I can create just about any printed marketing promotional piece you need. Direct mail and Magazine/Book layout are my favorite niche markets to work in, although Logo Design & Branding is a large part of my everyday work. I regularly work with non profits to create fundraising packages and help them put together fundraising events. From Business cards to Billboards and everything in between.

I work with a network of creative expertise to bring to life your marketing promotion. Photographers, writers, web developers, artists, marketers, interior designers, printers and producers of social content (pod/vidcasts) are amongst My network.

My life time goal is to create a successful design studio serving the rural area I live in. My Career goal is to create a magazine of my own. My personal goals include raising my children to be the best they can be and to teach them the world is theirs if they want it!
