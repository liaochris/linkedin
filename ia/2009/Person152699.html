Mark Stefanchuk’s Summary

Business Analyst, senior programmer, published author, and co-founder/creator of cadgurus.com an internet community for computer aided design professionals. Mr. Stefanchuk has 19 years of business, engineering, and design automation experience with degrees in mathematics, mechanical engineering, and Executive MBA from University of Minnesota’s Carlson School of Management.

As well, Mark's experience extends into the creative space; Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop.
