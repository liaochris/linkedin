Bari Lyman’s Summary

I founded this company 7 years ago out of a passion for helping people have breakthroughs. The goal of BreakthroughIT is to place technology pros in jobs they love and have clients feel wow'ed by our service and repuration.

Having been a computer consultant for technology companies including AT&T, Hoechst Celanese, Lucent Technologies, Excel Communications and others I always loved to help people find jobs and know what it takes to find top talent.

Our company has a team of technical recruiters with technical backgrounds who understand both client and candidate needs. BreakthroughIT is a WBENC certified company and a proven top performing vendor for it's clients.
