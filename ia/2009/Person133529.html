Ken Wallin’s Summary

I am a process improvement and project management specialist.

I use the Microsoft Visio application to map current processes in detail, and then, with clients, review the process looking for improvement opportunities. We work out the change needed and then implement that change. I like to stay in place to help engender the necessary behavioral patterns as change management is critical. (Remember it takes as much as 21 days to break a behavior pattern, and set a new one in place.)

I also enjoy creating the project management plan (I use Microsoft Project) with the phases and timelines. I believe in reaching deliverables on time, on budget, and correctly installed.

Have enjoyed nearly 15 years as a management consultant. Have done all the normal and usual consultant projects, and traveled offshore to India, Australia, and Indonesia for several projects.

Ken Wallin’s Specialties:

Meeting and presentation facilitation, Meeting management, training (teleconference and platform), process improvement, staffing management, project management, change management, and developing RACI-S (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed, and Supporting) matrices.
