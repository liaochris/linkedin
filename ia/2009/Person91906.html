Handre van der merwe’s Summary

Handre graduated from the Randse Afrikaanse Universiteit (now known as The University of Johannesburg) in 2004 after completing a Bcom (Informatics). He completed his Bcom Honours (Information Systems with Organisational Behaviour) from the University of Cape Town in 2005. He formed part of his residence Aflsaan’s Squash team and came7th overall in the summer tournament in 2004. In 2005 he participated and helped organise & structure a CLC (Computer Literacy Course), and with seven of his classmates presented a 2month computer literacy course at Polsmoor Prison in Tokai Cape Town for juvenile inmates, which ran on 8 consecutive Saturdays.

Handre has completed several other courses to extend his knowledge in the industries he has worked. These are SQL Server, Java Programming, Marketing knowledge courses, as well as a course in Web-design which included a wide range of all Adobe software products.
Handre joined Digiata in July 2008 as a member of the Implementation team. He is involved with a software conversion at Allan Grey.

Handre worked as a Junior IT Analyst for KPMG at the beginning of 2008, where he was involved in the IT System audit for a number of key clients in Cape Town and Johannesburg. Before that Handre worked as a Junior Project Manager for 4Ward-design, an Audio Visual company with focus on integration and control, for two years prior to joining Digiata. Here he played a crucial client facing role where he was involved and responsible for numerous projects, from conception through to implementation and client handover. Before that he was an Information Systems Tutor for 2nd year Business Science students during his Honours year at UCT.

Handre is an active Surfer, Squash player and Mountain biker. In his spare-time he enjoys exploring the West Coast in search of undiscovered waves with his friends and brothers. He also plays guitar for an acoustic band over the weekends.
