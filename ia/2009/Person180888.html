Paolo Caputo’s Summary

General Management profile, coming from Sales & Marketing, in the past four years fully enhanced in other business areas including Finance & Administration, Business Development, HR organisation and development, due to the entrepreneurial experience.
Paolo, since 1980 has a successful track record in leading marketing and sales organisations, and a strong experience in executive level relationships with customers, trade and executive international management, as well as shareholders and other stakeholders.
He mainly operates in the automotive business, in multinational customer-oriented environment, adhering to the highest standards of excellence and integrity. Most of the experience has been matured in leading organisations, having efficiently contributed to establish the leadership, while acting in senior management roles in marketing and sales, building strong business to business management competencies. Paolo has also dedicated himself to contribute to editorial products being publishing director of the Europcar house organ Green World and member of the Scientific Committee of the magazine Interauto Fleet & Mobility.
Significant traits of his action are recognised commercial vision, strategic marketing, business development, and communication skills, built in several experiences.
Married, a son graduated in Economy and Management and a daughter close to terminate the high school, are his beloved family. Paolo is currently based in Rome, but available to evaluate different opportunities.

Paolo Caputo’s Specialties:

Management area: work flows, procedures, costs/benefits analysis; markets analysis and segmentation; personnel, training, sales force management; incentives, targets; accounts management, finance and controlling, business planning; full utilisation of Microsoft Office; direct and co-marketing.
External relations area: customers at any level, shareholders and stakeholders relationships; team working; House Organ publishing; inter-functional relationships among the Car Rental association.
