Kim Joar Bekkelund’s Summary

Currently studying communication technology at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) in Trondheim. Especially interested in web development. I have working experience from a full time employment at Atmel Norway AS and co-founding Polar Bear ANS, and I have organizational experience from amongst others Studentersamfundet i Trondhjem as leader of the marketing group and as software developer at UKA and Nabla.

I love roaming between the fields of programming, design and the users.
