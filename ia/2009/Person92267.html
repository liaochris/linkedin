    Creative Company Conference

    (Media Production industry)

    October 2008 — Present (9 months)

    The Creative Company Conference is an international conference that brings business and creativity together in one arena, deconstructs the DNA of creative companies to understand their business models, presents the entrepreneurs that play the game of value creation, innovation and creativity in business.

    Held on April 1st 2008 at Amsterdam's spectacular Muziekgebouw, the CCC secured 10 leading innovators from four continents for its inaugural event, including Marc Mathieu, Stanley Hainsworth, Arkadi Kuhlmann, Bill Moggridge, Robert Brozin, Richard Reed, Ivy Ross, Carlos Miele, Peter Diamandis and John Howkins.

    The CCC is the place to connect, get inspired and learn lessons from business and creative thought leaders from every corner of the world.

    The next CCC will take place on Tuesday 26 May 2009, Muziekgebouw Amsterdam, The Netherlands.

  • Founder/Creative Director/Chairman

    Texelse Boys Foundation

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Design industry)

    November 1999 — Present (9 years 8 months)

    Texelse Boys is a non-commercial international network of approx. 900 creatives worldwide in every thinkable field of creativity. What all these creatives have in common is the passion to create, within but also far beyond their comfort zone. Since Texelse Boys was founded in '99, it rapidly grew and got international recognition. Result: dozens of projects with collaborating creatives, a truckload of publicity in the media worldwide, several events, innovative new music, tv shows and a lot more. One of the highlights: an embedded festival within the Lowlands Festival in 2005, visited by 55,000 people. Currently, the Texelse Boys Foundation is preparing new creative and innovative events in the near future.

We combine user-centered design, research, an ability to incubate ideas at a strategic level, design talent, to create outstanding and innovative design solutions for leading brands and ambitious challengers. We thrive on tackling the toughest challenges and designing elegant, functional, effective solutions that make people happy (and address our customers’ business and branding needs).
