Hilary Luros’s Summary

Athena Law Group LLP understands that clients today are more legal- and business-savvy than ever before. We know that clients want to engage with law firms that are focused on high-quality and cost-effective representation – law firms whose first priority is client satisfaction, not maximizing billable hours and fees.

We agree wholeheartedly that clients today need and deserve lawyers who answer the telephone, who are pleased to hear from their clients, and who will provide real and substantial expertise on demand.

From consistently meeting client needs, we know that clients want lawyers who are focused on getting the job done, making the deal happen, and acting as team players, not arm-chair quarterbacks.

We believe that clients deserve a firm whose “brand” is based on word of mouth from satisfied customers, not size or fancy letterhead. We believe that clients deserve a firm whose partners are focused on clients, not on internal politics or management issues.

Athena Law Group LLP is the wise choice for these clients. Our partners have completed thousands of technology and licensing transactions. Not a single client has had to contest the terms of agreements we’ve handled for them, and not a single client has been dissatisfied with our services.

Athena Law Group is committed to providing high-quality and cost-competitive legal services to our clients. Our practice is structured in a manner that maximizes accessibility to attorneys, responsiveness to our clients, and cost-effectiveness.

We are dedicated to working one-on-one with our clients to anticipate and meet their legal needs. We do this, in part, through alliances with other law firms of all sizes and practice areas. As a result, clients can now customize their legal experience with “best of breed” advice and proactively manage their legal costs at the same time.
