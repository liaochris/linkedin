Brad Parr’s Summary

I have had a very diverse working experience. I started my career in the Marine Corps. After school I worked as on officer at the Lancaster County Sherriff's Department. When I moved back to Texas I decided to pursue the family legacy, so I became a homebuilder. I have since built homes from 90k up to 5 million dollar estates. I am the President/Owner of Palazzo Luxury Homes, LLC and the owner of Fine Home Renovations. I am also consulting as the head of Constructon and Design for a current clients company, the Corliss Stone Littles Corporation. I help her by overseeing all of the retail construction that her company is involved with around the country. My goal would be to someday be able to build projects on my own because I want to, not because I have to. I want to build a lasting company that would provide homes for families and security for those who work with me.

Brad Parr’s Specialties:

I am an experienced Homebuilder who has built many award winning projects. I am a Professional builder with extensive experience in project development. As a project management professional I am skilled in securing local regulatory land-use approvals. I have an excellent background in project design, construction, scheduling and budgeting. I also have extensive experience in developing and implementing regional building programs for luxury homes.
