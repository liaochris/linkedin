Thomas Burleson’s Summary

Thomas Burleson has been communicating with customers, defining architectures, and building software solutions for more than 24 years. His leadership and extensive experience have been instrumental in providing e-Commerce and Internet solutions for numerous companies including Sherwin-Williams, SAP, Principal Financial Group, Adobe, and Universal Mind. He has worked for banking, B2B, and B2C companies guiding and delivering software solutions for intranet, WAN, and internet audiences.

During the last 4 years, Thomas has fine-tuned his skills providing leadership, mentoring, and best practices within one of America's largest, cutting-edge RIA and Web2.0 consultancy firms. Now Thomas directs the UStrive2 development efforts with a unique blend of communication skills, leadership, business development competency, technical excellence, and product development experiences.
