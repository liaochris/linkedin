PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

Global Business Development Director - SkySong
SkySong is a technology incubation center working in concert with researchers at Arizona State University. John’s role is to help International technology based companies move to the United States and successfully enter the US markets.

President - South Mountain Inc
South Mountain develops pre-employment Software Solutions including Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), pre-employment tests, psychological assessments, online applications and a host of other recruiting tools to help recruiters more effectively manage the recruiting process.

Principal - AzRecruiting -
AzRecruiting is the services arm of South Mountain Inc. AzRecruiting provides Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) services to Fortune 1000 companies. Our Industry specific knowledge combined with South Mountain’s technology enables AzRecruiting to provide highly cost effective Recruiting Services.
