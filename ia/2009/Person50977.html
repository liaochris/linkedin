Crystelle Topatan’s Summary

Crystelle Topatan has worked in the Events and Marketing Industry for 10 years, with over 4 of those years being in her own marketing business. Crystelle has a clear passion for helping people get their message out to the big wide world, having promoted and currently promoting such speakers as Peter Morrissey, Anthony Robbins, Dr.John Demartini, Donald Trump, Amir Zoghi, Christopher Howard, John Burley and many more. Above and beyond the speakers and events she has promoted Crystelle has worked in Professional & Personal Development Publishing, Educational Publishing, Legal Publishing and Marketing Specific Environmentally Friendly products & services, she has had a wealth of experience.

With Crystelle's career being based around Education, Entertainment and Development she has been fortunate enough to attend and soak up the knowledge of many inspirational and life-changing speakers she has promoted in her career. A graduate of Anthony Robbins programs, an Christopher Howard NLP Master and Quest for Truth Experience Graduate she has had access to a wealth of knowledge that not all have had the honour of having access to.

Crystelle lives her life through the words of... Be, do, have and create all that you love in life...

Crystelle Topatan’s Specialties:

She loves what she does and passes that love onto her clients and their businesses.
