Giacomo Tufano’s Summary

Judging from my professional life, I have been an accomplished manager of technical people (both individual contributors and first level managers), with a solid technical background on software and operating systems, Internet-related technologies, marketing, sales and executive sales support.
I can be defined as a person that likes to defy convention, very innovative and that uses his imagination to successfully solve problems and conflicts. I have a strong interest in what is new and exciting and this leads me to be very creative when it comes to finding novel solutions to complex problems. All successes in my professional life are related to creating things and leading change in structured organizations. Taking the above into account, Sun and some of my previous employers have assigned me with the role of change agent, a catalyst for the time bound and correct execution of planned changes.
I have been considered brilliant in presentation and communication skills, with demonstrated success both with customers, up to CxO level, and with the trade technical press. During recent years, I successfully managed under, and performed reporting duties to several different country and international Directors and VPs, both in direct and dotted reporting lines.
Sun has been my most extensive work experience, during which I have covered the many roles. After acting as the Country pre-sales Manager, at the moment I work as a people manager for the program office in charge of Quality, Processes, Customer Escalations and Project Assurance in the Customer Services Division of Sun Italy.
Prior to working at Sun, I have held positions in different Companies, ranging from being part of Startup teams, to holding management and individual contributor roles.

Giacomo Tufano’s Specialties:

Customer relationship, people management, technology consultancy, presentation, marketing, opensource.
