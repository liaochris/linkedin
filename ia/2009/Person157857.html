Colin McAllister’s Summary

Colin McAllister is a management consultant and business leader recognized for his unique drive, vision and passion for excellence, combined with practical experience in the delivery of value based solutions and execution strategies to bridge the technology-business gap with corporate requirements. He has demonstrated this internationally in the private and public sectors across multiple industries including software, telecommunications, healthcare and construction to name a few. The results of his efforts have empowered organizations to evolve and realize bottom line growth, competitive differentiation, and increased customer satisfaction.

A forward thinking professional focused on client centric, pragmatic actionable deliverables, Colin believes, “only when we provide people with information they did not have and demonstrate how to leverage this knowledge, can we create true value.”

Colin has a BSc in Mechanical Engineering from Dalhousie University and the University of Saskatchewan and a Master of Business Administration from Queens School of Business. He also is a Certified Project Management Professional, Licensed Professional Engineer and Certified Management Consultant (pending). Colin has held various leadership positions in the areas of operations, process reengineering, business development and engineering design with organizations ranging from multi national matrix organizations and Crown Corporations to small and medium enterprises and start up ventures.

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    2006 — 2008 (2 years)

    The Salveo Health and Wellness Platform is an end-to-end solution that allows users to take a proactive approach to managing their health. The HWP provides users a way to collect physiological and lifestyle information from almost anywhere and share with it their healthcare team, including professionals such as doctors and nurses or personal caregivers such as family.
    The Salveo HWP gives users and their families peace of mind knowing that they can manage their health more effectively than ever before. By managing their health they save money through less trips to doctors offices and less days away from work tending to their condition. Doctors and other professional caregivers are given a better insight into the lives of their patients while being able to manage more patients than in the past. Individuals that use a proactive approach to treating their health, such as the Salveo HWP, will live healthier lives, removing the strain from over-burdened healthcare systems.
