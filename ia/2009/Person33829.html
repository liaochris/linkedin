Brian Rawlings’s Summary

Creative entertainment executive with a proven track record in identifying, producing and monetizing highly successful projects. Have helped create and manage explosive bottom line growth in his division at a Fortune 50 company, growing revenue from under $10MM per year worldwide to over $100MM. Is currently in the process of securing financing for a "truly exclusive and proprietary" form of mobile entertainment for consumers under 25. He draws upon his unique experience with the Disney Channel blockbusters "High School Musical" and "Hannah Montana" to fine tune creative offerings to teens and tweens. Since 1983 he's scored 23 #1 records and led his companies to sales in excess of 50 million records.

"The High School Musical franchise was like having an entire career of success in one year. We broke every record on the Billboard charts, sold more records than anyone in the world, had more downloads than anyone in the world, and were even entered in the Guinness Book of World Records. That was great, but the next ten years in entertainment are about adapting to the new model, finding new methods of outreach and impact, and providing high quality stories and music. I believe there are more opportunities today than have ever existed in entertainment, but, the consumers are also the most educated, savvy, and skeptical audience we have ever encountered. The belief that today's consumers will buy what we feed them has to be smashed and replaced by high quality, high value offerings that meet every litmus test."
