Jimmy DeMesa’s Summary

My mission in life is to be a "positive, creative force for health and happiness". As a writer, speaker, physician, entrpreneur, and corporate executive, I have made great strides in achieving that mission.

I have been a business partner with the well-known personal development coach, Anthony Robbins, and have now published the first in a series of several books intended to imporve people's lives (the first book is called simply, BeHappy!)

You can learn a lot more - and become a lot happier - by visiting my constantly growing website at www.behappy101.com.
