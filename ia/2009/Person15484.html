Andy Yiu’s Summary

Andy Yiu is a Staff Software Engineer at IBM providing enterprise support services for IBM Lotus Sametime and associated products to many Fortune 500 companies. With over 5 years of experience in systems management and support services, Andy brings expert knowledge in the realms of directory services, networking and Internet technologies. Unwilling to wait for change, he is a change agent in any organization, bringing fresh and innovative ideas to streamline and improve existing processes and adding value to existing products/services. Many of the projects implemented have lead to higher customer satisfaction recognized through three consecutive IBM Bravo Awards for outstanding customer service awarded in 2005, 2006, 2007 and 2008.

Andy holds a Masters of Business Administration and Bachelors Degree in Computer Science and is currently pursuing an MBA.
