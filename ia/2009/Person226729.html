Surender Mohan’s Summary

I am an expert in the analysis, design and delivery of innovative, high-performance enterprise applications and services to address complex business problems. I bring over thirteen years of experience designing, building, shipping, selling and servicing software at Microsoft Corporation in three major profit centers: Office, Windows and MSN.

• Experience working on three v1 through v2 product releases (Microsoft Team Manager (Office), Microsoft TaxSaver (MSN) and Microsoft Systems Architecture(Windows). Experience taking an existing desktop product and turning it into a v1 server product for WebTaxSaver.
• Ship experience: Project 95, Team Manager 97, Project 97, TaxSaver 99, TaxSaver UK, TaxSaver 2000, Windows 2000 Server, Windows XP, Windows Server 2003, Windows Vista, Windows Genuine Advantage, MSA (Microsoft Systems Architecture) for Internet Data Center, MSA for Enterprise Data Center, Software Protection Platform (SPP), Office 2007, Shipped various online business critical services to enable Microsoft operations.
• Thirteen+ years of leadership and management experience at Microsoft with product development teams and IT teams in USA and India.
• Six+ years of consulting experience working with a broad range of customers. I helped drive the adoption of Microsoft's enterprise software in Server space when we had close to zero market share (e.g. relative to Sun Solaris and IBM) for the Internet backend systems.
• Passion for creating great products and services that users will embrace and use whether these are desktop, server, web or custom solutions for consumer markets, small/medium business and enterprise customers.
• Formal engineering and management education with entrepreneurial mindset.
