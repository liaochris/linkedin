Brent Holland’s Summary

Brent has an exceptional track record delivering high-return, proprietary employee selection, talent management, and succession management solutions. He has led the design of solutions ranging from succession management systems serving 300k globally dispersed employees to proprietary pre-hire assessments for entry-level through sr. management roles on behalf of some of the world's most recognizable logos. His internal/external consulting experience has also helped him hone his expertise around competency modeling, performance management, and executive coaching. Brent has successfully built human capital research, talent management, and selection/assessment practices.

Brent Holland’s Specialties:

Employee selection, succession management, pre-hire assessment design, talent management, job analysis, assessment, competency modeling, performance management, executive coaching, and business analysis.
