Kevin Hale’s Summary

As a freelance editor, I contract myself out to edit big budget, and not so big budget productions. I can either edit in my home office, or I will travel to production facilities to edit too. Since I've been in the promotional end of TV Broadcast for over 15 years now, the :30 commercial is my specialty, but I can edit pretty much anything.

As News10's Multimedia Promotion Manager, I oversee all aspects of News10's promotion: On-Air, On-Line, Outdoor, radio, podcasting, Widgets, mobile, etc. I look at traditional and non-traditional ways of getting News10's message out to the public.

Also, I'm a weekend actor. I've been in several independent films in Sacramento, some not so good... some pretty darn good (one went all the way to Sundance). Here's a link to my flicks on IMDB: http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0354957/

And finally, I'm the principal of Fade Away Sleep Sounds. A series of White Noise CD's designed to help fussy babies fall asleep. If you know someone with a new baby that's having problems falling asleep, give them this link: http://www.fadeawaysleepsounds.com
