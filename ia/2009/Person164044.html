Mike Cappiello’s Summary

Ongoing developement of Marketing and Sales strategies for the Bright Language Test in the US Market. The test, which offers a user friendly and convenient online software platform, is now deployed in over 180 countries and is utilized by an ever growing number of multinational corporations in their efforts to:

assess linguistic resources within the company (in many different languages)
assess the international communication skills of personnel
develop realistic training budgets
detect the pitfalls of misleading resumes
recruit the most qualified people
assess staff members at the transfer stage
utilize a uniform and cost effective assessment tool.

Please visit our website, www.brightlanguages.com for more information
