Gillian Pollock’s Summary

GP Solutions offers an expert resource for companies wishing to develop their presence and business internationally in the most efficient and cost-effective manner. We help companies avoid the pitfalls that can impede the initial profitability of and add unnecessary costs to doing business in new and unfamiliar countries

The service is tailor-made to ensure that the culture, mission and key characteristics of a company are successfully transferred during expansion into another country in a manner sensitive to cultural differences.

The service covers preliminary budgeting to full set-up according to the specific profile and needs of Head Office. GP Solutions will recruit staff to create the local team, locate suitable office space, furnish that space according to the company profile (from corporate colours to furniture, and computers to tea cups), contract services such as legal, accounting, communications, and many more, in addition handling the vast amount of details necessary to create a company ready to do business.

Gillian Pollock’s Specialties:

Setting up subsidiaries from zero to highly successful businesses in any country worldwide.
