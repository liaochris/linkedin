Jesse Ellenbogen’s Summary

I research & design hybrid hardware/software consumer experiences. I've been honored to contribute to a number of iconic, landscape-shaping products, such as Palm's Pre & Treo, and Apple's PowerBook & Newton.

In my 20+ year career, I've held UX research, design & management positions at Apple, Sony, Palm, E*TRADE and Digeo/MOXI.

Through my consultancy, UserFit.com, I've also provided UX consulting for 19 different consumer technology companies, including YouTube, Logitech, and Danger.

The consumer product categories to which I've primarily contributed are:

MOBILE DEVICES & CONTENT: 35+ products, including
• Palm's Pre smartphone and webOS
• Danger’s Sidekick 2 & 3 smartphones
• Helio’s mobile phones & media download UI
• Apple’s PowerBook & Newton
• YouTube for mobile phones
• Yahoo! Mail for mobile phones

A/V PRODUCTS & REMOTES: 30+ products, including:
• Digeo’s MOXI digital video recorder set-top box
• Sony’s DTR-HD1, their first HDTV set-top box
• DVDO’s Edge HDTV processor
• Logitech’s Wireless DJ multi-room music system

Jesse Ellenbogen’s Specialties:

creating hybrid hardware/software consumer experiences that are more than the sum of the parts

defining user interactions that scale across the spectrum of users' needs

gaining insight into customer behaviors & preferences through user research, including usability testing
