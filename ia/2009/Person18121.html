Antonello Volpe, PMP’s Summary

Project Manager and Project Management Consultant.

In my professional life I had the opportunity to face with different projects: Industrialization and production start up of International Defence System Programs; manufacturing of Satellites; industrial plants building; ship building; PMO organization.
This in several multinational organization such as Fincantieri, Marconi, MBDA, Alcatel Alenia Space, Novartis and Alenia Aeronautica.

I am responsible for the successful implementation of multiple projects as an integrated whole, delivering the total business solution on time, on budget and within customer satisfaction.

Project management methodologies and techniques are my most relevant areas interest.

Antonello Volpe, PMP’s Specialties:

Project / Program Management, Planning, Control, Risk Management, PMO
