Diane Aull’s Summary

Over the course of a long and varied career, I've worked for some of the biggest corporations in the world, and some of the smallest. I've worked with industries ranging from financial services to retail sales to manufacturing, in places as diverse as New York City and Jedburg, SC. My work spaces have included everything from a private office with a view of the Manhattan skyline to the counter of a coffee shop to a laptop on my back deck.

The day I met the World Wide Web, I fell irrevocably in love -- I've been designing, developing and promoting websites for nearly 15 years. Today, I bring all my experience and skills to bear to help companies and entrepreneurs earn more profits from their websites.

To learn more, visit my online résumé at http://www.aull.info.
