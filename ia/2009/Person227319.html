Sushmita Jha’s Summary

My Professional experience and aspirations' Snapshot

A masters in Psychology, i began my career with a stint of about a year with a training firm

Thereafter i made a move to SAVILLS in pursuit of quenching my hunger for sensible work. It was here that my foundation for generalist HR was laid. For Three years i finetuned my expertise and gathered immense knowledge under my Mentor and my Manager Mr. Inder Singh. The work was immensely satisfying and i dabbled in everything a learner could ask for. it was a breakthrough i had been waiting for - a move from Freud's realm to Maslow's world!

My movement to Bangalore post marriage in 2005 resulted in my shift to PharmARC and since then i have been growing in leaps and bounds in terms of knowledge gained and exposure to various facets of generalist HR under my Mentor and my Manager Siraj. We are a KPO and provide analytics and consulting services in Pharamceutical Vertical. The challenges and assignments are varied in terms of Human dynamics when it comes to handling analysts.

In next five years i am targetting to move towards one specific vertical of my interest in HR (Engagement Management, C&B, Competency Mapping, Career Planning - not clear yet...) - hone my expertise in the same and thereafter lead a hectic and yet serene life of a consultant !
