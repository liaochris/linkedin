Robert Dickie III’s Summary

Robert L. Dickie III is currently the Chief Executive Officer for Signature Management Team, a leadership development service provider and event management company started in Flint, Michigan in 2001. Robert leads a world-class staff of over 55 employees who specialize in customizing a vast array of products and web based services for a wide variety of customers in North America, Brazil, Mexico, Australia, and Singapore.

Upon graduation from the University of Tennessee in 1998 and he earned his Master of Science degree in Industrial Engineering at the University of Arkansas while in the military. Robert joined the Air Force in 1998 and was commissioned as a Second Lieutenant. He earned numerous decorations and promotions throughout his Air Force career while rising to the rank of Captain. As a Captain, he was hand selected to be the personal Aide-de-Camp to General William Begert, Commander of the Pacific Air Forces. On many visits to Washington DC he met with members of Congress and Pentagon officials. He was in daily contact with the highest levels of US Government, foreign militaries, and civilian and industry leaders. This invaluable experience and insight into the operation of industry and government at the highest levels would prove to be extremely valuable in his current role as CEO of Signature Management Team. As CEO he has helped lead Signature Management Team from 13 million dollars in revenue in 2004 to four straight years of double and triple digit growth and business operations around the world.

Robert is extremely active in the community and philanthropic endeavors. He is the Chairman of the Board of Kindred Spirit non-profit organization, President of All Grace Outreach non-profit organization, and is a member of the Young President’s Organization (YPO). Robert’s favorite hobby is competitive running and in 2003 he was the 3rd American and 23rd overall finisher in the Boston Marathon.
