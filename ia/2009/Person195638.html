Reed Overfelt’s Summary

My business mantra is “great marketing is the foundation of all breakthrough sales performances!” When all is said and done, I am a marketing guy. I have developed 100s of US and Global marketing programs for companies like Microsoft, EMC, and Amazon. I am currently the CEO of a global marketing consulting company.

My roles in the past have ranged from COO of Mural Ventures and CEO of Mural Consulting to Microsoft General Manager of the Mid-Atlantic Small and Medium Enterprise Business and Senior Director for the North America Hosting Partner Group

I built and led 2 highly successful start-ups at Microsoft: a hosting provider channel business, currently generating $1 Billion in global revenue, and $235M marketing driven sales organization focused on small and medium enterprise customers.
