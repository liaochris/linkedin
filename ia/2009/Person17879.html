    Social Phenomenon

    (Internet industry)

    October 2008 — Present (10 months)

    Every action is inherently social thus making us all part of the Social Capital equation... factor in the what's happening online and it's nothing short of a Social Phenomenon.

    Social Phenomenon is a Social Media Marketing Consultancy that specializes in planning and executing consumer and B-to-B interactive and social initiatives. We conceive and produce strategically branded social network buzz and fan activation for client campaigns in multiple social online and mobile venues.

    Our keen understanding of new Web 2.0/social marketing tactics allow us to reach the super-savvy, online media carnivore in its online media habitat. Currently we execute online strategies that require a well balanced and carefully optimized cross media mix of:

    - Web site and micro site development
    - Site content and messaging creation and optimization
    - Online Marketing: media, ad serving, SEO and SEM
    - Branded Rich Media widgets, applications and iPhone apps
    - User Generated Content (UGC) sites
    - Collaborative Media: Blogs, podcasts and RSS feeds
    - Desktop applications
    - Mobile Marketing: iPhone apps, WAP and SMS

    Most recently we have created successful optimization strategies of branded social network experiences by timing the engagement of the user with a balance of the right value proposition, messaging and brand immersion.

    Results include $20MM+ supplemental ad revenue for a top media powerhouse, massive user adaption of Facebook applications for a major music network and full ROI/performance analytics for each social initiative
