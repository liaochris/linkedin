Jim Bartlett’s Summary

With over 25 years as a Senior Level Retail Executive and an Executive Search Consultant in both Commercial Real Estate Development & Specialty Retail fields, I am excited to provide my expertise to both clients and candidates in the real estate and specialty retail areas. My clients range in size from start-ups to multinational conglomerates.
