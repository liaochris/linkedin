Angela J. Bass’s Summary

Angela J. Bass has seven years of professional experience in print, radio, photojournalism and multimedia journalism.

She is currently studying for her master's degree at UC Berkeley's Graduate School of Journalism (class of 2010) and earned a B.A. in Creative Writing from San Francisco State University (2004).

Her work has appeared in the San Francisco Bay Guardian, Bay Area Business Woman News and Via Magazine, among other national publications. She has also produced news segments for KPFA Radio, a Berkeley, Calif.-based public radio station.

Angela J. Bass’s Specialties:

Fact-checking, multimedia storytelling, photojournalism
