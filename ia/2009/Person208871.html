Sandy Kemsley’s Summary

My first career was an elementary school teacher in Waterford Michigan. I taught at Crescent Lake Elementary school for 22 years and Della Lutes for 2 years. I had a study abroad in England the summer of 1974 where I studied the British Open School which was theme-based. I worked on several committees including Language Arts, Science, Assessments, Parent Math and Science Nights, and more.

I started abcteach LLC in March of 2000. The website, abcteach.com, offers printiable downloads for teachers, parents, homeschoolers, day care workers, ESL studies for a global audience.
