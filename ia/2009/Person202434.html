Roger C. Ambrose’s Experience

  • VP of Creative Design Affairs

    BBTV- Better Black Television

    (Entertainment industry)

    July 2008 — Present (10 months)

    Vice President of Creative Design Affairs @ BBTV

    Creative Director & Head of the Art Dept & Production Design Team, overseeing all aspects of design for the Network.

    BBTV VISION: To create a dynamic, entertaining, and above all, socially responsible programming alternative for the African-American and Multi-Ethnic community through the creation and launch of this new, 24-hour television network designed to entertain, empower and uplift people of color and their communities throughout the world.

    Born in Hollywood, Better Black Television is the brainchild and passion of some of the most powerful, influential, and celebrated entertainers from the Television, Film and Music industries.

    Envisioned and headed by Percy Miller (aka Master P), a legendary music, film, and television entertainer.

    BBTV Advisory Board;
    Will Smith, Denzel Washington, Morgan Freeman, Whoopi Goldberg and Vicangelo Bulluck Ex-Director of the NAACP, Hollywood Bureau.

  • Production Designer-Art Director-Set Designer-Owner

    RCA Designs

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    June 1980 — August 2008 (28 years 3 months)

    As the Designer I establish a vision and then lead a team to complete the job successfully. I am the one focused on design details and quality control of the final "LOOK" on screen. "The Buck Stops Here" and I embrace that responsibility. It is my job to listen to the input from others, the needs of the project and desires of the client. My ultimate responsibility is to the final product, but diplomacy amongst the Team & Client is vital.

    • I am the Designer that imagines things unseen & asks "why not?"
    • I am the Architect that creates the plan & delivers the "blueprint".
    • I am the General Contractor that supervises & gets the job done.
    • I am the Artist or Painter who splashes on the color & adds the flair.
    • I am the Interior Decorator who makes the environment come to life.
    • I am the Coordinator who delivers the work on time & on budget…
    • I am the Production Designer / Art Director for the job.

    Santa Susana Repertory Company

    (Entertainment industry)

    September 1995 — January 2007 (11 years 5 months)

    Resident Designer for Santa Susana Repertory Company at the Forum-Theatre/Civic-Arts-Plaza, Thousand Oaks,CA. Responsible for all theatrical designs & set construction for a variety of theatre productions @ SSRC.

    NOTE of INTEREST: Designed the Premier Production of "Man of La Mancha" as the first production at the GRAND OPENING of the Forum-Theatre/Civic-Arts-Plaza, Thousand Oaks, CA.

  • Head of the Art Department -Senior Staff Art Director

    The Arthur Company

    (Entertainment industry)

    June 1984 — September 1987 (3 years 4 months)

    Senior Staff Art Director & Department Head of the Art Department on 4 National SitComs Broadcasted over WTBS -Turner Broadcasting.

    As staff Art Director, I designed and supervised over 250 episodes for the Turner Broadcasting original sitcom series: "Down to Earth", "Rocky Road" and "Safe at Home" along with designing the sets for "The O'Briens" with Darren McGavin, "Around the Bend" with Heather O'Rourke, "Comedy Challenge" hosted by Ray Combs and the Arthur Company original pilot of "Ace Diamond Private Eye" starring Adam West from the original 60s Batman series.

    The Arthur Company was a subsidiary of MCA Universal / ViaCom.

  • Production Designer--Art Director--Set Designer

    Freelance / Independant Contractor

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    September 1976 — May 1980 (3 years 9 months)

    With over 25 years of professional experience to my credit in Hollywood, I bring to the table a solid and unique background as one who has focused all his academic and professional endeavors in one area of expertise, that of Production Design. As an Art Director of note, in a field of many, I have strived to bring production value to the screen on every project I am associated with. I have received two Emmy Awards for my work, in recognition from my peers for a job well done.

    As a Production Designer / Art Director it is my responsibility to translate my client's vision into a real world environment/set that captures the "theme" or essence of the "story" as in a film and/or television program or in the "product branding" as in a commercial or DVD/Video. It is my job to understand the target audience, interpret the message being sent and communicate that message visually through my designs.
