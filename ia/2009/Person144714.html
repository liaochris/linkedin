Lucie Richards’s Summary

I joined Summit Marketing in December 2008. I was previously with Weber Shandwick where I assisted in the management of five accounts within the retail, hospitality, construction, home improvement and consumer product industries, including consumer/trade media relations, community relations, trade show support and events planning. Additionally, I managed the public relations campaign for J. Christopher’s Restaurants and served as the main day-to-day client contact. I worked with the executives to develop and implement public relations initiatives, draft and pitch press releases and case studies, as well as implement initiatives for support of store openings, franchising initiatives and menu promotions. I also supported multiple account teams in category-leading brands, including The Quikrete Companies, Rooms To Go, Chili’s Grill & Bar, Porter-Cable and Delta Machinery.

Before joining Weber Shandwick, I interned with KPMG in the audit practice, serving mutliple clients, including the City of Detroit. Prior to KPMG, I interned at Aylwin Communications in London, England, where I supported all media relations efforts for numerous clients in the property industry throughout Europe.

I have also served in marketing, promotions and communication roles at the Michigan Foundation for Education Leadership and the Perrigo Company. Additionally, my corporate event involvement includes the Komen Race for the Cure and Make-A-Wish Foundation. I am also a active member of the Public Relations Society of America and serve as a mentor for Michigan State Business college students.

Lucie Richards’s Specialties:

Experienced in integrated communications with expertise in brand management, marketing strategy and campaign implementation, trade and consumer media relations, business and creative writing, event planning and tradeshow support, media planning and client services. I have superior project management skills and have experience with product launches, issues management and have a working understanding of social media tools.
