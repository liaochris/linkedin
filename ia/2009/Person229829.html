Antony Chen’s Summary

Tony is a results-driven IT solutions and problem-solving executive with over 13 years of IT leadership and over 11 years of proven success in areas of IT strategy, business development, enterprise architecture, program management and delivery, and business process engineering in several industry verticals including government, financial services, insurance, automotive, energy, and high tech.

He is currently completing his Masters of Business Administration at the Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan with emphases in strategy and finance. He was a Manager with KPMG LLP in the IT Strategy and Performance Practice specializing in program management and business and IT alignment. At KPMG, he delivered one of largest, global engagements, spanning 15 countries.

In the past, he used his entrepreneurial spirit to start up his own IT consulting firm that serviced the needs of small to mid-sized businesses in the Metro Detroit area. The venture grew to over $1 million in revenues over three years before he sold it to take an opportunity at a firm looking to start their own project-based business. Over three years, he grew their book of project-related business from under $100,000 to over $4 million.
