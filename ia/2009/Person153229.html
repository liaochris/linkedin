Marsha A. Ostrer’s Summary

Since 1983, Marsha A. Ostrer has been the founder and president of Ostrer & Associates International, a conflict resolution training and consulting firm headquartered in Chatham-by-the-sea, Massachusetts. See http://www.all-things-conflict-resolution-and-adr.com/About-Us-Conflict-Resolution-Services-and-Trainings. html.
She is also the owner and developer of the website http://www.all-things-conflict-resolution-and-adr.com/index.html.

A lawyer, mediator and trainer, she is experienced in creating and implementing customized, skills based training programs. See,
http://www.all-things-conflict-resolution-and-adr.com/Our-Trainings.html. A skillful neutral, she specializes in entering and defusing highly charged conflicts through a targeted mix of customized training and consulting. See, http://www.all-things-conflict-resolution-and-adr.com/Our-Mediation-Services.html. Her trainings and services consistently exceed customer expectations as evidenced by her consistent trainings ratings of 4.6+ (out of 5) and testimonials from satisfied clients, http://www.all-things-conflict-resolution-and-adr.com/Testimonials.html. She is a strong strategic thinker and team builder.

Starting in 1987, after years of training and consulting for U.S. clients, see http://www.all-things-conflict-resolution-and-adr.com/Partial-Client-List.html, OAI entered the international market providing targeted conflict resolution training and consulting services to a host of international clients including the Zambia Privitization Agency, U.S.A.I.D., Artibrazh, the Russian civil court, the South African Ministry for Restructuring, the Malawi Privitization Program, and U.N.I.T.A. Several of those assignments were highly charged conflicts which Ms. Ostrer was able to successfully enter and defuse through a customized mix of training, consulting and neutral services.
