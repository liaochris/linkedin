Darren Todd, CFP®’s Summary

Todd Financial & Insurance Services, Inc. is a personal producing general agency that originated as a sole-proprietorship and was incorporated in February 2004. The agency serves as the Health Insurance Specialists for the San Diego office of Progressive Financial Concepts, a general agency of New England Financial, a MetLife company; the Pollakov Financial Group, a general agency of Mass Mutual; the Guardian Life - San Diego Agency; B.B. Graham & Co. in Orange, CA; and Western International Securities, Inc. in Pasadena, CA.

The agency services its own retail clients as well as the clients of approximately 40 other financial professionals.

Darren Todd, CFP®’s Specialties:

Employee Benefits Consulting; Designing and Implenting Benefit Banks for Employees to Prudently use their Benefit Dollars to best meet their own Family's Needs; Expert in Health Savings Accounts (HSA); Flexible Savings Accounts (FSA); and Health Reimbursement Arrangements (HRA).
