Jenny Evans’s Summary

Jenny Evans is an internationally recognized expert on stress, exercise physiology, nutrition and performance. Her dynamic presentations inspire, entertain and educate Fortune 500 audiences world wide to increase their capacity for stress and to discover how to recover from stress more quickly and effectively. Clients improve their performance and productivity, all while enhancing their health.

Stress hurts you personally and professionally - it costs the U.S. economy more than $200 billion per year. Companies need to run leaner and meaner, yet health care costs continue to rise. People are busier and more stretched for time than ever, making a healthy lifestyle difficult to achieve. How much does stress cost you?

As a solution Jenny Evans has developed PowerHouse Hit the Deck™, which can be accompanied by a 1-3 hour keynote on how to increase your personal and professional threshold for stress, as well as improve your recovery and resiliency in regards to stress.

PowerHouse Hit the Deck™ (www.powerhousehitthedeck.com) consists of an interval timer and 35 cards that provide a comprehensive cardiovascular and resistance training program at the same time, making your workout more efficient and effective. This workout can be done any time, anywhere, with no fitness equipment needed - it's perfect for the traveling executive or anyone who can't workout at the gym.
