Robert Gunning’s Summary

Our office is located in the new River Bluff Complex in Palm Bluffs at 8050 N. Palm Avenue, Suite 316. Fresno, CA 93711.
Founded in 1955, Wedbush Morgan Securities is a member of the New York Stock Exchange and one of the largest private, independent brokerage firms in the United States. Headquartered in Los Angeles, the firm specializes in providing wealth management, brokerage and advisory services to both institutional and private clients. The Private Client division has over 80 regional offices and over 100 nationwide correspondent offices.

Wedbush is uniquely positioned to deliver unbiased financial and investment advice through its in-house research and research provided by Credit Suisse. We provide the individual investor a wide array of services and investment vehicles to deal with today's complex financial environment.
Ultimately the value we bring to our clients is a combination of trust, expertise, and understanding of their needs. With all that we help our clients achieve their financial goals and provide them with peace of mind.
