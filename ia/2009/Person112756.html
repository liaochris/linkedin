Jill Triantis’s Summary

My experience spans 17+ years at the levels of sales, manager and proprietor in the fields of banking, retail and hospitality.

Banking: I worked in the Consumer Service and Trust Departments for two years. My time in the Trust department concentrated on overseeing all trades made by our investment group, confirming all transactions, balancing with holding bank, guaranteeing all local financial exchanges went forth without issue.
