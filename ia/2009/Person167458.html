Mohammed Johmani’s Summary

Mohammed Johmani is the entrepreneur founder of the award winning O2 Network Multi-talented, ambitious and passionate about business, Johmani is a professional at juggling the demands of managing three different company branches that has billed over 20 Million U$D in 3 years, while planning for regional expansion and global reach initiatives.

Johmani started his academic career at the Sydney University of Technology. He is a Chartered Marketer, holding professional postgraduate marketing diplomas from the Chartered Institute of Marketing, the global champion of best market practice. This institute is the leading international professional marketing body, designed to build the skills of marketing practitioners based on the latest, most rigorous British and European curricula. Currently, Mohammed is working towards his MBA in Business Administration at the University of Strathclyde.

A rising star, Johmani began his career as an Art Director; he readily progressed to become a Marketing Consultant, and then, the key Account Director at local and regional advertising companies. Johmani established O2 Network in Dubai on December 4th 2005, with a clear vision for a research-based marketing communications firm that would grow to be the first local agency with the strength to become a global player in the marketing communications global industry.

With a sharp eye for talented and dedicated individuals, Johmani has been integral to the success of the exceptionally creative O2 Network. This young company has earned several awards in just 3 short years, wining the prestigious title of Best Marketing and Promotion Business by HH Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai and have been awarded “Best PR Agency” fro 2007 and 2008 by the CNBC Arabian Property Awards, and “Best Interactive agency” by CNBC in 2008, and O2 Network’s success is on the rise.
