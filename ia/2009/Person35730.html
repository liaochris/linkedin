Brian Bundesen’s Summary

REALTOR and Partner in The Bundesen Team at Real Estate One in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

Consider us as your "Go-To" Real Estate Team for residential or investment real estate in Southeast Michigan. We pride ourselves on being educating real estate professionals, keeping you fully informed every step of the way through your transaction.

I have more than 25 years experience and success encompassing Sales, Marketing, Communications, and Operations of both small and large organizations.

Our simple goal is to help nice people buy and sell real estate.
