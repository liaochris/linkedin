Olivier Van Wijk’s Summary

My interest stretches towards art in the public space and making art accessible to the general public without compromising the professional maker and his work.
For the fortunate people I'm always on the look out for new pieces which can be a supreme addition to their collection.

Olivier Van Wijk’s Specialties:

- government liaison regarding art in the public space
- maintaining contact with architects, project developers and city planners
- maintaining contacts with clients & artists internationally.
