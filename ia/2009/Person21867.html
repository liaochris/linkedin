Nancy Astrid Lindo’s Summary

We are a full service interior design firm focused on building sustainable spaces. In viewing the scope of a project on a holistic level - from the core of the building, to the materials, through the interiors and out to the landscape.

By understanding various elements that compose of residential and commercial projects, we understand that each project has a unique opportunity to perform to its highest standard.

We strive to constantly to be on the cusp of all latest innovations and technologies.

As the principal of [ADS], design has always been a thriving part of my life. I have had the opportunity to explore realms of design that have always intrigued me, such as production design, photography and fashion. It is in this work, that I have been educated in a variety of skills achieving an aptitude that distinguishes my abilities.

My broad education, experience, and travels have provided me with a profound understanding of culture and aesthetic. I continuously experiment in other mediums from photography to visual art.

Regenerative sustainability is the focus that I have been involved in for several years. Since, completing the Green Building certification program at Sonoma State University, being LEED accredited and having received a certification in Permaculture has enabled me to take a new approach into living systems, design, and technology.

Educating people on how to live and work in more sustainable ways has been a critical factor in my inspiration to help others to live a more organic lifestyle.

  • Co-Founder

    Greening From Within

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    December 2007 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    “Greening from Within” is the practice of increasing the efficiency with which we use natural resources on Earth (i.e. energy, water) by first focusing on how we utilize our own personal supplies of energy (i.e. physical/mental/emotional). The integrity in which we use our own personal resources directly corresponds to the way in which we use environmental resources.
    “Greening from Within” offers a fresh, new perspective, a way of life that serves as a roadmap towards harmoniously living in a fluid state of natural regeneration of those precious resources (inner and outer) for the highest good of the individual, planet Earth, and humanity at large.
    This perspective offers the possibility that the Earth is actually a reflection of the individual. For example,, how is the depletion of the Earth’s resources reflecting the depletion of the individual?
    True sustainability starts from within.
