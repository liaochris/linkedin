Randall Petersen’s Summary

I have been working in the Internet world 11 years, programming and managing world-class websites in both a marketing and technical capacity. Currently I am managing Gannett Broadcast's family of websites from KARE and WZZM TV, to smoothly get news, video and up-to-the-minute weather to as many as 120,000 unique visitors daily.

I have an interest in new web technologies, new web trends and new media delivery platforms. I really enjoy finding ways to incorporate new technologies into our existing structure to attract more users and better serve our users' needs.
