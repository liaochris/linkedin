Arvind Thanu’s Experience

  • Co-Founder

    TheStorez.com Online Bookstore

    (Retail industry)

    January 2007 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    TheStorez.com (http://www.thestorez.com) is an Indian Online Bookstore, which was launched for Indian book readers during September 2008. I and Vasan were working on understanding book retail trade in India from as early as Jan 2007. We started this firm in Jan 2008 and were working together with the book industry for almost 9 months before launching our website. The response to our bookstore has been good so far, and we are working hard on a huge list of things to improvise our bookstore.

    Freedom and a huge passion to do what we like drives us crazy. It has been a great entrepreneurial journey so far and are confident that our passion and commitment will take us to much greater heights in the near future.

  • Product Manager

    CavinKare Private Limited

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Consumer Goods industry)

    June 2006 — January 2008 (1 year 8 months)

    I was a Product Manager, handing New Product Development initiatives in the Hair Care Division. Responsibilities involved heading a cross-functional team involving R&D, Purchase, Operations, Advertising Agency, Packaging, Logistics to formulate concepts of new consumer goods, implement them to produce the final goods, and launch them. Brands I handled involved: Karthika Shampoo for Andhra Pradesh, Meera Chemparuthi Thali for Kerala Market, Spinz Hair Colour, Meera Coconut Oil, Nyle Hair Oil. This stint has been one of the most valuable experiences for me in my career as it had involved extensive traveling to a huge number of towns and meeting consumers to understand what they needs. All these insights gained will then find its way into our formulating and communicating product concepts. It was a good company to work for and it has been a great career experience to be associated with CavinKare's brand management team.
