Charlie Dearborn’s Summary

Experienced mechanical/electromechanical technician/engineer with over 25 years in industry..
Performed all aspects of 1200PSI ships boiler overhaul. Led a crew of 18 people for 18 months for a boiler overhaul.

Worked as an Outside Machinist setting up and aligning remote milling equipment to machine steel foundations used to mount guns, missile systems and radar platforms.
Fabricated by cutting, welding, drilling and burning catwalks and deckplates. Certified as deck supervisor and crane operator for the Vertical Launch Missile systems.

Enhanced system throughput on high speed paper converting equipment such as napkin folders (5000 napkins per minute), roll towel rewinders, tissue folders and high speed wrapping and packaging equipment.

Trained and certified as as internal auditor to audit for compliance to QS9000 standards.
Brought a Corporate Semiconductor Reliability Lab compliant with JEDEC standards utilized for device qualification.
Worked with internal customers to insure timely qualification of new products and/or processes. This involved equipment needs assessment, bias circuit board requirements, electrical test readiness and procurement of all material to accomplish the qualification.
Worked with product development personnel to insure capabilities for future technologies.
Traveled to company and customer sites (foreign country) on as little as one days notice. Each customer visit saved Fairchild over $400k.
Trained on numerous Failure Analysis Techniques and tools for device deprocessing.
Certified Universal Refrigeration Technician
State of Maine Certified Solar Thermal Installer
