Mark Gallagher’s Summary

Brand Expressionist® Mark Gallagher has a passion for brands and branding. In 1994 he and partner Laura Savard founded the brand expression consultancy Blackcoffee®. Their company helps organizations unlock value trapped within their brands and business by clarifying, simplifying and amplifying their brand stories.

As a brand expression consultancy Blackcoffee is equal parts management consultancy and brand design agency, so the solutions Mark and his company develop aren’t bound to any single discipline. In helping clients bridge the gap between promise and expectation Blackcoffee crosses traditional boundaries to create multi-sensory experiences that bring brands to life.
