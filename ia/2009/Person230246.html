Ted Stravers’s Summary

Ted is a marketing executive with a broad experience in ICT. He worked in international ICT companies (American, Swedish, Dutch and Australian companies) for several years gaining experience in channelmarketing and business development. He developed and implemented channel programs and is able to boost channel sales for growing companies into EMEA.
