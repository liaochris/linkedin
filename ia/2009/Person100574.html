Ivy Mak’s Summary

I see graphic design as the perfect way to communicate ideas. I grew up in Hong Kong and studied graphic design in San Francisco. Derived from living in two cultures, I learned the most simple way to express my idea to people with different backgrounds via Graphic Design. My Design motto is to create designs that are simple enough but yet able to deliever the most important message to the user.

see http://www.ivymak.com for online portfolio
