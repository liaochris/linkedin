Mehul Shah’s Summary

Mehul Shah is the founder of XS Online Ltd (http://www.xs-business.com) He is a professional web development guru speciallising in the design, development and maintenance and hosting of website solutions. He actively works with small and medium sizes companies in the UK to give them a web presecense or improve their curent solution.

XS Online's Step by Step Website Development process is designed to, as well as get the online, save them time, money and headache in the process. Through XS Online, ML International (http://www.mlinternational.com) and the Gold Table Business Club, Mehul is working with and building a network of entrepreneurs, business people and product or service providers. Finally his most ambitious recent project is to start an online e-franchise(http://www.uk-warehouse.net).

Known for his professional and open approach to business, Mehul quickly discovers if he can help a client or if he can refer them on to another business contact. His ideas and knowledge about design as well as sales and marketing have layed the foundations of XS Online.

While working for the Council for Ethnic Minority Communities (CEMC) and the UK Consumer Protection Agency, Mehul developed his professional sales and leadership skills; these have been invaluable when starting in business. Having completed his BA (Hons) degree at Kingston University and his formal training for the UK Consumer Protection Agency, Mehul decided to concentrate on his business full time.

Mehul is an active member of his local Esporta gym, he is an active business promoter for ML International Ltd, and has recently joined the Gold Table Business Club. He is also an active member of Xing, Linkedin, MySpace, Eblogger and Ebay.co.uk.

In his spare time, Mehul enjoys reading, travelling (especially on Safaris to Kenya, his parents native country), listen to music and developing new business contacts. He now lives in North London, and his company is based in Southgate.
