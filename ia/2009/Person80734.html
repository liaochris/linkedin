Francisco J. Barragan CPA, CIA’s Summary

As the head of Internal Audit/Chief Audit Exec (CAE) at 4 multinational manufacturing companies (3 publicly traded and 1 privately held) successfully led and established Internal Audit departments. Reporting to Senior Management and directly to the Board/and Audit Committee Chairman since 1998.
CAE at a pharmaceutical (4 yrs): CAE at Biotech (2 yrs); CAE at a Constructions materials company (1 1/2 yrs); CAE (2 yrs) at Health and Beauty Products direct seller and Multi-Level Marketing (MLM) manufacturer.
Also led and managed co-sourced internal audit teams domestically and internationally from 3 of the Big 4.
Led the SOX section 404 teams, periodically reporting progress to the Board.
Implemented worldwide Ethics Hotlines.
Led Oracle pre-implementation reviews.
Conducted chemical plant manufacturing audits (traveling 90% of time for 3 yrs) - Earned Award from auditee (unheard of for an auditor).
Worked for Deloitte ( 1992-1994) as external auditor in San Francisco, CA

Trilingual. Traveled to 28 countries and 22 states.

President (2007-2009) - ALPFA (Association of Latino Professionals in Finance and Accounting) OC Chapter.

President (2005-2006) - The IIA (Institute of Internal Auditors) OC Chapter.

Founding Board Member; Treasurer, and Chair of Business/Entrepreneur events for TECMA-OC (Technology, Entrepreneurship and Culture Mexican Association).

US Marine Corps (1987-1994) - Top Honor Graduate from MOS school
CA Army National Guard (1994-1997)
