Mike Langton’s Summary

Corporate leader and sleeves-up entrepreneur. Digital online, mobile and tech-smart. Believes nobody worth working with can tolerate being managed. Only mentors and coaches add value.

Founding entrepreneur in 11 start-ups in advertising, digital marketing solutions, direct marketing, events, sponsorship and promotions in China, India, Korea, Singapore, Thailand, Philippines, Indonesia, Cambodia, Vietnam and South Africa. 5 market leading companies created in the process.

Corporate roles as President, CEO or Regional Head of Asia-Pacific networks of up to 20 offices in digital, direct marketing, promotion, events, sponsorship & retail marketing. Restructured and turned around both single market companies and large regional groups.

Mike Langton’s Specialties:

Making Ideas Make Money. Helping entrepreneurs turn new ideas into successful start-ups and established companies deliver greater profits through innovation and corporate entrepreneurship.
