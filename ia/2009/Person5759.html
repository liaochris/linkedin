Akesh Bhatnagar’s Summary

• I started my career as a Guest Services Representative at The Crowne Plaza Manhattan, New York in 1992.
• I then joined the Taj Mahal Palace & Towers, Mumbai as Duty Manager in 1994. I was with The Taj Group for 10 years. Last position held was as Accommodations Manager at The Jai Mahal Palace, Jaipur.
• In 2004 I joined Carlson’s first managed Hotel in India at Radisson Varanasi as General Manager, leading the Hotel for over 3 years.
• Having excelled at Rooms Division I have a broad experience in all aspects of Operations. My professional skills encompass Training & Development, Team Building, Guest Relations, Concept Development, Strategic Management, Retention Marketing & Loyalty Programs; Including Financial Controls & Revenue Generation and of course Renovation & Project Management.
• I have close to 18 years of experience working in some of the most renowned hotel corporations such as the Welcomgroup Sheraton, Crowne Plaza, Taj Group (Luxury & Leisure) and Radisson Hotels.
• I have proven ability to exceed and implement the hospitality standards and procedures while assisting and directing the Team.
• I have worked with a Leading Real Estate Developer, RMZ Corp heading the Hospitality Vertical.
• I aspire to work as Senior General Manager / Vice President / Director- Operations to be able to constantly endeavor towards driving the Hospitality vertical of the company in all spheres of work to enhance the organizational growth as well as increase personal augmentation.
