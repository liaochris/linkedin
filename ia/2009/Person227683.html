Sven Scheffers’s Summary

My name is Sven Scheffers and I'm a photographer. I have over 20 years of experience in photography. Mostly self-educated and last years I followed a study at the Fotovakschool (School of professional photograpy). I'm the owner of Sven Scheffers Fotografie. My company offers photographic solutions for the professional market. Our specialties are:

- Stock photography for companies
- Portraits of employees
- Air to Air photography (company aircrafts etc.)
- Air to Ground photography
- 360 degrees panoramic photography etc.

Besides a passioned photographer, I'm also a great aviation enthousiast. Started with reading aviation magazines and bringing visits to airfields and aviation museums. But later I combined both Photography and Aviation and started to take aviation related images. Nowadays I'm not only like taking pictures from aircraft, I also have a great passion to fly them. In 2007 I managed to get my pilots licence on a Touring Motor Glider, and want to get a rating for Single Engine Piston aircraft in the near future.

More information about me and my company can be found at my website: http://www.svenscheffers.com
