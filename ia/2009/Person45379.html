Christopher Overdorf, ASLA’s Experience

  • Principal / Partner

    Jones & Jones Architects & Landscape Architects

    (Architecture & Planning industry)

    April 2001 — Present (8 years 6 months)

    Jones & Jones is an award-winning architecture, landscape architecture and planning firm based in Seattle Washington. Our work cannot be categorized stylistically, for it is rooted in a profound respect for and celebration for the nature and culture of place. Our work is as diverse as the places it celebrates. Our practice focuses on five main areas: living places, living cultures, natural infrastructure, wildlife immersions and regions & communities.

  • VP Product Development

    Tour Graphics

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Graphic Design industry)

    April 1999 — April 2001 (2 years 1 month)

    Tour Graphics was a GPS-mapping company based in Bothell Washington that I formed with my brother and several other partners. We mapped golf courses with GPS collectors and then used the digital information to develop scorecards, yardage books, as-builts, 3D computer-generated animations and websites.
