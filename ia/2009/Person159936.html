Meri Gruber’s Summary

Competing on Execution helps companies innovate by applying the core principle of successful execution to their business: Companies with a clear and simple set of values perform better if these values are put to work everyday, with every decision, every customer and every time. And companies must do this in an era of customer engagement and expanding social networks.

Competing on Execution is led by Meri Gruber. Meri is an innovative high-tech industry veteran and an expert in Business Execution.

Previously, Meri held leadership positions including co-Founder, Vice-President Business Development, and interim CEO at high-tech start-ups. She also held senior roles at Fortune 100 global companies including Intel and Total where she was a recognized innovator of integrated business and systems solutions.

Meri is an Entrepreneur in Residence for INSEAD’s Entrepreneurship Network, a mentor for the Clean Tech Open and runs a collaboration community (pro-bono) for the Taproot Foundation .

She holds a BSEE Honors from the University of Washington and an MBA from INSEAD
