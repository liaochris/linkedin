    First Finacial Group's holistic financial planning methodology allows you to exceed the results that traditional needs-based plans have yielded. Our goal is simple - design a plan to achieve your hopes and dreams that works under all conditions.

    Your wealth-building road map is designed around our commitment to:
    - Analyze your financial needs and desires
    - Assess your financial options
    - Advise on sound, fully-integrated financial strategies
    - Implement programs that will grow wealth–and improve your life
    - Surpass your expectations

    Put our team to work to unleash the optimum mix of financial strategies to create, accumulate, utilize and protect your wealth.

    Dave Guido joined First Financial Group in 2008 after 15 years in mortgage and commercial banking. Dave and his wife Julie had been introduced to the firm through a close friend and realized it was a natural fit to join the firm. He saw firsthand the benefits of having a financial roadmap to work off of both in good times and tough times over the years.

    Dave has a tremendous passion for guiding people and takes great pride in the impact that strategic planning has on his clients. He takes a holistic view on planning and does not come to his clients with any preconceived notions. Dave carefully evaluates client's goals and creates a blueprint that gives every client control over their financial future.

    A graduate of Virginia Tech, Dave earned his BS in Marketing/Management. He is the father of three daughters – Emma, Grace & Olivia. He loves to play golf and spend time with his family.
