Kevin Graham’s Summary

The launch of EmpowerMePhoto.com is a new chapter in my professional career.

EmpowerMePhoto.com is in the business of providing prospects and customers inspiration through a vision of their future self, thereby empowering the necessary changes in their life to stay on course and to complete the transformation to their new self.

Previously, I served in the Tech Sector for nearly 2 decades, with my career progressing from wholesale distribution, vendor channel sales, oem markets, and the last 5 years in solution selling with new technologies.

I've managed teams of over 100 people and revenues in excess of $1B per annum.

Prior to tech sector, I held a Series 7 (Full Stockbroker Registration) and I have been a California Real Estate Broker for over 20 years.

My award-winning sales experience dates back to childhood, when I won my first legitimate sales contest at the age of 7, followed by a regional Scripps Howard award at the age of 15.

I have obtained President's Club (or similar status) in 3 different Fortune 500 companies, as well as 'Club' within Kofax.

Personally, I have been fortunate to work with at-risk youth as a volunteer for the last 20 years. My work in youth sports has included extensive training and certifications from a variety of nationally recognized programs and associations.

I was honored to play soccer in a National Championship at the collegiate level, and I carried the Olympic Torch enroute to the 1996 Atlanta games.

My wife of 15+ years continues to tolerate me, and my wonderful children are now teenagers. It remains to be seen whether their tolerance will remain strong in the exciting years ahead.

Born in the 'Show Me State' as the son of a preacher, I was fortunate to relocate and grow up in Pittsburgh where 2 things are deeply engrained: the love of the mighty Pittsburgh Steelers and a work ethic that is rarely surpassed. The last 15 years have included 4 corporate relocations.
