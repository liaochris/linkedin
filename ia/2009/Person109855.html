Jennifer Simpson-Black’s Summary

S & B Consulting Group is a direct hire headhunting firm that specializes in Technical, Accounting, Finance, and Medical Professionals. Our clients consist of Fortune 500 Companies and Hospitals Nationwide. Our Recruiters, Sales Executives and Partners consist of individuals who have been in the staffing and placement industry for the last Fifteen years.


This is how we are different:

For our clients we specialize in hard to find skill sets and are currently working with several Large DFW Companies. We look forward to the opportunity to meet with you to discuss what we can bring to the table that is different from other staffing firms. Please note we do not use Monster, CareerBuilder or Dice. We are good old fashion headhunters! We also are able to solicit for Talent from companies you can not reach out to.

For our candidates with a background in recruiting, we employ rigorous search techniques for all positions. We focus on a specific industry which enables us to become intimately familiar with the field, the market and the most qualified and passive candidates. We don't run ads for positions, garden the Internet, or interview every candidate who contacts us for jobs. We rely on user groups and referrals.

We would like thank everyone who applies and sends us a resume. Due to the large volume of candidates it may take some time to get with everyone. We will only contact you if you meet a specific position. We do not build large databases of candidates. We realize your time is valuable as well.

Call Us Today!
All career searches are kept confidential. Referral fees for any clinical position or candidate referral.
