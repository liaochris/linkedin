Michael DelGaudio’s Summary

My motivation for designing and creating technology products is to better understand how humans interact with technology in their daily lives. I have a fundamental belief in the user-centered design process and consider it to be one of my core design values. My approach comes from a strong background in visual design, including typography, composition, information hierarchy and color theory. I am highly capable technically and able to develop interactive web sites, web-based software and UI prototypes using Flash, DHTML and frameworks such as Rails. Looking to the physical, I am able to produce hardware/software prototypes using Arduino and Processing.
