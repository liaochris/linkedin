Dean Butcher’s Experience

  • Strategic Design Manager

    Samsung Design Europe

    (Public Company; Design industry)

    October 2005 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    Samsung Design Europe (SDE) is a mid-sized satellite Design Studio based in central London. Set up in 2000 the Studio’s primary mandate was to feed Samsung’s Corporate Design Studio in Seoul with innovative product design solutions, focusing mainly on Eurocentric design languages. In recent years the studio has expanded it’s core disciplines to include User Interaction Design, Ethnographic Research as well as Consumer and Visual Trends Analysis.

    Since Joining SDE in 2005 my role as a Senior Creative and Project Manager has allowed me to work for many of Samsung’s client divisions. As project manager my key tasks are to facilitate the successful delivery of design projects with particular emphasis in the areas of Creative Direction for product design and strategic direction. Additionally this role also includes project team building, budgeting & scheduling as well as client relationship management.

  • Senior Designer

    KEiD London

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Design industry)

    October 2004 — September 2005 (1 year)

    KEiD was established as the European Design Center for Kelon Domestic Appliances, one of the largest manufacturers of white goods
    in China. The primary role of the office was to provide design solutions appropriate for export to Europe and the United States. In addition to hands on product design and project management my role with KEiD was to bring the experience of designing for specific markets and consumer segments to the team and business alike. Liaising with our parent company in China in all areas of product development.

  • Senior Designer

    Philips Design

    (Public Company; Design industry)

    January 1999 — May 2004 (5 years 5 months)

    At Philips Design I was responsible for leading a variety of projects from initial start up to market introduction. I was involved in the total product creation process from research and concept generation to global
    market testing and product implementation. I was also responsible for the development of product identity and design strategy projects for the Consumer Electronics and Philishave business groups. Key areas of my responsibilities were:

    • Producing and Presenting Concept Directions to Product Management
    • Managing Creative Workshop Sessions in Concept Phases
    • Overseeing Qualitative and Quantitative Market Research activities
    • Developing Concept Directions in 3D for Rendering, Model Making and Soft Tooling
    • Providing 3D Surface Data for Product Development
    • Overseeing Project Capacity in User Interface, Graphic and Packaging Design disciplines
    • Overseeing and Directing Materials & Finishing Specifications
