IndiaMART InterMESH’s Summary

IndiaMART.com (www.indiamart.com) is India's largest online B2B marketplace. It is the proud recipient of the Red Herring 100 Asia Award in 2008. It was also honored with the Emerging India Award in 2008. IndiaMART.com brings buyers and suppliers together through business directories (www.dir.indiamart.com), online product catalogs (www.catalogs.indiamart.com), buy-sell offers (www.trade.indiamart.com), industry specific marketplaces, sourcing guides (www.indiamart.com/sourcing-guides/) and trade shows (www.biztradeshows.com) participation. Established in 1996, IndiaMART.com is an ISO 9001:2000 certified company with state-of-the-art web-development center which is one of the largest in the world. With approx 1000 employees covering a network of 100 cities pan-India, IndiaMART.com offers value-added services to its 500,000 members and over 5 million global buyers.

IndiaMART.com has the enviable track record of being the only profitable Indian dot com company since launch. IndiaMART's success has been widely acknowledged by media such as CNBC, BBC, Business Money, CNN, Businessworld, Economic Times, Financial Express, etc. Existing investors are Intel Capital and Bennett, Coleman & Co. Ltd. (Times Group), India's largest media group.

For more information, please visit www.indiamart.com/press-section
