Kerry O' Sullivan’s Summary

Established in 1995, Go West Conference & Event Management is owner managed by Kerry O’Sullivan and Kathleen Mc Donagh. Go West provides three strands of professional Event Management Services:

1. Go West is approved by Fáilte Ireland as a Professional Conference Organiser and has organised conferences for Organisations in the following sectors:

Association
Educational
Medical &
Public Sector

Previous conferences include: Cuisle Beatha 2007 & 2009, ECIS 2008, Comhairle National Conference 2005 & 2007.

2. Go West provide Event Management Services to organisations in the public and private sector.

Previous events include:

Údarás Na Gaeltachta – Gradam Gno Award Ceremony & Live Television Broadcast 2007 & 2008.

3. Go West provide Destination Management Services to Corporate and Special Interest Groups visiting Ireland.

The benefits of the service that we provide include:

A reduction in a client’s workload as we provide solutions and deliver results.
Our experience and knowledge reduces stress and eliminates risk.
Clients benefit from lower costs achieved through our buying power and negotiating skills.
We are constantly introducing innovative new ways to run conferences, ideas for events and tours.
We go the extra mile and strive to exceed a client’s expectations.

Achievements

We have worked on over 50 successful conferences, organised 100’s of World Class events and attracted over 350 international groups to Ireland with innovative and imaginative itineraries.

Go West work on behalf of Conference Ambassadors with the potential to attract International Conferences to the Country. We provide them with solutions and support giving them the confidence to run their Conference here. The services that we provide have encouraged Multinationals and other Organisations to run Events which otherwise would not have taken place in Ireland.

Kerry O' Sullivan’s Specialties:

Creating and planning memorable itineraries for overseas corporate visitors to Ireland.
Managing professional and innovative conferences including web design and online registration.
Imaginative events with top class entertainment and stylish room decor.
