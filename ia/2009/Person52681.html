Dana Romano’s Summary

As VP of Business Development at Interactive Blend, Dana focuses on creating strategic partnerships in media, PR, and marketing. She integrates her Fortune 500 experience with a start up environment to bring together structure and innovation.

Previously, she was a Regional Sales Manager for a division of Mars, Inc where she led and managed a regionally deployed sales team and territory spanning the Eastern United States.

Dana is particularly adept at maintaining and growing consumer confidence during volatile times through strategic business planning and superior customer service. Because of her executive recruiting experience early in her career she is also skilled at sourcing, recruiting, and on boarding top talent to build a great team that commands industry presence. This winning combination of skills drives sales results and provides a powerful customer facing advantage for her clients.

Dana has her B.S. from Centenary College.
