Robert Kraft’s Summary

Attorney and Owner -- Injury, Disability, Immigration & Bankruptcy lawyers. I have been a lawyer in Dallas, Texas since 1971, representing injured and disabled clients. Kraft & Associates has six attorneys and more than twenty staff members. We have helped tens of thousands of clients over the years.

We represent clients in the fields of personal injury, Social Security disability, Immigration, Bankruptcy, and Consumer Protection.

Robert Kraft’s Experience

  • Owner

    Kraft & Associates, Attorneys - Personal Injury, Social Security Disability, and Immigration Law

    (Law Practice industry)

    April 1999 — Present (10 years 6 months)

    Kraft & Associates is a Dallas, Texas law firm representing people who have suffered personal injuries, who are making claims for Social Security disability, who have immigration questions, or who have financial issues requiring a consumer protection lawyer or bankruptcy lawyer.

  • Partner

    (Law Practice industry)

    January 1988 — March 1999 (11 years 3 months)

    Kraft & Johnson was a Dallas, Texas law firm representing people who suffered personal injuries and people making Social Security disability claims. The firm became Kraft & Associates in 1999.

  • Partner

    Eakins & Kraft

    (Law Practice industry)

    October 1979 — December 1987 (8 years 3 months)

    Eakins & Kraft was a Dallas, Texas law firm representing people who suffered personal injuries and people making Social Security disability claims. The firm became Eakins, Kraft & Johnson, then Kraft & Johnson in 1987, and Kraft & Associates in 1999.
