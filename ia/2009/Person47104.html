Claudio Monari’s Summary

My professional path can be synthetically summarized as a 360° experience that has touched all marketing aspects, including corporate and product branding, product research and development, organization and communications in an international context and diversified scenarios. These 27 years of work activity and experience have been characterized by the need to promote, implement and manage processes of change and innovation, often radical, in consolidated and complex situations.
