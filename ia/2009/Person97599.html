Mohammad Al-Ubaydli’s Summary

me@mo.md

Physician - graduated in 2000 from Cambridge University with Class 1 degree.

Entrepreneur - founded HandheldsForDoctors.com, Idiopathic Publishing, Medical Approaches (a non-profit), and Medical Futures Ltd. Now running Patients Know Best.

Programmer - since 1995, languages include Java, Perl and PHP.

Author - wrote four books, including "Handheld computers for doctors" (www.handheldsfordoctors.com). Freelance journalist including several articles for the British Medical Journal.
