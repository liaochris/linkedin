Simon Coles’s Summary

Simon is a business transformation consultant with 25 years' experience working with UK central government departments and across many industry sectors at home and abroad (principally Holland, Greece, Denmark, Norway and Russia.) He has particular skills in Information Technology, Transformation/Change Management and Performance Improvement and has completed projects for more than 60 clients, with turnovers ranging from £2 million to £2 billion. Simon works with organisations undergoing major change and has designed and implemented operating models and processes in core business and support functions, including Shared Services and Business Process Outsourcing.

Prior to launching Tiro Consulting Simon consulted with Xansa, Oracle and Coopers & Lybrand. He has combined a consulting career with direct spells in industry, including Business Systems Director with the former BET plc and a number of interim management roles.

Simon Coles’s Specialties:

Change planning and management, business process re-engineering, shared services, business process outsourcing, project management, stakeholder management, client engagement, investment appraisal, IS strategy, staff management, business development, IS effectiveness improvement, interim management.
