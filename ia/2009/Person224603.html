Steven King’s Summary

Mr. King is the Grants and Capture Management Specialist for Appriss, Inc. In that position he identifies potential sources of funding for Appriss customers from federal, State and private sources and evaluates the appropriateness of grant funding relative to Appriss' products.

Mr. King serves as the Proposal Manager for developing bid proposals for government and priviate projects. This includes managing and directing all proposal efforts and personnel.

Finally, Mr. King provides research and analysis of the various competitors of Appriss' products.

Steven King’s Specialties:

grants management, government liaison, competitive analysis, proposal management

    (Government Administration industry)

    June 2005 — March 2007 (1 year 10 months)

    Mr. King served as the administrator for federal criminal justice grants that included, but was not limited to: Edward Byrne Justice Assistance Grant, National Criminal History Improvement Program Grant, and COPS Methamphetamine Initiative Grant.

  • Southwest Regional Manager

    (Government Administration industry)

    July 2001 — May 2005 (3 years 11 months)

    As the Southwest Regional Manager for the Governor's Commission for a Drug-Free Indiana Mr. King worked directly with Local Coordinating Councils at the county level to identify and address substance abuse problems using identified local, state and federal resources. This included assisting the LCCs in the development, implementation and reporting on annual Comprehensive Community Plans.

    During the 2003 and 2004 Infiana General Assembly, Mr. King tracked legislative issues and provided a weekly newsletter highlighting action on substance abuse related legislation.
