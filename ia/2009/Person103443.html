Jim Amos’s Summary

Seasoned Front-end Web Developer. Natural problem solver and excellent communicator. Passionate about writing smart, clean code as well as a keen interest in marketing, business, and customer relationships through Social Media. User Experience is my main priority because ultimately it makes for a better web and also increases ROI for business. Everyone wins.

6 years experience developing Web Standard's (W3C) compliant websites for small, medium and large corporations including GM, Chevrolet, USPS, U.S Navy, Alltel and Farmers Insurance.

Good understanding of the pro's/cons of choosing a CMS. Good knowledge of Accessibility best practices and government guidelines eg section 508/WCAG. Skilled in optimization of content for Search Engines (SEO). Experience implementing client-side analytics such as Google Analytics, Omniture Site-Catalyst, WebTrends and Mobilytics.

Skills include:

Expert XHTML/CSS front-end developer. Experienced middle-tier/Web Application Developer using PHP5, Pear, cURL, MySQL and Apache. Moderately dangerous with JavaScript and Ajax using Prototype, jQuery and Scriptaculous.

Familiarity with JSP/JSTL in a J2EE environment, some exposure to Groovy on Grails MVC framework.
Some experience building mobile websites (XHTML-MP) and making use of tera-wURFL device feature detection.

In-depth knowledge of WordPress publishing platform including theme customization, admin modifications, security best practices and Plugin development. Past experience with MovableType and Blogger.

Some experience developing Ajax functionality into apps for the Facebook platform, also have beginner experience working with Adobe Air SDK.

Good understanding of IA, wire-frames, site-mapping and the importance of User Experience. Skilled in Photoshop and crafting effective designs for web-based user interfaces.
