Sun Kwok’s Summary

I have been importing progressive dies, metal stamping parts and pneumatic components from the Greater China area since 1998, the year I started my own business. Prior to that, I worked for many years as a senior project engineer for a metalstamping press manufacturer as well as an international sales director (China) for a welding equipment manufacturer, both in the Midwest of USA.

I would like to connect with American companies doing business in China or would like to do so.

Sun Kwok’s Specialties:

Have years of experience in sourcing from and selling to Chinese manufacturers of industrial equipment and machinery. Also involved with many technology transfer projects between USA and China as well as between USA and Japan.

Sun Kwok’s Experience

  • President

    Integral World Inc.

    (Outsourcing/Offshoring industry)

    Miller Electric Mfg. Co.

    (Public Company; Machinery industry)

Also did a lot of direct sales to major end-users in China, as well as recruiting and working with Miller's distributors in Taiwan and Hong Kong

  • Senior Project Engineer

    Verson Allsteel Press Company

    (Machinery industry)

    March 1966 — May 1985 (19 years 3 months)

Provided technical support to the Sales Department and travelled extensively in the USA, Japan and China. Involved in many technology transfer projects in Japan and China with such end-users as Sumitomo, Nippon Steel, Kawasaki, Jinan Machine Tool Works etc.
