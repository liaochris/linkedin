Chris Brown’s Summary

360 degree tool for crowdsourcing and contingent recruitment :

1) Crowdsourcing: Use your employees, clients or other contacts to fill your position.
2) Contingent recruitment: Work with all recruiters and headhunters at one place and pay only for success.


FaceContact helps users find passive candidates quickly and cost-effectively. FaceContact is FREE to employers. You pay recruiters and referrers for their services only after you have hired a candidate.

FaceContact was included in the Top20 European start-ups by YEurope, Metalab Innovation Center, Big Bang Ventures, RISC Partners, i5invest, TechCrunch, GIMV ICT, Atlas Venture, Alven Capital Partners. (Plugg.eu)

FaceContact.com was selected by Index Ventures, Benchmark Capital, Atomico, DFJ Esprit, 3i, The Accelerator Group, Atlas Ventures, Partech International, Accel Partners, Highland Capital Partners, Wellington Partners, Advent Ventures as the finalist of Seedcamp out of 400 companies (September 2007, London).

Corporate Development Director of Google described Facecontact.com as a crack of the referral problem: effective (as a result of cash incentives) lead generation with guaranteed quality (by your social network).
