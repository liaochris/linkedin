Kate More’s Summary

Less is never more, especially when it comes to describing a Torontonian like Kate More.

Despite having only 25 years to her name, Kate has worn many hats in the city – freelance journalist and blogger, PR consultant, wine critic, award-winning actress, short story writer, fashion stylist, national charity fundraiser, restaurant server and general foodie, animal rights activist, B.A. and graduate student, indie-rock festival director, music video producer, international ambassador…

But ask Kate to describe herself and she will tell you she’s a die-hard urbanite. Her chameleon lifestyle may be accessorized by many hats but at heart Kate is a cultural observant – a woman on a perpetual date with the city.

And ask Kate why she loves to promote and write about Toronto? She’ll tell you that she’s a detective for soul – passionate to reveal the city’s character one homegrown step at a time.

Kate More is focused on the heart of Toronto.

And that’s just a little more about Kate…
