Martin Gillet’s Summary

Certified SAP HR Consultant (Multiple releases incl. latest) and SAP Trainer.

I have over 10 years hand-on experience and expertise on SAP R/3, specialized in SAP HCM also known as HR and all integration aspects among SAP and third parties products.

I started working with SAP in 1997 during my internship at Dow Corning's european headquarters, focusing at the time on Training and Events Management.

I started then developing my knowledge of SAP R/3 from March 1998 with an intensive SAP HCM / HR track at SAP France.

I meet the highest SAP standard in terms of quality and standard methodology; I am (or have been) working with SAP France, Belgium, USA, UK, Denmark, Arabia, Switzerland, Finland and Germany).
