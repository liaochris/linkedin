Ron Kok’s Summary

Ron is currently a postgraduate enrolled in the Informatiekunde (Information science) programme at the University of Amsterdam, and following the Human Centered Multimedia track. This program is focusing on the delivery and storage of information and communication between humans and computers, working together in an intelligent way via different forms of media such as images, video, speech and text.

He’s able to work in multidisciplinary teams to design, build and evaluate complex, interactive, human centered multimedia systems, and has knowledge about human-machine and ICT mediated human-to-human communication and interaction.

Ron has strong interests in new media, information visualization and user experience design.
