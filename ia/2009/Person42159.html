Cheryl Andonian’s Experience

  • Founder, Principal

    Momblebee (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    May 2009 — Present (2 months)

    Momblebee is a boutique branding, marketing, PR and design firm that specializes in building brands and creating buzz for businesses catering to children and moms. Momblebee's approach is innovative, inuitive, organic, and taps into a vast creative skill set that connects brands meaningfully with their consumers. Momblebee offers combined talents in communications, branding, design, along with an established network of bloggers to add value, connection and validity to your business and image.

  • Co-Founder, VP of Marketing and PR

    Polliwalks, Inc.

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    June 2007 — May 2009 (2 years)

    Responsible for all aspects of product promotion, branding, PR, communications for start up children's footwear brand.
    Successfully spearheaded and implemented an ongoing grassroots viral/word of mouth marketing campaign through reaching out to "Mommy Bloggers". Secured reviews and coverage on numerous blogs, in national parenting and trade magazines, and television product placement.
    Duties included: writing for web/marketing materials, press releases, pitches and coordinating coverage in key national and trade magazines, blogs, and other promotional outlets. Branding, line building, color and trend and working closely with design in product conceptualization and direction. Contracting and working directly with outside vendors for POP design and development, graphic design, printing, etc. Also played key role in managing the details of the startup to get the brand off the ground.
