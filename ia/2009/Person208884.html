Sandy McMullen’s Summary

Creativity and practicality
Sandy partners with coaches and consultants to put a working artist in their toolkit in order to bring processes to their clients that unleash the imagination and reveal the new and unexpected. In addition, Sandy helps with expertise in well proven tools that bring people the most powerful tool of all – self –knowledge.

Collaborating with coaches and consultants by:
o Co-designing in-house workshops, off sites and retreats that integrate imaginative thinking into the discovery or planning process and bring the results to life through painting, collage or other artistic practices.
o Facilitating MBTI workshops enriched through art
o Providing expertise in the Hay Groups Emotional Intelligence 360 assessment tool combined with follow up coaching that integrates MBTI
o Creating one of a kind “Insight Portraits” that capture the essence of a situation, a particular group or an individual’s gifts and talents.
o “Sandy makes me look good to my clients.”

Sandy’s private practice offers coaching and small group facilitation to entrepreneurs, coaches, consultants and independent small business owners. So often coaches and consultants can’t or don’t do for themselves what they do for their clients.

o Connect with your passion by getting clear on what really matters NOW
o Kick start your results with a brainstorming session that puts wheels on your enthusiasm
o Get out of that stuck corner by uncovering the “thing” that is stopping you.
o “Sandy asks me the questions I am afraid to ask myself.”
