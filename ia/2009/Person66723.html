Doyal Bryant’s Summary

Entrepreneur who is a proven, self-motivated, dynamic leader possessing the experience and business savvy that comes with a 25+ year track record in Telecom, Investment Banking and New Media businesses of driving business growth through aggressive sales, business and product development.

A strong background working with leading media companies and has recently worked with start-ups within the e-business and technology industries. Has extensive contacts with executives in local search, vertical directories, yellow pages, media, and technology.

He also has a strong knowledge and experience in Intellectual Property. Within the Patent world, Doyal has worked with over 25 Fortune 500 companies on IP leveraging or expanding their patent portfolio. He has also worked with several of the major investments banks and patent holding companies with patent sales, funding, and investment opportunities. He also has worked with patent technology due diligence and claim and infringement analysis.

Doyal is currently co founder and Executive VP of Business Development for the UniversalBusinessListing.org which is dedicated to providing an inexpensive, enhanced, business listing in one place that can be distributed uniformly to all major online Directories and local based search engines. He is also Managing Partner at BlueLake Capital Partners, which provides advisory services and management support to growth stage companies, both public and private, specializing in Internet Networking, Wireless and Wireline Telecommunications and Broadband and New Media related companies. Until recently, he served as President of IGC Acquisitions, Inc. and currently serves on the Board of Directors or Board of Advisors of several companies

Previously he held senior management or ownership positions in companies that provided financial and technical due diligence services for major investment banking firms with telecom related transactions valued at over $300 million.

Doyal Bryant’s Specialties:

Start up and Turn arounds in Wireless and Wireline Telecom and New Media Companies. Especially, Local Online and Mobile Search. Both public and private companies with expertise in raising or bringing capital from Venture Capital, Private Equity, PIPEs, and for M and A opportunities.
