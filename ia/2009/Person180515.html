Pamela Holden’s Summary

Pamela Holden is responsible for driving the business development efforts for the BDO Seidman office in San Diego. To date, this office has been very successful in this market because the culture within our office is collaborative and supportive of our clients' needs. The Partners of the Assurance, Tax and Consulting Practices not only provide the highest quality in technical support but they actually listen to our clients. This is what sets us apart.

The BDO San Diego team is committed to delivering independent, effective and comprehensive audits. They provide quality assurance services and are committed to values of integrity, trust, professionalism, independence and service. We make the hard deadlines our clients need!

Pamela joined the firm in 2007. She brings over 15 years experience and relationships, primarily in the finance and accounting community of San Diego.

She was at Robert Half International, where she managed the day to day activities of Management Resources for almost 2 years. There she provided experienced consultants on special projects for many of the top companies in San Diego.

Pamela was a principal in a start up executive search firm where she oversaw the contigency side of the group. As a flourishing boutique firm they were later acquired by one of the top international staffing companies.

She has over 10 years of recruiting top executive accounting and finance professionals and 4 years in management; both in operations and accounting for an international company (Japanese owned). Pamela oversaw the internal audit function within the company and collaborated daily with other departments.

Pamela is a member of the ACG, Corporate Finance Council, Chair of Programs, Corporate Directors Forum, Steering Committee for the Director of the Year Awards event, San Diego Venture Group, IIA, TechAmerica, membership committee, Athena, CleanTech San Diego, Co-Chair for Circle of Red, American Heart Association
