  • Co-founder, Principal & CTO

    Endai Worldwide

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Online Media industry)

    October 1999 — August 2006 (6 years 11 months)

    I co-founded Endai as "AdClip Networks" in the fall of 1999 along with Michael Ferranti. Our initial product offering was an online, interactive ad format designed to address some of the many performance and usability limitations of the primary ad format of the day, the banner ad. The company was re-invented and re-branded as Endai Worldwide in 2001, an online marketing & services agency with a strong set of proprietary software technology. We leveraged our technology strengths to deliver superior competitive performance for our clients' online marketing campaigns, and grew the company to a profitable and well-regarded agency in our space. I left the company in 2006 to pursue other entrepreneurial opportunities on the west coast.
