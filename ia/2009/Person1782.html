Abhijith Shekar’s Summary

Interested in product management in the technology sector - storage systems, networks, mobile and mobile application and gaming.


I am currently pursuing MBA at Melbourne Business School as a full time student with a focus on strategy and product management/marketing in the technology sector.

As part of my course work, I worked as part of a team for an Australian company looking to outsource manufacturing and market entry into China. It involved secondary research when in Australia and hands on work in Shanghai, China involving of analysis of the Chinese market and the production industry.

I previously worked with IBM India as a Technical Lead, leading a team of 5 developers managing a portfolio of around 30 HR and payroll, applications.

I started my career with IBM India as an intern during my last semester of undergraduate degree and was recognised for exceptional performance in a trainee pool of 6 people which lead to a full time position with the company. After working as an application developer for a year, I was promoted as a Technical Lead within a short span of time.

Apart from my responsibility managing my team, I also coached and mentored new graduates who joined the team in efficient programming skills who still continue to work successfully with the team.
