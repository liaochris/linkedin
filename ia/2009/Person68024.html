Debbie T. Lawrence’s Summary

As president of Lagniappe Marketing, my role is to build and execute strategies for companies seeking to engage their community via the social web. We focus on the core areas of listening, content marketing, community management, and outreach programs. Acting as a boutique Marketing/ PR/communications organization, Lagniappe Marketing extends your Marketing and other channels into the web.

Outside of work, I speak at several conferences a year and am available corporate retreats and non profit organizations as well. Individually planned, positive messages and humor spun into each keynote, no canned speeches and no stone left unturned in developing the RIGHT message for YOUR organization.

I am Founder of 1 Million Memories, an initiative founded to raise 1 million dollars for Alzheimer's research and education, 1 dollar at a time.

I am an Expert Blogger for Intent.com and have articles featured across the web.
