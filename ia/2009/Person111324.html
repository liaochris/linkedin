Jess Bennett’s Summary

Jess Bennett recently completed a Master of Professional Communication (MPC) in Sydney, Australia and is pursuing a career in corporate communications at Palette Public Relations in Toronto. She was recently presented with the 2009 IABC/Toronto MAVERICK PR Student of the Year Award.

Jess has strong leadership and project management skills. Energetic and self-starting, she is a bilingual team player who understands group dynamics and has a strength for the analysis and development of strategic communication plans.

Jess's career objectives include the attainment of her APR, ABC and MC credentials. She aspires to do great things using her passion for social media and hopes to one day work at Google.

Jess enjoys riding her bike around Toronto and doing yoga in the hot, hot heat (39ºC).
