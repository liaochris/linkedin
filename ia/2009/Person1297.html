Aaron Schwartz’s Summary

Biography: Aaron Schwartz

Aaron serves as the founder of The Schwartz Consulting Group, a practice that is focused on the study and application of ideas, trends & inspiration that remix, reclassify, reintegrate, reposition, revitalize and redesign existing brand assets to meet strategic organizational goals. As an industry veteran of retail, marketing and merchandising in the fashion sector, Aaron’s distinguished career has included positions as President of Polo Ralph Lauren Footwear, President and Chief Creative Officer for the BRUNOMAGLI Group based in Bologna Italy, and Vice President and Divisional Merchandise Manager for Women’s Shoes at Federated Department Stores. Aaron has also been part of leading two successful business/brand “turnaround” situations. Earlier in his career he was at Ann Taylor Stores and Macy’s West in various merchandising and executive assignments. Aaron is also a lifetime member of the Two/Ten Footwear Foundation and invited member of The Purple List. Aaron has lived and worked in Europe and The US. He has also been profiled and featured in several magazines and trade publications around the world including but not limited to Fast Company Magazine, WWD, Footwear News, Vogue Pelle, Vogue Russia, Vogue Italia, and French Vogue

Aaron Schwartz’s Specialties:

Superior business acumen, proven industry experience, articulate communicator, excellent leadership skills, strong decision making skills, strong organizational change management skills, capacity and know how to drive a business, uncompromising on quality, consistently focused on execution of actionable plans, experience with turnaround/mergers, excellent knowledge of product lifecycle processes, excellence in merchandising, excellent public speaking abilities; success is my reoccurring theme.
