Bárbara Ribeiro’s Summary

Nowadays is a member of PUC-Rio´s faculty, teacher of the Interior Design postgraduate course - of which was a co-founder. Graduated in 2003 - UFRJ | Architecture and Urban Studies -; Masters degree in Design was obtained in 2005 - PUC-Rio | Brown University. Postgraduated in Marketing & Design, certificate by PUC-Rio. Won international prizes in Japan and Spain, as well as a Visiting Scholar grant from Brown University. Has published articles in the International Congress P&D, amongst others... Developed - and still develops - mainly Interior Design and Urban Planning projects; also has exhibited several Plastic Art works in Galleries and such, like Les Artists, Cultural House Laura Alvin and so on...
