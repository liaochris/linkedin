mike young’s Summary

Windward Communication Group is now a FSC Certified Printer. Here is a good definition of FSC.

Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) is a non-profit organization devoted to encouraging the responsible management of the world’s forests. FSC sets high standards that ensure forestry is practiced in an environmentally responsible, socially beneficial, and economically viable way. FSC Website http://www.fscus.org/.

In a nutshell we can now print The FSC Logo on printed materials, giving your clientele and those you are marketing confidence that you are using paper that has been manufactured in a responsible manner.

The FSC logo goes well with Windward’s quality and craftsmanship.

I really like working with people and have a strong sense of service in my make up.
