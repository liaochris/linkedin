Erick Laubach’s Summary

I’m a seasoned entrepreneur with over 14 years of creative and new media experience. I’m also a founding partner of quite a few start-ups under the Cottonblend umbrella including:

Cute as Hell (Cuteashell.com)
A fun new community and shopping site made exclusively for pet people.

Driftr (Driftr.com)
An online travel community where you keep track of your travels, share your trip photos, reviews, and blogs with friends and family, and research destinations before you book a trip.

The Pixel Awards (Pixelawards.com)
A cutting-edge web awards site, annually honoring 20 compelling sites that have shown excellence in web design and development. Reached profitability in 3 years.

Prior to founding Cottonblend, I played a pivotal role in forming the creative group at Ticketmaster as the Vice President, Creative. I quadrupled the size of the team within four years, taking responsibility for the Design, HTML, Content, and Production groups.

I’m also an award-winning designer with strong experience in both online and offline media having held senior level creative positions for several companies including Caesars Entertainment, eToys, and StyleClick (IAC). My client list includes: About.com, American Express, BMX Plus! Magazine, FOX, Herbie Hancock, The Hollywood Reporter, Intel, Mountain Bike Magazine, NASCAR, PGA, TicketWeb, and The .tv Corporation - to name a few.
