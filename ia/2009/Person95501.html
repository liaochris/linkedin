  • Brand Strategist

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 2008 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    Holly’s love of branding and the creative process has been part of her DNA since graduating from the University of Missouri - the first journalism school - in the early nineties. Her first job at a KC newspaper allowed her to produce ads with an impact.
    Holly prides herself on putting herself in the mindset of her customers whether it be a non-profit agency or a corporate client, ensuring every element of the business adheres to the brand. Her work experience spans the spectrum from working on the McDonald’s account to working on consumer driven accounts like: Tsunami clothing, Hotel 43, The Grove Hotel as well as major corporate accounts like HP, Charles Schwab, Blue Cross of Idaho, Microsoft and Bally Technologies.
    Holly’s ultimate passion is in the non-profit community focusing on issues surrounding our youth and people of all ages with disabilities. She wants to ensure every citizen grows up with a sense of self-worth; ultimately contributing more fully to the larger community.
