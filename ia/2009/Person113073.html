Jim Horne’s Summary

Providing expertise in website Search Engine Optimization (SEO) design and consulting services, I assist businesses and consultants to achieve greater website visibility by leveraging their Internet Marketing resources. Design services include meta tag and keyword analysis, keyword suggestion, search engine saturation evaluation, copyrighted content, link building strategies and website navigation performance enhancements.

In my prior career, I attained over twenty five years of successful experience in sales, sales management and leadership in high technology, primarily semiconductors. I have demonstrated repeated success in deployment of resources and leading teams of sales and sales support professionals, manufacturers’ representatives and distributors both domestically and internationally. Skilled at focusing organizational efforts to execute strategic and tactical initiatives in a highly technical environment.
