Greg Buechler’s Summary

I am the Director of Franchise Development at Solar Universe in Livermore California, and I want to let you know about an opportunity to provide up to $1,000 (USD) to the green charity of your choice. This opportunity is quite simple. For each partner you refer that joins the Solar Universe Network (S.U.N.), we will donate a $1,000 to the green charity you select ($500 from corporate, and $500 from myself personally!) In today's economic times, charities need as much help as they can get and we want to be part of their support.

So, why are we doing this? The answer is simple - we see solar as a huge market, one that is a true win-win-win-win. Consumers lower their electric bills, less carbon is released into the environment, the nation becomes less reliant on foreign oil, AND jobs are created. Very few times in our lifetimes has an emerging market provided so much at one time.

Solar Universe is a turn key solar PV business solution. We are unique installation concept that is delivered through a network of owner-operators under a franchise model that includes education, standardized solar design, product distribution, project financing and technology. The system provides opportunity to get started in solar, and develop a self-owned business with all the benefits of a national brand and corporate support system. A Solar Universe franchise is ideal for the displaced corporate type OR an existing trades contractor.

If you want more information about us please feel free to research us via our website at http://www.solaruniverse.com, visit our existing locations at http://www.solaruniverse.com/franchise_tour_5, or simply give me a call.

Lets save the world together!
