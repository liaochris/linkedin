Mohammad Khan’s Summary

As far as selling goes, I sell products that bring any business to the internet in the best possible way (usually combining the best in the industry in pre-designed packages), from business email to hosting to website design to branding.
But that is just that, selling.
My idea of life is that of a constant challenge. I have developed a disliking for the much sought-after 'comfort zone'. My main area of interest has been and still is life itself (the now-famous 'lifestyle design'), probing, testing and implementing strategies to optimize one's self and enriching the lives of others.
Narrowing the 'field of and for life' to communications, I have tried to generate income from that very industry, with some very interesting observations and mild success, thanks to God. I write regularly (or so I would claim) on the Internet about life experiences and such. Business, being a part of life, is treated as a means to an end; the aim to improve quality of life. Period.
