John Skelly’s Summary

500lb was founded in 2004 with the idea that the fusion of technology and marketing would serve a growing need in the marketplace. The vision has proven true: 500lb has grown from two employees at its inception to an expert team of e-marketing professionals today, and has seen nearly 800% growth from 2004 – 2007. We pride ourselves on quality and delivering on properly set expectations, and enjoy great client retention. The company was recently honored by the Portland Business Journal as #21 in the Fastest Growing Private 100 Companies, 2009.

John Skelly’s Specialties:

We conceptualize and build the systems behind successful internet marketing programs. From email marketing campaigns to large-scale online communities, we've created customized marketing solutions for businesses ranging from exciting startups to Fortune 100 corporations.
