Mark Ross’s Summary

I am an experienced UK solicitor, former partner at Underwoods Solicitors and now VP of Global Sales & Marketing at LawScribe, one of the world's leading legal outsourcing companies.

My involvement in legal outsourcing dates back to Jan 04 when Underwoods became the first UK firm to outsource legal work to a lower cost common law jurisdiction. Chambers Guide 07 refers to Underwoods as "a pioneer of legal offshoring". I was responsible for the development of the workflow system for outsourcing cases to the firm's South Africa office. I also presided over succesful applications for Investor in People & Lexcel (UK Law Society quality standard) accreditation.

I moved to LA in Nov 06 and joined LawScribe where I am responsible for global sales and marketing and the company's overall strategic vision.

I am a recognized authority in the field of legal outsourcing, with numerous articles published on the subject. My blog has become a popular resource for those interested in the industry. I am Chair of the International Association of Outsourcing Professionals' Legal Outsourcing Chapter. I developed the first State Bar approved MCLE Ethics course, provided by an LPO, on the ethical implications of outsourcing legal work. I have subsequently created two further State Bar accredited courses dealing with patent support outsourcing and offshore document review. I have been invited to speak at conferences run by: Financial Times, U.C. Berkeley School of Law, American Lawyer, ALA, the Paralegal Superconference, ACI and IQPC.

My professional goals are to maintain LawScribe's position as a leading LPO and continue to play a role in the long-term development of the industry.

I am committed to the implementation of industry standards of accreditation. It is my firm belief that for legal outsourcing to succeed, the product delivered must be of the highest quality and providers should be held accountable to the most stringent security and confidentiality standards.
