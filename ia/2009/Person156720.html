Matt Sternberg’s Summary

Proven digital media executive with nearly two decades of senior management experience in the entertainment and technology industries.

Digital entertainment and marketing pioneer with solid track record of developing disruptive content distribution, monetization and marketing strategies. Extensive experience includes a unique mix of strategy, revenue generation, marketing, negotiating mutually beneficial deals quickly and evangelizing new ideas, technologies and partnerships.
