Curt Maly’s Summary

In my previous employment career I was involved in the call center/direct sales industry for over 15+ years. I have detailed sales experience in over 13 very different industry verticals such as technology, politics, publications, membership acquisition, cable television, telecommunications, software development and entertainment to name just a small sample.

I have assisted over 1,000+ sales agents in multiple marketing projects that include direct outbound/inbound sales, channel sales, lead generation, data base clean up, fund raising, appointment setting, customer retention, publication renewal/actuations, market research, new customer acquisition and MORE!

I have retired my employment positions!

I am now a Serial Entrepreneur who owns multiple businesses. Additionally I am real estate investor owning/controlling properties in multiple states using multiple investing strategies.

I can also assist people in showing them how to get involved in real estate investing.
