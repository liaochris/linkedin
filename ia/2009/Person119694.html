Jon Tucker’s Summary

I'm a 22 year old San Diego entrepreneur finishing up a marketing degree in at San Diego State University. I've launched a few ventures over the past 5 years and have learned some great lessons.

I'm involved in a range of internet marketing ventures and do internet consulting for businesses. Through 2009, I'm launching a series of online educational/informational products for niche markets, as I was involved in successfully promoting other client's educational/informational online products in 2008.

I'm always seeking to learn new things in marketing and internet marketing. Although I've been involved with Google marketing and other strategies, I'm currently getting heavily involved in the psychological side of marketing. I've been studying Cialdini and a number of other students of Cialdini that have made some amazing accomplishments in internet marketing in recent years.

The greatest learning experience I've had recently has been my involvement in the Lavin Venturestart Fellowship at SDSU. The program was created by Leonard Lavin, founder of multi-billion dollar Alberto-Culver, to provide a small group 10 SDSU students with a real-world learning experience in entrepreneurship. I've been fortunate enough to meet with Mr. Lavin a number of times, was able to fly out to Dallas to meet with the CEO of one of the companies he owns, met and learned from a range of successful entrepreneurs in various industries, and even been able to meet and sit in on presentations/discussions with investors in the San Diego area. The program has been a life changing learning experience and I'm excited to continue to learn through the program through the rest of the semester.

Through LinkedIn, I plan to meet other business minded students and young entrepreneurs in addition to seasoned entrepreneurs in both internet marketing and other fields.

Feel free to shoot me an email anytime, as I always enjoy a great conversation and chance to learn from one another's experiences.
