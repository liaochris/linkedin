Peggy Simmons’s Summary

I see every group of people as an ecosystem that thrives with the success of the individual organisms within it. I am experienced and adept at helping each member of a group understand and excel in the unique and critical role that he or she plays in the group's overall success.

I am looking to put my experience as a nonprofit and corporate manager, trainer, and facilitator to use

1) in a leadership role within a nonprofit focused on community service, young people, literacy, the arts, and/or combating poverty, or

2) in employee development or team management within the corporate sector.
