Brad Ball’s Summary

I was recently promoted to an Account Management role at Kinetic Corp. Prior to that, I was a professional developer for nine years. I started working part time while in college, and had been a developer ever since. I started with php and PostgreSQL databases at Bluegrass.net in Louisville, and continued working with php for the next five years. Four years ago, I took a position with Kinetic Corp. again doing web development; this time using the Microsoft .NET Framework, and ASP.NET. At Kinetic, I have expanded my skill set, working with Web Services, Windows applications, Windows Services, and Compact Framework development, while maintaining my focus on ASP.NET web development.

I love development, but it is not my primary goal. I have a CIS degree from the University of Louisville College of Business, and I plan to get into management, or maybe start my own company. My immediate goal is to get into systems analysis and design. I enjoy the development process, but the analysis and design process is where I can really excel. Beyond that, I would like to get into management, and perhaps start my own development company sometime down the road.
