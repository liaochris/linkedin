Jason Rose’s Summary

Jason started his career in real estate as a country agent back in 1987. Specialising in Auctioneering, Jason travelled the East Coast of Australia. Jason's determination to be the best that he could was proven with Jason twice being awarded Queensland Young Auctioneer of the Year and twice runner-up in the Australasian titles. After 10 years in the bush, Jason decided to wash off the dust and the mud and move to Brisbane.

In10 years Jason has conducted in excess of 2000 house auctions and is regarded by the Real Estate Institute of Queensland as one of the top property auctioneers in the state, being a finalist (Top 3) in the REIQ Auctioneer of the year in 2000,2001,2003,2004 & 2006. In May 2005 Jason competed against fellow Harcourts auctioneers from throughout Australia and was awarded the Harcourts Australia Auctioneer of the Year.

Jason made the move from real estate sales to property management with an exceptional knowledge and depth of experience in the property market and has been instrumental in the development of the Rental Express property management portfolio. Jason is now the Operations Manager for Rental Express and is responsible for implementing new systems and proceeds within the organisation and making sure that Rental Express is the number one choice for property management in Brisbane.
