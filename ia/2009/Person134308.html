Kripanidhi S M’s Summary

In over two decades of my career in software development, I have managed over 35 projects and 6 products successfully. Over the last 9 years of my IT consulting, I have trained and coached over 15,000 software development professionals at all levels of seniority worldwide.

I specialize in Software Requirements Engineering and Software Project Management. For the last 9 years I have been involved in emerging Agile Methodologies, Agile Project Leadership and similar Value Streaming based software development methodologies.

I coach project teams implementing Agile Methodologies like XP, Scrum, FDD and Lean. I also help organizations plan for and implement Agile Leadership, Lean, TOC/ CCPM in their management orientation and help them in understanding the new cultural transformation required to embrace such emerging paradigms.

I have been a contributing author in Agile Project Management for Cutter Consortium (http://www.cutter.com ) . I have delivered customized programs in Agile for Center for Technical Training (CTT), Phillips Research Labs, Netherlands and am an accredited Project Management Trainer for Logica CMG internationally. I am a keen follower of Evo Project Management of Tom Gilb ( http://www.gilb.com ).

I also conduct public workshops,tech talks, boot camps and training programs for project teams in the areas of my expertise.

"Priming Passions to Perfection" and "Process Innovations in Software Engineering" are my aspirations. It should help us discover new ways to build software products, faster, cheaper and better with a set of delighted customers and motivated teams who enjoy co-creating innovative software solutions.

Early in my career, I spent a little over 8 years as an officer in the Indian Air Force (Ex- Flt Lt) in the Air Defence stream (1977-86)
