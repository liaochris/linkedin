Nathan Singh’s Summary

A 6th year undergraduate student at SFU doing a joint major in Interactive Arts & Technology and Communication. Throughout my Communication studies, I have worked on persuasive & argumentative essays discussing hegemony and ideology in media practices and contemporary consumer culture; worked on communications campaigns and print & publication design (typography, colour, packaging, layout, grids). In Interactive Arts & Technology I have developed a wide range of skills, such as designing for user experiences (HCI, prototyping), information design (2D and 3D mapping, spatial configurations) digital videography and post-production (camera angles, framing, storyboarding, editing, sound design and immersion) and narrative design for new media. As an intensive, project based program, teamwork, collaboration, communication and design thinking skills are used regularly.
