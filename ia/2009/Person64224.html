Philip Finane’s Summary

The "Direct Source Network" is a Staffing Consultancy Co-Op bringing Professional Jobseekers from all over the world to the attention of International Organisations who require their particular skill sets.

Working closely with a growing number of International Clients within the Finance, Banking, Telecoms, Technology and Oil & Gas sectors, we are actively assisting these companies in their search to source the best available C-Level & Senior Management professionals available.

Direct Source Network is currently working with clients in the following geographical locations:

Ireland, UK, Benelux Region, France, Middle East, North & Sub Sahara Africa, China & APAC Region, Caribbean & South America.

To stay up-to-date with the International Career opportunities available via the Direct Source Network we invite you join the "Direct Source Network" LinkedIn Group today.

Search for us in the LinkedIn Group directory.
Membership is growing steadily!

For Irish professionals who have lost their jobs (or whose careers have been negatively impacted by the global recession), you are invited to join our new LinkedIn Group called "OBAIR - Working for Ireland". Join today. Again, search for the group "OBAIR" in the LinkedIn Group directory.
