Rick Srinivasan’s Summary

IT Professional: Aligning Technology with Business

As Vice President of Alternative Investments Technology at Investors Bank & Trust/State Street, Rick managed a team of 40+ professionals who employed best practices of SDLC and project management. As part of the IT PMO, he enhanced operating models, governance, reporting, auditing, support and business continuity while ensuring fiscal responsibility. Rick's efforts were instrumental in enabling the organization reduce costs and increase revenue.

As CIO at Rounder Records, Rick was responsible for modernizing Rounder's IT department and infrastructure. He implemented a formal structure to the department under an IT PMO. Rick changed the focus of the IT department from an ad-hoc group with little strategy and vision to one that met business needs and organization goals. The department also employed effective SDLC that allowed for flexible project management.

As Director of IT at Genesis Consolidated Services, Rick was tasked with the complete modernization of a legacy infrastructure that supported legacy applications and operating models. With minimal resources, Rick successfully transformed the company's use of technology to its advantage and helped the business attract new revenue streams. By designing and implementing an IT strategy that kept business objectives in mind, Rick helped a young organization realize that technology was an ally.

At Equinox Solutions, Rick gained a tremendous amount of experience in managing an off-shore development staff and learned about the advantages and pitfalls of such a model in 1993, YEARS EARLIER than most companies employing this model today. Rick has the added advantage of refining and perfecting an offshore development and support strategy over a 16 year period.

Rick was also instrumental in attracting new clients for Equinox Solutions with the offshore development model.
