Chris Bourke’s Summary

After 9 years in the sign industry, I moved into the area of Graphic Design, Advertising and Marketing, founding the Australian advertising agency, Advanti Advertising, based on the Sunshine Coast, Australia. I resigned from my position of company director to venture into the world of Web Design and Multi Media, forming Devision Design. Devision is now quickly becoming one of the Sunshine Coast's best known Design and Web companies, with clients both locally, nationally and internationally. Devision also manage a successful group of online Hotel Accommodation websites, and more recently, have started up Web Hosting company, Webcrowd.
