David Catzel’s Summary

MANAGEMENT, STRATEGY, BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT AND MARKETING
David is a senior executive with 20 years of international business experience within the media, entertainment and technology industries. His focus has been on the integration of technology advances and media to expand market share and increase revenues. He has built numerous start-up ventures, worked with Fortune 500 Companies, held executive positions with large companies as diverse as Lucasfilm and Intel and has developed, negotiated and managed multi-year, multi million dollar strategic alliances with technology and media companies, ranging from IBM to NBC Television.

He joined Intel in 1996 to develop and implement entertainment industry strategy in order to expand total available market for high-end Intel processors. He negotiated entertainment property rights and brokered multi-million dollar strategic technology, marketing and content engagements with Universal Studios, DirecTV, NBC, CBS and others to accelerate the development of the interactive TV and broadband markets.

David has been involved in a variety of groundbreaking broadband and iTV projects highlighting the capabilities of a digital sports platform. He then focused on building Intel Capital’s global sports investment portfolio of over a dozen technology and content companies.

David subsequently helped launch Convera, a new digital media services company which was spun out of Intel to service sports and entertainment industry customers.

David served as COO for Obvious Technology during its transition from a technology play to a services company; as interim CEO for Kompolt & Company, a high end online auction management agency and services provider to eBay and currently heads up a consumer products initiative for ESPN

David remains actively involved at the leading edge of the digital transition and is an active member of the Academy of Television Arts and Sciences.
