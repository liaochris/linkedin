John Runyan’s Summary

John Runyan is President of Runyan Public Affairs (RPA), an independent government relations consulting firm assisting clients with a full range of federal affairs services. John opened RPA in 2009 after more than 25 years in the government relations profession. Current clients include: International Paper, the R. R. Donnelley & Sons Company, the Coalition for Transportation Productivity, and the Business and Industry Political Action Committee.

Prior to opening RPA, John managed the federal government relations team for International Paper, a Fortune 100 global corporation. Before joining International Paper, John served as the Senior Government Relations Advisor to the firm of McGuiness & Williams where his clients included the R.R. Donnelley & Sons Co., and the HR Policy Association. He also spent more than a decade with the Printing Industries of America, the nation's largest graphic arts trade association, where his positions included Senior Director, Government Affairs. John started his career in Washington on the staff of former US Senator Donald Stewart (D-AL).

John has played leadership roles in the following major legislative coalitions during his career:

The Coalition for Transportation Productivity, Executive Director (www.transportationproductivity.com)
Mail Moves America, Strategic Advisory Committee (www.mailmovesamerica.org)
The Coalition for a Twenty-First Century Postal Service, Steering Committee Member
The BIPAC Prosperity Project, Chairman (http://www.bipac.org)
Americans for Affordable Electricity, Vice Chairman
The National Alliance Against Blacklisting, Executive Director
The Team Coalition, Executive Director
The Alliance to Keep Americans Working, Lobbying Committee Chairman
