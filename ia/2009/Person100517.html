Ivo van den Baar WANDSCHAPPEN’s Summary

Artist.
Developing and creating artworks for public spaces, companies and individuals by proposing them our ideas and, in this way, getting commissions to create them.
For our company art is an active part of society, providing new visions about daily life by being an independant player. The results can be real works of art but also temporary interventions of art in a specific surrounding. We sell our artworks and make exhibitions with it.
Since ten years we've discovered a new skil, comming from the feeld of visual arts.For Dutch designers we develope prototypes and small productions. As artists we're capable of inventing solutions for new techniques, materials or forms and we're able to visualize them. A new economic system is growing now: art, design and handcrafts with people from our neighbourhood. We only create very special design, handmade and very sustainable. This kind of work used to be done in Low Wages Countries, but now it's done in the Netherlands at our studios

Ivo van den Baar WANDSCHAPPEN’s Specialties:

Unique artworks and artprojects for projectdevelopers, communities and companies.
