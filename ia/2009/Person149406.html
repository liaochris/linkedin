  • Circulation Manager

    We do this by a combination of marketing activities (Direct Marketing, Telemarketing and loyalty campaigns), account management and sales through newsstands/single copy sales.

  • Manager Planning & Control

    De Telefoongids BV

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    November 2003 — November 2007 (4 years 1 month)

    Within the Planning & Control department I led 7 Sales Analysts. We determined an optimal market approach and translated this in segmentation, sales planning and target setting. Moreover we signaled, advised and forecasted (over deflections in) the result - and sales development of the sales organization.
    Thematically: Within the large-scale project ‘Rescope’ I was responsible for the optimal classification of the sales organisation.

  • CRM Manager

    De Telefoongids BV

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Publishing industry)

    June 2001 — November 2003 (2 years 6 months)

    Within the department Sales & Marketing Information I was responsible for (the development, planning, implementation, evaluation and steering of) all direct marketing activities with the aim to bring in, migrate and preserve customers. I advised all internal customer groups how to use direct marketing. Moreover I initiated activities, processes and procedures which lead to the improvement of DM such as data quality, - enrichment and contact registration.
    Thematically: In projects such as ‘complete pink’, ‘complete database’, ‘customer reaction policy, ‘optimise lead processes’ I focussed on the relation with the customer. I was a linking pin between SMI, IT and marketing.

  • Campaign Marketeer

    Delta Lloyd (Nationaal Spaarfonds)

    (Public Company; Financial Services industry)

    1997 — 2001 (4 years)

Besides this I have been the Chairman of the editorial part of the loyalty magazine ‘Profijt’.
