Sandra Dye’s Summary

Sandra has worked with children of all ages during her 26 years in private practice. She has developed a remarkable 5-step system that gives parents a framework of effective strategies to rely on in making parenting decisions and problem solving.

Parents find an end to guilt, chaos and constant battles and
find delight in parenting with her time tested system of connect, calm, listen, limits, and fresh start. Now her 5- step system is available to any parent who finds they are struggling.

One family was quoted as saying, “Sandra translates the acting out behaviors of children that feel incomprehensible into something simple that makes total sense.” A six year old with behavioral problems, whose parent were taught the 5-step system said, “My crazy friend at school needs to come see you.”

Sandra found with her own children that there were times when she tried to rush things. “When you self correct and go back and redo things at the proper pace you find everything gets back on track. While it may take time, the system has never failed our family.”


From private practice to the homes of parents around the world, Sandra helps parents get the positive results they desire. Sandra demonstrates that with the right tools, anything is possible for children and parents.
