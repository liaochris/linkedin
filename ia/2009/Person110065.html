Jenny Voigt DeVaughn’s Summary

Jenny Voigt DeVaughn successfully connects her exceptional candidates with many of the nation's Fortune 500 corporations, non-profit organizations, start-up companies, government agencies and law firms.

She plays an integral role in the placement of HR, recruiting and executive level talent. Jenny loves networking with other professionals, especially in recruiting or Human Resources.

She is a member of TopLinked.com, gladly accepts all invites and is a LION (LinkedIn Open Networker). Jenny values these connections and does not spam her network for any reason.

Jenny enjoys giving social media webinars, presentations and/or specialized social recruiting training for companies and professionals that want to leverage free social media tools to find job opportunities, clients or candidates.

As a working mom of two, Jenny started Busy Bee Baby Planner, Inc. to assist other career savvy moms-to-be. As a new mom consultant, she can help you save time and money so that your exciting transition into parenting is more rewarding and less stressful for you and your baby.

Her company offers a full-suite of baby planning services to pregnant or adopting parents including: baby registry consultation, customized nursery interior design, child care options, nanny interviewing/selection, first-time dad prep, green product research/recommendations, baby announcements, baby shower invitations/themes, maternity leave consultation and planning your babymoon.
