I am an IT-entrepreneur with an open and curious mind, a very good technical education, I have the courage to go where innvovation game leads, and I

I hold a PhD-degree in math, and a masters degree in computer science. My research training has given me the right tools, to move fast and learn about new fields quickly. I feel confident in new technologies, and can maintain a firm understanding of highly complex systems.

I have worked on several software teams, both as a developer, technical lead, project manager and software architect - the last role is my favorite. I am very committed to good team work, agile practices and test driven development.

I have strong beliefs about what good software quality is, and I will go a long way to fight for nothing less than excellence in all the projects, where I am engaged. I love to draw the longterm lines with a thick brush, but I am also dedicated to small details and meeting deadlines.

As a person I am very enthusiastic and creative, and my drive is a burning need to use my practical and theoretical tools to solve my customers problems.
