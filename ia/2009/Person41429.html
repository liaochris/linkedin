Charles Porter’s Summary

Is this where I talk about myself in the third person?

For more than 5 years I have had the pleasure to work in the video game publishing industry. The industry as a whole is much like the Wild West, where jobs are never certain and most of its actors have to wear many hats (the doctor is the mortician and a cooper.) While the Wild West style of the industry may seem scary or dangerous, it also allows those with drive and creative spirit to excel.

My expertise is in project and team management. I thrive on collaborating with others to achieve a common set of goals. I endeavor to better my team and the things we create by enabling them to do their jobs well. Many of the road blocks a team encounters are self created and are only perceived to be impassable.
