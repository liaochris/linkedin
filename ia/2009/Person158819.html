Meghan Groff’s Summary

CAREER PROFILE

Extensive communications career with more than 10 years of broadcast programming experience, research, coordination and direction of live, 24-hour news, traffic, sports, promotional and feature programs. Technical background includes managing automation communication systems and state-of-the-art audio, editing and scheduling systems. Played a key role in the growth of Rogers Kitchener Radio Group in Waterloo Region including the launch of 570 News (the region's first news and information station), and the addition of KIX 106. Completed a four week volunteer work placement in the television newsroom of the Uganda Broadcasting Corporation.
