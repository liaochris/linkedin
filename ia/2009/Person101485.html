Jacqueline R. Green (Pelkey)’s Summary

Parenting brought me to my knees, and challenged me in a way that no boardroom ever did. Learning to become the parent I wanted to be has brought me more joy, and helped me professionally more than anything else I encountered in my life.

SPEAKER: I am available to speak on parenting, with a focus on professional moms, and their issues.

PARENTING COACH:

Do you need one-on-one parenting help? I coach professional moms who are overwhelmed and having serious challenges with parenting. My sub-specialties include:

• Boundary issues
• Connecting with your kids
• Anger issues
• Depression
• Maturation
• Anxiety
• Workaholism
• Perfectionism
• Establishing a family vision
• In-law issues

Email me at info@GreatParentingPractices.com for a free, one-hour coaching call where we will discuss what your specific issues are, and how I can help you. I will refer you to someone else if that is appropriate.


PARENTING EDUCATOR AND INTERVIEWER:

I interview parenting experts on a variety of topics related to parenting help. The interviews are free to listen to via teleseminar or webcast. Audio replays are available for purchase.

This winter I will host the Parenting TeleSummit, Middle School Edition. Over 4 days I will have 16 experts on a variety of topics. The interviews will be free to listen to live via teleseminar or webinar. Audio replays will be available to purchase afterwards.

My professional experience is in communications. Although I worked for a number of years in government communications, I left that job to parent and build my own parenting education business.
