Christopher Wilson’s Summary

Innovative senior human resources visionary hosting almost 20 years of progressive experience and accountability in most functional areas of HR.

Successfully operate an independent HR solutions-based consultancy for more than 13 years representing a diverse client base. We are located in New York City and Chicago.

An ideal client is a small to mid-size enterprise seeking to improve performance and strategically take advantage of growth opportunities through human capital initiatives.

Expertise provided in the following competencies:

TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT: Curriculum design, implementation and facilitation. Additionally able to assess learning processes to ensure effectiveness and performance.

TALENT ACQUISITION AND PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT: Address the unique business needs while developing and supporting an appropriate strategy based on talent performance and retention. Leverage talent to improve management and leadership performance.

EMPLOYEE/LABOR RELATIONS AND COMPLIANCE: Provide investigation, conflict resolution and representation in a variety of employee relations issues. Provide training and policy for risk and loss remediation.
