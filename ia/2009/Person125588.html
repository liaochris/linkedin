Kale Gaston’s Summary

Franchise Source Brands International (FSBI) is the leading firm in the franchise business coaching/consulting market. There are four business opportunities available within the FSBI umbrella: AdviCoach, Business Partner, Expense Reduction Consultants and Decor & You.

* AdviCoach – business coaches supporting the small to mid-sized business market helping business owners increase performance and improve profitability.
* Business Partner Marketing Coach - marketing coaches supporting companies of all sizes with proven marketing strategies and a full suite of creative marketing and coaching services.
* Expense Reduction Consulting, ERC – business coaches offering sophisticated cost reduction and control processes to all businesses generating significant savings for clients across many industries.
* Decor&You – two investment opportunities; an executive multi-unit model with business owners functioning as CEOs managing professional interior decorators; or individual decorating units targeting specific territories.
