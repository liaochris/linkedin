Jason Spencer’s Summary

Not quite the jack-of-all-trades, Jason has experience that transcends the worlds of entertainment, education, the Internet, communication design, and entrepreneur.

Jason daily draws upon the teachings from his M.S. in Education Media Design & Technology (Full Sail University), as well his B.S. in Information Technology & Communication Design (CSU Monterey Bay, emphasis Instructional Design). These skills are constantly incorporated into his own self-employed Wedding Entertainment Business.

Prior to starting his own company in 2009, Jason held a thirteen-year stint with Sound In Motion, a Campbell/Santa Cruz based mobile DJ company. During this time, Jason performed at events for organizations such as San Jose Sharks, San Jose Stealth, Smash Mouth, Seal, and The Howard Stern Show. Trained to be a DJ/MC, he eventually became a regional sales manager for the company's Campbell and Monterey-Carmel locations.
