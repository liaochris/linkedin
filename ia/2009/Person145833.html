Lynn Dye’s Summary

As a graduate of AssistU, a nationally-recognized program for virtual assistants, I have the training, skills and ethics to help you make your small business succeed. I utilize emerging web technologies as well as phone, fax and e-mail, to provide administrative and personal support in long-term, collaborative relationships with small business owners.

As a highly-trained virtual assistant, I build relationships with partnering clients--meeting and even anticipating a variety of needs. Beyond completing tasks, my goal is to create value for my clients through a dynamic learning process. The more I know and understand your business, the more I can help. That's why I prefer to partner with only a handful of clients rather than accepting multiple “piecework” assignments from many clients.

My areas of expertise include email and calendar management, troubleshooting email and basic computer issues, knowledgeable in computer set-up and installation of the proper security software, setting up and maintenance of social networking sites, finding and setting up the right collaboration tools for you, creating eye-popping PowerPoint presentations, developing or maintaining a spreadsheet, database or newsletter, writing or proofreading, I have the expertise and tools to help your business.

My background includes working in the finance, medical and insurance industries. I’m experienced in working remotely and am keenly aware communication and trust are key in building a productive business partnership.

I flourish in a fast paced environment and enjoy meeting deadlines. I have the ability to grasp your business’ concepts and climb into your business to positively impact your bottom line.
