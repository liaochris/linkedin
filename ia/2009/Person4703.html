Adam Grossman’s Summary

Adam Grossman recently graduated with an MBA at the UCLA Anderson School of Management where he concentrated on Social Entrepreneurship and Environmental Business Strategy. His long term career goal is to work with firms to embed environmentally sustainable practices into their operational and business strategies which positively impact fiscal, environmental and social value. Adam is currently pursuing this goal through his work as a product development intern at The X-PRIZE Foundation by developing prizes with large monetary incentives that promote socially positive innovation.
