Chris Gilbey’s Summary

My experience is in developing opportunities where there is convergence of technology, consumer interest and opportunity to work with media.

I was CEO of ASX listed Lake Technology, which was acquired in a friendly takeover by Dolby Laboratories, and then consulted to Dolby on Consumer Market Strategy.

Prior to that I was SVP of BMG Records, and prior to that ran a JV publishing company with MCA/Universal.

I have an understanding of how to map and leverage business and consumer ecosystems particularly when there is the potential to build consumer demand.

My primary interest is to work with enterprises which can benefit from lateral thinking and want to build sustainability.

I work well with creative people and try to give them the opportunity to create their own success stories through mentorship.

I have been successful at fund raising to enable enterprises to be built and to grow and ultimately achieve exits.
