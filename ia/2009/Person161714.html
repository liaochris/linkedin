Michael Yublosky’s Summary

I began my career as a residential electronic security specialist in 1981 for ADT. In 1985 I went to National Guardian Security, also in residential. From 1986 to 1990 I was Residential sales manager, National Guardian. I branched into the commercial arena, in addition to residential, in 1993. National Guardian was purchased by SecurityLink (Ameritech), and my primary focus shifted gradually to commercial. My involvement in complex residential systems, bordering on commerical applications, continued and continues to this date. SecurityLink, was bought out by Southwest Bell for a short time before being purchased by The Cambridge Group. This group was headed by the original owner of SecurityLink. Within six months we were purchase by ADT. My ADT tenure was only extended back to my 1985 start at National Guardian.

My goals are to continue to know and learn the products ADT sells as well as the services it offers. To treat customers and associates with respect and professional integrity. To help my fellow workers improve their product knowledge and sales skills.

Michael Yublosky’s Specialties:

My first love is closed circuit television (see my interests). I am extremely well versed in fire alarm applications for small commercial and industrial applications, as well as burglar alarms, access control, system integration, alert broadcasting, etc.
