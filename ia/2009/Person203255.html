Ronald van der Aart’s Summary

The Rules of the Great PR Game have changed. Whether we like it or not. It is not about stakeholder dialogue anymore. No more b2b. Goodbye b2c. Currently our stakeholders are occupied discussing us on a wide variety of social media platforms. C2c in optima forma. Transparency tyranny.

And you know what? They do not like us to interfere. And is that not still exactly what we want as PR professionals? Contact. Exchanging views. Creating relationships. Building brands. Well, good luck ....

PR 2.0? You may wish! PR 3.0 is the new credo. It is about 24/7 direct interaction with our stakeholder groups. Creating engaging content. Crowd sourcing. Co-creation. Our profession has never been more interesting. Or did you not notice yet?
