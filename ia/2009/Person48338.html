Colin Minto’s Summary

Recognised as the UK recruitment industry's leading expert in on-line recruitment strategy, recruiting technology, software and innovation, specialising in candidate/talent acquisition, management, retention, online recruitment strategy, business and social networking, employer/recruiter branding, referral schemes and internet presence.

Previously Managing Director of Brainhunter's UK operations, operating 16 niche job boards in partnership with major professional and corporate membership organisations. Supplying staffing/recruitment services and innovative ATS solutions. Launched a team of flexible hours recruitment consultants supported by a specialist outsourced team of resourcers and lead generators in Hyderabad, India.

Previously the Acting Technology Director of and Managing Director of the technology subsidiary of the Recruitment and Employment Confederation, which is the trade association for the £28bn UK recruitment industry.

Passionate about the recruitment industry, the best application of technology and candidate acquisition processes into the traditional recruitment model, social and community engagement technology, innovation and local, regional and national community development initiatives.
