Martin Mesecke, CFP’s Summary

Martin Philip Mesecke, CFP® is the founder and President of Self-Worth Financial Planning LLC, a Fee-Only financial planning and wealth management practice. Martin takes his extensive knowledge of financial products and services and combines it with the "client-first" philosophy embraced by all Fee-Only planners.

Self-Worth Financial Planning is truly unique when it comes to offering our clients real choices in choosing financial planning advice. "Our clients can choose to engage our services on either an hourly, as-needed basis or a engage us in a complete financial plan." Martin continues, "We impose no income or net worth minimums in order to become our client, nor are there any long-term contracts to sign.

For clients seeking wealth management and investment advice, Self-Worth Financial Planning was one of the first NAPFA planners in Texas to be approved to offer clients direct access to Dimensional Fund Advisors.

Our investment philosophy is based on Nobel Prize-winning academic research on optimal long-term strategies, not Wall Street hype or fads. Our investment services are focused on the design, construction and management of investment portfolios specifically tailored to the return objectives and risk tolerances of each of our clients.

Martin Mesecke, CFP’s Specialties:

Martin specializes in providing fee-only financial planning services and investment advice to individuals and families, on a one-time or as-needed hourly basis. Clients can receive a complete financial plan or focus on one or more particular financial concerns such as:

• Retirement Planning and Distribution Strategies
• Comprehensive Financial Planning
• College Funding Advice
• Elder Care Issues
• Second Opinions
• Estate Planning

Martin Mesecke, CFP’s Honors:

Martin has been sought out by local and national media on numerous occasions and his insights regarding financial matters have appeared in The Dallas Morning News, Kiplinger's Personal Finance, and Inside Collin County Business among others. He was an instrumental contributor to "Just Give Me the Answer$: Expert Advisors Address Your Most Pressing Financial Questions", published by Dearborn Trade Publication. Martin is a regular contributor to Bankrate.com "Expert Comments" regarding interest rates and also has served as a technical advisor for money makeover articles for both Money Magazine and Kiplinger's Personal Finance Magazine.

Martin Mesecke CFP, is a frequent speaker and educator on financial planning topics and a past instructor in the Southern Methodist University Certificate Program in Financial Planning in Dallas.

He is past president of leadership Allen class of XVII and serves as an Ambassador in both the Allen and Plano Chamber of Commerce's.
