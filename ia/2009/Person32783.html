Bret G. Dudl’s Summary

“The Center of Wellness”
Bret G. Dudl, Developer ~ La Joya Perfecta, Costa Rica’s highest value wellness retirement community.

Prior to founding La Joya Perfecta and becoming Director of Sales, Mr. Dudl was started a 401(k) investment company from scratch and grew it in to one of the largest firms of it’s kind in San Diego with over $350 Million qualified in retirement plan assets covering more than 25,000 employees.

After selling that business in 2007, Mr. Dudl moved to Costa Rica to pursue the “Pura Vida” lifestyle. Pura Vida means “Pure Life” in Spanish and it is the unofficial national saying of all of Costa Rica. Mr. Dudl then purchased the 164 acre farm that boasts 5 waterfalls, white water ocean views, in Costa Rica’s famous Osa Peninsula, well knows as the most Bio-diverse region in the world. This farm became what is known today as La Joya Perfecta, “The Perfect Jewell”, Costa Rica’s Premier affordable wellness community.

It was in 2007 when Mr. Dudl’s Brother, Eric P. Dudl, PhD was diagnosed with terminal cancer, who passed away at the age of 33. This incident so moved the Dudl family to Found an entire community dedicated to providing affordable wellness services to the masses. With the commitment of helping people to Look Younger, Feel Better, and Live Longer…affordable, La Joya Perfecta is a unique community filled with holistic professionals and like minded individuals pursuing a clean health lifestyle in paradise.

For more information please contact Bret G. Dudl at info@costaricanwellness.com or check out www.costaricanwellness.com.
