Justin Farrell’s Summary

In my relatively short advertising career I have strategically chosen to work for organizations that provide me with the most product diversity and diversity of clientèle.

My work at Yellowbook allowed me the opportunity to work with small to mid-sized local businesses' across all industries selling advertising into the print yellow pages, online yellow pages, and search engines.

Working at Permission Data allows me to work on national advertising campaigns with large organizations and advertising agencies on many different pricing models. Furthermore, the consumer data I gather on behalf of my customers is used for their email marketing, direct mail, telemarketing and e-commerce initiatives so I am required to have a strong understanding of those marketing mediums as well.

Aside from my desire to know everything about everything I feel the more experience I have with different forms of media, types of industries, sizes of organizations, pricing models, etc., allows me to be a better strategic partner to my clients and in the long run benefit me personally and financially.
