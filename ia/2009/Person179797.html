My bio:

Jake Atwood is a Coach, Keynote Speaker, and Sales Innovator who has spent the last decade consulting with companies who have complex sales cycles. After conducting over 4000 coaching sessions with sales professionals, he founded Ovation Sales Group with a goal to change the way that people sell. Using sales strategy, personal experience, and a lot of creativity, he has developed Prospecting Systems for some of America's largest corporations.

What makes Jake so unique in the world of consulting is that he walks the talk. He has personally made over 100,000 phone calls, left over 25,000 voice mail messages, and conducted thousands of appointments to test his ideas. This in-the-trenches approach produces breakthrough solutions that are based on practical application and not on textbook theory. In fact, clients often comment that Jake easily relates to many of the challenges they face because he speaks from personal experience. Ultimately, his passion for sales and entrepreneurial spirit have been key reasons for his success and the success of his clients.
