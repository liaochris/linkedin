Christian Young’s Summary

My main career focus is in work flow analysis, usability, human factoring and UI design. I have been in the web, engineering, and UI field since 1998. I have worked in companies ranging from the United States Senate through small start-ups, and my most recent position is the SR User Experience Designer at Sun Microsystems.

My duties have included managing web-design and graphic design teams, creating design and UI specs, storyboarding websites and web-based applications, coding websites and web-based applications, optimizing websites using SEO techniques, localizing websites and applications, optimizing UI design, usability and human factor enhancements, optimizing and embedding video for websites, editing and encoding video for seminars and demonstration, building and maintain blogs in Wordpress and Typepad and maintaining content management websites.
