Scott Denson’s Summary

Responsible for setting strategic and technical direction in the all areas of operations including budgeting, contract negotiations and global staff management and contractor/outsourced vendor relationships. Operation Teams included Helpdesk, Voice technology (VoIP, PBX, Cell Phone Management), Global Networks, Communications (Email and Instant Msg), Identity Management, Microsoft Solutions, Novell Solutions, Unix Services and Datacenters Management.

Building organizations to be self managed is what I do. Sometimes having to make hard decisions to improve the organization to the next level, but always with a focus of what is best for the health of the organization and the corporation.

I enjoy taking on the tough projects or issues, bringing together people of all levels, focusing on the goals, and succeeding as a team. I try to instill an ownership of the initiatives within the teams so that they are part of the process and the successes.

3 Key projects of my recent success include….

Deployment of MPLS over a 2 year period for a fortune 300 company. Increasing bandwidth services by 200% while reducing cost by $3 Million. At the same time orchestrating the strategic need for a Disaster Recovery model and integrating that need into the MPLS design.

Active Director Deployment to over 44,000 employee’s world wide. Including architecting and designing the ability to remote manage desktops, application publishing and streamlining centralization management of services and servers. 30% reduction in staffing required, 50% reduction in Total Cost of Ownership.

Development of an IT security organization within a fortune 300 company reporting to the CIO; implementation of security organization was based on ISO 17799 best practices. Allowing for a stronger and more effective IT management of security and better protection of the corporation from virus attacks and information theft. Including use of ITIL/COBIT and other best practices.

ITIL V3 Certified.
