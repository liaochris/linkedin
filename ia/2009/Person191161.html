Paul Woodhouse’s Summary

Since obtaining my Computing Degree in 2004 I have worked for Wyre Borough Council in Poulton-le-Fylde as a Development Officer on the I.T. Development Team and now as a Software and Systems Engineer. Working in a small team I have accumulated a great deal of experience, working on a wide range of tasks.

My primary focus has been on the development of Web Applications, specifically the in-house designed and built website Content Management System (CMS) and now also leading the deployment and development of a corporate wide CRM (Lagan ECM) and a server virtualisation projects.

During my time at Wyre I have received training in a number of areas, primarily Windows Server 2003, SQL Server 2000/5 and IIS. In addition I have attended many shorter technical courses on specific products such as Lagan ECM, Dell Storage Area Network (SAN) administration, Civica Financials support and F.A.S.T. software auditing. Some of the more technical courses I have attended were provided for the purpose of bridging the technical support and development teams when working on projects together where IT projects are increasingly requiring knowledge from both sides to obtain the best solution.

As a Software and Systems Engineer I am called upon to provide end-user support for more technical software related Help Desk calls and provide, with other members of staff, end user training on basic Windows / Office applications and some 3rd Party Applications. I also have technical responsibility for several small and large scale systems and meet regularly with customers and suppliers to discuss user requirements and assist with their development.
