John Hamon’s Summary

Finding an interest in graphics a few years ago, I decided to pursue a degree in Graphic Design & Animation. My interests quickly grew into a passion as I decided to offer creative services to friends & family, in efforts to assist them in promoting their own ventures. These small services grew to a much larger scale as I officially became a freelance designer in the summer of 2006. Over the past few years, I was blessed with the opportunity to work for some great companies and with great people, but I knew it wasn't my calling. Once the year of 2008 rolled over, I knew it was time to pursue this on a higher level. Thus, stakkeddesigns, LLC was born.

I actually got my LLC for the business in February 2007, and taking that year to work for SDS / HealthPort, combined with freelancing on the side, got me to where I am today. Being able to provide creative services for small to mid-size businesses, in and out of the state, I have realized the true potential of hard work and honest business. For those who have heard in life, "Hard work doesn't pay off", or "Good guys always finish last", just realize that I, along with thousands of other successful entrepreneurs, disprove that theory.
