Will Kingwell’s Summary

Will has worked with SAP for the past 13 years, in the areas of SAP BW/BI, HR, FI, and Security. He has in excess of 15 end-to-end implementations, and involvement in numerous part engagements.

Will’s SAP experience begun with R3 Financial Accounting, becoming SAP-FI certified. This complemented his Commerce and IT degrees. He completed the Professional Year program with the Institute of Chartered Accountants during his time with PriceWaterhouseCoopers giving him a solid understanding of Financials. Will later moved into SAP-HR consulting, where he was involved in a number of full HR life cycles over three years. During this time he changed his SAP focus to SAP BW and SAP Portals.

Will has worked with SAP-BI for approximately 6 years in the capacity of BI Team Lead, BI Technical Lead, BI Contract Architect, and BI Consultant. This experience is across Project Systems, Finance, Logistics, Human Resources, Retail, and CRM processes.

Will has been responsible for delivering highly customised BI solutions though to “out-of-the-box” Business Package/Content solutions. Will has a very high level of skills in the BW and Portals integration layer. During this 6 years working with SAP BW/BI, Will was the owner of a the BW system for 2 years.

Will’s strong technical skills in SAP BI and Business Objects, combined with his business experience and Project Management skills, make him an extremely strong addition to any BW/BI team.

Will Kingwell’s Specialties:

SAP-BI/BW as primary skill, with strong SAP Security, SAP Portals, and Business Intelligence Architecture.
