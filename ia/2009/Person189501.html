Philip Ramsey’s Summary

I have worked primarily with internet-related technologies and have delivered research-based portals for some of the top legal, educational, and standards organizations in the world. I have managed development teams and products through their entire life cycle from cradle to grave and have maintained systems, developed processes, enforced quality assurance plans, designed applications for data delivery, data security, end-user tools, and alert management software. I have also designed, implemented and managed a successful internet hosting service.

I specialize in analyzing and streamlining processes and workflows to make future development cycles operate smoothly. I have excellent skills in working with people at all levels of the corporate hierarchy and at maintaining those relationships. I am very well known for his creative and out-of-the-box techniques to deliver simple and elegant solutions to complex problems, I strongly believe that the obvious and simplest answer is the one most people overlook.
