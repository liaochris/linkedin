Superior craftsmanship, and attention to detail are synonymous to our company's name. We're an accredited company with the Better Business Bureau Southeast division, giving you the peace of mind that you're dealing with a company that takes your best interest seriously and makes it our main priority. Our construction and remodeling services is offered all through Miami-Dade and expands throughout South Florida for both residential and commercial clients.

    CF Construction and Remodeling, Inc. specializes in the interior remodeling of luxury homes and luxury condominiums all throughout South Florida. We also specialize in the interior remodeling of corporate offices, medical offices, high-end restaurants and hotels. In addition to our remodeling services, we also offer construction services. Whether you're looking to add more living space and or working pace in your residence and or business by doing an addition, we can also assist you with that. If you're looking to build a brand new home, or business facility from the ground, we can help you with that as well.
