Christopher Flockton’s Summary

Christopher Flockton is a British voiceover artist, actor and writer based in New York City.

For the past six years and 110 episodes, Chris has been the narrator and personality behind VH1’s top-rated show, The Fabulous Life, taking a fast-paced and decidedly tongue in cheek look at celebrities, their excessive lifestyles, fashions and more. Chris is always available for voiceover work from his own fully-equipped New York studio.

As an actor, Chris was a member of New York's acclaimed Rumble in the RedRoom sketch comedy ensemble for 4 years, and later wrote and performed with his own sketch comedy group, The Belgian Summers. On stage, he has appeared in roles both serious and comedic at regional theatres around the country, and off-Broadway. In addition, Chris has appeared in featured roles in film and on TV, including a recurring role on The Guiding Light.

Chris has worked extensively in industrials and corporate events all over the world. From Boston to Bali, San Francisco to Singapore, Chris has appeared in videos, hosted product launches and trade shows, performed sketches, and brought his unique combination of buttoned-down British authority and hilarious improvised comedy to audiences from 50 to 5,000 people. He was also a corporate spokesperson for Oracle Corporation for several years.

Chris has appeared in dozens of TV commercials including spots for Diet Coke, Range Rover, IBM, HSBC, The Wall Street Journal, and Pilot Pens and has provided the voices for many, many more.

As a writer, Chris has created countless sketches, corporate theatre pieces, TV comedy interstitials, humorous essays, and has ghostwritten speeches for leading corporate executives.
