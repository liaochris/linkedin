Espen Sogn’s Summary

Character animator with broad ranging experience from TV shows and films to computer game animation.
My main interest lies in the artistic side of animation but I'm not afraid to tackle technical problems. In fact I quite enjoy that aspect.
I prefer working in a team as I like the synergy between different people and disciplines.

Espen Sogn’s Specialties:

Character Animation - I've worked with many styles from realistic to cartoony.
Motion Capture - From planning it and directing it to implementing it.
Cutscenes - I have a strong understanding of how games work and how to design and incorporate cutscenes.
Software - I've used Maya extensively and I have a working knowledge of Motion Builder, 3D Studio Max and Lightwave.
Other - I've done basic LUA scripting.
