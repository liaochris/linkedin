Sean Pisano’s Summary

I am Sean Pisano.
I have been involved in graphic and web design and web development for over 7 years. Based in Brooklyn New York, I consider myself a designer, developer and digital artist. I specialize in fresh, dynamic sites with a strong focus on usability, accessibility and web standards. Good design facilitates the smooth flow of information and clear communication makes it possible for a website to get its intended results. I create successful user-focused interface designs through exhaustive planning and by taking a user-centered approach that fulfills the clients’ business needs. Every business has a purpose and a promise; the right visual layout, typography, color, and graphics can accurately define the client's brand and influence how they are perceived. By helping the client clearly identify their desired market position, strategy, and goals, I create websites that best represents their business.
