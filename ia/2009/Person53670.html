Daniel James’s Summary

Executive software development manager and system architect with over 10 years of experience in information technology. Since 2004, virtualization technologies such as VMware have been a focus in my professional career. Major interests lie in virtualization, performance optimization, data warehousing, and the creation and use of very large databases.

Virtualization experience primarily with VMware. Built Raideil Inc., a professional services and hosting company, on a fully-virtualized, high-availability infrastructure that provides managed cloud computing to its clients.

Active software developer fluent in Java, JSP, SQL (various dialects), and XHTML. Also have large amount of experience with Perl (as well as various scripting languages), C#, VB.NET, and ColdFusion. Primarily focused on web application development with database backing.
