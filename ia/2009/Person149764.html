Margaret Bendet’s Summary

With thirty years' experience as a professional editor, writer, and interviewer, I serve as an editorial troubleshooter, getting wayward prose back on track. I have a particular love for helping people to tell their stories.

Editor: Everything from academic articles, memoirs, and cookbooks to professional websites and fliers. Nonfiction/fiction; developmental/copyediting.

Writer: Stories, essays, business letters, websites--communicating information of all kinds.

Interviewer: Personal histories and stories captured on audiotape, retold in essays that are far beyond scrapbook pieces.

Rewrite Specialist: In other words, ghost-writing--the edits come out of conversations so that I can make the piece what you truly want to say.

Creative Consultant: If you're stymied on a writing project, I can advise you on how to proceed toward completion.

Margaret Bendet’s Specialties:

International culture, Eastern philosophy, history, food, physical therapies such as Reiki, massage, and the Alexander Technique.
