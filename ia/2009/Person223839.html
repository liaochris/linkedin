Steve Enright’s Summary

Stephen J. (Steve) Enright is a veteran of leading change in human resources management, with 15 years in HR executive positions. He has built and developed many successful HR teams at companies like Wang Laboratories, Union Pacific, and Overnite Transportation (now UPS Freight) where he was VP – Human Resources. More recently he served as SVP – Human Resources for CDC Software , and is now VP - Human Resources at Caritas Christi Health Care.

Steve is a strategic and conceptual thinker with a proven ability to execute and take organizations to the highest levels of performance.

Steve is a graduate of the University of Connecticut School of Business, with an MBA from the University of Baltimore, and recently earned the Certificate in Strategic Human Resources Management from the Cornell ILR school. He holds an SPHR certification from the Society for Human Resource Management, and is an Eagle Scout.

Steve believes in a positive, proactive and collaborative approach to human resource management, in HR adding long term value, and in an integrated approach to people management systems. This combination of experience, education and philosophy provides a unique insight and capability.
