Laura Walden’s Summary

Director and Founder of SportsFeatures.com with 20 years of experience in the Olympic Movement working in the media and communications sector.

The website and SFC Press Point sports press release distribution system were launched to meet the growing demand for a one stop news gateway dedicated solely to the current issues in Elite sports.

In 2008 through a partnership with Keir Radnedge, SportsFeatures.com added original content sections with Olympics Features and Soccer World.

There are overall 9,000 breaking headlines on the Olympics, Premier League and the European soccer leagues allowing readers to stay updated 24/7/365.

Laura Walden’s Specialties:

World sport news monitoring, creating targeted news, PR and communications in the sports sector, magazines, translating, negotiating mergers, pitching stories to high level news and media from all platforms.
