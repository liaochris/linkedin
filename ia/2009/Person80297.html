Forrest Graves ☆Power Networker☆’s Summary

Forrest Graves
Business Consultant, Network Technologist, Entrepreneur
Forrest Graves’ career in Graphic Arts began in 1991 with BARCO Graphics of Ghent Belgium and continued as he held positions with companies such as Atlanta Graphic Services and Action Digital Color. His experiences and knowledge throughout his 18 years in the industry have enabled him to add value and insight in the areas of general management, operations, and sales and marketing.
In 1996, Graves founded and operated “InterLead” - an Internet & Technology company. His focus was to educate and prepare businesses to improve on their operating efficiencies via the Internet.
2002 marked the beginning of Forrest’s career with Hewlett-Packard where he served as a sales navigation specialist for HP’s Digital Publishing Solutions Group (DPS). His success is marked by his contributions in bolstering demand for high-end products and for his customer relationship management and sales abilities. His experience with HP spans several product categories and departments within the Imaging & Printing Group (IPG) including marketing, sales, and sales operations. Most recently, Graves was a Business Consultant for IPG’s Digital Publishing Solutions Group and held the distinction of HP IPG Ambassador.
Graves’ newest entrepreneurial venture is in the specialty coffee arena where he founded and owns “JumpinGoat Coffee Roasters.”
Forrest Graves earned a Computer Science Networking degree in 2002 and holds certifications of achievement in numerous digital, and fiber optic technologies. Most recently he has been pursuing study in Six Sigma sales methodology.
Graves has four children, and is three times a grandfather. He lives in Cleveland Georgia with his wife and youngest daughter where he enjoys organic vegetable gardening, raising game birds, ultra light back-packing, and fly-fishing.
Forrest can be reached @ 706-809-0143 or forrest@jumpingoat.com
