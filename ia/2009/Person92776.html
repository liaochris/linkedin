Neal Hart’s Summary

Attorney Neal L. Hart, is pictured above, with Office Administrator Rebecca D. Scott.

Mr. Hart competently and aggressively represents injured and disabled workers and citizens throughout the entire State of Arkansas. He also routinely represents injured citizens of other states who have Arkansas claims. Finally, Mr. Hart is privileged to represent an exceptionally select group of self-insured employers and insurance carriers, primarily in workers' compensation claims.

Neal Hart’s Specialties:

Hart Law Firm, L.L.P. consists of: Neal L. Hart, Attorney at Law; John R. Peel (of counsel), Attorney at Law; Rebecca D. Scott, Office Administrator, and; Jim Miners, Law Clerk. Our law practice concentrates primarily on high-exposure litigation, in the fields listed below. We pride ourselves in providing personalized service, at the highest levels of professionalism and expertise. We welcome and covet your telephone calls and questions about any size or type of legal problem.
