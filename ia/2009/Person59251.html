Davina Federgreen’s Summary

I have spent most of my life running my own businesses. For the last 11 years have been with Lifemates learning how people mentally and emotionally operate.As a relationship consultant for the past 11 years, it has allowed me to understand human behavior, cause and effect to their reactions, finding people trust in me to reveal their private lives, finding the quick solutions to their problems and implementing their positive new approach to the end result that they are looking for.

Because I am both logical and analytical, also creative, for the past 6 months I have taken on the role in the marketing side of the business. I always have enjoyed watching an idea come to fruition. Now I am doing a lot of consumer shows for the company to get us into more warmer type markets and be there to personally educate the client on our service. Also doing Gala events with a twist on choosing a non-profit sector to work with in each event. Networking and referrals are the best ways to achieve success, as they are more apt to join a service when there is a comfort level and a small amount of trust going in.

I have just started my own business again, to create Gala events to help the non-profit sector, as I feel that there are so many people out there that are not helping as much as they should. I also believe in Karma we give and we shall receive. Very important to have balance in ones life. I am also focusing on doing large consumer shows across North America, venturing into the European market eventually.

My forte is to create dynamic one of a kind, exciting, entertaining, interactive events that the audience will never forget and can't wait for the next event to take place.
