Alastair Adam’s Summary

* Corporate finance and M&A with Cazenove & Co., a leading London investment bank.

* Senior Principal at The Parthenon Group, a Boston-based international strategy-consulting firm.

* Founded The Callidon Group in 2002.

* Merged The Callidon Group with OC&C Strategy Consultants in 2007.

* Masters in Law from Cambridge University.

* Fellow of the Securities and Investment Institute (www.sii.org.uk)

* Fellow and former Executive Committee Member of The British American Project of the Royal Institute of International Affairs and the John Hopkins School of Advanced International Studies. (www.baponline.org)

* Chair of the Board of Trustees of The Action Center for Educational Services and Scholarships. (www.accessboston.org)

* Fifteen years advising private equity groups and senior executives across a wide range of industries, including publishing and online information, retail banking, commercial banking, insurance, flavor, fragrance and food manufacturing, food and general retail, building materials, building and construction, and electronic and electrical equipment.

* Have helped companies develop Growth Strategies, as well as working in the areas of Customer and Market Due Diligence, Acqusition Value Assessment, Post-Merger Integration, Pricing, Sales Force Effectiveness, Cost Reduction and Process Improvement.

* See www.occstrategy.com for more detail.
