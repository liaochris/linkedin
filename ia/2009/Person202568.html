Roger Mitchell’s Summary

Senior Executive with CEO and company doctor experience. Operating on the public markets and at Board level.

Through own company Albachiara.

Qualified Scottish CA, CFO level, Big 4 training.
International deal-flow, including leading an AIM IPO for a Chinese company.

Private equity investment and VC experience, in the area of investigation, due diligence and execution, often followed by ongoing advisory role.

Fluent in detailed financial reporting and systems/controls implementation.

Sector experience in exploiting content, (sports, media, gaming and entertainment), across digital platforms. Additional experience in FMCG, TMT and mobile Telco.

International and cross-cultural background with language abilities.

Excellent references and industry contacts at Board level.
Experienced Non-Executive Director, also on public capital markets.
