Ian Vanlint’s Summary

Indigo Star Consulting is dedicated to the Sales and Marketing needs of small and medium sized enterprises (SME’s). We work closely with ambitious SME’s in the UK who are determined to achieve strong and sustainable sales growth by implementing best practice. Many of these companies have new or innovative products with real potential
We help them realise their goals by offering state of the art advice, knowledge transfer, and direct involvement. As our expertise extends across both Sales and Marketing disciplines, we can ensure both functions are fully harmonised for maximum impact.

Ian Vanlint at Indigo Star Consulting has extensive experience of winning multi-million pound contracts from Blue Chip Companies on behalf of SME's.
