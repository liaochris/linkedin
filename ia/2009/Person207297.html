Bob Howard’s Summary

A recognized sales strategy innovator and thought leader, Bob is the Founder and Managing Partner of Sales OverDrive and an Advisor to Venture and Philanthropy.

Bob has had a long and distinguished career selling professional services and technology solutions. Since 1996 when he founded Sales OverDrive's predecessor company, GTM Logic, Bob and his partners and associates have been building successful sales engines for a wide variety of clients. The firm's focus has been on accelerating market momentum and sales velocity through design and rapid deployment of sales and marketing strategies, recruitment, training, and mobilization of inside and field sales professionals, the achievement of large wins with key accounts, and the attainment of critical business alliances and venture capital.

Earlier in his career, Bob spent 15 years in corporate financing services, principally with GE Capital and PruCapital. He was responsible for selling, structuring and closing a large number of complex financing solutions for Fortune 500 and smaller companies.

Bob holds a BA in Physical Anthropology and MBA in Finance and Administration from the University of Texas at Austin, where he was a Kleberg Student. He has been a frequent visiting speaker at the Graduate School of Business and has testified before the FERC and a number of other government agencies.

He has been a supporting member of a number of civic and business organizations including The Association for Corporate Growth, Dallas Museum of Art, Ernst & Young Alumni Association, Institute of Management Consultants, Richardson Chamber of Commerce, Technology Business Council, Texas Business Network, Turnaround Management Association, and the UT Alumni Association. Bob is also actively involved in prison ministry, is the father of three sons and an avid scuba diver.
