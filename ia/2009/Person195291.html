Rebecca DuBois’s Summary

I have over 22 years experience in the advertising, graphic design and marketing industry in the Capital Region. Not only have I advised my clients regarding advertising and printing, I have also been a small business owner myself for over 13 years. Being able to see things from both an advertising perspective and a business owner's perspective has given me a unique skill set. My career has now come full circle and my passion is helping small and medium sized businesses succeed by not only offering them advertising space but also advertising concepts and strategies. I have found an outlet to do this through the Coffee News. I enjoy networking and creating relationships with other business owners in the area and offering to help them grow their business and stand out amongst their competition through our EXCLUSIVE & TARGETED advertising opportunities.

Rebecca DuBois’s Specialties:

Advertising Sales, Publishing, Creative Copywriting, Graphic Design, Branding & Identity, Print Buying & Project Management and Advertising Consultation, Networking and connecting like-minded business owners with one other.
