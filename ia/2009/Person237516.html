Tracy Previte’s Summary

Founding president and current Treasurer of the Beachwood Chapter of American Business Women’s Association, Women of Wisdom. I work full time at Progressive Insurance where I have been since April 2006 as a Data Analyst. Previous to Progressive, I was with a mortgage title company for 13 years working in the administrative and operations divisions.

I enjoy the computer aspects of my work and life. This includes helping with the development of the new systems, advocating to end users the usefulness of incorporating the new systems into their routines, advocating the end users’ needs to incorporate into the new system, implementing and testing the new systems, training the end users on the new systems, and teaching computer newbies how to navigate Microsoft products, including Excel - my favorite. My newest adventure will be teaching people how to blog and what it is all about.

I graduated Cum Laude at Tiffin University in Tiffin Ohio for my undergraduate degree in Business Administration, and received my MBA at Baldwin-Wallace College with a focus in Entrepreneur studies and a grade point of 3.925. When not working or consulting with individuals, I enjoy volunteering at the Cleveland Natural History Museum as a docent, networking, playing around in ABWA Ohio’s Web site, reading business literature relating to women and success, and singing in local community choruses.
