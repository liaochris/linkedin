Deb (Roxy) Cross’s Experience

  • Owner

    roxycross designs llc

    (Public Company; GOOG; Design industry)

    March 1998 — Present (11 years 7 months)

    After years of marketing and media work, I turned a lucrative hobby into a business! Designing, planting, shipping eco-friendly planters and baskets! My award-winning designs have been featured in magazines, commercials and movies in historical homes, private residences, businesses, parks and municipalities.

    Although I did not create these economical, environmentally excellent products, I have promoted them for years! Using engineered octogon shaped holes in the sides of reuseable baskets and planters, we plant horizontally and vertically, creating 5 to 7 feet of blooms. Our planters are used in such highly visible places as DisneyWorld, Disneyland, Bouchart Gardens, Salt Lake's Temple Square, Macy's in New York and 6 Flags Over Everywhere!

    At roxycross designs llc, we teach others how to plant in these amazing baskets empowering people to garden when they might not have otherwise. Our planters and baskets replace moss baskets, which are depleting the Canadian Geese nesting grounds, invite parasites and wick moisture away from plant roots. Our baskets use less water, earth and space. Where one tomato plant will use 5 gallons of water/day in the ground, you can put several plus pepper plants, herbs with flowers and use 2 or more gallons/day for the whole basket of 42 plants!

    We design baskets to beautify homes and businesses and also create ways to grow tremendous amounts of food with no weeding or bug issues. In 2009 we partnered with Suzy and Dan Dailey and others to grow food for those in need in Ogden in the Ogden Community Garden at 30th St and Harrison. Our goal is to see how much food we can grow in our our planters with little impact to the environment! Stay tuned and we'll let you know how that goes.

    Still creating the business side with logo, website and such. In the meantime, our weblink is to pix on my Facebook page. Enjoy!
