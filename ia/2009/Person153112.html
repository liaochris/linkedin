Marlies Nijenhuis’s Summary

Having just finished the course of Communication, my head is filled with fresh knowledge from many lessons and books. During two internships I was able to use my knowledge for achievement, and I can’t wait to continue doing this.
I love writing since the age of six, and will never stop loving it. A well kept online journal during my foreign adventures in South Africa and Sweden is the digital prove. I have a special interest in development issues and politics. I was pleased to get a bookish knowledge from two courses in Sweden.

Over the years, I’ve started to believe that a better world really does start with yourself. By making the right decisions, buying the best products, and using your knowledge to create a better life for people in development countries, we can change the world. You just have to get started.

Open to foreign functions, preferably in Sweden, Norway, Denmark or the United Kingdom. Able to speak and write Dutch and English fluently. Basic knowledge of Swedish. Can easily adjust to new circumstances having done so in several places over the world. And despite being pro-biking: I’m in the possession of a driver’s licence.
