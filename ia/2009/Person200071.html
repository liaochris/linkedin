Rob Enslin’s Summary

I started my web career as an online project manager in 2001 working for Penton Media Europe. I then moved into technical web development & design as well as email marketing. Working as a contractor I've gained valuable consulting experience and by 2008 I moved into a full-time position as a Web Manager at CMP Information.

As my role developed and evolved and I changed my position to a Digital Manager where my role extended into various market-focused digital initiatives including web 2.0, social media, modern media and digital strategy. My other duties include providing solutions and support across our Live Media division.
