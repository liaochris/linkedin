Steve Levy’s Summary

I am a senior legal team leader with international focus and expertise. My experience covers a broad range of legal tools to manage the assets and exposures of companies at all stages of development from small startups through large, multinationals. I also have extensive experience with, and perspective on, the full range of intellectual property tools and assets (trademarks, copyrights, patents, domain names, etc.). I take a very pragmatic and creative approach to executive counseling and problem solving.
