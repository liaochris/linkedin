Bryan Alaspa’s Summary

I am currently a full-time freelance writer but I also work for K Squared Communications, a PR and Marketing firm from Chicago. While with them I have written press releases, by-lined articles and proposals.

I have written website content and marketing materials from companies as diverse as real estate agencies to urban warfar training companies. I have created brochures, white pages and other marketing materials such as e-mail blasts. I have written training materials for online courses and universities. I have written articles for magazines and ghost-written magazine articles. I have published five fiction novels and two works of non-fiction.

My goal is to continue to gain larger and larger clients. I wish to continue to support myself solely on my writing. I also hope to gain enough readership on my sports blog; Chicago Minor League Review... to eventually sell ads and make money from that.

I also currently contribute regularly on the website Management Issues. They can be found at www.management-issues.com. I am a blogger and writer there writing about business and management issues.
