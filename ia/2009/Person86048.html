Goli Fassihian’s Summary

Goli is a seasoned communications professional with 15 years work experience planning and implementing large-scale PR/media and marketing programs in agency, corporate and non-profit settings. She has a diverse professional background covering international affairs, public policy/advocacy and education, and consumer and public health (international and domestic) issues.

She has a proven track record mentoring and motivating staff, managing multi-million dollar budgets, developing aggressive media strategies and generating publicity and media coverage through established relationships with journalists and global media outlets.

Goli is intellectually driven and a creative self-starter with passion and interest in understanding different cultures. Goli has traveled throughout the Middle East, Asia, Africa and Europe and is a Native Farsi speaker.

Goli has represented pharma/biotech companies; government and multilateral agencies; nonprofit organizations including NGOs, advocacy, research and educational organizations; as as well as notable authors, scholars, Nobel peace prize winners and entrepreneurs.
