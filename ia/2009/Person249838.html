Ryan Yepsen’s Summary

I have recently accepted a position with the Chicago Youth Centers as a Recruiter / Program Director. Here, I will be in charge of program development for their summer camp in Dowagiac, MI, Camp Rosenthal. I am also charged with increasing attendance at this camp from within the organization as well as developing relationships with neighborhood organizations to encourage their youth to attend our camp operation. I will be responsible for hiring, training, and managing a staff of 50+ to ensure a quality program for our campers.

My prior position was that of District Executive with the Chicago Area Council of the Boy Scouts of America. Here, it is my job to build and maintain relationships with schools, churches and organizations throughout the central and northern parts of the city. While here, I have worked to help in the running of large fundraising events and helped to organize a number of events, such as campouts and recognition dinners. The main focus of my position is to increase the number of Scouting units and active Scouters in within my district; this is done mainly through relationship building with local volunteers. I was also chosen to run the Cub Scout summer camp at Hoover Outdoor Education Center where I have hired, trained, and managed a staff of 18 to ensure a fun, educational, and unique experience for the Cub Scouts and their families. More recently, I have assigned to be the Reservation Program Director of the Owasippe Scout Reservation in Twin Lake, Michigan. Here I have been in a managerial position over 4 sub-camp directors and over 100 staff members. I took charge in organizing our weeklong staff training as well as implementing a number of the courses.
