Wayne Huang’s Summary

Wayne Huang is Director of Information Systems at Prudential Financial Inc. He is responsible for claim and polic systems. He works with executives in Operations, Sales, and Finance to implement technology solutions to enhance customer service, business processes, and data quality. He has more than 18 years of experience in leading large IT system development and implementation projects.

He is a member of Society for Information Management (SIM) and Association for Information System (AIS). His community involvement includes serving as the Principal at the Northern New Jersey Chinese School and the Treasurer at the Prudential Toastmasters Club.

He holds Ph.D. in Information Management from Stevens Institute of Technology. He is a recipient of Herbert A. Simon Best Paper Award and Stevens Outstanding Dissertation Award. He studied MBA at State University of New York at Albany, receiving full scholarship from Nelson A. Rockefeller Institute. He graduated from National Central University in Taiwan with B.Sc. in Civil Engineering. His research interests are in business rules engine, workflow automation, data quality assurance, and analytics. He presents regularly at conferences on these subjects.
