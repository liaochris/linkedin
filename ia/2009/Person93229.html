Claudia Weber’s Summary

My expertise and passion includes counseling psychology, human resources training and development, marketing, advertising, change management, project management, organizational development and team building.

I have spent 6 years in the U.S. Navy, 3 years at Children's Hospital and Medical Center in Child Psychiatry and 8+ years at Intel Corporation in a variety of project management, business and system analyst positions developing and deploying systems and user adoption research. I enjoy working in the field of science and technology with the human interaction and adoption focus.

I am also a business owner and consultant for healthy home products. I provide community education on non-toxic home and business products, air and water purification and sustainable living. I am working with local non-profits to reduce their energy consumption and I am the Chair of the Forest Grove BEST team (Be Earth Stewards Today).

I was a member of the Portland G.R.O.W. conference in 2008 with a focus on affordable housing and childhood health. I support the Jars of Clay Blood:Water mission and continue to raise funds for wells in Africa. I continue to be a Jr. Youth leader at the Forest Grove UCC.

Claudia Weber’s Specialties:

I am certified in change management through PROSCI. I am often contacted to in the community regarding change managment and sustainable living.
