Ryan Coleman’s Summary

Facilitator, Consultant, Entrepreneur, Community Builder
===

Facilitator & Consultant
---
For over a decade now all of my roles and responsibilities have revolved around being a facilitator and consultant. Whether it has been working within my own organizations or with clients and partners I've worked to help others discover, acknowledge and apply the greater knowledge that exists within their business.

I do this through a mix of group & visual facilitation techniques as well as applying my own personal experience in building and growing businesses and communities

Entrepreneur
----
I founded my first company, Euphoria Media Solutions in the summer of 1997 and over the next three years grew it to 15 employees and approaching $1M in revenues before it was sold into a sister organization.

Following that I co-founded Clay tablet Technologies a software company focused on improving the efficiency of the translation life cycle.

Community Builder
---
Over the past few years I've become very involved with the Toronto technology, marketing and social media communities. In addition to being an active member in the community I've organized several BarCamps as well as some smaller events.

In 2007 I started an event called VizThink here in Toronto. Focusing on Visual Thinking & creativity. Since then VizThink has grown to become a global community with local meetups taking place in cities world wide, an online social network and conferences in both North America and Europe

About Me
---
I'm passionate about Visual Thinking, Facilitation, Innovation & Sustainability.

I consume information voraciously and actively take experiences and learnings from seemingly unrelated spaces and generate new ideas - I'm a synthesist & design thinker and happily sit in the "Creative Generalist" category

Goals
---
I want to work with innovative, responsible organizations who are looking to add something to the world, not just make a quick profit.
