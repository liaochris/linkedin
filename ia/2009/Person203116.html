Rommel Ondong’s Summary

I have been engaged in multi HR and Administrative functions for more than 5 years. The HR aspects of my work covers areas such as recruitment of personnel, labor relations, employee benefits, conceptualization of training programs for skilled workers and the facilitation of such programs, orientation on ISO-related topics. While in the administrative, I handle aspects in the organization that deal with community relations, canteen services, liaison works, processing of legal permits, and security issues.

At present, I'm concentrating on making a reality the opening of my company's technical training center, particularly in the conceptualization of its training programs and the supervision of both renovation and construction works in the center. While doing this, I also fulfill the other tasks mentioned above.

Aside from the above, I also had experience in overseeing the implementation of safety measures in a construction project site. Among the tasks I did was to conduct safety inspections and meetings with my group and with my counterpart in other subcontractors, toolbox meetings, making safety reports.

Before getting into the corporate world, I taught Philosophy and Psychology to college students with topics ranging from the Human Person, Logic and Ethics, Human Development, and Social Philosophy.

My goal has always been towards the integration of work and a strong sense of work-life philosophy. It has always been my endeavor to look for ways and means on how to bring philosophy to the needs of the society. Philosophy, in this regard, as social responsibility to educate and guide society in general, and the people I work and live with in particular.
