Nizar El-Khoury’s Summary

Established new business opportunities across a broad range of consumer products, working for Procter & Gamble. Categories included Automatic Dishwashing detergents, Snacks, Beverages, Diapers, and Feminine Hygiene products.

Extensive Global experience in Research & Development and Brand Franchise Management, leading innovation programs and building diverse high performance organizations in North America, Western Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Expert in Products Research, bringing together the proper integration of the Consumer, Technology, and Marketing functions through the product development process. Drove brands to market leadership behind powerful consumer insights and winning innovation strategies.

Lived and worked in North America (Cincinnati), Japan (Kobe), Europe (Switzerland), and Saudi Arabia (Jeddah). Established and managed on-the-ground R&D organizations in Japan, China, and Saudi Arabia to co-lead global programs.

Co-developed and administered workshops and training to build organization capabilities including Coaching; Developing Subordinates; Collaboration; Influencing; and Mentoring.
