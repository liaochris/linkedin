Ruud Stelder’s Summary

In the near past I was owner of Freestyle webdesign, a small e-commerce company. Nowdays I'm working for SEOshop a merger of two companies combined, FS webdesign and iFabriek.

I'm founder and CEO of Rutoo International BV. Rutoo is an innovative project I started sept. 2007. My idea was to find a new product everybody needs. With this opinion I started selling the electronic cigarette witch I found on the Canton Fair in China. The electronic cigarette is a product to stop smoking or smoke without tar and carbonmonoxide. Over 2007/2008 we sold about 65.000 cigarettes. At the moment I'm managing director of Rutoo besides SEOshop.

SEOshop BV is one of my newest company's. I'm CEO and investor of SEOshop. Our goal is to create a shopping platform and give companies the possibility to start their own professional webshop without knowledge of e-commerce. SEOshop is a very powerfull and professional e-commerce system and is also suitable for big companies. The concept of SEOshop is new. SEOshop users only pay a small percentage depening on their monthly sales instead of paying a fixed amount. Our customers getting free consultancy, because we only get paid when our customers make profit. Without any risks business owners could start a webshop with a estimated worth of 25.000 euro or more. If you interested in a demo account, please visit our site and ask for a demo using our webforms, website: http://www.seoshopecommerce.com
