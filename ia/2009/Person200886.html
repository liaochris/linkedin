Roberto Torrico E.’s Summary

Mechanical-Petroleum Engineer with progressive experience in Plant Management, project management and plant optimization engineering, field development, gas well control and Contracts Management to production fields and transport systems; twelve years of professional experience in Process, Operation, Maintenance of plant facilities, process Engineering, Construction and plant assembly; Petroleum and Gas Refining and Production, Process and Plant Management; rotating equipment, pressure vessels, process gas compressors, pipelines, ESD systems, safety in design (HAZID, HAZOP and Plant safety critical elements) maintenance, QA/QC and repairs; complementing the assurance via audits, reviews, trending and tracking all this applied technology of World Class in operational activities, maintenance and oil and gas production projects, implementing the Integrated Management Systems ISO 14001, OSHAS 18001, and ISO 9001. Elaborated management plans for production and hydrocarbons storage systems, optimization and transfer for custody, analysis of quality control and Environment Security and Health (QHSE) regulations. Participated in PETROBRAS` integrated Management Systems, performed auditing as a member of the Operational Guideline, Maintenance and Processes (D5) Committee. Knowledge in process simulation with Hysys system, apply regulations and standards relating to process and plant design. Ability to manage and work in a cross-disciplined team, focused on priorities, ability to learn and transfer learning into operation and value. Self-motivated individual with strong analytical, problem-solving, organizational, and people skills (including people of diverse educational and cultural backgrounds) the ability to manage multiple complex, and sensitive tasks successfully; and extensive exposure to handling business in petroleum Gas sector and cultural settings (having worked in Latin America with United States companies)
