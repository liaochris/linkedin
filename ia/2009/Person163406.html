Mickael R. Escroignard’s Summary

Mickael is an accomplished and experienced ICT Programme Manager and Consultant. He is experienced working with public and private sector clients. His experience is primarily with projects in the Health Service, Local Government (Social Care) and Education sectors.
Coming from a very technical background, Mickael has been specialising over the past 10 years into Project and Programme Management largely within the Public Sector but also in the Telecoms Industry. He is used to managing staff in a Direct and/or Matrix Style environment and managing budgets ranging from £10K to £12 million depending on the size of the Programme.
Mickael’s latest biggest achievement was managing the ICT Work stream on a Business Integration Project between North East Lincolnshire Council (Social Services) and North East Lincolnshire PCT in the creation of a Care Trust Plus Organisation.
Mickael is viewed by his Clients and his co-workers as being extremely dedicated to the success and quality of his deliverables.
His excellent track record coupled with a good sense of humour has owned him respect from his Clients and largely contributed to the success of his Projects.
