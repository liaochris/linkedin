David E. Achzet’s Summary

David Achzet has over twenty five years of experience in securities/investments, broker dealer compliance and supervision, and sales management. He is currently a Senior Vice President at NAF Funding, LLC, a life settlement brokerage group located in New York. He is also a registered representative with The Riderwood Group of Baltimore, Maryland. He was formerly the Eastern Regional Manager with the Seattle area based broker dealer Symetra Investment Services (formerly Safeco Investment Services) and was responsible for the sales management and supervision of approximately 200 registered representatives.

Prior to Symetra/Safeco, David was responsible for the sale of financial products and the investment expertise for Phoenix Equity Planning and Keystone Investments in the eastern USA while working as Regional Vice President. David has also worked for the broker dealer Gruntal & Co., in New York as Vice President and Director of Mutual Fund Marketing where he was responsible for the strategic development of mutual fund sales along with developing relationships with mutual fund organizations.

David holds a B.S. in Business from Niagara University and an M.B.A. with a concentration in International Business from the University of Miami. He holds a Series 7, 63, 65 and 24 license along with a life insurance license.
