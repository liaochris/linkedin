Joseph Malki’s Summary

Joseph Malki is an expert in the intersection of event design, executive level sales, production, information technology and creative partnership. He is recognized as a "green strategist" having paid his dues as one of the leaders of the Northern California Organo-Punk Activist movement of the 80' and 90's. Joseph served as a coordinator of Central Valley Earth First! in 1991's famous Redwood Summer Campaign: the largest environmental protest in history. He also served in the elite 1/184 Infantry Air Assault Unit with the California Army National Guard.

Since 1999, Mr. Malki has lead the growth of Seven-Star, Inc. the worlds largest green event producers. He is a co-founder, partner and the CBDO. Seven-Star has is the first event firm to produce a consistent 85%+ waste diversion at mass scale events.

Mr. Malki's past consultancy clients include the State of California, the City of San Francisco, Lowe McAdams Health Care, Alcone Marketing and Hample Stefanides, among others. In 2002, Mr. Malki served as the Director of Marketing for EventPro Strategies, developing projects for Fox, US Army, NASCAR, GM, Turner Networks, and Loews Theaters. He architected the largest model talent database in the US and went on to develop Seven-Star's database, also noted as the largest green business to business database in the world.

In 2006, Joseph forged a strategic alliance with Empowerment Works, a micro NGO with vision for a global event. Together with Empowerment Works' executive director Melanie St. James, Mr. Malki created the formation and event design for The Global Summit. Later in 2007, Mr. Malki, incepted the GreenCities and GreenBash event initiatives slated for launch in 2009.

In 2007, Seven-Star "greened" the Live Earth concerts in China, Brazil, S. Africa and Japan. Mr. Malki architected the Live Earth Global Volunteer mulilanguage recruitment and coordination website.

Mr. Malki is dedicated to advancing environmental and social responsibility..
