Robin Daultani’s Summary

Graduated from IIT Bombay in 2007 and have worked in P&G Japan as an Engineer (Corporate R&D).

After resigning from P&G in 2008, I have been working towards creating pre-recruitment solutions for employers and students. I re-launched India Interns in March'09, which addresses the internships segment of the pre-recruitment space.

In September'09, I launched Resume360 - a new framework to enable students and young professionals create powerful resumes from scratch. The framework, in the form of downloadable modules and practice sheets, is available for free downloads at www.resume360.org

Also, I freelance as a web and graphic designer to oil my right brains. You can visit www.robindaultani.com to view my works and services I provide as a designer.
