Simon Smith’s Summary

I thrive on communicating ideas for growth. My specialties are strategic internet marketing and personal and organizational development.

Currently, I lead an internet marketing optimization company called Commune that I founded in 2007. With a team of top-notch consultants, I help clients close the gap between their business objectives and internet marketing performance through analytics, strategy and implementation services that continuously maximize return on investment. Each day, I’m fortunate to learn and communicate strategies that help clients grow their business through online marketing and communication.

Over the past 10 years, I’ve done this for leading brands including Enbridge Gas Distribution, Rogers Communications, Bayer and Pfizer. Before Commune, I was Associate Creative Director for Excerpta Medica Interactive, a division of Elsevier, where I developed and implemented interactive marketing strategies for the pharmaceutical industry and online business strategies for Elsevier’s repository of 2,000 journals and 17,000 books.

Obsessively entrepreneurial (if this were dinner, we’d probably now be discussing business ideas), I also founded in 2002 and for several years ran sci-tech website Betterhumans.com, now run by the international nonprofit organization Humanity+.

I’ve supported this informal life education with formal degrees, including a Bachelor of Journalism from Ryerson University and a Master of Arts in the History and Philosophy of Science and Technology from the University of Toronto. (For what it’s worth, I’m also a Google Analytics and Google AdWords Qualified Individual. I admittedly have a bit of a Google fetish.) Which is far more education than my grade seven school principal, who rightfully expelled me for bad behavior, probably thought I’d ever achieve. (And, perhaps partly out of spite, I often think about pursuing a PhD.)

For more information, please visit http://www.simonsmith.ca.
