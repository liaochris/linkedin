Marc Theermann’s Summary

Mobile media has been my life as an entrepreneur, investor, and senior operating executive for over ten years.

Last year I incubated a mobile analytics and media buying dashboard concept at Polaris Ventures with the help of Bob Geiman and Sim Simeonov. Before the company ever hit the light of day, it was rolled into Smaato Inc.

Smaato is an Aeris Capital funded company with headquarters in Silicon Valley, and an engineering center in Germany. Smaato has over 30 employees dedicated to ad network optimization. Smaato’s platform is already connected to 20+ national/international ad-networks, which allows the company to fill mobile inventory in most markets around the globe.

I entered the mobile industry by co-founding two prominent Boston wireless firms: YellowPepper (est. 1999) and Everypoint (est. 2004).

More recently I was the Chief Strategy Officer at Get Connected, a 120 person high-tech firm that sold enterprise software to mobile carriers and retailers. Under the leadership of Ali Riaz, a team of seasoned software executives attempted recapitalization, and ultimately a management buy-out, of this amazing company.

On a personal level, I am passionate about enology, antique sports cars and of course revolutionizing interactive marketing.

Feel free to contact me via LinkedIn to talk about mobile marketing, angle investments, or cars. :)
