Rolf Provan’s Summary

In 2007 I founded Proviz LLC focusing on Marketing Mentoring for the leaders of medium-sized and small businesses.
Leveraging my extensive marketing experience, working with some of the best marketers and the best companies in the world, Proviz is dedicated to helping business owners improve the bottom line in their business.
Proviz offers a marketing mentoring approach that allows business owners and leaders to excel in every aspect of their marketing, always focusing on the bottom line. I guide, coach and challenge the thinking of my clients as they create and then execute an action plan to ensure progress and help make their goals reality.

Rolf Provan’s Specialties:

- Marketing strategy and planning
- Product development and brand management
- Sales and distribution management
- Advertising and communication development
- Export sales and marketing
- Market research and analysis

My experience ranges from Healthcare to Automobiles to Consumer Packaged Goods and from small agencies to Forbes Global 200 corporations.
I have operated at grass roots level all the way up to the boardroom.
