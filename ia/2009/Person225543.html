Stuart Flaum’s Summary

My financial, management, and entrepreneurial drive never stops as I relentlessly seek new and innovative ways to deliver information, products and services to consumers. I am very proud of the following three goals reached:

First ,for the past fifteen years I have been a public spokesperson and leader for families with individuals with developmental disabilities.Recently, incorporating my interests in finance and management I have co-founded the The Special Needs Family Planning Group at AXA Advisors. This is a one of a kind,unique mission meeting the financial needs of individuals with developmental disabilities,their families, and businesses.

Second, I had been on the forefront of specialty, self service store retailing ,which prior to the 1980's was relatively unknown in the mens clothing industry.Kandy Ruth's Tip Top Stores and UrbanMall were models for future retailers.

Third, capitalizing on local urban centered culture, called hip hop, when it was relatively obscure, UrbanMall.com was an early leader in e commerce and internet news. I enjoyed travelling throughout the country and speaking at most leading conferences on how to build an e commerce business and ship worldwide with confidence.

    UrbanMall.com

    (Internet industry)

    September 1999 — Present (9 years 6 months)

    Stuart Flaum has been an online personality since launching UrbanMall.com in the late 1990’s. UrbanMall.com offered hard core, hard to find hip hop and urban clothing at a time when hip hop music and clothing was not well known outside of urban centers. As an innovator , his online chats with designers shared information in a way never imagined before the web.     Kandy Ruth Corp

    (Retail industry)

    November 1985 — November 2007 (22 years 1 month)

    Kandy Ruth Corp dba Tip Top Stores and UrbanMall was a pioneer in self service shopping for young mens clothing, at a time when "Gap" looking stores were in its infancy. The industry looked at innovators , such as KRC's Tip Top Stores and UrbanMall , for direction. KRC recognized the fashion trend at the time, opened doors for others, before becoming one of the first strategic partners for urban clothing at Amazon.com. Twenty five years after opening its first store, KRC has closed , however, UrbanMall.com continues to be a leader in the hip hop clothing industry.
