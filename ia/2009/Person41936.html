Emily Brooks’s Summary

As the Chief Executive Officer of the EA Alliance, Emily Brooks serves as a community connecting point to unite grass-roots and community based organizations. Emily specializes in supporting organizational needs such as business planning, marketing support, strategic planning, grant writing assistance, market positioning, branding, and broadening customer bases. Emily passionately believes in changing the social norm towards agricultural sustainability and development through education and coalition building.

Emily Brooks graduated from Ripon College, Ripon, WI, with a degree in Chemistry-Biology in 1997. She graduated from Fox Valley Technical College with a degree in Culinary Arts December 2003 and from Clayton College with a Masters degree in Holistic Nutrition. She is currently working on her PhD degree for Holistic Nutrition and ND License at Clayton College. Emily’s diverse educational background allows her to effectively analyze all aspects of new development needs, effectively create and manage strong, yet diverse coalitions, and ultimately create the right project solution to fit the requirements of the community by creating sustainable and progressive systems.

    BRIDGES Healthy Cooking, relocating to Connecticut, is a non-profit, broad-based community initiative. The mission is to create Community Wellness Programming and nutritional awareness as a Community Wellness Service Provider. The vision of is to increase the physical health of the community through education and programming incorporating locally grown, sustainable foods as part of an all-encompassing approach to healthy living.

    BRIDGES approaches the concept of nutrition from the standpoint that good health arises from sustainability awareness and from consuming foods that have been sustainably prepared to retain maximum nutritive value, and served in moderation across all food groups.
