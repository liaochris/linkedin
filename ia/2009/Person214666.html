B. M. Shahrier Majumder, PMP’s Summary

I am a talented and accomplished IT Management professional, with extensive background in Project Management and Process Improvement, backed by proven ability and experience to direct technical, business analyst and SEPG teams. My career vision is to be a respected Management Professional.

My main strength is that I can take up any responsibility and can carry them out to the best of my ability. I am a problem solving and solution oriented professional. 8+ years of my career I have worked in Project Management, Requirement Analysis, Operations Management, HR Recruiting, Software Quality Assurance, Internal Quality Auditing and Process Improvement functions.

Experiences include Project Management for financial software development, HRM software development, RMG software development, educational institute management software, process improvement to comply with SEI CMMI, ISO 9001, Off-The-Shelf product implementation (Microsoft Project Professional & Project Server, SharePoint Portal); conducting training on Project Management, Software Engineering and Quality Assurance functional areas.

One of my major accomplishments is successful implementation of SEI CMMI (Level 3) model in Southtech within 18 months. I was also Project Manager of another successful project of Southtech for implementing Southtech Ascend Banking System at National Pension and Provident Fund (NPPF) in Bhutan. However, I have successfully completed 12 projects in my career.

I have working experience with MS Project Professional & Project Server, MS SharePoint Portal, MS Visio, Microsoft CRM, UML; having knowledge about MS Solution Framework (MSF), MS Visual Studio Team System (VSTS) & Team Foundation Server (TFS), People CMM and Six Sigma.

I achieved MBA degree with a specialization in HRM from BRAC University and BSc. (Honors) degree in Computer Science from Institute of Science & Technology.

I am a founding member and volunteer leader of PMI Bangladesh Chapter.
