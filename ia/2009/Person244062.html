RoseAnn Rotandaro’s Summary

I am President and Co-Founder of Virtual Law Partners LLP ("VLP"). My co-founders, Craig W. Johnson and Andrea E. Chavez, and I launched VLP in 2008 to provide an innovative solution at a reasonable price for corporations' legal needs. We are the leading innovators for cost efficiency and environmental protection in the legal industry. VLP minimizes overhead costs by using a proprietary, advanced digital platform to connect our lawyers to each and to our clients. VLP's technology infrastructure eliminates the need for centralized offices, thereby reducing the carbon emissions of commuting to work. As of January 1, 2009 VLP has 27 attorneys practicing in California, D.C./Virginia and Texas as well as an emerging practice in China. VLP plans to expand to become a comprehensive, worldwide practice in the next 3-5 years.

Prior to VLP, I founded Armor Legal Counsel, Professional Corporation which represented technology companies in the Bay Area in corporate transactions, financing and securities law compliance. Prior to forming Armor, I held the position of VP & General Counsel for two pre-IPO companies, Equator Technologies, Inc. and BenefitStreet, Inc., where I was in charge of all legal affairs. At BenefitStreet, Inc., I was also instrumental in sourcing investments for the company's equity financing. At each of the two companiess, I was part of senior management as well as general counsel. I worked very closely with the members of the Board of Directors as well as with the other executive officers, the marketing team and the engineering team.

Before going in-house, I practiced corporate law for six years with the law firm Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati. At WSGR, I primarily handled corporate securities transactions, including mergers and acquisitions, private financings and public offerings.

RoseAnn Rotandaro’s Specialties:

Corporate attorney experienced in representing emerging growth, high-tech and fin-tech (finance technology) companies as both outside and in-house legal counsel.
