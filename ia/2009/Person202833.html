Roland Lopez’s Summary

My partner and I founded Toroso, LLC & Toroso Investment Capital, LP to manage funds in diversified commodities. On the venture capital side we work on large opportunities in which we place equity and debt in various cases. With access to these resources in capital markets, Toroso has leveraged our contacts in several arenas for projects which include: Advising or consulting on serious, innovative projects in many areas of interest primarily in Oil & Gas (and commodity based products - such as gold), Real Estate and High Tech. As a company we identifying weaknesses, develop macro level marketing and financial planning and analysis as well as overall company strategies.
