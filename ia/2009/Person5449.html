Ajay Phatak’s Summary

Co-founder and CEO of Jopasana group of companies a Software products and solutions group located in the city of Pune - in India. Recently, Jopasana was acquired by CoreObjects Software Inc. and is now a wholly owned subsidiery of CoreObjects. Jopasana is now fashioned as a speciality group responsible for embedded and real time business of Coreobjects. Our expertise lies in the general domain of real time communication and control systems - from embedded to distributed applications. My experience lies in developing and executing business for high technology and manufacturing industry customers. Some of the industries where we have our customer base include - Life Sciences and Chemical Analysis, Process & Manufacturing, Telecom, Aerospace and Semi-conductor. As a company we have customers ranging from high tech start ups to multi billion $ global corporations. Personally my strengths lie in Business Development and Business Networking apart from product managmenet and new product ideas. Sunstantial interest and work in the area of architecting real time software products and solutions. Excellent cross disciplinary background in both engineering and computer science. Substantial management experience as a CEO (17 years).

We now focus on early stage companies and participate in creating a complete business ecosystems to make these companies successful.

Worked substantially on developing Reliability and Performance Management Systems for the Energy and Environment industry at large. More particularly, this involves working with Wind Energy, Waste and water solutions, HVAC and Manufacturing Automation sectors.

My current interests are in the areas of ecological economics and how businesses and consumers can respect carrying capacity of the planet a.k.a. live sustainably.

Invited speaker and panel co-ordinator at several events on topics ranging from Enterpreneurship, E-Waste, and Sustainability and Ecological Economics.
