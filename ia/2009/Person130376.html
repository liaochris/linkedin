Ken Winnick MBA, MSc’s Summary

Mr. Winnick helps fast growing clean tech and environmental companies, organizations, and departments become more efficient and more competitive through the smart use of information technology.

He will help you design information systems that will deliver on your business goals, and he will provide practical solutions to the work flow and data analysis challenges that are unique to clean tech and environmental projects.

Ken is an Information Solutions (IS) management consultant with 25 years of experience in automating the mapping, visualization, and analysis of economic, environmental, energy, transportation, and land use data.

Mr. Winnick holds an MS in Ecology & Marine Studies from the University of Oregon, and an MBA in Technology Management from the University of Washington Foster School of Business. He earned PMP certification in 2003.

Ken Winnick MBA, MSc’s Specialties:

* Renewable Energy (with Offshore experience)
* Ecosystem Markets
* Climate Change
* IT strategy, planning, design, and implementation

Mr. Winnick's consulting practice integrates expertise in economics, ecology, and information technology. He has the process and negotiation skills to make your project a success.
