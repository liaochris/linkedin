Christopher Swain’s Summary

I am a classic true entrepreneur! Yes, I was formally trained as an economist, but I think that endeavor really enhanced my ability to think critically and solve difficult problems. This training, along with my entrepreneur passions have allowed me to pursue challenges and achieve success in many diverse and various businesses.

I consider myself a life long student in the area of success and achievement training and continuously strive to be the best at what I do.
