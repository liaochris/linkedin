Richard Geller’s Summary

I have worked in the real estate, commercial and legal services industries for the past 14 years.
I have been the managing partner of the Law Offices of Richard L. Geller, P.C. and its predecessor since 1998. My firms have always been litigation based, with areas of focus including all areas of personal injury, real estate and commercial transactions.
I have worked with both large unions, and with banking organizations in the northeast in the commercial sector.
I look forward to speaking with anyone regarding their legal needs and/or their projects financing need, or their interest in putting capital to work in one of our development projects.

Richard Geller’s Specialties:

Construction site accident litigation, and medical malpractice, as well as "hard-money" lending.
