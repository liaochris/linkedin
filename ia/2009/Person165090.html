    Bluri Music Group

    (Music industry)

    March 2009 — Present (6 months)

    Bluri Inc. & Bluri Music proudly announce the formation of their new artist management firm & record label, The Bluri Music Group, which will forge relationships directly with artists and distribute recordings online as well as through traditional music channels. The new company will build upon the success of Bluri Music and all facets of their entertainment channels. This agreement gives Bluri Music a greater hand in developing music that fits within the profile of Bluri Incorporated and all its branches.

    This announcement is a natural next step in Bluri Inc.’s entertainment strategy. The Bluri Music Group will add tremendous value to the content offerings and distribution of great music Bluri audiences have come to expect,” states Michael Bohuslavsky (Mikey Bo), president of Bluri Music. “As we move forward with this new venture, The Bluri Music Group will seek out unique and compelling artists from a broad range of genres to help them reach the widest audience possible.” “At such a critical time in the music business, we are excited to be deepening our effort to help artists connect with audiences through all media channels.”

    Bluri Music will provide a powerful new way for great music to be marketed and distributed. Bluri is recognized throughout the entertainment industry for its groundbreaking marketing and imaging of music for both established artists and rising stars. The label will develop emerging artists with inspired vision and extraordinary talent and will also be a home for established artists with timeless resonance, providing them an extraordinary platform for reaching music fans. The Bluri Music Group advocates creative control for artists and encourages musicians to stretch and take risks; which, the new label believes will result in compelling music choices for consumers.

    Expect the unexpected. Coming in 2010.

    Mikey Bo Productions

    (Music industry)

    February 2005 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    Mikey Bo started out his dream of a music career with nothing more than hope and sooner than expected, he has become one of America’s most respected & requested entertainers. Today, Bo rapidly finds himself becoming an in-demand beat producer, recording artist, disc jockey, C.E.O. (Bluri Inc. www.bluri.com), endorser, record label manager, video jockey, model & songwriter.

    Mikey Bo first broke out in the entertainment industry (in 2005) claiming most of his fame as a pre-recorded mixshow deejay. After winning “Battle Of The Nations DJ’s (in southern Florida) his career sparked over night. His breakthrough performance broke down barriers for farm-boy, small-town deejay’s everywhere.

    He quickly compiled over 1,243 international mixshows under the watchful thumb of Cox Publishing. During that time, Bo earned ‘Mixshow DJ of The Year‘ & ‘Rookie Of The Year’ by The Disc Jockey Association of America ( 2005 ).

    In 2007, Bo left the mixshow market and began pushing his success to further heights within his musical production route. Bo’s productions have proved to be one of inspirational notice. He’s piled remixes with the likes of Britney Spears, Coldplay, Brandy, Kanye West, Timbaland, Bubba Sparxxx, Lil’ Wayne, The Pussycat Dolls, One Republic, Chris Brown, Missy Elliott and the list goes on. He was quickly crowned “Remixer Of The Year” by the likes of iDJ Magazine in 2008 & was just mentioned by MUCH Music TV as one of the top upcoming entertainment prospects of 2009.

    Mikey also holds prestige within his co-founding & presidential roles within Bluri Inc. (www.bluri.com), one of the most dynamic entertainment websites in the world.

    Mikey Bo has powerfully pushed his dent into the entertainment industry, inspiring noise on all levels. Relentlessly rising as one of the nations primary music luminaries.
