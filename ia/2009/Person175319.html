Nigel Rodliffe’s Experience

  • Owner

    SailTime South East

    (Privately Held; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    March 2008 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    SailTime South East holds the SailTime franchise from Dover to the Wash and is currently developing sailing bases at Ipswich and Chatham with a fleet of Beneteau Oceanis yachts.
    SailTime, the world's leading sailing membership company, provides low commitment, affordable sailing to both new and experienced sailors through our network of over 60 bases worldwide. We provide an easy and sensible way to sail a new yacht or powerboat for a fraction of the normal cost.
    Each yacht is shared by a maximum of eight members, who are all guaranteed exclusive use during their reservation, and a minimum of 6 weeks sailing each year. Members design their own sailing calendar using our highly advanced SailTime Scheduler™, a proprietary online, real-time scheduling system. They can book up to 12 months in advance, re-arrange their bookings with no penalties and have unlimited free bookings within 24 hours.
    Members pay a fixed annual fee and just turn up and sail. SailTime pay for the marina, insurance and maintenance, and ensure that the boat is serviced and available for each member booking.
