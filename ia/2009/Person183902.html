Paul Whiteway’s Summary

I've been working in the Internet industry for over ten years and am incredibly passionate about it. I love what I do and have been fortunate enough to work at some great companies and with great people.

I am currently Head of Audience for Yahoo in South East Asia where I manage a team of product managers, editors and product marketers.

Before joining Yahoo I was CEO of MyEmoticons.com, an internet company I founded in 2003 and subsequently sold. Prior to that I was Business Manager of Communications Products for MSN (based in London). Other roles have included Vice President of Online Business for a telecommunications start-up in New York City and General Manager of the Internet Advertising Sales Division for Yellow Pages in Australia.

I hold an Executive MBA from London Business School and am also an Entrepreneur in Residence at INSEAD where I coach aspiring entrepreneurs.
