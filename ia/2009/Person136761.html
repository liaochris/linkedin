Lao Watson-Smith’s Summary

My career started within the Charitable/ Humanitarian sector, working with a group of organisations mandated to bring relief to areas of Southern Africa.

I remain on the board of some of these groups. Whilst working within that field I was also studying a BSC in Accounting and Finance at the London School of Economics (LSE).

In 2002 I stepped back from the Charitable sector to all intents and purposes and became involved in Property Investment and Management in the South of France, and from that initial company others have grown in related areas, specifically Corporate hospitality offered to visitors to the South of France.

An exciting new area being explored is the spinning off of a technology company which will seek to bring to market our internal, web based systems which run our businesses on an SaaS model. Watch this space!

I have always belived in taking the long view, and am constantly setting foundations for future progress and growth, whilst nurturing already running projects and businesses.
