Jonathan Oakes’s Summary

Jonathan Oakes has been a web, media and mobile technology entrepreneur, investor and strategist for over 12 years. In 1998 Oakes co-founded ThinAirApps, where he served as CEO and Chairman leading the company to a successful acquisition by Palm Inc. in December 2001. Oakes spent over two years at Palm, as Senior Director managing corporate and product strategy. At Palm, he was responsible for the enterprise and "prosumer" markets, leading product management, business development and channel sales efforts. Oakes is currently acting as a technology and management consultant to companies ranging from funded start-ups to public companies. Oakes earned a BA in American Studies from Skidmore College in 1994 and an MBA from Harvard Business School in 2006 where he was the recipient of the Satchu-Burgstone Entrepreneurship prize.
