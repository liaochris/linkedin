Atif S. Farooqi’s Experience

  • Head of Consulting

    Binder International Private Ltd

    (Insurance industry)

    2005 — Present (4 years)

    An independent Management Consultancy in the name of Binder International Private Limited was established on February 01, 1992 to meet the growing need of business community in Pakistan for technical advice on insurance and management matters. During my tenure, Binder International handled project consultancy assignments for power and other projects valuing around US $ 3.5 billion. They are insurance advisors of National Bank of Pakistan (EX NDFC), the largest Bank in Pakistan. Hub Power Project, Uch Power Project, Rousch Power Project and Hub River Fuel Oil Pipeline Project and Raiwind Power are among the large projects being handled by Binder International for Private Sector Energy Development Fund (PSEDF) Government of Pakistan. Represented BUPA International of UK in Pakistan for some time for promoting Health Insurance need for the community in Pakistan

  • Director - Public Relations

    The Rope Private Limited

    (Media Production industry)

    2002 — 2005 (3 years)

    The Rope Private Limited was founded in 2002 as a premier Public Relations Agency, furnishing e-media solutions to the business community in Pakistan. As Director of PR I was actively involved in Public Relations Consultancy and Media Planning, Event Management, Product Launches and Profile/Image building. Corporate clients during my association with the company included Gillette, SwedeBus Pakistan, Millac Foods Private Ltd, & 3Com Pakistan.
