William Blackmon, PMP, BSEE’s Summary

William is a highly experienced Project Management Professional with a technical background in the electrical / electronic industry with emphases in electronic contract manufacturing and telecommunications. He has led teams of various sizes locally and globally in cross-functional matrix environments for NPI programs and high volume manufacturing phase.
