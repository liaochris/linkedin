Nichole Shannon’s Summary

I was the Webmaster for Slacker.com. I developed the entire front end in jQuery and PHP using Zend Platform and phpBB 3.x. This does not include the web applications which we develop separately. I work with these teams developing in Java, Velocity, Javascript, Mootools and CSS. This is definitely one of those dream jobs. I get paid to be a professional Slacker. You can't beat that!

In my previous life was:
A Web Applications developer. Sometimes called a generalist I specialize in no one technology, yet I have very strong skills and understanding of a broad range of Internet technologies. My strongest skills are in client-side web applications, accessibility and XML/XSL. Yet I am capable and experienced in all aspects of web application development form servlet to client, with a little DB on top. I regularly find myself fitting in where I am needed most. I am just as comfortable as a technical lead as I am programming or doing configuration management. I am almost always involved in the big picture as I can easily navigate complex architectures and solutions. I get things done. I know my limits and I know how to put the job into the right hands. Time is money and I believe in matching the right skills to the right task. I am a strong supporter of Open Source technologies. I am currently enjoying my newest ventures into content management and PHP driven web sites. I manage a small Non-Profit web organization where I receive untold satisfaction in bringing people together and helping them communicate. I would really like to build upon that in future opportunities.
