    "You google. You text. You chat. Do you poken?”

    Poken is your social business card - the easiest way to exchange contact details and connect on social networks.

    Pokens are small, sexy USB devices that exchange contact details, social networks and more. Pokens come in many shapes, colours and sizes, and are the most stylish way to connect in real life. Pokens are also used by brands and event organizers as a 'social engagement tool', as a way to start a conversation with consumers.

  • Co-Organizer

    Twestival

    (Events Services industry)

    February 2009 — February 2009 (1 month)

    "Tweet. Meet. Give."

    Twestival is a global charity event spearheaded by Amanda Rose and organized by volunteers using Twitter. Our landmark event was Twestival Global in February 2009, where we raised $250,000 for charity: water. With the money raised they build 55 water wells in developing nations, providing clean, fresh drinking water for 17,000 people.

    - Originated global sponsorship deal with Amiando and local sponsorship deals with Microsoft, British Airways and Poken.
    - Developed “Twestival FM” (www.twestival.fm), an online music project in association with SoundCloud (www.soundcloud.com) that’s “like Live Aid on Twitter”. Over 600 artists/labels including Imogen Heap, Erol Alkan and Bloc Party contributed tracks that could be downloaded in return for a charitable donation.
    - Organized the entertainment and music for the London Twestival event (800 attendees).
