Steve Edelson’s Summary

Steve Edelson’s Specialties:

Background in all facets of non-paper commodity trading. Knowledgeable of all grades of post consumer and post industrial plastics and metals recyclables. Experienced with request for proposal (RFP) and contract negotiations process. Skilled in risk mitigation through the use of price indexes and hedges.

Steve Edelson’s Experience

  • Director - Materials Marketing

    Waste Management, Inc

    (Environmental Services industry)

    May 1995 — February 2009 (13 years 10 months)

    Created a centralized marketing system for 125 plastic and metal recycling facilities with a staff of 7 marketing and trading personnel processing 700M pounds of annual transactions. Also launched a non-fiber brokerage group for the trading and marketing of post industrial/ consumer plastics, metals and demanufactured electronic based commodities.

    Established a for-profit brokerage line of business that provided new brokerage revenues exceeding $37 million in 2007.

    Built an optical separation site in Raleigh, NC to market the flaked PET and HDPE to diverse industries including bottle, fiber, carpet, decking and pipe.

    Developed an in-house commodity pricing newsletter that communicated market pricing and direction which enabled the traders to make accurate and immediate buy/sell decisions.

  • Vice President

    Resource Recycling Technologies, Inc.

    (Plastics industry)

    1990 — 1995 (5 years)

    Responsibilities included the management of 3 grinding and pelletizing facilities reprocessing polystyrene, ABS, PE and PP. Facilitated expansion by adding an additional site for the recycling of PET bottles including color separation, aluminum cap removal, grinding and washing. Also successfully created and expanded a plastics brokerage business.

  • Vice President

    Ricard Plastic Corp

    (Plastics industry)

    1987 — 1990 (3 years)

    Responsibilities included the management of several grinding and pelletizing facilities reprocessing polystyrene, ABS, PE and PP for use in the injection molding sectors. Also added site in Trenton, NJ for the recycling of post consumer PET bottles including color separation, aluminum cap removal, grinding and washing. Successfully created and expanded a plastics brokerage business trading styrene and ABS.
