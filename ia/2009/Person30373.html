Bob Keppel’s Summary

With nearly 20 years of marketing and management experience, Bob is a leader with a track record of high profit performance and strategic assignments with a strong background in diverse marketing channels and business operations.

Throughout his career, Bob has demonstrated the ability to maximize database marketing efforts. During his time at Rodale, Bob led the development of the initial contact strategy plans including the introduction of trigger-based hotline promotions. Targeted marketing efforts allowed his AOL team to reduce the number of online ad impressions by 50% with no reduction in the number of new customers. At HCI, his direction proved instrumental in the data design of a new marketing database platform which resulted in more accurate statistical models and more effective Winback campaigns.

Business lines under Bob’s management have produced consistently excellent results. While based in London, Bob and his team turned Rodale’s International Books business from a significant loss to a significant profit within 24 months. Online grew to represent 60% of customer acquisitions for the strategic Broadband business in his time at AOL. His tenure at HCI was marked by consistent levels of high profit for the International business for the first time in company history.

In recognition of his ability to understand the overall dynamics of a business, Bob has often been selected for strategic assignments. At Rodale, he led the launch of a $20mm Spanish language books business and acted as the internal leader during a Strategic Planning process. His online team at AOL was consistently chosen to test critical new product concepts. Most recently, he led a strategic review of internal and external fulfillment capabilities for HCI as well as exploring acquisition opportunities for growth.
