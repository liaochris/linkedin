Steve Perkins’s Summary

Seasoned professional possessing strong experience and expertise at improving both visibility into telecommunications spending and reduction of telecom costs. Nationally Recognized Subject Matter Expert within the Telecommunications Expense Management industry. Frequent writer and occasional speaker on "Best Practices" approaches to TEM.

Founder & Principal Consultant of boutique consulting practice focusing on Telecom Financial Management, Business Process Improvement, Cost/Benefit Analysis, Technology Assessment and Cost Management; developed objective relationships with a wide variety of TEM Solution providers in the commercial and federal government marketplaces. Objective and independent. -- We help clients find solutions that work for them.
