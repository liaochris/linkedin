Paul O'Brien’s Summary

The guy who automated his bedroom curtains at age 7 with a motor out of a printer, rubber bands, blu-tack and a ZX81 computer.... I'm an ideas man :)

I have been involved in the NZ IT sector the majority of my working career.

Managing Director at MHA - an application hosting business I started in 2002.

MHA's objective is to deliver a market leading, customer focused, integrated suite of business applications via hosted services via the Internet, enabling companies to forget about technology and focus on their core business.

Our product line includes Terminal Services running a wide variety of applications, Hosted Exchange, Sharepoint, Office Communication Server, Microsoft CRM and Spam & Virus protection

Client benefits include increased accessibility, security, storage, business efficiencies and cost savings within their IT systems.

MHA is a customer focussed company, devoted to the technology it works on.
