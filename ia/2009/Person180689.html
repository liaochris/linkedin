Paulo Andre’s Summary

At present he is Executive Board Member at LENA Concessions, GRUPO LENA.
Former COO and General Manager at SICE Inc. (ACS/Dragados) in the United States, where he contributed proactively with the structural changes expected in infrastructure for transportation.
Executive director since 1998 he was COO and CIO on two highway concessionaires (PPP/DBFO) in Brazil and Portugal (1998 to 2007).
During the past 22 years he was always involved in bid, finance, build and operate road infrastructures, with extensive expertise on matching technology and innovation with business models/operation processes for private transportation projects.
In 2004 he was invited to join the Directorate-General for Energy and Transport as an Electronic Toll Collecting expert, with view to the European Toll Interoperability.
He is also a member of the e-Safety Forum since 2005.
He's a cofounder of the ARMAS Project for ESA (European Space Agency) in 2003.
Frequent speaker at international congresses and seminars: IBTTA, ERTICO, ERF, e-Safety, ITS World and The Engineer.
Fluent in English, French, Spanish, Italian and Portuguese as mother language.

Paulo Andre’s Specialties:

Competitive analysis, team building, corporate identity. Innovation process.
Start up phase on new projects. Leadership.
