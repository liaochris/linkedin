Timothy Haley’s Summary

Advocate Financial Management is an independent, full service,
fee-only financial planning and investment management
firm located in Arlington, Massachusetts, focused on helping clients
in the second half of life.

Our clients can expect more than financial advice. We take a holistic approach to financial planning. Partnering with our clients, we begin by listening and helping them to identify, clarify and prioritize their goals
and aspirations.

We help clients organize their current affairs, creating for them a better understanding and clearer picture of their financial situation today. Our detailed analysis of their current financial position, provides a greater sense of control and a vantage point for making more effective financial decisions.

Only after truly understanding what is most important in their lives, can we begin to look at the options available and put together a financial plan to help guide them towards achieving their life goals.

Our clients are individuals seeking significance in the second half of life. Their focus is on family, community and the world. They're committed to helping others and to leaving a lasting legacy.

At Advocate Financial Management, our mission is to serve our clients as a trusted advisor, providing them with the tools and caring counsel necessary to simplify and take control of their financial future. Allowing them to focus their energies on what they're most passionate about.

Visit our website at http://www.AdvocateFInancialManagement.com
