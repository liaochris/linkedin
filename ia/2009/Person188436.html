Panteleimon Kioupakis’s Experience

  • Student on a group assignment

    M3 Housing

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2009 — May 2009 (4 months)

    During my postgraduate studies, I was involved in a group consultancy project for one of my modules which focused on the counselling of a company over an IT related issue. I was the Group Leader of the team that consisted of three other members.

    Dr Walter Skok, the Principal Lecturer and Module Leader, introduced our team to M3 Housing’s management team. Then at the company’s premises we discussed issues that concerned the implemented Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system and the vision they had for its future.

    M3 Housing allowed us to visit their premises again and explore the situation around the implemented CRM system, the opinion and experience of its users, its database and the associated programs. We also met with the very knowledgeable IT team of M3 Housing and reviewed the history concerning previous implementations.

    As a team, we hosted a presentation at Kingston University’s Business School campus where we invited members of the company’s management team to demonstrate the results of our research. The findings related to both M3 Housing and the latest developments in the CRM market, including the use of open source software. We further discussed the key issues of concern to the company and learned more about what they wanted to see analysed further in the final report.

    Without doubt, this was one of the most valuable experiences during my MSc course. I learned a lot about keeping a team together even when things do not go as planned, supporting the final goal and the decisions made and inspiring others to follow by applying a relation-oriented leadership style. I also had an excellent opportunity to apply the latest social media tools in project organization in order to keep everyone updated and monitor project progress. Subsequently, we set up a private social network that greatly enhanced communication between team members.
