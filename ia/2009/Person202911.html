Rolly Abarca’s Summary

Born and Raised in the Beautiful State of Hawai'i. I've been involved in the Audio/ Music industry since 2002. I started playing in a local Island/ Reggae Band that has changed their names from "Mana Leo" to "High Tides". Unfortunately the group had split in 2004 and ventured into different areas of the music industry. Since then, I've gotten myself in the radio industry, starting from the bottom of the "payroll" totem poll. I've been blessed to have achieve an Associates Degree of Applied Arts in Audio Production at The Art Institute of Seattle on the 13th day of June 2008. Currently I've been added as a Board Operator at Salem Media Hawaii Inc. and I'm currently the Audio Engineer for my church's outside ministry, G.A.P.(Gospel And Poetry) Music Ministry along side being the lead bassist/sound guy for my church. While trying to juggle all of this, i'm in the process of starting a music company of my own. Right now, I'm just hoping everything goes well.
A long term goal as a professional would be to gain the education and experience from higher education institutions and colleges, and the forever growing wide industry of Audio.
