Phillip W. Baker, CAS’s Summary

29 years experience in broadcasting, advertising and marketing.

Did you know that whatever your business is, you're also in the marketing business?

Because if no customers come through your door, then you don't have a business at all!

If you're having a hard time enticing those customers to come through your door, and keep coming back again and again, then you probably need some help with the marketing side of your business. That's where I come in.
