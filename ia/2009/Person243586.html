Vincent Mikolay’s Summary

As a 2000 graduate of Bethany College, I most recently served on the Global Leadership and Organization Development team with Disney Consumer Products. I focused on organizational effectiveness consultation to global executives in the areas of leadership, talent development, succession planning and organizational culture. I also led the annual Global Employee Culture Survey including action planning to continuously enhance the culture.

My education includes a Masters of Education degree (M.Ed.) in Executive Human Resource Development focused on Organization Development and a Masters of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Southern California (USC). I also hold certification from the Hay Group to administer the Emotional Intelligence Competency Inventory.

My background includes experience in both corporate and not-for-profit sectors, blending individual, team and organizational development experience with business development and planning.

My experiences include leading global initiatives, managing diverse and remote teams, defining the vision for a team a breaking it down into strategies and action plans to achieve success.

My strengths vary from executive presentation skills, negotiation, team and individual development, strategy and implementation.
