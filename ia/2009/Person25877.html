Benjamin Bykowski’s Summary

Benjamin Bykowski is Director of Technology for Optiem, LLC. In his role, Ben applies his education and knowledge in computer science and business administration with his technical and strategic skills to consult and direct client engagements. Ben's knowledge and expertise benefit Optiem clients in the areas of information architecture and design, emerging technologies, industry trends, and business development. Ben leads a team of UX developers and information architects in creating world class user experiences on the Web through the use of leading edge technology applied to well-planned architectures grounded in usability and user interface best practices.

A graduate of the Case Western Weatherhead School of Management MBA program, Ben continually keeps abreast of the latest trends in technology and design through an extensive network of professionals, by attending conferences and seminars, through active participation in social media, and by reading the latest publications both online and off. Off the grid, Ben enjoys sailing, snowboarding, reading, and is a practiced chef and DJ.
