Tina Kinirons’s Summary

Since qualifying as a psychologist Tina has specialised in how psychology can be applied within the workplace, and to the area of leadership and management in particular. She combines together her years of management experience, her consulting background and her psychology practice to work as a management coach, trainer and consultant.

She has over 20 years experience in business and worked for both IBM and Microsoft in a variety of sales, marketing, consulting, operations and management roles. She has been a manager for much of her career, including 5 years on the senior management team of Microsoft in Ireland. She has worked as a consultant for over 10 years.

She is accredited by the British Psychological Society to perform psychometric testing and is qualified in the use of the Myers Briggs Type Indicator® (MBTI®), the NEO Personality Inventory (NEO PI-R™) and a range of other psychometric tests.

She is a contributor to the leadership magazine “The Edge” published by the Institute of Leadership and Management.

In her spare time, Tina works as a volunteer for Women’s Aid in Dublin.

Tina Kinirons’s Specialties:

Management training, management coaching, psychological / psychometric testing, management consulting.
