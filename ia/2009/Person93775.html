Helanie Scott’s Summary

I am open to network! Just send me an invite: helanie@align4profit.com

Helanie Scott is an enthusiastic, creative and passionate speaker, consultant and coach. She brings an unmatched sincerity to her engagements, complimented by her unique style. She is a breath of fresh air in the realm of Leadership Team Development!

Her genuine passion for making a meaningful difference in the lives of those she touches is evident in everything she does. She is a street-smart coach, with an expanse of diversified cultural awareness, born of her life experiences in South Africa, Canada and the United States.

Helanie has 20+ years of experience in the industry and over 8,000 coaching engagements to her credit. She brings a crisp and edgy flare to the stage and the classroom, opening her Leadership Toolbox and giving away actionable tools her audiences can put into immediate use. She seeks and engages in a deeper connection with her audience, so much so, that many say she achieves a level of intimacy normally only found in the greatest of friendships.

Her goal is to empower others far beyond the face-to-face engagements for which she is in attendance. It is to inspire long-term, sustainable growth and change that leaves teams aligned to performance and purpose. Aligned for Profit.

In explaining one of her other goals, she says, “It isn’t to know what you do for a living,” but would like to know, “WHAT IT IS YOU ARE LIVING FOR?”
