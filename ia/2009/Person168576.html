Michael A. Pechony’s Summary

Managing Director at iStreet Global
2008 to present

Developed my financial skill-set working as a trading assistant for a leading market maker at the Chicago Board of Options Exchange (CBOE) 1997-2000.

Have a strong track-record as a successful entrepreneur, raising debt and equity for a start up "ads in taxis" advertising firm which was sold to a national taxi advertising firm at a healthy 130% profit for its investors within 1.5 years. 2000-2002

Subsequently bought into a low voltage and general contractor company in Chicago offering construction, smart home products and installation where worked as Director of Operations, increased the revenue of the company by over 200% in a 2 year period and sold the equity stake back to its original founders at a significant multiple of the original capital invested. 2002-2004

In 2004 opened a commercial mortgage practice, raising over 120 million in debt and equity for commercial real estate projects in the US and abroad.

During the beginning of the Real Estate turmoil, have restructured my commercial mortgage practice into a business finance solutions company offering various funding to the small business market. The portfolio of notes was later sold to a hedge fund at the end of 2007 and stayed on board as a consultant during transition.

Took a 3 months sabbatical, researched and focused on targets in the emerging markets specifically in the alternative energy sector. Later the same year founded iStreet Global which structures finance for US SME's working with emerging markets.

Currently iStreet Global is continuing to put together a global team and a board of advisors.

Please, contact me if interested.
