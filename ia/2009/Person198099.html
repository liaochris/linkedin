Richard Wan’s Summary

Richard graduated in May 2004 from Babson College and went on to become a project manager working on Fortune 500 client engagements with the senior partners at New England Consulting Group. His projects allowed him to play a main role in developing market roadmaps, segmentation studies, brand strategy and strategic roadmaps for major clients in the pharmaceutical, consumer packaged goods and high tech industries.

Leaving his consulting job on the east coast, Richard moved to the Bay area, first joining startup Velociti Partners to led consulting assignments on enterprise solutions software (branding, marketing and execution of implementing enterprise level applications) for major technology players in and around Silicon Valley. He then managed online campaigns for clients and agencies in the online media space at High Voltage Interactive.
