Seth Meinzen’s Summary

Seth Meinzen is a passionate entrepreneur committed to helping others, supporting local entrepreneurs, and to training businesses how to increase sales opportunities through the activation of networks, events, and social medias.

Seth's commitment to training is aparent in his role as a Partner in Core Advocates, a strategic relationship process that aids clients in increasing business development results by working to activate their networks. Core Advocates combines the principles in "word-of-Mouth" Marketing with that of Social Media & Networking to provide clients a systematic process for activating relationships into sales opportunities for both the offline and online worlds.

To support entrepreneurs, Seth cofounded startPATH, Inc., a virtual incubator guiding entrepreneurs starting or growing new ventures. The virtual incubator has a startup pathway which offers a systematic process for starting a new company, access to local support services and resources, mentoring, progress reviews for investor validation, and investor connections upon the completion of the pathway.

To continue his passion for helping entrepreneurs, Seth co-founded a physical incubator in North Kansas City, called the Avvio Business Accelerator. Which offers services and office space to startups and small businesses seeking to grow.

To help others, Seth co-founded two local events, startFEST & Just for Starters, to support the development and growth of Kansas City entrepreneurs and businesses. The inspiration for both events came from the need to create publicity and provide greater access to resources for startups and growing businesses. The need was there so Seth and his co-founders decided that they may as well be the ones to act on it.

Seth acts as a marketing/strategic consultant to help grow KidsTube.com, a monitored, user-generated video platform that focuses on providing kids a fun and safe community to create and watch videos.

Seth Meinzen’s Specialties:

Entrepreneurship, creative marketing, bootstrapping, Market research, advertising, strategic planning, gorilla marketing and selling, mentoring start-ups, product/service development, launching new products/services, fostering economic development, small business consulting, and state and local politics.
