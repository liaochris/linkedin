About MPI:
We are an organization organized as a response to world wide economic crisis. We feel that human potential is small, even micro, when unrealized, however, when realized, nurtured and given power, becomes an amazing force. Micro Power Incorporation is founded and dedicated to improve the quality and the life of humans by identifying their potential and transforming it into prosperity through training and support.We focus on individuals, families and small groups that often suffer the most and often don't get the help they need. As world economic crisis increases, these families and small groups will need more support than ever. We intend to identify them, identify their potential and resources, and match them to groups or individuals that can provide the training, help or resources they need in order to improve their quality of life and even prosper. MPI will provide much of the training within our organization, what resources we can't offer ourselves, we will find and recruit.We invite people from all over the world to become involved as a network of legends that will identify those in need and work with MPI to empower humans to reach their potential. We will lift the world, one life at a time. Are you interested? I welcome all invites.
