Julia Campbell’s Summary

Professional experiece:
Directing and Performing both in film and live theatre with Unicorn Productions both in NZ and Australia. Working as a Freelance Film-maker and Post-Production Artist.

Goals:
To continue to develop as a director and performer of film and theatre, as well as creating art that is challenging to both the creator and the viewer. I would like to continue to look at what constitutes theatre, film and art with my company Unicorn Productions.

Julia Campbell’s Specialties:

Video and Audio Editing, and Motion Design including extensive work with Final Cut Studio, Adobe Premiere, After Effects, Illustrator and Photoshop.

Physical Theatre, including work with Viewpoints, Suzuki and Butoh.
