Bree Nguyen’s Summary

Campus Explorer Bio:
Bree Nguyen, Senior Director of Marketing, leads the Marketing team at CampusExplorer.com. CampusExplorer.com is a venture backed startup company based out of Santa Monica, CA and is a complete directory and search engine of post secondary schools in the US. Bree is responsible for traffic acquisition including managing Campus Explorer’s search engine marketing, affiliate marketing and partner development. At Campus Explorer, Bree joins her former colleagues from Rent.com which was purchased by eBay for $433 million in February of ’05.

Warner Bros Bio:
Bree Nguyen, Director of New Media and Community Marketing, is highly skilled in new media marketing and leads the community-marketing department at Warner Bros. Records. At WBR, Bree develops word of mouth marketing campaigns and cultivates artist communities using new media and mobile strategies to establish an artist’s brand and engage fans and consumers online. Previously Bree has worked in new media marketing at Maverick Records and directly for numerous artists and companies ranging from Mariah Carey to Michelle Branch. Bree also has a history of marketing and business development at Internet and e-commerce companies including Rent.com, which was purchased by eBay for $433 million in February of ’05.

- Expert in community marketing.
- Expert in youth and teen marketing.
- Expert in Search Engine Marketing.
