Dick Hanna’s Summary

My history as an ecologist, businessman and artist gives me a broad base of experience and a unique set of skills. It’s not about using the “left” brain or the “right” brain, it’s about bringing the whole brain to bear on the task at hand.

Operating my independent graphic design business Studio One Design for over a decade, I have combined a passion for creativity with practical problem-solving skills to produce successful marketing communications for clients in a wide variety of technical, industrial, manufacturing, retail and non-profit organizations. With Taproot Bookkeeping Services, I can provide you and your accountant with the solutions you need to keep your financial records in top shape and help your business thrive.

My goal is always the same: To develop long term relationships as a dependable problem solver and reliable business resource. Whether that’s helping you get your new business up and running, keeping your existing business running efficiently with effective bookkeeping solutions, or developing your brand identity and reaching customers with marketing communications that resonate with your audience.

Dick Hanna’s Specialties:

Full charge bookkeeping: Ongoing management of your financial records, new business QuickBooks Installation, QuickBooks review & cleanup.

Creative services from concept to completion: Strategy, concept, art direction, graphic design & production.

Technical: Print and web design using Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat, Dreamweaver, Flash), Quark XPress, Word, Excel. Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor.
