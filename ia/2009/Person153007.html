Mark Payne’s Summary

If you balance the heart & spirit of an entrepreneur, the strategic mind of a chess player, with the passion to explore new horizons and challenges like that of a daredevil, your end result would be Mark Payne.

Mark has over 20 years of professional experience spanning the Aerospace & Defense, Commercial Aviation, Financial Services & Hospitality Industries.

Mark is a decorated veteran of the U.S. Navy. Mark specialized in the technical field of cryptology while working for a branch of Naval Intelligence which reported up to the National Security Agency (NSA). Mark previously held one of the highest levels of Top Secret (SCI) Security Clearances while conducting "SpecOp" submarine missions.

Mark graduated from the University of Phoenix with a Bachelor of Science in Business with a major in Information Systems (IS) while attending full-time at night while working full-time for Raytheon Missile Systems during the day.

Mark is a graduate of The Flight Control Academy at Tucson, Arizona. Mark has obtained Aircraft Dispatcher and Private Pilot Certifications. Mark has dispatched over 10,000 commercial airline flights.

A defined career path towards financial analysis or investment fund management would also be considered.

* However, Mark is not all about technical certifications, computing technologies, financial services and aerospace mumbo jumbo ...

* Mark's creativity, artistic talent, passion, and humor are evidenced by his portfolios of photography, illustrations & pieces of digital art which are currently selling worldwide. They are showcased and sell daily at various commercial stock photography agencies around the world.

* Additionally, Mark spent part of his early professional years working in the entertainment & hospitality industries. An opportunity to cross into a cruise line, hotel, resort, or travel company would certainly be welcomed.
