Ross Shott’s Summary

FUTURIST

Over 25 years Mr. Shott has led military units, manufacturing plants, service operations, retail establishments, construction projects, and distribution chains. He has worked with companies ranging from 20 to over 8,000 employees. He uses his unique blend of strategic foresight education and practical management experience to coach executives in enterprise transformation through leading from the future.

Master’s Curriculum: Introduction to Futures Studies, Social Change, Systems Thinking, World Futures, Futures Research, Organizational Learning and Performance, Global Management, Advanced Strategies, Pro-seminar, among others.

Domain Research: Transhumanism – focused on the technologies leading to life-extension and human enhancement; created a framework forecast on implantable cybernetic devices; conducted interviews and survey of foresight specialists regarding the future of cyborgs (human-machine interfacing).

Minor Research: Traced current trends and forecasts in modeling simulation, nanotechnology, ICT management, technology standards, regulatory frameworks, mobility of goods, and sources of talent.

Prior Interests: My personal hobby before beginning my academic study was investigating altered states of higher consciousness such as those described in the works of Dr. Charles Garfield (Peak Performance, Zone), Dr. Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi (Optimal Experience, Flow), and Dr. William James (Hyper Emotion, Rapture).
