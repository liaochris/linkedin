Patrick Toole’s Summary

Experience: over 14 years in strategy, marketing, advertising, call center, customer service and sales; Sr. Executive entrepreneur, both big company and start-up ventures, valued adviser to local Chamber, Young Professional Network, and Community Service organizations.

Education: Florida State University (1997) with BS in Marketing/Communications; MBA from University of Central Florida (2002); Harvard Business School, Executive Management Program (CTAMU – Class of 2009).

Mr. Toole is the proprietor of Melrose Media Group, LLC. (MMG, LLC.), an agency focused in the Premium Channel, Digital Media, TV Network, and Cable Operator sectors. MMG supports its clients by allowing their Direct and Indirect Marketing divisions to focus on their brand, strategy, and value proposition, while MMG quickly develops, tests, implements new marketing concepts through a network of online "indirect" sales channels. MMG leverages Search Engine Optimization and Search Engine Marketing (SEO/SEM) programs that are scalable and cost effective for driving and retaining RGUs.

Mr. Toole’s prior role was at Bridgevine, Inc., one of the largest SEO/SEM marketing companies that aggregates digital services. He was one of BV's first employees and investors. Bridgevine was named 2007 Inc500 and 2008 Inc500; and 2008 Deloitte Technology Fast 500. BV is backed by leading venture capital firms.

Patrick also worked for AOL, where he served under Customer Retention. He designed and implemented sales/customer retention programs across its national and international call centers (US, Mexico, Canada, and India). While attending college, he co-founded Campus Promotions, a collegiate campus marketing company that launched two publications and helped promote bands including Sister Hazel, Dave Matthews Band and Creed.

Mr. Toole is a community servant, active in the YPN, Executive of CTAM Fl, and Board of Directors for the Riverside Children’s Theater.

Patrick Toole’s Specialties:

Full and deep understanding of start-up ventures, retention marketing, SEM, SEO in the Broadband, Video, Phone, Digital Cable, Triple Play sectors.
