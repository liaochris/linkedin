Michael Goins’s Summary

Michael Goins is the co-owner of Blue Sky Communications, LLC. Michael has 16 years of experience in the communications field as an award winning television journalist and in senior level communication positions in the area of corporate, government and non-profit agencies.

Michael previously served as Director of Public Relations for Forcht Group of Kentucky, a holding and management corporation comprised of 95 companies. Before joining Forcht Group of Kentucky Goins held a variety of senior communications positions with the administration of Governor Ernie Fletcher, including serving as his Deputy Communications Director. Goins also served as Executive Director, Office of Public Affairs with the Kentucky Transportation Cabinet, Director of Communicaitions for the Kentucky Office of State Budget Director, and Executive Director of Communications for the Kentucky Education Cabinet.

Goins has also worked as an award winning television journalist in Kentucky, Ohio and West Virginia. During his time television news Goins was recognized for outstanding accomplishments in broadcast excellence by the Associated Press and the Kentucky Broadcasters Association, including Best Investigative News Story and Best Continuing News Story.

Goins, originally from Cumberland, is a 1991 graduate of Eastern Kentucky University and an alumnus of Lambda Chi Alpha fraternity’s Phi Beta chapter, where he served as Vice President and chairman of the organization’s public relations committee. He resides in the Lexington area with his wife Beth Goins, a senior public relations specialist with the University of Kentucky and their two daughters, Meghan and Madeline.
