Jane Asher’s Summary

Voice Over Vision is my voice over business that I own & operate from my home studio. I love being in my studio and you can find me there daily working on various projects that include commercial, industrial, phone-message and training videos. I offer a quick turnaround, competitive rates and will voice the job until you are satisfied.

I have the extreme pleasure of connecting easily with other positive individuals in the broadcasting, voice over and music business and through these connections the Voice Over Vision Group was born. If you are so inclined please join us, we welcome new members with a fresh perspective.

I still love radio and can be found on the air in San Diego for CBS Radio weekends on Sophie @1037 I also fill in for KyXy 96.5 when they need an extra voice.

I published my first children's book "With a Cherry on Top!" in 2006 which is available through amazon.com.
