Babita Jaishankar’s Summary

Built a career as an expert fashion designer and a certified skin and color analyst and enhanced the presence of lots of women. I have also done a management course for women entrepreneurs form Indian Institute of Management (IIM-Bangalore).
In U.S. I worked as a skin and color consultant for several years helping women learn to pick their colors, teaching the art of makeup .Helping clients to identify the right regime of skin care according to their skin type and also introducing them to the age defying products according to client’s specific needs.
I owned a fashion consultancy and used to design exclusive suits and blazers for women in Ahmedabad, the textile city of India after completing a course in Fashion Designing.
I am launching my own company, Wardrobe solutions(WSol), with simple credo that a dynamic personal and professional image could be created without having to relinquish personal style or comfort and also launching own brand of clothing .
WSol offers private consultation and remarkable makeovers for women, brides to be, moms to be.
