Robert Sofia’s Summary

Robert achieved success in business at a remarkably young age. By 22, he had developed and launched a revolutionary, relationship-based marketing model for which he gained international recognition in his industry. It went on to serve as a model for hundreds of Business Development Centers at Ford Dealers around the world.

At 24, Robert was hired into his first executive role as Vice President of Marketing for Cebert Wealth Management Group, Inc, a thriving financial planning corporation. During his tenure with Cebert, Robert acquired his General Securities and Investment Advisor registrations from the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA).

Robert was later recruited to serve as Vice President of Fross and Fross Financial, a brokerage firm that currently manages over $150,000,000.00 and which he is still a part of today.

In June of 2008, Robert founded The GAIN Group, Inc., which utilizes a program of motivational speaking and seminars to equip college students around the country with the tools to succeed in business. Robert also teaches business how to attract and retain young workers, how to enhance their image so they will appeal to consumers under thirty, and how to market to Generation Y.

Robert was recently contracted by Penguin Group to write The Complete Idiot's Guide To Business Success In Your 20's and 30's. It is scheduled for release in fall 2009.
