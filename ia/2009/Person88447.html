Gordon Chow’s Summary

Over 20 years of Food & Beverage management as well as an extensive background in customer service, retail and banking. Experience in design and start-up of restaurants and bars. Wide range of experience from Nightclubs, Country Clubs, Hotels and Resorts to privately owned establishments. Became Manager of Union Station Brew Pub at the age of 19, by the age of 25 had opened several Toronto Nightclubs before becoming Restaurant & Nightclub Manager for The Docks Nightclub, a 14000 capacity nightclub/entertainment complex in Toronto, Canada. Shortly there after, started managing in the corporate world with Marriott Desert Springs a JW Marriott Resort, also working for Indian Wells Country Club, Big League Dream Sports Park, Hilton Hotels, Larkspur Landing Hospitality and presently with Hawaiian Airlines as Station Manager Maui.
