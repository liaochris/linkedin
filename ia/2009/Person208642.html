Sandra Eisenberg’s Summary

A marketing and sales pro comfortable in new media and traditional. Sandra's marketing expertise spans guerrilla marketing, Pragmatic Marketing (for full product lifecycle management), Product Management as well as Search Engine Optimization (SEO) (so prospects can find you easily on search engines and directories), Paid Search advertising, online advertising, email campaigns (Constant Contact), public relations (Vocus), and competitive intelligence (pure research as well as Gartner, IDC and other sources).

Sandra brings twenty years of experience in sales, marketing and IT management. The career spans entrepreneurial firms (E5 Marketing) and senior positions in sales, sales management (direct and indirect), marketing, channel development and product management at Bell Labs and NCR Teradata.


• Vice President of wireless and wireline marketing at E5. Ran the marketing department for Global Locate, wrote the wireless standards guide for TIA, worked with Sprint’s largest reseller;

• Director of CRM Strategic Planning and Alliances at Avaya and NCR Teradata;

• Senior Manager of Product Management Bell Labs (business intelligence, data warehousing and CRM);

• 1st woman to win the AT&T and NCR Teradata national sales awards

• Delivered profitable marketing campaigns in the area of CRM, Business intelligence, contact centers and other high tech areas;

• Won NCR’s President’s Award for M&A of IBM’s Telco data warehouse OEM;

• Lifecycle product management of $100 million Telco product suite (NEBS compliant fault tolerant platform, OSS, BSS and data warehouse software);

• Run call centers (sales and customer service), sold call centers and been in product management of call centers (Avaya, AT&T and NCR).

Sandra lives in Orlando, Florida and can be reached at 407-919-9214.
