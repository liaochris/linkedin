Rosa Vela’s Summary

- Financial and auditing professional specializing in business process improvement, risk management, internal audit, financial reporting controls, and compliance with Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) reviews.
- International experience serving small to large size companies throughout Latin America and the U.S. in the mining, financial services, manufacturing and fishing industries.
- Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Visio and PowerPoint) and ability to understand new technologies. Fluent in English and Spanish and conversational French and Portuguese.

Rosa Vela’s Specialties:

- Identified business processes, conducted risk assessments, tested controls and developed recommendations to improve internal controls. Processes assessed included: entity level-COSO, accounting, purchasing, sales, treasury, fixed assets, human resources, information system controls among others.
- Main clients served: one of the world's largest gold producers, one of the world's largest automakers, a global leader in the beverage industry, one of the world's leading banks, among others.
