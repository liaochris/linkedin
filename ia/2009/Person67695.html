Dr. Susan Bernstein’s Summary

I help fast-track, high-energy professionals to transform "work" into an expression of what's most meaningful to them. In short I guide professionals to breathe new life into their careers, so they are inspired to contribute more, in ways that really matter.

Are you or your staff worn out, stuck, boxed in, blocked, or sick and tired of your work? Or perhaps you're freaked out about the current economy and immersed in constant anxiety?

It's time to change all of that. To attain greater satisfaction from your work, analytical tools and surveys won't take you to new, inspiring places. If it's time for a true transformation, you must to do more than "figure it out." Real change involves integrating your whole self. In addition to your intellect, the key ingredient you need to help you become vibrant, creative, and on-purpose is your Embodied Intelligence(tm).

Your Embodied Intelligence includes the non-verbal "smarts" of your heart, your gut, and the rest of your body, and there's a well-researched science of success behind following this inner wisdom.

I've spent nearly a decade researching and refining Embodied Intelligence as the key to greater satisfaction and success. I apply a sharp strategic business sense with cutting-edge research on the mind-body connection to teach you and your team how to have more "aha" moments of insights that allow you to tap into more of your vast potential. By combining your gut wisdom and heart's desires with your intellect, you can inform your career -- and your life -- in new, more enlivening ways.

You'll bring more of your best self to work, with better results and enjoyment.

I'm offering individual coaching for only a few more months as I launch workshops and speaking engagements for corporate clients. If you're at a career crossroads and want to get clarity about what's next and the confidence to pursue it, contact me for a FREE "get acquainted" session: http://workfromwithin.com/assessment.php
