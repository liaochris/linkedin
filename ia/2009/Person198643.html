Rick Naylor’s Summary

Rick comes from a classic sales and marketing management career holding key positions with well-known firms, including Intermec Technologies, IBM, and Intelligent Electronics. Rick has fulfilled numerous roles throughout his career from: sales, marketing, business development and product development. His credo is simple: understand what the customer wants and bring it to them.

Named Sales & Marketing Executive of the Year by Sacramento Magazine, Rick was the architect of one of the largest private-public contracts in the US. In his most recent assignment at Intermec Rick built a $32M government group into a $82M Public Sector division in just 3 years.

As the VP of Sales for Intelligent Electronics he built a new division that led to over $400mm in sales from its inception. At IBM he was brought in to reorganize a region where he doubled the regions revenue in IBM's Personal Software Division.

Rick Naylor’s Specialties:

Sales (public sector, retail, corporate, industrial), marketing, competitive analysis, market research, channel development, new market development, brand identity, product introduction
