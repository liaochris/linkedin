Jens Rasmussen’s Summary

Jens Rasmussen, is an actor, director and film maker with experience in nearly 100 stage productions and dozens of films. Based in New York City. He has performed for the United Nations, as well as Off-Broadway with Pan Asian Rep, Synapse Productions, Epiphany Theatre Co, The Assembly, and others. He has been a resident guest artist at theaters such as Mill Mountain Theatre in Virginia, The State Theatre of Georgia, Stage One, and the Kentucky Shakespeare Festival. He is especially proud of the award winning films A Boy and a Girl, Rendezvous with Rama (which was featured in American Cinematographer) and his recent documentary experience with the Jack Mountain Bushcraft and Wilderness Survival Program.
A dedicated autodidact, his education has led him from the foothills of the French Alps, to Michael Howard Studios in NYC, from Copenhagen, Denmark where he attended the International School of Theatre Anthropology, to the American Dance Festival at Duke University, and most recently to the New England wilderness to study Wilderness Survival and Traditional Skills.

Jens Rasmussen’s Specialties:

Stage Combat (broad sword & shield, rapier and dagger, unarmed), Dance (waltz, tango, swing, butoh, contact improvisation), Puppetry (shadow, rod, etc), Yoga (13 yrs), Didgeridu, Improvisation (long & short form), Juggling (hat, balls, pins), Fire Breathing, Stilt-Walking.
