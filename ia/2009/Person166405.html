Marcela Jenney’s Summary

Professional Translation and Localization coach, consultant and trainer with over 20 years of experience. Vast acknowledged expertise in translation and localization business administration encompassing all organizational areas, including language asset management, supply chain management, business development and management, communication, marketing, training and development. Experienced in designing and implementing processes, procedures and best business practices. Aptitude for recognizing and implementing cutting edge strategies and methodology. Reputation for producing the highest quality work and for developing and promoting leadership-focused and results-oriented operations. Former President of the Florida division of the American Translators Association. Fluent in English and Spanish.
