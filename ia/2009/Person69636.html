Susan Bryant’s Summary

I am a two-time Pulitzer Prize team finalist, have 15 years of newspaper experience and spent the last year broadening my skills at a Web design/development firm.

I am seeking full-time employment in publishing, writing, editing, social media, communications, marketing, management, research, media relations, or nonprofit organizations.

No matter the job or the field, I am known for high standards and consistent quality, encouraging and inspiring others to achieve the same level.

I'm a natural-born manager, with excellent people and team-player skills, honed over 12 years. I am highly organized, detail oriented and a master at meeting and enforcing deadlines.

As an editor, I assigned stories to staff writers and freelancers, who I supervised. I planned the content for my sections, while meeting budget and juggling multiple projects. Coaching and mentoring writers were the most rewarding parts of the job.

I know the dynamic and vibrant culture of South Florida intimately. As an ambassador of the South Florida Sun-Sentinel, I was highly involved in businesses, neighborhoods, communities, associations and groups as a Community News editor for nearly a decade.
