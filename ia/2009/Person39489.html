Craig Wiggins’s Summary

Entrepreneur and technology professional with broad work and life experience across several industries, memberships, and volunteer activities. I provide informed advice, recommendations, and action based on this broad experience.

Current and former clients include nonprofit, manufacturing, professional service, higher education, medical practice, and municipal authorities. Technology engagements have included general information technology support; database design, programming, and administration; messaging implementation, management, and support; server and workstation sales, service, and configuration; web site design, hosting, and maintenance.

As a leader, teacher, and mentor, I have helped organizations grow and succeed; connected people and organizations with others who can help them succeed; and coached and mentored others to do the same.

I speak English as primary language, am near-fluent in American Sign Language, comfortable with written Spanish, learning Arabic.

I am open to engagement opportunities outside the United States, particularly in the Middle East, in information technology, education, or nonprofit/NGO management.

Craig Wiggins’s Specialties:
