Dolf Wittkämper’s Summary

Always taken the opportunity to broaden myself in many fields of interest. As a result perform in various areas of competence and roles: design manager & team leader, new business creator, creative director, account director, interaction designer, product designer, project manager, consultant and new service director.

End responsible at Philips Design in various roles for 3 Philips divisions, 9 Philips business groups and 6 external market customers. Worked with many businesses, partners and suppliers in various market segments.

Gained experience in domains such as. Innovation and strategy, new business creation, people and trends research, corporate Identity, brand management, e-business strategy and design, new business creation, naming, product design, interaction design, online engagement, co-creation and virtual worlds.
I enjoy the ability to use these skills in an integrative way to match todays complex business requirements, especially in the domain of virtual engagement and innovation.
