John Fitzgerald’s Experience

    Crain Communications

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    January 1998 — Present (11 years 8 months)

    John Fitzgerald is Director of Business Planning and Online Commerce for the Automotive News Group at Crain Communications, responsible for all online product development, audience development, and client solutions for the industry-leading website www.autonews.com. He managed the $17 million Automotive News circulation department for six years, setting year-over-year revenue and profitability records. He has also led all budgeting and financial planning activities for the entire business unit since August 2001. John added direct P&L responsibility for online operations in June 2005, including spearheading the launch of digital edition, webinars and webcasts, Automotive News Today daily video newscast, custom e-mail newsletters, desktop alerts, career center (job board) and an online product and service directory. Online revenue - display advertising, classified advertising, digital/online subscriptions, data sales, and corporate licenses - has more than tripled since he took over the online commercial operations.
