Rajender Singh (rajs@oraclebrains.com)’s Summary

Since 1999, I am working in information technology field and specializing in Oracle technologies. All these years I have gone through various positions and have successfully managed the responsibilities that comes with the positions.

Apart from my work, Oracle has been my passion since first time I learn about it while studying relational database subject.

I am Oracle Certified Professional in Application Development and Oracle Certified Associate in Oracle Database 10g.

I am member of Oracle Technology Network since 2001 and member of MNCC (computer society of Malaysia) since 2004.

Apart from it, I am proud owner of website called oraclebrains.com which is dedicated to all Oracle professional and is one of the way in which I am trying to share my experience and knowledge with others.

Recently I have written a bookmark plugin for wordpress using PHP. Till date it has around 9300+ official downloads from wordpress.

Currently I am holding a official position of TECH LEAD in RQNET MSC Sdn. Bhd.

Concurrently I am also running a software development company called Datawiz Consultancy Services Sdn. Bhd. in Malaysia with RQNET MSC Sdn. Bhd. as my partner company.

The corporate name Datawiz is symbolic for "Data", reflecting the company's pursuit of data management related opportunities, and "wiz", signifying the company's talent as a wizard in managing any kind of data through our off the shelf product or a customize solution.

Under this identity, Datawiz focuses its product, marketing and development efforts on ERP Software & System, Business Process Management System and any adhoc requirement of a business entity using its proprietary ERP engine or through its business partner's solution which ever is appropriate and cost effective.
