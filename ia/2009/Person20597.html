Steve Koontz’s Summary

Artzen Consulting will provide professionally structured leadership to plan and develop high-value, quality, long-term cost effective facilities and landscapes that enhance the cultural and commercial missions of ourselves and our clients, embrace our partnership with our community, and reinforce our stewardship of the environment. To that end, Artzen Consulting endeavors to:

• Provide services with integrity and professionalism
• Enhance performance with each project undertaken
• Communicate clearly to both internal and external stakeholders
• Function as an efficient and supportive team leader
• Diligently pursue the common goals of our company and our clients
• Approach project challenges with creativity, respecting the ideas of others
• Balance the diverse needs and wants of all parties involved in our projects
• Endeavor to make the process rewarding and enjoyable

Artzen Consulting has four primary goals:

Cost - Deliver projects of long-term value within justifiable, benchmarked budgets.
Performance - Deliver projects that fulfill project goals, process expectations as identified and agreed upon by the project team leadership and, most importantly, satisfy the needs of the users.
Quality - Deliver energy efficient facilities, buildings and landscapes that foster and continue a tradition of high quality, to create and enhance a distinctive sense of place.
Schedule - Deliver projects in a professional, timely manner.
