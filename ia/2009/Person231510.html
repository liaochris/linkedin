Jan Lee Petersen | Teleseminar Marketing’s Summary

Teleseminar Specialist:

Many companies have great products or services and a list of prospects who still haven't bought from them. We can change this!

Much like a radio interview, we create, host and record interviews that are content-rich and designed to professionally promote your product or service. Unlike a salesperson who is out there "selling" with "prospects" ears closed, the interview process is geared toward "information" and answering your prospects real questions...the ones that are keeping them from buying. We help companies discover what their market's questions are and by having a professional interviewer pose those questions to be answered, this accelerates trust and initiates buying behavior.

We create the marketing materials before, during and after the interview. We design the website to help uncover your market's questions. We create the structure of the interview for maximum effectiveness. We provide the bridgeline, recording and can offer an audio player of the interview for your website. We host the interview and professionally promote your designated product or service.
