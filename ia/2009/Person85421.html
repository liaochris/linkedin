George Sims’s Summary

My personal goal is to spend 50% of the time traveling and sailing with my family and friends. My profession goal is to spend the other 50% of time helping others achieve their life's goals and dreams.

To achieve these goals I assist others with personal development techniques, sales training courses, and assist in selecting, setting up and running both online and off-line Network Marketing home businesses or small traditional businesses in order that they be able to reach their lifetime goals.

My early years of employment were spent in the Engineering field with activities ranging from survey to design to owner/management duties.

Several years spent in the Financial Services industry recruiting and training insurance agents and Registered Representatives is the experience which actually solidified my current goals. Many of those people today are millionaires or at least financially independent because of the opportunity and training they received from our company.

I also spent ten years as owner and manager of an area insurance brokerage agency working with independent life and health agents. We represented 10 to 15 insurance companies and recruited hundreds of independent agents for those companies.

Lifetime miscellaneous projects include the development of a subdivision, the ownership of a fast food restaurant, ownership of a four state long distance telephone franchise, the design of a computerized contact marketing system with integrated sales scripts and processes, and various consulting activities related to the personal development, sales psychology, the building and development of sales organizations and small and/or home based businesses.

I have now retired from all other activities to focus 100% of my working time mentoring and helping others achieve Financial and Time freedom by engaging in their personal development and starting and owning their own small business or home based business.

George Sims’s Specialties:

Understanding and teaching all facets and principles of personal development, sales psychology, organization building, and owning of successful traditional small or home based business.

In addition, we also intertwine a rarely used concept which enables small business owners, home based or not, to effectively increase their prospecting activities, their customer retention, and also their referral activity and within the same process, convert these traditional costs into profit centers!
