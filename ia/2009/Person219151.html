Sarah Lay’s Summary

I have worked on online content since leaving university in 2000 and have experience of both the private and public sector.
I am currenly within the eContent team at Derbyshire County Council and am responsible for managing and commissioning content across a number of websites and intranets; devising and delivering training to our devolved authorship on using the content management system and writing for the web; accessibility; usability; SEO; stats analysis and profiling; social media and digital engagement.
I also have experience of writing for print and radio broadcasting (mainly volunteer experience!).
My undergraduate degree was in Journalism and my post graduate study is in eCommunications. My thesis will be based on social media in local government.
I am passionate about online life and social media in my personal life also.
