Tori Martinez’s Summary

Tori has practiced and refined her tactical and practical skills in a wide range of public relations and marketing positions. Her experience encompasses industries as diverse as healthcare, cosmetics, education, energy, biometrics and human resources. She also has extensive experience in the high-tech sector, where she has held senior positions in both the agency and corporate environments.

While at such public relations agencies as Publicis and Springbok Technologies, Tori's clients ranged from high-profile corporations like Nortel Networks and Nestle, to small start-ups in all sectors. Her past and current client roster includes other well-known names such as BT Americas, TXU Energy, the Dallas Independent School District, AT&T, Compaq-HP and Microsoft.

Throughout her career, Tori has developed and executed media relations and marketing campaigns, worked with industry analysts, organized special events and trade shows, written and designed marketing collateral materials, facilitated media training, and conducted new business development and strategic account planning. She is also an experienced professional writer, specializing in bylined articles, internal and external corporate communications, as well as blogs and other new media.
