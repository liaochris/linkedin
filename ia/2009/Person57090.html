David Molina’s Summary

I help business-minded organizations advertise, recruit and retain bilingual [Spanish+ English] talent, while assisting bilingual talent in advancing their careers. I have over 11 years experience inspiring people to take the lead, increasing organizational performance and building community. I blend business strategies with cultural/linguistic understanding.

I build online resource marketing and personal career strategies, from connecting organizations with talent, both offline and online to organizing conferences.

I speak at several conferences and for private organizations, bridging talent gaps with innovative technology concepts and helping mid-management and executive potentials reach their heights.

I blog, write articles, develop and help organizations grow and expand in an ever increasing culturally and linguistically challenging environment.

My background is in politics, entrepreneurship, and community development. I mix my passion for transparent communication with my ability to fluently build communities using emerging technologies.

David Molina’s Specialties:
