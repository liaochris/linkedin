I began my insurance career in Buffalo, NY, with a nationally recognized auto insurance company as an inside adjuster. I was soon promoted to injury claims associate and relocated to Savannah, Georgia. Another promotion quickly followed and I was reassigned to their Metro Atlanta office where I was given a variety of responsibilities from Extended Handler to that of a Team Leader.

I continued to build upon this experience and expanded my insurance expertise working for with another major insurance carrier located in the Alpharetta, Georgia. There I worked as a Unit Manager in charge of the North Carolina and Georgia Fire and Theft department.

Still expanding my insurance career knowledge, I was again promoted and employed by another nationally recognized insurance company in Sarasota, Florida. While there, I managed Nationwide's South West Florida Casualty Claims district. I also assisted the executive management team in the restructuring of this district and that of the entire state's field offices. My diversified insurance knowledge was recognized and I was honored to be accepted into their “Nationwide Leadership Development Program”. During my time with Nationwide, my district exclusively handled attorney represented casualty claims and had an average tenure of 10+ years of complex claims experience.
