  • Sled Dog Trainer/Racer

    In Great Outdoors :-) (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; Myself Only; Sports industry)

    August 1986 — July 2000 (14 years)

    Moved backed to Minnesota where I continued to race and won nearly every mid-distance (60 to 200 miles) sled dog race in MN, MI and WI. I also raced in Canada and Alaska always finishing in the top four.

For three years I owned a business were I gave sled dog rides at a ski resort and also took people out on day trips and over nights and taught them how to "mush".

    For the years that I trained and raced sled dogs, they were some of the most incredible years of learning, growing, fun and one of those true great experiences in life.
