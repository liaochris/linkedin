Owned by Mac Brown and his brothers, HCC held the Newbridge Unix desktop support and replacement contract for over 10 years, was the largest Axil Var in North America (Hyundai Sparc server), and shared the seclusive distributorship for Network Appliance (Net App) with Network SAppliance itself. I managed the accounting /finance function and business process / software application development for 6 years of intense growth. HCC was top 50 for growth in Canada 5 sucessive years during this period.

  • Owner, Manager, CFO

    Plastic Accessories

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Plastics industry)

    July 1984 — December 1990 (6 years 6 months)

    Plastic Accories was a plastic fabricator, working with ridig plastics. We used Cad driven routers and engravers, and were among the first in NA to recgnize >DFX as a rising standard. We used AutoCad to covert DFX into HPGL to run our engravers starting in 1987. We combined AutoCad with remote access PC software and Paradox using Autolisp and parametric software to create a "product catalogue to tool path" process including sheet yeilding. This made Pastic Accessories a pioneer of on-line shopping prior to the advent of the Internet.
