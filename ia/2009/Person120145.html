Jorge Figueiredo’s Summary

Jorge has been recognized by peers as an entrepreneur. He opened a green consulting business “EcoSoluções”, a brand management business “Top Brands” and the the 1st Portuguese barter business “Trocamais”. He started with the media agency Initiative in the late 90’s. Within the international department (G. Villas-Boas), he provided support and training to recently opened Initiative offices in Latin America. He moved to Chile and in Jan 2000 he was settled by J-C Petit at Lowe Brazil (IPG group), to beef up the regional media business from São Paulo. Ten months later, Initiative won the Unilever account pitch for Latin America. He then moved to Poland (G. Hughes) where he was the Initiative regional process director for central and Eastern Europe region (Warsaw, Prague, Moscow, Budapest and Baltic countries). He was then invited by P.Oncken to come back to Brazil, as the media operations director for the regional Unilever account.
Back home in Lisbon he was hired a couple of times by Initiative, as a media consultant, for projects in South America and Nigeria in a Coca-Cola pitch. Today he manages the agency Blue Orange in Lisbon, now his big challenge is the São Paulo branch implementation which will help to promote Portuguese companies in the Brazilian market.
He still enjoys regular contacts with the scouts (old veterans and active ones) and with his navy comrades. Best moments come with his wife and hand walking with his two kids.
