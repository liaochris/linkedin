Andrew Jacob’s Summary

Andy Jacob is very familiar to many Americans who have seen him featured on Local and National Television, have heard him on Syndicated Radio Shows, and have read about his Sales and Marketing Exploits in National Publications, including the Wall Street Journal. Mr. Jacob has been spotlighted on “Oprah”, and has been a guest Financial Expert on NBC, CBS, ABC, FOX, Bloomberg TV, TNT, and many others. Jacob is the author of many published articles and periodicals including the “Invisible Process of Closing’, “The Closer’s Code”, and his “Secrets” series. Jacob, known to many as one of the world’s premier Sales and Marketing Gurus, is a frequently quoted expert in a variety of online blogs, publications, and news stories. Most recently, his Jacob Report, http://www.andy-jacob.com, has been voted as one of the Leading Sales and Marketing Reports in America.

Jacob Sales and Marketing Expertise have lead to the creation of LeadPile with his Partner, Mr. Florin Ilie. Jacob, the CEO and Founder of LeadPile, works tirelessly with the LeadPile team, to ensure that LeadPile continues its position as the World’s Premier Lead Exchange, providing a central marketplace for the buying and selling of targeted consumers or “leads.” Jacob’s creativity and vision, combined with over 20 years of extensive sales, marketing, and entrepreneurial experience, lead to the launch of LeadPile in 2003. Under Jacob and Ilie’s strategic planning and guidance, the company has rapidly evolved from being a lead generation company in 2003, into the most highly respected leader in the Lead Exchange and Technology Marketing Industry.
Jacob says, “The Internet LeadGen space has been a bifurcated marketplace for too long. LeadPile's centralized marketplace, and proprietary system, allows buyers and sellers of leads to engage in the World's largest and most efficient marketplace."
