Gene Grant’s Summary

Senior technology and business manager of global market products with key strengths in derivative system implementation and operation, management of global market businesses, and risk management of global market products. Key personal attributes include problem solving skills in dynamic environments, well-developed proficiency in the art of personnel management, team building, and a tenacious goal oriented work ethic.

I have experience with most major third party derivative systems, and am presently responsible for the global Murex application for FXO, IRD, Credit, and Commodity derivatives. I have developed new proprietary applications, and reverse engineered legacy systems. Recently supervised the development of a proprietary sales pricing application for Commodity Derivatives that will add significant value to the business.

Gene Grant’s Specialties:

Front to back senior Derivative system specialist. Familiar with risk management, valuations, derivative accounting standards and procedures, P&L reporting, capital adequacy requirements, and the analysis and implementation of financial models.

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; STAN.L; Banking industry)
