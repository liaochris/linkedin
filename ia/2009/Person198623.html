Rick McPartlin’s Summary

Rick is the President and Co-Founder of The Revenue Game; a consulting firm dedicated to fundamentally changing the way businesses generate revenue.
Rick and his partner, Jane Adamson, help companies develop a model that enables them to proactively generate more profitable revenue at less cost.

Since the 70s Rick has been implementing "Revenue Generation" strategy, launching new products and implementing "Revenue Generation" services. He has run businesses and worked in sales / marketing positions up to the senior management roles with private and public companies that include McDonnell Douglas, Ameritech, ASI and Compuware. For 18 of those years, Rick has run his own companies serving such clients as E&Y, Johnson & Johnson, AT&T, Siemens, BellSouth Business Systems, Ameritech, Sun Microsystems, and Dialogic.

Under Rick’s leadership his 1st firm became the leader in implementing "Rapid Business Development" strategies into all sales channels (both direct and indirect) and at implementing the Offer Management Life Cycle Strategy. Impact created results driven models that defined the "Revenue Generation" process, and then implemented the structure and tools necessary for a company to be successful. These "Revenue Generation" models and methods applied to all business-to-business situations and most business to consumer businesses with direct and/or indirect sales channels. The result for Rick’s clients was to "Design Sales In..." to their business models.

Having received a BBA, MA, and MSB Rick has kept his academic experiences fresh over the last 25 years by being a faculty member at Kalamazoo Valley Community College, Nazareth College, Central Michigan University and the University of Phoenix. In addition Rick has been a presenter at the “Conference Board,” many professional organizations, Executive Forums and is a regular Vistage / TEC speaker. All of this helps help him keep learning from the best in the field and winning The Revenue Game.

Rick McPartlin’s Specialties:

We specialize in putting CEOs and business owners in control of their revenue through the use of "Revenue Generation" technology and best practices.
