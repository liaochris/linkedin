Stephan Marks’s Summary

After spending two and a half years trading and developing new risk management strategies we have set up Lucian Merchant Securities. Lucian.M.S is a new semi-regulated discretionary investment and off-shore advisory firm, set up to provide clients with a range of alternative off-shore, savings and investment programs. With the help from one of the most profitable hedge funds in 2008 we are able to offer a range of products along the risk spectrum. We also provide boutique services to high net-worth and institutional investors.Currently we are expanding our client base to provide a wider range of services.
