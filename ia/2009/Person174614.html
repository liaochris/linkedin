Nicolas Bombray’s Summary

Game designer with experience in concept writing, core mechanics, content and level design, I’m interested in all the fields of game design and involved in all the stages of game development.
I’ve written concept documents for games that are now in production, as well as game design documents for console games - Wii, DS, Xbox 360.
Beside concept and core mechanics, I’ve worked on game content with a strong focus on story development, dialog writing and cinematics.
I have also worked on level design, providing level builders with guidelines, 3D lay-outs and test reports.
With strong communication skills in French and English, I’m always confronting my ideas with other team members in order to learn from them and help them identify with the project. I’m also constantly looking for new methods to document my ideas in order to facilitate their implementation by the development team.

  • Game designer

    ENJMIN - Psychedelia project

    (Educational Institution; Myself Only; Computer Games industry)

    October 2006 — March 2007 (6 months)

    Psychedelia is part of my training as a game designer at the Grad School of Videogames. The challenge was to develop a first playable within 6 months with eight other students from other fields of studies: programmers, artists, sound designers and ergonomists.
    As I came up with the concept, my role in the team was to share my vision of the game to the other team members. I also worked along with another game designer, on defining and testing the core mechanics of the game - which proved very challenging as we used a movement detection device to simulate the Wiimote - designing the levels and implementing them using Gamebryo Scene Designer.

  • Game designer

    ENJMIN - School Wars project

    (Educational Institution; Computer Games industry)

    February 2006 — June 2006 (5 months)

    School Wars is the first game I've ever made, and therefore, it's especially close to my heart. This creation was part of my training at the Grad School of Videogames. The assignment was to create a complete videogame with three other people (game designer, programmer, sound designer/artist) in 4 months.
    I came up with the idea of making an arcade RTS game set in a classroom where nerds fight bad boys using paper balls, "shushing" attacks and more to win.
    My tasks on this project were to write the game document to share the idea with the other team members, project management, extensive work on sprites and animations, and a lot of testing.
