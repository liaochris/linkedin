Jeffrey Chia’s Summary

Wirizon Pte Ltd, Managing Director
Created Wirizon Pte. Ltd to market and sell mobile phone for niche market.
Formulated the design and development of 1 Step Callback for mobile phone for use in Callback services; TalkNSave.com.sg.
Manage the day to day operation of Wirizon Pte. Ltd with a turnover of above S$1 million after 2 years in operation.

Motorola Electronics Pte Ltd, Program Manager
Created the i.Smartville program after realizing a need to have a smart phone reference platform and community that promotes it. The i.Smartville program leverages the strength of participating companies to band together to create and promote a smart phone reference design. Convinced IDA to buy in to this program and resulted in obtaining iTA grant of $360K to finance the program. In additional managed to recruit companies to work together for 6 months to share IPs and developed a smart phone reference design at their own expenses.
Established the Dragonball University program in Singapore, China, Korea and USA. The university program seeks to develop Dragonball savvy engineers and created a talent pool of worker for company and partners. In additional, the program also generated a library of applications and product including a heart monitoring device from Ngee Ann Polytechnic.
Recruited skilled third party design houses as company extended R&D alliances. This involved performing a technical assessment of the organization’s needs and matching the skills and interests of the third party design house to establish synergistic alliances to leverage on each other IPs and resources in both development and commercialization capabilities. This enabled company to expand its R&D abilities and reach markets that are closed previously due to lack of resources.
