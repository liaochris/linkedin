Donald Hindman’s Summary

Look at page 42 of the October 2009 issue of Nonwovens Industry magazine for an article about my companies.

I have a passion for training production floor employees to be excited about their jobs and to be competent. We need to build up our employees. They are the most valuable resource a company has. The return on investment can be tremendous.

I have been an engineering professional in the nonwovens industry since 1973.
A member of INDA, The Association of the Nonwoven Fabrics Industry.
A member of the Greenville, SC chapter of ASTD, The American Society of Training and Development.

I recently was a speaker at the Needlepunch Nonwovens Conference sponsored by INDA. My topic was Production Employees, The Most Valuable Resource for a Nonwovens Company.
