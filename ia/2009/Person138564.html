Lawrence Socci’s Summary

During my time in the U.S. Navy, I learned skills that will benefit me for the rest of my life. These skills consist of; communication, professionalism, and government ethics. After leaving the navy, I took on an apprentice position with an independent contractor working in Washington D.C., Maryland and northern Virginia. It was there were I learned how to use my ambitions and natural drive to earn an income and negotiate contracts. Helping develop talent has always been a personal interest of mine. In 2006, I began promoting shows and independent musicians located in Loudon County, VA. As of April 2008, I have been volunteering my time and services to organizations located in Luzerne County, PA, such as NAACP, CYC (Latino committee), and Arts Youniverse. In July 2008, I created the Funday Monday event, held at public square Wilkes-Barre every Monday of the summer. This event was made possible by Arts Youniverse providing a dance instructor to teach children basic dance moves, and the city of Wilkes-Barre for letting us use the public square. My current position at Eagle Ridge Apartments allows me to work directly with families with fixed incomes and provide recreational activities and at the same time provide social events for all of our residents.
