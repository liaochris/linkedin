Robert Haleluk’s Summary

I started DTC Designs out of my own 3 car garage. Within the first year I started to churn out custom magazine cover cars from all walks of life. I knew this was rapidly growing into a full time career, so I went to school to become certified in advanced automotive electronics. After securing a Masters in Auto Electronics, I secured an investment to expand my company and buy a 6 bay automotive shop in NJ. From there, I started to acquire new and exciting projects that ended up securing my name amongst the elite in the industry. I took it upon myself to offer the best service at the best prices! I went on to become the East Coast Manager of North America's most elite car organization, TWCompetition. This brought my company to a whole new standard. We toured with custom built high-end vehicles and exotics for a total of 2 years. This helped shape and develop some outstanding and truly amazing connections throughout the automotive industry. After doing so much, I knew the name of my game wasn't retail anymore.. So in the Summer of 2007, I sold my facility to account for the necessary funds to form the Tuned Group Inc. ("TG").

Since then, I have used my connections to position myself to take over and help with the very existence of the automotive industry. We have developed a very exciting and intuitive way to help users manage their respective lifestyles. This much needed approach will become a live entity as of 2009, and our model will be the first to usher in a new wave of both on and offline interaction.

Tuned.com - An intuitive automotive based social network, built for true enthusiasts by true enthusiasts.

Tuned Motors - A unique place where users can sell anything used to make their projects "Tuned".

Advolved - The only advertising system that has taken the evolution of the internet into account.

We are excited to bring you these products, along with some others, that will truly help reinvent of the automotive industry.
