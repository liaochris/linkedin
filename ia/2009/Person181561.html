Pat O'Donnell’s Summary

Executive Career Strategist who supports individuals over their entire careers and especially during crucial transitions. Keynote speaker for professional associations and corporations.

Executive Services: (varies with your needs and/or the team you manage)

* During periods of career transition: strategic guidance and recruiting services.
* While you are active in the work force: advice and coaching several times a year. Asking the right question at the right time so that you find more confidence with your decision process and know you have examined the issue from every possible angle.
* When feeling stuck or in career stagnation: high-impact advice to get unstuck and moving in the right direction
