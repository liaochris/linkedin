Andrei Scheiner’s Summary

Six years experience on internet projects as strategical marketing consultant.

e-Marketing Officer for Incubator Project at British Council Brazil.

Former coordinator of ICO, an NGO for collective intelligence and social network adoption on enterprises. Now acting as ICO's volunteer.

Former strategical consultant at Pontonet and coordinator of ICOX 4.0 project (http://www.icox.org.br), Brazil’s first open-source software for creating and managing social networks.

Planned and developed a Latin American marketing intelligence database for British Council's Online Transformation Programme.

Coordinated strategical planning of Globoonliners (http://www.globoonliners.com.br) - Web 2.0 project for O Globo newspaper - and RadioTube (http://www.radiotube.org.br) - for NGO Criar Brasil - using ICOX.

For over 4 years, lecturer in marketing, advertising creation, web writing, e-commerce, innovation, creativity on Social Communication degree at three Universities and team creative guidance of a student Advertising Lab.

Symposium coordinator of I ERPWeb (Regional Encounter of Web Thinkers) hold at ESPM, 2007. Author of academic papers focusing on Mass Communications and Consumption.

Coursing Digital and Services Marketing MBA at ESPM (2008). MSc in Social Communications at PUC-Rio (2006). BA in Social Communications – Advertising – at ESPM (2001).

Andrei Scheiner’s Specialties:

web 2.0, digital marketing and communications strategical planning specialist, creative conception and team guidance, product and consumer trends research, social networks development, branding and identity, cyberculture analysis, corporate innovation, services marketing specialist, academic research studies.
