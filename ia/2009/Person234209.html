Tim Trotter’s Summary

Tim Trotter has 15 years of experience in Enterprise Consulting, Account Management, Project Management, Business analysis, QA Management, Process Architecture experience working in Fortune 500 companies. Companies include PepsiCo, 7-11, Verizon, Texas Instruments, TXU, Reliant Energy, Wells Fargo J.P. Morgan Chase and Amoco. Tim has enterprise wide Project management, business analysis and technical experience in manufacturing, food and beverage, warehousing, banking, energy and telecommunications industries. Tim’s experience also includes start up of enterprise wide QA teams and methodology and operational into company culture through training and metric driven results. This also included managing multiple projects at a time and teams with members. This included on shore and offshore management. Tim builds and maintains effective relationships and works directly with clients to develop customized strategies. Envisions the big picture and develops the details to plan and create new projects. Ensures high-quality, on-time results, meeting the needs of the customer and making a positive impact to the business.
