Harvey Clay’s Summary

Human Resource Specialists provide managers and employees with information on, and interpretations of, compensation, recruitment, and position management procedures, policies, and guidelines. Specialists may conduct job analysis, prepare vacancy announcements, determine qualifications, make employment offers, set pay, and process personnel actions. Human Resources Specialists may also perform process mapping, policy development, workforce analysis, and management advisory services, and use their skills to develop, evaluate, or improve approaches, strategies, or systems necessary to implement new Human Resources programs.
