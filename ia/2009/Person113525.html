Jim Wind’s Summary

Senior operating executive, accomplished team-builder, innovative sales network designer, and field-tested turnaround expert with successful track record managing rapid growth businesses and distressed operations in fields of education, consumer products, business services, manufacturing and distribution, publishing, and technology. Background also includes Harvard MBA and core competencies in:

* General Management for organizations ranging from new ventures to $200+ million

* Field Operations management in complex, multi-site service and distribution businesses

* Marketing and Sales management through direct, wholesale, and retail sales channels

* Team-Building/Organizational Development in both large and small companies

* Acquisitions and Operations/Financial Turnarounds for product and service organizations

Recently founded new venture in consumer product/event services field. Previously scaled 1,000-employee, 60-site education organization. Also led profitable 1,300-employee 40-branch sales, service, and distribution business, growing revenue from $100 to $240 million; directed successful turnarounds for consumer product and international operating units; and bootstrapped Internet start-up to acquisition. Throughout career, recognized for recruiting, developing, and retaining talented management teams, and creating highly productive work environments.

Jim Wind’s Specialties:

General management, field operations management, marketing and sales management, operations/financial turnarounds, new ventures, consumer products, business services, educational services, technology, and distribution.
