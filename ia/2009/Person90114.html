Griffin de Luce’s Summary

I've consulted to clients in New York, Hollywood, Tokyo, Dubai, Korea and VR while dividing my time between Silicon Valley (SF), New York and Hollywood (LA.) My passion is helping people use web and mobile technologies to accomplish powerful change, simply and elegantly.

I've worked as advisor and/or executive with startups, corporations, universities and governments around the world including DEC, JP Morgan, IBM, MIT, Time Warner, AOL, American Express, Deloitte, McKinsey & Company, The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times and others.

I've enjoyed forwarding the visions of the heads of Industry (like the Chairman of the SEC & the CEO of MasterCard) as well as broader organizations (like the Writers Guild of America.) I've advised producers, executives and talent in print and broadcast media (BBC, CBS, PBS, Middle East Broadcast Center, NYT, Newsweek, Fortune, Wired & The Wall Street Journal) and founded new forms of media. (As President and CTO of Strike.TV (Hollywood Writers Strike) I helped reinvent the way in which television and film are financed, produced and distributed outside the studio.)

I dedicate much of my time to Environmental rEvolution through my work with green.org, wind.com and solar.com.
