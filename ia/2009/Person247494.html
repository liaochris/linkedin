Bill Ives’s Summary

I have spent the last 15 years consulting businesses on the use and purchase of telecommunications and technology solutions. Over the years I have worked with hundreds of companies that are currently using solutions that we have worked together to build. I have consulted for Government, Educational Institutions both K – 12 and Higher Education, Health Care, Non-Profit, Manufacturing, Law Practices, Real Estate Firms, Property Management, Retail, Hospitality and Restaurant Management, Information Technology Firms and many more.
I have used my ability to explain applications of technology in everyday business language to help provide a simple technology blueprint to meet the goals of all sectors of the Mid-Michigan Business Community.
Though and extensive network of technology service providers in the Great Lakes Area we can provide solutions for a vast majority of the marketplace.
