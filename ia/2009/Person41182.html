Charlene Kingston’s Summary

Senior information designer and project manager creating practical, effective business solutions through business analysis, task/job analysis, information architecture, usability audits, and audience analysis.

Finished projects include print (newsletters, feature articles, manuals, and job aids), online help systems, ebooks, website content, knowledgebase articles, e-learning courses, software and process demonstrations, and blogs.

Experience designing training and documentation responses from RFP requirements for proposals including costs.

Specialist in the design and development of user assistance tools for integrating software into the workplace.

Advocate for social media strategies to help companies maximize the benefits of user community support and feedback.
