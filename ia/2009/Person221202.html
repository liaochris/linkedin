Scott Regan’s Summary

Scott Regan is the managing partner of Regan & Partners, LLC, a privately held healthcare consulting firm specializing in strategic planning, brand management, corporate culture, and operational improvement.

Prior to founding the firm, Scott was the executive vice president of Juran Healthcare, a global consulting firm that assists healthcare organizations with achieving breakthrough improvements in quality. His work spanned two continents, as he assisted healthcare organizations in the United States and United Kingdom reinvent themselves.

Scott also spent nine years at Memorial Health in Savannah, Georgia, including a four-year stint as executive vice president and chief operating officer. During Scott’s tenure there, he was responsible for hospital operations, marketing, strategic planning, and human resources. He was the principle architect of the organization’s corporate culture initiative, which landed Memorial Health on Fortune’s list of “100 Best Companies to Work For” – four years in a row. Scott, who is a Six Sigma Master Black Belt and a certified Lean Sensei, also launched Memorial Health’s Lean Six Sigma quality program, which resulted in the medical center being named by Leapfrog as one of America’s 50 safest hospitals.

Scott has won numerous national awards and has served on the boards of national, regional, and state organizations. He also holds a Master’s of Business Administration and a Master’s of Health Services Administration. He is a nationally recognized motivational speaker and frequently keynotes state and national conferences.
