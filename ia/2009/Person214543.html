M. Shadab Lari’s Summary

Shadab is an MBA Graduate with an engineering background. He has about 10 years of experience and is currently associated with Patni Computers (an IT services company) in KM domain leading the Change Management team.

Prior to this he was associated, for about 5 years, with a Swiss based IT Product Company mainly working as a Product Manager and was driving key European projects. Apart from the Product Management role he was also a part of the global management team in Colayer.

He has been associated with one of the biggest consumer electronics organization in India as the branch head Pune (India) region. He has also worked as a Design Engineer for an engineering company primarily into Valves and Actuators business.

He likes challenges and believes innovation & learning in a part of day to day life. He like to be a change agent, driving continuous improvements in whatever he plans & delivers.
