Steve Cranford’s Summary

As a former trial lawyer I came to respect the power of effective communications in engaging an audience, and made it a career. The journey from state's attorney to corporate law to entrepreneur to brand strategy may seem unconventional, but each attest to the need for effective mass and one-on-one communications.

When all is said and done, I'm a communications strategy guy who is media neutral. One who also enjoys a good game of baseball.

Steve Cranford’s Specialties:

Brand and communications strategy, cross cultural understanding, content development, channel selection and planning, business strategy, governance, international, negotiation, franchising & licensing, and business development.
