Mary Spio’s Summary

A noted digital media and online video expert, entrepreneur and among Essence Magazine’s “Top 15 Women of Power” in 2006, I am one of the Co-Founders of Gen2Media Corporation (OTCBB:GTWO) and currently serve as its President and as a member of the Board of Directors.

On a day-to-day basis, I devote a large portion of my time overseeing our Company’s media sales teams operating in Orlando, Los Angeles and New York City; and advising brands and national advertisers on implementing effective brand-building strategies that leverage Gen2Media’s digital media network, our access to highly targeted affinity groups, and our patent-pending video publishing platform and Smart Content Management System.

I’d like to welcome the LinkedIn community to contact me to discuss all potential business opportunities for Gen2 – particularly those relating to ways we may assist with monetizing valuable video assets and original video content; developing winning interactive ad strategies for online publishers, brands and marketers; leveraging social networks to provide and drive brand affinity; and/or implementing Digital-Out-Of-Home (DOOH) marketing solutions -- also known as in-store video marketing, to access, entertain and optimize in-store traffic.

Mary Spio’s Specialties:

Digital media; online video; ad-supported digital networks; Internet Television; brand-building; digital advertising and online marketing; media sales; video publishing; video production and video post-production; mobile video applications

  • Satellite Applications Engineer

    (Defense & Space industry)

    2002 — 2004 (2 years)

    A former deep space rocket scientist (literally), I began my career designing, launching and operating communications satellites for PanAmSat before moving to Boeing. This disruptive digital innovation has been used to redefine how major motion pictures are distributed to movie theatres worldwide by way of satellite transmissions. High grossing pictures such as Star Wars: Episode II – Attack of the Clones; Ocean’s 11; Planet of the Apes; Spy Kids and Monsters, Inc. are among numerous that have been digitally delivered to big screens everywhere using technology I helped to pioneer.
