Steve Jones’s Summary

A certified Lean/Six-Sigma Black Belt and professional Engineer / Manager, with over 30 years of experience in industry, including 18 years in various leadership roles.

An accomplished Systems Design and Integration Engineer, with a deep understanding of the principles, concepts, tools, and real-world challenges of planning, performing, directing and facilitating Total Quality Management (TQM) programs. Engaged in a career of continuing self improvement, through self-study, formal training, and hands-on experience.

A creative problem solver; self-directed, versatile, articulate, and highly flexible. A dedicated “team player”. Equally effective as Lead Engineer, Engineering Manager, Trainer, Coach, or Consultant.

Process modelling; business modelling; facilitation of Kaizen & Value-Stream Mapping; Problem/Root-cause analysis; mistake-proofing / FMEA; Requirements Elicitation, Analysis and Development; Program Management; Strategic Planning; Balanced Scorecard (BSC)
