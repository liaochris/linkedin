Scott Winterroth’s Summary

My public relations experience is a mix of both agency public relations and corporate communications with expertise in the thoughtful planning and execution of public relations and communication campaigns. Some of my working areas include travel and tourism, government relations, healthcare, telecommunications, arts and culture, non-profit and real estate.

Most recently, my entrepreneurial spirit and passion for the musical arts led me to create CountryMusicChicago.com, a one-stop news and information resource for all-things country music in Chicago. Thanks to my expertise in journalism, marketing, design and photography, the Web site is quickly becoming Chicago’s premier online resource for country music artist and event news and information.
