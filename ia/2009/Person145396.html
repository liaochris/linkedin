Luke Morris’s Summary

WHO WE ARE

RAPIER Systems Provides Enterprise Clients and Local Governments with Specialised End-to-End Networking and Wireless Telecommunications Solutions.

Rapier understands that deploying a carrier-class network involves more than just choosing the right infrastructure components. It requires an on-going knowledge of technology advances, and an unwavering dedication to network planning, maintenance, and troubleshooting.

We also understand that network deployment is not your core business – it’s ours. Your network is critical to the smooth operation of your business, but it shouldn’t distract you from focusing on your core business objectives.

As such, it is our objective to simplify and streamline the process of planning, implementing and maintaining your network infrastructure by offering unparalleled technical knowledge and service. Everything to enable you to evolve your business safely, knowing your mission-critical operations are being handled with care by the right experts.

Staying up to date on technology evolution and constantly offering innovative, best-in-breed solutions to support and maximize network efficiency is our main concern. RAPIER’s proven expertise and ability to survey, plan, design, deploy, maintain, manage, support, integrate and optimise communication networks is complemented by strong partnerships with premium network product manufacturers.
