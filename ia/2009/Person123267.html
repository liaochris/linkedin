Judy Olsen’s Summary

Believes in: comparative advantage. Don't write your own copy before working out the full cost of your own time (salary + 40-60%) Multiply that by a factor which represents the value you add to your business (be modest). Add the potential revenue lost by delaying your website/brochure while you try to fit in the extra work. Now offer this money to a freelance copywriter.

Loves: making dull subjects sparkle; writing dialogue; semi-colons; leaving lots of lovely white space.

Is slightly obsessed by: grammar. Not in a finger-wagging "Eats Pandas and Shoots Up" kind of way. In a communicating effectively kind of way.

Is irritated by: copywriters who don't know where to put an apostrophe or a capital letter. Would you hire a chef who couldn't make toast?

Hates: SEO copy by UK SEO writers who SEO every few SEO words with UK SEO keywords so SEO copy with UK SEO gets a million UK SEO hits and worldwide SEO writers like the SEO UK copy and UK SEO keywords for SEO in the UK. Still here?

SEO copy that markets your product and adds value to your brand.
