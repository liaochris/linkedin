Eric Vogel’s Summary

I started out assisting students in the main general computer at the college I went too, along with some break/fix of the computers (PC's & Mac's). I then moved to a 3 month temporary job at a third party automotive supplier while the System Administrator was out. When she came back, I was asked to stay on longer, which turned out to be a full year of employment. I then started at a small employee benefits firm as a contractor for PC / Server support, maintenance, finding new technology to make the company run better. I also worked three months in that time frame for a contracting firm that contracted me to EDS, who then contracted me to GM to backup and upgrade salesmen laptops. Then I was asked to come work full time at a small employee benefits firm adding on administrative assistant work along with using my technical skills. I then started up my own computer repair business in 2007. I now am in Guest Relations at my current employer having alot of fun assisting the guests, and being able to have butterflies and hummingbirds around me for part of my day. Computer Security is very important and will always be in demand and I decided to go back to college for a Cyber Security Certificate.
