Paul O'Brien’s Summary

A search, online marketing, web design and ecommerce professional with over 15 years of experience on the Internet and a demonstrated leader in online commerce, marketing, and Search (optimization and paid) with relationships and skills in both B2B and B2C markets. Proven ability to generate revenue through win/win negotiating and solution based strategies. Solid project and team management skills with the ability to plan strategically and execute tactically. A skilled web designer with user experience insight, technical know how, and best practices forethought. Strong organizational, interpersonal and effective communication skills.

Online Marketing – Expertise in all aspects of online advertising, Search engine management (SEO & SEM expertise), Comparison Shopping, Banner Advertising, and Affiliate Marketing. Advanced experience with success in portfolio marketing, behavioral/Geo/Demo targeting, and purchase process/cycle optimization. Strategic ability to leverage technology and relationships to create scalable marketing programs with high ROI.

Leadership & Management – Respected leader and peer with successful track record of building winning teams. Strong ability to motivate people to exceed company goals. Personal interest in and ownership of team’s development and training.

Communication – Persuasive and confident communicator with well-developed presentation and people skills. Highly regarded by professional and personal peers as an exceptional negotiator and deal maker.
