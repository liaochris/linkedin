Tullio Siragusa’s Summary

I am a 19 years veteran of the Media (search, publishing, web/mobile and social networks), Software (product and services), Consulting (IT, M&A, Process Engineering, Professional Services), and Telecom (Data and Voice) industries.

My expertise is in developing execution plans that make vision and ideas marketable and valuable in their respective marketplaces.

My most powerful strength comes from the use of well guarded industry advisor secrets for setting strategy into action, from developing balanced score cards across the organization to orchestrating a well balanced approach to achieving corporate objectives, and developing action plans that pack week’s worth of work into days.

Past advisory clients include: Viacom, Disney, NBC Universal, MSN, Facebook, JP Morgan Chase, Citigroup, Bank of America, Dow Jones, Zurich Financial, McKinsey & Co, and GE. Advisory provided to the C level executive suite.

My background includes assignments at the CEO level of SEM/SEO expert Ansearch Ltd. (publicly traded on ASX), Sr. Global Vice President of Operative Media. (Edison Ventures), and Director and Partner roles with Softtek / Ernst & Young, Computer Sciences Corporation and PricewaterhouseCoopers.

Recently founded and launched (http://m3mob.com) a self publishing platform for entrepreneurs and organizations who are looking to expand their contact base, communicate with (and build) their community and allow their audience to find and meet each other.
