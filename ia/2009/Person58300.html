David Michaels’s Summary

At age 19, I founded Tudec Entertainment, my very first business venture. We produced original multimedia semi-animated comic books for the web -- years ahead of its time. I hired and contracted freelancers for the artwork, writing, and voice acting. We appeared at conventions and built a loyal following of fans and artists.

Later, after the dotcom burst of 2000, I restructured the business to meet with the changing marketplace. In less than a year using the new business model, I turned a profit and had a growing audience of more than 10,000 unique monthly visitors without spending a penny on advertising.

Simultaneously, I built a more spiritually-focused for-profit business called Spiritual Smarts. Through my own self-improvement book, inspirational blog, podcast, audio programs, and live events, I do my part to touch lives, inspire the greatness within each one of us, and give people the tools, information, insights, and strategies to empower themselves to live life at the fullest potential.

I am also the co-host of the weekly online radio show, The Small Business Podcast, covering topics and tips for new entrepreneurs in a simple, easy, and entertaining format. In the summer of 2009, we were featured as the top recommended podcast for small business owners in Business Weekly's SmallBiz magazine.
