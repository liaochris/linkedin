Phil Karl’s Summary

Phil Karl is a NYC based web enthusiast and visionary who operates various web properties spread across major core verticals. These web properties combined attract upwards of 2MM visitors (As of June 2008).

Phil Karl is recognized for his role as the Founder and CEO of DocShare, a social document sharing community. DocShare aims to simplify the way documents are shared online through its unique web document platform. Phil Karl is involved with the overall development, scalability, and user experience design.
