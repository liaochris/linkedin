David Sparrgrove’s Summary

Ah ... where to begin. In 1990 David graduated from Rhode Island School of Design with a passion for design burning in his veins. With only a strong sense of design, work ethic, and a Mac, David landed a job of a lifetime at Pittard Sullivan in Hollywood, CA. That was that. From 1990 forward, David has worked on hundreds of projects for every conceivable client in the entertainment industry. David was on the bleeding edge of desktop based animation, using the Macintosh for full blown animation as early as 1992. HIs most recent tenure was working with David Hwang at V12 for 8 years, where he helped grow the company to a recognized leader in the Broadcast arena. HIs most notable contributions during this time were a number of high end sports packages for ESPN. This included an emmy nomination for the X Games package of 2000. In 2006 David left V12 and joined Concrete Pictures, heading up the Creative in the Los Angeles office. Packages for Fox Sports Net, DirecTV and the Military Channel have been keeping him busy. There is much more to describe of David's career, but I would recommend just getting in touch with him and letting him talk your ear off!

David Sparrgrove’s Specialties:

David is especially adept at making something out of nothing, and making the best of every project and situation. He has strong, enthusiastic pitching skills, and a very high level of passion for his work. Design skills, hell yeah. Strong animation skills, too, in both After Effects and Cinema 4D. Loves Photoshop, Illustratrator, and can make a mean pot of coffee. David has shown over the years that he plays well with others, and is a strong collaborator.
