Jacek Brzezinski’s Summary

Currently I'm development director at CD Projekt Red. I'm responsible for the design, technology and production of our future PC and console game titles.

Recently I was project lead of The Witcher (www.thewitcher.com) -- a great role-playing game for PC, published worldwide by Atari, CD Projekt and Noviy Disk in 2007. Game won so far numerous awards listed here: http://www.thewitcher.com/community/en/awards/

Computer games I've worked on:
* The Witcher Enhanced Edition (PC, CD Projekt Red/Atari/CD Projekt/Noviy Disk, 2008)
* The Witcher (PC, CD Projekt Red/Atari/CD Projekt/Noviy Disk, 2008)
* Dreamwalker (PC, Metropolis Software, cancelled)
* Gorky Zero: Beyond Honor (PC, Metropolis Software/JoWood, 2003)
* Bloodline (PC, Metropolis Software, cancelled)
* Archangel (PC, Metropolis Software/JoWood, 2002)
* Two Worlds (PC, Metropolis Software, cancelled).

My pen&paper RPGs, published in Poland:
* Strefa śmierci (with Tomasz Kołodziejczak)
* Oko Yrrhedesa (with Andrzej Sapkowski, Andrzej Miszkurka)

Some other publications:
* Le demon du frigo (with Irek Konior) - graphic novel, published in Belgium & France).
* numerous articles and adventures for RPG games, published in Polish and Czech pen&paper-RPG magazines.

I've also translated many RPG books/articles and CCGs to Polish.

Also published some articles on AI (multi-agent systems).

Jacek Brzezinski’s Specialties:

game development, game design, interactive storytelling, role-playing games, computer and console games, project management, team management, AI, multiagent systems, engine & tools programming, software design, editing, desktop publishing, translation (English to Polish), writing (mostly RPG stuff), DTP & graphic design.
