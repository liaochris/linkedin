Laurent Pacalin’s Summary

As an accomplished business executive, both with start-ups and established companies, I bring a broad range of experience in the creation and execution of innovative product and go-to-market strategies. I have collaborated with, as well as, managed teams of diverse cultures for over 20 years. I am an accomplished listener and effective problem solver, skilled at diffusing conflict, gaining consensus, and helping individuals deliver timely results.
