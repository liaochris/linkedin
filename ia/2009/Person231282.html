Norma Richardson’s Summary

Elementary classroom teacher-experience went beyond working with children. Throughout the years, I developed programs that included adults within the community. The outdoor learning experience for sixth graders also included developing parent leader training for the outdoor modules. As a Staff Development Leader and a Computer Technology Leader, I worked closely with the district staff, as well as administrators and school board members. The after-school computer club that I established included parents wanting to gain experience with computers. At the Challenger Learning Center, as an Assistant Flight Director, I guided students through a successful mission as well as facilitated corporate groups using the simulation for team building within their organization.

My passion has always been to get the right information to my 'students' so that they can make the best choices for themselves. I believe every person has the potential and opportunity for wellness. Wellness is something that comes from you, not to you. That means that it must be cultivated and nurtured.

The word wellness has a different meaning to each of us. My goal as a certified health counselor is to:
•Inspire you to create your own definition of wellness.
•Encourage you by discussing your health history.
•Assist you in setting goals for attaining your desired level of health and well-being.
•Educate you in designing a realistic nutrition program to achieve your goals.
•Support you in making adjustments to your nutrition program based on progress, goals and unique needs.

For long-term success, nutrition programs must include a combination of education and lifestyle modification as well as inspiration and support. Wellness truly is a process. Engaging in that process and following through is what creates the most desirable results.
