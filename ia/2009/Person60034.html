Dean Kennedy’s Experience

  • DMK Business Systems Pty Ltd

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    November 1998 — Present (10 years 5 months)

    DMK provides effective local marketing, design, copywriting, printing and public relations services to a range of small businesses, entrepreneurs and non-profit organisations.

    I run the company with my wife Mel as Office Manager and a part time employee in a graphic design and marketing role.

    Whilst our work ranges across a range of industries, we are also an approved marketing contractor to the Harvey World Travel network throughout Australia, and provide direct local marketing services to around 160 individual shops around the country, as well as indirectly assisting all shops through key projects via the group's head office and state Business Development Managers.

    Given my background as a persuasive writer (persuasive political writing and guerilla-style marketing, business writing and public relations), much of the work we do is based on proven direct response marketing skills and techniques.

  • Parliament of Victoria

    (Legislative Office industry)

    October 1992 — November 1998 (6 years 2 months)

    Electorate officer primarily to Bernie Finn MP, Member for Tullamarine and also part-time for 12 months (1995) to Ian Davis MP, Member for Essendon. Political administration role and electorate communication/marketing duties. Created one of the first websites for a Member of Parliament in Victoria (created in late 1995, launched after the 1996 election). Active, key role in both the 1992 and 1996 campaigns in Tullamarine.
