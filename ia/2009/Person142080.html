Lisa Kaye’s Experience

  • Founder and CEO

    greenlightjobs

    (Internet industry)

    September 2002 — Present (6 years 8 months)

    Founded niche online recruitment career site to aid professionals, creative and technical talent in the entertainment and digital media industry with job opportunities, job postings, resume & portfolio review and career services to job seekers. Employers access a pre-screened, pre-interviewed candidate database of over 40,000 applicants. Technology driven resource, greenlightjobs also powers career sites such as www.livenationcareers.com and House of Blues. In addition to online recruitment services, Lisa Kaye offers consulting and premium search services exclusively to her clients.

    House of Blues Entertainment, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Entertainment industry)

    January 2007 — October 2008 (1 year 10 months)

    Served as Chief HR advisor for M&A of House Of Blues branded clubs by Live Nation. Managed the post-integration of the purchase of House Of Blues in 2007 by Live Nation. Managed the integration of staffing for nearly 3000 employees, employee benefits, contracts, plans, compensation and policies and procedures. Handled the integration of the business operations into Live Nation in a seamless transition post merger.

  • Chief HR Advisor

    Live Nation

    (Public Company; LYV; Entertainment industry)

    December 2005 — October 2008 (2 years 11 months)

    Served as Chief HR advisor for newly created spin-co Live Nation formerly, Clear Channel Entertainment. Advised CEO and senior management team on organizational, compensation and staffing issues. Handled M&A for company including Sports, Theatrical and Motorsports divisions. Managed complex strategic negotiations on behalf of senior management. Built HR function and managed daily operation of human resources for public company of over 6000 full time employees and another thousands of seasonal employees worldwide. Implemented polices, practices, staffing procedures and served as senior HR and business advisor with ongoing organizational issues.

  • Interim Head of HR

    Pixar Animation Studios

    (Public Company; Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    January 2004 — June 2004 (6 months)

Handled the daily oversight of function for nearly 1000 full time employees. Worked closely with recruitment team for Apple, Inc. who managed the company to seek and fill full time Human Resource leader for organization.
