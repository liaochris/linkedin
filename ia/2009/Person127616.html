Kathleen Marinaccio’s Summary

Kathleen has over 17 years of graphic design, printing and teaching experience.

A graduate with honors from Pratt Institute with a BFA in Communication Design, she has worked as a Graphic Designer at HarperCollins Publishers and Marvel Entertainment; Creative Director at the Lotus Group, New World Entertainment, and Applied Graphics Technology; and is currently Senior Partner at fishbrain and a Professor of Graphic Design and Computer Graphics at Otis College of Art and Design, and an Instructor of Photoshop and Illustrator at Knowledge Works Training Center.

Kathleen specializes in packaging, promotions, and corporate identity for the all industries, she also loves lettering comics and designing websites.

Kathleen Marinaccio’s Specialties:

Fishbrain is an award winning creative firm specializing in brand direction,
product development and promotion design, producing a wide array of
off-and-online materials for a distinct collection of the world’s most
well-regarded companies and charitable organizations. Owned and operated by Kathleen Marinaccio and Dana Moreshead, two Pratt graduates with 35+ years combined experience, Fishbrain’s LA based creative team is bolstered by a diverse network of writers, designers and illustrators.
