Alexander Bender’s Summary

I am a management consultant with more than 10 years of consulting experience, helping clients make dramatic changes implementing new processes, business approaches, and IT solutions. For example, I executed a strategic sourcing program for contract labor for a $6B BPO/ITO company. I led the analysis team during the selection process (RFP, auction, negotiations) to achieve $35M in yearly run-rate savings, an 18% cost reduction. After completion of the program, I stayed on as interim procurement manager to implement savings across all business units, including execution of large project RFP’s, and realized $25M of savings in the first year.

My functional experience spans strategic sourcing; (service) supply chain management; portfolio, pipeline, and program management; change management, IT strategy; and innovation management. I am part of PRTM’s Strategic Sourcing group. In addition to many large and medium-sized North American clients, I have served clients in Germany, the U.K., Finland, and Latin America.

I hold a Ph.D. in business administration from the University of Kiel and was a scholar of the German Science Foundation. In my studies, I concentrated on managing innovations and optimizing R&D budgets. I also hold an MBA and a master’s of science in economics.

My industry experience includes Electronics (incl. EMS), Telecommunications, Consumer Packaged Goods, IT Services, Medical Devices, High-Tech, Software, Retail, Chemicals, Financial Services, and Automotive.
