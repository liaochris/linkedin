Manoli Giuliano’s Summary

Our Organisation enters the market in the year 1991 to support the increasing demand in the promotional business coming from the big commercial companies.
Our team looks for economically feasible solutions for the companies supporting , at the same time, products with an interesting market value.

Our clients have also the choice if to buy the goods directly from the maker, through our trading company in Hong Kong, of else to buy the product once arrived in the European market already cleared by the customs by our company.

Our range of clients can be illustrated as follows :

Mail order companies
Editors
Banks, financial and insurance agencies
Public boards
Wholesale distribution companies
Foodstuffs companies
Radio and television networks
Retail companies
Famous “griffes”
Petrol companies
Marketing and advertisement agencies
Motor industries
Hotel chains
Holiday and travel companies
Sailing fleets

All our products are tested with severe controls carried out by qualified laboratories in order to obtain items that completely fulfil the new European laws in matter of security of the final consumer much more than in the past.

Our goal remains the same: invest in the research of the promotional items.
We are currently producing for patentees holding licences for bags and accessories of Worldwide designers.

Since 2001 we also offer the following services:
avdertising, visual identity, brand & product image, corporate identity, total look, launch and planning of advertising campaigns, communication strategies, direct marketing, packaging, design, building events and fairs stands, show room and windows, selling strategies, publishing, visual merchandising
