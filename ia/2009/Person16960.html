Anne Bain’s Summary

The travel industry has been part of my life for the past 12 years and I have owned a travel business since 2004. The travel group consists of 2 shopfronts Jetset Cleveland and Jetset Jimboomba and an online travel site www.ourgenerationtravel.com.

Both shop front business' have received many state sales and customer service awards over the past 2 years and in 2008 Jetset Cleveland was recognised with a national award from the Australian Business Womens Network for 'Outstanding Business Growth' in 2007 and as the 2007/2008 Jetset Office of the Year.

Now launched the new website http://www.ExperienceSeekersTravelClub.com especially for people who are seekers of experiences, it will be a community where members can learn, develop, give and travel and really experience life to its fullest! Members can have their own page on the site where they can promote their business and we will eventually have an interactive forum and live chat. AND, all members get the chance to come travelling with us on our especially designed expriential programs - 1st one off the blocks will be in October 2009 travelling the 'Mother Road' - Route 66!

Anne Bain’s Specialties:

No matter where in the world it is that people wish to travel, the Gran Emeral Travel Group provides tailor made itineraries and a full service consultancy, before, during and after.
The Experience Seekers Travel Club will offer personally escorted experiential touring, with the first tour travelling Route 66 in October 09
