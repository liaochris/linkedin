Tina Baker’s Summary

I am a partner in the London office of Brown Rudnick, an international law firm, and am the co-head of the European Venture Capital Practice along with Mark Dorff. I represent venture funds and emerging technology companies on financings, exits and commercial transactions, many of which are cross border. I am a US and UK qualified lawyer. I have served on various committees and task forces of the European Venture Capital Association (EVCA), including EVCA’s Professional Standards Committee and the Code of Ethics Working Group. Prior to embarking on my legal career, I was a recording artist and songwriter (see website for a full history: www.tinab.tv) and I currently perform opera in concert on a regular basis at a local London venue.
