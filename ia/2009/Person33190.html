Brian Courtney’s Summary

I have 19 years of experience working in the software industry. I have a well rounded background having worked in Operations, R&D, Marketing, Sales, and Global Service Delivery. I founded a company in the early 90’s and then consulted for 10 years. Most recently I'm the General Manager for CTE's, North American Governement Solutions division which focuses on innovational IT solutions for the Department of Defense and the US Federal Government. I have a BS and MS in computer science, and an MBA from MIT (SF05).
