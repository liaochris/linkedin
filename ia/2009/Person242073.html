Rob Finn’s Summary

I was previously with GTSI, a government technology contractor. I was employee #2 in a Business Process Change Department; you could refer to it as intrapreneur. I was part of a team responsible for updating business processes for a corporate wide rollout of Peoplesoft ERP, CRM, SCM. Additionally, I worked on six sigma projects in HR, Warehouse, Contracting, Supply Chain, Order Fulfillment, Purchasing, Finance, Product Management, and Sales. I documented processes and developed financial controls for all departments while working as sole business analyst alongside team of consultants on a six month SOX project.

I have held various roles at Kozmo.com, Campus24.com, Leerink Swann Retail Brokerage group, Helios Trading Group (CBOT) and MIT's Laboratory of Financial Engineering.
