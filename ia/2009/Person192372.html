Rahul Dewan’s Summary

Rahul has been heading Srijan and giving direction to it over the last 9 years. The tryst with open source was a chance event, which has transformed the company from a "we-do-anything" business to one of India's leading "open source consulting" firm.

We now specialise in designing and building content management solutions and intranet/internet business applications using open source software such as TYPO3, Drupal, Symfony, Seagull, Ruby on Rails, Django/Python, others.

Goals for Srijan
---------
- Establish Srijan as a globally recognised open source consulting company
- Establish Srijan's brand to be associated with "value oriented" leadership and vision
- Diversify Srijan into SaaS (ASP/hosted) products covering a wide variety of 'industry verticals' such as Tourism, Non Profits, and for several 'domain verticals' such as Project Management, Business Communication Management
- Gain global presence for Srijan specifically in Europe and North America
- Diversify Srijan into business areas such as Renewable Energy, Micro-finance, Organic Agriculture and similar areas of socio-economic importance

Personal Goals
--------
- To become a social entrepreneur creating businesses to uplift the social and economic status of rural India
