Andrew Hennessy’s Summary

With over 10 years of software packaging experience and additional 5 years of 3rd level technical support has provided a great platform and wealth of knowledge with global experience.

My goal is to grow our scalable Zaptz (www.zaptz.com) packaging business which I founded in 2005 to be a preferred global supplier of software packaging and software virtualization solutions. We live by our mission statement "Automating software installs across the earth, from your desk".

My wealth of experience is vast providing remote packaging, consultancy and training for blue chip companies directly via Zaptz or on behalf of Symantec Australasia. I worked in London in 2001 being one of the original 5 packagers at Camwood (www.camwood.com) soon becoming a technical lead responsible for all onsite packaging for their clients throughout the UK.

Whilst in London (2000-2003) I also gained valuable technical support experience. For example I managed, racked and implemented 220 Linux servers for a new American Online data centre. Worked at various "secret squirrel" security MOD sites, including 10 Downing Street implementing IBM server racks and creating and automating all the required server images from Linux, Citrix, Novell and Windows Server platforms. Looked after the Toussards group (example London Eye, Madame Tussauds) infrastructure and providing 3rd level support for Microsoft platforms, Citrix and Novell.

Recent Zaptz experience includes securing, managing and providing remote packaging and virtualization services for National Australia Bank, BHP Billiton, Incitec Pivot and Symantec clients with our Zaptz team.
