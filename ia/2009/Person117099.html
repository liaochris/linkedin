John M. Frymire’s Summary

As a Financial Advisor with Alexander Investment Services, Inc. (AIS), I help my clients build and preserve wealth. My work includes investment consulting, financial and retirement planning, estate planning-related services, life insurance and long-term care consulting services. Alexander is a venerable local firm founded in Louisville in 1965. Although strong in many areas, we excel in setting up and managing retirement plans for businesses.

I'm a Certified Financial Planner(tm) practitioner, having met rigorous experience and ethical requirements, having successfully completed financial planning coursework and passed the CFP® Certfication Examination covering the financial planning process, risk management, investments, tax planning and management, retirement and employee benefits, and estate planning. CFP® certificants must also agree to meet ongoing continuing education requirements and uphold the CFP Board's Code of Ethics and Professional Responsibility and Financial Planning Practice Standards.

I'm a Chartered Financial Consultant (ChFC) and a Chartered Life Underwriter (CLU) as designated by the American College, Bryn Mawr, PA, having fulfilled educational, experience and ethical requirements. The American College is the nation's leading educator of professionals in the insurance and financial services industry. I'm registered with the National Association of Securities Dealers (Series 7, 31, 63 and 65) and am a licensed life and health insurance agent.

Investments and services are offered through TD Ameritrade Institutional, member SIPC.

Certified Financial Planner Board of Standards Inc. owns the certification marks CFP®, CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER(tm), and the federally registered CFP (with flame logo) in the U.S., which it awards to individuals who successfully complete CFP Board's initial and ongoing certification requirements.
