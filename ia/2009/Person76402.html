Eugene Gibbons’s Summary

Stelfox is a specialist recruitment company with offices in Ireland and United Kingdom. We specialise in the IT, Sales, Pharmaceutical and Engineering sectors.

My role is to cultivate a relationship with both client and candidate based on professionalism, speed and accuracy using "outside the Box" recruitment solutions (Web 2.0, Network mapping, Business referrals etc) rather then traditional job boards.
