Poj Netsiri, Ph.D.’s Summary

Electrical Engineer and Research Scientist constantly providing genius solutions for several projects. Particularly interested in digital signal processing, image processing, machine vision, and automation. Trained in Tokyo and Cambridge. Samples of my development are: Real-time wavelet signal processing, real-time timing recovery, real-time data compression, PPM decoder, timing server, digital input output, independent component analysis, RF coils for MRI, fully automated PLC systems, machine controls for defense contractor. I am always interested in a challenging project.
