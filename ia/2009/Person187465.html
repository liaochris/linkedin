Robin Greaves’s Summary

My consultancy provides strategic coaching for individuals and organisations to achieve transformational change.

On Linkedin I have founded two professional discussion groups: "Psychosocial Mechanics", and "Changing Healthcare and Support".

I am a Member of the European Mentoring and Coaching Council, an Associate of the Coaching Academy and a qualified facilitator. I have acted in mediations in both the commercial and voluntary sectors, and in neighbourhood disputes. I am also a member of the Association for Project Management.

Before leaving for Finland and setting up Phoenix Change Consultancy, I was an Associate Director at Cyril Sweett in London, England.

My career has centred around building, developing and managing partnerships. Latterly these have been, at individual, team and organisational levels, between: inner-city communities; Local Government; Regional Health Authorities; the not-for-profit sector; and the private sector.

As part of this work I have also devised and managed projects in software and management system integration, both for local government and the private sector. I developed one of the first Early Excellence Centres in the UK for under 5's, and managed the formulation of some of the first hospital and grouped schools PFI projects in the UK.
