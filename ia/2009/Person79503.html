Dave Cropley’s Summary

Dave Cropley, the Creative Director of Evans Finch Limited, is a classically trained traditional 2D animator specialising in high quality 'digital 2D' Flash character animation and digital image manipulation. He is also an independent film-maker creating and releasing animated shorts as Fishboyfilms. With over 10 years in the industry he has experience in all aspects of production from script, storyboarding and visualisation through to editing and post-production work for live action, broadcast and web.

Dave Cropley’s Specialties:

10 years of experience in the animation industry as a 2D Character animator.
