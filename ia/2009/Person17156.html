Anne Orchard [LION]’s Summary

Over 2 million people in the UK are living with or after a cancer diagnosis. The hidden story is their family and friends, who are also affected, but often get little help. I am the founder of Families Facing Cancer, an online support organisation, and the only one focusing entirely on those in the Cancer Fallout Zone - families and friends who are also affected. We offer practical and emotional support to anyone who is affected in any way by someone else's cancer. By supporting the supporters in this way we help them to stay well for their own sake, and also so they can best support the person with the illness.
I am happy to connect with anyone else working with people affected with cancer in either a professional or voluntary capacity
