Tom Murrah’s Summary

More than 25 solid years of service experience within both military settings and industrial organizations requiring strong supervisory, technical and organizational skills. Team player with a commitment to high ethical standards and a proven track record of recognizing needs, developing solutions and implementing them. Highly interested in molding a team of experienced Technical professionals into a cohesive unit that specializes in correct answers, exceeding the customers expectations and going the 'extra mile'. Very analytical, detailed and committed to excellence. Above average written communication skills.

Tom Murrah’s Specialties:

Machinery troubleshooting and problem diagnosis; engineering liaison; experienced with OSHA, NEC, ANSI requirements for machinery; editing and publishing service newsletters; writing Technical manuals; creating an industry presence on internet forums; presenting Technical Training classes; creating emergency response teams; safety consultant.
