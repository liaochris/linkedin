Chad Holmes’s Summary

Twenty-five years experience analyzing communication needs, planning communication strategies and tactics, and managing the effective execution of strategic communication plans. Extensive experience in small-team leadership, military public affairs, and project management. Trained senior military officers to work effectively with the media and prepared high visibility officials for media interviews, news conferences, and briefings. Effective working individually and in teams to develop award-winning print, broadcast, and Web-based communication products. Entrepreneurial management style and strong team-building skills have consistently improved the communication programs at a wide range of organizations.
