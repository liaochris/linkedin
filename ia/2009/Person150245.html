Marian Rich’s Summary

Multi-talented human resource/recruiting professional with experience in the corporate and not-for-profit sector. Keen ability to learn quickly and develop in-depth understanding of the inner workings of companies and their corporate cultures. Excellent interpersonal skills with a strong commitment to learning and development. Creative and resourceful at implementing new initiatives; experienced at sales, marketing, and new business development. Solid track record in achieving goals and objectives.

Long time builder, fundraiser, community organizer and volunteer with the All Stars Project, a non-partisan, non-profit 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to promoting human development through the use of an innovative performance-based model. The ASP creates outside of school, educational and performing arts activities for thousands of poor and minority young people. Co-founder and performer at the Castillo Theatre, headquartered at the All Stars' arts and education center on 42nd Street in New York City.
