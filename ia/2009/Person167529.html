Molly Lopez, CAE’s Summary

Owner and President of Association Management, Ltd. (AML). Our company's vision is to be recognized as THE preferred association management company with Midwestern values, state-of-the-art-technology, association management experience, and a human approach. Our mission is to manage the business of associations. We provide our association clients with professional, personal customer service, experience and access to resources, and to support them as they strive to achieve their strategic goals.

Molly Lopez, CAE’s Specialties:

Since 1976, AML has provided management for international, national, regional and state trade and professional associations. Full-service management; meeting planning; financial management; publications; membership management; Web site maintenance; member services; convention management; government affairs assistance and certification management. AML serves nine associations with 5,000+ members from 57 countries. The company's personnel of eight have 100+ years of association experience.
