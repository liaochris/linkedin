Zheng Zhu’s Summary

RFID is supposed to be one of the revolutionary technologies of this century. I believe in this and prepared for this since my first job in the year 2000. Equiped with knowledge and experience, pumped by the image of wide adoption of RFID, hand in hand with partners, we started Quanray Electronics 2 years ago. I treated the business as one of my children (I am a father of 2 kids, one girl and one boy) and will try my best to get them well developed and finally reach their life summits.
