Ali Musa Nasution’s Summary

Ali had been working as Managing Consultant with Axon Solutions Sdn Bhd in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, from Nov 2005 until Oct 2008. He acquired more than ten years of experience in analysing, designing and configuring of the SAP-SD and SAP-LE Module. He also acquired configuration skills in all areas of SD module including the integration with other modules i.e. MM, PP, FICO, PS and CS. Involved in seven SAP Full Implementation Projects and has extensive exposure in various industries i.e. Textile, Chemical, Service, Manufacturing, Rental, Heavy Equipment, Steel, Distribution, and Food & Beverage/FMCG.

His responsibility including as the Functional Lead for the Logistic Team, supporting various clients, e.g., Britvic, Bespak, Textron, The AA, Lafarge, NRS, KPMG, and Hitachi.

As a seasoned SD Consultant and also SAP Certified Consultant in the SD module of SAP R/3 system, he acquired strong skills in ABAP/4 and Query. Developed varies of routines (copy routines, pricing routines, etc.), user exits, field exits, and customer exits.

He has strong knowledge in other Logistic Modules i.e. Material Management (MM), Plant Maintenance (PM) and Customer Service (CS), also having strong skills in Project Management, and certified for ASAP Methodology.
As the Managing Consultant, responsible to strengthen the relationship with the client, assess the opportunity and create improvement project for the existing clients. Ali is also working together with Business Development in pre-sales activities in order to win a new project.

In addition to his consulting skills, he also acquired more than eight years of experience in Sales, Marketing and General Management; he was appointed as Marketing Manager in a multinational company, managed 2nd biggest brand of beer in Indonesia (brand: Anker Bir) with more than 40 % market share, and was appointed as Operation Director in a local distribution company.
