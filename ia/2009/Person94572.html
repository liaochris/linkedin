Naftalie Hershler’s Summary

Naftalie Hershler is a serial entrepreneur in sustainable ventures, both for profit and not for profit. He is senior consultant at Oranjeveld Partners, a management consultancy and investment company. Previously, Naftalie founded Noterik, the leading European online video company. Naftalie is driven by vision, passion, creativity and (social) entrepreneurship. He is an expert in the fields of technological innovation and digital media, being professional active in these fields for more than fifteen years.

Naftalie is very experienced in developing new means of distant learning, digital training and e-learning. He developed several renowned e-learning products which are used by thousands of students worldwide. Naftalie is leading development in the field of V-Learning, learning with video.

He is frequently appointed as a consultant or manager within projects which require IT related communication strategies, knowledge management and organizational change. Naftalie is a very experienced coach and trainer. He's inspiring and motivating people and organizations to excel and use the maximum of their capabilities.

One important focus field is culture identity and storytelling, both for individuals and organizations. What is the identity and story of a person or company? Answering that question can help improve performance and satisfaction.

Within the Levende Herinneringen ('Living Memories') Foundation Naftalie specializes in audiovisual cultural heritage and oral history. Naftalie is one of the co-founders of Radio Benevolencija, a NGO specialized in conflict prevention. He founded joods.nl, an online community and magazine about jewish culture. Naftalie studied law at the University of Amsterdam and speaks fluently English, Dutch and Hebrew. Naftalie lives partly in Amsterdam, the Netherlands and mainly in Modi'in, Israel.

  • Co-founder

    Radio La Benevolencija / Humanitarian Tools Foundation

    (Civic & Social Organization industry)

    January 2000 — January 2005 (5 years 1 month)

    Naftalie is one of the co-founders of Radio La Benevolencija / Humanitarian Tools Foundation.
    Radio Benevolencija is a NGO devoted to helping people who have suffered from ethnic violence. Extensive academic research has proven that ethnic conflict and group violence always take place according to specific patterns. Radio Benevolencija develops media campaigns to make people aware of these patterns. The media broadcasts and grassroots activities raise awareness about the dangers of political propaganda, group pressure and the patterns that lead to group violence. The activities aims to contribute to reconciliation and the prevention of future ethnic violence . The program is designed under the guidance of academic experts from the University of Massachussetts, the Trauma Research Education and Training Institute (TREATI) and Yale University. Activities are based on academic research into the origins and prevention of genocide, as well as a community-based approach to healing. Currently Radio La Benevolencija is working in Rwanda, Congo and Burundi.

    http://www.labenevolencija.org
