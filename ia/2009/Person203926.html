Rose Bracamonte’s Summary

Rose graduated from Arizona State University with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and worked as an Accountant by trade. Rose Bracamonte was then recruitied over as an Executive Recruiter placing Accounting and Finance Professionals in 1996 with a National Recruiting firm. Rose was then recruited as President of a National Recruiting firm in 1998, where she staffed, grew and was successful at growing a successful search firm. During this period, Rose attended several National Seminars within the industry and learned the importance of the "Power of Networking" within the Business Community. Rose is now a Business Development Manager with a successful National Consulting and Recruitment Firm.
