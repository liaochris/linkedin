Becky Metcalf’s Summary

I'm in the Talent business! My experience includes recruiting on both the Corporate and Third Party Technical side locally in St. Louis as well as nationwide. Welcome to my profile!

Founded as a local computer consulting company in 1979, Levi, Ray & Shoup, Inc. (LRS) has grown to become a global provider of innovative information technology solutions with more than 600 employees. Corporate headquarters are located in Springfield, Illinois, and LRS offices are found throughout the United States and around the globe. As we have grown, we have diversified. LRS offers products and services in seven specific information technology areas: Enterprise Output Management, LRS® Consulting Services, PensionGold® Retirement Solutions, LRS® IT Solutions, LRS® Education Services, LRSSports® and LRS® Web Services.

LRS® software is in use at a majority of Fortune 500 firms and in 30 countries around the world, and the company is recognized as a global IT leader. LRS is ranked by Software Magazine as one of the top 200 software companies in the world.

We have various technical and sales openings in all of our divisions. Check them out at www.LRS.jobs
