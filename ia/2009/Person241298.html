Vance Jacobs’s Summary

Photography Clients Include: National Geographic Books, Sunday Times Magazine (UK), PricewaterhouseCoopers, BlackRock, The New York Times, The Ford Foundation, Nike, J & J, HP, Backpacker Magazine, Charles Schwab, The Robert Wood Johnson Foundation, KKR, Accenture, USA Today, and the Young Presidents Organization (YPO), among others.

My work has been honored in the Communication Arts Photography Annual four times (including in 2008 & 2009) and the Pictures of the Year (POY) competition, as well as being exhibited in galleries and museums throughout the world.

I split my time between San Francisco and New York.

---------------------------------------------------------
*Communication Arts magazine has announced the winners of its 50th annual Photography competition. Vance Jacobs’ work was one of the 156 projects (out of 8,432 entries) selected by a jury of creative professionals.

** From Nov. 4 - Nov. 9 Good Magazine will be profiling my project a Colombian prison on their website: www.good.is
after that you will be able to find it in the site's archive.
