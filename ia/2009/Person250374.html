Lisa Rothstein’s Summary

I've been a copywriter and associate creative director on Madison Avenue and in Paris for ad agencies Y&R, Ogilvy and Lowe, on brands including IBM, Hanes and Dr Pepper.

I recently launched a screenwriting career as I continue to offer marketing consulting and copywriting services to select clients.

My first script, Brit or Miss, was Winner of the Comedy Category, Screenwriting Expo 2; Top 12 Finalist, Slamdance Screenwriting Competition; semifinalist, Austin Film Festival; semi-finalist, Chesterfield Writers' Project competitions.

I am represented by Caren Bohrman of The Bohrman Agency in Beverly Hills, and manager Andrew Kersey. I've written five screenplays (comedy & romantic comedy) and am working on a sixth.

My extensive background in advertising on two continents has given me great preparation, both for writing and pitching my screenplays and as a marketing consultant and writer for select companies, ad agencies and authors in the US, UK and France. Beyond writing copy, I help my clients identify and refine their strategy and vision -- the story of who they are and what they want their brand to mean in the marketplace. I employ all media, including social media, to drive home my clients' messages and get them raving-fan customers.

See www.yourwriterforhire.com for samples of my copywriting and screenwriting work.

Along the way, I've also been a portrait artist, cartoonist, TV news translator/subtitle editor, musician at Disneyland Paris, English teacher in France and a street performer in New York City. I'm producing a radio show with host and TV Historian Jim Benson, on the history of television and its impact on society. (www.tvtimemachine.com)

Considering the above, it's no surprise that I'm co-authoring a book of life-management advice for multi-talented people, "The Da Vinci Dilemma", offering tips, strategies, exercises, role models & support for making the most of your many gifts. Stay tuned for the blog and book in 2009!
