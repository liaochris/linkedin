Steven Chiu’s Summary

Steven is currently leading Speak2Me's development efforts in Greater China.

As a social media marketing and community building strategist and adviser, Steven helps companies to strategically market themselves across various social platforms. He has launched internet projects in Asia, Europe and North America and is fluent in English, French and Mandarin Chinese.

Steven is the co-founder of China's first and largest internet recruitment web site, Zhaopin.com, launched in 1997. Zhaopin.com recently secured pre-IPO financing from Macquarie. As a long-term resident of Beijing, Steven provided information about the political, economic and social landscape in China to many of the world's leading multinational corporations, helping them to establish and grow their operations in the Middle Kingdom.
