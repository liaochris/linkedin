Greg Wallace’s Summary

EQ International specialises in Execution Quantware.

It was formed in 2001 to leverage off Greg's expertise in both quant and execution.

Our flagship product is an algorithmic execution engine known as EQUATOR.

A major Australian stockbroker has been using EQUATOR to execute its client's orders since June 2006.

A new version of EQUATOR is available for fund managers to execute their orders using algorithmic strategies via DMA with their choice of brokers.

Greg Wallace’s Specialties:

Quantware (quantitative software), algorithmic trading, algorithmic execution, quantitative analysis, execution research, market impact models, fund capacity reports, equities markets consulting, fund management consulting, strategic planning

  • Managing Director

    EQ International

    (Financial Services industry)

    2001 — Present (8 years)

    EQ International uses its quantitative expertise and execution experience to develop quality execution-related products and research, suitable for both institutional and retail stockbrokers and for fund managers, wealth managers and hedge funds.

    Its quantitative approach aims to increase execution efficiency and add structure and discipline to the investment process by providing advanced algorithmic execution with insightful analytical and feedback mechanisms.

    Its main products are:
    * EQUATOR - an algorithmic execution engine for the Australian equities market
    * EQ Impact Model - an advanced third-generation market impact model
    * EQ Fund Capacity Report - which determines how large an equities fund can grow whilst still meeting its objectives
    * EQ Order Viewer - an efficiency tool for retail advisors and compliance staff
