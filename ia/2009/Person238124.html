T.R. Griffith’s Summary

I am a builder of technical teams and technology solutions. I build teams from the ground up or I take teams with issues and I work with them to become outstanding organizations that can achieve the impossible. I am very good at finding highly talented people, experts in their field and listening to them, allowing them to lead themselves towards the goals I help them set. I excel in problems that technology can solve and then discovering and implementing the technical solutions that best solve for the problem.

I work with executive managers and customers alike to fully understand the goal and / or problems that need to be solved and then research, discuss with other experts and then act as a bridge between the technology and business, thus ensuring that the business goals don't get lost in the "network translation". And that all parties fully understand the solution that need to be implemented.

I am down to earth, approachable and an asset to every organization that I interact with. I'm always on the look-out for the resolution that will win for everyone.
