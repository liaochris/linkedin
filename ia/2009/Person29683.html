Benjamin Feigel’s Summary

I am a highly motivated, fast-paced, graphic designer who can effectively manage all aspects of design projects while meeting deadlines.

I have a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design from SUNY Fredonia and I have been a freelance designer since 2004, starting as a contractor for Carrier Coproration in Syracuse, New York. I currently work for XO Communications full-time in Herndon, Virginia. At XO I work on brand management and creative for web and print marketing materials from concept to final production.

With my education and experience I feel comfortable working in all areas of design, such as environmental design, product design, 3D design, packaging design, way finding, concept design and web design.

In my experience design is an exploration of thought developed into a visual message. As a designer I use structure to emphasize readability. However, design should not only be readable, it should visually communicate the values and purpose of the conveyed message.

Design should be intellectual, powerful, and timeless; it is a visual experience to be embraced.

Benjamin Feigel’s Specialties:

I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, QuarkXpress, and Microsoft Office on both PC and Macintosh platforms. Intermediate knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Microsoft SharePoint, and content management system integration (front-end). Basic knowledge of Adobe Flash.
