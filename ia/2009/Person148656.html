Marc Hinterlach’s Summary

An experienced manufacturing professional with over twenty-fives years of Management in Quality, Technical, Production and Operations disciplines.

I am a certified Lean Six Sigma Black Belt with a history of successful Lean, Six Sigma, Continuous Improvement and cost reduction projects. The accomplishments below were achieved through the successful building and leadership of Teams coached through the DMAIC process.

* Minimize process scrap for $100,000/year raw material savings in a continuous-line plastics process
* Cycle time reduction in Planning process: 65% reduction in wait time from customer order receipt to scheduling of order.
* Break press rejections: 50% reduction in product rejects resulting in raw material savings ($14,000) and cost avoidance ($10,000 for re-run/rework)
* 75% reduction in customer complaints for paint related issues: implemented Standard Operating Procedures and SPC on a continuous paint line operation resulting in Customer Satisfaction - Priceless!
* Reduced external Quality level for Caterpillar from 8,800 PPM to 440 PPM in six months. The internal Quality level was reduced to 22,000 PPM from a baseline of 90,000 PPM.
* Championed 5S and Visual Workplace initiatives.

I am comfortable in both Quality and Manufacturing leadership positions.

As Quality Manager I have owned the Corrective and Preventive Action processes, lead the RCA process, implemented product standards and work instructions and chaired MRB Review Boards. I have been the company's QMS representative for ISO 9001:2000 and AS9100:2004, leading both internal and external Quality System audits.

As a Manufacturing Manager I have managed up to 130 associates in the production, quality, maintenance and engineering disciplines. I have met and exceeded company metrics through empowerment, communications, coaching and accountability.

I am very interested in pursuing a Continuous Impropvement/Black Belt position.
