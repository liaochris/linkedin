Phil Antonelli’s Summary

over fifteen years of experience and leadership and management of instructor-led and Web-based training for corporate, university, and K–12 audiences. Demonstrated experience in the design and development of curriculum that changes behavior, enhances performance, stays within budget constraints and delights learners.

Areas of Expertise
-- Leadership in curriculum development, instructional systems design, instructional technology, the development of media-rich learning environments, learning solutions that blend live instruction with synchronous and asynchronous delivery methods, project management for learning and development projects
-- Design of classroom curriculum for large (+20,000 annually) global audiences.
-- Managing the design and development of complex curriculum that adhere to budget and time restraints.
-- Design and development of prescriptive learning curricula delivered via the Web.
-- Design, development and deployment of social learning networks

Achievements
-- Recipient of an Excellence in Learning award from Brandon Hall in the area of blended learning.
-- ACS executive development program including, mentor program, action learning, symposia, coaching, and stretch assignments
-- Blended learning program for top corporate managers including a multimedia classroom session for 1500 participants at Disneyworld.
-- Content and media for on-line graduate level program in Web-based learning environments at the University of Colorado at Denver.
-- Instructional design and development of Bank of America’s Enterprise Credit Curriculum (28 Web-based courses)

Phil Antonelli’s Specialties:
