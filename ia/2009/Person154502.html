Mary Jane Adams’s Summary

I excel in all aspects of communication through written and spoken word in print and interactive media. I take raw ideas to their best publishable state in destinations that are read, heard, clicked, rolled over, engaged, and observed.

I fiercely advocate user-centered design in software and Web development. My expertise spans complete development cycles from requirements definition to testing, launch, and user acceptance.

Frequently, I've facilitated communication among warring parties who fail to understand each other. Always, my highest goal is clear expression of ideas to produce a bountiful harvest of shared knowledge.
