Mike Minihan’s Summary

Born and raised just outside Chicago, Illinois, I attended Indiana University in Bloomington, IN and graduated from the Kelley School of Business with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business with a concentration in Computer Information Systems. Upon graduation I went to work as a management consultant for Ernst & Young, LLP in Chicago where I worked on eCommerce development. Decided to follow a dream to move to Denver while I was still young and single and was fortunate enough to transfer with Ernst & Young to Denver where I developed a new skillset in Telecommunications. After several years in the Telecom industry, I decided to leave Corporate Amercia in order to become self-employed. I gravitated toward the mortgage business where I worked for just under 2 years before moving to Atlanta with my girlfriend who would later become my wife Melissa. Upon arriving in Atlanta, I immediately went to school to get my real estate license and have been practicing real estate since 2004.

I went to work for about 4 years with Harry Norman, Realtors and in August of 2008 I transferred my real estate license to Keller Williams Premier Atlanta in Buckhead.

My specialty is residential real estate. I love to help people buy and sell their primary residences and have a passion for working with real estate investors as well who are looking to build a real estate portfolio of investment income producing properties. I work mostly inside the I285 perimeter, north of I-20 and in parts of the surrounding Metro Atlanta Area including Smyrna/Vinings, East Cobb and North Fulton Counties.

In my free-time, I enjoy playing Golf, Hiking and Backpacking, Photography and reading, and every chance I get, I try to get back to Colorado to Snowboard. My wife and I love to travel anywhere we haven’t been and like to SCUBA Dive when we can.
