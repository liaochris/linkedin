Vincent Vassallo’s Summary

Vincent is a catalyst for growth. Over the past 25 + years, his unique ability to link vision and organizational skills have ignited breakthrough results for over one hundred businesses, in a broad range of competitive environments.

Vincent has started and grown three different successful companies. Each in unrelated industries.

Starting from nothing, he grew each of these three to be a leader within its niche - Each with over a $ million in profitability - Each within two years - All with successful cash-outs for everyone involved. Further, he has been called in to lead the turn-around of three different failed companies - Each one succeeding beyond all projections. Vincent has been lucky enough to have never run an unsuccessful company.

As a consultant, he has coached over a hundred clients to growth and success. And, worked with one of the world's largest venture capital firms to assist their portfolio companies.

He is currently helping to lead the start-up of a solar energy company.
