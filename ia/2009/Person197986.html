Richard S F Clarke’s Summary

After over 30 years as Senior Partner leading the South Coast Consultancy and Accountancy firm Clarke and Partners, and 2 years as Director of Consultancy Services at C N A International he is now Director of Consulting with MA Consulting based in Wales rapidly becoming one of the premier solution providers in Europe.

His wide personal experience has embraced change management and corporate recovery, takeovers and acquisitions, advising on project accounting and budgeting, marketing and sales strategies, purchasing, systems and controls, HR Strategies and training.

He has experience in a wide range of business sectors including IT network installations, PFI’s, Catering and licensed trade, vehicle manufacturing, shipping and transport, building and property developing, contract and sub-contract management, theatre and stage production management

His previous Directorships include businesses in Structured Cabling, Bus and Coach manufacturing, Pub and restaurant chain, Golf Club, Theatrical Designers, Retail shops, Health club, Golf Club, Shipping and Transport Co, Insurance and Mortgage Brokers, Building and Licensed Property Developing Co, and a Stage School.

He has always had a keen interest in the theatre and was Chairman All England Dance Competitions 1983 to 2003, Chairman Royal Berkshire Young Dancers Trust1997 to 2004, Board member British and International Federation of Festivals of Music Dance and Speech 1991 to 1997 and is still a Vice President All England Dance Competitions

Richard S F Clarke’s Specialties:

A wide range of experience in the private sector heading and advising companies involved in acquisitions and mergers, marketing, union negotiations, product development and finance.
