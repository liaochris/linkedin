Russell Triebert’s Summary

My career has been eclectic at best, following connections to new positions/jobs initially on a five year plan, then about a decade each for the past three with them running concurrently.
I've worked with foreign and state governments, met a huge variety of interesting and different people from all over the country and have benefited from the majority of them, becoming friends with many.

Sales have been my forte, introducing new products into the fitness and physical therapy industry, as well as in the outdoor bicycle world.

In the bicycle industry I also consulted with new bike shops as they formulated their buying strategies and purchasing plans. Based on my 9 years as an outdoor goods and bicycle retailer/buyer, I assisted new retailers before they opened and as they got their footing in their local and regional markets.

I would like to not only continue to sell products that allow customers to improve their health and well-being, but also work with small incubation projects, launching new ideas into the outdoor industry.

Russell Triebert’s Specialties:

My specialty would be in the area of customer retention and the building of brand loyalty. Repeat customers form a solid base for any company, and my relationship sales are the foundation for such.
