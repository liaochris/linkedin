Raghav Tandon’s Summary

Raghav Tandon is the founding partner of ASP Global Works. An Engineering graduate from Netaji Subhas Institute of Tech., Delhi, India, Raghav has a Masters of Science degree in Computer Engineering from Texas A&M University and wrote his master's thesis on Leader Election Algorithm in Distributed Networks using Self-Stabilizing Mobile Agents. Raghav has several years of experience in various domains of software industry. His work experiences include two years of employment at Bell Laboratories as a member of the technical staff, and a wide range of projects such as LAN management software, enterprise level printer management software, micro-mobility architecture on GPRS. At Bell Laboratories, he was awarded the best professional initiative award for 2002-2003, the first to receive this award among the 500 employees at that time. He also developed the first web-based crash reporting system using Arc IMS as Research Assistant for TTI, TAMU System, College Station, TX, USA. His last job was as a Senior Developer for Xerox India.

Henceforth, Raghav became the founding partner of ASP Global Works. At ASP Global Works, he leads a team of technology enthusiasts. He and his team have managed several large projects in varying domains such Market Research, Idea Innovation Management, Education, Viral Marketing, E-commerce, Finance, SEO, Social Games. Recent assignments include the Tata Nano Web Project (tatanano.com), Airtel Wifilife campaign. Noteworthy to mention, is their success in helping many of their clients win huge funding, example include LearningHour.com which was successfully bought over by Educomp.

Raghav also conducts part time training programs in technology related areas. He has conducted training programs in places such as Xerox (J2EE), Keane India (J2EE), Knowcross (HTNG based web services) on varied topics related to technology.
