Caroline Shannon’s Summary

Caroline Shannon has been a journalist for more than six years, writing for several publications, including First30Days, Ideal Bite, Divorce360 and Carrie & Danielle, and past positions at the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette and Observer-Reporter. Caroline is a lover of all things related to fitness and nutrition, most often applying her passion for personal health to her work as a writer. She has been a runner for more than ten years and is a certified Pilates instructor. Caroline lives in Ohio with her fiancé, Dan, and four kittens, Emerson, Cooper, Madeline and Simon. Yes, she is the crazy cat lady.

Caroline Shannon’s Specialties:

Experienced, detail-oriented, systematic and confident professional with a background in media communications, and newspaper reporting and design. Always eager to learn and try new things, ready to ask pertinent questions or spend time pursuing the right answers in order to implement proper communication. Programs utilized include Microsoft applications (Windows XP, Microsoft Office), Lexis-Nexis, Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Quark Xpress and Wordpress.
