James Porteous’s Summary

I am currently the primary archivist and researcher at the Washington, DC Bureau of Al Jazeera English. Duties include archiving daily feeds using NewsBrowse browser, shot-listing AJE material and maintenance of database. I am also the sole visual researcher for the daily Riz Khan program, and primary visual researcher for the DC news unit, and occasional researcher for programs such as People in Power (including Cuba After Castro and Ethanol: Boom or Bust?) Everywoman, Josh Rushing’s AK-47 and various specials.

I have also worked as a freelance writer for 40 years, writing everything from feature articles, CD reviews and articles, internet website reviews, newspaper columns, magazine articles, travel items and more.

Was Music Editor of The Canadian Review and Playlist Magazine.

Worked as Associate Producer at CBC News: Sunday and was visual researcher for the award-winning documentary Deadline Iraq: Untold Stories of the War.
