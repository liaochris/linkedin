Ian Lewis’s Summary

Through my company WellTolled Pty Ltd I offer a
boutique consultancy with unparalleled experience in
the area of Multi-Lane, Free Flow (MLFF) tolling
systems. I have been associated with the majority
of successful MLFF tolling systems projects in
Australia to date, both in project governance roles
and in technical development and analysis roles.

My knowledge of MLFF system architectures, system
requirements and technical solutions, along with my
understanding of operators’ business needs, allows me
to offer informed opinion on project management and
technical issues extending from roadside systems to
billing and customer relationship management.

To provide this service I combine my thorough
technical knowledge and extensive experience of
MLFF project lifecycles with my personal attributes,
including:
* Very strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
* The ability to adapt quickly to new requirements
and challenges.
* The ability to rapidly master complex technical
problems.
* The ability to see lateral solutions to problems
and develop the means to enact these solutions.
* The ability to work effectively as a member of a
senior leadership team and earn the respect of my peers.
* The ability to communicate effectively with all
levels of the workplace, from senior executives to
line workers.
* The ability to structure and present complex
analyses and arguments in a way that enables
effective comprehension from a non-technical audience.
