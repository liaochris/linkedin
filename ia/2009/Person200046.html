Rob Drapeau’s Summary

Through my business writing company, wordSwell, LLC, I collaborate with clients to discover and share their unique metaphor, helping build their businesses by helping them connect with their customers on a personal level.

I write, edit, and "awesomize" anything business owners need to have read: web copy, newsletters, letters of introduction, blogs, marketing collateral, internal documentation, mission statements, training materials--including scripts for videos, manuals, and flow charts.

Over ten years experience as a teacher from 5th grade through adult education has taught me how to adapt my material to the different demands of different audiences.

Rob Drapeau’s Specialties:

Identity, marketing, translating difficult concepts into understandable terms, finding connections between seemingly unrelated things, brainstorming, collaboration, making good things better, making team members and clients look good, newsletters, blogs, business collateral, documentation, training materials, mission statements, company profiles. Equally comfortable in front of people or behind the scenes. Funny. Interesting at parties. Bilingual in Spanish.
