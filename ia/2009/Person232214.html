Cliff Aliperti’s Summary

Run several informational websites dedicated to vintage collectibles targeting everyone from beginning to experienced collector. Working to expand niche collectible markets by sharing as much information as possible and inspiring new collectors.

I've also been active in selling collectibles since the mid-1980's.

Currently specializing in movie cards and collectibles from the Silent Age through the 1950's and magazine back issues dating to the 19th Century.
