Mark Whiting’s Summary

I have worked internationally in Asia and in the West as a sole proprietor and in collaborations. My work has included managing design, working with development teams and providing research based strategic services such as international integration, cultural barrier bridging and creative problem solving. I run training workshops in creative strategy, design thinking, and using ethnographic research tools for innovation and design.

My hope is to help anneal organisations by providing creative thinking and research based design context.

I have a working knowledge of Chinese (Mandarin) and to a lesser extent French. I am now learning Korean.

Mark Whiting’s Specialties:

Design strategy, design thinking, ethnographic research, international exposure and communication, Chinese Language (Mandarin), problem solving strategies, team management and coaching, Solidworks, Luxology Modo, Shake, procedural graphics, software and workflow workshops in CAID, communication and problem disambiguation using Compendium, information mapping and IBIS.
