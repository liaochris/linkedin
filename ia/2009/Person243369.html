Vin Altruda’s Summary

After enjoying 15 years of success as a senior executive at Borders Group, I find myself in search of my next career challenge. I am seeking an executive position that makes use of my proven experience in a high-growth or turn-around retail or consumer services company.

I have a background of full P&L responsibility for a business unit generating $4B in annual revenues. My most significant accomplishments include:

-- Managed a team responsible for growing 19 stores to over 200 stores nationwide in five years exceeding $1.4B in sales. Achieved sales and profit goals in each of those five years.

-- Started the Borders International program in 1998, growing the division to more than 70 locations in four countries, and over $400M in sales by 2003. Achieved the five-year international expansion plan goals, including net income profitability as a division for the first time in fiscal 2003.

-- Launched one of the largest customer loyalty programs in specialty retail with over 15M members in its first year, 2006

My success to date is based on my ability to communicate a clear, compelling vision that can be embraced and shared by everyone paired with my demonstrated managerial courage to make timely, difficult decisions. I have been called an inspirational, hands-on leader who builds strong teams and leads them to success. These attributes drove market share, revenue and profit growth in a challenged industry environment and fostered a culture where “the preferred place to work is the preferred place to shop.”
