Tammy Guill’s Summary

What tourism does for you....Expands the array of marketable Washington state geotourism-oriented travel itineraries which feature the integrated product mix by season. Prioritizes direct sales promotions to domestic and international travel wholesalers and receptives through established trade shows, including Go West Summit, POW WOW, National Tour Association and ITB. Continues ongoing public relations programming targeting international travel and trade media. Seeks ongoing co-operative marketing programs with in-state and regional partners and overseas air carriers serving Washington State. Facilitates tourism's overseas marketing contractors as distribution vehicles for Washington travel products targeting international travel wholesalers and receptive operators.
