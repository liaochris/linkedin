Jackie Mitchell Edwards’s Summary

I am both a Wealth Management Advisor at Merrill Lynch and an Abstract Painter. I started my career in economics, practiced law in NYC, moved to NM, owned a gallery and began my financial advising career. I am a border crosser; crossing that border between the left brain and the right brain- the creative world of Art and the more linear world of Finance. My objective is to continue to bring my creative energy to bear on financial advising and bring my intensity and focus to bear on my painting. I will continue to grow my financial advising practice. I will also continue to show my paintings nationally. My goal is to live fully on both sides of my brain and to connect to other Border Crossers. I believe we are now living in the era of the Border Crosser. I find it natural, since I grew up on the US/Mexico border in El Paso and now live 45 miles away in Las Cruces, New Mexico! I have been crossing actual and metaphorical borders all my life.

Jackie Mitchell Edwards’s Specialties:

At Merrill, I work with high net worth individuals, families, businesses and small foundations, advising on investments,estate planning and wholistic financial planning.
In Painting, I paint large abstract works which are inspired by the spirit, forms, color and texture of the landscape and nature.
