Vicki Pandit’s Summary

Allow me to introduce myself as a user interface developer. Designing is my passion and I started designing early in my teens. From designing apparels and accessories for some of the best gift shops in Calcutta to designing intuitive, informative websites and designing use-friendly application interface, I have done it all.

My specialization skills include XHTML, CSS and JavaScript. I have extensive experience in Photoshop and Flash as well as many other software packages such as Dreamweaver and Visual Studio Web Developer. I have designed beautiful Web 2.0 web sites as well as user-friendly interactive web applications. For a brief period of time I have also been involved with successful extensive SEO projects. During application skinning projects, I have also worked on a couple of server-side technologies comprising basic ASP.NET, ColdFusion and PHP.

I have been a trusted partner with my team through the entire process, from concept to construction, from launch to promotion. I am very much very much fluent with each part of the complete process. In my current designation as Project Manager, I responsible for interfacing with the end Client Managers and business teams and converting requirements into functional design and specifications. Being an active member at online forums has helped me keep pace with emerging technologies. I also maintain a blog at www.merlinvicki.in during my spare time.

I believe in taking life as it comes, with a smile.
