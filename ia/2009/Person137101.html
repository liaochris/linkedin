Larry Staton’s Summary

TECHNO-LAWYER, NEGOTIATOR, PROJECT MANAGER

Experienced attorney with a technical background in software and a track record of managing large projects that deliver outstanding results on time and under budget. Extensive experience resolving civil and commercial conflicts for high-profile clients, avoiding extensive and lengthy legal proceedings. Excellent team player with the ability to establish priorities and work efficiently to achieve the team’s goals. Possess superior critical thinking and analytical skills honed through Juris Doctorate degree and Masters of Arts in Applied Economics degree. Lauded for ability to conduct accurate and thorough legal research using cutting-edge technology solutions.

I am licensed to practice law in North Carolina and Florida and in the United States District Courts for the Western District of North Carolina, the Middle District of North Carolina, and the Middle District of Florida.

I have more than 8 years of litigation experience, specializing in anti-counterfeiting, and more than 5 years of experience in proceedings at the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board. I have also arbitrated several UDRP complaints at the National Arbitration Forum.

I have used my extensive experience with FDA labeling requirements for dietary supplements and wine and malt beverage producers to clear labels for my clients.

In addition to my legal skills, I am a self-taught software developer with knowledge of several programming languages, Web frameworks, and databases. I have used these skills to increase efficiencies in office workflows and electronic discovery in litigation.
