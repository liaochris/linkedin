John Amaral D.C.’s Summary

Dr. John Amaral is a health care practitioner, speaker/trainer and author providing keynote and single-day topics and breakthrough processes that promote immediate transformation!

He has a unique message: Leadership, transformation and ultimately awakening, are beyond a mindset…they're biological!

John has served as a personal practitioner for Tony Robbins, Les Brown, Jay Abraham, Robert Dilts, and many other leaders in their fields, including CEO’s, world-renowned artists and professional athletes. His presentations combine fascinating discoveries in human biology, quantum physics, and psychology with easily applicable tools and exercises that inspire greatness.

John has over a decade of experience working with 1,000s of people from over 50 countries. Every week of the year in his private practice he is there with hundreds of patients, responding to their challenges, concerns, hopes and fears, strategizing their life direction, and providing solutions, and proven systems for finding the path of least resistance to their dreams.

John doesn't just talk about creating change, he guides you step-by-step using the most cutting-edge wellness technologies on the planet!

John brings warmth, depth, empathy, humor and systematic processes for attaining predictable transformation and true well being to any organization.

Topics for Interactive Workshops and Keynotes include:

• Body Leadership: How to Use Your Body to Transform Yourself as a Leader (aka.Leadership At The Quantum Level)
• Generative Body, Generative World: (With Robert Dilts)
• How To Think With Your Whole Mind
• The Soul Compass- How To Navigate The Invisible Forces That Rule Your Life!
• How to Beat Stress: Stop Reacting to Life and Start Living Your Purpose Now!
* Many more topics and trainings to choose from
