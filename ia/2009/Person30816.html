Bo McFarland’s Summary

Eleven year internet professional with deep product experience conceptualizing, designing and developing consumer-facing business solutions. Product experience includes search and directory (Infoseek/Disney), special interest sites (sports, auto & healthcare), community sites (Xoom), media sites (eCompany/Business 2.0), CRM e-commerce (Xoom & FreedomPay) and social networking applications (Facebook & Myspace).
