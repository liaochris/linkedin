Webster Jorgensen - Kansas City SEO’s Summary

I currently work as an online marketer and SEO Specialist at PlattForm Advertising and am the founder of the Kansas City SEO Club. My primary responsibility is to increase the amount of organic search traffic to both internal and client websites. The tactics used to do this are:

- Link Development
- Analysis of Information Architecture
- On-Site Search Engine Optimization
- (SMO) Social Media Optimization
- (SEM) Search Engine Marketing
- Web Analytics
- Multivariate Analysis
- Widget Development
- Mobile Marketing

I also spend a lot of time building my own web sites and have gained a lot of experience with web design and web development. These activities help me to better understand coding side of Search Engine Optimization. My current development projects involve the use of the Drupal open source CMS and DotNetNuke.

Before working at PlattForm Advertising I gained a lot of experience with database marketing, predictive modeling and CRM (Customer Relationship Management) at Zupreem here in Kansas City. The company I worked for ran a highly successful CRM system that integrated loyalty programs, e-mail marketing, direct mail, and personal sales.

My professional goals are to constantly learn new skills and become a better marketer.
