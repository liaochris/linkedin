Craig Mostat, Director of Operations’s Summary

Craig Mostat is a focused and influential leader who builds teams that achieve strategic vision. Through careful and articulate business planning, Craig communicates a strong message that engages employees to produce results. He is a problem solver who exceeds financial targets. His award-winning performance has seen him increase sales despite a declining market, and produce sales in excess of $165 million.

Consistently promoted as an inspiring and influential leader who drives corporate vision by delivering excellence and accountability throughout the organization, Craig rose through the ranks of one of Canada’s largest department stores to become the influential leader he is today.

Craig’s leadership has earned him multiple performance awards; three “best overall bottom line results” awards, two as a store manager in 1992 and 1995; and one as a District Manager in 2004. A three-year run of the best sales increase in the company culminated in the achievement of the Presidents Excellence award as a district manager in 2006.

As an inspiring and influential leader, Craig’s ability to lead is second only to his innate ability to connect with those around him, and empower them to seek the excellence he seeks in himself. Focused on his employees’ personal growth, Craig’s team nominated him top leader within the organization and he was awarded by an outside agency for producing higher quality results through employee engagement initiatives.

Married with two teen-aged daughters, Craig has found the time to write and publish a book on one of his favourite topics - leadership.

As a direct, dynamic, and confident leader, Craig’s self-discipline, motivation, and determination can easily be turned outward into a willingness to help, teach, and coach others. Leadership, truly a passion of Craig’s is his greatest strength and virtue – it has produced award-wining results for himself, his teams, his companies, and those who associate themselves with him.
