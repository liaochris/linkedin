Elizabeth Thompson’s Summary

Elizabeth is a stay-at-home Mom (an oxymoron, really, since she is found in her minivan running errands more than she's at home) and enjoys writing about the trials and tribulations of raising 4 children, a rambunctious lab and killer dust bunnies.

She has essays published with Nurturing Magazine, waxes poetic on her blog, thisfullhouse.com (a charter member of the BlogHerAds Network and Top 100 Mom Blogs) and hosts a bi-weekly blogging carnival on her review blog, thisfullhouse.com/reviews.

Liz is a columnist for The Imperfect Parent; contributing blogger for New Jersey Moms Blog, Green Mom Finds and Parent Bloggers Network; and has submitted essays as a contributing editor to BlogHer.org and Moms Speak Up.

She is also one of the Parenting Team Advisor Board at Disney's Family.com and featured mom blogger for Wondertime.com.

A native Jersey Girl, Liz is a first generation American - her parents immigrated from Hungary in 1956 - along with her twin brother, Army Dude, and is an avid genealogist, who will forever be thankful her grandmother taught her how to speak, read and write in Hungarian.

Liz lives with her husband, Garth (not his real name) and their four children (ages 14, 12, 9, and 6) and writes in New Jersey - you can view her professional bio and related PR/news blurbs at http://thisfullhouse.vox.com.
