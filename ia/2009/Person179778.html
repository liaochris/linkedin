Uma Baeko’s Summary

Remote Staffing- Programmers from Vietnam

The main aim of our service is to enable companies in high cost countries who are seeking IT professionals but have hard time finding them in their home country a safe and reliable way to find the right IT candidate in developing countries.

From our experience doing offshore software development, we saw that clients are constantly looking to establish a long term relationship with professionals who actually develop their projects and therefore we started providing this service which we call Remote Staffing.

We find, select, recruit, host, manage and train the candidates at our office in Vietnam. The entire process is customised according to clients wishes and company policies. Each hired software developer only works for the client either on permanent or temporary basis and for no one else at our office, so it is like having your own employee but elsewhere.

If the client request that the candidate should be onsite we can arrange that too.

Most importantly, we are here to assist clients to gain qualified people more easily, reduce the overheads associated with recruitment and last but not least provide them a safe path to do so. At the sametime enabling well qualified people in developing countries to have a good career.

In this way our Remote Staffing service is both satisfying and rewarding to both parties.
