Ron Antonette’s Summary

Experienced in both agency management and corporate PR roles, Ron Antonette brings nearly two decades of communications experience in brand marketing, corporate communications, integrated promotions and partnerships, and special events programming for some of America’s best-known brands and packaged goods companies.

Throughout his public relations career, he has been involved in promoting family & parenting products and programs, including more than 12 years as account lead for client Playmates Toys; agency and client roles for Mattel; launching Lego’s first learning-focused line; and projects for Amazon.com and The Walt Disney Company, among others.

Mr. Antonette spent more than 14 years at GolinHarris Los Angeles where he was the senior manager on the agency’s national Nestlé and Mattel accounts, senior counsel to Hollywood's Ralph Edwards Productions, and led many of the agency's initiatives for The Walt Disney Company, including the launch of Disney's Go Network (go.com) and the opening of Disneyland's Tomorrowland. His teams also have earned PR industry awards for the launch of the Toyota Matrix, a campaign promoting used music CDs for Wherehouse Entertainment, and programs for Cold Stone Creamery, LEGO, Mattel, Pasadena Tournament of Roses, and Playmates Toys.

He also previously worked in-house at Mattel, where he led brand communications for the toy giant’s Hot Wheels, Entertainment, Games, and Tech properties; and at ConAgra Foods, where he developed integrated campaigns for the company’s shelf-stable brands.

Ron Antonette’s Specialties:

Consumer brand marketing; social media strategy; corporate social responsibility campaigns; creating and partnering on integrated marketing campaigns; internal communications; crisis communications and plan development; special events development and management; news and creative copy writing and editing.
