Geoff Evans’s Summary

I have a passion for social media.

I believe that its impact is under-valued and overlooked. I believe that the world is constantly evolving and we need to embrace the opportunities that technology and globalization can present.

For businesses both large and small, Social Media can dramatically impact marketing, branding, networking and customer service. With the proper knowledge and skills, businesses can truly leverage the world of Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin, YouTube... and on and on...

The last 14 years of my career has been focused on training and coaching business owners. My mission is to help businesses (large and small) realize the vision that first led them to start their own business.

Geoff Evans’s Specialties:

I provide coaching and training on social media within the context of networking, marketing, recruiting, sales skills, business planning, process improvement and client management.
