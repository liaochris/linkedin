Trina Ogilvie’s Summary

Trina possesses fourteen years of marketing and communications management experience in launching software as service offerings in the enterprise, telecom and mobile markets (Electric Mail Company - acquired by J2 Global, Abatis Systems Corporation - acquired by Redback Networks in 2000 for 1 B and WebTech Wireless - International & profitable). While serving WebTech Wireless, Trina built channel distribution agreements with automotive OEMs, mobile operators (Rogers, AT&T Wireless, T-Mobile) and co-marketing partnering initiatives major integrators and developers (IBM,SAIC). Most recently, Trina has built and managed Greenfield Ventures, a specialized consulting practice focused on new ventures relating to mobile personalization. At the foundation of her career, Trina managed the direct marketing and publishing division of a boutique investor relations and communications firm acquired by www.quotemedia.com that was one of North America's largest direct marketing publishers for microcap public companies.

Trina Ogilvie’s Specialties:

business development, finance and marketing strategy, channel distribution, branding and identity, industry positioning strategy and planning, market research and business model analysis, strategic partnering, presentation development.
