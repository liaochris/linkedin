Larissa Ness’s Summary

Having been deemed as Austin's version of "Norah Jones on Fire," Larissa Ness connects with a broad spectrum of music lovers with her melodies and lyrics. As a trained classical pianist, her musical style transcends from pop and rock to easy listening and jazz to R&B/Trip Hop. Names often mentioned to describe Larissa's solo musical performance include Jewel, Sarah McLauchlan, and Tori Amos.
Larissa has released 5 cds of original music and performed her music all over TX . While still in college, Larissa toured in several European countries and the Carribean singing in an elite "a capella" vocal jazz group, called Voca Libre.
The true magic is all in Larissa's live solo performance. Catch one of Larissa's grand piano experiences at venues like the Four Season's hotel, Reed's Jazz and Supper Club, Sullivan's Ringside and III Forks.
Typically performing 5 nights a week in Austin, Larissa is actively looking for opportunities to get her songs in more films and commercials.

Larissa recently finished up in the studio working with HeLLo YOung Productions on her most recent album entitled Hello, which released in November of 2008. With guest rappers including Young Mischiff and well known radio artist Ray C, Larissa blends Hip-Hop beats with sexy, ethereal vocals to mold a unique new urban Trip-Hop/Pop/R&B sound.

In December of 2008, Larissa released, I’ve Found You, a side project E.P. of both jazzy and “Latin flavored” music, incorporating Flamenco, Salsa, Bossa Nova , and Samba beats to create an exciting “Norah Jones goes Latin” sound. With Larissa writing French lyrics and featuring players from the world renown Latin groups including Cienfuegos and Grammy-nominated Grupo Fantasma, these tracks are a refreshing new twist for Larissa's music.

In the upcoming year, of 2009, Larissa intends to promote the new albums as much as possible through radio, internet, film, & TV.
