Melanie Robinson’s Summary

Marketing Cafe's Commitment: Serve small business with passion and truth.

Marketing Café owner, Melanie Robinson, is a guiding light for small business owners no matter their challenges, no matter their budget. An MBA with a passion for entrepreneurship, she and the professionals at Marketing Cafe bring over 40 years of experience from major corporations, ad agencies and marketing firms.

Real marketing success requires that we first understand your company at its core; who you are, your core values, your company’s personality and your short and long-term goals and expectations. We do this through a unique set of activities that translate into a four-step approach.

Ask us about Marketing Café Right Start® Small Business Marketing Program !!
