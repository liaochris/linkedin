Jared Zwerling’s Summary

I am a multi-faceted sports writer and marketer who has worked for several high-profile sports organizations, including the NBA and Sports Illustrated. Currently I work for ESPN in several editorial capacities: a reporter/ researcher for ESPN The Magazine, lifestyle editor for ESPN RISE and GIRL magazines and a contributing writer for ESPNRISE.com. In addition, I am the founding editor of SportzUndercover.com, which discusses underreported sports and business topics. Some of my stories have been syndicated, and I was interviewed by a major newspaper in Cleveland for my entrepreneurial work. Capping off my interest in the expanding global sports marketplace, I received my Master's degree in Sports Business from New York University in December 2008.
