Anurag Harsh’s Summary

Winner of the 2009 Outstanding Asian American in Business Award.

www.IDG.com

IDG (International Data Group) is the World’s largest technology media company with $3.2 Billion annual revenues, 13,000 employees, reaching more technology buyers worldwide than any other media company with audiences in more than 100 countries.

Oversee marketing, syndication, and network development for the IDG tech group of media properties globally in 92 countries.

Focus on partner acquisition, network expansion, strategic planning, deal analysis & negotiations, setting priorities for corporate and publisher development.

Lead marketing efforts to package the IDG group for global media campaigns to reach tech buyers.

Responsible for syndication, licensing & packaging of IDG content from some of the world’s most trusted and tech media brands of 450 publications such as CIO, Games.net, PC World, Macworld, Computerworld etc.

Prior to IDG:

CBS: VP of National Strategic Solutions leading strategic media sales and strategy, partnerships including 727 streaming stations, mobile initiatives, over 3000 music events, and Super Bowl Half Time.

CNN: Director of Business Development responsible for driving strategic growth and revenue that led CNN to become the No. 1 news site of 2008.

Deloitte, Charles River, eIndia: 12 years as a management consultant and media executive advising the CMO's offices on key management issues.

Education: MBA from MIT Sloan and MS Management from Wharton.

Book Author: McGraw-Hill wireless media bestseller titled “M-Commerce Security”.

Music: MA in Classical Music. Performed a sold out solo concert at New York’s Carnegie Hall.

Leadership: Co-Chair, Wharton Media Network of NY.

Philanthropy:
