Bromme H. Cole’s Summary

In his present position as Principal of Hampton/Hoerter LLC Mr. Cole raises equity capital for large real estate projects and companies worldwide. While he has a substantial client base in Europe and North America, Mr. Cole specializes in "new frontier" countries.

Previously, as Chief Operating Office of Carlton, Mr. Cole’s responsibilities were allocated equally among internally concentrated corporate management functions and externally focused, strategic business development. Mr. Cole served as Managing Director of Carlton Strategic Ventures, the firm’s principal investment subsidiary, and was a Director of Carlton’s Executive and Management Committees.

Before taking on his responsibilities in executive management at Carlton, Mr. Cole was the principal of a private equity funds manager and investment advisor. In that role, he directed the firm’s strategic initiatives with respect to new business and managed all private equity offerings.

Prior to 2002, Mr. Cole was a Vice President in the Investment Bank of Bankers Trust/Deutsche Bank during which time he established a long track record of successful fund investing. In this capacity, Mr. Cole led the banks highly successful principal investment platform into healthcare properties. During his tenure at the bank, Mr. Cole worked on a wide variety of structured financings, portfolio restructurings, conventional debt and public equity offerings, and merger and acquisition transactions. Mr. Cole is a frequent speaker and sought after panelist at real estate conferences worldwide.

Mr. Cole holds various professional licenses including NASD Series 7, 63, 65 and multiple real estate licenses. He completed undergraduate work at Boston University and received his MS from New York University. Mr. Cole speaks Spanish, French and conversational Dutch.
