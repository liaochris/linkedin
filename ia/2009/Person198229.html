Rich Gaasenbeek’s Summary

Senior marketing, sales and business development executive with 25 years experience in the technology industry with both large firms such as Hewlett-Packard, as well as smaller firms and start-ups. Excellent team leadership skills, and comfortable selling to C-level executives and managing major account and partner relationships.

Current focus on helping B2B companies tackle tough sales and marketing challenges successfully:
• Driving a growing flow of highly qualified leads to the sales force.
• Measuring the business impact and ROI of the money invested in marketing.
• Aligning the efforts of sales and marketing.
• Developing a high-impact unified brand position that is implemented company wide.
• Creating and executing strategic marketing plans that are measurable and relevant at the Board level.

Proven leader skilled at building, directing, coaching and motivating marketing and sales teams to achieve extraordinary results. Innovative thinker with the ability to be both strategic and hands-on. Equally effective in corporate and entrepreneurial environments. Creative yet process oriented.
