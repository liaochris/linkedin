Joan Lockwood’s Summary

Joan has a diverse entertainment and technology background with 12 years experience working with Web 2.0, social media, digital media, Open API, mobile and entertainment industries. During her career, she has successfully launched numerous digital media and social media marketing campaigns and developed high profile digital media partnerships with entertainment companies, record labels and A-level artists including Avril Lavigne (Nettwerk Music, RCA ), 50Cent (Aftermath/Interscope), Lil Chris (Sony BMG), The Used (Reprise). Entertainment companies include Warner Bros, 20th Century Fox, Paramount Pictures, MGA Entertainment, Crystal Sky Pictures and more.
