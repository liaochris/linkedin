Larry Heimendinger’s Summary

I have begun to focus on small businesses in the area, assisting them with networking and in particular with QuickBooks and Point of Sale. I recently completed Partner Training videos for Microsoft on the installation and setup of Small Business Server 2008, and I have been deploying it for small businesses who want to leverage their work force with its new features. I am still accepting a wide variety of assignments, however.

My specialty is strategic business problem solution including technology applications. Because of my extensive experience in IT and other technologies, management and business background, and industry knowledge in a number of domains (banking, insurance, manufacturing, software, public utilities, public sector, etc.), I am able to bring many perspectives and skill sets together. Equally comfortable in the board room and the computer room, clients know that they will get a world class set of skills applied to their problems whether I am engaged from a technical or a business perspective.

My personal style of leadership and (project) management is to act as mentor and team builder. One-man shows rarely deliver to companies the best of results, and eventually, even a full-time employee moves on to other opportunities in the same or different organizations. Those who remain must be able to keep the processes going with the same level of creativity and completeness, and as part of the project, including them in idea creation, decision making and execution is the very best way to build future leaders.

I am an extremely quick study and have a great love of problem solving. During my career, I have often been called on to functionally operate outside my normal duties, and in doing so I have acquired financial, management, sales and marketing, public relations, vendor negotiations, and customer relations skills, among others, normally only found in persons who have held specific titles related to these areas.

Larry Heimendinger’s Specialties:

In a departure from what you expect of a C-level person, I am quite technical and competent in networks, server management and security, and in software design and architecture, as much at home working with developers as with board members.

Operationally, I am a skilled process designer and evaluator, excellent as a motivator, talented as a presenter, and throw myself fully into projects I become involved with.

These are key elements to integrate in the BMP approach.
