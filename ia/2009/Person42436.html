Joe Chicharo’s Summary

My most significant leadership roles include (in no particular order):
•Senior Engineer at BHP Steel (1983-1985),
•Research Director of the Switched Networks Research Centre - a centre of excellence funded principally by Telstra and by many other multinational ICT companies (UOW, 1994-1997 and 1999 - 2001),
•Managing Director of the Telecommunications and Information Technology Research (TITR) Institute (UOW, 1997-2001),
•Research Director of the CRC for Smart Internet Technology (CRCSIT, 2000-2002),
•Head of the School of Electrical, Computer and Telecommunications Engineering (UOW, 2002-2003),
•Dean of the Faculty of Informatics (UOW, 2003-present),
•Chair of the ARC Mathematics Information and Communications Sciences panel (ARC, 2005-2007), and
•President of the Australian Council of Deans of Information, Communications and Technology (2008-present).

Joe Chicharo’s Education

  • University of Wollongong
