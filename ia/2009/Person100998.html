    (Sports industry)

    January 2008 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Having dived with a number of different centres around the world, I quickly realised that standards can vary enormously, safety can sometimes be compromised at the expense of profit. So, how do you know which dive centres are professional and safety aware? Well, the short answer is 'you don't'. There is a certain amount of dispersed information available on blogs, forums etc but no single site which just focusses on the quality of dive centres and operators around the world. So, knowing how much I would personally value such a site and, given than none was readily available.

    Scubadviser is a review site for dive centres and liveaboards. Written by divers for divers, the reviews rate safety, standard of equipment, care for the environment and much more. With over 5000 dive centre and liveaboard listings, Scubadviser aims to provide the most comprehensive and accurate information about diving destinations, dive centres and liveaboards worldwide.
