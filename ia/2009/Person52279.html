Dallas Teague-Snider, CMP’s Summary

Goals- Conference Direct

As a certified meeting planner, I have more than 20 years experience in the industry ranging from Director of Sales & Marketing, National Sales with a CVB and now a great asset to my clients with ConferenceDirect. Having an industry insider on your side is a true asset.

My goal is to offer first class service to my clients that save them time and money. Partnering with others that have an area of expertise in a given field is the way to positively affect your productivity without sacrificing your time and the quality of the work.

Goal - Make Your Best Impression

As a speaker and trainer, my goal is to encourage and inspire a company, organization or individual be their best self. Offering an amiable approach to learning offers skills to help outclass the competition. We have 3 to 5 seconds to make a first impression.. What are you saying to your potential prospect?

Goal- Sales & Marketing Consulting

Often times we are so overwhelmed we cannot see what is right in front of us. Being creative and taking action makes the difference in a company that just exists through economic ups and down or one that thrives. Thinking around the box is the goal.

Offering a new prospective or just assisting you with a project, a fresh set of eyes can make a difference.
