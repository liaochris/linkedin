Vidyuth Arni’s Summary

Vidyuth, a seasoned and experienced Oracle Financials specialist excels in Implementation, Support, Training, and Business Development. With over 10 years of ERP experience, including a wide array of Oracle Financials implementations - from fast track projects to large complex projects with phases having a mix of business process reengineering, change management, system development, integration, and financial transformation - Vidyuth has taken on various roles - Solution Architect, Project Lead, Team Lead, Training Lead, and Educator - to facilitate the successful and timely delivery of projects he has undertaken in the US, Middle East, and Asia.

Vidyuth started out his career in Public Accounting and Industry. As a CPA/MBA from the US and a Chartered Accountant from India, he has over a decade of international Public Accounting and Industry experience that includes mid/senior level positions with the Big 4 and Fortune Companies.

Vidyuth Arni’s Specialties:
