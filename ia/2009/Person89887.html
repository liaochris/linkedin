Greg Raaum’s Summary

Greg is a web-based software architect and programmer, network administrator, and database administrator. He is the developer or co-developer of commercial software products ImageFolio, SecureDAM, Storefront Pro, @ssembly Line, TrustDOX, iBULC, MySlider, FotoJazz, StorefrontPhotos, Secure Document Xpress, and many additional custom client-specific software products.

Clients at BizSecure include General Dynamics, National Geographic, Dow Chemical, Penn State University, Berkshire Hathaway, Mandarin Oriental Hotels Group, PPG Industries, Polk Audio, ESPN, Belkin Corporation, Pfizer, Thomson FindLaw.com, Luminex Corporation, CBS Radio, San Diego Zoo, Times Newspapers, H. Bauer Publishing, Wilson and Associates, Warner Brothers, Philip Morris, Avery Outdoors, Audi of America, San Jose Mercury News, Boston Globe, Cross International, Children International, Princeton University, University of Illinois, Saddleback Church, McGraw Hill, Colliers International, Starwood Hotels and Resorts, Wyndham Worldwide, and many additional Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and universities.
