Gareth Davies’s Summary

I specialise in Information Security and particularly in Network and Infrastructure security. With a personal passion in this space and years of experience dealing with large corporate clients such as government bodies, national telco providers and financial institutions it enables to me to excel in both the technical side and service delivery.

My passion is what is going on in the Information Security arena - the latest developments, techniques, tools and happenings around the globe. This is reflected by the 'top 5' security blog I founded and write for at http://www.darknet.org.uk - which I update every weekday with the most useful infosec tools and news.

I also co-founded http://www.security-forums.com in 2002 which lead me to make a lot of useful contacts in the security industry and learn a lot about areas which I wasn't so competent in previously (cryptography, VPN and reverse engineering).

Recently I used my experience in community building to found http://www.shutterasia.com, a Malaysian based photography forum (which now has more than 10,000 members in less than 2 years).
