Rod Black’s Summary

In brief, my experience in the world of multimedia includes and is not limited to television, film, music, radio and a multitude of design elements.

I am the current Creator, Executive Producer and Editor of The Chronicles Of Rock National Television project and President of the Music For Military, a concert tour supporting the men & women of the Armed Forces. My ultimate goal and to take this project global while also branding and marketing it's unlimited potential. Future BMBtv, LLC projects such as The Chronicles Of Rap and The Chronicles Of Country are also in the works.

In 2009, I will act as producer and co-director for the feature film, Welcome To Detroit; a Hitchcock-esque thriller filmed on-location in Detroit, Oregon and within the Oregon State Mental Hospital in Salem, Oregon.

I was the former publisher and editor of S.P.A.M.M./Son Of Spamm! Magazine, a publication distributed worldwide through Tower Records & Books from 1996-2001. This project then developed for television first as SOStv (Son Of Spamm! Television) and soon changed to BMBtv: Bomb Television. The BMBtv project was produced and developed as a music video and animation program for SpikeTV.

In the late 90's I earned critical acclaim as the radio host for X94.1FM's "The Pit" and "Projekt X". Both specialty programs specifically created to support independent rock and hip-hop format music. The shows aired for 3 years and had exceptional ratings for the Central Oregon radio market.

I have also appeared in feature films as an extra and featured extra. These films include Zero Effect, Bandits, Assassins, 12 Monkeys and The Postman.

Further credits include the Battle of the Bands & Babes concert series (Creator), In Search of El Diablo (Writer, monthly column for Exotic Magazine), mrBlack Presents Concert Series (Promoter) and Rock 98.3 The Twins (Voice/Production).

Rod Black’s Specialties:

My experience is media and design includes pre and post video production, audio creation and editing, professional voice talent, web site design and coding, overall company branding and graphic design...and the list goes on.
