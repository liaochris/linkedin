David Camfield’s Summary

I work in the Mental Health Care field. Since starting as a staff RN in 1995, I now am the weekend House Supervisor at the primary Mental Health facility the Texas Panhandle, Eastern New Mexico and western Oklahoma, The Pavilion. I supervise all aspects of care including, acute psychiatric emergencies, acute severe Mental Illness, general adult Mental Health Care, geriatric and older adult Mental Health Care, pediatric and adolescent Mental Health Care. On the weekends I act as the administrator on-site for general business activities. I am the primary person that deals with patient/family concerns, physician interactions and dealing with the main medical hospital, Northwest Texas Hospital. I supervise all direct care staff including nurses, both RNs and LVNs, Mental Health Techs, therapy staff - social workers, licensed counselors, volunteers, emergency room Mental Health assessors and act as a direct go-between between administration and direct care staff.
My personal goals are to continue my education until I get my advanced practice license as a Mental Health Nurse Practitioner. I am also continuing to become a better Legal Nurse Consultant by being "The consultant that lawyers go to!"
