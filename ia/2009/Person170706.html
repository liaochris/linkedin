Nadia Gruzd’s Summary

Nadia Gruzd is a proven Entrepreneur and Worldwide Leader in the Executive Search Industry

Nadia's 20 year career in International Healthcare Recruitment started in South Africa in 1989. She lived in Canada from 1992 to 1997 where she became an immigration expert for healthcare professionals. Since 1997 she has been living in San Diego and is the USA's leading healthcare recruiter

In 1999 Nadia Gruzd was inducted in the International Professional Business Woman's Hall of Fame for outstanding achievements in Human Resources, Recruitment and Personnel

In 2002 Nadia was awarded the honor of Woman of the Year by the American Biographical Institiute & its Board of International Research

In 2005 Nadia was selected for membership in the Pinnacle Society, a recruiting industry honor society recognizing the top 75 executive recruiters worldwide

In 2007 she was recognized by the CSP as California's Staffing Professional of the year

In 2009 Nadia received an award from The National Association of Professional Business Women that recognizes her as having demonstrated excellence within the Healthcare Recruitment Industry

Nadia served on the Board of Directors of the NAPS for 2 years. She is a Certified International Personnel Consultant and one of the very few that have been awarded the designation of Certified Employee Retention Specialist by the National Association of Personnel Services

In 2004 & 2006 Unlimited Nurse Search was nominated for Best Employer Work Place Excellence Award by San Diego Society for HR Management
