Josh Perrymon’s Summary

PacketFocus.com voted "Top 5 Coolest Hacks of 2007" by Dark Reading

CEO of PacketFocus.com, focused on providing best-in-class security services within the Global Finance community. Specializing in Penetration Testing, Technology Audits, Compliance Assessments, IT RIsk Assessments, and speciality consulting.

PacketFocus was founded to fill a void found within most IT Security Services companies. We found that 90% of security tests were being generated by Nessus and called a vulnerability assessment. This not only reported false-positives, it didn't provide a real world view into risk other than a list of missing patches on each device.

To ensure that out tests are accurate, PacketFocus utilizes a proprietary methedology that is followed by each of our testers and analyst. Basically, we feel any of our testers should get the same results if testing the same client at the same time. Our quality is repeatable and based on OWASP, NIST, and ISECOM testing standards.

We strive to package testing results in a clear and concise format showing both Business and Technical risk. If you like more info visit out website www.packetfocus.com

90% of our clients prefer to engage us over a period of 2-3 years to perform all of their yearly testing services. (Pen-Test, Internal Vuln Assessments, Social Engineering, VOIP Testing, Wireless Testing, IT Risk/Compliance Assessment) .
