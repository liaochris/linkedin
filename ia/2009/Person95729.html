Chris Allman’s Summary

Professional recruiter that works with businesses and individuals in the fields of accounting/finance, healthcare, and sales. On occasion, I will accept assignment in other fields if I feel that I can be of benefit to the corporation or individual. I am in the business of finding the ideal match between the employer and employee, and am not in business to just fill an opening. I believe that people work best in an environment that fits their ideal specifications.

As an additional service to job candidates, I offer a resume and cover letter service that is designed to catch the reader's attention and land interviews. If you are having trouble in the interview process, I have a mock interview service that provides you with a phone interview and critiques your responses to questions that would be encountered in a real interview. Afterwards, you would receive a detailed report of your performance and recommended responses to the questions provided.
