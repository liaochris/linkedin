Sarah Morgan’s Summary

As a director at a healthcare public relations agencies, I develop and implement campaigns, programs and events.

That's the technical way to say it. It's thinking, writing, editing, creating, making things happen and getting people to understand why you care about what you're talking about. It's knowing a lot and learning fast. Some days it all works; other days, not so much. Those days happen, but it always gets done, and it's almost always fun.

I delight in digging into the burgeoning implications of social media. Being a geek, it thrills me immensely. It's public relations at its most essential. Transparency and personal effectiveness are obvious. Which is why it's so much fun speaking and teaching people about it and figuring out how to make it work.

Goals? To keep getting better. Then to get a little better than that. To sleep in Africa, swim in the Aegean, and have dinner in Tokyo. Although maybe not in the same day.
