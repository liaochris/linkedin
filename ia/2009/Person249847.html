Sharon Coleman’s Summary

Innovative entrepreneur and effective digital content manager experienced in all editorial and production aspects of print and online publishing seeks employment opportunity in digital media.

Sharon Coleman has specialized in the creation of content for traditional and new media for more than 20 years now. She was among the first wave of traditional media workers to migrate to the Web. Always on the cutting edge, Ms. Coleman was the first to use multimedia at Philly.com (formerly Philadelphia Online) and the first to independently produce an enterprise piece expressly for the regional Web portal. Editor & Publisher magazine listed Philadelphia Online among the "10 Most Heavily Visited Newspaper Web Sites" while Ms. Coleman was author and steward of the home page, news and special interest sections.

As a member of the managerial startup team for the free busines news and information portal dowjones.com, Ms. Coleman launched the Industry Oracle program in which industry leaders and top analysts provided commentary and answered user questions about industry trends, news developments and other factors affecting business. The program debuted in November 1999 and was continued via the marketing department at Dow Jones & Company after the national Web portal was acquired by a California-based company and renamed in 2000.

In 2001, shortly after Ms. Coleman started her own New Jersey-based business as a custom content provider, South Jersey Magazine cited her as one of 22 People to Watch in 2002. She has since served on the Technology Workforce and Government Affairs committees for the New Jersey Technology Council. Her editorial musings have been featured in TechNews, NJTC's monthly magazine covering information technology, communications, media, and emerging technologies, among other fields.

Sharon Coleman’s Specialties:

Internet content management and creation. Editor, writer and journalist. Proficient in HTML and most desktop publishing applications. Can work cross-functionally, especially with marketing, product, technology, and business development.
