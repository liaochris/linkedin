Scott Van Kerkhove’s Summary

Scott Van Kerkhove is the Founder of The Energy Group, LLC in 2008, and is currently the President and CEO.

Mr. Van Kerkhove is also the Chairman of EnergyWise SE, LLC and was responsible for landing the company’s sales and marketing contract for the EnergyWise Guaranteed Energy Efficient Building System. At The Energy Group, Mr. Van Kerkhove utilizes his construction experience, a background in building science, and his passion for energy efficiency/sustainability to service his clients effectively.

He has engineered and managed over 140 energy efficient building projects.

Prior to joining The Energy Group (From 2004-2008), Mr. Van Kerkhove was the Founder and CEO of Gulf South Construction Services, a multi-trade contracting firm specializing in insulation and drywall services across the Gulf Coast of the U.S. Gulf South Construction Services was instrumental in providing post-Katrina construction services on over 500 projects during his tenure. He was also the first contractor awarded Preferred Contractor status on the Gulf Coast as part of BaySystem’s roll-out of the EnergyWise Building System in the U.S.

In 2004, Mr. Van Kerkhove ended a 19-year career managing and consulting in the health care field. While he served as a CEO in operations that included hospitals, surgery centers, and public health programs, his most notable accomplishments include the start-up of five hospitals, two surgery centers, and one state public health network. His proudest accomplishments in health care include his work with state agencies in support of the disabled community.

As a Veteran of the U.S. Navy and the Gulf War, Mr. Van Kerkhove proudly served his country and was widely recognized for his leadership and team-based approach. He was honorably discharged in 1991.
