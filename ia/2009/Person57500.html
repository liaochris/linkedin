David Friedberg’s Summary

Seasoned veteran of the hospitality industry is leading business development for the hottest new upscale hotel in NYC-the $85M Re-Invented (1/1/09) 770 room Crowne Plaza Times Square. Over the tenure of his career, he has led sales efforts in hotels such as the Waldorf Astoria in New York City, and in hotel chains such as Hilton, Sheraton and Marriott. The bulk of his career has been spent with Marriott, where he was Director of Sales Strategy and Support for Marriott International's Lodging Sales Division. He provided strategic direction for sales leaders throughout the company network. While at Marriott he founded The Marriott Toastmasters and created The Golden Circle- a recognition program based on individuals exceeding specific goals, The Golden Circle became so popular that the program has been integrated into every Marriott worldwide.

Strong public speaking specialist achieved 2nd Place on 3 occasions at the Division Level in the Toastmasters International Annual Speech Contest. (6 competitors at this level representing the best of 1200 Regional Toastmasters)
