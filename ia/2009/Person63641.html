Diego Remus’s Summary

After 7 years helping groups of children and professionals develop convesational English language ability in a small Brazilian town, I graduated in Social Communication and lived an intense and upgrading 4 years editorial experience, also in another state.

While I was preparing to launch a new methodology as editor-in-chief for a business-related national magazine, I convinced myself it was time to take the opportunity of working as marketing coordinator for a knowledge management and IT group in São Paulo. Half an year later, I started experiencing work as a consultant, what led me to, another six months later, join a startup team and develop business models for virtual experiences.

Analizing the referential background, it makes sense having focused on the study of hypermedia interface customization with a passion for editing and researching. Evaluating the career, I see having gone from business journalism until corporate consulting leveraged me into being very happy. Now, as a relationship model developer, enthused on social entrepreneurship and engaged with friendly inspirational accomplishers, I measure success by improving work experience quality and enabling positive usage active perspectives with providers, collaborators and clients.
