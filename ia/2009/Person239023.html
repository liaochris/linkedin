Tushar K Ray’s Summary

I am a professional scientist (membrane biologist intensely focused on gastric Hydrogen and Potassium Ion transport) and a Vedantist with lifelong dedication to yoga and meditation. During the years I developed a deep scientific insight into the modes of yogic healing at the mind-body level. Based on my experience I introduced a skillful self-help technique, called Quantum Meditation (QM) that I have been teaching in my yoga school for the past eight years. As the name (QM) implies the healing occurs at the quantum level bringing a radical change in body physiology of an individual.

I have noticed a void on the scientific, philosophical and practical aspects of quantum healing amid my scientist friends around the world. The purpose of my LinkedIn group consisting of scientists and intellectuals from various fields is to generate a lively discussion on the rational aspects of quantum healing. It is hoped that such scientific discussions based on individual mind-body issues will help resolve many questions and motivate them to practice this hightec science of QM for their own health and harmony.
