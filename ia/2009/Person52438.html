Damien Porter’s Summary

I have extensive experience in the fields of advertising sales, publishing, online marketing, copy writing, graphic design, exhibitions, telecommunications and recruitment.

My background consists of 6 years of magazine advertising experience working for some of the biggest selling magazines in the UK such as Max Power and FHM.

I then worked on a freelance basis for 3 years on behalf of several clients I had worked with previously, producing catalogues, brochures and raising funding for such projects through advertising.

I also worked with several major retailers selling their franchise opportunities in order to expand their operations into new territories.

I now work for the Trinity Mirror group, specialising in larger accounts selling a range of services available via their digital portfolio. Services include online display advertising, directory advertising, design consultancy and search engine optimisation.

Damien Porter’s Specialties:
