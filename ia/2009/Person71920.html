Elizah Leigh’s Summary

While I lack formal social ecology training and have not yet cultivated expertise in renewable energy technologies or other green collar industries, my eco-intellect grows in leaps and bounds with each step I take along my journey as an environmental steward and wordsmith for the green generation. I am the type of individual who is unfailingly committed to each and every greenification project that I take on. Sure, I have degrees -- go ahead and count them -- but they amounted to nothing until I finally identified my raison d'etre.

Not surprisingly, I am extremely passionate about greenifying the world, reaching out to like-minded individuals and offering a digestible perspective on how to shift over into a more eco-enlightened lifestyle. I am a voracious reader of all environmentally-related news & resources and feel that I have become somewhat of a self-taught expert, but at the same time I am also always eager to expand my green knowledge database.

I'd like to continue to utilize my solid research/writing skills as an eco-writer-blogger-educator-environmental steward but I am always open to additional opportunities that will enable me to exercise my grey matter while making a positive impact on the environment and the world at large.

Elizah Leigh’s Specialties:
