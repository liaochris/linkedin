Chef Morris Buck’s Summary

I was recently inducted in to the Texas Culinary Academy’s Wall of Fame in the inaugural year. This was truly an honor as only 8 Chefs out of thousands of TCA graduates were chosen for this.

In my most recent position as Executive Chef at Cafe Castronova, I was featured on the cover of the Homeowner Times, a local Lakeway magazine, as well as being written up 3 times and mentioned twice in publications such as the Austin Chronicle, Texas Monthly, and Eat & Drink Austin. I

was also featured in 5 different commercials “A Minute with Chef Morris” for the restaurant that ran on cable channels such as Bravo TV and Food Network (in our local area) which were highly successful. You can see one of these on my website at www.chefmorrisbuck.com
