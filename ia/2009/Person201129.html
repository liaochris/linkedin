Robèrt Verweij’s Summary

TV chef & freelance / independent wine teacher with broad interest in both wine and food. I just love to share knowledge and my passion for gastronomy with the general public. This is made possible by TV, articles and courses in my training center: "Culinair Centrum Beverwijk".

Besides TV, workshops and culinary courses, I write for food- and wine related magazines and websites like Perswijn and Winelife. As a sommelier I like to participate in wine competitions like "Vinoloog van het jaar", "Copa Jerez" and the "Grand Prix st. Hubert".

It's possible to book me for culinairy demonstrations and workshops. Business to business is possible besides aiming on consumers directly. My passion for food and wine plus the name I've made by cooking on TV can be used for marketing and PR purposes. Bookings can be made trough Roadstar Agency.

Robèrt Verweij’s Specialties:

Bookings for culinary education and writing about food & wine. Speaking about gastronomy, food & wine during diners. Teaching about cooking, wine, cheese, gastronomy.
