Christopher Burns’s Summary

I have been writing (first published) since age 7, including short fiction, poetry, non-fiction, technical, screenplays, legitimate theatre, and music. In my directing career, I have made 4 films (which have screened at many film festivals), a music video, and produced/directed 23 theatrical productions.

In 2006 I created a two-and-a-half hour long Dolby 5.1 soundtrack to the acclaimed TheatreWest production of "A Lion in Winter" for which I won both the Ovation and the ADA.

Currently Mythos Productions is in full-swing with creative consulting services for companies such as The Bob Roberts Company, ThinkWell Design and Production, and Disney Imagineering. Our game consulting services are also expanding with teaching forays for DeVry university, which bring our concepts and techniques to the next generation of console and computer game designers and developers.

Christopher Burns’s Specialties:

Storytelling of all forms and mediums, script writing, directing actors, immersive entertainment experiences.

As an eclectic, hyper-curious student of EVERYTHING: Marine Biology (with emphasis on syngnathid husbandry and conservation), Computer Science (over 10 languages, multiple methodologies, multi-million dollar budget project leadership and technology steering), Firearms, Sword-Fighting and Choreography, Medicine, Wine, Games, Theoretical Physics, Psychology/Sociology, and Philosophy.
