Anthony Arko’s Summary

As a real estate professional during the last 4+ years, I have successfully counseled buyers and sellers in over 240 transactions in the Northern Virginia area. With an emphasis on residential listings in Loudoun County Virginia, I have successful transitioned my business from being a specialist on a top performing team to becoming an independant real estate consultant. At the same time, I have created an online marketing presence that has allowed me to replace all traditional marketing with digital marketing.
