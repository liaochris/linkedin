Liz Jones’s Summary

Liz Jones is an accomplished entertainment executive with a strong record of success in both online and mobile marketing.

As Vice President of Entertainment & Media, Liz was responsible for the direction and growth of The Hyperfactory’s entertainment division, which leverages the marketing power of the mobile medium to support film and TV studios, record labels, video game developers, media companies and more. Under her direction, the Los Angeles-based division created mobile strategies and campaigns that built relationships with consumers and supported new movies, TV shows, artist and album releases, video games, and more.

Prior to The Hyperfactory, Liz was consulting with creative and media agencies, bringing her expertise in mobile and online marketing to the agencies' clients. She was also been hired to consult on theatrical marketing and working with the film studios for advertising partnerships.

Previously Liz spent 10 years at Fox. She founded Fox Mobile Studios where she developed & produced cutting edge mobile content. Prior to that, Liz was VP of Digital Marketing at Twentieth Century Fox, where she created and executed digital marketing campaigns for over 100 films. Liz won multiple awards for her work, including 2 CLIOs, a Key Art Award and a One Show award

Mobile Marketing, Online Marketing, Management, Building Teams, Creating Original Made-For-Mobile Content, Content Strategy.
