Anuj KHANNA’s Summary

Anuj is the Founder and CEO of Wireles Expertise LTD.

Anuj has been working in the mobile industry since 1996 and specialises in developing new business and implementing strategic business plans which achieve exponential growth targets and revenues.

Anuj is a recognized as a mobile industry expert and is the author of five globally published mobile industry books and appears regularly as a telecom industry commentator in the international media.

Anuj is the Director for the Mobile Data Association representing over 100 mobile services and software companies in the UK.

Anuj has been in the wireless industry since 1996 and has previously worked in senior business development roles in with leading brands including Hutchison Telecom, Netsize, Tanla and Dialogue Communications.

Anuj’s expertise lies in growing technology start ups into the most successful companies and leading players in their respective fields which has been proven and tested by the companies he has worked for.

Anuj completed his MBA in Marketing from The University of Sheffield and holds a BA in Economics from The University of Bombay.
