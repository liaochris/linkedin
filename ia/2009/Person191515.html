Rick Binder, MBA’s Summary

I am an expert at developing consulting practices for staffing and recruiting companies. As the employment market changes I believe this is where the true money will be.

Temporary workers will not have the skills to handle the more specialized and advanced issues that come up throughout the course of a year. By the same token companies have laid off so many highly skilled executives and they are not presently inclined to hire them back in the necessary numbers to get this work done on time and with the necessary degree of accuracy. That leaves interim projects requiring specialized talent, and very few staffing or recruiting firms are equipped to ramp this up with much success.

Most recently I was able to more than double this business for a very large firm, and I am confident I can do the same for you. I am available to develop this very necessary and extremely profitable market sector for you on a local, regional or national level.

Please feel free to reach out to me at (818) 324-0462 or via email at rabinder2006@gmail.com
