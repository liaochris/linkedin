Tim Day’s Summary

Professional Online Media Designer with substantial experience leading teams of creative professionals to develop engaging communications tools for blue chip corporate intranets, extranets and websites.

Tim Day’s Specialties:

Interface design, Information architecture, Proposal writing, Project documentation, Creative writing, Team management and direction, Project management, Client consultation, Flash , Illustration, Photography

Tim Day’s Experience

  • Director

    Codex - Clarity Communications

    (Internet industry)

    March 2002 — August 2007 (5 years 6 months)

    I was principal Director of Codex, formed in partnership with Clarity Communications, in 2002, to develop an integrated communications agency, offering Graphic Design, Public Relations and Web and multimedia services under the one roof.

    Together with the overall team I worked with some of Western Australia's biggest blue chip clients, such as Rio Tinto, Alcoa, GRD Minproc and MDA National.  This blue chip work was complemented by a variety of smaller NGO clients such as Greening Australia, Youth Focus, and the Heart Foundation.
     
    The work consisted of a wide variety of projects - from promotional sub-sites, content management solutions for both Internet and Intranet sites, rich media applications for corporate intranets, and other online communication tools such as staff newsletters, surveys, and event registration and promotion tools. 

    Key Clients:
    Rio Tinto, MDA National, GRD Minproc/Global Renewables, Greening Australia, Cockburn Cement, Police and Nurses Credit Society

  • Creative Director

    Method + Madness Pty Ltd

    (Online Media industry)

    1997 — 2002 (5 years)

    I have worked in the web design and development industry since 1998, initially as senior designer at Method and Madness, which was later bought by Sausage Software, along with SMS Management Technologies, and the resulting entity was rebranded nationally as Method. During my time at Method I was primarily responsible for design direction, team and project management and client liaison for Method's key client :ING Australia (previously Mercantile Mutual). Our work for ING received multiple awards over the years I was involved, including winning the 2000 Telstra Internet Awards in the financial category for the ING Adviser extranet.

    Key Clients:
    ING Australia, Sealcorp/Asgard, Netwealth, Perth International Arts Festival.
