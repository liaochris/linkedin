Tom Corbett’s Summary

Starting off at the De Morgen newspaper I was responsible for everything logistics. Organizing the office and making sure everything kept working. I learned a lot about how to deal and communicate with different people. I was also at the time responsible for the new database of their Adverrtising Sales Team. Transfering the sales team from loose, individual excel-sheets to a full "relational Filemaker Database" was my responsibility; one of my first in my professional career :).

Then I was offered a job as "Information and Project Manager" at one of the two Belgian "Newspaper Advertising Sales Houses", called Full Page. On one side I was the liaison between the IT guys and the rest of staff. On the other I was responsible for implementing the new booking-system.

Then I worked briefly at an Apple store as sales person, but left 5 month later to become the Project Manager at INMA European Division. Organising conferences and representing the European Office of this International Newspaper Marketing Association.
