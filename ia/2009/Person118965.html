Jonathan Lloyd’s Summary

Sedo is an online marketplace for buying and selling Internet domain names. It is a division of AdLINK Internet Media, a European advertising network which provides a range of services for advertisers, publishers and marketers. These include: display, email, domain and affiliate marketing.

In the UK, Sedo has overseen the sale of high profile names including sport.co.uk for £135,000 to Media Corp plc.

Acquiring keyword URLs enable companies to grow their online brands, drive traffic, reduce PPC spend, improve SEO and boost sales.

To buy, sell or market your web domain names, please feel free to get in touch with the Sedo UK team.
