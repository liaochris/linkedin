Johan du Plooy’s Summary

Masters degree in Electronic Engineering, and an MBA. Many years experience in Senior management positions in a large variety of Industries and own businesses, and have started up several new businesses successfully. Most recently have specialized in International Business Development, and Internet marketing, and have set up Extreme Business Brokers Inc.

I enjoy sailing and cycling. I have been sailing for more than 25 years, and sailed across the Atlantic on my 54ft Bruce Roberts Ketch from Cape Town to Rio, Brazil in 2000, and spent two years as a Charter Skipper on large luxury Catamarans in the Caribbean, before coming to the US in 2001.
