Søren Sprogø’s Summary

Need advice or inspiration on how to run a successful consumer oriented webshop in Denmark?

Want someone who KNOWS how to increase conversion rates, increase up sales, generate traffic, and not just talk about it?

I'm an independent, freelance consultant with a little knowledge of just about everything concerning B2C eCommerce, and a lot of knowledge on how to execute it.

With a background in both Technical IT and Web Development I have a strong foundation. And build on top of that is knowledge, education and a strong passion about subjects such as web design, usability, IA, communication, organization, marketing and much, much more.

All though I have more than 7 years of IT oriented education, my more than 15 years of experience in the industry makes me a practical kind of guy. I don't just talk-the-talk, but I certainly walk-the-walk! And having several of the biggest and most successful webshops in Denmark as my regular customers proves that.

As a consultant my core beliefs are based on what I call a Good Karma Policy: I believe that if I give good, strong, independent advice to my customers without any thought about personal gain, it will come back to me ten fold!

So when using me you can always be sure that I will work 100% on giving value to YOUR organization, and not having any alternate motives like trying to earn money off you.

Anything else just isn't Good Karma!
