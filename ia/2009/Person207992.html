Mehta's Children Hospital (MCH) with over 80 beds provides the best neonatal and pediatric care amongst the hospitals in Chennai. MCH has an excellent combination of one of the top pediatric and neonatal teams (including many star neonatal and pediatric doctors, surgeons and specialists in India) coupled with a state of the art NICU and PICU facilities.
