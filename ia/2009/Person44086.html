Chris Stegall’s Summary

Our exclusive "Trusted Advisor" process ensures clients get more for their insurance dollar by offering additional, no-cost services.

"Trusted Advisor" is more than a tag line - it's the way we manage our business. Our team approach to service guarantees that you interact direct with our partners and not the latest client service associate to be assigned to your account.

We have decades of experience across our team. We are a locally based but nationally capable broker/consultant firm offering property & casualty, employee benefits, 401k, payroll and HR consulting services.
