Rui Pedro Caramez’s Summary

I teach at the Maia Higher Institute in the Sport Management program since 1998.
I deliver training workshops and presentations on issues involving personal development, public speaking and sport.

Being well-accomplished, all-rounded, caring, resourceful, focused, visionary, creative, and open-minded are ingredients for my success. Especially keen in sharing and learning mutually!
I have learned to become multi-faceted, multi-tasked, and muti-skilled.

Most receptive to putting my expertise to better use, and for the human good! Education, qualifications, mentoring, and developing skills, while helping others to do the same, have always been of my passions.

Through my passion and desire to help others, I have built a great network of people with similar visions.

I am fun-loving, humorous and entertaining to people who knows me. Always looking at ways to bring more cheer into other people’s lives, and in the process enrich my own.

I develop a unique combination of executive, entrepreneurial, business development, academic, and professional training experience in areas of personal development, marketing and management consultancy in most cases related in the sport environment.

To complete my profile, my professional background and experience:

• Over 12 years of educational experience at different levels: high school through university.

• Proven ability to lead and manage multinational teams especially in events.

• Extensive experience managing and developing projects in multiple organizations related with different economic activities (local cities, national sport organizations, sport clubs, companies)
• Researched, developed, and conducted seminars and workshops for managers and working professionals to satisfy their continuing learning needs.

Highly-rated lecturer and much sought after trainer/facilitator for workshop sessions. Designed seminars and workshops locally and nationally.
