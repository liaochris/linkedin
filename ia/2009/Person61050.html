Deidre Hughey’s Summary

My primary interest is help entrepreneurs understand how to utilize the resources available in Social Media to drive business to their website. A solid plan and strategy are necessary for maximum effectiveness - most of my classes are online seminars.

Speaker, Trainer, Writer, Wife, Mother - A current member of Toastmasters, the National Association of Women Business Owners (NAWBO), the Women's Business Owners Network (WBON) and several local Chambers. A columnist for Women's Edge Magazine, a founding member of and served on the board for the United Professional Sales Association (UPSA) and the Morrisville Chamber's KNOW (Knowledgable Network of Women)
