Megan Gray’s Summary

I am currently working with the Biotechnology Program at Johnson County Community College. I am working with students, educators, high school faculty and businesses in the science industry. We are having different programs this summer and fall to really promote the biotechnology industry and help to grow this sector in the Kansas City area. We will be having advanced biotechnology classes including: Cell Culture, Microbiology, Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), and DNA Sequencing. These are intensive classes that are designed for persons already in the industry that could use a refresher, intensive learning, or are looking to gain some extra knowledge in a certain area. We are also going to be having educator workshops for high school teachers from around the KC metro area. Please contact me at mgray32@jccc.edu if you would like more information on any of these classes.
