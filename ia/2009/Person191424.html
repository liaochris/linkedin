Duffy O'Craven’s Summary

I have worked at several companies. At each we continuously shipped new and improved products, delighted customers, and multiplied sales. At each company where I have worked, I advanced to team leader or group leader positions. I am proud of the products that we produced, among them: Tech Graphics workstation, AT&T Safari notebook, DragonDictate for Windows v1.3, Naturally Mobile Organizer v1.0, Cimetrics BACnet Explorer, BACtiveX script component.

I will continue to produce products until I draw my last breath. As developers, products are what we should be producing. Everything else we do is self-indulgence.

"If your cool new thing doesn't generate enough money to cover costs and make a profit, it isn't innovation. It's art." - January 2004 Fast Company article
