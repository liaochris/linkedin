Karla Santi’s Summary

Integrally involved in the design and creative direction of projects done at Blend Interactive. Has worked with a wide range of clients extending from small local and regional clients to large national clients. Her conversational media work has included campaigns for many large firms, some of which include BMW, Microsoft, Hewlett-Packard, Sony, JCPenney, and Nintendo.

Karla Santi’s Specialties:
