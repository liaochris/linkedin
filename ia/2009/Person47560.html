Clive Maclean’s Summary

Marketing Agency Executive who excels in identifying strategic customer opportunities, launching major product initiatives, and increasing marketing effectiveness. Significant accomplishments in building new businesses, driving innovation and creating successful marketing and new product solutions that enhance revenues. Expertise in international business and global markets with emphasis on Europe and Africa. Experienced leading the integration of successful entrepreneurial organizations into cohesive operating units.

Leadership: Strong cross-functional leader accomplished in building and leading executive management teams, recruiting talent and coaching subordinates and peers in varied cultures. Effective at generating buy-in and commitment among management.

Strategy Development : Developed business and marketing strategies to integrate acquisitions into existing businesses. Demonstrated vision and analytical ability by creating and implementing mid- and long-term plans that produced results. Led new business initiatives in global markets, revitalized brands and launched new products.

New Business Development & Innovation: Pioneered new businesses that stretched the company beyond its core business to deliver incremental growth. Applied innovative marketing technologies to broaden consumer reach.

Financial Management : Expertise in driving all aspects of business’ P&L to deliver on revenue and profit goals for domestic and international operating budgets. Applied financial systems and technology to more effectively optimize resources.

International Business : Significant experience in global business development, joint ventures, and management of international, cross-cultural teams.
