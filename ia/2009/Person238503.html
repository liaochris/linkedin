Troy Groberg’s Summary

I've been working with the Internet and websites since 1997. I have been the designer on more websites that I can remember (that's a lot) and I have always been fascinated with the business behind the websites. In many cases I have been able to consult on the core business in conjunction with the website. This has become my passion: business and how it can benefit from the Internet.

I noticed that most "Internet" companies focus on just one thing. And while I agree that it is good to focus on your core abilities, I think most of them are missing the big picture. When a company hires an SEO firm, they don't want them to just increase traffic, they want them to increase revenue. When a company hires a web designer, they don't just want a nice pretty looking website, they want a website that converts traffic into revenue. Too many times have I heard people say, "Oh, that is outside the scope of what we can do for you. You will need to have your web people do..." The sad part is that even though an SEO or a designer has done an outstanding job, it is perceived as a waste of money because the desired end result requires more than just that one function.

In early 2006 I decided to start my own business offering a full range of web services. My goal was to offer businesses the ability to get the services they need without having someone drop the ball because "it wasn't in the scope of the company's offerings". Things have gone well for Main10 and business is growing faster than we had hoped.
