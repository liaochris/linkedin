Alvin Singh II’s Summary

Alvin Singh is a documentary producer and writer, as well as the historical curator of the Lead Belly Archives in Seattle. He specializes in journalism, digital media, marketing and a professional DJ.

Singh has worked with a wide range of small businesses, corporations and non-profit foundations providing a diverse set of skills. Working in management positions has also sharpened his business experience. He co-founded an online lifestyle company named BOOKiDO.com that has continuously evolved its web presence and social media.

Currently producing and filming Midnight Special: The Life of Lead Belly as the first full length documentary about the life of the controversial blues/folk singer. The project is very intimate to Alvin since the “King of the 12-string guitar” is his great uncle. The film will be screened at film festivals, and eventually available in a digital format through podcasting.

He is also a contributing author in a newly released photography book titled Lead Belly: A Life in Pictures published by famed German publisher Gerhard Steidl. Featured in New York Times Book Review,O Magazine, American Legacy, and National Public Radio.
