Charlie McVeigh’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur operating in the licensed sector. Now looking to build a big business with the launch of The Draft House, an exciting new branded bar concept. The first Draft House is The Westbridge in SW11. Site two will open this summer, also in South West London. Our objective is to have ten sites within the next five years.

  • Managing Director

    (Food & Beverages industry)

    September 2007 — Present (2 years)

    Launched November 2007 by Rowley Leigh and myself, Le Cafe Anglais won aAim Restaurant of The Year in September 2008 and had established itself as a popular and influential member of the restaurant scene.

  • Proprietor / Managing Director

    Draft House Holding Limited

    (Food & Beverages industry)

    July 2004 — Present (5 years 2 months)

    The Westbridge is a busy local bar in Battersea, London which prides itself on serving the finest Irish oysters and a huge range of immaculately-kept draft beers.

    We are also the incubator for a new bar concept, The Draft House, which currently seeks its first site. The Draft House is all about a huge range of Draft Beers, immaculately kept and served with pride.

    We are the Home of the Third: our mission is to re-establish the 1/3 of a pint as a serving measure for draft. This is especially relevant in an age where customers are drinking less and specialty beers are getting stronger.

  • Proprietor / Managing Director

    Crimrate Limited t/as Woody's

    (Food & Beverages industry)

    March 1999 — December 2006 (7 years 10 months)

    Acquired very cheaply a derelict nightclub site in Westbourne Park (W9) with unique (for this neighbourhood) historic license to sell alcohol until 3am. Raised the finance to acquire freehold (in my own name) and also - separately - to fund the refurbishment through an operating company, Crimrate Limited which took a lease on the premises. The nightclub was launched in November 1999 to rave reviews and quickly became a feature on the West London scene. It was operated profitably until 2006 when Crimrate Limited and the freehold were disposed profitably to a private buyer.

  • Managing Director / Principal Shareholder

    Bush Bar Trading Limited t/as The Bush Bar & Grill

    (Food & Beverages industry)

    March 2000 — December 2006 (6 years 10 months)

    Acquired the freehold on a derelict former milk-bottling factory in London W12 which had traded as a large theme-bar. Assembled a Board of Directors with strong industry background including Tony Mackintosh (founder of The Groucho Club and now Chairman of Le Cafe Anglais). In November 2000 we launched the site as The Bush Bar & Grill, a large (115 cover) neighbourhood restaurant which quickly established itself as both a local hero and destination restaurant. After trading successfully for six years the company was sold to private investors in 2006.

  • Managing Director / Principal Shareholder

    Grand Union Public House

    (Food & Beverages industry)

    July 2001 — October 2006 (5 years 4 months)

    Acquired freehold on and refurbished this run-down canal-side pub in Westbourne Park. Revived, in a contemporary setting, specialising in a great selection of draft beer and really excellent pies. This business was operated very profitably until 2006 when it was sold to Jacomb Guinness Limited. It is now owned by Fuller, Smith & Turner PLC.
