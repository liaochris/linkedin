Rob Reynolds’s Summary

A senior level .NET developer that has been working in the industry for 6+ years and with agile methodology (Scrum) for 4+ years.

A great understanding of source control (SVN, TFS, Vault), continuous integration (CruiseControl.Net), automated build process (Nant, MSBuild) and the values of unit testing (NUnit, MSTest, MbUnit, Rhino.Mocks, TestDriven.NET) to create high quality, low defect rate software!
