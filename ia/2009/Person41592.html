Charlie Li’s Summary

Product Marketing, sales, IT Consultancy, business development, and account management experience across multiple industries in a global capacity.

I am currently the Vice President of global Quality Management and Testing practices (1,700 consultants) at Capgemini responsible for business development, solutions strategy and alliances for the FSBU.

I was recently the Director of world-wide Product Marketing at HP/Mercury responsible of the $700+ million Quality Management software product line. Solutions in the product line include Quality Center, TestDirector, WinRunner, QuickTest Professional, Business Process Testing, Change Impact Testing, SOA test management, services test, etc.

Previously I served as the Director of consulting services at HP/Mercury, managing the overall Americas quality consulting business. I have also managed the Americas southeast region consulting team responsible for P&L and managed $1+ million dollar quarterly services sales quota delivering up to ~200% quota. I have worked with hundreds of customers on a consulting capacity including: Bank of America, AT&T/Cingular, Fedex, Dell, JPMC, Metlife, WellsFargo, Home Depot, Unum Provident, Michelin, Starwood Hotels, etc. Before joining Mercury/HP, I also spent time in Supply Chain consulting and started my career as a software engineer with Texas Instruments.

I am a published Author of "Optimize Quality for Business Outcomes", the first book from Hewlett Packard targeted to align Quality Management with the business; soon to be also released in Japanese.

Fluent in English, Mandarin & Shanghai Chinese. Conversational in Cantonese and French
