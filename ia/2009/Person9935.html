Allison Lee’s Summary

Allison Schreiber Lee's extensive civil litigation, family law and criminal trial experience provide her with the knowledge to assertively and consistently advocate for her clients' best interests. Ms. Lee focuses her practice on civil litigation and domestic relations/ family law including divorces, modifications of custody, chlid support or maintenance/ alimony, medical malpractice and nursing home defense, prosecution of the tobacco industry on behalf of numerous Missouri hospitals, and criminal defense. Ms. Lee's practice has also included investigation and preparation of document-intensive multi-party products liability litigation from inception to trial. Specifically, Ms. Lee has conducted more than 40 jury trials to verdict and in excess of 100 non-jury trials. Ms. Lee has taken numerous expert and lay witness depositions. In addition, she has prepared witnesses for deposition and has defended those depositions on behalf of hospitals and nursing homes. She has argued Motions to Dismiss, discovery motions and Motions for Protective Order in courts throughout Missouri. Ms. Lee has drafted legal briefs, Motions for Summary Judgment, Motions to Dismiss, and Suggestions in Support thereof.

Ms. Lee also serves as an Adjunct Professor of Law at Washington University Law School where she teaches Pre-trial Practice and Procedure.
