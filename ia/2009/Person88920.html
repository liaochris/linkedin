Graham Street’s Summary

My company, Evington Grey, offer services in system auditing and project management. The system auditing services are based around the ISACA framework and all audits carried out by CISA qualified executives. We can be contacted for IT due diligence or an independent view on the IT systems or Projects being carried out in your company.

In addition to system auditing the company also conduct IT related project management based on the PMI framework. Project managers are PMP qualified.

We generally offer a comprehensive IT services but we cannot ignore our specialization in Market data. Areas related to usage, cost cutting, compliance, improving efficiency of the systems and data usage.

Example of service is in advisory on exposure on market data usage before an audit is carried out by by vendors like Reuters or the data creator like the Exchanges.

The services are mainly taken by senior managers concerned with business process and systems in place. However the services offered can be useful to a program director or internal auditing department that require a temporary resources.
