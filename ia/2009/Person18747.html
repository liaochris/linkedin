Andrea Perego’s Summary

Andrea Perego holds a Post-Doc position at the University of Insubria. He received a MA degree in the Humanities, a Master's degree in Computer and Communication Sciences, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Milan. His main research interests concern the Semantic Web, Web content rating and filtering, privacy, trust, and access control in social networks. Results of his research work have been published in proceedings of international conferences and workshops. He has been involved in the EU projects EUFORBIA (IAP 26505), DHX (IST 2001-33476), and QUATRO (SIAP 2003QL01). He currently participates to the EU project QUATRO Plus (SIP 2006-211001). He is an invited expert at the Protocol for Web Description Resources (POWDER) Working Group of the W3C (http://www.w3.org/2007/powder/).

Andrea Perego’s Specialties:

Semantic Web; Web content rating and filtering; Privacy, trust, and access control in social networks
