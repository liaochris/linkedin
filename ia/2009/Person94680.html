Hans Fischmann’s Summary

Strategic / Partnership Marketing & Business Development Professional at the intersection of TV and Tech

For well over a decade my career has been based on my ability to define, strategize and implement revenue and marketing opportunities in the new and emerging media space. My particular focus for the last several years is in the digital marketing realm and my unique experience allows me to often bring a different perspective on problems and work towards a better approach and solution.

For Time Warner Cable I am tasked with pushing traditional television advertising in an entirely new direction. The end game is making broadcast advertising accountable, targeted and much more effective. This involves extensive internal cross-functional work to develop innovative and powerful Marketing tools and the accompanying sales packaging and presentation materials. We are literally re-inventing television advertising and have quickly been identified as the team to watch in the industry.

While with Apple Computer I helped build the QuickTime TV content network and the relationships that ultimately led to the creation of the iTunes music store. I was primarily responsible for developing, negotiating and maintaining syndication agreements with major brands including Disney, Time/Warner, Primedia, Viacom and Universal with an emphasis on music and video. These complex, relationships required the creation of completely new business models.

As a consultant I was repeatedly hired, and recommended, for finding and developing better mousetraps on behalf of clients.

Having worked for several dynamic and innovative companies I learned that I love to work in challenging environments that require passion and creativity. Working with smart people that are focused and driven to create commercially viable products, that have a social impact, makes me feel all warm and fuzzy inside.
