Sean Hoffman’s Summary

A fascination with the then-dawning phenomenon of the internet led me to explore the edges of its technical depths as a web master over a decade ago, and since then I have grounded myself in the theory and practice of the backbone technologies that drive global commerce and enable high-speed digital communication.

My focus over the past 7 years has been in troubleshooting and building networks for global companies. However, my knowledge of server-side process and of protocols outside of routing and switching has served me well in diagnosing particularly difficult issues and forseeing underlying problems with emerging designs before they are deployed.

It has also allowed me to integrate existing tools for network management with various customer environments to best purpose. Remote management and other proactive tools have served me well in striving to quickly diagnose issues for demanding clients as well as vital infrastructure devices, and I understand the role that good design, accurate documentation and clear process serve in enabling maximum uptime for my customers.

I seek intellectual challenge and reasonable recompense, and I like companies with vision.
