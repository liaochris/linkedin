Claire Curzon’s Summary

Brighter Directions have vast knowledge and experience delivering a range of services, including:

• Strategic Marketing Development
• Marketing & Sales Training
• Business Development Consultancy

Based in Chesterfield, we cover Derbyshire, Sheffield, Nottingham and throughout the East Midlands region, striving to deliver the best solutions for small to medium sized businesses.

As we are a small company we don’t carry the overheads larger competitors do, meaning we pass those savings onto you.

Our approach to our customers is to develop a lasting relationship so that we can give the best possible service and work together to achieve fantastic results.

All our services include a full after care service, which helps you monitor the results achieved, develop your action plans and ensure support is on hand at all times.

Customer service is key to running my company, I pride myself on the reputation I have built over ten years to put customers first - yours and mine..

I have worked with various 'established' clients including: Royal Mail, HSBC, Johnson Publishing, Q Financial, VK, International Holidays, and various National Charity organisations.

For more information please visit our website: www.brighterdirections.co.uk or contact us on 01246 287414

We offer a free no obligation assessment on your company which will help us to assess your strengths and build on them with strategic development and actions agreed together.
