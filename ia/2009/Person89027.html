Grant Keir’s Summary

Grant Keir is a creative film and television producer and writer whose short film, ‘Relativity’ won the UIP Prix Berlinale in 2002. Grant develops and produces through Faction North Ltd, (factionnorth.com). Grant produces film, TV drama and documentary projects including the highly innovative ‘MyDangerousLoverBoy’ cross platform initiative, backed by National Film Board of Canada and Northern Film and Media, with partners Screen Siren Pictures Inc (Vancouver, Canada) and new media specialists, Quba New Media.

Grant attends all the major film markets and festivals and has an extensive network of national and international contacts. Grant has produced films and TV programmes on four continents for broadcasters and financiers including BBC, C4, ARTE, ABC (Australia), UKFC, National Film Board of Canada, Screen Yorkshire, EM Media and NFM. For credits see www.imdb.com

Grant is an active member of the Sheffield Docfest Board and graduate of the European Audio Visual Entrepreneurs (EAVE) professional producer training course.

Grant is Senior Consultant at Inspiral Ltd, a company providing specialist business support services to entrepreneurs in the Creative and Digital Industries (CDI), across the UK (www.inspiral.biz).

    Faction North Ltd works at the cutting edge of drama, documentary, TV and cross platform production. Led by award winning producer Grant Keir, the company was established in 1998 and is based in the city of Sheffield in the North of England.

    Faction North combines the talents and experience of producers Grant Keir and Peter Day, together with writer / director Virginia Heath. We produce international award winning drama, such as our short film ‘Relativity’ that won the UIP Prix Berlinale in 2002, and we were the first company to produce an ‘on-line’ documentary series, Smart Hearts, for Channel Four. We regularly work with UK and international broadcasters including BBC, C4, ARTE, ABC (Australia), UKFC, National Film Board of Canada, Screen Yorkshire and New Zealand Film Commission. (www.imdb.com/company/co0104590/ )

    The company is currently developing two new feature films, the East European set, Love Is Like Vengeance, and the New Zealand set Mystery Ride. To this end, we attend all the major film markets and festivals, including Berlin, Cannes and Edinburgh and recently attended the co-production market, Connecting Cottbus. We have an extensive network of national and international contacts and a strong track record of nurturing new talent and working with producers, writers and directors from around the world.
