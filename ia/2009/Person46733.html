Claire Palmer, Career & Coach Development’s Summary

I work over the phone and face to face depending on circumstances and my client’s needs. Different time zones are not a problem. My services include;

~Executive Coaching; for you, if you are already successful in Sales or Business yet see that ‘fine-tuning’ 1 or 2 leadership skills could make a huge difference in terms of results.

~Transition Coaching; for clients wanting to make smooth and successful transitions to new roles.

~Career Coaching; if you want to have a clear picture of the career that is right for you.

~Coaching Supervision Mentoring; if you're a coach wanting to take your coaching to a new level of mastery. Also ICF accreditation mentoring. If you have specific questions around coaching then I also one-off consultancy sessions.

~Assessments; For increasing your self-awareness - Myers Briggs, Birkman, 360 around Influencing Effectiveness, Leadership.

Please contact me direct, either email or phone, if you'd like an exploratory conversation or more information.

Follow me on Twitter ClaireCoach.
