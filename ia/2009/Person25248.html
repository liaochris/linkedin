Brian DeMarzo’s Summary

I am a well-rounded information technologist with extensive experience in project management, software development, database administration, systems/network infrastructure, and general IT consulting. I am actively involved in open source software initiatives and am familiar with many different applications, systems, tools, frameworks, libraries, and design patterns.

Most recently, I am working full-time as a Senior Developer for BIA, Inc., while I continue to run my own custom software development company, ComputerSims, specializing in developing web-based games (notably CSFBL, a multiplayer baseball management simulation).
