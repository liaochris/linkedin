Melissa "Mel" Edwards’s Summary

Mel also is a blogger, playwright of "Shout: Kiss My Art" and an award winning poet. It is Mel's mission in life to help people live their creative dreams, and share positive stories of people who do exactly that.
*Publishes press releases from artists, performers, creatives for free on her blog www.votrevray.blogspot.com
*Public speaker and workshop leader on a variety of topics for creative people (or those afraid of creativity)
Professional educator since 1991, Mel Edwards holds a MA in Storytelling, is also a Reiki master teacher, and is a certified Astanga yoga primary series instructor.

Melissa "Mel" Edwards’s Specialties:

Motivational speaking - True Stories of Creative People, blogging about living "your truth," and workshop facilitation.
