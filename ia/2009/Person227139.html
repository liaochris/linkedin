Susanne Johnson’s Interests:

Susanne is a Career Advisor in Denver, Colorado. Her clients benefit from her successful professional experience in counseling, education, human resources, career development and educational placement. Prior to doing career counseling full-time, Susanne spent 15 years in the education field in positions ranging from private school counselor to public school principal to director of university admissions. Her skills in relationship-building, marketing, professional writing, employee relations, information databases and developing effective partnerships help her to assist clients with career transitions. She is energized by designing effective solutions to complex job placement issues. Susanne’s "motivator" personality helps her to inspire people to identify, pursue and achieve their career goals. A native of the Chicago area, Susanne has been in Denver for 10 years. She enjoys travel, music, sports, hiking, painting and performing arts.
