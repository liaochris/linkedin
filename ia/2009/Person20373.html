Arthur Reis’s Summary

Thirty years of experience in the clothing industry have given me knowledge encompassing all aspects of men’s clothing.

• An eye for fashion. I have to be astute on what colors, patterns, and fabrics go together on different people. Styles and fashions are always evolving, so clothiers need a sense of what works best on a person, not just what is in fashion.
• Knowledge of fabrics. A person’s size and wear of the suit affect what type of fabric should be chosen. This aspect goes beyond look and feel.
• Not everyone can wear the same type of suit. Body types affect what type of styling, color, and fabrics a person should choose for a suit.
• An integral part of men’s clothing is tailoring. I have an in-house tailor shop with old world tailors that have at least twenty years of experience. When you have invested in quality menswear, it is imperative to have exceptional tailors that know how a suit should fit and how to properly alter garments.
