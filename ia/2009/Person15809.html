Angele L'Abbe’s Summary

Born and raised in the Lower Mainland, I've had the fortune and opportunity of acquiring a great understanding and appreciation of one of North America's most vibrant real estate markets. My love for my city coupled with my passion for my profession make my job all the more satisfying ~ as I aim to make my clients' real estate experiences as rewarding and enjoyable as possible.

I joined the real estate industry in 2002 and throughout my career, I've taken pride in the lasting relationships that I've developed with my clients and the results that I've been able to deliver for them.

The foundation of my business is service ~ whether it's a market assessment, open house, or an afternoon of showings, my goal is to exceed your expectations.

''I treat my clients like friends. I'm committed to giving them the best of me, always; taking service to the next level.''

My commitment to my clients is to provide professional and personal service in all aspects of helping them buy or sell their home. A meeting is better than a phone call; a phone call is better than an email. You won't just see my face once when you deal with me; I take my business personally.

~Angele
