Mary Anne Nagy’s Summary

My years as an attorney taught me speaking, writing, and analytical skills and sharpened my innate compassion for the underdog. I'd never get rich as a lawyer - I had to ride the horse for justice, especially to the underserved.

When living in states where I did not have a license to practice law, I worked with the title "paralegal," but produced work beyond that expected of the average paralegal.

In 2005, I was disabled and could no longer work outside the home. Eventually receiving a diagnosis of multiple sclerosis, I applied the years I'd spent fascinated with the Internet (which for me began in the 1990s) and pursued my stay at home entrepreneur dream working as an online marketer.

Mary Anne Nagy’s Specialties:

An excellent speaker and writer, good communicator and understanding of people from all walks of life, from the very privileged to the very poor, I am able to walk in most any environment with a great deal of comfort. I use my talents currently to continue building my own businesses and to serve others, especially Boomers and the disabled, in learning how the Internet can serve them as a social and financial interface with the outside world, alleviate the destructive effects of loneliness.
