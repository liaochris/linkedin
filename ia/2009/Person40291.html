Ceren Guven’s Summary

Ceren Guven is a Sr. Technical Recruiter in Los Angeles, CA focusing in Information Technology. She completed her undergraduate degree at Hacettepe University in Ankara, Turkey with a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration. Upon gaining professional work experience in project consulting, coordination, and analysis in multinational organizations and Turkish Government, she decided to further her education and move to Los Angeles.

Human Resources Management and theories surrounding Organizational Psychology have always been areas of fascination for Ceren. Coming from an analytical and consultative background, she believes that every function of an organization serves essential duties, therefore efficiencies in each function should be sought for. However, the only resource of every organization that varies drastically and has the most direct impact on any potential efficiency and effectiveness is Human. She completed her MBA with a concentration in Human Resources Management and an emphasis in Marketing at CSULB. She then combined her experience and expertise in theories and practice of Management and Human Resources Advisory with a forward focus in Information Technology.

She worked at boutique as well as international Fortune 500 Technology Staffing Firms and gained industry expertise in Information Technology and Recruitment and Retention fields. She now serves as a partner to several Information Technology Professionals and Organizations in the Los Angeles area, through advising on Human Capital Management and Personnel Planning and Procurement.
