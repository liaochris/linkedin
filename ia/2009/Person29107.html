Amitava Biswas’s Summary

I am a practicing technologist and management professional. I have worked in various roles: as a manager, technologist and a business functional expert for 9 years in telecom software, business applications, industrial control and electronics industry. During this time I have directed large technical teams and projects. Computer systems, software and managing technology business are my areas of expertise.

At present I am completing my PhD. After this I intend to return to industry. Last 3 years I am in academic research and have 11 publications. My past research work were in: networking, operating systems, performance and software engineering, real-time, energy aware, embedded, distributed and information systems. At present I am working in information retrieval, data structures for representation of meaning, techniques for semantic comparison and advanced distributed index structures. More information about my professional work can be found at my website.

In past I was awarded the National Council of Educational Research & Training Scholarship by Government of India. As part time faculty, I had taught at graduate level for 3 years in management institutes and universities. I am a certified Information System Professional in Canada. I have training in various aspects of research management. My past and present professional and honor society memberships are with IEEE Computer Society, IEEE Engineering Management Society, Indo-American Chamber of Commerce of Greater Houston, Society for Research Administrators, Canadian Information Processing Society and Phi Kappa Phi.

I had worked and volunteered for non profit organizations like: BAIF, Oxfam and Global Action Network, Akshaya Patra, which provides 1000,000 meals every day to needy school children in India. At present I am operating a scholarship fund (www.sbeducationtrust.org) to support needy school students in India. At present I am supporting 16 school children.
