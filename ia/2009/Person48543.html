Roger Allbrandt’s Summary

I work for Absolute Concrete Flooring based in Denver, Colorado. We grind and diamond polish commercial and industrial grade concrete flooring up to 1500 grit (almost gemstone quality), creating a highly reflective, non-slip, and chemically resistant surface that doesn't fail or need an ongoing maintenance contract like epoxy coatings or VCT tile.

I started Grant Rider Consulting in August of this year as our work on various LEED certified projects began to require specific documentation and occasional research. Drawing from several years of legal and scientific research and writing, Grant Rider was born. Grant Rider is a professional grant writing and research firm focused on obtaining government and philanthropic grants for pilot projects, entitlements such as Small Business / Woman or Minority Owned certification, and anything else which most people find unpleasant and tedious. Hire Grant Rider and get the process started TODAY.
