Carter Abbott’s Summary

I completed medical training in London, Canada in 1983 and have been practicing in a wide range of opportunities since 1984.
My wife and I moved to Iowa in 1997, and relocated to Omaha in 2006.
Omaha Med Spa was formed in 2006 to provide high quality specialized care in non surgical aesthetics. Our focus is to help clients achieve their aesthetic goals without having to undergo surgery.
I have a special interest in spider vein sclerotherapy and find it to be the gold standard for eliminating small spider veins.

Carter Abbott’s Specialties:

Discreet confidential provision of aesthetic services in a warm trusting environment.
