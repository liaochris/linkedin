Alex Putman’s Summary

"Never quit. It is the easiest cop-out in the world. Set a goal and don't quit until you attain it. When you do attain it, set another goal, and don't quit until you reach it. Never quit"...Paul "Bear" Bryant

I have the pleasure of working with one of the best teams in recruiting! We are a process driven group with a shared goal; satisfy the customers need!

In the past decade, my teams have been responsible for the fulfillment of 3,200+ requisitions and job orders.

My passions include social networking, social media, human resource services, talent acquisition, career coaching and assisting people in their search for career fulfillment.

I enjoy consulting & coaching other recruiters in techniques that enhance their marketability and capabilities. I have lead workshops for new and seasoned recruiting professionals via a managed training program focused on goals, objective and time management.
