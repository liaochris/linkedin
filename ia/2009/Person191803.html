Rachel Weidinger’s Summary

I provide strategic consulting to social entrepreneurs, often working with visionary founders in times of organizational transition.

In the past four years, my focus has been on effective participation in emerging web technologies, particularly online social networks.

My approach for nonprofit clients often uses earned revenue as a tool for diversifying funding, strong stakeholder communications for stabilization, mission-based program development, and an awareness of the public policy environment focused on creating long-term impacts.

I am marketing generalist and can develop and implement comprehensive marketing plans.

And I love my work.
