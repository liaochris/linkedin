Jenn DePaul McNamara’s Summary

I believe that by leveraging games and game technologies we can change the way people learn, work, and live. I am dedicated to applying my background in training and education, AI and agent-based systems, human factors, usability, game development and general M & S to explore new avenues and applications for serious games.

Games are proving to be a disruptive technology and an industry is developing around their serious uses. I strive to contribute to the advancement and professionalization of the serious games industry.
