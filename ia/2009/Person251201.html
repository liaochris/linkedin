Zarko Susnjar’s Summary

Beginnings of my computer and electronics related history were on 1984 Amstrad CPC 464. That machine made me interested in something more than just using it for playing games. I remember my first programming triumph, I made small star moving and rotating on psychedelic flashing screen in Locomotive Basic 1.0.
Of course after few years this was not as fun as it was before, my 64K had to go to basement, my new 486 was to expensive to experiment with, and I switched to electronics. I was making alarms, hi-fi systems, house gadgets and as a cherry on the top of the cake, GPS receiver with Intel controllers as my finals in high school. After that I realized that hardware is not promising and challenging anymore and tried to find something else to do. Since then, I was experimenting in various fields, web design, video production, audio engineering and composing, system administration, networking, databases and programming. After 2 years in interactive TV business, in which my manysideness and comprehensive experiences brought me many great moments and triumphs, I decided to take a blank canvas and do something fresh and new, rich internet applications.
