Kathryn Holloway’s Summary

At the age of 22 Shropshire Entrepreneur Kathryn Holloway had an idea.

“To help businesses find an easy way to get their brands noticed, and most of all make promoting their business fun”.

Kathryn’s education and training is in marketing and graphic design, but her main interest is marketing communications and branding so she wanted to put her skills into practice. After studying the market and speaking to her existing clients, she spotted a gap in the market. Then the exciting part of starting the business could begin. With some help from her business mentor Roy, Kathryn was able to turn her idea into reality.

The next thing we needed was a name and after many long hours and much deliberation we’d got it…... and ‘Promofix’ was created. As all Kathryn wanted to do was s help companies fix their promotional problems and get them in the spot light.

Over the years Promofix has expanded their range of products sourcing items from all over the world. Now the team are designing and manufacturing bespoke items for clients, allowing businesses to choose what they really want! From day one Kathryn has kept the same enthusiasm in the business, dealing with each customer as an individual and continued the same ethos running the business as it grows from strength to strength.
