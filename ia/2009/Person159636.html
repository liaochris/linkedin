Melissa Mashburn’s Summary

I have worked at a mega-church in the Ft. Lauderdale, FL area for 4 1/2 years and in that time I have done just about every ministry type job you could imagine, but the thing that I absolutely LOVE the most is empowering women.

My heart's desire is to launch a new ministry outside of the church walls for women that will provide a "step" closer to finding a church home. That will not compete but complement the church body. Where women can learn to live life with an authentic faith while being transparent, renewed, and transformed (Romans 12:2).

I have already taken the steps to make this dream a reality and have spent the last 6 months planning, preparing, and praying for this new ministry. I know that God has equipped me for such a time as this and can't wait to watch what He does with this.

I love mentoring women and encouraging them to be all they can be for Him. I love to network and help connect people with who they need to know to make their dreams come true too!
