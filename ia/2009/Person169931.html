Rachel Murray’s Summary

Once we start working together it will be a long term partnership where I can really add value to your career and recruitment strategy. I have 12 years digital recruitment experience which spans across all disciplines in digital. My career started out in IT as a professional resourcer, developing to Digital Manager at Recruit Media and Aquent, to my current role as a Director of Futureheads Recruitment Ltd. My flexible approach means I can work across all industry sectors my core being; entertainment, publishing, media/broadcast, public sector, charity, and agency both freelance and permanent. I have a solid background in sales and account management. As Digital Manager my focus has been in supporting, training and mentoring a team of 12 consultants in their sales strategy and account management approach helping them develop into really commercial and successful consultants. There is nothing I love more than supporting a colleague through their career and seeing them develop into a really respected and knowledgeable digital expert.
