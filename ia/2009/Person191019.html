Rob Harvey’s Summary

Technical Project Manager having over twelve years of overall IT experience with nine of those years specializing in the design, configuration and implementation of networking devices and systems, as well as hands-on troubleshooting, problem resolution and security pertaining those network devices and systems. Prior to Jefferson Wells, he was an experienced team leader and has developed national engagement strategies for a Big Four consulting firm. He obtained a Bachelor of Business Administration in Management from Georgia State University focusing on Behavior Management and Organizational Theory.

The last couple of years have been focused on Technology Risk Management including but not limited to Attack and Penetration, IT and Network Security Audits, General IT Controls documentation and testing for Section 404 of the Sarbanes Oxley Act and PCI engagements.

Certifications include MCSE, CCNA, CCDA, HISP and QSA.
