Andrew Wagle, CIR’s Summary

Human Resources professional with over 12 years of experience including recruitment and retention, labor relations, benefits administration and management development. Possess broad knowledge of human resources policies and practices supporting union and non-union environments. Demonstrated skills in labor and employment law, coaching and counseling, employee orientation, HRIS reporting and performance management. Strong ability to develop and implement plans for talent acquisition and retention.

Currently a Senior recruiter for Sodexo supporting the Education Facilities Division. Currently recruiting for Facility Managers, Engineers, Environmental Service Directors, Grounds and Landscaping Managers, and other facilities related managment staff.
