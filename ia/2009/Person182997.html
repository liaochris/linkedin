  • Chief Executive

    Evaluate Technologies

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Utilities industry)

    September 2006 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    Co-founded Evaluate Technologies - a web development services provider to the Financial Services industry.

    Evaluate has developed software that enables us to accurately represent the lending policy of all UK banks. Users may complete and submit a single mortgage enquiry, receive the best responses from all lenders in an easy-to-compare format, select a product, and submit their application online.

    This solution is offered:
    - to financial intermediaries to enable them to source mortgage for their clients.
    - to consumers via mortgage broking or finance comparison sites capable of generating sufficient volmes of relevant traffic.

    Our consumer-facing proposition provides the user with the tools they need to control the mortgage advice process from end to end. This is a major advance on existing propositions that are designed to elicit the user's contact details so that they may be sold as a lead to mortgage brokers.
