Ellyn Spragins’s Summary

Self-discovery, personal growth and meaning are the focus of the What I Know Nowtm brand. WIKN Enterprises' products and services inspire women to discover their inner wisdom and share it with each other. The newest book in the series is IF I'D KNOWN THEN Women in Their 20s and 30s Write Letters To Their Younger Selves. The next one due out in 2009: WHAT I KNOW NOW ABOUT SUCCESS.
I speak regularly at an array of corporate, media and association events about what I've learned working with the celebrities and well-known accomplished women in my books and leading the audience through the experience. I'm also using the letter-writing concept in two women's leadership development programs for corporations, called WHAT I KNOW NOW Seminars and Workshops, as well as coaching a limited number of female executives. Coming soon: What I Know Now videos

Ellyn Spragins’s Specialties:

Inspiration, validation, bonding among women executives, showing superstars' inner journey.
