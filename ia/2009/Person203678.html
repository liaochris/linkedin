Ron Simone’s Summary

For a complete profile, please visit www.visualcv.com/ronsimone.

A results-driven Executive offering a solid record of leading substantive business lines. A strategic leader with analytical drive, creative dexterity, and a discipline for building new profit streams. Expert at cost-extraction and unlocking hidden growth potential in mature businesses. Proven success building high-performance direct and cross-functional teams, leading cultural change, and managing complex turnaround assignments.

A President’s Award-winning head of a $100 million EBT business unit which achieved consistent double-digit growth and above-Plan results for ten consecutive years. A business line builder with extensive practice in mass and direct-to-consumer marketing channels, used to launch and scale business units and products. Expert at developing and managing recurring-revenue and cross-sell programs, using best-in-class methods to optimize margin, accelerate revenues, and minimize loss ratios.

Work experience has spanned the consumer services and financial services sectors with leadership assignments in the Caribbean, Mexico, Puerto Rico, Taiwan, Thailand, the United States, and in the UK.

Line of Business Leadership, Gross Margin Optimization, Cost Extraction and Productivity Management, High-Dollar Negotiation, Strategic Alliance Partnering, Value Growth Strategic Planning, Business Development, Mass and Direct Marketing, Call Centre Operations
