Roberta Nedry’s Summary

Works with industry leaders on how to generate repeat, referral and additional business based on exceptional service. With more than 30 years of executive training, client service and marketing experience, Ms. Nedry consults with clients and hospitality leaders on guest perception studies and programs to enhance profitability while making guests happy. Previously, she served as the General Manager for the International Concierge Institute and the International School of Tourism for the United States, Latin America and the Caribbean. Trained in Guest Relations through Walt Disney Productions and a two time Disney “Ambassador to the world” finalist, Ms Nedry holds a B.A. In Linguistics from UCLA and is fluent in French, proficient in German and Spanish, with studies in Japanese and Italian.
Ms. Nedry is active in the Hospitality and Tourism Industry and has been a featured speaker at numerous hospitality, concierge and business platforms. Her work has been published in China, Australia, Poland and throughout the United States. She writes an international business column on service excellence and guest service in the Hotel Business Review and is a guest columnist for The Triton, an international mega-yacht publication. Ms. Nedry was recently awarded honorary membership and honorary gold keys in Les Clefs d ‘Or, the international association of professional concierges from top four and five star resorts worldwide. She is a member of the Editorial Board of HotelExecutive.com, the Gold Coast and Southern Florida Concierge Associations and is published regularly in HCareers.com, eHoteliers, 4Hoteliers and numerous other hospitality news sources and publications. Considered an expert in customer, client and guest service, Ms. Nedry’s expertise is also published and sought by the funeral, financial, healthcare, commercial real estate and non-profit industries.
