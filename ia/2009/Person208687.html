Sandra Lie’s Summary

Founder and Director of Infusion Networking Group.

ABOUT MY BUSINESS
Infusion Networking is based upon a distinctive concept. The more that Australia and Asia and the greater business world are continuing to do business with each other, the more important it will be for business owners and professionals in both worlds, to network and develop relationships that strengthen its business or organisation.

From this belief, I established Infusion and started organising casual networking events in Sydney of 2006 to introduce the concept of cross-cultural networking between Asian and Australian business personalities.

We are a young organisation and today our cross-cultural networking group has the most diverse group of people attending the events; incorporating different age groups, industries and management levels.

Infusion Networking has become a membership-based organisation that is exposing itself through-out Australia and the world. Designed for business owners, entrepreneurs, professionals and leaders, our membership program offers something for everyone.

There are various reasons why people attend Infusion events or join the networking group

* Interested in cross-cultural networking between local Asian and Australian personalities
* If you or your company has an Australian-Asian business interest
* Currently deals with Asian businesses
* Requires more understanding about how to do business with Asia
* Interested in Business Matching
* Considering doing business with Asia and meeting direct contacts
* You are a start-up or established company from Asia wanting to market your services to the Australian business community and meet new contacts
* You are a representative from an Asian country wanting to make new contacts, partnerships or affiliations
* You are simply wanting to build new business relationships or generate referrals amongst the Australian business community
