Mike Dunn’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL
I am a senior level UX Designer/ Developer. I have been a part of several high-profile online initiatives in several capacities, from concepting and information architecture, to visual and experience design, to front-end programming and user-testing. With ten years of experience in the interactive industry I bring to the table a diverse skillset and a meticulous eye for detail.

GAMING
I am currently on the administration staff of GamingTrend (formerly the console-centric sister site to GoneGold, Console Gold), and in addition to my development and user interface duties I am also a staff writer and manager of marketing.

Mike Dunn’s Specialties:

Front-end programming (CSS/XHTML, Javascript), Interactive Design, Multimedia Design, Logo Design, Video Production, Extensive experience in the Gaming Industry, Conceptual Consultation, Information Architecture, Motion Graphics(Flash, After Effects), training and supervision of junior level developers and designers.
