Darian Rodriguez Heyman’s Summary

Dot Com refugee done good. Darian moved on from co-founding digital ad agency Beyond Interactive and has since decided to focus his energies on helping people help. His work at Craigslist Foundation is centered around educating, empowering, and connecting emerging nonprofit leaders. Instead of focusing on one cause, one organization, or one person, Darian seeks to improve the effectiveness and efficiency of all nonprofits.
