Berend Jan Verheijen’s Summary

Started as an account manager for ilse media bv, the first Dutch search engine. After a few months I became Marketing Analyst. One year later I became chief editor for www.ilse.nl. In 2002 I made the switch to the Dutch broadcasting station BNN, where I became chief editor for of the New Media department. Since november 2004 I'm the Head of the New Media department.
