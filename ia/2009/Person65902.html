Donna Sweidan, MCC, LMHC’s Summary

Expert in career exploration issues, assessment, job search, on-line identity, resumes and strategic coaching. Over 15 years of providing highly personalized career and life transition counseling to individuals ranging from 17 to 71 years of age.

Clients are typically professionals, career changers, re-entry women, baby boomers, college students and graduates, graduate school seekers.

*Industry Specializations: Financial Services, Human Resources, Media & Communications, Publishing, International Affairs, Music & Entertainment, Visual and Performing Arts, Non-Profit, Education, Training & Development, Health, and Social Services

*Counseling & Job Search Specialties: Pre & post-college career planning, baby boomers in job search or career change, career and skills assessments, resumes and cover letter development, interview coaching, cutting edge job search techniques. Internet job search, international candidates/foreign nationals in job search, disability, age, multi-cultural, GLBT, gender issues in the job search

My style is to combine a strong sense of intuition with concrete and practical tools to counsel and coach my clients. My goal is to provide the guidance, support, knowledge and inspiration you need to manage the career or life transition ahead of you.

If you are in transition and looking for a new path, I will help you examine your dreams, passions, interests, skills, personality, values and any other influences that might impact your choices. The goal is to help you develop insight as well as the skills to make informed decisions, establish personal goals, and develop a plan of action.

* I counsel and coach nationally and internationally, via telephone, internet (SKYPE) and video conference.

*Popular Speaker: I have presented at colleges and organizations in the tri- state area on topics such as: How to use linkedin, Using the Internet in your job search, and other Job & Career related topics.
