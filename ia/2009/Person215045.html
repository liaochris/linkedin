Shankar Nagarajan’s Summary

Seasoned IT Professional with over 15 years experience in architecture design, development, management & deployment of full lifecycle software projects (SDLC)

Skilled and experienced in the areas of Program Management, Application Development, Change and Incident Management, Configuration Management, Operational Quality, Risk Management, Release Management, Service Delivery, Regulatory Compliance etc.

Successful track record of managing people, working collaboratively with partner organizations, vendors and completing projects across multiple product lines on time and within budget with focus on Quality, Service Delivery and ROI.

Defining and designing IT policies and procedures to comply with Sarbanes-Oxley requirements and managing IT SOX audits.

Experienced at developing technology and business integration methodologies for implementing enterprise wide applications such as Remedy, Oracle Applications etc.

Hands-on experience in network programming, database driven application design, software programming and database administration for real time systems in Client/Server architecture.

Strong overall experience in all the technology aspects of a company with excellent communication, leadership, mentoring and team skills.

Nominated by EMC senior management for the 2009 Boston Fellows program, a year long leadership program aimed at identifying and training next generation leaders among Boston area companies.

Executive Producer of the 2 successful music albums “H1Bees” and "Padal" which were widely profiled on NPR, Washington Post, BBC UK, Radio France, NBC Universal, Voice of America, CNBC, Baltimore Sun, Asia Pacific Arts, The Hindu, Times of India etc

Managing Partner in music production company “H1Bees Inc” (www.h1bees.com) which creates music for albums, film soundtracks and documentaries.

Core member of charity organization OPEN (www.openindia.org) which funds education and infrastructure for poor and needy children.
