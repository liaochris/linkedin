Tim Slavin’s Summary

Tim Slavin helps businesses communicate effectively with their customers and stakeholders. He has worked as a business and technical writer, as well as an internet marketing expert creating web sites, web applications, email marketing, and search marketing.

Tim has written technical documentation, online help, speeches, articles, email newsletters, press releases, and other forms. He also has 13 years experience with web sites and web applications, from business planning and documentation to information architecture, interface design, usability testing, database design, software programming, user acceptance testing, rollout, promotion, and analysis of results from these activities.

He has done writing, internet marketing, and programming work, as an employee and for clients, as well as hired and managed staff and consultants to perform these tasks.

His professional blog, ReachCustomersOnline.com, published since 2002, ranks high in Google for important but lightly documented internet marketing topics, for example, how to code html email newsletters.

Tim also created and manages RedWrangler.com, an online service that helps web professionals manage client work. Red Wrangler allows anyone to publish websites easily, publish a Contact Us page then manage leads from that page, and store logins for Google Analytics, email delivery services, shopping carts, and website control panels. Tim created Red Wrangler to manage his clients after getting fed up with the chaos of clients, tools, leads, and logins.

Tim's combination of business, creative, and technical skills make him a perfect fit for organizations in need of staff or consultants who work well with all disciplines in their company. He understands first hand how business, technology, creative, and support people can mix to make great products and services. He is very entrepreneurial.

Tim works in Phoenix, the San Francisco Bay Area, and New York City.

technical writing and editing, content management, lead management, online help creation, web content creation, documentation, processes and procedures, web blogs, web publishing, marketing communications, software documentation, usability, information architecture, interface design, internet strategy, presentations, database design, software programming
