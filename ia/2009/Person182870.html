Paul Denlinger’s Summary

Based in Beijing from August 2007 where I am consulting with startups and Internet startups in the fields of strategic marketing and market positioning globally.
Moved to Shanghai in April 2007 to work as marketing/public relations consultant for Chinese and multinational companies, with special emphasis on technology companies and technology startups.
Business consultant for the Shanghai Jiaotong University Business Club of Silicon Valley at http://www.jtubiz.com, a business club for entrepreneurs.
Consultant with hands-on experience working in Greater China at senior management levels.
Supervised early investment deal (2000) in Shanda Interactive Entertainment (NasdaQ:SNDA), China's largest online entertainment company.
Have experience in retail consumer marketing and advertising and Internet companies in China. Fluent in Mandarin Chinese (speak, read and write).
Helps companies expand and build their presence in China.
Member of Institute of Management Consultants and National Speakers' Association.
For more information, please visit www.china-ready.com.
