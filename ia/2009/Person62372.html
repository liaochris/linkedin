Paul deSousa’s Summary

Paul brings business acumen and success to the process of management, assessment, design, development, implementation and evaluation of organizational performance improvement interventions.

Using the slogan, “Innovating Performance Improvement,” and an uncommon blend of communication skill, creative ability plus business and technology skill, he has built and managed pioneering performance improvement and organizational development initiatives for: BetterInvesting, Ford Asia Pacific and Africa, E*Trade, AmeriTrade, Charles Schwab, SBC, Docent and others.

Paul has a Masters Degree with distinction in Performance Improvement and Instructional Design from the University of Michigan.
