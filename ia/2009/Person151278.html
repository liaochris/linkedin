Mark A Santillo’s Summary

Providing programs and services that set the standard for innovation, creativity, and responsiveness, informed by the understanding that all stakeholders want the same two things: to feel valued for their contributions, and to perceive value in the services they receive.

I have broad experience in academic administration (student services and admissions), customer service, marketing, and especially program development and management in the non-profit sector. I have coordinated a workforce development program held on-site at corporate locations in seven states, managed a multi-faceted marketing plan, crafted and sold corporate sponsorships, managed programs from conception through execution, and created content for national trade shows and consumer conventions. I have developed programs for and with manufacturers, retailers, freelance creatives, community groups, national and regional non-profits. I have also developed fee-for-service agreements with major corporations, and worked closely with the Hospitality industry and Convention and Visitors Bureaus.

I take my work very seriously, but not myself. I like to foster an energetic, fun, creative environment, where team members work supportively to further the mission of the organization, placing emphasis on people and relationships over policies and procedures.
