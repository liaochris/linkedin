Paulino Dias’s Summary

I've started my career at Shell Cabo Verde as focal point of the company in the island of Santo Antao. One year after, I was moved to capital Praia, to coordenate LPG division. In 2006 I was appointed as Marketing Manager, but decided to leave the company in 2007 to hold a top executive position in a public real estate company.

Since August 2008 I'm an executive member of the management board of the second biggest bank in Cabo Verde, Caixa Economica, where I coordinate the commercial area, marketing, organization and quality management, and micro-credit division.

In Abril 2009 I was sellected to attend the "Desmond Tutu Leadership Program 2009" an iniciative of the African Leadership Institute (www.alinstitute.org) and Oxford University, aiming to identify and network young potential future african leaders.

As a Consultant, I've coordinated important projects for external investors in Cape Verde, companies and the Government. I've also leaded the team that prepared the Tourism Master Plan of Cape Verde, and I've participated in the project "Business Opportunities in Cape Verde", among others.

Member of Rotary Club Maria Pia da Praia
