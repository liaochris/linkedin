Russell Reeder’s Summary

As President and Chief Executive Officer at LibreDigital, Russell P. Reeder is responsible for the vision, direction and strategic growth of the fastest-growing digital media service company in the publishing vertical. As a business leader and entrepreneur, Russell enjoys a dynamic reputation - in starting up and spearheading successful technology companies.

Most recently, Mr. Reeder served as president and COO of NxTV, leading the third largest IVPOD provider in the hospitality vertical, which was named to the Inc. Magazine Fast 500 list in 2007. Before joining NxTV, Reeder founded RightsLine, Inc., a company that provides the most-used Software as a Service (SaaS) legal copyright licensing system in the Media and Entertainment industries. Previously, Reeder spent over 12 years in technology sales and software development roles at leading global companies including, Oracle, American Management Systems and Mobile Oil.

Russell P. Reeder is a graduate of James Madison University in Harrisonburg, VA where he completed a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Information Systems and is a member of the Young Entrepreneurs Organization (YEO). In 2005 he was named Entrepreneur of the Year by the Southern California Software Council.

Digital Publishing, IPVOD, DRM, Digital Content, Software, Content Licensing, Sales, Motivational Speaker, Complex Negotiation Training, Change Management
