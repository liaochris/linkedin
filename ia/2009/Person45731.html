Chris Whittam’s Summary

I am a hands-on senior manager with experience in all aspects of starting and running a business. The company that still bears my name grew from 5 staff to 50 plus staff in 4 years, building annual turnover from zero to £3M profitably and to the business plan.

I have had a great deal of experience and success in motivating, directing and leading staff to bring out the best in them for the long term good of the company.

I am keen to expand that expertise and am looking to work with other companies who would benefit from increasing the performances of their internal and/or external sales teams.
