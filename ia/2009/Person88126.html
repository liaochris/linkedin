Thomas A. Walters [www.goldcrossofamerica.com]’s Summary

Gold Cross of America/International, Inc. is the Foremost Non-Denominational Faith-Based Not-For-Profit 501 (c) (3) Membership Organization. The Organization was founded in 1983 and reorganized in 2006 and 2009 for the purpose of providing assistance to homeowners that are in jeopardy of losing their homes to foreclosure. Gold Cross of America works to provide free counseling services to assist homeowners in their time of greatest need: when they are facing the loss of their homes.

Over one million homes were lost in this country due to foreclosure last year alone. Three million families are in jeopardy of losing their homes to foreclosure this year. To stem the rising tide of homes lost, it is our purpose to provide immediate assistance to homeowners and to save as many homes as possible. Our mission is to "Save the American Dream by Preventing the American Nightmare" (tm) of home foreclosure.
