Ace Le’s Summary

I am a communications executive with a strong focus on advertising, branding and corporate citizenship.

In 2006, together with my friends, I set up the non-profit Gentle Fund Organization (GFO) to support underprivileged Vietnamese children in Vietnam on the educational front.

As the Director of Marketing & Communications, I managed all communicative channels at both corporate and project levels. Also, as the Head of CSR Programs, I successfully implemented a collaborative project with Singapore Exchange in Ho Chi Minh city (and that was how I got to join its force soon afterwards). Other collaborations were with DBS Bank, Sun Microsystems, CapitaLand, etc.

Currently, I am a Senior Associate in Marketing Communications at Singapore Exchange, doing what I love :).
