Simon Brender’s Summary

I started working immediately after finishing college and found a position with a Brand Management company (Harkess-Ord) in London, following only 6-months with the company I stumbled across a software development company, Sphere Business Group Ltd (rebranded as Coretexa in 2003), who offered me a Project Co-ordinator's role.

After a few months with Sphere I was sponsered by the company to study towards a BA and help improve my understanding of the business analysis tools and methodologies.

Since then, I've gained experience across a number of sectors in both London and Dubai and have obtained certification as an ITIL Service Manager (version 2) and ITIL Expert (version 3).

I've gained a solid understanding of all aspects of Programme, Project and Service Management, have worked with all levels of management within both SME's and large multinational/international corporations and have extensive experience of the PRINCE2 and ITIL frameworks.
