Nick Dixon’s Summary

Involved in the print and direct marketing industry for over 30 years covering the full spectrum of communication channels through a range of different businesses involved in database marketing, insight and analytics, on line marketing, print, print management, fulfilment and creative services.

Completed MBO, MBI, acquisitions, private equity funding, start ups, purchase of distressed assets, turn arounds and corporate trade sales to realise shareholder value.

Lateral Group has a turnover of £54m and employs 400 people. It offers clients a range of products and services that cover both on line and off line marketing techniques.

Lateral Group company capabilities span data hygiene and analysis services; email, web and mobile marketing; highly customisable print services, both web and digital: print management and channel management both on line and off line.

Combined together we offer Marketing Communications Management (MCM).
