eva stuckmann’s Summary

EDUCATION
I started with studying languages. Then I discovered to be quite interested in the ethymology of words, in their different meanings in the course of time and their relation to the life and the behaviour of man. So I studied psychology and psycoanalysis undergoing myself a professional analysis.

During my work as a psycoanalyst I was often concerned with the amount of time needed for a person to get rebalanced. I started to look for other methods. This got me to NeuroLinguistic Program (NLP) and Pranic Healing.

NLP is a method for developping personality and the necessary resources to achieve personal excellence. It doesn't focus on the causes of a problem but focuses on naming a goal and on finding the way to achieve it.

Pranic healing (prana=energy) is a method first of all to induce relaxation in a person. Complete relaxation is a very important condition for our health and for healing.



WORK
I'm a NLP Problem Solver and Pranic Healer.
I treat whatver makes you feel uncomfortable and ill-balanced...

I treat psychological and psychosomatic issues. I treat illness when presumably it has a psycosomatic origin. Where does illness start? In the beginning there are just negative emotions: fear anger sadness worry ... body and mind are both involved.

Concerned with a big emotional problem a person can either become desperate of it risking illness, he can live with it accepting the limitations it puts on him or he can try to resolve it either by himself or seeking help and advice.

That's where my work comes in.


(A note on my way of doing Pranic Healing: since years I'm doing concentration and yoga exercises to improve my pranic power. I work in compliance with the rules stated by the German "Dachverband Geistiges Heilen" (Main Association for Spiritual Healing). For ex. I don't diagnose, I don't promise complete healing, I explain that I'm not a doctor).
