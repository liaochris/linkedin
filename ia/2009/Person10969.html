Ari Massoudi’s Summary

Knowledge and innovation are the keys to guarantee our common future. In this purpose, my ambition is to bring my support, my energy and my training at the service of your evolution.

Because of the globalization of business, commercial competition is everyday harder and harder. To maintain a leadership position, or just to survive, companies must propose rapidly and efficiently to their clients new attractive products and/or services. For this, they have to innovate, to evolve by creating new market opportunities or by adapting themselves to changing markets.

From the market towards the industrial feasibility, my objective will be to facilitate your entrepreneurial innovation.

The management of an innovative project can be summarized as follows:

- Plan and manage the transfer of technology from the academic research to the company, or conversely help the company to innovate in conjunction with public research and / or develop a strategy for appropriate R & D
- Ensure the transformation of the invention / creation to its ultimate commercialization; identify potential markets, assist in positioning strategy
- Reinject the capital "knowledge and know-how" produced during the innovation process to the next innovation process

- And of course, find money to finance the innovation process (public or private funds)

http://www.aread.eu

This activity requires scientific competence but also and especially an understanding of the relevant business, market and the specific innovative projects by nature risky.

If my managerial training and my scientist skills can serve and support your company, I will be highly motivated to bring my contribution to your success.
