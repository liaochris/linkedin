Steven Bryson’s Summary

I have built and sold several companies during my career. Global Electronic Technology, Inc. where I currently serve as Founder, Chairman of the Board, and CEO has enabled me to employ, insure and assist a large number of men, women and children.

I dropped out of Kingsbury High School in 1977 and joined the United States Army as a paratrooper serving with the 82nd Airborne Division. I obtained a GED shortly after arriving at Ft. Bragg NC. My career has spanned 3 years of military service and 28 years in sales of Real Estate and Financial Services. I have been honored to train and deploy more than 2000 sales people.

The last 15 years I have moved up the ranks within the financial transaction industry also referred to as Credit Card Processing or Bank Card Processing to name a few. The positions held were as a commissioned sales person with Financial Alliance which at the time was a wholly owned subsidiary of Deluxe Data Services, as a District Manager and a Regional Manager with Lynk Systems and later as Senior Vice President of a competitor and as an owner of First Financial Services.

My crowning achievement will always be as a father to Scott and Samantha Bryson. They are the bravest and most honorable people I have ever known. Scott recently returned from 15 months of direct combat in Iraq’s Sunni Triangle and Samantha is currently serving as a English Teacher by day and a care giver to HIV infected children in Addis Ababa Ethiopia. I am in awe of my Son and Daughter. My new wife Patricia (Trish) Bryson is a major nerd and a gentle woman of the first order. I am proud and honored to be her husband. I am one of the rare people who is lucky enough to still have and to often visit my child hood friends. Lastly (and it is also an achievement) to have an American Labrador named Bella who is actually well trained and one loving pup.

Steve Bryson
