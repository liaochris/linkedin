Annika Hipple’s Summary

I am a freelance writer and editor with more than 13 years of experience in editorial work for publications and corporate, nonprofit, and academic clients. Projects have included articles, technical reports, newsletters, press releases, presentations, web content, proposals, direct mail packets, and other marketing and fundraising materials. My writing has appeared in local, regional, and national magazines and newspapers.

Though I work with clients in many fields, I specialize in:
- travel
- environment
- international development
- multicultural issues
- Scandinavia
- Latin America

I offer crisp, clear writing with style and flair to help my clients target a particular audience. I am particularly good at bringing together multiple sources of information and transforming complex ideas into clean, accessible copy.

As an editor and proofreader, I am an expert at fixing grammatical errors, catching typos, and smoothing out stylistic issues to create a quality editorial product. I can clean up a document without losing the individuality of the writer or streamline a product with multiple authors so that it speaks with a single voice.

I possess excellent research skills and am efficient, accurate, and attentive to the needs of my clients. I also offer editorial services in Swedish and Spanish.

In addition, I am a skilled travel photographer. See my website for an online portfolio of images from around the world.
