Joe Wagner’s Summary

I have been in sales | marketing management with the Whitmore Group since 1998. My client's are from Virginia, Baltimore, Washington D.C., New York to California. I am a solution provider to printing and communication projects.
In 2005, I saw the value in a networking group for women in the communications profession. We started B'more Creative, a group that now numbers 435+ members.

Joe Wagner’s Specialties:

I work with marketing, creative and production teams for small companies, large corporations, national advertising agencies and design firms. My expertise is in the project management of printing projects from digital and commercial offset to mailing and fulfillment, Giclee and wide format. Simply put my job is to make it happen and at the same time keep us within budget. From new business and mentoring, developing marketing strategies, identifying and developing opportunities.
