Thomas DePaoli’s Summary

I'm a senior online marketing professional, consultant, and speaker with over 8 years experience in Internet marketing campaign management, e-commerce and e-marketing strategy, and technology and media research. Colleagues and clients say I'm energetic, growth-orientated, a creative problem solver, and results and data focused.

● Driving E-Business Performance in Tough Economic Times

I combine strong multichannel and client-focused experience to help Internet retailers, international businesses, start-ups, catalog marketers, B2B businesses, and technology organizations achieve peak performance in their online marketing and direct marketing efforts.

I help plan integrated marketing campaigns and programs, build change-oriented project teams, create marketing strategy initiatives, and purchase online advertising. Companies I've worked with and clients I've helped have achieved top rankings in the Internet Retailer Top 500 and Forbes Best of the Web. Most importantly, they've increased revenue and gained market share in highly competitive industries.

I've worked on projects focused on Search Marketing, Social Media, Customer Acquisition, International Business, Market Research, Affiliate Marketing, Web Analytics, Lead Generation, E-Commerce, and Brand Development.

● Focused Technology and Media Investment Research and Emerging Market Research

I help investment firms, consumer marketers, B2B businesses, and technology organizations research markets and companies in North America, Europe, and emerging markets in Latin America - especially Brazil. My clients include top private equity and venture capital firms.

● Global Marketing Insights

Ask me about Brazil, Latin America, and Italy and you will be in for an interesting conversation. I am a native English speaker, but also speak Brazilian Portuguese at an intermediate level and Spanish at a high beginner level.
