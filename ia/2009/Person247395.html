William Blesch’s Summary

Imagine your company, band or product on national Television, featured in print magazines, or in newspapers across the country!

Companies need to sell their products or services, and bands and solo artists want to get signed by major labels and sell CD's and related merchandise.

The benefits of exposure in relevant markets ought to be clear to anyone wanting to advance their visibility and awareness to those in the general public!

This is where William Blesch can help!

William represents an award winning team of Public Relations and Film/Television professionals, a virtual ad agency that can put together projects no matter where in the world they happen to originate.

Our corporate clients have included the premier, online dating company, Match.com as well as Maidenform Bras. In terms of Musicians, we are proud to include among our clients Grammy Award winning artists, and we are actively looking for musicians who are either on the verge of breaking out, or are already known in the regions they`re in.

We are also looking for new business clients as well.

Our combined skill-sets are those which you will find bring you the knock-out results you desire. More than this, we work with the client to achieve the artistic goals they have in mind.

We offer the following dependent on budget:

1. Development and distribution of press kit: Includes: bio, press releases, photo layout and a page that shows recent events
2.Logo Design
3.Website Development: graphics and programming
4. Launch Parties and Events
5. Promotion in print media
6. Television Commercials, PSA's and Advertisements
7.Writing of sales materials to print, radio and television markets
8.Promoting and ensuring that television advertisements air on televised markets
9. MTV/VH1/BET quality music video production
10. Promotion of music videos to acquisition executives at the aforementioned networks

Let's discover new and amazing synergies together!
