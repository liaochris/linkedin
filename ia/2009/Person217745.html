Simeon Simeonov’s Summary

Simeon Simeonov is the founder of FastIgnite where he helps entrepreneurs shape promising ideas, raise capital, build teams and execute. Sim is also co-founder and interim CTO of San Francisco-based Plinky and a technology advisor at Polaris Venture Partners in Boston. Sim blogs at simeons.wordpress.com and lives in the Greater Boston area with his wife, son and an adopted dog named Tye.

Simeon Simeonov’s Specialties:

Guiding early-stage startups from concept through fundraising to revenue and enabling later-stage companies to achieve their potential.
