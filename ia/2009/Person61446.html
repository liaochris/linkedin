Denise Pirrotti Hummel’s Summary

Denise Hummel was a nationally recognized litigator and mediator in the employment law arena. She was named one of the Top Twenty Young Attorneys in the United States by the American Bar Association in 1992.

For the past decade, through her company, Universal Consensus, she has specialized in services for international business that serve to promote consensus and understanding within a business or organization, as well as with clients, business partners and adversaries. These services include executive coaching and corporate cross-cultural training related to any individual or corporation doing business or seeking to do business abroad; mediation of any dispute which involves the domestic or international workplace; mediation training to equip in-house staff to mediate in the workplace; liability prevention training related to diversity issues, and; speeches, both live and remote, related to all of the above issues. Denise L. Hummel, Attorney and Doctor of Jurisprudence, founded Universal Consensus as an international Alternative Dispute Resolution and Cross-Cultural Communications Consulting company, based on eighteen years of experience in employment law and international business.

Denise is also the Executive editor of an on-line travel magazine, JUST THE PLANET (link here) designed to transcend the barriers of the travel industry, through all the trials and tribulations of modern times.

Denise is the author of a book, SPEAK MILK. DRINK WINE. which is pending publication, and has written countless travel-related articles, has been quoted and been covered by the Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, The Denver Post, the Denver Business Journal, the New York Daily News, WVIP Cable Network, and NBC and was recently an honored speaker at the International Institute for Peace Through Tourism Global Summit in Pattaya, Thailand, where she spoke on the image restoration and recovery of destinations affected by terrorism and natural disaster.
