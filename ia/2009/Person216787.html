Shirley Tang, SPHR, MBA’s Summary

Hi, my name is Shirley Tang. I'm a Senior Human Resources Professional from Hong Kong with permanent work authorization and no visa sponsorship required in USA. I have over 17 years of Human Resources Management and Office Administration experience in Greater China region.

I hold an MBA from University of Warwick, UK. I'm a Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) certified by the Human Resources Certification Institute, USA.

My previous employers included IBM, Johnson & Johnson, Wyeth Pharmaceuticals, etc. I managed HR functions ranging from sales organizations to manufacturing operations in commercial sector and non-profit educational organization. Throughout my career, I have exhibited strong leadership, customer focus while building strategic business partnership that enabled me to build trusted HR teams in my previous employers.

As a true generalist, I enjoy many facets of human resources - taking special interest in compensation & benefits, performance management, corporate culture transformation, and staff development. My experience includes HRIS project, Staffing, Employee Relations, PRC Individual Income Tax Administration, Organizational Development and office renovation.

Ideally, I am looking for an HR position where I can apply my all-rounded HRM skills. I would like to work for an international corporation where I can take advantage of my Asian experience of HRM and tri-lingual skills in English, Mandarin and Cantonese.
