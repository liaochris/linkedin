Laurie-Ann Murabito, CPCC, ACC’s Summary

Laurie-Ann is a Results & Leadership Coach, speaker and trainer with more than fifteen years of experience. She is the founder and president of Results 2 Success, LLC, a personal and professional coaching organization that energizes, motivates and inspires clients to step up and achieve their desired results to succeed.

Laurie-Ann assists clients in identifying, planning and implementing strategies to achieve the next level of success. She coaches both individuals and businesses on how to “close the gap” between their current state and where they desire to be. Laurie-Ann is also a dynamic speaker and has worked with numerous organizations to provide powerful seminars that engage, entertain and challenge people to think differently on the path to success.

Laurie-Ann has been trained and credentialed through the Coaches Training Institute and the prestigious International Coaching Federation.

Laurie-Ann is also a published author and has written a variety of articles on topics such as stress management, relationship improvement, improving habits, careers and professional development.
