Trista Meehan’s Summary

Trista Meehan is a certified user experience strategist with 10 years of industry experience. She has worked with small and mid-sized businesses, as well as several Fortune 500 companies.

Whether she is leading a research initiative or designing the ultimate online (or offline) experience, Trista is a versatile leader who tackles each project with great attention to detail and enthusiasm for customer-centered design.

Usability testing, competitive analyses, ethnographic/field research, personas, website evaluations,web strategy, training/mentoring, new media communications
