Richard Blain, MBA, CRHA’s Summary

Specialist in human resources and management with more than 20 years experience in business, Richard Blain has worked in various industries, in particular in the retail trade, the mining sector and public transport. He is known for his management skills and his ability to implement practices that increase organizational efficiency and performance growth and changing environments.
