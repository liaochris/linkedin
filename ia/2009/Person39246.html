Catherine Patterson’s Summary

Catherine joined Kurman Communications in January of 2008, and has since worked with a number of clients including A Silver Lining Foundation and Sweet Baby Ray’s. Catherine’s responsibilities include client and media relations, social media programs, account management, creative writing, campaign research, and new business development. Much of her time at Kurman has been spent building strong connections within the media industry, which she utilizes on a daily basis.

Catherine is a graduate of Michigan State University, where she received a Bachelor of Arts in Communication, specializing in public relations. She also spent a summer abroad, studying Mediterranean sociology at the University of the Aegean in Greece.

Catherine is a member of PRSA and YP Chicago.
