Heather Rast’s Summary

16 years marketing communications experience earned at AAAA-agencies across the midwest and south, building national and global consumer brands primarily in technology, healthcare, foodservice, and consumer packaged goods sectors.

Client work includes: Telecom/High Tech (IBM, Sony Ericsson, GTE Wireless); Healthcare (Columbia/HCA, Blue Cross/Blue Sheild of Tennessee, United HealthCare Plans of Florida, BroMenn Health System); Consumer Packaged Goods (The Iams Company, Schwan's); Foodservice (PepsiCo, Tyson, The Campbell's Company, Cracker Barrell Old Country Stores, Pioneer and White Lilly); Retail (Tractor Supply Company, National Auto/Truck Stops, Universal Tire Co.); and Utility (Alliant Energy).

I’m a sixteen-year steward and student of brand-building through the development and cultivation of consumer connections. I look for the intersection of relevance, differentiation, and needs fulfillment so that I can help craft holistic strategies that deliver organizational value and nurture consumer affinity. Scope includes all aspects of brand management, including: leading discovery, secondary research synthesis, evaluating IA, directing visual design, SEM, SEO, content and social media strategy, CRM planning.

Heather Rast’s Specialties:
