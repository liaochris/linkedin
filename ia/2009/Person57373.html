Davide Lorenzato’s Summary

AS CHOIR AND ORCHESTRA CONDUCTOR:
How to prepare orchestras and choirs for concerts is my main ability and skill: I can work very well with professional and amateur player and singers, especially with young people. Since 1994 I've being playing or conducting a lot of music genres (ancient, baroque, classical, romantic, contemporary, jazz, rock, ethnic, folk) with an average of fifty concert in a year.
AS CONTINUO PLAYER:
My ability in orchestrascore reading and my experiences in baroque music allow me to improvise the continuo part with organ or cembalo in function of the group, interpretation and hall acustic I'm playing with.
My motto is "You should not hear that the continuo is playing: you should only notice if it's not playing!"
MY LECTURE "INTONAT" for conductors, choirs, musicians: "What's intonation? Which factors do form it? How can a person, a choir or an instrumental group improve it? Can intonation be an expressive resource like dinamics, agogic and sound colour?" Doing a journey through pure intonation, overtones, acustic, phisiology and music INTONAT will try to give an answer to those questions. More info on my website.

Davide Lorenzato’s Specialties:

Historical informed performance practice, natural intonation and eartraining, music theory, conducting, continuo playing.
