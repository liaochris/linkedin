Sandi Logan’s Summary

Sandi is a longtime journalist practitioner who moved to issues management and public affairs in the foreign service and related agencies with a commitment to high standards of professionalism and integrity. He joined the Immigration Department at a time of great cultural and policy change, and has been a part of the leadership team's efforts to re-tool the staff culture embracing a renewed commitment to fair and reasonable dealings with clients, and openess and transparency in its operations.
