David Geertz’s Summary

My primary objective is to incubate ideas into brands that can be attached to tangible assets and business models.

Keeping it clean (non violent and above board) is really important to me.

I have expert knowledge in the media sectors relating to all aspects of DVD, film development, and film finance.

I created the Principal Protected Private Venture Fund (P3V Fund) that allows accredited investors to earn better than average yields and participate in potential upside of riskier projects, and still protect their capital.

My past work:

Pixeltoys.com - DVD authoring house, replication factory, and packaging brokerage. Clients included - Warner Music, Warren Miller Films, Nettwerk Records, Fox Loriber, UBC Medical Labs, Classic Video, Vancouver Art Gallery, and Abbey Road Studios.

Mighty Empire Entertainment - Film Company. Worked with the two partners to help form a specialty brand of horror film content called "Modern Evil". The brand holds several film properties, and trademarks under its horror franchise.

I am currently seeking films that are finished and that need distribution in the US and Canada for all markets. If you have a library of product or a single finished film, television series, or specialty interest DVD please get in touch.

David Geertz’s Specialties:

P3V Funds, preproduction bridge finance, packaging, international sales, arbitrage, theatrical distribution, DVD distribution, media recycling and destruction.
