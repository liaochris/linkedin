Bram Van Londersele’s Summary

I was born on the 28th of May 1986 in Aalst, Belgium.
After I grew up and had a lovely time in Meerbeke (Ninove), I went to study at the Ghent University.

At this moment I am a 2nd Master student in geomatics and surveying, so I am graduating next year.

My bachelor thesis (2008) dealed about 'airborne LIDAR hydrography'.

This year I am writing a master thesis. I monitored moving objects by using bluetooth, so called bluetooth tracking. The data collecting has already been executed. I was tracking the bluetooth devices at Rock Werchter in 2009.
Here for I we had an alliance with 2 Dutch companies: Flucon B.V. and Realworld Systems.
Now I have several months to analyse the data. I'm looking forward to query the data in DBMS's and GIS suites...

Besides a university student I am a part-time surveyor at Teccon bvba as well.

In my spare time I sport (volleyball, ski, cycling, running, fitness, squash), travel and I manage some LinkedIn Groups:
