Stella Boeva’s Summary

My research, writing and editing experience for publications of different scale and audiences is vast. So is my experience in public speaking and presentation in public: for the purposes of teaching, representing institutions/businesses, or advocating for groups and issues. My creative thinking and problem-solving capacity have always led employers to trust me with responsibilities way beyond those defined in the job description.

During my PhD career I discovered I was much happier when creating & making things happen - than when doing academic research & writing. I am excited, motivated & fulfilled when partaking in hands-on work and activities, which add to a concrete product/event and reach people. While I have enjoyed and excelled in teaching, I have found life as an academic to be too closed off. I wish to be applied in an industry that is more people-related - in terms of both end product and production process.

Work in communication, visual communication or cultural production would engage both my skills and my knowledge base. Hence, it is in this direction that I seek to develop professionally.

In the meantime I am doing my small bit for the green cause & for empowering people: www.bgbui.com.
