Diane Yarnell (dmyarnell@ grsi.com)’s Summary

GRSi is a woman-owned, small business that offers diverse engineering and technology resources to government, military, and high-tech clients. Attentive management and dedicated people have earned GRSi a reputation for high performance on strategic government contracts.

As a seasoned Recruiter in the DoD environment, Ms. Yarnell provides leadership for six full-time staff members dedicated to GRSi's recruitment efforts. She performs full life-cycle recruitment services to The Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory (APL), the National Institutes of Health (NIH), the National Naval Medical Center (NNMC), Bureau of Medicine and Surgery (BUMED), U.S. Army Medical Materiel Agency (USAMMA), and Space and Naval Warfare Systems Center (SPAWAR), among others. Within the past three years, Ms. Yarnell has led her team to successfully fill 460 out of 488 competed IDIQ requirements, giving them a 94% success rate. In addition, they have also maintained a 99% incumbent capture rate.
