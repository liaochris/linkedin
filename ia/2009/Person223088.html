Stephen Baishya’s Summary

My current role is focused on implementing business process management and customer experience management using tools drawn from a variety of approaches. I work with executives and senior managers to increase their understanding, develop their teams' understanding, and support their implementation plans. I also manage the design authority team responsible for designing our approach, including development of a business process strategy and architecture.

My previous role was in change management, where I was responsible for managing a team that used both business and IT skills and knowledge to help the business solve the right problems in the right way at the right time.

Prior to that, I was a technical specialist in IT strategy and architecture. My responsibilities were to ensure that business IT systems are robust and scaleable, and to develop IT strategies. My experience includes operating systems (Windows and Unix), software development (including J2SE/J2EE and VB), EAI (Websphere Application Server, Business Integration and MQ), systems design (UML), and databases (Oracle).

I have worked on a number of multi-million pound projects, most notably architecting and developing the middleware integration on Britannia's £60M award winnning Really Big Programme.
