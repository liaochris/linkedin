Jennifer Boggs’s Summary

My arrival at Scholastic has been the result of a roundabout journey, a career that has encompassed public relations, writing and editing, managing programs at a children's museum, working with teens, advocating for children's rights and, of course, Shakespeare. All roads led me to Scholastic where I currently enjoy using my sundry skills to promote educational technology, classroom magazines, the Scholastic Kids Press Corps, the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards and much more.

My professional and personal goals remain the same: to keep getting better at what I do, and to keep learning to do more.
