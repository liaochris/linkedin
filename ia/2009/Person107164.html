Joe Doyle’s Summary

Currently a principal at Apply 2 Save, a loss mitigation firm dedicated to helping homeowners in foreclosure save their homes. Executive in charge of all marketing and business development activities. Responsible for building a trusted brand, consumer acquisition, public relations, strategic marketing and business/corporate development.

Previously on the corporate development team at Fidelity National Real Estate Solutions and responsible for content, advertising and marketing partnerships for the FNRES consumer websites: Cyberhomes, HomeSeekers, BuyBankHomes and GoApply.

In 2005 joined Get Lower as part of the founding team as SVP of Marketing and Business Development. Responsible for all marketing and business development activities across a wide array of verticals including mortgage, real estate and insurance.

From 2001 to 2005 was a co-founder and led the marketing, corporate strategy and business development efforts at RealtyTrac. Instrumental in growing the company from 3 employees to over 180 and helping it gain a position as one of the top real estate web properties.

Previously, I spent several years as a strategy consultant working directly for the CEOs and executive teams of Fortune 100 companies such as McDonalds, HP, General Electric, et al

Online marketing, strategy, business development, traffic acquistion, usability and optimization. Expert in brand strategy and lead generation.
