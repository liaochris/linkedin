Lelah Baker-Rabe’s Summary

Lelah Baker-Rabe is helping you organize. Lelah combines her lifelong interests of organization, entrepreneurship and writing through her professional organizing business and blog. Using skills honed working both from home and in corporate settings, Lelah is an expert in transforming conventional and cluttered home offices into usable and dynamic creative workspaces. She also specializes in teaching people how to organize and backup their digital information. One of her organizing passions is helping clients get out of storage units they may be paying hundreds of dollars a month for, by showing them how to sell, recycle and donate unused possessions.

Lelah is a graduate of Wesleyan University in Middletown, Connecticut. At Wesleyan, she dedicated her time to improving student life through residential programming and received the Heideman Award recognizing her services to the Wesleyan community. She is an active member of the Wesleyan Alumni Admission Volunteers. Having lived in Los Angeles since 2005, Lelah has a keen appreciation of car culture, and the specific organizing needs of Angelinos. As a writer, she understands the importance of an inspiring workspace. Lelah avidly learns about new organizing trends and topics through reading, seminars and workshops.
