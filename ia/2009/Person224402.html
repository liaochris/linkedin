Steven Heaney’s Summary

Steven Heaney has eighteen years of experience in a variety of domains within Business and Technical Consulting, and Information and Communications Technology.

Steven currently manages the Risk Management & Security practice within the Atos Consulting Private Sector business. He is Head of Security Architecture, and leads teams delivering major information security and risk programmes for international clients in market sectors including Transport, Financial Services, Energy and Retail. He also has a leading role in the Atos Origin technical architecture community, delivering information security training and mentoring to Atos Origin solution architects.

During his career he has undertaken a wide range of roles ranging from software developer and analyst positions to enterprise architect on large e-commerce projects as well as project and programme management and consulting.

Prior to joining Atos Consulting, Steven worked for Schlumberger, the oilfield services company, in leadership positions for several large software development projects including project management, software process and quality assurance functions.

Steven is a qualified CISSP and CISM, and has a Masters degree in Computer Science. He also holds a patent for an Intranet Publishing System.
