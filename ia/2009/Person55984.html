Dave Bantz’s Summary

Dave Bantz is an architectural designer from La Jolla, California and currently resides in Silverlake, Los Angeles. Among many architectural projects, he has participated in exhibition designs for both the Venice Biennale 2009, with Ball + Nogues Studio, and the Invitational SCI-Arc Gallery Series, with Hodgetts + Fung.

Bantz received his Bachelors of Environmental Design (B.EVND) from The University of Colorado - Boulder, where he was selected for a year of architectural study in Copenhagen, Denmark. He is an avid designer, reader, surfer, and musician. Bantz is currently attending the graduate program at the Southern California Institute of Architecture (SCI-Arc).
