Stephan Lomp’s Summary

Lomp is the german artist, illustrator and designer Stephan Lomp. He has been working since 1996 for print and web agencies, publishing houses and event marketing companies like:

BBDO, Grey, Ogilvy & Mather, Vodafone, V2A, BBDO Interone, Argonauten G2, XEO, pro in space, comspace, kogag, uniplan, Die Favoriten, Castenow, Die Produktion, Palladium, max.sense, nu-communications and best webnews

And for clients like: Daimler, Sony, Toshiba, Nokia, Vodafone, Asics, Coca-Cola, ARD, WDR, E-Plus, Henkel, Wella, Dunlop, LBS and Payback

Born 1973 in Düsseldorf, Germany
At the age of 5 blueprinted superman comics
Studied visual communications at FH Düsseldorf
His belly is rapidly growing in size
He once ate a whole chicken
Currently likes: Hot Chip, Go Team, Joanna Newsom and rediscovered Devo
His all-time favorites are: Sonic Youth, Alan Moore and Chris Ware

e is a co-founder of the underground comic anthology “Herrensahne” and published 10 issues with fellow illustrators like Max Fiedler, Tobi Dahmen, Roman Klonek, Leo Leowald and others. Together they had exhibitions in Luxemburg, Luzern and Cologne.

With Marc Ewert he collaborated as HFURHUR for two comics in Witteks “panik elektro” and one short movie called “Brain Projection”
