    Writer, Thinker & Entrepreneur

    (Internet industry)

    August 2008 — Present (11 months)

    I am an experienced entrepreneur turned writer who bring along the experience of working in fields like, education, technology, media, web content writing, website designing, graphic design & animation, marketing communication agency. I have a clear, concise and easy-to-understand writing style. Writing is thrill and freelance writing is doubly so. It gives you an opportunity to encounter new subjects and learn about it. This indeed is a learning experience and what shape your learning curve will take depends upon the challenges you take.

    I am here to accept whatever challenge is thrown my way. I believe as a writer, I have this basic ability to read and gauge the scope of any given subject. Like any good writer, I also have an intuitive understanding that helps me understand what is important and what is not for the reader of any article. And the research takes care of rest of the things.
    It has been more than 10 years since I am using search engines, from Altavista to Cuil but still love searching Google as a habit and may be because I know the way to search on Google.

    I am sick of reading mediocre contents and ill-researched articles written in a language filled with unnecessary filler words. This will hurt you and your business.

    I still go by the rules set by Prof. William Strunk Jr. in his remarkable book, elements of style. Like Prof. Strunk Jr. I also detest inserting any useless word in my articles. I believe, writing should be crisp and compact and this is the force that pulls and retains the visitors.

    A good article will make your image and will get some loyal visitors to your website and/or blog. This is my objective and this is what I strive to achieve for my clients.

    I've been writing for various clients on different topics; from casinos to paintings, from marketing dance schools to poker, from branding services to direct mail marketing strategies, from casinos to home improvements.
