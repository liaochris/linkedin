Tom Paffen’s Summary

Since 1998 active in Venture Capital with a successful track record and focus on growing innovative high tech companies. Background in telecom/technology since 1985. Founded Byblos Ventures and co-founded Capital-C Ventures (fka Residex Ventures) in 2003 as a spin-out of Residex B.V. (Private Equity Captive of Achmea). Active in investments and advisory services, Byblos has the reputation of being the oldest city in the world, attractive because of the successive layers of debris resulting from centuries of human habitation, reflecting our approach towards Ventures: more than just providing capital, investing in long lasting relationships balancing people, profit and planet.

Available for fund management/investment/venturing/advisory/board opportunities.
