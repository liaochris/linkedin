Vanessa Siu’s Summary

I have three years of experience working with a family friend’s import and distribution company, whole selling tea and tea accessories from Japan, China and Germany. With them, I got a chance to create all their catalogues, price lists, and attend the Canadian Gift and Tableware Association Trade Show where we were the exhibitor! I also got to see a company go through a selling process when the owners decided to sell Herbal Republic to another Tea Company. Today I’m the administrative assistant at Delta-Q Technologies Corporation.

Through Capilano University, I also got a chance to moderate a panel of discussion with industries leader around the topic of “Leadership”. The best experience so far is working as an “agency” and with 2 others in my class. We had a chance to work with a real life client and providing him with a creative strategy, media and promotional plan for his product.

Outside of my work and my education, I am a lawn bowler. Through the game of bowls, I have also gained may opportunities to work on some promotional materials for Bowls BC Association, the Vancouver and District Association, and the Pacific Indoor Bowls Club.
