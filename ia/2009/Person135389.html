Kevin Thompson’s Summary

I'm a corporate attorney with in-house legal experience. I'm proficient with regulatory issues affecting the network marketing industry and I'm also well versed with litigation, intellectual property matters, and contract drafting and negotiations.

Currently, Team is involved in commercial litigation against Amway. As a result, I've had the privilege of working with some of the finest lawyers in the country.

I enjoy blending emerging technology trends with the practice of law. The Web 2.0 world is fascinating and I aim to introduce technology solutions to the legal profession. It starts with Advanced Advocates, LLC, which is scheduled to launch in January of 2009. With a fabulous team of programmers, we've built an exclusive professional network for law students full of relevant tools.

I blog, write articles, and share media at http://www.nextgenerationlaw.com.com

I serve on the board of directors for two nonprofit companies: All Grace Outreach and EP Books, Inc.

My background is in litigation and transactional advisement. I mix my passion for emerging technology with my ability to solve problems for my clients.
