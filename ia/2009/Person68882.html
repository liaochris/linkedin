Ernest Lewis’s Summary

Dedicated to helping businesses and organizations increase performance levels and Create a Better Work Place. We help organizations identify what they need to do to improve and then help them make it happen through strategic planning, executive coaching, professional development, continuous improvement, and team building programs.

We also serve the community by working with numerous non-profit organizations and provide advice and expertise in organizational development including 501C3, board development, volunteer recruitment, community relations, fundraising, grant writing, strategic planning, and management and leadership skills.

Our consultants are excellent presenters and skilled facilitators providing for productive and memorable learning experiences. We help clients get better results while finding joy and energy in their work and their life. Programs such as team building, professional development and even continuous improvement programs are more fun, exciting and much more productive.

Our approach is to to identify deeply rooted organizational problems, develop the best solutions, and implement those solutions through employee involvement and commitment. Based on fundamental principles of accountability, mutual trust and mutual respect, even the most significant barriers to high performance can be overcome!

Ernest Lewis’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, continuous improvement programs, team building, leadership development and executive coaching through customized programs that include workshops, seminars, and key notes as well as team and one-on-one support.
