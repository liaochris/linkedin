Joan Abraham’s Summary

I have an extensive background in new media, strategies that crate brand distinctiveness through the online environment. My distinction is combining fashion, entertainment and media to speak to women with sophsitication and style, the universal language for a global economy.

My specific skills are creating and executing innovative programs using new media communication tools, identifying and managing the appropriate talent for the content needed, and delivering on the promise of high quality, breakthrough communication content.

The unique interactive fashion programming reflects a sedctive, hip attitude cultivating the best of creative thinking and technology. It capitalizes on the emotional appeal of fantasy and good storytelling.

My understanding of interactive merchandising and the ability to create the sedction of the sell at the point of purchase benefits the new business development of any communications agency or product looking to grow it's distribution in a global marketplace.

Joan Abraham’s Specialties:

Idea creation, talent selection and management, identifying and enhancing awareness of the unique DNA of a particular brand.
My point of distinction is the use of fashion and style in communicating a Brand image.
