Kirt McMaster’s Summary

A dynamic and highly skilled digital media executive with significant experience in online multi-platform consumer services, mobile entertainment, social media, UGC and broad platform convergence. Particular expertise in the development of new businesses from concept stage through to set up, launch and business as usual. Key characteristics include those of high motivation, commitment and professionalism combined with a passion for cultural transformation brought on by an increasingly connected world. McMaster possesses an intimate knowledge of the networked economy and its impact across the tapestry of global enterprise. This experience has evolved through many years of managing engagements encompassing digital media, internet strategy/development and e-commerce to mobile data/commerce and convergence.
