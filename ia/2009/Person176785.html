Norbert Barszczewski’s Summary

Norbert Barszczewski is a web developer and internet marketing expert. His area of expertise includes all aspects of professional, high quality and dynamically driven modern web development. His comprehensive internet marketing approach is demonstrated by a number of successfully completed HTML email marketing campaigns, online advertising management and vast search engine optimization experience. No stranger to graphic design, computer and network security, not to mention social networking, Norbert is your best bet for a project well done and completed on time with high quality customer service all along.

Norbert Barszczewski, together with his wife Joanna, is the founder and president of JP NetQuest, Inc. The company was founded in Chicago in 2007 and has over the years served a great number of successful local businesses. Norbert Barszczewski serves as the chief web developer and internet marketing expert. In 2009 JP NetQuest was accredited by the Better Business Bureau of Chicago and Northern Illinois.

Norbert Barszczewski is also active in the local community. He tries to give back at least as much as the community helps him. He has served as the director of a business networking group and remains active in a number of other organizations of non-profit character. His life motto: You can never go wrong with helping others.
