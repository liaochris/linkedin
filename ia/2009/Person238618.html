Nguyen Thanh Trung’s Summary

I studied engineering physics in College of Technology, Vietnam National University, Hanoi, focused in embedded system and nanotechnology. After working for Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology as researcher of nanotechnology, I moved to FPT Software to work in embedded software field for Japanese market. I were leader of The Realtime OS Nuclear laboratory in FPT Software (TRON Lab). This lab enhances ES technology of FPT Software to look for embedded system outsourcing opportunities from US, EU, Japan.
In addition, I worked for BinhAnh Electronics Ltd as sale engineer and Quality Assurance Manager. I'm in charge to develop marketing system in technical view and develop quality assurance system using adapted CMMi5 and BS7700 standard.
Now I’m Technical Director in V-Com JSC. Currently, my main reponsibillites is build telemedicine system for diabetes. In the near future, I will deploy some other e-health application in Vietnam.
In brief, as applied physicist, I can involve in any fields of science and technology which can be applied in the real life, from nanotechnology, electronics, embedded system technology, information technology, telecommunication, e-healthcare ...
