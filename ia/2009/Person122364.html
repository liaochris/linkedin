Jake Romigh’s Summary

I have held an internship in which I managed a team of around 10 student employees to service the college campus and its Novell network. During this time, I also created computer images and contributed to documentation for the education of incoming technicians.
My senior thesis was implementing a procedurally random level generation algorithm for computer gaming and comparing it to old-fashioned data storage. The extra challenge was that the generated level would be as fun to play as a level that was designed by hand.
I also worked with Archive Entertainment and gained experience with game development and design, as well as developing new projects for the community to take part in. I designed a subsection of the site devoted to user contributed fiction.
I am now working on software development for my build team, as well as oversee software builds and our internal documentation.
On the side, I am designing small games and I hope to some day make the leap to doing it full time.
