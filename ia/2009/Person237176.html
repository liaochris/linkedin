Tori Randolph’s Summary

I am a Fresno State senior and will receive a bachelor of arts degree in mass communication and journalism with an emphasis in public relations, and certificates in special event planning and sign language in May.

During my undergraduate studies I have participated in public relations internships in the nonprofit and corporate sectors, as well as established my own public relations firm and the first student-operated public relations firm at Fresno State. I also gained journalistic writing experience through freelancing for several Central California publications.

When I graduate in May 2009 I look forward to moving back to the San Francisco Bay Area and getting into the industry as an entry level public relations professional.
