Lilian Brzoska’s Summary

I have thirty years experience as a Teacher and Theatre Arts practitioner, twenty years
of that independent of any institution. I recognise the relationship between Theatre Arts and Mystery School Training practises, especially where the breath empowers the process of shamanic journeying. I've explored many layers of Being through the Arts.

I work with people in all walks of life, and of all ages, strengthening their creative intelligence and encouraging them to express their talents fully in the world. I encourage relationship with the Natural world,and with one another, co-creating peer group circles and assisting individuals to express themselves with confidence and clarity. I am also a poet-performance artist in my own right, speaking for the Voiceless Ones, singing to the children and laughing with The Earth, moving with the mountains.

Lilian Brzoska’s Specialties:

I specialise in Voice Production and Speech, Improvisation and Co-creating Celebrations for the inter-faith Community of Eco-aware lovers of Life.

    (Partnership; 11-50 employees; Performing Arts industry)

    October 1982 — September 1989 (7 years)

We travelled to Nurnberg with Ewan McVicar as part of the Open Circle Exchange and trained many actors still working in the professional theatre in Britain.
