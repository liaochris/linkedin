Rey Umali’s Summary

Rey Umali has over 15 years of creative design and marketing, working in corporate communications, publishing and technology. He has produced successful projects with companies including Apple, IBM, Panasonic Broadcasting, Hitachi Corporation-Japan, Times Mirror-Mosby, CB Richard Ellis, Met Life, and Wave Technologies International. He has earned numerous design and marketing awards; among them are The American Graphic Design Award, The Arrow Award, Communicator Award, and The Printing Industries of America Award. He has the ability to learn and understand clients’ needs and successfully communicate them in effective ways.
