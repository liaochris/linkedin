Ad van der Ree’s Summary

After a stormy career as a rock drummer, travelling Europe with several Dutch bands, I ‘calmed down’ and started a carrier as a media specialist. From the early years of the Internet I worked on projects that changed the media landscape. Starting of as a media consultant. Now operating as editor in chief and concept maker on multi media projects. In 2006 my department and me won the Prix Italia with the Kassa website; a multi media and cross media project that reaches more than one and a half million people a month. Including a vast community of 92.000 people visiting Kassa on a daily basis. In 2007 I advised other public broadcasters in several European countries on the possibilities of building multi media projects involving communities. In 2008 I worked on some new projects, an event, presenting workshops and will appear as a guest lecturer on several universities. 2009 started as a very succesfull year with 21% growth of the Kassa website audience!
