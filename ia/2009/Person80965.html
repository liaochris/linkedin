Francois Retief’s Summary

With over 10 years of experience in multiple aspects of the development of complex multi-disciplined software, firmware and electronic hardware technology solutions, I am well equipped to practice my passion, which is to help technology developers solve the difficult questions to be successful in a high pace, fast changing world. Questions like:

• How to I start or run a multi discipline, multi team development operation?
• How do I release quality software on time?
• How do I keep my many clients happy?
• How do we successfully outsource this project?
• How do I create high performance, self managed teams?
• How do I manage the risk of unforeseen future requirements?
• How do I keep meeting customer needs in terms of rate of change and delivery schedules as the product complexity increases?

I can assist with challenges like these in the short to medium term by providing additional capacity as contractor in my speciality fields or by enlisting and managing additional contractors to provide a specific solution. In the long term I aim to facilitate a process where a company find their own permanent solutions.

I believe that problems are solved from within, and that solutions are stronger when everyone is part of creating it. I enjoy facilitating smart solutions using peoples strengths rather than forcing them to work harder on their weaknesses. Finding an elegant, simple and synergistic solution to a problem that seems very difficult and complex provides me with great satisfaction.
