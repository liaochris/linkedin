Robert Galvin III’s Summary

I have been working in EMS for the past 14 years, 7 of those years in Fight Medicine. My Goal is for the Improvement for EMS on both a State and Federal Level. My passion is the Line of Duty Deaths. My Wife and I started the new leg of the EMS Memorial Bike Ride from Kentucky to Virginia in 2007. Due to my Wife being diagnosed with Kidney Cancer, I was unable to attend this past year.
While I was unable to attend, I will always remain a Muddy Angel. Plans are in the works to attend in 2009 and God willing, my wife will be along side of us. I have been working hard to establish Death Benefits in the State of Kentucky for EMS providers. Presently only Police Officers and Fire Fighters are eligible for Benefits if killed in the line of Duty. According to the NHTSB FARS report, more EMS providers are killed in the line of duty than both Police Officers and Fire Fighters combined. I will continue to remember those fallen "Semper Memoria".
