Elizabeth Petry-Lee’s Summary

Elizabeth works directly with planning teams and owners of mid-size businesses in the acquisition/ disposition of commercial property, delivering innovative yet pragmatic solutions. She has an ability to drill down into an idea to see the potential, extrapolating not only the benefits but the feasibility and profitability of its implementation.

Elizabeth thrives in a creative and collaborative setting, yet understands the tremendous importance of the bottom line.

Working as an event director for BI Worldwide she developed leadership skills uniting cross-functional teams to execute international and domestic programs for Fortune 500 clients such as GM, Sprint, Verizon, Toyota, GE Capital, Pfizer, Dr. Pepper, and H & R Block. She is as effective with the CEO as with line employees.

At Window on the World she served on a highly collaborative team in coordination and delivery of cross cultural training for individuals and families departing on international assignments. Clients: Cargill, 3M, General Mills, Daimler Chrysler, Deloitte, St. Paul Companies, Starbucks.

Her career began working with Hispanic migrant workers. Then she moved to the Dept. of Spanish and Portuguese, University of Texas--Austin, as an assistant to the Chairman. Selected for scholarship for a year of study in France she returned to the US to work with the French hotel chain, Sofitel. Her position in restaurant management led to a return to France, and eventually to work with BI Worldwide as an Event Director, focused on food & beverage operations.

The perfect career opportunity allows Elizabeth utilization of her cross cultural skills, public speaking abilities, languages, one-on-one coaching, and project management. Her passion: being hands-on in the strategic development of new products and practices to drive business globally and domestically.

Elizabeth seeks a high energy, innovative company. Her approach: customer-centric, determined to exceed expectations.
