Domenico Suppa’s Summary

P&G SGR is a niche Asset Management firm specialised in Alternative Investments, with strong expertise in relative value and derivative based strategies.

The Risk Manager has supervisory, monitoring and reporting responsibilities for a number of specific issues within the business:

- Gathering, analysing and aggregating risk and exposure data relating to underlying collateral.

- Validating pricing models.

- Controlling operational limits, calculating statistical risk parameters, stress test modelling, etc.

- Analysing underlying investment risk; reviewing geographical, currencies and sectors allocation.

- Ensure robust and sustainable process is maintained to identify watch list/problem accounts and implement watch lists procedures and strategies to protect assets and minimize credit losses.

The role includes liaising with the traders, recommending and motivating limits, and with counterparties and investors, bringing in a risk management expertise. Regular management reports with detailed analysis of all trading positions are produced to ensure that the Board of Directors is aware of the risk profile being run by the trading businesses and that there is an appropriate early warning mechanism in place should the risk profile be deemed to be out of alignment with risk appetite or prevailing market conditions, and be able to take affirmative action when any breaches occur.
