Thomas Ruscitti’s Summary

Tom Ruscitti is a broad-based capital asset investment advisor, real estate management consultant, and licensed architect, with over twenty years of providing professional services to the capital asset investment industry. With specialization in facilities location strategies and design, wireless telecommunications infrastructure, real estate economic development and incentives negotiations, program/project management, process improvement, technology integration, litigation support, dispute resolution, project diagnostics, and cost audits, Tom is fully versed in the intricacies and phases of the design and construction process. His extensive involvement in creating operational efficiencies and reducing operational costs and business risks of a broad range and scale of commercial and institutional projects has benefit clients across multiple industries and market segments. He is experience in communicating and working with a variety of client types; – corporate owners, real estate operating and service companies, investors and advisors, REITs, construction companies, homebuilders, hospitality, gaming operations, and public sector..
Industry experience in real estate, construction, and architecture includes positions with the Capital Program Development Group of the real estate services firm of Jones Lang LaSalle, and the architecture/engineering firms of Perkins & Will, and Skidmore Owings & Merrill.
