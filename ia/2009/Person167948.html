Monte Lee-Wen’s Summary

Monte Lee Wen founded The PPA Group in 2004 and currently serves as its President and Chief Executive Officer. In the last four years he has grown the firm 10 fold: from six employees to over 65. The company has grown to a lead position in the commercial real estate sector with a portfolio valued at over $165 million and commercial property in several states. With Monte at the helm of The PPA Group, the company's portfolio has continued to expand exponentially. Monte has led his company to be a recognizable force in the commercial real estate industry and the company has been recognized by city and foreign leaders for its efforts to not only invest in its communities but also in the families that resided at its communities. The PPA Group's acquisitions have appeared in numerous published news sources.

Most recently in June of 2008, Monte founded PPA Real Estate Management to serve as the company's property management company. PPA Real Estate Management currently manages a diversified portfolio valued in excess of $165 million consisting of multifamily, emerging office, and development properties.

Monte's real estate investing philosophy involves highly calculated evaluations and taking advantage of opportunities whereby superior risk-adjusted returns can be realized. This approach has allowed The PPA Group to realize tremendous returns on investment regardless of market and valuation trends at the time the investment is made.

Prior to his active career in commercial real estate, Monte enjoyed a successful career in Information Technology working in areas of Intrusion Prevention, network architecture design, software development, and policy development. Monte is also a seasoned entrepreneur having started and ran several companies since the mid-1990s. Philanthropy is also very important to Monte. He has been actively involved in, including sitting on and participating on committees of many private and public initiatives nationwide.
