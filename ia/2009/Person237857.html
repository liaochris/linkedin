  • Blue Ribbon Digital

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2004 — July 2008 (4 years 6 months)

    Blue Ribbon Digital is an online ad agency focused on driving offline transactions with online media. We work with large manufacturers and retailers to help them reach an audience currently changing their media consumption habits.

    As VP/Managing Director, everyone from Directors on down reports through me. Ultimately, I'm the one that makes sure our clients are happy with the product and work they receive from my team. I keep in close contact with all clients and make sure that we're exceeding their expectations.

Advertising today is a very different beast than it was five years ago, and online advertising is no exception. I'm proud to have helped such great organizations like Google and Yahoo! build out new and exciting products and offerings.

  • Discount Tire Company

    (Privately Held; Retail industry)

    July 1990 — February 2004 (13 years 8 months)

    Discount Tire Company is the largest independent tire dealer in the country. I rose through the ranks over 13.5 years, managing two stores, liaised between store operations and IT and ultimately spearheaded the newly formed eBusiness Group in 1999.

    I managed a team of 20+ developers, designers and specialists as we took a legacy brochure-wear application to a fully-functional ecommerce engine, responsible for millions of dollars in monthly transactions.

    My primary role was creating the company's online strategies and explaining those plans to the retail operations group. Or more frequently, adapting those plans to meet the needs of the stores -- the lifeblood of the company. Selling tires online isn't an easy process, but we managed to do it more successfully than anyone else.

  • PDC Productions
