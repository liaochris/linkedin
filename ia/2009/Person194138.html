Roy Tokeshi’s Summary

Systems Engineer with experience in several vertical markets including Government, Education, Healthcare, Real Estate, and Telecommunications. While my recent focus has been in application virtualization, I have experience in hardware virtualization and database administration as well. I may be best known for my light development, scripting and all around refusal to admit that there isn’t a way to do something.
