Dr. Lynn Kerew’s Summary

Dr. Lynn Kerew is your neighborhood chiropractor, trained in both force and non-force adjustment techniques

Dr. Kerew, D.C. utilizes her extensive background in Nutrition and Public Health to design an individualized and personalized approach for reaching your specific health goals and peak potential.

Dr. Lynn Kerew, D.C. , M.P.H. is a graduate of Cleveland Chiropractic College in Los Angeles, CA. After receiving her first Chiropractic adjustment at the age of seventeen, Dr. Kerew, D.C. experienced profound and immediate, ongoing health benefits. This included relief from headaches and low back pain caused by an exhaustive routine of competitive gymnastics, running and high school swimming events.

In addition to her Doctor of Chiropractic (D.C.) degree, Dr. Kerew, D.C. holds a Master's Degree in Public Health (M.P.H.) from the University of California, Los Angeles and a Baccalaureate degree (B.S.) in Nutrition from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst.

Dr. Kerew, D.C. utilizes her extensive knowledge to provide a comprehensive and common-sense approach to wellness with the goal of enhancing each patient's healing and ongoing health.

In addition to providing family care to expectant mothers and their "new arrivals," Dr. Kerew, D.C.'s practice focuses on providing revitalizing chiropractic care to teenagers and adults as well.

By continually upgrading her own knowledge of advancements in Chiropractic techniques, nutrition, wellness, and patient education, she is able to offer a multi-disciplinary approach to each patient's unique health goals.

Dr. Kerew, D.C. provides educational lectures on a wide
range of health topics. She is available for public speaking engagements at your company or business health fair or wellness event. She has been the Corporate Chiropractor for Electronic Arts L.A. since 2005 and currently serves as an Ergonomic Consultant to Jakks Pacific - Malibu.

Dr. Lynn Kerew’s Specialties:

Ergonomic Assessment of both products and people in the Corporate environment. Establishing and maintaining a successful chiropractic office in the black since 1996.
