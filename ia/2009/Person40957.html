Chander Singh’s Summary

Chander Singh in the current role as Operations Director for Europe, Middle East And African region of Polaris, is responsible for regulatory compliance mainly on immigration, taxation and labour matters besides managing key functions at the UK office. Another key responsibilities is cost control and optimization, revenue collection and enhancing profitability.

Chander has also been leading initiatives in identifying new emerging markets and strategic partners and tie-ups for the EMEA region, to create legal entities in these new markets.

Chander is also leading few identified sales initiatives of strategic nature.

Chander before moving to UK, was responsible as Business Head for Polaris in India during which period the best banks were added to the Polaris marquee client list.

Before joining Polaris in 2001, Chander was the CIO at Bank of Punjab, a new generation private sector bank in India since its inception and was instrumental in creating a robust IT infrastructure, and operations framework since setup.

Chander had headed the Total Branch Automation in IT Division in Canara Bank, with hands on experience as a banker managing the retail branch banking environment. Chander had started his career with New Bank of India in 1976 after a Bachelor's degree in Science from Delhi University.

With hands-on expertise in retail branch banking and transitioning into IT systems development, delivery, management and sales and operations in a global IT firm, Chander has acquired a full life cycle experience of IT organization in financial services environment with a unique experience and skillset to strategically advise senior executives on product management and IT management besides Business Process and IT Outsourcing, BPR and optimization, and many business and operational initiatives including large project management in financial institutions.

2. Program management – Implementation of large transformation IT projects.
