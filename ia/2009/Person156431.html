Matt Laurence’s Summary

Admittedly, I stumbled ass-backwards into this world of online media, web and interactive design, and advertising/marketing through a combination of geekiness, curiosity, fate and wind-blown serendipity. But I couldn't have kept at it for 18 years now if I didn't love it - who wouldn't enjoy combining your nerdy proclivities with some visual whiz-bang, a few wacky concepts (when you're lucky), music (when even luckier), and a cadre of the best kind of freaks to work with... even taking into account the frequent doses of very cold water thrown on you by those ever-present client-types.

My goal is to keep having fun while providing the very best creative and technical work to accomplish business goals for my clients, find a group of people to work with that can become mutual support and family while on the job, and play as much music as possible while being a solid and present father and husband to the most important people in my life. 'Nuf said.
