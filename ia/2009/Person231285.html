Piergiuliano Bossi’s Summary

I started developing software when I was 11 years old on a Sinclair ZX Spectrum. I was a Fidonet Point in 1990 (2:331/308.8 at Euforia BBS in Milan). I have 20 years of professional experience in software development, throughout which I have investigated many computer science aspects. With a solid base of design patterns, object oriented paradigm, software architectures and programming languages such as Java, Ruby, C++, C, and SQL, I have worked on mission-critical and multi-channel applications, applying distributed computing, messaging, artificial intelligence and computer graphics techniques. Today I am particularly interested in methodologies and practices emerging from the Agile Movement like Extreme Programming (XP), refactoring and evolutive/iterative incremental approaches to software development. I am always looking for better/faster/cheaper ways of developing software.

You can read my not so frequently updated blog at http://thinkingbox.wordpress.com/

Piergiuliano Bossi’s Specialties:

Extreme Programming, agile software development, OO paradigm, evolutive OO design, design principles & patterns, project management, TDD, testing, continuous build & integration, pair programming, software process improvement, coaching, mentoring
