James Penman’s Summary

23 years of experience as an applications architect, software development team manager, project manager, technology program manager, service delivery manager, and software developer. Has industry expertise and focus on the financial service industry -- notably asset management, brokerage, private banking, trust companies, hedge funds, investment management, Seperately Managed Accounts, Unified Managed Accounts, Wrap Accounts, and Registered Investment Advisors (RIA).

Is now assisting several medical information technology companies with technology strategy and product development.

Most notable successes have been in the roles of lead architect and the senior most technology manager for Wachovia Securities and Bank of America's Separately Managed Account and Wrap Account platforms. During my tenure, their platforms rapidly grew to become industry top-10 (now exceeding $300 billion in combined assets under management). While with Wachovia Securities, lead the technology consolidation of Everen's and Wachovia's managed account platform, which at that time, was the largest ever consolidation of managed account systems.

One of the applications I personally designed and lead development Bank of America has cumulatively produced over $1 billion in incremental revenue and saved over $50 million in direct expenses. The performance reporting and fee billing systems I designed and lead development for Wachovia Securities (now part of Wells Fargo) has likely saved nearly $50 million in direct expenses.

Takes great pride in having hiring and mentored 100's technology and operations personnel. Has concurrently managed over 40 direct reports in 5 software development teams across 4 distinct geographical locations. Has successfully setup and managed off-shore and near-shore development teams.

James Penman’s Specialties:

Business expertise in financial service products & operations, separately managed accounts (SMA), wrap accounts, mutual funds, unified managed accounts (UMA), advisor compensation, and compliance. Application and technology expertise in portfolio management, FIX equity trading, mutual fund trading, fee billing, account opening, performance reporting, sales reporting, compliance monitoring, equity aggregation reporting.
