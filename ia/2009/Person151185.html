Marjie Carmen’s Summary

Innovative, seasoned, transformational leader with strong collaborating and analytical skills. Adept at figuring out how current practices can be improved, developing and implementing new ones and knowing the right balance between process and delivery.

Able to quickly assess organizational capabilities in the area of Software Development and suggest areas where small changes can make the biggest impact.

Highly skilled at building and developing cohesive and collaborative teams, leading teams through change and teaching teams how to resolve internal conflict and move from chaos to results

Decades of implementing process/methodology changes across organizations.

Enthusiastic, pragmatic problem solver. Objective focused with an eye on the bottom line.

25+ years in Software Development specializing in Software Quality Assurance, Project Management and multiple SDLC's including XP and SCRUM.

6 years collaborating with people in the music industry in topics such as:
* Learning about the different temperaments (MBTI) and Satir Interaction model of communication to better identify issues/resolve conflicts as well as other techniques
* Team building skills, recruiting and on-boarding people to ensure the team can work together to produce the sound and attain the group synergy they are looking for
* Organizational skills/Time management


Relentless in the pursuit of knowledge and information that I can use to better the organization I am part of as well as to improve my own capabilities.

Strong belief in decentralization of management with an emphasis on building teams that become self-managed.
