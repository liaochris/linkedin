Frank Papsadore’s Summary

I am an award-winning entrepreneur, marketing executive, and advertising agency creative with more than 20 years of experience leading successful campaigns for both large firms and small start-ups. I've proven my expertise in product / service positioning, brand development, market strategy, creative tactics, copywriting, direct marketing, Internet marketing, and efficient, profitable program implementation.

I am part writer, part designer, part manager, part mentor, part entrepreneur, part jack-of-all-trades. I provide forward-thinking creative leadership and inspire creative collaboration inside and outside an organization. I am an expert at effectively translating branding and marketing strategies for a wide range of clients into exceptional creative concepts and executions. I have historically and consistently created breakthrough innovative concepts - while keeping within project scope, i.e., strategic positioning, business requirements, timelines, and budgets.

I am an excellent team leader - creatively pushing my staff's intellectual and imaginative limits for the delivery of outstanding work for all things physical and digital: advertising, print, mail and broadcast, large scale web engagements, online advertising, digital marketing, business development initiatives, and more. I am particularly skilled at presenting creative concepts effectively and managing the entire creative process, implementing large-scale projects start-to-finish.

For a more detailed illustration of what I can offer, feel free to contact me directly at 508-737-4798 or fpapsadore@comcast.net. You can also view or download more information - including my creative portfolio - at www.papsadore.com.

Frank Papsadore’s Specialties:
