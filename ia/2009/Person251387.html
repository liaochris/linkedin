Zen Chu’s Summary

FOCUS AREAS:
- Medical devices with fast approvals
- Software & connected healthcare
- Solutions with high patient engagement & emotion

I am devoted to the business of medical innovation and helping translate inventions into real-world therapies, including venture philanthropy and low-cost solutions that can extend to the rest of the world. I have been lucky to work alongside some of the brightest scientists, clinicians and investors on worthy projects, especially in the rare moments when the complexities of healthcare can be distilled into a simple solution. I work at the earliest stages to envision, create, patent, de-risk, develop and sell ideas and products.

Zen Chu’s Specialties:

I wear many hats within early stage ventures: invention, intellectual property mapping, clinical markets, regulatory strategy, technical assessment, physician focus groups, fundraising etc. I have worked across the disciplines of medical devices, surgical & robotic tools, biomaterials, healthcare software & data, nutraceuticals, controlled drug delivery, pharmaceutical discovery & regenerative stem cell therapies.

Zen Chu’s Experience

  • Founder

    Accelerated Medical Ventures

    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    2007 — Present (2 years )

    Accelerated Medical Ventures specializes in helping physician and surgeon-inventors invent high-value clinical innovations and accelerate them to the patient bedside. The company serves as an early stage investor, inventor & operator of these companies. Active investments are in the areas of surgical tools, medical devices, robotic surgery, biomaterials, healthcare information technology, consumer healthcare, clinical nutraceuticals.

  • Advisor on Healthcare Innovation

    Partners HealthCare

    (Non-Profit; Hospital & Health Care industry)

    2007 — Present (2 years )

    I currently advise multiple academic & clinical institutions on healthcare innovation including Brigham & Women's Hospital, Harvard University, Cleveland Clinic Innovations, CIMIT (Center for Integration of Medicine & Innovative Technology) BioDesign & the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

    Some of my work and views can be found at http://cimit.typepad.com/cimit_forum_blog/2008/05/zen-chu-slides.html

  • Director & Co-Founder

    3DM Inc.

    (Biotechnology industry)

    September 2002 — Present (7 years 3 months)

    Co-founded, bootstrapped and led as CEO the venture capital backed biomaterials company based in Cambridge, MA. PuraMatrix nanofibers as synthetic injectable scaffolds for medical devices, drug delivery and regenerative medicine. Worldwide partnership with Becton Dickinson. Collaborations with Boston Scientific, Medtronic, J&J and others. Eminent MIT Scientific co-founders include Drs. Shuguang Zhang, Alan Grodzinsky, Robert Langer, Alex Rich & Carlos Semino. Company sold to NMJ Japan in 2008.

    (Privately Held; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    October 1999 — January 2002 (2 years 4 months)

    Early-stage venture capital fund founded by Adam Kirsch, co-founder of Bain Capital. Invests in software, consumer internet and medical devices. NetVentures seed-funded 3DM Inc.
