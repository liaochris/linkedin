Alex Drummond’s Summary

I am a fulltime inhouse concept artist at a south brisbane game studio working with a team of game developers and other artists to deliver a AAA title to all the current platforms.I also work on my own indi games in my free time as well as pen and paper development of gameplay mechanics.

I have given guest lectures on creating game environments at Queensland University of Technology(QUT), as well as live demonstrations of speed painting environments at Supernova events.

I am comfortable working to deadline and like getting art direction from the team.

Alex Drummond’s Specialties:

Environmental concept art, prop design for game levels.
