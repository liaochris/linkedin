Gerard K M Lim’s Summary

Experienced professional who has spent the last 18 years in the
Information and Communications Technology (ICT) industry, having been involved in the area of software development, multimedia, Internet, wireless broadband, mobile, networking and Voice services.

I have implemented many successful solutions and projects in various governments, private corporations and multinationals in Asian region as well as in other parts of the World.

I am a specialist writer in the field of multimedia and Internet applications, and has written numerous articles for trade journals, national newspapers, and regional publications. I was the columnist for Multimedia Directions and Visible Web that covers the topic of Web Marketing, which appears in a weekly national computer pullout.

I am also a frequent speaker in the seminar circuit, and speak at least 6 times a year at ICT industry and Entrepreneurship related events.

I have served as appointed Councilor on the Association of Computer Industry Malaysia (PIKOM) year 1997-1998, as Chairman of the Interactive Multimedia Association of Malaysia (IMAM) 1998-1999 and Chairman of the IP Telephony Special Interest Group (SIG) at the Malaysian National Computer Confederation (MNCC) in 2006.

A serial entrepreneur, I have spent the last 15 years building various businesses in Multimedia content, E-commerce, Internet consulting and Mobile IP communications.
