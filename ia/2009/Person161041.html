Michael Cylkowski’s Summary

Mike is an Executive Coach, Career Coach, and Business Coach certified by the ICF & PCI;

Mike has been the creative genius behind multiple entrepreneurial companies in the Phoenix and Las Vegas markets. He has been the executive decision maker in companies that provided perfusion services, just-in-time medical supplies and equipment, physician practice management and billing services. Altogether, he has 40 plus years experience in the medical arena having worked in various capacities in hospitals, selling to hospitals, and providing consulting to hospitals, physicians, and hospital executives.

Michael Cylkowski’s Specialties:

Mike specializes in Executive Coaching, Entrepreneurial Coaching, Business Coaching, and Career Coaching for Healthcare Executives and Physician Leaders.
