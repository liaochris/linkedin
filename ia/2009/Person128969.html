Keith Gordon’s Summary

Keith Gordon was a product manager for professional audio and music products in the Audio & Wireless Division of Gennum Corporation. He is an MBA graduate from the Schulich School of Business (York University). He graduated in April of 2004 with a specialization in Marketing and concentrations in Strategy, Entrepreneurship and Arts & Media Management. In his work as an independent consultant and contractor, Keith developed top-notch practical experience in client relations, marketing and business. Keith’s work as a supervisor with the Manitoba Government provided valuable experience in the complexities of overseeing a department, managing staff and working within formal structures.

Keith Gordon’s Specialties:

product development, live sound mixing, recording music, video editing, presentations, public speaking, team management
