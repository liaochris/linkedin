Ralph Talmont’s Summary

Hello, dzien dobry, guten Tag, jai siyaram, hajimemashite, hola, nihao, bonjour.
Thanks for stopping by.

Right now I'm building on twenty five years' experience in photography, multimedia and publishing to create a new mobile and web platform which combines all three. Working with my UK-based partner we're creating a new kind of a online environment for travellers - part village market, part a communications centre, part editorial platform. The aim of our little startup venture is to democratise travel publishing.

Highly skilled in photography in all formats, photo editing, book design and publishing, multimedia production, creative direction and strategic planning, project management, rights and contract negotiation. Interested in world music, film, social sciences, food, and modes of expression which bring all these together.

I'm comfortable dealing with all cultures, having lived in Europe, Australia and New Zealand, spent long stretches of time in Hong Kong and the USA, dealt in a professional capacity with clients and suppliers from Mumbai to Monaco and travelled extensively throughout Asia, Europe and North America.

Ralph Talmont’s Specialties:

Large scale strategic thinking coupled with detail driven execution using web, photography, words, moving image and print media

A media / creative guy fully at home in a technical setting
I have an established track record of producing complex high-end projects, from concept to conclusion, on time and on budget, and am good at collaborative team work in visual and theatre arts, print and electronic publishing and music.
