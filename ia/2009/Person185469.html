Peter Anderson (peter.d.anderson@gmail.com)’s Summary

Peter Anderson graduated from the University of Victoria in Canada with a Bachelor degree in Computer Science. He joined ACD Systems in 1998 as a software engineer and has designed and developed many of the features of ACDSee and that has helped make the product successful. During the last 10 years, he has held various positions within the company. Now, he is the Director of Business Development for APAC and also holds the position of Chief Representative for the ACD Systems Representative Office which opened this year in the Beijing CBD. Presently, his main focus is the emerging China and Asian markets, but he continues to provides strategic direction for the company’s global efforts.
