Eliana Simonetti’s Summary

Bachelor - Journalism at Universidade de São Paulo – Escola de Comunicações e Artes
Bachelor - History at Pontifícia Universidade de São Paulo
Specialisation – Oral History at Universidade de São Paulo

Reporter, writer and editor for 27 years at major Brazilian press media, I also work producing books and educational material as consultant for UNDP and IADB, and elaborating institutional material - as Annual Reports - for Brazilian Companies. I'm also on Facebook and Naymz.
