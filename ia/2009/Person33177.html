Brian Collins’s Summary

Brian Collins is CEO and Creative Director at interactive design studio Stickstone Design, and Vice President of Interactive Design at e-commerce software company Trade Extensions AB. His solid background in digital media, web 2.0 design & development, social networks, internet marketing, and sales has allowed him to grow his client base by 300% and revenues in excess of 600%.

Since 1990, Brian Collins has been providing award-winning design solutions in traditional and online media to a variety of fortune 500 & 1000 companies. His work has achieved recognition from top industry publications such as Business Week, BtoB, InternetWeek, Business 2.0, as well as from the Smithsonian. Most recently, Brian Collins has received the Web Marketing Associations WebAward for "Excellence in Web Design and Development".

Brian has recently moved back to Chicagoland and is seeking full-time opportunities in Interactive Design or Sales. He is looking to utilize his experience establishing and growing a profitable interactive agency to lead in the sales of web and print-based strategies for a variety of fortune 500 clients. He has over 15 years experience with a multidisciplinary background in interactive design, GUI design, search engine optimization (SEO), as well as traditional marketing and advertising.

Brian Collins’s Specialties:

Sales, Interactive Online Advertising and Marketing, Branding and Identity, Web Site Design and Development, Graphical User Interface (GUI) Design, Internet Marketing, SEO, E-Mail Marketing & Campaign Management
