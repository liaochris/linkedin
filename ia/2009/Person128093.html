Katie Winchell’s Summary

As the marketing and communications director for the nonprofit California League of Schools, I develop, implement and measure marketing and community relations campaigns to boost membership; attract K-12 educators to state conferences, regional workshops and webinars; and engage educators in community through social media avenues such as Ning, Twitter, YouTube and Facebook. I also help run our multi-day statewide conferences, attended by 500 to 3,500 teachers and administrators.

The Principals to Watch program that I created and obtained funding for is a model for improving the leadership at public middle schools serving high percentages of diverse, low-income students. Participating principals join a network of peers to share successes and challenges, tour thriving schools, and receive mentoring from successful principals.

In addition to my full-time position, I consult part-time as a copywriter and inbound marketing specialist, helping clients define their strengths, build communities out of customers, and target communications to business objectives.
