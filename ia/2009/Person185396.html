Peter Brissette’s Summary

GSP America is developing new lines of revenue for system integrators in IT, Telecom and the electrical industries. Video security is a natural progression for anyone who is working with low voltage applications.

I am the author of the GSP America Video Surveillance Buyers Guide Version 1 and Choosing the Right Security Camera Application Guide. I have also compiled and edited the largest CCTV Glossary of Terms available currently in its fifth revision.

My training videos on Understanding PoE and Field of View-Focal Lengths have been viewed over 1000 times and posted to the Security Sales and Integration website.

I have worked a number of years in technical sales working with engineers to correctly create specifications based on capabilities in order to drive projects to completion. I bring that same capability to the video security industry.

A large part of my success is my commitment to customer service. That includes follow through and follow up in a quick and efficient manner so that my customers can have the edge they need to close more business.

My goals include continuing to develop the training necessary to help you succeed in the video security business. We have regular webinars dealing with CCTV basics as well product specific training. Visit our website, blog and webinar schedule pages to learn more.
