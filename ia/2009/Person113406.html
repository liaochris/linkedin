John Birse’s Summary

John has built the Jim’s Bookkeeping system up over the last nine years drawing on his background in training, technology and business. After commencing his career as a Commerce Teacher he spent 15 years in the classroom before taking on a Regional Consultancy position with the Victorian Education Department followed by managing the School Administrative Computing Unit which developed a computerised accounting package for all Victorian schools. He then moved into Private Enterprise working for Kambrook, ICL and Labtam in the computer divisons assisting in the manufacture of a local 486 PC and the licensing of Microsoft Office to all Victorian TAFE colleges.
John established his own computer training college – Taskforce Training working with many small business owners and writing training manuals he also became the first externally appointed Principal to a Self Governing School in Victoria – Concord School. One of his clients was the Jim’s Group and after being asked to present a number of training sessions to their National Conferences he decided to take on a Jim’s Division – Jim’s Bookkeeping to service the growing demand of support to small businesses using the well tested Jim’s Franchising model.
With over 100 franchises taken up around Australia since the year 2000 John is looking to double this number in the next few years and take the franchised bookkeeping model to Canada and other locations.
John is actively involved in developing the bookkeeping industry though his involvement as a Director of the Institute of Certified Bookkeepers and through the development of training materials for bookkeepers and the provision of excellent service to small business owners.

John Birse’s Specialties:

Small business performance management systems, experienced based training, MYOB and Quickbooks professional partner, franchise management, VoiP communication systems, business start-ups.
