  • Assistant Representative (Chennai, India)

    UNICEF

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    1996 — 2001 (5 years )

    Field Office for Tamil Nadu and Kerala, Chennai, India
    Responsible for UNICEF cooperation with two medium-size Indian States, with a combined population of over 76 millions, of whom 35 million children. Member of the India Office Management Advisory Group and of the audit committee. Co-facilitated management training and management reviews for other state offices (Bhopal and Lucknow).

    Led the preparation of a major international consultation (October 2000), which contributed to updating India national policy on the young child. Developed new strategic initiatives to address system issues, including child labour, which were integrated at the national level. Tested and strengthened efficient approaches in school water, sanitation and hygiene, becoming a major partner into the state-wide “sanitation programme” with documented and quantified results. Promoted a "network and task force" strategy in crucial cross-cutting areas such as nutrition and communication, supported by senior levels of the state. Our direct support to the network of NGOS "Campaign Against Child Labour" led to high returns in public awareness, as well as to government action.
