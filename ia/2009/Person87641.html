Glen Zamanian’s Summary

I have over fifteen years as a senior executive in sales, sales management, and field service for high-tech hardware and software products. I have created offices in numerous cites in Europe, Asia and the USA including Amsterdam, Tokyo, Singapore and Albuquerque.

My experience includes managing teams selling complex, multi-year installations of process equipment and services for semiconductors, electronics, disk drives, instrumentation, flat-panels, and medical devices. I have extensive experience selling directly to customers in overseas and North American markets.
