Mike Del Ninno’s Summary

Mr. Del Ninno possesses an eclectic mix of business skills; most recently assisting the Department of Homeland Security by providing leadership training to key managers within the Transportation Security Administration. Previously, he was recruited by the Ormond Beach Chamber of Commerce. As Executive Director of the non-profit, he sharpened his political skill by working with various committees to grow membership, increase retention, work with local government(s) and fundraise for key events.

As Executive Vice President of Roush Sports Group d/b/a Roush Racing, Mr. Del Ninno developed the initial business plan for growth spearheading the development of the “Jack Roush Signature Series” collectible die-cast product, which led to the eventual acquisition of Team Caliber, LLC. Other accomplishments included the acquisition and development of an in-house trackside merchandise division, growth of the promotion division, securing NASCAR sponsorship for Matt Kenseth’s Busch program, and managing Greg Biffle’s sponsor merchandise program.

Mr. Del Ninno’s sales, marketing and management career began with the Valvoline Company. Over the course of 15+ years, he has held various positions including Trade Marketing Manager, Regional Marketing Manager, Retail Sales Account Manager, Division Manager, District Manager and Sales Representative. Always pushing the envelope, Mr. Del Ninno was instrumental in driving unique initiatives including account specific planning, trade marketing analytics, post-promotion baseline evaluation, and strategic business planning.

Mr. Del Ninno enjoys directing, writing, editing, producing and acting. He has performed both in-film and on-stage, as well as worked the camera. He’s produced several short-films, as well as received a local Silver ADDY award. His creative personality is essential when working with artistic-types and advertising agencies.
