Anuj Kumar’s Summary

I have rich experience of developing both front end and back end (physical design) EDA tools. I have worked extensively on programming with C, C++ and data structures. In physical design domain, I have worked on enhancing hierarchical partitioning, incremental extraction, parasitic correlation and engineering change order features of EDA tools. In front end, I was earlier involved in the development of a behavioral synthesis tool for FPGAs. Among various other activities, I have coordinated project development with off-shore team, provided specifications for new feature development and collaborated with chip designers in solving many critical problems. I have also authored technical papers ( accepted at ICCD 2008, IWLS 2008, VLSI Design 2009 ) and been reviewer for International VLSI Design 2006, ISLPED 2007, DAC 2008.
Currently I am contributing to Design/Design verification efforts of a Crypto IP which will be used in the next generation Cisco Switching product line.
