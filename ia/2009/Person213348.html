Jill Konrath’s Summary

Jill Konrath helps salespeople crack into corporate accounts, speed up their sales cycle & win big contracts. She's the author of a bestselling book, Selling to Big Companies which Fortune magazine just selected as a "must read."

Additionally, she's a/an:

• Internationally-recognized B2B sales strategist & sales trainer.
• In-demand speaker at annual/regional sales conferences, kickoff
meetings and industry events.
• Writer/publisher of popular e-newsletter and industry-leading blog.
• Founder of the Sales SheBang conference for savvy saleswomen.
• Passionate spokesperson for turning the sales force into a
competitive advantage.

For info on bringing Jill's expertise to your sales meeting or to learn more about selling to big companies, email jill@sellingtobigcompanies.com
