Hisham Ashour’s Summary

Hisham Ashour, an Executive Director at Abraaj Capital, brings 17 years of international turnaround and performance improvement experience in senior roles in the consumer goods, retail, security and logistics, construction, energy and engineering industries. Previously he lead A&M’s restructuring and corporate performance improvement practice in the Middle East.
Mr. Ashour brings an in-depth strategy development and implementation expertise to the firm with emphasis on turnarounds, restructuring, cost savings, and operational and business planning, both in Europe and the Middle East. He has led the development and implementation of cost savings and operational efficiency programmes, as well as consumer marketing and retail turnarounds. Mr. Ashour has led the turnaround of various retail and branded consumer goods across a diverse range of business and product categories in both developed and developing markets.
Most recently at A&M, Mr. Ashour served as the Commercial Director for SecurLog GmbH, the,German market leading cash and valuables logistics company. In this role, he led the development and implementation of the company’s contract management system and back office operations. Prior to that, Mr. Ashour was part of the A&M team at Ihr Platz GmbH, one of the largest German drug store chains. Mr. Ashour served as interim Commercial Director working alongside Ihr Platz’ CEO and CFO to help accelerate the turnaround of the company through efficiencies on buying and retail operations.
Prior to joining A&M, Mr. Ashour spent 12 years with Procter & Gamble as a category leader on the sales, marketing and operational side, where he was responsible for the business’ P&L and $200 million turnover and leading the restructuring and turnaround of businesses such as Vicks OTC, Pampers, Ariel and Olay across Europe, the Middle East, Africa and Asia. Mr. Ashour is fluent in English, German and Arabic.
