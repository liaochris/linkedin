Paula Jago’s Summary

I build and run great Agencies: a commercially-minded Senior Director with 7 years at Board Level. My experience is fully integrated: the past 5 years in Digital, but my career was originally founded on ATL Production and Project Management across a portfolio of Global accounts. This deep, Delivery-focused foundation enabled me the flexibility to take a broader onward route through Creative Services, Client Services, and also a valuable Business Analyst stint Client-side, which ultimately lead to my more recent positioning in Operationally-focused roles. I have worked profoundly (and at all levels) on the management of campaigns that have run across international markets and multiple channels; Print, Consumer and National Press, DM, Outdoor, POS, Radio, TV, Digital and Mobile, and I have a particular interest in Search and Social Media. A Senior Communications, HR & Finance generalist; I build relationships, high-perfomance teams + efficient delivery frameworks, and I deliver retention, profitability and innovation. I create strong and robust Agency Infrastructures: bringing together talented people, solid delivery processes and high-quality products. I take responsibility for making the big decisions that enable this to happen, and take ownership of the Change Management process that is required to support it. Every day brings a different challenge, and I love what I do.
