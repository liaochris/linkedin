Bob Knorpp’s Summary

Do you have rhythm?

I'm not talking about your ability to shuffle across the floor in 4/4 time. I mean, do you understand that there is a rhythm to marketing? Each touch point is a beat. Each message a crashing cymbal. Some are heavy. Some are light. Some are staccato. Some roll in like thunder. And mastering your perfect rhythm generates more than just results. It's the key to true customer engagement.

That's where The Cool Beans Group can help. We call it a Cadence of Communication™. And it's a model that's changing the way organizations are thinking about their marketing efforts.

Finding your right cadence gives you the ability to look beyond a single program and see that each prospect and customer is reacting to the ebb and flow of all your messages. Timing, media mix, messaging structure and interaction opportunities — all are blending together into a singular brand understanding for each customer.

Cool Beans can help determine how much of your marketing effort is creating a symphony and how much is just adding to the noise. We work to develop a more melodic use of all your promotional channels. And we deliver results that deliver both sales and brand loyalty.

Find your rhythm today.
