Peter Ellis’s Summary

I am a current University of Washington Master of Science in Information Management candidate with a passionate interest in information organization and how information influences our lives. I am a technology evangelist, a believer in and strong proponent of environmental sustainability, a writer, a thinker, and above all, a synthesizer. I live by the mantra of "everything is interconnected; context is king": we cannot ignore connections between ideas or actions, but we also cannot ignore the context of those connections and the context in which actions are taken. These two things taken together determine how information is interpreted.

I believe I can help people conceptualize the role that information plays for them, and concentrate primarily on information organization. I seek to connect information management and sustainability in a way that allows me to leverage both passions.
