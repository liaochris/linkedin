Raymond McCauley’s Summary

Working on practical nanotechnology, genomics, and bioinformatics. Good communicator with an entrepreneurial bent, who enjoys working on small, fast teams in start-ups. Can speak both science and engineering, and translate between the two. 15 years of experience in the biotechnology and high tech arenas, working with Illumina, Ingenuity Systems, QIAGEN Genomics/Rapigene, consultancies with various pharma and governmental agencies, and start-up experience in data mining and media ventures, including Insurgent Technologies and TANSTAAFL Media. Background in computer science, electrical engineering, biochemistry, biophysics, and bioinformatics, including graduate studies at Texas A&M University and Stanford University.

Raymond McCauley’s Specialties:

computational biology; nanotechnology; MEMS; ontologies; pharmacogenomics; bioinformatics; gene expression applications; sequencing; primer, probe and siRNA design; intellectual property; technical project management and staffing; algorithm design and development; data-mining and machine learning; web-based applications; streamlining data pipelines; storytelling.
