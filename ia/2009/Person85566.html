Gerald Baroudi’s Summary

Gerald Baroudi has 14 years of commercial lending experience working with a variety of industries and startups. Prior to joining Mid-Atlantic Business Finance Company, Gerald was Vice President and Business Development Officer with MainStreet Lender servicing the Baltimore/Washington, DC Market. Mr. Baroudi has a diverse banking background that has served him well in assisting Small Business Owners manage their growth strategically. He has worked for regional banks such as Provident Bank as well as banks with nationwide presence such as Wells Fargo Bank and Bank of America. He has a B.S. Degree in Finance from University of Maryland University College. Mr. Baroudi has spoken at a number of events on topics ranging from access to capital, lender to lender networking and franchise acquisitions. He has been interviewed by a number of local news publications such as Smart CEO, The Washington Post, Daily Record, Baltimore Business Journal and Baltimore Sun. He is a graduate of the Leadership Anne Arundel Flagship class of 2005.

He currently serves on the Maryland Commercial Lenders Quality Circle Committee and is an active affiliate member of MABIA (Mid-Atlantic Business Intermediaries) and MAREMA (Mid-Atlantic Real Estate Marketing Association).
