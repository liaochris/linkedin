Sarah Lian’s Summary

Regarded as Malaysia’s Most Wanted Woman in 2008 by FHM Malaysia; the summer of 2008 has escalated her to a new level of fame landing covers in Stuff, FHM Malaysia, Clive and features in many more to come. Anticipate to see more of Sarah Lian for the upcoming year of 2009! With projects and music videos with indie pop group Seven Collar T-Shirt and hip-hop star Joe Flizzow lined up for the year, Sarah is already has gone beyond the scope of her abilities with slated shows piling up both locally and regionally.

Sarah is a host who rarely shys away from the camera her presence is exuded in her sublime confidence and wit – she co-hosted 8TV’s The Ultimate Prom Nite 2, which aired in mid-November 2008. In May, she made her silver screen debut as a writer and host of NTV7’s new informative show NUVO. Sarah Lian is a name gaining momentum in the Malaysian entertainment industry. She inaugurated her presence at Media Prima’s online portal Gua.com.my with a spoof news comedy at The Gua Centre’s Second Season and event coverage program Randommania.

Sarah was seen in Home and Garden Network (HGTV)’s newly launched series Mansions as the stern designer in their pilot episode, from the Fall 2008 line up. In 2007, she landed her first movie credit as the “Hot Asian Girl” in American Pie 6: Beta House and continued her hosting abilities in UTOURS, a show revealing student life at Canada’s Top 20 Universities.

Her involvement in numerous stage productions also included the Asian-centric premiere version of Sex in Vancouver produced by the Vancouver Asian Canadian Theatre. From the stage to your living room tv, Sarah has a few commercials under her belt ranging from Adidas’ Womens FIFA 2003, Soo Beef Jerky, Circuit City, Hong Leong Bank in Malaysia, Cadbury Chocolates, Tic Tac, and a small feature for Celcom’s EURO 2008.

For more information, please contact mui@sarah-lian.com.
