Pamela Hughes’s Summary

Consistent success in marketing and sales for financial services, real estate, and consulting businesses.
Track record of success as a business owner and investor has taught me that success in business is about connections, effective problem solving and creativity. It is my passion to connect people to their desired outcomes.

Today, most of my work in this effort is through real property ownership or improvement. My vocation is helping people who want to build a custom home or remodel a home they own. My avocation is connecting people with solutions for life. That may be helping someone find a new property, improving a property, helping someone find a new position or career or helping someone design and implement a business process improvement.
