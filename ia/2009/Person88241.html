Valerie Gonyea’s Summary

I am a Networking / Career Coach. Additionally, I developed The Complete Career Campaign ™; a highly customizable system that helps you achieve your professional goals by focusing on effective interpersonal networking mixed with Web 2.0 strategies. I will work with you to help you get where you want to go.

My success is founded upon my relationships with people...they are my most prized professional asset. Listening to client's needs and clarifying concerns is a strength I bring to my relationships.

My blog, at www.careercourageously.com , is designed to explore career issues including interviews, resumes, employee retention, "social" and in-person networking in order to create the Complete Career Campaign ™.

My professional history includes many different industries:

In addition to my own work, I also provide career counseling expertise to current MBA students and Alumnae at the Haas School of Business at UC Berkeley.
