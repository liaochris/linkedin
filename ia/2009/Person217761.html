Gene Simmons’s Summary

Certified Technical Consultant for enterprise data center environments. I've been a Consultant in the industry since 1998 working on various projects for companies ranging in size from small Law Offices to large multinational Fortune 100 companies. My career started off as a Microsoft Specialist and quickly migrated to Storage as SAN technologies became more prevalent. From there I stayed ahead of the curve with Disaster Recovery and Replication solutions as more and more companies required shorter downtime strategies and better recoverability as mandated either through corporate policy or Government policy. As this technology has become more common, I now find my self immersed in Consolidation, Virtualization and Green Datacenter Technology.
My aim as a consultant has alway been to stay in tune with the latest technologies so that I might better serve my customers though updates, guidance and training on those technologies thereby showing true value by making them more self reliant.

Gene Simmons’s Specialties:

Specializing in Storage and storage related solutions such as Disaster Recovery, Data Backup. Replication, Storage Area Networks, Capacity Management and Fault Tolerance. Also Specializing in Virtualization technologies such as Storage VIrtualization, Server Virtualization, Consolidation, Blade Technology and Cloud Computing.
