Chris Johnson’s Summary

Chris Johnson is a Music Producer / Music Supervisor / Composer, based at the Gigapix Film and Television Studios in San Fernando Valley, California. Known for his creative vision, programming and sound sculpting on the multiple Grammy Award winning, 12 times platinum (worldwide) "Fallen" by the modern rock group Evanescence. Chris is a musical "one stop" for his broad list of clients. As a producer and composer, Chris has worked with artists from punk to pop to divas including Grammy and Academy Award winners. Chris had a 2008 Top 20 Billboard Dance single with Justin Lanning, produced the solo album of 2007 Grammy nominee, Buckcherry singer Josh Todd, remixed music from the number one album by teen sensation Hillary Duff, and composed and produced the theme songs for the Los Angeles Clippers, Indiana Colts and Chicago Bears sports franchises. He has scored and performed the music for recent National Hyundai and Chevrolet campaigns, and has worked extensively on commercials, television and films including work with Academy Award winning composer Hans Zimmer.

Chris Johnson’s Specialties:

Music supervisor, composer, producer and problem solver for major and independent record companies. Artist, concept and project development specialist for the music industry. Consultant and general strategist for the entertainment industry and related businesses.
