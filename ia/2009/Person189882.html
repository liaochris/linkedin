Prem Gyani’s Summary

26 years in the IT Industry. An IT engineer in the days when a 10MB drive was as large as a desk drawer. A sales-bum in the days when you could do no wrong. An e-business director of a $100m play in mid 90's. An Internet entrepreneur with terrible timing in 2000. Executive Management positions across International markets for the last 5 years. Always made a difference. Energy is still off the scale.

Now outside IT and enjoying the freedom and wide open spaces. It's a whole new world.

I wanted to be the top sales guy in the companies I worked for - I almost always was. I wanted to make a million dollars on an Internet venture - I made a million pesos. I wanted to secure the Internet for kids - I helped do that in the UK. I wanted to do something that would help change the world's way of thinking before I was 50 - I joined Quickstart 9 weeks before my 50th birthday.

I want to help entrepreneurial companies go global. I want to open a world that is normally associated with only the largest companies. I want to help innovate at a time when SMEs need it most.

AT THE MOMENT I AM LOVING THE EXCITEMENT OF HELPING ENTREPRENEURIAL EXECUTIVE TEAMS ACCELERATE THEIR BUSINESS PLANS BY BREAKING DOWN THE WALLS THAT EXIST 25 MILES AROUND THEIR OFFICES. MY EXCITEMENT IS SHARED WITH THEM WHEN THEY REALISE THAT THEY CAN IN HOUSE ANYWHERE.

I ONLY ASK THEM TO “IMAGINE IF YOU COULD...”

TO DATE I HAVE SUCCESSFULLY GREASED THE THINKING OF OVER 30 EXCEPTIONAL OWNERS/CEOs OF ENTREPRENEURIAL SMEs (RANGING FROM START-UPS TO 10 YEAR OLD COMPANIES LOOKING FOR THE NEXT WAVE). ALL HAVE SURPRISED THEMSELVES ON WHAT CAN BE ACHIEVED.

NOT BAD FOR 18 MONTHS. FOUR OF WHICH WERE “PL” (POST LEHMAN)

Eventually, when I get over the excitement of meeting the types of people I meet every day, I will move on to the next challenge and succeed, again, in providing something that breaks the model of the world at that time.
