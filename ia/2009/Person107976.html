Jeff Flowers’s Summary

I have been playing baseball since 1973, and it has been a passion of mine since the first day of putting on the glove.

Since 2002 I have participated in PONY, Little League and Adult Softball programs in the areas of managing teams; field maintenance; I have held the position of Little League president, Little League Umpire coordinator, Church League softball vice president, as well as sports website management; etc.

As I have advised candidates over the past 12 years to find a career that they are passionate about, it is time for me to follow my own advice.

My goal is to provide excellent customer service to youth baseball/softball programs as well as to high school, college, university and other baseball and softball programs across the south central Texas area.
