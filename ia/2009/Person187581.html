M Coleman Horn’s Summary

With 15 years of experience working in product-development, Coleman has a strategic focus on the business of design. Having worked with Nike, Polo, Gravis, Nixon and owning MEDIUM and VAEL; Coleman blends a diverse background combining product development and business acumen. Accomplished in both retail and wholesale sectors with expertise in identifying new business opportunities, Coleman conceptualizes and launches products through hands on product development, usually at the factory level. Coleman is an extroverted leader and inspirational staff manager, with a solid foundation in industrial design, new media, and client relationships; his newest ventures PHYLA and VÆL deliver more quality work.
