Thomas Horan’s Summary

Petro Telemetrics Inc. is a solutions provider, bringing the most advanced telemetrics solutions to the upstream petroleum and natural gas sectors. PTI focuses specifically on each customer’s unique needs, crafting customized, cost-effective solutions that ensure a high level of security to protect data and assets.

PTI can function as a systems integrator, a system developer, an installation and roll-out contractor, or all of the above.

PTI’s primary offering is an advanced wireless mesh network that is capable of delivering vast levels of bandwidth securely and reliably in areas ranging from a single building, to an oil platform, to an entire oilfield. Our experienced team of engineers, installers, developers, and government interface specialists can deploy our network rapidly and efficiently, and maintain maximum network operations throughout the harshest conditions.

Once PTI’s wireless mesh network has been deployed, we can offer a wide variety of services that have been optimized for use on the PTI network, including but not limited to personnel and asset tracking, security, mobile applications, SCADA applications, and more.

The PTI network is also capable of connecting securely to most standard network interfaces. PTI has developed special interface devices to allow the connection of many legacy devices and systems safely and securely to the PTI wireless mesh network.

PTI can offer single or dual level encryption as well as enhanced virtual private network connections to guarantee extraordinary levels of data security.
