Nicolas Solari’s Summary

Nicolas C. Solari (M.H.C.I.M.A., C.H.A) is a seasoned hospitality professional by heart, from Lausanne, Switzerland, who started his career in the family restaurant.
He has served for over 20 years in some of the world’s renowned hotel corporations such as the Hyatt Hotels, Moevenpick, Radisson and Gloria Hotels in Asia.

He has been successful in integrating multiple cultures and dynamically developing local nationals to executive level positions.

Recently the opening hotel manager for newest and most luxurious resort in Sharm El Sheikh:The Maritim Royal Peninsula Resort & Spa

Also fully in Charge for the SHICC (Sharm El Sheikh International Congress Center)

Well-educated and forward-thinking, Nicolas Solari’s talents are reflected in his personal and professional lives. His professional skills range from Training & Development to Team Building, to Guest Relations, Concept Development to Strategic Management, Retention Marketing & Loyalty Programs to Financial Controls & Revenue Generation, and of course Renovation & Project Management to Food & Beverage and Rooms Management.

Nicolas C. Solari intends to further his career in the hospitality industry by continuing to actively participate in hotel operations, expanding his human resource, sales and marketing pursuits, and providing astute financial management for his own company.
