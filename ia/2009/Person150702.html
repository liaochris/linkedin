Marina Molenda’s Summary

Marina Molenda is a marketing research professional with five years of marketing research and client management experience. In 2003 she joined Walker Information, a customer loyalty research firm, and managed research projects for global and Fortune 500 clients. In 2006 Marina moved to Deborah Wood Associates Healthcare Communications Group. As a senior marketing project manager she focused on identifying physicians who are thought-leaders in specific disease states and analyzing the effectiveness of exhibit marketing at medical association conferences for pharmaceutical clients. Marina earned her Bachelor of Science degree from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University. She is pursuing a master’s degree in integrated marketing communications at Northwestern University.
