Jeremy Ross’s Summary

As a Practice Manager for RSA, I manage both the business development efforts, as well as the service delivery model associated with our Managed IT Services. These services are designed to provide high value at a fixed price. We provide a blended service that incorporates vendor management, network monitoring, and a fully-staffed helpdesk, all of which are intended to deliver enterprise-level support to the small and medium-sized business. Our service offering is focused on not only cutting costs, but also allowing an organization to re-focus their efforts on their core business processes.

RSA Corporation, aka. Robert Shields and Associates has 29 years of experience specializing in business and technology consulting, managed services, business technology projects, staffing, and recruiting. We can assemble entire teams to handle large IT projects including Enterprise conversions involving the Oracle line of products (such as Peoplesoft, Oracle Financials, & JD Edwards), as well as the IBM family of applications including WebSphere, Rational, Tivoli, and Z/OS. We also provide business continuity planning, CIO consulting, technology blueprints, infrastructure refreshes and build-outs. In addition, we provide clients with Help Desk Support and Remote Monitoring on a large and small scale. When it comes to IT, we know our business and hold ourselves accountable to understand yours, in order to ensure the technology strategy we present for your company aligns with your business processes and overall business objectives.
