Vishal Singhal’s Summary

Vishal is a strong technical player and possess good analytical skills. His problem solving techniques have been quite outstanding. Being from a part of an entrepreneurial family, Vishal inherits great managerial skills.

Vishal worked for 4 years with Infosys Technologies limited serving their Telecom clients - Sprint Nextel and Level3 Communications in U.S(H1-B Visa) and India. Having worked on operational section (OSS/BSS) of the IT system, vishal generated ardent interest in the field of Telecom and Finance. The "Finance interest" triggered his interest in the stock market in India. He managed his personal portfolio with returns over 25%YoY.

Vishal has a great sense of networking and making new friends quite easily. With a wide range of contacts across domain, industries and geographic locations, it makes it quite difficult to be not in touch with Vishal. His ability to go beyond to help others in many ways makes him special. He has been quite resourceful to his friends and colleagues.

Hailing from a business background, Vishal understands the tips and trades of market quite well. His strong interests in the field of Animation, Gaming and Design prompted him to start his first official entrepreneurial venture known as Digital Folks. He also is a partner at Sun Beam Merchantile Pvt Ventures (a sole importer for various quality products from the world to India) along with his brother.

Vishal has been instrumental in bringing in a lot of business deals for DigitalFolks. The recent tie up with huge corporations across US and Singapore displays his skills to effectively use his networking skills.
