Victoria Millard’s Summary

I primarily do Freelance Design and Painting for Stained Glass Studios throughout the U.S. and I also assist other exceptional stained glass artists.
Additionally, I am the Director and Producer for Vidreaux Productions which produces educational digital video discs for the Stained Glass profession. I am also the co- founder of The Antrim School.

Victoria Millard’s Specialties:

design, cartooning, painting and assisting other artists
