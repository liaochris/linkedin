Margaret Lembo’s Summary

Margaret Ann Lembo is the owner and creatress of The Crystal Garden, a Gift Store, Book Store and Spiritual Center in Boynton Beach, FL, in business for 20 years.
Margaret has been facilitating workshops, weddings, lectures and private sessions in a variety of venues. Margaret creates a safe, loving environment for the energy of spirit to guide the process, whether it is a private session or a large workshop. Margaret charms her audiences with her enthusiasm, grace, authenticity and depth of knowledge. These speaking qualities, combined with her comedic nature, bring teachings to her audience in a joyful way that aids in the integration of the teachings.
Margaret is a author, motivational speaker and spiritual teacher and travels to other stores and centers like her own to teach and counsel. Her six Guided Meditation CDs published in 2005 are distributed nationally to assist people with their meditation experience. Her first book will be published in 2009 by Llewellyn Worldwide.
She is a Reiki Master, Ordained Minister with the Alliance of Divine Love since 1991, trained Spiritual Healer, and certified Angel Therapy Practitioner®. She has facilitated hundreds of workshops and classes.
She provides certification programs for Crystal Healing, Spiritual Healing, and an Angel Messenger Program(TM) on a regular basis. She teaches classes on the Intentional Creation of Reality, Dreams and Dream Interpretation, Colors and Chakras, and the Angelic Realm as well as facilitating Drumming Circles and Crystal Singing Bowl Meditations. She has a full teaching schedule and teaches throughout the country at metaphysical bookstores.
She has been recognized by INATS and New Age Retailer Magazine as an authority in the world of crystals and gemstones. Margaret Ann Lembo is currently the president of the Coalition of Visionary Resources -COVR - a national trade organization.
