Swati Nitin Gupta’s Summary

I am a journalist looking for freelance writing opportunities with magazines, newspapers, and publishing houses.

Having been in this field for ten years I have experienced the best of the two main departments reporting and editing. I started my career as a trainee journalist at one of India's leading dailies The Asian Age based in Gujarat. I worked as a correspondent.

Prior to that I worked as a Reporter for a children tabloid The School Post privately distributed to them in various schools. It was a learning experience writing stories related to children.

After marriage I shifted to New Delhi where initially I worked for an IT specific tabloid called Indian Computrade News ICN as an associate editor. I was responsible for all the news content that went in the paper.

I later joined The Hindustan Times as content editor. I was part of a team of editors subbing the content online and sending it back to the parent source.

After a year and a half I felt it was time to go back to mainstream and that's when I joined The Asian Age again with the features department.

Here I learned how to design pages. I was in charge of some pages. I enjoyed reporting on the humane side of stories, writing about food, art, music, fashion, and religion among other topics.

Then for a year I took a break from journalism and tried my hands at copywriting at Via Infotech.

Later I got the opportunity to work for PlanMan Media as an assistant editor for their online newspaper The Daily Indian. Here again I was responsible for the content. Since it was an online paper I was co-ordinating with reporters all over the country for news.

Then I got a job at Gulf Daily News, Bahrain as a sub editor, an English daily.

Although throughout my career I was at the desk , it is reporting and writing that I enjoy the most.

And at Helium I got that chance. I have won two credentials here before geographical reasons forced me to quit the website .
