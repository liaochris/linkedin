Julian Joy’s Summary

Julian Joy, CSP, is Managing Partner at Joy Partners. He specializes in executive search & placement.

"I partner with my clients in a unified effort to create a search strategy for each unique position and execute talent acquisition strategies with unparalleled results. I keep the search process simple by taking a direct approach to penetrate my Client’s competitors and analyze their internal talent to deliver those professionals to improve the organizations of the Client’s with whom I partner."

He serves as Chairman of the Professional Section Policy for the American Staffing Association and is active in the Tulsa area Multiple Sclerosis Society.

Julian Joy’s Specialties:

Joy Partners specialize in the areas of Accounting and Finance, Information Technology, Engineering, and Healthcare.
