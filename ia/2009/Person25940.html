Benedict Dodd’s Summary

I have 8 years experience working in London and New Zealand's Internet industry (2 as a studio manager, 6 as a senior level freelancer), for a range of well respected agencies and organisations in a range of sectors (corporate, HR, charity, e-learning, TV etc.).

I have a first class design degree and excellent knowledge of the relevant industry standard design applications. I am also proficient in development and database development, mainly in X/HTML, Flash (using ActionScript 1/2) and CSS using PHP/JavaScript/MySQL.
