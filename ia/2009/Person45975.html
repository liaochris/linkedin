Chuck Steeg’s Summary

* Licensed FL & AL Real Estate Agent, and FL CAM (Community Association Manager)

* Founding Board Member of VRHP (Vacation Rental Housekeeping Professionals), serving on the Board of Directors for 9 years. Designated as a CVRHP.

* Board Member for the FVRMA for 3 years, serving as Annual Conference Chairman in 2007, and Website Chairman for 2007 & 2008.

* I have been in the Hospitality Industry for 23 years. The last 10+ years in the Vacation Rental Industry. I started with Meyer Real Estate in 1999 as the Housekeeping Supervisor, revamping processes and developing the first ever software specifically targeted at the challenges that face Vacation Rental Housekeeping Industry. Eventually I was leading all Service (Hskp, Maint & Assoc Maint) for Meyer Real Estate, until being moved to lead the Vacation Rental Management Division (Reservations, Owner Relations, Guest Relations & Bus Dev). Over that 5 year span, I led the growth and expansion of Meyer Real Estate’s Vacation Rental Program from 1,100 Vacation Rentals under management to over 2,000 that we manage today, which included expansion of operations from the Alabama Gulf Coast into Florida.

* I currently serve the Operational Efforts of Meyer Real Estate as the VP of Operations. These areas of responsibility once again have me closely working with Houskeeping & Maintenance, which I am very passionate about, as I have always been a Housekeeper at heart. In 2008, I lead the development of a first for the industry, which is a highly automated Logistical Distrubition Center for supplies to the 2,000 properties we manage spread over 45 miles and located in 180 condo buildings and 300 homes. We have changed the business model for Meyer Service which includes the recruitment and hiring of 50-120 International College Students (depending on ability locally to fill positions) that we house & transport.
