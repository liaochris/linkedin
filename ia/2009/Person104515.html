Janet Fernando’s Summary

My primary field of expertise is in healthcare delivery systems. As an RN, I managed the care of acutely ill patients in intensive care specialities including: Cardiac Care, Cardiothoracic Surgery, Neurosurgery, Urology, Orthopedic Surgery, Neurology and Post-Anesthesia Care Units. I continued my clinical work as an Independent Nurse consultant working with a Radiation Oncology practice managing administrative and clinical duties for the Maryland and US Cancer boards.

Throughout my career, post-nursing, I entered the elementary and high school education field, providing relief for the nurse in a private school setting. I had the pleasure of teaching middle school and high school students and participating in Early Childhood Development for infants, toddlers, and pre-school children.

I returned to clinical nursing for a short time in a primary care setting, working with a small practice comprised of 3 MD's, 1 PA, and 4 nurses. After the private practice was sold to a large hospital system, I transitioned into working for commercial healthcare insurance carriers, supporting large groups in administration and assignment of benefits. Assisting with claims handling, reimbursement, resolution of outstanding bills between members and providers for 60 individuals daily, I became very knowledgeable of how benefits administration for the Blue Cross/Blue Shield organization exists.

After a brief contract assignment with the State of California working with legal/healthcare litigation, I was recruited into Robert Half International, where I currently work as an Account Executive managing accounts and marketing to clients in San Francisco. In short, my job is to find other skilled healthcare administrative professionals a job of their own!

Each day presents new challenges, personally and professionally, and I look forward to a continued future in the Staffing and Consulting industry!
