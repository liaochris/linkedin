Geoff Morris’s Summary

Helping homeowners in three major ways:

1) Those wanting to STAY in their homes who can no longer afford their mortgage: www.LoanModServices.biz

2) Those wanting to SELL their homes themselves at a fair price (not some low-ball offer)

3) Those wanting to BUY a home, but who can't qualify because of overly-restrictive lender requirements

I connect motivated "For Sale by Owner" (FSBO) sellers with people passionate about owning their own home, while helping those wanting to stay in their homes with debt-management solutions.
