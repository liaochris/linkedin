Michael Cole’s Summary

I specialize in political development in conflict, post-conflict, and political transitions, focusing on the MENA-Eurasia regions. With experience in Iraq, the West Bank, and Washington, I am highly adaptive and perform well in difficult circumstances.

A lifetime of traveling, a lifelong interest in public service, and a sincere desire to contribute to solutions for pressing problems lead me to study and pursue a career in political development. My Master's degree in comparative politics synthesizes international relations, political economy, and area studies, each of which is required to achieve creative solutions for political, economic, and social problems.

I have worked with governments and NGOs, taught at the university level, and conducted graduate-level research. I presently seek a position in government, the private sector, or the nonprofit or think-tank communities as an analyst, program officer, or consultant. In addition to policy and development work, I am interested in applying my knowledge and skills to political intelligence for industry.
