Jill Cruxton-Wright’s Summary

I have over 7 years experience in property management, looking after my own property portfolio.
After being constantly disappointed with the quality of service I received from apparently professional companies, I gave up my previous career and set up my own company ... called Cherish, because I do!
My partner then set up his own property investment company.
Firstly, he began educating people on how to invest in property, safely. Then our business grew naturally as people were asking us to source them property.
We now deal directly with developers, so we are able to secure big discounts on new build and off plan developments.
