Ruth Garrett PhD, MSc’s Summary

Do you know how organizations have a strong desire to be the best they can be to attain their objectives and goals? Well Ruth assists them in making those desires, objectives and goals a reality.

Ruth's focus is on strengthing organizational and individual capability and capacity through consultation, facilitation, coaching and mentoring.

For the past 17 years, Ruth has been an organizational behaviour and change consultant, as well as a coach, mentor and facilitator assisting organizations in turning their strategic goals into operational excellence.

She has worked with a wide range of clients in the corporate, retail, manufacturing, public and education sectors to resolve issues and facilitate in challenges, which, in the past, hindered their goal of realizing the highest levels of bottom line growth.

As a consultant, who in the past grew a consultancy to 125 clients, Ruth recognizes the challenges an organization and its individuals face when growing an organization to its highest potential. Ruth is well known for her empathic and pragmatic approach to any issue or challenge, as well as for her energetic, proactive and experienced laser focus to these challenges and drawing out the best possible solutions.

Ruth earned a PhD researching the authentic engagement of individuals within organizations and a Master of Science degree in Organizational Change. She also holds a Certificate in Advanced Alternative Dispute Resolution (Mediation) from the Faculty of Law, University of Windsor
