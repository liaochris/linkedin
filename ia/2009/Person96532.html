Hugues Lacroix’s Summary

Mr. Hugues Lacroix is currently Investment Vice-president for the Fonds de Solidarité FTQ, Quebec largest venture capital fund responsible for the "Buy-out transaction" group.

He is looking for ownwers of profitable companies located in Quebec, with strong growth potential and wishing to sell it in the near future. His group can facilitate the ownership transfer to management and/or family and ensure it's perennity by providing a strong capitalisation.

Mr. Lacroix has served on many corporate boards (public, private and not-for-profit). Teaches Governance, Venture Capital and Due Diligence in various Universities. Other certifications include :
- Chartered Accountant
- MBA
- Certified Director (ICD.D)

Hugues Lacroix’s Specialties:

Mr. Lacroix has held various senior executive positions in the retail, real estate, distribution and consultation sectors and has extensive experience in governance, finance and strategic planning.
