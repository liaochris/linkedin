Mike Keough’s Summary

Rich mix of sales management, business development, and corporate planning for technology driven organizations. Diversely experienced from start up to work out, turnaround and rapid growth - remedying problems and creating successes. Developed new strategies that propelled new products/division to industry leadership. Catalyst for creating external business partnerships.

Combine relentless desire to succeed with dynamic leadership and business acumen to produce extraordinary results. Expert at energizing and revitalizing stalled sales teams to drive revenue growth and increase market share. Six Sigma Champion.

Mike Keough’s Specialties:

Raising equity capital, SEC corporate governance, merger and acquisitions, developing highly effective distribution channels, rapid growth, and turnaround experience.

Mike Keough’s Experience

  • CEO/President

    QComm International

    (Telecommunications industry)

    August 2004 — September 2007 (3 years 2 months)

  • CEO

    ClearOne Communications

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; CLRO; Telecommunications industry)

    September 2002 — September 2004 (2 years 1 month)

    Hired to lead the turnaround of ClearOne as company was going through an SEC investigation due to revenue recognition issues from prior leadership. Set up a new executive team consisting of a CFO, VP of Product Management, VP of Operations, and VP of Worldwide Sales.

    - Over hauled the entire corporate strategy--product plan direction, engineering teams, corporate positioning, development of a two-tier sales channel and marketing plans.

    - Skyrocketed sales by 60% while managing the SEC and DOJ issues. Rebuilt trust with reseller and integration channel partners and removed all channel conflict.

    - Increased inventory turns 3 times, reduced inventory on hand by 50% and decreased A/R by $22M to under $6M after leading the company through cash flow from operations with zero corporate debt. Implemented Oracle's ERP.

    - Developed new strategic partnerships and brought 5 new products to market, paving the way for new wireless/VOIP product road maps for the next two years.

    - Penetrated key vertical markets (government, health and medical providers) with online learning business with leading ASP and ISP partners.

Managed a $50M budget, and a sales team comprised of approximately 250 people (Channel Sales Managers, Territory Sales Managers, Inside Sales, Technical Support, Network Consultants, and Financial Analysts). Customers included channel partners - distributors, master resellers, corporate resellers, VAR's, catalog's and retailers.
