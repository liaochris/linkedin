Andy Parng’s Summary

• Currently managing the entire Trend Micro consumer product line (http://us.trendmicro.com/us/home/home-user/?WT.mc_id=2008HP_Home_Tab, Trend Micro Internet Security, Trend Micro Internet Security Pro, and Trend Micro Internet Security Online Service Edition) with a 60 million user base, 20 million active users, and over 300% revenue growth from US$130 million to over US$400 million in four years.
• Architected Trend Micro consumer portal site TrendSecure.com (http://www.trendsecure.com/) and developed Trend Protect (http://www.trendsecure.com/portal/en-US/tools/security_tools/trendprotect) from concept to finished product.
• Key contributor of the largest business deal in Trend Micro history ($6 million Trend Micro Internet Security Pro license contract with TD Ameritrade).
• Over 5 years of consumer Internet security product design, development, and management experience over a broad range of products and services including smart phone based mobile security, router based home network security, client and web based Internet security, etc.
• Received across-the-board #1 rating on Internet security suite, antivirus, anti-spyware, and anti-spam in the September 2007 issue of Consumer Report.
• Winner of the Trend Micro Solution Champion Award in 2007.
• Over 20 years of product development and management experience working with interaction designers, visual designers, software engineers, web developers, and project managers to develop world class products and web sites.
• Excellent academic records (4.0/4.0) with Ph.D. in Human Factors and MS in Computer Science.
• Founder of clubphoto.com and founding technical member of Healtheon/WebMD with in-depth knowledge of Internet and web-based application development.
