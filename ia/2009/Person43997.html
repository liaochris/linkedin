Chris Saracino’s Summary

I am a communications and media professional. I self started in the music industry and moved into the communications and marketing realm, where I've been since.

I founded Standard Media Services, Inc. with the intention of catering to the full spectrum of a client's media and communications needs in terms of product, service, and consultation. The aim and goal of our company is to become the leading provider of communications products and services for organizations and companies of any size.

I've developed and managed accounts that have included Johnson & Johnson/Lifescan Canada, The Palms Hotel & Resort, The Momentous Group/Zip.ca, The Government of Canada and Adobe. My core area of expertise is within the optical media and creative service/multimedia industries. I've been either involved or responsible for creative idea development in the planning stages of almost all of the accounts I've dealt with.

Chris Saracino’s Specialties:

My specialties are business development and sales in media development, optical media and specialty print, and creative services.
