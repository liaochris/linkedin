Ben Still’s Summary

Welcome to my profile. If you’re looking for a new Linkedin buddy, hit the big blue “Add Ben to your network”. You never know- we might end up liking the same blend of coffee (I’m a Single Origin YeeHaa addict).

I manage a successful web design & development company in Sydney, Australia called Red Ant. By successful I mean that we’ve been around since 1996, been profitable each and every year, and I still really love what I do. I work with 15 pretty awesome professionals, and we have some great clients and interesting projects.

What is it that I do? I’m quite involved in the initial phases of making a site- the thinking, planning, and wireframing. I love the initial creating, explaining and expanding of ideas- especially teasing various ideas out from everyone involved.

I also like the doing. Lots of people can come up with some form of great idea for a site, but the real trick is being able to pull it off. There is an incredible amount of attention that needs to be paid to so many areas. I could list out all the various projects I’ve worked on here, but you’ll probably get a better idea by taking a look at our site.

Outside of work, I live with two wonderful little people (and my wife), who keep me very busy reading stories, playing dressup, and general mayhem. I’ve given up my dream of joining the pro fishing tour, but the pro cycling career is still on the cards (Lance- I’m still available this year). I am told I have too many bikes, and my friends don’t understand why I shave my legs.

Ben Still’s Specialties:

Web site IA, wireframes, interactive web design, managing a web development company, paying more tax than I probably should, and reading bedtime stories.

Ben Still’s Experience

  • Managing Director

    Red Ant

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    1996 — Present (13 years)

    Red Ant is a web design and development firm, based in Sydney Australia. We are 16 Ants. We're a bit fanatical about this web stuff. We work on the visual design of web sites, from wireframe through to finished designs. We're also quite experienced with designing & making web sites for multi-variate testing.

    We also build sites. Everything from making the HTML templates that form the building blocks of a site, to creating applications that help you manage stuff on your site. We work with a development framework called Ruby on Rails, which helps us make some great stuff.

    Many of our clients are large consumer brands, so we've got quite a bit of experience at making sites that work well and are easy to use. Some of these generate quite a bit of traffic, so we've also got some experience in designing & making sites for high user load.
