John Smart’s Summary

Freelance consultant specialising in Enterprise Java, Web Development, and Open Source technologies, currently based in Wellington, New Zealand. Well known in the Java community for his many published articles, and as author of Java Power Tools, Helps organisations to optimize their Java development processes and infrastructures and provides training and mentoring in open source technologies, SDLC tools, and agile development processes.

John Smart’s Specialties:

Java, J2EE solutions architecture, Continuous Integration, Agile development practices, Test-Driven Development, Training, Mentoring, Maven, Subversion, Spring/Spring-MVC, Hibernate, JSP/JSTL, JSF
