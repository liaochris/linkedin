Phil Davis’s Summary

THEFranchiseLookup.com is a premier Franchise Search Directory. We have hundreds of franchises listed where future franchise owners can receive free information regarding the purchase of a franchise. Our goal is to provide our partners with the most creative franchise directory and powerful lead generating tool available. THEFranchiseLookup.com utilizes aggressive advertising and marketing techniques to maximize our site’s visibility to all potential future franchise owners.
We pride ourselves on our ability to reach your target market and to convey accurately your company’s franchising objectives and available opportunities. Our website generates highly qualified leads by offering future franchise owners accurate, thorough and easily accessible information. THEFranchiseLookup.com was designed to be user friendly and easily navigated. This allows future franchise owners to quickly identify opportunities and contact your internal Franchise Sales Associates.

THEFranchiseLookup.com harnesses the power of the internet by utilizing keyword pay-per-click campaigns, a blog based website for search engine optimization and viral marketing campaigns which will allow your company’s listing to generate qualified leads that you won’t find on other sites.
The majority of other websites offering similar franchise directory services offer multiple advertising packages or programs. As expected, with the increase of “bell and whistles” in each package, the price per month also increases substantially.

THEFranchiseLookup.com offers one all inclusive plan at one low monthly price.

Our 100% Customer Satisfaction Guarantee:
If you advertise your franchise opportunities on THEFranchiseLookup.com for one full year and are not completely satisfied with our lead-generating results, we will keep your listing active for a second year at no additional cost.
