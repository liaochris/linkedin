Tim Trent’s Summary

I'm driven by a mixture of fun and success. I love the things I do. I get passionate about Data Protection and compliance with the law ensuring positive marketing from ethical corporations. Part of this is running regular Data Protection workshops for the BSI, still more is helping large corporations understand the positive PR and business benefits that accrue to them once they take the moral high ground.

Tim Trent’s Specialties:

I specialise in Direct Marketing, Data Protection, Permission Based Marketing, Campaign based Landing Pages and Offers, Web site consultancy and search engine optimisation. Strong additional skills in sales oriented call centre selection, setup, and operation.

Alongside my consultancy practice I run partnermine.com, one of the newest and brightest dating sites around. My business partner and I conceived the idea in June 2004 and we went live on the 7th April 2005
