Vikram Chadha’s Summary

Vik is the Managing Director at the Enterprise Corp (www.enterprisecorp.com) and the Director of the Louisville Innovation and Commercialization Center.

He has helped 70 clients secure over $75 Million in financing, and 8 of his clients have sold their companies for an aggregate of over $150 Million.

Prior to joining the Enterprise Corp, Vik worked as the Entrepreneur-in-Residence at bCatalyst where he had the opportunity to assist in the process of identifying, evaluating, and funding innovative early-stage high-tech startups.

Vik serves as director of the Venture Club of Louisville. He was a member of the executive committee and the chairman of the presentations committee from 2005 to 2007.

He is the co-founder of Passion4Art (www.passion4art.com), one of the largest online communities of visual artists in the world, with over 2,000 registered artists from 65 countries.
