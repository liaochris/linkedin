David Neufeld’s Summary

David S. Neufeld, J.D., LL.M. (Taxation) practiced law for over 20 years before forming Integrity Financial Partners.
In his law practice he has advised individuals on sophisticated wealth planning, encompassing income tax, estate tax, executive compensation, asset protection, insurance and investment planning.

Within IFP, which designs and implements creative tax, estate and wealth planning solutions, David and the staff of IFP assist accountants, attorneys and insurance producers to deliver to their affluent and high net worth clients programs and techniques to achieve tax savings and enhanced investment returns primarily through the use of life insurance products. Of particular note is IFP's unique fee-based program--Life Settlement Analysis(t)--where the company analyzes life settlement offers in light of alternatives and consults on courses of action.

David is Co-Chair of the Insurance & Financial Planning Committee of the Real Property, Trust & Estate (RPTE) Section of the American Bar Association. In 2006 David became Chairman of the Synergy Summit, a leadership organization whose members include the top leaders from within the ABA, AICPA, SFSP, NAELA, NAEPC and ACTEC. In 2007 David was appointed ABA Advisor to the NCCUSL drafting committee for the “Uniform Insurable Interest in Trust” Law. David has been attorney-advisor to Judge Julian I. Jacobs of the United States Tax Court.

Recognized for his pioneering work with domestic and offshore limited liability company law, including drafting the Caribbean island of Nevis’ LLC ordinance, Mr. Neufeld was one of four lawyers in the state to be awarded the New Jersey State Bar Association’s 1998 "Advocacy Award."

David has published over 25 articles and has spoken around the world on the areas of his expertise, as well as having been featured in BusinessWeek, Medical Economics, The Wall Street Journal and CNBC, among others.
