Nick Vamvakas’s Summary

In view of my thirty-four years at The Lord Nelson Fine Dinning in Burlington, I would have to say that it is a 24 hr a day job. You have to get to know your customers and be there to greet them and make sure they have the best service possible while in your establishment. We pride ourselves on great food, good service and a wide varity of seafood, steaks and great wines. Every day that I wake up I wonder how can I make my buisness more successful. I think it is important to know what is going on in the community and act accordingly. I look forward to our challenges ahead of us and try to do better every year.
