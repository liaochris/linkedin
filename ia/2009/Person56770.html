Dave Williams’s Summary

I have worked extensively in both sales and marketing in the professional imaging industry. I have a passion for finding better ways to design, implement and sell complex software systems.

I have a history of defining new market and product opportunities, defining detailed requirements for the new product, leading the development process and leading the sales organization in bringing the new product to market successfully.

Dave Williams’s Specialties:

Digital Imaging Systems Design, Copy Writing, Technical Writing, Software Specification, Major Account Sales, Marketing Management, Sales Management
