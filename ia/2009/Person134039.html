Kostas Tsatsaris, MSc, MBA’s Summary

Kostas is currently Chief Technology Officer and Director, Strategy and Architecture at HM Revenue & Customs. He has 17+ years experience of working at senior level within complex Oil & Gas, Financial Services, FMCG, Logistics & Government organisations. He understands the commercial realities at Executive and Board level having experienced this himself first hand.

Kostas has worked as an employee and consultant for some of the world's Top 50 companies: SHELL, DHL, BAT.

Kostas has strong governance and strategy skills coupled with detailed understanding of formal processes, tools, frameworks and methods. Over the years he has acquired unique experience in business, application & integration domains of enterprise architecture
