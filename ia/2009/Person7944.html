Alex Crittenden’s Summary

My professional background involves almost 15 years of cumulative and varied experience in enterprise technology (software, services, and hardware) and strategic financial services. My ability to work with Fortune 1000 clients to build long-term relationships and bring value to that relationship has allowed me to succeed with organizations ranging from Best Buy to SuperValu to rue21 to American Eagle to Pep Boys and to many others both within the retail space and without.

My recent roles have been focused on sales and marketing; however, I also have significant experience in building and leading teams, territory management, business partner development, corporate branding, technology strategy, and building vertical focus.

Given my unique experience in both technology and finance, I understand how IT strategy needs to be in alignment with the overall organizational goals and how investments in technology and security can impact the business in meaningful ways.
