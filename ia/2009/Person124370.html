Jun Huang’s Summary

I have over fifteen years of strategic and tactical experience from the industries of financial services, health care, e-commerce (B2B and B2C) and telecommunications. Over the last few years I have been instrumental in linking strategic technology decisions with business processes and business drivers. My approach to architecture and technology involves thinking strategically but acting pragmatically, connecting IT architecture to business and customer demand, collaborating with key business and IT stakeholders, and taking into account other factors such as people, process and organizational culture.

Jun Huang’s Specialties:

enterprise architecture, web architecture, business process modeling and management, SOA, technology and vendor evaluation, IT project methodologies (UP and Agile), governance, mentoring and consulting, research
