Andrea Eppolito’s Summary

My career in events and special events began before I even realized that you could do this type of work as a career! Discovering a passion for this profession at a young age helped me focus with regard to the types of training, schooling, and employment positions I sought out. I have been incredibly fortunate to work with highly talented people in amazing organizations over the course of my career.

While I love closing the sale with a client or on an event, my greatest satisfaction comes from imagining the function from start to finish, utilizing all five senses to bring together a memorable function, and then watching all of the time and planning and thoughts come to life in the execution phase.

In all I just want to impact people, to design events and provide experiences that they would not have were it not for my assistance...to create memories and hopefully turn a handful of connections into trusted associates and friends. At the end of the day, I just want to be proud of what I have done.
