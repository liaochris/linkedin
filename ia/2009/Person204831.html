Richard Sink’s Summary

In today’s economy the implementation of well conceived plans & programs is essential. I help companies focus the most time, money and resources on the few projects that will deliver the greatest business value. As a result, my clients gain greater visibility into what they are spending and why and then position them to make better acquisition and implementation decisions on an ongoing basis.

I participate on business strategy development projects where I am responsible for developing an understanding of the IT strengths that can be better leveraged by your business. On your IT initiatives, I take a leadership role in leveraging your IT strategy framework to assess and develop a vision for IT as it relates to:

- Business and Strategic Alliances
- Revenue and Profit Generation
- Technology and Architecture
- Best Practices

Specific responsibilities:

1. Develop a thorough understanding of the client's business strategy and determine the primary implications and requirements of the IT function to deliver that strategy.
2. Quickly develop a baseline understanding of the IT function in line with the company's IT strategy framework.
3. Develop a future vision and a prioritized roadmap of achievable steps to evolve the IT function to maximize its value to the business.
4. Represent expertise in your business through best practices in deployment, return on investment models and outsourcing requirements.
5. Understand the concepts of enterprise architecture, application and technical design enough to work closely with your staff in these areas.
6. Ability to assess an IT organization's capabilities and develop challenging but achievable plans to improve the IT function.
7. Establish a how and when follow-up plan.
8. I share, and in many cases own, the responsibility for the success of your IT operations.

"Even more important than making the right decision, is to make the decision right."
