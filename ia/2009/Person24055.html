Barbara McGill’s Summary

Results-oriented leader who makes order out of chaos while aligning a team in pursuit of a shared goal. Takes complex and ambiguous tasks or programs, breaks them into manageable pieces, doing detailed process design, delivery and implementation. Strong cross-functional team builder and visionary who asks the hard questions and links ideas and people to accomplish goals.

Created and managed custom executive education programs for Fortune 500 companies and directed customer experience engagements.

*Managed the creative and delivery team that designed three-day workshops on Business Acumen taught to HR executives at a Fortune 100 financial institution. These workshops were designed to help HR personnel develop their financial acuity and hone their skills in interpreting business documents, understanding performance indicators, preparing a compelling business case, and assessing the financial implications of key human resource actions. They were rolled out worldwide.

*Directed (including P&L responsibility) nGenera's multi-client IT Leadership Development and IT Relationship Manager Professional Development Programs--targeted at developing Fortune 500 IT leaders.

*Facilitated the customer experience redesign for a major health insurance carrier including conducting research to understand the “Optimal Agent Experience.” Identified and designed specific improvements that strengthened the carrier’s differentiated value proposition to measurably improve customer and agent retention.

*Conducted focus groups to study Gen Y in the workplace.

Before joining nGenera, had over twenty years of cross-industry experience in the healthcare and insurance fields, and built a multi-million dollar national nutritional supplement marketing and distribution company. Earned MBA from the Harvard Graduate School of Business Administration.
