Michael Nassif’s Summary

• My first job in radio was at KWWK in Rochester, MN. I learned everything from spinning 45's to board operation of play by play to news, sports reporting, and weather. I eventually became the number one afternoon drive announcer in Rochester.
• I stepped into my last radio position as KMFX was going on the air. I started on the evening shift and was soon back to the afternoon drive in Rochester and back to the number one spot. I designed and updated the web sites for both Fox Country 102.5 and Laser 101.7. I was the Music Director and Promotions Director.
• Since I left radio, I have moved my entertainment agency and mobile DJ business from a part-time business to a full-time career. I have increased my business almost 65% since making the switch. This business started for me in 1976 as a part-time venture in high school, and I have been doing it ever since. I handle my own advertising, marketing, and networking. I have three DJ's and several different talented artists on my staff. I personally work with every one of my clients to plan their event. We plan everything together, and I and my staff implement the entire day for them. Regardless of the event (wedding ceremony, wedding reception, dinner, awards banquet, holiday party, business meeting), I handle all planning and sales. I then delegate to my staff appropriately, depending upon the client’s needs. I have also acted as event coordinator for those clients who prefer not to be as involved in the planning. I and my staff have arranged approximately 15 concerts for clients, and over 100 annual events for various clients over the years.
Recently I went back to college and obtained a degree in Graphic & Web Site design. I have since began a small graphics business. I can produce advertising items from the beginning of a project to producing a full line of items from Business Cards to LED message boards.
