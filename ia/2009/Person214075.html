Otmane Serraj’s Summary

Started in the Telecommunications business in 1996 with a pretty good experience of the development of Telcos in Europe.

Then moved to the Online Retail business with the creation of an ISP in 2000, which became the 3rd ISP in France in 2002.

Then sold the ISP in 2003 to a large european group leader in Membership & ePayment services, specialized in the Student Community across 5 countries and joined them as the Group CTO.

After having developed an Online Payment platform, joined one of the leading Group in the payment business, specialized in Electronic Transactions on the Point of Sale as head of Operations for Southern Europe in 2005 managing over 25,000 retailers.

In 2007, I took a senior management position at Dell in Casablanca (Morocco) to develop and manage their Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) center for Dell EMEA Enterprise business.

Recently, took over a Sales division for Dell France Small & Medium Businesses, based in Casablanca, and got appointed General Manager of DELL Morocco
