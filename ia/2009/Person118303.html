John Tremblay’s Summary

John is a proven entrepreneur and executive with a wealth of mobile, social media and web content delivery experience who has worked extensively with service providers and media companies developing new business plans and service offerings.

John Tremblay co-founded and served as Vice President of Marketing at Azuki Systems, responsible for corporate marketing and product management/marketing for the company's white labeled interactive mobile media service (SaaS) for content publishers, mobile operators and ad agencies.

Previously, John was Vice President of Market Development at Tatara Systems leading business development, strategic partnerships and early market requirements definition for solutions involving femtocells, mobile computing, mobile content and mobile convergence of 3G, Wi-Fi, WiMAX and IMS technologies.

Prior to Tatara, John was Director of Marketing and Business Development at Sycamore Networks where he drove vertical market strategies, application marketing programs and solution oriented sales activities for their industry leading optical networking solution. John was also an integral part of the Sycamore sales organization that grew revenues by $300M in 1 year. Previously at Intraplex, John defined and led a pre-WiMAX wireless broadband initiative, participated in the initial 802.16 standardization and oversaw marketing activities for multi-service access products for the broadcast and wireless industries. Intraplex was subsequently acquired by Harris Corporation in November of 1998 where he assumed additional corporate responsibilities. John has also held both product marketing and engineering positions while working on wireless and broadcast media technologies for World Access, Adaptive Broadband and Raytheon.

John has both high technology MBA and bachelor of science in electrical engineering degrees from Northeastern University where he also conducted research through their Center for Electromagnetic Research.
