Steve Murchie’s Summary

Seasoned software executive with experience growing new products and resuscitating old businesses in large and small industries, across a range of customer segments, and through a variety of channels. Specialized experience in business intelligence/data warehousing marketplace; large-scale Web content delivery; enterprise and indirect channel sales and marketing; product positioning and messaging.

Recently moved to a role building a local presence for a large angel investor group, and getting more intrigued by early-stage funding processes. Using the opportunity to refocus on old passions in alternative energy and sustainable manufacturing, and pursuing social entrepreneurship as a way to give back.
