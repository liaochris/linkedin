Jeff Rea’s Summary

I am drawn to challenges and get my greatest satisfaction in helping improve the capability of companies to drive growth. My passion was seeded by my early experience in Fortune 50 America where I built new organizations at Miller Brewing Company and Whirlpool Corporation to deliver against new product and new market segment objectives. It came to full fruit during the past ten years working within startups and with entrepreneurs developing and executing strategies to expand into new businesses, putting my foundational skills together with a hands-on practical approach to marketing, product and service development, channel management and sales.

I am currently looking for opportunities to leverage these skills in a startup to mid-size business, either full-time or on a project consulting basis.

Jeff Rea’s Specialties:

Brand marketing, product development, internet marketing, business planning and sales management
