Keith Rodbell Whiter Image’s Summary

My name is Keith Rodbell, Founding Partner at Whiter Image, the industry leader in the spa, salon, tanning, and beauty markets for innovative, effective, and stylishly packaged teeth whitening solutions for on-site and retail options. We are a proven leader in12 countries with premier accounts including Trump Towers, ULTA, Wynn Las Vegas, Starwood VO, Treasure Island, Boca West, Jon Ric Salons, and more totaling over 1500 locations. We are also a founding member of the Council for Cosmetic Teeth Whitening and co-authored the Best Practices Protocol. Whiter Image has also been featured in numerous industry publications including Global Cosmetics Industry (GCI), SELF, New Beauty, Beauty Store Business, Modern Salon, NAILS, BOHO, Spa Magazine, and Salons Unlimited. We have a reputation for excellence and dedication to helping our accounts realize results and profits with unprecedented dedicated account support.

For instant results, we offer one of the most advanced, yet simplified, products in the industry with our Premium Prefilled Tray Whitening Kit featuring patent pending European technology. This product is self-administered by your customers and delivers immediate gratification when used with our Advanced LED light system. This product offers incredible profits and outstanding results

For whitening on-the-go, we offer our stylish and effective retail TOGO Pens. The “Whiter Image TOGO Pen” includes 60+ applications of our triple-action, stabilized hydrogen peroxide whitening gel supported by hydrators and accelerators designed for results even with sensitive teeth. Our “Chic-Flic TOGO Duo Pen” features our premium whitening gel and our proprietary Lip Shine Plumper in one stylish casing with white stones and mini LED lights and mirrors built in for easy application!
