Nima Hakimi’s Summary

Mr. Hakimi is currently the co-founder and CEO of SafeSoft Solutions, a leading provider of innovative and powerful on-demand call center solutions. SafeSoft Solutions designs and develops cutting-edge contact center software for mid-sized and large organizations around the world, empowering them with the technology tools they need to increase efficiency, reduce overhead expenses, and achieve the greatest possible returns from their phone-based campaigns.

A well-known and respected member of the on-demand contact center solutions industry, Mr. Hakimi oversees key operational aspects of SafeSoft’s business, including:
• Financial planning and management
• Marketing and branding
• Strategic alliances and partnerships

Mr. Hakimi has a broad background in both the telecommunications and contact center markets, and has spent the majority of his career involved in the design and development of enterprise-scale software applications for both the B2B and B2C markets. He has established himself as a pioneer in the creation of state-of-the-art solutions for companies looking to enhance all aspects of their contact center operations. Over the years, he has built numerous software-as-a-service (SaaS)-based systems that have empowered call centers to eliminate their cumbersome and expensive on-premise applications.

Additionally, Mr. Hakimi has almost a decade of experience in various aspects of online marketing. Throughout the course of his career, he has successfully launched and managed numerous content-driven Web properties, building them from the ground up and transforming them into popular destinations for consumers and businesses alike. He brings expertise to SafeSoft Solutions in areas such as:
• Search engine marketing and optimization (SEM/SEO)
• Social media
• Email marketing
