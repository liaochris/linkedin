Jeremy Tuber’s Summary

Business savvy graphic designer:
A business savvy graphic designer is often a contradiction in terms; however, Jeremy is a unique combination of sharp business marketer and creative designer. This one-two punch provides clients with targeted marketing, advertising and design projects that yield outstanding results and a terrific return on their investment; they actually work. Companies looking to feel more confident and credible with their business brand, tired of getting lost in a crowd of competitors and always feeling like they have to compete on price, call on Jeremy to take their company to the next level of success.

Goals:
Develop trusted relationships with a select group of clients who need someone to be their marketing/advertising and design consultant. To make a significant positive social, emotional and financial contribution to the business owners that hire him. To provide value, service, enthusiasm and results beyond what clients expect.

Help young or inexperienced graphic designers avoid the rookie mistakes I made, and learn the insider secrets on how to run a more profitable, enjoyable freelance graphic design business.

Jeremy Tuber’s Specialties:

Jeremy has a decade of experience with brand identity, web design, marketing strategy, marketing collateral, advertising creations, direct mail, content editing, custom photography, photo editing/manipulation, and multimedia presentations.

Helping clients make at least 10%-20% more revenue and bring in more clients through marketing, advertising and design. Helping clients truly see through their customers' eyes, and what's unique about them and separating them from their competition.
