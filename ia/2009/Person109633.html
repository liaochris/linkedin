    The Jasmine Group

    (Logistics and Supply Chain industry)

    February 2003 — Present (6 years 3 months)

    Founder/CEO Jennifer (Middleton) Koehl created the Jasmine Group. Since 2003, the company has helped to cultivate customer success.

    The core competencies of the Jasmine Group focus on the Sales function. Although each customer has unique needs, the most prevalent methodology is as follows:

    1. Ascertain the overall business objectives and learn what eminent issues (if any) would prevent the customer from success.

    2. Review existing revenue goals; determine if customer is on track to meet those goals.

    3. Observe, coach, and participate with sales activities of existing Sales Representatives (meet with management to discuss personnel changes, areas of strength and opportunities for improvement).

    4. Commence Direct Sales activities; determine necessary levels of activity, number of opportunities in funnel, average duration of sales cycle, objections and/or issues potentially preventing deals to close.

    5. Document all findings; as a by-product of all documentation, recommendations, a "Sales Methodology" guide is created and provided to management for use as a management tool.
