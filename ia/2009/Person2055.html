Mark Dawson’s Summary

Mark Dawson’s perspective combines studio experience with a strong new media background. He brings to the industry a track record of success in launching products into nascent marketplaces -- including DVD, Online, Interactive Gaming, Interactive Television and portable media devices – as well as a strong understanding of brands and the consumer experience as necessary to create interactive experiences that are compelling and engaging.

Over the past 15 years, Dawson has built an impressive list of accomplishments with start-ups and established brands.

His accomplishments have included:

• The launch of the My TV Guide suite as a true cross-platform navigational solution for Cable, Satellite, Consumer Electronics, Mobile and Online;
• Launches of “FOX Sports Fantasy Football,” the first fantasy football game released by FOX Sports Interactive Media, as well as other PC and gaming console interactive titles for FOX Interactive;
• Development of a Digital Media Distribution Platform for portable devices;
• Executive production of high profile media events, including the Billboard Music Awards for LIVE-to-Internet productions for FOX;
• Development of digital media devices for Microsoft and Nintendo Gameboy Platforms.

In addition, Dawson has been responsible for driving the development of a variety of content and business initiatives of such FOX digital media properties as FOX.com, FOXSPORTS.com and FOXNEWS.com. He was also involved in early days of the DVD market as a charter member of the DVD Video Group – a working alliance for the standards and go-to-market strategy for DVD and produced 10 of the first DVD titles.

His Interactive Television projects include: social networking and personalized video guidance; and Video-on-Demand interfaces that featured Web-like navigation including rich media, trailers, metadata and search capabilities for major Cable Operators, Distributors and CE manufacturers.
