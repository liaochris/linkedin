Corey Lafferty’s Summary

LEED Certified and over 10 years of experience with a wide range of commercial, residential and institution projects. Such projects included a 56,000 sq.ft. Boutique Hotel in the South Beach Historic District, a landmark restaurant in South Beach, to a 4,000 sq.ft. high-end residential project with a monumental grand stair.

Recent responsibilities included but not limited to organizing and directing team members on multiple projects simultaneously while advancing integration of design intent with detail, function and constructability. Cultivating strong relationships with clients, building officials, team members, contractors and the community to ensure projects move in a quick and coherent pace. Evaluating and establishing office standards and protocols to create fluent operations to help boost productivity.
