Les Fehmi, PhD’s Experience

  • Director

    Princeton Biofeedback Centre LLC

    (Mental Health Care industry)

    1973 — Present (36 years )

    Dr. Fehmi is the Director of the Princeton Biofeedback
    Centre, LLC, located in Princeton, New Jersey. He
    received his Ph.D. in Physiological Psychology at
    U.C.L.A. in 1966 and he served as post-doctoral fellow
    at the Brain Research Institute at UCAL. He was an
    Assistant Professor at the State University of New York
    at Stony Brook for six years, where he conducted
    research in the area of attention and EEG biofeedback
    training.
    Since 1973, Dr. Fehmi has been in private practice in
    Princeton, New Jersey, putting into practical application
    the knowledge derived from his years of research in the
    field of biofeedback. He originated the OPEN FOCUS™
    Attention Training techniques in 1973.
