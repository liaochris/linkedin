Babatunde Banjoko’s Summary

CD and book cover counseling. Professional designer. Several record sleeve designs to his credit. Jazz, reggae and world music records in the U.S, in Jamaica, in England, France, Spain, Italy, in Switzerland, and Africa.

Ten literary book covers and interior illustrations for "Karnak house" in Britain.

Divers still photography Projects.

Consultancy on all creative fine art projects, Stained glass, Tapestry, pottery, clay sculptures and ceramic mosaic.

Babatunde Banjoko’s Specialties:

Logo creations, graphic layouts, film graphics, colour processing, finished artwork prints and packaging.
