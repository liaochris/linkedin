Thomas Demachy’s Summary

Scientific programmer for several years, I jumped on the opportunity to work in the game industry, when Titus hired me.
Working first as a programmer, I became project manager, and eventually took the team lead of the whole Titus Studio in Paris.

But the Titus group decided to shut down all development studios, and I moved to the publishing side, as production manager.

Working at Titus was a good experience, since I could work on so many different positions, but the public opinion of the company was very poor, and for good reasons. Finally, Titus group was completely shut down in January, 2005.

Since then, I work on creating a game publishing company, which should be active early 2006.
