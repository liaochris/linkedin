Serge Groeneveld’s Summary

We are called Hidden Solutions because we are experts at solutions that are not easily seen, but are critical to the success of a company.

LEAD GENERATION: Hidden Solutions above all else is a "cold calling company". Everything we do revolves around the ability to map out decision makers in key companies! Nobody in the business world likes “cold calls”. It is a dreaded term that brings shivers to even the best sales person. At Hidden Solutions we are expects at turning every cold call into a “warm call". Of course you only pay for good leads, not for the bad ones!

RECRUITMENT: We are not an agency, we are an RPO that only charges you for a filled position, and never for a fee higher than one month’s salary! At Hidden Solutions we are fast! We can provide you with candidates usually within 24 to 48 hours! With our global reach we can work your open positions anywhere in the world, and being that we speak 17 different languages, we can communicate with your Hiring Managers and candidates in their local language. Hidden Solutions has worked on over 7,000 different positions in virtually all industries and specialities. At Hidden Solutions we provide you with the “best talent in the market, not the best available”.

PRODUCT REPRESENTATION: If your company has a new and exciting product but cannot afford a sales force, or is an established company that is looking to get their products into other countries but does not have the international presence or experience....look no further! Hidden Solutions has access to distribution channels, wholesalers, and large retail chains worldwide. Plus we speak the local languages, and have a deep understanding of the foreign trade rules and regulations.

For all our services Hidden Solutions' company motto is, “Pay us for our successes, NOT our failures”
