  • General Manager

    Staged4more Home Staging & Redesigns

    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    February 2006 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    Staged4more is now a sub-division of Swift Real Estate Productions LLC.

    Founded in early 2006, Staged4more is one of the most unique home staging companies in San Francisco bay area that supports its clients not only through flexible home staging services, clients can also receive green real estate, online marketing/web 2.0 and graphics support.

    Filled with inventory, Staged4more sits in a 3,600 square warehouse in South San Francisco. To date, Staged4more has staged $45+ million worth of properties.

    Staged4more has received notable awards such as 2009 CSP Green Bud Business of the Year Award, 2008 Sam Walton Emerging Entrepreneur Award, 2008 awardee of Make Mine A Million business competition in Micro category, and several other honorable mentions. Staged4more has also done work for HGTV, interviewed by San Francisco Chronicle, San Mateo County Times and many other online and off-line media including Inman blog, Curbly.com, and How to Build A Successful Home Staging Business authored by Barb Schwarz.

    In 2009, Staged4more starts offering Live Green Live Smart 1-day seminar for anyone who wants to learn how to green their living environment and leverage green building for higher resale values. Realtors can take this 1-day seminar for LGLS CP designation, which also qualifies for DRE approved 8 CEU in Consumer Protection. (www.GreenREtraining.com)

    As General Manager of Staged4more, Cindy oversees the division, internal and external/site operations, and maintain personal contacts with potential clients, relationship building and retention, styling & designs, inventory management, marketing & PR of the company.
