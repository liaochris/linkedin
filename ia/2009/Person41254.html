Charles Collins, MBA’s Summary

Charles has over 10 years of IT experience. He has worked 8 years in SAP implementations and post-implementation support. Charles has knowledge of both business processes and expertise in configuration in the SD and MM modules. He has worked in the telecommunications, pharmaceutical and consumer packaged goods industry. Charles has full project lifecycle experience, from business process mapping and requirements gathering to implementation and rollout of various SAP modules.
