Shane Curd’s Summary

3 Areas of Experience

1. Product Manager with hands-on experience in field/remote implementation, beta program management, authoring of requirements documents/specifications.

2. Seasoned Client Account Management for both small and national healthcare accounts (Quest Diagnostics, Tenet, HCA, etc.).

3. Hands-on technologist experienced in hardware and software hacking, Asterisk VoIP, SIP, EDI, Linux, OS X, Windows, server/network administration, data center and infrastructure architecture, systems integrations, installation, management.

Prior to my current role as IT Administrator, I led consulting engagements for TechConsult (my own company) and worked for Onsite Health as the National Director of IT until my position was eliminated due to planned relocation to Washington D.C.

Before that, I worked for Healtheon / WebMD, MedUnite / ProxyMed, and other firms handling EDI Implementation and Account Management as well as Sales Engineering and Solutions Architecture.
