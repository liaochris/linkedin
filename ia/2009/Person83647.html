    Duties of the Trustee

    Township government is by far the most localized form of government providing easily assessable and efficient services to all township residents. Elected by township voters every four years, the Township Trustee performs many duties serving as the Executive of the Township. Below is a summary of those duties.

     Fire Protection
    Trustee must provide fire protection and ambulance service for the areas of the township not incorporated into a city or town.

     Emergency Assistance

    Trustee must care for the poor with the most economical means available. Trustee is required to see that the necessary needs of an individual or family are met.

     Burial Assistance

    Trustee is to oversee the burial of the indigent when no other means of payment are available.

     Cemetery Maintenance

    Trustee must maintain cemeteries in the township including those that have been abandoned.
