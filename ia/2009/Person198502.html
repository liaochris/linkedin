Rick Dolphijn’s Specialties:

Dr. Rick Dolphijn holds MA degrees in cultural studies, philosophy and cultural theory. His dissertation Foodscapes was published in 2004 by Eburon Academic Publishers iaw the U of Chicago Press. Currently he works on a book provisionally entitled Manifesto for the Mouth
