Kees van de Vendel’s Experience

  • Directeur

    GMT Logistic Solutions b.v.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    2006 — Present (3 years)

    GMT is an international solution provider with comprehensive technological know-how. A successful example of this is the Transport Management System Parsis Logistics. With over 20 years of experience in this industry, we have developed TMS for logistics servicer providers involved in transport, warehousing, shipping and the like.

    Parsis Logistics has been in use by a large number of leading hauliers since 1999 and the number of users grows steadily year by year. GMT Logistic Solutions is located in Duiven, just off the A12, from where the company provides services across the country.

    GMT’s philosophy is that data should be entered only once, thus reducing manual effort and errors. That is why Financial, Operational and Warehouse administrations are integrated into one system. GMT also provides interfaces with Route Planners, Route Optimisation Systems, Board Computers, Web Applications, etc., in order to automate and optimise actions.

  • Directeur

    GMT Systems b.v.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    1996 — Present (13 years)

    Headquartered in Hengelo, GMT Systems is part of the GMT Groep. Given its years of experience within the care sector, GMT Systems boasts in-depth industry knowledge. That quality is reflected in the MICOS software solution that GMT has developed for the healthcare sector. The application offers broad functionality that is tailored to the specific needs of the industry.
    This integrated solution is used daily in rehabilitation equipment, specialty medical shops, orthopedics, hearing aids, aids for visually impaired, homecare, lending/lease/rental sectors. All product, financial and document flows from manufacturer/caregiver to customer are fully managed and monitored with one system.


    GMT Systems has long been operating in the Netherlands, Belgian and German markets, building a significant customer base. The MICOS functionality is designed to meet all current and future changes in the industry.
