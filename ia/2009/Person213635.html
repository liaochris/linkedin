Josh Denning’s Summary

If you are considering consulting with us, spend some time considering your objectives and goals. Just to give you an idea if you are currently receiving 10,000 visitors to your site per month; your objective might be to increase that to 30,000 in 3 months time and to double your conversions. This is a very tangible objective and allows us to work with you and help you achieve that goal.

Its also great if you have your current benchmarks for example: current monthly traffic, conversion rate, average sale amount (is this made up by a few big sales or is it all similar transactions), an idea of your budget and your current cost to acquire a customer. The more information you have the better we can benchmark you and start to work on doubling or even tripling your profits.

The great thing about conversion optimisation is, if you have already hit break even in your cashflow, then when you double conversions it doesn't just double your profits because all your fixed costs are already covered; it could be 1000% extra profits or more. This is not uncommon, quite the opposite. This often amazes clients.

We are open to talking with anybody, we love our work, please call any time.
