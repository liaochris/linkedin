Tito Bautista’s Summary

Management and Information Technology Consultant with over 20 years of experience. Highlights include.

• Formed an international consortium, which included Unisys, Cooper's & Lybrand, SGS of Switzerland and won the bid to computerize the Bureau of Customs of the Philippines.

• Instrumental in introducing Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) technology to major manufacturers, brokerage and shipping companies in the Philippines.

• Designed and developed a television network show distribution system for Warner Bros.

• Designed a B2B Invoicing, Credit System and Miscellaneous Billing System for Mercedez Benz Corporate which was distributed to over 240 Car Dealers across the country.

• Consulted in the integration of Traveler’s Insurance Premium billing with MetLife’s billing system after the acquisition of the former.

• Consulted in re-engineering General Electric Credit's Lease Application System. The new system was then distributed to over a thousand GE Leasing Centers across the country and streamlined the lease application approval process from 4 days to 24 hours.

• Managed a team of programmer/analysts and programmers and implemented a very complex system that was NBC's first major development effort on an IBM environment. This system was responsible for scheduling network shows throughout the season and clearing/tracking over 200 primary affiliate stations for compensation purposes.

• Consulted for AT&T in the implementation of an on-line Inventory Tracking and Invoicing System of hardware, software inventory expenditures for over 50 Company Data Centers located across the country.

Tito Bautista’s Specialties:

• Serves as a key link in providing Business Clients, consulting and support in the areas of planning, marketing, training and utilization of automated systems.
