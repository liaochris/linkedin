  • President

    Delco International Ltd.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Wholesale industry)

    1984 — 2003 (19 years )

    As President of Delco International Ltd., Rob led a successful manufacturing and distribution company of tableware products for the food service and transportation industries.

    Rob was known for his responsiveness to changes in the marketplace from both a strategic pricing and product development perspective. He spearheaded all promotional and advertising programs including collaterals, promotional sales programs, advertising and public relations.

    Rob produced all-time highs in profit and revenue for Delco with a team of 125 direct and indirect staff. This led to the successful sale of the company in 2000 at 10X EBITDA to Oneida Ltd, the world’s largest flatware and dinnerware manufacturer.

    Delco was often a recipient of both the Long Island Top 50 Private Companies and 25 Fastest Growing Private Companies award sponsored by KPMG Peat Marwick and Hofstra University School of Business.
