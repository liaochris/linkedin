Nadege Hopman’s Summary

Nadège HOPMAN is Manager, CO2 Storage Operations, South Europe and Africa at Schlumberger Carbon Services.
She has been working in the Oil and Gas industry for the past eight years in the Well Productivity Testing business. She was based mostly in Africa and held various positions, from field engineer to services manager. She moved to Paris as product manager for multiphase meters applications in gas fields, which have since enjoyed a very successful deployment. Nadège joined Carbon Services in 2008, and is leading projects in Europe and Africa.
Nadège holds a Masters in Civil Engineering, a Masters in Geophysics for the IFP School in Paris and an MBA from INSEAD.
