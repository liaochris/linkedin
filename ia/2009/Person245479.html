Walter Pacifico’s Summary

I've run a very productive career in Feature Films, TV commercial, TV shows, Music Videos, Documentaries and Corporate Videos, spanning over 15 years.
During these exciting times my focus has been on educating myself on the art, the technique and the business of filmmaking.
By the year 2001 I'd reach the point where I felt absolutely confident in the solid knowledge and expertise gathered through my previous works as a technician and then I decide to take it to the next level and position myself as a Cinematographer.
Fortunately it wasn't long till I found myself on demand.
With time and hard work I've got rewarded with participating on amazing projects, many of them involving travels to some mesmerizing places around the world.
One of my last experince of this kind was on April 2008, when I was commisioned to shoot a Documentary in Addis Abeba, Ethiopia.
Suddenly the most interesting world of characters, situations, locations, culture, history, arquitecture, geography and poltics was unfolding in front of my eyes.
It was this mesmerizing experience that pushed me to start persueing a new and higher level of career development, this time as a writer-producer-director.
After completing the R&D stage of the project, I'm today inmersed in a very passionate although difficult task; raising the necessary investment for the film I envisioned when I was in the fascinating hub that is Addis Abeba.
This has became one of my main professional goals now, and my near future will be undoubtedly tied up to it.
