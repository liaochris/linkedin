James Kellogg’s Experience

  • Amazing Hypnotist, Master Illusionist, Hypnotherapist

    James Kellogg Jr.

    (Entertainment industry)

    January 1981 — Present (28 years 7 months)

    Hypnotist James Kellogg Jr. is an award-winning speaker and entertainer, but his biggest joy comes from turning ordinary people into superstars. His show is a delightful mix of comedy, music and hilarious hypnosis, where audience members discover their inner celebrities.

    He has appeared in television shows on NBC, NBC Fox, MTV, Court TV and the Discovery Channel in Europe and America as well as several news stations. He has been featured on TLC’s hit show LA Ink when he hypnotized Kat Von D to quit smoking He has donated his skills to charity groups including Children’s Hospital the San Diego Blood Bank and the American Cancer Society. He traveled to Belize and performed 5 shows to raise funds for homeless children there. Eventually, he sold his business and devoted his full energies to helping people.

    More than ten years ago, he became a registered, certified Hypnotherapist and is currently working towards his clinical degree in Hypnotherapy so that he can teach others who want to make helping others their passion too. He began working with individuals, helping people overcome phobias and other issues like stopping smoking, building confidence and losing weight. He helped one person drop from 350 pounds to 180 pounds over a fourteen-month period. Kellogg’s work was so persuasive that the weight gain never returned.

    Today he has more than 30 self-help CD’s and MP3’s available to the public. He loves sharing his gift through speaking engagements and entertainment, because hypnosis is one of the few sciences where people can change themselves without drugs or surgery. It gives him an enormous amount of pride to know that he has guided someone through their path of self discovery and personal empowerment. “Every person has a superstar celebrity inside of them” he explained. “I love freeing that inner spirit!”
