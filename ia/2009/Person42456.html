Radhakrishnan Srinivasan’s Summary

Dr. S. Radhakrishnan has over 15 years of experience wide and deep experience in several areas of information technology and is an expert in various enterprise architecture disciplinants. Over the last 10 years he has been functioning as chief enterprise architect, technology manager, architecture manager, lead solution architect, and lead information technology governance expert.. He can strategize as well as execute technology programs, manage people and budget. He possesses extensive knowledge in the following areas:

1. Information management (Data warehouse, data marts, ETL, Corporate Performance Management, Balanced Scorecards)
2. Knowledge Management (Enterprise Content management, Collaboration technologies, Enterprise portals, Enterprise Search,)
3. Business Applications (Oracle ERP, Several package solutions)
4. Enterprise Integration (SOA, Enterprise Service Bus, BPM&E, Legacy Integration)
5. Enterprise Infrastructure (Servers, Networks, Storage, Mobility)
6. Enterprise Security (Identity and access management, enterprise single sign on, directory and access)
7. Enterprise IT Processes (ITIL, CMM, TOGAF)
8. Business Process Management

Radhakrishnan is a strategic thinker and an operational executive; he is a self initiator and posses ability to influence and sell ideas and see through the implementation; he is a thought leader as well as a seasoned technology manager.
