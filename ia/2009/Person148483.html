Marcelo Fleitas’s Summary

We are Network & Company, a staffing and consulting agency (Est. 1979) that specializes in finding the best talent available at rates that work even for difficult budgets.

What sets us apart from other agencies:
1) We have real industry experience and are qualified to screen applicants thoroughly, which simplifies your life. We have a database of over 30,000 pre-screened candidates, and respond to orders in less than 24 hours.

2) We partner with our clients, we aren't just a vendor. By setting expectations together, we help you make smart decisions regarding talent acquisition.

3) We work within our core strengths, not over extending ourselves to more business than we can handle. Thus, we are the top supplier for our clients, with relationships going back decades.
