Steen Andreasen’s Summary

I have been working with Dynamics AX/Axapta since the very first beta was released in 1997. I was a team member on the very first AX project localizing AX for the Turkish market. For 4½ years I was a part of the team who designed and programmed the HRM modules for AX. I have since spring 2007 worked as a freelance AX developer.

I have experience with both designing/programming Dynamics AX verticals and AX customer solutions. In the past 10 years I have been a team member on about 50 different AX projects in Europe and USA with roles as AX developer, Architect or managing the development project. I have development experience on any AX
modules in the standard package and experience on how to integrate AX with a wide range of programs. I have a special knowledge on the HRM modules as I know the full history since the first release of the HRM modules.

In 2006 I wrote and published the first Dynamics AX programming book called MORPHX IT which is available in 4 languages and sold to VARs and AX customers in more than 50 countries.

Also I am used as expert witness or litigation consultant
in Dynamics AX matters.

Check out my website at http://www.steenandreasen.com
