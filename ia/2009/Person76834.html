David Warner’s Summary

I have been in the Special Events business for 32 years. I started out as a Mobile DJ and quickly branched out to all aspects of the industry. I am also a co-founder of the Northern New Jersey Chapter of the International Special Events Society (ISES). Currently, I am their Immediate Past President. This worldwide association has over 5,000 members. For 2006, 2007 and 2008- Events Plus has been ranked the #1 Event Planner in New Jersey by the NJBiz Newspaper. Visit the Events Plus website at www.eventsplus.com for 40 pages of pictures, descriptions and pricing.

David Warner’s Specialties:

We provide a large assortment of equipment and ideas for any Special Event. We own and operate our equipment. The most popular being Casino tables, Carnival equipment, Corporate Game Shows, Photo Novelties, Variety Acts, and a huge selection of themed backdrops. We are the exclusive providers of "Event Radio". This is a portable radio station that broadcasts a 90 second loop to the FM radio of cars that are coming or going to an event.
