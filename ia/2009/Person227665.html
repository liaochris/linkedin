Sven-Ingve Rasmussen’s Summary

I have been part of a team at EIDEL developing a secure communication system for the Royal Norwegian Air Force in the period 1997 to 2001.
I have invented a concept of Remote Crypto Distribution System, RCDS, which is now one of EIDEL's key defence products (2003-2006).
I presented the RCDS concept at the International Data Link Symposium in Sydney Australia 2005 and in Copenhagen, Denmark 2006.
My later focus was on development of a new State of The Art Flight Test System (EDDAS) for SAAB's figther air craft Jas Grippen, and development of a new security system for space systems based on the RCDS concept (2006-2009)
At EIDEL we had the best Software & Hardware designers in Norway under my management! I left EIDEL June 2009.

I am Executive member of NIFRO " Norwegian Industrial Forum for Space Activeties".

I am owner of Rasmussen Spacetronix Consulting, a personal activety I set up in order to stay in touch with the Space Industry and Data Link Enviroment.

My overall professional Engineering goal is to be a part of, and contribute with key technology and inovative concepts to a deep space exploration program.

My professional Carrer goal is to be the Managing Director of a large Technology Company prefereably operating in the defence or space segment.

Sven-Ingve Rasmussen’s Specialties:

Design of advanced space electronics, advanced system architecture design, Modern management, Development of new technology and product concepts, Advanced programable logic, Tactical Data Link Systems, Crypto Distribution systems. Space Electronic System Design. Concultancy Services.
