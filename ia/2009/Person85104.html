Geoff Tucker’s Summary

Geoff Tucker has over 11 years of economic research experience in both the private and public sectors and is widely recognised as one of the leading experts on the Irish property market, having previously been responsible for managing the research function at one of Ireland's leading estate agencies and mortgage brokers. Geoff also makes regular contributions to both print and broadcast media on a wide range of economic and business related issues.

Geoff Tucker’s Specialties:

* Irish national and regional economics
* EU and national macroeconomic policy
* Government housing policy
* Housing demand and supply forecasting
* Property finance
* The retail sector and retail planning in Ireland
* The Irish labour market
* Social inclusion policy
* Project appraisal and policy evaluation
* Sectoral studies
* EMU and the changeover to the euro
* Monetary policy and interest rates
