Jack Klemeyer’s Summary

People describe Jack Klemeyer as creative, energetic, passionate, inquisitive, supportive, and loyal.

Jack graduated from Ball State University with a Bachelors Degree in Business Administration. He minored in Art. Jack spent over 13 years with a major multi-line insurance company. There, he qualified multiple times for high achievement in sales and sales with profit awards. Actively involved in Toastmasters International since 1984, Jack has held several offices from Club President to Division Governor.

Currently he is an active member of the National Speakers Association and the International Coach Federation. He has been a student and practitioner of effective sales and communication for over 25 years.
