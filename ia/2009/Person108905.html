Jeff Ziegler’s Summary

After being qualified as a teacher, coach and then trained in NLP during 2008, I have formed Specialist Resourcing and Coaching LLC (previously known as Specialist Search Limited). We provide in-depth coaching and mentoring services to individuals and teams as well as headhunting services for a range of technical industry clients. I am still Owner of Euro-Contrat (a specialist contract IT personnel provider for France and the EU) and Managing Partner of Certus IT Contractors in France.

Former UK Director of Infinity Search, Inc. and previously experienced interim manager turned CEO/Managing Director of Interim Management Service provider in the UK. Previous roles include Director of Strategic Marketing (ICL-Tplc), CEO (Newlands Relocation), Marketing Manager (Industrial Purification Systems), Managing Director (AllWorld UK Ltd.) and numerous others.

Other professional experience includes Marketing Manager (Fujitsu-ICL Computers), General Manager/Marketing and Business Development Manager (FCB Associates), Executive Director - National Security Advisory Board (Council for Inter-American Security) and others.

Jeff Ziegler’s Specialties:

From bases in the UK and France, we offer coaching, recruitment and staffing solutions for permanent, long and short-term contracts across all disciplines and industry sectors.

Specialist Resourcing and Coaching LLC is an affiliate of Infinity Search and exclusive UK provider for the Worldwide Executive Search Network (WESN).

Specialist Resourcing and Coaching LLC - helping individuals and companies go from good to great!
