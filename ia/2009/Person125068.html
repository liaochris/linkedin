Johann Van Tonder’s Summary

Over ten years experience in online media, having started out in newspapers (internet wasn't around).

In the late 90's I got involved with 24.com at its inception on the content production side and soon became one of the first newspaper multi-media editors.

Post-MBA (focusing on e-strategy), I joined the management team and now work on Internet strategy at Naspers.
