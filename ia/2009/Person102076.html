Jala Smith-Huys’s Summary

After leading the creative direction of nationally-known natural and specialty foods grocer New Seasons Market for almost nine years, Jala has taken her design, promotion, project management and retail branding experiences on a new adventure. Embark Creative is a full-service graphic design shop specializing in start-ups and retail brands and collaborating with diverse clients around the world to create effective, functional and successful visual solutions. Jala also hopes to offer consulting services in social media marketing. Have laptop — will travel.

"Jala Smith was one of the first hires I made when we started New Seasons Market in 1999. She is the person who led the development of the visual aspects of virtually all of our advertising and marketing materials. Jala deserves much credit for our success. She is an extraordinary project manager and supervises others gently but firmly. Jala also has the surprising combination of skills of being a very talented graphic designer and an excellent writer. I would give her my highest recommendation for almost any design project."

-- Brian Rohter, CEO New Seasons Market
