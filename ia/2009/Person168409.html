James Chan’s Summary

A receipient of the EDB Singapore Inc. Overseas Scholarship, James graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a Double Major: B.Sc in Electrical & Computer Engineering and Engineering & Public Policy. He then spurned cold dreary Boston and MIT for the warmer and more welcoming sun and shores of California to complete an M. Sc in Management Science and Engineering (MS&E) at Stanford University.

James returned to Singapore in 2006 to join the International Operations team at Industry Development Division of Infocomm Development Authority (IDA) of Singapore, before switching to IDA's equity investment arm, Infocomm Investments Pte. Ltd. (IIPL) as an investment manager. James is also New Media Strategist and Consultant to the CIO of National Trade and Union Congress for its flagship U-Portal Web 2.0 + Enterprise 2.0 project.

Apart from the occasional gaming (iPhone, PC, Mac), photography (20D + L lenses turned Lumix LX-3), jogging and swimming, James also loves to see the world (through his eyes and his lenses) with the love of his life, Uzia.
