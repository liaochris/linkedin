Michelle Mathews’s Summary

I have been working in the IT training sector for 20 years. I hold the following qualifications MCT, MCASI, MMI, TDipITS, TDipIT (Adv), IITD Dip in T&D, CTP and am a member of the ITBTI. In 2000 I established Training Solutions and now work on a contract basis for many local, national and multi-national companies delivering a wide range of IT Training Programs.

I have extensive experience in managing trainers, training programmes, training needs analysis projects, training delivery, courseware development, assessment and projects during this time. I am confident with all forms of instructional delivery - classroom led, one to one tuition, demonstration seminars and online learning. I specialise in delivering high quality, professional training, and have extensive experience with Microsoft Office, Lotus SmartSuite, Word Perfect Office and Open Office applications. I also have widespread experience delivering Train the Trainer, bespoke applications, soft skills, and non-Microsoft courses as well as floor walking and rollout project experience.

I have excellent courseware design and development expertise, combined with full cycle training needs analysis, training programme design and delivery skills, and is also a qualified assessor for ICS Skills, FETAC and JEB.
