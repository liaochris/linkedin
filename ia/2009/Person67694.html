Dr. Susan Aydin, D.C.’s Summary

I gained my sense of business knowledge at a young age, when I became a supervisor/assistant manager at the age of 19/20 for a national bank, NationsBank,N.A. For the following 13 years, I managed to put myself through school to complete a Computer Information Systems degree to a Doctorate of Chiropractic Degree. In the duration of those years, I found myself helping others in making wise business decisions in enterpenuerships. Before becoming a doctor myself, I quit an incredible salaried job in the IT force to become a receptionist for a chiropractor in Atlanta, GA. Through this time period, I met some incredible and impressive doctors in the chiropractic profession. I devoted nearly 10 years of my life, visiting practices and interviewing doctors to gain a sense of knowledge of how to successfully run a chiropractic office. With this being said, it was only then when I was influenced by a number of doctors to seek a Doctorate of Chiropractic Degree myself. In the process of being heavily involved in the profession, I created and collabrated, through out the years a Chiropractic Business Management Package. With this tool, I performed numerous seminars to students and graduates alike, educating them on essential tools to become a successful chiropractor. The package ranges from negiotiations to business plans to patient management. I do not believe in competition, I only believe in my profession; therefore, for every single chiropractor that I help succeed I know that there is a patient's life who has changed for the better!
