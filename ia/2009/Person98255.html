Kristen Eckstein’s Experience

  • Executive Producer

    Imagine! Studios

    (Graphic Design industry)

    August 2004 — Present (5 years 5 months)

    Imagine! Studios is a professional art and media production company located in the Triad of North Carolina.

    We offer top-quality graphic design for mid-quality prices. We work primarily with the small business seeking to create a corporate brand to compete with big business - without the corporate prices. Our specialty is book design - covers and interior layouts.

    The "I am Published!" Book Coaching Program was developed to help authors change the world - one book at a time. Having owned our own publishing house, we coach authors to do what we do - become their own publisher without the use of a subsidy press - saving $500 on their first book and $2,000 on additional books.

    We also offer custom photography, including product shots, architectural interiors and fine art portfolios. Our nature and macro photography available for licensing and may be viewed on our website.

    Our illustrators are top notch with a special attention to detail. They can develop your children's book or greeting cards into something that can compete in today's market. Some of our illustrators also offer their illustrations for licensing to greeting card companies, calendar companies and more.
