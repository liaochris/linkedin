Graeme Richardson’s Summary

Graeme brings a creative blend of fine arts and design. He is well versed in the latest design applications including all of the Adobe CS3 suite as well as Mac and Windows operating systems.
As a freelance graphic designer he has created concepts for a variety of subject matter, including print ads, logos, and interactive media. It was through freelancing that he sharpened his capability in areas such as digital photography, interactive design, product and logo design. Samples of his work can be found at bygriz.com.
