David Molden’s Summary

I specialise in NLP applied to Leadership and Coaching in business. I do this through a mix of coaching, a range of prestige open programmes and in-house assignments. I have authored and co-authored 6 books on NLP in 19 different translations, and an audio CD.

With over 25 years' corporate experience, I have worked in a range of industries including IT, Retail, Engineering, FMCG, Government and SME's.

Clients include: ADAS, Quicksilva Solutions, Philip Morris, Greene King, Lehman Bros, B&Q plc, Computacenter, Carbogen-Amcis, Wagamama, Customs & Excise, Inland Revenue, Ox&Bucks Mental Health NHS Foundation Trust, International Paper, Logicom, London School of Economics, Alstom, IXEurope, JP Morgan, Ferguson Modular, LA International.

Specific Projects
- Coaching and facilitating exec teams to increase their performance.
- Coaching sales people, CEO's and key decision makers.
- Facilitating change among teams.
- Modelling effectiveness among high street retail store managers.
- Modelling effectiveness among IT service engineers.
- Helping managers get to grips with the issues of outsourcing to India.
