Michael Novack’s Summary

Senior executive with progressive vision of media production, sales, marketing, and technology operations. More than 15 years of experience directing landmark television, Internet, mobile, and editorial media ventures. Proven capacity for team leadership and change management, development of creative business models in international markets, and focus on results based on innovative strategies. Seeking world class, leading edge initiatives not intimidated by the future.

Michael Novack’s Specialties:

Leading media companies to transform and adapt to new advertiser and consumer needs. Managing cross channel audiences and investment, generating exploitable business intelligence. Creating strategic revenue driven production models and international alliances based on convergent distribution channels to stimulate development of social networks, communities, segmented audiences, and behavior profiles. Delivering sustainable monetization and survival strategies for media groups in the new market.
