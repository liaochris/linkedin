Day Merrill’s Summary

As a Transition Advocate, I help people get from where they are to where they want to go. On the corporate side, I have extensive experience helping individuals, teams and organizations increase work/life effectiveness and satisfaction. On the personal side, I use my teaching, coaching and co-creating skills to help people make changes that will positively impact the quality of their lives and relationships.

My goal: supporting you in reaching for the stars; even if you fall short, you'll have the sun and the moon!

Experience:
Human capital management firms in Canada and the US. Prior roles include VP Consulting at KWA Partners, Senior Consultant at Feldman Daxon Partners, Career Services Manager at JVS Toronto, Regional Practice Head for the Performance Optimization division of Thomson Learning, Vice President of Specialty Services for DBM Canada and Manager of Career Services for Citibank NA. Previous career positions: Associate Director of the MBA program at New York University’s Stern School of Business, secondary school educator and 23 years as a parent.

Education:
Graduate, Coach U Core Essentials Program
MA, Communication from Wesleyan University
BA, English/Education from Connecticut College


Affiliations:
International Coach Federation, Association of Career Professionals International and HRPA (Organizational Behaviour Instructor in the CHRP program)

Day Merrill’s Specialties:

Expertise using coaching, training, consulting, leadership and management skills to meet a variety of needs: human capital management planning; career transition and outplacement; organizational change and transition support; team building; communications and conflict resolution; performance management, leadership development and coaching.
