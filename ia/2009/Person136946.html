Larry Bushey’s Summary

As a technology advocate, I use my communication and facilitation skills to help my podcast listeners, blog readers, and training seminar participants. I assist them in building their confidence and competence with alternative technologies such as open source software, and with mainstream commercial applications as well.

My broad background in professional training, technology management, management and sales gives me a unique understanding of using today's technology to get things done. As creator, producer and host of a top-rated Internet audio program since 2005, I help computer users with practical, day-to-day advice on how to use their personal technology and software applications.
