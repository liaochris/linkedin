Paulina Wojnar’s Summary

I have worked in editing, project management, marketing and teaching over the past few years. I have actively blogged since 2006 on topics ranging from technology and media to arts and socio-economic issues at ligress.wordpress.com. I have gained in-depth knowledge of online marketing through conducting research for my graduate dissertation on language use in online advertising and its relation to the Gricean communication theory. I currently work in the Media Arts & Marketing department of Space (spacestudios.org.uk), where my work focuses on planning an interactive site for artists and expanding and promoting a new online resource dealing with issues at the intersection of art, technology and social change.
