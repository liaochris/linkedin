Jim Lambert’s Summary

Successful Marketing Executive in Mobile, Enterprise and Commerce Software Markets

My 20-plus year career in technology industries has included marketing, sales and operations roles in multiple disciplines. It has resulted in a powerful record of accomplishments, including:

• As senior marketer and member of executive staff at Octane Software, lead all marketing efforts through start-up development, launch and eventual sale for a record $3.2 billion dollars (largest sale of private software company ever).

• Grew branding and awareness using unique and industry leading tactics such as: Industry portal (SUPPORTZONE.COM); analyst and executive Webinars (as many as 1,200 attendees); establishment of new market category with analyst and press community (IRM); high-profile customer and executive summits.

• Established extensive partner marketing program driving growth, participation and sales. Results included development of co-op marketing program, variety of global lead generation events (with receipt of over $700k in partner provided funding) and integrated PR.

• Created and managed global marketing organizations and operations in Europe (UK, France and Germany), Asia Pacific (Australia, Hong Kong and Singapore), Latin America (Brazil, Mexico) and Japan.

• Achieved significant demand generation growth through awareness and direct marketing programs that utilized web marketing, direct mail, Email, Webinars, advertising, Internet search engines, referrals, installed-base programs, and telemarketing.

• Managed Creative Services team and operations providing print and multi-media, sales and corporate collateral, advertising and Web-based demos.

• Managed marketing and operations aspects of key acquisitions at Quintus (a CTI software company and a call center consulting company), Nortel (three sales and service companies) and Pacific Telesis (various small-to-medium size telecom equipment suppliers in western U.S.)
