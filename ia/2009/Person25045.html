Brian Berthold’s Summary

A professional writer/producer. Six years as a creative director, eight years as a broadcast writer/producer. Reputation for going the extra mile, juggling multiple projects. Adept at creating concepts and copy for broadcast, direct, retail, and the Web. Both a visual and a strategic thinker. Ability to manage campaign generation, writing, layout, editing, and art direction.


Copywriting/Scriptwriting
Created and directed unprecedented campaigns integral in helping DAZ 3D Productions achieve first three million dollar months during first year of employment (2007-08).
Earned numerous national awards including five Tellys, a Mobius and a Gabriel for broadcast (TV), and national print awards. Many radio spots played nationally during prime time, including “Time Out.”

Creative Direction
Provided leadership and creative direction that helped Deseret Book launch new catalog and increase sales 130% during 2005.
Collaborated effectively among executives, creatives, vendors and other stakeholders.
Led creative teams in execution of company directives.

Broadcast
