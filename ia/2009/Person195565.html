Brian Redfield’s Summary

Brian doesn’t subscribe to any particular style, but one that is constantly evolving. His work is fresh and unique and always on target with his clients. When he’s not designing for the masses, Brian resides in Long Island with his amazing wife Tina and their Chocolate Lab, Sidney.

On every project, large and small, Brian oversees the strategic direction of the project. In our business, the client always comes first. We enjoy helping smaller brands define themselves in the marketplace and committed to providing them with professional, high-quality, affordable print and web design solutions in one convenient place. By understanding the bigger picture and finding out what’s fresh and different about you we can help you develop a digital strategy that speaks volumes about your business.
