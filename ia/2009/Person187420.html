Phil Wilkins’s Summary

Phil Wilkins is a software architect and project manager with a wealth of experience in enterprise and time sensitive applications, with over twenty years experience in the software industry. Prior to joining Specsavers Phil managed development at SeeWhy and architected the integration layers. Havinf previously been the technical/architect lead at Hi-Q for developing a middleware product for adoption across the back office applications within the UK armed forces. Before this Phil performed technical and team leading roles for EDS and GEC group on a range solutions, from military mobile radar through to Trans-Atlantic ATC and innovative operational displays of real-time synthetic environments, employing a diverse mix of technologies from Ada with X/Motif to Java (SE/EE) and Oracle.

As a side line Phil runs several websites for community organisations using CMS based technologies.

For more detailed information on skills and experience please visit my website.
