Kevin Carlson’s Summary

He is an engineer who “thinks outside the box”, with an extensive background in systems automation, such as: Cisco, Microsoft SQL, Windows Servers, Solaris, Exchange Server, and many more. In addition he does web designing, graphic arts and photography. Kevin is on the cutting edge of technology and gives his client the benefits of his knowledge and skill.

Kevin specializes in meeting the needs of his clients. Managed Services was the trend mentioned years ago in systems like N-Able and Kaseya. Having worked with numerous clients he recognized what was needed;
• Lower monthly invoices;
• Stabilized networks;
• Leading the client in technology.

All of Kevin’s clients were unique in their own way, however they all had one thing in common, with his expertise, “their networks could run themselves”. From Automated Boot Recovery CD’s to User e-mail profile auto creation, the end-user learned self-help. Kevin soon became the Chief Information Officer (CIO) of the business and was able to concentrate on the projects, client relations, and growth.

After watching companies only sell monitoring systems, and make just a decent return on their investment, Kevin knew there had to be something better. About a year ago he found a patch management system. Scripting was built into this revolutionary framework. Things that could never be done before, such as looking at all Exchange Server database sizes at a 50K foot view, running Ad-aware or Spybot and reporting on the findings was just the beginning. The future of managed services at a Lower Cost and more Stable Networks is now available. Kevin knew what was to be expected, IT Managers/Personnel working on a reoccurring basis could now be on a more limited time schedule, saving the client much needed time and money. The end result is now your company becomes leaner and better equipped to handle more business.
