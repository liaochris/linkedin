Shawnna Childress’s Summary

Ms. Childress is an Associate Director with Navigant Consulting, Co-Author of the book "eDiscovery Plain & Simple" and also the Co-Founder of Women in eDiscovery, a non-profit world-wide organization, that brings together businesswomen interested in technology related to the legal industry and provides opportunities for them to help themselves and other businesswomen grow personally and professionally through leadership, education. The Women in eDiscovery has grown to over 4,000 members since its founding in 2007.

Ms. Childress has more than 15 years of experience in the legal services industry. She started her legal services career in her family’s court reporting agency where she learned first hand the intricacies of depositions and the court room. Ms. Childress then moved into the legal technology realm where she spent 10 years at LexisNexis in roles such as Legal Research Specialist, Legal Editor and Large Law Firm Account Manager along with a few years at Westlaw as their Manager of Premiere Accounts. During a hiatus from LexisNexis, Ms. Childress worked as a Legislative Assistant in Washington DC to Congressman Joel Hefley where she spent time assisting the Congressman with legislative issues.

During the initial boom of eDiscovery in the early 2000’s, Ms. Childress was offered a position with Fios, Inc, a national eDiscovery and computer forensics provider which she accepted. Ms. Childress then Co-Founded Computer Experts Associates which was a Computer Forensics and Electronic Discovery company which was sold to a national provider. She then went on to become a Legal Consultant with Stratify, Inc. and then moved to Encore Legal Solutions, Inc as their Director of National Accounts.

Ms. Childress is currently an Associate Director with Navigant Consulting where she consults with corporations on setting up Litigation Readiness Plans and law firms on matters involving eDiscovery and Computer Forensics.
