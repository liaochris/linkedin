Oonagh Monahan’s Summary

Oonagh Monahan t/a Alpha Omega Consultants is based in Dromahair, Co. Leitrim and has extensive experience in devising, delivering, facilitating and managing technical and business support programmes for a range of SME enterprises in general, with specific experience in the food industry, and cross-border programmes.

Fusion programme (InterTrade Ireland) – currently the Managing Agent in the region for the FUSION programme for Intertrade Ireland, www.intertradeireland.com/fusion which engages with SME businesses and collaborating with Third Level Institutions.

Oonagh has recently been appointed as Facilitator and Managing Agent for the Sligo Women in Business Network, which is funded by the Sligo County Enterprise Board.

She is a mentor with Enterprise Ireland for new food start-up companies.
