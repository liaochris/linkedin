Doug Kyle’s Summary

Doug is a solutions provider with expertise in custom business solutions, especially in the industrial, oil & gas, energy and construction sectors. Doug's upbringing and subsequent career in and around the trades makes him a natural fit in working with people at all levels in the industry including CEOs, project foremen, project controls, internal systems groups, union workers and office administrators.

Doug has numerous partnerships with application providers and solutions developers to ensure you get the best solution. Working on a project-by-project, consulting basis, he is happy to share his insight and expertise.

Expertise includes real-time data capture in challenging environments, union rules, audit trails and developing intuitive systems to minimize learning curves for low-tech / high-turnover user bases.

Projects have included senior roles managing the development of the industrial applications OnCore (www.ddytech.com) and Digital Time Capture (www.DigitalTimeCapture.com). Custom solutions for industrial companies looking for a competitive edge include solutions for materials management, rate management, fleet tracking, scheduling systems and drug & alcohol and employee certification tracking.
