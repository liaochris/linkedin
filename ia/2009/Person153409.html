Martha Roden’s Summary

My goal is to "make the complex simple."
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
I work with high-tech, low-tech, and no-tech companies to create easy-to-read documents, easy-to-navigate Websites, and easy-to-use software.

I even take esoteric and philosophical material and make it engaging and understandable for the lay person.

No fluff, no filler, no fanfare ... just plain and simple writing and design. That's my style. And here's how I do it:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
- I focus is on doing the simplest thing that will work for software interfaces, Websites, textbooks, or manuals. Just because it's high tech, doesn't mean it has to give the reader a headache!

- I use my skills in clear writing, presentation, and user-friendly design to ensure that deliverables are attractive, intuitive, and easy to use. After all, if it's hard to use, it will hardly be used.

- I make sure your customers can find what they need and understand what they read on the Web or in print, which leads to a more positive user experience, faster adoption, and increased sales.
