Mimi Tenace’s Summary

Is your business getting the personal exposure that it needs to result in customers calling you? If not, then I can help you get your face out in front of the people looking to purchase your products and/or services, and introduce them to the most important part of your business….YOU! Using video, and harnessing the power of the internet. My philosophy is, "What good is a website if no one ever sees it?" I will drive people to your website using videos posted to the many video hosting sites. This shows your potential customers your personality and some will even call you before they ever click on the link to go to your website. If you don't have a website, no problem, I can work on something that will allow you to start gathering information from those who visit your website. Building a list is key in marketing and I will show you how to do that effectively as well. By using these and many other facets of marketing effectively online I will create a "web" that will attract and catch more customers.

I enjoy helping businesses that currently have a website which doesn't show up on the 1st pages of Google when they are searched for with relevant terms. If you are a business who is spending a lot of money on print advertising and are not sure if it is getting you a good return on your money, or if you are paying a service to "optimize your website" and can't track the results to see if the service is working, then you would benefit by a system where you can track and see results.

I cannot describe to you the joy I feel when one of my clients comes to me and tells me how they are getting more customers and their business is increasing. There is no better feeling in the world than being able to show others how to grow their businesses and watch them achieve their dreams by assisting with the most important, but sometimes most overlooked part of business…..marketing. I love to work with people, that is my passion!
