Flavio Piccioni’s Summary

I have developed a deep experience in the mobile telecommunication industry, retail, PR and marketing and have successfully developed marketing and merchandising strategies for some of the largest operators in Italy, combining the technical knowledge of my degree with my interpersonal skills.

I am an accomplished lecturer and public speaker and have regularly run training courses for clients and executives.

I am currently looking for new opportunities internationally, to work with or build a strong team to deliver on challenges and growth potential in the telecommunications industry.

I always enjoyed being in an international environment, attended short courses at foreign universities, and have been involved in international groups in my hometown. I love to travel and to learn cultures and foreign languages: English is an essential tool and I feel lucky to be fluent in it. Furthermore I studied some German, Dutch, Romanian and Spanish.

I am also a good organizer of events. In the past years I organized meetings, social and cultural events for people from all around the world in the Rome area.

Flavio Piccioni’s Specialties:

Sales, Marketing, Public Relations, Technical Trainings, Events Organization, good knowledge of mobile devices (PDAs, Smartphones, Mobile Phones, Windows Mobile, Symbian, Software applications for mobile devices, GPS systems - PNDs and Navigation softwares), deep knowledge of the Italian Retail Market, really fluent in English and willing to learn more languages.
