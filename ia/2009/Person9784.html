Allen Goh’s Summary

Allen Goh is a Partner in Squar, Milner, Peterson, Miranda & Williamson, LLP’s (Squar Milner Peterson) Audit and Assurance Services Department and has approximately 10 years of public accounting / audit experience at both local and Big 4 Firms and approximately 5 years of experience in private industry.

Allen Goh is also the Treasurer, Executive Committee member and Board member of the Friends of the Santa Ana Zoo, Board member of Families Forward, and formerly an Instructor at Orange Coast College. He also holds the following certifications: Certified Public Accountant (CPA), Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE), Novell Certified Engineer (CNE) and Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA). Allen was also a CPA exam question writer.

Allen Goh’s Specialties:

At Squar Milner Peterson and KPMG, Allen has been responsible for providing audit and other services to technology and manufacturing / distribution clients including experience with various software, manufacturing, and retail clients. Additionally, Allen also has extensive experience with real estate and construction clients, including experience with various commercial real estate developers, construction contractors, property management, partnerships, joint ventures and corporations.
