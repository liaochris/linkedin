Cathy Arreguin’s Summary

Instructional designer focused on effective and compelling educational media, online instruction and virtual worlds. Specializing in the design and development of educational virtual world environments, instructional experiences and activities. Also specializing in training educators in effective educational virtual world design and development. Interested in the integration of virtual world technologies with mobile and other web-based e-learning.

Cathy Arreguin’s Specialties:

Design of virtual world experiences for teaching and learning, online educator professional development, curriculum development and project management, distance education, ADDIE, ISD, educational video design and development.
