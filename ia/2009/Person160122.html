Susan Cook, RDCPro™, NVSI-Certified’s Summary

I have been a full time agent in Northern Virginia for over 5 years and have sold over $10 million each year. I have extensive REO experience and great 'ratings' with companies such as IAS, Goodman Dean and National Default Servicing and many more. I always complete tasks on time and in an accurate manner and go above and beyond what is needed to accurately assess a property, repairs, etc. so that the seller has all the information they need to make a decision. I currently have over 30 active and prelist REO listings and my staff and I are prepared for more business. My full time staff and I understand the needs of asset managers and the fast-paced REO industry. We have a very aggressive marketing plan to get REOs sold. We upload an interior virtual tour for all of our REO listings which allows the listings to be viewed 300% more online. We have buyers' agents on call, and pride ourselves on showing buyers our listings within 24 hours. I look forward to working with you!

We are also very experienced with short sales. We will analyze your situation, keep you updated on progress made with your lender, apply all of our marketing skills to each of our listings, no exceptions. We can and do close!

For all your distressed sale needs, call the Metro Homes Team!
