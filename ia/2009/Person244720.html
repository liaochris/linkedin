Victor Mateus da Silva - LL.M’s Summary

Educated in law, I gradually built up an excellent sense of Business. I have to admit that I am now attracted more by the business side of transactions than the legal side only.

Recent conversations with several lawyers and my job experiences helped me discover new opportunities. Being a lawyer is foremost being a story teller towards an audience, with a convincing message.

Through my education then , I came to understand the importance of words, of their meaning, their link with emotions. Words, Intonation of voice, Physical presence are just among the few tools used by lawyers: the right dose of all these components makes the alchemy bring about the most amazing impact on people.

Personally, I chose my stories to go beyond courts and cases, and help individuals and companies tailor their stories, to their audience.

That is what Vanguard * Creative Communication is all about. I am currently redesigning the concept and write the business plan to expand it. (More info soon)
