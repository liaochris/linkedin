Megan Fitzgerald’s Summary

I'm an expat career and personal branding coach and the founder of Career By Choice. I help expats worldwide use their unique value or personal brand to build a career or business that fits who they are and their international lifestyle.

Having designed my own portable career that supports my expat life in Italy, I take a creative, entrepreneurial approach to help others find the same freedom and success in their professional lives. It's the combination of my global experience and natural ability to quickly identify and strategically explore opportunities that equips me to empower others to take action and make choices that work for them.

My clients are expats ready to explore new frontiers in their careers and businesses. They are energized by the idea that communicating their unique strengths and value in a compelling way both on- and off-line is the key to their success. Eager to learn and ready to take action, they enjoy applying new strategies to thrive in the global marketplace.

With over 15 years of experience in career and business development, I've worked with such professionals and entrepreneurs from across the globe. My passion for travel, culture and service has taken me to over 25 countries on 5 continents to live, work or play.

Continuously learning and applying strategies for my clients' success, my credentials include:
• Certified Personal Branding Strategist - 1 of the first 100 worldwide
• Certified Career Coach and Executive Coach
• Certified Online Identity Strategist – 1 of the first 35 worldwide
• Masters Degree in Multimedia Communications

I've shared my expertise in expat careers, businesses and personal branding with global audiences, including the Women Who Tech Summit. I've been featured in expat publications such as Career in Your Suitcase and Italy Magazine.

When not working I enjoy travel, photography and a caffè macchiato to start my day.
