Pascal Coppens’s Summary

Currently active as CEO of Sundance Asia Ltd., a private company developing and distributing high-performance processor and I/O modules for use in single and multi-processor DSP and FPGA solutions in a variety of fields including, imaging, communication, simulation, industrial control.

I also act as CEO and owner of PaCoVision. PaCoVision provides Eureopean high-technology companies with a spectrum of services related to entering or growing the Chinese market.

I have been involved in multi-processing technology for over seven years. At Eonic Systems, NV, a software and hardware solution provider of high-end Digital Signal Processing systems, I served as business development manager Asia working out of the Shanghai, China office. In 2001, when Eonic was acquired by Wind River, I became product marketing manager of Wind River’s Embedded Platforms Division. End of 2003, I co-founded PolyCore Software with CEO Sven Brehmer, previous president and CEO of Diab Data and senior director of Wind River Systems’ Embedded Platforms Division. In August 2005, I decided to set-up my own company in Hong Kong.

My eductaion includes a Bachelor’s degree in Business and Engineering from the Business school of Solvay at the Free University of Brussels, Belgium and a Master degree in Oriental studies from the University of Ghent, Belgium.
