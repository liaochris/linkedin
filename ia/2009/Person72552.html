Steven Cooke’s Summary

INTRODUCING:

Steve Cooke is known for his tremendous passion and energy in the staffing field and for 15 years has been involved in all aspects of recruiting, hiring, coaching, developing and mentoring professionals.

Steve has accelerated his growth in the Staffing and Recruiting Industry by partnering with Emergent Search Partners, a boutique leader in new technology roles.

Led by Scott McDonald - a visionary in the staffing and recruiting world - Steve has an opportunity to harness the human energy of some of the top recruiters in North America to provide outstanding roles to our candidates and immediate solutions to our clients.

Among some our clients are industry leaders and emerging start up ventures that are cutting edge dynamic opportunities for our candidates.

We've been on the ground floor of some major players in the Carrier Network Space, Mobile and Wireless and include top software vendors.

Steve can now be reached at scooke at emergentsearchpartners.com

Prior to Emergent - Steve was the director at Lightspeed Human Capital Management, where he had played an integral role on the executive team, taking on a variety of management positions as the organization grew.

His career progression within the firm has reflected his significant contribution to the growth of the firms “Permanent Solutions” division – his specialization in understanding new and emerging technologies.

He was the driving force behind the development of our technology platform, enabling Lightspeed to recruit and locate tier one talent, track interview progress and create a stronger relationship with both candidates and clients.

Steve bases his recruiting technique on integrity and on a foundation of understanding from the candidate’s perspective, where their interests lie.

He continues winning clients and candidates over, with an honest approach to building trust.

Steven Cooke’s Specialties:

I specialize in Senior Sales opportunities for emerging technology companies in Security/Networking/Storage and Mobility. We have some exciting opportunities in the ISP Global Marketplace and are looking for top level SVP candidates.

As well I specialize in recruiting for SE roles all over the US and Canada.
