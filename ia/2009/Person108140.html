Jeff Krock’s Summary

Jeff Krock focuses his expertise in three areas: transforming human suffering, thriving in the 21st century, and accelerating personal, professional, and spiritual growth.

He has given more than 30,000 individual and group consultations and has led hundreds of meetings, seminars, and training retreats. His clients and students have come from all walks and all stages of life.

Goals: Jeff's current work is focused on bringing his breakthrough body of knowledge to a global audience via downloadable Kiyamani training courses. As Kiyamani grows, Jeff looks forward to building his international presence.

Jeff Krock’s Specialties:

Integrating personal, professional, and spiritual development.
Demystifying the spiritual. Development of language and technique to simplify and speed up the evolution of human consciousness. Help people awaken and live their higher levels of consciousness in their everyday world.

  • Principal

    Kiyamani helps individuals and groups:

    1. Awaken the higher states of being that have been written about for centuries.
    2. Operate and communicate from the highest levels of wisdom, clarity, and decisiveness.
