Scott Tegethoff’s Summary

I believe that to be a valued media partner, it’s essential to continually seek new ways to approach situations. As a deeply creative and innately curious person, I have used my desire to discover novel perspectives in order to develop insight and build unexpected, innovative marketing programs that push the envelope and truly resonate with brands.

I have over 20 years of experience putting these skills to use for two of the world’s largest marketing and advertising companies, IPG and Publicis Groupe. Currently as CMO and Global Managing Partner of Universal McCann, I passionately lead over 50 people in the U.S. and in more than 20 countries across the globe to nurture partnerships, create inventive cross-platform solutions and produce quantifiable results for iconic brands including the Coca-Cola Company, ExxonMobil and L’Oreal, directing over $1B in expenditures.

I coach my teams to deliver innovative consumer-based communication insights that add impact to each client’s brand message and speak with consumers from a deep understanding on the emotional and rational levels.
