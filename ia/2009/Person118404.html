John Whitsell’s Summary

I currently provide Lean Process Manufacturing Consulting for an aviation company. While in the Navy I served as a ship’s Chief Engineer, a Diesel Engineering Inspector, a Fleet Maintenance and material Officer on two staffs and in director level management positions. I have hands on as well as a senior management background.
Most recently I was the Inspection and Logistics Operations Manager and Corporate Facilities Manager for Honeywell HPG. This encompassed the inbound and outbound document and inspection, and shipping of aviation hardware from their facility in Roanoke, TX. In this position I managed the FAA certification compliance of over 500,000 SKU’s and 178,000 distinct parts. I managed the daily work of three supervisors and 130 team members. As the Facilities Manager, I was responsible for three corporate sites. I received two Honeywell Silver and two Bronze awards for my work.
Prior to that, I was the Plant Maintenance Manager for GAF Materials Corp. I managed the maintenance, repair and training of the largest roofing manufacturing facility in the U.S... This included the equipment as well as 13 acres of grounds, warehouses, office buildings and forklift repair shop. I developed the use of the MAXIMO CMMS into a management tool for daily and long term maintenance planning. I managed a $4.8 million dollar capital improvement and preventative maintenance budget with five supervisors and 27 maintenance personnel.
