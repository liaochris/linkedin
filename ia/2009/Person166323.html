Mitul Thobhani’s Summary

In my early years all of my 'dabblings' stemmed from the joy of discovery and the appreciation of beauty and I worked on research and development in the telecommunications and knowledge management sectors, but as responsibilities started to bite and the economy dived, profits took a firm grasp, but I didn't stop yearning for those playful days and so I left the corporate world and started a new journey as a freelancer specialising in web applications.

In 1998 I met my business partner Mike and realised that we share similar goals and ideals and so we decided to start our own company resulting in the launch of Portfolio Software in 2002.

The business was built and profited from my expertise in web technology, Mike's business process knowledge and our complete understanding that the world is indeed flat, but behind the scenes we both pandered our passion for mathematics, semantics, ai, visualisation and rich experiences.

In 2007 we decided to split the business into specialist divisions and spawned Epsilica Consulting which operates in the management and implementation of change.

Since 2002 we have delivered software solutions and consultancy to public and private sectors organisations across the range of business domains from pharma, finance, and media through to startups and not-for-profits.

One of the core skills we have mastered is the management of delivery using distributed teams, whether onshore, near-shore or offshore. This experience and understanding has allowed our clients to achieve their project goals whilst extracting the most value from their investment.
