    April 2005 — Present (3 years 11 months)

    For the last several years I have been working for Construction Resource Technology, Inc., creators of BidTool.net. BidTool is an online subscription based service that provides general contractors, subcontractors, suppliers & manufacturer reps crucial construction leads and data. In a nutshell our customers use the information we provide to win projects. More than 90% of the projects reported by my company are won by BidTool subscribers!
