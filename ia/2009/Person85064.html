    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    October 2008 — Present (1 year)

    As a Client, what can we do for you?
    First and foremost, our recruitment teams will find the people that you need. The right people, with the right skills, at the right cost and at the right time. Whether contract or permanent, whether a single individual or a whole team, we have access to an enviable pool of available expertise which can be put at your disposal.

    In addition to our IT recruitment activities, we offer a range of consulting services which either assist in the selection, management, review or replacement of your HR suppliers; or help you to improve and control your internal recruitment process; or just help you make the best of the staff you have; or ensure that your current staff are fully compliant with local legislation.

    As a Candidate, what can we do for you?
    Simply put, we’ll go further to find you the right job whether contract or permanent. Our Client relationships provide us with a unique perspective and advantage over much of our competition to provide you with the inside track on current vacancies.

    Where do we operate?
    Throughout Europe and beyond with our business being largely spread evenly amongst the major industrial nations.

    What is our speciality?
    As a recruitment company, our main focus is the supply of IT and Telecoms professionals on a contract and permanent basis through all the major industry sectors, technologies and applications. We also have niche activities in technical sales, executive and interim recruitment.

    As a consulting company, we try to offer a range of services to minimise your recruitment burden by improving your recruitment processes and maximising the use and compliance of your existing staff.
