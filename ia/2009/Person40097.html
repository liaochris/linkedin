Lisa B. Helfer’s Summary

Lisa Helfer is the President and Founder of Celesta Public Relations. She has over 15 years experience in the development of public relations and marketing campaigns, event planning, and branding initiatives. Helfer has advanced expertise in: traditional and online public relations, new and social media, SEO, PPC, SEM, product launches, branding, media strategy, media analytics, media placements, media relations, talking points, lead generation, executive coaching, crisis management, and business consultancy.

For global audiences, she has produced the following types of high level marketing and public relations collateral: fact sheets, talking points, white papers, radio ads, reports and proposals, press releases, pitch letters, web concepts and content, direct mail, e-mail, brochures, journalistic articles, newsletters, event invitations, and much more. She is proficient in AP Style, Chicago Style, AMA, and MLA, Twitter, Facebook, MySpace, PR Newswire, Business Wire, Nexis, Burrelle’s, VMS, Critical Mention, Vocus, and Cision.

She has extensive experience with reporters and the media and has organized events, press briefings and press conferences and conducted media trainings with high-level executives and entertainers before they speak to the press. Her content and project management abilities are at an expert level.
