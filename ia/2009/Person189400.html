Pradeep Sanyal ’s Summary

Currently leading the AOL - Search team based out of Bangalore. The teams include:
- Core search research team, Mobile Search
- a business management team driving search-based revenue and defining business models and advertising initiatives.
- Development and QA teams working on AOL Search
- DataWarehouse, BI and reporting team and Data Quality management

In my earlier role as COO at Asian CERC, I was responsible for complete operations management around delivery of multiple software products. This included defining and managing the key processes across all delivery functions, project management, building delivery teams, building and managing key client relations, managing vendors, defining and architecting the product roadmap, understanding the trends in the industry as well as in technology, overseeing the delivery timeliness and quality, HR activities and liaising with sales, finance and support teams.

In my past roles, I have successfully led software delivery projects, architects and consulting teams, done marketing, pre-sales and technology/solution evagelization, defined architecture for enterprise systems, have consulted for clients in various domains like telecom, finance, eCommerce, manufacturing, transportation. I have led business solution conceptualization and development. I have good experience in developing business solutions, developing reusable frameworks, project management, architecture and design and marketing. I have published thought leadership papers in renowned publications. I am a keen follower of trends in the software industry.
