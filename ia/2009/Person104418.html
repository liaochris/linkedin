Jared Chung Lundy’s Summary

In the year 2004 I relocated to Southern California to expose myself to new opportunities and challenges. While studying television production and AVID non-linear editing at Goldenwest College in Huntington Beach, I started networking myself into this highly competitive, yet rewarding industry. After school I began an internship at Torrance Community Television, and soon moved into a position at Warning TV where I honed my production skills while expanding my network connections. After about a year I put a solid plan together and started Checkmate Films; the rest, well… is history!
