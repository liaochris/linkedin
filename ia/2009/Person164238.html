Mike Fish’s Summary

An inspiring and motivational business leader with a track record of growing and transforming companies in high technology, VC investment, consulting, media and utilities – both established or growth businesses. Has worked in Europe, North America and Asia-Pacific – directly managing direct sales teams (up to £400m/yr) and distribution partners. Experienced Board Chairman and Board member, but equally at ease in interim General Manager role. Prior investor experience ensures best possible alignment between boards and their investors.

Operational support and investment-readiness for accelerated growth - especially for B2B companies developing international markets. Strong on refining value proposition articulated to investors and/or early customers, and on improving sales effectiveness.

Introductions to investors, partners, acquirors, and end-user enterprises (including European market entry for non-European vendors).

Recent experience with ventures in the emerging Software-as-a-Service market. Available for new projects - both board and operational.
