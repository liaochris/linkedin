Troy Jensen’s Summary

Troy Jensen has spent his career integrating the emerging technologies and strategies of digital marketing, business development and sales, and operations into sophisticated  offerings built on the strengths of industry-leading technology solutions for many Fortune 500 clients over the past 12 years. His areas of expertise comprise both the advertising agency, publisher, and ad network sides of the marketing space. Areas of strong experience include generating new business; structuring world-class sales and account management teams to maintain a longtime loyal client base; assisting online media brands create revenue by building sales capabilities; monetization and business development models; early-stage fund raising; growing quality site traffic and monetizing traffic with inventory yield management models. Other areas of experience include intricate cross-media campaign integrations; advanced Web 2.0 social media executions; mobile marketing and application development; digital ad agency operations; creation of branded and unbranded digital media content; branded online gaming; and extensive experience in online video content syndication and distribution. Troy has lead both agency, ad network and publisher teams responsible for over $400 million dollars in digital advertising campaigns, mobile campaigns, social media campaigns, cross-media integration initiatives, and online content syndication revenue during his career.

When not consumed by all things digital, Troy passion is in the air - he has his visual private pilots license and is currently in training for his instrument rating. Other hobbies include reading just about anything he can get his hands on, and is an avid Miami Dolphins fan. Troy has spoken at many digital forums and events, and has contributed to articles and research in relation to the latest happenings in the emerging digital mediums, and early-stage funding and growth strategies for startups.

    (Internet industry)

    January 2007 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    Jensen Digital Media is a digital revenue consultancy based in Los Angeles with offices in Phoenix and New York dedicated to assisting digital media companies create revenue for their digital assets by building site traffic, developing sales capability, ad sales representation, raising early-stage capital, enhancing business development, traffic monetization models, and assisting with exit strategies. Jensen Digital Media provides digital revenue consultancy services for several high-profile online publishers, entertainment brands, content providers, production studios, online gaming firms, digital ad agencies, online ad networks, as well as a deep expertise in digital content distribution and syndication across across multiple delivery platforms, including online video, mobile and an expertise in social media viral distribution through share-to-social technology.

  • Senior Vice President, Global Sales and Marketing

    Optimate Networks, Inc.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2007 — July 2008 (1 year)

    Troy Jensen served as Senior Vice President of Global Sales and Marketing for Optimate Networks, a digital video syndication and distribution network, delivering professionally produced video over the web, mobile and through viral social media applications and widgets. The Optimate content management system ingests video content, transcodes it for the production studios or broadcast networks, and infuses the video files with proprietary meta data that allows Optimate to target video contextually and segment traffic. Jensen's tenure with Optimate saw the network achieve record year-over-year ad sales revenue increases in every quarter. In his first three months with Optimate, ad sales revenue jumped 800% from the preceding quarter. He also assisted with publisher and video entertainment content acquisition efforts content producers, and in early 2008 Jensen lead Optimate in obtaining it’s first major digital content syndication agreement with a major broadcast television network.

  • President and Founder

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2007 — August 2007 (8 months)

    Troy served as President and Founder of Edge Interactive Media, Inc., a full-service interactive ad agency specializing in producing online video productions and Interactive marketing advertising campaigns. Edge assembled a team with expertise in navigating the constantly-changing Interactive landscape as mediums merge, video content migrates, and consumer behavior shifts. Edge Interactive excelled at helping advertisers and publishers alike in creating the intimate brand connection so important in building awareness and meeting defined campaign goals. Troy also oversaw online video content syndication strategies and site monetization. As a result of Jensen's long history and expertise in online advertising, Edge was able to form strategic partnerships with several high-profile Interactive technology platforms to solidify an agency technology infrastructure with the ability to provide granular targeting and reporting in real time.

Included in his responsibilities was new business growth, and he helped drive annual increases in revenue for eight of the almost nine years he served at the agency.
