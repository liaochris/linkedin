Heather Lewis’s Summary

I have my BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts) degree in Fashion Design from the International Academy of Design and Technology (Chicago). I sold vintage clothing online during college. After graduating, I worked in the fashion industry as a Product Manager for approximately a year for a company called Heyman Corporation.

I returned to Houston, Texas in 2004 and started my Masters degree in Education with a focus in Curriculum and Instruction. I completed my Masters degree in July 2007.

I currently design and develop apparel.

Heather Lewis’s Specialties:

Product development (working with overseas vendors), sewing, pattern drafting, tech packs, costing, sketching, draping, illustration, buying and selling vintage clothing and accessories.
