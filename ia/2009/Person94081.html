Hemant K. Sabat’s Summary

Hemant is President & CEO of Coscend Communications Solutions.
• In 2004, the US Government recognized him as an “Extraordinary Ability” business leader for being in the top 1% in the telecom and IT industry.
• He received a national award from the President of India in 1992.
• His leadership has received over 500 recognitions and citations.


As a global business leader at Perot Systems, Nortel Networks, Sabre and Fannie Mae, he has:
• Led an outsourced organization that helped build a 6-person start-up into a $250M acquisition;
• Built 7 next-generation telecom product portfolios ($2B);
• Built a $10M global telecom-IT operation with a 35% operating margin in 6 months;
• Executed programs ($1B);
• Led the transformation of a $6B telecom business during the last recession; and
• Reengineered a $15M operation.


His contributions as an industry leader include:
• Presenting as an industry visionary and keynote speaker in the US and Europe;
• Serving as an industry advisor to telecom company boards in the US, the UK and Canada;
• Serving as an honored guest at industry events; and
• Serving as a member of 7 IPR review boards.


This leadership has been published, as best practices, in over 25 books and trade journals by the world's largest media companies. These publications have been incorporated in a few MBA curricula in the US and the UK. He has served as a Visiting Professor in the University of Dallas MBA program as well.


He graduated first in the university summa cum laude with Bachelor and Master (GPA 4.0) of Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology (India), and received his MBA with two majors, two minors and three certifications from Indiana University Kelley School of Business (US).


A former tennis and field hockey professional, Hemant has hiked six 20,000-foot peaks in the Himalayas in 15 days, won a 20-mile mountain marathon and is proficient in seven languages. He jogs five miles daily.
