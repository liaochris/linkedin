Holly Gunter’s Summary

With 9 years in the lab, I have the skills, knowledge, and expertise to find the best available talent in the scientific arena. My experience includes 3 years at one of the world's top forensic science facilities, The McCrone Group. After receiving my MS, I worked as a Research Associate at Michigan State University in the esteemed Biomass Conversion Research Laboratories. My project was to develop different protein mixtures (through expression or purification) to optimize biomass conversion into sugars to be fermented into ethanol.

Holly Gunter’s Specialties:

I have a MS in Medical Biochemistry and Molecular Biology and am an Adjunct Professor, teaching Organic Chemistry. For my MS, I researched the tertiary structure of chromatin by developing methodolgy using synthetic DNA and recombinant, modified histones. This was funded by NIH and The American Cancer Society.
I excel at placing chemists, biochemists, engineers, and QA/QC personnel from assistants through C-level in Pharma and biotech companies.
