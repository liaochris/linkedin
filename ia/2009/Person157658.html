Michael Davidson’s Summary

Michael Davidson is a results-oriented B2B marketing professional with over 9 1/2 years of marketing and communications experience with proven success working in highly matrixed, challenging and ever-changing business environments. He is currently a Marketing Manager at CNSI, a provider of innovative IT business solutions for government and commercial enterprises based in Rockville, MD. Prior to CNSI, he spent the bulk of his career serving in various marketing capacities at BearingPoint/KPMG Consulting, one of the world's largest providers of management and technology consulting services, and as a Marketing Manager for Ingenix, a leading healthcare information company and subsidiary of UnitedHealth Group. He began his career working for a boutique telecommunications provider, ACN.

Michael Davidson’s Specialties:

Marketing Planning & Strategy, Business Development & Lead Generation, Direct Response Marketing, Market Research & Intelligence, Alliance & Channel Marketing, Program Design & Implementation, Program Measurement & Reporting, Sales & Marketing Automation, Product Marketing, Brand Management, Media & Analyst Relations, Marketing Communications, Advertising and Events Management.
