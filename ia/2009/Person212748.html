Sean Stoner’s Summary

I am a serial/parallel entrepreneur with a strategic focus with broad experience in all aspects of starting, operating and exiting the business. I focus on value creation, sustainable growth, competitive advantage and innovation through a philosophy of technology enablement, driving growth through referral marketing, customer satisfaction through intellectual capital leadership and employee esprit de corps, cost efficiency, and strategic leadership. I also possess significant experience with corporate formation, capital structuring, private equity finance planning and private placement memoranda creation.

My focus is on developing new ideas into successful companies. Industries of interest include Information Technology, Finance and Services.
