Michael Donnelly’s Summary

As Group Director, Worldwide Interactive Marketing for The Coca-Cola Company, I am responsible for increasing the understanding, testing, adoption and effective use of Digital Marketing and Emerging Media.

Working in the interactive marketing arena since 1998, I've recently been focused on creating effective programs in Social Networking, Consumer Generated Media, Virtual Worlds and on expanding the company’s use of Search Engine Marketing.

Programs under my supervision have recently won “Best in Show” at OMMA, Silver and Bronze awards at MIXX, a Creative Media Award from Media Magazine, 2 achievement awards from the Society for New Communications Research and an Emmy nomination for "Outstanding Achievement in Advanced Media Technology for Best Use of Commercial Advertising on Stand Alone Broadband Devices“.

I was with Johnson & Johnson for 9 years prior to joining The Coca-Cola Company. There, as part of their Global Marketing Group, I led their Interactive Marketing Center of Excellence and focused on developing an incoming pipeline of emerging media opportunities and on creating a community for marketers to test, learn and share experiences.

Before J&J, I spent nearly five years each with Beiersdorf Inc. and SmithKline Beecham in consumer product sales management positions focused on accounts such as Wal-Mart, Walgreens, CVS, AHOLD, COSCO and many others.
