Dayvid Jones’s Summary

I have a diverse set of media design and production skills in TV, film and new media. And over 10 years of experience building multimedia and Flash web sites, including more than 5 years building an online kids community and game Flash site.

I produce, product manage, design, animate and program. I’m a Flash expert familiar with all it’s many facets and uses, from art to programing. I thrive when working with the creative team bringing to the table technical insight with creative solutions and ideas, and then building the production process that bridges art and technology through prototypes, wireframes, and information and design layouts. I’m an ActionScripter able to program in AS2 and AS3.

I specialize in kids content and games.

I also compose music loops and create sound FX, and have produced and engineered live audio and video streaming events.
