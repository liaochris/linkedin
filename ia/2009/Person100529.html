Ivo Legon’s Summary

After having built up a broad experience as a Sr. management, functional and business analyst for many years I've been involved in a multitude of national and international projects in several roles; as a pre-sales consultant, technical, business & management consultant, project manager and as a trainer. With this experience a CRM tool implementation methodology was developed some years ago and used for several projects, the methodology was called "In Control". Over the years I have been working on CRM and Business Intelligence implementations on different platforms and applications, which makes me very flexible when it comes to assisting in product selections, performing analysis or training on newly implemented environments. I am very communicative and have good language and presentation skills. I also taught CRM Sales & Marketing at the Antwerp SCVO-BIRM highschool division Marketing & Publicity for adults & professionals and was a guest teacher at the Antwerp University's Master in e-Business program.
