Kerry Cassetta’s Summary

During the course of ten plus years, successfully fulfilled roles in project management, business development, systems integration, change management and business process analysis and re-engineering. Fulfilled roles in each functional step of the software development life-cycle including project manager, business analyst, system tester, technical system administrator, configuration analyst, technical documentation specialist, training course developer and implementation analyst.
