Allen Borza’s Summary

Co-Owner, Chief Environmental Officer - Facilitate the synergy of beautiful design and eco-friendly printing practices such as using FSC certified paper and recycled paper and eco-friendly inks in our print media. I help clients to understand their availability of green choices when it comes to marketing materials and their image. Corporate responsibility is the first step in changing the world.

Whether you are a company with a green product, or a company that doesn't have the first clue about going green, we can help you to cut through the greenwashing, and help you to implement green practices from the marketing materials to office policies.

Be a part of something greener. Green Group Studio.

Allen Borza’s Specialties:

corporate identity and branding, print media (brochures, direct mailers, invitations) business & community websites/forums, 3d rendering & animation & studies, presentation graphics.
