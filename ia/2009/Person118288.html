John Morley’s Summary

Over the last sixteen years John has invested thousands of hours and hundreds of thousands of dollars studying and being trained by recognized world masters in the cutting-edge sciences of influence and persuasion as they apply to sales, advanced training/learning, and marketing strategies.

Masters such as Professor Wyatt Woodsmall (he trained Anthony Robbins who is said to be the world’s best personal development coach) and Master NLP Trainer Marvin Oka.

John was also personally trained by Jay Abraham who is said to be the world’s highest paid marketing man achieving over $3.5 Billion in extra profit for his client companies.

John has applied these skills to various sized companies, from BP’s training managers to individual coaching projects and franchised businesses.

John has had extensive experience with small business, having owned and managed a number of companies: He set up a tourist resort which won a Victorian Tourism Award in 1980; he owned a sawmill where he introduced strategies that increased sales and equipment productivity; he has been a registered builder for over 25yrs building over 100 houses, and also owned a successful restaurant on the Mornington Peninsula for 5 years.

He improved training design and effectiveness at BP's technology transfer division. He increased retail sales by 33% at Philip Murphy Wines & Spirits and 50% at Nomad Telecommunications.

He ran the marketing segment of the BRQC (Builder Registration Qualification Course).

His skills include creating “effective communication”... clarifying and aligning a company’s critical objectives and marketing and training message to have one powerful voice that creates a market advantage and living vision, that generates sales.

On a personal level, John is committed to helping people remove any self imposed limitations that may limit their success.
