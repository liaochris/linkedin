Barbara Nowak-Rowe’s Summary

Barbara is a high achieving and recommended IT executive with experience working for major international and local corporate leaders. During her career Barbara has had various Senior Management roles that included responsibility for Managing National Delivery, PMO Management, National Programme Management, National Product Development.

Barbara is commonly regarded as a person that takes responsibility and gets things delivered, always meeting her customers’ needs.

The nature of the Barbara’s executive roles required building of close working relationships with many internal and external Business Customers, IT, Senior Leadership Teams and numerous vendors. This has enabled Barbara to gain experience in a range of accountabilities required to successfully lead, build and manage projects as well as project teams. In her career Barbara gained extensive experience in managing strategic initiatives and programmes of works. She also on many occasions established, built and managed project management offices.
