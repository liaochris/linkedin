Tim O'Connor’s Summary

Tim O’Connor is a talent acquisition consultant, strategist, and speaker originally from Northampton, Massachusetts, now residing in Southern Maine. Mr. O’Connor has just joined Hewitt Associates in September 2008 as their Talent Acquisition Sourcing Manager. Prior to Hewitt, Tim was the Strategic Sourcing Lead for Capgemini North America, one of the largest IT Consulting Firms in the World, with over 80,000 employees located in 75 countries. In this role Tim was brought on to create and implement Capgemini’s first ever Strategic Passive Sourcing Program. He was also brought on to manage the existing Sourcing Team in Kolkata India, which is responsible for developing both active and passive pipelining for NA recruiting. Mr. O’Connor made several trips to Kolkata to work with the team in person to deliver in-depth training and conduct reviews of the team’s performance. In addition, Tim was part of the North American Recruitment Management Team where he has major influence over the strategic direction and solutions for recruiting in North America. Tim has also created and delivered multiple in-depth trainings to all of the North American and Kolkata recruiters on how to find candidates through deep web mining and leveraging sites such as LinkedIn, Google, and Live.

Because of his passion for the Internet as a recruitment tool, Mr. O’Connor has developed a reputation as an authority in passive candidate research and talent pipeline development. Tim has been invited on several occasions as a guest speaker and panelist by such organizations as HCI (Human Capital Institute), SourceCon, and ERE (Electronic Recruitment Exchange).

Most recently, Tim spoke on “Digging through Diigo" at ERE’s March, 2008 Conference in San Diego. He also spoke at the Kennedy Recruiting Conference in Orlando in November 2008 on what it is like to work and build an Offshore Sourcing Team.
