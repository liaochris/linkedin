Ron Albertsen’s Summary

Ever since I was a kid in the sand box I have had a love of large machines. I began my background with heavy equipment in The USAF, as a Special Purpose Vehicle Technician and Technical Instructor. I also served as a US Navy CB 8 years and retired from the US navy with twenty years of service. Since that time I have been a Field Service Tech, Service Training Manager for a Cat dealer, College Instructor with Oklahoma State University, and now am employed with Komatsu America as a Service Engineer on the Electric Drive Mining Trucks
