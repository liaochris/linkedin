Kimberly Doss’s Summary

Accomplished and respected Senior Corporate Recruiter with demonstrated results driving recruiting and staffing initiatives for companies such as Genentech, Cisco, IBM, Esurrance, Packeteer, Bank of America and Charles Schwab. Industry disciplines have been in Pharmaceutical, Software, Technology and Financial Services.
Outstanding background utilizing effective interpersonal & motivational traits and philosophies have proven beneficial to the placement success and achievement of corporate staffing initiatives.
Instrumental in developing and delivering executive search reports to identify corporate attraction, competitive assessments, impact of the positions, core intellectual capital, and sourcing strategies.
Partnering with the hiring managers to develop position specifications and formulate search methodologies to practice effective recruitment of the most qualified candidates.
Utilized effective closing techniques during the offer stage clearly identifying candidate strengths while working through objections and addressing potential challenges.
Negotiate and extend offers of employment including any necessary conditions, base salary, incentives, bonus and relocation services.
Developed sustainable business opportunities through effective marketing, tenacious cold-calling and solid networking while leveraging referrals to engage new long-term clients.
Effective development and delivery of specialized workshops and programs in Social Media, Teambuilding, Achieving Stellar Customer Service, Train the Trainer and Powerful Presentation Skills.
Developed Instructor Guide and Participant Handbook necessary to personally deliver educational workshops on Diversity and Sexual Harassment to more than 800 employees, while serving on the Greater Bay Region’s Diversity Council.
