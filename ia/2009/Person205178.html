Rudy Torres’s Summary

Career Objective:

An executive / director level role involved in IT Strategy, Architecture, Planning and Enterprise Architecture to assist in the alignment of technology to support the needs and requirements of the business, preferably in the Telecommunications industry - but open to other industries as well (e.g. Finance / Banking sector) - leading to a CTO/CIO type role in the long-term.

Professional Experience Summary:

Rudy has almost twenty years of experience in systems consulting and integration, outsourcing, and complex Business Transformation projects involving Business and Operational Support Systems (BSS / OSS). He has a widespread background spanning many roles and functions in business strategy, business development, sales support and pre-sales technical consulting, business and systems consulting, systems integration and outsourcing, proposition development and bid management, project and team management, enterprise and solution architecture, technology strategy and planning, software development and implementation, and technical support. Additionally, Rudy has six years of professional military experience (Active Duty and Reserve Duty - US Army),

Rudy's obtained his experience working as a Principal-level Consultant, Engagement Leader and Lead Solution Architect on numerous large scale and complex BSS / OSS and IT/IS engagements and Business Transformation programmes derived from many years in North America, EMEA (Europe, Middle East and Africa), ASEAN and Asia Pacific for customers (Network Operators, Service Providers, etc.) and consulting and systems integrators involved in the IT and Telecommunications industry (such as, Oracle, BSkyB, Vodafone, IBM, CSC, Swisscom, AMS and CBIS).
