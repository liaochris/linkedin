Paolo Iacono’s Summary

Degree in economics at the Bocconi University in Milan (medal of honor for best graduates), several trainings in marketing and strategies, I developed my professional background by serving during several years in blue chip FMCG multinational corporations (Unilever, Sara Lee, Mondadori,). I had occasion to lead for almost 7 years a leading company in booming videogames market, driving its start up and to create Videogames Industry National Association (AESVI). Actually I’m leading a leader company in Toy and entertainment business My core competence is in the fields of team building operations management, business development, and leadership, marketing and sales, strategy and communications. Nevertheless, during my work experience I have had the chance to manage and face all aspects of a company life , which gave me a broad exposure to all finance matters, licensing, legal affairs. Over 10 years of MD and executive management team experience
