Cory Mausolf’s Summary

Cory joined the staffing industry in 2002 working with hiring managers to fill positions in Management, Information Technology, and Sales.

Originally born and raised in Minnesota, Cory relocated to Indianapolis to join the Management Recruiter Incorporate (MRI) Indianapolis-North office. Her specialty area with the top-ranked MRI Office was recruiting Sales Management and Sales Professionals.

From there she noticed her love of helping people find the right career path and assisting them in achieving their goals. Fortunately she had found a company that would allow her to do just that. Working with a top career coaching company introduced her to the concept of coaching and gave her the ability to gain certifications in Resume Writing, Career Coaching, and Etiquette training.

Coaching has come naturally for Cory and since starting her business she has helped many people obtain their authentic career through coaching. With her five years of experience in recruiting, and now three years of personal coaching she has found her dream job now let her help you.

For the candidate with either entry level or years of experience, working with Cory affords you a professional relationship with an experienced coach. With her years of experience she has the understanding of what employers are looking for and can help you market your skills. You'll find her to be responsive while treating your career with the utmost respect.

Employers working with Cory will find that she is an asset to your organization because she can help reduce recruiting cost by introducing someone who knows what their strengths and interests are in the position they want to pursue. This allows for the position to be staffed correctly and with the most qualified candidate eliminating lost production and costly set backs.

Cory provides one-on-one and group training in Business, Career, and Etiquette training. Please contact her today for your free consultation on services.
