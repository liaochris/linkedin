Mark Schoneveld’s Summary

I am the Senior Community Manager and corporate blogger at XLNTads.com, a web-based community connecting major brands such as Nestle, Unilever, Anheuser-Busch, New Line Cinema and Callaway Golf with independent video creators for commercial media development. I am active in many areas of the business, from project management to UI development to social media marketing and strategy.

My company, Poverty Jet Set Productions, is an award-winning video production and social media company. We created one of the first fully-sponsored web series in Philadelphia, The Illadates Show on uwishunu.com which won a 2008 Bronze Anvil Award from the Public Relations Society of America. We create and consult companies on cutting-edge, web-based new media: video podcasts, blogs, music videos, live music performance videos and documentaries.

I have several side projects online: a music blog (http://yvynyl.tumblr.com) and my personal blog (http://thepovertyjetset.com).

Please feel free to follow me on http://twitter.com/povertyjetset
