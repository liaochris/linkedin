Mike Harrahy’s Summary

Human Resources Leader with strong experience in Employment, Compensation, Cost Reduction and Organizational Development. Strategic ‘HR-as-Value-Add’ thought leader. Capable consensus builder, who is able to craft tactical implementations solving complex problems and producing results. Possesses extensive Employment and Human Resources experience in verticals which include Enterprise Software, Consumer Electronics, Networking, Telecommunications, Healthcare and Insurance industries. Employer and Client References are available online, or by request.

Since 1987, my career has been directly connected to the Employment and Staffing industry, working in both the Boston area and national markets. As an experienced recruiter and degreed engineer (BSEE UMass 1981), I have developed a network of contacts which allow me to successfully add critical staff to technology companies ranging in size from VC funded start-ups to Fortune 100 organizations.

I am *always* interested in hearing about new Employment and Staffing consulting opportunities.

Some of the types of openings I have experience recruiting are:
* Enterprise Software Professional Services (Architects, Analysts, Project Leaders)
* Data Architects, Data Warehouse Specialists and DBAs
* Java Developers
* Analog Hardware Engineers
* Embedded Software Developers
* Mechanical Design Engineers
* Linux & Network Administrators
* Finance Professionals (Controller, Accountant)

Mike Harrahy’s Specialties:

Supercharged contract, contingency and retained staffing services combining hands on dedicated recruiting services, with an 'instant-on' candidate pipeline. Our clients are technology companies ranging in size from VC funded start-ups to Fortune 100 organizations.
