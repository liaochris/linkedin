Sampsa Kiianmaa’s Summary

In WWF Finland I'm in charge of our work on sustainable production and consumption. In the ways we all can make a difference in our planet's future and that way, not only improve our own well-being but the planet's as well.

I coordinate national Market Transformation work, Heroes of the Rainforest operation and a magnificent field project to save about 1 000 000 hectares of tropical rainforest in Heart of Borneo.

I run my own company Vehmas Assembly. The idea is to live to the fullest and share the fun with people I collaborate with and with customers. Vehmas Assembly's main goal is to increase well being, the second goal is not having to pay for doing it.

I'm also the Head Editor of community web pages and enthusiastic community developer. Professionally my aim is to continue working with passion.

Sampsa Kiianmaa’s Specialties:

project and campaign coordination, sustainable production and consumption, CSR, forestry, bioenergy and biofuels, tropical commodities, development cooperation, social media, well being, happiness
