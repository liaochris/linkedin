Kevin Potts’s Summary

Prior to founding what is now one of Europe's most up and coming Internet Marketing Academies, I was a full time minister of religion with the Cambridge Community Church. Hence why you'll often still see me presenting and coaching with a "dog collar" - in reality I never used to where one in my church - far too stuff - but it has helped give me the Englebert Humperdink effect in the IM space - once seen never forgotten or in "Engs" case, "once heard":-).

From a standing start in May 2008, the academy has attracted hundreds of thousands of students all across the globe. I quickly realised online marketing is simply about making relationships, creating value and then delivering it, time and time again.

My mission in life is to help identify and create a new breed of entrepreneur. Ethical, impacting & successful.

The proceeds of my endeavors - which are increasingly becoming vast compared to a pastors salary - will go a long way to helping make a contribution to my charitable strategies both in Europe, the States and in increasingly around the world.

2010 will mark the launch of a new charitable foundation, where my team and I will take people who have hit hard times, living on the street etc, and get them into a division of the Academy supported by drug and addiction specialists, as we seek to help identify those who can make a transition and find emotional and financial Independence as home based Internet Marketers in their own right.

Watch this space as the story continues to unfold.
