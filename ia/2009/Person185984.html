Peter Osicka’s Summary

“Entrepreneurs are simply those who understand that there is little difference between obstacle and opportunity and are able to turn both to their advantage.” Niccolo Machiavelli

Combining creative insight along with promotional marketing and retail trade expertise, Peter is recognized as ‘award winning’ marketing professional. He's been called upon to judge marketing awards such as 2008 MAA Globes, 2008 Reggie Awards and 2004 CMA Awards. Always on the leading-edge, he has personally developed a ‘breakthrough’ promotional marketing campaigns such as BIC “Kick for $1 Million” – Cadbury Beverages/Aeroplan Loyalty Program – High Liner Foods “100th Anniversary of Stanley Cup”, Jelly Belly "Spin to Win" contest with social media campaign among others. He possesses an extensive depth of experience working with global marketers such as Jelly Belly, Nestle, Cadbury, Unilever, Ferrero and BIC. His unique understanding of both the creative and strategic issues of brand marketing, advertising and new media help clients drive their business forward.

Peter Osicka’s Experience

  • Owner

    OptiCom InterActive Marketing & OptiComTookMyPicture

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    September 2002 — Present (7 years 2 months)

    OptiCom InterActive Marketing & OptiComTookMyPicture provides strategic promotional counsel along with social media marketing and 'lead-generating/consumer data' capture photo op services. We bring together marketing campaigns in both "virtual" and "in-store" domains and leverage their effectiveness.

    Our services enable clients to evolve traditional marketing techniques, such as sweepstakes, contests and special offers into complete electronic exchanges, instantly building consumer databases at significantly lower costs.

    OptiComTookMyPicture unique photo op and proprietary web-enabled delivery platform is used by marketers, traveling exhibitions and learning institutions to drive incremental revenue, enhance visitor's experience and capture consumer data for future CRM initiatives.

    We work with clients across North America on strategic business plans and ‘win-win’ financial arrangements to deliver flexible turn-key programs with any combination of services, products and media support.

  • Senior Vice President, Promotions

    The Marketing Store Worldwide

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

  • President & Creative Director

    OptiCom Promotion Group Ltd.

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    January 1983 — August 2000 (17 years 8 months)

    As co-founder, Peter has guided the company since 1983. His creative & innovative executional approach, has helped OptiCom solidify one of the leading positions in the promotional marketing industry in Canada. Peter has personally developed numerous 'award winning', promotional marketing campaigns for Fortune 100 companies such as Nestle, Cadbury Schweppes, Ferrero, High Liner, BIC Inc. and many others.

    In August of 2000 OptiCom Promotion Group Ltd. was merged with The Marketing Store Worldwide, Chicago, IL.

    In the spring of 2002 Peter has formed OptiCom Marketing to handle Cadbury Beverages consumer loyalty initiative with Aeroplan; the first program of its kind in Canada within the consumer package goods sector.
