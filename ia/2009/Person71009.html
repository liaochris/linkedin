Elaine Gaither’s Summary

I specialize in coaching parents of teens. Parents are you worried about your teen? Does every conversation with your teen end i n yelling, door slamming, eye rolling or shoulder shrugging? Does your teens moods control your household? If you answered YES to any of these questions,you are in the right place!

I have a 7 step guide to YOUR Peace of Mind-that is just the relief that you have been waiting for, because we weren't given any manuals that came with our teens.

I know because I have been where you are, I have found solutions and I want to share them with you! My goal is to empower parents to change their mindset, develop coping skills, become the role model that they want to be, enhance communication, build lasting relationships, to be creative with value based discipline and to understand the millennial generation.

Parenting is a dance of wisdom and insight and through group coaching parents will receive leveraged knowledge, insights,awareness and support from like minded peers.

I have worked in an Adolescent Psychiatric Unit, have been a Youth Minister and a wellness coach for a large hospital partnership. I have worked with teens for the last 17 years.
