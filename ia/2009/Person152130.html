Mark Kurtzrock’s Experience

  • President and CEO

    SMART Warning Systems, Inc.

    (Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    June 2008 — Present (9 months)

    SMART Warning Systems, a new spin-off from Sima Products Corporation, is dedicated to designing, manufacturing and selling a unique emergency warning system.

    The SMART System is a set of wall-mounted alerting devices, that are strategically located in rooms and buildings across a university campus. Through a secure website or via a mesh network, the system operator is able to send different messages to different locations. In 10 seconds or less, voice and digital data messages are activated on all selected devices. To maximize effectiveness, the units alert with lights, siren and voice while displaying text on a large LCD screen.

    SMART has been developed with a steady focus on a market where growth has been driven in recent years, by a series of tragic events on university campuses. The SMART system and products have completed prototyping and testing, are primed for entry into a market that is demanding a superior solution because existing systems are inadequate.
