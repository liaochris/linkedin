Nathalie Leroux’s Summary

Double background: Management & Technology

Expert in special effects materials and specialist for over 10 years in metachromatic materials (which change colors) like liquid crystal dyes, thermochromic, photochromic, electrochromic, piezochromic materials, both in their design, production and applications (flat panel displays, inks, plastics ,...).
I just finished an International MBA where I acquired transverse competencies in management, finances, marketing, management accounting and business strategy.
I am very involved in the field of renewable energy, in particular I write a blog about this: http://natler.over-blog.com/
I studied the various stockholders of this industry in my region and particulary my MBA report is at the origine of the creation of the "Energy" Cluster in the Pays de la Loire.
I have a very strong motivation to work into the field of Renewable.
But I'm open to any opportunity to develop a business in any competitive and innovative sector.

As a key technical leader, I have always lead my team to develop new business using the adequate tools always focusing on key innovation drivers to develop market and sustain competitive growth.; and I participated to the strategic decisions that develop Business and ensure the company‘s growth.
I am result oriented and good at anticipating short and long term issues or opportunities; I am efficient at managing high number of projects with high number of partners.
I really enjoy innovation and leading teams.
