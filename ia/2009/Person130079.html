Kenneth Peeters’s Summary

As a Search Engine Marketing (SEM) manager, I am responsible for the international search engine advertising (SEA) activities of various leading (healthcare) customers of Across (e.g. Johnson&Johnson, Lifescan, Janssen-Cilag, Roche, Omega Pharma, Movetis, Northgate Arinso). SEM is arguably the most dynamic growth area in online healthcare marketing; My activities include the assessment, set-up, management and optimization of pay-per-click (PPC) campaigns and evaluation/finetuning through advanced web analytics. Additionally, I also consult on (non-technical) SEO, webcopywriting and run Social Media Optimization campaigns for several key accounts.

Before joining Across I worked at QueroMedia, Belgium’s leading SEM agency at that time. As one of their key SEA consultants, I successfully managed several search engine advertising accounts, including Vacature.com, Robert Half International, Cap Gemini and Vanden Borre. I am highly experienced in the management of large and complex search marketing campaigns. In addition, I have a thorough knowledge of pay-per-click advertising systems such as Google AdWords, Yahoo! Search Marketing, MSN AdCenter and web analytics tools such as Google Analytics, Omniture and WebTrends.

I am highly passionate about the internet and all of the exciting and forever changing (marketing) opportunities it has to offer. When I am not at work you’ll most likely find me in a nearby movie theatre – a passion I have had since birth…
