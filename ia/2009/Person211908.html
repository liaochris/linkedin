Scott R. Kline’s Summary

As Dilbert will attest, cubes aren't a very popular occupation, but we try to make them work for our customers by listening and then delivering what the want fast and for a good price. At Better Source buy and sell used office furniture. We keep a huge inventory on hand.

At GQA we have developed a cubicle from 90 percent re-used content. The rest will be new paint, fabric and laminate from as sustainable sources as possible. There will be no "greener" cube available.

When not selling furniture, I run JCEP, a charity providing scholarships to children in Cuidad Juarez, Mexico. We also build libraries for after school studies and teach English.

I blog about that and Hamburgers, and Office Furniture. I am an amateur photographer and love to travel with my wife and daughter.

Scott R. Kline’s Specialties:

Extremely fast delivery of furniture and services our customers which tend to be start-up companies looking for good value. Our reconditioned furniture from locally used stock saves petroleum use and keeps the jobs local. We also buy furniture from companies looking to liquidate or trade in what they can no longer use.
