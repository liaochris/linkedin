Nick Ris’s Summary

I am a 7 year sector specialist in the provision of Mobile Connectivity and Billing solutions to companies and organisations of any kind.

Founded in June 2000, MX Telecom is a multi award winning leader in the field of 3rd party mobile connectivity and billing services provision. Primarily an IT company, MX Telecom offers an easily accessible gateway to voice and data messaging services, including 2-Way SMS and MMS, Premium SMS, Interactive Voice Response Services, Video Short Codes, Location Services and a range of other related solutions.

MX Telecom works with over 500 clients and is based in NY, London and Sydney.

Sample partners include; 118118 The Number, London Congestion Charge, Nokia, BBC, News International, Amazon, Sun Microsystems, Logica, Three UK and other household names.
