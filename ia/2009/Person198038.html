Richard Theuws’s Summary

In the Netherlands there is a saying which goes "door de wol geverfd" ("painted by the wool" I guess). It means something like 'learned (the hard way) through experience'. That would be me.

Richard Theuws’s Specialties:

I'm kind of the allround specialist. Except for planning, administrating and invoicing there are not many secrets in merchandising for me left to discover, and, as soon as I DO find an area in which I feel not experienced enough, I 'conquer' it.

Richard Theuws’s Experience

  • Owner

    Merchandise.nl

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 2003 — Present (5 years 8 months)

    We started this company when one of my customers at Sportief asked me to develop a clothing line for his company. At first my plan was to integrate these services into Sportief but the owner didn't feel like it and encouraged me to start it up myself. We've grown into a one stop merchandiser, therefore being able to create virtually any product and specialised in distribution and exact-timed delivery all over Europe and if necessary worldwide.

    Since the standards for merchandisers active in the Entertainment industry are much higher than the standards for creators of promotional items our company also works a lot for large advertising agencies, business promotions and sports related items (fan merchandising for soccer clubs, team clothing for Formula 1 etc.)

    Dutch readers; be sure to visit our blog every once in a while, there might be useful information there.

  • General Manager

    Sportief Eindhoven B.V.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Consumer Goods industry)

    September 2002 — October 2003 (1 year 2 months)

    A business in sports-items. Market leader in the creation of punch bags (Europe). My position was mainly focused on new business, product development and maintaining good relations with customers (Everlast, Sport-Thieme amongst others), but I was also responsible for P&O. When working here the idea to start up my own business grew and the plans at first where to integrate this to Sportief. However the owner didn't feel like expanding and encouraged me to start this thing up myself.

  • Sales Manager Europe

    Hiteker Europe

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Consumer Electronics industry)

    March 2001 — September 2002 (1 year 7 months)

    The hectic world of consumer electronics. Most funny detail about this job is that I got it when working on the building of the owner for my previous company; he noticed my working and offered me this job. My main working area was marketing and sales. Customers included the Metro Group, Maxwell, Veronica Publishing. I established distribution deals in Scandinavia, Estland, Lithuania en Spain, besides working local (Netherlands & Belgium). Biggest achievement together with my colleague was a promotion offer with video rental shops, where you could receive a DVD-player "for free" when buying € 200,- worth of credit.
