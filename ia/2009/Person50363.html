Trevor White’s Summary

Creative Branch Consulting

Business Improvement Specialists for the food Industry

Our mission is to assist your business using a hands on approach to problem solving, with the aim of increasing your efficiency and operational profitability.

Our key values of innovative creativity and a passion for quality food services are coupled with the timeless values of engaging people in business through trust, integrity and respect.

Creative Branch offers a unique mix of culinary and commercial services, seasoned by 24 years of food industry experience, from small start ups to international corporate enterprise. We have the culinary credibility to integrate with any size kitchen brigade.

Creative Branch Consulting can work with your enterprise alone or in conjunction with diverse consulting teams. We are available for short term consulting or long term project work.

Core Services:

• Enterprise consumer experience review
• Trouble shooting & root cause problem analysis
• Food product development and menu design services
• Specification management, raw material & recipe formulation
• Supplier development and out sourcing review programs
• Internal ordering, receiving & stock control systems
• Change management, workplace training

Business Benefits:

Developing a program of interventions can achieve sustainable change while improving on foodservice quality outputs.

• Deliver raw material and labour cost reductions
• Core process improvement facilitates repeatable efficiency & accuracy
• Invest in staff capabilities to manage ongoing improvement programs
• Marketing benefits delivering consistent brand product & service experiences

Trevor White’s Specialties:

Cost driver analysis, where can we improve our kitchen operations? Trouble shooting unprofitable kitchens. Business improvement planning. Engaging teams in change management.
