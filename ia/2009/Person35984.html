Jamie Hancox’s Summary

I run an outsourced sales and lead generation company called Buyingtime. We specialise in working with services companies such as management consultancies, IT Solutions and Marketing agencies.

Buyingtime was started to help the owners of growing services companies who didn't and don't want the risk of hiring a salesteam, don't have all the skills and time to open doors themselves, but want any help they get to be of the very highest quality.

We deliver both Pay-per-meeting and Pipeline Management services to our clients, and fortunately this really works... our clients stay with us and we are successful together.
