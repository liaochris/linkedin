I have been instrumental in building 3 engineering teams for new and complex products leveraging the Enterprise Java Platform. As a Software Architect I have been successful in designing and implementing applications from conception through deployment onto production systems meeting and exceeding company goals. I am seeking a Software Director, Software Engineering Manager, Technical Lead, or Architect position for a company that is focused on engineering, development, and delivery of innovative products that leverage the Enterprise and Desktop Java Platform.
