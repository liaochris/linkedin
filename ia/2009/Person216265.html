Shelley Champine’s Summary

Recently, I started an interactive website www.marinlocalmusic.com where fans of live music can find out what is happening in Marin County. The site consists of a comprehensive, interactive event calendar, links to venues and performer profiles as well as a professional social network for performers, venues & resources.

I have been working in event production & stage management since 1989 when I graduated from Michigan State University in with a BA in Theatre (directing, stage management, set design & lighting design). For 11 years I worked for Beach Blanket Babylon (the longest running cabaret show in history) as a stage manager, PR event manager, scenic artist & lighting designer in San Francisco and London. I have also done a variety of event projects for Metro Events, The International Spine Intervention Society and Marin Country Day School just to name a few.
