Mike Kunkel’s Summary

Creator of award-winning book "HEROBEAR AND THE KID"
Writer and Artist of the DC Comics Book "BILLY BATSON AND THE MAGIC OF SHAZAM!"
Designer of the Cartoon Network Show "THE LIFE AND TIMES OF JUNIPER LEE" and also "MY GYM PARTNER IS A MONKEY"
Co-Creator of the book "THE LAND OF SOKMUNSTER"
Story and Development Artist, Character Designer, Animator, Screenwriter, Director, for Walt Disney Productions, Universal Pictures, Sony Pictures, Warner Brothers, Cartoon Network and Nickolodeon, on such projects as HERCULES, TARZAN, CATS DON'T DANCE, PIRATES OF THE CARIBBEAN(animated), DILBERT, DORA THE EXPLORER, MY LIFE AS A TEENAGE ROBOT,JUNIPER LEE, MY GYM PARTNER IS A MONKEY

Mike Kunkel’s Specialties:
