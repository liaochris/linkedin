Dan Evans’s Summary

A serial entrepreneur with a passion for creating refreshing, innovative and exciting business opportunities.

Fuelled by an education in Architecture and subsequent career as a Business Analyst and Project Manager I take great pleasure in bringing new projects to life and ensuring the continued growth of exiting business projects.

The application of the right tool for the right job is the philosophy that feeds my hunger for helping businesses get more out of less. This is reflected in the three businesses I'm involved in: Priority Management North, Altum V and Be Social.

In each of these businesses we continue to work with a great mix of big names and smaller companies integrating our proven approaches into their daily life to help them squeeze that little bit more out of each and every day.

As an energetic, optimistic and genuine people-person, I really enjoy getting the best out of individuals and their passions, both personally and professionally. A keen car and motor sports fanatic (not to mention aspiring golfer, runner, cyclist and squash player!) I apply the same enthusiasm I have for business to my personal relations and interests.

I look forward to exploring exciting opportunities with you in the very near future.

Dan Evans’s Specialties:

• Identifying business opportunities and breathing life into them;
• Providing clarity and focus to any opportunity to achieve the desired outcomes;
• Identifying and implementing best of breed technologies to support best practice business operations;
• Inspiring and motivating others to achieve.

Dan Evans’s Experience

  • Owner

    Be Social Limited

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    May 2009 — Present (5 months)

    Be Social was born out of Altum’s work with clients harnessing the power of the exponential growth of social media marketing. As the only provider in the UK of an end-to-end approach to social media we help organisations engage, implement and integrate social media into their organisations.

    This approach provides clients with a balanced and informed view of the opportunities available to them through social media and just as importantly the associated risks.

    Through a combination of training, tools, consultancy and on-going support clients will maximise the greatest opportunities and minimize the risks of taking their business on-line.

  • Owner

    Altum V Limited

    (Privately Held; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    October 2008 — Present (1 year)

    Founded in 2008 Altum V redefines Business Advocacy with the goal of getting our clients in front of the people they really need to be doing business with.

    By implementing our proven approach to business development through advocates, strategic partners and extended networks our clients are experiencing the real benefits of leveraging their existing networks of contacts who become their best advocates.

  • Owner

    Priority Management North

    (Privately Held; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    May 2006 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    Priority Management is an international training company that specialises in Time and Workload Management. In the last 30 years over 1 million people worldwide have been trained on Priority’s proven approach to best practice personal and team effectiveness.

    I co-founded the North of England office of Priority Management in Leeds in 2006 after experiencing the truly remarkable “Working Smart using Outlook” programme in Glasgow and Dublin.

    In July I was asked to speak at the 29th Priority Management International conference in Las Vegas where our office was also awarded the “Global Growth Award for 2009”.

    It’s true to say that without the control and focus that this training programme provided me we wouldn’t have been able to achieve the success we have with this and my other businesses.
